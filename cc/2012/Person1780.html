Christian Josefsson's Experience

Owner

Sverige-Norge.se

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Staffing and Recruiting industry

November 2008 – Present (3 years 4 months)

Sverige-Norge.se connects Swedish workforce with Norwegian employers/companies. Christians role as Owner, Art Director (AD) and Head of Web means responsibility regarding company strategies, graphic/marketing profile and web based system/company database (external and internal). The website www.Sverige-Norge.se was launched the 17th of May 2008. The company was started the year before that, in September 2007. Since the 17th of May 2008 we have had 30.350 active online users, 40.000 unique visitors & 250.000 page views per month. (Statistics 2011-01-08) During 2008-2010, 3.500 Swedes were sent to Norway through our help.


Norsk:
Sverige-Norge.se er det ledende rekrutteringsbyrået av svensk personal til Norge. I løpet av 2008 ble mer enn 1.500 svensker rekruttert til Norske virksomheter. I gjennomsnitt mottar de 60 nye CV hver dag. Deres database består av rundt 30.000 svensker som alle ønsker å jobbe i Norge. En av Sverige-Norge.se sin styrker er deres web baserte verktøy for rekruttering som forenkler prosessen veldig og dermed medfører vesentlig lavere kostnader.
