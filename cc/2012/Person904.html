Brandon Hollembeak's Summary

I'm an ambitious entrepreneur, whether working inside of an existing company, on a startup, or consulting on other SMB's, questioning the status quo and legacy ideas and methods to disrupt the ordinary way of thinking and invent solutions of high value and low cost.

I focus primarily on identifying significant customer needs inside specific industries, and assist in designing solutions that are beneficial to both the creating organization and the customer.

I'm experienced with both Waterfall and Agile product development methodologies, but favor Agile utilizing Lean techniques for product innovation and optimization, focusing on specific, actionable metrics and short delivery cycles to maximize value and learn quickly.

Specialties

Entrepreneurship, Strategic Planning, Business Development, Project Management, Digital Marketing, and Customer Service and Operations Management.

Brandon Hollembeak's Experience

Product Manager, Apartments.com

Classified Ventures

Privately Held; 1001-5000 employees; Internet industry

February 2009 – Present (3 years 1 month) Chicago, IL

Apartments.com is the leading national Internet Listing Service (ILS) for the multifamily rental industry, providing advertising solutions to owners, management companies and individual properties to find renters and lease apartments across the United States. The Apts.com family of sites also includes ApartmentHomeLiving.com and RentalHomesPlus.com.

Brandon manages the entire suite of B2B products for Apts.com, including innovation, customer testing, KPI/metrics definition reporting and product optimization through the Growth, Sustain and Sunset phases of the product lifecycle. He was also part of the Core Team of individuals responsible for the transformation of Apts.com to Agile business and development processes.

He manages a team of Associate Product Managers that act as the Product Owner of two Agile Product Teams for Apts.com: 1) The data and feature integrations with our industry partners, and 2) the tools & services provided to our customers to manage their advertising effectively.

He also assembled and manages the Business Process Team, comprised of key individuals critical to managing the customer lifecycle through the three phases of Customer Relationship Management (CRM), Billing and Fulfillment, in order to make sure the optimal processes and systems are in place for maximum value and to accommodate innovation needs.
