Chris Hambly's Summary

I run Social Media in Business (SMiB) - we educate businesses in how to use social media.

As a consultancy it has racked up numerous B2C commercial digital experiences with brand names such as The Coca Cola Company, SAE Institute, Direct Line, Big Wipes. SMiB has also planned, organised and hosted a yearly B2B focused social media conference with attendance from the likes of facebook, PayPal, e-bay, Edelman, iPadio, Microsoft, Emoderation and many more. I’ve also freelanced for various agencies helping them build pitches and providing consultation for their digital strategies.

I come with a Masters in the field Open and Distance Education which translated into value to you means I am qualified, and experienced, to create on-line social learning communities for you and your clients, or if you like, edutainment networks, which yield avid followers.

I'm available for digital freelancing, training, speaking, consulting, and new client servicing.
