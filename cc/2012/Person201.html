Adrian Bold's Summary

I enjoy helping website owners cut through the jargon and complexity often associated with improving the marketing of their website online.

My fascination with 'search' started in about 2000 following my first attempt at creating a website. Although I soon realised I had little design ability, I discovered a passion for understanding how the search engines work and how different factors contribute to ranking success.

I've developed my skills in search engine marketing, including both Search Engine Optimisation (SEO) and Pay per Click (PPC) management and still retain the same passion and thirst for knowledge that started over 10 years ago.

I created my first Google AdWords campaign in 2002 and have sat the AdWords exam on four separate occasions.

Please get in touch for an informal chat if you'd like to review your search engine marketing strategy or simply want a better understanding of how I may be able to help you.

If you'd like to connect, let me know why, i.e. at least change the default invite! ;-)
