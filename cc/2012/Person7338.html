Tom Martin's Summary

I help companies learn how to monitor, create and engage in digital conversations. When I'm not doing that I write for Advertising Age Magazine. You can find my articles at http://budurl.com/AdAgeLinkedIn

I've spent almost 20 years helping companies understand their consumer and use new technology and communication channels to reach that consumer.

I was digital fluent before it was cool and spend a good portion of each day staying current and trying to look around the corner to see what will be the next big thing. I find it helps my clients stay ahead of the competition.

Specialties

Marketing, branding, advertising strategy. Social Media strategist helping companies enter and leverage the social media space for better customer service and relationships.
