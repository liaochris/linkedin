Teleco Upstate's Summary

Based in Greenville, South Carolina, Teleco Upstate has the ability to connect the world. Although we proudly work with national chains and up-and-coming multi-branch companies, we are dedicated to serving the tried-and-true local businesses in Greenville and surrounding communities, such as Anderson, Taylors, Greer, Woodruff, Spartanburg, and more! We also serve customers in North Carolina, Georgia, and Tennessee.

Specialties

* Business Phone Systems
* VoIP Business Phone Systems
* Voice Mail Systems
* Data Networking
* Video Conferencing Systems
* Video Surveillance Systems
* Card Access Control Systems
* Digital Signage
* The Perfect Communicator, our very own system designed to help you manage customer service and communication


At Teleco Upstate our business is to provide support for your business.
