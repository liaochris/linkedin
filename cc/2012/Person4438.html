Martin V. Higgins,CFP,CLU,AEP's Summary

Founder of Family Wealth Management LLC, a fee-based financial planning and wealth management firm specializing in providing tax-efficient strategies for preserving and growing family wealth.

A Certified Financial Planner (CFP)TM practitioner and past President of both the South Jersey Chapter of the Society of Financial Service Professionals (SFSP) and the Estate and Financial Planning Council of Southern New Jersey (EFPCSJ).Also a member of the FPA (Financial Planning Association) and a Lifetime Member and 2009 Top of the Table Qualifier of MDRT (Million Dollar Round Table), a Chartered Life Underwriter (CLU) and an Accredited Estate Planner (AEP). A Paladin Registry Five Star Advisor and was inducted into Research Magazine’s 2002 Advisor Hall of Fame, recognizing excellence in the profession.

Co-author of the book, Dream, Inc which features 32 Philadelphia area entrepreneurs and how they built successful companies from scratch. Has also contributed to Dan Kennedy’s book The Ultimate Success Secret.

Co-authored the book, Promises Kept which features 13 short stories of how a relationship with a trusted financial advisor impacted the success of an individual, family or business during a time of financial crisis.
