Beth Campbell Duke's Summary

I work with groups, providing motivational speaking and hands-on workshops designed to help people take a new perspective on work and giving them the tools they need to make it a reality. My public speaking presentations are based on a theme of work and career-education empowerment for students and adults alike. These presentations have been described as ‘compelling and humorous’.

In a ‘past life’ as a secondary school educator, I engaged a wide variety of young adults in their education and future careers, regardless of area or level of study. I've worked with students in modified, regular and advanced stream programming, and was honoured to be selected to work with colleagues on the re-design of a ‘Career and Academic Readiness’ program for ‘at-risk’ students.

Find out more at http://CampbellDuke.com.

My 'Work You LOVE' program is focussed on providing a complete range of Personal Branding services to job hunters and career changers. http://WorkYouLOVE.ca.

Beth Campbell Duke's Experience

Speaker & Facilitator

CampbellDuke Personal Branding (Sole Proprietorship)

Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Professional Training & Coaching industry

2009 – Present (3 years)

Personal Branding is a process that helps people recognize and build on their strengths and then create an effective personal marketing plan designed to move them towards their goals.

Personal Branding is most commonly used by job hunters and small business people - but it's also a FANTASTIC way to help engage youth in their own educations and set them up for work/life success. While the current focus of personal branding might seem to be on using Social Media for personal marketing, it's really about building true esteem through recognizing strengths.

CampbellDuke Personal Branding currently runs 2 programs:
* The 'Work You LOVE' program is focussed on providing a complete range of Personal Branding services to job hunters and career changers. http://WorkYouLOVE.ca.
* 'Werd OUT!' is a youth engagement program for secondary and post-secondary students. http://WerdOUT.ca.

See my TEDxVictoria talk below - and find it at http://CampbellDuke.com.
