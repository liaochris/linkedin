Tom Murphy's Summary

Who I am:
I have been greatly blessed with several successful business careers; I spent 20 years as a wholesale beer distributor (Bud, Coors, Corona & Killians) and then 20 years as a mortgage banker (Chase, CitiBank, Wells Fargo & Bank of America)..

Now, I help ambitious individuals create a home-based social networking business combined with the unique benefit of "INSTANT PAY." Make a sale, anywhere in the world, and you get paid within 3 minutes!

My company is 4 years old and we have expanded into 120 countries and been translated into 25 different languages. We are currently ranked, by Alexa, as the fastest growing direct sales company in the world.

What I do:
I teach folks how to have Fun and make Money from Home! We invite people to view a 30 minute online video presentation. That’s it, we don't try to sell it or explain it. How do you use words to effectively describe video communication? Some things just have to be seen; so, we let them watch it for themselves. Most people see it, love it and want it.

Who I help:
I help anyone who is ambitious, who wants more time, more money and more security. My partners and I show them with step-by-step instruction and personal coaching how they can radically increase their monthly income.

We teach them how to focus their efforts with a simple and duplicatable 4-Step System. Best of all, you don’t have to be some kind of techno-guru or some super-duper sales guy! You just have to be someone who enjoys helping and building relationships with other people and have the ability to invite folks to view a video - that's it!
