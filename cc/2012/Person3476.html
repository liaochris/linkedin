Jason Blackketter's Summary

Upper Cervical Doctor - Genesis Chiropractic - Bentonville, AR.

My purpose is to HELP YOU Revitalize Your Life...through Upper Cervical Care!
Our unique practice is the only place in NW Arkansas providing this vitalistic, scientific approach to health care.

My primary focus is to restore your Brain-to-Body CONNECTION & FUNCTION - particularly the Brain Stem & Spinal Cord.
"Hole in One" Upper Cervical Specific technique is safe, gentle and precise.
I adjust the upper neck - Atlas (C1) & Axis (C2) - by hand to improve the Alignment & Balance of the entire spine.
We NEVER do any twisting, popping, cracking, or traction of your spine!

Vision:
~ IMPACT people with Truth about how the body works
~ ENCOURAGE you to Make a Change & Perform Better
~ INSPIRE you to Be Your Best and Live Your Potential
~ HELP people be Stronger & Healthier so we can make a Positive Difference in NWA

Experience:
Intern Preceptor under Blair Society Chiropractor, Dr. Tom Forest of Pleasanton, CA. His office is part of Upper Cervical Health Centers.
I earned the Advanced Certification of the Knee Chest Upper Cervical Specific group under the leadership & teaching of Dr. Rob Kessinger of Cape Girardeau, MO. I also participated in 2 chiropractic mission trips to Guatemala with KCUCS World Missions.

Leadership & Growth:
Consulting and business coaching under Dr. Kevin Donka about Chiropractic Vitalism has influenced my approach with clients.
I graduated with honors from the Palmer College of Chiropractic, Davenport, IA. I was Co-founder & Lead Instructor of the KCUCS Club, Member & Instructor of the Blair Club. My Internship was with Blair professor, Dr. Todd Hubbard of the Palmer Clinic.
From 1995 - 2005 I was in pastoral church ministry, serving in music & worship, missions & evangelism, and leadership training & development.

Contact me: I'd love to be a Guest Speaker for your business, support group, church, school, or club. Learn how to grow stronger & healthier and handle stress better.

Specialties
