Keith Moore's Summary

Proven executive, lead generation pioneer and innovative business builder with over 16 years of Internet product development and marketing experience. Keith is currently on the Board of Directors of CoverHound and is CMO of AcademixDirect, Inc. located in the heart of Silicon Valley, California.

At AcademixDirect, Keith has led all lead generation efforts since Q4 2010 that have resulted in the company’s +100% year over year growth in 2011, making it the fastest growing marketing resource in the post-secondary education lead marketplace. In addition to dramatically increasing AcademixDirect’s operating margins and profits, Keith has also successfully established new consumer brands and product offerings (including K-12 marketing) that will fuel the company’s profitable growth in 2012 and beyond.

Prior to joining AcademixDirect, Keith was CMO of iContact (a SaaS ESP), where he significantly increased the company’s valuation and new SMB customer growth, which led to the company raising a $40 Million Series B (from JMI Equity) in less than one year. Marketing leadership efforts at iContact included expansion of the internal Marketing and Communications teams, bringing most of the mission critical direct response marketing efforts in-house, launching the new iContactPlus branded service offerings, and rebranding iContact to reflect the company’s new position as a highly recognized resource for email and social media marketing for small to medium businesses. The Web Marketing Association recognized the re-launch of the new iContact.com web site for outstanding achievement in Web Design & Development with a 2010 WMA Award.

Prior to joining iContact, Keith served as General Manager and as a Senior Vice President at LendingTree, LLC. as part of IAC, and then with the IAC spinoff of Tree.com, where he was responsible for owning multiple P&L’s, which included the Short Form Mortgage division and the new Emerging Businesses division.
