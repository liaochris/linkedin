natalie hodge's Summary

Health care futurist Natalie Hodge has 14 years of experience practicing pediatric medicine. Dr Hodge was a featured speaker at the Mayo Clinic's Transform Symposium in '09 on "customer experience" in health and has been named by Brian Dolan of Mobi Heath News as "The First I Phone Doctor"

Dr Hodge is excited for disruptive innovation to transform relationships between primary care practitioners and patients. She connects primary care doctors in transparent direct patient practice relationships to patients in attentive mobile medical practice.

Dr Hodge was recently featured by MIT alum networks of Palo Alto on a panel representing emerging Health IT, as well as an Advisory Board member for the Institute for the Future's recent Body Shock award for the best idea in healthcare futurism.

Dr. Hodge is a member of the Healthcare Information and Management Systems Society (HIMSS), a Fellow of the Academy of Pediatrics' Quality and Innovation Network, the AAP's Council on Clinical Health IT and Section on Telehealth and is a Founding Member of the Society for Participatory Medicine.

Dr Hodge is listed as a " Top Pediatrician" alongside Andrew Weil and Alan Greene as a source of reputable health content online by health content aggregator " Organized Wisdom" Dr Hodge also serves on the Physician Advisory Boards of Emerging Interactive Health Businesses Healthtap.com and Doximity.com.
