Scott Kemp's Summary

My passion and experience is in the computer science field, mainly C++ programming, although lately I have been doing some html programming. I have founded/co-founded two companies in the computer field.

My latest venture with my wife is the contruction and operation of a very nice Alaska Fishing Lodge near Ketchikan Alaska. I still spend plenty of time in the computer field, as latetly I have written a web site content management system for my web site (www.salterylodge.com)

I have also become very active in photography, originally to support the software businesses and the lodge, but also on a personal level as well. I plan to be selling my photography to tourists in Alaska in the 2010 season.
