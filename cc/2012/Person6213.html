Spencer Maus' Summary

Spencer Maus has 16+ years in public,investor and media relations. He has a strong background in business development, with over 20 years in industries that required strong sales and marketing skills.

SpencerConnect, was created with three unique differences in the approach to promotion using traditional public/media/investor relations and social media.

First, the only reason for any promotional efforts is to "Increase Revenues by Raising Profiles."

Second, every client, project, promotion challenge is unique. To meet your challenge, a senior-level, talent team is assembled. Team members MUST have at least 10 years of experience. Team is created based on the skills needed and your business' industry. , and entered the world of what is now know as social media monitoring chat rooms in 1996.

Lastly, we work within your budget, not ours. Most agencies have set rates for a project or campaign. We create a campaign to fit your budget.

SpencerConnect has represented public and private corporations, private equity, investment banking, venture capital, banking and corporate finance firms; law firms, technology companies, health care, international trade, entertainment and petroleum products.

In 1996 Spencer began his involvement in social media monitoring chat rooms for clients. He was an early adopter of LinkedIn (February 2004), and active with Twitter, Google+, Facebook, YouTube, Blog Talk Radio, Foursquare, Pinterest, Facebook, Blog Talk Radio, WordPress and Plaxo. He uses Google Alerts and Tweet Deck to track client news for reputation management. He currently manages or has created over 16 LinkedIn personal and company profiles.

In addition, he has over a decade of experience in the financial services industry as a retail and institutional broker, and product manager, including involvement in internal syndication of IPOs. Registered principal with NML/RW Baird for the Los Angeles area OSJ. Worked for Morgan Financial, a private equity firm.
