Joep Wijman's Summary

Joep is an executive management consultant with a passion for making change happen and making knowledge work.

After researching the impact of organizational change on the quality of working life for the Dutch Productivity Board in the mid seventies, Joep held senior positions at AMRO Bank and Royal Philips Electronics. His last assignment before he left Royal Philips focused on helping the company transforming from an engineering mindset to a market mindset.

Throughout his career he has been leading and teaching organizational change, strategy implementation, process improvement, marketing capability improvement, knowledge management and educational programs.

Joep taught at Dutch colleges on applied behavioral science, R&D management and industrial marketing.
Joep has been a member of the Advisory Board of the Dutch Information Society and chairman of the Committee on Social and Organizational Aspects of Information and Automation. He also was a member of the Chief Knowledge Officers Summit,
Joep performed as a speaker on knowledge management and marketing capabilities in professional conferences including EBIC.
He also spoke on Innovation processes in the 2000 PDMA in New Orleans.

Specialties

WijmanCoaching is able to:
• be strategic in thinking while understanding and appreciating details;
• work with teams to produce productive action;
• design processes that lead to effective implementation and change;
• help people deal with change;
• develop the educational and e-Learning programs that make change and capability improvement stick.
