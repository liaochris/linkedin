Angela Arnold's Summary

I wear a lot of hats - wife, mother, grandmother, coach, consultant, author, entrepreneur – but above all, I treasure the things that money can’t buy, like my loving family, service to others, and the golden rule.

I love life, and I live it on my own terms. I value financial freedom, but I delight in time freedom. Time is our most precious resource, and I zealously guard my time by partnering with people who want more out of life.

I’m a former teacher and college consultant who specializes in helping students get accepted to their top-choice colleges.

More recently, I’ve been involved in network marketing. Although, I’m not a millionaire yet, I have, over the years, earned enough money through MLM to help support a large and growing family, pay private school and college tuitions, fund foreign travel and international student exchange, summer camps, music lessons … you name it, I’ve done it, and I’ve done it well.

I am open to collaboration with serious entrepreneurs who want to play a bigger game.
