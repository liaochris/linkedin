Catherine White Photography's Summary

For over fifteen years Catherine worked in Politics, and Government in the NSW Parliament, and the Federal Parliament.

Responsibilities included all communications within the Macarthur electorate:

Speech-writing, Media and advancing all PR related to the electorate of the former Australian Finance & Administration Minister.
