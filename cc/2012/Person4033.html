Lance and Leon's Summary

Scored the horror feature "CHROMESKULL: LAID TO REST 2" for writer, director Robert Hall. Produced by Chang Tseng and Kevin Bocarde of Dry County Films. "ChromeSkull" is the sequel to the 2009 horror hit "LAID TO REST." It brings back ChromeSkull, who barely escaped death in the first movie and is hell-bent on continuing where he left off... and forging a new path of terror and destruction. Starring Brian Austin Green, Thomas Dekker, Danielle Harris and Nick Principle. Distributed by Image Entertainment and Epic Pictures Group.

Scored the horror/thriller feature "THE TAKEN" for Emmy award winning director Richard Valentine of Valentine Entertainment. Produced by Ryan Gulick and Jessica Von. It's a horror thriller that combines the suspense and terror of "Saw", with the surprising plot twists of "Seven". "THE TAKEN" is six strangers, taken against their will, wake up to find themselves prisoners of a masked Psychopath. One by one the abductees are ripped from their restraints and hauled away, only to fall victim to their captor's demented experiments.

Scored the comedy/horror feature "MONSTERPIECE THEATRE VOLUME 1" for writer, director Ethan Terra of Blood Worx Films. Starring everybody from Tiffany Shepis ("The Violent Kind" & "Beg") to Kane Hodder ("Jason" in "Friday the 13" pts 7-10 & "Hatchet"). Produced by John Carl Buechler ("Troll" and "Friday the 13th Part 7") and Ryan R. Johnson ("Visible Scars" & "Chasing 3000").

Scored the trailer of "THE UNDERTAKERS: ROADS END" for Director Kiven Head. Featuring Richard Hatch ("Battlestar Galactica"), J. Larose ("Saw 3"), Edwin Neal ("Texas Chainsaw Massacre") and Lindsey Haun ("True Blood"). It's a live action film with a comic book feel. It is a story of two small town flunkies who stumble upon an organization that guards an age old secret....Sometimes the dead walk, and sometimes THEY FIGHT!
