Per Håkansson's Summary

Per Håkansson lectures on emerging consumer technologies, mobile applications and data-driven entrepreneurship across the world. He also advice startups on product management and growth strategies leveraging his 17+ years experience from global Internet successes as Icon Medialab, Yahoo!, Blurb, CareerTV, Springboardr.com and Hyper Island. He won the Webby Award in 2008 and has been featured in Fast Company.

He is currently working on a series of Master classes for tech entrepreneurs and writing a book on how to successfully work, live and play in the new always-on mobile world. Per resides in San Francisco with his family where he cooks and enjoys social tennis when not traveling the world.
