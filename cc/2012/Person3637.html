Joel Holland's Summary

Joel Holland is the founder and CEO of Footage Firm, a leading stock footage company that shoots video around the world for over 60,000 customers in the television and video production industry, from NBC to MTV to Discovery and virtually every other major network and cable outlet on air.

For his work with Footage Firm, Joel has been named one of the "Top 25 Entrepreneurs Under 25" by BusinessWeek Magazine, and "Young Entrepreneur of the Year" by the United States Small Business Administration.

In addition to his entrepreneurial activities, Joel writes a monthly column for Entrepreneur Magazine, and in 2011 he was named a Global Shaper for the World Economic Forum.

In 2008, Joel graduated Magna Cum Laude from Babson College, a school ranked #1 for Entrepreneurship by US News and World Report. He currently resides in the greater Washington, DC area.

Specialties

Digital video, online marketing, email marketing, conversion optimization, statistical modeling.
