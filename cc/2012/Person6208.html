The 'Mei Dance' company has been founded by Sophie Mei - who shot to stardom for a bellydance skills on Britain's Got Talent. Mei Dance is the owner of Sophie Mei Schools which supplies bellydance teachers, performers and live drummers for various events, workshops and parties. Mei Dance is proud to represent the MEI dance troupe that consists of five highly-trained dancers - specializing in Eastern Fusion. The Mei dance troupe specializes in fusion bellydance as they combine the ancient art of bellydance with other forms such as, Street, Bollywood and Salsa. The troupe of young women aged between 18- 26 years old are set to be the pioneers of fusion bellydance in the UK.
