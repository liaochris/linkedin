Scott Dylan's Summary

My name is Scott Dylan and i'm an Entrepreneur, I currently invest and work in various industries, including Media, Creatives and Technology. I'm currently based between London, United Kingdom and Dublin, Republic of Ireland.

Entrepreneur

I regularly invest in start-up projects, mainly in the technology and media sector. My most recent investment went from turning over a few thousand a month to now turning over around £250,000 a month. I am always looking for new projects to get involved in.

Creative Consultant

As a creative consultant & graphic designer, I specialised in design and corporate branded materials such as brochures, business stationery & websites.

I have worked on a diverse range of projects over the last 10 years and this has allowed me to achieve a great depth of experience in producing clean & simple (logo) design solutions that directly benefit businesses.

My client list spans the globe and includes an eclectic mix of brands and organisations, from multinationals to startups and charities.

If you are in need of a graphic designer for a project that requires attention, or simply for a revamp of your existing ideas, please don’t hesitate to get in touch.

Web Developer

With over 10 years of commercial web development experience, I can offer your business a high quality solution that conforms to the strictest of web standards, while still being pleasing to the eye.

I specialise in creating functional websites which encompass secure, scalable, object-oriented programming. As a strong advocate of web standards, all of my work is developed with conformity and accessibility in mind. This not only means your site will appear the same in all browsers, but also allows for your website to be accessed by as many people as possible including the disabled.

Previous clients include British Gas, Microsoft, Oxford University, Carphone Warehouse, Virgin Money, SimplyBe, Ministry of Defence, Criminal Justice Network, NHS, and Disney.
