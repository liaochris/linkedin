Network Analyst

Verio

Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Internet industry

December 1998 – March 2000 (1 year 4 months) Boca Raton, FL

Main responsibilities consisted of monitoring shared web hosting servers to quickly diagnose any failures and resolve or escalate issues to minimize downtime. Services monitored on the servers included Apache, Stronghold SSL, Real Audio, POP3, SMTP, FTP, anonymous FTP and MySQL. Any problems affecting more than one customer were escalated by the technical support department and looked into. The customer service department was kept up-to-date on any outstanding issues. I implemented a network status page accessible from customers' control panels to note any outages lasting over 15 minutes. Server response-time, CPU loads and disk usage were monitored to proactively deal with any customer abuse and identify necessary upgrades. Gateway routers were monitored to ensure connectivity. Finding resolution for issues dealing with internal diagnostic, accounting, and tracking tools was a tertiary responsibility.
