Terrance Barkan, CAE's Publications

  • Global Growth Strategies

    • Self Published
    • September 1, 2010
    Authors: Terrance Barkan, CAE

    Global Growth Strategies encompasses the experience of more than 20 years of working with international trade and professional associations and how they developed their international business.

    This 60 page white paper highlights:

    - Global trends affecting international organizations
    - The growth opportunities unique to international associations
    - The obstacles that all organizations must address when going international
    - A description of different business models with their related pro's and con's

    This publication has been used as a reference document by hundreds of organizations, their Board members and senior staff. This document lays out in an independent and neutral fashion key issues and options and is an excellent reference source for all organizations that are either contemplating international operations, or that want to get better results from their global business.

Terrance Barkan, CAE's Summary

I am passionate about helping organizations develop social media strategies and international growth business plans. I use my more than 20 years of rich experience to help organizations grow.

Having served as the executive director and CEO for international professional societies, trade associations and foundations, I speak from personal experience. I have had the same responsibilities of the people I now help and can empathise with their challenges and objectives.

I not only consult on how to grow and manage online professional communities, I have created and now manage my own communities. When I observed that most organizations needed advice on the legal aspects of social media, I created a group that now includes more than 170 attorneys focused 100% on this topic.

I am the author of numerous articles on international association management and social media strategy issues including White Papers and articles that have been published by the American Society of Association Executives and other sector publications. My work has helped make me a recognized Global Thought Leader on association management and social media strategy.

As a former Board Member of the European Society of Association Executives (ESAE), a current member of the International Section of the American Society of Association Executives (ASAE), and as formerly active volunteer in the Association Management Company Institute (AMCi), I have invested my own volunteer time in leadership positions.

My international experience includes a period serving as a special advisor to the Chinese Federation of Industrial Economics (CFIE) in Beijing, China, an umbrella group that includes most of the major Chinese trade associations.

I have had the privilage to live or work in more than 50 countries around the world, developing a significant body of knowledge and experience that I apply for the beneifit of my clients.

Specialties

Social Media Strategy, International Strategic Planning, Business Plan development for trade and professional associations. Unlocking the potential of organizations to grow and develop in new and existing markets.

Developing Social Technology and Social Media strategies for organizations, specifically helping senior management teams understand the role and opportunity of social media in their global growth strategies.
