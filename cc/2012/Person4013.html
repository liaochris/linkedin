Kristen Keene's Summary

Kristen Keene received her coach training at Erickson College International through an International Coach Federation Accredited Coach Training Program. Kristen earned her Bachelor’s Degree in Human Services/Management and is currently earning her Master’s Degree in Mental Health Counseling. She is also completing her Biblical Counseling Degree at Light University through American Association of Christian Counselors.

Kristen has completed a three year co-dependent recovery course through Living Well Ministries, Inc., an inter-denominational Christian lay ministry dedicated to helping individuals address obstacles to abundant life in Christ by using biblical principles and skills. Through this course, she has learned how to identify and heal issues that were keeping her in emotional and spiritual bondage.

Kristen and her husband battled the effects of sexual addiction for many years and are finally free from the bondage of sexual sin. She now supports others by volunteering her time as a facilitator in recovery support groups and writing articles for an online ministry fighting against the sex industry. Kristen also serves as Program Director at Simple Love, Inc, a Christ centered outreach devoted to assisting all involved in the sex industry. She is very passionate about helping anyone enslaved to sexual sin and bringing hope and healing into their lives through the power of Jesus Christ.

Kristen’s work/volunteer experience, education and Christian faith merging together with her life experiences give her a unique approach to developing solutions and helping women overcome life challenges and live in freedom. Kristen specializes in coaching women dealing with a wide range of challenging issues including marriage, relationships, spouses of sexual addiction and grief issues.
