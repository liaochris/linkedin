Tony Bierman's Summary

Mr. Bierman currently works as Managing Director, Commercial Software Division at SharePoint Solutions, where he oversees the design, production and delivery of commercially packaged software add-ons for the Microsoft SharePoint platform. Over his career, Tony has developed and managed implementations of value-added business solutions for Customer Relationship Management, Sales Funnel Analysis, Contact Center Operations, Enterprise Job Scheduling and Product Data Management. Microsoft awarded Tony Bierman the Microsoft Windows SharePoint Services™ MVP award in both 2007 and 2008.
