Jeff Mirisola's Summary

I have run the gamut from assembling machinery to fixing it to designing it. I have worked on designs that help stabilize your lower back to machines that will hydro-form a piece of steel 2" thick.
I am ambitious, driven and dedicated. I believe in hard work, and getting the job done right, period.
