John Lusher's Summary

Hello, my name is John Lusher. I decided to put twenty years of sales & marketing experience into practice by starting my own business. We work with businesses to create and build social networks. These networks identify and solidify your online brand while integrating your existing marketing plan.

I am a speaker, trainer and user of social media platforms for my consulting firm as well as for my clients. Do you need a speaker for a trade-show, class or organization? If so let's chat.

Our firm has a strategic alliance with The Social Buzz Lab and Erika Design to provide HandZon Social Media Training, as well as Graphic Design and Brand Development/Management.

I am Co-President of Meetup 2.0; social media users, novices & professionals that meet to discuss how social media is used in our daily lives and businesses.
