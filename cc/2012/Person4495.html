Jim Banahan's Summary

Founded Banahan.Communications in 1986 with the creation of an extensive medical marketing agency with a specialty in cardiac advertising for the Arizona Heart Institute in Phoenix, Arizona. This campaign increased awareness of heart disease in the community and established my agency's expertise with medical marketing by the benefit the Arizona Heart Institute and its bottom line. This was the beginning of Banahan.Communication's success and the official launch of this agency as one of the leading national medical marketing agencies. Since then, other clients include Mount Sinai Medical Center, New Hampshire Heart Institute at Catholic Medical Center, Banner Baywood Heart Hospital, Blue Cross Blue Shield of Arizona and many more. Banahan.Communications is recognized as a leader in the medical marketing field and is instrumental with increasing patient volume, revenues and awareness for all of his client’s products and services. As an experienced strategic planner, medical marketer, healthcare advertiser, and community relations professional, I look to connect with likeminded businesses and physicians to help them grow and be successful.
