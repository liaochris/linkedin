As a Mentor and Global Leadership Coach / Consultant, I partner with executives and senior managers who are committed to improving their leadership talents. Our focus is often on interpersonal savvy, strategic development and virtual team building. I am a Master Certified Coach with the International Coach Federation.

This year, along with my business associate we have launch our new From Coaching to Mastery Program:

I am known for my energetic training style and expertise in introductory team building activities. In particular, I have delivered True Color workshops to scores of teams.

Another one of my passions is Assessments Now, an online assessment delivery service. We assist organizations through our world-wide network.

Visit this blog at: http://www.AssessmentsToday.com
http://www.AssessmentsNow.com

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

My background is over 25 years in Organizational Development and all aspects of change management. I now focus more of my attention individually and in team settings. In 2005, I relocated from Ottawa, Canada to Geneva, Switzerland, where I continue to coach and consult in corporate settings. Due to my move to Paris in 2010, I designed a special Expat Family Program to help plan and strategize new challenges.
