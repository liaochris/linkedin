Alexéy Sudachén's Projects

  • MoleBox Virtualization Solution

    • May 2002 to Present
    Team Members: Alexéy Sudachén, Olga Marchevska

    MoleBox Virtualization Solution (VS) creates private and secure virtual environment for your software applications, isolating them from the underlaying operating system and other software installed and running on the host device. It is achieved by placing the application and all of its elements into managed packages which form virtual file system and virtual registry in runtime.

  • RUNtoGO

    • July 2009 to Present
    Team Members: Alexéy Sudachén

    RunToGo is an innovative approach to software distribution and management, that gives end-users immediate access to applications from internet or local network.

    With RunToGo, software applications may be launched directly from internet browser or another network client in just one click, without installation on the host computer.

  • Teggo.CL

    • September 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Alexéy Sudachén, Olga Marchevska, Anna Morózova

    Teggo.cl is the pioneer of digital videogame distribution in Chile. We´re developing a subscription based entertainment service that allows people to play all games from our growing catalog without limitation.
    We use a unique technology RUNtoGO that streams games directly to your PC, saving your time and allowing you to start enjoying the game immediately.

  • Dotpocket

    Team Members: Alexéy Sudachén

    dotPocket is a product that allows you to create a virtual display on your Pocket PC. It allows viewing your screen in portrait or landscape mode at many different resolutions. In addition, it includes remote control software allowing you to control your Pocket PC from your desktop PC using your mouse and keyboard.
