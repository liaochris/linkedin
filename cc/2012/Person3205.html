Beth Ehrlich's Summary

Dr. Ehrlich is licensed by the state of California as an Audiologist and Hearing Aid Dispenser. She has been an Audiology practitioner in the Bay Area for over 30 years and the past 28 years in private practice as the owner of The Hearing Center of Castro Valley. She also teaches at AT Still University, Leadership and Professionalism in the Audiology Doctoring program.

Dr. Ehrlich is a founding member of the California Academy of Audiology and past president. She is active in national and local professional organizations. Through these memberships and affiliations, Dr. Ehrlich continues to maintain a continuing high level of education so that she is familiar with the most current developments in a rapidly changing hearing healthcare industry.

Specialties

The Hearing Center of Castro Valley follows its superior Hearing Aid Service with more services and opportunities for a seamless lifestyle. When it comes to your precious hearing, we make no promises...only guarantees!
