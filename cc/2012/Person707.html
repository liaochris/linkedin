Austin Massee's Summary

Austin is a graduate of Bethel University in St. Paul, MN where he earned his degree in Business Entrepreneurship and Business Marketing. He is the owner of a local IT consulting firm established in 2003. He loves to be active in the local and academic community and spending time outdoors. Austin formerly served as the Vice President of The Bethel Entrepreneurship Club at Bethel University. He is an Eagle Scout and naturally loves to go camping, fishing, canoeing, hiking, and rock climbing.

SA Computer Services started over eight years ago with only a business card and a dream to help others with computer issues. This small business has grown into an expanding company serving over 200 small business and residential customers in the Twin Cities and around the world via remote access. Austin is currently forming a new business entity to better serve his small business clients.

In an effort to better serve his clients, in 2008 Austin received training on identity theft protection with Pre-Paid Legal Services, inc. (NYSE:PPD) He is qualified to offer legal memberships to individuals and businesses to protect against identity theft and offer preventative legal service plans.

Austin formerly worked with Lifetouch, inc, (Eden Prairie, MN) the world's largest employee-owned photography company. He served under the Corporate Vice President of E-Business and Integrated Solutions and was a member of the Lifetouch Innovation Lab.
