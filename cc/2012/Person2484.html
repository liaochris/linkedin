Deneil Merritt's Summary

In 2007, The internet entrepreneur within came to life. I first began my career online freelance writing and then I moved onto using wordpress to build websites and blogs that are set up to be SEO friendly for website flipping and blogging. I started learning and applying search engine optimization at the same time I began flipping websites.

In 2008, I began blogging, learning and applying social media marketing to gain followers for the blogs I was writing for as well as adding article marketing to my set of skills with SEO.

In 2009, I began using aweber and learning how to run email marketing campaigns for affiliate marketing and internet marketing altogether.

2011 I have turned my focused on personal branding. Learning all the different ways to brand one's self in a given industry. From being a pro blogger, social media marketing, and etc.

My goals are to find every way to develop my personal brand and teach others how they can develop their personal brand in their niche.
