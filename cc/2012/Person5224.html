Ona Vinyamata Tubella's Summary

I co-founded and co-owned Bamboo for more than 8 years. Bamboo is a private society formed by three communication professionals dedicated to research, strategy and creativity on the Internet and Tech fields.

I work as Strategist and Creative at Bamboo as well as Media & Technology Consultant as Co-Editor of The Bamboo Report.

Bamboo is my favorite spots to rebel against old structures and believe I can make an impact that will push things forward. As I like to say, with all I learn I then train an army as visiting professor at the Open University of Catalonia, SAE Institute and Esade Business School.

Specialties

In no particular order: online strategies and business models, leadership, social communication, cross-media, media & tech trends, data visualization, project management, disruptive thinking.

Internet Curator

Bamboo and You

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Internet industry

March 2005 – Present (7 years)

The Bamboo Report is the base of Bamboo's knowledge, a digital curator.

Bamboo works on the Internet much like a Museum Curator works with Art. As Steve Rubel says we are "sherpas and guides, experts who dive through mountains of digital information and distill it down to its most relevant, essential parts", depending on the interests of each of our clients: news, trends, technologies, resources... We even go a little step further. Art curators are not creators, but they do know when a piece is worth the gold. Bamboo curates information for others on demand, and suggests concrete formats, formulas and strategies based on its slow-cooked expertise.

* Bamboo observes, selects and processes its curation amongst 5000 digital assets every month.
* The final monthly selection results in about 10 executive articles of around 400 words each.
* Each of these articles includes a list of exclusive opportunities for each client.
* Extra services: visualizations of data, in-person explanation of the monthly observatory and Online Media Management for VIPS and brands that seek excellence and control of their online interactions.
