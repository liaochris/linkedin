Mark Figart's Summary

Having spent over twelve years developing software and digital marketing platforms for clients such as Goldman Sachs, Schlumberger, and Invesco AIM Management Group, I launched Digett in July of 2001 to further empower clients with powerful, turnkey, web-based marketing solutions. Built on the application of authenticity in marketing, content management and excellence in design, Digett has evolved into a full service digital media agency focused on helping our clients get prospects into—and move them through—the "funnel".

Specialties

Marketing automation (hands-on experience with Act-On, Hubspot and Marketo) lead generation and nurturing, content strategy and promotion, search engine optimization, search engine marketing, web analytics, systems architecture, team development, process improvement.
