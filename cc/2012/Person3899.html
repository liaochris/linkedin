Justin Brady's Summary

Justin Brady is a quick witted advocate for goal-oriented graphic design. He founded Test of Time Design in the frozen tundra of Minneapolis but eventually moved operations to his home town of Des Moines.

In an aesthetically noisy world, he continues to improve on goal-oriented methods with local, national and global companies throughout the US, UK, and Australia.

Specialties

Print Production, Graphic Design, Web, Logos and Identity, Web Applications, packaging design, Environmental Design, etc.
