Tom Flanagan's Summary

A graduate of the New England Institute of Technology and armed with a degree in Interactive Multimedia, Tom has a diverse background in digital innovation.

In addition to being the technology director at Residential Properties Ltd., Tom is a syndicated technology columnist at Inman News.

Tom has been a featured speaker at both national and local events. He has been interviewed in the Providence Business News (PBN) and has appeared on the popular local radio show, Real Estate Insight, broadcast weekly on 920 WHJJ.

Tom’s passion for design and usability may or may not be directly attributed to his fascination with all things Apple.

When not hacking away on various projects, Tom enjoys checking into cafés on the East Side of Providence and spending time with his wife Kathy and daughter Marley.
