Paul Smith's Summary

I am a sustainable business consultant, social media demystifier, digital PR expert, and prolific business blogger who uses his keen sense and the written word to make the complex accessible for those who need it most - the consumer market.

Whether it’s as a ghost writer for a major green company CEO, high impact social media based PR for green companies, or writing weekly at Triple Pundit about innovative startups and influential major initiatives, I am a catalyst for viewing sustainability in a completely new way. My unique network of connections across multiple realms within the sustainability field continues to be a boon to my clients.

>>> More:

It's simple: I create interest in, conversations around, and business for green (and greening) companies, via social media. Essentially, I get your company noticed by, written about, and discussed on the web by the people who matter, with results to go along with it, and the depth of knowledge and resources to support it. Who I have and want to work with are primarily consumer product companies, a dash of B2B, and I have a soft spot for well funded high impact NGOs and social ventures.

This newly evolved form of public relations that I offer ensures that your business not just survives, but grows. Maintaining your current customer base while finding effective, trackable, meaningful ways to build beyond it is the most powerful way to address the tough times we are in.
