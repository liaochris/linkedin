Robert Becker's Summary

Robert Becker designs and develops innovative learning — including serious games and training simulations — for regional, national and global organizations. He advises clients on learning strategy, leads the implementation of instructional technology and produces engaging interactive multimedia content. He also applies expertise in employer branding, customer experience management and marketing communications to help propagate organizational mission and vision

Dr. Becker's creativity centers on learning interaction as the driver of engagement, accomplishment and outcome. As a researcher, practitioner and advocate he is passionate about heuristic learning strategies including serious game and training simulation.

Direct industry experience includes architecture, banking and securities, bottling, broadcasting, civil engineering, cruise line, executive coaching and leadership development, health care, health insurance, health science, heavy equipment, higher education, information technology and services, life science, nonprofit, nuclear power generation and electrical transmission, Olympic sports, pharmaceutical research, quick-service restaurant, railroading, telecommunications, retail (convenience, department, grocery and specialty stores) and vehicle rental.

Specialties
