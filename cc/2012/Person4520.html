Digital Marketing Director | Internet Marketing

A hands-on digital internet marketing leader who has achieved success in small business and large scale complex global businesses.

10 Years Experience
A decade of experience I have helped clients in business start ups and internet marketing strategies for customer lifecyle programs as brand awareness, lead gen., sales conversion, retention, win-back and market share growth. I've attained this through domain expertise in Search, Email, Social Media, Affiliate, Online Advertising, Analytics and Conversion Optimization and Media/PR.

Hybrid Skills
Experienced in corporate, agency and consulting for BtoB and BtoC clients in over 40 industries. I bridge gaps for business and marketing, business intelligence, creative and technical web design and software development.

Entrepreneurial DNA
This is my fuel having launched an Internet marketing & web development agency, two dot com start ups, two social networks including the HarryPotter brand and a Facebook-like featured-rich social network, a Facebook app. social game and helping teach and coach small businesses in business start ups and internet marketing.

Accolade projects include brands from Olympic Dairy, Bodog and family brands, Business Development Canada (BDC), Yoplait, VanCity, Harry Potter brand, Auto Trader (lease guide), Inside The Line Sports, Cake Poker, Snoop Dog, Sirius Radio and others.

My Radar
On the look out for young digital start ups with an entrepreneurial environment and talent with a sense of humility. Preferably in social gaming, online gaming or mobile. In the end, I follow curiosity and intuition.
