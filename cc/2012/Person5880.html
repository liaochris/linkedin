Rory Siems's Summary

With over a decade of experience in the software engineering field I set out to learn Marketing and Advertising while launching my own successful Real Estate practice in Southern California.

Through leveraging marketing to generate business, I learned and mastered SEO. My current company, Business Web Coach was founded to provide a way to lend my talents for Search Engine Marketing to fellow agents, as well as businesses in other industries.

While in the final stages of launching Business Web Coach, I had the fortunate opportunity to work with the best in the business for online marketing. These experts included Email Marketing, Affiliate Marketing, Lead Generation and Lead Conversion pros.

Without their expert knowledge Business Web Coach may have only been able to offer SEO, but now I am proud to say that we offer much more.

In the I.T. Industry, I have a well rounded background with a basis in Release Engineering, Build Management, Internet Marketing and Web Development. As a Release Engineer, I have had successful positions at industry giants such as SunGard, Verizon, and GE.

That experience allowed me to learn to deal with business on global terms. That I why I am passionate about studying and learning foreign languages, and international travel.

My goal on LinkedIn, is to continue to learn from other industry experts, to grow my sphere of influence, and whenever possible to create lasting and valuable business opportunities.
