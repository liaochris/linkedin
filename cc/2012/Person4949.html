Steve Shepherd (MobileGive.us)'s Summary

2010:
Had such a great time developing the MobileCause.com site and helping the team grow to become the leading software for mobile fundraising, that we decided to branch out. Now we can offer mobile web sites and mobile fundraising for nearly any 501c-3 or even political candidates.
-Text2Give: $5 or $10 keyword works with all major carriers in US/Canada
-Text2Donate allows for text donations of ANY amount
-Free web widgets
-Text2Screen for any size events
-Full web-based, real time reporting

2009:
New site and platform launched: MobileCause.com. Text Donations and Audience Response Systems via mobile and Twitter. Clients include: Charity Water, EarthDay DC, Causecast, National Breast Cancer Fdn, Ovarian Cancer Research Fdn, Union Rescue Mission, Virgin Mobile Freefest, Invisible Children, Big Brother Big Sisters, ....

2008:
Moved from Bend, Oregon to Orange County, California.
Launched new marketing and web consultancy, ZFARI in 2008. (www.zfari.com). The primary focus is to help businesses and non profit corporations succeed by strengthening and extending their brand to customers through the internet (web development and marketing) and new technologies.

Most recent projects include concepting and developing mobile marketing strategies and new revenue production models for clients through MobileCause. (www.MobileCause.com)

Founder, Director of BendWebCon (www.BendWebCon.com). Featuring executive level keynotes from Yahoo!, Facebook, Microsoft...

1st VP, Advertising Federation of Central Oregon (www.adfedco.org)

Background in marketing, publishing, creative writing and broadcast production spans two decades and covers dozens of industries and every type of media. Past clients include a NASCAR and Super Bowl champion to a New York Times #1 Bestseller; from professional golf to televised beauty pageants...
National experience and personal attention to clients of all sizes.

Specialties

Mobile giving, fundraising, branding, creative marketing solutions, web development, mobile web sites (WAP sites), internet marketing, mobile phone marketing, e-mail, PR, book publishing and GREEN business marketing.
