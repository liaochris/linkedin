Andrew Lewis' Summary

Andrew is a highly creative forward thinker, leader and strategist. He brings a lifetime successfully developing and implementing innovative plans, products and the capital to meet high-stakes challenges. With powerful intuition, he often sees what others do not -- paradigm changing synergies with existing assets that create new opportunities in organizations, products and services.

His plans are built on a coherent mix of analyses, models, policies and actions in written form that can be used to build, redeploy and/or restructure the company. These plans are built on real world situational realities and constraints.

Not just ideas -- plans to execute with a focus on start-ups, emerging growth and restructuring.

Specialties

Business strategy, plans and models in both a verbal and financial context. Fast growth planning and execution. Financial analysis, private equity, due diligence, governance, finance, engineering management, marketing, branding, product engineering, technology licensing, web apps, negotiating, next-gen social media, film production, writing. Executive advising & mentoring.

Industries: aerospace, digital products, internet, social web.
