Rich Allen's Summary

Following over 20 years experience in corporate environments, both large and small, with responsibilities ranging from human resources to general manager to executive level leadership, I now work with owners of small to medium-sized businesses to help them think strategically about thier business, build and execute their growth strategies and create high-performance teams to their business will work without them being directly involved. In short, I help business owners work "ON" their business rather than "IN" their business.

Specialties

All aspects of people management (development, compensation, employee relations, etc.), operations and manufacturing including continuous improvement methodologies, sales leadership, strategic planning,large group facilitation, negotiations, mergers and acquisitions, customer relations, team building.
