Tom O'Halloran's Summary

As a businessman, I get that business owners don’t want their employees down because of IT issues, I believe that computers are just a tool, users just want to know that when they hit the ‘print’ button, a document will come out of the printer… no geek-speak, just a fine tuned network.

My clients come to me and stay with me because they know that I am their ‘eye in the sky’, watching over their network to ensure that any problems are resolved BEFORE they happen.

From end users to small and medium businesses, Shamrock IT and Shamrock Online Backup are there to provide as much support as you need, whether it's a PC tune-up, a virus removal, end user training or complete managed IT services for your company.

With Shamrock IT's remote monitoring tools, there is no need for most small to medium businesses to hire a full time IT department, we can monitor everything remotely & keep all your computers fine tuned preventing most problems from ever happening.

For a small, easily budget-able fee, we can relieve you of all your technology concerns and let you focus on your business.
