Kathleen Lyons' Experience

President

FastNet Media

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Internet industry

1997 – Present (15 years)

FastNet Media began as an online learning consulting firm that built on my experience as a staff trainer for small and large organizations.

With the maturity of the Internet both as a learning platform and a client-engagement space, the scope of FastNet Media has broadened to bring principles of adult learning and performer support to the development of the small business web presence. Consulting services include needs assessment, content development, hosting account setup, programming for web sites, owner and staff training, and ongoing site maintenance and content writing as required.
We also design communication tools and social media strategies, review current business and marketing plans, assist with goal setting, execution and maintenance of visibility programs, building and monitoring traffic and statistical data.

Principal

FastNet Travel

1996 – Present (16 years)

FastNet Travel is a full-service travel agency offering travel counseling and booking services for independent and group travel. We can recommend land-based and cruise vacation itineraries for any size group going anywhere in the world, and we will soon be booking seats on Virgin Galactic to the stratosphere!
We particularly like to book sustainable travel, family reunions and tours of Ireland and the UK. We also lead groups of fiber lovers on adventures worldwide through our subsidiary, Textile Travel (http://www.textiletravel.com).

Principal

Textile Travel

April 1994 – Present (18 years 2 months)

Textile Travel is the fiber-obsessed division of FastNet Travel.
Each year, we craft an agenda of travel exploration, shopping, dining and textile appreciation and learning, geared toward knitters, crocheters, embroiderers, quilters and sewers. Each adventure is custom-crafted to bring together a congenial group of obsessed people who like to learn, share and laugh in the presence of their chosen textiles.

IT Consultant, Trainer & Performance Support Specialist

FastNet Media, Consultant to state and federal IT departments

July 1991 – June 2006 (15 years)

Designed and installed online learning, reporting and performance support software for statewide distribution.
Designed physical plant, purchased hardware and created curricula for a statewide Learning Center for public health program staff.
Traveled throughout the state to conduct IT installations, train staff and troubleshoot performance issues.
