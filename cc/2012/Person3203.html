Hayden Creque's Summary

Hayden Creque is a regular speaker and author on business, law, technology, and social media.

Hayden provides counsel in diverse matters, including business, governance, employment, litigation, information security, privacy, and internet law.

Hayden is an experienced civil litigator and an accomplished transactional lawyer. A seasoned negotiator, he has worked on myriad agreements including search agreements (Yahoo!), software distribution and licensing agreements (Symantec, Microsoft, and Sega), and business unit sales.

Specialties

BUSINESS & CORPORATE LAW
- regulatory matters, litigation management, contracts, intellectual property, licensing, sales, merger and acquisition agreements.

EMPLOYMENT LAW
- Employment agreements, employee handbooks, compensation, discrimination, harassment and termination disputes.

INTERNET, PRIVACY & INFORMATION SECURITY
- Certified Information Privacy Professional, Member International Association of Privacy Professionals and past member of the Mobile Marketing Association.
