Courtney Ramirez's Summary

I'm an online copywriter and content marketing consultant. As an SEO copywriter, I focus on delivering compelling, targeted messages to your prospects in order to increase conversions and improve search engine results. Writing alone won't make your organization money - but top quality writing combined with SEO and web usability knowledge will.

As a content marketing consultant, I can help you establish a strategy for engaging your prospects through quality content used in the right way. Your prospects are looking for information - and if you're the one to give it to them, you're the one who will get their business.

I've been writing online exclusively since 2005. I started Six Degrees Content in 2010 to bring my style of content marketing and copywriting to more clients. I work closely with clients as project manager and head copywriter and I'm dedicated to customer satisfaction.I consistently search out and educate the writing team in the best practices in all forms of online content including landing pages, blog posts, white papers and email marketing messages.

Specialties

At Six Degrees Content, our expertise lies in SEO copywriting, SEO content, content marketing plans, business blogging, ebooks, reports and white papers.

We specialize in these industries:

• B2B Services
• Small and Local Business
• Real Estate
• Online Marketing and SEO
• Weight Loss and Fitness
• Natural Health
