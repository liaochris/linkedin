Stephen Lowe's Experience

IT Director, North America

Arvato Digital Services

Privately Held; 10,001+ employees; Outsourcing/Offshoring industry

January 2010 – Present (2 years 1 month)

arvato digital services is a leading provider of integrated solutions that include every step of the value chain, from post-production, replication, fulfillment and distribution/supply chain management, to financial services, electronic software distribution and customer service solutions to support all aspects of the fulfillment chain including supporting the end user. This entire process chain is supported by end-to-end IT systems. Our solutions create value for our customers and ensure that they are well-equipped for the challenges of their markets.

As North American IT Director, I am responsible for directing and leading the technology innovation for multiple sites across North America and some APAC locations ensuring that our systems provide the high level of innovation and reliability our clients demand while maintaining standards and interoperability in a global enterprise environment.
