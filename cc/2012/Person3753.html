Josh Preston's Summary

A young veteran of the indie music scene, Josh Preston has spent his career in every form of the business. Prior to becoming the Director of Operations for Oh Boy Records, he was the manager of the royalties department for the now defunct TVT Records in New York. Additionally, he formed Me and the Machine Records in 2006 to create a platform for non-country and outsiders artists in Nashville. Josh's daily life includes a heavy focus on current and future digital mediums in which music is delivered and consumed.
