Rick Fowler's Summary

I provide business development, sales, and coaching for Kahler Leadership Group. We help leaders and managers who believe in developing their employees but have little or no time to provide the coaching and development.

Our specialty is showing managers in fast-paced, high-pressure environments how they can build both performance and development of staff and ultimately save themselves time.

We've refined and tested Coaching on the Go™ in sales, customer service, service-to-sales, quality management, and regulated call center environments. Managers coach effectively in minutes, not hours, while reducing demands on their time. Employees increase performance and feel more engaged.

Specialties

Consulting and coaching for leadership development and human performance improvement.

Leading teams, building collaboration and knowledge sharing, change management, group and individual transformation.
