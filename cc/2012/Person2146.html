Dave Borzillo's Summary

I am an experienced leader and developer of large enterprise software and engineering automation projects. These solutions have generated results for large corporations such as EDS, Pratt & Whitney, Computer Sciences Corporation, Goodrich, and DIRECTV. I have also helped international customers such as Embraer Aircraft in Brazil and was a guest lecturer at the Politecnico di Milano in Milan, Italy

I have also worked for several start-up software companies whose products are still in use today. Some projects have been published in professional journals and magazines. One project was submitted to the US Patent Office to protect the intellectual property captured by the software solution our team developed.

I also have a masters degree in Space Systems from Florida Institute of Technology. In my spare time I am involved in research projects using various aspects of aerospace technology. Currently I am helping design software for a satellite sponsored by the United Nations.
