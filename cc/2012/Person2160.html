David Donde's Summary

I regularly give public talks on coffee, write articles on coffee and am busy writing a book on Barista Technique. At TRUTH. coffeecult, we are constantly trying to live up to being "The simple elegance of coffee as it should be". Needless to say, we roast coffee. Properly


I write on cars, food, good living. As a motoring journalist I am a member of the SA Guild of Motoring Journalists and I have contributed to GQ, GQ Cars, Blaque and MPH. Currently Motoring editor at motormag.co.za

I present a weekly show on Cape Talk 567 and 702 as a talk show host

I speak on Competitive advantage and all sorts of other stuff. My current favourite talk is "the ethics and economics of tree and bunny hugging and the collapse of the middle market". I am moving more and more into strategy and seeing and describing potential new opportunities.

Lately I seem to be doing the odd bit of MCing

Specialties

I am seemingly a coffee evangelist.
We at TRUTH. coffeecult are Speciality Artisan Coffee Roasters, I function as a Coffee & Tea Consultant. I help build brands. I spend time as a strategist.We run a full time Barista School and I teach occasionally
We end up being marketers for speciality coffee.
