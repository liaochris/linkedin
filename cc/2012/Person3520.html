Jeff Tincher's Summary

I am an experienced professional with six years of Interactive Marketing and Strategy experience managing Social, Email and Paid Search Campaigns; analyzing and reporting on web traffic; on-page SEO; and possess great networking skills to interact with new customers via social networks. During this time I have worked with both small businesses to large e-commerce brands to establish and grow the social channel by knowing when and what messaging will engage people and grow awareness. Not only do I have Interactive Marketing and Social Media Skills, I also come from a long career spanning 15 years of hands-on experience in the Information Technology field. This experience has given me the ability to quickly learn new applications and the latest technologies; also great problem solving and analytical skills.

During my time at TrueAction Network I managed the social channel for professional sporting league clients including the NFL, NHL, ESPN, and NASCAR. I also worked to revise the social channel's guidelines, detailing a three tier approach and roadmap to create and manage client's social marketing campaigns.

In March 2011 I started the Chester County Interactive Marketing Meetup.com Group (http://bit.ly/ChescoIM) to offer a networking group to the Philly Suburbs. The group meets monthly and has 15-20 attendees out of a 150 person membership. ChescoIM discusses the latest news and topics around social media marketing, SEM, podcasting and other questions that members have.

In my "off work hours" I enjoy cooking, tending to my yard, home brewing, and DIY projects. I'm also involved with my children's activities and was an assistant coach for my son's t-ball team and head coach for a youth soccer team.
