Jessica Kane's Summary

Humbled and honored to be a 2012 Woman of Influence Award Winner from the Portland Business Journal.

In a world with a million options, it's hard to find an agency/person who can see the entire BIG picture, I am one of those people. I am a self-made, self-taught, award-winning marketing and branding strategist that gets a thrill off helping business owners get that AHA moment, where social media and marketing all make sense and they can go forward using it wisely to ensure continued success.

I have spent over 10 years learning every design program there is, analytics tools, blogging/content platforms, photography, videography, editing, copy-writing, social media platforms, how the human brain reacts to advertising and subliminal messaging and how to connect all of these to engage your customer and create a relationship.

Branding and marketing is way more than a logo or a website, it's the pulse of your business, it's the blood that keeps it all working. And, times are absolutely changing, it's no longer just about placing media and having great customer service - you need more - you need an upgrade.

When I assist a client - the first thing I do is look at the entire brand - and access where the cracks are. Then, I craft a "marketing upgrade" to help that brand connect with it's wanted customer. I will create, train, strategize and monopolize to make sure that client is on the right path.

Specialties

Marketing, Branding, Social Media, Blogging, Relationship Marketing - Leader in the Plus Size Fashion Market with popular lifestyle blog www.lifeandstyleofjessica.com and publisher of SKORCH Magazine.
