Thomas Gaida's Summary

For almost 20 years we have been helping software publishers to find licensing partners for their titles all over the world. The focus is to take a local product and localize it and sell it via local publishers.
We know the European markets for entertainment and productivity mass market software as well as the North American market.
Recently we started to add software design and product management.

In addition to licensing third party products, we also have a large stock photo collection optimized for computer use, e.g. textures, backgrounds, objects.

Specialties

Inter-Continental licensing and product development (We bring different cultures together).
Software design and product management for consumer print-shop and digital imaging software.
Stock photo collections for consumer software.
