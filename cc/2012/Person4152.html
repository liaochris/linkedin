Macala Wright's Summary

Macala Wright is a Los Angeles-based luxury lifestyle marketing expert, digital media consultant and social media personality. She is widely recognized as a social media pioneer and digital futurist in the fashion industry.

Wright has consulted for lifestyle products brands for 10 years. Successful client projects have included work for eBay Fashion, WWDMAGIC, MAGIC Trade Shows, Teen Vogue, Cynthia Rowley and Sprinkles Cupcakes.

Wright frequently writes about retail, e-commerce and technology for Mashable. In addition to her digital journalism, Wright's digital online magazine, FashionablyMarketing.Me, has amassed an international cult following in the fashion and lifestyle industries.

Wright has been quoted or has had research cited in The New York Times, ADWeek, Women's Wear Daily, Sportswear International, Gawker, Defamer, NRF Smart Brief, The San Francisco Chronicle, Mashable, Los Angeles Times, Apparel Magazine and The Next Web.

Specialties

Luxury Marketing, Mobile Marketing, Social Media, Communications, Digital Strategy, Strategic Partnerships.
