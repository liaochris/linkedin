Brett Greene | Digital Marketer | LION 15k+ 1st Tier's Summary

♦ Social Media Strategist | Trainer | Marketing Consultant | Connector | Speaker | Advisor with over 20 years marketing experience.

Brett is a seasoned marketing executive and mentor who has been engaged in digital marketing services since 1999. Previous to the digital age, Brett was a DJ and promotion director in the music industry for labels including Geffen, I.R.S. and Sire/Warner Bros.

He has developed integrated marketing strategies tied to business objectives for client companies ranging from tech startups to multi-million dollar national corporations. These strategies served industries including retail and ecommerce, mobile applications, social media tools, cloud-based applications, events, entertainment, manufacturing, software developers, finance, education, non-profits, government, and publishing.

The digital marketing strategies and campaigns Brett has created include elements of social media, online advertising, search engine marketing, email marketing, website conversion and usability audits, article marketing and mobile marketing.

By applying these digital production and brand strategy management techniques, Brett creates premium consumer experiences to build digital brands.

Brett advises Techstars startup companies including BlipSnips, SpotInfluence & FanMix and blogs about social media marketing strategy & life at http://www.huffingtonpost.com/brett-greene and http://blindinfluence.com. He has spoken nationally for organizations including Blog World, Pod Camp, Davinci Institute, Pivot Guild and Denver University's Daniels College of Business MBA Program. You can also connect with Brett and his 50,000+ followers on Twitter at @brettgreene.
