Nathan Williams's Summary

Over 15 years experience in digital. Deep understanding of technology and it’s impact upon business and society.

I began my career working for Clarity IBD (later to become Proxicom) developing some of the first instances of web sites for brands such as The Carphone Warehouse and Railtrack.

In the following years I have worked in a variety of leading digital, advertising & branding agencies across a broad range of sectors, developing along the way a deep understanding of how digital affects a business, it’s brand and customers.

Most recently I have worked at The Partners (WPP) as Head of Digital, where I was responsible for defining and growing their digital offer, capability and business. During this time I also sat on the WPP European Digital Advisory Board.

With a broad understanding of technology and technical implementation, and a detailed knowledge of (User) Experience Design, I’m able to bring a very rounded approach to digital strategy for the Brands I work with.

I am currently employed as a Strategist at Wolff Olins London tasked with developing it's technology offering across a broad range of clients....

My goals are to:
• Develop collaborative, long term relationships with the brands I work with
• Deliver innovation in product & brand experience
• Create experiences that build long-term customer engagement and advocacy of brands.
• Understand the key trends that drive consumer & market behaviour and their impact upon brand experience.
• Have fun
