Michael Preston's Summary

Design
I qualified as a designer in 1981, having completed a three year City and Guilds 532 Certificate in Typographic Design for Print.

I've worked for all kinds of organisations from community groups/small businesses to national organisations/blue chip clients and for the print and publishing industry gaining a solid background of more than 28 years experience.

My experience spans all aspects of design and typography and I've handled everything from business cards to 96 sheet advertising posters. I'm especially skilled in corporate identity, annual reports, brochures, flyers and leaflets.

Previous clients include: The Daily Telegraph, The Quakers, Unison, Larousse Kingfisher Publishing, the MS Society, National Childbirth Trust, London Civic Forum, Wish, Birmingham & Solihull Mental Health Trust, Watford Borough Council, Clays Lane Housing Co-operative, Housing 21 and Purple Sofa Publications.

Photography
I'm also a skilled digital photographer and I'm capable of originating high quality images for reproduction in both printed and web based media. I've produced images for a wide variety of commercial clients in addition to working freelance as a press photographer supplying images to agencies specialising in news. I'm listed on the NUJ freelance directory and have a current and valid UK press ID card.

Previous clients include: Clays Lane Housing Co-operative, the Camping and Caravanning Club, London & South Eastern Railway, Crisis, and various newspapers, including: the Morning Star, the Hampstead Highgate Express, the Marylebone Express, the Wood & Vale and Disability Now.

News Images - www.demotix.com/users/creative19/profile - A link to my general portfolio is below.

Other Information
I have excellent interpersonal and communications skills as well as a 'can do’ attitude and a willingness to work hard. I'm an enthusiastic, involved and meticulous worker and have a good eye for detail. If you use me as your key creative you won't be disappointed.

Specialties

Design/Print: I've got strong experience in litho, screen and digital at all stages.

Photography: I specialise in disability/disabled sport, current affairs, cycle sport but I'm versatile enough to cover any press/PR/commercial photography need.

General: I've strong computer skills on Apple and PC platforms and experience of Apple LAN systems admin. I'm proficient in a wide range of software, including Adobe CS Suite - InDesign/Photoshop/Illustrator/Acrobat, Quark XPress and Microsoft Office.
