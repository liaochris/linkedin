Board Member

Circus West

March 2006 – March 2008 (2 years 1 month)

This was an interesting opportunity for me.

Cirkids was in the midst of a major strategic plan and was assisted in the process by a consultant Rick Knowland.

As a small business owner, I'm more used to being able to move quickly and make snap decisions.

An organization the size of Cirkids has to move much more methodically and engage all the stake holders in the decision.

At the end of a 2 year process, the organization emerged with clear goals related to growth and a stronger organization with buy in from all the major stake holders (Board, Teachers, Parents, Funders).

The new organization is now called Circus West and includes both training and semi professional troops extending past just kids to include training and professional opportunities for adults.

While sometimes rocky, I'm proud to have been a part of the transformation and value the experience that it brought me in working in a larger group.
