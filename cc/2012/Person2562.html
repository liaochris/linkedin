Scott Cooksey's Summary

Scott Cooksey holds the positions of Marketing Director and North American Sales Coordinator for Clock Spring Company, L.P. in Houston, TX. He was invited to join the team of one of his former clients to help develop and install enhanced marketing and sales-related systems of this rapidly growing company.

Prior to joining the Clock Spring team, Cooksey spent over a decade in the financial services business before traveling the globe for nearly three years as a consultant and corporate trainer.

He has provided leadership and skills training across the United States, Canada, the Caribbean, and Australia on topics including:

* Leadership & Team Building
* Fundamentals of Project Management
* Effective Communication Skills
* Presentation Skills Development
* Managing Multiple Generations
* Much, much more

Cooksey currently serves as Director of Communications and President-Elect for the Houston Chapter of the National Speakers Association.
