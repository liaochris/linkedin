Steve Hopper's Summary

► IF YOU MANAGE A SUPPLY CHAIN OR OPERATE WAREHOUSING AND DISTRIBUTION FACILITIES...Do you want to improve your logistics processes and implement lean distribution solutions that drive down costs, increase capacity, improve service levels, and reduce risk? If so, Steve Hopper can help.

Steve is passionate about helping clients boost profitability by saving time and money in their production, warehousing, distribution, logistics, and supply chain operations. His in-depth expertise includes:

♦ Lean Supply Chain Operations Planning, Management, Modeling, Analysis, Design, Reengineering, Process Improvement & Cost Reduction (Lean Six Sigma, 5S)
♦ Strategic & Tactical Industrial Engineering for Warehousing, Distribution & Logistics
♦ Improvement of Labor Productivity, Resource Utilization & Service Levels
♦ Reduction & Optimization of Inventory
♦ Assessment & Proactive Mitigation of Supply Chain Risk
♦ Assessment, Analysis, Design, Economic Justification, Specification, Selection, & Project Management of Supply Chain Information Systems & Material Handling Systems
♦ Business Development, Consultative Sales, Marketing & Sales Management for Supply Chain Solutions
♦ Team Leadership, Training & Mentoring for Results

To boost both your top and bottom lines, Steve can help you enhance the performance of your people, inventory, facilities, equipment, material handling systems, and supply chain information technology (IT) systems.

Steve earned his Bachelor of Industrial Engineering (BIE) degree from Georgia Tech and is a registered Professional Engineer (P.E.).

See a SupplyChainBrain video interview with Steve: http://bit.ly/w4yED7

Steve is Vice President of the Atlanta WERCouncil of the Warehousing Education & Research Council (WERC).

A longtime drummer, Steve enjoys playing jazz, rock, R&B, funk, and blues as often as possible. He is a committed follower of Jesus Christ, and he lives with his wife and two children in the northwest suburbs of Atlanta, Georgia.
