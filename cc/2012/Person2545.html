My passion and profession is photography that meshes art with client objectives in unexpected and compelling ways. During projects, I stress professionalism on all levels including: coordinating shoots, managing sets, models, makeup, creating memorable images, and finishing with impeccable photo retouching and on-time delivery to meet deadlines. Extensive credits in the fields of beauty, health and wellness, and high-end consumer goods comprise my track record; pushing the boundaries of what is possible for both clients and myself is my goal.

Specialties

Distinctive beauty & wellness photography that achieves target-audience impact for domestic and international clients. High-end beauty and branding campaigns that result in international exposure & multi-million circulation magazine covers. Collaborate with health & fitness clients to create exciting layouts, DVD & book covers.
Full-service by one team of experts: make-up, hair and nail artistry, stellar retouching services,
videography incl. sound & high end editing.
Fluent in German & English
