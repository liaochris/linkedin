Kenton Johnson's Summary

Kenton Johnson is Chief Collaborator and Founder of Prosper Systems, and successful 44-year veteran of marketing and sales, business start-ups, financing, investments and project development for: business, government and military, in both technical and non-technical markets.

While earning an MS in Inter-Disciplinary Systems Engineering, he was instrumental in developing one of the world’s first computer traffic control systems as well as ground-breaking city and Forest Service full multi-criteria transportation planning.

Upon graduation, Kenton became a lead officer for TS-SI intelligence gathering in Vietnam, then a Systems Engineer for the NORAD Cheyenne Mountain upgrade in early 70’s. After obtaining his Professional Engineering license and while studying Management and Engineering in California then Colorado Universities, he lead the Engineering and Planning use of mainframe computers for local water delivery, then state road construction.

He founded an international computer consulting firm for the 80’s to guide Engineers, Architects and Contractors in real estate design and development, launched an international newsletter, developed a full mapping system for eleven state governments, and launched several computer-aided design systems in US firms. He then moved to technical marketing services for the building, computer then health industries, pioneering websites in the 90’s.

The 21st Century encouraged him to add commercial real estate then $100M and larger mines sales, in which he is rapidly expanding both people/financial resources and properties.

Kenton is a member of technical and mining associations and several online professional groups; speaks, reads and writes some Spanish and French; has taught highly-technical courses across the US and in Europe; written and published five books, two journals, five websites, and 100’s of technical columns and articles; has a superior online presence; and has had P&L responsibility to multiple $M.
