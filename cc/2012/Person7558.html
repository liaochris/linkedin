Yael Rozencwajg's Summary

Thanks for stopping by.

My name is Yael Rozencwajg and I specialise in Digital Marketing since 2003.

Founder, director of YOPPS Digital Media Agency providing digital marketing solutions for international businesses, where we realize high-valued Internet services and develop effective online strategies.

YOPPS' clients range from startups to global industries including global brands (Danone, L’Oréal), startups and small businesses based in Paris, Tel Aviv, London, New York.

I also have the honour of regularly speaking about the Digital Ecosystem and the importance of including Digital Utility in corporations and I'm also teaching at schools of management (HEC Paris, Sciences Po, Chambers of Commerce and Industry in France).

As an entrepreneur, my daily activities are focused on the launch (and the evolution) of digital platforms to become wide evolving social networks and helping online communities to enhance their viral communication.

If you run or manage a business and need help to improve the effectiveness of your digital marketing, feel free to contact me at yr@yopps.com or (+33 183 624 015).

Specialties

Digital Marketing, Business Development, Social Utility, Engagement, Online Collaboration, Content Efficiency, Digital Education, Customer Awareness, Crossover Conversations, Startups, Community Growth, Funds and Investments.
