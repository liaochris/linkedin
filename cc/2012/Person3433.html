James Laslavic's Summary

James Laslavic believes that making modern technological advances usable for everybody is one of the keys to dramatically improving the world. He is a student at Carnegie Mellon University majoring in Communication Design and minoring in Ethics. A few of his favorite areas of study are user experience design, typography, and psychology.

In the past, James has been involved in open source community projects like Sabayon Linux, and has also done work for commercial clients such as Howard-Sneed Architecture and Primary Colors Theatre.

During the summer, he is the archery director at Montecito Sequoia Lake Resort in the Sequoia National Forest, California.

Specialties

web design, user experience, interaction design, psychology, program interfaces, branding and identity, graphic design, ethics
