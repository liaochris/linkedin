Sherry Hitchcock's Summary

I am a residential and investment real estate broker for the San Francisco Bay area. My area of focus is the Peninsula and South Bay. I have a background in strong corporate negotiation and the tenacity to keep the interest of my clients first and foremost. I believe that home ownership directly affects ones quality of life. I am known for my goal-oriented approach and comprehensive results-oriented marketing plans for sellling my lisitings at the highest possible price. If looking to purchase, let my experience help you make a wise investment.

Specialties

Residential - primary residence and investments, single family and multi-unit. 1031 tax exchange, probate, trustee and divorce sales. Working with both buyers and sellers. I have a special interest in historic homes and equestrian properties.
