Tom Vaughan's Summary

Product management and marketing professional with 20 years experience in digital media products. A decade of experience managing award winning audio, video, photo, data and mobile product portfolios and business segments in excess of $100M through retail, OEM and enterprise channels. A skilled and experienced manager of highly effective marketing teams.
