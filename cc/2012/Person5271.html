Paul Evans' Summary

I am an experienced programmer that takes pride in building great things.

I relocated to Austin, TX from the UK in May 2011 and am currently working on social games at Portalarium.

I am credited on two AAA games (Fable II and III) and have shipped commercial products for libraries. I have developed many internal tools and also worked for games yet to see a release at Lionhead Studios - including some unannounced, Milo, and Fable: The Journey.

I contribute to the programming community via #AltDevBlogADay - a site dedicated to sharing knowledge about the development of games.
