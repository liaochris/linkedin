Ryan Erickson's Summary

I'm currently stationed at USCG Sector Juneau as the Chief of it's Incident Management Division (IMD) overseeing pollution response in the Southeastern Alaska.

Previously I've been a Command Duty Officer for the Atlantic Command Center located in Portsmouth, VA; and I have supervised the Seattle areas Vessel Board and Search Teams/ LE Teams and was a C-130H Navigator.

In my spare time, when not with family, I'm a contributor and social media evangelist for 1790.us, a U.S. Coast Guard centric blog and the United States Naval Institutes' blog.
