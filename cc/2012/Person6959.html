Tammy Kahn Fennell's Experience

CEO

MarketMeSuite

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Computer Software industry

October 2009 – Present (2 years 5 months)

MarketMeSuite's latest version "The Social Inbox" provides an end to end solution for businesses to manage, engage, curate and measure the ROI of their social marketing activity. MarketMeSuite has a fast growing global base of 20,000 small businesses and marketing agencies.

MarketMeSuite's Social Inbox: The concept of a social inbox is very powerful. The way we have designed MarketMeSuite's Social Inbox is without a network bias, giving the power of prioritizing the workflow to the user through Smart Inboxes. The tech team has built a very scalable set up that will allow us to continue to add networks over time. They've solved a lot of issues around calling multiple networks through a unique technical set up that calls an entire team's messages at once, so there's never any network API limits hit by a business. The ease of use of the Social Inbox encourages a lot of activity and gives us a fantastic way to measure what we're calling "interactive analytics" so we can feed information back to the business on how they are using MarketMeSuite and help them measure the ROI.
