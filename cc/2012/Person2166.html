David Eyes's Summary

Broad experience in many phases of product management, product management management, and product marketing, with a consistent record of innovation and adaptation to new challenges in a process of continuous competitive- and market-driven product enhancement. In both management and influencing roles I have demonstrated vision, direction, and the ability to align groups towards business results.
