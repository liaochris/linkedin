Dan Zukowski's Summary

Starting on the showroom floor as a BMW salesman fresh out of college, my career has led through journalism to my current position as Senior Vice President of the Automotive & Corporate Practice at GolinHarris-Los Angeles. I head the Toyota automotive public relations account, for which we are agency of record. My work includes product communications for Toyota and Lexus, corporate communications, community relations, and crisis counsel. My primary role is to provide senior counsel to client executive management.

Most recently, I managed the debut of the Lexus concept sports car at the 2012 Detroit auto show, and developed the global debut strategy for the new 2012 Toyota Camry. This groundbreaking launch generated two billion impressions from a simultaneous, multi-city live event and webcast. I was also responsible for the launch of Toyota Entune at the 2011 Consumer Electronics Show, where the new telematics service won two awards.

Previously, at other agencies, I have run the Honda account (autos), Kawasaki (motorcycles, watercraft and ATVs), and Suzuki (autos). I've also worked with Hyundai, Isuzu, Kia and Mitsubishi in my 30-year career in the industry.

I've done too many auto shows and vehicle launches to count, and I've been involved in most forms of motorsport from Supercross to NASCAR, IndyCar, and SCCA Pro Rally. I've created legendary, award-winning campaigns such as the "Get Together for Good Times" three-year series of motorcycle rallies for Kawasaki and "Dave or Bust!" for Hyundai.

In addition, I've served as the editor or senior editor of half a dozen magazines and newsletters and I was the New York Editor for Ward’s, a leading Detroit-based publisher of automotive industry trade magazines and newsletters.

My byline has appeared in the San Francisco Chronicle, Roanoke Times, Newsday, San Jose Mercury News, Changing Times, Trains magazine and Martini Republic, and I've appeared on ESPN, TNN, CBS Newsradio and public radio.
