Patrick Schwerdtfeger's Summary

Patrick is a regular speaker for Bloomberg TV and is the author of "Webify Your Business - Internet Marketing Secrets for the Self-Employed" (2009) as well as "Make Yourself Useful - Marketing in the 21st Century" (2008) and has spoken about Online Branding, Modern Entrepreneurship and the Social Media Revolution at conferences and business events around the world.

Patrick has evolved into a world-class professional speaker and has presented for organizations including Bloomberg, the American Marketing Association (AMA), the American Society of Travel Agents (ASTA) and Meeting Professionals International (MPI). His speaking career has taken him to various locations across India and Europe as well as 200+ events across North America.

Patrick is the founder of Tactical Execution, an online marketing agency and is also the organizer of the Entrepreneur & Small Business Academy, one of the nation's 50 largest entrepreneur clubs. Patrick's educational podcasts have been downloaded over 50,000 and his articles have been featured on thousands of different websites and blogs.

Specialties

Patrick has a gift for explaining complicated subjects in simple language, making his presentations empowering and insightful. He is a prolific content creator and offers resources including YouTube videos, podcasts, articles, downloadable PDFs, blog posts and tips via Twitter.
