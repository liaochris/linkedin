Ted Ernst's Summary

You know how executives sometimes find that the business seems to take more and more hours to sustain acceptable growth?

What I do is show executive teams how to both reliably grow the business and drastically reduce the amount of time required to manage it day to day, leaving them free to meet with customers, think strategically about the future, and never miss another dance recital or soccer game.

You can have it all: growing company, thriving family, and connected, healthy community.

If any of this sounds like you, let's get together for a no-pressure coffee to talk over what's happening in your business and see if there's a fit between us. Email me: Ted@TedErnst.com or call me 503 877 9214 to set something up.

Specialties
