Patrick Quattlebaum's Summary

Patrick Quattlebaum is an executive and consultant at Macquarium Intelligent Communications, a User Experience Firm with offices in Atlanta, GA and Charlotte, NC. Over the past seven years, he has been instrumental in developing Macquarium’s family of practices which provide thought leadership, consulting and training to a varied roster of Fortune 1000 clientele. Patrick’s expertise includes user experience management and process design, cross channel experience strategy and design, user research and testing, experience strategy, information architecture, interaction design, and information design.

At Macquarium, Patrick has consulted on a wide range of solutions, including web applications, digital asset management systems, corporate intranets, marketing web sites, ecommerce sites, and elearning experiences. His current and past clients include The Coca-Cola Company, Yahoo!, Cox Communications, Sabre, UPS, Lowe’s, Estée Lauder, TRX, Deloitte Consulting, and Intercontinental Hotels Group.

Patrick is a passionate evangelist for incorporating user experience methods into the product/service development lifecycle. Recent presentations (prs), panels (pnl), and workshops (wor) have included:
• Building Bridges: Co-creating X-channel Experiences (prs)
• "Everything you ever wanted to know about UX (*but were afraid to ask)" (prs)
• "The Engagement Experience - Examining Meeting Design, Personality Management, and Project Culture" w/ Kevin Hoffman and Andrew Hinton (wor)
• "How to integrate UX into the business, design & dev process" (pnl)
• "Why, What, How: The Value of UX-BA Partnerships" (prs)
• "Why User Experience Matters in the Workplace" (prs)
• "User-informed Design in the Enterprise" (prs)

Patrick holds an MS in Information Design & Technology from the Georgia Institute of Technology, and a BA in English from the Honors College at the University of South Carolina. In his academic research, he focuses on the intersection of new media and film studies.
