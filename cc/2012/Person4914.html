Matthew Mace's Summary

Delivering Competitive Advantage through Business Analytics

At BlueGranite, we believe that competitive advantage is achieved through the strategic use of business analytics. Business analytics begins with monitoring key performance measures and analyzing results from multiple perspectives. Business analytics also includes making use of advanced data mining techniques to uncover hidden patterns and predict future outcomes. When empowered with relevant and timely insights, individuals are equipped to make better decisions, resulting in improved business performance.

BlueGranite delivers world class business analytics solutions to wide variety of organizations in the private and public sector, including those in health & life sciences, retail & consumer goods, financial services (insurance and banking), and manufacturing.

These data warehousing, business intelligence, and predictive analytic solutions serve the needs of individuals across the organization, including sales and marketing, finance, operations, and human resources. BlueGranite delivers these solutions with a team passionate about technology, and highly skilled in the Microsoft BI platforms (SQL Server, SharePoint, PowerPivot, Excel), and Predixion Insight.
