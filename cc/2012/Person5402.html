Ravi Jayagopal's Experience

Founder & Co-Developer

DigitalAccessPass.com

Partnership; 1-10 employees; Internet industry

January 2008 – Present (4 years 2 months)

DigitalAccessPass.com (DAP) is a premium Membership Plugin for WordPress that allows you to easily create membership sites with WordPress.

You can sell one-off products, subscription-based products, offer bonuses, do coupons, and drip content little by little (we call it a "ContentResponder").

DAP allows you to accept payments using most leading payment processors and shopping carts like Paypal, Authorize.net, 1ShoppingCart, ClickBank and e-Junkie, among others.

DAP has a built-in email autoresponder module, does email broadcasting, an Affiliate module, can protect normal files, videos, audio files, and more.

See http://DigitalAccessPass.com for details.
