Mark Greenmantle's Summary

I'm a freelance professional photographer based around inner northside Brisbane, catering to all photographic fields. I have extensive experience with large scale event photography, having run teams of 20+ shooters at festivals for several years.

I'm a regular photographic contributer to Fiend magazine and have been published internationally in Broadsword Magazine, The Courier-Mail, Byron Bay Echo, and several smaller regional newspapers. I am available for event photography, weddings, fashion, band shoots, model folios, portraits, pet portraits, formals, advertising images, corporate functions, sports events and I also work as a professional image retoucher and photoshop artist.

Current and previous clients include: Allianz Insurance, BankWest, Colorado Clothing Group, Australian Executive Protection Group, Templar International, Procon Leisure International, Nighteshade Jewelry (New York,NY), Underground Press (South Africa), POMO Advertising, United Underground Press, Big and Little Media, Voodoo Lulu, Broadsword magazine, Rivetting Promotions, Bizoo street press, Australian Quality Lacquers, Bayside Bulletin, Byron Bay Echo, BMagazine, The Abbey magazine, Scene magazine, The Courier-Mail, The Courier-Mail Online, Demonista online magazine, Fiend Magazine, Abbey Museum Medieval Festival (annual), History Alive 2007(annual), Faith Nightclub, Club Blink & Club Trash, Bar Burlesque, Lovecats Nightclub, Schverkraft Nightclub, Pure Nightclub, Beserk Fashion, Marxbrüder Fight School, Full Tilt Jousting, Qld Living History Federation and over twenty bands including Wolfmother, Psyche (Germany), Apoptygma Berserk (Norway), VNV Nation (Germany).
