Mike Pearson's Summary

I've worked in the field of Information and Communication Technology (ICT) for over 25 years. I have 15 years operational experience and 10 years strategic experience.

If you read my recommendations, you'll see my peers recognise I have a strong interest in emerging technologies and their innovative use in the business. I'm often called an evangelist, generating enthusiasm for business change enabled by technology. I love helping people work better with technology and I'm excited by the new possibilities opening up with the consumerisation of ICT.

I've been waiting my entire career for the device we now carry in our pockets, and we are only starting to grasp its full potential.
