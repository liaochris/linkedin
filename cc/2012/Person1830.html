Neil Blumberg's Summary

Education: Commerce and Law degrees from South African universities of Rhodes and Witwatersrand. Multiple real estate courses. Mediation certification from Sullivan University.

Work and Residence: Live in Louisville Kentucky. President and Principal Broker of Metro1 Realty. Licenced broker in KY and IN. Offices in Middletown, East Louisville.

Commercial Real Estate: Broker to investors and business owners throughout the US. Specialize in apartments and strip centers. Also handle property management, site selection analysis, distressed properties etc. KY and IN.

Residential Real Estate: High tech, state of the art marketing and mobile apps. Focused on East and South Louisville and S. Indiana. Find solutions for investors, baby boomers, cant-sell-my-home sellers. Creative Finance, short sales, foreclosures. Funding for first-time home buyers.

Professional Organizations: Current and past member of various organizations including National Association of Realtors, Greater Louisville Association of Realtors, Kentucky Association of Realtors, Southern Indiana Association of Realtors, Kentucky Commercial Real Estate Association, Kentucky Real Estate Exchangers, Indiana Association of Realtors, Forms Committee and SUPRA Committee of Greater Louisville Association of Realtors, Real Estate Cyber Society, Louisville Area President, member of ff chapters: Indiana, NY City Area, Kentucky, Phoenix Metro, United Kingdom.

Community
Current: Volunteer as a CASA (Court Appointed Special Advocate) in Jefferson County Family Court, advocating for at risk, abused and neglected children.

Past: Wayside Christian Mission, Jewish Community Center, Legal Aid Society, Pro bono attorney work, South African Volunteer Service.

Overseas: Lived in East London, a seaside resort on the east coast of South Africa, renowned for its beaches and surfing. Practiced law in Israel and had ownership interest in 3 restaurants there.
