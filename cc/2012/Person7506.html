Scott Wade's Summary

What is ‘Wrock Solid Design’? Proper image placement? Good typographical choices? Many so-called designers when given a new task or job will go immediately to a blank document and begin producing. Is this design? To many they are one in the same. But not for true designers. Software is a tool for production, not for designing. Layout, color, form, typography, hierarchy, ‘the golden section’. These are the tools that I utilize to communicate my designs and my clients message into a singular vision. Whether that vision is informing a consumer about a product, raising awareness of social issues or promoting an event to the world through both print and interactive design. I do this through a rigorous work ethic and properly planned workflow.
However, I also bring more than tried and true design concepts. I bring imagination! A personal touch of creativity that cannot be taught and a deep understanding of both the nature of design and of functionality. When all of these elements are put into motion the creation is what I like to call, ‘Wrock Solid Design’!
