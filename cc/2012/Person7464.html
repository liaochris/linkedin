Tyler DeAngelo's Summary

Over the last 10 years Tyler DeAngelo has created hundreds of advertising campaigns for brands in almost every category including: retail, fashion, travel, spirits, consumer goods, technology, health care and entertainment. He has spent the majority of his career helping clients develop marketing strategies and creative messages within digital and emerging medias. Before joining DeVito Verdi as the interactive creative director, Tyler was partner and creative director at boutique digital production company Modern Publicity.
