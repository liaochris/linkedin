Dimitri Mastrocola's Summary

I am Wall Street's Best Connected Legal Recruiter.

I recruit general counsels, chief compliance officers and other senior attorneys for corporate clients in a variety of industry sectors, including financial services, media & advertising, consumer products, life sciences and engineering/strategic consulting.

My area of strongest focus is placing attorneys within financial services firms. I regularly work with alternative asset management firms (hedge funds, private equity firms, family offices), proprietary trading firms, broker-dealers and corporate/investment banks.
