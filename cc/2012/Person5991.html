Sadhbh Royce's Summary

I'm am a qualified and experience interior design based in Doha, Qatar. My experience is in both commercial and domestic interiors and I have worked on a variety of projects ranging from 5 Star hotels to private homes including bespoke furniture commissions.

I am currently seeking a challenging opportunity to bring my design skills and knowledge to a fast developing region, while fostering my career growth.

Specialties

I also design bespoke furniture pieces. My most recent collection being “The Elegance Collection”. It is handmade in Wexford, Ireland by a team of talented craftsmen. These bespoke pieces represent uniqueness and sophistication crafted in solid walnut.
