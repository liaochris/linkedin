President

Bringing Strategy Together

May 2011 – Present (1 year 1 month) Austin, Texas Area

At Carey Lenss - Bringing Strategy Together - We support you in two critical areas (1) Developing the strategic direction; AND (2) Implementing the tactics for success!

This is a firm that brings individualized services and tactics to your organization in core performance areas. Employees, Operations, Technology, External Relationships, and Future Growth Areas are all critical to an organizations success.

A good business strategy defines the direction and scope of an organization, and will advance the company’s resources and meet market needs. Supports organizations and businesses towards positive transformation. This transformation could be:

~ Developing Business Plans & Connecting to Strategy
~ Implementing Social Media & Expanding Digital Relationships
~ Identifiy Business Segments & Tactics to Minimize Competition
~ Organizational Development & Design
~ Outsourcing COO Functions

The goal is to enhance revenues, operations, participation, and enthusiasm. Development can occur through understanding a wide array of potential growth areas:

~ Restoring organizational effectiveness thru subsystem analysis
~ Facilitating technology applications within operations/market
~ Intelligence gathering on market, customers, and competitor and applying it back to your mission
~ Improving business operations without adding bureaucracy

There are numerous methodologies to perfect an organization that uniquely fit its needs. Every business is different and I tailor my solutions to fit your uniqueness.
