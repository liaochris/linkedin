Staci Baird's Summary

I'm a new media specialist with more than ten years of experience working with a variety of traditional and non-traditional media outlets. I'm a skilled user and avid champion of social media.

As the Internet and Social Media Strategist for the Stanford School of Engineering I'm responsible for formulating and executing a comprehensive web and social media communications strategy for the school.

Before coming to Stanford, I taught digital and social media classes for the journalism department at San Francisco State University. In spring 2011, I led the relaunch of the SF State student newspaper and magazine websites and the first iPad version of the student magazine.

As an Internet Marketing Manager for Lucasfilm, I helped launch LucasArt's first social media campaigns. Before moving to San Francisco, I was the Innovations Project Manager for E.W. Scripps Newspaper Interactive in Knoxville, Tenn. As Multimedia Manager for MSN Money in New York I worked with a team of multimedia journalists to design and produce a series of multimedia stories. I have also worked as a content producer for CBS Interactive and SFGate.

I helped design the Stauffer Multimedia Newsroom at the University of Kansas where I taught online journalism classes as a graduate teaching assistant and multimedia coordinator. I also taught online journalism at the University Tennessee where I helped design and build a content management system for the Tennessee Journalist.

I'm a founding member of the Intercollegiate Online News Network (ICONN).

Specialties

Online strategic planning, production management, social media planning, business development, systems analysis, technical documentation, technical consulting, project management, mobile media, journalism and online media education, multimedia training.
