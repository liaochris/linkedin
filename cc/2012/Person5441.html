Richard Sedley's Summary

20+ years in the creative/customer experience field, the last 16 in online/digital. In recent years I've focused on psychology and persuasion, in the context of creating employee and customer engagement.

As Commercial Director at Foviance I over see the company's Client Relationship, New Business and Marketing teams. Our clients include: Barclays, National Lottery, Dell, Sky, Nokia, BBC, Aviva, Mulberry, William Hill, Dunhill, Fujitsu and many more.
http://bit.ly/client-list

Foviance is one of the UK's leading customer experience consultancies. We combine customer behavioural research (analytics, surveying, user testing inc. eye-tracking, accessibility testing, ethnography etc.) with user experience strategy and planning.

Between 2007-11 I was the Course Director for Social Media at the Chartered Institute of Marketing (CIM). In 2012 I stepped down to become an Associate Member of the CIM Faculty.

Prior to Foviance I spent 11 years at the digital agency cScape where I set up the cScape Customer Engagement Unit (CEU) in 2006 and developed a team of specialist consultants that delivered on all aspects of online marketing and communications.

March 2008 also saw the publication of 'Winners and Losers in a Troubled Economy: how to engage customers online to gain competitive advantage' which looks at the importance of customer engagement during harder economic conditions.
http://scr.bi/winners-and-losers

Specialties

persuasive design, customer strategy, employee engagement, web design, psychology, digital, user experience, social media, marketing, speech writing, customer research
