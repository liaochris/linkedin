S David Drewelow's Summary

I am on a great adventure. My vision is a coach in every business. My purpose is to help people change the way they think.

Fulfilling my purpose helps my vision become a reality. The big benefit will improve businesses, create more jobs and dramatically grow our economy. This is vital to our future, so we need more coaches.

First, about me...

I am an Iowa boy and the oldest of eight kids. I am the son of a serial business owner. I learned many lessons from my large family and our family business. These experiences influence me today as a franchise business owner, coach recruiter, leadership trainer and public speaker.

As a University of Northern Iowa alum (Go Panthers!), I majored in music, accounting and graduated with a Broadcasting degree. College is where I discovered my passion for lifelong learning.

My career includes working in radio, print, ad agencies, media research and for MDA and the Jerry Lewis Telethon. Today I own two businesses.

I am the past president of the Master Coaches Association and an active Rotarian. I am the special music guy for my church, photographer for the Kennedy High School band and I chair the Mount Mercy University golf tournament. I like being involved.

I have two sons, two very active grandsons and I am blessed to be married to my very best friend. I love spending time with my family, traveling, golf, singing, photography and college sports.

My mission in life is to “To glorify God by helping people achieve their dreams!" I help people figure out where they are, where they want to go and then a plan to get them there. Coaching people makes their dreams happen. This process really gets me jazzed!

The market is demanding more coaches so we need to add 10-12 new coaches to my firm in 2012. This is my 'great adventure.'

Who do you know who would make a great coach? What's your journey all about? Let's connect to see how we can help each other out.

Specialties

Coach recruiting, territory development, franchisee support, team training, leadership training, marketing, systems development, special event promotion, public relations, public speaking, workshop & seminar presenting.
