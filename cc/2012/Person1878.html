Curt Roese, CPA's Summary

Curt Roese, CPA is a dedicated and accomplished professional with more than 25 years of successful experience in Public Accounting, Finance, Asset Management, Real Estate, Home Building, Sales and Construction.

Curt is extremely motivated and energetic with strong entrepreneurial and leadership skills. His diversified experience was attained serving roles as a Public Accountant, Controller, CFO, Asset Manager, Real Estate Broker and Business Owner.

He believes in "Making a Difference" and is often Involved in charitable fund raising.
