Guillaume Castevert's Summary

After 15 years of Customer Support & Sales experience in the aeronautics and outsourcing industries, for Safran, EADS and ISS groups, I am now managing my own small business, a Bed & Breakfast located in Bordeaux, astutely called "Bed & Bordeaux" ;-)

I am also President of AutoCool, cooperative company providing car-sharing services in Bordeaux and its urban area.

Specialties

Creating, managing and growing a customer relationship: approaching the customer, negotiating, ensuring customer satisfaction and following the contract till full payment of the invoices. Starting again...
