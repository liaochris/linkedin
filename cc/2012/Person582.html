Ankur Jain's Summary

Few things about me:

* I have a simple mission - build products that delight the end user and make profit for the company! I have built many award winning internet products which are currently being used by millions of users all over the world. Few examples of the products I developed are: kosmix.com, tweetbeat.com, ecorner.stanford.edu, khelocricket.in, hotontwttr.com and indianumbers.com.

* I have worked in five countries, three continents and with team members from 25 different countries. I can say 'hello' in 10 different languages! My favorite experience was in the jungles of Cambodia where I spent over a month to connect remote villages to the information highway.
