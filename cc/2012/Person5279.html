Paul Swift's Summary

Delivery orientated programme manager with strong ICT, finance and transport background. Extensive track record of delivering complex, high value programmes within blue chip companies. Effective communicator and team builder with a hands-on style. Considerable experience of multinational projects.

Summary of career history

Recent roles include Consultant at Galderma SA, Programme Manager at Belron (Autoglass), Northern Rock, Serco-NedRailways and Nationwide Building Society. Paul has also held Programme Management and Director positions at many UK and European Blue-Chip companies.

Former employment includes Principal Consultant with PA Consulting group, ICT Director for a major Italian insurer and International Project Manager for Norwich Union (now AVIVA plc).

Main strengths

The lateral, flexible and global approach required to manage complex programmes and projects supported by detailed technical knowledge and extensive experience. A change initiator and hands-on problem solver. Experienced at board level but able to build strong relationships at all levels of an organisation. At ease working on multinational projects and always sensitive to differing national or cultural perspectives. Able to build effective delivery-orientated teams. Client-focused, self-motivating and calm under pressure.

Specialties

Specialist in the management of large, complex, high risk and short timescale programmes in the UK and Europe.

Experience of programme management process design and implementation.

IT background with extensive experience of ICT systems delivery, software development, service delivery and management, business continuity planning, technical project management and master data management on the Lawson M3 platform.
