Janet Wallace's Summary

Writer, public speaker and success strategist focusing on social marketing and new media applications for entrepreneurs, authors and entertainers. I have clients who range from start-up mode to over $1 million+ year annual revenue.

Demystifying new media and teaching people how to integrate it into their business and life in a real, and unstressful way, gets me up in the morning. I believe in campaigns over chaos, the power of giving, and taking risks. Who will join me?

Specialties

I offer one-day intensives, workshops, short- and long-term retainer and projects, as well as MP3s and ebooks to help people get over their fear of technology, being seen and getting left behind in the social space so they can go from "I can't" to iconic.

I work with dedicated clients committed to integrating a social presence that works for them, at their pace, with real results.
