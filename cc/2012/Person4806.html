Mike Husman's Summary

AdviCoach is a business coaching and consulting company that works with privately held small and medium sized businesses helping business owners who have a desire to improve their business operations and take their company to new heights of performance and profitability. The Entrepreneur's Source is a coaching firm that works with prospective business owners who wish to own a business, or find a business that matches their needs, goals and expectations. Mike is currently bringing on new Operating Partners who want to be a part of one of the fastest growing coaching businesses in NC, SC, FL and GA. Mike has over 30 years of experience in executive management and leadership, business development, business ownership, franchising, human resources, and business education.

Mike's experience includes: COO for a consumer finance company with over 2500 locations throughout the US; founder and owner of a full service restaurant concept; Executive Vice President, COO of a fast food chain with over 350 locations in the US; President of a franchise chain of quick service restaurants; has been responsible for, or directly involved in the opening of over 500 businesses throughout the US; and other business experience in venture capital, golf, insurance and real estate industries.

Mike enjoys coaching businesses and individuals to exceed their business and profit expectations.

Mike graduated with degrees in Management from the University of South Carolina.

Mike is married and has two daughters. His interests other than family and work include woodworking and golf.
