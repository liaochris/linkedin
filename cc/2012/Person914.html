Brent Jordan's Summary

An automotive specialist with over 15 years experience providing advanced driving safety, public relations, marketing, educational services to automotive industry, teens, parents, driving enthusiasts.

Founder of DriveMark, an organization dedicated to advanced driver education for motorists across the United States. DriveMark also represented top-tier manufacturers Mercedes, Volvo, Ford, Chevrolet, Toyota, Mazda, Hyundai, Buick, Pontiac Honda, Hummer and Scion focused on sales training, safety technology, performance.

Utilizing my high-performance driving skills, I dedicate my time as a Senior Racing Instructor at ProFormance Racing School as a racing coach & instructor teaching street survival skills, advanced performance driving, & racing skills to racers as well as the general public. Founded in 1987, ProFormance has become a leader in the driving industry with clients ranging from Microsoft & Cisco Systems, to Ferrari of Seattle, BMW Car Club of America & MTV Road Rules.

I have extensive knowledge of new active safety technologies, including ABS, Traction Control, Stability Control, Roll Stability Control and Adaptive Cruise Control and have developed both street and closed courses to help students experience and understand each technology. Also experienced in all areas of four wheel drive and all wheel drive vehicles and has facilitated educational, real World experiences in the areas of off-road, towing and hauling for owners and dealers.

Additionally, I have more than 20 years experience in sales management, marketing, sales coach, (Linkedin more recently), and customer service for companies that include AT&T, MCI, GTE, Tandy, Firestone. I have spoken in front of large audiences and live television and radio on subjects including public safety technology, child Internet safety, automobile safety and automobile technology.

I advocate carbon-neutral technologies, providing the safest possible place for our young drivers to evolve!
