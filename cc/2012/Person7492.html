Vincent DiAngelus's Summary

For the last 10 years I have been helping Pennsylvania residents who have had issues with driver's license suspension.

In the course of helping PA residents I have learned all about PennDOT and our state legal system. I am an independent paralegal who specializes in Title 75 (Transportation and Drivers License) and work for attorneys all across the state. I help hundreds of people a month correct problems with their driver's license and get them back to being productive members of society.

I am an expert in PA work license and probationary license application.

I have spent the last several years marketing and building my brand www.licenserestoration.com. In the course of marketing my company I have become an expert in SEO and SEM strategies for small medium business and have successfully held my company and website as one of the top search results in our market.

I now have a passion for SEO and SEM services. I am looking for opportunities to expand my knowledge and discuss strategy in marketing.

I am always looking for strategic partners to improve services I provide to suspended PA drivers.
