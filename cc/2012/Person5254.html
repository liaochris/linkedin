Business Social Video Strategist & Thought Translator: Architecting Your Ideas for Revenue Growth (through social media).

Hired by companies looking to expand their vision & strategy for revenue growth. I use my business architecture knowledge to help you to use video and other social media platforms to add value not only to your customers…but to your customer’s customers.
