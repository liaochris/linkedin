Sunny Conley's Summary

SUNNY CONLEY, Scottsdale, Ariz., is a professional writer, author, editor and researcher with more than a decade of experience working in major media outlets including newspaper and magazine, radio and television, and the Internet.

Sunny takes her extensive writing, research and marketing expertise into the latest social media generation that includes Twitter, Facebook and LinkedIn. As a Social Media Manager, Sunny's goal is to work closely with small locally-owned businesses and restaurants, and organizations to create innovative, effective campaigns to drive brand awareness, engagement and traffic to social media pages.

Sunny is also a food columnist. Her column, “Chile Knights” has appeared in New Mexico newspapers for 12 years. “Chile Knights” (http://www.lascrucesbulletin.com) features chile pepper tidbits, tales, and hot and spicy recipes – topics that are also tweeted on Twitter and blogged on her Facebook profile. The majority of the photographs that appear in print and on the Internet are captured by Sunny.

As a journalist, she has penned more than 2000 articles and features in newspapers and magazines and online. Her newspaper and magazine columns included Community Kudos, Palate Patrol (restaurant reviews), Weekend Chef, Sunny's Southwest, and Cafe Hopping. Sunny is the author of four nonfiction southwestern books. She hosted, Hot on Chile!, a one-hour PBS hot and spicy dining show (http://www.myspace.com/video/garyworth/hot-on-the-trail-with-sunny-conley/60955465). Produced in New Mexico, the program aired nationwide.

Sunny’s goal is to continue her work as a writer, editor and researcher with special interest in "everything Southwest!"

Specialties

Writing, editing, copywriting, blogging and tweeting, promotion, research, Microsoft Office 2008, hot and spicy cuisine, cooking and recipe development
