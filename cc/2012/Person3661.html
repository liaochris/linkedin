John Dilbeck's Summary

My main goal is to help locally-owned small businesses in Murphy, NC market their goods and services more effectively using a variety of online venues.

I accept a limited amount of online marketing consulting from people in other areas, but it is not the focus of my efforts.

I no longer accept any computer programming or computer systems consulting work.

Specialties

Marketing consulting, Macintosh computers and applications, website development, online marketing, social networking, social marketing, email marketing, blogging.

Dilbeck Consulting

September 1977 – July 2000 (22 years 11 months)

Consulted with small businesses, mostly, and a few divisions of large corporations. I greatly preferred working with smaller companies.

Designed, programmed, and installed custom computer software and trained staff how to use the systems.

Taught seminars and classes on multiple computer operating systems, programming languages, and computer applications.

Programmed mainframes and microcomputers using mostly COBOL, FORTRAN, and Basic+. Programmed microcomputers using assembler, Basic, FORTRAN, COBOL, Pascal, C, C++, and other languages.

Notable clients included:

John C. Campbell Folk School, where I designed, programmed, and installed a database system for managing classes, instructors, and students. It was used for about 10 years.

Grove Enterprises, where I worked first as a consultant and later as their Systems Administrator. I was on the team that created the first ISP in far western NC: Grove.net, which later became WebWorkz.
