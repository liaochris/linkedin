Robert Matelski's Summary

I am currently a 2nd-year MBA student at The Johnson School at Cornell University, where I am pursuing a general management degree with a focus on sustainability and management consulting. Prior to business school, I spent over five years rising through the ranks in the architectural field, where I was managing clients as well as junior staff and consultants. My architectural experience and expertise includes historic preservation, green building, higher education, healthcare, and retail design.
