Kami Gray's Summary

Kami is based in Portland, Oregon providing art direction / set design and wardrobe styling for film, video, and still photography. Kami is also a personal fashion stylist for men and women.

Her work can be seen on television shows including House, Veronica Mars, and Hell’s Kitchen, and in commercials for Toyota, Nike, Intel, HP, and Discover Card.

Kami contributes regularly to television, print, and online media outlets including MSN, CNN, AOL, Real Simple, Women’s Day, and Redbook.

Kami is a fashion instructor at The Art Institute of Portland and recently co-founded Student Stylists, placing her superstar fashion students in freelance positions in film/video, still photography, personal styling, visual merchandising, and fashion event production.

Kami earned a Bachelor’s degree in Fashion Design from The Art Institute of Portland in 2002.

To find out more, please call Kami at 503-957-6122 or email her kamigray@gmail.com.
