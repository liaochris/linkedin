Liam Wong's Summary

I am a graphic designer who loves games.

I have been interested in art for games ever since discovering Deluxe Paint on the Amiga. My aim is to improve the overall quality of graphic design within games; focusing on making game environments more believable and experiences more immersive.

Currently working as a 2D Artist at Crytek. (Crysis, Crysis 2, Homefront 2)

Strong background in 2D with expertise in interface design, icon design, branding, web design, motion graphics, concept art and the creation of promotional material. Also possess knowledge of 3D software.

Worked as an artist (in a team of 5) on the double BAFTA nominated game Colour-Coded. Have participated in numerous teams working on game-related projects since 2008 without intermission. Spent a year working alongside BBC Scotland and in collaboration with other students whilst at university. Have also worked as an individual on numerous freelance projects since 2002.

Serious passion for gaming, the games industry and also helping graduates get into the games industry by volunteering my time as a mentor for SIGGRAPH, Dare to be Digital and the Scottish Games Jam as well as presentations given at Eurogamer, BAFTA and Protoplay.

Specialties

Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, After Effects, Premiere, Flash, Dreamweaver, Illustrator, Fireworks, InDesign, Soundbooth). Autodesk Maya. Sony Vegas. Google SketchUp. An understanding of HTML/CSS/Action Script... traditional art and uhhhh Deluxe Paint on the Amiga.
