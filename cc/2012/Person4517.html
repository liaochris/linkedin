Michael Acevedo's Summary

Hi, my name is Michael Acevedo. I am a Web + User Interface Designer in Riverside, California. I graduated from Platt College with Bachelors Degree in Visual Communications.

I have been designing simple, clean websites and interfaces that are beautiful and easy to use for about 4 years. The work I've done over the past years include full time web production and lead designer for a national leading Internet Marketing Company, freelancing for local clients, as well as designing and building custom Wordpress themes.

My aim is to bring my industry experience in the field of Web + User Interface Design, advertising, marketing, and branding to your company, and develop a creative graphic solution to any situation. I am available for freelance or contract design work. Please check out my portfolio or follow me on Twitter.

When I'm able to put my computer aside, I love spending time with my beautiful wife, going to the beach, discovering new music, eating sushi 'til I get sick, hiking, motorcycles, art, pale ale, and learning anything my brain is willing to retain.
