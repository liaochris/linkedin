Kenneth Tan's Summary

I have been educated and trained in different aspects of design and have graduated Bachelor of Arts in Multimedia Arts.

Arts and Design has really been a love of mine. I've been involved in the internet since the 90's (in my preteens) and I've managed to self-study making websites when I was 12. By 13 I already knew how to code up my websites in HTML via notepad and have self studied and have become pretty well equipped with Photoshop and Illustrator. Nevertheless, tradition art is not to be left behind.

It's really a passion of mine to go with technology and I've been continuously studying up to now the ways of building, developing, and establishing websites that could very well harness the wonders of the world wide web alongside the factors of domains and search engine optimization.

My goal is to make the best out of everything while I'm young and while my body can still take all the heat it can get. It's always great to exhaust all that you can when you can.

Soon enough, I'll be handling my own successful and world-famous design firm that will always bring out quality work and be sought after by the big dogs.

I'll be handling large enterprises and push forth the advancement of life here in my archipelago.
