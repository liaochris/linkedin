Emrah Özer's Summary

Inspite of being an industrial engineer and having an MBA degree, I've never worked as an industrial engineer.

But with the help of this education I gained an engineering approach which helped me to think analytically. IE also provided me the mentality that "everything can be done better and learning isn't tough if you do not scare of it".

I've started flash aiming to make cartoon animations at 2000. By that time I also started 3d modeling and tried to do animations with 3d max and bryce. I learned as2 and developed some unimportant web sites for groups, friends etc.

After graduation I worked as a 3d modeler and during that time I continued flash development with freelance projects.After lots of architectural visualization projects I gave up modeling at 2008 and decided to do flash development only. After a smooth migration to actionscript 3, I concentrated on flash development and developed lots of advertisement campaigns,microsites and advergames.

Since late 2010, I gave up advertisement campaigns and focused on game development and mobile development.Nowadays I've been learning Objective C and C++ and also experimenting with Unity3d.

I've been trying to be inside the community. I attended FITC @Amsterdam 2011, FOTB @Brighton 2011 and OFFF @Istanbul 2012 . I'm trying to drive communities in Turkey also. Thus I created following facebook groups which had meetings, little seminars and workshops.

- Actionscript Developers @ Turkey (Since 2011)
- Game Developers @ Turkey ( Since 2012 )
