Jill Castle's Summary

Jill has over 20 years of professional and practical experience in the field of pediatric nutrition. She is a child nutrition expert and is passionate about equipping families with knowledge and "know-how" when it comes to raising healthy eaters. She works with a variety of pediatric nutrition challenges including obesity, eating disorders, autism, allergies, and feeding dynamics.

For businesses and organizations, Jill can help with program & product development, educational materials, consultation, and speaking or writing about topics in pediatric nutrition.

Specialties

Pediatric Nutrition especially normal feeding, eating disorders, obesity, allergies, veganism, feeding difficulties, growth failure, family feeding dynamics, sports nutrition
