Martha McBride's Summary

Specializing in the direct sales industry, I help companies get started or improve.

I'm grateful for a professional journey that has included creative self-employment, business development, writing and editing, research, coaching and training, talent search and acquisition and board positions. Every piece has contributed to a unique skill set and a network that inspires me and allows me to help others with their goals - or quickly put them in touch with the perfect person for the job.

Specialties

I'm a talent scout, teacher and coach by nature, with a special interest in building vision in others. Nothing inspires me more than helping someone experience more satisfaction and success at work, while accomplishing their personal goals. I'm a big proponent of a solid Plan B. Pick one. Have one. Provide your own financial security in an unpredictable world and you'll have the confidence to be more creative and have more fun in everything you do.
