Tony Beshara’s Summary

Since 1973 I have been a sales recruiter here in Dallas and since 1986 I have been placing IT sales professionals. I am always looking for good candidates for my clients.

If you are in the DFW area and need a new job or career, call us a Babich. We deal with just about every professional type of job..

Outside the DFW area and need help? Go to www.thejobsearchsolution.com It is a 45 hour online program that imparts everything I have learned about looking for a job since 1973...or my books The Job Search Solution and Acing the Interview and my newest book "Unbeatable Resumes".

Specialties

Any kind of software sales, sales support, consulting services...staff augmentation to offshore to project consulting.
