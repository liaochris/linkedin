Belinda Ang's Summary

Child of the dot-com generation, Belinda is a digital native who has built a reputable online influence in Singapore.

She is currently an independent digital consultant & also artiste manager for regional mandarin pop singer, Joi Chua. Belinda also developed the curriculum and teaches at Singapore Media Academy as trainer for a WDA-accredited Social Media course.

Her last employment as Digital Specialist with MSLGroup, Singapore (member of Publicis Groupe) puts her in a position to lend support and counsel the network's regional office and clients. Projects she led such as the campaign for P&G Whisper to shift women's perceptions in the taboo topic of periods, came in runner-up in the global Bees Social Media Awards in 2010.

Aside to this, Belinda is also a personal social media consultant for world-renown C-level business & brand strategist, Mr. Martin Roll. She spearheaded @SGbrands, a Twitter account to help promote brands & businesses in Singapore. She also wrote marketing articles for Lianhe Zaobao in 2009 & has been interviewed on "Good Morning Singapore", 938LIVE, Asia360, DigitaLife & MyPaper.

Belinda was originally a film major trained in Temasek Polytechnic, Singapore & Beijing Film Academy, China. She kick-started her own food & beverage venture upon graduation, managing multiple business functions in her 2 outlets. The business was sold after 5 years as a career move to pursue her interest in integrated marketing.

She then joined premium event companies, A Group of People Pte Ltd as Event Director & Festival Square Circle as Senior Account Manager, managing key accounts eg. South Australia Tourism Board, General Motors, Fuji Xerox, Singapore Tourism Board, WDA, Marina Bay Sands & Singapore International Water Week among others.

Belinda's fundamentals in creative, diverse management experiences & exposure in cross-marketing disciplines places her in a good position to take on versatile roles in organisations that motivate changes at the forefront.
