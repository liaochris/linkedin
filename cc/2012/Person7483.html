Victoria Edelman's Summary

Victoria Edelman is a Lead Associate with Booz Allen Hamilton’s Cyber Technology Team, supporting the firms’ clients during complex litigation discovery projects. She is a member of the Sedona Conference Working Group 1 on Electronic Document Retention and Production, the EDRM Jobs Project and ARMA. Victoria is based in Washington, D.C.

Victoria is a frequent moderator, panelist and presenter on topics surrounding e-discovery for CLEs, webinars and college-level courses. She has taught more than 4,000 students and has presented or facilitated more than 150 webinars. Her e-discovery topics have included: project management best practices, budgeting, social media, the impact of cloud computing and the sustainability of jobs in the industry.

Prior to joining Booz Allen Hamilton, Victoria served as the Director of Education and Professional Services for iCONECT Development LLC. Previously she served as the Director of Training and Development for Encore Discovery Solutions, and the lead Concordance trainer for LexisNexis Litigation Services (formerly Dataflight) and was Wave Software’s third employee, where she ran Wave University. Victoria graduated from William & Mary Law School where she worked at the Center for Legal and Court Technology (formerly the Courtroom 21 Project), and has a B.F.A. in art history summa cum laude from Virginia Commonwealth University.

Specialties

Consulting, ediscovery, litigation support, technical training, attorney training, public speaking, business development for training and consulting services, litigation data management business process best practices.
