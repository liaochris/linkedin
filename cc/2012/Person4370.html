Mark Walztoni's Summary

I am a co-founder and Chief Business Development Officer for Lotus Leaf Coatings, whose superhydrophobic and superhydrophillic coatings technologies were developed at the University of New Mexico, Sandia National Laboratory, and within our lab. Our web site is www.lotusleafcoatings.com.

I have also assisted entrepreneurs and investors to commercialize technologies developed at Sandia, Los Alamos. and Argonne National Laboratories, Harvard University, and the University of New Mexico by building robust business development pipelines and securing private equity funding. Similarly, I assist private equity firms located elsewhere explore investment opportunities in New Mexico. My commercialization focus includes advanced materials and medical devices.

To increase the supply of entrepreneurial talent available within the New Mexico entrepreneurial ecosystem, I have provided human capital advisory services to global and local clients which included board and executive recruiting and executive coaching.

My recent clients include Flywheel Ventures, Lockheed Martin, Peacock Myers Law, Adidas, Homewise, Business Leadership Network, TruTouch Technologies, Patni Computer Systems, CVI MellesGriot, Pajarito Scientific Corporation, and the New Mexico Angels. My board memberships include the New Mexico Angels and New Mexico Biotech and Biomedical Engineering Association..

I have been an international speaker on technology recruitment, retention, leadership development and individual and organizational transition. Past professional speaking clients include the American Society of Training and Development, Society of Human Resource Management, Organizational Development Network, Fortune Magazine’s China CIO Forum, and Expert Advisory panelist on career transition, comprehensive on-boarding, and mergers and acquisitions for the Human Capital Institute.

Specialties

Technology licensing and commercialization, developing entrepreneurial leadership teams, mergers and acquisitions, turnarounds, and rapid growth organizations, human capital services and due diligence, onboarding, executive search, angel investing, venture capital, executive coaching, licesning agent
