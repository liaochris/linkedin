Jeff Zelaya's Summary

Jeff Zelaya specializes in teaching individuals and organizations how they can leverage online marketing (Affiliate, Search, Social Media, Display, Email, and Data Acquisition) to reach their goals. Jeff is a professional speaker and has presented at Florida International University, Broward College, ADP, Chevrolet, USDA and several other organizations. Jeff is a dynamic speaker with a real passion to help others.

His motivational and inspirational delivery will educate and entertain the audience. He draws from his diverse experiences to craft and present the message in a way that will resonate with those present.

As a marketing consultant his advice and strategies have helped companies improve their marketing and sales efforts thus improving their bottom line.

When he is not busy with work most of his time is occupied with volunteer projects and self development. He leads a volunteer group of about 30 people that regularly visit the Broward Outreach Center to serve meals to the homeless.

Jeff served as the Vice President of Professional Development for the American Marketing Association at Florida International University (Ranked #1 in the Nation) and as the President of the FIU College of Business Student Leadership Group.

Jeff majored in Marketing Management with a Certificate in Sales & Customer Relationship Management from Florida International University and is also a member in Beta Gamma Sigma, a honor society that recognizes the top 10% of business students worldwide.

He placed 1st in American Marketing Association's National Northwestern Mutual Sales Competition.
