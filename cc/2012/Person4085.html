Lex Lindeman ★'s Summary

Creator of the Leadership Accelerator Workshops©. I assist and train African ★ executives to optimize organization and personal performance by enhancing managerial competencies, talent management, building collaborative teams and improving virtual work effectiveness.

I coach and train managers, talent and leaders to achieve their personal best; through this I also achieve to improve the entire organization.
I facilitate leadership development and team building through Leadership Accelerator Workshops©. I coach and develop (virtual) teams and teach managers and teams how to improve their leadership and team effectiveness.
