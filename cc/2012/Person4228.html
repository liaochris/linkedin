Mark Anthony Spiteri's Summary

Having been working with Ixaris Systems for the past six years I have recently moved on to becoming Product Manager for the Ixaris EntroPay services. EntroPay gives both businesses and consumers convenient, self-service access to a range of global payment solutions.

My responsibility is to further develop the EntroPay suite of products with new offerings and bring the brand to the mainstream market.


Prior to this new role I was responsible for the Service and Applications Management team at Ixaris. This role compromised the following units:

Service Control: Making sure that all the Ixaris applications are operating within the agreed service levels;
Service Introduction: Rolling out of new products internally and externally;
Data Management: Database administrator, reporting and data control;

Specialties

Innovative Technology, mobile technology, global payments
