Financial Analyst

Alta Capital Corporation

November 2010 – March 2011 (5 months) Greater Seattle Area

Alan served as lead buy-side analyst for a private equity-led (world top twenty firm) management buyout of a major renewable energy firm. He built from scratch a risk-weighted discounted cash flow model that incorporated data from twenty projects and mapped out the sensitivity of return to changes in costs as well as exit multiples. The model included:
- A toggle between production and investment tax credits.
- A tab in which an investor could change the probability of success for intermediate exploration, permitting and construction stages.
- Independent cost and capital structures for each project
The model provided significant flexibility to changes in cost and timing assumptions and enabled Alan to rapidly adapt to new requirements and respond to inquiries. The private equity firm approved the valuation model but has elected to delay execution of the transaction.
