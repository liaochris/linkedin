Eric Rock's Summary

Travel Marketing and Operations Manager with extensive experience in planning and analysis, market segmentation and program execution in over 30 countries. Most recently created promotional partnerships with hotels, restaurants, attractions and retailers were offered to 20 million AAA Members nationwide. To drive country branding associated with the Sydney Olympic Games and event engagement with consumers worldwide, conceived and delivered Australia 2000, a 34-city community arts festival hosting 4,000 performers from Asia, Europe and the Americas in partnership with Present Australia and sponsored by Qantas Airlines and the Australian Tourist Commission. Early in his career, Eric earned his BS in Accounting at the University of Maryland, MBA in Marketing, was licensed as a C.P.A., and honored as a national leader by ODK.
