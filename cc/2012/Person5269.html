Patti Rose's Summary

If you’re concerned about running out of money in retirement, I can help.

When you work with me as your retirement income planner, your retirement savings is protected so that the accumulation in your account value will never decline, even in bear markets. Your contract value grows when the market is good and it’s protected (you DO NOT lose money) when the market is bad. None of my clients have lost money in their retirement accounts even in a volatile market. In fact, some accounts grew quite nicely due to entry bonuses I was able to secure for them.

I help you protect your retirement nest egg from:
•Future Taxation
•Inflation – consider that current inflation does not include food and energy.
•Stock Market Losses – Are you tired of risking your principal in order to get a decent return?
•Home Value Losses – Were you planning on selling your home in order to fund your retirement?

Need to Save More?
If you are not saving enough money for retirement because of current cash flow, I can help you find more money to save for retirement without reducing your current lifestyle.

Here’s what I can do for you: Reduce the amount of transferred wealth in your finances (the money that you unknowingly pay out each year in unnecessary taxes, fees, interest and other charges) and, instead, redirect that money to fund:
1) Your retirement nest egg;
2) Pay off your current debt or mortgage loan ahead of schedule;
3) Misc expenses such as: College tuition, paying for a wedding, Long Term Care, home purchase, family vacation.

My top priority is to build you a bucket of retirement income that you cannot outlive, regardless of your current financial situation. It's never too late and the sooner you get started, the better.
