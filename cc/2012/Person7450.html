Trevor Clendenin's Summary

I have enjoyed providing a variety of small & medium sized businesses and government agencies with dynamic websites, social media solutions, and quality hosting services for over 10 years. Presently, I am happy serving as the Director of New Media for the Virgin Islands Public Television System WTJX Channel 12, a PBS affiliate. In addition to fulfilling traditional webmaster duties, I am the station's social media strategist, community manager, online content producer, and developer.

It is my goal for WTJX to effectively engage the public in a rich discourse of the relevant issues of the day, and to continue to lead as the Territory's premier online source for news, education, video, and entertainment. Follow me!

Specialties

HTML, CSS, Drupal: Views, WordPress, Mobile Web Design, Web Accessibility (ADA Section 508), Social Media Optimization (SMO), Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Streaming Video.
