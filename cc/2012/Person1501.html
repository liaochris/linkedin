About WOW Logistics
Headquartered in Appleton, Wisconsin, WOW is a nationally recognized leader in the third-party logistics industry. In nearly 35 years, WOW has grown from a single 108,000 square foot warehouse to 7 million square feet encompassing 23 warehouse locations in three states. In addition to public and contract warehousing, WOW offers a vast array of value-added services including Transportation Management Solutions, a Cheese Financing Program, 3PL Consulting and Build-to-Suit options. WOW distribution centers offer dry, refrigerated and freezer storage. Learn more at www.wowlogistics.com.
