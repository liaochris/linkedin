Michele Bosi's Summary

I am a software engineer specialized in the 3D graphics and visualization fields and the author of Visualization Library (http://www.visualizationlibrary.org). Previously worked at Molecular Discovery (data & 3d molecule visualization - Italy), Nokia (Ovi Maps rendering - Berlin), Crytek (R&D tools programmer - Budapest). At the moment I am working at Framestore (London) as a Pipeline TD.

Specialties

2d-3d graphics visualization, real-time rendering, OpenGL, GLSL, C/C++, Python, QT4, MFC, GUI design.
