Robin Seidner's Summary

Accomplished sales and business development professional with demonstrated expertise in direct sales and other customer-facing roles (account direction, business development, consulting strategy, client services), as well as branding, marketing and PR, product and marketing strategy. Emphasis in online and social, I have a deep interest in social computing technologies, social media measurement and social media execution strategy.

My passion is bridging the connection between strategy and tactical execution. I love to think through a strategy and hammer out what's needed to actually get a strategy working. Too often at the strategic level, not enough thought is put into what's required to accomplish goals. I am really good at putting those pieces together.

I understand social media as a way to transform companies to a social business enterprise -- how these technologies and communications platforms transform product development, customer service and almost every touchpoint with partners, investors and customers.
