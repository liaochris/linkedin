Rod Githens' Summary

I'm an Assistant Professor and Graduate Program Director in Workforce and Human Resource Education in the Department of Leadership, Foundations, and Human Resource Education at the University of Louisville.

My work centers around fostering humane, accessible, and diverse workplaces through workforce and human resource development. I teach our undergraduate, master's, and doctoral programs, primarily in the area of organization development and change.

Specialties

Workforce and Human Resource Development, Organization Development, Creating Diverse and Humane Organizations, E-Learning and Distance Education, Increasing Access to Self-Sustaining Careers through Postsecondary and Community-Based Workforce Development, Action Research, LGBT Employee Issues
