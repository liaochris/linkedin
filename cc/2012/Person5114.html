Amy Christensen's Summary

Although I work with clients of every imaginable size, my favorite niche to work with is small business owners and non-profits as I excel in providing these clients with high quality print & web design solutions within their budget constraints.

While my design style places emphasis on appealing and creative design, my designs place an even stronger emphasis on marketing strategies to help my clients receive the maximum result on their marketing projects.

Through the years, as my business has grown, I've often considered taking it to the "next level" and hiring more designers to assist me in meeting the needs of more clients. However, I love the "hands-on" attention I'm able to provide my clients - from concept to completion. So instead of going the "agency route" with my business, I've opted to travel a less traditional path and remain a sole freelancer and just take on the projects I have time to complete as I can. This allows me to keep my overhead and pricing as lean as possible for my small business market.

Specialties

My print design experience includes extensive knowledge about the print industry, as well as handling print brokering for clients who do not wish to work directly with printers. My web design experience includes providing clients with inexpensive "base" websites they can expand upon as time and their budget allows, as well as incorporating more complicated programming and cutting edge technologies like content management systems that allow clients to perform updates on their site themselves.
