Jinny Bromberg's Summary

I consider myself a global-minded, innovative and energetic person. I started my career in the language industry early on in life and soon developed a passion for different languages, cultures and communication.

Bromberg & Associates was founded in 1999. These days our company provides services in over 150 languges, ranging from on-site and telephonic interpreting, document translation and website localization, to voice over talent and bilingual staffing. We are a WBE and DBE certified business and a holder of the GSA Federal Supply Schedule award. Our company is looking forward to many more decades of innovation and success on the forefront of the language industry.

To improve the quality of interpreting services we developed and now publish various training materials, including Interpreter Training Guides and Linguistic Resources. In addition I have co-authored a Language Interpretation in Health Care report, highlighting the disparities in meaningful access to health care.
In 2010 we also published an illustrated children's book, A bird in the hand, a collection of proverbs in 7 languages.

Specialties

Developer of online language-specific training for legal and healthcare interpreters.
Passed State Certification Exam in MN and currently am the first of only three State Certified Russian interpreters. I am also an interpreter with the State Department.
