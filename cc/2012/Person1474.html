Carol Schreitmueller's Summary

Currently consulting with branded North American CPG firms on product and category innovation strategy and execution.

Product Management executive with 15+ years of hands-on product leadership experience, combined with advanced skills and training in four specialized areas critical to product success:

INNOVATION: Delivery of products and services which solve consumer problems and drive profitability, new channels, first-to-market entry

SUSTAINABILITY: Integration of current sustainability best practices into existing and new processes to enhance offerings, profitability, speed to market

INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY MANAGEMENT: Identification, protection, defense, and negotiation for sustainable competitive advantage

CONSUMER INSIGHTS: Synthesis and translation of consumer trends and market research into strategic roadmaps with profitable results
......................................................................................

My expertise is in facilitating the development of groundbreaking new products, services, and business partnerships that leverage connections between consumer needs and available or emerging technologies, materials, and processes.

Prior to joining PMI in 2001 as a Product Manager, I was a Business Development Manager and a Merchandise Manager for an online retailer. I worked with glass design and technology for 25 years, was the principal of Prisma Glass Jewelry, and launched a specialty gourmet catering company focused on solutions for food/environmental allergies.

My deep involvement with engineering plastics technology began in 2002, evolving into the launch of innovative products manufactured from a custom-formulated 25% post-consumer/75% pre-consumer content recycled resin known as PMI's eCycle.

eCycle 100% recycled polypropylene material is a 2009 winner of the iF (International Forum Design) Material Innovation Gold Award.

Member of Advisory Board of NBIS/Network for Business Innovation Sustainability.

Specialties

Product management, Roadmap development, Product lifecycles, Feature definition, Partner management, Business development, Consumer insights, Market research, Trends research, Competitive intelligence, Merchandise management, Product planning & strategy, Requirements definition, Supplier & Distributor relations, Sustainability management, Go-to-market strategy, Intellectual property strategy, Developer relations, Innovation management, Product marketing & evangelism, Branding, Change management
