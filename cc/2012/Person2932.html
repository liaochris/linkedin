Farid Mheir's Summary

With 20 years in high technology, Enterprise Architecture and Software Engineering, he brings experience in Services Oriented Architecture (SOA), corporate IT organization and governance, Enterprise Architecture (EA), Business Process Management Suites (BPMS), Internet and e-commerce application development and operations.

As an entrepreneur, he founded and grew a 60-person software development company with clients in Canada, USA and Europe. His vision and direction expanded the services from a web ecommerce platform to a distributed, scalable order fulfillment system. He innovated in various domains, from ergonomics to visualization.

Technology and Enterprise Architecture consultant to large organizations in the financial, insurance, retail, telecommunication and electronics industries, he helps create strategic plans, select software solutions, capture and translates business requirements into actionable project plans.

Ambitious, rigorous and intellectual, he is a subject matter expert and a visionary leader that constantly works in teams for input in others.

Analytical and futuristic, M. Mheir remains business-focused, results-driven and motivated in environments where business evolution can be enhanced by technology in new and creative ways. He is a passionate problem solver when technology can yield business results.
