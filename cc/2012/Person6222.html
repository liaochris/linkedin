Stacy Lukasavitz's Summary

NOT "social media strategy."

There's a lot more that goes into a strong digital presence than that — for both brands AND people.

I build integrated marketing/PR strategies based on data from multiple digital touchpoints to give brands the upper hand online. I help them understand "the point" of digital marketing, which could include everything from SEM/SEO to website traffic, online outreach, social media, text analytics, mobile campaigns, and more. It all starts with the data. What happens next depends on a brand's goals, audience(s), and the analysis of that data. That's where I come in.

My approach spares you of the B.S., decapitating the fluffy unicorns and scaring away the rainbows with data-driven strategies and analysis . . . and a smile. :)

Specialties

• Website performance metrics evaluation, competitive analysis, and reporting
• Comprehensive social web presence auditing, assessment, and strategy
• Social conversation data mining and competitive qualitative and quantitative analysis
• Content strategy, regardless of medium
• Copywriting for web (SEO) and traditional marketing materials
• Personal and brand storytelling through various media
• Personnel training customer/guest service
