My Mission is to be passionate about continuing my hands-on experience within premiere establishments, that will compliment my business background, as well as further my entrepreneurial knowledge and skills.

My Professional Experience has taken me from Shipping/Receiving to Manufacturing Engineering, and Executive Chef to Business Development Manager... and I look forward to continuing on my path! These combined experiences have allowed me to learn & grow a diverse skill set that I am able to apply to my clients business development needs.

Philip Jordan Creative isn't your traditional web development & consulting agency. Our team doesn’t fit that mold, and we aren't big on conforming. We're thinkers. We like getting outside the box to get the results small businesses deserve. But we don’t exit the box, just to be cool or hip. We care too much about conversions to let that cloud our vision. What we are is a web-centric consulting firm that allows creativity, technology and strategic marketing, to meld seamlessly into small business solutions.

"Living ThePhilLife" is more than a catchy slogan; it’s a way of approaching each day. The experience we create for our clients is our passion, and this is the fuel that drives us to succeed. We don’t just “talk the talk” we make sure the rubber meets the road! Our customers are treated with the highest level of integrity & honesty and we nurture a positive & inspiring working environment.

This is Our Culture - Lead through dedication, Listen to your heart & Learn from your experience… Business is good when you’re living ThePhilLife!
