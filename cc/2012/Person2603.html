Mark James's Summary

Our economic and business development team at American Electric Power is staffed by experienced economic development professionals. We provide comprehensive economic development services across AEP's 200,000 square mile (518,000 square kilometer) territory in portions of Arkansas, Indiana, Kentucky, Louisiana, Michigan, Ohio, Oklahoma, Tennessee, Texas, Virginia and West Virginia. We provide:
-Location advisory services
-Comprehensive location research assistance
-Area tours
-Due diligence assistance
-Energy planning, pricing and service options

We also support our existing national accounts and major employers with energy audits, expansion assistance and a variety of services designed to support their investment in our service territory.

Lastly, our economic devleopment professionals support the 1000's of economic development organizations throughout the AEP footprint.

Specialties

Utility, site location assistance, economic development
