Amy Broghamer's Summary

Since beginning her professional career in sales 13 years ago, Amy has worked in three of the most competitive sales industries. At Toshiba Business Solutions, Amy was the #1 Salesperson in her first year and a President’s Club Winner. Amy then entered the Cardiovascular Pharmaceutical Arena, where she won President’s Club 3 of her 4 years.

Extraordinary customer service coupled with a relentless work ethic is the pinnacle of Amy’s success and what has led her to her latest and lifelong challenge – Real Estate.
Real Estate is a natural choice for Amy. Her mother has been a realtor in Kentucky and Ohio for almost 20 years, and her father a commercial lender for nearly 30 years. In her first year of eligibility with the Cincinnati Area Board of Realtors she has earned Circle of Excellence award, given to the top 10% of Realtors in Cincinnati OH in her second year, she finished amongst the top 2% of Realtors in Cincinnati. Amy received Ohio Association of Realtors “Award of Distinction” for selling more than 5 million dollars in one year three times, and 4 ReMax International awards for outstanding performance.

A constant student of marketing, Amy differentiates herself in the Hyde Park and Cincinnati market place. Amy earned the Accredited Buyers Representative Certification based on coursework and practical experience representing buyers. Amy has earned the Certified Residential Specialist, which in the Residential market would be similar to earning a PHD in Real Estate. She holds a Real Estate license in the states of Ohio and Kentucky. Amy is also the only Certified Luxury Home Marketing Specialist in Cincinnati and Hyde Park. Amy attended the National Invitational Certified Residential Specialist Forum in Honolulu in January of 2008, and San Francisco in Feb 2009, and received a National Marketing Nomination there for her innovative marketing approach.
