Brent Swanson's Summary

I lead every creative effort from project planning to launch for Circadian, a Twin Cities-based creative agency, specializing in brand development, management & advocacy. I know that, to produce effective communications programs for you, I need to have an intimate understanding of your brand, your goals and your strategies. That’s why (unlike a lot of creatives) you’ll see my face from start to finish, and throughout your relationship with Circadian.

I wear many hats, and work hard to make them all look good – by combining strategic insight, creative finesse and practical expertise. Over fifteen years, I’ve skillfully transitioned between creative, strategic and account management roles, on both consumer and business-to-business assignments, from both the agency and client sides of the aisle.

In addition to Circadian’s current client list, I have also worked with great brands like 3M, Best Buy, Boston Scientific, General Mills, IBM, Johnson & Johnson, Land O’ Lakes, Medtronic, Northwest Airlines and Target.

Originally from Duluth, I now live in Minneapolis with my wife, Jen, and sons, Max & Sam. In my spare time, I enjoy many passions including movies, cycling, and a unique collection of radio-controlled trucks & boats.

Specialties

Branding & Identity, Graphic Design, Advertising, Marketing & Interactive
