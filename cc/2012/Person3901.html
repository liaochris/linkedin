Justin J. Tsang's Summary

I'm a Gen Y professional with successful experience in digital production and strategy; traditional and social media measurement; and graphic and presentation design with a diverse set of employers and clients. I'm passionate about Web 2.0, mobile technology and creative PR and marketing. Most of all, I'm excited to be working in this new era of innovation and change.

Originally from Vancouver, Canada, I am a die-hard hockey fan (go Canucks go!) and harbor a love for coastal cities and almost any type of food you can think of. On my free time, I enjoy catching blockbuster action films on opening night and taking my pug, Riggz, out to the dog park.

Weber Shandwick is a leading global public relations agency with offices in 77 countries around the world. The firm’s success is built on its deep commitment to client service, our people, creativity, collaboration and harnessing the power of Advocates - engaging stakeholders in new and creative ways to build brands and reputation.

The Seattle office of Weber Shandwick represents local, national and global clients across multiple industries. Within the technology sector, the Seattle office specializes in clean technology, digital media & entertainment, and mobile/telco/wireless markets. While the office’s heritage is technology, it also has strategically and successfully extended its purview to include clients in the healthcare, food/beverage, education and travel sectors, in addition to non-profit organizations and foundations.
