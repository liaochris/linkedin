Résumé de Jeremy Korwek

I am currently a part time news photographer and studio camera operator at WJET-TV in Erie, PA. I was the associate producer on local director Mark Steensland's production of "The Weeping Woman," as well as working as a production assistant on other short films.
Throughout my years of working with video and photography I have been mostly self though, learning about upcoming technology and other important breakthroughs.
