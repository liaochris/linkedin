Cameron Sowder's Summary

Founded in 2004 by the management team responsible for building AT&T’s TEM programs. Sowder Communications has the knowledge, resources, and experience necessary to be successful in any area of Telecom Expense Management. We are a proven and established company focused on Telecom Savings, Solutions, and Staffing.

Employee Highlights:

• Project managing the largest TEM implementation ever undertaken to date.
• Audited the telecommunications billing of more than 90% of the Fortune 1000.
• Millions in savings for both small business and enterprise customers.
• Authors of a new Global Telecom Management book due out in 2011.
• Created and managed the TEM solutions offered by AT&T for several years.
• Designed and trained various departments within many other TEM providers.
• Educations in telecom and management. Including PMI, Indiana, Cornell, and Harvard.
• Improved and tested carrier billing systems as well as TEM software applications.
• Attendance of various TEM events, and memberships in key TEM organizations.
• Building leading online Telecom Management resource.

Products and Services:

• Telecom Expense Management (TEM) – Sourcing, Procurement, and Contract Negotiation with Telecom and TEM vendors. Ordering and Provisioning Management. Invoice Processing and Bill Payment. Inventory and Asset Management. Wireless and Mobile Management. Audit, Optimization, and Dispute Resolution. Call Center and Help Desk Support. Network Management. Reporting, Analysis, and Business Intelligence.
• Telecom Project Management (TPM) – Comprehensive Management of TEM Projects and Implementations. Network Management and Maintenance. PMI and TPMA Certified.
• Telecom Workforce Management (TWM) – IT Staffing and Recruiting. HR, Training, and Performance Management. Call Center Operations and Help Desk Support.
