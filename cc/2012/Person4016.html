    MANAGEMENT RECRUITERS OF ST. CHARLES’ TOP 5 RECRUITER SHARES TIPS FOR FINDING A JOB IN A TOUGH MARKET

    St. Charles—Kristi Ramsey knows the job market is tough for candidates right now. But as a top recruiter with the world’s largest recruitment organization, she also knows exactly what a candidate needs to do to get an interview and land the job.

    Kristi Ramsey started with Management Recruiters of St. Charles in March 2008, and by year’s end she earned the number 4 spot on MRINetwork’s prestigious “Rookie of the Year” list. She ended the year in the top 5 percent of more than 1000 recruiters in the Midwest Region.

    Now Kristi is offering her top tips for finding a job in a tough market. Here are just a few of her favorites; the full list is attached.


    • Apply for jobs that actually relate to your background. If you are a VP of Sales don’t apply for a position titled “Astronaut”.

    • Spend very little time on job boards or help-wanted advertising. Everyone’s looking here. Instead, try networking sites like Linkedin.com to meet people at the companies you are interested in, and ask them for help in getting your information to the right person.

    • Google yourself – you would be surprised how many hiring authorities do the same. If you aren’t finding results that show you in the best possible light, it could be time to do damage control.

    Management Recruiters of St. Charles is part of MRINetwork, the largest and most successful recruitment organization in the world. Started in 1987 by Daniel Lasse, Management Recruiters of St. Charles is currently ranked in the top 10% of more than 1100 offices in 25 countries.

    For more information about Management Recruiters of St. Charles, please visit www.stcsearch.com.
