Michael Chinn's Summary

Michael Chinn is an accomplished creative professional and executive manager with over 15 years experience producing award winning brand identities, integrated multi-platform marketing and internal communications, advertising, and promotion for international media and entertainment, lifestyle, corporate, and not-for-profit markets.

An entrepreneur and a corporate executive, Michael is a motivational leader who has directed the efforts of multi-faceted creative and technical teams, as well as collaborative partnerships with global agencies, including Grey Advertising, McCann-Eriksson Worldwide, and Bozell Wilsenach Africa.

Providing support for such clients as NPR, CNN, ESPN, Easton Sports, National Geographic, Baltimore Museum of Art, CapitalOne, Habitat for Humanity, Hearst Radio/98 Rock, NASA, T. Rowe Price, and WorldSpace Satellite Radio, has garnered him international recognition. Michael’s work has been showcased in numerous industry publications and books.

In addition to his professional career as a brand consultant, Michael had been an integral part of the creative staffs of WorldSpace Satellite Radio, Discovery Communications, US Interactive and MCI Telecommunications. The extensive breadth of these organizations has fostered his global perspective and expertise in designing for multicultural and multilingual audiences.

Michael was honored in 2007 to be selected by digital media pioneer Peggy Miles to serve on the Digital Media Executive Advisory Board. Due to his concern and involvement with environment and humanitarian issues, he is Co-founder and COO of Canopy Ethiopia [501c3], a reforestation and alternative energy initiative, where he additionally serves on the Board of Directors.
