Albert Qian's Summary

Albert Qian is a web and social media project manager currently living in Silicon Valley (San Jose, California). A 2010 graduate of Santa Clara University with a degree in MIS, he boasts a rare combination of technical skill, marketing mind, creative drive and analytical insight, all of which are necessary to succeed in the web marketing-social media space. He also understands that in order to succeed in social media, you must put on many different hats.

As of March 2012, Albert is currently working for Cisco in Supply Chain Operations working as a project manager focused on collaboration and web technologies and also serves as a business analyst.

In his previous roles, Albert has also worked on client-facing roles with special orientation towards web and social media, in addition to email newsletter marketing and white paper writing. Notable clients have included the European Union Parliament Commission, Santa Clara University and Emmy-Award Winning newscaster Sydnie Kohara.

In August 2010, Albert also started his own blog called "The Social Media Dude" (http://www.albertqian.com), focusing on branding, simplifying complex issues in social media and social media news in the industry. Since its launch, the blog has been syndicated across several other sites, including Global Good Group (http://www.globalgoodgroup.com, top 100,000 in Alexa), Social Media Mags (http://www.socialmediamags.com, 13M readers per month), and Smedio (http://www.smedio.com).

Albert is also a co-founder of Socialize With Us (http://www.socializewith.us), a site built by social media professionals aimed at social media professionals looking for a forum to bounce ideas, read about industry news and tips and network.
