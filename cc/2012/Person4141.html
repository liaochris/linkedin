Luis Fernando Martinez's Summary

Luis Fernando has a management consulting background at one of the top firms in the world: Mckinsey & Company. From then on he has accumulated experience on data analysis and problem solving for management and strategy solutions. After McK he joined Invista where he was developed as an analyst in the strategy and CRM areas, doing business development, market research, price recommendations, customer management and logistics.

He currently provides management and strategy consulting with a strong focus on Social Business (Web 2.0, Social Media, Enterprise 2.0). Working with his partners at FuneStrat they help integrate them into value adding strategies in areas such as Sales, Marketing, CRM etc.

He co-founded and directs Teterita Inc. with Paola Badillo. A graphic and web design agency with a clear, innovative and creative focus; mixing the best of graphic arts with new and inspiring social business strategies for their customers.

He has a strong activist side which has led him to co-organize 2 Twestival events in his city as well as several fund-raising efforts and speak at several events about social media and non-profits.

In his free time he blogs in several sites about his passions: video games, movies, social media, technology, etc.
