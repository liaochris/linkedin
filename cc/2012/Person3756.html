Joshua Hammond's Summary

Josh Hammond is a Director at Zócalo Group, Omnicom’s (NYSE: OMC) premiere Word of Mouth marketing agency. Prior to joining Zócalo, he was Director of Marketing for NM Incite, a Nielsen McKinsey Company. Josh has also worked as a Sr. Consultant in Nielsen's Digital Strategic Services Group and as a Public Relations Account Supervisor at Northlich, a communications agency focusing on corporate, crisis and digital communications.

He's also held a variety of political and government affairs positions in the Midwest and Washington DC. He worked for U.S. Senator Dan Coats (IN), U.S. Senator Dick Lugar (IN), and U.S. Congressman Steve Buyer (IN-4). In addition to his time on Capitol Hill, he lobbied for the high-tech industry, working for the Computer Systems Policy Project and Varian Associates.

He is a graduate of Hanover College and is currently working to complete a MS in Interactive Media from Quinnipiac University.

Josh is an accredited (APR) member of the Public Relations Society of America.

Reach him at joshua.hammond "at" gmail.com or @joshuahammond on Twitter.

Specialties

Word of Mouth Marketing, Social Media, Defensive Branding, Corporate Communications, Ideation and Strategic Planning
