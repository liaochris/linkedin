K. Todd Storch's Summary

My grandfather taught me 'It isn't what happens to you that matters, but how you react to it that does'. It has shaped me in my personal and business career for the better!

My experience ranges from "Big 8" accounting work where I was fortunate to be on teams that took companies public and also worked on the first "Operational Consulting" team at Arthur Andersen.

A few things to know:
- I've sold products and services
- I've managed and lead sales teams that did the same
- I've helped companies build Digital/Interactive divisions
- I've started a non-profit business from the ground up
- I'm really a technology geek at heart

My curiosity and never ending thirst to learn led me to wonderful opportunities in Radio and then consulting of all media types (Digital, Cable, Newspaper, Broadcast TV and Radio).

In April of 2010, my wife Tara and I founded Taylor's Gift Foundation. I serve as President of Taylor's Gift and am responsible of the Foundation's mission of increasing organ donation to Regift Life, Renew Health and Restore Families.

I love endurance sports and completed my first Ironman at 2007’s Couer d'Alene, multiple Half Ironman triathlons and four marathons, including the 2006 New York City Marathon.

My wife Tara and I have three children, Taylor, our oldest daughter, who was nearly 14 when she passed away in 2010; our son Ryan who is 12 and our youngest daughter Peyton who is 10.

Specialties

- Matching talent to task
- Developing people to do their best
- Develop and execute web based, new media and local events to meet clients' and organizational needs
- Managing and developing a sales team to exceed revenue goals
- Managing and developing other sales managers (both Local and National) to help their teams exceed revenue goals
- Profitability analysis of local events
- Cash flow analysis
