Marlon Torres, MBA, PMP's Summary

I have a diverse professional and educational background. I am a certified project management professional (PMP)® with experience in marketing and communications. I have an MBA with a focus in marketing from the University of Leicester in the U.K., and a B.A. in theatre from Binghamton University in NY, USA.

I have performed lead dance roles on stages throughout the Americas, Europe and Asia, including venues such as the Kennedy Center in Washington, DC, the Jerry Lewis Telethon, the Kravis Center in West Palm Beach, FL, California's Napa Valley Opera House and the Singapore Arts Festival.

For the next phase of my career, I want to focus on projects in the medical devices field, sales, marketing and import/export.

Specialties

Self-starter with the desire to work in the field of medical devices.

Experienced leader who plays well with others.

Speak English and Spanish fluently.

Project Management Institute certification.

Proven ability to close sales.
