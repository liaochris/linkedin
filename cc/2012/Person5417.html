Rafal Kwiatkowski's Summary

I'm a freelance L10n/I18n/G10n(G18n/G12n) DTP Specialist and Engineer from Poland. I have over 7 years experience as a DTP Specialist (professionally since 2005), L10n & I18n Engineer and Proofreader & Translator.

Starting in SDL International, Lionbridge Technologies, ForeignExchange Translations, Studio Gambit, Transware, Lidex and other (updated CV available upon request). I can handle CAT tools (Trados, SDLX, Synergy, DejaVu, Passolo, Across, Transit, MemoQ etc.), DTP Tools (FrameMaker+structure, InDesign, PageMaker, XPress, Passport etc.), Graphic (Illustrator, Draw, Photoshop, Fireworks, Freehand etc.) & Office (MS Office & Open Office) Suites etc. I learn very quickly and i'm also very flexible what means that the time zone doesn't matter for me - i'm available when i'm needed.
I prepare files to translation from InDesign, FrameMaker, Quark, Page Maker, PDF, Word, XLS and many other file formats to translate using Trados, TagEditor, SDL Edit (SDLX) or other CAT or l10n tool. After translation i revert the conversion to the original file format. I am able to exchange the translation and TMs between CATs (f.e. SDLX MDB to Transit TM) using f.e. XBench tool, Access or my own procedures. There's no problem with QAing the translation by spot/visual checking, Editing the modules (UI, GUI, structure, source code) or QA Distilling to check the consistency and find & correct other potential issues in the final product (such as local suitability, typos, units localization errors etc.): publication, software or game.

Performing whole production process starting with engineering, preTranslation, preDTP, QA, LSO, Translation, DTP, QA, FQC, post CR. I am reproducting, typesetting, designing, manipulating documents and graphics, producting publications, OCRing PDFs and rebuilding scans or graphic files.

Sometimes i do construct the procedures for client's internal problems, writing manuals and guides to prevent or solve some technical issues - looking for the solution.

Specialties

customer focus, detailed eye, adobe oriented, typesetting, dtp knowledge, typography, printing, printing preparation, prepress, well organized
