Scott Bolzan's Summary

Professional Speaker and Author of my memoir "My Life, Deleted" published by Harper Collins and is due out October 2011. The book details my journey of dealing with profound retrograde amnesia after a fall in 2008. For16 years providing Professional Private Jet Management and Aircraft Sales.
