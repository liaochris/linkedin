Jessian Choy's Experience

Engage City staff to buy green, use less-toxic pest management, recycle toxics

San Francisco Dept. of Environment

2007 – Present (5 years)

Despite letters from the Mayor and SFE in 2007-10, only 6 departments accepted invites for Buy Green Trainings and none chose Buy Green Leaders.

In 2011, 38 departments participated in consultations and chose Buy Green Leaders after these new strategies:
- Partnered with SFE Climate Team, which provided in-kind outreach.
- Created online survey that instantly shows Buy Green Score to motivates staff to be greener before they submit it.

Trained over 1,300 staff through over 50 consultations. 200 staff surveyed thought it was funny and helpful.

Led the creation of SFApproved.org to make it easier for you to buy green.

Helped create green contract technical specifications with consultants, vendors, end users and Office of Contract Administration for lighting and industrial lubricants/motor oil.

Managed two consultants and eight interns, including providing public speaking, job and conference opportunities.
