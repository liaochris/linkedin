LED Source® supplies only top quality indoor and outdoor LED lighting products.

We specialize in full-scale evaluations and retrofits in both commercial and residential properties.

We can provide solutions on any scale - whether it's just a few bulbs for your house, or if you're remodeling an entire office space with new energy-efficient fixtures.

LED lights are up to 85% more energy-efficient than incandescent or fluorescent fixtures. They last significantly longer at 50,000 hours or more.

They render more "true" colors than other lighting and produce almost no heat. Best of all they contain no hazardous materials such as MERCURY!

Recently on the TODAY SHOW it was mentioned that if you install a good LED fixture today and you had a child - you won't replace that light until the child is in college!!!
