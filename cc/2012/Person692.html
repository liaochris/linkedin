Ashley Woods's Summary

Ashley Woods was born in Aldershot, UK in April 1967 and grew up in Far East Asia (Brunei, Malaysia and Singapore).

Having worked for such international brand names as Magnum Photos (Paris), Lookat (Zurich), VII (Paris) and Tien Wah Press (Malaysia), Ashley initially earned himself a reputation managing and promoting internationally known artists and photographers; negotiating copyright, coordinating image and print production and organizing major public and media events internationally. Ashley has excellent cross-cultural communication and interpersonal skills and speaks English, French and German.

He presently works as a Senior Consultant for Mach Media, a specialised MarCom agency based in Belgium. He is also an Honorary Consultant for the International Peace Bureau (IPB) Geneva, the oldest and largest peace federation in existence and was responsible for organizing their Nobel Peace Prize centenary celebration in 2010 in Geneva entitled Making Peace. www.makingpeace.org

In 2009 he founded REAL Exhibition Development, a non-profit organization and network bringing together professionals from the media and advertising who wish to bring about social change by offering their expertise to civil society, international networks and charities.

In 2008 he co-produced an educational film and photographic exhibition with the International Red Cross Museum, Geneva entitled 'In-Security: The Nuclear Dilemma' www.nucleardilemma.org. The exhibition has been endorsed by the International Peace Bureau (www.ipb.org), Engineers and Scientists for Global Responsibility (www.inesglobal.com) and Mayors for Peace (mayorsforpeace.org).
