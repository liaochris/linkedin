Patrick Moorhead's Summary

Patrick Moorhead is one of the most quoted industry analysts in the world. He is President and Principal Analyst at Moor Insights & Strategy, focused on disruption in the smartphone, tablet, PC, and living room ecosystems including the hardware, software, apps, and social media. Moorhead is a 21-year high-tech executive who, unlike others, has actually held executive positions leading strategy, marketing, and product groups in F500 technology companies.

He recently departed AMD where he served as Corporate VP and Corporate Fellow in the strategy group. He developed long-term strategies for mobile computing devices and personal computers. Moorhead also served in his 11 years at AMD leading product management, product strategy, business planning, product marketing, regional marketing, channel marketing, and corporate marketing. Moorhead also worked at Compaq Computer Corp. during their run to the #1 consumer market share leader position in personal computers. Patrick also served as an executive at AltaVista E-commerce, pioneering cost per click e-commerce and infomediary models.

Moorhead also has significant board experience. He served as an executive board member of the Consumer Electronics Association (CEA) and the American Electronics Association (AEA) and chaired AMD's Global Consumer Advicory Board. He currently chairs the board of the St. David’s Medical Center, designated by Thomson Reuters as the 100 Top Hospitals in America.

Patrick has been quoted globally in publications like the Wall Street Journal, Business Week, USA Today, NY Times, Computerworld, ZDNet, PC Magazine, Financial Times, Les Echos, Der Spiegel, and the South China Morning Post. Moorhead also appeared on TV shows like CNBC’s Squawkbox, Bloomberg Asia, and the BBC. Patrick also has presented at conferences such as CES, Computex, CeBit Asia, CeBit Europe, Comdex, Digital Life, CONNECTIONS, RetailVision, and PC Expo.
