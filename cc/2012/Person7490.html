Ville Tapio's Experience

Director, Ventures

Fountain Park

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Management Consulting industry

September 2010 – Present (1 year 6 months)

Fountain Park is a hybrid of a management consulting and a software company specialized in strategies, leadership and influencing. Our Ventures unit is a network of academic and industry experts developing new business models based on results of academic research projects. Current focus is on developing new management practices using web 2.0 technologies, social network analysis, social psychology and neuroscience.

Currently active research projects:

1) Richweb (with Universities of Oulu and Stanford)
Studying and developing design principles and software functionality for the next generation of web and mobile applications. Special areas of interest are key web 2.0 functionalities, social network analysis, distributed user experience, and end-user persuasion.

2) Do You Mind if I Read Your Mind? (with Aalto University and University of Helsinki)
Utilizing knowledge acquired in neuroscience and scientific research field investigating physiology of stress will provide totally new information about how good and bad leadership is reflected in human brains and subsequently in work organizations.

Software Consultant

Ville Tapio Tmi

September 1999 – December 2007 (8 years 4 months)

I started my entrepreneurship career as a self-taught hardware and software hacker at the age of 18. My first business was online computer, peripheral and component retailing. During the 8 years, I helped many different kind of organizations to plan and start up software and internet-based business models and learned a lot about different industries.
