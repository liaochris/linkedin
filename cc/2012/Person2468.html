Amplifi Commerce

November 2011 – Present (4 months) New York

Amplifi Commerce is a leading provider of cross-channel commerce and interactive marketing services enabling clients to navigate and thrive in today’s digital economy. The company’s e-commerce and e-business consultancy provides business strategy, digital marketing, technology implementations and operational management solutions that make a measurable difference to brands across the globe including the American Heart Association, Claire’s Stores, Conde Nast, Fox Head Racing, Laura Mercier, lululemon athletica, Movado, Musician's Friend, Shoe Carnival, Sur La Table and Vera Wang.

Amplifi Commerce relies on its deep cross functional expertise within the commerce lifecycle to extrapolate and comprehend the unique business challenges faced by clients trying to excel online. Based on an organization’s online maturity, Amplifi provides a proprietary framework for assessing not only what organizations can and should do but how to execute their plan in coherent phases to create maximum impact while mitigating risk.

Truly serving as an extension of their client’s team, Amplifi Commerce is passionate about operating in the best interest of its clients as well as making a measurable difference in the world. This is reflected in Amplifi’s corporate mantra and tagline: Your Purpose. Our Passion.

The company is headquartered in Dallas and has offices in Chicago, Los Angeles and New York.
