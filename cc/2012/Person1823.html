Stuart Cam's Experience

Co-founder

Nebius Ltd / Lead Magpie

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Financial Services industry

June 2010 – Present (2 years) Bristol, United Kingdom

Lead Magpie is an online marketplace for high quality, real-time UK mortgage leads.

The aim of Lead Magpie is to differentiate itself from the competition by operating a fair pricing policy and providing its customers with total flexibility in the selection of leads they are interested in.

Lead Magpie operates by entering leads in an eBay-style auction where the highest bidder wins the lead. Leads are automatically checked for invalid data and are delivered within seconds via e-mail and text message. All leads have given their true express consent to be contacted and full refunds are offered for invalid leads.

My role as Technical lead was to architect the overall solution and to provide leadership and technical problem-solving abilities within the development team. My client-facing responsibilities included running requirements workshops and supervising sprint meetings. I prepared and conducted technical interviews and recruited 4 developers. I supervised and led 3 other C# developers, an AJAX developer, 2 flash developers and liaised with 4 non-technical staff.

I contributed significantly in the education and adoption of agile development methodologies. I introduced the Scrum framework for the project, winning ‘Employee of the Month’ in the process.
