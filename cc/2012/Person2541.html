Don Patrick, CPC's Summary

Offering executive search and recruiting in the telecommunications, technology, and wireless space for 21+ years. Our experience includes activity in North America, Europe, CALA and AsiaPac. Highly effective wireless recruiter via both retained and contingent models.

We are certified in The Gabriel Institute's "Technology of Teaming", a completely new way to predict how people will perform on teams. It is a disruptive technology that utilizes interlocking behavioral indicators and measures Teaming Characteristics and Role-fit. Ask us about a No-Cost Solution!!

In 2009 Sanford Rose Associates celebrated 50 years in the executive search and recruiting business, and partnered with the International Executive Search Federation (IESF) to become the largest retained search organization in the world, in 40 countries and 130+ cities.

Specialties

Executive search, wireless specialty, and advanced behavioral assessment capability.
