Yuri Vieira's Summary

Yuri Vieira dos Santos was born on October 24th, 1971 in Sao Paulo, Brazil. He studied at the University of Brasilia — where he attended a course in cinema taught by Nelson Pereira dos Santos — and, after two years being secretary of the writer Hilda Hilst (1930-2004), he works today as a screenwriter and video director. "The Academic Tragicomedy", his first book, was published in 1998. "Espelho" (Mirror) is his first short film as a director.

Writing, Directing, Editing, Managing, Programing, Designing
