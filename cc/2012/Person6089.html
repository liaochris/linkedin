Neal McFee's Summary

I am a SharePoint professional that has been working with SharePoint since its release. I started my career in the network server room configuring IIS for enterprise ASP applications. I progressed from ASP to application servers such as Biztalk, SharePoint Team Services, and Siebel. I liked SharePoint and kept with it until the present day SharePoint 2010. I straddle infrastructure and developer segments in SharePoint and have designed my career path to interact with project managers, developers, system admins, and company leadership. I am a wear all the hats professional and I like it that way.
