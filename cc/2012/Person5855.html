Rob Rich's Summary

With over three decades of experience in insurance adjustment, Mr. Robert G. Rich has become a trusted name to those who have suffered extensive damage to homes and businesses. Having served clients across the United States, Rob Rich is an expert in winning claim settlements from insurance companies for flood and water cleanup, storm damage, residential and commercial fire loss, structural repair, mold contamination, and theft. Recognizing that many individuals can not go through the property claims process without getting taken advantage of, Rob Rich specializes in reducing the stress and guesswork associated with insurance by taking over all aspects of insurable losses. Acting as his clients' representative, Rob Rich seeks out the maximum settlement that the client is owed through aggressive negotiations with insurance companies. Through Rob Rich’s keen understanding of the loss management process, his firm has acquired settlements for as much as 500% of the insurance company’s original offer.

Rob Rich first became interested in the field of public insurance claims adjustment after working for a time as a structural estimates writer. Deeply experienced in preparing estimates for insurance companies, Rob Rich saw firsthand the lengths to which insurance providers go to control costs, including underestimating, cutting corners, and rushing. Taking a stand against such practices, Rob Rich began his career in Chicago as a public adjuster over 30 years ago. He established Rob Rich & Associates, LLC in 1997 to defend Arizona client interests with the most professional and complete results. Today, Rob Rich & Associates caters to a broad range of clients. With Spanish-speaking associates available, Rob Rich also ensures that the large Latino population in Arizona can be represented.
