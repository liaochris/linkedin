Steve Rosenbaum's Summary

As founder and owner of ZapPowee.com (http://ZapPowee.com), I provide businesses with turnkey solutions that automatically find more leads and convert them into sales.

STOP!

We're not talking about SEO (see below...)

ZapPowee systems make businesses and their salespeople more successful by sifting through large amounts of data to find the best qualified prospects that are most likely to buy now. In addition, our systems educate and nurture prospects so that they become more likely to buy from your company in the future.

ZapPowee also improves the sales process by improving closing ratios and providing measurable statistics for each salesperson. Everything is automated so that nothing falls through the cracks.
