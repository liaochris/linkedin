Joshua Simmons's Summary

Joshua Simmons is a technology generalist, social entrepreneur, and North Bay native. He finds his zen in complexity, and leverages his talents as a web developer to optimize, engage, and simplify.

He's the CEO of startup Bluebird Interactive, a Santa Rosa based firm whose bread and butter is providing white label development to other agencies. Bluebird leverages its earnings from client services to invest in incubating its own projects.

A North Bay native, Joshua spends much of his time giving back to the community. He's the co-founder of Web & Interactive Professionals (WIMP) and organizer of the 2012 North Bay Web Design Conference.

Specialties

User experience design, operational efficiency, LAMP web development, and community building.
