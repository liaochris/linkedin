Rachelle King's Summary

Now Specialing in Web Analytics

Do you know how well your business is doing in all areas of marketing? Can you tell how you’re performing for organic search marketing, (SEO) online paid advertising (SEM), social media efforts, TV ads, radio, direct response, print ads, trade shows and PR? There are so many different analytic tools and software available that it can feel like a nightmare trying to understand all the different ways you can view your data that impacts your bottom line. I have been in online marketing for 15 years and have worked with almost every single reporting tool since way back when; you know when Google Analytics used to be Urchin? Yes I worked on reports using Urchin, I also worked with Hitbox which became Visual Sciences which is now merged and called Omniture. I have not only used a huge variety of home grown in-house reporting tools, built from multiple different data bases and adhoc reports, I have also helped build reporting tools for large to mid-size companies. I am always happy to help give free consultations around reporting and to show you what you might be missing if you don’t know how to use all the tools to your advantage.

Extensive experience in SEO, SEM, & SMM Involved in online Marketing since 1994, knew Net Gravity before they got bought by DoubleClick - In Cable Advertising since 1991

Able to plan, drive and execute marketing strategies to improve business ROI. I have a successful record of increasing online unique user traffic and increasing higher sales conversion ratios.

Excellent communications and negotiation skills both verbal and written to help improve content advertising online through word tracker and other keyword, and reporting tools including Omniture, hitbox, and many log analyzers.
