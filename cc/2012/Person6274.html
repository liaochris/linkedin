Stuart Henshall's Summary

Based in San Francisco and Mumbai, Stuart has 30+ years of experience in consumer products, communications, innovation and technology. Clients include Nokia, Wikimedia, Intel, Godrej, Kimberly Clark, Dell, Unilever.

• He co-founded Convo and is building the Convo Mobile and Online Research Platform. He also guides overall project deliverables, and strategic recommendation. He is the lead facilitator at co-creation sessions and breakthrough workshops

• For the first twenty years of his career, Stuart held senior marketing and sales positions; eg. Marketing Director, VP Marketing and General Manager, in several FMCG, Telecom and Retail organizations, across countries like USA, Netherlands, New Zealand and India.

• Stuart is acclaimed as a thought-leader in knowledge management, social media, and the VoIP space, and shares his views on his blogs, Unbound Spiral (2002 onwards) and Skype Journal (2003-2005). Stuart has been invited to speak at and live-blog many conferences including Mobilize, SuperNova, Von, eComm, KMWorld, Reboot.

• He builds smart applications, solutions and mashups like Mobibook (Mobivox + Facebook), Phweet (Phone + Tweet), Twitmart and Equals (unified communications)

• He's enjoying immersing himself in ethnography and qualitative research in emerging markets like India.

Specialties

Running a business, product marketing, branding, design and innovation, workshops and facilitation, scenarios and futures, insights and strategy. Industries: personal products, foods, beverages, telecommunications, VoIP, technology, Internet, social media, big retail.
