Lawrence has over 12 years of experience in application development and architecture, systems integration, infrastructure and project management. As a highly qualified senior technical leader, Lawrence has an established track record of building, managing and mentoring project teams working on complex, integrated and highly scalable consumer and retail applications for the telecommunications, publishing, media and retail industries.
