Eric F. Olson, P.E.'s Summary

-Licensed Professional Civil Engineer with broad experience in international development, water supply, wastewater, groundwater remediation and sustainability education.

-I am currently involved in engineering design of Low Impact Development water infrastructure including rainwater catchment systems, greywater systems, bioswales and rain gardens.

-My goals are to continue to expand my design and project management capabilities, to take on larger projects, and to work internationally.

- I have lived for over two years in Latin America, working in international development and civil engineering and I speak spanish.
