Will Duggan's Summary

Will Duggan is a valuable and welcome thinking partner and asset to communications teams faced with difficult challenges. His approaches are deeply grounded and tested from his 20+ years working as a protégé to media gurus Tony Schwartz and David Hoffman. David Hoffman calls Will “the best writer I’ve ever worked with.” Clients appreciate his talents as a quick study, strategic modeler, and adroit integrator of trends and methods.

Partial Client List: 3 Degrees, Natural Logic, Earth Justice, GE, PetroSun, MasterCard, PBS, Discovery Channel, Corporation for Public Broadcasting, Turner Entertainment, Jack Morton, The Kennedy Center, Pratt Whitney, Energy Action, US Department of Education.

Will is founding board chair of the US Partnership for Education for Sustainable Development

Duggan is the recipient of more than twenty media awards, including an Emmy and CINE Golden Eagle.
