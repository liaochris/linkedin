Shirley Dudley's Summary

Shirley Cress Dudley has a background as a provider (licensed professional counselor and nationally certified counselor,) a medical practice manager, and now as an online marketing consultant for medical and dental practices.

After moving her own business online and learning the skills necessary to develop a strong web presence, Shirley helps medical and dental practices (and a few other local businesses)
-attract more traffic to your website
-get more calls from prospective clients
-grow your business and generate more revenue

She is the founder and executive director of The Blended and Step Family Resource Center, located online and internationally known, best selling author of the book Blended Family Advice (Xlibris 2009.)

After learning how to optimize her website, rank well in the search engines and use social media- people began asking her to help them with their online businesses. SCD Consulting Services was formed.

Shirley's focus is to help healthcare related practices show up on the first page of the search engines, reduce expenses, attract more customers, and increase their revenue.

Shirley Dudley's Publications

  • Improving Morale in High Stress Medical Practices

    • ACMPE Executive View
    • October 8, 2008
    Authors: Shirley Dudley

    This professional paper, through a literature and media search, a survey to 100 practice managers in the United States, and additional questionnaires sent to 15% of the survey participants, seeks to explore the patients’ views of visiting a medical practice, and the employees’ perspective of serving these patients.

    This paper will discuss how patients’ negative emotions, physical and psychiatric conditions, along with various other factors, contribute to the low morale of the medical practice employees.
    This topic is relevant to all medical administrators and physician leaders to better equip them in the retention of their employees by keeping moral high and staff energized. Several different methods and educational processes will also be presented to train the staff and raise morale.
