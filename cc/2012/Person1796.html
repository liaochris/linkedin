Chris Reinhard's Summary

Since 2005, Chris has been a core member of the creative team at Motive: A Hybrid Agency. His experience in traditional, digital, experiential and social media marketing places him in a unique group of marketers capable of satisfying a range of creative and executional client needs. Since joining Motive, Chris has aided in the execution of nontraditional programs for Pepsi, Mountain Dew, SoBe Beverages, Sierra Mist, Qdoba Mexican Grill, Native Eyewear, Boingo Wireless, Udi's Gluten Free Foods and Good Times Burgers & Frozen Custard.

Before Motive, Chris acted as a sampling coordinator for PepsiCo, assisting in the orchestration of several programs surrounding both the pre-launch and launch efforts for Pepsi Blue and Pepsi Twist. In 2004 and 2005, Chris sat on the Board of Directors for Boulder Freeride, the nation’s largest collegiate club. Additionally, in 2001 Chris founded PunkDiscovery.com, an online music journal for which he acted as CEO, head articles editor, columnist and head webmaster until 2006. In 2007 and 2008, Chris participated on the advisory board for Zuzingo, a social media company based in Boulder, Colorado.

In 2011, Chris was awarded the prestigious Event Marketer Grand Ex Award for Mountain Dew's DEWmocracy program, as well as a Silver Peak Award, GENNY Finalist Award, two Denver 50 Awards and four Art Directors Club of Denver Awards. He also received a SxSW Viewer’s Choice nomination as part of the OneRiot editorial staff.

In addition to his professional career, Chris has completed lead acting roles in three short films: 'Delivery' by Eli Berg, 'Incall' by Andrew Litvak, and 'Young Adults' by Cassandra Moore. His film production experience continues to increase as a part of Motive's digital creative team. He has also written editorial pieces for The Denver Egotist.

Most importantly of all, Chris has mad swag.
