Phil v. Sassen's Summary

I am currently, Head of Operations (COO). TRIBAX is a community provider enabling users to create their own social websites and social networks.

Prior to TRIBAX I studied at the International Business School Berlin and did a diploma in International Business Studies.

Within my graduate program I did an internship at the Robert Bosch Korea Mechanics and Electronics Ltd. in Seoul (Korea). While at Bosch Korea I did a market analysis about the independent automotive aftermarket in Korea and developed an intranet platform to provide research data.

Previously, I served as Manager Business Development at Buongiorno Deutschland GmbH. In this role, I successfully completed projects for several clients including Lycos, doyoo and others across Germany and Austria. My responsibilities included developing and implementing direct media strategies and provided consultation and training to important clients.

As a speaker I represented Buongiorno at important direct marketing fairs (eg. (CRM-Expo/Cologne, DIMA/Düsseldorf).

Prior to joining Buongiorno, I worked for rabbit software AG. While at rabbit software AG, I built up and managed the Berlin sales office, reported to the CEO and implemented and supported CRM software projects.


I am member of Facebook, XING, LinkedIn and asmallword.
