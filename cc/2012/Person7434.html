Tony Collins's Summary

I help businesses compete better.

I believe that a business' strategy is not an annual event but a daily way of thinking and decision making that keeps the entire organization fanatically focused on what they do better than anyone else. The data shows that companies that think this way consistently earn above-average margins and are always formidable competitors.

My work focuses on helping business owners think more strategically, focus their businesses more intensely and win higher-margin business by creating high-impact strategies and strategic plans.

Companies I work with are often at turning points due to increased price competition, slipping margins or new game-changing opportunities.

I offer:
o Strategy Workshops & Presentations
o Strategic Plan Facilitation
o Strength-of-Strategy Reviews & Assessments
o 60-Day Growth Plans
o Competitive Strategy Development Consultation
o General Business Advisory
