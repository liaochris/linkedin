jenifer daniels' Summary

after jenifer daniels found her voice, she dedicated her professional self to helping others find theirs. also known as ‘the friendraiser’, her unique nonprofit career has led her from event planner to college level educator and communications strategist.

as an award-winning educator and communicator, jenifer ''friendraises' for candidates and causes; helping them obtain new friends, educate them about their mission, mobilize them to act as advocates, and acknowledge their efforts in doing so.

she's been seen on Fox 2 Detroit, heard on Sirius/XM's 'Make It Plain w/ Mark Thompson', and quoted in the Chronicle of Philanthropy, the Detroit Free Press, the Charlotte Observer, and USA Today. she is currently a political ‘Insider’ on Carolina News 14’s Capital Tonight.

jenifer is a graduate of the prestigious Congressional Black Caucus Boot Camp, has been a featured speaker at Netroots Nation, and crafted the strategy to help an unknown candidate earn a city council seat in the 9th largest US city. she writes regularly for her blog, 'the art of friendraising', as well as CLTblog, and the Netroots Foundation's 'Winning the Internet'.
