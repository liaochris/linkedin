Bruce Caswell's Summary

I have spent the most of my working life helping clients strategically improve their business. Working in different market sectors, across industries and in companies large and small, the key challenge has always been the same; to identify and implement opportunities for improvement.

Interventions have not always been at a strategic level; helping an organisation to overcome a particular business challenge today can be crucial to ensure success tomorrow.

I am also committed to my own professional development and helping organisations enhance the skill levels of the staff by engaging in Continuous Professional Development.
