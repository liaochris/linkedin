Simon de Jong's Summary

"Simplicity is an exact medium between too little and too much." - sir Joshua Reynolds

I have 10 years of experience in designing apealing products, graphics and interfaces for both leading brands and SME's, national and international. I prefer a continuous thread to loose ends. With my training in Delft and my love for design, photography and the theatre, I'm driven, versatile and a real team-player.

At the moment we are designing & developing a multi media terminal, a chairlift, a series of remote controls, a web portal, graphic user interfaces, vending machines, bicycle accessoires and a consumer bed.

Specialties

Product design • Identity • Styling and graphics • Interaction design, graphic user interface • Design consultancy • Context mapping • Design for experience • Photography
