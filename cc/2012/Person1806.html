Cimarron Buser's Summary

Cimarron Buser is a leader in high technology who has worked in the industry for over twenty years. He is a creative and visionary executive who has built highly effective teams, and has transformed “product ideas” into market leading products. Cimarron has the unique ability to understand how to apply technology to customer requirements.

Specialties

Cimarron has extensive experience in product management, business development, marketing and sales. He is a strategic thinker who can quickly learn new markets, and he has established a reputation as an expert in industries as varied as e-books, electronic publishing, internet telephony, unified messaging, and network security. Throughout his career, he has been successful in working with Fortune 500 executives to create innovative solutions with strategic, long-term value.
