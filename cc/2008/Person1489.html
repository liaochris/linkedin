Andy Ebon’s Summary

Andy Ebon lives and breathes marketing. His core talents are the ability to observe and assimilate the strengths and weaknesses of businesses, help them clarify their objectives, and to provide strategies and tactics to help them achieve greater success.

Self-employed for most of his post-college life, Andy has always provided the marketing lift to his businesses, partnerships, and clients.

As a speaker-writer-consultant, his outstanding skill is the knack for taking complex issues, often outside the expertise of his client or audience, and communicate in a way that is easily understood.

As the calendar moves to 2008, Andy's primary goals are to release his first book, on Wedding Marketing, and accelerate his speaking opportunities.

Andy Ebon’s Specialties:

In 2007, EBS Virtual Communications spawned The Wedding Marketing Network, to focus, directly on businesses that sell to the bride and groom.

Andy's experience in wedding marketing is, in part, drawn from two stints as owner-operator of mobile DJ companies (Music Man and Designer Music), as well as many of his clients for the past decade.

Andy has been a frequent speaker and presenter at Music, Wedding and Hospitality industry conferences over a 25-year period.
