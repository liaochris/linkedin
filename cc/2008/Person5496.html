Danny Glix’s Summary

The mission of my design firm www.dannyglix.com is to brand mid-sized companies so that they can come off as 'big players' through their legitimacy invoking graphic design. We welcome the challenge of visually designing brands. Our suite of services range from logo design to brochure designs, fully integrated websites and flash animation, to top of the line specialty graphic design solutions.
