Lisa Spieth’s Summary

LSRCS
A Human Resources and Recruitment Services Company
A Human Resources and Recruitment Services company that specializes in providing hand selected professionals from a vast network strategic source. We look after the entire recruitment process from conception to closure. Our clients include the biggest names in global business, who chose us for the quality of our candidates and the professionalism of our services.
In addition we also offer services to manage and retain human capital as a strategic asset by aligning your company's workforce with organizational strategies and using metrics and measurement to drive decisions, monitor performance, and improve results.
LSRCS is not just another typical recruitment company. Our services help you identify and create methods for attracting and retaining the talent you need.

Job Description Analysis and Creation
Salary Evaluation and Computation

Organizational Development and Alignment
Employee Moral and Incentive Consultation
Training and Employee Skills Enhancement Services
HR Consultations for Internal HR policies, procedures and training
