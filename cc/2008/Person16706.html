Martha Alburquerque’s Summary

Martha Alburquerque has had 2 successful business ventures. 1st was the creation of an upscale NYC Art Gallery, Eklektik Art, catering to NYC's elite socialites. 2ndly, she franchised an eBay drop off center, a unique e-commerce concept which incorporated more than eBay. Both companies were successfully acquired by top organizations.

She is an avid Financier and gets her thrill from creating influential strategic partnerships and turning unique large scale projects into reality.
