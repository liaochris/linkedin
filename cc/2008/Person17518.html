Michael Cecire’s Summary

Currently serving (as of 03/10/2008) as Deputy Director for the Policy Office of the PA Department of Labor & Industry. Work involves labor & workforce related issues, particularly concerning economic development and workforce investment programs for the state.

Past experience includes considerable experience in community-based economic development (Peace Corps, Jewish Camden Partnership) and non-profit management.

Presently pursuing part-time graduate studies in economic development & growth at the Fels Institute of Government at the University of Pennsylvania and interested in getting into main street revitalization and/or urban policy, particularly related to the built environment, whether in the not-for-profit, private, or public sectors.

Michael Cecire’s Specialties:

Public policy analysis, Media functions/development, grant/proposal writing, desktop publishing (annual reports), web design, research & report drafting, database maintenance, fund raising, and advocacy/lobbying. Well read in public policy, urban affairs, economic development, international politics.
