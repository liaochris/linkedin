Rachel Olivier’s Summary

Professional proofreader, copy editor and freelance writer since 2000; 15 years experience in proofreading, copy-editing and other visual detail work. Twenty years in problem solving and trouble-shooting.

Proofreader:
Anything in regards to the written word needs to be proofed and edited before it goes on public display. I provide the fresh eyes to find the misspellings, grammar, punctuation, and missed or wrong words. $20/hour

Copy editing:
A more extensive form of proofreading that checks for awkward sentences, continuity, and logical flow. $30/hour

Document Transfer:
Content rewritten or reformatted from one type of document to another (PDF to Word or Excel for example). $30/hour

Resumes:
Revisions and updates done in a couple of hours. $20-30/hour

From scratch, give me an hour or so with you on the phone to go over where you see yourself headed. $150 flat fee

My resume has been used in the companion CD ROM to "How to Write and Design a Professional Résumé to Get the Job" as an example of how to put together a good resume.

Reviews and articles:
Do you have something that you have just gotten out and want written up and reviewed? I can do that (it helps if you can get me a copy of whatever it is you want me to review). .05-.08/word

Office Help:
If you're in Los Angeles and have a home or regular office in need of attention, I can organize it for you. I can spend a day or three organizing, tidying, filing, and doing data entry to catch you up so next time you need to take care of business it's not so frustrating. $15/hour

Rachel Olivier’s Specialties:

Book editing, creative writing, researching and writing content as specifically ordered by clients in a variety of areas, and resume writing. Proofreading or copy editing whatever needs to be checked. Creating order out of chaos.
