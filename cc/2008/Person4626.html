Cindy King’s Summary

After a quarter of a century in international sales and marketing, out in the field (in non-English speaking territories), I recently started my own cross cultural marketing and copywriting company for international sales.

My field experience has honed my skills in cross cultural communication and international business development.

But I became fascinated by cross cultural web communication.

So the last few years have been online where I have acquired new skills: internet marketing and international B2B copywriting.

My latest ventures online?

...www.cindyking.biz and www.getinternationalclients.com

Today, in addition to creating my own information products, I offer two services:

- International English copywriting and...

- International web strategies for businesses

For international business development and using both my new and my old skills.
