Jeff Cohn’s Summary

Jeff currently acts as a "buy side" venture consultant for family offices who have a interest building and investing in Southern California technology companies. Jeff sources and builds teams for Texas based Hunt Ventures, San Francisco based MHS Capital and the Tech Coast Angels. Jeff has been the Investment Screening Director for the Tech Coast Angels since 1999 and has built a substantial rolodex of entrepreneurs, angel investors and professional fund managers. Jeff has managed the growth of the Tech Coast Angels membership from 70 to 300 investors and 32 venture capital affiliates and has helped sourced the private financing of more than 130+ Southern California based technology companies. Jeff is also the founder of two nationally recognized social media sites DeadCellZones.com and PhotoEnforced.com. Jeff’s technology career includes consulting for Oversee.net, iXL (Former NASDAQ: IIXL), and the enterprise software company Computer Associates International, Inc. (NYSE: CA). Jeff has a B.S. in Business Administration, Finance from the University of Colorado
