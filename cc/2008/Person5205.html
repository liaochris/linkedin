Cyril Rimbaud’s Summary

After graduate studies on E-learning, Multimedia Communication and Artificial Intelligence, Cyril Rimbaud began his career in 1996 by working for the first websites of the video game publisher Ubisoft.

After a two-years experience in Africa (Comoros) as a Media Counselor, he joined the interactive agency [BaBeL@StaL] (200 employees) in 1998 and got specialized in the conception and ergonomy for websites, portals and e-shopping : Ile de France, BNP Paris, Fimatex, Axa, CCF, Crédit Agricole, Arcalis-AGF, Ministère des Affaires Etrangères, Ministère de l’éducation…

In 2004, he became Internet and New Technologies consultant for BNP Paribas.

He joined Lowe Stratéus in september 2006 as an Account Manager with a main mission: transform the digital department into a competitive one. He therefore developed the interactive part of several international budgets of the agency (Carefree, o.b) and also work on ADEME, MAIF, CAPS...

Ergonomie, Usability, Web 2.0, User experience, E-marketing, E-business, E-commerce
