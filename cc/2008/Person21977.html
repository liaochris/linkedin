Rolf Marvin Bøe Lindgren’s Summary

Headhunting - occupational testing - management coaching, by licensed psychologist.

I also do coaching of technologists and managers. I don't have

In my present position as a senior consultant & responsible for selection procedures at Dignus (http://www.dignus.no/), I manage press contacts, keep colleagues up to date on recruitment theory & research, perform occupational testing, arrange workshops, provide creativity training and yes, hunt for heads.

For DNV (http://www.dnv.com/) I worked in industrial research & development for five years, working with knowledge management, improvement of human factors in technological project work practices, mobile workers, etc.

I also headed the development and deployment of DNV's certificate of competence in occupational testing.

I will write for fun.

Rolf Marvin Bøe Lindgren’s Specialties:

Occupational testing, creativity, teambulding, coaching.

Certified on the MBTI (IPU), JTI (Optimas), e-Interplace & WorkSet (Belbin/InMente), OPQ32 (SHL), Shapes & Scales (Cut-E), FRT (Harcourt).

Conversant on 16PF5, NEO-PI, various other tests. Familiar with the Big 5 and the five-factor model of personality, behavior analysis, human/machine-interaction and whatever else I think is fun.
