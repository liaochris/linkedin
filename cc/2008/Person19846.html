Paul Jacobs’s Summary

Human Resources specialist with a particular interest in leading practice recruitment, strategic jobseeker sourcing, and employment communications.

Work with client organisations so that they can become the ambassadors of their own employer brand and cleverly target both active and passive jobseekers.

Excited by the opportunties that emerging technologies and social media are offering jobseekers and employers.

Founder of NZ Recruitment 2.0 (http://thoughtleaders.ning.com), an online New Zealand community of professionals interested in recruitment innovations, recruitment technologies, social media etc.
