Tina Cush’s Summary

Tina has spent her entire career focusing on the marketing communications industry. At Ziff Davis Media in 2000, Tina focused on her business development skills as a Marketing Assistant for The Net Economy Magazine. Over the past 7 years as a Marketing Director for L.I.S.T. Incorporated, Tina was sought as a leader in business development and marketing communications. Through hard work and extensive training her skill set expanded into web marketing, both paid and organic, graphic design, trade show and event management, blogs, opt-in email management, in compliance with CAN-SPAM laws, newsletter broadcasts and social media marketing.
