Dr. Annette Colby, RD’s Summary

A leader in her field, Dr. Annette Colby, RD is a magnetizing catalyst for healing who has dedicated her life to the evolution of a joyful human experience. Annette holds a doctoral degree in Nutrition and Exercise Physiology, is a Registered Dietitian, a Certified Master Practitioner of Neuro-Linguistic Programming, Focusing Guide, Reiki Master, and Master Energy Therapist.

Annette has a unique gift to help women and men realize their full potential mentally, physically, and spiritually. She gently guides individuals through the process of healing by working with beliefs, emotions, body, and spirit. She can be of enormous help when you're dealing with:

● Emotional Distress
● Binge or Emotional Eating
● Compulsive Overeating
● Stuck in Chronic Dieting Cycles
● Depression
● Low Self-Esteem
● Inner Criticism
● Obsessive Thoughts
● Limiting Beliefs that Block Your Success
● Negative Mental Attitude
● Feeling stuck and needing ways to move forward

She is the author of three books:
1) Your Highest Potential: The New Psychology of Understanding and Working with Self
www.YourHighestPotential.com

2) Body Redesing: Goal Setting Secrets for A Thinner, Happier You
www.LovingMiracles.com

3) Coauthor: 101 Great Ways to Improve Your Life Today, Volume 2.

To contact Annette, please send an email to Annette@AnnetteColby.com
