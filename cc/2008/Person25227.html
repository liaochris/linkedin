Kevin Boles’s Summary

I have developed and administered database applications since the early 1990s. I have worked exclusively with SQL Server since 1997, mostly as an independent consultant. While I consider myself an expert in several areas of the product and enjoy teaching as well as consulting, my true passion is performance tuning and analysis. The most important benefit I bring to my clients however is my mentoring skills. I take great pride in the fact that their staff will be much more effective at interacting with SQL Server - which pays dividends long after I am gone. I hold the following SQL Server certifications: MCT, MCITP - Database Developer, MCITP - Database Administrator, MCDBA and most importantly MVP.
