Salvatore Larosa’s Summary

Senior business consultant working on business development issues, market and technology scenario analysis and interactive / multichannel services analysis and modeling. I am particularly focused on consulting activities related to Web 2.0 and social media, Marketing, eBusiness strategies and digital convergence. I work on

- development of new business areas (new markets, new technologies), market analysis and prospect exploitation;
- business modeling, strategy definition, service analysis and positioning for Web sites, wireless VAS, digital TV services;
- applications of social media and social networking services for Publishers, Public Administrations, mobile operators and advertising companies;
- interactive service analysis and modeling;
- definition of interactive marketing campaigns;
- CRM/community strategies and process modeling.

I have worked with various firms belonging to different industries (finance, consulting, telecom, ICT).

Goals:
- keep working on business exploitation of interactive / convergent services and support business startups;
- support Customer executives/managers in achieving their business improvement goals.
