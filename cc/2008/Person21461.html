Rich Jackson’s Summary

I'm a "gadget guy" who has watched and experienced the consumer electronics industry evolve from shellac-based 78's to the indescribable. I have developed a good understanding of high-end residential control systems including the associated audio-video portions. I have also developed good relationships with the key implementers of these systems, as well as those who manufacture and distribute these types of products and systems.

Rich Jackson’s Specialties:

Analog and Digital Audio, Residential Control Systems, Systems Integration, Lighting Control.
