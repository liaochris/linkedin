Rod Ford’s Summary

Rod Ford is the founder & CEO of CognitiveDATA, Inc. A Mechanical Engineer by degree, CognitiveDATA is the 4th technology company he has launched in the past 19 years. CognitiveDATA recently celebrated its six year anniversary by being named to the Inc.500 list as the 208th fastest growing private companies in the U.S. with 4 year growth rate in excess of 1,000%. The company currently has over 300 direct national marketing companies using its innovative marketing response technology.
