Matt Munoz’s Summary

Innovative and results-oriented public relations and event production professional with experience in activities management and product performance oversight.

Strong background in media relationship-building and negotiation with high-profile clientele that amplifies product and event promotion. Skilled communicator; fluent in Spanish and English.

-Since 2005, I've been running Bakersfield's premier social networking website: Bakotopia.com, and now oversee our bi-weekly publication available all over Bakersfield, CA. I'm also a staff writer at Mas Magazine, Bakersfield's best Latino publication. Mercado Nuevo is the name of our publishing house, and is owned by The Bakersfield Californian.

-My resume is a mix of entertainment and media jobs - NARAS during Grammy season, LAMC Production Manager, House of Blues opening up retail shops all over the US, Paramount Studios records department, and as a personal asst. in Hollywood.

-My band Latin ska reggae band Mento Buru has been performing all over California since 1991. We've released two full-length CD's, been featured on numerous compilations, as well as TV shows.

-Making connections is what I'm all about. My goal for 2008 is to bring the Bakotopia brand name to the masses, and to continue playing music for our fans.
