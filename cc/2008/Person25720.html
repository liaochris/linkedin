Dr. Tim McGuinness’s Summary

Tim McGuinness, Ph.D.
Web & Technology Business Process, Product, Marketing, & Business Development Professional - I make it happen!

A seasoned professional in various subject areas, with extensive experience in Marketing, Internet, Business Processes, Regulatory & Information Technology.

I continue to focus in Large Scale Internet Publishing, Business Development, Product Management, Marketing, Regulatory & Technology areas in my day to day professional capacity, and academic activities on personal levels. This profile include computer industry and business activities, and does not include academic or scientific activities.

As a seasoned professional for 30 years, Tim McGuinness has been inventing, developing, and deploying compliant technology, products, and processes to improve business and individual's daily lives. One such software product has been continuously published for OVER 22 years! That product FormTool™ is a top ten all-time best seller (for more details about his product history click here).

With 30 years experience and a primary focus on applying technology to the improvement of commercial technology products and business processes, in compliance with complex requirements and regulatory environments, I have been responsible for the successful implementation of numerous products and services in a broad spectrum of industries and market sectors from public to private.
