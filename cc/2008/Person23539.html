Silvina Georgieva Furnadjieva’s Summary

My current job as translator in .net Magazine is translation of articles on web design, development, marketing, SEO, etc. in the Bulgarian version of this respected British magazine for web people.
My most valued ex-job up-to-date was as a Regional Manager at iCentres Project. After more than two years of work, telecentres in the iCentres Project were divided into several regions. As a regional manager, I was responsible for the successful work of telecentres in Bulgarian administrative regions of Sofia City, Sofia Region, Blagoevgrad, Kyustendil, Pazardzhik and Pernik. I worked in a team with one assistant manager.
I am also professionally interested in event management and I am developing in this field. I have taken part in the organization of several local editions of OpenFest, the annual open-source conference in Bulgaria, and several conference events and meetings of international delegations in my job at iCentres Project.
