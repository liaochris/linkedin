Simon Belt’s Summary

Simon Belt established the Web and Technology Consultancy 'Simply Better IT' to provide computer consultancy services to small businesses in Derbyshire, helping to transform the IT infrastructure and business processes of small businesses, to those best suited for engaging others. Established in 1999 in Manchester, Simply Better IT is now based in New Mills, Derbyshire, providing on-site and remote computer services in Derbyshire and Manchester.

From the Internet to your PC I can help you to use technology better:

* registration of your Domain Name
* internet connections - ADSL broadband, SDSL, satellite and cable
* website design, optimisation and hosting
* network cabling, including patch panels, floor ports and switches
* firewalls, antivirus and antispam
* server specification, procurement, installations and support
* email setup, database development, file storage, data backup and restore
* computer services, procurement, installations and support
* printer and scanner specification, procurement, installations and support
* remote access to file storage, email access from your mobile phone

I concentrate on long term trusted relationships that allow me to deliver computer services as an integral part of your business rather than as an outside agency. For all your IT consultancy and computer services in Derbyshire, contact Simply Better IT to see how we can help you. See www.sb-it.co.uk for further help.
