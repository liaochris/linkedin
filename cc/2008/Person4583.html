Chuck Green’s Summary

Principal of Logic Arts Corporation--a design and marketing firm--and the host of ideabook.com--a popular center for the exchange of print and online communication ideas.

Designs and produces corporate identification and marketing/advertising materials for organizations such as Batten Institute at University of Virginia, PrintSoft, Aduro BioTech, KPMG Peat Marwick, and the United States Postal Service; Templates and style sheets for organizations such as NetPost Mailing Online, Oki, and Lotus/IBM.

AUTHOR OF

The InDesign Ideabook (ISBN 0966958756, Logic Arts, 2005 )

The QuarkXPress Ideabook (ISBN 0966958764, Logic Arts, 2005)

The PageMaker Ideabook (ISBN 0966958748, Logic Arts, 2005)

Design-It-Yourself: GRAPHIC WORKSHOP (Paperback) (ISBN 1592533485, Rockport Publishers, 2007) (compilation)

Design-It-Yourself: Newsletters (ISBN 1564967670,Rockport Publishers, 2002)

Design-It-Yourself: Logos, Letterheads, & Business Cards (ISBN 1564967689, Rockport Publishers, 2001)

The Desktop Publisher’s Idea Book, 2nd Edition (ISBN 096695873X, Random House, 1997), a collection of project and marketing ideas for word processing and desktop publishing users

Clip Art Crazy (ISBN 0201883619, Peachpit Press, 1995), a collection of art on CD-ROM and a how-to guide for finding, choosing, and using high-quality clip art

CONTRIBUTOR TO
