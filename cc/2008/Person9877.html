Ilyce Glink’s Summary

I have been writing and talking about real estate and personal finance for the past twenty years through my nationally syndicated column (appearing in 100+ newspapers and websites), eleven books, my radio talk show, online at ThinkGlink.com, and now in my new online video series, "Expert Real Estate Tips." The goal is to help consumers make smarter choices about the money and real estate options that they have.

In my other company, Think Glink Publishing, I create strategic content for companies to help them connect better with their customers and clients. This might include custom video, custom online or offline content, books, podcasts, webinars, speeches and other interactive opportunities.

Ilyce Glink’s Specialties:

Content creation, writing, editing, video production, radio hosting and production, television reporting and production, organization, and team building and management.
