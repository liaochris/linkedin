Amy Powers’s Summary

Amy has been involved in the Interior Decorating field for many years. Her interest in becoming a Home Stager began when she realized the opportunity to partner with sellers in the sale of their home - fast and for top dollar. She has had a life long passion in redesign and specializes in decorating using tools and resources the homeowner has in their home, costing them much less than an expensive and full Interior Design service. She is constantly refreshing her design skills through research and education. Amy has a strong passion for staging design, curb appeal and working with people. Her forte is understanding people's needs and motivations. She understands the pressure sellers face when placing their home on the market - and the stress buyers have in finding a place they can call home. Amy likes adding special touches inside and out to immediately draw the buyer in and entice them to want to see more of your home - from the portico to the patio.

Amy Powers’s Specialties:

Interior Decorating, Redesign, Organization & Home Staging. Primary function to make homes marketable.
