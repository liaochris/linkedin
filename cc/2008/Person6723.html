Dilip Abayasekara’s Summary

Accredited speaker, communications expert, and proven leader, owner of Speaker Services Unlimited. Enhances the performance of organizations and individuals by improving their communications, connections, and presentation skills. Provides one-on-one customized professional and executive speech coaching as well as coaching in voice and performance, training seminars on public speaking & presentation skills, leadership, creative thinking via mind mapping, and sales. Professional speaker providing customized inspirational keynotes. Author of
"The Path of the Genie -- Your Journey to Your Heart's Desire."
www.drdilip.com www.speakerservicesunlimited.com

Dilip Abayasekara’s Specialties:

Speech coaching (customized for individuals), performance coaching (excelling in human performance, whatever the field of endeavor), voice coaching, human behavioral styles. Seminars and workshops on: communication/public speaking/presentation skills; leadership; teamwork, Mind Mapping; sales training; and inspirational, customized keynotes.
