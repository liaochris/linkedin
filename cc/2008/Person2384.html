Barbara French’s Summary

Barbara French is co-founder and managing editor of Tekrati, the world’s most popular source for news and directories on the high tech industry analysts. Tekrati websites serve more than a quarter million unique visitors each year. In addition to her role at Tekrati, Barbara is a consultant at Influencer50, a services company specializing in influencer identification and engagement. She also sits on the editorial advisory board of Business Trends Quarterly magazine. She is based near San Francisco.
