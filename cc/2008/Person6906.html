Darin Warren’s Summary

With over 11+ years in web development and web design with clients such as MTV2, Mandalay Resort Group, IDT, Skullcandy, Candesa, PSNet, The Entity, LDS Church and countless others. Currently running his own web design and consulting company Darin is also the Director of Multimedia at WorldVuer.com a online community communications company.

Designing, developing, and building websites in HTML, Flash, CSS, php, asp. Clients include MTV2, IDT, Sandman Studios, PSNet, WorldVuer, Skullcandy, Candesa, The Entity, LDS Church, Homebuilders, RealEstate companies and many many others. PHP and ASP experience.

Experience in setting up Red5 Media Server, Apache, MySql and developed www.NapkinJournal.com.
