Johan Lequien’s Summary

Throughout my academic career, I have had the opportunity to develop my knowledge of consulting, within my involvement in the association ESCONSULTING and the framework of a one-year apprenticeship.

Having negociated in the United Nations in New York thanks to the "National Model United Nations", I had experimented diplomatia in an international context. My negociations in the USA were completed with my studies in the UK in Anglia Ruskin University of Cambridge, with a specialisation on Business Economics, Financial Markets & Institutions, and Retail Marketing.

The project I am actually leading with CAP GEMINI as a ERP consultant is very challenging. I bring my expertise in consulting and Project Management to structure companies, control their costs, analyse their Information Systems needs and analyse the global behaviours of workers. I enjoy fast-changing, dynamic and turbulent business environments.
