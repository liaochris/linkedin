Brian Lewandowski’s Summary

Currently I am a professional photographer in the Boston area. I specialize in Architectural and elemental photography... but do not limit myself to just those fields. Work can be see at bglewandowski.com

And....

My new photography book "Hub Hub-Bub" is hot off the presses! Take a sneak peek at this tome of photographs taken in and around Boston and Cambridge... and place your order if you're so inclined...

It's a book release, and you're invited -- check out my new book at Blurb:
http://www.blurb.com/bookstore/invited/217316/9feba38bef04d94e51c981d56050624b



I also have recently performed as a freelance Writer and Editor for the likes of Mullen, OLN and others, many elements and past experiences that make up my background are being used to drive home those two key elements.

In addition to my daily musing, I have served as a Neighborhood Editor for the Not For Tourists Guide to Boston and just released my first book (Slop and Swill from a Festering Mind ISBN: 1-4116-0357-5). This daily humor site and related interview site (www.fiveforthefamous.com) receive multitudes of visitors.

Additionally, relating to the role of a Senior Interactive Producer and Designer, I have completed prior work requiring knowledge of budgets, technical skills, design work and more for major corporations and design groups (NASCAR RacePoints, TripAdvisor, Genuity, GM, Nextel, Mullen, MCI-Worldcom). I am well-versed in audio, graphics, video production, writing and design. I have overseen Creative Direction on those projects (and additional ones) and advised staffs of up to 15 people.

I was also the Project Manager for all of the Southeast and Caribbean for Bay Networks (Nortel). I've written technical documentation, marketing documentation and more. I spent years in television production with the likes of AMC, IFC, Bravo and ESPN.

Oh, and there was that wee bit of stand-up comedy in my background.

Brian Lewandowski’s Specialties:

Digital photography, and more... architectural and interior design photography and well as fine art gallery work.
