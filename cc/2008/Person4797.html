Colleen Johnson’s Summary

Trained Virtual Assistant focused on serving and supporting the administrative needs of entrepreneurs, business owners, and individuals within the historical and genealogical communities.

I specialize in working with Microsoft Office applications, proofreading, blogging and genealogical research.

I have over 19 years administrative experience and training within the government, marketing, insurance, business, education, medical, and genealogical/historical fields.

I am a contributing author to the Gritty Business Buzz.

I also provide family history research support. I have memberships to several organizations including NEHGS and Ancestry.com. Genealogy is a passion of mine.

I am a Genea-Blogger and an active contributor to the Carnival of Genealogy and Carnival of Irish Heritage Blogs.
