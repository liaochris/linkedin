Jeff Crites’s Summary

I am an Open Innovation and Social Media evangelist, preaching the power of innovative ideas and new communication tools to transform business. I also have more than dozen years experience in Broadcast (radio) and online Media, and am known to hundreds of thousands of people who listen to the nationally syndicated Morning in America radio program.

My consultancy, Brick and Click, specializes in Online Suggestion Boxes, Consumer & Employee co-creation/idea platforms, Crowdsourcing, Social Media/ Online/Traditional Media Marketing, E-mail newsletters, Copywriting/Editing and Blogging; serving Business, Traditional Media (radio especially) and Government.

Formerly Director, North America at Fellowforce.com, an Amsterdam based Open Innovation portal and platform. Helped this internet startup with online marketing, community managment, PR, project management, editorial/branding, and consulting on ways to improve the platform and user experience.

I have two blogs that enable me to pursue and showcase my interests and passions: BrickandClick.com and ConsumerPassion.com. A third is a work in process: MainStreetReport.com.

My career includes deep experience in broadcast (radio) and online media:

Operations Director/News Anchor (I was an integral part of Bill Bennett's Morning in America syndicated talk show. I ran all technical and creative aspects of the show, and offered on-air analysis of various topics including business, the economy, technology, trends and politics)
Operations Manager/Program/Creative Director (in charge of 3 XM Satellite Radio Channels)
Creative Director (Commercial Writer/Radio Producer) w/ four First Place Awards
Podcast Developer/Producer (TownHall.com)
Network Radio News Anchor/Editor (SRN News, serving 1100 radio affiliates; I specialized in business and the economy)
