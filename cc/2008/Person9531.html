Herve Didier’s Summary

With some of the most respected brands in online sports communities in Europe, Keep Essentials Sport Network consists of 100 000 registered members who fuel some of the most dynamic sports talk on the web.

Keep Essentials Sports Network's portfolio includes a dozen action-specific websites that range from Football to Diving to Snowboarding, including the largest Skateboarding and Mountainbiking communities in France, and the second largest BMX, Diving and Sport Travelling online communities.

2.0 Million sports enthusiasts choose every month Keep Essentials Sports Network communities because they know they will find like-minded enthusiasts to engage in intelligent sports discussion, which makes us a premium place for top sports brands and major advertising agencies.

Find out more about Keep Essentials Sports Network on our corporate website : http://www.keep-essentials.com

On a more personal basis, I offer informations, studies, advice and useful resources on E-commerce, Web Marketing, Online Media and Internet Start-Ups. All details are here : http://www.hervedidier.com.

Feel free to connect !
