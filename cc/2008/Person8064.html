Felipe Memoria’s Summary

<!-- HUGE official Bio -->

Felipe Memoria creates user experience strategies for large scale engagements at HUGE, including Family.com, Cellular South, Westlaw, Fandango, Adult Swim and Reader's Digest.

Felipe has over 10 years of experience and prior to joining HUGE, he was the Creative Lead for Sports Products at Globo.com, the online division of the largest media company in Latin America, Rede Globo. While there, he designed and oversaw Globo's interactive sports initiatives, including GloboEsporte.com, the most visited sports site in the country.

Wile in Brazil, he was a professor at two post-graduate courses in PUC-Rio and authored the book Design para a Internet: Projetando a Experiência Perfeita (Design for the Internet: Designing the Perfect Experience) published in 2005. Felipe was also engaged in pioneer accessibility studies, testing systems for users with special needs for the Brazilian Government. In 2007, he endorsed the Brazilian edition of Jakob Nielsen's book Prioritizing Web Usability.

Felipe earned a MS in Usability and Human-Computer Interaction and a BS in Visual Design, both from PUC-Rio.

Felipe Memoria’s Specialties:

Usability, user experience, accessibility, strategic thinking.
