Brad Chmielewski’s Summary

We live in a world where styles change constantly and fresh, edgy images are always in demand. Professionals in every field, especially broadcast and web design, must be aware of these changes and possess the flexibility and imagination to stay ahead of the trends. I believe that innovation does not sprout from merely a great idea but in the brilliance of its execution. A great motto that I try and live by is Creative By Nature, Passionate by Design.
