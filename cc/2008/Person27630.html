Tom Zazueta’s Summary

From Apple, to CKS, Foveon, eBay, and now Coakley Heagerty, my career has focused on marketing and technology. I am currently President and Chief Strategy Officer of the longest thriving integrated marketing firm in Silicon Valley.

We help clients capitalize on their markets effectively and profitably. Our strategic focus offers clients multiple ways to interact and engage with their target audience. By integrating efforts across media and through key customer touch points, our clients see real, quantifiable increases in customer interaction with their brands and product offerings.
