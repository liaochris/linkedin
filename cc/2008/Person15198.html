Lance Polikov [LION]’s Summary

I am an accomplished recruiter with a progressive career and proven track record in generating revenue. I help companies hire top talent and impact players find better career opportunities.

I work hard to understand the objectives and goals of both clients and candidates to successfully match long-term positive relationships. I believe in creating a win-win opportunity for both and I will represent both interests fairly. My recruiting philosophy is based on honesty and integrity with a strong commitment and persistence to successfully match solutions.

My specialty has been recruiting in the Commercial Property & Casualty Insurance industry. Some of the positions I work on include, but are not limited to, Branch Management, Commercial Underwriting Manager, Commercial Underwriter, Production / Marketing Underwriter, Multiline and Monoline Underwriting, Loss Control Representative, Risk Control Management, Risk Manager, Wholesale Broker, Account Executive, Account Manager, Claim Adjuster, Premium Audit, Insurance Sales / Producer, Insurance Accounting / Accountant, Employee Benefits, Operations Manager, Customer Service Representatives (CSR's), Marketing Representative, Program Manager, Excess and Surplus (E&S) Broker, Medical Malpractice Broker / Underwriting, Professional Liability Underwriting and Claims.

I would like the opportunity to speak with you to determine whether I can help you with a staffing need or if I can help you personally take your career to the next level. Please feel free to contact me 770-642-1230 or send an email to lpolikov@cjwalshgroup.com. I work on an extremely confidential basis.
