Brian Hughes’s Summary

Brian G. Hughes is a Network Engineer a Coleman Technologies, currently concentrating on an enterprise scale deployment of secure wireless LAN technologies for an enterprise corporate healthcare company with campuses throughout the United States.
Until recently Brian was a Senior Engineer at Worley Consulting, a firm which offers services designed to provide an extension of current capabilities in the design, acquisition and on-going maintenance of network infrastructure. He worked regularly with clients, contractors and vendors to provide project definition, system integration, and program management for a variety of telecommunications infrastructure projects.
In addition to holding a Bachelor of Science degree from the University of Memphis he is also a Registered Communications Distribution Designer (RCDD) as certified by the industry standards setting body - Building Industry Consulting Services International (BICSI) as well as a Certified Wireless Network Administrator (CWNA). With the exception of a few short dalliances into the Bio-Medical Engineering industry Brian has spent most of his more than twenty years in electronics concentrating in telecommunications.

Brian Hughes’s Specialties:

Within the broader spectrum of telecommunications, Brian has an affinity for wireless, Terrestrial RF, Satellite communications, and Free Space Optic communications. This in turn has led to interest in the television industry, specifically the design and implementation of satellite down-link headend facilities. An industry rapidly evolving with the development and deployment of IPTV systems.
