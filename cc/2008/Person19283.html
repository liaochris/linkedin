Oliver Picher’s Summary

I have gotten tremendous value out of using LinkedIn and other social media tools to raise my visibility, increase my influence, and attract business opportunities. In essence, I'm applying my 20+ years of experience in public relations to social media marketing, networking, and LinkedIn. I'm sharing what I've learned in my blogs, FunWithNetworking.com and PersonalVisibility.com.

My focus is on helping executives, professionals, and businesses make themselves more visible and attract more business through their social media networks, Web, and public relations activities. I work as a consultant, coach, writer, and speaker. I also work as an interim marketing or PR counsel, building momentum and excitement for new businesses or programs. I have specific expertise in the high tech, software, professional services, medical research, and financial services industries.

I'm not an open networker, but I'm happy to connect. Send me a pitch on why we should connect and how I can help you.
