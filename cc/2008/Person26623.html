Vojtech Horna’s Summary

Vojtech is a public relations professional with a degree in public relations from the University of Southern California. Prior to joining Atomic Public Relations in San Francisco in 2007, he worked for Edelman, Ogilvy PR and Weber Shandwick.

Vojtech has secured coverage for Atomic's clients in numerous business, tech and consumer publications including the Wall Street Journal, Financial Times, CNET, TechCrunch, GigaOm and on NBC's TODAY Show.

Vojtech was also part of a team that created and helped to execute an award-winning national campaign for the launch of General Motors' 2007 Chevy Aveo. The campaign emphasized the use of social networking, blogging and webcasting to reach college students, its primary audience. The five day campaign generated 217 million media impressions.

Vojtech Horna’s Specialties:

Vojtech primarily focuses on working for technology, digital entertainment and e-commerce clients. He has experience in media relations, analyst relations, event planning, and creating and executing campaigns with an emphasis on new media and new and nontraditional tactics.
