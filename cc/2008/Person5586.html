Darren Fast’s Summary

Demonstrated expertise in technology and market evaluation, in-licensing, company formation, project management, clinical trials, product development, and commercialization. A results-oriented professional committed to personal and organizational success.

PROFESSIONAL EXPERTISE

• Effective leadership and team building strengths: able to analyze requirements, identify skills and motivate a team to successfully complete projects on time and on budget.
• Innovative problem solver and decision-maker; able to proactively develop and implement efficient solutions to challenges in a variety of work environments.
