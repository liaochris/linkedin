Wm Marc Salsberry’s Summary

With over twenty years of experience in creative design and direction, Mr. Salsberry has had the pleasure and good fortune to work with many industry innovators and professionals. From his initial work with ground-breaking multi-media production companies such as Camfel Production and Motivational Media Assemblies to his more recent work in the ‘Custom Install’ (Electronics and Home Theater) and iPod accessory industries, he has been able to use his talents to help start and develop companies and products that have won awards and accolades from industry organizations, print publications and his peers.

In 2005, after helping to launch a company that designed and manufactured high-end home theater speakers, and was described as the ‘Apple of Audio’ Mr. Salsberry was asked to join another project closely related to and dealing with Apple. Called mStation Audio, this new company set out to design and manufacture iPod speakers and accessories. Tasked with the creative direction and branding of mStation, Mr. Salsberry was also an integral part of its Marketing and Public Relations. All of this led to his recruitment of NBA All-Star Carmelo Anthony as both an investor and endorser of mStation products. Still in its infancy, mStation Audio has already exceeded its first year sales goal of $1,000,000 USD and garnered much attention and interest for its marketing, style and design.
