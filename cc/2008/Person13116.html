Jorge Parra Photography’s Summary

Advertising and Editorial Photographer with over 20 years of experience , dealing with the largest Agencies and accounts in my country, mostly focused towards Lifestyle Photography, Health/Beauty work and Travel and Tourism, along with a long list of Direct Clients from the Fashion Industry, and the Beauty/Cosmetic/Hair Industry, including Avon, Loreal, Triumph Lingerie, Christian Dior Swimmwear, etc.

I have relocated to Miami, and have begun operations here, working for local Advertising Agencies, Direct Clients and magazines. I have also participated in 6 photo exhibitions in galleries in the Miami Design District, Miami Art District and Coral Gables, The Miami Children Museum, Ft Lauderdale Arts Museum and the Leonard Tachmes Gallery, displaying Fine Art and Fashion photography from my personal projects.

Please check my websites for additional info and don't miss my Photo-Blog for more about me and my personal approach to vision, photography, art and commerce, in the link:

http://purevisualenergy.blogspot.com/

Cheers!!
