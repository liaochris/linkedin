Robert Redding’s Summary

I am a nonpartisan nonvoting independent known as "America's Independent Voice." I have more than a decade of experience in the media - radio, print and television. I look to make Redding News Review and its program, of the same name, the top destination for news. I am also continuing to develop my new D.C.-based newspaper The Washington Continent.

Robert Redding’s Specialties:

Black News and events. No one knows this area better than my company. I am also well versed in independent politics and abstract art.

Robert Redding’s Experience

  • Owner

    RCI

    (Public Company; 1-10 employees; Writing and Editing industry)

    1996 — Present (12 years)

    Redding Communications, Inc. (RCI), is a domestic profit Georgia corporation with operations in Washington D.C. RCI was founded May 5, 1996 by CEO Robert "Rob" Redding Jr. to provide the best in media - including but not limited to independent publishing and broadcasting.

    RCI is the operator of ReddingNewsReview.com, founded by Redding on March 25, 2002. The independent nonpartisan hub for breaking news about blacks and beyond averages more than 190,000 page views a month and more than 500,000 readers yearly. The Web site's growth is due in part to its news being syndicated by Google News, and its exclusives landing in the national media.

    The company also operates The Washington Continent, a general interest continuously updated Web site covering politics in and around Washington, D.C. from a nonpartisan Independent perspective, and the growing NewsBlack.com. NewsBlack.com hosts the Redding News Review's weekly podcast downloaded by hundreds.
