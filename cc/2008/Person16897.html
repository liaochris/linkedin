Matt Balara’s Summary

I "designed" my first web page in 1993, which is hard to believe, even for me.

Since 2001 I've worked as an Art Director responsible for client consultation, launch and relaunch design and concept development for web sites, mobile and "out of the browser" internet apps for clients in the automotive, retail and tourism industries. I'm also proud to lead a team of happy designers.

I hope to make a few corners of the internet not only easier to use, but just as importantly, more fun to use.

Matt Balara’s Specialties:

Creation of functional and enjoyable designs, presentation and explanation of complex ideas, development of interface concepts, and interface simplification.

Fluent in written & spoken English and German.
