Jonathan Vanasco’s Summary

I am an energetic professional with intimate knowledge of internet/traditional media & extensive experience with consumer trends and new media positioning/development.

I have a strong technical knowledge spanning multiple languages, methods, and protocols; coupled with a multi-disciplinary background in the marketing, media and business sectors, I am able to develop more efficient and strategic approaches to problem solving than conventional means.

I accurately predict technologies and systems of communication before markets develop. I've authored multiple patent applications on Social Media Technologies that address the concerns of consumers and industry ( marketers, advertisers, networks and publishers)

Since 2005 I have been running startups at the intersection of Marketing and Technology. Long before people saw the value of marketing data in social networks, or perceived a desire for portability, I maintained the market would follow that path, and started companies to capitalize on both needs.

• Founded online startups focused on management, aggregation and syndication of published data and internet identities to empower users and originators
• Founded startup to optimize online advertising and conventional media buys through deeper understanding of consumer patterns across online properties
• Developed key patent-pending technologies in social networking, online identity, and online adverting arenas
• Worked with leading IP firms to secure patents and devise strategic protection/litigation strategies
• Worked with managers of online Social Networks to better address technological needs, retention/abandonment concerns and optimize revenue streams
• Consulted top media brands, political & non-profit campaigns, and celebrities with online strategies - emphasis on Social Media
• Consulted companies on IP development and protection strategies
• Consulted companies on product development: technology, infrastructure, revenue stream generation
