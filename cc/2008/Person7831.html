Fergus Sharpe’s Summary

Giving You "the Edge" in Toronto Real Estate. "The Edge". To him, it's a state of mind, an aura of independence and individuality. It's about boundless energy and limitless opportunity.

A Sharpe Eye: "I've felt that energy my entire life", says Fergus. A native of Ireland, Fergus grew up instilled with the philosophy that you have to seize every opportunity and nobody can hold you back from your dreams.

His love of travel took him all over the world. After meeting his wife, a Canadian in Australia, Fergus chose Canada as his home. When you meet him today, you quickly notice the twinkle in his eye and that sense of energy and adventure.

With over 15 years of successful sales experience under his belt, there is no doubting Fergus' strong work ethic and devotion to his career. "I see real estate as a career with no limits," says Fergus. "Ever since I first helped my Dad as as young boy build an extension on the family home, I was hooked! My philosophy is that you achieve a goal and then move on to something bigger. I'm a very driven person and thrive when given a challenge."

Fergus' "edge" produces winning results for home buyers and sellers alike. It's what inspires him to go the extra mile on behalf of every client her serves. It's a competitive advantage you can always count on to help you make informed decisions.

"Real Estate can be a lot like travelling," Fergus says. "There are so many different paths to take. If you know what you are doing, you can go anywhere you want."

Fergus' approach to real estate is straightforward. He listens to your needs and puts his determined personality to work for you. By focusing on your needs and goals, he does whatever it takes to make your home purchase and/or sale as successful as possible.

When the time comes for your next move, don't settle for the ordinary. Give yourself "The Edge." Count on Fergus Sharpe for all your real estate needs.
