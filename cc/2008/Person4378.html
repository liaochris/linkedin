Chris Munro’s Summary

25 years in company, general management and transformation leadership in United Kingdom, Western Europe, North America and the Asia pacific region.

Deep expertise in reshaping private, public and private equity owned businesses from financial and strategic perspective while developing high performing teams to exceed expectations of stakeholders.Recent focus has been with service, industrial and logistics companies.

Current role as CEO for $500m private equity roll up in intermodal logistics with Roadlink - combining the role with Operating Partner of Logistics Practice with Fenway Partners.Latter role led a rapid transformation of a $1.2bn Australian based family services company to become a leading player in the Asia Pacific region with people, strategy and operational changes. Prior to that completed transformation and turnaround of public company through chapter 11, refinancing and two acquisitions resulting in privately held company sold to private equity group.
