Karen Kersten’s Summary

I am a Technical Recruiter seeking candidates who strive to be the best in their field. I am presently working on the Staffing team at BAE Systems and am responsible for contract and direct hire staffing within this global organization. I currently recruit for professionals in a variety of technical positions within the defense industry, including, but not limited to: Engineering and IT, including Mechanical, Electical, Software, Hardware, Test, and Systems Engineers; EVMS, Engineering, and Systems Analysts; Pro-Engineer Designers / Drafters / Checkers; Systems Administrators; Technical Trainers; Technical Writers; and much more. The locations I am focused on are the Silicon Valley (CA) and the Detroit Metro Area (MI). If your background is technical (including engineering or IT), I would be happy to review your credentials. Feel free to contact me if you're looking for a new position.

As for me, I have close to twenty years of experience in corporate and contract staffing environments and have been working on IT and Engineering opportunities for most of my career. I have also managed the human resource function for a number of organizations. Living in Michigan and being a Recruiter has been a tough combination for the past few years. If you are interested in finding a stable company which values its employees and is willing to invest for the long-term, please contact me.
