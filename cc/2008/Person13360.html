Jos Konincks’s Summary

Since 2006 I am active as general business adviser and B2B broker for companies. My goal: With the support of the international group of accredited company advisers assist the management of companies with their question in respect of subjects like: Business plans, Buying and selling of company resources such as buildings and equipment, Cost reduction utilities, Exit route for owners or company transfer, Export and Import, Innovation, Investments and Risk management.
Because of this international cooperation I ensure form and level on which support is provided connect to the specific needs, wishes and objectives of companies.

As background I have 30+ years experience with insurances. I worked as underwriter & insurance broker. My activities were in the field of risk management (RM) & insurances, especially aimed at the dangers related to operational management, material & image damage. I participated in project groups for insurance and prevention related subjects. On request gave insurance & RM presentations to interested parties.

My goal is to be your busines friend/adviser & B2B broker.

For further information I invite you to visit the company website www.k-c-s.eu

Jos Konincks’s Specialties:

Independent analyse, advice, implementation and control of Business, Risk and Insurance Management solutions. Broker in offers and demands in businesses of my clients.
