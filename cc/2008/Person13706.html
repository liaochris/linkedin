Julia-Isabel Davenport, MBA’s Summary

Julia-Isabel Davenport, MBA has worked in Phoenix for fourteen years with businesses and non-profits to effectively tell their story to the media. Maximize Your Publicity, LLC, a six-year old firm, specializes in equipping entreprenuers with the skills they need to effectively tell their story to the media. As the creator of the “Publicity Planner” ™, she inspires attendees to action, offering a multitude of ideas for community outreach projects to help you create goodwill. Recognized by the media as a publicity expert, Davenport shares her skills with audiences who want to save thousands of dollars in their marketing budgets.

She is the author of the “How to Maximize Your Publicity” and creator of the Community Megastar Award, the premiere award for recognizing community outreach by micro-businesses. Davenport also facilitates monthly Business Strategy Lunches™.

Julia-Isabel is a speaker to international associations and published in international trade journals. She has achieved international acclaim from articles published in international trade journals in the funeral, spa, campground, equipment rental and meeting industries.

To help her clients achieve their best, she published "Biblical Affirmations for a Successful Life", a compilation of verses from Genesis to Revelations proclaiming God's promises and blessings in affirmation format. An additional tool is "Creating a Blueprint for Success" outlining 9 steps to achieve success in all areas of your life.

Professionally, Julia-Isabel is a member of IMPACT for Enterprising Women, Toastmasters International, Meeting Professionals International, National Speakers Association and is involved with numerous civic organizations. She has been recognized with awards from AZ Sunbelt Chapter of Meeting Professionals International and IMPACT for Enterprising Women.

Learn how to unlock your PR potential by visiting www.MaximizeYourPublicity.com.
