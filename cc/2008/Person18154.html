Melvin Patterson’s Summary

Fifteen years as a litigator specializing in employment law matters, including wrongful termination, discrimination, harassment, wage & hour, breach of contract and trade secret issues. Experienced arbitrator.

Counseled and advised businesses and employers, designed and provided training programs to comply with state and federal laws, assisted employers in designing and implementing HR policies and procedures to meet their individual needs. Emphasized litigation avoidance.
