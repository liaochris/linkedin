Joel Hickenbottom’s Summary

At Engage, we believe in engaging life through community, compassion and creativity. It is our hope that our simple ideas, whether they be a website, writing a book or creating a storefront, can all be incubators of these three symbols.

Community
Engage believes in community that is fluid, always changing, yet at the same time intrinsically together, sharing in friendship, in interests and in resources.

Compassion
Engage believes in compassion that can transform and pave new ways of living. Caring for the people, places and things around us, man, creature, earth and sky should all benefit from the work we do.

Creativity
We at Engage believe that there is innate creativity built into all of us. Whether you are a painter with a love of hues or an awith a love of numbers, creativity exists and thrives all around us.
