Ryan Johnston’s Summary

I have over ten years experience in the online space - developing outstanding online brand experiences, search engine optimization and providing business analysis. This experience encompasses all aspects of online services including code development, search engine optimization, search engine marketing, business requirements, data-flow diagrams, needs assessment, task analysis, stakeholder interviews and client presentations.

I Recently presented a panel at Search Engine Strategies New York, 2007 about search engine optimization with Web 2.0 technologies including AJAX technology, specifically on Rolex (ROLEX.COM) and Mercedes-Benz USA (MBUSA.COM).

I am currently seeking contract assignments that allow me to provide my expertise to small, mid-sized, and Fortune 500 companies.

I am also available to present and speak at conferences, functions and private events.
