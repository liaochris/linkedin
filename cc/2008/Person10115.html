Jack G. Conrad’s Summary

I currently work with the Research & Development group within Thomson Reuters. I focus on a broad range of technical application areas involving artificial intelligence and textual data processing. I also foster cross-group collaboration and communication in the process of implementing and deploying technology to meet business needs.

Previously, I was employed as a research assistant for Prof. Bruce Croft at the University of Massachusetts-Amherst. In former lives, I served as a foreign instructor at the East China University of Science & Technology in Shanghai. I also taught high school-level math and science for a number of years in Southern Africa (Swaziland) and West Africa (Togo) with the U.S. Peace Corps.

Jack G. Conrad’s Specialties:

Research areas of particular interest include information retrieval, data clustering, machine learning, sentiment analysis, and system evaluation.
