Rommel C. Caibal’s Summary

Running my own freelance business has allowed me to gain experiential assets in designing and creating for offline and online advertisements, building corporate identities and brands. I’ve designed, built and developed complete websites, rich media banners, corporate identities, icons, and more.

I have over 10 years of print and web design experience. I started as a freelance graphic designer focusing on branding, print collaterals and web development. Working with small- to medium-sized businesses, it’s a must to work tight deadlines, be organized and remain focused.

Being on the web and working with clients’ budgets, it’s imperative to know how to research and often utilize open-source software to bring projects to a successful completion.

I live and breathe the design industry. Please visit my website at http://rccgd.com and my list of work at http://claimid.com/rccgd for more details.
