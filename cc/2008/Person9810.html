Nico Westerdale’s Summary

Iconico, Inc. provides high quality software for design and development to a wide range of professional computer users. Our software ranges from small tools to full applications, and we design our software to be intuitively simple to use. Nico Westerdale is the President of Iconico, Inc. and runs the central office in New York City.
