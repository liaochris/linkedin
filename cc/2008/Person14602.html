Ken Van Wyk’s Summary

I have been a career IT Security technologist for over 20 years, specializing in the niche operational fields of Incident Response and Software Security. I am a published author of two popular O'Reilly books on the above topics, a monthly columnist for eSecurityPlanet (http://www.eSecurityPlanet.com), and have considerable experience in delivering training as well as public speaking engagements. Lastly, I have extensive background in the academic, military, and commercial sectors. References available upon request.
