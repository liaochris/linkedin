Rob Greenlee’s Summary

Digital media management, online marketing and business development professional with a proven record of success at all levels of business. My skills combine a comprehensive range of creative online and offline marketing, mobile content development, advertising, media development, business development, broadcast content creation, project management, public relations and website development capabilities.

Digital Content Management:

Directed editorial content management of over 8,500 RSS-based audio and video program sources and managed the production of short-form mobile device audio programs. These audio and video programs included RSS-based media file enclosure feeds from NPR, MSNBC, New York Times, Mercury News and CNN for the Melodeo Mobilcast mobile phone podcast listening platform. I also developed and manage content partnership relationships with NPR, MSNBC, InfoWorld and many other podcast content providers.

Marketing Management:

Have planned, built, and managed comprehensive, creative and integrated marketing campaigns using all forms of media (web, print, billboard, TV, radio, trade shows, mobile and Internet radio). I have worked with many large PR and advertising agencies to develop campaigns. Established and managed marketing budgets up to $2.6 million. Have marketing experience in mobile, national brand foods, photo gifts, Internet access and high-tech shipping services. Set sales goals and objectives, determined timelines and tactical deliverables, ensured cost-control, and maintained budget discipline.

Content Producer:

Architected and administrated a network of colocated windows web servers and streaming media servers. I have founded, produced and hosted an 7 year-old nationally syndicated broadcast, webcast, satellite and podcast radio show called “WebTalk Radio”. The first broadcast radio show in the world to start podcasting.
