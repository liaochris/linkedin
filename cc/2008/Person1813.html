Ann Stanley’s Summary

We offer 3 main services:
1) web development using our unique Re-Flex WCMS (website content management system) that allows you to change elements of the design as well as the content. We also offer a range of database modules including property/product database, search functionality, ecommerce, customer registration, profiling and CRM, emailing
2) online marketing services, including search engine optimisation (SEO), pay per click (PPC), auditing, and others as required
3) online marketing training particularly hands-on training on PPC and SEO.

Customers
We offer our web and marketing services directly to clients and provide training to EMDA, Business Link and other organisations. We also specialise in helping other agencies by providing training, consultancy or sub-contracting their web development or online management to us.

Website for control freaks!
Our websites can be completely controlled by you! Not only can you add or modify the page content, tags and menus (CMS) - if required you can change the layout, create your own registration forms & format the way you manage your data.
