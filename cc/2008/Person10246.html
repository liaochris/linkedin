James Endicott’s Summary

James Endicott currently serves as President and Chief Executive Officer of Exact Wave, a leading provider of on-demand Postal Data and List Management solutions. As a founder of Exact Wave, James has successfully directed the company to over 500% growth in the first 5 years. James is also founder and Chairman of Gruvie, an on demand web based community software company. Prior to Exact Wave and Gruvie, James worked as a strategic Internet marketing consultant for companies like Natures Sunshine, Intel, Qualcomm, and Knight Transportation.
James is a serial entrepreneur with a passion for building companies. He has built numerous startups and understands the process of growing a successful organization. Previously, James founded and served as CEO of Millennium Quest, A software consulting company which was acquired in 1999.
