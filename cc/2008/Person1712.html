Alexis Mueller’s Summary

Alexis Mueller has been at Cowan, DeBaets, Abrahams & Sheppard since January 2005 and has been practicing primarily in the areas of digital media law, internet and technology law, transactional entertainment law, and intellectual property law with a focus on the licensing and prosecution of trademarks. In addition to his transactional work, he frequently assists litigation attorneys in intellectual property and general commercial cases in federal and state courts. He has extensive experience with computer programming and networking technologies.
