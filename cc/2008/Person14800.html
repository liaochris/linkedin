Kim Freedman’s Summary

I am a leadership and executive coach. I work primarily with mid-level and senior level leaders who want to accelerate their learning and to achieve greater success. I specialize in working with technology organizations in identifying and developing leaders. I also teach professionals how to communicate more effectively with clients, staff, and management and to achieve business and personal goals in less time.

Kim Freedman’s Specialties:

Coaching and training in time management, project management, interpersonal communication, leading teams, business communications, and emotional intelligence.
Certified to administer and debrief leadership/career assessments and 360-degree feedback.
