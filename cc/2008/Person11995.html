James Douglas’s Summary

With over eight years experience within the STM & Online Publishing Industry, working on pan European bases, I have gained extensive knowledge of the publishing & information sectors. As a consultant I work with some of the biggest names within the STM sector. I have been involved in sourcing many strategic positions throughout Europe, finding roles for some of the key people within the industry. Due to this success I’m regarded as a preferred supplier to a number of top global publishing houses.

Today, the competitive battle for global talent is at its fiercest. The success of businesses in ensuring that they can identify, attract and retain the best available minds has never been more crucial to corporate prosperity.

As specialist recruiters with established backgrounds within your industry, KSDR Search actively and constantly engage with the most influential professionals in the market. Our knowledge allows us to understand your business strategy, the talent needs that will support the delivery of that strategy, and the recruitment solution that is most appropriate. This means that we can identify and attract the very best talent, and present a compelling picture to them of the challenges, opportunities and possibilities that exist within your business.

Our methodology ensures a discreet, transparent process for both candidate and client throughout the whole recruitment phase. 80% of our business is from long standing clients that rely on us to provide excellence in process and outcome, and we complete over 97% of our retained assignments.

We go to extraordinary lengths to find the best people. We face to face interview anywhere in the world. This enables us to ensure the candidates we provide are not just able to deliver great results, but also share the same values and aspirations of their potential employer.
