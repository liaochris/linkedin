Aaron Riddle’s Summary

8+ years experienced art director, graphic / Web designer and illustrator. Highly skilled in concept development, communication through visual image (print and Web), visual branding, job production, coordination, pre-press preparation and Web site planning, design and implementation.

Aaron Riddle’s Specialties:

Extensive knowledge of design theory in print and Web. Advanced knowledge of print production including: pre-press preparation, ink and paper stock selection, direct mail rules and regulations, printing and mailing specifications. Development of marketing campaigns for various organizations utilizing both print and Web media communication methods. Designing front and back end concepts for small to large scale Web sites, and working with Web developers to implement them correctly.
