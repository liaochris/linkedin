Julie Walton Shaver’s Summary

That photography class I took in college changed my life. Took 20 years for me to figure that out, and good thing it did! Those 20 years as a graphics professional while raising my two boys have led to me a photography career that uses my artistic photography and design skills to celebrate the families I photograph. My goal is to encourage my clients and friends to replace store-bought art in their homes with displays that celebrate the truly important people -- the people they love.

One catch though: I don't pose people, except to encourage some fashion modeling. My signature style is the "unportrait."
