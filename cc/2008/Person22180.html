Rory Jaffe’s Summary

Accomplished senior executive with over 15 years experience in a broad range of academic health care leadership positions. Expertise includes medical group management, health system strategic planning, financial planning, contracting strategy, compliance, health information privacy and security, quality of care measurement and improvement, licensure and accreditation, risk management, provision of clinical services and governmental relations. Servant-leader, coalition builder, dynamic speaker.
