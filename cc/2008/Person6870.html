Duncan Bolam’s Summary

Duncan specialises in coaching his clients to fulfil their potential whether in Career Coaching, Executive Coaching or Workforce Alignment. He is also a skilled sports performance coach specialising in swimming, golf and triathlon. He has an innate ability to see the restriction and free up the natural ability within everyone.

Anyone who knows Duncan knows he is extremely passionate about the benefits of people finding their true sense of vocation. In 2006 he was invited to talk on the USA’s Martha Stewart Living Radio coast-to-coast phone-in show on his favourite topic Career Sustainability. The payback to employees and employers alike are limitless: happier workers, less absenteeism, greater productivity, value growth, profitability, collective intention, respect for self & others, sustainability, more joy all around!

Duncan's had a variety of management experiences in the leisure, retail and hospitality industries in such diverse roles as managing the UK’s largest squash and fitness club, initiating a Ryder Cup bid for a 5 star golf resort, liaising with the EU Tour and the BBC; launching Lacoste's first concession in London, work as Professional Golfer and being invalided out of the Royal Marines.

In Summer '07 Duncan swam the English Channel in a 6 man team in 12.53hrs; work permitting he’ll go solo 2009! Sport has always been important; he formerly competed at county level in Golf, Basketball, Shot, Discus and Rugby. He swam 100 & 1500m Freestyle in the 1984 National Student Championships and competed in the 2004 London Triathlon.

Duncan is heavily involved in raising the profile of the career profession, including membership of the ICG’s CPD committee, acting as Marketing & PR Director of the ACP International’s UK Board and Chairing FedPAG 2004-06. Duncan is also proud to have participated in the International Life / Work Planning Workshop 2000 with world famous author of ‘What Color Is your Parachute?’, Dick Bolles.
