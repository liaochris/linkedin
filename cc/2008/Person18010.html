David Crichton’s Summary

Mild Frontiers is an entertainment and media business with projects aiming at the exponentially developing realm of digital cinema, and contemporary methods of mobile and online distribution of creative, global, intelligent,quality products.

Also available for commissioning of
Original Soundtrack Compositions, Audio Resources and acquisition of Video Assets for projects.

Over 20 years of professional experience in arts, creative, entertainment and media industries, including performances in stadia around Europe, promotion of pop concerts, production of recordings, including oscar winner, Melissa Etheridge for Radio Clyde, and visual production for ClydeCableVision, and other freelance projects.

Current developments for Mild Frontiers sees the creation of " The Saint Enoch Christmas Market" digital film. A creative admiration of the happenings in this popular Glasgow city centre market in the lead up to Christmas 2006.

Mild Frontiers also has over 7 hours of Original Soundtrack Compositions and Audio Resources in prototype form, and awaiting deployment in productions.

Also in development is a network of international co-producers looking to collaborate on entertainment and media productions, covering sensitive documentary to comedy feature films. A call for collaborators has already found in the region of 170 production personnel, with talents in the majority of areas required for the creation of quality product. Other personnel can be brought on board as required.

Particular attention is due to digital distribution of audio visual product, especially the transition of the cinema exhibition industry to the more economical and dynamic digital transmission.
