Magnus Klausen Revang’s Summary

User Experience and User Centered Development advocate. To find the right balance between the different aspects of the User Experience and to raise awareness of usability and desirability issues within my project teams. As a consultant I always strive to be seen as an investment and make my customers winners.

My experience spans many fields of the development process. I have at times been Project Manager, Usability Specialist, Programmer, Interaction Designer and Implementor. This has given me the knowledge and experience to communicate with diverse groups, from graphic designers to programmers to marketing, at their terms and make them work toward a common vision for a project.

SELECTED CASES:
- Key Account Manager, User Experience Consulting and Information Architecture for Navico and the launch of brand sites for Simrad, B&G, Lowrance, Eagle and Northstar
- Interaction Design and Implementation for Avinor
- User Experience consulting for Malmö Aviation
- User Experience consulting for Zett.no
- Clientside and serverside development of online ticket purchasing for SASBraathens in the period 1999-2005.
- Game concept development, Global Countdown, Telenor ASA
- Game concept development, Kampen om Kvotene, Norveg Kystmuseet

IMPORTANT ACHIVEMENTS:
- Navico launched 5 websites on EPiServer Enterprise platform in 4 months in the summer of 2007 with a huge boost in traffic
- ELSPA included Global Countdown as one of three international success cases in their report "Unlimited Learning - Computer and Video Games in the Learning Landscape" in 2006
- Being part of a small team that brought ticket ordering of airline tickets on SASBraathens from a low percentage to becoming their primary sales channel from 1999 to 2005
- Creating animation om mobile phones using WAP 1.0 browsers when nobody thought it was possible in 2000
- Being the first Presentation Programmer employed by the then leading Internet developer in Norway, New Media Science in 1998
