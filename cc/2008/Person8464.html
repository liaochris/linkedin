Gary Aminoff’s Summary

Real estate investment advisory services, real estate syndication (group investment), real estate development, commercial real estate brokerage, 1031exchange expertise. We have been in business since 1975.

We specialize in providing secure single-tenant long-term net-leased properties to sellers of property who need an exchange property to complete a 1031 exchange.

We also represent buyers who are seeking commercial real estate investments, and sellers who are interested in finding buyers for their property. We cover the entire country through our national brokerage network.

We will be happy to discuss your real estate acquisition, disposition or management needs.
