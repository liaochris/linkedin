Chris Paton’s Summary

I divide my time between lecturing in Health Informatics at the University of Otago and running New Media Medicine Ltd, a medical e-learning development company based in the Centre for Innovation at the University of Otago.

New Media Medicine creates interactive e-Learning courses for the Healthcare industry. We specialise in:

- Online Communities (we operate an online medical community with 35,000 members)
- 3D Animation
- Flash Animation
- Online Learning Content Mangement Systems
- Web Design and Database Programming

Chris Paton’s Specialties:

Health Informatics - Electronic Health Records, Mobile Computing (PDAs, Tablets, MCAs), Healthcare e-Learning

Web Development - Web 2.0, Virtual Communities, e-Learning, Flash Development
