Domenico Tassone’s Summary

Overview: A high-energy Internet marketing professional with a broad and deep online media background; have worked to accelerate business primarily for and using online media but also with Internet ad technology companies; offer a combination of hands-on skills with strategic marketing, operational know-how and financial insight.

• Co-founded one of the earliest Web agencies, Streams Online Media Development in 1994; Streams was a digital-focused boutique providing strategy, creative, media and in-house Web software development; we pioneered analytics with our Lilypad application.

• Innovating in the online industry since the 1993 launch of the seminal, Planet StarChild, a multimedia BBS that allowed fans to "try before they buy."

• Technologies: ad serving, behavioral/CRM targeting, content personalization; video(ITV/IPTV/VOD), cable TV, streaming/Webcasting, podcasting/RSS, wireless/mobile.
