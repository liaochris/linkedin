Ron RAYMOND’s Summary

PST Handicapper Ron Raymond, 39, is a 4-time handicapping champion and one of the industry most recognizable leaders in the Sports Handicapping business. During the past 11 years, Ron has established 10 sports gaming related web sites which includes the development of a sports betting database (ATSDatabase.com), a sports betting statistical calculator (ATSCalculator.com) and a score result web site (Livescoreboard.com).
Ron Raymond is a strong believer in creating his own numbers versus the bookmakers and is the creator of the "ATS Ratings" system which hit over 60% in the NHL last season. Ron's formula takes 8 different scoring situations from both the offense and defense and produces a line on the side and total, which Ron then compares to the opening line.

Ron Raymond has quickly established himself as one of the sharpest handicappers in the business and has captured 4 handicapping championships with over $30,000 dollars in contest winnings! As you can see by Ron's winning accomplishments below and his aggressive nature in building strong gaming networks, his picks will bring you to the winners circle more often than not and his picks and opinions on big games are much sought after by the media.
