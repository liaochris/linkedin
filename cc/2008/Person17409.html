Melissa Goetsch’s Summary

I have worked in the medical field for 8 years now. I have been a phlebotomist for 5 of those years. Before becoming a phlebotomist I was a lab assistant and worked my way up to be a lab assistant 2. I enjoy working in the medical field and am currently employed for Labcorp.

I opened The Recycle Bin in 2005 and have been dedicated to recycling used electronics ever since. If you have any old electronics that you can no longer use just let me know and I will refurbish it and either give it or sell it for a very low price to someone who can't afford a new computer. We also take other items such as scrap metal and scrap cars and will travel for bulk jobs.

I finished my associates degree in Information Technology from Western International University in 2006. I am currently enrolled in a bachelors program in Business Management with a dual minor in Small Business Development, and Marketing with WIU.

I would eventually like to work with companies such as Labcorp and others in their marketing and PR departments. I enjoy taking small businesses and promoting their work and products online and in the community. Being a small business owner myself I love to watch businesses grow and want to be able to help others to achieve their goals.
