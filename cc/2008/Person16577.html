Mark Kerrigan’s Summary

I have written web-page copy for a couple of local businesses.

I have written numerous press releases for businesses which have either moved into the area or are launching a new website.

Became involved with Link to Nashville in April of 2007, and was appointed to the position of Administrator by founder Jay Deragon. Since that time, I've been writing a bi-monthly newsletter for Link to Nashville, while posting to the site and also to Link to New York, Link to Chicago, Link to Charlotte and others. During the summer of '07, membership of Link to Nashville has increased by 40%.

In September of 2007, On the Mark Writing began writing the monthly newsletter for Link to New York.

Professional Goals:

Link to Nashville will become the predominant networking site for middle Tennessee

Link to Nashville will become the most comprehensive site on the 'net for visitors and new residents of the area to go for information about where to find the best doctor, what nightclubs are most fun, etc.

On the Mark Writing will continue to aspire to be the best "freelance" writing service in the country, always learning more about the most effective means of communications.

On the Mark Writing will write newsletters and press releases for businesses all over the US.

On the Mark Writing strives to become the Communications Factors expert.

Mark Kerrigan’s Specialties:

Personal Branding, Communications Factors, merging Relationship Economy, editing, writing, Relationship Capital
