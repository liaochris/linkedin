Philip Calvert’s Summary

I'm a professional speaker on sales and online communication skills. I speak at conferences and seminars on how businesses can boost sales results through improved face-to-face and online communication skills.

I help companies and sales people to use seminars and social networking sites like Ecademy, LinkedIn, Facebook and others to win more business through more effective communications to groups and online communities.

I'm also the author of 'Successful Seminar Selling', an acclaimed marketing book which helps businesses to communicate and sell through seminars. See www.seminarsellingexpert.com.

I can add further value by:

Providing a high-energy opening and close
Acting as MC to reinforce your event theme
Moderating panel discussions and managing audience questions
Delivering short, motivational introductions to breakout sessions
Coaching executives and delegates in professional networking techniques
Summing up conference speeches and closing the event powerfully
