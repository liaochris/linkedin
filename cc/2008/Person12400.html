Johan De Keulenaer’s Summary

Joined These Days after having spent a lot of years in a number of different consultancy and management positions for projects in interactive marketing, e-commerce, database marketing and ict. Working area focused in the Benelux, with occasional travel in Europe, UK and US.

At These Days Johan is managing a team of CRM professionals while offering his own profound knowledge to key clients as a senior consultant. Addtionally he manages the ICT department and related platforms for delivering digital marketing solutions.

Over his career Johan has worked on international clients such as: Pioneer, ThomasCook, Bikkembergs, CenterParcs, Citibank, Fortis Bank, Frisk, GlaxoWellcome, Ijsboerke, Nokia, Orange, Danone, Nespresso, P&G, Robert Half International and Sony.
