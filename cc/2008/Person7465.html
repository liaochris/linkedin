Erik McLaughlin’s Summary

Erik McLaughlin is one of the country's leading experts on the Wine and Restaurant industry. Erik has worked in all tiers of the wine industry and is one of the few wine professionals with the unique expertise to manage projects through all stages of the life cycle.

Erik's career started working as a cellar rat for Oregon wineries while he earned his undergraduate degree. Since then, he has worked in wineries, retail, restaurants, and distribution. Erik has served as Corporate Wine Buyer for one of the largest retailers in the country and Vice President/Partner for the largest wine Brokerage Firm in the mountain west. Currently, he is the General Manager for Hamley and Co., a multi faceted Steak House and special events company on the edge of the Walla Walla appelation.

Erik conducts speaking and educational engagements for industry, public, and Universities and has written on wine and business for numerous publications.

Erik is based on the Walla Walla area.

Erik McLaughlin’s Experience

  • General Manager

    Hamley & Co.

    (Restaurants industry)

    August 2008 — Present (3 months)

    Hamley & Co. is the premiere brand of the American West. Founded in 1883 as a saddle company, Hamley is still the oldest saddle maker in the West and considered one of the finest saddle makers in the world.

    In 1905, the Hamley brothers opened their now famous Western Store in the wild west town of Pendleton, OR where it is still based.

    Fast forward to 2007. Hamley & Co. made a dramatic expansion with the opening on Hamley SteakHouse, Hamley Coffee and Wine, and Hamley Custom Events next door to the Western Store. This combined operation offers one of the finest Steak House experiences in the West, a comfortable Coffee and Wine Bar, and Custom Events in a choice of four private dining venues.

    In addition to carrying products from some of the finest ranches, farms, brands, and designers, Hamley & Co. offers custom saddles, leatherwork, silverwork, western apparel, arabica coffee, and ultra-premium wines under the Hamley name.

  • President

    Veraison Consulting, LLC

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Wine and Spirits industry)

    2001 — Present (7 years)

    Veraison Consulting LLC is a diversified consulting company with local, national, and international clients ranging from vineyards, wineries, property developers, retailers, distributors, importers, exporters, and restaurants.

    Services include vineyard site analysis, vineyard and winery feasibility, business plan development/analysis, organizational structure, marketing, brand creation and development, graphic and label design, merchandising, floor/fixture/equipment/lighting planning, product assortment, real estate analysis, commercial development, legal compliance and license acquisition, supply and distribution chain development.

    "Veraison" (pronounced veh-ray-zohN) is a French viticultural term for the stage of wine grape berry development which marks the beginning of the ripening and sugar development, when the grapes change from the hard, green state to their softened and coloured form.
