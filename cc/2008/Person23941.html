Sanjay Singhal’s Summary

Founder & President of a Global Telecom Solutions provider using technologies such as Satellite, Microwave/FSO, Fiber, Wireless- Wi-Fi/Wi-Max, RFID and integration with customized software application & platform, ICT solutions for last 22+ years. Offices & field representation in many countries worldwide including Africa, Middle East, Europe & Asia. www.sintelsat.com

Extensive international network and partnerships in more than 50 countries, two decades experience in business development, technology solutions provision, critical problem solving, negotiations, strategy, high end consulting. Advisor to several US domestic and off-shore telecom services, software and IT support businesses. Relationships extend from large multi-billion dollar organizations to SME with unique technology solutions. Working on projects in Africa with humanitarian and social uplifting goals, providing employment to youths which have far reaching economic impacts in the region.
