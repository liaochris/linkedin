Padma Nagappan’s Summary

San Diego based freelance business writer who contributes business features to newspapers, magazines, online media, industry web sites and blogs.

In addition, I work with long term corporate clients, creating targeted marketing literature that produces results; crafting corporate profiles and press releases; and suggesting strategies to enhance their marketing and PR efforts and harness their sales tools.
