Joelle Reeder’s Summary

Joelle Reeder and Kathy Scoleri are the design duo behind popular web design firm Moxie Design Studios.

The Moxie Girls, Joelle Reeder and Kathy Scoleri, are authors of "The IT Girl's Guide to Blogging with Moxie" (Wiley, 2007) and have spoken at events such as SXSW Interactive.

Look for The Moxie Girls Podcast and the return of the popular PutDowntheDonut.com in early 2008!

For media inquiries or speaking engagements, please contact CJ Bergthorsen at cj@typeapr.com or visit http://themoxiegirls.com.
