Alessandro Nadalin’s Summary

Building Websites is a great experience : imagine that you cannot have limits, so, this is what I mean when I put myself in front of the monitor.

Interacting with PHP and SQL I can write lots of applications which fit perfectly for every customer, or develop some kind of Joomla! extension ( modules, components... ) which allow you to upgrade your Joomla! site, having new functionalities or improving the current ones.

I also create templates for every kind of site, and it's a great experience, because i really like CSS matters : so I put myself in front of the stylesheet and I must do the best I can to realize an high - level template, which, seen after the whole work, appear awesome and easy to customize.

This is a little part of my job; and the greatest thing is that I really enjoy it.
