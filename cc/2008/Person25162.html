Dominique Preyer’s Summary

Dominique Preyer is an experienced music licensing professional with a background in music publishing and songwriting. He has also served as an executive producer and producer on two films.

Dominique's music supervisor credits include feature films "Harmony and Me," "Mars," "Lovely, Still," "I'll Come Running," "Deadly Obsession" and "The Lights." Short film credits include "The Funeral Singer," "The Spin Cycle," "Jo FM" and "Me, Myself & I." He has also music supervised several documentaries such as "Year At Danger," "Light, Bright, Damn Near White" and "Ruby's Town."

In addition to his music supervisor work, Dominique writes a weekly blog on the Film Industry Bloggers site. He is also a moderator on the Supervising Music Yahoo group. Dominique is also slated to appear on Kathryn Raaker's radio talk show, "Let's Just Talk" on 1050 WCVX Saturday, May 31 at 10:00 am.

Dominique Preyer’s Specialties:

Specialties include music clearance and licensing, rights negotiation, copyright law, song searches, music research, copyright ownership research, cue sheets, on-camera sequences, etc.
