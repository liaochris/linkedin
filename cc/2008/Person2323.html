Joseph Bachana’s Summary

DPCI offers a wide array of consulting services to help organizations create, manage and deliver content to multiple media.

The types of projects that DPCI is retained to assist customers with are:

Content Management Strategy -- Corporations rarely have the internal expertise to define a multi-year content strategy and then map that strategy between existing and proposed infrastructures. These companies typically turn to DPCI for help in order to improve their strategic decisionmaking early on.

Digital Asset Management Strategy -- Organizations are concerned that digital asset management, similar to content strategy, isn't just about technology. DPCI staff has the wisdom to help you make the right decisions about people, process, and technology.

Product Selection Support -- While companies often have procurement departments, rarely do those departments have expertise in writing and managing CMS/DAM-specific RFP's. In fact, without product selection support, companies are more likely to make mistakes by neglecting to add important selection criteria. Companies look to DPCI to help write and manage RFP and RFI initiatives.

Portal, CMS, and DAM Implementation Support -- Many companies benefit from DPCI’s premiere support services by using a hybrid of their own staff developers and content engineers alongside our developers, field engineers, trainers and support staff. The result is not only an accelerated and smoother project but also quicker learning times for customer support staff when 'trailing' DPCI experts.

General Project Management and Project Office Outsourcing -- Companies that do not have certified project managers or project office capabilities turn to DPCI to help run mission-critical projects as well as to set up project offices and train internal staff in project management best practices. For companies that do have project managers on staff, DPCI is used to augment the team when project load is overwhelming.

Joseph Bachana’s Specialties:

DPCI is a New York-based professional services firm that delivers integrated technology solutions for organizations that need to publish to multiple media.


DPCI helps customers by integrating multi-channel publishing solutions with Digital Asset Management, Enterprise Content Management, Marketing Resource Management, and other mission-critical applications.

DPCI serves Fortune 1000 customers throughout the United States.
