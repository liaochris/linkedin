Thomas Petty’s Summary

Thomas W. Petty specializes in:

• Search Engine Optimization (SEO)
• Search Engine Marketing (SEM)
• Pay Per Click Advertising (PPC)
• E-mail Marketing Tools and Campaigns
• Client Conversion Tools
• Web Site Analytics

WSI works with Google every day as well as other search engines to help businesses get their web sites promoted on the search engines.

He specializes in helping businesses understand exactly why their site is not showing up on the search engines. He offers a complimentary WebScan® analysis report on clients’ web sites, and helps them understand what is preventing effective placement.

By using specialized tools, WSI provides research and analysis to find key phrases that are generating a lot of searches on the search engines, but that do not have a lot of competition. This helps get the client placed more easily for specific phrases people want.

In addition, WSI provides analytics software tools to help the client understand:

• Current web site traffic
• Traffic sources
• Key phrases that are being searched
• What is and is not being looked at on their web site
• What pages are converting their clients
