Neal Levene’s Summary

I am CEO and founder of InnovaTech, Inc. We help organizations improve decision making through integrating enterprise data and producing actionable information from an organization's operational data. We are a knowledge management and data warehousing specialist company.

As CEO of InnovaTech, I constantly seek new relationships with industry leaders who are struggling with improving the information from which they make decisions. I am also interested in creating partnerships and teaming arrangements with orgnizations with complimentary skill sets.
