Todd Asevedo, SPHR’s Summary

I have devoted my professional life to the HRMS industry specializing in consulting. I have been involved through all phases of HRMS from initial automation of manual processes to more strategic projects dealing with Performance, Comp, and Talent. I have studied the various vendors in the space and understand their strengths and weakness relative to functionality, delivery, sales channels, vision, marketplace, price and total cost of ownership.

After deciding in 1993 to make my job at ADP a career in HRMS I went on to become accredited as a Payroll Professional through the APA and a Human Resources Professional through HRCI. My time is now spent in helping my clients understand the HR, Time and Attendance, Recruiting, and payroll applications for thier needs and within thier budget.
