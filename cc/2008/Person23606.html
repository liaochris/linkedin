Simon Stoltz’s Summary

Generalist in HRM. Worked as an employee for several companies and local governments as (international) personell manager. In 1989 started my own business as an indepandent senior HR consultant, managementtrainer, and interim HR manager. Experienced in Retail, FMCG, Local government, Business Service companies, Financial Service companies (accountancy), and not for profit organizations such as voluntary agencies and museums

I am now available as interim HR manager/ interim HR director for international positions all over Europe as well as The Gulf region.
