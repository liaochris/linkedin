Erik van Luxzenburg’s Summary

Having worked in consulting and accountmanagement positions, I now specialise on sales. After working in the banking industry, I recently switched to an IT company as coordinator (supervisor) of the inbound sales team. This team functions as a central department within the organisation. These tasks are scheduled for 70% of my time.


Besides coordination I recently freed 30% of my time for other tasks. First of all I received an assignments as processmanager for the Sales department. As processmanager I am responsible for all internal processes (workflow management, hardware and software optimalisation).

And secondly I started as junior projectmanager with two projects. First of all a commercial project to create a complete products and service overview. And second to optimalise all internal systemsregistration of products and services.
