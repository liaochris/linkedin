Karl Thompson’s Summary

Creative Director / Senior Designer, designing and producing user interfaces, websites, identities/logos, multimedia, illustrations, print work, presentations, digital photography, signs etc. The complete branding package.

I undertake the branding or re-branding of a companies identity (or just website/software).

This typically involves:
1. Creating a company logo and product logo(s).
2. Creating a corporate style guide to be used any person or company that produces any future work for the company.
3. Creating a new company website - design user interface, build and maintain the site (or advise on a suitable CMS solution to enable the client to update).
4. Design and produce company stationary, business cards, letterheads etc.
5. Design and produce product/services brochures.
6. Produce a template for corporate presentations.
7. Produce any required illustration work.
8. Produce any required displays or signage designs (or advise on signage).
9. Shoot any required digital photography (products, locations etc).
10. Consultancy - maintain relations with the client and train staff (when required) or train-in a replacement "in-house" designer, to complete any ongoing work in the style and quality of my work (using the corp style guide).

Karl Thompson’s Specialties:

Web/User Interface Design, Print Design, Illustration, HTML, CSS, Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Flash, Dreamweaver, InDesign etc. Also some knowledge of JavaScript, ASP, Cold Fusion, PHP etc. Client liaison and consultation. Idea generation. Full creative services solutions. Vast experience and knowledge.
