Kent Ninomiya’s Summary

Seasoned professional writer, journalist, executive and on-air talent with a proven record of thinking on his feet and effectively handling high pressure deadline situations. More than 20 years of on-camera experience communicating to a live audience both with and without a script. Management experience supervising a newsroom staff producing multiple daily newscasts on strict deadlines while simultaneously balancing long term complex projects. Strong motivator helping colleagues develop and implement result oriented strategies for success. Finely tuned ability to take complex situations and quickly break them down into a format easily understood by the general public.
