Michael Walsh’s Summary

What started out as a simple curiosity for the English language ended up with a passion for online communication in all its forms.

As a customer, I am thoroughly sold on the idea of businesses using social networks as part of the way they interact with me.

As a marketer, I am convinced the arrival of networking, blogs and other social media technologies and applications is a clear signal that change is coming and that we have an obligation to embrace it, encourage it and develop it.

If you ask anyone who knows me what I do, they'll say I am the strategic writer for Acer. What they mean is that my skills have outgrown the medium we're all used to, and have started exploring a world very few of them fully comprehend.

Suddenly copywriting just do it for me.

Alongside all of the above I have started to broaden my experience of the power of Web 2.0 by developing one or two web-specific projects of my own. Too early to say whether they'll take off but at least I'll have tried...
