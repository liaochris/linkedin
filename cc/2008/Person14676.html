Kevin Conlon’s Summary

I graduated with a Bachelor's of Arts in Advertising from Michigan State University in May, 2008. During school I studied Account Management and Brand Strategy with some experience in interactive advertising. Currently, I am an intern at Ingenex Digital Marketing in Ann Arbor, MI under the direction of CEO Derek Mehraban. I gained experience in Social Media Marketing, Search Engine Marketing, Mobile Phone Marketing, Interactive Branding, and Consumer Generated Content (CGC). I also worked as a state-wide administrator for the Michigan Coalition for Progress and help manage the Madison Heights campaign office.

In the past, I have worked for ABN-AMRO in Troy, Michigan for two summers as I was an Assistant Credit Analyst in addition to the Customer Service Representatives Traffic Coordinator.

I currently work for Campbell-Ewald in Southfield, Michigan as an account coordinator.
