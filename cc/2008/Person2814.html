Bill Erickson’s Summary

In 2003, I combined my passion for graphic design and business by starting my own web design company. Since then I've been an entrepreneur with a focus on the tech industry.

In 2007, I gathered a group of local entrepreneurs together and we started The Creative Space, a coworking office in Downtown Bryan.

In the future, I'd like to be an Entrepreneur in Residence for a VC firm, or continue starting my own businesses. While I enjoy design, I'm an entrepreneur at heart and love working with startups. I just want to work on something interesting.
