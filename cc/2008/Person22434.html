Salvador da Cunha’s Summary

General Manager at Lift Consuting and Owner/CEO Bairro Alto Group
Specialist in Financial Communications, Private Equity; IPOs, M&A, Media Relations, Crisis Communications and Public Affairs with more than 100 clients in the last 11 years.
