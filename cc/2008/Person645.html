Alana Moceri’s Summary

Non-profit and political organizer with over ten years of experience in building, organizing and motivating networks of activists. A professional and volunteer fundraiser for causes including the Democratic Party, American Heart Association and Arthritis Foundation. Expertise in using online technology and networking tools to enhance grassroots organizations. Strong networking skills and the ability to connect people with similar interests and goals. Dynamic and bi-lingual public speaker and press spokesperson. Effective trainer and coach using participatory teaching methods.

Alana Moceri’s Specialties:

fund-raising, volunteer organization, board management, language training, communication coaching, public speaking coaching, government relations, e-activism
