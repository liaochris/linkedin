Jeff Peterson’s Summary

Jeff Peterson helps people SAVE MONEY IN TAXES.... with standard deferred 1031 exchanges, build-to-suit construction improvement exchanges, reverse exchanges and aircraft personal property exchanges. He really knows 1031s!

Jeff received his B.A. degree from the University of Minnesota and his J.D. degree from the University of Minnesota Law School.

He is a very good public speaker for business and professional organizations on numerous issues related to taxes and 1031 exchanges.

Jeff is a member of the Minnesota State Bar Association and the ABA - Tax Section. His company is one of the top Minnesota Qualified Intermediaries for 1031 Exchanges of Real Estate and Personal Property.
