Giovanni Maria Riccio’s Summary

I have more than 10 years of experience working on Internet-related law issues. My other work activities include providing litigation assistance, counseling, opinions regarding copyright law, data protection and intellectual property. I teach Comparative media law and I have been visiting professor at the University of Grenoble (France) and at the University of Constanta (Romania).
