Brian Bundesen’s Summary

Marketing and sales professional with over 25 years experience and success. Currently a REALTOR and Partner in The Bundesen Team at Real Estate One in Ann Arbor, Michigan.

My Mission: To know God's purpose for my life, to grow to my highest potential, and to sow seeds which help others.

My Passion: To start and grow businesses, and to contribute to the growth of dynamic organizations. Within real estate, I love to help nice people buy and sell residential or investment properties.

My Joy: The opportunity to help my children get a great education. To watch them grow into great human beings. To watch them have lives full of joy, love, friendship, and fulfillment.

My Goals: To be a great father, son, husband, brother, friend, and business associate. An additional goal is to develop and grow professional relationships through the LinkedIn platform, and to meet as many people as possible in my growing network.

My Experience: More than 25 years experience and success encompassing Marketing, Communications, and Operations of both small and large organizations.

My Search: To network with like-minded, dynamic business professionals. Actively looking for new career and/ or business opportunities, inside and outside of the real estate industry. Also, I continue to look to help people buy and sell real estate in the Ann Arbor, Michigan area.
