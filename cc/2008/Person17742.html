Michael Wright’s Summary

Founder and Player/Manager of a highly regarded IT Recruitment and Executive Search business - delighting customers since our foundation in 2001. Offices in Stockley Park, London, Beachampton in Buckinghamshire and now fully operational in Hong Kong & Chaoyang District - Beijing.

Specialist with a proven record in providing any scale of staffing solution in these areas:

Enterprise Software, 3G and Wireless Technology, Security & Storage, SOA SaaS, Web 2.0 and E-Business, Consumer Electronics and Embedded Systems, IPTV & Digital Broadcast, Digital Media, Business Intelligence, Data Analytics & Visualisation, Datawarehousing, Social Media, 'Impossible to find' skills, CRM, Financial Services, BPO/Offshoring, LINUX and Open Source Software, Grid, High Performance and Distributed Computing, Pervasive and Disruptive Computing, Social Media & Entertainment, Mobile Advertising, Digital Content & Supply Chain, DRM.

Goal: To connect the most talented people in the world with the most exciting businesses in the world. To create competitive advantage and value with every interaction.

Michael Wright’s Specialties:

End to end staffing stragies and execution, offshoring, BPO, recruitment branding, sales strategy, marketing, web branding and identity, salary surveys and benchmarking, CV advice, career counselling, staff retention, competitor analysis, market analysis, social media, executive search and seletion, advertising campaigns and management, PR & analyst relations, technology trends, employment trends.
