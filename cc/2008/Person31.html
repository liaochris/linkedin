Paul Hobcraft’s Summary

I specialize in building innovation capabilities for organizations, teams and individuals so they can relate to the many different aspects needed to be successful. To help them grow their skills and proficiencies in this critical area of innovation understanding, so as to acquire, absorb and apply these so as to provide novel, distinctive and valued outcomes within their environment.

What is innovation capability building made up of?
- Providing leading edge tools and techniques
- Skill development and coaching
- Learning methods to collaborate and work more effectively across diverse teams and different environments.
- Process and methodology application to support structured innovation solutions.
- Cultural aspects that support and promote innovation capability enhancement
- Taking innovation beyond traditional borders to leverage skills, technologies & collaborations.
- Exploring leadership styles and strategy options that align innovation with goals
- Focusing techniques for understanding the different 'needs' of our customers and markets so as to translate these into winning commercial solutions.
- Seeing beyond the known, exploring the world of emerging opportunities through workshops, visioning techniques and gaining foresight.
- Supporting and enhancing the process to generate and manage ideas to fruition.
- Provide a deeper understanding of the innovation development process in a more comprehensive way.
