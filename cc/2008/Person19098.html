Robert L. Noakes III’s Summary

I have been in web programming for over 10 years. I started developing professionally in 1999 when I was hired by Birdsall Designs (now Birdsall Interactive). In the beginning, my job consisted of basic HTML edits and website builds, but my talent was quickly recognized and I began working on projects involving more complicated development, such as dynamic websites and backend tools utilizing ASP and SQL Server. Before parting ways in 2002, I had become a Senior Developer, and worked on some of the company's largest projects.

After Birdsall Interactive, I worked for Pacific Union GMAC Real Estate as their Web Initiatives Manager. I started off as a Marketing Associate in their Danville branch, but quickly found that there was possibility for growth. Before my departure, I was responsible for the maintenance of current tools and websites as well as the development of new ones. The flexibility allowed me to continue expanding my web programming skills.

From Pacific Union, I moved on to 729 Solutions and Telava Networks doing ASP.NET Programming using C#. Combined with SQL Server 2005 and AJAX, I have updated and developed some of the most complex applications I have ever worked with.
