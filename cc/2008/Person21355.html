Rich Gee’s Summary

My mantra: "Energize . . . Empower . . . Motivate . . . Act."

When all is said and done, I'm a Coach. I established my professional reputation as a marketing/advertising/web evangelist in many major corporations over the past 20 years. Now I am business' go-to-guy for advice and guidance.

I also continue to reshape the landscape of companies worldwide. Each year, I assist thousands of people (just like you!) live to their true potential. I present, facillitate, and keynote in front of many Fortune 50 and 500 executives.

As one of the nation’s predominant (and busiest) executive coaches, I'm frequently tapped by The Wall Street Journal, The New York Times, Fortune and CNN for advice on business and career issues.
