Massimo Pettiti’s Summary

Head of new products team, Head of technical development, Product director, Product design and user interface director, R&D director and Innovation & new media director, in I.C.T., Telecommunication and Media industry.

Visiting professor in: Universita' Cattolica-MI, NABA (Nuova Accademia di Belle Arti) -Mi, Salzburg University, Salzburg-A.

18+ years in Telecommunication Industry and 15+ in Mobile and Wireless Industry. I’ ve always had responsibilities into new technology/new services areas, at the intersection between technology, media, marketing and business.

In March 1994 I was part of Omnitel Pronto Italia startup (the first GSM operator to break italian monopoly of the incumbent player).
In January 1999 I was part of Omnitel2000 (now known as “Vodafone live!” brand) Omnitel “spinoff”: the first Service based Company to conceptualize, develop and sell Value Aded Services for a Mobile Operator.

In March 2000 I was part of Andala team (now 3 Italy, H3G) to Bid for 3G frequencies in Italy and create a new UMTS Operator. H3G was the first to create network infrastructures, services and sell 3G handsets in Europe, and the first one to rollout a broadcast national network (DVB-H) to give customers Mobile TV in high quality.
