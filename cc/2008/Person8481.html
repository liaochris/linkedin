Gary James Littlefield’s Summary

Initiated my sound engineering career at 17, London, UK.

Working through the ranks from cassette duplicator to client liaison off. and snr account mastering engineer.
(Masterpiece Mastering, Chop 'em Out and Sanctuary Music Group)

A new millennium, at 25yrs change of career, football (soccer). After completing my UEFA B certificate, under the watchful eye of David Kerslake I had the pleasure of working with Tottenham Hotspurs' community program.

Moved into Academy football. Position, Academy coach and youth development officer with PASS. On completion of my UEFA B Licence I took a coaching role at 'Walthamstow Avenue'. A season later I took over the Managerial reigns with a third place finish in the league and a cup semi final.

30yrs old, Italy. Again in the Football Industry, combining semi professional coaching, player placement, consultancy and scouting for various European organisations.
