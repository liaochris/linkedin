Keith Hopper’s Summary

Product manager, strategist, and emerging technology evangelist, bringing internet-based business solutions to market from conceptualization through leading the design, development, launch and integration.

Contributing author for the book "Collective Intelligence: Creating a Prosperous World at Peace." Published on innovation and business strategy. I am a recipient of awards in design strategy and human engineering and sat on the Board of Directors for AIGA Boston and Advisory boards for two University design programs.

Keith Hopper’s Specialties:

VRM (Vendor Relationship Management), product design & analysis, web application strategy & functional design, online communities & social software, marketing performance measurement, web application development
