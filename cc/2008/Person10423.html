Jari Holland Buck’s Summary

"as much as i try to be an easygoing, stretch your wings and fly type... i just can't stop trying to burst people into flames with my mind."

JARI HOLLAND BUCK is a seasoned "people-focused" consultant who builds partnerships, encourages respect for others, works with openness, develops leadership in everyone, has fun and focuses on results. She previously held key leadership positions with Target Stores (National Training Director), Payless Cashways (National Human Resource and Training Director) and Montgomery Ward spanning 20+ years.

Jari has been recognized in the International Who's Who of Entrepreneurs, Who's Who of American Women, Who’s Who of the United States, Who’s Who in America, Who’s Who in Finance and Business, Who’s Who of Business Executives and Who's Who of Emerging Leaders.

Jari Holland Buck’s Specialties:

Executive coaching, training and development, organizational interventions, career/outplacement consulting, human resources, labor/management, change management strategies, communication skills, coaching and feedback skills, conflict resolution, personalized leadership development.
