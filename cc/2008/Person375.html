Adam Markus’s Summary

AN ADMISSIONS CONSULTING EXPERT
Since 2001, I have worked with over 300 applicants, a wide variety of individuals who successfully obtained admission to top graduate schools. Since 1995, I have worked in international education as a counselor, instructor, manager, and marketer in the US and Japan. Based on my experience, I believe that I can add significant value to my clients.

日本語: http://adammarkus.com/Jindex.html

I offer the following services:
-Email and telephone based admissions consulting worldwide
-Face-to-face sessions in Tokyo or elsewhere by special arrangement
-Individualized services to meet specific needs
-Customized group instruction
-Consulting to higher education organizations on admissions and Japan-based localization

A RECORD OF SUCCESS:
MBA: I have helped applicants get into every top program in the US & Europe. I have successfully served as an essay & interview counselor for numerous applicants accepted to HBS, Stanford GSB, Chicago GSB, Columbia Business School, Kellogg, Wharton, Yale SOM, UC Berkeley Haas, Duke, Tuck, INSEAD, & the London School of Business.

Other Graduate Degrees: My clients were admitted to top programs in such fields as Masters of Law (Harvard, Columbia, NYU, Chicago, Northwestern, Berkeley), Public Policy (Harvard Kennedy School of Government), International Relations (Columbia, Fletcher & Georgetown), Finance (London Business School, London School of Economics), Economics (University of Chicago), Engineering (Stanford), Computer Science (Stanford), & Environmental Studies (Yale).

COMMITTED TO SHARING GREAT INFORMATION WITH APPLICANTS
My blog, http://adam-markus.blogspot.com/, demonstrates my commitment to being an independent source of information & opinions about admission to graduate programs.

Interested in my consulting services? See http://adammarkus.com/ or email at adammarkus@gmail.com.
