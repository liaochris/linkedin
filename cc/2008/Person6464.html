Douglas Klugh’s Summary

At GoDaddy.com, I am developing custom BI tools to support our efforts to remain the world's largest Internet domain registrar and North America's largest Internet hosting company. My applications are used to analyze utilization metrics, forecast market trends, and monitor business performance for everything from domain auctions, shared and dedicated hosting and e-mail services, proprietary consumer products, SSL certificates, and e-commerce tools.

As an IBM Rational Consultant, I have delivered innovative software solutions to Fortune 500 companies throughout the United States. These solutions include n-tier applications, business intelligence solutions, enterprise data management and administration, software process engineering, project planning and management, security analysis, consulting, training, and mentoring. All software products and services are developed utilizing software engineering best practices to enable continuous enhancement throughout the software lifecycle. By leveraging industry-leading modeling tools, I am constructing robust architectural models to ensure resilient product design and to accelerate time-to-market.

While leveraging my education and background in software engineering, my primary emphasis is on developing iterative solutions utilizing tools and methodologies such as the Rational Unified Process® (RUP). I also adapt various Agile methods for less structured approaches to meeting various business needs. As much of my industry experience includes insurance, investments, and banking, I am collaborating with businesses and organizations in other industries in providing e-business services (including portal and e-commerce solutions).

Douglas Klugh’s Specialties:

My proficiencies in software engineering disciplines include implementation of iterative development methodologies, business process modeling, software process engineering, administration of software configuration & change management, writing and managing functional & strategic requirements, designing object-oriented & service-oriented architectures utilizing UML, and coding application software on platforms including .Net, Windows, IIS, Eclipse, Apache Tomcat, WebSphere, UNIX, and MVS.
