Alan Fairweather’s Summary

Alan, The Motivation Doctor, has for the past fifteen years been turning ‘adequate’ managers and team leaders into consistent top performers.
After a successful career as a manager he founded his business in 1993 and works with people and organisations speaking, consulting and running training programmes in the UK, Singapore and Indonesia.
He specialises in how to motivate people at work so that they deliver business results.
Alan did the job of a middle manager and did it effectively - for fifteen years. He now does it on a daily basis through his seminars and workshops. Alan knows the real challenges that managers face every day with their people. He's used the '3 Secrets of Motivation' described in his book. They worked for him and they will work for anyone who manages or supervises other people.

Alan is the author of ‘How to be a Motivational Manager’ (released October 07)
