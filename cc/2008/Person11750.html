  • President & CEO

    Lexicon Systems, LLC

    (Management Consulting industry)

    April 2002 — Present (6 years 8 months)

    Jill Barson Gilbert founded Lexicon Systems, LLC, an independent, woman-owned management consulting firm with Houston, Texas headquarters.

    The firm helps industrial and utility companies, small to mid-sized software vendors and investment firms to become more agile. Lexicon appies technology to enable clients to make more informed decisions and meet their objectives. The firm strives to help its clients to

    - understand business risks and market opportunities
    - manage business risk portfolios
    - improve compliance
    - drive efficiency and improve business performance

    The firm provides Information Systems/Information Technology (IS/IT) and Business Advisory services. A fusion of industrial, management consulting and IS/IT skills permits the firm to work well with people at all levels of client organizations, in diverse disciplines.
