Stephan Tual’s Summary

I have recently joined 5one as Technical Director.

Until March 2008 and as Terapad.com's Founder and Technical Director, I grew the company from a startup in August 2004, to today where it now operates over 10,000 websites in 156 countries including national landmarks such as the world-famous Cutty Sark. I single-handedly wrote the application that is now ZDNET's highest rated web publishing and Content Management solution, winning the Editor's Choice award and surpassing Microsoft Expression Web, Dreamweaver, WordPress, etc. in their December 07 review (http://tinyurl.com/yttre9).

Prior to launching Terapad I acted as Macromedia's (now Adobe) Regional Master Certified Instructor for the UK. During that time I have provided consulting services on scalability, security and programming for a broad range of over 50 prestigious companies, including BT OpenWorld, Cable & Wireless, Churchill Insurance, GMTV, the Royal Air Force and the Department of Defense.

Stephan Tual’s Specialties:

I have 11 years of extensive experience in client and partners' management, solution selling, complex project delivery, business development and technical sales. I also hold a far-reaching technical background, including developing dozens of enterprise-level web applications for companies such as Creative Labs and Mobil Oil.

As an EU citizen who worked in the US, France and England, I am open to regular and international travel.
