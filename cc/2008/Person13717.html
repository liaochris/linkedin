Julian Elve’s Summary

An experienced technology leader, with an extensive knowledge of television broadcasting and business information systems, with wide experience of the strategic application of technology to business problems.

Management experience in a fully commercial subsidiary of a public service broadcaster, in both line and project positions. Experienced in the development of strategic plans for the delivery of technological support to business processes and business goals, and the delivery of those plans using external suppliers.

An experienced change manager, with Programme Management experience backed by qualification as both "Managing Successful Programmes" Registered Advanced Practitioner and PRINCE2 Practitioner.

Leadership coaching expertise backed by training in Neuro-Linguistic Programming to Master Practitioner level.

Julian Elve’s Specialties:

Technology implications of mergers and acquisitions, development of technology strategies, commercial negotiation, project & programme management, change management, supplier management, business process consulting, applications of information security, leadership coaching, mentoring, NLP, Neuro-Semantics.
