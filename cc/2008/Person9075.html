Greg Lins’s Summary

My vision is one of prosperous organizations, growing economies, and improved lives; locally and globally. We are in the business of "Innovating Prosperity."

TLG Innovation, Inc.
TLG Alliance Network
TLG Prosperity Initiative

Prior to founding TLG, I led consulting practices for companies such as Arthur Andersen, CNA Consulting, and Applied Theory. I have worked with startups through Fortune 50 companies in situations ranging from rapid growth, to turnarounds, to mergers and acquisitions.

Progression: technical training, project leadership, sales/marketing management, executive leadership, entrepreneurial pursuits, trade and economic development, fighting poverty, social change, and mentoring others.

Greg Lins’s Specialties:

Building business, social, and other communities. Business advisory, strategic planning and execution, turn-arounds, merger integration, start-ups. Networking. Marketing, sales, operations, sourcing, negotiations, business transformation, facilities, real estate, and project leadership.
