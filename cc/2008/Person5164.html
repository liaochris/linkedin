Claude Vedovini’s Summary

Claude Vedovini is an IT expert with 15 years of professional experience and a broad IT culture.
Passionate about his job and recognized mentor, Claude will tailor the latest technologies and trends to your very own needs.

Claude is currently interested in localization and documentation management, social networks and the semantic web.

Claude is the founder of the DITA Open Platform, a free, open-source project which goal is to provide an enterprise platform for the edition, management and processing of DITA documents.

Claude Vedovini’s Specialties:

- Development methodologies, processes and tools.
- Model Driven Architecture and Design.
