Shelby Swatek’s Summary

A copywriter who "gets tech" and consistently delivers clear, compelling copy. Call me when you need superlative copywriting for brochures, web content, sales and data sheets, demand-generation deliverables, case studies, success stories, press releases, white papers, direct mail, sales letters, advertisements, newsletter articles, executive presentations, backgrounders, and more. With 7+ years in high-tech marketing, I bring an excellent understanding of the high-tech arena to your communications project. My growing list of clients includes companies in computer software, hardware, electronics, telecommunications, and other areas of high-tech. Whatever your corner of the high-tech industry, I look forward to creating compelling copy for your marketing, advertising, and PR that will hit the mark and increase your high-tech sales in today's challenging economy.
