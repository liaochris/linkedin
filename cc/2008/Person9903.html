Mark Crutch’s Summary

Mark Crutch is the son of Eugene Crutch and Aida Valasquez, his mother came to New York from Puerto Rico his father from a small farm 5 miles outside of Elizabeth City, North Carolina called "CrutchTown." It was named by his great grandfather who first purchased the 40 acres piece of land that grew to be a small town. He founded CrutchTown for future generations in hopes to provide land to live and prosper without the ridicules of the surrounding world.

INARTMEDIA:
Frustrated with the lack of independent art being exposed and showcased to the word Mark Crutch created InArtMedia.com as an upbeat, uncensored, viral webisode network with syndicated shows devoted to "Showcasing The Independently Famous".

The format and style of "Inter-Dramas" (Interviews Dramatized) is it's brand, meaning each webisode is individually formatted with it's own cross-cutting skits, pacing, and tone.

iAm is viral and can be accessed everywhere from top online video and social communities, to phones, iPods, gaming devices, to whatever the next platform is.


BIO:
Mark Crutch has over fifteen years experience in the multimedia production industry. He made his entrance into the industry in 1987 as a fashion photographer of new face models in New York City. For the next five years he gained valuable experience in his field by working for several NYC independent agencies during which time he attended the Fashion Institute of Technology in NYC. In 1992 he took up Film Studies and latter added Graphics Design, Still, Motion to his resume of talents in art. His interests progressed naturally to directing, and since 1995 he has Directed, Produced, Edited, many Trailers, TV Commercials, Music Videos, Short Films, Commentaries, Concerts, Multi-media Events and Features.
