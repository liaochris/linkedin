Jay Langdon, SPHR’s Summary

Business Development through Human Resources . We provide a fresh prospective to help clarify and communicate your business's direction, align your people, and strengthen your Human Resource practices to improve positioning for success.

People Are The Competitive Advantage. How you utilize, treat, interact and develop your employees determines your success. We help companies improve their "People Processes"; hiring not just the 'qualified' but the 'right' people. Terminating the wrong ones has become a specialty.

Comprehensive management processes link people to your plan: Strategic Human Resource Planning, Hiring & Terminations, Training and Recognition, Employment Branding, Performance Management Systems, Compensation and benefits, all work towards one goal – Your Success.

Jay Langdon, SPHR’s Specialties:

On topics ranging from legal compliance to cultural change, recruiting and employment practices, we work with your “people issues”. Using a personal touch, highly educated, experienced and certified professionals utilize a series of strategic assessment tools and techniques to clarify your plan and improve (or develop from scratch) your Human Resource Operations. Our vision includes a long term focus with immediate actions that improve your Results Now!
