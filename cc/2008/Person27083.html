Wendy Belancourt, CPRW’s Summary

After 8 years of working for other resume companies, Creative Resumes (provider to Dice.com, ComputerJobs.com, and WallStreetJournal.com), and Tools for Transition (a Ladders provider), I have a rich history of writing effective resumes that get the results you need.

If you need effective career documents at an affordable price, contact me for an initial free evaluation at wendy@trendresumes.com.

I can develop career documents such as resumes, cover and thank you letters, bio's (general, web, LinkedIn, and Ladders), and employment proposals. During our collaborative project, I will perform considerable research and learn quite a bit about you. With this knowledge, I hand-pick and deliver information critical to a successful job search with your final documents. This includes short and informative how-to guides, on-line tools and resources, and information about your online reputation.

I would love the opportunity to speak with you about how I can help you get your next job. You can reach me via email, connecting through any social network, or by phone at 636-390-8580.
