Bob Beringer’s Summary

Bob Beringer is the Chief Executive and Senior Technologist for the Electronic On-Ramp, a Native American Indian and Veteran Owned Small Disadvantaged and 8(a) Certified Business that is based out of the Washington D.C. Metro Area. Mr. Beringer has approximately 20 years of experience in the IT field, and specializes in Systems Design, Integration, and Security. Mr. Beringer has several years of experience working with Linux Clusters, Storage Area Networks, Enterprise Backup and Fibre Channel Technologies. Mr. Beringer has received advanced training from leading organizations like: IBM, Red Hat, SNIA, Oracle, Veritas, Brocade, CEDIA, and currently holds the following certifications: Microsoft Certified Systems Engineer (MCSE), Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA), Marine Corps Certified Unix / Intelligence Systems Administrator, Certified Information Systems Security Specialist (CISSP), Certified Trainer, Certified Internet Webmaster (CIW), A+, N+, and I-Net+. His e-mail address is: Bob.Beringer@usa.net.

Bob Beringer’s Specialties:

Incident Response and Crisis Management, Computer Security, Forensics, SDLC, R2P2, Datacenter Operations, Callcenter Management, Certification and Accreditation, FISMA Compliance and Software Development.
