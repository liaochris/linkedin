Bart Vanmaele’s Summary

Bart Vanmaele completed his Masters in Communication at the reputed University of Leuven. As the commercial Internet was coming up (late 90s), he specialized in Internet and Multimedia. Combined with his intrest for the telecommunication sector, he wrote a thesis on Voip/Internet Telephony, which was then a new upcoming technology.

The combinated passions for Internet and Telecom found its way in an internship as a webmaster for the Telenet portal site, where Bart was responsible for the coordination of the 4 interns, who collaborated on the content and graphical side of the portal site. This internship was combined with an evening education at the Mediacenter in leuven, to get a degree as Professional Webmaster.

In 2002, Bart became the Webmaster for Krëfel, number 1 electro retailer in Belgium. He successfully transformed krefel.be into an e-commerce site, which sold the same products as the 'physical' Krëfel shops. He was in charge of full coordination of these e-commerce activities and related services, as well as content updates and graphic design of this website.

In 2006, Bart took a new turn in his career and moved to E-Zone NV, a Belgian company specialized in Internet and Mobile solutions, where he took charge of the communication and marketing of the brand MobileWeb.be. Among his duties are setup of online and offline marketing campaigns, organisation and coordination of events, web design, corporate communication, etc... He is currently still active in this position.
