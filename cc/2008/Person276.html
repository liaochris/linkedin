Rajat Bhadani [rbhadani@gmail.com]’s Summary

Traveling around the world while working in Sales, Marketing & Business Development in relation to IKEA in Newcastle, ADT Security in London, Forex in Barcelona and 3 Mobile in Melbourne, I was always looking for an opportunity to start up something on my own when I settle down in one country.

With my background in graphic designing, I had been doing some work on and off and finally started ABUZZ (dynamic, CMS, E-Commerce and flash websites) with a couple of friends of mine. Now, after 2 years, we take pride in doing some classy and professional work for UK and US companies and a few more across the globe & have increased to a team of 10.

Currently I have started a new venture called BloggingSaurus which will be a netwok of technology blogs like the one we already have - www.reviewsaurus.com and we also offer professional blog designing services - www.designsaurus.com

I am always looking forward to link up to people in similar industry for tie-ups and business opportunities and also just as to increase my knowledge in IT enabled services and designing in general.

Also, if you are somebody who is interested in adventurous traveling aimed at interacting with people from different cultures, please contact me to share experiences and also for possible future trips. I have bag packed through 30 countries across Europe and Asia till date and am living the dream of touching 50 countries before the age of 30.

If you are still reading, please contact me on rbhadani@gmail.com with anything you have on your mind right now.
