Mike Boas’s Summary

Mike has always loved cartoons. Years of drawing led him to pursue a degree in art at SUNY Geneseo, where he also developed an interest in broadcasting. After graduating, Mike worked for several years at WCMF radio in Rochester. Eventually he discovered that his drawings could not be seen on the radio, no matter how many times he held them up to the microphone.

Pressing on, he linked up with Animatus Studio as they were working on their first "Derf the Viking" cartoon. He has since designed and animated various Vikings, Norse gods, drunken clowns, and dancing sumo wrestlers. He even contributes a voice now and then.

Mike also creates his own pieces, which reflect his love for the horror genre. His Friday the 13th fan film, "Jason: The Rebirth," has won top prizes at ManiaFest and NecroComicon events in California. His latest project, "H.P. Lovecraft's The Other Gods" is currently burning up the festival circuit.

Mike acts as webmaster to AnimatusStudio.com, designing pages and creating original Flash content. His personal website is maddogmovies.com

Mike Boas’s Specialties:

Hand-drawn animation, Flash, Toon Boom, After Effects, Photoshop, Premiere, Encore, VT Edit
