Toni Greaves’s Summary

Innovative, vibrant, award-winning creative leader with fourteen years experience translating creative strategy into compelling results-driven conceptual and visual solutions. Passionate user experience advocate, expert in on-time, on-budget delivery of both small and large interactive brand experiences, as well as online advertising and marketing campaigns. Excellence in managing and developing high-level client relationships, outstanding written and verbal presentation skills. Strong cross-discipline and cross-cultural understanding, experienced in working and managing work in a variety of global settings. Great team player and motivator, inspiring, supportive, flexible and on-the-ball. Industry reputation for world-class creative excellence.

Personal attributes:
Enthusiastic and positive attitude, luminously creative, articulate, patient, precise, tenacious, well-grounded, dependable, fun, and always... a never-ending desire to learn.

Toni Greaves’s Specialties:

Branding and identity, interactive design and print design, creative team leadership, photography, photojournalism.
