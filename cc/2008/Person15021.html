Kristi Serrano’s Summary

Kristi Serrano has been working in the Internet and marketing industry for the past four years in multiple facets. From Ad Trafficking and Campaign Management to Email and Online Marketing, she is no stranger to the dot com marketplace. Kristi is currently the Editorial Marketing Manager at The Knot, Inc. and heads up Interactive Femme, a networking site for woman in the interactive and technology industries. Kristi has also recently started her own company online. Prior to her recent endeavors, Kristi headed up the marketing team at Prime Visibility where she oversaw and monitored the marketing tactics used to generate traffic and increase ROI for an assortment of clients. Kristi also created detailed marketing plans for clients as well as Prime Visibility that include SEO, Email Marketing, Public Relations and Lead Generation. Kristi has been active member of 212 – New York’s Interactive Advertising Club since 2004 and speaks at several local Universities on Internet Marketing. Kristi also teaches an Internet Marketing course at Long Island University, C.W. Post. She was interviewed and published in the Spring 2007 issue of Search Marketing Standard Magazine in regards to her educational efforts. She currently has a B.F.A. in Public Relations from Long Island University, C.W. Post.
