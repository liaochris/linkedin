Don Davis’s Summary

Don would like to reach out to the voters of Eastern North Carolina. By bringing a message of unity he hopes to bring the people of Wayne, Greene and Pitt Counties years of growth and prosperity under a unified and community way of thinking.

Don Davis’s Specialties:

Donald G. Davis, 36, is the mayor of Snow Hill located in eastern North Carolina. He was born and raised in Snow Hill. As an adolescent, he cropped tobacco to help his family make ends meet. Don learned three important values early in life-hard work, educational excellence, and passion for people.
