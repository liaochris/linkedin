Derek Mehraban’s Summary

A graduate of Michigan State University, Derek Mehraban has 14+ years advertising, marketing and interactive experience. Mehraban is focused on digital and internet marketing, event marketing, web search, web 2.0, social media marketing, blogging and generational marketing.

Mehraban is CEO of Ingenex Digital Marketing, based in Ann Arbor, Michigan. Ingenex provides a full suite of Digital Marketing services including interactive branding™, web site design, generational marketing, search engine optimization, pay-per-click management, optimized public relations, direct marketing, email marketing, event marketing, SEO, SEM, PPC, podcasting and blogging.

During his career Mehraban has helped the following brands with their marketing and advertising: the Pontiac, Cadillac and Chevrolet divisions of General Motors, ACDelco, Subway Subs, GDI Infotech, b’drizzled Gourmet Popcorn, Innovative Composites, Peliton, The United Way, NuStep, Asahi Kasei, ICPSR, The University of Florida, The University of Michigan, Eastern Michigan University and Terumo Cardiovascular.

Mehraban founded Connect Ann Arbor in 2007, a non-profit group focused on organizing compelling Ann Arbor based events around marketing, entrepreneurship and technology topics. Connect Ann Arbor brought Seth Godin to A2 in May and helped plan ArbCamp w/ Joseph Jaffe in November 2007. Mehraban and his business partner Ross Johnson run The Digital Bus Blog + Podcast. www.thedigitalbus.com where they interview leaders in digital marketing and buisness.

Mehraban has built a strong network in the marketing, technology and business communities by participating in groups such as Ann Arbor Spark, The Inforum, Ann Arbor Ad Club, Marketing Roundtable and A2B3. Mehraban is the brother of Darius Mehraban, Darius is a lawyer at Gibson Dunn in New York City.

Derek Mehraban recommends web 2.0 tools such as Linkedin, Plaxo, Facebook and Twitter to stay in touch.
