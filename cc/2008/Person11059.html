Jeff Paiva’s Summary

Jeff Paiva is a digital immigrant proud of his fluency in the new language that is spoken these times.

Born in an age where the top tech gadgets were the color TV set and the electric typewriter (on which he had his first writing experience), this journalist-turned-copywriter fell in love with digital communications and advertising in the early age of internet (circa 1999).

After some really cool gigs – copywriter for big traditional advertising agencies in São Paulo, editor of the official 2006 FIFA World Cup™ Brazilian website and for International Sailing Federation's events in Brazil – Mr. Paiva has been coordinating the establishment of a brand new area at AgênciaClick, the leading digital and interactive ad agency in the country.

Working with social networks, both 2D and 3D, Jeff is thrilled to be part of a brave new world, connecting people and changing the one-way language of the traditional advertising into a brilliant and lively conversation.
