Kirk Dupuis’s Summary

I am continuing on my journey to create. I am currently dialing in to an eventual Senior Executive and Director position with a World-Class Architectural Sign Company or Advertising or Entertainment-oriented Company... Maybe all three in some strongly creative and ideative capacity.

I have been fortunate enough to work for and with some fantastic people throughout my entire life. I have had great mentors, and now I am stepping up to mentor new young talent where I am able.

With an unextinguishable passion to draw, paint, create and design, I intend to travel the world and eventually open a specialized creative studio and learning institute, with the help and partnership of some unique leaders and team members in my network. We can share, teach and help new talent find direction and focus and maybe avoid some of the "hard knocks" we had to endure in more formal educational, vocational and life situations.

I wish for my life adventure to provide a way to take my wife and dog and maybe even my grandson and travel the world, adding experiences to our repertoire to share through art and design with others. Our current concerns are definitely "Green" issues and how we can help impact the planet in a positive practical and effective way for the future.

I will always want to paint murals, forever.
