Joseph Szala’s Summary

I'm the managing principal of an interactive branding strategy firm that specializes in companies operating in or focusing on the restaurants & entertainment, luxury beauty & fashion, and the architecture and design industries. I have worked with the likes of New York Times, Kerastase, Flexjet, Mark Garrison Salon, Dov Salon, Modern Planet Clothing, American Salon Magazine, Bruno Gaget, Dayna Spitz, Evian water, Pfaltzgraff, Datum Filing Systems, and more to extend the impact of their messages. The services my company provides help change and form the perceptions of my client's customers and market.

I've always had a taste for style and fashion. I love fine, classy clothing and believe it says a lot about a personality. I've been naturally attracted to people who are as passionate about their work as I am mine. Because of this, I've built unbreakable relationships with most of my clients.

Everyone makes judgments based on appearance alone. We live in a visual society with a super-competitive mentality. Understanding this has helped me help my clients win their markets attention and keep it. That's what I do.

Joseph Szala’s Specialties:

Focus on branding all business aspects, building customer and employee loyalty, boosting word-of-mouth advertising and other grassroots initiatives. We also center heavily on the web and new media.
