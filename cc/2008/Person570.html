Todd Curtis’s Summary

Using the Internet as a means to both enhance the public's understanding of aviation safety and security issues and to support aviation safety and security public policy efforts. In addition to safety engineering and online publishing, my company's areas of expertise include providing insight and guidance to organizations that seek to systematically use online resources to support marketing activities, including advertising, promotion, and intelligence gathering.

Todd Curtis’s Specialties:

risk assessment, online traffic analysis, search engine optimization, online marketing, podcasting management, online multimedia development and planning

Todd Curtis’s Experience

    AirSafe.com, LLC

    (Internet industry)

    1996 — Present (12 years)

    Launched the web site AirSafe.com in 1996, and have gone on to become a noted expert in aviation safety and security, appearing frequently as a guest on CNN, Fox, MSNBC, and other television and radio news shows.
