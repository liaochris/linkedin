Matt Mauldin’s Summary

Matt Mauldin's experience involves a wide range of marketing and advertising tactics and overall strategy to build effective brands, create awareness among consumers, and increase return on investment for advertising expenditures.

Internet Marketing & Advertising
Matt's interactive advertising experience includes Search Marketing on search engines such as Google, Yahoo, MSN and more. During his time at Captura Matt managed campaigns for all types of companies such as start-ups, mid-size companies, and Fortune 500 companies for both e-commerce sites and lead generation programs. Matt managed web development projects for large websites such as CashNetUSA.com, Rifle-Scopes.com, First Command Bank and True.com. Matt also lead the natural search engine optimization (SEO) efforts for a number of companies to increase the number and ranking of unpaid search engine results for the client's website. In addition, Matt also managed large banner campaigns and affiliate programs for web advertisers.

E-commerce
In addition to managing campaigns for other companies, Matt also owns and runs his own e-commerce site, SolarBatShades.com. SolarBatShades.com is a platinum dealer for Solar Bat sunglasses, and sell to over twenty countries around the world. This company launched the e-commerce site in July 2006 and is currently profitable and making a competitive return for investors and company owners.

Traditional Advertising
Matt's traditional advertising experience comes from Media Buyer and Media Planner, Account Executive, Market Research Coordinator, and Director of Media positions at a number of advertising agencies.

Matt Mauldin’s Specialties:

Marketing and advertising strategy. Interactive media, Search marketing, pay-per-click advertising, natural search engine optimization, web development management. Traditional media, media buying, media planning.
