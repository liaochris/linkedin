Vicki Hancock’s Summary

Vicki has over 30 years' experience as an educator - teacher, university faculty, professional developer, and consultant. Over 40 of her articles have been published in professional journals. Since 1989, her non-profit organization duties have included new product development, constituent outreach, training program development and delivery, strategic planning, and process improvement.

She is a results-oriented professional who is recognized for designing and delivering instructional programs, creating innovative learning resources, teaching adults and children, writing for publication, and using technology for teaching and learning.

Vicki holds a Ph.D. in Education Communications from the University of Maryland and is a member of ASAE, the American Society of Association Executives.
