Tim Parsons’s Summary

Passionate about creative people and inspired by how technology can transform possibilities, I'm an entrepreneur, digital media producer and industry strategist who's been designing, building and delivering interactive digital products, media experiences and services for the past 11 years for leading investors and corporate clients in Australia, US, UK, and Asia - such as Apple Computer, American Express, Audi, Charles Schwab, Drambuie, Intel, Mazda, McDonalds, Medtronic, Mobile Mondays, Nikon, Southcorp, Channel Seven, Sony, Toyota and Woolworths.

These days I'm working a lot in the venture space with new companies and private investor partners - working out how to match talent and capital to launch global projects. As usual, i'm in over my head !

Other stuff I've done/am doing: co-written and presented a 1 hr BBC 2 documentary ['Cyberpunks & Technophobes',1993 - got to meet Douglas Adams and Craig Charles], lectured on the future of media at the Australian Film Television and Radio School, at the University of New South Wales, and other film- and media-related institutions [I’m nuts about the on-demand and audience-mediated future of TV]. I’m also a co-founder of Mobile Monday Sydney (www.mobilemondaysydney.com), a monthly event focused on bringing the mobile industry together.

I am available for consulting and strategy engagements, am always on the look out for interesting people to collaborate with, have a strong professional and consulting services toolbox having run branding workshops, information design brainstorming sessions, business seminars and management meetings for everyone from boutiques to banks.

Drop me a line !
