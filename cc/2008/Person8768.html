Gina Furia Rubel’s Summary

Gina is the owner of Furia Rubel, an award-winning, full-service women-owned certified, public relations and marketing agency advising law firms, service providers, educational institutions, retail property management groups, pharmaceutical companies and nonprofits internationally.

A communications expert, attorney and publicist, Gina is a recipient of the 2008 Best 50 Women in Business Award given by Pennsylvania Governor Ed Rendell. She received the 2007 Deanne White Award for Community Service from the Public Relation Society of America (PRSA) Philadelphia Chapter.

Her latest book, Everyday Public Relations for Lawyers, is available at www.theprlawyer.com.

A Philadelphia native who practiced law for several years, Gina focuses on her passion for communications. Her clients have been seen on Good Morning America, The Today Show, 20/20, and in USA Today, Family Circle, Wall Street Journal, New York Times, LA Times, Philadelphia Inquirer, Business Journals and many others.

A graduate of Drexel University and Widener University School of Law, Gina is a frequent lecturer and is regularly published.

She is chancellor of The Justinian Society of Italian-American lawyers, immediate past president of the Bucks County Women's Business Forum, Chair of the Bar-News Media Committee for the Philadelphia Bar Association, a member of the Legal Marketing Association, Philadelphia and American Bar Associations, Philadelphia Public Relations Association, and Public Relations Society of America. She was elected to the Drexel University Alumni Board of Directors in 2006 and serves as the Communications Committee Chair and on the University Magazine Advisory Committee.

Gina resides in Doylestown (Bucks County), Pennsylvania, with her husband and two children. In her spare time, she loves photography, travel, gardening, cooking, bird watching and nature.
