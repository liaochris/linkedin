Robert Godden {L.I.O.N. and TopLinked.com}’s Summary

In 2008, I decided to stop working for other people, and formed Dreamer's Ball Pty Ltd with my wife Anne, in order to pursue opportunities which interest us both. This is both within recruitment and outside. most notably with a recent restaurant acquisition.

I have been working on the research aspects of recruitment since 2001.I specialise in tracking down hard-to-find candidates and in salary benchmarking and consulting. I have often appeared as an expert witness in various Courts on salary and employment issues.

Prior to joining the recruitment industry, I owned a small business for seven years, specialising in market research, general research, information publications and database systems analysis and programming.

I have also taught general research skills, market research, marketing and business legal issues to Small Business Management Trainees as part of a Federal Government Programme.

I am the author of "1001 Nights in The Trans-Arabian Corporation's Boardroom", a book which uses fiction to spark creativity in business operations and career management.
