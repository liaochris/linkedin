Marnix Bras’s Summary

For Dance4Life I will be creating strategy, concepts and coordinating new online opportunities.
Keywords: web2.0, communities, wisdom in the crowds, two-way communications. etc. etc.

Before that, I specialized myself in Internet and online communications. Graphic design as well as consultancy and advise for online marketing and email marketing.

My background is in conceptual design for public spaces, from where I worked for a creative office. From there I developed presentation of concepts different techniques, Animation in Flash, Vector art in Illustrator, Company styling and profiling in Indesign or photo-editing in Photoshop, You name it. I can dream it all, for web and print.

Furthermore I have a decent amount of HTML-CSS knowledge, e-mail marketing knowledge and experience in creative concepting.

My dream is to develop towards a creative concepter that advises clients and defines strategy for splashing online campaigns and communications, This in narrow cooperation with graphic designers, identity designers, front-end developers and (database) marketeers.

Marnix Bras’s Specialties:

Online communications, email marketing, (or e-mail marketing, depending on where you live) branding and identity, graphic design, online marketing, English, Dutch, broad perspective, open minded
