Randy Green’s Summary

Provider of high-end illustration talent for the Aviation/Aerospace/Military industry. I also specialize in technical and historical research and large format graphic design for museums, trades & institutions. Past clients include, Breitling, Martin Marietta Aerospace, CH Computer Products, Revell-Monogram Models, Lindberg Models, White Mountain Puzzles, FX Schmid, Senator Patrick Leahy, 8th Air Force Museum, National D-Day Museum, National Civil War Museum, Cowles Historical Media Group, The Retired Officer Association, and a host of others.

Randy Green’s Specialties:

Illustration, Graphic Design, Concept Design, Exhibit Design, Research Services, Wide Format Digital Media Output Expertise, Graphic Project Managment, Graphic and Art Consultation, Exhibit Consultation.
