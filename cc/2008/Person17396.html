Melanie Votaw’s Summary

I am a professional freelance journalist who has published ten non-fiction books on a variety of subjects. I have written for such publications as Woman's Day, Travel Savvy, His & Hers, and Medical Economics, and I'm also a copywriter for businesses and individuals. I have written and edited websites, brochures, speeches, sales letters, and more. In addition, I edit and doctor books for agents, publishers, and individuals, and I'm open to collaborations and ghostwriting.

Read more about me on my website at http://www.RuletheWord.com.

I'm a member of the Authors Guild, the Society of Professional Journalists, the Society of Environmental Journalists, and a former member of the American Society of Journalists and Authors.

I'm also a travel writer and photographer interested in hearing from PR specialists in this area with story leads. See my image galleries taken on six continents at http://www.VotawPhotography.com.
