Anand Sharma’s Summary

A bonafide "social media addict", I am passionate about technologies that simplify "interactions", be it between human-to-human, human-to-machine or machine-to-machine. Not surprisingly, my current areas of interest are in figuring out the business applications of Social Computing along with helping design and build Solutions based on SOA/WOA.

After graduating from Arizona State with my dual Masters degree in Industrial Engineering (Information Systems) and Computer Science, I have had the good fortune of working in two great Fortune 500 companies in these past ten years - Lucent Technologies and Cisco Systems.

I live in "the" Silicon Valley with my wife, a daughter and our Wire Fox Terrier. When I am not at work and I am not on my laptop reading about the latest political chicaneries of US Politics or Blogging or Twittering, or dealing with the latest fad rolled out of the Web (2.0 or n.0) assembly line, I like to take short and/or long road trips, watch movies and enjoy fast-food delicacies..;-)
