Eric Welter’s Summary

The Welter Law Firm represents and advises employers on all aspects of the employment relationship and represents businesses in commercial and franchise litigation. Eric A. Welter is an experienced litigator who has represented national corporations in complex litigation, including class actions, involving a wide range of employment law, public accommodations and business issues. His practice focuses on employment law, including advising clients on discrimination and reasonable accommodation issues, handbooks and personnel policies, employment contracts, and wage and hour laws; and litigation of discrimination, wage and hour, pension and benefits, defamation, breach of contract, and noncompete claims in federal and state court, and before the Equal Employment Opportunity Commission and the Department of Labor. Mr. Welter’s practice also includes representing organizations in commercial litigation involving breach of contract, franchise disputes, or trade secret misappropriation cases.
