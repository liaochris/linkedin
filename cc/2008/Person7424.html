Eric Powell’s Summary

I have been an interactive designer for over 10 years working with both small and large-scale advertising agencies, interactive shops, and in-house teams like TBWA\Chiat\Day, Avenue A/Razorfish, Rare Medium, Virgin Entertainment, and DNA Studios. I specialize in hands-on Art Direction and Flash Design for online media and have been responsible for the concept, design, direction, and deployment of projects for clients such as Coors, Molson, Nintendo, Dreamworks, Néstle, Lucas Arts, Sony, Microsoft, Nissan, Levi's and Apple.

In addition I have been a signed recording artist and performer with a popular underground band since 1991 where I have released 6 full-length albums on major and independent labels as well as extensive touring.

I am also an accomplished composer having scored movies for Lionsgate, MGM, and Open Sky Entertainment, provided music licenses for Microsoft, Speed TV, CBS, MTV and Sony Computer Entertainment and more.

Eric Powell’s Specialties:

Music production and composition, Interactive Art Direction and Design for online advertising.
