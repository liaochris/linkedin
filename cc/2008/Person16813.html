Mary-Lynn Foster’s Summary

I spent 18 years in the radio broadcasting industry, and have had the joy of hosting every shift, in many formats, in five markets. At one time, I co-hosted mornings and hosted mid-days on one station, while simultaneously serving as the Production Director for all five stations in my employer’s group. Busy times!

My radio career highlights include:

* Being known as The First Lady of Morning Radio in Champaign-Urbana, Illinois, where I hosted my own morning show.

* Being recognized as the Employee of the Year for the Illini Radio Group.

* Being nominated for numerous Illinois Broadcasters’ Association awards as both a personality and a producer.

* Realizing my dream of broadcasting in the same market (Chicago) as my childhood hero, Bob Collins. I've co-hosted a couple of shows on WLS, 890AM. I also do frequent commercial voice work for WLS from my Bigg Studio.

Now, I'm building my own brand in new media, and tapping into my entrepreneurial spirit! I am the Vice President, and Executive Producer of Bigg Success (biggsucces.com).
