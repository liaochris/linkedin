Sean Smith’s Summary

As the Web becomes more and more crucial to our lives, finding what we need online becomes the killer app of any online business. Search engine marketing, an important part of the maturization of the Net, becomes an essential foundation for the success of the Web as an indispensable tool in human growth, business, and evolution. The culture, the philosophy, and the architecture of the Web makes it a community like none this species has ever seen. We must continue to ensure that its' growth is both organic and highly determined so that it remains useful and fulfilling to those who use it.

In a short time, the Web will become so prevalent that we will be unable to exist peacefully without it. And while the work I do, be it web design, IA, or SEM, may be a small part of it, it is the combined efforts of those like me, who want to see the Internet reach it's obvious potential, that shape it. I intend to be as much as part of that as possible.
