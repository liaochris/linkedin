PAOLA FIORE’s Summary

Italy-based independent professional freelance: Environmental Manager, Green Communication Strategist, Corporate Responsibility Consultant, Journalist, Documentarist.

Graduated in 1999 in Law and Economics, with a specialisation in Environmental Law and Management, under the supervisoring of Emilio Gerelli, Emeritus Professor of Public Finance and Financial Law at the University of the Studies of Pavia (Italy).

In 2003 she obtained a honours degree in the BA (Hons) Documentary Photography Course, established in the late '70s by the Welsh photographer David Hurn of Magnum Photo Agency at the Newport School of Art, Media and Design of the University of Wales College Newport (UK).

Paola Fiore is particularly committed to work on Environmental Management Scheme Projects, Green Communication Assignments and Corporate Social Responsibility Actions to implement Private Companies, Public Institutions and Non Governmental Organizations’s wider Environmental and Social Responsibility Strategies.

PAOLA FIORE’s Specialties:

Environmental communication, law, policy; Management systems (SA8000, ISO 14001/EMAS, EUETS, ECOLABEL/EPD/LCA, AGENDA21); Green copywriting, newswriting, editing; Orientated companies policies, green marketing; Events, seminars, exhibitions, trade publications; Relationship networks with media, opinion makers; PR system with clients, media relations, press offices; CSR activities, policies, tools; Seminars for employees/customers; Customer orientation, labels for products, reports for locations.
