Dr. Angelo Cacciola Donati’s Summary

To introduce myself, I am Economist, qualified as a licentiate of the University of Parma (Italy) and as Economics Doctor of the University of Fribourg (Switzerland). But I have a lot of different skills, both as a ICT expert and professor and as an artist.
Just after the achievement of my Doctorate I have started working in the ITC sector where I had the opportunity to do significant educational and professional experiences. I have taught ITC to more than 2.000 persons (young people and adult, student, workers and managers) in Spain and Italy. I have conceived and direct four educational projects (Fabulando, New Fabulando, Relate your town, Libera Libertà: la donna nel ‘900) with the partnership of European Commission. I have also realized hundred sites web and
collaborated with a lot of enterprises (see: www.fabulando.net) .
From the artistic side, instead, I have written and published numerous (20) fairy-tales in Italian and Spanish.
I have experience in performing art and working together as a team with my wife I have produce various shows performed in different Italian towns (see: www.voicefusions.com).
Now we are achieve the presentation of FuturistiSiti a new spectacle concerning the Futurism (see: www.futurismo1.com/now)

Dr. Angelo Cacciola Donati’s Experience

  • Artistic Director and Manager

    FuturistiSiti by La Convivialité

    (Non-Profit; 1-10 employees; Performing Arts industry)

    August 2007 — Present (1 year 3 months)

    Artistic direction and management of artistic project concerning the Futurismo.
    After the success of the Futurist Week of Rapallo (January 23-29 th 2006), of the Futurist Days of Recco (July 2006) and of other smaller events, on the occasion of the 100° anniversary of the birth of the Futurism, the project foresees the realization of an itinerary among various places constituted by a series of events that will allow to rediscover and to relive the dimension and the artistic contents of the Futurism. What futurism refused it was the concept of an art aristocrat and decadent, confined in the museums and in the spaces of the courtly culture; in this direction, the anticipated demonstrations will be articulated so that to involve different and unusual places of the city.
