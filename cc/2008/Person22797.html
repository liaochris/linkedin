Steve Beauregard’s Summary

REGARD Venture Solutions infuses expert intellectual & technological capital to innovative entrepreneurs. We focus on emerging growth-stage technology companies that either need to transform ideas into a working proof of concept, or need to evolve a software product for investment consideration.

Steve Beauregard, President and Founder of REGARD Solutions Corp. (Santa Monica, CA.) Steve mobilized REGARD into a wireless industry leading position as charter member of RIM’s “Preferred” ISV Alliance Partner Program and one of Microsoft’s first Mobility Partners. REGARD has established a nationwide reputation as a reliable, top-tier wireless solution provider.

In 2003, Steve formed a subsidiary REGARD Solutions Pvt. Ltd (Nagpur, India) as the company’s software development arm. REGARD Solutions serves enterprise customers and other wireless product companies as their outsourced Java and .Net software development house.

REGARD’s R-Mobility solutions (R-Approval® & R-Charter®) are a family of mobility solutions that build the bridge between enterprise applications and wireless Smartphones.

Since the late 90’s, Steve has acted as the outside wireless strategist for countless “C-Level” executives of Fortune-500 and SMB Businesses. REGARD has an impressive track with marquee customers including numerous divisions of Sony, Universal, Wells Fargo and Company, Countrywide, Hughes, TRW, LIONSGATE Films, E-Entertainment, Creative Artists Agency, as well as, local, state & federal government agencies.

Steve received two Bachelor of Science degrees from Frostburg State University in Computer Science and Accounting; he further supplemented his education with Master studies in Computer Engineering at The John Hopkins University. Steve is often sought as a wireless industry expert quoted in trade publications such as Motley Fool, CRN and Entrepreneur Magazine and invited to universities and global mobility conferences as a guest speaker and panel expert.

Executive, Carrier relationship development, Major Account Development and Penetration, Business Development and Channel Development, Large Scale International Project Management, Commercial Software Product Development, Fortune 500 CIO Level Experience
