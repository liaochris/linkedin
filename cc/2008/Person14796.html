Kim Chitwood’s Summary

Voted the "Most Innovative Real Estate Company" by Inman News, Keller Williams® Realty takes a different approach, one that is built on personal touches, a professional approach and positive results. Now number 3 in the nation for number of agents affiliated, Keller Williams has become a fast-growing force in Real Estate.

Kim & Teague Chitwood utilize the latest technologies, market research and business strategies to meet your expectations. However, more importantly, we listen and that means we find solutions that are tailored to you. Whether you are buying or selling a home, we want to put your needs first and make your experience pleasant instead of stressful.

The client is our most valuable possession. Without you, we don't have a business. For this reason, we know the Realtor/Client relationship is something to be cultivated and built from the first meeting. Trust takes time. We want to prove our loyalty to you from day one and will be there ever step of the way.

We live in Coweta County and specialize in this area as well as Fayette County, Henry County, Clayton County, South Fulton, and other South Metro Atlanta areas. If you are looking in North Atlanta, we have a wonderful team that works on the Northside of town. Please call us for an agent referral to this area. We work with top notch referral agents because we want to put you in the best hands possible.

First time homebuyers are a joy to work with. We are patient and will walk you through the process. If you have questions, please feel free to call us. No obligation. If you are relocating from out of town, call me for a list of property for sale in the desired area or just to get our opinion on the best counties, school systems, etc. We'd love to talk! 770-328-9169
