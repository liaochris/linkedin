  • Managing Partner

    Nerd Search, LLC

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Staffing and Recruiting industry)

    July 2006 — Present (2 years 1 month)

    Taking what has been an eight year IT search career and the best of different recruiting and search firm models Nerd Search has been created.

    The practice has four areas of service:

    Full time permanent (contingent and retained)
    Contract/staff augmentation
    Contract recruiting: access to recruiters with proven track records
    Project help: job fairs, writing job ads, sorting resumes, setting interviews, background checks

    Aa the name would suggest the firm focuses on search in information technology, from the Help Desk to the CIO, including Business Analysts and Project Managers.

    As important is it is for the firm to generate revenue it is important for Nerd Search to give back to the community. I am exploring ways to do this including starting a nonprofit or foundation.

    To find more about Nerd Search go to www.mnheadhunter.com/nerdsearch.

  • Managing Partner

    e-Strategies Group, Inc.

    (Public Company; 1-10 employees; Information Services industry)

    April 1998 — June 2006 (8 years 3 months)

    e-Strategies group was a 3rd party IT search firm working with companies of all sizes in the Twin Cities and their offices across the country for eight years.

    The firm evolved during the eight years. From a small staff to me working on my own to a small confederation of recruiters. From dot com growth and Y2K to the recession of the early 2000's. I saw it all.

    e-Strategies Groups was folded in the summer of 2006 as the concept of what was to become Nerd Search was finally implemented.
