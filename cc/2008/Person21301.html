Richard Wilson’s Summary

I am a hedge fund consultant, guest speaker and hedge fund blogger. Every morning I write a new article in my blog on hedge funds - http://HedgeFundBlogger.com.

I work full time as a consultant for a third party marketing firm in Boston/New York and I raise assets and perform sales activities for a select group of institutional investment managers including hedge funds.

I am currently attending Harvard University earning a degree in the Psychology of Influence & Persuasion. My past education includes financial modeling and an MBA with a focus on Finance & Marketing.

I have written two books. The first was completed while earning my MBA and it is focussed on Negotiation, Relationship Cultivation and Sales (Rainmaker.ws). The second is a hedge fund blog book that is a work in progress but can be downloaded right now at http://hedgefundsbook.com.

Please visit my blog at http://hedgefundblogger.com or http://richard-wilson.blogspot.com.
