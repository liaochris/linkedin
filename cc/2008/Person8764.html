Gilli Moon’s Summary

For the past 10 years, Gilli (pronounced as with a "J"), has been one of Australia's most influential and iconic figures, forging an independent path before anyone knew what "indie" was. She has produced and released music albums of her original music, to critical acclaim, winning songwriting and artist awards, performing across the world many times, and landing songs in film and television.

Beyond being the Artist, she is a music producer, as well as an event producer (in 07 she co-founded and co-produced the inaugural Los Angeles Women's Music Festival, - over 60 artists - 3,000 visitors, raising funds for animal rescue.) Notwithstanding she has produced many corporate and rock events in her lifetime.

She is the CEO of her own record company and production house Warrior Girl Music (which is home to several internationally based artists including over 100 female artists on her compilations "Females On Fire"), and offers services in film music supervision, artist development and music production. Inspired to make a difference in the music industry for songwriters, Gilli co-founded Songsalive!, in 1997, and is now the largest internationally based non-profit songwriters organization.

Gilli has written a motivational book for artists of all genres called "I AM A Professional Artist - the Key to Survival and Success in the World of the Arts" based on her experiences in the music and general arts businesses as an artist and entrepreneur, and is currently writing her sophomore book release "Just Get Out There" due in 2008. When time permits, gilli offers artist coaching and speaks on panels about DIY, the indie movement and how to survive the business.

gilli currently lives in Southern California.

Read her expanded professional bio at http://www.warrirogirlmusic.com/gillimoon, and her artist bio at http://www.gillimoon.com/bio
