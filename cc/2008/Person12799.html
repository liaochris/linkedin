Tina Jonasen’s Summary

I am The Certified Accountant who after an "International Maverick Life" has become "The NLP Networking Business & Life Coach".

My personal background can be summarized like this:
I had my own Certified Accountant Company and worked almost 24/7.
Then I thought; Is this really the way I want to live - and die ?
Do I have to wait until I am old and all stressed up - to "slow down/retire" ?
NO WAY !!! So I sold my company and moved to Spain!
I kept some clients and an 1 employee in Denmark + worked for the TimeShare Industry - doing legal and contract work & translations, I did Real Estate, a Call Center, had girls photographing Golfers etc. - until I got divorced and was so stupid as to buy a big restaurant :-0
In 2006 I went to Holland to work with a Real Estate agent - planned to move there but NO, I couldn't settle there; brand new language (the 6 I speak is enough now I think), and NO Sunshine - so I had to move on.
Then in the beginning of 2007 I was introduced to 2 Danish investors who had a brilliant idea - of investing in inventors and their inventions; Business´ who struggled to go to market themselves.
So as they needed help to set up the marketing structure/network - I sold everything in Spain, bought a big car, in which I packed a suitcase and my 2 dogs - and drove up to my home country to work with them.
