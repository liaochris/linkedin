Gerrit Visser’s Summary

Works since June 2008 as Digital Information & Documentation Officer for ICCO/KiA, the interchurch organisation in the Netherlands for development cooperation.
A senior Information Specialist with over 25 years of experience as professional knowledge worker, starting in 1975 at KPN's Corporate Information Services (ITS). With a focus on IT, the Telecommunications industry and internet services I became an Industry watcher for KPN Research in the time of the internet boom (1998-2001).

Webmaster, projectleader Knowledge Management for KPN's Corporate Education Services. (2001-2004)

Core competences and personal skillsets are in business-intelligence, knowledge management and news provision.
