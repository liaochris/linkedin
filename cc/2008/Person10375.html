Jan Evett’s Summary

As a real estate consultant, I work with buyers, sellers, and investors from Panama City/Beaches of Bay County to Rosemary and Seaside Beaches and the Sandestin-Destin areas of South Walton County. Centrally located in Panama City Beach, I have access to multiple listing services (MLS) in Bay, Franklin, Gulf, Escambia, Santa Rosa and Walton Counties.


I specialize in residential home sales including Fine Homes and Waterfront Properties. I consider it a privilege to help people find the home that best suits their family and lifestyle.


I am active in the local real estate industry and in the local community. I hold GRI (Graduate Realtor® Institute) and e-PRO professional designations through the National Association of REALTORS®, and I've served as an ambassador for the FL Association of REALTORS®, as well as past chairman for RPAC (REALTOR® Political Action Committee) with the local Bay County Association.

I received my real estate salesperson's license in 1989 and broker's license in 2005. If you're looking for a seasoned agent who has experience in the best and the most challenging real estate markets........contact me! I'm ready to earn the right to be your real estate consultant for life!
