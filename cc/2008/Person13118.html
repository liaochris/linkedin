Jorge Restrepo’s Summary

Founder and chief analyst at Eureka Facts, the marketing research and analytics firm that serves assocaitions, nonprofits and government agencies. Areas of expertise are:
- Survey research, needs assessment program evaluation
- Marketing analytics and marketing strategy (branding, positioning, pricing, targeting)
- Advanced analytics - especially in segmentation, customer profiling and predictive modeling.
- Demographic and geodemographics

Jorge has conducted hundreds of research studies for nonprofit, government and private organizations covering a wide range of issues, populations and geographies. His skill in marketing, project management, and market analysis are strengthened by twenty years of market research and marketing operations. He has particular expertise in customer profiling, market segmentation, and effective messaging efforts.

Involved in the community, he serves on the boards of directors of the Montgomery County Chamber of Commerce, the American Youth Philharmonic Orchestras and Career Transitions Committee. He is president of the board of the Hispanic Chamber of Commerce of Montgomery County, the Hispanic Business Foundation of Maryland, and is a past chairman of the Maryland Hispanic Business Conference. He has been actively involved in the leadership of the DC Chapter of the American Marketing Association and is a member of the Marketing Research Association and the American Society of Association Executives.

Jorge holds a Masters degree in General Administration with concentration in Marketing and a Bachelors of Science degree in business with minors in economics and finance. Prior to establishing EurekaFacts, He served as Chief Marketing Officer and Market Research Director at international nonprofit organizations and international private sector conglomerates. He has written articles on marketing and received several awards and recognitions in the industry and the community.
