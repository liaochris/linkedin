Ramon Thomas’s Summary

Professional speaker with a focus on the psychology of technology and how to reduce complexity and stress as well as improving decision making. Keynote presentations cover business & technology trends, blogging, social networking and academia. One of my popular talks is delivered across South Africa to parents & teachers on the impact of technology and the development of children.

As a online researcher with ground-breaking research into the online dating, Internet Cafe industries in South Africa as well as cellphone-based instant messaging platform, MXit. I also conduct web-based competitive intelligence reports. And recently I begin offering research support for recruiters and head-hunters on using LinkedIn to find candidates.

Past experience include 7 years in Information Security in the Internet (ISP) and Bankding industries in South Africa, England and Middle East.
