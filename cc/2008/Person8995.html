Graham Sanders’s Summary

Highly creative, motivated, funny, polite, energetic, passionate, flexible, and modest professional.

A fully qualified Graphic Designer with several years commercial experience, possessing a high degree of design ability and business acumen. Dedicated, motivated and naturally inquisitive, able to demonstrate an ability to work under pressure with a flexible, enthusiastic approach. Eager to take on new challenges, learn new skills and further develop existing ones.

An open minded, creative but practical approach to design, fully aware of the need to provide innovative and effective solutions in order to meet a client’s specific design requirements, timescale and budget. Experience in a variety of design requirements including, logo design, packaging, advertising, exhibitions, livery, signage, corporate design, branding, book design, and web design.

Excellent communication, presentation and negotiating skills, evident in portfolio and in the interaction between clients, suppliers and colleagues. An excellent team player but more than capable of operating on an individual basis from brief to deadline.

Graham Sanders’s Specialties:

Highly creative, Focused and experienced team leader, Experience in systematically managing staff, budgets, schedules and several projects side-by-side, Mac and PC literate, Ability to take a brief from initial client meeting to final delivery, Fully literate in all mainstream design packages, Experienced artworker, Proven knowledge of DDA and W3C compliance, Programming capabilities
