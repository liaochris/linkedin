Amitai Givertz’s Experience

  • Principal

    AMG Management Advisors

    (Staffing and Recruiting industry)

    August 2007 — Present (1 year 3 months)

    My work involves advising clients on recruitment, talent and workforce management issues. I specialize in business development, talent management and process improvement.

    The services I provide include talent acquisition, organization and business development, and coaching. My main areas of practice are in sales, sales management and business development.
