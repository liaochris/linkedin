Jeff Roach’s Summary

Jeff Roach is one of North America's leading marketing communications experts specializing in building youthful brands. Jeff is the VP, Marketing Strategy of Y Syndicate, an innovative consumer insight and marketing communications agency collective that includes Youthography, Riot Brand Experiences and District Lifestyle Marketing. Jeff's experience is rich, previously as the VP, Client Services with experiential youth marketing firm MASEV Communications, and as COO and Co-Creative Director of marketing communications agency JWA Group. Jeff is one of a rare breed of agency executives with both creative and account backgrounds with a career start as an Art Director, and is a Communications and Advertising Accredited Professional (CAAP). Jeff is based in Toronto and manages an L.A. satellite.

Jeff Roach’s Specialties:

Marketing strategy, strategic planning, youth marketing, marketing communications planning, account planning, marketing communications execution, advertising, experiential marketing, digital/interactive, promotions, PR, word of mouth marketing, media planning, consumer insights
