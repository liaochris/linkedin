Michael Baker’s Summary

I'm a polymath. I have many and varied professional interests. These include Joint Programme Director with the College of Sustainability and Environmental Management where I also teach a courses on Life Cycle Assessment, Disaster Management & Preparedness, Management of Oil & Gas Exploration, and Energy Policy and Management in Business and manage the College's website. I have previous experience as a software engineer and project manager.

I am currently available for short term consultancy and advisory roles on Environmental Sustainability projects.

Michael Baker’s Specialties:

Environmental Sustainability, Life Cycle Assessment (LCA), Consultancy, Teaching, Project Management, Management Systems, Energy Research, Civil Engineering, Town Planning
