Erik Akopiantz’s Summary

As chief executive officer, Erik Akopiantz oversees business development, business planning and process, operational systems, and financial operations. Prior to co-founding Digital Reach, he was director of corporate development and later, director of finance for Sabre Holdings where he represented $1.5 billion in revenue. Akopiantz formerly served the executive committee of a private equity firm as consultant for three global companies with combined revenue of $65 million.

Akopiantz brings a combination of entrepreneurial, operational, strategic, and transaction experience that uniquely qualify him to lead a customer focused organization. From Fortune 500 organizations to start-ups, he has gained exposure to diverse industries, venture capital funding, rapid-growth enterprises, and a multinational publicly traded company. In all settings, he achieved success through focused leadership.

Akopiantz holds an MBA in Finance from Rice University, the Jesse H. Jones School of Administration. He was honored as a Jones Scholar, recognized as one of the top students in the graduating class, and is the recipient of a Jones Graduate School Alumni Scholarship.
