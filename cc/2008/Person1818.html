Ann Wendell’s Summary

My specialty is finding, analyzing, organizing, and presenting information in a way that maximizes its usefulness for decision makers. The value of this was demonstrated dramatically in a study conducted at five large corporations. Four out of five of the surveyed executives felt that the information provided by an information resource expert helped them decide upon a course of action. And three-quarters of them felt the information helped them avoid making a poor business decision. That's what I'm about - finding, synthesizing and using information to succeed.

We also know that one good idea can launch an industry, change a company’s direction, or position a person on a successful life path. That's also what I'm about - searching for, recognizing, and championing ideas that make the difference. As an intelligence expert I’m dedicated to finding information, turning that information into knowledge, and putting that knowledge to work – both for individuals and for companies and organizations.
