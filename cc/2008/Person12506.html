John Elder’s Summary

Licensed psychotherapist (CA MFT42730) emphasizing behavioral health care. Specializing in chemical dependency, anger management, stress management, assertive communications, emotional intelligence and mens’ issues. Focus on helping professionals, executives, veterans, and first responders overcome ineffective behaviors that can potentially sabotage careers. Certified executive coach, certified Critical Incident Stress Debriefing Level I, certified anger management facilitator and trainer. Trained in clinical hypnosis and community building. Interest in teaching, philosophy, and social justice.

John Elder’s Specialties:

Executive coaching, stress management, anger management, men's issues, couple counseling, chemical dependency, hypnosis, trauma counseling.
