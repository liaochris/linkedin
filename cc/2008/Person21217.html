Martin Weitzman’s Summary

Executive Resume Writing Credentials / Qualifications

A recognized career professional, Certified Career, Resume Writing, and Branding Coach, Marty works personally with his clients to analyze their expertise, identify key accomplishments, and develop their career documents. As a certified branding expert, your resume will maximize your assets, establish your value proposition, and position you to be selected for premier jobs. With more than 35 years as an resume development specialist, career coach, and executive recruiter, Marty understands the nuances of candidate marketing and can develop effective targeted recruiter and VC mailing campaigns that will put your resume in the hands of the decision makers.
