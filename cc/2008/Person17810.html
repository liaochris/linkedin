MikeMann.com .’s Summary

See http://mikemann.com/ -- Mike is Chairman and Founder of WashingtonVC, a fund whose portfolio of companies share resources, talent and technologies to deliver innovative digital products and services.

In '94 he founded Internet Interstate, which he sold to Verio in '97. In '98, he founded BuyDomains.com, the world's largest secondary market for domain names, and sold majority interest it to Highland Capital and Summit Partners in 2005, along with the "Seeq.com" search engine portal.

Between '98 and '01, he served as the founding Chairman of ByteBack.org (501c3), which operates free computer training centers serving inner-city communities.

Mann serves as the Founder and Chairman of Grassroots.org (a 501c3), a global network providing free services to nonprofits and promoting social action. He also manages Make Change! Trust, a charitable fund that supports select 501c3 organizations.
