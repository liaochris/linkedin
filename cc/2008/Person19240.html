Oisin Grehan’s Summary

Owner Nivot Inc, independent consultant and software developer architect; over 12 years' experience in the Web industry. Currently Microsoft MVP (2008) for Windows Server Admin Frameworks (PowerShell).

My first foray into internet technologies began in the mid-nineties, when I built Ireland’s first large-scale eCommerce system for Allied Irish Banks (known as First Trust in North America). Programming is a passion for me, and I enjoy high-pressure environments and the company of talented colleagues to inspire me.

Oisin Grehan’s Specialties:

Cross platform web & enterprise multi-tier development including but not limited to, com+/entsvcs, asp, asp.net (esp. sharepoint), winforms, remoting/wwf/wpf/indigo, soa/webservices and networks. While my primary platform expertise is with Microsoft, I am comfortable with a wide variety of unix flavours and OSS platforms.
