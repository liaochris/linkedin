Bob O'Haver’s Summary

Bob O'Haver is a technology teacher and consultant with years of related professional experience ranging from jobs as a Visual Effects Producer and Supervisor for blockbuster feature films to Web site Publisher for Creative Planet, an online community for the entertainment industry. This wide breadth of experience has given Bob a unique knowledge of the use of computers and Internet technologies. He is both a passionate and patient teacher. His teaching philosophy is one that empowers his students. As a Visual Effects Producer and Supervisor he is accomplished at creative problem solving, cooperative team building, project management and has always brought in his projects on time and on budget. Finally, Bob is a blogger and the host of the weekly technology-based radio show, "Computer Therapy" on 97.7 WBCR, Great Barrington, MA Listen to the Podcast at Apple.com search Podcast "Computer Therapy"
