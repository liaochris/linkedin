David Petherick § Digital Biographer’s Summary

► My current work is focused on adding gravity to individual and corporate footprints through online profiles.

I enable individuals, authors and executives to achieve outstanding visibility and credibility through the design and promotion of their blogs, vlogs, social network profiles and podcasts.

I call this digital biography, and I am 'The Digital Biographer', according to the BBC - http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/technology/6900376.

► I make words make sense, and am skilled in grasping and creating new ideas and strategic connections from this.

► I find partners, forge alliances, match ideas with opportunities, and am always open to new avenues for collaboration and synergy. I am an excellent writer with fluent Russian and good Spanish language expertise.

I also write for The Next Web Blog, closely associated with the Next Web Annual Conference held in April in Amsterdam, focusing on what's next on the web, with an audience of Web Startups, Influentials and VCs.

► See http://thenextweb.org
