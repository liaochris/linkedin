Chris Hartwell’s Summary

inlayout, llc

I started designing websites in 1998, and in 2002 started my own company while in graduate school at Tulane. Since my company's inception, I have completed many web site design jobs and search engine optimization jobs for small businesses and non profit organizations. I have given numerous small, local businesses a worldwide presence in the seach engines.

My work experience involves sales, customer relations, web site design and concept development, web site marketing in print, radio, and television, search engine optimization, search engine marketing, pay per click campaign management, project completion and website maintenance, as well as ongoing customer relations.

In 2003, after having great success early on with SEO, I started "The Family & Marriage Counseling Directory" as a project of my own. The directory quickly became one of the top ranked sites in Yahoo! and Google for popular search phrases like "marriage counseling," and now has over 400 advertisers.

Since then I have started numerous smaller niche sites that are now the focus of my business. I also started NewOrleansInternetMarketing.com which is a more accurate representation of what my business has evolved into over the years.


Reciprocal Consulting

My professional services are available only through Reciprocal Consulting. We are an internet marketing firm who specialize in search engine marketing and optimization. We also consult on usability and social media as well. For more information, visit www.ReciprocalConsulting.com.

My current activities involve my work with Reciprocal Consulting, and management of my own niche websites.
