Jorge Eugenio Ubeda’s Summary

Senior developer , with more than twenty years of experience in programming, design and implementation of computing systems at Latin América (Argentina, Chile, Uruguay, Paraguay) and Spain, in Logistics, Manufacturing, Retail. Having worked as auditor and being trained in Total Quality Management, the preoccupation by the quality and efficiency of information systems has taken to me to the interest by the new technologies in code generation, like Model Driven Design, Software Factories and Product Lines. I´m convinced that only using advanced techniques of development would be possible to manage the current and future challenges that modern and global business implies.
