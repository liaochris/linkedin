Carolynn Duncan’s Summary

I've recently moved from Idaho to the Seattle area to work more closely in the hi-tech, entrepreneur, and angel/venture capital networks in both Seattle and Portland.

Previously, I was the assistant managing director at the Eastern Idaho Entrepreneurial Center, helping improve the economy of Idaho by developing a capital infrastructure, facilitating technology transfer and commercialization at the Idaho National Lab, and assisting local businesses in getting to the next level.

While at the Entrepreneurial Center, I developed and managed a student consulting program, with 15 business & engineering interns, and 80 student researchers, servicing 25+ business clients per semester.

Prior to my work at the Entrepreneurial Center, I worked for four startup companies in marketing & web-based technology applications, as well as started and operated four "Hundred Dollar Business" micro-business projects.
