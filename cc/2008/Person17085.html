Matt Schlegel’s Summary

A seasoned high tech leader with over 20 years of telecommunications and technology experience, Matt focuses on finding clients the best solutions for their communications needs. Located in the San Francisco Bay Area, Matt is proud to be a member of the Schooley Mitchell team, delivering solutions to meet the unique telecom needs of business clients.

Schooley Mitchell Telecom Consultants is the largest independent telecom consulting company in North America, with offices from coast to coast. Schooley Mitchell Telecom Consultants deliver unique telecommunications expertise to companies large and small and from all industries. We offer a broad range of services that include analysis of existing and future telecommunications needs, assessment of best alternatives and implementation of cost-effective telecommunications solutions. The company is fully independent of all telecommunications providers.

With bachelor’s and master’s degrees in engineering, Matt has worked as an engineer and a manager for California-based telecom companies in the video-satellite, video-conferencing, and cellular technologies markets. Fluent in Japanese, Matt was a research fellow at Tokyo University and worked for a Tokyo telecommunications equipment company.

For more details, please see www.schooleymitchell.com/mschlegel
