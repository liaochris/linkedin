10,000 Little(micro) Ideas to Keep You Believing in Cleveland’s Summary

10,000 Little (micro) Ideas to Keep You Believing in Cleveland was started by a small group of Emerging Leaders with a vision to provide an outlet for residents to share and implement ideas to improve the Region. The organization focuses on Idea-Initiatives like: Nonprofit, Wind Turbine, High School Apprentice Program, Community Lighting, Technology Entrepreneurship, 10,000 Movement.


We are leveraging LinkedIn and Facebook to connect our members to our organization and to each other.
