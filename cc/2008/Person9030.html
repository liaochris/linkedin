Stephen Aguilar-Millan’s Summary

Our main focus is to identify future business trends and to assist our clients in becoming future ready. We tend to work with individuals and small teams, although we are also called upon by larger organisations from time to time. Our current interests are geopolitical futures and globalisation, and how they apply at an individual level.

Our areas of specialisation are financial futures, strategic futures, and the future of the organisation. To this, we add a blend of skills that allow our clients to project the right business structure, into the right market, with the right business model, at the right time.

Stephen Aguilar-Millan’s Specialties:

Over the years, we have built up a certain amount of expertise in the area of small business, entrepreneurship, and business building. We operate in a global environment, and we have clients from all over the globe.
