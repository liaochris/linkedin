Stéphane de Luca’s Summary

Bio: In May 2007 I have joined the startup MobiLuck.com as the Chief Technology Officer (CTO) which is focused on geolocalized social network for mobile phones. This LBS friend finder service has attracted more than 150K users since its launch in August 2007 and currently recruits an average of 1000 users per day as per the current trend. Our technology is carrier-independant and the service works worldwide as it is available in more than 10 languages.

Before and for about 4 years, I was the In-Fusio/MobileScope's Advanced Technology Manager where I created mobile phone massive cross-platform multi-player technology and service for the Group.

Before, I've been in the video game industry for 10 years where I successively developed titles, created and sold realtime 3D technologies and, finally, took in charge one of the business unit of Kalisto Entertainment to handle two 4M€-budget games.
