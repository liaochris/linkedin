Buhle Dlamini’s Summary

Buhle is the founder and managing member of Young & Able Youth Advisory Services. His experience in the area of youth development has enabled him to offer a wide range of services to youth and young entrepreneurs. His previous involvement in youth development includes serving with SOS Mission Team in South Africa, Youth For Christ Team in Germany, UK and USA. He also served as National/Territorial Director of Youth Ministries in the Youth Department of The Salvation Army. He has served as a National Corporate Liaison for The Salvation Army in public relations and fund raising. Added to this is his involvement as board member for the Edge Consulting (2000-2001), and the Mass Media Project (2002-2004). He is currently a Project Manager for Television Drama for The Mass Media Project Heartlines. He is a well sought after business speaker and consultant (an associate of TomorrowToday.biz) having spoken to numerous business audiences in South Africa and abroad. Buhle is also an author of Youth Devotional Books (Godly Generation 1 and 2), and his new book- 'Young and Able' and has developed training programmes for young people.
