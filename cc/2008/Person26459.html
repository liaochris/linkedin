Vander Fujisaki’s Summary

After spend all my childhood playing videogames, arcades, and reading comics, i can say without fear that one of my dreams have been true: work in what i love. I started making amateur fanzines, and writing in websites about manga and videogames. Shortly after that i did the jump to the professional press, and i think that i´ll be in that world forever.

I love to write, but i love to be with a good team too. I work with a team of freelance collaborators since 2000, and we are like a family.

Im specialized in videogames, manga, comics, japanese music, movies, and television.

In 2007 i wrote a book "Viaje por la diosa", initially like a personal gift, and later commercialized by Lulu.com.

Vander Fujisaki’s Specialties:

I write about Videogames, Manga, Movies, TV, and Comics. Also, im in love with Japan, and some of my travels to this country have been narrated in form of adventure blogs.
