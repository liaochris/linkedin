Jennifer (Jenny) Tucker’s Summary

Jennifer Tucker (Consulting Director, OKA) is an experienced group facilitator and trainer, management consultant, and project manager. Past and current work includes facilitating technical and scientific work groups, conducting leadership development and teambuilding workshops, leading organizational assessments, and managing public outreach and communication projects.

Jenny is the author of the booklet "Introduction to Type and Project Management," part of the Introduction to Type Series from CPP, Inc.

Jennifer (Jenny) Tucker’s Specialties:

Organization development, workgroup facilitation, leadership development and training, teambuilding workshops, management consulting, strategic planning, project management

Certified Project Management Professional (PMP) 2005

Qualified administrator of the Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI), the Strength Deployment Inventory (SDI), the Leadership Spectrum Profile (LSP), the Work Environment Scales (WES), Reversal Theory / Apter Motivational Styles Profile (AMSP), and FIRO-B.

Jennifer (Jenny) Tucker’s Experience

  • Consulting Director

    OKA (Otto Kroeger Associates)

    (Management Consulting industry)

    April 2006 — Present (2 years 8 months)

    Founded in 1977, OKA is a training and consulting firm specializing in leadership and team development. Expert in a variety of psychological instruments and group processes, OKA offers high-impact leadership and team development programs, and a variety of instrument qualification workshops. Jenny currently leads OKA's consulting practice. Recent projects have included:

    * Strategic planning workshops with both government and commercial clients.
    * Design and delivery of "Critical Conversations: Delivering Effective Feedback" workshops.
    * An organization culture assessment and leadership development workshops with a non-profit association.
    * MBTI and SDI team development workshops with several IT project teams.
    * Teambuilding with senior leadership teams in the public and private sector.
    * Ongoing management consulting with a large NIH biomedical informatics program.

  • Associate/Sr. Associate

    Booz Allen Hamilton

    (Partnership; 10,001 or more employees; Management Consulting industry)

    October 2001 — April 2006 (4 years 7 months)

    While with Booz Allen, Jenny first served as a Program and Project Manager in the Information Technology Team; and then in the Organization Change team. Primary clients were in Federal government and included OPM, EPA, Census Bureau, and DoD. Example projects included:

    * Organization assessments for a large DoD program office.
    * Group facilitation and focus groups to support DoD information technology policy program.
    * IT project management for projects at OPM and Census Bureau
    * Leadership of a personality/team dynamics research project for Defense Acquisition University.
    * Facilitation of scientific and emergency response policy teams at EPA.
