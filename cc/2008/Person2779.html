Gabriele Gresta’s Summary

I started working at a very young age in the software field. By age 15, I was Software Development Director in a multinational company (Alpha Center). At 16 I created the first super-learning system with various technical internships in the UK, US and Australia.
Discovered by Marco Biondi (Radio Dj), I unleashed my musical creativity with the Mato Grosso digital dance music project, that hits the first position of the Italian Dance Chart bringing me on a international tour for 11 monts. In five years Mato Grosso hit N°1 several times with a total production of 21 records and one "All the best" album.
I then moved on to television as host, author and producer of hit programs on national channels such as MTV Italy and TMC2 and coordinated Telecom Italia's special events strategy.
In 1995 I founded Bibop Research Int, a cross-media agency that creates interactive events and projects such as the Mediaset portal, the Tv format "2008" for Italia1, Futurshow for Telecom Italia, Tim Tour, and the I-Tv Alice Live for Telecom Italia.
In 2003, I founded the Digital Magics Group, a digital media company focused on the conception, production and distribution of interactive cross-media contents, events and original formats for different technological platforms.
Having gained extensive experience and expertise in all aspects of content production, I'm the Group’s Chief Content Officer, the strategist behind the Digital Magics product offer.

I'm more specifically involved in viral projects, participative TV and new TV formats for the various platforms, working closely with key Italian and international broadcasters and phone operators (H3G, Telecom, Vodafone, Rai, Sky) . “Occhio alla spesa” on Rai TV, for example, is the first TV show where the audience interacts with a studio host via mobile video-phones.

I'm also the inventor of MyCast, a patented web, mobile and television platform that offers video-participative services to Editors (web, telcos and broadcasters).
