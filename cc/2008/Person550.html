    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    H2H Interactive Inc. is Ad Operations service for publishers, agencies and advertisers. Our services include trafficking, third party adserving, data analysis/advanced reporting, advanced flash ad creation and publisher adserving plateform.

    We are the exclusive distributors & technical support for SMART AdServer in Canada. The Smart AdServer plateform supplies innovative technological adserving solutions to interactive advertising users. With more than 80 customers on 4 continents, SMART AdServer is a key player in the online marketing market.

    Our success is based on 2 key axes: innovation and service.

    1. Innovation: Video, Rich Media, Real time, Geotargeting… SMART AdServer is constantly enriching its offer with new formats and functions.

    2. Service: we support your teams in their daily work so that you get the most out of our solutions.
