Ed Eliot’s Summary

Web Development Manager and Web Developer with many years professional experience in several industry sectors.

Focused on web standards based frontend development with experience in developing high traffic sites including the Yahoo! front pages in Europe and the Kelkoo shopping site. A solid understanding of internationalisation and localisation issues.

Experience of helping to grow a world class web development team. In depth involvement in Yahoo! web development recruitment processes at all stages.

Management of technical teams, (X)HTML, CSS, JavaScript (with an emphasis on Progressive Enhancement), PHP, MySql, Web Standards, Accessibility, SEO. Previously worked with TCL, ASP, Sql Server and other proprietary technologies.
