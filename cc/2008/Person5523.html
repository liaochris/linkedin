Dante Lee ’s Summary

Dante Lee is the 26-year old president and CEO of Diversity City Media, a multicultural marketing and public relations firm based in Columbus, Ohio. Founded in the year 2000, this award-winning company helps Fortune 500 advertisers reach African American consumers online.

The company is highly regarded in the multicultural marketing industry, and has annual billings of more than $500,000. Their long-time clients include Nationwide Insurance, Choice Hotels, McDonald's, BET, NASCAR, Verizon, Nissan, and TV One.

Diversity City Media produces several online properties that are used as tools for their clients, including:

+ BlackNews.com
+ BlackStudents.com
+ BlackHistory.com
+ HBCUconnect.com (partnership with Moss Interactive)

+ BlackPR.com
+ BlackExperts.com
+ BlackSpeakers.com

In addition to running Diversity City Media, Lee is also the author of "How To Think Big...When You're Small" and "The 2007-2009 African American Scholarship Guide". His latest book, "Diversity-Preneur: How To Enhance Your Company Through Diversity", is due out in late 2008.

Lee also serves as a motivational speaker and diversity consultant. He has addressed several groups including the African American Business Summit (AABS), the Tavis Smiley Leadership Institute, and the National Association of Black Telecommunications Professionals (NABTP).

Lee was born and raised in the Washington, DC area. He graduated from Bowie State University with a Bachelors degree in Computer Science, and was the only person in the institution's 100-year history to graduate from a 4-year program, in three years.

He currently resides in the Los Angeles area, but runs his company from Columbus, Ohio.
