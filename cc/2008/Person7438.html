Eric Wyatt’s Summary

Life Insurance. It is one of those things we tend to put off. It is like we are admitting we might one day die if we think about it too long...or maybe we have some basic coverage, but we've failed to review it regularly to see if it still meets our current needs.

Of course, not having adequate insurance can be a strain if we get sick, can't work, are hospitalized, become disabled, or pass away unexpectedly.

That's why I'm here: To help you plan for your financial security in the future, while protecting your loved ones in the present.

I'm proud to be affiliated with AIG - American General, one of the leaders in providing life insurance. We are opening a brand new office in Kokomo in the coming months, so AIG may be new to you, but the company has been around since the early 1900's, and is consistently rated one of the top insurance carriers in the nation.

In addition to my insurance endeavors, I have experience in political campaigning, public administration, public policy, public relations, fund raising, planned giving, and media production (print, radio, TV).

I am a writer of fiction, non-fiction, and the occasional verse.

I also enjoy photography and other creative arts.
