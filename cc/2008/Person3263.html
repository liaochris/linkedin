Brett Anderson’s Summary

Brett Anderson has been working in the field of Graphic Design for over 4 years. He has a knowledge of Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Dreamweaver, Modo 301, and Vue 5. He has worked for a biomedical company to produce catalogs and marketing literature. Has worked as a freelance artist for a casino game. For the second time he is the Layout Editor for the school yearbook. Recently he has been working on the book series: Maps for Modern Magellans™, doing all of the graphic design and layout work for the book.

He is also very involved with traditional art. Using such mediums as colored pencil, charcoal, pastel, graphite, scratchboard, and chalk. He has won many awards for his artwork. One in particular, he entered the John Wayne Airport Contest, winning 1st for his grade level, then 3rd in state, 2nd in the nation, and finally 2nd out of 16 other countries in the international contest.

Brett is also very interested in many other forms of art such as photography and t-shirt making. He has been making his own t-shirts for the past year and doesn’t wear any other t-shirt than the ones that he makes.
