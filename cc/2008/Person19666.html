Patricia Howitt’s Summary

A lifetime of creating Art - producing, exhibiting and selling works "on the sidelines" while in fulltime employment for years as a government lawyer, mainly in the medico-legal field..Works held in private collections in Wellington, Auckland, Whangarei, Kerikeri, Hamilton, Bay of Plenty, Christchurch, Ashburton, Dunedin - New Zealand;
Queensland and New South Wales - Australia;
California, Carolina. Washington - USA;
Canada; France.

Ten years freelancing on the Internet in graphics and webdsign, using Photoshop, Illustrator, Fireworks, some Flash. I do have Dreamweaver but I prefer working straight in the HTML and CSS.

Patricia Howitt’s Specialties:

I have a real flair for customizing image-based website templates, being able to work directly with the graphics, and the HTML and CSS.

    Solicitor and Advocate - NZ Customs, Ministry of Transport, Accident Compensation Corporation. Legal advice on all aspects of Departmental activities. Appeal Authority advocate throughout the Hamilton / Thames / Tauranga / Whakatane / Rotorua / Taupo / Taumarunui area. Responsible for Review hearings in the region, conducted in person, or under my supervision by a Review Officer who was a qualified solicitor, and for all loan securities and debt recovery.
