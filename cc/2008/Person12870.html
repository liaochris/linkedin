Jonathan McClintic’s Summary

I am a 3D Modeler and Artist. I received my BS in Game Art & Design from the Art Institute of California, San Diego. I am coming from 6 years in the Graphic Design industry when I worked with companies like Pfizer, Copley Press, Isis Pharmaceuticals, and DivX Networks, among many others. Before that I received my BA in Art from Western Michigan University. I decided to switch to Game creation because I feel I am better at drawing then fonts and am fascinated with animation and video games.

3D artist, 3D modeling, Maya, 3D Studio Max, Mudbox, ZBrush, 2D concepting, Texture painting, PhotoShop, Illustrator, InDesign, UV mapping, Normal Mapping, Art Team Lead, Coloring, Presentations, Writing, Sales, and making people laugh.
