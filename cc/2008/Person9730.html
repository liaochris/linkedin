Scott Allen’s Summary

Scott is the CEO of Hybrid6 Studios, an internet marketing and web design firm. A hybrid of marketer, programmer, and designer, he thrives on all aspects of internet marketing and web development. Having been involved in search engine marketing and web design since 1996, he and his team consistently achieve top search engine rankings for clients in competitive markets, and have won several web design awards along the way.

Scott authors the WebGeek blog, where he shares insights on SEO and web design issues. He also developed the popular WP-SpamFree anti-spam plugin for WordPress, and is a contributing blogger on Search Engine Guide.

Hybrid6's design team specializes in creating and marketing web sites that require high-energy design, including action sports, swimwear/fashion, rock music and more. They also have an uncanny ability to switch gears and create clean corporate sites.

Scott's extensive background includes working for Fortune 500 companies including MetLife, followed by heading the web design team at an action sports ad agency in Orange County, CA where he increased the web department revenue by 900%. Previously he served as Marketing Director for a fitness equipment company where he increased the revenue by 1200% in just 2 months through a strategic multi-pronged internet marketing campaign.

Having designed and marketed everything from web sites to snowboards, Scott has a versatility that's hard to find. Not only is he a strategic internet marketing expert, but he also is a high-energy graphic designer and a pro with web programming.

Scott's business card design for Hybrid6 is featured in the book, Best of Business Card Design 8, which includes an elite collection from top designers around the world.

If you need to improve your site's internet presence, including ranking in search engines, or you need a fresh new web site designed, Hybrid6 Studios will partner with you to create and implement the perfect solution for your business.
