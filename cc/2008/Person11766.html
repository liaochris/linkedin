Jill Balkus Anderson’s Summary

Introducing Jill Lynn Design—your source for sleek + lively designs that captivate, entertain, and get noticed in print and on the web. Whether it's book covers, book interiors, packaging, catalogs, promotions, websites or the like, Jill will bring your vision to life.

THE CREATIVE PROCESS
Communicate > Research > Conceptualize > Design > Refine > Deliver

First, we'll delve into your business, target clients, competition, and the style and impression you want to evoke. Most importantly, we'll define the goal of the project. Next comes the research part where I glean inspiration from design annuals, award-winning work, your industry's current materials, and so forth. Now it's time for conceptualization through brainstorming and sketching followed by execution of the design and presentation. With your feedback, we'll revise and refine the design, and Jill Lynn Design will deliver a final project that meets (and exceeds!) the goals of the project.

Learn more about how design can enhance your business. Visit JillLynnDesign.com for design articles, resources, link and to sign-up for my free newsletter.
