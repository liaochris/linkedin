Wendy Shepherd’s Summary

I have worked for about 11 years on the internet. I create and run many websites. I have worked as an Affiliate Manager / Marketer, put the local monthly newspaper online, and have done website design & banner ad creatives for companies on the internet, plus more! I do all aspects of my website promotion to increase traffic and I send out newsletters each week.

NEW! * OfficiallyPluggedIn.com is my new project. It showcases videos and mini interviews of REAL PEOPLE.

NEW! * Nookling.com is to allow folks to have at least a one-page nook online. An official webpage that can link to their social network accounts, etc.

MovieVine.com - offers movie news, movie reviews, and allows people to sign up to find movie buddies. It is an open site, not requiring membership. I am looking for people to offer suggestions, or input movie news from the industry. I can assign logins to those who are interested.

TipzTime.com was my first site created, which is a home and garden website.

ChartJungle.com is an educational type website, with free printables.

Dreamish.com which was created to help people be inspired to fulfill their dreams. I have some interviews up and continually look for people who would like to be interviewed.

Other sites can be found at WendyShepherd.com

I am open to ideas.

(I have been interviewed by The Emmitsburg Dispatch newspaper, and featured in Revenue Magazine in 2004 as one of “Five Who Thrive” and again in a follow up interview in 2006. I've been interviewed for an article in the Wall Street Journal and for a book. I plan to write a book about my progress and success to help encourage my children and others.)
