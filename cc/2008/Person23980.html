Stan Relihan’s Summary

I am President & CEO of Expert Executive Search, Asia Pacific's best-connected and most influential Search, Selection & Recruitment firm.

With a background in Advertising & Media Production, I was privileged to be involved in the genesis of Pay TV, Broadband Internet and Mobile Telecommunications in Australia.

Over the past decade, I’ve leveraged this knowledge & experience to establish an enviable track record of success in assisting startups, blue chip multinationals, and leading local & regional players with their Human Capital requirements.

This success has come as a result of my unique ability to understand the needs & motivations of both Client and Candidate - and then to match the appropriate Skills, Desires, Personalities & Cultures in a way that maximises potential synergies for both parties.

I enjoy meeting people - and my philosophy involves developing relationships with smart, ambitious & successful individuals who share my interests and passions - well in advance of requirement.

As a result, I’ve developed an extensive network of friendships and business alliances across the Asia Pacific region and around the world.

For International Corporations seeking to establish operations in Asia Pac, or Australian Employers that truly believe that people are their greatest asset – Expert Executive Search is able to discreetly run rings around any and all competitors in Precision and Speed of Response - as well as Candidate Pedigree, Motivation and Cultural Alignment.

Feel free to contact me with your next Retained Search or Senior-level Recruitment requirement – and put us to the test.

I can be contacted at srelihan@gmail.com
