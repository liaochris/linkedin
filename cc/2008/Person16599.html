Mark Montgomery’s Summary

Mark Montgomery is a caring, compassionate educator with deep experience in higher education, classroom teaching, and college administration. He is a college insider who uses his knowledge of schools and empathy for students to help young people decide for themselves their educational journey.

Mark Montgomery’s Specialties:

College counseling, admissions consulting, higher education administration, curriculum development, instructional design, project planning, creation of foreign exchange programs, teaching, education policy, textbook analysis.
