Secure Relations’s Summary

The IT Security Suite Network ~TITSSN~ is a technology leader specializing in IT services and support in the US. We are a Managed Security Services Provider/Value Added Reseller offering information technology hardware and software sales, services and support for all levels of consumers, small/medium businesses, corporate and enterprise clients.

TITSSN is a valuable resource driving our security governance, guidance and counseling initiative filled with great benefits and networking for all members. Our unique network provides a one stop resource for the IT Security Threats Landscape (sales, services, support, training, education and awareness) for everyone.

Tapping into these resources will be of value in all levels of IT as we try to address the issues and concerns of the IT Security Threats Landscape and its effects. We are Security - your Security – our Security - IT Security. Our Security is Safe and Secure.

TITSSN, is actively looking for new information technology vendors to be a part of our elite network. As we are expanding into new areas across the country and our portfolio of services are growing, the need for new technology, products and services warrants new partnerships and alliances to stay current and ahead of the IT Security Threats Landscape and its effects. Our meetings, events and public outreach programs are geared towards elevating the awareness and education of the public at large to the IT Security Threats Landscape and its effects on consumers and businesses. We provide services to everyone from the consumer to the enterprise and across all vertical markets.

Local chapters are located in NYC, Washington DC, Jacksonville NC and New Jersey. New chapters on the radar for 2008’ – Charlotte NC, Pennsylvania, Denver/Colorado, New Orleans and Virginia.

For more information please visit our website at http://titssn.net or contact us by phone at (718) 928-6516.
