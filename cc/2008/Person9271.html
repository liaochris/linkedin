Estela Haese’s Summary

Estela T. Haese brings a wealth of experience with her; a practitioner in real estate in San Antonio since 1978, a broker/owner from 1980 to 1989, a senior member for the Residential Sales Council and home builder and developer of Scenic Oaks Subdivision.

When you hire Estela T. Haese, you get the benefit of her knowledge and judgment. She is eager to share her experience with clients as well as with fellow Realtors.

To paraphrase Will Rogers, “Estela T. Haese knows what she is doing, she loves what she is doing and she believes in what she is doing."

Estela Haese’s Specialties:

Buyer and seller residential real estate representation, military relocation, first time home buyers, real estate investment, excellent negotiator, fluently bilingual (Spanish and English).
