Brian O'Neill’s Summary

I am a skilled user-experience professional with a strong design, development, and business background. Before I began consulting and playing music full time in 2007, I directed the design group for Eons.com, a new "boomer-generation" startup led by Monster.com's Jeff Taylor. Some of my current projects include:

* Consulting at TheUpDown.com (social networking for investors), http://theupdown.com
* Consulting at My Happy Planet (connecting language learners across the globe) http://myhappyplanet.com
* Recent work: planetrewardsinc.com, MITRE corp, frenchlib.org redesign

What do consult on?

I provide design and user experience guidance/management to my clients and execute on projects as needed at various places in the product development cycle. Sometimes this is broad level strategy work; other times, I am seeing projects through from concept through code.

My goals as a designer:

* Design useful, usable and, beautiful software
* Help businesses with their online strategy and the balance of buisness goals with user experience
* Combine agile / rapid development methods with user-centered design
* Act as a bridge between product people and software engineers
* Apply XHTML/CSS (Web Standards) and encourage accessibility awareness
* Get stuff done fast. Execution matters more than Ideas.
* Use open source when possible. Contribute back.
* Hire smart, diverse, well-rounded people over Experts
