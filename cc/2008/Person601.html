Arn Kashino’s Summary

Arn has over 15 years of experience in the information technology consulting industry including 9 years implementing Workbrain, PeopleSoft, SAP, and Oracle applications. Arn has extensive experience in the analysis, design, development and implementation of information systems technology with a focus on project risk management, project management, planning and strategy, business process re-design, systems analysis, systems design, data warehousing, and quality assurance

Arn’s information technology background is extensive, ranging from Engagement Manager overseeing project delivery, Project Manager managing projects, Functional Lead, Project Testing Lead, Business Process Consultant, and Risk Mitigation Specialist on some of Canada’s largest IT projects.

Arn’s strong project management, oversight, and leadership skills have enabled him to effectively lead teams to complete their deliverables on time and within budget. His functional and technical skills have enabled him to successfully research, analyze, design, and support systems to meet customer requirements.

He has been a strong leader, working with Business Development teams, guiding successful License and Service pursuits to a successful close.
