Sridhar Turaga’s Summary

Sridhar is a founder of AeNwis, a new Indian consumer oriented internet business, launched end of 2007. www.open2save.in is one of the first services launched by AeNiws. open2save is India's largest aggregator of consumer deals.

Prior to this, he was one of the founders of iSeva (www.iseva.com) - rated one of the top BPO companies in India by clients and employees - and helped manage the transition into e4e after the merger. At e4e he was the Vice President of Global Accounts and executive lead for the onDemand services business strategy (www.e4e.com. He continues as an adviser at e4e). Prior to starting iSeva, Sridhar worked for Deloitte Consulting in New York and TELCO in Jamshedpur.

Sridhar lives in Bangalore and writes at sridharturaga.blogspot.com. He holds an MBA from IIM Calcutta and B.Tech from IIT Madras @ Chennai.
