Andrew Barden’s Summary

What Andrew Barden Can Do for You:

Andrew can help you avoid the wasted time, wasted money, intense frustration, and hopeless confusion you experience when you ineffectively market your small business.

Andrew Barden is adept at helping small business owners understand the psychology of their ideal clients/customers: why their ideal client will buy what they are offering. With a thorough & accurate profile of your ideal client guiding your strategy, all of your marketing campaigns, online and offline, will produce a significantly higher ROI.

After working with Andrew, you'll be able to make marketing and sales process decisions with confidence and clarity, knowing exactly how to send the right message to the right people in the right way. Visit http://www.AndrewBarden.com to sign up for a free initial one-on-one small business diagnostic coaching session.

Andrew is also a gifted ad copy writer. He can review your marketing campaigns to make sure how you design them, what images you use, and, moreover, what words you use will motivate your prospects to take the action you request. There are proven formats that consistently yield the highest ROI, and Andrew can show you how to implement those principles.

Andrew takes the time to stay on top of all the latest traditional marketing and internet marketing strategies that are producing the best results for small business owners. Andrew lives & breathes marketing & sales strategies, & it shows as his clients leave his office with confident smiles & deeper pockets.

Andrew leads training events in Los Angeles on topics like "Which Business Should I Start?," "How to Write a Business Plan," and "Target Marketing."

Read "Andrew Barden's Small Business Marketing Strategy" blog here: http://andrewbarden.wordpress.com where you can also read more about Andrew's history as a successful entrepreneur. Feel free to invite Andrew to connect with you here on LinkedIn as he is an "open networker."

Andrew Barden’s Specialties:

Start ups, business model proof of concept/feasibility studies, target marketing, sales process strategy, psychological profile of ideal customer/client & catering the marketing message & sales process to fit that customer's natural tendencies. Result: decrease in marketing costs & increase in ROI. Adept at understanding "why people buy" & developing marketing strategies that allow those who are most likely to buy hear your message-- sales strategies that are most effective at closing the sale.
