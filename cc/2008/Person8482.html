Gary Davis’s Summary

Led or participated in interdisciplinary teams for the design of major features and services. Provided User Interaction Design support including competitive benchmarking, workflow analysis, architecture, graphics and nomenclature, usability test planning and reporting, wire-frames, storyboards, style guides and detailed specifications.

* Designed print drivers that improved success rates over 200% on key tasks and were adopted as the company-wide design standard.
* Increased reseller interest in Xerox equipment over competitors by devising a complete end-to-end system to direct sales of supplies to the original reseller.
* Enhanced usability and customer satisfaction by unifying several divergent authentication, accounting and access schemes into a single coherent design.
* Led an interdisciplinary team in the creation of a ground-breaking new walk-up network email service that was adopted throughout the product line.
* Improved usability and simplified the workflow for the Build Job feature on Xerox products, improving success rates in excess of 50% and productivity more than 20%.
* Provided a simple solution to accommodate both novice and advanced users for the Color Balance feature UI.
* Increased the collection of revenue over $1M per year through redesign of the billing meters UI and establishment of coherent nomenclature across devices.
* Successfully scaled UI architecture across various display sizes and resolutions while maintaining a common look and feel.
