Steve Newcomb’s Summary

We just sold Powerset, my last company, to Microsoft for about $100 million and that makes my total on exits now a little over $2.4 billion. Now that I have been a part of 5 successful startups, I am concentrating more on creating a portfolio of startups that I either found or lead as the chairman of the board. My goal is to take what I have learned over the years and help as many young entrepreneurs as I can. Keep a lookout on my blog, www.blognewcomb.com for updates.

I currently hold the following board positions.

Virgance - Chairman of the Board
Serious Business - Chairman of the Board
SF Green - Chairman of the Board

Also, I am actively working on the policy committee at the SF Chamber of Commerce. I am proud to announce that we just got both of the initiatives that I was working on for San Francisco passed on the June ballot. They are the Green Campus Initiative and the Solar Incentive Program Initiative.

thanks
Steve.
