Mark Taylor’s Summary

Mark A. Taylor, MBA, DLP, with three decades of experience consulting thousands of organizations, including Unisys, Ford, General Electric, the United States Postal Service, DHL, and Standard Register Corporation, is considered to be the nation’s authority on increasing profit and productivity in shipping operations. He has been featured as an industry expert on ABC News and in the New York Times, and is the author of Computerized Shipping Systems: Increasing Profit & Productivity Through Technology. Taylor has been named a Distinguished Logistics Professional (DLP) by the American Society of Transportation & Logistics in recognition of his 30 year, career-long contributions to the field of logistics.

Taylor is in demand as a speaker at major industry events, including the Parcel Shipping Forum, the National Conference on Operations & Fulfillment, the National Postal Forum, the Council of Logistics Management, Mailcom, and the Parcel Logistics Expo. He writes a monthly column for PARCEL magazine, and is a frequent contributor to numerous industry publications, including Operations and Fulfillment, MAIL, and Frontline Solutions magazines.
