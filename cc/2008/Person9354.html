Philip Edward Alexy’s Summary

I have over 13 years of experience in both major film and television post-production projects, including "Charlotte's Web", "Star Wars, Episode One", "Casper", "Flubber", "Angela Anaconda" and "Stargate SG-1". I have worked in both large and small studio facilities and have seen all aspects of CG animation, from bidding, pre-production, team building, production crunches, and post-production PR.
