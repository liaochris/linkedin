Gautam Guliani’s Summary

Gautam Guliani is a seasoned technology executive with over 17 years of experience developing web-based solutions to business problems in media, finance and education areas. He currently works as Vice President, Research & Development at iVillage, a division of NBC Universal.

At iVillage, Gautam is responsible for researching new technology trends and developing new technology platforms.

Before iVillage, Gautam served as chief software architect at Kaplan Test Prep & Admissions and was responsible for technical design of all enterprise systems, integration amongst them and for guiding and governing all aspects of enterprise architecture for all of KTPA's systems. Before KTPA, Gautam served as software architect and head of development at Fortunecity.com, developing web applications, systems and data integration solutions in the managed web hosting space. Previously, Gautam has worked on web-based systems at Citibank, AOL/Time Warner, The New York Times, TheStreet.com, Audible, and at Grassroots Technologies Inc, a professional services company he co-founded.

Gautam specializes in promoting and managing effective use of mature open source technologies in the enterprise and is the author of a book on the subject with Oreilly. More info at http://www.oreilly.com/catalog/opensourceent/

Gautam lives, works and plays in New York City. He blogs about life, universe and other things at http://gautam.guliani.net/ and in his spare time desperately seeks ways to not refer to himself in third person.
