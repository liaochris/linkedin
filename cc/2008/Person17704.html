Michael Scaramozzino’s Summary

Digital media generalist running the award-winning interactive multimedia studio DreamLight.com for over twenty years. Constantly striving to push the limits of digital media in all DreamLight's work, for both client projects and in-house projects. Expanding DreamLight's in-house projects to build new digital assets and original online content in 3D animation and interactive multimedia.

Michael Scaramozzino’s Specialties:

3D Modeling, 3D Rendering, 3D Animation, Interactive Multimedia, LightWave 3D, modo, Adobe (Macromedia) Director, Programming
