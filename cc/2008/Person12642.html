Ioannis John Neocleous’s Summary

Ioannis John Neocleous is the founder and Managing Partner of NCI Law Group, Neocleous & Neocleous law firm and NCI Finance Group. Ioannis has over 14 years of professional experience in the areas of international tax, corporate and criminal law, both as an academic and also as a practitioner in over 50 countries worldwide.

Ioannis John Neocleous’s Specialties:

Contract negotiation, company registration, corporate tax structuring, personal tax advise, litigation, criminal litigation, collections
