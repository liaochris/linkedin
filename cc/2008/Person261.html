Abraham Lloyd’s Summary

I am a passionate, driven, and energetic individual looking to leverage eight years of development and management experience in challenging new environments. I have excellent communication skills, a committed belief that team dynamics are critical to success, and am seeking opportunities that leverage my skills while providing me new growth opportunities in business creation, software architecture and development, and project management disciplines.

  • Application Architect and Developer

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

  • Senior Developer
