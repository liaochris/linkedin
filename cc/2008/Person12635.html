John M. Muraski’s Summary

Praxilient provides people and organizations with structured ways to realize opportunities, solve problems and manage efficiently through:

• Process Management, which is really about "doing things more effectively" leading to "organizational transformation";
• Project Management, which is about "choosing well and executing well"; and
• Control Management, which is really about "achieving a competitive advantage through risk management, governance, and compliance".

Praxilient provides solutions, staffing and training in each of these disciplines.
