Cristian Sturek’s Summary

Founder and Principal SOA Architect of XWebServices, Inc., a boutique SOA Consulting firm based in Los Angeles. Have over 12 years Enterprise Architecture (EA) experience primarily in the Financial Services and Health Care fields.

Developed SOA principles and best practices that guide planning, development, integration and management of application infrastructures. Successfully architected AND implemented Service Oriented Architectures for companies such as:
- First Data Corporation – formerly a Fortune 500 company now privately owned, a leading provider of electronic commerce and payment solutions for businesses worldwide
- Alliance Payment Technologies – an ACH Gateway provider, with numerous banks and financial institutions in the United States as its clients
- SCAN Health Plan – a "Medicare Advantage" HMO based in Southern California, one of the original four Social HMOs in the United States
- First American Corporation – a Fortune 200 company, America's largest provider of business information and real estate related services

Also held technical leadership positions at AFX, a medical billing solutions provider, Artful Style, an online art gallery, Realtor.com, the largest provider of real estate listings in the United States, IXL Inc. and Genesis 2000. Served in the United States Marine Corps and was honorably discharged.

Strongly believes that all SOA Architects should have a strong understanding of the enterprise from the business perspective. Expertise is in Financial Services (EFT, Transaction Processing, Accounting, Risk Management, Loss Prevention), Healthcare, E-Commerce, Real Estate, Mortgage Banking and CRM. Areas of technical expertise include Enterprise SOA (Service Oriented Architecture), Enterprise Service Bus (ESB), Composite Applications, SOA Governance, Web Services / SOAP, XML, Microsoft SQL Server (VLDBs), Microsoft BizTalk Server, .NET and IBM iSeries.

Cristian Sturek’s Specialties:

Enterprise SOA (Service Oriented Architecture); ESB (Enterprise Service Bus); Composite Applications; SOA Governance; Web Services / SOAP; XML; Microsoft SQL Server; Microsoft BizTalk Server; .NET; IBM iSeries / AS 400; MC 400; Financial Industry - EFT, Transaction Processing, Accounting, Risk Management, Loss Prevention; HealthCare - HIPAA: ERA, Claims, Eligibility, Benefit; ECommerce; Real Estate; Mortgage Banking; Sales - CRM, Lead Management.
