Chris Hoover’s Summary

Experienced leader in high-tech marketing, product management, media and analyst relations, and business and technology partnerships. Also an ex-comedian.

Likely one of the few marketing managers whose product was the subject of a "Frontline" documentary (http://www.pbs.org/wgbh/pages/frontline/homefront/view/)

My marketing expertise is complemented by a strong technical acumen. Specific geek credentials include co-author of the first IMS Push-to-Talk (phase I and II) standards, and participation in OMA IMS standardization bodies. Author of three patents (one granted, two pending) related to IMS. Co-author, "Dynamic WAP Application Development" (Manning, 2001, ISBN 1930110081).
