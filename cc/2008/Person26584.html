Ronald Vereggen’s Summary

Are you looking to increase the success rate of your IT projects? Then you are looking for someone that has the technology background, management skills and coaching experience that can mentor your team to the next level.

With my technology, management and coaching experience I focus on helping organizations increase the success rate of their projects by improving commitment, courage and communication within their teams.

I am always on the lookout for IT Management or Consulting roles where I can leverage my extensive leadership, project management, coaching and team building experience. Ideal positions would include:
- Delivery of multiple IT Projects in a dynamic team environment.
- Transform and/or build-up a low performing team.
- Leading an IT Team to achieve outstanding results.
- Coaching managers to achieve new levels of performance.

By combining my extensive corporate management and project experience with the hands-on application of coaching I am able to enhance the success rate of project deliveries by focusing on both the people and the process. My key strengths include:
- Process – Improve business processes by looking to reduce waste and improve efficiency.
- Management – Experienced leader of teams with over 40 IT professionals including on-site, near-shore and off-shore.
- People – Passionate about motivating and empowering people through coaching to achieve new levels of performance.
- Creativity – Extremely creative in my approach to solving problems and designing solutions.
- Focus – Drive objectives and projects to completion with both a passion and enthusiasm toward the work.

Highlights of my diverse experience include:
- 5 plus years coaching experience
- 10 plus years entrepreneurial experience
- 12 plus years leadership and management experience
- 17 years experience within corporate environments (large Canadian bank)
- 17 years experience within information technology
