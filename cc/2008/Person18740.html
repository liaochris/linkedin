Neil Pike’s Summary

Independent consultant living in Duxford, near Cambridge in the U.K. Working in IT since 1985, starting with mainframes. In 1992 moved to work with client-server and network infrastructure starting with Windows 3.x, DOS, OS/2 1.x, Lan Manager and SQL Server 1.1.

Specialises in infrastructure, architecture, implementation, performance-tuning, support and troubleshooting of client-server technology, networks (mainly Cisco), middleware, firewalls, proxy/ISA/web, Citrix, virtualisation, mainframe connectivity, NT/Win2x and SQL Server.

Neil Pike’s Specialties:

Expert in the business processes and systems of card issuing, card acquisition and PCI DSS, having worked on systems in this field for many years.
