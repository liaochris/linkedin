About Industry Grind -

Industry Grind's primary focus is on what drives the entertainment and media industry. Founded in 2003 by Sencera Bright a public relations and marketing consultant with a passion to support her peers through knowledge, connections and resources. The company has evolved naturally over the past few years and with great influence from peers has refocused its indie spirit to include corporations, organizations, associations and talented people who are on the forefront as well as behind the scenes.
