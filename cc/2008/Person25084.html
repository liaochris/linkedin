Jason J. Culbertson {LION}’s Summary

With a family of 9, my wife and I are always busy with our 6 girls, 1 boy, our various businesses and networking online and in person with many of you reading this.

I am a very organized person though, and love to share ideas and creative works with other business owners and thriving entrepreneurs. My philosophy is you can never know too many people. So get out and network either live, or in person and bring the world together in business and relationships. You never know when that new friend you meet online or in person will be just the resource you need to get the job done, or just the friend who will be there for you in your time of need.

My wife Susanna is a very big asset to our family and our businesses.She keeps everyone motivated, we work well as a team, and most importantly she is a great mother and wife. I honestly couldn't have it any better. When we take time off for family, we have a karaoke night weekly at our house. With our children and me on the mic, we are constantly having fun. Now this is something I would never do in public, but when you are singing to 7 children under the age of 8, they really don't have any clue what good singing is. We also love to play board games and sports outside with them. We do homeschool our 3 oldest along with everything else we accomplish. Now you are probably thinking to yourself, where do they find the time? We do it all with strategy, organization and time management skills we have learned over the years.

My wife and I enjoy scrapbooking, photography and just spending quality time with each other. We are constantly thinking of how to improve our clients businesses which we are passionate about. We actually call these our hobbies.

Do you like to meet new people? Email me sometime and let me know more about you. Add me if you would like us to network more. Send me your email address today!

Keep it Positive and Successful,
