Ian Bell’s Summary

I have a passion for building solutions and services using new technologies, but I shun terms like "visionary". I'm not a cheerleader. I have a hands-on technical background in IP Communications, but I maintain a diligent pragmatism. I'm keen to experiment with bleeding edge gadgets, gimmicks, and protocols however I prefer to think that my primary skill lies in identifying, positioning, and monetizing emerging technologies at just the right time.

I have extensive experience creating and marketing technology brands and services. I've experienced a number of successes in conceiving & launching innovative and disruptive products and services for the consumer, enterprise, and service provider markets. My goal is simply to keep on plugging away, shaking things up a bit, and making your lives easier or more enjoyable in subtle but impactful ways.
