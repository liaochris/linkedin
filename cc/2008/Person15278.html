Laura Francis’s Summary

I have over 10 years experience designing and delivering digital projects. Most recently I have focussed on User Centred Design, Information Architecture, Interface Design, Usability and Accessibility on the web. I am also experienced in implementing agile project management in a production environment, and integrating user centred design into an agile development environment.

I am a natural born organiser, which lends my talents towards project management and production. I am also really excited about the web, web design and development and what the future of the industry holds.

My background in marketing I think gives a rounded view of projects as holistic not just discrete pieces. Marketing is also in its purest form really user focussed so a transition to user led design process for digital felt like a natural transition.

Laura Francis’s Specialties:

Usability, User Experience Design, User Interface Design, User Centred Design, persona creation, workshop facilitation, Information Architecture, project management, production management, agile, SCRUM, XP, XHTML, CSS, Accessibility, Web Standards, Marketing strategy and planning, advertising buying, print production.
