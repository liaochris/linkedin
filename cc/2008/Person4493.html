    LHUMP is the Dutch students network for sustainable development.

    It works with & for students in higher education and aims to inspire students to integrate values of sustainable development and environment, ethical lifestyle in their personal life and in the choices they make regarding their study & professional career.

    LHUMP does this by organizing events, workshops, and conferences, by carrying out comparative research on university policies, by being the overarching body of local environmental platforms and by making & employing partnerships with other organisations in the field.
