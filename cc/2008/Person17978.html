Mike Urbonas’s Summary

I am a software product marketing and product management professional with a record of achievement in the following areas:

Product Marketing & Management: Manage product launch of data conversion/information & Management delivery solution, including new market expansion, develop live demos, webinars, case studies, white papers, byline articles, etc. Serve as company’s "technology evangelist." Excellent writing and presentation skills.
