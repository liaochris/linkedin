Jill Walker Rettberg’s Summary

I research the ways we tell stories online, and have studied hypertext fiction, weblogs, World of Warcraft, MOOs, electronic literature and spam fiction. My book, Blogging, just came out on Polity Press and an anthology of critical essays about World of Warcraft I co-edited was published by MIT press earlier this year. I frequently give talks about blogs, electronic literature and the web.

Jill Walker Rettberg’s Specialties:

Blog research, new media research, narratology, teaching with blogs, lectures on blogs and new media.

I have also published various academic articles on blogging, including a much-cited definition of weblogs for the Routledge Encyclopedia of Narrative Theory and a book simply titled "Blogging" to be published by Polity Press in 2008.
