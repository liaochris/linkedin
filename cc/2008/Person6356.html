Prof. Diana Derval, MBA’s Summary

Prof. Diana Derval, MBA, Founder and President of the marketing research firm DervalResearch, helps innovative and fast-growing companies define winning sales and marketing strategies.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
About DervalResearch
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
DervalResearch, with a strong focus on new product development and commercialization, is the marketing research firm for innovative and fast-growing companies. Operating worldwide from Amsterdam, Netherlands, the firm founded in 2006 identifies and turns the latest market trends, such as wait marketing, into easy-to-implement best practice. DervalResearch helps general managers, marketers and entrepreneurs build winning sales and marketing strategies with solutions, including product and technology roadmap, positioning analysis, customer insight, marketing plan, opportunity and feasibility study, use cases, business plan, workshops and seminars. For more information, please visit www.derval-research.com.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Inventor and guru of wait marketing, author of the book "Wait Marketing: Communicate at the Right Moment at the Right Place" available on Amazon.com (www.wait-marketing.com), Diana Derval has gained a 360° marketing expertise from 15 years working with major brands (TomTom, Michelin), agencies (Purple Guru) and media (TF1 Advertising).

Professor of Marketing at the University of Wales MBA/Robert Kennedy College, Diana Derval gives lectures and workshops within large corporations, prestigious business schools and leading professional associations.

You can contact Diana Derval by email at diana.derval@derval-research.com or by phone +31 61 824 0803.
