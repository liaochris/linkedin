Namaskhar....
Let me give you a modest version on what I do for a livelihood.

A little history…
Let me start by telling you that I am from Hyderabad (India) and love being back after a 20 year break. We, my family and I returned to Hyderabad owing to an accident that I had in the hills of Mussoorie. I had to change my line of work from the Hotel Industry to Freelance Training, and now have grown to love the challenges that are thrown my way.

What I do
My wife and I started out on our own and formed our company called “C O L T S”. We trained three BPO’s together and now I am sailing on my own. I currently am training with two companies [Junior Level] for Soft Skills and Spoken English. And in the same company, I am training their middle management on Basic / Business Etiquette and Grooming. I am also an Outbound Facilitator.
•Companies trained (BodhTree, Promantra, A T F L, Deloitte, G 4 Securities, A M I Industries, Evoke Technologies, Systel Systems, Fernandez Nursing School and Kanbay / Capgemini in Hyd, Vizag & Chennai.)

My Forte
Coming from the Catering field, I have capitalized on its plus points in basic etiquette and grooming. This particular field brings out the gentleman in many a man. I have inculcated in my training all the formal attributes of behavior and the class of being a gentleman on any given day. I also train young men to be better persons and to go through life with poise and charm.
