Rick Marshall’s Summary

Web-savvy writer/editor and all-purpose word jockey with significant experience developing, managing and marketing a high-profile Web site and its editorial content from the ground up. From politics and hard news to pop culture and criticism, I have firsthand experience writing, editing, designing, marketing and otherwise developing content from initial concept to publishing. My interests lie in contributing and/or consulting at all stages of the editorial process, particularly in the realm of online and other new media.
