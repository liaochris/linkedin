Norma Jackson’s Experience

  • CEO

    NIPCOM Consulting Group, LLC.

    (Partnership; 1-10 employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

    January 2007 — Present (1 year 7 months)

    NIPCOM helps organizations identify, analyze and resolve challenges involving business development, and implements activities that target specific federal government agencies to respond to their issues and requirements. NIPCOM has a diverse client base in the financial management, telecom, and IT technology arenas. We increase revenues for our clients by searching for strategic partnerships, introducing appropriate teaming opportunities, and developing strategic plans to create awareness in the federal government for the clients’ products and/or services.

  • Civilian Business Development Lead

    TOWER Software, Inc.

    (Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Computer Software industry)

    December 2005 — January 2007 (1 year 2 months)

    Leader of the Federal Civil Government sales team tasked with increasing traction in the government marketplace. Leveraged strong networking contacts to introduce TOWER’s Enterprise-wide Content Management solutions for records management at CIO levels across key targeted accounts in the civil government. Developed a pipeline of opportunities by engaging large systems integrators (i.e., Lockheed Martin, Northrop Grumman, BearingPoint) and qualified mid-tier and small businesses for continued business growth.

  • Director of Facilities Management Sales

    (Privately Held; 501-1000 employees; Facilities Services industry)

    February 2004 — December 2005 (1 year 11 months)

    Directed sales contacts with CFO’s, CAO’s and Vice Presidents to identify outsourcing opportunities in major associations, law firms and the federal government in Maryland, Washington, D.C. and Virginia. Combined a knowledge of hardware, software and services to promote Balmar’s on-site managed services.
    Responsibilities included communicating with business analysts and operations managers to draft client statements of work, plan strategies for hardware/software upgrades, and to write effective proposals. Developed networking relationships within the DC Chamber, Greater Washington Board of Trade, the Association of Legal Administrators and the International Facilities Management Association to identify and close facilities management opportunities.

  • Regional Manager

Planned and executed software tradeshows and conducted Knowledge Share training seminars for resellers quarterly. Spoke to audiences of 300+ in size regarding disaster recovery and archiving.
