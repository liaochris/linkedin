Options Executive Search Private Limited was started in 1992 with the mission of providing a personalized touch to the professional service of finding the right person for every job, by ensuring the right job for every person.

Presently, we also have an active network of similar minded recruitment consultants, based across India, in the 10 major cities.This helps us provide a single window access to all candidates, and an offline presence in major markets in India for clients wanting to have an extended arm.
