Edward O'Hara’s Summary

Visionary strategist and relationship manager for Top 1000 companies in Northern Europe. Edward is a self proclaimed Digital Champion in Northern Europe and Management Partner for NORDIC RESEARCH PARTNERS in association with Jupiter Research providing on-line consumer behaviour analysis in Europe with programs of Web & Mobile research as well as strategic advice on eBusiness, Search and Social Marketing (Web 2.0).

Edward held strategy briefing sessions for major groups: IBM Europe, Google, Microsoft, Lego Group, Siemens, Nordea Bank, Egmont, Aller Media, Mecom, Sanoma, Nordisk Film, Ericsson and Nokia. Member of Board at CDM A/S and Retail Planit A/S and advisory partner to Venture Investment company NorthCap(BankInvest). Advisory partner to several media and investment groups.
