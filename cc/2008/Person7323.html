Ian Hughes’s Summary

Ian is a Certified Consulting I/T Specialist with 17 years technical experience in IBM Services, and Hursley Lab and now the CIO office.
A full public cv is here http://feedingedge.co.uk/IanHughesCV2008public.pdf
He is a Metaverse Evangelist leading the way for IBM in the new technology and social revolution caused by Second Life and similar technologies.
There has been massive growth in IBM's presence in the metaverse, and as part of the CIO innovate quick team he is leading the charge.

Client Server ->Web 1.0 Vauxhall,Wimbledon- > Web 2.0, Mashups, blogs, IM's -> Second Life skunkworks -> eightbar external blog -> eightbar Second Life group leaderless and self organizing and viral across corporation, internal wiki -> IBM world innovation jam -> CEO in Second Life with us -> Press work, fortune, business week, bbc etc-> seeing IBM emerging business unit form-> major customers in virtual worlds-> multi million dollar new industry.

In the role of Technical Continuity Director for Blue Room in the centres for e-business Innovation. Setting the technical direction of customer e-business solutions. Providing technical leadership to 50 new media programmers, through practical day to day involvement. Ian has been working in interactive media and e-business for a number of years. He has both designed and implemented many key technical solutions for customers as a developer and as a technical leader and architect. Previously to that he has worked on leading edge technical solutions on a variety of platforms, from host based systems to full functional client server developments.

His strengths are a wide range of technical abilities combined with practical working knowledge of real customer projects. Changing the way customers do business through the web and other e-business mediums is core to all his work.
