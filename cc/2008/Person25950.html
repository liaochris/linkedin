Anthony Arko’s Summary

As a real estate professional during the last 4+ years, I have successfully counseled buyers and sellers in over 240 transactions in the Northern Virginia area. With an emphasis on residential listings in Loudoun County Virginia, I have successful transitioned my business from being a specialist on a top performing team to becoming an independant real estate consultant. At the same time, I have created an online marketing presence that has allowed me to replace all traditional marketing with digital marketing.

As a Stockbroker and Financial Analyst, I gained an understanding of complex financial instruments and the complex financial markets they were traded in. This understanding led me to a successful career in upper level management positions in several financial concerns.

Financial analysis of real estate markets, understanding of financial securities underlying real estate industry, successful negotiation of distressed property sales to include short-sales, foreclosures, loss mitigation specialization, listings and sales of residential real estate, real estate negotiation, digital media marketing, social media marketing, real estate 2.0 concepts,
