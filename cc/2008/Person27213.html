Willie Favero’s Summary

I am currently a Consulting IT Specialist with the IBM Software Group’s North American Lab Services working primarily with DB2 UDB for z/OS, and an IBM Certified Solutions Expert for DB2 UDB for z/OS Version 8. I have over 29 years experience working with database with my last 21 years working primarily with DB2. My responsibilities include consulting to IBM's customers on DB2 for z/OS, DB2's tools, and PeopleSoft, SAP, and WebSphere from a DB2's perspective. Examples of some of the engagements I am involved include performance and recovery health checks for DB2, design reviews, and migration planning for DB2 for z/OS Version 8.
