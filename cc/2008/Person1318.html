Andrew Back’s Summary

* Open source focus group lead at a networked IT services company - involved in advocacy and building of frameworks to support use of open source in the enterprise.
* Infrastructure solution design at a networked IT services company - consulting on application non-functional requirements, and enagaged in the production of high level designs for infrastructure products.
* Network architect at an ISV & consultancy - leading on IT infrastructure elements in consultancy work, and engaged to carry out research in connection with health informatics, peer-to-peer systems, and wireless networks.
* Design authority at an application service provider - leading the design and implementation of a carrier class messaging and collaboration platform, and carrying out investigation into new technology.
* Systems engineer at a dotcom - responsible for the design and implementation of a corporate directory service, and EDI services to support supply chain and banking operations.

In recent years I’ve become interested with the move towards towards utility computing and the technologies that will enable the delivery of infrastructure as a service. I also follow developments in software defined radio, and am really excited about its potential.

I am a huge advocate of open source software and support its use wherever practical. I believe this model translates to and will benefit hardware development, and it is only a matter of time before we start to see the liberation of devices. And I believe in the freedom of information infrastructures.

I support professionalism in IT and am active in the British Computer Society.

Andrew Back’s Specialties:
