James Schellman’s Summary

I have a passion for communication and leadership, and have taken that passion and directed it at educating others to attain significance in their lives.

I have spent over a decade exploring, investigating, and refining behavior that communicates efficiently in the social and professional arenas, and now share my advice with single and married men alike in my new book, Developing a Million-Dollar Relationship: a Man’s Strategy to a Real Relationship.

During and after completing my Bachelor’s Degree from Colorado State University, I spent six years as a professionally sponsored wakeboarder. In addition, I have been employed by a Fortune 50 Corporation, been a radio disc jockey, consulted for small businesses, own/co-own several businesses, am a devoted husband, and most recently became a proud father.
