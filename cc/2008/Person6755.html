Donna Ritter, PMP’s Summary

Do you know what your direction is in your life right now? Not the day to day family/career things; but your life’s purpose – what gives you passion. What makes you excited about the day and ready to do it again the next day? What do you dream of doing with your life? What do you want to be remembered for? Does your career give you this fulfillment or do you drag yourself to work each day. These are the questions we address in Life Coaching.

I have 30 years of Software Development/Project Management experience and I found what really gave me passion is to work with others to help them realize the wonderful person they are and what they can do with their lives. I have extensive experience in practical application of best industry practices with teams who must deliver quality products despite significant time, resource, and technical challenges. I have proven abilities to form and manage cross-organizational teams and programs achieving breakthrough results; so I know the pressures put on Computer professionals.

I am building my Life/Career Coaching business and invite you to contact me for a free session!
