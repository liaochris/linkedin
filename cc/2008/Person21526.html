Rob Cuesta’s Summary

Rob is a consultant with a difference, focused on supporting professionals who have built a career on technical knowledge and expertise as they prepare to move into leadership roles.

He has worked across the globe with some of the largest, and smallest, organisations in the world, and uses this experience and his education to help professionals and knowledge workers make the most of their career.

Rob knows first-hand what it’s like to invest time, energy and money in professional education, and the uncertainty as you wonder how to capitalise on that investment. He also knows what it’s like when knowledge and expertise become a straitjacket. With this in mind Solution Academy supports, encourages and challenges clients to ensure they have the resources to get what they really want from their work.

For Rob, excellence is the measure of personal success; identifying and transforming strengths into assets to set you apart from peers and competitors. “It's as much work turning something below-par into something slightly above average,” he says “as it is to turn something strong into something truly superb. Why waste time and energy making up for your weaknesses when you can take your strengths and stretch them into real gifts?”

And Rob lives his vision, building a successful company on what he does best: communicating, supporting, and applying his expertise and creativity to help clients find truly innovative ways to stand out.
