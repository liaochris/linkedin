Bram Timmer’s Summary

Bram Timmer is an award-winning designer, developer, and digital artist that specializes in innovative new media design. Bram applies his creativity and expertise across a variety of media, including: web design and development; e-learning and interactive media; print materials; information architecture; and illustration. Bram’s past and present clients include commercial, financial and government institutions of all sizes, including MasterCard, Nivea, SC Johnson, Hewlett Packard, Adobe, CTV, CFL, Kraft, Bombardier, Hershey, Applebee's, TELUS, Hilton Garden Inn, AMD, EnCana Corporation, University of Calgary, Mount Allison University, Meridian Credit Union, Alex Cuba, Kahn Consulting Inc., FITC, amongst others.
