About John Gilham
Principle Consultant, john@gilham.org

As a IT versatilist, with exposure to a variety of industries, I have been in a position to apply technologies in simplified, modular, and well architected manner. By applying these technological solutions toward business requirements, I have assisted business stakeholders in streamlining their IT operations through cost savings (automation, virtualization, operations, ITIL) and innovation (collaboration tools, business process engineering, knowledge management.).

I have achieved excellence in business and IT integration through leading, managing, and contributing in technology and business automation projects over the past 12 years. I have performed at every level of technology integration including architecture, solution design, and project management. Solution engagement clients have ranged from Fortune 100 companies to well funded startups all across North America.
My core technical focus is on making Customer's realize the potential of their existing Microsoft platform. However, my business experience allows me to apply these skills on any technology platform or network infrastructure.

My extensive business and technology experience has given me abundant opportunities to identify accurately the suitable technological solutions to business problems. I have honed my skills to avoid the pitfalls of adapting technologies for technology's sake at an organization to focusing on business goals and end user adoption.

I enjoy the opportunity to assist in shaping an organization's technical directions.
