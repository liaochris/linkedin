Joshua Gutteridge [Skyte Media]’s Summary

During my time in the web design industry I have built strong skills which enable me to provide professional solutions with evidence of this ranging through private, public and corporate sectors of business.

Trading as Skyte Media I offer my clients backend design services that are bang up-to-date with the latest XHTML and CSS standards. DotNetNuke (DNN) solutions are also offered to enable the client to add/remove/move content on their own site. This is important for a 21st century website to have such client usability.

On the front end I incorporate brand design with photography and content is created to compliment search engine optimisation.

Skyte Media is now well known amongst it's clients and their clients for providing such a high standard of design work. I am currently designing some sites for companies in London and am looking to expand my business around the country to gain inspiration from many different industries.
