Marci Ikeler’s Summary

I have worked in the web and software industry since 1999. I'm passionate about creating usable, engaging interfaces for business and interactive projects. I have experience in the following areas:

* Project Strategy - I strive to balance user requirements with business goals, leading to a result that is efficient, effective, and profitable.
* Project Management - I work with clients to determine project strategy and follow through by creating statements of work, project plans, and budgets.
* Interaction Design - I design interactive solutions using a variety of user-centered design practices, including contextual inquiry, usability testing, and heuristic reviews. By working with diverse mediums and industries, I have acquired extensive knowledge of best practices and design patterns.
* Communication - I appreciate the importance of refining words until they communicate the right message to the right individual. I create all types of written deliverables, from technical functional specifications for software developers to executive level marketing summaries.
