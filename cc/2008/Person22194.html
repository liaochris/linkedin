R. Todd Stephens, Ph.D.’s Summary

Dr. Todd Stephens is the Sr. Technical Architect of the Collaboration and Online Services for the AT&T Corporation. Todd has served as the technical architect since 1999 and is responsible for setting the corporate strategy and architecture for the development and implementation of Enterprise Metadata, Knowledge Management, and the integration of Web 2.0 technologies including both Social and Collaborative Technologies.

Todd has delivered keynotes, tutorials and educational sessions for a wide variety of technology conferences around the world. Todd is a student of organizations trying to get a handle on their internal knowledge management- how they acquire, how they broker it, and how they disseminate it throughout the corporation. On this, Todd has been awarded seven U.S. patents with several more pending.

In addition, he has authored or co-authored 6 widely read books on the application of technology in business. His most recent book, Trademark 2.0: Defining Your Value in the Web 2.0 World, made it to the top 5% on Amazon in 2007.
