    United States Patent 6,081,602, “Arrayable two-way loudspeaker system and method”,

    speakers in the array share a common acoustic center, thus causing the array to act as a
    point-source in the way it radiates sound in the horizontal plane. This latter goal is achieved
    by creating a frequency independent focal point for the loudspeaker: Loudspeaker systems
    commonly change their radiation pattern with frequency.
