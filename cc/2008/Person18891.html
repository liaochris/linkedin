Nick Finck’s Summary

Nick Finck is best known in the web industry as a craftsman by trade. His skills transverse web design, web development, information architecture and web publishing. He is the publisher of Digital Web Magazine, "the Web Designer's online magazine of choice," and he found Designs by Nick Finck in 1994 and co-founded Blue Flavor in 2005 where he currently is the Director of User Experience.

He is the author of numerous web-related articles for publications such as A List Apart, WebReview, and his own Digital Web Magazine. He has been a judge for the Webby Awards, Philippine Web Awards, The Bubu Awards, and the South by Southwest Web Awards.

He has presented lectures to groups including the American Society for Information Science and Technology (ASIS&T) Information Architecture Summit, The Society for Technical Communication (STC), Internet Professionals Northwest (IPN), DevGroup NW, and at conferences including Web Builder, SXSW Interactive, Web Design World, and the IA Summit, to name a few.

Nick Finck’s Specialties:

Web Design, Web Development, Information Architecture, Information Design, Web Application Design, User Experience Engineering, Usability Engineering, Interaction Design, Quality Assurance Testing, Technical Writing, Creative Writing, Branding, Photography, Film, Video, Animation and 3D Design.
