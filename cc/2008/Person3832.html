Carole C. Edman, SPHR’s Summary

Owner of HUMAN RESOURCES MANAGEMENT CONSULTING practice since 1986, with clients in established and start-up firms in high tech, low tech and biotech industries.

- Provide small and mid-sized clients with full range of HR projects, programs, and guidance by phone, email, and on-site consultations.

- Particularly effective in guiding and coaching managers or HR staff who are facing tough issues in hiring, retaining, and managing employees or who want to implement proactive “Best Management Practices” for improved employee relations and productivity.

HUMAN RESOURCES MANAGEMENT EXPERIENCE with Silicon Valley, CA high technology firms as:

- HR Director reporting to President/CEO of Quasel, Inc., a semiconductor start-up

- Division HR Manager for GenRad, a systems test equipment manufacturer

- Corporate Manager of Human Resources, Office Services, Security, and Facilities reporting to President/CEO of Granger Associates, a telecommunications manufacturer

Certified as a Senior Professional in Human Resources (SPHR) by the Human Resource Certification Institute (HRCI), through completion of The SHRM Learning System and the Society for Human Resources Management (SHRM) Certification Course

Carole C. Edman, SPHR’s Specialties:

Human Resources consulting, coaching, training & strategic business partner for CEOs, managers & HR facing performance, hiring, retention or start-up issues, including recruitment, training, handbooks, ER, compliance, assessments, performance management. Provide clients with experienced guide with successful track record; who understands compliance, employee concerns and business priorities; and can bring value with proactive strategies and implementation of critical HR programs.
