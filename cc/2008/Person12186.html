Jo Duxbury’s Experience

  • Owner

    Freelancentral.co.za

    (Privately Held; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    October 2005 — Present (3 years)

    Freelancentral.co.za is an online directory of freelancers in the creative, IT and film industries in South Africa. I started the business as there was no single place where freelancers and clients could find each other in this country.

    Freelancers can upload their work to online portfolios which clients can browse through - and once clients see someone they like, they can simply get in touch directly, as all freelancers' contact details are visible on the site. The service is free for clients; freelancers can select from three membership levels, two of which involve a monthly subscription fee.

    Thanks to the internet, our freelancers are getting work from several countries around the world (Germany, the UK, India, Canada...). South African freelancers are known for their creativity, reliability and speak English either as a first or second language.
