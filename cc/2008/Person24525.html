Susan Kullmann’s Summary

Susan Kullmann is a Renaissance woman in the information age. She works to give people the confidence and knowledge they need to make the leap into the world of modern technology. Susan benefits from considerable experience as a learner-focused trainer, creative program developer, democratic IT manager, and outstanding university history and women's studies professor.

Dr. Kullmann is the owner of DoctorGeek, a Claremont, California-based firm that offers web development, consulting, and training services to small businesses, professionals, and educational and non-profit organizations. Susan offers 360-degree technical expertise. Clients value her ability to explain computer technology in plain English so they can make informed decisions.

One current project is a photographic essay that illustrates the breadth of modern American women’s spirituality and will be published as a book. "Blessed Are These Hands" captures American women from infancy to 80, each holding something that represents her deepest values. The photos of women’s hands, with their commentary, create a photographic essay with texture–one that speaks about the evocative images from each woman’s viewpoint.
