Tamara Gielen’s Summary

Tamara Gielen is internationally recognized as a thought leader in the email marketing industry. She is a regular speaker at international email marketing events and she is an active member of the IAB's Email Marketing Taskforce and the US DMA's Email Experience Council.

Tamara is the author of "BeRelevant!" (www.b2bemailmarketing.com), a leading email marketing best practices blog with over 1.400 international subscribers. She is also the founder of the "Email Marketer's Club" (www.emailmarketersclub.com), a rapidly growing social network that brings together 900+ industry thought leaders and email marketers from all over the world.

In her current role as Director of Email and Digital Dialogue Strategies at OgilvyOne, Tamara helps local and international Ogilvy clients with the development and optimization of their email marketing strategies. Before joining Ogilvy, Tamara was Email Marketing Manager for eBay.
