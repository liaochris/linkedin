Seth Rosen’s Summary

I have spent the last decade working to further the missions of academic, international development and human rights nonprofit organizations as an attorney, fundraiser, and executive. During this time I have continuously evaluated and restructured major gifts, foundations, and planned giving operations while creating strategic management plans to increase organizational efficiency and mission effectiveness. My experience with leading multi-national nonprofit organizations has allowed me to develop a deep understanding of the social justice, reproductive rights, international development, and human rights landscape in the United States and abroad and form strong connections within the legal and nonprofit sectors.

I received my Masters of Public Administration with a concentration in nonprofit management from New York University and law degree from New York Law School.

Seth Rosen’s Specialties:

Nonprofit management, major gifts fundraising, planned giving fundraising, constitutional law, international development, human rights and reproductive rights advocacy, new business and partnership development, Web 2.0 and social networking by nonprofits.
