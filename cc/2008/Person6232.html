Derek Britton’s Summary

IT industry experience since 1989. Proven success in leading product management teams, helping shape corporate strategy and vision, and implementing and managing associated roadmap and products; all backed by relevant industry-leading professional training and strong natural interpersonal skills.

Strong track record of meeting time-to-market, functional, lead-generation and 8-figure revenue targets. Key personal contribution on notable seven-figure transactions working with Global 2000 accounts.

My process passion is the bedrock for a highly pragmatic approach. Best-practice, process, governance, functional definition and service delivery are key components of my experience. Recent immersion in Agile development methodology and Scrum Product Owner experience including the famous Kenny Rubin's Scrum Training course.

Derek Britton’s Specialties:

Software product life-cycle management, customer and partner relationship management, product marketing and launch processes. Software domain expertise includes enterprise management, portfolio and analysis tools, IDE's and modernization products, across several platforms.

Marketing skills include excellent written communications, experience with direct customer program execution, user stories, white papers, articles, web sites, press/analyst relations.
