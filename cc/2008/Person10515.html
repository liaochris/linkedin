Jason Nazar (jason@docstoc.com)’s Summary

Jason Nazar is the founder of Docstoc.com, an online community and web 2.0 social networking site that allows users to store, search, and share business, legal, and tech related documents for free.

Jason was previously a partner at Venature LLC, a consulting group focused on accelerating the growth of new ventures. There he worked with dozens of tech companies to improve their strategy, sales, and prepare them for financing.

He previously worked in start ups as both a founder and head of sales and marketing. He holds an JD/MBA from Pepperdine University and a B.A. from the University of California at Santa Barbara where he served as the student body president of both universities.
