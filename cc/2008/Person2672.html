Paul Bergami’s Summary

A self-motivated Senior IT Manager with 28 years experience at large Financial and Consulting Industries.

Strong Software Quality, System Testing and Process oriented acting with intensive use of Methodology and COBIT, ITIL and CMM frameworks application
Results orientated with a high work autonomy and strong commitment to the Company, to the Customers and to the Processes.

Spoken and written fluency in Portuguese, German and English languages, with International work experience in London-UK, Long Island NY-USA and basic in Spanish language, experienced during a Brazilian-Argentinean joint team management.

Head of Quality IT Consulting, projects implemented according to the Cobit, ITIL, CMMi models and to the ISO standards, as well as compliant with the Sarbanes & Oxley and Basel II rules.

Quality projects results achieved reducing more than 70% re-work effort and increasing productivity on more than 20% of the testing efforts, improving significantly the client application quality perception.

Development of SOA - Service Oriented Architecture testing process, release controls and testing environment.
