Erica Erkkila’s Summary

Primarily a CGI applications programmer with extensive background in writing tools that communicate with back-end LDAP, Remedy, Oracle, and other servers. Also develops shell applications including auditing scripts that help admins locate and correct bad or inconsistent data in various database or directory-based back-end servers. Some limited unix systems administration experience related to dialup ISP operations.
Previous to this, had a background in research engineering, usually applications development on both unix and windows systems to demonstrate various image processing algorithms, with some experience in digital hardware development (educational background is Electrical Engineering with an emphasis on signal and image processing).
