Simon Barrow’s Summary

I am a writer, consultant, researcher, theologian, community developer and trainer / adult educator with nearly 30 years worth of professional experience.

I aim to help people think through and implement person-centred and (where appropriate) theologically-grounded approaches to organisation in a complex, plural and ambiguous world.

I am working on new approaches to religion in public life - beyond those based simply on mutual antagonisms or the preservation of institutional interests.

I also assist those with similar commitments to social justice and peacemaking who want to make waves in the media, but with integrity and thoughtfulness.

I offer work consultancy and personal coaching to individuals seeking greater connectedness and effectiveness in their professional, personal and recreational lives.

Simon Barrow’s Specialties:

Organisational development, designing workshops and seminars, PR, media relations, writing and editing, adult education and training, theology and philosophy, negotiation skills, vision building, policy development, work consultancy to individuals and organisations, group work skills, coaching, political and social analysis, dialogue skills.
