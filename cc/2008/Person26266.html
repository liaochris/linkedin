Uday Gajendar’s Summary

Uday Gajendar is a UI designer in silicon valley, whose work has included enterprise software, creative tools, web applications and phone-based interfaces at a range of firms, including Fortune 100 and boutique studio. He earned a Master's in Interaction Design at Carnegie Mellon after completing a BFA in Industrial Design at Michigan. Uday strives to advance the field with frequent talks and writings about user experience, aesthetics, product development. Visit www.udanium.com to learn more. You can also read his latest thoughts at www.ghostinthepixel.com.

Uday Gajendar’s Specialties:

new product innovation, information architecture, taskflow/interaction analysis, process design, visual concepts, experience foresight
