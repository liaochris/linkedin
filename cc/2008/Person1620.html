Angela Todd’s Summary

I create exceptional, personalized spaces for small businesses and homeowners. I have a listen first approach when I work with clients. My work has a sustainable focus, and my designs infuse my client's personalities into their homes and workplaces.

One Day Room Transformations - In only one day my team and I can transform a room in your home using your existing furnishings and accessories. You will be delighted at the result of our fresh eyes in your home.

Full Service Residential Design - I offer full service residential interior design services in Portland including style, color and surface selection assistance, custom furnishings, window treatments and remodeling and new construction.

Light Commercial Interior Design - My commercial clients include hotels, salons and doctor's offices. My past corporate background in marketing and sales sets me apart from other interior designers, because my design focus is based on the personality and brand of the business. I help businesses and practices relay their message in visual form.

I turn an average 'hohum' space into something memorable and spectacular. I am a great resource if you know someone who is considering redecorating, painting, or remodeling any part of their home or office.

To view examples of my residential transformations visit: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cb56kUtqNHg

Angela Todd’s Specialties:

Interior Designer focused on commercial and residential projects including remodeling, new construction and color scheme, redesigns, home staging, and branding development. Also a great resource for home furnishings, accessories and one day room makeovers.
