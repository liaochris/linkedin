Amir Peleg’s Summary

Since March 2008 I advice Microsoft on topics related to YaData Ltd. - a BI technology venture which I founded in Summer 2006 and was its CEO until its acquisition by Microsoft in early 2008 (M&A exit after 18 months with a team of 18 employees).

YaData was a leading supplier of Micro-Segmentation (Behavioral Targeting)solution for several industries: Online Advertisement, Telecom, Banking, Insurance, and Credit Cards. YaData's product is based on novel core technology developed by a team of PhD's and IDF's data mining experts.

Prior to YaData, I founded Unipier Ltd (formerly Cash-U) in Jan 2000 and was its CEO until February 2004 (www.unipier.com).

I began my professional career as an R&D officer during the military service. Later was one of the founders of EVS (Elbit Vision Systems, a subsidiary of Elbit Ltd) and played a key Sales & Marketing role in Europe and Japan. Between 1996 and 1999, I established and managed EVS-US Inc, in charge of North & South America.

I hold a B.Sc. in Mathematics, Physics, and Computer Science from the Hebrew University of Jerusalem (IDF TALPIYOT program), and an MBA from INSEAD, Fontainebleau, France.
