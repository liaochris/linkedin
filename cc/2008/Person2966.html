Brandon Luhring’s Summary

Brandon is an art director and web developer working in southern Indiana. Where he works on branding, advertising, and design for a number of national clients. His typical projects include anything his clients may need to advertise or market themselves.

His personal portfolio boasts a wide variety of mediums including drawing, painting, photography, and web design.

He spends most of his free time (not much) with his family. Occasionally he does freelance work and contributes to online design communities.
