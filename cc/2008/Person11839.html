Jim Kendrick’s Summary

Plan, measure, and analyze strategic change through programs/projects. Integrate performance management with strategy, management, capital investments, business process, and change. Align information technology with business drivers. Recognized author and workshop leader. Over 20 years of results in government and NGO environemnts.

Certifications – Certified Management Consultant (CMC), Institute of Management Consultants. Project Management Professional (PMP), Project Management Institute.

Jim Kendrick’s Specialties:

Planning and development for new initiatives. U.S. Presidential transitions. Corporate performance management (CPM). Design and analysis of performance metrics, financial modeling, budgets, evaluation, portfolio management, and Earned Value Management (EVMS). Federal regulatory compliance: Capital Planning and Investment Control (CPIC), OMB Circulars, FAR, security, privacy, information burden, EA, risk management, alternatives, SLC documentation, business cases, studies/reprots for Congress.
