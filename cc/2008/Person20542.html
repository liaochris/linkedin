Alexis Rosenzweig’s Summary

In Colorado, Alexis was the owner of a production company, mysoundsource.com, promoting and booking events in the Boulder/Denver area as well as setting up National shows for Blackalicious and Money Mark. Alexis also worked with MTV in New York City creating and promoting private events as well as working with new artists in the hip-hop genre and with House of Blues developing and managing new artists

I also book and promotes bands in several venues throughout Philadelphia, including the Kimmel Center, as well as works as a private contractor on many different events in the arts.

The mission of POPPED! Festival, Inc. is to produce an independently run music festival in the City of Philadelphia that will be recognized on a global level and in turn bring in more business to the city and raise the city to a first tier market.

The POPPED! Festival is a 3-day festival that will occur annually in Philadelphia. POPPED! will host globally recognized artists as well as breakthrough local artists, creating lineups each night with unique performances that can only be seen once in a lifetime.
