Melanie St.James’s Summary

Believing that the key to a healthier future lies in the multi-disciplinary engagement of the public, private and non-profit sectors of the world economy, she founded Empowerment Works so the world could see it.

In an effort to stop cycles of exploitation of indigenous herbalists and the forests that sustain our planet, she founded BioNova Medical Corporation, which supports medicinal plant based research, development and equitable opportunities for herbalists to improve both public health and biodiversity.

Her training in Transformative Mediation and International Negotiations has influenced EW’s collaborative approach to sustainable development.

Melanie serves as the driving force of the above institutions that work in synergy to promote a more sustainable world.

Melanie St.James’s Specialties:

Private/Public Sector Collaboration, Event Production, PR, Sustainable Resource Management, Social Marketing Strategies, Integrative Project Design, Monitoring & Evaluation, Transformative Mediation/ Negotiation.
