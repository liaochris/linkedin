Massimo Carraro’s Summary

Entrepreneur in the Italian communication industry since 2000, and senior advertising creative for ten years before then.
Involved in definition of branding and communication strategies.
Approach focused on creative output, delivered in straightforward manner.
Interested in media innovations and new marketing challenges, such as corporate blogging and "marketing dell'ascolto".
Company goal: to deliver inspiring creativity and interesting content platforms for clients and target audiences.
Actively involved member of the Art Directors Club Italiano since 1992.
D&AD Elected Associate member since 2006.
Copywriting teacher at Accademia di Comunicazione from 2000 to 2006.
Blogger on nine sites, four of which directly controlled: http://ohmymarketing.wordpress.com (personal);
www.monkeybusinessmilano.it (company); http://AziendeconleOrecchie.wordpress.com (internet marketing directory for Italy); CoworkingMilano.wordpress.com (coworking initiative @monkeybusiness).
Also, corporate blogger for Università Cattolica, Febal Cucine, Lago dealers.
Coordinator of COWO Milano, first coworking and space-sharing initiative in Milano.
