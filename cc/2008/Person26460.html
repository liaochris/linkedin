Boyd Vander Houwen’s Summary

For years media was media. You knew where it had been and you had an idea where it was going. Then along comes the Internet, social media is born and everything changes. Many companies ignored social media for a long time and some still do. At VHPR, we help our clients utilizing social media applications and tools to increase communication and drive business.

Our Social Media Connection for Non-Technical Companies program has helped others step into the social media realm. We don’t expect everyone to jump in all the way, but you do need to realize it’s a whole new world and take the first step towards the future of communications.

For more information about how we can help you please visit www.vhpr.com
