Ivan Farkas’s Summary

Our company, Ivan Farkas, LLC, helps savvy investors to buy commercial properties, Luxury Real Estate in the U.S. (Florida, NY), Bahamas, Dubai, Majorca and other very popular destinations. Our participation in relocation networks and a large contact list enables us to help you to find the best real estate agent internationally to help you or your client moving from one town or continent to another. We also advertise your properties to the new Central/Eastern European elite. For investors we can help to achieve their goals by taking care of them, mentoring them throughout the holding period from entry to exit and keeping them well informed on regular bases about how their assets are performing on an increasingly globalized market. We help you to buy properties in bulk from banks (REO) or directly from the developer for in many cases substantially less than 50% of the market value. We pay %20 referral fee for qualified people.

We also help our clients to purchase their dream luxury boats, jets, airplanes and vehicles, exporting luxury products from the USA to the global international marketplace, taking advantage of an ever weakening Dollar. We offer new and used luxury boats, motor yachts and cars made by respected US manufacturers. Our close relationship with the boat and yacht manufacturers and dealers enables us to provide exceptional prices to our customers and also satisfying the highest level of customization based on detailed specifications. Our aim is to provide our customers with the highest level of personalized customer service through the entire shopping experience, from the selection to delivery to their desired destination. Every customer and each transaction is carefully managed by a customer satisfaction oriented individual in four different languages to ensure and establish a successful and long term relationship.
