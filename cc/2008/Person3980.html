Chris Reed’s Summary

Stiff, Formal Profile: More than 16 years of experience as a publicist as well as work as a reporter, editor and manager for newspapers, magazines and online media.

Not-so-stiff, Informal Profile: Chris Reed has already had a varied career as a journalist - from sportswriter, to assistant news editor for a newspaper to senior editor at a trade magazine company to a publicist for an aerospace firm.

Reed's best moments came when he married the former Felisa Carter on April 8, 2000, when his son Dylan was born on November 19, 2002, and the recent birth on September 20, 2006 of Dylan's brother Daniel.

Reed was a sportswriter for the defunct streetzebra.com and StreetZebra magazine in Los Angeles. Among those he interviewed for stories were
Kobe Bryant, Shaquille O'Neal, Gary Sheffield, Mo Vaughn, Luc Robitaille, Lindsay Davenport and Mia Hamm.

Reed's past work includes a writing stint at the Los Angeles Daily News, heading the sports department of the Palisadian-Post in Pacific Palisades, Calif., assistant news editor at the Daily Bulletin in Ontario, Calif., and a senior editor at Torrance, Calif.'s Bobit Business Media.

He is currently the director of publicity for Bigelow Aerospace in Las Vegas, Nev., which is developing expandable space station modules for private spaceflight.

Time not devoted to family and career are devoted to.... well, what time is there left? Someday, he'll find time to surf the Web, be an avid lover of movies and sports, playing NFL2K5 on his X-Box, attending the latest U2 tour, and just veging out.

After a 33-year lifetime growing up in Southern California, Chris hitched up the minivan in April 2006 and headed east with Felisa and Dylan into their dream house in Henderson, Nev., on the outskirts of Las Vegas.

Reed is a delegate to the Nevada State Democratic Convention for Sen. Barack Obama, and also serves on the Clark County Central Committee for the Democratic Party.
