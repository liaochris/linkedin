Rowena Aitken’s Summary

Multi-disciplinary digital designer, artist, interactive designer and marketer with 3 years of progressively advanced design and marketing experience, specialising in graphics, promotion design, sales copywriting, conceptual drawings and Flash design, across both MAC and PC platforms. Strong experience in corporate re-branding and brand design, marketing content, as well as employing marketing techniques and sales strategies through effective design. Comfortable in many artistic roles with excellent knowledge of industry standard design tools. Excellent communication, presentation and organisational skills complement strong industry experience, a thirst for developing my artistic skills further and a passion for designing digital content.
