Ken Rayment’s Summary

With a varied manufacturing background, I started the Better Process Podcast to talk about the innovation and progress small and mid-sized manufacturing firms make in this country. Here is the byline for the show: "Did you know that manufacturing in the US could, on its own, be the 8th largest economy in the world? We interview people from manufacturing and logistics from all across the US. Come join us for a short sprint through the industry that keeps America prosperous!"
