Patrick Beeson’s Summary

I'm a currently a project manager with E. W. Scripps Interactive Newspaper Group, which provides corporate support for the daily and community newspaper Web sites of Scripps based in 18 markets nationally.

I'm a detail-oriented Web standards geek with a passion for innovative solutions to problems. I believe in well-formed, preemptive communication techniques that utilize new media.
