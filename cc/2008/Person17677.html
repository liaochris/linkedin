Michael Netzer’s Summary

CONCEPT
Integrating entertainment and communications media into socio-cultural evolutionary trends. Utilizing global communications tools, socio-economic strategies, science philosophy, political & religious theologies and proliferation outlets to positively influence the collective state of our human endeavor. Striving for an evolutionary leap within our lifetime, placing us into our next frontier, the colonization of our near solar system environment.

STRATEGY
Utilizing the springboard which is the comics industry and populist comics fandom, as a base from which to inspire and incite the voice of the people, in pursuit of the positive advancement of our collective state. The comics industry, at the base of the entertainment world, acting as the spark igniting a wondrous evolutionary leap awaiting us around the corner.
