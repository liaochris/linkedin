David Cancel’s Summary

I am the co-founder & CTO of Lookery.com. Before starting Lookery I started Compete.com where I was the CTO, now a part of Taylor Nelson Sofres [LON:TNS]. Before Compete I was the CTO of BuyerZone.com, now a part of Reed Elsevier [NYSE: RUK]. In the late 90’s I was a part of the founding team of Bolt.com & part of Lycos.com. I’m originally from New York City and now live in the Boston, MA area with my superstar wife and daughter.

In addition to Lookery I sit on the board of directors of Geezeo.com and the advisory boards of Compete.com, Visible Measures, MyPerfectGig, Sonian Networks and Swift.
