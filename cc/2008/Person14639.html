Kevin Bury’s Summary

Over 20 years experience designing user interfaces for a wide variety of products. Kevin is currently a Principal Designer at LinkedIn.

Prior to LinkedIn, Kevin was Director of UED for Yahoo! Front Doors, where his team was responsible for the design of some of the most popular internet destinations in the world - including the Yahoo! Front Page, My Yahoo!, Yahoo! Buzz and several other products.

Kevin has also held senior design and design management positions at Ariba, WebMD, Apple (Claris) and IBM.

Kevin Bury’s Specialties:

User Centered Design. Design Management. Rapid prototyping.
