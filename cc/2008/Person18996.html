Niels Helles Borgen’s Summary

After completing business school in Denmark I held a two year apprenticeship with a Danish auditing and financial services company. Subsequently in 1995 I joined A.P. Moller Maersk as a International Shipping trainee and have up to now worked in 9 countries world wide within both the liner shipping and logistics sides of our business.
I am currently heading the Maersk Logistics activities in the Eastern Meditteranean Area, which I passionately aim to lead to new heights of profitability, people development and customer satisfaction before taking on my next assignment.

My professionnal goals are to participate in the leadership team in bringing Maersk Logistics to be the Best Logistics Provider Globally.
