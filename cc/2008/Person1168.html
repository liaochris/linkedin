Amy Jacob’s Summary

I am a dedicated mental health professional with special expertise in the area of adolescent mental health and suicide prevention. I have extensive experience in both providing clinical services to adolescents and their families and in providing dynamic training experiences for school and community groups on suicide prevention and other topics related to adolescent mental health. I also have special expertise in the areas of child and adolescent trauma, traumatic grief and juvenile justice.

Amy Jacob’s Specialties:

Adolescent mental health, working with resistant kids, trauma, traumatic grief, juvenile justice
