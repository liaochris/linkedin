Greg J. Smith’s Summary

Freelance designer with a primary interest in architectural practice but also expertise in new media production & curation, graphic & web design, 3D modeling/rendering and the marketing ideas.

I also have an interest in education and teaching. I recently taught my first Undergraduate course, "Interactive Digital Culture" at the Communications and Multimedia Studies Department at McMaster. I have two design related publications due for 2008-2009 and I also write regularly at my blog Serial Consign.

architectural design, technical drawings, editing/curation, graphic design/illustration, HTML/CSS & CMS driven web design, branding, grant writing, etc.
