Josh Cunningham’s Summary

I am a hard-working, broadly-trained individual with a desire to learn as much as possible. I get a kick out of other people's "a-ha!" moments, especially if I had a hand in making them happen. I like the look on people's face when the expensive piece of technology in their hands starts working like it should and doesn't end up smashed against the wall. I like solving problems, which is why I like chemistry and computer science.

I am more or less self-employed right now and, as scary as that is, it's also invigorating. I like having my future in my hands and I like hard work that pays off.

I'm Josh and I want to help you out! In San Diego or elsewhere, come find me at JoshCanHelp.com!
