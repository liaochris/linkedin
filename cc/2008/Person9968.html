Eoin O'Leary’s Summary

I'm an Internet Marketing Consultant, I've been learning Internet Marketing since 1994 and I continue to learn and keep up to date with Internet Marketing trends.

I help Small and Medium Sized Enterprise, (SMEs) and Small Business Owners make more profits, by showing them how to get more business from the Internet.

I first research how a client "works" and then blend an Internet Marketing Strategy into their sales process.

I then add more tools and stratagies to gradually and painlessly build their Internet marketing effectiveness.

I normally take a slow approach to the implementation of an Internet Marketing Strategy. I find this shortens the learning curve into "bite size" phases and also allows time for the client and his/her staff to adjust to each change.

I am a member of The International Internet Marketing Association, www.iimaonline.org and the Irish Small and Medium Enterprises Association www.isme.ie
