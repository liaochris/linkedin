Tim Noble - Lean & Six Sigma Recruiter’s Summary

The Avery Point Group is a global executive search and recruiting firm that assists companies in identifying, assessing and recruiting mid-level management to senior executive talent. Our firm leverages its principals’ decades of executive Lean Six Sigma operations and staffing experience to provide the highest quality executive search and recruiting services.

We create executive recruiting and search strategies based on a client’s individual critical to quality staffing needs. We leverage our Lean Six Sigma DMAIC-Search™ process, our broad industry knowledge and our extensive database of contacts to find candidates who are uniquely qualified to fill our clients’ most critical mid-to senior-level leadership roles.

Specialty: Lean Executive Recruiting - Six Sigma Executive Recruiting - Supply Chain Executive Recruiting - Operations Executive Recruiting - Finance Executive Recruiting
