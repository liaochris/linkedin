Wulfrano Moreno’s Summary

I am a certified SAP NetWeaver Technology Consultant with extensive experience in Java Web Application Development, SAP Composition Environment, SAP Master Data Management, SAP Enterprise Portals and SAP Knowledge Management. In my 3 years with SAP I have been involved in a large number of SAP projects, both internally and externally with Local and International costumers as well.

With the knowledge gained in different roles throughout all phases of a project I am providing valuable assistance to customer projects in an advisory role during the design and realization phases. Customers also benefit from my quality solution reviews and problem solving skills.

Before joining SAP I mainly work helping Mexican and Latin American customers to create their corporate websites and implementing custom development solutions that involved the integration of legacy systems using web technologies.

Additionally I have special interest in new technologies and development techniques like SOA, Complex Event Processing, AJAX, Spring, Java 5, Mashups and Rich Internet Applications among others.
