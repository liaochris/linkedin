Darren Mass’s Summary

Mission Statement:
“At MASS Communications, our goal is to provide a full service, quality oriented experience, incorporating the best of the communications world, without any of the hassle.”

MASS Communications is comprised of former “big company” leaders and performers in the industry. We are Management, leadership and sales from some of the big name carriers. We are the Engineering know-how to help our clients design the best quality network to suit their needs.

Let our experienced sales team, show you the many ways to reduce costs, develop new ideas & strategies, design a network that will meet & exceed your future demands, while working with you, every step of the way. Customer service, in an ever-growing industry has diminished. Let us deal with the transition.

Quality of service and reduced cost, is our major objective for our clients. Customer service and support is our desire. Our support team has the expertise in the industry, to deal with the major carriers. We will choose a carrier more suited to your needs while ensuring nothing but professionalism and courtesy.

Designing the perfect solution that is the correct match for our customers is our passion. Our knowledge of all network solutions will not only aid in a more efficient network, but allow for an unmatched quality. We will research your current network and pick a solution that will suit your needs, keeping cost and quality in mind.

Unmatched personalized service and support in the industry. We are your single point of contact. Our team will order the solution with the negotiated carrier while acting as your direct liaison throughout the entire implementation process.

Our customers are our passion. We do not offer our clients promises, we offer them reality. A dedicated timeline with deliverables will be presented and upheld. After the implementation has concluded, we are always in the background and will continue to be your one stop solution, ready to assist in any way.
