Jim Rutherford’s Summary

Currently seeking either large or small contract/freelance opportunities. I have a tremendous passion for this industry. This passion is demonstrated on my blog http://www.digitalmediaminute.com where I have been sharing my experience, passions, and knowledge for the past three and a half years. My participation in the conversation about technology has garnered me much respect in the web development community. Digital Media Minute has grown to the point where it now receives in excess of 4500 unique visits each day. I continue to get excited everyday to post and share the information that I come across as I research new technologies, methodologies and tools.
