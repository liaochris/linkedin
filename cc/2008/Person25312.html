Thomas M Alisi’s Summary

University of Florence:
- Master degree in informatics engineering with a thesis on computer vision for motion capture.
- PhD in informatics engineering, multimedia and TLC with a thesis on semantic based multimedia content transcoding and distribution.
- Post-doc / project manager at Media Integration and Communication Center (MICC).
- Module leader for web programming languages (HTML, CSS, PHP, J2EE, AJAX) at Master in Multimedia.
- Member of the board of operations / steering committee at Master in Multimedia.

Owner and Managing Director of interactive media agency RedNoodles.com - Florence/London.
Co-founder and President of MediaBoulevard.org, no-profit organization for new media festivals and communities.

IT projects production and management. Web development and technical direction for projects in HTML, CSS, AJAX, FLEX, J2EE, PHP.
