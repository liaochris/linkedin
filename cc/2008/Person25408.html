Thomas Wolf’s Summary

Living and breathing for my professional interests, and can not think of anything better to do than how I'm spending time right now.

Enjoy spending time with equal-minded persons assumed to share common moral values as honesty, loyalty and sense of full fairness.

Honesty, loyalty and fairness are moral values that always have been important to me. In the years gone by, these values have become increasingly important and VITAL to me. This is a result of my 13 years of experience managing people in several companies. Having the opportunity to base companies upon these foundations, at the same time as me and my co-workers are having fun, enjoying every minute of working together, makes me a proud man.

Our driving force is to develop a business software / system that is vital, practical and useful, where no-one else has walked the path in front of us.

"NextCom Evolution takes control of the whole company, it is full automatic, zero human failure and based on our ground rules."

NextCom Group will act as a contact unit to secure quality and to see that everyone is maintaining "the act of cooperation".

The starting point for "the most perfect climate for cooperation" lies in our ideological platform: honesty, loyalty and fairness....
