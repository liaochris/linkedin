Geert Lybaert’s Summary

I started my career working as documentalist to support the legal and marketing department of the Dutch Chamber of Commerce in Brussels. I was responsible for maintaining the library, assisting with legal & market research and events organising. I was involved in the preparation of a new ICT development program for the organisation.

After a few very interesting years in Brussels, I got the opportunity to start working for my hometown educational publishing house: Van In.

As publisher I am/was responsible for a range of subjects and methods (brands).

For Van In SE I'm currently responsible for research on e-learning and ICT in publishing and advising management and colleagues on strategies and opportunities in this area. It's a very challenging business!
