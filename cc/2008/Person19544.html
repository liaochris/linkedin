Pam Kumpe’s Summary

I have a nickname -- it is "Pokey," but the phrase has nothing to do with how I approach life. I am compelled to share a piece of my heart whether I'm teaching kids in "Super Church, writing my newspaper column, speaking to women or hosting my "Daybreak Devotionals" podcast.

I have spent years writing articles, producing newsletters, and journaling. For more than eight years I have worked as a correspondent writer for a daily newspaper, have covered news stories, worked as a weekly religious columnist, and written more than a 1000 features.

I must talk of my Savior. I must be about his business, not my own. Time is short. I'm staying low to the ground, head first and taking one step at a time.
