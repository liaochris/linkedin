MARK WHITFIELD’s Summary

Currently employed as an IT Manager at Insider Technologies Limited in Salford Quays, Greater Manchester (UK).

My main area of expertise is HP NonStop (formerly Tandem Computers & COMPAQ Himalaya). This current role involves me with RTLX, Reflex, BASE24, BASE24-eps (formerly BASE24-es) & the certified integration of ITL's primary product set with leading Electronic Payment/Transaction applications & Enterprise Managers such as BMC Patrol (COMMAND/POST), TIVOLI & HP OpenView Operations (HP OVO) (for Windows).

RTLX (Real Time Log Extraction) is an ATM, POS & EMS monitoring product that enables multiple Browser based views for real-time alerting of transaction & interchange SLA issues, e.g. MasterCard, VISA & LINK. RTLX deploys lean extractor agents to the HP NonStop platform(s) to relay TLF, PTLF & EMS data to a Windows Server running MS SQL Server.

http://www.insidertech.co.uk/html/messaging_rtlx.html

Previously employed at Deluxe Data International Operations, Runcorn, Merseyside (see NOTE below) in Electronic Banking & developing the sp/ARCHITECT (sp-architect) product & host interfaces. I also supported the Periphonics Voice Processing systems (VOS) (bought by NORTEL in 1999) & interfaces developed for GiroBank in Denmark.

NOTE : The Software Partnership (TSP) was bought out by Deluxe Data to become Deluxe Data International Operations in 1994 (based in Runcorn in the UK). This in turn then became eFUNDS (EFD) in 2000 which recently became Fidelity National Information Services (FIS) in 2007. The UK based wing of FIS is based in Daresbury in Warrington (UK) - see link below.
