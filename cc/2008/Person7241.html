Emil Stoyanov’s Summary

Technology has always inspired me, made me go further and pushed me towards the never-ending maze of Software and IT. I consider me lucky to have had the chance to work on exciting, cutting-edge technologies within both academic and industrial environments, with main focus on distributed systems management and technology evaluation.

Recently I founded Forschung-Direkt GmbH - a start-up based in Bulgaria, which offers consulting and R&D services for the IT sector. We are aiming at cost-effective, highly qualitative consulting services, research in distributed systems and prototyping with cutting edge technologies.
