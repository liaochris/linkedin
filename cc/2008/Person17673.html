Michael Moyal’s Summary

An independent business analysis, process improvement and enterprise architecture consultant, specialising in the development of strategies, enterprise and solution architectures which allow businesses to get the maximum benefit from their IT resources while streamlining their core processes.

For the last 10 years has been involved in various consulting, project and account management roles, working for enterprise software vendors, telecomm service providers and management consultancies.

Most recently been working mainly with healthcare / public sector organisations to improve service delivery and deploy new technologies.
