Julien Carnot’s Experience

  • Chargé de mission eTEN Procure / e-bourgogne

    Conseil régional de Bourgogne

    (Government Agency; 1001-5000 employees; Government Administration industry)

    February 2008 — Present (10 months)

    This project aims, after a preliminary study of the specificities of public purchasing in different European countries, to make the following possible:

    -Adapt the service currently used in the Burgundy region to enable its usage in other European countries (compliance with European directives and with local legal constraints) and to better address SMEs’ specific needs regarding public procurement,
    -Check the suitability of the adapted services by conducting 5 pilots in 4 European countries (France, Czech Republic, Spain and Sweden);
    -Communicate the results of this work to all organizations involved in public procurement at an European level.

    I am in charge of:
    -Cowriting various deliverables (including the business plan) with the partners
    -Enforcing the training programs and assisting companies to embrace the new online processes
    -Elaborating a process and toolkits to allow easy adoption of the service by EU regions
    -Public relations and official website authoring
