Josh Farkas’s Summary

Graphic designer and professional Illustrator focused on making things:
1) Functionally intuitive
2) Emotionally impactful

I've illustrated 13 children's books, self-published 3 graphic novels, tackled the lead role in multiple internet start-ups, and designed/implemented hundreds of websites. I currently lead the start-ups Cubicle Ninjas, MonaghanFarkas, and Water Media while working on my next two fiction books.

Skilled Using:
Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Dreamweaver, Flash, PowerPoint, Word, Painter and many others.
