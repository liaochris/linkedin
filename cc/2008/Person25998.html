Toon Vanagt’s Summary

Entrepreneur with broad experience in all aspects of setting up and managing ventures in the BeNeLux. Hands-on experience with e-business, consulting and ICT projects. Holds a "commercial engineering degree" from Solvay Business School at the Vrije Universiteit Brussel and was awarded "ICT manager SME of the year 2002" by the Belgian Datanews jury. Has followed the Virtualization industry for over a decade and is the publisher of Virtualization.com. Interested in early-stage startups as well as companies “on the bubble”.

Toon Vanagt’s Specialties:

SME directorships, ICT project management, strategy consulting, innovation projects & ventures, publisher (running blogs and broadcasting video coverage). Experience in technology, insurance and construction sector.
