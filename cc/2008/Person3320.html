Brian Hancock’s Summary

An intelligent, creative, self-starting individual with an extensive background in operations management, Internet marketing, and web site design and usability. Beginning with a background in computer sciences and business, I have been engineering ways to monetize the web since getting my start as an independent consultant in the 90’s. Able to work independently, I am a well-organized and detailed oriented individual with excellent communication and problem solving skills. I have helped grow companies in fast-paced startup environments and have the knowledge and experience to build teams, create and implement policies and procedures, and make tactical and strategic recommendations to improve operations and create a framework for success.
