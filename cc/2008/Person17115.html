Maura McHugh’s Summary

My aim is to become a full-time professional writer: to create original short stories, novels, plays, and screenplays, and to see them published or produced.

I work part time as a webmaster, blogger, and consultant, and otherwise I write as much as possible.

Work published:
"Mirror, Mirror", a short story, in College Green
"Who Hears our Cries in Forgotten Tongues?", a short story, in Flash Me Magazine
"In the Woods", a short story, in Cabinet des Feés
"Bone Mother", a short story, in Fantasy, an original anthology
"Tattoo Destiny", a poem, in the Jabberwocky 3 anthology
"Home", a short story, in Shroud Magazine
"Homunculus", a short story, in Aoife's Kiss
"Water", a short story, in Doorways Magazine (forthcoming)
"Grave Taster", a poem, in Doorways Magazine (forthcoming) *
"Vic", a short story, in Black Static (forthcoming)
"The Diet", Arkham Tales (forthcoming)
"Moments on the Cliff", a short story in All Hallows (forthcoming)

*"Grave Taster" placed second in Doorways Magazine's annual poetry competition.
