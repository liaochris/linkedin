Julian Luckham’s Summary

Creative Professional with ideas, design and photographic skills, technical expertise and... a passion for creativity! Web and Print Design, Photography and Image Editing, Training and Consulting.

My objective has always been quite simple; to do what I love! For over twenty three years I have done this by dedicating myself to providing the best in Creative Services, and I will continue to do so, whether through my own work or through the direction and training of others.

Web and Print Design, Logo/Identity Design, Search Engine Optimization, Photography and Image Editing, Creative Direction, Consulting and/or Training.
