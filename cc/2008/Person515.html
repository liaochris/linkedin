Manu Agarwal’s Summary

Have been a serial entrepreneur.

After completing by engineering from IIT Kanpur in 1992 I pursued my Masters in Computer and Electrical Engineering from University of Minnesota.

I worked with WSI Inc in USA for few years and moved back to India in 1998 to start Design Expo. Grew Design Expo to a 125 member team and later the same was acquired by a Payment Systems Company, SLMsoft. Worked with SLM for couple of years handling Bid Management and Indian Operations.

I founded ANMsoft in Mid 2003. ANM is a bootstrap company and is now 200 member strong. I am confident to scale ANM to a $B company and make it a leader in providing solutions in Banking, eMedia, eRetail, and eTravel.
