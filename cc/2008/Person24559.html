Sebastiaan van der Vliet’s Summary

Over 10 years experience with development of web-based applications, using a variety of Open Source products. Certified eZ publish developer.

Current clients include Hitachi, Arcadis, International Centre for Water and Sanitation, Technical Centre for Agricultural and Rural Cooperation, Netherlands Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Hivos.

Worked for not-for-profit organizations such as IICD, the International Institute for Communication and Development (The Hague, the Netherlands) and QCA, the Qualification and Curriculum Authority (London, UK); and for commercial enterprises such as AntFactory (London, UK), GorillaPark (Amsterdam, the Netherlands) and KNPQwest (Hoofddorp, the Netherlands).

M.A. in development sociology from the University of Leiden, specializing in statistics and qualitative methods and techniques of research.

Sebastiaan van der Vliet’s Specialties:

Content management systems (eZ publish), online-collaboration tools, multifunctional web platforms and Internet information and technology audits.
