Darren Cox’s Summary

I am an extremely creative person who can take on any project that requires a fresh perspective.

Instead of jumping into the corporate world after college I chose to build my own graphic design company from the ground up.
I figured that the smaller the company the more facets a person is required to learn and a two man organization that films, edits and produces HD DVD and broadcast video along with graphic design, audio recording and artist promotion would be a long term investment in my marketability.

I take most of my art director duties from a fine art perspective rather than a traditional graphic design one. I trust my vision to get the message across in a creative way that doesn't get lost in the multitude of similar projects. I aim for uniqueness and while most corporate clients are conservative by nature there are enough who are willing to take risks with a maverick art director such as myself.
