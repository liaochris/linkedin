Kevin Staniec (kevin@ISMcommunity.org)’s Summary

Kevin Staniec was born in Chicago and raised in Southern California where he graduated from the school of Film and Television at Chapman University. He is an independent filmmaker, photographer and author of poetry, fiction, non-fiction, and screenplays.

A dynamic, results-driven Marketing Manager/Creative Director who has demonstrated the ability to lead and contribute to a team environment with continued success in high pressure, deadline driven settings. With extensive experience in creative programming, public and private relations, production management, design and sales for print and online publishing and a strong established network of industry professionals and corporate clients.
