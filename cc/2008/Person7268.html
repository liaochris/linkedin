Adam Teeuwsen’s Summary

I have been involved in the health and wellbeing industry for the past eight years. Starting with my own personal journey overseas and experiencing what some may describe as a ‘cultural reawakening’, I began is journey of self discovery by reading a variety of texts and looking around his local community.

It soon became obvious that there were many others who were also on the same path. So I started joining social discussions which explored such questions as ‘what is the meaning of life?’, ‘why am I here?’, and ‘how can I be happy and successful?’.

With this in mind, I began studying a variety of different academic courses in the hope that I may find the answers to such questions and learn the skills necessary to empower my own life. Now, after years of studying and exploring, I am now ready to share his wisdom and knowledge with the rest of the world.

I am a proud member of the Australian Reiki Connection (ARC), the International Institute of Reiki Training (IIRT), the International Institute of Contemporary Therapists (IICT), Reiki Australia, the Meditation Society of Australia and the Tarot Guild of Austalia.

I am qualified practitioner in the arts of meditation, crystal healing, reiki, tarot and past life regression. I have completed studies in each modality and have completed extensive research on the subjects at large.
