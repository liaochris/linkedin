Barbara Saunders’s Summary

I am at my best when leveraging knowledge and experience among diverse projects and intiatives. My expertise is in communicating in plain language about complex ideas. I help organizations gather intelligence; create, manage, and document knowledge; and market products, services, ideas, and information.

Mid-sized and large companies: freelance, contract, or full-time copywriting, writing, and editing services. I specialize in B2C collateral with scientific and technical content.

Nonprofits: I apply my business experience and background as nonprofit staff, manager, and board member to consulting in strategic planning, program and organizational design, marketing, and outreach efforts. I am available to facilitate board and staff retreats and for advising on Web 2.0, social media, and other communications and outreach initiatives.
