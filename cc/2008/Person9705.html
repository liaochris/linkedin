Hugo Raposo, PMP®, SCEA’s Summary

PROFISSIONAL PROFILE

I am a certified Project Manager Professional (PMP) and a Senior Enterprise Architect certified by Sun Microsystems (Sun Certified Enterprise Architect), IBM (IBM Certified SOA Solution Designer) and BEA (BEA Certified SOA Architect and BEA Certified Enterprise Architect). I have a graduate degree in Computer Science and I am currently studying at UNICAMP to obtain a master’s degree in Computer Science.

In the last 15 years, I acquired a deep experience in Information Technology, Service Oriented Architectures and integration Architectures. I have worked for multinational companies in the Industrial, Research and Development (R&D), Telecommunications and Service Rendering segments.

I possess a strong strategic vision for new businesses and a wide international experience in reduction of costs, self-sustainable growth and operational managerial methods. I am result/challenge-oriented and skilled in the leadership of large range projects.

My knowledge of system architecture tends to complement my project management skills and allows me to better control the efficiency and quality of projects. Furthermore, my business knowledge allows me to go beyond the traditional IT scope in my thinking in order to recognize and execute on strategic opportunities for the firm.

My technical and non-technical specialties include Enterprise Architecture, Enterprise Application Integration (EAI), Service Oriented Architecture (SOA), Business Process Management (BPM), PMBOK, SOA, ITIL, COBIT, CMM/CMMI, ISO, RUP, UML and Function Points analysis. I also possess a broad technical experience with software lifecycle, leading, architecting and designing software systems based on the following technologies: Java, J2EE/J2ME, HTML/XML, ESBs, Java development tools, Internet technologies, Windows/UNIX/Solaris and RDBMS using object oriented methodologies.
