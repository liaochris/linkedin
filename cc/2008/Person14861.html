John Kivus’s Summary

I’m an energetic programmer, looking for new challenges to conquer and difficult problems to solve. I want a work environment that lets me do both of those things as often as possible. I want to be involved with all phases of a software application's construction, from specification to design to implementation.

My technical writing teachers would probably have an ulcer if they knew I started off a blurb that way, or that I used “I” in my resume. I am not someone, however, who feels comfortable filling out templates to get my point across and I very rarely omit the subject of a sentence when I’m talking in the first person.
