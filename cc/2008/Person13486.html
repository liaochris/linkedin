Jerry Recht’s Summary

As a partner at National Personnel, a 37 year old executive search firm, Jerry specializes in assisting companies in filling executive positions with candidates that meet exacting qualifications. As a leader in several distinct and prominent network organizations, Jerry is able to find leaders in many major industries such as manufacturing, healthcare, telecommunication, technology, bio-tech, and retail industries.

With significant executive experience in large corporations, Jerry understands the inner workings of large corporations. Jerry has worked for such well known firms as BEA Systems, Business Objects and Oracle Corporation. This gives him an edge in working with both candidates and employers. Jerry has had great success in placing executives into a variety of organizations and industries.

His experience also applies to both small and mid level companies. He is involved in both the entrepreneur as well as investment communities in Atlanta. Jerry participates as a member of the Board of Advisors for the StartupLounge Capital Connections events and is also a private investor.

Jerry prides himself in taking sufficient time to really understand what both the employers and the executive candidates want and need out of the job search process. He feels that is it only in the way that he can successfully accomplish a win – win scenario for both parties.

He has consulted for such notable companies as…
AT&T, BellSouth, Cingular, Verizon, Intercall, Bristol Myer Squibb, Schering Plough, HCA, BlueCross BlueShield, Sara Lee, Worldspan, Procter & Gamble, FedEx, UPS, Disney, GE, Maersk Sealand, ICE, CitiGroup, Morgan Stanley, Standard & Poors, Synovus, FHLB of Atlanta, The Department of State, Strategic Air Command, NSA, Department of Energy
