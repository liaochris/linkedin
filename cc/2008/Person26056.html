Tracy Viselli’s Summary

Online media and communications strategist who successfully combines traditional public relations, communications, and online media strategies to increase brand awareness and online reach of web properties, and to build communities that take action. Regular duties include:

-Team and freelancer management
-Blogging strategies
-Social networking strategies
-Media list management
-Application development
-Project and campaign management
-Brand management

I also develop websites, including content and marketing strategies that make the best use of social and traditional media, grow brands, create and manage online content, attract user-generated content and build and manage online communities. I have forged valuable connections among high-level online strategists that I use and strengthen every day.

My political opinions are featured on the National Journal, Online Politics, and Political Voices of Women. I have also won numerous blogging awards and have been an active participant in some of the biggest online progressive campaigns including:

- Twitter Vote Report, a grassroots election reporting project
- Creative Commons campaign to license presidential debate video
- Fox News Democratic Nevada primary presidential debate boycott
- Associated Press boycott over free use issues
