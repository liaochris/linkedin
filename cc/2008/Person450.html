Adrian Hall’s Summary

Adrian is an excellent communicator who tirelessly strives to develop his skills
He has spent many years coordinating VIP delegate campaigns for international business summits and works with integrity to identify the most appropriate delegates and to influence the development of the summit programme and structure so that it reflects the requirements of those attending VIP's.

Adrian has just accepted the role of President for 2008 - 2009 at his local Toastmasters club 'York Eborators'. Toastmasters has provided Adrian with fantastic personal development, communications and leadership development skills and he welcomes this latest challenge to develop his skills still further.
