Karl Swanson MD’s Summary

Karl Swanson MD is a graduate of the University of Illinois medical school, one of the top medical schools in the country and the largest in the United States. Karl Swanson MD is board certified and was a distinguished graduate of the U.S. Air force flight surgeon program.

Karl Swanson MD is the medical director of Ultimate Image Cosmetic Medical Centre, located in Clearwater, Florida. Ultimate Image is the largest and most comprehensive cosmetic medical center in the Tampa Bay area. Ultimate image offers nearly every available cosmetic procedure in an attractive, safe and friendly atmosphere. The caring staff of Ultimate Image and Karl Swanson MD thoroughly evaluate patient concerns and provide solutions using the most up to date procedures and equipment.
