John Philip Green’s Summary

I am an entrepreneur in Web 2.0. My current venture is LearnHub.com.

My core strengths are in software engineering, but I count consumer marketing, product management, human resources, sales, and strategy among my competencies.

Everyone once in a while I accept offers to advise other companies. Right now I advise just SmartHippo.com.

I enjoy public speaking and presented at: Online Educa Berlin 2005, a major international e-learning conference; Mesh, Canada's Web 2.0 Conference; and at various BarCamp, DemoCamp, Ruby on Rails, and university events.
