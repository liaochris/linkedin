Ken Deshaies’s Summary

• Professional Experience and Positions

Licensed real estate broker, Colorado, 1992 to present.

Owner, SnowHome Properties, February, 2004 to present.

Board of Directors, Summit Association of Realtors, 1996-2004; Chair of Professional Standards Committee, 1996-2000; President 2003.

Director, Colorado Association of Realtors, 2000-2004; member Grievance Committee, 2003 to present.

Designated Brokerage Task Force, Colorado Association of Realtors, 2001-02 (resulted in changes to real estate agency laws in 2002).

• Market

We serve the ski resorts of Summit County, Colorado, including Breckenridge, Copper Mountain, Keystone and Arapahoe Basin, and the surrounding communities of Frisco, Dillon and Silverthorne. We also serve parts of Park, Lake and Grand Counties.

• Publications

“How to Make Your REALTOR® Get You the Best Deal”. The book explains to home buyers how to work best with a Realtor®, why using a buyer agent works to their benefit, and explains the home buying process. As of January, 2008, 37 additional editions are either in print or in the publishing process, with a Realtor® in each state co-authoring. The Realtor® co-author in each state is responsible for rewriting the chapter on state laws and having it reviewed and approved by a real estate attorney.

“Get the Best Deal When Selling Your Home”, takes potential home sellers
through the process of preparing their home for sale and explains
how to get the best price when selling. As of January, 2008, 58
additional editions (by market) are in print or in the publishing process.

“Colorado Real Estate Client Strategies”. Advice to real estate agents on how to structure their business for success.

“Sustainable Housing and Building Green: What Agents Should Know”,
Technical Advisor. A course of instruction for real estate agents on energy efficiency, building “green” and alternative energy systems.

Ken Deshaies’s Specialties:

Author of two books, one for home buyers, one for sellers. Investment consultant and broker, from second homes to large developments, residential and commercial.
