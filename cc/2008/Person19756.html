Paula Constantino’s Summary

Professional Experience: I fully embrace continuous learning and believe in continuous learning as a way of life – “We are all works in progress.”

As a trial attorney in New York and as in-counsel for a high-tech startup in Maryland, I am analytical, creative and comfortable with technology. With network marketing experience, I acquired motivation and team-building skills.

As the concept of building an online team of experts evolved, I created a web-based resource with women helping women achieve more success with less stress globally through Mentors, Coaches, digital informational products, affiliate solutions, articles, resource links, and more at www.WomesSupportTeam.com.

Then, I launched Find A Coach Online at www.FindACoachOnline.com, making it easy for anyone to find that “right” coach to lead to success and to fit individual needs.

Embracing my true passion, I founded the1000 Women Can Change the World Corporation (nonprofit) to inspire, enlighten and empower women worldwide.

What I am looking for in LinkedIn:

Creative people who are in PR and know of ways to make our not-for-profit 1000 Women Can Change the World expand its visibility… since this is a truly worthy endeavor with power to create true global change enriching the lives of women worldwide.

People with knowledge, resources and or contacts for fundraising, linguistics and languages, women’s cultural issues, international women’s organizations and other ways to make the vision of our not-for-profit a reality.

Women who would like to achieve more success with less stress – mentors, coaches, consultants and advisors looking to broaden their market scope and/or develop informational toolkits.

Coaches, mentors, consultants and advisors who want to broaden their market scope by having a internet page featuring their offerings with an online shopping cart so that they can deliver their services by telephone with email follow up.
