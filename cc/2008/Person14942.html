Koos-Jan Schouten’s Summary

After some 25 years in the Travel Industry, I turned my focus towards the Internet in 1995.
First, I was involved in the recruitment of ITC staff for the financial markets in the City of London, Frankfurt and Milan.
In 1998 I visited Bulgaria for the first time and within a few weeks Webfactory was founded.
I am currently involved in growing Webfactory by appointing Regional Partners in key locations across Europe.

Koos-Jan Schouten’s Specialties:

We make websites for most industries, but we are specialists in Telecom, Real Estate, Travel and Hospitality and Public Information websites.
