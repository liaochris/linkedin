Jeremy Holton’s Summary

Professional artist working in a wide variety of media and styles. Accepts commissions on the basis of no payment until completed work accepted by buyer.

Background experience; worked as geologist, managed IT for oil company, director IT consulting for major CA firm, stonefruit orchardist, established first web gallery in Australia
