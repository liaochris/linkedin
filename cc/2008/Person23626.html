C. Todd’s Summary

+Personally funded and runs a Mastering House and Post Production Facility.

+Organized live events, designed flyers and managed talent for music and art gatherings.

+Produced albums for multiple artists.

+Mastered albums for Abandon Building Records, X! Records, and others.

+Aims to become the premiere Production, Mastering and Post-Production facility for experimental and electronic media in the United States.

+Released 7+ albums for international labels and curators, contributed to multiple compilations and has collaborated with other musicians in remix and partnership capacity as Phylum Sinter over the past 6 years.

+Has signed deals to release 3 albums in 2007, with worldwide touring, physical, and digital distribution options currently in negotiation.

+Loves an audience.
