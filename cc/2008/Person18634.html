Nathaly J. Vermette’s Summary

Nathaly J. Vermette is a lawyer licensed to practice in the Province of Quebec, Canada and is a registered Canadian trademark agent. She practices law in the field of intellectual property and commercial transactions. She has published several articles and has made numerous presentations on the intellectual property issues related to the Internet.

She is a member of the Federation of Defense and Corporate Counsel ("FDCC") and of the International Trademark Association ("INTA"). She is also Chair of the Intellectual Property Law Committee, Tort Trial & Insurance Practice Section of the American Bar Association for bar years 2006-2008.

She was also elected to public office in November of 2005 for a four year term as a counsellor for the municipality of Wentworth-North in the Laurentian region of the Province of Quebec.

Ms. Vermette is presently sharing office space with the law firm of Brouillette & Associés (“B&A”). Although her practice will remain legally independent and distinct from B&A; both offices will work together adding, when required, mutual legal support and back-up to their respective clients.
