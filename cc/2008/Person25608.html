Tim O'Hare’s Summary

I've experienced a successful career as an innovator and Flash Developer focusing on video products. With an entrepreneurial mindset and a strong understanding of making ideas come to life quickly, I find the strategy of an idea more rewarding and mostly enjoy molding ideas into businesses. I involve myself with products, presentations, and conversations in order to bridge the gap between business and technology, in turn creating products that enable users to better their lives.
