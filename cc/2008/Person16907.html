Matt Brewer’s Summary

I have been integral in building enterprise applications for over ten years in a variety of roles. I have designed and developed service oriented architectures, led requirements gathering meetings, analyzed data flows, performed project management functions and mentored development teams. I have over twelve years experience with object oriented design methodologies and have a working knowledge of software patterns and best practices. After six years of designing and developing back-end business services in C++ and Java on Unix, I have moved into C# .Net Windows and Open Source Web development, which I have performed for the past five years.
