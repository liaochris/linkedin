Grzegorz Skaruz’s Summary

My name is Grzegorz Skaruz and for 7 years now I have been creating and improving Internet websites. I cooperate with several agencies on a regular basis, work as a freelancer (www.verdox.co.uk) and provide my own webdesign company (www.verdox.pl).

In April 2004 I started an Internet shop with natural cosmetics (www.mydlandia.pl) which I run until now. All the works connected with the shop from the concept, through developing the service until promotion and SEO I’ve done myself.

In March 2005 I launched another project – online translation services Almand (www.almand.pl).

In April 2007 next e-shop with beads for jewelry (Kadoro.pl) and in December 2007 third e-commerce - hand-made jewelry Dantemo.pl

At some point I also developed a hobby service that supports solving crosswords and scrabble called MOPSiK - http://www.mopsik.pl Its task is to search for words matching a given pattern in dictionaries containing 127 thousand or 1.3 million words. There is also english version of that website: www.casmos.com
