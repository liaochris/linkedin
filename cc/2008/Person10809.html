Jay Cuthrell’s Summary

I am a technology strategist and business leader possessing tactician chops to back it up... and the frequent flyer miles to show for it.

My professional life is marked with senior roles at companies where I straddle the line of development, operations, and rewarding travel to both close business and revisit my customers.

It's been a great ride so far.

Presently, I am CTO at Digitel and travel all over the US with a strong focus in the Southeast.

Previously, I was CTO for NeoNova (an Azure Capital Partners portfolio company) which took me to various trade events and RLEC customers all over the rural US.

My years consulting at Scient (formerly iXL) gave me the chance to work both domestically and internationally with Fortune 500 companies and .com's.

Yes, I get around.

I have homes in Research Triangle Park, North Carolina and Missoula, Montana. I look for any excuse to visit them when I can :)
