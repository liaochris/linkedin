Vitor Domingos’s Summary

Mostly known as "vd", with a civil engineer degree, is a long time opensource evangelist and system administrator, deeply involved in technology, began working on several portuguese telecoms doing support and infrastructure, then jumped into the B2B bandwagon as web architect when the bubble burst, went for a three year ride nurturing online corporate and home banking systems infrastructure on the national public bank, with a short stop at portuguese Ministry of Justice while head of software development and presidential election voting system, is now doing marketing and operations as founder of 7Syntax, a global company focused on bringing innovative, easy to use Internet services to the consumer market. In his spare time, he's bootstrapping a local mobile group and an online video show. In real life, he's a proud father of one daughter, kitesurfer, driver addict, living 30km nearby north Lisbon, Portugal.
