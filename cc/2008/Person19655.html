Pat Gohn’s Summary

Having recently completed my Masters Degree in Theology, I plan is to continue writing for Catholic media outlets as well as begin to seek positions where I might serve to teach the Catholic Faith to adults in the classroom, in church settings, or at conferences, or in retreat settings.

Visit my column at Catholic.Mom.com. I also contribute at Catholic.Exchange.com, among others.

My articles are also occasionally found among the pages of Canticle Magazine.

Pat Gohn’s Specialties:

Teaching/writing interests: Catechism of the Catholic Church; Theology of the Body; Bible Study; Spirituality & Prayer, especially Marian devotion & Rosary; the lay vocation.

Public speaker for conferences, retreats, and days of reflection:
Topics include the interests above, plus Women & Prayer, Creating the Catholic Home, The Real Presence of Christ in the Eucharist, and How Catholics Cope with Cancer--using prayer and sacraments. (Your emailed suggestions welcomed!)
