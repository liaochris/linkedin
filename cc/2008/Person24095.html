Stephanie Malone’s Summary

I am a multiple award-winning copywriter, designer and creative director with over 12 years professional experience. Currently, I serve as the director of an in-house creative group at a financial institution where I have worked for 7 years. I adeptly manage a staff of nine writers and designers, lead the development of custom creative campaigns for more than 100 high profile clients, design and pitch creative concepts to large potential clients and support extensive sales and marketing efforts. Prior to my current job, I was a Communications Manager at a high-tech start-up where I was responsible for print and web design, web programming, HTML email marketing and corporate branding. I have extensive experience and capabilities in executive management, staff management and creative management/art direction, as well as managing existing long-term client relationships and new client development opportunities.
