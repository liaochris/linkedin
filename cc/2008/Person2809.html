William P. Corrigan, MBA, CGB’s Summary

I am a highly successful serial entrepreneur who enjoys helping non-profit, global, mid level and small corporations succeed in fulfilling their missions.

I am a multi-skilled marketing, business development and training expert specializing in needs analysis and consultative atomization with top notch clientele such as Goldman Sachs, Merrill Lynch, the NYSE, GE, 3M and ExxonMobile. I am an award-winning manager with a high level of sophistication for a broad range of B2B and B2C services. I have a long history of partnering with a vast network of enterprise owners and corporate officers at the CXO level of Fortune 500 and bulge bracket companies that have enabled me to always exceed my on-boarding quotas and revenue goals.

A consummate team player, I had worked my way up to become a Vice President on Wall Street and have developed and managed many large scale revenue generating projects. Most recently I have embarked on a career change and taken on a new role - lead learning application developer – in the training sector.

As a certified Six Sigma Green Belt I also value quality.

I earned my MBA in Marketing from the Tobin School of Business. I have been inducted into the Sigma Iota Epsilon Management Honor Society and was hand selected to be an Executive-in-Residence alumnus.

I currently sit on the Board of Directors of a charitable foundation and I am often sought out for my creative services in that space.
