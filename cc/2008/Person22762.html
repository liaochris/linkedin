Saundra Hadley’s Summary

Owner and event engineer (planner) for corporate, weddings and social parties. Saundra and her staff offers a unique, all-about-you-approach, boutique-like event planning company that goes that extra mile for their clients.

You can watch Saundra every Wednesday morning on AM Evansville show (local Fox 7 affiliate WTVW) give her personal wedding tips! Every year, planning...forever events also contributes to the Evansville Courier & Press Wedding Book.
