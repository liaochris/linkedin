Micah Alpern’s Summary

Micah Alpern is the Director of User Experience for Social Search at Yahoo! He's currently responsible for several initiatives including Yahoo! Answers, the worlds largest Q&A site and the Delicious social bookmarking service. Prior to Yahoo! Micah was a lead designer for Selling and Mobile at eBay and was a founding Director of the Interaction Design Assocaition (IxDA). Micah is a alumni of the Human Computer Interaction program at Carnegie Mellon.

Micah Alpern’s Specialties:

HCI, interface design, product management, social software, social graph
