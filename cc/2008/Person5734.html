David Allio’s Summary

David Allio is an international award-winning photojournalist and digital artist. He is an accomplished and honored photographer with a career that spans four decades. This resident of Kauai, Hawaii, is also an instructor, mentor and public speaker on a variety of art and photography topics. He is currently a contract photographer with nearly a dozen major news organizations. In motorsports, he has been an offical NASCAR photographer, the track photographer at more than ten speedways, and is currently the chief track photographer at the Las Vegas Motor Speedway.

David Allio’s Specialties:

Allio frequently lectures on technical photography topics, plus copyright, web site development, motorsports marketing, regional history and art history.
