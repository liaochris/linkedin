Joe Gruessing’s Summary

I currently manage a staff of developers and engineers at ORBCOMM, Inc. The company provides a rapidly expanding system of "Little Leo" satellites and terrestrial network services focused on customer M2M applications. I am responsible for all ground segment software and system development and integration--including the core ORBCOMM message switch, the AIS message switch and raw NMEA feeds, Satellite Control Center infrastructure, messaging gateways/internet proxies, core ground station control software, NOAA VMS gateways, Network Management Systems, message tracing, provisioning, and automated billing systems--including the "ORBCOMM Web Services" portal.

We are moving and upgrading our entire network and are working on our next generation of products and services.
