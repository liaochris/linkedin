Advantage Title - Chris Breck’s Summary

Advatage Title Company specializes Purchase Settlements all across Maryland – We work closely with local Realtors and Homebuyers to lessen the stress of the home buying experience. We are approved with many Down Payment Assistance Programs, including but not limited to: CDA, MY Community, Nehemiah, as well as FHA/ VA loans & 1031 Exchanges.

We offer our Realtors the opportunity to join our Platinum Club which allows them access to FREE CE CREDITS and the ability to have their COMMISSIONS WIRED directly into their account the day of settlement.

Advantage Title Company specializes Nationwide Refinance Settlements – We are licensed nationally and offer In-Home Closings across the country. Our friendly and experiences title processors and licensed settlement agents will walk you through the closing process. From title search to recording, we have teams dedicated to handle various types of loans including Reverse Mortgages, Streamlines, Bankruptcy Buyouts, and REO's.

If you are looking to buy or refinance a home, call me for a FREE ESTIMATE of your total closing costs.

Please visit www.advantitle.com for more information
