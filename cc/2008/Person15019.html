Kristin Reign’s Summary

I'm the Marketing Manager for Visible World, a Video Advertising Technology company that is revolutionizing how television commercials are made…the footage is instantly interchangeable to customize according to audience, channel, date and celebrity – and it can happen in real-time! You simply have to check it out! http://www.visibleworld.com

Recently the Marketing Account Manager at the McMahon Group (a medical publishing, events, and communications company), where I developed key relationships and marketing materials for “MusicMed Magazine” and an online store, “JazzMedicine.com.”

My previous experience includes a complete brand development and rollout of the Leadership Development Institute at the University of Maryland and small campaigns via Collins Communications in Tallahassee, FL. These roles, and my need for a mobile marketing career to support a second career in music performance, encouraged me to run my own consultation business for several years, PerforMedia Studio. This unconventional move afforded me the opportunity to work in many states, with wonderfully talented artists of all kinds.
