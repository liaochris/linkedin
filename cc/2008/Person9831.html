Ignacio Hernandez’s Summary

Ignacio Hernandez, Founder, President and CEO of MexGrocer.com LLC, brings over 30 years experience and three generations of family persistence in the Mexican food industry to this venture. His track record in management, marketing and leadership gives him broad corporate strategic accomplishments in U.S.-Mexico business operations. As a senior executive and member of the founding family of HERDEZ, the leading food processor in Mexico with brands such as Herdez, Doña Maria, Bufalo and McCormick, he founded and managed the USA division. Mr. Hernandez then established two important Mexican food businesses in North America, these include popular brands such as Del Fuerte and La Gloria in Mexico, La Victoria and Embasa in the U.S. Ignacio is also a member of the Board of Directors of the Institute of the Americas in La Jolla, California.

MexGrocer.com is a California Limited Liability company and was founded in early 2000 in San Diego, California.
