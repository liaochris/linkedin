Kim Karalekas’s Summary

This fall I will enter my fourth and final year at the University of Oregon, double majoring in Advertising and Spanish and double minoring in Communications and Latin American studies. This year I’ve taken on the role of Brand Manager/Account Director for the New Venture Championship account in our student run ad agency, Allen Hall Advertising. I am currently interning with the CoLaboratory internship program sponsored by Portland Advertising Federation and will be interning with the account management team at Funk/Levis this upcoming year.

My experiences thus far have allowed me to and gain insights into the world of advertising/marketing/branding as well as learn Spanish and understanding other cultures through immersion. Through my experiences, I have also gained insights into the fields of: project management, new ventures, social networking, integrated marketing and interactive marketing.

Upon graduation, I hope to work at an agency with fellow forward thinking, creative, collaboration-driven individuals.
