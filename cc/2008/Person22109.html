Rosemary Fernandes’s Summary

Rosemary Fernandes, President, Phoenix Rose LLC and Vice-President, Ferntech Incorporated, has been instrumental in achieving various technological firsts for Ferntech Incorporated by nurturing a highly dedicated team giving it a technical edge that set the standards in global software development for the company. Having the foresight to see the long-term advantages of offshore offices, she has established and operated offshore facilities since 1997 in India and Malaysia at phenomenally lower costs.

Ms. Fernandes has over twenty years of multinational experience encompassing years of progressive management, software engineering and entrepreneurial experience in Preventive Healthcare, Information Technology, Telecommunications, Civil /Structural/Environmental/Cellular Engineering, Utilities, GIS and Mapping industries. She has utilized Internet and web technology to improve almost every aspect of business. She is an accomplished engineer and software professional having sourced, developed and executed projects in USA, Europe, Malaysia, India, Brunei, Thailand & S. America.

Ms. Fernandes holds a Bachelor's degree from Indian Institue of Technology, Madras and holds two Masters degrees: one in Structural Engineering and another in Computer Science.

Currently Ms. Fernandes' focus is on developing a global business related to Preventive Healthcare Centers handling various functions in healthcare, IT, operations, management, business development and marketing, investing and training aspects
