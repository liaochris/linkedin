Betty Mercey - Invite to connect’s Summary

As the founder / chairman of the AMCF our goal and aims are mentioned below.

Aim of the AMCF
To support shelters worldwide with medicine, medical equipment and other related care products. In incidental cases help out with funding for emergency surgery.


Policy
The AMCF is non political, unbiased and impartial. We do not interfere with local or governmental politics because we aim to be a “Red Cross” for animals. We feel that there are other organisations who are much more equipped to do so.
The AMCF does not adopt dogs or cats, nor intermediates for adoption.
The AMCF support the No Kill strategy.
The AMCF does not support shelters with financial means, only in incidental exceptions for emergency surgery.

Shelters
The AMCF supports > 30 shelters worldwide from Bahrein, to the Netherlands, to South Africa. Shelters for dogs, cats, horses, donkeys, wildlife, rodents etc.
