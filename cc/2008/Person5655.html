Dave Getzschman’s Summary

Dave Getzschman (pronounced GETCH-man) is a San Francisco-based photographer providing fresh, compelling imagery for clients spanning the editorial, corporate and commercial markets. In 2004, his work as a staff member of the Ventura County Star earned him runner-up California Press Photographer of the Year. Credits include Time, Newsweek, Maxim, The New York Times, The Los Angeles Times, The Washington Post, the San Francisco Chronicle and MSNBC's Week in Pictures. Getzschman's vision is regularly recognized with honors from national, state and local press associations.

Dave Getzschman’s Specialties:

To each shoot, Getzschman brings empathy for his subjects, humor appropriate for the occasion and a sense of joy for the creative process.
