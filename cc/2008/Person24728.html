Terence Armentano’s Summary

Assistant Director of Online Education at Bowling Green State University (BGSU) with substantial experience in instructional design, web design, and multimedia development. Directly responsible for the design and development of BGSU's 3 week Online Faculty Training Program, eLearning Newsletter, Weblog and Podcast System, and the Non-Credit Online Training Course Learning Management System. Follow Terence's passions and interests for education at his internationally read weblog (http://terenceonline.blogspot.com), which is dedicated to the exploration, application, and sharing of information about education, emerging technology, and web 2.0. Terence is also the owner of Discoverly, Ltd, an elearning consulting and design company.

Terence Armentano’s Specialties:

learning management systems, content management systems, photoshop, flash, html, css, web 2.0, audio and video production, online pedagogy

We strive to ensure not only rock solid technology for course deployment, but also promise the highest level of quality in online teaching and learning through sound instructional design and best pedagogical principles.
