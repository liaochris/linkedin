    (Partnership; 1-10 employees; Management Consulting industry)

    1999 — Present (9 years)

    I partner with Esther Derby, Don Gray, Johanna Rothman, and Jerry Weinberg to host this exceptional annual conference.

    I co-founded the conference; my partners and I organize the conference; and I lead experiential workshops during the conference, which is the role I enjoy the most.

    Rather than attendees, AYE has participants. You aren't treated like a blob who only knows how to sit in a chair. The conference is structured to maximize your conferring opportunities, which enables both you and the other participants to learn from each other. All sessions are 3-hour experiential workshops with zero PPT rather than the traditional 90 minute PPT conference lectures.

    I love this conference. It has the energy of an open space conference but with sufficient structure and facilitation to enrich everyone's learning.
