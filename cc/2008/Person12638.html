John Watkins’s Summary

I coach and consult with schools, school districts, government agencies, and non-profit organizations to support innovative leadership, team development, and positive, planned organizational change. My work focuses on helping organizational leaders and staff members clarify their values, purposes, and vision, to guide conscious creation of programs and activities that enact those values and vision. I help build broadly networked learning communities within and across organizations. This leads to the ability to design programs that hold together in coherent and aligned ways that feel seamless, personalized, and responsive to clients and membership, and to the broader community in which organizations do their work. I help craft real time professional development and knowledge management systems that support new organizational structures and a culture of innovation and results. I work hands on and over the long term with organizational members to develop leadership, engage in strategic planning, and create emergent organizational systems necessary for effective implementation of a transformational (fundamental rather than incremental or piecemeal) change agenda. I hold high expectations for the learning and performance of people and teams, and believe that healthy, equitable, and wise organizations can be created to serve a more democratic, socially just, and ecologically sustainable community and society.

John Watkins’s Specialties:

Innovative leadership development in non-profits and public education; facilitation in complex systems; user-focused and collaborative research & evaluation; developing whole systems coaching and consulting organizations, professional learning communities; inquiry driven strategic planning; whole-system organizational change management; knowledge management for the new organization
