Taylor Banks’s Summary

Extensive background in all aspects of the computer networking and information security industries. Over 10 years designing, implementing, teaching and selling secure information systems to Federal Government, US Military, private universities and public companies, from start-ups to Fortune 100.

Currently focusing on virtualization and its impact on information security, specifically VMware and Xen implementations.

Through KnowThreat, I provide contract training for "VMware: Installation and Configuration," "VMware: Deploy, Secure and Analyze," and "VMware: FastTrack" courses and am actively developing vendor-neutral courseware on effective security for enterprise virtualization infrastructure.

Taylor Banks’s Specialties:

Background in IS and IT security consulting, education, and courseware development; focus on and specialize in virtualization, virtualization security, and VMware and Xen in the enterprise. I also have significant experience in business development and partnerships, security sales, technology research and development, and sales engineering management.
