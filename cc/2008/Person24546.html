Susie Blackmon’s Summary

My experience over the course of a diverse career path during the past 33 years includes approximately 12 years as controller/administrator for the largest independent tire dealers in Hawaii, numerous years as a real estate paralegal, corporate and labor law legal secretary, flight attendant with TWA, caterer, retailer of high-end western boots, apparel and tack, and a passionate owner and breeder of Quarter horses while living in Sarasota, Florida. I was raised in Hawaii by parents who were born and raised in the Hawaiian Islands, and spent quite a few years in Sarasota, Florida, before returning to Hawaii from 1989 through 2006.

In 2006 I moved from the Big Island of Hawaii (Hilo) to Maggie Valley, NC in the beautiful mountains of Western North Carolina, decided to be involved in real estate from "the other side of the table," and began my real estate education and career as a North Carolina Real Estate Broker. I am affiliated with COLDWELL BANKER in Waynesville, NC.

Look for me on my blogging website, BuckingtheRealEstateTrend.com, COLDWELL BANKER.com, Active Rain, Real Estate World, Facebook, MySpace, Twitter, and cre8buzz.

It would be my pleasure to answer any questions you may have about homes, land, buying or selling, in the beautiful mountains of Haywood County, in Western North Carolina.
