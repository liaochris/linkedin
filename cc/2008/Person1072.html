Amaury d'Aviau de Ternay’s Summary

Asatys Consulting's founder, Amaury de Ternay, has a thorough financial markets experience.
In the last 20 years, he has built and managed operations in high constraint environments. This within a constant technical and regulatory evolution.
He has brought his teams to the best European and international standards.

In 2007 he was "buy-side personality of the year" nominee and his team was "buy side trading team of the year" nominee at the TradeTech Awards.

With an American academic background, MBA with Corporate Finance & Strategic Marketing majors, he has an industry insight and a permanent open mind with out-of-the box vision. He brings his multi field know-how to the firm.
