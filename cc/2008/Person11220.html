Jennifer Akers’s Summary

I am a freelance writer, primarily for online publications. At MyShelf.com, I write book reviews and edit other reviewers' copy. My writing experience includes proofreading, copywriting, and copyediting. My journalism background includes publications for online and print media.

My articles cover topics in:
*human interest, children, blended family, relationships, and education.

My writing focuses on:
*strengthening families, nurturing children, and ending domestic abuse.

I review books in:
*Fiction: mystery, suspense, contemporary, women's, and children's.
*Non-fiction: business, finance, self-help, health, family, and home.

My editing experience includes:
*book reviews, research thesis, web content, reports and novels.

My copywriting experience includes:
*newsletters, brochures, business cards, business letters, resumes, and articles.
*copy for my sites plus individual and business clients, including other writers.

Jennifer Akers’s Specialties:

Non-fiction articles, social networking, research, copywriting, editing and web content. Publications in both online and print media.

Web content, business letters, brochures, newsletters, and articles for business and individual websites.
