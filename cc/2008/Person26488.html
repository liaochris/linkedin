Elizabeth Van Moppes’s Summary

Workplace Counselor, the Law Office of Elizabeth Van Moppes, assists employers in identifying legal issues, building a solid defense, meeting compliance requirements, and developing best practices – practices which preserve management rights while honoring the workplace laws. The goal is to partner with employers, allowing them to concentrate on running their business by providing legal guidance in everyday workplace related issues and advice on complicated employee management situations. Ms. Van Moppes is also available as legal counsel to individual managers who are negotiating employment contracts with a current or prospective employer. Additionally, Ms. Van Moppes's practice includes independent workplace investigations working either directly with company HR departments or as hired by counsel for a company.

"I have advised employers in matters related to workplace law for more than ten years. The reward for me is in the relationship and partnership with my clients. Forming Workplace Counselor allows me to concentrate on this relationship as a foundation for my services, setting me apart from the numerous impersonal lawyers and law firms, providing clients with an outside counsel relationship that they trust, at an hourly rate they can justify." - Elizabeth Van Moppes
