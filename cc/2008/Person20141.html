Peter Evers’s Summary

Peter Evers is an allround communications talent with the ambition to innovate and create new forms of marketing and advertising. In order to achieve this goal, he studies Communication Science and has worked on several jobs in order to gain a broad range of experience in communication. Peter ran his own communication agency along with partner Ernst-Jan Pfauth where he worked on communication strategies, websites, professional texts and presentations. Moreover, Peter blogs at the award-winning communication blog Spotlight Effect and trains and inspires people in workshops about blogging. At the moment he works as an intern mobile business development at WebAds, an interactive advertising agency, where he's learning lots about mobile marketing. His growing passion for mobile marketing also led him to the start of his personal blog PeterEvers.net about mobile marketing and web2.0.

Peter Evers’s Specialties:
