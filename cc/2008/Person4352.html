Chris Kieff’s Summary

How do you make a good marketer even better? Give him a quota of $2-$3 million per year and make him sell, successfully for 6 years. After over a decade of Product Management experience with both large (NEC) and small companies I sold over $8 M of telecommunications hardware to Chase Manhattan.

Then how do you make that better marketer more relevant in the 21st century? You send him to school for web design and have him build websites and then learn SEO and PPC.

Next you mix in 18 months of agency experience to give him a well rounded perspective of marketing. Including an immersion into Social Marketing by creating blogs, and rocketing them to the top of the rankings. Being sure to touch all of the bases of email marketing, web site usability, link building and working with a myriad of companies from small to Fortune 500.

What this strange brew delivers is a technology minded marketer with a broad background skilled in negotiating and understanding all of the aspects marketing touches. Check out my background and then check out my blog at http://www.1GoodReason.com, then call me and let's work together.
