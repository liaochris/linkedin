Diego Magnani’s Summary

Professional journalist with communications and PR experience, currently dedicated to Corporate Communications duties and running an online media portal.
In the last 5 years I have been chiefly writing about Mobile Devices and Mobility (eg. PDA and smartphones), while focusing since 2006 on the Windows Mobile platform, developed by Microsoft.
Previously I have been active in the areas of Television, Tourism, Music, Arts and Culture.
Interested in linking to other professional with similar interests and to companies operating in the same areas in order to share knowledge, experience and co-operation opportunities.
