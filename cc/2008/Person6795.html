Dr. Deborah McFarland’s Summary

I have been a chiropractor in multiple settings including being an exam doctor, a fill in doctor for very busy clinics, operation of a very high volume clinic as an associate doctor and now as the owner of my own practice. My favorite thing about practice is seeing changed lives right before my eyes! I love to be a health coach to my patients, helping them to make healthier choices for their lives.

I have also become quite involved in insurance consulting and peer reviews, assisting as a fair third party in determining medically necessary and appropriate care decisions.

In addition to Chiropractic, nutrition and rehab, our clinic also does drug/alcohol testing, lab testing, CDL physicals, pre-employment and school physicals and DNA/Genetic screenings. We offer home based care including physicals, adjustments and blood work.

My goal is HUGE!!!! I desire to have an integrated medical hospital with a natural approach to healing.
