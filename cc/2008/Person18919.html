Nick Rice’s Summary

I work with the owners of successful B2B professional service firms who are great at what they do, but struggle to consistently attract new clients.

What I do is assess their current marketing/sales efforts and work with them to create a roadmap so that they have a very clear path to growth. It's not rocket science, but you will not find professional service marketing answers in a text book.

I specialize in developing marketing plans that:
► Generate more leads for your business
► Convert more leads into profitable clients
► Can be executed with confidence and clarity

Discover the secrets to getting more clients with less effort in my Free Report, "The 7 Principles of Attracting More Clients,” available at http://www.nick-rice.com/freereport

I am a published author, frequent contributor to Fast Company Magazine, Certified Action Plan Marketing Coach and PMI Certified Project Manager.
