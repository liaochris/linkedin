Valeria Maltoni’s Summary

Conversational marketer, consultant and speaker who works on the client side - in corporate America. Works effectively with CEOs, and senior management teams to make a significant impact on the areas that are important to them: the organization's measurable objectives of growth and profitability.

Versed in the business environment and opportunities of five different industries. Negotiated alliances, launched new brands in crowded and highly competitive marketplaces, and rescued mature brands to favor top line growth. Extensive knowledge of sales cycles and future trends.
