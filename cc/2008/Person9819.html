Sheila Satin’s Summary

Sheila Satin found her niche in crystal healing and passion for creating in I Dig Crystals. Satin graduated from George Washington University with a bachelors degree in International Affairs. After years of dull desk work, she went on to manage a boutique clothing line called Spiked Punch Clothing from 2001-2003.


In 2003, Sheila entered the family business, Satin Crystals, selling mainly at tradeshows and to her late-father's crystal healing students. Her interest in crystals grew as she continued to learn and experiment with the different properties of natural stones. The healing aspect of the company became increasingly attractive after Sheila earned her certificate in Crystal Healing. She also accessed the power of the mind after completing her certification in Hypnotherapy from the California Hypnosis Institute. Combining these two techniques, Sheila now practices Hypno-Crystals for clients in search of a positive healing method.

In 2006, Sheila branched out on her own with "I Dig Crystals", selling healing crystals online, at tradeshows and directly to clients. She has given talks on Crystal Healing at metaphysical events in the USA and at the International Congress of Natural Medicine in Peru. Sheila maintains a healing blog for energy related matters.

In 2008, she started the custom, handmade crystal healing line, "Sheila Satin Originals," now selling online and at artist craft fairs.

Sheila Satin has a passion for creating unique crystal healing items, artwork, the power of the mind, energy healing and positive affirmations.
