Philippe Bogaerts’s Summary

After 10 years of experience in the domain of high frequency electronics and radar technology, i decided to turn my Internet hobby into a professional career.
With the assistance of a great colleague, I had the opportunity to focus on TCP/IP and Check Point VPN-1 and security in general.

After a few years, I started the development of the pre-, post sales and training services at Risc Technology Belgium.

After Risc Technology had been sold, I helped starting up an application security firewall, based on neural network intelligence, with some of the finest and divers experts in the security arena.

A few months ago, I decided to start to realize my dream (after years of thinking about it) and I started my own company. The technical experience I gained in the past years as well as my commitment to this project will be my biggest motivation to make this a success.
