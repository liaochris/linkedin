Sebastien Petit’s Experience

  • President

    France Webradios (The French Union of Webradios)

    (Non-Profit; 11-50 employees; Music industry)

    February 2006 — Present (2 years 9 months)

    Founded in February 2006, France Webradios is the main union representing the non profitable webradios in France. It legalized the broadcasting of webradios in France by establishing agreement with the societies that collect the music royalties (SACEM / SCPP / SPPF). It is also at the origin of the first experimental in Digital Terrestrial Radio between 12 webradios and the broadcaster TDF.
