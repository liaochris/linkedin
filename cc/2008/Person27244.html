Terry Sullivan’s Summary

Terry Sullivan is a former teacher that created Wine Trail Traveler, LLC with his wife Kathy. Wine Trail Traveler is a winery and vineyard online virtual visitor center. Terry and Kathy visit wineries and write about their experience. In 2007 they visited 155 wineries and wrote over 175 articles including winery reviews, opinions, columns and learning opportunities and learning modules. In 2008 they plan to visit 100 additional wineries. They produce a monthly newsletter consisting of two or three articles about wine. The website also hosts a recipe section that uses wine as one of the ingredients. Trip itineraries are two day trips to locations that have several wineries. The trip itineraries are useful to people who want to visit a region and do not have the time to plan a trip.
