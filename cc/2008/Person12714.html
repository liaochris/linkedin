John (Jack) Stack’s Summary

3 Big Heads services and methodologies help organizations achieve long term software evolution and revolution. Our services and methodologies include assessment and high level planning, risk management, resource (internal and external) management and ROI measurement. As a result, 3 Big Heads customers are enabled to drive cultural change, increase adoption rates, retool (skills and technologies), leverage abandonment/adoption methodologies, and achieve successful migrations. Ultimately, we function as an extension of an organization’s executive team to facilitate managed change and enable organizational transformation. 3 Big Heads are in the Microsoft and IBM partner programs.
