Russ Unger’s Summary

Seasoned Information Architect / User Experience Designer / Team Leader with Interactive & Web Design and Development background. Skilled at client-facing, building strong team environments and fostering open communications while retaining world-class talent. Experience includes the roles of Product Manager, Information Architect, User Experience Designer, Creative Director, UI Designer, Project Manager and Business Analyst.
