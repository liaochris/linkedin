Deanna Cross’s Summary

Deanna Cross is a national expert on how to buy and sell cars in today's world using the right tools and techniques to experience a simple, smooth and quick transaction. Ms. Cross was born and raised in the automotive retail and finance industry and has a successful 30 year career in assisting consumers and professionals.

85% of car buying decision makers and 50% of car buyers are women. Only 2% of women are employed in the auto retail and finance field.

WomensAutomotiveNetwork.com online tutorial utilizes auto related affiliate web sites to teach women how their credit works, to obtain loans, trade-ins, warranties, negotiating, accessories, various job positions, etc.

WomensAutomotiveNetwork.TV includes: Three .TV shows with how to and DIY advice on auto related topics on buying, selling, ownership issues. Curb Side Chat, Make That Savvy Car Deal and CARNUZ with news that matters most to women on safety, styles, economy, ecology, etc.

New Car Models: Approved by each auto maker to present their news and info in an entertaining and educational manner. We cover the LA auto show. Photo and video gallery.

Your Videos: Social networking to share auto related experiences and topics that empower women.

Your Car 4 Sale: New for consumers to "sell your ride on video classifieds."

Dealer Directory: New for dealers to introduce themselves over a video presentation so consumers or job seekers can have a virtual tour of the dealership and personel before actually going there to visit.

Accessories, Books and Gifts: shopping mall where viewers can purchase auto accessories and electronics, books, DVD's, etc. Reviews of certain items and services of interest.

We have received attention from CBS Radio, NBC, automakers, auto dealer magazines, top womens organizations, Google, MSN, Yahoo, AOL and more.
