Dominique Mangiatordi’s Summary

Senior marketer with 11 years of business strategy consulting and implementation management experience, a HEC Liège BA. Work history spans Fortune 50, start-up companies, and top fifty Belgian, French, Luxembourg companies. Worked in partnership with top advertising/public relations/interactive agencies, with strength in both B2C and B2B marketing. Direct expertise in financial services, software technology, health, FMCG, telecommunications, retail, and automotive verticals with marketing project budgets from €15K to €5million. Handling business strategy development and competitive research and marcom programs as a consultant as well as director. Key interactive projects/clients : Procter&Gamble, Master Foods, Coca-Cola, Franklin Templeton, Robert Half International, Universal Pictures, Cisco.
