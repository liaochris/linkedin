Rich Knitter’s Summary

Starting in July 2006 I began working full time as an Internet marketing consultant for several small to medium sized businesses. It is my hope to grow from a consultant into a full fledged business. I am interested in working with small businesses who are in need of Internet marketing.

Before July 2006, I worked in the nursery industry. I was hired as a "tech guy" due to my computer knowledge. I was given the opportunity to watch a start up business grow from the early stages and helped grow the business myself by implimenting an online outlet for the business's plants.

My passion grew from web site design to Internet marketing in that period of time. I have self taught most of my computer skills and also have a formal education in business.

I thrive in creative environments and am self managed. My passion is working to help small businesses grow.
