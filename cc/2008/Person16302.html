Marco Bardus’s Summary

I'm a Public Relations and communications consultant, a journalist (classified as "publicist" in Italy) since July 18, 2005, and a blogger. I consider myself as a "PR addicted" life-long student, since I like to learn things using a PR perspective.

I am also a Joomla! evangelist, since I try to spread the use of this open-source Content Management System.
