Adam Wooten’s Summary

Adam has experience working in all areas of the translation and interpretation industry. He has worked as a court interpreter, an in-house translator, a translation project manager, an interpreter coordinator, a translation technology instructor, and director of sales and marketing. He also holds a BA Spanish Translation, MA Translation, & MBA International Business.

Adam is currently using his language expertise as US General Manager at Elanex. He has also recently taught courses on translation technology at the Graduate School of Translation & Interpretation, part of the Monterey Institute of International Studies. Contact Adam to find the best solutions to your language needs or to discuss the latest trends in the translation and interpretation industry.

Adam Wooten’s Specialties:

Translation & localization project management, translation technology strategy, translation memory use & training, interpreter management, information technology for the language industry, marketing of translation and interpretation services, marketing strategy in the language industry
