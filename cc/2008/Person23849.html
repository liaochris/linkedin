Gary Kelly’s Summary

Gary Kelly serves as the Chief Executive Officer and Vice Chairman of the Board of Southwest Airlines. A 21-year Southwest veteran who became CFO in 1989, Gary has worked closely with Southwest's legendary Chairman Herb Kelleher and President Colleen Barrett to build the nation's sixth-largest airline -- and the undisputed low-fare leader.

Gary began his career at Southwest Airlines as Controller, moving up to Chief Financial Officer and Vice President of Finance, then Executive Vice President and CFO, before being promoted to CEO and Vice Chairman in July 2004. Prior to joining Southwest Airlines in 1986, Gary was a CPA for Arthur Young & Company in Dallas and Controller for Systems Center, Inc.
