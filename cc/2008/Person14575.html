Ken Savage’s Summary

I started off back in 1996 with making my own website just to learn what all this www stuff was about. After about a year of my and a friend messing around with Allaire Homesite. I got more into Microsoft FrontPage and then writing HTML code.
I made my pages at this point sometime in 1998 from clean HTML and began learning how to make graphics in Adobe Photoshop.

Somewhere before Y2K I thought that making some websites to do marketing online might be a big idea. So I started to throw up some affilaite sites and get them linked (spam?) from other sites and linklists. I did pretty good.

Fast forward 5 years I'm a professional webmaster now with skills in HTML/CSS complaint and accessable code and design. Much better with my graphics skills as well as administrating Micrsoft and Linux web servers.

I know feel that web marketing is where I want to be. Nowadays anyone can easily make a blog or website from some WYSIWYG editor so I want to drive traffic to myine and clients sites. The trick is learning all the latest ways to linkbuild and drive targetted traffic to the right places and doing it at the least amount of cost possible.
