Dylan Conroy’s Summary

Dylan Conroy attended UCSB and studied screenwriting while earning a BA in film and new media. Dylan has a certificate of Entertainment PR and screenwriting from UCLA. He is a graduate of the Act One Screenwriters program.

While at UCSB Dylan interned at The Santa Barbara Film Festival and Ivan Reitman's Montecito Pictures.

Upon graduating from UCSB, Dylan took a job as a junior account exec to Howard Bragman of BNC. He worked with exciting entertainment clients such as Stevie Wonder, The Red Hot Chili Peppers, Mark Cronin and Phil Rosenthal.

Dylan then served for two years as Bruce Nash's personal Research Assistant in development. While at Nash he developed numerous reality television and feature film concepts.

Dylan left Nash to pursue his production experience as a Segment Producer on the Profiles TV (Amazing Race) Harpo (The Oprah Winfrey Show) joint production called The Big Give for ABC.

Shortly after Dylan joined Executive Producer and Creator Chris Usselman as a producer and development consultant on a reality show called The Hearts and Minds project which is currently under consideration at major productions companies and agencies.

Dylan now runs a successful consulting business where he helps clients develop, package, sell and produce reality content as well as manage other creative producers and writers.

Dylan currently has a show in option with National Lampoon.

Dylan is a member of multiple entertainment professional development groups including Connecting Reality, Media Bistro and Act One. He is also the founder and president of Sigs in Entertainment, a networking group for Sigma Chi's in the entertainment industry.

Dylan is also the Founder and CCO of FlashCity.com a new media company in the arena of celebrity gossip.

Dylan is the twelfth most connected person in Entertainment on Linked In.
