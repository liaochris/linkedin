Aaron Abraham’s Summary

Aaron has more than a decade of experience in the Banking and Financial Industry and has always been the top sales in each of the companies that he has worked for.

Starting his career in Citibank in the mortgage industry he is a member of the prestigious X-Club achievers ( sales of more than 10M in a single month ).
He was later picked to join Southern Bank as a Regional Manager in Sales where he was the top sales personnel for 6 months in a row.

Being an entrepreneurial person he later joined International Netherlands Group as a Sales Manager the youngest person in this role at the time. In this role he only took 3 months to do a sales volume that a senior would normally take a year to hit and was promoted to Group Sales Manager.

Having the vision and dedication Aaron Abraham has always been at the forefront of the Sales Division in the Banking and Financial sector.
