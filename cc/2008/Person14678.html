Kevin Johansen’s Summary

Call me a social capitalist. I put a lot of energy into insuring the health of the communities I belong to, because if they're not in good shape it's harder on all of us. What this translates into is that I work every day to improve the business ecology, and I'd like to think that I'm talented enough and well enough connected to make a difference. So if you're a 'Keystone' provider of infrastructure, or have a new product or service that you think creates new and sustainable efficiencies for a market, I'd love to talk to you.

Kevin Johansen’s Specialties:

As a Director for CMG I help companies with at least 5 yrs. of operating history and with more than $8M in revenue w/ management buy outs & M&A opportunities.

As a Huge Partner I help Colorado emerging growth companies access 'accelerant' funds and to improve their operations & marketplace development strategies.

As the EVP BizDev of the BusinessCatapult.com, I work to create new resources for the business development ecology that utilizes 'evidence based entrepreneurship'.
