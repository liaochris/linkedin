Peter Bromberg’s Summary

I am the Assistant Director of non-profit library consortia in South Jersey. I have worked in libraries since 1992, including work as a reference librarian, a young adult librarian, and a special librarian the EPA. Prior to working as a librarian I worked for Nordstrom and got all customer servicey.

My areas of experience and interest are:

- Customer Service
- Training (including train-the-trainer)
- Communicating for results
- Speaking
- Coaching
- Virtual Reference
- Practical application of Web 2.0/Social Software
- Improv

I' am available to speak or present on these topics.
