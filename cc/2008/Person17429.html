Richard Menneveux’s Summary

Entrepreneurship - Web 2.0 - Internet - Media - Marketing - Communication - CRM - HR

Moovement is a search engine for jobs. You can access in one clic to many jobs (>250 000 in France), published on thousands of websites, as majors job boards, newspapers, associations and company career pages.

With Moovement, you can discover job's opportunities easily, you just have to indicate the job you're looking for and where. Then you'll access to a simple list of job post, which order is function of their relevance, and freshness.

In one clic you will have many informations about the company which recruits, its location with Google Map, news in the media about its activity with Wikio, who in your network via Linked In or Facebook works in, or pictures and Movies about it, with Flickr, and YouTube.

Moovement will be soon available in USA, Canada, United Kingdom, Australia, Germany, Austria, Belgium, .... You can discover the french version: http://www.moovement.fr
