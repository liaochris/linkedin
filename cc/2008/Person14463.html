Ken Braney’s Summary

I have been involved in the plastics industry for over 30 years and have been involved in markets from the USA, through Europe, into the Middle East and India.
I have been responsible for company start ups in new countries, such as the start up in the USA in the late 1990's in Michigan where we were supplies to GM.
I have been responsible for governance and good practice in both Profit and Not for Profit organisations. I have recently been elected to the position of President-elect 2009-10, which means automatically I become President of the Society in 2010-11. Currently I am Senior Vice President of The Society of Plastics Engineers, formally Treasurer and have been Chair of both SPE Europe (one of the founders) and The European Thermoforming division of SPE. My goals are always to achieve the best possible result in any activity I undertake. I have always been a leader and am rewarded when the goals set by my teams achieve the positive results.
My activities at present are split between making SPE the best in can be in both increasing membership and making sure the members receive the best possible support in education, job experience and all the necessary advantages of being a member of this vibrant society and ensuring that my work with Thermoforming Solutions Ltd and Greencourt RTM Company Ltd where I am Chair, are guided forward to achieve their aims and goals
