Sarah Salukas’s Summary

Kensho is the Zen Japanese word for “Path to Enlightenment.” Kensho’s vision is to provide superior Technology and Executive Search services based up on an enlightened approach to understanding the true needs of both client and candidates. Recruiting is inherently about understanding and connecting with people. With the Kensho Group you get seasoned Technology Executives who themselves have hired and managed the same types of people you are searching for. This allows us to understand the actual position requirements better as well as ascertain which candidates will bring the most value to the position – just as we were hiring them for our own teams
