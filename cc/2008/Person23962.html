Cindy Lin’s Summary

Mission Statement: Staged4more is about presenting a comprehensive packaging for the biggest consumer product on the market – the home, with an affordable price tag. We are dedicated to making spaces beautiful and showing well both online and in person. Therefore, all our staged listings come with professional photos and online support.

### Results: In 2007, 62% of Staged4more’s staged & sold listings were sold Over-Asking with an average of 3.34% increase in selling price, an average Return On Staging Investment (ROI of Staging or ROSI) of 1129.24%. In a $800,000 listing, for example, that’s an average of $26,720 increase over the original listing price.


In the Press: (print) San Francisco Chronicle Sunday Real Estate section, California Home + Design Editorial, San Mateo County Times, (online media) Curbly, Inman Blog, Carnival of Real Estate, (book) How to Build a Successful Home Staging Business by Barb Schwarz, Inventor of Home Staging, Founder and CEO of StagedHomes.com, (TV) HGTV: Sleep On It

Staged4more is also a Better Business Bureau (BBB) accredited business.

Cindy Lin’s Specialties:

Our Services Aim to be Flexible and Custom to your needs. Think of us as a makeup artist for the home, we enhance your home's existing features.

Staging investment: Estimate is FREE.

Staged4more can do as much or as little as our clients would like.

For more information on staging, to see before & after gallery and find out more about Staged4more the company, visit www.staged4more.com
