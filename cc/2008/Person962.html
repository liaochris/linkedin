Alistair Brown’s Summary

Alistair Brown is a General Manager with broad experience of media and digital publishing industries. Key skills lie in developing and managing highly trafficked, highly accessible, user focused commercial web sites - responsible for all aspects of the online business - digital strategy, product development, operational infrastructure, sales and commercial development. Fifteen years publishing and media industry experience - over ten years in digital publishing with Financial Times Electronic Publishing (FT EP) and The Scotsman Publications Ltd. (TSPL).

Alistair Brown’s Specialties:

Online / Digital Publishing; Media, Newspaper Publishing; Strategy; Product Development and Project Management; Media Sales and Management; Display, Classified and Contextual Advertising; Partnership and Business Development; Digital Archives / Digital Editions; Aggregated News Services; Business Information; Gaming; Content Management; developing online audiences and monetising them.
