John Macpherson’s Summary

Experienced Program & Project Manager, enterprise-level technology and change programs, predominantly in Insurance & Investment Banking. Now managing PIPC's new Hong Kong office. [PIPC is a specialist project management consultancy, and is one of the UK's fastest growing companies ranked in the Sunday Times Fast Track 100: www.fasttrack.co.uk]

12 years working in large multi-national financial services companies, initially in IT Support & Management, then Project Management, in business facing roles across a broad spectrum of technologies (PC, mainframe, client/server, n-tier, Web, MIS, POS, package implementation, bespoke development, open source, rules engines, etc etc!).

Consulting since 1998, predominantly project & program management roles, working variously with end-user clients and with vendors/consultancies.

Internationally mobile, extensive regional and global project experience. Asia-based since 1993.

Strengths include ability to "bridge the gap" between Business and IT, and a notable track record of "repeat business".

Selected Achievements:

> Mobilised and managed a global program to standardise and off-shore Investor Services business processing, and migrate multiple legacy systems to a single global platform.

> Co-founded an Asian insurance and healthcare B2B service provider.

> Defined complete technology strategy and requirements, and selected appropriate systems for new Asian regional life and health insurance business - including support for multi-line and flexible benefits products.

> Solved the management information needs of a crisis-hit subsidiary company, which thereby allowed the business management to understand their potential exposure, define reserves, reduce or eliminate claims and minimise the size of the losses.

> Managed development and implementation of Point Of Sales systems for agents, brokers and sales offices in UK, Hong Kong, Taiwan and Thailand.
