Peter Shafran’s Summary

My firm provides a wide range of services to individuals and businesses in the Tri-State area (CT, NY & NJ). With over 20+ years of experience, we deliver premium service while providing excellent value to our clients. We focus our practice on real estate transactions - residential, commercial and industrial; family and matrimonial issues (including post-judgment support enforcement); criminal and traffic court representation; probate and estates; general litigation; and personal injury. We strive to bring friendly, honest service to our clients. We can provide our services to our Spanish and Hebrew speaking clients as well.
