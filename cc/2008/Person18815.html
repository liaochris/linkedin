Janet Meiners - Janet@AffiliateFlash.com’s Summary

I worked for Internet startups as a web developer for 4 years. Then I discovered marketing and internet marketing in particular. I love it! I'm passionate about online marketing, especially affiliate and blog marketing.

I do SEO blogging and copywriting including SEO Press Release Writing and Optimization. I train bloggers and have several contracts blogging for businesses. As for SEO press releases, you will see my testimonial on the home page of PRWeb. I've written dozens of optimized press releases for businesses.

Recent Career Highlights:
- Grew Google Adwords clickthrough rate from 300 per month to around 3000 a month in 3 months with no additional spend.
- Started a corporate blog for a company and took it from 30 to about 300 hits per day in less than 2 months, spending a few hours a week.
- Sent out a social media press release that got noticed by one of the top reporters in internet marketing. Got over 70K hits to our web site a month week after week with SEO press releases I wrote.

I'm a professional writer both online and offline most recently in Connect magazine (www.Connect-Utah.com) on affiliate marketing and VoIP for businesses. I have written for Citysearch, both reviews and feature articles. I've also written for magazines.
