Maximiliano Yakin Bozek’s Summary

Project Manager in a wide variety of arts, design and cultural developments. Particularly interested in modern and avant-garde multidisciplinary artistic design projects.

Art director for identity design, branding and editorial projects such as magazines, books and catalogues, mostly for the fashion and culture industries.

Senior designer with broad experience in all aspects of developing corporate identity programs, typography, logotype design and brand management.

Interested in academic work and research as well:

Teach Graphic Design and Typography for basic introductory courses, intermediate and advance students.

Give workshops, conferences and lectures in international congresses and seminars.

Currently devoted to manage multimedia conceptual projects being the link between different disciplines such as arts, design, photography, typography, film, literature, music, poetry and fashion.

Periodically exhibitions of paintings, illustrations, photography and other multimedia projects in cultural events and art galleries around the world.
