Niko Nyman’s Summary

Flash ActionScript developer for technically challenging projects. Director of a Flash development company. Our special interest is in combining physical devices, electronics and Flash programming.

Co-author of "Yhteisöllinen media ja muuttuva markkinointi 2.0", an awarded book on social media and marketing (in Finnish). Interested in solving near future problems, discussing user experience and helping businesses realize the value of providing good experiences.

Past experience includes strategy consulting, importing products, music production, art direction and technical web development tasks.

Niko Nyman’s Specialties:

Actionscript, understanding of programming patterns and when not to use them, user experience, common sense, WTFP
