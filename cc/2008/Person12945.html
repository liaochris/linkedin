Jon Gelman’s Summary

The firm of Jon L. Gelman is a specialty law office concentrating its practice in the litigation of catastrophic and serious injuries resulting from work-related exposures and traumatic events. We are dedicated to using our knowledge and skills in conjunction with the most modern technology to provide the best possible legal representation. Our practice is built on a strong foundation of in-depth knowledge of the subject matter which is reflected in the 3-volume treatise entitled Workers’ Compensation Law (West-Thomson-Reuters) that we authored. Our scope of representation ranges from complex workers’ compensation actions to product liability claims throughout the country.

Jon L. Gelman is admitted to practice law in the States of New Jersey, New York, Nebraska and Ohio and the District of Columbia. He is also admitted to practice before the U.S. District Court, The Circuit Court of Appeals for the Third Circuit and the U.S. Supreme Court. In addition he is a contributing columnist to the New Jersey Law Journal on the subject of workers’ compensation law and he is a commentator to LAWCAST.

He has been involved in asbestos litigation since 1971 and is a member of the Asbestos Litigation Group. He is a member of the New Jersey State Bar Association’s Workers’ Compensation Section and a member of the Passaic County Bar Association. Mr. Gelman was a charter executive committee member of the NJ Advisory Council on Worker Safety and Health. He is the former Vice President of the Workplace Injury Litigation Group of the American Trial Lawyers Association, and a member of the NJ Trial Lawyers Association. He is widely published and a frequent lecturer to professional organizations.
