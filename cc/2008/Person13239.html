John O'Shea’s Summary

I'm a CTO, a team leader, a project manager and a skilled systems architect and multi-language software engineer with over ten years experience producing successful commercial web services, servers and tools. My main focus at present is building a scalable platform for publishing, syndicating and tracking huge volumes of commercial and retail product data feeds via RESTful APIs for use by social commerce mashups, widgets and applications.

I have worked in startups, national and international corporations, creating and leading software development teams in architect, team lead and CTO roles. I employ agile software development practices and processes such as the scrum methodology, continuous integration, test driven development, extreme programming and pattern-based software design.

I have in-depth knowledge and understanding of programming using both open source and commercial tools to produce software services and products based on HTTP and official(IETF, W3C, OASIS, JCP, OMG) and de-facto standards and I have designed, developed and deployed many large-scale enterprise solutions.

I maintain an active professional interest in the ongoing evolution of internet and enterprise software architectures, patterns, platforms and technologies and trends in software development processes. I hold an honors Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Systems.
