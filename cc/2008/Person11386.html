Jeremy Likness’s Summary

I have over 14 years of industry expertise with over two decades of software development experience. As a software developer, architect, DBA, manager, and director, I've worked with multi-million dollar projects from conception to implementation and beyond. Recently I created a successful health and wellness business that was 99% Internet-based and ranked as one of the top sites through organic searches. I'm an entrepreneur and even when working with another company focus on an ownership-attitude with attention to the bottom line.

I'm not a typical "techie guy" who hides behind the keyboard. I've thrived in sales through building a network marketing organizing, growing my own health and wellness business and learning techniques to successfully market and sell over the Internet and through creative media such as podcasts and RSS feeds. I conducted several wellness workshops, produced an audio series, pioneered a podcast to drive traffic to my site and published a novel about healthy living that has been sold in over a dozen countries and remains a solid 5 out of 5 stars at Amazon.com.

I've been with companies through all stages of their lifecycle from starting my own home-based business to working with a company that went from 100 to 1000 employees and from private to public. I enjoy creating new projects and being involved in multiple aspects of the business. For those reasons, I'm very excited to partner with Wandering WiFi to build their technology infrastructure and be a part of the process of taking the company to new levels. For me, personally, it is the opportunity of a lifetime to work alongside very successful entrepreneurs to create a company that's great, not just "good."

Jeremy Likness’s Specialties:

Public speaking, coaching, and mentoring. All aspects of Wi-Fi wireless gateways from network and hardware to software and supporting technology. Building web-based applications (Everything from C#/.Net, PERL, Java and PHP to RPG/LE, C++, VisualBasic, and more). My motto - "If you need it, let me know, I'll get it. If I don't have it, I'll find it. If I can't find it, I'll build it." Live in the solution, not the problem!
