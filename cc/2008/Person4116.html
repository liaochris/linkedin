Channy Yun’s Summary

Welcome. This is Seokchan (Channy) Yun lived in Jeju, South Korea. I had 10 year experiences of internet business especially online music, e-commerce, e-payment and internet security of world-wide web.

During past 10 years, I made such own business models and developed them in my first internet venture company. I learned account, marketing and management as well technology as a member of board. It gave me an experience of strategic partnership with various companies such as internet portals, recording and telecommunication company. Especially I had opportunities to work with VeriSign and Thawte together and I became to be good at global business through these experiences.

In Daum, I involved processes of technical strategy and support of software engineers over 300. My job forwards to technical evangelism to connect between my company and 3rd party developers.

I'm also very interested in open source software. I have engaged Mozilla project since 2002 as a korean L10N module owner for Firefox and Thunderbird as well as a leader of korean Mozilla community.

My second interest is web standards by activities of early web community in W3C and Mozilla's standard evangelism. I has been a leader of korean Web Standards (http://webstandard.or.kr).

Finally, the web 2.0 is my third interesting things. Because my job is to search trends and plan strategies on the web, naturally I do that. I gave several presentations for web 2.0 technology in many korean conferences. You can track korean web 2.0 in http://koreacrunch.com of my english blog.

My technical writings are continued in my Channy's Blog (http://channy.creation.net/blog). Especially I have written for ZDNet Korea about various technical subjects on the web since 2004.
