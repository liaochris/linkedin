Timothy Conner’s Summary

Two years of immersion in local community development led to pursue and complete a Master's in the field. However, at the completion of that degree, I was led to complete a Master's of Divinity degree and into pastoral ministry.

After twelve years of ministry experience, with the CD fires ever burning, I was launched into the creation of the Missional Church Network which combines both my CD and ministry backgrounds into one. The Network at three years of age is a purely local expression of a much large Kingdom vision, which is acting locally, while thinking globally. Who knows how the Lord will lead but the Lord!
