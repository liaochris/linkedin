Thomas F.X. Licari’s Summary

Thomas F. X. Licari has been a recruiting force in the Greater Chicago area for fifteen years. Mr. Licari has learned how to inspire people to achieve their recruiting potential while forming methodologies to train and mentor them through the full life cycle recruitment process. Licari is the catalyst to which companies turn when traditional recruiting techniques fail. His individually tailored processes for recruiting and formalized evaluation methodologies are a value-added legacy of excellence he establishes within each company that he has helped to build.

Thomas F.X. Licari’s Specialties:

Provide Human Capital Management solutions to clients throughout the US, from recruitment and selection to establishment of recruiting operations and divisions for start-ups, expansions and established multinational companies.
