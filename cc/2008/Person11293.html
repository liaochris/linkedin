Jen O'Sullivan’s Summary

Jen O'Sullivan is an award winning photographer in Southern California who loves any reason to pick up a camera. In addition to her many private clients, Jen has also worked with corporations and non-profits such as the Los Angeles Philharmonic, the Huntington Memorial Hospital, the Mattel Children's Hospital, the Children's Hospital of Los Angeles, the YMCA, and Nokia Mobile Phones, to name a few. Jen holds a BFA in Photography from Art Center College of Design in Pasadena, California. Professional work also includes graphic design and book arts. She has taught photography and book design at Art Center College of Design, College of the Canyons, Mayfield Senior School, and Flintridge Preparatory School. Jen lives with her husband Tim in Santa Clarita.

Wedding Photography, photojournalism, editorial
