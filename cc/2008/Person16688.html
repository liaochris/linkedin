Jeff Marmins’s Summary

Known as a marketing savvy entrepreneur, Jeff is articulate and persuasive in company positioning; how to add value, differentiate competitively and improve customer retention. Jeff's projects have included building sales organizations, re-creating work environments for increased productivity, and building community and membership through engaging events and marketing outreach.

Jeff performs speaking assignments about connecting core values to being effective in work and life. He is author of www.effectivehuman.com and co-author of the fun dining experience articles at www.culinarycowboys.com.
