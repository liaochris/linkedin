Jim Mercure’s Summary

Jim has 15 years of experience as a Web Designer and Photographer with with several major hi-tech firms in the Seattle, Washington. He has 3+ years working for Microsoft and designed their first enhanced music CD as well as several popular CD-ROMs. He has worked with Cranium's Whit Alexander creating the most detailed interactive world atlas ever created.

He has also worked as a Web Designer for Paul Allen's first e-commerce venture Mercata.com. Jim also worked with world famous photographer Art Wolfe where he successfully completed an internship and worked as an Associate Workshop Instructor.

Most recently he has worked for the Haydrian Corporation as their Senior User Interface Designer developing XM3 an Anti Money Laundering Solution for banks and financial institutions.
