Chase Roper’s Summary

I am very passionate about writing and sharing my rational point of view on topics related to religion, spirituality, pop-culture and current events. I post regularly at PunchlineMagazine.com, my personal site Chaseblogger, as well as comedy writing on my MySpace blog. People have often commented that my writing is witty and sarcastic, but they're wrong, it’s smarmy and juxtapositional. I have experience working with publicists to set up interviews along with interviewing comics and reviewing shows. Samples of my work have also been highlighted on the Comedy Central Insider Blog.

I am a dedicated Christian family man with great ambitions to write thought provoking screenplays, articles, and short stories that are entertaining and inspiring, and also make people laugh.
