Jennifer Walker’s Summary

Jennifer currently resides in the Del Ray neighborhood of Alexandria. She loves her home and the "friendly community." Jennifer is licensed in the Commonwealth of Virginia and works with buyers and sellers on both residential and commercial real estate transactions. She can help guide the first time buyer as well as provide expertise to experienced buyers and sellers.

Prior to real estate, Jennifer was involved in Franchise Development and Operations for Hard Times Café and Chesapeake Bagel Bakery. Her dedication to providing top quality customer service has contributed to her success in real estate.

Jennifer is a member of the Northern Virginia Association of Realtors Multi-Million Dollar Sales Club. In 2006 Jennifer produced over $30 million in sales was ranked 14th out of 20 by the Northern Virginia Association of Realtors in number of units sold!

Jennifer is an active leader in her community. Currently she is a proud member of the Class of 2008 of the Alexandria Chamber of Commerce sponsorsed leadership program, Leadership Alexandria. Jennifer also serves as a dedicated member of the Del Ray Citizens Association and also is a board member of Potomac West Business Association. She is also on the Advisory Board of Burke & Herbert Bank & Trust Company and Rebuilding Together Alexandria. Jennifer previously served on the local YMCA Board as a member and is the Past Chair of First Thursday's on The Avenue. As if that's not enough, Jennifer is the brains behind our neighborhood "Cinema Del Ray" which is an outdoor movie screening every third Saturday of the Spring & Summer (May - August) for kids & families!
