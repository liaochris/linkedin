Mark Nissley’s Summary

GROWTH, PERFORMANCE, PROFITABILITY

Mark Nissley is a proven high-performer focused on impacting brand strategy, market delivery, and organizational development. He has 15 years in business leadership roles, primarily participating in entrepreneurial start-ups and turnarounds. These include the creation, transition, and growth of multiple high-growth ventures with rapid revenue realizations of up to $20 million. A documented ability to propel business development and operations has allowed Mark Nissley to achieve dynamic success across multiple industries and in multiple functions.
