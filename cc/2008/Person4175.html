Charlene (Charly) Leetham’s Summary

I have over 20 Years experience in IT, ranging from Field Service to Contract & Business Management. I also have extensive Business Management experience having owned my own businesses for the past 6 years.

I am a natural educator and have experience in training and coaching others to overcome their technology challenges.

As the Training Manager for Tuggeranong Dog Training Club (Canberra, ACT) for several years, I managed the team that wrote and implemented the Instructor Training Manual, conducted Instructor Training Courses and oversaw the quality of the training provided.

Today, using my unique blend of business and technical skills, I provide services to solopreneurs and small business who wish to sell or promote their products and services online. From Website Design, Product Creation and Packaging, Marketing and Traffic Generation, I have the skills and resources to help you.
