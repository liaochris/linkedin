Andrea Stenberg’s Summary

The author of the forthcoming book "The Baby Boomer Entrepreneur: A Guide to Growing a Business After Age 40". Publisher of the blog TheBabyBoomerEntrepreneur.com with business and marketing advice for the 40 plus entrepreneur.

Also a marketing consultant and commercial writer working with solopreneurs and small businesses owners. I work with entrepreneurs to develop and implement marketing strategies for sustained growth. This allows the owner to focus on their core business without the need to take on more employees.

Andrea Stenberg’s Specialties:

TheBabyBoomerEntrepreneur.com publishes content to help the 40 plus entrepreneur with marketing and managing their business. Included are interviews, profiles, tips and soon, podcasts and teleseminars.

As a marketing consultant my specialty is small business marketing that tells a story while promoting the business's products and services. This includes creating newsletters, case studies, press releases, web copy and ghostwriting articles.
