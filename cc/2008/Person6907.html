Dwayne Conyers’s Summary

ENTERTAINMENT INDUSTRY

Mr. Conyers is a graduate of the Warner Bros. Comedy Writers Workshop with Samm Art Williams. He studied screenwriting with the late Luigi DiFonzo at Harvard and the late Carl Sautter (ABC’s “Moonlighting”) via the American Film Institute. He also studied with guru Michael Hague.

Dwayne’s professional writing career started with an episode of the late ABC series “Living Dolls” (starring Oscar® winner Halle Berry and Leah Remini). His portfolio also includes multimedia writing, including training video for the White House and interactive video programming for the Ministry of Defense, Republic of Singapore.

He is Executive Producer of independent film “High Point,” raising the majority of the production budget and arranging foreign and domestic distribution opportunities. Dwacon currently has other feature projects in development.

Dwacon, Inc. is looking to produce a series of web-based entertainment properties with accessibility to broadcast, cable, and/or DVD distribution channels. In that regard, we are open to investors who can see the profitability of our dynamic vision.


TECHNOLOGY CONSULTING

Dwayne's right-brain/left-brain ambidexterity includes work in the information technology arena. He is a Systems Engineering and Technical Assistance (SETA) consultant for commercial and government customers.

Dwayne is currently on assignment at the White House, providing technical architecture design and development in addition to managing customer requirements. His specialties include Software Architecture, Knowledge Management, and Business Intelligence.

Dwayne is a practicing member of the Worldwide Institute of Software Architects and holds certifications in Microsoft and IBM/Lotus technologies.

Many of his engagements have utilized his project management skills. Dwayne utilizes traditional and agile techniques. He became a Certified ScrumMaster in 2006 and anticipates PMP certification in 2009.
