Omar Uddin’s Summary

With an interest in early-stage ventures, start-ups and companies "on the bubble", Mr. Uddin has over 15 years of experience with SDLC, web and client/server architectures. As a serial New Media entrepreneur with broad experience in technological fields including Information Technology and security, web/database, client/server, CADD, 3D animation and 3D FEA analysis, Uddin has worked on business development initiatives, strategic planning, for over 10 years. Mr. Uddin specializes in studying business objectives, building requirements and developing plans for product execution. This experience translates into flexible, cost-effective technology solutions.

Mr. Uddin's experience has helped UVision differentiate itself with a powerful set of core methodologies, core values, and core competencies that have helped hundreds of clients in various verticals achieve their vision.

Previous positions in the Information Technology and Services industry include:

While building UVision, from 2005 to 2007, Uddin concurrently held the position of co-founder and CEO at UpSolv, a mobile media solutions company based near Washington, DC, and was responsible for managing business development, developing marketing & sales strategies, raising venture capital and leading the steering committee.

From 2001-2004, prior to UpSolv, Uddin served as IT Director for HotelsCentral.com, an international travel e-booking service based near Washington, DC, helping the company gain further market traction, optimize business processes, minimize operating expenditures, decrease e-commerce fraud and leading to increased quality, higher value of offerings to customers, and increased profitability.
