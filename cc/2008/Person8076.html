Antonio Fonduca’s Summary

Best selling author of “Conquering China”, a guide for Westerners to succeed in their business relations with the Chinese. Advisor to global companies and senior executives on the topic of Sino-Western business negotiations, stratagems and techniques.

Executive Director at Brighter, a Swedish company impacting the lives of millions of diabetics worldwide by introducing a revolutionary solution making their lives easier. Former Senior Research Scientist & Project Leader at AstraZeneca. Deep knowledge in the consulting, pharmaceutical and medical device industries. Solid competence in marketing and management within life sciences. Industry expertise ranging from chemical engineering and organic synthesis to regulatory approvals and quality assurance.

Studies at UC Berkeley, the Royal Institute of Technology, Stockholm University and Stockholm School of Entrepreneurship. Winner of Guldräven (Best Business Plan of the Year at Stockholm School of Economics) and Venture Cup (Stockholm). Global Finalist at Idea to Product and Finalist at the European Business Plan of the Year.

Serial entrepreneur having worked with small startups as well as with global companies. Experience from general management, internationalisation, sales, marketing and business strategy. Member of Toastmasters and JCI.

I am always on the lookout to expand my network with fellow minded people. Feel free to drop me a line at antoniolinkedin(at)gmail.com
