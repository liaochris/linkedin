Monika McGillicuddy’s Summary

Read about my thoughts and Real Estate experiences on my New Hampshire Real Estate Blogs.

I live in Hampstead NH with my wonderful husband and business partner Jay. Hampstead is a small southern NH town. Some consider us a suburb of Boston since we are about an hour north of the city. The location is ideal and we travel all over listing and selling real estate. We've been working full time in real estate in the Hampstead and surrounding area since 1984. Thats a lot of years and because we love what we do...everyday is exciting.

Jay and I work together as a real estate team for Prudential Verani Realty. Prudential Verani has 10 offices from Concord NH to points east and south. We literally can work wherever our customer and clients needs take us. Together we have over 44 years of real estate experience behind us. We know our market...the communities, the homes and if you're looking for a personal touch...people who really care about helping you...you've come to the right place. We know that you don't care what we know ....till you know that we care!

I mainly focus on residential real estate sales including land acquisitions and new construction. My primary area is Southern NH, covering towns located in Rockingham County.

If you'd like to search for New Hampshire real estate check out my real estate website.

New Hampshire has a lot to offer so be sure to read my real estate blog full of community information as well as New Hampshire information all from an insiders intimate point of view.

Monika McGillicuddy’s Specialties:

I started teaching real estate related topics 1988. I teach GRI, Quick Start and Ethics courses for the New Hampshire Association of REALTORS and teach both Agency and REALTOR professionalism courses through out the State. While I love training and sharing my skills and knowledge I still work in the field listing and selling real estate on a daily basis.

Monika McGillicuddy’s Honors:

Since becoming a REALTOR® in 1984, I have been honored to have been twice selected REALTOR® of the Year by my peers. Being REALTOR of Year once was incredible...twice was something I never expected and will never forget. I've served on and chaired several REALTOR committees for my local Board of REALTORS as well as being a State and local Director and an orientation instructor.

I am very active at the New Hampshire Association of REALTORS®, a long-term member of and have chaired the professional standards committee, the legal committee, served on the Executive committee for 2 years. I have just been selected to chair the Professional Development committee

I am also a National Association of REALTORS® Professional Standards and Mediation instructor. Currently I am serving a 3 year term on the National Association of REALTORS® Professional Standards committee.
