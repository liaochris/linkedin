As a graduate of one of the finest engineering schools in the country you may be wondering how Laura Rubinstein got into the world of marketing and executive coaching. Well it’s a long story. But what's important is that she uses her analytical nature along with her interpersonal skills and creativity to help business owners sky rocket their business success. Laura will align with your vision and co-create a strategy to reach your professional and personal goals.

Laura has trained with many top marketing experts in the United States. She has helped hundreds of financial advisors and small business owners create a flow of ideal clients and increase their level of purpose and passion in their work and with clients. Her clients find creating more abundance of clients easier than ever because of the simple and effective methods she guides her clients with.

If you’re ready to take your businesses to the next level, you must work with her. Laura is passionate about relationship based marketing strategies that get results while limiting expense. Her approach uses a combination of developmental coaching and marketing consulting. You will be guided to create and implement a marketing system that works for you.
