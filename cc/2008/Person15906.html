Lucas Janin’s Summary

I work as an Effects Animator at Dreamworks Animation in Glendale, California.

My main task is to analyze and develop Effects set-ups, and these automatic or semi-automatic tools are used by the whole effects team. It's always fun to get back into the production side of things.

I've worked on several movies namely Kung Fu Panda, Journey to the Center of the Earth, 300, Final Destination 3, Fantastic Four, Exorcist 0, Scooby-Doo 2 and Decoys. I have worked on quite a few TVHD shows (James Cameron's Expedition: Bismarck, 10.5 Apocalypse, Journey to the Center of the Earth, Dinosaur Planet,...). Before Montreal, I lived in Barcelona (Spain) / where I worked for Videoefecto on commercials.

Technical Skills
FX Developer / Animator :
->Analyzing and developing set-ups and tools for FX and others departments (automatic or semi-automatic).
->Doing fx shots.
14 years of 3d experience in production.
Excellent programming knowledge in MEL (Maya scripting).
Good renderman knowledge : RIB, Slim, Aflred,..
Good communication and interpersonal skills with development team (pipeline and tools).
Good problem-solver technical limits to reach the wanted effects in respect with the delays.
