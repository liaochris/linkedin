Massimo Morigi’s Summary

PR consultant for both B2B and B2C firms, in charge of their entire communication campaign in Italy. I help them in raising their brand awareness and positioning as leaders in their respective industry. Highlight results include coverage on IT, vertical and business press, major daily and weekly publications and TV as well.

Ideal future: working with international companies helping them to create/maintain business relationship with Italy.
