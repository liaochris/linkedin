Erik Fors-Andrée’s Summary

My goal is to develop the use of the web as a tool for communication, entertainment, business and democracy.

I currently operate Stockholm Creative (Strategic Advertising Agency) and am involved in two startups: Wobserve (user generetad editorial content) and Smallfolks (ubiquitous online social presence). As project manager in internet strategy and production I've worked with clients such as the Swedish Armed Forces, the Swedish National Television and the National Board of Health and Welfare.
