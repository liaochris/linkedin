Robin Troy’s Summary

I have a long history in the digital design field. In the early days of multimedia I designed interactive kiosks and presentations for the Center for Multimedia and Parallel Communications. During the interactive CD-ROM boom I worked as an Art Director and Production Manager for Mindsai Interactive to launch titles such as the "Animal Planet" CD-ROM (Discovery Channel) and the "Disney Explorer" CD-ROM (Disney interactive.) I was a Product Designer for Microsoft in the MSN division for eight years. After my stint at Microsoft I worked as a Project Manager for Filter Studios, a small design studio that produces high quality interactive projects primarily for Microsoft. I am currently working as a UX Designer 11 for the Office Design Group at Microsoft.
