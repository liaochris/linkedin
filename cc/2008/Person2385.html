Barbara Hastings-Asatourian’s Summary

Barbara qualified in nursing midwifery and health visiting before entering higher education as a Senior Lecturer in Public Health.

She now develops and authors unique sex education materials and resources such as Contraception the Board Game, Contraception the Computer Game and Safer Sex:An Interactive Learning Resource on Video and DVD.

Her aim? To have an impact on sexually transmitted infections and unwanted pregnancies amongst teenagers worldwide, and promote assertiveness and negotiation skills for relationships.

Barbara Hastings-Asatourian’s Specialties:

Writing for publication in health topics. Links to several publications available on http://www.contraceptioneducation.co.uk/profile.htm
