Naina Redhu’s Summary

Shifting base every two years - the effects of being an Army Officer's daughter. Unforgettable school days from The Army Public School, Dhaula Kuan, New Delhi. From a regular B.Com. to an interesting MBA in IT & Systems from SCMHRD, Pune to a one-year stint as Management Consultant. Bashing on regardless into the creative space by setting up a design studio by the name 'aside' [ www.aside.in & www.aside.in/blog ] delivering graphic design, logo & identity design services to clients from all over the globe.

Naina is a freelancer. A huge proponent of the power of the Internet. Logo design is a passion - with a goal to be the best logo designer on the planet. The obsession for travel is helped immensely by the freelancing lifestyle. Picture perfect - a Moleskine sketchbook & pencil designing a logo for the latest software startup in the States, while sitting in a Snake Boat, navigating one of the waterways in Kerala.
