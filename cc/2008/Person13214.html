Jose Roy’s Summary

Hello,

Thank you for visiting my official profile!

I have vast experience and profound knowledge in varied businesses since I have been in this field for more than quarter of a century as an entrepreneur and later as a business strategist to my previous company. I was fortunate to play a pivotal role in creating several business models in making my previous company climb up the ladder in e-commerce.

My capability to provide timely solution and innovative ideas in various fields including marketing, sales, organizational ergonomics, recruiting judgements, etc. have helped both companies to avoid unwanted expenses, and thereby insured higher profits.

Today, I can proudly declare I am confident to provide business strategies to any company or organization on a temporary as well as regular basis.

Business Strategies

• Ascertaining the potentiality of a product/service in a particular market (feasibility Study).
• Determining the location, budget, initial strength of the team, outsourcing, etc.
• Setting up diverse marketing strategies on a ‘street to street’ basis.
• Sales strategies such as potential tie ups, possible offers, fixing price flexibility, etc.
• Time-bound programs to achieve realistic growth taking into consideration the effects of market variables.

The second phase, ‘expansion strategies’ will include different set of parameters, yet it would be, moreover similar to the start up strategies.
