Laura Mangraviti’s Summary

Significative experience in the public relation world – within the international agencies and networks in Milan.
Experienced account, from five years I’ve been developing contacts and projects for multinational clients mainly on IT and TLC, and other sectors (entertainment, sport, consumer, PA), and progressively perfectioning my competences and relations especially with the press, and the marketing players, always by focusing on the business generation.
