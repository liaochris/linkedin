Callie Carling’s Summary

Moonpoppy Enterprises is a “Virtual Assistant” business, providing administrative, secretarial and specialist support and consultancy services to clients throughout the UK.

With 18+ years of executive-level experience within HR, legal and public sector environments, Moonpoppy Enterprises was set up to provide exceptional high-quality, efficient, flexible, confidential and expert ‘virtual’ solutions to meet today's fast-paced requirements for every business type.

Moonpoppy Enterprises acts as a professional “virtual hand” to individuals, new businesses, SME's (small-medium enterprises) and organisations who fully appreciate the value of partnering with a Virtual Assistant (VA) and the benefits that such a partnership can bring, enabling them to focus on developing and growing their business while the more routine elements are tackled by their cherry-picked VA.

Our expanding client list includes small businesses who need assistance with spreadsheet development, developing HR and administration systems and any other type of service which can be easily handled 'remotely', organisations who need an overflow service for transcription or development of system manuals (e.g.) and complementary therapists who need know-how and IT skills to make best use of their marketing resources.

Holistic Feathers is my "other hat" - my inquisitive mind led me to train as a holistic therapist and energy healer - helping clients to heal, de-stress and re-focus in a safe, gentle, nurturing environment by using specialist therapeutic treatments and tools. An eclectic mix indeed ... I now specialise in facial rejuvenation (natural face lift massage), Raw Gaia living facials and hot stone massage, in addition to being a Ripple wellbeing presenter.

I am committed to empowering people to develop their own talents through networking and individual opportunities.
