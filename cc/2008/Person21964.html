Rolando Hernandez’s Summary

A thought leader in business rules management and knowledge management, Rolando founded BIZRULES in 1995 to help companies manage and digitize rules, processes, knowledge, and decisioning. He has led and contributed to strategic digitization projects for Mobil, Pepsi, a large mfg firm, IBM, EDS, BK, RCCL, Cunard, and CSX.

Rolando’s expertise is in eliciting, documenting and digitizing knowledge, business rules, and business requirements from Subject Matter Experts (SMEs) so it can be deployed using BPM and BRE/BRMS technologies.

By automating the Knowledge Supply Chain, Rolando and his firm BIZRULES helps companies:
• prevent business mistakes
• reduce time to market
• improve quality
• ensure regulatory and statutory compliance (SOX)

BIZRULES is a business and technology consulting firm specializing in:
• Business Proces Redesign
• Business Process Management
• Business Rules Management
• Knowledge Management
