Michael Gibbons’s Summary

• Talented writer, producer, and translator with a focus on the independent and world film community.
• Extensive experience with content development for online media, from marketing to publishing, as well as a strong personal interest in new opportunities presented by this industry.
• Broad festival experience, including participation in the Media Panel at Cinequest, It's All True International Documentary Film Festival, coverage of São Paulo International Film Festival, Mix Brasil, São Paulo International Short Film Festival, FIICAV (Feira Internacional da Indústria de Cinema e AudioVisual), IFC Center iQ&A, Veneza Cinema Italiano. Attended Berlinale 2003-2004, with programming experience in the Reeling Chicago Lesbian & Gay Film Festival and LesGaiCineMad (Madrid).
• Focused on the emerging Latin American film scene, from new talents to industry developments.
• Simultaneous interpretation, translation, and subtitling for television and film.
• Fluent in English, Portuguese, and Spanish.

Michael Gibbons’s Specialties:
