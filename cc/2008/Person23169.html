Marcel Sendejo’s Summary

Passion for sales profession & technology which is why I am always studying. Sales equals continuing education by constantly surveying relationships, business environments & communication. College was the cornerstone. Studying business in day to day sales events & beyond my career. I attend summits & training on sales & marketing so I can study processes & new technology developments. I attend many conferences like Selling Power, Buying & Selling eContent, Direct Marketing Association, Sirius Decision Summit, Warrillow Summit, SLA, SCIP (Society of Competitive Intelligence Professionals), SIIA, Inc 500 & many more. Also attending training on negotiations with companies like ThinkInc.

Another top priority is networking & cultivating a positive & mutually beneficial relationships & network. I'm happy to make a network connection with you in LinkedIn if I have negotiated, trained or met you.. I'm very careful to properly cultivate our mutual network of professional contacts. *** I won't burden my contacts with requests more than "two degrees" away & please write compelling & detailed requests for me to consider forwarding.

I have worked & studied direct sales & marketing at Dell, learned software & network security at Netiq. I have participated in startups trial & tribulations involving setting up processes for success at companies like Nuview (sold to Brocade) & AlterPoint (an Austin Ventures startup). I have run my own real estate investment company as a sole proprietor. Currently I am studying & closing business for Hoover’s & work heavily in acquisition sales & trade show events. I have been invited to be panelist & speaker at a couple events & have grown through discussion of technology. Hoover’s has given me the opportunity to grow through attending many events which I feel has help our organization to grow, learn, & adapt especially communications & processes between sales, marketing, & product.
