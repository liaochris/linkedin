YL Catino’s Summary

Hello! Thanks for viewing my information!

I have been in HR-OD for over twenty years, doing everything from generalist work to recruiting to organizational development and effectiveness. This combination of experience brings added value to organizations when dealing with Human Capital strategy issues.

Specific work experience with F500s has centered on strategizing with leadership on a variety of issues: process improvement (efficiency/quality), leadership readiness evaluations, and technological interventions for HR. Other specific experience includes: executive recruitment, corporate culture analysis (incl M&A), strategic workforce planning, assessments, and success composites.

Innovations:

1. The Strategic Leadership Model (C)
2. Cognitive Interviewing for Recruiters(C)
3. Co-developer of a 360 assessment (44,000 companies use it)

Companies that I have worked with include Lawson Software, Honeywell, NASA, Google, eBay, Sun Microsystems, Microsoft, United Technologies, Wells Fargo, USBank, TCF Bank, Wal-Mart, Joint Chiefs of Staff (White House), Boston Scientific, Medtox, and SSA as well as smaller to mid-size companies. I also serve as a Board member for the Center for Strategic Information Technology and Security.

Please feel free to connect with me at 612.321.6273, or linkedin (ylcatino@yahoo.com) to network, consult, or just connect for future possibilities. All inquiries are kept in the strictest of confidence.
