Nancy Laine’s Summary

Also known as "The Matchmaker", Nancy Laine is a professional Connector with a heartfelt passion for promoting humanitarian & green projects worldwide.

Nancy's delight in life-long learning, coupled with her unending quest for personal and spiritual growth, has led to widespread recognition of her talents as a highly-intuitive Life Coach, enthusiastic YouTube Video Producer, expert Numerologist, prolific writer and an entertaining talk show guest.

Although she has been a longtime resident of Tampa Bay, Nancy's healthy sense of humor and love of adventure has led to a life full of spice and variety.

At the age of 19 she visited Alaska on a 1-week vacation and stayed for 22 years. During that time she experienced history in the making while working on the Alaska Pipeline, an opportunity resulting from a networking contact.

Nancy went on to graduate Cum Laude from the University of Alaska in Anchorage with a B.A. in Journalism & Public Communications, specializing in Television Production.

Immediately after graduating, she was offered a position with the local CBS station where she assisted in the sales and production of a variety of television commercials and documentaries.

This marked the beginning of a successful sales career for Nancy, during which she became a $2+ Million Producer in the health and life insurance industry with a extensive personal and professional network.

She has met many of her most valued friends and contacts while attending live events and trainings such as The School for The Work of Byron Katie, CEO Space, Money and You, Tony Robbins' seminars, Avatar, and The Millionaire Mind.

The story of the highly-synchronistic events leading to her star-studded career as a professional Numerologist, Life Coach & Online Video Producer can be found on her website at www.NancyLaine.com.
