  • Accredited Home-Staging Specialist

    First Impressions by Carlee Colonneso

    (Design industry)

    July 2007 — Present (1 year 4 months)

    Home staging is giving a home that is ready to be put on or is already on the market, a move in ready feel. How this is achieved is by enhancing the home's curb appeal, making the home look larger, creating ambience through colors, lighting and smells, bringing attention to the home’s most appealing features, and making sure there are no physical items or issues with the home that would turn a potential buyer off.

    Sellers have to look at their home as a marketable item and try to look at it from a potential buyers perspective.

    As a Professional Home Stager, this is where I come in.

    I can determine how to ready the interior of a home for listing photos and open houses. Plus, as a bonus I can make recommendation’s to the outside of the home to prepare it for day to day viewing.
