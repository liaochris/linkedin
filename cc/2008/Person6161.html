Dave Elchoness’s Summary

I am the Executive Director of the Association of Virtual Worlds, a virtual worlds association focused on the advancement of the industry, education, and outreach.

I am also founder & CEO of VRWorkplace, where I advise companies on how to use virtual worlds technology for maximum impact, primarily in the workplace.

I am an accomplished employment law attorney, HR consultant, and former Fortune 200 IT outsourcing executive.
