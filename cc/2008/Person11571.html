Jessica Williams’s Summary

I just completed a MBA in Sustainable Management. My graduation venture plan focused on potential tools and services to help municipalities achieve their ghg emissions reduction goals. In particular I am interested in marketing and communication programs in sustainability, with an emphasis on encouraging people to take action around climate change.

This work leverages previous experience as an education and sales executive in project management, sales, marketing, and organizational leadership for technology education companies and non-profit organizations.

I am looking for an influential management position in an organization committed to incorporating sustainability into its operations and customer offerings.
