Robin Jamison-Hernandez’s Summary

While my Bachelors degree is in Sculptural Ceramics, with a minor in Art History, I have extensive training and experience in painting, sculpture, drawing, fiber arts, printmaking, graphic design, and web design. Currently, I enjoy working as an artist and art instructor, a web designer, and the founder and manager of an online international artists cooperative. Within the next year, I will begin work on my Masters in Art Therapy.

My professional background includes art instruction (painting, ceramics, and sculpture), graphic design, web design, pre-press service bureau operations, business management, and accounting.

Robin Jamison-Hernandez’s Specialties:

Ceramics, sculpture, painting, drawing, printmaking, fiber arts, graphic & web design, business management, teaching, writing & editing, organizing, problem solving, listening, and motivating.
