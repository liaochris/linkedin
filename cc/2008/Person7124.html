Sally Elatta’s Summary

Ms. Elatta has an extensive background in software development, consulting, mentoring, course development and training. She is experienced in Architecting, Developing and Mentoring clients on SOA, Agile/Scrum, Java and .Net technologies.

As a trainer, she has helped companies upgrade the skill set of their employees by designing custom programs tailored to each company and each individual student and includes an individual assessment, self study resources, courses and a final project. Sally has received extremely high ratings and excellent feedback on the results of this type of training program. She has delivered many Java, .Net, Service Oriented Architecture (SOA) and Agile courses to various companies. Her passion for seeing her students succeed contributes greatly to her success as a trainer.

As a consultant and coach, she has helped many organizations transition to using new technologies by coaching them through the process. Sally has done well mentoring various technical team members at any level (beginner to Sr engineers) and has also helped coach business analysts, project managers and senior executives on adopting these new technologies to their projects and culture.

As a speaker she is involved in presenting various seminars for conferences, user groups, public seminars held at ASCIT's site and many custom seminars for various clients.

She has worked with medium, large and fortune 500 clients and helped them take advantage of the power of Agile development and Service Oriented Architecture and worked with issues such as governance, service identification, agile acceptance in a waterfall culture and many others.
