Thelia Foster’s Summary

I create beautiful, one of a kind pieces, using the uniqueness of each stone in a magical capacity.. I love creating fine items, whether sculpture, jewelry, or decorative items. I consider my work to be a blessing and I put a lot of energy in to making sure that each piece is perfect - perfect for the one it is intended for. The possesiveness of my clients with their pieces confirm what I know when I create them;each piece is destined for someone specific, it is special for them.

My goal is to have my jewelry available internationally.
My Ethinc Origins line will be available on line very soon, as well as develop a comprehensive men's line.. I hope to expand my audience for the Unique Pieces found in my Goddess line, and just continue sharing beautiful designs with people.
