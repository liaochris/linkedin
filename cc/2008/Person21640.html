    As president of Felber & Felber Marketing, Rob Felber is responsible for the overall direction, administration and positioning of the firm. He provides clients with advice, directs associates on projects and is directly involved in all business development.

  • Team member and public information officer

    Hillcrest Technical Rescue Team

    (Government Agency; Government Administration industry)

    January 1992 — Present (16 years 5 months)

    The Hillcrest Technical Rescue Team (HTRT) is a nine community rescue team comprised of firefighters from the Hillcrest (NE Ohio) area.
    The HTRT has been trained to rescue victims trapped on ice, in swift water and dive rescue & recovery incidents. Additionally, the team utilizes extensive rope and rigging experience to rescue victims in ravines or other places inaccessible to fire service ladders. Other treacherous situations include high towers (such as radio and cell towers), confined spaces, trenches & excavation collapses. The team has also been trained in hazardous materials (HAZMAT) and structural collapse.

    The HTRT is registered with Federal, State and County emergency response agencies.

    July 1983 — Present (24 years 11 months)

    Part-time firefighter and paramedic for the City of Pepper Pike. Duties include rescue, fire supression, medical response and public safety.
