Dustin Wax’s Summary

I hold an MA in cultural anthropology from the New School for Social Research and a BA from UC Santa Cruz, both with honors in the major. For the last 4-plus years, I have been teaching anthropology at the College of Southern Nevada, and for the last two I've been teaching women's studies at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas. I have worked with non-profit organizations, museums, and dot-com startups, where I have done everything from organizing catering to putting together exhibition brochures to interviewing researchers to writing marketing and educational copy to redesigning websites.

My edited volume on anthropologists during the Cold War is due out soon from Pluto Books. I also do freelance writing for print and online publication, focusing primarily on issues in diversity and education, as well as on technology, writing, and organization skills. My work can be seen regularly at lifehack.org.
