Abigail Shaw’s Summary

Abigail Shaw has been working on the issues of web-facing knowledge management for Fireman’s Fund Insurance for nine years. Her experience there spans the development and maintenance of an enterprise intranet, the implementation of a variety of content management systems, overhauls of public websites and extranets, a podcasting program, on-demand video, and consulting with internal customers on very specific knowledge management programs and problems. She is currently Director, External Websites, but for five years her title was simply Infomaster.

Before joining Fireman’s Fund, Abby was a Senior Project Director for Systemhouse Corp. in their image management technology group. Previously, she was President of Shaw, Novinger & Odell, which was purchased by Systemhouse. In that role, and for Rothchild Consultants, Abby provided business and technology consulting advice on image and document management systems and technologies. Her clients included Federated Department Stores, Genentech, the Veterans’ Benefits Association, Fleet, Pfizer, the State of California, the State of Alaska, the European Patent Office, and Caterpillar. She has spoken at many industry conferences, including Comdex, AIIM, ARMA, IMC, TOC, and KM World. You can contact her at abbyshaw@earthlink.net.

Abigail Shaw’s Specialties:

Website management, information architecture, website strategy, interface concepts, usability analysis and testing, web project management, editorial and writing support, marketing product management, distributed publishing, digital standards, web content planning, distribution channel communications, integrated online and print campaigns, marketing campaign planning and execution, online lead generation programs.
