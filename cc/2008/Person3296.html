Brian Critchfield’s Summary

Marketing doesn't happen in campaigns. It comes from the navel. I have developed a unique formula that teaches companies how to first develop a business worth talking about and then build armies of customer evangelists to spread the word. I have developed this formula through my work with hundreds of companies over 16 years in marketing and sales, the last 5 as a consultant, Chief Strategist, and President of successful word-of-mouth marketing agencies.
