Ed Hale’s Summary

Optum Consulting Business, based in New York, is a business consulting and services firm that has one very clear objective: to provide solutions to help people. We supply valuable insight, information, knowledge, and expertise to entrepreneurs and small to mid-sized businesses to take what they have and make it better -- so they make more money. Often times entrepreneurs and start-ups just need simple information to get started or to move to the next level in their industry. We handle projects that range in time from fifteen minutes to several months. We are a hands-on database that can answer any question that someone might need to move up and make more money.

Optum Consulting Health was a simple side business that started innocently enough offering nutritional supplement consulting to friends and friends of friends due to my being in the health industry for many years. It soon turned into a full time job due to word of mouth. The idea was simple enough: answer people's questions and offer advice on what vitamin and nutritional supplements they should take based on their specific needs, their age, gender, and health concerns. I do this on a per-minute fee basis -- not hourly -- with no minimum, and no appointment or consultation necessary.

That way, the client receives only the information they want or need, they don't have to spend an initial "consultation fee" of two to three hundred dollars, and they can email or call as often as they need to and only get charged for the amount of time it takes us to answer their questions or concerns.

Clients are more than happy to use the service and continue to recommend us to their friends and family. A real win-win for all involved, both the company and our clients.

Transcendent Media Group is an entertainment company in New York City that houses a record label, a publishing company, a PR firm, and a television production company. Primary focus is music, publishing, online video content, and television.

  • Founder and President

    Sebastians Smoothies Juice Bars

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; JUICE; Restaurants industry)

    January 1996 — October 1998 (2 years 10 months)

    Hale founded the Sebastians Smoothies Juice Bar chain in 1996 in FL with 2 partners due to their shared passion for juicing fresh fruits and vegetables and concocting healthy and delicious smoothies. Two years yielded much fun and learning but the juice bar business is not a high-profit-margin business model. A lot of waste, high employee turn-over due to the necessity for low wages, and Smoothie King and Jamba Juice were about to enter into a fierce competition with one another.
    So with a touch of sadness and much relief we closed the stores after two years. A good call. Jamba Juice got a truckload of venture capital less than two years later and started becoming the behemoth that it is today.

    I am still asked on occasion by friends and clients about their desire to start a juice bar. My advice is always the same. If you want to run it yourself all day and night and make it a little mom and pop, you might be able to squeeze a living out of it. But you aren't going to get rich.
