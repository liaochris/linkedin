Cliff Hall’s Summary

Software Architect with over 25 years experience in the industry.

My career has run the gamut from writing games in machine language for Commodore 64 and Apple II to general accounting applications on archaic mini-computers like the Datapoint and Data General to the implementation of large scale, object-oriented, enterprise applications.

Currently, I own my own consulting firm, Futurescale, Inc, an Adobe Solution Partner. I spend my time architecting Rich Internet Applications in Adobe Flex, Flash and AIR for major corporations and extremely cool startups worldwide.

I also run the open-source PureMVC Framework project, which implements a single methodology for creating robust scalable applications. It has proven so successful in its original implementation for ActionScript, it is in the process of being ported to all major development platforms.
