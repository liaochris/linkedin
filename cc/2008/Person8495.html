Gary Thomas’s Summary

For more than 27 years, I have helped businesses realize the full potential of their products by applying creative approaches to product, solutions, channel and direct marketing. My experience includes Marketing Manager at Cisco Systems, Product Marketing Director at Nortel Networks and Bay Networks, Solutions Marketing Manager at SynOptics Communications, Group Manager for Network Products at AT&T Bell Laboratories and Field Support Engineering at Verizon.

I have been responsible for sales readiness and marketing to the Fortune 1000 for product lines that generated in excess of US$1.4 billion and have developed new channels through direct marketing, cross-selling, selective recruiting and applying Internet technologies.

My networking experience includes Gigabit Ethernet, Fast Ethernet, Ethernet, Token Ring, ATM, FDDI; routers, switches, appliances, access points, hubs, management. My security experience includes firewalls, IP-SEC, URL filters, VPN, SSL VPN, NAC, Cisco IOS Intrusion Prevention System (IPS). My Voice over IP (VoIP) experience includes: Functions (PBX, IVR), Media (PoE, VoBB), Gateways (SIP, H.323, PSTN/IP) and Protocols (MGCP, TGCP, SRTP, SS7/C7, PESQ). My wireless experience includes WiMAX, WiFi, 802.11b/g/n, 2G/3G/GSM, Cisco Land Mobile Radio.

For more information, please visit http://thomasland.org/resume where you can download HTML, DOC or PDF versions of my resume.
