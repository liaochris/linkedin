William Bateman’s Summary

* National advocate of fathers and fatherhood; have recently launched a national public media event called, "Hike4Fathers Across America." This project will require my hiking nearly 2,200-miles from Georgia to Maine; a journey requiring a full six months of wilderness hiking. This experience will become the basis for an inspirational book encouraging fathers to consider the incredible importance of their role in the family, community and nation. This effort will benefit a wonderful organization for teens called, "Fellowship of Christian Athletes." Hike4Fathers begins in earnest on June 1st, 2008 at Mt. Kitahdin, Maine (Baxter State Park).

* A veteran mid-level executive; extensive experience in full service landscape management services for resort club destinations, multi-family apartment home communities and property owner/homeowner associations. Coordinated the construction, maintenance and operation of a 125-square mile fully automated satellite radio controlled system designed to maintain the largest "Live Oak" tree transplant program ever attempted anywhere in the world at Sea Island Company's 3,000 acre Frederica Residential/Golf property.

* Well versed in community based outreach projects directly related to non-profit management, board presentations, networking and national scale public media events involving the fatherless, fatherhood and abused and abandoned children.

* Efforts to bring national and local awareness to the plight of fatherless children; have been featured on National Public Radio, numerous magazine and newsprint publications, corporate speaking invitations to Carnival Cruise Lines and Bank United of Florida and Christian radio stations.

* Created and produce a daily Internet radio program featuring 30 years of Contemporary Christian music called, CCM Gold Gospel Rock Radio. This program is currently heard in more than 125 countries, numerous U.S. military bases and stateside; in all 50 states and commonwealths.
