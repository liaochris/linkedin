Heather Boerner’s Summary

Heather Boerner is a freelance writer and editor with more than 10 years experience in journalism. She's worked as a daily and weekly newspaper reporter, in the trenches of a start-up Web site and as an independent freelance writer who draws on all that experience to produce prompt, accurate and engaging copy for consumer and business publications as well as corporate clients. Her work has appeared in publications including Alternative Medicine Magazine, Yoga Journal, The San Francisco Chronicle, The San Francisco Chronicle Sunday Magazine, Common Sense Media, BabyCenter, and Yahoo! Hot Jobs. She specializes in any topic or style of writing that allows her to make connections between sources with something to say and her readers. For more information, visit her Web site at www.heatherboerner.com.
