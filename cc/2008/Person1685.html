B. W. Smith’s Summary

Author of "Venomous Snakes in Captivity: Safety and Husbandry".

Owner of Animal South LLC (formerly Southern Reptile Education) providing innovative educational programs and conservation initiatives as well as a wide array of professional services. Current conservation initiatives include the first captive breeding program in Georgia of the Eastern Indigo Snake (Drymarchon couperi) as well as Gopher Tortoise Surveys in Southern Georgia.

Professional Animal Wrangling Services for Television and Film.

SHHS Educator of the Year.
