Richard Kastelein’s Summary

Specialist in Social Media Architecture, Analysis and Strategy - Community/SNS building using Open Source technology.

Adept team player - a publisher, writer, photographer, marketing director, web developer and graphic designer with more than 20 years experience in the development and operation of newspapers, magazines, web media and marketing of multinational, multimillion dollar companies in international settings.

A well travelled individual and yachtsman (24,000 nm offshore) with trips overland and by sea (two TransAtlantics/one TransPac) to over 50 countries around the world, with outstanding problem solving skills and a diverse array of skills. Published first article to crewing on boats as a lifestyle called 'The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Sea' in 1991. Lived aboard several sailboats in Caribbean for more than a decade and built 1.5 million dollar catamaran in Brazil in 2004.

• Social Media Analyst and Publisher, Advisory Board Member of Tel Aviv-based Zephyr Financial Technologies.

• Chief Networking Officer, board member and shareholder of Sparkling Media UK heading the Social Networking, Community, and web components of Twinners - a Cross Media Social Platform (CMSP).

• Built own company in Holland – Expathos – focusing on Information Architecture - the construct and analysis of the user experience in relation to delivery of information on the net.

• Managed the marketing departments of two Caribbean-based multinational marine companies.

• As an editor of several publications, management duties included 'the buck stops here' roles – directing freelance writers and photographers.

• Skippering crewed sailboats over oceans over 15 years entailed many critical management skills including emergency and lifesaving training, ensuring of adequate food stores and water, storm preparation and coordination of crew for transoceanic voyages.
