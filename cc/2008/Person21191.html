Renee Chirico’s Summary

I am a second generation auto dealer, and am one of the few female dealers in the area. Out of the almost 250 Ford/Lincoln-Mercury dealers in the tri-state area, only 7 are women. I have embraced my position with the responsibilty to address the needs of all clients, both male and female:

* Caring customer service in all departments
* Respect and fair treatment throughout the purchase process
* Attentiveness to our female customers
* Building a woman-friendly environment
* Honesty and high standards in all departments
* Corporate involvement in activities for women
* Recruitment of female personnel in all departments

Dedication to this commitment is demonstrated to our clients at
all levels of our organization. Our entire organization has worked
hard to make us one of the fastest growing dealerships in the area, ranking us among the top 25 dealers in the Nation.
