Urvaksh Karkaria’s Summary

Born and raised in Mumbai (Bombay) India, Urvaksh is a fourth-generation journalist who has worked in newsrooms across two continents.
Most of Urvaksh's experience has been as a business writer. Over the past nine years he has covered the health care, insurance, manufacturing, transportation and economic development beats.
Urvaksh has diversified beyond the print medium — breaking news stories on Fort Wayne’s ABC affiliate and local radio as part of convergence partnerships. The experience boosted Urvaksh's credibility with sources and introduced him to the discipline and demands of working across mediums.

Urvaksh Karkaria’s Specialties:

Ability to turn around stories on tight deadlines, grasp complex issues quickly, connect the dots and provide the information in a reader-friendly language.
