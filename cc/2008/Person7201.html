Mark Elster’s Summary

Hello.

My name is Mark Elster, an award-winning Interactive Creative Director. I have a proven track record of translating business goals into innovative designs for brand identities and bringing them to fruition on the web, in print collateral and for corporate presentations.

With a passion for typography, color, and imagery, my creative expertise and thorough knowledge of CSS and web standards enable a smooth transition between design and implementation. I am focused on designing a better user experience for community-driven websites using the “latest and greatest” Open Source Content Management Platform. ;)

My leadership experience has directed fast-paced teams in strategic planning, creative development and technical implementation, often bridging the gap between designers and engineers. I can produce results in high-pressure situations.

I have worked on projects for 3Com, BeVocal, Brightmail, C&H Sugar, Contivo, E.piphany, Fujitsu, Macromedia, Nuance, Palm Computing, Reconnex, Sun Microsystems, Symantec, and Verisign to name a few.

Oh, and I am available for hire.
