Khaiersta English’s Summary

Khaiersta leads the creative team with a strong and varied background in graphic design and copywriting. Khaiersta started Flower Press Creative Studio in 2001 to serve the design needs of small to medium-sized businesses. She has worked in various full-time creative roles including Creative Director for The Trada Group in San Francisco, Copywriter for Jackson & Perkins online and Harry and David Stores, and Interactive Designer for Nike US Public Relations. She holds a degree in Advertising from the School of Journalism and Communication at the University of Oregon.

Graphic Design, Website Design, User Interface Design
