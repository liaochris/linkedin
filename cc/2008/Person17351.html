Dave Meeker’s Summary

In January 2008, I accepted a management position at Roundarch and came on-board as a User Experience Strategy Lead in the Chicago office. With offices in New York, Boston, DC and Chicago, I will be working with national clients on user experience strategy, and providing other strategic consulting services.

I recently moved on from a 2-year tenure as managing partner of EffectiveUI, a leader in strategy, design and development of experience innovation and customer engagement experiences.

Before joining EffectiveUI, I was a principal at Machete, a Chicago firm that
specializes in the strategy and design of passive and interactive user experiences, broadcast TV, museum installation concept design / production, and interactive (Software) design and development. Prior to Machete, I led the rich media and web innovation efforts and served as a Director of Custom Application Development for divine, inc. and later WHITTMANHART, inc.

I have been responsible for the direction of creative technology used for client solutions, business development, strategic design and executive counsel on digital design and technology issues, and am considered an expert in experience design strategy, interaction design and customer engagement.

Clients have included Northern Trust, NBC, Dow Jones, McDonald's, eBay, Scion, Ford, GE (NBC Universal), Shure, inc, Harley-Davidson, Bank of America, Progressive Insurance, CBOE, Macromedia/Adobe, Dentsu Holdings/Dentsu America, DraftFCB, Digitas, Taylor, Bean and Whitaker, The Cleveland Indians, The Cleveland Browns, The State of Ohio, Sunglass Hut, Lending Tree, Blockbuster Entertainment, The Florida Marlins, Purell, American Greetings, Abbot Laboratories, Safeguard Scientifics and others.

Dave Meeker’s Specialties:

Customer Engagement, Design & Technology Strategy, Experiential Marketing, Web application architecture and design, interactive content strategy, online brand development, streaming media, video and interactive experience production, digital design and development process best practices and professional services management.
