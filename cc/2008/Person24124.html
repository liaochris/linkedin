Stephen Fairley’s Summary

Stephen Fairley is a nationally recognized law firm marketing expert, a Master Business Coach, professional speaker, best selling author of 10 books and CEO of The Rainmaker Institute, the nation's largest law firm marketing company specializing in small law firms.

Stephen specializes in helping attorneys at small law firms and and solo legal professionals rapidly increase their revenues by building a referral-based marketing system.

Over the past several years more than 6,000 attorneys at hundreds of law firms have discovered how to find more clients fast and generate more referrals using the proven Rainmaker Marketing System.

Stephen is the bestselling author of 10 books and 6 audio programs including: The Rainmaker Retreat CD set (2008), Practice Made Perfect for Lawyers (2005), Becoming A Rainmaker CD (2005), The Business Coaching Toolkit (2007), 6 Keys for Successful Leaders (2003) and Getting Started in Personal and Executive Coaching (2004) (http://www.GettingStartedinCoaching.com), which became a best seller less than two months after release.

In addition to his work with attorneys, he is also an active Real Estate Investor specializing in high end resort properties, water front homes, and multi-family rentals. He is a member of Arizona Real Estate Investors Association (http://www.AZREIA.com), the Real Estate Investor Network (http://www.REINclub.com). He is also a member of the National Speakers Association.

When he is not speaking to a group of attorneys, coaching a business owner or working on a new book, you will find Stephen either traveling (he's visited 25 foreign countries), fishing anywhere they will let him, off-roading in the Colorado Rockies, or relaxing on a cruise ship (9 cruises in the last 4 years).

His personal philosophy of running a business is, “If I'm going to work hard, I have to play harder to stay focused.” You can reach Stephen at 888-588-5891.
