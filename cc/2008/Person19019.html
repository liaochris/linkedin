Nikki Lamagna’s Summary

Nikki has been a professional graphic designer since 2001, working both in-house and as a freelancer. Her experience ranges from production artist to lead designer. Whether she is working on a spot illustration for a catalog or developing a website, her speed, creativity and attention to detail have garnered respect amongst her peers and clients. Nikki’s expertise is in digital illustration, complex page layout projects and standards-based web 2.0 site design.

Nikki brings to the the table a unique perspective that utilizes the trendy, chaotic themes of her generation with the classic, clean lines of post-modern design that results in the creation of informative, usable and visually appealing projects. She is quite personable and easy to work with, meeting challenges with a quick smile and can-do attitude while always holding the client’s ideas in the highest regard. When she is presented with a new ‘problem’ situation, she uses it as an opportunity to expand her knowledge in order that she may become the catalyst between the client’s needs and a quick, successful overall project. As the Buddhists preach, we are all beginners.

Never one to abide by the status quo, Nikki has pushed boundaries in all aspects of her life. Her unique experiences from backpacking across America five times has taught her something about the human condition that she puts forth into every project she has the honor of being part of.

Nikki looks forward to meeting new clients. Even if you don’t need a designer, she’d be happy to meet you and swap a few stories. Call or email her. If for nothing else, you’ll get to hear about her times on the road.
