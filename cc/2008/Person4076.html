Chad Cooper’s Summary

As an editor- I follow a personal motto in all of my roles: Keep your user at heart when making your decisions. Without them- you're nowhere.

I am one of the few geeks who happens to really understand people/users. Some people have called me a type-A personality; stuck in a comedians body. This harmony is why I love positions where I am not only at the table for driving marketing/communications/sales- but, also having my hand in the overall user experience- such as assisting in the streamlining of UI's, hardware availability, network security, latency, and- above all CONTENT.

I am very easy to work with- and find myself meeting/having great discussion with people throughout the web/IT sphere about the new .com era. I am very proud to be apart of the new internet boom!
