Justin Sherman’s Summary

Recently I accepted a transfer to the Austin Medical Center, part of Mayo Health System, I began working there in late December 2007.

Previously I worked in process engineering and quality management at Lake City Medical Center, part of the Mayo Clinic Health System. Here I worked to secure a grant from Minnesota Job Skills Partnership, MJSP, for $340,000 to implement a lean health care training program at our hospital. The grant is a partnership between SE Tech, Riverland College and Lake City Medical Center. Our training began in April 2007 and will last 3 years. More than 500 employees will be trained due to this grant.

Previous experience with successfully managing all aspects of transportation; including implementing automated dispatching; selecting loads, managing drivers and training staff; developing and managing the account forecasts and budget; establishing and monitoring productivity goals; and leading teams on key account improvements.

I have designed layouts, organizational processes, and procedures for successful on time deliveries and most recently for patient care. I daily use proven leadership skills gained from managing large accounts, as well as increasing and managing multi-million dollar budgets.
