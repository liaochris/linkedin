Jonathan (J.J.) Thompson’s Summary

Mr. Jonathan "J.J." Thompson is a Partner with Rook Consulting, a professional services firm providing security, IT, and legal executives with discrete advisory services. Mr. Thompson also serves as the President of the Silicon Valley chapter of the Information Systems Security Association (ISSA).

Previously, as the Director of Business Development for Emagined Security, Mr. Thompson focused on Compliance Services, Channel Development, and Marketing. Mr. Thompson joined Emagined Security from Ernst & Young, LLP, where he led teams in delivery of services for Fortune 100 clients. Mr. Thompson focuses on IT compliance, IT controls assessment & design, audit impact reduction, incident response, security assessments, and risk assessment projects. Mr. Thompson lead special projects focused on financial system and control failures where he developed triage and incident handling methodologies focusing on the Financial Services and Healthcare industries.

Outside of Information Security, Mr. Thompson has held leadership positions for a variety of organizations and has been published. He has served as the Director of the Northern Region Conference for the American Marketing Association, President of the Management Information Systems Association & Board Member on the Henry B. Tippie College of Business Young Alumni Board. Mr. Thompson specializes in stimulating process improvement & organizational change by leveraging marketing, psychology, and technology principles to business and organizational challenges. By focusing on the efficient, scalable growth by leveraging technology and a thorough understanding of branding, Mr. Thompson has increased scalability, decreased costs, and increased brand recognition for a variety of organizations and companies.
