Scott Hample’s Summary

Currently a Marketing Research Consultant at XM Satellite Radio. Responsibilities include market analysis and competitive intelligence.

History of providing Marketing Research; e-Commerce, Search Engine Marketing and Search Engine Optimization; Customer Satisfaction and Customer Loyalty; CRM; market segmentation; Win/Loss Analysis; Advertising testing; as well as new product and package testing for such firms as McCormick & Company, ADP, IBM, BellSouth, Chick-fil-A, and Atlanta Gas Light Resources.

Created and managed competitive intelligence systems for use in strategic market planning. Designer of marketing information system (MKIS) to retrieve and analyze internal sales and marketing data. Developer of analytical models for use in sales territory realignment, deriving quotas, and forecasting in shaping pricing and sales strategies.

Data mining expert. Models used to predict amount of consumer consumption; sales trends and forecasting; and segment customers based upon profitability and future revenue streams. Model market share data to spot higher profitable customers and product/service lines. Data used to identify possible new geographic and customer markets. Skilled in SPSS, SAS, Business Objects, Crystal Reports, and Cognos.

Adjunct Marketing professor for Towson University and University of Phoenix; both graduate and undergraduate levels. Teach Principles of Marketing, e-Commerce, Consumer Behavior, Marketing Research and Statistics online and on campus.
