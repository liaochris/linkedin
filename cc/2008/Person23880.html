Stephen Pierzchala’s Summary

I am dedicated to the concepts of Web performance excellence, and actively promote these concepts in my work at the leading Web performance measurement firm. I have also designed my own experimental test-bed to better understand the complexity of a global measurement system.

I am also actively following trends and concepts in Social Media, and how people interact with each other with modern technologies.

Stephen Pierzchala’s Specialties:
