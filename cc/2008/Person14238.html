Jim Kaubisch’s Summary

Spearheading complex, customer focused initiatives from green fields to customer deliverable, in a wide spectrum of technology areas and delivering creative solutions has been a hallmark of my 20+-year career. I have managed complex endeavors requiring keen understanding of technology and business requirements and ability to "sell" solution approaches.

Experienced in directing top-performing teams and product groups in end-to-end development process, with a proven track record of delivering complex projects on time in high-stress environments.

Skilled at merging technology expertise with business, sales, and leadership skills, providing vital insight and direction to all aspects of corporate goals.
