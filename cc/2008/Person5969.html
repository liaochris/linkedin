David Verbustel’s Summary

Co-founder & CEO of iStockCV.com, an innovative job board using social recruiting features that help graduates and young professionals up to 3-years experience searching for a job. Let's say that iStockCV is halfway between job-boards like Monster.com and social networks like Facebook.com.

Previously I had spent time with Capgemini Consulting as a technical analyst on Oracle (more specially on financial solutions).

Education: I am a Management Engineer (BSc and MSc in Management at the Louvain School of Management Engineering, 2007) with majors in strategy and innovation, plus corporate finance; I got a 3-year interdisciplinary training in business creation (Université Catholique de Louvain, 2007). I also spent a term on exchange at the McCombs School of Business (University of Texas at Austin, 2006).

Interest: my areas of interest are entrepreneurship, new venture creation, small business management, innovation and web2.0

My cause: While in management school, I co-founded a non-profit organization to promote entrepreneurship. I'm and I will always be glad to help any young entrepreneur in her/his venture.
I was also member of the jury for a belgian-based business plan competition Start Academy for Young Entrepreneurs.
