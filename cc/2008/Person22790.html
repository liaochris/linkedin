Kare Anderson’s Summary

A foremost expert on profitable partnering and on becoming frequently-quoted. President of the Say it Better Center LLC. Publisher of the Say it Better eNewsletter, with 38,000 subscribers in 26 countries. Presenter and Emmy-winning former NBC TV and Wall Street Journal reporter. Author of Getting What You Want, SmartPartnering, LikeABILITY, Beauty Inside Out, Resolving Conflict Sooner and Walk Your Talk: Grow Your Business Faster Through Successful Cross-Promotional Partnerships. Former board member Annie's Homegrown. Co-founder of nine PACs. Strategic communication coach to 18 pre-IPO management teams.
