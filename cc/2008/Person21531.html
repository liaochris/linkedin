Rob Duncan’s Summary

Senior manager and technology commercialization professional. Market research and competitive intelligence expert. Sub-specialty in teambuilding and leadership development. Highly adaptive, resourceful professional. Polished communicator and public speaker. Long-time college instructor in market research, competitive intelligence and relationship selling. Author of "Haul Away!: Teambuilding Lessons from a Voyage around Cape Horn (2005)" and "Competitive Intelligence: Fast, Cheap & Ethical (2008)."

Rob Duncan’s Specialties:

Competitive intelligence, qualitative & quantitative market research, technology commercialization, marketing strategy development, online social networks, organizational transformation, teambuilding, leadership development, public speaking, keynote speaking, teaching and training.

Rob Duncan’s Experience

  • Director, Applied Research Liaison Office

    British Columbia Institute of Technology (BCIT)

    (Higher Education industry)

    2007 — Present (1 year)

    Responsible for managing a team of research services and technology commercialization professionals in BCIT's Applied Research Liaison Office (ARLO). The Office is responsible for: technology commercialization strategy and planning; intellectual property management; prospecting, negotiating and executing licensing deals; business development; fostering and supporting applied research and innovation; and serving as an intake for business clients looking to take advantage of BCIT's consulting services. Our research focus is primarily in the medical devices, alternative energy, natural health products and high technology fields. We currently have technologies available for licensing.

  • CEO

    Great Capes Consulting

    (Management Consulting industry)

    2003 — Present (5 years)

    Keynote speaker, author and consultant in the areas of teambuilding, leadership development and marketing intelligence. Popular keynote speech "Teambuilding Lessons from a Voyage around Cape Horn" recounts Rob Duncan's voyage as part of a crew that rounded the dreaded Cape Horn on a square-rigged sailing ship. Duncan is also the author of the book "Haul Away!", a set of management and teambuilding lessons based on the same epic voyage.

  • Technology Commercialization Officer

    British Columbia Institute of Technology (BCIT)

    (Educational Institution; Higher Education industry)

    2006 — 2007 (1 year)

    Project Leader in the BCIT Technology Commercialization Office, Helped entrepreneurs and faculty inventors get new products to market through the effective use of marketing intelligence and planning. Actively involved in the evolution of the office into its current form, the Applied Research Liaison Office (ARLO). Recently promoted to Director.

  • Marketing Intelligence Instructor

    British Columbia Institute of Technology (BCIT)

    (Educational Institution; Higher Education industry)

    1995 — 2006 (11 years)

    Lead Instructor in Marketing Intelligence. Developed and taught courses in quantitative and qualitative market research, competitive intelligence, data analysis/SPSS, database marketing and relationship selling. Seconded to the BCIT technology transfer office, and went on to become its Director.

  • Partner

  • Research Director

  • Management Consultant
