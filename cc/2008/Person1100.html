Andre Koen’s Summary

Hello, I am Andre Koen, President and Founder of AM HORIZONS TRAINING GROUP. It is my honor to pull together the best and brightest minds I know to create our training and education consulting group.

We have been promoting the concept that "the way you think impacts the way you live". Often people are waiting for their luck to change or to “get a break”. At AM Horizons we believe that you have the power to improve your situation, and we can show you how.

Many things have changed over the years. One thing that remain true is that a small group of people are able to shape and change the world. Let us help you plant the seeds of change.

Andre Koen’s Specialties:

Andre Koen is an energetic presenter, a powerful communicator, and a dynamic teacher. His educational influence spans the North American continent. Andre integrates his abilities as an improvisational comedian, large group facilitator, keynote speaker, and classroom teacher to captivate and educate his audience. There are many words that define Andre Koen, but the most definitive is, “Andre, the Enkindled Spirit.”
