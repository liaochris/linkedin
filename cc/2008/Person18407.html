Erika Jones - Clary’s Summary

MsJones (http://www.msjones.com) began when a friend needed a simple well laid out web site with easy navigation to promote her consulting company. I encouraged her to stay away from over used templates and for her to insist on an intuitive navigation design.

While searching for a web designer, she was unable to translate her Liberal Arts needs into techno mumbo jumbo. Frustrated, she asked me to take a stab at the design. I was hooked.

Specializing in web site design for non profits and public affairs. Advertising design has been featured on MSN, Yahoo!, Netzero, Juno, America Online, Ask Jeeves, Infospace, 411web and FoxSports.
