Frederik Questier’s Summary

Prof. dr. Frederik Questier is head of the Educational Innovation & Educational Service Center at the Vrije Universiteit Brussel.
He is teaching Educational Technology at the same university.

Biography and full curriculum vitae can be found at http://questier.com

Frederik Questier’s Specialties:

Frederik is an expert in e-learning, e-portfolios, Free & Open Source software and copyright licenses. He is author, editor and reviewer of several scientific & educational articles and books. He is often invited to give talks at (international) conferences.
