Pete Fellows’s Summary

I run SaccoMann's Patent & Trade Mark division throughout the UK, Europe, the Asia Pacific and North America. I am keen to interact with individuals who operate within intellectual property for mutual advantage via effective networking. We are a successful division and our clients include high profile international private practices and corporations. Placements have included Partner level hires; heads of departments as well as some part qualified and newly qualified recruitment.

I can be contacted as follows:

Tel: +44 (0) 113 245 3338
Fax: +44 (0) 113 245 3339
Email: pete@saccomann.com
Web: www.saccomann.com

SaccoMann is a management and recruitment consultancy. We are not only able to help our candidates find a new role but can also improve your career prospects via our range of candidate support programmes. We can improve interview technique (training on competency/behavioural interviewing, general HR strategies, etc.); improve negotiation strategy (particularly useful for renegotiating salary) and help develop your networking skills.

We also work with firms to improve their selection strategy such as Partner training on interview techniques, how to market your firm to prospective candidates and how to retain your existing staff.

Many of our additional services are offered free of charge to our clients and candidates. For more information on this please contact me.

Pete Fellows’s Specialties:

Patent Attorney recruitment, Trade Mark Attorney recruitment, interview & selection training, management consultancy, headhunting and advertised selection.
