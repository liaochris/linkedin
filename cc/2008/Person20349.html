Phil White’s Summary

Founder and CEO of PSFEI Limited (http://www.psfei.com).

PSFEI is a consultancy and services based company specialising in the online gaming industry, project management and business development.

I am a specialist in the video gaming industry, project management and business development.

I have released over 5 video games, working from concept to release to ongoing support and maintenance. I have project managed the world's largest Microsoft development and implementation of Sharepoint and also Europe's largest development and implementation of Biztalk.

I have also identified, negoiated and signed numerous multi million pound deals across the US, Europe and Asia - ensuring the deal promises turn into actual results.

A safe pair of hands in any situation, able to react and adapt as needed to any situation.
