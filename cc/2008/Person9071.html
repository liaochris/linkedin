Greg Perry’s Summary

17 years of media experience has produced a manager capable of producing award-winning publications that generate a healthy profit. The bottom line is the bottom line and is not to be ignored. Making a product that not only informs and entertains but makes a profit is my passion.

Future goals involve marrying my print/media/publishing abilities with my motorsports interests. This would be the ultimate job.
