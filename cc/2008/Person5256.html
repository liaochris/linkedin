damali ayo’s Summary

Revitalize your body,
enliven your mind, and
increase your zest for life.

Fitness, exercise, and making healthful choices can bring a renewed vibrancy to your life that you deserve. We all need help as we learn how to incorporate these things into our busy lives. Together we will uncover a new you, a modern you, a beautiful, strong and healthy you. In fact, you'll probably feel more like the real YOU than you have in years.

You can do it. I can help.


damali's passion for personal change underlies her passion for social change. "I have seen that change starts with people, relationships and courage. This fuels my passion for connecting people with resources and information to help them be all they can be."

damali's personal health journey led her to triumph over Chronic Fatigue Syndrome through diet, exercise and neurofeedback. damali has incorporated these lessons into her one-on-one work with people to achieve their best health and well-being to bring joy into their own lives and to the world.

damali is a NASM (National Academy of Sports Medicine) Certified Personal Trainer. She has practiced yoga for ten years and kickboxed for twelve years. She is a Reiki Master (energy healing) and holds an MFA in art. damali is an avid gardener and life-long learner. She is committed to green living and lives with her blind adopted dog, Stevie.

  • Founder & Director

    Now Art Grants

    (Fine Art industry)

    January 2007 — Present (1 year 10 months)

    Now Art Grants combine the small donations of many individuals and make these available to artists whose work creates art that catalyzes social change. These artists often work with no pay and slim budgets because the activist nature of their work is often dismissed as "not fine art" or because art remains devalued as a viable career choice by society. Members of the damali ayo and now art mailing lists donate any amount they choose to the grant pool. This money is combined and granted to artists who show their commitment to social change through their work. The art created by Now Art Grants will have a component that is delivered directly to those who have donated the grant funds as a direct return of the funders' investment.

    Now Art holds that the primary role and responsibility of art is to mirror the dilemmas of society to its citizens, offering a catalyst for change and development.
