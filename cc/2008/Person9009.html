Grant Cooper’s Summary

Goal: To help business professionals, executives, and professionals attain their goals and dreams through creating powerfully worded, concisely written, well-researched, and attractively formatted resumes, business plans, letters, proposals, presentations, and other materials.

Strategic Resumes® has recently (2007) been ranked as the #3 online résumé provider in the U.S., and is the only résumé writing firm recommended by the award-winning national website, CollegeGrad.com and the book, The College Grad Job Hunter, available in all Barnes & Noble and Borders bookstores nationwide.

Headquartered in New Orleans, Louisiana, Strategic Résumés provides a nationwide custom-written résumé, career services, job search skills training, and business document writing service, with a 14,000+ resume database representing clients in virtually all career fields, and has operated continuously since 1994. In addition to résumés, Mr. Cooper designs and writes websites, business plans, proposals, presentations, marketing materials, and other business documents.

Recent examples of clients have included the CEO of a $2 billion company, the President of Louisiana’s #1 per capita income Parish, NFL football players, as well as physicians, bankers, police officers, scientists, professors, intelligence agents, politicians, engineers, executives, white and blue collar employees, nurses, attorneys, students, and accountants. Perform extensive community service with dozens of community service organizations.
