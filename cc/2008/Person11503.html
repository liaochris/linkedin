Jerzy Drozda Jr’s Summary

I’m a multimedia, motion graphics and vfx artist from Poland. I’ve been in the business since 1997. During this time I’ve created tons and tons of graphics, software, flash applications, websites, videos and other things that involve creative thinking and approach.

In 2006 me and my friends have started up an advertising agency called Polygon Studio. We deal with 3D graphics, animation, web solutions, DTP, postproduction and compositing for film, commercials and video clips.
