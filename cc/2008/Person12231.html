Joe Gerstandt’s Summary

I illuminate blind spots.

I help people and groups of people truly understand and benefit from Diversity, Inclusion, Authenticity, Innovation and Transformation.

I help smart people and smart organizations avoid functioning in non-smart ways...I do this by increasing individual and collective awareness of blind spots, by increasing individual and collective capacity for communication and by increasing the authenticity of interactions and relationships.
