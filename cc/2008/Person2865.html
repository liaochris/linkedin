Bill Wise’s Summary

Internet and online marketing executive with extensive management and financial background.

Bill Wise is an extremely sound executive, who has the ability to hang with finance, sales, marketing, product development, and engineering folk alike. That kind of broad skill set has proven to be an invaluable asset in the interactive industry. Bill Wise is also a popular industry speaker, online advertising veteran, author (you can read his blog at: www.hallmarkmedia.com), weekly columnist for MediaPost's Search Insider, and in the past, a regular contributor to DM News.

Bill Wise’s Specialties:

Strong skillset in taking small online companies with semi-proven business models and turning them into viable, large businesses. Skills also include competitive analysis, financing, mergers and acquisition, business management, pricing & yield management strategies, strategic sales, company positioning, product roadmaps, and executing strategic business and operating plans.
