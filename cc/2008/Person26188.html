Thomas Trenz’s Summary

I have 22 years in business consulting and over 24 years in B2B sales experience on 3 continents.

Identifying a problem by multi- level communication and creating economic, practical and mutually beneficial solutions is one of my core strength, but more important, I genuinely care about people.

1993-1998 Certified in sales and personal management.

Business consulting and financial planning for 400+ clients.
