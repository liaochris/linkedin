Bob Cantrell’s Summary

I am the owner and developer of niche industry Job Board and Resume Bank services within targeted segments of the Construction Industry. Our services are, and always will be FREE to Job seekers regardless of association affiliation.

A Job Board is an interactive, online employment classified advertising platform whereby organizations pay a nominal fee to post, or advertise current and/or future vacancies, with the intent to attract active job seekers to apply to those advertised opportunities.

A Resume Bank is an interactive, online means of accessing and identifying those job seekers who voluntarily make their professional and educational experiences available for employers looking to match those skill sets to current and/or future organizational needs. A nominal fee is charged for access to a Resume Bank. Again, all Job Board Network, Inc. sites are free for job seeker use.

Currently we are developing a monthly E-Newsletter: "JoB'N" targeting economic, and industry events that impact jobs within our industry focus. We will also be identifying Featured Employers within our e-newsletter, and will have additional industry related information that we hope will benefit the community.

We now have 7 networked job boards and will be expanding into even more over the next 24 months. Keep us on your radar:

www.GeneralConstructionJobs.net
www.ConstructionEquipmentJobs.com
www.FireandSecurityJobs.net
www.RoadandBridgeJobs.com
www.VoiceandDataJobs.com
www.WallandCeilingJobs.com
www.WindowandDoorJobs.com

More to come!

Our Job Board Network sites are affiliated with or members of: SimplyHired.com, Indeed.com, MySpace.com, FaceBook.com, JobTarget.com, International Association of Employment Web Sites. We are active participants within our represented communities' industry related trade associations.
