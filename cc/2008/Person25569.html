Tim FitzGerald’s Summary

Tim FitzGerald is a leader of Avnet Technology Solutions Virtualization Solutions business. Tim leads a team of Business Development Managers that are responsible for recruiting and enabling Value Added Resellers to develop a practice to deliver virtualization solutions in partnership with Avnet's industry leading supplier partners (AMD, DoubleTake, EMC, HP, IBM, Intel, LeftHand Networks, Microsoft, NetApp, Oracle, SUN, Symantec and VMware).

Tim has over 23 years of experience in High Tech sales, marketing, alliance development and management: 19 years with Avnet.
