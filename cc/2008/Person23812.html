Sonia Pasqual’s Summary

I am a freelance Lighting Designer & Consultant working in Theatre, Dance, and Special Events.

Sonia Pasqual’s Specialties:

In addition to working in numerous traditional theatrical settings, my work has had a strong focus on site-specific and experimental theatrical. I have developed unique skills for working in various and conflicting styles and situations from traditional theatrical venues and non-traditional venues to installation pieces.

My goal for 2008 is to learn to fluently program and become more proficient in programming on more moving light consoles and drafting programs :)
