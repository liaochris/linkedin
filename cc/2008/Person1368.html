Andrew Kavanagh’s Summary

I have been in the graphics industry for more than 15 years now.
I started in advertising in New York City and have worked for
Young & Rubicam, Grey Advertising, Tarlow Advertising/Revlon,
and Carlson & Partners. I have worked for Time Magazine,
Godiva Chocolates, Herbalife, The Gap, Old Navy.

Lately I worked on a series of large scale banners for Intel
for the Intel Developers Forum through Jack Morton Worldwide.

I am the assistant organizer for the Los Angeles Photoshop Meetup/User Group.
Since February 2008.

Andrew Kavanagh’s Specialties:

Digital imaging, retouching, color correction, compositing,
print & web production. For print production I use InDesign,
for web production I use Photoshop & Dreamweaver.

I am also a Photoshop tutor.
