Dave Curbow’s Summary

Throughout my career, first as a software engineer, then as a user interface designer in product and advanced development, I’ve always focused on finding solutions to user problems. Often my solutions combine technologies in unexpected ways to produce creative and compelling solutions.

Dave Curbow’s Specialties:

I'm a Senior User Experience Designer and have led software projects solving UI-related problems. I work on large, complex systems, devices and tools to support collaboration. I'm a "hands on" manager who likes to be involved in "lightweight" ethnographic research, designing UIs and software architectures and writing code.
