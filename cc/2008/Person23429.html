Sheraan Amod’s Summary

Hi! I'd like to help you in any way that I can.

I am the improbable combination of a maven, salesman and connector: a technology evangelist with solid experience in successful startups, global NGOs and large corporations. I move easily between technical programming discussions and executive boardroom meetings. My qualification is a BSc in Electrical and Computer Engineering, and I am currently pursuing an MSc in Engineering Management. My company, however, is my foremost passion. My knowledge and skill sets are inter-disciplinary, but I specialize in online social media strategy (particularly community building), hi-tech entrepreneurship, Web/Enterprise 2.0 tools, wiki-based knowledge management and virtual collaboration of work groups. As a strategic thinker with a holistic understanding of the Internet (proficient in technical, commercial, social and public policy aspects), I am well positioned to deploy new ideas into this space.

At this stage I'm happy to offer my services in speaking, advisory, consulting and people connector roles on exciting projects. I enjoy doing pro bono work when time allows and like to assist friends wherever possible. I am based across South Africa but am also interested in (temporary) international opportunities.

I love meeting new people and building relationships- connect with me and let's talk.
