Jason Loftis’s Summary

Jason Loftis received his undergraduate degree in education with an emphasis in history and social studies. In addition to teaching, Jason has served in Christian ministry both in US churches and on the mission field overseas.

He is currently completing a Master of Arts in Theology at Fuller Theological Seminary in Pasadena, California. The degree program is unique in its effort to blend the study of theology with an exploration of art and culture. Students must submit an art portfolio prior to admittance. Jason is a self-trained media artist, and he undertakes freelance graphic and web design projects as his schedule allows.

In recent years, he served as a pastor at Calvary Church in Santa Ana, CA. Although he is no longer a staff member, Jason continues to assist Calvary with its graphic and web design needs.

Upon completion of his seminary degree in 2008, Jason hopes to find a job that blends his unique sets of skills and training both in Christian ministry and in media design/communications.
