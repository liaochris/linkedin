David Waggoner, CMT’s Summary

Founder of TheMarketDetective.com

Expert in Elliott wave principle and Fibonacci analysis.

I use the Elliott wave principle and Fibonacci ratio analysis to uncover clues about the direction of the market. I use other forms of technical analysis to corroborate my findings in such a way as to assign a probability factor to them.

Manage personal equity and provide market advice to private and commercial clients by providing technical / fundamental analysis in financial markets.
