Rubens Gomes’s Summary

Rubens Gomes is a senior IT professional having over 12 years of professional experience working with computer networking, UNIX systems administration, telecommunications, and software development engineering.

Mr. Gomes has worked in software development positions at Sprint (Overland Park, KS, USA), Fujitsu (Raleigh, NC, USA) and EDS (Rio de Janeiro, Brazil). He also held a systems manager position at Diveo Broadband Networks (Sao Paulo, Brazil). Mr. Gomes currently works as a senior software developer at Handango, Inc in Hurst, TX. Prior to Handango, he worked as a Senior Consultant in the "AA.com" project at American Airlines in Dallas/Fort Worth, TX.

Mr. Gomes holds a BS degree in Computer Engineering (1993) and a MS degree in Electrical Engineering (1996) from the University of Kansas, USA.
