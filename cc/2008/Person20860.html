Raj Khera’s Summary

Created and grew several internet portals to become dominant sites in their industry:

MailerMailer.com, email marketing and newsletter management service provider serving thousands of customers worldwide

MoreBusiness.com, small business portal -- one of the most popular searches on Google and Yahoo for sample business plans, marketing plans, contracts, and other information to help small businesses grow, visited by over 125,000 small business owners every month
