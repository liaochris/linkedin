MicroShield Can Help You Establish the Health of your Home or Office. "You can’t control the Quality of the Air outside your Home or Office but you can take control and improve your Indoor Air Quality."

Indoor Air Quality (IAQ) deals with the content of interior air that could affect health and comfort of building occupants. An Environmental Consultant is a professional who can identify the causes of poor indoor air quality.

IAQ problems can arise from moisture, insects, pets, appliances, materials used in household products, furnishings, building materials, smoke and other sources, all of which affect your indoor air quality and become a health concern.

Isolating and Identifying the source of IAQ Issues in your Home or Office and providing a solution is what MicroShield specializes in. MicroShield offers a broad range of highly specific IAQ and Mold Inspection services to identify the problem areas before they become major health concerns.

Microshield has IAQ & Mold Inspection techniques for analyzing Indoor Air Quality and/or Mold utilizing the most effective analytical or sampling techniques available and applying a full range of IAQ & Mold Testing. The resulting IAQ Inspection samples can be analyzed for:

Mold, Bacteria, Infectious Disease, MVOC’s (microbial volatile organic compounds), & VOC’s (volatile organic compounds).

MicroShields can quickly conduct an IAQ or Mold Inspection with minimal disruption to occupants. These inspections can lead to an understanding of the sources of the contaminants and ultimately to strategies for removing the unwanted elements from the air.

After our IAQ Inspection MicroShield prepares a report for you that clearly outlines any potential IAQ risk to occupants making recommendations for further action and solution. Armed with our analyses and recommendations, you can then make informed choices to improve your IAQ.
