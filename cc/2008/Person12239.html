Joe Grimm’s Summary

Michigan State University School of Journalism, 2008 --
I teach news reporting and writing II and am involved in helping us bring talented high school students into the MSU School of Journalism and helping our students find professional internships and job placement.

Detroit Free Press, 1990 to 2008
As the news recruiter, I have coordinated hiring and staff development for a newsroom staff of 300 through a 21-month strike, the move into our competitor’s building, the rise of online and the overnight switch from Knight Ridder to Gannett. Have been a media recruiter with both companies’ corporate recruiting teams.

• Helped the Free Press achieve staff diversity of 31 percent, highest among the nation’s 20 largest newspapers.
• Organized 16 job fairs since 1991 and am helping ONA organize one for 2008..
• From 1991 through 2008, I will have recruited at 57 of 60 NABJ, AAJA, NAHJ, NAJA and UNITY conventions, more than any other recruiter in the country.
• Produced 100 Questions and Answers About Arab Americans in 1999. I posted it early on Sept. 12, 2001, it had runaway traffic and was named the educational site of the week. More than 30,000 hard copies distributed so far.
• In 1997, launched the JobsPage, the dominant newspaper recruiting site. For its 10th anniversary, I reverse published it as a book (my fifth) and launched www.newsrecruiter.com.
• Created Ask the Recruiter blog in 2003. Since late 2006, it has been the featured element of Poynter Online’s Career Center. Traffic for May 2007 reached 200,000 views.
• Pinch hit as Web editor, news editor and copy editor.
• Led sessions in harassment prevention, selection interviews, evaluations, conflict resolution (Achieve Global), diversity
