John Counsel’s Experience

  • Owner

    The Profit Clinic

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    June 1993 — Present (15 years 5 months)

    I own and operate the business as a profitable enterprise. It succeeded a previous business called The Small Business Adshop, one of Australia's most successful small business training firms. I changed the name and focus because I found that most small business advertising failed because of lack of planning and strategy to drive it. By focusing on the causes instead of the effects, we provide much more effective solutions for our clients.

  • CEO and Creative Director

    John Counsel Advertising

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    September 1988 — Present (20 years 2 months)

    I launched John Counsel Advertising in September 1988 after recovering from the illness that forced me to relinquish my former position as Creative Director at Campaign Pacific earlier that year.

    The primary reason — apart from needing to earn income — was the refusal to take "no" for an answer by a couple of former clients with urgent needs that they insisted only I could meet to their satisfaction (flattering, but questionable) and requests from other ad agencies for strategic and creative troubleshooting support. I'd built a reputation for being able to turn tough challenges around quickly, with exceptional results — and I could keep confidences. And I could handle 'difficult' clients very well.

    While JCA is no longer a full-time business for me, I still use it for some projects, especially troubleshooting work for other ad agencies.

  • Founder and CEO

    The Profit Clinic

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    February 1984 — Present (24 years 9 months)

    We help small and home-based entrepreneurs and direct sales companies to sell more, more often, to more people, for more profit.

    We specialise in marketing strategy, branding strategy, market communications, training and professional support.

    We operate a network of more than sixty specialised web sites. You can down load a list of clickable links from http://profitclinic.com/sitelist/.

  • Creative Director

    Campaign Pacific Advertising

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    February 1987 — March 1988 (1 year 2 months)

    Campaign Pacific was a small agency owned by long-time friends who wanted to boost billings. I was hired to help attract new clients and to ensure better results for all clients.

    Our strengths included branding strategy, dramatic growth in market share and the establishment of entire new market segments. I resigned in early 1988 due to serious illness and uncertainty of recovery time.
