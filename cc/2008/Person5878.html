David Lidsky’s Summary

My career has exposed me to the forefront of ideas in the worlds of technology, design, professional development, media and advertising that affect the future of business.

I have demonstrated the ability to transform those ideas into compelling stories and visual presentations, both in print and online.

I manage multiple projects simultaneously and deftly.

My goal is to remain on the cutting edge of ideas while still being firmly rooted in great storytelling techniques.
