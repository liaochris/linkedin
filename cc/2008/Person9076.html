Greg May’s Summary

I am an attorney focusing on civil and criminal appeals and select civil litigation. My appellate practice leverages the research and writing experience I gained in nearly a decade of litigating high stakes business cases with large firms for six years and against large firms for three more, but puts it to work in all areas of substantive law.

My practice also includes select civil litigation. Interesting cases get my attention.

To both aspects of my practice, I bring the perspective of a former client. As former general counsel of a software development company, I know full well the stresses and disruption caused by litigation, and closely counsel clients with an aim toward minimizing the inconvenience of litigation while still providing aggressive representation.
