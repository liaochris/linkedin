Michael Trup’s Summary

We are the largest distributor of Linux in the UK. We work with RedHat, Novell, SuSE and Mandrake. We also work with Sybase, Arkeia, Codeweavers, Kerio , Bullguard, AVG, and others.

We specialise in distribution to both the corporate reseller (esp. Linux based products) and etail channels. We can also offer a comprehensive web processing and fulfilment service on a pan-European basis.

We are always looking for more enterprise class products as well as corporate, government and educational resellers seeking to source software at competitive prices.

In a separate capacity I co-founded www.afrotalk.biz an online business networking site for African businesses and professionals to connect with the diaspora as well as companies interested in developing African commerce, on the basis that Trade is better than Aid.

I am also heavily committed to making a difference and am on the business advisory panel of an NGO www.afrikids.org and assist www.restorehopemission.org and their work in Congo.
