Joseph Steig’s Summary

I am a CFO and advisor to business and non-profit entrepreneurs working with entrepreneurs on strategic plans, financial forecasts, business presentations and on business process and technology implementation.

I am most interested in companies and non-profits producing better ways of living in the world; technologies and approaches to world problems that shift how people work, live and collaborate; people who challenge themselves to create a more just, profitable and sustainable society.

Joseph Steig’s Specialties:

financial modeling, data visualization, presentation coaching, presentation development (PowerPoint and Keynote), strategic planning, small business software (including accounting software, CRM, Mac/Windows/mobile integration)
