Greg Habermann’s Summary

Greg Habermann is the Vice President of Web Marketing and heads the Paid Search division at SageRock.com. He has been integral in creating proactive and efficient SEO, PPC, and SMO management business systems that ensure clients are regularly and proactvely involved with the improvement and optimization of their online presence.

As someone heavily involved in his local community and proficient in organizing online local groups in the various social outlets, he understands the importance, relevance, and many facets of highly targeted regional marketing and has spoken nationally on this topic.
