Gayle Nicholson’s Summary

Gayle Nicholson blends her awarding winning background in public speaking, journalism, and metaphysical studies to effect life altering shifts for her clients. Her unique ability to see into the core of any loss of power has benefitted hundreds of individuals, families and companies by providing them with the knowledge they need to empower themselves at will. Her presentations are lively, engaging and dynamic. Gayle can be heard occasionally co-hosting on MetroChick radio, Detroit's only talk radio show for women, as well as hosting her own program, The Writer's Block..
