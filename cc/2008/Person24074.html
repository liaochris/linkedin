Stephan Osmont’s Summary

Extensive engineering experience in top web and software corporations such as Persoft, Netscape Communications, AOL, Amazon.com (greenlight), Epicentric, Vignette Corporation, releasing countless versions of world-class enterprise products.

Co-author of Wrox Press best seller book Professional Java Server Pages.

Contributor to several Open Source projects such as Liferay Enterprise applications and Cofax content management system.

Ex-Partner and principal architect at Seolan Multimedia (now Xsalto, France).

Experience in helping successfully starting software companies, advising company executives on key technical and managerial issues, formulating architectural principals and building core engineering teams.

Expertise in Internet Technologies, Software Engineering and Development Processes, Software Project Management and Architecture, Technical Sales Leadership, Business Development and Technical Partnership, Product Evangelization. Online marketing, search engine marketing, SEM, Pay Per Click, PPC, search engine optimization, SEO, online media buying, online media planning
