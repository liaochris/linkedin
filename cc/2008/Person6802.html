EyeLogic Media Announces New Press Release Division


Ontario, Canada October 27, 2006 -- Responding to the increasing demand for effective internet marketing techniques, Ontario-based EyeLogic Media has announced the launch of its new TeleBuzz division, aimed at providing businesses with professionally written press releases to increase visibility and recognition. "Press releases are a vital ingredient in a successful marketing plan," EyeLogic CEO Erez Domb stated, "yet many businesses lack the time or the staff to produce them." TeleBuzz aims to fill this need by providing affordable, professional press release packages, with various packages available to fit different needs.

"With the internet becoming such a mainstay in the lives of so many Americans, it's more important than ever to capitalize on web marketing," says Domb. "A powerful press release delivers a company's message, increases brand recognition, and improves the overall perception of the company. Businesses that are unable to target this market will be missing out on an important opportunity to deliver their message to a wider audience."

About EyeLogic Media
EyeLogic Media specializes in Search Engine Marketing techniques, a growing field in business marketing. Based in Richmond Hill, Ontario, EyeLogic performs extensive analysis of a client's business, its web presence and any current marketing efforts and identifies opportunities for improvement. Their marketing approach includes three main areas of focus: search engine optimization, link popularity building and pay-per-click management services. EyeLogic works with the client to identify their needs, analyze their current position, and develop an effective approach.
