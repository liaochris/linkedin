Grozea Florin’s Summary

I am the founder and member of the best Pop band in Romania, Hi-Q. We had one #1 national hit every year, for 7 years in a road, including "The Song of the Year" in 2001 ("Un minut"), 2003 ("Dor de tine, dor de noi"), 2005 ("Gasca mea"), 2007 ("Buna dimineata").

From 2002 me and my friend Mihai Sturzu have one of the most succesful independent label and audio studio in Romania: MOF Records. We've produced hits for artists and bands like Akcent, SISTEM, Blondy, Cristina Rus, TNT, Unu', Hi-Q, Simplu, and so on.

"Dragostea din tei", who sold over 12 millions singles all over the world since 2004, was produced in our studio, MOF Records.

TV: for 6 years, I co-hosted TV shows in prime-time at four national televisions: Antena1, PrimaTV, Acasa TV, TVR. The “Totul va fi bine!” show was top audience leader for 3 years in a row.

Radio: co-host of the morning show “Fantastic” (Radio 21), which has been #1 in audience during all its broadcast period of time, with double audiences than those of the radio station on the second place.

Online: eOk.ro – the best romanian music website; Moose.ro – online MP3 shop; Hi-Q TV – the first online TV of a romanian band

Offline: speaker (Digital Marketing Forum, IMTO Iasi 2008, Da Afaceri 2008); music business articles (in “Şapte Seri”, “Freestyle”, “On”).

More information at: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hi-Q_(band)
