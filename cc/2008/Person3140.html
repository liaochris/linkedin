Brad Garland’s Summary

My expertise is where people and technology collide.

My goal is to change the financial industry as we know it by creating sites like Banktastic.com that break down the walls of sharing knowledge in the banking industry and improving the individual through the support of many.

My passion is for the Web and how it is fundamentally changing our society today. Social media is changing the way we conduct business and our customers now have the control over our image and I pride myself on understanding those niches and where technology can fit and where it doesn't.

Although I hold the title of CEO I'm always working to be better and always have something to learn. I'm blessed to have a strong team and family around me to help along my career.
