Alessandro Perilli, CISSP [www.virtualization.info]’s Summary

Alessandro Perilli is an independent industry analyst based in Rome, Italy, mainly focused on two markets: virtualization and IT security.

During its career he covered several technical roles, achieving multiple certifications about different platforms and technologies, including Microsoft, Linux and Solaris ones. Last achievement in chronological order is the CISSP.

At today he provides a broad range of strategical and technical research analysis, like companies profiling, market trends forecasting, competitive strategy planning, products evaluation and comparison.

His customer base includes venture capital firms, IT vendors, system integrators and multinationals in all continents.

He currently maintains tight and healthy relationship with top management of all players involved in virtualization market.
As part of his job, he has several NDAs in place with many of them, which provide him technical informations about upcoming products, technologies and alliances.

Alessandro is also a recognized conference speaker, performing keynotes and lectures in US and EMEA events about virtualization and security:
http://www.virtualization.info/about#speaking


Alessandro is also the founder of virtualization.info (www.virtualization.info).

virtualization.info publishes news and insights in English language about every virtualization vendor and technologies in the hardware, OS and application virtualization markets since 2003.

At today it counts over 150,000 pageviews / month + over 175,000 feed views month, and it's recognized as the most authoritative and unbiased news source in virtualization industry.

It's often linked from top virtualization vendors websites (including VMware, Microsoft, XenSource, SWsoft, Virtual Iron), and most popular news magazine from prestigious media companies (like CMP, IDG, TechTarget, SYS-CON).

virtualization.info counts more than 25% of Fortune 100 companies among its readers.
