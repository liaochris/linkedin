Gareth Young’s Summary

I am building an independent and diversified revenue stream after a professional career on both sides of the Atlantic that included seven years of Big Four audit, three of corporate finance and accounting, and a decade as a strategy and M&A principal in telecom. My current businesses are:

- finanSight: "For Better Business Decisions." Combining accounting excellence with business leadership to help entrepreneurs, small businesses, VC's and PE, and not-for-profits;

- Novelist: I am in the process of publishing my latest two novels and a non-fiction work on meditation.

Gareth Young’s Specialties:
