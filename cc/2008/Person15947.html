Luigi de Costanzo’s Summary

Luigi de Costanzo was born in 1976 and joined Junior Consulting in 2003. Over the past 5 years, he has worked with clients in areas such as: technology strategy, organization and business process improvement. A common denominator of his experience is working closely with management teams and operative teams to meet the challenges of aligning client requirements to efficient solutions.

His previous experience includes working for Procter and Gamble in their London office, ELIS NGO. Mr. de Costanzo also graduated with a degree in Telecommunications Engineering from the University Federico II in Naples.
