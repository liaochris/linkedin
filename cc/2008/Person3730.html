Carissa Vivirito’s Summary

Carissa Vivirito is a 10 year Internet veteran, focused on crafting the best Internet business strategies for clients and Internet resource teams. Her wide range of experience from information architecture, web design, and usability; to strategic Internet business and marketing planning and execution arm her with the ability to drive success on a multitude of projects.

Carissa released her first published book in the fall of 2007. 'You Can't Get There From Here' (www.youcantgetthere.com, also available on Amazon.com) has been endorsed by two NY Times best sellng authors.
