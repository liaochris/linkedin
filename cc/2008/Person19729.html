Patrizia Bertini’s Summary

My working experience can be divided in 3 main sectors:
- research
- communication
- consultancy.

My deep curiosity and passion for everything new concerning ITC and society, lead me to undertake research experiences, both in Italy and abroad. The result of such experiences are books, papers and publications available in Italian and English.

My academic background in Sociolinguistics has improved my interest in communication and part of my working experiences concern several aspects of communication, including press agent, PR, giving presentations (both in Italian and English), deal with communities, content management and creative writing.

Consultancy had been the main activity I have been doing in the last 8 years. Consultacy involved several tasks, including professional training (both IT and marketing teams), project management, team co-ordination, problem setting, support in communication strategies, web development, usability and accessibility auditings.

All these experiences lead me to gain strong reporting skills, analitical capabilities and great problem solving abilities together with good relationship and team-building competencies.
