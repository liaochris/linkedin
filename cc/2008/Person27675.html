Ziga Vavpotic’s Summary

Žiga Vavpotič, a student of international relations at Faculty of Social Science, University of Ljubljana, born 14th of December 1984 in Ljubljana, has finished Bezigrad high school with one of the highest average. Currently he is a journalist at Children and Youth Program National Radio Slovenia, representative of Youth Employment Summit and Salzburg Seminar.
He did some consulting work for different government on youth issues. Since 2004 he is a member of Board of Director of EcoVentures, in 2003 UNESCO Asp Net gave him a title Youth Ambassador, in March 2005 he finished work as columnist in a project Global Youth Voice, The Kansas City Star Newspaper, 2004 he became a youngest member of consulting board of Microsoft, who was called upon request from Bill Gates (Microsoft Office Information Worker Board of the Future). The entire time he is active on youth issues, so he established in 2003 Slovenian Youth Association. Together with a Law School of University of Ljubljana, he found Economic Forum. He is also founder of University for life. From 2006 he is running with Monika Bizjak Slovene office of Hakes - organisation consulting people
on studying abroad and organising entrance exams for selected higher education institutions.

He is educating himself at home and abroad, and for his work he received different scholarships and is welcomed guest at different conferences. He was in head of different youth delegations and is member of different organization in Slovenia and around the world.

In February 2005 he found company Global Dream Vision, which offers people more quality and better life. Satisfaction in people brings us for sure quality global development ˝, says Ziga.

He is writing blog.
He is not married, although happy in love.

His motto is Live life and leave an imprint!
