Manigandan Gopalakrishnan manigandang@hotmail.com’s Summary

Senior Management Professional with 12 years of IT Solutions and Services experience in various roles such as Consultancy, Project Management, Global Delivery for European and U.S Customers, Partner Management and Account Management. I am passionate about achieving Success through Team work, Actions and Results(STAR).

My experience spans various Verticals such as Manufacturing, Media, Retail, Local Govt. and Retail Banking. My expertise spans diverse Horizontals such as ECM, BPM, BI, SOA, Enterprise Collaboration, KM and CRM.

I currently head Vishwak Solutions Limited, the wholly owned European Subsidiary of Vishwak Solutions Private Limited, Chennai, India. My goal is to grow Vishwak as a Global Player by developing the European Business for Vishwak.

In my previous role at Vishwak, I was heading the Enterprise Solutions SBU at Vishwak focussing on Media Vertical and Professional Services.

In my previous assignment at Siemens Information Systems Limited, I was heading the Application Development and Customization team of the Manufacturing Vertical and was responsible for Partner Liaison, Account Management, Delivery, CMMI implementation and SEPG Leadership. During my 10 years work experience with Siemens, I have worked in the U.K for 3 years and have been actively involved in project management and presales, working closely with customers and partners in U.S and Europe. During this period, I have successfully managed application development projects of various sizes, product development, migration projects, support operations and services portfolio.

Prior to my experience with SISL, I worked with Sonata Software Limited where I began my IT career.

Hold a Diploma in Business Management from ICFAI after my graduation from BITS, Pilani. Am a PMP, a certified Siemens Project Manager (PM@Siemens), a certified Internal Auditor (TQMI) and Six Sigma Green Belt Professional (IIPM, Chennai).
