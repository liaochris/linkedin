Cesar Viteri’s Summary

I have 9 years of experience as consultant in the IBM GBS and PwC Consulting, leading organizations in bussiness and IT professional services, where I have specialized in Project & Team Management, Change Management and Systems Integration Solutions.

During this time, and coinciding with the deregulation of the Telecom Market in Spain and the birth of new operators, I have participated in several large system implementation projects for telco companies. My experience is not limited to Telco, I have also worked in transformation and change projects in the Air Transportation industry. In addition, I have played an active role in sales proposals development, as well as in knowledge management and education activities related to my profession and areas of expertise.

My combination of technical, business, communication and management skills make me a usual candidate to lead and coordinate teams facing complex tasks involving IT-related business transformation in our projects. As an example, one of my latest assignments has been to coordinate the definition process of strategy alligned financial targets for IBM consulting business in Southwest Europe for 2007, following an organizational change.

I have degrees both in Business Administration and Computer Science, and I have started IESE Executive MBA program on September 2006.
