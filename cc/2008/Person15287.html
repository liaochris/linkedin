Laura Levitan’s Summary

Laura is a dynamic integrated marketing executive with 25 years of demonstrated leadership & business building skills among world class, high profile marketing organizations across a wide variety of categories.

Laura has held executive posts with Ogilvy & Mather, Clarion Marketing & Communications, Omnicom, Iceland Spring, Revlon, Zyman Group & the Keller Fay Group.

From 1997-2003, Laura established The Levitan Group, an integrated marketing consulting & services company.

A graduate of Ohio University’s School of Communications, Laura resides in Atlanta, GA.

About Mr. Youth, LLC and RepNation Media

Mr. Youth® leverages critical relationships & unparalleled experience to provide marketing, partnership, & promotional solutions to corporate marketers looking to reach the elusive 14-24 demographic.

We serve as a powerful resource in the development & execution of marketing tactics designed to build brand awareness, drive retail traffic, generate buzz/WOM & PR coverage.

Mr. Youth has just been named to the prestigious Inc. 500 List of the nation’s fastest-growing private companies. PROMO Magazine named us #16 in its 2007 PROMO 100. In 2004, we were awarded the prestigious International Globes Award for Best Use of Event Marketing in the world.

Our proprietary properties include CollegeFest® (www.CollegeFest.com), the nation’s largest back-to-school college marketing event.

www.MrYouth.com.

RepNation is shaking up the world of traditional marketing with the world’s first Consumer Powered Media (CPM) network. The RepNation CPM network enables companies & agencies to reach consumers via the personal interactions of their peers, in contrast to traditionally impersonal mass media.

RepNation offers five innovative campaign types & patent-pending technology, providing a new communications channel for innovative marketers to leverage the next wave of media, which can be deployed as easily as conventional marketing.


www.repnation.com
