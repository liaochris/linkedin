Jorge Arguello’s Summary

As an Internet and Online Entrepreneur, Jorge Arguello has managed success in different niches including music/songwriting art, author of a single dad parenting expert guide, spiritual awareness instruction/spiritual healing and the health and wellness industries.

Since his recent accomplishments, Arguello created a non profit organization helping teens express emotionally in a positive and uplifting way through his Artistic Creative Expressions program.

Too many deaths, acts of violence, teen suicides and depression is becoming the norm and his program is aimed at controlling what seems to be spiraling out of control by providing a balanced life for our youth.

Arguello has a center in Redding, California but is expanding his centers across the nation and world with 300 to be constucted by 2017.

Help him help our teens with curbing this trend and thereby securing our future with positive loving light. Contact him to find out more.
