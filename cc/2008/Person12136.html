With over 23 years in the real estate industry, Joanne brings a deep knowledge and expertise of Land Development, sales of Custom Lots, New Construction, Mixed Use, Affordable Workforce & Senior Housing, and Commercial Real Estate, and Entitlement Consulting and working with home buyers on a referral basis ~ all throughout California!
