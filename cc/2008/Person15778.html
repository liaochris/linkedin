LordAlex Leon’s Summary

LordAlex is an active member of the Flash Platform Community and a well respected industry leader with over 6 years of experience creating content and applications for the Internet and Devices. He is the founder of LordAlex Works (LW) a Flash Platform consultancy firm based in Montreal, helping new media and content providers; deliver intelligent rich internet content and powerful applications with Macromedia Flash, Flex and Flash Lite.

LordAlex runs a trilingual Flash Platform blog where he keeps in touch with the community in many corners of the world and shares scripts and tutorials in English, French and Spanish. He actively participates at FlashADN, the Montreal Flash User Group, speaking at meetings and evangelizing other developers about the Flash Platform.

Among his clients we find Bell Canada, Hydro-Quebec, Quebec Government and Resources Canada.
