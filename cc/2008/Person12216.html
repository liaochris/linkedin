Joseph Dunbar’s Summary

Since 1990, I have focused on food cost control projects in a variety of market segments. In this capacity, I have worked with over 300 clients.

Prior to starting my company, I worked for Sodexho. I held positions as Operations Researcher, Financial Manager, Team Leader of the Competitive Bid Group, Director of Corporate Purchasing, and Chief Financial Officer.

I recently started a blog at foodcostcontrol.blogspot.com. Please visit my blog and feel free to participate.


I'm located in Fairfax, VA.

Best Regards,
Joe Dunbar
