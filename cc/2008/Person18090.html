Misty Khan’s Summary

I am an Entrepreneur with experience in business development, CRM implementation and optimization, business process mapping, project management, and engineering.

My company offers consulting services that give executives confidence in their business pipeline and intelligence. Our primary focus in on helping to collect business development information that is relevant to accomplishing company specific goals. If used inappropriately, even the best software on the market will yield worthless information - our job is to make sure that that information is priceless.

Although our CRM consulting practice is inclusive of all CRM software, we also offer our own software and coaching solutions for executives and sales professionals who would like to use Microsoft Outlook as their primary e-mail, calendar and contact management tool. Our coaching sessions help executives increase their efficiency in handling everything from processing emails to scheduling meetings and assigning tasks.

Check out our new course How to Profit by Networking & Prospecting at the New Course link posted in this profile.
