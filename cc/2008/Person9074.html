Greg Linch’s Summary

My first experience with journalism came as a high school sophomore, reporting for the Cypress Bay High School newspaper, The Circuit. I pioneered the layout editor position the next year and served as managing editor as a senior.

Since then, I have freelanced for The Miami Herald Neighbors section, interned at Forum Publishing Group, interned at the South Florida Sun-Sentinel and contributed as a freelance correspondent to a St. Petersburg Times story on student loans.

I currently work as a local news intern at The Miami Herald in Broward.

My experience with online journalism includes creating The Circuit's first Web site, creating a personal site, contributing to a class travel blog while studying abroad in China, contributing to an intern blog at the Sun-Sentinel and creating a personal blog about journalism called The Linchpen (www.greglinch.com).

I have done multimedia work for The Miami Hurricane, my blog and some of my classes. I have worked as a freelance videographer for New America Media.

My goal is a work as a reporter/online journalist after I graduate in May 2009.

Greg Linch’s Specialties:

Writing and reporting (news, features, sports, entertainment), editing, blogging, AP style, Web content management, multimedia reporting (photo, audio, video), video editing (Final Cut Pro and iMovie), audio editing (Audacity and Soundtrack Pro), photo editing (Photoshop), page layout (InDesign), community management (e.g. Ning)
