Mark Rabideau’s Summary

Mark Rabideau has more than 30 years of Process Improvement, Software Management, Six Sigma, Engineering and Software Development experience. Mark works with software clients throughout the U.S., Europe, the Middle-East, and Asia, as an IT and SPI consultant, trainer, appraisal leader, mentor, and management coach. He is an SEI licensed SCAMPI High Maturity Lead Appraiser (HMLA) and Introduction to CMMI instructor; he is also a certified Six Sigma Black Belt.

In addition to his involvement with PEP, Inc. Mark Rabideau, along with his wife Becky, is a co-founder and owner of eirenicon l.l.c. eirenicon l.l.c., is an IT Services consulting firm involved in providing ‘world-class’ business development and Information Technology (IT)/ software consulting support to developiong and emerging software organizations.

Most recently (2007) Mark founded Ambient Webs LLC one of the world's first commercial firms focused on creating, developing and implementing Ambient, Semantic, and Ubiquitous web technologies (Web 2.0 & Web 3.0). AmbientWebs is dedicated to building and implementing affordable, sustainable, and meaningful web-based information infrastructures for our clients and users.
