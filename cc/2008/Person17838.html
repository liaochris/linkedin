Mikael Sandblom’s Summary

Mikael holds a Master of Architecture Degree from the Technical University of Nova Scotia which is now part of Dalhousie University. He is self taught as a internet software developer - "I got in early on when it was simple, and I've just kept up with the technology" More recently, he as earned an MBA degree from the Rotman School of Business through a (grueling) three year part time program. He is a Project Management Professional (PMP) certified by PMI.

Mikael is a partner and co-founder of Space Database Inc., a company that provides software and services to commercial real estate landlords.

Some of Mikael's accomplishments at Space Database include:
○ Development of web based applications for real estate: These applications are delivered to real estate companies as a subscription service and provide recurring revenue to Space Database.
○ Government recognition of innovation in software: Mikael's innovative development has earned a grant from the National Research Council as well as funding in the form of Scientific Research and Experimental Development tax credits.
○ New marketing tools for real estate clients: Space Database offers new ways for clients to leverage internet technologies in the leasing of commercial space.
○ ISO 9001 certification: Mikael's designs of the company's work flow and internal processes are ISO certified for quality control.

Mikael has also had a minor career as an artist. His work is represented in various collections including the provincial Art Gallery of Nova Scotia.
