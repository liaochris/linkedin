Garry Tan’s Summary

I'm a cofounder at Posterous.com. We're dead simple blogs by email. Email post@posterous.com, attach photos, video, music, documents and files of any sort, and we'll reply with your new website online.

Previously, I cofounded the engineering team for Palantir Technology's quant analysis platform, now deployed at one of the most successful global macro hedge funds in the world.

My multidisciplinary work experience has allowed me to wear many hats -- from engineering, to product and project management, to user experience design.
