Bas Hoogink’s Summary

In order to survive in a world with a free market economy, organizations should continue to put their efforts into "Profit Increase". In general this ambition require enormous efforts of "Human Capital".

Unfortunately, the definition and communication of strategic objectives to the workplace often are inadequate. The focus on input rather than on output is often the consequence. This results in confusion among employees with regard to the expectations of the employer. These employees live simultaneously in a world of further individualization with a growing need for self exploration, entrepreneurship and "Qualitytime". In the quest for satisfy these needs they request for huge efforts of their employers who, in general, are unable or unwilling to deliver.

A poor performance, absenteeism and an unwanted waste of energy and money is unfortunately often the outcome. Calculations and estimates from throughout the world come to an average inefficiency of the use of "Human Capital" of more than 30%!

Closing the gap with 4SUC6! The dedication, commitment and the patience of you and your organization in the wake of the expert services and the unique concept of 4SUC6 enable you to achieve great success:

*Increase your customer satisfaction
*Increase the satisfaction of your employees
*Reduction of your sick leave
*Increase the effectiveness in the recruitment and selection process
*Reduction of undesirable outflow and increase the desired outflow
*Increase of productivity
*Growth in the commercial results in a "healthy" working environment!

4SUC6 is a young, innovative and ambitious executive and advisory organization that operates in the field of Performance Intelligence (a combination of Performance Management and Business Intelligence). Each issue will be examined by four "spectacles" in the following order:

1) Staff and culture

2) Organization and management

3) Business processes

4) Information and Communication Technology
