Pam Keller’s Summary

Being the formulator and creator of all my products, I can guarantee the quality of my products. I sell directly to consumers in my retail location and via my online catalog. Developing a more diverse population of wholesale accounts is one of my top priorities at this time.
I have been making soaps and other skin care products since 2003. I use my educational background to formulate and create my products. I custom make many products on an individual basis as requested. I am constantly researching new ingredients and products. I aim for the most natural products possible, while still ensuring a safe and beneficial product for the consumer.
