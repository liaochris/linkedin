Michelle Fischer’s Summary

Michelle's experience of working within Talent Management and Resourcing within complex Professional Services, agency and outsourced organisations has delivered proven results. She has demonstrable expertise in recruitment and resourcing, training and development, coaching people to achieve results, outplacement relationship management, marketing, strategy and change.

Michelle is BPS level 2 qualified, an NLP Practitioner and holds a Certificate in Coaching from the Coaching Academy. She is a member of The ANLP, NLP Dorset, British Psychological Society and Business Exchange Wessex. She is also a volunteer mentor for the Prince’s Trust
