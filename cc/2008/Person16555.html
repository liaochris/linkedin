Mark Heyer’s Summary

Leadership positions with information companies dedicated to exceptional customer focus whose mission includes creating information value for its customers.

With a strong leadership record in information technology and a dedication to customer service, Mr. Heyer brings a unique vision of both technology advancement and customer focus. His operating principle is to “Evaluate everything we do as a business from the point of view of the customer - then use people and technology to make it better.”

Mark Heyer’s Specialties:

Special skills include information architecture, database development, writing, presentation and engineering

    (Public Company; 201-500 employees; sofn; Telecommunications industry)
