Martin Cleaver’s Summary

Broadly speaking, I seek a Knowledge Management consultancy role, with a home base in Toronto. My MSc thesis project ignited my imagination of the possibilities of being able to deeply integrate and traverse people's intellectual capital: my consulting career at Andersen taught me practical rigour and deep skills to implement enterprise application integration solutions; and my MBA broadened my understanding to allow me to garner a holistic view of what strategy means and taught me how organisational culture can be used to harness innate entrepreneurial spirit that otherwise gets increasingly dampened as companies grow. I am confident that I can bring these together to generate insight and competitive advantage in the KM space.

I'm open to a range of roles, but a primary objective is to develop a better understanding of the KM vendor space, the niches that the vendors occupy, and the value drivers that make them competitive.
