Chris Brogan’s Summary

I show businesses how to use social media technologies for external community building and outreach, and for internal collaboration. I have over 10 years experience with social media and 16 years experience in enterprise computing environments. I blend emerging tech knowledge with enterprise culture understanding.

I speak at several conferences and for private organizations, bridging business-minded people with technology concepts and helping tech-centric types understand the business implications of their efforts. For more on my speaking, check out http://chrisbrogan.com/connect

I blog, write articles, and make media of all kinds at http://chrisbrogan.com

My background is in applications engineering, project management, and telecommunications. I mix my passion for emerging technology with my ability to build communities using social software.

I am cofounder of the PodCamp unconference series, exploring the use of new media community tools to extend relationships and build value.

I have speaking experience on topics related to Internet video, social media, presentation skills, project management, and life hacking. I have advised companies on these strategies and more.

Chris Brogan’s Experience

  • Vice President, Strategy & Technology

    CrossTechMedia

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

    November 2007 — Present (9 months)

    CrossTech Media is an interactive media and events organization, focusing on educating mid-sized companies on emerging technology, through face to face events, as well as via webinars, newsletters, blogs, and other online media.

    My role is to define the Second Circle technologies, those which will impact organizations within the next 6-18 months, and that will require strategic consideration for your business. I build content for events, produce webinars and conference sessions, and write for the newsletter, blogs, and other publications we produce.

    The other half of the business, CrossTech Partners, delivers capabilities in the Second Circle space, including enhanced digital marketing technologies, social media platforms, virtualization, business intelligence technology, and more. I work with the Partners team to set guidance, consult on strategy, and shape the execution of our projects.

  • cofounder PodCamp

    PodCamp.org

    (Non-Profit; 1-10 employees; Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    June 2006 — Present (2 years 2 months)

    PodCamp is a free unconference dedicated to helping people understand new media community tools (blogging, podcasting, videoblogging, social media) to extend relationships and build digital communities. Founded in September 2006 with Christopher S. Penn, the event has seen over a dozen implementations across the world, with another dozen already slated for 2007-2008 so far.

    This position taught me everything from fundraising to event planning, including logistics and small conference organization. Since launching PodCamp, we have started the PodCamp Foundation to help other organizers better execute their events.
