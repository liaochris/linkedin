Tina Monod’s Summary

My work in IT has involved creative design with BC technology leader MDA Corporation: gs.mdacorporation.com, radarsat2.info and landsat.ca; as well as creative collaborations with the Canadian Space Agency & CTV Discovery Channel. I have delivered senior web design and development solutions with creative direction in the technology and geospatial industries.

Current skills are in enhancing online media with itworldcanada.com, itbusiness.ca, directioninformatique.com, intergovworld.com and pcworld.ca of IDG Media. Prior to this my creative campaign contributions have been with geoimaging.com and spatialnews.com of Q Links Media Group; realweddings.ca and completeweddings.com of Media Hill Publications; britton.com and PMAC. Pro bono work with worldvision.ca and practicum with City of Vancouver.

Awarded with creative excellence, I have a diploma in Advanced Internet Development & Design. As well a Bachelor of Fine Arts Degree, Honors B.F.A.

Prior to the dot.com I freelanced writing scripts for “Creators” and performing on WTN, the W Network. I also animated television pilots and commercials for Vancouver agencies Bardel Entertainment and Delaney & Friends.

Tina Monod’s Specialties:

Ad banner design, CSS, Flash, HTML, JavaScript, mobile design, user interface design, usability, web standards, WordPress and XHTML. Remarkable design using Adobe CS3.
