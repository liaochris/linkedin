  • Partner

    Indigo Venture Law Offices

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Law Practice industry)

    July 2007 — Present (1 year 5 months)

    The name "Indigo Venture Law Offices" was inspired by our clients who are innovative business leaders and entrepreneurs with vision and passion for seizing new business opportunities. The concept of "Indigo" venture was derived from the phenomenon, first recognized in 1999, of a new generation of independent thinkers who have a strong sense of purpose, are self-confident and creative, and see better ways of doing things; a generation inspired to change the world, and who have extraordinary talents and abilities that enable them to do it. An Indigo venture is an organization that takes the best qualities of this new generation of people and applies them to business. Our mission as a law firm is to support and facilitate the success of those business leaders and entrepreneurs as they start, grow and manage their "Indigo" ventures.
