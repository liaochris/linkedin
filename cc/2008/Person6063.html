Deanna DellaCioppa’s Summary

This bi-coastal (NY/LA) Singer & Songwriter has had the honor of performing with Stevie Wonder, Diana Ross, Yolanda Adams, Cece Winans, Crystal Waters, Veronica, Joan Osborne, The Chieftains, Martha Wash, Ann Nesby, Angelo & Veronica, to name a few. Deanna has entertained Patti Labelle, Oleta Adams, Arif Mardin, Alan Silvestri, Alan & Marilyn Bergman, & Pat Metheny.

Deanna's singing and songwriting can be heard in film and tv projects such as: "Sex and the City", "The Sopranos", "Melrose Place", "Seventh Heaven", MTVs Laguna Beach and the Showtime original movie "The Tiger Woods Story, to name a few. Deanna also has sung radio and TV jingle spots for Nabisco, Proctor & Gamble, Mattel, Siemens Technologies, Fila, ESPN, and BJs Wholesale Club.

Deanna was selected to attend the ASCAP EAST COAST EXTENDED SONGWRITING WORKSHOP (NY) , THE LESTER SILL SONGWRITING WORKSHOP (LA), & the BMI Foundation Contemporary Professional Songwriting Workshop. She was also awarded The Heineken Music Initiative / The ASCAP Foundation R&B Grant for her song "I've Been Waiting (Someone Like You)".

In 2000, Deanna co-wrote and sang "Higher", which went to 3 on the Billboard charts, produced by 1999 Grammy winner, David Morales & Albert Cabrera. Deanna also sang & co-wrote two songs on Satoshi Tomiie's SONY release "FULL LICK". Deanna's song "I Don't Wanna Sleep" was used for the Mattell project "The Barbie Diaries".

She is currently very excited about working with: Capitol Records recording artist: Bret Ryan, Universal Classics/Decca recording artists: Three Graces, Interscope recording artists: Clique, Sony/BMG artists: Storm, EsNtion recording artist: Danielle Bollinger, Strip City Records recording artist: Dian Diaz.

Deanna launched BOMB CHICA ENTERTAINMENT, her own Record Label, Entertainment, Production & Clothing Company. Her first full-length album, 8:45 AM, is scheduled for a Spring 2008 release on BOMB CHICA RECORDS. BOMB CHICA CLOTHING COMPANY...coming soon!
