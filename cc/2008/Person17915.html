Mike Madura’s Summary

Has successfully completed searches for candidates at all levels in the Information Security, ERP, IT Audit, Attestation, IT Strategy, BI, Outsourcing, M&A, PMO, Risk Management, Internal Audit, Operations, Change Management, CFO Advisory, Trading, and Sales/Marketing verticals. Major networker with a firm belief in paying-it-forward networking. From a LinkedIn perspective my network is #80 worldwide and #5 in Chicago with respect to the number of level 1 connections (as of 6/2008).

Joined KPMG (1/2007) as a Manager of Recruiting for our Midwest Area Advisory Services practice. Our Midwest area consists of: Chicago, Cincinnati, Cleveland, Columbus, Des Moines, Detroit, Indianapolis, Kansas City, Milwaukee, Minneapolis, Omaha and St. Louis. Assumed Lead Advisory recruiting role for the Midwest in 10/2007.

Particular emphasis is on building relationships with and sourcing individuals for the following Advisory Service Lines: CFO Advisory, Internal Audit, Regulatory and Compliance Services, IT Advisory and Operations Risk Management.

The primary reason that I joined KPMG is that we have an excellent value proposition for our employees, not only career-wise, but also with an emphasis on professional growth, work/life balance, corporate responsibility and diversity. We have projected 75% growth for our Midwest area by 2010 which is creating many excellent opportunities at all levels. Not only opportunities for growth but also to help us mentor our staff and to help shape our practices as we continue to refine our service offerings based on industry trends and changes.

To learn more about our KPMG culture, values and opportunities please visit www.kpmgcareers.com.
