Abe Serrano’s Summary

www.thinkabe.com

I am a media designer with experience in creating online interactive environments, prototyping, and online product development. I am currently living in Los Angeles and I am available for freelance or full-time work.

I have spent the past two years working on projects for clients such as Yahoo, ABC, Nissan, EA, Maxis, Warner Bros, and Target.

I have a passion for creating engaging interactive experiences and I am currently interested in branching out from online media to interactive exhibits and mobile technology.
