Jeri Denniston’s Summary

Do you have a clear vision of where your business is headed? Are you working ON your business as well as IN it? I am passionate about helping business owners understand the importance of integrating these two areas in order to achieve long term success.

I have researched and launched new products, been published in national trade magazines, become adept with web 2.0 tools such as blogging and social networking, and trained clients in the use and application of strategic thinking as a tool for business and personal growth.

I am a change agent who uses systems thinking as the foundation of everything I do. That means I apply a holistic approach to planning, marketing, and problem solving. How can I help you?

    (Management Consulting industry)

    December 2006 — Present (2 years)

    Strategic Thinking, Marketing, Leadership and Change Management training using Systems Thinking.

    We are interpretors and translators of best practices research in strategic thinking, planning and management.

    Systems Thinking is about thinking, planning and acting strategically on a daily basis to achieve your desired results. You start with your future desired outcomes and work backwards to identify the strategies necessary to close the gap from today to that future vision. This is different from analytical thinking (how most US people are taught) which starts with today and tries to problem-solve solutions, with no roadmap of where you're headed, just the elimination of the problem.
