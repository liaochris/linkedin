Nik Friedman TeBockhorst’s Summary

Who cares about the technology? It's what you can DO with technology that's so amazing!

I've gone from IT geekdom up through data-driven marketing and now I'm driving the train of web/online marketing for the gaming/casino industry. Somewhere along the way, I picked up my MBA, clung for dear life to the (no longer) sinking ship of B2B publishing, lost my hair, and found happiness in my family. I credit my professional successes to my deep knowledge and interest in both business and the application of technology.

I'm always interested in hearing from new people, old friends, former colleagues, and anyone who can help me find my keys. (I think I might have left them on the bus.)
