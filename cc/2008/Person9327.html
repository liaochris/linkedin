Hans von Sichart’s Summary

I am currently working with the Voice Technology Group at Cisco in San Jose. Occasionally, I teach at the University of Santa Cruz, Silicon Valley Extension. In the past, I was a designer at firms such as frogdesign, Sapient, Philips Design and Apple. I was lucky to work with very talented people, our work has received international awards and hopefully made quite a few users happy.

Hans von Sichart’s Specialties:

User centered design, interface design, visual design, interaction design, innovation design, interactive branding, growing individuals and lifelong learning.
