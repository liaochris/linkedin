Tom Anthony’s Summary

Tom Anthony serves as Vice President of Client Services for Web Teks, a Hampton Roads based eBusiness Consulting and Strategic Web-Based Solutions Company. Tom has a deep background in both the business and art of media and is focused on the strategic Internet.
