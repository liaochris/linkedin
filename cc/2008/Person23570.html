Simon Goland’s Summary

I am an accomplished facilitator of transformational learning in organizational and individual settings. I bring a wealth of past and present knowledge and experience into my work, which includes technology and business consulting, organizational training and development, adult learning methodologies, educational theories, and diverse personal development modalities. Passionate about creating and cultivating lifelong learning environments, in small and large settings, both face-to-face and online.

A few core competencies:

-> A natural team leader who is passionate and experienced in building relationships of trust, creativity, and collaboration in diverse organizational settings.
-> A confident, engaging, and creative instructor and public speaker, able to establish rapport and connection with different audiences and convey information with clarity and enthusiasm.
-> Over nine years of experience in design and delivery of program development and training services to diverse individuals, groups, and organizations.
-> Over six years of integrating individual coaching and mentoring modalities with lifelong learning programs and workshops.
-> In-depth knowledge and practice with adult learning, transformational, and experiential learning theories.
-> Experienced and comfortable with investigating, customizing, and applying technological solutions to augment and enhance teaching and training experiences.
