Michael Lester’s Summary

I am a DSP Engineer who specializes in Real-Time Embedded Audio Processing and Algorithm Development. I have experience in novel Audio Algorithm Development, Real-Time Audio Coding, Perceptual Algorithms, Filter Bank Design, RF Signal Integrity Measurement, Stereo Encoding for wireless transmission, as well as Psychophysics and Audio testing/benchmarking. I use tangential experience, such as being a musician and resource planning, to solve problems and inspire creativity in my work.

In addition, I have interest in applying intelligence modeling, such as cortical processing theory and algorithms, to efficiently process and classify audio in ways similar to humans.

Audio and Wireless Signal Processing, Real-Time Audio Data Compression, Multi-Rate Design, Fillter Banks, Multi-media Processing, Adaptive Filtering

Matlab and Simulink Modeling

ADI, TI, and Freescale DSP processors in both Assembly and C/C++

Objective Testing, Subjective Listening Testing and Psychophysics

Knowledge Management and Project Planning
