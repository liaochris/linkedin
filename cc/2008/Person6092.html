Deb Dib’s Summary

Expert in CEO / C-level / President-level branding, resumes, on-line identity, and strategic coaching for visionary, gutsy, fun $500K to $1M+ executives with a strong profit motive and a social conscience.

My clients are typically entrepreneurial leaders of small- to mid-caps /start-ups, heads of enlightened Fortune companies, or rising stars targeting the C-suite. They are not interested in the status-quo and they haven't allowed the personality and passion to be sucked out of them by the demands of work.

I help these cool leaders stop, take a breath, figure out why they tick and how that translates into success -- and then help make them even more successful

It’s an intense (but FUN) process, so I can select and work with just 2-3 clients each month, with projects extending for upwards of 4 months. My most recent clients landed in record time, and with an average 25-50% bump in compensation.

A while back I took a fun political quiz that pegged me as a "liberal leaning libertarian" and that contrarian description seems to fit my "profit with a purpose" vision. My unabashedly idealistic (and practical) mantra? "Changing the world one executive at a time!"

I LOVE my job - I get to help the coolest people catapult their careers AND achieve their particular dreams. What's better than that?
