Eric Roch’s Summary

PROFESSIONAL SUMMARY

Eric Roch has over 20 years of experience in various aspects of Information Technology including IT executive level management within industry and consulting, technical architect, and application and systems development. He has over ten years of executive leadership in IT consulting services, and is currently Chief Technologist with Perficient (NASDAQ: PRFT) – a leading provider of business integration solutions. Prior to Perficient, he was CTO of ZettaWorks leading the technology strategy and product development for the company resulting in a $10.7M acquisition by Perficient. He has also been an IT industry speaker and author for many years.
