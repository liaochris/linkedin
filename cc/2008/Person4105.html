Christophe Hamerling’s Summary

I am R&D engineer and PEtALS product leader at eBM WebSourcing the SOA open-source editor.
My activities are mainly around the PEtALS Entreprise Service Bus project (http://petals.ow2.org). This open-source SOA runtime is based on an ambitious architecture. This is the core module for the products we develop at eBM WebSourcing and for European and French Research projects we are involved in.
Today, my main domains of interest are Web Services, SOA, distributed computing, Open Source and all the Java stuff.
