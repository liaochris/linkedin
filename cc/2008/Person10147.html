Jacob Cynamon’s Summary

Jacob works at BlueBolt Solutions, where he is a web project manager. This involves overseeing timelines and project resources to ensure timely completion of client projects, as well as strategic planning of company-wide operations improvements.

Previously a corporate real estate advisor at UGL Equis (http://www.ugl-equis.com), a multidisciplinary corporate real estate services firm that delivers integrated services exclusively to business space users. Jacob worked with Chicago and Fortune 500 businesses to help them align their real estate decisions with their business priorities. During this time, he managed projects to find office spaces for the US Census Bureau's 2010 Census project.

Prior to Equis, Jacob served as a partner relations lead at Microsoft Corporation, advising valued Microsoft partners with strategies to maximize their new business development and business-to-business growth opportunities.
