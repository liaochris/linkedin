Steven Woods’s Summary

A professional since 1999, i've worked for numerous agencies for clients large and small.

In 2007 I formed my own tiny studio called SWOO to cater for the design and development requirements of local SMEs, as well as to pursue my own ideas based upon the online marketplace.

My goal at present is to turn SWOO into a full service design and development house by opening a small office from which to conduct business, although at the moment I am currently building a client and contact network while establishing myself.

Steven Woods’s Specialties:

Website Design, Website Development (Classic ASP, ASP.NET C#), Software Development (C#.NET), Front-end Design and Development (XHTML/CSS), Branding, Search Engine Optimisation (SEO), Affiliate Marketing
