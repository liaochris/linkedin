Debra Murphy’s Summary

Debra, a marketing mentor and consultant, is founder and president of Vista Consulting, a successful marketing agency that works with high technology companies and service businesses, helping them market their products and services more effectively.

Recently Debra launched a new venture called Masterful Marketing™, a forthcoming series of information products targeting the independent professional, partner in a service business, entrepreneur and business owner. The first product available now is an 8 step marketing system that helps these professionals develop their personal brand and better market their service-based business.

In addition to running these businesses, Debra gives workshops and clinics to businesses and organizations covering a broad range of topics on marketing and branding. The current workshop is "Using Social Media to Develop Your Personal Brand". These workshops can be for the benefit of your company or an organization.

Debra is the author of a forthcoming book under the Masterful Marketing™ label and a contributing author of “Create the Business Breakthrough You Want: Secrets and Strategies from the World's Greatest Mentors” featuring business giants Brian Tracy, Robert G. Allen, Bob Proctor and Mark Victor Hansen (Chicken Soup for The Soul) and endorsed by Dr. Stephen Covey and Ken Blanchard.

Debra Murphy’s Specialties:

Mentoring and advising independent professionals, entrepreneurs, partners in service businesses and business owners on how to develop their personal brand and market their businesses more effectively. Consulting services for high tech companies and services businesses.
