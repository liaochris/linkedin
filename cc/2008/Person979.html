Allan Metts’s Summary

Nationally-published technology leader, with strong skills in information assimilation, organizational leadership, and strategic planning. Allan also has a proven track record in the design and implementation of complex, high-performing, distributed systems using C++, Django, and other programming languages. Experienced in establishing international business operations, reliable data centers, and feature-rich software applications (for use both internally and on the internet), Allan has established Arcato Global as the corporate extension of his talents.

Allan Metts’s Specialties:

Establishing Latin American technology operations, deploying low-cost corporate data center operations using Linux, S3, and EC2, and designing complex software applications with web-based front ends. Programming language proficiencies include C++, Java, and Django.

  • President & CEO

    Arcato Global LLC

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    February 2008 — Present (11 months)

    Arcato Global offers a solid menu of technology services for the small and medium-sized business. We specialize in...

    * Enabling technology operations in Chile, tapping a highly-educated and motivated workforce that works in the same time zone as the US.

    * Designing complex custom software solutions, either for internal or external use. We’re experienced with behind-the-scenes data crunching, and scalable, Internet-based front ends.

    * Deploying open-source enterprise applications without the open-source headaches. Whether for customer relationship management (CRM), office productivity applications, or content management, we can deploy proven open-source alternatives to the pricey commercial applications at a fraction of the cost.

    * Establishing low-cost data center operations using Linux-based servers, EC2, and S3 (established cloud computing solutions) -- avoiding the huge markups that IT equipment manufacturers and managed service providers levy on their customers.
