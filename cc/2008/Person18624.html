Nate Hardy’s Summary

Get the intelligence you need to make more sales. Thorough in producing quality results and recommendations, I help organizations become more efficient and effective at targeting markets and increasing sales.

Nearly 20 years experience in marketing research and consulting in a variety of industries: energy, utilities, transportation, financial services, pharmaceuticals, healthcare, education, aerospace, publishing, media, nonprofit, local and state and federal governments.

Specialize in strategic research and consulting on advertising, promotions, media, communications, publicity, customer needs, satisfaction, loyalty, retention, marketing performance tracking, brand and corporate image, baseline and benchmark studies, and employee surveys/behavioral studies.

Nate Hardy’s Specialties:

Marketing research, management consulting, cross-tabulations, statistical analysis, and data analysis/management. Microsoft Certified Professional.

Business and executive coaching, life coaching, and training and education through affiliated services. Award-winning writer that provides writing and editing consultation.
