Dana Lookadoo’s Summary

Dana Lookadoo founded Pixel Position in 2003. Conversational SEO combines best practices of Search Engine Optimization (SEO) with Social Media & Online Marketing.

Dana's passions include:

* Search Engine Optimization & Copywriting
* Online Marketing, Public Relations and Social Media
* Information Architecture & Planning
* Technical Training & Curriculum Development
* Web Development and Graphic Design

******
Dana began a career in computing in 1984 and the Internet in 1996. Internet marketing provided the medium to merge interests in people, writing & technology.

Adjunct Faculty at Cal State University, Monterey Bay, 1999-2000

Corporate Trainer: Provided technology curriculum training for Sun Microsystems, Monterey Institute of International Studies, U.C. Santa Cruz Extension, General Colin Powell's Youth Peace Corp, PowerUp - America's Promise, Steve Young’s NFL YET Center. Guest Lecturer at University of Reno, Info. Technology

Author: Wrote “Traveling the Internet” for Sun Microsystems, a 100-page guide for how to travel safely and securely on the WWW, 2005.

B.S. (magna cum laude) in Telecommunications, Multimedia and Applied Computing, minor in Instructional Technology; A.A. in Business Administration. Studied Virtual Reality Modeling Language at the Naval Postgraduate School - focus on visual application for instructional design.

Goal? Be a Prof Mountain Biker when I grow up.

Dana Lookadoo’s Specialties:

Search Engine Optimization, Copywriting, Online Marketing, Search Advertising, Usability and Conversion, Ecommerce Development, Design, Web Development/Programming, Web Analytics, Training, Social Media and people power in marketing
