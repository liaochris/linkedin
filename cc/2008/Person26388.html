Bob Waun’s Summary

My new book - Besting: Better Nesting can be found at: www.BetterNesting.com about the dramatic shift in real estate needs and desires caused by Boomer generation. The first wave is just hitting the beach in 2008.


Bob is CEO of Vacation Finance, www.vacation-finance.com, America's First Secon Home Lender. Specializing in residential, commercial and wholesale mortgage originations in the condo hotel, fractional, mezzanine, resort development, timeshare receivable and second home areas.

Bob has 19 years experience in mortgage banking, he holds an MI real estate broker license , and a Master’s degree in finance/economics and BBA in finance from Walsh College. He has been a professional guest speaker and taught numerous courses and seminars on real estate finance. Since 2002, Bob has worked with condo hotel developers and lenders to improve the market for condo hotel financing. He has reviewed over 75+ projects and has been nationally recognized as an expert in vacation ownership finance.

Bob Waun’s Specialties:

condo hotel, fractional, resort and cottage financing. commercial, mezzanine, timeshare financing, boomer demographic trends.
