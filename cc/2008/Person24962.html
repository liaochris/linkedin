Terence Donnelly’s Summary

Nearly two decades experience in executive search with tier one companies. Acted as both advisory and board director for a number of comanies. Facilitated venture capital investors with building executive teams, managed mergers & acquisitions, and have sat on numerous philanthropic and not for profit boards. Active investor and entrepreneur. I've conducted over 20000 in-person interviews in my career, and innumerable telephone interviews. Mandrake is the largest Canadian executive search firm, and has partners in over 60 countries.
