Mary Ellen Schutz’s Summary

Mary Ellen Schutz, your Gentle Editor, is a gifted writer, editor, documentation project manager, and translation project coordinator.

With more than 20 years experience working in the computer, scientific instrument, and publishing industries, Mary Ellen’s writing and editing skills give you the technical expertise needed to move your work from well-done to excellent. Her project management work ensures that your project is done right and on schedule!

Have a global market to address? No time to find a vendor, review your publications for translation readiness, monitor the translations and reviews? Put Mary Ellen’s 15 years of experience coordinating translation projects to work for you.

Need a website? Ensure that your presence on the web truly reflects your organization, welcomes your customers, and is so simple to use that they decide to stay a while...
