Dominik Mueller’s Summary

Dominik Mueller has been in the domain business since 2003. Since that time, he has successfully sold or purchased lots of domain names at high-end market prices.

His track record includes the sale, acquisition and development of Credits.com, Kid.net, USACreditCard.com, Manhattan.info, NewYork.info, Filez.com, GVG.com, Science.info, WineStore.com, YG.com, Lilies.com, Trusted.com, ZUM.com, WeddingSong.com, BookSellers.com, Xmas.com and several other premium domains, most of which have been subject to non-disclosure agreements.

Currently, he is working on the development of category-defining domain names into operating and profitable online businesses.

Dominik also publishes a popular blog about domain names and online marketing at DMueller.com.

He is an open networker with more than 1,000 direct LinkedIn connections. Invitations may be sent to domainbrokerage (at) gmail.com.
