Michael Claprood’s Summary

Michael Claprood is a Director of Client Services for Novations Group Inc. Michael specializes in working with key strategic accounts in Fortune 500 and Fortune 1000 organizations in implementing human capital solutions. He consults in all of Novations core products and services including, leadership development, talent management, selection, succession planning, retention, engagement, diversity and incusion, executive coaching, needs assessments, and project management. Michael has over 11 years experience in assisting corporate clients in the entertainment, manufacturing, financial, retail, technology, healthcare, and food industries. He has managed multi-national accounts that cover Europe, South America, Asia Pacific, and all of North America.

Prior to joining Novations Group, Inc., Michael was an Account Director with TMP Worldwide and managed a team with revenues in excess of 5 million dollars. Before his position at TMP Worldwide Michael Worked at Right Management and DBM for over 6 years. He sold their entire portfolio including; Leadership Development, Executive Coaching, Outplacement, Needs Assessments, and Change Management. Michael achieved Presidents Club and Team awards each year, and began a mentoring program that assisted in the on-boarding of new employees. He covered the Southern California territory and achieved annual revenue goals of 1.5 million dollars.
