Man Arenas’s Summary

Production Designer and Art Director with a broad experience in all fields of motion pictures production and visual development in animated film industry since nearly 20 years.
Man Arenas started his career in Brussels in 1988, working for la S.E.P.P. in the visual development department for several animated TV series. Since then, he has worked on many animated feature films for companies such as Warner Bros, A Film, Cartoon Film, Studio Hamburg or TFC Trickompany.
From the beginning, he developed an interest for production design bringing to the normal design tasks a strong level of originality and poetry, inspiring other artists, not only in traditional animated films but also in 3D animated feature films and computer games.

Man Arenas’s Specialties:

Visual Development and Concept Art, Illustration, Workbook and Storyboard Art,
Production Design, Graphic Novels, graphic Design.
All medias.
languages: English, Spanish and French
