Ted Wallingford’s Summary

Ted is the co-founder of Best Technology Strategy LLC. Our company allows businesses to become more efficient and profitable through the use of information technology.

We provide objective, vendor-neutral expertise in the specialties of advanced networking, I.T. strategy, data communications, storage systems, the telecommunications business, and the core technologies underlying them.

Ted is an expert in the specialty of unified business communication, and has written two books about VoIP (voice over IP) for O'Reilly Media, the world's most recognized tech publisher.

Ted has been heavily involved in information technology since 1994, and has served a variety of hands-on and technology management roles in manufacturing, advertising, logistics, consulting service, and construction industries.

Best Technology Strategy LLC provides services to the networking industry in marketing, decision support, and testing. The firm also offers thought leadership and technical guidance to businesses seeking better utilization of networked systems, and business requirements and solution design services for innovative service ventures considering expansion into the telecommunications business. Ted is has also been a technical author for Macworld Magazine, O’Reilly Network, and Weblogs Inc. He resides in Ohio.

- Guest on National Public Radio's Talk of the Nation program, to discuss Voice over IP technology and its impact on consumer habits and the telecom industry at large.
- Speaker at VON Enterprise Atlanta 2006.
- Speaker at MuniWireless Boston 2007.
- Personally selected by Jeff Pulver, the publisher of VON Magazine, as one of the top telecom blog writers of 2005.
- Awarded technology consultant of the month by LCG Technologies in February of 2006.
- Published VoIP Hacks, second book about Voice over IP, in January of 2006.
- Featured in a story about VoIP telephone services in the Chronicle-Telegram newspaper in April of 2006.
