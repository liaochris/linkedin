Toby Morning’s Summary

Deep vertical experience in Music and Film distribution, Digital Rights Management,and the home consumer electronics market. I have founded music distribution company's, software company's, streaming media hosting company, wireless ISP, and most recently a BPO provider from India named Blue Monitor.

I was the first west coast distributor for Rawkus Records in 1997, and was the first vinyl distributor to sell battle tools, and music to Pro Sound Stage and Lighting, the largest DJ catalog service in the US.

I have had over 10 years of hands on layer 2, and layer 3 network equipment management, and 802.11 product evaluation based on real time use needs in 2 start ups I was part of (IP Band, and WIFI Phone Network).

I have been an Investor in Set Top Box makers, clothing companys, and even Raves, and Hip Hop concerts in my younger years.

Most recently I have Co -Founded a software company which has developed a web services platform for company to quickly, and cost effectively built application around digital media.

Toby Morning’s Specialties:

Branding and Identity, Graphic Design, Web Design, Web Development, Airbrushing, Water Colors, Aerosol Art Curator (Spray Paint), Network Administrator, System Administrator, Streaming Media Speacialist, DRM, Web Services and SOA, SAAS business models, and business development.
