Beth Jackson-Bates’s Summary

I am a social media junkie. With over 11 years in web design, development and marketing, I have enjoyed working with non-profits and associations as well as small-medium sized businesses. While most of my experience has been in custom application development, web design and strategic web development, my current interest is in social media, particularly how it can be leveraged within non-profits.
