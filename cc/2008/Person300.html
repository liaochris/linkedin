Anne-Catherine LORRAIN’s Summary

I am currently writing a PhD paper on the online exploitation of music (contractual practice of rightholders for legal online music offers, international law). As a member of CERDI, a research center specialized in IP law and new technologies (directed by Professor Pierre Sirinelli), I am part of several research programs.

I also publish in scientific reviews and am regularly invited to participate to seminars and conferences. I also teach in Communication and Telecommunication Science Schools (see My Website to read more).

I have a past professional experience in the music industry, as a lawyer at IFPI (International Federation of the Phonographic Industry) French group (SNEP) for 3 years and as a trainee at IFPI Brussels Office.

Anne-Catherine LORRAIN’s Specialties:

Intellectual property law, music business, "new digital uses" (podcasting...), negociation of rights (contracts), international law
