Rene Flores’s Summary

Arnie Rene Flores is a graphic designer and illustrator with more than fifteen years of experience providing visual solutions for the high-tech and public sectors. Specializing in visual design and illustration for web and multimedia applications. Winner of multiple national awards, including the American Advertising Federation's Team Gold Addy and New Media Magazine's Invision Award.

Rene Flores’s Specialties:

Creating the visual design, graphics, illustration and animated interactivity for Web sites, interactive CD's and other multimedia applications.
