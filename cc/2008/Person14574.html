Ken Rubin’s Summary

Over 17 years of professional experience in channel management and direct sales of digital and streaming video systems to the broadcast and cable television markets in the United States, Latin America, and internationally.

One of the pioneers in the creation of turnkey digital video systems and considered by many as an expert in digital and streaming media applications for broadcast and cable television. A featured presenter at several broadcast tradeshows and quoted many times by several broadcast industry magazines.

Contact Ken anytime at 407-677-7930 or KenRubin@RubimarEnterprises.com.

Ken Rubin’s Specialties:

Consultative selling that creates win-win relationships, showing broadcasters how to gain efficiency and/or increase revenue by implementing digital video systems or infrastructure.

Technological expertise of digital and streaming media solutions.

Direct sales and marketing to thousands of television and broadcast entities in the United States, Latin America, and worldwide.

The creation and management of sales teams and selling systems that produce record sales of broadcast technology.
