Mirona Iliescu’s Summary

I love connecting people, ideas, projects, and organisations that think or act alike. For a living, I help people communicate better. My company assists the storyteller in each of us with catching stories--expressed in any form from visions to events.

While I'm in London most of the time, I still visit Bucharest about once a month, so drop me a line if you would like to meet in either city.

Some professional highlights, having worked across borders and industries:

2007 - advising Trend Consult, training market leader in Romania on its swift transformation from a company to a group of four companies targeting the European market (branding, communication strategy, coaching)

2006 - managing the opening of the 1st lifestyle mall for interior design in Romania, Galeria Mobexpert, and the launch of the 1st stakeholders engagement program for Shell Gas Romania

2005 - managing PR for Heineken Music Thirst (1st Romanian edition) and Red Bull Flugtag (1st Romanian edition)

2004 - managing the opening of the Esplanada/ CEGIS shopping gallery (Cora Pantelimon commercial park, Bucharest)

2003 - managing the design, implementation, and content administration of Nokia Mobile Phones' WAP application in Romania

2002 - managing the design, implementation, and content administration of Brau Union's 1st website in Romania (they've become Heineken meanwhile)

2001 - managing the design, implementation, content administration, and exponential traffic growth of Nokia Mobile Phones' 1st website in Romania

2000 - managing the launch of the 1st IT incubator in Romania, nest (GED Capital Development & RPPF)

1999 - launching Santana's "Corazon Espinado" as 2nd single from the "Supernatural" album in Romania

Due to time constraints (I pursue life more than online life), I'd appreciate if you don't invite me to other professional/ business networks.
