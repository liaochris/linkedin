Simon Baker’s Summary

Simon Baker is a co-founder of Energized Work, a consultancy offering agile and lean coaching, and software product development expertise. He is an agile coach, lean thinker and scrum practitioner with 15 years experience in software product development and has worked in the internet, media, retail, financial services, and banking sectors. Through an energetic and inquisitive style of leadership and an unrelenting focus on people, continuous improvement and delivery of value and quality, Simon specialises in helping teams and organisations become more effective.

Simon Baker’s Specialties:

Coaching and leading agile software development, specifically Extreme Programming, Scrum and Lean. Scrum Master.
