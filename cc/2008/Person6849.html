Duane Edelman’s Summary

As a former pilot and manager at Northwest Airlines for over 35 years, I possess a thorough understanding of the processes involved in the development, publication and enforcement of industry guidelines. In addition, I have a detailed knowledge of the workings of both industry associations and regulatory agencies and have been an active contributor to the evolution of standards for operations and safety through his association with organizations such as the FAA, ATA, SAE and NASA.

As founder and president of Aircraft Data Fusion, my interest is driving forward-looking initiatives that are defining the future of aviation. Our specific focus is on how mission-critical data is collected, circulated and used to support profitable airline and airport operations.

Duane Edelman’s Specialties:

Airline industry consulting, process review and improvement, data integration, mobile and wireless applications, operations control center, electronic flight bag (EFB), online documentation
