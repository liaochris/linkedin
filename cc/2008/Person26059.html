Barbara Hofmeister’s Summary

25 years in Tourism and Management, 11 years in professional Training and Coaching. 6 very active years with Toastmasters International, DTM and Toastmaster of the Year for Continental Europe.

I work with great passion and enthusiasm, always focused on helping my clients define and reach their goals in life or business.

Live seminars, interactive workshops and speaking engagements plus one on one Speech and Life coaching.

My greatest passion are confidence building workshops which I do for young and old. "Confidence Speaks" is my core product and the results truly speak for themselves. There is also a "Confidence Speaks Youth Leadership" program in which teenagers build their confidence and the belief in themselves and their unlimited possebilities in life! You have to see it to believe the results -.)
