Bogomil "Bogo" Shopov’s Summary

Currently I am Product Manager "Internet infrastructure and payments" at SEP Bulgaria in Sofia. Also I am board chair of Web Technology Conference - WebTech and Enterprise 2.0 conference, editor of 2 bulgarian e-magazines about Web 2.0 and OpenSource.

I am proud member of Open Projects Foundation (OPF), Free Software Foundation (FSF) and Electronic Frontier Foundation (EFF).

I am running for a Member of the European Parliament on the 2009 elections. I would like to work for FOSS adoption, for our nature, for our civil and digital rights, for open standards and open ideas. My campaign is under Creative Commons license . Yes, YOU can help.
