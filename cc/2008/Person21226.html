Crystal Curran’s Summary

With specific experience in supporting real estate professionals in business building and strategizing for over 10 years and an over 30 year background in technology and business marketing, I have been featured with such dynamic companies and individuals as Michael Russer a.k.a. Mr. Internet Featured VA Profile (Feb.2006), Showcased in Articles: Staging Your Home (April 2007), Branding (May 2007), The Many Faces of EVA (August 2007), as well as featured partner with Point2Agent, Eric Jordan Coaching, Advanced Access, IREAA, VA4U, REVA Network. An on-going member of RealTown, Active Rain and Real Estate 411, I will guide you through the development of the perfect business or team for you.

Billing myself as the "Executive VA who likes to get her hands dirty", I love marketing and web design as well as being an Executive VA. I often do the templates for such things as your website, html stationery, postcard templates etc during the time I am helping to develop your brand, niche and systems

So what is an Executive VA, anyway?

The best description to pass on to you is this one from Michael Russer, a.k.a. Mr. Internet in his new book The Obsolete Employee. It states:

“An executive level VA is someone who is highly skilled and experienced in providing strategic support, including project management, virtual human resources (i.e. hiring and terminating other VAs as needed), writing business processes for each member of your virtual team, and making sure you stay on track as per their written objectives. They also strive to understand the big picture of your business and focus on making sure you achieve your goals and objectives using available resources.”

Here at REVAville, we keep track of your objectives and relieve you of your "adminis-trivia" so you can do what you do best - Sell Real Estate!
