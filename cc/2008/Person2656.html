Ben Throop’s Summary

Ben is a nine year industry veteran of Sony and Activision studios, specializing in technical art, pipeline production, and getting the most out of hardware, engines, and the people that make them.

After finishing Spiderman 3 Wii/PS2 with Vicarious Visions, Ben embarked on a new venture by founding Emergent Behavior LLC, which leverages his experience designing tools for game development to build rich web applications for niche markets.

He now spends his days immersed in web technology while keeping a toe in games through consulting.
