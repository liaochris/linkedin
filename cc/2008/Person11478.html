Jerry Gilreath: jerry@jerrygilreath.com’s Summary

Jerry Gilreath is the Director of Client Support Services at DHAP Digital, where he directs the support operations and Corporate Information Technology groups. Jerry brings over 15 years of experience with mission essential systems, implementation and process design to DHAP's technology support practices.

Prior to joining DHAP Digital, Jerry designed and implemented highly available, highly secure, technology systems for leading e-commerce companies and the US Air Force. His work history includes the largest online salvage auction company in the world as well as an award-winning national value added reseller and provider of technology solutions. He has previously owned his own successful technology services company.

Jerry holds more than 20 certifications across various technologies, including Sun Microsystems engineering, data storage, systems clustering, systems monitoring and administration, database administration and networking.

Jerry served on the SunTone Architecture Council and has dedicated his time to various not-for-profit and educational groups, enhancing their use of technology for service delivery. Jerry has also served on various technology advisory committees for startups and other companies where technology is not a core practice. He has a fascination with and writes about using electronic gadgets to make life simpler.
