    May 1999 — October 2005 (6 years 6 months)

Directed financial analysis; internal and regulatory reporting; accounting operations; budgeting; forecasting and Human Resources that led to long-term improvements in cost savings, profitability and productivity. Ask Jeeves was acquired by IAC Interactive in 2005.

Responsible for managing, preparing, administering, and directing the control of the operating and forecasting plans. Managed the activities of the financial analyst staff and approved recommendations for financial planning and control.
