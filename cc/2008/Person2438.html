Paul Barsch’s Summary

Paul directs professional services marketing for Teradata, a $1.8B company and the world’s leader in enterprise data warehousing and analytics. At Teradata, Paul is responsible for driving marketing support and deployment of specific data warehousing service offers as well as differentiating Teradata’s global professional services from low cost consultancies and platform vendors.

Prior to Teradata, Paul was marketing director for EDS' $1.3B healthcare industry which includes payer, provider, and life sciences segments. In this role, Paul administered all aspects of EDS' healthcare marketing including press and analyst relations, thought leadership, sales support, industry awareness, and revenue generation programs.

His consulting career started at BearingPoint. As a senior manager, Paul directed marketing programs for the wireless, CRM and supply chain management practices--with global revenues totaling over $700m.

Speaking and writing are two of Paul's passions. He has over fifteen technical and marketing articles published in Health Leaders magazine, Teradata Magazine, and MarketingProfs. Paul is a member of Toastmasters International, and was nominated for Toastmaster of the Year (2007), Area 18 (Southern CA). He has received the Advanced Communicator Gold (ACG) award, Toastmaster's highest speaking distinction.

Paul earned his Bachelors of Science in Business Administration degree from California Polytechnic State University, San Luis Obispo, with a concentration in marketing management.

Paul can be reached at paulbarsch(at)alumni(dot)calpoly(dot)edu. He and his family reside in San Diego, CA.
