Karen Krajenbrink’s Summary

Need dogs designed? I'm your artist. Need flying pigs? Sure. FIFTY FOOT ROBOTS ATTACKING MARS?! Sure. I'm currently an art monkey for Made in Paradise, doing whatever tasks are needed to be done. I specialize in story, visual development, and general insanity. I normally juggle three jobs at once because I'm a workaholic, but I can't see myself doing anything else but being a creative kook.
