Chris Hunter’s Summary

I am currently in charge of Sales and Operations for all of my companies and am interested in business development, Internet marketing, software development and web design. I currently own three companies:

Web Unlimited
SmartISD
Brazos Valley Design, Inc.

----

Web Unlimited - http://www.webunlimited.com

Web Unlimited currently designs between 20-30 websites per month that ranges from small to medium business websites to government and education websites. We focus on creating websites that actually work & can be found on Google.

We are in the process of ramping up our nation-wide efforts and we hope to be producing around 100 websites per month by late 2008.

Specialties:
Web Design, Web Development, E-Commerce, Hosting, Domain Registration, Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Search Engine Marketing, Internet Marketing, Logo Design

Brazos Valley Design, Inc - http://www.brazosvalleydesign.com

Brazos Valley Design, Inc is the parent company for all of our other companies and products. We purchased the Web Unlimited name in 2005 to further our nationwide interests.

Brazos Valley Design, Inc.'s specialties:
Web Design, Web Development, Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Search Engine Marketing, Internet Marketing, Logo Design, Letterhead Design, Envelope Design, Business Card Design, Identity Design

SmartISD - http://www.smartisd.com

SmartISD is my brainchild and pet project. SmartISD is a School District Content Management System software that allows teachers and administrators to easily help manage their school district's website. We are currently focusing quite a bit on accessibility on school district websites..
