Keith Tatum’s Summary

I am the principal of Slingthought, an independent consultancy that focuses on User Experience Strategy (UXS) for the Web, Mobile Devices and Interactive Marketing Efforts.

The Slingthought multidisciplinary approach brings together rapid prototyping and visualization, illustration, information architecture, usability, visual design and interactive marketing, to enable you to quickly see what your strategies and visions look like.

Slingthought provides a blend of strategy and visualization services for your interactive projects. Whether you are looking for buy-in to sell ideas, proof of concept before moving into development, or lack internal user-centered design resources, Slingthought is the trusted partner for translating ideas into action.

Visit www.slingthought.com for examples of work and additional details.

Information Architecture, Rapid Prototyping, Usability Testing, User Interface Design, Graphic Design, Brainstorming / Workshop Facilitation, Web & Marketing Strategy, Email Marketing Strategy, Ecommerce Strategy, Goal-directed Design, Visual Explanations
