Arthur Chapman’s Summary

Arthur Chapman is our principal broker, having been a top producing agent at the area's largest real estate agency for over ten years. He was born and brought up in Newport, spent time in commercial real estate in New York before returning to become a Rhode Island realtor initially based in Providence and then in Newport County, where he now lives.

David Enstone, a local businessman and entrepreneur based in Jamestown, RI, is the other principal business partner. He is British by origin having moved to the USA in 1993 with a background in senior company management and most recently in major corporate turnarounds.

Chapman Enstone's philosophy is to hire and develop the most talented agents and back them up with a team of licensed support staff using a state-of-the-art operating system to ensure we offer world class customer service. We thrive on a culture of winning and continuous improvement and we believe we can offer a superior package of benefits to assist sellers and buyers alike.

Arthur Chapman’s Specialties:

Full service real estate, mortgage, insurance agency throughout New England with connections throughout the US and internationally.
