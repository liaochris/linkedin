Melissa Ward’s Summary

Web development is both and art and a science. While most web development firms focus on solely on aesthetics, our firm creates a custom web interface that is both pleasing to the eye, representative of your business and highly targeted to your market. NewWard helps you create more then just a web site; we help you create an online marketing model that enables you to track leads and sales. At NewWard Development, LLC we know that the bottom line to all your efforts is to increase your return on investment. An effective web site increases both your business income and your productivity.

Internet Marketing Specialist: We get to know you and your business, and delve deep into your target market. Don't know who your market is? We will help you figure it out. We utilize proven solutions for organic (free) Search Engine Marketing Methods and set up and monitor PPC Campaigns. Create Web Based marketing plans and track ROI on web based marketing efforts. We implement a system that allows you to take advantage of social marketing methods to increase web traffic and target the customers you want to do business with.

Our goal is to ensure that your web site serves the visitors you want to attract and do business with. We have several applications that allow our clients to maintain their own website without costly updates and does not require you, the client, to know HTML. You wear enough hats, let us make sure that your web site is working for you instead of you working for it.

Application Development: With focus on PHP/MySql, HTML, Flash, Java & CSS. We also offer ASP, ASP.net and access database development.

Have an idea that seems out of reach? Call us... our "out of the box" thinking gets our clients online faster, cost effectively and more efficiently then they ever thought possible.

At NewWard Development, LLC it is our job to Make Your Website Your Best Employee.
