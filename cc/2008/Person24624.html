Don Synstelien’s Summary

I have experience in new business development, strategy and acquiring talented teams, both creative and technical. I've successfully launched several products for companies like Myspace, Fox Sports and The Weather Channel among many others. Most recently, My team and I designed MySpace for the iPhone, an app that was commented by users as the best way to access Myspace *ever* and that is rated at four stars in the iTunes store. (Facebook only got two)

Other products of note: I founded SpringWidgets.com, Initiated and led the creation of a complete cross FIM analytics/metrics product, I demoed, conceptualized or spearheaded nearly every major initiative at FIM for the first two years, including such diverse projects as a self serve ad system, a developer network, an ad network, Financial website, Several redesigns of Myspace products, more top-secret projects than public ones and helped influence over 30 million in sales across Myspace and other various FIM properties.

Currently I'm working for Myspace, co-heading FIMLabs, leading design/experience efforts for Myspace Mobile, providing strategy for several secret projects and working with patent attorneys.

Don Synstelien’s Specialties:

Specializing in strategy, vision and R&D for social networks. Very knowledgeable about widgets, apps, mobile, brand advertising in social networks, monetizing social networks, advertiser metrics and building companies and products on a shoestring.

I dream.

I tilt at windmills.

I run the gauntlet.
