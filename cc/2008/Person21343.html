Richard Wooden’s Summary

I’m a business catalyst and consultant that specializes in high value, CRM solutions for my clients who want to grow their business. Specifically those businesses that have a strong desire to:

• Improve Sales Performance
• Create Delightful Customer Experiences,
• Building Lasting Customer Relationships, and
• Execute Profitable Customer Strategies.

I help my clients understand and clarify the solution options available. We work together to Diagnose what is needed as I help them Discover what is possible. We then Design for and Deliver on their expected results.


Clients that I have helped as their trusted solution provider of Customer Relationships Management systems with ERP back office integrations include:

- Paint Manufacturers for Automotive, Military, Aero-Space, Marine & Commercial Industries

- Providers of Waste-derived alternate fuel and systems.

- Professional services such as CPA's and the CRM-4-CPA's vertical.

- Fabricator and distributor of concrete reinforcing steel and related products.

- Discrete manufacturer of electric motors

- Distributor of water filtration systems.


I have hands on experience as an Application Designer, Developer and Support Manager for 5 - 100+ user integrated ERP and CRM systems.

Additionally, experience in the areas of IT Technical Network, Application Development and Database management.
