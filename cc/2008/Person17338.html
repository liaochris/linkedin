Carson Conant’s Summary

CEO and Co-Founder of Mediafly, inc. (Mediafly.com). Mediafly is a new media technology company providing technology and services to improve the entire new media ecosystem. Mediafly makes New Media... "Easy Media" for the masses (and profitable for publishers).

Consumers use Mediafly's innovative management portal, Mediafly.com, to create personalized channels of their favorite shows. Consumers can then deliver those shows to their computer, mobile device, or internet-enabled home stereo device -- same channels; same experience across all devices.

The free Mediafly SyncClient software integrates Mediafly Channels with most popular media players, such as iPod, iPhone, Sansa, iriver, creative zen, Sony, and more. But the best experience is on devices which integrate Mediafly directly on the device, such as the Chumby (see Chumby.com).

Mediafly offers a media management, presentation, and delivery platform for websites and media companies to deliver a better user experience reduce costs of managing and delivering media, and increasing ad-supported income. Here are a few companies currently leveraging Mediafly’s innovative suite of technologies: www.PodcastPickle.com, NBC5.com/Podcast, BestBuy.com/summer
