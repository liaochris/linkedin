James Burchill’s Summary

James Burchill is an Internet marketing and social media expert, SEO consultant, corporate blogger, speaker, publisher and author. He consults with businesses on how to create web marketing that gets you found, gets you noticed and gets you more busine$$.

James is also a certified Coach and creator of numerous web sites and other educational and training materials. His unique approach to web marketing (along with his personable training services) makes him the preferred choice for select companies of all sizes: from ’solo-preneurs’ to large corporations including Goodyear, eBay, Esso, CAA, Rolls Royce, BMO, and DoFasco.

Prior to starting his own consulting practice in 1999, James was Vice President of IT & Consulting for an international management and recruiting firm in southern Ontario. There he lead numerous multi million dollar development projects and managed a team of more than a dozen skilled web programmers and designers.

James has lived in Europe, Africa and Australia and along with numerous credentials including public speaking, psychology, communications, automation and technology, James is a certified Neuro Linguistics practitioner and learned the craft of penmanship at England’s prestigious London School of Writing.

James enjoys public speaking, training and writing about Internet technologies (such as social media and content marketing) and why you need to embrace the Internet for both personal and professional use. Current presentations and keynotes include: social media marketing, online brand and personal reputation management, and strategic content - blogging for business.
