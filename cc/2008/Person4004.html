Hendy Irawan’s Summary

I started my programming life in Delphi for developing desktop applications, later on I also used Microsoft .NET, PHP, and Ruby on Rails. Presently I focus on Java and enabling technologies (OSGi, jRuby, Groovy, Spring, ... too many to mention for this particular platform.)

My goal is to develop the most practical solutions which maximizes business profits while giving the best value and experience for customers. This does not necessarily involve the best or newest technology, but requires cooperation between businesses and solution providers, in addition to feedback from customers, so a win-win-win solution can be achieved with the least effort.

I currently handle the bulk of the management and overall direction for Soluvas, an IT consulting headquartered in Bandung, Indonesia.
