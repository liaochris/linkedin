Ole Cram is president of Marcobe Investments, Inc. which researches investments for his own portfolio as well as for other investors. Mr. Cram has invested in oil and gas drilling ventures, real estate, stocks, options, commodities, business startups, and other ventures. Mr. Cram now shares his experiences with others to help them avoid some of the lessons he has had to learn the hard way. Mr. Cram links accredited investors with appropriate direct energy investments he participates in that fit their investment strategy. You can view his "Accredited Investor Talk" blog at http://accreditedinvestortalk.blogspot.com/ and the Marcobe Investments, Inc. corporate website at www.MarcobeInvestmentsInc.com.
