Bill Dollins’s Summary

I have been working in the GIS industry for 15 years. I currently lead the GIS business at Zekiah Technologies. I am interested in the integration of geospatial technologies with traditional enterprise systems. My background is software engineering and database development and I am proficient with most modern development languages.

Bill Dollins’s Experience

  • Software Developer

    zigGIS

    (Non-Profit; 1-10 employees; Computer Software industry)

    January 2007 — Present (1 year 5 months)

Efforts focused mainly on enhancing spatial reference support, code cleanup and porting to ArcGIS 9.2. Joined the project after its inception and collaborate with two of the best developers I have had the pleasure of working with.
