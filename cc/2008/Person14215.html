Katie Lips’s Summary

Creative Innovator and Entrepreneur; passionate advocate and designer of social web services.

Cofounder of Treasuremytext a popular Mobile 2.0 startup which enables users from all over the world to save their treasured SMS messages online. Treasuremytext is currently in a period of expansion, new product updates and partnerships to be announced soon. http://www.treasuremytext.com

Cofounder of Kisky Netmedia a successful boutique agency specializing in Social Media Strategy. Since 2006 I have expanded the business and grown the client base to include lading UK public sector organisations and blue chip clients. Kisky delivers Social Media Strategy for the Health and Safety Executive, bfi, FACT, Merseyside Fire and Rescue Service, ICDC, The Liverpool Culture Company, Holidaybreak PLC and Unilever.
