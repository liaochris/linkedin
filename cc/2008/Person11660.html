James F. Robinson’s Summary

Filmmaker and visionary entertainment and mobile executive with strong international experience and relationships. A proven leader in getting results and innovating new forms of entertainment for mobile devices and bringing new creative services to market. Founded CinemaElecrtic Inc. and built a mobile distribution network in more than 50 countries before taking the company public. Extensive experience in motion pictures and television, wrote, produced &, directed the award-winning independent motion picture, STILL BREATHING which was distributed by Paramount Pictures, Universal and Disney. Recently created TEXT-ME TV for client Active Loop, a live broadcast Participation TV project in association with CW Network affiliates.

  • Motion Picture Producer/Director

    ZapPictures

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Motion Pictures and Film industry)

    May 1996 — November 1998 (2 years 7 months)

    Secured financing for, and produced, wrote and directed the feature film, STILL BREATHING starring Brendan Fraser, Joanna Going, Lou Rawls, Ann Magnuson and Oscar® winner Celeste Holm. STILL BREATHING won the best actor award at the Seattle Film Festival for Mr. Fraser and was nominated for the Grand Prix des Amériques at the Montreal Film Festival. The film was distributed theatrically in the US by USA/October Films and internationally via Paramount Pictures and The Walt Disney Company. The soundtrack was produced by Mr. Robinson and distributed by Will Records. STILL BREATHING was chosen by Blockbuster Films to launch their “Only at Blockbuster” exclusive video series and has been seen on HBO, Showtime, Cinemax, The Sundance Channel, The Movie Channel, Independent Film Channel, ABC Family Channel, American Movie Classics and Bravo. www.stillbreathing.com
