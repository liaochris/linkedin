Brooks Cole’s Summary

With 26 years experience in communications design and over a decade of experience in interactive design, Brooks is the founder and creative leader behind HoloCosmos - a S.W.A.T. team of media magicians at the cutting-edge of human-computer interaction, and now HoloGenesis, an exciting startup in the area of semantic search and navigation.

HoloCosmos creates next generation user experience and branding for websites, media devices, online communities, web applications, and channels for making change and advancing sustainability and thrivability.

Brooks is a relentless information age inventor. "We're creating new lenses to see deeply into emerging information and media networks. I believe that a lot of our society's problems come from vision problems. Not problems with our eyes, but problems with our ability to see the truth -- even when it is right in front of us -- or when it is skillfully and deliberately hidden from our view. I believe most people and organizations would individually and collectively do the right thing if we could see what is really up."

His areas of development include "HoloMap," an "interdependence technology" to be shared by changemakers and their organizations, and "PhilanthroMap," a similar technology to advance transformational Philanthropy. "HoloLearning" is an electronic curriculum standard combining realtime online interaction, 3D gamelike navigation and DVD/Streaming digital video; "HoloFinder" is a VR knowledge finder for dynamic navigation of spatial information arrays; "MusiVerse" is a 3D Music Universe Navigator for exploring dimensional relationships and affinities in digital media stores and libraries; "ChronoFinder" is a navigator for historical and time-based information as an explorable landscape; and "HoloServer" is an engine for the delivery of dynamically-edited and composited "MetaMedia" fashioned on-the-fly from multiple mediatypes and sources.

See http://holocosmos.com/brooksbio.html

Brooks Cole’s Specialties:

User interface design, interactive television and IPTV design, information architecture, brand architecture, visual design, open source beauty advocate, applied futurist, synaesthete.

"Visual turnaround" specialist called in to intervene to redesign websites and rebrand organizations in mission-critical situations.
