Sharon Eucce’s Summary

With over 25 years in the printing, paper and graphic design industry, I'm providing consultative insight and insider knowledge for packaging projects. Startup customers work with me to roll out their products. Corporate clients work with me to have their design & marketing departments inspired while their procurement managers are satisfied. My personal interests in art, design, branding, architecture, gardening, people, food, wine and history all contribute to my approach to this business.
