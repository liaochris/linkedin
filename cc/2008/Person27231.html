Will York’s Summary

News reporter and editor with broad experience in hard news reporting, features writing, news research and news gathering, investigative reporting and editing. Direct experience with graphic news design, advertising design and page layout. Experience in overseeing news ideas from concept genesis all the way to final production. Advertising experience includes demographic research, creative work and sales. Interest in convergence media and multimedia reporting, with past work in Web maintenance (HTML and database). Interested in writing, editing and consulting projects.

Also interested in intellectual property and First Amendment/media law, and graduate plans include law school and graduate studies in journalism.

Will York’s Specialties:

Investigative and issues reporting, news design and layout, graphic design, media management, advertising and sales
