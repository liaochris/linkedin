Dipl.-Kfm. Michael Borst (Career-Brokers.com)’s Summary

Michael Borst (born 1968) studied business administration at the University of Bayreuth. He launched his professional career with Xerox and held international sales positions with OpenShop, Akamai, GFT and Polaris Software Lab. In July 2004 he founded Borst Consulting Group.
