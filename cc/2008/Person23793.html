Brett Bumeter’s Summary

Brett is a freelance writer and social marketing expert with a focus in blog marketing. Brett writes regularly for a websites and publications and provides freelance articles for several others. He also ghost writes for multiple CEO blogs in industries covering finance, marketing and the environment.

He brings 18 years in business experience working in multiple disciplines from sales, finance, marketing, law, and consumer electronics to a developing blog and social media industry that he has been integral in developing during the last two years. He is responsible for building, planning and executing blog marketing campaigns that generate SEM results, buzz marketing results, and social media plays in a market where more people are turning to other people for recommendations and advice on purchases and services.

Brett has developed relationships with over a dozen different blog marketing networks, along with hundreds of writers, editors, Pro Bloggers, Podcasters, and video podcast organizations. Brett's reach can push a message out over tens of thousands of websites.

Utilizing Brett's blog marketing and blog SEO techniques, it is possible to move a message out through the internet touching hundreds of thousands or even millions of viewers. These techniques can be applied to a client's own blog or pushed out across the blogosphere across other blogs in the US or around the world.


For more information on Brett's professional experience you can view his leadership profile at Softduit Partners. (http://www.softduit.com/leadprofile.htm )

Brett is working to revolutionize the entertainment industry currently. He is building the foundations for a future empire that will transform the medium and industry as it exists today.
