  • Editor in Chief

    LesNouvelles.net

    (Online Media industry)

    February 2008 — Present (7 months)

    Since february 2008, online web site LesNouvelles.net took the "Pro" route, with a strong focus on the CSOs and their team. Besides our own original stories, we already have enlisted a team of high profile CSOs who agreed to contribute opinion papers and experts from other domains we did not cover yet (Economic Intelligence or Risk Management for example).

    Additionally, our own stories are no longer limited to "desk type" news. Our policy so far was to not be a competitor to our regular print customers by publishing on our own website free stories of the same quality as what what we would sell them. We changed this and starting Feb,1st our original stories are of the same quality as what we do produce for major IT magazines like 01 Informatique.

    With its eight years history, LesNouvelles.net is already very well established in the French Info Security landscape. We are now making it a stronger brand even amongst the CSOs and the experts community, as a qualified audience.
