Ajith Abraham’s Summary

Ajith's research experience includes over 18 years in the Industry and Academia spanning different countries/continents in Australia, America, Asia and Europe. He works in a multi-disciplinary environment involving computational intelligence, network security, sensor networks, e-commerce, Web intelligence, Web services, computational grids, data mining and applied to various real world problems. He has authored/co-authored over 400 research publicationsand is also the winner of 6 best paper awards. He has given more than 20 plenary lectures and conference tutorials in these areas.

He received Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Monash University, Australia and a Master of Science degree from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore.

Currently works in Norwegian University of Science and Technology and also
holds an Adjunct Professor appointment in Jinan University, China and Dalian Maritime University, China. He has held academic appointments in Monash University, Australia; Oklahoma State University, USA; Chung-Ang University, Seoul and Yonsei University, Seoul, Open University of Calalyunya, Spain and Rovira i Virgili University, Spain. Before turning into a full time academic, he was working with three International companies: Keppel Engineering, Singapore, Hyundai Engineering, Korea and Ashok Leyland Ltd, India where he was involved in different industrial research and development projects for nearly 8 years.

He serves the editorial board of more than 30 reputed International journals and has also guest edited 29 special issues on various topics. In 2008, he is nvolved as the General Co-chair / General Chair of AMS 2008, Malaysia; ISDA'08, Taiwan; IAS'07, Italy; HIS'08, Spain; CSTST'08, France; EMS'08, UK and the Program Chair/Co-chair of ICDIM'08, UK; ECDM 2008, Netherlands; CISIM'08, Czech Republic and the Tutorial Chair of WI-IAT'08, Australia.

For further information, please visit: http://www.softcomputing.net

Ajith Abraham’s Specialties:

Research expertise: Hybrid soft computing, neural networks, fuzzy systems, global optimization, machine learning, multi-agent systems etc.

Application areas include: information security, Web intelligence, Web services, financial modeling, weather analysis, energy management, knowledge management, multi criteria decision making, scheduling etc.
