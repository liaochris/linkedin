Rob Nickel’s Summary

I am a professional speaker dealing with child safety on the internet as well as a Canadian Best Selling Author with my book "Staying Safe in a Wired World". My company is dedicated to helping protect children on the internet from all dangers, and we accomplish this by doing mainstream media, producing educational material, and speaking to students, parents, teachers and communities on Internet safety.
