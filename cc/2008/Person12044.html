Jonathan McCoy’s Summary

Serial entrepreneur and technologist with a diverse experience in semiconductors, software, and investment.

Presently, acting as founder of early-stage startup, Perception Labs, including oversight of R&D, development of IP strategy, and management of alpha customers.

Interested in discussing languages, information, communication and computing; testing innovative technology tools; analyzing large, complex problems; meeting big thinkers and innovative entrepreneurs; and the ongoing study of related science and history.

Past professional experiences include projects as an engineer (test / verification, software, networking / telecommunications), working as a Product Marketing Manager (semiconductor product line), and successfully managing active investments in several small ventures (IT, Logistics, Retail/Food). Also, currently an investment partner in a growing venture management firm in Austin, TX.
