Giorgio Occhioni’s Summary

With nearly 2 decade of professional experience in all aspects of Application Software Development, I recently started ABET ICT Consulting in London with the mission of helping Small and Medium Enterprises to innovate in their business by using efficiently Information and Telecommunication Technology Solutions.

I provide advice and support in the identification of Business processes that can benefit from Information and Communication Technologies; I analyse, define, manage and translate Business Requirements into Technical Requirements or Technical solutions.

I am keen to take Consulting (Business Analyst/Problem Solving) roles in UK/EMEA region, and I am happy to travel for short/medium assignments.

Worked for more than 17 years on Software Development Lifecycle (and that's not counting another 10 as student in Electronic Engineering and then in Computer Science), Business Analysis (Telecom and Aerospace) and Project Management.
I enjoy new challenges, I moved to and from different arenas, from Newtorking protocols to GSM service provisioning to Maritime applications, Grid Computing, e-Healthcare and Semantic Web.

I still enjoy hands-on technical and tactical challenges together with more long-term business strategic problems.
