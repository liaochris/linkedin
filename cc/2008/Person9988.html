Iose' Magno’s Summary

Iosè Magno is the founder of Blucape. He planned all the Blucape product conceptual and basic technological constructions from the settlement of the product vision to the technological and functional cores.

Iosè devoted his career to design new technologies let everyday users overcome the current communication systems functional limitations and barriers.
When he is not working on some innovative idea to improve technology, Iosè spends his time praying and meditating, walking in nature, playing his guitar or reading sacred scriptures.

He is happily engaged.
