John Holleman’s Summary

My second book, "Understanding American Idioms Using Word Puzzles," has been accepted for publication and will be forthcoming through the Commerical Press, the oldest publisher in China. My first book, "American English Idiomatic Expressions in 52 Weeks: An Easy Way to Understand English Expressions and Improve Speaking" was published by the Chinese University Press in 2006.

I also offer a series of seminars to improve the business English competencies of non-native English speakers. The English Speaking and Business Communication series of workshops include:
* Improving English Speaking Skills
* Personal Effectiveness
* Interpersonal Communication
* Communicating with Your Team
* Powerful Presentations
* Resolving Problems and Conflicts
* Communicating With Customers
* Technology of Communication

John Holleman’s Specialties:
