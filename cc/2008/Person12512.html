John Eyles’s Summary

I'm an ideas person, an educator and a businessman obsessed with pragmatic outcomes. My current expertise is in understanding trends, risks and opportunities in the area of Online Social Networking, E-Learning, Web 3.0 and Future Telco Services.

My Vision:

Education towards freedom for an inclusive and sustainable world though cooperation, mutual respect and understanding, supported by new communication technologies.

My Goals:

* Working to create a sustainable future through engaging, globally connected, student centred education
* Working with visionary thinkers and creators
* Working in the online learning, social media and communications space
* Working to develop visual literacy and image based learning
* Working to meld audiovisual mobile media and virtual world gaming into learning games and new knowledge systems
