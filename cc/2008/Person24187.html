Steve Blamey’s Summary

Steve has more than 10 years experience as an IT manager, support person, analyst and administrator in small and medium sized businesses. He has developed all-round skills and knowledge while working on IT planning, process improvement, system implementation and support, often in organisations with international offices and remote workers.

Smaller organisations generally have limited time and resources and need their people to be flexible and adapt to changing situations, Steve remains hands-on with wide ranging technical knowledge and the experience to communicate and deliver benefits at all levels. He has implemented a wide range of IT solutions and provided practical help with technology.

In 2005 Steve became a self-employed consultant and contractor and now helps small businesses use IT effectively as well as building affordable websites using open source software.
