Colin Savage’s Summary

Over 13 years in Executive Recruitment within the Technology sector and co-founded Kingston Consulting 11 years ago . Extensive experience of working with some of the most progressive and exciting Global Technology organizations.

An active Principal Consultant, focusing on Senior Sales, Marketing, Executive and Board level assignments in the UK and across EMEA, managing specific Specialist Practice areas in the Technology sector, including a focus on senior sales & marketing positions.

Career Management advice given to senior executives looking to make their next career move.

At Kingston Consulting, we pride ourselves on our professionalism, attention to detail and knowledge of the industry. See our testimonials on our website

www.kingston-consulting.com

These combined skills ensure that our interview to offer ratios are the lowest in the industry at 2:1 – which means for every 2 people we send on interview, one is offered.
