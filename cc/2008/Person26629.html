Vic Beck’s Experience

  • Captain, Public Affairs Officer

    U.S. Navy Reserve

    (Government Agency; 10,001 or more employees; Military industry)

    1985 — Present (23 years)

    Active duty from 1985 to 1992. The first five years I served as a Surface Warfare Officer on the USS Enterprise (CVN 65) and the USS Richard E. Byrd (DDG 23). Since 1990 I have been a Public Affairs Officer in the reserve and on active duty. During my 23-year career I have been actively involved in several major military public affairs activities throughout the United States, Europe and the Middle East. I have been a spokesman for New England during Operation Desert Storm, I headed up the reserve public affairs support for the historic sailing of the USS Constitution in 1997 (the US Navy received a PRSA Silver Anvil Award for this event), and I have earned more than ten medals for personal achievements.
