Bruce Masterson’s Summary

Hands-on manager with entrepreneurial passion and energy tempered with the discipline and experience gained from domestic and international senior executive roles (CEO/COO/EVP) in the information services business. Extensive experience in the US, UK & India building and running business units in internet publishing, financial information services, healthcare informatics and business process outsourcing (BPO). Key strengths include business development finesse coupled with astute operational and financial management, and interpersonal skills honed in a multi-cultural environment. Demonstrated ability to build a strong corporate culture grounded in quality metrics with teams that deliver sustainable operating results that are exceptional.

In summary, I spent the fist half of my career learning how to manage people and an I/S in a multi-national setting, and the second half learning how to manage a B/S and the ins and outs of corporate governance in the US and India.

Highlights:

•Developed and implemented internet strategy to aggregate market of 10mm landlords;

•Established and directly managed +50 FTE capitive offshore operation in India, reducing costs by +60% with higher quality and bandwidth for innovation. Unit was ISO 9000:1 certified in August 2007;

•Managed high growth business units with 650 and 125 staff respectively in Chicago and London in the information services industry;

•Pioneered new venture in healthcare informatics for Fortune 500 Company.

Bruce Masterson’s Specialties:

International business (UK, India, EU), business development, managing information services business entities, capital structure, mergers and acquisition,
