Kelleen Griffin’s Summary

EXECUTIVE COACH & Leadership Consultant

As an experienced executive coach, Kelleen supports leaders in creating work that works and a life that works--at the same time. What makes her work unique is her ability to draw from many disciplines and experiences including leadership, business, entrepreneurship, emotional intelligence, blended learning methodologies, psychology, and a variety of healing approaches. She brings a range of tools and the expertise to observe and assess breakdowns from many vantage points. Kelleen is able to provide a sounding board, support, training, and opportunities for growth no longer available from traditional sources like family, friends, partners, teachers, even bosses. She helps clients leverage their dissatisfaction in any domain of their life to facilitate meaning, increase effectiveness, promote new interpretations, improve communication, clarify legacy, and create positive and lasting awareness. Kelleen has over 20 years of corporate experience, including leadership development, coaching, training, facilitating and has travelled all over the world to speak with clients. She’s been an Executive Coach since 2003, after completing her Master’s of Science degree in counseling at The Johns Hopkins University in Baltimore, Maryland. She is a certified consultant of the emotional intelligence assessment: Eq in Action. She also holds an MBA from Columbia Business School in New York City.

Kelleen Griffin’s Specialties:

Broad experience in all facets of facilitation, coaching, training, and leadership development. Combined 20 years of experience in banking, finance, and business development with 7 years as a coach, counselor, and intuitive in business situations. My job is to help you achieve your goals by guiding you through the energetic process of your business dealings and goals. There's always more in any situation than the facts and the term sheet - the 'human' side of enterprise is my specialty.
