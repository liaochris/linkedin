Goal or Mission Statement

To Make a Difference in the World

Those that know me would make one generic statement about me: "SKI eats, drinks, and sleeps business." My passion is to make the world a better place, and few venues afford the opportunities available to those that own a small business in America.

A consultant for over twenty years, I am driven to produce results... in fact, my slogan for my Throughput.us LLC consulting practice: "Simply Results."

Up next: Rhino Island Media

In 1998 I made my first appearance on a cable TV talk show on Hilton head Island, South Carolina. I have had the bug to do more with video (especially given my internet skills) and recently partnered with two others to make "Network Quality Internet Video" a reality.

We are actively seeking collaborations to push network quality video produced from conferences, talk shows, infomercials and all other channels onto the information super highway with one goal in mind: help business owners make money now, and even more money in the future.

Once flush with cash, business owners can attack the world's challenges head on with sufficient resources to make a real difference.
