Cathryn Symons’s Summary

As a Project Manager, I bring focus and structure to IT projects ranging in size from £300k to £3M, providing a framework in which team members can give of their best

I have considerable experience in project recovery, taking on projects which have come off the rails and putting them back on track to delivery. I have managed development, infrastructure and package implementation projects pragmatically applying structured methods.

Having worked successfully for blue-chips, consultancies and as a contractor, both in the United Kingdom and abroad, I now run my own independent practice.
