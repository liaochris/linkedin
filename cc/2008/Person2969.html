Cliff Etzel’s Summary

I have worked as a visual content creator for over 30 years. I worked as a photographer full time up to 1997 and then part time up until 2002 where I began the transition over to video. From 1997 to the present, I immersed myself in learning what the Internet was becoming and have adapted my work to reflect that trend. I worked in newspaper and magazine for over 10 years and since 1997, have been a published writer on several portal websites devoted to the sport diving industry, specializing in freediving.

I blog about shooting in the solo vj paradigm at solovj.com in addition to my company website at bluprojekt.com

Cliff Etzel’s Specialties:

I produce, shoot, edit, and deliver video projects to clients as a single person video production entity. I work in SONY Vegas Pro 8, Sound Forge 9, Acid Pro 4 and Cinescore 1.0, which provides an efficient post production platform for the Solo Video Journalist paradigm. In addition, I also work as a web designer for specific clients that I feel can benefit from my services.
