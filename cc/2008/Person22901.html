Scott Bellware’s Summary

I am a software product designer, developer, and agile coach living in Austin, TX.

I speak at software industry conferences and teach agile development practices and software production methodologies in workshops in the US and Canada.

I'm the founder of the AgileATX community of agile software practitioners in Austin.

I'm the organizer of the ALT.NET Open Space and Kaizen conferences in Austin, and I served as the content chairman for the agile development track at the DevTeach conferences, as well as the chairman of the INETA Speaker Committee.

I am a recipient of Microsoft's Most Valuable Professional award.

Scott Bellware’s Specialties:

.NET; Ruby; Rails; Software product design, analysis, requirements, implementation, testing, team organization, and lifecycle; Web; Data access and databases; Data Modeling; Domain Modeling; User Experience and Customer Experience review; Agile Planning and Development; Lean Software Production; Test-Driven Development; Continuous Integration; Behavior-Driven Development; Windows; Mac.
