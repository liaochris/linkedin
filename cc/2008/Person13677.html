Judy Bradt’s Summary

I make government contracts easier! As top expert author, speaker and consultant for over 20 years, I bring you tested tips, success strategies and fresh ideas you need to explore government contracting and win.

Find out whether this is good business for your business. Get answers to essential questions: What do you need to know? Who do you need to know? Where do you go from here?

My mission: to enable you, with your unique capabilities and solutions, to reach more government buyers and through them serve citizens better.

I have been doing this work for over 20 years, including 15 as a Trade Commissioner at the Canadian Embassy in Washington DC, where I helped over 5,000 companies win over $250 million in US government contracts.
