Tracy Brant’s Summary

What is web hosting? Rather like being a real estate developer, only digital. I have servers in data centers, and I rent out space for websites, email, and file storage.

Who are my clients? Small-to-moderate businesses and non-profits, and the web designers and developers that serve them. We have special plans for web development and marketing firms that support multiple web clients.

My education is in community planning, oddly enough, which has turned out to be very suitable to the work I do now. There is no part of my professional or my personal life that has not turned out to be useful to one development project or another.

You can read more about the development of Gryphyn Media here: http://gryphynmedia.com/about/

"The Internet is one big poorly-organized library, flea market, peep show, soapbox, town meeting, theater, switchboard, museum, and bar fight... and I have plenty of work I can do as a community planner, information organizer, activist, "interior" design contractor, and even as a parent."

I am available as a speaker about Internet use, e-commerce, email, web development, and other technical topics. Recent speaking gigs include the Philadelphia Area Computer Society and the Philadelphia Writer's Conference.
