Walt Verastegui’s Summary

eCommerce manager with 4 years SEM/SEO experience. Strong skill set for maximizing relevant search traffic and search engine exposure through proven search marketing strategies.

As eCommerce Manager, I am responsible for maximizing relevant search traffic and search engine exposure for Bizjournals and other American City Business Journals’ online properties. This includes 42 local online business journals, SceneDaily.com (online presence for NASCAR Scene magazine), our partnership company OnTargetJobs (parent company of niche job boards including Hcareeres, CareerBank, HealtheCareers, MedHunters, BioSpace & HireHealth), Dow Jones Business & Relationship Intelligence (formerly Generate Inc), and Hemmings.com (online motor news and world’s largest collector-car marketplace).

My role involves developing and implementing proven SEO strategies site-wide for Bizjournals; the nation’s largest publisher of metropolitan business newspapers, with an emphasis to deliver relevant traffic via organic results.
