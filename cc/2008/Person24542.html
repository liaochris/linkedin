    (Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Writing and Editing industry)

    January 1983 — Present (25 years 7 months)

    I write books for children, from picture books to young adult. I also teach writing to all ages.

    I like to visit schools and give students the chance to meet and work with a real author, sharing my passion for writing with students and teachers alike. Presentations include: “Storyparts,” an introductory writing workshop, “Quiet Heroes”, where children learn that not every hero has super powers, "Family Writing Workshop" where families write together and “Retell It as a Poem” which encourages students to write in narrative verse.

    I am available for motivational speeches for writers and readers of all ages. Popular speech topics include, "Once Upon a Time There was a Girl Who Wanted to Write", the story of how I became a writer, "Write On Right Now", a writing workshop suitable for all ages, and "You Have to Want it Bad", about being willing to take the risks you need to take in order to follow your dreams.
