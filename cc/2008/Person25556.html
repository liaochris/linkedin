I also have experience using J2EE in Tomcat talking to an Oracle backend, custom C servers on HP-UX, Windows MFC clients and mobile clients for Blackberry and J2ME devices.

Much J2EE app server setup/configuration as well (mainly Websphere/Tomcat). The last few years of my BOBJ work was digging into the client SDKs, more Java than .NET. Delivered lots of training in the middle of the nine years -- ramping people up on critical thinking/troubleshooting skills or authoring training materials for new product releases or new cooporative technologies (i.e. Java app servers as they came into prominence).
