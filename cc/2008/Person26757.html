Vincenzo A. Pistorio’s Summary

I currently live in Italy, dividing my time between Catania and Rome. I particularly love travel and socio-cultural photography, trying to "tell" stories by images. My work is available on my website where it is also possible to buy Fine Art Prints or to license images for commercial or editorial use.
I develop my website on my own accordingly with W3C and CSS standards, as described on my blog.

I am also a Project Manager with regard to ICT projects, with a solid background in complex financial-technical procurement projects in TLC industry and in a wide variety of innovative technical applications for the National Procurement Agency in Italy. I am also a member (for TLC industry) of Green Public Procurement team in order to introduce environmental requirements for sustainable projects.
