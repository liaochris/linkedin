Jennifer Leggio’s Summary

- Jennifer Leggio has more than 13 years in the communications industry, eight of which have been focused on technology and network security.

- She has managed influencer relations programs for more than five network security companies either in an agency or in-house role; complementary background as journalist and editor.

- Led interactive social networking efforts for industry conferences including SOURCE Boston and RSA Conference (Security Bloggers Meet-Up 2008), as well as a project for Black Hat USA 2008. Jennifer also forged a community for network security professionals on Twitter, a microblogging platform.

- Currently runs strategic communications, digital media and analyst relations programs for Fortinet, where she also oversees communications for the company’s security research team.

- She also writes a blog on social media trends, primarily those relevant to enterprises, at ZDNet (URL above)

- Jennifer also provides pro bono communications support for SOURCE Conference, the parent company of SOURCE Boston and SOURCE Barcelona

Jennifer Leggio’s Specialties:

Social media; security research; analyst relations; market share reporting and competitive analysis; crisis management
