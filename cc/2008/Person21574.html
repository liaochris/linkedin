Robert Sodaro’s Summary

I have been writing professionally for over 30 years, with experience in radio ad writing, as well as print and the Internet. I have written two books Trivia Mania: Commercials and Ads (Zebra Books) and Kiddie Meal Collectibles (Krause Books). I am also the author of a comicbook series entitled Agent Unknown (Renegade Press). Currently I am contributing short stories to a horror anthology (Psychosis! — Guild Works Productions).

Robert Sodaro’s Specialties:

Comicbooks, film reviews, collectibles, fast food toys, non-sports trading cards, toys
