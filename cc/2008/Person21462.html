RJ Salicco’s Summary

I am currently working as a consultant. I am Java developer with core JEE experience with an emphasis on SOA and Web development. I am a Sun Certified Java Programmer and I have an interest in and experience working with Open Source technologies such as Spring, Hibernate, Groovy, Grails, Google Web Toolkit and Flex. I have my technological preferences but I have the capabilities and personality to adapt to any work or development environment.

I have "front-end" to "back-end" application design and implementation experience and believe in stakeholder interaction from the beginning of development through delivery and deployment. I have experience with business analysis and system design. I have a passion for solving unique problems as well as approaching each client with a best-fit solution that aligns with their goals and vision. My free time is spent working on a few open source projects, presenting topics at Java User Groups, attending conferences and learning new technologies.

I started Axiomatic IT Incorporated in 2006 to provide small and medium sized businesses with reliable software solutions designed to meet their unique requirements. I have confidence in the Open Source community and try to spend my free time contributing to Open Source solutions and projects. In the future, Axiomatic IT Incorporated will have a list of projects that are completely Open Sourced for public consumption and customization.
