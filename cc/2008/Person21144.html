Rehan yar Khan’s Summary

After several years as flower seed importer into India, I started Flora2000.com in 1999, because I saw that there were no professional full fledged services that delivered flowers overseas. Sure there were Interflora, Teleflora, FTD, but these were highly developed domestic services; and you had to pay an arm & a leg to get your flowers anywhere ($19 deliver charges I recall); they took 3 days; gave you no delivery confirmation; and if your address was wrong or the recipient wasn't home it was all over.

Flora2000 is today the world's premier overseas flower delivery specialist. We deliver flowers in 153 countries, same day, with an extensive catalogue of unique products from each respective country. We back this up with local prices and free or minimal delivery charges, giving the best value on the Internet (I daresay better than most local florists offer). We provide 24hr customer service and what I like to call a 200% guarantee of satisfaction: no quibble, full refund and replacement. Lastly we have made it a policy to go the full distance and more to deliver customer's orders no matter what.

All this from a company from India? India is not known for its service quality and customer friendly policies. Its taken an exceptional team working exceptionally hard and exceptionally smart to build and maintain what we do. We have backed this up with extensive process driven operations and tons of IT.

Flora2000.com now grows rapidly, with new distribution channels; and recently launched brand building activities in New York and Bombay.

In the course of my career and especially at Flora2000, where the tasks both of starting up from India and delivering in so many countries were extremely challenging, I have been able to hone my innovation, team building and management skills. Today I can confidently bootstrap projects on a shoestring and also manage extremely complex tasks.
