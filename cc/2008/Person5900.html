Hello, I’m currently a student pursuing an B.Sc. in Computer Science at the University of New Hampshire being scheduled to graduate in May of 2009. My professional interests include Artificial Intelligence and Software Design. One of my jobs is at GameReplays.org, the premier RTS media and community site on the web, where I am a global administrator in charge of content publication and managing volunteer staff. At this site, I am involved in the Starcraft 2, Dawn of War, and Age of Empires III portals. I have worked with online communities for a number of years, having formerly been a senior staffer at Heaven Games involved in the Age of Empires III and Empire Earth portals.

The University of New Hampshire’s Interoperability Laboratory also employs me where I work on the cutting edge of networking technology. I am in the IP Consortium having experience in IPv4, IPv6, TCP, MPLS, IGMP, and the numerous routing protocols. My responsibilities center around standards based testing and development including java programming and TCL scripting.
