Cara Carriveau’s Summary

Cara has been in the radio field for two decades, most of it in Chicago, IL. She has hosted every daypart and done everything from "jock" to "news anchor", "sidekick", "traffic reporter". She also was "Program Director" for many years and "Production Director". Her unique, memorable voice and strong work ethic are admirable qualities in the broadcast industry.

She is a leader in new media - all over the internet - youtube, twitter, myspace, NBC5 Street Team, Cara's Basement, Cara Communications, etc...
