Oliver Bohl’s Summary

Oliver Bohl works as a Consultant at Accenture GmbH, Munich and is a Co-Founder of The Montecito Group, S. á. r. l., Paris. Before joining Accenture he was working as a Project Manager and Senior Researcher at the Department of Information Systems at the University of Kassel, Germany. His research activities were concentrated on aspects of eLearning networks and business models for eLearning institutions as well as for mobile services. His scope of activities concentrates on the projects „Education Network WINFOLine“-in which he has been since August, 2001-, "eduXchange - Educational Brokerage: A business framework for content exchange between Higher Education Institutions and Corporations" and "mik21". He is furthermore involved as an associated partner in the NoE PROLEARN. Mr. Bohl has a Phd and a master degree in Economics both from the University of Kassel, he also holds a Diploma in International Marketing from the Institute of Commercial Management in Bournemouth, UK. Mr. Bohl has work experience in the field of marketing, since he worked as a marketing assistant at the Illbruck Inc., and in the field information technology consulting, since working as an analyst in the Communication & High-Tech-Consultancy of Accenture Inc.
