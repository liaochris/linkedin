Lucas Ng’s Summary

Lucas Ng is currently a senior search marketing analyst at Fairfax Digital in Sydney, Australia.

Lucas is a champion of in-house search engine optimization and search engine marketing. His role includes the management of multi-million dollar PPC campaigns, working with product & technology teams to improve websites for both users and search engines and the education of corporate employees from junior developers up to CEOs in the fundamentals of search marketing.

He has strong website analytics experience, primarily using the Omniture SiteCatalyst suite to provide illumination on traffic and search performance. Lucas also provides regular competitive analysis on industry trends using data providers such as Nielsen NetRatings and Hitwise.

At Fairfax Digital, Lucas works primarily with online classifieds such automotives, jobs and real estate. He believes SEO has evolved into a school of thought centered around three objectives, increasing traffic, increasing conversion & increasing ROI.

Online, Lucas can be found on numerous search marketing forums under the username 'shor'. Most recently, Lucas was invited to help judge the 2007 SEOmoz web 2.0 awards alongside industry figures such as Danny Sullivan (Search Engine Land), Pete Cashmore (Mashable) and Anil Dash (Six Apart).

Lucas was also 'one of 37 leaders in the world of organic SEO' that contributed to the popular SEOmoz article, Google's Search Ranking Factors.
