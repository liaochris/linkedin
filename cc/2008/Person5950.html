David Stinner’s Summary

David is currently a Senior Consultant in Business Risk service area, focusing on data forensics, of Protiviti where he has served since March 2006. David has participated in over 50 data forensic acquisitions and investigations. David is experienced in forensic preservation of data from a variety of hardware including PC’s, laptops, servers, SANs, removable media, and handheld devices. He is familiar with forensic acquisition and analysis in several operating systems including Windows9x/Me, Windows 2000, WindowsXP, FedoraCore Linux, and Mac OSX. David is familiar with forensic acquisition and analysis software including EnCase, FTK, PRTK, NEMX, and Email Examiner.
