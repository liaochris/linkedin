Patrick Bonnett’s Summary

Each year, during the month of May, Insurance Agents all across America pay a special attention to their clientèle's Disability Insurance need. In fact, May has been deemed Disability Awareness Month by the national LIFE Foundation.

Disability Insurance is coverage that replaces a percentage of Income. Protecting your earning capacity is the one priority above all other financial concerns.

At Encore, we strive hard to protect and preserve the lifestyles our clients aim to achieve. To that end, we advocate that each of our clients review their Disability Insurance policies each May with us to determine if their coverage continues to meet the need or if an upgrade or change is in order.
