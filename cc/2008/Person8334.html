Friso Dijkgraaf’s Summary

“Novelty is never innovation if it doesn’t create value (Peter Drucker).”

I'm a business process analyst developing the knowledge management / information structures and main applications for Poste Italiane' s corporate intranet portal serving it's 154.000 employees throughout the country.
P.I. provides besides core postal services, integrated products, as well as communication, logistic and financial services all over Italy.

I analyze internal and external communication, and thus exploit electronic systems to improve communication in a business or an organization.

I gained my B.A at the Academy for Digital Communication of the University for Professional Education in Utrecht- the Netherlands.
In July 2004 I got my master's degree in management and information technologies at the Alma graduate school of communication of the University of Bologna.
