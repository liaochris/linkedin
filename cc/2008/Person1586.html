Andy Zain’s Summary

+ Over 15 years experience in the ICT industry with proven track of record in product/service development & managing operation of tech-based businesses.

+ Pioneer and expert in the field of Internet, Mobile and New Media. Initiated few "industry first" since 1999, including setting up FIRST mobile VAS business, FIRST mobile music business, FIRST WAP portal, FIRST mobile banking in Indonesia, etc.
