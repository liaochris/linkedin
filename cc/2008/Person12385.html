Joseph Pauley’s Summary

Prior to my employment at Luxury Real Estate, I designed internal applications for GMAC Commercial Mortgage. I moved to Seattle three years ago from Philadelphia, PA for a change of scenery and lifestyle. I truly love the temperate climate, mountains, water, and even the rain Seattle has to offer.

I have been working in the Luxury Real Estate Web Department since moving to Seattle. As a technical account manager, I am ready and willing to help you with a variety of web services we offer. If you are interested in a new website, sprucing up your old website, Search Engine Optimization, or have any other technology-related questions, just ask. I am here to help you.
