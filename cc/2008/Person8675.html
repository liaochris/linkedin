    Lisja

    (Non-Profit; Myself Only; Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    June 2007 — Present (1 year 2 months)

    After my work as secretary study group Java and my move to Limburg, I wanted to start a comparable study group in my natal region. After a meeting with 15 persons of enterprises / high schools / university from Belgian or Dutch Limburg on 15th of june 2007, I knew there was a possible platform for this study group. . The group is called Lisja (Limburgse Studiegroep Java). Website is hosted on a server of NLJUG.

    Goal of this study group is to start networking among Limburgish industry and high schools / universities and organise sessions about Java or Java related subjects

  • functional analist

    Antwerpse Waterwerken

    (Public Company; 201-500 employees; Utilities industry)

    January 2000 — May 2006 (6 years 5 months)

    in 1992, when I started to work at AWW, the functional analysis was done in the departments outside ICT. From about 1999, there was a shift to get the functional analysis inside ICT. Some analists kept their work and changed their department. Other analists decided to stay at their department and let the analysis be done by ICT. I got a part of the functional design. Inside ICT a functional cel was started and got at most 3 members. In this period, we were shifting from AS400 and iSeries to Axapta, the ERP from Microsoft. This meant the end for the functional analysis and design and the start of use cases and UML.
