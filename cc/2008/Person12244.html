Joe Hornback’s Summary

I create web and graphic designs that look good and get traffic. These sites are designed to sell the products and services once the potential customer is on the site. This is achieved using color psychology, video, audio and search engine optimization. I don't just put up any site or design. What separates me from other designers is my ability to step back, take a look at the clients' wants and needs, analyze the clients' basic design for their purpose, and then make recommendations so they have the best design possible. I am not afraid to suggest to my client ideas that will improve their website. For Professional Web Design visit www.GeekyBobcatDesign.com.
