Talbott Crowell’s Summary

Solution Architect for enterprise applications with a focus on Microsoft technologies such as Microsoft Office SharePoint Server 2007 (MOSS), BizTalk Server 2006 R2, and the .NET framework, Talbott and his company, ThirdM, deliver solutions and services to large and small companies including Fortune 500. Talbott also assists companies with the development process by using a blend of Agile and traditional methodologies, Team Foundation Server and Visual Studio Team System, Scrum, Test Driven Development, performance testing, and other proven tools and practices.
