Colin Lenton’s Summary

Commercial and documentary photographer with a creative, energetic style. Particularly interested in portrait and location creative work, as well as photojournalism.

Breaking news and events images are represented internationally by World Picture Network, WpN, of NYC.

Creative stock library is represented by Photoshelter Collection.

Skills include transmitting images on tight deadlines from location; multimedia presentations, and standards compliant web development. Adheres to the NPPA code of ethics with regard to all editorial images.

Colin Lenton’s Specialties:

Portraiture, Breaking News, Commercial, Advertising, Real Estate, Adventure Sports.
