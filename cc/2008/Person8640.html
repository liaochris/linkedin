George E. Williams, IV’s Summary

"...a Carnival barker’s megaphone aimed directly at a complacent citizenry.."
-Kevin Moreau, Kingfish Magazine 2001

Despite difficult conditions, the three bloggers behind Humid City managed to produce a series of podcasts in the wake of the hurricane as they became "refugees".
The blog chronicles their decision to leave New Orleans and their struggle to plan for a future after the hurricane.
-BBC.co.uk 2005

"George has made the Radio2020 blog (and by extension the blogging voice of the radio giants [NAB, RAB, HDA]) into something that I feel captures the essence of radio. Namely, something - like George, and like radio - that has real personality."
-Tun3r.com Blog 2008

A social media and blogging specialist operating out of the New Orleans area, I emphasize the use of blogging for community empowerment and ethical non SPAM based promotional efforts. I have had the pleasure of working as a blogger for clients including WWOZ 90.7 fm, the CEO of Kaiser Permanente, the National Association of Broadcasters, Defend New Orleans and others.

I instruct the social media portion of the Online PR Class that is produced by the International Association of Online Communicators and Patron Saint Productions. Under the banner of HumidCity I am also a partner in the Hurricane Digital Memory Bank.
