Georgette Perna’s Summary

Finalist for "Best New Voice" for 2008 Voicey Awards

Full-time commercial and animation voiceover professional in Los Angeles.

Currently co-starring in the animated series "Ring Force Five", anticipated to air on NBC during the 2008 Olympic Games. Also the voice of "Dagny" on a series in development entitled "Magellan's Magic Map", the commercial voice for Jellybean Friends (toys) (now airing on Cartoon Network, Discovery Kids, Toon Disney, Nicktoons, and PBS Kids), and providing character voices for Kidsland.tv and Kidsmania.

Formerly an Attorney Recruiting Consultant for Kass/Abell & Associates. Placed attorneys in Fortune 100 Corporations, and top-tier law firms across the country. Also a former practicing attorney.

From attorney, to attorney recruiter....to funny voices for cartoons. Obvious career changes, I know, but hey, I'm not one for taking chances ;)
