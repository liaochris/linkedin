Shannon Nelson’s Summary

Shannon has always had a passion for writing, beauty and fashion. She is thrilled to be able to combine the three through her blogging and work as a Publicist. It was writing A Girl's Gotta Spa, which she began in 2005, that launched her career. Now she writes Makeup Minute for b5media, is the Chief Blogger for Pierce Mattie PR and is also their Publicist.

When Shannon is not working, you can find her chasing her four sons around whose ages are 9, 6, 3 and 1. Sometimes she is glad to be blessed with the ability to multi-task.
