Jon Varsano’s Summary

Web Retool was founded by Jonathan Varsano as a company that provides marketing services for real estate professionals. Expertise in both the real estate and web development industries is the core value of Web Retool. In cooperation with business partners such as SCOJA & Constant Contact, Web Retool strives to provide the most comprehensive service for real estate agents, brokers, property managers, and developers.
