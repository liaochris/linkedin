David Lorenzo’s Summary

Who else wants to make more money and have more fun doing exactly what they want to do each and every day?

Everyday you wake up and make a conscious decision to go to work. You may not feel like you have a choice – but you do.

How do you feel when you make that decision?

How do your employees feel when they make that decision?

How do you think these feelings will impact your customers, clients or patients?

Answering these three questions is a good start toward building a successful business.

There are two kinds of businesses in this world – FREE Businesses and FAKE Businesses.

A FREE business is:

Fun – You enjoy what you are doing and who you work with.
Rewarding – Your work helps fulfill your personal mission.
Easy – Your business comes naturally to you.
Engaging – Your business magnetically attracts others.

You know your business is FAKE if it is:

Frustrating – You can’t seem to break through to the next level.
Annoying – Going to work bothers you.
Killer – Your business is literally taking years off of your life.
Exhausting - You're tired all the time and you don’t know why.

My mission is to help FAKE business become FREE businesses. My team and I do this by rearranging things that already exist in your work environment.
