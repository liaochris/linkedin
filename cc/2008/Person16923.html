Matthew Digesti’s Summary

HRD advises its clients on a wide variety of business law strategies in both the transactional and litigation context. We combine our experience in business, economics and the law to advise clients on issues including formation, operation, employment, intellectual property, business sales and purchases, partnerships, joint ventures and strategic alliances. HRD is becoming a pioneer in e-commerce legal representation as it continues to draft, negotiate and close such e-commerce partnership and joint venture agreements encompassing merchandising manufacture fulfillment, merchandising distribution fulfillment, online sponsorship, VIP ticketing, and direct social networking between artists, athletes and fans. HRD has worked closely with attorneys and management for entertainers and athletes such as Kobe Bryant, the Game, Back Street Boys, Boys to Men, Paul Oakenfold, Thalia, Third Eye Blind, Eric Byrnes, Chris Daughtry, and T-Pain, to name a few.

In today's digital age, intellectual property protection has become paramount. HRD handles all aspects of intellectual property protection, transaction and litigation. Our clients include established corporations and visionary entrepreneurs in the social networking, e-commerce, web site design, videography, music, athletic, advertising and real estate industries.

HRD leverages its experience in these areas to shoulder the legal aspects of each client's business while freeing valuable time for the client to focus on what it does best--business.

Sasson Management Group is a full-service management agency specializing in sports and entertainment representation. Our agency's roster includes professional athletes, musicians, radio personalities, and reality TV show personalities to name a few. SMG specializes in leveraging each client's media appeal across all media platforms to truly turn the client into a "brand", thus creating new and enhanced revenue streams otherwise unavailable to the client.

Matthew Digesti’s Specialties:

Corporate formation advisement, corporate formation and ongoing operations in the Internet, music, athletic and real estate industries; corporate governing document drafting; e-commerce contracts; social information management; merchandise agreements; intellectual property protection, intellectual property licensing; video and music production and distribution; endorsements; record deal contracts and deal memorandums.
