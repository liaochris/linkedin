Tim Bryce’s Summary

Tim is a management consultant and writer located in the Tampa Bay area of Florida.

He graduated from Ohio University in 1976 with a BSC degree in Communications. As Managing Director, his responsibilities include supporting the company's "PRIDE" methodologies on a worldwide basis.

Tim is the principal author of MBA's "PRIDE"-Enterprise Engineering Methodology and the designer of the Computer Aided Planning tool (a tool for calculating corporate priorities and performing an organization analysis) and Automated Systems Engineering, a tool used to generate system designs. He was also the principal author of the company's Automated Instructional Materials.

A prolific writer, Tim has considerable experience in writing technical documentation, help text, and web design. He has authored several articles on a variety of computer and management related subjects and was the co-author of the book, "The IRM Revolution: Blueprint for the 21st Century", and penned the "PRIDE" Methodologies for IRM eBook. More recently, he published "THE BRYCE IS RIGHT! Empowering Managers in today's Corporate Culture," and "MORPHING INTO THE REAL WORLD - A Handbook for Entering the Work Force" (ISBN: 978-0-9786182-5-4).

In addition to his writings, Tim hosts "Management Visions," a weekly Internet broadcast featuring essays and news pertaining to management issues. The broadcast is listened by I.T. and management professionals from around the world.

Mr. Bryce regularly maintains a blog entitled "Moving IRM from an Art to a Science" which is read by thousands of systems professionals worldwide. In addition, he has over 30 years of experience in writing and producing industry newsletters. He has also made several presentations at computer trade related meetings and has given lectures at various universities.

Tim provides training and consulting services on a global basis. For information or to schedule a presentation, he can be contacted at: timb001@phmainstreet.com
