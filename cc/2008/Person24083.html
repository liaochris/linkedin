Stephanie Agresta’s Summary

Stephanie has been a leader in Internet marketing for over 12 years. She launched her New York City career at iVillage, where she managed relationships with premier online merchants such as Amazon and J.Crew. She later took on a high-profile role managing Barnes & Noble.com’s affiliate program and business development partnerships.

Stephanie’s career is also highlighted by her re-launch of Register.com’s LinkShare program, which resulted in exponential sales growth. Subsequently she served as Director of Business Development for SpaFinder, the spa industry’s leading magazine. Stephanie then expanded into the real estate arena, taking the position of Director of Internet Marketing for Foxtons, a leading international brokerage serving major markets in the U.S. and U.K. She also launched the Affiliate Marketing division of Commerce360, developing and managing all affiliate business for the company.

As a recognized expert on online marketing and web2.0 strategies, Stephanie has sat on numerous industry panels, spoken at trade shows, and been quoted in major publications such as Crain’s Business News, Realtor Magazine, Revenue and ClickZ. A tireless networker and mentor, Stephanie also founded the Jersey Shore Chapter of Webgrrls, an international organization for professional women in new media and technology. She holds a B.A. from Luther College and a Master’s degree from the University of Minnesota.

At the helm of Stephanie Agresta Consulting, Stephanie is advantageously poised to provide her clients with personal attention and unparalleled expertise.
