Derrick Sorles’s Summary

Internet Marketing Coach Helping You And Your Business BE FOUND By The Search Engines - The Future of Doing Business is All About SEARCH!

Is your website a glorified business card? Are you even being "found" by search engines? I'm a Chicago-based consultant (in a virtual world) and new business development-expert, specializing in strategic planning and implementation, around leveraging the endless opportunities to take your business to the next level via Web 2.0. Increase exposure for your company. Establish your company as the "experts". Blogs and the Web 2.0 are the most misunderstood and under-utilized tools out there.

The cheese is being moved ALL THE TIME! We are focused at present on business development strategies, Internet marketing and Web 2.0 strategies for clients utilizing new technologies (blogs, podcast, video podcast) and other social media and new media networks to drive their web traffic, facilitate online communities and conversations and build a "business celebrity" web presence.

We coach clients on understanding the impact of these new technologies to their business and the importance of first mover advantage and SEO discovery over their competitors. Our goal is to get clients to come up on top in an organic search listing for their respective search terms like "GAY TRAVEL" or "CHICAGO FINANCIAL EXPERTS" or "HEALTH WELLNESS EXPERT." We have many clients on Google Page 1 organically (for their search terms), and have demonstrated success stories and case studies available for review. On the Web, it doesn't only matter how good you are at what you do-its how many people know about it and can find you through search.

Seasoned entrepreneur, speaker, trainer.
