Bernadette Boas’s Summary

"Driving Change, Delivering YOU Results"

Bernadette Boas is an experienced and versatile change leader with a passion for people, and for steering determined and committed businesses and individuals to complete business success (improved profitability, productivity, wealth and health) through business planning, strategy definition, team enhancement, organization development, and overall sales and marketing execution.

As President of The Boas Group, a business excellence consulting and coaching firm, she leverages over 20 years of driving change and delivering significant revenue and profitability improvements to clients such as Wal-Mart, Target Corporation, Office Depot, Sears, American Retail Group and more.

Leveraging her years of experience with general business management, retail, global knowledge management, organization development, and solution methodology and processes, Bernadette brings her wealth of international and local experience to benefit the firm’s small and medium size businesses

FocalPoint Coaching of the Boas Group, is the coaching arm, powered by world renowned speaker, author and coach, Brian Tracy, and his Focal Point International business coaching and development organization. This provides the firm the knowledge, expertise, and tools, business leaders need to stay competitive, and take their business to new heights.
