Konstantine Kyros’s Summary

Pioneer in online legal marketing services and digital lawyer marketing. Internet and technology law practice since 1997.

Leading expert in law firm business growth using digital marketing. Directed marketing programs for over 100 law firms throughout the United States. Developed web-based strategies for connecting clients with lawyers. Worked with some of the most prominent plaintiff's law firms in the United States handling pharmaceutical, patent, tax, aviation, insurance and asbestos/mesothelioma litigation matters. Implemented and designed one of the most successful results oriented legal digital marketing programs in the United States.

Generated over $100 million dollars in gross legal fees for my clients (2004-2007) including $29.4 million in 2007 by connecting thousands of consumers with law firms using internet marketing. Managed an over $12 Million online budget in last 4 years with annual online marketing expenditures of over $3 Million (2007). Created internet marketing strategy for the national Law Office of James Sokolove.

Actively partnering with law firms in joint ventures to get new clients in all areas of practice.
