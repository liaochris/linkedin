Jorgen Johansen’s Summary

WHAT I AM DOING
CEO and founder at BlueCon – Management Consulting.
We provide business advice and support to companies through a wide local and global network of +5.000 business advisors and consultants.

CURRENT ACTIVITIES
• Chairman of 5 companies and 2 professional business associations in Denmark and non-executive director, board member or advisory board member of 4 further companies.
• Heading a business consultancy organization in Denmark counting 50 business advisors.
• Heading a public initiative in North Zealand counting 15 experienced business mentors and 55 start-up companies. Aim is to help start-up companies to survive and grow.
• Business Advisor to a number of SME type companies.
• Mentor and business coach to experienced executives across Europe starting their own consultancy business and to a number of company executives and start-up companies.
• Strategist teaming up with other consultants to facilitate strategic processes in major companies.

PAST EXPERIENCE
12 years as CEO/MD in subsidiary companies of well known public groups in Scandinavia serving the high-tech industrial and trading B2B marked.

8 years in R&D.
