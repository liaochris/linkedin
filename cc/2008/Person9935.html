Mark Ingraham’s Summary

Innate skills in art led to a 20+ year career in Chicago Ad Agency
Creative Departments as an Art Director.
Art Directors are advertising-focused 'Designers', that are
trained in the craft of defining a company's strengths in
the competitive marketing arena.
In short, The Art of Selling and Persuasion.
Taking the reins, Mark is now Proprietor of ingraham.creative,
a Design and Art Direction firm specializing in dynamic branding
solutions built on a strong strategic foundation.
The key is to hone in on a company's most unique point of difference,
and then amplify that single-minded message in a different,
relevant, and bold fashion.
Mark's background enables him to apply this focus in all visual mediums;
from web to print to TV to guerrilla tactics.
Mark is working to build ingraham.creative into a known 'go to' shop;
providing aggressive communication for aggressive marketers and advertisers.
