Mario Romero’s Summary

The Romero Team offers the much sought after combination of a strong work ethic coupled with a true passion for real estate. As a licensed real estate professional since 1983, Mario brings the requisite experience and in-depth knowledge to the team. The team consistently delivers the highest level of customer service when representing both sellers and buyers. Mario and his Team have extensive experience in negotiating contracts, marketing listings, showing properties and utilizing the latest technology to truly excel in the real estate field. In addition, The Romero Team is proud to be part of the Melcher Agency, which is one of the oldest and most prestigious agencies in the Phoenix area. The Romero Team specializes in the Phoenix metro areas of Phoenix, Scottsdale, Tempe, Chandler , Paradise Valley, Glendale, Peoria, Estrella Mountain Ranch and Paradise Valley.

The Romero Team is also the relocation expert in the Phoenix area. www.talktomario.com also offers a plethora of resources to anyone thinking of relocating to the Phoenix area. When the time comes for your next move in the Phoenix area, look no further than The Romero Team to help you make your home sale or purchase the smoothest, most successful transaction possible.
