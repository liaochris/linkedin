Olivier Long’s Summary

I am a trial lawyer with 31 years' experience in the state and federal courts of Maryland, Virginia and Washington, DC.

Outside the legal services provided to clients, I am particularly interested in law office technology and have lectured on hardware and software products for the practicing attorney.
