Spot Spot’s Summary

I specialize in the development and implementation logistics of online worlds and social networking properties. Over a decades experience across multiple fields from IT and creative to customer service and advertising management, have provided me with the ability to approach situations from a diversity of angles.

As a founder of deviantART, the world's most popular and largest art related top 100 social network, I have suffered in the trenches of growing an organic community from the seed stage into a global audience of millions. With this comes extensive familiarity with the needs and concerns of scaling technological infrastructure at a dizzying pace to keep up with massive growth rates.

I focus on community development, monetization of verticals, and massive community dynamics, product design, brand management, and networking. Business is an art, and anything you can throw at me, will most likely catch my interest.
