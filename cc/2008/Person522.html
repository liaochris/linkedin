viktor ageyev’s Summary

Viktor Ageyev is a managing partner of Ageyev, Berezhny and partners - an Ukrainian law firm based in Odessa and Kiyv.

We advise on Ukrainian legislation, legal proceedings, administrative proceedings and litigation. Our practice areas are Ukrainian business law, law and practice of international trade, tax planning, law regulations of letter of credit, bill of exchange & promissory note, collections and another payment instruments, law aspects of offshore business, law and Internet & e-commerce, Ukrainian criminal law. We speak English, German, Russian, and Ukrainian.

See our Firm web-site on http://www.ageyev.org
or contact Viktor Ageyev via e-mail: viktor@ageyev.org
