Regina Baker’s Summary

Regina has a background in corporate America in the fields of Information Technology and Procurement as well as a successful career in Network Marketing (featured in The New York Times and reached a *High* Executive Level position in a former company as the 1st African American Female).

More importantly, Regina has a strong entrepreneurial spirit that has led her to more fulfilling roles as a Motivational Speaker, Trainer, Ecommerce Advisor, Producer and Certified Biblical & Business Coach.

Her strong passion for networking – both online and in the offline world – and her love for creating relationships have taken many forms in the years that she has been focused on online businesses.

Regina is Co-Founder of Wahmcart.com a web based ecommerce solution for small businesses, Ecommerce Resident Expert and Founder of ShoppingCartFaq.com, a frequently asked questions website concerning shopping carts, autoresponders and affiliate management systems.

Even through the passing of her husband Ruben to cancer in 2002, Regina has learned to rely and trust in the wisdom and will of God. God’s grace and mercy has allowed her to share with men and women who are going through tough challenges.

Regina is a passionate and engaging speaker and radio show guest. She has traveled various cities and states, coaching and mentoring thousands of men and women on how to achieve anything they want out of life with the right attitude.

Favorite Quote: "Your Life goes in direct porportion of your most dominant throughts ~ As a Man Thinketh, So He Is."
