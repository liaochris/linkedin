Cybergrrl Oh’s Summary

I'm an inworld marketer, community builder, social networker, event planner and host, SL blogger, and founder of the Second Life Entrepreneurs Club, Second Life Marketers Club, Second Life Women's Club, Moms in Second Life and Community Builders Virtual Worlds. I host and co-produce the SLCN.tv show REAL BIZ in SL, featuring real world companies and nonprofit organizations in Second Life. I also consult real world companies and nonprofit organizations about Second Life as a communications, networking, brand-building and marketing tool. In my "spare time," I design slogan and logo tshirts including my own line called Cybergrrl Teez and have an SL tshirt shop called Snark Attack as well as a shop called Athena Wares and the Athena Gallery of Women's Art.
