Jérôme Perani’s Summary

Jérôme Perani is in charge of Media and Internet industries at TERA Consultants, an independent firm providing economic and strategic expert appraisal and consultancy services.

Jérôme joined NDS Group, a News Corporation Company, in February 2006. At NDS Technologies France, he was responsible of strategic marketing of MediaHighway middleware used by more than 50 million Pay TV subscribers. Prior to NDS France, Jérôme was in charge of marketing studies and strategic marketing at Viaccess S.A. for 3 years.

Between 2000 and 2002, Jérôme was Business Development Manager at the Canal+ Group. He developed digital entertainment strategy at European level, and promoted the creation of broadband services, including plans to launch Television and Video On Demand via DSL networks.

Before joining the Canal+ Group, Jérôme was in charge of strategic analysis related to Media and Internet business at France Telecom's Corporate Strategy Department.

Jérôme Perani has a Masters Degree in IT and telecoms from the Ecole Nationale Superieure des Telecommunications Paris, and a PhD in Economics from the University Pantheon-Assas.
