Jen Randolph’s Summary

Talented interaction designer with 5 years' experience in all aspects of web and interactive design and development. Extensive and first-hand knowledge of how people approach the Internet and mobile devices, and a good understanding of how to create experiences that empower them. Emphasis on design and user research, psychological and ethnographic study, usability, and smart typography. Interested in developing creative, sensible, and possibly humorous concepts to help you satisfy the needs of your target audience. Looking for full-time, part-time, or temporary work in the interaction design or information architecture field, probably at a larger design or advertising agency. Also considering small to medium size design and advertising firms, in-house design departments in other companies, and freelance and consulting offers.

Jen Randolph’s Specialties:

Smart and creative, superb speaking and writing skills, HCI, user psychology, design history, usability, accessibility, free thinker, both a leader and a team player, Adobe CS, MS Office, XHTML, CSS, JS, PHP, XML, AS2.0, procedural programming, OOP, MVC, hand-coding, standards-compliance, cross-browser compability, semantic markup, copy editing, web typography
