George Hepworth’s Summary

George specializes in designing and building custom data management systems for small and medium sized businesses.

We build database solutions using MS Access and MS SQL Server.
We analyze organization workflows and design data management processes to support them.

George Hepworth’s Specialties:

I specialize in revising existing databases (primarily table schema) which are not performing as expected. Inappropriate table design is the most common reason "home-grown" databases are inflexible, inefficient and prone to collecting bad data.

Normalization, or the process of converting bad table design to good table design, is one of my key strengths. I build relational models that work now, scale up as your business grows, and offer flexibility as your business model changes.
