John J. Seng’s Summary

A public relations professional since 1979 and founder of Spectrum Science Communications, John leads Spectrum's staff of approximately 50 professionals who work on as many as 150 programs for more than 30 national and international clients.

Based in Washington, D.C., Spectrum ranks among the top three health care-only independent public relations firms in the USA, and is now the number one health care PR office in the Nation's Capital.

John J. Seng’s Specialties:

Integrated communications services for health industry, associations and institutions.
