Melissa Donovan’s Summary

Professional Writing Services by Melissa Donovan

Writing is the single most powerful form of communication. The written word has longevity. It has the capacity to educate, inform, sell, and entertain. Whether destined for internet, print, or digital publication — writing must be clear and compelling. A writer’s job, then, is to produce words that shine.

Do you have time to create truly stellar written content? The words that represent you and your business are critical to success. That’s why hiring a professional writer is in your best interest.

Like any other professional, a writer must invest a great deal of time and energy in order to hone the skills that are required to become an expert in the field. The person who manages your writing should have thorough knowledge of proper grammar, spelling, and punctuation, and a clear understanding of style and voice.

I have a broad range of writing experience, so I can help fulfill all your writing needs, from a business plan to web site copy, and I offer a wide scope of writing services, including:

* Proofreading, editing, and critiques
* Web content
* Copywriting
* Revisions, and rewrites
* Technical and business documentation

In addition, I can provide you with a detailed web site review, which analyzes the design, content, and structure of your site. Please visit my web site www.services.writingforward.com for more information.

Melissa Donovan’s Specialties:

I specialize in writing for the web. In addition, I'm highly focused on proofreading and editing awritten content ranging from creative writing to business and technical documentation.

Web: copy, content, blogs, e-mails, e-books, newsletters, ads, marketing blurbs, product descriptions, help files, and user guides.

Print: brochures, newsletters, fliers, ads, training and workshop materials, how-to guides and instruction booklets.
