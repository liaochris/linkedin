Chris Heidelberg PhD’s Summary

I have approximately 20 years in the entertainment field as a producer, director, host and writer and I have produced more than 200 national pieces, worked as a lead coordinator on numerous marketing and public relations for governmental, corporate and private entities on location and in studio. I have produced films, packages and live events for the White House, The Center for Medicare Services, The National Archives, The Department of Health and Human Services, and other government organizations. I have also conducted live presentations at events as both the speaker and the event host for several events for corporate, non-profit, educational and government organizations in Washington, Los Angeles, San Antonio, Atlanta, Miami, Seattle, and Chicago. I also procured government services and managed all of my projects as a federal project officer. Recently, I have been creating podcasts, blogs, video sharing sites and other new media entities for governmental, educational, small business and non-profit groups.
