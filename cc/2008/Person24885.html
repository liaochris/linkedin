Randall F. "Randy" Chambers’s Summary

Search firm specializing in senior engineering and executive management for telecom, construction and mining since 1994.

Our reputation was earned on our ability to accuately match our candidates with client job requisitions. The Telecom Search Group (TSG) has over fourteen years of RF, wireless, telecom and public safety recruiting experience. Moreover, the President of TSG, Randall F. “Randy” Chambers, has over thirty-five years of direct executive management experience with wireless carriers, telephone interconnects, real estate and construction industries.

Our clients range from nationwide Fortune 1000 companies to well-funded start-ups with staffing requirements ranging from senior engineers to mid-level and senior management positions. Whether you have a difficult job-order to fill or need a high-profile confidential search, we can help you find the right candidate for the task-at-hand at a cost minimally more than an in-house search, but with substantially more convenience. Additionally, we work on retainer or contingency depending on the attention needed to perform a search and the difficulty that might be incurred in an assignment. Consequently, there is never a fee to the candidate for our services.
