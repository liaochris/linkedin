Olivier Tupet-Menguy - [LION]’s Summary

After studying law I entered the French Navy in 1997.
As a sub lieutenant, I was appointed in 1998 Principal Private Secretary to the General Commissioner for the French Pavillion of the International Exhibition of Lisbon. I then joined the French Naval Personnel Directorate as a Public Affairs Officer from 1998 to 2000.
In 2000, I became Flag Lieutenant of the Flag Officer Submarines.
I served aboard the FFG Primauguet in 2003 during Operation Enduring Freedom as Public Relations Officer and then became PAO of the Strategic Ocean Force in Brest.
Promoted to the rank of Lieutenant in 2004, I was appointed Executive Officer at the Naval Station of Le Havre. From 2005 to 2008, I wasDuty Officer of the Ministry Of Defence. Since 2008, I am Responsible for Nuclear studies at MOD - Public Relations Department
