Andy Getsey’s Summary

Communications strategy collaborator with CEOs and senior executives of high profile technology companies.

Recent/current clients include Chris Alden of Six Apart, Rich Garwood of Verizon, Michael Birch and Joanna Shields of Bebo/AOL, Ken Oshman of Echelon, Tom Chavez of Rapt, Reid Hoffman of LinkedIn, Aaron Patzer of Mint.com, Clifford Boro of Kidzui, Peter Hoddie of Kinoma, Jeff Smith and Ge Wang of Smule, Robbie Vann-Adibe' of Method, Tom Baruch and Jim Watson of CMEA Ventures and others.

Additional key client work includes/has included Photobucket, BitTorrent, IMAX, Echelon, Sigma Designs, Callidus, Ingres, Vontu, Outerbay, Sanera, Oracle, Fujitsu, Hitachi 9Squared and others. Nearly two dozen clients have been acquired by major brands in past three years.

Originator of Atomic ComContext™, an on–demand analytics application for communications research, PR planning and program measurement.

Co-founder and managing director of Theorem Communications, a small private holding company whose agencies include Atomic Public Relations, Civilia Public Relations and Random Animal (a mutant digital communications firm to be released into the wild shortly).

Previously president of Shafer, an independent technology marketing agency with clients ranging from Oracle and Quantum to Toshiba and Earthlink. Before that, head of global communications for digital graphics company CalComp Lockheed–Martin. Started at database marketing agency Krupp/Taylor Foote Cone Belding with technology and consumer clients from Apple to United Airlines.

Bachelor's degree in psychology and an MBA from California State University.

Personal profile at Facebook.com
