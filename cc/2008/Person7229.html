Cindy Emch’s Summary

In both my personal and professional life I have a long standing love of film. Having studied film, media trends, viral marketing, and the intersecting lines of pop culture across media platforms since 1992 - I have an integrated view of the types of media and content that can appeal to a wide range of demographics.

Cindy Emch’s Specialties:

I work online promoting, sourcing, discovering and spreading the gospel of film, social networks and community. I have a keen eye for hot viral and traditional content and have a historical knowledge of pop culture and how those past influences feed into future trends.
