Janis Pettit’s Summary

Twenty years of experience as an entrepreneur owning four highly successful businesses--10 years as a small business consultant and coach. Extensive experience as a motivational speaker. Articles published locally and internationally.
Hosted a business cable talk show in NY and was named to "50 Women of Achievement under 50".

I focus on the key challenges that small business owners need to overcome to maximize their business profits and reach their life goals. Focus areas include marketing, systems development, sales, business strategy and planning and achieving independence and life balance.

Services include:
- small business growth and marketing consulting
- CEO coaching
- Online marketing training and web design
- Tele-seminars
- Group coaching
- Live presentations and keynotes
- E-courses and E-books
- Business brokering and staging (buy and sell) with our partner, Enlign Business Brokers

o Our corporate resource center is a partnership of some of the best trainers around. We cover a number of key topics including sales, leadership and team building.

o My goal and passion is to see small business owners and corporate professionals reach their performance potential and career goals. Our approach is cutting edge and innovative in that we always combine the teaching and mastery of "outer" skills with "inner" focus or self-awareness skills.

Janis Pettit’s Specialties:

Ways to maximize profits, marketing strategy/planning and implementation, growth business model and systems, sales strategy and skills training, entrepreneurial skills, business planning, expansion strategy, business buying and selling. Our hundreds of clients have experienced significant business growth.

Our niche - small service business owners, CEO's and independent service professionals.
