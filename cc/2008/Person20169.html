Peter Kima’s Summary

Peter is part of a very exclusive small group of Real Estate agents in Raleigh...he is not only an agent he is also an Accredited Staging Professional. This means that you have access to information that most agents tell you they have, Peter truly has the knowledge and desire to stage your home for SUCCESS!

When it comes to providing the utmost in personal service to his clients, Peter Kima is on the cutting edge. His demanding work ethic doesn't allow him to rest until he's done everything in his power to help achieve your goals. And as a husband and father, his understanding the importance of a family's home is reflected in his every move.

When Peter is not helping one of his clients he can be found volunteering his time running a charity Lobster Bingo event, cooking burgers at the fire station to benefit children, swinging a hammer for Habitat For Humanity, or ringing the bell for The Salvation Army. His favorite pastime however, is spending time with his wife Jennifer and their two sons, 3 year old Cole and newborn Camden.

Peter has resided in Germany, California, Georgia, Indiana, Massachusetts, North Carolina, Oregon, and Vermont. Having lived in North Carolina for 10 years, Peter definitely refers to North Carolina as home.

Don't underestimate the value of Peter's moving experience. He and his team understand the rigors involved in making a family move. Rely on his experience to help you.

When the time comes for your next move in and around the Triangle, don't settle for ordinary! COUNT on Peter Kima for all your real estate needs. He has A High Energy Approach to Real Estate that will make your move a rousing success. "Personal Service. One Family at a Time!"


So when you decide that you'd like to buy or sell a home in the Raleigh area, please contact Peter.
