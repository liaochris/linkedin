Jason Fincanon’s Summary

I graduated from The Art Institute Of Dallas (major in Computer Animation) in 1998. I've been working with Flash for the past ten years, in the advertising industry for the last four, and I recently finished writing a book called "Hands-On Guide To Creating Flash Advertising" (http://www.flashadbook.com). I am definitely more of a Flash Developer than a Flash Designer and the main software I work with are Flash, Flex, Photoshop, Illustrator, and the rest of the usual suspects that fall in that same range. In addition to those, I also do a little PHP work in my own time.
