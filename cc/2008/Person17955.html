Mike Schinkel’s Summary

Mike is the web marketing/technology guru extraordinaire. Unlike most web "specialists" today Mike's broad and varied background spans successful business and seasoned web technologist, database, development implementation and training.

BUSINESS/WEB MARKETING
Mike's expertise include monetization strategies. leveraging the latest effective techniques without succumbing to being a "me-to" trend follower, and effective website architecture-based SEO strategies/

WEB TECHNOLOGY/DEVELOPMENT
Mike's experience include a specialty in the Drupal CMS as well as MySQL, Apache, PHP, HTML, CSS, HTTP, REST APIs, AJAX, jQuery, RSS, Flash, Flex, FlowPlayer, XML, JSON, URL and Database Design, WordPress, and the OpenX Ad Server. Mike uses video and photos, email marketing, social media for SEO, user-generated content, and web analytics to track it all.

NETWORKING/MATURITY
Mike combined marketing and tech skills give him expertise that most "web gurus" simply don't have. And his highly successful Atlanta Web Entrepreneurs, for people interested in starting, funding, running, growing, and eventually exiting web-based businesses with over 15 successful programs and 750 members has given him insight and connections like no-one else in Atlanta.

PAST EXPERIENCE
From 1994 to 2006 Mike founded and grew Xtras, a company ranked #123 on the 1999 Inc 500. That experience gave Mike a unique combination of entrepreneurial business, sales, marketing, and ecommerce skills few others can claim. Recently Mike lead ATV Illustrated's web from worst to first where they now top the search ranks for most of the long tail results.

SUMMARY
Mike's understands not only "how" but more importantly "why." If you need web strategy expertise, be it marketing or tech, Mike is one you want. Mike can train your team and evaluate and recommend your strategy and architecture for your website, both before you build it and when you take it to the next level.
