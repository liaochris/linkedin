Sean Nelson’s Summary

Hello, my name is Sean and the two most important things I can tell you are:

1. How I can help you; and
2. How you can help me

How I can Help You:
For the past 7 years I have helped thousands of individuals, self-employed folks and small businesses save 20 to 50% on their health insurance. Who would like to save even just 20% from their current plan (raise your hand!)?

There's no secret to what I do and no super health plan that works for everyone. I simply ask the right questions to determine what you are looking for and then I research all of the available companies to see who has the best fit and price.

Just as important as finding the best price, is explaining the differences in coverage in a way that the average person can understand. Then each year at renewal we do it all over.

How You Can Help Me:
Simply give me an opportunity to be of service to you, your family and friends and your clients. I'm currently the Highest Rated insurance professional in Atlanta on LinkedIn. Take a look at what my clients have to say on the Recommendations page.

That's as simple and straight forward as I can be. It's how I work with my client's.

BONUS: I also write the Linked 101 blog which can be read at www.Linked101.com/linked101blog.

Warm Regards,

Sean Nelson
