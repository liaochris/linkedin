Michel Lent Schwartzman’s Summary

Michel Lent Schwartzman, founder and CCO of 10'Minutos S.A., was born in Caliornia, USA, and raised in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. A Graphic Designer holding a MA degree in Interactive Communications from the New York University, started his career in 1994 in New York at EURO/RSCG. In 2000 joined DDB Brazil as Creative Director before starting his own agency, 10'Minutos, in 2002. His company was shortly acknowledged by the industry as one of the leading interactive agencies in the country. Michel has rendered digital marketing services for world class clients such as IBM, Shell, 20th Century Fox, BMG, Warner Music, International Paper, ABM AMRO Bank, Philips, Siemens, Honda, Santander and Coca-Cola.

In the course of over a decade Michel has lectured at universities and other institutions all over Brazil and abroad, developed innumerous digital marketing campaigns, structured and managed departments, teams and processes being awarded in key international festivals such as Cannes, Clio, Art Directors, One Show, The New York Festivals, and London Festivals.
