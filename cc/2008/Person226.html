Abhay Singhal’s Summary

MBA with proven leadership skills, strong solutions management and enterprise architecture skills with over 10 years of professional experience.

Currently working as Solutions Lead at MDM Center of Expertise at Teradata. Work involves leading team of solution architects, interfacing with sales, marketing and customer account teams to position MDM solutions and to manage cross functional teams in USA and India to develop and deliver solution to customers. Also working with key accounts to position, define, develop and strategize enterprise data management solution including project and infrastructure planning. Involved in both pre-sales (positioning, sow creation, project planning, resource planning) and post-sales (architecture and planning).

In my prior position, worked as solution and product manager for i2 Master Data Management to work with many hi-tech, telecom, consumer electronics and retail customers in USA and abroad.
