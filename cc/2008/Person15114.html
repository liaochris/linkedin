Kyle Cooney’s Summary

Interaction design professional focused on the intersection of online services and social software for high traffic consumer oriented sites and services.

Particular interest in democratizing the design process through agile practices to constantly include feedback from all stakeholders -- from development teams, to marketing, legal, privacy, advertising, through to product management and most important of all, our customers, all the while building consensus on optimal user experiences.

Kyle Cooney’s Specialties:

Interaction and experience design, information architecture, designing search and social software experiences, content management architecture and design, SEO, standards based front-end design (CSS/JavaScript).

Use of common wireframing and diagramming tools such as Illustrator, Visio and Omnigraffle.
