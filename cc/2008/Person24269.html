Steve Williams’s Summary

An experienced MMO Designer with a passion for social play, economics, exploration, and a solid background in system design. My favorite part of the design discipline is bringing the player into friction with the game world - creating situations in which the game and player come into contact in a meaningful way. My experience includes designing gameplay systems for MMOs, writing lore and backstory for MMOs, and bringing to life NPCs, quests, and dungeons.

Steve Williams’s Specialties:

System design and/or content implementation of all sorts - I emphasize my experience designing social and economic dynamics, alternative gameplay styles for MMOs, and writing backstory/fiction/lore for MMOs as my particular talents.
