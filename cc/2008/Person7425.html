Eric Pratum’s Summary

Recently, I worked as a researcher for an annual real estate report and as a research assistant for a center that coordinates educational visits by executives. Also, I worked as a researcher for an executive search firm that focuses on world-changing technology firms, as a screening coordinator for an Angel Group, and as a sales developer for an online sports retailer. In a previous life, I worked in sales for one of the most prominent athletic shoe and apparel manufacturers in the world, and I was a first-year German instructor at one of the largest universities in the United States.

My areas of expertise are market research, business development, and financial analysis, and most of my colleagues would say that I'm a "people person."

Outside of my professional life, I run, bike, swim, and hike as well as lift weights. I love to play disc golf, have founded and currently manage a band, and established my university's triathlon team.

I hold a BA and MA in German, speak decent French, am competent in Norwegian and Swedish, and am familiar with Spanish and Portuguese. At one point in my life, I worked on a goat farm, played for the Swedish Olympic baseball team, and worked at a carnival.
