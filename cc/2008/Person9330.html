Paul Chambers’s Summary

Award-winning, formally trained Interactive Creative Director with over ten years experience conceiving and articulating pure digital campaigns and interactive experiences that extend campaigns originating in traditional media.

Acted as brand steward for a number of high profile clients over the years, with particular specialty in pitching new business.

Paul’s work has appeared in (among others) AdWeek, Advertising Age, The New York Times, The Wall Street Journal and Creativity magazine.

Paul is a proud member of the New York chapter of the AIGA.
