Shawn Graham’s Summary

I hold a PhD from the University of Reading in Archaeology, and a Post-Graduate Certification in Learning and Teaching in Higher Education from the University of Roehampton. I am a Registered Professional Archaeologist, and a Member of the Institute of Field Archaeologists.

Throughout my career, I have worked as a cultural resource management specialist, as a university lecturer, in adult education, continuing education, distance education, online education, and special-needs at the secondary school level. I have a demonstrated aptitude for learning new software products quickly; my blog on new media for research and learning currently has a Technorati 'Authority' ranking of 45.

Hi-lights of my community volunteering and service to the academy include:

-MRC Pontiac Heritage Advisory Committee
-Director on the Board of the Friends of Gatineau Park
-Chair of the Social and Cultural Heritage Committee, Friends of Gatineau Park
-Member of the Public Education Committee, Canadian Archaeological Association
-Assistant Editor, Robert Welch University Classical Press
-Member of the Committee, Past Discussed (Quarterly)
