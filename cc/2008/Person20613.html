Preston True’s Summary

Accomplishment Coaching is a firm that provides personal and professional guidance, training and tools which challenge you and your organization to re-think what is possible in your life or business, and to gain access to your personal best. This may be in any area including relationships, business growth and development, personal dreams, career, or overall life questions.

Certainly, you have done your best with what you already know. You may have even consulted with professionals, read books, attended seminars, and/or attended classes to get the right advice, perspective or information. The outcome, though incrementally better, is not always satisfying and is rarely permanent.

A coaching engagement with Accomplishment Coaching is designed to break through what is normal or predictable for you. Coaching provides access to that which is unpredictable, unknown and discontinuous with your past experiences. Like an Olympic champion, you have a coach to reach what seems unattainable regardless of the past.

Indeed, breakthrough results do not come from simply trying harder or being in the right place at the right time. Personal excellence comes from commitment, and being in action with your coach as a guide. You speak, listen, do the training and get the results. You are accomplishing from a new awareness in your life. No longer confined to reacting, or acting from the past, you are able to create a powerful future.

We work with people and organizations who are interested in a once and for all shift in a particular area of their life or business. The shift is permanent, and alters who you are now and in the future. You are able to see, and to take action from, a new and more powerful place in your life. You are able to attain, have and keep what you really want.

Accomplishment Coaching provides you with the tools and support to have a life or organization by design…your design.
