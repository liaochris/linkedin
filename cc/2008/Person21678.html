Robin Palmer’s Summary

Robin B. Palmer is the President and Co-Founder of My Successables, Inc., which is a privately held company with a product base of several inventions. We create products that help people to successfully achieve their goals and dreams.

Robin performed in Broadway musicals for fifteen years. Some favorites include playing Linda Ronstadt’s little sister in “The Pirates of Penzance” with Kevin Kline, co-starring opposite David Cassidy in “Joseph and the Amazing Technicolor Dreamcoat,” and singing “Memory” in “CATS."

Robin Palmer’s Specialties:

I have invented and patented "My Wake Up Call," which is a unique motivational alarm clock! Please visit our website at www.mywakeupcalls.net.
