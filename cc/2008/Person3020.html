Bob Hafer’s Summary

Robert E. Hafer’s 36 years as a leader in the new home building industry have given him an unparalleled insight into the mysteries and marvels of successful new home sales. His work embraces progressive success in consulting, management, administration, sales, marketing, merchandising, research and sales training.

As a top executive with some of America’s leading home builders, he has recruited, trained and mentored some of the top sales executives in the nation. A long-time member of the National Association of Home Builders, he holds NAHB’s prestigious designations as a Member of the Institute of Residential Marketing (MIRM) and Certified Sales Professional (CSP). He is a recipient of the NAHB Sales and Marketing Council Silver and Gold Award and was chairman of the Sales and Marketing Council Board of the Home Builders Association of Greater Dallas.

Bob Hafer’s Specialties:

Offer complete marketing and sales support for new home builders and Realtors, from original marketing plan to the final close-out sales. Conducting sales training and coaching for new and experienced salespeople. Publication and release of Building Results – The Ultimate Guide for New Home Sales. A second book; Building Results – 101 Exercises to Successis scheduled for release in 4th quarter, 2008.
