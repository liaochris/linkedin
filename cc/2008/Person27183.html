Will Handley’s Summary

Progressive Inspection Service was founded in 1985 to provide independent certified home inspection and real estate inspection services of residential, commercial, industrial and/or resort properties for private consumers, construction, development, banking, lending, insurance, litigation and or government entities. I've completed over 14,500 real property inspections over the past twenty two years. Inspections are available seven days per week throughout California, including Los Angeles, Orange, Ventura, San Bernadino, Riverside, Imperial, Kern and San Diego Counties, while offering larger commercial and resort property inspections throughout the United States and Mexico.
