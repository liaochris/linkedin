David Biernbaum’s Summary

CONSUMER PACKAGED GOODS:

http://biernbaum.com/

David Biernbaum:

Consumer packaged goods specialist in the field of marketing, retail sales, broker management, and business development, with a far-reaching background and resilient history developing major national brands, niche items, licensed goods, and premium store brands. History of building success, equity, and rapid development of entrepreneurial brands and small businesses in niche consumer goods.

 Leadership was instrumental to help develop Zooth Inc., during its most propelling growth years, leading to the profitable sale of the company to Gillette and later, Procter and Gamble.

 Helped develop Vi-Jon Laboratories to become a major force in personal care products, after developing sales forces, high-impact marketing, and record breaking sales at the Gillette Co., (Oral-B) and Smith-Kline Beecham.

 Led the initial product launch of Aquafresh.

 Presently helping Dr. Harold Katz, LLC (TheraBreath) and other growing companies expand to new levels of success and achievement.

 Recently became associated with UpSpring Baby, a company dedicated to creating innovative consumer goods that make the world a safer better place for parents.

 Frequent columnist, panelist, and contributor for trade shows, instructional workshops, and panels, and has written several columns for trade magazines such as MMR, Chain Drug Review, and Private Label Buyer.

 Engages in frequent teleconferences and small board room meetings to help companies with trades show strategies, marketing, and sales leadership.
