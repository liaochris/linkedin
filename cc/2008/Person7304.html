Bonnie (Bo) Dash’s Summary

I provide Technical Editing and other document QC services to Environmental, Geoscience, Bioscience, Engineering, Transportation, and Health & Safety consultants. My firm is a DBE-certified, woman-owned business located in the Bay-Delta area of California. I serve clients in the greater SF Bay Area & Sacramento/Stockton metro areas, as well as throughout California and the U.S.

I am also interested in expanding my scope of editing services (to any field except high-tech). I am particularly enthused about editing text for radio, as I worked in broadcasting during the early to mid 1980s and have maintained an interest since that time.

Bonnie (Bo) Dash’s Specialties:

Technical editing, QC, and production of all type of environmental-related documents: soil/groundwater monitoring reports, Phase I & II ESAs, tank monitoring/removal plans, site remediation and closure reports, biological assessments, habitat conservation & restoration plans, geotech reports, work plans, implementation plans, SPCCs, EIRs/EISs, transportation studies and plans, HASPs, IIPPs, proposals, etc. Also have experience with radio news writing/editing.
