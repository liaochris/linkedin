Patti Anklam’s Summary

I've worked with networks all my life. I help companies understand their social networks, how to select, introduce, and manage social software to enhance people-to-people connections.

An experienced practitioner who held leadership positions in large computer and telecommunications companies, currently assist clients from a range of industries in understanding the context of information and knowledge flow in organizations and in developing strategies to leverage existing infrastructure and social networks.

Net Work: A Practical Guide to Creating and Sustaining Networks at Work and in the World, was published by Elsevier/Butterworth-Heinemann in April, 2007.

Patti Anklam’s Specialties:

Social software and collaboration -- infrastructure assessment, design and tool introduction, social network analysis, communities of practice, contextual design, work practice modeling, organization development
