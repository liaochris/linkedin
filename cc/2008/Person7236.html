Emiliano Cappai’s Summary

Emiliano Cappai finally founded in 2008 its own Search Engine & On line Marketing Agency, Mamadigital.com, together with his lifelong colleague Andrea Rogani and some of the best professionals in the Internet field.

For 6 long years, since 2002 to the end of 2007, Emiliano built his skils in Ad Maiora, as Resellers and International Partnerships Division Manager.

Expert and passionate about Search Engine Marketing, he coordinated strategic projects for Lufthansa, Sun Microsystem, Fiat Group, Citibank, Ferrero, Unicredit and many other top European brands.

In 2004, Emiliano led Ad Maiora into the constitution of e-SEMA, the Alliance of Leading European SEM agencies, aiming to expand opportunities in International markets.

Since 2006, as International Business Development Manager, was also in charge of Ad Maiora's relationships with partners in the United States, with the objective to build solid business relationships with the other side of the Atlantic.

Dedicated to the Internet since 1995, Emiliano started his professional career in 2000 between Milan and London, with the start-up of the Italian branch of QXL plc (www.qxl.com), Pan-European site of online auctions.

Journalist publicist, graduated in Business Communication with a Master in Web Content Management, Emiliano is the proud founder of Travelmama.it (www.travelmama.it), the community of alternative travelers.
