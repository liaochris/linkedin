Andrey Golub’s Summary

Mr. Andrey Golub brings 10 years of experience in IT/ Software Development, and 5+ in TLC/ R&D/ high-tech and Internet sectors. Scientific Research activity in the field of AI after successfully completed PhD studies on Systems Analysis and previously earned bachelor's diploma (with honors) in Applied Mathematics.

Broad experience with Web 2.0/ Social Media and Community projects, deep understanding and a good vision for Social Networking and Social Computing scene. After few years of active studies of what the others have done, he is now Social Media Researcher and Social Networks Manager for some upward-looking Open-Source and Open-Community projects.

Andrey is co-founder and resp. for IT/ Web/ Networking of a Milan-based Business Networking Club "Milan-IN", a LinkedIn base in Italy, which is a Business Community founded by a group of passionate Networkers, that after 2 years of successful growth presented the Club to LinkedIn and became recognized as their Official Supporter Club.

Andrey Golub has been assigned in 2007/2008 such important recognitions as "2000 Outstanding Intellectuals of the 21st Century", "Leading Scientists of the World" from IBC, and "Who's Who in the World" from Marquis Who's Who.
