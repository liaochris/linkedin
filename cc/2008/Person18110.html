Mitchell Ozog’s Summary

Mitch have extensive experience and he is educated and influenced an entire generation of computer users in areas of graphic design, the Mac, PC and web. He has used the Mac and PC platform since 1990 and continues to follow a career of digital creativity. Mitch as an evangelist for the adoption of new technologies and strategies to helping and developing new graphics for print and web site. Having worked as a Graphic/Web Designer and Electronic Desktop Publisher. Mitch skilled in a broad range of media arts. He have 20 years + experience as creative artist and 10 years experience in the field of graphic design developing print, photographic, and various multimedia and web based projects.
Mitch work at the Bonisteel Masonic Library as member of BML Board - VP/Editor-in-Chief producing The Rising Point International Masonic Review. His work includes editing content and page design, laying out text for a newsletter and BML web site, www.bonisteelml.org.

THE RISING POINT
Is a quarterly publication of Bonisteel Masonic Library. The primary objective of Rising Point is to provide access information of previously published Masonic research works. The publications of Rising Point are dedicated to Freemasonry information and education and are available in electronic vision as PDF files at no cost. Hard copies are available by request.

He is member of the Masonic fraternity - 32nd Degree Scottish Rite Freemason and Master Mason AAF 262 in Ann Arbor, Michigan. For more info you can visit Bonisteel Masonic Library website at - http://www.bonisteelml.org
