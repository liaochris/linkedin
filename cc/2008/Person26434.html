Valery Hronusov’s Summary

I have been graduated from Politechnical institute, my M.S. specialization is machine's dynamics and durability. After graduation I followed to work on the chair of mechanics of composite material. Specialization: durability calculations. FEM. Stochastic processes. Mathematical modeling.

Since 1992 to 1996 I was working at 2-3 works at the same time – undermined.

1. Geomechanics (I have defended the PhD dissertation)

2. Cryogenic technology

3. Elaboration software. (Both individual and in a team). Directions - durability calculations, ensuring systems data acceptance and calculations (power engineering), accounting. First realtime software development, work with controllers. FEM, work with relation databases, elaboration interfaces, low-level programming, visualization.

Since 1996 to 2000 I was working in the scientific part of projects institute. Specialization – geomechanics calculations. CAD systems.

2000 – Begging of the work with GIS. Leader of contractual works. Client-server applications (MS SQL+Access+Visio) for geological service adventures – these programs are really working nowadays. Another specialization – ArcGIS. Numerical micrographics, morphometry analysis of elements. The first group was created.

2004. Academic institute of Mines. Visualization of geodates, GIS researches. Leading elaboration software major components. Lead a site, work with its design, and go on interesting with applied mathematics. Nowadays I do not write program code. The main part of my work is target settings, testing and deliberation of targets, which are curetted with these programs. I try to achieve optimal combination between result and labors-output ratio.

Experience jobs with space photos of Landsat with SRTM, Digital globe ( I before buy and use data of Quick Bird satellite for Oil&Gas deposits research).

Experience of jobs with datamining. We create and support now the large geodatabase.
