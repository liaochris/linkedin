Teri G. Rasmussen’s Summary

I have helped guide clients in making legally informed business decisions for more than twenty years. To learn more about my approach with clients, visit my Ohio Practical Business Law Counsel blog and click "About This Blog and my Approach to Lawyering".

As an attorney in the Central Ohio office of Plunkett Cooney , I focus on enhancing the law firm’s capacity to handle bankruptcy, workouts, and other creditors' rights matters for lenders and other creditors. In addition, my experience strengthens Plunkett Cooney's ability to provide legal services to Ohio clients in the transactional business, corporate, commercial, contract, and real estate law practice areas..

I concentrate my personal law practice on matters such as
Creditors’ Rights
Business Insolvency, Turnarounds/Workouts, and Bankruptcy
Loan Documentation, Structuring and Negotiation
Commercial Collections
Business Acquisitions, Mergers, and Sales
Business Formation
Debt and Equity Financing
Contract Review, negotiation, and preparation
Commercial Litigation
Real Estate Purchase/Leasing

Teri G. Rasmussen’s Specialties:

Chapter 11 Bankruptcy and Workouts Creditor Representation; Relations among business owners; purchase or sale of business
