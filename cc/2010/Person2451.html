Benjamin Lukoff’s Summary

I was at Amazon.com for over five years, working my way from proofreader to copyeditor to editor, ending up in the music department. Before that, I spent time at Microsoft as an indexer and proofreader on Encarta Encyclopedia. My latest position at Microsoft was Zune.net site editor. I am currently engaged in freelance writing and editing but am certainly interested in more permanent work.

Experienced user of Microsoft Office, Photoshop, and ProTools and Windows, Mac OS, and Unix operating systems; over 16 years of Internet experience; good working knowledge of HTML/XML; familiar with Chicago Manual of Style and other general editorial conventions

Benjamin Lukoff’s Specialties:

Writing, copyediting, editing, indexing, blogging, wikis, project management, freelancer management
