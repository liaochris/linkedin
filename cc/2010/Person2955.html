Candy Beauchamp’s Summary

My name is Candy Beauchamp and I own and operate OffAssist, a virtual bookkeeping service located in Austin, Texas. We work with companies all over the United States free them up from the task of bookkeeping to do what they do best.

Why virtual? Not only can I work with companies I want to from all across the United States, but I can also choose my team members based on their work and not their location.

We have our own servers and other technologies that allow us to work with clients seamlessly and we wouldn't have it any other way. Email me for details, you'll be amazed at how much your life can be enriched by just getting that bookkeeping gorilla off of your back.

To find out more about the services I offer and what I can do to help you, please visit my website at http://www.offassist.com.
