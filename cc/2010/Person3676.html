Stacy Lukasavitz’s Summary

Stacy has a diverse background in writing, internet technologies, music publicity, marketing, PR, nonprofits, chambers of commerce, water skiing, and coffee. She was blogging back in the late 1990s when it was “online journaling,” way before the term “blog” was coined. A natural early adopter, she joined Twitter in April of 2007 and is amazed at how mainstream it has become. Her writing occasionally makes an appearance at Social Media Today and other random places around the internet. Unsurprisingly, it frequently graces sticky notes everywhere she goes. You can read her semi-regularly at http://www.thatdamnredhead.net.

Becoming frustrated and impatient after graduating college with her organizational communication degree and unable to find work, she went into business for herself as principal of TDR Communications, LLC, a public relations/social media consultancy that specialized in nonprofits and small businesses, relying heavily on her experience growing up involved in chambers of commerce. After a strange twist of events, she accepted a position with the Detroit Regional Chamber in August 2009 as their Interactive Marketing Coordinator, where she remained until December 2009. In the meantime, she maintains TDR Communications as her freelance business, now specializing clients in the music business and specialty clients from Africa.
