Francisco Jose Palacio’s Summary

CTO/Founder
Media Mentoring Societies (MMS)

MMS Membership enables current and future entrepreneurs and business owners to expand and leverage their footprint onto the newest media technologies, whether for themselves and their existing businesses or, if they see fit, to provide such high-end services for others.

At MMS, you can actually see, participate, interact and create most of what we're talking and discussing with strategic partners and amongst ourselves towards building/creating/establishing a valid and authoritative "Certification Path" in Media Technologies (sm)

Levels include Professional Media Specialist (sm) and Professional Media Mentors (sm) Certifications

Certifications for Specialists require both approved and standardized academic work at local institutions; as well as the completion of a Pragmatic Leadership (sm) and Networking Activism (sm) Practicum

Certifications for Mentors include above as well as a series of Business Development Practicum meant to not only monetize their efforts, but to build a Case Study archive that can not only be shared and reference by Members, but also serve as a repository of Business Ideas that members can cross reference and build together upon in myriad localities.

All of the above do imply a Business/Commercial interest, yet, tied to Giving Back to the Community, Volunteering, being an Activist Networker, and overall, really exploting the so-called "Social" aspects of this new media spectrum - and how it can also be tied back to many existing disciplines as they extend themselves into these spheres.

Please stop by and chime in, as we also have "Crowd Sourcing" (a.k.a. "Wisdom of The Masses") as yet another cornerstone of our Foundational Principles.
