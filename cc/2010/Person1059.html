Doug Lewis’s Summary

I specialize in management consulting in the healthcare, business, and legal sectors. I have a very diversified 30 plus year background in healthcare in a variety of clinical, administrative, and legal/compliance levels as well as experience in the hospitality and entertainment industries. I routinely speak and present at many conventions and professional and community meetings and groups. I also practice law, clinical audiology, insurance, and currently teach for six universities along with membership in over 35 professional associations and sitting on several boards. I also enjoy entrepreneurial activities and in the creation of new business ventures. I enjoy continuing my music endeavors and routinely sitting in with musical groups and performers playing a variety of instruments.
