Daniela Araujo’s Summary

Daniela Araujo has over 5 years of experience in the online marketing industry. She worked in the account manager team at ValueClick media, managing and optimizing display ads through ad serving technologies. She also worked as a Paid Search Account Manager at LessonPlanet.com. Daniela most recently worked at EngineWorks Inc as a Paid Search Account Manager and Strategist. Currently, she works as an independent consultant and editor at TMMPDX helping businesses promote their websites through paid and organic search marketing. Daniela’s online marketing experience covers a broad spectrum of businesses: from education and technology to tourism, real estate, automotive, and e-commerce. She loves to work with online marketing precisely because it is thoroughly modern, measurable, and has the capacity to reach worldwide audiences.

Daniela Araujo’s Specialties:

Paid Search Marketing and Search Engine Optimization for English and Brazilian-Portuguese Websites.

Google Adwords PPC campaign creation and optimization; search query management. Google Adwords content network direct response and branding campaign creation and optimization.
