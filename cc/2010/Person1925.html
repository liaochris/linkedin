Jerry Won’s Summary

Jerry was born in Seoul, Korea and immigrated to California at age 9. After junior high school, he moved to New York City, where he graduated from the prestigious Bronx High School of Science in 3 years.

While attending USC through the Resident Honors Program and as a Presidential Scholar, Jerry served as President of the Korean Student Association, Tour Guide, and Orientation Advisor. He also was selected to take MDA 365: The Art and Adventure of Leadership, a leadership class taught by then-University President Dr. Steven B. Sample and Leadership Guru Dr. Warren G. Bennis.

After graduating from the Marshall School of Business, Jerry began his career in Real Estate development at Pulte Homes. He then went onto successfully manage many of Downtown LA's condominium sales offices. He currently works at Wells Fargo Home Mortgage's LA Builder Team.

Jerry is also the Founder and Chairman of the USC Korean Student Association Scholarship Fund and a Board Member of the USC Alumni Club of Downtown LA. Most recently, Jerry co-founded the Red Tigers Fan Club, a support group for Korean and Korean-American sports and athletes.
