Ken Scheinblum’s Summary

With over 25 years of experience in the global investment industry Ken has a wide range of professional & consulting expertise. Ken has provided innovative consulting & solutions for institutional investment managers. He has a unique broad & deep holistic understanding of the markets, products, & administration of the industry from end to end. Ken is well versed in the complexity & issues challenging this specialized arena. He offers management consulting, evaluations, project management, process analysis, & strategic planning. Software evaluations and implementations typically use our proprietary methods that are easily adaptable to the nature & specific needs of clients.

Over his career clients have included Ameriprise Financial, Driehaus Capital, Fidelity, MassMutual/Babson Capital Management, McMorgan & Co., MetLife, Microsoft Treasury, Putnam, SSRM (now Blackrock), SSgA, Vanguard, technology providers & others.

Before entering the consulting world, he held positions in portfolio management specializing in equity & alternative investments, headed investment accounting for a $100 billion company, managed an operations group, & in international treasury.

Ken has been published in industry publications. He is a contributing author to the book, Straight Through Processing for Financial Services Firms. He is noted for several important whitepapers including a whitepaper & presentation to the Federal Reserve Board with recommendations on the use & regulation of venture capital & private equity funds as investment options by national banks. He has been a speaker at seminars & events.
