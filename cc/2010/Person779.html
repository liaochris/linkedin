David Crouse’s Summary

I am an Associate Professor of Soil Science at North Carolina State University and a Geographic Information Specialist for the North Carolina Cooperative Extension Service. I joined the faculty of the Department of Soil Science at NC State University in 1996 after having completed three degrees at NC State.

I am a founding member of the NC State University Geographic Information Sciences graduate faculty and have served as the GISc representative for numerous students pursuing post-graduate degrees. I currently serves as the coordinator for the College of Agriculture and Life Sciences Geographic Information Systems Education Laboratory.

David Crouse’s Specialties:

I have a broad educational background that spans horticulture, waste management, soil fertility, precision agriculture and geospatial technologies.
