Graeme Rocher’s Summary

Graeme Rocher is a software engineer, consultant and dynamic language expert who serves as Head of Grails Development at SpringSource (http://www.springsource.com). Graeme is project lead of the open source Grails web application framework (http://grails.org) and co-author of "The Definitive Guide to Grails" (Apress - http://apress.com/book/view/1590599950).

In Graeme's current role as Head of Grails Development at SpringSource, the professional open source services company behind the Spring framework, Graeme leads the development of the Grails web framework and provides consulting, training and support to SpringSource's clients. Graeme is a frequent speaker at industry conferences on subjects related to Groovy, Grails and dynamic languages in Java.

Prior to joining SpringSource Graeme co-founded G2One - the Groovy/Grails Company - who were later acquired by SpringSource.

Graeme Rocher’s Specialties:

Dynamic Languages, Groovy, Grails, Ajax, Java enterprise technology, Server Architecture, Web Application Technologies, JavaScript, CSS
