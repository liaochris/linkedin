    Strategic Attorney LLC

    (Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    August 2009 — Present (7 months)

    Providing one-on-one mentoring to ten estate planning attorneys nationwide in the three areas of professional development (legal technical skills), rain-making (sales & marketing), and practice management (law firm technology, profitability analysis and improvement, team-building, accounting and more). We provide this mentoring through weekly teleconferences that include goal setting based on our participants' life commitments, those important "must dos" that precede specific goals (such as "be a good parent" or "earn enough money to live well").
