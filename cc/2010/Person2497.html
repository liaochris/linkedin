Marc Duchesne’s Summary

I am doing Fiber Optics and Telecoms since 25 years. Thanks to the Mac and the Internet, I am doing business as unusual since 15 years. Customer Satisfaction always been my motto.

I have had the chance to begin my career at the very beginning of Optical Communications. I had the chance to work for a US giant before, during, and a little (too short) while after the Dotcom' Era. I have had the chance to learn Web 2.0 and Social Media before it became mainstream. Since 2 years, I am a lucky guy: I work daily on the reality of FTTH Fiber-To-The-Home and Broadband matters at large.

Today, I am focusing on everything Sustainable Development: from truly innovative applications and usages of Broadband to Muni networks, from CO2 Footprint management to CSR Corporate Social Responsibility reporting. To make your life easier, and better.
