Joseph Husseini’s Summary

Joseph is the head of acquisition and sales for all 5 TV stations within the Lebanese Broadcasting Corporation (LBC): the number 1 television station in Lebanon and one of the leading satellite stations in the Middle East, and 3 subscription channels across the world.

Joseph is the founder and project manager with Bright I, a consulting and production company that provided advisory services to regional broadcasters, as well as coaching TV Presenters.
