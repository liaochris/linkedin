Reed R. Heustis, Jr., Esq.’s Summary

A workers' compensation defense attorney for an Orange County law firm, Reed represents both large and small employers, insurance companies, claims administrators, and homeowners throughout California.

Meanwhile on a part-time basis, Reed secures families and small businesses with affordable legal plans; identity theft restoration; and entrepreneurial opportunity with a 38-year-old company publicly listed on the New York Stock Exchange.

Reed earned his Juris Doctor in 1998 from San Diego's oldest law school, California Western School of Law; and his Bachelor of Arts in Political Science in 1993 from University of California, Irvine. In 2001 Reed graduated from the National Litigation Academy with a scholarship endowed by the Alliance Defense Fund, a servant organization that provides resources that empower the spread of the Gospel of Jesus Christ through legal defense and advocacy of religious freedom, the sanctity of human life, and biblical family values.

Reed and his wife raise two daughters, and are members of a local church that is affiliated with the Association of Reformed Baptist Churches of America.

His interests include baseball, soccer, fantasy sports, golf, politics, writing, classical music and network marketing.

Reed R. Heustis, Jr., Esq.’s Specialties:

Defending against excessive and unreasonable claims; negotiating fair settlements with legitimately injured workers; deposing applicants; interviewing witnesses; litigating in all venues in Northern and Southern California; drafting diary letters to client claims administrators; advocating at Status Conferences, Expedited Hearings, Mandatory Settlement Conferences, Lien Hearings and Trials at Workers' Compensation Appeals Board and other tribunals; disputing medical provider liens; and more.
