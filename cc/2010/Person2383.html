Larry Genkin’s Summary

In November 2008 Larry has created The Blogger & Podcaster Media Network by merging together 4 new media pioneering companies (Fuel My Blog, Podcast Pickle, Social Rank and Blogger & Podcaster Magazine) to create an advertising network consisting of thousands and thousands of bloggers and podcasters. Details can be found at www.bloggerandpodcaster.net.

As founder and CEO of Larstan Publishing Inc. and the creator of the Thought Leadership Marketing methodology, Larry has helped over 50 of the Fortune 500 companies and hundreds of small and medium sized businesses increase their bottom lines. Throughout his career he has studied and dealt with nearly every kind of business problem you can imagine.

Larry is arguably the best in the world at being able to look at a business and uncover hidden sources of business opportunity. He has captured the respect of Billionaires, CEO's, best selling authors and marketing gurus. He's personally owned 6 businesses in 6 different industries and has created over 170 joint ventures including partnerships with companies like: USA TODAY, Forbes and Time Inc. Larry is a frequently sought after business expert by the media.

His forte is being able to think "way outside the box" to identify exponential business gain with minimum effort. This business development "6th sense" is uniquely combined with an attention to detail that allows Larry's clients to translate concept into true business reality.
