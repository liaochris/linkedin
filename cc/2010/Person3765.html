Diane Nudelman’s Summary

Swag Connection is based in Portland, OR and was founded by me in early 2002. We sell custom-imprinted promotional products, business gifts, awards & apparel to companies across the US.

Our Ecoswag.com division was launched in 2009 to meet the growing demand for eco-responsible choices in our industry. We've taken the best eco-friendly products offered by our top suppliers and put them all on one easy-to-shop website.

While many of our clients find us through our websites and my blogs, most of our business is done over the phone or by email. Our clients appreciate the high level of service our team provides and we always have great suggestions to fit their needs & budget.

I invite you to visit our websites (links above) to learn more about what we do and the products we have to offer. Please feel free to contact me through LinkedIn if you would like to connect or talk with me about your company's swag needs.
