Rich Sadowsky’s Summary

In my third decade as a professional software developer, I keep finding new challenges that provide the opportunity to create game changing software. From the breakthroughs in development tools in my early years, to major contributions to strings of award winning software at Symantec, to the thrilling startups of the last decade, I keep delivering quality software on aggressive schedules with limited resources. If you own a computer you are using at least one product I have worked on. You may use one of my inventions every day to stay safe from malicious attacks on your computer.

Whether a given job requires me to lead innovation by architecting the software or pumping out code, or if my role is to raise VC funding by showing investor the technology roadmap, I am tenacious and will get the job done. I know how to align with sales and marketing, creative teams, and every facet of software companies to break down walls and ship award winning products.

My goal is to continue to join great partners to make stellar products that provide meaningful value to customers.
