William Buist’s Summary

We specialise in rapidly improving buy-in business leaders get for their critical work.

We understand the challenges individuals face when asked to work in collaborative teams.

You can read more about what we do in a book called BusinessWise, itself the result of a collaborative team of 19 Authors and others - you can read more about the book at www.high-performance-teams.co.uk

We will transfer these techniques and skills to you so that you don’t just have great returns on investments made now but also long term, improving, benefits.

You’ll know when to use different tools, techniques and skills and you will avoid re-inventing the wheel.

When you need to reach collective decisions, we’ll give you the environment and tools to reach a conclusion with the maximised buy in and minimised time.

Don’t just focus on the effectiveness of what they do (a real customer focus), nor just the efficiency of how they do it (a real cost focus). After all inefficient effectiveness can’t be a market leading strategy can it?, customers won’t keep returning to ineffective efficiency, will they?

Efficient, Effective companies win, and keep, more business, and make their customers their most ardent fans.

    We help Directors and Senior Managers to enhance trust with their teams for critical implementations. One client, a trade association, was advised by the Government that a new law would take effect in 15 months time. They needed to get all the relevant member companies to change their modus operandi, and IT systems all at the same time. It was not clear to the teams why this work was more important than the pressing activity their own businesses required, but for the trade association missing the deadline would mean a significant threat to their ability to influence future regulation. The trade association was unclear how to achieve what was required but together we helped to ensure that all the businesses made the change three months ahead of schedule.
