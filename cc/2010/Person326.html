Bart Caylor’s Summary

New media expertise and consulting. Designer with web specific development since 1995 working in information architecture and overall strategy. Currently working with clients to implement new media strategies into integrated marketing plans, including recent launches of blogs, podcasts, websites, social networking strategies, Flash tutorials and demos.

I also work with my background as a print designer to develop brand communications and create an integrated approach to marketing and advertising.

My goal is to partner with my clients to provide them expertise and solutions facing their marketing and communication challenges.
