Ken Schmitt’s Summary

Ken has been placing mid and senior-level financial and operational professionals for over 11 years. A native of San Diego, he possesses a unique understanding of the local marketplace, its diverse industry base and business culture. Ken prides himself on approaching each engagement from a holistic perspective, taking into account his client’s tactical, strategic and cultural needs and customizing each project accordingly.

Under Ken’s leadership, TurningPoint Executive Search delivers on its Passion Statement:

Building Southern California’s premier resource for mid and senior level professionals by offering unique career opportunities, providing personalized coaching, and driving corporate success.

Having successfully placed over 175 professionals in positions such as CFO, COO, Controller, VP Operations, VP Sales & Marketing, Treasurer, Director of Human Resources, President, Board Member, Supply Chain Director, Finance Director and Director of Internal Audit, Ken delivers a wide range of functional expertise to each engagement. While incorporating his 18 years of leadership experience, Ken successfully applies a unique process of in-person screening, business evaluation and cultural assessment to ensure that each executive placement is the ideal fit.

Ken Schmitt’s Specialties:

recruiting in finance, accounting, internal audit, tax, sales, marketing and operations professionals; industries include public accounting, hi-tech, software, biotech, medical device, manufacturing, retail, restaurants, services, distribution, experienced working with start- ups, mature organizations, pre-IPO companies, and expanding businesses both private and public. Career Coaching for professionals at all levels, from multiple disciplines to support their search for the ideal position.
