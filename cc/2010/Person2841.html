Murat Ersoy’s Summary

Mr. Ersoy is the founder of Multipotentials. Breaking through conventions and inexhaustible searching for solutions is his motto. Alongside building the MultiPotentials-movement he is accountable for business development, project strategy, stabilizer, motive force, administrator, and impossibilities solver. He is been asked to speak on conferences regarding: bottom line marketing, high-potentials, biculturalism, and innovation matters.

Murat holds a Masters degree in International Business and a Bachelors in Marketing. Born in The Hague and being a Dutch citizen with the Turkish/Circassian heritage of his parents, he has experience of work in Dubai, and has done Masters studies in EU, the US and Shanghai, as well as speaking 7 languages; Dutch/ Turkish/ Uygur-Chinese/ English/ North-Caucasian/ Kazakh/ and basic Russian.
