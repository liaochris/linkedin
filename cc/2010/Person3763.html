Suzanne Engels’s Summary

Do you have a web site plan? Do you use Facebook for business? Team up with a company that understands web site design, search marketing AND web apps like Facebook, LinkedIn, Local Maps and more. Contact Suzanne at http://www.webartntech.com

We produce results, because results matter.

DefeatingALS.com:
Supporting the fight against Lou Gehrig's disease. Having lost 2 family members to ALS, my husband and I cofounded this website which, along with an email newsletter, has generated over $10,000 in direct donations and has helped spread the word about numerous local and national fundraising events.
