Edward Branley’s Summary

NOTE: I am NOT an open networker. If you've arrived here from MLPF or another LinkedIn-related site, please tell me WHY we should connect, don't just issue a generic invitation.

My primary occupation these days is doing SAN training, for Hitachi Data Systems, through Merit Systems, LLC. I'll be teaching several classes as well as doing course development work.

Additionally, I've been a street railway enthusiast ever since I was a small child, riding the Perley A. Thomas streetcars from the cemeteries to downtown with my parents to go shopping on Saturdays. In 2004, I wrote a book on the history of New Orleans' Canal streetcar line. The plan was to use the book as an impetus to start this effort, but the storm became a bit of a speed bump in the process. As part of the process of rebuilding our city, NOSRA's mission will be to educate on the importance and value of our historic streetcars, and to raise funds to preserve and restore several of the NORTA-owned streetcars currently not in service.

I plan to combine my expertise in education, courseware development, Internet development, business networking, public speaking, and small business management with my passion for history and streetcars to make this venture a success for streetcar fans and the community at large.
