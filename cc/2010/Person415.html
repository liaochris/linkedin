Bob Phibbs, the Retail Doctor®’s Summary

Since 1994 companies worldwide have turned to Bob Phibbs, the Retail Doctor® for the passion and expertise necessary to grow their sales.
* Worked with some of the largest retail brands in the world including Hunter Douglas Window Fashions, Yamaha, LEGO, True Value, Brother, Husqvarna, Viking.
* Retail expert with a proven track record of increasing sales 20-50% within months.
* Sales management of winning teams
* COO with a broad range of responsibilities creating a culture of exceptional experiences.
* Training from the most basic to the most advanced sales techniques.
*Professional speaker coaching and mentoring business owners.
* CMO of second-fastest growing company in Los Angeles two years running.
* Built two successful businesses from scratch.
* Author of "You Can Compete: Double Sales Without Discounting"
* Author of "The Retail Doctor's Guide To Growing Your Business" (April 2010, Wiley & Sons)

Helped thousands of businesses succeed, especially when their customer base has been challenged by big-boxes or economic uncertainty.
