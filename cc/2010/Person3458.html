Sabrina Nespeca’s Summary

Sabrina is a new librarian but is already professionally experienced in reference, circulation and serials in academic and public libraries.

She is particularly interested in helping libraries "come to users." Her background as a student of psychology, education, design, and information technology converge in an unusual approach to librarianship; one rooted in the ground of traditions but watching the skies of 'cloud computing.'


She started life not knowing there was a profession for people seek to learn everything: librarians. Before this epiphany, her other jobs made her a patient tutor, an inspiring trainer and coach, and responsive customer servant.

Sabrina Nespeca’s Specialties:

Electronic resource licensing and management, databases, ejournals, Web 2.0, reference, access services, circulation, collection development, information competency, information-seeking behavior, web usability, student success, human-computer interaction, market research
