Joe McDonald’s Summary

I am a co-owner of Durrani Design, a full service creative design and web development agency located in Encinitas, CA, just north of San Diego on the historic Highway 101.

Durrani Design is also the home of these sites:

TeeCompressed.com, a division of Durrani Design, provides promotional compressed t-shirts and custom silkscreen printing.
