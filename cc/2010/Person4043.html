Vitaly Golomb’s Summary

Over 14 years of startup, agency and consulting experience in business and product development, branding, analytics-driven online marketing, ecommerce, information architecture and software interface design. Award-winning work in web, multimedia and print design.

Guest lecturer at Northwestern Polytechnic University, Cal State Hayward and UC Berkeley. Consistent involvement with the Bay Area startup ecosystem, industry groups and non-profits. Chair of the Technology Ventures SIG at Silicon Valley Innovation Society.

Studied Communications at Santa Clara University and received a BA in Computer & Video Imaging from Cogswell Polytechnical College.

Live in San Francisco with my wife, son and daughter.

Vitaly Golomb’s Specialties:

Internet product development; information architecture and user experience; branding, positioning and go-to-market; analytics-driven online marketing
