Tim Grace’s Summary

Armed with a diverse background in online product leadership, consumer marketing and research, I am seasoned in creating delightful & profitable customer experiences.

Consistently empowered and expected to investigate the enormous potential of technology in the face of fierce competition, I'm a self-achiever who thrives in results-driven environments with a focus on innovation, growth, and profitability
