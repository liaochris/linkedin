Ron Davies’s Summary

Declared the “Obvious Choice” by New York Times Best-Selling Business author Elsom Eldridge. Ron works with business owners in Canada, US, and the UK helping them better leverage social media. His unique strategies have a profound impact on corporate bottom line through laser-focused social media, brand monitoring automation & mobile device leverage.

A decorated war veteran with 23 years of service to his country, Ron started the first of his two companies in 1998, where he proved his techniques by taking two of his own companies from capitalization of under $1,000 to million dollar companies in no time flat.

Ron is the CEO of JoePro solutions, an interactive Offline Marketing company located just outside Toronto, Ontario, providing coaching & consulting to businesses & professionals that want their web presence to actually work for them. Ron exploded onto the public speaking scene quickly becoming one of the most in-demand speakers in his field.

An MBA with over 14 years Internet experience, he teaches individuals & companies how to leverage their businesses online. His company owns and manages dozens of websites and membership systems. Over 10,000 members, 230,000 subscribers, and published in many languages.

One of Ron’s clients proclaimed, “Ron literally turned my business website around in a single 15 minute conversation, and all he did was glance at it!” Dennis Becker, speaking event organizer, said of Ron’s presentation, “Ron absolutely wowed attendees with his presence, passionate delivery and skills".

Ron is the author of the book “How to Win the $1,000,000 Pillow Fight” which tells his Cinderella story, & provides actual techniques readers can use to explode their business in a very short time through social media and sound Internet marketing methods.
