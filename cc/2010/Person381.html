Bill Black, CMP’s Summary

As a Certified Mortgage Planner, I see my role differently than a traditional loan officer. My goal is to help you integrate the loan that you select into your over-all long and short-term financial and investment plan to help minimize taxes, improve cash flow and minimize interest expense. We want to help you manage your home equity to increase liquidity, safety and rate of return. We strive to really understand what is important to you about a loan.
