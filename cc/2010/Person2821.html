Matthew Reyes’s Summary

Exploration Solutions Inc. is a consulting firm dedicated to improving the success of research, science, & technical programs through mission planning & documentation, technical advising, and the development of education & public outreach programs. Matthew founded Exploration Solutions as an S-Corp to serve the education, science, and edutainment sectors. Since it’s founding, Exploration Solutions has completed a number of science related activities, including:

• July 2007: Exploration Solutions co-facilitated an expedition to CSA & NASA’s Haughton-Mars Project Research Station in the Canadian Arctic. Matthew filmed & co-hosted a series of webcasts to 14 Challenger Learning Centers with NASAWATCH editor Keith Cowing, & Astronaut Leroy Chiao. The trio inspired hundreds of children by sharing our observations and stories while living & working alongside researchers preparing future missions to the Moon & Mars
• Oct, 2007: Exploration Solutions worked with the Space Sciences Institute as a videographer for a microgravity education documentary about the Northrop Grumman Foundation.
• Dec. 2007: Exploration Solutions developed video, conducted interviews & supported students from Rollins College as they hosted an ecological conservation education campaign to mainland Ecuador and the Galápagos Islands.
• Feb 2008: Exploration Solutions developed a small format, microgravity flight research program for Space Florida sponsored parabolic flights by the ZERO-G Corp. As Technical Manager, Matthew developed the pilot program’s research manual and worked closely with a competitively chosen group of University students to insure a safe and DOT, TSA, & FAA compliant engineering process.
• Nov 2008, Exploration Solutions partnered with Zero Gravity Films for work with National Geographic TV as a creative & technical consultant for a video production of props and acrobatic maneuvers during parabolic microgravity flight.
