Halley O'Brien’s Summary

Hi. I’m Halley.

I’m a storyteller by nature with a knack for public relations – it’s what sets me apart from the other ski bums toting a camera and the PR pros in expensive Italian suits.

Videography, photography, writing, blogging, networking… I wear a lot of hats, both literally and figuratively. (I’ll show you my beanie collection if you don’t believe me.)

It’s all about getting the message across and having fun doing it.

I love snowboarding. If we’ve ever spoken, you probably know that already. It’s hard to hide my enthusiasm.

Everything is connected. I immerse myself in the snowboarding realm, paint a PR picture and love every second of it.

Get to know me at: www.HalleyOBrien.com or shoot me an e-mail: halley.obrien@gmail.com.
