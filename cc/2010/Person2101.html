Josh Giese’s Summary

Some people may know me as Josh Highland. I'm an internet aficionado and entrepreneur who currently lives in Redlands California. You can follow my online activities at JoshHighland.com

I am an internet applications developer that specializes in building online communities through social media technologies. I love to empower users to connect and contribute with each other. I am passionate about the limitless possibilities that software and the internet offer when combined with an individuals personal passions.

Currently I develop web applications for ESRI, the world leader in GIS technologies. As the lines between web based applications and desktop software continue to blur, the projects I work on have become a mission critical pieces the ESRI product line.

I contribute to TechZulu.com, where I am a correspondent. TechZulu is a website that focuses on technology based companies and events in Southern California, focusing on video interviews with key individuals in the tech world. TechZulu is gaining ground rapidly, and I am proud to help them grow.

I am also the owner and lead developer of notPopular LLC. The goal of notPopular is to connect fans of underground music through social media technologies. Established in 2002, notPopular has become one of the best know resources in the Southern California underground music scene, helping fans connect with bands, and for bands to gain new followers, as well as delivering the latest music news and information.

My latest endeavor is Hold Fast Products LLC. I couldn't find a mens hair pomade that fit my needs, so I decided to create my own premium hair product. In October of 2008, the first batch of Hold Fast Premium water based pomade shipped to customers. The marketing efforts of Hold Fast are primarily conducted through social networks, and HoldFastHair.com

I earned a BS in Computer Science at the University of California Riverside (UCR)
