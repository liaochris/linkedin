Paul Collins’s Summary

Over the last two years I have been working on All about Croydon, a large web portal for residents, business and visitors to find information quickly and easily. In the last few months we have re-designed the web site from it's old HTML format to the new Drupal 6 CMS System.

Converting this has been a major task but it is now quicker and easier to use and update. The goals for the next 12 months is to launch new sites throughout the UK for the B2B market.
