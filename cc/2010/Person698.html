Cheryl Meril’s Summary

After having worked ten years in legal document production at law firms such as Kirkland & Ellis LLP, Chapman and Cutler LLP, Pillsbury Winthrop Shaw Pittman LLP and Morrison & Foerster LLP, Ms. Meril obtained a paralegal certificate at National Paralegal College. Ms. Meril then obtained her Notary Public Commission from the State of California in October of 2008.

In late 2009, Ms. Meril founded At Your Service Legal Document Prep and Nob Hill Notary Services, both serving the San Francisco community.

Prior to working in the legal field, Ms. Meril worked in marketing divisions for such companies as Xerox Worldwide Business Research and Epson America.

Notary Public, Legal Document Assistant, Loan Signing Agent/Mobile Notary, Digital Courier Specialist and Process Server
