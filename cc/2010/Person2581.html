Mathias Lin | 林马’s Summary

Mathias has 11+ years of professional experience in IT and has been consulting clients ranging from startups to Fortune 500 corporations. He is an enthusiastic proponent about technology and its impact on business and productivity.

For the past 6 years (2004-2009), he served as managing director for SYSVISION in Germany, Hong Kong and Mainland China and the US. The company serves national and international clients such as Lufthansa, Bayer, China Telecom, Unilever, Malteser Hilfsdienst, Benchpark, Dräger, META Group/Gartner, Philips, Atos Origin. Core business is the development of tailor-made software solutions for ERP, CRM, CMS, DMS.

From 2010, he's an independent systems analyst and software engineer, focusing on large, complex IT systems. Technology focus on Java EE, SOA, Google Android, Open Source technologies. Industry focus on healthcare management. Expert with OpenCms and Alfresco DMS.

His interest lies in healthcare IT and how open source and open knowledge concepts and projects can contribute to the healthcare systems in developing and emerging countries.

Mathias Lin | 林马’s Specialties:
