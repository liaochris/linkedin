Roelf Woldring’s Summary

I soar when I am doing what best fits my personality and temperament - which is being an innovative organizational problem solver and team builder. I love solving complex work place problems which require coordinated change implementation on the people, the process, the tooling and the information level. I do it well, either as the leader responsible or as a coach / guide to the leader responsible. Transformation Partners' business direction reflects this.

I have been fascinated by the way that people behave at work for all of my life. This has led me to become, through practical experience and academic training, broadly knowledge about people, organizations and the way the two come together.

But theory alone just does not do it for me. Ideas are a guide to practical action and demonstrated results. I need to prove them through pragmatic application. All the reading I have done, and all the reflection on experience which is so much part of who I am, mean nothing without the ability to apply - to produce valued results.
