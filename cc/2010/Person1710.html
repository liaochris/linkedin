Ishak Kang’s Summary

My entrepreneurial focus is devising ways to make consumer electronics easier to use for everyone. I draw from my experience designing high-end "smart homes," but the real market opportunities is to implement these features at a fractional cost using standards-based, sustainable technologies.

I am active with the Consumer Electronics Association, serve on the Board of the Small Business Council, and chair a committee to develop social networking. I serve on the board of the Reverse Logistics Association's Consumer Electronics Division and its Standards Committee. I am also a principal member of the Universal Remote Console Consortium which has just had its pluggable UI standards ratified by the ISO/IEC 24752.

By promoting these standards to the CE manufacturing industry, I hope to solve the crisis of too many product returns, too much e-waste, too many user interfaces, too many devices, too many wires and too much confusion.
