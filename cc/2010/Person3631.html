Simon Pielow’s Summary

Enthusiasts for the New Age of Rail, Fay Lejeune & I manage Train Chartering, which occupies a small but significant niche in the rail industry - sourcing and supplying passenger trains and private carriages (rail cars) for charter / hire similar to an air charter broker but with intensive project & guest management.

We provide consultancy and advice for:
- potential charter clients;
- those seeking to purchase luxury trains or rail cars;
- train companies wishing to upgrade existing onboard services.

Take the time to enjoy the journey.

Simon Pielow’s Specialties:

This is the new age of rail; I am lucky to be part of the renaissance!

The world's rail industry needs better marketing and commercial skills.

My background merges formal sales training with marketing skills and data analysis which. to date, has served Train Chartering well.

However I am committed to continual learning and to adopting best practices for whatever the future holds for Train Chartering and me.

Rail is the best way to travel on earth, and we are onboard!
