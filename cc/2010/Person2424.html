Lori Ruff’s Summary

As “The LinkedIn Diva,” I have been instrumental in building a speaking and training business based around LinkedIn and Social Media.

In fact, Integrated Alliances literally invented LinkedIn training in 2006. I joined the executive team in 2008 after I had been training others how to become more successful via Internet and computing technologies for over 10 years.

IA teaches businesses how to build & protect their presence on LinkedIn and Social Media and build a strong network of connections. From there we teach communications and business development.

How can we help you? What qualifies “The LinkedIn Rockstars”?

Beyond years of experience speaking & training on the subject of LinkedIn & Social Media and related topics, we truly live and breathe the LinkedIn & Social Media lifestyle. This is a way of being for us. We are fully immersed and that investment of ourselves gives us tremendous insight to share with others as a result.

Along with my partner Mike O’Neil, The LinkedIn Rockstar, I am Tuned-in, Turned-on, 24/7! You might say that we are relative experts in the area of full on business development. This lets us help others too, those that are driven, mobile, always on and looking for a way to make Social Media work for them in a super time-strapped environment.

As the 9th MOST CONNECTED WOMAN on LinkedIn and full-time social media advocate, we are often asked to speak and train companies, organizations, and associations. We coach individuals & teams from Managers and Directors to C-Level Executives.

Our company, Integrated Alliances has trained 10,000+ professionals in 500+ classes, workshops, webinars, seminars & conferences all over the world. In fact, we've trained over 100 other LinkedIn trainers!

We are authentic and helpful. We have connections worldwide with which we can also connect you in order to help you achieve your goals.
