Mark James’s Summary

ED Solutions, Inc. 614.505.7702 is a provider of a variety of technology-based consultancy and value-added services to the economic development profession.

We offer web site reviews, web site redesign advisory services and advice and assistance in such areas as contact management solutions, lead process management, site and buildings databases, GIS, etc. One of our newest offerings is helping in the development of social media strategies. We are also a prolific trainer to the profession.

Our client list includes economic development organizations from 45 US states, Canada and the UK.

We are also a co-founder of Development Results, the first web tracking tool design specifcially for the profession- www.developmentresults.com
