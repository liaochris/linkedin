Connie Sweet’s Summary

Develop marketing, branding and public relations materials for companies and organizations to resonate with the target audience while retaining integrity of each organizations philosophy, product, service or event. Development of press releases, copywriting, graphics, distinctive electronic and print communication materials. Develop brands, corporate identity, print collateral, product marketing, packaging, publication, trade show displays, signs, advertising, educational materials, electronic newsletter campaigns, and web site graphics and pages.
Management and marketing including presentations, networking, non-profit board leadership, sales, estimates, budgets, schedules, concept development, design and production management. Client presentations to order fulfillment. Manage staff, printers, web programmers and other vendors for bids, and fulfillment. Industries served include Associations, Education, Environmental, Publishing, Printing, Building, Real Estate, Retail, Arts, Service companies, and Non Profit agencies.
