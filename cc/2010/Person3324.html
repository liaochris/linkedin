Richard Rosa’s Summary

I am the co-founder and co-owner of Buyers Brokers Only, LLC, which is an exclusive buyer brokerage firm located in Haverhill, MA. All our real estate brokers also are lawyers.

I am a member of the National Association of Exclusive Buyer Agents, Massachusetts Association of Buyer Agents, and National, Massachusetts and North Shore Association of REALTORS. I am also a member of the Real Estate Bar Association of Massachusetts.

I strongly encourage individuals considering the purchase of a home to visit my company's Web site (http://www.BuyersBrokersOnly.com).

Our goal is to grow the Buyers Brokers Only, LLC by associating around Massachusetts with real estate brokers that also are lawyers and who are interested in providing home buyers with superior customer service and ethical representation. We presently have associate brokers in Boston, MA; Andover, MA; and Marshfield, MA; Hingham, MA; Lynn, MA; Cambridge, MA; Canton, MA; and Millis, MA. Massachusetts and New Hampshire attorneys interested in working as a real estate broker for Buyers Brokers Only, LLC should contact me.

My law firm, Rosa & Kres, LLP, primarily represents buyers purchasing real estate. Previosuly I practiced in the areas of criminal defense and civil litigation. I am admitted to practice law in Massachusetts and New Hampshire.
