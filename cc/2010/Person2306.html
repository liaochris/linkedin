Kurt Godden’s Summary

Innovative manager of technology and analytically-based change initiatives. Broad-based understanding of knowledge representation, data analysis, business process modeling, speech recognition, computer programming, and project management. Excellent people skills, oral and written communication. Extensive international experience in both Europe and Asia. Good German skills and some French.

Sole inventor on two patents: “Location-Based Notifications,” U.S. Patent 7,394,405, issued July 1, 2008; and “Dynamic N-Best Algorithm to Reduce Recognition Errors,” U.S. Patent 7,421,387, issued September 2, 2008. Co-inventor on several additional pending patents. GM also awarded me 3 tool-method inventions (trade secrets).

Kurt Godden’s Specialties:

Recent work included business process modeling and marketing data analysis. Much experience in text mining, speech recognition, machine translation, controlled language, ontologies and knowledge representation.
