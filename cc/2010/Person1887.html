Jeff Steinmetz’s Summary

As the owner of Urge Productions, I stay busy with freelance photography, HD video and audio production.

I knew early on that arts and technology were merging. This led
to the creation of my own custom bachelors degree - a blend of Theatre, Music and Computer Science.

I blend a creative eye & tuned musical ear with technical know how.

Recent work includes shooting models from Look Model Agency, Audio production for L.A. based dance performances, remixes, audio supervision, live sound and large scale event production.

Jeff Steinmetz’s Specialties:

Photography: Canon DSLR, Medium Format Film, Dark Room, Color Correction, HD Videography, Adobe Premiere, Adobe After Effects, Final Cut

Technology & Languages: .NET, Visual Basic, C++, ASP, JavaScript, PerlScript, Embedded Assembly, PHP, SQL

Web: HTML, DHTML, Flash, ActionScript, XML and XSL, WordPress

Audio Tools: Ableton Live, Logic Pro, Cakewalk Sonar, ProTools HD, Sonic Foundry Sound Forge, audio processors, digital audio mixers and tools.
