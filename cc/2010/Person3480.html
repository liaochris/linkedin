Sandra Read’s Summary

Sandra's varied work experience spans the staffing, financial, government, retail and hospitality industries. She is a customer savvy, enthusiastic, outgoing, intelligent worker who is quick to pick up new tasks. In all of her past positions she has been highly rewarded for her outstanding achievements through internal recognition programs and has been promoted numerous times in short time spans. Sandra has extensive experience using computers including an alphanumeric data-entry rate of approximately 100 words per minute. She continues to expand her knowledge through self-study as well as academic courses. She comes highly recommended from all previous employers.
