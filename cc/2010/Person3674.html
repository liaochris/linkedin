Stacey Stockman’s Summary

"We are a success incubator, growing great businesses and powerful leaders." ~ Stacey Stockman

As Founder and President of Sylutions.com, Stacey Stockman directs strategic initiatives, vision and new business development on behalf of this “success incubator.” Ms. Stockman brings with her over 15 years of advising, counseling, and experience supporting the growth of emerging technology, startups and green space organizations, as well as some of the world’s most recognized companies such as United Airlines, Sony, Eddie Bauer, Travelocity and EMC. Drawing upon her years as an Applications Developer, Business Analyst and Operations Manager she has successfully sourced, recruited and secured both the talent and funding required for her client’s expansion.

As a business luminary, Ms. Stockman strategically partners with her clients incorporating her hands on business and technical skills, along with over a decade of highly specialized recruitment training, including her work as a Division Director with Robert Half International. As a result of many successful placements and network building, Stacey has spearheaded many partnerships on behalf of the company including a recent partnership with John Hancock Retirement Services.

Ms. Stockman has a B.A. in Psychology and Philosophy from Shimer College including advanced studies at Oxford University, Balliol College. She is an active philanthropist supporting earth friendly initiatives as well as nurturing to those extreme cases of child illness and grief counseling. Stacey has also been the recipient of the Bahi Award for cultural awareness for her work promoting literacy. An advocate of women entrepreneurs and business visionaries, she has been an active member of both the National Association of Woman Business Owners and the National Association of Female Executives since 1994. Stacey’s personal life consists of meditation, yoga, surfing, travel and enjoying the antics of her dachshund puppy Zoey.
