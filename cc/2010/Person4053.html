Volker Hamel’s Summary

Due to my long international experience and social as well as technical skills in sales support and project management I can successfully and actively contribute to the company success. My skills and experience allow me to fill and develop a position in middle management for pre-sales / sales support and project management.

Holding a master degree in economics, I currently fill a permanent position with a world-leading provider of IT-based identity solutions. In this employment customer care, sales support, partner networking and project management are key aspects of my work.
I have worked more than ten years in the advanced IT sector servicing various markets from leisure and area security, network applications, airports and national security to government solutions. Dealing with telecommunication based biometrics and secure credentialing in large networks I gained experience in pre-sales, partner support and project management on a global level. As proven by my long-term, fruitful cooperation with business partners and customers, I am able to take a sensitive approach to overcome cultural barriers and work efficiently in a multi-national environment. I successfully ran challenging projects especially all over Asia-Pacific, Europe and Middle East

During the participation in the Security Program for Federal German and International (NATO) Projects including Classified Information my employment at the German Branch Office included the position as Deputy Security Representative. In this role I held the highest official Security Clearance (Germany Ü3) for access to classified material the Management of Security and Confidentiality related Issues.

In both English and German language communication is my strength. Team play, leadership, outstanding self motivation, sales and market oriented thinking and acting complete my skills.
