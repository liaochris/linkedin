Ramon Guiu’s Summary

Ramon is an entrepreneur passionate about technology.

Ramon is currently completing a full-time MBA program at IESE Business School, where he is co-president of the Entrepreneurs Club, to strengthen his managerial skills. Before the MBA, Ramon worked for more than 6 years in Internet and software start-ups (2.5 years as an entrepreneur) occupying different roles, from CTO to QA engineer. He has developed a deep knowledge of search engine, CMS and web tecnologies and is passionate about providing creative and elegant solutions that help users and companies get things done in a more convenient and efficient way.

Ramon has a broad international experience in Europe and the Unites States. He is used to manage projects with teams spread across several countries and to deliver on time.

Ramon holds a masters degree in Telecommunications Engineering from the Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya in Barcelona and a Masters of Software Engineering from the École National Supérieure de Télécommunications de Paris. He is fluent in English, Spanish, French and Catalan and is currently learning Italian.

Ramon Guiu’s Specialties:

Internet and mobile technologies, IT solutions design, project management & execution.
