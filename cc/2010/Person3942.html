  • MS in Entrepreneurship Student

    SMU Cox School of Business

    (Educational Institution; Higher Education industry)

    January 2009 — Present (1 year 1 month)

    The SMU Cox MSE program is one of the most recognized entrepreneurship programs in the US and throughout the World. A 'good idea' is nothing if it isn't in the hands of a capable, tenacious entrepreneur- and that is why I enrolled in this program. Having already attained my finance degree (SMU BBA '05), and successfully launching a cashmere line in New York, this program has strengthened my core abilities and further developed my business acumen.

    This program is best described as an opportunity to learn the nuts and bolts of creating a successful, fund-able company. I am now well versed in concept evaluation, due diligence, financial modeling, financial forecasting, market research, competitive landscape analysis, business viability assessment, top level strategy, execution, cash flow management, stimulating organic growth, and the ability to identify opportunities in emerging industries, opposed to reading about it in entrepreneur magazine.

    SMU Cox provides a tremendous number of networking opportunities, mentor opportunities and leadership opportunities to all of their graduate students. All of that aside, this program is like most things in life- you only get out of it, what you put into it.
