James 'Jim' Decker’s Summary

Design, manufacture, and sell souvenir merchandise for live entertainment properties with a specialty in Broadway shows to build and enhance brand recognition while providing a supplemental revenue stream for the producers. Creating unique retail stores and themed sales points, we develop novel and innovative marketing items to be sold or given away to promote individual productions. Directed merchandising for dozens of events including the upcoming 2009 ING New York City Marathon, Paul Simon’s Concert in Central Park, Chitty Chitty Bang Bang, The Color Purple and The Phantom of the Opera and worked with hundreds of theatres across North America and venues as diverse as Yankee Stadium and NYC Central Park.
