Ricardo Guerrero’s Summary

Career Goals:
Growing Stwittergy to become the premier provider of metrics for companies using Twitter. Consulting with companies to help them develop effective Social Media strategies.

Experience:
• Created the Dell Outlet Twitter program, which generated $1M in sales in its first 18 months, and led the development and strategy of Dell's Twitter presence.
• 9 years at Dell in roles ranging from Web Content Developer to leading Global Web Developer Training, then Website Consultant/Strategist, Project Manager, Program Manager, and now Online Advertising Manager for Dell Latin America's Consumer team.
• Entrepreneur prior to joining Dell, and tutor of Math, Physics, and Spanish for many years before that. Love to share useful information and knowledge, and know how to teach in a fun way so people will "get it."
