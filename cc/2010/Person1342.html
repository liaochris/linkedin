Andrew Lauman’s Summary

Andrew Lauman, B.Sc, Founder and Owner & Business/Diversity Consultant

Andrew is the president of Gateways International Services where we offer training and recruiting solutions to a diverse group of clients and a broad range of international industries. Andrew, who graduated from the University of British Columbia, Canada, with a Bachelor of Science, built this company over two years ago and continues to develop the vision and strategy of GIS. The company first started off as a recruiting business, but now it also has education and consulting divisions.

Andrew has over six years of business-to-business experience and has developed working relationships with more than 20 international companies. The learning centre educates more than 60 students in any one semester and continues to add new courses to its existing ones. Andrew also has over 10 years of teaching experience, a background that informs and empowers his daily activities.

Andrew has lived in Canada, Korea and the United States and has traveled and worked internationally throughout Canada, Korea, the United States, Mexico, Thailand, Bermuda and Japan. Andrew has come into contact with many people of diverse nationalities; both he and the many GIS staff and consultants draw on their corporate wealth of intercultural experience as they look forward to a prosperous future in business.
