Brian Cork’s Summary

Brian Patrick Cork's experience spans that of a proud and deeply involved father; successful businessman; professional athlete; volunteer firefighter; and, entrepreneur.

Brian has been a highly successful executive and entrepreneur, and has raised over $500 million in private placement capital.

Brian has led sales teams exceeding 1400, and built and sold several successful businesses.

In 2003, Brian was named to London's prestigious 'Ol Man Society for his contributions as a businessman and philanthropist. In 2004 Brian was the G2 economics "Coalition Member of the Year". He has been recognized as one of the most successful entrepreneurs in the Southeast. In both 2007 and 2008 brian cork Human Capital was named to the Inc. 500. Brian has also been selected by Catalyst Magazine as a "Top 50 Entrepreneur" in 2004 and 2005, and a "Top 25 Entrepreneur" in 2007 and 2008.

Brian Cork's interests include the Prudent Society of Optimistic Gentlemen, a global group of business investors, intellectualista's, and theorists. Brian is also the a Founder and Resident Entrepreneur of Ministry Accelerator (now called Mr. Jefferson's House), and, most recently the Alpharetta-based ACCELERATOR, focused on developing start-up companies that can be self-sufficient and self-sustaining within eighteen months.

Brian also Coaches the Fusion U14 and U16 Shockers Soccer Team.

Cork is the author of the Blog: The Unsinkable brian cork. You can view this Blog at: www.unsinkablebriancork.com.
