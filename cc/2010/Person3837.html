Andy Smith’s Summary

As a successful hands-on tester and test project consultant, I have shared a beneficial mix of 'testing' and 'people' experience with scores of clients for over a decade or more.

Consequently, I have made the acquaintance of hundreds of fellow test professionals, whose levels of influence, breadth of knowledge and depth of delivery capacity have helped these projects succeed and prosper. These are my testingpeople.

I have always been keen to explore innovative, progressive and collaborative solutions to the timeless business conundrum: how can we do this better?
