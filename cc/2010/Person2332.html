Larry Scott’s Summary

Larry Scott is a relationship-focused global HR leader and connector with over 25 years of diverse experience in international, HR, tax, and legal. He is Director of Corporate Relationships at FosterQuan, the largest minority-certified immigration law firm in the world. In addition, Larry is President of Houston International HR Roundtable, an international HR education and networking organization.

Larry has diverse international experience, including start-ups from pre-licensing stage through operation. His international experience began in 1992 in Hong Kong with one of the global accounting firms, where he ultimately became coordinator of the US expatriate tax practice. As an expatriate in three countries, first-world and developing, and with children, Larry brings a unique perspective to the world of global mobility and immigration. His international assignments have allowed him to travel extensively and provided opportunities to develop cultural and diversity awareness. Larry is a social media enthusiast and frequently teaches others how to use it for developing relationships and international communication. He is committed to developing Houston as the Global Center of Excellence for International Human Resources.

Larry is @ldscott on Twitter.

Larry Scott’s Specialties:

Relationship and community building, US and global immigration, employer compliance and strategy, domestic and international HR, diversity and inclusion, international tax, global employment, multicultural environments, social media, strategic thinking and creative problem solving.
