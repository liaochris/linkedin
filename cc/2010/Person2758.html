Mike Voss’s Summary

A German native, having lived in Ireland from 2002 to 2010, Mike has just ended his tenure as Technical Director at X Communications in Dublin, Ireland, leading a team of ASP.NET web developers, using C# with a strong specialization in CMS solutions.

Prior to joining X Communications, Mike served as Ecommerce Consultant at Centralwings Poland and LSA Link Ireland. During his studies, he also had the opportunity to work as Assistant Systems Analyst at Walsh Western Int. and as Customer Care Agent at PayPal Europe.

Earlier in his career, Mike served for several years as Retail Banking Executive at Deutsche Bank Germany while pursuing his further interests in game design and development on the Windows platform. He is the co-founder of the German freeware label Horizont Enterainment.

Mike went back to education after emigrating to Ireland, attending Tipperary Institute where he studied EBusiness to Honours Degree level and graduated with 1st class honours as best in class in 2006. He is currently in the process of obtaining a Masters in Computer Science (M.Sc. in MIS) at Trinity College Dublin.
