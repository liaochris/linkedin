Marsha Diamond’s Summary

Marsha is positioning clients/audiences in the right direction for financial and individual success in the areas of sales, marketing, and operational efficiencies.

With expertise in needs analysis, service systems, business strategies, revenue generation, customer relationship management and a solid footing in the foodservice and technology industries, Marsha is a pro at problem solving and innovation.

She has an amazing ability to listen and customize approaches to clients’ initiatives and challenges.

Marsha’s scope of positions prepares her for understanding operational, people and technology challenges in working with a diverse work force from professionals to entry-level staff

Marsha’s uniquely diverse background spans the full range of positions–from foodservice operations, sales/marketing, business development, to the executive director of a national association.

She continues to deliver insightful and practical presentations and workshops related to sales, marketing, health, cultural diversity, customer loyalty, social media, generational diversity, and thriving in these times to national associations, universities, corporations, sales companies, restaurants, hotel chains, hospitals, healthcare associations, and executive retreats.
