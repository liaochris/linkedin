CONNECTING TO MY NETWORK

Please read the following so that we can ensure that our connecting will be of benefit to you:

1. I love connecting people to great resources and other people that can assist them in achieving their professional goals, so yes please feel free to connect

2. I will always accept invitations from friends, business acquaintances and clients to join my network on LinkedIn

3. If we haven’t met, I am open to connecting but please send an introductory note providing a little information about your background and how we would both benefit from connecting

4. As I consider connecting people in my network to others as a recommendation and therefore want to ensure a quality connection for all concerned I will not usually forward requests to connect you to my network if I do not know you and your work. Please write a detailed request outlining the benefit to the person in my network and why they would find it of value in connecting to you. In return I promise to only send you relevant, well written requests.

6. If you do connect with me, so that we can stay in touch I will add you to my FREE newsletter "Biz Growth Express" and you will get immediate access to our Teleseminar Masterclasses so that you can "Bring YOUR Brand To Life".You can unsubscribe at any time.
