Jan Blencowe’s Summary

Professional artist, Jan Blencowe, helps people bring the beauty and serenity of nature and the joy of color into their homes and workspaces through her original paintings. Landscapes offered in several styles and all sizes including impressionism, tonalism, contemporary realism, semi-abstract and abstract. Specializing in serene atmospheric landscapes of marshes,waterways,fields,wildflowers and vibrant textural abstract and semi-abstract landscapes. Working in both oil and acrylic, plein air as well as studio.

Commissions to individuals and sales to the interior design trade welcome.

Current gallery representation in CT, GA, & PA, and on the internet at www.janblencowe.com, www.ThePoeticLandscape.com, www.DailyPaintersInternational.blogspot.com, www.FineArtAmerica.com, www.ArtGuideGallery.com

Collectors worldwide including US, Canada, Australia, UK, Germany, Finland, Brazil, Portugal and the US Embassy in Honduras.

    Tracy Art Center

    The Tracy Art Center, founded in 1989 by Nancy Tracy, a notable local artist and art teacher, has continually and consistently supported the Connecticut shoreline arts community. To support its mission, the Tracy Art Center pursues a variety of avenues such as gallery showings, community events, artist seminars and extensive classes for artists of all levels, ages, interests and media. In 2004, the Tracy Art Center applied for and received federal non-profit status to ensure that it could perpetually contribute to the local arts community. With year-round classes for adults and children, over 75 members, gallery showings throughout the year and community events, its no wonder the Tracy Art Center has become the popular place to experience the arts across the Connecticut Shoreline.
