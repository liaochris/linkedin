Yo Le’s Summary

I believe everyone has the ability to be successful and happy. For some it's easy, for some they make it look easy, and for some...they will never reach that point. Insanity is doing the same thing over again and expecting a different result. I have learned my lessons in life. Recovering alcoholic, meaning I'm not afraid of challenges. I believe if you help people get what THEY want, you will ultimately get what YOU want. Always dream and shoot higher than you know you can reach. Don't bother just to be better than everyone else, be better than yourself.

I got into business for a couple of reasons. The first reason was to get out of the restaurant industry, where I spent my time building someone else's dream. I wanted my time back to do the things that I wanted to, which included giving back to the people that have given me so much.

Which leads me to my second reason... My Parents! They are the hardest working people that I know and I'm afraid that eventually they're going to work themselves to death. I want to be able to pay my parents to never work again.

To tell you a little about myself now, I am an aspiring personal trainer. I enjoy working out and I am a natural athlete. Music is the key to my soul and I can't live without it. I have always had the entrepreneurial spirit and a thirst for knowledge. Working for someone just never worked out for me.

Having been raised the way most people were raised, that is to go to school, get a good education, and then get a good paying job. I never realized that this type of opportunity existed. It sure does exist and it can be a reality for a lot of you. If you aren't happy where you are in life then stop and make some changes. Most people will not change until the pain of staying the same is greater then the pain of change. I've learned that the Yes's will build your business and the No's will build YOU. I accredit my success to my ability to stay positive and focused throughout the bumps in the road.
