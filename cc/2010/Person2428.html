Lou Stanasolovich, CFP’s Summary

I am the founder, Chief Executive Officer, and President of Legend Financial Advisors, Inc.® (Legend), a financial advisory firm providing fee-only Wealth Advisory Services, including Financial Planning and Investment Management strategies, to affluent and wealthy individuals as well as business entities and non-profit organizations.

I am also the editor of Risk-Controlled Investing, a monthly subscription-based service which guides financial advisors on how to build investment portfolios with lower risk, and a frequent provider of information to and source for the media on a variety of topics relating to financial planning and investing in the current markets.

Lou Stanasolovich, CFP’s Groups:

Co-founder of The Alpha Group, an exclusive association comprised of 17 senior investment advisors. All are principals of nationally recognized independent investment management firms throughout the United States. Collectively, they manage more than $4.0 billion of client investments. Member of National Association of Financial Advisors (NAPFA), the nation’s largest fee-only professional organization. Member of the Financial Planning Association (FPA). Elected to the International Association for Financial Planning (IAFP; a forerunner of the FPA) National Board of Directors for four consecutive one-year terms (1988-1992). Served as a member of the IAFP’s National Ethics Committee, Registry Committee, Education Committee, Investment Committee, and the Review Committee of The Registry of Financial Planning Practitioners. Former member of Certified Financial Planner Board of Standards Pass Score Committee.
