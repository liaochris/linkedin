Japman Bajaj’s Summary

I have a potentially unhealthy passion for entrepreneurship and entrepreneurs. I like to think of myself as a doer, first, while possessing above average abilities to think. I'd rather be getting my hand dirty than thinking about what kind of dirt to use in order to get my hands dirty.

My work experience started off in retail with the world's largest retail chain. I then had the fortune to work with a cutting edge, medium-sized technological firm in Ottawa's vibrant high-tech community, learning about RFID and mobile technologies.

Most recently, I have spent summers with a Canadian Government Crown Corporation in policy development and strategy.

In University, I got exposed to the SIFE program which has unleashed my passion for entrepreneurship and grassroots initiatives. I have managed multiple projects, and single-handedly created over $150,000 in economic opportunity in the Ottawa area in recession-plagued 2008.

As a passionate recent graduate, I have an incredible amount of energy, a fresh outlook, and a desire and craving to engage in worthwhile work and worthwhile projects, and the abilities and an above average track record of success. I am an enabler of dreams, and if I can help your organization in any way, let me know.
