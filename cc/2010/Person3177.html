Peter Kearney’s Summary

Peter's vision is a Creative and Harmonious Workplace & Society where all members of the team are Engaged and a Culture of Learning is of core importance to the organisation.

Peter has worked in Ireland and Europe in Training & HRM. Worked on a freelance basis with Daimler Chrysler and Banca Commerciale Italiane, training staff in Business English communication. Worked for Dorset College, Meteor Mobile Communications and ADS Ltd.

Peter has coordinated a number of business development projects such as:

o Managing and developing Performance Management and Recruitment & Selection processes through a Competency based approach.
o Learning Needs Analysis and organisational Training Programmes
o Mediation & Employee Engagement Projects
o Designing Induction programmes
o HR Policies and Procedures for the business
o Company Handbooks
o Employment Law consultation
o Contracts of employment
o Management of Redundancies in the north and south of Ireland.
o HR and Training quality programme
o Implementation and coordination of CIPD certificate courses

Has been a regular assessor and lecturer on CIPD, JEB, FETAC and ACCA courses covering such topics as Employment Law, Human Resources Management and Communications at Dorset College as well as on in-house training courses at ADS Ltd.

Holds a BA in HRM from the NCI and an MA in HRM & Industrial Relations from Keele University. He is a member of the Institute of Management Consultants & Advisers (IMCA) and holds membership of the IITD and the Irish Computer Society.

Peter Kearney’s Specialties:

Mediation of Employee Disputes & Conflict, Employee Engagement, Employment Cycle implementation, Return On Investment, Career Planning, HR Training for Supervisors & Managers, Redundancy & Restructuring and Employment Law
