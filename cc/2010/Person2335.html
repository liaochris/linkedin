Laura A. Davis’s Summary

I possess 15 plus years of leadership and team development training and coaching experience for Fortune 500 companies in a variety of industries. I have worked with people at all levels of an organization to help prepare them for the new world of work. I do this by assisting them in developing greater self-awareness and emotional intelligence as well as solid skills for leading and collaborating in today's complex, virtual, global, and rapidly changing workplace.

I also assist individuals in transition in understanding their life and career goals so that they can achieve personal as well as professional fulfillment and work-life balance in accordance with their values. Please visit my website at www.lauraadavis.com for more information and resources.
