Richard Gee’s Summary

I am an attorney with substantive law firm experience as well as in-house experience. My practice focuses on business, real property, internet advertising, new media, digital intellectual property, music, film and television transactions and litigation.

I am a native Spanish speaker, and can deliver legal services in both Spanish and English. For the last two decades, I have represented clients throughout the United States as well as Latin America, Asia and Europe with their legal needs.

For corporate counsel, I offer my experience as former in-house counsel as well as real property, corporate and intellectual property transactional and litigation legal services. Together with my legal colleagues, we can deliver experienced legal practitioners tailored to fit your transactional and litigation legal needs at a fraction of the cost of larger law firms.

For law firms, I welcome referrals and am comfortable working in co-counsel or of-counsel capacities. I have a no-poaching policy with respect to referrals of clients from attorneys needing specialized counsel.

Please feel free to contact me at info@gee4law.com if you should have any questions or need legal assistance.

Richard Gee’s Specialties:

Business: Corporate formations and dissolutions, including LLC’s, partnerships and limited partnerships; mergers, acquisitions and asset sales; employment agreements.

Real Property: Purchases and sales; commercial leasing; redevelopment; hospitality and resort industry contracts; and land use matters.

IP and Entertainment: Internet advertising, internet, domain name disputes, copyright, trademark, music, film, television, new media, digital media, interactive media and UDRP proceedings.
