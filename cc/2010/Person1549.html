Heimo Hänninen’s Summary

CAREER GOALS

A leading role such as Business Consultant or Information Architect position in a knowledgeable, versatile professional team. A position that will allow growth with the dynamic company in international field.

SUMMARY

Information processing professional with a solid technical background and creative attitude towards problem solving. Excellent analytical skills and strengths in methodology implementation. Extensive professional experience in the portal content area. Experienced in information modeling and process analysis for larger corporations.


KEY COMPETENCIES

• Skilled to analyze complex processes and business requirements for larger organizations and capable to describe them clearly to the management as well as the design team.

• My competence covers information architecture strategies and practices such as, user experience driven domain modeling and information management design for large information portals.

• I am familiar with EA methodologies. Togaf-8 certified, certificate: http://dyn.fi/658668988, Metis EA Frame Work, DoDAF, Nokia Unified Approach.

• I master the whole life cycle of information modeling, starting from use cases till formal schema design.

• Versatile experience on information systems design, integration technologies, and a deep knowledge of XML standards and technologies.

• Experienced in UML and familiar with object oriented analysis and design principles. Skilled to develop methodologies and transferring knowledge as part of learning process.

• Competent and creative in information analysis and data modeling. Successfully used XML, RDF and Topic Maps techniques in various metadata and schema architecture projects.

• Rehearsed in business case development. Enjoying working in multicultural environment and comfortable with working in North-American business culture.
