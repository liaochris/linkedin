Shari Weiss’s Summary

My future is in helping companies and people learn how to use social media strategies and tools to build communities. As a social media marketing trainer and strategist, I am looking for opportunities to work with businesses that want to leverage the power and resources offered by blogging, LinkedIn, Facebook, Twitter, Friendfeed, Ning, and a host of other social media platforms.

After more than 15 years as a university marketing professor, I have begun my own educational journey to learn and chronicle All Things Social Media on my blog SHARISAX IS OUT THERE [http://sharisax.com]. My focus is providing information that helps my readers learn the basic strategies and how to implement them on this revolutionary new communication landscape.

My work these days is my passion: As I create more and more content on various platforms including Wordpress, Facebook Fanpages, Ning communities, Twitter, Friendfeed, and Squidoo, I'm convinced that we are building more supportive, authentic, human communities through our technological advances and the subsequent engagement and interaction now becoming possible around the globe.
