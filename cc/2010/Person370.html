Mike Hunter’s Summary

betterlanguages.com Ltd. is one of the fastest growing translation agencies in the UK. We provide translation services to leading international brands including mothercare (For whom we are sole providers translating into 9 languages including Simplified Chinese and Arabic), Marks and Spencers, and Iceland Foods. We provide website translation and localisation, as well as translating online and offline PR. We are specialists in label translation and packaging translation. We think customer service is extremely important, and we work closely with our clients to deliver the best possible service.

Mike Hunter’s Specialties:

We translate in all major specialisms including website translation and localisation, legal translation, label and packaging translation, translation of instructions and user guides, business document translation, PR and marketing translation.
