Rich Drinon’s Summary

Rich Drinon helps emerging leaders develop necessary relationship, task and change management skills. This is accomplished through small group training and individual coaching utilizing instruction, dialog and learning exercises, including on camera role playing practice.

Over the past 22 years he's conducted thousands of training and coaching sessions for hundreds of organizations throughout North America. Rich's current clients are in the fields of: banking software, municipality management, surgical center management, funeral home management, broadcasting, gaming, construction, communications and association management.
