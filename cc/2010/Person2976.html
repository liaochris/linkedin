Francisco (Pakko) De La Torre-Rocha’s Summary

I'm a Creative Director/Art Director with Internet, Advertising, Broadcast & Print Production background with a strong passion for finding unique ways to reach consumers and solve difficult marketing problems.

I believe that there is no real difference between "traditional" and "digital". It's all about having a great idea and figuring out how it works everywhere. My specialties are in blending it all into one seamless experience.
