Are you an editor or corporation? I want to work with you.

I adore working with editors and corporations. I don't mind making revisions and changes based on client needs or the needs of the publication.

If you are an editor or corporation that is looking for a flexible, smart, funny writer with a marketing streak - contact me. I love giving corporations and editors what they need for their readers.

Wondering what makes me special?
