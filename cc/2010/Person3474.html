Samuel Valme, MBA’s Summary

Located at Fort McPherson Georgia, I am currently working for Serco-NA International under a government contract as an Army Career and Alumni Program (ACAP) counselor. The purpose of ACAP is to assist military service members transition from active military service to civilian life. This includes facilitating Resume, Interviewing and Federal Job Application seminars. Assist with resume writing and job hunting. Manage Employer Days and Job Fairs. Conduct weekly Pre-Separation Briefings on benefits that the Army offers to transitioners including relocation assistance, employment assistance, financial management assistance, health and life insurance, and reserve affiliation; as well as training on interview techniques, using the federal job application system.

I am always looking to connect with organizations willing to work with and hire Transitioning Military service members. If you would like more information on hiring transitioning service members feel free to email me (samvalme@gmail) for more information.

Feel free to contact with any questions about the Army Career and Alumni Program (ACAP). I am always happy to help.

I am happy to pass on requests to connect at any time as long as the receivers profile is open to connections.
