Anis TEBIB’s Summary

Thanks to a cross-cultural and rich background spent mainly in the automotive and industrial business, my goal is now to use these sales experiences to open the scope of my responsibilities by launching ANTIDHOT.

ANTIDHOT provides support for purchasing and production outsourcing, as well as business development.

We are acting in the fields of electronics, mechanics and tooling, mainly in the area of global industry, automotive and consumer goods.

ANTIDHOT will support you to:
- Optimize and drastically reduce your production and purchasing costs
- Support your abroad sales strategy (niches, local operations,...)

We offer you outsourcing facilities in the main growing countries (India, China) and are able to support you in France, Japan and North Africa.

Please feel free to contact us for any request: contact@antidhot.com
