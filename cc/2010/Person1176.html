Erica Moore-Burton, Esq.’s Summary

Erica Moore-Burton is an award winning recruiter that has worked successfully in the recruiting and legal field in the Los Angeles market since 1999. As the Executive Director of Special Counsel she oversees the operations for the Los Angeles office of Special Counsel which focuses on Attorney/Paralegal placement (both full-time and contract), and large scale document review projects.

Ms. Moore-Burton is a graduate of the Middlesex University School of Law in London, England and is a licensed US attorney. She is a member of the American Bar Association, Toastmasters International and the National Association of Female Executives (NAFE).
