Gerrit Heijkoop’s Summary

FIRST AID FOR EVENTS

Now is the time for creativity; no one can afford to miss any opportunities; relations become crucial. Now is the time to effectively use events as a manner of emphasising your strength, reliability and relationships with customers, partners and your workforce. However, now is not the time to have expensive talented event managers on your payroll as a fixed cost.

As a freelance event manager I can help you with:

- defining your event goals and strategy
- thinking about the ‘take-out’ of your event: what impression do you want make on your visitors?
- writing up a clear briefing to effectively explore the creativity that exists in the market
- setting up and leading your event’s project team (with both internal and external members)
- communication with the rest of your organisation
- making the event a unimagined success

Main strenghts are enthousiasm, execution power, analytics and communication.

Specific fields of intrest: Social Media, Events, Hospitality & Recreation, Mainports, Technology and Innovation.
