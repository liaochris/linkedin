Maximiliano De Muro’s Summary

Since the age of 22 and over the course of the last 7 years, Maximiliano has been start upping several successful companies.

His main achievements include "SX Networks" (Now Crea Labs - http://www.crealabs.com.uy - Software development company sold in 2008 to "Grupo Crea"), and "MediaGambling" (The biggest gaming traffic engine in the world, sold in 2009 to "CMedia" - http://www.cmedia.es)

Since 2008, he has been involved in several startups related to Lifestyle Management (http://www.mardulcelifestyle.com), and Health information (http://www.adnblogs.com).

Nowadays Maximiliano is the CEO of Mediterranean Gaming LTD (Malta) and a Member of the Board. Also in year 2009 he became the Strategic Advisor of "Grupo Crea" and "CMedia"

Maximiliano works an active member of several uruguayan associations such as AJE (http://www.aje.org.uy), ADM (http://www.adm.com.uy) and CUTI (http://www.cuti.org.uy), which are related to entrepreneurship and IT industry development respectively.

Furthermore, Maximiliano is an active speaker in several conferences about Entrepreneurship and Online Gaming all over the world.
