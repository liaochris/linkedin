Matt Pippen’s Summary

Matt Pippen, an 19-year-old student and entrepreneur, has been involved in various new media fields for nearly the past four years. Beginning his journey with his own personal blog and podcast in 2005 at the age of 14, he has ‘problogged’ for b5media and continues with MacFocus Magazine as well as freelancing. He is now actively involved in new media evangelization.

Matt is currently the President/CEO of Teen Media Productions, a digital media production company that produces and distributes several forms of teen-produced digital media on its sites. TMP is an entire community run by teens, for teens that is split into three different sections: new media networks, services, and in-house productions.

In addition to Teen Media Productions, Matt is an IT consultant for his own firm. He consults personal clients and many different scale companies that make anywhere from thousands of dollars per year to in upward of one million of dollars per year.

Matt attended Monahans High School in Monahans, TX where he graduated in 2009 as the Salutatorian of his class. He participated in cross country, band, one act play, UIL, FTA, student council, and speech/debate (UIL, TFA, and NFL). He currently attends Texas Tech University, studying mechanical engineering as a member of the Honors College. He is also a member of the Texas Tech debate team and the Texas Tech chapter of Engineers Without Borders.
