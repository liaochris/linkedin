Alvin Foo’s Summary

Alvin Foo is currently the Vice President, Asia of Velti PLC, a publicly listed global Mobile Marketing company based in the United Kingdom and Greece. Prior to this, he was the Head of China for Nokia Interactive, Nokia's advertising business unit and the Executive Director of Madhouse Inc, a leading Mobile Marketing company based in Shanghai, China. An internet and telecommunication expert with over 10 years experience. He primarily specialises in wireless, internet networking, and telecoms fields. His present focus are in the areas of digital media and mobile technology, especially the evolution in mobile marketing. He was formerly the co-founder and CEO of VCN , a broadband communication company based in Malaysia.

Alvin Foo is hoping to assist entrepreneurs and young startups in fulfilling their visions: raising funds, networking contacts, establishing the winning team, Business Development, PR and marketing.

Alvin Foo’s Specialties:

Start-ups, all stages of funding, IPO, corporate strategy, board of directors advisory, exit strategies, product line rationalization, building management teams, product marketing strategy, consulting for entrepreneurs and VCs, international business development and with specialisations in :

VoIP, Networking, Satellite technology and connectivity, Internet, Interactive Marketing, Mobile technology, Digital Media and mobile marketing.
