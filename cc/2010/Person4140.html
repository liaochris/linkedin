W.W. 'Bill' Daugherty’s Summary

30 years experience in mechanical and electrical design. Worked since 1979 in CAD environment. Have spent a majority of career in aerospace and military companies.

I am fully versed in Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing, Military Specifications and design for manufacturability. I have worked on very close toleranced systems (optics), aircraft structures (missile and airframe), commercial aircraft interiors and production tooling.

I have worked as an assistant CAD system administrator. I have served as a group lead, as a checker and as a mentor to less experienced colleagues.

Secret level DoD security clearance

40 years as a radio amateur (HAM) currently K4WWD
