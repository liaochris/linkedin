Sandra Smith-Doghmi’s Summary

I have been in the hospitality industry since 1991 enjoying several departments-front desk, concierge, Sales & Marketing and MOD. Since then, I have combined my experiences to form my new business Red Carpet Concierge of Chicago.com. Our new addition to our group is the Red Carpet Concierge Physician program. It is a preventive care system to encourage physical and mental fitness.

Prior to that I enjoyed a 25 year business as owner of a disposal system. During this time I also owned and operated a day spa for 20 years and owned and operated a private social club.

My community involvement in that span of time included producing and directing several worldwide pageant preliminaries, teen social clubs and president of my church altar guild.

Sandra Smith-Doghmi’s Specialties:

I connect business opportunities for growth and promotion. I am experiencing new avenues in the media and print business and look forward to watching this bloom.
