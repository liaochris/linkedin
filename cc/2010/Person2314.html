Kym Gordon Moore’s Interests:

Kym Gordon Moore is an award winning poet, author, syndicated columnist and freelance writer whose published credentials include hundreds of articles, essays, poetry, newsletters and various corporate reports and booklets. She has over twenty eight years of writing experience throughout her corporate career in industries from marketing, sales, public relations, fashion and special event coordination and has been a guest speaker for several civic, benevolent and business organizations. She is the President and Public Relations Administrator for Moore 2 It Productions, a firm dedicated to coordinating creative, strategic marketing packages for budget conscious new businesses and new published authors. She's completing works on two book projects scheduled to be released this year.
