Richard Nacht, Esq.’s Summary

Entrepreneurial strategist in diverse technology enterprises, innovating and driving initiatives for breakthrough team performance, profits, and savings.

Author, frequent speaker, educator and consultant on the topics of social media, social networking and new media.

• Demonstrate expertise in business development, operations direction, system management, program and project management, account management, fiscal oversight, and client satisfaction.

• Specialize in planning and execution of major initiatives pertinent to social media marketing and social networking.

• Customize technical solutions for wide-ranging product and service enterprises.

• Excel in product management to include invention, development, engineering, branding, multimedia marketing, launches, sales, and channel management.

• Secure and maximize key business relationships and strategic partnerships.

• Orchestrate internal and external entities to achievement of enterprise mission.

• Train, focus, and motivate teams for excellence in rapidly evolving environments.

• Identify and seize opportunities to expand market share and achieve continuous improvement.

Member Inc. 500 list of America’s Fastest Growing Private Companies,
Member of the Bar of the United States Supreme Court, Co-author of Realty Blogging: Build your Brand and Outsmart Your Competition.

Adjunct Professor in the Graduate Programs, Department of Leadership and Human Capital Management, New York University,
Senior Research Fellow and Chairman of the Education Committee of the Society for New Communications Research.

Holds a B.A. from the University of Pennsylvania, a Juris Doctor (J.D.) from the New York Law School, an Executive MBA degree from the New York University Stern School of Business and is a current doctoral candidate at the International School for Management in Paris, France.
