Pablo Izquierdo Lopez’s Summary

I am in the fourth and last year of an Engineering Doctorate with Surrey University on Environmental Technologies on integration of Energy from Waste technologies within the built environmnent. Therefore, I have indepth knowledge of Anaerobic Digestion, Gasification, Pyrolysis and Incineration technologies.

I have written several waste strategies for building developments ranging from the Tate Modern Museum in London, to a summer resort in Egypt and cities in United Arab Emirates and China.

I am particularly interested in all type of EfW information as well as data on waste generation and recycling and waste management within buildings.

Pablo Izquierdo Lopez’s Specialties:

Waste estimation for buildings and masterplans.
Technical knowledge of anaerobic digestion, gasification, pyrolysis and incineration technologies.
Economic assessment of energy from waste technologies.
Environmental assessment of EfW technologies and waste management systems using WRATE software (Waste LCA software from the UK Environment Agency)
Multi Criteria Analysis.
