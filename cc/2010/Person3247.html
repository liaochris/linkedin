Randi Harlev, Ph.D.’s Summary

Senior pedagogical professional in both commercial and academic environments with in-depth experience in e-learning design and both product and project management; focus on online ELT products (English Language Teaching). EFL teacher trainer and educator. Expert in organizational behavior with emphasis on intercultural teamwork. Outstanding interpersonal skills and strong belief in mentoring as an integral part of management.

Randi Harlev, Ph.D.’s Specialties:

e-learning design and development, instructional design, project management, product management, professional training and training development, ELT materials development, EFL/ESL teacher training and development, organizational development and change management
