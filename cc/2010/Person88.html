Alden Crissey’s Summary

Senior-level, tenured executive recruiter with experience delivering results for ENR Top 400 Commercial General Contractors by identifying and attracting passive candidates within construction management firms and development groups throughout the Eastern United States.

Responsible for co-leading Kaye/Bassman's East Coast Construction Team's new client development and strategic marketing efforts. Additionally, responsible for attracting and placing senior-management-level positions for some of the Eastern United States most well known and respected development groups and commercial builders.

My team has successfully placed professionals at all levels in diverse disciplines including Executives, Project Managers and Engineers, Superintendents, Real Estate & Commercial Development Managers in Construction, Operations, Business Development, Safety, Finance & Accounting and Specialty Services.

Founded in 1981, Kaye/Bassman is the largest single-site executive search firm in the nation, impacting companies and enhancing careers by providing the finest in professional, executive, technical and scientific search. We are ranked among the Top 10 U.S. Search Firms by Executive Search Review and have been named the Best Place to Work in Texas and the Dallas/Fort Worth area multiple times. We are recognized as an industry expert by The Wall Street Journal, Fortune, Business Week, USA Today, Fox, CNN and many others.
