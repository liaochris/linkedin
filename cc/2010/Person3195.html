Pradeep Chopra’s Summary

Pradeep is an entrepreneur, a Digital Marketing thought leader and an international speaker. Presently, he is a co-founder of Digital Vidya, a Digital Marketing learning place and Whizlabs Software, a global leader in IT certification preparation.

Till recently, he was one of the co-founders of OMLogic Consulting, one of the India’s leading Online Marketing companies. At OMLogic, Pradeep played an instrumental role in branding OMLogic as one of the most sought after firms for Online Marketing. Prior to OMLogic, Pradeep was a co-founder of Whizlabs Software, an IT education company, where he built a global brand (using online marketing) for which Whizlabs won the 'Most Innovative Indian IT Company' award from NASSCOM in 2004.

Online marketing is Pradeep’s passion & he is among the pioneers of online marketing in India. He is an international speaker & an author on online marketing & entrepreneurship. His previous speaking assignments include speaking at SES (USA), Global Entrepolis @ Singapore, NASSCOM India Leadership Forum, TiE and Web Innovation conferences.

Pradeep graduated from IIT Delhi & is also an active member of the SEMPO's (Search Engine Marketing Professional Organization) Global Committee. In his personal life, Pradeep enjoys juggling, swimming and loves travelling to not-so-common destinations. His obsession for internet has led him to believe that the internet connectivity is more important than the water supply :).
