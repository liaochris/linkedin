Mike Demler’s Summary

More than 25 years of semiconductor industry experience spanning marketing, sales and operations to R&D. Previous business development, strategic and product marketing positions include Synopsys, Nassda, Cadence, and Antrim. In my technical roles I have been a product development manager and IC designer for Micro Networks, Datel, GE Corporate R&D, and Texas Instruments.

As a strategic technology analyst, my focus is on emerging disruptive technologies in the wireless industry - from chips to 4G networks, and the rapidly developing Android ecosystem.

Along with an MBA from San Jose State University, I earned an MSEE from Southern Methodist University, and a BSEE from State University in Buffalo, New York. I am an inventor on eight U.S. patents and have authored the book High-Speed Analog to Digital Conversion, published by Academic Press.

GOALS: Opportunities in Strategic & Product Marketing, Product Management, Business Development, Market Research & Analysis.

Mike Demler’s Specialties:

Strategic marketing, business development, high-tech market analysis, forecasting, startups, new product roll out, wireless technology, WiMax, LTE, Android, analog/mixed-signal IC design, data converters, mobile television, Web 2.0 marketing, social media.
