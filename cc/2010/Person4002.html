Valorie Ford’s Summary

Hello and Welcome! As a real estate professional, I am dedicated and committed to being there through the entire process helping to walk you through the financing and real estate muck so you can get into or sell your home as quickly and stress free as possible! With over 5 years of real estate and mortgage experience, I have the tools to help you succeed in today’s tough market.
That being said, I don't want you to choose me based on my credentials alone. I would rather you choose me because you know from my past clients and testimonias that I will do a fantastic job and I am your "go to" agent for all of the latest real estate trends in Central Virginia!
In today's market, education is everything and I know that one size truly does not fit all. Downturns make my job more difficult, but I realize that what works selling one property, may not work for another. Until everything picks back up, you need every trick in the book to get your home sold or to get the best deal on a new home. Right now it is more important than ever to have someone that knows the ins and outs of Charlottesville real estate on your side and that is educated in the latest techniques. That is exactly why I want to provide the facts you need to know before you choose a real estate agent.
How do you size up the dozens of home choices? How are you going to get the BEST deals? Easy! For me, your results are what is important...not how many listings I have sold, buyers I have found homes for or open houses I have held. For this very reason, I would encourage you to give me a call and see what I can do for you!
