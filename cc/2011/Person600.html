Paul Stock's Summary

I'm a versatile editor, writer and researcher experienced in online editing and legal publishing with diverse interests in current affairs and popular culture. I'm currently a homepage producer at ninemsn where I program and commission stories for the network's key entry points.

As a former librarian, I bring my professional research and fact-checking skills to my editorial work, where I have demonstrated my ability to quickly come to terms with new subject matter and deliver professional copy for publication.

Editing; writing; bibliographic research; legal humour; legal editorial; current affairs; popular culture; film reviews; book reviews; bibliography; ephemera; metonymy; semiotics; national identity; popular memory.
