Matt Foster's Experience

Manager

ITN Consulting

Privately Held; 501-1000 employees; Broadcast Media industry

January 2009 – Present (3 years 1 month)

ITN was established in 1955 to produce a national and international news service for the UK’s first commerial televison channel, ITV.

Since 1982, ITN has also provided Channel 4 with all its news programming and has run a number of 24 -hour news chanels including the ITN News Channel, EuroNews and Setanta Sports News as well as producing ITV’s regional news service for London and operating the IRN radio news contract.

The company has built up a number of leading subsdidiary businesses including the world’s largest moving image archive and a productions business creating factual and multimedia content for broadcasters, online and mobile operators.

ITN Consulting combines this heritage and expertise with strategy consulting experience to deliver services to media companies worldwide.

Projects include channel turnarounds, market assessments, feasibility studies, regulatory support, business plan development and financial modelling. We also provide training and production support to broadcasters across the globe.

September 2007 – November 2008 (1 year 3 months)

Quickly established position as self-starter with strong client presence. Worked alone with high profile clients such as BT and Siemens and supported on projects across media, telecoms and sport including clients in the UK, Europe and the Middle East

• Assisted a consortium of local investors and a world-leading incumbent telecoms operator in bidding for the second fixed telecoms licence in Oman. The consortium won the bid, scoring first in all categories of adjudication.

• For Optima Telekom, analysed key market trends and conditions in Croatia and surrounding markets in preparation for the company’s Initial Public Offering

• For BT Openreach, the owner of “last-mile” fixed line services in the UK, conducted a wide-ranging review of their product development process, covering customer communication and organisational strategies. The project included around 100 interviews and recommendations for improvement across Openreach

• For Invitel, prepared a white paper for presentation to the Hungarian regulator supporting the introduction of a fourth mobile licence

• Conducted an assessment of the impact of changes to must-carry regulations applied to cable network operators
