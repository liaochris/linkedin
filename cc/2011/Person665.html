Rodney B. Murray's Summary

Dr. Murray is an educational technology executive, podcaster, pharmacologist, and an internationally recognized author and software developer. With 20 years of experience leading entrepreneurial software development teams in the higher education, healthcare and biotech sectors, he has brought innovative cutting edge solutions to clients. Using the latest technologies, he has helped organizations grow revenue by building online educational offerings, improving customer service, attracting and retaining talented staff, managing business partners, and expanding markets.
