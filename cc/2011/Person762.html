Todd Leiser, CFE's Summary

Experienced Digital Media professional and Franchise Sales/Management leader. I've had several leadership positions during 14+ years at Valpak. Past responsibilities include digital sales and online coupon strategy for Valpak's Owned & Operated franchise locations, development of online & offline strategic sales initiatives for all of Valpak's National and Network sales channels as well as a Founder & General Manager of Valpak.com®, the familiar blue envelope's online coupon consumer-facing website.

I've always enjoyed being an early adopter (LinkedIn member #113215)and inspiring innovation within my team. In addition to launching Valpak's original Digital (distribution) Network and Business Profile Page products, I alo developed the initial deployment of Valpak's response-based call tracking product for small businesses which has become a very successful & profitable initiative for the Valpak franchise network.

I am a Certified Franchise Executive, achieved by completing a comprehensive course of study in franchise management offered by the Institute of Certified Franchise Executives, the academic branch of the International Franchise Association’s (IFA) Educational Foundation. I am an active member of the IFA, currently serving as the Vice Chairman of the Marketing & Public Relations Committee as well as serving on the IFA Convention Planning Committee, and have spoken at IFA-sponsored education series and been published in the IFA's monthly magazine "Franchise World" - (December 2010). I am also a Certified Radio Marketing Consultant, a designation I earned when in the radio industry.

Valpak is America's Leader in local cooperative Direct Mail for over 40 years and reaches nearly 45 million households in more than 120 markets in North America every month. Valpak is a subsidiary of Cox Enterprises and part of the Cox Media Group.
