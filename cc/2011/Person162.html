Catherine S. Read's Summary

My greatest strengths and passion are working with businesses, professionals, non-profits and other organizations in leveraging the power of social media tools in marketing and business development. I focus my efforts on working with organizations in a variety of industries to craft a unique strategy and implementation plan that works for that specific enterprise. I provide one-on-one consulting services, hands-on group workshops, seminars customized to a specific agenda, and keynote presentations.

It's important to help organizations harness the power of online social media tools and to integrate those efforts into an overall cohesive marketing strategy. Using social media marketing strategies is imperative to pushing the reach of other initiatives already being successfully employed. I bring my skills to the collective wisdom of a group to emerge with a one-of-a-kind business plan that meets the needs of the organization and draws on the strength of the existing culture.
