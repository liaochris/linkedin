Joe Mannion's Summary

Joe consults in the International Sales and Marketing of industrial products (e.g. Aerospace, Power & Telecoms), as well as f.m.c.g., consumer durable goods. Working throughout Europe and the United States, and as far as Taiwan & China he has also covered Australia and South America.

Over the last 20 years, and prior to setting up EuroLink in 1997, Joe has held a series of demanding and challenging senior international management positions. Responsibilities have included: negotiating with major industrial groups & retailers throughout Europe; (re)constructing and managing sales organisations; developing marketing strategies and business plans; working with state aid agencies; new product development with international teams from Europe, the States and the Far East; market research; developing multi-country logistics bases; customer and sales staff training.

A graduate of Trinity College Dublin, he was awarded an honours B.A. Moderatorship degree in Modern Languages (German & French) with distinction for his spoken fluency, and later also received a Masters degree from here. He is also a graduate of the Marketing Institute of Ireland with whom he undertook the Graduateship degree in Marketing, combining his studies with his successful international marketing career. Joe is fluent in German, French and Spanish, and is at ease conducting business in all these languages. Having lived and worked in the UK, France, Germany, Spain & Portugal, he can combine a deep understanding of cultural and business differences with his expert knowledge of and contacts in the European retail environment.

He is a past Chairman of the Marketing Institute of Ireland’s southern region. He is also a past Chairman of a trade body representing exporting North American manufacturers, and continues Board level involvement.
