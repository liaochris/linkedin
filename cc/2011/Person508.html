Matthew Everitt's Summary

I believe work should be a place people can come to grow, have fun, and do great things. My focus as a leader has largely been on shaping cultures to deliver on that belief.

Through my firm, Reach SL, I serve as CFO and advisor to emerging companies including Fort Ashford Holdings, a private equity firm that develops world class companies through the application of Deming management principles. Systems thinking and statistical process control are among the core tenants of the company's management philosophy. In my role, I oversee the company's finance organization, treasury and risk management activities, controller functions, information systems, and taxes. I also serve as an advisor and mentor to leaders in subsidiary companies, helping to guide corporate strategy and development.

Throughout my career, I have been viewed as a trusted advisor and transformative leader who helps companies make money, not just someone who knows how much money has been made. Prior to joining Fort Ashford Holdings, I served as a senior finance executive for software, marketing, communications, and event production firms, including five years as Vice President of Finance and Administration for PainePR, another process-driven, humanistic enterprise inspired by Deming's ideas. While serving as Vice President of Finance and Administration for Vision Entertainment, I was instrumental in growing EBITDA by 185% within 12 months and consummating the sale to a large private equity firm.

My educational credentials include a master’s degree in accounting and financial management as well as a BSBA degree in business economics, entrepreneurship, and finance. I hold designations as a Certified Management Accountant (CMA) and Certified Financial Manager (CFM).
