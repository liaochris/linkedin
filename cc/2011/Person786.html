Victor Welling's Summary

An autodidact in software development, through experience I've learned what works as well as what does not. I love finding simple, elegant solutions and weeding out complexity. I'm at my best in an architectural role, working on concepts that solve actual problems, not just the hypothetical ones (the "architecture astronaut", for the readers of Joel on Software) and I'm constantly on the lookout for ways to improve the way my colleagues and I develop software; increasing productivity, quality and programmer happiness by adopting better tools and technologies and by fighting hard to reduce our technical debt. This is by no means to say I don't like to get my hands dirty and code away on cool new features or debugging some mysterious bug.

Specialties

Web development, software architecture, search engine optimization. Although I don't really like to limit myself to any particular language or technology, here's the mandatory list of acronyms: PHP, SQL, JS, CSS, (X)HTML, XML, REST.
