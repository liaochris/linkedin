Cedric Colpaert's Summary

Cedric is a high performing technology consultant and SAS certified developer with more than 6 years of proven success. His combination of both business/economics and technology has offered him a unique perspective to bring to his clients. When implementing technology solutions he always keeps in mind that each solution should generate strategic business value for the customer.

Cedric studied management and computer science at Hogeschool Gent. In parallel, he continued to build his foundation across rich internet technologies and offered freelance consulting services in this domain. Cedric combined his theoretical knowledge and hands-on experience in his thesis on rich internet applications.

After his graduation in 2008 as master in management and information technology, he followed an intensive one month training at SAS Institute. He believes attending this training resulted in a strong foundation on which he can still rely upon in the upcoming years.

In October 2008 Cedric joined Business & Decision, a SAS alliance partner, as a business intelligence consultant focusing on risk management solutions. He continued to develop his skills in all aspects of the business intelligence area: data collection, data storage, reporting and analytics.

In February 2011 Cedric was recruited by SAS Institute to strengthen the customer intelligence team. He has already proven to be a strong asset in areas that combine the SAS framework and internet technologies such as SAS Social Media Analytics and SAS Web Analytics. Driven to master the entire customer intelligence suite, he is also focusing on solutions such as SAS Marketing Automation and SAS Digital Marketing.
