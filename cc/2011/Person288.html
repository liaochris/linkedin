Hans Nipshagen's Summary

Hans is sales manager at Akamai Technologies with responsibility for the Benelux region. Prior to joining Akamai, Hans was sales director enterprise markets at Verizon, sales director in Fiberlink’s international sales operations, sales director at UUNET Netherlands and global account director at UUNET/CompuServe’s global account organisation.

He specialised in the management of large global sales engagements at multinational accounts, combining his in-depth knowledge of people management and sales management processes with his business education and his IT, security and network service provider industry experience.
