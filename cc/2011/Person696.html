J Schwan's Summary

I am the Founder and President of Solstice Consulting and Bottega Sol corporations.

Solstice is Chicago's leading enterprise mobility consulting firm. Solstice's holistic set of mobility services provides forward-thinking organizations with mobile strategy, mobile development, mobile security and infrastructure services. Solstice has been recognized by Consulting Magazine as one of America's Seven Small Jewels and has made the Inc 500|5000 Fastest Growing Companies list for the past 2 years.

Bottega Sol is the product development arm of Solstice Consulting. We've created a set of Mobile as a Service solutions for businesses and individuals that make their lives more efficient and improves interaction with their customers.

In addition to helping our teams build great products for our customers, I enjoy speaking about emerging technology topics at many educational institutions, technology conferences and focus groups.

Specialties

Mobile Strategy, Mobile Application Development, Cloud Computing, Social Media, Enterprise Architecture, Agile Development, Lean IT, Technology Resource Acquisition, Technology Consulting, Technology Strategy, Web 2.0, Software Development
