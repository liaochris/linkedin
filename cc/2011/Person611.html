Peter Hanschke's Summary

I am a seasoned product management / marketing professional who is passionate about bringing products to market that meet the needs of the market. Being a Product Manager at various levels for over 15 years, I bring a wealth of technical and business experience to the challenge of managing products in large, medium and small companies. By applying my key strengths - analysis, planning and implementation - I have achieved a successful track record of bringing products to market and managing existing products that meet customer and market needs while adhering to corporate objectives.

Specialties

* Introduction of new products and expansion of existing products into new markets
* Transformation from a services-oriented revenue model to a product model
* Definition and implementation of processes for SaaS and on-Premise delivery models
* Management of the entire product lifecycle - from concept to delivery
* Product/release planning
* Elicitation, prioritization and translation of customer needs into product requirements
* Training and mentoring of product managers
