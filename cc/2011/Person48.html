Andrey Golub's Summary

As a graduate of Applied Mathematics/ Software Engineering at the Polytechnic University of Minsk/Belarus (1998), Andrey Golub went on to earn a PhD in Systems Analysis and Design for Innovative Technologies from the Minsk Polytechnic Academy (2001). His first job was as a Software Engineer at the Europe’s largest software development company, EPAM Systems, where he was quickly promoted to the position of Analyst and Project Manager. At the invitation of the ITC’s Center for Scientific and Technological Research of Trento- IRST (now FBK), one of Europe’s foremost institutions for Artificial Intelligence, Golub moved to Italy in 2002 and worked there as a Sr. Systems Analyst and Researcher.
In 2004 he moved to Milan, where he worked in the high-tech, telecommunications, internet and retail sectors, focusing on technological start-ups, IT departments of leading companies, always within or next to R&D teams. In 2008, he joined the leading mobile digital entertainment provider Zero9 Group as Business Intelligence Manager, then served to Sicuritalia Group, Italy’s leading Security and Investigation Operator, as R&D Manager for Innovation in B2C new products line.

Since 2009, Golub works as an independent strategy consultant, R&D adviser and entrepreneur for high-tech projects, supported by a reliable technological team in Minsk/ Belarus. His interests center on new technology, new media and social computing projects as well as his passion for the web 2.0 marketing and communication.

Andrey Golub has been assigned in 2008 and 2010 such important recognition as "Who's Who in the World" from Marquis Who's Who committee.
