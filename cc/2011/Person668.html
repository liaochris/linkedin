Matt Stone's Summary

I'm an experienced sales and marketing professional who enjoyed a great career with SAAB, Toyota and Rolls-Royce & Bentley, through to the late '90s.

That's when the entrepreneurial gene kicked in and I headed to the Manchester United start-up, MUTV - the first ever daily, digital TV Channel.

Having discovered digital marketing at Bentley in the 90's, the time was right in 2002 to set up ROI - a digital marketing consultancy. The last decade has been even more rewarding than my first Corporate career.

I'm fortunate to have worked with some great start-up brands, including Amazon, Net-a-Porter, Zopa and ingenie - my desire to innovate is as stong as ever.

The common thread running through my business life, is a relentless desire to achieve the highest possible ROI for the brands I'm involved with.

When I'm not working, you'll most likely find me with my family, on the tennis court or at White Hart Lane watching Harry Redknapp's blue and white army.
