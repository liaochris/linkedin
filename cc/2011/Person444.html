Dan Kurani's Experience

Founder and CEO

Thumb

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Internet industry

May 2010 – Present (1 year 9 months) New York

Thumb (aka Opinionaided) is a utility-based social network that helps people get instant opinions. The company and service has been featured by CNN, TechCrunch, VentureBeat, Ad Week, and many others as one of the first social networks with a core value proposition. The service is growing quickly, is regularly compared to a structured version of Twitter, and has engagement numbers on par with Facebook and Angry Birds.

CEO

Kurani Interactive

December 2000 – September 2010 (9 years 10 months)

Award-winning interactive agency that delivered UX design for companies that included Nike, Mannington, Universal, and a number of successful digital startups.

COO
