Tony Marino's Summary

Our top priority is 150% "magical and delightful" client satisfaction. We make each and every client the "star of the show." It is about "them" and NOT what's in it for us.

Every new client comes our way in an all-out effect to:

1. Achieve a goal,
2. Solve a problem, or
3. Satisfy a need.

We only accept new work from clients to whom we know we can deliver at full capacity with nothing missing and nothing broken. Our power comes from pouring out a firm foundation of reality along with notable time lines and benchmarks so that every valuable client never experiences the "surprise factor." Every client knows what they are to receive and when and precisely what it will cost long before we ever sign a final assignment agreement.

Tony Marino is the Founder and Senior Consultant of Trinity Web Works, Senior VP at SmartGuy.com, the Executive Producer and host of the Marketing Antics Live Radio Show, Founder of the Audio Video Streams, the International ePublisher's Association, Christian Times eBusiness Newsletter, and the author of the ePublishing Master's Course. Additionally, he holds Email Compliance Officer status for many of today's leading Network Marketing companies.

He has also worked with the likes of legendary Direct Marketers Ted Nicholas and Gary Halbert. Best-Selling Authors, Harvey McKay, Vic Conant, Jack Canfield and Mark Victor Hansen. ABC Television's, Jimmy Kimmel and NBC's, Carson Daly. Online Marketers, Dale Calvert, Alex Mandossian, Shawn Casey, Cory Rudl, Mark Joyner and Jay Abraham. He has represented and marketed products for Microsoft, Herbalife, Office Depot, Ford Motor Company, American Honda and Vonage. His offices are located in Los Angeles and Portland.
