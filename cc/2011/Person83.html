Blaed Spence's Summary

Blaed Spence's Experience

Principal

4word

2000 – Present (12 years)

At  *4word*, we’re passionate about storytelling. Whether we’re re-invigorating brand messaging, enlivening taglines, developing Web content, crafting romance copy, doctoring scripts, or creating collateral that sings, we’re dedicated to telling a story that engages your audience and leaps off the page or screen.

Whether devising Big Ideas or a subtle hook, we develop content that’s memorable, relevant, and refreshing — on time, and on budget.

*4word* is the creative brainchild of Blaed Spence and Colin Berry. We each bring 15 years of heavy-hitting editorial experience to the table. Our clients range from blue-chip to boutique, and our expertise spans a host of specialties, including: brand-based storytelling; marketing collateral and tagline development; package and product romance copy; website and interactive content; concept, character, and story arc development; shooting scripts for video games, sizzle videos, and industrial films; and much more.

We’re as comfortable with creative properties as we are with corporate directives. We’re as adept at writing as we are at editing. And we're masters of creative problem solving, brainstorming, and ideation.

*4word thinking* , the creative consulting arm of our business, offers conceptual brainstorming and strategizing expertise to ideation firms, think-tanks, agencies, corporations, and production companies. As project-based team members, we bring rich experience, fresh perspective, and singular creative energy to any team. We’ve helped develop new products, and create strategies, processes, and possibilities for a host of Fortune 100 companies.

Overall, we’re creative powerhouses. Executives of the imagination. Word nerds. Your secret weapon.

Contact our nerve-center, *4word space* at 323.662.2801 and let us move you 4word.
