Dave Katz's Summary

Veteran television sports production pro.
Pioneered the score strip for live TV sports in 1994, first to display in-game lower thirds off the score strip in 2003, introduced the video transition for highlights and replays and the walk-up tombstone for baseball, hockey and basketball graphics.

Specialties

Font coordinator extraordinaire, producer, associate director, researcher, writer and Chyron Duet operator.
