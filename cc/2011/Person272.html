Gwenaël Le Bodic's Summary

I am directing Greencopper, a company I founded in 2009. Greencopper offers innovative event and cultural apps for smartphones and is leading this field with the publication of apps for 60+ events, 100+ apps for iPhone, Android, BlackBerry). Greencopper has local offices in North America (Montreal, Canada) and Europe (France).

In the past, I worked for several telecommunications companies and was in charge of building large-scale telecom-grade solutions in the domain of mobile messaging. I authored several reference books as an outcome of these activities.

I occasionally act as expert witness in the domain of mobile communications.
