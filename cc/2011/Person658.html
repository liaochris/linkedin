Roberto M. Cabral's Summary

Roberto M. Cabral is a second-year participant in the Edison Engineering Development Program (EEDP) at GE Healthcare, which he started working for in July 2010. He is currently on his third rotation at the NGB site in Troy, NY. Prior to joining GE, he worked at Raytheon as a software engineer for over two years.

He is a graduate of Northeastern University with a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Engineering (2008), a Graduate Certificate from the Gordon Engineering Leadership Program (2009) and a Master of Science in Electrical and Computer Engineering Leadership (2010).
