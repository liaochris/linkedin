Paul Ratazzi is a Principal Engineer at the Air Force Research Laboratory in Rome, New York where he is the Technical Advisor to the Cyber Defense and Cyber Science Branches. Besides his broad interest in the field of cyber operations technology, his specific technical interests include physical layer techniques for threat avoidance in wireless devices, moving target networking, and cyber deterrence. Mr. Ratazzi is a Senior Member of the IEEE, Past Chair of the Mohawk Valley Section of the IEEE, and Chair of the Mohawk Valley’s joint Antennas and Propagation/Microwave Theory and Techniques Society Chapter.
