Debbie Nixon's Summary

Debbie Nixon is known for her innovative people solutions that deliver bottom-line performance results. Her ability to engage cross-functional leaders and subject matter experts produces solutions aligned with business goals and core values.

Debbie's HR and organizational development expertise helps employers make necessary changes to remain successful in challenging markets.

She uses her instructional and curriculum design skills to create blended learning solutions that fit the organization's training needs. Debbie has designed and delivered web-based, multi-media, and traditional training programs that incorporate ADDIE principles and other adult learning principles.

A skillful presenter, Debbie is sought-after by business associations and companies to speak on relevant topics such as: Growing a Top Business in the "New Normal" and "More Revenue, Less Work" She is a published e-zine expert contributor to financial publications (www.horsesmouth, www.senior advisor), e-book and blog author.

Debbie's ability to help clients adapt successfully in changing conditions is the common denominator that made her sought after by Fortune 500 companies and solo-preneur businesses alike.

Debbie holds PCC-level and NCC certifications through the International Coach Federation and Newfield Networks. She holds a MS in Human Resource Economics.

Debbie brings her love of learning, NY humor, compassion and laser-like focus to help her clients get the best results from her work.

She currently lives with her husband Mark in Clearwater where they enjoy its year-round warmth and outdoor lifestyle. Mark's wonderful son, Josh, lives in L.A. as a theatre and film professional.
