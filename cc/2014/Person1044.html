Dr. Veronica Anderson's Summary

***connect with me by sending an email to veronicaandersonmd@gmail.com or VAnderson@AmericanCarExporters.com***

I am an entrepreneurial spirit with a love for travel. That lead to American Car Exporters. I use my diagnostic skills in a very different way to be able to deliver high quality vehicles of all types to the global community. ACE now has staff in Benin, Cote d'Ivoire, Kenya, Ghana, Philippines, India, Pakistan, Bosnia and Herzegovina , Tunisia and Mauritania.

I am still passionate about improving wellness, as well as helping businesses and medical practices manage their digital strategies through my Wellness For The REAL World and Rich Doctor Coaching programs. Through Rich Doctor Coaching, I provide healthcare consulting to practitioners looking to transform their practices into profitable businesses through extensive branding and social media marketing. I also offer valuable advice from expert guests such as Celebrity Apprentice star and right hand man to Donald Trump, George Ross. For more information on the Rich Doctor Coaching program, visit www.richdoctorcoaching.com.

As host of the talk radio show Wellness for the Real World with Dr. Veronica, I explore life from the intersection of health, politics and pop culture. Streamed on Blog Talk RadioI have appeared on a number of news and commentary programs, including Nancy Grace (CNN), Our World with Neil Cavuto (Fox News Channel), and Live with Adam Carolla. I am also a blogger for The Huffington Post.

Dr. Veronica Anderson's Experience

Chief Executive Officer

American Car Exporters

December 2012 – Present (1 year 11 months) New York, NY

American Car Exporters provides quality pre-owned vehicles to the global community. As fully licensed vehicle dealers, ACE provides cars, fleets, trucks, buses, vans and heavy equipment. We carefully select, inspect and recondition prior to shipping to provide our clients peace of mind with their purchases. We also can provide independent certifications, when requested. ACE only provides "clean" vehicles. No salvage, accident or branded title vehicles. American Car Exporters serves corporations, governments and institutions as well as individuals interested in assuring that they receive a quality vehicle wherever they may be in the world.

Besides in the USA, we have a site in Cotonou, Benin and will be opening another site in Abidjan, Cote d'Ivoire in 2014. We do export to any port in the world.
