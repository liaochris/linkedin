David Fraga's Summary

Vice President of Corporate Development at Shutterstock (NYSE: SSTK), leading the company's M&A, business development and strategic growth initiatives. Shutterstock is a global marketplace for imagery. We license more commercial images each year than any other company in the world.

Prior to Shutterstock, worked at Insight Venture Partners, a $5 billion private equity and venture capital firm focused on software and Internet investments. Executed investments, including in Twitter and eVestment Alliance, which together represented over $172 million of transaction value.

Board member at TechiesGiveBack and Co-founder of LendforPeace.org, an international microfinance non-profit focused on the Middle East and funded by the Clinton Global Initiative.

Specialties: Corporate Development, Business Development, Strategic Partnerships, M&A, Strategic Planning, Business Analysis, Pricing Strategy, Affiliate Programs, Product Management, Venture Capital, Private Equity, Deal Sourcing, Financial Modeling, Deal Structuring, Due Diligence, Nonprofits, Internet, Startups, Digital Media
