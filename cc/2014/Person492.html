Projects

World's Coolest Offices(Link)

November 2012 – December 2012

For this project at Inc., I researched how other brands were engaging their users on Facebook. It just seemed like pushing articles to our readers on a platform not built for heavy information consumption was an inadequate strategy. After identifying Napkinlabs as an ideal partner, we decided to use their platform built on the Facebook API to hold a World's Coolest Offices Contest that would be fun and engaging and supplemental to the Editorial Package living on our website. The results were great and it gave Inc. a working case study on how to co-create social content by engaging our FB audience in a manner that enhanced their time spent on that platform. See the case study on Napkinlabs.
