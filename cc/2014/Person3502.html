Jim Racheff's Experience

Chairman and CEO

DMS, Inc.

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Information Technology and Services industry

October 2008 – Present (6 years) Frederick, MD

At DMS, Inc. we apply our expertise in information and management sciences to help other organizations further their goals and success. We do so by working closely and collaboratively with our clients, developing individual solutions to meet their unique needs. DMS is an award-winning organization with more than 30 years experience working with researchers and physicians to further their efforts. DMS (and its spin-offs) may be the only small business to ever serve as an administrator of one the U.S. Government's 39 Federally Funded Research and Development Centers (FFRDC).

As Chairman and CEO responsible for: facilitating the success of the organization and - by extension - it's clients and partners; shaping and executing the organization's strategic vision; cultivating an environment that fosters collaboration, innovation and continual learning among highly-qualified professionals.

Principal Manager

NCI at Frederick & Frederick National Laboratory for Cancer Research

Government Agency; 1001-5000 employees; Research industry

1999 – Present (15 years) Frederick, MD

The National Cancer Institute at Frederick (NCI at Frederick) is a composite of Frederick National Laboratory for Cancer Research, NCI Center for Cancer Research (CCR) intramural labs that comprise almost half of CCR's Principal Investigators, several other NCI divisional programs and the NCI Campus at Frederick. The facilities also host and support several programs of the National Institute for Allergies and Infectious Diseases (NIAID) and the NCI at Frederick is is a founding partner of the National Interagency Confederation for Biological Research (NICBR).

Frederick National Laboratory for Cancer Research is a Federally Funded Research and Development Center (FFRDC), functions as a Government Owned Contractor Operated (GOCO) facility, and is the only National Laboratory of the Department of Health and Human Services.

Computer & Statistical Services (C&SS) is responsible for providing a wide array of information science related services to the diverse community of researchers from the NCI, Frederick National Laboratory and NIAID. C&SS is one of two peer contracts that provide service and support to, and operate these facilities for, the Department of Health and Human Services.

As Principal Manager, responsible for all aspects of C&SS including strategic planning, operational oversight, budgeting and resourcing, developing and instituting governance mechanisms, and coordinating with other facility executive management in delivering high-quality/high-availability information systems and services across the enterprise.
