Nathan Armstrong's Summary

I have been blessed to have the opportunity to be a part of producing over 150 live and studio events in a wide spectrum of venues (educational, religious, theater, corporate, studio) including the Best Buy Theater in Times Square, the El Rey Theater in Los Angeles, and the Daytona International Speedway. More importantly, I have benefitted from industry leading experts like the professionals at the Motor Racing Network, music producer Michael Omartian, the staff at the Grammy Foundation, and many others, who have been willing to share their knowledge and life experience with me as we have worked together.

From producing live events, to editing video, to producing music, I strive to understand and develop the ever-changing intersection between music and technology. I devote my free time to learning, reading, promoting the njarmstrong Projects (@njaProjects) artists, running my production business, producing live events and working in the digital imaging department at Walt Disney World.

In February of 2014, I connected with an old Elementary school friend that had begun to do local music gigs around Memphis. He was looking for help in getting his music seen by the world. Due to my social media reach and networks in the music industry, I began to help Jeffery Jordan (@Jeffery_Jordan) grow his social media footprint, book gigs, and get into a studio to record. By July of 2014, Jeffery had 30,000 Twitter followers, thousands of views on YouTube, and a schedule to tour at 5 mid-south state colleges several times in early Fall of 2014. Out of this success, I began njarmstrong Projects (@njaProjects) to help other talented artists grow their footprint and let their lyrics and voice be heard.

Despite an often busy schedule you can usually find me @ the intersection of music and technology.
