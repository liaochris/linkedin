Responsibilities have included technology issues, financial requirements (profit and cost), and all contractual and business obligations.
• Technology Evaluation – working with non-technical customers to define needs, evaluating technology including likely future developments to ensure safe, cost effective, and as non-disruptive a project implementation as was possible.
