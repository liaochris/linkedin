Debi Silber, MS, RD, WHC, FDN The Mojo Coach®'s Summary

I'm Debi Silber MS, RD, WHC, FDN The Mojo Coach®, President of Lifestyle Fitness, Inc. and founder of www.TheMojoCoach.com. As a recognized expert in nutritional counseling, weight management, weight control, wellness coaching, fitness training, stress management, health, behavior change, personal development and more, I help others get to their physical, mental and emotional best so they create the personal and professional success they want most.

I'm also a motivational speaker, spokesperson and author of 2 books recommended by Brian Tracy, Marshall Goldsmith, Jack Canfield and many more. My clients branded me as The Mojo Coach® because I've helped thousands of others to achieve their ultimate body, mind, image and lifestyle; inspiring them to "get their mojo back" and help them transform into their personal and professional best. Through my proven 5 step system, I show others how to finally create a lean, fit body, radiant health, soaring confidence, endless energy, rewarding relationships, a dynamic image, charismatic style, and a rewarding, fulfilling lifestyle with lasting results. I also helped many companies boost morale, productivity, loyalty…as well as their bottom line, by inspiring employees towards making small yet dramatic lifestyle changes.

To help clients lose weight while improving their health and energy, I also created deliciously healthy Mojo Fuel Bars™, hold inspirational business/wellness events, retreats, test clients (upon request) for stress related illnesses/conditions while offering a protocol to heal, have appeared on/contributed to The Dr. Oz show, Shape, Self, Working Mother, Psychology Today, WebMD, Yahoo Shine, MSN, Ladies Home Journal and Glamour to name a few.

If you're eager to look/feel/perform better, implement a customized weight loss program, exercise program, move past limiting beliefs, create/implement a corporate wellness program or have me speak at your next event, email us at Debi@TheMojoCoach.com.
