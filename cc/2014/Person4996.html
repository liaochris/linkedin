Sharon McRill's Summary

The Betty Brigade is a full-service concierge company based in Ann Arbor, Michigan. Our services include organizing, packing, unpacking, selling antiques and other household items, party and event planning, gift shopping, pet care, and other household projects.

If you are overwhelmed by all you have to do, feel like you're being buried in paperwork, or just need some extra help now and then, call The Betty Brigade.
We can help you get your home, office, and life back in control.

We'll get your errands done, sort out your filing system, and even walk the dog when you can't... so you'll have more time to spend with family and friends.

Specialties: Efficiency experts, organizing your home or office, party and event planning, moving coordination. Visit us at www.bettybrigade.com for details.
