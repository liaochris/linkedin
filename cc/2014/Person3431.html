Selected by his peers for inclusion in The Best Lawyers in America 2011, 2012, 2013, 2014 and 2015 "Personal Injury Litigation"

Selected by the American Trial Lawyers Association as one of the top 100 Trial Lawyers in State of Illinois.

Selected by peers as one of the Leading Attorneys in Illinois for the past eight years.

Selected by peers as a Super Lawyer in Illinois for the past eight years.

Selected by Chicago Lawyer Magazine as one of the Top Plaintiff's Personal Injury Attorneys.

Taught a Deposition Practicum Course at John Marshall Law School

Illinois Institute of Continuing Education's Most Valuable Volunteer Given to Top Instructor in CLE Courses (2008)

Taught Trial Advocacy at Northwestern Law School.

Taught Trial Advocacy and Medical Malpractice Law at DePaul University College of Law.

AV rated by Martindale-Hubbell.

Lectured to lawyers and various bar associations throughout the country on over 135 occasions.

Published over 70 articles for lawyers, most relating to tort litigation or trial work.

Currently author a column for the Chicago Daily Law Bulletin,

I have taken cases to verdict or have settled cases throughout Chicago, the State and other parts of the country in the areas of Transportation Negligence (automobile accidents ,pedestrians struck by cars or trucks, bicyclists struck by cars, trucks and buses, bus accidents, taxicab accidents, truck collisions, train accidents, train collisions, train derailments, L train accidents and L train derailments), Premises Liability, School Liability, Bullying and Hazing, Dog bites, Explosions, work place injuries, Medical Malpractice and a variety of Mass Tort cases.
