Gary Ng's Summary

Do you want to grow your profits by 100%?

E-Web Marketing can get you there.

Our digital marketing team is passionate about identifying and executing opportunities for any business to yield immediate increases in revenue.

We succeed by using marketing expertise that targets profit.

The strategy we developed is called the 7 Profit Triggers. It’s a structured approach using a proven multiplier-effect and it has helped our clients earn millions of dollars.

Initially my personal ambition was inwardly focused – to become a millionaire. However, over years of learning and personal growth, different goals became dearer to my heart.

My new objectives became the E-Web Marketing vision:

‘We bring Happiness, Success and Fun to everyone we encounter. We are E-Web Marketing.’

Our simple aim is to spread ‘HSF’ to as many people and companies as we can, by helping to maximise their profits.

At E-Web marketing, we believe that work doesn't have to be something to be endured - work is something all of us should enjoy.

If you resonate with the values I stand for, I would love to connect with you!
