Scott Jones, CRPC®, RFC®'s Summary

Our firm is a leading wealth advisory firm that helps individuals and hard-working families with building comprehensive financial plans that help grow and preserve wealth, protect assets, generate income and align with your values and lifestyle goals. Integrity and confidentiality are the cornerstones of our firm. As independent advisors, we provide unbiased recommendations & solutions to our clients without the pressure to sell proprietary products.

• Obtained the Chartered Retirement Planning Designations from the College for Financial Planning in 2008
• Obtained the Registered Financial Consultant Designation from the International Association of Registered Financial Consultants in 2012
• Philadelphia Magazine named me a 5 Star Wealth Manager in 2009, 2010, 2011 & 2013. (http://www.fivestarprofessional.com/awards/2012_wealth_managers_research_overview.php)
• Was also named in The Philadelphia Business Journal as a NABCAP Premier Advisor in 2010. (http://www.nabcap.org/fap-rankingProcess.cfm)

I enjoy giving back to my local community by volunteering with several local charities, including the American Diabetes Association, Cancer Research Institute of New Jersey, Habitat for Humanity and Bancroft NeuroHealth. In addition, I volunteer for the Career Transition Partnership by speaking about managing finances through job transition and networking for career advancement.

I also actively support the South Jersey business community through various organizations such as:
•South Jersey Business Alliance-Founder
•Business Networking International-Past President & Vice President
•National Association of Women Business Owners NAWBO South Jersey-Marketing Director 2011-2012

When not at the office, I enjoy spending time with my wife, JoAnne, who is a special education teacher in the Jackson School District. I enjoy golf, saltwater fishing, reading, playing guitar, and attending local sporting events.
