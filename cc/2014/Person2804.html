Dickie Aguado's Summary

Dickie Aguado is the Executive Director of Magna Kultura Foundation, an arts and cultural organization implementing educational, cultural, and socio-civic programs at the grassroots of Philippine society. Dickie Aguado is a Social & Cultural Entrepreneur who implements educational programs in the academe and professional communities, using arts and cultural activities as springboard for engagement. His work encompasses sectors: creating advocates to help build an ecosystem for transformation. Aguado establishes grassroots organizations and trains local citizens to sustain campaigns and programs at the grassroots.

In June 2, 2003, Dickie Aguado established Magna Kultura Foundation: a non-stock, non-profit corporation registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission.

Concurrent to his stint as Executive Director at Magna Kultura, Dickie Aguado is a Strategic Marketing and Creatives Consultant to private and government corporations; developing marketing communications and corporate social responsibility programs.
