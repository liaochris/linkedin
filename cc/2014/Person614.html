Background

Summary

A highly multi-skilled, qualified and enquiring individual with over 30 years in the marine and offshore oil industry. This stems from my in-depth knowledge and experience in the marine and offshore oil industry, along with my academic background. I am an excellent communicator with an outgoing personality and the ability to relate equally well to industry, media, university, college and student. An experienced international marine archaeologist and pioneering deep-water marine archaeologist with particular skills in the planning and implementation of underwater archaeological investigations, watching briefs and desk-based assessments. A highly experienced marine safety and survival training consultant, a professional seaman a trainee Side-Scan Technician and ROV Pilot.

Director of my own consultancy company Rovarch I have been involed in many high profile projects and in 2008 I was one of the main stars of a Discovery Channel 12 part reality show called Treasure Quest. I have done many press and media interviews and I am always on the look out for future TV and media work..

Projects include:
SS Gairsoppa, initial survey
SS Mantola, initial survey.
HMS Victory. English Channel. Initial survey and cannon recovery.
Black Swan Shipwreck. Archaeological investigation and coin recovery.
Washington State, Manned-submersible investigations of shipwrecks.
Atlas Project, Western Approaches to the English Channel.
Eastern Mediterranean, Investigation of various shipwreck sites.
Western Mediterranean, Investigation of various shipwreck sites.
HMS Sussex Project, Straits of Gibraltar.
SS Republic Project, Gulf Stream off Savannah, USA.
Duart Point Shipwreck, Isle of Mull, Scotland.
HMS Exmouth WWII warship, Moray Firth Scotland.
Struma Jewish refugee ship, Black Sea.

Specialties:Deep-water shipwreck investigations and excavations.

Guest lecturing, TV and media interviews and reality shows. Good in front of a camera.

Offshore marine safety and survival instructor/examiner specialising in lesson plans and course planning. Also 23 years as a leader and member of a HM Coastguard Rescue Team.

HSE Part III Commercial Diver and sport diving instructor.

Ballast Control Room Operator/Barge Engineer on semi-submersible and jack-up installations
