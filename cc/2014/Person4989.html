Priya Shah's Summary

A social media professional (not a ninja or guru, just a curious observer) with a strong foundation in public relation, corporate communications and mapping customer experience. My previous experience that spans over 8 years coupled with my education at NYU has equipped me with the skills required to be efficient and effective.

My strengths lie in guiding fast-growing companies to initiate and sustain quality engagement using social channels. Prior to joining SquareTrade, I worked for start-ups in New York and India. I hold a master’s in public relations and corporate communications from NYU.

Bollywood loving, Horror movie hating, beach bum lazing, Tea Junkie. An Eternal Entrepreneur. Currently Director, Social Media for @SquareTrade.
