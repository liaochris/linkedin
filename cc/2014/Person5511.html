Raymond J. Arroyo's Summary

Senior Level Executive Search Consultant.

My background includes significant senior level human resources experience in several industries and geographies. As such, I have first-hand experience of the many challenges that HR and business leaders face and can connect the right people with the right opportunities.

Fortune 100 bilingual leader with more than 25 years of HR and business experience assessing talent needs in consumer goods, professional and financial services, association/nonprofit, and healthcare sectors.

Recognized as a top 100 Most Influential Hispanic with robust, professional network across many industries and geographies with a LinkedIn connection reach of more than 20 million people;
Has written extensively on the power of networking, including as a Huffington Post contributor; and B.S. and M.S. degrees from New York University; Governance certificate from Harvard University.

Serve as Secretary and Chairman-Elect of the Board of Directors of the U.S. Hispanic Chamber of Commerce, an Officer of the Board of Trustees of the Wheeler Clinic and a member of the Board of Directors of the National Hispanic Medical Association.

Served on ALPFA's Corporate Advisory Board, as President of PRIMER, as Chairman of the Corporate and Philanthropic Council of HACU and as a member of the Board of Regents of the University of Hartford.

In 2010 and 2011 was selected by Hispanic Business magazine as one of the 100 Most Influential Hispanics; by the National Society of Hispanic MBAs for the Corporate Executive Excellence Brillante award; by PricewaterhouseCoopers as one of its exemplary alumnus and featured on its KeyWord magazine; by Urban Professional magazine as one of the 100 Top Executives in Corporate America, and was the feature cover story on Diversity Executive magazine.
