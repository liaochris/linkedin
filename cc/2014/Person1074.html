Eric Eckardt's Summary

A versatile financial professional and accomplished entrepreneur with over seventeen years experience comprised of working on Wall Street in Investment Banking and launching new online business ventures.

Since departing Investment Banking in 2004, Eric has launched several startup companies including an online real estate brokerage firm, mortgage company, high-end retail brand (eCommerce), online crowd funding platform and advisory financial intermediary working with companies in raising capital through direct public offerings.

Specialties: Real estate, investment banking, and entrepreneurship with proven results in corporate development, sales, and strategy. Sound financial background as a credit trained financial analyst with former transaction experience in senior bank financings for acquisitions, mergers, leveraged buyouts, and joint ventures for Fortune 500 and middle market companies.
