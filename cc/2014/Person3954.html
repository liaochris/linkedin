Leon Versfeld's Summary

Represent a variety of small and Fortune Companies both nationally and internationally. Provide counsel to their Human Resource Departments on the hiring of Foreign National Workers, Executives and Specialized Workers; and advise them on Labor Certifications and all issues relating to Immigration Employment Compliance (I-9, E-Verify and local immigration laws).

Work together with other attorneys advising on Immigration matters in the areas of Mergers and Acquisitions; Formations of Companies and Foreign Subsidiaries; Immigration Employment Compliance ( I-9, Social Security "No Match Letters", and undocumented workers).

Often asked to present on the topic of U.S. Immigration both nationally and internationally .
