Akos Straub's Summary

Akos Straub and his team are ranked top 1% in Chicago. Straub has consistently been recognized as one of the top producers for Coldwell Banker and has been recognized as a member of the Coldwell Banker International President’s Circle for outstanding sales performance, an honor bestowed upon the top 2 percent of all Coldwell Banker NRT sales associates. He remains a multi-million dollar top producer year after year. He serves on the 2013-2014 Board of Directors for the Chicago Association of REALTORS® (C.A.R.), responsible for helping guide the preeminent local membership organization that serves over 11,000 REALTORS® in all specialties across Chicagoland.

Straub is a Previews® Property Specialist, continuously providing an unparalleled real estate service to his clients. He looks forward to developing a long-term relationship and achieving success one client at a time. He has built an extensive resource network as a luxury expert in Chicago’s Gold Coast, River North, Lincoln Park, Bucktown, Lakeview East, and other areas along the North Shore and West Suburbs. Akos Straub was selected by Chicago Real Estate Magazine as on of the “Top 40 under 40” in Chicago Real Estate, and was also recognized as a Top Achiever and recipient of the Top 5 Producers Award. He was in a featured topic in the Chicago Realtor Magazine titled: Secrets to Today’s Investment Market and 10 Pitfalls to Avoid. Straub has served on multiple committees for the Chicagoland Chamber of Commerce and the Chicago Association of Realtors.

Straub is extremely marketing savvy. He has a great understanding of how homes should be marketed. He uses skills learned from his professional history to deliver a solid business presentation to assist clients in making well-informed real estate decisions whether purchasing, selling or leasing. His priority has always been to offer his clients the highest level of service, market knowledge and integrity.
