EB-5 Jobs For Massachusetts, Inc. - Our USCIS certified EB-5 Program Regional Center works closely with qualified business professionals and regional real estate developers who are able to present well documented job creating investment opportunities to the large Accredited Foreign Investor community. This important segment of the worldwide populous desiring to obtain permanent residence for themselves and their immediate family in the US with American citizenship their ultimate goal may find a very good alternative in securing, at times more quickly than other alternatives, a permanent US visa by participating in the EB-5 Program sponsored by the United States Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS). We at EB-5 Jobs For Massachusetts Regional Center aim to be catalysts for such economic stimulation of new investment capital injected directly into qualifying local and regional Massachusetts economic areas in need of such assistance.
