Melanie Edwards' Summary

With a degree in Industrial & Systems Engineering and over 10 years experience in Information Technology, Melanie Edwards founded ModernMami.com in 2006. Combining her education with 3 years of e-learning industry experience, Melanie applies the various techniques she acquired to digital marketing and social media consulting. Collaborating with individuals, and small to large businesses, Melanie focuses on multi-layered strategies for implementing social media. Through Ella Media, Melanie’s focus is to connect businesses with today’s digital Latina.

Melanie performs content development, blog management, marketing, SEO, and social media monitoring and promotion.

As a former IT Manager for a state-wide e-learning project, Melanie performed various duties on a daily basis, including web and course design, technical support, project management, desktop publishing, SEO, and marketing.

Whether you’re an individual or an established business, Melanie can help you with digital marketing and social media promotion.

HTML, CSS, WordPress, social media, blogging, digital marketing, technical support
