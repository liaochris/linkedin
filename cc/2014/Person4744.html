Richard Eaton's Summary

I am a life-long learner that regularly looks for ways to improve myself and the organizations that I support. I’ve re-invented myself multiple times in my career as an IT manager, IT project manager, agile project portfolio manager and I am now working on Career 2.0 as a project management consultant. As indicators of my commitment to continuous learning, I hold the Project Management Professional (PMP) designation, the Certified Scrum Professional (CSP) designation, the PMI Agile Certified Practitioner (PMI-ACP) credential and the Scaled Agile Framework Program Consultant (SPC) certification. I love the challenge of working with organizations to help them re-invent themselves, serving as guide, coach and mentor; leveraging agile thinking and agile methods to increase project productivity and throughput.
