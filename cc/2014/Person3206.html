Background

Summary

I am a bilingual marketing and branding professional with 8+ years of marcom and advertising experience, coupled with Bachelor's degrees in both Spanish and Latin American Studies from the University of Texas at Austin.

The past 4 years I have been running my own marketing firm, Knockout Marketing Strategies. We are a full-service agency that provides marketing services for amusement, retail and restaurant clients, specifically within the franchise industry, with a focus on local store marketing.

My previous full-time position was as the Field Marketing Manager at Monkey Joe's, a family entertainment center, that is owned by Raving Brands, an international franchise incubator. In this role, I was responsible for developing and executing all corporate and franchisee marketing and branding initiatives. I have also been employed both as a Marketing Projects Manager for a small art promotions company and managed a team of account executives dedicated to healthcare clients.
