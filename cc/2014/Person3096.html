Background

Summary

DATA is one of the most powerful four letter words in the English dictionary and the most sought after asset every business owns. Making DATA relevant is the main ingredient to business prosperity. Knowing how to make sense of fact based, unemotional, always available, insightful DATA is what George knows best.

As a passionate leader in the field of DATA logistics & management George is a proponent of the BIG DATA initiative. Not for the sake of Buzz-Word-Bingo, but for the focus it brings to DATA access and insight. George helps leverage DATA with leading edge storage technologies, advancing predictive analytics, designing machine learning, enabling data mining, facilitating business intelligence to move people from reactive-survival to prosperity-preparedness.

Kreatek - Protective Life APD
January 2001 – January 2008 (7 years 1 month)

I started Kreatek to launch my contracting career with Protective Life's Asset Protection Division. They needed to consolidate a variety of administration systems they inherited through M&A and settled on a SQL Server / Cognos toolset. I was brought in to architect and build the solution. I successfully created ODS's that served up Cognos analytical cubes and Cognos reports to an intranet portal. I used SQL Server’s DTS ETL services to transfer data from the source systems to a staging area where the data was updated prior to moving to the reporting ODS.

I created many interfaces to other systems, supporting g/l financial reports, commission payments and other third party requirements due to the accuracy and performance of the data warehouse.

I was invited to tour each division office, from Los Angeles to North Carolina, to teach end users how to pull Cognos reports for self-service analytics.

Most important were the relationships developed with business professionals who were passionate about their work. I learned a great deal about insurance products and the business processes that supported the environment.
