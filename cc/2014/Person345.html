Background

Summary

Technology industry marketing executive with 18+ years experience across all marketing disciplines, including branding, analyst relations, public relations, product/solution marketing, social/new media, web strategy, demand generation, marketing communications, executive communications and events.

"You make great technology, I’ll make your technology great!"

I am currently the Sr. Director of Product Marketing at Sensity Systems - Lights. Data. Action!

Prior to Sensity, I was the Director of Product Marketing for Aerohive Networks (NYSE:HIVE), responsible for understanding market trends and customer needs and marrying them to Aerohive’s product lines to bring to market game-changing solutions. With over 20 years experience in the technology industry, an Electrical Engineering degree from MIT, I have spent my career researching technology and working with companies to move from just creating technological innovation to marketing and selling high-value, business-changing solutions.

Prior to Aerohive, I led product marketing and management teams at Meru Networks (Nasdaq:MERU), Cisco, CopperCom, was one of the first 20 employees and the lead for Netgear’s (Nasdaq:NTGR) entry into the wireless market. I have successfully brought over 100 products and services to market worldwide, garnering awards ranging from Comdex best-in-show to a nomination for a ComputerWorld Smithsonian award.

In my spare time I marry my passion for technology with my passion for wine and I am the co-producer and founder of the international Wine Bloggers Conference which brings together the most vibrant community of wine writers in the world.

Specialties: Marketing for high technology companies. I focus purely on what it takes to move from a building robust, extensible technology to marketing high-value, business-changing solution. Smart people make great technology, I make technology great!
