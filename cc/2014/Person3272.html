Background

Summary

Research and product development. Finding the latest technologies to create unique attractions and applying them to the special event industry. Marketing these turn key products and attractions to the special event industry....

President of Interactive Entertainment Concepts, Gregg Dukofsky brings a uniquely innovative and entrepreneurial spirit to the company. Through first hand experience, he believes that you must do “all the little things right” to ensure a well-coordinated, truly memorable and truly sensational event. Gregg is also extremely community-minded, serving on the board of directors of numerous local organizations, as well as being an active member of ISES and MPI.

Specialties: Having earned High honors in communications and audio/video production, and previously owning a successful video production company, Gregg brings a wealth of experience including a dedication to total customer satisfaction.

Role: New product research and development. Taking today's technology and creating new products for the event/party/promotion industries. Making IEG the leader in interactive entertainment.
