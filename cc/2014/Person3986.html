Lisa Dawson's Summary

Economic and community development professional with 20 years experience in rural development. A large part of my work experience has been helping business owners and entrepreneurs access the resources they need to start and grow their businesses. I also enjoy working with non-profit and ad-hoc organizations to help them define and meet their goals. Grant writing, project development and management, and reporting on federal and state grant programs are a regular part of my work. I have a good grasp on financial administration, fund accounting and financial controls.

Specialties

Facilitation of decision-making processes
Facilitating business planning classes for small businesses and entrepreneurs
Consultant for non-profit boards
