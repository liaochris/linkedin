Carlos Smith's Summary

Animation filmmaker and producer. Involved in animation production since 1993.

Founder of HIERROanimación, Bogotá, together with Anna Ferrer, 2010.

Founder of Smith&Smith, Bogotá, together with Jaime Smith, 1996.

Co-coordinator of the Master's Degree in Computer Animation, Institut Universitari de L’audiovisual (UPF), Barcelona, together with Marcelo Dematei, 2002-2004.

Member of enReposo experimental animation collective, 2002-2008.

Specialties

- desarrollo y producción de IPs en animación.
- Proyectos transmedia animados
- Documental animado.
- Animación bidimensional y tridimensional.
- Animación para formatos no convencionales.

    In this first season (13+2 x 7’) children from Portugal, Brasil, Poland, Paraguay, Chile, Mexico, Colombia, Cuba, Venezuela, Ecuador and Argentina living in Barcelona (ESP), Bogotá (COL) and Barranquilla (COL) talk to each other about their arrival, the neighborhood, the school, the park, games, food, customs and all new and already known words from here and there.
