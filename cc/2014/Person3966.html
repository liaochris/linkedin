Dr. Sheri Kaye Hoff's Summary

Dr. Sheri Kaye Hoff,The Inspired Action Success Coach, PhD., MA, CGCL, Coach and Trainer. Inspirational and transformational Amazon and Barnes and Noble Author of multiple books. "I work with business owners who want to make a real difference and make real money. I help them create clear plans so they can relax into making more profit. My coaching approach is transformational, inspirational, energy boosting, intuitive, spiritual, and action oriented. I coach individuals, coaches, consultants, and small biz through high-level customized one one one sessions, VIP events, Custom VIP Days, mastermind sessions, and my signature member site, I am a transformational leadership expert and a college professor. I have created coach certification training programs, organizational change management programs for small business, and corporate training and development programs. I earned a PhD, a Master's degree in Organizational Management, plus two bachelor degrees- a B.S. and B.Msc. I teach various business classes including organizational change management, human resource management, ethics, and others at the collegiate and adult learning level. My corporate background includes management (HR, training, business development, and operations). I am a certified as a group coaching leader, executive coach, business coach, spiritual coach, and life coach. I host my own podcast on itunes and am booked on international radio shows as a guest expert. "

Specialties: Thought leader, change agent, business coaching, leadership coaching, inspiring massive action, catalyst for creativity, organizational development, personal development, holistic life coaching, telesummits, mentor coaching, coaching audio programs, coaching ebooks, individual development plans, life coaching, coach training, writing coaching, training and development,and operations management.
