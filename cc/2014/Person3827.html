Kevin Houchin. Esq.'s Summary

I do three things very well. And, when considered together, many people say I’m the best they’ve seen.

•Understanding Situations
•Developing Strategy
•Communicating Solutions

Understanding situations is really a matter of understanding the goals and fears of the people affected by any given situation. I am able to read people quickly and take the time to refine my understanding before moving into strategy.

Developing strategy translates the goals and fears of the people involved into the practical business, legal, logistic, branding, marketing, sales, product, service, or other tactical area of the solution. I’m personally able to manage many of these tactical implementations, although for most I prefer to work with a team of subject-matter experts.

Communication wears many faces. When it’s moving product or service, it’s called “sales.” When it’s educating people, it’s called “teaching.” When it’s aligning people toward a common goal it’s called “leadership” or “motivation.” When it’s resolving conflict, it’s called “mediation.” Ultimately, it’s all communication, which can only be accomplished when one has taken the time to develop the understanding of people’s goals and fears as discussed above.

Specialties: Public Speaking - Motivational Keynotes & In-depth Workshops, Education and Workforce Policy, Consulting, Sales/Marketing, Business Development, Start-up Planning, Creative/Artistic Expression, Integrating Spirit in Business, Copyright, Trademark, Conflict Management, Mediation, Professional & Leadership Development & Coaching, Intellectual Property Protection & Licensing, Branding
