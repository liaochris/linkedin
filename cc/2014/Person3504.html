Lead by Jim Seabold, the BOLD Marketing Team of Coldwell Banker Burnet specializes in New Construction, Condo, Single/Multiple Family homes, Short Sale and Bank Owned (REO) Sales.

We provide direction in necessary real estate sales processes including marketing and advertising coordination, knowledgeable pricing recommendations, administrative detail and reporting, sales training and management and clientele management.

The BOLD Marketing Team brings extensive experience, knowledge, and passion to the Twin Cities. Our team of experts will help you understand and execute every aspect of the real estate process from buying to selling. Our thorough understanding of the marketplace provides the ability to develop and maintain positive relationships and focus on customer service and buyer satisfaction.

Jim Seabold has been a licensed realtor with Coldwell Banker Burnet since 1987. Over time, Jim has established his niche in working for multiple corporations with properties exclusively within the Twin Cities metro area. Today, with the help of his experienced employees at BOLD Marketing he is one of the leading realtors in the twin cities. He takes pride in creating a quality of life that goes beyond the big picture of city living, into the nitty gritty of actually choosing a place to live and making that choice a reality. He does this through a team of agents that are completely dedicated to the cities, neighborhoods, and people they work with.

Jim Seabold’s personal system of values rooted in community, architecture, diversity and style means that he cares about the vitality of urban communities and in creating a positive home buying experience for you more than anything. What matters to you matters to him.
