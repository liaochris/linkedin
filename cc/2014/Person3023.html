Falon M. Wrigley's Summary

Falon Wrigley represents employers in both litigation and administrative proceedings and advises clients with respect to state and federal employment laws. She routinely handles litigation involving discrimination, harassment, breach of employment and separation agreements, the Fair Labor Standards Act, the Americans with Disabilities Act, and the Family and Medical Leave Act. Ms. Wrigley also counsels management regarding employment issues, including employee discipline and termination; personnel policies and handbooks; wage and hour policies and practices; and investigation of employee misconduct. She is also a frequent lecturer on a wide variety of employment-related topics.

Falon is also a member of Constangy's Diversity Council.
