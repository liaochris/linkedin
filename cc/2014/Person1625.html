Rick Backus' Summary

Rick Backus is the CEO of CPC Strategy. He started working with online retailers at PriceGrabber.com and co-founded CPC Strategy in January of 2007. As an industry expert in ecommerce and retail search intent, he has spoken at national conferences including IRCE, SearchLove, and SES. Today, Rick focuses his efforts on helping over 220 retailers thrive on bottom-of-the-funnel retail search channels.
