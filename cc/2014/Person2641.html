Dan Rushing's Summary

Passionate for Jesus, entrepreneurship, leadership, and games, Dan Rushing is an entrepreneur, Pastor, Evangelist, and Chief Executive Officer. He founded his first company, an internet reseller, in 2002. Since then, Dan has accumulated a unique blend of ministry, startup, and business development experience.

Dan is Founder and CEO of Xtari, LLC, the parent company of Xtari Games and Xtari Business Services. Dan is also founder of Xtari Games, that brings social gaming to web platforms and iOS, and Xtari Business Services, which provides online business software services with its leading software RhinoSEO.

Previously, Dan served as Senior Pastor in New York. He specialized in cultivating the presence of God, healing, and evangelism. Dan also created and ran marketing campaigns to connect people in the surrounding area to God and the body of Christ. He also spent time preaching at local and regional ministry events.

Dan graduated with his Master of Divinity from Nazarene Theological Seminary in 2005. Prior to that he graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in Communication from MidAmerica Nazarene University in 2000.

Known for his passion to reconcile the world to Christ and advance the kingdom of God, he founded an evangelistic church ministry, Dan Rushing Ministries.

Specialties: Evangelism, teaching, preaching, healing, general management, startups, product development, business models, strategy, negotiation, deal structure, international, marketing, brand development, brand management, business development, public relations, press strategy, payments infrastructure, financial services, fundraising and investing, mergers and acquisitions, business development, software development.
