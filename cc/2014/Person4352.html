Michelle McManus' Summary

I am Director, Digital Marketing at American International Group, Inc. (AIG), a leading international insurance organization. Prior to working for AIG, I spent nearly 11 years working for the Nashville-based global logistics company OHL.

I have a varied range of skills which include search engine optimization, social media, social compliance and policy, copy writing, trend analysis, branding and product promotion, development of consumer loyalty and rewards programs, and gamification techniques.

I am a former Microsoft Dynamics CRM administrator and played an instrumental role in the migration to Salesforce.com. I have overseen the complete overhaul of a global website, built an intranet site, and managed client engagement programs utilizing Eloqua, Marketo and now Exacttarget.

I have served as past e-Communications Chair for the Transportation Marketing and Communications Association, founded a community website, a blogger and podcaster group, received acknowledgments in two books. I appeared in the November 2010 issue of Fast Company in an article on their Influence Project. I ranked in the top 10% of online influencers.

I have a first degree black belt and am an assistant instructor of Chil Sung Kwan Tang Soo Do.

Member of Linkedin since June 2005.

Specialties: Social Media Marketing, New Media, Search Engine Optimization, SEO, Public Speaker, Author, Entrepreneur, Drupaler, Marketo Admin, Microsoft CRM Admin, Digital Marketing Strategy, ExactTarget, Social Business Engineering, Salesforce.com and Marketing Automation
