Jonathan Shariat's Summary

My mantra is “Dream. Think. Do.” and my passion is to create user-minded designs that are aesthetically pleasing, natural to use, and take every effort to reduce waste. I believe in the refining power of simplicity and elegance through the fusion of logic and creative inspiration. I embrace the humility of the function that each design serves for the user. Lastly, I create experiences that tell stories and leave people with their jaws on the floor.

I have six years of design experience, two years in User Experience Design, and four years of leadership experience. I am a pixel perfecter in photoshop and illustrator, a data nerd of User Experience, and a HTML-CSS-Javascript-php-AS3 coding fiend. I lead projects, I improve everything, and I love collaboration.

Specialties: UI, UX, Web & mobile Interaction Design, gaming, and product.
