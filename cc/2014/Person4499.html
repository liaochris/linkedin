Kelly Kubrick's Summary

Kelly is the owner of Online Authority, a digital analytics services firm and co-founder of Digital Strategy Conference.

Designed to help organizations stay relevant, competitive and resilient, Digital Strategy Conference delivers the essentials of planning, implementing and improving digital initiatives at its events in Vancouver, British Columbia and Ottawa, Ontario.

Kelly specializes in digital analytics as a methodology to assess the effectiveness of digital efforts - websites, email, social, search - relative to your business strategy. Based on that objective evidence, she provides recommendations to clients across public, private and not for profit sectors. Kelly also develops and delivers training in digital / web analytics and writing for the web.

Her online expertise stems her years of experience in media and publishing. Prior to founding Online Authority in 2002, she spent six years in New York with Time Warner subsidiaries Time Inc New Media and Time Consumer Marketing Inc. There, she oversaw the planning, development and launch of more than 60 websites for such brands as In Style, Fortune, PEOPLE, Sports Illustrated and TIME.
