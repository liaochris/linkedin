Rev. Dr. Constance Cook-Core's Summary

Graduated with a Doctor of Divinity degree from New Thought Seminary of the Sierra in 2010. This is leading to authorship and an interlaced career of teaching and being a keynote speaker in my area of expertise: heart-based practical spirituality.

Chief of Operations at San Diego Center for Spiritual Living: includes mastering (and managing staff to) perform every aspect of running a growing non-profit, service-oriented business.

Education Coordinator: selecting and scheduling courses and instructors, training Practitioners for licensing and teaching, developing curriculum, and doing what I love best - changing lives through teaching universal spiritual principles and seeing those changes appear from the inside out for the students over the course of a term.

Private Practice: Spiritual Counseling, Certified Life Coach

Applicability: Ability to use all the wisdom and skills I have garnered over the expanse of my life in my current position. Isn't it amazing that Systems and Software Research and Development would use the same skillset as the Ministry!

Specialties: Teaching & Training, Curriculum Development, Spiritual Counseling, Professional Coaching, Business Process Analysis and Management, Systems and Software Research and Development
