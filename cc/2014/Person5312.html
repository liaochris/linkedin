Tom Pick's Summary

As an independent digital marketing consultant and through marketing and PR agency KC Associates, Tom helps clients increase their visibility, credibility, and business success online. His expertise in web presence optimization, SEO, search marketing, social media, content marketing and social PR has helped clients ranging from single-person businesses to $1 billion+ corporations. A few recent success stories:

* Helped a large office products company, a KC Associates client, grow organic search traffic six-fold, quadruple total website traffic, and increase online lead generation by 150% over two years.
* Coached the president of a small sales management training company to become a widely-known figure in social media, capture the #1 Google search spot, and develop a highly productive online lead generation system.
* Tripled AdWords average monthly conversion rates while slashing cost per lead 72% for an enteprrise software vendor.

His award-winning Webbiquity blog covers B2B lead generation, web presence optimization, social media, interactive PR, SEO and search engine marketing. He's been named one of the 50 most influential B2B marketing thought leaders, written numerous articles on web and social media marketing for various publications, been quoted in publications including Fast Company, Forbes and Inc. magazine, and presented at blogging and social media conferences. He's a co-founder of WPOinc, a provider of web presence optimization metrics; the B2BMarketingZone.com portal for B2B blogs; and the Social Media Informer social media content aggregation site.

Prior to becoming an independent consultant in 2006, Tom served in technology marketing leadership roles with supply-chain outsourcer Zomax, ERP vendor SoftBrands, and digital prepress supplier Printware. He has an MBA from the University of Minnesota and a bachelor's degree in engineering from St. Cloud State University.

Specialties

Web presence optimization, SEO, search engine marketing, social media, writing and interactive PR. Industry-specific experience in digital hardware, software and services.
