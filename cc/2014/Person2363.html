Brooks Cole's Summary

With three decades' experience in communications design and over two decades in interactive design, Brooks is the founder and creative leader behind HoloCosmos - a S.W.A.T. team of media magicians at the cutting-edge of human-computer interaction, and now HoloGenesis, an exciting startup in next-generation search, discovery, navigation and learning.

HoloCosmos creates next generation user experience and branding for websites, media devices, online communities, web applications, and channels for making change and advancing sustainability and thrivability.

Brooks Cole is the Chairman and CEO of a new technology venture called HoloGenesis. We're developing a personalized media discovery platform for individuals, organizations and marketers. We're creating new business models and technologies for the next generation of online commerce and collaboration, employing some truly novel ideas around the "holographic experience of discovery."

Brooks Cole and his partner Dave Fisk have invented a game-changing approach to calculating the relevance of information for individuals. "We're creating new lenses to see deeply into emerging information and media networks. I believe that a lot of our society's problems come from vision problems. Not problems with our eyes, but problems with our ability to see the truth -- even when it is right in front of us -- or when it is skillfully and deliberately hidden from our view. I believe most people and organizations would individually and collectively do the right thing if we could see what is really up."

We aim to transform your experience of using knowledge and social media to understand and impact the world around you. Our technology has applications in Search, Finance, Media and Entertainment, Marketing, Socially Responsible Investing, Health, and Transformation to name just a few examples.
