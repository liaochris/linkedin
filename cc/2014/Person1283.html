Josh Margolis' Summary

I help people use CRM, accounting/ERP and eCommerce technology to meet their customers' expectations. These people are frustrated because they can't get the information they need to meet their customers' expectations.

Typically, my CRM clients are:
• Challenged working with multiple systems that they’ve bolted together to run their business.
• Struggling to produce an accurate, timely sales forecast.
• Aggravated with training hurdles and low employee adoption of their CRM system.
• Disappointed with opportunities falling through the cracks.

Often businesses that started with Quickbooks or some other small business accounting application have outgrown its capabilities. Using the power of cloud computing, NetSuite provides CRM, ERP and eCommerce in one package. The combination of speed, cost-effectiveness and superior manageability helps my clients save time and money every year.

What is distinctive about my approach is that regardless of the software application selected, I do not regard technology itself to be a business strategy.
