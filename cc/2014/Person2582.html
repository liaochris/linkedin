Connie Antoniou 847.508.7775's Summary

Connie Antoniou began her career in real estate in 1985 after taking a real estate course on a whim! Since then, her professionalism and dynamic personality coupled with her sincere desire to help people has propelled her to the top echelon of elite real estate brokers in Chicagoland. In 2007 Connie became Broker/Owner of Hunter’s Fairway Sotheby’s International Realty in North Barrington. Connie’s continued success throughout the ups and downs of the current housing market are a reflection of the esteem of the Sotheby’s Brand and Connie’s own personal dedication and expertise. She opened up a second office in downtown Barrington's Cook Street Plaza in the spring of 2012. With an uncanny understanding of the local marketplace, Connie is the consummate deal-maker whether representing buyers or sellers. Connie is a licensed Broker in Wisconsin, where she represents sellers of hunting estates, lakefront front homes and other unique properties.

Connie specializes in unique properties; large estates, horse properties, historic homes and golf course communities. Her all-star team of elite real estate professionals utilizes the global reach of the Sotheby’s Brand Network through state-of-the-art digital marketing, world-wide exposure and innovative social media. She has earned the prestigious designations of CDPE (Certified Distressed Property Expert), CIPS (Certified International Property Specialist), CRS (Certified Residential Specialist), GRI (Graduate REALTOR Institute), SFR designation (Short Sale/Forclosure), and NAR’s Green Designation (by the Green Resource Council).

Connie Antoniou has all the credentials plus a unique understanding of the luxury consumer. She is a past director for the North Shore Barrington Association of Realtors, and currently serves on the Board of the Wynstone Golf Community Property Owners Association and the Board of Directors for the Barrington Area Chamber of Commerce (BACC).
