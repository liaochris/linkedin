Ed Trefzger's Summary

I oversee day-to-day operations for a broadcast and digital media corporation. I'm responsible for ongoing management and the implementation of brand development and business plans for four broadcast stations and associated web and streaming properties, where our current focus is on growing sales and listenership. My over 30 years of broadcasting experience also includes hockey play-by-play, sportscasting, radio production, music directing and hosting, newscasting and news radio programming, traffic reporting and radio/records promotion.

I'm also experienced in managing and implementing web development and application projects, with an emphasis on integrating open-source software and new technologies for maximum customer ROI and minimum cost of ownership. I am expert in project management, programming, development and interface design and have served clients ranging from Fortune 100 companies to VC-funded startups.

Specialties: Expert at implementing and customizing third-party libraries and CMS products, including WordPress, PhoneGap, vBulletin and e-commerce platforms. Expert HTML/XHTML and CSS coding, including cross-browser compatibility. Expert object-oriented PHP and JavaScript programming, MySQL development and administration, JavaScript and AJAX development, including jQuery.
