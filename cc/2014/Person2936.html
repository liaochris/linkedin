Catrice M. Jackson The BOSSLady of Branding's Summary

Catrice M. Jackson, International Speaker, BOSSLady of Branding, Magnetic Message Mentor and two-time International Best-Selling author passionately co-creates Irresistible BOSSLady brands with women entrepreneurs who want to release their Irresistible Trademark (IT), own it and flaunt it in the marketplace for more visibility and business vitality. Catrice’s personal mission is to live her truth and evolve into her highest self.

Her professional mission is to teach women how to live their authentic personal brand and speak their voice with irresistible confidence on any platform and her global mission is to liberate the voices of women worldwide so they can live authentically free.

Catrice M. Jackson, is a two time international best-selling author and the author of 4 self-help and inspired business books. Catrice has been featured in the Law of Attraction Magazine and currently contributes to various online magazines and blogs such as Little PINK Book, Vivid Life, Feminine Soul, and She Is Dallas. She received the 2012 Stiletto Women in Business Award for Entrepreneur of the Year in Education and Training for her dedication and innovative flavor of inspiring and empowering women world-wide.

Catrice showcases her love for inspirational speaking on her weekly online radio show, "Catriceology Radio" and has appeared on hundreds of national and international radio shows over the past five years. Catrice frequently speaks live on stage and on international tele-conferences and passionately pioneers her calling to release the voices of women and INspire them to live brave, bold and brilliant lives.
