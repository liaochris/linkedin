Background

Summary

Entrepreneur with focus on innovation in the intersection of tech and media. Specific interests include consumer centered web products.

As the CEO at TAKE ZERO, I lead the business aspects including customer development, sales, marketing, partnerships, and fundraising.

During my undergrad days at college, I started my first company AlphaBeta Labs - as a platform to build a strong culture in learning and experimenting with innovative ideas. I co-founded FoodKhoj, an online food ordering portal for campuses. Post FoodKhoj, we started building Paceable which was initially conceptualized as a solution for managing incoming support emails at FoodKhoj.

I also invented the 108counter for japa during a much needed sabbatical in the mid of 2013.

Specialties: Customer Development, Strategic Planning, Sales Strategy, Negotiation, Business Development, Entrepreneurship

Experience

CEO

TAKE ZERO
January 2014 – Present (10 months)Mumbai Area, India

TAKE ZERO is the premier platform for performing artists like models, actors,singers and dancers to showcase themselves, discover job opportunities and get connected to the industry.

TAKE ZERO is backed by Times Internet Limited, the digital venture of Times of India Group - India's largest media and entertainment conglomerate. Our partners include a range of players in the industry including some of the top production houses, casting agencies, ad and creative agencies, and lifestyle magazines.

TAKE ZERO was an incubatee at Times Internet's TLabs - a startup accelerator and early stage seed-fund in India for internet and mobile technology startups to shape their ideas into world-class products.

Director - Business Strategy

BollyHolly Media
August 2013 – Present (1 year 3 months)Mumbai Area, India

Founder at BollyHolly Media, specializing in tailoring marketing and talent sourcing solutions in the cross domain of media and entertainment industry.

Our products at BollyHolly Media include an Online Magazine (mag.bollyholly.net) - a crowd-sourced dose of movies, celebs, humor, fashion, reviews and more, and an Online Platform (takezero.in) connecting creative and performing artists to various job opportunities.

Co-Founder

Paceable
July 2012 – July 2013 (1 year 1 month)San Francisco

Co-Founder at Paceable - a software for teams working in collaboration over email.

Paceable was initially conceptualized to manage support emails at FoodKhoj, but was later modeled to ease communication and increase productivity among teams working in collaboration.

In 2012, Paceable was incubated by Start-up Chile - an initiative of the Chilean Government to attract early stage, high potential entrepreneurs to bootstrap their startups using Chile as a platform to go global by providing an equity free $40,000 USD grant, a one year resident visa and access to one of the biggest startup communities in the world.

Director - Business Development

AlphaBeta Labs
June 2010 – July 2013 (3 years 2 months)Bengaluru Area, India

Co-Founder & Strategist at AlphaBeta Labs, an innovation platform that specialized in development and deployment of solutions in the intersection of design & technology primarily in the domain of web, automation and analytics.

We built FoodKhoj for enabling easy food options for students in campuses. We helped design, develop and conceptualize various elements at INKtalks (inktalks.com), ground up from scratch.

To solve the problems we faced in managing group emails, we built the MailSyncr technology, later known by as Paceable (paceable.com). In the process of running a completely bootstrapped startup, we also conceptualized Fuzzy Angles (fuzzyangles.com) as a platform to connect bootstrapped entrepreneurs and students in universities.

Co-Founder

FoodKhoj
October 2010 – September 2012 (2 years)Bengaluru Area, India

Co-Founder at FoodKhoj, an online food discovery portal that enables residents of a Campus or University discover, recommend and order food from the nearby restaurants.

FoodKhoj initially started off in late 2010 at BITS Pilani Goa Campus as an online food ordering portal based on a group delivery model for campus residents, and after considerable scale pivoted with the vision of linking restaurants to residents in a more personal manner.
