Sam Parnell's Summary

With 16+ years of experience delivering sites for companies of all sizes Sam provides deep experience in all aspects of web development, product management and technology.

Sam currently works as CTO for Bleacher Report (bleacherreport.com), overseeing product management and technology. Bleacher Report is the 2nd largest sports site in the US reaching 80 Million+ users. He is also a VP at Turner Broadcasting providing strategic input into a variety of areas of the business.

In previous roles as a product and technology consultant for agencies such as Razorfish and Blast Radius, Sam’s clients included Nike, Electronic Arts, The LATimes, Genetech, KightRidder Newspapers, Franklin Covey and General Motors.

Specialties:
- Product Strategy, Technology Strategy, Management and Leadership
- User Experience, Acquisition and Growth
- Cloud Architecture, Open Source, High Volume Websites, Scalable Architectures
- Agile Methodology Coach and Practitioner, XP, Scrum
