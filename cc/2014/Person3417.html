Jean Burk's Summary

Jean Burk is the author of College Prep Genius and a veritable SAT/PSAT expert. She currently travels and speaks about the importance of college preparation, how to get university scholarships, and how to prepare for the SAT & PSAT. Her expertise has been featured on WE, Fox, NBC Today, Good Day Dallas,TXA21 Primetime News, and The Homeschool Channel. Her "Master the SAT Class" is all across America and other countries including China, Korea and Thailand.

She has been a featured and keynote speaker in numerous conventions in the US.

Specialties: SAT/PSAT test prep, FREE College, professional speaking, educational goals counseling, author, entrepreneur,

Her program has been awarded the TOS Excellence in Education Award, the 2013 Homechool Seal of Approval and the 2013 Blue Ribbon Award for Best and Favorite College Prep Resource by Homeschool Crew Review.

She has taught thousands of students a year since 2004 how to ace standardized tests by raising scores as much as 600 points, become National Merit Scholars and even go to Ivy-Leagues for FREE! Colleges go up on their rankings based on their test scores so the higher the score-the more money you get!
