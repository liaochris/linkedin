Background

Summary

Lawyer, advisor and executive with 18+ years experience advising entrepreneurs, investors, early stage startups through Nasdaq 100 growth companies in the full range of legal matters. Former VP / global head of legal affairs at eHarmony.com and original in-house counsel at MySpace.

Trained at top-tier Wall Street and Silicon Valley law firms, followed by in-house roles at publicly traded market leaders in consumer Internet and digital media sectors from broadband Internet access to Web search, online advertising, e-commerce, social media, entertainment content and more, ranging from $100 million to $1 billion in annual revenue.

Now in independent law practice, drawing on years in the trenches as legal and business affairs executive at consumer Internet pioneers, I offer unparalleled depth and breadth in legal and business issues unique to social Web and mobile startups. With offices in San Francisco and Santa Monica, we are well positioned to bridge the gap between Silicon Valley and Hollywood business and legal cultures.

Specialties include:
• New business formation and structuring, early stage startups, seed financing, venture capital, angel investments, intellectual property (IP)
• Internet law, privacy, e-commerce, user-generated content, social media, content and technology licensing, online advertising and marketing, mobile apps, related SaaS and enterprise software
• Strategic corporate and business development transactions, M&A, international joint ventures
• Founder equity and executive compensation matters, stock option plans, employment and consulting agreements
• Risk and crisis management, litigation management, law department management, government affairs
