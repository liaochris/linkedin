Jon Saia's Summary

If you are looking for a criminal defense lawyer from Columbus who can represent you, it may be in your best interest to contact The Law Offices of Saia & Piatt, Inc. The firm was established by Attorney Jon J. Saia, who has been practicing law for over 23 years. Over the years, Mr. Saia has gained recognition for the high quality services he offers individuals who have been charged with an OVI (operating a vehicle under the influence) or other criminal offense. As a certified member of The National Highway Traffic Safety Administration, Attorney Saia maintains an extensive understanding of all Ohio OVI laws, and has the ability to provide aggressive representation and trusted legal counsel for individuals facing OVI offenses. Regardless of how simple or complicated your situation may seem, you can be confident in the services Mr. Saia will provide you with. To find out more about retaining the representation of Attorney Saia, be sure to contact the firm today.
