Kathy Rapp | Open Networker's Summary

Kathy Rapp: SVP of hrQ. hrQ is a Human Resources professional services firm focused on Human Resources search, Human Resources contract staffing and Human Resources consulting.

I'm also a Human Resources Executive with leadership experience at companies such as Four Seasons Hotels and Resorts, Morgan Stanley and First Data Corporation. Additionally I have a blend of start-up experiences within the technology, entertainment and healthcare industries and worked internationally in Asia Pacific.

I speak frequently at HR and C-level conferences. I'm a blogger at Fistful of Talent, an oil & gas blog, WorkforceNEXT, Inc.com and of course hrQ's blog, hrQuips.

Check out www.hrQinc.com for more info on our team, our professional development event calendar, our blog and current Human Resources searches and projects.
