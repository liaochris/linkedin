Background

Summary

Jose Julian Acosta, is a seasoned advertising executive producer with over 15 years experience working virtually in every production facet of the advertising industry. In addition to his Ad experience for Fortune 500 Clients. His expertise ranges from broadcast to print and digital production, all at National and International levels and across nearly all major product segments for leading global advertisers. In his extensive production management and brand consulting experience, Jose has worked with diverse categories such as Automotive, Packaged Goods, Beverage, Retail, Communications, Financial Services, Airline, Pharmaceutical, Sports & Health, Educational and Restaurants.

His exposure as a former owner of a production management company, coupled with his prior leading roles as Executive Producer, agency management, video editor and musician has garnered Jose a unique 360-degree perspective that has helped many clients boost efficiencies without undermining production quality, as well as provide solutions that allow brand stewards lead more effective relationships with agency partners.

Born in San Juan, Puerto Rico. His experience with production started at a very early age when Jose was often casted as a child actor for various TV commercials in his native Puerto Rico at the same time growing up in the corridors assisting in his family's FM Radio stations. As a result, he became extremely comfortable and knowledgeable with equipment as well as with the demanding expectations of content production.

Jose was educated in the liberal arts field and studied all facets of the Music Industry, graduating with a degree in Music Production and Engineering from Berklee College of Music in Boston. Jose is proficient with the trumpet and minor percussion.

Specialties: Natural leader, personalized client service during production, Able to put the best tools at the creative hands to allow them to create award winning advertising. Passionate about solving problems.
