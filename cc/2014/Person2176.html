Athena Micropoulou's Summary

Summary
Athena Micropoulou

Athena started her career in advertising as an account executive @ J.N. Leoussis Co, in 1986.
She continued with McCann Erickson, DDB and LEO BURNETT to return in late 2000 to McCann's where she worked up to March 2011 as the VP, Chief Growth Officer of McCann Erickson WorldGroup Hellas.
Since May 2011 she works as an independent consultant in the marketing and advertising business.
She believes that learning, exploration, experimentation and innovation are key to success
Specialties: Brand Strategy,Brand Building, Integrated Marketing Communications,Social Media Marketing
