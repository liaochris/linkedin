Summary

Richard Arias resides in Houston, Texas and is a graduate of Fairleigh Dickinson University with a BA degree in History with a minor in Education. Richard has spent over ten years in the local IT field, specializing in selling enterprise technology services, hardware, Fiber networks, and data services to support project based applications in the US and globally. Specializing in energy, financial and the government sector. Richard has worked for global IT providers and has invested - packaged a reverse merger for a New Mexico based computer start up.
Richard's main focus is to insure his clients deliver on their core business through people, technology, and enabling services and products.
