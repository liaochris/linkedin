Brad Johnson's Summary

I am a seasoned, customer-focused enterprise software professional with a proven record multi-million-dollar revenue generating achievements in a variety of critical roles in global public & private organizations.

My leadership style is described by colleagues & management as passionate, effective, dedicated, strategic, focused, practical, technical, ethical and driven. My career is devoted to helping global enterprises improve and optimize the value of IT. Prior to my current role as head of Product Marketing & Business Development at SOASTA, I held senior positions in sales, product management, strategic alliances, development, and operations in global, public companies and small, wear-every-hat startups over the past 19 years.

I've been promoted nearly annually, carried and exceeded 8-figure quotas, managed strategic acquisitions, developed first-of-kind strategic partnerships with HP, IBM, CSC, CA, VMware, Adobe, Microsoft, Rackspace, Siebel, BMC & SAP, launched new products, revived old ones, & managed $600M solutions. Under my watch at Borland we achieved market-leading growth, and at SOASTA we've grown from small cloud startup to Gartner-recognized industry leader.

Additionally, I have driven strategic projects around Mobile Testing, Real User Monitoring, Cloud Computing, Cloud Testing, virtualization and open source technologies, plus process areas like DevOps, Continuous Integration and Agile development. Moreover, I have practical, customer-driven philosophies about how to address these in the market.

My unique path through both life and career has molded me as a natural leader with equal comfort in the board room or the development lab. This ability to relate to customers and colleagues at all levels - across organizational, geographical and political boundaries - is possibly the most important factor in my success throughout my career.
