Suzanne Alexandra Ascher, Esq., CPA, Tax LL.M.'s Summary

Suzanne Alexandra Ascher, Esq., C.P.A., Tax LL.M., is a solo practitioner in Staten Island with both law and accounting practices. Ms. Ascher is admitted to practice as an attorney at law before all New York State Courts, the United States Tax Court, and the Supreme Court of the United States of America. She is also licensed as a certified public accountant in New York State. As an attorney and certified public accountant, Ms. Ascher is qualified to practice before the Internal Revenue Service.
