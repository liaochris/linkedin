Jeff Madison's Experience

CEO

RPG Diffusor Systems, Inc.

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Building Materials industry

February 2014 – Present (8 months) Washington D.C. Metro Area

RPG has recently completed its 30th year of researching, designing and manufacturing high performance acoustical products. In the more recent past, we have also partnered with like minded and credentialed manufacturers to be able to now offer the broadest and most stringently tested acoustical systems to clients around the world who demand the highest standards for their facilities.

Our extensive and enduring client relationships are due in large part to our dedication to continuing research in acoustics, the novel aspect to many of our product designs, our capability in customizing our products to meet the demands of every new design and our commitment to ongoing collaboration with architects and acoustical consultants toward the best possible solutions for their clients. Our record for delivering high quality products and the performance benefits realized time and again clearly establishes RPG as a trustworthy supplier to meet the goals of today's building designs; whether they are technical, performance, learning, worship, healing or gathering spaces.

Joining the team responsible for founding and shepherding RPG through its first 30 years, from a dream in a garage to a global leader in acoustics, is at the same time exciting and intimidating. I believe that the compelling nature of what we attempt and the methodical way by which we go about achieving it will continue to drive us forward to ongoing success; not only for RPG and every next project, but for the acoustical industry at large.

The entrepreneurial spirit that founded the company endures today as we continue to research and develop innovative product solutions that will bring new possibilities and shed new light into the world of interior acoustics. In 30 years the mission has never changed: to expand the acoustical palette of tools available to the design community and their clients by developing innovative product systems through a continual commitment to fundamental research in acoustics.

-JM
