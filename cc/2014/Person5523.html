Joe Raimondo's Summary

Over 20 years of progressive and increasing experience in information technology and organizational development. Skills and interests lie at the confluence of IT, strategy, and innovation. Big thinker -- adept at seeing the whole, and creating new integrative models and paradigms. Excellent writer combining technical insight with fine-tuned training in editorial production processes.
Specific areas of expertise:
- development of knowledge management platforms to support modern innovation approaches, such as open innovation
- portal design that integrates the "voice of the customer" into a process that connects sales, marketing and R&D
- simple and elegant marketing ideas in the areas of content management and collaboration that project a desirable future state
- design of systems that integrate back-end processing with knowledge capture, amplification and dissemination
- development of systems that feed business metrics into the visualization and measurement of the impact of transformation efforts in organizations
- development of a comprehensive system architecture that integrates business planning, business development, knowledge management, and back-end processing
- development of a platform to support and on-line diagnostics business from initial item development, item testing and measurement, through to client-facing front-end
- development of a unique interface device that enables modeling and capture of personal and group activities in multiple dimensions
- development of an integrated project management platform for a global professional services firm that provides rapid debrief of activities in order to feed the firm's knowledge store
- development of a whole system design concept for developing a "peer-global" architecture that connects all of an individual's system activities into a background weave of connections and resources.

Specialties

IT systems strategy design and architecture, experienced front-line change management practitioner, innovation and research, methodology and tool development for professional services firms.
