Simon Carr's Summary

Simon believes: "Design is the visual representation of strategy"

Simon has been interested in design his entire life. After graduating with a degree in graphic design, he worked with many talented art directors, designers, developers and strategists in the agency world. Lucky enough to work with good folks, smart enough to learn from them.

Since 2009 Simon has been focusing all his attention on working directly with clients. As creative director of workbsimon, he provides strategy, concepting, design & implementation. Typical clients are technology companies, startups, architecture firms, design agencies and large companies in need of new ideas.

Design Services: Modern solutions for responsive websites and applications. Including UI Design, wireframes, Mood Boards and layered photoshop files.

Development Services: front-end development, responsive code, css3, WordPress and mobile development.

More info at workbysimon.com
