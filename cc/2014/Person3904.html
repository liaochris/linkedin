Lance Allison, CCE, IOM's Summary

After 18 years in small business I became the President/CEO of the Murray-Calloway County Chamber of Commerce in 2008. In 2013 I became President/CEO of the Panama City Beach Chamber of Commerce.

My goal as the CEO of the Chamber of Commerce is to provide networking opportunities, advocacy, and advancement for members of the Panama City Beach Chamber.

Specialties: I hold a BS and MS in Organizational Communication. Organizational Communication is an area of study that includes; conflict management, negotiation, interpersonal relationships, small group studies, and leadership research. In 2012 I completed the US Chamber of Commerce's Institute for Organization Management at the Southeast Institute located at the University of Georgia.
