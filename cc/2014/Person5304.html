Tom Goettl's Summary

tom.goettl@georgekonik.com http://www.georgekonik.com/job-listing

Tom Goettl is a successful Technical Recruiter in the Minnesota market for nearly 15 years. He has focused on placing superior engineering and architectural candidates for growing, successful companies in a variety of industries including medical device, consumer products, manufacturing, alternative energy and retail. Representative direct hire and contract positions include Engineering Manager, Principal Engineer, Medical Quality Engineer, Senior Mechanical Engineer, Embedded Controls Engineer, Electrical Engineer, Chemical Engineers, Mechanical Designer, Architect and Construction Project Manager.
Tom firmly believes having a passionate work ethic and a strong sense of responsibility to clients are the keys to his success.

Specialties: Engineering, Architecture, Medical Device, Chemical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Quality Engineering, SolidWorks, Pro/ENGINEER, Autodesk Inventor, Finance, Accounting, Recruiting, Talent Acquisition, Sales, Consulting, Career Counseling, Human Resources.
