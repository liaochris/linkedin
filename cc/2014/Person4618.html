Craig Pravda's Summary

Team-oriented professional with background in the start-ups of design firms that specialize in large scale, fast-track hospitality projects, independent/assisted living development, master planned residential development, Destination Healthcare, office condos, monastic complex design and mixed-use development.

As Co-founder of OFFICEHOMES™ : Workforce Housing for Independent Professionals our goal is to design and market I.D.E.A.S. that build community and stregthen identity.

Officehomes(tm) is a franchise of work/live housing that creates a community of like-minded professionals and offers affordable housing solutions for independent professinals, entrepreneurs and corporate refugees, while providing cost effective access to enterprise scale technology, systems and education through the aggregation of PEOPLE, PRODUCTS, INFORMATION, SERVICE and SPACE.

During the past 15 years as an architectural strategist / designer / real estate analyst and lifestyle consultant, I have assisted in managing or marketing of mixed-use projects between $.5M-$50M

Specialties: OFFICEHOMES™ : A work/live community of like-minded professionals is attractive for municipalities, developers and lifestyle center retail owners seeking to build the "Social Capital" infrastruture fostered in an OFFICEHOMES™ community.
