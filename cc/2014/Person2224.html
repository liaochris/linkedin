Belinda Liversedge's Experience

News reporter, SuperyachtNews.com

The Superyacht Group

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Maritime industry

January 2011 – October 2013 (2 years 10 months) London, United Kingdom

At the privately owned media company, The Superyacht Group, I delivered daily news content for its core news channel, SuperyachtNews.com, read by an audience of 23,000 industry professionals. I independently wrote and researched off-diary news and exclusives, with particular focus and interest in environmental, legal and political stories and marina development. News was global, requiring initiative and an eagle eye not to miss any event a competitor site might be covering.

In addition, I contributed features for our luxury title, TheSuperyachtOwner.com. Read by an exclusive readership of UHNWIs and their advisors, articles had to be informative, advice-orientated and elegantly written.

I also managed PR for the Group's several magazines and websites, including social media and positioning editors as spokespeople in the Financial Times, the Economist and Times Luxx.
