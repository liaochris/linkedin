John Simelbauer's Summary

I’m John Simelbauer, owner of Assisted Transition in St. Louis, MO. I am a senior advocate here to help serve the needs of seniors and their families in both St. Louis County and St. Charles County.

Rest assured that I know the challenges and decisions you are facing because I have been down this path both professionally and personally with my own family. My family has faced and successfully dealt with the challenges of keeping loved ones safe and addressing special needs of those who have been diagnosed with Parkinson’s, Alzheimer’s, Stroke, Brain Tumor, and Diabetes. These experiences have provided myself with first-hand knowledge of dealing with the confusion, stress, and frustration we all face when trying to find the correct elder care community. These experiences have also allowed myself to speak to the joys of successfully finding the perfect community for our loved ones.

I strive to make each placement ‘personal’ by working side-by-side with each senior and their family throughout the entire senior living and care journey. We will do this journey together. This will not be a click and search nightmare. My goal is to contact only the communities best meeting the needs and wants of my senior clients. This is accomplished by knowing a wide variety of communities in the St. Louis and St. Charles Counties.

I am here to help you and your family get the answers to your questions about any elder care issue. While my focus and expertise is on placement, I can also assist with your needs with other professionals who serve the senior community. These professionals include: Elder law, Estate Planning, VA benefits, Medicare, Medicaid to name but a few.

I look forward to the opportunity to meet with you, assess your needs, understand what is important to you and help prepare you to make the most informed decision possible.

Call me locally at (314) 775-7221. I am here to serve you and your family.
