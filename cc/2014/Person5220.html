Ted Banks' Summary

More than 35 years' experience in adding value by focusing on the needs of the customer/client and providing practical solutions to accomplish business goals.
On the legal side of my practice, I am focusing on antitrust counseling, as a partner in the firm of Scharf Banks Marmor in Chicago. My experience here has included extensive work in corporate transactions and all aspects of product distribution. In addition to working dozens of signficant corporate transactions, I am the author of a multi-volume treatise, Distribution Law: Antitrust Principles & Practice, published by Aspen/Wolters-Kluwer.
As a consultant, I assist corporations of all sizes in creating or enhancing compliance programs. Effective systems can support corporate business goals, prevent legal and ethical violations, and make employees proud of their company -- and thereby better employees. I have edited a multi-author text on the subject, Corporate Legal Compliance Handbook, also published by Aspen/Wolters-Kluwer.

Specialties: Lawyers aren't really allowed to list "specialties" except in very limited areas. In this section I am not listing official legal specialties, but wish to indicate that over the years my legal practice has included antitrust, compliance, corporate transactions, complex litigation, employment, environment, securities, international business, and all aspects of product distribution (including advertising, sales, promotion, contracting, logistics, merchandising).
