Chris Lindsley's Summary

Twenty-five years as editor of award-winning sports and medical publications and Web sites.

Ten years as Web site editor for major academic medical center -- the University of Maryland Medical Center (www.umm.edu) -- and a consumer health Web site -- accenthealth.com.

Experience managing search engine marketing, social networking and ROI documentation campaigns.

Award-winning writer, editor and blogger whose work has appeared in the Washington Post, Pittsburgh Post Gazette, the Washington Business Journal and many other publications.

Organize workplace volunteering efforts, and am a part of UMMC's employee loyalty team, which is charged with making it a better place to work. I've also volunteered for Columbia Lighthouse for the Blind, Big Brothers and Big Sisters and as a youth sports basketball, baseball and softball coach, as well as scout leader.

Specialties

Web site development, writing and editing, managing staff and contractors, search term marketing, social networking, developing interactive tools, blogger, ROI, physician relations/communication, executive reports, employee satisfaction, volunteer coordinator
