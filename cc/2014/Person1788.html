Summary

Sven Johnston is a Partner & SVP Business Development at GigaSavvy, an advertising agency with a core focus on SEO (Search Engine Optimization), Social Media Marketing and Web Design & Development (Wordpress & Magento eCommerce). He has 15+ years experience in international business development and has a passion for building long-term strategic partnerships. He has worked for Fortune 500 companies all the way down to small entrepreneurial businesses.

No stranger to complex challenges, he thrives on linking businesses to success. He has a passion for taking on projects that seem impossible, even a company like LinkedIn® could not detour his vision. After a 5 month online media campaign, he convinced the Board of LinkedIn® to give Orange County its own regional location code.

Sven is a LinkedIn® power user with 6666+ connections (Not an open connector). He has taught several thousand people how to maximize LinkedIn® for their job search as well as business development in one on one sessions and speaking at events throughout Southern California.

Using his passion for online marketing (SEO - Search Engine Optimization, Social Media Marketing and Web Design & Development) he helps businesses to be more successful in building the company brand and increasing sales online.

A very active member of the Orange County community, Sven runs a successful LinkedIn group called “We Are Orange County!™” with over 7700 members and actively volunteers in the community.

Sven is looking for like-minded people to have various discussions, share referrals to build business, turn virtual connections into long term relationships and most importantly “Pay It Forward”.

To contact Sven please send him a message via LinkedIn or reach out via the contact form on www.GigaSavvy.com.
