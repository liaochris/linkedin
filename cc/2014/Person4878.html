Ryan Gahl's Summary

I am a self driven full stack modern web architect with a well rounded background that includes nuclear reactor operator, founder and tech lead of two web technology startups, full-featured web framework author, team lead, and perpetual tinkerer.

I have been delivering highly scalable cloud based web applications and sites since 2007. Built rich reporting UI for Mashery.com, full-featured LMS for BankersEdge.com, and a complete video interviewing platform for MontageTalent.com, to name just a few.

I have been using node.js since its v0.1.x days, CouchDB since Damien started it, and have been creating web frameworks to facilitate the creation of complex single page applications since before AJAX was a buzzword.

I thrive in startups and high pressure environments. I am adept at moving teams and products forward. I keep up to date on new technology, but am not one to jump at every squirrel just because it's new and someone wrote a blog article about it.

I am passionate about UX, to a fault. My favorite UX designer is Jacky Powell, great friend and designer of the PS3 XMB interface.
