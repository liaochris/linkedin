Joe Stampone's Summary

Joe Stampone is Vice President of Investments at Atlas Real Estate Partners. In his role, Joe is responsible for sourcing and analyzing value-added real estate investments and developing the company's strategic vision.

Joe received a BA in Economics with a minor in Entrepreneurial Leadership from Tufts University. While at Tufts, Joe was a four-year varsity soccer player. Joe also received a masters in real estate finance from the NYU Schack Institute of Real Estate where he served as President of the Student Association and he is currently a TA for the Real Estate Finance & Investments Class.

Joe's passion for real estate is also evident in his blogging where he has been named one of the top 10 most influential commercial real estate bloggers.

Joe also enjoys skiing, boating, golf, cooking, and traveling.
