Background

Summary

Julien Codorniou serves as Director of Platform Partnerships at Facebook, managing the team responsible for Facebook’s developer relations and platform marketing. The team's mission is to support the growth of the Facebook ecosystem in EMEA by helping developers to build, grow and monetize their apps and games with the Facebook Platform.

Formerly director of business development at Microsoft Corp where he created and launched the BizSpark program for startups. Julien started his career in the finance/VC industry.

Since 2012, Julien serves at the board of the french newspaper Le Monde, he’s also the co-author of the book: "The Kelkoo.com success story", published by Pearson in 2005.
