Noelle Salerno's Summary

My specialties include marketing, branding, and strategy development. My strengths lie in telling a community’s unique story and developing their authentic brand and marketing messages. While at Avalanche, I have worked on marketing and branding strategies for the Greater Houston Partnership, Research Valley Partnership, Charlotte Regional Partnership, and Lowcountry Economic Alliance. I served as project manager on a strategic plan for Decatur EDC and as assistant project manager on strategic plans for Brownwood EDC and Bastrop EDC. I have also completed marketing and website reviews for Henry County, Georgia and Hot Springs Metro Partnership.

Prior to joining Avalanche, I was Executive Team Leader at Target, where I oversaw guest relations and drove sales via strategic planning and management of daily store operations. My additional experience includes creating and implementing marketing plans for various fundraisers and product launches as the Program Assistant for the University of Florida CPR & First Aid Training Center. Prior to that, I interned at Centa Business Services in London, where I helped carry out their marketing initiatives and developed presentations for business start up seminars.

I am a 2012 graduate of Leadership Austin’s Emerge program. I hold a M.A. in International Business and a B.S. in Business Administration from the University of Florida.
