Alex Linebrink's Summary

Hi - I'm Alex.

I currently serve as CEO at Core Detroit / Core Merchant - a financial and business services technology company in the Quicken Loans / Rock family of companies which I co-founded in Detroit, MI in July 2010. In addition to a strong business leadership training at Bizdom U, I've served in leadership positions with 20+ organizations, spent 7 years heading up a design and marketing firm, and I have a diverse educational background ranging from philosophy to electrical engineering. With a strong skill-set in design, marketing, and communications, I have a unique combination of abilities to spot trends, develop strategy, and communicate vision - and I love to use it.

As an entrepreneur, I plan to continually bring new and innovative ideas from dream to reality by building teams, communicating vision, and leading both to their fullest potential.

Specialties: Leadership, communications, speaking, organizational strategy, vision, design, and a pretty intense hobby of staying up-to-date on the latest in connected technologies and social media.
