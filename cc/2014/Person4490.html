Oliver Díez Hart's Summary

Oliver has large experience in RE, having worked in new property developments, asset management (Aktua/Centerbridge Partners) and consultancy firms (PwC) for national and international clients.

- Advise and market distressed RE assets in all the Spanish territory.
- Valuation of RE assets. REOs/Collateral Portfolio analysis. Transactions management.
- Business processes set up.
- KAM of the RE platforms from the spanish intervened banks that have transferred their RE assets on to SAREB.
- New sales channels release (Special Servicers, Own RE agents network, Auctions, etc.)

Oliver holds a degree in Business & Administration and an Executive MBA from IE Business School.
