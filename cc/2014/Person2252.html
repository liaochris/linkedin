Beverly Garland's Summary

Beverly Garland is on a mission to inspire others who share the dream to make the world a kinder place. She is a freelance artist, adventurer and personal development guide with formal training as an Avatar® Master, Life Coach, and Agile ScrumMaster. She gained 18 years of “cat herding” experience managing teams of computer game developers for EA and NCsoft, and provided career coaching and resume services to entertainment executives through Mary-Margaret.com. In addition to producing art that celebrates nature, she empowers people to discover and live their inspired life purpose via the Avatar Course, and leads tours at the Houston Museum of Natural Science.
