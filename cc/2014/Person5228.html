Teri S's Summary

Soulful R&B Singer, Songwriter and Recording Artist. Teri S has an extraordinary effect on her audiences. Teri's style and sound captures all ears. Accompanied by an all-star 5-piece band and sultry background singers, she is extremely blessed with such a powerful and beautiful voice. Her ultimate dream is to share her gift with the world! Teri S's career started at an early age. Teri S has performed at Inaugural Balls, singing for the late Dr. Dorothy Height, and the late Coretta Scott King and a host of other dignitaries and political figures. Teri S fans, family and friends consider her to be very talented, entertaining and original. She has performed at the “Apollo Theatre” and landed a major singing and acting role on an Off- Broadway play at the Lincoln Theatre in Washington, D.C. She has opened and sang with seasoned artists such as the late Ollie Woodson and Dennis Edwards; Delfonics, and Blue Majic. She has shared the stage with Regina Belle, Kem and Kenny Lattimore. She has appeared on Fox 5 Morning News for a Food Drive Benefit. She surrenders her voice to fans at Washington's annual outdoor concerts, as she recently opened for ZAP. Over the years, she's done voice-overs and several local and national shows, affording her the honor of singing for Patti Labelle in Philadelphia, PA and the syndicated Tom Joyner Morning Show at the DAR Constitution Hall. She has performed at the Legendary “Blues Alley”, consistently over the past several years, and always captivates a star-studded audience including Joe Herndon of “The Temptations”, Dr. JC Hayward, WUSA – Ch 9, and many other influential figures. Teri S was fortunate to perform live at the White House in the past few years. Teri released her Debut CD entitled “In The Middle’ in 2007 which has and still is receiving rave reviews all over the world. She released her sophomore project in July 2012 and plans to release a worldwide distribution very soon!! Teri S has a sound you will not want to pass up!
