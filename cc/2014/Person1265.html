John Wehr's Summary

I am a hands-on technical leader with a track record of building successful teams.

As co-founder and CTO of Tred I am changing the way people buy and sell cars. Tred launched in 2013 after completing the Techstars program. Featured in Time, Fox News, and Autoweek, Tred partners with the largest names in the automotive industry.

As CTO of HiiDef I assembled the fifteen person team behind Flavors.me, a website creation platform, and Goodsie, a social e-commerce platform. Launched in 2009, Flavors.me grew to more than one million active users with 142% growth in 2011, and was acquired by Moo.com in 2013. Goodsie, a Business Insider top startup, hosts thousands of storefronts and experienced 20% month over month growth during my tenure.

Before my roles as CTO I worked as a contract developer in a wide array of verticals, including travel, supply chain management, health care, and gaming. I apply my diverse experience to leverage the best practices, processes, and ideas of each industry. I provided robust solutions for global corporations such as Wal-Mart and custom software to emerging startups.

You can reach me at johnwehr@gmail.com. I look forward to hearing from you.

A year later I helped build a second version of Flavors.me, redefining the crowded space. I was responsible for the core technology behind innovative new features like real time stream processing, extracting non-linear updates from dozens of third-party services.
