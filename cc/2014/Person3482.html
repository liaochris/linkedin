Jessica Ward's Summary

Jessica Ward is a marketing professional with over 8 years experience in many areas of expertise. She is a Senior Account Executive at Amplify Interactive who is focused on Social Media, Paid Search, and SEO while also performing the role of Project Manager for several accounts.

Before moving to Portland, Jessica was a Senior Marketing Analyst for Forest Health Services, parent company for Barix Clinics, in Ypsilanti, MI. She was responsible for all marketing and media strategy for up to seven locations around the US and reported directly to the Senior Vice President of Marketing. Jessica holds a Bachelor of Business Administration with a concentration in Marketing from Eastern Michigan University in Ypsilanti, MI.
