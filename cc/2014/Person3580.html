John Hollon's Summary

I'm a leader and pretty much always have been. I am also a manager, an accomplished and award-winning columnist and blogger, and a sharp, thoughtful and visionary editor who has a lifetime of media experiences from print to online.

On top of that, I'm also a workforce management expert -- largely because of my deep management experience but also because I have seen so much management done so badly, by so many, for so very long. It's why I believe that the TV show "Undercover Boss" is actually an inside joke to show just how bad and clueless so many executives and senior managers really are.

I'm proud to say that my overall approach is thoughtful and pragmatic. You won't see me embrace the latest flavor-of-the-month management practices espoused by so many noisy commentators who are full of provocative opinions but terribly light on evidence or experience to back them up. Yes, my expertise actually comes from years of hard work, occasional failures, and frequent successes. But, I survived and learned from all of it and I continue to evolve, experience, and work very hard.

My expertise flows from a lifetime of good and bad experiences managing people in groups from large to small. Want my philosophy? It's this: I believe in the power of people working together to make something greater than they could ever build on their own. And, I believe in the ability of smart and focused management to help lead them in the quest to do it.

I am good at most everything a manager and executive should be, but some of my specialties include:
** Change management
** Employee engagement
** Best practices
** Editorial leadership in both digital/online and print
** Writing and editing
** Union negotiations
** Common sense leadership
** Civility
