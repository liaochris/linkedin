Carolyn Goodman's Summary

Strong strategic marketer, with solid experience in both B2B and B2C marketing.

With a heavy emphasis on the initial Discovery Process, I pride myself on listening, thinking, asking questions, analyzing and unearthing the real "problem" behind the marketing need. Then we design and execute smart marketing solutions that align to deliver measurable results.

In 2002 I started a full-service, fully integrated, direct marketing agency after spending years working for "the big boys" -- and discovered that by building a team of talented, seasoned professionals who have "been there" and "done that," we could compete with the best agencies out there.

Our services include the design and development of integrated marketing programs, direct marketing programs (including direct mail packages and direct response advertising for print and broadcast), lead generation programs, retention programs, and content marketing programs. Check out our website for samples of our work at www.goodmanmarketing.com.

Email me (carolyn@goodmanmarketing.com) or call me (415) 507.9060 ext 222 -- I'm always happy to talk about YOUR marketing need, and offer my POV and new ideas in our very first conversation.

I also invite you to read my marketing insights on blog on Target Marketing called "The Power Punch" -- http://www.targetmarketingmag.com/channel/the-power-punch.
