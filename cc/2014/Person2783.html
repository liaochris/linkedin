Gia Milazzo Smith's Summary

Designs by Gia is a full service interior design firm specializing in Residential and Commercial Interior Design. Our services include, Design and color consultation, Custom Design Planning, and Complete Project Management. We are very well known for our Custom Kitchen and Bath Designs.

We believe that a space should convey the personality and tastes of the owner, and function to meet the needs for which it was designed. A well designed space will work to enhance the lives of those who use it, providing both comfort and inspiration. Our firm will work with you to create a space which meets all of your design needs.
