Rich Hein's Summary

As the web evolves so must our thinking about how we use it. Many organizations are finding that having a website just isn't enough. To have a successful company today you need to have solid Internet strategy that includes good content creation, social media, search engine optimization, a site that connects customers to the products they want in a simple elegant way and an astute understanding of what your eCommerce goals and metrics are. This is where I come in.

Whether your objective is:
- Audience Development
- To create new compelling content;
- To grow your site's traffic;
- Increase client engagement;
- Launch profitable new products and services;
- Create a sustained competitive advantage through service excellence; or
- Position your team as recognized thought leaders in your industry

Jack of all trades, master of a few. My ethos is simple, do what you say you're going to do. I have over 11 years experience in Internet strategies, content creation, search engine marketing, search engine optimization and site analytics. I have advanced skills across a wide range of software, systems and languages and offer a unique combination of technical expertise and business savvy to support you in your Internet publishing and marketing objectives. I can help you create a website strategy to meet your specific business and program goals.

I have earned a respected reputation among my peers and clients as a leader who combines an exceptional level of technical expertise with unwavering integrity, professionalism, and commitment to finding the right solution to the right problem, for the right price.

Specialties: Problem solving, Writing, Editing, HTML, PHP, JavaScript, Search Engine Optimization, Technical Authoring, Microsoft Developer Technologies, Screencast/Video Creation, Production and Distribution
