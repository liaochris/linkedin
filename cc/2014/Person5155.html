Sujit Jasani - @SujitJasani's Summary

Sujit Jasani is an award winning sports communications and strategic media consultant who has more than 10 years' experience working with UK and international sports clubs, federations and governing bodies, world class athletes, global brands and countries/cities bidding to host major sporting events. He is currently a Director at Vero Communications, a sports communications agency founded by Mike Lee OBE.

Sujit’s communications experience includes winning campaigns for the successful:

*Rio 2016 Olympic Games Bid
*PyeongChang 2018 Olympic Winter Games Bid
*Buenos Aires 2018 Youth Olympic Games Bid
*Takeover of Liverpool FC by Fenway Sports Group

He has also advised and directed communications programmes for sports organisations, including:

*UEFA
*Liverpool FC
*Chelsea FC
*Leo Messi Foundation/Aspire Academy
*Rio 2016 Organising Committee
*International Rugby Board
*International Triathlon Union
*UK Sport
*British Athletics
*ICC Cricket World Cup

Sujit additionally created and implemented international communications campaigns for global sponsors at the Athens 2004 Olympic and Paralympic Games, the Torino 2006 Olympic and Paralympic Winter Games and IRB Rugby World Cup 2003 and 2007.

He has worked with and advised high-profile sporting figures including Leo Messi, Pele, Tiger Woods, Michael Schumacher, Maria Sharapova, Michael Phelps and Sir Steve Redgrave.

In 2007 Sujit received the PRWeek '29 Under 29' award, an accolade handed out to the UK’s top 29 PR professionals under the age of 29. He was also a judge for the PRWeek '30 Under 30' award in 2013 and is a judge for the forthcoming 2014 PRWeek Awards.

Sujit has presented at major sports conferences, including the International Sports Event Management Conference in London, the Sports & Events Tourism Exchange in Durban and the UK Sport Rugby League World Cup 2013 Observer Programme. He is also a lecturer for the International Centre for Sport Studies' FIFA Master course at De Montfort University.

Sujit Jasani - @SujitJasani's Experience

Director

Vero Communications

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Sports industry

2009 – Present (5 years) London, United Kingdom

www.verocom.co.uk

Vero was established in January 2006 by Mike Lee OBE – former Director of Communications and Public Affairs for London’s successful 2012 Olympic bid – with the purpose of offering campaigning style communications, brand positioning, campaign planning, and stakeholder and media relations solutions to clients in the business of sport.

Most recently, Vero advised British Cycling President, Brian Cookson, during his successful campaign to become president of Union Cycliste Internationale. The agency was also at the centre of Buenos Aires' successful bid for the 2018 Youth Olympic Games. Vero additionally worked on PyeongChang's successful bid for the 2018 Olympic and Paralympic Winter Games and Rio’s historic 2016 Olympic and Paralympic bid. Vero also supported the IRB for Rugby Sevens’ successful campaign for inclusion in the Olympic Games.

Vero works with a variety of clients including:

- Domestic and international sports federations, major event organising committees, national sports agencies and sports governing bodies who want to enhance their profile
- Countries bidding to host FIFA World Cup
- Premier League football clubs
- International/National Federation Presidents on their election campaigns
- The Olympic Movement, National Olympic Committees and cities bidding to host the Olympic and Paralympic Games
- National government and tourism agencies
- Brands and sponsors that want to leverage sporting associations
- Individual sports personalities, sports leaders and business executives

Burson-Marsteller

Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; WPPGY; Public Relations and Communications industry

October 2002 – June 2008 (5 years 9 months) London, United Kingdom

Burson-Marsteller is a global public relations and communications firm focusing on building and sustaining strong corporate and brand reputations for its clients. B-M's clients are global companies, industry associations, professional services firms, governments, and other large organizations.

Whilst at Burson-Marsteller, Sujit was responsible for devising and implementing creative campaigns for corporate and consumer clients. He led B-M London's sports and sponsorship PR offering and also managed national and international communications programmes across a diverse range of industry sectors including entertainment, automotive, FMCG, and technology.

Sujit’s client portfolio included global brands such as Visa, BT, Sony Ericcson, Alfa Romeo, Bacardi and Heineken.
