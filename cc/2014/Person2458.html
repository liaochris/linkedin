Chad Massaker's Summary

While pursuing a degree in psychology at Kennesaw State University, I got caught up in the information technology bubble and skipped my plans to pursue a terminal degree in psychology. When I graduated, I went to work for a New York-based hospitality management company called Sentry Hospitality, that specialized in managing 4-star+ conference centers and resorts. Four years later, I had designed, installed and managed the networks of several large resorts and conference centers across the country while also providing ongoing IT support to those properties.

Then 9-11 happened & the hospitality market tanked and I found myself without a job. I essentially was forced to resign & become a vendor to Sentry which was how Carceron was born. I partnered up with the man who had gotten me into computers in college, Jeffrey Lee, and we never looked back.

Now, well over 12 years later, we serve clients all over the country, primarily in the professional services industries such as healthcare, financial services, legal services, etc. We specialize in helping businesses maximize productivity and collaboration with their IT investments. We accomplish this by providing a suite of various managed IT services, including:

► IT Support (Helpdesk, Monitoring, etc.)
► Outsourced CTO Services
► Cloud Services (SaaS & IaaS)
► Telecom / VoIP Services
► Regulatory Compliance Solutions
► HIPAA Compliance Training
► Internet Connectivity (Broadband) Solutions
► Strategic IT Consulting
