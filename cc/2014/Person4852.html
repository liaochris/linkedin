Richard Sink's Summary

“The companies that perform best over time build a social purpose into their operations that is as important as their economic purpose.” —Harvard Business Review

What's your strategy for being found in today's networked world?. The challenge is that most businesses find they don't know which tools or processes to use or how to effectively utilize them. My goals are to generate demand for your business via thoughtful, meaningful, time-tested expertise and fresh ideas that are shared by word-of-mouth and enhanced by influencer outreach, organic SEO and earned media that will drive anticipated results.

Here's how I help you:

• Strategic sessions with dedicated solutions designed to amplify your value proposition in the marketplace in order to attract and engage in the right relationships, even when they’re not looking for you.
• Research and identify social mediums through which your target market can be reached and engaged.
• Make your competition potential collaborators.
• Attract and retain more and better qualified prospects to your events, web site and call-to-action initiatives.
• Ensure that your business has a better online presence than your competitors.
• Establish a how and when follow-up plan that maps out how our work interacts with others.
• Add value by helping to accelerate the process, minimizing false starts, helping you anticipate roadblocks or problems, enabling you to make better decisions.
• Demonstrate ways to simply and effectively leverage your strengths that help you invest in your own solutions.
• Build a connected online strategy that incorporates established social properties and brings to life your innovative and compelling value proposition.
• Share, and in many cases own, the responsibility for the success of your online business engagement initiatives.

Can social media deliver qualified leads and revenue generating opportunities to your business initiatives? Yes it can and in many ways!
