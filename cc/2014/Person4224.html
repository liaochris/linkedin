Megan Geyer's Summary

Megan is an experience design professional striving to ensure that all audiences are understood and represented throughout the design and implementation of any service or product. Megan joined MISI Company in 2009 (now NTT DATA Americas) and now holds the position of Experience Design Strategist. Megan has worked on many cross-channel audience research and design projects with pharmaceutical, financial, industrial, and non-profit clients.

Previous to her joining MISI Company, Megan was project coordinator at Radiant Digital where she not only managed user experience projects but also worked on information architecture and interaction design for consumer-facing websites in the nutrition, education, music/entertainment, and financial industries. Megan is a member of the Usability Professionals Association. She holds a Bachelor’s degree in Psychology from Rutgers University.

Specialties: usability, user experience research, experience design, brand identity design, spatial design, information architecture, interaction design, change management, B2B, B2C, game design/production
