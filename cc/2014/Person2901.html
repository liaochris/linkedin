Distinguished Engineer

LSI Corporation
2007 – 2012 (5 years)Allentown, PA

Distinguished Engineer (top 2% of technical population)
Multi-Core DSP Software Developer:
- Primary technical contact for first customer of SP2603 multi-core media processor (in production).
- Planned, developed, and executed subjective and automated tests of media gateway echo canceller.
- Developed software tools for test automation: hybrid echo generator for capacity test; enhanced P.501 CSS generator; G.160 Gaussian noise generator; G.168 filters; signal analysis for power and delay.
- Developed Serial RapidIO driver and service for SP2704 media processor. Verified at wire speed across 8 DSP cores.
- Integrated and developed regression tests in LSI media gateway for echo canceller and RTCP protocol.

LTE Baseband Software:
- Studied 3GPP LTE standards through Release 10. Read books and papers on SC-FDMA, MIMO. Wrote papers proposing software architecture on planned basestation L1 SoC.
- Learned software development on Silicon Hive (now Intel) vector engines. Developed optimized matrix inversion module.

Network Processor Subsystem Validation:
- Developed bare-metal executive and driver software on dual-core ARM11MP to verify TDM and SPI interfaces of APP3000 network processor as they would be used to transport VoIP traffic. Validated on Cadence Palladium simulator, then in first silicon.
- Developed TDM PBX demo among FXS and FXO ports on LSI multi-service business gateway. Wrote serial driver including command-line editor and monitor program for demo.
- Ported PBX demo to validate new FXO phone line interface devices, now available on the market.

Standards Development:
- Represented Agere / LSI in TIA TR-30 standards committee continuously for over 10 years.
- Standards editor for TIA-793, TIA-921, G.1050 network impairment models.

Consulting Member of Technical Staff

Agere Systems
2002 – 2007 (5 years)Holmdel, NJ / Allentown, PA

Consulting MTS (top 2% of technical population)

- Specified requirements for SV92PP and SV92EX companion ICs for PCI and PCI Express modems, respectively. Sold over 100 million CSP1040 to date, plus millions of SV92PP and SV92EX devices.
- Primary technical contact for lead PC-OEM modem customer for over 10 years. Received customer recognitions and awards in this role. $50M/yr peak revenue.

Distinguished Member of Technical Staff

Lucent Technologies

- Performed extensive competitive assessment. Rapid test turnaround led to timely delivery of PCM modems to the market.
- Conceived, designed, equipped, and managed worldwide field test of first V.90 PCM modems. Uncovered product defects in time to repair, avoiding expensive post-ship rework.
- Extended test lab with all the world's V.92 digital server modems, crucial to prove inter-operation.

Program Manager for PCI Modem IC, First V.92 Modems:

Other relevant expertise:
Developer, and then team leader, for series of transmission and data communications systems, all of which shipped to the field. Applied skills in logic design, microprocessor system design, C and assembler programming, customer support.
