Russell Hammond's Summary

I help firms develop scalable business models. Currently I serve as a Product Manager for Connexio Labs and formerly was a member of Mood Media's New Product Development (NPD) team.

In addition to my work at Connexio Labs and Mood Media I have partnered with developers on different ventures such as myHeatmap.

Before I earned an MBA I spent 7 years working for a non-profit, Sigma Nu Fraternity Inc. I helped refine our approach to chapter creation and expansion and then learned the skills necessary to fund major programs while working on the foundation side.

Over the years I have also worked as a ski instructor in Colorado and a white water raft guide in North Carolina.

Specialties: Business model development, product development, crowdsourcing, and fundraising.
