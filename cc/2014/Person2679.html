Craig Danuloff's Summary

Craig is a product-focused entrepreneur who has delivered products in the earliest stages of four important software and internet technology markets: desktop publishing, electronic commerce, online marketing, and personal data. In each case, he defined a compelling product vision, designed and managed the development of marketing leading products, and played a leading role in the promotion and evangelism of the markets and products.

Specialties: Strategic vision, recruiting, agile product planning and management, business development & strategic partnerships, product marketing.

Craig Danuloff's Experience

EVP

Appinions Inc.

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Computer Software industry

January 2014 – Present (9 months) Greater New York City Area

Appinions is an influence marketing platform, using natural language processing to identify who's driving a market, what they're talking about, and where those conversations are taking place. It's used by marketers at Fortune 1000 B2B brands to better understand their markets and competition, and better execute marketing programs.

Founder, CEO

Rewind Me Inc.

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Computer Software industry

January 2012 – December 2013 (2 years) Greater New York City Area

Rewind.Me is a rewards platform for life. It offers people the chance to privately share their personal information in return for personalized offers and rewards. Our SaaS platform enables marketers to turn rich permission-based data (social, transactional, and behavioral) into new ways to analyze, segment and personalize for existing customers, and to reach pre-qualified prospects.
