Christopher Hayes' Summary

I have practiced with Borowsky & Hayes LLP and its predecessor firm since 1996. As co-counsel in most of the firm's cases, I have successfully represented clients such as these:

• 60 physicians and allied professionals defrauded of their medical practice group, causing them tens of millions of dollars in damages: Partial settlement of nearly $5 million from the directors and officers of the business that defrauded them, and ongoing lawsuit to recover from their own financial advisor.

• A nurse manager fired for protesting violations of federal and state safety standards at a psychiatric ward for elderly patients: Substantial jury verdict, upheld on appeal.

• A physician improperly reported to the state medical board because he treated a patient at her request: Judgment restoring his record.

• An individual denied life insurance benefits when spouse's accidental death was wrongly deemed a suicide: Full recovery.

• Other individuals and businesses in employment, insurance, personal injury, and contract cases.

I serve as Chancellor and general counsel of the Episcopal Diocese of California, advising the Bishop and clergy and lay leaders on canon law and external legal affairs.

I am a member of the State Bar of California, admitted to practice in the following courts:

United States Supreme Court
United States Court of Appeals for the Ninth Circuit
United States District Court for all districts located in California
California Supreme Court, California Courts of Appeal, and California Superior Courts
