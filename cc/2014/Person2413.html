Carlos Sanchez's Summary

Improving development process for faster delivery of reliable software through the development lifecycle, implementing Continuous Delivery and DevOps philosophy, for Fortune 100 and Global 500 companies.

Development of distributed applications' full stack using Java, Ruby, JavaScript,...; maintenance and deployment to production using Puppet, Docker and different cloud and virtual providers.

Interacting with global and highly distributed intercultural teams in different continents, mentoring and enabling people for increased productivity.

Applying open source solutions to the enterprise, component and community oriented development and global team management.

Contributing to open source projects for more than 10 years. Member at the Apache Software Foundation, project lead at the Eclipse Foundation, committer at the Codehaus Foundation, contributor to Puppet (top contributor of Puppet modules), Fog, Spring Security,…

Speaker at development conferences and user groups in several years: JavaOne, EclipseCON, ApacheCON US/EU, JavaZone, Fosdem, PuppetConf, Agile Testing Days, Javagruppen, Paris JUG, Los Angeles JUG,...

Coauthor of Better Builds With Maven (Mergere, 2006), the first book on Maven 2, and reviewer of other books on Maven, Puppet and Spring.

Specialties: DevOps, Continuous Delivery, Open Source in the Enterprise, Cloud Computing, Architecting and Best Practices, Agile methodologies, Offshore and Worldwide Distributed development, Team building.
