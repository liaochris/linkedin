Joseph K. Levene's Summary

Joseph K. Levene Fine Art, Ltd., an International Dealer of secondary market Modern, Post War & Contemporary Fine Art, was established by Joseph K. Levene in 1991. Robert Grunder, Director and Joseph K. Levene, Principal, together have 3 decades multifaceted Fine Art experience as Art Dealer, Art Collector, Fine Art Executive and Online Art Sales.

A graduate of Wharton, Joseph K. Levene was previously President/COO, Petersburg Press, Inc., International Fine Art Publisher; Director e-commerce, artnet.com; Charter Member, Sothebys.com online fine art auctions.

In 1999, we launched the Joseph K Levene Fine Art Ltd Store on eBay featuring Blue-Chip Art and Fine Art Photography; since the time of our launch in 1999, Joseph K. Levene Art, Ltd. has guaranteed Authenticity for all Fine Art offered and sold on eBay.

Specializing in Andy Warhol, Jasper Johns, Cindy Sherman and important Blue-Chip Art, Joseph K. Levene Fine Art, Ltd. represents the Estate of Tony Rosenthal, Sculptor of Public Art, best known for creating Alamo, 1967, aka Astor Place Cube, the first Contemporary Public Art Sculpture purchased by New York City.
