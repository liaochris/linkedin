Summary

Executive-level business development expert with demonstrated success designing creative strategies to achieve organizational goals. Passionate about wellness, integrative medicine, neuroscience and improving the delivery of cutting-edge patient centered care.

Community learning experiences, professional patient advocacy services, writing, and public speaking are the ways White Horse Medicine™ serves its goals to empower patients and caregivers alike to:

• Facilitate empathy
• Relieve stress and build resilience
• More easily navigate personal health crises or organizational change
• Thrive in a fast-paced, increasingly technology-driven world
• Create an inspired family, organization, or workplace that supports true human development
• Develop self-responsibility for health and business outcomes
