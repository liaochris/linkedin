Steve Pacheco's Summary

Managing Director of Advertising & Sponsorship at FedEx - a company built on the shoulders of great marketing and outstanding advertising and widely known for “absolutely, positively” creating one of the world’s most powerful and recognized brands in the world.

Directs all advertising and sponsorship marketing for various FedEx brands including national TV, print, outdoor, interactive and Sports & Event Marketing. Responsible for the day-to-day management of agency relationships, determining advertising research and testing needs, administering advertising and research budgets, managing a professional staff, monitoring all competitive advertising activity and serving as the lead liaison between FedEx Global Marketing and their numerous agencies.

Specialties: World class advertising campaign management, Sports Marketing, Sponsorship Marketing Global Brand building

Honors and Awards:

Under his leadership, the company has developed and produced the highly acclaimed “Relax, it’s FedEx” U.S. TV advertising campaign, now in its third year. The campaign has received widespread acclaim and has won numerous industry awards including ADDYs, ANDYs and One Show Pencils. It was one of only three U.S. TV campaigns to receive a Gold Lion at the Cannes International Advertising Festival last year, and in August 2006 was awarded a Prime Time EMMY Award.

Since joining FedEx in 1997, Pacheco has overseen other award-winning efforts. These include being named a Finalist for the 2000 Prime Time EMMY award, being selected one of Ad Age’s top campaigns of the year in 2001, earning a Silver Lion at Cannes in 2002, receiving BtoB magazine’s “Advertising of the Year” award in 2003, Gold Lion at Cannes in 2005 and the Prime Time EMMY in 2006.
