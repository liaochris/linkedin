Ake Rudolf's Summary

Ake Rudolf is an independent curator, programme and project developer. His work focuses on the complex framework of social, cultural, aesthetic, technological and economic factors that underly design processes. He organised design exhibitions and presentations during events such as Tokyo Design Week, Seoul Design Festival, Taiwan Design Expo, St.Etienne Design Biennial, Milan Week of Design among others. From 2008 - 2011 Rudolf shaped the content strategy of the DMY International Design Festival Berlin. He currently develops projects and international programmes for the International Design Center Berlin.

Specialties: Developing concepts of contemporary design programmes, managing projects, building international relations, commissioning creative projects, conceptualising workshops, developing conferences, match-making designers and industry decision makers, supervising project implementation.
