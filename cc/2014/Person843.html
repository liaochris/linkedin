Benji Rabhan's Summary

Specialties: Conversion Rate Optimization, Marketing Consulting, Web Development, Marketing Automation

Benji Rabhan is the founder and CEO of MorrisCore and its subsidiary companies ConversionCore, ClickCore, and AutomationCore. Through speaking, training, and consulting, Benji helps large and small businesses improve their online presence and make more money with their websites

Benji is a thought leader in conversion rate optimization, marketing automation, and online marketing. He is a New York Times Best Selling Author, a #1 Barnes & Noble Best Selling Author, and an Amazon Best Seller Author.

As a recipient of the Empact100 List of Top American Entrepreneurs, he now consults with multi-million-dollar companies helping them maintain sustainable growth despite a challenging economic environment.

Benji's first book, “Failure is Obsolete", reveals an easy-to-use formula for testing high-risk ideas in a low-risk environment, predicting likelihood of success before you waste a lot of time and money moving in the wrong direction. It is an Amazon bestseller.

His newest book, “Convert Every Click,” shows readers a holistic approach to conversion rate optimization that encompasses an entire business—online and offline—to drive more sales and referrals, and increase bottom-line profits. It is a New York Times, Amazon, and B&N Bestseller.
