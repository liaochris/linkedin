Devin Hughes' Summary

I am currently an Account Executive at Global Prairie in their Cleveland, OH office. At Global Prairie, I am heavily involved in our social media analysis & community management, as well as media relations activities. I am passionate about using data to identify strategic, integrated marketing strategies with measurable results.

I graduated in 2011 from Ohio University's E.W. Scripps School of Journalism, where I studied public relations. I also obtained a minor in business administration. After graduation, I worked as a Social Influence Specialist at The Adcom Group / Landau Public Relations in Cleveland, OH until March 2013.

I have held communications internships in a variety of different arenas, including agency (Fleishman-Hillard), corporate (Cardinal Health), nonprofit (O'Bleness Memorial Hospital) and higher education (Ohio University College of Education).

At Ohio University, I held leadership roles with our PRSSA chapter, as well as our award-winning student-run PR firm, ImPRessions. At the end of my senior year, I was selected by the faculty as the Outstanding Graduate in Public Relations, as well as being honored with a Central Ohio PRSA Award of Excellence for Outstanding Graduating Senior.

Specialties: Social Media Analysis, Community Management, Professional Communication Strategy, News Writing, Media Relations and Public Relations Planning.
