Bernhard Steiner's Summary

Bernhard is a highly motivated global travel, aviation, and business management professional with expert proficiency in delivering outstanding customer service, driving sales revenue using conventional as well as electronic and social media platforms, exhibiting charismatic leadership, and providing time-sensitive and innovative logistical solutions to worldwide operational challenges. Being accustomed to the dynamic nature inherent to travel and private aviation, Bernhard is well-versed in continuously exploring new horizons, innovative strategies and leadership with a strong international and multicultural focus.

Bernhard has gained his professional acumen in the areas of business development, international operations and product development while managing teams and business units with full P&L responsibility.
