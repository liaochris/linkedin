Summary

I am inspired by the transformative nature of the social web. As a Digital Strategist, I combine design thinking with data analytics to make sense of human behavior. Then I work with teams to unearth opportunities, solve problems, develop strategies and implement solutions.

Thought-leaders who have influenced my thinking include Peter Drucker, Malcolm Gladwell, Susan Kane, Eric Ries, Jason Fried,Tim Brown, Jeff Jarvis, Simon Sinek, Nate Silver, Paul Adams and Richard Florida. In addition to reading their books I also enjoy watching their fireside chats online.
