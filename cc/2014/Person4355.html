April 2011 – Present (3 years 6 months)

BELLE Capital USA, LP is an early stage angel fund focused on building great companies in Michigan and other, underserved geographic regions in the Midwest and South.

We look for capital efficient companies with a unique product or service filling an urgent market need. We target the mobile/internet/IT, technology-enabled services, life sciences/medical devices, advanced manufacturing and CleanTech market sectors.

Companies seeking our capital must have at least one female founder or C-level exec, and/or be willing to recruit top female talent to the C-suite and Board of Directors. We prefer teams with strong domain expertise, and proven commercialization and entrepreneurial experience.

We are active investors who believe our human capital is more valuable than our financial capital. We seek bold enterprises who welcome our expertise and insight, and who seek to leverage our leadership and experience.
