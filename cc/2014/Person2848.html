Chaplain

US Army

Government Agency; 10,001+ employees; Military industry

May 1975 – August 1999 (24 years 4 months)

I served in the US Army Reserve in a Control Group status as Chaplain Candidate, a Chaplain to the 464th Engineer Battalion (Combat Construction) of the 98th Division (Training), and a post active duty Chaplain.

Training opportunities were numerous in the Reserve. I attended many Army installations, chief among these were the Introduction To Hospital Chaplaincy Program at Brooke Army Medical Center in San Antonio, TX - and the Introduction To Prison Chaplaincy Program at the U.S. Disciplinary Barracks at Fort Leavenworth, KS

While on active duty, I served as Troop and Designated Site R Chaplain at Fort Ritchie, MD (also On-Call Protestant Chaplain to Camp David); Battalion Chaplain of 2/6 Field Artillery, and Deputy Division Artillery Chaplain, both of the 3rd Armored Division (Spearhead) Hanau, Germany. My final assignment was a Hospital Chaplain at Walter Reed Army Medical Center.

I received received various letters of commendation, three Army Commendation Medals, and an honorable discharge. My rank at separation from military service was Major (MAJ).

Minister - Pastor

April 1967 – April 1997 (30 years 1 month)

Served various churches in the New York, New England, Western New York, West Central North Carolina, and Western North Carolina Conferences.
