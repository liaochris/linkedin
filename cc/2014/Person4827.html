Ronnie Ann Himmel's Summary

CURRENT COACHING: I'm delighted to be working with private coaching clients to help them figure out their next career moves, whether in a current workplace or exploring their desire for a brand new career. I also help with all aspects of job search, including resume preparation advice, job interview tips, and caring support throughout the search process. Clients include recent graduates, as well as career changers looking for new jobs in non-profits, academia or the private sector. And I'm excited to be working with writers now, helping to motivate, encourage and support them in the creative process.

HOW I GOT TO BE A CAREER COACH: I was always fascinated by the world of work. Then, as part of my many years working as a management / business process consultant, I was often asked to help with hiring - and loved doing that. Plus I've had lots of personal experience getting jobs & advancing my own career in academia, government, non-profit and private industry.

I also enjoy working with people of all ages and stages of their career, and sharing real-world experiences with them. And now I get to combine the things I love as a personal career and writing coach! For more information, contact me via my Career Nook blog:

=> http://www.careernook.com/career-coaching-services/

MY BLOGS: I have a career blog called Career Nook, where I offer comfort, support and advice to career changers, job seekers, and pretty much anyone looking to get more out of their career. I also founded Work Coach Cafe, which I sold in 2011.

EDUCATION: MBA in Finance from NYU, and a B.A. in Theater with Psychology minor from SUNY Albany. Completed certificate in Career Development & Planning at NYU (2010).

CONTACT ME: Feel free to contact me (RonnieAnn2007 at aol.com) if you are looking for a career or writing coach.

SPECIALTIES: Career coaching, career transition, career change coaching, job search coaching, job interview support, resume advice, career mentoring, writing coach.
