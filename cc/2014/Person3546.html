Joe Griffin's Summary

Joe Griffin is recognized as one of the original Search Engine Marketing engineers and is referenced by tens of thousands of web pages and several books including New York Times Best Selling Author, Jodee Blanco’s “The Complete Guide to Publishing”.

He began his Search Engine career in the 1990s tasked with helping the community establish SEO and submission standards and created the very popular online application which allowed owners to send their websites to hundreds of Search Engines and Directories with just the click of a button.

That process led to the standard industry phrase: "submit a website". Over 2 million submissions were made through Submitawebsite from 1998 to 2000. He incorporated the name in 1999 and opened his first office in Scottsdale, Arizona.

Submitawebsite.com quickly became a multimillion dollar operation which Joe sold to Web.com Inc., (NASDAQ:WWWW) in March of 2007, and which is now referred to as Web.com Search Agency. See reports: http://tinyurl.com/4pa4y7p

In 2010 Joe Griffin launched PostionKing, Inc. and maintains a leading edge within the Search industry.

He is also the mastermind and president of StopDog.com™, the first fully filtered search engine and once a directory second in size only to Yahoo with millions of registered members. StopDog still receives over 6,000 site inclusion requests per week.

Joe's clients have included Turner Classic Movies, JVC, Wells Fargo, Microsoft Small Business, Sundance, and many others.
