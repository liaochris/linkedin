Alessandro Cuttin's Summary

I obtained the BSc in Communication Engineering in 2007, from the Trieste University, Faculty of Engineering, where I am currently a MSc candidate.
My BSc thesis set the baseline for the communication architecture of the AtmoCube Satellite.
After the degree (2008), I obtained a fellowship from the INFN, in partnership with Area Science Park, to work on the System Engineering of AtmoCube.
In Summer 2008 I was selected to attend the Space Station Design Workshop (held at ESA-ESTEC), where I focused my interests in Systems Engineering and Concurrent Engineering.
Starting from 2009, I am member of the AtmoCube Steering Commitee, as deputy Project Manager. My activities consist in: supervise the systems engineering together with the Project Manager, coordinate team members (mainly students), supervise the communication architecture.

Specialties

Small satellite communication system design,Systems Engineering, Concurrent Engineering
