Background

Summary

At Engage Presentations we are specialists in presentation strategy, skills and content. We combine expertise in marketing, sales, business development, negotiation and the psychology of communication, to offer unique capabilities in the successful delivery of speeches, presentations and commercial messages to professionals and industry leaders. We offer training and coaching to executives, professionals, brand name and generic pharmaceutical companies, medical device and diagnostic firms, as well as health care professionals. We also provide direct support for presentation and briefing content development, including message refinement, message prioritization and message evolution. In addition, we offer clients customized visual branding and graphic innovations, ranging from purpose-built images and video, to carefully selected stock image integration, to deliver multimedia target audience engagement.

Specialties: Communications - specifically presentation strategy, development, design, delivery and execution.
