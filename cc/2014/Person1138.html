Glyn Mitchell's Summary

Glyn Mitchell
CEO of Promessa Organic UK Limited.
Freeman of the City of London

Promession is an environmentally cleaner and ethically preferable process of dealing with organic matter, saving the environment and leaving only healthy soil and no polluting emissions.

Why do the favoured processes of deep burial and cremation need changing, after all they have worked for over 900 years now?
A friend and colleague could not have put it more simply, thank you Miles..

If you started with a clean sheet of paper and the problem of how to dispose of 70+Kg of organic matter, which is 70% water, you would be pretty unlikely to come up with the idea of burning it, without drying it first. There is 50Kg of water to get rid of…
Of course the paradox is that once you have dried it you do not actually need burn it - it is perfectly OK to put it into the top soil with some very minor adjustments, or you can burn it if you prefer and it will cost nothing to do so.

People don't like being a being a burden to future generations on the whole, if the equipment is available to enable this to happen in a humane caring way to both the environment and nature, everything would benefit.

Promessa is the company that offers the new Drop in Eco Burial process promession.
74 countries have signed up for promession.
Thousands have registered their preferences to be promated on the link www.polifera.se

Glyn Mitchell is an experienced Project and Change Instigator and Leader. With a background in launching environmental products and better processes, on an international scale.
