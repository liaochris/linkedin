Dan Wiese's Summary

I have been at the marketing research game for a long time. My only personal goal is to keep doing it as long as I am able. I do my best to focus on the exact purpose my clients have and craft a research solution that achieves that as efficiently as possible. I treat their money as if it were mine.

Because I am a one man company I have enormous flexibility and I am able to help the full range of client needs. If $200 or $300 does the job for an inventor or small start-up company, that's what I recommend. On the other hand, I have relationships with a multitude of specialists throughout the world and can put together very large projects if that is what is needed to do the job.

Specialties

Marketing research consulting, questionnaire writing, report writing, focus group moderating, free lance assignments, all types of marketing research.

Research Director, Successful Farming Magazine

Meredith

Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; MDP; Publishing industry

1967 – 1976 (9 years)

Responsible for planning and administration of all editorial, circulation and advertising research for top farm magazine. Conducted and produced continuing series of studies describing magazine’s readers. Successfully marketed 1974 Doane Farm Media Measurement Study, which SUCCESSFUL FARMING had funded, to other media, ad agencies and advertisers on a co-sponsorship basis. Developed and administered Agri-Media Selection System, an analytical, computer-based comparison of the reach and frequency against target markets of alternative farm magazine advertising schedules.
