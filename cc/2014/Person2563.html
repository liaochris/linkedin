Clive Dickens' Summary

Clive is currently Director of Digital and Innovation at Southern Cross Austereo (ASX:SXL) and oversees all strategic & operational aspects of the company's digital content & digital revenue activities.

Southern Cross Austereo (www.sca.com.au) is one of the world largest Radio & Free to Air Broadcasters outside of the US (by $ revenue) and owns digital and broadcasts assets that engage over 10m Australians each week.
