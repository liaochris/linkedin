Sankarshan Mukhopadhyay's Summary

I have deep experience in building engineering organization to create enterprise class products which present an unique value proposition to the end customer.

I lead the delivery of Certification Engineering Services in Red Hat’s Customer Experience and Engagement Organization covering ISV Application Certification, Certified Cloud Provider Program, Red Hat OpenStack Cloud Infrastucture Platform Certification and Red Hat Container Certification. We collaborate with globally distributed teams of Partner Engineering, Product Marketing, Product Management and Support Services to ensure that we have a highly agile, heavily customer focused suite of products that amplifies the value of Red Hat's subscription.

Previously, I have managed engineering efforts around around Red Hat's ABI Assurance Initiative, kABI efforts with ISVs.

In different roles I have built and sustained partner relations with OEMs and IHVs in India for Red Hat Hardware Certification Program . I have also delivered Custom Engineering Services including small foot-print Linux installations based on specific client requirements.

I am motivated to work in Product Teams building innovations with an emphasis on growth and monetization around the innovation. A great customer experience is a guiding principle for product roadmap and engineering efforts I am eager to be part of.

I participate in groups and forums around the topics of Product Management, Software Engineering, PaaS and IaaS, OpenStack and, Systems Engineering.

I am an active participant in communities of scale like GNOME, Wikipedia and, Mozilla. I have over the years helped build and sustain language (and, locale) communities for large Free and Open Source software projects, develop content for workshops and sprints, hold talks and demonstrations of released software etc. You will find me on IRC channels of these projects and, the multiple mailing lists.
