Background

Summary

Remi is the lower cost alternative to equipment manufacturer service contracts and extended warranties. Since 1998, Remi has helped organizations of all types by providing a more cost effective, centralized approach to repairing and maintaining electronic devices.

Remi is also an award-winning company. The company has won Inc. 5000 and the Charlotte Business Journal Fast 50 two consecutive years in a row. Remi has also won the Business NC Fast 40 and several Stevie Awards.
