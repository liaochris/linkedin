Rica Ruiz's Summary

I was raised in the San Francisco Bay Area and I have vast experience in Real Estate with both Buyers and Sellers in Foster City, Burlingame, San Mateo, the Peninsula and the rest of the Bay Area. My history of developing highly targeted marketing campaigns and years of comprehensive sales success have resulted in profitable sales for both residential and commercial real estate. I am an entrepreneurial leader dedicated to providing the highest quality service possible and helping individuals find and afford their dream home.

My clients know that I am a trusted advisor with the experience and knowledge essential to achieve outstanding results. I am known as a savvy negotiator and I take a relentless approach to ensure that my clients get the best possible outcome. I am committed to integrity and honesty and I believe my reputation as a trustworthy individual is the foundation of my success.

I develop extremely successful strategic plans and provide disciplined advice to help individuals achieve their Real Estate goals. As a dedicated member of the community with years of experience supporting a range of civic organizations, I have a stake in helping the Bay Area remain one of the best places to live, work and raise a family. I am a Licensed Real Estate Agent, Realtor, Short Sale & Foreclosure Resource (SFR), and Internet Marketing Specialist Designation (IMSD) and I am affiliated with the San Mateo County Association of Realtor (SAMCAR), the National Association of Realtors (NAR) and the California Association of Realtors (CAR). For further information you can review my bio at www.ricaruiz.com or www.thericaruizgroup.com

Specialties: Real Estate Sales, Buyer Representation, Residential Real Estate, First Time Homebuyers, Short Sales, Foreclosures, Contract Negotiation, Marketing Strategies, Open Houses, Social Media.
