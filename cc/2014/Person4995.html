Shara Karasic's Summary

Social product development + social content + community management + social media

Social done right from a holistic perspective across web and mobile.

Social strategy at Appolicious.com, an app discovery service for iPhone, iPad, and Android. Web and mobile user experience (UX), interaction design, product management and product development.

My community/social media/product management experience has spanned multiple industries: education, business/entrepreneur, non-profit, mobile, giving me a nuanced view of what will engage users. I embrace innovation and guiding companies into the interactive age.
