Background

Summary

I work on the Engage team on behalf of our enterprise and telecom clients, comprising big data, analytics, data visualization, security, CRM, as well as mobile and fixed network infrastructure. My responsibilities on the team include driving meaningful media coverage and relations, analyst relations, social media program development and execution, planning, market/competitor reporting, and almost every other aspect of our PR programs.

My experience covers a broad range of marketing and PR roles at different organizations, and I’m able to bring perspective to clients based on my background in other unique management, sales, and advisory roles throughout the years. I've worked on a Bahamas cruise ship, operated industrial sewing machines, and corralled meetings with members of Congress-- all experience from the real world, with grit developed from hard work in a variety of challenging positions beyond the realm of everyday PR practitioners.

Current clients include TIBCO, Procera Networks, Opera, and Infinera. I’m fascinated by driverless cars, automotive and wearable technology, smart appliances, and the future of the Internet.
