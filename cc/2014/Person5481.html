Yancy Strickland's Summary

Enthusiastic, innovative professional looking for right opportunity to shine. While Strickland has extensive experience in higher education and the nonprofit world, he has also worked in the private sector including time spent in start-up environments in high tech and professional services.

Yancy's social & electronic media forays over the last ten years, combined with his entire body of work, yield a level of professional acumen that motivates audiences to action, regardless if face-to-face or halfway around the world. Or even just down the street. No matter if it's the chairman of the board or a first-time volunteer, Strickland can get them on-board and "all-in."

Strickland's professional interests also include start-up ventures, technology and biotechnology, consumer and commercial electronics, alternative energy, sustainable social enterprise and social entrepreneurship. Not to mention a toe in the music and entertainment industries.

Yancy Scot Strickland is a passionate agent of change, a catalyst that can be the difference for you. Now and in the years to come.

Specialties: Non-profit organizations: fund development, strategic planning, administration, operations, governance . Real estate, contracts, code & legal compliance. Government relations. Start-up and structuring of corporations. Development - both capital acquisition and organizational for nonprofits and private / public partnerships and corporate ventures. An entrepreneurial streak that proves itself invaluable often.
