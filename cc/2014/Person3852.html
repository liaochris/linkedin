Kim Cayes' Summary

Award-winning, hybrid communications professional with a portfolio of project experience in Fortune 100 and 500 corporations. In addition to communications, an M.A. in Organizational Development gives exceptional insights and experience to employee, change management, HR, executive and sales communications. Known for creative, innovative approaches that engage employees to drive success.
