Background

Summary

Professional. Experienced. Educated.

JoAnn Carpenter has 14+ years of hands-on experience in the Internet Marketing and Web Development field. Having served hundreds of businesses and organizations across the U.S., she is an expert in the online industry. She also has 25 years of experience as an entrepreneur, having owned businesses in Connecticut and Florida as well as West Virginia. An early corporate background rounds out her professionalism.

JoAnn has a Regents Bachelors of Arts degree from Potomac State College of West Virginia University, prior college from Western Connecticut State University, and is working on her Master's degree in Integrated Marketing Communications at WVU.

She continually strives to enhance the services she provides clients, staying up to date with new technologies, and putting excellence at the forefront of all that she does.

Giving back is one of JoAnn's most valued beliefs, and a number of local and state charitable organizations benefit from donated services.

JoAnn has lived in Keyser, WV since 2003. She grew up in Brewster, NY, and lived in San Francisco, CA, Brookfield, CT, and Amelia Island, FL prior to making West Virginia her permanent home. She has two adult daughters living in Keyser, a husband, a dog, three cats and many wonderful friends.

JoAnn works from her home office, located near Potomac State College in Keyser.
