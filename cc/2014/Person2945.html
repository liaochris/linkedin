Michael W. McCormick's Summary

I am an engaging teacher and manager in the genealogical field. I excel in presenting to groups and in one-on-one settings. My enthusiasm for family history is contagious, even with those who were previously indifferent or disinterested. I am a competent trainer, regarding genealogy skills and friendly communication. My conflict resolution skills are apparent as I communicate with clients, and patiently address their concerns. My problem solving skills are in constant use as I manage the US workflow for a major European heir search firm.

I believe that family history awareness has the ability to improve the quality of life for everyone around me. This shows through to my personal and professional associates. My desire is to serve and influence an ever broadening range of individuals through teaching and management roles.

Specialties: Teaching, management, networking, genealogy
