Joe Sonka's Summary

As Director of Accounts, I am responsible for the quality of work and communications of all client/partner engagements. I act as a link between clients and the Ackmann & Dickenson staff to coordinate all the details of every project. I work closely with our clients, managing administrative and organizational work and ensuring that work is completed on time and within budget. Furthermore, and most importantly, I focus solely on their business and discover data-driven strategies and associated tactics that will enhance their position in the market. It's an extremely engaging blend of creativity and analytics.

Specialties

Creative concepting, project management, client interaction and relationship building, strategic planning and tactical execution, search marketing, interactive strategy, digital design, web application development, online marketing, analytics
