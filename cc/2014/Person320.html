Background

Summary

I am a patent litigator at Feinberg Day Alberti & Thompson LLP in Menlo Park, California. Previously, I spent seven years as an associate in the intellectual property practice group at McDermott Will & Emery LLP, where I started in 2007 after graduating from Santa Clara University School of Law. I have extensive experience litigating patent cases at the U.S. International Trade Commission and various U.S. district courts. Prior to law school, I was a software programmer for almost five years at a successful financial services industry software startup in Sacramento.

Since 2009, I have coached Santa Clara Law School's trademark moot court teams in the International Trademark Association's Saul Lefkowitz Moot Court Competition. I am also active in Silicon Valley's Ingram Inn of Court.
