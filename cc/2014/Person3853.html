Kim Chandler McDonald's Summary

High-tech entrepreneur, innovation & collaboration advocate & advisor, & thought leader in disruptive approaches & transformational trends, Kim is an award-winning author currently in the pre-production phase of her next book.

Her tenacity, work ethic & people skills have placed her in a perfect position to be the world’s first branded Flat World Navigator. A topnotch communicator, Kim has a far reaching network of trusted relationships with a wide range of global thought leaders, many of whom were the inspiration for her ‘Capital I Innovation’ Interview Series that celebrates Innovations and Innovators and, which was the precursor to her acclaimed, award-winning book, 'Innovation: How Innovators Think, Act and Change Our World'.

Published in October 2013, by Kogan Page - in print and as an eBook - it is also available as an Enhanced Edition/ Online Ecosystem, which integrates the latest interactivity, web publishing, cloud, mobile and responsive technologies along with KimmiC's FlatWorld 'secret sauce’ and SmartMarks - interactive, intelligent bookmarks. www.flatworld.me

KimmiC, which Kim co-founded with her husband & business partner Michael, specializes in disruptive approaches, robust technologies & leading edge innovations such as FlatWorld™ - an FIA (Future Of Internet Architecture) business platform designed for the Collaboration Economy.  It is a 'White Label' simple, secure, inexpensive, multi-lingual platform which enables data and knowledge assets from any source (including programs, applications and processes) to be captured, collaborated and capitalized upon. www.flatworld.co

Formerly, Kim was a writer/editor for national & intl newspapers & mags &host/producer of an award winning radio program. During this time she sought & secured interviews with 100s of fascinating people such as: former US Sec. of State, Madeleine Albright; film director, Terry Gilliam; theoretical physicist, Brian Greene; & authors John Irving, Amy Tan & Tom Wolfe.
