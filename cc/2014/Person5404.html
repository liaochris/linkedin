V Ivana Foster EdDc- The Confidence Architect™'s Summary

Attention to abundance, a plethora of prosperity-driven tips, and magnificent manifestation; ask Ivana, The Confidence Architect™, and she will direct you on how to achieve your greatest potential in life, love, business, and in SELF. 

This multi-talented author, educator, speaker, and ever evolving life coach takes pride and joy in loading clients and couples up with affirmations, meditations, positive actions, and honest, no-nonsense coaching and guidance that not only changes lives, but also changes generations.

You can catch her regularly in RENEWED Magazine where she serves as a featured columnist delivering practical, refreshing tips and coaching on balancing work and life while attending to the mind, body, and spirit.  As a holistic life coach of her eclectic, affirming, coaching practice; Ivana excels in cultivating confidence & prosperity conscious lifestyles that supply a positive return on the lives of actualizing women.

Ivana’s success has garnered national acclaim and recognition, making her much sought after by others that are dedicated to seeking and obtaining their Deserved Abundance.  She was featured in the 2012 People You Need to Know Women’s Magazine-Atlanta, Georgia as well as the 2012 edition of Black Business Women Online Directory.

Ivana is a co-founding member and instructor of the Jewell Jackson McCabe Emerging Leaders Institute, Inc, in Atlanta, GA and also serves on the Board of Directors of Women Healing Women, Inc, in Decatur, GA.

Obviously dedicated to the plight of women and their impact on global society, Ivana is committed to working for and supporting those platforms that celebrate equality for women; overcoming and reconstructing life after domestic & intimate partner violence; the successful release from detention services; and equality for all.

Specialties

Subconscious Restructuring®, Life/Personality Colors, Law of Attraction, Myers-Briggs® Type Indicator, Transformational Coaching, Positive Psychology, Emotional Intelligence (EQ), Self-Actualization, Appreciative Inquiry, SWOT Analysis, DiSC® Profile Personality, Spiritual Coaching

We are particularly focused on providing services to marginalized communities.

CORE VALUES

Our core values drive the design, creation, and implementation of all programs and services:

1.Every woman has a right to be free from sexual and domestic violence, without compromise.
2.Programs and services are client-centered and mission-driven, serving only the client’s highest good.
3.Every woman is empowered with the choice to recover and heal at her own pace without judgment or discrimination.
4.Violence is an attach of the mind, body, and soul. Thus our overall programming must address each of these areas to have the greatest impact.
