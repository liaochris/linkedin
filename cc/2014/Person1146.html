Background

Summary

Greta K. Kolcon is a Partner in the firm's Litigation Department. She concentrates her practice on civil litigation in state and federal courts, and arbitration forums. Her focus is on effective and efficient dispute resolution. She is also provides court-directed and private mediation assistance. She serves on the neutral panel for the United States District Court for the Western District of New York.

Ms. Kolcon currently serves as statewide Treasurer for the Women's Bar Association of the State of New York (WBASNY). She is the immediate past co-chair of WBASNY's Environmental Law Committee. She is a past president of the Greater Rochester Association for Women Attorneys (GRAWA). Ms. Kolcon is also an active member of the Monroe County and New York State Bar Associations, as well as the Rochester Women's Network.

Specialties: Complex Federal & New York State civil litigation, including environmental litigation, petroleum, asbestos, lead paint poisoning, toxic torts, energy, cost recovery actions, products liability, negligence, a variety of business disputes, and transportation industry law.
