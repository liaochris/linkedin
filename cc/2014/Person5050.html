Sherrie Borden, CAE, ACC's Summary

Certified Association Executive with significant experience in health care nonprofits in the areas of strategic planning and organizational development, public affairs/coalition building, integrated communications, fund development, publications, professional/program development, and health policy with additional experience in clinical medicine (Physician Assistant).

Executive management and career/life transformation coach who partners with clients to discover and achieve their true potential by identifying the energy blocks that are fueling negativity and stagnation, and then creating the positive energy to achieve their success. Coach clients to help them re-focus their assumptions, beliefs, and perceptions so that their individual success, and the success of those around them, is promoted. Partner with clients to shift from focusing on problems and fighting fires to proactively creating solutions.

YourWork.YourLife.Transformed™

Understanding how important the integration of physical and emotional health are to personal and workplace well-being, I partner with clients from all backgrounds and circumstances to find and achieve career and life transitions and balance with purpose and integrity. I motivate clients to discover and achieve their true potential, facilitating the transition from negativity and stagnation to positive energy and success.

As an Energy Leadership Index™ Master Practitioner I can help you discover what is blocking your ability to achieve personal and professional success and happiness.

My diverse experience with nonprofit associations as a leader in public and professional relations and fundraising, and as an advocate in the public health arena provides me with a unique understanding about transitioning and transforming. I work with upper management nonprofit executives as well as with individuals who are seeking career transition and work life balance or who want to realize their life purpose.

I inspire my clients to develop meaningful goals for themselves and take significant strides to reach those goals. Recognizing that a strong coaching relationship is a partnership, I provide a safe, secure, and confidential environment that makes it easy for individuals to discover their true passions, the next step in their career, or achieve a more balanced and healthier lifestyle.
