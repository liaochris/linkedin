Pre-Need or Pre-Arangement is planning a funeral before the time of death. It is an important part of your estate and retirement planning. Without pre-arrangement, the details of the funeral are left to a small group of family members who have little warning or time to plan this major life event. Pre-arrangement relieves your family of that responsibility. You are able to leave detailed instructions about your desires and wishes. Pre-arrangement gives peace of mind to both you and your family.
