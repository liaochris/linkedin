My current ventures include:

RevivedMedia.com - User Acquisition Platform

Revived Media utilizes our proprietary RevMax™ ad serving technology to break down incoming traffic and optimize user acquisition in real time. With over 50+ key performance indicators we're able to instantly detect and serve each user the advertisement best fit for them. Utilizing our ROI Optimizer™ we're able to scale traffic in real time drastically increasing ROI for our publishers and advertisers. Built on our HyperSpeed Network™ we're able to have blazing fast redirects and instant comprehensive analytics. The end result is superior quality to our advertisers yielding our publishers eCPMs much higher than traditional ad serving platforms.
