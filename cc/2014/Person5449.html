Will Dayble's Summary

Please note: I am currently on sabbatical and may take a few months to get back to your emails / messages. :)

----------

I'm a tech geek turned social entrepreneur, passionate about things like measuring impact, breaking dumb systems, diversity within digital and supporting the Australian social economy.

Companies founded include Squareweave, a digital agency that helps change-makers do good tech; Grace Cafe, making fine coffee and kind food; Clarity.io, measuring and rewarding social impact; and The Fitzroy Academy of Getting Shit Done, teaching Australians to solve problems that don't exist yet.

I'm on a few advisory boards, I deliver keynotes / grace notes internationally, and I'm a featured writer across various mediums. some favourite clients include people like the Foundation for Young Australians, the Centre for Workplace Leadership, The Climate Council, Commonwealth Bank and a slew of not for profits.
