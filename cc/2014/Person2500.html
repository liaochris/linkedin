Chris Cliff's Summary

I am a life long construction and real estate person, raised by a father that was a general contractor and a mother that was a real estate agent. I have been around one or the other industries all my life and that doesn't look like it will change any time soon.

The goal for my business is to offer the best quality remodeling services that we can, using the best quality materials possible. My preference is to use 'green' products whenever possible and utilize as many low or no VOC products in our work as we can.

Just a few of my medium and long term goals are to own over 1000 rental units, develop a geothermal power generating company and build a high speed train line from Vancouver B.C. down to Los Angeles and then on to Las Vegas. Lofty goals I will admit, but I don't see why any of them are not possible!

Specialties

Residential remodeling, painting, kitchen and bathroom renovations.
