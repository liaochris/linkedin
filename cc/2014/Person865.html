Robert Kraft's Summary

Attorney and Owner -- Personal Injury, Social Security Disability, Medicaid Planning for Long-Term Care, Veterans Pension Benefits, and Elder Law. I have been a lawyer in Dallas, Texas since 1971, representing injured and disabled clients and families who need help planning for long-term care. Kraft & Associates has experienced attorneys and staff members. We have helped tens of thousands of clients over the years.

We represent clients in the fields of Personal Injury, Social Security Disability, Medicaid Planning for Long-Term Care, Veterans Pension Benefits, and Elder Law.
