Nathan Wallace's Summary

Seeking a position as an embedded systems engineer, embedded software engineer, electronic technician, field service or technical service engineer. I have expertise in the creation and maintenance of new, or existing designs for a wide range of embedded and industrial systems. A proven problem solver with unique troubleshooting skills, excellent understanding of most embedded hardware, software, and real-time operating systems. Strong background in customer support, project leadership, and engineering management. Extensive experience in multi-threaded environments, device driver design, system implementation, custom FPGA design, and some mechanical design.

Specialties:
• 25+ years C/C++ and assembly languages using GNU, IAR, Microsoft.
• 20+ years RTOS, pSOS, iRMX, Nucleus, modified round-robin MS-DOS, custom real-time & pseudo-real-time OSs for micros.
• 5+ years Linux OS (Red Hat & Timesys) & VxWorks.
• 25+ years Micros X86, PPC, Renesas M16C & M32C, 68HC11, ColdFire, H8, 8051, Z80, & MC6809.
• 20+ years comm protocols, RS-232, RS-422, RS-485, USB, I2C, SPI, IP, TCP, UDP, SNMP.
• 14+ years industrial controls, plant automation, HV power electronics & plant EMI/EMC standards..
• Experience with Agile, AutoCad, PADS PCB Schematic Capture and PC layout tools.
