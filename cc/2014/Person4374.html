Alex Park's Summary

Alex started as an entrepreneur at the age of 14, building a profitable online business enabling music producers to sell their work to performing artists. A few years, profitable businesses, successful business plan/pitch competitions later, Alex was invited to join the GE | OMD Incubator in NYC, where he had opportunity to learn from the founders of companies like Twitter, Square, Tumblr, Foursquare, and more, while building his own mobile startup.

Alex then joined cloud music technology startup, Gobbler, as the Director of Special Projects, where he works closely with the founding team on user growth, fundraising, business development, and recruiting initiatives. He is fascinated by the interaction between people, technology, and the arts, and directs his creative energies into these areas.

Outside of these pursuits, Alex loves jam sessions and playing basketball competitively, and will break your ankles or your ears if provoked.
