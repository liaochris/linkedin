Tomasz Stasiuk's Summary

Tomasz Stasiuk is a disability attorney handling Social Security claims throughout Colorado. Tomasz Stasiuk also writes and presents on legal technology issues including taking your firm paperless, practicing in the cloud and social media. Tomasz Stasiuk has presented at the Solo & Small Firm Practice meetings, 2009 & 2010 CLTA conventions, & the Macs In Law Offices 2009 national convention. Tomasz Stasiuk has been featured in The Learned Lawyer, Law Week Colorado, State Bill Colorado & Lawyers Weekly (Canada). Tomasz Stasiuk writes on technology matters at Planet10tech.com.

Specialties

Social Security disability claims, legal technology. Disclaimer: Colorado does not certify lawyers as "specialists" in any field.
