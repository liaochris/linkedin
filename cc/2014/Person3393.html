Jay D. Locklear's Summary

I have been a worship leader for over 10 years and have led worship in a variety of settings, including local church worship services, retreats, and district and conference events for the North Carolina Conference of the United Methodist Church. I am passionate about leading worship and collaborating with others to plan worship experiences.

I believe that youth and young adults are vital to the ongoing life and ministry of any church, and am passionate about finding ways to develop their faith and their gifts and connecting them with opportunities to express both.

I am excited about technology and the potential that it represents for extending and maintaining community and connection, both inside and outside the walls of the church.
