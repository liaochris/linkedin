International Federation of Fly Fishers

Public Company; 1-10 employees; Sports industry

January 2011 – Present (3 years 10 months) Livingston, Montana

The Federation of Fly Fishers established in 1964 as an international non-profit organization dedicated to the betterment of the sport of fly fishing through Conservation, Restoration and Education. The International Federation of Fly Fishers and its Councils are the only organized advocate for fly fishers on a national and regional level.
