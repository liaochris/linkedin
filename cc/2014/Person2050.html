Amy Rees Anderson's Summary

I am the Managing Partner and Founder of REES Capital, which I created to provide entrepreneurs and business executives critical guidance and support to help their companies grow. In addition to seeking out opportunities for potential equity investments, we provide expert mentoring and consulting services across a broad range of business functions, including leadership training; product and market assessment; marketing, sales and public relations strategy; financial modeling; organizational structuring; strategic planning; acquisition and exit strategy; and other key areas.

I also write a weekly article for Forbes.com on angel investing, entrepreneurship, and other business topics. My article is called adVentures of a Super Angel and you can read the articles at http://blogs.forbes.com/amyanderson/

Prior to founding REES Capital, I was the founder and CEO of MediConnect Global, Inc., a worldwide leader in medical record retrieval, digitization, and one of the largest cloud-based health information exchanges. I led all aspects of MediConnect’s business, including a worldwide workforce of over 1,500 employees. Under my direction, the company achieved over 1500 percent growth since 2004. In March 2012, I successfully led MediConnect to being acquired for $377 million.

Specialties: Mergers and Acquisitons, Raising Capital, Entrepreneurial consulting and mentoring, Public Speaking, Business Process Outsourcing, Healthcare and Healthcare Technology
