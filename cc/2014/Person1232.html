425.681.2124

"Building Creative Teams to Transform Medical Technology"

The Cambridge Office of Sanford Rose Associates (SRA) is an executive search firm that specializes in recruiting top talent for medical device and regenerative medicine companies developing products to treat patients with cardiovascular disease and other major medical conditions.

Our competitive advantage is the ability we have to recruit the best talent in the industry by leveraging our 30 years of executive medical device experience at leading surgical, minimally invasive and interventional companies such as St. Jude Medical, Baxter Healthcare and CR Bard, and innovative startups such as Spiration (acquired by Olympus) and CircuLite (acquired by HeartWare).

We are a member of the SRA network of 60 executive search firms that has filled over 100,000 positions since 1959. SRA is the US affiliate of the International Executive Search Federation (IESF) the largest retained search group in the world with 130 offices in 40 countries.

Executive Search Review recognized the totality of the Sanford Rose Associates Network as being one of the Top 12 Search Firms in North America.
