Social Media Manager

Strella Social Media

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry

June 2010 – Present (4 years 5 months) Greater Philadelphia Area

Because business owners wear many hats and have competing priorities, they often need to abandon marketing tasks in favor of taking care of existing customers. If that sounds like you, you’re not alone! Unfortunately, that makes it extremely difficult to harness the business-building potential of social media.

Our comprehensive Social Media Strategy and Management Services solve that problem! We not only develop a strategy tailored to your specific business needs, your target customers, and your goals, we also take on the day-to-day social network posting and interaction activities.

With a #Strella social media manager, you can maintain an active, goal-driven, and consistent social media presence. So while we’re helping you increase brand awareness, build your online audience, and drive traffic to your website, you can focus on operating your business and service your clients and customers.

We offer partial and full-service management plans to fit your objectives and budget.

Our Social Media Strategy and Management packages include everything you need to start and maintain a social media presence for long-term benefits:

- A customized strategy, including a ‘how we’ll work together’ guide
- Content development, coordination, editing, and posting
- Account monitoring and engagement
- Online community/audience building
- Brand monitoring
- Monthly (or quarterly depending on your preference) social media progress reports
- Unlimited Priority Email Support
- Value-add email blasts that share helpful marketing and social media tips and news.

We can help you by developing strategies for and managing your social media accounts as well as developing content and posting to blogging platforms such as WordPress and Blogger.
