Joe Topinka's Summary

Recognized as a game-changing career CIO, my IT Business Partner Program™ has driven exponential business results consistently across industries by bridging the chasm between business stakeholders and IT organizations.

I believe that when you create a trusting environment and empower people they deliver the best possible results.That philosophy has helped the companies that I've led achieve great results and has enabled career growth for my colleagues.

My proprietary Business Partner Program demystifies IT for the C-Suite and achieves business growth through:

► CONVERGED IT – business stakeholders and IT leaders are intertwined and inseparable

► MOVING BEYOND ALIGNMENT – C-Suite and IT leadership: thinking + acting + achieving together

► OUTSIDE IN THINKING – Getting immersed in the customer's environment to make it easy for them to love what your company does

My revolutionary IT Business Partner Program program has successfully delivered results in multiple industries, including banking, brokerage, accounting, manufacturing, retail and digital commerce.

As the CIO and VP of Multichannel Commerce for Red Wing Shoe Company, I lead the company’s first ever global, multichannel commerce go-to-market-strategy which has revitalized the organization and created new distribution channels for the company’s products and services.

In addition, as the founder of CIO Mentor, LLC, I am focused on leveraging the power of technology to achieve profit-driven business results.

In 2013, I was honored as CIO of the year by Minneapolis/St. Paul Business Journal. My approach has been featured in Forbes.com, the Heller Report, Profile Magazine, the Forum on World Class IT, and numerous other publications.

As an author and sought after speaker, I present to IT and business industry groups about the power of IT Business Partnerships.
