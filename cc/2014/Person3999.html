Larry Ketchersid's Experience

Owner and Author

JoSara MeDia

Sole Proprietorship; 1-10 employees; Publishing industry

April 2006 – Present (8 years 6 months) www.josaramedia.com

What started as an experimental combination of storytelling and software development has turned into JoSara MeDia, the part-time book, eBook and app publishing company run by my wife and I.

Award winning authors like Paul Levinson (winner of the Locus Award), Robert Flynn (Texas Literary Hall of Fame, winner of the Spur Award from Western Writers of America) and others collaborate with us to produce print books, eBooks, enhanced eBooks for tables and apps.

My own contributions include the novels, Software by the Kilo and Dusk Before the Dawn, plus the non-fiction Voices in My Head and the top-rated enhanced eBook on the Grand Canyon for iPad and iPhone.

From Robert Flynn we've published the Jade series (Jade: Outlaw and Jade: The Law) in print and eBook, the out-of-print classic The Last Klick in eBook, and the highly rated Antarctica: Standing on the Bottom of the World, an enhanced eBook for iPad and iPhone.

With Paul Levinson we've collaborated on several "author's cuts" including the classic time travel novel The Plot to Save Socrates. We recently published the sequel, Unburning Alexandria, in eBook and print.

We've collaborated with the renowned Texas State Historical Association to publish an enhanced eBook on San Jacinto for iPad and iPhone, which includes panoramic pictures of the historic battleground.

For the rest of our books and apps, please visit our website.

Chairman and CEO

Media Sourcery, Inc.

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Computer Software industry

2005 – Present (9 years)

Media Sourcery provides innovative services and solutions for secure content delivery and workflow, including static content, forms, streaming and live media. Our systems provide automated digital signatures, compression and encryption for handhelds (Apple, Android, Blackberry, Nokia, others), browsers, applications and servers.

Our customers are in healthcare (hospice, home health, DME), energy, field service and other industries. Our solutions include point of care visit notes on handhelds and tablets, patient referral systems, RFP/RFI control systems and secure backup.

    Odyssey Healthcare, which employs 6,500, is one of the largets hospice care providers in the U.S., including care that's provided in patients' homes.     An American educated Mayan sits atop a Temple in Tikal National Park, waiting. A father helplessly watches his family fall into some kind of sleep, or trance. Then he too succumbs, begging a stranger to save them. A PhD scientist awakens, remembering only hours ago she was in her nanotechnology lab, trying to work a solution to make incredibly small robots replicate. But she finds that she is now ill, or a prisoner, or both. The world has change irrevocably, in one swift movement, by the actions of a meglo-maniacal genius. Can they undo what he has done? Should they?
