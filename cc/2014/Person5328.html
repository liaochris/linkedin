Tracy Do's Summary

We do it by listening; being attentive, straightforward, giving first priority to our clients and their needs. Like a boutique hotel that delivers a personalized travel experience, or a favorite restaurant that makes things just the way you like, TDRE delivers a level of service that is seamless, detail-oriented, never intrusive. As your advocates we are prepared, informed and tough when we have to be. Our long list of satisfied clients includes seasoned home owners, first-time buyers, investors and large-scale developers, whom we have guided through hundreds of successful transactions.

From our home at the John Aaroe Group we work in the communities of Los Angeles including Silver Lake, Los Feliz, Northeast LA, Pasadena and beyond. The effort is shared and carefully coordinated, each of us having a specialty including buyer services, investment properties, marketing coordination, IT/social media and copywriting. Our extended team features a dedicated graphic designer, photographer and staging services. We’re big enough to move mountains for you, but never so big that we’ll forget who you are or what you’re trying to accomplish.

If you have a property to sell, we will provide an honest, carefully considered valuation. If you want to purchase a property, we can advise and guide your search, loan and escrow process. Visit our popular blog for information on our exclusive listings along with thoughts and insights about our local housing market, covering topics like inventory, architecture, design, and financial trends in real estate.
