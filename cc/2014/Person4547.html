Background

Summary

Paul is the top Cleveland area REALTOR in Howard Hanna's Pepper Pike-Lander Circle office serving clients in the eastern suburbs of Cleveland including Pepper Pike, Orange, Beachwood, Lyndhurst, Moreland Hills, Gates Mills, Hunting Valley, Chagrin Falls, Bentleyville, Cleveland Heights, Shaker Heights, Solon, Hudson, Bainbridge, Aurora, Russell, Highland Heights, and University Heights. His extensive knowledge of these communities, their properties and real estate listings, allow him to counsel clients toward intelligent real estate decisions..

Paul has successfully guided home buyers and sellers through the sale and purchase of more than 1400+ properties. He has established a positive communication channel throughout his career with other agents, buyers and sellers, mortgage officers, title personnel and attorneys involved in the home buying and selling process.

For Real Estate sellers, Paul utilizes a complete marketing approach to help sell properties quickly and at the best possible price. His Total Marketing Plan includes virtual tours that are linked to a number of highly visible Internet web sites including HowardHanna.com, www.PaulBlumberg.com, Realtor.com, Trulia, Zillow and others. Professional quality brochures, direct mail, and email campaigns are directed at his extensive database of potential buyers. Highly visible local newspaper and magazine sources are also used to target buyers. He provides television advertising on Howard Hanna's Open House Preview on Sunday morning reaching as many as 90,000 households.

For properties over $600,000, Paul also uses Howard Hanna's Luxury Home Marketing Plan which includes The Plain Dealer Metro Section, The Plain Dealer Satuday Real Estate Section, Playbill, Christie's Great Estates magazine, Currents and a unique marketing brochure.

Paul's business is strongly based in referrals as satisfied clients refer their family, friends and work associates for direction in buying or selling real estate.

Specialties:Qualified Homes of Distinction Specialist, Expert in evaluating your homes worth, contract negotiations and strategic pricing. Creative, innovative marketer, Detail knowledge of local neighborhoods/communities and in depth knowledge of zoning and tax laws
