My Journey:

My professional experience started around 2009 when I stumbled upon my first client on GetAFreelancer.com. It was highly unexpected and the client ended up hiring me full time. At first I was excited because the pay was amazing, then I stopped caring about that as much as the work itself. I fell in love with being a Web Designer, even though the client had me working in Smarty; a template framework using PHP. Quickly I fell in love with Web Development just as much as Web Design. Since then I haven't specialized in neither Design nor Development, it's always been both.

I've learned a lot, accomplished some things and made some mistakes but all of it has made me a better and more productive member of the design & development community.
