Aaron Newton's Summary

I’m at my best when I’m working on highly technical, user facing challenges that combine numerous technologies to create something novel. I’m well versed in agile development concepts, product management, hiring and management. The ideal product is one that enriches my life in some way; I don’t have to be the primary customer, but it’s nice to be one of them.
