Jule Gamache's Summary

I am a professional intern in Account Management with Disney Global Recruitment Marketing, where I support and develop marketing strategies for businesses across the entire Disney Enterprise.

I've been with the company since 2012, when I did the Disney College Program as an Attractions Host. Then, I spent the following school year promoting the program as a Campus Representative, before returning to a seasonal role in attractions. Now that I've graduated, I am excited to be back in a professional role.

I am a hard worker with exceptional organizational and communication skills. I am very motivated to learn more and gain further experience. I am interested in any opportunities to enhance my skills in public relations, marketing or communications.
