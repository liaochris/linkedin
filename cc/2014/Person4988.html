Severin Sorensen, CPP ★'s Summary

Severin Sorensen, CPP is a security advisor to Chief Security Officers, C-level executives, and boards of directors. Sorensen is President & CEO of Sikurity LLC, and he has 24+ years senior-level executive management consulting experience in physical security. Sorensen is founder of the LinkedIn CPTED (Crime Prevention Through Environmental Design) Group with over 2,500+ members; author of "Economic Misery and Crime Waves" (2009) available at Amazon.com; he advises security industry thought leaders on emerging trends in security, advanced video surveillance, perimeter protection technologies, and Crime Prevention Through Environmental Design (CPTED). Sorensen is Past Academic Program Advisor, ASIS Assets Protection Course I - Concepts and Methods (2008-2010); Past Chairman, Physical Security Council (2006-07), and member of the ASIS Physical Security Council, International Subcommittee. From July 2005 to March 2007, Sorensen was VP, Business and Applications Development, for Westec Interactive Security. From 1994 to July 2005, he was President/CEO of Sparta Consulting Corporation, a security consulting, security systems integration, and remote video monitoring solutions; and Program Manager of HUD’s Crime Prevention Through Environmental Design (CPTED) initiative from 1994-2002. Sorensen also served in the White House (1992-93) as a Special Assistant to the President and Regional Liaison, Bureau of State and Local Affairs, Office of National Drug Control Policy (ONDCP). Sorensen has consulted commercial industry, state and local governments, the US Government, Organization of American States, the World Bank, and other international bodies. He earned the Certified Protection Professional (CPP) certification (1997), the M.Phil. Degree in Economics (International Political Economy) from King’s College, Cambridge University, England (1988), and B.S. degrees with honors in Economics and Political Science at the University of Utah (1986).

Specialties

Subject matter expert in Physical Security, and the security of critical assets including people, processes, and assets. Primary focus has been on Crime Prevention Through Environmental Design or CPTED, and wide area surveillance programs involving sensor based monitoring including IP and CCTV video.
Part-time CSO, C-level executive advisor and coach

President and CEO

Sparta Consulting Corporation

1994 – 2005 (11 years)

Sparta Consulting was a security management consulting firm providing security assessment, security systems design, integration, and remote video monitoring services. Founded in 1994, and sold to Westec Interactive Security in July 2005, the Sparta focused on security consulting projects primarily in the public and assisted housing markets. Early projects included Crime Prevention Through Environmental Design (CPTED) and youth violence prevention funded by HUD. Security assessments and trainings were conducted through the US for public housing authorities, police departments, municipal leaders, and architects. In 1997, the company expanded into security systems design and installation, and housing management supportive services (youth violence prevention, HUD community and supportive services). In 2001, the company consolidated focus on security markets and began the process of establishing a state-of-the-art remote video monitoring network that was ultimately sold to Westec.
