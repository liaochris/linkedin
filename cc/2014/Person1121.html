Gary Shouldis' Summary

I started my internet marketing career in 2006 running Pay Per Click advertising campaigns for several businesses that were part of a large franchise chain. Quickly expanded to other local businesses and in 2008, began providing Search Engine Optimization (SEO) and Content Marketing services as well.

I have extensive knowledge in working with franchise chains in regards to implementing web marketing solutions across hundreds of individual units. I’ve successfully implemented local SEO and PPC campaigns for individual businesses as well as regional and national chain stores.

My Specialties are:
 Search Engine Marketing (SEM)
 Local Search Engine Optimization (SEO)
 Content Marketing
 Customized Web Analytics and Site Audits

I’m the founder and CEO of 3Bug Media, a Web Marketing company that works with small businesses and service professionals in building their online presence and their bottom line.

In 2010 I started The Small Business Playbook, a small business blog dedicated in helping small business owners find resources and advice that they can take action on. In just over a year, I built traffic to the website to over 15,000 visitors a month, using a combination of content marketing, link building and social media marketing. I love to write about all things small business and enjoy helping other business owners build a successful business.

I'm an area developer for a national franchise chain and have extensive experience with franchise sales, operational planning and web marketing strategies for multi-location businesses..

I spent 13 years with the NYC Fire Department, several of them as an officer. I’m a certified crises management consultant and have completed the leadership training program for senior fire officers. I frequently speak and consult with business groups about marketing, leadership and management principals.
