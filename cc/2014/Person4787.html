Robert Minton's Summary

Who is Robert Minton? He is an executive passionate about unlocking the real potential of a product, a business, and a team.

He thinks creatively to identify and forge internal and external strategic alliances and partnerships that deliver results.

Formulates creative marketing strategies and directs the execution for product launches, sale lead generation, leads product development and research, and digital, mobile, and social initiatives.

International business management experience working with Europe, Japan, and North America.

Has lead content development initiatives with emerging and traditional media, working with top-tier consumer and lifestyle brands.

Balance vision and instinct with strong analytic and fact-based decision-making skills.

Solves difficult and complex issues in a corporate setting by building necessary alignment across stakeholder groups, driving profitable outcomes.

Excellent ability to articulate value-based solutions to complex business problems, develop high level strategy, and lay out the path for successful implementation and measurement.

Extensive working knowledge of brand management, advertising, marketing, public relations, market segmentation, visual communications, sale, business development, product development, and consumer research, organizational structure, compensation strategies, asset allocation, and business evaluation.

Proven ability to achieve revenue goals and manage expenses while spending strategically to meet key business objectives. Knows how to lead, motivate and coach both individuals and teams.

Strong quantitative and analytic skills, with the ability to distill large amounts of quantitative and qualitative information down to actionable plans and tactics.

Consulting work for some of the worlds best, including McKinsey & Company, Bain & Company, HighBridge Capitol, as well as other media, nonprofit, and technology clients.
