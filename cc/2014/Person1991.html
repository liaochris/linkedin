Alex Harvey's Summary

Venture, Growth and Leveraged finance professional working with a broad range of technology and innovation businesses specifically across telecoms, mobile, internet, cloud computing, semiconductor and enterprise software & solutions.

Previously involved in operational and strategic planning roles for large corporate technology companies and management consultancies.

Specialties: Working closely with technology company execs and their equity partners to fund early product development, accelerate growth, provide working capital solutions and ultimately help finance exits.

Analysis of business models, product rollouts, market opportunities and growth strategies at a variety of different company life stages; early-stage, growth and corporate mid market.

Business development, transactional, and portfolio management skills.
