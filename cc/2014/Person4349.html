Michael Lamy's Summary

I am a highly driven photographic professional with a passion for related technology, learning and education. An early adopter of digital mediums who digs deeply into current software and hardware related to graphic design and photography, while remaining ahead of the curve by keeping an eye on "the next thing". I get energized by working collaboratively with similarly motivated creative professionals.

My career path has included positions as staff photographer for the marketing department of May company department stores, ownership and operation of my own digital advertising photo studio, the set up of digital studios for companies such as TIMEX, BIC, Ethan Allan and Black & Decker.

My strong creative aesthetic has been called upon by Hallmark Cards, where I served for 6 years as Senior Retoucher. I remain current and at the forefront of my industry through the achievement of Adobe Expert and Adobe Instructor Certifications in Photoshop CC, and I have shared my real world experience at Universities, seminars and online training productions since 1990.

Specialties: Adobe Certified Expert/Adobe Certified Instructor-Photoshop CC, Adobe User Group Manager, Apple Macintosh system configuration, troubleshooting and suport. Softproofing & color management in design/prepress environment. Workflow development and optimization. Experienced in set up of high volume digital commercial advertising photo studios, including initial assement, systems configuration, installation, training and support. Creative leadership through shared passion and inspiration.
