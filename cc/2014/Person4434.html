National Notary Association

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Nonprofit Organization Management industry

January 1957 – Present (57 years 9 months) Chatsworth, CA

Established in 1957, the NNA is more than the nation's largest, most active Notary community and leading provider of products and services. We work closely with government to set standards of practice, guide Notaries through important duties and provide comprehensive protection.
