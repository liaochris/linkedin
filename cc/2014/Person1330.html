Kevin Kraushaar, Esq., CAE's Summary

Kevin has a notable record of accomplishment in government relations, association leadership, and public speaking, having served as the chief of federal and state lobbying for a major national trade association and the head of the Washington, DC office of the lobbying subsidiary of a prominent national law firm. At the federal level, he has used his knowledge of the legislative process and his extensive network to champion several pieces of legislation that preserved channels of distribution, assisted in bringing new products to market, secured federal appropriations, and encouraged more efficient utilization of our healthcare resources. He has also directed lobbying strategies in 30 states and is currently managing a multi-state advocacy program for a national healthcare client.

Kevin is also a nationally recognized speaker, trainer, and coach on developing and implementing successful advocacy strategies and effectively lobbying Congress and state legislators. His presentations to government relations professionals and association volunteers include, "Smart Lobbying: Using Emotional Intelligence to Improve Lobbying Outcomes," "The Four Essential Practices of the Successful Government Relations Professional," and "Citizen Advocates: Making the Most of Your Day at the Capitol."

Kevin is an experienced coalition builder with a proven ability to work with whatever political party is in control. He has worked effectively with members of Congress, cabinet secretaries, governors, state attorneys general, state legislators, mayors and local officials. He serves as a mentor/advisor for the University of Michigan in Washington program and he has chaired the ASAE Government Relations Symposium. He is the only advocacy consultant, trainer, and coach who has served as a legislative staffer at the state and federal level, is a licensed attorney, and the holder of the Certified Association Executive (CAE) disignation from ASAE.

Specialties

Federal and state government relations consulting, training and coaching. Public policy expertise in healthcare, FDA-regulated products, DEA regulations and enforcement, and federal taxation.
