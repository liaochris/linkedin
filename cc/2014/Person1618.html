Richard Michie's Summary

I’ve worked in Marketing for a long time. In that time I’ve worked in lots of different industries from Retail to Travel, Digital to Mail Order. In that time, I’ve learnt a lot and achieved some amazing things.

My day time job

Director of Marketing and Technology for Global Lingo a language services company based in Leeds London, Singapore and Romania. The whole company is passionate about delivering high quality language services we go the extra mile with quality because that what companies love us for.

In the night

Co-organiser of Bettakultcha a series of events in which people with passion talk. The twist is that they have to stick to our rules: 20 slides, each changing every 15 seconds, NO SALES PITCHES. I’ve now organised over 50 Bettakultcha events across the North of England including a 400 seat sell out at Leeds Town Hall.

Organsised speakers to appear over several days at the Magic Loungeabout in 2012 and Beacons festival in 2014 including a Hurdy Gurdy player and a Musical Saw player!

My passion

Social networking is my passion (what did I do before twitter?). Twitter, Facebook and LinkedIn are pretty much my life. Used in the right way they bring people together and help businesses make money, which is after all what we all want. As a knock on from this, I’ve been on the panel for several Social Networking discussions and I’m always keen to help others and learn new ideas at the same time.

The real me

The father of four, step dad to one, I was born and raised in Sheffield but lived in West Yorkshire for a while now. Now happily ensconced in Allerton Bywater I love taking photographs (maybe too much).

Get in touch

If I can help at all please let me know, always happy to meet for a coffee and have a chat.

We provide language services to two of the worlds largest oil companies, three current Prime Ministers as well as a whole raft of other organisations.
