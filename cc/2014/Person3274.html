Ignacio Paredes' Summary

Ignacio Paredes has a M.S. in Computer Science and works as Studies and Research Manager at CCI. Since 1999 he has been involved in different projects related to information security for important industrial and telco companies. He is an expert in the design and deployment of technical and administrative security solutions, including topics such as applications security, secure network design, critical infrastructure protection, ethical hacking or business continuity planning. He is also a security expert assisting the implementation of the ENISA work programme in the areas of Information Security Considerations, Information Security Risk Management, International Standards and Best Practices and CIIP.

Specialties: ISACA: CRISC, CISM, CISA; (ISC)2 Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP); PMI Project Management Professional (PMP), GIAC Global Industrial Cyber Security Professional (GICSP); GIAC Systems and Network Auditor (GSNA); GIAC Assessing Wireless Networks (GAWN); BS 7799 Lead Auditor by BSI (British Standards Institution); EC-Council Certified Ethical Hacker (CeH); Optenet Certified Systems Engineer (OCSE); Sun SCNA and Sun SCSA
