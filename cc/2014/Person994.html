David Carroll's Summary

David M. Carroll is eager to connect and network with other professionals and have a positive impact on the community. Having several years of recruiting, admissions, and enrollment management experience in undergraduate and graduate education, David serves as the Director of Recruitment for the MBA for Professionals program at Willamette University, serving both the up-and-coming leader or the executive who wants to develop their management skills. Please feel free to contact him for more information on the MBA for Professionals program with classes in both Portland and Salem, Oregon.


- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

Willamette University is seeking qualified professionals who are looking to advance themselves in their career and join a top ranked MBA program. The MBA for Professionals is designed with your success in mind. Having based our accredited, well designed curriculum on what upper level management seeks in employers, you will have the opportunity to immediately apply what you're learning in your workplace. Courses are offered in an evening format in both Portland (The Pearl, 905 NW 12th Avenue Portland, 97209) or the Salem campus.

Interested in learning more about Willamette's MBA? Contact David if...
*You know a motivated individual seeking an advanced degree in business...
*You know a company with lots of up and coming leaders...
*You know a business with future succession plans...
*You are ready to take the next step in advancing your career!
