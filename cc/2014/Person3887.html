Kushtrim Xhakli's Projects

  • Mobile Payments Solutions

    • July 2009 to Present
    Team Members: Kushtrim Xhakli

    Telecommunication media company Fast Europe Ventures and internet portal Telegrafi.com have teamed up to introduce a payment service for mobile users in Kosovo. The service enabled customers to use their mobile phones as mini-credit cards, and make payments using the available credit on their mobile phone accounts. Since January 2009, Fast Europe Ventures has been closing agreements with operators and partners based in Kosovo, Albania and Macedonia to launch the mobile payment service in the region. The company served 2.8 million mobile subscribers in the Western Balkan region. According to Fast Europe Ventures data, spending for m-payments in Europe reaches EUR 30-50 per person on average, with EUR 10 annual spending per person.

  • Baltic E-Banking Report

    Team Members: Kushtrim Xhakli

    eBanking Report aim is to reflect the financial sector and show where the banks are vis-à-vis their peers, and motivate them to perform better services and solution by selecting a leader in the market and strengthening the financial sector.

    Report is put together using top-notch, unbiased researchers and closely examines each bank's e-banking system functionality, security, convenience, innovation, customer service responsiveness and mobile banking experience. The methodologies used are laid out in the report's subsequent section and in accordance with European Banking Authority (EBA).

  • Digital Kosovo

    • May 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Kushtrim Xhakli

    Kosovo citizens face a number of barriers using internet to its full potential. From shopping websites to travel, people simply cannot select Kosovo on websites around the world. This initiative seeks to help overcome these virtual barriers and advance Kosovo’s digital presence across major websites around the world, so that citizen of Kosovo can access the internet in the same way as all global citizens.

    The primary objective of the Digital Kosovo is to advance Kosovo's position in digital world and assist the citizens of Kosovo to overcome virtual barriers in web portals around the world.
