Adam Richard's Summary

Adam Richard has spent the past 9 years as an entrepreneur in the following industries: software, branding, marketing, design, film production, photography, and vertical market supply chain. In recent years he has studied integral theory, social media and marketing strategies.

Adam founded Noble Imaging, LLC in 2009. Noble Imaging is a full service brand identity and web development firm that specialized in company conceptualization, brand strategy, marketing collateral, and web 2.0 content management systems.

Adam co-founded Metal Networks, Inc., an exchange system for the buying and selling of industrial metals between large and small companies. As the CTO of Metal Networks, Adam developed and deployed SaaS enterprise solutions for buyers and sellers of industrial metal.

Adam is also the founder of the Houston Integral Salon and the Houston Bitcoin Meetup, volunteers for Carrie's Heart, and was the headline sponsor for NAMI Walks 2012, to better the lives of individuals and families living with mental illness.
