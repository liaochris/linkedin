Background

Summary

I prefer a business approach to technology projects. It is my belief that you can have attractive and compelling web design and marketing material without losing focus on results. I provide innovative and professional web designs, web sites, logos, print, SEO, and SEM services. My specialty is in online marketing, including web design and search engine visibility results. Based in Fort Wayne Indiana, but providing nationwide service.

I've also been hosting the Social & Marketing Breakfast Fort Wayne (SMBFW) monthly events since 2011. We have a thriving local marketing community and would love to have you join us.

I speak frequently at conferences, seminars, webinars, college classes, radio, occasionally on TV, and have been an announcer at events for over 30 years. Please contact me about speaking engagements, strategy development, or consulting. I can be found either participating, guest appearing or hosting, on Twitter chats almost every week.

Specialties: Online & Offline Marketing, Inbound Marketing, Content Marketing, Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Web Design, UX/UI, Brand & Identity, Search Engine Marketing (SEM), Sales, Event Planning, Photography, and Lan Parties/Events.
