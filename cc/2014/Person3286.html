Instant Tax Solutions' Summary

Instant Tax Solutions was birthed out of a vision to join expert tax relief services with an absolute commitment to integrity. Instant Tax Solutions partners Byron Pedersen and Ian Woodman were also committed to the goal of offering reassurance and hope to every client. The two founders of Instant Tax Solutions vowed to offer each potential client a free consultation that would include a financial analysis and a plan for resolving tax debt. The Instant Tax Solutions partners also resolved that any person not qualifying for IRS tax relief would be advised at no charge.

As Instant Tax Solutions grew, Pedersen began speaking with other leaders in the tax relief industry and found there were many who connected with his passion for doing it the “right way.” Tax attorneys, CPAs, former IRS agents and Federally Enrolled Agents became part of the Instant Tax Solutions family. With this connected network, Instant Tax Solutions was able to help many more clients and experienced exponential growth.

Today, with over forty tax professionals on staff, Instant Tax Solutions continues to grow with one primary focus: a genuine concern to help hurting people. With a perfect rating from the Better Business Bureau, Instant Tax Solutions boasts clients from across the United States. Instant Tax Solutions continually receives referrals from past clients based upon their integrity and education, genuine compassion, up-to-date knowledge of Internal Revenue Service tax relief programs and superior rapport with the Internal Revenue Service.

According to Instant Tax Solutions co-founder Byron Pedersen, Instant Tax Solutions continues to seek knowledgeable partnerships, credible agents and industry experience that have solidarity with the company’s mission statement and value of integrity. Pedersen adds that prior to requesting any form of payment, Instant Tax Solutions makes it a point to determine if the client can truly be helped, and is prepared to tell them the truth up front.

Specialties

With genuine concern for the Instant Tax Solutions’ clients as a foundational cornerstone, and the combined tax relief experience of the partners and staff of Instant Tax Solutions equaling well over 100 years, the professional and highly educated staff continues to remain current with tax changes. For more information about the assistance offered by Instant Tax Solutions visit them online at http://InstantTaxSolutions.com.
