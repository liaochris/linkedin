Ken Jacobs, CPC's Summary

I'm the principal of two companies, serving two different audiences:

Jacobs Executive Coaching helps executives and managers at corporations and companies empower themselves to become more skilled, insightful, inspiring, and effective leaders, to achieve breakthrough results, and meet and surpass their career, organizational and personal goals.

Jacobs Communications Consulting, LLC helps public relations and communications agencies grow business, and enhance staff performance, communications, and leadership skills.

We do so via consulting, training and coaching. Jacobs Communications Consulting advises on agency business development plans, agency marketing-engagement-communications campaigns, and prospect client proposals and presentations; consults on agency structure and profitability; offers leadership, communications and management skills, and business development workshops; designs agency training programs, and facilitates idea-generation sessions.

Training programs include "Five-Star Client Service," "Managing Up," "Managing and Motivating Your Teams," "Managing Difficult Clients," "From Manager to Leader," "Account Supervisors Boot Camp," "The Art & Science Of Effective Delegation," and "Having Difficult Conversations," among others.

In addition, Jacobs Communications Consulting offers coaching to PR executives and teams.

Specialties: Executive Coaching, Consulting, Training, Business Development, Agency Management, Staff Retention, Professional Development, Agency Marketing, New Business, and Improving Staff Performance in Client Service, Leadership, and Communications Skills
