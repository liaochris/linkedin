Chris Mendez's Summary

Director of New Media for USC Radio, technology consultant for non-for-profits, Corporations and, B-corp start-ups. As a digital producer, I split my time between building (or enhancing) brands and building applications. In my experience, both toolsets go hand-in-hand and provide a clearer path for market success. When it comes to building great brands, I help companies with their market research, ideation, project-based mission statements, value propositions, aesthetic design and business plans. On the software-side, I've done everything from Javascript prototypes to app architectures designed for a 1M+ users. Since the scope of any website, app, API, software supply chain, arduino sketch or physical product can vary, it's my role to help companies find a balance between their core competencies and their audience. Above all, my primary function is to help companies find new ways to connect, engage, empower and delight communities online.

    Take Classical KUSC with you anywhere you go for iPhone and iPad.   • Geotunes (Spotify)

    • January 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Chris Mendez, Grant Kindrick, Marc Neuwirth, Carly Gillis, Tim Herscovitch, Albert Diaz

    Geotunes provides Spotify users with fun, new ways to discover relationships between songs and the world around them. Explore brief features that tell the hidden stories behind songs and connect interesting tunes to their not-so-obvious geolocations.

  • WorldVenues (iPad, iPad mini)

    • November 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Chris Mendez, Grant Kindrick, Carly Gillis, Sherwin Zadeh, Marc Neuwirth, Albert Diaz

    World Venues gives music fans a way to virtually visit concert halls and opera houses around the world by navigating an interactive map of sharable venue profiles.

    Whether it’s a stadium or a single stage, a venue is an epicenter of music appreciation. By showcasing the ambiance, architecture, and history of each site, World Venues helps users understand and explore the places that house the performances they love.

  • Soundsnips (iPod Touch, iPhone)

    • August 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Chris Mendez, Grant Kindrick, Sherwin Zadeh, Carly Gillis, Tim Herscovitch, Albert Diaz, Melissa Schwartz

    SoundSnips provides curious listeners with an educational place to tell the stories behind the music by combining interesting facts with their favorite tunes.

    We believe the more you know, the more you hear. From identifying what exactly a theremin sounds like to telling the strange history of the world's most famous composers, Soundsnips simultaneously gives listeners the context they need while they're experiencing the music of their choice.
