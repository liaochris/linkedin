Mark Glubb's Summary

I have been hiring tax specialists in the accounting, legal and private banking markets since 2003 with a particular focus on Tax Director and Tax Partner moves. My focus is on the UK, Europe and the Middle East and I pride myself on having excellent direct contact to the tax leadership across the market.

If you are interested in a potential move, making a key tax hire or simply a career/market update then please do call me on +44 (20) 7332 2126 or email on markglubb@brewermorris.com.

Established in 1987 Brewer Morris was the UK’s first recruitment consultancy to focus exclusively on the recruitment of tax professionals. We are firmly established as a market leader and take great pride in the enormous number of long-term working relationships that we have developed throughout the tax and treasury world.

Brewer Morris is a member of The SR Group. The SR Group is a specialist recruitment consultancy dedicated to raising standards in the business environments that we serve. The sectors we cover include tax, legal, human resources, marketing and executive search.
