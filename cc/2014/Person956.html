Background

Summary

Let me tell you a little about me...

I'm an enterprise focused agile coach dedicated to providing large scale agile transformation services to help pragmatically, incrementally, and safely introduce Agile methods into any sized organization. As of late I spend most of my time doing sales and business development, running and growing our company, developing our team, and providing strategic coaching for our clients.

Before LeadingAgile, I was the Regional Vice President of Pillar Technology Southeast. We were building the Southeast region, and I was responsible for new client acquisition and engagement design. It was important to me to stay involved as a hands-on Agile Coach and Trainer for our larger, more complex agile initiatives.

I've also served as an Agile Coach and Trainer with VersionOne where we helped customers lead large-scale Agile transformations and adopt Agile Project Management best practices. Before joining VersionOne, I worked as a Senior Project Manager for CheckFree Corporation (now a part of Fiserv) where I cut my teeth on large scale agile program and portfolio management.

I'm a certified PMP Project Manager, a PMI Agile Certified Practitioner, and a Certified ScrumMaster. I was on the steering committee that created the PMI-ACP certification and I co-lead the creation of the DSDM Agile Project Leader certification. I'm also an honorary member of the DSDM Consortium and a fellow of the Lean Systems Society.

I've served on the board of the APLN, the Lean Software and Systems Consortium, and the PMI Agile Community of Practice. I run the local Scrum user group in Atlanta and I blog with our team at http://www.leadingagile.com.
