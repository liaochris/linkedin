Rahul Vohra's Experience

LinkedIn Intro

LinkedIn

Public Company; 5001-10,000 employees; LNKD; Internet industry

March 2012 – Present (2 years 8 months) San Francisco Bay Area

In 2012, LinkedIn acquired Rapportive, an email company that I co-founded. Since then, we have been furiously working together to build a groundbreaking mobile product: LinkedIn Intro.

The growth of mobile email is simply staggering. In 2009, less than 4% of emails were read on mobile. In 2013, half of all emails are read on a mobile device!

So we set ourselves the challenge: bring the power of LinkedIn, and the technology of Rapportive, straight to the Apple Mail app on your iPhone.

We call it… LinkedIn Intro.

With Intro, you can immediately see what people look like, where they're based, and what they do. In just one tap, you can unlock a rich, interactive, application-like experience — right in your iPhone Mail app. You can see a summaries of people and your strongest mutual connections. You can see occupations, past and current, and their full descriptions. You can even see where people went to school.

With Intro, you can craft the perfect response.

Intro gives you everything you need to put faces to names, establish rapport, write the most effective emails — and ultimately — be brilliant with people, and better at at what you do.

To get Intro for iPhone, head to intro.linkedin.com.
