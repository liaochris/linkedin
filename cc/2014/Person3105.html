Michael Story's Summary

TIDBITS:

Born in Connecticut, grew up in South Carolina, lived in Florida, North Carolina and now California. Also lived in Mexico for about a year and half if you count all of the surf trips down to Puerto Escondido. Got my first real opportunity in Action Sports from Hunter Joslin and Carol Holland working as a travel agent for Surf Express (free surf trips, right?).

Produced surf movies with Kevin Welsh, Sold surfboards for Ricky Carrol and Pat Rawson, owned a car wash business, surf/skate retail shop, and since 1998, have embraced the online world producing digital media, marketing, strategy, design, events, eCommerce, and simply immersed myself in the whole digital agency shin-dig for many outstanding organizations like the Action Sports Group, Quiksilver, Roxy, Vurb Moto, House of Blues, and many others.

My passions are Tennis, Surfing, Music, Marketing and People

    SURFER Magazine has been the mirror to the surf lifestyle and culture for more than half a century. Our tradition of employing the best journalists, photographers, and artists the surf world has to offer has enabled us to thoroughly document our culture, from the first surf session at the Banzai Pipeline to the shortboard revolution to “discoveries” like Tavarua and Tahiti to the careers of every surfer from Miki Dora to Dane Reynolds. If it’s relevant in surfing today, then SURFER is bringing it to our readership with intelligence, beauty, perspective, humor, and authority. They don’t call it “The Bible” for nothing.
