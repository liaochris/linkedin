Background

Summary

Mike Henry is a leader in the digital evolution of brand advertising and CEO of Outrigger Media. He founded Outrigger in 2009 and has since built the company into the definitive source of content and quality metrics for native online video. Through its OpenSlate analytics platform, Outrigger today measures all ad-supported content on YouTube and helps blue-chip brands develop and execute successful media and marketing campaigns on the site. Mike previously led digital ad sales at The Wall Street Journal and was the Chief Revenue Officer at Veoh, a pioneer in native digital video monetization.

Mike studied Economics at Bucknell University and lives in New York City with his wife and two children.
