July 2010 – January 2013 (2 years 7 months)

Original Fine Art, Junk Art, Wearable art plus Unusual Collectibles sold to support charity organizations. STAIN is an artist who gives all time energy effort and resources to making a difference. Whether it is creating something from nothing or finding a new purpose for something, the goal is to generate funds for the many charities in need from disaster relief to daily needs. True Thrift is a non-profit charity that sells items for local charities and ebay giving works missionfish charities. STAIN donates original art to help fund True Thrift which helps many causes.
