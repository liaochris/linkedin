Background

Summary

Kevin Sangsland is a marketing and public relations professional, with expertise in the technology, healthcare and automotive sectors.

As a member of an executive leadership team, he has led business development efforts internationally and set the market strategy for a technology and innovation focused agency's long-term growth plan. As a company and industry leader, he has given numerous presentations and speeches to internal and external audiences.

Over a 15-year career, Kevin has led account teams to develop contemporary integrated marketing communications and public relations programs to help engineering and technology clients improve their corporate positioning, corporate reputation and engage their audience through focused content development, while optimizing the key messages, content medium and mix of delivery channels to create the greatest impact.
