J. Kent Gervasini, CPC, CSP's Summary

Direct Hire Search & Recruiting | Temporary & Temp-to-Hire Staffing | Event Staffing | Payrolling

In July 1979, I started a professional Denver staffing agency and a direct hire contingency search firm called J. Kent Staffing with five goals in mind:

1. One central Denver location.
2. Build a respected, independent staffing firm with a small team of staffing experts able to effectively execute ever changing staffing best practices and technologies.
3. Provide value to Denver employers by continually sourcing and vetting the best employees available in our Denver marketplace.
4. Provide hands-on, day-to-day staffing operations management while continuing to connect with clients.
5. Remain connected to the Denver civic community.

My Career as a Denver Recruiter
I understand the on-going complexities of staffing and recruiting and the new, changing laws that today greatly affect the Denver employer’s hiring decisions, for example, the Affordable Care Act. I also understand the spirited Denver employment marketplace from the job seeker’s perspective. Therefore, whether you are an employer looking to hire, or a job seeker looking for a new Denver employment opportunity, choose the right staffing option for you.

Denver Staffing, Global Experiences and Golf!
I have lived in Europe, Canada, and the United States and have traveled abroad extensively. I have also had the good fortune, and unusual opportunity to experience hospital systems in the jungles of Ecuador, South America, in China in the cities of Hong Kong, Guangzhou, Beijing, Xian, Guilin, and in England and Ireland. These experiences have broadened my global perspective and have allowed me to operate a small staffing company in Denver within an informed national and international context.

Late in life, I was introduced by a wonderful friend to the game of golf, a sport that I believed to be boring, too slow and would not hold my attention. So, what’s my next T-time?

  • 60 Denver Event Workers Hired by J. Kent Staffing for the Annual 9 NEWS Parade of Lights

    • December 2013 to Present
    Team Members: J. Kent Gervasini, CPC, CSP

    The spectacular 39th Annual 9NEWS Parade of Lights is a two-mile parade route in Downtown Denver. Downtown Denver Events, Inc. (DDE) is the Downtown Denver Partnership’s charitable 501 (c) (3) that organizes the parade every year. This is one of Denver, Colorado’s most illuminating holiday traditions. Most importantly it is free to the public and features marching bands, ornate floats, and, of course, a special appearance by Major Waddles the Penguin and Santa!

    J. Kent Staffing Managers Executed Staffing Activities Working in Cross-Functional Teams

    The 2013 Parade of Lights took place on Friday, Dec. 6 and Saturday Dec. 7, two frosty nights with temperatures in the single digits. J. Kent’s Recruiting team knew that the weather would be challenging and would affect the recruiting process. Hiring 60 Denver Event Workers for 4 different shifts was the recruiting target.

    In the end our J. Kent Recruiters did an amazing job. The staffing team created a recruiting and staffing plan, and cross-functional teams were identified and assigned required staffing activities in order for J. Kent to meet its staffing deadlines and goals.

    J. Kent’s Denver Event Workers Have Undergraduate and Master’s Degrees & Great Attitude

    The Denver Event Workers hired by J. Kent’s Staffing Managers presented with a broad range of degrees, experience and backgrounds. Many of our project staff was employed and looking to work this fun event to earn extra Christmas money, others were between jobs, and some had just moved to the city. Thank you ladies and gentlemen. Great job!

  • J. Kent Hires 20 Intercept Surveyors Using Mini-iPads for DIA Market Research Project

    • April 2013 to Present
    Team Members: J. Kent Gervasini, CPC, CSP

    Denver Market Research Firm Hires J. Kent Staffing to Complete 4,000 DIA Surveys in 6 Weeks
    In Q4 2012, a nationally recognized, Denver based market research and consulting firm, with substantial experience evaluating economic and financial issues surrounding transportation services, and infrastructures contacted J. Kent Staffing. The charge: hire 16-20 technically savvy, “Tablet” Intercept Surveyors/Interviewers to complete 4,000 DIA traveler surveys, with an estimated completion timeframe of 6 weeks.

    Denver Surveyors use Mini-iPads for Intercept Survey at DIA
    J. Kent’s market research client, experts in designing custom research projects, analysis and reporting, required the execution of the 4,000 intercept surveys to be conducted at DIA using “tablets”, specifically mini-iPads. The benefits of tablet intercept interviews include:
    Real-time reporting – instant feedback
    Opportunity to interview people in close proximity to their experience
    Location awareness - multiple airport locations/concourses
    Faster project turn-around time – reducing time between fielding and reporting
