Srikanth Ramanujam's Summary

With my distinctive lean and change mindset, my aspiration is to continuously execute steps to be one of the most efficient scaled master orchestrator of customer value delivery by appropriately enabling and aligning business and technology teams.

A Lean Agile and Scaling Scrum Delivery Coach who focuses on process efficiency in order to achieve value delivery for clients. With over 30+ years of global software development and delivery experience, played valuable role in multi-million software and product development delivery endeavors in the last 15 years. Experienced with extensive work in outsourcing, on-site and off-shore software development models.

Expert in several large scale Enterprise Scrum based initiatives for the last 10 years, is certified as Disciplined Agilist DAD Green Belt, a Scaled Agile Framework (SAFe) Program Consultant, PMP, PMI-ACP, Scrum Alliance CSP, CSPO & CSM, ITIL and SixSigma Black belt.

As mentor, coach and trainer, strongly believes that there is no necessity to invent new processes, methods or frameworks. Effectively using existing frameworks like Disciplined Agile Delivery, Scaled Agile Framework or Scrum.org as guidance, fine-tunes the deployment and efficiency of the tailored process and applying Lean methods depending on the organization or situation at hand. Strongly believes that a team approach in leveraging your internal team members and their skills and external expertise as the situation demands will get the right things done in order to succeed.

Experience in finance, banking, cards, investments, payroll, HR, travel, airline, and insurance – clients like JP Morgan Chase, Charles Schwab, FedEx, Delta/Singapore Airlines, Bank One, HP, Ceridian, in a wealth of technologies covering Web, CRM, ERP, B2B/B2C, SaaS/Cloud and ECommerce systems, understands the right mix of things to do in SDLC and Agile Scrum best practices in Product Value, Requirements, Architecture, UX, QA, Tools, TDD, ATDD, CI, Build and Release Management
