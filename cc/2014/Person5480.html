Yahir Vergara's Summary

Freelance Audio Engineer | Sound Designer.

Air Talent | Producer Moody Radio Spanish Programming.

Volunteer Live Sound at Iglesia del Pueblo in West Chicago, IL.

Yahir Vergara is a graduate of Tribeca Flashpoint's recording arts program in 2012 with an emphasis in sound design for film. He is a native of Mexico and moved to the United States when he was 12 years of age which makes him completely fluent in Spanish and English.

He has worked in worship events at his local church. Post production credits include dialogue editing and rerecording mixer for a cooking show called "Radhika Desai", other credits include sound design, dialogue edit, rerecording mixer, location mixer and boom operator for the 48 hour film festival for the short film called "Grimm Reality". Amongst many more projects.
