K. Patrice Williams, J.D.'s Summary

Patrice is an accomplished Brand Strategy Executive and Speaker. As CEO of 123 Target Marketing and President of its Strategic Branding for the Government division, BrandGOV, she prides herself in the ability to make an immediate contribution to an organizations marketing, advertising, website or mobile application development needs.

“Brand distinction is the most beneficial decision an organization can make. The brand is the cornerstone of the corporate marketing strategy” says K. Patrice Williams J.D.

Patrice is also an International Speaker to Government, Corporation and Association audiences on the Topics of
- Create powerful Global Brand Strategies for your Business
- Using Mobile Applications to Engage and Influence

Patrice@brandGOV.com - Government Agencies and Non Profit Organizations
Patrice@123targetmarketing.com - Small Businesses
