Ron Dayan, MBID.'s Summary

Ron Dayan, director of design for Beverly Hills-based BEVERLY HILLS INTERIORS still wins the kind of assignments most interior designers would almost - - - for. The former Brit, a European-trained designer, serves high profile often celebrity-status clientele. And yet, he states: “I don't aim to impose my own taste on the client but try to be sensitive and intuitive to their needs, and the property's needs, sort of a Sherlock Holmes.”
So whether the project is an apartment on the beach or a mansion in the hills, Dayan, who is enriched with TV Set Design background from London and Hollywood, sets to transform villas and private jet planes in a dramatic and graceful fashion. And estate agents love him for increasing value of properties tremendously, which often go back on the market after completion by Dayan, at great profits to the owners!
