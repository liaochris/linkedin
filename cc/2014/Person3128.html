Background

Summary

The Gold Shield Agency, Inc. is the result of 20 years of exemplary law enforcement experience, many in detectives, which makes it THE best source for property and apartment managers who want to significantly reduce the risk of renting to a "bad renter".
What makes The Gold Shield Agency special? Unlike most applicant screening sites we are Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA) compliant which means our searches are, by law, more accurate and up to date. We verify the applicant's name, date of birth, and SSN to avoid a case of mistaken identity (which can easily happen) plus 6 additional screenings to create a truly cost effective risk management tool to save time, money, and aggravation. When you contact the agency, you get me..not a long recording asking you to push 1 for English.
