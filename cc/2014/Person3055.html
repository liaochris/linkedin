Andrew Fryer's Summary

Aside from a few years experiencing management roles in other disciplines, borehole geophysics has effectively been my life since 1982. I have worked for Schlumberger since 1988, enjoying many roles and responsibilities, in many countries. I do feel that it is important to understand and experience the operator’s perspective of borehole seismic, particularly in terms of communicating with their asset teams to determine whether borehole seismic can solve their technical issues. I thoroughly enjoy using the network of expertise that I have built up after 30 years in the industry that will further enhance borehole seismic solutions. It is important to me that any borehole seismic project is acquired and processed in a manner that is fit for purpose and is a financially sound investment in terms of rig time and cost. I have many publications, and frequently make presentations on many borehole geophysical topics.

As a footnote, my wife and I are British and have recently received our United States permanent residence. We are very happy living in Houston.
