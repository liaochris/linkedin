Background

Summary

My career includes a blend of my planning and environmental experience and with with food systems planning. Food supplies and safety are rapidly being compromised through the modern agricultural process; more focus is needed on our food systems for agricultural preservation and public health. I am currently working to develop the new Culinary Arts District in the downtown area of the City of Oakland Park. I am very excited about working with food systems and redevelopment! I have a strong need to be a part of improving the places we live, work, and play.

Senior Planner

City of Oakland Park, Florida
October 2013 – Present (1 year 1 month)Oakland Park, FL

I am a Senior Planner in the Engineering and Community Development Department at a medium-sized city in Broward County, Florida. I regularly process all the private-sector development applications (managing about 10 active projects at all times) and I contribute to various local government planning activities (Land Development Code updates, new City Ordinances, Climate Resiliency efforts). I am also working with the Oakland Park CRA to redevelop the downtown and the new Culinary Arts district. We recently approved 5 new restaurant spaces downtown; the changes underway are incredible!
I Chair the Development Review Committee and I am the staff liaison to the Planning and Zoning Board.

Planning Specialist & Recording Specialist

St. Lucie Transportation Planning Organization
December 2010 – October 2013 (2 years 11 months)St. Lucie County, Florida

I worked with the Safe Routes to School program (sidewalks and education initiatives relating to safety) including contributing to the local Google maps segments to show bicycle and walking trail connections. I kicked off the implementation of the 2012 Public Involvement Plan including the launch of a social media presence for the TPO (Facebook, Twitter, and Foursquare). Representing the TPO, I was a charter member of the Transportation Coordination Network (TCN), working to meet the needs of the Transportation Disadvantaged population. I also served a member of the FDOT Community Traffic Safety Team for St. Lucie County/Martin County.
Most recent projects include the Transportation Disadvantaged Service Plan (TDSP) - prepared completely by TPO staff, and the Transit Development Plan - prepared along with a planning consultant. Both involved public outreach and survey data analysis. I also supported staff in designating the signage locations for the County's first segment of the East Coast Greenway.
In addition to committee work, I prepared staff reports and presentations for the meetings, reorganized the reference library, conducted research and analysis for various planning projects, prepared surveys, supported the preparation of the 2012 Federal Certification materials, documented safety and transportation needs, and prepared public outreach efforts.

As a Recording Specialist, I prepared the minutes for various TPO board meetings such as the Bicycle Pedestrian Advisory Committee and the St. Lucie TPO Board, Local Coordinating Board for the Transportation Disadvantaged, and the RTO Board.
