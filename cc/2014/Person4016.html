Louise Tonkinson's Summary

I am a highly experienced communications professional, with 16 years’ continuous employment in senior private and public sector roles within which I have made a major impact. Recognised as one of the ‘Top 40 to watch under 40’ by the Municipal Journal, my campaigns, programmes and initiatives have not only received accolades such as CIPR Gold and Silver awards, but have also significantly contributed to the positive transformation and growth of the organisations I have worked for.

Dynamic, tenacious and with a true strategic outlook, I have the ability to both lead and support major organisational and cultural change. Dedication, integrity and ambition are ever present values in my approach - yet I believe this determination belies the sensitivity upon which my conduct within the workplace is based. Rapidly earning the respect and trust of new colleagues at all levels within an organisation, my ability to play such diverse roles as advisor, confidant, ally and friend is a genuine characteristic and one which adds tangible benefit to day-to-day operations. Commitment, integrity, and real leadership qualities are amongst my skills that make me ideally suited to a senior communications role within any organisation.
