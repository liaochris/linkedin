John James Newton (FCIM, FInstS, M IDM, MIoD, UXPA)'s Summary

Chartered marketer with digital marketing, offline, leadership and marketing strategy experience.

Over the last nine years I've worked in online advertising and affiliate marketing for market leaders such as Yahoo!, Aroq and ClickThrough - and previously spent time working in TV and outdoor advertising. I am also the author of the first-time managers manual, 'Nurture'.

I am a Fellow of both the Chartered Institute of Marketing and Institute of Sales and Marketing Management, and a member of the Institute of Direct and Digital Marketing, User Experience Professionals Association, Digital Analytics Association and Institute of Directors. I am also a Lean Six Sigma Yellow Belt (LSSYB) and a Marketing Society Business Leader.

I am currently Director of Marketing / Chief Marketing Officer at Aroq - a global online publisher that provides information, insight and intelligence to the automotive, beverage, food and apparel industries.

Digital Skills: Online Advertising, Content Marketing, Internet Advertising, Digital Advertising, Online Marketing, Conversion Optimisation, Web Usability, Internet Marketing, Google AdWords, PPC, Pay Per Click Advertising, Digital Marketing, Web Analytics, Website Project Management, SEO, Search Engine Optimisation, Search Engine Optimization, Email Marketing, Affiliate Marketing, Online PR.
