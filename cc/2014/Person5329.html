Tracy E. Austin's Summary

A proven leader capable of consistently bringing structure to projects and processes that are not well defined; with a passion for constant improvement, applying an entrepreneurial spirit in securing resources to grow and sustain business. With fifteen plus years of experience providing successful customer service, I bring out-of-the-box thinking to development and management.

My experience is qualified by academic preparation and enhanced by philanthropic service. My philanthropy is exemplified through: (1) the belief in the worth of every individual; (2) a commitment to nurture healthy relationships and influential networks; (3) and a vow of personal betterment through continued education and civic engagement. My ongoing desire is to serve the community at large with a spirit of excellence.
