Delft University of Technology

Educational Institution; 5001-10,000 employees; Research industry

September 2009 – June 2010 (10 months)

Summary of research proposal:

The proposed research focuses on the preparation and execution of facilitated, Group Support Systems supported collaboration processes. The focus is on the reduce and clarify patterns of collaboration. Very little studies on this topic are published, it is a necessary but difficult step in a collaboration process and currently there is little support for this step in the available GSS’s. The research question is what are the capabilities that a GSS needs to effectively support the facilitator and the group when executing a reduce and clarify pattern of collaboration? This question will be answered in three steps. First an overview is made of the approaches to reduce and clarify that currently exist (background), these approaches will be evaluated and the reasons for good and bad performance will be extracted. Second the most effective way of executing these patterns of collaboration within a GSS environment will be determined (theory building). In the third and last step a design to implement the found approach into a GSS will be described (theory testing). After implementation the approach will be evaluated using case studies.
