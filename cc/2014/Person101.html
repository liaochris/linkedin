Background

Summary

I am a software engineer, primarily working in the Java realm. I believe in doing things the right way, which means following engineering principles of procedure, documentation and design. On a more specific level, I am a proponent of test-driven, agile software development practices, design patterns, automated unit tests and constant refactoring.

Above all else, I'm committed to a mode of constant learning that sustains my engagement in my profession. I read books, participate in the virtual communities of the software that I use, and both attend and present at various conferences and developer groups. I'm also the author of two books: Struts 2 in Action and the SCMAD Exam Guide.

Lately, I've been enjoying the challenge of building a lightweight, but feature rich, content management system based on the Java Content Repository specification.
