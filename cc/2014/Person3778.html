Kay Gallegos, CPO®'s Summary

Kay Gallegos is a Certified Professional Organizer and owner of Clutterbusters!! Fort Worth and Clutterbusters!! Orlando. Clutterbusters!! provides professional organizing services for clients in their homes, offices and businesses.

Specialties

Home office organizing including time management and paper management; residential organizing services (garages, closets, kitchens, pantries, kids' rooms, bedrooms, and any other area of the home where there is clutter); workshops; work with busy families and individuals to help them declutter, simplify their lives and get organized so they can more efficient and less stressed.
