Gail Perry MBA CFRE's Summary

Nonprofit fundraising consultant, coach, blogger, keynote speaker and workshop leader.
Author, "Fired Up Fundraising: Turn Board Passion into Action." (Wiley and AFP).
Creator, Easy Fundraising for Board Members Retreat.

Popular international speaker on fundraising and board members; fundraising trends. Led national consulting firm for over 14 years, helped nonprofits raise hundreds of millions. My "FiredUp Fundraising INSIDERS" professional development program teaches smart nonprofit leaders the very latest in fundraising strategies and tactics.

Goals: Write another book or two. Create techniques into a model that can be easily diseminated and licensed. Have fun while helping to change the world! Throw the best party in town!

Specialties: Fundraising consultant, major gifts consultant, capital campaign consultant, board training and development, nonprofit webinars, motivational speaker, strategic planning, board retreat facilitator, nonprofit marketing and messaging, lobbying and generating political support, trends in the nonprofit fundraising environment.
