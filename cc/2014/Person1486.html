Michael Sheldon Reed's Summary

I am a graphic designer. Design, and from that the art and science and branding, is what I love.

My love of design is a huge part of what wakes me up in the morning. Growing up in Oakland, California, I was mesmerized by the excellence of the San Francisco design scene of the 70s and 80s. It inspired me to learn about design, about design people, and become a design person.

The road has been hard, the journey has been difficult, but I cannot turn my back on a field that, in its own small way, has the power to affect and change lives.

What I want to do, probably most of all, is to use my talent, my skill, and my knowledge to make other people's lives better, happier, more fulfilled. Isn't that what we are all called to do on this earth? To be of service to one another in some meaningful manner?

I want to converse with you about branding, about design. Talk to me. Ask me what I know, and share with me what you know.

I should also mention that I'm an Open Networker. Add me to your network. Perhaps there's a way I can assist you. Let's get to know one another and share ideas.
