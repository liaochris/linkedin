AVON Representative

AVON

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; AVP; Cosmetics industry

April 2012 – September 2013 (1 year 6 months) Portland, Oregon Area

AVON is a wonderful company that has been around for more than 125 years! Many of us have fond, nostalgic memories of growing up with AVON. They have grown as an organization to offer and feature so much more than make-up and skin care. World-class fashions, accessories, jewelry, home goods, children's and men's products are now all available through AVON. Excellent service and quality products at an affordable price have made AVON a leader in the global industry. The AVON Foundation is also the largest corporate supporter of the breast cancer cause and in 2004, created the Speak Out Against Domestic Violence campaign. Already hundreds of domestic violence agencies have received grants from AVON to help end the cycle of violence and save lives. In addition, AVON helps to keep the entrepreneurial spirit alive by allowing individuals to run their own businesses.
