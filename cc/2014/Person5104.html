Stephanie Belly Wilkins' Summary

I am seeking to network and grow my business specifically in the area of risk management for cross contamination and operator protection in the pharmaceutical and biotech industries. I am also interested in gaining and sharing knowledge in best business practices, operational excellence and social media.

I am a proven Project Manager with over 26 years of experience in managing and designing facilities with particular emphasis on pharmaceutical facilities. Strengths are in team building, communication and solving complex problems (breaking down complex issues into manageable parts leading to successful solutions), transform/ transfer ideas/ techniques/ initiatives for use in real world situations.

Techniques and programs like Lean Six Sigma, Risk Management, Project Planning and Control.

Specialties

design and implementation support for containment systems that protect patient, product and worker
