Jon Dean's Summary

A games industry veteran, Jon has been making digital entertainment for more than twenty-five years, building teams and studios, designing games, managing products "hands-on" from initial concept through funding, development and live operation. Most recently he was a Vice-President at the World's #1 videogame company, Electronic Arts; General Manager of their Salt Lake and Raleigh/Durham development & production studios, and Executive Producer of many hit games for EA and EA SPORTS.

Last year Jon co-founded a new company, XacFAQ, Inc, to focus on developing apps for smartphones, tablets and other connected devices such as Google Glass. XacFAQ has built a technology platform that allows content owners to quickly and easily deliver that content to connected consumers, with the entire development and live service managed by XacFAQ.

As a practicing game development executive, he also offers consultancy to parties interested in current game development -- creative, process or industry. Clients include brands who want to move into gaming, game publishers needing expert development problem solving, and developers that want advice from a seasoned executive. Yes, he has advice and is not afraid to share!

Jon sits on several advisory boards for technology companies and universities and regularly speaks at conferences and colleges on videogames, mobile gaming industry and game production process. He is a leader in Utah's game industry, a member of the Utah Digital Media UCAP Committee for the State of Utah and co-founder of a new digital media trade association.
