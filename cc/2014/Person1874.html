Background:
I have been reading, writing, and speaking about social class on campus for a many years and conduct keynote speeches and staff development experiences on social class on campus. http://socialclassoncampus.blogspot.com/

I teach in classes in a traditional classroom, in the TV studio, on-line using audio/video, at conferences, in staff development, and in print, and I have a nice collection of teaching awards. My favorite way to teach is still face to face, but text based chat classes come in a close second.

I know program evaluation well, both as a practitioner and as a teacher. I have been helping campuses, fraternities, and sororities collect student learning outcomes data using the University Learning Outcomes Assessment that I developed with Mark Frederick. We have masses of data and are working on several writing projects.

Specialties:
Social class on campus is my current specialty. I do a lot of staff development workshops on campuses making people aware of social class as an issue in student recruitment, retention, development, and learning.
