David Gadarian's Summary

Digital Planning & Social Media Marketing:

In my new capacity as the Digital & Social Media Marketing Manager for Dassault Systems I look forward to helping to continue to develop rich and compelling stories, while engaging with a tremendous brand at an amazing time in its evolution. It promises to be a great ride!

Through my former company Gadarian Digital I enabled businesses of all sizes to craft a comprehensive digital narrative that allows their brand to connect and communicate with their customer base. With strategic digital planning, I empowered business to more effectively tailor their message and manage their efforts, both on-site and through the utilization of social media marketing.

Some of the areas I focus on include the introduction and implementation of a blog, the usage of social media marketing tools including Facebook, Twitter and LinkedIn as well as the utilization of video, all working in concert towards a common goal. While I can work within a variety of frameworks, I am a big advocate of WordPress.

I also self published my first book, Better Business Blogging (http://www.amazon.com/dp/B00AD95M2E) currently available through the Amazon Kindle marketplace.

Television Development and Production:

Additionally I have tremendous knowledge and experience working in television having set up over 20 scripted projects for prime-time development at networks including CBS, NBC, The CW, TBS, TNT, SyFy and Comedy Central. Television remains an ongoing professional interest.

Collectively all these experiences serve all the clients of my consulting practice extremely well as I am very cognizant of both how companies want to project themselves and also how consumers are willing to embrace these messages.

Specialties: Social Media Marketing, Social Media Consulting, Twitter, Facebook Development, Digital Planning, Website Development, Brand Management, Business Development, Website Project Management, Online Journalism, Television Production
