Caroline Baxter's Summary

Caroline Baxter is a serial Entrepreneur, Motor Trade Business Owner, Music Manager, Sage Business Expert and Business Consultant, with over 17 years experience and Author of No 3 bestseller on Amazon Kindle – Why Women Do It Better.

Starting her first automotive business - SB Motors aged 24, Caroline created her first 6 figure business by the end of her first year. Based on the Beautiful Sussex Coast, her Other Interests Include Family, Fast Cars, Seagulls And Skimming Pebbles For Inspiration.
