Rachel Ferrucci's Summary

Social Media Architect and leader of social media tools and technologies, with a track record of creating and implementing successful social media programs. Keep up-to-date with constantly evolving technologies in online social networking, the blogosphere, search tools and Web 2.0. Partner with clients to create innovative, effective campaigns, bringing brand awareness through intelligent use of social media.

Rachel has worked with top brands including P&G, YesVideo, Cover Girl Cosmetics and Coca Cola where she implemented successful social media programs.

Rachel consults for multiple companies, which cater to dynamic online communities. Among Rachel’s consulting responsibilities, she creates social media strategies to further brand awareness, develops and facilitates virtual parties aligning client goals with live events, meetups and executes promotions using such platforms as Twitter, Facebook, Instagram and Pinterest. This experience led to her founding The Brand Connection in 2012, a social media boutique agency connecting brands and bloggers with event and conference sponsorship opportunities, tv placement on local and national programs, media training, social media branding.

She has interviewed such personalities as veteran newsman Sam Donaldson, celebrity trainer Angelo Sorrenti, and singer Ender Thomas of Yanni Voices.

Rachel works with various brands and attends events to spread social media messages for such clients as ABC-TV’s “General Hospital and “The View,” The UPS Store and Smarties Candy.

Having developed her multi-tasking skills early on as a working mother of two children and continuing in the tradition as a grandmother, Rachel created an organic product line Touch’d Bath & Body in her quest to ease her daughter’s eczema. The line has been embraced by family, friends, spas, salons, and select boutiques.

Connect with Rachel on Twitter (her handle is @RachelFerrucci) or Facebook.
