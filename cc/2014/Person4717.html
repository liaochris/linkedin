Randall Groger's Summary

Randall (Randy) Groger has over 42 years’ experience in the transportation field, including 28 years as a manager and executive in the international moving Industry. He is currently president of RKG Associates, which he founded in 2008 as the only consulting company focused on the International Military and GSA Personal Property Programs. He began his career in this industry in 1983 with Suddath Van Lines affiliate AirLand Forwarders, Inc., where he served as operations manager, general manager, vice president and president until 2008 when he founded RKG Associates LLC.

Randy was Chairman of the International Association of Movers (IAM) from 2007 thru 2011 having previously served for 6 years as Vice Chairman . He has been heavily involved representing the HHGS industry during the planning and implementation of the DOD Families First Program (Now DP3) and served on several Business Process Working Groups (BPWS) examining the requirements and administration of a quality based DOD personal Property Program. He also represented Industry’s position in numerous conferences with Transcom and SDDC leadership concerning on going programs, issues and plans through a variety of public forums and committees.

After graduating from Eastern Kentucky University in 1969 with a BBA, he served 5 years in the U.S. Army Transportation Core, attained the rank of captain and was awarded the bronze Star for Meritorious service in the republic of Vietnam. Randy lives in Jacksonville Florida with his wife Dianne. They have two children and four grand children.
