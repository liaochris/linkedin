Marianne E. Giltrud, MSLS's Summary

Marianne E. Giltrud, is a Knowledge and Information Professional and Adjunct Faculty, at The Catholic University of America where she leverages her experience in knowledge services, technology, business, and program planning in support of the University, the University Libraries and School of Library and Information Science. As the Director, of Membership and Recruitment for the KM Division of the Special Libraries Association, Marianne seeks to build community, educate and collaborate in order to effect positive change. As the Outreach and Program Manager for the CUA SLIS , “A Century of Knowledge, Service and Discovery” Centennial Celebration, Marianne engaged stakeholders and obtained buy-in. Moreover, she created an overarching vision for the year- long university program, wrote the marketing plan, and achieved successful outcomes. A deep generalist, Marianne advances education, pedagogy, learning and technology to achieve a clear and focused potential.

Specialties

Educational Technologies, Pedagogy, Information Literacy, Digital Media and Knowledge Management.
