Steven Gonzalez's Summary

Steven Gonzalez is leading authority on Radio, Digital & Sponsorship Sales. Over the past 10 years, Steve aka Gonzo, has sold over $8 million dollars in Sponsorships, culminating in a massively successful 2006 with his highest sales to date with over $900,000 in sponsorship sales. Not only is Gonzo a leader in the sponsorship sales category, he also has command over the sales process as it relates to Radio and Digital Advertising.

Currently with Radio One, Gonzo is a sought after leader in the organization, working with teams effectively and efficiently, while keeping an eye on the bottom line results.

Some of Gonzo’s career successes include:

1. Over $8 Million in Sponsorship Sales
2. $927,000 in Sponsorship Sales in 2006
