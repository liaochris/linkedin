Alison Green's Summary

I write the Ask a Manager website, where I answer readers' questions daily on workplace and management issues. Ask a Manager, which receives more than a million visits each month, has been recognized as a Forbes Most Influential Career Site, won a 2013 Bloggie Award (Best Topical Blog), and has been featured in USA Today, The Wall St. Journal, Business Insider, CBS News, Inc., ABCnews.com, Fox Business News, Marketplace Money, The Globe and Mail, Glamour, and more.

I also write a twice-weekly column on career and management issues for U.S. News & World Report, and I'm the co-author of Managing to Change the World: The Nonprofit Manager's Guide to Getting Results (Jossey-Bass 2012).

My writing on other topics, from etiquette in bars to what vegetarians eat, has been published in The Washington Post, The New York Times, Maxim, and dozens of other publications.

Previously, I was the chief of staff for a national nonprofit lobbying organization, where I oversaw day-to-day staff management.
