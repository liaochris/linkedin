Background

Summary

I co-lead the Education Practice at The Parthenon Group, with a focus on global education markets. For more than 15 years, I've led client engagements on general strategy, profit improvement, and investment due diligence across nearly all education sub-sectors. Current and past clients include high-growth companies, publicly listed Global 100 companies, financial investors, and international governments. I've participated in numerous high-profile corporate turnarounds, mergers, divestitures, and privatizations in Europe, North America, Latin America, the Middle East, and Asia.

I am a frequent speaker and discussion moderator at leading global forums in the education sector. Some of my recent speaking engagements include:
