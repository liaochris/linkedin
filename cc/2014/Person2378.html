Bryce Raffle's Summary

I am a professional sound designer currently working for Ironclad Games, as well as a freelance sound designer and recordist. I know what it takes to build a successful and impactful soundscape. I'm self-motivated, highly determined, and the experience and knowledge I've gained from Vancouver Film School and Ironclad Games has been invaluable in preparing me for any opportunities that should come my way.

Specialties: Music Editing, Composing, Sound Design, Sound Effects Editing, Sound Effects Recording, Backgrounds Editing, Foley, Game Audio Design and Implementation including Wwise and FMod, Dialogue Editing
