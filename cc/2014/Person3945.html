Lelia Hood's Experience

Director of Contract Counsel Legal Services

Law Offices of the Public Defender

Government Agency; 1001-5000 employees; Legal Services industry

February 2014 – Present (8 months) albuquerque, new mexico area

The Contract Counsel Legal Services (CCLS) division oversees and administers the Law Offices of the Public Defender contract attorney representation system. There are about 140 contracting attorneys statewide with a budget of 10.4 million in FY14. Contract counsel represent about 25,000 clients per year in New Mexico. In judicial districts where the Law Offices have district office operations, CCLS assigns contract attorneys primarily in cases where the district office has a conflict of interest. In judicial districts with no district office operations, CCLS assigns contract attorneys both as primary and conflict of interest counsel. Overall, the Law Offices provide contract counsel legal services in all of the state's judicial districts and appellate courts.
