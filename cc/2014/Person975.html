Dan Gellert's Summary

Grammy award winning audio mixer, engineer, producer. Worked as chief music recording engineer in New York, Los Angeles, and Seattle. Experienced in remixing, mastering, and restoring theatrical movie mixes for home release.

Interested in enabling creative people accomplish their desire through technology, psychology, and a bit of luck.

Currently writing the Beats Music Now blog - a kind of running history of Beats Music subscription service since its launch. Now part of Apple.
http://beatsmusicnow.wordpress.com/

Specialties: Being the liaison between the creative and the technical.
Music recording and mixing.
Audio mastering and restoration.
