Michael Boucher's Summary

I am an Interactive Media Developer looking for freelance and full-time work in the Washington, DC metro area as a Flash Designer / Actionscript Developer with PHP/MySQL capabilities. I have fifteen years of experience in the interactive media industry specializing in social media, content sharing, WordPress, Facebook, online games and social widget development. Past work has included projects with Disney, ESPN, FOX Sports, Paramount Pictures, Sony Pictures, DefJam Records, Live Nation and many other globally recognized brands.

I am the rare breed of hybrid, existing somewhere between the two poles of duality, feeding off of the energy created between design and development. This energy propels me through each project, with a desire to touch and contribute to all aspects of its success. From developing initial concepts to designing and producing the final product, my creativity and technical ability become an asset for the full life cycle of a project.

Specialties

Web design, Web development, Interactive Media, Flash, PHP, MySQL, E-commerce Development, Rapid Applications Development, Ajax, Interactive CD-ROMs, Motion Graphics, ActionScript, MDM Zinc, AfterEffects, Premier, Dreamweaver.
