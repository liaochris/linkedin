Munthir Al-Ansari's Summary

Al-Ansari began his career in the private sector, and after 10 years joined the Saudi Commission for Tourism and Antiquities. In his current role he manages the Tourism Information and Statistics Department which is responsible for all types of research and the subsequent creation of Key Performance Indicators for the Kingdom's Tourism Development Strategy.
Al Ansari is a Certified Knowledge Manager, candidate for a Doctorate of Business Administration from Maastecht Management School in Holland. He has a Post Graduate Diploma in Marketing Management from the Institute of Public Administration (IPA), and a BHS in Economy from Imam Mohammed University in Riyadh.
Al Ansari is a member of the Investment Committee at the Riyadh Chamber of Commerce and Industry, and is a member of the Panel of Tourism Experts for the World Tourism Organisation (UNWTO).
Also, he is a member of European Society of Market Research ESOMAR.
