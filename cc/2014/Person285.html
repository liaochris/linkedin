Background

Summary

Irfan has a diverse professional background, ranging from communication management to web/social media development. He has more than 14 years of experiences particularly on Information and Communications Technology (ICT). He is currently working for Australia-Indonesia Facility for Disaster Reduction (AIFDR) – Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade (DFAT) - Australian Aid as Communications Specialist. He is manage the AIFDR communications strategy, and develop supporting communications materials.

Previously working for @america, the US Embassy Cultural Center, Irfan developed online presence strategy of the site using social media channels. Working together with the other team, Irfan building networks with online communities & create integrated approach for the media development of the site. With his expertise on online analysis, periodically he provide recommendations for the site activities.

He also has strong passion in grass-roots activism and social marketing development, both online and offline. He is active in open-knowledge movement such Wikimedia and CreativeCommons. Since July 2011, he holds the Deputy Director of Wikimedia Indonesia, where he appointed as member of the selection team for Cipta Media Bersama (CMB). CMB is an open call for media projects that aims to support new and innovative ideas and practices that promote citizen rights and equitable media access in Indonesia.

Received around 800+ proposal, and funded 35 of them with a total fund of US$ 1.000.000, CMB is aim to advance the rights of poor and excluded populations in establishing, accessing and participating in a representative media environment. This program is funded by Ford Foundation, co-managed by Wikimedia Indonesia, AJI Indonesia and ICT Watch.

Irfan is an avid internet user since 1996, social media person. But prefer to spend time working with grass roots activist and village communities in the field.

Geek. Likes good joke. Love to write. Tweets a lot.
