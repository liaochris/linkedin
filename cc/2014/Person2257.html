Bianca Consunji's Summary

Experienced journalist specializing in web video, social media and long-form reporting.

I am a video producer at Mashable, described as "the largest independent website dedicated to news, information and resources for the connected generation."

As a freelance multimedia journalist, my work has been published in The New York Times, City Limits and the Gothamist, among others. I was previously based in Manila as a senior magazine editor for three years, and worked in the culture/lifestyle media industry for more than eight years.
