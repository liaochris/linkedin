Mark Bradley's Summary

Mark Bradley launched into his current role within HP Software as Senior Software Technical Product Manager for Strategic Analytics with a globally extensive charter to support sales of HP Executive Scorecard & Financial Planning & Analysis. His career progression with HP spans various BTO product set, from the legacy ServiceCenter to modern Executive Scorecard. Mark is continually both teacher and student as he embraces the depth and possibility of the product whilst refining skills and demonstrating to customers the value and potential of HP software.

His customer interactions and knowledge presentations have woven a journey of disseminating methodologies to customers, partners, and the HP team around the world of how to maximize their benefit of using HP product set. Challenges of proof of concept installs only provide opportunity to develop better product features. Matter of learning what customer needs and teaching them what they need for better success.

Mark is a contributing author of Implementing Service and Support Process: A Practical Guide (Van Haren Publishing, 2005.) The book is a reference tool compiling IT management best practices and IT Service Management within the ITIL framework from the vantage point of international experts. Mark’s chapter on Service Level Management aligns well with HP’s focus on ITSM implementations.

Mark has a BA in Computer Science & Speech Communication from Monmouth College & Masters of Science in Computer Information Systems from UOP. Mark has obtained several computer industry certifications over the years.
