Rosanne Gain's Summary

I have more than 25 years experience providing public relations services, writing and editing. My media experience is in radio and cable TV as on-air talent, copy writing and advertising sales. I have experience working with media as a: chamber executive, PR director of a fine art corporation and as an owner of a public relations firm.

Our company, Gain-Stovall, Inc. is a PR firm. We know how to help a client gain visibility through the non-advertising side of traditional media and online through the use of blogs, articles and social media.

We want to work with clients seeking a professional team with an extraordinary record for planning and achieving public relations goals, successful special event publicity, a strong background in policy analysis and the ability to work on multiple projects and meet deadlines.

Bob Stovall and I formed Gain-Stovall, Inc., a PR and Government Relations firm, in January 2009. We offer 50+ years of combined experience to clients seeking professional services to help their business grow.

Specialties: Creative expertise in public relations, copy writing, editing and policy analysis. Passionate about the written and spoken word.
