Adriana Elliot's Summary

Adriana is a New Hampshire Certified Family Mediator, located in Keene, NH. Adriana offers compassion and insight, as well as experience with the legal system.

By working with Adriana, you will have the opportunity to:

- work toward informed, fair and durable agreements
- reach decisions that are tailored to the individual needs of your situation
- avoid the excessive costs and emotional stress of litigation, whenever possible
- retain dignity and respect as you work through conflict
- improve your relationship or part ways more amicably

Adriana mediates privately, as well as for the New Hampshire Courts and for Cheshire Mediation in Keene, NH. She also works as a Guardian ad Litem in Cheshire County. She is a member of the national Association for Conflict Resolution (ACR) as well as the New Hampshire Conflict Resolution Association (NHCRA).

Specialties

Mediation and Facilitated Communication for Relationship Renewal, Separation and Divorce, Co-parenting, and Elder Care Decision-making.
