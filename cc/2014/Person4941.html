Sheila Avenson's Summary

I have been employed with The Office Shop since 2001, however, have worked in the "office" setting for over 30 years using the supplies, machines and furniture I am now selling. I am excited for all the changes in this industry and I enjoy educating myself on the new products coming in so that I can show our customers how I can save them time, money and space with these new items! Customer Service is my number one goal and that of The Office Shop as well! I will work hard to help you and would rather we use my time to find what you are looking for so your time can be spent doing your job.

Specialties: Honest, no strings or smoke and mirrors. We are here to help make your job easier. The Office Shop has everything you need in your office. The Office Shop..."where your office shops!"
