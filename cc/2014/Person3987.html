Lisa Sterling, HCS's Summary

A certified Human Capital Strategist by HCI, and highly experienced management individual with over 16 years of experience in software and product marketing strategy, design and execution. Broad range of expertise in recruitment, performance, succession, rewards/recognition, leadership development and retention solutions.

A thought leader to influencers, clients and colleagues. Effective blogger and disruptive thinker in the practices of sourcing, engaging, measuring and managing talent. Provides insight into talent processes and consultation for business-driven solutions to complex HR issues.

Specialties: Talent Product Strategist | Talent management consultation | business vision creation | strategy creation and execution | change management consultation and communication | public speaking engagement | social media engagements and content creation | product marketing and branding | resource planning and allocation | Market Strategy.
