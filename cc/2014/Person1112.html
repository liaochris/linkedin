Gabriel Mederos' Summary

I am a passionate and creative Senior Social Media, Public Relations and Communications professional, dedicated to the delivery of excellence and the enhancement of organizational profiles. I love to share my knowledge and strive to exceed both personal and organizational objectives.

I use an innate leadership ability and strategic vision to develop and implement successful social media, communications, CSR, digital marketing and PR strategies, programs and campaigns and have a proven track record of organizational achievement and recognition.

I am a strong customer advocate who works well across all areas and levels of organizations, committed to the delivery of value to my clients. I have also forged strong partnerships throughout my career with a diverse range of external audiences, including the media, in order to promote the organizations for which I work.

In recent years, I have studied a range of up-to-date marketing strategies and techniques, particularly the use of digital marketing and social media for organizations and brands. I have used this knowledge and training to develop comprehensive online programs and strategies, deliver social media training programs, and develop online community management guidelines and point of view documents on new social media platforms.

Gabriel Mederos' Projects

  • 2013 Purina Animal Hall of Fame

    • January 2013 to May 2013
    Team Members: Gabriel Mederos, Frances Manlucu, Jennifer Duggan, Mary Siemiesz, Lorna Freeman, Robin Shimkovitz

    For more than four decades, the Purina Animal Hall of Fame™ has honoured an elite group of pets and service dogs for their extraordinary acts of heroism and bravery, which have saved human lives. From sensing deadly medical conditions to saving their owners from wild animal attacks, more than 150 inductees have been recognized including 132 dogs, 26 cats - and one horse!

    Since 1992, the program expanded to specifically recognize the key role of service dogs in protecting the public and providing independence, comfort and hope to so many Canadians. To date, 18 service dogs have been honoured for their acts of extreme bravery and courage.

    New pet heroes are inducted annually into the Purina Animal Hall of Fame™ at a private ceremony in the spring
