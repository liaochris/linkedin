Background

Summary

Licensed in Illinois and New York.

I am a Chicago based personal injury attorney and trial lawyer concentrating on representing victims of personal injury sustained as the result car accidents, dog bites, slip and falls and other premises liability causes, medical malpractice, product liability and all other injury cases.

I am a life member of the Millions Dollar Advocate Forum which is exclusively reserved to accomplished trial lawyers with at least one verdict of one million dollars or more.

I have tried numerous personal injury cases before juries, and have participated in countless arbitrations and mediations.

I have settled millions of dollars in injury cases and I take great pride in getting results as fast as possible and obtaining the highest possible compensation for my clients.

In June 2007, I obtained my official certification from Center for Conflict Resolution as a Certified Mediator.

In 2008 I was selected to the Top 100 Trial Lawyers by the American Trial Lawyers Association.

I am also life member of the Cambridge Who’s Who.

Taradji Law Offices concentrates its practice in personal injury cases. Mr. Taradji’s goal is to make sure the highest compensation is obtained for his clients. Consistently achieved favorable results for clients, including two verdicts of more than $4 million.

Oversee complex cases, including claims for wrongful death and personal injury arising out of premises liability, product liability, and moving vehicle accident claims.

Regularly liaise with clients and develop trial strategies based on client needs.
