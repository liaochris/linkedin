Adam Gordon's Summary

I am the Director of Executive & Corporate Education at Wits Business School (WBS), University of the Witwatersrand, Johannesburg. I have been Acting Head (Dean) of School, and remain involved in senior executive management, strategic partnerships, and university advancement.
I have worked in executive development and education management since 2002, based in the UK while delivering primarily in emerging markets as an MBA lecturer (professor), a facilitator of executive education short courses, and instructional designer of in-company executive learning and leadership development.
As an academic, my terrain is industry foresight and business acumen. In addition to publishing and teaching in this area, I have facilitated many dozens of strategic planning workshops and speaking events, and have worked in-company with the CEO or Chief Learning Officer to align responses to industry-change imperatives. I was in a boutique futures consulting firm in Washington D.C. for four years in the mid-late 1990s, and prior to this I was a business journalist.
In addition to journal articles, my book is “Future Savvy: Quality in Foresight” (Amacom Press, NY). I also write "Management By Looking Ahead" at Forbes.com. (www.forbes.com/sites/adamgordon/)

Specialties: executive education, corporate education, business school management, university advancement, leadership development, educational design, workshop facilitation, strategic foresight, scenario planning, strategic planning, emerging markets, public speaking
