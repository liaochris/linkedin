TerraCORE Panels
December 2011 – March 2014 (2 years 4 months)Dallas/Fort Worth Area

As innovators in the natural stone industry, TerraCORE transforms the ageless beauty of authentic natural stone into a reinforced, lightweight stone panel cladding system that can be used for any type of exterior or interior application. TerraCORE panels are constructed of a natural thin stone veneer that is bonded, using the highest quality epoxies, to a variety of CORE substrate materials. This CORE may be comprised of Honeycomb, Polymer Aluminum, Ceramic, Glass, Fire Retardant or Fiberglass backing. The thin stone veneers can be cut from most stones, including: Granite, Marble, Limestone, Travertine, Onyx and Sandstone. TerraCORE’s unique and transformative stone panels are the ideal choice over dimensional stone projects now considered to be weight, time and/or cost-prohibitive.
