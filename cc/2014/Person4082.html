Marcus Neto's Summary

I've always loved technology. I built my first website back when Geocities was still around (circa 1997). I started Blue Fish Design Studio when we moved to the Gulf Coast in 2004. Since then Blue Fish has done work with both local and international organizations.

Blue Fish focuses on implementing websites that are viewable on the various devices availabe using Responsive Web Design. We can design, program, and help market your business' website. Standards-based development and sites that are easy for my clients to manage are important to us.

I was also the Product Evangelist for ExpressionEngine, and we are a member of the ExpressionEngine Pro Network.

Our Specialties Include: Photography, Web Design, Web Development, Graphic Design, Ad Layout, Marketing Consulting, Search Engine Optimization, E-Commerce, Social Media Consulting
