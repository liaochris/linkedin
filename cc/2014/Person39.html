Background

Summary

Marketing & Product Strategy — as career marketer and product strategist with over 20 years of experience, I have developed and launched several successful software, SaaS and information products in both domestic and international markets. I now use my experience as an industry analyst and product strategist to help organizations synthesize market signals into actionable go-to-market strategies.

Marketing Execution & Sales Enablement – having managed product-launch and demand marketing activities for several renowned software and information-services brands, I have strong opinions on what constitutes good (and bad) business-to-business (B2B) marketing. As a quota-carrying salesman, I learned to deeply understand the unmet needs of customers; and having directly managed teams of over 50 salespeople, I know that elegant marketing plans and pretty PowerPoints are worthless unless they actually drive measurable business results.

General Management – over the past 15 years, I have managed full P&L's in both high-growth and turnaround environments. I understand revenue and cost pressures from the CEO perspective. This experience underpins my pragmatic and revenue-driven approach to product strategy and marketing.
