Dico du Toit's Summary

I am a qualified winemaker with experience in the production and general management environment. I have a keen interest in the wine industry, business development and general management including wine production as well as the management of teams and processes within a framework of systems and internationally accepted principals and practices to the benefit of all interested parties.

I started my professional career as an assistant winemaker but was soon recognised as someone with the potential to develop into a manager due to my calm, systematic and analytical approach to planning and organising of teams and processes. I also have the ability to get along with a wide variety of people, both colleagues and clients. The solving of challenges in the workplace comes very natural to me. I am comfortable within a computerised environment and the use of management software to make decisions to enhance efficiency and profitability. I can recognise and explore opportunities, strategise and negotiate around such opportunities.

I have gained valuable international experience through various job opportunities, This has also allowed me to establish an international network in the global wine industry.

Specialties

Problem solving, planning and organisational skills, leadership and motivation of teams, analysis of issues, "getting things done"
