Brenda Nixon, M.A.'s Summary

Author or contributing author, 34 books ♥ Writing non-fiction book and a blog about ex-Amish, BrendaNixonOnAmish.blogspot.com ♥ Substitute High School teacher

Cultural Literacy is my goal as I Write/Speak/Blog or answer Media questions about the Amish. I break Amish mythinformation when I speak about this misunderstood culture, with emphasis on the Swartzentruber Order.

My husband and I open our hearts and home to Amish runaways, helping in their transition to the "forbidden" English life. Our son-in-law grew up & left Swartzentruber Amish. We're English "parents" to two sons from Swartzentruber Amish - Mosie and Monroe. I'm blessed to be friends with many others who've gone BEYOND BUGGIES & BONNETS, and I've learned intimate details about the life they left.

More than 118K readers worldwide come to my BEYOND BUGGIES & BONNETS blog for rare, true stories and authentic facts.

So far, my contribution to the literary world includes my award-winning THE BIRTH TO FIVE BOOK, A SCRAPBOOK OF CHRISTMAS FIRSTS, and 32 other titles I've contributed to such as seven CHICKEN SOUP FOR THE SOUL books.

In 2014-2015, parents will enjoy spiritual guidance through a curriculum I was contracted to write for the Nazarene Publishing House.


Specialties: Swartzentruber Amish, Ex-Amish struggles, WRITER, SPEAKER, BLOGGER, MEDIA GUEST and resource on Amish Culture
