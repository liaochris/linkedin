Alison D. Gilbert's Summary

I am retired from social media marketing and graphic design consulting. I have become an avid blogger. I gather information and create blog posts. I compose online magazines using venues to share them.

My present intent is to provide relevant, educational and even entertaining information. I 'curate and create' in two areas of concentration: small biz marketing, formerly aka MARKETING BYTES, and creative activity in ART & DESIGN | FOOD & HEALING, aka ALISON*S ART. Both MARKETING BYTES and ALISON*S ART have active pages on LinkedIn, have weblogs and have an abundance of pages on facebook as well as many other social media venues.

I come from a family of business owners and entrepreneurs. I have many interests and have survived for over 30 years exploring passions and curiosities that became business enterprises.

Specialties: artist and craft artisan, brand management, brochure design, budgeting, business development, charts, community relations, consulting, corporate identity, customer relations, education, grant writing, local marketing, marketing and advertising, media buying, media placement, Microsoft office suite, newsletters, organizational skills, painting, presentation skills, press releases, profit, promotional materials, radio, social media marketing graphic design,spreadsheets, teaching, media appearance, web site production, copy writing,
