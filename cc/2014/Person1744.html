Volunteer Experience & Causes

Founder & Director

Meet The Trees Foundation
October 2013Education

Imagine a foundation whose purpose it is to operate or fund programs responsible for getting kids in touch with nature. Dare we also add that perhaps many of these kids don't have easy access to natural habitats. Possibly not even a yard. And the park? Well maybe that has challenges as well.

Meet the Trees Foundation is that organization. One conceived by Toys R Us as a stunt. But we've breathed life into it. And we're serious. The Meet the Trees Foundation website offers two stories. One story is the irresponsible corporate behavior of Toys R Us. It has to be told if only as a case study for marketers about what not to do. The other story is the happier story about steps that can be taken to introduce more kids to nature. We'd prefer to dwell on the happier story.
