Oz Benamram's Summary

White & Case Chief Knowledge Officer (CKO) and global leader in legal KM, recognized for innovative, strategic thinking combined with practical implementation skills to achieve business objectives.

Experienced in managing multiple projects and motivating KM and IT teams in the introduction of new applications, revision of processes, and consensus building for successful cultural change.

White & Case is a global law firm with longstanding offices in the markets that matter today. An overview of professional experience is available at www.whitecase.com/obenamram.
