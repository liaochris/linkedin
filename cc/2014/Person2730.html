David Temple's Summary

David Temple is a search, social and digital marketing strategist with 15+ years of experience starting with website development and SEO with US Internet. His experience includes content development and marketing, search engine marketing (SEO/PPC) email, mobile, video and social media marketing.

David has increased the fortunes of a wide variety of companies from small and medium businesses to global brands such as Adobe, American Express, British Airways, Cisco, Danone, Dell, DHL, IBM, Logitech, Motorola, OgilvyOne Worldwide, PayPal, P&G, Samsung, SAP, Unilever, Yahoo! and others.
