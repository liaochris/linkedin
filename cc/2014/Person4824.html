Ron Fleming's Summary

I am a business development specialist who has owned a portfolio of several successful businesses. I also have a deep and passionate interest in supporting the small business community and helping other entrepreneurs to become more successful. The conventional wisdom says that business ownership isn't for everyone. After all, in our current system, EVERYONE can't be an owner and nobody be an employee, right?

Well I actually believe that business ownership isn't out of reach for anyone, but one must be READY to step into that responsibility, just as one must be ready for that first job, or to learn how to drive, or to enter into marriage. It takes a certain mindset, a certain skill set, and a certain level of resources available. And while it is true that some people may never in their lifetimes put themselves in a position to be ready, I don't believe that's because they were predestined NOT to. It's the choices we make in life every day that better prepare us for tomorrow, or leave us ill prepared. And by educating and informing those who want more from life than they've currently achieved, I can help them make better choices that will ultimately open to them new options that were previously unavailable.

With that in mind, I am on a mission to help would-be entrepreneurs acquire the mindset, skill set, and even the resources necessary to succeed at business ownership. And I have built a large and powerful network of others who not only share my vision, but also have more experience and sharper skills than I do in their respective areas of expertise. They span many industries and hail from many backgrounds, but have all achieved significant success in their chosen endeavors. My mission is one of empowerment, designed to take away excuses and instead create opportunity.
