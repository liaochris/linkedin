Joe Zlomek's Summary

Course author and PA-licensed instructor for real estate since 2001. Marketer since 1989. Journalist, photographer and videographer since 1971, with international awards and recognitions.

Specialties: Real estate sales and marketing education. As a journalist, specializes in reporting, photographing and editing news about Philadelphia and its suburbs, real estate issues, technology-based industries, business marketing, and travel.

Honors and Awards:

During the first half of 2014, Zlomek taught the popular Polley Associates' course, "Catching Up With The Web-Driven Real Estate Consumer, " to several hundred real estate sales and broker licensees at its Southampton and Blue Bell PA classrooms. He has been a Pennsylvania-licensed instructor of real estate since December 2001.

Zlomek in April 2014 was named as a senior contributor to TripAdvisor.com, one of the world's most-visited travel information websites. During March 2013, TripAdvisor reported he was among the top 1 percent of its most-read reviewers of destinations and facilities.

Zlomek and The Post Publications LLC were the subjects of a March 2, 2012, article in Columbia Journalism Review's "Guide To Online News Start-Ups."
