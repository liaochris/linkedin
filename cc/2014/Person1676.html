Ryan Ricketts' Summary

Ryan Ricketts is a digital marketing specialist at Whirlpool Corporation in southwest Michigan. He is the lead search engine optimizer (SEO) for all North American appliance brands such as Whirlpool, Maytag, KitchenAid, Jenn-Air, Amana, and Gladiator GarageWorks.

Ryan is an avid hiker, runner, biker, and traveler who also volunteers as a youth leader and technical producer at First Church of God in Saint Joseph, Michigan.
