Biography

Teppo is co-founder and Principal Designer at Nordkapp. He is an engineer by body and designer by soul.

Teppo has been building internet, multimedia and mobile services since 1997, first more on the technical side, then later in design. He's done everything from engineering a startup e-commerce product to various early days digimedia agency work; from backend solutions to rich & interactive social sites before there was social media. Everything from mobile power plant maintenance system to DJ software, and anything from Video-on-Demand to healthcare and banking.

Teppo designs with empathy for the users, always aiming for the simplest possible solution (but not simpler). He questions and solves business & technical obstacles that hinder the user experience. Teppo's technical background helps him work with agile engineers on the end product — a skill which is essential for producing high-end results.

Recent platforms Teppo has designed for include iPhone, iPad, Android tablet, Android, Windows Phone 7+, Windows 7 and Windows 8, and of course multitude of web services.

He has graduated as a BA of Media Engineering and MA in New Media (TAIK Medialab), both with high grades. He also studied abroad in the Shanghai University of Technology (China) and in Swinburne / The National School of Design in Melbourne (Australia).

— Winner of Emma (Finnish Grammy) 2007 for work in advancing digital music.
— Innovation patent pending.
— Winner of digital / social marketing award Grand One (2005).
— Some other awards & honorary mentions like Vuoden Huiput.
— Evangelist and researcher on Urbanflow Helsinki, a concept featured on Wired, Archdaily and shortlisted at IxDA Awards 2012.  http://helsinki.urbanflow.io/

Specialties: User interfaces for complex data driven applications, their user experience and interaction design.

Teppo's hobbies include photography, composing music and DJing.
