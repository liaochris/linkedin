Agustin Gau's Summary

Agustín Gau started his professional career in 1999 when founding Mediatica, a company dedicated to developing Interactive CDs.

In 2001 due to the Internet growth, he focused himself on this new industry, initiating AdVerit, a Digital Marketing Agency with more than 8 years in the Argentine Market.
Nowadays, AdVerit hires 30 multidisciplinary professionals who develop projects for more than 10 countries worldwide.

Its portfolio includes top-brand companies in different industries: Accenture, Accor, Bayer, FujiFilm, Gatorade, General Electric, Gillette, Grupo Diario Clarin, Grupo Diario La Nación, Procter & Gamble, Readers Digest, Roche, Royal Canin, Sheraton Hotel & Resorts, Unilever, Umbro, among others.

Agustin studied Business Administration in the University of Buenos Aires where he has got his degree in 2004.

Since then he actively participates in Internet & New Media local Bureaus & Associations so as to promote the industry at a regional level.

In 2009 he also started his academic career as Internet Business professor for CAECE University.

In 2010 he founded SOICOS, the first affiliate marketing network in Argentina, and actually the most important of Latin America with offices in Chile, Mexico & Colombia.

Specialties

internet marketing, email marketing, SEM, SEO, web development, social network marketing, mobile marketing, affiliate marketing.
