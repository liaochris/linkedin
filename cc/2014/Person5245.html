Jim Hickman's Summary

I consider myself a modern day Renaissance man, minus the pantaloons. My passion is design, my talent is creation and my strength is intuition. I began a career in advertising at the bottom of the totem, right down there with the angry beaver. Everyday since I have worked to make my skills and talents more current, valuable and effective.

Over the years I have accumulated a wealth of knowledge, I have learned from mistakes and have rejoiced in victories. I have spent time creating for both print and interactive. I have studied, and worked in, B2C and B2B philosophies. I have strategized for global companies and start-ups alike. I have taken direction and given it. I have etched all things Adobe Suite into my DNA and even worked in Microsoft Office, though I am hesitant to say so in fear that I will become the PowerPoint Expert that everyone is tirelessly searching for.

Today you will find me managing client needs, navigating projects, throwing creative darts, sharing insight and talking people into (or out of) something. I love what I do because it allows me to utilize my strengths and talents on a daily basis.
