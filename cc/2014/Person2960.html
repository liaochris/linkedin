Eric Mitsch's Summary

Education coach and instructor with over 5 years of experience working in the Clark County School District. Representative of CCSD's Student Services Division (SSD), supporting new and veteran special education teachers. Experienced public speaker and trainer, currently recognized as an IEP Process Overview trainer for district schools and an invited speaker at CCSD’s annual Early Childhood Conference. Outsource member of a state Child Find team which assists parents in the early identification of children with disabilities through free screening measures to determine initial eligibility for special education and related services.

Alumni and advocate of the non-profit, Teach For America organization and an elected Governing Board Member of the Nevada Association for the Education of Young Children (Nevada AEYC). Currently hold a Master's in Special Education with expected graduation with second Master's in Education Leadership & Administration in Spring 2015.

Experienced in project management, market research, sales pitching, and new venture development. Participated in four collegiate level business competitions, including:

- UCSB’s TMP New Venture Competition (2009), AcceloMed Technologies
- USC’s New Innovators Showcase (2009), AcceloMed Technologies
- Nevada Center for Entrepreneurship and Technology (NCET) Donald W. Reynold's Governer's Cup (2011), TheVegasList.com
- Crowdfunder’s Vegas Tech Fund Competition (2012), Chexplore.com

In 2009, AcceloMed Technologies earned "Best Business Plan" honors with a $5,000 prize at the New Venture Competition in conjunction with UCSB Engineering's Technology Management Program. In 2011, TheVegasList represented UNLV as state finalist & $1000 Prize Winner for TheVegasList.com at the Nevada Center for Entrepreneurship and Technology (NCET) Donald W. Reynold's Governer's Cup in Reno, Nevada. In 2013, Chexplore earned top 10 finalist honors at Crowdfunder’s $500,000 business plan competition in Las Vegas, Nevada.
