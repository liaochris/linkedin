Carlos Scarpero's Summary

I am an internet marketing consultant who specializes in LinkedIn training, email marketing training and consulting through Constant Contact, search engine optimization (SEO) and web design.

I'm a trainer and facilitator of a LinkedIn workshop called Mr. Leads Power Networking that shows business owners how to use LinkedIn effectively. I also created an online version of this training called Mr. Leads LinkedIn Bootcamp.

I'm also the Dayton region's exclusive Authorized Local Expert and Solution Provider for Constant Contact. In this role, I facilitate workshops where I teach people how to use email marketing and social media effectively to maximize ROI. Additionally, I manage Constant Contact accounts for people that don't have the time or motivation to do it themselves.

I'm good at what I do and passionate about helping Dayton area business owners reach their maximum potential by utilizing internet marketing. I built my first website in 1997, about a month after I got online.

In 1999, I got my first professional website gig and have been doing online marketing ever since. My areas of expertise are SEO, Google Places, email marketing with Constant Contact and Aweber, simple WordPress based websites that get results, simple mobile sites and LinkedIn training and consulting.

Be sure to visit my website at www.Mr-Leads.com to read my blog and get my newsletter.

Specialties: LinkedIn training, LinkedIn trainer, LinkedIn consulting, internet marketing, SEO, search engine optimization, Google Places, WordPress, web design, email marketing, Constant Contact, mobile web design, social media
