Laura L. Weller's Summary

Starting out in journalism - newspapers, trade journals and magazines - was a hump to climb out of but now I specialize in fiction. And yes, I also worked for a year on NPR radio. My deepest grooves are cut in Fortune 100 companies as a sales and marketing manager. I connect well with people, a great asset in crafting characters and when I'm done with a book I wantb to get it out there, teach writing classes, talk about it, sell copies.

Specialties: Writing YA Fiction, writer-networking, marketing strategies, and social media.
