Aaron Street's Summary

Aaron Street works as a Program Attorney for the Minnesota State Bar Association Continuing Legal Education (Minnesota CLE), where he helps develop continuing education programs and manages Minnesota CLE's New Lawyer Initiative.

Aaron is also an entrepreneur and advises Minnesota technology start-ups.

He is the co-founder and Publisher of Lawyerist Media, the leading digital media company serving solo and small law firms.

He is a multi-talented business and organizational leader with broad experience leading high-profile public and private projects in Minnesota. He has spent the past five years as an executive leader in operations and management of complex organizations, including university research institutes and technology and services startups.

He also brings over fifteen years of leadership in state and national politics and public policy development.
