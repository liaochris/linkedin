Cricket Bo's Summary

At The Cricket Contrast, we’re experts in helping companies of all sizes get maximum results from their marketing efforts, whether in print, on the air, through the mail or over the internet. One of our distinctive qualities is that we can create a full marketing plan for your company and make recommendations on how to stretch your marketing budget.

Our services comprise branding, marketing planning and marketing execution including graphic design, corporate identity and brand management, marketing collateral, web site design and development and online marketing solutions including social media strategies and implementation.

We are particularly proficient in: strategic planning and execution, marketing and consulting, creative direction and design, project management and organizational skills.

Unlike many firms in our field, we are happy to work with our clients side-by-side on an as needed basis, leading and/or cooperating with staff to achieve the best possible results. To partner with companies that are ready to grow and to support their growth plans with results driven strategic planning, marketing and design is the most exciting part of our work.

I enjoy working with companies that know an expert can help them succeed with their goals.

Specialties: technology, healthcare, architecture, aviation, construction, development, education, government, high tech, interior design, manufacturing, medical/health care, non-profit/foundations, pharmaceutical, publishing, real estate, senior living, small businesses
