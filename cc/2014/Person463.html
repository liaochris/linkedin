Background

Summary

Social media and digital media strategic marketing professional with more than 30 years international experience in the digital media, communications, consumer electronics, motion picture and video entertainment industries. Entrepreneur and a member of senior management at multiple digital media and technology startups. Former award winning newspaper, magazine and newsletter journalist.

Specialties: Social media strategy, content marketing, communications, strategic alliances & partnerships, media and technology licensing and distribution agreements, corporate development, company management, technology assessment & market research.
