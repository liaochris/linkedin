Fran Melmed's Summary

Founder of context, an award-winning communication and change management consulting firm that specializes in workplace wellness. Creator of Hotseat (gethotseatapp.com), an app and web-based tool to decrease the risk of "sitting disease" and increase physical activity through short breaks and social game play.
