Greg Stanhewicz's Summary

Pine Lake Solutions is a premier Executive Search Firm that specializes in the placement of mid to C-level Sales, Business Development, Account Management and Marketing professionals. We have emerged as the leading Search Firm within the Digital Marketing ecosystem. We focus on high-growth areas such as mobile, social and video advertising, and fast emerging ad technology platforms such as programmatic buying, RTB, native ad exchanges and enterprise data management. Pine Lake has developed and grown a vast network in all major metropolitan areas, and have built a business by establishing credibility along with a true value proposition by working with only the best companies and elite candidates
.
By becoming specialized market experts, our team consistently identifies and delivers top performing talent. Pine Lake Solutions is an extremely valuable and powerful resource for companies looking to attract prominent industry players to their teams.

Specialties: Display, Mobile, Social, Video, Programmatic Media Buying, Real-time bidding (RTB), Native Ad Exchanges, Demand Side Platforms (DSP), Supply Side Platforms (SSP) Data Management Platforms (DMP), Agency Trading Desks, Re-targeting, Ad Servers.
