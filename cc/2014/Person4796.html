Robin Cangie's Summary

I am an experienced digital marketer with a history of success in content strategy, demand generation, and integrated cross-channel campaigns at every stage of the marketing funnel. I'm well-versed in start-up and corporate environments.

In a fast and unpredictable world, I believe agile marketing is the best and perhaps the only way for companies to achieve lasting success with their marketing efforts. In practice, my approach to agile marketing involves responsible risk taking, constant measurement and iteration, and celebrating failure as a way to learn.

I am also a committed content marketer. I believe the future of marketing belongs to those who are able to provide genuine, lasting value to their customers, not through sales pitches but through the continuous development and distribution of high-quality content that is helpful, meaningful, and relevant to the needs and interests of the customer, backed up by an exceptional product and user experience.

Specialties: Agile marketing, demand generation, content marketing, marketing automation, lead nurturing, copywriting, social media marketing, blogging
