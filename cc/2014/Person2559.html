Clement Charles' Summary

Based in Geneva, Switzerland, ATC Future Medias SA delivers to the market a unique combination of media knowledge, productions skills, IT capacities and innovative visions.

Founded in 2001, the company has been an acknowledged pioneer in cross platform content licensing, online editorial workflows, vertical communications, community contents and complete cultural localization of news.


AllTheContent (and local brands ToutLeContenu.com, TodoElContenido.com, ++)

AllTheContent provides the right content enabling medias and corporations to attract and retain traffic in all verticals, platforms and languages since 2001. Both news agency with 1800+ freelancers (journalists, translators, producers) and aggregator of selected third parties like Reuters, AllTheContent's combines of the best of media and technology to deliver value to websites, publishers, broadcasters, telcos, IPTV/OTT platforms, airline, digital signage services and all new types of screens. Clients get content via API or on our online shop.
You know that content is king. Welcome to the kingdom.


Contenu & Cie
Contenu & Cie provides content services (corporate magazines, corporate web TV and films) and community management to brands and corporations. We also offer translation from and to a large array of languages, with proven experiences in more than 35 different languages.

Commu’Comm
Focused exclusively on public institutions such as cities or governments, Commu’Comm is a dedicated partner to institutional communication. In the Swiss market, the company creates and operate local television channels (ex: Lancy TV), city magazines, media archives funds and multimedia touristic guides.

ATC Properties
Publishing websites, mobile apps, OTT services, YouTube channels and IPTV channels, ATC Properties operates a portfolio of 20 brands with various regional versions in the local languages, with a unique access to great content, interactive publishing technologies and community management services.

Specialties

- cross plateform content services (mobile, web, print, tv, signage, ++)
- editorial / localized content in multiple local languages
- content licensing and acquisition
- tv format creation and implementation
- production process optimisation in traditional media companies
