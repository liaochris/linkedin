Kyle Bunch's Summary

Kyle Bunch is an award-winning director with R/GA's Mobile & Social Platforms group, where he works with global clients including Nike, Samsung, Beats by Dre, Google, Converse, Fossil, L'Oréal Paris, Coca-Cola, Royal Caribbean and MasterCard to develop products, services and communications optimized for emerging platforms. He's also served as a visiting professor at the IE Business School in Madrid and the Miami Ad School.

Kyle is also an entrepreneur who, through his work at Bunch Enterprises, has built several businesses, including the first influence measurement application (Blogebrity), the largest sport-focused digital & social media events series (Blogs with Balls) and a robust search analytics suite (SEOintelligence, which he sold in 2010).
