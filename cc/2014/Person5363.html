Val Brown's Summary

Val Brown works with CEOs, C-Level Executives, entrepreneurs and business owners to drive brand growth and innovation. Val helps new and existing businesses develop strategic growth plans; devises solutions for stagnant sales, increased competition, revenue or market share loss; revives the fortunes of heritage brands and creates new avenues of growth for healthy ones; and develops brand solutions for weak or negative brand awareness. She employs creative vision and strategic acumen to create a new story for your brand and a transformation of your business that leads to sustainable growth.

Working in the U.S. and international arenas with entertainment, hospitality and lifestyle brands, Val has conceptualized and launched businesses, created global brand awareness and innovative business solutions.

Before starting her own consultancy, Val spearheaded global development of one of the world’s best known leisure and entertainment brands as VP of international marketing and brand management at Hard Rock Café; as CEO, planned the moved of Liverpool's famed Cavern Club into traditional and digital media and global markets; and as President, created a content division for successful US live entertainment business Rock 'n Roll Fantasy Camp. In the digital world, she created the brand launch strategy for Music.com.
