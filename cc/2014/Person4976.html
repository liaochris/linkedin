Seán Bulger's Experience

Community Manager

Electronic Arts

Public Company; 5001-10,000 employees; EA; Entertainment industry

October 2013 – Present (1 year) Austin, TX

I operate as a community manager for games in EA's Mobile division. So far, I have worked on four different titles: MONOPOLY Bingo, SCRABBLE Digital, Tetris Blitz and Heroes of Dragon Age. I have managed up to three games simultaneously.

Much of my work focuses on the retention and acquisition of community members, including on our social channels (and I do manage two Facebook pages of nearly one million Likes each). I develop our content calendars, draft posts, and hold direct, two-way communication with members of the community. I perform in-depth analysis on community and social metrics to determine the effectiveness of our outreach, community sentiment, top issues from players, and more.

I maintain a close relationship with third-party license owners, such as Hasbro, Mattel and Blue Planet Planet Software, to ensure that their IPs are being well respected and represented.

I also work very closely with our other internal departments:

- QA, to ensure that issues identified by the community are reported and tested

- Customer Service, to ensure that players are getting the help they need, both from the CSRs and from me. We are also able to keep each other informed about common issues, and how they can be resolved.

- Marketing, to better maintain consistent messaging across all platforms, and to assist in the development and execution of promotions, announcements, and other events.

- Development, to keep them informed on player feedback, be it praise, critiques, or requests, to help them better plan future updates.

Social Media Manager

Qualcomm

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; QCOM; Wireless industry

September 2011 – October 2013 (2 years 2 months) Austin, Texas

After Qualcomm's acquisition of Bigfoot Networks, I transitioned my responsibilities to their Atheros division, while also expanding my role.

I simultaneously managed the social media accounts and communities for Qualcomm Atheros, Killer Gaming and Skifta, with a focus on the acquisition and retention of high-value followers. As primarily a B2B business, we targeted members of the media and executives of major partner/customers, along with highly influential technology enthusiasts. I also collaborated closely with partners, including Cisco, Nokia, Wi-Fi Alliance, HomePlug Alliance and others, on cross-promotions and events.

To measure the success of our social promotions, I performed in-depth analysis of our analyzed and tracked metrics on the health and growth of all our social media channels.

I was the managing editor and contributor for the Qualcomm Atheros blog, The Wire. I planned the editorial calendar, interviewed engineers, project managers and executives on cutting edge new technologies to create content.

I have attend and covered major industry events, including E3 and CES.

- Major game companies (CCP, SOE, Trion, ArenaNet, Turbine, EA, etc...)

- Prominent hardware manufacturers and OEMs (SteelSeries, Alienware, GIGABYTE, ASUS, etc...)

- Our sponsored eSports organizations (SK Gaming, Team Dignitas, Evil Geniuses, Fnatic, CLG, Team Solomid, etc...)
