Laurent Gauthier's Summary

I am a Toolsmith at heart.

Professionally there is nothing I enjoy more than automating boring, error prone or tedious software engineering tasks. I am strongly convinced that processes and tools should work for people, and not the reverse.

I am currently doing software optimization and general TI DSP support for customers working on basestation software for WCDMA and LTE standards.

In the past I have worked on the test and validation of 3GPP baseband software. Test automation of large test-suites for embedded DSP software.

I have also been involved in the release management activities and customer interactions over the years.

Expert in SCM (Software Configuration Management) with advanced knowledge of Rational ClearCase, ClearQuest, MultiSite, as well as a significant experience with Git. Many years (>15) working in geographically distributed projects, multi-cultural environments.

Significant experience in scripting of all kinds: Perl, Python, shell, XSLT, PHP, you name it...

Significant experience in software build tools, and the process of automating builds.

Professional programming experience in C, C++, and Java.
