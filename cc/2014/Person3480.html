Jess Hartley's Summary

I am a published novelist and writer/editor/developer in and out of the gaming industry. My magazine articles have been published in technical, educational and historical publications, and I've written or contributed do more than four dozen fiction and non-fiction works to date.

More specific information can be found at my website: www.jesshartley.com

Specialties: Fiction, from short story/flash fiction to novel-length. Game mechanics and settings. Editing.

Writer

White Wolf Games

January 2003 – Present (11 years 9 months)

I began writing for White Wolf in 2003, when I wrote the Exalted novel, “In Northern Twilight”, which was a Runner-Up for Pen & Paper’s 2004 Fan Awards in the category of Best RPG-Related Novel/Anthology.

Since then, I have written content for most of their new World of Darkness game lines, including the WoD line, Werewolf: The Forsaken, Mage: The Awakening, Promethean: The Created and the upcoming Changeling: The Lost.

I have also contributed to their Exalted 2nd Edition line, being one of the authors for “The Compass of the Celestial Directions 1: The Blessed Isle”.

Most recently, I've been working on the non-WoD White Wolf game line, Scion.
