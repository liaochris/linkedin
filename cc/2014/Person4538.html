Patrick Valtin's Summary

Mr. Valtin is the president/CEO of New Era Management, International (an M2-TEC USA, Inc. company) and the author of the book "No Fail Hiring" (www.nofailhiring.com). He has an extensive experience in the fields of Management consulting & training, and this on an international basis. His specialties as a consultant extend from recruitment to sales and sales management, leadership, organization and corporate strategy.

His professional experience with US and European corporations plus his early background as a salesman (started at the age of 15) gave him a strong taste for down-to-earth management principles which can be applied by pragmatic business owners around the world.

Mr Valtin has traveled in more than 30 countries over the last 24 years and has trained more than 100,000 people in the areas of sales, marketing, Human Resources Development, organization and leadership.

Patrick Valtin was an International Business consultant for the Belgian Government between 1986 and 1988. His customers and seminar attendees have come from all industries, from growing businesses to multi-national corporations (USPS, Ford Motors, GM Motors, BMW, Renault, Mercedes, Toyota, Motorola, AIG, IBM, Remax, Century 21, ATT Canon, France Telecom, Hotels Accor, etc...).

An MBA from USC in 1982 (Master of international Business Studies), he also graduated from the "Hubbard® Organization Executive Course" in 1992, a specialized course for consulting the business owners and executives of small-to-medium sized companies. The Hubbard Management® Technology is used by hundreds of thousands of companies and has received worldwide recognition as a unique, practical management system for those who thrive to succeed.

Specialties:

- RPO (Recruitment Process Outsourcing) - visit www.nofailhiring.com
- sales and sales management training.
