  • How To Get People To Buy From YOU First - Social Media Sales Strategies That Create Millions

    • Jon Rognerud Media/Amazon
    • March 26, 2012
    Authors: Jon Rognerud

    Many books exist on social media, online marketing and use of social networks. But, most don't get into the sales and buyer mindset of the top online communities. For example, Facebook may be on pace to a billion accounts world wide, but the environment itself is not built for "selling". It still represents huge opportunities, and millions are sold and transacted in this environment. You must learn to leverage this, and the right ways to do it. Once you learn it in one environment, you can join other social networks, and continue to generate and close sales within a simple and effective framework.

    This book may have been called "The Social Media Sales Guide for Finding New Clients and Closing them". The book focuses on the social dynamics of the Internet in current time, how to get started using social media and how to build authority and trust. These are two important pillars to success in better lead flow and higher sale closes from the social media communities.

    It also includes case studies from the finance / banking world, and shows you how businesses in most markets can use the success stories and principles found in this book.

    Inside, you'll see how to build large amounts of traffic focused around quality and particular segments within your market, and how to do proper research to win.

    Finally, key aspects of search, links and content are broken down into readable chunks that you can take action on immediately.

    Sales & Marketing Executives, Business Owners, Entrepreneurs, Coaches, Consultants and Social Media Marketers whose aim it is to get and close more business will benefit from this comprehensive, eye-opening read.
