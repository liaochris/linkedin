Lisa Wires' Summary

I am a "Successful Professional Retail Mortgage Recruiter" with several years in the mortgage industry. My entire tenure as a recruiter spans over ten years with "Full-Cycle" recruiting expertise in various industries including; biotech, aerospace, I.T., healthcare, and wholesale mortgage where I was employed by a TOP 10 wholesale mortgage banker.

At my last position in wholesale banking I was an HR and ASM liaison supporting seven branches within the Southern California Region. Previously, I was also an account executive, and maintained various professional business contacts. Headhunting nationally to provide various clients with "Top Talent" within their industries. Working with "Top Talent" executives in providing relocation, nationally.

My network thrives on successful connections and reviews. If you are working with me currently or in the past, please feel free to write a recommendation for me here on LinkedIn.com. It would be greatly appreciated.
