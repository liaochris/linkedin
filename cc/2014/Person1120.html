Gary E Green's Summary

Currently I am Indiana Wesleyan University's AVP for Information Technology and CIO. I am responsible for IWU's technology services at its main campus, sixteen education centers, and Online programs. This includes IT Infrastructure services, End-user Support services, Media & Classroom Technology services, Data Center services, and Application Development and Integration services. In this role, I provide vision and leadership for the planning, implementation and management of communication and information technologies that support learning, teaching and administration as well as direct and manage Information Technology strategic plans, roadmaps, policies and programs. In addition, I work with key suppliers and leadership personnel to ensure the University is maximizing the value of its technology investments. I am also an adjunct professor for IWU's College of Adult and Professional Studies where I teach Business and Computer courses.

Previous to IWU, I was a Mobility Architect/Consultant at EDS/HP working with clients on their enterprise mobile strategies, roadmaps, and solutions. This involved collaborating with leadership teams, software and hardware vendors as well as delivery groups in a global environment. Activities include such initiatives as sale’s due diligence activities, proposal developing, architecting, conducting Proof-of-Concepts, and establishing mobility support services. In addition, I have played a key role in the development of EDS/HP's mobility workplace services portfolio offering. With my experience working with clients on their enterprise mobility strategy, I provide depth and breadth expertise in business development.
