Tony Baldwin's Summary

A versatile public relations professional, social media manager and digital marketer with a proven ability for pitching stories and executing campaigns.

Developed a strong track record of leading social media and digital marketing campaign strategies for several higher education athletics departments, including the University of Texas at San Antonio (UTSA), Concordia University Texas and University of the Ozarks.

Professional experience over the last 11 years has included works in many different aspects in public relations and social media marketing, including graphic and publication design, press release and feature writing, digital photography and website management.

Specialties: social media manager, digital marketing, public relations, website content management, press release writing, media pitching, graphic design, desktop publishing, online listening.
