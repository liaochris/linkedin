Roger Madsen's Summary

I'm a passionate and dedicated senior software architect/developer. Quality and end user experience is of highest importance to me when I develop software. I enjoy the visual aspects of creating software applications as well as what goes on behind it. I put high value on architecture design and writing good code but even more important is getting things done.

I have been working full time with Android development since it was released (5+ years full time Android experience). I have very good knowledge about every aspect of Android application development and Android architecture.

I have 10+ years experience in telecom.

* Software consultant at Ericsson working on a billing gateway for mobile networks.
* Software consultant / staff engineer at Sony Ericsson working on the telecom software inSony Ericsson phones.
* 5 years as lead architect for the telecom software in Android on Sony Ericsson / Sony Mobile phones.
* 3 years as expat in Beijing, China as a lead architect for telecom on Android.

I switched track recently and I'm now building my competence to develop cloud software for Sony products.

I have created Train Info Sweden. It's one of the most popular Android app for train commuters in Sweden. It is currently rated 4.3 in Google Play with 61 votes. https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.claycuckoo.tis
