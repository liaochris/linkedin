Bill Ruddy's Summary

After graduation I honorably served 4 years in the United States Navy where I learned management skills, discipline when under stress and had an opportunity to travel the world via the Pacific Ocean. Upon returning home I apprenticed for a large commercial flooring installation firm prior to becoming a self employed flooring installer. After several years I did installation work for Holiday Inn Miami Springs and was offered a position renovating rooms. Within a year I was advanced to the Chief Engineer Position and through hard work and training I was advanced to Rooms Manager overseeing every aspect of Hotel Maintenance and Housekeeping. This lead to an offer to become a Project Manager for a hotel firm in Daytona Beach conducting renovations. After relocating my family and finding out this was not for me I returned to my roots and starting installing floorcovering for David Stark and his Carpet One stores. After a strong professional relationship David Stark and Jeff Kidd offered me the opportunity to open and manage their ProSource Wholesale Floorcovering store. After ten years managing the business I felt it was time for a change. Along came a great opportunity within a growing industry. I was hired as the Sales Manager for Solar-Fit, a locally owned, family run business. Developing the sales staff and helping streamline training as well as building our presence through networking was my goal. In January 2014, I decided to embark on a new path and opened Inspired Installations, Inc in order to offer a top quality resource for the re-emerging construction and remodeling sector. My goal is to pick up where the traditional installation companies may leave off and join some of the elite installers to service in every capacity of floorcovering. I can't wait to see where it takes me.
