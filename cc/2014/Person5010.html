Director of Operations

Music Health Alliance

Nonprofit; 1-10 employees; Entertainment industry

October 2013 – Present (1 year) Nashville, TN

Music Health Alliance is Music’s Resource for Health Care. Our confidential, safe and non-profit services PROTECT, DIRECT and CONNECT music professionals with health care across America to remove barriers and find solutions. MHA protects the music industry by providing access to affordable and appropriate health insurance. MHA directs music people through the health care system as their personal advocate, educator and navigator. MHA connects music makers with healthcare resources from financial aid to medicine, doctors, clinics and hospitals. Music Health Alliance walks with and fights for the music community to Get Well and Stay Well!
