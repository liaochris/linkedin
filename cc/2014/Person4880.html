Ryan Reichert's Summary

I am a marketing manager with an International MBA focused on strategic global marketing. My sweet spot is that place where data, storytelling and the consumer all work together. This is where value in marketing is created. And ROI is crucial.

Balancing qualitative and quantitative, I can help tell your story to any audience. My goal is to understand what drives your business and help you meet short-term needs while supporting a sustainable, long-term strategy.

I love my work. But outside of that I also enjoy vegan cooking, learning about wine, traveling and learning languages.
