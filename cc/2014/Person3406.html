Background

Summary

With over 23 years of experience in residential Architectural design and construction Bryant is considered an expert in the field. His firm has been honored with awards and recognition in magazines and in the state legislature. Bryant has served his community by participating as a board member and then chairman of the Georgetown Main Street Board, the Downtown Design Guidelines Committee, and the Downtown Tax Increment Finance District which helps fund the redevelopment of the downtown Georgetown area.

Specialties: Residential Design, Remodeling, New Construction, Consulting

Education

Texas Tech University

BARCH, Architectural Design
1985 – 1991

Bryant received a Bachelor of Architecture degree in 1991. He was a Cum Laude Graduate with a 3.8 GPA and was honored as such during the graduation ceremony. Bryant was also honored by the professors and staff of the College of Architecture as an outstanding returning student and represented the College of Architecture at the honor ceremony.
