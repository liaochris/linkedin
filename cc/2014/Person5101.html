Stephanie Hedquist's Summary

Stephanie Hedquist is a Senior Associate with PwC’s Health Industries Advisory People & Change Practice. She has over five years of health care, health IT and public health consulting and research experience. She has successfully collaborated with a wide range of organizations in the commercial, public and nonprofit sectors. Most recently, she has focused on designing and implementing transformational change initiatives in major health systems.

Prior to joining PwC, Stephanie was a Principal Research Analyst for NORC at the University of Chicago focusing on state health information exchanges, health IT privacy and security initiatives, community college and university health IT education programs, assistive services technology for persons with disabilities and seniors, and initiatives expanding health care access to uninsured populations. Earlier in her career, she provided consulting services for health-related non-profit organizations focusing on organizational development, policy advocacy and leadership coaching. She also served as a Campaign Organizing Fellow for Barack Obama’s 2008 Presidential Election Campaign, and an AmeriCorps National Civilian Community Corps (NCCC) member in the Gulf Coast.

Stephanie has earned an MA in Public Policy with a focus in health care from Northwestern University, and a BA in International Affairs with a concentration in public health from George Washington University.
