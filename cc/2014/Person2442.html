Summary

Hard Working, Intelligent, Love to Learn, Read, Write, Think, and continue to better myself in many ways. I'm always seeking new opportunities.

Experience:

I have 9+ years of experience in Digital Marketing and Project Management and have a great mix of business and technological skills. I'm skilled in nearly all things web-related, including content marketing, PPC, SEO and SEM, E-Mail marketing, Web Content Management, Wordpress, Copywriting and many other Digital Marketing roles.

I'm also highly skilled in the Agile field with multiple years of experience in the rapidly evolving field of Scrum development. I currently hold the Scrum Alliance CSP certification (the highest before becoming a Scrum coach) and operate in a Scrum Master role.

I have an MBA in Business Administration from IU Southeast Graduate School of Business, and a BS in Information and Operations Management.

Currently, I work at Samtec as a Web Project Manager, where my role is quite dynamic from day to day, allowing me to work on many facets of the web, from management of software development and design, to front end performance optimization, to content marketing, to overall team development.

I also own a small website called Academy Success, which is a self-improvement website that teaches its readers how to improve their lives through productivity, fitness, personal finance, and their overall mindset.

Academy Success is fun and challenging for me, keeps me sharp, and allows me full creative freedom over its content.

Additionally, I create websites for small businesses around the Louisville area as needed. It's not something I market too heavily, but it's another way for me to keep my skills sharp and help those in need of the service.

I believe my work history to this point in my life has prepared me for a very successful career in the Web space as a business leader. Moving forward that is where I see myself, however I am open to any opportunity that fits my education and experience.
