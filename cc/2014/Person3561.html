Joe Siecinski's Summary

Joe has helped hundreds of small businesses get more customers, make more money, and have more time. His book Brainshare: http://www.amazon.com/Brainshare-Business-Owners-Make-Money/dp/0615976948 gives some insight to how he works and helps his clients. His background includes responsibility for the P & L of a $250 million global business organization where he consistently drove his group to exceed sales and profit goals. He owned the relationships with over 1500 businesses and personally conducted over 2,000 face-to-face sales calls.
