Robyn Kaufman's Summary

I have built a solid reputation as a highly successful Realtor for more than 24 years. Utilizing years of experience with real estate developers and selling high rise luxury residences, I have garnered a knowledge base that consistently benefits my clients. With this knowledge, I provide my sellers and buyers with up to date information to help them explore every option and make sound real estate investment decisions. You can expect a relationship that is built on high integrity, premier service and a commitment to quality results.

Since 2003, I have specialized in the sale of luxury high-rise condominium residences in San Francisco with an emphasis on the Transbay neighborhoods including; Rincon Hill, SOMA, South Beach, Yerba Buena and the South Financial District. Moreover, it is in this dynamic part of the city that I have a proven record of success with over $100M in sales.

I am ranked within the Top 10 South Beach Agents for 2014-YTD.

I provided my clients with all their real estate needs including; selling, buying, financing and leasing.

As your Realtor you can expect:

*Expert knowledge of Rincon Hill,South Beach,SOMA,Yerba Buena & Mission Bay neighborhoods.
*Detailed and proven marketing expertise to sell your property for it's maximum potential.
*Effective negotiating.


In 2014 I have helped many of my clients achieve record breaking sales. These records were achieved in South Beach buildings that include; The Metropolitan, 81 Lansing and One Rincon Hill. This year I also helped my clients achieve record breaking sales in the neighborhoods of Mission Bay, Downtown and Hayes Valley.


My clients enjoy the benefits of my exclusive marketing and networking. I am a member of San Francisco's Top Agent Network that consists of only the city's top agents. I am affiliated with Pacific Union and Christie's International Real Estate.
