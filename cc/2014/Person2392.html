Steve Cadigan's Summary

With over 25 years of leadership and executive experience in the field of HR, the depth and breadth of industry experience in apparel, retail, insurance, semiconductor, networks, entertainment, and internet, has given me a deep understanding of business cycles, patterns, high growth, and what it takes to forge an organization’s sustainability. In addition, I’ve led WW HR efforts on over 60 M&A transactions in 3 large Global organizations since 1998.

Globally, I have practiced HR across Asia, Canada and the US, including over ten years of experience living and working overseas. Over the course of my career the teams and organizations I’ve led and helped build, have been recognized as exceptional performers and "world class" in every way.

I have built a personal and professional brand that is focused on winning creative talent solutions, and breaking down any barriers that suffocate, limit, or create impediments to high performance and the evolution of talent.

Similar to my love of sport, my overall professional objective is to do all that I can to make individuals, teams, and organizations the best they can be.

Specialties: Acquisition Integration, Turning Culture into a Strategic Advantage, Executive Coaching, Change Management, Leading and scaling in Hyper Growth, Leading organizations successfully through pre and post IPO transitions
