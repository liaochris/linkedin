Prof. S.Shiva Kumar's Summary

To channelize the professional experience gained from more than 38 years of Teaching, Training & as Scientist in the Government of India, to create & nurture an institution that will earn the status of a centre of excellence for academic & research activities,apart from providing consultancy & advocacy services in creating Built Environments that are responsive to the present day needs of sustainability. It is a challenging task to be in a position to make difference by grooming young professionals who can spearhead the age-old dictum of ' Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam'(From ancient Upanishads to mean earth as a family,similar to the ancient African concepts,like Ubuntu which means humanity to others). There can be no better place to achieve this objective than to be in an Academia for Built Environment study & research which identifies with the cause.

Specialties

Environmental Management of development projects, Urban Environment Management, Sustainable Architecture & Urbanism.

Institute for Housing & Urban Development Studies (IHS), Rotterdam

Diploma in Housing,Planning & Building, Housing & Urban Development

1978 – 1979

As a part of the study programme, undertook a visit to Tunisia to study the housing situation there, visits included Tunis,Sidi Bou Said & Carthage.Best part of the programme was that participants were from different developing countries in Asia, Africa & Latin America providing an opportunity to know & learn from each other's experiences. There were two very committed & involved tutors in Edgardo Martinez & Raul D'lullo with whom I developed an excellent rapport.Also made it a point to visit places of architectural interest in London, Rome, Paris & Athens.On the way back,visited Cairo & Dubai. Samy Mansour,my Egyptian friend took me to the great Pyramids & other important places around.Cities & people reveal all the history we hear & read about.

Activities and Societies: In the initial programme in 1978, conducted a study on the 'Evolutionary approach for housing the urban poor:A Design Model'. Attended the Research programme by re-invitation in 1979. A joint project entitled 'Planning Model for a squatter development:case study,Bangalore' was published as a Priced Research Bulletin by the Institute for Housing & Urban Development Studies (IHS), Rotterdam in 1982.

Secured First Rank & was awarded the University Gold Medal.Professors who mentored me were Prof.Rattan Kumar & Prof.Kuldip C Kambo. The course was renamed as Masters in Urban & Rural Planning (M.U.R.P) in the later years.Friendships with Prof.T.Oommen & Gaurav Murlidhar Rau I still cherish.
