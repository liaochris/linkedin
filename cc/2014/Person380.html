Background

Summary

As an eclectic designer, I constantly feed myself with everyday things, I seek to cause the "extra-ordinary", the wonder induced by a mixture of daily and magic among the users. I provide precision and care to the conceptualization and technical development of the project so that these crucial steps give way to the experience and sensations.

Objects that are working well, this is the minimum that a designer can do. But to sublimate this ambient functionalism it is important to know how to listen carefully and capture the essence of our time. We must be attentive to the users expectations. Be there to answer a physical, a practical and an emotional need.

Sometimes obvious, sometimes complex, my formal universe tends toward more pragmatism and adapts to the reality by preferring the practice of a simple design without being simplistic. I make great the symbol so it can call people to use the object, to make it live and "grow old".

For me design is an everyday quest, that's why my professional experiences allowed me to explore different design field such as industrial products, limited edition, high hand objects as much as mass market products, scenography and retail design.

"Design should not dominate things. Not dominate people. It should help people." Dieter Rams
