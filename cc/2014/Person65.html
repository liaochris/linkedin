October 2014 – Present (2 months)United States

For every 1 million members in a health plan, approximately $160 million in medical claims are incurred by members diagnosed with advanced substance dependence--10 times higher than non-substance dependent members. Most health plans have realistic cost savings opportunities in the millions of dollars--and Catasys can help deliver significant savings through our OnTrak program. Catasys helps health plans significantly reduce medical claims cost in high utilizing substance dependent members by applying a unique face to face and person to person intervention designed to give members the support they need to get healthy and stay healthy.

The Catasys OnTrak program addresses many of the challenges experts highlight within the existing treatment paradigm for substance dependence. Specifically, OnTrak:

» Seeks out and enrolls high-cost members
» Is a unified provider offsetting a fragmented delivery system
» Uses innovative treatment approaches built upon proven science

When high utilizing members are sober they have fewer hospital visits from acute injuries and fewer complications from comorbid conditions – significantly lowering a health plan’s overall health care costs.
