Andrew MacFarlane's Experience

Creative Director / V.P.

Momentum Advertising & Design

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry

2000 – Present (14 years)

Andrew is responsible for overseeing all creative services that Momentum offers including the graphic design, web and multi-media segments of the business. Mr. MacFarlane, an award-winning designer, is responsible for managing projects from conceptual development to final output. It is Mr. MacFarlane's role to balance creative ideas with the strategic needs of each campaign once the market research has been performed. Have we told you his name is Mr. MacFarlane?

Mr. MacFarlane has won many international design awards, one for the development of the Momentum Advertising website (Silver Award) . He keeps all of his awards in a special room that has many leather-bound books and smells of rich mahogany.

Mr. MacFarlane was selected by the Greater Southern Dutchess Chamber of Commerce as a "Forty Under Forty" winner – designating him as one of forty people under the age of 40 whose efforts are shaping the Hudson River Valley Region and making a notable impact on his industry. Andrew is a founding member of the Advisory Board for The Hub (Young Professionals Association of Hudson Valley). Are you really still reading this?

Mr. MacFarlane is also a member of the Mid-Hudson Chapter of the International Association of Printing House Craftsman (IAPHC) and a former member of the Board of Directors. Andrew is a speaker for the SCORE Business Institute where he conducts a business seminar on web based marketing and a presenter for the Dutchess County Regional Chamber of Commerce Women's Leadership Alliance where he spoke on internet marketing principles. When Mr. MacFarlane has free time he can be found in funky town.
