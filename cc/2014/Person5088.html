Frederic St-Arnaud's Summary

Frederic St-Arnaud (starno.net) is a visual effect Matte Painting Artist and an Art Director, focusing since 17 years on high-end photo-realistic digital Matte Painting (also known as Matte Art or Digimatte), set extension and concept art in the film industry.

Credits includes: Pacific Rim, The Lone Ranger, Indiana Jones and the Kingdom of the Crystal Skull, Silent Hill, Babel, Sin City, Sky Captain and the World of Tomorrow, Sharkboy and Lavagirl, Racing Stripes, Spy Kids 2 and 3, Once upon a time in Mexico, Nouvelle-France, The last sign, A different loyalty, etc.

Specialties: Matte painting / Set extentions / Concept Arts / Art Direction
