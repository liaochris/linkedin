Background

Summary

John Blackburn is the administrative principal of Blackburn Architects, P.C. and is responsible for overall firm management. His experience includes the management of a full range of project types and services, including programming, existing facility evaluation and master planning, new construction, adaptive reuse, and historic preservation.

John's 30 years of experience in equestrian design has garnered several awards and positions him as a leader in equine design as well as an equestrian expert and consultant. His equestrian experience ranges from polo barns to thoroughbred and training facilities to therapeutic riding centers. Throughout all of his designs, John focuses on the health and safety of the horse to design facilities that function for the owner and riders while protecting the horse from injury and disease. His award-winning work has been featured in Southern Living Magazine, Cowboys & Indians Magazine, and Town & Country.

John also writes a blog about equestrian design called Stable Minded.
