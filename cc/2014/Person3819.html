Keren Solomon's Summary

I help companies deeply understand their customers so that they can improve their current products and create new ones.

As a researcher, my work has impacted product design, product development, sales, customer service, operations, and marketing. I've worked for and with Fortune 500 companies, software/technology companies, consumer product companies, retailers, and entrepreneurs.

My strengths include top-notch research skills, curiosity, a global outlook and international experience, and very strong client management and leadership skills. I have a consistent record of success in taking on new challenges and in translating customer information into insights and business opportunities. I believe it's important to mentor, teach and learn from others, and to create a positive working environment. I've also sought out volunteer opportunities (for example, after September 11th, I provided pro bono marketing assistance to three small business owners whose businesses were adversely impacted).

Personally, I'm an avid traveler who speaks good Spanish and "survival" French. I like Latin music, improv and general exploring.

Specialties: Ethnography, ethnographic research, observational research, point-of-sale research, qualitative market research, moderating, in-depth and expert interviews, consumer insights, user experience, user research, workshops, teaching, mentoring, new product development, project management, client management, new business development, consumer software, consumer products, technology, global, emerging markets.
