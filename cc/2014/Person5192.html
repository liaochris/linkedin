Taariq Lewis' Summary

Currently, Taariq is the founder of DigitalTangible, an online precious assets and digital money platform founded in April 2014 with a team of SF based engineers and advised by professionals in hard asset finance. DigitalTangible is a bitcoin2.0 company working on linking the physical world to the Bitcoin Blockchain and the Ripple Ledger.

Taariq is the founder of Bitcoin Business, a research blog targeting merchants accepting bitcoin and "Bits of Coin" an android app for bitcoin news, insights, and applications for digital currencies.

Previously, Taariq, founded Orb, an expertise marketplace for alumni, Stanzr, a social Media chat platform, led initial Sales, Marketing and Channel development for HiveFire and Terametric.

Taariq has held positions at other companies such as TradeMax, Demandware, Lucid Imagination, MoMeMo, Compete, Inc. and BringItOn!

Specialties: Bitcoin, Real-time communications, Python, Ruby on Rails, Node.js, Javascript, CoffeeScript, Data optimization, Machine Learning, Statistical Analysis, Business Development, competitive analytics, Mobile Commerce, product branding, product positioning, marketing strategy, B2B Sales, consultative sales, legal IT, Online marketing, Consultative Sales and Product Pricing Strategy, Network Security & Management
