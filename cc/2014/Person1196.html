Background

Summary

Jason Lopez is a second generation real estate professional and licensed broker in California. Over the past 20 years he has dedicated himself to helping his customers and team navigate the Southern California real estate market. His experience includes working in management capacities with large independently owned and operated real estate firms affiliated with Keller Williams Realty, Century 21, RE/MAX, and RealEstate.com. Jason also currently serves on the San Diego Association of REALTORS Business Development/Technology and Membership/Education committees.

Keller Williams Realty La Mesa is growing. My focus is to help our agents build "Careers worth having, businesses worth owning and lives worth living." My One Thing is growth & the goal is to grow agent count, market share, sales volume, closed units, profit and profit share. That's right we offer profit sharing to our agents and allow them to build a team within the market center. If you are looking for an innovative brokerage that truly supports your goals, then we should talk.
