Kim Bohinick's Summary

As experts powering business, we work to ensure that an optimal match is created through a deep understanding of your business needs – a match that will drive results and help you reach your specific goals. When it comes to connecting IT experts to the organizations that need their talent most, Randstad Technologies has the edge.

Randstad Technologies has four key service offerings for clients, including the recruitment of contract, contract-to-hire and permanent placement; consulting for strategy, solutions, process and governance; project management for technology implementation, technology survey, process integration and applications services; and outsourcing including end-user services, operations and infrastructure.
