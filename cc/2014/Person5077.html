Srinivas Rao's Summary

I'm the CEO of Unmistakable Media and the host of the Unmistakable Creative Podcast, which I started as BlogcastFM in 2010. In 2013, self published The Art of Being Unmistakable which became a Wall Street Journal and Amazon Bestseller
