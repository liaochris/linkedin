Background

Summary

Jessica Jablonski is a top producing agent at Keller Williams Realty. She grew up in the beautiful mountains of Park City, Utah and then moved to Arizona for college. In 2005, she graduated from the W.P. Carey School of Business at Arizona State University where she received her Bachelor of Science in Accounting.

She has always had an entrepreneurial spirit and in 2011 she founded the Jablonski Real Estate Group at Keller Williams Realty. In addition to providing excellent service to her clients she also serves on the Agent Leadership Council as the chair of the Culture Committee. She has organized many events to help raise money and resources for those in need including the Annual Charity KWAZ Golf Tournament and Annual Red Day at the Phoenix Rescue Mission. She was named the Cultural Ambassador for the Southwest Region in 2012.

In today’s market, having the assistance of a Realtor with experience is more important than ever. Buyers need a Realtor that can negotiate the best deal possible. Sellers need an experienced agent that can help prepare their home for sale and negotiate the most money in the least amount of time. Jessica is consistently ranked in the Top 10 Agents of her office for production. In 2011 she was awarded the Top Individual Agent for Units and Commission Revenue.

Jessica firmly believes the home buying and selling process is “ALL ABOUT THE YOU”, the client. Excellent service and building relationships is the key to success. This is why 90% of Jessica’s business is repeat and referral.

Jessica has a wonderful relationship with her husband, Geoffrey, who is also a vital part of the business. They have a beautiful son, Jordan, who is the light of their lives. They love the outdoors and spend their free time traveling, scuba diving, and hiking.
