Richard Taylor, AIA | Residential Architect's Summary

I'm the Owner and President of RTA Studio, a small personal service-oriented Residential Architectural Studio in Historic Dublin, Ohio.

RTA Studio creates Architecture for clients who value well-crafted, timeless, and thoughtful design; who treasure a sense of place and the meaning of "home"; and who want the place where they live to be a lasting, positive contribution to their community and the world.

RTA Studio works throughout Ohio and across the United States.
