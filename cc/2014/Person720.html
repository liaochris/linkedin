Background

Summary

Family Law Attorney

All I practice is family law.

Family Law is code for divorce law. I am one of the few divorce lawyers who works to reconcile marriages. I do so using Biblical principles.

I have been practicing family exclusively for years. Prior to that, I clerked for a federal judge, and worked for the U.S. Department of Justice.

I have lived an aspect of family law. I became a single dad to our three sons after my first wife died. I later remarried, and became a step-father. I have walked the walk, and can talk the talk. I know this business.
