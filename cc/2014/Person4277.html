Michael S. Seaver, CPBA, SPHR, MBA's Summary

Michael S. Seaver is a Certified Professional Behavioral Analyst (CPBA), a Senior Professional in Human Resources (SPHR) and a Thunderbird School of Global Management MBA graduate. He is owner of Seaver Consulting, a coaching practice specializing in unlocking human potential and career advancement, is author of books entitled Fearing Mediocrity and Incorporate You™, and is Thunderbird's Director of Career Management Alumni Services and Engagement. Michael is also Vice Chairman of the Phoenix Business and Workforce Development board. Prior to his current roles, he spent more than a decade in recruitment, training and development, operations management, and career coaching with organizations such as the Four Seasons Hotels and Resorts, Banner Health, Grand Canyon University, and the W. P. Carey School of Business.

CAREER COACHING – supports driven individuals in the development of resumes, cover letters, LinkedIn profiles, networking strategies, interviewing techniques and compensation negotiation

PERSONAL BRANDING – develops personal mission, vision, constraints, and value proposition statements helping others to exude their personal brand face-to-face and online

POSITIVE PSYCHOLOGY – creates a “go-giver” mentality where positive thinking and visualization work together to build meaningful and lasting relationships

FOR BENEFIT/PURPOSE – focuses on the triple bottom line (people, planet, and profit) to challenge others to do something they believe in and to “out-behave” others when moving from success to societal significance

INCORPORATE YOU™ – using the DISC assessment, trains leaders to view themselves as corporations discovering and applying their core behaviors, communication styles and strengths to apply business strategy concepts to career advancement

Gallup StrengthsFinder Top 5: Achiever, Responsibility, Maximizer, Individualization, Learner
