Alexis Monville's Summary

Alexis Monville: Alexis is Chief Agility Officer of eNovance, a major contributor in the Openstack code and an active player of the open cloud ecosystem. Alexis is tasked with creating and nurturing an Agile culture that pervades the whole organization. Over the past twenty years, Alexis had worked in diverse sectors, from the automotive industry, the epic Internet start, IT consulting, public sector, which led him to found Ayeba, a management and organization consulting firm. Alexis is involved in associations and lecturer in the fields of management, agile methods and digital.
