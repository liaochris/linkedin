Shawn Murphy, MBA's Summary

Change Management Consulting

I am a passionate, self-proclaimed “geek” for change management and leadership. I direct my passion, skills, and ability to lead change projects for small businesses and organizations of all sizes – private and public.

I've led change programs for culture change, leadership transformation, employee morale, new employee assimilation, and technology implementations.

Learning and Development

Over 19 years, using ADDIE model to deliver learning solutions to adults from senior executives, middle managers to individual contributors. Topics range from technical training, sales training, business skills, leadership and coaching, values training, and diversity training.

Coaching

Over the last 19 years, I've coached individuals and teams to perform at a level critical to the success of people and the company.

Communications Specialist

I have a natural ability to connect with people. This ability transfers into my written communications, presentations, and digital media. It's critical to understand what's important to the audience and tailor the communication approach to maximize the opportunity to achieve understanding. I love using the principles of social media to connect with people in new and sometimes profound ways.
