Andy Vitus' Summary

I focus on Scale Venture Partners' investments in next generation enterprise software and new datacenter technologies including virtualization and cloud computing. Additionally, my area of expertise includes innovations in consumer electronics and semiconductors, particularly analog and mixed signal technologies.

Prior to joining Scale VP in 2003, I worked as a hardware engineer for Electronics for Imaging, designing embedded print server platforms and video compression circuits. Previously, I was a software engineer for De Beers Research Labs, doing research on computer vision for both medical and mining applications.

I earned an MS in Electrical Engineering from Stanford University and a BS in Electrical Engineering from the University of Cape Town, South Africa.

Specialties

Technology investing, venture capital, cloud computing, data center technology, Web 2.0
