Joel Chudleigh's Summary

Digital Marketer and wet behind the ears retailer; running a digital marketing agency between the UK and Tokyo, Japan as well as a UK based retail business.

Digital Marketing:

I am passionate about the ability of the internet to connect like minded people and to seed relationships that would never have existed had it not been for this fantastic technology.

I prefer the road less travelled and like to help clients develop innovative strategies that support them in differentiating themselves and growing an online business that they are truly proud of.

My specific skills are focused as a Pay Per Click Consultant (PPC Consultant), Search Engine Optimisation Consultant (SEO Consultant), Social Media Consultant and on Affiliate Marketing. But more than that I love piecing together the right strategy for each of our clients and also being a working cog in the day to day process of delivering that strategy.

I can quickly understand and assess where businesses are missing opportunity and where they need to change, I can also analyse competitors, industries, and service offerings to develop innovative strategies and campaigns that push my clients forward fast.

The most enjoyable part of my job is seeing my customers businesses grow. When they have to employ new team members due to our work it is incredibly rewarding.

Specialties: PPC Consultant, PPC Expert, SEO Consultant, Social Media Consultant, Affiliate Marketing Consultant, Email Marketing, Online Advertising.

Retail:

Having worked in retail as a marketer (both in house and with Deep Footprints) I know how to use the internet to sell. MADE TO LAST offers a route to market for the talented and passionate manufacturers of high quality products that last.

My role has been very broad so far - defining our offering and communicating it on the website, overseeing the design and build of the website, relationship building with suppliers and from here on in lots of awareness raising (marketing).

The key difference to any other retailer in the past is that our product pages all show a clear guarantee on the product alongside the price so that consumers can more easily understand the value of a product.
