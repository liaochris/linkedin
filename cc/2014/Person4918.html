Meeting Expectations, Inc. (MEI) is a full service pharmaceutical meeting planning company providing logistical support, including arranging advisory board meetings, investigator meetings, sales meetings and convention activities (such as room blocks, registration, pre-con meetings, symposia logistics, etc). MEI coordinates programs in the US and Europe.

MEI's clients include start-ups, mid-size and major pharmaceutical and biotechnology companies. We also provide meeting support to medical education companies, medical societies and healthcare-related non-profit groups.

MEI arranges programs from ten to 1,000 attendees. We can handle a program from start to finish or manage specific tasks to best meet our individual client’s needs.

MEI employs both full and part time trained meeting professionals with relevant experience in the pharmaceutical, medical education, public relations and hotel industries. In addition, MEI has an established network of suppliers and local staff throughout the US and Europe.

MEI utilizes sophisticated IT solutions using state of the art technology in order to protect the privacy and confidentiality of clients and their customers.

MEI is well informed regarding PhRMA guidelines and compliance issues. In addition, MEI is a preferred meeting planning company for several pharmaceutical and biotechnology companies, and is familiar with procurement processes.

MEI offers professional services at a competitive price and is happy to bid on projects.
