Coach Kevin Brown brings positive energy into every room as he steps inside. Once on the podium, he radiates that energy with his incredible life story that is documented in his critically acclaimed novel, From Ghetto to Greatness. From there he engages the audience with rhetorical questions and clever metaphors that penetrate deep into the subconscious, forcing the listeners to take stock of the gap between their full potential and their current reality. He then gives them the confidence and tools to bridge the gap, leaving them totally excited about living every moment of the rest of their lives.

BPS also offers coaching services to at-risk teens.

BPS has published two books with more in the works.

BPS goal is to help everyone discover their God given purpose and live their life accordingly.
