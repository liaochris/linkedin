Steen Lynenskjold's Summary

Responsible for the defense and security business area in Terma. This covers the global supply of airborne and naval self-protection systems, SCANTER radar systems, tactical command and control systems for mission critical defense and security applications as well as high-technology electronics manufacturing services.

The responsibility covers:
- Business lines within the airborne, land and naval segments
- Product solutions within avionics based on the ALQ-213 electronic warfare management system, SCANTER radars and command and control systems based on the T-Core platform
- Global marketing and local presences in North America, Singapore, the Netherlands and India
- Electronics manufacturing services for space, radar and avionics products

Chairman of the Board of Alexandra Instituttet, www.alexandra.dk

Experienced within software and systems engineering, project management, business development and general management.

Currently residing in Århus, Denmark. Earlier colocated in Darmstadt, Germany and Seattle, US.
