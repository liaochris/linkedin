Chris Peck's Summary

I am a senior IT executive experienced in building and leading consulting teams to solve our clients business challenges. My broad experience and education gives me the perspective to understand the application of technology to improve business results. I am currently leading CTS' newest office in Nashville, TN, building a team of consultants who help our local clients tackle their toughest technology challenges.

When I'm not working I can usually be found at my kids soccer practices and games, volunteering with my church's men's club, working out or watching Gator Football or Duke Basketball.

CTS is an IT consulting firm that provides software development, quality assurance, business intelligence, application integration, application support and portals & collaboration consulting services to enterprise clients across the Southeast. Our mission is to deliver superior solutions for our customers, careers for our employees, and growth in our communities. We have offices in Atlanta, Georgia; Birmingham, Alabama; Charlotte, North Carolina; Chattanooga, Tennessee; Mobile, Alabama, and Nashville, Tennessee. CTS is a Microsoft partner with gold competencies in Software Development, Web Development, Application Integration, Portals and Collaboration, and Business Intelligence. For more information, please visit www.askcts.com.
