Kevin McGuire's Summary

I am a Solicitor (non-practicing) and Chartered Quantity Surveyor with 25 years experience of working in or associated with the construction and engineering industries.

I trained and qualified as a Chartered Quantity Surveyor with a regional contractor before working as a Claims and Contracts Consultant with a leading practice gaining international dispute experience. I than re-trained and qualified as a Solicitor practicing exclusively in construction and engineering law.

Before establishing Construct Legal & Commercial, I was a Solicitor/Associate in the Construction and Engineering Group at Pinsent Masons.

The services I am able to provide fall into 3 broad categories:

1. Dispute & advisory – I have extensive adjudication experience and have lectured on adjudication at the University of West of England. I am experienced in litigation, mediation and arbitration. I regularly advise parties on legal/contractual issues and on appropriate resolution strategies. I am also able to deliver training on a range of construction/legal issues;

2. Transactional – I can draft, review, amend and advise upon construction contracts and ancillary documents;

3. Commercial – From my experience as a QS, I am able to assist at all stages of the construction process from the tender stage through to final account including contract administration and the preparation, defence and negotiation of claims in relation to additional cost and time.
