Frédéric Vandendris' Summary

Today, my heart still clearly lies in the digital marketing sphere in which I keep finding the same excitement in looking at new initiatives coming from all sides, interacting with dtabases, the social web, and discovering the rise of new technologies and the user and business opportunities they create.

- Operational processes and IT projects management;
- Fluent in French, English and Dutch.
