Summary

The Blass Group of Companies is a Michigan based consulting firm that provides unique services in each of the following business units. Visioneering and imagineering is the foundation of The Blass Group of Companies.

Sports Marketing (Unit) is a effective strategy to take marketing to the next level. Collaboration and teambuilding are both key as well to success that crosses all verticals. The use of team sports is a very effective knowledge transfer between the corporate world and team based sports.

Marketing Research (Unit) It is always the results of intensive marketing research that creates the difference.The real challenge is to mange the process of blending relationship building with creativity that controls the shared risks while sharing the subsequent rewards.. To become visible being creative creates what I call the differential edge over content. We work with clients that need to upgrade their best practices to become either profitable or more profitable through leveraging relationships and partnerships.Qualifying vs quantifying are clearly two functions of the board room that need to be studied and implemented individually and collectively throughout the organization

Blass Ideologies (Unit) is a creative service offering that consults with organizations to bring the creative thinking process to repackaging the product or service value offered To survive in today's business world we assist in bringing creative delivery models to attract new clients. We bring fresh ideas from outside the existing teams to introduce new and exciting ideas.

Events Marketing (Unit) provides assistance in producing very effective marketing and unique events that are event driven.

Marketing and Public Relations (Unit) the practice of marketing and public relations is highly customized to deliver value ad to clients (See Specialties)

Should there be the opportunity to discuss how we might be able to assist please contact us me at your earliest convenience

Specialties:Teambuilding, differential marketing, public relations , marketing research, video creation, media management, creative thinking and events management are all offerings by The Blass Group of Companies.
