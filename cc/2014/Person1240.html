Background

Summary

How Tenants View Their Office Space is Changing --> EcoSpace Commercial is the brokerage firm leading this change.

Tenants are realizing that the large corner offices, long term leases and non tech friendly buildings are a way of the past. The new generation of workers require a more open work environment, collaboration, flexibility, access to cutting edge technology and a fresh feel to their building.

How are companies finding that space? They contact Ecospace Commercial!
Let's Get Started! Learn More Here --> http://www.ecospacecommercial.com/about

----------

As a true farm boy (I can tell you these stories for days), I learned the lesson of hard work and entrepreneurship at a very young age. Before graduating high school, I had the privilege of starting three businesses. One is still in operation today, one that was sold for profit in 2000 and one that was a complete loss. All three provided their own unique lessons on entrepreneurship.

My passion and focus since 10+ years has been helping businesses make smart office leasing decisions. I have helped over 400+ entrepreneurs and business owners in the leasing or acquisition of commercial office space which is one of the most rewarding aspects of my job.

When not improving the bottom line of mine or my clients' businesses, you’ll find me in the mountains, backpacking my way to 100+ countries (no exotic hotels for this guy), on my stand up paddle board or simply enjoying the wonderful life I've have been blessed.
