Steve Swinsburg's Summary

Steve is a Senior Software Engineer at Orion Health. He holds a Masters Degree in Information Technology and a Bachelor of Science Degree. He also has several years of tertiary teaching experience.

Steve is a Sakai Foundation Board member and a founding member of Apereo. He is an active member of the Sakai 2 Technical Coordination Committee, the Sakai CLE Release Team, the Kernel Team and the Security Working Group. He is also a member of the Jasig Incubation Committee and is a uPortal committer.

Steve developed Profile2, the social networking tool for Sakai, as well as the Shortened URL Service, a remote administration utility, has enhanced the Sakai App Builder for new developers and has committed over two dozen new web services to the Sakai suite. Steve also actively contributes to the various mailing lists and assists other developers within the community.

Steve has written over a dozen articles on Sakai best practices and how-to's, and regularly blogs about Sakai related developments. In 2008 he had a paper on Sakai in distance education published in the Australian ascilite journal.

Steve is also an active uPortal contributor and has developed several JSR-168 portlets, notably the Sakai Connector Portlet and the Basic LTI Portlet, both implementing the IMS LTI specification.
