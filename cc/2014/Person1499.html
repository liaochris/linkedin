Background

Summary

I'm an experienced Illinois freelance writer dedicated to helping businesses and individuals succeed. I do this by writing content for their websites, blogs, publications and/or other projects. I've been providing writing services to clients worldwide since 1999. During that time, I've had over 150 feature articles published in various electronic and print publications, have ghostwritten a number of blog posts and SEO articles, and written content for newsletters. Furthermore, I'm the founder and editor of CoffeeBreakforWriters.com as well as the author of a writer's reference book and Christian fiction novel (published under Misti Jackson).

Specialties:Topics I write about: blogging, buyer/consumer guides, camping, caregiving (e.g. caring for the elderly or terminally ill), celebrity news, city/state guides, domestic violence (I'm a survivor), family, freelance writing, frugal living, frugal shopping, inspirational fiction, marketing, marriage/relationships, money management, parenting, reality TV news, SEO, small business,social media, and tutorials with screenshots.
