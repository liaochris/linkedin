Sara Bacon's Experience

Creative Director & Founder

Command C

Public Company; 1-10 employees; Design industry

January 2004 – Present (10 years 9 months) Brooklyn, NY

Command C started in 2004 because we recognized that websites had either strong design and terrible technology, or great technology and terrible design. Technology has evolved considerably since then, but our core goal of making the web an easier, prettier place has remained the same.

Our mission is simple: to merge timeless design with cutting edge technology. Command C works with agencies, forward-thinking independent businesses and non-profit organizations to provide high caliber website design, development and maintenance that stays abreast of the latest web technology and trends. Our clients range from local to international, our jobs from large to small.

Clients consistently say that in addition to our design and development chops, Command C’s emphasis on project management and ability to untangle technical jargon is what sets us apart.

Owner

Greenpoint Coworking

Public Company; Myself Only; Think Tanks industry

December 2010 – February 2014 (3 years 3 months) Brooklyn, NY

I started Greenpoint Coworking, a shared workspace for independent workers whose goal is to build and nourish community in a beautiful and professional environment while fostering individual work, as a means to an end of the isolation we experience by being home based businesses. I developed GPCW from concept to launch and did all of the brand identity and website design for the organization.

Art Director

GO Magazine

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Online Media industry

2004 – 2007 (3 years)

As the Art Director for GO Magazine, I was in charge of Art direction, layout, creation of style guide, photo editing, stock image selection, communication with writers and management of junior designers.

Graphic Designer & Project Manager

Print Icon

2004 – 2005 (1 year)

Print Icon is an in house print and graphic design house based in NYC. My roles there included: graphic design, pre-press production, and project management.

Photo Retoucher

Angelo Musco Studio

2004 – 2004 (less than a year)

Angelo Musco is a successful NYC based artist and photographer. While working there as the lead Photo Retoucher, my roles included: photo editing, high end retouching, digital archiving, and image processing for web.
