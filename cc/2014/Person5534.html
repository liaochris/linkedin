Lukas Pleva's Summary

I graduated from The University of Chicago, where I studied political science with concentrations in technology and international relations.

I currently work as a B2B junior account executive in the Chicago office of DigitasLBi, a global advertising agency with over 6,000 employees in 25 countries, where I am responsible for helping manage and drive forward the agency's relationship with a $24b Fortune 500 manufacturing and technology provider. I have previously assisted with B2B customer acquisition efforts for a major, U.S.-based telecommunications carrier, and have prior experience executing SEO, email marketing, social media, web development, and content marketing initiatives for a variety of small and mid-sized businesses.

In my free time, I am involved with Moneythink, a startup nonprofit with a focus on financial literacy education, as a part-time volunteer web developer, database administrator, and technology strategist.

Specialties: social media marketing, search engine optimization, search engine marketing/PPC, web development, online brand monitoring, email marketing, database administration
