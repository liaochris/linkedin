Background

Summary

I am a lifelong Columbus resident, giving me “home field” expertise in the Columbus and Central Ohio market. As a licensed realtor since 1985, I will make sure whether you are selling, buying or creating an investment opportunity, it is as seamless and simple as possible from beginning to end. I will share my expansive knowledge, eliminating any stress by giving you the information edge to make decisions based on facts. With client satisfaction as my main priority, your goal becomes my number one goal.

I am one of less than a dozen realtors in Columbus with the National Association of Green Realtors designation. Along with my own research, this designation provides up to date green resources and ongoing green education.

Besides my passion in real estate, I am devoted to trying to reduce my own footprint on our environment. I am involved in a community garden with several close friends, a member of Simply Living and The Clintonville Community Market. I try to shop and eat locally whenever possible.

Single family homes, condos, mutli-family properties, short sales, bank owned, or HUD homes, let me educate you on the selling and buying process, making it easier for you to achieve your goal!

Specialties:Seller and Buyer Representation, First time buyer information, Short Sales in Columbus, Ohio, Home owners who need help with mortgage and possible short sale, Homes for sale in Clintonville, Columbus and Surrounding Counties, Condos
