Arunjeet Singh's Summary

Software professional with 12 years of experience in the industry including experience leading teams in mobility, enterprise application development and consulting engagements. Experience developing and designing complex enterprise-grade line of business applications as well as mass-market software products.

I have worked for Microsoft for 7 years now. Currently I work as a Senior Program Manager on the Windows Phone team. Specifically, I own functionality in the Windows Phone developer platform. The developer platform defines the Application Programming Interfaces (APIs) developers use to write applications for Windows Phone. Before coming to work for Windows Phone, I was part of the team that successfully shipped SQL Server 2012.

I have also worked for Microsoft Global Services (Consulting) as a Consultant. As a consultant I ran the mobility team within my division and this included driving pre-sales engagements, putting together sales collateral and leading/managing teams on mobility projects. The remit of the mobility team included phone/mobile applications, tablet-based applications and mobile-enabled web applications.

Before moving to Microsoft I worked as a Senior Engineer and Team Lead in Xchanging Technology Services (http://www.xchanging.com). While at Xchanging I worked on Genius (http://www.xuber.com/genius), a comprehensive software solution for the Property and Casualty insurance market. Xchanging recently spun its insurance software business off into its own company called Xuber.

Skills: Team management, Functional management, Pre-sales, Sales collateral, Estimation, Project Management, Software development and design

Technical Skills: ASP.NET, WPF, Master Data Services, SQL Server, Mobility technologies (Windows Phone, Windows), Cloud technologies (Windows Azure)
