Matthew Osborne-Smith's Summary

Professional with fourteen years of experience in international development and health as well as nonprofit and social enterprise management. Main technical areas include 1) program design, monitoring, and evaluation; 2) organizational and program management; 3) capacity development; 4) market-based development initiatives; and 5) health system strengthening. Adept program manager and management consultant with a wealth of experience encompassing work in over fifteen countries and has a strong proposal writing track record. Overall objective is to address social problems with sustainable entrepreneurial solutions.

Specialties: Management; strategy; monitoring & evaluation; marketing & communications; business development; partnership building; operations management; organizational development; public health; social entrepreneurship; social enterprise; private sector/enterprise development; nonprofit management; micro finance
