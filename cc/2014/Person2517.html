Christina L. Maki's Summary

I have been behind the lens since I was a freshman in high school, back in the days of film and dark rooms and praying to the photography gods that your exposure was correct, because you only got one shot to get it right. It was a labor of love. Now I’m well-versed in the ways of the digital SLR, which is a far more forgiving format than a roll of 35mm film.

I love photography and I put 100% into every session I do. It doesn’t matter whether you are booking a simple half-hour head shot session or one of my “Hello, Baby!” Birth Sessions–your photos will be created with equal parts joy and passion.

My specialty is maternity, birth, and newborn photography. I am a mom myself, and parenthood is my first passion. I had a wonderful natural birth in July 2013, so I know exactly how much work and pain go into bringing a baby into the world! It also means I am able to navigate your birthing venue in a manner that allows me to take beautiful photos without disrupting the labor process or being a nuisance to your birth team.

I am an indiscriminate photographer. It doesn’t matter to me what your family looks like. As a matter of fact, I adore doing photo shoots with alternative families. If you’re interracial, a same-sex couple, a little (or a lot!) on the crunchy side, or anything else that may set you apart from the classic nuclear family, rest assured that I will make you feel totally at ease during our shoot and produce amazing portraits of your beautiful, unique relationships. Love is love, and it comes in all colors, shapes, and sizes.

To view my portfolio or schedule a session, visit http://ad-astra-photography.com.
