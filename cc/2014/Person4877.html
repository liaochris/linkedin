Ryan Dwyer's Summary

Ryan is a musician that is building a fan base by helping other musicians and entrepreneurs. One day he got sick and tired of participating in fake social media following and likes. This inspired him to set out on a mission to make a platform community for people to start "liking" and "following" each other authentically. Join the Facebook group to stay updated: http://www.facebook.com/groups/contentprince
