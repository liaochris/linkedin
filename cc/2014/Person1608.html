Robert Small's Summary

Robert Small writes about the Sacramento Kings and Android operating system for Examiner.com He also writes about general news, business and sports for AOL's Patch.com in Sacramento. Covering the Kings since 2009, he is one of the top ranked NBA writers for Examiner.com. His writing experience covers newspaper, television and magazine and has also spent time in the radio industry.
