Mike Hamman's Summary

For individuals who want to own and manage their own business, mine is a free service to help identify and acquire the ideal business given the seeker's personal wants, needs, and aspirations.

My service is unique because I have designed an efficient process to guide the candidate quickly to the opportunities that most closely match their requirements.

This process saves a great deal of time and effort. It helps minimize risk, and it leads the seeker to the best-of-the-best options that meet their needs.

It provides you with high-level, professional introductions to executives at the best-fit franchise companies.

And it helps you avoid mistakes and wasted time as you examine and select from among the best-of-the-best opportunities.

Specialties

My unique process is designed to do these things:

• give you powerful leverage in your search (by expanding your knowledge)
• reduce your uncertainty (by developing your personal model and using it)
• save a great deal of time and effort (by focusing on exactly what you want and need)
• maximize profitability and growth potential (by relying on my experience and access to franchise information)
• minimize risk to you (by using a proven franchise business model).
