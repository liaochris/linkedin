Celeste Bishop's Summary

Celeste is the founder of Bishop Market Resources – a firm that assists companies, teams and individuals on how to build and leverage a Web presence that contributes to greater business success.

Celeste is a frequent public speaker, published author, consultant, service provider and entrepreneur with the following expertise:

- How to effectively develop websites as strategic assets
- How to build a Web presence to achieve business goals
- How to build relevant Web strategies and plans
- How to identify the pragmatic mix of Classic & Web 2.0 tactics
- How to build a competitive advantage online

Bishop Market Resources has created unique new programs and services, which identify potential opportunities available through more effective use of the Web. This necessarily involves websites and the ever-expanding universe of prospective opportunities online.

Celeste Bishop's Publications

  • OnCompeting Blog

    • Current

    Authors: Celeste Bishop

    OnCompeting is a place to learn what works in online marketing and online competition… and what doesn’t work, best practices, pragmatic new approaches and debunk what is hyped but not delivering.

    In short, our goal is to shorten your learning curve, put you in the game faster and give you substantive food for thought.

    OnCompeting, will provide you with a steady flow of insights, tips and resources that focus on helping you to develop your skills for faster business growth... the kind of business that achieves better results with less effort than their competitors.
