Antonio Cangiano's Summary

Technical Evangelist and Software Engineer, specializing in Ruby and Ruby on Rails application development. Part of the Big Data and DB2 team at the IBM Toronto Lab, where I was the initial author of the adapter for DB2 on Rails.

I'm really interested in cutting edge technology, programming languages, databases (relational and NoSQL ones), XP/Agile methodologies, Cloud computing, Big Data, science, mathematics, and anything on the bleeding edge of the web. Beside Ruby, my interests are geared towards programming languages such as JavaScript, Python, Clojure, and Haskell. In a past life I was really into C# and the .NET framework (as an early adopter and community leader since the very beginning) but I moved on several years ago.

Beside programming, I have a great deal of interest in technical writing, marketing, social media, and the world of entrepreneurship and startups in general. (I've written and presented on the subject.)

I'm an avid reader and my main goal is to improve myself and positively affect other people in the process.

Specialties: Ruby, Ruby on Rails, HTML, JavaScript, DB2, and relational databases (including MySQL), Hadoop, Big SQL, and Big Data. I also have experience with mentoring and coaching other developers, and promoting technologies within the Enterprise and development communities.

Technical marketing is definitely one of my specialties and passions, and so is inbound marketing, blogging, and social media.
