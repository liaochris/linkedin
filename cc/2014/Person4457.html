Nicolas Weil's Summary

As a Solutions Architect, my goal is to design and drive implementation of innovative multiscreen workflows that allow media actors to distribute interactive, synchronized and protected video contents.

Specialties:
- Web platforms architecture design
- OTT, Streaming & Second-Screen technologies expertise
- CDN deployments for OTT & Web services
- Design of audio/video production tools and platforms
- Management of development projects
- Consulting for impossible Digital Media projects
- Technology integration : DirectShow, Cisco Media Processor & Transcode Manager, Elemental Live & Server, HbbTV, Flash Access, Second-Screen technologies, Akamai services, MPEG-DASH...

Co-founder of the now famous Online Video French Squad (http://ovfsquad.fr) and member of the DASH Industry Forum (http://dashif.org).
