Daniel Marcos' Summary

Daniel Marcos is the CEO and Co-Founder of Gazelles Growth Institute, The Leading eLearning Executive Education Platform by the Top Experts in the World.

Gazelles Growth Institute is leading current movements in hybrid education and business growth. Our core purpose is to expose C level executives to business thought leaders through a community that facilitates learning and growth. Quite simply, we want to make it easier for busy executives to learn new techniques and methodologies that will help their businesses.

IT TAKES A “VILLAGE OF GURUS” TO GROW A COMPANY.
We bring you only the very best. Specialized employee development equals company development.

IT TAKES THE ALIGNMENT OF AN ENTIRE TEAM TO BUILD A COMPANY.
This cannot be expressed enough. Success is an ecosystem of team knowledge.

THE HYBRID MODEL OF EDUCATION IS THE MOST POWERFUL.
Hybrid models of education provide the skills and knowledge training that you need now in a way that is customizable to your bottom lines.

NO TIME FOR AMATEUR HOUR, ONLY THE BEST.

With vast entrepreneurial experience, Daniel has built and sold several companies on and offline and is currently focused on creating the world’s leading and most exclusive executive eLearning platform for executive teams around the world.

Daniel currently lives in Austin Texas with his beautiful wife and two children. He holds a B.S. in Industrial and Systems Engineering from I.T.E.S.M. (Monterrey Tech) and an M.B.A. (cum laude) from Babson College. He is also a graduate of the Entrepreneurs' Organization’s premiere CEO program, the “Birthing of Giants,” and its continuation, “Gathering of The Titans.”

You can read Daniel's tips on achieving entrepreneurial success at http://GrowthInstitute.com/Blog/ and http://Capitalemprendedor.com, or follow him on Twitter: @capitalemprende.
