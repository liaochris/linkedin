Stephen B. Sanders' Summary

I have 30 years of experience in the national title underwriting service industry. As a result, my clients have a title insurance advocate that knows their business, solves their problems, and works closely with them through every transaction. Additionally, I offer ALTA Survey Services, 1031 Exchange Services and UCC Insurance Services for all types of non-single family properties and all types of commercial transactions. Call me at 303-291-9856 or 415-999-4566, or email at sbsanders@fnf.com and allow me to walk you through how I will personally handle your transaction.

 It’s about experience: I have spent more than 30 years closing transactions from all sides of the deal. I am an innovative problem solver and have been a proactive business-minded leader in commercial real estate, title insurance and transactional real estate. My experience includes serving as a former commercial foreclosure trustee, real estate paralegal, manager of a real estate industrial development company’s closing department and as a commercial title plant manager.

 As Senior Vice President for a National Commercial Services operation, I am your advocate within the Fidelity National Title Insurance Group and related enterprises, bringing the power of our company to manage your transaction.

 Single Point of Contact Philosophy: I am your point of contact for escrow closings, underwriting and record title resolutions for properties across the street or across the nation. I will be your guide throughout the entire process and will access the strength of Fidelity National Title Group NCS to serve you and your team.

 Specialties: I offer diagnostic analysis, efficient underwriting resolution, project management and coordination, and access to national real estate data. I offer responsive, thoughtful, and solution-based services. My industry knowledge, depth of experience and relationship focus provides a seamless experience for each client.
