Charles Alday's Summary

Pipeline Performance Group has worked in 2010-2014 on over 120 projects related to pipeline control rooms for hazardous liquids and natural gas pipelines. We have worked with companies in the USA, Canada, and China. These projects included developing Control Room Management (CRM) plans, evaluation and audit preparation of CRM plans, new control room design, workload assessments, fatigue mitigation, operating procedures development, and training programs.

We already have over 60 projects scheduled in 2014.

We develop fatigue risk management plans and are providing fatigue management educational materials to many companies. We partner with the provider of FAID Safe fatigue modeling software.

The company is the only human factors consulting company that applies practical pipeline shift work experience, control room management and consulting experience, and doctoral qualifications to the development of control room management plans, pipeline human factors consulting, and fatigue risk management programs.

In the past five years, we have:
• Developed, implemented, improved 16 Control Room Management Plans and assisted with audits
• Performed over 70 workload analyses to meet regulatory requirements and determine if workload is acceptable or not
• Developed operating procedures
• Developed training materials and conducted training
• Reviewed and advised on shift schedules, using fatigue modeling software
• Developed and delivered fatigue management training and educational materials
• Developed control center design criteria
• Performed assessments of control room ergonomic, environmental, and spatial factors
• Developed procedures for backup SCADA tests, evacuation plans, point to point tests, alarm management
• Performed human factors and organizational assessments of organizations
* Developed training programs and control center forums
