Dusan Soravia Kosaric's Summary

In my almost 15 years long career in Information Technology (IT) I went from being a web developer and creating e-commerce web sites to the level where deeper knowledge in more complex underlying systems and servers like MS SQL server and Windows NT operating system was needed. The life path guided me to develop highly professional skills and obtain deeper knowledge in Microsoft underlying technology by successfully completing Microsoft Certified Systems Engineer certification. As I was more focused on Microsoft and its technologies in my professional life, I used other platforms and technologies in my spare time, to gain broad spectrum of knowledge available today, which I crowned with my Master's degree in IT.

As the time passed and I grew in personality as well technically, logical step was to start delivering my knowledge to others. I always try to create a productive climate and confidently motivate and coach others to meet high performance standards.

My everyday activities at work included 3rd level support for customers and project management for successful implementation of technology in most, largest Slovenian enterprises.

Currently I am working as Key Account Manager, providing customers with advanced IT learning options and possibilities in Cisco, Citrix, VMWare, HP...
