Sales & Marketing Support Coordinator

Adjusters International / Basloe, Levin & Cuccaro

Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Management Consulting industry

March 2012 – Present (2 years 8 months) Springville, New York

I coordinate regional marketing and support efforts for the nation's premier disaster recovery consulting organization. Our core focus is maximizing and expediting our clients' financial recovery from insurance and FEMA claims. We have two well-established areas of expertise: providing the nation's leading public adjusting services; and, guiding FEMA grantees and applicants through the public assistance program.

For more than 100 years, our firm has been guiding businesses and residential clients through the unpredictable territory of preparing and settling insurance claims.

Our offices follow the professional precepts of direct knowledge, in-house expertise, and a team approach which creates a successful, coordinated financial recovery strategy. The goal of every client interaction is to help you make informed decisions and give you back control of your claim. We want to make you comfortable with the tedious adjustment process and plan for recovery.

Adjusters International is the largest and most experienced public adjusting firm in the world. With over forty offices across the United States, we have the expertise to properly adjust your claim. Adjusters International served as consultants to the Government of Kuwait with respect to its UN war reparation claims and utilized our consulting teams that assisted the Port Authority of New York/New Jersey following both the 1993 and the 2001 World Trade Center Attacks. Recently, Adjusters International was retained by the State of Louisiana to prepare financial models to assist in the case of the BP oil spill.

With offices nationwide, we are able to help you recover from your losses no matter where they occur.
