Carlos J. Arboleda's Summary

Carlos J. Arboleda is Managing Director at COI access, a Talent Advisory & Corporate Development firm based in South Florida. In his role, Arboleda leads the firm’s strategy and delivery efforts, and focuses his energies assisting clients coming into US markets from abroad or expanding their US footprints domestically. Arboleda joined COI access in 2011, and prior to that his career includes over 15 years in senior recruitment, business development, executive search, and talent consulting. Immediately before COI access, Arboleda spent 11 years with one of the largest global recruitment firms, a multi-billion dollar, well-known conglomerate, where he co-founded, developed and led the national practice and teams responsible for executive search and recruitment at the mid to senior executive level nationally and for Latin America, managing hubs in Philadelphia, New York, Baltimore / DC, Boston, Kansas City, Atlanta, Charlotte, and Miami.

Mr. Arboleda is invested in the community as a Board Member of the South Florida Workforce Investment Board; a Board Member of the Broward Workforce Development Board; a Board Member of the South Florida Association for Financial Professionals SFAFP; a Cabinet member of The United Way of Greater Miami’s Tocqueville Society; a board member of the University of Miami’s Citizens Board; a member of the Dean’s Advisory Council of the Barry University School of Business; and a Board Member of the Greater Miami Chamber of Commerce. Mr. Arboleda’s leadership posts at this chamber include Immediate Past Chair of the Human Resources Committee; Chair of the Executive Outplacement and Re-Employment Taskforce during the Great Recession; and current Chair of the Business Innovation Institute. Mr. Arboleda graduated from The University of Florida in Gainesville and lives in Ft. Lauderdale with his wife and their three children.
