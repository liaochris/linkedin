Background

Summary

I have been a practicing attorney for over twenty-five years in the Atlanta, Georgia metro area. During that time I have started two law firms and have developed an expertise in two areas of consumer law - personal bankruptcy and Social Security disability. I currently practice law with my wife, Jodi, a worker's compensation claimant's lawyer.

At my current practice, Ginsberg Law Offices, P.C., I market my firm's services - including consumer bankruptcy, Social Security disability and workers' compensation - solely on the Internet. I currently maintain over 60 web sites, blogs, and video channels to market Ginsberg Law. I put special emphasis on video marketing and I am skilled at creating, producing and distributing educational legal topic videos.

My Social Security disability video channel can be found at http://bit.ly/subscribeSS.

I speak regularly about Social Security disability and consumer bankruptcy for several continuing education vendors and I serve as the Social Security disability instructor at Solo Practice University, an online education resource for solo practitioners.
