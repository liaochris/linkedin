Background

Summary

Michael currently serves as head of product for SocialEdge, a leading platform for social and content marketing campaign management and social media talent portfolio management.

In addition to his duties at SocialEdge Michael has also served as a Board Director for the Southern California Market Research Association. He has consulted on projects for the American Film Institute's Digital Content Lab. He previously co-coordinated the LA Geek Dinner and the LA Dataportability meetups and has volunteered for BarCampLA, Mindshare Labs, LAGFF, DonorsChoose.org, and other community service projects. He speaks at events on a number of topics including neurological brain research, interaction design, online metrics and modern consumer research, at venues including Annenberg at USC, SXSW, Digital LA, East LA College, Loyola Marymount, Mindshare LA, BarCampLA, and the IxDA LA Salon.

Michael previously spent 10 years as a Sr. Research Dir for Nielsen's Intl Film (NRG) and TV groups working with many studios and channels in media buying, tracking, pilot testing, ad effectiveness, diversity, and international media. Prior he sat in the Strategic Marketing Science group working advertising, brand and content strategy for film, television and technology clients using segmentation and modeling methods. During his tenure he piloted a new network of digital labs for testing consumers response and interaction to various forms of media & advertising across multiple content platforms. Michael was one of the first to evaluate Neurofocus before acquisition. He has served as TV product lead on entertainment opinion engine based social network HeyNielsen. He started in the Software Development designing online research applications for the primary television research group and helped to create ViewerCast, an online video platform for collecting qualitative research from webcams.
