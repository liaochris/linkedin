Tanya Bamford's Summary

In 2006, I launched TCB Marketing LLC, a full-service marketing and advertising services firm. I serve in an outsourced marketing leadership role for my clients, planning and executing multichannel marketing strategies for their small to medium sized businesses. My clients represent a broad spectrum of industries with both B2B and B2C focus, from international third party logistics firms, to legal and financial service firms, healthcare companies, building contractors, retailers, IT consultants, and others. My expertise encompasses:

• Strategic marketing plan and budget development
• Brand identity development
• Demand generation
• Marketing communications
• Content marketing
• Website design
• Traditional advertising, including print, outdoor, radio, cable, and broadcast
• Search marketing (paid and organic)
• Email marketing
• Direct mail
• Video production
• Event planning and execution
• Social media marketing
• Referral marketing

With the help of my talented team, I provide clients with quality traditional and digital advertising services from graphic design, to copy writing, landing page development, search engine optimization (SEO), pay per click (PPC) campaign management, copy writing, and public relations. My portfolio includes a wide range of marketing content development, from brochures, to sell sheets, white papers, case studies, blog articles, webinars, video tutorials, and search optimized website copy. As the owner of a rapidly growing small business, I handle all new business development, proposal writing, as well as client and vendor relationship management. As the head of the company, I manage a team of four, including an account coordinator, copy writer, bookkeeper, and intern, as well as our network of freelance creatives.
