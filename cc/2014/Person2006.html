Alicia Greenberg's Summary

Alicia’s passion for creative expression began in elementary school when her active imagination often brought her the designation of “Artist of the Week.” Following her artistic intuition from childhood to the present, she takes pride in a successful career as an award-winning Senior Graphic Designer. After graduating with a BFA degree from Purchase College, a conservatory arts school in New York, Alicia landed a graphic design position at a high-powered advertising firm in Manhattan. Working in the fast-paced advertising capital of the world with a variety of clients, including prominent names in fashion and resorts, set her up for success.

With comprehensive experience in advertising, Alicia’s professional focus shifted when she moved to Colorado and accepted a graphic design position for a well-established marketing firm in Denver. Working on various marketing materials with clients in the education industry broadened her design skills and ingenuity.

Alicia’s quest for new challenges led her to California where she teamed up with the marketing department of a fashion-forward jewelry company. She art-directed various online projects and social media campaigns in addition to launching the company’s print and online magazine.

Her creative capabilities currently reside with the international award-winning marketing department at LPL Financial, the nation’s largest independent broker-dealer. She is an integral part of the BrandPartners team, a mini-business line within the corporate structure. As part of this in-house agency, she builds partnerships with individual financial advisors and financial institutions to create cutting-edge print and online branding solutions.

A classic background in fine arts allowed Alicia to use her creativity to master contemporary design and today’s technology aesthetics.

SPECIALTIES: Adobe Creative Suite, Branding, Corporate Identity, Creative Strategy & Execution, MAC OS X, Print & Front-End Web Design

  • Visionary/Creator – The Rock Project

    • April 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Alicia Greenberg

    Vision
    The Rock Project is a social experiment combining visual art, nature and technology to create an international movement of creative curiosity and human connection.

    Share, Create, Communicate
    When visionary and founder of The Rock Project Alicia Greenberg planted her first hand-painted rock with her Twitter handle on it in Tijuana, Mexico, on April 7, 2013, it was just for fun to see if anyone would find the colorful piece of art and respond. After a year of painting dozens of rocks with her signature abstract designs, adding multiple social media outlet addresses on them and handing them to friends, colleagues and strangers, as well as leaving them in random public places, her idea to communicate by melding rocks, art and technology has taken root in a big way.

    As a lover of nature and art and a fan of social media, Alicia thought: Why not mix all those elements together to make communication a little more personal and creative? She believes it’s a way of spreading joy and bringing a smile to someone — maybe a stranger, maybe a friend — and starting a chain of communication.

    The project depends on you to keep the line of creativity, exploration and communication flowing. It’s exciting to see what will happen next and how participants will transform The Rock Project with their own interpretation.
