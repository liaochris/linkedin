Diane Hunt, MBA's Summary

TRANSFORMING BUSINESS THROUGH MARKETING EXCELLENCE

Strategic and visionary executive of industry leading healthcare companies with a proven track record of increased revenues and market share. Recently served as Senior Marketing and Communications Leader for Philips Healthcare, GE Healthcare, Baxter Healthcare, and UnitedHealth Group.

Accomplished in all aspects of marketing, communications and branding. Marketing programs include award-winning BTB and BTC initiatives with global and domestic focus. Leader of diverse teams.

Known for vision-driven strategy, pursuit of marketing excellence, and collaborative multi-stakeholder relationship building across the enterprise and with external partners to reach business goals.

MARKETING EXCELLENCE

* Culture of Continuous Improvement - Mindset is always asking "How can we make it bettter, or more effective?"
* Empowering Leadership - Put the right people in the right seat and provide what's needed to succeed - course correct when needed.

COLLABORATIVE RELATIONSHIPS
