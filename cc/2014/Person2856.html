Larry Rosen's Summary

University professor for 38 years, doing research on the impact of technology on adults, children, adolescents. My current work is looking at the new iGeneration of children born in the new millennium and also studying the impact of technology on health. I am intensely interested in neuroscience and how the brain handles distractions to learning and communication. My goals are to continue to do research, write books, give speeches, consult, and teach. My current quest is to try to decide what I want to be when I grow up. I had better figure it out soon since I am 63!
