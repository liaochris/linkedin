Vipin Chawla's Summary

Vipin has over 20 years of management and leadership experience at Fortune-100 sized global organizations as well as small/medium sized companies and the public sector. He is a founder and CEO of Instant Systems Inc, a cash-flow positive startup which is developing the next generation of Software-as-a-Service products for small/mid-sized enterprises and government agencies. In addition, he is an advisor/investor for a number of startups in software, offshore services, and life sciences areas.

Prior to founding Instant Systems, Vipin was the Chief Technology Officer and VP of Products for PublicBuy.Net, a leader in electronic procurement for state/local governments, which was acquired by Tier Technologies Inc (NASDAQ: TIER), in May 2004. Vipin came to PublicBuy in November 2001 as a result of its acquisition of Simplexis, where as the CTO and VP of Engineering, Vipin helped raise over $35 million in the first round from well-known venture capital firms including Internet Capital Group (ICG) and General Electric (GE) Equity.

Prior to Simplexis, Vipin reported to the CIO/CTO at Bechtel Group, the well-known engineering-procurement-construction firm with over $20 billion in annual revenues and about 45000 employees. Vipin led the development and implementation of Bechtel Group’s world-class supply-chain systems that are being used by many organizations and thousands of users worldwide. In his career, Vipin has delivered industrial-strength software applications using state-of-art technologies in various technical and management roles for diverse client organizations like Exxon, DuPont, Monstanto, Motorola, Ford, CIBA-Geigy (Novartis), SC Johnson, Saudi Aramco, Lucky-Goldstar, GE, Posco, Alcan, Comalco, Thelen, and the U.S. EPA.

Vipin has a BS/MS/PhD in Engineering. During his academic and research career, Vipin developed innovative artificial intelligence applications for advanced process control and earned many national and international awards.
