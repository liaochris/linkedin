Daniyil Onufrishyn's Summary

My design aesthetic consists of concept-heavy thinking, with an emphasis on the experience segment of every project. Utilizing strategic thinking to execute engaging and innovative solutions to complex design problems, while producing interdisciplinary conceptual work that speaks to design matters, while communicating in a visual sense. Paired with experimental exploration of multiple mediums and alternative ways of printing products. Multi tasking several projects at a time, developing numerous solutions to a problem. Working in fast paced environments, with solid deadlines to meet. Strong Conceptual Development & Creative Direction skills, fast and able to work under pressure, capable of doing a diverse range of styles other than my own.

Specialties: Branding, Consulting, Advertising, Editorial, Creative Direction, Presentation Design, Production, Front End, Motion Design, Print Design.
