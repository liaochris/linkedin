Toby Beresford's Summary

Digital stategist and planner with social media and gamification specialism. Founder of Leaderboarded.com performance management platform.

I've designed and delivered over 100 apps, both utilities and games, for both B2B and B2C over the past 20 years working in digital.

Career hits have included: Leaderboarded (over 100k players), Buzz the Friend Quiz (500k players on FB), poweRBrands (award winning graduate recruitment game), Tube Warning (FB hackathon winner), PDR (gamified Performance Development Review process for senior management at BAE in 1999), DermaClinic (early telemedicine tool) and DCUTT (Automated Test tool).

Toby Beresford's Experience

CEO

Leaderboarded

Public Company; 1-10 employees; Computer Software industry

March 2012 – Present (2 years 8 months) London, United Kingdom

Leaderboarded offers the ability to build better performance through scorekeeping.

I started creating a list of the Top 40 gamification gurus in November 2011 based on multiple digital metrics. It was a great success for both reach and engagement but was taking too much work to produce each month so I created a tool to automate the process. This tool is Leaderboarded and I am now bringing to the market for others to use to save time when scorekeeping for performance management.
