Andy Smit's Experience

Founder & CEO

Furlocity, Inc

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Internet industry

March 2012 – Present (2 years 7 months) Greater San Diego Area

Furlocity, Inc. (www.furlocity.com) is a dynamic online platform for the pet-friendly travel, pet boarding and pet accommodations industries. Pet owners can now search, compare, review and instantly book their next pet-friendly vacation, pet boarding facility or favorite veterinarian all in one sitting.

Our goal is to finally bridge the gap for which exist in the current pet services industry between both consumers (pet owners) and trusted pet accommodations providers (pet boarding facilities, veterinarians & pet-friendly hotels/travel), thus ultimately creating a lasting symbiotic relationship.

We are a company built on a mission to help pet owners with the uncertainty that may arise when choosing a trusted pet accommodations provider for their beloved pets. We are pet owners ourselves so we understand and that is why we created Furlocity.com for you and your furry family members!
