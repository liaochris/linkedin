John Soares' Summary

Do you need an experienced freelance writer for a test bank, student study guide, lecture outlines, or other textbook supplement?

I’ve got you covered. I love to help college students learn better and college instructors teach and test better, and that’s why I’ve specialized in this field for over 20 years.

I’ve created nearly 300 textbook supplements and ancillaries in the following subjects:

* History
* Political Science
* Geography
* Environmental Science
* Geology
* Meteorology
* Biology
* Education
* Sociology
* Business
* Communication
* Social Media

And I’ve done nearly every type of supplement/ancillary:

* Assessment questions/test questions
* Lecture outlines in PowerPoint
* Instructor’s manuals
* Student study guides and companion websites
* Internet/article exercises
* Map exercises
* Video scripts

My clients include:

* Cengage Learning
* Pearson
* McGraw-Hill
* Macmillan
* Private universities
* Nonprofit organizations

HERE'S WHY YOU SHOULD HIRE ME:

1. I have extensive experience with all major types of textbook supplements. I’ve been doing this for a long time!

2. I’m a full-time writer who can handle large and complex projects.

3. I’m detail oriented and committed to ensuring I do high-quality work that is accurate and corresponds perfectly with learning materials.

4. I have a broad academic background that spans social sciences, earth sciences, and life sciences. I hold a master’s degree in political science from the University of California at Davis (with additional coursework in history), a bachelor’s degree in biochemistry from UC Davis (with many life-science courses), and an A.S. degree in Engineering (which included geography and earth science courses).

5. I’m very comfortable using Bloom’s taxonomy to write test and quiz questions that assess all facets of a student’s knowledge and understanding of the material.

6. Most importantly, I am committed to making my clients very happy. See my recommendations for verification.

  • 75 Hikes in California’s Mount Shasta and Lassen Volcanic National Park Regions, revised edition

    • The Mountaineers Books
    • 2006
    Authors: John Soares

    This the only book that covers in detail the spectacular hiking trails of northern California's volcano country: Mount Shasta and environs, Lassen Volcanic National Park, and the areas in between.

    When you get this book, you have the one complete hiking guide that takes you to the most beautiful and accessible trails of this stunning region:

    * active evidence of the volcanic nature of California’s Cascades: steaming fumaroles and bubbling mudpots at Bumpass Hell in Lassen Volcanic National Park
