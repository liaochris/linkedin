Fern Gale Estrow's Summary

A leader in her profession, public speaker and educator, Fern Gale Estrow, a Registered Dietitian, is a member of the faculty at Queens College of the City University of New York and founder of the FGE Food & Nutrition Team. She is the Director of the Dietetic Internship Program and Professor lecturing in the Department of Family, Nutrition and Exercise Sciences. She and her company consult to agencies, organizations, educational institutions and communities. Work includes but is not limited to providing policy and program assessment, development, training, implementation, management and evaluation. Areas of focus include child nutrition, obesity, breastfeeding, nutrition education and counseling, case/data management, anti-poverty/hunger initiatives, media literacy, menu planning, food service management and food systems.

The FGE Team seeks to improve health and quality of life, with access to healthy food and safe water for all. A systems/team approach is employeed, working on policy and program across the life cycle. The intersection of health, nutrition education, agriculture, healthy food access, conservation, poverty and hunger are brought together to identify problems and solutions.
