Healthin30.com Founder, Huffingtonpost Writer, Sharecare Editorial Advisory Board, Health Expert
April 2006 – Present (8 years 7 months)

Barbara Ficarra, RN, BSN, MPA is an award-winning journalist, founder Healthin30.com, media broadcaster, medical blogger, freelance writer, international speaker, health educator, consultant, media trainer and registered nurse. Barbara’s blogs and articles on Healthin30 feature a nurse's perspective and practical trusted advice on personal health focusing on prevention, patient empowerment and healthy living. She covers a variety of health topics including social media, telehealth, patient engagement, doctor|nurse team and professional development. She is creator, executive producer and host of the Health in 30® radio show, a live 30-minute program that brings listeners the latest health and medical news and information with leading medical experts, and empowers listeners to take charge of their health. [The Health in 30® Radio Show is presently on hiatus.] Barbara is a featured writer at The Huffington Post, contributor DoctorOz.com and many other health sites. She is a member of the Advisory Board and consumer health educator for Sharecare, and Advisory Board member for the University San Francisco for HealthIT. She is an administrative supervisor at a level 2 trauma center and covers multiple medical and surgical units; including critical care, ED, oncology, cardiology, psychiatric in-patient unit & women’s and children’s units including the pediatric emergency room. She is an international speaker at many professional conferences and her presentations range from patient engagement|empowerment, health care social media, and telehealth to professional development and media training. She has been quoted as an expert in health care for multiple journals and magazines. Barbara also is experienced in video production as host and executive producer for Web/TV. She is one of the leading nurse voices in health, her focus is patient empowerment, patient engagement, health care social media, telehealth and healthy living.
