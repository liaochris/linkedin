Background

Summary

You work massive hours for yourself or someone else. Question… can you quit the grind and really start enjoying life? Feel younger, look fantastic, eat your overhead? Build a financial legacy, while doing this!

Ten years working at a newspaper, the bug of entrepreneurship bit me. I learned the art of dog grooming, and was a shop owner, until I discovered the power and leverage of real estate. Sales, brokerage and appraising was fun for 5 years, but missing something, I went to college at age 38. An art degree and 10 years as a graphic design instructor, I also owned a mural painting business. Fun merging art with real estate and investing with rental properties and fixer uppers. Also fun!

2008, escaped the winters of Pennsylvania, and moved to Florida to be near family for the first time in 35 years.

Living in Paradise… and too young for SS. A job, a business or a great savings account were our options. Started a mobile dog grooming business. Wildly successful, but trading time for dollars is not fun. That nagging feeling returned...

The power of the internet, networking, leverage and residual income was the answer. Network marketing provided the vehicle.

I felt like a slug with no energy... my pants were getting tighter and larger, and I couldn't imagine the next 20-30 years feeling this way. My friend introduced me to a company and WOW, after starting on the products I got my life back. 40 pounds lighter, 3 pant sizes smaller, and more energy than the Energizer bunny. And retirement… ahh!

2014 was time to sell my dog business and jump into network marketing as endorsed by Robert Kiyosaki and many others.

I'm in a wonderful "quality-of-life" company creating a beautiful lifestyle.

If any of my story resonates with you, contact me! I'm a LION, open networker. I never spam or I.D.K. All connections accepted. Even if you're not interested in my company, I'd love to chat with you for a few minutes on the phone or Skype. Love helping others reach their goals.
