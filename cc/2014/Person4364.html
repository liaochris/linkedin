Monroe Yohey's Summary

Monroe James Yohey is an International Entrepreneur & Business Consultant focusing on Startup & International Ventures. He is Founder and Creative Director at Image is Reality™. Areas of expertise include Globalization, Strategy, Creativity & Innovation. Monroe maintains strong interest in advertising, media production, computers, electronics, networks, green technology, infrastructure, city design, energy, and robotics.

Image is Reality™ develops & executes strategic plans/media productions to assist organizations to formulate & spread ideas, information, and stories. Other services include advertising, brand consultancy, and innovation planning utilizing a vast distributed network of business & creative talents worldwide.

Monroe is a Polymath and Thinker and is Interested in How ideas are Formed and Spread.

Monroe has a B.A. in Art History with a History minor as well as a substantial background in Anthropology, International Business, and International Relations.

Monroe holds the Advanced Certificate through the Wine Spirits Education Trust (WSET). He also holds a Certificate of completion from the Artisanal Master of Cheese Program. He conducts wine tastings and events when time and scheduling allows.

Business Specialties: Dynamic Strategy, Lateral Thinking, Globalization, Creativity, Innovation, Disruption, Digital Storytelling, Advertising, Internet Marketing, Social Media Marketing, Distributed Agency Models, Inbound Marketing, Business Management, Economics, Corporate Culture, Change Management, Business Valuation Assessment, Venture Capital, Startup Company Formation & Operations, Currency & Debt Crises, Corporate Governance, International Tax Strategy & Implications, Energy, Robotics, Stock, Options & Currency Trading.

Notes*
Image is Reality™ Strategic Consulting and Advisory Services are Project Based Contracts Determined by Specified Objectives and Value Added Outputs. Other services are hybrid billings.
