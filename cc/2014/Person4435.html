Nicolas Buache's Summary

I am a Swiss citizen, living in France and working in IT for an international company based in Geneva.

I have more than 15 years of experience in IT, I obtained different technical certifications, and I own a Master of Science in Information Systems Management. The University of Liverpool’s online MSc in ISM offers the knowledge and skills traditionally associated with an MBA, but in the context of fast-moving developments in technology.

I have strong technical knowledge on computer and server infrastructure, with a very good overview on security, network, and telephony solutions, and a good understanding about the business. I like to search, define and implement new solutions on various environments, share my knowledge and experience with others, and take the lead to manage people.

I have always been able to learn new technologies or infrastructures very fast, and I can easily understand business requirements or constraints. I am also able to adapt the discussion in function of the audience (technical, manager, business).

My strength is to always have a global overview on all the different technologies, get the vision from the business when defining or troubleshooting a solution, and be creative to find global solutions that are easy to implement and to maintain. This helped me a lot when I had to work with retail, manufactures, and logistics in a SAP environment.

I am always open to talk about new challenges. Feel free to contact me if you have a very good opportunity for a new challenge.
