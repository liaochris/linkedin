John McTigue's Summary

Passionate about inbound marketing, the content-rich strategy for attracting qualified sales leads and converting them into customers. My partner and I have built a leading inbound marketing agency to serve growing businesses, primarily in the technology, healthcare and specialty manufacturing sectors. I've been in marketing for 30+ years, but now I am excited about leveraging the latest marketing strategies to generate new business for our clients.

You can read much more about inbound marketing and our services from our website and blog at www.kunocreative.com.

Specialties: inbound marketing, content marketing, demand generation, lead nurturing, marketing automation, KPIs and marketing analytics, closed loop marketing.
