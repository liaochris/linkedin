Christopher Vo's Summary

Robotics researcher, skilled programmer, and experienced IT director.

Currently researching robustness, scalability, and performance in robotic motion planning. Significant experience with robotics software, electronics, and hardware. Developed both humanoid robots and unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs). More than 12 years work experience with IT management, as well as Linux and Windows server administration.
