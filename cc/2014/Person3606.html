Jo-Lynne Shane's Summary

Jo-Lynne Shane is a professional lifestyle blogger, freelance writer, brand advocate and community manager living in the suburbs of Philadelphia.

Named one of Nielsen Online's 50 most powerful and influential women in social media, Jo-Lynne's open and genuine writing style has created an ever-expanding and inclusive community online and on the local and national blogging scenes.

Jo-Lynne is known for her authenticity and humor and positive presence in the blogosphere. With 5000 subscribers and over 200,000 monthly page views, Musings of a Housewife is a personal memoir and lifestyle blog about food, fitness, fashion, family travel and the busy life of a work-at-home mom. Find wholesome recipes, information on gluten-free living, weekly meal plans, fitness inspiration, relatable style and beauty posts for the suburban housewife, and reviews of family travel destinations.

Jo-Lynne is also the author of the children's story The Uninvited Houseguest and The Everything Gluten-Free Breakfast And Brunch Cookbook (Adams Media). She enjoys representing like-minded brands and is currently an ambassador for Krusteaz baking products. Jo-Lynne has been a member of Target's Inner Circle of select social media influencers and an ambassador for Udi's Gluten Free Foods. She has made appearances for Shout Laundry, Huggies, Bloomingdales and Neiman Marcus. Jo-Lynne has been featured on 95.7 BEN FM and in Cosmopolitan Magazine. Her recipes have appeared in Redbook, Elle, Cosmopolitan and Southern Living magazines and on DoctorOz.com. She recently appeared on the Dr. Oz Show, and she writes a monthly recipe column for DoctorOz.com.

As the community manager for the Philly Social Media Moms, Jo-Lynne helps provide community, support and education for 230+ local mom bloggers and social media professionals. PSMM seeks to empower members with information and resources to run successful blogs and grow their social media presence.
