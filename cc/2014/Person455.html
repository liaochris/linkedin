The assessment tools we use:
The Core Values Index™ (CVI™) is a revolutionary human assessment that provides a description of the innate, unchanging nature of an individual and provides a highly accurate and reliable picture of the core motivational drivers of any person. CVI™ measures innate preference (suitability) rather than ability or adaptability.

The Energy Leadership Index™ (ELI™) assessment is an online instrument which measures how an individual perceives and approaches work and life and reacts to various circumstances and experiences. The ELI™ graphically illustrates an individual’s perception of the world in a calm state and what happens to that perception when stress is introduced.
