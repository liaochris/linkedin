Arthur Chapman's Summary

I am a Broker & Partner with William Raveis Chapman Enstone. We are a full service real estate agency serving all of Rhode Island & southeastern MA.

We are part of the 8th largest real estate company in the US, encompassing all aspects of real estate. From mortgage to new home construction, moving services to insurance.

We have 5 offices in RI, 65 Bellevue Avenue, Newport, 423 Hope Street, Bristol, 235 Main Street, East Greenwich, 203 South Main Street, Providence, RI and 911 Main Street, Westport, MA.

I was born and raised in Newport, RI and spent time in commercial and residential real estate in New York City before returning home to become a Rhode Island realtor initially based in Providence and then in Newport County.

Specialties: Full service real estate, mortgage, insurance agency throughout New England with connections throughout the US and internationally. RI real estate, vacation rentals, luxury waterfront properties.
