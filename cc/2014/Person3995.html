Jake Taylor's Summary

Jake Taylor is aimClear’s powerhouse online marketing production specialist. From video and sound editing to ad production, research and ecommerce product feed management, Jake is responsible for a variety of back-end projects that keep the aimClear wheel spinning. Capitalizing on his technical, creative and analytic skills, he also supports the company’s groundbreaking proprietary predictive-modeling software, personaDriver™.

A Duluth native, Jake graduated from University of Minnesota Duluth with a BBA in Marketing. When he’s not perfecting his online marketing skills, Jake can be found running, skiing, playing disc golf and enjoying low-budget movies and the occasional foreign film.
