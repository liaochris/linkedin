Connie Erickson's Summary

Specialties

I specialize in listing and selling Door County real estate. I sell vacation and retirement properties including residential & waterfront homes, condominiums, golfing communities, vacant land, and commercial real estate.

My passion for real estate shows both in my selling success and through my enthusiastic approach to teaching, speaking, and training REALTORS. My motivational speaking and real estate training is high energy, full of humor, and provides tons of useful information.

Connie Erickson's Experience

Broker/Owner

Door County Realty, Inc.

1985 – Present (29 years)

Connie, along with her husband, Bob, is co-owner and Vice President of Door County Realty, Inc. The Nov. 2009 issue of the Wisconsin Real Estate Magazine quoted Connie in "Staging for Success", in 2008 Connie was featured in the book, "All In A Day's Work for Real Estate Agents", Moxie Magazine (Autumn 2008) featured Connie in their article entitled "Second Homes" and in May 2004 Chicago Magazine featured Connie in "The Perfect Weekend Home-A Guide for Buyers". Connie was the subject of a CRS Magazine article in 2002 about top producers and their marketing and she was featured in REALTOR Magazine’s 'Top 300 Residential Salespeople,' 2001.
