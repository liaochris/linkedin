Ron Rapp's Summary

Gulfstream IV-SP charter pilot and CFI based in the greater Los Angeles area. 7000+ hours total time with extensive tailwheel, aerobatic, formation, experimental, multi-engine, radial, and turbine experience. Experienced aerobatic competitor and national judge. CFI-I and MEI with ratings for SE, multi, gliders, and sea planes. Current government secret clearance. Current CDPR (cropduster) certification.

Specialties: Part 135 pilot (Gulfstream IV/SP/300/400). Specialize in high performance aerobatic aircraft (especially Extra 300 and Pitts) and Cirrus/Columbia glass panel instruction. Expert with G1000, Avidyne, GNS430/530, CNX80, and more. Also fly a wide variety of specialty aircraft, including RVs, Wacos/Stearmans, T-34s, T-6s, Eagles, etc.
