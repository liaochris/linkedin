Anne Landon's Summary

In my higher education role, I connect accounting, business, economics, and criminal justice students with employers to gain real-world experience and help make a smooth transition from backpack to briefcase. If you are interested in mentoring a Lycoming College intern or learning more about our programs, please email me at landon@lycoming.edu.

Lycoming College students and alumni: I invite you to join me in the Lycoming College Alumni LinkedIn group. The group is open to all students and alumni and is my recommendation as the best resource for networking with the college community on LinkedIn. See you there!
