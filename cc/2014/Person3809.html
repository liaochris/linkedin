Ken Hobbick MBA, PMP, CRA, SSGB, CNMT's Summary

Experienced, dedicated, results oriented leader wit sharp wit who happens to be a MBA graduate with 15+ years healthcare experience in radiology. Combination of managing departments and managing mulitple complex projects ranging from $20K - $2M in budget. Business development projects include starting 3 nuclear cardiology labs and PET/CT imaging department for oncology practice. I seek ways to leverage technology and improve opeartional process such as implementing Computerize Physician Order Entry (CPOE) and Bar Code Medication Verification projects while using Six Sigma principles of LEAN and Voice of Customer to ensure project success and exceed stakeholder expectations.

I posses excellent interpersonal communicaiton and high emotional intelligence to effectively manage departments or complex projects. I have been fortunate to work with extraordinary people and accumulate experience in a wide array of environments ranging from world renowned multi-center non-profit hospitals, large academic physician practices, small community hospitals, cardiology practices, oncology practices, pre-IPO imaging center, and successful independent contractor. Including enough life lessons learned and illustrations to write a book when I finally find the time or receive a generous offer from a book publisher.
