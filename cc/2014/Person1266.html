Background

Summary

Jonas Fredin is on paper a Creative Technologist at Creuna - Scandinavia's leading full-service digital agency, but he spend equal amount of time working with everything from concept development to interaction- and graphic design and front- and back-end development. He has played a key part in the creation of Creuna's "User Experience Process", also called the "UX-process". An agile work methodology unlike the more established "Waterfall-method". Jonas is also behind the statement which claims that a modern creative team should have a Front-end Developer and a Graphic Designer in its core, not necessarily an Art Director and a Copywriter.

Jonas completed his Bachelors Degree in Graphic Design in 2011 and started working with Creuna from that day on. Three semesters in, he started teaching at the very same University College - one and a half year before his own graduation day. Today his lectures covers responsive development and basic JavaScript. He is also in charge of the Illustrator- and InDesign introduction courses for new students.

On top of this, Jonas started working with a concept called rethink conf in early 2011 together with his good friend Arnt Eriksen. rethink conf is a bi-annual conference focusing on strategic business usage of digital marketing and social media, where they want to bring together the leading international experts within marketing and provide extensive networking with the goal of highlighting and exploring emerging challenges and opportunities. Over the years this has grown to be much more than just a conference and led to the creation of other unique concepts focusing on the very same principles; rethink session, rethink dialog and rethink mag to name a few.

Jonas has worked with clients and brands such as: Avinor, Bodø, Coop, Cubus, DNV, Dressmann, FHL, Hydro, Jotun, KIMS, Kraft Food, MIX, Myrkdalen, NARF, Nordea, Posten, Rottefella, Ruter, Sigdal, Sparebank 1, Storebrand, Stortinget, Telenor, Tropicana and Umoe Restaurant Group.
