Clark Linehan's Summary

Linehan Photography works with ad agencies, design firms, directly with client in-house advertising and publications departments, or directly with business owners to provide photography for use on the web, in print and for trade show displays. We work primarily with clients who need images illustrating interior and exterior architecture, food, manufacturing, industrial and consumer product, jewelry, semiconductors and high tech/defense products, manufacturing and/or services. We specialize in hurry-up; the deadline was yesterday projects and understands that our clients are often faced with demands and deadlines that aren't readily or economically serviced by others. Our capabilities include full digital photography capabilities including very extensive lighting capabilities that can handle just about any studio or location demand, from very small product to large industrial locations. We provide complete in-house Photoshop retouching and image compositing services for all categories of web, print and display materials and can also provide production and prepress services to in-house publication departments and graphic designers. Our goal is to service clients who need creativity on time and on budget when both are at a premium. We also partner with a network of other professionals to provide full services for web, print and display design needs.

Specialties

Digital photography, web, print and display production services for brochures, catalogs, trade show display and web use. Current clients include architectural, consumer product, defense, food, industrial, medical, product and technology companies. We work in studio and travel world-wide for our clients. We specialize in working with a range of clients including small startups, design firms, independent art directors, and in-house corporate advertising and publications departments.
