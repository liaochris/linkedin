Background

Summary

For more than fifteen years I have been assisting companies, families and individuals in resolving their legal issues with the agencies of the United States government responsible for administering the immigration laws.

I look forward to another ten years of ensuring family unity, assisting businesses in legally finding qualified workers, and protecting the rights of refugees and others who choose to live in the United States.

Specialties: The New Hampshire Code of Professional Conduct prohibits claims of legal "specialization" except in Admiralty & Maritime law and Patent Law. My usual area of practice is Immigration Law
