Tim Girvin's Summary

I'm looking for stimulating partnerships -- for intellectual and business alliances -- for companies that are trying to enter the US markets effectively, or for organizations that are considering exploring the US, Europe or Asia. We strive, using imaginative collaborative "thinkshops", with our partners, to build the business of our clients, internationally, through balanced and thoughtful marketing / creative, coupled with empowered, collaborative design. Tim Girvin's Experience

Founder and Chief Creative Officer

GIRVIN | Strategic Branding & Design

Sole Proprietorship; 11-50 employees; Design industry

January 1976 – Present (38 years 9 months)

Lead the firm through 35 years of global work, in all media, to clients ranging from Nintendo and Shiseido, to Microsoft and Apple, Paramount Studios to Wynn Resorts.

I personally participate in all holistic identity development programs for our clients, messaging and branding strategic work, soul finding and brand storytelling, as well as motion picture and entertainment marketing design.

I work nationally and internationally to create opportunities for our teams, as well as our clients.

I lead the firm in strategy, personality expressions and hybrid integration of messages in all media.

I advise a team of 20+ Girvin resources, team members and consulting colleagues.

I teach, in corporate settings, creativity, branding innovations and storytelling, emotional and sensate content in brand media and personal "human brand" work.

I lead nearly all Girvin branding, corporate leadership, innovation, soul-finding and positioning workshops.

Tim Girvin's Honors and Awards

  • Eagle Scout

    Boy Scouts of America, Troup 313, Inland Empire Council, Spokane
    • October 1968

    The Eagle Scout award is the highest honor of the Boy Scouts of America. My Eagle Scout community project was the discovery of an old concrete watering trough on Grand Avenue, used to water horses, coming up the Grand Avenue from downtown Spokane. I designed, hand lettered, built and installed a roadside signing treatment that remained in place for many years until it was mowed down by a winter snow removal truck. The watering trough was installed originally in 1902.
