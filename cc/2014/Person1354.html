Dan Kurani's Experience

Leading Millennial insight firm that acquired Thumb in 2013. Helped mesh the massive data generated by the Thumb community and leading insights from the Ypulse team into a cohesive SaaS dashboard that grew syndicated revenues by 300%.

Founder and CEO

Thumb

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Internet industry

May 2010 – June 2013 (3 years 2 months) New York

Launched and built Thumb into the leader in the mobile opinion space. Thumb had millions of downloads, raised $6MM in venture capital, generated over 3 billion opinions, yielded the highest monthly usage time on mobile next to Facebook, and was featured by CNN, TechCrunch, Ad Age, etc.
