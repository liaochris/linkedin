Jeff Shjarback, MBA's Summary

Digital Marketing professional with over 10 years of marketing, sales and account management experience. Jeff has won multiple awards for sales, marketing and service throughout his career, and he has a strong passion for technology, marketing and sales.

Jeff has operated several of his own websites, in addition to working as an Internet Marketing Consultant, Internet Marketing Manager, Digital Advertising Strategist and Paid Media Manager for various digital agencies. Jeff helps businesses distill big data into actionable marketing insights and helps companies/organizations to increase leads & sales through their website to ultimately achieve their business marketing goals.

Jeff is a certified Google Advertising Professional. He graduated from Elon University with a degree in Business Administration, and completed his MBA from Pfeiffer University.
