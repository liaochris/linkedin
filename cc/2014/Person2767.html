Dennis Goshorn's Summary

As a seasoned professional in higher education I have had the opportunity to work with a variety of students and professionals.

I am, also a history enthusiast, but there’s more to me than that. While it’s true that I’m usually reading about history I’m passionate about other things as well.

I am also a tech-junkie and I am "living the dream" as a Sales Specialist at PeachMac, the largest Apple Specialist in the nation. My store is in Macon, Georgia.

I think the answer to "Tell us about yourself" can be summed up with the analogy of a three-legged stool—each leg is integral to the stool and each is tied to a quote.

1. Jesus said, "You search the Scriptures because you think that in them you have eternal life; and it is they that bear witness about me, yet you refuse to come to me that you may have life." John 5:39, 40—reminds me that I'm just a sinner; I need a Savior; I need grace.

2. “Sooner or later we all discover that the important moments in life are not the advertised ones, not the holidays, not the birthdays, the graduations, the weddings, not the great goals achieved. The real milestones are quieter. They come to the door of memory unannounced, stray dogs that amble in, sniff around a bit and simply never leave. Our lives are measured by these.” It’s hard to pin-point exactly when we first noticed the “stray dogs” that came into our lives unannounced and have never left. These former Berry students have become our family, complete with traditions. Three of those 14 are “second generation,” or our adopted grandchildren! This is who I am; I build lasting relationships; deep relationships

3. The third leg in this analogy is this quote from a letter I received a few months ago from a former Head Resident: "Your godly influence and leadership in our lives has traveled well outside of 4 years and a college campus. You cannot know, this side of eternity, the difference you have made" This isn't just a job to me, it's my calling.

Dennis Goshorn's Experience

Sales Specialists

PeachMac

Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Retail industry

April 2014 – Present (6 months) Macon, Georgia

I am new to this role as a sales specialist. I don’t see myself as simply a salesperson. I am a resource for people. Listening is where it all begins—really listening to what the customer wants and needs. This means that I have to know the products so well that as they are describing their needs I have solutions popping into my head.

I think that sales is all about relationships and building trust. That can be difficult when the relationships are short-lived—so I have to exemplify a commitment to excellence in customer service and communication. Customers are going to run the gamut from tech-junkies to novices; I have to relate to all and make all feel comfortable.

Specific duties:
—making customers comfortable no matter their level of technical knowledge or expertise
—build pertinent solutions that meet customer needs
—build customer loyalty by explaining to them all the ways PeachMac can support them before, during and after the sale

Interests:
