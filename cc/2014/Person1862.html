Background

Summary

In 2003 I started an online business concerning sweepstakes. With venture capital and these activities, we started Clansman, an internet investment company, in 2007. Clansman builds valuable business systems by investing money, knowledge and resources in young, profit-making internet marketing initiatives. My role as co-director and business developer is to create new substantial business and to optimize and grow existing projects.

Specialties:My specialties are performance based / affiliate marketing, e-mailmarketing, optimizing websites and businesses, and developing creative business concepts. Creating profitable situations for all interested parties is one of my goals in doing business, but the most important aspect for me is having fun.

Experience

Co-Founder / Director

Clansman
July 2007 – Present (7 years 4 months)

Clansman helps advertisers to achieve their online goals. We invent, create, manage and promote creative online marketing campaigns to generate leads. We also develop and manage our own media; around 1.5 million Dutch/Belgium newsletter subscribers and several discount, sweepstake, freebie and travel websites. We work 100% performance based, so advertisers only pay us when we generated more income for them. We have a great team of 40 professionals. (november 2011)

Clansman is listed #5 in the 'Benelux Technology Fast 50' in 2011. We're busy rolling out our business in Europe.

To help the business grow even faster, we integrated Sizl into Clansman, another company which I co-founded.
