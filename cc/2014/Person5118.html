Background

Summary

An experienced business leader with expertise in leading and transforming organisations, structuring large complex propositions and maximising the delivery of shareholder value.

Has served on the boards of several companies with experience of Private Equity backed investments including start-ups, turnarounds, acquisitions (M&A), disposals and JVs. He was rated #45 in the Top 100 Angel Investors to Follow on Twitter and is an investor and advisor with four UK-based businesses in industries as varied as automotive, recruitment, advertising and sport.

Worked as a consultant with clients across Europe, East Asia and in the US, principally in the technology, telecoms, media, utilities and automotive sectors, advising them on performance improvement, cost reduction, governance, risk management and
business change.

Holds an MBA and is a master practitioner in Nero Linguistic Programming (NLP).

Has been a Partner in three of the largest professional service firms with a proven record of delivering strong businesses performance combined with innovative leadership.

Known for: developing the original Bluetooth proposition back in '95, launching mm02 - the UK's leading mobile telco in '01, co-creating PwC’s ground-breaking shareholder value management tool and leading its thinking on strategic competitive advantage.

Specialities: business transformation, technology-driven business performance, customer and brand
management, digital innovation, multi-platform web and social media, financial/investment strategies, contract negotiation, complex P&L management.
