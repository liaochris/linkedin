Background

Summary

Concept Smoke Screen have a deserved reputation as the best security smoke manufacturer in the world. Our history can be traced back 40 years when we designed, manufactured and installed the first ever security smoke system - to protect gold bullion vaults.

Since then our techniques, engineering know-how and processes have evolved to produce the best security fogging systems available, capable of producing an impenetrable barrier of dense fog in a instant. This loss prevention technique has proven effective time and time again.

Don't forget to look at our website and YouTube channel and Like, Follow and +1 us!

Specialties: security fog, security fogging, security smoke, smoke screen, smokescreen, retail security, loss prevention, business security
