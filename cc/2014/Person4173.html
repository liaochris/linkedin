Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry

February 2001 – Present (13 years 8 months)

Matchstick is an independent social media marketing agency.

We believe in the power of consumer word of mouth, and we bring that perspective to all of our work.

Our guiding philosophy is that the most powerful selling of products and ideas takes place not marketer to consumer, but consumer to consumer.

Matchstick is a charter member of WOMMA (The Word of Mouth Marketing Association).
