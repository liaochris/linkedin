Tarnya Damen's Summary

I’m an accomplished business process and service delivery/service management professional with 15+ years experience in team management, business analysis, process re-engineering and optimisation, incident and problem management and customer service delivery.

Career successes include successfully developing integrated business processes to improve service levels and customer service in front line IT support to consistently achieve monthly SLA KPI’s. I was the key stakeholder and major contributor of business data and processes for the successful outsourcing of infrastructure service support at Western Power. At the Independent Market Operator I was the lead project manager for the successful outsourcing of infrastructure service support and final award of contract to an external vendor. The above projects saw a high level of key business information input and management from myself and involved considerable business analysis, process mapping and engineering, tender reviews, contract development, stakeholder and vendor management. I have written businesses cases for approval to upgrade internal software solutions and seen the project through to completion, providing increased customer satisfaction and improved incident and problem reporting thereby reducing service outages and incident calls. All projects were implemented successfully, on time, to budget and provided added value to the businesses in terms of increased customer satisfaction, improved business processes and consistently achieving KPI’s.

I have hired, trained and mentored team members across diverse service delivery teams in IT service Desk, IT training, IT asset management & procurement and IT desktop support. I have a broad range of business knowledge and technical skills garnered from career experience in various industries that include Information Technology, Oil & Gas, Electrical Utilities, Geological & Mining, financial and stockmarket.
