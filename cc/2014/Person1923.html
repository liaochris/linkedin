Adam Kampmeyer's Summary

Industrial Designer, Artisan and Owner of Pittsburgh Custom Woodworks. Specializing in Architectural Millwork, Cabinetry and Furniture Design.

Prior to PCW, Lived and worked in the Chicago Metro Area with Creative Wood Concept as a lead designer and engineer. Where I designed high-end millwork, cabinetry and furniture for clients up and down Chicago’s Gold Coast, an surrounding suburbs.

Originally from Pittsburgh and a graduate of The Art Institute of Pittsburgh with a degree in Industrial Design and Technology, focus turned to creating functional and artistic solutions for home and office with a goal of personalized usability.

With a background In the arts as well as technology. I'm turning my direction toward innovative home solutions integrating furniture and millwork with computing and sustainability features.

Specialties

Ingenuity, Creative problem solving, Furniture Design, Human Factors & ergonomics, Acrhitectural Planing & Cabinetry, Lean Manufacturing, Estimating & Purchasing, Value engineering, Team building and management, Contracting, AutoCad, Formz, CabinetVison, Skecth-up, Wood, Metal & Plastices Fabrications
