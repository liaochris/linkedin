Charlie Breit's Summary

My passion is conceptualizing, enhancing and redefining digital experiences to surprise and delight consumers and re-imagine how businesses connect, engage and interact with their customers across web, social and mobile platforms. Success starts with your customer and building an experience for them that is easy to use, intuitive and provides value. Digital storytelling enhances traditional business models and provides an immense opportunity to attract and build a strong community and engagement around your product, service and company.

Leadership: Over 14 years experience working in digital marketing and over 6 years experience with direct reports. Led digital strategy at Fortune 500 companies: Allstate & Northwestern Mutual.

Online Experiences: Launched a new Allstate.com in 2013 to improve user experience and delivery of information across devices. Created AllstateTeenDriver.com and related content that improved teen engagement and won 2010 Media Vanguard Award for best overall experience on a website.

Mobile: Conceived and developed Allstate Digital Locker home inventory app and DigitalLocker.com, which received an A+ in Corporate Insight's P&C Monitor, and led development of m.allstate.com to improve consumer experience on mobile devices.

Interactive Tools: Led development of My Insurance Personality, What’s Your Stuff Worth?, Life's Unpredictable and Bumper to Bumper Basics to improve delivery of Allstate insurance content to consumers.

Optimization: Achieved an average lift of 67% in key online conversions over control across digital properties such as Allstate.com through leadership of multivariate testing program.

Branded Content: Produced and co-wrote "The Lines" web series as part of Allstate Teen Safe Driving program.

Writing Awards: Winner of 12th People’s Pilot and 3rd place in 12th Spec Scriptacular for original screenplay works.
