Background

Summary

Tanveer founder of Concert Care she is an experienced entrepreneur with almost two decades of experience in Information Technology in the healthcare arena. For many years, she has worked with small to large companies (fortune 500) including GE medical Systems. Her business experience, as well as, her expertise in handling growing companies, managing risk and developing international relationships is a keystone to ensure our long term commitment to clients and excellence of our services.
Ms Patel has been investor and adviser for multiple organizations, including Healthcare companies such as Comply MD and Secant Healthcare.
She founded and led CircleSource a Healthcare Software Development Company until its acquisition in 2010.
She has also served in the Board of HIMMS, was a member and Founding member, Vice President of the Red Crescent Clinic of Alabama and currently serves as President of Birmingham Venture Club.

Mrs. Patel is a gifted public speaker, and routinely gives talks related to strategic planning, start up development/growth, and international business.

Specialties: Strategic planning; Startup development/growth strategies; International business.
