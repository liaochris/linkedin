~ Robin's Bio ~

Robin is known as one of the Top 10 Resume Writers in the Bay Area - per Marky Stein; best selling author, Monster.com Interview Expert and contributor to the Wall Street Journal.

"Effective at dramatically turning around unemployed professionals chances of landing interviews - Robin has what it takes to change your resume into a powerful document that grabs an employer's attention and initiates action." says Stein.

As an expert resume writer and recruiter, Robin has been on both sides of the hiring process and has a keen understanding of the employer's needs and their approach to reviewing resumes.

Additionally, as a Career Consultant for a Fortune 500 career management firm, Robin has coached and trained thousands of individuals in their quest for new career opportunities. Her way of engaging with people at a personal level and her willingness to explore, learn and discover, allow her to tap into her inventive way of thinking to help clients uncover new opportunities in business and career endeavors.

With her complimentary blend of career expertise in HR Management, Recruiting, Staffing and Coaching; her educational focus in Psychology, as well as advanced coaches training from Coaches Training Institute, Robin is an ideal coach for anyone who is open to creative possibility and possesses a forward-thinking mindset.
