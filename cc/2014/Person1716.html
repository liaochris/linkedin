Shannon Stange's Summary

I am in my mid 40’s, am married and have a 25 year old daughter (I know I look younger and yes my photo is recent). Maybe I was blessed with good genes but I am kept young at heart by all the younger people in my social circles.

I am concerned for those entrapped in modern day slavery and those lacking economic opportunity. I am a strong supporter of Kiva.org where I lend money to entrepreneurs in developing nations.

Born in northern BC, raised in Whistler and Vancouver and educated in both BC and Alberta, I have an understanding of both urban and rural ways of relating to people and doing business which has helped me in my business relationships.

As an entrepreneur I have developed a broad range of skills over the years that encompass writing, sales, marketing, finance, project management and construction.

In my early years I showed a flair for building. From designing and drafting to swinging a hammer and getting it built. I loved my blue collar roots. But, I understood the key role that sales and marketing play in building companies so in the early years of my career I focused on jobs that developed these skills. Then I ventured out into the world of finance to round out my business building skills before launching into building and construction.

For the past decade I have been working on construction projects around the lower mainland starting with custom homes and then exclusively on commercial projects. The challenge with this was that project management took me away from the sales and marketing which I had enjoyed so much. In a move designed to bring all my interests into alignment I have moved into Business Development in the Construction, Oil & Gas and Insurance sectors.
