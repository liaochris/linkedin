Michelle Rollins' Summary

Michelle’s southern manner belies her passion and sharp focus for retail branding and design. A quick trip to the store can easily turn into hours of design critique. As a creative lead at Periscope, Michelle is helping shape brands through retail promotional design, new product launches to strategic customer engagement in both print and digitally integrated work. Always quick with a smile, Michelle has genuine talent for winning over friends in seconds. Just like the brands she works on.
