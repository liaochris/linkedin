Lin Tam's Summary

Lin has vast experience producing, marketing and developing entertainment content across various media platforms. Her digital media experience has been recognized by the Producer's Guild of America, inducting her into the New Media Council. In the mobile gaming world, she was the Chief Creative Producer for "Slingshot Cowboy," which reached #1 spot in the All Games category and #2 spot in the Top Free App category in the US and had about 8M downloads overall.
