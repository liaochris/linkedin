The History and Mission of Our Company -

OHIO INTEL, a professional investigative agency, was established in 1988 by Michael L. Robertson, a retired Special Agent of the United States Secret Service, Department of the Treasury.

Our agency specializes in fraud investigation, criminal, civil and domestic related matters.

We provide litigation support and assist our clients in resolving their personal and business related problems.

Our office is located in North Canton, Ohio.

Specialties:Fraud, surveillance, background investigation, child custody and domestic related cases.
