Erik Haahr, COBIT Certified's Summary

Working to enable "The best decision possible" and to ensure "Better IT at a lower cost"!

Through his education and career, Erik Haahr has enjoyed being in the field of tension between technology and management. Business opportunities in new technology balanced with the potential risks interests him. The challenge is to find the optimal point in time to implement the right technology in order to maximize benefit in the shortest possible time‑span with an acceptable level of risk.

Erik Haahr has long experience within IT governance, enterprise architecture, information management, data warehousing, business process integration, business process reengineering and IT development methodologies. Within these areas Erik Haahr has proven his expertise as well as his management and leadership skills.

Erik Haahr has been working with methodology and architecture for a long time within utilities, education, government administration, pharmaceuticals and transportation. Erik is among the thought leaders within data warehousing and has been presenting several papers on related topics at major conferences throughout the European and Nordic regions.

Erik Haahr has very strong analytical skills and a good sense of humour. This combined with stamina and endurance gets the job done - and always with a smile. He communicate naturally with people at any level and from any background, and culture.

CobiT, ITIL, and Teradata certified consultant.
