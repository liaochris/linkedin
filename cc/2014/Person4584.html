Peggy de Jonge's Summary

Hi there,

Nice to meet you. My name is Peggy de Jonge. I'm an entrepreneur and legal advisor. And I have a passion for creativity. So what I like to do is help creative companies, organizations and entrepreneurs deal with their legal issues. I have a wide range of experience and I am an expert in translating complex legal issues into effective solutions. Most importantly, I speak and write in plain language, so you can easily understand what I'm talking about.

Combining my own creativity and network with my legal expertise to find the best possible solution for my clients is what makes me happy. That's why I love my job so much. Being an entrepreneur myself, I understand the issues you face better than your average law firm and I always keep a keen eye on budget and timing.

So if you need legal advice, especially anything related to Intellectual Property and (new) Media Law, shoot me an email to make an introductory appointment, free of charge.

Looking forward to meeting you!
