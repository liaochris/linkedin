Jay Eskenazi, Ph.D.'s Summary

Senior User Experience professional with 15+ years experience as a consultant, researcher, and manager of high performing user experience teams - helping to play a leadership role and create successful products with an intuitive, user centered design experience. Created User Experience Research Team at Expedia, Inc. to improve User Experience of Expedia.com, Expedia Corporate Travel, Supplier Extranet, and International Points of Sale (Japan, Australia, Canada, UK). As a consultant, I've been privileged to work with some of the most well known brands in the world including AMD, Microsoft, Hewlett-Packard, Dell, Publicis Group, Expedia, Hotwire, Hotels.com, eHarmony, Glacier Media, Clear, HouseValues/MarketLeader/Trulia, Sharp Healthcare, and many more.
