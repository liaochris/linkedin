Background

Summary

I have been a licensed real estate agent in Columbus Ohio since 1993. Over the years I have been fortunate to be involved in helping hundreds of people with their real estate needs. I have utilized the knowledge I have gained through my continued study of real estate and my real world experience learned from daily practice in the industry. My certifications range from certified new homes specialist, buyers certified representative, relocation specialist dealing with buyers and sellers, negotiations specialist and investment property counselor. I am also and active real estate investor and have been doing so for more than 15 years. I have over the years developed several training programs and have taught groups of agents’ different aspects of real estate marketing. I never forget that for the vast majority of my clients real estate will be their single largest investment.

Specialties: Relocation Certified, New Homes Certified, Negotiation Specialist, Integrity Selling Certified, ePro, SFR - "Short Sale and Foreclosure Resource" and real estate investment advisor.
