Linda M. Hanley's Summary

Real Estate in the Western Suburbs has been my full-time career for the past 30 years. I have a passion for helping people find the right home for them. I believe my clients are happy at the end because I worked with them to accomplish their goals. I know from experience that finding the right home takes time and patience. Many hours are put into the on-line search and then you cannot shortcut the process of actually going and seeing the real thing. Most buyers that I work with do not purchase the home they like best on-line.

When selling your home I inform you of the market conditions, the trends in your area and keep you up-to-date on the customer reaction. I hold periodic broker tours and Sunday open houses. I, personally, meet with you throughout the process. My main goal is always your satisfaction.

Please read my recommendations and see if I am the right agent for you!

Specialties

I specialize in offering you personal service throughout the transaction. I will always be available to answer your questions and guide you.
