Background

Summary

I'm a former television journalist with a passion for public speaking and connecting people. I love to write...in 140 characters - and longer formats, too. :)

At Syracuse University, I enjoy helping our alumni to maintain strong ties to our alma mater. I manage the LinkedIn group 'CuseConnect, making connections between SU students and alumni for career advice and professional development. I also oversee the Twitter handle @WorkingOrange, which features Syracuse University alumni sharing "day in the life" tweets and offering advice to students and fellow alumni. I use social media extensively (primarily as @kimincuse) and have helped our office to develop a strong presence on Twitter (@CareerSU and @WorkingOrange), Facebook, YouTube, and Pinterest. I also contribute to our office's blog, A Slice of Advice.

In addition to making connections both on and off campus and to teaching students the ins and outs of using LinkedIn, I also help to plan SUccess in the City networking events - perhaps in the city where you live! The events offer Syracuse University students and alumni the chance to connect face-to-face.

If you're a Syracuse University alum looking to hire students and/or fellow alumni, please reach out to me! We love sharing #HireOrange opportunities.

Specialties: Networking, alumni relations, alumni engagement, community building, public speaking/presentations, event planning, social media consulting, personal branding
