Background

Summary

Legal Insights, Business Enabler.

Experienced, results-oriented, values-based Executive and GC whose Legal Department's mission is to drive business results by ensuring the freedom to operate lawfully while promoting LinkedIn’s values and culture. Excel at navigating uncharted waters, building bridges and bringing parties together -- solving sticky wickets. Passionate about building high-performing teams, by walking the talk on culture and values.

Privileged to help mentor women professionals, pre-professionals, students as well as other general counsels, and advise companies that are scaling and tackling the evolving global privacy and data security landscapes. Committed to "doing good" and making a difference in our world professionally as well as through my avocations, whether through Girl Scouts, pro bono, or by lending a helping hand, whether locally or globally.

Prior to LinkedIn, I was SVP, GC and Secretary, for Nasdaq-listed SumTotal Systems, a talent management company. Previously, I was Vice President, Strategic Development and General Counsel, for Nasdaq and Singapore Stock Exchange listed Creative Labs, the company that brought multimedia to the PC with the Sound Blaster sound card. I earned my law degree from Berkeley’s Boalt Hall School of Law and started my legal career at the Silicon Valley technology law firm Cooley Godward, where I practiced corporate and employment law. I began my professional career as a schoolteacher in Anchorage, Alaska.

Experience

VP, General Counsel & Secretary

LinkedIn (LNKD)
July 2008 – September 2014 (6 years 3 months)Mountain View, California

Passionate about always ensuring our members come first and driving business results, as LinkedIn's first full-time attorney and General Counsel, I've built and led an awesome legal team -- through the early days in 2008 of 18M members, $78M revenue, 250 employees, to today's 300+M members, $2B revenue and 6000+ employees.

As a global legal team, we navigated the predictable and unpredictable -- dealing with issues faced by a hyper-growth, fast-paced, disruptive internet company. A few of the accomplishments I'm most proud of include earning and maintaining our members' trust, our IPO and follow-on offerings, navigating and helping shape the ever-evolving and conflicting global privacy and data security landscape, our offensive and defensive IP strategy, our values-based China launch, ensuring our members and the LinkedIn community have transparency into what data we collect and how we use that data, from both company and government perspectives, and, most importantly, enabling my fellow executives and colleagues to be great at what they do, by ensuring we avoid legal roadblocks. I'm also proud of pushing the envelope on pro-bono service, the "patent troll" tax, and "value-based legal fee" arrangements. Mostly, I just love avoiding and solving big legal, regulatory and policy issues on a global basis, thinking outside the box but being guided by in the box thinking, so that we can enable the business to achieve its goals.

Expertise: privacy and data security, working with governments and regulators, corporate (governance, public offerings, M&A), board advice (counsel to Board of Directors, and its governance, compensation and audit committees), intellectual property (IP), commercial, dispute avoidance and resolution, litigation, advertising, employment, compliance, and global expansion, and all other matters facing a fast-paced, hyper-growth global private and public internet social-media company.

SVP, General Counsel & Secretary

Attorney

Cooley Godward
January 1993 – August 1996 (3 years 8 months)

Knowing that I wanted to move in-house and be a part of the business, I practiced general corporate law in the Firm's Sand Hill Road office, where I worked with terrific corporate lawyers on IPOs and Secondary Offerings, Mergers and Acquisitions, Venture Financings, and general representation of start-up companies. I started at the firm as an employment lawyer focusing mostly on counseling and transactional work, in addition to litigation. I also chaired the firm's participation in the East Palo Alto Volunteer Attorney Project, a legal aid clinic in a community which, at the time, had the highest per capita murder rate in the country.
