Sid Goodloe's Summary

I am a Producer/Director/Writer/Camera Operator with SingleTree Films and McAlvany Studio based in Durango, Colorado Formerly in Dallas, Texas and Southern California, I have also served as Creative Director/Development Producer for Powerhouse Productions and Creative consultant to a variety of businesses and ministries. After growing up on a working cattle ranch in New Mexico, I began my career in live performance and entertainment in the Southwest working on Feature films, stage productions and made for television movies as a wrangler, riding double, stuntman and actor. I received a Communications: Directing and Writing degree and a minor in Biblical Studies at Biola University. After internships with the Directors Guild of America and Focus on the Family Films, I served as Personal Assistant to international author and speaker Josh McDowell and while with Josh, as Associate Producer/Marketing on the television special Too Young To Die: the Steve Sawyer Story. From here I embarked on my Freelance Career, working on dozens of commercials, industrials, Music Videos and several feature films. I spent two years working with the Second Unit film crew on Walker , Texas Ranger and Sons of Thunder as needed. I have had the opportunity to film in several countries and be a part of a variety of unique major entertainment events. Under the SingleTree Films banner, I continue to produce and direct corporate, ministry, commercial and documentary projects and develop feature film projects in conjunction with Powerhouse Productions, BedHead Productions and others.

Specialties: Film/Video Directing, Producing, HD+ Camera Operator , writing, FCS2 editing, idea/vision development, creative consultant, western/ranching lifestyle consultant
