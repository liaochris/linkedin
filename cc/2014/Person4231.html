Melih ÖZCANLI's Summary

20 years of professional experience in:
E-commerce, startups, fashion, company and team building, executive management, digital marketing, social media, flash sales, private shopping clubs, market & trend knowledge, strategy development, global sourcing and brand buying, managing & developing supplier relationships, private label, fabric & garment quality expertise, merchandising, planning, budgeting.

Seasoned expert in buying and sourcing of clothing, footwear, bags, jewelry, watches, eyewear, accessories, gadgets, home textiles, décor, cosmetics. Global network of more than 1000 suppliers, manufacturers and brands, mostly in Europe, USA, Turkey and Middle East.

Melih recently served as the Chief Sourcing Officer for MarkaVIP.com and General Manager of Turkey operations, where he helped grow the flash sales site from startup to an internationally recognized brand with $100 million in revenues and operations in eight ME/GCC countries between 2011-2013. Previously, he founded Texfolio Global Sourcing, and catered the European and American fashion retailers and brands as their apparel manufacturing and supply-chain solution provider between 2005-2010. Between 1995-2005, Melih worked at the leading American buying offices, vertically integrated textile mills and garment factories where he acquired his fashion sourcing, merchandising, sales and marketing skills. Melih received his MBA from the University of Bridgeport (Connecticut, USA) and his BA in Economics from the Bogazici University (Istanbul, Turkey).

P.S. I accept all invitations, however I'm not interested in apparel, fabric, and accessories production at the moment. I am not looking for a business partnership with suppliers, factories, and sourcing agencies.
