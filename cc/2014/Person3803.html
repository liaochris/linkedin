Ken Burbary's Summary

Ken is a digital marketer and technologist with a keen understanding of how technology can drive business results. With over 18 years of online marketing & advertising experience, including a deep background in digital and social media, he is considered a nationally recognized expert in the digital marketing space.

Ken drives strategic thinking and ideation based on in-depth digital knowledge and analytics; guides development and application of new media tools and technology to gain consumer insights; and integrates talent, technology and capabilities to optimize use of digital channels creating game-changing solutions that build and grow clients’ business.

Ken has held digital leadership positions at Ernst & Young and Digitas and has worked with some of the biggest names in marketing, including American Express, Proctor & Gamble, Kraft, General Motors, Chevrolet, Delta Airlines, Walgreens, MillerCoors, Pfizer, the U.S. Navy and the U.S. Postal Service.

He also recently coauthored the book book Digital Marketing Analytics: Making Sense of Consumer Data in a Digital World. http://bit.ly/UF4qKi
