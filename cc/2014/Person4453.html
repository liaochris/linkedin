Nicholas Herinckx's Summary

Nick Herinckx is a published, tech-savvy CEO of Obility Consulting, a fast growing online marketing agency focused on helping B2B companies with long sales cycles drive pipeline, increase sales and track marketing's efforts to revenue.

Nick began his career in information technology as a database programmer, but quickly realized that online marketing had the combination of strategy, data and technology he was really passionate about. Nick's past IT experience combined with his passion for marketing allowed him to quickly understand complex technology businesses, easily interface with IT teams and quickly learn and adopt new marketing technologies. This led to his success partnering with high-tech B2B companies to grow their businesses, and eventually led to his growth into corporate leadership positions.

Nick joined Obility Consulting as CEO in 2012, helping build the company with his partner from 2 to over 10 employees with 500% revenue growth, all while maintaining high client satisfaction, retention and low employee turnover. Nick enjoys serving Obility's customers, partners and employees, which all help Nick live his core values of intellectual development, compassion and achievement.

Nick's articles and client case studies have been published in industry blogs and magazines including MarketingProfs, MarketingSherpa, SEOMoz, PPC Hero and Marketing Experiments. Nick has also spoken at industry events for the PPC Hero Conference, SEOMoz, Portland Business Journal and SEMPdx, and has also instructed a class on advanced Google Analytics for the Online Marketing Institute.

In his spare time, Nick volunteers for the Gonzaga Alumni Mentoring Program where he works with college seniors and recent graduates on improving their job search skills while also teaching them about the online marketing industry. He has been playing blues guitar since middle school and enjoys spending time with his lovely wife and family.
