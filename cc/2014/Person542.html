Background

Summary

I come to work every day to build enduring client relationships and drive overall client satisfaction through resilient Agile methodologies and Sprint planning. The most exciting part of my role is in understanding and aligning project approaches to business objectives, roadmaps and success factors of my clients. I strive to be a thought leader in my space, but also to lead my project teams through strong project risk management and problem solving.

How do I lead as an Engagement Manager at Effective UI?
- Lead efforts to provide oversight regarding project resource allocation for designers, developers and customer insight consultants on web projects
- Develop control procedures for internal process improvement
- Oversee requirements definition, agile projects product backlogs and dependencies using project management tools
- Manage UX product teams and programmers over the project life cycle in daily stand-ups and weekly/bi-weekly sprints
- Develop, execute and maintain project plans for web-based projects and native applications for client program managers
- Make client-focused recommendations to the PMO regarding project management processes and cross-functional team management
- Assist product managers, tech leads and managing directors in gaining strategic consulting approaches for solving our clients toughest challenges in new business development

In our design thinking and the software development life cycle, the teams that I lead are the most brilliant developers and creative designers I have ever met. The deliverables that I contribute to supporting their work are in defining functional requirements, technical requirements, scope definition, user stories, resource plans and specific project information.
