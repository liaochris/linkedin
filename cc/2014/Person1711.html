Steven Ghesquiere's Summary

I am a recipient of an honours degree in Industrial Design, (B.Des.Ind.) has worked within the Dingle Peninsula tourism industry since I was a teenager. As a former director of a local web design company and the Dingle Accommodation Centre, with five years experience working in a top Dublin architectural practice and 12 years experience in graphics, computing and photography, I bring positivity, energy and enthusiasm to dodingle.com.

With an appreciation for all things local, I am keenly aware of the needs of the market and dodingle.com has developed with the local hospitality in mind. This exudes from the website and this labour of love has been brought to fruition through years of effort. With a growing global following, the positive feedback dodingle.com has generated is note-worthy.
