Annette Bingham's Summary

Annette Bingham comes from over a decade of experience in holistic wellness. In her role as a life coach and massage therapist Annette taught that change is necessary for growth and moving into one's highest potential. Her transition to the arts has allowed her to pursue a variety of art forms, such as writing, mixed media and illustrations. The arts have allowed Annette to move into her highest potential.

As an artist, Annette taps into a variety of art mediums, such as acrylic, watercolor, pen, alcohol ink, mixed media, and in genres such as illustrative art and children's art.

In addition, Annette pens non-fiction genres that include: self-help, metaphysical, and paranormal. Her past experience in holistic healing and life coaching provides a compelling platform for her work. Her book titled, A View from the Threshold: True Ghost Stories and Other Spirit Encounters (2013), features tales from Annette's childhood and her role as a paranormal investigator.
Annette has also teamed up with wonderful guest hosts in exploring the world of radio. The most recent being 'Girl Power-Half Hour on Blogtalk radio.
