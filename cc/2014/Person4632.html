Scott Dunklee's Summary

I worked in the hotel and resort industry for almost 23 years from my first job at a resort / casino in Lake Tahoe to 5-star / 5-Diamond hotels such as the Phoenician resort in Scottsdale AZ and Montage Laguna Beach. I was part of the Starwood pre-opening team that opened new hotels / resorts within the company and opened hotels such as the St. Regis Monarch Beach, Westin Diplomat in Fort Lauderdale Florida, The Sheridan Wild Horse Pass and the Westin Kierland in Arizona.

I then moved to Southern California and worked at the St. Regis where I was the Guest Services manager and then moved onto the Montage resort in Laguna Beach.

I own and operate a business branding company called Dunklee Industries. Finding specialty items to help marketing and branding of businesses. Using my 23 years of customer service skills to help business create an identifiable image. Dunklee Industries has over 3500 suppliers and works with some of the best embroiderers and screen printers in the southern California area. I am an avid Ballroom dancer and specialize in robes, pens, journals and team jackets for dance studio's.

In May 2012, I joined Deviget, LLC as a Project Manager. Deviget, LLC is a custom software development company with a great team of developers and leaders. My current focus is on staffing, recruiting, quality control and project management of both existing and new product development. Became a Certified SCRUM Product Owner in November of 2012.

Specialties: SCRUM Product Owner, Project management, recruiting and staffing

Branding and identity, promotional products, uniforms, hats, embroidery, screen printing, sublimation.
