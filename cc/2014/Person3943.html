Leigh Anderson's Summary

Expertise in high-tech product management, product marketing, and business development, with a focus on digital imaging, and massively-parallel computing markets, tools and applications software since 2003.

Created a highly profitable $440M/first-5 years software product line from scratch, including leading one of the more successful acquisitions in industry history, while co-authoring a fundamental software performance patent.

Very early employee of two innovative semiconductor startups: in 3D graphics/video chips and massively-parallel processors. Both were acquired, one with a $nine-figures exit.

Proven ability to match a market need with technology opportunity to create sustainable competitive advantage. Led and executed high-impact product definition and marketing campaigns to introduce both entire companies, and new products in new categories.

Negotiated with Apple and Avid to obtain rights and offer the only commercially available embedded libraries for ProRes and DNxHD video codecs. Also obtained rights to AVC-Intra from Panasonic and trade secret API access at Adobe.

Experienced in software, hardware or systems companies, with a depth of experience in software primarily - in pro video, imaging, standards-conversion, video codecs, camera technology, massively-parallel computing, developer tools, big data, host or embedded systems and software.

Specialties: Technology product management, product marketing, business development, acquisitions, video and imaging software, video codecs, image-processing, signal-processing, medical-imaging, bioinformatics, massively-parallel computing tools, software, hardware, embedded and host-acceleration systems, and database systems.
