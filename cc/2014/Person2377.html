Bryan Bamford's Summary

Technology, design, games, and new ventures are my passions. I have a BFA in Graphic Design from the Maryland Institute College of Art (MICA) and I currently manage and act as Design Director at Brinkbit, a small game studio in Baltimore, MD.

Independently and with great teams, I've successfully published dozens of apps and games now to the Apple and Google App Stores that have had millions of downloads worldwide.

I've been working in the mobile app/game industry for the past 5 years and in web for about 8. I have experience designing as well as developing mobile apps and games in every stage of the process from idea to implementation. In addition, I've played key management and marketing roles responsible for company vision, budgeting, branding, to designing creative monetization strategies leveraging a number of mobile ad and currency partners.
