Tim Hoerl's Summary

° Before my career as a Graphic Designer I worked in Finance for larger corporate firms, so I understand and can appreciate the corporate culture completely. Before I learned how to thrive in an artistic atmosphere without many rules, I learned to thrive in those environments where rules were steadfast and rigid, and where a shirt and a tie were a must.

° As a student of design, I excelled in all classes and was consistently at the top of my class. My hard work and dedication to the program paid off when I was awarded Best New Media at the CSUS Spring Show in 2007 for my student work in time-based media.

° Working in a small design firm has had its benefits, the least of which is being able to have "casual friday" every day of the week. The most rewarding aspect of this job, aside from carrying projects from start to finish, has been the ability to build relationships with clients and vendors alike. That face-time has been an essential tool in the constant battle to improve communication skills - skills needed to take a clients concept and translate it into a final piece of art.
