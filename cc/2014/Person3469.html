Jeremy Johnson's Summary

My experience as a User Experience and Product Design Leader:

» Worked directly with technology, product, and marketing organizations.
» Integrated Lean UX into the development organization - streamlining UX with Agile Development practices.
» Helped launch a number of mobile websites and mobile apps on both iOS and Android.
» Experience with forming User Experience and Design teams from scratch.
» Integrated prototyping into the UX process - experience with rapid (paper, sketching), High Fidelity (Photoshop, Axure), and functional prototyping (HTML/CSS/JS).
» Set creative direction for UI Branding as well as sales and merchandising visuals across large sites.
» Managed large UX and Design teams, both directly and within a matrix organization.
» Personally recruited UX and Design team members.
» Established customer focus programs (usability testing, personas, "Starbucks" testing).
» Documented UI Pattern Libraries for Design teams and UI Developers.
» Worked directly with CTO - with an understanding of the full technology stack, and helped drive UX decisions to benefit our customers.
» Promoted Lean Startup, and other product design centric practices.
» Presented to C-Level, and a number of conferences on UX, design, and technology related topics.
» Won a number of innovations awards for bringing new ideas into the company.
» Worked with and managed teams in Buenos Aires, London, Kraków, and India.

With my Design background, I'm lucky to be on the front lines of how we'll be interacting with the growing number of devices that are now crucial to our lives. If we thought the last 10 years were amazing, wait until everything has a virtual interface that needs to be designed and tested - for ease-of-use, as well as for enjoyment.

Specialties: User Experience, Customer Experience, Mobile Design, e-commerce, Design, Marketing, Product Strategy, Social Media, Presentations, Public Speaking, Visual Design, Art Direction, Technology, Development
