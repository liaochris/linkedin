Angel Garcia Olloqui's Summary

(Please, check out my webpage at http://angelolloqui.com or download CV in the PDF attached at the bottom of my profile)

Computer engineer with a Master in Web technologies. Specialized in iPhone, iPad & Android application development, with experience using Agile methodologies, mainly SCRUM and some XP practices.
I started my career as an Oracle trainee (2006-2007) developing some internal management applications for the sales department, where I was given with the "Oracle's best trainee of the year" award.
After finishing my training at Oracle, I decided to start up my own company with 2 friends, Wixel Solutions (2007-2008); we developed Open Doors as well as some other web applications for external customers. However, we didn't have the results we expected, so 15 months later, I joined Mobivery (2009-2011) as the recruiter and manager of a new mobile developer team in Madrid.
In April 2011, I moved to San Francisco (USA) to study and try new projects, but due to personal issues I decided to come back to Europe on January 2012.
Right now I am living in Amsterdam working for Xaton as a senior iOS developer.

Especialidades: iphone, ipad, ios, objective-c, android, ruby, rails, php, symfony, java, html, software
