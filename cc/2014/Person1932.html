Adam Waaramaa's Summary

After earning my Master’s in Mechanical Engineering at Brown University, and three years learning the ropes as a Consultant in High Tech and Pharma at The Boston Consulting Group, I headed to The Wharton School to figure out how to start a business.

For two years, I was the CEO and Co-Founder of Ground Transportation Search Engine Catapulter.com, a graduate of the Betaspring startup accelerator program and Wharton's incubator, the Venture Initiation Program.

Now, I work at Google in Silicon Valley, with a few projects on the side.
