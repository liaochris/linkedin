David Merriam's Summary

Originally from Houston, TX, David is an American Rock singer and songwriter with over 15+ yrs experience rocking clubs, festivals and arenas... David has done tons of interviews on radio and television over the years and have gained quite a bit of notoriety for himself. Recently, David had the pleasure of being Featured on the widely popular radio show, "The Big Rock Show with Tina Peek" and released two video games on Rockband 3 for XBox360 and PS2. David also had the honor of being chosen by Paul Stanley and Gene Simmons, of KISS, to open for their show in Houston with his group, Before Nine. David has also done Stand-In work on Tokarev, starring Nicolas Cage, Danny Glover, Max Ryan and Peter Stormare. He continues to pursue his music and acting.
