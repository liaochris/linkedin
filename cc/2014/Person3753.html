Karen Cohen's Experience

CEO

Corporate Health Resources, Inc.

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Health, Wellness and Fitness industry

1986 – Present (28 years) Rockville, MD

Corporate Health Resources, Inc. (CHR) is a nationwide examiner network that provides occupational health exams in all 50 states and many international locations as well.

• CEO of Corporate Health Resources, Inc., a firm that manages a nationwide network of highly qualified occupational and executive health clinics and ancillary providers. Corporate Health Resources, Inc. has an established network of over 10,000 Occupational Medicine providers throughout the U.S. and Canada.

• Manage programs for numerous Fortune 500 companies in the areas of occupational health and executive health. Types of examinations coordinated by CHR include pre-placement and periodic health examinations (both regulated and non-regulated), DOT examinations, drug screen collections and processing (including MRO services and random drug screening programs), immunization scheduling and tracking for international travelers, Independent Medical Evaluations(IME’s), and Functional Capacity Examinations(FCEs).

• Experience using a Joint Applications Development (JAD) methodology to assist clients in industry in the development and customization of occupational health computer systems.
