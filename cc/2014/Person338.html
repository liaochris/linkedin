Summary

Joe Aakre is a Solutions Engineer at NAVEX Global.

Prior to joining NAVEX Global, Joe managed the Solution Consulting group at Exterro, Inc.

In addition to managerial duties, his primary responsibilities included maintaining ongoing relationships with existing clients and performing wellness checks on key accounts through status calls and in-person visits.

He brings over 20 years of industry experience from major corporations and large law firms. His background also includes work as a legal investigator, paralegal, litigation support manager, project manager, and software product manager.
