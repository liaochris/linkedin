Trond Lyngbø's Summary

Trond Lyngbø is Head of SEO at MediaCom Norway, and SEO columnist at Search Engine Land.

As a digital marketing consultant and analyst, I help businesses get more targeted website traffic and sell more of their products and services using online methods like search engine optimization (SEO), PPC, social media and analytics.

I've written some of the most popular SEO columns at Search Engine Land three years in a row. I'm the author of two books about SEO and Social Media Marketing and have achieved more than 20 IT certifications during my career.

Strengths: Creative thinking, passionate, well-structured consultant with analytic skills and a deep understanding of modern technology and the online business ecosystem. I get things done.
