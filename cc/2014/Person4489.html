Odalys Girado's Summary

An HR and Accounting Professional with over 16 years in the Staffing Industry. Currently serving as Sr. Business Director of Right Hire Staffing Solutions overseeing all aspects of the organization to include business development, recruitment, operations, accounting, sales & marketing, and networking.

Serving as a "Staffing Expert" for CBS4 with Al Sunshine. A GMSHRM Board Member for the past 10 years, for 2012 serving as Community Relations Director. A member of Executive Association of Greater Miami, former President of the Associates Group for EAGM. In addition, a member of West Kendall Business Associates, B2B Miami, Chamber South, and Greater Miami Chamber of Commerce.

A former Miami Dolphins Cheerleader and now a member of the MDC Alumni Association.

Specialties

Staffing and Recruitment of Qualified Professionals in all industries, Full-Service Staffing, Temp, Temp-to-Perm, and Direct Hire placements. Human Resources consulting services, payroll services, and much more.
