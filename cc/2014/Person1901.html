Aaron Kopel's Summary

Aaron is a Management Consultant and Enterprise Agile Coach with experience across various industries and company sizes ranging from early-stage startups to Fortune 100 firms. As a coach and advisor, Aaron is passionate about helping individuals, managers, leaders, and executives to to realize their vision for success, which enables them to become successful leaders and change agents in achieving their organization's vision.

He is founder of Project Brilliant LLC, a management consulting firm specializing in Agile transformation and the application of Lean and Agile principles to entire organizations. His passion for innovation have led him to found two startup companies, which provided his entry into to the world of Agile.

Specialities: board member, entrepreneur, lean startup, speaker, advisor, mentor, trainer, facilitator, change agent, agile, scrum, coaching, lean, kanban, agile transformation
