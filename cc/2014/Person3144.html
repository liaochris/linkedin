Greg Olson's Summary

Digital and Mobile expert
Helping companies develop mobile marketing strategies.
Working with customers to implement digital strategies within their event marketing programs.

Expertise in B2B, medical technology and healthcare launches. Utilizing social networking within business. Helping companies determine how to move forward with mobile, blogs, webinars, podcasts, and other digital media

Understanding the intricacies of medical and healthcare launches, Greg knows how to navigate regulatory hurdles while advancing a launch through each critical gate. With twenty years of marketing experience, he helps companies launch new products and relaunch existing products. He has developed or enhanced many successful marketing and launch plans, along with expertise in branding, naming and re-branding campaigns. He is often called upon to impart his expertise via on-site training sessions.

Combining digital expertise with trade show marketing allows clients to further their lead generation and sales strategies.

Currently serving on Business Marketing Association National Board as the VP of Chapter Development. Enjoys public speaking, sharing insight on product launches, web 2.0, and social networking. Active member in Business Marketing Association, Colorado Bioscience Association, American Marketing Association, Mobile Marketing Association, AIGA and Art Directors Club.
