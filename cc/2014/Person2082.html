Andrew Wolfenson's Summary

Attorney with multi-disciplined practice focusing primarily on Divorce, Child Support, Emancipation, and other family matters, Bankruptcy (Chapter 7 and Chapter 13), Litigations, preparation of wills and probate matters, and who regularly appears in courts of both Law and Equity (Chancery). The firm also handles transactional work, including corporate and LLC acquisitions/formation as well as both commercial and residential real estate matters. Representative clients include numerous small businesses, individuals, and real estate offices.

Andrew is also the author of two novels, the recently-released "Deadly Fantasy: A Baseball Story" and the legal suspense thriller "In His Own Defense", both currently available on Amazon.com and for Kindle.

With a main office centrally located just off of the Garden State Parkway in Union, New Jersey, the firm also possesses a Morris County satellite office in Cedar Knolls. Staff is fluent in both Spanish and Portuguese.

Specialties: Divorce/Child Support and other Family matters, Bankruptcy (Chapter 7 and Chapter 13), Civil Litigation, Wills, Probate and Estate matters, Corporate/ Limited Liability Company acquisitions and formation, Criminal matters including Municipal Court and Expungement, and both Commercial and Residential Real Estate transactions.
