Technical Director

DSDM Consortium
1994 – 2005 (11 years)

Jennifer led the development of DSDM from its inception. She defined the basic architecture of the method and created a fair proportion of the content over the years in collaboration with experts from across the agile world.

The Chairman of the DSDM Consortium stated in his foreword to Version 1 of the method that "she more than any other individual must take credit for what we hope will become an industry standard".

Managing Consultant

Provided consultancy both internally within Logica and externally to clients on the effective use of development methods and tools, e.g. SSADM, OO techniques and agile development.

Was Logica’s leading Rapid Application Development consultant and represented Logica on the Euromethod project.

As an SSADM specialist, worked in a three-person team to develop SSADM 4.3 which added GUI design, prototyping and a more user-centred approach to SSADM. Major contributor to the two HMSO books: SSADM and GEMINI (a KBS method) and SSADM and GUI Guide.

Summary

Independent consultant since 1996 specialising in the improvement of portfolio, programme and project management. Providing consultancy and training at all levels from the Board to the smallest project office.

APM-registered PPM and MSP consultant.

Designing and facilitating workshops for senior management teams, especially when introducing major change.

As Technical Director of the DSDM Consortium, led the development and evolution of DSDM for 11 years.

Good trainer and presenter who has delivered keynote addresses at several conferences and seminars.

Specialties:Designing portfolio, programme and project management processes and structures. Tailoring OGC guidance to suit particular organisations. Business process design, systems development process design, process improvement. Programme and project management mentoring. DSDM.
