Stephanie Smirnov's Summary

Seasoned PR professional with over two decades of experience at public relations agencies and in-house communications teams. Agency experience includes oversight of $25MM+ P&Ls and a track record of success with new business development. Deep expertise in CPG, food and beverage and wellness. Award-winning cause marketing practitioner. Highly-sought speaker on topics such as social media, women entrepreneurs and cause marketing. Joined Edelman in 2013 as Managing Director of Consumer Marketing in New York office. Connect with me on twitter (http://twitter.com/ssmirnov).
