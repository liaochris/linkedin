Mitch Pauley's Summary

I have been working in the field of Human services and Autism for the past fifteen years. My background is diverse and I have been fortunate to work in a variety of situations which have given me skill and experience in my field. The place which I have chosen to further my career and make a life is in Vermont. I have lived here for several years now and continue to develop my interests and skills. I have my Masters Degree from the University of Vermont in Special Education Consulting. It is my goal to utilize my skills in collaborating with professionals to develop and implement programming for people with disabilities.

Specialties: Program Development and Implementation, Behavior and Academic Intervention, Data Management and Progress Monitoring, and Crisis Management using the interventions developed through the Life Space Crisis Intervention Model (LSCI).
