Helen Stepchuk's Summary

Helen Stepchuk is the vivacious, energetic owner and managing director of Cucumber Marketing Inc., a web design and internet marketing company that helps businesses get noticed online. Helen studied Physics at the Volgograd State Pedagogical University, and followed up her degree with further education in Business, English, Integrated Marketing Communications and Strategic Marketing Management. A self-professed bookworm with a thirst for knowledge, Helen is inspired by reading about the people and organizations she admires most; The E-Myth helped Helen set priorities and learn how to operate her business, whileSteve Jobs provided insight into how intense passion and focus can become the foundation for an empire. Now, Helen envisions Cucumber Marketing as a brand that will in turn inspire others to pursue their business dreams. Besides being devoted to her business, Helen is also committed to healthy, sustainable living and a daily dose of yoga.
