Christopher (Chris) Roberts III's Summary

Christopher Roberts has a strong brand pedigree, holding leadership positions at PepsiCo, Coca Cola and is currently President of a division of Cargill Inc., a $150B international producer and marketer of food and agricultural products and services.

As President of Cargill Kitchens, he is transforming the business by defining a new growth paradigm, raising the leadership capability and reinvigorating the innovation pipeline resulting in delivering sustainable profit growth.

Mission:
• To grow / build a global business that is sustainably profitable and as a byproduct helps people to do good by enabling them to focus on feeding and educating. In effect, to leave the planet better than we found it.

Expertise:
• Leading and driving growth in brand-driven, consumer oriented businesses with a specialty in companies in the food industry by creating holistic and profitable business strategies.
• Selecting, developing and leading high performing teams.
• Optimizing the operating structure to improve bottom line productivity.
• Assessing the product portfolio and implementing processes and strategies that drive innovation and topline revenues.
• Identifying profitable growth opportunities in adjacent geographies and categories.
• Developing and deepening customer relationships.

Specialties: President, Leadership, Strategy, Change Management, Business Excellence, Talent Development, P&L Management, M&A, Food Expert, Field to Fork Process, Grow Food, Make Food, Sell Food, Food Marketing, Field Marketing, Brand Building, Customer Management, Consumer Insights, New Product Marketing, Food Innovation, Supply Chain, Food Logistics, Food Procurement, Risk Management, Global Food Operations, Food Ingredients, Food Production, Pricing, Food Manufacturing, Food Cross Border Trade, Foodservice, Sales, Grocery Retail, CPG, Food Joint Ventures, Food & Beverage, Social Media, Keynote Speaker, Board of Directors, Nonprofits Boards
