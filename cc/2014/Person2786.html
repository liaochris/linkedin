James Devereux's Summary

Devereux & Company, LLC is a Certified Public Accounting firm with experience in Corporate, Individual, Partnership, Fiduciary, and Estate Taxes. Our business has been serving the greater St. Louis & St. Charles area for over 35 years. We offer a variety of services to our clients including tax planning, business consulting, auditing, financial planning, education, accounting software integration, and much more.

We realize the high cost of accounting and strive to give you professional services at a reasonable cost. Our priority is our clients, we stay in business by keeping our clients in business.

Our staff offers experience and friendliness. We provide professional service with a personal touch. Give us a call today for free consultation.

Specialties

Quickbooks ProAdvisors, Peachtree Training, CRM integration, Custom Peachtree Reporting, Business Valuations, Loan Acquisition, Business Plans, Budget Preparation and Audits
