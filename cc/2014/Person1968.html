Alayne Green's Summary

SENIOR BUSINESS LEADER
Astute, highly effective leader supports strong fiscal performance and growth by innovatively driving organizational and business efficiency. In a top-performing managerial role is capable of improving the bottom line through systematic planning and implementation as well as client relations. Building consensus with key leaders and solving the critical problems, that block growth, serve organizations seeking competitive advantage achieved through adaptive change.

Alayne began her career at TVG, Inc., the premiere medical marketing research & promotional events company for the pharmaceutical industry. TVG was a training ground to develop expertise in numerous of areas – competing on value versus price, design/implementation in a customized environment, & management over highly diverse teams and disciplinary areas, ultimately assuming responsibility for all of the operation centers.

Refining influence and collaboration skills – Alayne needed to achieve results in a large regulated environment without control of groups. She promoted change, increasing compliance, identifying quality/cost-reduction outsourcing opportunities, & introducing new cutting-edge technology & logistical solutions resulted in Alayne receiving the Wyeth Presidential Award.

Alayne was pursued by Scientific Voice (SV) to consult and design business plans & lead teams during an entrepreneurial re-birth of high growth, new services, and enhanced technology. Alayne assessed and led the key initiatives that restructured operations/account management, repositioned key marketing messages, developed leaders and promoted growth.
