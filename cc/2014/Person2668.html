Danny Lagrouw's Summary

In 25 years of working in IT, I have gained a wealth of experience with all kinds of methods and technology – and yet I still learn something new every day. That’s my motivation.

I prefer to work as a programmer/software architect or programmer/Scrum Master. I have deep technical understanding as well as the ability to explain technology to non-technical people. I like to coach people and learn from others.

I have mainly programmed in Java (Java EE), Objective-C (iOS) and Scala in recent years; also in C, PL/SQL, PHP, Perl and Ruby longer ago.
I have experience with a variety of subjects: from data modeling and SQL query optimization to image editing in Photoshop and building SOAP or REST web services.

In 2011 I co-founded Trivento Touch, a startup within Trivento specialized in frontend and mobile development.
