July 2013 – Present (1 year 4 months)New York, New York

New York City-based makeup artist Shannon Grey Williams’ signature style is elegant, clean, simple beauty. Shannon’s natural, easygoing demeanor has helped her thrive working with models and clients. She has the ability to make people feel fantastic in their own skin and is thrilled when she can help them see their own natural beauty.

Shannon is a Licensed Cosmetologist and Paramedical Camouflage Makeup Artist. She is trained to address the cosmetic needs of post-plastic surgery patients and persons with congenital, accidental or dermatological conditions.

Shannon thrives in the studio or on location, with experience working on the tops of glaciers to exotic Caribbean beaches. Shannon has worked on hundreds of commercials (American Airlines, Campbell’s Soup, Johnson & Johnson and Kraft). Her corporate clients include Lilly Pulitzer. L’Oreal, Intermix, Izod, Nautica and Levi’s. Her celebrity roster includes Christy Turlington, Will Arnett, Candace Bergen, Megan Fox, Kevin Bacon, Eve, U2 and LL Cool J.
