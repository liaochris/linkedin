Ngure Nyaga's Summary

I co-founded Hyperion Consulting Limited in 2009, while still at the University of Nairobi. I served as managing director for the first year of the company's existence, before switching over to the technical director role. I still serve as the company's technical director.

In 2011, I co-founded eLab Services (K) Limited - a company that aimed to bring "healthcare intelligence" to the mainstream. The team behind eLab Services (K) Limited merged with another startup team in late 2012 - to create Savannah Informatics Limited.
