Teri Morin is founder of TweetTravelers.com, a travel agency and travel social community for travel and leisure. We work help travelers plan the perfect vacation as well as offer social media services to travel and tourism related companies. She is also founder of MickeyMagicTravel.com, a authorized Disney specialist for Disney Resorts and Adventures By Disney which travelers can book the perfect vacation to Disney Resorts.

Tweet Travelers serves as an internet based travel site and online travel social media network community that also offers social media services for travel and tourism.. Travelers can search over 140,000 hotels and resorts around the world on Tweet Travelers. Travelers can book travel online or our travel concierge department will plan the perfect trip from flight, hotel, activities, rental car and completely plan your vacation. . We are a full time travel agency with social media services and online travel community where property owners and travel companies can post travel listings.

Tweet Travelers social media services works with tourism destinations, hotels, vacation rentals, cruise lines, travel related companies.

Vacation Rental Managers and Property Owners can post vacation rentals or travel listings in our social community. Tweet Travelers travel community is a unique network of passionate travelers. Tweet Travelers is highly active across social Media channels - Pinterest, Google +, Twitter, Facebook, etc.

Teri is passionate about travel and tourism as well as social media management. She is also passionate about helping travel related companies online using social media. Travel is her passion in all aspects from helping travelers plan the perfect vacation to working with travel related companies.
