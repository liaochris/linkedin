Craig Hepburn's Summary

Over the 15 years Craig has developed an exceptional understanding of Internet, Intranet, Multimedia Technologies and how to apply it to maximum effect for business. He is a focused leader, self-starter with the ability to adapt quickly to new environments and the ability to communicate at various levels. Craig has an extensive and deep knowledge of digital technologies and has been involved in developing social media and digital strategies for some of the worlds largest brands.

Craig comes from a well-educated background in Glasgow, Scotland but made his move to London when he was 21 years old to pursue a career in digital marketing. Craig is well renowned for being very passionate, outspoken and driven and through this has helped him develop an incredible network of highly exceptional individuals from business and industry across the world.

Currently Craig is Global Director of Digital & Social Media at Nokia, responsible for leading a world class digital and social media strategy for the worlds largest mobile phone brand. Heading a team of highly skilled experts who drive the centre of digital and social media excellence.

Craig has presented and keynoted at many of the worlds largest conferences in digital and social including eConsultancy JUMP, AIIM, Social Media Influence, Enterprise 2.0 Conference, Internet World, EyeForTravel, AdTech, Revolution Search Marketing, OT Content World and iStrategy events.

Craig was voted as one of the UK's rising stars in the digital marketing sector by Revolution Magazine, shortlisted for digital person of the year by the DADI awards and contributes to many publications on social media and business.

Craig is a real family man with an amazing wife and 2 young boys who he says really drive him to succeed at home and in business but keeps him very grounded and focused on what matters in life.
