Shannon Smith's Summary

Shannon Smith is Founder | CEO of Blueye, founded in 2005, a company that helps brands define the engagement journey and build tailored social programs that drive loyalty and ROI. With a blend of process, strategy, technology and creativity, we believe we can help turn customers into advocates. She has been featured in Crain's Chicago, Adweek, and under her leadership Blueye has been listed as one of the top 10 design companies in Forbes 2009, and as one of the top 10 web development companies in Entrepreneur Magazine in 2008. In addition blueye received the accolade of becoming one of facebook's preferred marketing partners, holding 2 of the 4 available badges, which is an accolade Blueye holds high. Over the past eight years Shannon has led Blueye through a period of rapid growth and change, which includes our newest edition, The Blueye Hub, a technology that helps brands turn data into sales. The Blueye team have developed a number of web, mobile and social apps across a range of platforms and devices, for clients such as the Salvation Army, Amway Global and Starwood Hotels to name a few. In addition to the many hats she wears at Blueye, Shannon devotes what little spare time she has to saving animals through her rescue that she co-founded in 2013.

Specialties: Entrepreneurial, Sales, Customer Service and Marketing
