Michael Gold's Summary

I spend my time working on startups, producing startup industry events, and connecting entrepreneurs, creators, and innovators.

I have been quoted as an Internet marketing expert on Mashable and was featured in a Techcrunch article about meeting influencers at SXSW.

In addition to startup work, I have led experiential strategy and development on products for brands including LG, HBO, Starbucks, and Target.

I financed my first tech company in 2001 by moonlighting as a wedding DJ / MC, and still emcee tech events such as Ultralight Startups, The New York Tech Meetup Simulcast, AngelHack, and other prominent hackathons.
