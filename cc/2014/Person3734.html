Jordan Kachmarik's Summary

I hold a Master's of Business Administration from the University of Toledo specializing in Information Systems. I have been employed with Pontoon Solutions (An Adecco Group Company, Formerly Adecco RPO) since August 2009. I am a motivated recruiter with more than 4 years of experience in interviewing, candidate generation, screening and sourcing. I have been able to work for a number clients for Pontoon including HSBC, Baker Hughes, Cydcor, Owens Illinois, Global IM, and DHL Express. I have experience of recruiting for several different industries including Financial, Retail, International Logistics, and Energy & Gas. I also have experience of recruiting on various types of roles including operations, sales, transportation, and technical.
