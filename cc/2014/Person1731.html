Co-Founder and Advisor

OpenSignal.com

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Telecommunications industry

January 2010 – Present (4 years 10 months) London, United Kingdom

OpenSignal is the world's largest crowdsourced mobile sensor network. With over 7 million downloads, 1 million active users and billions of unique data samples gathered per day, the OpenSignal application provides wireless market insights to major customers such as McKinsey, Telefonica and ChinaMobile. At the same time, the OpenSignal app uses the same database to help users find better signal in realtime, locate free WiFi hotspots and get personalised recommendations on the best mobile network for them.

OpenSignal's second application, WeatherSignal, repurposes the pressure, temperature and humidity sensors on smartphones to collect data to provide better weather prediction to over 50k users, and has lead to a scientific publications in collaboration with the Royal Meteorlogical Society of the Netherlands demonstrating the potential of crowdsourced data for improving short term weather forecasts.

We're pioneering the field of crowdsourced sensor networks and looking for smart, determined people to help us do it. If you'd like to join us check out our list of open positions or just get in touch: http://opensignal.com/jobs
