Background

Summary

PROFILE AND SUMMARY- Accomplished 20+ years of leadership, experience and education in the ministry of worship, music & arts, and media. Skilfully encourage and engage both the worship ministry & congregation in the worship experience. Incorporate various worship styles such as CCM, Traditional, and Urban to unite cultures in the worship experience. Coordinate and collaborate with leaders & staff in planning effective worship services and special events. Organize and administrate the overall music & art aspect of the church including the worship band, dancers, drama and flag ministry. Conduct seasonal music workshops & worship training sessions to enhance the worship ministry's performance and ability. Encourage and inspire the worship & arts ministry to pursue passion for God and to live a lifestyle of worship. Personally mentor and develop worship leaders, worship teams & band in perfecting their craft and in effectively leading worship. Prepare worship for children and youth services. Arrange and produce music for worship services, live recordings and special music events. Assist and co-produce sketch scripts for plays and monologues to be used for services & special events. Collaborate with media ministry in creating graphic, web and media designs for worship and church events.
