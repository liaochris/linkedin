Rachel Lee Cherry's Summary

I've spent 15 years applying my expanding skillset, my knowledge of a little bit of everything, and my experience in publishing to a strange and wonderful assortment of subject matter. I’ll edit, double-check, and polish your novel to help your readers be swept away by the story, not dragged down by awkward sentences and inaccurate details. And I'll apply the same diligence to nonfiction texts until the final product meets your standards and communicates effectively, down to the last syllable. Let’s work together.
