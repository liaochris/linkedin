Sharon Fitzgerald, MA's Summary

Sharon Fitzgerald, MA is a Los Angeles native with a unique background in marketing, entertainment, academia, and corporate communications. Sharon started out in marketing with A.D.D. Marketing, Momentum Marketing, and Sony Music in the US and Europe. After obtaining her master's degree in art history, Sharon became an adjunct art history professor, published author, and still lectures art history monthly in private and public venues throughout Los Angeles.

For the last two years Sharon is the fulltime public relations, social media and advertising manager for NAI Capital, a commercial real estate firm with 14 offices throughout SoCal. She loves to embark on new frontiers and embrace opportunities to grow.
