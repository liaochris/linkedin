Janine Moon's Summary

> Professional speaker and seminar facilitator addressing the issues associated with Workforce Change and 21st century leadership including Career Partnering, the Aging Workforce, Generational Dynamics, Total Engagement and "the hard truth about the soft stuff."

> One of 5 Master Certified Career Coaches in the U.S.; over thirty years mentoring, growing, developing and coaching people—both individually and within organization development initiatives.

> A former utilities executive with C-level responsibilities as well as lobbying experience with national and state legislative and executive branches.

> College and secondary teaching as well as training line workers, professionals and executives; conference keynoter and break-out moderator; large and small group (both agreeable and antagonistic) facilitation; leadership and design of change initiatives; and executive coaching of professionals, teams and C-level leaders.

> Created “Career Ownership” program, unique in its approach to career development inside organizations. It is grounded on extensive work within organizations, knowledge of economies and productivity, 21st century global competition, and a deep understanding and extensive experience with what engages individuals to do their best work and connect deeply with an organization’s mission.

> Mission # 1: shifting the Industrial economy career paradigm at two levels:

1) individuals take responsibility for dynamic and lifelong career satisfaction; and
2) organizations adopt Career Ownership as a Best Practice...a tool for retaining talent.

> Mission # 2: coaching organizations to leverage the best of their workers--physical, mental and emotional--for success in global markets.

Specialties: coaching leaders to create cultures of engaged partners v. disengaged employees, developing world-class 21st century Learning organizations & Leaders, executive development and coaching.
