Lucas Fladzinski's Summary

I believe that impressive design deserves commanding photography. With thorough investigation and subtle refinement, each project is handcrafted to uniquely capture the artistry of the original.

Lucas Fladzinski is a professional photographer specializing in architectural and editorial photography throughout the US and abroad. His diverse portfolio has been featured in several books and numerous blogs and magazines including a recent four-page spread in Dwell Magazine and an image featured in the new iPad Mini advertisement for Apple Computers.

With over 90 clients, more than 500 commissions and 20 major publications since 2005, Lucas has built a reputation among the architectural and design community as their choice for photographic assignments in the Bay Area.
_

Specialties: Lucas specializes in professional interior and exterior photography for residential and commercial projects. For interiors, he uses controlled lighting with strobes and natural light to produce lively, atmospheric spaces. For exteriors, Lucas manually combines numerous layers of varied exposures in optimal lighting conditions resulting in rich, dynamic environments.

Lucas Fladzinski's Projects

  • Split Bread Shoot

    • November 2012 to December 2012
    Team Members: Lucas Fladzinski

    This was one of my favorite projects of 2012. I was commissioned by long-time client, William Duff Architects to document their recent restaurant project opening at the Metreon in San Francisco. We began the exterior shots around 5am in order to finish the exteriors and interiors by 11am when the staff arrived. I was worried about the lack of warm light on the facade from the tall buildings across the street blocking the AM sun as well as fog however, this time the cool light conditions added a crisp, classic look to the overall shots. I’m very pleased with how the assignment turned out.
