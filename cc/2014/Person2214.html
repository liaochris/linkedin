Barbara Hoskins Sakamoto's Experience

Author

Oxford University Press

Nonprofit; 5001-10,000 employees; Publishing industry

1989 – Present (25 years)

Coauthor of the Let's Go series with Ritsuko Nakata, Karen Frazier, and Carolyn Graham. Let's Go is one of the world's bestselling primary English coursebook series. Millions of children around the globe have learned English with the trusted methodology of Let's Go. The seven level series is now in its fourth edition.

Program Director / Author / Faculty Member

International Teacher Development Institute

Education Management industry

1988 – Present (26 years) Kitakyushu, Japan

The International Teacher Development Institute (iTDi) aims to support and empower teachers through a collaborative and engaging online community. We bring together newcomers and veterans, native and non-native teachers from a wide range of contexts, but we all share a common belief that being a teacher means a never-ending commitment to growing and learning. iTDi: For Teachers By Teachers.
