Alicia Calzada's Summary

I am an attorney based in Texas and specializing in serving the needs of a variety of media clients, including newspapers, television stations, movie companies and creative professionals, focusing on intellectual property, media law, First Amendment issues and small business support with experience advocating for journalists and creative professionals to all branches of government.

In my past life I was a professional photojournalist, shooting for variety of magazines, newspapers, wire services and corporations dealing with issues such as immigration, agriculture, international politics, poverty, culture, disasters, news, entertainment and sports.

As an Attorney, Advocacy Chair and past president for the National Press Photographers Association, I work to support the association's policies and priorities on national issues relating to photography such as free press issues and issues that help photographers protect their business interests. I have launched several services within the NPPA that support and serve photojournalists.

Author of the blogs: www.photoblawg.com, and www.slappedintexas.com

Specialties: Attorney, Intellectual Property, Advocacy, Photography, Photojournalist, Annual Report Photographer, Commercial Photographer, Public Relations Photographer, Photo Editing, Corporate Photographer, Magazine Photographer, Newspaper Photographer, Spanish Language media, Law, legal,
