Jason McDowall's Summary

Jason has spent his career doing three things: building and leading strong teams, innovating toward where the market is headed, and delivering results through enhanced executive team thinking and execution. Currently he is head of product at Hothouse Labs, a startup in stealth mode where they are building mobile applications to make "work" fundamentally better on the phone.

Prior to Hothouse Labs, Jason spent almost 6 years as a director or VP of product in B2B, Software as a Service companies - most notably Salesforce.com - where he primarily delivered relationship management, collaboration, and analytics tools. Previously he was a co-founder/CEO of two mobile-focused startups. Prior to working in mobile and software as a service, Jason served as a Captain in the US Air Force where his day job was as a product manager for large scale computer and communication systems. He holds an MBA from UCLA as well as a BS and MS from Carnegie Mellon University.

Specialties: Building exceptional teams, Crafting and communicating executable strategies, Bringing order from chaos, Recognizing emerging trends, Developing products efficiently, Managing operations, Growing a user base (marketing/growth hacking), SaaS, Mobile
