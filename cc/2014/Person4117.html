Mark Habibeh's Summary

Mark Habibeh, President of Excelsure Insurance Services is responsible for the oversight and management of the new business production for the Commercial Lines division. His responsibilities include developing customer relationships, developing insurance carrier relationships, employee hiring & training and ongoing sales of Property & Casualty insurance. Prior to Excelsure Insurance Services, he held similar responsibilities with Farmers Insurance Group and IDL Insurance. Many years ago, he originally started his career in insurance with Farmers Insurance Group’s Irvine office as a Property & Casualty Producer.

Mark Habibeh is a resident of Huntington Beach and is married with three children. He prides himself in being the hardest working insurance agent in the industry and working for his clients to achieve the best coverage at the most affordable price.
