Dr. Royal Benson's Summary

Dr. Royal Benson is a leader in the fields of obstetrics, gynecology and cosmetic surgery in the Bryan / College Station area. Offering over two decades of expertise, Dr. Benson and the Benson Ob-Gyn Center exist to renew and restore both the health and well being of patients across the Brazos Valley. Dr. Benson uses the latest innovations in medicine to provide superior care, total comfort and greatly reduced recovery time.

Bringing "big city" options to the Brazos Valley, I spend time with and listen to my patients to determine the best course of action for their own specific needs. It's our goal to ensure no patient leaves our clinic without fully understanding everything discussed in their consultation, as well as clear up any reservations or concerns they may have with their health.
