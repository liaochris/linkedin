Kayotsarg Music Festival for Cancer Cause(Link)

June 2013

Guitarmonk launched KAYOTSARG musical festival for Cancer Survivors on National Cancer Survivor’s Day.

Surviving cancer means having strong will power to respond to all the treatments and overcoming that deadly disease. Guitarmonk, a 360 degree music concept, working on the cause of cancer announced the release of music festival ‘Kayotsarg’ on the National Cancer Survivor’s Day. Kayotsarg meaning uprising consciousness is a live musical concert series that will invite musicians across globe along with cancer experts to join at a platform for cancer awareness.

Cancer survivor’s day is celebrated just to inspire a vibrant, celebrating life after overcoming cancer and the Kayotsarg implies ‘Kaya’ i.e. body + ‘Utsarg’ i.e. to rise above. Kayotsarg is an initiative which implies rising above cancer and leave cancer behind i.e. for both survivors and fighters.
