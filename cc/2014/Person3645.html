Summary

Led teams to win significant strategic contracts in Europe, Middle East, Africa and Latin America in a very challenging and competitive environment. Established strategic alliances to enhance the Co's portfolio and was also instrumental in seamlessly merging the new organization with over 2500 employees. Named by the Chairman and CEO as Corporate Vice President, Global Strategic Account Manager for Telefonica/O2, one of the most strategic and most valuable accounts, representing $1.4 billion in revenue. He was entrusted to develop the market for a new technologies. His previous positions in Motorola have included various leadership roles such as General Manager for the United States and executive positions in Motorola’s paging sector and cellular subscriber sector.
Prior to Motorola, worked for IBM in several executive positions including one of IBM’s most prestigious branch offices, the Aerospace Branch which handled McDonnell Douglas Corporation & General Dynamics.

Outstanding ability to rapidly transform failing organizations into fully functional, profitable entities, using excellent communications and interpersonal skills, as well as a unique talent for revitalizing work environments and employees.
