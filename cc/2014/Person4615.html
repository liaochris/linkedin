September 2011 – Present (3 years 1 month) Andover / Winchester

Poppins' Childcare provides flexible Ad Hoc childcare for you and your family. It is designed to meet the needs of families who require that flexible one to one care for their children in their own home giving children that one to one attention.

I am available for flexible daytime care or for babysitting in the evening. This can be for an hour or two or for a whole day

Additional childcare is available through a network of professional nannies in the Winchester and surrounding areas that are qualified to NVQ level 3 or equivalent. If you are looking for a nanny on a more permanent and contractual basis or extra ad hoc care; I could help. I have worked in Winchester for 20 years and have met a variety of both families and nannies; I have successfully matched many families with nannies. Often parents work full time and never really see a nanny with their children; they rely on references for that reassuring help. I have helped a number of parents to find a reputable nanny that I have seen personally at work with children and that I have known many years, I do not run a nanny agency as I will only pass on details of select nannies and babysitters of the highest calibre who I have observed personally and professionally at work with children.
