We also offer talent buying services, buying talent for both venues, as well as Festivals and events.

Editor

West Texas Blues Magazine

January 2013 – Present (1 year 9 months) Midland, TX

Exploring Our Community and Creating Connections

West Texas Blues is a quarterly lifestyle and leisure magazine that explores West Texas communities and creates connections by reaching beyond the music into other areas of adventure such as art, leisure, nightlife, travel, entertainment, food and health.

West Texas Blues is geared primarily toward a readership aged 21-65. Our readers contribute to their communities, embody the entrepreneurial spirit, and live their lives with gusto! They inspire; they create; they relish in the joys of life and they live abundantly. Our readers travel with curiosity; they purposely seek adventure; they think in possibilities. They are a diverse cross-section of people who aspire to be better tomorrow than they are today.

West Texas Blues is a complimentary, quarterly magazine distributed throughout the West Texas area four times a year in January, April, July and October.

The distribution area includes the larger markets of Midland, Odessa, San Angelo, the Trans-Pecos region of Alpine/Marfa/Fort Davis/Marathon/Terlingua, as well as extending to the small surrounding West Texas towns amongst this larger-market radius.  

We look for distribution points in each community that are independently owned and operated.

West Texas Blues is published by Promising Projects, Inc. and is a full-color publication trimmed to a finished size of 8.5 X 11.

We specifically seek advertisers that embody the spirit of entrepreneurship and add vibrancy to their community by making thoughtful products, services and experiences available to West Texans.

Tall City Blues Fest

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Music industry

April 2010 – Present (4 years 6 months) Midland, TX

Now in its fourth year, Tall City Blues Fest 2014, presented by Mazda Midland/Odessa, will be held July 25-27, 2014 in Centennial Plaza (Downtown Midland, TX).

The Fest is a multi-stage Blues music festival hosting award-winning performers across multiple stages. Tall City Blues Fest also hosts educational workshops, a silent art auction, a midnight spectator jam, an autographed memorabilia raffle and a Sunday Gospel Brunch.

Our Mission is to create irresistible fun in a family-friendly environment that contributes positively to the West Texas quality of life.

Go to www.tallcitybluesfest.com for more info!
