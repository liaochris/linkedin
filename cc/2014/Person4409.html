Nancy Rae Evans' Summary

I have been guiding people in managing both their personal and business finances for nearly two decades. My certifications: Financial Recovery Counselor by the Financial Recovery Institute, Financial Health Counselor by the National Association of Certified Credit Counselors and Registered Tax Return Preparer.

My holistic approach to money issues helps clients up their earnings, break the debt cycle, and build meaningful savings. They learn practical money management skills and identify the hidden aspects of money that have blocked them from financial success. I also assist high-earning professionals to reconnect to their values so they can shift their focus from work to other passions, without downsizing their lifestyle. Prior to my financial counseling practice, I worked with business owners in managing cash flow for six years and as a mortgage professional for almost ten years. My practice includes live seminars and tele-counseling sessions to clients throughout New England.

For info on the financial counseling process and upcoming seminars visit http://www.EmbracingMoney.com

Nancy Rae Evans' Experience

Personal Money Coach & Speaker

Embracing Money

November 2009 – Present (4 years 11 months) phone and in-person sessions

Through either private or group coaching, Nancy Rae Evans guides you through the process of discovering your beliefs, attitudes and behaviors around money that have been getting in your way. Common issues addressed include breaking the debt cycle, underearning, compulsive spending, anxiety over sudden wealth, and building meaningful savings. It's a two-pronged approach that heals your emotional money issues while building practical day-to-day money skills. As you identify what’s been getting in your way, you’ll find new ways to respond to your wants and needs—ways that support fulfillment of your dreams and passions!
