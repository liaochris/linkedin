Being a buyer in today's market can be very stressful! You hear so much about falling prices, people overpaying for houses, not qualifying for a loan under today's strict lending rules, and the pitfalls of buying foreclosed properties from banks. Don't worry, though! Mark Lackey, President of Atlanta Housing Source, has got your covered! Mark's team of highly trained real estate professionals can help you find the right property at the right price. They can also help you with everything from repairing your credit to saving thousands of dollars at the closing!

Sellers are finding this economy just as difficult as buyers, if not more so. If you're like most sellers, you're probably wondering if it's even possible to sell your house in today's market. No one wants to list their house and have it sit there with no offers. The good news is in most cases you can sell your house. To help you in this important decision, Mark's team will give you an honest assessment of what your house can actually sell for. They'll also suggest ways to increase your house's value without costing you a lot of money. If you're behind in your payments, they have team members who can help you negotiate a possible reduced payoff on your mortgage. This is called a short sale, and it's much less damaging to your credit than a foreclosure.

Mark Lackey has been listing and selling real estate since 2005. He's the President of Atlanta Housing Source and the Vice-President of Solid Source Property Management. He's an associate broker and is EcoBroker® certified. He also taught at Barney Fletcher RealtyU. Mark's team consists of inspectors, credit restoration experts, lenders, and buyers agents, all of whom are highly trained in their areas of expertise. Mark's number one goal is to help buyers and sellers alike achieve their goals in today's real estate marketplace. Mark is an active member of his church. In addition, he's very involved with many Georgia area Real Estate Investor Associations.
