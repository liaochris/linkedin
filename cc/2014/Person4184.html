Maura Mendoza's Projects

  • "¿Ding-Dong: Quién Es? - Playing and Learning Latin Rhythms"

    • August 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Maura Mendoza, Jeremy Quick

    Multicultural and Bilingual show for kids “Ding-Dong: Quién Es? - Playing and Learning Latin Rhythms” is an interactive act that aims to introduce families to Latin songs and Hispanic heritage.

  • "ViVA!" EP production of 7 original tracks

    • January 2011 to November 2011
    Team Members: Maura Mendoza, Jeremy Quick, Jungho Kang, John Servo, Brant Grieshaber, Mina Cho, Roy Gonzalez, Julee Avallone

    From bossa nova to pop, from reggae to rock, our album ViVA! is a pristine showcase of Latin rhythms packed in a jazzy flavor!

  • "Singing for Public Speaking" Empower your voice with vocal techniques

    • June 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Maura Mendoza, jeremy quick

    “Singing for Public Speaking: Empower your voice with vocal techniques” is an interactive workshop where you will learn fun tools (like singing!) to have your voice ready every time you give a lecture or conference, lead crowds, have phone meetings, and more! Maura Mendoza shares some of her vocal skills in a two-hours workshop. You will learn smart and easy tips to bring out your voice with a natural volume without stressing your vocal chords.

  • "Ciudad Mujer: La Fuerza de Nuestra Voz"

    • May 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Maura Mendoza, Paulino Espinoza, Julio Herrera Rohrer

    A full length album with more than 110 women from El Salvador including indigenous groups, minorities, musicians, composers and performers. Sharing credits with talented musicians from my country of origin and Julieta Venegas, from Mexico.
