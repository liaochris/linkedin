Kireet Khurana's Summary

- 5 President's National Awards, 3 Int'l awards besides others.
- Conceptualiser, Co-writer & Director of "TOONPUR KA SUPERRHERO", India's 1st live-action and 3D animation combination feature film. Starring: Ajay Devgn, Kajol. http://www.toonpur.com
- Directed and Co-produced award-winning short films for NATIONAL FILM BOARD OF CANADA, UNICEF International (1995-97)
- Creator, Director, Screenplay of award-winning series "ADVENTURES OF CHHOTA BIRBAL" (2002), Premiered on Cartoon Network, India's 1st animation character to be licenced.
- Jury member: President's National Awards (2007), Mumbai Int'l Film Festival (2007), CNBC Golden Cursor Awards (2008), Jaipur Int'l Film Festival (2011 & 2012), Sony-Animax Awards (2007), Indian Documentary Producers Association (2003), Frameflixx & others.

Specialties: Film-making, Commercials, Animation production & Education
