Tai Aguirre's Summary

I am passionate about my role as Senior Incentive Consultant and Director Business Development for Taico® Incentive Services. Taico® is a proud WBENC, certified woman owned enterprise. We specialize in employee recognition and incentive programs. What differentiates us is our technology platform, unlimited choices of awards, consultative partnering approach and performance measurement.

In 1987 we launched Taico® Incentive Services and in 2001 Taico® Productions was founded. Both companies have received awards and recognition from their respective industries as innovators and being on the cutting edge.
Taico® Productions, a separate company works closely with Taico® Incentives for media projects for our clients.

In 1996 Taico® Incentive Services received 'Best New Product' award at NYC’s Premium Incentive Show Jacob Javits Center for YOUR PERSONAL SONG™, (original music for any occasion). In 1998,Taico® Incentive Services again won 'Best New Product' for its technological innovation INCENTIWEB®, "One of the first in the incentive industry to create corporate, promotional and incentive programs on-line.
Taico® Productions Corporation provides audio products, media programming/production and recording services.
In 1998 Tai moved to radio and radio production. As producer, director, host, community affairs director and VP of Sales at WTBQ 1110 AM in New York.
Selling airtime to local and national businesses, Rotary Clubs, business groups, school and community organizations, publicizing radio programs and station features and benefits. Tai’s radio creations include THE TEEN SHOW™, a hit program for teens to express related issues and ideas, featuring positive role models such as teen entrepreneurs and musicians. “Could YOU Be Next? "News Stories the Mainstream Media Won’t Dare Talk About!" and The Expat Show™ global show about the expat community.
My jazz trio performs weekly. Shadow Over Hawk Lake is my original screenplay drama.

Specialties

Incentive consulting, marketing and sales, Business development strategies. Film and music composition, guitarist, original programming for media and screenwriter.
