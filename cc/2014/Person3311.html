Jack Patton, MBA, MM's Summary

As IPFW's Executive Director of Marketing Communications, Jack serves as the university’s Chief Marketing Officer and oversees its marketing communications efforts, including strategy, creative and web services, social media, advertising, analytics, and photography. His experience in the creative services industry includes working with small, privately held businesses, as well as publicly traded companies through strategic omni-channel endeavors on agency and internal marketing teams. Interests include creating access to information, idea execution, and delivering measurable demand. Specialties include omni-channel marketing and analytics (b2b and b2c), strategic planning, marketing and creative services operations, brand management, market research and data analysis, and project management.
