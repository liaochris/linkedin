Barry Feldman's Summary

Want to turn on the power of online marketing?

I'm Barry, your point man at Feldman Creative, where we deliver content marketing, copywriting, and creative direction for online marketing projects of every kind.

Feldman Creative was founded in the Silicon Vally in 1995, when I escaped alive from the advertising agency business. It's been going strong for 20 years. The company has partnered with hundreds of corporate marketing groups, small businesses, ad agencies, and design firms across a spectrum of product categories.

The company creates:
Websites/blogs/eBooks/video/podcasts/presentations/all types of brand-building content...

Mostly my team and I help you get more traffic, leads and sales by using inbound marketing tactics.

Consulting services are offered for:
Strategic marketing planning/social media/content marketing/SEM

As chief writer, I author articles, eBooks, presentations and webinars on all of these topics. Barry is a frequent contributor to over 10 of the web's top publishers on the subject of online marketing.

I'm also available to speak, coach and deliver training for online marketing, blogging, copywriting, and personal branding.
