Gahlord Dewald's Summary

Gahlord Dewald is a strategist. He works with organizations on several continents to solve wicked problems involving audience, technology, and developing business capabilities.

For more than 15 years Dewald has developed web sites, built and improved marketing teams, made creative things like animation or design or music or film or branding and identity materials. His blending of number and narrative, analytics and creative, have become admired by his clients and by the industries he serves.

He can be found speaking to audiences who value his not-gonna-water-it-down approach to making complex things clear. He has presented from the stages of Blogworld, The Expeditionary War School at Quantico, Inman Connect, Global Strategic Management Institute and others.

Outside of business pursuits, Gahlord Dewald is active in the athletic and creative cultures of Burlington, Vermont. He performs on occasion as a bassist in situations ranging from community orchestras to improvisatory rock ensembles. He is an amateur biathlete, practicing martial artist, and bicycle commuter.

Dewald is most motivated by projects that develop data and information in order to assemble meaning and make things people like.
