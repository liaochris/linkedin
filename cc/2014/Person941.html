Chris Johnson's Summary

I am currently the President & CEO at Pegasus Lighting, a nationally recognized e-commerce website that sells unique lighting products to a wide spectrum of customers. We carefully blend technology with the personal touch of outstanding customer service and an information-rich website.

I joined the Raleigh office of Pegasus Lighting in April of 2007 as Director of Marketing & Technology. At the beginning of 2009 I was promoted to Vice President and in January 2014 I became the President & CEO. I graduated with a B.A. and an M.B.A., both from Virginia Tech. While working on my M.B.A., I focused on Information and Decision Support Systems. Since graduation I have worked for Arthur Andersen Business Consulting and BlueCross BlueShield of North Carolina.

Specialties: Web technology, Wordpress, phpBB, Yahoo e-commerce, SEO, social media, personnel management, branding, server architecture, marketing, public relations, database technology, data warehouse
