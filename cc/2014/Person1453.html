Melissa Daniels' Summary

An experienced Program and Community Manager, Melissa is focused on developing strategy and consumer/brand relations.

Melissa's background includes a strong focus on retail, technology, hardware and consumer goods, connecting users and companies in unique ways through technology. Her experince as a Program Manager has helped bring new products to market as well as bring up new countries (internationalization / expansion of retail presence of both on and offline channels).

A Community Management veteran (8+ years), Melissa uses her experience to guide brand presence across the web leveraging social media In new and creative ways, ensuring that a company has a positive presence wherever customers are having conversations about that product.
