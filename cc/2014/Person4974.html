Sean Reid's Summary

Sean has worked on Staff for the TV Guide show "Square Off", "E! News Live", the CBS show "Day & Date", Fox's "The Rob Nelson Show" and the long running ABC franchise "America's Funniest Home Videos". Freelance Produced Network Promos for Fox, Starz, Tyler Perry's "House of Payne", PBS Sprout and DIY "Let's Get Dirty" Campaign that included a run in theaters (the first time for DIY) to name a few.

After a successful festival run the the Indie Feature"Redemption" that Sean served as Producer/Director and Writer was released on Home Video by Artisan Home Entertainment(Lions Gate), aired on Starz in Black for eighteen months as well as receiving distribution in Europe and Africa. Sean was a key contributor to the E! Concert Series that featured groups like Everclear, Smash Mouth, Steel Pulse, Sixpence None the Richer and Robert Palmer. As well Directing/Co-Producing the DVD Premiere Awards with Host Ben Stein.

Under his new banner New Cinema Tribe Sean has recently completed the web series "NorHollywood" and is in pre-production on the "Mulholland Report".

An accomplished Non-Union 1st Assistant Director often working as a Producer/1st AD it enables him to remain fully involved in a production.

Specialties: Single and Multiple Camera Direction,1st/2nd AD, Screenwriting, Post Production, Field Production, Crew Booking, Deal Negotiations, Research, EP Scheduling and Budgeting, AFTRA / SAG Paperwork, Music Clearance, Live Shots, Work Flow Problem Solving, Fixer.
