Bill Church's Summary

Bill Church, brings extensive experience as a journalist to his present role as a freelance writer and blogger. Since moving to Austin, Texas in 2009, Bill has founded and written a very popular blog about the Austin TV news scene, "The Austin Listening Post." As a freelance writer, he has written for the Impact newspaper chain and produced marketing materials for corporations.

From 2004-2009, Bill founded and ran his own communications company, Florida-based Sandpiper Public Relations. He interviewed leading South Florida executives and wrote numerous company and personal profiles for the Scripps Treasure Coast Newspaper chain. For National City Bank’s (now PNC's) Florida operations, Bill researched and wrote a quarterly online employee newsletter. Bill also served as an adjunct instructor in communications at Florida Atlantic University, teaching courses in news reporting & writing and in public relations.

Previously, Bill had a very successful career in television news at four different major market network affiliates. After early years as a news writer, on-air reporter and news producer, Bill rose to news director, leading all news writing and production operations of a 40-person San Antonio, Texas network television affiliate.

Bill then began a second career, directing media relations at a Fortune 500 multinational manufacturer, at a large utility and for a major region of one of the world's largest international professional services firms. A major portion of his time included the writing of news releases, news “pitch” letters, internal print and electronic newsletters, and speeches.

Bill earned bachelors and masters degrees in journalism from the Medill School of Journalism at Northwestern University,

Specialties

Writing for print or web, editing, specialties in personal and corporate profiles, business, sports, politics, music, food & wine, internal and external corporate writing, media training,especially for broadcast.
