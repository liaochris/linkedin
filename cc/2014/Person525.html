Honors & Awards

Rocky Mountain Emmy for Investigative Reporting

National Academy of Television Arts and Sciences
October 2010

I received this award as part of an investigative team. In 2010, the advanced media (online) categories were combined with the broadcast categories, so we entered the online portion and the broadcast as a package. The broadcast portion, which I did not work on, consisted of a nearly 10-minute report and followups on abuse of power allegations against Maricopa County Sheriff Joe Arpaio. I produced the online portion of the entry, which included a Vuvox interactive featuring information about Arpaio's alleged targets and video of him blowing off our reporter; a blog about Arpaio's latest activities; and a special section with previous coverage on the sheriff.

Rocky Mountain Emmy Award for Advanced Media - Investigative Report

National Academy of Television Arts and Sciences
October 2009

I received this award as part of a team for a report on the border patrol. The five-day series of television reports was complemented by an online section that I produced, which included multiple stories based on the television reports, videos of the original reports as well as extended editions, raw footage and links to related content, among other items.
