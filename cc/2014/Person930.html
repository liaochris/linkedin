Chris Boggs' Summary

Digital marketing specialist with 14 years of experience of driving ROI with organic search engine optimization (SEO), paid search advertising (PPC) campaign creation and management, social media consulting, and use of Web analytics to improve integration between online marketing and branding performance.

Recently founded my own company, Web Traffic Advisors, LLC, to coach individuals and teams and provide consulting related to SEO and digital marketing integration. See more at webtrafficadvisors.com

"SEO Rockstars," podcast co-host at WebmasterRadio.fm and iHeartRadio. Long-time part-time blogger at Various including Search Engine Watch, State of Digital, and others. Regular speaker at industry conferences including Search Engine Strategies (Now ClickZ Live), Pubcon and i-com.org (serving on Board since 2012). Search Engine Marketing Professionals Organization (SEMPO, Inc). Board member since 2006 serving as President 2010-2012 and Chair 2012-2014. Judge, U.S. Search Awards 2013 and 2014.

Specialties: coaching, digital marketing integration, search engine optimization, paid media, social media, web analytics, ethical seo, training
