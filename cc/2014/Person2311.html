Boris Dragovic's Summary

Boris is an experienced manager with proven track record of 8+ years. His professional experience ranges across the board from Academia via foundation of a High-Tech Startup, extensive exposure to Management Consulting (McKinsey & Company and Bain) to having assumed a key role in the Government of Serbia.

Success in the aforementioned roles illustrates Boris' entrepreneurial, self-starting and challenge driven personality. Variety of assumed positions has enabled Boris to acquire excellent managerial, analytic and leadership toolbox which is complemented by strong communication skills and an open, easily-approachable personality.

Boris' formal education is in engineering (IT/Telco, PhD) and business (MBA) providing him with depth of knowledge required to understand and tackle complex challenges in today's business/ industry environments.

Particularly passionate about the following functions and industries:
