Greg Matthews' Summary

I advise Global and Fortune 500 healthcare companies on social media, communications and enterprise change management. I'm currently focused on understanding physicians' online behavior, and am the originator of MDigitalLife.com. I've also developed a model for teaching business leaders how to activate their professional and personal networks online, and am preparing to publish my first book on the subject.

I've spent my career helping to build, grow and transform businesses. Today, that usually involves using social media - the power of collaboration and community - to change the way businesses work. Every business has its own set of unique stakeholders - from customers to suppliers; from strategic partners to its own employees. I'm helping companies to make the changes that lead to business transformation by helping them to change the way they communicate, organize and plan around those key stakeholder groups. I was a charter member of #HCSM (Healthcare/Social Media professionals) and am the co-founder of the CoHealth Workplace Wellness tweetchat.

As Director of Consumer Innovation at Humana, I had two key areas of work. The first was to guide Humana in its quest to be a leader in the coming collaborative economy. The second was to leverage the power of social networks to help people live healthier and more fulfilling lives.

During my tenure at Humana, I have had responsibility for the start-up HR operations for joint venture companies like Green Ribbon Health and Sensei, and in Humana’s first European subsidiary in London. I led the design and launch of the innovation center's first web site, CrumpleItUp.com, and built my industry's first social analytics tool, myTPSreport.com.
