Adam Guerin's Summary

I built my first commercial website in 1995 and have never looked back. Funny thing is, back in 1995, most people we’re still telling me that I needed to stop wasting time on this “internet thing”. :)

In the last two decades since then I have built literally thousands of websites, ran hundreds of digital marketing campaigns and trained countless small businesses on how to get the most out of digital.

I am an experienced Web Designer and Builder, and my focus is on delivering high quality websites that are responsive in design to all modern devices and use a content management system. This allows my clients to take full control of their website, and reduce their ongoing costs by removing licence fees and expensive contracts.

I have run many genuine search engine optimisation campaigns (SEO) which have actually worked - because I do them right. It’s all about content and understanding keyword relevancy. Tricks aside, my focus is on a campaign that delivers measurable results.

I currently also provide live workshop training and mentoring for small businesses around Western Australia. Whether it’s a group workshop or one on one mentoring, my aim is to educate and empower small businesses so that they can do digital right.

Social Media and customer engagement are key to digital success, and much of my training and strategy is focused in these areas now. Having been involved with a project to actually build a social network some years ago (before Facebook became the behemoth it is today), I am well versed in social networking and most certainly one of the original early adopters.

My goal and passion is really simple - helping small business “do digital right”.
