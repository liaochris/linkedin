Erika Feinberg's Summary

For more than 20 years, Erika has assisted some of the top brand software and media institutions, and venture capitalists plan and launch businesses, resulting in market domination. She also funded and co-founded two very successful businesses in the digital marketing and healthcare spaces. Since being appointed CEO and Chief Happiness Officer of ActiveForever.com, she has grown the business over 130X, as well as established ActiveForever as one of the highest ranked, credible and beloved brands in the industry.

ActiveForever is filling a mission of infusing creativity and alternative solutions for the improvement of care outcomes and quality of life for both the care providers and patients alike.
ActiveForever.com is a national eRetailer, Retailer, Print Publisher & Warehouse/Distributor of Medical Equipment, Safety & Productivity Supplies, Physical Conditioning, Healing Devices and other Occupational Health & Safety Equipment. The company is well known for its ability to invigorate, market and sell innovative product lines, and its ability to keep healthcare providers and consumers abreast of the latest product innovations available to help treatment & conditioning outcomes.

Erika has earned an undergraduate Bachelor of Science degree in Marketing and Mass Communications, and Applied Physiology from Boston University in 1985, as well as Advanced Studies in Business from Boston University in 1987.

Erika is also one of the founding investment partners of a now world renown non-profit venture philanthropy organization named Social Venture Partners.

The company is also known for it's work with the pharmaceutical and surgical parts industries; creating and executing on compliant and very effective lead generation programs.
