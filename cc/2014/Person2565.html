    He turns to close friend, Krish, a researcher in Artificial Intelligence, in the hope that they can come up with ideas for the Military and the Entertainment market.

    Krish gets a job at the prestigious A.I.R.I. Using AIRI's lab, Krish and Dan, create an advanced visor with Augmented Intelligence built in.

    A Board member at AIRI sees potential in the Wizer other than what Dan and Krish have in mind.

    At a test in a nightclub, things go wrong...

Clyde DeSouza's Projects

  • User Experience Room

    • July 2008 to December 2010
    Team Members: Clyde DeSouza

    Experiential Marketing is the art of creating an experience where the hoped-for result is an emotional connection to a person, brand, product or idea. It is a form of marketing or advertising based on the principle of marketing a product or brand through an experience rather than the placement of advertisements.

    While an experience can be had in many ways, for example a wine tasting to promote a new brand of wine or champagne, to a virtual golf simulator to promote the sport, in the year 2009 and entering 2010, what would be considered ground breaking or a next-gen way of providing an experience to an audience or consumer? This was the challenge Real Vision was faced with when appointed to brain storm and create a solution for the sales center for an ambitious "city" township coming being constructed...

  • 3D Lab for the Government of AbuDhabi

    • June 2010 to Present
    Team Members: Clyde DeSouza

    RealVision has created and implemented the Middle East's first Stereoscopic 3D Lab. The 3D Lab aims to foster best practices in stereoscopic 3d production and is a collaborative environment for media professionals and Production Departments, to come together and contribute to the evolution of stereoscopic 3D in Broadcast TV and Film production.

    Hardware and Software manufacturers as well as the media industry have shown keen interest in the 3D Lab activities

  • 3D media Lab - Nanyang Polytechnic, Singapore

    • March 2009 to Present
    Team Members: Clyde DeSouza, Jacques Frety

    Setup of a stereoscopic 3D Lab for students of Nanyang Polytechnic, Singapore.
    The Lab connects to a collaboration theater, with stereoscopic 3D projection. The environment encourages student/Teacher review and brainstorming of media production.
