George Easton's Projects

  • Red Dot Project

    • January 2012 to Present
    Team Members: George Easton, Eryn Corbeil

    The Red Dot Project is a issues-based media center aimed at eroding apathy among Canadian voters. Our mandate is simple; explore the issues that are nationally supported while examining how parties and traditional media work to distort the message. We believe Canadians are more complex than a battle of Left VS Right. Instead, we believe Canadians are versatile, with views that break down the political barriers built to polarize our country.

  • The Lowe Down

    • November 2012 to Present
    Team Members: George Easton, Kristin Lowe, Ed Regan

    The Lowe Down is a weekly webisode for the Design-It-Yourselfer. Kristin Lowe embarks on a knowledge sharing, and confidence building foray into the world of designing your dream home, kitchen and/or bathroom. The goal of The Lowe Down is to inspire everyone with a hint of vision to be able to accomplish the dreaming, start the planning and begin the art of successful implementation of their desired renovations. These webisodes are designed to raise the bar of knowledge and expectation one notch at a time, taking its viewers from dreamers to doers.
