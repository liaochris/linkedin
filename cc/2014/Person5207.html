Tarun Wadhwa's Summary

Tarun is a writer, researcher, and entrepreneur working at the intersection of technology, international development, and public policy.

He writes regularly for Forbes on his column, “Disruption and Democracy,” and his articles regularly appear in The Huffington Post, Venture Beat, and others. His work has been published in several major media outlets and he has given talks at universities and conferences around the world.

Identified: Why They Are Getting To Know Everything About Us, a book he’s authored about the global rise of digital identification systems, will be out in 2014. It analyzes the technologies that governments and companies use to see who we are — things such as national identification systems, biometrics, and online identity. He tracks these systems over time to see how they are changing governments, institutions, social norms, and the daily lives of people all around the world. Learn more, and sign-up for updates at http://identified.info

He is also working on a start-up related to water purification and international development.

You can see more of his work on his website: http://tarunwadhwa.com. Follow him on Twitter - @twadhwa, or you can reach him directly at: linkedin@tarunwadhwa.com.
