Background

Summary

Specialties: branding + positioning; business dev; mktg + product strategy; product design; teambuilding; product + market fit; usability; tech development; writing + promotions; social; research; digital health and wellness; innovation; business networking; fundraising

I'm a creative tech leader with a strong product, business dev and mktg background. I have over 20 yrs of experience in new pdct development, B2B and B2C software, educational IT, healthcare software, payment tech, gaming, sports, retail, venture capital, econ development, and the non-profit sectors. My leadership philosophy focuses on transparency, empowerment, communication, and growth.

I currently run a startup accelerator based in Ohio -- started by Dan Gilbert, Founder of Quicken Loans Inc. and majority owner of the NBA’s Cleveland Cavaliers.

I consult with a range of startups and established businesses. Past projects include product strategy, design, mktg and business dev for large organizations including Intel, Motorola, Adobe, Palm, Ubisoft, Diebold, and Harvard Medical School and many high growth startups.

I'm the founder of Tribe of Angels, an international network of over 8000 angel investors, researchers, journalists, executives, and entrepreneurs in 25 countries.
