Stephen Liguori's Summary

“INNOVATION CAN’T BE DONE IN THE VACUUM OF THE R&D LAB ANYMORE”

Ask Steve Liguori how innovation can work in today’s organization, and he’ll tell you to get everyone involved. It’s part of “horizontal strategic thinking” principles Steve espouses as Founder and CEO of Liguori Innovation. Steve helps organizations identify and create new sources of growth for both existing lines of business and potential new categories. In large measure, he applies start-up principles that stress leadership, speed and improved customer outcomes in complex organizations.

LED INNOVATION AT GENERAL ELECTRIC THAT TRANSORMED THEIR SPEED-TO-MARKET

Steve acts as an “Innovation Accelerator” says Beth Comstock, CMO of General Electric, “if you’re looking for leadership and action to accelerate innovation in your firm, Steve is your ‘go to’ expert. He helped advance GE’s critical initiatives over the past decade and his passion for Innovation is infectious.” As GE’s Executive Director of Global Innovation, Steve was instrumental in major efforts that helped transform and re-invigorate GE’s organic growth and speed-to-market with measureable results.

LEVERAGES DIVERSE INDUSTRY EXPERIENCE TO QUICKLY ASSESS OPPORTUNITIES

Steve brings thought leadership and in-market successes in categories as diverse as consumer packaged goods, heavy industry, financial services, software and healthcare. This broad range of industry sector experience enables Steve to “hit the ground running” to assess an organization’s challenges and surface opportunities not seen before.

Steve is currently an adjunct Professor at Columbia Business School, and serves as Global Chairman of the Business Marketing Association – the world's largest organization of business-to-business professionals. Steve’s distinctions include Marketer of the Year, Harvard Business Review author, Chairmans’ Award winner at both Citigroup and GE, and launching the Super Bowl halftime show with none other than Michael Jackson.
