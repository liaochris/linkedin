Mike Connor's Summary

A forward-thinking business marketing leader with an extensive background in selling and marketing cutting-edge technology including cloud and SaaS solutions.

Passion: Creating Insanely Great Customers through value led innovation

Mike Blogs at: http://www.creatinginsanelygreat.com/blog/

Highly regarded for customer insights, market and technical knowledge, and keen business acumen in preparing and executing strategic and tactical plans. Credited with key contributions in penetrating new accounts and markets, attaining product leadership positions, and driving sales with start-ups as well as Fortune 500 companies.

Engaging and decisive; able to empower and lead cross-functional, top-performing teams in fast-paced environments. Strong believer in agile design, development and delivery. Passionate about working smart to deliver great results.

Mike Connor's Publications

  • Creating an Insanely Great Customer Journey

    • Spice Catalyst
    • March 2, 2013
    Authors: Mike Connor

    Creating an Insanely Great Customer Journey is an Agile Planning and Management Guide. It establishes a vision for and step by step guidance and worksheets that enable companies to align their vision, people process and systems to create a next generation customer journey. Next generation journeys establish on ongoing interactive relationship of continual learning and innovation between companies and their highest value prospects and customers.

  • Predator or Prey | An Agile Business Acceleration Guide

    • Spice Catalyst
    • November 10, 2012

Agile Business Acceleration aligns vision and strategy, people, organizations, processes and systems to support deep customer engagement and continual learning and innovation.
