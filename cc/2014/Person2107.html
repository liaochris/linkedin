Anita Gulotta-Connelly's Summary

Anita Gulotta-Connelly has over 30 years of experience in the transit industry. In 2011 she retired from her position as President and Managing Director of the Milwaukee County Transit System. She is currently providing consulting services to the public transportation industry through Connelly Coaching and Consulting, LLC. She is also providing life coaching and career transition assistance both directly and through the University of Wisconsin Career Transitions Center.

She is also the Immediate Past Chair of the Wisconsin Urban and Rural Transit Association (WURTA).

Gulotta-Connelly joined MCTS in 1979 as a planner and research analyst. She held a variety of positions including assistant director of operations, director of scheduling and planning, and director of administration. She has previously served as WURTA vice-chair and an at-large board member, as well as president of the Wisconsin Women’s Transportation Seminar. She is also a graduate of the American Public Transportation Association Leadership program. She has a degree in Political Science from Marquette University in Milwaukee.
