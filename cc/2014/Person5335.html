Trevor Bezdek's Summary

Trevor Bezdek founded Tryarc, a technology consulting firm headquartered in Los Angeles. He led the business, grew it without investment to over 100 employees, and sold it in 2007 to NTT DATA. NTT DATA is the systems integration division of NTT, a Global 50 company. He has served as Senior Vice President for Interactive at NTT DATA and was the Managing Director of the West Region for the Advisory Services division. Prior to Tryarc, he co-founded Biowire (now subsidiary of Infotrieve), leading the development of information tools for researchers. He served as a board member for RightsLine Software. Trevor graduated with honors in 1999 from Stanford University with degrees in Biological Sciences and Computer Science, and received the Fox Award for the top Biology undergraduate.
