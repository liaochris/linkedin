Rochelle Veturis Coles is the media and public relations director for green architectural design firm, LPA Inc. Since working at LPA, the company has received five times as many news clips (per month), with an estimated ad value in excess of $13.1 million. Last year alone, she worked with more than 400 newsprint, television, and online media outlets.

Additionally, Rochelle works with business and government organizations to get them up to speed with the "socialmediasphere" through seminars and private coaching.

She's been recognized as a Twitter Powerhouse by the Huffington Post and in 2012, created and produced the O.C. Social Media Summit. Through her involvement with social media Veturis has supported the Children’s Hospital of Orange County, Cameron Siemers Foundation, Irvine Public Schools Foundation, Compassion International, HomeAid Orange County, and Baldwin Park Dogs.
