Perry van Beek's Summary

Thank you for visiting my LinkedIn profile. My name is Perry van Beek and it is my mission to help you find more clients through LinkedIn using the principles of Smarter Social Selling.

Most of my clients tell me that once they get to talk to an interested prospect, they often manage to turn them into a client! My reaction: “Great, so what do you need me for?" They then usually share with me that getting to that interested prospect is an unpredictable, tedious and long road. Do you recognize that?

So why is it that clients aren't yet knocking down your door to buy from you? How long can your company survive without consistently getting more clients?

The answer lies in Social Selling. You may have heard of it yet you’re unsure where to start and may even feel a little bit overwhelmed.

I have developed a proven system that helps you get appointments with your ideal prospects in three easy steps:
 ● COLLECT 
● CONNECT
 ● CONVERT
It's easy to learn and to apply so that you too can start attracting new clients into your business.

Since 2009 I have developed, perfected and proven this system and I have since helped more than 5.000 entrepreneurs and sales professionals drastically turn their sales results around.
