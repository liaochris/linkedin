Angel Anderson's Summary

I like making new things and making existing things easier and more delightful to use. For the last 10+ years, I've been of humanizing digital products and services through goal-driven interaction design. My experience and skills have been utilized for the creation of enterprise software and large-scale online solutions for clients such as Dominos, Old Navy, Fox, Pepsi, Disney, and Mattel.

In addition to fostering intelligent design and brand love for clients at CP+B, I'm co-founder of a mobile/fashion startup called NailSnaps that lets you turn your Instagrams into custom nail art. http://bit.ly/nailsnaps.

I am active in the local design community as a guest lecturer at USC, UCLA, and The Arts Institute of California. I speak about interaction design techniques, human behavior, and evangelizing design as a means to advance business goals and humanize user experiences. Additionally, I promote and interaction design through on-going initiatives with the Interaction Design Association (IxDA.org).

Specialties: Mobile design, Internationalization, Large-scale online software, Social Networks, Information Design, Qualitative User Research, Taming complexity into submission.
