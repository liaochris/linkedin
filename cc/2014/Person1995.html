Alex Leo's Summary

I am a digital native, passionate about product, content strategy, user experience and business development. I've helped companies create products, editorial strategies, and sales plans across platforms and have supported all other stakeholders in the process.

I was named one of Forbes 30 Under 30 in Media in 2012 and chosen by NBC New York as one of the top 20 people to follow on Twitter. I am on the board of advisors to Internet Week New York, have spoken at and mentored students at Columbia’s publishing course for the past decade and am a regular contributor to PBS's MediaShift.

I have strong leadership capabilities and have built and managed teams across times zones at several organizations.
