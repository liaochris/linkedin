Mariem Henaine's Summary

I'm an actress with a broad range of theatre, film and dance experience. I studied acting at Vancouver Film School, ballet with the Royal Academy of Dance and I recently branched into production by studying Entertainment Administration at UBC. I have a lot of experience with short films and I'm looking to move on to bigger projects.

I am also very interested in film and theatre production, which is why I studied a Bachelor's Degree in Entertainment Business Management at one of the best business schools in Mexico City, Escuela Bancaria y Comercial.

Specialties: Fluent in Spanish and English (including British and Standard American accents). Dance skills include Scottish dancing, Classical Ballet (Vocational and Graded Examinations from the Royal Academy of Dance, London), Jazz, Ballroom dancing, Flamenco, and Latin.
