Quentin Aisbett's Summary

I'm a digital marketer with a strong focus on strategy but specific interests in analytics, SEO, conversion rate optimisation and content marketing.

Long ago, even before Facebook and YouTube, I graduated with a Bachelor Public Relations. My journey from university to today involved junior experience in a PR agency, before moving into a management role in the sporting industry.

There's a moment in your life that you just have to go where the energy is and that's why I started OnQ in 2010. We provide SMEs with marketing support packages so they can access a specific set of online marketing skills and technical know-how without hiring a digital marketing manager or a big agency.

We're always on the search for clients with ambitions for greatness but if I don't find you I'd be rapt with working with a wine client??

Specialties: Ok i'll list a few marketing disciplines or specialties: analytics, SEO, conversion rate optimisation, social media and mobile. Love them all and it's the passion that drives the ability.
