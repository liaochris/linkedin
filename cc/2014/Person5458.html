William Pegues, FCSI CCS SCIP Affiliate's Summary

37 plus years in writing architectural specifications for commercial, high rise residential, hotel/hospitality, interiors, government, and historical restoration projects. Founded the Northern Virginia Chapter of the Construction Specifications Institute (CSI), Fellow of CSI. Currently head of specifications for WDG Architecture for both the Washington, DC and Dallas, Texas offices.

Virtual Worlds/Second Life and Benefits for Real World Architecture and the Construction Industry: A presentation with slides, web and online tour of Second Life.

Specialties

Materials research and writing specifications, including coordinating civil, structural, mechanical, plumbing, electrical and other disciplines.
