Catherine Mulbrandon's Summary

As you will see my background is an unique mix of economics, finance, and design. My data graphics and dashboards present economic and financial data in context that helps people understand —at a glance— what the numbers really mean.

I launched VisualizingEconomics.com in 2006, a website dedicated to publishing infographics about economic data. In 2013, my book "An Illustrated Guide to Income in the United States" was published.

I worked for 5+ years on large data-driven web applications including an online brokerage software for independent financial advisors. Previously I spented 7+ years working for a financial consulting company as both a senior research associate and later as a programmer.

MDes Interaction Design, Carnegie Mellon
AB Economics, University of Chicago

View selected projects from my portfolio covering data visualization and interaction design at VisualizingEconomics.com
