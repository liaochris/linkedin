Background

Summary

Nick Gilham is a Community Manager at Nestle Purina focused on Purina Pro Plan. He manages Pro Plan's social media presence including Facebook, Twitter and Instagram.

Before joining Purina, Nick worked with clients in a variety of industries including Yahoo! and Energizer and has over 10 years of experience in information technology and digital marketing. He holds an MBA from Washington University’s Olin Business School and a BS in Information Systems from the University of Missouri – St. Louis.

He enjoys networking and giving back to the community. Nick is a board member of Social Media Club St. Louis and serves on marketing committee for the St. Louis Area Foodbank.

He is an adjunct professor at the University of Missouri – St. Louis where he is creating and teaching an Introduction to Social Media Marketing class in the newly developed digital marketing program. He also serves on the university’s Digital Media Marketing Advisory Board.

Specialties: Social media marketing, social media strategy, consumer marketing, project management, personal branding, LinkedIn training, web development, search engine optimization, content creation strategy
