Background

Summary

Philippe Reinaudo has over 20 years of experience in post-production for film, television and advertising. It has, among other things, been special effects supervisor in the 90s and Éclair laboratories CTO in the 2000s.

It is in this company that was in charge of the creation and optimization of one of the world leading digital cinema laboratories. It is in this context that he supervised the finishing of more than 100 feature films and the creation of the first European DCP and the first 4K DI in Europe.

In 2010, Philippe Reinaudo founded Firefly Cinema. Firefly Cinema is specialized in software development for film. It offers a complete pipeline from on-set rushes management to film post- production.

Firefly Cinema products are FirePlay, a free player for DOP and AC, FireShoot, on-set rushes management tool, FireDay, digital dailies software, and FireCloud color grading and finishing solution for the post-production.
