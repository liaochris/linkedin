Valerie Valenzuela's Summary

Well rounded real estate professional with over 15 years of experience working in the San Fernando Valley and bordering cities. With a background in banking, working as Foreclosure Specialist, double major in Finance & Real Estate, and family with over 38 years of experience; I have been successful in providing invaluable information and resources to consumers and sellers of real estate.

I have great work ethic and I believe in providing people with honest information to let them make informed decisions. As a real estate professional, people perceive my profession as being a "salesman" and that's not my job. "Selling" is a by-product of what I do and my main objective is to inform people, provide them with resources, and share my experience and knowledge to ensure they have a great experience and a smooth transaction.

My goal on Linked-In is to network with professionals in different industries and become a resource to one another, in the event that assistance is ever needed.
