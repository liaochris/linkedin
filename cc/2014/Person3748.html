Kara Skruck Williams' Summary

I'm an award-winning freelance journalist who has covered topics ranging from business and babies to skiing and spas in my 23-year writing career.

Of the many editorial hats I've worn, I've been a corporate copyeditor, magazine editor, newspaper reporter and online-marketing writer.

Lately, my interests have turned to travel writing and blogging, but I continue to keep my toes in the corporate world, working with clients for whom I write newsletter copy and press releases.

My articles have appeared in Every Day with Rachael Ray, Ocean Home, Thomas Cook Travel, Working Mother, Interval World, Sunset, American Eagle Latitudes, 5280, Denver Life, Dallas Morning News and Mountain Parent. Online you can find my work at Away.com, Fodors.com, FamilyVacationCritic.com and PracticalTravelGear.com.

I pen a monthly travel column for The Denver Post, and I write regularly about worldwide destinations for the Expedia Viewfinder blog.

I have written for custom publications produced by Weaver Multimedia Group, Inspirato, The Broadmoor, Vail Resorts, AffordableTours.com, Aspen Chamber Resort Association and Glenwood Springs Chamber.

I have spoken about travel writing, social media and blogging at multiple conferences and events across the United States.

Specialties: Writing, editing, proofreading. Editorial articles, newsletters, press releases, advertorials, ad copy, brochure copy, blog posts.
