Brad McCarty's Summary

I believe in content.
I believe in the power of stories.
I believe that everyone has a story to tell, but most people simply don't know the words to use.
I believe that companies should spend less time talking and more time listening.

I believe that there's never been a better time than right now.

At FullContact I'm in charge of all of our content marketing, but I also handle corporate communications and a range of other duties as the needs arise. From wireframing new site designs to managing a group of extraordinarily talented contributors or running a mass of A/B tests, no two days are alike.
