Background

Summary

Dawn McDaniel was born in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada. At a young age her mom could not figure how how Dawn could memorize every TV commercial, jingle, slogan and logos for different companies. It wasn't until working for a production/post facility that she realized and embraced her passion for the advertising / marketing industry. Dawn's experience ranges from production to post, print, interactive medias (both online and offline) and more.

Dawn brings a unique combination of skill and experience to the field of emerging technology applications. With a background in multi-media concept & design, she offers clients and colleagues the best of two worlds – one where the market demands up-to-the-minute technological expertise…and the other where business only respects success and a proven track record.

Dawn’s range of success is as varied as the clients she’s worked with. From the budget-conscious needs of start ups to the intense requirements of world-class entertainment companies like Universal Studios, Marriott Vacation Club International, EA Sports and more.

In a business world where opportunity evolves and accelerates at the same pace that technology advances, Dawn’s background has prepared her to not only accept innovation but to embrace it. Emerging technological applications that address developing markets need visionaries who see trends, identify favorable circumstances and seize opportunities. Dawn McDaniel has the ability to do all three.

They have no idea how hamburgers are made, all they know is they got it from McDonald's or the local grocery store. They think all the plants at Lowes and Home Depot grow right there in the store. We need to raise awareness to our youth that those things do not just magically appear. Farming isn't for everyone but if you don't know about it, how do you know it's not for you?
