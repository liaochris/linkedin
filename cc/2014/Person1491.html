Mike O'Horo's Summary

35+ yrs exper in sales, marketing, strategy, sales training. Now created a virtual world where lawyers learn by doing. In simulated networking events, sales calls, beauty contests, etc., lawyers manage an avatar through a series of "say/do" decisions, receiving context-sensitive video coaching that explains why each choice was or wasn't optimal. Learn, make mistakes and polish your biz dev skills privately before applying them successfully in the real world.

Buy/sell decision wizard. Unlettered sales process engineer.
