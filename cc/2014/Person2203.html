Andrea Zweifel's Summary

Andrea currently works for UWM Lubar School of Business's Center for Technology & Innovation and SAP UCC as an Administrative Program Specialist.

Andrea attained her Master degree in Information Systems in 2009 and Bachelor degree in Business Management in 2007. She has provided help desk activities, has experience with C# programming, and teaches classes in various software applications. She is certified as a Crucial Conversations trainer and Microsoft Office Specialist (MOS) in Excel, Word and PowerPoint plus she has a Professional Certificate in Training.

Previously, at UWM's School of Continuing Education (SCE), Andrea was a Senior Program Associate. She was chair of the Classified Staff Committee and a previous member of the Classified Staff Advisory Council. She implemented and has led the SCE annual picnics (since 2001). She is acknowledged in various manuscripts, books, and articles for her proofreading skills. She enjoyed being a member of SCE's Website Redesign project team and on other project teams at SCE, such as Organizational Mapping, Workshop Hosting, Strategic Planning Working Group, Project Management Software and SCE's Green Team.

Andrea is a strong believer in professional development. She is involved in the International Association of Administrative Professionals (IAAP) and is the past President of the Downtown Milwaukee Chapter (DMC) (2013-2014). Currently, she's the 2014-2015 Vice President of DMC. She's held several roles, such as the Newsletter Editor (2011-current), Fundraiser, and co-Webmaster. She previously has held the roles of DMC Vice President (2012-2013); Webmaster (2012-2013) for the WI Division, DMC, and the Milwaukee Chapter; Instructor for the 2012 Administrative Professional Day event hosted by DMC. She has strong leadership skills. She has experience with event coordination and planning as well as coordinating and conducting conference calls, podcasts, webinars, and web-meetings.
