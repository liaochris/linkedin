Jane Boursaw's Summary

Hey Editors & Publishers - We sell editorial content! We're hooked into the entertainment industry with writers in New York and Los Angeles and all points in between. Movie reviews, red carpet coverage, photo galleries, TV reviews, celebrity interviews, list-y features, film festivals, books, music and more. Hit the Reel Life With Jane Shop link below and see what's new this week for kids, teens and grownups.

As for me, I'm the founder and editor-in-chief of Reel Life With Jane, and a veteran entertainment writer, reporter and blogger. I've written for hundreds of print and online publications, including The New York Times, People Magazine, Variety, and AOL's Moviefone, TV Squad and PopEater, where I was an editor and staff writer. If it has anything remotely to do with the entertainment industry, I've probably covered it during the past 30 years.
