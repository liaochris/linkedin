Summer Hagy's Projects

  • GAME:IT 10,000 - A STEM Initiative

    • May 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Summer Hagy, Kari Tatge, Jessica Rice, Carter Tatge, Tyler Tatge

    We want to excite students about STEM by offering a FREE STEM curriculum to all high schools!

    STEM Fuse is proud and excited to announce GAME:IT 10,000 – A STEM Initiative. We are offering our flagship course – GAME:IT – for FREE to the first 10,000 U.S. high schools who wish to participate. We were founded with the intent to develop a wave of STEM savvy students. GAME:IT uses the power of game design to introduce a new generation of students to extremely valuable and employable STEM skills. STEM Fuse is proud to be able to help our schools and be part of the solution in promoting and improving STEM education nationwide!

    To learn more about GAME:IT 10,000 – A STEM Initiative and to participate, click here: http://stemfuse.com/shop/gameit-10000-initiative

    What is GAME:IT?
    • Full semester (18 week) game design course that encompasses ALL facets of STEM
    • Contains absolutely ALL needed materials and is EASY TO TEACH
    • Designed at an intro level, this course assumes no previous technical experience by teacher or student – this is for ALL interested teachers and students
    • Content is 100% digital allowing the course to be delivered in a traditional room/lab setting or easily adaptable to any distance learning platforms
    • Can be used as an elective (CTE) course, as an after-school club or summer learning program, as a supplement to a math or physics course, and is a great student retention tool.
    • Aligned will ALL applicable state and national CTE standards, ISTE.NETs, 21st Century Skills and Common Core technology, math & science standards.

    See what other schools are saying about GAME:IT http://stemfuse.com/testimonials/

    We’d love your help in promoting this FREE, no strings attached, STEM Initiative.
