Michael Stearne's Summary

Currently VP of Technology & Development at Path Interactive an expert in traffic analytics, application development and scalability, Michael has developed business processes and systems that contribute to the aggressive revenue growth of Path Interactive. Michael’s core responsibilities include managing the search platform architecture, client development and providing strategic insights and management to the Search Engine Optimization and Social Media teams.

Michael is an experienced developer and entrepreneur with numerous ventures across many verticals in Internet related technologies. Before joining Path Interactive, Michael managed Internet application development projects in industries ranging from real estate to high tech to television production. He has worked with small businesses as well as Fortune 100 companies.

In his previous positions, Michael assisted in the creation of a portfolio of web-based ventures including recent projects like Tenant.com, Kolat.com, GotTempo.com as well as consulting for various firms.

Michael has degrees in Computer Science and Sociology from Rutgers University. His educational background gives him the unique ability to find the intersection between the offline and online behaviors of users and industry.
