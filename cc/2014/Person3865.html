KVNY, Inc Real Estate's Summary

At KVNY, we have over 25 years of experience in all aspects of Manhattan Real Estate. Our qualified team is comprised of experts in residential rentals and sales, property management and luxury short-term rentals/corporate relocation. We pride ourselves on our personal service, provided by a staff of genuine, dedicated agents who are passionate about real estate.

Our location allows us deep insight into one of the most sought-after areas in Manhattan: SoHo and Greenwich Village. We’re in the heart of the neighborhood, and we have forged personal relationships with landlords and a reputable name for ourselves in the area. However, the reach of our highly knowledgeable team extends well beyond the limits of our neighborhood, into the whole of Downtown Manhattan and beyond; wherever your home awaits, we will find it! We also have both furnished and unfurnished buildings and apartments that are accessible exclusively by our in-house management company, which are located all over Manhattan. Our furnished sales agents work to find corporate and vacation housing in these luxury apartments, and never charge a broker fee.

We value leadership and honest communication at KVNY, and we’re proud of the customer service we offer to our clients, many of whom return to us time and time again. We are confident that we won’t just find you an apartment – we’ll find you a home.
