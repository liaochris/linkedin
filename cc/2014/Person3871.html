Kris Best's Summary

I enjoy making a difference in my community. At The Works museum, I'm involved with giving kids from all walks of life a hands-on engineering experience. It's amazing- they may come in unsure, but they leave thinking that engineering is fun and that they can do it!

At the museum they explore the hands-on exhibits then dive into a workshop where they build a motor-powered fan, a marshmallow-flipping catapult, a slimy polymer, or some other project. This is important because while they're creating they're reinforcing math and science skills and gaining confidence in their abilities. We're starting the pipeline of tomorrow's scientists and engineers!

Specialties

science and engineering education, informal education, sponsorships, elementary education, special events
