Jay Rollins' Summary

Rollins is well known as a real estate industry leader. He is a frequent speaker at national real estate investment and he is frequently published by leading real estate industry publications. He also has been a guest lecturer at the University of North Carolina and Denver University, as well as, major industry functions.

In addition to speaking and writing on commercial real estate finance, Jay Rollins is also the author of "Commercial Real Estate Uncovered," a comprehensive reference guide to the Commercial Real Estate industry. The book complements his seminar series titled, “Commercial Real Estate Finance Uncovered”: This seminar series has been conducted at numerous venues around the United States, in including corporate training for CB Richard, and other national real estate organizations. Both book and seminar series are available at www.creuncovered.com

Rollins is also known for calling the top of the commercial real estate market in 2007 in an article titled “As Good As It Gets”, published in National Real Estate Investor.

Over the course of his career, Rollins has invested in over 300 real estate transactions totaling over two (or $2,000,000,000) billion dollars, with a principal loss rate of less than 1%. He focuses on a value-added investment approach, that protects principal while providing a current return as well as, high all in returns. This approach has made him a popular real estate fund manager.
