Summary

With over 15 years of experience creating change in the corporate and startup worlds I am a shape-shifter as I can fit into a number of different environments. I am particularly comfortable at navigating ambiguity. You can find me working on user experience (UX) projects, communication projects, design projects or strategy projects.
