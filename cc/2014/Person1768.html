Steven Haines' Summary

Are you looking to drive growth and build more effective product and business strategies? Incorporating Product Business Management into your growth strategy moves you from dreaming about what you want to achieve, into knowing precisely how you will achieve it.

I’m Steven Haines: author of three category-defining books on Product Business Management. I’m a public speaker, advisor, and passionate advocate for revenue-generating products that help firms grow the business because they deliver extraordinary value to customers.

Contact me to learn more about how we can work together: sjhaines@sequentlearning.com.

Working closely with senior business leaders, I provide focused strategic guidance that helps improve business results through consistently utilized product business management. What could your company accomplish if everyone, from your highest-level leaders to your front-line talent, was aligned around the same vision? From result-oriented strategies to implementation systems, my work with organizations empowers leaders to inspire creative collaboration within their cross-functional teams.

My firm, Sequent Learning Networks, is here to provide you with the tools and best practices to cultivate successful organizations that produce great products backed by the insight and expertise of almost three decades’ experience in the field. I work closely with senior or leadership teams, facilitate workshops and deliver keynote presentations customized to your industry, function and attendee level. I have a passion for great products and the business people who create and manage them.

Check out more about my company, my foundation, and how I can help you below.

Steven Haines' Experience

CEO, Founder, and Author

Sequent Learning Networks - Product Management Professional and Organizational Development

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Professional Training & Coaching industry

2001 – Present (13 years)

Sequent Learning Networks is an internationally positioned training and organizational advisory services firm. Sequent provides the wherewithal for executives in the world's leading firms to improve the success of their products and the effectiveness of their organizations.

Sequent's diagnostics assess the people and processes of mid-size to large complex organizations so that we can recommend what's needed to organize more effectively to produce the products that customers clamor for.

Sequent delivers workshops to large and mid-sized corporate clients on the subjects of Product Management and business management. These are organized based on the learning objectives. Foundational workshops are delivered in a facilitated classroom-style setting using a combination of interactive learning, impactful story-telling, and hands-on exercises. Advisory workshops allow Sequent's facilitators to work with designated teams so they can produce actual product strategies, Business Cases, launch plans, etc.).

Sequent also provides a host of resources, including books and templates. Sequent's Product Management Executive Board produces twice yearly Product Management Leadership Summits and frequent Product Management Leadership Roundtable Breakfast Meetings. Finally, Sequent provides an unparalleled product manager certification program.
