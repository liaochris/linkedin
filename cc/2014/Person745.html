Adam Justice's Summary

As Managing Director of Social Media Sun I developed the entire property and built monthly traffic to over 40,000 unique visitors in less than 5 months. The online magazine has received praise from industry leaders for our strong display of ethics and quality of content.

I am currently the principal at Elkhorn Media, a Social Media-online marketing and web development firm. I develop regional clients in Eastern Kentucky, as well as for limited clients around the world. Our business has expanded rapidly, and I am currently in talks to merge with a national online marketing firm.

I'm also a part time Engineering Technician for Abbott Engineering in Prestonsburg, KY, as well a featured contributor for Yahoo! in the areas of Technology, Automotive and Politics.

AutoCAD, SurvCAD, Microsoft Office Suite. Web Design & Development including graphic design, scripting, website layout, advertising & management. Writing and Editing, web content production, SEO, valid markup. Analytics interpretation, online marketing strategy development and integration. HTML, CSS, PHP Integration, Graphic Design and profile optimization.
