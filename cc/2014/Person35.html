Background

Summary

I believe that the companies and organisations that I work with transform the world to make it a stronger, healthier and more vibrant place to live. Together we harness the power of technology, creativity, strategic thinking, business savvy and a strong ethos to deliver award-winning products, services and experiences, keeping people at the centre of every solution.

I practice research-driven, human-centred user experience (UX) design as in order to innovate, it is best to understand deeply both the people at the heart of the challenge and the context within which they lie. UX design for me encompasses ethnographic research, storytelling, idea and insights generation, product strategy and development, interaction and UI design, IA and content strategy, and creative direction and branding.

I inspire clients and collaborators alike to push the bar and think beyond the obvious and apparent.

For more than 15 years I have worked with a wide range of industries including but not limited to arts and culture, media and publishing, finance, healthcare and fashion + luxury and social entrepreneurship for clients in the US, Europe and Asia.

Accessibility minded. Focus on sustainability, branding + communications.
