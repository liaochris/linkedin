Mike Murray's Summary

I have a diverse background of technical skills, business and management skills and I try to spend my time doing incredibly cool projects with passionate and interesting people. For me, that usually involves designing/building something or hacking some system (technical or otherwise) to solve a problem that people have.

Most of my career has been in information security - I've spent 15 years working from companies across all range - startups that built security products, consulting companies, and even a large financial services firm. In 2008, I partnered with a couple of other like-minded individuals and built the Hacker Academy, and then, in 2010 we co-founded MAD Security which subsumed The Hacker Academy as part of a security consulting, resale and system integration firm. We went from 3 founding partners in 2010 to over 25 employees in 2013, and bootstrapped the company to over $11M in revenue in 2013.
