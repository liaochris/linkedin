Patric Fransko's Summary

I am a business development professional with a broad portfolio of experience in sales, marketing, operations and customer relations roles. I have an extensive background managing business to business relationships between manufacturers and their sales channel with a track record of success and achievement in all positions I have held. I am a proven leader that has demonstrated the ability to analyze situations, define a vision, set priorities, assign roles and execute a plan to success. My strong relationship skills have enabled me to attain aggressive growth objectives while maintaining very strong relationships with both channel partners and customers. My goal is to work in challenging positions with companies that offer the premier products within their industry.


Specialties:

- Seasoned executive with experience in all areas of the successful operation of an organization.

- Strong relationship building and networking skills with individuals at all levels of the sales channel.

- Able to identify growing trends and create products or programs to capitalize on those trends.

- Cultivate strategic relationships to pursue growth and customer retention objectives.

- Innovative collaborator that succeeds in bringing multiple parties together in order to reach goals.

- Experience creating and overseeing production of marketing programs including print media, point of purchase displays, websites, blogs and social media groups & pages, etc.

Chief Operating Officer

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Consumer Goods industry

March 2009 – May 2013 (4 years 3 months) Fullerton, CA

Based on my past experience selling installation tools to the window film industry, Zola Distributing was launched to service the installation tool needs of the window film, vinyl graphic/wraps and paint protection film installers. Zola Distributing worked in conjunction with Service Group Distribution to offer clients a one-stop-shop for all the items that they required to properly run their businesses.
Starting this company from scratch, I utilized my network to create strategic partnerships that allowed us to launch the company faster and at a lower cost than would have been possible otherwise. I was also responsible for the creation of an e-commerce website along with all associated social media platforms for Zola Distributing. I also oversaw the creation of a 40 page print catalog that was part of a direct mail campaign to reach the industries that utilize these tools. Finally, I deployed a full-time tool supply truck that serviced the installation shops throughout southern California. During my tenure at Zola, the company went from concept to nearly $500,000 in annual sales.
