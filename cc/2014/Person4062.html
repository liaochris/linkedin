Malene Barnett's Summary

Malene B Carpets celebrate patterns, people and places in our global community. Our distinct designs offer bold, textural and unique solutions to transform your space, adding beauty and creating a conversation.

Your journey can begin by selecting a design from our curated collection or we can start from a blank canvas to create a one of a kind carpet that will perfectly compliment your space in your choice of color size and material.

Using only the finest materials and traditional weaving techniques, our handcrafted carpets are brought to life by master weavers.

We invite you to explore our collections and consult with us on your next project. From boutique hotels to luxury residences and anywhere in between, our goal is to provide you with the perfect carpet.

Set the tone. Complete your story. Bring your vision to life.
