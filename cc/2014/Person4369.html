Kevin King's Summary

Has your brand lost its cool?

Cool is about self-assured confidence, knowing exactly who you are. And that’s extremely compelling to your customers and employees alike.

I can help you figure out what makes you—your brand, your company, your culture—cool. It’s not about following trends. It’s about tapping into that spark inside that makes you unique, and then expressing this new-found sense of Self throughout all of your communications, both with your audience and within your organization. Once you are clear about who you are, the magic starts to flow.
