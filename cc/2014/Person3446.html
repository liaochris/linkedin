It is also a suitable help to determine the right mix of investments in hardware, software and services.

A unique aspect of the BRAIN FORCE approach is that our knowledge, skills, and expertise are bundled into best practices: the BRAIN FORCE Infrastructure Framework.
This contains all the documents and scripts for designing, standardizing, and implementing Microsoft, Citrix, and VMware technologies. You know beforehand what the final situation is going to look like and you can be certain that the various aspects of the implementation will be retained in the IT organization.

BRAIN FORCE develops its own software in addition to existing technology from market parties. BRAIN FORCE Workspace Manager simplifies the administration of user profiles in desktop infrastructures, both physical and virtual. BRAIN FORCE has developed two unique solutions in the field of application lifecycle management: BRAIN FORCE Packaging Robot and Application Manager contribute to more efficient application inventory and application rationalization processes, license compliancy, application compatibility, packaging (MSI), and virtualization (App-V and ThinApp).

BRAIN FORCE supplies its customers with extra IT capacity for dealing with peak loads through its secondment service BRAIN MATCH. The unique aspect is that a complete support center is ready and waiting behind each and every IT professional you hire in.
