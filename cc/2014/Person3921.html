August 2013 – Present (1 year 2 months) 13154 Coit Rd Dallas, Texas 75240

The City Crime Watch provides protection, security and surveillance to both residential and commercial customers. Crime victims are often forgotten by the criminal justice system. They also frequently experience difficult financial, psychological and legal problems as a result of a crime. The City Crime Watch has a network of programs and resources available to help victims and their families cope with the effects of a crime.

There’s a crime committed every 4.6 seconds in the U.S. With all of today’s resources and social media, its amazing all of the information about programs and services aren’t offered on one site. The internet has done a great job with the information, but I feel that it’s harder for someone to get to the point, or find the services that they are looking for. So, the vision of the City Crime Watch is to accomplish the following three things. Provide awareness, assurance and support to all individuals. The City Crime Watch is a crime prevention advocacy group designed to provide resources and information to all individuals in every community.

Our security consultant we are not affiliated with any one security services company, product manufacture or distributor.
