Background

Summary

I’m an instructional designer who develops engaging e-learning that makes a difference in how people do their jobs. My goal is to create online learning that uses technology and storytelling to draw participants in and lets them practice new skills in ways that will easily transfer to the workplace.

Specialties: Curriculum design, instructional design, e-learning, project management, accessibility, obscure Microsoft Office shortcuts.
