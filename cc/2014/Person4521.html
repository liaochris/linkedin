Patricia Collins' Summary

Patty Collins' experience includes commercial litigation, employment law and healthcare law and compliance.

Commercial Litigation: Litigate and counsel clients regarding business disputes, business dissolutions and separations, partnership and shareholder disputes, noncompetes and restrictive covenants, nondisclosure and nonsolicitation agreements, commercial property disputes, and business torts.

Employment Law: Counsel small to medium-sized business clients and nonprofit entities on employment compliance and risk management issues on a daily basis; engage in litigation on behalf of employers and employees under federal and state discrimination and wrongful termination laws, relating to restrictive covenants, unfair competition and unlawful use of trade secrets, and compliance with state and federal regulations; Prepare employment agreements; Provide employment law seminars and training for employees and businesses.

Healthcare Law and Compliance: Advise nonprofit social service providers, physician practices and other providers compliance with federal and state regulations, and licensing issues; Train physicians, mental health and social service providers, and ancillary service providers on applicable federal and state regulations and best practices.

General Counsel: Serve as General Counsel for large behavioral health hospital serving children and adolescents, providing advice and guidance on all issues relating to the daily operation of the facility, including: compliance with federal and state laws, employment issues, third party payor relationships, insurance issues, licensing issues, relationships with regulators and government agencies, and litigation management; Act as “in-house” counsel, for small to medium-sized business and nonprofit entities; Work with business clients to comply with laws and manage risks in a manner that suits their business needs.
