Rob McGonigle's Summary

Rob has completed 9 years of delivering Google Adwords managed services for a range of clients as funded assistances and over the last 4 years in his own business.

The pace of changes generated by Google Adwords, Google Analytics, Google+, etc., has continued to accelerate in 2013. The move to Enhanced Campaigns has introduced opportunities and a lot more to be managed. Phone numbers in your Ad text are not disapproved but the new Call Enhancements work well on mobile with much better reporting. Google Shopping listings will soon be paid only. There are some fiddly things to set-up so that you can control your bids and get full visibility of product listings on your Analytics.

PPC Promotion are managing over £50k per month of Adwords spend in the UK and for Global brands based in the UK, Greece & Turkey.

Rob's in-depth experience helping Small to Medium Engineering Enterprises with b2b ecommerce has led to him specialising in Search Engine Optimisation and Search Engine Marketing, e.g. Google AdWords. Rob has published on the use of Google Adwords as a market research tool & has supervised MSc research projects that have used Adwords as a research methodology.

Rob has captured the experience of the CCM online collaboration tools and evaluated the additions to Google and Microsoft's online suites of applications as intranets, extranets and easy-to-change collaborative websites.

Specialties:
Search Marketing including pay-per-click, PPC Google Adwords, and Search Engine Optimisation.

Tracking business performance using Analytics and visitor behavior to improve conversion rates.

Collaborative computing 'in the cloud' using Google Sites, Microsoft Office Live 365 etc.

Migration strategies for re-branding and changing website hosting.
