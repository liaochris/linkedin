Joe Kovacs APR's Experience

Director of Marketing

Gelman, Rosenberg & Freedman, CPAs

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Accounting industry

July 2010 – Present (4 years 4 months) Bethesda, MD

In partnership with our management team, I drive business development and marketing initiatives to attract interest in the accounting firm's services. I also work with professional staff to recognize the value of relationship-building skills. Lead generation and nurturing, consistent technological adaptation, cradle-to-grave program management, campaign analytics, ROI measurement and business proposal management are some of my key roles. Within the last few years, I have led a marketing project to save the firm $30,000 annually, launched a new website that brought our site's Google search rankings to page 1, built a client database (CRM) from scratch and secured a page 1 article in the accounting industry's trade publication, Accounting Today. I have presented on social networking and/or niche development for the American Institute of CPAs, the Greater Washington Society of CPAs, the Maryland Association of Nonprofit Organizations and CPAmerica International. I have been a board member of the Association for Accounting Marketing, the leading membership organization of accounting marketers in the United States and Canada, since 2013 and work to help accountants recognize the value and contributions of marketing within their firms. (I earlier served as the firm's marketing director from 2003 to 2007, as noted below).
