Background

Summary

For over ten years, I have been managing the food allergies of my two children with the following combined allergies: milk, eggs, fish, shellfish, peanuts, tree nuts, bananas, kiwi, avocado, penicillen, dogs, cats and grass.

Through local Boise schools and the St. Alphonsus Hospital Family Center, I have been facilitating allergy awareness classes for both children and adults in Utah and Idaho since 2007.

Safe Allergy~Friendly Education was launched in 2010. My primary goal is increasing allergy awareness and education via classes in community hospitals, daycares, schools, and summer camps. My educational classes focus on sharing the products and resources available to individuals and groups enabling them to more easily help keep others SAFE in their learning environments.

Through the support and donations of strategic alliances such as St. Alphonsus hospital, video promotions through the Boise CO-OP, local allergy~friendly businesses and community centers, my goal can be achieved. I would like to put into place as many programs as I can for children, adults, teachers and health professional groups wanting to be better prepared with resources/ideas for families they work with who are managing dietary restrictions of any kind.

Through my many years in public speaking and classroom presentations, my primary passion is inspiring and motivating food allergy families to know that depsite their dietary limitations, FOOD ALLERGIES and FUN can go together!

Looking to promote/sponsor a great product/resource within the Boise community? Want to advertise on my site? Contact me directly for ideas on how to set up marketing alliances or partnerships..

My mission:
Alleviating food allergy fears one classroom and blog at a time.

Specialties: Public Speaking, Motivational Speaking, Food Allergies, Allergy Education Awareness Facilitation for Children and Adults, One-on-One Consulting, Coaching, Guest Blogging,

My multi-dimensional business offers a variety of educational opportunities for children and adults.

I facilitate Allergy Awareness classes to preschoolers and early elementary aged school children. My goal is creating a strong network of allies for ALL of our children by increasing their peers' and teachers' understanding of the seriousness of living with food allergies, especially life threatening ones.

Through St. Alphonsus Regional Medical Center I facilitate product/resource classes for adults new to managing food allergies including new parents and health care providers. Presentations focus on sharing resource materials such as: local/national resources; on-line websites/blogs; cooking alternatives/recipes/cookbooks; home 'toolkit' ideas and strategies for reducing stress; and school preparation.

I provide one-on-one consulting for families wanting a more individualized experience in assisting them with managing food allergies or wanting to set up their own school education programs.

My blog, smartallergyfriendlyeducation.com, appeals to those who are looking for everyday products/resources to assist them in their day to day living with food allergies, especially those managing dairy, egg and nut allergies.
