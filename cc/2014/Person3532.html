Joanie Sams' Summary

Joanie Sams has over thirty years of experience in the hospitality industry. Sams has earned a reputation for creating exquisite settings for the banquets and special events the hotel has hosted, while nurturing The Townsend Hotel's position as one of the most prestigious locations for a special event in Michigan.

A graduate of Central Michigan University, with many steps in her career incorporating hospitality, she has called The Townsend Hotel home for more than 20 years.

Specialties

Special events, weddings, non-profit, Food and Beverage operations, budget and forecasting, standard operations procedures implementation and training.
