Steven Leibold, EA's Summary

Enrolled Agent - Federally Licensed Tax Professional
We provide business owners with tax solutions to help them better manage their tax liability. No business is the same so we customize a tax plan strategy that works best for the owner to help them achieve their goals. We have had a lot of success helping businesses in the start up phase and into the growth phase. We are working to develop a team of professionals that will also assist business owners with an exit strategy that will allow them to maximize their financial potential and minimize the tax liability on the transaction. Personal service and helping our clients through tough times are what we pride ourselves on. We are there for our clients and will help guide them through our recommendations.

Specialties

Income Tax Planning and Business Development for New Business Owners
Income Tax Planning and Business Development for existing businesses with net revenues exceeding $75,000 per year
Exit Strategy Planning for business owners looking to transition their business either through sale or transfer.
Certified Quickbooks Pro Advisor
