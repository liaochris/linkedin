Katarina Tafvelin's Summary

I have worked with international relations for 15+ years, mainly for the Swedish Foreign Ministry but also at a UN Peacekeeping mission and a Baltic Sea Region networking organization. Outside of Sweden and Denmark I have lived and worked in East Timor, Democratic Republic of the Congo and Spain.

At present I promote Sweden in Denmark through cultural or business related events, press contacts and digital channels, with close cooperation between the Embassy, Business Sweden, VisitSweden and Danish counterparts.

My previous work experience includes public diplomacy, media relations, event management, multilateral and bilateral negotiations, UN and humanitarian affairs, development cooperation, international law as well as civil and criminal judicial procedure.

I have a Master of Laws degree and a five years work experience as assistant judge, which made me affected to analytical assignments and dedicated to details. I am fluent in English and Spanish and good enough in French.

Specialities: International Relations, Politics, Public Administration, Communication, Digital Media, Social Media, Nation Branding, Event Management
