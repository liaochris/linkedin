Oliver Repenning's Summary

Creative hybrid marketeer engaged in digital driven business development.
As Digital Business Development Manager at Arla Foods, a global FMCG company with brand top 100 ambitions, I set to drive consumer centered end-to-end solutions that can leverage awareness and digital capabilities across brands and categories by showcasing the P/L impact of activating the digital touch-points of the consumer journey. I have a strong passion for development and change and I am a firm believer of challenging the way we work and the (more than often) entrenched perception of status quo equaling good enough. With an MSc. in Information Technology, I see myself as an digital minded business developer - or a business minded digital native strayed in a connected world. Either way, my aim is always to design second-to-none solutions.

Personally, I'm married to Janni Bjerkelund, a fashion industry entrepreneur, and the proud dad of Karla and Elias and lucky #3 on the way. We are exploring life as a young and energetic family just south of Aarhus, always on the lookout for new experiences and a dream of working and living abroad - but currently quite busy restoring an old farm house.

MBTI profile: ENTP

Specialties: hybrid marketeer for the digital age, business development, concept development, digital projects, digital hybrid marketeer, innovation, digital strategy and growth hacking
