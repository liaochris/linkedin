Background

Summary

What’s in it for you?

A business minded, hybrid, creative.

Seeing possibilities comes natural for Wout. He is a sharp observer and discovers what is happening tomorrow. A creative thinker who creates innovative and challenging ideas. These ideas are complete from start to finish. Wout is able to put the ideas in a context, visualise and structure them, develop prototypes and in the end pitch the ideas.

Wout’s goal is reached when these ideas are brought into the real world. His way of operating is simple, he keeps control of the whole process, from his innovative concept al the way through to visualizing, designing and finally reality. This is not a stand alone operation, he uses his considerable knowledge, creative thinking and his vast network for bouncing of ideas every step in his creative process. During this process he inspires others and motivates them, mostly due to his enthusiasm and leadership.

The best example is The Pop Up Agency. Together with five friends and fellow Hyper Island students Wout founded this creative agency. From March till June 2013 they toured the globe working for clients in Europe, Asia and North America. In fifteen weeks, they worked in fifteen countries for more then twenty different clients. For every client they worked 48 hours. They focused on creative strategies, new business ideas and digital solutions.

Cooperation with Wout is working with a truly motivated young man. Someone with an healthy attitude to work. He identifies goals and does his utmost to reach the goal with his whole team. He takes control when needed, he inspires. His “can do” attitude motivates and inspires. Young, enthusiastic and entrepreneurial are words to describe him. A creative person with a mind and heart full of ideas.

Specialismen: new business development, digital solutions and creative strategies.
