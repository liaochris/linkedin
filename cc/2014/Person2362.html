Brooke Hemphill's Summary

Brooke Hemphill is a writer, editor, presenter and video producer.

Brooke's career in the media began with a regular feature in the Sydney Morning Herald titled Table for Eight.

She then went on to present the online series Sexperts for the SMH, Age and Brisbane Times websites.

This was followed by a period of freelance feature writing with articles published in Women's Health and Marie Claire USA.

In 2010 Brooke joined Mumbrella as head of video content.

In 2011 she was appointed the editor of Encore magazine and oversaw the title through two relaunches and its transition to a tablet only publication. Encore ceased publication at the end of 2013.

Her career highlights include the online series Generation L which she co-created, shot and edited. It has had more than a quarter of a million views.

Brooke is currently writing her first book Lesbian for a year - a memoir.

Specialties: producer, presenting, after effects, producing, writing, video/TV editing, indesign, magazine editing
