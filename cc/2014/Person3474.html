Jerome Klein's Summary

Accomplished educator, ADR neutral, insurance trial attorney and claim coverage analyst/manager with many years of experience in the successful negotiation and settlement of bodily injury, property damage, and specialty risk claims. Secures just and fair resolutions of disputes. Strengths include: an ability to work effectively as a team member to create a proficient and comfortable work environment and an ability to analyze details of complex cases and insurance coverages for use in making persuasive presentations to clients, jurors, opponents, or neutrals.
