Background

Summary

Why choose Jennifer Erickson and what makes Jennifer Erickson different from other professionals in our field?

As real estate agents we all work hard to find our clients their next perfect home. Where I add even more value is my extensive real estate background in both the legal and mortgage lending fields. For the past 20 years I have worked in the real estate profession first as a real estate closing paralegal for two law firms in Boston and then as a mortgage loan processor. I bring to my clients a thorough understanding of the entire home buying process. My clients will appreciate my ability to be a team player in buying or selling their home. The home buying process has a lot of moving parts. I can speak the language of the mortgage lenders, processors and Attorneys involved in the home buying or selling process. I have experience in title clearing, reviewing and explaining loan conditions, reviewing appraisals and home inspections, and reviewing and explaining the loan closing documents. I have been involved in the closing of over 10,000 real estate transactions in my career - I have done it all. Once we have found the perfect home my clients can lean on me to ensure their dream becomes a reality.

Specialties:Specializing in distressed properties, short sales and foreclosures. I have received the designations for CDPE® (Certified Distressed Property Expert) from the Charfen Institute, the ABR® (Accredited Buyers Representative) from the National Association of Realtors and the SFR® (Short Sale and Foreclosure Resource) from the National Association of Realtors. I have assisted many first time homebuyers as well as investors.
