Estrella Alamo's Summary

Skilled marketing professional with 15 years of experience in Digital Marketing and Product Management. Expertise in launching and development of large online projects for multinational companies in Spain as well as in smaller projects with an international focus. I was a member of the team who created Terra for Telefonica and later founded the Ya.com network (Viajar.com, Finanzas.com…) Afterwards, I worked in the media business (Vocento) and most recently in the entertainment sector for one of the most renowned start-ups in the country (Planeta Red). All this has provided me with a deep knowledge of digital content, its marketing and communication principles along with a solid understanding of social media, mobile platforms and their users.

My aim as Marketing Manager is to help companies to create, grow and evolve their digital strategy. I can contribute to the definition and tactical execution of the strategic marketing plan and apply the acquired business knowledge to the planning, development and operation of the promotion and online sales channels.

I consider myself to be a forward-thinking, creative person. I am eager, flexible and results-oriented. I like teamwork in an international and multilingual environment.
