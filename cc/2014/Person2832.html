Don Wood, PMP's Summary

I have over 25 years of experience in strategic planning, business process improvement, and project/ program management in information technology for the Real Estate industry. I have worked with clients in retail, corporate real estate, multi-family, homebuilding, hospitality, healthcare, and commercial property owners and managers.

I have extensive experience in assisting clients select and implement the best software application to fit their needs. I have experience in selecting and/ or implementing many software packages such as Oracle Property Manager, Oracle Accounts Receivable, Oracle MDM - Site Hub, JD Edwards OneWorld, Oracle Projects, SAP Real Estate, Meridian Project Systems, Yardi, RealPage OneSite, MRI, Timberline, Virtual Premise, and Quarem ALT. In addition, I have experience helping real estate organizations improve and maximize their back office processes.
