Nabilla Sharil's Summary

Professional experience in management consulting with focus in change management, training, leadership development and M&E of community development and government social projects. Corporate industry experience include large telecommunication corporations across ASEAN region, national airline carrier, financial services and public services.

She is a RYT 200 Yoga Alliance Instructor with over 200 hours of teaching experience, with primary style of teaching heavily influence and drawn from Ashtanga, Vinyasa Flow and strength based Vikasa Yoga.

Nabilla also writes for Healthworks,my and contributes occasionally to print media on the benefits of yoga and her ongoing journey in spreading the light of yoga.
