Background

Summary

Higher, Further, Faster! - I get a charge out of making things perform better. I was the kid who took things apart and put them back together, so I knew how they worked, and could tune them up. I've always been fascinated by numbers, patterns, technology, and machines and I enjoy web optimization, trading stocks, and flying airplanes.

I spent six years in the U.S. Navy and am a Persian Gulf War vet. I developed missile guidance software and was awarded the Joint Service Commendation Medal for technology innovation by the U.S. Strategic Command. Growing into leadership & management, I led several eCommerce divisions and numerous strategic initiatives for Cabela's - contributing to over 500% growth in online revenues. As a manager, I've helped dozens of individuals leverage their strengths to improve job performance, satisfaction, and reach new career heights.

In 2010, I formed a consulting practice that helps companies optimize their web properties to drive more sales. Since then, I've been joined by a few extremely talented associates and have been in the lab working on our own marketing technology. Most recently, we've created a tool that makes it easy to add custom images, branding, and clickable links to YouTube videos. Check it out at www.videoredirect.com - Make Your Videos Interactive!
