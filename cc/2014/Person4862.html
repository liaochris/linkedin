Russell James Smith's Summary

I have over 14 years professional experience in creating and managing online digital experiences.

I graduated in 1999 with a BSc Computer Visualisation from the University of Teesside in the UK. After university I joined a marketing agency and developed my expert hands-on web front-end skills. I introduced CSS layouts and a web-standards based approach; I promoted a user-centered approach to design.

Continuing in agency roles I grew my skills to also become a key player in developing and managing new projects, starting from business objectives and being involved in the specification, design, build and delivery. Often working directly with clients I evaluated technologies & platforms, wrote proposals, designed & documented user experiences.

In 2007 I took an in-house position with Apple where I managed a team of seven that created and maintained content on Apple's e-commerce sites across Europe. I liaised closely with business stakeholders, marketing and developers and managed my teams budget. The team responded to the business needs and strategy with creative and technological solutions and took care of day to day operations.

I relocated to New Zealand in late 2010. In Auckland I have worked for RAPP Tribal as a Senior Producer managing a wide range of digital projects. I am currently working in-house as the Web Manager for Working In where I drive digital strategy, manage website operations and oversee new development across multiple business critical websites.

I am a problem solver with a methodical approach and great attention to detail. An experienced team and project manager; I make informed choices to ensure projects are delivered. I also have the know-how required to implement business strategy online, consider the best digital tools that will solve a problem and deliver results. I thrive in an environment that enables me to continuously learn and explore new possibilities.
