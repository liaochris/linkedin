Andrew Blum's Summary

Business and thought leader at the forefront of bridging strategy, leadership and culture in order to help companies effectively execute the right business decisions the right way.

As Founder and Managing Partner of The Trium Group, a boutique management consulting firm that specializes in organizational transformation and strategy execution, Andrews has an impressive track record leading engagements across a wide array of sectors and geographies, for such globally renowned companies as AXA, Cisco, Gap Inc., Genentech, McKesson, Nokia, Symantec and Flextronics.

Previously served as a leader in Towers Perrin's Strategy and Organization practice, and before that as an operational leader and instructor in the United States Marine Corps.

Specialties: Strategy formulation and implementation, corporate transformation, organization and process design, and high impact executive decision-making and leadership-driven change management.
