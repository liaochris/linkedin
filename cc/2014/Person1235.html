Jim Littlefield's Summary

Jim Littlefield is a sales leader with an impressive background in complex solution sales achievement, product management and marketing with a focus on enterprise software solutions. Jim spent 12+ years at Oracle/PeopleSoft holding key positions as it transformed from a small industry disrupter into multi-billion dollar industry leader.
