Kathleen Quill's Summary

Over 29 years of management in not-for-profit trade associations. Have taken current organization, serving business credit grantors, from #5 in the country to #2 and from financial instability to strong financial position with a full array of essential business credit & financial services, products and member relations.

Association business is like no other, requiring a unique blend of non-profit mindset and for-profit knowledge. It is not easy, but it IS challenging every single day.

Specialties: Strategic Planning, Credit practices, repackacing and repositioning, Business Credit, not for profit governance & best practices, Board of Directors best practices and governance policies, member services
