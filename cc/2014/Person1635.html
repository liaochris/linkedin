Robert Martin's Summary

Together, Rob Martin and Terry Traxler make up The Martin-Traxler Real Estate Finance Team. Each possess over 13 years of experience in the Central Ohio and SW Florida residential real estate mortgage industry. Over the years their partnership has demonstrated unprecedented professionalism and expertise in structuring home loans for over a thousand clients. Their reputation is one of which Central Ohio real estate agents have come to recognize as knowledgeable, reliable and trustworthy. Offering flexibility and great availability speaks of the great dedication that Rob and Terry have for their clients. With no help from advertising or telemarketing, their business has been built solely upon referrals from satisfied past clients or trusted business colleagues.

Robert A. Martin
Rob has an extensive past in mortgage lending and real estate. His knowledge of the area and financing available is possessed by very few. He is a 2002 graduate of the Ohio State University with degrees in Finance and Economics. Prior to college, he grew up in Fremont, Ohio on a large agriculture farm and learned true work ethic and business at an early age. He resides in both Columbus, Ohio as well as Naples, Florida and active member of both communities. What you will find with Rob, is that he takes his career and reputation very seriously, and strives to provide a service to his clients that he would feel truly satisfied if received himself.
