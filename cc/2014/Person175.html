Background

Summary

A former journalist turned PR, social media & content consultant w/ 15-plus years of experience, Dawn is well known for her unique, polished voice & passion for social good. Whether writing a story about philanthropy or highlighting a regional nonprofit for Main Line Today or Philadelphia Family magazines—& more currently online via blogging—Dawn’s passion & storytelling skills demonstrate an appreciation for the mission of nonprofits & NGOs.

After attending Social Good Summit 2013, she realized that blending her writing, public relations & promotion skills w/ organizations seeking to “be the change,” was the foundation for her next career move. She is a passionate, unbiased people-person who understands the value of relationship building—& culling potential donors. Her genuine optimism also comes in handy when representing organizations serving those who may not feel hope in them or around them. In regard to her writing, standout qualities include strong interviewing skills, an ability to recognize compelling angles & to ask questions that elevate a story’s overall quality, whether writing branded content or third-party articles, blog and social media posts.

Dawn has media experience as both radio & TV guest: 10! Show, WBEN Real Mom Radio & Women to Watch WPYL 1180 (now WWDB AM 860), & on-camera interviewer for Radnor Studio 21 project. Blogging experience includes: ShopMoxie.com, WomenonBusiness.com, No Kid Hungry, read.eat.DEW.write., Mom Bloggers for Social Good’s Global Team of 200. Member: Philly Social Media Moms
