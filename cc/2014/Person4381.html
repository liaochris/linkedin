Mark Slaughter's Summary

Mark Slaughter has more than 30 years of experience in the energy, telecommunications and remote communications industries. Currently the CEO, President and a board member at RigNet (NASDAQ: RNET), he has served in executive and management roles at Halliburton Company, Reliant Energy, United Technologies and Stratos Global Corporation. Mark serves on the boards of Geoforce (international provider of remote asset tracking and utilization solutions for the oil and gas industry); Boys and Girls Country of Houston (Christian home for children from families in crisis); and the Center for Christianity in Business at Houston Baptist University. Mark was selected as an Ernst & Young Entrepreneur Of The Year 2014 Gulf Coast Area Award recipient. He attended United Technologies' Executive Program at the University of Virginia's Darden Graduate School of Business as well as Stanford Law School's Directors' College. Mark received an A.B. from Harvard College and an MBA from Stanford's Graduate School of Business.
