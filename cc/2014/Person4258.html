Michael Sher's Summary

Michael D. Sher is a member of Neal Gerber Eisenberg’s Litigation, White Collar Criminal, Regulatory & Internal Investigative Services and Corporate Board Services Practice Groups. Michael represents clients in complex civil litigation and in business-related criminal investigations, trials and appeals in federal and state judicial and administrative proceedings. Michael is a Lecturer in Law at the University of Chicago Law School teaching a seminar on Fourth and Fifth Amendment issues relating to emerging technologies, and previously was a Lecturer in trial practice. Before joining the firm, Michael served as a Staff Attorney with the Federal Defender Program for the Northern District of Illinois. He is a Fellow of the American College of Trial Lawyers.
