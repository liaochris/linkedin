Background

Summary

I am (almost) a Civil Engineer. I decided to be so because I like to build things, to see them grow. It feels like you are improving, at least a little bit, the world where you live.

I am one Master's Thesis away from getting my 'Ingeniero de Caminos, Canales y Puertos' degree, a Spanish 'five-year-plus-thesis' degree equivalent to a MSc Civil Engineering. I have had internship experiences in Structural Analysis and Product Management, participating commonly in international projects and even working abroad for several months. I speak fluently English, French, Spanish and Catalan and I am currently learning German.

Please do not hesitate to contact me at: miquel.lahoz@gmail.com
