David De Franco Levi's Summary

Having been one of the pioneers in the precious metals industry in Panama City, Panama with GoldBullion Panama, I acquired specific and general knowledge about every player in the business here in Panama. I sold GoldBullion Panama in January of 2013, so I do not buy or sell precious metals anymore. However, I do offer myself as a consultant for anybody who is interested in the Panamanian precious metals market.

Currently, I am managing a new venture, SimplyEconomic.com. This venture aims to simplify some of the most complicated economic terminology to the level in which anybody can understand.

Lastly, I have the ability to create and promote professional blogs for businesses and companies around the world. Please don't hesitate to contact me for any of the services mentioned above.
