Cameron Sowder's Summary

Considered to be a leader in Telecommunications Management, particularly Telecom Expense Management (TEM), Telecom Project Management (TPM), and Telecom Workforce Management (TWM). Accomplished 16 year career successfully enhancing operations and managing projects for Fortune 500 businesses, including many on the Fortune 20. Unique background includes working from all 3 perspectives with enterprises, telecom carriers, and TEM providers. Top TEM salesman and speaker. Expert witness in telecom related legal cases. Co-authoring a book on Telecommunications Management. Developed and sold TelecomExpenseManagement.com and WirelessExpenseManagement.com. Entrepreneurial manager with proven skills and demonstrated expertise in telecom for entire career. Masters in Telecom Management and Bachelors in Technology Project Management, both accredited by PMI. Pursuing Doctorate in IT/Telecom Management. Multiple certifications and Ivy League programs. Active committee member of TEMIA publishing and presenting wireline, wireless, and mobile information. Telephony and IT/IS experience includes SDLC, financial, and general management. Known for being available nearly 24/7 and successfully managing remote/virtual/international projects. Consistently ranked at the top of TEM related searches. Recently nominated as TEM Professional of the Year and TEM Consultant/Project Manager on the largest wireline TEM implementation. Executive Board Member at Telecom Expense Management Industry Association (TEMIA).
