Applicant company for .med top level domain

On June 20, 2011, the board of directors at the internet regulatory authority ICANN approved a decision to expand top level domains (TLD) from the 22 currently available (.com, .info, .net, .org, etc.) to include any new extension desired.

With this initative, a door is opened to potentially forming new namespaces to compliment brands, sectors or professional communities.

Leveraging over 10 years’ experience in domain names and nearly 3 years managing sectoral domains for the French Network Information Centre, or AFNIC (.medecin.fr, .chirurgiens-dentistes.fr, .pharmacien.fr, etc.), Hexap is equipped to handle the anticipated technical, operational and financial challenges that this opportunity presents.
