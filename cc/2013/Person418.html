Matthias Zeitler's Summary

Matthias is the founder and CEO of MarkTheGlobe, a Global SEO company focusing on search engine optimization for international markets.

Prior to founding MarkTheGlobe, Matthias career included executive management positions at leading localization companies like STAR, Trados and SDL International. He has helped Fortune 1000 companies like Daimler, John Deere, NetApp and Nikon to master their global web content management and business process automation challenges.

With a deep understanding of multilingual web content, he is inspiring his organization to deliver a Global SEO production platform that successfully scales to the requirements of customers like Red Bull and Orderman.

His academic background is in computer science and he holds a MBA from Santa Clara University.

Specialties

Global SEO, Product Management, offshore, globalization, localization, translation, BPO, process automation, process reengineering, software development, CMS, Content Management, WCM, GMS, Globalization Management, TWS, Translation Workflow Automation, online communities, social networks, web 2.0
