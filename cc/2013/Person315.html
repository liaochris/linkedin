Joel Vincent's Summary

Joel is the Director of Product Marketing for Aerohive Networks where he is responsible for understanding market trends and customer needs and marrying them to Aerohive’s product lines. He has over 20 years experience in the networking and wireless industry, an Electrical Engineering degree from MIT, and has spent his career researching technology and working with companies to move from technological innovation to high-value, business-changing solution.

Prior to Aerohive, Joel led product marketing and management teams at Meru Networks, Cisco, CopperCom, was one of the first 20 employees and the lead for Netgear’s (Nasdaq:NTGR) entry into the wireless market, and designed enterprise networks for Fortune 500 companies as a part of International Network Services (now a part of British Telecom). He has successfully brought over 70 products and services to market worldwide, garnering awards ranging from Comdex best-in-show to a nomination for a ComputerWorld Smithsonian award.

Joel is also the co-producer and founder of the international Wine Bloggers Conference (focusing on the North America edition) which brings together the most vibrant community of wine writers in the world.

Specialties

Product management and marketing for high technology companies. I focus purely on what it takes to move from a robust, extensible technology to high-value, business-changing solution. Smart people make great technology, I make technology great!
