Virginia Hottel's Summary

Virginia was in the first graduating class of Virginia Commonwealth University with a BFA in Interior Design. Since 1985 she has successfully run her own firm working with numerous clients throughout the Washington DC metropolitan area. In addition, she has worked for clients throughout the United States including Maine, Florida, New Jersey, Colorado and Delaware. Virginia takes on all aspects of a project from design concepts to installation of the last accessory. Her business has been built primarily by client referral over the years and she's fortunate to be working with different generations of the same families. Your visions for your home become a reality when you entrust your ideas to Virginia and Wright-Whitmore Interiors.
