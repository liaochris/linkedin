Emma Langman's Summary

Conjuring up magical change that re-integrates vertical and horizontal silos from boardroom to front-line, from supplier to society. And making complex problems and staff morale issues disappear in a puff of pink smoke. For good.

Engaging and experienced consultant in performance management, change management and other aspects of business improvement. Professional facilitator with a skill for overcoming the "middle manager resistance" common to change programmes. Able to work at all levels in an organisation to align process improvement work with strategic aims. Particularly interested in work that increases the efficiency and performance of organisations whose main goal is to improve the lives of fellow citizens (e.g. Third Sector, Public Sector, Engineering etc).

Co-creator of the Great Insiders tribe (with Louise Ebrey). We work with your internal consultants, specialists and advisors to maximise their effectiveness and help them earn and get the recognition they deserve within your organisation. Why pay for additional support before fully utilising the fantastic people you already have on your pay role? Save money and boost morale with our offering to you and your Great Insiders.
