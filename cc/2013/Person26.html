Richard Fawal's Summary

Passionate communications and operations executive with more than twenty years experience guiding local, state, national, and global campaigns for non-profits, trade associations, corporations, start-ups and candidates.

Expertise in: Management; budget & finance; project and database management; member recruitment; global partnerships and international coalition building; IT infrastructure; lean methodology; digital publishing; social media platforms; web, tablet and smartphone app development; Internet marketing; video production and distribution; media relations.

Specializing in: Public policy; internal and external communications; public relations; non-profits and association; strategic planning and governance; entrepreneurship; executive management; product development; strategic alliances.
