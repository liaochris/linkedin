Robert Berstein's Summary

Over 15 years of investment banking and venture capital experience. Investment banker at Signal Hill. Prior affiliations: Morgan Keegan/Revolution Partners, BMO, Needham, Jefferies Broadview, Merrill Lynch, Advanta Partners LP. Education: Cornell, AB, magna cum laude. Co-founder, former Chairman, New York Private Equity Network (NYPEN).

Specialties: Finance and M&A deal execution, both private and public, and corporate/business development. Specialization in Digital Media, e-Commerce, Internet, Interactive Marketing, Software, Services, IT.
