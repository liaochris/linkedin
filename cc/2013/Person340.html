Julie Gordon White Bestselling Author, Coach & Broker's Summary

After 25 years in the business world, and most recently after working with hundreds of business owners over the last 10 years, I felt called to share in a much bigger way, what I've learned from selling million dollar plus businesses, as well as running my own business brokerage firm with 7 plus figures in transactions.

So from many to millions, The WELL for Women Entrepreneurs journeys towards it's mission of teaching and inspiring EVERY woman business owner who passionately wants to grow her business to a million and someday sell for a million.

In addition to The WELL, I continue to work with a few private clients to facilitate the sale of their companies through my firm, BlueKey Business Brokerage Mergers & Acquisitions founded in 2003, specializing in companies with an enterprise value between $1 to $10 million. I also oversee BlueKey Media Group - the publishing and speaking brand extension of my Amazon bestselling book, "EXIT! 12 Steps to Sell Your Business for the Price You Deserve".
