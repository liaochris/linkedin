Charles Brandt II CSP,CFPS,CBCP,CBCLA,MS's Summary

Charles W. Brandt, Vice President, Senior Risk Control Consultant providing risk management services for high profile clients.

Stengths include Executive presentations, Risk Analysis, Training & Education, technical Subject Matter Expert (SME), Occupational Safety & Health, OSHA Compliance, Emergency Response Planning/Audit/Review/Development, Incident Management ICS/NIMS, Bussiness Continuity development, ERM , Security Management/Active Killer procedures,Workers Compensation/Claims/ Cost Continment/Medical Management, RTW process, Behavior Based Safety, Fire/Special Hazards Protection & Property Risk Control, Crisis Management. General Liability & Life Safety, Transportation Risk.

Specializes in identifying risk, implementiing a heiarchy of controls and managing exposures related to industry practices and operations. Drives Executive change to improve profitablity, reduce risk/threat exposures. Specific experience in Retail, Warehousing, Property Management, Non-Profits, Hospitality, Health Care, Manufacturing, Transportation.

Charles holds a BS in Fire & Safety Engineering from Eastern Kentucky University and a MS in Occupational Safety & Health Management from West Virginia University.

Charles is a board Certified Safety Professional (CSP), a Certified Fire Protection Specialist (CFPS) awarded by the (NFPA)National Fire Protection Association.

Mr. Brandt is also a Certified Business Continuity Professional (CBCP) and a Certified Business Continuity Lead Auditor (CBCLA) by exam /peer review through DRI. Professional Member status , American Society of Safety Engineers (ASSE), the National Fire Protection Association (NFPA) and the Association of Contingency Planners (ACP). ALICE trainer.

Mr. Brandt remains an active member of the Maryland volunteer fire service for over 30 years and is a Level III, NFPA nationally certified Fire discipline instructor with the University of Maryland, Fire & Rescue Institute (MFRI) in College Park, Maryland.

Specialties
