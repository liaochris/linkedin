Meredith Brace Sloss' Summary

Meredith is an adaptable young professional, living in and loving Los Angeles, California. She came to the West Coast as so many do: packing everything that could comfortably fit in the trunk and backseat of her Toyota Corolla, crossing her fingers, and charging groceries on her credit card until steady work came around.

Two years later, Meredith is assisting multiple high-profile entertainment professionals. She avoids sleeping by writing (screenplays and personal essays), keeping up with going-ons in the entertainment industry, and watching as many movies and as much TV as she can. She does this because her long term goals are to write and produce film and television.

Past and present, her roles have demanded attention to detail, reliability, and resourcefulness. While she fully appreciates the importance of work/life balance, Meredith is, undoubtedly, a restless workaholic.
