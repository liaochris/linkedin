Stephanie Jordan's Summary

Multilingual marketer specialized in luxury wine & spirit marketing/communication/PR with excellent understanding global brands due to international upbringing. A range of experience from working with local wine producers, government bodies; to the largest wine and spirit company in the world. Graduated in Marketing from l’école de commerce européenne in Lyon, trained in luxury capabilities selling, WSET as well as various other qualifications in the spirits industry. Fluent in English, Spanish and French.

Dynamic and fresh thinking with great comercial skills, responsive to cultural diversity with strong drive and high energy levels.

Co-Founder of: VINGLISH® IS THE SPECIALIST IN ENGLISH FOR THE WINE & SPIRIT TRADE

We are specialists in "Wine English", 100% dedicated to the wine & sprit trade with extensive knowledge and experience of the industry and the challenges facing producers today.
