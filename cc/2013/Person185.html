Denise Wakeman, Online Visibility Expert's Summary

I am an online marketing advisor and founder of The Blog Squad™.

Since 1996, I have been assisting professionals and small businesses to leverage the Internet with online marketing strategies using business blogs, ezines, content development plans and ecommerce systems. I have more than 20 years experience in small business management and have co-authored numerous blogging programs and books. Most recently I contributed a chapter on business blogging to "Success Secrets of Social Media Superstars" (2010).

I frequently speak at conferences and workshops about business blogging and how to create massive online visibility as well as gain expert status through social marketing so you can get more traffic, leads, prospects, clients, media inquires, JV opportunities, and well, more business opportunities in general.

You can get my free 7 day e-course on how to boost your visibility on the web at http://BoostYourVisibility.com
