Kelly Alcorn, Business Activation Strategist's Summary

Kelly Alcorn is The Business Activation Strategist. As speaker, coach and mentor, Kelly works with individuals and groups to help them first identify obstacles and then create an activation plan that’s life transforming.

Kelly’s unique approach inspires people to dream about and identify their goals; then she successfully moves them to action so they become successful achievers.

Prior to becoming The Activation Strategist, Kelly worked in the mortgage industry. For over 10 years Kelly was a top company producer, built a six figure income and earned President’s Sales designation awards.

Kelly earned her Bachelors of Science and Business Administration degree at the University of Missouri-Columbia. An avid lifelong learner Kelly continues to attend seminars, workshops and online study courses so she can offer cutting edge business activation coaching to her clients.

Kelly previously held a leadership position on the board of ESPW (Encouraging, Supporting and Promoting Women) and is active in several local chambers and networking organizations. Kelly was a top ten nominee for the Spirit of St. Peters award and was recognized by Zonta as a woman of leadership in the community

Kelly is available to speak, facilitate your event or to provide one on one coaching.
