James Tsismanakis, CDME's Summary

James Tsismanakis is the Executive Director and CEO of the Discover DeKalb / DeKalb Convention and Visitors Bureau, Destination DeKalb and Sports DeKalb in Metro Atlanta. He serves on the Board of Directors of Atlanta Metro Travel Association, Georgia Association of Convention and Visitors Bureaus, Georgia State University Cecil B. Day School of Hospitality Administration Industry Advisory Board, DeKalb History Center, DeKalb Chamber and the Georgia Chamber Tourism and Entertainment Committee. He was selected by the Atlanta Business Chronicle in 2011& 2012 as one of the Who's Who 100 Leaders Who Are Shaping Atlanta's Hospitality Industry. James resides in the North Lake area of Atlanta with his wife Bethany and their three children.

Specialties: Tourism, product development, destination management, destination marketing, and foundation management.
