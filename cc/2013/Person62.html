Peter Vincent's Summary

A CISSP certified Senior Managing Consultant and Security Architect with IBM Security Services. An experienced Security Team Lead and Lead Security Architect, I have led teams of security specialists and architects. I have a diverse role, from working on individual projects to develop the security element of the solution, to working as the lead security architect on larger programmes and leading the security workstream to develop and deliver the overall security solution. I work through all phases of the project lifecycle, bid and proposal, build, delivery and run.

As a senior member of the security practice I am involved in the day to day running and strategic direction of the practice. I take responsibility for security consulting opportunities as well as focusing on my main role as a security architect. I am a career manager for the security professionals within the practice.
