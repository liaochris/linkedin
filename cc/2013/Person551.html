Will Fugette's Summary

For over 16 years, Will Fugette has established a proven track record for implementing creative and cost effective customer response solutions for Fortune 500 clientele around the world. To be successful, Will works with employees and clients at all levels to break down communication barriers, implementing team based solutions and partnering with clients to develop strategic plans for growth, service delivery and efficiency.

Specialties

Expertise in all areas of response center operations, including but not limited to: automotive, technical support, customer service, sales, telemarketing, HR, Recruiting, IT Operations, P&L management, consulting, proposal writing, business development and financial services.
