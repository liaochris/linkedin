Jim Corley's Summary

I am focused on the recruitment, hiring, and development of next-generation State Farm agents. I emphasize sales and business consulting from an emotional intelligence perspective.

My mission is to help agents stay on the path of opportunity.

I help multi-line agents develop long term trust based relationships with clients. My goal is growth with profit. We have fun and make a difference for our clients and the company I represent.

Specialties: Sales, new agent recruitment, negotiation, litigation management, dispute resolution, teaching and public speaking (I provide workshops for small business owners on Social Media, Networking and Marketing).
