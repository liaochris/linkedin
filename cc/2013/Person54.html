Nabil Hafez's Summary

At GenomeQuest, I conceptualized and led product management and market strategy on GenomeQuest's clinical product through close interactions with clinical labs across the country. Most recently, l worked with management to create a new healthcare strategy department to capitalize on emerging market opportunities - crafting ideas, developing strategies, and executing on those strategies to spawn and execute new businesses.

Previously, I have spent over a decade working on solving problems in genomics, bioinformatics, and subsequently next generation sequencing technologies. Spending eight years at the Broad Institute managing a team working on the finishing of the Human Genome Project. I later transitioned to the Technology Development Group as next generation sequencing technology emerged and worked with NGS vendors’ products from their infancy and helped develop these early prototypes into genome center production scale platforms.

With that gained experience I founded Seqwise LLC a tech consulting firm that helped laboratories, institutes, start-ups, and pharmaceutical companies transition to NGS through technology and strategy consulting as well as informatics services. As managing partner, my focus was on business development, operations, as well as product, market, and bioinformatics consulting.

I hold an MBA from the Fuqua School of Business at Duke University, a MS in Bioinformatics from Brandeis University, and a BS in Biology from Binghamton University.
