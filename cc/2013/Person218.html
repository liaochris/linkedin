Evan Denbaum's Summary

A seasoned content strategist and online marketing professional for video game companies such as Electronic Arts (Origin), Sony PlayStation Network and OnLive cloud gaming. Additional experience in corporate communications and public relations.

Excellent management and interpersonal skills with the ability to delegate to large teams and work simultaneously on multiple projects toward eCommerce acquisition, engagement and monetization goals. Extensive experience working cross-functionally with product producers and PMs, Operations and international counterparts.

Prior to working in Silicon Valley, Evan specialized in increasing user stay and engagement at sports entertainment mega-sites WWE.com (World Wrestling Entertainment) and SportingNews.com, which share a core demographic with the video game industry.
