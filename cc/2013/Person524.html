Jeremy Tissot's Summary

Jeremy L. Tissot is the founding partner of the California based law firm, THE TISSOT LAW FIRM, P.C., a firm whose partners are highly experienced litigators, with a winning track record in the courtroom. We serve all of California, with lawyer staffed offices throughout the state; we have litigated cases in every county in California. Call us 24/7 at 888-661-0444.

Mr. Tissot has handled litigation matters, including representing both individuals and entities, for more than 17 years, in both federal and state trial court. Mr. Tissot has special expertise in complex matters where the cases at issue demand the knowledge of an experienced trial lawyer. He has spoken to groups and associations on many topics, including defamation, employment law, entertainment industry issues and insurance/risk prevention.

Mr. Tissot was formerly a partner in the LA office of the prominent Callahan, McCune & Willis firm, where he was elevated to the position of partner after only six years of practice and became the youngest partner in the firm’s history.

Specialties: Business Litigation; Major Personal Injury; Defamation; Employment Law; Entertainment Law and Class Actions
