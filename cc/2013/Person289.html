Jeremy Davis' Summary

Highly regarded as a turnaround expert and startup leader in the banking industry with a proven, results-oriented executive leadership style, Davis is currently Head of Mortgage Banking for the Synovus affiliate, The Bank of Nashville leading the mortgage divisions that serve Tennessee.
Synovus maintains three affiliate banks in Tennessee, The Bank of Nashville in Nashville,TN; Cohutta Banking Co. in Chattanooga, TN and Trust One Bank in Memphis, TN.

Honored in 2009 by the Nashville Business Journal as one of Nashville’s Top 40 most influential business leaders under 40, Davis has developed a rare mastering of sales productivity and operational efficiency that transcends multiple facets of the banking industry.

Previously, Davis led a high profile, successful growth campaign for Fifth Third Bank, raising the bank's profile among Realtors and builders, ultimately helping to establish Fifth Third among the top mortgage lenders in the area. Jeremy has also executed sucessful expansions into new market territories for JPMorgan Chase/Washington Mutual Bank.

Davis has become a sought after leader proving himself to be a visionary thinker, easily adaptable in changing market conditions. A top sales executive, he has utilized his results driven leadership style to recruit, build, motivate and retain members from the most visible and productive sales and operations teams in the country.

Specialties: Retail Banking, Mortgage Banking, Mortgage Sales & Operations, Commercial Lending, Credit, Credit Policy, Investment Banking, Team Building, Business Development, P&L management, New Market Development, Market turn-arounds, Sales Effectiveness, Operations efficiency, Banking Cross-Sell Development, Sales Management & Motivation, Executive Coach, Business process coach
