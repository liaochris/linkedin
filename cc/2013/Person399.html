Michael Davis' Summary

My background includes work as a creative director, cinematographer, technologist and agency business leader. I'm fortunate to have been awarded three Personal Achievement Emmy Awards for individual excellence. Work has been recognized at Turin, Cannes, One Show and international film festivals receiving critical acclaim for approaches to content creation.  

In 1998, a group of us started a HBO funded digital media channel that grew to support more than 200 employees. Our work blurred the lines between browser and tube with a social content strategy that launched one of the largest advertising supported channels in the US and Europe. Several campaigns are featured in Motion/TVFILM, a hardcover book published by HarperCollins. http://tinyurl.com/AmazonTVFILM 

In 2002, the agency world called - off to start an emerging media practice. Spanning an eight year journey at Draft Worldwide and Draft FCB, I led digital, emerging media, broadcast and content development disciplines.  

As a 2009 executive MBA graduate of The Berlin School of Creative Leadership at Steinbeis University Germany, my thesis chronicled a group of international students who authored "Rise of the Technology Class." The students created a journal of their opinions and ethical contributions that are helping to change the world. http://www.ethicalcontributor.com  

Currently, I'm focused on building the holding company Agencies of Change. AOC work has been featured by Adage, Adweek, Contagious, Forbes, Fortune, Mashable's Best Social Media, OMMA and Forrester Groundswell Awards. I'm an advocate for the media industry, serving on juries for Effie Global and US Awards, Internet Advertising Competition (IAC), Addy, AICP as well as speaking engagements at Cannes Lions & co-keynote with Sir Richard Branson of the Virgin Group.

Specialties

Brands: Alcoa, Audi, Burt's Bees, Cadillac, Coca-Cola, Cuervo, CVS/pharmacy, Dannon, Enterprise - Alamo, GSK, HBO, JCPenney, Kellogg's, Louisville Slugger, NBC Universal, MoneyGram, MM's, Nike, Nokia, Motorola, P&G, Pfizer, Pizza Hut, RadioShack, Samsung, SCJ, Smirnoff, State Farm, Stolichnaya, United States Postal Service.
