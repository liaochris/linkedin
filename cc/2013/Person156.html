Chris Kinsey's Summary

Expert Turnaround Strategist plus Services and Operations Executive experienced across multiple business disciplines, including Operations Management, Human Resources and Procurement, Shared Services, Information Technology Operations, and Finance and Accounting. Highly experienced in captive shared services and outsourcing business models, including profit and loss management.

Relied upon for expertise in turning around troubled operations and organizations. Innovative problem-solver and successful strategist, managing all outsourcing contracts, contractors, and quality metrics to ensure profitable results. Leads both development and implementation for operational improvements, with emphasis on profit, process, and quality.

• Expert manager of the outsourcing cycle from analysis and identification of needs through contract negotiation and stakeholder buy-in on multi-year, multi-discipline contracts.

• Highly experienced with resource acquisition and allocation, as well as the successful creation of globally matrixed sourcing environments to create integrated, connected, and high-performing teams without borders or time zones.
