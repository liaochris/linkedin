Ashley Woods' Summary

Ashley WOODS

• Experienced event organiser with international marketing & communications background.
• Solid commitment to development issues with strong NGO and media relations.
• Excellent networking, interpersonal & cross-cultural skills.
• Ability to conceive & implement ideas single-handedly.
• Articulate native English speaker + French and German.

Ashley Woods was born in the UK but grew up in Far East Asia (Brunei, Malaysia and Singapore). He has excellent cross-cultural communication and interpersonal skills and speaks fluent English, French and German.

Over the past 15 years he has worked and organised events for such brands as Microsoft, UBS Banking Corp, Magnum Photos, Warner Independent Films and the World Economic Forum amongst others.

As Senior Consultant for the Belgium based international communication agency 'Mach Media' (www.machmedia.be/) he handed all PR, media buying and event management for Fokker Aircraft Services. Prior to moving to Belgium he work closely with the ICRC and the International Peace Bureau (IPB), Geneva on a number of large public outdoor events including the IPB's Nobel Peace centenary along lake Geneva which attracted over 35,000 people.

Ashley can be contacted by email: mail@ashleywoods.com
