Stuart Browne's Summary

Stuart Browne is responsible for Institutional Sales and Marketing at Good Harbor Financial. Prior to Good Harbor, he was responsible for corporate and business development / marketing for Decision Analytics, a leading provider of investment consulting services for Fortune 400 companies and the public sector. Prior to Decision Analytics, Stuart was the director of domestic acquisitions and capital raising for Silverleaf Holdings, a private real estate investment company. He has also served as Vice President of Investments for the southwest at Pinnacle Realty, which maintained a $17B portfolio. He has been head of corporate development at two private investment banking companies (Diagonal Integration and Six Sigma Group), and has advised companies in technology, real estate, and other industries on capital raising from the private and public capital markets. While still in college, he founded the fifth largest mortgage banking company in St. Louis and sold it to J.P. Morgan Chase. He attended St. Louis University and Maryville University and holds a bachelor’s degree in management.
