Bob Watson | CRB | @TopBrokerOC's Summary

Bob is known within and outside the real estate industry as a leader, coach and trainer in the integration and use of technology, social media tools and social networking for real estate and small business.

An early adopter of technology and the Internet, Bob was one the first N.A.R. members to receive the ePro designation. As Director of Education and Product Development for the Prudential Real Estate Affiliate franchise organization Bob created and rolled-out the first-of-its-kind Prudential eCertified program.

Bob is currently working with Krisstina and Garry Wise, owners of The Good Life Team and “Coffee with Krisstina” on the roll-out of The Paperless Agent” iPad for Real Estate program/

A key influencer on social channels, Bob studies the latest in technology, the Internet and social media developments to identify best practices in delivering a best-in-class service delivery for today’s consumer.

Bob began selling real estate in the exclusive luxury market of Georgetown in Washington DC and quickly became a Washington DC Association of REALTORS® Multi-Million Dollar Sales Award Winner. Bob’s real estate career has spanned sales, management and brokerage ownership.

The internationally acclaimed Social Media Mastermind Orange County (SMMOC) was founded by Bob in 2009 to provide a free venue and forum for the discussion and sharing of ideas related to social media, technology and the Internet.

Bob continues to sell, recruit and manage as an Associate Broker for TopBroker Network Real Estate. He is a licensed broker in California (#01469641)
