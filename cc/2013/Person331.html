Jon Waldman's Summary

Jon is an all-purpose communications coordination and consultation expert in Winnipeg, Manitoba. Experience includes article/press release/other media preparation (both writing and editing), photography, radio broadcasting and other facets of publication and audio media. He is also experienced in press conference and other media coordination, as well as event planning.

Jon is an Account Manager with Winnipeg's Hep Communications, where he provides Internet-based and traditional marketing solutions for companies of all sizes.

Specialties: Writing, copyediting, radio host, photo editing, photography, online marketing, writing for the web, design, consulting, marketing plan development, press release preparation, media relations, event planning and management
