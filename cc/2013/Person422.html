Nestor Villalobos' Summary

An Ironman Triathlon finisher and new business entrepreneur, Nestor’s drive in work and personal life are unequivocal. With an undergraduate and MBA degree from Bentley College and over eight years of work experience from publicly traded tech companies Dell and Systemax, Nestor has become an expert at product launches.
