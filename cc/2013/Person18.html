Davin "Hutch" Hutchins' Summary

Davin Hutchins is an award-winning journalist, manager, motivator, digital strategist, video producer and consultant specializing in the Middle East, international social issues, media training, social media and digital platforms. He has a solid track record in defining digital strategy and overseeing restructuring of legacy organizations.

Currently, Hutchins is Editorial Director at MBN Digital - the digital division of Middle East Broadcasting Networks which includes Alhurra and Radio Sawa, part of the the BBG family of international broadcasting. Before this, Hutchins was Managing Editor of Middle East Voices at Voice of America / BBG where he conceived of an Arab Spring social journalism site and supervised a team of multimedia reporters covering grass-roots democratization in the Middle East. The site won the Topical Reporting category in the 2012 Online Journalism Awards (ONA) beating CNN and the New York Times.

Hutchins has worked for and with CNN, CNN International, PBS, ITVS, BBC World, Headline News, TechTV, Witness, Internews and Huffington Post. His projects have taken him across the Middle East and Africa.

In 2008-09, he was Managing Producer at American News Project in Washington, D.C. He produced investigative video reports on U.S. politics and the 2008 presidential elections which appeared on Huffington Post, Al Jazeera English and CNN.

Among career highlights, Hutchins directed The Art Of Flight in 2004-05, his feature documentary debuted at AFI Fest Hollywood, International Documentary Festival Amsterdam and Bangkok International Film Festival.
