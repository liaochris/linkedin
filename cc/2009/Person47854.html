Jeff Owen’s Summary

In a nutshell... I make indoor spaces healthier places.

My company provides Healthy Living products and services including nutritional supplements, weight loss programs, air purifiers, water purifiers, truly GREEN surface cleaners and protectants, mold/IAQ inspection services and solutions, and money saving electricity management systems.

I have been trained to come into your home (or office) and do a complete Assessment Interview looking at the occupants, the exterior of the building, and the interior living space in an effort to develop an IAQ (indoor air quality) profile of your environment. Based on that investigation, we take samples of the air and surfaces to help determine whether sanitization or remediation might be recommended.

Should sanitization be suggested, which is most often case, we are able to recommend specific solutions, including warrenty programs, to indoor air quality issues. We are not about specific manufacturers except as those manufacturers make products that actually solve, rather than mask, IAQ problems.
