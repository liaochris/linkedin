Lance Osborne’s Summary

Lance Osborne is a web design professional with more than 12 years experience in interaction design, user interface design, and information architecture.

Lance has exceptional knowledge of hand-coded (X)HTML and CSS, web standards, accessibility, and usability testing. He is known for a user-centered approach to creating clean, simple, easy-to-use interfaces that meet business objectives and user needs. Lance also has proven leadership and team-building skills.

Lance has a rare combination of creative and technical expertise with an excellent understanding of how design impacts development (and vice versa).

Lance Osborne’s Specialties:

accessibility, hand-coded (X)HTML and CSS, information architecture, interaction design, prototyping, simplicity, training, usability testing, use cases, user interface design, web standards, wireframing
