Grzegorz Gman Kozakiewicz’s Summary

Grzegorz Kozakiewicz is an accomplished art director, designer + a craftsman. His ability to physically build mixed media art into digital experiences has garnered him international awards for various interactive mixed media, stop motion and film projects.

Most notable is Grzegorz’s Pencil Rebel project which has been featured on a large list of publications and websites, including Disney, recognized by Flash in The Can, Webby Award and FWA.

- Creator of one of the top performing games on Disney XD Portals,
- Winner of Digital Yorkshire Award 2009 for Best Game,
- Webby Awards Honoree
- Nominated to Golden Joystick Award 2008 for best online game.
