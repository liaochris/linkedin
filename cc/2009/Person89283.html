Sarah Cohen’s Summary

I am a therapeutic massage therapist dedicated to providing excellent service to my clients at a reasonable price.
A graduate of McIntosh College in Dover New Hampshire, I am licensed in the States of New Hampshire and Maine as a Massage Therapist. My attainment of a Reiki Level II practitioner has allowed me to offer a broader spectrum of services to my clientele than ever before. I bring a fresh enthusiasm to the field, as well as over 6 years of personal experience in the massage therapy field. My personal and professional experiences have led me to where I am today.
I work with my clients to improve their wellbeing and comfort through massage. I also maintain a friendly, fair, and creative work environment, which respects diversity, ideas, and hard work.
