Clint Stonebraker’s Summary

I began Stonebraker's Inc. in 1993. Stonebraker's Inc. manages adolescent substance abuse treatment programs, counselor certification training programs, and a publishing company. I have been a counselor since 1987.

I am the author of Relationships for the Intimately Challenged and a speaker. I specialize in facilitating workshops and seminars focused on family relationships.

Clint Stonebraker’s Specialties:

Individual counseling, family counseling, substance abuse counselor training, writing, public speaking, staff supervision
