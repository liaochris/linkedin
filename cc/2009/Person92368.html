Mike Simpson’s Summary

I have close to twenty-three years' experience programming, and fifteen years' professional experience building applications for business using the best available technologies. This experience helps me make sound judgments about how best to design a system and arrange development processes to achieve business goals.

I have a broad but keen interest in software, and enjoy exploring new programming avenues as a hobby. I take an interest in software quality and good design, and treat my work as a profession, not as a job. My business communication skills are top-notch, and I can communicate effectively with both business leaders and technical specialists.

I hold a number of Microsoft certifications for VB 6.0, .NET 1.1 and 2.0, as well as a programming certification from Sun for the Java 2 platform. I am also a member of the IASA.

Ultimately, my goal is to found my own software or consulting company, implement some of my own ideas about effective software development, and see those ideas blossom.

Mike Simpson’s Specialties:

I specialize in designing and building database-driven business applications using web technologies. I am very knowledgeable about Microsoft's .NET platform and the C# language, but I do not shy away from other technologies and will use whatever is appropriate. My client work for the last seven to eight years has exposed me to regulatory compliance and auditing topics (FDA), and financial services including ACH, wire transfer, fed funds, (re)insurance, etc.
