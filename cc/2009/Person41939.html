Jon Sorensen’s Summary

Mr. Sorensen is an ITS Engineer and Project Manager with 18+ years of experience in all aspects of the planning, design, construction, integration and operations of Intelligent Transportation Systems, including Advanced Traffic Management Systems, Advanced Traveler Information Systems and Electronic Tolling/Open Road Tolling Systems. Mr. Sorensen has served as ITS Project Manager for projects ranging from smaller local ITS implementations to large-scale, multi-million dollar regional deployments.

Intelligent Transportation Systems, Regional Operations, Planning and Design, Advanced Traffic Management Systems, Advanced Traveler Information Systems, Electronic tolling, Fiscal Analysis, Value Engineering, Policy and Program Support
