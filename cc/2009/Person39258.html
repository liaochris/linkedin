Heidi Barack’s Summary

My professional career has been 21 years in the music industry. I’ve done everything from being a buyer at Tower Records, Field Marketing Rep for Sony/BMG and most recently, Field Rep for Warner/Elektra/Atlantic.

Artwork -
Commissioned by Eddie Vedder of Pearl Jam to design buttons for their 2006 World Tour ***http://www.pearljam.com/goods/ ***

Poster artwork for Warner/Elektra/Atlantic featuring Regina Spektor and The Subways.

Artwork has appeared in a variety of venues through out the Seattle area. It is also featured in two movies, “Half Nelson”, starring Ryan Gosling and soon to be released “Normal Adolescent Behavior”, which has my Coo-Coo Birds © featured. *** http://us.imdb.com/title/tt0468489/fullcredits#cast ***

Artwork for the band Spiritualized. I designed buttons for their “Amazing Grace” Tour *** http://www.spiritualized.com/pharmacy/tshirts/badges.php ***

My whimsical Animal Fiction© line has become a permanent fixture at Seattle’s legendary annual Bumbershoot Music Festival and has sold as buttons at Hot Topic stores nationwide.

Artwork for Gregory Page's "Sleeping Dogs" and "Knife In My Chest" CD's - ***http://www.gregorypage.com***

Hobart #4 - a literary journal, which features stories by RICK MOODY, Aimee Bender and Sjon, the Icelandic writer who pens lyrics for Bjork), Brian Ames, Ray Vukcevich and the art of HEIDI BARACK *** http://www.hobartpulp.com/orders.html ***

Music and art are my passions. I would love to do more poster/album/merchandise design for record labels.
