Karen Krajenbrink’s Summary

While I didn't envision myself as a concept/character designer... here I am! I've found my storyboard training has really helped me set up layouts and shape my characters. Odd but true.

I'm currently an art monkey for Made in Paradise, doing whatever tasks are needed to be done for our projects where we specialize in pre-production for feature films. I normally juggle three jobs at once because I'm a workaholic, but I can't see myself doing anything else but being a creative kook.
