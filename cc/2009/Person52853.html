John Stewart’s Summary

I am a Realtor® specializing in four primary real estate market segments:
• international real estate for both incoming and outgoing investments
• seniors and estate planning changes
• relocation
• housing for university students

I can support your wish to sell or purchase real estate anywhere in the US, Canada, UK and in many other countries around the world. I do not participate in dual agency because I believe that my clients deserve undivided attention and advocacy. My knowledge of the international and Pacific Northwest real estate markets will help you, or a friend, make informed and confident real estate decisions.

John Stewart’s Specialties:

I currently hold the National Association of Realtors® designations as:

• Certified International Property Specialist (CIPS)
• Transnational Referral Certification (TRC)
• Seniors Real Estate Specialist (SRES)

These designations connect my clients with very exclusive networks of similarly qualified agents in over 55 countries around the world. It also gives my clients added exposure in marketing their properties internationally.
