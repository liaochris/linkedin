Agostino Priarolo’s Summary

Currently working on my painting skills and knowledge to become a good concept artist.
My dream is to write and direct one (or more) of the next Star Wars films, but before doing that, I'd really love to join all the great concept artists at Lucasfilm/arts/ILM and work there with them a few years.
