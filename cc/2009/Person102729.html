Posh Rags Klothing’s Summary

Posh Rags Originals is an innovative designer, marketer and distributor of premium quality young men’s and women’s clothing, accessories and related products. The Posh Rags brands, symbolizes and driven by the LA Kouture, as well as being creative and forward thinking in its design. Posh Rags has consistently followed its motto of (“Quality not Price ”). And the brand is inspired by the energy of its Creator Vikter, Posh brand products are sold via its website and throughout the United States and Internationally. Posh Rags news announcements and other news are available through the company’s website at www.poshrags.com

Posh Rags Klothing’s Experience

  • Designer

    Posh Rags Originals

    (Apparel & Fashion industry)

    January 2001 — Present (8 years 7 months)

    Starting in Los Angeles Kalifornia

    Posh Rags Originals, was formed by Vikter, out of the inspiration of his surroundings a mixture of Suburbanite youth San Fernando Valley and Central and South of Central Los Angeles youth on a mission to rebel. It was a counter culture of low brow, music and tattoos.

    Posh Rags Originalz celebrates everything uniquely different and independent. Over the past 8 years Posh Rags Originalz has become a brand that people wear with pride. Posh Ragz, sticks to its time honored tradition of the anti-hero and doing it yourself. We always push the envelope Posh Rags has remained pure to its vision and continues to create apparel and accessories for the rebellious sprit.

    Posh Rags Originals, are the Creators and Purveyors of the Posh Ragz Clothing Lines! Poshweyr, Posh, Posh Brand No.13 Wicked Bitch, and Wicked, Crem Du Crem, and High Maintenance Known to many as "Posh" Posh Rags Originals, and Posh Punx were Established on 01.01.01.

    Vikter, a former LA Radio Talk show Host, Model, Actor, Film & Record Producer. as well as a Personal Manager, and with degrees both in Criminology and Psychology, Met Zhi, and Becka, both former international models, who came to Los Angeles to study fashion design, and who are known as the "Wicked Bitches" who live in Berlin Germany, and Los Angeles. Posh Rags, is about Respecting, and celebrating the very uniqueness of LA. A native of Los Angeles, embraces the LA Kulture. Vikter, decided to create Posh Rags Originals, literally by wearing it! We make our clothing to capture Simplicity with style. Posh Rags, is about self expression in a classy Simple way.
