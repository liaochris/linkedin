Molly Mangan’s Summary

Ms. Mangan is a leader in strategic planning and project management for the public sector. With 20 years experience in consulting and public management, Ms. Mangan founded Even Keel Strategies in 2007 to work directly with her clients to bring down-to-earth solutions to their challenges.

Molly Mangan’s Specialties:

Strategic Planning, Project Management, Project Reviews, Geographic Information Systems, GIS, Database Design, Geodatabase Design, Business Process Analysis, PMI Training, Project Manager Mentoring, eGovernment Applications
