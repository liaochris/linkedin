Richard Wolfstrome’s Summary

I'm an entrepreneurial award-winning creative, with over 21 years’ experience, whose body of work includes projects across a diverse range of sectors, from public, blue chip corporate, film and media, to the arts. I'm relentless in furthering information graphic design by continually challenging the technical and creative disciplines.

Primary strengths lie in determining how to communicate information optimally across different media, and in project management from gestation to completion. I'm a blue-sky thinker, who will look at unique and value-added solutions to enhance product.

I've worked with clients such as the Foreign & Commonwealth Office, DrugScope, Office of the Deputy Prime Minister, Deloitte, Brighton Festival, Arts & Business, Creative Partnerships, RNLI, London Fire Brigade, Central Office of Information, University of Chichester and Ecopark.

I sit on the boards of British Design Innovation (South East) and the International Society of Typographic Designers. I'm also a member of the steering committee for the South Coast Design Forum (Brighton).
