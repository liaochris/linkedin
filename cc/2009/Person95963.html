Susan Biali’s Summary

My passion is helping you to discover yourself, create vibrant health, and design the life of your dreams!

I used to be a depressed, frustrated doctor, until I left full-time practice to follow my dreams as a flamenco dancer in Los Cabos, Mexico. It was tough at first, but I didn't give up and eventually ended up performing for celebrities, the owners of major sports teams such as the Dallas Cowboys and the Lakers, and other high profile clients. Last year, I was invited to perform on the E! Entertainment Network, and my husband and I even taught Pink how to salsa!

It's been quite the ride, and the miraculous experiences, joy, and wonderful health that I discovered now fuel my work as a media wellness expert, author, life coach and inspirational/motivational speaker.

My keynotes and seminars/workshops focus on teaching you how to create a happier, healthier, more fulfilling life, on many different levels, and I combine dance performances and workshops with my speaking.

My upcoming book, "Your Prescription for Life: 7 Steps to Extraordinary Health & Happiness", will be released in Jan 2010 by Beaufort Books (NYC)

I'm currently back in Vancouver, focusing on my newest passion: educating the public through media across North America. I've appeared on MSNBC and The Good Life Network, and in Self, Fitness, Hello!, Chatelaine, Today's Parent, the Medical Post, etc. I write two columns for Canadian national magazines, and am an expert for HealthyCanada.com.

I'm also developing my own television show, "Prescription for Life" (TM), with a team of producers - watch for it!

I'd like to increase my work with media across North America, syndicate a widely distributed column on life and wellness, and increase my speaking engagements internationally. I'm fluent in Spanish, though I don't actually have a drop of Latin or Spanish blood!

That simply confirms that all things are possible,when you believe in yourself and your dreams.
