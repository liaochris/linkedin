Recruited and retained talent; then led team’s strategic vision to market to the gaming culture. Cut 20% in labor costs by tapping into internal staff talents and avoiding outsourcing.

    • Campaigns: Headed in-house conception and rollout of integrated campaigns for Return to Zork, MechWarrior 2, Battle Zone and hundreds of others. Averted anticipated expenditures; gained buy-in and directed advertising.
