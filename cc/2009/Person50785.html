Joseph Corr’s Summary

Allegedly, Joseph Corr is a Senior Technical Lead with Crispin Porter + Bogusky, Creativity, Advertising Age and Adweek's 2008 Agency of the Year. Previously, he was the Manager of Technology at IQ Interactive, a founding SODA member.

An Interactive Developer/Designer with over 13 years of professional experience designing and developing for the web, Joe has a background in Flash, Flex, AIR, Silverlight and other Interactive tools. Aside from working on several clandestine, weaponized advertising “projects” with CP+B, Joe is currently finishing his MFA thesis from the Savannah College of Art and Design in Interactive Design.

When he’s not in the top-secret CP+B Media Laboratory, Joe enjoys creating digital and theoretical plastique, and walking his dog with his wife.

Joseph Corr’s Specialties:

ActionScript 1/2/3 programming, Flex, AIR, SQL, PHP, Coldfusion, CSS, XHTML, JS, Silverlight. Adobe suites including After Effects. 15 years of HTML experience from Mosaic 1.0 and Lynx. Interests include Papervision3D, Augmented Reality, Physical Computing, Art Installations, Information Visualizations and the future of Video.
