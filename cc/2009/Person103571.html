Virender Vaira’s Summary

International Marketing & Business development professional with over 30 years experience.

Since 1978 I have been handling business opportunities relating to Government procurements overseas, which generally follow process of International Competitive bidding / Global tender process. Also procurements under World Bank funding, UN Procurements, relief supplies, Government line of credits & grants etc. Have worked in Government, public sector undertakings and private sector Corporates, covering Import and Export of Electrical switchgear, Engineering Equipment, General merchandise, Agro-commodities etc.

Focus markets - Middle East, Africa, Europe & Russian block.

Also have experience in representing Foreign Companies for their business interest with India.

Goal: Marketing tie up with Foreign companies for Indian market.
