Eric Glazer’s Summary

I am VP, Marketing at an innovative consulting firm that serves the life sciences industry (www.chacorporate.com). My focus is on practical lead generation through relationship building. I have developed numerous strategic marketing plans and executed hundreds of successful, revenue generating marketing programs that support those plans including the launch of a B2B community, organization of hundreds of webinars and live events, and production of numerous thought-leadership papers.

I am also part of the Marketing Studio webinar series: "Lead Generation Tactics for Small Business" and assist small businesses in the execution of lead generation programs.

http://www.marketingstudio.net
http://marketingstudio.eventbrite.com

Eric Glazer’s Specialties:

Lead generation, execution, marketing strategy and communications, marketing and sales operations, project management, translating and articulating business objectives into results-driven marketing strategies, plans and deliverables, 14+ years experience B2B marketing and selling with a focus on the life sciences industry.
