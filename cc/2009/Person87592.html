Ryan Youens’s Summary

As a composer, Ryan's music has been performed throughout New Zealand and internationally, gaining enthusiastic responses by audiences and performers alike. As a copyist, in only his third year as a full-time professional he is now regarded as one of the leading copyists in New Zealand, working worldwide for leading ensembles, composers and music organisations. As an arranger, his work for concerts, films and publishing have also been very well regarded.

Ryan is also a busy conductor and teacher. For all credits, see: www.ryanyouens.com/credits/.

Ryan Youens’s Specialties:

COMPOSER - concert, film, advertising, weddings and special occasions.

COPYIST - copying, engraving, typesetting, transcription, part preparation, proofreading, reductions, MIDI translation, transposition, score re-creation, on-location session work.

ARRANGER AND ORCHESTRATOR - Of any style for any ensemble or occasion. Additional string, brass or orchestral arrangements. Orchestration from piano, ensemble or MIDI reductions. Orchestral proofreading of "synth" score for "live" orchestra.
