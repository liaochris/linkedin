Evette Pauleen Tan’s Summary

Marketing is about finding a niche, and this is the story of my life. I started out as a Business Administration major at the University of the Philippines before I decided to pursue a master's degree in Marketing Communications at the Illinois Institute of Technology's Stuart School of Business, moving halfway across the world in the process.
