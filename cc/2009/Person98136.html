Dr. James E. Dowd’s Summary

The Vitamin D Cure is the first book to specifically outline how to fortify your vitamin D with sun or supplements safely. It discusses how dietary acid excess makes us more suseptible to symptoms of vitamin D deficiency and how to correct this. It describes the role exercise plays in enhancing vitamin D function and provides a simple exercise program. Go to the website: www.thevitamindcure.com and take the quiz to find out if you are at risk of having vitamin D deficiency.
