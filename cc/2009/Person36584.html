Giuseppe Camarda’s Summary

The Insight Program is a western continental Europe (WCE) wide initiative born for supporting Oracle's license business with strategic consulting and C-level business development services. The insight team advise customers on how to align their business and IT strategies, providing vital examination of an organisations most critical business challenges to identify feasible, credible, affordable solutions that help them achieve their strategic goals. Available exclusively to select customers, the Program ensures that organisations understand the value technology solutions can provide to transform their business and enable to move ahead of the competition.
