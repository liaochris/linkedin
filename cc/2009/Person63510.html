Mark Notess’s Summary

IT consultant specializing in field research, user experience design and evaluation of online learning tools and environments. Emphasis is on adult learning in higher education, corporate, and life-long contexts. Ph.D. in educational technology, masters degrees in both education and computer science.

Mark Notess’s Specialties:

Human-computer interaction, workplace ethnography, contextual design, usability, e-learning, technology support for learning in the humanities/higher education, course management systems, digital libraries, older adults, collaboration tools, portals, and ethics of system design.
