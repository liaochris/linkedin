Mark Metcalfe’s Summary

As a technical publications professional, I have worked in the commercial software and electronic design automation industries on a wide variety of platforms and authoring tools. I have a consistent track record of adapting to new technologies and leading others into best-in-class products and solutions. I consistently exceeded job expectations as indicated by awards and performance reviews.

With many years of technical experience and a degree in computer science, I combine excellent communication skills with technical disciplines, generating product content that has won the highest recognition from the Society for Technical Communications (STC) and other organizations.

My goals are to:

* Develop strategies for an organization to design and deliver technical information in concise and useful ways using state-of-the-art methods.
* Apply customer-focused expertise to influence user interface design and user documentation.
* Sharply focus a writing group on business objectives.
