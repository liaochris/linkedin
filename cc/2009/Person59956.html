    Ublog

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Internet industry)

    September 2003 — July 2004 (11 months)

  • Founder & CEO

    Tekora

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Internet industry)

    September 2000 — September 2002 (2 years 1 month)

    Tekora introduced affordable packaged website solutions for small companies. Tekora developed its own technology, raised 6M € from 4 major VCs and was acquired by Access-Commerce SA, a public European sofware editor.

  • Founder & CEO

    B2L

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Internet industry)

    May 1996 — December 1999 (3 years 8 months)

    B2L was one of the first web agencies in France. I created it with a students loan of 10k € on the HEC campus in Paris. With clients such as Chanel, Peugeot, 20th Century Fox, Mars etc, it quickly became one of the leading web agencies. I sold it to the ad network BBDO at the end of 1999.

  • Founder

    RapidSite France

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Internet industry)

    September 1997 — December 1999 (2 years 4 months)

    RapidSite was the first to introduce in France shared web hosting and quickly achieved a leadership position with 20 to 50€ monthly packages and a large dealership network. I sold it to France Telecom and it became part of its ISP, Wanadoo.
