Tim Anderson’s Summary

My qualifications represent a relevant list of achievements that predict positive contributions. Most notable among my strengths include multiple modes of writing and research, public presentations/public speaking, and a deep understanding of the history, present-day status and trends involved in communication technologies.

My positions have included Visiting Lecturer at Indiana University-Bloomington, Department of Telecommunications, and previously, Assistant Professor, Department of Communication with Denison University, Visiting Assistant Professor with the University of Arizona, Department of Media Arts, and Instructor with Northwestern University. I am now in a long-term position at Old Dominion University in the Department of Communication and Theatre Arts.

My areas of expertise have been comprehensive and include mass media curriculum development and adaptation, writing and publication on mass media development (particularly popular music), and the numerous classroom issues involved in Web 2.0 technologies such as blogging, wikis, podcasting, etc.

An important element of my background and adding true value to my career history, is focused on my formal education, publications and writings, invited columnist contributions, refereed conference papers and invited lectures, scholarships, fellowships, TA assignments, grants and research awards, professional editorial services, and university services.

If you are searching for an innovative professional with a true understanding of the dynamics and importance of a solid communications program, backed by integrity, please contact me to arrange an interview. I am eager to learn more about the challenges facing your organization and to discuss how I can make a difference.
