Laura Smith Dunaief’s Summary

Twenty years' experience in retail and wholesale financial services organizations, with expertise in sales, people, project and training management.

Currently a consultant working with financial services and Fortune 500 companies to customize and deliver employee and management training.

Most recently, a Director with FT Knowledge, a division of Pearson Education. FT Knowledge and its sister division, The New York Institute of Finance, develop and deliver training to financial services professionals.

Formerly was Director of Training and Development for Schwab Capital Markets, a division of Charles Schwab & Co., Inc. In this role, was responsible for directing training strategy development and implementation for the company’s trading organization. Key elements of this strategy included management development, product and service training, and professional skill development.

Prior to managing the training organization, held roles as diverse as Director of Customer Service Initiatives and Manager of Correspondent Execution Services, both for Schwab Capital Markets, Vice President and Branch Manager of a Boston-area retail brokerage office for Charles Schwab.

Laura Smith Dunaief’s Specialties:

Experienced course designer and facilitator. Areas of expertise include: Financial Services product and sales training; Management development; Diversity training; Customer Service training; needs assessments; development pathing.
