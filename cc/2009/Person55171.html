Kevin Packler’s Summary

Campaign and channel optimization to standard metrics including return on ad spend (ROAS), cost of sale (COS), net revenue, and ad spend. Prior B2C experience leveraged into B2B new client acquisition and account management. Spent several years working on a contracted basis, consulting.
