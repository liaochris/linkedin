Victoria Quintero’s Summary

*Assisting first time homeowners with the purchase of their home and providing information on Colorado down payment assistant programs such as CHAFA & CHAC
*Negotiating short sales on behalf of clients that are facing foreclosure, have no equity, or are going through hardships.
*Finding and navigating through the purchase of short sale or bank owned properties for buyers.

For home owners that need mortgage help, Victoria can go over options and is familiar with loan modifications. She recommends her clients seek counsel and try all options available before working out a short sale. She is a passionate advocate on behalf of her clients.
