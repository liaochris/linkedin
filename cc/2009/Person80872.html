Pattie Simone’s Summary

Pattie Simone is nationally acclaimed Speaker, Writer & Business Success Activist with oodles of innovative revenue and personal success drivers. She is seeking more speaking & training opportunities for corporate, academic and entrepreneurial audiences. From motivational women's programs to general audience skill-based tracks on leadership, relationship-based new age marketing and social media, Pattie's upbeat and cutting-edge insight gets winning reviews. She is also pursuing new business and feature writing opps in print and virtual spaces.

Ms. Simone is President of Write-Communications - a marcom strategy, branding, and messaging consultancy and Marketing-Advantage.net, a top-notch viral & graphic design resource. Services include smart Communications Audits & Planning, Digital Asset Advice & Management, Graphic Design and Wordsmithing for print, web and radio.

Ms. Simone is also Founder of WomenCentric.net, a sassy women's speaking bureau seeking to represent women leaders in every sector. If you deliver powerful programing for executives, entrepreneurs, & students we want to talk to you! Clients include Citigroup, KPMG, & professional associations like ANA and NYPPS. For exceptional keynotes, workshops, or panel presenters - contact WomenCentric.net!

Pattie is also an experienced journalist; with 450+ bylines for Entrepreneur Media, The Times Herald-Record, The Hudson Valley Business Journal, & New Jersey Bride magazine.

Pattie has appeared on radio and TV, including ABC TV's America This Morning, FOX 5 TV on Good Day New York, Entrepreneur Magazine radio, and RocklandWorldRadio.com.

Ms. Simone is a member of The Newswomen's Club of New York, The New York Press Club, Women in Communications, BMA, the Mahwah Regional Chamber of Commerce. and the New Jersey Association of Women Business Owners.

Ms. Simone is working on several books, an entrepreneurial lifestyle guide for women, a chick lit fable and a children's series.

Pattie Simone’s Specialties:

Business Success Speaker and Writer, meaningful social media, new age marketing model evangalist, consumer conversation drivers, messaging and overall business development strategy & implementation. Additional in-the-trenches expertise in cold calling, branding, networking, public speaking and image management, PR and feature writing.

Published journalist, reporting and commenting on business trends, marketing & sales tips, company profiles/editorials, lifestyle and bridal topics.
