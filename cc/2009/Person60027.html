Lonny Gulden’s Summary

After a career in technology sales and marketing I became a technology recruiter in 2006. More recently, I have launched my own recruiting and consulting firm here in the Twin Cities, ChiefConnectionsOfficer.com. I recruit nationally in the areas of IT and all things Internet, technology sales and marketing (hardware, software and services), interactive/new media, etc. In addition to recruiting, I teach workshops on the use of LinkedIn for business development, job search and employee recruiting.

On a more personal note, I have been active in assisting individuals through career transition for over 18 years. I currently facilitate the Wooddale Church Job Transition Support Group every Monday morning at 7:15AM. All are welcome to attend.

My new career allows me to align my professional activities with my passion for helping others.

Lonny Gulden’s Specialties:

Technology talent acquisition, social networking, LinkedIn training, advanced market knowledge.
