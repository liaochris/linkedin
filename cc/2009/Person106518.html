Zen Chu’s Summary

FOCUS AREAS:
- Medical devices with fast approvals
- Software & connected healthcare
- Solutions with high patient engagement & emotion

I help to accelerate the development of high impact clinical inventions, by bridging science & the venture business, enabling the confluence of good people and worthy ideas. I am devoted to medical innovation and I work at the earliest stages to envision, create, patent, de-risk, develop and sell medical devices, healthcare information technology, and consumer medical products.

Helping physicians and scientists translate their inventions into real-world therapies is very satisfying, especially in the rare moments when the complexities of healthcare can be distilled into a simple solution. Along the way, I have been very lucky to learn alongside world-renowned scientists and some of the best venture capital investors while helping accelerate important discoveries.

Zen Chu’s Specialties:

I wear many hats within early stage ventures: invention, intellectual property mapping, clinical markets, regulatory strategy, technical assessment, physician focus groups, fundraising etc. Experience in medical devices, surgical & robotic tools, biomaterials, healthcare software & data, clinical nutrition, controlled drug delivery & regenerative therapies.
