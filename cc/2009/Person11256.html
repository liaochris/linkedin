Bill is an Authorized Duct Tape Marketing Coach and the owner of Rebar, a marketing coaching and consulting firm. At Rebar, we help professional service companies "get found" by profitable customers who value the services they provide.

Typically, before they worked with me, my clients told me:

♦ They were frustrated with wasting money on marketing that doesn’t work or they don’t know if it’s working or not.

♦ They were tired of the feast or famine nature of their business even though they receive referrals.

♦ They were disappointed because they spent a lot of money on their web site and it’s not generating enough business for them.

♦ They are having difficulty planning or budgeting because sales are too inconsistent from month to month

What We Offer...

We install marketing systems. Are you a victim of the “marketing idea of the week” syndrome? We show you how to treat marketing as a business system so you can spend less time chasing business and more time working with profitable customers.

Who We Work With...

Professional service firms and other people who sell services. They may also sell products, but the chief benefit they provide comes from their service and specialized knowledge. The Revenues these people enjoy are directly related to the Relationships they create. Our typical clients include:

- Accountants, Lawyers, Architects, Doctors
- Real estate agents, insurance agents, financial planners
- Hi Tech consulting firms, training companies, coaches
- Soloprenuers
