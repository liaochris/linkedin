Ricardo Rojas, PHR’s Summary

I am a Bi-Lingual human resource management professional with over 19 years of domestic and international experience with a proven track record in leading, evaluating, and redefining business processes that strategically impact corporate operations. I specialize in Organizational Development, Human Capital Management and Recruiting.I am always looking to network and would be happy to help anyone if I am able to. I have solid written/verbal communication and executive level presentation skills that would nicely contribute to an executive leadership team. Some of my fortes and past contributions are:

- Developed, re-organized and re-aligned corporate HR departments and provided strategic long-term contributions that directly relate to company missions and objectives.
- Transformed leadership’s view of HR from a cost center to a profit center while strengthening internal operations and expansion goals through system selection and process automation for numerous clients.
- Successfully partnered with management to develop proprietary cost effective programs that allow organizations to maximize their HRM employee initiatives by providing hands on administration of policies, employee relations, training and development, performance management, coaching, compensation, benefits, and policy interpretation.
- Successfully leads, partners and advises and oversees leave programs for clients, including Disability, Family and Medical Leave, and Workers’ Compensation and policy development to impact Organizational Management.
- Designed and implemented customized training programs to help increase employee an organizational development.
- Facilitated relationships with external parties and streamlined implementation of cutting edge marketing, e sourcing, and advertising strategies to build employer-branding Initiatives.

I am always open to networking so please don't be shy and send me a request to connect...
