Daniel Schiavone’s Summary

During 15 years working with technology Daniel Schiavone has focused on creating applications that save people time and tedium. Snake Hill: Practical Technology Solutions, is Schiavone's third start-up. Snake Hill provides hosted solutions for SMBs and custom software to automate business processes. Previously, Schiavone helped start an IT services company that grew to over $2 million in revenue in four years and an arts non profit that operates out of a renovated movie theater with an annual budget of over $1 million. He is involved in revitalization efforts in his SouthEast Baltimore neighborhood, creating highly visible events and leading efforts to capitalize on Highlandtown’s Arts and Entertainment District designation.
