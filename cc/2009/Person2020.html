Shirley W. Mitchell’s Summary

Raised in Northern Alabama on a tenant cotton farm working with all her family members in the tenant cotton fields, she had a "Vision and Passion" to make her life and others' lives more productive, seeing what hard, long work days and getting older did to those around her. She was writing about, and promoting, a "Positive Aging Lifestyle" before most were even thinking about it.
Known today as "The Golden Egg of Aging™" and the Lady with "The Most Stunning Smile", she is highly recognized as one of the Top Writers and Authors on Aging, Seniors-Senior Lifestyle, the Baby Boomer Generation, and Women-Women's Issues of today. We call her "The Original Baby Boomer Promoter" because of her tremendous insight years previous to the coming of the Baby Boomer Wave of Aging Younger.
She is the Author of 5 Books: "The Christian Writer's Desk Diary", "The Beauty of Being God's Woman", "Spiritual Sparks for Busy Women", "Fabulous after 50®" and "Sensational after 60®", and the Co-Author of 3 books - "Love Notes for Mom", "Love Notes for Dad" and "101 Great Ways To Improve Your Life".
She is the Celebrity Radio Talk Show Host of the Syndicated Radio Show "Aging Outside the Box™" on the ArtistFirst World Radio Network - "Aging Outside the Box™" is a weekly One-Hour LIVE Broadcast Radio Show heard every Wednesday night from 2PM-AHST, 3PM-AKST, 4PM-PST, 5PM-MST, 6PM-CST, 7PM-EST AND 8PM-AST, as well as World Wide, and the Celebrity Radio Talk Show Host of "Aging Outside the Box™"-"Christian Spiritual Sparks™" with WBXR-AM1140 Greater Huntsville, AL area airing at 7:30-7:45am-CST and KXKS-AM1190 in Albuquerque, NM airing at 6:45-7am-MST. The Stations are owned and operated by Wilkins Communications Network, Inc.
