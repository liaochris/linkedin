Kelly Dingee’s Summary

Kelly Dingee is a Sourcing Researcher and Technical Writer with AIRS-A Company of The RightThing. In addition to her research and course development duties, Kelly also trains all AIRS classes. She is the editor of the AIRS Sourcing Report, which provides free sourcing tips and information on a monthly basis. Kelly also is a guest blogger at the talent management blog, Fistful of Talent. She has 15 years of HR experience, with the last 10 specifically focused in sourcing and recruitment. She has had the opportunity to work for companies in the Defense, Telecom and Healthcare industries. Originally from Ann Arbor, Michigan, she received her BBA in Human Resource Management from Western Connecticut State University.

AIRS ACIR since July 2007, AIRS CIR since 2000, Sourcing, Research, General HR, Recruiting (in the Telecommunications & Defense Industries)
