Rebecca McClain’s Summary

As an entrepreneur, author, speaker and coach she is recognized as an expert in personal and professional success. Before launching out on her own, Rebecca worked for fifteen years in the telecommunications industry. She resigned from her position overseeing Tax Research & Planning for the western region of the U.S.

She is committed to assisting individuals in finding their passion and purpose, and inspiring them to reach their potential. Rebecca is the author of three books including her newest release, Redesign Your Reality. She writes life-changing articles on topics ranging from money matters, leadership to self-empowerment. Her weekly E-newsletter, Inspiration for Practical Living, delivers ongoing tips and inspiration to help transform the lives of her subscribers. She also publishes the monthly Ezine, Entrepreneur Digest, which provides timely resources and articles for enterprising entrepreneurs. Rebecca regularly presents seminars and workshops for career professionals, business owners, ministry groups and students. Some of her seminar presentations have focused on "Intentional Living", "Redesigning Your Reality" and "Achieving Work/Life Balance."

Rebecca is also passionate about helping women to flourish in business. She hosts a Self Employed Womens' Teleforum on the first Tuesday of each month to support and inspire female business owners (and those who aspire to be). The call provides a safe place for enterprising women to talk about their challenges, successes and brainstorm solutions, in growing and developing their businesses.

As a former business consultant, she advised and prepared financial statements for individuals and small businesses, and provided accounting solutions and tax planning. Rebecca also formed a number of non-profit organizations for ministry-affiliated groups.
