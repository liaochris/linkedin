Hazel M Walker’s Summary

My experience:

I have a strong background in the Insurance Industry so spend a great deal of time working with insurance professionals on increasing their business by referral. I work beyond the common, get referrals from your clients, to implementing the Science of Referrals from trained Referral Partners.

Networking is an Art, everybody has a different view, and it is subjective. What may be art to me may be junk to you, there is no right or wrong.

Referrals are a science, measurable, predictable, and tested. I teach the Science of Referrals.

Ask yourself these questions: What is NOT happening that SHOULD be happening in regards to your networking activities? What is it costing you? Do you know?
