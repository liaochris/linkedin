Murray Calder’s Summary

10 years in the whiksy industry, latterly as Global Marketing Manager for Scotland's best selling whisky, The Famous Grouse, has given me a solid grasp of strategic brand management in a global context.

Over that time, and subsequently at Baxters and with Glenmorangie, I have created a strong track record in developing winning brand marketing, communications and media strategies.

Having now jumped the fence to agency land I have added the ability to identify and manage organisational change projects to my skillset.

I am now responsible within MediaCom Edinburgh for New Business, Strategy, Research and Marketing Analytics and am loving the opportunity to work with some of the country's best brands to help identify growth strategies.
