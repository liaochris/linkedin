Angi Holmes’s Summary

I recently decided to pursue my passion of graphic arts and opened up my own shop called Vivid Valentines and couldn't be happier. Vivid Valentines is a web-based company that creates unique photo announcements and invitations for all occasions. I have found it very fulfilling to create such beautiful works of art that can be shared with loved ones today and for generations to come. To find out more and see my designs, go to www.VividValentines.com
