Richard J. Krasney, CFP®’s Summary

The Philanthropic Advisor- "I help successful "Do-Gooders" figure out answers to complex wealth issues."

You might be a "Do-Gooder" if you serve on a nonprofit board, donate and are passionate about causes, have a family foundation or have thought about creating one, or know the meaning of triple bottom line. If you have a passion for helping others, you are a Do-Gooder. If you are a Do-Gooder and would like to explore what I do further, let's connect.

Financially speaking, I specialize in the process of understanding values that drive people and turn them into financial strategies. I coordinate and oversee the entire wealth management process, working with existing professionals on a client's team and bringing in outside experts when needed.
Major financial transitions including business sales, legacy planning, retirement, succession plans, charitable giving, education funding, and other major life event transition planning are our specialty.
