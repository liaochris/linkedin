Rich Knitter’s Summary

Starting in July 2006 I started a company with my business partner Chris Davis. We had a successful run for about two years.

My company slowed pace after that. It was initially successful due to the contacts I had made through my original clients. Unfortunately it could not sustain itself on the original network of contacts I had built. My least favorite part of running my own business was sales, so once my contacts began to run dry I decided it was time to transition my career.

My partner started working at AirTight Design as a programmer and within a few months he informed me they needed a project manager. Since my interest was waning in growing my own business (but I still desired to stay in the web industry) I saw this as a perfect opportunity and now I am a project manager at AirTight.

Before July 2006, I worked in the nursery industry. I was hired as a "tech guy" due to my computer knowledge. I was given the opportunity to watch a start up business grow from the early stages and helped grow the business myself by implementing an online outlet for the business's plants.

My passion grew from web site design to Internet marketing in that period of time. Most of my computer skills are self taught. I also have a formal education in business.

I thrive in creative environments and am self managed. My passion is working to help small businesses grow.
