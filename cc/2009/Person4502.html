Antony McGregor Dey’s Summary

Antony is a serial entrepreneur and globally recognised expert on mobile technologies with experience from concept to execution and a proven ability in successful business development.

During his 10 year association with the telecommunications sector Antony has always been at the cutting edge of early adapter mobile based technologies. From Dial-up to UMTS, SMS to web browsing, QR Codes to Image Recognition, on-device apps to mobile web applications. Antony knows what works with an in-depth knowledge of mobile multimedia deliverables and the platforms and technologies that drive them.

  • CEO

    QMCODES

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

    February 2007 — Present (2 years 6 months)

    QMCODES was the first company to introduce QR codes and 2D mobile barcode solutions to Australia and is a global pioneer in mobile interactive print solutions combining emerging cell phone technologies to blur the lines between print and digital media.

    With QMCODES proprietary Interactive Print platform, print publishers can link readers out to richer digital content on the mobile Internet directly from the article in a magazine or newspaper.

    The platform's ability to track individual user behavior across multiple articles and publications coupled with a highly targeted mobile ad-network offers print publishers new revenue channels, in a first for true media convergence.

    QMCODES will revolutionise the worlds print media, changing consumer trends and empowering brands with a new and extremely personal way of connecting with their target audience in the offline media channels.
