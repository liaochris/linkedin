Ray Wallin, P.E., LEED AP’s Summary

Senior Project Estimator with experience in conceptual budgeting through 100% construction documents; handling long term preconstruction efforts as well as lump sum bids; estimating areas of expertise in Divisions 1 - 14, with knowledge of Mechanical and Electrical systems.

Have worked on projects valued up to $1 Billion, and have led estimating efforts on projects up to $400 Million.

Licensed as a Professional Engineer in New Jersey and a LEED Accredited Professional.

Ray Wallin, P.E., LEED AP’s Specialties:

Projects that I have worked on have been diverse in nature (both Public and Private), including R&D, Hospitals, Healthcare, Pharmaceutical, Industrial, Office, Mixed Use, Residential, Academic, and Commercial.
