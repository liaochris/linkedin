John Bartlett, CCIM, CPM, RPA’s Experience

  • Property Manager

    Paragon Property Management

    (Commercial Real Estate industry)

    October 2006 — Present (3 years 3 months)

    As Property Manager, John Bartlett’s responsibilities are to manage and coordinate the daily operations of all Paragon properties and to serve as the primary contact for all of our tenants. Prior to joining Paragon Property Management, LLC in 2006, John had over 20 years of commercial real estate and property management experience, including the management of shopping centers, office complexes, and other commercial properties across several states. John attended Ball State University in Muncie, Indiana, majoring in business, real estate law and accounting. He has been awarded the prestigious Certified Property Manager® (CPM) designation, and the Real Property Administrator (RPA) designation. He is a Certified Commercial Investment Member (CCIM) and also holds membership in the Business Owners and Managers Association (BOMA) and the Institute of Real Estate Management (IREM). In 1994, he was elected Commercial REALTOR® of the Year by the Indiana Commercial Board of REALTORS®.
