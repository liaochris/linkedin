Penelope Lombard’s Summary

I'm a stand up comedian and writer. I create original shows, do punch up writing for episodic television, and perform as a comedic actress. I'm looking for acting and writing work.

My comedy has been featured on Comedy Central's Premium Blend, Make Me Laugh, NBC's Friday Night Videos and the FX pilot Filthy Liars. My comedy career has included appearances at the Montreal Comedy Festival, The Toyota Comedy Festival, and clubs and colleges around the country.

I've done punch up for networks and individuals including Disney and NBC and had an original short film produced. My most current show, Licking My Chops, was recently seen at the Comedy Central Stage at the Hudson Theatre in Hollywood. In 2007 I completed my Master's in Spiritual Psychology, so now I sometimes write jokes about angels, E.S.P. and I can hear the thoughts of flowers.

Please come and visit my blog at www.penelopelombard.com.
