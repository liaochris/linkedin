John Meulemans’s Summary

I have a very broad background in online marketing. In the 90's I started in ecommerce and online media, in the last years I specialized in social media and performance based advertising .

I help brands with their online presence, getting their customers engaged with social media and delivering results with search- and affiliate marketing.

One of my talents is to translate online trends to real business concepts and with this giving my clients a competitive edge.

Some of my clients: Reed Business, TUI, The Red Cross, Discovery Networks, Skyradio, AdLINK, O'Neill, Tommy Hilfiger, EMC2 and totaljobs.
