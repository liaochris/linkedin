Robert Beverly (Rab)’s Summary

I am a freelance commercial writer and consultant. I have been writing to sell for over 10 years.

I specialize in writing for financial services industries and business-to-business sales. I write sales letters, brochures, ads, scripts, web pages, feature articles and more. Let me know what you have in mind.

I am happy to work with agencies to write copy on an overflow or ongoing basis.

I can often recommend people in different creative fields. These include graphic design, photography and web design.

I am an educator at heart, and that comes out in my business. I work with select clients to design training programs that:
1. Engage new employees and get them up and running quickly.
2. Distribute important knowledge and experience throughout the corporation.

Please call me to discuss how we can work together.
