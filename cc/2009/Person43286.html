Jamie Steigerwald’s Summary

Jamie is a small business entrepreneur with over 20 years of experience. He has a passion for technology and finance – a winning combination for creating successful organizations. Jamie started his first business at age 12 using his Apple II computer printing banners for local family, friends and neighbors. Since then Jamie has been involved as a principal in diverse companies from a cyber coffee shop (where we roasted our own beans) to an independent cell phone store owner. More recently he has been involved in companies focusing on web application development, mortgage, credit repair (we prefer calling it FICO improvement), Web 2.0 social media, online video content creation and distribution, and IT services.

Jamie has a talent for taking complex ideas and presenting them in easy to understand strategies focused on bottom line results. His mantra “Think Big - Start Small - Move Fast” sums up his methodology for success in such diverse areas. Jamie is just as comfortable presenting a business plan to an investor as he is rolling up his sleeves and getting his hand dirty, weather it is fixing a failed computer or creating financial projections for a new business opportunity.

In a corporate setting Jamie is a successful project manager and team leader. He has planned, managed, and completed numerous large scale projects including corporate moves, web application developments, creating and managing help desks, and corporate web sites.
