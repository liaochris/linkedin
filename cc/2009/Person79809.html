Pim Rakers’s Summary

I recently started my own company to make it easier for clients to hire me as a freelance graphic designer. I like to design things in a new way and make a more out of the box design that stands out and gives you a different perspective on the subject.
Working with my hands is something that comes along easily. Drawing, silkscreen printing, etching, analogue photography. As long as it gives me dirty hands I like it.
