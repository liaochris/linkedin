Paul Elosegui’s Summary

Serial entrepreneur in online services, together with my partners, since 1994. Rode the Web 1.0 wave at the crest; voted "Most Influential Internet Personality of the Year" by Expansion (Financial Times equivalent in Spain). I have gone through the entrepreneurial life cycle, from seed to IPO, and back from public to private ownership. Currently active in online related ventures across Europe.
