Kerin Cantwell’s Summary

Kerin Cantwell, broker associate in Coldwell Banker's San Marino office, puts her energy, enthusiasm and knowledge of the real estate market to work for her buyer, seller and investor clients in the San Gabriel Valley.

Formerly a partner of Chadbourne & Parke, LLP, an international law firm, Kerin brings unparalleled negotiating and contract management skills to the table to help her clients achieve the best possible results. Her analytical ability, attention to detail, and experience managing complex transactions give her clients confidence that their most important purchases and sales are being handled by a diligent, successful professional.

Kerin grew up in Pasadena and graduated from Polytechnic School, Wellesley College (with a degree in Chinese) and Harvard Law School. Kerin lives in Pasadena and is active in numerous community and charitable organizations. Having lived and worked in Hong Kong for many years and fluent in Mandarin Chinese, she brings an ability to connect with people from diverse cultural backgrounds to her real estate practice. Kerin takes the time to get to know her clients and their needs, and uses her in-depth knowledge of the market to find the right property for a buyer or create the best marketing strategy for a seller.

Serving the communities of Pasadena, San Marino, South Pasadena, Sierra Madre, La Canada, San Gabriel and Arcadia, Kerin Cantwell is a trustworthy and dedicated agent for her clients.
