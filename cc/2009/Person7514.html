Ard Crebas’s Summary

In my career with the Netherlands Foreign Investment Agency, The Development Agency East Netherlands and the Food Valley Organisation I have been projectmanager for a large number of FDI projects. I convinced companies to locate in the Netherlands and assisted them in many ways during and after the decision-making process. I assisted companies such as Firestone Industrial Products (European Headquarters), TREK Bicycles (European Distribution Center), Kikkoman Corporation (European R&D Center), Nippon Suisan (European R&D Center) and TAXI (European Office), which all decided to locate in the Netherlands.

During two temporary assignments for the Netherlands Foreign Investment Agency in New York and London I advised several companies about investing in the Netherlands. During both assignments I specifically focused on the creative industry sector and the possibilities in the Netherlands, especially in Amsterdam.

Summarized I have extensive experience in the following fields:

* Marketing
* Business Development
* Real Estate
* Assisting in building business cases for FDI (Foreign Direct Investment)
* R&D matchmaking
* Venture Capital search

Ard Crebas’s Specialties:

Marketing, Branding and Business Development, Building FDI business cases, R&D matchmaking
