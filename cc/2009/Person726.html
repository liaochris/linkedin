Abelardo Velasco’s Summary

I am a structural engineer with a large expertise in design and construction of a variety of structural systems, from bridges to commercial and residential buildings. Covering industrial plants, manufacturing industry, refineries, fuel and gas stations worldwide, airports, petroleum industry, food industry, airport facilities layouts, among the most important projects managed.
With time I becomed a manager in a family owned contractor company, which was involved in the construction of all kind of structures, involved in the design, planning, construction and start up of plants, buildings, roads, public and private industry. At the present time I am involved in the development of real estate business, developing my own residential projects in Peru, and as a consultant for third party real estate investors, keeping up to date my expertise in structural design.

Abelardo Velasco’s Specialties:

Real Estate, Structural engineering, Manufacturing and Import - Export of equipment and wood furniture, residential buildings, engineering consulting. Besides the construction industry, we are deeeply involved in developing a market for our very unique peruvian furniture designs, which we are in the final steps to introduce into the NorthAmerican Market, we invite you to connect us if you are interested in this business.
