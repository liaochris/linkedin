Trond Wilhelmsen’s Summary

I am a creative with 16 years experience in interactive media and print. My digital experience started 12 years ago at Dorling Kindersley Multimedia where I was introduced to user experience and interaction. I am a great believer in teamwork and welcome input from all disciplines and all levels of experience. My background – from designing corporate identities, book publishing, multimedia and the web – gives me a broad palette to work from.


Major achievements in the last three years:
• Worked on a pitch with MetaDesign AG in Berlin which to me was a self-indulging experience in design
• Helped grow the client list at Agency.com from two to sixteen, and contributed to build a first-class reputation in the industry. In particular I enjoyed a close and successful relationship with the clients of Dulux, BT and a major new luxury hotels brand.
• I line manage designers and Art Directors – I encourage the development of concepting skills, design principles and best practice. At Agency.com I mentored the team and saw each member being promoted to more senior positions.
• Implemented processes to ease the sometimes, mundane tasks involved with the production of projects.
• Well versed in accessibility issues, information architecture, copy and strategy.
• Designed some lovely digital experiences.


I find inspiration in everything; literature, travel, the arts, popular culture and urban living. I am inspired by the Modernist movement and enjoy their principles in product design and architecture.

I never go anywhere without a camera and I maintain a keen interest in both photography and typography – my two great creative passions.

Trond Wilhelmsen’s Specialties:

Design and interactivity, art direction, design direction, concept skills, typography, photography, experience architecture, copy writing, line management, accessibility and usability issues, project management and mentoring.
