Eduard Prats Molner’s Summary

I am an engineer focused on interactive applications development and information design. I started to work as a freelance in 1999 and I have a personal site called jocabola (www.jocabola.com). I am a developer with a feel for motion and graphic design, and I like to pay attention to details.

By the end of 2006, I founded Mocoro (www.mocoro.com) together with Hugo Ahlberg (www.melanchology.com), an excellent interface designer made by the Hyper Island school (crew 9).

We specialize in interface design and development, and we have been responsible of some web projects that have been recognized and awarded such as:

http://www.aaaid.net
http://www.filippasmedhagensund.com
