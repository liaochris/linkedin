Gary Wood’s Summary

Psychologist, Coach, Consultant (Research; PR), Author & Broadcaster.

Specializes in bringing psychological insights to all aspects of human social life.

Broadcasting: Appears on national and local radio and in Europe and Asia. Psychological insight on a range of news stories and features.
