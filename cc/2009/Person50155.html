Jon Borlin’s Summary

I understand the Internet and the fundamental technologies and techniques used there in. Using strategic marketing and marketing communication, I work to ensure the overall brand integrity, dynamic functionality, image coherence, search engine capability and absolute accuracy of online corporate marketing materials. I am results-oriented, technical, analytical and self-motivated; I excel in application experience of numerous design and development programs and have strong communication and project management skills.

With over fifteen-years of off/on-line marketing experience, I previously worked in SAFC Marketing Communications, as Technical Marketing Specialist, and was promoted to Electronic Marketing Product Manager in December, 2007. During my SAFC tenure, I managed and maintained all aspects of safcglobal.com, including a complete redesign and relaunch of a new 600-page company site. My experience in search-engine-optimization, usability, look-and-feel and best practices have resulted in substantial visibility increases and an enhanced presence for SAFC's website within the fine chemicals industry.
