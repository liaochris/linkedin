Steven Winokur’s Summary

Dynamic management consultant with entrepreneurial spirit focused on driving top-line growth. Experienced Brand Strategist focused on helping companies build their business by articulating their value proposition.

Developed proprietary analysis tool, The Growth Gap, designed to drive top-line growth by focusing on attracting more qualified prospects, converting more to customers, retaining them for longer periods, and selling them more products at premium prices.

Clients include LASON, SelectiveHR, Witness Systems,GE Power Systems, Pfizer, Merck and Macy's, Inc.

Steven Winokur’s Specialties:

Strategic consulting, focusing on positioning, messaging and differentiation. “Operationalizing” the brand internally by developing new processes and procedures to ensure brand promise is being met.
