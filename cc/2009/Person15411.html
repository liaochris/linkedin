Carl Madsen’s Summary

20 years of leadership experience with the US Army, Army Reserve, Iowa National Guard and Minnesota National Guard as an enlisted Supply Specialist, Airborne soldier, Cadet, Lieutenant, Captain, Company Commander, Staff Officer, and Major, trained as a Combat Engineer. Mobilized twice in the defense of our nation, never placed in a combat zone. Received a Meritorious Service Medal before retiring in 2005.

Project management experience goes back to 1989 leading a construction crew at Camp Dodge, Iowa, then again as a Landscape Architect and eventually as an Electrician.

My goal is to find challenging work where I can make a difference.

Carl Madsen’s Specialties:

My MBA helps me understand the complex nature of today's business environment. I have experience teaching and mentoring others, both as a leader and as a peer. I have a strong background in the Union and serve proudly on the Joint Apprenticeship Training Committee.
