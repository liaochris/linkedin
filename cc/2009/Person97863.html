Laurie Hayes’s Summary

Laurie is a certified business coach helping corporate and government employees cross the bridge from employment to home-based entrepreneurship.

She's the author of numerous online and offline business and self-help articles and co-author of the self-help book, "101 Great Ways To Improve Your Life" with 100 other expert authors including Mark Victor Hanson, Byron Katie, and Les Brown.

In 2006, Laurie released "The Complete 12-Step Guide to Starting a Home-Based Business: How to Transition From Employee to Entrepreneur Without Losing Your Mind or Your Shirt," a home-study program for aspiring business owners.

She's also the creator of The HBB Survival Guide - an owners manual, training guide, succession and dissolution plan template for home-based business owners.

As a Senior Manager with SendOutCards, Laurie teaches business professionals how to build stronger client and customer relationships through personal touch and appreciation.

From car dealerships to doctors, hardware stores and real estate agents to work-at-home mom's, Laurie teaches the importance of showing personal appreciation and acknowledgement to increase customer loyality and referrals.
