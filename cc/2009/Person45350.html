Jeffery Hicks’s Summary

Jeffery Hicks (MCSE,MCSA,MCT) is an independent author, trainer, consultant and a Microsoft MVP Award recipient for PowerShell. He is the co-author of Advanced VBScript for Microsoft Windows Administrators (Microsoft Press 2006) and Windows PowerShell:TFM (SAPIEN Press 2007), now in its 2nd edition. He is also the author of WSH and VBScript Core:TFM (SAPIEN Press 2007) as well as several training videos on administrative scripting. His latest book is Managing Active Directory with Windows PowerShell: TFM (SAPIEN Press 2008) He is currently a columnist and contributing editor for REDMOND Magazine and MCPMag.com. Jeff is a frequent contributor to several online IT community web sites as well as an invited speaker at computer conferences and seminars. Jeff has been an IT professional for 18 years, much of it spent as an professional consultant. He has helped a wide range of clients from small school districts to global enterprises. Areas of expertise include Active Directory, Windows Server 2000-2008, Microsoft Exchange, Microsoft Sharepoint, Microsoft ISA Server and more. Throughout his entire career, Jeff has leveraged the available tools and techniques for automating Windows administration. His experience with a wide range of organizations and technologies provides a wealth of knowledge he is eager to share through teaching, writing, community participation, mentoring and conferences.

Jeffery Hicks’s Specialties:

Windows Scripting Automation including VBScript, ADSI, Powershell, WMI and command line scripting. Windows management and infrastructure products such as Exchange, Active Directory, ISA, MOM, and SMS.
