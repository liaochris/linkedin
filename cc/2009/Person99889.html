THOMAS GRASTY’s Summary

Recently named an "Innovator to Watch" by the USC Stevens Institute for Innovation, Tom Grasty is an entrepreneurial digital and media strategist with a diverse professional background across the entertainment, advertising, public relations and Internet industries. His 10-plus years experience includes creative development, production oversight, business development, and new product launch.

With a strong background in strategic partnership affiliation and content monetization across traditional and new media platforms, Tom has overseen the development of over 100 hours of film, television, documentary and digital properties.
