Andrew Burrows’s Summary

Are you the owner or CEO of a business turning over between £1m and £25m?

Are the business finances giving you pain?

Do you wish you had an expert in business and financial management working alongside you, giving you confidence you were heading the right way to maximise profits and cash, but you don't want to employ an expensive full-time Finance Director?

Charis FD provides affordable expert finance and management assistance to businesses like yours, working with you flexibly on an ongoing basis to help you to make more money, generate most cash and gain confidence in the business finances. See our website www.charisfd.com.
