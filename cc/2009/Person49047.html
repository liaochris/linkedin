John David Olsen’s Summary

Originally from the Bay Area of Northern California, I've called Southern California home for the last ten years. I've spent the better part of my career in the technology and consulting business, working for some start-ups and non-profits, as well as the global Fortune 500 companies Microsoft and The Walt Disney Company. During this time I've had the opportunity to travel widely, including being based for a year and a half in Paris, France and attaining legal residency in Australia. Over the years, I've gained an expertise in software and systems and had the privilege of working with award-winning teams in the process. One of the latest ventures is a web application where science coincides with spirituality on the internet - a fascinating mix of intention and quantum physics. Its intended to be the next evolutionary step is social networking.

I persist in having a long-lasting, inquisitive relationship with technology, with a particular fascination for its effects on humanity. Having lived abroad gave me an ongoing passion for the study of different cultures and I continue to be an ardent student of history, leadership and the human endeavor.
