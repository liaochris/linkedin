Sonal Panse’s Summary

Creative person with expertise and experience in Art, Children's Book Illustration and other Illustration genres, Print Design, Web Interface Design and Writing.

Professional writing experience includes 1000 plus articles on a wide variety of topics. Topics covered are Health, Beauty, Fitness, Style, Culture, Home Improvement, Fashion, Food, Children and Family, History, Art, Art Market and Collecting, Sports, Pets, Travel, Business, Finance, Real Estate, Technology, Science, Education, Career and Gardening.

Professional design and illustration experience includes clean, user-friendly web designs, children's picture books and various illustration assignments.

Clients include Worthpoint Corporation, Buzzle, eHow, Bright Hub, wiseGeek, Tribal Fusion, Traffic Monsters, JAM Magazine, Katha Publishers, Scholastic India, Kappa Publishing Group and Polymer Enterprise.

Owner, since 2007, of Maysun In C, an Art Studio specializing in Art, Illustration, Design and Writing Services.
