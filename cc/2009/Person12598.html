Boyd Carter, PMP’s Summary

Goal: To provide Elegant Solutions to Small-Medium Businesses (SMBs).

A brief history:

The early part of my career was spent as a banker, mostly doing Business Process Reengineering and Data Warehousing. This led to an early retirement in 1995 to pursue Data Warehousing full-time.

The next part of my career was as a consultant working in the field of Data Warehousing / Business Intelligence, incorporated as Data Warehouse Builders Ltd.

The third part of my career started in 2005 when I was assigned as the IT rep to a Sarbanes-Oxley team. Using Data Warehousing concepts, I provided information and knowledge about regulatory compliance to legislation such as The Sarbanes-Oxley Act of 2002 (SOX) and Canada's Bill 198 / MI 52-109. I didn't find this work very satisfying (no one wants to be told "bad news"), went back into "my 9-5 job" as a Data Warehousing consultant and started shaping my career in such a way as to exit the nine to five world. This took place "doing business as" ElegantSolutions.ca.

When the opportunity came up to help SMBs better manage their IT Business Platform, I quickly jumped into the current part of my career as a Business Consultant and Reviewer of tools that make life easier for SMB owners.

Boyd Carter, PMP’s Specialties:

As a Business Consultant, the skills developed earlier provide an insight into the challenges of Small-Medium Business in the area of: Governance, Compliance, Internal Audit, Banking, Consulting, Data Warehousing, Business Intelligence, Sarbanes-Oxley Compliance Expertise, Bill 198 Compliance Expertise, SOX, MI 52-109, Compliance Software Designer.
