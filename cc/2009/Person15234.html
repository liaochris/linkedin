Kevin Wilson’s Summary

Capitol CREAG is a national, full-service commercial real estate services consulting firm headquartered in Washington, DC. We provide government and corporate clients with the comprehensive range of services needed to successfully acquire, develop, manage and dispose of real estate assets of all sizes and types through our three divisions: Advisory Services, Real Estate, and Technical and Professional Services.

We handle every aspect of the real estate process:

* Strategic Development
* Feasibility Analysis
* Project Programming
* Scheduling
* Site Selection
* Brokerage Services: Leasing/Acquisition/Disposition
* Transaction Management
* Budgeting
* Design Review
* Construction Oversight
* Portfolio and Asset Management
* On-site Staffing to Support Large Capital Programs

We are an SBA 8(a) certified firm (SBA Certified 8(a) Firm #106141 Multiple NAICS Codes) with the extensive experience and expertise needed to deliver solutions in less time for less money to all our clients. While headquartered in Washington, DC, we have national reach and handle multi-facility projects through our offices and affiliate locations across the country.

Founded in 1999, our roots are in the General Services Administration. Our owners are former GSA consultants who have completed over 230 leasing transactions totaling over 14 million square feet of real property.

Recognized as top broker by leading analysts, we provide clients with a single source solution to their toughest real estate challenges.
