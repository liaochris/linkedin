Russ Savage’s Summary

I'm an Enterprise Architect/Strategic Planner engaged in probing for the solid business case when pursuing same or better service offering for same or less cost. The business case will be specific for the particular business services provided. Each business situation will require tracking specific technology trends that build on what the client has in use.

And currently noodling on the communications media divide between boomers and digital age adults.
