Andrea Mercado’s Summary

I initially shifted to library science because I'm an information junky, and I needed a way to constantly feed my fix and get paid at the same time. As a natural networker and technologist, it only makes sense that I'd fall in love with social networking, media, and technology, so I've decided to switch gears away from the library world and back to my first career in technology.

Almost in spite of my undergraduate degree, I worked in technology for 6 years before returning to school to obtain a Masters in Library Science. I've coded HTML for clients such as Sprint, Ethan Allen, American Express, and the Wild Lily Tea Room. I'm constantly playing with new technologies, a persistent tinkerer, and avid problem-solver.

For more information about me, my insights in librarianship and technology, please visit my blog at http://www.LibraryTechtonics.info.
