Rupak Rathore’s Summary

Rupak Rathore is a Principal Consultant with HCL Technologies and looks after IT Strategy and Governance Consulting under Information Technology Transformation Services of HCL. He is an expert on IT Strategy Definition and Enterprise Architecture Assessments and is responsible for all IT Strategy and Governance engagements.

He carries with him over a decade of experience in IT industry in both consultative and delivery roles and has been involved with various Fortune 500 and World 500 clients. The commercial clients he has worked with include Mercedes Benz, NBC Universal (NBC, CNBC, Universal Studios), Fidelity Investments, KLM Airlines, ING Bank, NEC Corporation, Lucent Technologies, Liberty Mutual, Tower Insurance Group, NeuStar Inc., CIMB, and many more that can't be disclosed. He has also worked with various federal and state departments/agencies in India and US.
