Don Lenehan’s Summary

• Career foundation of 14 years of client service in blue chip NYC advertising agencies working with Proctor & Gamble, Kraft/General Foods, Kimberly-Clark, The Block Drug Company, Peugeot, Heineken, and The Coca-Cola Company.
• Marketing leadership positions at The Coca-Cola Company, CNN, Royal Crown and Symon Communications. I have managed a $185 million budget, supervised 40 people, had P&L responsibility and reported directly to the CEO for the last 15 years.
• Rare Breadth of Experience in B2C and B2B, Major Corporations and Smaller Entrepreneurial Businesses, and All Aspects of Marketing (agency, corporate, media and digital communication technology)
• I have had general management responsibilities, launched new businesses, attracted new clients, helped engineer turnarounds, built high performance teams, participated in acquisitions and prepared for an IPO.

I've demonstrated that I have the experience, expertise, creativity, persuasiveness, team-building and general management skills to help any type of business raise the effectiveness of their marketing and sales efforts to new heights.

Don Lenehan’s Specialties:

Branding, marketing, advertising, new product/service and packaging development, collateral development, promotions, lead generation, trade shows, sports and entertainment marketing, creative services, interactive marketing, PR, guerilla marketing, packaging, Internet marketing, web site development, turnaround expertise and event marketing.
