Denis Lirette’s Summary

Strongly rooted in the creation of visceral interactive experiences, Denis Lirette brings passion and enthusiasm to everything he does. His philosophy that "good design is strategic and concept-driven" is echoed in the diversity of projects that he has touched - each one engaging with its audience on a visual, emotional and intellectual level.

Over the years, Denis has had the privilege of partnering with several top tier brands including Coke, Kraft, IBM, Cisco, American Express, Bell, RIM, Post, SC Johnson, Procter & Gamble and Mars Inc. (to name a few) - helping them to integrate interactive solutions with traditional marketing efforts and ensuring that the brand experience has the most impact possible.

Denis Lirette’s Specialties:

Imaginative, idea-first talent with flexible traditional and interactive art direction abilities.

Working directly with clients to solve their business challenges with new ideas grounded in creative and business strategy.

Bringing concepts to life in many mediums: from multi-component programs to interactive experiences and identity systems.

Collaborating with copywriters, mentoring designers and syncing with strategists.
