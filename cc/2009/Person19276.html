Chris Knoll’s Summary

My strongest professional talents revolve around the creation of processes, procedures, tools, and documentation. My favorite challenge is to be placed into an organization or team that is lacking in process, procedure, documentation, and teamwork, and make dramatic improvement within a very short time. I've received multiple awards over my career because of this ability, and the "perfect job" would be one where I could jump into a "mess" and create not only a great (and fun) environment for the team, but also to make everyone's job easier and more productive, and improving the company's bottom line in the process. I am a very strong Project/Program Manager with the ability to keep multiple simultaneous projects on track, and can obtain results without having direct authority over project resources and personnel.

Chris Knoll’s Specialties:

Process, procedure and documentation creation where none exists; fostering extremely positive and collaborative team environments; looking for ways to improve existing processes and make them even more efficient and cost-effective; creation of tools that help the users to do their jobs better, more efficiently, and with reduced redundancy. Highly productive with an ability to multi-task, high energy level, enthusiastic, ability to handle ambuiguity and loose definition.
