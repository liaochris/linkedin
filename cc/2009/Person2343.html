Andrew Turner’s Summary

Neogeographer and Technologist, and interested in applying online geography and mapping techniques for mobile information, user-generated geodata, environmental awareness and scientific collaboration.

I've written an O'Reilly book on Neogeography, mapping your stories, history, places, photos, trips, hikes, and anything else. Building several sites and services for sharing and searching geographic locations, and creating personalized, localized maps.

Also writing for MacTech Magazine, Make, and Linux Journal.

Available for freelance development of geospatial applications and services.

Engineer with long-term experience in multi-disciplinary groups developing hardware and software for Airships, Satellites, and Ground Vehicles. Extensive development of software simulation, hardware-in-the-loop integration, and technical management. Rearching and integrating new technologies with computer systems such as autonomous controls, user interaction, and hardware control.

GIS/mapping, web2.0, volunteered geographic information, open-source software, open data formats, application design, interaction
