Abraham Lloyd’s Summary

As a Managing Partner of the Omnicypher Corporation, a company that provides Social Media Strategy and Planning services, I am a passionate, driven, and energetic individual looking to leverage a career in community-driven web application experience to guide and assist companies in being successful leveraging and incorporating Social Media in their day-to-day activities.
