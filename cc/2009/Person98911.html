Tim Lawson’s Summary

An experienced consultant, with a variety of interests covering Cost Reduction, Process Improvement, Change Management, Project & Programme Management. Over 27 years of commercial experience in a variety of roles in Consultancy, Retail, Media & Publishing and Distribution & Logistics.

I have a strong track record of delivering strategic change into organisations together with an extensive list of achievements. An MBA graduate from Henley, my extensive knowledge of systems, processes and organisational design has produced significant achievements.

Over the years, my work has won many awards including the prestigious European Retail Technology Award in 2007 and the BT Award for Innovation & Creativity in 1997.
