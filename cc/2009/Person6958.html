Anthony Marlowe’s Summary

Anthony Marlowe, 30, served as the CEO of TMone from its inception in 2003 when he co-founded the company with COO John Burchert, to 2007. Marlowe is a principle and now serves as the company’s President and CMO. Marlowe was recognized by Inc. Magazine in 2007 as one of the 25 youngest CEOs of the 5000 fastest growing companies in America. In 2008, Marlowe was named to the Corridor Business Journals 40 under 40 list. In 2009 he was featured in the annual Entrepreneur 101 magazine.

Headquartered in Iowa City, IA, TMone is a leading Business Process Outsourcing (BPO) firm specializing in Customer Relationship Management (CRM) and Database Marketing (DBM) as well as one of the area's largest employers. Some clients include: EarthLink, Nextel, DirecTV, ReMax, Wells Fargo, State Farm, 8x8, Vonage, Fidelity, IDT, Bank of America, All State, Washington Mutual, AT&T, Assurant, and Office Depot.

Prior to starting TMone, Marlowe served as the Vice President of Business Development at Infinity. While there, he was responsible for acquiring several large accounts that increased annual CRM revenues by 50%. Marlowe also held a senior field sales role with the May International Company, a Chicago based management consulting firm. Marlowe began his career at MCI and held several sales force management positions in Iowa and Virginia. His contributions were consistently recognized by MCI executives with several awards including MCI Mass Markets “Best of the Best” and MVP. Marlowe consistently performed in the top 1% and as Sales Manager with responsibility for over 200 Agents and Supervisors became the youngest member of management at 22 to be inducted into the MCI Hall of Fame.

Marlowe maintains relationships with many industry leaders and in addition to representing TMone’s services, Marlowe is a sounding board for executives nationally when it comes to their outsourcing and CRM strategies.

A native of LaGrange, IL Marlowe now proudly resides in Iowa City, IA.
