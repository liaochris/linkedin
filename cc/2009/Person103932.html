Wayne John’s Summary

Worked at CED (Consolidated Electrical Distributors) for 15 years (1986-1997 & 2001-2005). Worked Outside Sales for many years. Left in 2005 as a Profit Center Manager in Anaheim, CA.

Siemens Energy & Automation (Manufacturer of electrical switchgear and automation controls). Inside Sales Engineer (1997-1999).

Eaton-Cutler Hammer (Manufacturer of electrical switchgear and automation controls) Inside Sales Engineer (1999-2001).

2005-Founded Electrical Career Specialists, Inc. (ECS) as the only recruiting and staffing firm dedicated to the electrical wholesale distribution industry. I have parlayed my reputation, network and contacts to build a successful business.
The business has grown from a local Southern California recruiting firm to a
nationwide business working with some of the leading A-List manufacturers and distributors. We proudly work with members of NAED & NEMA.

My firm operates with the highest degrees of integrity and confidentiality. This is paramount in the recruiting and staffing industry. Since I own my firm, I have control as to how we operate. We only work with clients and candidates who subscribe to the same code of ethics.

My goal is to maintain and grow ECS in our industry. Creating a brand name over time is also an attainable goal.

2009-Founded Energy Career Specialists as a subsidiary of ECS, Inc. catering to the exploding energy markets. We work with solar, wind, tidal and other emerging technologies.

Wayne John’s Specialties:

Staffing and Recruiting in the electrical wholesale distribution industry. As we work closely within the NAED membership, we have many contacts and have made many placements within this core and important group in our industry. Our reputation involving satisfied customers & excited candidates speak for themselves.
We also have many contacts in the manufacturing and agency/rep fields.
