Patricia Frame’s Summary

Advise executives on how to achieve their vision and goals through more effective treatment of employees. Support organizations and boards with human capital issues; developing, improving, and managing HR functions; organization development; talent management; productivity; and the human aspects of mergers, acquisitions and divestitures. I consult with small to mid-size knowledge-based organizations, including businesses and associations.

My favorite clients seek to thrive in chaotic situations, are facing significant change management needs, or are senior executives who seek to achieve strategic goals more effectively.

Speaker and writer on hiring, job search, human capital, and changing nature of work and retirement. Have spoken to national conferences as well as local groups.

See my management columns on
www.WomenGrowBusiness.com

Patricia Frame’s Specialties:

Effective human capital practices, Strategic human resources for organizational success, Interim HR executive, Performance management, organizational development, management training. Seminar and conference speaker on Human Capital, Human Resources, Career Management issues.
