Jane Chin, Ph.D.’s Summary

Like many of you, I have basic material goals like paying off the house, saving up for my kid's education, and investing toward "retirement".

I see Life as an adventure, full of magic and beauty.

My role as a human being is first to serve as case-study for others: I want to inspire others to discover their own adventures and design viable futures by sharing my life lessons and how I produce sustainable and outstanding results.

My expanded role is as catalyst for, and collaborator with, like-minded individuals looking to change the world through authentic thinking, personal mastery, and alignment of actions with our highest values.
