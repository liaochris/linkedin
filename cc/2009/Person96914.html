Tarra Hartl’s Summary

I have been a long time advocate and volunteer for women with Polycystic Ovary Syndrome, currently serving as a member of the board of directors for Project PCOS as well as a graphic designer on the website admin team. I also serve on the board for IPump, inc., centered around diabetes support and education especially for parents of children with diabetes. I am the ND State Services Captain for the Patriot Guard Riders as well as the webmaster for the ND PG site and the Help On The Homefront Coordinator.

I previously worked with the online communities at Project PCOS and PCOSliving, PCOSA as Director of Online Communities and then President of the Board. I also worked with Islets of Hope online community.

I studied music in college, mainly vocal music performance.
