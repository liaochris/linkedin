Willie Jackson’s Summary

Willie Jackson is an information technology consultant, small business owner, and web designer living in Atlanta, Georgia. By day, he is employed by a management consulting, technology services, and outsourcing company. By night, he brings pixels and padding under subjection using (X)HTML, PHP, CSS, and Javascript.

Located in Georgia since 2007, Willie has been serving clients professionally by assisting with enterprise portal and content management implementations. Entrepreneurially, he has served numerous clients with website design, branding and positioning, and personal marketing consulting services in a freelance capacity.

Willie is a 2007 graduate of the Florida State University, where he studied Information Technology at the College of Information. Joined by a group of talented individuals from across the nation, Willie currently sits on the FSU College of Information Leadership Board and the FSU GOLDen Ring Development Council.
