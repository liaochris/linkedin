Cameron Barrett’s Summary

Pioneered the blog format in the late 1990s with personal blog started in June of 1997. Founder of the cms-list (www.cms-list.org), largest community on the Internet for the discussion of content management systems (3000+ members).

Second-longest-running weblog on the Internet - camworld.org (since June, 1997). Designed and developed the blog and online community strategies for both Wesley Clark's (grew to more than 15,000 members/blogs in 8 weeks) and John Kerry's presidential campaigns.

Co-founded Confabb.com, an online social network built around the largest database of conferences in the world.

I build online communities, blog networks and user-generated content initiatives for Fortune 500 companies, startups and major brand labels.

Cameron Barrett’s Specialties:

More than 10 years experience with dozens of CMS packages, including Drupal, Movable Type, Word Press, Blue Martini and many more.
