Zee Shan’s Summary

I am currently working as Web Designer at Emedia FZ in Dubai Media City, UAE. I'm Jack of all Designing and Animation work . I'm a young and ambitious designer with a talent for delivering unique and aesthetically pleasing results. Through my self-learning and experience I have learned to be a specialist of these areas such as Web Design, Flash Action Script, Web Languages and Graphic Designing.

My professional experience mainly involves a variety of interactive projects made with an emphatic use of Flash Action Script and graphic/web designs for the production of Flash and html websites. I'm a designer but as such I have not limited myself to a particular area of expertise and I have a large interest in a variety of environments. I consider myself well capable of many different tasks involving Web Applications......I specialize in web Designing, Flash Motion, Action Script Development, Photography, HTML, CSS, JS etc.... I design, animate and develop many of my projects on my own from start to finish (and everything in between). This is fun because I'm typically juggling 4 or 5 projects all in a different phases and with different requirements so things rarely get stale. I also do a few collaborative group projects. Those are also fun since I work with a very talented group of people who challenge and teach me new things every day.
I've been fortunate to work with a lot of really cool people and clients over the past 5 years. Some of those clients include: JVC, Symantec, OASIS Water , IFFCO , Emax, Nabeel Perfumes, Tally Gold, Sura Group and Cyber SQR to name a few.
Technical Skills & Tools – Web & Graphic Designing. Through self-learning & professional experience, I have developed a professional level of proficiency with many industry-standard technologies & Tools.
