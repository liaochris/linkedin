Timothy Hale’s Summary

Greetings,
My name is Tim Hale and I own Infinity Fotos, an award-winning, veteran-owned small business founded in 2003 with the sole purpose of providing high quality photojournalism ranging from sports action to location portraiture.

Based in the Atlanta area, I've been involved in photography since 1979. Since that time, years of practical experience have led to a distinctive photojournalistic style.

The key is to bring the viewer to the instant - that moment in time captured forever. Whether it be a game-winning play or a cherished image of a loved one, the moment only happens once.

Let me put my professionalism and experience to work for you today.

Timothy Hale’s Specialties:

Prep, college and professional sports action and features; scenics, landscapes and wildlife; location portraiture and stock photography.
