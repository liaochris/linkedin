Chris Kelly’s Summary

In my 25+ years as a technologist I have never seen such a radical and fast moving paradigm shift in how business is transacted. The Internet has the potential to make or break small businesses.

My company, Agapē Development Services (ADS) works with clients to determine the impact of the Internet on their business and to implement effective and cost efficient marketing strategies designed to dramatically increase profits while ensuring inspired levels of customer service.

Capacity Development is what my mission is all about. That is, building the capacity of companies by helping owners find their voice and a competitive edge using Internet marketing strategies.

My mission is to use technology to enrich lives.

Chris Kelly’s Specialties:

We catering to small businesses, non-profit sector including labor unions, and specialize in creative Internet Marketing strategies, systems analysis, design, implementation and maintenance.

Over the years more of our projects involve helping clients stay competitive by deploying what we call the F.I.D.O. Principle™ that is: F)ocus on your niche I)nnundate your niche with high value content D)ominate the online conversation O)bliterate your competition.
