Rebecka Holub’s Summary

Human Resources and Corporate Recruiting Professional. Over 13 years of HR experience in the Financial Services Industry as well as placement services and the Transportation Industry.

AEGON Americas includes the U.S., Canadian, and Mexican operations of AEGON N.V., one of the world's leading life insurance organizations.

AEGON's (Transamerica Life Insurance Company) core business is made up of Life Insurance, Annuities, Investment Products, Pension, Supplemental Health Insurance and Reinsurance. We are currently searching for Accountants (with IFRS experience preferred to assist with our Market Consistency Project), Actuaries, Investment, Real Estate, Insurance Professionals and IT professionals in several of our offices across the US. If you are looking to expand your knowledge and learn more about our career opportunities, review our website at www.aegonusa.com or for specific career information contact me at rholub@aegonusa.com.
