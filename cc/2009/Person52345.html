Julie Weight’s Summary

I have over 20 years' experience in the medical transcription industry and have now transitioned to domain names and web site management.

Information about my transcription niche domain names is available at TranscriptionConnections.com.

I write and communicate effectively. I have a talent for cutting through the distractions and getting to the heart of an issue. I don't believe in change for the sake of change but I have the ability to see when it's a good thing and readily embrace it. I maintain the philosophy that there are people you like - and people you don't know. I like getting to know people. I like hearing about people and knowing what they're doing. I'm good at remembering what they tell me and keeping it to myself.

I'm looking forward to successful changes in my career path and the people I will meet as I get there.
