Daniel Spira’s Summary

For the past 40+ years, rogenSi has worked behind the scenes with the top companies globally, helping them drive measurable, exceptional performance through their people.

As a consultant at rogenSi, I'm lucky to be working with some of the world's best and brightest, in three main areas:

* Developing Leadership Capability
* Managing Through Change
* Driving Sales Growth


With over 13 years of diverse, hands-on experience as a designer, serial entrepreneur, executive and consultant, I approach my work with a creative sensibility that is grounded in analytical discipline, sound strategy, best practices and business acumen.

My functional responsibilities as a rogenSi Consultant include program design, delivery (training & coaching), business development, client relationship management, and engagement (project) management.

The programs I work on encompass the following areas of talent development, with a focus on "soft skills" and personal effectiveness:

--- Talent Inventory / Gap Analysis
* SOLUTION DESIGN
--- Instructional Design / Accelerated Learning Principles
--- Learning Sustainability / Reinforcement
* IMPLEMENTATION
--- Program Delivery (classroom/virtual)
--- 1:1 Coaching
* MEASUREMENT
