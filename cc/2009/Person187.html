Publication:

MAXIMIZE YOUR POTENTIAL WITH POWERFUL TOOLS... AND NO STRESS

DESCRIPTION ___ Based on more than 25 years' observation of the undeclared, self- inflicted psychological war raging in the backyards of our mind, this book covers a wide range of topics – from how we function as humans to how to attain our objectives. Written in script-form with down-to-earth dialogues to enhance and enliven reading, it includes real examples of how this subconscious war is fought and demonstrates how it can be won. The dialogues and examples were taken from interactive training sessions conducted with trainees from all social, professional and cultural back- grounds, including executives from senior-level management coming from Europe, Asia and America.

KEY BENEFITS ___ It provides a no-nonsense, step-by-step, easy-to-understand approach to maximize our potential. Identifies who our real enemies are – in our private and professional lives – and how to disarm them. And much more... Penetrating and to the point, it combines Western psychology and Eastern philosophy, uses common sense and strategic reasoning to broaden our vision. Initially published in France under "The Art of Self-Combat" by Pearson Education and in Japan by PHP Institute, Inc., the book is a "martial" art of a new kind to fight mentally against our internal enemies, handle even the most difficult situations, and evolve.
