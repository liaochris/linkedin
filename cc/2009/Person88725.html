Sarah LaLiberte’s Summary

As a resourceful and energetic leader and team player adept at implementing high-profile, image-based public relations, corporate communications and marketing programs, Sarah is an expert at working with people to create integrated strategies to develop new/existing customer sales, brand/product evolution, and media endorsement. In addition to holding a BS in public relations and an MBA, her extensive professional experience highlights her proven abilities in effective time, project, budget and staff management. Sarah currently heads up the public relations department for Griffin York & Krause (GY&K) one of the top full service, mid-size and independent marketing idea and innovation agencies in the country (located in Manchester, NH). Before GY&K, Sarah directed and managed all the public relations and corporate communications activities for Segway (yes, the personal transportation company), was a PR Manager at Aware, Inc. and worked at several PR agencies. She also owns Image Perfected, a personal branding company dedicated to helping people market themselves in today's difficult job economy. In addition to her work as a PR/marketing pro during the day, Sarah enjoys spending time with her family, camping on Lake Champlain, ocean and fresh-water fishing, personal and professional networking, new technology "stuff", and blogging.
