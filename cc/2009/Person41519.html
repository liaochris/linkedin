Cathie Dalziel’s Summary

I am the founder of INSPIRE-ites!, Toronto’s only monthly inspirational speakers forum whose goal is to spotlight inspirational and motivational people and give them the opportunity to share with others. INSPIRE-ite speakers are both the known inspirational leaders in our community and the "hidden heroes" whom we hope to help make others aware of. They are people who make a difference. They spark your creativity, alter your outlook and give you new tools for living life.

INSPIRE-ite events are small, informal and a wonderful way to personally meet best selling authors, speakers and award winning life coaches as well as mixing and mingling with other people who believe in the power of change. It's also a great way to preview speakers for your upcoming events.

INSPIRE-ites! is contagious. After listening to keynote speakers and talking with others at the events you can’t help but feel enthusiasm and know that how we touch each others lives really does matter. You know that whatever is being felt that night is going to be carried back into each person’s community the next day. The result is personal change that extends into your home, social and business life. It’s an empowering way to spend an evening.

INSPIRE-ites had it’s kick-off event on October 28th, 2008. In addition to the main speaker, Spider Jones, we also had 3 future speakers come and share about themselves. Barbara Ashcroft, Joanne Chorbot and Tsufit wowed everyone with their enthusiasm. Momentum is high surrounding their upcoming events.

We want to know who inspires you. Who have you heard or met that you think we could help in letting others hear their message? There is an INSPIRE-ites! group on LinkedIn. Join and share who motivates you and get involved in networking and advertising opportunites that will help to grow your business and social circles. Visit www.INSPIRE-ites.memberlodge.com to purchase tickets for upcoming events and to become a member.
