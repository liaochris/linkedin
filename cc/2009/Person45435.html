Jeff King’s Summary

Experienced electronic product designer in all aspects from conception to production. I can take your idea, and make it a reality. Or, help you test and debug an existing product.

Extensive experience in all aspects of the process, including product concept, technical & competitive analysis, electronic and firmware design,component selection, design for manufacturability, RF certification and cost reduction.

  • Senior Product Engineer

Operation was keyed off of GPS and situational data. Designed 2.5 GHz Alfred slot antenna. Interfaced J1708 to vehicle data computer. Designed ruggedized vehicle power supply/audio amplifier using buck regulators and PIC16C621 microprocessor with J1708 interface. Wrote serial drivers to decode Trimble GPS TSIP protocol and derive PVT from coordinate based XYZ positions. Wrote passenger sign interface for VULTON 'DigiDot' transit sign using IBM assembly and Borland Delphi. Wrote video interface using Microsoft Video for Windows.
