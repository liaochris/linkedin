Alan Noble’s Summary

Alan is Co-Founder and a Managing Director of Venturehaus. He has 29 years’ experience of business development and transformation in financial services. He has a track record of restructuring, developing and managing financial businesses, in onshore and offshore markets, in banking and finance, investment management and third-party administration. He has worked in the UK, US, Continental European, South African and HK/Singapore environments and is bilingual in German, fluent in French and currently learning Spanish.

Over the last 7+ years at Venturehaus he has advised and assisted senior executives in major wholesale and retail financial institutions at a strategic level in deploying business improvement initiatives to achieve operational excellence, He has also trained and coached over 800 senior managers in executing these strategies and in leading successful major change programmes in their businesses.

Prior to founding Venturehaus in 2002, Alan led the creation of a business consulting practice for IBM, specialising in business strategy, business transformation, and organisational change in the financial services industry.

Before this, Alan spent 22 years with Citibank. During this long career Alan held executive management positions in customer and business strategy; country, subsidiary and branch management; corporate and institutional client relationship management; transaction banking product management and sales; middle and back-office administration; and treasury, banking and investment operations.

Alan Noble’s Specialties:

Business development, transformation, restructuring, improvement and management of financial service businesses; implementation of Operational Excellence initiatives to address the management of service, cost and risk; Lean and Six Sigma business process improvement deployment and training
