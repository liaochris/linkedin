Jeff Click’s Summary

Since his start in 1998 at the age of 22, Jeff Click Homes has built over 250 homes in the OKC/Edmond area. In just 10 years, Jeff has developed his company into what was ranked among Inc. Magazine's 2007 "Inc. 5000" fastest growing companies. JCH's homes are award-winning, as well, recently winning "Best Overall Design," "Best Kitchen," and "Best Merchandising" for its category in the 2007 Parade Of Homes.

A native of OKC, Jeff graduated from Putnam City North and attended OK Baptist University, where he earned his degree in Telecommunication and minor in Computer Science. He then went on to attend the Univ. of OK School Of Business for graduate studies in the MBA program. Jeff was recently honored with OBU's "Graduate Of The Last Decade" award.

An OK State Certified Professional Builder, and one of the youngest independent builders in Oklahoma, Jeff brings a fresh, modern perspective to building, and a bold sense of style that trademarks each home. Given his expertise in the technology field, Jeff has been the subject of numerous print and television feature stories. JCH was selected by the Central OK Home Builders Assoc. to build the 2004 "House Of Hope", which raised a record amount of over $70,000 for 5 local children's charities. Jeff is currently Vice President of COHBA, which has over 1400 members, will serve as President in 2009.

Jeff and his wife, Deziray, have one daughter, Alessondra, born in March of 2005. Deziray is a graduate of the Univ. of Oklahoma School Of Medicine and aside from enjoying motherhood, she volunteers part-time as a Physician Assistant at the R.O.C. Clinic, a faith-based free family-care clinic. They are active members of LifeChurch.tv-OKC, where Jeff volunteers in the video production department and Deziray periodically goes on mission trips to provide medical services to impoverished communities. Jeff and Deziray also lead a Life Group for young married couples that meets weekly at their home.

Jeff Click’s Specialties:

Technological and media-related integrated home solutions, energy efficiency, metropolitan interior design, public and media relations, new media marketing, graphic design.
