Xavier Patau’s Summary

I am a creative director and multimedia designer. I have worked in several advertising agencies and design studies, having formed part and directing equipment with high creative level and of exigency.

The last years I have specialized in creativity and applied design to new media, as in Web and interactive television. In addition, I have developed to processes related to usability engineering and development multimedia.

My anxious personality has taken to me to experiment in fields like music, the photography, the programming… During seven years I have arranged my work with teaching in several universities of matters like creativity, graphic design and usability engineering.

Something had to be the wire of all this. I believe that it is the necessity that I must to communicate ideas, using for it any type of support.
