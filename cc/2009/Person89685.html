Scott Reese’s Summary

Scott's current responsibilities are that of CEO of Wool Labs. Wool Labs develops and markets emotional media technologies to industry. Wool Lab's WebDig is the leading social media monitoring and surveillance application currently serving clients in life sciences, financial services, hospitality, gaming, advertising and media. Prior to joining Wool Labs Scott served as the Senior Vice President for DIGITAS, a member of the Paris-based Publicis Groupe. Scott focuses on the unique marketing issues of pharmaceutical and biotechnology companies and helps these clients understand the digital dimensions of the healthcare continuum. This work manifests in multi-channel marketing and communications platforms.

Prior to joining DIGITAS, Scott served as Brand Architect, Director of Creative Strategy, for Scient/iXL, a tier-one creative, technology, and strategy consultancy. This exclusive leadership position was developed to help clients understand the differences between online and offline brands and to synchronize the “brand experience” across all mediums.

Scott has led creative endeavors for some of the largest and most recognizable companies in the world, including AstraZeneca, Galderma, GE, Harrods of Knightsbridge, Nextel, and AOL Time Warner, to name just a few. Scott also has extensive international experience in Latin America, Europe, and Israel, where he worked on “globalization/localization” brand issues for Gilat Satellite Networks, GE Spacenet, and Carlink du Brasil (AutoPlaza).

Over the course of his career, Scott has won numerous awards and has been a guest speaker and presenter at many industry events including the Center for Business Intelligence, AIGA Design Camp, Design Management Institute, and Art Center College of Design. Scott has written bylines for a number of industry publications and has been the subject of articles including Pharmaceutical Executive AdStars, MM&M and The Business Journal.
