Sherry Heyl’s Summary

Overview of Concept Hub, Inc
Launched in 2005, Concept Hub, Inc is a full service social media agency. We help our clients leverage online tools and social networks to stay connected and collaborate with their growing and evolving audience. Services include; Social Media Business Plans, Customized Training, and Campaign Management.

As the premier social media agency in metro Atlanta, Concept Hub, Inc has helped a number of organizations optimize their communications efforts to meet the evolving world of social media by integrating social media into communication plans. Concept Hub, Inc has consulted for B2B, B2C, Non-profit, Entertainment, Media and Higher Education organizations.

The Concept Hub, Inc team includes nationally recognized social media experts as well as local PR and Marketing experts who continuously evaluate creative solutions to achieve the goals of our clients. Our focus is on social media, collaboration, messaging, behavior targeting, brand image, and overall participation and influence in the emerging trends that are empowering the masses.

The founder of Concept Hub, Inc, Sherry Heyl, has been recognized as a thought leader in social and collaborative technologies and trends through invitations to speak for many varied associations, conferences, and private events.

Sherry was a key organizer of SoCon07, the first Social Media un-conference in Metro Atlanta and Co-Chair for SoCon08 and SoCon09. Sherry is the Chair of the Technology Association of GA Enterprise 2.0 Society, on the Board of Advisers for the Social Media Club, on the National Advisory Board for KSU Communications Department and is the Social Media Director of Digitiainment, a conference and related events to celebrate Georgia’s digital entertainment industry.
