Tyler West’s Summary

I do not believe in doing business for the sake of doing business. I believe business can be harnessed as a tremendous engine of value, which creates more value in society than it takes. I design, develop and collect business performance frameworks that support the twin ideals of commercialism and sustainability in the marketplace.

Everything I'm good at comes down to the principals of good design. I like to make acquaintance with other people who posses a monk-like devotion to whatever it is they do. Creative people are my tribe.

Companies offering products with value beyond what it costs to create them will get the most out of a relationship with me. I enjoy working with small, flattish organizations. It is within this structure that we can increase the velocity of cash-flow and direct more of it into the hands of a few high caliber players.
