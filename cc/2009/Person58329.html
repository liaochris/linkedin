Lee Garland’s Summary

Professional photographer for 9 years. Former Chief Staff Photographer & Picture Editor for Concept For Living, Spaces and Zero magazines in the UK. Nominated Best Student Photographer 2002 by The Independent, some of the large campaigns I have worked for are for clients such as Manchester United, Silverjet, Starbucks, the BBC, The Guardian, Urban Splash, Harvey Nichols and Gateway to London. Agencies I have worked with include Weber Shandwick, Cohn & Wolfe, Camron, Campaign Works, Talk, Colman Getty & TBWA.

I have had work in The Guardian, The Daily Telegraph, Blueprint, Concept For Living, Spaces, Blueprint, Asian News, the MEN and Homes & Gardens.

I also write the Architecture & Design sections of Concept For Living & Spaces magazines. The pdfs are on my website.

I am a Visiting Lecturer on the Photography and Digital Imaging Course at MMU/Manchester College 2009-2010.

Lee Garland’s Specialties:

Architecture, Portrait & Product photography.
