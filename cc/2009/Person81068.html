Paulo Vital’s Summary

Paulo Ricardo Paz Vital is a Sofware Engineer at IBM's Linux Technology Center working in the IBM ServerGuide Scripting Toolkit - Linux Edition project. He has worked in following projects: IBM Installation Toolkit for Linux on POWER, Yaboot, IBM Server Consolidation Tool and Multicore Development Tools (Cell IDE). Also an IBM Academic Initiative ambassador (www.ibm.com/university/) and an inventor with two patents under registration at USPTO.

He is also a Certified Scrum Master, and likes to study about project management in general.

He is interested in software development and R&D in Open Source, Distributed Systems, High Performance Computing and Computer Architecture projects.
