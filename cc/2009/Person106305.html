Zach King’s Summary

King of All Trades Design, aka Zach King, is based outside of Portland, OR. I have extensive experience with a wide variety of design and marketing projects, from graphic design to web site development, packaging to tradeshows, e-marketing to print collateral. On a personal level, I also have some core values and beliefs I carry over to my business as well. Environmental responsibility, philanthrophy and charitable giving, and social and community involvement are part of not only my day to day life, but that of my business and client choices as well.

Zach King’s Specialties:

Web site design, development and programming. Web site promotion, management, analytics, and search engine optimization. Corporate identity, branding, logos, symbols and icons. Print collateral and literature. Direct and electronic mail. Publication design. Event marketing. Tradeshow booth and exhibition signage. Interactive design and multimedia. CD-ROM installation graphics. Packaging design. Copywriting, editing, and proofreading. Pre-press and print preparation of files.
