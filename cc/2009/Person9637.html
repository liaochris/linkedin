Bryan Arthur’s Summary

Bryan has significant experience involving search assignments for executive and senior professionals in a number of industry areas including oil and gas, renewables, construction, sports and athletic clubs as well as not-for-profit. Prior to joining BluEra, Bryan gained key experience with a leading international recruiting firm, becoming Division Manager of Accounting and Finance and going on to join Conroy Ross Partners in 2006 as a Senior Consultant.

Bryan possess superior IT and research abilities which, in a tight labour market, allow him to find even the most difficult of skill sets. Bryan’s experience working with clients to find the best candidates for their needs combined with his dynamic nature and dedication to providing quality customer service enable Bryan to attract the best talent for our clients and make him a valued asset to both our clients and our firm.

Bryan is a graduate of the Sobey School of Business at Saint Mary’s University. Bryan holds a Certified Human Resource Professional Designation (CHRP) and is an active member of both the Human Resource Institute of Calgary and the Human Resource Institute of Alberta. Bryan is involved in volunteer work with the United Way as well as a number of other Calgary associations.

Bryan Arthur’s Specialties:

Leading search assignments for executives in a number of key industries ranging from Oil and Gas, Renewables, Oil and Gas Services, Construction, The Private Club, and Not for Profit.
