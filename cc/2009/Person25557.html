Deepak Srinivasan’s Summary

Deepak Srinivasan has over 25 years business experience, with the last 15 focused on the interplay of consumers, devices and new media. He has conceived and launched service delivery models in the US, Europe and Asia. His expertise includes strategic sales, marketing and product management. He has successfully managed P&Ls from $2 million to $200 million.

He currently serves as the VP of Corporate & Bus Dev at Nokeena Networks, a Silicon Valley startup, pioneering technologies for new media infrastructure. At Nokeena Deepak is responsible for creating new markets, new verticals and strategic partnerships.

Till recently Deepak shared his time between Bangalore and Silicon Valley, helping incubate and mentor startups including C-Drive Careers and The Kamla Show. From Aug '07 through Jan '08, Deepak also headed Mobiance, a mobile LBS company.

As VP of Product Management at 24/7 Real Media, Deepak was responsible for the coordination of product management, product marketing, and product innovation worldwide. Prior to this, Deepak was with Reliance Infocomm (India) and was involved in planning and engineering for a multi-million home Video-over-IP rollout.

Before moving to Reliance, Deepak co-founded Enscaler Inc., a provider of middleware, applications and services for rights managed and secure streaming media.

Deepak's past experience also includes working with the Product Management team at Sharewave, a wireless home networking chip startup, since acquired by Cirrus Logic. As Director of Product Marketing with Elcom Technologies, a home networking startup, Deepak launched several consumer electronics products into North American retail, OEM and service provider channels. Earlier in his career Deepak consulted in software /IT in the US, Europe and India.

Deepak has a BS in Aerospace Engineering from IIT Kanpur and is a passionate military aviation and geopolitics buff. He counts a flight in a vintage Tiger Moth among his happiest moments.
