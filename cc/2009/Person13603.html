Brian Walker’s Summary

Currently Senior Analyst at Forrester, focusing on eCommerce and multichannel technology, strategy, and operations.

Brian contributes to Forrester's syndicated research and consulting for the eBusiness, Channel & Product Management professional with a focus on eCommerce and multichannel technology, strategy, and operations. His research focuses on eCommerce strategies, technologies, and best practices for retail, direct-to-consumer eCommerce, and online business-to-business. Brian also provides strategic consulting for Fortune 500 companies on their eCommerce technology strategy, vendor selection, and operations.

Experienced in senior roles within large, established online retailers, eCommerce technology providers, and has started his own firm serving B2B, SMB retailers, and non-profit web-marketing and eCommerce needs. Brian’s roles span both business and technology leadership.

Brian Walker’s Specialties:

eCommerce technology, operations, online customer experience development, interactive design, content management, online content strategy, online marketing, online merchandising, retail systems integration, online travel, organization & process development, product management, and software development.
