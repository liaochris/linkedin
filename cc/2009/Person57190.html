Marko Lackovic’s Summary

I have started my professional career as a research assistant working in Zagreb and Lausanne, Switzerland. Further professional development has been continued in Ericsson R&D in Zagreb on the project manager position, and Huawei Technologies Co. Ltd., Representative office in Zagreb, Croatia, on the key account manager position. I currently work as a sales director in Atento d.o.o. I have participated in several European Union projects, and have authored and co-authored numerous scientific publications.

I have obtained PhD degree from the Faculty of Electrical Engineering and computing in 2005 respectively, with the focus on telecommunication networks. My technical education has been extended by the MBA degree from the Faculty of Economics and Business, Zagreb in 2007.
