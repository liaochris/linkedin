Scott Korkowski - LION’s Summary

Experienced recruiter of Information Technology professionals with a Retail and Hospitality background focused on Training and Customer Service. Familiar with the full life cycle recruiting for government, commercial and private companies for their staffing needs. Government staffing includes the sourcing of cleared professionals holding DoD, DoS, and NSA clearances as well as CI and Fullscope Polygraphs.

Currently a Corporate Recruiter responsible for the full life cycle recruitment of internal positions with ITSolutions LLC. Also provides support for Codin Solutions and ITEQ, ITSolutions LLC companies with the search of IT professionals in the Intelligence and National Secrutiry sectors of Technology. Skills sets include .Net and Java Developers, Systems/Network Engineers (including Storage, VMWare, Desktop and SMS), Program and Project Managers, as well as all levels of Networking Technicians.
