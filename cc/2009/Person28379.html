Dr. Henry Sobo optimalhealth@optonline.net’s Summary

Practice Philosophy
Dr. Sobo believes that the best of both medical and nutritional approaches to medical care is more effective than either approach alone.
Our practice focuses on strategies to understand and correct the underlying causes of illness. Natural treatments may enable you to avoid the use of medications and their risks or side effects.

Utilizing the most up-to-date research, Dr. Sobo has developed natural protocols to utilize diet and the correct combinations of vitamins, minerals, amino acids, and other nutrients needed for optimum results.

Natural hormones are also utilized when appropriate. Non-invasive testing to uncover the underlying causes of illness are emphasized. Lifestyle changes are encouraged to enhance the benefits of treatment programs.
