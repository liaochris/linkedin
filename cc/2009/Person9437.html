Barbara Stennes’s Summary

I'm President and CEO of Resources Unlimited, a global training and consulting firm I started in 1976.

Resources Unlimited is the #1 worldwide distributor of the Edward de Bono Thinking Systems, including Six Thinking Hats and Lateral Thinking. Our Network of Certified de Bono Trainers facilitate meetings and deliver customized in-house training seminars that teach:

* Creativity & Innovation
* Critical Thinking Skills
* Meeting Management
* Team Building
* Problem Solving
* Decision Making
* Leadership / Management Development
