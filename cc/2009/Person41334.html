Valerie Heritage’s Summary

The Communication Challenge (established in 1992) specialises in improving motivation and engagement in order to increase job satisfaction and overall performance.

Our practical solutions utilise our unique on-line 360 degree feedback process branded index360© which provides honest and reliable information that acts as a starting point and catalyst to open up conversation in order to gain the ownership and commitment from individuals and teams to work together to improve their performance.

I am very interested in collaborating with other consultants to develop both our businesses.
