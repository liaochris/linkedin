Fito Pardo, AMC’s Summary

Cinematographer and Still Photographer, wide experience in all media related to imaging, from pre-production, production, post-production to print. Wide knowledge in all media: 8mm, 16mm, 35mm, 70mm, NTSC, PAL, HDV, HD, HDR, DNG, RAW, NEF.
Has been nominated for a campaign of "International Red Cross" as creative Caracol de Plata, has Editorials in National Geographic, Marie Claire, OHM, Blink, Deep, C3, Hinge, Architectural Digest, spAce, Cuarta Pared, Europaconcorsi, and some others.
Has Shot as Cinematographer several Music Videos, Commercials, Short Films and some Features Films.
Fluent Spanish and Eanglish, spent more than 9 Years Living in USA.
