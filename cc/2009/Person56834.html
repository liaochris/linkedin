George Overmeire’s Summary

All-round conductor, with broad experience in opera, operetta and church music.
I play the piano, organ and synthesizer/keyboards and I teach at a secondary school.
I'm also a composer/arranger of mostly choral music and a musicologist.
Finally I'm a self-made webmaster and, apart from my own websites/blogs and the websites I maintain for some organizations, I'm developing an Electronic Learning Environment for the school where I teach.
