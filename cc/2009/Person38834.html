Harp Arora’s Summary

Brand consultant helping entrepreneurial organizations and individuals clearly communicate what makes them unique and desirable to their ideal clients. Unique blend of expertise integrates corporate, academic, and agency experience in branding and marketing.

Our clients are professional service organizations including those in financial services, insurance, health care, legal, real estate, not-for-profit, government services, consultants, coaches, trainers, authors, photographers, and other creatives.
