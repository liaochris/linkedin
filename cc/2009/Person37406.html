Rob Gray’s Summary

12+ years in software industry in consulting and business roles. Founder / Owner of Blue Ocean IQ which is a business that provides services and advice around social networking, smarter working, innovation strategy, and the use of technology. Also worked as UK MD of blueKiwi Software and before that 4.5 years at Microsoft, working in the business intelligence group and in the business marketing organization as SharePoint product manager.
Early on in my career I worked as a business intelligence consultant and a solution architect for some of the UK's largest eCommerce sites.
