Jason Hatch’s Summary

I have been a professional entertainer for 22 years and a FT professional DJ for 12 years.

I founded Hamilton Promotions in 1988 shortly after graduation to be the marketing company for my entertainment endeavors and have since worked as a freelance consultant for companies such as WOLF Radio/Radio Disney, Bally's Wild West, Centro, The Pros Entertainment, 1-800-DISC JOCKEY, Kevin Colangelo Premiere Disc Jockeys, 8-Tracks Tavern, The Syracuse Symphony, Museum of Science & Technology in Syracuse, Destiny USA, AXA Financial and many more.

As a Disc Jockey I have had the pleasure of entertaining clients and their guests at over 1,000 events helping them to turn their event dreams into a reality. I've performed for groups from 25 to 25,000 and give 100% each and every time.

In 2000 I moved my DJ business from the Philadelphia area to Syracuse, NY. In 2005 I decided to focus my DJ business solely on weddings and completed the change over in 2008 with the launch of my re-branded wedding entertainement services as JH Weddings...More than just a DJ! Since that time it has been my pleasure to entertain at upscale venues and private receptions all over the Northeast U.S. I believe that my,discretion, attention to detail, commitment to customer service and focus on the Bride & Groom and their personal style are why 95% of my clients are the result of referrals.

In 2008 I launched CNY Photo Booth Rentals. The company has continued to grow with bookings into 2011 for weddings, corporate events and Bar & Bat Mitzvahs. In 2009 the development and production of our own Table Top Photo Booths has allowed expansion into other event markets with more modest budgets at a price point just below $500 for a 4 hour party!
