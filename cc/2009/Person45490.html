Jeff Molander’s Summary

Jeff Molander is an accomplished entrepreneur in the digital marketing space having co-founded a performance marketing agency that is, today, the Google Affiliate Network. Prior to entrepreneurship he worked in various corporate communications roles with a video/multimedia focus for both agencies and internal groups.

He’s an author, passionate speaker, business blogger and Web video publisher. In 2009, he is slated to release Ignorance Economy, an insider’s exposé that will change the way business-leaders and marketing professionals view, use, invest and build careers in interactive marketing.

Jeff is widely recognized for helping business leaders build profitable businesses by challenging accepted marketing practices and sharing closely-held secrets of today’s multi-billion dollar “digital middleman economy.” He’s been helping entrepreneurs, investors, ad agencies, multi-channel retailers and government agencies make smart investments in digital advertising and ecommerce since 1997 –- specializing in performance-based interactive marketing strategies. Jeff is a top 5 percentile international Technology & Media consultant ranked by Gerson Lehrman Group.

In addition to academic, trade and corporate lecturing, Jeff guides a handful of early stage companies and is an investor and board member of Calgary-based Shopster.com.

Jeff is a syndicated writer, business blogger (the UK’s eConsultancy, Shop.org and JeffMolander.com), veteran business podcaster, spirited Web video publisher and a regular, sought-after speaker at a variety of conferences and academic functions.

Jeff Molander’s Specialties:

E-commerce and e-advertising consulting concentrating in direct response marketing/advertising. Expertise focused in performance (cost-per-click, CPA/affiliate and search) marketing strategy development & execution aimed at increasing sales transactions and/or acquiring new customers / leads.
