Paull Young’s Summary

I am an intuitive online communicator with a passion for the strategic application of social media. I'm experienced in leading social media strategy for large organizations from the inside out, working across PR, Marketing, Customer Service, R&D and other functions.

I'm currently working as a Senior Account Executive for social media communications consultancy Converseon in New York city. I've had the opportunity to work with brands such as Telstra, Newell Rubbermaid, Graco Children's Products and the Smithsonian Channel and to have been an integral part of projects that have received awards from WOMMA, PRSA, the Webbys, PR News and OMMA.

My blog 'Young PR' is ranked by AdAge amongst the world's top 150 marketing and by B&T Magazine as Australia's third best PR blog. My thought leadership extends to regular speaking engagements, media appearances including the Fox Business Channel and Christian Science Monitor and my work being featured in several PR/Marketing textbooks.

Paull Young’s Specialties:

Social Media Strategy - online monitoring and measurement, internal consulting (training, policies, infrastructure etc), WOM campaigns, influencer outreach and blogger relations, social media tools (blogging, YouTube, Facebook, Twitter, Wikipedia),
