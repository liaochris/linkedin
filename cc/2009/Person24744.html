David Turnbull’s Summary

Yeeld Solutions is a pan-european hotel revenue management consultancy and distribution service provider, with its mission to unlock conversion potential.

This aim is realised through the provision of integrated distribution solutions and hotel reservation services via the Yeeld Solutions Central Reservations System (powered by Synxis) as well as straight forward training and consultancy support.

Through its network in Berlin and Barcelona, Yeeld Solutions is engaged with a wide range of European hotels, hotel groups and marketing organisations, providing high value, cost-effective reservations, revenue management and online marketing technologies and services.
