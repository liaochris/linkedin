Julie Arduini’s Summary

My goal is to journey with my audience as they seek surrender. My writing and presentations cover all areas of surrender for upper elementary school age through senior citizens.

I accomplish this through authenticity and encouragement.

My mission is for women to find freedom through surrender. My tag line is "Surrendering the good, the bad, and maybe one day, the chocolate."
