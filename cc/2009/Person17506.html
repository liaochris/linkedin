Chip August’s Summary

From 1990 through the present, "Chip" August has facilitated personal growth workshops, worldwide.

In 1991 Chip developed the "Free Your Passion Workshop" (originally named the Healing Anger Workshop). The Free Your Passion Workshop transforms your relationship with your emotions, releases you from your fears of feeling your feelings, and dramatically enhances the quality of your life.

Chip and his partner Kat Calderon offer numerous trainings and workshops for couples, under the umbrella of their Relationship Effectiveness Institute. Chip and Kat are also a certified Instructors of PET (Parent Effectiveness Training) leading adult education workshops to teach listening, conflict resolution and communication skills to parents.

Chip also leads various other workshops, support groups, and leadership trainings. Chip is a founder and consultant with Team Transformation, creating and leading seminars and workshops for businesses on communication, interpersonal relations in the office environment, and other organizational issues.

Chip August’s Specialties:

Listening Skills Training, Conflict Mediation and Resolution, Anger Management, Team Building, Presentations, Hiring Supervising and Training Management Personnel.
