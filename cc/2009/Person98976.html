Timo Paloheimo’s Summary

Advertising agency online creative/planner with marketing/business background focused on but not restricted to digital marketing communications. Several years experience in web design and development and commercial film production. Extensive knowledge and skills in content production for new and traditional media. Interested in new technologies and companies, storytelling, creating user experiences, social networking, games and ROI.

Blogger interested in Finnish web startups on Startupbin (http://www.startupbin.com) and Digital Marketing on Ruotsi kiinni (http://ruotsikiinni.com).

Developer of Google minus Google, a search engine that lets you to search with Google, but omits results from Google owned sites.
