Dick Pirozzolo’s Summary

Pirozzolo Company Public Relations provides public relations and marketing communication services by principals with 20 or more years of experience in the US and internationally.

Our clients include Trayport, a GFI company (GFIG/Nasdaq), headquartered in London, and a leader in carbon trading technology, J/Brice Design International, Inc. in Al Khobar, Saudi Arabia and Boston, MA, hotel and resort development, Wright Express Corporation, IT for the transportation industry and Morpheus Technologies, biometric technology, based in Portland Maine.

Pirozzolo Company has also served clients based in Germany, Israel, India, China, Turkey and Governments of Japan and Vietnam.

Services include public relations, publicity, marketing communication and public policy issues management.

Our Editorial Group provides ghostwriting and author agent services to corporations and corporate CEOs.

Public Relations Affiliate Offices in Frankfurt, Germany, Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, Birmingham, UK and Tokyo, Japan.

Dick Pirozzolo is the author of three non-fiction books on home design, construction and decor and two corporate profiles "For All the Years," a History of Television in New England for WBZ-TV (CBS), Boston and "Oriental Rugs of the Silk Route," (Rizzoli, New York, for A. T. Gregorian Oriental Rugs.

Dick has also written extensively about green building practices and climate change issues and policy.
