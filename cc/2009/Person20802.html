Craig Hanna’s Summary

Experience digital marketing consultant with over 10 years experience. My current role is to help businesses transform themselves and their people to take maximum advantage of the opportunities that digital can offer. Training is a key mechanism for this but we also offer change and interim management, consultancy, recruitment, e-learning, qualifications, bespoke research, events and conferences. Currently working for leading brands including Thomas Cook, IPC Media, AMV BBDO, Turner Broadcasting, Random House, CMPi, Reed Business Information, DLKW.

Previously I established Internate, a full service digital agency, in 1999 and grew it to a top 50 UK agency (NMA) as Managing Director before merging with Designate in 2003. I continued in the merged agency as a partner and Head of Strategy until joining E-consultancy.

Qualified Marketing professional with over 15 years of experience.
