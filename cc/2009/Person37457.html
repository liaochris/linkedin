Jessica Franke’s Summary

I specialize in Website Design, SEO, and Search Engine Marketing (SEM). I also perform Keyword Research and Online Marketing of all kinds.

I have been a computer geek literally my entire life. When I was 8, I got my first computer, a Commodore 64, and later moved on to an apple IIE and later to a long string of secondhand IBM PC's. In 1995, I began designing websites for fun, and over time, I became an expert in that, and many other Internet related disciplines. .

Today, I have over 13 years of professional website design and internet marketing related experience. I can build and update websites built with HTML, PHP, SQL, PERL/CGI, and other related languages. I am an expert in SEO and SEM. I can also build computers from scratch, fix almost any error message or technical problem, and quickly figure out anything new I encounter. In general, electronic things tend to obey me.
