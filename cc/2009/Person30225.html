Elliot Savitzky’s Summary

Elliot is a highly experienced financial research specialist with advanced marketing and business development skills. He has been successful in building sales and establishing profitable client relationships.

- He has devloped a long-standing and solid network of marketing and market research leaders across a wide range of financial services institutions, as well as relationships and experience in many other categories including: construction equipment manufacturing , consumer packaged goods, IT and sports marketing.

- Elliot has an established record of new business development, with a history of developing incremental business.

- He has deep experience managing teams that deliver on-time with high-quality results. His focus has always been on individual development in the context of teaching his team the formula for successful client management.

- In addition to providing market research services, Elliot routinely acts as a marketing consultant to clients, particularly those in smaller, less-resourced companies.

- He has developed unique and creative approaches to research when traditional methods were not ideal.

Elliot has spoken at industry conferences, and has written the following white papers:
- “Linking Corporate Equity, Customer Loyalty and Profitability in Financial Services”
- “Segmenting Markets for Greater Profitability”
- “Pharmaceutical Issues Facing the US Population”
