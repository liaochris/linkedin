SERGE MANOUKIAN’s Summary

I'm an award winning Creative Director with thirteen years of experience in creative strategy, conceptual thinking, branding and design for the digital marketing industry and online media, including eight years in management and mentoring.

I use my skills to conceive and design innovative and engaging brand experiences, along with integrated and interactive campaigns. I have rich expertise in pan-European campaigns, brand identities, world-class websites for Fortune 500 clients, viral marketing solutions, rich-media content, accessible websites, online games, smart-phone applications, direct marketing campaigns, CD-Roms and limited edition toys. I also develop innovative concepts for viral movies, branded web applications, guerrilla marketing, events, services and applications for social media.

Creative direction, creative strategy, brand strategy, conceptual thinking, brainstorming, mind mapping and storyboarding.

MANAGEMENT SKILLS:

DESIGN SKILLS:
Art direction, branding, graphic design, illustration, retouching and typography.
