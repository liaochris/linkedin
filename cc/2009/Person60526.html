Luca Perugini’s Summary

Luca Perugini is currently Business Developer for WideTag, Inc. - Massive Data Collection | Internet of Things

Luca Perugini was a Search & Retrieval Expert consultant for Soloinrete S.p.A.

Before became an S&R Expert, he was Technical Project Manager for Soloinrete, with responsibility of USG IT infrastructure deployment (Università di Scienze Gastronomiche/University of Gastronomic Science - Slowfood).

Before became a Soloinrete consultant, Luca Perugini was a co-founder of Oxys S.r.l, an IT VAR and consultant company.

Before founding Oxys S.r.l he was consultant for _Yond S.p.a. where played a key role on project Pagine Utili and for this reason was hired by _Yond S.p.A as Technical Project Manager.

Before came to Milano, he worked at ITNet S.p.a. Roma.

Luca Perugini started his career as consultant for UISP (Unione Italiana Sport Per tutti) as IT Project Manager where he played a key role on Internet adoption (1996).

Luca Perugini was also a co-founder member of BEST Roma (Board of European Student of Technology). In BEST he was Treasurer and Responsible of BEST relationships with Faculty.

Luca Perugini had started the PHP Italian Documentation Translation on 1995.

Luca Perugini is a Linux advocate and fan from the 1995.

Luca Perugini is also Founder of Agenda Expo 2015 Wiki public control project.
