Bart Hansen’s Summary

Bart is an experienced ENTERPRISE ARCHITECT / PROJECT MANAGER with 15 years experience acquired in a series of demanding roles within the IT arena.

Bart has a vast experience in process and requirements modeling, OO analysis and design, UML, project management and Enterprise Architecture.

The mix of Bart's practical based experience and knowledge of a series frameworks and foundations give him the ability to quickly identify customer concerns, develop multiple viewpoints on the problem, manage a team and communicate efficiently between business and ICT stakeholders.

Bart holds a Master in E-business, qualified as Certified Software Architect, and has a passion for software technologies. Bart is currently attending a Certified Business Process Manager training.

On top of that I enjoy working in teams, communicate and working towards goals. His mother tongue is Dutch. He fluently speaks English and has a good knowledge of French.

Bart Hansen’s Specialties:

* Manage projects (PMBOK, PRINCE2)
* Business and functional analysis (process, requirements)
* Object Oriented Design and Analysis (UML, Patterns)*
* Modeling Enterprise Architure or large scale ICT applications (based on IEEE 1471-2000)
* Create base technical IT architecture (using SOA, CMS, DMS, Portals, ERP suites...)
* Selecting vendor solutions or outsourcing partners
* Defining master plans, programs and projects
