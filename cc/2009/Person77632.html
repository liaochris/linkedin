Paul Jerome’s Summary

Paul Jerome, Principal Consultant for Jerome Consulting, is an experienced management consultant and business executive. He has comprehensive expertise in executive, management, and team development, and in strategic, tactical, and project management.

Paul has extensive international experience developing business leaders throughout the U.S.A, Europe & Asia, and has applied the DiSC Classic Personal Profile System since 1979, and The Feedback Planner (Coaching Through Effective Feedback) since 1988.

Paul is widely recognized for his engaging, challenging, & enthusiastic style of developing teams, often with a blend of humor! He is known for being accomplishment-driven, straight-forward, and objective as he offers practical and creative leadership tools and techniques. Paul has a proven track record for helping executive teams sort through chaos, establish clear strategic and tactical plans, and achieve valued goals.

Paul has demonstrated his proficiency in linking business goals and practical systems in a wide-range of organizations, including:

Aerospace Corp (1988-Present), ABET, ASML (2007-Present), Associated Travel Services, Avco, Callison Architecture (2006-Present), Canon, Carl’s Jr., ConAgra/Hunt-Wesson, Daycom, DGA/PacVolt, Edwards Lifesciences (2003-Present), Epson, ESI, Exult/Hewitt, FEI (2003-Present), FloTool, Genentech, Genigraphics, Goodrich,
InFocus (1999-2003), Kaiser Permanente, The Koll Company, McDonnell Douglas, MCI, Mitsubishi, NEC (2006-Present), Nissan, Northwestern National Life, OCTA, Precor, Professional Community Mgmt, QSC Audio, Regence/Blue Cross Blue Shield, Reynolds&Reynolds, Scientific Atlanta, Sempra Energy, Smartflex, Steljes Trade,
Tektronix (2003-Present), Toshiba, TRW, Universal Studios, U.S. Sales, and Young&Rubicam (2001-03).

Paul is the author of 3 practical guidebooks, “Coaching Through Effective Feedback,” “Evaluating Employee Performance,” & “Re-Creating Teams During Transitions.”
