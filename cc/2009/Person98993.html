Timothy T. Carter’s Summary

More than half of my 18 years of experience in the food manufacturing industry have been spent at the executive level working to build value in high-growth business units and companies. I grew up in this industry, starting in retail grocery stores stocking and merchandising food products for a distributor. My career evolved through leadership roles in all functional disciplines—Marketing, Sales, Finance, and Operations—prior to my invitation onto the Executive Team at Bell-Carter Foods, Inc. The goals at each stage of my career have been similar: build equity by developing a unique strategy, assemble a competent team, and implement sensible management processes.
