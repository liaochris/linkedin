Derrick Farmer’s Summary

Derrick currently works at Vertek Corporation as a network administrator. He graduated from Champlain College with a Bachelors Degree in Computer and Digital Forensics and has an Associates Degree in Electronics and Computer Technology. Derrick's career interests lie in the fields of System Administration, Information Security and Computer Forensics.

Personal Projects and Commitments:
-Derrick is working as part of a vulnerability assessment team (www.nycomputernetworks.com) under the supervision of Christian Balan, CISSP.
-He is studying for his ISC2 SSCP (Systems Security Certified Professional) certification.
-Derrick has written a technical article on the forensic analysis of the windows registry, which has been featured on forensicfocus.com and will be published this fall in HEX: The Collegiate Journal of Computer Forensics.
-Last year Derrick completed an internship with C3DI, where he conducted digital forensic research projects for the Vermont Internet Crimes Task Force (ICTF) and assisted a forensic examiner at Burlington Police Department with imaging digital evidence using FTK.
-In 2007, his dedication and technical abilities were recognized by Champlain College faculty, who presented him with the Student Leadership in Computers & Digital Forensics Award.

Derrick Farmer’s Specialties:

System hardware and software troubleshooting, end user support, Windows, Linux/Unix, Network+ certified, Security+ certified, telecommunications, Avaya System Administration, Symantec Backup Exec, Symantec Endpoint Protection, Active Directory, IpMonitor, WSUS, web design, Experience with forensic and security software - FTK, EnCase, WinHex, Helix, Registry Viewer, GFI Languard, Snort, Backtrack/Security Auditor and OWASP security suites.
