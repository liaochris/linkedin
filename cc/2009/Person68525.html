Mike Hahn’s Summary

Just about anyone can learn the tools of this trade. A lucky few also possess or can develop an "eye" for good design. But passion -- the deep-seated drive to create -- is a less common commodity. I was born to do this stuff.

Mike Hahn’s Specialties:

I temper a fiery creative passion with careful analysis and forethought. I maintain a sharp focus on my clients' goals and strive for design that communicates clearly.
