Clay Robeson’s Summary

In 2003, I left my $60K/year corporate job to pursue a life in the creative world.

Currently, I'm working as a Producer and Writer on Moron Life, an internet Sketch Comedy show based in San Jose (http://www.MoronLife.com). We're producing our fourth season of web comedy gold. I work extensively with the San Francisco Improv Alliance, and am serving as the Associate Producer for the San Francisco Improv Festival. I've also founded my own production company, Clay Robeson Creative. We just finished a two minute publicity video for A Single Drop (a global clean water initiative) and we're actually in pre-production for our first Indie film project. In the little free time that I have, I'm an Ensemble member and Digital Outreach Coordinator with the Un-Scripted Theater Company.

I'm always interested in new production projects and avenues to explore.

http://www.ClayRobeson.net
