Mr. Marketology | Jeff Beale’s Summary

EXECUTIVE BIO

Jeff Beale is passionate about Internet marketing and the different techniques involved in bringing customers and businesses together through interactive and informative means. As founder and CMO of Jazar Dezign, Jeff puts his MBA from AIU as well as his 12 years of marketing experience to work.

Jeff began his career as a web designer for the United States Air Force providing them with a registration portal for their annual symposium thus saving them over $35K in the first year alone. He then migrated to the corporate sector working for a small digital agency providing web design, search engine optimization and rich media marketing for them and their clients.

With ability for strategic planning and capacity to generate results, he developed great part of his career in the digital marketing space earning him a position as a SEO Manager with 360i, an award-winning digital agency that helps Fortune 500 marketers reach their target audiences. This providing him the opportunity to work with major retailers such as Saks, The Children’s Place and Audible as well as major firms in the financial space such as E*TRADE and TrustedID.

SUMMARY OF QUALIFICATIONS

Successful mastery of Internet marketing in the areas of interactive web site development, SEO, PPC / SEM and strong relationship building with social media marketing.

With over 12 years of web marketing & online branding experience, I am able to help achieve online business goals by identifying market niches, improving ranking and increasing traffic. Internet marketing demands successful lead generation methods combined with a process of lead capture and a measurable analytic to identify new opportunities.

OBJECTIVE

To deliver business marketing strategies that improve company's competitive edge while expanding their customer base through increased referrals, saving them time and money which reduces marketing costs.
