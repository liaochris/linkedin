Randy Terbush’s Summary

Fortune 500 Executive and Technology Leader: A technology and business visionary with wide range of expertise, tested at local, national and international levels. Over 16 years experience pursuing and creating winning technologies during the most rapidly changing time in computer technology history. A creative problem solver who has demonstrated skills in both early stage startups as well as multi-billion dollar Fortune 500 companies.

Entrepreneurial Experience – Technology Turnaround: Enlisted to bring the entrepreneurial pace and can-do attitude to large stagnant technology challenges. Experience moving an organization out of a legacy IT inertia and into a more cost effective and scalable new Enterprise Architecture. Innovated, developed and led the strategic direction for more than 120 products, serving 600,000+ clients and over 50 million end users. A recognized expert on creating successful business strategies utilizing Open Source software and development methodologies.

Proven Record across All Strategic Business Functions: Highly developed entrepreneurial skills along with Fortune 500 leadership experience, has produced a well rounded executive with broad experience in Product Management, Marketing, Sales and Corporate Finance. This rare combination of corporate business acumen coupled with a deeply technical background makes for an ideally prepared leader for a modern and progressive 21st century business.
