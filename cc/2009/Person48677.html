Joe Rotella SPHR’s Summary

Joe Rotella, SPHR, is ““…something of a one-man think tank, has been doing more innovative things with the internet than anyone I’ve met yet.” (Bob Scott, Editor, Accounting Technology, April 16, 2009)

With over 20 years of web/application design and development experience, Joe is the CTO of Delphia Consulting and leads their Usability / Web Design & Development Practice. His team is responsible for the design of several government sites including Chicago’s tourism site (www.ExploreChicago.org) and the Las Vegas city site (www.LasVegasNevada.gov), and many private sector sites, including Weiser LLP (www.WeiserLLP.com) and GBQ (www.GBQ.com).

He is in demand as a speaker, presenting on usability, internet marketing, web design and strategic HR. Look for his sessions at the Ohio Hospital Association Annual Meeting, CPAmerica International Marketing Conference, the Society for Human Resource Management (SHRM) Annual Meeting and the National Association of Government Webmasters (NAGW) Annual Conference.
