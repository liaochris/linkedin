Kevin Gilnack’s Summary

I am a young nonprofit professional working for a membership association of human service agencies. In this role I enjoy
• growing our membership
• developing new benefits and programs that support the human service sector
• exploring social media to share resources and raise awareness for the work that we and our members do to strengthen Massachusetts communities
(among many other things)

When I'm not at work, I am focused on my roles as Chair of the Greater Boston Young Democrats, Board Director for the Young Democrats of Massachusetts, and member of National Council of Nonprofits Products & Services Committee.

I also enjoy following politics, listening to music (or seeing it live when I can), exploring Boston and her nightlife, learning about new technology, and meeting new and interesting people.

I'm always happy to be a resource to anyone interested in the nonprofit sector, affinity programs, nonprofit marketing or social media, progressive politics, and anything else that my input would be helpful for.

Kevin Gilnack’s Specialties:

Membership development, affinity partnerships, social media, human service public policy, nonprofit management and leadership, advocacy and nonprofit marketing & communications
