Geetika Agrawal’s Summary

Create Integrated Interaction Design solutions and strategies for multi-channel digital projects including digital marketing campaigns, transactional experiences, rich internet and mobile applications, consumer devices, physical environments, events.

Passionate participant of social media, digital culture, physical interactive art and new technologies. During my evenings and weekends, I co-produce art and music events, volunteer, meet new people doing interesting work and dance!

Subject of interest: Creating user-experiences that help bring Social Change and Cure through elements of casual play and performing arts.

Geetika Agrawal’s Specialties:

1. System Design i.e. going out in the field to understand user and business needs, framing the problem, identifying innovative opportunities and finding solutions.
2. Translating user-experiences across multiple channels and varying scales.
3. Breadth of skills include working collaboratively with strategists, planners, visual designers, technologists and producers, help in business development and build the company's talent resources.
