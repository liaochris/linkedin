Christopher Clarke’s Summary

I'm currently a full time university student studying the Internet (BA Internet Studies) at Curtin University of Technology and expect to graduate in 2011. I graduated secondary college in 2007.

I don't yet have any formal professional experience, but I do run web fiction directory Muse's Success (http://muses-success.info) which I coded from the ground up using the CodeIgniter framework. I have also created several content management systems and forums over the last few years for my personal use, but none of them are currently being used and have since been deleted from my hard disk. They were mainly created for the sake of learning after all.

I feel my skills in HTML and CSS would be considered advanced while my skills in PHP would be considered above Intermediate but not advanced as I still feel there is more for me to learn. I'm not a great designer yet (see my portfolio page for examples), but I do aspire to improve in that respect.
