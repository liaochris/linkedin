Lynnelle Wilson, Marketing 2.0-Business Growth Specialist’s Summary

You can transform your business just by leveraging new media. Benefits can include improved marketing results, which can increase your sales, which can increase profitability and so-on. This "inbound" approach to marketing is just one social media strategy that integrates social media and web 2.0 technologies into traditional marketing and business strategies.

Our goal is to improve your business and sales results through new media education, advice and ongoing support.

In 2003 I moved to Maine by way of New York by way of Dallas, Texas. I took the lessons learned in my years with JPMorganChase and now apply them in my company, Bold Vision Consulting.
