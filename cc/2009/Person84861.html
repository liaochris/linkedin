Robert Boyd’s Summary

Helping you design and manage technology development/expansion projects more cost effectively so you can stay focused on your business instead of getting tangled up in the challenges of managing technology. Helping you plan ahead for contingencies to minimize and eliminate losses that occur during implementation.

How do you manage when you run into obstacles? Or find that something that used to work just fine doesn't? And when things change & you face figuring out what to do next? What resources do you have to help shift through those times? How well do they work? Have you ever used outside support? What if I could show you a way to shift & find new ways within your organization to make the impossible possible, the possible easy, & the easy elegant? What would you need to bring me in?

I work with individuals & groups to facilitate dynamic shifts in growth & well being through Feldenkrais® & InterPlay®. I combine novelty, awareness, deep listening, & play to structure incremental lessons leading to lasting transformation.
