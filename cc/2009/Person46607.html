Jerry Smith’s Summary

One of the key challenges for a small business owner is attracting quality clients to the business on a consistent basis.

Most of us set up a practice or business because of a passion we have to deliver something of real value. The assumption is that we are good at whatever we do and want to make that a career. It can be frustrating and demoralizing to find out after 12 month or less that this expertise isn't enough to make the business really fly.

The result is harder work, stress and eventually can lead to business closure. That is a tragedy for all involved. My passion is to help business owners and independent professionals figure out this marketing "thing" so they have the clients flowing into their business.
