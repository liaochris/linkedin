Steve Sieck’s Summary

Business strategist and industry analyst focusing on digital information content and services. Over twenty-five years' experience helping the world's leading content and enabling-technology providers develop new products, enter new markets, make strategic investment decisions and align organizational resources with strategic and financial objectives.

Steve Sieck’s Specialties:

Business planning, corporate development, industry / portfolio analysis, new product development, market entry strategy, business / technology strategy integration.

Clients include publishers and information services in the B2B, legal, scientific, technical, medical, education, financial, news, and consumer media segments, as well as government agencies, information technology vendors, telecommunications carriers, financial services providers, and non-profit associations.
