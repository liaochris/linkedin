Danielle Holke’s Summary

With over 13 years experience, Danielle is an expert in interactive, with a special focus on the arts.

Danielle began her career at Edge 102 as a regular tech contributor to Humble and Fred's morning show, offering insights on all things web. As a member of the station's creative team, she wrote copy for local and national advertising campaigns which included the Art Gallery of Ontario, Universal Music Canada, Molson and Labatt's.

As Qualitative Researcher and Recruit Manager at Youth Culture, Danielle worked on projects for leading brands such as Levi's and Rogers. Projects included focus group moderation and online survey design. As Web Operations Manager, she directed the launch of Youth Culture's portals, vervegirl.com and fuelpowered.com.

For three years, Danielle was the Manager at Sony BMG's Digital Department where she directed the production team and developed digital marketing strategies for artists like Justin Timberlake, Sloan, Pink and Canadian Idols Ryan Malcolm and Kalan Porter. Projects included mobile marketing initiatives with MuchMusic and a cross-Canada mall tour with Sympatico.MSN for Avril Lavigne. Danielle was also a member of the Canadian Recording Industry Association's Value of Music Committee. The committee was formed to communicate the value of music to Canadian youth, a demographic known for aggressive downloading behaviour, with the most notable campaign "Keep Music Coming".

Most recently, Danielle completed a contract as a digital consultant on the launch of CHARTattack.com, where she worked on the team that oversaw the evolution of a well-established print publication to a leading social media platform in Canada.

Her unique expertise is contracted to several clients including TIFFG, AVLA (Audio-Visual Licensing Agency), CRIA, CBS Outdoor, and artists Peter Bloom, Chris Dedrick, Joan Watson and Simon Wilcox.

expertise and strategy in digital marketing, project management and development; integration of new technologies; strategic use of social media; content acquisition and licensing; mobile marketing protocol and regulations around use and implementation; interactive marketing/privacy laws (PIPEDA), email marketing, online campaign measurement
