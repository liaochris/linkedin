Paulo Monteiro’s Summary

Accomplished executive within mobile and fix telecoms industry with proven track record of leadership performance in international markets from a career working with industry leading blue chip companies such as Comverse, Dilithium and start-ups. 18 Years International experience, 12 Years B2B experience having worked with over 100 telecom service providers, emerging markets expertise, extensive contact network.
• Global experience – having lived in Portugal and Spain and experience of working with telecom service providers in Southern Europe and Mediterranean, Latin America, Middle East, Africa and Southeast Asia.
• Proven ability to analyze telecom markets, and growth opportunities - then introduce strategic and tactical solutions that improve competitive performance while increasing revenues and profits.

Customers and Partners have included Vodafone (UK, DE, IT, PT and ES), T-Mobile (Europe), Fastweb, FT Orange (Europe), Portugal Telecom (Europe, Africa and Latam), Telefonica O2 (Europe and Latam), TIM (Europe and Latam), Etisalat, Orascom, Wataniya, Mobily, MTN (South Africa), STC, Zain, Banglalink, Starhub, SingTel, Excelcomindo, Indosat and IM2, Telkomsel, Maxis, OTE, BT (UK and Spain), COLT, C&W, KPN, Sprint, Verizon (Wireless and Corporate), Telenor, TeliaSonera, Ericsson, Italtel, Alcatel-Lucent, Cisco, Nokia Siemens Networks, Nortel, ZTE, NEC, HP, Atos Origin (Europe and LATAM), Cap Gemini, IBM, Neoris, Logica and Accenture.
