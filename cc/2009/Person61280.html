Kim Jacobs /Maddie James’s Summary

Kim Jacobs has worked in the education field for 30 years, as a classroom teacher, direct services provider, program administrator/director, and as a provider of professional development (training and technical assistance), working in both public and private/non-profit sectors.

Aligned with her educational experiences lies a passion for books and writing. Always eager to write a research paper (imagine that), Kim loves putting words together to both inform and entertain. She has published professionally as a freelance writer since 1990 and a romance novelist since 1997. She has several dozen non-fiction articles/chapters/manuals to her credit and over 20 novel/novella length works of fiction to date.
