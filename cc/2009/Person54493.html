Ken Brady’s Summary

I have a varied background in film, television, theatre, education, writing, software, marketing, virtual worlds, and publishing. I've sold a screenplay, a few stage plays, many short stories, and have produced an indie feature film. I've spent considerable time traveling in Asia - Japan, Taiwan, Hong Kong, Thailand, Malaysia, Myanmar, Laos, Singapore, Indonesia, Korea and China. I speak some Chinese, Japanese, and very little Thai.
