William Klein’s Summary

I've spent more than twenty years advising heads of non-profits, candidates for president, Congress, state and local office on message marketing and direct mail. Working with talented graphic designers, I create print brochures, ads. leaflet and flyers that find what Tony Schwartz called "the responsive chord."

I also write newsletters, speeches, website copy and other material for clients and am a widely published op-ed writer myself. I am offering a new service of rapid response op-ed writing, providing in most cases a same-day turnaround on op-eds to help newsmakers take advantage of breaking news. Go to www.urgentopeds.com for more information.

I blog about politics, the media, books and music at www.headlineupdate.com

William Klein’s Specialties:

Political campaigns, non-profits, public relations, media consulting,ghostwriting, speechwriting,advertising,unions,media relations. Very fast and very good writing that delivers targeted messages.
