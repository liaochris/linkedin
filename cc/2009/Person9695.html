Arctic Fox’s Summary

Primary experience in Military/Aerospace, with extensive knowledge and understanding of electronics with Class 3 assemblies as well as troubleshooting and rework. Operate, programming, troubleshooting, streamlining operations and repair of CS-400E Parts Placement Machines. Operate, streamlining procedures, program adjustment, troubleshooting and repair of BGA Rework Stations. Experienced with both removal and re-installation work. Certified in Electronics Handling, IPC Specialist (J-STD-001), IPC-610-Rev D (Class 3), Through-Hole Soldering, IPC-610-Rev D (Class2), SMT/TH Identification/Value.

Perform visual inspection for quality control under microscope for small electronic parts. Prepare failure-proof, mission-critical wire harnesses for Boeing and military aircraft. Built projects according to original blueprints and assembly aids according to strict MIL-SPEC, ISO 9000:1994, and most recently IPC-610 standards.

Working on surface mount assembly and rework, thru-hole assemblies / repairs / inspection / rework, wire from 28AWG to 3/0, standard and Lead-Free ROHS solder, crimp, splice, connectors, housings, power supplies, circuit boards, heatsinks, displays, voltage operations from 3V through 480V, single and three-phased, environment sealing, waterproofing, harnesses, motors, controllers, water well pumps and turbines.

Quality inspection and control, rework, assembly, packaging, inventory control, shipping and receiving, task delegation, communications, hiring / lay-offs / firing of employees, training, delivery, computer experience on Windows 95, 98 and XP Pro.

Business Ethics, Business Law, Public Speaking I & II, Business Mathematics, Physics & Astronomy, General Science, Real Estate Law & Theory, Computer Science, Basic Web Design, Advanced Web Design, Forklift Certification, Electronics Certifications, Microsoft Office 2007.
