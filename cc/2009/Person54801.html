KEY QUALIFICATIONS
A solid experience from the telecommunication and IT industries. More than 25 years of experience from assignments as Telecom Engineer, Development Manager, and Project Manager with Televerket/Telia, and from numerous international consultancy assignments within the telecom and IT sector.

EDUCATION
M. Sc. Electrical Engineering and Telecommunications, Chalmers University of Technology, Gothenburg.
Post-graduate training in: Business Economy, Management and Leadership, Project Management and Project Administration, Information Technology/Telematics, and Digital Telecom Networks.
