Emily Davis’s Summary

For 15 years Emily Davis, EDA Consulting's Founder and President, has been committed to the nonprofit sector as employee, trainer, board member, philanthropist, and consultant. She started her work in the field on ending violence against women and now works closely with various grassroots organizations serving both the U.S. and international stakeholders.

Emily currently consults and trains nonprofit organizations in the areas of nonprofit leadership and management, emerging leadership, philanthropy, resource development, event planning, marketing and organizational communications, volunteer management, and board and organizational development. Emily recently completed innovative research, Preparing the Path to Leadership, on emerging leaders within the nonprofit and philanthropic sectors.

She has served on numerous nonprofit committees and boards nationally and in Colorado where she recently completed her Masters of Nonprofit Management with honors from Regis University. She now lives in San Diego, CA where she continues her consulting work; founded and serves as board chair for YNPN San Diego; and focuses on issues related to emerging leaders with nationally-renowned organizations.
