Vanessa Trost’s Summary

- Multi-talented communications specialist for distinguished companies

- Experienced lifestyle and shelter magazine journalist, switched to luxury goods product PR turned to corporate communications and international brand marketing.

- 20+ years of professional experience in media, Public Relations and marketing.

- Founded 021c communication concepts, a brand marketing agency in New York City, in 2001. The company represents clients such as Bosch Siemens Home Appliances, Gaggenau, Ritter Sport, MAN, Rosenthal, Droege & Company, Humboldt University, the Institute for Applied Creativity, Novartis, Nestlé, Unilever, Barilla.

Vanessa Trost’s Specialties:

021c communication concepts is an international marketing communications agency tailored to the needs of global businesses and organizations. International integrated marketing communications is our specialty – we successfully position our clients' brands in international markets and build brand equity across various communications channels. Our field of expertise is effective marketing communications between the U.S. and Europe, to connect companies and brands with their target audiences.
