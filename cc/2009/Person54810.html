Keridan Elliott’s Summary

I am passionate about the entire process of creating believable Characters. Bringing a character or creature to life on screen, seeing it breathe, think and perform is an incredible experience that I am fascinated with. The intertwined combination of Art and Technology needed to create them constantly pushes me to learn new techniques, to revisit old ones, and to come up with fun solutions.
