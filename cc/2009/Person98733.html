Tim Andren’s Summary

Tim is Vice President of 360º Strategic Marketing, a marketing firm that specializes in effective strategic marketing and business development, branding, and social media.

Read Tims' blog, a resource on the subjects of trend forecasting, marketing, business and media. http://blog.timandren.com/

Some of Tim's notable speaking engagements focus on social media uses for marketing admissions, retention, branding and career services.

Tim has spoken at several conferences, notably the California Association of Private and Postsecondary Schools (CAPPS), The Massachusetts Association of Private Career Schools (MAPCS) and California Business Education Association (CBEA).

An avid researcher and writer, Tim is working on a book and has been featured in numerous interviews on the subject of marketing, social media and behavioral economics.

Tim Andren’s Specialties:

Strategic marketing and business development, education marketing, social media and branding.
