Justen Hong’s Summary

Justen Hong is owner and head designer at Visual Lure, LLC, a graphic/web design company serving St. Louis, MO, St. Louis Metro East, Southern Illinois and beyond.

His art career began in high school when he was designing ad layouts and comps for a small Chicago advertising firm. After high school, Justen went to Eastern Illinois University majoring in graphic design and completed his design degree at Southern Illinois University at Edwardsville.

After college, he immediately received a job at Print Design, a graphic/web design company located in Alton, Illinois, which later became Brick Street Creative. There, he designed logos and identities, print products and websites, eventually becoming the web director and a lead graphic designer.

Justen has been freelancing heavily since college, and in 2006 he had enough clientele to start Visual Lure, LLC., where he is today.

identity and logo design, website design and development, print design, advertising, brand support
