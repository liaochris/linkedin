Mike Wills’s Summary

I have been developing for the IBM System i (aka AS/400, iSeries) since 2000 working in many environments including custom applications, Lawson Software, and Infinium. During this time I have also done many administrative type tasks. Recently I have started to do some .NET development and have done a bit of PHP and Java in the past. I have also been troubleshooting and fixing computers for the past 15 years.

My hobby is working within the independent media industry. I have blogged in different forms since 2001 and have run several podcasts since 2005.
