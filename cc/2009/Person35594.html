Gene Cross (AKA: Eugene W. Cross, Jr.)’s Summary

A 30 year track record of success in achieving form, fit, function, financials, and pizzazz factor in more than a dozen significant new products, from submillimeter diameter medical devices to airborne laser radars and meter class telescopes.

Philosophy:
"Our greatest failures come not in our knowing, but in our doing." -GC, June 3, 2008

Gene Cross (AKA: Eugene W. Cross, Jr.)’s Specialties:

Product development, from feasibility to system and component design, and delivery of functioning and documented hardware. Design and tolerancing of optics and opto-mechanics for producibility.
