Kit Cody’s Summary

With 12 years experience in the Internet and media industries, Kit has provided the leadership and vision to build profitable, market-leading online businesses. His organizational skills, work ethic and management style have fostered passionate work environments, where innovation and best practices flourish. Entrepreneurial by nature, and adept at bridging the gap between marketing and technology objectives, Kit has owned and operated his own businesses, been a principal in successful Internet start-ups, and led strategic engagements for Fortune 500 companies.

Kit Cody’s Specialties:

Internet Business Strategy, Software Product Management, Agile Application Development, Online Business Operations, Social Media & Web 2.0
