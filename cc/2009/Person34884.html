Gail Dunn’s Summary

As a former collision center manager, I learned the automotive industry from the ground up, literally. I am I-CAR platinum certified, ASE certified, and chair the North Georgia I-CAR Training Committee.

I believe in straight and fair treatment and want to bridge the gap between consumers and the automotive industry.
