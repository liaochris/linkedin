Lane Raichert’s Summary

I've had 29 busy and happy years in video game and TV animation wearing many creative and production hats, earning hundreds of production credits. From sugary preschool series to hardcore video games, I enjoy a broad range of paid work. Please see www.raichertmedia.com for a list of clients and projects over the years. I'm proud that 95% of our clients are repeat customers and word of mouth recommendations.

Lane Raichert’s Specialties:

Video games: Concept art (speedpaints, character/prop design, environments, vehicles, kinetic events/gags), storyboards, animatics (including full SFX editing/design), cutscenes, VO Production, in-game scripts, cutscene scripts, branding/intellectual property consulting, art direction, pitch meetings.
TV animation: Writer, Story Editor, Producer, Director, VO Director, Storyboard Artist, Lyricist, Offline Editor, VO director, 3D animation, designer, series development, brand consultant.
