Fabio Lamanna’s Summary

I'm working as Indipendent Civil Engineeer on several projects about road networks capacity and design in Italy. Moreover I'm currently developing some websites of Civil Enginnering Societies.
+++
My research activity is related to transportation network robustness by means of physical complex networks algorithms. I have developed a tool to analyse the vulnerability of such systems, under different spatio - temporal constraints.
+++
I'm looking to extend the approach to several situations; the model has been tested on Berlin Trasport Network with good results. The physical approach could be extended to urban flow nets, railway stations tracks layout, and more.

Fabio Lamanna’s Specialties:

Matlab, Octave codes. Web design (XHTML and CSS), Network Workbench Tool, Pajek, Transportation networks Efficiency. Railway Simulation. Opentrack. CAD. Traffic Modellization and Simulation.
