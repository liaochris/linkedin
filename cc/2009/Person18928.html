Chuck Bonfig’s Summary

My goal? To leave the world a better place than I found it. That should be what we all strive for, but many of us lose sight of that along the way.

Winston Churchill once said “We make a living by what we get, we make a life by what we give.”

So make sure as you sit there measuring your paystubs, your awards and your promotions that you also take stock of what you give to your family, those whom you work for and those who work for you, your friends and your community.
