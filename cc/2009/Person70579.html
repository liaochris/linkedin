Mohammed Said HJIOUIJ SAHLI’s Summary

The first Arab Blogger to make a full-time job out of professional Blogging. And I'm the first Arabian was Titled as Community Manager in an Arabic start-ups.

I established in the second half of the year 2009 an annual Award for the best Arabic blogs.

More than six years experience in the web development and management of communities (Forums, ezine, social networks, ... etc.). And I have managing, since 2002, a number of training courses in computer and web development.

I am author of a book on Blogging (ABC Blogging) was the first of its kind when the first edition published in 2006 (and the second edition published in 2009). Also Co-authoring with a chapter on social networks in a book about the Future of Web, will publish soon by Al-Imam Muhammad Ibn Saud Islamic University (KSA).

I worked in journalism since 2003. I'm the Co-founder and Editor in Chief of a literary journal (Tanjah Al-adabiya). I Co-authoring a short stories book (Things occur, 2004) and published an other (Postponed suicide, 2007). I'm also win a number of prizes in poetry competitions in the years 2002-2005.

I am currently interested in the development of web sites, management of web communities, marketing and new media.
