Richad Mitha’s Summary

Richad brings to companies his experience in the Digital TV, Mobile TV and Web 2.0 Video services.

In his latest position, he was the VP Business Development of Eyeka Asia a Web 2.0 Community of Creative Talents, where he developed Eyeka's network of Agencies.

In his previous roles, he spearheaded the development of Thales Broadcast & Multimedia solution for the South East Asian markets. Following the acquisition of Thales B&M by Thomson, he led the digital TV and video networks sales for the South East Asian region.

Richad holds a Master degree in Strategy and International Business Management from ESSEC and an Engineering degree in Telecommunications from INSA Lyon.
