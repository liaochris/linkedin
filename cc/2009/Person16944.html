Charles Alday’s Summary

Operational Excellence Consultant with over forty years in the pipeline and construction industries. From the frontlines to the boardroom, the company works well with all levels of the organization. We design and implement customized solutions for clients who want to achieve operational excellence. Our goals are human performance improvement, organizational learning, and systemic analysis and corrective actions for problems and threats to excellence.

We believe a company and individuals need standards, operations philosophy and policies, processes,procedures and practices. Our goals are "practicing what matters." There are plenty of things that matter, and some companies shift what matters based on events. Using PLENTY as an acronym, Alday Consulting Services, Inc. believes performance, learning, excellence, nurture, thinking and you always matter.

Charles Alday’s Specialties:

Organizational development, human factors, change management, positive culture change, quality improvement, incident analysis and prevention, training, lessons learned programs, speeches and presentations, meeting design and facilitation, coaching and mentoring.
