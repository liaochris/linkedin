Richard Miles’s Summary

I help organizations get the results they promised their stakeholders and investors. I help build successful businesses as an entrepreneur, consultant, or employee.

I started my first business in 1980 and sold it to a Fortune 100 company in 1984. We built computerized systems that reduced energy use in highrise office buildings, a "green business" long before the term was coined.

After 5 years managing worldwide sales and marketing in the Fortune 500, I started a consulting firm to help tech companies go from startup to successful venture, writing business plans, raising money, building sales teams, designing advertising, packaging, and websites, and much more. I helped launch 321 Studios from a startup to $50MM in revenues in less than 3 years.

I am happy to have had the opportunity to apply my unique talents in building organizations, increasing reach, scope, impact and emotional immediacy for progressive companies.
