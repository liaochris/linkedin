Mark Nielsen’s Summary

A Manager of Enterprise Architecture with is a blend of over 13 years of industry experience in the fields of network engineering, app development and information technology. Mark possess a sound business acumen working with the business owners, managers and stakeholders in various engagements over the years in transforming their business visions into technological artifacts and leading those efforts to provide robust, extensible and scalable architecture and design frameworks. Champions the idea of aligning technology with business initiatives and had been a catalyst of change in bringing the technology and business teams together.

Mark enjoys to collaborate with business leaders, visionaries and stakeholders at one end and the technology teams on the other to transform the business vision and processes into architecture and design artifacts, mentor and guide the technology team for sucessful implementation of these initiatives. Solid communication, organization and leadership skills capable of communicating with staff in all departments and at all levels.

My core passion is focused on systems management and administration specializing in messaging and collaboration. In the new Web 2.0 era, this passion has carried to focus on social networks and virtual teams.

In addition, I love to research emerging communication technologies, conversing with companies, journalists, industry analysts, business bloggers and other influencers.
