Bill Slawski’s Summary

I am the President of SEO by the Sea, Inc., helping others with:

- Search Engine Optimization
- Local Search Optimization
- Keyword Research
- Linkbuilding
- Content Development & Creation
- Writing & Copywriting

I've been involved in internet marketing and web promotion since 1996, providing consulting services to help people make their web sites easier to find on the internet, and easier to use.

I have extensive experience in developing Search Engine Optimization (SEO) strategies and tactics to help site owners meet the objectives of their sites, to increase traffic to web sites from targeted audiences and increase leads to sites through online direct response marketing, performing keyword research, optimizing site and information architectures, and making web sites search engine friendly.

I've worked on a wide range of sites, from nonprofits to Fortune 500, educational institutions to e-commerce, professional services to consumer goods to business-to-business web sites.

I actively research search-related patent filings and white papers from the search engines, and write about those and other topics related to search and internet marketing on my blog at www.seobythesea.com.

My personal interests include doing what I can to help the environment and nonprofits, and I hope to meet others here who share those interests.
