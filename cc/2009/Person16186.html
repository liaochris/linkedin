Carmen Barker Lemay, Ph.D.’s Summary

Integrative Growth, Inc. is a team of consultants with deep experience and diverse credentials across multiple industries. We are systems oriented thinkers, evaluating and considering how all elements of your business impact success. We are an independent, yet authoritative voice with proven abilities serving energy companies, corporate sustainability teams, government agencies, other management consulting firms, non-profits and small businesses working in the new energy and green economy. We act as either a prime or tier II supplier to our clients.

Integrative Growth supports the green economy through the projects we take on and the way we do them. We are a certified small, woman-owned business by the Women's Business Enterprise National Council (WBENC) and have been recognized by Green America for our environmental and social commitments.
