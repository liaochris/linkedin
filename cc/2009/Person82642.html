Reba Kennedy’s Summary

Reba is a professional writer who takes her 20 years experience as a trial and appellate lawyer in complex business litigation and high-dollar personal injury matters to provide legally related SEO web content to clientele that include many nationally-recognized premier law firms, practicing in a variety of specialty areas.

A graduate of the University of Texas School of Law, she is a member of the prestigious Texas Bar Foundation, American Mensa, and has an AV rating in Martindale-Hubbell.

Reba continues to practice by judicial appointment in the Bexar County Children's Court as a dual role attorney/guardian ad litem for abused and neglected children.

Reba Kennedy’s Specialties:

Search Engine Optimized Legally Related Web Content; legally related articles for print publication; additional focus upon simplicity as a lifestyle, as seen in Reba's nationally syndicated, award-winning blog, http://everydaysimplicity.blogspot.com
