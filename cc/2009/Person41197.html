Ilona Meagher’s Summary

Interested in covering topics important to yet often neglected by society, with a special focus on issues involving the support and reintegration of military veterans and families.

Select highlights from recent years:

- 2008: Received Illinois Journalist of the Year Award-Student Scholarship from NIU.

- 2007: Testified before Congress on the issue of suicide/combat PTSD in returning veterans.

- 2007: Authored Moving a Nation to Care: Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder and America's Returning Troops.

- 2006: Honored with ePluribus Media citizen journalism award category for work on the PTSD Timeline.

- 2006: Featured in the book unConventional: Citizens, Focus and Action.

- 2005: 'Best Post - Finalist' in the blogger's Koufax Awards for "Clinton Moves to End Strike."

Ilona Meagher’s Specialties:

Online research, online journalism, new media content and web development, seo, graphic design, flash development, marketing pieces design, press release copywriting, public speaking, opinion writing, military family advocate, combat ptsd expert
