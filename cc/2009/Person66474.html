Melanie Negrin’s Summary

Melanie R. Negrin is a management-level marketing and development professional with expertise gained from working within large companies and leading her own company. She has partnered with more than 30 nonprofit organizations and entrepreneurial businesses to help increase their visibility, enhance their business building and fundraising efforts, or guide them through a strategic planning and visioning process.

Melanie’s on-the-job know-how is backed by an M.B.A. in Marketing and International Business, a B.A. in Communications & Psychology, and significant volunteer experience working as a member of nonprofit boards.
