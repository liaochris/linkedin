Paula Snyder’s Summary

Paula performed folk & pop music as a soloist, top-40 & oldies dance music in a duet, later returning to nostalgia & show tunes as a soloist again in more recent years.

Her writing has similarly spanned styles from folk to adult contemporary pop, jazz & blues, country-rock, additionally including some child-friendly material. Always a strong vocalist, her instrumentation stayed in the background, allowing the audience to hear the lyrics, and connect with the emotions of any given song presented.

Paula has worn many hats through the years from vagabond to wife, musician to agent, creative soloist to board-member, teacher to parent. Her life experience touches every song and her every song touches someone's life. Since releasing her debut CD "Renewed" in October of 2007, she has been working on a variety of new original pop pieces that range in feel from positive old-fashioned love songs to some contemporary political issues.

Future creative plans include publishing illustrated story-songs, creating music gift products, and writing original
songs/poetry/copy for others.
