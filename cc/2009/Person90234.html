Sebastian Campion’s Summary

I am a designer working in the overlapping area of research, creative planning and design.

I am particularly interested in designing services, experiences and interactions that create synergy between products/services as well as digital/physical worlds.

Projects I have worked on include: viral, mobile and user-generated campaigns, websites and online strategies, public services, future scenarios and spatial installations.

In addition, I am teaching, curating and writing with a focus on new media, creativity and innovation. Occasionally, I exhibit at international media festivals, museums and galleries.

Clients I have worked for/with include:

The National Gallery, Denmark - Museum of Contemporary Art, Denmark - Fiat Auto, Centro Stile Torino - Mindlab - Danish Filminstitute - Danish Broadcast Corporation - Danish Ministry of Foreign Affairs - Danish Minstry of Finance - Danish Enterprise and Construction Authority, Rail Net Denmark, City of Copenhagen.
