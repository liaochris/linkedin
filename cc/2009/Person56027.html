Douglas Klugh’s Summary

As a 20+ year veteran of the IT industry, I have delivered innovative software solutions to various Fortune 500 companies. These solutions include n-tier applications, business intelligence solutions, e-business services (including portal and e-commerce solutions), enterprise data management and administration, project planning and management, consulting, training, and mentoring.

Utilizing my formal education, continued training, and years of experience in nearly all software engineering disciplines, I have successfully led numerous high-profile software projects on platforms ranging from enterprise mainframe systems (including IBM ESA/370) to midrange systems (including Solaris and AIX) to various web platforms (including ASP.NET and J2EE). As a certified specialist in software process, I have mentored various companies in the adoption of agile technologies including Scrum and the Rational Unified Process (RUP).

My financial industry experience includes securities, investments and portfolio management, capital markets, consumer credit products, bank acquisitions, along with compliance auditing for both SEC laws and home equity regulations. Within the insurance industry, I have developed solutions for claims processing, other party liability, customer service administration, and benefits management.

Douglas Klugh’s Specialties:

Delivering business applications utilizing software engineering best practices and applying established software design patterns. I am highly proficient in software engineering disciplines including design, implementation, & management of agile processes, business process modeling, elicitation of various types of functional & strategic requirements, design of various data & software architectures utilizing UML, and coding application software utilizing various programming languages.
