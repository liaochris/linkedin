Brent Clanton’s Summary

Each morning for the past 9-years I have greeted the dawn and my listeners with stories and analysis of the world and economy in which we live. I have helped usher in the day with high-profile guests from all walks of life: Congressmen and comedians, analysts and astronauts, CEO's and policy makers. I am a consummate writer and interviewer, Radio host and confidant for listeners looking for better understanding of how things are, and how we might all live better, more productive lives.

I can tell your story, too.
