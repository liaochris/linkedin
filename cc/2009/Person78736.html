Peter Gray’s Summary

Experienced, globally focused and performance driven senior marketing and sales professional with over 17 years experience in the chemicals adhesives, sealants and coatings industry serving a broad range of end market segments including industrial, construction, transportation, automotive, composites, and consumer hardware. Superb strategist with a solid track-record of creating and leading business unit start-up, realignment and turn-around strategies and tactical plans with world-leading multinational manufacturers. Highly skilled in evaluating current and forward political, economic, social, and technology market trends, customer behaviors, competitive threats, value propositioning, and applying critical judgment towards revenue growth, profitability and customer service excellence. Solid leadership skills exampled though defining, implementing and driving operational, financial, and marketing and sales imperatives, and unifying corporate, regional and professional cultures. Proficient in establishing and aligning brand propositions with customer perceived value. Available for global relocation.

Seeking a new role that leverages and optimizes my international experience, education, and determination to excel.
