Corrado Guidobaldi’s Summary

I’m currently working as System Engineer in H3G Group, employed by Altran. This group manager the handset acceptance procedure, with a special focus on applications and technologies at the state of the art.

Epertise: handset/USIM interoperability analysis, NFC technology, OMA Device Management, OTA Provisioning, Handset and Datacard Engineering, Defect Analysis, Technical Specification Definition, Technology Scouting.

Previously I worked for more than 3 years at ST Incard (ST Microelectronics group) where I gained experience in a multinational and varied market: Smart Card for Mobile (GSM/UMTS), for Digital Identity, and for Digital Signature. I worked for the R&D department and involved in project and development of all the software layers of the Smart Card embedded systems (i.e. OS, File System, Application layer) and Common Criteria certification of Digital Identity and Digital Signature Smart Card based products.

Epertise: Project Manager – Project developement: VAS applications for GSM/UMTS (JavaCard 2.2.1, GSM 03.19, 03.48, 3GPP TS 31.130 (USIM API), ETSI TS 102.241 (UICC API), Smart card OS and FS management, Common Criteria Certification (ISO/IEC 15408), Smartcard ST19 Platform, ISO T0, T1, and Contactless (14443-B) protocols, Crypto: 3DES, RSA, AES, SHA)

Before I joined ST Microelectronics, I got a PhD at University of Napoli, "Federico II" in Computer Science Engineering, more in details in Computer Vision field. During my PhD, I gained international skills in research and teaching.

Expertise: Graph Teory, Pattern Recognition, Expert Systems, Image Processing, Neural networks, OO Programming, International journals referee, Inernational pubblications
