Will 2 B’s Summary

Will 2 B has been with POWER 105.1 FM NYC since before they "Flipped the Switch". When the opportunity arose to produce one of the most powerfull morning shows that New York City has ever seen, Will 2 B took a step back behind the scenes and grabbed the reins.

"I always knew I would end up in the music business somehow, but who knew it would be in radio?" says Will 2 B. "Someone asked me a question the other day that I couldn't answer. "What song changed your life?" Thats what radio is about to me, changing peoples lives through music and personality!".

Will 2 B respects the "art" of music production and all the new technology that is in play. He loves to hear what the up and coming producers are thinking and what they are making.You can find Will 2 B remixes on-line, hear them in the clubs, or even right here on POWER 105.1 FM.

Will 2 B was also the voice of a major clothing company's ad campaign heard across the country. Will 2 B also voices various commercials that you know and love right here on POWER 105.1 FM.

Growing up in Queens, New York was hard for Will 2 B since he is a huge New York Yankees fan. There is never a day that he doesn't have a Yankee hat on. (and YES he has a full head of hair!)
