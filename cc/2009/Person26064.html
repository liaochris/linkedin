Deryk Harper’s Summary

Worked 10 years in the IT field setting up and managing small business networks. Transitioned to real estate in August 2001. Now entering my 8th year in real estate. Our company specializes in residential real estate resale, rental and property management. Key Locations Property management LLC will handle all of our residential leasing and property management business.
