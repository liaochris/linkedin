Kristen Stein’s Summary

I have been a professional contemporary fine artist since 2000. I currently have artwork online through my website http://www.StudioArtworks.com and a variety of online venues including http://kristensteinfineart.etsy.com and http://kristensteinfineart.imagekind.com. My artwork is also available in galleries and boutiques throughout Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, San Francisco and part of Ohio.

My goal is to continue to create beautiful works of art fueled by passion and imagination. I'd like to further expand my artwork into galleries, boutiques, retail catalogs, and set design for television and motion pictures. I'd also like to broaden the reach of my artwork through licensing and through the help of an artist representative or agent.

In addition to LinkedIn, you can stay up to date on Twitter: http://twitter.com/kristensteinart or facebook: http://artist.to/kristensteinfineart

Kristen Stein’s Specialties:

Contemporary Artist specializing in a wide-range of art and styles:: Series include art deco cubism, modern minimalist atmospheric landscapes, whimsical enchanted gardens and custom whimsical pet portraits. Kristen also creates art jewelry featuring her original artwork and handcrafted wire-wrapped sea glass and glass bead jewelry.

Kristen's work is available through her website, or you can request a custom commissioned painting through her site. http://www.StudioArtworks.com .
