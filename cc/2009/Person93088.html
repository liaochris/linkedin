Solveigh Calderin’s Summary

Over 30 year experiences in all areas of the office: marketing and sales, customer care in all areas, bookkeeping tasks as well as managing the every-day workflows in various industries, in small companies as well as in concerns.

These experiences lead me to hereke carpets.

I felt in love with these awesome and luxurious masterpieces of hand knotting art in the internet and after I saw them in reality, I knew that I will turn my passion into business. After almost for years learning all about these genuine and therefore unique Hereke Carpets, I now launched with my business partner the Hereke Carpets UG to bring these artworks into the world and to give these incredible unique craftmanship the chance to keep the place in the world, what credit is due to him.
