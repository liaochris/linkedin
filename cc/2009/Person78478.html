Perry van Beek’s Summary

Who am I and why am I here? And for that matter, why are you here reading my profile? I’d honestly love to hear it.

So, who am I? The elevator pitch:

I am 39 years old, I live in the Netherlands, I am married and have three children. I have been in international business for 15 years and I have lived in Wales, England, Germany and the US for a total of 11 years. I have travelled to about 40 countries and I speak several foreign languages fluently.

My absolute passion in life is to inspire people to fulfill their dreams! My other passion is travel. To me traveling is about the trip! I know it’s cliché, but hey, isn’t the destination just a part of the journey?

During my career in international business I have tried and failed many, many times. And… I have tried and succeeded perhaps equally as many times. I learnt many valuable lessons along the way, the most important being to always, always follow my heart and my gut!
_________________________________

So, in an effort to combine my experiences with my passions, I decided to establish Beekery Sales Coaching. Beekery offers a three-month, feet-to-the fire, sales coaching program called:

”The Seven steps to Servant Salesmanship.”

At the end of the program, participants will:
• understand the very basics of sales again,
• understand how to maximize the results for the client, for the company and for themselves,
• understand the positive difference they are making for others whilst achieving their own goals,
• understand how to be truly committed to their own goals,
• have (re)discovered how much fun sales can be…
_________________________________

Are you still here? Excellent! Seriously, I love to meet and be inspired by new people in my life, so let’s get in touch! You can contact me directly at perry at beekery dot com. I genuinely look forward to it!
