Peter Abraham’s Summary

I've been in the internet and creative industry for the past 10 years +
New technologies are driving rapid change, but it doesn't have to be high tech and highly rich content to create massive change and interaction. Currently i'm particularly interested in mobile and social/business networks, wifi and how the 'mobile web' will transform a world which was wired and static into one which is always on, mobile and accessible to all.

I started as a graphic designer, got caught up with the web in '97 implementing web project management, website implementation and on-line marketing strategy. I was Digital Services Director at a leading Digital Agency in London. My knowledge spans websites and all of the marketing mediums that support a presence online.

Currently I work at Econsultancy.com where we have over 70,000 professionals like you relying on our insight and maximising their network. If you're a Marketer or e-commerce professional check out Econsultancy.com

Peter Abraham’s Specialties:

I have a good understanding of Online Marketing and E-commerce: branding, design and implementation, tracking and reporting, with specific focus on data capture, transfer, security, protection and database management.
Social Media, Search, Affiliate, Email, Web Analytics, Usability, Online Adverising, Online Customer Service, Web 2.0 and Mobile are all very familiar to me.
I'm currently studying an MSc in Digital Marketing through E-consultancy and Manchester Metropolitan University.
