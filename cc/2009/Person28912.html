Dylan Wan’s Summary

Dylan Wan is working as a senior development manager for Oracle BI Applications at Oracle He has more then 10 year experiences in Oracle E-Business suite application development. Since joining Oracle in 1996, Dylan has worked in a number of Oracle applications, including Oracle Financials architecture, Multi-Org architecture, Shared Services architecture, Trading Community architecture, Customer Data Hub, and Oracle Projects.

He played a key role in introducing the Trading Community Architecture into Oracle E-Business Suite. He is also a founding member of Oracle Project Portfolio Analysis product. Dylan holds several professional certifications, including Oracle Certified Professional (OCP) of R11 Application Consultant, in both Procurement and Fulfillment tracks from Oracle. a certified Project Management Professional (PMP) from Project Management Institute (PMI), and a certified Earned Value Professional (EVP) from Association for the Advancement of Cost Engineering (AACE).

He is a contributor to the book Multiple Organizations in Oracle Applications, and a co-author of the B2B e-Marketplace Guide.

Dylan has a master degree in information system from Heinz School, Carnegie Mellon University, and a bachelor degree in statistics from National Chengchi University, Taiwan.
