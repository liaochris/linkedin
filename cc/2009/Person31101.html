Eric Fowles’s Summary

Voltage LTD is a boutique advertising and design firm specializing in high powered creative thinking and graphic design. We believe small is the new big, ideas are the gravy train, and lightning bolts are cool. We produce and design websites, microsites, digital and viral advertising, packaging, publications, identities, brochures, and all around branded collateral and goods.
