Chris Noeth’s Summary

My name is Chris Noeth. I’m a freelance comic book artist from Germany specialized in creating entertainment art with many years of experience in the game industry.

I’m working as an artist professionally in the game biz since 1999. I do everything from thumbnails, pixel graphics, pixel animations, concept art, character design, environment design, storyboards, illustrations, digital paintings to 3D modelling, texturing and animation.

Some of the PC games I worked on are Lula3D, Far West and Railroad Pioneer. Some of the mobile games I worked on as a lead artist: Ancient Ruins, Space Guerillas, Pirates Ahoy, Townsmen 2, Townsmen 3 (this game got 14 awards with graphics done 100% by me), Townsmen Racing, Flitzer, Glory of the Roman Empire, Funky Monkey, My Model Train… and many many more.

Since May 2007 I'm working as a freelance artist in comics and illustration.
My projects include:
The creator owned comic 'Alpha Bettie', written by IDW editor Dan Taylor.
'Tales from the Crypt' comics for Papercutz.
'It came from beneath the sea... again!' Ray Harryhausen Presents comic for Bluewater Productions Inc.

I'm also working as an author on different game- and art-related workshops for magazines like the 3D magazine 'Creative Live' and the biggest German game developer magazine 'GameStar/dev' and I'm teaching a course at the University Of Applied Sciences in Wuerzburg about '3D modelling and animation'.

I'm the cover artist on the new horror / sci-fi spinoff series to Maddrax 'Das Volk der Tiefe' from Bastei.

For updates on my projects and to see some art please visit my online blog at:
www.chrisnoeth.com

You can contact me via mail at:
chris@chrisnoeth.com

Chris Noeth, August 2007
