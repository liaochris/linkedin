Roy Blumenthal’s Summary

I make pictures of meetings, seminars and workshops in realtime, on my tablet pc. These can be projected live on the wall. And they can be digitally printed the size of a door. Or bigger. Or smaller.

The field of visual facilitation is all about helping people to anchor the learnings and concepts in an event.

I charge between R8 500 and R12 500 per session, depending on a whole bunch of factors that we can discuss in person if you need to look into using me in this role.

(If you're a Not-For-Profit, I charge a flat rate of R5 000 per day or part thereof for my services. Call me to discuss. My number is 074 104 6386.)

---

I also run creativity workshops, and operate as a creativity consultant.

---

Not to mention my ongoing work in writing and directing corporate videos, industrial theatre, and specialised communications. One of my key clients in this field is BLUE MOON.

Roy Blumenthal’s Specialties:

Brainstorming, troubleshooting, meeting-effectiveness. I help people liberate their innate creativity. Even if they think they're not all that creative to start with. They're amazed at themselves. When I do visual facilitations, clients get a very powerful 'different angle' on the content.
