Chris McDermott’s Summary

I lead implementation and development efforts for MediaRich, Firecat Studios and Interactive Ensemble, as well as other companies in the San Antonio/Austin area. A true generalist, I excel at web application design as well as information and technical architecture. I have an uncanny knack for asking the right questions at the right time, solving an underlying problem that makes lots of other problems go away. Insatiably curious and detail-oriented, I chases down the details and the final answers.

I honed these skills working the web since 1994, and multimedia and 3D modeling applications before that. I hold a B.S. in Computer Science from the University of Texas, San Antonio. But what really got me started was my first computer, the TI994A home "pc" (with data recorder) soon to be followed in 84' by the Commodore SX64 (yeah, the first full-color portable computer) which I still own in working condition.

Thanks, and as always, have fun!

McD :)
