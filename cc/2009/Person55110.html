Kevin Leversee’s Summary

“Everyone has been made for some particular work, and the DESIRE for that work has been put in every heart.”-Rumi

I Currently hold two positions one as Co-Founder at ManageSocial a Social Media Services Company, the other (which is a passion of mine) as Co-Founder of Manifest IT, a non-profit organisation that seeks to enable individuals to reach their full potential through technology. I personally believe that every human has the capacity for greatness no matter their economic or cultural situation. Whatever a person can dream they can do.

I have 15 years working in Information Technology and over 12 years in a Sales and Marketing Role. ManageSocial is my fifth start-up company and I love innovation, passionate people and no nonsense solutions that work. I love being an evangelist on the edge, from building social networks, to participating in the first blog conference in Australia, Blogtalk Downunder 2005, and led the first Web 2.0 Conference in the Philippines 2006.

I speak publicly on the Open Mesh, Social Media, Web 2.0, Social Networks, and Digital Lifestyle Integration. I teach as often as I can to help enable and empower people on a personal level.
