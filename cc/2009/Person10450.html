Robert S. Bennett’s Summary

Attorney Robert S. "Bob" Bennett is certified as a specialist in consumer and commercial law by the Texas Board of Legal Specialization. There are fewer than 30 Texas attorneys presently certified in consumer law. Such a distinction marks an attorney as being exceptionally qualified in both expertise and experience in a particular field. Nationally, no other state in the United States has this type of certification.

It means, specifically, that Mr. Bennett, who has been practicing law for over 25 years, has counseled and represented clients in numerous consumer claims; he has regularly attended consumer law seminars to keep his legal training up to date. In addition, Mr. Bennett had to be evaluated by fellow lawyers and judges and pass a day-long written examination by the Texas Board of Legal Specialization.

Mr. Bennett is a owner of the Bennett Law Firm, L.L.P. The attorneys in this law firm are especially proficient in cases involving, matters before the Office of Chief Disciplinary Counsel, the Texas Medical Board, the State Board of Law Examiners, and other Boards involving professionals and DTPA violations, ethics defense,... and white-collar criminal defense.

The Firm's website, www.bennettlawfirm.com has an extensive listing of professional articles that may help with your specific problem or concern.
