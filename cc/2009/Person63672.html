Mark Storey’s Summary

CERTIFIED NETWORKER of COLUMBUS
Certified Networker (www.cnofcolumbus.com) compels you to:

- understand your business, and why you are in it
- develop a target market so you can focus your resources and be more productive
- develop high level referral resources instead of settling for low quality leads
- measure your networking performance
- And, much, much more!

Navigate to www.cnofcolumbus.com to register for an upcoming preview!

SPEAKER/CORPORATE PRESENTATIONS
I work closely with corporate clients to develop interactive, meaningful, useful, fun presentations and keynote addresses for audiences from 5-500 people. Primary subjects are leadership, management, coaching, sales, and communication.

CAREER COACHING
As a CCMC (Certified Career Management Coach) I work with people who were laid off, downsized, right-sized, fired, feel under-employed, not respected at work, and want to invigorate new life into their current careers. With Head and Heart Career Services coaching, clients discover:
Who they are
What they want to do
What they are best suited to do
Their personal path to a worthwhile job and a fulfilling career.
