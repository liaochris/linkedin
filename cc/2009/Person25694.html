Anthony Demenagas’s Summary

ProFishing Hellas Corp. is one of the leading companies in Greece, dealing with recreation fishing, commercial fishing & aquaculture, diving & spearfishing equipment.
We are a very innovative company, enjoying good reputation within our clientèle who consist of fishing importers/wholesalers & distributors, diving/spearfishing resorts and importers/wholesalers & distributors, commercial fishing companies and aquaculture units.
We are manufacturing fishing tackles for recreation anglers, also under OEM/ODM basis and distributing some strictly selected brands, while we are shareholders in HelNet SA and BNP Corp.

HelNet, is our manufacturing unit-company for our commercial fishing sector, mainly for commercial fish netting and fish farming cages. Is a very high-reputation company with several innovation breakthroughs and patent registers during its history. The production is considered as state-of-the-art.

BNP Corp. is our manufacturing unit-company for our diving/spearfishing sector. The production is mainly for diving/spearfishing and triathlon/watersport uniforms (under the PROTEUS brand), and is also considered as state-of-the-art and applied with many innovative materials and manufacturing ways, making "hi-end" products.
Regarding the spearfishing equipment, also in the same manner, the production comes under the famous brand of EUROBALCO, well known in as much as 50 countries around the world.

New, innovative and joint projects are always in our interest.
Distributors' inquiries are welcomed.
