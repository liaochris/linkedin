John Walker’s Experience

  • Program Director

    Business Enterprise Mapping Inc. (BEM)

    (Privately Held; Management Consulting industry)

    August 1996 — Present (13 years 4 months)

    Only now is process-based business management (BPM) becoming widely acknowledged to be ESSENTIAL to the efficient and effective management of 21st century enterprises. Most (80+%) enterprises are still managed traditionally, i.e. as collections of siloed departments, and not as networks of inter-related processes.

    BEM is a 15 year thought-leader in BPM.

    BEM's proprietary, process mapping-based methodology offers a proven, business-centric (not IT-centric) approach. We GUARANTEE the rapid, sustainable transformation of both enterprise performance and culture while delivering at least two sequential, 'disruptive' quantum leaps in business management maturity in rapid succession. As opposed to being 'just another quality project' this is tantamount changing the enterprise's DNA to create a new state-of-control as the foundation and framework on which to deliver continuous improvement and/or confidently define and drive significant strategic enterprise projects (e.g. M&A, outsourcing, enterprise IT implementations (ERP, CRM, etc), global strategic alignment, compliance to regulations and standards etc..)

    Our signature "We teach YOU to fish!" approach guarantees sustainable gains via a fully-engaged and trained workforce. Cultural/morale transformation then complements the inevitable performance transformation.

    The fully-integrated BEM methodology provides clients with one-stop shopping for:
    - JIT delivery of training
    - facilitated sessions to apply the new material.
    - skillfully balanced integration of popular BPI tools - (e.g. Lean, Six-sigma, Value Analysis etc.)
    - fully-integrated program coordination management
    - performance guarantee not possible with disparate vendors
    - guaranteed pricing
    - distillation of 15 years of proven best practices
    - minimum 5:1 first year ROI

    Over 200 successful clients, many household names, from over 60 different industry segments, bear witness to the power of this methodology.

  • VP Engineering & Quality

    TechniKrom Inc.

    (Industrial Automation industry)

    2002 — 2007 (5 years )

    Positioned this small, privately-owned supplier of custom-designed, highly-sophisticated pharmaceutical manufacturing equipment for significant growth and eventual acquisition. Created a Quality Management System that invariably 'aced' external client audits, thereby allowing us to compete against much larger competitors whose quality audit results were invariably much lower.
