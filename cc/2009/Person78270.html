Pedro Trentin’s Summary

Pedro Trentin is the Founder and Managing Director of GreenVenture Partners, an entrepreneurial merchant banking firm for the Agribusiness and Renewable Energy industries.

GreenVenture Partners act as an intermediary in capital raising efforts, mergers and acquisitions (M&A) transactions, and provides other financial and strategic advisory services.

Mr. Trentin is responsible for general management of the company, strategic planning, and deal origination/execution. He is a specialist in agribusiness and renewable energy (mainly related to biomass and biofuels - biodiesel and ethanol) and Corporate and Project finance.

Previously to this, Mr. Trentin was advisor in several consulting projects in the brazilian agribusiness industry. He holds a BS in Agribusiness Economics and Management and a MBA in Information Resources Management (focused on Agribusiness) from UFLA/UFJF.
