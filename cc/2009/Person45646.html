Jeff Ullman’s Summary

First half of my adult work life: Help singles in search of a life partner, co-created & exec produce 3 television shows; author two books; raise million$ for charities; co-create & manage numerous online ventures.

Now....it's conventional to look for a mate online. But...back in 1975? Then, it seemed to some that people who joined Great Expectations video dating, were thought by some others as romantically-challenged (In other words: Do not touch them with a 10-foot pole.) We were the pioneers of new courtship rituals; the ones who placed a greater priority on actually living life with the one we loved. We were okay with letting others complain about their frustration with spending too many personal resources looking for the right person. The silly logic of the frustrated was: "No one of quality who sincerely desires a quality relationship would meet THAT way," Oh yeah? Tell that to the 50,000 couples who met & married through my several creations: Great Expectations and Greater Relations Worldwide; Co-creator: Love Connections (12 seasons) and Taildaters (4 seasons); Author (The Singles Almanac; 12 Secrets for Helping Find Love & Commitment; The Workaholic's Lost Love Life).

I was honored to work with fine co-workers, franchisees, and vendors who helped make so many peoples' lives so romantically enriched.

Transitional portion of my adult work life: Many dot com (and some dot.gone) companies.
1. Design and develop glamorous Hollywood, CA residences updated from Hollywood's Golden Years, with 21st Century style and technology.
2. Help couples stay happily in love: www.HappierCouples.com (3Q 09); and
3. Co-Creator and Co-Executive Producer of even more television game and reality lifestyle shows.

In a summary: In the 20th Century I helped more singles meet and marry than anyone. In the 21st Century with my Co-Chief Happiness Officer wife, will help more couples live happier ever after...
