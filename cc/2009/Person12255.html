Bob Pearce’s Summary

Over two decades of experience as a creative television photojournalist with an emphasis on special projects. Expertise in storytelling through extensive National Press Photographers Association training. Winner of multiple broadcast news, photography, and editing awards, including two Emmys and an Edward R. Murrow Award.

Bob Pearce’s Specialties:

Experience with a variety of cameras and video formats, from 3/4 to Beta SP to Sony SX to DVC Pro and the Sony XD. A talent for composition, creative editing, special lighting and imaginative reporter stand-ups. Proficiency with digital editing on the Sony DNE, Avid Media Composer, and Avid Newscutter Adrenaline. Additional Experience operating Electronic News Gathering microwave trucks.
