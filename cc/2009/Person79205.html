Phil Ayres’s Summary

Phil Ayres is versatile software industry professional and product manager who helps organizations meet their business goals through the use of innovative process and content management solutions. He recently served as Director of Product Management for Global 360, Inc., where he identified the biggest business challenges of major financial services and government organizations, driving the development of new solutions and software products to address them.

Phil has over 12 years experience working with leading organizations worldwide to develop solutions that focus equally on business goals and enabling technology. Originally from London, Phil relocated to Boston 5 years ago, after a stint of travel to expand his appreciation of language, culture and the few remaining unspoilt places on the planet. Prior to joining Global 360, Phil worked for Vignette, Tower Technology and EDS, interacting with business leaders to identify the problems that were driving the need for process and content management technology.

Phil Ayres’s Specialties:

Software product and solutions management, architecture, strategy and marketing related to BPM and ECM products.

Startup of solutions for business issues including compliance and governance, healthcare, records / document and business process management .

Sales engineering — technical lead in sales proposals and presentations

Software implementation lead, including direct software skills in systems architecture and software development
