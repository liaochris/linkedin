David Chambers’s Summary

David is a compensation, benefits and reward communications specialist, with a particular focus on employer brand and total reward communication, strategy and training. David works as a Senior Communications Consultant with Watson Wyatt, following an 11 month assignment as the European Reward Manager with the same firm.

David is an expat Kiwi, who has lived in the UK for the last 9 years.
