Kevin Dean’s Summary

I have been performing locally and regionally since 1989. I began touring nationally/internationally in 2002 when I joined Brand New Sin.

Tours:
UK tour with Soil, Godhead and Lennon in 2007
North American tour with Type O Negative and Celtic Frost in 2007
North American tour with Mushroomhead, Soil, and Autumn Offering in 2006
East Coast/ Mid-West tour with Motorhead and Corrosion of Conformity in 2005
North American tour with Black Label Society in 2005
Two dates with Slayer, Killswitch Engage, and Mastadon in 2005
North American tour with Saliva, Breaking Benjamin and Greenwheel in 2003
East Coast Tour with Clutch and Suplecs in 2003
Show archive: http://brandnewsin.com/

Kevin Dean’s Specialties:

Drum set, piano: I teach private drum lessons and beginner piano lessons. Before joining Brand New Sin I was a drum instructor at The Music Center in Syracuse, teaching as many as 54 students per week. I am also a session drummer in the Syracuse, NY area.

  • Drum Instructor

    Brand New Sin

    (Music industry)

    January 1991 — Present (18 years 11 months)

    Drum Teacher Syracuse, NY:
    I have been teaching drums both full time and part time since my college years (1991-1993). I try my best to keep drummers drumming. It is tragic to see a musician give up. I want my students to continue to grow without getting bored. I try to give them the most extensive music vocabulary they can have. I design lessons that are relevant to the student's style and tastes in music.

    Details:

    *I charge $15 per half hour lesson and $30 per hour lesson.
    *I will travel to your home (within Onondaga county - Further destinations are negotiable)
    *I focus on: reading, technique, coordination, timing (click track), music theory
    *Styles: concert/orchestral snare, rudimentary snare, drum corps, Pop, Rock, Latin, Blues, Jazz, Reggae, Hard Rock, Metal, Funk, Dance, Industrial and more

    If you are serious about drumming and you live in the Syracuse area please contact me at: kevinjamesdean@hotmail.com or 315-849-4920.
