Brandy Tanner’s Summary

After working outside of the home for 10 years in various office type positions such as office clerk and administrative assistant, I decided to start my own virtual assistant & unique marketing business. From my private home office I am able work hard to provide any size business the office help it requires. I have advanced multi-tasking skills, which enables me to handle heavy work loads and still meet each clients deadlines.

I am also passionate about writing, this is a skill I seem to have been born with. I started writing poems around twelve years of age and have continued my writing in the form of blog posts and articles for online communities as well as on my own personal blog.

My goal is to gain clients who require my expertise in any area pertaining to my virtual assistant & unique marketing business or my blog.

If you asked what my three best qualities are, I would respond with the fact that I have an outgoing personality, a friendly voice, and the ability to be multi-talented.
