David Brown’s Experience

  • Co-founder

    Better Looking Records

    (Music industry)

    March 2000 — Present (9 years 5 months)

    Established successful independent record company. Better Looking Records has built a large catalog and sold over two-hundred thousand records worldwide. Excelled in artist development, digital strategies, marketing, publicity, advertising, graphic identity, TV/film licensing and video production. A key highlight was booking and licensing several clients on Conan O’Brian, David Letterman, The OC, The Real World, Grey’s Anatomy, Nip/Tuck, Oprah Winfrey, Rockstar Games, and national Cadillac and Hummer commercials. Additionally, a national marketing and distribution deal with Warner Brothers Records was established in 2004.

  • Founder, Creative Director

    Holiday Matinee

    (Privately Held; Online Media industry)

    February 1999 — Present (10 years 6 months)

    Holiday Matinee is an organization dedicated to promoting creativity through our blog, charitable events and consultative work.

    We've executed successful campaigns for Fox Television, DreamWorks, Urban Outfitters, MTV, Warner Brothers, San Diego Museum of Contemporary Art, San Diego Padres, Starbucks, Microsoft and hundreds of bands (including Death Cab For Cutie, Jimmy Eat World, Sigur Ros, Cat Power, Interpol, Bright Eyes, The Album Leaf). Among the company’s successes was the ability to secure high profile record label, publishing, licensing, and digital rights contracts nationally and abroad (USA, Asia, Europe) as well as produce compelling documentary films (MOOG, Kumar, Boxers & Ballerinas).

    Visit us at www.holidaymatinee.com
