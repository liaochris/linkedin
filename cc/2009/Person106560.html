Zac Hinton’s Summary

Founding partner and joint owner of JZ Partners, a business advisory and technical implementation consultancy serving the financial services industry in mainland China and Hong Kong. Particular expertise in credit risk, operational risk, Basel II and regulatory compliance, having worked for clients in Europe, the US and Asia.

An inquisitive and enterprising individual, always open to meeting new people, connecting people together and providing advice and help when requested.
