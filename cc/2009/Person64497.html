Massimo Carraro’s Summary

Massimo is a copywriter, in love with the task of communicating.
His company, Monkey Business, is devoted to crafting the right message to the right people, using the right language.
Assignments include international brands and friends' little companies, both online and offline.
Oh, at Monkey Business we also enjoy coworking, since 2008.
