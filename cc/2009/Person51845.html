John Tuvey’s Summary

You may remember John Tuvey from the pages of Fantasy Football Weekly or a variety of fantasy sports sites; now he serves as the Senior NFL Analyst at The Huddle. John has been employed in the fantasy sports industry since 1999, and his work has been featured in USA Today's Sports Weekly as well as on AOL, Yahoo, Fantasy Football Champs, Fantasy Football Trader, Draft Stock, Fantasy Players, Fanball and RotoTimes, just to name a few.

John was a finalist for the Fantasy Sports Writers Association title of Fantasy Football Writer of the Year for 2007, so he must be pretty good.

John also co-hosts the popular Fantasy Football Weekly radio show with Paul Charchian, Bo Mitchell, and Christian Peterson on KFAN in Minneapolis. He has also appeared on FOX Sports Radio nationwide with JT the Brick as well as radio stations in St. Cloud, MN; Lawrence, KS; Pittsburgh, PA: Tampa, FL; Baltimore, MD; Honolulu, HI; Omaha, NE; and New Ulm, MN. Additionally, John and Bo—along with producer Anthony Maggio—co-hosted the Fantasy Football Pants Party, which (along with Fantasy Football Weekly) can be found on iTunes.
