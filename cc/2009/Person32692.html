Kenneth Farrall’s Summary

Ph.D. (ABD) at Annenberg School for Communications, University of Pennsylvania. Research expertise in social networks, electronic communications technology, privacy and surveillance. Internet entrepreneur. Eight years experience consulting for and managing Internet companies in China. Interested in academic or private sector positions focusing on data privacy and surveillance issues.
