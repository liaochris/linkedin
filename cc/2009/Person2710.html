Jennifer Thompson’s Summary

I am a veteran problem solver and courageous leader with more than 8 years experience managing the production and delivery of hundreds of industry leading, critically acclaimed, and award-winning multimedia education products. I also have more than 10 years of deadline-oriented management, design, photography, writing, and editing experience in the fields of journalism and print publishing.   

Almost all of my experience has come from getting new endeavors off the ground or from revamping existing operations. I've been instrumental in helping a startup product development company, several new print departments, magazines, newspapers and more thrive and reach strategic goals in short order. Drop me into any situation and I will analyze, collaborate, and then orchestrate successful outcomes, earning the trust and respect of my colleagues, clients, and vendors along the way. I am passionate about teamwork and at my best leading creative professionals through difficult challenges.
