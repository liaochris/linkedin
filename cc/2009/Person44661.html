Jane Baker’s Summary

Over the years the extent of my experience has given me opportunities to create outdoor signage, trade show booths, consumer packaging, corporate brochure collateral and templates, style guides, advertisements, web banners, presentation graphics, B2B2C campaign materials—just about anything you can think of which has allowed me to become a “jack-of-all-trades” in the world of graphic design. The joy is the great client satisfaction and feedback after ability using my experience and knowledge to help them succeed and achieve there goals.

On the horizon I look to expanding my knowledge base with learning more interactive programs such as Flash and Dreamweaver to be able to more than adequately apply my design skills to the Web as well.
