Ann Drinkwater’s Summary

17 years professional experience with 10 years specializing in technology project and program management (exceeding $8M); full eLearning and software development lifecycle management for high profile clients such as: FEMA, Martha Stewart Living Omnimedia, American Diabetes Association, Arthritis Foundation, Reba McEntire, National Guard, Ulla Popken - US and International, Sky & Telescope Publishing

Active author in the project management community with published work for Cutter Consortium, Project Connections, Projects at Work, PM Boulevard and eZine Articles as well as a monthly PM blog columnist for Project Connections
