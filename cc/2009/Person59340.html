Ron McManmon’s Summary

ronm@linkedcv.com

"Follow the passion, and you will be successful in every way!"

Ron is the visionary and Architect of the "Career Marketing Program" (CMP). A successful sales and marketing process developed and integrated into a comprehensive technical solution that helps people take control of their careers. Ron has been in Executive Search and coaching people on all levels for the past decade and this is his passion.

2007 the CMP was purchased by NextJob, a division of the Hire Calling Holding Company. The results are the development and launch of both NextJob and LinkedCV, which is coming soon.

2002-2007 Owner: MTR Executive Search

Ron's past experience includes but is not limited to:

May 2001- Ranked the #1 MRI Recruiter (out of 5000 recruiters) in the Pacific Region within 18 months from start and maintained a Top 5% status, nationwide.

Back in the old days: Ron's career sales exceed $300 million. While the majority of these sales were 'Grandfathered-In" Ron's achievements in this sector of his career was providing tireless Sales and Marketing support and training to thousands of customers. This said, Ron is a hunter but lives by the saying,"anyone can sell product in, but the art is helping your customers sell it through"!

FOR FUN: Ron searched for the Santiago El Grande (a Spanish galleon) on the Bahamas Banks for over six months resulting in finding 4 ships (that had already been found). Additionally, prepared a complete business prospectus, on-site evaluation and operations process for the archaeological excavation of King Louis XIV’s Royal fleet, which sank off the coast of Venezuela in 1678. The excavation was coordinated in conjunction with the government of Venezuela.
