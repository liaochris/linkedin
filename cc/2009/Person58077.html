Lawrence R. Gelber’s Summary

I litigate securities and stock market disputes in court and in arbitration. Complete details about my practice and background are on my website at www.GelberLaw.net.

I have been developing an "encyclopedic" glossary of securities industry related terms, called the GelberLaw Glossary, also located on my website at www.GelberLaw.net/Glossary.

Lawrence R. Gelber’s Specialties:

My practice focuses on securities arbitration, securities regulatory agency defense and defense of registered representatives in adminsitrative proceedings.
