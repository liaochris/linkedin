I am the Online Media Manager for S&A Cherokee, based in Cary, NC. In this role, I oversee all of S&A Cherokee's online media, which includes a network of 11 Web sites, an online advertising network, e-mail marketing and Web/e-mail analytics.

My background is quite diverse ranging from experience in politics, communication, state and local government and, now, new media.
