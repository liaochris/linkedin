    (Music industry)

    December 1992 — May 1997 (4 years 6 months)

    Based in southern California, Cosmic Voodoo was on innovative band in the early Psychobilly sub-culture. The band featured Jeff Roffredo on upright bass, who went on to play in Tiger Army. The band released one 3 song EP on English label Raucous Records , and a posthumous CD collecting rare and unreleased studio tracks on Kaiser Records.
