Mike Panetta’s Summary

Mike is currently Vice President for Public Affairs and Emerging Media at Grassroots Enterprise where he manages a number of the firm's non-profit, trade association, and political clients and leads the embrace of new technology for political, marketing and advocacy purposes.

In November 2006, Mike was elected to the position of U.S. Representative in the the District of Columbia. The position is often referred to as the "Shadow Representative" and was re-elected in 2008 by a large margin. In this role, Mike is a leading voice in the fight to give the District of Columbia statehood and votes in the U.S. Congress.
