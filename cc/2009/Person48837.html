Johan Post’s Summary

At the moment I am a fourth and last year student Communication & Multimedia Design at the Rotterdam University of Applied Science, where I work towards my Examination Project. I am specializing myself with my minor in the field of User Experience Design, next to my expertise’s Interface Design and Graphic Design.

In the third year of my study, during my internship, I worked as Interaction Designer at Green Valley, in Delft. They work on CMS’s for different kinds of large companies and organizations. I had the possibility to work on clients like ‘Consuwijzer’ and ‘Ministerie van Economische Zaken’ (both government based organizations: consumers advice and the economics ministry). I mostly worked on Interface Design related projects and Functional Design matters.

Next to my study I recently started my own company, called ‘Wit Licht Webdesign’. In english 'Wit Licht' stands for white light. I focus mostly on Designing and Building Websites and Visual Brand Identities. Since the start of ‘Wit Licht’ I work on an a couple of projects for different companies and organizations.

Interface Design, Graphic Design, Interaction Design, Usability.
