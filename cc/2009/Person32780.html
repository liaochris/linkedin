Francois Bedard’s Summary

Linking brands to audiences, understand behaviors and drive revenues.

Cumulating more than 14 years in digital media, François Bédard is recognized like one of the pioneers of the sector in Montreal and also has a keynote speaker. Francois is married to Anouk and they have 3 children (Lili, Romy and Emile). He worked has an Associate director, strategic alliances at SYMPATICO.MSN and was a strategic adviser / consultant, marketing director and senior account executive for technological groups like NOVIPRO/IBM, DVcolor and also for Public Technologies Multimedia with the creation of the MyVirtualModel.com . He also launches new media divisions for advertising agency like PALM Publicité, Publicis and Keyrus in Paris.

Invited guest speaker at MIT for a workshop on Advanced Television. http://cips.mit.edu/09.01.21NX_TV_Wksp.htm
