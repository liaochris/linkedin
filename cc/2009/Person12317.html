Bo Carrington’s Summary

I am an experienced business leader with a track record of hands-on experience in achieving business results through creating common sense alignment between human capital processes and business objectives.

I am a forward thinking professional that works hard to maintain and exhibit superior communication/presentation skills and to maintain an in-depth awareness of multicultural issues. I also have a proven ability to influence and lead in fast paced and ever changing environments.

My clients would hopefully say that I am committed to investing in our future today by helping companies develop and execute the processes centered on identifying and growing the leaders of tomorrow.

My goal in life is the truly make a difference for my kids, and their kids using the abilities that I have been gifted with. It's not about fame or glory, but truly about doing all that I can do while I am here to do it!
