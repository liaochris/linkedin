Marc Rudov’s Summary

I am the WhiteNoise Doctor™: a branding-strategy advisor, the creator of GutShare™, and an internationally known media personality.

I have exec-level experience in marketing, sales, business development, and capital-raising in the technology, movie, and publishing domains. In addition, I am a regular guest on radio talkshows around the world and on Fox News Channel -- mainly "Your World with Neil Cavuto" and "The O'Reilly Factor."

Recently helped a patent-law firm improve its messaging and customer-acquisition strategy, and did the same for a marketing/sales-outsourcing startup.

Past engagements include helping an animation studio line up Wall Street investors and retail merchandising partners, as well as helping a VC raise a first institutional round of $63M ($40M of that coming from Harvard University).
