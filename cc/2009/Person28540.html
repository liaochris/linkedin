Darren Tessitore’s Summary

Thinking Phone Networks developed a unique hosted communications platform, engineered to integrate voice and business applications such as CRM and Business Intelligence.

To date, most businesses have taken a telecom centric approach to their communications strategy, attempting to bolt-on applications to legacy infrastructure - which is costly, time consuming, and prone to failure. What makes us different is our application centric, open systems based approach to the problem of unified communications, which is not hampered by legacy constraints.

We deliver our services in five, fully redundant data centers which are strategically located across the country, enabling us to optimize reliability and quality of service for each of our customers, regardless of physical location.

Ultimately, Thinking Phone Networks is the only company that offers a fully hosted communications platform that seamlessly integrates voice and business applications. Our unique partnering with specific data carriers around the World allow us to provide Hosted VoIP solutions anywhere with true QoS and a 99.999% uptime guarantee.
