Elyse Bruce’s Summary

Elyse Bruce’s Experience

  • Founder and Creator

    MIDNIGHT IN CHICAGO

    (Fund-Raising industry)

    2006 — Present (3 years )

    MIDNIGHT IN CHICAGO is an international tourism and culture initiative that raises awareness and funds for Autism. We do this by way of a number of activities including an annual Art Competition for under-recognized artists, the "Art From The Heart" and "De la part des beaux arts" events and silent auctions, podcasts on Autism that are FREE to download, keynote addresses at conferences and symposiums and talks at public and private schools.

  • Artist Management

    Artwork By TDT

    (Fine Art industry)

    September 2006 — Present (3 years 3 months)

    Thomas D. Taylor is a professional artist who has been drawing and painting for 30 years. His artwork embellishes subjects via absence of shadow, vibrancy of colours, and smooth, small brushstrokes. His acrylic on canvas paintings range from modern and abstract to surreal but all fall under the artform he has coined "Precise Impressionism."

    Mr. Taylor is a great believer in environmental, humanitarian, animal rights and Autism rights causes and is the co-creator of the International "Midnight In Chicago" Culture and Tourism Initiative raising awareness and funds for Autism. He is also an Administrator for 9 web-based forum groups for people with Asperger Syndrome.

  • Owner

    ELYSE BRUCE

    (Entertainment industry)

    1977 — Present (32 years )

    A composer, songwriter, educator, clinician, performer and author, Elyse is a member of the American Federation of Musicians Local 446 (AFM), the Guild of Canadian Film Composers (GCFC), the Association of Managers of Volunteer Services (AMVS), the Saskatchewan Recording Industry Association (SRIA) and SOCAN.

    She balances her time writing and recording music and audio podcasts, mastering new technology, teaching songwriting and marketing courses, leading the very successful MIDNIGHT IN CHICAGO initiative, raising her totally awesome child with Asperger Syndrome (a form of Autism) and Myasthenia Gravis (a rare complex neuromuscular auto-immune disorder that is 5 times more rare in children and diagnosed at a rate of 1 to 2 per 500,000 children), and thinking up new and exciting projects that involve -- and benefit -- as many people as possible.

    Recent CD Releases include "Quietudes" in 2005, "Midnight in Chicago" in 2006 and "Countdown to Midnight" in 2007.
