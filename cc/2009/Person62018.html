Marcel Jansens’s Summary

Mr Jansens's profile is online, Mr Jansens is online. In his current position as eBusiness Consultant he uses his knowlegde and that of his online network to provide answers to the most difficult online issues. His motto is: "De een ziet een half leeg glas. De ander ziet een half vol glas. Ik vraag me af waar de kraan is! " (sorry that was Dutch)

With his strong communication skills and a great deal of technical experience he can quickly adapt to new environments. He is a good listener who knows to ask the right questions to get the right answers the creative way.

His knowledge and experience lie primarily in the connection between business and IT, focussed on internet.

As eBusiness Consultant, he has been the “spider in the web” for customers like SNS Bank, Bank Mendes Gans, KLM, NS International, RAI, PaperlinX, ConQuaestor, Funda and AutoTrader.

Next to his work as eBusiness Consultant, Mr. Jansens gives workshops and courses on the field of Enterprise Content Management, New Media and Online Marketing.
