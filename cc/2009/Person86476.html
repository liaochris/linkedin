Ross Rylance’s Summary

My grandfather was an architect in Brisbane, Australia. My father was a builder and developer in Australia, Florida, Illinois and Minneapolis. I have grown up around building and selling real estate all my life. My goal is too help enough people that I can start a charity that will help homeless people back into a home! I currently am involved with helping Safe Haven a family homeless shelter here in Nashville.
After two years, I am certified e-Pro and designated ABR, I am working on more personal development through education and active involvement with the Greater Nashville Association of REALTORS working on the Special Events and Young Realtor Committees. In February I was a graduate of Camp 443 and have attended my first Family Reunion with Keller Williams in Las Vegas, February 2007 and my second in Atlanta in February of 2008.

Ross Rylance’s Specialties:
