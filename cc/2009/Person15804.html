Carolyn Williamson, MBA, PMP’s Summary

Carolyn Williamson is a Scientist at Bristol Myers Squibb located at the Biologics facility in Syracuse NY. Carolyn has 11 years of experience in Validation of Biologics. Her experience includes equipment and utility validation as well as process validation. The past 6 years Carolyn has focused on the areas of Cold Chain Qualification, mixing validation, Disposables/Consumables validation, including filters and bioprocess containers.

Carolyn is member of the of the PDA and ISPE and a volunteer for the PDA's Pharmaceutical Cold Chain Interest Group. Carolyn has been a guest speaker on the validation of disposables for both the IBC and PDA. Additionally, Carolyn has provided non-profit organizations with tools on Effective Meeting Management.

Mrs. Williamson received her Bachelor of Science degree at The Catholic University of America in Washington, DC and her MBA at LeMoyne College in Syracuse, NY. She is a certified Project Management Professional since January 2007. Carolyn is committed to service within her community and has been an active member of her local Toastmasters group.
