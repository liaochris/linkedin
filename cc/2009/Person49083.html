John Reasor’s Summary

I have more than 27 years experience in the wood component industry, including estimating, design, sales, office management and shop management.

My goals are to continue to grow and expand my management skills. By using new technology to improve productivity and accuracy in the office and the production environment, giving my customers the best in quality products, timely, accurate designs and on time deliveries.

John Reasor’s Specialties:

Production oriented design of wood components, office management, electronic document storage and control, production related work-flow, man power forecasting and scheduling for on time delivery.
