Ramona d'Viola’s Summary

Freelance writer, columnist and award winning photographer, Ramona d'Viola has garnered a loyal readership throughout the West Coast.

Adept at creating compelling editorial and marketing copy, as well as eye-catching imagery, her work, written and photographic, appears with alarming regularity in regional magazines such as San Diego Home/Garden Lifestyles, San Diego, Riviera, Biz San Diego, What’s Here Visitor’s Guide, and the Concierge Connection magazines.

For the past three decades she has traveled extensively throughout Europe, Central America, Australia and the Caribbean Islands, and has participated and covered noteworthy events such as the 1985 Women's Tour de France, the 2001 Australian Paddleboard World Championships, and the 2003 World Record setting Women's Full Moon Paddle from Cuba to Key West.

A frequent contributor to sports and travel publications, including Adventure Sports Journal, Inside Surf, Surf Life, and Wahine magazines, d'Viola is an accomplished cyclist, surfer, sailor, and a veteran of the United States Marine Corps.
