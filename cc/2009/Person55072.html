Kevin Smith’s Summary

I am currently working as a Test Engineer/Onsite QA Lead at Infosys Technologies Limited. I spent five months training and working in India, and am now deployed to a client site in San Jose, CA (for a leading networking services/hardware firm), as part of their Product Engineering and Validation Solutions group, performing a variety of tasks in the testing/QA field.

I have experience in information technology support in the public sector, allowing me to understand the dynamics and requirements of implementing IT in large governmental and educational institutions. I worked with end-users from a variety of communications and engineering disciplines as well as cultural backgrounds.

In addition, I have had experience within the Media/Advertising industry, primarily on the Media Planning end of things. As a Media Intern for PHD Detroit, Media Agency of Record for DaimlerChrysler Corporation, I helped to plan and execute a comprehensive media plan to launch the 2006 Jeep Commander. Due to my dedication and commitment to quality work, my superiors gave me responsibilities normally reserved for full time Assistant Media Planners.
