Mark Hauler’s Summary

Internationally focused professional with a strong mix of business and technical skills and proven experience in Partner Relationship Management, Client Services and Project Management. My experience includes integrating EDI and ERP systems, consulting on e-Business projects, and contributing to an international project team defining a new Messaging standard for industry.

I began in Sales and Marketing roles but have spent the largest part of my career in systems and operations. I have worked with companies ranging from the Fortune 50 to bare-boned startups; from manufacturing based CPG companies to pure play B2B internet exchanges. The common component in each is a focus on Client Services and acting as a liaison between partners to achieve a win-win for all.

I have also consulted with several European companies to adapt and refine their websites and marketing collateral to reflect a more sophisticated and polished use of the English language. I love working globally,learning languages and hope to continue that as much as possible.
