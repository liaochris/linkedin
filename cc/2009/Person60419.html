Lui Sieh JD PMP’s Summary

Lui Sieh is a seasoned professional with extensive experience in Fortune 100 and startup companies. He has a broad business background focused in program and project management, IT operations management, and client relationship management. Lui is particularly knowledgeable in the areas of project turnaround, business and leadership training, quality assurance and control, knowledge management, and customer relationship management.

Lui graduated from Carnegie Mellon University with a Bachelor of Science. He is a graduate of Brooklyn Law School. Lui is certified as a Project Management Professional (PMP) and Prince 2 (Foundation and Practitioner).
