Christopher Schmitt’s Summary

I have over 20 years of experience in emerging telecommunications technologies, application development, business process management and improvement, and Canadian telecommunication industry regulation. I have a proven track record of helping start-ups and traditional companies develop and implement innovative and forward-looking cost-savings business solutions.

In my spare time I enjoy learning about web and open source technologies, programming, sketching and painting, skate skiing, snowboarding, cycling, cooking, and keeping fit. I have a beautiful wife and three wonderful boys.
