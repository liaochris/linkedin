Raul Almazan’s Summary

Raul Almazan has over 20 years of management, public speaking, and mentoring experience and currently works for Raytheon Missile Systems in Learning & Development as their Learning Management System Lead and a Frontline Leadership Development Instructor. He is on the board of the Tucson Hispanic Chamber of Commerce and Youth Education Security Program, as well as a Steering Committee member on the United Way Inclusion Council. Raul is a bi-lingual diversity advocate whose core competencies are collaborative leadership, strategic planning, project management, process excellence, and business communication. He enjoys sharing these skills with others to help them develop professionally and reach their personal aspirations.
