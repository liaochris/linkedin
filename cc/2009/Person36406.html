Ginger Dodds’s Summary

For over nine years at Shaker Recruitment Advertising and Communications, I have been working with my client base to develop and implement strategic advertising campaigns for their recruitment needs. In addition to recommending specific media for their open positions, I coordinate with my copywriting, creative and interactive teams to ensure my clients' message is consistent with their employer brand. My areas of expertise include: career site development, job board contracts, search engine marketing (SEM), media recommendations and planning, and social media / Web 2.0 strategies. Duties also include writing ads for both print and internet job postings, follow up with my clients, and developing additional business from my current client base as well as new business development.
