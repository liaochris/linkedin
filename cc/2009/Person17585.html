Chris Bailey’s Summary

Chris is an experienced and innovative online community developer who understands how to use technology to cultivate meaningful relationships. In his early career as a nonprofit association membership professional, he led successful initiatives to both attract prospects and retain members. Later, working as an account manager for a leading software company serving the nonprofit sector, he provided clients with expertise to help them build interactive and action-oriented websites for their constituencies.

Today, as both Principal of Gravit8 Social Relationship Marketing and Co-partner of BaileyHill Media, he brings this wealth of expertise, insight, and creative ideas to clients who recognize that engaging online communities provoke meaningful customer connections that lead to higher satisfaction, greater likelihood of purchases and stronger loyalty to their brand.

What makes Gravit8 and BaileyHill Media different from other agencies is an integration of in-depth marketing experience with insights from social media, business anthropology, leadership coaching and industrial design. We take a multidisciplinary and holistic approach that makes us - and you - different from the crowd.
