Brandi Jo Newman (mail@brandijonewman.com)’s Summary

Born into a family of bankers, cattlemen, oilmen and good ol' boys. Brandi Jo learned at an early age how to communicate with different personalities and egos. She started playing golf at age 6 with lessons from her mother, a cattle baroness. She attended Stephen F. Austin State University in pursuit of a forestry degree. She later left college to pursue her dream of playing on the LPGA Tour. After injuries and a drive for something more, she moved to Scottsdale, Arizona and started working with a V.P. at Prudential Securities marketing his financial services practice.

Moving on to A.G. Edwards and Merryll Lynch, Brandi Jo soon learned that the financial services industry is about people and not products. Trust and integrity between client and advisor are the foundation to the relationship.

She founded her own insurance and financial services practice in 2003 to help everyone she can begin to realize their dreams. Taking action is the first step towards accomplishing your dreams. Sometimes there are so many options or choices you cannot move towards your dreams. I give you the tools to decide which option is the best and how that choice will maximize your life.

To work with Brandi Jo you must read a book first. "Becoming Your Own Banker" by R. Nelson Nash is an eye-opening text about the 'flow of money' and how to capitalize your current cash flow. Capital finances your dreams. Capital gives you options.

To order your copy go to www.InfiniteBanking.org.
