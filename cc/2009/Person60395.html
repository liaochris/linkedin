Louis-Philippe Riel’s Summary

Louis-Philippe Riel is a freelance art director living in Montreal. He began his professional career as a designer and flash developer for local and national clients at Infivia, and later on CloudRaker. He then moved on to Diesel Marketing, now Sid Lee, an integrated advertising agency, where his responsibilities increased with international clients such as Red Bull, Cirque du Soleil, Suzuki and adidas. While at Sid Lee, Louis-Philippe excelled as a designer and art director, earning numerous awards such as a Créa, Boomerangs, and FWAs, as well as being featured in the book “Guidelines for Online Success” by Rob Ford.
