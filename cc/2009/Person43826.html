Jason Baer (@jaybaer)’s Summary

Jason Baer is a forest-dwelling restaurant critic with a passion for Tequila and all things social media.

As President of Convince & Convert, Jason consults with corporations and public relations firms, helping them harness the awesome power of social media strategy.

He spreads his "think social media tools last, not first" message through numerous conference speaking gigs, his Convince & Convert blog, and social media training seminars.

Jason spent 15 years running digital marketing agencies, and has consulted for more than 700 companies - and 25 of the Fortune 1000. He's @jaybaer on Twitter.
