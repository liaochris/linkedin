Ian Balina’s Summary

Computer Engineering major at The George Washington University. Experience with building embedded systems and microcontrollers, electronics, computer hardware, networking, and programming.

Co-founder of Leximo (http://leximo.org), a Semantic Multilingual Social Dictionary. The idea behind Leximo is that today's print dictionaries are poised for a transformation in the internet era. If we are given the choice of asking a native speaker the meaning of a word, and asking a scholar the same question, chances are many of us will go for the native speaker.

Research Associate at Diversified Portfolio Strategies LLC. I am responsible for researching alternative investment funds (Hedge Funds, Private Equity, etc) seeking to invest in profitable private companies. I interact with money managers, analysts, and investors. I also assist in the strategic and business planning of the organization in order to enhance operations and long-term profitability.

Part time Social Media Strategist, where I help entrepreneurs and startups better understand how to leverage the internet and social media to their advantage.

Coordinate and manage technology services at Gifted Child Resources Inc., an educational institution with schools all over the country.

Ian Balina’s Specialties:

Technical: Embedded Systems and Devices, Microcontrollers, Networking (TCP/IP, Packet Switching, etc.), Linux, Ruby on Rails, C, C++, Java, MIPS, HTML, CSS, Dreamweaver and WordPress.

Marketing: Social Media Marketing and Optimization (SMO), Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Web Strategy, Product Launches, Internet Marketing and Social Networking.
