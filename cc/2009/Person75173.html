+ MY GOALS:
I am a consultor in brand communication & creative director. Since my background is art direction & graphic design I like to express everything graphically. Always looking the world trough graphic eyes in a deep and funny way most of the times.
As because I am very courious person I like to know about what new technologies can change the conception of relationship between clients and creatives, and how any kind of networks will modify our conception of work.
I like very much working in team, because I do believe that some different points of view always have a better result. Finally, I guess I am a good conductor.

Looking to the future through and with any kind of networking!
