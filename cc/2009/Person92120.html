Simon Ashwin’s Summary

Simon is a senior digital-creative consulting professional with 14 years demonstrable accomplishments across strategy and operations.

A project manager and program manager with demonstrated high levels of leadership and accountability. Managed programs of up to $6M, and staff up to 30.

Experience in publishing, digital, process improvement, change, innovation, CMS, pre and post deal M&A projects combined with an industry specialism in travel, leisure tourism and the environment.

His project management expertise is matched by his experience in design and art management across print & digital. Simon is an accomplished "suit" with experience in branding, usability and information architecture with a focus on consumer and business orientated outcomes.

Innovative problem solving and implementation is his niche. Combining excellent management skills, ability to motivate multi-disciplinary teams to influence change and deliver innovation across businesses. He is also a visual, creative and conceptual thinker. This diversity gives Simon the ability to drive business, creative and technology needs and deliver into a viable, engaging and innovative solutions.
