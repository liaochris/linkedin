Josh Tabin’s Summary

I like building businesses.

Right out of college I built an office supply company right down the street from Office Max, Office Depot and Staples.

In the mid-nineties I built "one stop shopping" markets for Southwestern Bell in Houston and Austin.

After the "dot.com" bubble burst, I helped build Rackspace into the greatest hosting company there is.

In less than two years, I built RigNet into the fastest growing managed communications provider for upstream oil and gas.

For the past three years, I have been consulting with great startup entrepreneurs on both coasts helping guide them to the next level.

Now, I am looking for my next challenge. Is it your business?

Josh Tabin’s Specialties:

Experienced in guiding fast growing start up companies through hyper growth periods. Background in Telecom, Internet ventures, e-commerce, staffing companies, product marketing groups, light industrial and manufacturing, wellness...you name it.
