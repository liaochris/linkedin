Volker Glaeser’s Summary

- Director Internet Services, Vodafone Germany
- Global Head of Online, Vodafone Group
- Director Media & Search, Yahoo! Germany
- Head of Online & Interactive Media, Kirch Group (Premiere DigitalTV)

Internet and media executive with 17 years of experience including general management, business operations, product and content development, and content production.

Currently serving as Internet Services Director and Authorized Officer at Vodafone D2 in Germany. Leading the German Internet portal businesses for PC and mobile (Consumer and Enterprise). Running Vodafone 360 and live! including Mobile Advertising, Music, Games, and Mobile TV.

Within the Vodafone Group, also held the position of Global Head of Online from 2007-2009. Oversaw Online sale of Vodafone products as well as transaction partnerships and the expansion of Vodafone’s eCommerce platforms.

Before joining Vodafone, member of the European Leadership Team of Yahoo! Consumer Search. Managing the German speaking markets. Prior to that, member of the Yahoo! Germany Management Team. Led expansion of all information, entertainment and community products.

Particular skill in delivering business transformation in high growth environments.
