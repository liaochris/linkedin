Boris Chykulay’s Summary

UKRAINE OFFERS

A rich and varied landscape featuring mountain scapes reminscent of fairy tales, valleys and villages from another era, unspoilt forests of varying types, serpentine rivers, the Azov and Black seas, cities with distinctive old and modern quarters, monumental buildings from steel-and-glass to 19th century types, meandering cobbled streets, a range of shooting sites in industrial areas, and even some Soviet-era holdovers.

The array of locations advances the opportunity to enrich your projects with an atmosphere currently underexposed in film and advertising. As an additional kicker, filming in Ukraine means significant savings in production costs.

Ukraine has a developed culture of film appreciation and with a population of 46 million there presents a range of facial types and memorable characters to be utilised in film and advertising projects.

We await your ideas, film scripts, and treatments for which we will prepare a scrupulously calculated budget and convenient timing, organize a wonderful team, and guarantee dependable implementation and a successful life on the screen.

Looking forward to our collaboration,

Respectfully yours,


Boris Chykulay and the A Production team
