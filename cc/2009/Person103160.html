Emre Kavlakoglu’s Summary

I have over fifteen years experience working on digital media applications and services in the area of project management, product management and brand advertising. My long-term industry knowledge combined with creative thinking skills provides an ability to research and decipher new technologies quickly and effectively. Proficient in defining requirements, functionality, product road maps and project plans; I am successful in managing deliverables against outside dependencies with internal or external teams, ensuring successful systems and/or product launches.

I have unique expertise in digital media merges an understanding of product (UI) design and functionality, systems integration and implementation, video operations management, workflow, asset and metadata management, and film/television post-production processes. Platforms and systems I have contributed to range from distribution -such as metadata systems, IP/satellite distributed content networks, broadband/IP/wireless streaming media, VOD (or movies on demand), I-TV; to stand-alone business or consumer products –such as Internet web site, video kiosk and CD-ROM.

I hold a Bachelor's degree (HONS.) in Information Design from Hornsey School of Arts/Middlesex University, London/United Kingdom, (1983).
