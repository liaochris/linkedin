    This a NYC-based meetup group is like a trade organization for network marketers where we come together to share best practices and refer customers to each other. Why network marketing? Put the income claims aside for a moment. Network marketing companies offer wonderful free training on how to develop certain skills that are highly marketable in Corporate America, and that serve as a great springboard to creating your own business. You can keep you job, and sharpen your skills at the same time. I myself have never made a ton of money in network marketing, but it has given me a unique skill set that has enabled to steadily increase my salary in Corporate America by leaps and bounds. I highly recommend network marketing.
