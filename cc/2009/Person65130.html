Matthew I. Snowden’s Summary

Matthew Snowden's primary responsibilities include all financial and organizational matters, the planning and developing of marketing and lead generation strategies, client account development and conducting the company’s bi-weekly training sessions. Mr. Snowden also runs a full recruiting desk; working exclusively with the top echelon of medical professionals.

Mr. Snowden, originally from Gallipolis, Ohio, is a graduate of the W.P. Carey School of Business at Arizona State University. Mr. Snowden attended ASU as one of four national recipients of the Leadership Scholarship, a four-year full-tuition plus stipend scholarship program.

While attending ASU, Mr. Snowden developed a personal and professional interest in real estate. During that time, Mr. Snowden began acquiring real estate personally and developing a mortgage practice under a successful local brokerage, which lead to a prosperous six year relationship that still generates referrals.

Following graduation, Mr. Snowden accepted a position as an analyst with a boutique investment banking/management consulting firm in Phoenix. Mr. Snowden was involved with public and private companies and gained exposure to major corporations in the franchising, real estate, home building, manufacturing, financial services and insurance, BPO and automotive sectors, among others.

Mr. Snowden subsequently organized Snowden Investments, LLC and is its sole owner. SI accepts financial consulting assignments and has acquired, unwound and continues to manage real estate positions, makes direct investments into client companies and takes an opportunistic approach investing in sectors familiar to Mr. Snowden. One such investment is AHCG and its operation of a UMS Healthcare franchise.

Mr. Snowden resides in central Phoenix and maintains an active relationship with ASU, the Leadership Scholarship Program and serves as a volunteer advisor and mentor to the undergraduate members of his collegiate fraternity.
