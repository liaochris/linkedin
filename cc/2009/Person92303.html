Simon Rumble’s Summary

I work at the boundary of marketing and web tech. I'm highly technical by inclination, but my interest is in using it to improve customer experience and increase sales.

My most recent large project was a complete redesign of our web front-end, both for the sales side and existing customers. I had to manage the launch project and integration, as well as perform much of the testing and tweaking to get it over the line. The hardest part was actually the internal paperwork and processes, engaging with four different change management procedures.

Much of my focus these days is complex web systems integration and JavaScript coding. I've been getting my hands very dirty with JavaScript, specifically jQuery, to smooth things through.

Simon Rumble’s Specialties:

Web production, marketing, content creation and management, web analysis and design, user experience. JavaScript development and backend integration.
