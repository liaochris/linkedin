Anthony Banek’s Summary

Anthony Banek has recently moved from destra Hosting to BlueCentral after Bluefreeway Ltd (ASX:BLU) successful listing in December 2006. Bluefreeway aquired the Business Hosting Assets of destra Hosting of which Anthony has been with now for over five years.

Anthony as Managing Director is responsible for overseeing the division's expanding business, including overall Sales, Marketing and Product Direction Strategy, New Initiatives, Technologies and Market Penetration.

Anthony has twelve years experience within the IT and hosting industry in the Asia Pacific region. He developed one of Australia's first internet based classifieds portals in 1996 and founded the web hosting company, Webtrader (a leader in offering Microsoft Windows based hosting technology) in 1997 and acquired by destra Corporation in 2001.

Previously, Anthony held the following roles within divisions of destra Corporation -

a) Roles withing destra Business after consolidation of acquisitions –
- Strategy & Corporate Development
- Strategy & Marketing Director
- NSW State Manager

b) Founder and CEO - Ozhosting.com Advanced (Enterprise Division)

c) CEO - destra Hosting
