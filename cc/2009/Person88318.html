Sandi Cunningham’s Summary

In Maryland, Modern Babies & Children is the only full color, high gloss, resource magazine published for Parents, Grandparents and Families. Our interactive website features local products and services, a useful Events Calendar for families, editorials focused on local interests, and much more. The entire Modern Babies & Children resource magazine is also viewable online!

In January 2006, MBC began franchising. Local editions are published in 6 cities currently, with additional franchise expansion in 2009. Our mission is to continue the growth of the brand nationally, support and develop our franchisees, and ensure that families in every local community throughout the country have a Modern Babies & Children resource.
