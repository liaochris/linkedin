Richard Teed’s Summary

LBi develops custom applications when off the shelf software does not fit the need.

Our niche is:

1. Human Capital Management (HCM) – We specialize in Web-Based and IVR Self Service solutions for the HCM market. Our solutions fully integrate with PeopleSoft, JDE and Oracle.

2. Sports Information Management (SIM) – Our Sports Information Management systems division integrates every aspect of league information including contracts, drafts, transactions and scouting. We understand the business of sports.
