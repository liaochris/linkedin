Brian Ussery’s Summary

Brian E. Ussery is Director of SEO Technology at Search Discovery Inc., an Atlanta, GA based SEM agency. He is an authority on issues related to SEO for Flash, considered an expert in the search engine optimization community and recognized by Google as a Top Contributor on Google's Webmaster Help Forum. Brian's research has been featured by The New York Times, Wired, Gizmodo, ReadWriteWeb, MarketingSherpa, Macworld, The Register, PC World, PC Magazine, InformationWeek, CNET, MediaPost, Valleywag, NPR, SearchEngineWatch, Sphinn, Search Engine Land, Google Blogoscoped, SERoundtable, SEOBook.com, WebProNews and Google Operating System.

Brian is a frequent contributor at Blogoscoped.com, the "editorial board and echo chamber for all things Google" according to David Vise's national bestselling book, The Google Story. Brian is a Moderator for SearchEngineWatch.com, the oldest and most respected resource site for SEO online and in addition, he manages their LinkedIn and Facebook groups.

A lecturer and speaker on SEO for the Terry College of Business at the University of Georgia, Goizueta Business School at Emory University, Search Engine Strategies Conference and eMarketing Association Executive Conference, Brian has consistently combined his entrepreneurial spirit with his passion for SEO.

Away from work, Brian retains his status as a University of Georgia Official Fine Artist. He is authorized by Nike and the Collegiate Licensing Company, a consortium which consists of more than 200 colleges and universities, bowl games, conferences, The NCAA, Final Four, College World Series and Heisman Trophy Award.
