Dan Kahn’s Summary

* Public Relations mastermind with experience designing and executing media campaigns for major international consumer products brands, automobile manufacturers, and local service-based businesses. Campaigns targeted to reach specific consumers that fit each client's ideal demographic.

* Specialties include cost-effective media outreach and converting skeptical CEOs into PR believers due to the ability to turn campaigns into RESULTS and NEW BUSINESS.

* Former journalist over 14 magazine cover stories and hundreds of print and online articles published over the past 13 years. Experience ranges from Men's Fitness and Men's Health Magazines to automotive titles such as Rod & Custom, Hot Rod, Drive, Edmunds.com and more.

* Social media campaign architect responsible for helping the largest consumer-focused automotive car care company in the world create a cutting-edge online media room and social media campaign that resulted in over 250 million unique impressions in less than 12 months.

* Advertising program management experience includes designing and negotiating ad programs for several major motorsports manufacturers, including advising creative teams and leveraging ad buys for maximum impact and editorial support.

* Complete turn-key creation of catalogs and custom publications in conjunction with Kahn Media's associate graphic design team.
