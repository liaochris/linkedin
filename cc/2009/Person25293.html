Debbie Corlet’s Summary

I have 20+ years of senior PA experience in large corporations. I now own my own virtual assistance business from home and around 2 small kids. My services include secretarial - word processing, excel, powerpoint, databases (basic), CRM packages like ACT!, administrative - managing emails etc.

Not listed here, please email me at deb@deb-e-secretarial.com.au or have a look at my website www.deb-e-secretarial.com.au where you can also download my flyer.

The advantages of using a VA are the costs you save and also the time you save.

Debbie Corlet’s Specialties:

I specialise in helping individuals and small businesses (and large) with secretarial and administrative type tasks. This enables the person or business to concentrate on running and maintaining their own business and not having to be snowned under by the admin tasks that need doing every day.
