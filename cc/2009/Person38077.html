Guillaume de Montgolfier’s Specialties:

www.montgolfier-consultants.com
MONTGOLFIER CONSULTANTS is a consulting company helping clients to get better results through a better collaborative work.
We help individuals and organisations to improve their way of working together by providing assistance in project management, by training teams and by coaching individuals. The Montgolfier Consultants way of working is based on a high people respect, modesty in relationships, culture of service, customer focus and demanding quality.

Guillaume de Montgolfier’s Experience

  • Owner

    Montgolfier Consultants

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Management Consulting industry)

    January 2007 — Present (2 years 7 months)

    Montgolfier Consultants is a consulting company helping clients to improve their collaborative work, in order to get global and sustainable performance.
    Experience shows that many organizations invest energy, time and resources to achieve their best level of expertise in their dedicated departments. But they do not have much energy to spend on the collaboration with others (organizations and/or individuals), which leads to deceiving services for clients as well as poor results for senior managers or shareholders.
    Montgolfier Consultants help individuals and organizations to improve their collaborative way of working, by providing support to managers of transversal projects, by training teams and coaching individuals. The method is operational, based on a client-supplier approach, and begins with a listening of actors before beginning to work together. Respect of people is at the center of the approach.
