Darijus Strasunskas’s Summary

Darijus Strasunskas is Researcher at NTNU. He received his PhD in Information Systems from the Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU) in 2006. He has master degree from Vilnius University (2000). From 1997 to 2001 he was employed by Kraft Foods International in Lithuania as finance analyst and was responsible for financial control of Snacks Factory construction project and ERP system implementation, later he became a key user of ERP system manufacturing module. For his performance he was awarded with a letter of commendation in 2001.

His research interests include conceptual modelling, requirements engineering, semantic technologies, in general; and ontology and information quality evaluation, rule-based notification, information retrieval, distributed cooperative systems development, traceability and semantic interoperability of information, in particular. His publications include more than 35 articles in international refereed journals, books, conferences and workshops. He has assisted in the review process of international conferences including ER, RE, CAiSE, NLDB, ISD and other. He has been serving as a member of program committee for the CAiSE Doctoral Consortium, the IRMA Conference, the BIR Conference.

Darijus Strasunskas’s Specialties:

Information management and semantics, information quality and business value assessment, information system analyst, ERP system implementation specialist, finance analyst, manufacturing costs analyst, lecturer.
