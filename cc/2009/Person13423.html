Brian Carter - SEO/PPC/Social Media’s Summary

Brian is a competitive optimizer who helps businesses understand how to get more results faster.

Brian Carter is Director of Search Engine Marketing (PPC), SEO, and Social Media at Fuel Interactive, an interactive marketing agency in Myrtle Beach, South Carolina. He is responsible for the SEO, PPC, Social Media Management, and Online Reputation Management programs at Fuel and its partner traditional agency Brandon Advertising & PR.

Brian previously was an E-Commerce Manager, a freelance PPC and SEO consultant, CEO of an online magazine, and a co-founder of Healthprints.com.

AdWords Qualified Individual Brian Carter is an AdWords Qualified Individual, SEOmoz pro member, SEM 2.0 Group contributor, and all-around good guy that your mom, dad and grandma would like.

Brian is an accomplished speaker who informs, motivates, and entertains businesses, associations, and groups on topics including marketing, management, the internet, search, and social media. His blogging, speaking, and media interviews have positioned him as a leader in the search industry, and definitely one of the funniest.
