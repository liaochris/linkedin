Melanie Warner’s Summary

Design a stronger brand.

Melanie Warner has completed hundreds of dynamic, broad-reaching projects for a variety of industries across 18 different states. Her diverse experience, fresh ideas and forward thinking provide a well-rounded design resource and give clients the confidence to succeed.

PROFESSIONAL GOAL
To offer creative, targeted, well-executed graphic design to innovative companies, and transform them into industry leaders.
