Gijsbert van der Sleen’s Summary

Since 1995 a believer in the possibilities of business improvement by the use of internet initiatives. Love to make complex matters simple and achieve results. Try to keep on the edge of new developments and ahead of the game.

Specialised in guiding organizations to improve Digital Marketing Performance by developing internet marketing strategies, online organization alignment & empowerment and online measurement.

My focus is on digital marketing: customer facing processes (marketing, sales and support processes).

Gijsbert van der Sleen’s Specialties:

* Digital Marketing Organisatie: meer rendement halen uit digital marketing door het in balans brengen van strategie, management, processen, afdelingen, mensen en competenties.
* Digitale Marketing Strategie: vertalen van marketing-, sales- en service doelstellingen in een digital marketing strategie.
* Digital Marketing Measurement: opstellen & implementeren van digital marketing balanced scorecards (organisatie breed)
* Digital Marketing interim & programma management
