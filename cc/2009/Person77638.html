Paul Masquelier’s Summary

Since 1996, Dr. Paul Masquelier has been engaged in web site strategy development and the design of web sites. A major focus of his work has been in the design of sites in a way that enables the client to easily maintain and update the content of their own site. MasquelierOnline.com specializes in the design of sites for consultants, small businesses and non-profit organizations.

Paul Masquelier’s Specialties:

MasquelierOnline specializes in the design of sites that are optimized for maintenance by the client, using Adobe Contribute to update and maintain their sites. Paul Masquelier has been designing sites to take advantage of Adobe Contribute since 2001 and has led professional workshops on the use of this tool.
