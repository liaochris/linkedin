Kate Thomas’s Summary

Lotus + Lama, Inc. is an Apple Macintosh (Mac) computer consulting, Web site design and marketing firm established by Kate Thomas + Martin Thomas in 1995. We are based in Minneapolis - Saint Paul, Minnesota. We work with advertising and talent agencies, voice-over talent, recording studios, graphic designers, non-profits, businesses and individuals in the creative community in the Twin Cities and nationwide. We're amazed at the variety of creative projects our clients ask us to work on, but mostly we do:

* Apple Macintosh computer consulting
* Website + blog design
* Website + blog copywriting and editing
* Website + blog content-based search engine optimization (SEO)
* Website + blog marketing, public relations + press/media kits (SEM)
* Digital photography

Kate Thomas’s Specialties:

Kate Thomas has worked as a stylist and set designer for national TV advertising. Martin Thomas has worked as a recording studio engineer and audio editor doing mastering. Our work in the audio and video production communities translates into great insight into the website and marketing needs of creative professionals and businesses. Visit our website at www.lotuslama.com to see our design portfolio.
