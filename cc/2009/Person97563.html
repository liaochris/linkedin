Terry O'Gara’s Summary

15 years core experience as a Producer and Creative Director of music, sound design & sonic branding projects for TV, radio, electronic media and venue. 5 years in Artist Management in the capacity of Director, Branding and Content Management. Produced on-air talent and programming for projects with music budgets of up to $200,000, for international audiences, and in every conceivable genre (dance, electronic, hiphop, rock, orchestral, country, jazz, world, etc). Managed creative accounts in excess of $5 million annually. Outstanding success translating brand, marketing, message & story objectives into creative strategies. Additional entertainment marketing and Art Direction/Design experience gained as a Creative Consultant.
