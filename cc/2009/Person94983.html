Steven Moseley’s Summary

I am a Graphic designer, Illustrator and digital painter, In my work I use both traditional and digital mediums. I am currently working as a freelance artist/designer but i would like to find something permanent so as to contribute to the growth of a larger group of creatives.

I am able to work in various styles and enjoy developing effective solutions to suite my clients needs for each individual job, I have been working as a freelance artist and designer for the last 2 years and i am always eager to learn new applications and improve my abilities. Currently i work with : photoshop cs3/4, illustrator cs3/4, ndesign cs3/4, acrobat, powerpoint, corel painter x and Corel sketchpad. I am in the process of learning Flash cs3/4, Dreamweaver cs3/4 and Zbrush.

Graphic Design / Character design / concept / editorial and narrative art have always been a passion of mine and i would love the opportunity to share that passion with your organization.

I have had experience with working to demanding briefs and tight deadlines as well as heavy work loads, as i currently run my own freelance operation i am capable of handling briefs from concept to render and then to screen or print ready imagery. I pride myself on clean efficient work and I am driven to make a difference in the design world.
