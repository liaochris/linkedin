Lance Scoular’s Summary

Lance assists people negotiate the minefields of International Trade in two main ways.

1) As a TradeStart Manager/Export Advisor:

The NSW Business Chamber, in partnership with Austrade, delivers the Australian Government’s TradeStart program - a package of services to assist Australia exporters accelerate their business into international markets.

The TradeStart program is available for new or first time exporters looking to make their first export sale, and for existing exporters that are looking to do business in a new market or expand in an existing market.

2) As CEO of Key Directions:

As an International Trade and Transport Facilitator and Mentor, providing courses, training and mentoring on one-to-one or groups both face-to-face and online. From basic to advanced and tailored-to-your needs options.

Our most Popular Course:
"IMPORTING & EXPORTING - An Overview of the Basics" the course Lance has developed and presented since 1997 at various Universities and Adult Community Colleges in Australia.

Lance is Co-Author of:
"Exporters and Importers - Survive and Prosper"
John Yelland and Lance Scoular
Halstead Press for AGPS Press in association with the National Executive of Small Business Agencies
Copyright Commonwealth of Australia 1994
ISBN : 0 644 32430 9

Lance Scoular’s Specialties:

Experience ranges from SME’s multinationals to running own Customs and Forwarding Brokerages and Contract Training to Government Agencies. Developed and presented various courses on Importing, Exporting and International Trade and Transport for industry and tertiary institutions.

Licensed Customs Broker (Australia)
