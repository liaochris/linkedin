Sandeep Agarwal’s Summary

A seasoned professional who has worked in the textile industry for over 13 years(specially the DENIM Industry) .Started the career in 1993 and quickly travelled around the world on various marketing assignments.Took up some international postings (Hongkong,London etc) from previous companies to further market the products .
Became an entrepreneur in 2005 to trade in various textile products . Starting with casual browsing of the net, I got deeply involved and interested in the Internet related businesses and activities. My interest made me evaluate internet from the perspective of a layman and made me aware of the difficulties in accessing correct information speedily from the net. This led to the launch of some portals and also the creation of my blogs like Denimsandjeans.com
