Peter Salmon’s Summary

Experienced senior executive, ex Big 5 partner with extensive business/consulting/practice management experience. Have experience in CEO role. Good risk and business assessment skills. Have led significant change projects.Worked in many countries including UK, US, Canada, Bermuda, Indonesia, Brunei, Malaysia, Australia, Singapore, New Zealand. Have led multi - functional teams and had sales responsibility. Strong insight into operational,cultural and diversity issues. Key sectors Public Sector, Insurance, Re-insurance, Banking, Commercial, IT , BPR and Healthcare. Experienced in practice management, implementation of organisational change and organisational performance, including practice management and assignment profitability reviews. Work has included practice sector reviews, programme reviews, practice office assessment and related process re-design. Extensive involvement in proposal development, proposal assessment and presentation. Multi-year period with responsibility for Project Quality Office and the provision of services for project remediation, reviewed all significant proposals from business, technical, resourcing and management perspectives. My personal drivers include, desire to meet the challenge of the day, focus on an outcome which meets client needs. My goals and objectives are varied, but include the following: provide value to my clients, avoid compromise of my principles and values, i.e. apply rigour to my work, enable my clients to succeed, develop the people I work with and build my knowledge. Significant achievements include, but are not limited to:-- winning major new engagements, significant change projects, regional programme review, enhanced project and programme reporting, enhanced performance review capability, improved programme and project metrics, improved practice profitability.

    Coopers & Lybrand, Singapore

    (Partnership; 201-500 employees; Accounting industry)

    January 1976 — August 1979 (3 years 8 months)

    In April 1975 I married my wife, a Kiwi, and in Nov 1975 took up an appointment with C&L Singapore. This was probably the best decision I ever made. I learned more from this than I could ever have imagined prior to actually going to Singapore. All my preconceptions proved to be wrong. This experience has coloured everything subsequently. I learned so much, and I am a much better person through the nearly 4 years I spent in Singapore. 30 years later, I still draw upon the skills I gained then and I am still in contact with a number of my staff from those days. I was one of 2 expatriate managers, my peers, staff and superiors were all Chinese, Indian or Malay, many of my clients were local. This was a defining experience. I value it still.

    My experience encompassed a broad range of entities, manufacturing, banking, insurance, shipping, oilfield services and salvage. A number of my projects took me to other countries in the region such as Brunei, Malaysia and Indonesia.
