Kristi Donaldson’s Summary

Kristi specializes in all types of real estate transactions. She predominately lists and sells in Macomb, St. Clair, Lapeer and Oakland Counties; representing both buyers and sellers. Kristi has built a "Unique" one of a kind approach to today's ever-changing market. Her methods deeply identify how bank owned properties have affected today's buyers and sellers as well as understanding specific trends and turns in the market. By understanding these areas she's able to further exploit them and give her clients an edge that's unfounded...truly setting herself and her clients apart from the rest. Kristi has made it a mission to seek out those individuals who unfortunately have been adversely affected by today's tough economic times, offer her help and get their lives back on a road to recovery
