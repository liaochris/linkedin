Marie Lancup’s Summary

A charismatic, entrepreneurial leader, corporate strategist, skilled communicator, and effective negotiator with 25 years experience in senior and board level positions for multi-nationals and start-ups. Proven ability to analyse operations and identify potential opportunities, then develop strategic change initiatives and lead the change journey to deliver sustained growth. Adept at retaining and motivating staff to perform at maximum levels through periods of uncertainty. Successful in fast-paced, turbulent environments.

Experience gained internationally across a broad range of industries with a particular focus on Finance, Logistics, e-fulfilment [FMCG], and the Consulting Services Industry. Established and developed a number of startups, and transitioned small & med-sized enterprises [SMEs] to their next level of success.

Government of Canada Security Clearance obtained 07 July 2008.

Graduated with B.Sc. [Computer Sciences] 1st Class Honors.
