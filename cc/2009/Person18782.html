Christopher Turman’s Summary

Over my more than fifteen year career, I have worked in the public policy, political, issue campaign and communications arenas. Prior to joining the staff of National Strategies, I was an independent consultant working with nonprofit organizations and political candidates on fundraising, communications and development strategies.

Strengths include:

• Helping organizations identify opportunities for growth;

• Managing complex relationships within organizations and with partners/affiliates;

• Developing public policy agendas and proposals;

• Identifying federal legislative and regulatory opportunities combined with a track record of successfull interaction with federal and state elected officials;

• Building and managing diverse coalitions;

• Executing grassroots campaigns and mobilizing supporters;

• Developing media strategies & press coverage;

• Raising revenues through direct mail acquisition & appeal campaigns and through direct solicitation of high-dollar donors;

• Marketing & Event Planning: Producing publications & collateral materials; organizational & issue branding; focus group & public opinion polling research; planning conferences & fundraisers;
