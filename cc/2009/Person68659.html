Mike Mead, Enrolled Agent’s Summary

I entered the finance industry in 1994 after serving ten years with the United States Air Force. I have served as an Investment Accountant and a Financial Controller prior to establishing my own practice with my wife Lana in 2002. I earned my Bachelor of Science degree in Management/Accounting from Park University while serving in the Air Force and later earned the professional designation of EA Enrolled Agent from the Internal Revenue Service. I am active member of the National Association of Tax Professionals, National Association of Enrolled Agents, National Association of Accountants, and of the Financial Planning Association. I am currently preparing to take the certification exams to receive my Certified Financial Planner designation.

My focus is to assist individuals and small business in pursuing their financial objectives through education, personalized accounting, tax, financial, and investment management services.

My goal is to help my clients pursue their financial goals the same way they would do it for themselves if they had my expertise, my experience and the time to do it. I am committed to delivering the highest quality personal financial service that any client has ever experienced.
