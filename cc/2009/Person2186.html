Adrian Cotterill’s Summary

Over the past 18 years, I have worked for a wide variety of technology related businesses, in positions such as Chief Technology Officer, Head of Internet Technology, Business Development Director, Head of Strategy and Interactive Marketing Manager.

Over the years my experience has gravitated towards high level strategic management, providing expertise on product development, entry to market strategy, business development (including sales), business planning and the development of sustainable business models.

This has typically been done within the context of organisations at the forefront of technological advancement including Internet Service Providers, Internet Start-ups, Wi-Fi Operators, Broadcasters, Digital Signage / Screen Network Operators, Retail Music providers and most recently a number of Web 2.0 startups.

Adrian Cotterill’s Specialties:

* Digital Out of Home (OOH), Digital signage, narrowcasting, screen network and media sales

* Internet, E-Commerce, Web 2.0 and Content Delivery

* Identifying and exploiting new business (and revenue) opportunities

* Managing Business Development, Sales and Technology teams

* Introducing new business relationships

* Running partner programs

* Building and deploying real-world Wi-Fi + RFID systems
