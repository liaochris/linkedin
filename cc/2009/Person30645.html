Lisa Nirell’s Summary

Hello! I am the Chief Energy Officer of EnergizeGrowth LLC. My firm helps growth companies and professional services firms who aspire to maximize performance, attract great clients, and reach their company’s full value potential.

Through my CEO research, 26 years of experience, strategy workshops, and highly interactive learning programs, I have helped clients secure $83M in new business within just three years.

I am also the author of "EnergizeGrowth NOW: The Marketing Guide to A Wealthy Company ." This practical strategy and marketing guide features Seventh Generation, Eversheds, JELD-WEN, SageWorks and other successful growth companies who have weathered these economic times. Visit www.energizegrowth.com to download your sample chapter by signing up for EnergizeNews, or visit http://blog.energizegrowth.com for more details.

Our clients include OppenheimerFunds, IBM, Sony, Microsoft, and dozens of professional services providers.

We are interested in collaborating with other professional service firms (estate planning attorneys, venture capitalists, CPAs, investment bankers, and business brokers) who work with CEOs who face these challenges.

We also welcome introductions to CEOs who want to maintain their market leadership and successfully exit their companies in 3-7 years.

Please visit www.energizegrowth.com/client.shtml for details. This will explain how we work, our values, and our background. If we can help you in any way, please let us know.
