Robert Brzozowski’s Summary

Bob is a proven manager with a strong technical background and analytical skills, and is completing a transition from technical management to technical marketing. He is currently seeking an analytical/strategically-focused marketing position in the following target areas:

- scientific and/or technology-based company
- analytical laboratory
- market research firm
- business consulting firm
- academic institution

He possesses strong collaboration, communication and writing skills. Bob is experienced in acquisition, analysis, interpretation and presentation of data. He has a track record of customer-focused service, support and productivity as manager of a corporate analytical laboratory. His background includes plant and business support, research and plant operations, and support of business marketing efforts as a leader and participant on cross-functional teams.

Bob completed his MBA (June 2009) at the Pennyslvania State University Smeal College of Business, focusing on leadership, strategy, and communication. He also holds a Marketing Certificate from the Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania. Bob previously completed a PhD in chemistry at Wesleyan University, with postdoctoral work in chemistry at Princeton University. He holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Chemical Engineering from the University of Connecticut.
