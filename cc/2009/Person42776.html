James Breeze’s Summary

User Experience Strategist with extensive experience helping businesses make the most out of technology. I am dedicated to improving the implementation and success of all types of online media and software applications to improve the global community, drive profits and reduce costs.

I am a keen social networker and strive to help people create value with new communication and collaboration media.

I work with clients right throughout the software development lifecycle, preferring to get invovled right a the start where I can have the most impact.

When I partner with companies I take an objective stance making sure that they do the right thing by their customers whilst maintaining a focus on budgets, business goals and technical constraints.
