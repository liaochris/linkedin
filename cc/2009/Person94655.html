Steve Durkee’s Summary

I am an analytical and results-oriented professional that has a lengthy track record of meeting time-sensitive objectives while exceeding performance standards. I specialize in highly compliance driven workplaces. I am also a detail-oriented person who does not believe in shortcuts, but I do believe that there is usually a better solution to work and life processes that may lead to a quicker and more cost-efficient result.

I use an evaluative and systematic approach to problem solving. I seek to understand the sides of an issue, define the deficiency, look beneath the surface for the underlying cause, and then find actionable solutions that can be implemented quickly.

Using adaptive process modeling, I have innovated new processes across industry that have effectively changed the market position of multiple firms. I believe in taking a long-term perspective to decision making and seeking the value in tough decisions that create large competitive advantages within an industry.

I also believe in the power of volunteerism and volunteer my time as a member of the Harvard Business Review’s Advisory Council, on the Government Affairs Committee for the local Chamber or Commerce, and on non-profit initiatives that seek social justice and create a more secure tomorrow for future generations.
