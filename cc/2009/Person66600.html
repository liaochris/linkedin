Melita Ivkovic’s Summary

Melita is the member of the Zagreb Guitar Quartet, the exciting ensemble of young musicians that has performed from Canada to India, from Russia to South Africa; successfully appeared at several prestigious international competitions; premiered 12 dedications by contemporary Croatian composers; given a world premiere of Johann Sebastian Bach's The Art of the Fugue arranged for four guitars.

More on: http://www.guitar4.com

"Melita Ivkovic of The Zagreb Guitar Quartet is a wonderful example of how musicians are leveraging the power of the blogging and social media." - MusiciansWhoBlog.com
