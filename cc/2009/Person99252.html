Tish Tisherman’s Summary

I’m the Pragmatic Career and Lifestyle expert who helps people navigate their business lives to actually coincide with their true passions because, frankly, life is just too short and too precious to wake up one day and wonder – what have I done with my life?!

I owned and operated a staffing and executive placement firm for over 25 years. I coached so many people struggling to find their place – make enough money, enjoy their daily work-life, find time for family. And although I was an entrepreneur and outside of the corporate box, I found myself being influenced by that mindset through serving it – 110%; which is how I serve anything I’m involved with – from boards to business to best buddies.

I’m in the Personal Development Industry now, and instead of placing executives in corporations I’m helping people develop from leaders to entrepreneurs. I’m vibrant and I’m passionate. I’m evolving with new thoughts, new processes, new ways of communication, all of which benefit my associates while, at the same time, guiding me towards both personal and professional development to live my life at this amazing level of joy!

Along with my other projects - this is nearest and dearest to my heart....

I have been coaching and mentoring all kinds of "kids" for over 15 years. This is my joy and passion and the kids sense it. I teach real world topics to navigate the world of business and work. I am a frequent guest speaker at community organizations and local high schools and colleges such as Santa Monica College, USC Marshall and USC Annenberg.

All children are beginning to want control over their own lives. They want to pursue their dreams, whatever they may be. They want to hear that it is possible – even in today’s climate. My kids learn that it truly IS possible if they are coachable, committed, and want to develop conviction, persistence and a mindset that Failure is not an option.

With that, we make their dreams and visions a reality.
