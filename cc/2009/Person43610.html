  • founder

    Dominican Watchdog

    (Real Estate industry)

    January 2008 — Present (2 years )

    Dominican Watchdog is the first and only international consumer protection agency for the Dominican Republic. We offer free advice to any foreign investors or retirement home buyer coming to the Dominican Republic.
    Dominican Watchdog has published a Property Guide on our website with 10 simple rules how to avoid scams when investing in the Dominican property market.
    We monitor fraud and corruption in the Dominican Republic, and we issue a list of trusted lawyers, estate agents and building companies.
    Don't pay any money to anybody in the Dominican Republic before you have read our Property Guide. If you have been conned in the Dominican Republic then Dominican Watchdog can also help you.
