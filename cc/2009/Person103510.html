Valerie Rigsbee’s Summary

Based out of the Twin Cities of Minneapolis and St. Paul, Valerie Rigsbee has been designing and developing web sites for the past 10+ years. Specializing in front end development and skilled in xhtml, css, search engine optimization, revenue earning channels, social media, photoshop and flash, she has created full sites, flash projects and e-blasts for numerous individuals and businesses. Her personal web project, broadwaymusicalhome.com, now sees between 15,000-30,000 unique visits a day, appears first with a google search for "broadway musical" and has become highly profitable. As an actress, she has performed with various companies, and is also available to her clients for voiceover and commercial work. For more information, visit www.valrigsbee.com.
