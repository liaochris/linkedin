Judith Germain (jude@dynamic-transitions.co.uk)’s Summary

Judith is the Founder & Principal Mentor of DTL, a company which enables organisations to significantly improve the leadership performance of the key brands or influencers in the organisation. These key influencers may be managers or individual team members. A particular specialism is working with mavericks or Troublesome Talent®.

Judith is particularly well known in the industry for working with wilfully independent people who are talented & different (whom she defines as Mavericks). Believing in developing leadership talent, she enables people & companies to achieve Maverick Mastery®.

The types of mavericks that she works with can be found here: http://bit.ly/2raU4d

Her mentoring & training programmes, consultancy & keynote speeches, centre around enabling organisations & mavericks to harness their willful independence, build their reputation, improve their communication and interaction with others & become more effective leaders.

She explains how to improve performance by harnessing the creative tension of mavericks or Troublesome Talent® in teams. Judith shows individuals and organisations how to demonstrate true leadership & leverage their reputation & influence to ‘get what they want’ & achieve real change & impressive results.

Having taught over 100 businesses how to improve their reputation & credibility, her Leadership DRIVE™ model challenges audiences to consider the impact of their reputation & influence on the performance of the business and the impact on those around them.

She can also demonstrate how your Social Media Strategy can lead & influence online & demonstrate your expertise, therefore building advocacy, loyal customers & improving reputation for you & your organisation. As a direct result of her expertise, business owners have seen their revenue double through adopting her smarter business strategies.

Her expert opinion has appeared in numerous publications including national broadsheets and leading industry press.
