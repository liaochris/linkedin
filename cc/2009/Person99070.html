Tim Paymans (tpaymans@dissint.nl)’s Summary

********
I'm open to LinkedIn invites at tpaymans@dissint.nl.
********

Born (1978) and raised in and around Amsterdam, wanted to be a fighter pilot and was tested ok (5 out of 1000 candidates) to fly F16’s for the Royal Dutch Airforce, but decided to do something more challenging and went to university.

Tim studied Psychology and Social Science Informatics at the University of Amsterdam, and graduated at the TNO Human Factors research institute (2003).

In 2004 Tim decided to follow his entrepreneurial drive again and founded his second business: an international administrative centre in South Africa, servicing European clients. The business model creates jobs and economic prosperity overseas while delivering cost and quality advantages for businesses in Europe.
