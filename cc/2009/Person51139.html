Joshua Kaufman’s Summary

I'm an interaction designer and user experience consultant living in San Francisco. My work focuses on on cross-platform interaction design and strategy for web and mobile applications. I hold an M.S. HCI and I have over 8 years of experience in both the United States and Europe. As an active member of the user experience design community, I'm currently the community manager of the IxDA San Francisco chapter.

Joshua Kaufman’s Specialties:

Interaction and Interface Design, Web and Mobile Applications, Social Media, Usability
