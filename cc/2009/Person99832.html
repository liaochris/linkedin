Tom Cunniff’s Summary

Entrepreneurial, well-rounded creative executive with 24 years experience in traditional advertising; as an interactive agency owner; and on the client side. Proven leader and problem solver. Strong strategic, marketing, and consultative selling skills. Track record of building and maintaining client relationships at top management levels, and delivering results.

Interested in everything that relates to the future of media and advertising, especially social marketing, BT, and IPTV.

Tom Cunniff’s Specialties:
