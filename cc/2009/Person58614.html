Leonardo Cavallari Militelli’s Summary

Leonardo is an information security specialist and researcher with knowledge on network and application vulnerability analysis, Ethical hacking testing EHT, wireless network audit/design, DoS testing, Malware analysis and security trainings. He holds an Engineering Master Science degree on Computer and Network Security from Polytechnic School of Sao Paulo University (USP) and Wireless Specialist certification GIAC GAWN from SANS Institute.
