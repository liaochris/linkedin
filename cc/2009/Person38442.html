Jody Lockshin, CRS, GRI’s Summary

Habitat Hunters Real Estate Services is one of Austin's oldest real estate firms. We handle residental and commercial, sales and rentals, in all locations and price ranges.

A boutique company, in business since 1972, I've owned the business since graduating with a BBA from the University of Texas/Austin, in 1983. Our office has been in the same conveniently located, central Austin house at 503 West 30th St., 78705, since 1985.

With the help of our many satisfied repeat and referral clients, our strong web presence, and our diversfied services, Habitat Hunters has weathered the up's and down's of the economy and the real estate market.

Jeff Plotkin, our on-staff attorney, CPA and Realtor, gives us another advantage over our competitors.

Skill, knowledge, experience, and longevity, coupled with excellent negotiating skills, make us stand out among the others.
