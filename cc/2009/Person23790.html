David Avrin’s Summary

After two decades of strategic marketing and public relations positions and brand consulting, my focus now is on professional speaking, consulting and executive coaching. I help professionals and organizations discover, develop, hone and promote a unique, memorbale and marketable brand.

I am also a group chair with Vistage International the world’s leading chief executive organization. I lead CEOs and Business Owners to increase their effectiveness and enhance their lives. We meet monthly in peer groups and one-to-one sessions helping to create better leaders that make better decisions and achieve better results.

My speaking has taken me to audiences across North America and as far away as Bangkok, Singapore, Melbourne, London, Brussels and Paris. I speak primarily to associations of business owners to help them craft and market, unique, memorable and dynamic brands and with individual professionals to help them refine and promote their personal brands. (www.visibilitycoach.com.)

I am always in search of speaking opportunities nationally and globally as well as connection to business owners and leaders in the Denver area who might be interested in connecting with their peers through Vistage.
