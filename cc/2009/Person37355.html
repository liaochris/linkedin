Grant Davies’s Summary

Web and desktop application architect specializing in Flex and Flash, with a background in enterprise application development.

Recent work :
http://www.itsasoftdrink.com
http://www.atlantasymphony.org


I have over 16 years of professional experience. Specialized in rich internet application development in Flex flash, JAVA .NET and PHP.

I was C++ developer for over 10 years and a J2EE architect for 12 years, that uniquely qualifies me to be a software architect and have an appreciation for both small and large enterprises with my experience with Fortune 500's and small privately held organizations.

Having led teams of developers for various organizations as a contractor and an employee for clients such as Reebok, Timberland, Sprint and CNN.

I formed Bluetube Interactive so we could specialize in unique solutions for our clients. We build online application using flash, flex or ajax connected to backend services such as .NET or JAVA. We build online customer dashboards, event and ticket purchasing and publication systems, asset management applications, and games. Our clients include CNN, Merryl Lynch, The Atlanta Symphony, Classic Chastain, Ackerman McQueen, Awesome Incorporated, Ackerman McQueen, Three Squared and Multicast Media.

Sometimes we are brought directly into a client to provide a complete solution, but often we are brought in by an agency to be the heavy hitters in technologies like flash and flex, we have architected many large applications for top agencies and everyone we have worked with is an ongoing client or referral.
