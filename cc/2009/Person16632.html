Michael Miller’s Summary

CFO 911 professionals (www.cfo911solutions.com) are experts in solving accounting and finance problems as operational, hands-on CFO's and as investment bankers in the capital markets. CFO 911 professionals have amassed a unique depth and breadth of operational and finance experience developed by having worked as CFOs inside Fortune 500 firms, CFOs of micro cap publicly traded companies, CFOs of private emerging growth companies, CFOs of not for profit organizations, CPAs with Big Six accounting firms and as investment bankers with bulge bracket, regional, and boutique firms. Utilizing both disciplines provides a broader array of answers for any company needing accounting and finance help.

Advisor to CBRID (Center of Business Research, Innovation and Development) located on the campus of Cailfonia State University Dominguez Hills, Carson, California.
