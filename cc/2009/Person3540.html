Alex Crawford’s Summary

Media professional who has spent over 4 years freelancing for the BBC and numerous private broadcast companies and community stations including Resonance 104.4 fm.

Now working as a freelance Project Manager in the Creative Services industry.

Also has extensive managerial and EA experience having worked for various investment and private enterprise firms.

Fanatical about cycling, music and tech, I also enjoy writing and campaigning for various organisations including London Cycling Campaign and acting as press officer for local community cycling groups.

I left London in April 2008 to travel around Asia and Australasia for a year (I have an Australian Passport and Citizenship) but I am now working in the creative media sector as a Project Manager. My skills are varied and extensive and I am a very enthusiastic, sociable person.
