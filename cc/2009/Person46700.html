Jesse Levine’s Summary

I have been working in video production/multimedia creation for the past four years. I began my career as the co-founder of an adventure based video production company specializing in producing feature length backcountry ski & snowboard films. After a few years of producing adventure based films I moved into corporate and real estate video production. I now run a new company called Reel Motion Media, which specializes in custom video production, social media, web video distribution, web development, and video journalism.
