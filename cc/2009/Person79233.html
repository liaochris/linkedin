Phil Eichorn’s Summary

After several years of prosecuting criminal cases, I entered private practice. As a defense lawyer in both the immigration and criminal practice area, I have a unique skill set stemming from my prosecutor background.

I recently opened my own firm. I practice all forms of immigration law including but not limited to citizenship, deportation/removal, employment based immigration, family based immigration and asylum.

Phil Eichorn’s Specialties:

My primary area of practice is immigration law. There are many different sub-categories under immigration law such as deportation defense, family visas, employment visas and citizenship.
