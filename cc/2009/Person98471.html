Thomas Stirling’s Summary

Thomas Stirling has an extensive background when it comes to designing for the world wide web. With well over 100 web designs under his belt, he has worked on a broad spectrum of projects that range from sites for growing businesses all the way up to major brands such as Mercedes Benz, Toyota and Lamborghini franchises.

Thomas was born raised and educated in Glasgow Scotland and gained a H.N.D in Visual Information at the Glasgow College of Building & Printing and then a BSc in Graphic Design from Glasgow Caledonian University.

His expertize ranges from creating initial client mock ups to final coding and implementation of SEO. His main focus is to design websites that are marketing-focused to drive client revenue.

Thomas Stirling’s Specialties:

Front End Web Design, XHTML, CSS, Flash, Dreamweaver, Photoshop, Image Ready, Fireworks, Illustrator, Soundforge, SEO, Traditional Artwork, RSS Feeds, Social Media & Networking Expertise.
