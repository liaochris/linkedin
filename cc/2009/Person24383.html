David Lukas’s Summary

David Lukas brings devotion and discipline to the process of mortgage planning and execution. His broad experience spans the mortgage banking, real estate and financial services industry. Among his many accomplishments is that of being the only Certified Mortgage Planning Specialist in the State of Arkansas.


The David Lukas Lending Team: David believes in a TEAM Philosophy. He has built his team by strategically placing each team member in a role where their strengths and abilities can be used to the maximum.
