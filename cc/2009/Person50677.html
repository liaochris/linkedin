José Crettaz’s Summary

He is a journalist and has a master degree in Busines Administration (UADE Business School). He has been worked for La Nación, the most traditional newspaper in Argentina, and for other important media in this country.

He teaches Media and Entertainment Business, and Economic Structure of Entertainment Industry at the University (UADE and USAL).
