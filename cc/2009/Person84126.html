Rijk Willemse’s Summary

Rijk Willemse has a background in (computational) linguistics, philosophy and communication. As partner in LaVerbe (www.laverbe.nl) he is a senior consultant, sparring partner and coach. He specializes in conveying complex matters in a straightforward yet strategically motivated way, and in coaching others to do the same.

Rijk Willemse’s Specialties:

Building community networks around cultural and governmental issues.
