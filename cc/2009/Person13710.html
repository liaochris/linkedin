Brian Brown’s Summary

Brian R. Brown is Lead Consultant and Natural Search Marketing Strategist with Netconcepts, the leader in natural search marketing, serving the needs of leading retail brands through natural search technology and professional services. Brian provides thought leadership on SEO (search engine optimization) best practices to premier online brands.

In addition to Netconcepts, Brian manages his web presence development practice, Identity Developments, a full service, web standards based web presence company serving the needs of small to mid-sized businesses, providing domain management, web hosting, xHTML/CSS web design, and natural search marketing.

Brian believes that a website is an extension of the business and brand identity. The growth into Search Marketing, especially focusing on SEO, from web-standards based website design, building on top of traditional consumer packaged goods (CPG) marketing and sales has been a unique, but natural fit.

Brian also serves as a volunteer counselor with SCORE, wrote a monthly column called "Presence Pointers" (republished online http://www.instillery.com/) in Dane county's former BusinessWatch Magazine, wrote for the Searchlight SEO blog at CNET.com (http://www.cnet.com/seosearchlight/), published at MarketingProfs (http://www.marketingprofs.com/) and MultiChannel Merchant (http://multichannelmerchant.com/), and participates on SEOmoz.org (http://www.seomoz.org/users/view/16534) as well as other web marketing and SEO/M venues.

Brian has presented at the American Marketing Association's (AMA) Fall and Spring Marketing Workshops (2008 - 2009) and was a faculty member at Lindsay, Stone & Briggs renowned Brandworks University in 2009. Please contact directly if you would like Brian to present on SEO and natural search marketing for your organization.
