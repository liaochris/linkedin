Bob Davis’s Summary

Bob Davis combines 20 + years of experience as a world class Information Technology Executive with 3 different Fortune 500 companies with a passion for music, culture and global concerns.

He is an online communications pioneer that has successfully merged his professional skills with his private passions to create some of the most important communications and content platforms on the internet.

His commentaries on technology/culture have been widely read/published both online and in print, but have become an enabling force in the lives & careers of many people. His broadcasts and podcasts over the years have set the standard for music/information distribution via the internet.

Bob is a dynamic public speaker in both large and small settings. He has given speeches, multi-media presentations, served as a panelist and as a group facilitator in corporate board rooms, universities/high schools, business/technology trade organizations and on radio/television.

Bob Davis’s Specialties:

CMMI, JAD, Online Content Development, Online Communities/Social Networking Management, Search Engine Optimization, Radio Station Operation/Management, Gartner TCO Manager, Microsoft SharePoint, Various Process Engineering/IT Portfolio Management and Governance Tools
