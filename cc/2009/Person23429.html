Dave Crenshaw’s Summary

Dave Crenshaw is author of "The Myth of Multitasking: How 'Doing It All' Gets Nothing Done" (Jossey-Bass/Wiley; Hardcover; $19.95) In his groundbreaking book, management expert Dave Crenshaw offers clear solutions for dealing with today's information-saturated world. He shows what to do when interrupted by co-workers and how to deal with distracting electronic communications such as e-mail and phone messages.

Learn the secret of why multitasking is worse than a lie and uncover hundred of hours in lost productivity. Dave is an entertaining expert as to why the catchphrase of multitasking has increased our stress, reduced our productivity, and damaged relationships at work and at home.
