Tsega Dinka’s Summary

I am a Designer currently residing in Los Angeles and employed as the Senior Designer for Tsavo Media. I design websites that are clean, intuitive and provide a rich user experience.

I enjoy being involved in projects from concept to finish and consider my input to be valuable not only in the design but also in the business, content, interaction and information architecture decisions.

In my designs, I always aim to bring a common sense approach with a goal of simplifying complex data and tasks with the end user in mind.

My skill-set covers XHTML, CSS with a touch of PHP and Javascript with knowledge of Photoshop, Illustrator, Indesign and Flash.

Tsega Dinka’s Specialties:

User interface design, Web design, XHTML & CSS, Layout, Typography, Photography, Illustration
