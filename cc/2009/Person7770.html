Arnau Bunyol’s Summary

Arnau Bunyol Bartrina has most recently served as the Retail Distribution & Planning Manager at Tempe, Spain from November 2004 till January 2008. Tempe is Inditex's footwear and Handbag Company which manufactures and distributes shoes for all the stores that make up Inditex's business formats: Zara, Pull and Bear, Massimo Dutti, Bershka, Stradivarius, Oysho, Zara Home and Uterqüe. In this role, he was responsible for enhancing the retail distribution processes, implementation of new tools, systems and reorganization of the teams for all the brands of Inditex such as Zara, Massimo Dutti, Pull & Bear, Stradivarius, Bershka, Oysho, Zara-home and Lefties. He was responsible for ensuring the correct stock coverage per store and improving the turn-over speed of products delivered to more than 3000 stores. Previously, he has worked as Planning & Forecasting Manager at Camper (COFLUSA), a Spanish shoe company from 2003-2004. Mr. Bartina has more than 11 years of experience in the retail industry with having worked with Levi's, Dockers and Nike.
