David Musgrove’s Summary

The modeling and simulation industry powers innovation across every industry, including aerospace, defense, energy and life sciences. The interactive entertainment industry offers unparalleled creativity and opportunities across hardware technologies, cloud computing, middleware, virtual worlds, games and animation.

Our consulting and production solutions create opportunities for game studios, entertainment technology companies, modeling and simulation enterprises as well as federal agencies. We specialize in the convergent space of modeling, simulation and interactive entertainment. The federal world is both beneficiary and driver of this convergence.

David Musgrove’s Specialties:

Our production solution offers game studios and publishers the ability to leverage the federal world as a dynamic source of creativity, expertise and authentic content. We work with studios and publishers to execute (1) federal agency partnerships, (2) unique approaches to in-game advertising and (3) structured financing.
