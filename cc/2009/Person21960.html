Dana Todd’s Summary

LinkedIn profile: http://www.linkedin.com/in/danatodd

Dana Todd is considered one of the pioneers of the search marketing industry, but her expertise includes all forms of online marketing and interactive visibility. With 13 years of experience in exploring marketing methods in the Internet space, she can happily count herself amongst the "old-timers" who survived and thrived in the Internet new economy.

Recently, Ms. Todd helped launch startup Newsforce, which aims to expand the communication and monetization capabilities of the News industry. Its first products help corporate communications professionals and marketers increase news visibility using direct-to-consumer PR platforms.

Prior, Dana Todd led SiteLab for 10 years, developing a deep expertise in search engine marketing and highly effective online media. Her passion for helping clients understand and dominate the search space while still upholding core branding principles evolved SiteLab’s best practices in information architecture and online marketing.

In 2004, Ms. Todd helped to establish SEMPO, the Search Engine Marketing Professional Organization, now the largest independent trade organization serving the global search marketing community. She currently serves as Chairperson of the Board.

Press (Wall Street Journal, Business Week, The Street.com), industry analysts and technology leaders frequently tap Ms. Todd for her knowledge of the search engine marketplace, particularly search engine advertising programs. Ms. Todd regularly appears as a top-ranked speaker at national conferences such as Search Engine Strategies, Ad:Tech, Kelsey Group and DMA.

Ms. Todd started as a newspaper journalist and editor before switching to advertising in 1991. She became physically addicted to the Internet in 1995. Her hair color is not even close to natural.

Dana Todd’s Specialties:

Search Engine Marketing industry expert, and specialist in full spectrum of online advertising and marketing methods. User experience and information architecture are also a passion. Expert speaker and consultant in search marketing, online PR, social media and search engine optimization.
