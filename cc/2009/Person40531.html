Miah Hussainuzzaman’s Summary

Experienced in laboratory based testing for waters, necessary for designing and operating of drinking water treatment system.

Besides, field level experience on water and some sanitation projects (planning, supervision, construction management, installation of different water options; planning and construction management of low-cost sanitation) have helped to get the skills required for a better Environmental Engineer.

Economically and environmentally sustainable project development and implementation in the rural community is the dream of carrier.
