Paula Bardell-Hedley’s Summary

I am the Director of Research & Marketing for LDS Tourism Services Ltd, a highly-respected brochure marketing company in North West Britain. For most of my working life I have also been a freelance columnist, critic, editor and content provider.

I was born and bred in Llandudno - a popular seaside resort in North Wales - and became involved in the tourism and leisure industry from the ground up. During this period I also worked as a librarian and was recruited into several local wildlife groups.

For several years I worked as a conservationist and countryside ranger. I also travelled around Europe, the Middle East and many other parts of the world (including Ecuador and the Galapagos Islands), but always enjoyed discovering the nooks and crannies of the British Isles.

I currently live on a small-holding in North East Wales.

    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    1982 — 1991 (9 years)
