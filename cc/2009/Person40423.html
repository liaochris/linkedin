Hugh Saalmans’s Summary

Specialise in matching complex requirements with innovative geospatial solutions.

Over 15 years of expertise in Pre-sales, Consulting, R&D and Product Management in the areas of Geographic Information Systems (GIS), Location Based Services (LBS) and Mobile Resource Management (MRM) software and services.
