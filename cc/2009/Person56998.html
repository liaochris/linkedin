Kendall Ware’s Summary

KWARE specialize in problem solving solutions in the area of visual communications for B2C, educational and entertainment industries. I have a deep passion to excel in the area of design, and I’m looking forward to a challenge as well. With this kind of motivation it will give me a fresh head start on the competition that will benefit your company or firm to reach their goals in the market place in no time.

As an accomplished and well rounded designer, I work on several successful campaign/branding launches to help firms to reach their goals during my career. In addition to my passion and desire to join a creative design team or agency, you will find I am dedicated, driven, and an energetic professional.

Kendall Ware’s Specialties:

Experienced in logo design/branding ID development, print media, conceptual design, 3D modeling, 2D/3D animation/motion graphics, video/image compositing, web/interactive designs and front-end development: HTML, DHTML, XML, CSS, JavaScript, ActionScript etc. I also have experienced in CD/DVD/VOD/iTV interface designs, storyboarding, illustration, art direction and project management.

Some experience with Mudbox, ZBrush, Flash Game development and toy prototype design.
