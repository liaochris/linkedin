Jason Ross’s Summary

TruSun Media, Inc. is a technology corporation that provides a full spectrum of multimedia services. TruSun Media is supported by a large team of experienced professionals that each specialize in the various fields of media and technology.

The Company

TruSun Media is a privately-held corporation located in Winter Springs, Florida. CEO Jason Ross founded the company with partners Ryan Baker and Jose Perez on March 8, 2004. Within its first 18 months, the company's reputation for excellence rose to the point of garnering contracts with national and international clients. TSM works mainly in the entertainment and corporate industries. The Goal has been to create a true “one-stop” shop for everything from media production to marketing and advertising the productions. We live for results states the staff of TSM when asked about the goals of the company. “We plan on being around for a long time and giving clients the superior edge that other company’s lack” says CEO Jason Ross when asked about the past, present, and future of TSM STUDIO.


The target market for TruSun Media's services literally encompasses the full spectrum of businesses. Being a full-service company, TruSun Media is in the position of being able to offer everything from multimedia production, ecommerce development, Viral and Traditional Marketing, Advertising, and Consulting. With the pace of technology growing at an exponential rate, companies have more and more options available to both market themselves and deliver their product or service. In order to stay competitive, we understand the potential of technology to command attention. Further, these applications enable companies to increase their employees' efficiency.
