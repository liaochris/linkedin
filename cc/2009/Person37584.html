Greg Hyer’s Summary

Welcome,

I am a Rhode Island born individual that graduated from high school in Florida then moved to Northern Virginia to improve my professional experience to make a final stop in the Raleigh - Durham, North Carolina area to live the rest of my life.

What I would like out of where I work is to be a champion, not only for my own interests, but also for those around me. My colleagues think of me as supportive, encouraging, and proactive. I offer a talent for working cross-functionally and a commitment to advocacy. I have an innovative leadership style, expressive work personality, strong multi-tasking skills, and trusting work style.

I enjoy professional networking. I am a giver rather than a taker. I look at networking relationships as an opportunity to know what someone can do for someone I may know. I try to help where I can and expect nothing in return. I encourage other networking professionals to examine their networking strategies and show them that putting others before themselves will get them much further.

I am a believer in spiritual capitalism. Integrity and commitment are just as important to a business as efficiency and profitability. It is important to take into consideration how the decisions of a business affect the world at a macro and micro level. The mentality in business of the survival of the fittest is over.

I welcome networking with all member of Linking Raleigh, NC. Please feel free to send me an invite to connect so we can get to networking! HOWEVER, before we can start networking you must include the reason why you want to connect. Please do not use a canned message. It does not start the relationship off with value.

I will never solicit invites to connect to people I have not met in person or to people I have no intention of doing direct business with.
