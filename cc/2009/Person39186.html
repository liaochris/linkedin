Heather Newman’s Summary

Heather is a strategic marketing and corporate event consultant who specializes in building partner channel relationships, creating successful marketing plans, and continually raising the bar for the corporate party circuit. She has worked on hundreds of marketing projects and corporate events during her career and has an highly sought-after team of marketing/event consultants in co-located in both Seattle and the Bay Area. Unforgettable events, professionally-designed marketing materials/plans and key client connections are what we do. Heather is also highly skilled in Microsoft Office SharePoint Server 2007 and Microsoft Office system products and technologies. Creative Maven - We keep the balls in the air...

Creative Maven has recently branched out from the high-tech world into the music and wine industries and is thrilled to be supporting some up and coming musicians as well as working with some of the finest wineries in Northern California.
