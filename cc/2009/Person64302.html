Maryann Gorski’s Summary

Training and development, writing, marketing, assisting with career transitions, even doing a little stand-up comedy for charity - these are all things I've been doing as a Renaissance woman for the 21st century!

An employee assistance and training professional, I provide consultation to business and organizational leaders on workplace issues like harassment, violence, reorganization, stress, diversity, and customer service.

I love to watch groups come to life during my presentations at workshops, conferences, team-building events, supervisory skills training, and small-group consultations.

Please request a free consultation about how I can ignite your team. And if you're a recruiter or HR person who thinks I'd be great working at your company, you may also feel free to contact me! Thanks.

Maryann Gorski’s Specialties:

Give me a topic, I'll write a presentation for you and present it with elan! My curiosity about cultures has led me to focus on many aspects of diversity training, including cross-generational and multicultural communication, gender-related issues, world religions and religious pluralism, disability awareness, and a very special program called "Welcome at Work" about making the workplace a safe and productive environment for LGBT employees.
