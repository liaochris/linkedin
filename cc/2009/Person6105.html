Andy Mathers’s Experience

  • Senior Interactive Designer

    Scheffey Integrated Marketing

    (Privately Held; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    2003 — Present (6 years )

    Andy’s role at Scheffey includes design, strategic planning, development and management of interactive media. This includes web-based applications, e-commerce implementations, email marketing, and database-driven websites for both B-to-B and B-to-C companies. His approach is to unify sound business strategies with functionality while delivering the information in an engaging environment with focused attention to ROI. Andy is continually monitoring new online marketing opportunities, technologies and trends. A graduate of the University of Delaware, Andy has more than 14 years of professional experience in the advertising and interactive industries.
