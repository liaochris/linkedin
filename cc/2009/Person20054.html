Colin Shelbourn’s Summary

Professional author with ten books to my name, covering the fields of travel, walking, cartooning and the Alexander Technique.

As a cartoonist, I have produced the front page cartoon for The Westmorland Gazette for 25 years. My work has appeared in Private Eye, The Times and Reader's Digest. Illustrated innumerable books, publications and websites.

I have been running cartoon workshops and holidays throughout the UK for groups, business and education establishments for over ten years.

I am now Britain's only radio cartoonist - at www.radiocartoonist.co.uk
