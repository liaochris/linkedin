Ariel (AJ) Vanderhorst’s Summary

I’m AJ Vanderhorst, and Words w/ Verve (http://freelance-copywriting.arieljvan.com/) is my freelance copywriting business.

I grew up reading compulsively and writing numerous articles and stories (usually about ninjas, back in the day). Coached by my parents, who both taught English at Kansas University, I honed my writing, got stuff published, and eventually earned an English degree with an emphasis in Journalism.

Along the way I attended a summer institute with WORLD Magazine on scholarship and acted as Editor-in-Chief of my campus paper, managing 20+ employees.

When I enrolled in grad school, starting a freelance copywriting business was a no-brainer.

I currently live in Kansas City, Missouri, and have a decade of experience in commercial copywriting, blogging, and editing, with a proven ability to create clear, conversational prose–and I’m ready to put my skills and savvy in your corner.

Are you looking for dynamic writing with a bit of an edge? Let’s talk. Or maybe you’re in the market for a more formal, classic style? Can do. In terms of job descriptions, here’s a short list of ways I can help you:

Freelance Copywriter

Commercial / Advertising Copywriter
Copy Editor
Freelance Blogger
Corporate Blogger
