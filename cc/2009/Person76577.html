Pam Scheibenreif’s Summary

After spending over 16 years in the temporary staffing industry, selling & implementing human resource services to the manufacturing and distribution industries, I transistioned to the PEO Industry and now use my knowledge and experience as a workforce planning consultant. At Impact Outsourcing Solutions we help our customers plan, implement and manage a long-term non-core workforce.

Pam Scheibenreif’s Specialties:

How to build a more cost effective workforce, how to shift workforce liabilities and how to increase productivity through the retention of a non-core workforce.
