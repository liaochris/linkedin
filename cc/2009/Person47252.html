Jim Antonopoulos’s Summary

I am ultimately passionate about investing a business, product, organisation or event with an insightful brand idea that can translate across multiple mediums and engage with a customer on a far more powerful and meaningful level.

My career has shaped the way I perceive the role of the graphic design firm and how it deserves a seat at the boardroom table as adviser, creator and catalyst for change and innovation.

I work with clients to develop insightful strategy on their projects ensuring that the ideas we generate are relevant to our client's brief and continually push the envelope to ensure our ultimate goal of client satisfaction is met.

My 16 years experience working in design firms and branding consultancies, means that I am able to improve business performance through both creative design solutions and insightful brand strategy. Throughout my experience I have worked with both local, national and international organisations - ranging from the local St Kilda Festival, Triple R and Sustainability Victoria to Telstra, TRUenergy, Melbourne Commonwealth Games and Lonely Planet.

Continually proactive in working to improve our industry, I am also a member of the 2007 AGDA council.
