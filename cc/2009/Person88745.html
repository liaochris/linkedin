Sarah Taylor’s Experience

  • Real Estate Professional

    Re/Max Masterpiece

    (Real Estate industry)

    November 2008 — Present (9 months)

    I work with the best Re/Max teams in St Lucie County. My partner, Denise Allen, and I are an unstoppable team who assist both buyers and sellers in the Treasure Coast market.

    I define myself as a Real Estate Maven.

    In life there are experts who know a lot about particular niches - like a gear head or a foodie.
    Ask them about cars or cooking and they’ll tell you everything you want to know.

    Then there are mavens.
    Mavens are similar to experts in that they collect information.
    However, a maven uses this information to YOUR benefit.
    They derive joy from helping others succeed based on their expert advice.

    As a Real Estate Maven I collect information about the market, buying, selling, and everything in between and I dispense those benefits directly to my customers, friends, and anyone else who needs a hand.

  • Owner & Videographer

    Local Exposure, LLC

    (Online Media industry)

    March 2008 — Present (1 year 5 months)

    Local Exposure creates the ultimate 24/7 Open House of real estate for sale. We shoot, edit, and distribute movies of properties for sale by owner or listed with a real estate agent.

    As video in real estate is gaining momentum, Local Exposure seeks to provide affordable options for home owners to market their property in as much detail as possible.

    Our mission is to prove that 360 degree tours and panning-stitched photos don't work. Home buyers want the nitty-gritty when they click on "virtual tour" and that's what we deliver.

  • Banking Loan Consultant

    Washington Mutual

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; WM; Banking industry)

    March 2003 — April 2008 (5 years 2 months)

    My corporate career began with WaMu as a bank teller.
