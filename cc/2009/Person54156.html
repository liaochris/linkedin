Keith Bray [LION] toplinked.com’s Summary

Awakening to your life's purpose,knowing "who you truly are and realizng your personal potential to achieve your goals We work with people in these areas who have to courage and honesty to make change.We help clients create a more abundant life, and for those who are in need, a life free from addictions.

Keith is a person who is trained in coaching, and whose own real life experiences allow him to constructively coach others by relating. Since 1994, Keith has been on a dramatically altered life journey with no fixed destination, it just keeps getting better!

Keith's journey was seen outwardly as one of business success in both the corporate and entrepreneurial worlds. On the outside, all was well. The inside did not match and several avenues were used to "Feel Better" or alter mood! It was like a journey of two different people. One you saw, the other you didn't. Finally, the conflict caused Keith to hit a wall.
Action and change were required .Help was sought

We offer help to those who need change!

We offer unique, personal, confidential one-on-one coaching .
From the challenges, and with help, comes hope and serenity. As testimonials show, Keith has positively touched peoples lives, and employs "foot prints" that have helped millions find freedom and a new way of life.

Keith is able, today, to be a good family person, and an asset to his community. Most importantly, he has become a person who is happy to look in a mirror.
There is a process that Keith as your coach and "Trusted Confidant", will guide you through. At the end of the process, you will find a new you, with hope and serenity going forward!

Our goal is to coach people through a transformation process to be a person with self love.

We can be reached at 905-477-7972. Keith is committed to total confidentiality. Skype at lifecoachkeith.
