Adrienne Nadeau’s Summary

Performance poet with a successful program on creative expression that is both engaging and educational. National touring spoken word and slam poet, that regularly performs in the Tampa Bay area. Featured at diverse venues such as high schools, colleges, nightclubs, religious centers, radio stations, correctional institutions, and open mic nights.
