John Watch’s Summary

I have been conducting data analysis and statistical profiling in the real estate industry for over 25 years. Developed customized software applications which are adjusted for each user to meet there specific needs. Started MJW consulting, Inc in 1989, in 1998 started Accuriz LLC. Over the past ten years these companies have morphed into a single source for accurate property data and valuation consulting.

John Watch’s Specialties:

Market Valuation Trend Analysis based on stratification levels determined by market participants. Provide value estimate on Hundreds of Thousands of properties at one clip, usually in under 30 days.
