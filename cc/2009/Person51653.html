Jason Sherry’s Summary

Seasoned consultant, product manager, systems engineer, and Windows administrator with extensive experience in Microsoft Windows, Exchange, Active Directory, SharePoint, SQL Reporting Services, and related technologies.

Proficient presenter, public speaker, project manager, and writer, with fifteen years in-depth hands on systems experience.

Microsoft technologies has been my passion since the 1986, and Windows NT since 1993. I am currently an Exchange and MIIS/ILM MVP and have focused specifically on AD, Exchange, and Windows for over ten years.

In addition to my AD and Exchange work, I have performed implementations of SharePoint, MOM, SMS, and various other system management solutions. My contributions to the industry include multiple articles, white papers, participation in various forums, MVP related work, and speaking and training engagements.

I have been working with Exchange 2007 since the early beta versions, and would like to continue this work as an AD\Exchange architect. In addition to my extensive technical experience, I have strong product management, communication, public speaking, and documentation skills.
