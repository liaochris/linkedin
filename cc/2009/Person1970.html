A Gala Event / Hugh Wunderman’s Summary

A Gala Event is an International Event Design, Entertainment & Production Company with headquarters in New York. We have been producing Unforgettable Events for over 25 years; On Budget and On Time!

We Travel the Globe providing Innovative Solutions for Unforgettable Events from Thought to Finish.

Pushing Boundaries and Exceeding Expectations are the Hallmarks of our Award-winning Creative Team- Since 1980.

Since its inception, the Company has emerged as one of the World's Leading Event Firms, often re-defining traditional Event standards. We provide our clients with fully integrated, turn-key services.

Whether you need a Sword Swallower to Perform in Manhattan or Theme Gala produced in Beverly Hills, a New Product launched in Barbados or your International Conference planned in Beijing- A Gala Event will oversee your entire project with an Uncompromising eye to detail.

At the helm of A Gala Event is the Visionary Senior Producer & Creative Director- Hugh Wunderman, who has been translating dreams into memorable events for more than 30 years.

Experience for yourself why Corporate and Social Clients such as; Nestle's, Ashford & Simpson, Studio 54, Citicorp, Saks Fifth Avenue, Mayor Giuliani, The NY Yankees, Coach, Keyspan, American Express, Marvin Hamlisch, The Metropolitan Opera House, The NY Post, Rubenstein Communications, as well as many others; Keep coming back for their most important and High Profile Events.
