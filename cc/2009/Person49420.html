John Watkins’s Summary

I coach and consult with schools, school districts, government agencies, non-profit organizations, and a few select private sector companies.

My work supports innovative leadership, team development, and positive, planned organizational change. I focus on:

• helping organizational leaders and staff members clarify their values, purposes, and vision

• guiding the deliberate creation of programs and activities that purposefully enact those values and vision

• creating organizational coherence and alignment in ways that feel seamless, personalized, and responsive to clients and membership, and to the broader community in which organizations do their work

• building broadly networked learning communities within and across organizations

• crafting real time professional development and knowledge management systems that support new organizational structures and a culture of innovation and results.

I work hands on and over the long term with organizational members to develop leadership, engage in strategic planning, and create emergent organizational systems necessary for effective implementation of a transformational (fundamental rather than incremental or piecemeal) change agenda.

I hold high expectations for the learning and performance of people and teams, and believe that healthy, equitable, and wise organizations can be created to serve a more democratic, socially just, and ecologically sustainable community and society.
