Vlad Trifa’s Summary

As a young, dynamic and creative researcher in robotics and intelligent learning systems, I'd like to expand my knowledge towards the "real world", outside of a lab. I am eager to learn about the processes that link bright innovative ideas with concrete marketed products.

With very good communications skills and languages knowledge (I speak fluently French, German, English and Romanian, I understand Italian and Spanish, and I'm currently an intermediate Japanese learner), I enjoy interacting with people from any cultural and educational backgrounds, and help them solve their problems the best I can with my knowledge and willingness to learn about our society, and how new technolgies shape it.
