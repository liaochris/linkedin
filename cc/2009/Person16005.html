Gilbert Wilkes’s Summary

PhD Rhetorician specializing in online discourse, messaging, communication, and computer supported cooperative work. Has taught courses in English composition, rhetoric, professional writing, technical writing, computer mediated communications, human computer interaction and other subjects. Teaches face to face and blended courses. Academic administrator in charge of bachelors program at Royal Roads University.

Goal? To make the humanities relevant again by grounding it in the world of practical work and activity.
