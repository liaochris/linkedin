Katie Konrath’s Summary

I fell in love with creativity and ideas when I was 11, and haven't looked back since.

Since my undergraduate college degree in Ancient Greek and Latin is completely impractical, I spend hours and hours looking out for the coolest new ideas - just to prove I understand the modern world too.

I've worked with one of the world's top creativity gurus, exercised polo ponies in Malta, studied at the TRIZ Institute in St Petersburg, and earned a Masters degree in innovation from the Institute for the Design and Development of Thinking.

My creativity blog, getFreshMinds.com, was recently named a top innovation blog by Guy Kawasaki. I write it because I love highlighting cool ideas, and helping others learn how to be more creative. Also, it's a lot of fun.

I'm also a contributing author to the Personal Branding Blog. My articles generally run on Sundays.

Professionally, I specialize in creating online marketing content for companies who want to improve their presence online. I write articles, ebooks, newsletters, blog posts, white papers and website content.

My chapter in The Age of Conversation 2 (a book created to earn money for the Variety, the children's charity) is called, "It's not enough to just talk about ideas."
