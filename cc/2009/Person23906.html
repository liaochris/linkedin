David C Ballard’s Summary

My background is diverse from walking the ocean floors in different parts of the world as a Commercial Hard-Hat Diver to the surgical operating rooms as a medical rep for cardiovascular and critical care products in the State of Florida, to owning my own real estate brokerage for the past fifteen years in Salt Lake City, and finally acting as President of db Security Inc., with my partner Bill Wardell, who is also an authority for online security.

It is the goal of db Security Inc. to become the leader in developing a new Net-Generation of "Responsible CyberCitizens" and provide for a safe Internet for your children and your families.

Our battle is not against organized cybercriminals, hackers, and deviants. Our battle is against complacency. As consumers we already own 80% of the Internet, it's already ours, we need to become the "Responsible CyberCitizens" of the Net. db Security Inc. was established to provide the resources for all the owners of the Internet, so your children and your families can enjoy a safe and hassle-free computer experience. And stories about children's innocence being stolen and living a life of tormented memories will be avoided.
