Justin Gardner’s Summary

Currently, my position at AMC involves researching social and new media technologies and developing strategies for the company.

In addition to my job, I maintain a popular multi-partisan, political blog called Donklephant.com, which boasts a roster of over 20 authors, over 4M page views since July 2005 and has been highlighted on sites such as New York Times, CNN, Real Clear Politics, Huffington Post, Yahoo Sites of the Day, Guardian UK, FiveThirtyEight, Rasmussen Reports, Media Bistro and many more.

From 2004 to 2006, I worked as a Communications Manager for kozoru, a startup that built a search technology that delivered answers to questions inside a wide array of IM clients.

Before that, I was a Copywriter for Muller + Company, where my work was recognized by Creativity, AdWeek, Graphis, AIGA and garnered numerous industry awards, including several Best of Show Addy awards.
