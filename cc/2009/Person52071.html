What is the PowerFlare?

The PowerFlare is an electronic beacon traffic safety device invented by a police officer and designed to replace the traditonal flares. It weights less than 7oz (200 g) with 1 CR-123 Lithium battery and a shelf-life of 10 years.

The unit is about the size of a hockey puck and very strong to withstand being run over by motor vehicles. At night, its visible to 3,000ft on road or water and 10 miles from air. During the Day: visible to 1,000 ft.

They come in different LED light colors (Red, amber, blue, green, white, Infrared (IR) and Custom colors are available) and 10 Flash User-Selectable Patterns/Settings (solid-on high, rotate, alternate, quad blink, double blink, fast blink, slow blink, solid-on low, "s-o-s", flashlight)

PowerFlare Electronic LED Safety Lights comes in the following styles: Non-Rechargeable; Rechargeable and Tactical Applications Infrared.

They are available in pack of 6 or pack of 12.

In today's world of tight budget, the powerflare electronic beacon system saves you money by paying for itself in the first 24 hours of use versus the old fashioned flares, There are no health or environmental concerns. There is no smoke to inhale, no residue to worry about, no fires to extinguish.

Approvals & Certifications: Complies with U.S. DOT (FMCSA) 49 CFR 392,25 & 393.95 (g). MUTCD Type A (flashing) and Type C (solid on). Designed to meet MIL-SPEC/MIL-STD 810 for shock, vibration, and water-resistance
