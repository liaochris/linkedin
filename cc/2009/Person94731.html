Do you have a warehousing and distribution operation? Steve can help you discover process improvements and implement lean distribution solutions that drive down costs and boost service levels. Stephen T. Hopper, PE, is a results-driven supply chain professional with a record of achievement in management consulting, consultative sales, sales management, and business development.
