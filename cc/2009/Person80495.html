Krishna Prathab R V’s Summary

Krishna Prathab is the Founder & Chief Executive Officer of XLabz Technologies, a software product based company located at Pondicherry which specializes in creating products and solutions based on the Adobe Flash Platform. He has almost a decade of experience in various technology practices like Product Development, eLearning, Mobile Learning, User Experience (UX) design and Rich Internet Applications (RIA) and has worked for quite a few Fortune 50 companies like Adobe, Siemens, Convergys and General Electric in various capacities.

A partial list of clients for whom Krishna has architected and created solutions for include Siemens, Honda, Adobe, Nokia, GE Power Systems, GE Aviation, Houghton Mifflin, McGraw Hill, Pearson and many others.

Prior to setting up XLabz, Krishna was a Technical Director of a technology firm in India, where he was instrumental in setting up and managing the technology practice area and was also responsible for manging client relationship in the US and Europe. A die-hard technologist and a Flash fanatic, Krishna has worked on and created in-numerous number of Flash and Flex applications for various domains ranging from Defense to K-12 Education.

On the entrepreneurial front, Krishna is actively involved in setting up open door entrepreneurial forums which creates awareness among the young minds of India. Krishna is well networked with many fellow entrepreuners, VC's and visionaries around the world who also work with him in nurturing entrepreneurship in India and particularly amongst students.

Krishna hold a Masters Degree in Software Engineering from a premier institute in India, has a gold medal for academic excellence and is a university rank holder.
