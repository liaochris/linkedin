Marcia Hansen’s Summary

Innovative and passionate marketing manager delivers results above and beyond expectations. Responsible for building Direct Marketing social media strategy and tactics from the ground up including educational and entertaining content for VehicleVibes.com with videos and games, plus the new Vehicle Vibes Radio show.

Marcia is also the voice behind the Allstate Twitter channel (@allstate), manages the Allstate Facebook Fan page (http://www.facebook.com/allstate), and other Allstate social media initiatives, including FrienderBenders.com, Allstate’s user-generated content site about bad driving habits and AllstateUpdates.com where site visitors can get all the info about Allstate they want to blog, tweet and share.

Marcia demonstrates leadership and out-of-the-box problem-solving experience in social media marketing, small group event marketing, social networking, content syndication, SEO and link building, email marketing, program outreach, non-profit development, website management, web analytics, and Internet/Intranet content management.
