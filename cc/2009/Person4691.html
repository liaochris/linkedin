Andy Lewis’s Summary

I am a highly experienced front line IT Manager, specialising in Service Delivery and IT Operations. I have a robust technical pedigree, respectable commercial knowledge and an extensive clear understanding of the totality of providing business information systems and IT infrastructure services. I have in-depth knowledge of processes, personnel, hardware and software issues on large and complex systems, complemented by significant technical support and transition roles including UNIX, Mainframes, PC architecture, Database and Networking. I have a track record of determining and delivering standards, motivating staff and building lasting profitable customer relationships and teams.

In my current and previous roles I am and have been responsible for managing data centres, operations, mainframe and server infrastructure, storage management, database support, customer accounts, media management and locations management, in-sourcing, outsourcing and developing and managing improved 24x7 operational services. I strive to provide an effective service for all operational aspects of a Company's core business systems and other ancillary systems, and customer services. I've been responsible for managing hardware and software maintenance agreements, hiring and developing company staff, departments and 3rd party personnel, and generally directing and coordinating the activities of technical support staff to ensure that the availability, operation and support of systems meet current and evolving business and customer requirements.
