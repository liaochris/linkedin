Dellwyn Oseana’s Summary

I am chiefly motivated by three words: potential, possibility, and change. As a coach, my focus is primarily geared towards uncovering the potential of my clients. As a consultant, my drive is unleashing the possibilities within a given situation.

Over the last 13 years, change has been the undercurrent of how I view potential and possibilities. Still, many see change as a force mostly externally motivated. I prefer to see change as an internal drop of water. The ripples will become most powerful by the time they are externally visible.
