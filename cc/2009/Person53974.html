Kay Colley’s Summary

Editor and writer; public relations strategist specializing in media relations. As a professor, my specialties include student media, coaching writing and teaching public relations strategies. As a consultant, my specialties are writing and editing, coaching writing, and designing public relations strategies. My research focuses on use of social media in public relations, student media, the use of experiential learning in the classroom and creating community through the media.
