Lisa Hendrix’s Summary

I am the author of six published romance novels, including IMMORTAL WARRIOR (Book 1 of The Immortal Brotherhood), RUNAWAY BAY and TO MARRY AN IRISH ROGUE. My seventh book, IMMORTAL OUTLAW, is to be published in June 2009, with others in the series to follow.

I have presented well-received workshops at the following conferences:

- Whidbey Island Writers Conference (Workshop Presenter, Chat House, 2009)
- Romance Writers of America National Conference (Workshop Presenter, 2001, 1999)
- Pacific Northwest Writers Conference (Panelist, 2001, 1999)
- Northwest Bookfest (Panelist, 2001)
- Emerald City Writers Conference (Workshop Presenter, 2006, 2000, 1999, 1998)
- Sleepless in Silverdale Writers Conference (Workshop Presenter, 2000, 1998)

I am available to speak to conferences, writers groups, bookclubs, and other interested groups. Please contact me via the Contact form on my website, listed above.

Lisa Hendrix’s Specialties:

Workshops on Plotting, Setting, Character and Characterization, The Nuts and Bolts of Submitting Your Manuscript, and other topics.
