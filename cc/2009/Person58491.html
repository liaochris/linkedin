Leigh Newport’s Summary

I am an Accredited Staging Professional providing Home Staging in Northern Virginia, Frederick County, MD, and Jefferson County, WV. My company, Staged by Design, specialize in both vacant and occupied homes and also provide interior redesign services.

Staged by Design works with homeowners and real estate agents to help you sell your home for more money in less time. How do we do that? By setting the stage within your home to show it to it's very best advantage, both in person as well as in print and online advertising.

We offer consultations as well as full-service Staging for both vacant and occupied homes.
