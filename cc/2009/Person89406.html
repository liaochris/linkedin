Scott Coplan’s Summary

Scott is a project manager, as well as an educator, author and speaker in the field of project management best practices. He is the founder and president of COPLAN AND COMPANY, an Information Technology (IT) acquisition project and change management firm with offices in Seattle and Louisiana. Scott focuses on IT acquisition projects and FOUR, an IT acquisition tool developed and licensed by COPLAN AND COMPANY.

Scott has 30 years experience providing IT project management services involving planning, definition, acquisition and implementation. Scott’s project management success includes transferring a consistent approach for developing and managing projects that achieves strategic organizational objectives. Formerly with Touche Ross, Booz Allen and Hamilton, and Battelle Memorial Institute, Scott holds an MPA from the University of Washington and a BA from Beloit College. A certified Project Management Professional (PMP®) and Certified Professional in Healthcare and Management Information Systems (CPHIMS), he is also a member of the IEEE Computer Society, Health Information and Management Systems Society (HIMSS), HIMSS Enterprise IS Steering Committee and Project Management Institute. Scott also holds a faculty position at the University of Washington School of Medicine where he teaches project and change management for health care information technology.

Scott Coplan’s Interests:

COPLAN AND COMPANY, an IT consulting firm headquartered in Seattle, Washington provides comprehensive project management services to clients throughout the United States. Founded in 1991 by Scott Coplan, we quickly established a reputation for integrity, thoroughness and success in completing IT projects. To ensure success, we organize each project by defining a work plan to stay within budget and schedule, reduce risks and achieve our clients' goals. At COPLAN AND COMPANY, Quality Management (QM) is our focus. This means providing project management services that include preparing project standards and conducting Quality Assurance (QA), Quality Control (QC), and Independent Verification & Validation (IV&V) to execute and control projects according to industry standards. Our project management services support all phases of IT projects, including: project planning, requirements definition, system acquisition and implementation services.
