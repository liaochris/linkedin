Denis Olenik’s Summary

I'm an independent graphic designer with experience in different fields of design from editorial and corporate identity to web design and illustration. But my passion and specialization is business communication services: corporate and brand identity programs, branding, environmental design, signs and websites.

My approach is simple: my work is my passion and I love what I do for my clients. I love when my client's business is successful and when it is growing.
