Francis (Hank) Henry, MBA, CUA’s Experience

  • Principal

    Francis Henry Consulting

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    October 2008 — Present (1 year 2 months)

    Francis Henry is Principal of Francis Henry Consulting, an independent consultancy firm in Dallas specializing in the creation of an engaging user experience for websites and software. Previously, he has been a Consulting Manager in the User Experience Group at EDS, where he led teams developing user interfaces for both internal and external clients. He has also been Usability Manager for GSI Commerce, a leading eCommerce platform provider, and Customer Experience Manager for JCPenney.com, where he built the company's first usability lab. He is on the Conference Committee of the Usability Professionals Association. He holds an MBA in eBusiness and is an HFI-Cerified Usability Analyst.
