Denise Wakeman’s Summary

I am an online marketing advisor and co-founder of The Blog Squad™.

Since 1996, I have been assisting professionals and small businesses to leverage the Internet with online marketing strategies using blogs, ezines, content development plans and ecommerce systems. I have more than 20 years experience in small business management and with my Blog Squad partner Patsi Krakoff, I am co-author of numerous blogging programs and books including

*Build a Better Blog: The Ultimate Guide for Boosting Your Business with a Professional Blog
*The Blog to Book Project
*Better Business Blogging

I frequently speak at conferences and workshops about business blogging and how to create massive online visibility as well as gain expert status through social marketing.
