Petri Lillqvist [LION plillqvist@me.com]’s Summary

I'm the Director of Risk Management and Corporate Security. With background in law enforcement with experience in facility and personnel security, my current responsibilities include also industrial safety work.

I'm interested in developing enterprice wide approach to operational continuity and business resiliance, in order to create enterprice security model, that can better answer the needs of today's corporate security issues.

I'm active in developing Security Management profession and co-operations in ASIS International. I'm a member of CSO Roundtable, the by-invitation-only group of ASIS International that serves senior security executives in the world’s leading organizations, and I was recently appointed as a member of Advisory Board for CSO Roundtable.
