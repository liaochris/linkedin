Paul Blanchard’s Summary

Personable, passionate, pro-active ‘whatever it takes’ attitude. Driven, with strong leadership skills

Long-standing background in corporate communications: nearly ten years’ experience running my own PR/marketing firm (www.right-angles.info) working with listed companies and brand-names, at senior level and across a variety of sectors.

Extensive media contacts across TV, radio, and digital; in general and specialist sectors. Confident and experienced public speaker, regular studio appearances on national TV and radio (usually live). Delivered media skills training at board level internationally.

Excellent public affairs skills: former parliamentary advisor, served six years as an elected councillor (unitary authority); and stood for Parliament at the 2005 general election, my campaign receiving the largest vote increase in the whole country.

Dedicated marketing specialist with focus on branding, art direction, and online strategy. An active ‘door-opener’ for clients, and prolific networker with contacts in politics, national & local government, NGOs, public affairs, pressure groups, business, industry, education, law, media & public relations

I also have 2:1 honours degree in Law; and won a number of awards for my campaign work, which has been featured extensively in ‘People Power’, a Daily Telegraph book on campaign communication.
