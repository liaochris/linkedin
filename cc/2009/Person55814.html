Michael Kirsch’s Summary

Michael Kirsch is owner of >>mlyk consulting - mobile technology consulting.

Before he was Head of Business Development at U-Turn
Media Group responsible for U-Turn’s mobile marketing solutions, strategy, partnerships and business development.

Before he was Project Manager for Mobile Data Services at Mobilcom Multimedia GmbH responsible for UMTS mobile data services, partnerships with middleware vendors and strategy planning for new mobile technologies.
