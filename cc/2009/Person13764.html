Brian Teal’s Summary

Throughout the past 6 years I have worked as a commercial lender for a leading community bank within Chico, CA. This opportunity has provided an excellent opportunity for me to expand my network and integration within the community. I am now a Business Banking Client Manager with Bank of America based out of the Chico Main Banking Center in Chico, California. My territory covers all of Northern California from Yuba City / Marysville up to the Oregon border and over to Eureka. Responsibilities within my role as Business Banking Client Manager include all Commercial Lending relationships from $500,000 up through $50 million.
