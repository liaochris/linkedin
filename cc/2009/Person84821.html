Rob Howe’s Summary

Sustainability consultant and business builder, I am the president of Sustainnovation Consulting, Inc. providing "systems thinking" and TBL expertise to Fortune 500 businesses such as Proctor and Gamble and HJ Heinz. Specialties include zero waste, renewable energy, continuous improvement strategies, and executive CSR training. Experienced in Natural Step Framework and ZERI.

I am the former CEO of Recology Solutions, Inc., a sustainable packaging products and zero waste services company. Clients included Nvidia, Doris Duke Foundation, Vanderbilt University. A sabbatical in Indonesia shifted my focus to application of business strategies using the principles of sustainability.

Before moving to Indonesia, I was a business development IT executive for 13 years managing Fortune 500 clients such as Starwood Hotels, Thomson Companies, Zurich Financial, and Fidelity. Today, as a founder of the Greater Newburyport Eco Collaborative and leading member of various environmental and community efforts, I am actively promoting regional development of sustainable and economic initiatives.

GOALS:
Drive business to adopt sustainable practices to realize the benefits of sustainability as a strategy.
