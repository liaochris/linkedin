Conrado Morlan, MBA, PgMP, PMP, IPMA Level C®’s Summary

Thought Regional Program Manager and International Project Manager who excels at managing complex global and regional programs and projects in disperse locations, managing multidisciplinary and multicultural high performance teams.

As a Project Portfolio Manager has establishing project governance, project portfolio management processes; and aligning IT strategy to fulfill business objectives.

During his tenure on different capacities has demonstrated capabilities of running Information Technology (IT) as a business, achieve competitive advantage through Information Technology transformation and highly adaptable to constant changing environments.

Strong relationship builder capable to influence organizations across functions and levels, skilled negotiator and stress resilient personality. Multicultural background, knowledge of global business etiquette and fluency in Spanish and Portuguese conform the foundation of a successful global career.

Holds a Master in Business (MBA) in International Business and a Bachelor of Science (BS) in Computer Science. Achieved Project Management Institute (PMI) Credentials: Project Management Professional (PMP) and Program Management Professional (PgMP).
