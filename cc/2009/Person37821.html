Gretchen Enger’s Summary

I provide strategic direction and tactical oversight to improve the ease of use of web experiences. My goal is to make those web experiences more powerful, engaging, and rewarding to use by:

Assessing data from multiple sources, synthesizing it, and developing strategies for my projects

Creating user-centered designs by considering market analysis, customer feedback, and usability findings

Designing user interface, and interaction flow with an emphasis on user goals and experience

Developing conceptual diagrams, wireframes, and experience prototypes

Planning and conducting usability studies

Developing and maintaining detailed user-interface specifications

Interacting with and facilitating communication between stakeholders, developers, and end users
