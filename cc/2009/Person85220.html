Robin Meadows’s Summary

Robin is an award-winning freelance science writer and editor. She has a broad background in the life sciences -- ranging from molecular biology and development to neurobiology and behavior -- and is an expert in conservation, ecology and the environment.

Her credits include Audubon, California Agriculture, ChemMatters, Conservation, Ecosystem Marketplace, Environmental Health Perspectives, Frontiers in Ecology and the Environment, Muse, Nature, Public Library of Science Biology, the San Francisco Chronicle and Smithsonian Zoogoer. Her articles have been reprinted by MIT, the U.S. Academic Decathlon and state standardized reading comprehension tests.

Robin has been a project manager and editor for clients including the California Native Grasslands Association, the Society for Conservation Biology and the University of California.

To see samples of Robin's work, please visit her website: http://www.nasw.org/users/rmeadows/.

Robin Meadows’s Specialties:

Agriculture, animal behavior, biology, chemistry, conservation, ecology, endangered species, energy, environment, environmental policy, natural resources and wildlife.
