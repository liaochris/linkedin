Daniel Suchenski 蛋蛋’s Summary

Mr. Suchenski's experience over the years has included considerable sales, marketing, general management and political work. Mr. Suchenski facilitated management and business development for several non-profit organization through-out the US and China. Some of these NGO's include: (Covenant House Washington (DC), Habitat for Humanity (West Virginia & Pennsylvania), Hands on Shanghai (Shanghai, China) and Orphans International (New York City). After experiencing first-hand the consequences of environmental degradation due to inefficient human enterprise from living two years in Taiwan and China, Mr. Suchenski began to rediscover the necessity of business that acknowledges a 'triple' bottom line. The 'triple' bottom line, as opposed to the 'bottom' line acknowledges that while the 'bottom line' is still an important aspect of growth it is only enhanced with the consideration of the environment and society. This discovery, coupled with a life-long passion for sustainability lead Mr. Suchenski to became an environmental consultant. Currently Mr. Suchenski is a LEED AP (Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design) as well as Sustainable Building Advisor and Green Advantage certified. Finally Mr. Suchenski holds an advanced proficiency in mandarin Chinese, and is working on becoming fluent in Spanish.
