Alan - Robert "Alan" Black’s Summary

I have been a creative workplace consultant full time since 1984. Since 1977 I have traveled and worked in 73 countries and 49 US states. My client list includes a mix of Fortune 500 - 10,000 companies in the US and several countries. Since 1979 I have been presenting at several creativity conferences annually around the globe. My primary focus is helping clients and their staffs develop four sets of skills:

Productive Leading
Effective Communicating
Successful Teaming
Highly Creative Thinking and Problem Solving

and developing Cre8ng Communities (tm) throughout their organizations from their front and rear doors to their top floors.

Visit my website for in-depth information: testimonials, lists of clients, sample speeches, workshops and presentation materials.

http://www.cre8ng.com
