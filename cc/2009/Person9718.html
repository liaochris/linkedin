Bas Meelker’s Summary

Bas Meelker (1972) is a well known professional Dutch wildlife and landscape photographer. He began his photographic carreer in 2002 and has been published in several national and international media. He gives workshops and talks and his work area has been extended with project -, travel photography and advertising photography.

His wildlife and nature photography has won several awards in national and international photo contests and several renowned magazines have published articles and interviews concerning Bas Meelker and his work. Among his customers are renowned advertising - and communication companies, several national and international stock agencies and clients such as Canon and Hallmark. His photography reflects his passion for the natural world. His style is characterized by intense landscapes and pin sharp, warm colors full of contrast.

Nature photography and wildlife photography combines Bas' love for nature with his love for photography. Respect for the natural world, not disturbing his subjects and leaving no traces are the rules he plays by.

Bas Meelker’s Specialties:

Wildlife and nature photography, travel photography, search engine marketing, webdesign, communication.
