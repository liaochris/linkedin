Wayne Kessler’s Summary

Wayne Kessler is a customer-focused, results oriented, internet marketing consultant, who helps people create, update, and optimize websites, use the internet to market products or services, and understand how to use the internet for the most effective online and social media marketing business growth techniques. His business, Accelerated Business Services, is based in Massachusetts and provides in-office, or remote services to anywhere in the world.

Wayne Kessler’s Specialties:

Specialty work includes Internet Marketing, Graphic Design, Identity, Branding, Social Network Education, Linkedin/Facebook Consulting, Blog Marketing, Social Media Speaker, Small Business Consulting, Online Marketing, Coaching, Mediation, Counseling, SEO, and Social Media.
