Deb Casper’s Summary

As a full service REALTOR® I strive to provide exceptional service at all times. In addition to servicing clients in the entire Twin Cities Metro, I specialize in the entire Northeast Metro areas of the Twin Cities, including White Bear Lake, Dellwood, Grant, Mahtomedi, Stillwater, Lake Elmo, Hugo, Centerville, Lino Lakes, Maplewood, North St. Paul, Shoreview, Arden Hills, and Woodbury.

Both my e-PRO Certified Internet Professional and Certified Luxury Home Marketing designations offer my clients additional value! You should be aware that out of more than 24,000 REALTORS in the state of Minnesota, only 704 of them have earned the e-PRO Certification. In addition, I am a Founding Member of the Institute for Luxury Home Marketing and have earned the coveted Certified Residential Specialist Designation, the highest designation awarded to sales associates in the residential sales field.

In my continuing effort to stay on top of the market and my commitment to continue educating myself to add value for my clients, I am now a "Green Certified Real Estate Professional" as awarded by the Association of Energy and Environmental Real Estate Professionals. The newest education/designation I am undertaking is the brand new NAR endorsed SFR (Short Sale Foreclosure Resource) certification. I've completed most of the coursework and am on the road to better serving clients who are either buying or selling in this category!

I consider the communities I serve to be excellent places to reside and would love to share with you my enthusiasm for the many neighborhoods that encompass them. I pride myself on providing unparalleled service and look forward to developing a long-term relationship with you. Please feel free to contact me if you would like to discuss how I can best assist you with your real estate needs.
