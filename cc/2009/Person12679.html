Brad Burnett’s Summary

Whether you`re moving across the country or across the street, whether you`re looking for a first home or a place to retire, you've come to the right place! With a diverse range of buying and selling programs and marketing techniques, a network of agents that stretches across North America and beyond, and well developed relationships with top notch service providers, I have a team that is ready to go to work for you!
