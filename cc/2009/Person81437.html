Rafael Marxuach, AIA’s Summary

Curiosity and mathematical aptitude had a big role on the way I grew, since childhood I had a strong inclination for numbers and the unexplored. Those traits were reinforced and further developed during my formation as an artist and architect at Rhode Island School of Design. Practicing architecture has strengthened my ability to: investigate and break up into parts a problem to come up with a creative and focused solution.
As Principal of RML Arquitectos I had managed and led professional teams to perform in a efficient and creative environment, the necessary tasks, to execute many types of architectural projects.

Currently I'm MyarchN, LLC. founder/CEO and RML Arquitectos Principal.
