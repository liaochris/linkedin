Andrew Lohmann’s Summary

Good at interpreting what is actually required and helping to bring that about. Working with people in a consensual way across disciplines.

From concept through simple elegant design of circuitry and software, To Design files parts list and test procedures that result in a good solution. And full lifetime support. Good feel for how electronics and printed circuit boards will actually behave. And how to ensure the circuit functions well

I have a low intervention people management style that is liked and productive. I endeavour to find out the requirement and the lessons from the past at the outset and continuing during development, and product life.

Andrew Lohmann’s Specialties:

My experience includes; Analogue, digital and micro-controllers electronics, embedded C, and assembler.

Interface to sensors including; shaft encoders, temperature sensors, photo diodes, and linear image sensors that require fast data transfer into a micro-controller.

Low voltage and some high voltage switch mode and linear power supplies and power drivers. DC and stepper motors, and other electromechanical devices.
