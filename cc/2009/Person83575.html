Richard Donaldson’s Summary

Richard is a cross between techologist, proven business leader, evangelist/visionary and public speaker.

Richard recently has begun to work on some stealth projects and will be continuing his work inside globalized infrastructure optimization.

Richard worked at UnitedLayer starting in Jan’06 thru the acquisition of ASPextra, a company he co-founded, by UnitedLayer. Over 3yrs, Richard nearly quadrupled UnitedLayer’s size thru organic sales and gorilla marketing tactics. Richard introduced the "Technocratic Oath" - "We will do no technical harm" - and brought a vision of customer service to the company along with evangelizing the vision of a one stop shop providing access to optimized global internet infrastructure.

Prior to co-founding ASPextra, Richard was a strategic, marketing, & business development consultant for three years. His clients ranged from startups to enterprises all of which had technologies that they were bringing to market. Before building his own robust consulting practice, Richard was a business development executive for two leading venture backed IT services startups, SeniorSurfers, bringing the Internet and its services to Older Adults thru a peer tutoring model, and TechPlanet, brining IT services to the small and mid sized business market much like Best Buy's Geek Squad.

Richard started his near 15 year Silicon Valley career with Montgomery Securities (acquired by Bank of America) as an analyst in the private equity group.

Richard hold a B.A. from Colgate University and has completed graduate level work in finance at UC Berkeley. Richard is a frequent speaker on technology, marketing and strategy.

Richard grew up in Minnesota, lived and worked in Europe and upon receiving his B.A. in International Relations, packed a backpack and traveled for a few years primarily as a chef and SCUBA/Ski instructor before landing in the Bay Area. Richard can be found swimming in the Bay regularly training for triathlons.

Richard Donaldson’s Specialties:

Strategy/Mktg - idea creation to execution, change management and "fresh-eye" perspectives, brand building, messaging, campaign design & execution, collateral development

Business Development - lead generation, contract negotiation, alliance building, cross-team communications, large rolodex, economic modeling, pitched many VC & PE firms.

Ops - systematization and integration for replication, scaling up a business, hands-on experience in wired, wireless, internet and HW development
