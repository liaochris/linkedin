Web Developer and Site Optimization Consultant with a strong career history in advertising, marketing, graphic design, sales, seminar and training instruction, analytics, SEO, PPC advertising, ecommerce and web technology.
What we do: We help you raise your ROI using the principles of Optimize - Connect - Convert.
Optimize: How strong is your SEO? Simple HTML "brochure" websites are dead. Dynamic, automated CMS (Content Management System) solutions save you time, and optimize your web mission.
Connect: You need to Connect with and Engage your prospects, because people buy from people they know.
Convert: "Hits" and "Visitors" mean nothing, unless they are converting to leads and customers. And, you should always be testing to increase percentages in your conversion goals.
