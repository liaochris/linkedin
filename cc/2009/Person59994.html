Lone Meineche’s Summary

Licenced systemic in-and outdoor trainer with Master Degree in Strategy and Management offers coaching and seminars to companies and private persons. I have worked within the field of leadership development within companies in Denmark and Germany. I have worked with adults and children, and have also been involved in sales in various ways.

I have specialised in working with people, who stand at professional or private crossroads after which their lives change significantly, and who need a glimpse of their nearer future or next possible action steps. My work is based on a systemic foundation and it often takes place outdoor. Sofar my customers come from Spain, UK and Denmark.

Further work themes include biography (personal or managerial), teambuilding, project start-up, open space technology, personal and leadership development, and systemic coaching.

I am interested in new ventures and network partners, who are interested in my competencies and skills or whom I may cooperate with.

I speak English, German, Danish. I am still working on perfecting my Spanish.

Lone Meineche’s Specialties:

I work with individuals and groups within themes such as development of perspectives for the future, personal resources, biography, team-building and team-development, open space, project start-up. I work in a systemic way of manner in- and outdoors. The target group is companies and private persons. I offer coaching and seminars primarily in Denmark and Spain. I am open for other geographic suggestions.
