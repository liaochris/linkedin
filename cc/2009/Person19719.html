Ifor Ffowcs-Williams’s Summary

E4@clusternavigators.com

Recognised globally as a leader in the development of clusters:
• Training economic development professionals in the process of cluster development;
• The start-up and review of cluster, innovation and competitiveness programmes;
• Establishing local cluster development initiatives;
• Leading cluster workshops;
• Speaking at economic development conferences on five continents.

Global client base includes World Bank, IFC, OECD, UNIDO, Commonwealth Secretariat, Swedish Aid Programme (Sida/SAREC), US Aid and New Zealand Aid.

Also national and regional public agencies across Australia, Bahamas, Bangladesh, Cambodia, Canada, China, Czech Republic, Denmark, England, Estonia, Finland, Georgia, Iceland, Ireland, Kenya, Korea, Latvia, Mozambique, New Zealand, Norway, Pakistan, Poland, Saudi Arabia, Scotland, South Africa, Sweden, Switzerland, Tanzania, Thailand, Trinidad, U.A.E., Uganda, USA, Wales ...

Ifor Ffowcs-Williams’s Specialties:

Introducing the relevance of cluster based economic development; presenting interactive cluster training workshops.

Founder and Past President of The Competitiveness Institute (Barcelona, Spain), the global organisation for cluster practitioners. Now serving on the Board of Advisors.
