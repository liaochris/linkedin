Cece Salomon-Lee’s Summary

I build and execute successful strategies for public relations, customer communications, conferences/speaker programs and analyst relations. I have over 15 years experience working with start-up organizations. This includes enterprise software and digital entertainment. Throughout my career, I have developed best practices and processes for combining emerging tech trends with existing strategies.

I am also the author of PR Meets Marketing blog. The blog highlights the intersection of public relations with marketing. My blog is listed on the Advertising Age top 500 blogs and has been ranked on several PR lists.
