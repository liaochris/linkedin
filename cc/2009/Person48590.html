Joel Warwick’s Summary

Joel draws from 20 years of experience with media and publishing technology in his independent consulting practice. Over the last 10 years he has led teams developing content strategies, business cases, and in the design and implementation of content management and publishing systems – with an emphasis on rich-media and digital asset management. Involved in developing and deploying a wide range of technologies, from large scale enterprise systems to consumer shrink-wrap software. Built numerous enterprise media, publishing, and content management systems as a consultant to end use firms, technology vendors, and in management positions with software companies.

Consulted for media, entertainment, and publishing firms, global marketing operations, and technology vendors. Prior to consulting, held senior management positions in software firms that developed media-related solutions for print, online, and multimedia operations, and content distribution systems.

Maintain in-depth knowledge of media and publishing technologies and related business processes. Offer a unique blend of business and financial analysis, workflow and process design, and enterprise system implementation experience – both front-end user applications and core systems. Developer-level technical knowledge with ability to effectively interpret and translate among non-technical audiences across all organizational levels. Expert in imaging and color science.
