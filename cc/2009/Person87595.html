Chris Rychter’s Summary

As a design consultant, I'm focusing on delivering proper design to companies that need a visual facelift to improve and promote their business. I also offer an exceptional level of book and journal/magazine cover and layout design in any publishing setting (academic, trade, b2b). My roots are in book design, but I also embraced the path of 'proper' art by getting involved in Book Arts. One of my works has been bought by the Tate Library and is featured in their Book Arts collection (cat. no. 0511925 and 0511926).

Chris Rychter’s Specialties:

Layout design, typesetting, typography, artworking, production management, liaising with printers, PDF production, magazine and book publishing, production process supervision and planning. Adobe Creative Suite and QuarkXPress skills.
