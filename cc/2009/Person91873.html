Mark Shuler’s Summary

Over 20 years of professional experience as an Architect and Business Owner demonstrating strong organizational, oral and written communication skills, entrepreneurial hustle and the ability to work with others on a project team. Results oriented leader with proven success in design, project management and business development culminating in a portfolio of over 350 completed projects. Critical thinker and adept manager who applies a comprehensive understanding of the mind-set and skills of building owners, financial sources, architects, engineers, contractors and governmental agencies having jurisdictional authority while managing, motivating, and guiding project teams.

Mark Shuler’s Specialties:

Urban infill development; high-end single family residential architecture servicing custom and real estate development clientele; mixed-use; multi-family; international aid and development focusing on medical facilities and water purification projects.
