Jon Osterholm’s Summary

I am a writing, creative and publishing professional with a B.S. degree in business administration. I have passion for practical creativity and real teamwork. I am creeped out by people who seemingly cannot laugh.

Included in my experience are small biz development and management, news writing/editing, technical writing/illustration, publication production, prepress/printing, and marketing creative. I have knowhow in Web, video and audio media, and interests in many fields.

Over several years, I helped start a multimedia studio, founded and managed a suburban newspaper, and was a vital leader in the launch of a big city daily (these days, an online source that prints twice weekly). I continued in news media at a Tribune Company operation and writing freelance for a city magazine. About four years ago, I began doing technical writing, illustration and proposals work.
