Rajeev Singh’s Summary

My passion is to focus on organization design and set up teams and structures that can accelerate change. I like to address challenges such as improving effectiveness of teams, increasing the penetration and adoption of new methodologies, and interweaving innovations in IT with business strategies.

My clients have included large multi-national corporations, small start-up businesses, and non-profit endeavors. I routinely engage in consulting assignments for Healthcare, Management Consulting, Insurance, Financial Services, Energy, and Online Media organizations.

At ThoughtWorks, I have been heavily focused on Agile and Lean practices to enhance business effectiveness. This has also provided me opportunities to coach and train clients in change management where I have used a mix of analysis, solution design, technology, project and delivery management, and strategy skills.
