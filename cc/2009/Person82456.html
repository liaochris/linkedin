Rodolfo Rigo Calabrezi’s Summary

Co-founded four companies, one focused in IT and three related to computer games.

With multidisciplinary knowledge and self taught, has been learning computer language since 7 years old. Has developed skills on several areas such as software engineering, graphics, music composition, 3D modeling, 2D animation and art, desktop publishing, design, game and web development, entrepreneurism, creative direction and business.

Produced and developed various applications, web 2.0 front-ends, intranets/extranets, techdemos and games. Has worked on different platforms like PC, mobile/PocketPC, Amiga, MSX and TRS-Color. Knows Linux, Windows and Workbench operating systems as well as hardware assembling, set up and electronic basics. He is also skilled in researching, project management, writing, lecturing and coaching.

His latest endeavor, Quantix Games, is a great project focused on the casual games market, having a studio and providing, along partners around the world, services regarding game production and outsourcing.
