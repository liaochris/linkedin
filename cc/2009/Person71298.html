Nalini Marthi’s Summary

I have driven myself to comprehend the entire gamut of software applications right from chip level to the real world IT applications; this skill gave me a unique opportunity of working with appropriate technologies.

As I grew from a scientist level to an entrepreneur level, I am able relate the evolution of these technologies and their contribution to prosperity of various communities in the world.

While building my philosophies of work during my career, I unequivocally admired and admitted that ‘Object Orientation Methodologies’ contributed much more to my product building abilities, organizational management skills, and conflict management while leading technical personnel than any other management philosophy.

I want to submit to the world’s management community that Object Orientation Methodologies are not just for developers to develop huge software systems, but they are meant for today’s managers to comprehend global organizations, their work cultures and their evolutions into highly productive units.

I want to propagate this philosophy of Object Orientation effectively in all Management Institutes.
