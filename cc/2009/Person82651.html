Rebecca Roebuck’s Summary

I grew up in Lancaster, OH and received a BA from Kenyon College in Gambier, OH. At Kenyon, I became interested in Sociology and the different ways people find truth and meaning in modern society. I was especially interested in how people found meaning online through online communities, fandom and internet relationships. This lead to my personal and professional interest in social media and online networks. I have built and created social networks and communities for clients nationwide. I am excited to be involved in the new and rapidly growing world of social media.
