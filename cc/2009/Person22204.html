Daniel Brody’s Summary

A pioneering and accomplished Business Development, Sales / Marketing and Professional Services Technology Executive with profit and loss responsibility as well as extensive experience in product launches, ERP, systems analysis, network administration, hardware/software, disaster recovery, IT security, help desk operations, production scheduling and major system upgrades.

Daniel Brody is the Managing Director of Steelman Software Solutions Inc. Since 2002 Daniel has performed high-level client needs analysis and led sales, marketing, strategic partnership efforts and project management, as the senior manager.

As part of a three-person management team controlling an operational budget of three million dollars, Daniel increased revenues in products and services from start up to ten million dollars within three years.

He has extensive experience selling directly to C-level executives, General Managers and Presidents. Daniel has been published regularly in leading metal trade magazines and has sat on the Steel Industry Systems Association board.

Daniel has a wide range of experience with over 19 years in the industry, starting in 1989 with Midland Doherty Financial Corporation who merged with Walwyn Inc. to form Midland Walwyn in 1990. He is a specialist in marketing, financial, operational and information technology, with a track record of successfully managed implementations in manufacturing, supply chain and warehouse management facilities across North America.

Daniel has a successful history of launching and building software companies. He has gained a wide range of industry experiences in successful complex projects across the world. He provides clients with best-of-breed information on current technology.
