Simon Samuel’s Summary

Set up Talentarc in July 2006 as a Media and Communications search and recruitment company. Based just north of Soho in Great Titchfield Street.

Affiliate company Edge specialises in entry to mid level roles in the same sector.

In 2008 set up a joint venture, Pacific Group, a finance and tax specialist focussed on the employment sector.

MBA from London Business School and prior 9 years within a global HR, Consulting and Recruitment Group. Specific experience included:

* Headed up International (non UK) Division of US Listed employment services group
* Mergers and acquisitions, commercial due dilligence, valuation.
* Branding - intenal and external communications audit and architechture.
* Built division of UK business organically to £30 million and multi client
* Recruited several successful teams both internally and for clients
