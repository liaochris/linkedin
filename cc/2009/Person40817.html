Ian Renert’s Summary

Ian Renert knows a few things about risk. As the Chief Projects Manager for Keats & Co Ltd., Renert is responsible for conceptualizing projects, as well as designing and marketing them. His ability to gather research on distressed properties helped Keats & Co gain above-average returns on their investments. One reason for Renert’s success is that he is able to understand the real estate market from a global perspective.
Previously, Renert was the President for Hawthorne-Sterling & Co. Inc. where he spent 11 years as a researcher, author, and manager. The company specializes in research for niche areas in international banking and finance. The company has a proven record, selling their research to internationally renowned financial institutes as well as top tier law firms. His knowledge of certain subjects made him a key expert in some legal cases.
His tenacity to research and then analyze it proficiently can be derived from his experience in marketing, working for the likes of Cavendish Management Group as the Director of Marketing. Renert ensured that the private equity company gained the exposure necessary to sell its products and services to financial advisors. The company provides consulting and investments for forex markets and complex financial transactions. In addition, Renert worked for Mortimer & Co as an Assistant Marketing Manager, designing and marketing financial services in order to ensure that they fulfill clients’ needs in-house.
Ian Renert spends his spare time tutoring university students on business subjects as well as designing couture for men. Furthermore, he is a member of the Chamber of Commerce, Word Trade Centre Association, and the Better Business Bureau.
Ian Renert graduated from Boston University with a BSBA in 1983.
