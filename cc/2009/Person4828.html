Amy Kolln’s Summary

Why I am here on LinkedIn:

• I am seeking opportunities to provide consulting services to help others with their Customer Relationship Management system needs. I help review current keep-in-touch systems and develop a campaign of greeting cards and postcards that will fit the needs of the salesperson/business.

• I am particularly experienced and talented at creating single and multiple-card campaigns, working with other contact database systems to import contacts and developing custom cards with graphic design in Adobe Photoshop Elements 5.0.

I am interested in talking to others who are looking for an online, FUN, business opportunity. 80% of my team is in Iowa and I am looking to help others outside of Iowa to know about this NEW, EASY way to send REAL CARDS from your computer in LESS than 60 seconds.

I host networking events that attract other relationship/business type personalities to an exciting, convenient and fun venue. We also host a larger special event periodically, called the "Treat 'Em Right" Seminar.

In August of 2006, I began recruiting people to work from home and now have over 730 people on my team. Many of these people have promoted in our company. Our average card sender sends 3 cards/day.

Businesses and Non-Profit associations hire me to help them increase their customer retention rate and warm market referrals.
