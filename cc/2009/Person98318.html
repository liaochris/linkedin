Thomas DeLorenzo’s Summary

I am known for taking relatively unknown entities and developing them into major players. I think outside the box and utilize every method possible to achieve my goals on behalf of my clients. I firmly believe in taking advantage of new technology to achieve these goals.

Some of the talent I have worked with or am currently working with include: Kathy Joosten (Desperate Housewives), Heather Tom (Bold and the Beautiful), Chad Allen (Save Me), Sheryl Lee Ralph, Allison Mack (Smallville), Nancy Sinatra, Carter Oosterhouse (Trading Spaces), Jensen Ackles (SuperNatural), Bobbi Eakes (All My Children) and Cameron Romero. I have launched several new products, including Clothing of the American Mind, Something Wicked Candles, and Aromabands.

For major events, I assist my clients with styling -- helping maintain and expand their image -- through my relationships with major couture houses and jewelers.

In addition to my public relations work, I also write for the HuffingtonPost.com on the politics page discussing health care.

Honors I have received are Unsung Heroes in the Fight Against HIV/AIDS in The New York Times and Angel of the Year from the San Antonio AIDS Foundation. The Today Show utilizes me as an industry expert in Celebrity Journalism and Web 2.0. A&U Magazine also profiled me for my work on behalf of people with HIV/AIDS. Other magazines that have quoted me include Page 6, InTouch, TVGuide, and Life and Style.

In these challenging economic times, I am working to create new revenue streams for my clients, which includes developing new markets for their products, creating speaking opportunities, and branding their name on a variety of products.
