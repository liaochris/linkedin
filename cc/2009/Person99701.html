Tolga Özek’s Summary

Tolga Özek has graduated from Hacettepe University, Statistics Department. He worked for Escort FSTATS as Turkcell Super League Ankara matches operator for 3 years and Deputy Project Manager at BNB Consulting for 6 months.

Then he chose focusing on Internet Media. He began working firstly editor for Kimkazandi.com (owned and operated under Akro Tanıtım advertising agency) Turkey's best promotions & campaigns information web site. During improvement process of web site traffic, he decided to take advertisement position too and with indiviual efforts he acquired many ads such as Bilyoner, Reader's Digest, Reklamizlekazan.com, AkilFikir.com.tr, Hayat Su(Danone), Milka M-Joy, Votka1967, Turkcell, Efiyat.com, Secmenkonusuyor.com, Blinko, Veronelli, Samsung Fun Club.

He wrote on NTVSPOR.NET between 2005 November-2009 May since web site has been founded. His main focus on articles was Turkcell Super League Ankara teams. He is sports writer @ SporX.com which is the highest-traffic sports web site in Turkey.

He has begun working with Yarismaca.com, Turkey's one of best competition web site, on media planning and gained Lipton "Her Seyi Bilen Kadın", Magnum "Sadece Oyna", Magnum "Ev Kampanyası" and Samsung Fun Club banners. And his new field in media planning is Hangibar.com, entertainment & live music information web site.

He begun media planning for two blogs with great visitors / pageview figures recently. So, on NefisYemekTarifleri.com and Tarifiyemek.com, there will be ads online working with Zap Medya for Ulker Halk. First ad on TarifiYemek.com working with Ping for Sutas and secondly Veronelli ad on working with Sr Medya.

The latest web site he handles is Sozluk.net which has almost 2 million visitors and 10 million pageviews per month. And the first ad will be on air at August 24 working with Maxus for Samsung.



He wants to go forward in sports writing and internet media & advertising career
