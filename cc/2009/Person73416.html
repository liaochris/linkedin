Nel Santiago’s Summary

A Creative Director based out of Dallas, Texas. I have been in the Broadcast News Industry for nearly 10 years, 15 years freelancing. If there was one thing I knew early on, it was that I wanted to design.

Learning the basics of graphic design, silk screening and pre-press preparation. I started out doing flyers, ads, shirts and CDs, basically anything I could do to get my foot in the door. I had quite a few projects under my belt before I even started college, coincidentally enough the same week I turned 17 (yes I got an early start). I eventually graduated from the Art Institute of Fort Lauderdale, with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Animation. Many years later and a few stations in between I am now a father of one and enjoying a successful career doing the stuff I've always loved.

I am currently on staff as an Editor and Creative Director at Hothaus Creative. Using my skills to create high-end graphics, editing and compositing for many of today's top Cable and Television Networks.
