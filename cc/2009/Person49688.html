John Tobias’s Summary

Electrical engineer and scientist specializing in electrical power, lightning, electromagnetic radiation, grounding, electrocution/electrical accidents, electrical fires (including batteries,) lighting/illumination and safety. Licensed Professional Engineer (New Jersey.) Provides consultation services for design, intellectual property, and litigation.

Past experience includes design work for government telecommunications and power systems.

Military experience includes service in an infantry unit (1st Battalion, 5th Infantry Regiment, 2nd Infantry Division) and service as a military intelligence officer. Completed military service at the rank of major serving as a brigade intelligence officer.
