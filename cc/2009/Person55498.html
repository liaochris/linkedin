Kilian Valkhof’s Summary

I am a Dutch webdeveloper working for Fluxility Design. I am passionate about standard and the semantic web, and take great pride in writing lean and meaningful code. Next to that I also has a weblog filled with ideas about CSS, javascript and other web-related issues.

I knows my way around HTML/XHTML, XML, CSS, the DOM, PHP and graphic design programs like Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator and Inkscape (SVG).

I developed a CSS Framework: SenCSs (http://sencss.kilianvalkhof.com) which has been downloaded over 5000 times and has helped me cut down about 50% of my development time without resorting to unsemantic class names.

Kilian Valkhof’s Specialties:

excellent XHTML and CSS coding skills, good graphic design skills, good PHP and Javascript/AJAX/DOM coding skills. Writes semantic code based on web standards and the dutch quality model for websites.
