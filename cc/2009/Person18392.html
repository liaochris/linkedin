Christian Wernberg-Tougaard’s Summary

I believe in technology. But technology acts transformational. Everydays tasks and challenges are altered by technology. And if you don't understand how technology works today and tomorrow, you won't be able to strategize the right actions to make tomorrow a better place.

So - that is what I do - I try to influence people and organizations to see opportunities in (new) technology. Through my network I lobby, strategize and envision how change can be implemented, how innovative measures and tools can be applicated to the world of today to pave the road for the world of tomorrow.

If you want to better understand the options for the companies of tomorrow through the world of today, I will urge you to read more on in4change.com and to contact me.

Christian Wernberg-Tougaard’s Specialties:

Government special advisor, full-value chain knowledge, branding and eIdentity, policy-presure, organizational influence, lobbyisme. ENISA WG expert on "Awareness Raisning". Member of the "IT-panel" that recommends on IT-security to the Danish Government. EU reviewer under FP7 program.
