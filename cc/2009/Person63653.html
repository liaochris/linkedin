Mark S. Meritt’s Summary

Whether starting with an inspiration or a need to be filled, I excel at inventing ideas to achieve desirable results. Combining a wide pool of knowledge across countless disciplines with research skills that empower new learning, I analyze a situation and make connections to generate novel possibilities for meeting the challenge.

Informed by an understanding of general systems principles, I identify the most compelling, highest leverage options and develop comprehensive plans for implementation. When an idea taps into my core competencies in music, writing or information analysis and synthesis, I can transform the blueprint into reality with high quality.

At present, I am focused on innovative artistic ventures, including original and custom-created musical, dramatic and written works, as well as workshops and coaching to facilitate collaborative creativity. To provide additional support as I pursue these goals, I am also seeking business consulting work, especially when it can use my high level of expertise creating tools, doing analysis and problem-solving with Microsoft Excel.
