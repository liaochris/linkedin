Giulia Blasi’s Summary

I am currently working as blogger and content writer for Mondadori, Condé Nast and Yooplus. I am also in the process of completing a book for Einaudi Stile Libero.

have a keen interest in media and communication, and am eager to learn new things and make new experiences. I would particularly like to do more television work, which I really enjoy and have done with satisfying results in the past.
I am particularly attracted to web television, a relatively new media that is developing quickly and opening up a lot of new opportunities for doing work that is truly groundbreaking.

In summer 2007 I worked as a script doctor for film company Pixit. The feature film I worked on, Stefano Bessoni's début "Imago Mortis", was released in January 2009.

From 30th May, 2005 to 8th January, 2007 I worked for Rai, the national television network in Italy. I started out as part of the cast of presenters for Futura TV, and later went on to write and host Larsen, a weekly live music show that started out as an afternoon 30-minute slot and ended up as a 120' prime-time show.
