Lisa Ann Martin’s Summary

Founded and launched an image consulting service to enable people to coordinate their inner and outer beauty. Empower clients to promote a confident personal brand image that fuels success. Provide a holistic approach to style choices.

I have always had a passion for fashion; providing portfolio fashion tips and fashion advice. For years, I have helped family, friends, and co-workers select pieces ideal for their individual comfort and style. I parlayed that experience into a successful business as an image consultant.

I have an eagerness for helping others and assisting them in reaching their goals and full potential. I believe that when you feel great, you look great. I trust my real expertise has come from overcoming many obstacles and loving the life I created for myself.

I make several buying trips a year to fashion meccas such as New York, in order to evaluate the clothing and footwear markets. Having met many designers personally, I cherish my meetings with Manolo Blahnik, which led to a photo opportunity with him in the New York Post in 2003. In addition, a desire to pursue a path in fashion footwear, prompted me to attend the Puccio & MacKay School of shoe making and design in 2005.

I worked many years in the financial banking business, corporate charity arena, with most recently producing a satellite radio show. With this array of experiences, dealing with a variety of personas, continuing to learn about the importance of appearance and the impression it can leave upon a person within seconds. All leading to the pursuit of my passion: style.
