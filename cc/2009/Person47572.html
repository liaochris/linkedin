Jim Swauger’s Summary

I am a licensed private investigator that specializes in computer forensics and corporate investigations. In over 11 years of service to both law enforcement and private industry, I have managed hundreds of civil, criminal, and corporate internal cases. I am regularly engaged to investigate various employment issues and analyze digital evidence for litigation. In some cases, I am then called upon to present investigative findings during administrative proceedings (such as unemployment hearings) or provide expert testimony at trial. Additionally, I help clients identify potential sources of digital evidence and advise on proper digital evidence collection and preservation techniques.
