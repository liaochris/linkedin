Vlad Lodzinski’s Summary

A pioneering and creative Media Executive; an expert in TV and video rich cross-platform entertainment formats, content & delivery; strong knowledge of the strategic, business, technical and logistical processes involved in making success in the competitive multi-platform media & TV environment. A leader: dynamic; determined; lateral thinker; motivational; visionary.

Vlad has now taken his creative, production, entrepreneurial and operational skills to the video-centric music business LP33.tv which launched in beta at the end of 2008 to much acclaim in the press. The site is developing and establishing itself aggressively and quickly.

Vlad is an experienced Executive, having launched channels (creatively, operationally, commercially) for both large conglomerates (MTV, UPC) in the UK and in Europe, as well as smaller, entrepreneurial businesses. Vlad has also had a number of high profile creative roles at companies as diverse at LWT (ITV), Universal Studios and The Betting Channel (Avago). Vlad has also worked entrepreneurially with a number of new media ventures as well as acting as a consultant.
