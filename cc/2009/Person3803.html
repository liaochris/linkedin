Ali Kabas’s Summary

Based in Istanbul, Turkey, photographer Ali Kabas works in fine art, advertising, corporate, aerial and underwater photography.

Ali was born into a family involved in the arts. He completed his business and art education in the US. He holds WPI BS and Columbia MBA degrees.

Ali Kabas gives complete and reliable digital and film photographic services of highest quality to his clients in his studio and on location. He loves creative lighting and capturing what is invisible to the human eye. His technical capabilities are in par with modern technology. Ali understands the importance of vision and its ultimate realization. His view on photography is that it is a tool with no limits for bringing together reality and dreams.

He's had four solo exhibitions in Istanbul and one in New York. His clients include international corporations as well as numerous international magazines and publishers. Ali is a member of the American Society of Picture Professionals, Stock Artists Alliance and a Getty Images Photo Assignments photographer. His web site is www.alikabas.com.
