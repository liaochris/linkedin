Tom Ball’s Summary

Quick introduction, I am a passionate innovator - I live for new ideas. I love to build companies. To change industries (aim high!). To achieve "the impossible".

I was once told that I am "helplessly in love with people". Not sure if it's a good thing - but I rather liked it, and hope it's at least partly true! I LOVE spending time with good people. To me "good" means people that are passionate about life and something that they do - you get "good" people in every walk of life - and often where you least expect it. I'm getting an ever increasing soft spot for kids - how much energy do little folk have?!

Favourite Days: Getting engaged (*The* moment Jenny said yes below ;o) Getting married (Got lots of photos of that - one below!) and the CD9 office launch party in November 2000 - 200 amazing people, Frank Sinatra live in the board room, fireworks out the back, a bar in the lift, me in Red tails & white tie ... and the team from heaven - joy! People often ask me where I get my energy, enthusiasm and zest for life from. Hopefully you'll meet her one day.

Recent History: I spent five years building an Internet consultancy to 25 full time people, many blue chip clients and $2M turnover. Then restructured to a new niche and then finally gone through a full liquidation. A fairly educational few years.
