Bob Moran’s Summary

Mortgage Planner that helps clients use their home mortgage as the centerpiece of a sound spending plan to eliminate debt and create wealth. My practice is built on referrals from Financial Planners, CPA's, and people in my professional network.

Because what I do is unique to the mortgage industry, I also market "voluntary mortgage benefit plans" to corporations.

I am an accomplished humorous speaker (former instructor for Dale Carnegie and a Toastmaster for over 20 years). I deliver seminars and give entertaining presentations about mortgage topics to outside groups, association meetings, and conventions.

I am the author of the upcoming book "How to Use a Mortgage Plan to Get Out of Debt and Create Family Wealth" and have developed mortgage planning software that I market to mortgage professionals.

For samples of client materials send me an e-mail, or contact me via twitter at: www.twitter.com/BobMoran
