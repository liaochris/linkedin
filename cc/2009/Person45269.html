Jeffrey Buchanan’s Summary

I have over 26 years of national and international experience in finance, law and business. My background as both an executive utilizing professional services and as an accountant, lawyer, and investment banker rendering professional services has given me a deep appreciation of clients’ needs in an advisory relationship. I also have had a broad based experience in a variety of industries and my multi-disciplinary background is helpful when dealing with complex issues. Prior to my investment banking career, I was the CFO of a NYSE company that provided a broad range of electronics manufacturing services throughout the world. I have was also practiced at two large law firms and at a Big 4 Auditing firm.
