Georganna Hancock’s Summary

First published in 1964. Active freelance writer and public relations consultant since 1970. Now writing, editing, ghostwriting, and creating websites for fellow writers. Also assisting authors on the path to publication.

Georganna Hancock’s Specialties:

Designing and developing websites for writers, books and authors.
