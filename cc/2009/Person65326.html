Matt McGee’s Summary

I'm an online marketing consultant and trainer. I help clients acquire organic traffic via SEO, social marketing, local search marketing, blogging, and more. I'm a speaker on the Search Marketing Expo and Search Engine Strategies conference circuits, and I speak at other events around the country (like ad:tech, Small Business Marketing Unleashed, Learn About Web, etc.)

I'm a prolific writer. I blog at SmallBusinessSEM.com and HyperlocalBlogger.com, two of my own sites. I also write industry news and articles for Search Engine Land. I'm also a regular contributor to various search marketing forums and blogs.

I founded, manage, and write regularly for @U2, an independent site for U2 fans online since 1995. My first book, U2-A DIARY, was published in November, 2008.

Matt McGee’s Specialties:

Organic search engine optimization techniques, SEO, natural SEO, social media marketing and consulting, local search marketing, local SEO, business consulting, online marketing, blogging.
