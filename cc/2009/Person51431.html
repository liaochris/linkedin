Jim McNamara’s Summary

Jim McNamara is the founder and President of Wasabi Mobile. He is a business development and sales leader in the mobile marketing and advertising global marketplace.

He has developed mobile advertising sales strategy and led mobile advertising sales for two top tier global operators/carriers; Sprint (US) and Telefonica/O2 (UK/EU). Prior to Sprint he spent 12 years leading strategic marketing, sales growth, and operational development for a number of firms and ad agencies in the digital marketing filed including Harte Hanks where he was VP of the global direct marketer's digital practice.

Mr. McNamara has strong experience across all aspects of mobile advertising/marketing, digital marketing, direct marketing and CRM. He has served as an executive, board member, consultant and speaker with start-ups, emerging growth firms, industry trade groups and establishing new mobile/digital divisions within large corporate organizations.
