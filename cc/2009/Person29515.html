Edward Clark’s Summary

With nearly 20 years of Professional IT experience, I am well seasoned in the discipline of Application Development. During my 12 year tenure with The Home Depot, I have built strong interpersonal relationships with valued internal customers. My strong service orientation and bias for action has served well in responding to the needs and concerns of our customers.

Edward Clark’s Specialties:

Application Development, Project Manager in a wide variety of business applications. Particularly strong in client/server and relational database design using Informix/Oracle/Sybase/mSQL. Strong knowledge of Java J2SE/J2EE, HTML/XML, Perl, PHP, AJAX, Web Page Design, Web Services. I have developed Home Automation/Security Software (www.edtreehouse.com) currently in beta and being tested by numerous Home Automation enthusiasts.
