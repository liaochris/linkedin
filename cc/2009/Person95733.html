Bernt Sulebust’s Summary

I have many years of experience with complex operation and supervision of multiple international Domino Notes networks and domains (e.g. Hydro, StatoilHydro and Yara) including a large number of servers, thousands of users and a range of Domino-related products and services on different operating systems and versions.

During the last year I have also been working almost full time in projects implementing, migrating and operating Exchange 2007 and Office Communications Server solutions for large international customers.

I have also participated in several national and international projects. These projects have handled a range of tasks from consolidation of solutions, splitting of networks and systems to testing and implementation of new new products and services (including writing procedures and documentation).

In addition to Domino Operations, I have had special focus on archiving solutions (e.g. IBM CommonStore) and different collaboration tools like Teamrooms, Sharepoint, IM-solutions/Sametime, Webmetings, etc, both for internal and external use.

I hold several certifications within my field of work.

Part of my job has been to follow up and collaborate with external vendors to solve problems and find appropriate solutions as well as 3rd. line troubleshooting and user assistance/support on both software and hardware for our customers.

I have also worked with general network, user- and server administration on different platforms and operating systems.
