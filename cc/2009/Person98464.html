Tom Davis’s Summary

Executive with deep telecommunications technology and financial industry experience. Proven record in identifying, analyzing and communicating critical success variables. A thought leader with impressive breadth of vision and problem solving skills.

President of Cygnus Associates, a competitive intelligence consulting company that helps clients better analyze their competition, their strategic customers, suppliers and partners. Over 20 years specializing in market intelligence in the telecommunications and financial services industries.

Currently exploring opportunities to contribute my expertise in financial analysis and competitive & market intelligence.

My goals are to help clients identify their information needs then develop and deploy new competitive tools such as Predictive Markets that rank, value and aggregate disparate sources of internal and external competitive data.
