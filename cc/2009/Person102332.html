Vitor Domingos’s Summary

Mostly known as "vd", with a civil engineer background, is a long time opensource evangelist, systems and mobile catalyst, deeply involved in technology, began working on several portuguese telecoms doing support and infrastructure, then jumped into the B2B bandwagon when the bubble burst as web architect, went for a three year ride nurturing online corporate and home banking systems infrastructure on the national public bank, with public duty at the portuguese Ministry of Justice while head of software development, presidential election voting system and national citizen card, was recently doing marketing and operations for 7Syntax. In his spare time, he's bootstrapping Mobile Monday Portuguese chapter and an online video show. In real life, he's a proud father of one daughter, driver addict, living 30km nearby north Lisbon, Portugal.
