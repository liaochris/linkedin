Jim Casper’s Summary

I publish and edit Lens Culture (www.lensculture.com), a popular international online magazine celebrating contemporary photography, art, media, and world cultures. The site currently attracts 8,000 - 10,000+ unique visitors each day.

As an active member in the contemporary photography world, I collaborate in formal partnerships to develop and promote important international photography events and conferences, curate arts exhibitions, write about photography and culture for magazines and books, lecture, serve as an international juror and portfolio reviewer, and act as an international artists' agent.

I work as a consultant with people and organizations to develop successful business programs to promote products and services to influential international cultural, arts and education audiences.
