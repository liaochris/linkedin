Catalin Zafiu’s Summary

As a Project Manager in real estate field, at spanish developer Grupo Lar, I have coordinated 3 projects that worth more than 100 million euro, having the ability to mange multiple tasks, to organize and prioritize work to meet deadlines.

Before Grupo LAR I was employed at Colliers International as Marketing and Design Coordinator. My primary responsibilities included positioning the company on the market and help my colleagues to handle all the situations regarding the company image.

My background includes both architecture and marketing experience, which contributed to my abilities to supervise projects and develop the marketing function of the firm through development of a standard design for the company materials and promotion of the Colliers brand, organizing events and successfully dealing with negotiations.
