Daniel Lynx Bernard’s Summary

Strategic communications. Combining the rapid research and analysis of journalism with the vision and tactical coordination of public policy. Coordination of advocacy campaigns strengthened by experience as a trainer and instructor.

Daniel Lynx Bernard’s Specialties:

Expert in media and communications strategy, policy analysis, and training.
Longtime political journalist with experience in print, broadcast, and Internet. Consultant in internal/external communications, public awareness, outreach strategy for U.S. and international governmental agencies and nonprofits.
Specialization in governance and civil society in the U.S. and Mideast.
Writing coach, trainer, university-level instructor. Proficient in Modern Standard Arabic, Egyptian Arabic, French.
