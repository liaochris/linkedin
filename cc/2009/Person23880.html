David Businelli’s Summary

Architecture that brings meaning, value, and the highest quality to the lives of our clients by creating signature, world class living & working spaces.

How do we do that?

As a world class Architectural Firm, Studio 16 Architecture creates:

Homes that are unique to our clients' lifestyles; Living spaces that nurture the family and surround you with beauty; spaces that energize vacations and make retirement your true golden years.

Commercial spaces in which to grow your business, serve your customers with excellence and increase your bottom line.

Restaurants that provide your customers a unique dining experience and keep them coming back.

Institutional spaces that inspire learning, maximize occupant comfort and safety and reflect the values of your organization.

David Businelli’s Specialties:

Custom homes and apartments, restaurants, community facilites, offices, retail establishments. mixed use buildings, adaptive reuse and alterations.

    I conceived and founded, along with Timothy Boyland, AIA, the AIA Staten Island Design Awards as a response to the Staten Island Chamber of Commerce Building Awards. The program's mission was to educate the public about what good architecture is and why it is important as well as to celebrate excellence in architectural design.
    This world class program featured internationally known architects Peter Eisenman, FAIA, Steven Holl, AIA, Terrence Riley, Marlon Blackwell, AIA, Duo Dikinson and landscape architect James Corner as keynote speakers.
