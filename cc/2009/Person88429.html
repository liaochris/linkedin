Sandy Weaver Carman’s Summary

I spent three decades on the air in major markets, evenly split between Top 40 and Country formats. In both, logged a bunch of hours as Music Director, Assistant Program Director, Assistant Promotions Director and Public Affairs Director.

In 2008 I launched Voicework On Demand, Inc., a company which provides audio services to radio & tv, speakers, coaches and authors.

In 2009 VOD has begun producing a line of audio books, and in the near future will begin producing programs for radio. I look forward to being part of something completely different, completely entertaining, and completely fun!

Sandy Weaver Carman’s Specialties:

RCS proficient, Prophet experienced, Talent Coach, music editing, voicework of all kinds, including cartoon voices.
