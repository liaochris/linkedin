Mo Elnadi’s Summary

For the past 10 years, Mo’s online expertise focused on Online Marketing, eCommerce and Search Engine Optimization (SEO), with deep knowledge of end-to-end online customer experience roadmaps.

Mo is well versed in online digital strategies as well as usability best practices, web 2.0, social media campaigns, and web analytics.

Mo has spearheaded several Online/eCommerce portal projects for multinational corporations in the EMEA region and US including: Vodafone, Philips, Microsoft, Corel, Adobe, Otis, Carrier, Panasonic, MobiNiL, and UTC.

As an online marketing professional Mo is regularly invited to give early recommendations on future online services by Google and Microsoft.

Interested in networking with professionals who are involved in Online Marketing, Social Media, Search Engine Optimisation, Viral Marketing and User Centric Design.
