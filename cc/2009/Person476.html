Aaron Dodd’s Summary

Formerly, Odin Consulting (founded 2002), The Mindset Group provides the expertise and tools to assist leaders to transform an organisation’s culture, capability and processes, thereby sustaining change for themselves, their people and society.

We help leaders drive growth with our three specialist divisions:

* transformation
* talent services
* technology for HR

Mindset are organisational development and transformation specialists providing an effective method of driving meaningful change in SME and large more complex organisations.

The world is changing fast – changing demographics, changing buyer behaviours, increasing globalisation and increasing competition create the need to constantly reassess, refine and redirect your strategy.

Mindset offers a comprehensive range of best practice human resources and organisational development solutions that improve your organisation's performance.

Mindset solutions can be integrated into your organisation to assist with transformation, talent retention & attraction and performance efficiencies through technology. We offer more than just good ideas; we work with you to bring about real bottom line results.

Mindset was established by Don Holley and Aaron Dodd to help organisations cope with this constant change – and thrive through transforming organisations and attracting & retaining the best staff.

Mindset have three specialist divisions that work together to provide a total solution. Originally known as Odin Consulting in NSW and VIC since 2002, Mindset represents a rebrand, refocus, reenergising and redefining of customer needs.
