Mary Smaragdis’s Summary

Mary is Senior Director, Brand Marketing at Yahoo! Prior to joining Yahoo! she spent nearly 13 years at Sun Microsystems where she held principal roles in Social/New Media, Web Editorial, Brand Marketing, eMarketing, Lifecycle and Audience Marketing, PR, Executive Communications, and other functions. She also served as the Executive Director of the Sun Foundation. She has a background in journalism, having spent five years at USA Today. A prolific contributor to the blogosphere, Mary has been featured in Fortune magazine, CMO and other publications.
