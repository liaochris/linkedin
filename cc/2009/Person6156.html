Andy Savvides’s Summary

A chartered information systems professional with over 15 years experience working for world class information technology organisations (including IBM and Microsoft) in client facing consulting and senior leadership roles. I consider myself to be a focused self-starter who can quickly establish accurate and detailed business needs and who is capable of working with a number of stakeholders to achieve the successful design and delivery of effective business solutions. During my consulting career, I have gained a strong background in Microsoft and IBM platform technologies. I have the structured approach, field experience and the technical background needed to play a valuable team role during most stages of the it life cycle from solution selling, envisioning and engagement planning through to conceptual design, deployment planning and on-going operations
