Carol Clay’s Summary

As full time real estate brokers with Keller Williams Realty, the Clay Team helps others realize their dreams of enjoying the mountain lifestyle here in this beautiful part of western North Carolina, specifically the Brevard area.

We start by listening because whether you are buying or selling property, this is about more than real estate. This is about your life… your hopes... and your dreams. By listening first, we believe that we are better prepared to bring buyers and sellers together. Every step along the way we pay attention to the little things that can make a big difference like returning phone calls, being accountable and available, and following up on all the details.

If you are relocating, we go a step beyond your expectations of finding a new home by helping you get to know the area whether it’s finding the nearest grocery store, a hiking trail that takes you to one of the over 260 waterfalls, area restaurants, or information about upcoming festivals or events.

If you are ready for change, we'd appreciate the opportunity to help make it happen for you. Discover for yourself why "it's good to have friends in the mountains".
