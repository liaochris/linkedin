John Olsen’s Summary

I have over ten years of experience in the field of interfaith hospital chaplaincy and pastoral care. This includes one year as a home hospice chaplain, two years of clinical pastoral education residencies and internships, and more than seven years of service in full-time staff chaplaincy. I also have five years of experience as a local pastor in the American Baptist Churches (ABC-USA).

I hope to continue to develop and advance my skills and experience as a full-time hospital chaplain. At some point in the future, I would like to explore supervisory training.
