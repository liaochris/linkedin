Michael Wasylik’s Summary

Mike handles a variety of litigation matters, including homeowner's rights, foreclosure defense, and mortgage fraud; commercial and business litigation; consumer rights; and abusive debt collection litigation. Mike has also recently earned national and international press coverage for his aggressive representation of defendants in file-sharing lawsuits filed by the music recording industry.
