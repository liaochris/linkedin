Katherine Brungs’s Summary

Katherine lends advanced experience holding senior positions as Corporate Communications Manager at a Fortune 500 Company and Assistant Vice President of Marketing, along with other leadership roles at three major U.S. corporations. Throughout her career, she developed strong competencies in strategic, integrated brand and marketing communications management. She is well versed in global project management, and has demonstrated sound business acumen and leadership in developing B2B and B2C communications strategies.

~About My Company~

Perceptions between you, your clients and the public should be dynamic, captivating and responsive. It all begins with your brand.

Lacewing Communications collaborates with you to create and transform your brand, which truly reflects your style and reputation. Our blend of forward thinking, high creativity and precise execution directs the expression of your individuality and competitive strengths, and is managed to produce measurable results.
