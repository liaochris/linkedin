Alan LaRotonda’s Summary

I am a Talent Acquisition Professional with 15 years of experience in the Semiconductor, pharmaceutical, biotech, and medical device recruitment arenas. I have worked with major corporations including Intel, Motorola, Dell, AMD, Samsung and Wyeth. I have participated in and managed major semiconductor facility ramp-ups, managed staffing at Wyeth's largest production site, and helped implement an RPO (outsourced staffing solution) at Regeneron Pharmaceuticals. I am a charter member on the Board of Directors for the New Jersey Metro Employment Management Association www.njmetroema.org , and was the 2006 Chapter President for that organization. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Human Resources Management from Saint Edward’s University in Austin Texas, and now reside in northeast Pennsylvania where I specialize in innovative internet sourcing and recruitment strategies for the semiconductor, pharmaceutical, biotech, Energy, and medical device fields.
