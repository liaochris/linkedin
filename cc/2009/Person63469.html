MY GUIDELINES FOR CONNECTING VIA LINKEDIN:

The importance of a network of contacts in business and life cannot be overestimated.

That being said, since a small minority will often abuse the majority's trust and openness, here's how you and I can optimally benefit from the LinkedIn (LI) environment.

Please read carefully!

1- I will accept LI invitations from coaching and writing clients, business partners, (ex)colleagues and friends. Anyone else? Probably not. But see #2.

2- If we haven't yet met then please send a short note about yourself and the key benefit in connecting with you. (That doesn't mean I will, but at least you're knocking politely on my LI door - which is a good start!)

3- Once we are connected, please note that unless I know you and your services very well I am unlikely to recommend you to others in my LI network - trust can of course be built over time, and is a 2-way process.
