James Tunkey’s Summary

James is Chief Operating Officer of the international operations of the investigations and security group I-OnAsia. He and his team have worked in over 50 countries on complex due diligence, fraud investigations, asset searches and recovery, FCPA compliance, and international games security.

For the past 3 years James has worked to develop an international games security practice within I-OnAsia (see www.ionasia.com.hk/ionsecurity), although he remains engaged in work on behalf of US and European clients.

James Tunkey’s Specialties:

James has worked with and led elite teams on a body of significant case work. He has investigated organized crime, money laundering, smuggling, child labor, illegal logging and mining, and State sponsored theft of trade secrets amongst other issues.
