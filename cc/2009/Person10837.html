Beth Blair’s Summary

Beth Blair is a freelance writer specializing in lifestyle, bridal, culinary, spirits and travel, which runs the gamut from aviation topics to exotic destinations. Her articles have been featured in Aviation Security International Magazine, AirTran Airways GO Magazine, Hybrid Mom, Draft Magazine, Arizona Bride Magazine, Oro Valley/Marana Magazine, Tucson Bridal Magazine, Christian Science Monitor, Central Penn Parent, Long Island Mothers Journal, BabyZone.com and various other magazines and newspapers. She has also contributed to numerous anthologies, including the Chicken Soup for the Soul series.

Beth has a strong online presence. She is an AdventureUS.com "Outsiders" correspondent and has written for Away.com, iExplore.com, and Fodors.com. She pens the monthly USATourist News Magazine and content pages for USATourist.com (translated into five languages).

She is co-founder of TravelingMamas.com and the recently launched TheVacationGals.com, where she blogs about her travels and offers valuable insight and expert advice on hitting the road (and skies). Her blog posts have been featured on major news websites including Travel Meredith, Fox and NBC affiliates, Reuters, Chicago Sun Times, and others. Traveling Mamas is on the Nielsen Power Moms list of the 50 most influential bloggers. She is a regular contributor to Homewood Suites blog: SuiteTrip.com.

A former flight attendant, Beth enjoys sharing her insider’s perspective and knowledge on all aspects of travel. She is frequently quoted in the media as a travel expert, most recently FoxNews.com travel, Woman's Day, RealSimple.com and has appeared as a guest on ABC-15 Sonoran Living Live in Phoenix, Arizona.

Beth has been selected as first place winner in the 2010 Writer’s Market “Freelance Success Stories” Contest and is a member of SATW (Society of America's Travel Writers), Toastmasters and Aviation for Women.
