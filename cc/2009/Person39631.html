Hervé Kabla’s Summary

In the beginning of 2008, I created blogAngels, a company whose missions is to assist french companies in their "Corporate Blogging" strategies. Corporate Blogging is the new trendy way to develop a useful web 2.0 approach in a corporate website, in order to increase audience, start 2-way discussions with customers/users/partners, and develop a positive image of your company on the Internet.

Is your company already using a Corporate Blog? If the answer is "no", just follow this link: www.blogAngels.net
