Joel Farris’s Summary

Drupal CMS developer and themer, with a strong propensity for training and education of clients, coders, and community members alike. Heavily involved in the Drupal Dojo training system. Patch contributor and tester for both the core Drupal framework and the contributed modules repository. Technical reviewer for multiple Drupal books covering various aspects of this fabulous framework.

I believe in the ideals of the open source Drupal community so much that I donate several hours of my own time each week to educating and training and helping the community via the #drupal-support IRC channel.

Joel Farris’s Specialties:

Specializing in custom themes for Drupal websites, intelligible communication skills, great documentation writing, and friendly work ethics.
