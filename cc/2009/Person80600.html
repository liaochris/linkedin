Frank Nieves’s Summary

Frank held the position of Telecommunications Engineer by 1980 working for main telecommunication corporations such as: President of TelDar Telecommunications Solutions Inc., VP Operations ACONI Telecom, Puerto Rico, VP Operation for MR Communication, Puerto Rico, VP of Sales for Siemens. This exposure to the corporate world gave Frank a vast experience in management as well as participating in business expos throughout the Caribbean, Central and South America .

Frank was able to open markets for Enhance Communications Group, in charge of the International market in Colombia , Venezuela , Salvador , Panama and others. This gave him the need to get to know the Latin communities, their culture and consumers habits in these countries.
 
In addition to the above, some positions, award and committees that Frank has devoted time are: Consultant and Expert for the Puerto Rican Telephone Company, Executive Director for Enhance Communications Group International for Central and the South American Market, VP of The Puerto Rican Chamber of Commerce of the United States, Inc.  

Award winner of Entrepreneur of the year 1997 and 1999 by the PR Chamber of Commerce of So. Fl, Business of the year 1997 by: Imagen Latino Americana Hispanic News Paper. Served in Boards for Family Care Council, ASPIRA, Family Network on Disability, Broward Community College Hispanic Advisory Board, World Trade Center Advisory Board, Broward County , Small Business Advisory Board, MWBE Broward School District Advisory Board

Frank Nieves’s Experience

  • President

    PR Fiestas Patronales, Inc

    (Entertainment industry)

    September 2001 — Present (8 years 3 months)

    PR Fiestas Patronales & Biz Expo ware created in 2001 with the mission of protecting and highlighting the Hispanic culture for over 600K Hispanic in Broward County. PR Fiestas is the most known and celebrated event among the Hispanic countries.
    In most Hispanic countries, these festivals are colorful displays of pride and joy, featuring parades, games, rides, regional food, and live entertainment. Most Fiestas Patronales feature verbenas (in Spanish-speaking cultures, a verbena is a country fair, modest amusement park, and/or a dance party, especially one held at night with live entertainment by famous international or local singers), amusement rides, and street vendors, among other, during the celebration. The famous group “ Menudo” and many others began their career by singing at these events in Puerto Rico
    Today PR Fiestas Patronales and its division Frank Nieves Production is a full entertainment and special event production company specialized in the Hispanic market.
