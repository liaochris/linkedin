Andy Komack’s Summary

KoMarketing Associates, LLC is an online marketing services firm specializing in B2B SEO, Pay Per Click, Social Media Marketing, and Blogging Strategy. We use a strategic consulting approach in developing high-ROI programs for our B2B clients to drive and attract visits to their websites via search engines, social networks, and strategic link relationships. We combine our business and technical expertise to help our clients achieve a well-rounded, robust online presence. We provide solutions across a broad spectrum of industries, with a tight focus on B2B, and we are proud of our high ethical standards.
