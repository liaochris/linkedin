Lee-Ann Schmidt’s Summary

A certified PMP and PRINCE2 Practitioner, with four years experience in successfully managing and overseeing project teams and their related deliverables on all targeted regulatory risk projects. In addition, I possess experience in building and maintaining customer relationships and managing a line team.

Over eight years experience in all facets of marketing. Experience in driving revenue growth and improving competitive market positioning for leading telecommunications firms. Managed marketing and marcoms activities at the national and international level for both start-up and established companies. Well developed presentation, negotiation and relationship building skills gained through extensive interaction with senior level executives and partners. Recognised for building, educating and motivating top performing marketing teams. Professional background includes business development and sales support, event management, public relations, corporate communications, branding, website development/maintenance and team management.

Serve as the president of the Nedbank Toastmasters Club (2008-09 term).
