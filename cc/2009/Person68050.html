Michelle Leonette’s Summary

Michelle is an interactive designer and front-end developer for the web, constructing persuasive narrative devices and award winning work. Her client list is numerous and includes American Healthcare Solutions, Pittsburgh Glass Center, Jewish Healthcare Foundation, First Commonwealth Bank, Robert Morris University, Shady Lane School and Sophisticated Alloys to name a few.

Her award winning work has been recognized by the Webbys, Interactive Media Awards, Web Marketing Association Awards, Horizon Interactive Awards and the Davey Awards. Most recently her work for the Pittsburgh Glass Center was recognized for Best in Class Non-Profit by the Interactive Media Council for their Interactive Media Awards. She has also served as a Judge for the WMA Awards for several years.

She is an avid proponent of web standards and design excellence. Details are her mantra. She has just as much fun geeking out on learning new code as well as new artistic techniques.

Michelle holds a Bachelor of Arts degree in Fine Arts and Business Administration from the University of Pittsburgh and possesses a unique blend of creative, technical and analytical skills.
