Jeffrey Davidson’s Summary

Jeffrey Davidson [jeffrey@davidson.net]

Acknowledged people leader, with the ability to build exceptionally strong business analyst and quality assurance teams. Understand a variety of SDLC methodologies ranging from traditional Waterfall to Scrum (Agile) and the ability to train and transition teams from one to the other.

As a consultant, introduced requirements gathering and requirements management to the existing SDLC. Senior Business Analyst for a wide variety of business software applications. Specialized in quickly developing comprehensive requirements for large software projects.

Experienced with strategic planning within a large corporate environment; including strategy plan development, communication, organizational alignment, and execution.

Provided the process training for passing CMM Level 5 audit. Developed control systems for Sarbanes-Oxley (SOX) audit compliance within IT.
