Jame-Ane Ervin’s Summary

Jame Ervin is interested in helping organizations use technology to improve their business, especially for marketing, sales and customer support professionals. Throughout her career, no matter her role, Ervin has made an impact on each organization by helping implement technologies to improve efficiency, capture better metrics or increase revenues.

Previous experience:
Ervin was the Product Marketing Manager for StoneFly, Inc. (subsidiary of Dynamic Network Factory) As product manager she is responsible for managing public relations efforts, developing marketing communications, working with cross-functional product development teams, and increasing StoneFly's presence in the rapid growth IP SAN market. Ervin started at Dynamic Network Factory in 2004 as an account executive, later growing to sales engineer. Prior to joining Dynamic Network Factory, Ervin worked for Apple (then Apple Computer) as an Apple Solution Consultant in the retail sales organization, in the frontlines for the company's burgeoning relationship with computer retailer Best Buy. Prior to joining Apple Computer, Ervin worked for Gallagher Public Relations, a PR Firm focusing on early stage startups in the networking and telecommunications space. Ervin concentrated on American Studies at the University of California at Berkeley, and completed her studies in 2000.
