Joe Grady’s Summary

With twenty-two years experience in sales and management positions, and ten professional certifications in seven different areas, I have experienced a wide variety of business environments. I have worked in the airline and hotel industry, held public office, owned my own successful real estate company, served as an adjunct faculty member, and managed several companies. A dedicated community volunteer, with experience forming and leading community organizations for the past twenty years. I have served as Public Relations Director for a 400+ member Chamber of Commerce and then served three years on the Board of Directors. I have worked for very small companies to large national franchises and during this time I have achieved local, state, and national recognition for my sales leadership and volunteerism in the community.

"I am interested in working with a community-focused type company/organization where I can utilize my skills and talents in organization, public relations, leadership, and event planning. An ideal position for me would be a Director or Community Relations Specialist in a community based company or organization."

If by reading this you know of a position that would be a good match for me please contact me. You will not find a more dedicated, creative, and hardworking individual!

Joe Grady’s Specialties:

Excellent organizational management, negotiating, marketing, and public relations skills. Experience in teaching, instructing, mentoring, recruiting, and motivating staff and volunteers. Successful and creative event planner, fund raising organizer, and project development manager achieving great financial results. Consistent sales leader in several organizations, local and statewide, both profit and non-profit. Very task oriented and leader in achieving what others thought was impossible!
