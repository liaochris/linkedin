Eugene Kashpureff’s Summary

Management:
Wide experience from start-ups to large corporate environments, leading employees from blue-collar workers to doctoral engineers. Strong project management, people management, vendor management and budgeting skills.

Network Storage:
Currenlty an instructor for NetApp University. Twenty years experience in storage networking.

Networking:
Cisco, Ascend, and Livingston routers. Have designed and deployed high reliability data center networks with redundant Cisco 7500 routers, Cisco Catalyst 5500, 6500 and 2924 switches utilizing internal OSPF with external BGP4 routing, and spanning tree fail over links. Have designed and deployed internal corporate networks using Cisco PIX and Sonicwall firewalls with WaveLAN clients. Experienced with large scale network monitoring using SNMP with Micromuse Netcool, BMC Patrol and Mon.

Network Security:
Have served as primary authority responsible for security in a publicly traded company, as well as given technical assistance in government investigations. Experienced with complex sets of access control lists and routing policies in wide area, local, and storage networks. Experienced with secure systems configuration, monitoring, and auditing in large diverse environments. Cisco PIX and Firewall IOS, Sonicwall, Firewall-1 and Linux based firewall experience.

Systems Administration:
Operating systems experience with Solaris, Linux, BSD, HPUX, Windows NT/2000. Experienced with enterprise management of 500+ host environments, as well as tuning of high performance Sun E450, E3000, E4000 and E6000 series installations. Experienced configuring VMware on Linux systems in development as well as large scale virtualization environments.
