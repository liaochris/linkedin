Cynthia Vasconcellos’s Summary

With Bachelor degree of Fine Arts, for 18 years has been working in graphic design field, specially for fashion market. Nowadays develops studies in social medias for fashion.

research | fashion | fine arts | graphic design | trend forecaster


2008 - Created Mélange logo - based on concepts of toy art such as customizing, multiplicity and unicity. Colours and combinations vary between application however the requirement and collection always have the same trademark. It is an elastic logo concept and is adaptable depending on application.

Since the creation of her Random Issues blog, Cynthia has been researching communication tools on the Internet. She has participated in a collective project related to future speculation in the scientific community. The results of this have impacted different creative fields, including her first professional activity - fine arts.

2009 - Workshop during a month at IED São Paulo for Master de Design Estratégico class, as professor - subject: graphic design in fashion market.

Completed a presentation about Social Medias for fashion public, but also for people interested in build marketing actions on Internet.
