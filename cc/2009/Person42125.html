Tim Richards’s Summary

My freelance articles have appeared in a range of magazines and newspapers, on a variety of topics.

As an author employed by Lonely Planet Publications, I've contributed chapters to its series of guide books.

I've successfully completed a number of projects for corporate clients, drawing on a range of skills involving copy editing, proofreading and copywriting.

I also teach occasional courses in travel writing for TAFE institutions. And I present talks on travel writing to libraries, clubs and other public groups.
