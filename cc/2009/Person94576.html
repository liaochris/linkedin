Stephen Blum’s Summary

Steve Blum is president of Tellus Venture Associates, a management, business development and market analysis consultancy for the broadband, digital media and telecommunications industries. He is a 30-year industry veteran and recognized as an expert in developing new wireless, fiber optic and satellite broadband systems and services.

Steve is the author of seven books on the Internet and satellite broadcasting and is a frequent contributor to professional journals and industry events.

Stephen Blum’s Specialties:

Start up companies, business plan development, business case validation and funding, business modeling, new venture creation, market research and analysis, market forecasting.
