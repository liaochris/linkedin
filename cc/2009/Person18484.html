Christine Kraft’s Summary

Experienced free agent dedicated to working through editorial and strategy challenges. I work with start-ups, CEOs, private companies and non-profits in the health, tech and lifestyle space. I work on large public projects, small interests, and 1:1 as a confidential adviser.

My passions include understanding the patient experience, building targeted content that gets results and helping with environmental causes. I also like to have fun collaborating with the smartest people out there. (I've been lucky in that regard.)

I work on a project basis, deploying a workshop approach that is fluid, affordable and transparent. Retain me for just an hour, for 20 hours, or for longer depending on the complexity of the challenge. No project is too small; in fact, sometimes a focused hour is all that is required to ignite innovation.

Picture me as a resource for your team, not a "consultant."
