Eric Marschall’s Summary

Eric Marschall is the owner of EJM Designs Limited, a web design and internet marketing company located in Cincinnati, Ohio focusing on adding value to businesses through professional web design, search engine marketing, internet consulting, and technical training.

Eric has worked graphic design in the news media, print, and web industries for over 10 years, began in web design and development soon after that, and has been focusing on search engine marketing - along with his other talents - for the last six years.

Originally from Cleveland, Eric works with companies throughout Ohio and the Tri-State area as well as maintaining several other out of state business relationships.

"I consider myself a resource first and service provider second. Never hesitate to contact me with questions concerning anything web or internet. I'm more than happy to take the time to educate and inform. Have a great day!"
