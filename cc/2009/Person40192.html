Howard Olsen’s Summary

Howard Olsen, is one of North America’s leading sales experts. As founder and President of High Output Training Systems, his positive and profit-producing messages help thousands of individual and corporate clients increase their confidence, revenue and profit each year. Leveraging two decades of sales success in over 20 countries, his “3 Truths of Selling” system takes sales people, business professionals and entrepreneurs through a simple, natural communication process they can easily learn and immediately apply to build extraordinary personal and business lives, step by step.

When he’s not infusing his audiences positive influence he loves traveling with his wife Michaela, and riding his Harley with dog Stanley on the back both of which are favorite pastimes, providing relief from the pressures of a demanding speaking and training schedule.
