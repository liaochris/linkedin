Rita Pardue’s Summary

Rita Pardue, MA, BA Radio/TV/Broadcasting is the Production Manager at 89.3 KPCC, Southern California Public Radio. She has extensive experience in radio and voiceovers. She is the owner of Angel Wings Productions in Los Angeles. She was the Mid-day LA Host for KKLA, 1998-2004

Rita Pardue’s Specialties:

Award-winning audio production including two Silver Microphone Awards, 2008 & 2009 Voicey Lifetime Achievement Award Finalist, 2008 (Bronze) & 2009 (Gold) American Advertising Federation ADDY Awards for excellence in creativity and execution. Client list includes Disney Home Video, Sony, Fisher-Price, Sanrio, Time-Warner Cable to mention a few.
