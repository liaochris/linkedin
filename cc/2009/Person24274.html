David Jordan’s Summary

• An MCIPS qualified senior procurement professional with extensive public sector experience in a range of roles including procurement management, consultancy, contract management, research and best practice. Highly focused on delivery and achievement of efficiency savings.
• Experienced in the full range of public sector procurement from call-off frameworks to BPO, including project management principles, risk management, major IT projects, contract management the Gateway process and UK and European procurement law.
• Managed procurement and commercial work stream of £35m IT outsourcing project including procurement strategy development, influencing key stakeholders, process planning, developing and negotiating commercial terms leading to an award of contract to ambitious planned timescales.
• Author of OGC report on faster procurement timescales in complex projects. Included extensive research on recent major procurement projects and recommendations to improve advert to award timescales to deliver successful projects.
• Strong experience of commercial contract management of a £40m major public sector IT contract, including negotiation and administration of extensive contract change to respond to changing business requirements and delivery of best value & efficiency savings.
