Frode Stenstrøm’s Summary

I started out with working a few years as a software engineer before taking my masters degree. I continued working while studying, but more in the areas of IT operations. From then on, I have developed and focused more and more on project management and management in general, using different frameworks such as ITIL, PMBOK, CobiT and BS7799 along the way.

I have experience both as a supplier and customer, and have good understanding of how the IT marked works.

My professional goals for the next five to ten years are:
- To learn more in the areas that I already work in. I find the more I learn, the more I need to know!
- Establish a new consultancy firm alone or in partnership with past coworkers and friends.
- Work outside of the Norwegian borders for minimum one year.
