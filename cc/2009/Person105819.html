Todd V. Yeadon LGDC’s Summary

Halifax based Todd V. Yeadon has been involved in the New Media Industry since the early 1990's and is widely recognized in his field as a leader, innovator, educator and participant in the growing Atlantic Canadian New Media market. Some of the recognition for Todd's work has come in the form of an Honourable Mention and a Best of Show Award at the Baddeck International New Media Festival. Todd was also nominated as Web Designer of the Year (2002) by the Music Industry Association of Nova Scotia.

Todd has personally developed over 15 major New Media Properties in the Atlantic region and many major national and international properties –including local and regional community portals (MTT Sympatico and MTT MediaPipe), preliterate children’s content (PBS Kids site for Theodore Tugboat) and, innovative sponsor developed solutions for the entertainment market (East Coast Music Awards 2001, Neptune Theatre and others), complex and multi layered community sites.

Combined, Todd’s work has generated over 30 million individual sessions to date.

www.textureweb.com
