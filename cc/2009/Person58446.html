Andrea Cannavina’s Summary

My name is Andrea Cannavina and I am happy you are viewing my LinkedIn profile.

1. A Bit About Me

I've been a "legal" Virtual Assistant about 8 years. I specialize in helping law firms and other service based businesses upgrade to a digital workflow so everyone can work securely from anywhere.

I accomplish great strides in productivity and cost savings for my clients through simple to use and easy to implement web based technology. This technology can be used by solos and large companies alike, with or without the purchase of additional equipment and perfectly integrates with existing software and processes.

I'm considered a digital coach - but prefer the term Digital Assistant. Along with helping mid-sized businesses and law firms upgrade to a digital workflow without the need for software, servers or even training, I work with individuals to properly configure and utilize as much of their existing technology and software as possible.

2. How Can I Help You?

Many ways! Seriously, it all depends on the tech you have in place, the number of people in your company/organization and what you are trying to accomplish.

Over the years I have spoken with 100's if not 1,000's of people just like you so the only thing I am certain of - everyone does everything differently!

That's not to say there isn't room for improvement - especially if you feel like you are running all the time but not getting anywhere. However, before I can make my recommendations, I need a quick assessment of your tech and processes.

3. So Where Do We Go From Here?

That's easy! I offer a complimentary one time 20 minute consultation. Just head on over to my site and click on the large gold starburst to set up your call.

You can also use the AskAndrea form located in the lower right corner of each page of http://www.lawfirmsolutions.com to send me your quick question(s).

I look forward to being of assistance!
