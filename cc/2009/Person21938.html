Dana Morris’s Summary

Achieving results is at the core of my marketing program management strategy: lead generation, awareness creation, and most important, revenue.

My career has been focused on technology based senior level marketing program management. I have successfully worked on projects in many different vertical industries, and many product segments.

This broad background in marketing has provided a solid perspective on the processes and tools available in today’s marketplace. From implementation of a regional lead generation program, to utilization of social networking strategies, my singular goal is to provide a platform for product positioning and differentiation that ultimately sells product and services.

I provided a varied client base with cost effective, revenue and lead generating marketing programs. Computerworld/IDG, Davox, Bose, Mitre, Cape Dory Yachts, Datamarine and many other companies benefited from these programs.

On the cusp of the internet boom I worked for PC Connection as their electronic marketing coordinator, providing guidance and strategy for utilization of emerging technologies.

Positioning early adopters of cutting edge media, at Convergent Media I served as project manager on a variety of projects, working with a talented team of artists, writers, programmers, and program managers to produce cost effective and timely internet, CD and print deliverables.

I was privileged to manage the launch of two software products for Hitachi Computer Systems, and served as Vice President of Marketing for the Object Management Group, where I managed a vast array of strategic and tactical marketing initiatives in very diverse fields such as healthcare, space, business modeling and integration, transportation, robotics, finance, software based communication and insurance. Involvement includes the branding,launch and marketing of the of SOA Consortium, Governance, Risk Management & Compliance Roundtable and Business Ecology advocacy groups.

Dana Morris’s Specialties:

Strategic and Tactical Marketing, Advertising, Public Relations, Promotion, Partnership Development, Event Management, Advocacy Program Development and Support, Project Management, Product Launches, Lead Generation, Budget Forecasting and Management, Revenue Generation through a variety of channels.
