Aaron Myers’s Summary

Aaron Myers was born July 27, 1983 in Dallas Texas. He began playing the piano at the age of 3 and opened for 15,000 people at 13.

In 2001, he joined the United States Military, where he served as the Brigade Minister of Music, earning the Battallion Comanders Coin, and Asst. Post Chaplain coin. After leaving the Military, Aaron went on to Navarro College, majoring in Theatre and Business, and during this time he ran for the office of Mayor in Corsicana, Texas, the first African-American to run for this position.

In 2005 Aaron relocated to Los Angeles, California where he co-hosted Comedy Night at Miyagee’s on Sunset with Josh Nassar (guests included Jay Davis, Dane Cook, Tony Rock, Darren Carter, and Reuben Paul, Lisa Lampenelli). Working at Youniversal Jamz Entertainment as Vice-President, he's worked with The American Biograph Mutoscope Company the oldest movie production company in America, The Barack Obama Campaign, The Los Angeles Aids Healthcare Foundation, and recently with the Stop Hating Campaign, sponsored by Nick Cannon.

Mr. Myers coordinated the campaign for Navarro County serving as Precinct Chair for the primaries, Nomination at Large Chair for the Navarro County Convention, and serving on the Nomination at Large Committee for the Texas State Democratic Convention in June 2008. Mr. Myers worked in the state of Florida for the Obama-Biden Campaign for Change during the general election as a Field Organizer.

Mr. Myers moved to Washington DC to oversee the closing of the Global Family Program acting as the National Director. In March 09’ , he was invited to serve as the National Director at the Alliance for Education and Progress (AE&P), a newly formed nonprofit who’s goal encourages students to take ownership of their educations. You can find Aaron serving on panels, speaking to young people, a regular political & entertainment pundit, and working on many causes to aid communities all over the Country.
