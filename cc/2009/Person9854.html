Brian Collins (BC)’s Summary

Mr. Collins has over 15 years of professional experience in (sales, support, and management) with a focus on business and technology, business process improvement, systems integration and project management. Mr. Collins has experience with various roles; ranging from a independent consultant, team leader, project manager and director. He works equally well in team and self-guided situations and excels under pressure.

Most recently; Mr Collins focused on technical solution sales and consulting. As the manager of the Professional Services (PS) team at NewsGator, Mr. Collins was responsible for crafting and delivering advanced technical solutions required to sell and/or integrate NewsGator's RSS and Social Networking technologies into the enterprise.

This role required direct and consistent contact with high-level contacts within the Fortune 500 and a keen eye for polish and detail.
