Christian Guthier’s Summary

Possessing strong ideas, practical skills and an enthusiastic approach to the design process. I am a motivated, client focused designer with bags of experience, having worked in both small and large design studios.

Background covers concept development and design for print/new media, with a particular emphasis on integrated communications.

Always open to ideas and opportunities. I have worked for, and started up, several new venture projects as a Creative Design Director / Production Manager.

Christian Guthier’s Specialties:

A hands-on, strategic thinker who can manage large-scale projects. I have an excellent attention to detail and can communicate well through my work, and my relationships with others. Concepting, copywriting, art direction, photography, creative direction, staff management, strategic campaign development.
