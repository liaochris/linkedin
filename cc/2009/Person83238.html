Ben Reynolds’s Summary

I have worked across North America on varied projects and positions. My experience includes: Designing characters for a 3D animated series for FOX, working as an illustrator/designer on online advertising and games for clients such as Sony, Ubisoft and McDonald's, and providing illustrations for the covers of Premier School Agendas across Canada.

My goal is to constantly learn in a creative and fun environment as well as use my talents and abilities to bring excellence to the property I’m working on. I am looking to find a challenging position to meet my skills, capabilities, education and experience. To always be learning, growing and striving to improve.
