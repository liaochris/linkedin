Dave Thomas’s Summary

Dave Thomas is currently Vice President of CeruleanBlue, Inc. a managed IT services company with offices located in the Greater Binghamton NY area. No stranger to the IT industry, Dave has been actively engaged in the channel for over 10 years.

Now focused on the massive Small- Medium Business sector, Dave is pushing CeruleanBlue's Managed IT Solutions and services to help other businesses move forward in regards to lowering their IT overhead, increasing productivity and minimizing problems and issues in the future.
