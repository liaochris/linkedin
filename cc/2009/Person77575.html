Paul Golding’s Summary

Global mobile/web 2.0 technology/strategy/innovation expert. Been a pioneer and thought-leader in mobile industry since 1990 and inventor of various patents. Implemented numerous wireless and Internet projects, including many industry firsts, and continue to invent new techniques for solving various mobile problems. Have trained, mentored and consulted for many wireless practitioners in major operators and leading companies globally. My book "Next Generation Wireless Applications" is widely use in industry and academia, with the 2nd edition now available, including major addition of Web 2.0 and Mobile 2.0 themes. Extensive business/industry experience on many continents. See wirelesswanders.com for more details.

  • University of Southampton

    MPhil/PhD , Mobile Virtual Reality and Fuzzy Logic (Part-time) , 1994 — 1996

    Collaborative industry research whilst working as Wireless Research Engineer for Motorola, exploring visionary methods for conveying virtual worlds to mobile devices for VR and augmented reality applications.

    After advice from faculty, subject was converted to more "conventional" mobile problem solving in field of co-channel interference due to lack of prior-art in mobile VR.
