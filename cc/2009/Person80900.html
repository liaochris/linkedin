Stephanie Vaughn, Psy.D.’s Summary

I have conducted individual and group psychotherapy for 5 years. I have a specialty in Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT) and plan on one day having a clinic exclusively devoted to the treatment of Borderline Personality Disorder and comorbid issues. My husband, David F. Street, M.D. is a Forensic Psychiatrist at Vanderbilt. This gives me the advantage of having a colleague whom I can consult 24 hours a day for tough issues related to treatment.
