Maria Thurrell’s Summary

Last summer, I founded Media Awaken, a social media boutique that combines public relations, marketing and social media into a powerful package that enables our clients to establish themselves as thought leaders in their respective industries, engage with customers and potential customers, spread brand awareness, and reach their communication goals.

We work with nonprofits, socially conscious organizations and a wide-range of start-ups.

I recently helped launch Vook, a startup that created multimedia digital books that blend text and video into one complete story. I was the Social Marketing Manager and was the social facing voice of Vook and assisted with other creative social marketing initiatives. I still run the Vook Twitter account.
