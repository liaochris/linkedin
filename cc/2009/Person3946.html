Khalid Alkhudair’s Summary

Khalid Alkhudair is a Saudi Marketing professional who has spent the majority of his education and career in North America and Europe. Khalid recently achieved the Chartered Marketer status from the Chartered Institute of Marketing. He is currently registered as the 26th Saudi worldwide to achieve this grand recognition and an MCIM member of the Institute.

Khalid currently manages all aspects of KPMG's Marketing strategy, Communication,PR, Events & Corporate Social Responsibility at all three KPMG offices in the Kingdom and reports to the Head of Sales & Markets and the Executive Committee.Khalid has been also tasked with establishing & overseeing KPMG in Saudi Arabia's in-house PR function after two previous years of outsourcing. Throughout his time at KPMG, Khalid has been playing a key role in KPMG's employer of choice function and hence the firm has for the past two years achieved a top 5 position in the Best Saudi Company to work for award. He has also led the firm to great awards such as Consultancy Firm of the Year, a top 20 position in the Saudi Fast Growth 100 awards, Hall of fame award (CFO Summit 2009) and the Best Services Company to Work For award. He also established the first ever communication handbook within the KPMG member firm network which earned high praise and currently sits on the KPMG global Intranet site.

Khalid is also an active writer and contributor in national and regional publications such as Gulf Marketing Review, Saudi Gazette, Arab News & Saneau Alhadath .
