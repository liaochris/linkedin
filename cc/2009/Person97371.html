Telmo Valido’s Summary

Investment professional at Permira with a regional focus on the Iberian Peninsula and Latin America and a strong connection with the global Industrial Products & Services team.

Prior to joining Permira, spent 7 years with The Boston Consulting Group, developing strategy and organization development projects within the Industrial Goods and Energy practice areas.
