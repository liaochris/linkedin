David Block’s Summary

Graphic Artist with substantial experience in web design, from direct marketing to deep site design. Possess a broad range of skill sets bridging web and print mediums. Adept at the use of Photoshop, Flash, Illustrator, and the rest of the Adobe software library. Intimately familiar with the implementation and administration of digital asset management systems. Interested in new and traditional media, and specifically interested in branding campaigns.

David Block’s Specialties:

All Adobe products: Flash, Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Acrobat, SoundBooth, Dreamweaver, Fireworks, After Effects, Premier. I'm also familiar with both PC and MAC environments.
