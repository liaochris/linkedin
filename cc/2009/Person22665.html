Danish Thanvi’s Summary

DataFlow Group is head quartered in Singapore and operating through out Middle East and South Asia. We are global provider of Immigration Screening, Pre Employment Screening and Emerging Market Criminality, Fraud and Risk Data. Legal and Professional, Comprehensive and Current, DataFlow is the choice of leading corporations and governments worldwide.

DataFlow Pak has in-depth local knowledge with a network of resources all over Pakistan which enable us to provide our services in the remotely located and troubled areas. Besides document verification services , we have abilities to conduct criminal record search, civil litigation/bankruptcy search, site visits/address verification, ID verifications etc all over Pakistan in timely, efficient and cost effective way.

DataFlow Pak also occasionally offers Information Security, Digital Forensics and Security Support (Executive Protection, Security Reviews etc) related services to our clients as One Window Operations to meet their requirements.

Our solutions enable corporations to mitigate fraud risks in many areas. We can design low cost and effective programs, ad hoc or comprehensive, to meet the requirements of your organizational compliance and regulatory policies for HR, Fraud and Security risks.
