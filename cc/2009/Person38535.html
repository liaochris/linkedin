Kevin Hallenbeck’s Summary

Kevin began his sales career in 1988 as a sales engineer for CC1, Inc where he sold computer-based image processors to the web printing & flexible packaging industry. In 1994 he left CC1 to start BestSalesPeople.com, a franchisee of the Sandler Training. Since then Kevin has worked with thousands of salespeople and their organziations to increase their effectiveness. In 2008 Kevin received the David H. Sandler Award, given once per year to the Sandler Training franchise owner who most closely represents the ideals of the late David Sandler.
