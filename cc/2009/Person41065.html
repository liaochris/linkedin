Igor Kryltsov’s Summary

Deep Shift Labs is a small web applications development company that was founded in Sydney, Australia.

We are team of 7 people residing in all four hemispheres across three continents. Our distributed office approach enables our company to provide 24 hours a day service and support for any customer, anywhere in the world.

Primarily we develop complex and custom web applications and services using PHP and JavaScript technologies on both UNIX and Windows platforms.

Deep Shift Labs started life in 2002 to develop and deliver a web services project for Amity Group (now Bupa Care Services Australia).

The applications associated with the initial project have been in production since then, and have successfully evolved with the growing needs of a growing company. After seven years, and iterative project stages, the project continues today with the active code base now exceeding over 400k lines of code.

We are not strictly a 'web application company', as we have been known to let ourselves get involved in 'something different' like porting Mac application to Windows with Cocotron. We have a diversity of skill that we like to use for the benefit of our customers.

What else do we do?

- We write Selenium tests on PHPUnit and TestNG and test web applications on our Selenium Grid service
- We provide manual testing of web applications and services
- We provide remote system administration services on *nix systems to our clients.
