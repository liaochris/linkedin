Jennifer Abe’s Summary

I love hats. Maybe it’s because I’ve worn many hats throughout my career, from very technical roles to management and team lead positions. I’m passionate about bringing product vision into reality by tapping into the knowledge and experience I’ve had in all of my various roles in order to help teams succeed in whatever capacity is needed at the moment.

My experience spans project management, product planning and design, development, testing, release and ongoing customer service. I have repeated achievements in guiding geographically dispersed teams, as well as the ability to build synergy between business and technical teams in order to achieve strategic objectives.

I recently received my ScrumMaster certification and am eager to join an agile team and help deliver business value with high quality and productivity. I am flexible, hard-working, have a team oriented attitude and am excited about solving problems and creating great products for customers.
