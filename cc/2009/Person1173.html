Adam Creighton’s Summary

I'm a business development and technology leader interested in leveraging my proven track record of building teams and growing business & revenue. I'm also a professional actor in voice, film, commercial, animation, video game, and other projects.

Currently, I'm the Product Manager for Emergent Game Technologies, responsible for the market relevance of our products (Gamebryo and Gamebryo LightSpeed), including building, maintaining, and maximizing strong relationships with our platform partners.

Prior to Emergent, I was responsible for business development and managing multi-million dollar budgets and local and distributed software engineering teams for Visa, the world's largest payments technology company. I switched vertical markets because I'm passionate about interactive story-telling, and working in gaming lets me leverage big-gun tech and biz dev leadership with my indepedent creative skills.

And I wrestle llamas (and sometimes win).
