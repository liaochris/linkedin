Kimberly LeRiche’s Summary

As a Virtual Assistant, I act as an online business development manager to assist with internet and social media marketing assistance to small business owners, solo entrepreneurs and other busy professionals. As a Social Media and Internet Marketing Assistant, I have experience and intimate knowledge of the social media and internet landscape and the importance of one's online business presence and reputation management.

I help to create a strategic social media and internet marketing plan for your unique needs and then assist you with implementing that plan. As a Social Media Marketing Assistant I can help you get started with your online social networking and social media marketing by setting up your profiles, finding possible contacts and suggesting ways to begin marketing or publicizing your social profiles. Increasing and growing your online visibility can be very time consuming because it requires not only skills to navigate the platform or application properly but it also requires good optimized content.

I also offer assistance with other small business services such as research, competitive analysis, keyword research, article marketing and press releases, word processing and digital and print media services.

JK Virtual Office Resources provides a cost-effective and efficient solution for those businesses or solo entrepreneurs that cannot justify the cost of hiring a full or part time employee. Outsourcing and delegating some of your internet marketing and social media task and processes will give you more time to focus on your revenue generating business tasks. With JK Virtual Office Resources you pay only for the time spent on your projects and tasks. As a partner in your business success, we look for ways to optimize your business processes for maximum efficiency.
