Scott Quick’s Summary

Scott is the CEO of The Catalyst Group, a professional services firm that provides senior executives pragmatic advice on how to harness Business Technology to achieve strategic growth goals, adapt to changing market conditions and enhance competitive advantage.

Throughout his career, clients have relied on Scott to successfully navigate a myriad of business challenges and deliver solutions that produce measurable results.

Prior to founding The Catalyst Group, Scott held positions with aQuantive's Avenue A | Razorfish Division, Enterprise Solution Group, where he was responsible for strategic client acquisition [NASDAQ: AQNT - Acquired by MSFT].

He was Regional Vice President at STEP Technology -- an advanced custom application development firm -- where he founded the Portland CIO Forum.

Prior to STEP, Scott was a consultant on retainer to a Portland area hospital and assisted senior executives on various strategic initiatives including the expansion of the Emergency Department and Family Birth Center, Patient and Family Centered Care policies, new technology rollout and education, among others.
