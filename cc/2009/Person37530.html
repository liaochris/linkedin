  • Owner

    Emergency Preparedness Systems LLC

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; E-Learning industry)

    September 2002 — Present (7 years 3 months)

    Through exceptional training experiences and world-class resources, Emergency Preparedness Systems LLC helps clients create, design, distribute, and facilitate rapid e-learning for emergency responders.

    EPS uses a proven and efficient rapid e-learning production process to create narrated multimedia Flash movies that are engaging and educational. Our team does three things:

    1. We convert existing classroom training content for online delivery
    2. We design and deliver new lessons and curriculum for online delivery that honor student’s knowledge, experience, and time
    3. We teach our proven rapid e-learning for emergency responders production process to educators and training officers

    EPS can support your online emergency responder training program with a full spectrum of training services. We will design a training strategy that will effectively reduce your training time and cost and increase your ability to prepare for and excel during emergencies.
