Sam Fallenbaum’s Specialties:

Our focus is not on the equipment we are taking as collateral against the lease but rather on who we are lending to and solving a financial solution. As such, we offer debt refinancing and consolidation, sales leasebacks for the creation of working capital, new or used equipment purchases, early stage companies, M & A financing, SBA loan facilitation as well as challenged credit situations.
