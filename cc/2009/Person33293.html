John Martinez’s Summary

An in-demand drummer, songwriter, and producer, John Anthony Martinez first came to the public's attention in a 1995 article in Modern Drummer magazine.

Update November 2008: John is associate producer and drummer on Season One of a new TV series called "Rhythms of the Road". Visit http://www.rhythmsoftheroad.com for more information about this new music/travel/reality series from Hallmark/Lightworks.

John has recorded or performed with Larry Blackmon of Cameo, Bailey Brown (from FOX-TV's "Americal Idol"), Kacey Musgraves (from USA Network-TV's "Nashville Star"), Jesse Powell (MCA), Ronnie Laws, Joseph Vincelli, Tom Browne, Howard Scott (WAR), Jean Carne, Kim Waters, Bernard Wright, Phil Driscoll, Dennis Edwards and the New Temptations, Russ Taff, the Drifters, Yarbrough & Peoples, Rodney Bowens, Doug Allen & the Chicago Mob, Shelly Carroll, Andy Timmons, Brittany Holmes, Larry Willis, Bill Ham, Tim Ishii, Randy Phillips, Cindy Cruise-Ratcliff, Dallas Jazz Orchestra, Scott Springfield, Lani Bartley, Don Johnson, Nathan Brown, Chuck King, Bradley Knight, Deion Sanders, Karen Taylor (live broadcast from T.D. Jakes' Potter's House), Ray Davis and Pocketful of Blues, Robert Whitfield, Sycamore, Bobby Sparks, Keith Anderson, Chuck Rainey, Buddy Miles, Bill Tillman, 2002 Grammy nominated (Best Contemporary Jazz Recording) George Anderson Signature Band, whose CD "Faces" received a wonderful review in the March/April 2003 edition of Jazzizz magazine, and many others.

John's goals are to continue writing and producing music for various artists while building his music production company, Fingerfoot Music Productions (ASCAP), his partnership in Goldberg Entertainment , and his work with Australian composer Shueh-li Ong on her project "Xenovibes". Future releases include new CDs by Jennifer Perryman, Doug Allen, a book he's co-writing with Bernard Wright titled "SEVEN Elements of Music", and his own CD called "Catalyst" featuring Bernard Wright and Mike McKinney.

John Martinez’s Specialties:

Fingerfoot Music Productions (FMP) is a music production company based in the Dallas – Fort Worth area of Texas (USA), providing songwriting, music and audio services to the music and media industries. In 2004, FMP joined forces with Singapore based Electric Muse, consultants and developers of original music and multimedia, to leverage on the strength of both businesses.
