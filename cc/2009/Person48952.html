John Bouantoun’s Summary

An avid Technologist with a love for finding the end-to-end solution, and a passion for getting the most out of my team members.

I have extensive experience building software development teams, able to use my technical, process and people management expertise to engineer an environment that fosters high-performance.

I come from a Computer Systems Engineering background having learned to work independently in my early freelance days. This strong technical independence has been re-enforced through my contributions to the open-source community over my development career.

Early on I discovered a passion for management which started with consulting positions focusing on project management but has since developed more deeply into people management and high-performance team leading.
