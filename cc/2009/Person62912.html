Mario Leone’s Summary

Italian Patent and Trademark Attorney, European Patent and Trademark Attorney - Mainly focused in the drafting and the prosecution of patent applications in Italy, Europe, in the USA, in further foreign countries and under the Patent Cooperation Treaty (P.C.T.), in any field of the engineering and for software - Expert in setting out patent strategies and in patent portfolio management, in assisting clients for technical evaluations related to patent validity and patent bars. Specialized in patent searches, opposition procedures and technical expertises required in litigation proceedings before Italian and foreign Courts - Lecturer in specialization courses for IP operators from universities and industries, for patent practitioners and for patent formality paralegals.

Mario Leone’s Specialties:

patent drafting and prosecution, prior art searches, opposition procedures, due diligence and technical expertises
