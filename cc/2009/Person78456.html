Per-Olof Lindqvist’s Summary

I am CTO for IBM IT Delivery services in Nordic. I am the overall responsible for all technical solutions and the quality of the services delivered to our clients. I work closely with IBM’s clients and support the delivery of our engagements by dedication to every client’s success. As CTO I unite the strength of IBM as a globally integrated corporation and our global skills and resources to deliver innovation that matters. I travel to all Nordic countries supporting our delivery teams to create trust and personal responsibility in all relations. I provide guidance and technical leadership to both the technical and business organisations. I am part of the European IT Delivery technical leadership forum and I represent IT Delivery in the Nordic Technical Leader Team.

I have 20+ years experience in banking, finance and retail solutions. Anything from core mainframe systems to internet solutions on both Intel and Unix platforms. I have deep technical knowledge of all solutions and services delivered by IBM. I have been the leader for several Infrastructure, Application and Integration projects. I have experience from application and technical development and as manager for a large infrastructure department. I have extensive IT operations and development experience both as manager and as lead architect designing services for large and complex clients. I have lead several large transformation and integration projects for both IBM and other companies.

Per-Olof Lindqvist’s Specialties:

Leadership skills, embracing challenge and earning trust, broad technical knowledge, deep understanding of IT processes, mainframe technology, database technology, very good collaboration and communication skills, I enjoy challenges in building strong teams with new partners and clients.
