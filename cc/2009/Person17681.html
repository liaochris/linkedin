Chris Burdge’s Summary

Marketing communications professional with 17-years of experience on both the agency and client sides. Demonstrated expertise and proven results in developing digital brand strategies, social media marketing, email & mobile marketing, and user experience design.

Chris has lead client successful engagements with hundreds of companies from fortune 500 corporations like HP, General Motors and Nestle to start-ups and regional companies like Planet Organic Markets, Megson FitzPatrick Insurance and Tofino Beach Homes.

Fluent in spoken Japanese with eight years experience working in Tokyo ad agencies in a Japanese-language work environment.

Chris Burdge’s Specialties:

Strategic planning, creative thinker, campaign measurement and analysis, client service and team leadership.
