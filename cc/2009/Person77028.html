Patricia Hughes’s Summary

Patricia Hughes is a marketing professional with over 20 years of experience. She is able to combine analytical and highly creative aspects of marketing to produce comprehensive marketing strategies. These strategies currently, involve Internet marketing tactics, but also include overall brand strategy. As a result, her clients have increase revenues and profits and reduced cost.

Patricia also has experience with International Internet marketing. She enjoys the challenges of global marketing and developing marketing strategies for various cultures. Recently, she has obtained a Localization Certification. Localization is the process of adapting products and services such as Web sites, documents, etc. in accordance to linguistic, cultural, technical and other locale-specific requirements of international target markets.
