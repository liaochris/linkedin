Theresa-Maria Napa’s Summary

Theresa-Maria Napa, CPCC, LOACC - President of www.RightTrackCoaching.com is dedicated to training and coaching professionals and executives in leadership and transition, especially during the development phase where there is so much uncertainty.

Her varied career has included positions as vice president of operations, director of marketing & administration, and business owner. In these positions, TM gained substantial experience in leadership, marketing, and transformation.

She is a Certified Professional Co-Active Coach and a Law of Attraction Certified Coach.

Testimonies from her clients speak of her masterful coaching to challenge them to transform their lives, become effective leaders, and to discover the love in their lives. They speak of her running on hard drive with a firm, yet kind, approach to achieve solutions that are invaluable. She believes in the individual and their special gifts. Her client’s success is foremost in her mind.

TM offers programs to train her clients to SOAR with Trained Wings in Leadership and Transition. The emphasis is on coaching and training to self-awareness toward powerful self-leadership to lead effectively and have the life they are meant to live from Passion to Mastery.

Her blog offers daily postings to help you achieve your life's desires: www.PassionToMastery.com She offers a complimentary leadership e-course and assessments on her website.


The testimonials at www.RightTrackCoaching.com offer an insight to what her clients say about her style and what they accomplished during and after they were coached by TM.
