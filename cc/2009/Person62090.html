Marcel Sorger’s Summary

There are a lot of topics I'm interested in. They range from hard core coding to psychology and management.
Throughout university, in my proffesion as well as other aspects of my life I always tried to understand how things worked and influenced each other, both in tactical and strategic ways.
As an analyst and developer this helps me to design and create systems that fulfill business needs now and in the future. These interests have also given me a extensive knowledge of computer systems as well as a understanding of the IT sector as a whole.
This understanding makes me ideally suited as an architect, because it helps me make technical choices that can satisfy business demands now and in the future.

Effective communication skills and political insight help me keep my projects moving in the right direction. Seeing all sides of a problem is critical for creating the optimum solution.
Possessing creativity and down to earth logic enables me to come up with unconventional but practical solutions.
Professionally I am always trying to sharpen my skills and to learn new technologies.

To create a quality solution I always try to balance the requirements of management, users, engineers and developers. Most requirements in the SPECTRUM dimensions witch stands for Secure, Performant, Elegant, Configurable, Transparent, Robust, Usable and Maintainable. Normally not all the stakeholders get what they want, but they all get what they need.

Architecture is key to any successful solution. Whether it’s an OO design with Patterns or a SOA, I’m always trying to achieve “Lose coupling, high cohesion”.

Talent 4 MS development
I currently specialize in .NET development. The ever increasing framework make a solid foundation for any application. The new features and tooling have the potential to revolutionize the development process, making it easier to collaborate with many different stakeholders creating a better and more cost effective solution.
