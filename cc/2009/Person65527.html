Maura Neill’s Summary

I have made real estate my chosen profession and passion in the Atlanta area since 2001 - I have worked exclusively with The Gebhardt Group since then. The Gebhardt Group is a full-service boutique real estate team. We work primarily on referrals from past clients, friends and family, and other Realtors in our extensive network. We serve the North Metro Atlanta area, including Alpharetta, Johns Creek, Suwanee, Cumming, Duluth, Norcross, Dunwoody, Sandy Springs, Decatur, Lawrenceville.

Though not my exclusive concentration, I specialize in working with first-time homebuyers -- guiding them through the homebuying process and educating them every step of the way.

I pride myself on providing first-class personal service to my clients and providing them with the best service available. I want my clients to become "raving fans" because that is how I grow my business -- through the referral of happy clients! My business is almost entirely referral-based, thanks to my satisfied clients.
