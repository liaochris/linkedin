Barry Campbell’s Summary

Technical writer, instructional designer, information architect, proposal manager, corporate communications manager and strategist.

I have led, managed and contributed to the development of proposals that have brought in significant new business for companies ranging from small consulting firms to multinational corporations.

Teams I've led have created everything from documentation and training for software and business processes to complex, feature-rich web sites.

I blog about proposal management, strategic business communications and related topics at Knowledge Work (http://campbell-online.com/blog).

Proposal and RFP management, Project management (trained and certified in PRINCE2 methodology), IT service management (trained and certified in ITIL), Business process analysis and documentation, Technology consulting, Technical writing and editing, Information architecture and usability analysis, Training development and delivery.
