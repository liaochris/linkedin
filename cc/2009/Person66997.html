Michael Hillenbrand’s Summary

A principle-centered leader with a strong passion for IT Service Excellence. I spent a good deal of my career leading managed services practices focused on remote database administration and hosting.

I am currently serving as an IT Director at ThermoFisher Scientific where I am leading efforts to provide global shared services for database support, data warehousing and business intelligence efforts.

I started my career as a DBA at USX / US Steel. I then moved onto ALCOA where I managed a group of 10+ DBAs providing support for over 300+ databases worldwide. For over 8 years I served as the Director for the RemoteDBAexperts division of Contemporary Technologies where I managed a group of over 25 DBAs who supported over 50 customers worldwide. I also led remote dba efforts for Access Enterprise Solutions and Management Science Associates.
