Deirdre Kilroy’s Summary

Deirdre's practice is primarily focused on technology, and her interest and knowledge in many other areas stem from this. She is the Head of the Intellectual Property and Technology Unit. She has a considerable amount of experience in assisting clients of all sizes, both domestic and international.

Deirdre's approach to her work is to help her clients to achieve their commercial objective. Whether dealing with IT contracts, intellectual property matters, outsourcing, data protection, software licensing or e-commerce, she ensures that the client's objective is always to the fore.

Deirdre is also a leading adviser in the area of gaming and gambling law, and is a member of the firm's Gaming and Gambling Unit.

Deirdre has been widely published on IT, intellectual property and related matters.
