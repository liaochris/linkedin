Patrina Mack’s Summary

We deliver vision and execution. An expert management team, experienced in a number of industries, helps Global 2000 companies and start-ups alike, successfully bring new products to market. We provide vision to create strategic initiatives, expertly assessing market opportunities through customer focus, market understanding, business process expertise, and insights on sales and distribution. We cost-effectively execute your tactical initiatives through pragmatic marketing and sales programs that deliver on strategic objectives, ensuring that programs remain focused and on track.

Sample clients include: Agilent, BeVocal (acquired by Nuance), Cisco, e-Cumulate (acquired by HP), Intuit, Ivus, Macromedia, NetIQ (acquired by Attachmate), Oblicore, US MedTrans, Visa, VRagent, Voxify, Wind River.

Sample projects include:

o Telecom:
Asesesed current product development process gaps and identified improvements to reduce time to market, improve quality and reduce costs for bringing new products to market.

o Medical IT/ASP Service:
Developed market penetration strategy in support of national launch for medical services business.

o Semiconductor/Peripherals:
Conducted market analysis and developed go-to-market strategy with robust financial analysis to support business case for components manufacturer seeking to enter end-user marketplace.

o Network Management/Enterprise Software:
Developed and "productized" consulting services to increase channel partner revenues for network management solutions.

o Consumer Software/Financial Services:
Optimized phone channel for major financial software company with extensive product list, growing annual incremental revenues by $12 million.

o Consumer Software:
Guided strategy and implemented affiliate marketing program for market leader in web tools, improving partner relations and growing sales for the previously underutilized channel.
