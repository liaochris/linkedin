Melissa Kojima’s Summary

My GOAL:

I would like to work with art directors and editors in editorial and book publishing.


My STRENGTHS:

I work quickly and meet all my deadlines.

I work well with art directors and editors, seeing directions, suggestions and critiques as a collaboration and an opportunity to grow as an artist.

From living & working in Europe and learning French, I think abstractly, understand complicated ideas and distill this into visual commercial message for my clients.


My ART:

My art work is witty, whimsical and fantastical.

I am good at likenesses for portraits.

I am influenced by the Urban Contemporary and Low-Brow Art Movements.
