GENERAL SUMMARY:

Peter Jacobs helps business professionals define their career goals and market their services. His clients choose careers that meet their needs and position them to excel, find jobs or attract their own clients, increase income, and improve work-life balance.

He bring his clients over 14 years cumulative business experience up to the manager and director level at Arthur Young, Price Waterhouse, Intel, and a high-tech start-up. Since 2001, He has helped hundreds of MBA's and professionals to successfully market their services.

Having made several career changes himself, including starting his own coaching practice, he has experienced first-hand the kinds of challenges and opportunities you face in your own business or career.

7 years of experience living and working outside of the U.S. particularly position him to help professionals build international careers.
