Amy Prior’s Summary

My goal is to raise the bar in online copy writing for myself, and for others; to challenge traditional schools of thought about writing by being imaginative and effective with my own writing.

I have ten years of solid Web copy writing experience that includes Web sites, landing pages, banner ads, paid search ads, blogs, e-mails and more. Clients include: Ford, PNC Bank, Quicken Loans/Rock Financial, Things Remembered, Bush's Beans and more.

Unlike other writers, I appreciate that user behavior, site functionality, search engine optimization (SEO), graphic design and site architecture inherently influence a Web site's copy content and as a result, the site's effectiveness. I do things the right way because I balance the end user's perspective with the client's voice in order to generate the desired action. Plus, I have a notoriously fast turnaround and expert grasp of writing/editing in AP style.

Amy Prior’s Specialties:

Interactive copywriting (websites, news releases, banner ads, paid search text ads, landing pages, promotional emails/newsletters, blogs, etc.)
