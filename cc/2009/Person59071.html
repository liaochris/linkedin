Julia Stege’s Summary

My expertise is in creating beautiful branding and marketing pieces that speak your mission to the world and attract your perfect customers.

I love to design and I also write content and help people create their marketing plan.

I have been getting in to a lot of product and package design lately, having recently created a gameboard and book for Cafe Gratitude, and labels for a flower essence line, a skin care line, and a women's supplement.

I love to help people attract perfect customers using the Strategic Attraction Plan TM combined with thrifty self-marketing techniques. I offer free teleclasses both live and by recording to help entrepreneurs become wildly attractive. Please join my e-newsletter for marketing tips and invitations to my free tele-classes by signing up on the form on my website home page.

I am also a Rainforest Ambassador for the Amazon Herb Company, engaged in a sustainable eco business that preserves the rainforest through a trusting partnership with indigenous tribes.
