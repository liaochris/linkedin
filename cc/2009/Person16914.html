Karen Huller, CPRW’s Summary

I am a Certified Professional Resume Writer with a background in Executive Search and IT Recruiting. My mission is to teach job seekers how to harness and present their value to a targeted audience of employers, propelling them toward a career that fulfills them and pays them what they are worth. Charesume, LLC offers a full suite of transition services, but customize packages based on each individual needs with flexible, a la carte options in consideration of the job seeker's restrictive budget.

Professional goals:
- Understand the goals and motivations of the jobseekers with which I work.
- Be a catalyst to career optimization by helping people focus their transition activity.
- Continue to fine-tune my processes to maximize the quality of résumés, thereby improving the quality of opportunities offered to my clients.
- Stay in tune with hiring processes and employment trends.
- Generate long-lasting professional relationships with people and teach clients how to leverage their network to acheive their goals.
- Foster an environment of shared contacts so that all of us will find the help we need. "GIVERS GAIN"
- Follow through.

Karen Huller, CPRW’s Specialties:
