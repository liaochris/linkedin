Derek Mehraban’s Summary

A graduate of Michigan State University, Derek Mehraban has 15+ years advertising, marketing and interactive experience. Mehraban is focused on digital and internet marketing, event marketing, web search, web 2.0, social media marketing, blogging and generational marketing.

Mehraban is CEO of Ingenex Digital Marketing, based in Ann Arbor, Michigan. Ingenex provides a full suite of Digital Marketing services including interactive branding™, web site design, generational marketing, search engine optimization, pay-per-click management, optimized public relations, direct marketing, email marketing, event marketing, SEO, SEM, PPC, podcasting and blogging.

Ingenex launched www.socialharbor.com in summer 2008. Social Harbor is business social networking, optimized. The Ingenex team answers the question "how do I build my Linkedin profile?" by building professional profiles on Linkedin and other business social networks for companies.

Mehraban teaches the New Media Drivers License (sm) course at Michigan State University. This course teaches students all about digital marketing, social networking, blogging, search optimization, Google and more. Mehraban blogs at The Digital Bus http://thedigitalbus.com.

Mehraban has built a strong network in the marketing, technology and business communities by participating in groups such as Ann Arbor Spark, The Inforum, Ann Arbor Ad Club, Marketing Roundtable and A2B3.

Derek Mehraban recommends web 2.0 tools such as Linkedin, Plaxo, Facebook and Twitter to stay in touch.
