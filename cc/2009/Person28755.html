Dustin Luther’s Summary

Through 4realz Consulting, Dustin works with companies to develop social media tools and strategies that reach targeted audiences. His clients range from small businesses and start-ups to some of the largest corporations around. Regardless of their size, all of his clients are looking for ways to generate business through social media.

Prior to founding 4realz Consulting, Dustin was the Director of Interactive Marketing at Move, Inc, where he led a marketing staff to build products and develop outreach strategies to better reach real estate consumers and professionals for all the company's brands including Realtor.com, Top Producer, and Move.com.

In addition to consulting and speaking, Dustin founded and runs one of the first, and still the largest, real estate agent blogs in the country: Rain City Guide (http://www.raincityguide.com/). Because of this blog and the success he's had using social networks like Facebook to reach consumers, Dustin has been featured on numerous national publications, including the New York Times and the Los Angeles Times, as a leader in the social media space.

In addition to a strong marketing background, Dustin is also a licensed Transportation Engineer (CA #2168) who relishes the opportunity to discover insights through analytics. As a transportation consulting, I managed on a variety of complex planning projects for local governments.
