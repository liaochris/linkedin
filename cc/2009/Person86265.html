Ron Sylvester’s Summary

I have covered just about every beat available at a newspaper, from sports writing, to arts and entertainment, medicine, health, science and technology, crime and legal affairs. I currently am entering the new frontier of on-line multimedia reporting with Kansas.com. I want to continue to stretch the bounds of journalism, using applications such as Twitter to report the news.

Ron Sylvester’s Specialties:

Open records, specialty and beat reporting, writing, emerging uses of technology in journalism.

I presented workshops andseminars about multimedia reporting and social network journalism at regional and national conferences. I was named Regional Director of the Year in 2007.
