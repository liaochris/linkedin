Vladimir Tsanev’s Summary

Despite the fact that I had limited background in the Internet industry and sales, I started working at Aplus.Net as a sales representative. After a couple of years I was promoted to Sales Trainer at the Sofia office of the company, based on my performance and the skills that I developed. This gave me the opportunity to learn and grow, while leveraging my skills in a different area.

The expertise and experience in online business that I gained while working at Aplus.Net, combined with my degree in Business Administration gave me the required confidence to start Web Start Ltd.

I find the interaction with many different business owners to be very rewarding, as is the scope and broad nature of the projects I work on. I study in detail the business operation and organization and the goals of the online presence. It's inspiring to find creative ways to accomplish their objectives and further grow their businesses. There is satisfaction in knowing the work I do has the ability to have a major impact on the client.
