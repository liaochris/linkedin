Richard Alderson’s Summary

Entrepreneur working between Mumbai & London.

Passionate about start-ups, incubation, the web, mentoring, leadership development, career change and making the world a better place.

I am the co-founder and Director of UnLtd India, a new initiative to incubate start-up social entrepreneurs in India.

I am also the co-founder of two social-purpose businesses:
- Journeys for Change, which takes exceptional leaders to learn about innovation, entrepreneurship and leadership from the world’s most inspiring social entrepreneurs.
- Careershifters, which helps people who want to make a career change find what they want to do and make a successful shift.

Always keen to hear from - and help - fellow purpose-driven entrepreneurs.

I can be reached at richard [at] richardalderson.com.

Follow me at www.twitter.com/RichardAlderson.
