Zvonimir Dusper’s Summary

37 years young/old. Living in Croatia. Currently associated with IDM Music, the exclusive representative of EMI Music Publishing, Peer Music, Kobalt and other music publishers for the ex-Yugoslavian territory; writing and producing music (for nation-wide TV networks, marketing, artists etc.); managing the creation of IT and Web solutions for IDM Music; running a private company DIDA; translating music & new media oriented web stuff; blogging localy on Music 2.0 issues.
My specialities are broadness,creativity, speed and finding the right musical surroundings for whatever comes to mind - from vocals as in pop and jazz arrangaments, over theater plays and movie scripts as in scoring for theater/movies, to creating idents, jingles and background music for TV Nets/Marketing companies.
Music vise,I can skillfully operate inside a wide range of music genres, from jazz and orchestral music, over pop and mainstream, all the way to contemporary club grooves; from completely acoustical arrangements all the way to pure electronics; since I play all the main instruments, I can produce a whole song in several hours from the scratch, if there is such a need; at the same time, I enjoy collaborating, so if the budget permits, I will always try to enrich the production with brilliant instrumentalists.

I am also developing a couple of software/web projects.
