Michael West’s Summary

25+ years experience in Commercial, Advertising and Fine Art photography. Self Employed since 1980. I have an extensive client list and expertise in a variety of specialty areas of photography.

Michael West’s Specialties:

Beverage and Food Photography, Product and Still Life photography. I also specialize in stop action photography of liquids in motion.

My fine art photography focuses on evocative black and white imagery and bold, graphic studies of architecture. My work is in many private collections and numerous corporate and hospitality installations nationwide.

Please see my website for image samples and additional information about my clients and my studio.
