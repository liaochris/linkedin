Andra L. Watkins CPA CMC’s Summary

We perform business advisory services exclusively for small and medium sized businesses. We do not perform traditional accounting services or income tax preparation services, though we do give second opinions on the services clients receive from existing traditional providers. The principal spent 10 years in public accounting, rising to tax manager in a regional firm. She was frustrated by all of the client needs she saw for help running their businesses and the lack of assistance traditional CPA firms provide. She left public accounting to run a multi-million dollar company, gaining the expertise she lacked by working exclusively in public accounting for her entire career. We work closely with clients on a project basis and provide the hands-on approach the average client simply does not get from other professionals who must churn client volume.
