David Rowe’s Summary

David Rowe is a senior technical consultant and team leader with over fourteen years of experience who has worked in a number of major global organisations since graduating with a 1st Class Honours degree in Computing in 1995.

David Rowe’s Specialties:

During his working career, David has undertaken a wide range of assignments in the public and private sectors, the UK and abroad, specialising in Internet and intranet applications, more recently with an emphasis on Content Management Systems (CMS). With excellent customer facing skills and attention to detail, he is able to understand client/business requirements and effectively design, document and deliver successful technical solutions.
