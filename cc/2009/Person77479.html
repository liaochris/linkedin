Paul Comi’s Summary

Paul Comi has been a sales and sales management leader since graduating from the University of Southern California with his degree in 1986. His experience has largely been focused around selling business to business and has an extensive and successful track record both selling to regional and national accounts as well as hiring, training and managing sales teams.

Since 2000, he has been handling various mid to large accounts as a sales professional for WorkflowOne-the nation's leading full service provider of managed print and promotional marketing services.

Paul Comi’s Specialties:

Electronic print and mail, literature fulfillment, outsourced business processes
