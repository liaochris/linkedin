George E. Williams, IV’s Summary

"...a Carnival barker’s megaphone aimed directly at a complacent citizenry.."
-Kevin Moreau, Kingfish Magazine 2001

Despite difficult conditions, the three bloggers behind Humid City managed to produce a series of podcasts in the wake of the hurricane as they became "refugees".
-BBC.co.uk 2005

"George has made the Radio2020 blog (and by extension the blogging voice of the radio giants [NAB, RAB, HDA]) into something that I feel captures the essence of radio. Namely, something - like George, and like radio - that has real personality."
-Tun3r.com Blog 2008

As a social media and blogging specialist operating out of the New Orleans area, I emphasize the use of blogging for community empowerment and ethical non SPAM based promotional efforts. I have had the pleasure of working as a blogger for clients including WWOZ 90.7 fm, the CEO of Kaiser Permanente, the National Association of Broadcasters, Defend New Orleans, Small Plate Radio and others.

In 2008 I instructed the social media portion of the Online PR Class that produced by the IAOC and Patron Saint Productions. Also that year I was an instructor in Patron Saint Productions' Blog Buddy Campaign, 2008 Golden Bulldog Award winner for PR Innovation of the Year. I am a current partner in the Hurricane Digital Memory Bank.
