Scott Farr’s Summary

While serving in the U.S. Air Force Scott received broadcast production training from the American Forces Radio and Television Unit (AFRT) and the Air Force News Agency (AFNA).

During Operation Desert Shield, Scott gained hands-on training in Electronic Field Production (EFP) while covering the fire crews fighting the Kuwaiti oil fires.

Since 1996 Scott has traveled to 20+ countries. Be it traversing a 9,400-foot peak in the Sierra’s with wind driven snow, on oil platforms in the North Sea, or 200 feet below sea level in Death Valley he brings more than enough experience and tools of the trade to get the job done quickly, quietly and efficiently.

Having worked on commercials, films, documentaries, news and reality TV he is able to pay attention to the smallest detail while still maintaining the ability to 'just get it done'. His technical know-how, amiable personality and ability to improvise, as well as his commitment to the goals of the producer, make him a strong asset to any production.
