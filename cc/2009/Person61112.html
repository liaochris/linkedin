Jacquie Cliff’s Summary

As a real estate agent and loan officer I have worked with a great diversity of clients on a wide range of projects. From single family homes for first time home buyers to investors buying and selling multi-family properties, I have been there.

What I bring to the table for my clients is the willingness to work aggressively for them and to communicate openly, honestly and consistently with them. Too often agents forget that their clients want to know what is going on.
