Pieter van Beesten’s Summary

The UK Recruiter site is an information resource for all levels of recruitment professional. We offer a range of services across the whole industry sector whether your from an agency/consultancy, HR or RPO background. Our objective is to provide the most comprehensive set of information, services and links to all in the UK recruitment industry. We like to think of ourselves as creating a community of recruiters and researchers. It is completely free for anyone to use the site.

Our main areas of activity are:

- a weekly email newsletter
- an industry supplier directory

- quarterly networking events for recruitment professionals
- Facilitating introductions for Mergers & Acquisitions of current trading business's (M&A)
