Nicole Sell’s Summary

I am an Adobe Certified Expert & Certified Instructor in Adobe Acrobat, Captivate and Dreamweaver as well as a Microsoft Office Specialist. I am a full-time software instructor/consultant with Easel Solutions formerly Technology Education Solutions of Lincoln, NE. I have been in the technology training industry for several years specializing in Adobe Acrobat, Elearning and Microsoft Office. I initiated the Adobe Acrobat User Groups in Lincoln and Omaha, Nebraska in 2006. As the Chapter Leader, I host monthly meetings for over 200 members. I also am the Chapter Leader for the Nebraska Adobe Captivate/eLearning User Group.
