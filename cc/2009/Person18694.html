Christopher Mengel’s Summary

Christopher Mengel is an entrepreneur, corporate developer, business strategist, and educator, - and he spearheads Razorwest's corporate skills development initiative.

As founder of Razorwest, Christopher oversees the company’s direction and strategy and manages its day-to-day operations. Occasionally, he serves in an interim management or advisory consulting capacity. His business expertise is within the areas of sales and marketing, operations, recruiting, and skills development.

His company, Razorwest, provides strategic talent and workforce development solutions to emerging and mid-market companies looking to deliver innovative business improvements, build better processes and customer experiences, and advance the skills of individuals and teams.

To discuss a business challenge or to connect, email him at chris (AT) razorwest.com.
