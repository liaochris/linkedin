Omar Bassalti’s Summary

I took BSc degree as Mechanical Engineer at Politecnico di Milano in march 2006. I'm completing MSc degree in Industrials' Plans at the same university.

From April 2009 currently I work as PE & PM I’m responsible for ensuring all project’s components respect the specifics, delivered on time and within budget and quality constraints. Overall I’ve responsibility for Fire & Gas projects as well as creation and control of project’s documents. Projects included ESD, F&G.
Our Fire & Gas systems are provided mainly to Oil & Gas sector. Working with following PLC: Autronica, Allen-Bradley (Rockwell), Simplex, Siemens.

From September 2008 till December provision of control and instrumentation systems to Oil & Gas sector. Creation of engineering documents.
Projects included ESD, F&G, and process control solutions in Southern Italy (Basilicata Inshore plant, ENI), Pakistan (Inshore plant, OMV Austria), Africa (Offshore plant, Nigeria), Middle East (Kuwait and Qatar) etc...

2007 - Provision of consults about energy saving in real estate business. I have been nominated Counselor Coordinator of Energy Conservation and Innovation. The aim of this team is to research and exploit all the existing ways of energy and environment conservation which can help to save money.

Since 2004 I work on ICT technologies, I studied at a Cisco System Academy attending CCNA course and then working in Broadcast TV, All Music Spa, and pharmaceutical Factory, Recordati Spa.
