Reece Dano’s Summary

I work as an embedded research librarian with the Consumer Insights and Trends group for Ziba Design -- a leading global consumer experience design company.

This link to my April 2009 interview with Information Outlook provides an in-depth description of my skills and experience:

http://www.ziba.com/pdfs/press/SLA_Reece_April%202009.pdf

My goal is to provide targeted, value-added business intelligence to Ziba Design. This allows Ziba to provide clients with solutions to make meaningful, authentic connections with their consumers.

I am also an active member of the Special Libraries Association, helping to advance the knowledge and career development of all information professionals. I'm a member of SLA's First Five Years Advisory Council. As a part of the Council, I'll be enthusiastically working to help new professionals develop the skills they need to make the most of their careers.

Reece Dano’s Specialties:

Competitive intelligence, industry research, company profiling, trends analysis, brand auditing, cultural and social research analysis, webography/netography, information management, information architecture, collection development; a smidgen of primary research
