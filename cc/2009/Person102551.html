Frank Scarpaci’s Summary

Seasoned strategic and tactical project development, business operations consultant and corporate social responsibility champion with years of local and national experience directing complex projects from concept to fully operational status.

The Vianova Group, LLC helps organizations improve performance and gain competitive advantage by integrating social, ethical and environmental standards into business operations.

At Vianova, we believe that a strategically aligned and integrated corporate social responsibility (CSR) strategy creates tremendous opportunities for business growth and sustainability while at the same time leverages the power of business to benefit society and the environment.
