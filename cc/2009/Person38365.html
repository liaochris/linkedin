Gabe Wachob’s Summary

A combination of deep technical experience (CS undergrad) and a law degree from UC Berkeley's Boalt Hall School of Law.

Technical experience dealing with a wide variety of Internet technologies, including enterprise, peer-to-peer and distributed architectures.

Experience as a very early employee of several Internet startups, particpating in all roles from strategic product planning to hands-on development and network administration.

Experience in leading and participating in open standards development in the Internet space.

Experience in open source as well as commercial software development.
