Valerie Gonyea’s Summary

I am a Networking & Career Consultant. Additionally, I developed The Complete Career Campaign ™; a highly customizable system that helps you achieve your professional goals by focusing on effective traditional networking mixed with Web 2.0 strategies. I will work with you to help you get where you want to go.

My success is founded upon my relationships with people...they are my most prized professional asset. Listening to client's needs and clarifying concerns is a strength I bring to my relationships.

My blog, at www.careercourageously.com , is designed to explore career issues including interviews, resumes, employee retention, "social" and in-person networking in order to create the Complete Career Campaign ™.

My professional history includes many different industries:
Media/Communications
Internet Service Provider
Telecommunications
Videoconferencing
Public Accounting
Recruiting

In addition to my own work, I also provide career counseling expertise to current MBA students and Alumnae at the Haas School of Business at UC Berkeley.

Valerie Gonyea’s Experience

  • Founder / Career Encouragement Officer

    Career Courageously ™

    (Human Resources industry)

    2008 — Present (1 year)

    I founded Career Courageously in early 2008 because I wanted to create an organization which would guide clients toward the identification and successful achievement of their professional career goals.

    I work with Solopreneurs who are trying to build their businesses as well as those in the corporate world who are looking to increase their visibility and attract new opportunities.

    My approach focuses on strategic use of social practices; specifically, effective interpersonal networking supported by Web 2.0 strategies.

    I created “The Complete Career Campaign” ™ which is a suite of tools and strategies designed to help my clients put their best foot forward toward achieving their stated career goals.

  • Adjunct Career Advisor

    Haas School of Business @ UC Berkeley

    (Educational Institution; Higher Education industry)

    2008 — Present (1 year)

    I provide independent career counseling services to the MBA students currently enrolled at the Haas School of Business at UC Berkeley.

    I do resume/cover letter reviews and conduct mock interviews with the students so that they can be their very best when approaching the job market for internships and their first job upon graduation.

    I have also done several presentations to the student about the importance of social media to job search activities. As a result, I've started to do one-on-one coaching in the area of social media profile development with the students and other professionals.

    (Non-Profit; Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    2007 — Present (2 years)

    Provide professional guidance to the management of this fine non-profit organization. One of my primary goals is that of recruitment. I am seeking new Board members and an Audit Committee Chairperson at this time.

    Easter Seals has been helping individuals with disabilities and special needs, and their families, live better lives for more than 80 years. Whether helping someone improve physical mobility, return to work or simply gain greater independence for everyday living, Easter Seals offers a variety of services to help people with disabilities address life's challenges and achieve personal goals.

    Easter Seals Northern California covers 17 counties, starting from the Golden Gate Bridge (Marin County) to the Oregon border.
