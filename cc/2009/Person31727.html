Errol Chung’s Summary

I have spent several years building the knowledge and tools necessary to help individuals make the right Real Estate decisions.

My first publication was The LiquidSalesman. 2005.
The LiquidSalesman. - www.TLSlive.com
The concept was simple; Friends feel better working with friends and friends refer friends to friends. All you had to do as a Sales Professional was to shorten the time it took for someone to consider you a friend. To make that person feel like they have an inside track on a great deal offered because of their unique relationship with you. My book is now available on over 280 retail locations and online.

Bear Intellect Inc. - www.BearIntel.com
As a consultant and educator, I have taught many how to use the internet’s free resources to help them build residual income. I put together this site as a simple “How-To” for all that are interested and were willing to take the time to learn to do this themselves.. There are so many widgets, affiliations, graphics and tools out there, that it is easy to become overwhelmed. This site not only helps you choose your topic, but gives simple step by step instructions on building your online business using only free tools.

www.RepYourGroup.com – Giving the power of online networking to your group.
This is my newest site. I am currently working on the SEO (Search Engine Optimization) and (Search Engine Marketing) portion. This site was primarily designed for small groups of professional networking organizations. Its purpose is to give these groups that do not have extensive financial resources the opportunity to use the internet to extend their reach. Through online member collaboration and an extensive array of tools including video posting, job search, member invitations, newsletters and many more these small groups immediately have the communicative power of major international organizations. My gift to those who will stop at nothing to find the answer to success.
