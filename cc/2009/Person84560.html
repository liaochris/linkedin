Rob Bowman’s Summary

Senior “C” level healthcare executive with board level presence and over 20 years of successful leadership experience across the provider, payer, government and life sciences spectrum, managing and growing consulting P&Ls of $25M - $50M. Technology visionary and disciplined leader with exceptional integrity and proven ability to develop strong client, board and physician relations, influence diverse stakeholders, and motivate teams to embrace new visions and aggressive goals. Problem solver, change agent and catalyst for enterprise performance management, process innovation, profitability and margin improvement for public and private healthcare systems and organizations. Astute healthcare and technology policy adviser with federal and state experience, including analysis of public policy, legislative and regulatory issues.

Recognized expertise in healthcare strategy, IT and operations, revenue and expense optimization, technology-enabled business & clinical transformation, delivery systems integration, clinical data management, health economics and data analytics, consumer health and web technologies, electronic health records, medical management systems and development of high performance enterprises, health information exchanges and regional healthcare networks.

Diverse technology background, with eight years of early career experience developing large scale geographic information systems (GIS) and advanced telecommunications for military (DoD) applications, including work with the National Geospatial-Intelligence Agency (NGA) and U.S. Army Strategic Defense Command; security clearance: DoD Secret (inactive).

Significant professional interests include emerging health technologies, NHIN architecture (functionality & interoperability), health surveillance systems, social computing & semantic web technologies (Web 2.0/3.0), business intelligence, CPM & performance analytics, service-oriented architecture and IT governance.
