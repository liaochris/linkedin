Jackie E. Barnett’s Summary

Ms. Barnett has achieved revenue growth and minimized market development risks by analyzing current situations and future opportunities. Adept at working with multi-disciplined teams and business leaders to achieve shared results, her skills are in building business through relationships, as well as characterizing business risk and market opportunities.

She has worked since 2001 primarily as a retainer-based consultant for individuals and companies interested in protecting and growing assets, building new markets, and integrated communications and positioning. She has also taught strategic business management and marketing courses as an adjunct professor for the University of Phoenix. Previous to that, she was on the startup team for an advanced technology manufacturing company, developing foreign markets.

Jackie E. Barnett’s Specialties:

Ms. Barnett has successfully implemented strategic marketing and business planning, with over 15 years experience in growing market share. This includes experience in collaborating with cross-functional, virtual teams to develop and implement programs. Ms. Barnett has a history of managing the details to get clients what they need to manage their business position and risks, when they need it, at a cost that fits their budget.
