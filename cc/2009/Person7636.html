Ari Herzog’s Summary

As a consultant, speaker, and writer, I provide online media strategies to benefit business and government. I write frequently about social media and online marketing at AriWriter.com and also am a contributing writer to the Huffington Post and Mashable.

Are you a business or government agency eager to hire a social media consultant or online community manager? I help companies and individuals reduce overhead, increase productivity, and engage with customers and constituents using interactive marketing and online branding. I can assist you in determining which social networking tools are best.

I am actively seeking opportunities in social media and online marketing to leverage my 10 years of experience in information technology, community journalism, and government administration.

I use a holistic approach to explain why it is important to embrace the social Web, focusing on different, customized approaches for every client. For instance, every company does not need to blog and every PR firm does not need to tweet. By the same token, every company needs to blog and every PR firm needs to tweet. Confused yet? I can help you cut through the Web 2.0 clutter.

I am also passionate about narrowing the digital divide and empowering civic engagement and cross-agency collaboration. In August 2008, I created an online directory of U.S. Congress members who use Twitter. You can view this at [http://tr.im/usgovtwit]. I frequently interact with some of the leading government employees and contractors around the world.

I enjoy connecting with people on LinkedIn, in accordance with my contact settings at the bottom of this page. Thanks!
