Ted Kaemming’s Summary

I'm a web developer.

The majority of my work pertains to creating the back-end structures of enterprise web applications, but I like to get my hands dirty with a little bit of front-end development every now and then (and sometimes I'll even take a stab at some design work.) I speak fluently in PHP, XHTML, Javascript and SQL, dream in Python, and enjoy a little snack in Ruby from time to time, all depending on the needs of the project at hand.

I also enjoy marketing (specifically the non-invasive kind), business and process development, and a whole host of other not-so-technical things.
