Nannette Saunders’s Summary

My vision is to create a strong referral based business with life long client relationships, not only in Lynchburg Virginia but in areas all over the country. I achieve this by guiding my clients through their major financial and emotional decisions with wisdom, understanding and exceptional service. Honesty, Integrity, Family, Business and Education are my core values.

My mission is to strive to understand my client's deepest needs and values. Then provide real estate services that go above and beyond all client expectations so that I am considered valued, and a referable consultant rather than just a salesperson or real estate agent in Lynchburg Va. I work hard and my clients are important to me and enjoy working with me. Using leading edge technology, continually educating myself and improving my systems, I add value to the lives of others. Through helping others I am rewarded emotionally, spiritually and financially.
My name is sometimes spelled incorrectly. I am use to it by now. Some people will spell it with one "n" in the middle like this: Nanette Saunders. Other will call me Lynette Saunders, Jeanette Saunders.
