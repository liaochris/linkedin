Russell Hyzen’s Summary

Serial entrepreneur who has started or help start many different companies throughout his lifetime. Since Quote.com and XOOM.com/NBCi he again has been involved in helping start more businesses and has been an active advisor, board member, and investor in early stage companies. Mr. Hyzen has also been involved in many real estate partnerships. Russell Hyzen served as XOOM.com's/NBCi’s Vice President of Business Development and Corporate Development from December of 1997 to June of 1999 and as XOOM.com's Director of Business Development from November 1996 to December 1997. Prior to joining XOOM.com, Hyzen served as Business Development Manager for Quote.com, an Internet Financial Services Company from December 1995 to October 1996. Prior to entering the Internet industry, Hyzen spent eight years in the real estate industry, holding positions in real estate acquisitions and development, commercial real estate sales, and mortgage brokerage. Hyzen graduated Cum Laude from California State University, Northridge with a B.S. in Business Administration with concentrations in Finance and Real Estate.
