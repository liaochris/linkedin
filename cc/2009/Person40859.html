Ian Yip’s Summary

Experienced Information Security Consultant focusing on working with customers to strategise, architect, design, inform and advise on Security solutions and strategy. My areas of expertise are in the following areas:
* Identity and Access Management (IAM/IDM)
* Data Security and Data Leakage Prevention (DLP)
* Governance, Risk and Compliance Management (GRC)

Most of my experience has been in a customer facing sales and architecture capacity, although I have a background in Information Security services and have previously worked on consulting/implementation engagements and projects in Security, Identity and Access Management.

I have also spent a sizable amount of time recently working in the Data Security arena, predominantly around Data Leakage Prevention (aka Information/Data Loss Prevention) solutions, strategies and architectures.

I blog regularly (http://blog.ianyip.com) and have co-authored an IBM Redbook on Identity Management.

Ian Yip’s Specialties:

Identity and Access Management (IAM/IDM), Data Security, Data Leakage Prevention (DLP), Application Security, User Centric Identity, Federated Identity, Digital and Online Identity, Governance, Risk and Compliance (GRC).
