Amanda McKenzie’s Summary

Amanda McKenzie is a successful marketing strategist, fair trade evangelist and soulful entrepreneur. She is passionate about helping small businesses succeed, in particular entrepreneurs seeking to making a positive contribution to society.

Co-founder of the popular and growing website http://souleconomy.com/ - dedicated to promoting and connecting soulful entrepreneurs and building ethical consumerism - Amanda understands the importance of building communities and is passionate about fair trade and sustainability. She also performs marketing and web consultancy services for SMEs.

Amanda has 20 years of expertise and experience in marketing, advertising, branding, sales and business development - both online and offline. She has developed and grown businesses both large and small, locally and globally, across a wide range of industries. Added to this, Amanda has an intimate knowledge of the European, China and Hong Kong markets, having travelled and worked there extensively.

She has the ability to see the big picture while at the same time being able to develop and implement innovative, practical and effective strategies. She is dedicated to promoting and growing ethical consumerism, is customer focussed and committed to achieving a clear return on investment for clients.

Amanda holds a Masters in Management (Major in General Management) as well as a Bachelor of Business (Major in Marketing). She is also a member of the Sydney Committee for http://www.goodcompany.com.au/
