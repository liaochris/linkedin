Maree Conway’s Summary

After a long and successful career in university management, Maree established Thinking Futures in 2007 to work with people, institutions and professional associations to integrate long term thinking (AKA futures thinking) into strategy processes. Using a range of futures approaches, I faciliate processes and provide strategic scannng to allow organisations to develop strategy that is based on a systematic and rigorous exploration of the future

I have particular expertise in managing strategy development and implementation projects for universities, vocational and further education providers and other educational institutions, and for professional associations in the education industry.

Maree Conway’s Specialties:

- strategic thinking using futures approaches such as scenario planning and casual layered analysis
- strategic planning and developing meaningful strategic plans
- environmental scanning to inform strategy development processes
