Jeroen Pluimers’s Summary

My main skill is helping people learn new things by providing them with my (technical) skills.
My main drive for that is their smile during and after that process.

Work is all about having a good time and enjoying the benefits of a broad set of skills.

On the technical side, that includes .NET, Win32/64, Databases, SQL, Visual Studio, Delphi, communication, and a lot more.
On the personal level, I fit with almost any organization, from completely blending in to very present where needed.

Having a keen eye on quality, architecture and risks, clients and prospects often are very glad that I prevented them from throwing loads of money at things they won't benefit from.
