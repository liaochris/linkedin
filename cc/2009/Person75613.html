Olivia Prince’s Summary

At Wind River Realty, it is our job to make meeting your goals as easy as possible. Quality customer service is possible because we have a team of highly specialized assistants, agents and support staff that work together to offer the best service possible for ALL of our clients. Our office manager is constantly in the office so that no messages are missed and there is always someone available to help with any need that requires immediate attention.

I go above and beyond to make sure my expertise is cutting edge. Keeping up to date is essential with the ever changing real estate market.

* I enjoy the benefits of a Business Coach
* Participate in a Power Up group, a group of women business owners working together to increase forward thinking and success for BOTH business owners and their clients.
* I am a Member of the Star Power Club which keeps me on top of the newest marketing ideas and educational opportunities available to Realtors across the country.
* The National Realtor Association keeps me up on industry changes
* I play a key role in bringing upper end education to the Realtors in our county through the in the Fremont County Board of Realtors.
* I am also a Chamber Board Member
* These activities help me not only to be more knowledgeable about our area and community but to grow my contacts which create a larger circle of influence to find the right connections for my clients.
* I regularly participate in Continuing Education and Seminars throughout the United States. This education keeps me up to date on trends in the industry and with new technology that helps me to better serve your needs.
