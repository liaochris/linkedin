Alan Warms’s Summary

Currently evaluating several investment, executive management, and startup opportunities.

Versatile executive with proven track record in moving the needle in organizations ranging from large ($100MM plus) to startups.

Most recently worked at Yahoo! as Vice President, General Manager Yahoo! News, Tech, and Education from the sale of BuzzTracker in September, 2007 through completion of earnout in November, 2008. While there, led turnaround in performance of News, including: development of the first major site redesign in 3.5 years; implemented BuzzTracker into every News story page as a Most Blogged About feature; extended Yahoo! News lead as the number one News site for Reach in the world according to Comscore; and grew revenue and operating cash flow double digits percentage-wise on a year to year basis through innovative application of monetization techniques while consistently and significantly beating budget targets.

Current angel investments include Stocktwits,Trada, AdGooRoo, Directory of Schools, DOS Partners, and Moving Station. Previous angel investments include Wallstrip (sold to CBS).

In January 2007, successfully exited investment I led in RealClearPolitics and stepped down as Publisher as part of transaction. Summary of deal can be found here: http://www.participatemedia.com/2007/03/13/realclearpolitics/

Sold last company, Participate Systems, to Outstart in November 2004 after founding it 1997.
