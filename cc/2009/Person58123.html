Lara Bersano’s Summary

Grupo Empresarios is an IT and communications’ company based in Argentina with operations in all of Latin America and the US Hispanic Market. The company was founded by Gustavo Flamerich in 1998 in Caracas, Venezuela, and in 10 years it has grown and developed more than a dozen projects and programs; web based solutions for medium and small companies in the Americas.

As President of Communication Strategies at Grupo Empresarios, I am the first person to talk with the client and identify its needs and I lead an expert team of communication, technology, editorial, design and media experts.

My business approach includes working together with the client's executive team, identifying every single piece of important (or irrelevant) information, and in particular:

1.- Identify the company's needs and goals.
2.- Listen to the market, and what it is saying about the client.
3.- Consider every person in the company as a potential spokesperson.
4.- Create a comprehensive Public Relations' plan that includes every person as a valuable asset for the company.
5.- Plan for the short, medium, long and very short term.
6.- Evaluate the actions taken and their consequences and reconsider positions.

Grupo Empresarios has been a blessing for my professional career. As a communications’ and e-government expert I have the opportunity to use technology solutions to help clients in various industries: government, manufacture, editorial, media, commerce, international, and many more. I can be coordinating an editorial agenda for a client in Chicago today and developing an IT solution for a local government tomorrow, or preparing an international business agenda with another client while flying back home from an event in Miami. I always dreamt of leading a multi-tasking, multi-lingual, multi-cultural endeavor and having had the opportunity to lead a professional and committed group of professionals has been a blessing.

Lara Bersano’s Specialties:

Understanding the client’s goals and needs and developing communications’ solutions based in technology to provide accurate, timely and reliable information for the day-to-day decision-making processes. Developing a comprehensive strategy and understanding the company’s key-players and allies will exponentially increase the chances for success in business.

Our Specialties: IT solutions for communications and decision taking.
