Melanie Mitchell’s Summary

Melanie Mitchell is a results-oriented leader with proven success at building and leading world-class marketing and technical teams for companies. She has 11+ years of experience in web site promotion (9+ years exp in search/online marketing). She has worked for numerous large and big brand sites across several industries, including travel, finance, entertainment, health, ecommerce, news and sports.

Melanie is currently the SVP of Search Strategy at Digitas where she is responsible for managing the Search Engine Marketing Knowledge Center. Prior to this role, she was VP of Marketing for Folio Investments, Inc., in charge of marketing efforts such as SEO, PPC, display, PR, video, and email to acquire customers, traffic and publicity.

Prior to this, she was at AOL where she leveraged her strong business acumen and ability to execute a wide range of marketing strategies designed to establish market presence, both domestic and international, and increase revenues. She developed strategy, laid the foundation for success (processes, procedures, tracking technology, reporting) and managed the team tasked with increasing AOL.com's free content business via paid (PPC) and organic (SEO) search.

Prior to AOL, she was at Network Solutions; managing direct response marketing campaigns through display, PPC, SEO and strategic alliances for both Network Solutions and NameSecure.com.

Melanie is an Advisory Board Member for WebmasterWorld Pubcon (http://www.pubcon.com/advisory.htm), a speaker at Search Engine Strategies (SES), Pubcon, Search Marketing Expo (SMX), AdTech, WebGuild, Public Relations Society of America (PRSA), and for such noted roundtables as Business Wire and the National Women's Business Center as well as a contributing writer to Search Engine Watch (http://searchenginewatch.com/). In early 2007, she was named one of the "top in-house SEO's" by Natural Search Blog (http://www.naturalsearchblog.com/archives/2007/02/27/some-top-in-house-seos/).
