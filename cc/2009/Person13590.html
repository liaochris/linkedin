Brian Kinser’s Summary

Brian Kinser and Integrated Software Solutions are experts at providing proficiency in custom business process assessments and improvements to small, mid-sized and global corporate clients.

Integrated Software Solutions currently has three (3) operational divisions; services, products, manufacturing and distribution divisions. The services division includes the consulting business that ISS is well known for however, the merger and acquisitions business is also part of the division and provides ISS with many M&A opportunities.

Brian Kinser is a well known expert in the field of business process automation and has helped write or prepare several .NET course wares and books for Microsoft as well as helping to create the BizTalk 2006 certification exam (70-235, 70-240, 70-241), .Net 3.5 Pro and TS Exam (70-505, 70-563). Mr Kinser is also a sought after speaker and has spoken at many conferences to date.

Brian has provided custom development, migration, and integration solutions that capitalize on the Microsoft Solutions Framework and emphasize security, scalability, and flexibility. He is an experienced architect, developer and trainer surrounding Microsoft information worker solutions.

Mr. Kinser has over 13 years of real-world business experience and most recently completed projects for Microsoft building a business model out of the business process automation, workflow, BI and integration solutions that the Partners have produced.

Mr. Kinser is now back at the helm of Integrated Software Solutions, the company he founded 8 years ago. ISS is now the lead M&A company in its field and has numerous business aqcuisitions on the horizon.

ISS and has many client implementations including Eli Lilly, MediSpan, ACS HealthCare, Loreal, Golden Rule Insurance, Conseco, Wells Fargo, RCA/Technicolor, PING Golf and both state and federal government.
