Jeff Johannigman’s Summary

Versatile, innovative leader who combines skills in client relationship-building, consulting, training, and team leadership with extensive experience in the software industry. Degrees in Human Services and Computer Science demonstrate a unique ability to connect people and technology. An award-winning public speaker recognized for presenting complex topics with clarity, creativity, and enthusiasm. Consistently forge strong relationships based on trust and open communications. Effective in large corporations and small startups.

Major accomplishments include:
• Managed development of over 50 eLearning courses for such companies as Dell, Whole Foods, Centex Homes, and Motion Computing.
• Established the college recruiting program and managed worldwide staffing for Tivoli Systems (a division of IBM).
• Created one of the country's first alumni career services for the University of Texas
• Personally coached hundreds of people to find greater career success and fulfillment
• Founded two successful software companies, and managed development of over twenty-five published computer games.
