Stéphane Thomas’s Summary

Senior Engineer specialized in web applications. As a Consultant, I provide services to startups exclusively. And as an Entrepreneur, I am the founder of Loomiz, a new social bookmarking service meant to be simple, elegant and addictive.

Living in the South of France, I also initiated and organize an OpenCoffee Club in the Sophia Antipolis technology park to promote the entrepreneurial spirit and to meet entrepreneurs, investors and other people working in the Internet industry.

  • Founder

    Loomiz

    (Internet industry)

    October 2006 — Present (2 years 10 months)

    Loomiz is a new social bookmarking service meant to be simple, elegant and addictive. This service will let people store addresses (i.e. bookmarks) of their favorite websites online so they can access them from anywhere. Loomiz will also feature the possibility to organize addresses by keywords to find them more easily later on, as well as sharing them with his or her circle. It is not yet released, but I am still working hard at it. All my other professional activities are currently targeted at funding this new service which is entirely bootstrapped.
