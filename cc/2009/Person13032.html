Brandon Yates’s Summary

We design, install and support high end professional editorial, effects and audio workstations for video post production and visual effects companies in the metropolitan NY area. We can help you design your new facility, create workflow strategies and solutions, install full edit suites, update your machine room, clean up your cabling and patch bays, help you migrate between platforms, and much more.

• P2 and RED workflow training
