Eduardo M. Sousa’s Summary

I've been working as a freelance multimedia designer since 2000. I found myself wishing to learn more about Film and Video and so I took a 4-year degree in the period 2001-2005.

Since 2000 I've worked in a number of web-related projects as freelancer or as independent contactor, and since the last year of college I've worked, also freelance, in a number of audivisual projects, mostly as editor but also in the image department.

I'm also pursuing a carreer as a writer/director, having done a couple of (very) independent award-winning short films.

Eduardo M. Sousa’s Specialties:

Deep knowledge of film and video editing using tools such as Final Cut Pro or Premiere Pro. All-round knowledge of desktop post-production with After Effects. Highly familiar with HD and DV cinematography. Lighting for video, and some knowledge of lighting for film.

Deep knowledge of Photoshop. Webdesign using Dreamweaver or raw HTML+CSS. Competent understanding of server-side programming with PHP.
