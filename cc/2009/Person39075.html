Hugh Deura’s Summary

Help clients to succeed in Information security and compliance. Assist customers with short term InfoSec goals and developing long term startegic business objectives.

• Acted as an interim security professional
• Ensure that risk identification, analysis, and mitigation activities are integrated into life cycle processes
• Report significant changes in the risk to appropriate levels of management on both periodic & event driven basis
• Identify current and potential legal and regulatory issues affecting information security and assess their impact on enterprise based on threat and vulnerability pair.
• Created a policy requiring that all critical assets are protected and signed off by business owner on residual risk that exceeds agreed thresholds
• Establish, assess and maintain information security policies that support business goals & objectives
• Ensure the development of procedures & guidelines that support information security policies.
• Extensive experience in security design, implementing & reviewing controls
• Conducted security reviews based on ISO 27002 to provide a road map for ISMS

I help businesses and individuals who are working to develop an effective information security management system to succeed in their business.
