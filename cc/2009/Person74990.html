Norm Levy’s Summary

Developing a new-media "sharing" and "delivery" platform called LifeGoRound. LifeGoRound aggregates ALL of your social media content and allows you to share it in a new and clever fashion! Furthermore, all content that you add to our LifeGoRound magically appears on your LifeGoRound-compliant device such as the "eMotion Social-Connect Digital Photo Frame", iPhone, and other "screens" near you...

Developed a new music format that is bringing Physical Music Distribution back to Retail.

vAMP (virtual Album Music Player).
Is the shape and size of 3 credit cards stacked together and we embed a full album (in MP3 format) as well as photos, video(s), calendar of events, MySpace Skins and Audible Liner Notes.

We print the Album Cover Art on the Front and the Track Listings on the Back of the unit.

Not only do you receive a full album embedded on the vAMP, but you also get 1GB of internal memory to add hundreds of your own MP3 files to enjoy!

vAMP fits in your pocket, wallet or purse.

It has a patent-pending design for its built-in flip out USB cable that makes it so convenient to connect with devices while on the go without worrying about lugging extra cables.

It also comes with ear phones so you can begin enjoying the music as soon as you walk out of the store!

vAMP it Up!
First vAMP Albums to hit the stores Fall 2008!
Enjoy!
