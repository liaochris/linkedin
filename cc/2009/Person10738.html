Berry Insurance’s Summary

Berry Insurance is a marketing organization providing a full range of insurance and risk management services to over 3,000 individuals and families, and to 500 businesses throughout New England. We have offices in Franklin, Quincy, and Waltham, Massachusetts.

Berry Insurance is committed to providing our clients peace of mind with superior risk management solutions. We are sensitive and responsive to our clients needs and devoted to their satisfaction. We strive to operate with the highest level of integrity, courtesy and professionalism in all our relationships.

Berry Insurance’s Specialties:

Personal Lines: Auto, Home, Wedding, Flood, Earthquake, Umbrella, Boat, Motorcycle, Life. Commercial Lines: Liability, Property, Umbrella, Auto, Workers Compensation, Flood, Life, Group Health.
