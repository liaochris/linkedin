John Keister’s Summary

Since 1987, John Keister & Associates (www.johnkeister.com) has helped companies identify, recruit, and hire top Leaders and Innovators in Science and Engineering.

Our areas of speciality include:

• Engineering
• Science
• Mathematics & Statistics

We complete searches at the following leadership levels:
