Remco Bron’s Summary

Remco’s main expertise and passion is transforming business ideas into successful internet and marketing solutions by vision building, developing strategy and business plans, and bringing the right people together.

Since 1999 he has been actively involved in several internet and marketing related projects as entrepreneur and/or consultant. He has recently founded so many thoughts a consulting and project management agency which focuses on marketing and internet solutions.

Where business leads, IT will follow!

Remco Bron’s Specialties:

Remco is a vision builder, strategy developer, and can manage complex business and IT projects. He masters the ability to manage people and processes in complex business and technical environments, and translate them into marketing and sales opportunities. Proven leadership in multi-cultural team environments. Exceptional in crisis-management and/or interim-management of complex business, marketing, and IT related projects.
