Ksenia Oustiougova’s Summary

Founder and CEO of Lilipip! Studios, a network of independent artists helping businesses tell their story in a 1 minute animated video.

Founder of GoldenLeaf Productions, LLC - writer/director/producer for childrens animation/live action. "Walking Past" short animation screened at SIFF 2005. "The Golden Leaf" short film is in post-production currently.

Interior designer at Merrit+Pardini.

Founder of KO Design studio - interior architecture, industrial/product design.

Industrial design assistant at David Ryan Design Studio.

Have interest in children's educational entertainment, both live action and animation. Past experience in interior architecture and industrial/product design. Currently interested in early stage start-ups and entrepreneurial networking.
