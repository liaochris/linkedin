Rob Graham’s Summary

Four year in the industry and the learning continues. I love real estate and hope the increase in business I saw in 2008 only continues in 2009. About two thirds of my business is devoted to home buyers. In particular, I have worked with a large number of first time home buyers. First time home buyers bring an energy and enthusiasm to the process that is a lot of fun to work with.

At the moment I am trying to wrap my head around the new medium of on line marketing, blogging and networking. It is all a little overwhelming, but exciting all the same. Web 2.0 is here to stay and is clearly the future of the industry.

In the next year or so I hope to start the process toward my brokers license. No intention of leaving Windermere, but more education is always a good thing.
