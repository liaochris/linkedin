Shay Wheat’s Summary

I work with busy business owners to solve the problem of loosing business because they are not in regular touch with their clients and customers through Sendoutcards.com/shay. We provide an automated, yet highly personalized card system for customer follow up, thank yous, birthdays, product or event announcements, even for generating referrals.

With Sendoutcards you send your customers highly personalized cards through the mail using our unique website technology without ever leaving your office! So using our product does NOT add to your busy day, yet it gives you an easy way to have that personal customer contact. You could send 5 or 500 cards in 5 minutes, in your own handwriting, and even upload a picture or logo into the card.

Are you are ready to appreciate your clients and customers with a turn key system? Please contact me now at shay@millionairepartnerships.com

Together, let's change lives...one card at at time.
