Mike Warren’s Summary

A 26-year industry business prospectus that includes demonstrated accomplishments in the design and serviceability (of semiconductor components, motherboards and add-in cards), market and business development, and sales (for fab/fabless semiconductor merchants, system software engineering service companies, and intellectual property licensing companies) that span major blue chip giants to leading edge upstart companies.

Comprehension of hardware architectures, system software, firmware and device driver development, and active participation within industry standards trade associations and working groups enables direct effective communication with engineers, and simplifies translation of technical functionality into sales and marketing plans, technical proposals and contracts. A unique skill set to create and execute marketing strategies and match industry leading system software development expertise to real world customer needs.

Earned a B.S. degree in E.E., an M.S. in Engineering, and an M.S. in Engineering Management, all from Northeastern University in Boston.

  • Managing Director

The FirmLogix client list consists of semiconductor companies, original equipment manufacturers and various service companies.

    We offer test services including firmware utility and test development, certification (e.g., Microsoft ® Windows 7 ® / Windows Vista ® / Windows XP ® DTM (Driver Test Manager) and WHQL testing services) and QA testing.

This allows us to provide immediate support by phone, email, or on-site for our clients. There are no “night-and-day” time zone or communication barriers. You have direct access to the veteran developer that you select to lead or assist your project.

  • Advisory Board Member

    Stegosystems

    (Computer & Network Security industry)

    August 2006 — Present (3 years)

    EMBEDDED SOFTWARE SECURITY

    FirmLogix serves as a firmware, device driver, and hardware development center and as a silicon alliance partner for
    Stegosystems.

    Stegosystems is a Massachusetts company providing a software assurance service to eliminate malware and pirated software in virtualized environments.

    Stegosystems' innovative approach to software assurance permanently watermarks application and system software to dynamically protect critical, high value information resources throughout execution. Using patented steganography techniques, our unique approach makes no change to the object code footprint, its execution topology or its performance. This powerful new capability is complementary to existing software assurance techniques and delivers greater software assurance.
