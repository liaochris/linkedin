Jim Atkins’s Summary

I am a restructuring/bankruptcy attorney and founding partner of Law Offices of James E. Atkins P.C. My practice centers on helping small and mid-sized businesses adapt strategically to difficult financial circumstances. These can be brought on by a combination of factors, including poor economic conditions, disputes with customers or vendors, underperforming product and service lines and tax claims.

Jim Atkins’s Specialties:

Business restructuring, distressed M&A, chapter 11, creditors' rights, commercial litigation, bankruptcy, contract disputes, loan workouts and related projects.
