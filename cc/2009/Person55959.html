Klaas Weima’s Summary

Energize is a creative agency and specializes in interactive marketing. Energize builds memorable online experiences for consumers to increase brand awareness, traffic and sales. Energize develops effective marketing and brand activation campaigns for top brands in Europe. We are making brands meaningful in people's lives.

In short: Energize. Boosting Brands.

Key accounts include GlaxoSmithKline (incl. Sensodyne, Parodontax, Aquafresh), Walt Disney, H.J. Heinz, BlackRock, Unive, Mentos, Unicef and Disneyland Paris.

Klaas is founder and managing director of Energize. He leads a creative team of 18 professionals.

Since 1995, Klaas has dedicated his career towards interactive marketing. Besides Energize, Klaas has worked for the leading online media agency Reddion and promotional agency Free for One. In cooperation with the Erasmus University Rotterdam he conducted the first European Study on the effectiveness of banner advertising. As a result, he won the eBusiness Thesis Award and the DDMA Scholarship Award.

Klaas wrote the book "Webvertising: Tools for an Effective Campaign" and was co-author of “Industry Changes caused by the Internet”. He frequently writes for renowned marketing magazines and blogs. Titles include: AdfoResult, Web Desk, Interactive Advertising Bureau Handbook, Emerce Magazine, MolBlog and Tijdschrift voor Marketing (Journal of Marketing).

Also check out his podcast interviews with renowned marketers on Energize's iTunes Channel: http://bit.ly/18fLrs

For more information, please visit: www.energize.nl
or book him as a speaker through: http://thenextspeaker.com/
