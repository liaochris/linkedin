Brad Davis’s Summary

I have 8 years of design engineering experience covering RF Design in the wireless industry (GPS, 802.11, WiMax), test automation for wireless products, research and development on 17 GHz radio-over-fiber systems, and systems integration for data logging and control applications. I enjoy taking on challenging problems that improve peoples lives. Especially if this can be done through the application of technology but also by building great relationships with, and supporting, the people around me.

Brad Davis’s Specialties:

RF circuit design, system level design, ATE development, test equipment operation. Team leadership, organization, and motivation. Software Development in LabVIEW, Visual Basic, and C. Schematic and PCB capture.
