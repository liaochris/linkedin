Mohan Babu K’s Summary

I am a technocrat who has been working in the field of Software development since 1994. During the course of my career in Information Technology, I have had an opportunity to work on a variety of systems including Income Tax, Credit Card processing, retail and telecommunications. My responsibilities have included architecting and managing complex technology initiatives, analyzing system needs, coordinating system installations, writing user requirements and training users.

I am a regular columnist, Thought Leader and Corporate Blogger. Apart from my regular columns, I have also written articles for a number of international technical and non-technical publications including IEEE, Silicon India, Business Integration Journal and Deccan Herald. I am also author of a book on Offshoring and Outsourcing, titled "Offshoring IT Services : A Framework for Managing Outsourced Projects" [Publisher: McGraw-Hill, India, April 2006] (URL http://www.offshoringmanagement.com/)

I graduated from Mysore University in India where I received a Masters in Computer Applications (MCA). I also have an MBA (Technology management) from the University of Colorado, Colorado Springs.
