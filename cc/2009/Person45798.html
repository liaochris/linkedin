Jelle Bos’s Summary

My specific experience at the moment exists of three parts: marketing, communication and projectmanagement in the broad sense.

At FC Twente i managed a project last year where we had to communicate with all supporters (existing and new) about the new stadium. The process lasted 9 months in which a special website informed people about the building (www.thuisbijfctwente.nl), about their new seats, the new view from their seats (with a 3D model). The project existed also of the whole path of informing with a letter, costumer contacts, faq's, organizing events for sponsors, organizing the opening acts for the first game, organizing the backoffice etc. Another nice project was to make a companyfilm about FC Twente with a professional tv-team.

At Wegener i started as marketeer and have seen very different specialties in the marketingprofession (marketing intelligence, productmanagement, communication (internal & external)). My last experience is online marketing (www.deweekkrant.nl) for which i arranged a national campagne in the weekly papers and also did actions for readers in the weekly's.

At the moment i work at the program/projectmanagementdesk with companywide projects, including the total reorganization of Wegener. The goal is to make this great publisher ready for the future within a year. In this program i manage all communication to the employees and accompany two (marketing)projects that are part of this program.
