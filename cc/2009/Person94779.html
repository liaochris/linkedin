Steve Leaver’s Summary

Working with senior leaders and their teams, both locally and globally, enabling them to explore their hidden depths and enable their peak performance whilst ensuring they maintain a clear focus on generating benefits by positively impacting business results.

An experienced coach working in the area of leadership development for many years, developing the PeakDepths philosophy based on a personal and pragmatic approach to building high performance. With an initial technical and management career in telecommunications before moving into a people development role.

A passion for developing and embedding formal and informal coaching into the leadership and management of individuals and teams at all levels, or as part of a wider programme of development or change.

Can also offer a unique blend of coaching and development built around an introduction to scuba diving (for non-divers) or a diving experience for existing divers. Dive qualification courses can also be integrated into wider coaching and development programmes (as can other experiential activities).
