Michael Hurston’s Summary

My primary focus the last 8 years and currently is the creation of advertising and branding for a commercial real estate brokerage with offices in CA and NV. My previous three years of work history involved freelance design for many small and large companies. I also currently work on creating marketing collateral such as email blasts, flyers, brochures, offering presentations and featured property websites.

While working for Colliers International I have had the pleasure of living and working in both the bay area in California as well as Las Vegas, Nevada. I also have traveled to our Reno office and others to train new hires. I enjoy creating new and exciting promotional materials and I also enjoy traveling and collaborating with others to find the best solutions for our clients.

During my freelance period of graphic design focus I worked with many small and large companies through Kinko’s former technology Services branch as well as directly with tech companies like Mighty Micro.

In addition to Michael’s design and marketing experience he is also one of the main writers for the game Ave Molech, a D20 Fantasy based RPG set in a Steampunk world setting.
