Hassan Jawad (formatix@hotmail.com)’s Summary

Hassan Jawad was employed with Oracle Corporation, Mid Market Technology Sales in Pakistan.

He has been working with Ora-Tech Systems (Pvt.) Ltd. as a business Development Manager for HP and Oracle ERP Ebusiness Suite. Previouly with Synergy Computers (Pvt.) Ltd. marketing Sun Microsystems RISC/CISC Systems and Solaris and before that with International Office Products (Pvt.) Ltd. (Now Gulf Business Machines (GBM) marketing Microsoft Software, Cisco Networking & Wireless and IBM Computing Products in Lahore & Faisalabad territory. Key achievements in these roles were Sale of Infrastructure and Software Solutions to many Companies like TRG, Ibrahim Group, Chenab Group, Shaukat Khanum Memorial Hospital, PITB, University of Management & Technology, University of Engineering & Technology, Microtech Labs, WASA etc.

Prior to this Hassan was the director of Formatix Data Center, offering Training and other IT Services and Internet Sales. Developed of Loyalty Card System (Identification / Authentication), B2B ecommerce portal, online book store and B2C website for shopping.

Before entering into the IT Industry he was a partner in RediRack Systems where he was leading the marketing of breakthrough products in Pakistan like Slotted Angle Shelving, Storage and Material Handling Systems. Previously he worked with Synthetic Products Enterprises (Pvt.) Ltd. as Business Development Manager handling the marketing of Engineering Plastics and Disposable Plastic Packaging. Clients served were Pak Suzuki Motors, Millat Tractors, Paktel Ltd., Pepsi Cola Pakistan, Alcatel Pakistan, Atomic Energy Commission of Pakistan etc.
