Kevin Robinson’s Summary

I like to have fun and create cool stuff. My main interests lie in 3D modeling and motion design/video editing. I fancy the web and interactive design as well. You could call me a "digital creative," if you're into that sort of thing.

I have held challenging positions with Advanced Concepts at Lockheed Martin and international marketing at Marriott International.

I am looking start my career in the video game industry as a 3D Artist and Character Modeler.

Kevin Robinson’s Specialties:

3D Modeling and Rigging with Maya and 3DS Max, Sculpting and Painting Textures in zBrush, Virtual World Development, Digital Illustration, Interactive & Motion Design, Flash & Actionscript 2, Video Editing, Audio Editing, XHTML, CSS

Experience with C++, Java, Javascript (including the MooTools library), PHP5, and Actionscript 3
