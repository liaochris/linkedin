Chuck Kellner’s Summary

Charles Kellner helps clients with the use of electronically stored information used as evidence in litigaitons, government investigations, and criminal defense matters. This work is often described as eDiscovery, electronic data discovery, litigation support, or computer forensics.

Chuck specializes in projects for early case assessment, preservation, acquisition, search, and production of email and electronic data. He teaches CLE seminars with law firms, corporate law departments, and industry groups. Consulting and services clients are typically general or litigation counsel in Fortune 500 companies or litigators or litigation support managers in large law firms.
