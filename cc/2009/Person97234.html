David Flint’s Summary

Director of Marketing/Communications and Volunteering at Teaming for Technology (T4T), a program of United Way Metropolitan Chicago.

Founder of TechVenue.com - Since 1998, Your Venue for Business Technology Events, News and Networking. David is a dynamic, thoughtful professional with a career rich in marketing, advertising, promotions, & technology project management experience. Always seeking new challenges to use skills in leading teams that produce high-quality results & client satisfaction – all accomplished by working closely with senior management, aligning business-side goals, technology resources & staffing with organization’s overall business strategies - public or private.

A leading futurist, digital entrepreneur, corporate storyteller and author, David's experience has given him insight about the future of technology and its impact on businesses, individuals and society; translating brick & mortar workflows into new digital frontiers for companies & infusing the human element back into technology.

Mr. Flint also spends a great deal of volunteer time with the United Way Metropolitan Chicago in MarCom and Technical Consulting roles.
He has managed grant writing and external technical assistance needs of partner agencies. He is responsible for securing grants from IBM and other companies totaling over $3,000,000 in cash, equipment, and gifts-in-kind over a three year period. David helped lead a Katrina database collaboration project: Data shared with Red Cross and other local agencies – soon over 7000 evacuees were able to access continuing services by registering online at www.unitedwaycrisisrecovery.org, or by phone.

Mr. Flint is available for focus groups, speaking engagements, interviews, and of course - business networking here in LinkedIn and via VisiVenue.com.
