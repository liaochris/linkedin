  • Sales & Marketing Director

    TrustMission

    (Privately Held; Information Technology and Services industry)

    July 2009 — Present (5 months)

    Historical leader in correspondance dematerialization (letters, invoices, documents).
    First electronic correspondance system with legal probative value in the world.

    TrustMission’s technological platforms TrustINVOICE, TrustLETTER and TrustSECURE allow outsourcing of the handling of mail and structured files (letters, contracts, transaction and collection notifications, etc.).

    These flows are sent:

    * either in a hybrid version (paper original and/or electronic management copy),

    * or in a dematerialised version (original archived in an electronic safe).TrustMission thus guarantees the security and indisputable legal value of all Professional Electronic Exchanges.

    For sensitive “paper” letters or management mail, the TrustLetter platform allows pooling and outsourcing of:

    * the production of the letters (printing, enveloping and franking),

    * the dispatching of the letters (handover to the carrier),

    * return management (acknowledgements of receipt, “not at this address” mail returns, etc.),

    * online monitoring of the whole process (hybrid mail),

    * probative value archiving and the legally binding nature of letters, meanwhile, are ensured by the TrustSecure platform.

    In the context of dematerialised exchanges with probative value, TrustMission’s solutions also free companies from the constraints of paper correspondence by automating the composition, validation, probative value archiving and transmission steps with an electronic signed acknowledgement of receipt (fully digital registered letter).

    TrustMission’s innovative approach thus makes it possible to switch progressively from paper invoicing to electronic invoicing while complying with the requirements of articles 289a or 289 V of the French General Tax Code.
