shelagh watkins’s Summary

Shelagh Watkins began writing in 1998. To date, she has two published novels, one published poem and one published short story.

Mr. Planemaker's Flying Machine (2005)
Hope for a Safer Place in the anthology Stories of Strength (2005)
Leo's Daughter: Mistress of the Web in the Greek Science Fiction and Fantasy Magazine Universe Pathways (2007)


She set up Mandinam Press in January 2008 and published:

The Power of Persuasion (2008)
Forever Friends (2008)

Shelagh Watkins set up the Published Authors Network on August 5th 2007
Shelagh Watkins set up the Published Authors Forum on January 11th 2008
