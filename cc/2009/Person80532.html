Patrick Dunn’s Summary

Patrick Dunn has over eighteen years of experience in providing Information Technology (IT) services that have focused upon Pandemic, Disaster Recovery & Business Continuity Planning, Crisis and Incident Management, Information Security and IT Management The specific IT services that have been provided include, Disaster Recovery & Business Continuity Planning, Crisis Management, Pandemic and Incident Management Exercises, Information Systems & Data Center Operations Division Management, Information Security, IT Assessment and Project Management for multi-national, Fortune 500 and Big Five Accounting Firms. Patrick has also extensively focused on Pandemic Planning and has developed core Pandemic Methodology that is currently in use by an International consulting company and has been used on hundreds of engagements across North America.

Patrick has outstanding communication and analytical skills which he couples with creative problem solving and astute trouble-shooting to provide exceptional consulting and business continuity guidance. He has provided consulting services in the North America and in Europe.


His recent consulting projects in the business continuity arena span many subject areas - including Pandemic Planning and Exercises, and include a range of industries, including financial and brokerage, healthcare, and local and regional governments.

He is a well-known conference/webinar speaker and has published articles and whitepapers in a variety of professional journals.
