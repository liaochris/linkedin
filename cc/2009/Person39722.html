Robert Higgins’s Summary

Robert Higgins Fenestrator, PMP, Entrepreneur and Digital Coach. He is comfortable working in a Global Environment. He has solid Daily Operations Management and Project Management skills. As a Vice President he has had the opportunity to work on hundreds of high profile Residential Homes and Commercial Buildings.

In addition to his extensive Architectural Project background, he has Initiated, Planned, Executed and Closed a variety of projects. For example; Financial, Training, Technology and Market Research. Robert Higgins has been working in and out of China and Japan for the last 8 years.

He has a balanced personality of Entrepreneurial Risk Taking and Consensus Building. He is experienced in working with complex teams of distributed people. Robert is skilled at provoking teams to visualize solutions.

Having lived the West meets East life he can appreciate the subtle difference interacting with people from different backgrounds. He believes in providing team members with expression channels, and being nimble enough to rapidly take advantage of positive risk events.

"I especially enjoy complex structures and communicating the essential information."

Currently, he is residing in Tokyo. He is deeply interested in Asian Culture. He is always improving his bi-lingual Japanese reading, writing and conversational skills.

In addition to his PMP credential he holds a Bachelor of Arts in Cultural Anthropology from Fort Lewis College in Durango, Colorado. He is an expert at Fenestration. As a practitioner of Project Management he is enthusiastic about promoting good project skills and sharing his knowledge of Projects and People.

He has held Seminars, Workshops and Coached. He has taught "Introduction to Project Management". He has given Presentations for The Project Management Institutes Global Congress. Please contact him directly for information on cast glass, bronze windows, workshops, digital coaching and other opportunities.
