Lisa Withers’s Summary

Currently I am responsible for maintaining ResortQuest NWFL inventory online to include complex and unit descriptions, photos and rates. I also help set up and define new programs that we will be using as a company both web based and on our PMS system. Social media is my next venture and have started to get my feet wet with different areas.

Database Administrator - I have spent over 10 years maintaining the rental database for over 3000 units including owner and unit records, rates, taxes, packages and cleans. Was responsible for trouble shooting programs for our IT department to determine if there was a problem with hardware, software or user error.
