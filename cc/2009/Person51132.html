Joshua Butler’s Summary

I have been working in the entertainment industry for ten years. I run a media non-profit (Texas Filmmakers) which in turn runs a film festival (Thin Line Film Fest). I have released two DVDs; first is One-Eyed Girl, a feature doc about Bay St Louis, MS after Hurricane Katrina; second is Bohemia Rising: the story of fry st. I also manage the public access television station in Denton, TX. I want to grow support locally and regionally for Texas Filmmakers while expanding the statewide awareness of Thin Line.
