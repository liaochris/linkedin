Matt Van Bergen’s Summary

My professional experience has included positions with large technology companies such as Motorola to small startup companies where I was hired as the first engineer. In the past 10 years, my focus has been in architecting and implementing highly available and scalable software based applications for commercial and public sector organizations.

My role today includes establishing technical direction, business development and service delivery management for CityTech, Inc. a boutique enterprise software consulting firm located in Chicago, IL.
