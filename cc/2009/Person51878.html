Juan David Gomez’s Summary

I am a highly Innovative Engineering Professional with 6 years of experience in the software industry; I’m a certified Microsoft Trainer and I also hold other Microsoft certifications like MCSD, MCTS MCAD and MCP.

I was the Technical Reviewer for Microsoft's official course on Windows Mobile 6 Development (6066a). I also contribute on other fronts of the Windows Mobile community, like being part of the “Board of Experts” at the Smartphone & Pocket PC magazine Best Software Awards.

I have a strong technical background in electronics and telecommunications, a proven track record of successful software projects, solid understanding of the software development life cycle (SDLC) and a high proficiency in various platforms including Microsoft’s .NET and Java.
