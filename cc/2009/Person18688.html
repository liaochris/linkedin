Christopher (Chris) Litster’s Summary

A hands-on, roll-up the sleeves, senior level marketing and CRM professional with fifteen years experience in various industries and corporate environments. My experience has allowed me to nurture a unique combination of expertise in traditional marketing disciplines coupled with a proven track record of leading successful CRM initiatives.

I have had the incredible opportunity to match my keen understanding and knowledge of the entirety of the marketing lifecycle with my extensive knowledge of the underlying marketing systems, processes, and technologies to ensure that companies gain the desired insight and understanding of the varied audiences with whom they interact and are able to transform that insight into actionable strategies and programs.

I have put these skills to use in a variety of corporate settings:
- a small family owned 100 year old company (the A. W. Chesterton Company)
- a rapidly growing software company (Rational Software)
- one of the largest companies in the world (IBM Corp.)
- an innovative market leader focused on enabling Small Businesses with leading edge marketing best practices and tools (Constant Contact)
