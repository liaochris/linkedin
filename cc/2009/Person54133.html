The National Graduate Preparatory Program: GradPrep
==========
The National Graduate Preparatory Program:GradPrep is the national program and faith-based fellowship for graduate study. It is the first national graduate preparatory program in America. The organization offers a multi-dimensional approach to service both the spiritual and intellectual needs of today's positive change agents.

Offerings include GRE, LSAT, MCAT, DAT (Dental), OAT (Optometry) and USMLE discounts and scholarships,
interdisciplinary and cross-cultural fellowship and events,
ongoing professional development and training, missionary travel and faith-based seminars.

==========
The MBADiversity Organization: MBADiversity
==========
The first inclusive multicultural organization of its kind, MBADiversity was started in 2003 by TMR Group Inc., Keisha Dawn's 501 (c)(3) corporation consisting of a faith-based leadership team of motivated, strategic professionals.

The organization offers many services and events including:
GMAT and GRE discounts and scholarships, the National Diversity Symposium, the Global Immersion Module (GIM), LEAD and FAITH modules and diversity training.

MBADiversity is collaborative and partners with other organizations and consultants that have expertise in various areas.

With the help of innovative Business schools, alliances, and companies, this organization has not only cleared the road but also uncovered new paths to cultivate a successful business pool.
