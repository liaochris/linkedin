Seema Kumar’s Summary

Working as Asst. Professor after having worked in the apparel industry - in apparel buying houses, as well as export houses.

Backed by professional qualification of Postgraduation in 'Knitwear Design and Technology' from the 'National Institute of Fashion Technology' (NIFT), Mumbai, and Graduation in 'Drawing & Painting' (Honours)

Work experience in the field of product design and development, knitwear design, export merchandising & teaching. Have worked with various US and European customers and handled varied product categories like sweaters, fine knits, denim, wovens and also a brief experience in Leather.
