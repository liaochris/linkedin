Tim Leach’s Summary

Joined the Air Force out of High school in 1975 where I was assigned work in the Weather Service. While in the service I started College and completed my Bachelors in Computer Science, Business Administration, and Electrical Engineering in 1982. At this point I applied for Officer positions in the Air National Guard and was accepted and graduated the Academy of Military Science in 1989. I retired from the Air Force as a Logistics Officer on Sept 2 2001 with 26 years of service. My first civilian job was in Nashville right out of college programming with Sperry/Rand company which later was renamed to Unisys. I was part fo a team that developed financial applications for mid-level companys in COBOL and RPG. I then moved to Cincinnati working in data communication with ICC which merged with DCA later merged again with Attachmate where I worked for over 15 years in development and testing, when I left I was a Director of Quality Assurance. I then started working for Great American Insuracne Company in Testing, I left AFG for a short stint with Fifth Third as a Test Manager, then came back as Testing Director at Great American. I recently started working at ACS managing a Global testing service for HRIS.
