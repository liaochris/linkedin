Massimo Marini’s Summary

Coordinator of R&D, Operations, Logistics and Technical Assistance areas within Staer Sistemi and subcontractors.
Long experience in HW, FW and Product design for SCADA and TLC equipment.
Project Manager in a variety of SCADA systems in Italy, Middle East and North Africa (Algeria).
Recently involved in Photovoltaic Power Plants SCADA systems and public street lighting control.
System designer of SCADA and Telecommunication Networks.
HW/FW designer of Remote Terminal Units for SCADA Applications.
Product designer/Project Manager of a number of Telecommunication appliances based on ARM/XSCALE embedded Linux: a modular Router, an SNMP Alarm Collection Unit, a Programmable Logic Controller based on IsaGraf and self-designed remote I/O.
Product designer of ancillary telephone systems for PDH/SDH Microwave Networks.
Product desinger of a satellite modem based on Thuraya network.
Proposal manager for SCADA systems in Oil&Gas, Broadcasting and Telecommunications, and for custom designed appliances.
Strong experience in 8051 microcontrollers and Linux Embedded appliances.
Design and implementation of WAN networks based on Cisco routers and interface converters, especially over wireless point-to-point microwave networks.
I have been involved in projects for important customers:
RaiWay, ENI - AGIP, SNAM Rete Gas, Hewlett Packard, Telvent, Sonatrach, Cegelec, ABB, Nokia Siemens Networks, Selex Communications, Alcatel-Lucent, ENEL, Wind
