Gary Roelke’s Summary

30+ years experience in the funding, execution, and intermediation of corporate finance and M&A transactions, with highly successful corporate and entrepreneurial experience. Frequent speaker on M&A topics, including advanced credentialling/masters programs at Columbia, Rutgers and NYU Stern School of business. Most of my business is sourced through personal and professional referal, and I delight in paying substantial finders fees.

Gary Roelke’s Specialties:

M&A: (i) full service sell-side/divestiture dealmaker (including valuation, preparation for market, initiating and maintaining buyer contact, deal negotiation, tasking the other professionals on the deal team, and ensuring that the client's deal actually closes); and (ii) imaginative, professional acquisition dealmaking for serial, strategic, well-funded acquirers. Sweet spot: transaction values of $5 to $200 million sell-side; $25 to $500 million buy-side.
