Andrew Jenkins’s Summary

Volterra Consulting specializes in business design and innovation with emerging technologies in the Internet, Wireless and Social Media sectors.

Volterra Consulting helps organizations:

• assess their current state

• identify what, where and how emerging technologies might help them with their corporate objectives

• devise the appropriate strategy incorporating those technologies

If a company wants to understand the technology trends that will matter to them and why, Volterra Consulting provides the necessary actionable insights.

Andrew Jenkins, Founder and Principal, is an action-oriented and innovative senior manager with Information and Communication Technology (ICT) experience spanning social media/networks, wireless, e-business, telecommunications, internet, software, and hardware solutions. Well versed in the various media platforms/channels (web, wireless, television). Extensive international travel and business experience throughout North America and Europe.

Interested in opportunities in social media/Web 2.0, cross platform, wireless/telecom and the internet that build on past channel, alliance, business development and strategy experience.

Interested in Advisory Board or Corporate Director opportunities.

UK Ancestry Visa holder enabling work in the UK without sponsorship.
