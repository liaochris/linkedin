Sven Hensen’s Summary

Successful corporations always rationalize the way they operate. They are continuously searching for information and systematically applying it for sound analysis and reliable forecasts. They want to be sure that they are making the right decisions because they consistently act on these decisions. They demand to know more about their customers and the marketplace than their competitors and to continuously improve.

With the inception of mayato, a top-class consulting team is embarking with the objective to make your organization more successful through perfect decisions. Huge budgets are being invested in the development of Business Intelligence solutions and most organizations are not able to achieve the hoped for return on investment. This insight and the comprehension that the key to success lies in the integration of analysis and application were the driving forces behind the creation of mayato. Our mission is to enable your organization to act on facts and make reliable, perfect decisions.


As a founding partner Sven's objective is to implement mayato's vision and make mayato the go-to company for Business Intelligence and Business Analytics.


Since 1995 Sven has gained experience designing and implementing SAP solutions with a focus on planning, reporting, and information systems in a variety of industries. After graduating with a degree in business, Sven started his career at SAP AG focusing on the implementation of finance and controlling solutions. For more than six years, he has helped organizations improve their business performance with SAP’s business intelligence suite of products. Sven is a distinguished project manager and coach, and has managed projects in Europe, Latin America, and North America. Besides English and German, he is fluent in Portuguese, and has lead successful project teams in all three languages in different cultural environments.
