Dan Hausel’s Summary

I have >30 years professional experience in mapping complex gold systems in Archean and Proterozoic terrains - (mapped 5 greenstone belts), exploration of Tertiary gold systems in Wyoming, Utah & Alaska. >30 years experience in exploration and research on conventional and unconventional diamond deposits and colored gemstones, >30 years experience in exploration of copper, platinum-group metals, nickel, ferrous and other strategic metals.During my career, I found and identified several hundred gold, diamond, colored gemstone and other mineral deposits and anomalies.

Dan Hausel’s Experience

  • CEO

    W Dan Hausel Geological Consulting LLC

    (Mining & Metals industry)

    January 2007 — Present (3 years )

    I find mineral deposits & identify high-quality mineral projects. Over the years, I discovered 2 world-class colored gemstone deposits, several hundred gold anomalies & deposits (including a previously unrecognized district), a palladium-platinum-gold-nickel deposit, & several hundred diamond occurrences and deposits with many unexplored cryptovolcanic structures characteristic of kimberlite (http://gemstonehunter.blogspot.com).

  • Vice President of Exploration, DiamonEx USA Ltd

    DiamonEx Ltd

    (Mining & Metals industry)

    September 2006 — March 2008 (1 year 7 months)

    2006-2008 - As a consultant, DiamonEx assigned a project to me to organize a subsidiary (DiamonEx USA), hire employees & acquire diamondiferous kimberlite (Sloan Ranch) in the State Line district & search for additional targets - I found more than 100 high-quality targets for the company within and surrounding the district (http://discussionsondiamonds.blogspot.com).

    Prior to this, I was a research geologist for the Wyoming Geological Survey (1977-2007) & consultant for several diamond & gold companies including Western Archon, Ice Diamonds, Teras Gold, Western Gold Exploration & Mining, Chevron, Echo Bay, Bald Mountain Mining, etc.

  • Senior Economic Geologist, Deputy Director

    Wyoming Geological Survey

    (Mining & Metals industry)

    August 1977 — January 2007 (29 years 6 months)

    Conducted research & geological mapping on Archean greenstone belts, mining districts, gold deposits, gemstones, diamonds, platinum-group metals, strategic & ferrous metals. Published more than 600 professional papers and contributed to 30 books as well as published dozens of detailed geological maps. Also presented more than 400 lectures and field trips to companies, the general public, research institutes & the Wyoming legislature (http://wygemstones.blogspot.com).
