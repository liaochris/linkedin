Greg Angelillo’s Summary

I am accepting LinkedIn invitations. My email address is info@getimarketing.com

I have over a decade of Internet related marketing experience, am an active member of the American Marketing Association and eMarketing Certified by the eMarketing Association.

I apply a proven methodology to developing a business's online web presence with a focus on driving traffic to the business's website and then keeping the visitors returning to the online property. Intense focus on SEO best practices, keyword analysis, as well as overal web analytics are all incorporated to maximize marketing results of online traffic (new and returning).

My professional career includes Vice President/General Manager of Business Development of Connecticut’s largest independent Internet Service Provider. There, I project managed the development of online businesses both large and small, ranging from small business to Fortune 100 companies. I was also responsible for mergers and acquisitions for Omega Communications, Inc.

I have been strongly focused over the past few years in online marketing techniques including search engine optimization, pay-per-click marketing, web site content development, web site analytics and creating a proven methodology for creating and maintaining total online marketing strategies.

In summary, my experience includes social media marketing, web site development, project management, web site design, search engine optimzation/marketing, web analytics, css, xml, javascript, business strategy and development, business marketing, affiliate networks, Web 2.0, keyword analysis, web content, and web site monetization.
