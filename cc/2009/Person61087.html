Lynn Hoganson’s Summary

I am very excited to be a part of the team at KLEINFELDER. We are an Employee Owned Company with many exciting opportunities in the fields of environmental, geotechnical, energy, water, transportation, federal contracts commercial, industrial and construction materials testing. We have a dynamic staff of 2,200 employees nationwide. Since we are employee owned (and we believe our employees are our future), we build strength in our staff by encouraging continuing education, mentoring and developing leadership from within, and vesting each employee with the authority to provide responsive, value-added service. Our talented and diverse staff includes a wide range of disciplines in the fields of engineering, earth sciences, construction management, and information systems management.

Working as a team, our bright people will deliver the right solutions.

We exist to serve our clients, our people, and our communities. We are driven to help them recognize their potential and realize their goals.

To learn more about us, please visit us at www.KLEINFELDER.COM.
