Kevin Wilson’s Summary

I'm the co-founder of ERPGenie.COM (formerly SAPGenie.COM) the largest independent online web resource for ERP consultants on the web. I am the webmaster for all of our websites and manage all parts of operations regarding them.

In addition, as part of the Q Data consulting team I provide consulting services to clients in the areas of EDI / ALE, workflow, XI and SAP Event Management.
Having working in the SAP field since 1996, I have been fortunate to have worked many projects in the interfacing areas of SAP.

Prior to my SAP career I worked on implementing large scale integrated information systems where I was both project manager and workflow / image management guru.

In the next couple of years I'll be focusing on consulting as per usual, as well as on expanding our Genieholdings.com business and making ERPGenie.COM THE place to go, when searching for a job or ERP content, on the web.....
