Douglas Levin’s Summary

A visionary, strategic and market-savvy executive and entrepreneur with an outstanding record of building VC-backed technology businesses. Over twenty-nine years of software industry and twenty-three years of global enterprise experience. He has broad experience in finance, operations, business development, marketing and sales, and an excellent track record of creating and serving on boards of Directors and Advisors.
