Steve White’s Summary

Steve is a professionally qualified senior project manager/programme manager with more than fifteen years IT related change management experience with strong demand creation skills. He is a proven leader of multi million pound, multi-threaded projects and programmes, managing interdisciplinary teams in complex, multi-site, high value organisations.
Steve negotiates effectively at board level and with colleagues to ensure quality is not compromised whilst working within the parameters of time and cost to deliver tangible business benefits. A key specialism is providing clients with practical strategies, plans, and roadmaps for streamlining IT's ability to align with the business, deliver IT-driven solutions, and manage costs.
Steve is experienced in all aspects of IT Services sales and delivery having delivered multi million pound projects and managed campaigns to develop similar sized opportunities
