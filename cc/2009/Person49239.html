Johni Fisher’s Summary

The core of Looppa’s strategy lies in the B2B space, where companies already have an existing group of members waiting to be energized as a social network.
This can be the audience of a TV show/radio program
the newspaper/magazine subscribers, the loyal consumers of a product,or the clients of any company, which as a community could be more interactive with its clients.

Although our technology is applicable to a wide range of industries,we are focusing initially on traditional
media companies – television networks, radio stations,
printed media, etc.Media companies have a
tremendous asset base in their viewership or subscribers, but the traditional advertising models are no longer generating enough revenues in the face of competition from web-based
alternatives.

Looppa gives them a way to actively engage their customers in well-defined segments, allowing them to monetize their client base through greater loyalty and a platform for
higher-value, targeted advertising.


These days in the online world, professional website development is absolutely vital to the creation of a powerful and respected online reputation as well as the overall success of online based businesses. Simply moving your company from off line to online is not enough to make you a serious player in the online world
The commercial solution is the one that most of us are looking for and with Social media new tools and applications we can help publishers and any corporate to increase or create new revenues
Looppa aims to change the way people work, create and develop markets, using social media and collaboration technologies that allow publishers or corporate to make money from their content facilitating the companies’ engagement with their target audiences.
Using the social media applications provided by Looppa , our customers communicate more effectively with their client base, reduce marketing costs and generate measurable results that equate to revenue.

Johni Fisher’s Specialties:

We provide a complete solution for companies wishing to deploy their own branded social media platforms including consultancy, software products, professional services and on-going support.Looppa is the partner of choice for Media companies , wishing to deploy enterprise social media applications.
Our professional services include both development & design capabilities that allow a customer to completely customize their environment to their brand identity.
