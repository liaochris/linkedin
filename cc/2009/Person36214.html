Gianni D'Alerta’s Summary

Gianni D'Alerta has worked in the interactive and creative market for more than 14 years. He started as a designer for print and rapidly moved through the ranks from directorial positions to even working 2 years for himself., Gianni supervises and mentors interactive teams overseeing all aspects of client interactive advertising and marketing, SEM and SEO campaigns, creative and development, and results tracking. He is also on the forefront of social media marketing and new media initiatives. His passion for integrated marketing that brings the offline and online worlds into compelling and results oriented directions fuels him everyday. Gianni loves to be in front of clients selling great ideas that become successful solutions.He's fluent in English and Spanish but, being a technologist (nerd), he might be caught speaking mumbo-jumbo.

Gianni D'Alerta’s Specialties:

Making the complex simple and the simple beautiful. Expert web and graphic communications services, emerging technologies and social/new media marketing... with a touch of guerrilla marketing!
