Anora McGaha’s Summary

Anora McGaha has insight, passion and experience in a variety of areas, after 20 years in major US organizations in Boston, New York, Washington DC and Raleigh, NC. Blending extensive experience as an analyst, writer, graphics development and research, Anora serves her clients with strategic intelligence, marketing savy, global perspective and search engine conscious writing.

As with everyone who works with the internet, no service would be complete without search engine optimization, reputation audits and management, and publicity campaigns.

Anora's strong command of language; rigorous corporate training & experience; creativity, focus and intelligence all serve her clients well.

Anora offers consulting and coaching on internet publicity, writing, and business startups.

The daughter of a diplomat and educated in over four languages, Anora's fascination and experience is with communications, intra-cultural communications as well as inter-cultural! She is fluent in conversational Mandarin, and understands and reads French and Italian and some Spanish.

Business startup consulting; Internet publicity; press releases; presentation development; speech writing; articles; newsletters; web content; blog development; marketing and writing; photography; interviews; business and financial analysis.
