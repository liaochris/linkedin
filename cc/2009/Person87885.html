Ryan Murray’s Summary

SalesQuest is the home of CRUSH Reports & CRUSH Alerts. We track and report on the Fortune 1000, the Global 500, and the top spending Federal, State, and local government agencies. CRUSH Reports highlight IT architectures, strategies, capital expenditures, relevant sales trigger events, insight on initiatives/projects/budgets, contact info, and org charts. CRUSH Reports help enterprise sales reps make an immediate positive impact on the sales pipeline by eliminating research time and accelerating sales cycles.

CRUSH Alerts is a sales trigger email alert service that delivers timely and relevant info on your target accounts, industries, and territories. We track 56 sales triggering events, monitor 5 Million web sites, and pull data from over 30,000 info sources. We filter out the noise and only deliver what's valuable and actionable. CRUSH Alerts enable sales reps to be the first one to the sales opportunity and to be 'in the know' about their target accounts, territories, industries and competitors.
