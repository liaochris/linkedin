Sue Perry’s Summary

I am a highly experienced online manager who has worked at a senior level both as a consultant and in-house across various sectors for over 10 years, assisting a number of organisations with shaping their online strategies, improving their business processes and delivering their programmes and service improvements.

My goal is to keep doing what I love, and that's to work with business stakeholders and web specialists in challenging commercial environments, delivering real business value through the effective and innovative use of the web.

Specialisms (within the online / ebusiness / web space):
* Programme and Project Management
* Relationship Management, including at Partner level and with senior stakeholders
* Supplier Management (onsite and offshore)
* Governance and Risk Management

I have helped a number of organisations to develop and implement online strategies including for B2B, ecommerce and Intranets and have advised them in areas such as SEO, accessibility and other web standards, usability, social media and online security.

I have also worked with a range of web technologies and platforms and with a number of web related tools (such as Gomez and Omniture) and have delivered projects using both waterfall and Agile methodologies (such as Scrum).

I have a pragmatic approach to programme delivery and consider myself to be able to work effectively with people at all levels and from different areas of the business (commercial, technical and support).

Sue Perry’s Specialties:

* front-end web development skills (standards-compliant xhtml, CSS2, javascript, PHP, mysql)

*wordpress implementation and theme customisation

* implementation, customisation and use of various google web tools including analytics, webmaster central, website optimizer and adsense
