Neil Fisher’s Summary

As an experienced freelance medical writer within the healthcare communications arena, I provide a wide range of writing and editorial services to the pharmaceutical and medical communications industries. My services include writing and adapting text for a number of target audiences, searching, proofreading, and product analysis.

All therapeutic areas covered. My extensive training in pharmacology and wide-ranging experience in medical writing enable me to adapt quickly to any subject. My most common areas of expertise have been as follows:

• Diabetes
• Rheumatology
• Cardiovascular and cerebrovascular disease
• Analgesia
• Oncology
• Epilepsy
• Female health
• Toxicology

I have been in the pharmaceutical field since 1985, and regularly continue to update my skills and knowledge by attending conferences of the European Medical Writers Association (EMWA).

Pharmaceutical companies and medical communications agencies rely on my reliable, professional and friendly service, and flexible approach, to deliver clear, concise work of the highest quality, to specification, and on time.

I have a long-standing portfolio of satisfied clients (recommendations below). For further information, or to discuss a specific project, please contact me at your convenience.

Neil Fisher’s Specialties:

• Journal manuscripts: primary papers, reviews, case studies, letters to the editor
• Conference coverage: proceedings, highlights, summaries, competitor reviews, advisory board, working party and other internal meeting reports, slide kits, abstracts and posters
• Product support: reports and analyses, planning, searches, monographs and brochures, summaries of key papers, detail aids
• Training & education: training manuals, internet/intranet content, patient education material, newsletters
