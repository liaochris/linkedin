Julie Maloney’s Summary

Julie Maloney has been an active member of the blogging community for nearly a year. In that short period of time she has grown a vast readership and following on both Twitter and Facebook. She blogs the Cool Mom's Guide to parenting on her personal site, Momspective (www.Momspective.com), and hosts a wide array of product reviews and giveaways on her sister site Momspective Reviews (reviews.Momspective.com). In November 2008 she wrote an inspirational story about her journey to weight loss using the Nintendo Wii Fit (www.Momspective.com/Wii-Fit). She met two other enthusiastic bloggers and co-founded the Wii Mommies virtual fitness website (www.WiiMommies.com) in December 2008. In its 6 month existence Wii Mommies forum has registered nearly 800 members and is the source of great support and encouragement for women and men around the world to lose weight and get fit together.

In March, 2009, Julie participated in the EA Sports Active 30 Day Challenge with 11 other team members, including Allison Sweeney, host of The Biggest Loser. She met one-on-one with Oprah's personal fitness trainer, Bob Greene, and talked about how to better reach members of her community on achieving their personal fitness goals. When the weather cools and football teams take the field Julie can be found at home, cheering on the Carolina Panthers. She is using her passion for football to help women enjoy the game and help strengthen relationships with their spouses by teaching women about the sport through Fantasy Football.

She has since formed her own Corporation, Cool Mom Guide, LLC, doing business as both Momspective and the Wii Mommies. She has over 7000 followers on Twitter, reaches over 60,000 page views a month and has mentions on numerous shows and national magazines for her work online.
