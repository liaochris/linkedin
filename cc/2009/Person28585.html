Duane Bowker’s Summary

I have over 27 years experience in communications systems design, development, integration and testing. I hold eight U.S. patents and numerous international patents for inventions related to voice and data communications. I enjoy challenging assignments that provide opportunities for working with highly talented people, for learning new technologies and for advancing my skill set.

Duane Bowker’s Specialties:

Software systems design and development, web services design and implementation, Service Oriented Architecture (SOA), systems integration, data communications protocols, inferential statistics, data mining and predictive data modeling, communications systems engineering, systems test, visual and auditory psychophysics, active high-level U.S. security clearances.
