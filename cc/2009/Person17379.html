Cheryl Norman’s Summary

After a 25-year career in telecommunications, I now write full-time. My first mass-market paperback novel, RESTORE MY HEART, earned me a mention in Publisher's Weekly as one of ten romance authors to watch. RUNNING SCARED earned a Perfect 10 from Romance Reviews Today. I'm also co-authoring a project with Judith Leigh for Highland Press called ROMANCE ON ROUTE 66 out this summer, followed by my next Medallion Press release, RECLAIM MY LIFE.

Cheryl Norman’s Specialties:

I offer grammar help for writers at my blog and also conduct workshops on grammar and various aspects of writing for writers groups.
