Rod King, Ph.D.’s Summary

OVERVIEW

Dr. Rod King is a thought leader, consultant, and trainer on Trade-Off Mapping & Customer Experience Innovation as well as Blue Ocean Project Management. The goal of these approaches is to facilitate visual analysis, innovation, and decision-making for 'hit' products, services, and business models.

As an expert on visual trade-off analysis, decision-making, business modeling, and customer experience innovation, Dr. King helps individuals, teams, and businesses to visually develop business models that deliver greater competitive advantage and profitability. He offers clients a better way to ask and manage breakthrough questions not only for visually designing 'hit' products, services, and business models but also for innovating on, monitoring, and managing customer experiences.

Dr. King is the inventor of a portfolio of visual tools for Trade-Off Analysis & Design (TOAD) as well as Blue Ocean Project Management (BOPM). His tools for BOPM visually integrate 3 powerful tools in business management: Value (Supply) Chain; Blue Ocean Strategy; Balanced Scorecard.

Dr. King is the inventor of the “Fractal Box,” a visual organizer that has pending US and international patents. The Fractal Box is being applied as a zoomable interface of a visual search engine. He uses the Fractal Box to facilitate visual idea generation, business model design, and customer experience management. Another notable invention of Dr. King is Trade-off Mapping.
