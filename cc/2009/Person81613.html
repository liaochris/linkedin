Raja Choudhury’s Summary

I have been designing and building successful online and multimedia brands, kiosk networks, buildings, websites and videos in the US, UK and Indian markets since 1993. I am always seeking new challenges to take on and large projects or campaigns to manage.

In the past 2 years I have built 2 multi-million dollar Webby award winning properties for investment groups, created numerous cutting edge real estate marketing sites, relocated to India, had a baby daughter and just built India’s first broadband multimedia entertainment kiosk network in partnership with Coca-Cola India.

I am considered an expert in Web 2.0 and Multimedia marketing, design and brand building. In the past 15 years I have built digital brands for Tower Records, BT, HMV, Sony Entertainment, IPC, Coca-Cola (UK and India), LifeStageMedia, Coldwell Banker Commercial, INTA, the American India Foundation and many other blue chip companies. I have built Websites, Kiosk Networks, Documentary Films for PBS, Videos, educational CD ROMs and animated short films. My work has earned 2 Webby Honorees, 3 W3 awards, 2 IMA, 1 Adobe SIte of the Day and a Milia D'Or.
