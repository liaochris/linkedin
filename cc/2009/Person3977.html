Allan Metts’s Summary

Technology executive with a hands-on style -- and a proven track record in the design and implementation of complex, high-performing, distributed systems. Experienced in establishing international business operations, reliable IT infrastructure, and feature-rich Internet applications. Industry knowledge includes telecommunications, financial services, and intelligent transportation systems.

Allan Metts’s Specialties:
