Terry G. Plank’s Summary

Having performed over 2,000 marriage ceremonies, practiced as a licensed Family Therapist, served as a Pastor and performed as a stage actor, singer, and director, I bring experience, maturity, and versatility to wedding planning and the ceremony day.

I encourage an atmosphere of relaxation and humor and include only those ideas a couple wants in their wedding ceremony. It's their day and it needs to be a direct expression of the bride and groom. My goal is to have a warm and unique experience for the couple and their guests..

Some wedding officiants bring a religious perspective to your wedding ceremony. Being a Secular Humanist I don't bring religious content into the ceremony, but I'm happy to include anything from a couples tradition in the ceremony at their request.

Terry G. Plank’s Specialties:

Secular wedding ceremonies and celebrations are my specialty, but I respect and will include the anything from the religious traditions of a couple if they want them.
