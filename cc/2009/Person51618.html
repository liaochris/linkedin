Jennifer Clement’s Summary

As a multi-award-winning advertising art director, Jennifer has always been driven by a passion to create influential design and effective communication. In addition to creating successful campaigns and design projects for clients, Jennifer has also been a long-time study of current art and branding trends and their influence in the market place.

She has wide-ranging experience in many diverse industries: from healthcare, banking and finance, to mall property development and management firms, satellite programming, and ceramic tile. Additionally, Jennifer has helped develop many winning political campaigns, has worked on non-profit accounts such as the Tulsa Area United Way and City Arts Center, and serves as C3B’s Design Director for ECCOPartners, a government-sponsored community outreach organization promoting and educating the community on environmental concerns.

Before joining C3B, Jennifer created a successful internet-based business—Sage Stitchworks—that revived the lost art form of paper embroidery known as the Victorian motto.

As part of the C3B team, Jennifer serves as Design Director and Project Specialist— providing assistance in graphic design, creative strategy, copywriting, and production management.
