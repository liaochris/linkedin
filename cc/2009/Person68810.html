Mike Thebado’s Summary

Consultant, strategy analyst, team leader, product management professional, program manager and new product development specialist with extensive experience in technology, back office service, and financial product components of solutions for the wealth management industry.

Proven track record of evaluating industry trends, identifying future implications, eliciting and articulating client requirements, and defining and implementing successful solutions to capitalize on opportunities.

Mike Thebado’s Specialties:

Business requirements identification and communication, alternatives evaluation and prioritization, trends research and analysis, new products development and launch, focus and user group organization and leadership,

Client reporting, investment performance measurement, managed accounts, asset allocation, global investments, securities settlement, asset pricing, trust accounting, asset-backed securities.
