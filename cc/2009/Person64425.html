Wayne Schulz’s Summary

I am a (non-practicing) CPA who provides advanced consulting services on Sage MAS 90 and MAS 200 ERP Accounting Software.

Schulz Consulting offers primarily advanced technical support (100% of this is through prepaid annual support agreements) to users of MAS90 or MAS200 (and related add-ons).

These services may be for new or existing users of the systems. My clients range in size from startups to $ 500 million.

About 25% of my time is spent performing systems analysis and looking at a company's existing procedures so that I can make recommendations on improvement.

In my spare time I have authored technology articles for Journal of Accountancy, Accounting Today, Accounting Technology and the Connecticut Society of CPAS.

I am Senior Editor and write seven days a week for Geardiary.com which is an online ,Technorati 5K rated,technology blog.
