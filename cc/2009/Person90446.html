Christian Senn’s Summary

Seventeen years of professional experience resulting in over nine-hundred million dollars in sales for companies like Sega®, Activision®, and Pixologic®. Over sixteen titles and thirty-six products shipped including original and licensed product lines such as Spiderman®, Call of Duty®, Shrek®, True Crime®, Sonic the Hedgehog®, and ZBrush™. Career dedicated to video game design, 2D/3D art and animation, and web design for PC, console, and arcade game systems.

Christian Senn’s Specialties:

Design (game, mechanics, level, systems, user interface), creative writing, character/logo art (design, 2D/3D art/animation), pre-visualization, and creative/production management.
