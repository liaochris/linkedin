Margo Johnson’s Summary

Margo Johnson (Purrington)

Award-winning agency creative director and brand marketing specialist with MFA who combines business, design & technology to deliver profitable results. Experience with top tier brands including CBS, Microsoft, Apple, Sun, Vistaprint, American Airlines, Wal-Mart, GM and Orbitz.

National awards & work published in The Wall Street Journal, LA Times, Metropolis, The One Show, Emigre and many books. Served on board of directors for the American Center for Design, colleges and community non-profits. Held adjunct faculty positions at USC and other colleges.
