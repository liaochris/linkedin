Don Rudisuhle CFE’s Summary

Currently pursuing consulting assignments to provide services to organizations requiring strengthening of internal controls and financial reporting as well as effective measures to prevent or investigate instances of fraud and waste of valuable resources.

Don Rudisuhle was born in Honolulu, Hawaii. At age 5, his family moved to Mexico City, Mexico, where he received his early schooling. Later, he obtained his undergraduate and graduate degrees in the US. He has also studied at the University of Paris and the Institut d'Etudes Politiques in France and the Dante Alighieri Institute in Italy. Mr. Rudisuhle speaks fluent Spanish, French, Italian and Portuguese and has considerable experience conducting business in foreign languages. He resides in Laramie, Wyoming where he enjoys sailing, cross-country skiing, ice skating, tennis, horseback riding and mountain biking.

Don Rudisuhle CFE’s Specialties:

Audit and internal control, fraud investigation, budgeting, public finance, infrastructure planning, contingent liability management, endowment fund management, program evaluation, health care finance, pharmaceutical manufacturing and distribution, database design, data collection and validation, human resource administration, trial exhibit preparation and courtroom expert witness testimony. Strong and effective verbal and written communication skills and interviewing abilities.
