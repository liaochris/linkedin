Rick Mahn’s Summary

With 20 years of professional experience in the Information Technology and Communications industries, Rick has learned the art of Personal Brand through his own career. Now a writer as well as an IT Infrastructure Architect, Rick is bringing New Media concepts to traditional IT consulting. Currently Rick owns his own technology consulting firm, Avatar Consultants, where he works with small and large sized businesses to prepare infrastructure for continuous growth. A self-described technology evangelist, Rick also writes about Personal Branding, Corporate Blogging, and Mobile Lifestyle on a number of websites. Recognized as a thought leader in Personal Branding, Rick has been featured in Fast Company & The Wall Street Journal, and is a regular columnist for Personal Branding Magazine.

Social Media Entrepreneur, Online Community Developer & Evangelist, Personal Branding Specialist, Blog Consulting, Project & Team Leadership, IT Project Management, Windows IT Architecture & Systems Design, Disaster Recovery & Business Continuity Planning & Execution, Systems & Operations Management, Non Production Environment (Test Lab) Management, Remote Access Technologies, Mobile Communication Technologies, Automated Client Provisioning, Systems & Software Implementation Planning & Execution
