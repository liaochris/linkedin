Billie Hawkins’s Summary

Service business customer by monitoring, testing, installing, activating and troubleshooting their products and services 24X7. The services range from internet, frame relay and ATM to DS0, DS1 and DS3 private line data services as well as long distance voice services. Troubleshooting consist of layer 1-2 issues. Responsible for service related customer contact during specified business hours. Communicate to customers in a professional and helpful manner

ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS:

• Troubleshoot customer related circuit problems relating to frame relay,
ATM, Internet, private line, dedicated long distance/toll free services
• Responsible for customer contact after hours and weekends
• Monitor customer and network segments 24X7, creating trouble tickets and
begin troubleshooting as necessary
• Support Data Center customer needs
• Perform troubleshooting, installation, activation of various physical access
mediums such as DSO, T-1’s, DSL and others.
• Monitor and troubleshoot network switching hardware
• Provide after hours support for other Norlight business units
• Work live hot-cuts on customer Move Add or Change requests to
their products and services
• Escalate maintenance related trouble tickets to appropriate vendor/partner
until resolved

JOB QUALIFICATIONS AND REQUIREMENTS:


Education/Training/Experience:
• 2-5 years direct telecommunications industry or networking experience
• Ability to troubleshoot and isolate circuit level problems
• Experience with CPE such as: Routers, CSU/DSU’s, and Firewalls
• Experience installing and maintaining IP packet networks, frame relay,
ATM and switched long distance services
