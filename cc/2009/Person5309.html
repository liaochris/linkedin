Andreas Umland’s Summary

I study the post-Soviet extreme right at the Eichstaett Institute for Central and East European Studies (ZIMOS) in Upper Bavaria, focusing on current Russian political and intellectual history.

Most of my publications are available here: http://ku-eichstaett.academia.edu/AndreasUmland/Papers and http://digg.com/users/Umland/history/submissions/ . My books are listed here: http://ku-eichstaett.academia.edu/AndreasUmland/Books .

I would be glad to hear from people with similar research interests, and from those wanting to publish their dissertations, monographs, edited volumes or document collections in the book series "Soviet and Post-Soviet Politics and Society" where 85 volumes were published in 2004-2008 (www.ibidem-verlag.de/spps.html ).

ZIMOS's web journal "Forum noveishei vostochnoevropeiskoi istorii i kul'tury" (www1.ku-eichstaett.de/ZIMOS/forumruss.html ) invites Russian-language scholarly papers in the fields of contemporary East European history, post-Soviet politics, and Eurasian cultural studies.

If interested, please, do not hesitate to join the groups "Russian Nationalism" (links lists, papers, listserv & newsletter) at groups.yahoo.com/group/russian_nationalism/ , "Researchers of Russian Nationalism" (networking, updates & discussion) at www.linkedin.com/groups?about=&gid=157897 , and "Researchers of Historical and Neo-Fascism" (networking, updates & discussion) at www.linkedin.com/e/vgh/1640147/ as well as at www.facebook.com/group.php?gid=52910686741.

If you use AmazonConnect, I would be glad to add you to the "Interesting People" or "Friends" sections at www.amazon.com/gp/pdp/profile/ABDE6L94T5H8R/.

If you have taught in the former Soviet bloc, I would be interested in comments, additions and corrections concerning this guide www.amazon.com/gp/richpub/syltguides/fullview/REKTGK0DL1N8F/ .

For networking, I also use these sites: www.facebook.com/people/Andreas-Umland/1037655458 and ku-eichstaett.academia.edu/AndreasUmland .

Andreas Umland’s Specialties:

TEACHING EXPERIENCE:
Late Tsarist, Soviet and post-Soviet history; post-communist transitions; Russian politics; ultra-nationalism in today Russia; rise and fall of fascism; Nazism in comparative perspective; inter-war German ultra-conservatism; contemporary European right-wing radicalism; history and problems of European integration; EU institutions; introduction to the methodology of social science; macrohistorical comparison; political concepts and terminology.
