Noah D. Roth’s Summary

Below is my most recent Corporate Bio.

"Noah is the Managing Director of Hawkes Peers & Co., an Executive Search & Alliances boutique that specializes placing top talent with experience from the elite management consulting firmst. Under Noah’s term as Chief Executive, HPC has established clients which include large corporations, high-growth companies, and Venture Capital / private equity firms, while remaining committed to delivering added value with every placement.

Noah's past roles include Vice President of Business Development at Conduit, where he was responsible for driving Conduit's growth, and managing the day to day operations of the business development team. He oversaw the expansion of the user-base through strategic partnerships for content, distribution, and publisher acquisition.

Prior to Conduit, he served as VP of Global Sales; Business Process Outsourcing at Fortune 1000 corporation IDT Global, where he forged deals with AOL, American Greetings, and Aon Consulting.

As VP of National Sales for University Sports Publications, Noah developed sales techniques which earned $130 million in annual sales to Fortune 500 brands like Hoover, Boeing, BestBuy, GenDyn, Sears and OfficeMax. In his career Noah has built 4 successful P&L units totaling nearly $1 Billion USD in total revenue to date.

Noah is also the CEO/Founder of Sales Acceleration Consulting Group, a sole proprietorship.

Noah holds an MBA in International Marketing Management from the Zicklin School of Business at Baruch College, City University of New York, and earned a B.Sc. degree in Marketing Management from the Sy Syms School of Business at Yeshiva University.

Noah resides in Bet Shemesh, Israel with his wife Ilana, and his three children, Rena, Gavriel, and Yehonatan."
