Dexter Bryant Jr’s Summary

I am a dance/rock producer and electro-urban singer/songwriter. My signature style is Nintendo-punk rock.

My expertise areas are commercial music production and music publishing. For the past 6 years I have been a producer, songwriter, and vocalist for indie urban music labels and hip hop collectives in my home state of Florida.

Currently I am the primary record producer and songwriter of DbryJ Music Media Group LLC, my music publishing and multimedia music production firm.

DbryJ Music Media Group provides music production, songwriting, sonic branding, marketing consulting, and music business consulting services. We specialize in digital media marketing and niche market song plugging.

My reputation with peers and supporters is one of an inventive and energetic music producer and performer. The passion, energy, truth, and originality that I exhibit in my music are qualities that attract people to me.

I am a fearless leader committed to a lifetime of learning and of helping others. My goals are to educate, empower, and inspire others to reach for the stars.

Life Missions:

1) Provide family counseling, education, and material/financial support to families in under-privileged communities around the globe.
2) Give every audience an unforgettable live music experience.
3) Be a pioneer electronic dance/rock producer and encourage international cross-cultural unity by promoting Peace+Love+Music.
