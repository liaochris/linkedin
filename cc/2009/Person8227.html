Ashish Kolvalker’s Summary

Currently employed at Hewitt Associates in the Talent & Organization Consulting practice, Ashish is an alumnus of MDI Gurgaon, one of the premiere B-schools in India.

He finds interest in areas like Talent Management, different forms of Behavioural Assessment including Psychometric Testing, Competency Frameworks, OD Interventions like Team Effectiveness and Leadership Development, Employee Engagement, Assessment & Development Centres etc. He is well-versed with several Psychometric tools and instruments used in the corporate world. He is also passionate about assessing people through Interviews.

He has worked on several academic as well as live industry projects in the areas of Talent Management, Compensation & Benefits Management, Competency Development, T&D, Human Resource Information Systems, Leadership etc.

Ashish has had varied experience in the Consulting domain, having worked with Oracle, Stratandops & now Hewitt. He looks forward to being a successful HR Consultant and a leading HR professional in the country.
