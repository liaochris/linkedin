Peggy Duffy’s Summary

Peggy Duffy is full-time real estate agent who has been in the business since 2002. In addition to sharing responsibility for the Colonels Ridge and Pleasant Valley Road office condominium projects, Peggy's list of clients include business owners and principals in need of retail, restaurant, office and warehouse/flex space. She also works with clients to market properties for sale and for lease.

Peggy managed the sale and leasing of the retail space for the Piedmont Commercial Center project in Gainesville (fully leased and 90% sold) and Colonels Ridge Phase IV (50% sold out). Having started her real estate career in the residential market, she understands customer service. She is not your typical commercial agent. In addition to being easily accessible and knowledgeable, she understands the demands of running a business and makes herself available to clients to accommodate their hectic schedules.

Peggy Duffy’s Specialties:

Retail and restaurant leasing, office leasing, commercial sales and leasing including office, retail, restaurant and flex/warehouse space, as well as land procurement, sales and ground leases.
