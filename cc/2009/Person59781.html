Liz Strauss’s Summary

Businesses fail for two reasons: they grow too fast or they grow too slow.

In any economy, business is strategy and celebrating the people who contribute. I work with business to build and execute strategic plans that deeply connect with customers on five distinct levels.

Focus and Identity;
Online / Offline Strategic Networks and Partnerships;
Digital, International, Generational Realities;
Product Strategy, Life Cycles, and Legacies;
Execution, Tracking, and Performance Goals

I founded SOBCon, an elite social media / online business conference and work with businesses, universities, and service professionals on strategic growth through networked relationships, communication, and performance.

My eBook "How to Write a Successful and Outstanding Blog, An Insider's Guide to the Conversation that's Changing the World," describes how to hear what's happening online and how to write to create spaces that invite people to participate.


Worth noting:
On the board of Invincebelle.com,
Web Publisher of Successful-Blog.com, LizStrauss.com, and one other. Contributing writer at TheBlogHerald.com, TheBloggingTimes.com, Performancing.com and others

Established core strategies and nationally-recognized brands based on consistent quality, customer-centered experience, and appealing design.

Developed business models of continuing 20+% growth. (707% in six years, 132% in three years). Net EBITDA ranged 35–45%, with IRRs at 65%–145%.
