Beer Bergman’s Summary

As manager of beerbergman.com (Communication Agency, 1996-2008 / conferences and training 2008-now) and of Quixys France Internet Services (since 2006), I have been working in the field of communication and Internet since 1996.

Quixys France delivers tri-lingual Web site hosting and Domain name registration services, realization of web sites with our content management system Quixys CMS and Web 2.0 consultancy for clients all over the world.
By using specialized and highly trusted partners we offer a wide range of products, such as development and hosting of ColdFusion web sites and development of ColdFusion applications.
To complement services, Quixys now offers Website Audits, Workshops and Consultancy services.

Quixys Internet Services hosts Linux/PHP, Windows(/ASP) and ColdFusion web sites and applications.
Since our servers are part of a VMWare cluster, downtime to hardware failure belongs to the past. From our data center facility in Amsterdam we are directly connected to the largest internet exchange in the world, thus offering excellent bandwidth.

Above all, we are proud of our services and we believe that our added value lies within the personal contact we have with our customers.

If teaching fine arts has taught me a great deal about perception and treatment of visual information, years of designing Web sites and printed matter have taught me to achieve an extensive know-how in realization of products that accomplish the client’s objectives.

Fields of interest and know-how are tourism, arts ("patrimoine"), education and administration (local, departemental and regional).
Inter-cultural matters (France - The Netherlands) have my special attention.

My personal goals are to develop consultancy in communication, enabling clients to render both the human and the communication parts complementary to the techniques of the Internet, and to make this knowledge available to clients who feel lost in the field of Web 2.0, "bientôt" Web 3.0 …
