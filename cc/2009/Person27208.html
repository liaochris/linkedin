Don Campbell, PMP’s Summary

Don Campbell is an Information Systems professional with solution planning, delivery, project and process experience in large, mid-sized, and small companies. He has a strong foundation in project planning, administration and management; team leadership, facilitation and coaching; delivery management; business process re-engineering; client relationship management; business and process analysis and requirements planning, definition and prioritization; and technology solution implementation. His disciplined approach to project management facilitates the alignment of solutions with business goals and objectives, and focuses on the value of best practices, understanding business requirements, planning, analysis, communication, teamwork, collaboration and accountability.
