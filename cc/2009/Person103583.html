Vincent Patton’s Summary

Over 30 years of military service in the US Coast Guard, and active involvement on a number of military-related nonprofit service organizations, government-appointed positions; "on-air" commentator and consultant to TV, radio and documentary production organizations. Major expertise in military transitioning, education and career opportunities, support for military families and veterans.
