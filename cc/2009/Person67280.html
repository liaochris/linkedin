Michael D. Scott’s Summary

Currently professor of law; previously attorney in private practice and corporate general counsel; specializes in intellectual property, computer, Internet and e-commerce law. Continue to write and update my books on IT, multimedia and outsourcing law.

Michael D. Scott’s Specialties:
