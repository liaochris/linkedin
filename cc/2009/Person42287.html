Jackie McClure’s Summary

As president of JCI Design, Jackie leads a team of very talent communicators who use their artistic skills to create UNEXPECTED marketing communication tools for our clients.

JCI Design is known for strategic thinking, superior customers service and creative communication solutions that get results are on time and on budget. The tools used varies based on the client, their product or service and their audience(s). JCI is currently creating opportunities for clients to use social media/networking as part of their communication plan.

A life-long Dearborn, Michigan resident, Jackie serves on the Dearborn Chamber of Commerce Board, the Historic Aviation Subdivision Board and IABC-Detroit Board.
