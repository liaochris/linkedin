Akhil Shahani akhil(AT)shahanigroup.com’s Summary

I am the Managing Director of The Shahani Group which has interests in education, venture capital, new media, commodities & non-profits.

Our major group companies consist of:

1) Smart Institute - A global business school that creates Smart People for Smart Companies.

2) Kaizen Global - A Venture Capital Fund investing in India's $50 Billion education sector

3) Smart Entrepreneur Network - Web portal that helps entrepreneurs succeed.

4) East India Paper Company - One of India's oldest & largest paper & paperboard merchants.

In the Non-profit space we have:

1) The Shahani Trust - More than 60 years of charitable work in education, healthcare & low cost housing

2) SAGE Foundation - Provides education and skills training for India's needy.

3) Smart University - International foundation promoting educational reform

4) Unltd India - Supporting start up social entrepreneurs in India
