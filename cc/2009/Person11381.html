Bill Lublin’s Summary

WILLIAM H. LUBLIN is the CEO of CENTURY 21 Advantage Gold which has been recognized by both Real Trends , & Rismedia as one of the largest real estate firms in the U.S. It is currently the only Century 21 firm with offices in both Pennsylvania & New Jersey. He is co-founder of BuzzBuilderz, a Social Media Marketing Company.

Bill has served numerous times as a director of the Pennsylvania Association of Realtors,President of the Northeast Multiple Listing Bureau, President of the Greater Philadelphia Association of REALTORS, and Professional Standards Chairman of the Pennsylvania Association of REALTORS In addition, Bill has been a member of the National Association of REALTORS Professional Standards Committee, and is, in 2008 chairman of that committee. Bill is also an NAR trained Professional Standards Instructor, a trained DRS Mediator, and a Certified Mediator Trainer.

Bill is an Expert in the field of Organizational Real Estate Owned & represents several large national lenders in the sale of their real estate including Fannie Mae, Homesteps (Freddie Mac), & many others. He has trained & spoken Nationally on Foreclosures & Short Sales, leading the Short Sales Working Group for the National Association of REALTORS.

Bill has received numerous awards from professional real estate organizations, including the honor of being inducted into the REALTORS Hall of Fame.

Bill has also been recognized for his participation in Blogging, writing for Agentgenius.com (winner if the 2008 Inman Award for Most Innovative Blog) as well as speaking on Social Media & authoring MovePhilly.blogspot.com, REreflections.com, & C21agVoices.com

Of all of his achievements, Bill is proudest of his family. Bill was happily married for 33 years to his lovely wife Sheila. He works with his son Hal who is President of BuzzBuilders. Hal and his wife Jennifer Marie Kelley also are actively pursuing careers in the entertainment industry and are principals in JHM Entertainment.

Bill Lublin’s Specialties:

Real Estate Management, REO disposition, Real Estate Investing, Real Estate Education,Mergers and Acquisitions, Real Estate Consulting, Expert Witness, Real Estate Training, Certified Mediator Trainer, Social Media Speaker and Consultant, experienced in single and multi-author Blogs, and the use of Web 2.0 marketing strategies.
