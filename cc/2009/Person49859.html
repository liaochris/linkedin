Jonas Bonér’s Summary

I am a pragmatic who is passionate about technology, learning new things and helping others to work better and more effectively.

I am the founder of the AspectWerkz AOP framework and committer to the Terracotta project, the Eclipse AspectJ project and various other Open Source projects.

I enjoy solving challenging problems, coaching (agile thinking and practices), mentoring, speaking (conferences, lectures, trainings etc.) and writing articles.

Find out more about me at: http://jonasboner.com/
