Lewis Green’s Summary

Get A Step Ahead of the Competition

Businesses like yours hire us because we grow your revenues and we promise measurable results that achieve your goals. How? We focus only on services that will grow your customer base and lead to increased sales to your current customers. L&G Business Solutions is a full-service business development, marketing and communications firm, specializing in business plans, marketing strategies, marketing plans, and business management.

We help you create a business plan that works, a marketing plan that sells, business development strategies that grow revenues, marketing consulting without the fluff, and lead generation that is qualified and gets you in the door.

Our team of business consultants serves all of North America. We deliver only those services we do best, at a price customized for you. Our fees are performance-based because we want you to pay for results, not for time.
