Duncan Freeman’s Summary

Duncan Freeman is a technology professional and entrepreneur with substantial experience building, managing and marketing a variety of technologies with an emphasis on Internet based products and services since 1994. With a music background as a former member and manager for two Southeastern based independent bands, Duncan has combined his technology and music experiences in creating his third startup, Band Metrics, a data analytics and decision support system for the music industry. He also blogs @ IndieMusicTech.
