Christopher Mills’s Summary

Writer of comic books, graphic novels, prose fiction, and non-fiction. Currently have three creator-owned comics projects placed with Ape Entertainment and scheduled for publication in 2008. I have also written fiction, comic book scripts or articles for Alpha Productions, Big Entertainment, Tekno•Comix, Shadow House Press, Image Comics, Rorschach Entertainment, CinemaGraphix, IDW and Moonstone Books. My short fiction has been published by Tanagram Publishing, Thrilling Detective and Moonstone Books.

As a graphic artist, I've designed over 300 magazine/tabloid covers, DVD covers for Retromedia and Pop Cinema, Inc., created various logos and lettered comic books for Moonstone Books and other small publishers.
