Trip Young, CSAM’s Summary

After receiving my undergraduate degree from Davidson College and my Masters from Villanova University, I transitioned my professional career from United Way to the Q Works Group. I spent three years with the Q Works Group as a Senior Account Manager, responsible for full cycle recruiting and business development within a cross section of industries.
