Jed Wunderli’s Summary

I have been in the mortgage industry for over 15 years. I have written several articles for the Scotsman Guide and just completed my book: Think BIG, Succeed BIG. Additionally, I have created The Wealthbuilder Financial Analysis System to help loan officers help their clients understand how money works and the principals of wealth creation.

Jed Wunderli’s Specialties:

I look at a client's complete financial picture and then recommend a mortgage that will give them the opportunity to help achieve their financial goals. The software that I developed is my primary tool for performing the analysis.
People who want to learn more about preparing for retirement by using advanced cash-flow management strategies, equity management, leverage and other wealth-creation techniques should visit www.TheWunderliTeam.com or contact me via email.

  • Certified Mortgage Planner

    Indymac Bank

    (Public Company; IMB; Banking industry)

    August 2007 — August 2008 (1 year 1 month)

    The credit market seized in July of 2007 necessitating a move to a bank that I thought was stable enough to weather the storm. With home prices falling rapidly, I had to switch from an equity management model to a Realtor-based purchase model. I implemented the Home Buyers' Scouting Report as an added-value tool for Realtors and their clients and started getting in some doors and slowly building a base of Realtors. Ultimately as I was just gaining some traction, IndyMac was taken over by the FDIC and I had to start over at my next company - Greystone Financial Group.
