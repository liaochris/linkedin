SOEB FATEHI’s Summary

I have spent more than two decades in manufacturing, consulting and training. During that period i have worked extensively in reengineering and application development for all manufacturing industry including metals, cement, chemicals and environmental.

Now my focus is on ergonomics and reverse engineering.

My company also undertakes electrical safety audits for installations and conducts inspection and factory acceptance tests for all electrical and electronic products as a consulting agency.

Training and coaching for quality upgrades and new skills is conducted by my company for clients who run manufacturing industries.
