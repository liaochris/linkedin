Sue Cartledge’s Summary

Over 12 years experience writing and editing on health and science and commercial topics for industry groups, business clients and consumers.

Editor, Hospital&healthcare (now Hospital & Aged Care), Australasia's largest publication for the health and aged care industries for five years.

Contract Sub-editor at Key Media, Sydney business and financial publishers, for 18 months.

Contract sub-editor at The Irish Echo, Sydney community newspaper, for 18 months.

Regular contributor to Hospital&healthcare (http://www.yaffa.com.au/mags/hahmag.htm) and The Veterinarian (http://www.theveterinarian.com.au/) magazines, and health and science contributor to Suite101.com (http://www.suite101.com).

Occasional contributor to Australian Farm Journal, Climate Control News, Food Service News, Nature and Health, Rag Trader, Toy and Hobby Retailer
