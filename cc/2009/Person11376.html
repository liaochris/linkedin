Bill Kutik’s Summary

Leading independent market expert on vendors and their products and services in all forms of technology used for HR. Have organized and moderated 78 panels on this subject in since 1995. run the world's largest conference and trade show on the subject since 1998; and now host The Bill Kutik Radio Show(sm). Narrow but deep expertise and historical perspective.

Bill Kutik’s Specialties:

Specialist in Recruiting, HCM, Outsourcing, Talent Management Suite, Web 2.0
