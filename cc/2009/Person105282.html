Wouter Van den Berghe’s Summary

Experienced senior consultant with 2 Masters degrees in Engineering and over 25 years of professional experience. Specialised in analysing complex situations and providing strategic advice and training. Experience in a broad range of management and organisational issues, both in the profit and non-profit sector. Excellent oral and written communication skills. Author of over 20 professional books and reports in several languages. Trilingual English-Dutch-French with good knowledge of Germen and elementary understanding of Spanish.
Major clients so far are: European Commission, Vlerick Management School, Cedefop, European Training Foundation, PROSE, Flemish Ministry of Education, Afinia Plastics, Higher Education Institutions, Red Cross, Tourism Flanders, Belgocontrol, Deloitte, Ja-Ye Europe, European Schoolnet, King Baudouin Foundation, ...
