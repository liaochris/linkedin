Valery Hronusov’s Summary

I graduated from Politechnic institute, my M.S. specialization is machine's dynamics and durability. After graduation I followed to work on the chair of mechanics of composites. This is: durability calculations, FEM, stochastic processes, math modeling.

Since 1992 to 1996 I was working at 2-3 works at the same time.
1. Geomechanics (I have defended the PhD)
2. Cryogenic technology
3. Elaboration software. (Both individual and in a team). Directions - durability calculations, ensuring systems data acceptance and calculations (power engineering), accounting. First realtime software development. FEM, work with relation databases, elaboration interfaces, low-level programming.

Since 1996 to 2000 I was working in the scientific part of projects institute. Specialization – geomechanics calculations. CAD systems.

2000 – Beginning of the work with GIS. Leader of contractual works. Client-server applications (MS SQL+Access+Visio) for geological service adventures – these programs are really working nowadays. Another specialization – ArcGIS. Numerical micrographics, morphometry analysis of elements. The first group was created.

2004. Academic institute of Mines. Visualization of geodates, GIS researches. Leading elaboration software major components. Lead a site, work with its design, and go on interesting with applied mathematics. Nowadays I do not write program code. The main part of my work is target settings, testing and deliberation of targets, which are curetted with these programs. I try to achieve optimal combination between result and labors-output ratio.

Experience jobs with space photos of Landsat with SRTM, Digital globe ( I before buy and use data of Quick Bird satellite for Oil&Gas deposits research).

Experience of jobs with datamining. We create and support now the large geodatabase.

For now i (my group) use SVN, Trac for development
Also we use: Amazon EC2/S3 , Google App Engine, Django, Cloud services.
