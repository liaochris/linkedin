Patrick Antrim, Jr.’s Summary

Mr. Antrim has experience in high-density multi-family development, property management, financial reporting, budgeting, and P&L management. Prior to leading PEAK Real Estate Management Group, he was Senior Vice President of Property & Asset Management for CORE Realty Holdings. While at CORE, Mr. Antrim managed over 1.1 Billion in Assets across 13 states reaching annual revenues of 80 Million. His portfolio was comprised of 7,500 Multi-family properties and approximately 4.5 Million Sq. Feet of Commercial, Office, Retail, and Industrial properties. Mr. Antrim developed the in house property management Company for the CORE Board of Directors.

Prior to CORE, he was a Director with Mesa Real Estate Management Co. Mr. Antrim’s primary responsibilities were to set and articulate the direction of the Corporation, ensuring that the management practices and approaches were up to date, and in line with industry standards. He customized market-driven strategies and implemented plans for positioning and marketing of new developments as well as value added opportunities.

Mr. Antrim served the Arnel Management Company before joining Mesa managing a multi-family portfolio for the former Ambassador of Spain and Real Estate Developer, George Argyros. Arnel has been involved in the development, construction and management of well over 5,000 apartment units, in addition to several million square feet of commercial and industrial property.

Prior to his experience in the real estate industry, Mr. Antrim was a professional baseball player in the New York Yankees organization from 1995 to 1998. As a professional athlete with the New York Yankees, Mr. Antrim’s experience transcended popularity, visibility and resources into areas far greater than athletic endeavors.

Mr. Antrim was inducted into the Saddleback College’s Collegiate Athletic Hall of Fame in 1999. Antrim studied Organizational Leadership at Pacific Christian College in Fullerton, CA.

Patrick Antrim, Jr.’s Specialties:

Antrim is a Candidate for (CCIM) Certified Commercial Investment Member, and a candidate for (CPM) Certified Property Manager with the Institute of Real Estate Management.
