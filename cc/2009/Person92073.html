Silvia Pfeiffer’s Summary

Co-found and business leader for a online video metrics company. Technical involvment in design and development of metrics product. Taking company to profitability. Writes a frequent video metrics blog.

Former research scientist with experience in developing new applications for digital networked media since 1996. Invented the Annodex (http://www.annodex.net) technology, which extends the World Wide Web to digital media by reducing the complexity of binary media data to the text level, which allows searching, hyperlinking, and composition as though they were html pages. Researched into compressed domain audio analysis (MPEG Maaate). Contributed to MPEG-7. Was part of the MoCA movie content analysis project at Mannheim University, Germany.

Silvia Pfeiffer’s Specialties:

Annodex, automated audio-visual content analysis, R&D into new applications for new media
