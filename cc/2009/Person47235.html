Jill Maltezos’s Summary

I am a freelance makeup artist, specializing in special event, print and film/video and high-def TV makeup application. In addition to my freelance business, I have successfully opened and operated, since September 2006, the only state-certified vocational art school now existing in the midwest dedicated to the training of makeup artists. The School is located in the heart of downtown's loop in Chicago, and has received attention from New York, Los Angeles as an outstanding program.

Jill Maltezos’s Specialties:

Print, film/video makeup for both the corporate as well as the fashion industry; high-definition makeup for a comedy series as well as for local and visiting celebrity appearances on TV, as well as special events, such as weddings, trade shows, music concerts. Trained in airbrush makeup application. Teacher of Media, Advanced Media, Camouflave and Airbrush and Bridal Makeup at Make Up First School for Makeup Artistry. www.makeupfirstschool.com
