Successfully debugged / maintained third party (proprietary) and open source codes, which resulted in the prompt market release of the product.

Served as one of the clients' junior-level specialists, design reviewer and code reviewer for modules that mainly involve networking protocols.

Served efficiently in the effective bridging of off-shored projects which resulted in successes and high praises from the client.
