Tiffany Lyman Otten’s Summary

I work specifically on helping newer markets for GlobalSpec break into or enhance their marketing to the industrial, manufacturing, engineering, and technical space. This experience has helped me generate very specific CRM strategies and experience, ad copy "best practices", copywriting, and general internet marketing experience.

My "attention to detail" is known, almost to the point of being comical, as quite astute. I use this thoroughness to ensure that my clients get the exact program they need from me, and the results that they are best capable of getting. I enjoy helping smaller companies enter the ring, and Fortune 100 companies gain market share, along with everyone in the middle.

I can be quite helpful in the above areas, and am always looking to learn from my peers and share what I've learned, but have limited experience in print and do not work with TV media/upfront buys.
