Ianik Marcil’s Summary

I'm trained in Economics and Political Sciences. Both in my academic and professional activities, I had the occasion to present several speaches and seminars in technological innovations, economic transformations and business management. For many years, I taught ant the Institute of applied economics at HEC-Montreal as well as at the International Institute of Information as a Visiting professor in 2007.

As consultant, I carried out during the last twelve years more than one hundred of sectoral and market studies. I worked in particular in the high technologies, industrial sectors, and social economy. I am Vice-president, Corporate affairs and Senior economist at Intelegia, a company specialized in formation and business coaching, subsidiary of E&B Data, specialized in economic development research.
