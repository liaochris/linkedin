Greg Frucci’s Summary

Registered Architect with over 20 years of experience in design through construction administration. Using my experience as a part-time professional actor, my strength in sales and marketing have increased dramatically.

PROFILE
Creative, motivated, innovative. Dedicated to delivering profits to all involved in my film projects. Professional film producer since 2006. Professional actor since 2005. Honor graduate from the College of Fine Arts in Architecture from Carnegie Mellon University in 1984.
