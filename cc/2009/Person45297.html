Jeff Coon’s Summary

I enjoy working with first time buyers, it's very rewarding helping someone purchase their first home. We specialize in mortgages to purchase a home, or to consolidate debt & reduce payments. We offer programs with high loan to value ratios, with NO PMI for qualified applicants. I can help improve credit scores. We allow for higher debt ratios on many products. As a Direct Lender, we offer FHA, VA, USDA, Fannie Mae & Freddie Mac loan programs.

In my office, we like to work directly with local real estate agents. We provide expert advice & service to both our referral partners & their clients. I stay up to date with the ever changing market & provide creative solutions to helping people obtain a loan that is well suited for them.

Our NJ office is licensed in NJ, FL, NY, CA, AK, GA, HI, IN, MI, MS,NM, ND. We can also originate loans in any other state through our corporate office.

If you know someone in need of a home loan, please have them contact me.
