Anthony Coppola’s Summary

Sixteen years of Software Quality Engineering, software test, SQA with most of the time spent in Project leadership role. Areas of specialty include software for video and audio editing, ingest, playback, shared storage, 3rd-party applications, and networking. Strong focus on Apple Macintosh, OS X, Avid Media Composer, Apple Final Cut Pro.

Project leader and technical lead on Macintosh products for 11 years at Avid Technology and 5 years at Digital Equipment Corp.

Anthony Coppola’s Specialties:

Apple Macintosh software testing, usability testing, troubleshooting, mentoring, customer training, support, project leadership.
