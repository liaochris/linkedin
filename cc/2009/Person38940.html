Harvey Mover’s Summary

Harvey has over eighteen years of Information Technology industry experience, and extensive success using his engineering background to drive down a client costs while solving their business problems and issues. As a Certified Software Asset Manager, he has used his knowledge to save clients over $7M in software volume licensing, utilizing terms and conditions of agreements, contracts and negotiation techniques favorable to each client. He has a unique ability to understand a clients business needs, and find ways to employ agreements to their advantage and has a proven track record of success. He maintains over a dozen manufacture certifications for security, storage, virtualization, network management and asset management and has been a contributor to a variety of publications and newsletters.

Harvey is married to Debra and is a father of Mia and spends his free time with his family. He also has donated his time to assist numerous charitable causes. He is founder of the Loop for Life charity event (CEO & President) and is responsible for raising more than $1.5M. He also has been active on several boards of directors or acts as an adviser and consultant to many charitable organizations and events. He also plays the saxophone, is a jazz historian and has been a competitive power lifter into his 40's even bench pressing over 500lbs.
