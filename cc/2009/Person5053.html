Anders Hoff’s Summary

I work with user experience at Netlife Research, Oslo, Norway. My focus is within interaction design, strategy and usability for web, software and devices. I often utilize my technical skills (for prototypes and deployment), but my main focus is within functional design.

Tasks include strategy/planning, prototyping/mockup/sketching, accessibility, user testing, teaching/courses, writing articles/blog posts and more.

I completed a Masters/Graduate degree in IT Product Design in Sønderborg - University of Southern Denmark. The course focused on three areas of design: User Centred Design, Interaction Design and Design Research. I wrote my Masters thesis in collaboration with FAST Search & Transfer, a world leader in enterprise search.

Anders Hoff’s Specialties:

search, search design, search concept, search engine optimization,
accessibility, interaction design, user-centered design, usability, participatory design,
html/css/javascript, user experience, user interface design, user testing
