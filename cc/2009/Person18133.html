Christopher J. Petroff’s Summary

During the past six years, Chris Petroff has played an instrumental role in the Valley of The Sun's real estate market. During Petroff’s time in residential development and luxury home brokerage, he was responsible for selling and managing nearly $100 Million in new home and luxury home inventories. During his tenure Petroff operated under the Pulte Homes/Del Webb & Shea Homes umbrellas in residential development and Russ Lyon Sotheby’s for Luxury Residential brokerage.

In early 2008, Austin Commercial hired Petroff as the Western Region Marketing and Business Development Manager. His knowledge of the Valley’s real estate development market and tenacious passion for building lasting relationships were a great match for he and Austin Commercial. His ability to grow Austin Commercial’s market share and presence in the Western Region has assisted in the strategic pursuit of new business in the Private and Public markets. Petroff has drastically enhanced Austin Commercial’s presence among Private Developers, Architects, Real Estate Attorneys, and Public Officials.
