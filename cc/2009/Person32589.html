Ann Rice Banno’s Summary

A Gartner Senior Vice President coined the term for Ms. Banno's ability to discern the best candidates for clients’ requirements and zero in until they were on board: PERSONAL RADAR ®.

Ms. Banno is a 15-year talent acquisition professional with a demonstrated high degree of integrity and credibility with all levels of customers. Her search career is unique because of her extensive technology background and acumen, proving her to be an unusual "quick-study" regardless of client technologies or industry. Most recent 10 years as hands-on working President of boutique retained executive search firm, managing two staff, personally winning business away from incumbent, top-ten, multinational search firms with over $250 million in billings and 60+ offices worldwide. Prior technology firm success at two major Fortune 500 computer/services firm marketing to clients across many industries (U.S. Surgical, Smith Barney, Dean Witter, Sikorsky, Norden). Member, SHRM (Society for Human Resources Management)

Ann Rice Banno’s Specialties:

Full lifecycle search, research, talent strategy and acquisition in High Technology across its own and many other industries, including Healthcare/Managed Care/Informatics, medical equipment and supplies manufacturing, management, scientific and technical consulting services, software/hardware, and more. Recruiting executive suite and high-level candidates not limited to CIO/CTO but also all roles from research & development to sales, marketing and support, for clients from startups to F500.
