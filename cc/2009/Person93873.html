Rebecca Blakiston’s Summary

Working in various positions at the University of Arizona Library since 2003, I obtained my MLS in December 2007 and the following January became an Assistant Librarian with the Undergraduate Services Team. I enjoy working in the areas of reference, outreach, and instruction. I have a passion for promoting equity of access, freedom of information, and information literacy. I love helping students, faculty, and the community connect with information and always enjoy taking on new challenges.

Rebecca Blakiston’s Specialties:

reference, environmental scanning, outreach, marketing, training, policy-making, learning environments, public services, online content creation, instruction and instructional design
