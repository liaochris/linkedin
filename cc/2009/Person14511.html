Joe Quinn’s Experience

  • Regional Sales Manager

    Enercept, Inc.

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Building Materials industry)

    February 2008 — Present (1 year 10 months)

    Enercept is one of the world's leading manufacturers of structural insulated panels, known in the building industry as SIPs. Enercept SIPs, similiar in design to "tilt-up" panels, are composed of a solid layer of EPS (expanded polystyrene insulation) laminated between OSB (oriented strand board) to form an exterior framing system of superb energy efficiency and strength.

    Engineered studies and performance tests have shown SIPs to be stronger, quieter, safer, faster to construct, and 50% more energy efficient than traditional methods of construction.
