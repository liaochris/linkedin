Destry Wion’s Summary

9+ years in user experience design across all sectors. I've held positions as web manager, project manager, ergonomics specialist, and webmaster and owned my own business as a web designer. In the course of this work, I have provided services in content strategy, information architecture, user-research/user-centered design, interaction design, and web standards development. My principle interests are in user-centered design and content strategy.
