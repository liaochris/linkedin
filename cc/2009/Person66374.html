Meg Maker’s Summary

Meg Houston Maker is the Director of External Information Services for Dartmouth College development, where she oversees web strategy for fundraising. At Dartmouth, she has designed the interactive marketing programs for both annual fund and campaign priorities, helping the institution achieve its first-place national standing for online fundraising.

Maker has 19 years’ experience in software design and interactive product management, having served clients including CNN, National Geographic, The Image Bank, ABC News, and the Associated Press. She is a frequent lecturer on user-centered design and the effective use of technology in online communications. She writes about experience design and digital culture at www.engagingexperience.com.

A summa cum laude graduate of Dartmouth College, Maker is currently pursuing her master’s degree in creative writing at her alma mater.

Meg Maker’s Specialties:

E-communications strategy, e-commerce design, online customer experience design, electronic marketing, and social media marketing.
