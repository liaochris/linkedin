Erik Ojakaar’s Summary

Seasoned user experience and research professional with a solid track record of successfully integrating user centered design practices into the product development process while helping businesses identify strategic opportunities. I have a passion for translating consumer needs and business requirements into innovative solutions. Published author with a Masters in Human Factors in Information Design.

Erik Ojakaar’s Specialties:

leading research projects, facilitating ideation/innovation workshops, participatory design, ethnographic field research, usability studies, competitive analysis, survey design, I have a broad range of skills in qualitative and quantitative methodologies.

Recognized ecommerce expert.
