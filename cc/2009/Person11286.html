Bill Davis’s Summary

I am a computer programmer/analyst with over 25 years of experience (my first software job was writing educational software for my high school math teacher during the summer while I was still in high school in 1979.) I have programmed everything from mainframes to minis to microcomputers in a variety of languages (C, Pascal, BASIC, COBOL, Java, Ruby, assembler, etc.) on operating systems such as OpenVMS, Mac OS X, Windows, AIX, and more.

I also have experience supporting users, writing user and technical documentation, managing groups of technical support people and doing contract programming.
