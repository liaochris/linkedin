Ms. Erin Nicole Johnson’s Summary

My broad range of interests has led me to diverse experiences. Trained as a professional photographer, I have worked as a freelance photographer for over four years, photographing events, portraits, destinations, and my own personal work that concerns personal storytelling.

Most recently, I was granted an internship at my alma mater in the Communications department. As the sole photographer on staff, my responsibilities included not only creation of photography, but the subsequent editing of the photographs and relative copy. In addition, I was involved in each step, from inception to output, in print and web publications.

Simultaneously to my internship, I volunteered at the Park Nicollet Clinic in the Whittier neighborhood of Minneapolis as a Co-Curator. In this role I worked alongside a mentor to establish a gallery within the hospital to aid in the health of the community through art. I was involved with the creation of the mission statement, promotional materials, remodeling of the gallery space, selection of artists, and installation of exhibitions.

I would like to continue to work within the fields of education and non-profits. Currently I am a freelance photographer interested in working with local communities.
