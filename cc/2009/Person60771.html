Luis Luarca’s Summary

Luis Luarca is the Managing Partner and a Management Consultant for Allectus LLC where he is responsible for managing consulting projects via small, mid-cap and large U.S corporations. Allectus LLC supports business in the areas of Operational Accounting, Process Operations, Financial Analysis, Organizational Management/Structures and Technology integration.

Currently pursuing a Ph.D. in AMDS with a concentration in Leadership and Organizational Change, Luis Luarca brings years of relevant finance, accounting and marketing experience. Having worked for and with notable corporations such as Nestle USA, Heinz USA, Starbucks Coffee, Kraft Foods. Luis' hands-on business experience and knowledge is unmatched, securing quantifiable profits for each and everyone one of his clients to date.

Luis does find time to facilitate seminars to small business owners via the City of Los Angeles’ Business Assistance Programs (LABAP), and Barrio Planners Inc. Allectus LLC is also an authorized service provider for the Small Business Administration in Los Angeles. Luis Luarca is bi-lingual and bi-cultual speaking, reading and writing in Spanish as well as English.

Luis Luarca’s Specialties:

Operational Accounting Consulting, Job Costing, Organization and Structure Management, Process Operations Consulting, pricing analysis for profit and marginal contribution, preparation of annual operating plans, reporting package development (KPIs), Functional Requirement Analysis (FRA) as it relates to organizational management, improve cash flow and cash management, development and installation of accounting policies and procedures.
