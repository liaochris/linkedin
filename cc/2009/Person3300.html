Alex Cureton-Griffiths’s Summary

I'm all about helping people connect. I started Shanghai Networking News (http://shanghainn.com) with the aim of helping people make valuable business contacts around Shanghai, and am looking to expand to other cities in China and around the world. We tell people what's on, what's worth going to and offer general networking know-how, especially helping westerners network with Chinese and vice-versa.

We also train people and companies in improving their networking skills through China Networker (http://www.chinanetworker.com) covering everything from building up your confidence to establishing lasting rapport to making the most of your network.

More background:

After living most of my life in England and New Zealand, I spent several years working around the Greater China region, including the Gobi Desert, Taipei, Inner Mongolia and Shanghai.

Before China Networker, I was an advertising salesman for the largest expat website in Shanghai (possibly in China), a corporate trainer and a product consultant for a corporate gift company.

Alex Cureton-Griffiths’s Experience

  • Chatterbox in Chief

    Chatterbox China

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    February 2009 — Present (6 months)

    Chatterbox China is a business unit of AgrIntel marketing, specialising in managed word-of-mouth campaigns. This includes:

    * Product consultation: Making products and services word-of-mouth-friendly to encourage word-of-mouth from existing customers and amplify word-of-mouth from our network
    * Product seeding/word-of-mouth amplification: Sending out product samples to let our network experience it themselves and motivating them to spread the word
    * Word-of-mouth tracking: Online and offline tracking/analytics of who is saying what about clients' products

    Chatterbox's aim is to help companies create and spread honest and natural word-of-mouth about their product or service to the Shanghai (and greater China) market.

    What we do that others don't: We have the know-how and network to implement, manage and track word-of-mouth marketing programs to build client brands, increase customers and generate positive buzz.
