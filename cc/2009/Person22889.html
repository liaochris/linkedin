Dan Keating’s Summary

Co-Owner of Coast Home Staging
www.CoastHomeStaging.com

A full service Home Staging company that provides a variety of Home Staging services as well as furniture and accessories rentals. We help home owners spruce up their homes to sell in the Real Estate Market to allow them to sell as fast as possible and at the highest price. Home Staging has proven that it helps sell homes in any kind of Real Estate Market. It allows buyers to better see the potential of a home and consider purchasing it. We do this by depersonalizing, decluttering, and organizing furniture, layouts, and the "look" to make it look like beautiful. We provide everything from a consultation to a full Home Staging Service. We can rent furniture and accessories to compliment a home owners, or a full vacant home.

Dan Keating’s Specialties:

I am an ASP Accredited Staging Professional (trained by StagedHomes.com) and am also the President of the IAHSP (International Association of Home Staging Professionals) for the Orange County Chapter. I am also running for President-Elect for 2008 for the Los Angeles County Chapter.
