David Sundrum’s Summary

I have worked for 12 years as a print and web designer, and I have been working with visual imagery is some way for most of my life.

My experience as an artist and designer put me in a unique position to explore an incredibly wide-range of visual and interactive possibilities. Visit Sundrum Arts www.sundrumarts.com for more information.
