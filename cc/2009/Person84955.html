Roberto Henry Massaron’s Summary

Since 1993 I have been overseeing ICT in the banking area with great satisfaction both as regards the work in itself and a sincere passion for the intricacies of the job and the results obtained.

I enjoy working within a motivated team in which my role is often that of catalyst and driving force. My aptitude for mediation is also appreciated.

As regards my private sphere I have ameliorated some aspects of my personal development in order to pursue various objectives. This better knowledge of myself also enables me to get a better grasp and comprehension of the situation on hand both as regards construction and management.

I am firmly convinced that a feeling of belonging to the company and a will to make things work within the actual working unit is imperative to pursue the objectives I aim at. I am convinced that this approach affords the best fertilizer for planting one’s technical capabilities and personal motivation. Consequently I do my best to further this state of affairs.

I consider myself determined and well calibrated. Moreover, I have a solid technical and professional background with a large company where I have also gone through various organizational shakeups with participation and a positive outlook. Other characteristics evident in my work are capacity of analysis and the gift of being able to circumscribe and solve any problems which may occur. I am also an attentive listener and am capable of making independent decisions to bring them to their just conclusion, after careful scrutiny of their complexity.
