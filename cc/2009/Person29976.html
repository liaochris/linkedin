Héctor Padilla’s Summary

+ RadicalGolem: Game development for social platforms
+ Lead Game designer for Wii title to be released in summer 2010.
+ Helping to create and grow a game development industry in Mexico.

Academia:
+ I have been the chair of the Digital Arts Department in ITESM (México)
+ TA for two years in an academic project between the UofA and Bioware.
+ Consulting for Video Games program for the UAD
+ Multiple workshops in interactive media and game design

--

My passion is designing engaging interactive experiences. I love prototyping ideas and trying new things. Game design and the psychology of why people play games and how they remain engaged in them is a personal interest.

I greatly enjoy programming/designing and getting my hands dirty to make things happen. Yet more and more life is pulling me towards project and team management and I'm loving it.

---

Over the years I have done studies on traditional art, figure drawing, hand drawn animation, music (percussions), singing and theater. This, among my academic formation in Computer Science has given me a good edge in what comes to Digital Art, Interactive Media and Game Design. :-) I feel like I can talk to everyone in the team and empathize with them. :-)

Héctor Padilla’s Specialties:

Game Design and prototyping (I enjoy prototyping a lot!).
Project management and production (Recently took SCRUM training).

Strong programming skills and logic thinking due to CS education, complemented by good sense of aesthetics and design by artistic formation. Very good inter-personal skills that allow me to communicate with everyone in a game development team.
