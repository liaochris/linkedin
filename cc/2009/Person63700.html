Mark Toohey’s Summary

In addition to acting for clients in the sale or purchase of their businesses, I’ve provided a lot of broad-ranging legal advice on employment contracts, distribution agreements, website development agreements and many other commercial aspects of running a company.

Services provided to ventures raising capital commonly involve: structuring the investment vehicle; incorporating companies; preparing information memorandums; holding investment funds on trust; drafting shareholder agreements and constitutions; engaging directors, executives and staff; as well as overseeing company secretarial compliance and procedures.

The ventures I’ve recently advised operated in the following sectors; solar technology; service industry franchise; ticketing website; new media production; pharmaceutical invention; robotics invention; incorporated legal practice; property development; bio-metric invention; super-fine wool products; medical software; executive recruitment; payroll management software; and website development.

I have worked closely with venture capital companies to develop projects in their investment portfolio and a number of my clients are COMET-grant recipients. I’ve also advised companies raising capital for mining ventures, childcare centres, advertising services, email encryption software, video conferencing and commercial fishing ventures.

My practical experience in website management and design means I revel in the opportunity to work with technology based clients (OK... I admit I am a geek at heart).

My other passion is business development and the drama of corporate strategy and business building. I can really relate to clients who are fast growing SMEs or those doing the hard yards of establishing and building a new business. That's exactly what we're also doing at Adroit Lawyers: we're walking the talk....

Visit my blog: www.accelawrate.com
