Paul Barton, ABC’s Summary

Seasoned communication professional with more than 12 years of experience finding creative, cost-effective solutions to complex communication challenges in a variety of industries. Successful track record of providing vision and counsel to steer organizations through periods of organizational complexity. Recognized leader in planning and implementing communications programs to support large scale, dynamic change initiatives.
