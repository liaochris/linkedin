Marina Garrison MBA PMP’s Summary

Project Management Professional (PMP) and MBA with 11 years experience delivering technology projects and managing technology operations.

With a background in project management and business analysis, her expertise includes leading large software projects for utilities companies (BC Hydro, BC Gas) at Accenture; and introducing project management methodologies including establishing a PMO at an Internet Marketing firm: managing client projects to profitability.

Marina gained her Law Degree LL.B. (Hons) from London Metropolitan University and her Executive MBA from Athabasca University in Canada. She is an active member of the local project management community, training and mentoring others in project management practices.
