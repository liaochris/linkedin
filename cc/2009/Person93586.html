Shalav Risal’s Summary

As Human Resource Officer of SNV, Netherlands Development Organisation in Nepal, I am responsible to support over 70 staff members in human resource related aspects.

As Coordinator for Human Resource Management Consulting team at Bikalp, the primary assignments typically involved strategic HR interventions including HR strategy and policy development, restructuring, salary revision, performance management system development, HR audits, climate surveys and customised HR interventions.

My recruitment/headhunting assignments includes over 500 placements in leading FMCGs, Banks as well as development sector organisations.

My interest is helping management create exemplary environment for harnessing best talent in Nepal. I have extensively researched and interacted with professional managers throughout many research interventions.
