Manny Rodriques’s Summary

I'm a Worcester/Boston Area software developer with 20+ years of experience. I have worked in a variety of technical areas throughout my professional career, these have included embedded systems development, GUI design and development, network programming and, most recently, client server application development. During this time I have worked with various assembly languages, C++, C, ADA and more.

Currently, I am doing application development for the enterprise environment on Windows and Unix using C & C++. I've recently revised the product's API specification and been part of the team working on the Threat Assessment Model for the product.

I'd like to continue to explore the various aspects of secure development in addition to agile development techniques.
