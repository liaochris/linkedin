Lars G. Teigen’s Summary

I was a co-founder of Making Waves in 2001. From 2007 I am working on a new venture called Secondbrain in close cooperation with my previous company.

Second Brain is a social content community where you can manage all your social media, bookmarks and files in one place. Organize everything in your library, follow your friends’ updates and discover good content.
