Darren Van Soye’s Summary

OBJECTIVE: Director of Software Development

Darren Van Soye serves as ISD Corporation’s Director of Software Development. His responsibilities include leading a team of software developers through the process of architecting, developing and testing leading-edge software systems. Mr. Van Soye has accumulated six years of direct-line technology management, eight years of IT project management and 10 plus years of hands-on software development experience, including recent ASP.NET/C# 2008 experience. He has worked in both the aerospace and financial services industries. Mr. Van Soye earned a B.S. in Computer Science and MBA from University of California, Irvine. He also holds PMP, ITIL and Scrum Master certifications.

Darren Van Soye’s Specialties:

Hands-on software development including ASP.NET/C# 2008, business development, website usability, process redesign, technology adoption, project management (PMP), service management (ITIL) and Scrum.
