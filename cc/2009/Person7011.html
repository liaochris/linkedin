Elyse Nielsen, PMP, CPHIMS’s Summary

Elyse Nielsen has over 10 years of experience in health information technology management and leadership roles. Currently serving as a Senior Project Manager for St. Vincent’s Health System, an integrated healthcare system including 2 acute care hospitals, a nursing home, outpatient services and a growing multi-specialty physician group practice, Elyse is charged with implementing a Program Management Office to help facilitate capital investments and execution.

Elyse is a creative and strategic “outside the box” thinker with excellent communication, customer service, inter-personal and organizational skills. Additionally, Elyse has a solid track record and reputation for providing strong leadership and management with a keen understanding of applying information systems and technology to enable business strategy. She knows how to transform an organization to reach strategic goals.

Elyse is the co-chair of the Project Management Special Interest Group with the Healthcare Information Management Systems Society (HIMSS) as well as an active member of the American College of Healthcare Executives and Project Management Institute. She has been certified as a professional in healthcare information management systems (CPHIMS) and a Project Management Professional (PMP). Ms. Nielsen earned her Bachelors Degree in Mathematics from Siena College.

Elyse Nielsen, PMP, CPHIMS’s Specialties:

Leadership, strategic planning, project and program management, clinical transformation, organizational change management, staff development, performance management, vendor management, customer relationship management, facilitation and group processes, full project and systems life cycle, methodology development, $MM budget accountability.
