Venkatraman Subramanian’s Summary

Venkatraman has over 20 years of experience in the IT industry having worked in various parts of the globe. He has worked in leading edge enterprise software, Business consulting and research for strategic IT directions.

He has also responsibly and successfully managed large of teams that has provided business process design, technical design and implementations for fortune 100 and Fortune 500 organizations

For the past few years he has been primarily concentrating on Outsourcing strategies, management of Outsourcing projects and building the Enterprise Applications practice for various Multinational Firms in India and Startup companies in United States

As part of the above activities he has been a key figure in pre-sales presentations and responding to RFPs and helping clients prepare RFPs and the evaluation criteria s for shortlisting of the vendors.

Within these occupations he has developed and interacted with Chief Architects, Directors, and Senior and Executive Management from various vendor and client organizations and in some of the ventures have played a key role as an Solutions architect.

He has worked for companies like PricewaterhouseCoopers LLP, Oracle Financial Services, and had been a consultant to companies like Accenture, Ernst & young, Cognizant.

Apart from this as part of he is into philanthropy work and volunteers in my free time to tutor children in Middle Schools in New York in Math, Science and Social Sciences, and on the weekends at a school for special Needs and Mentally challenged.
