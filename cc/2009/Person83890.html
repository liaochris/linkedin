Rich Tatum’s Summary

I have been working with information technology and internet technology since 1993, but I am also trained as a minister, communicator and presenter. I have a wide array of skills and experiences from project management, web design and development, client support, call center management, writing, editing, multimedia authoring and editing, online and internet analysis, research and report generation, and strategic product development. I am a skilled presenter, public speaker, preacher and teacher. Most of all, I am a "big picture" thinker able to assimilate new information and skills quickly and add value to every team I've been a part of.

I get things done.

I am open to new contacts who need my help, who who can help me, or simply people who have a lot in common with me. If that's you, feel free to contact me, or connect with me!

I do prefer to connect with people I've had some contact with previously. So, if we haven't met or emailed before, please don't send me a boilerplate invitation — introduce yourself so we can get to know each other and discover what connections we can make!

I am happily employed at Zondervan Publishing in Grand Rapids, a great company with a great history. If' you're in my network, please let me know if you need an introduction. I'll see what I can do.

Meanwhile, if you're up to a few pretty pictures, check out my photo gallery!
