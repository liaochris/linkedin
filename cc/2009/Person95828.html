Sunita Woodcheke’s Summary

You know, some people claim it’s not good to mix God and business. I can’t help myself since I committed my life to God and to Jesus nearly 20 years ago. I choose to bring this up at this time because, if you read the Book of Genesis – right there in the beginning, Chapter 1:26-30 it says that God created us male and female in His image – to rule over the entire creation. So my next question is however are we to fulfill such a BIG JOB if we run around tired and exhausted?

I can’t tell you how many people I have spoken to over the years who suffer from constant exhaustion. Doctors can’t help. As much I love and am grateful for the doctors I have had over the years, let’s face it. Doctors are trained to deal with disease, not dis-ease. So what’s a person to do who has no disease but is severely afflicted with dis-ease?

I have a few goals in life and they run in this order: My main being to glorify God in my personsal live and in my business. I am committed to helping women regain vibrant health and vitality through spirituality and nutrition so that they can continue to take care of their families. All too often, we take care of our families but neglect ourselves. My second business commitment is to empower women to become financially healthy.
