Mark Calomeni’s Summary

Experienced and very accomplished Software Development Executive with extensive experience leading and managing successful software engineering organizations in start-up, mid-size and Fortune 100 sized companies. I bring a valued and strategic background in engineering leadership to your organizations through a collaborative, yet decisive management style.

I am currently the VP of Engineering for Accept Software an Enterprise Web 2.0 SaaS Application company developing applications for management of Innovation/Ideas, Product Requirements & Portfolio (web communities/portals, etc.), utilizing Agile and proven enterprise development methodologies.

Prior experience includes:
- VP of Engineering for Asoka Inc.,
- Senior Vice President of Engineering for Anthology Solutions, provider of the Yellow Machine, a NAS 1TB RAID consumer appliance for aggregation and management of digital multi-media content, with development teams in the US and India.
- VP of WW Engineering for Wyse Technology, leading all new and sustaining development with development teams in the US, India, China and Taiwan.
- VP of Engineering for OnDemand Inc..

My career spans both start-up ventures and a lengthy and progressive career at HP where I led a number of very successful software development teams such as Storage/System/Performance Management Apps, Operating Systems (MPE and HP-UX) High Availability and Performance product definition, design and development.

A strong commitment to customer aligned/focused engineering, executive presence with customers, demonstrated through responsiveness, effective presentations, communications, strong technical leadership and follow through, have all led to success in the Executive positions I've held. I have a verifiable track record of developing strategies, plans, roadmaps, leading and bringing together geographically dispersed development teams to successfully deliver new products to market.
