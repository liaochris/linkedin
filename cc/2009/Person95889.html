Eric Joiner, Jr. , DLP’s Summary

I am a 25 year logistics industry veteran with experience in both domestic and international supply chain practices. I have successfully implemented and managed a wide variety of client supply chain solutions ranging from consolidation, deconsolidation, warehousing, domestic trucking, ocean liner shipping, heavyweight air freight and parcel express.

I have specific experience working with Fortune 500 customers in the CPG, Electronics, retail and engineering and manufacturing verticals. Above all, I am a builder and leader of effective teams who plays well with colleagues at all levels.

I am looking to build my network of similarly interested professionals both in my industry and those that I serve.

Sincerely,

Eric J. Joiner, Jr.
