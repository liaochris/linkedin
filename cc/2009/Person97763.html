Derrick Daye’s Summary

Behold the power of trust. With it relationships are started - and kept, purchase decisions are made, sales multiply and brands flourish. At The Blake Project our sole focus is helping organizations create brands that build and sustain trust.

With renowned brand strategists Brad VanAuken (Author of Brand Aid) and Jack Trout (Legendary Branding Pioneer) we help growth oriented companies design, manage and build brands that drive profitable revenue growth through differentiated customer experiences.

Located in Rochester, New York, Tampa, Florida and Frankfurt, Germany we currently serve clients in North America, Europe, Asia and the Middle East.

Derrick Daye’s Specialties:

The Blake Project offers the following services to organizations/entities requiring strong brands: brand audits, brand research, brand strategy, brand (re)positioning, brand architecture, brand identity, brand management, brand measurement, brand marketing, brand extension, brand education, internal brand building, brand building on the Internet, "out-of-the-box” marketing, sensory branding, and international speaking engagements on branding. We also help with customer touchpoint design.
