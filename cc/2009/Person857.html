Stephanie Abouatallah’s Summary

More recently, as a part of the Indigo Love of Reading Foundation, I am working in a Coordinator capacity by organizing and executing marketing initiatives that raise and distribute money to high needs elementary schools across Canada.

Historically, my professional experience has been built on assisting other companies grow and develop. By providing strong internet marketing strategic advice and solutions; my clients were able to grow their companies with an abundance of incoming leads at a very LOW cost per lead.
