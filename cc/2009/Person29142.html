Julian L. Wong’s Summary

I am seeking to create collaborative, integrated and adaptive platforms to launch regenerative, clean food-energy-water systems solutions. Such platforms will necessarily consider the full panoply of social, political, technological, financial and business and ecological characteristics of the local environment. How can we change the world for the better, in a practical yet scaled-up manner? I invite you to contact me.

I have just joined the Center for American Progress, a progressive think tank in D.C., where I specialize in international climate policy, China energy policy and clean technology development. Most recently, I was a Fulbright Fellow in Beijing researching China's renewable energy policies and clean tech industry development.

I combine an understanding of the global energy market with professional experience in finance, corporate law and policy research in both Asia and the U.S. I post my reflections on my website: http://greenleapforward.com
