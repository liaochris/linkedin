DubLi Network’s Summary

DubLi seeks to breakthrough the traditional concept of bidding and revolutionize the concept of reverse auctioning in a big way with the sole mission of substantially reducing the cost of purchasing name brand products and services for all affiliates and buyers online. With DubLi’s reverse auction model the price of an item decreases in price with bid made by a potential buyer instead of increasing in price like traditional auctions. You can offer your customers magnanimous discounts, and also provide them with immense savings on gift certificates with stores like Wal-Mart and Target and travel vouchers. Customers can register for free and the most they would ever have to pay for a single bid is .80¢! Just imagine, you don’t have to shell out any money to inventory any of the products or services offered in the DubLi auctions – Yet YOU earn money every time one of your customers or the customers in your global organization places a bid on DubLi!
