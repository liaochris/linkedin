Jonathan Faustine’s Summary

I have been an entrepreneur, and "intrepreneur," a community builder, a strong team member and a transformational leader. I have helped to build both companies and organizations. I have been a senior executive in a startup software company, I have served as the senior internet strategist for a Fortune 500 firm And, I founded and led the internet practice in the 90s at a large consulting firm.

I have spent the last six years focusing on community service and philanthropic work around the needs of children and in education. It is possible to make positive change in the educational landscape and one of my most satisfying projects has been starting a new independent high school in San Francisco.

So now I have begun a new career stage working in the non-profit Education Management world as the senior business officer in a Charter School management company. LPS offers a college prep program to under-served high schoolers in some of the most marginalized communities in the Bay Area.

Most recently I have served as a consultant to start-ups. I have helped a number of software and services startups as a coach/advisor to the founders and CEOs. I help these companies with marketing strategy, sales, team development, communication, and customer access. I continue to provide occasion coaching and advice for start up founders and execs, but I am not taking on new clients.
