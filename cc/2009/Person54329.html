Kelli Richards’s Summary

Twenty year plus digital music/media executive (10 of those driving music & entertainment initiatives at Apple, and the past 10 running the All Access Group, a digital music & entertainment consultancy). Focused on strategic business development creating opportunities for premium branded content & artists with new business models and disruptive, innovative technologies for digital distribution.

Separately, I've been producing concerts, awards shows & celebrity fundraisers for over twenty years -- and am also a former A&R exec at EMI. Recently co-authored a critically acclaimed book/DVD entitled "The Art of Digital Music". I'm also on the Board of Governors of the San Francisco Chapter of NARAS (group behind the Grammys). See my company web site at "www.allaccessgroup.com" for more info.

Kelli Richards’s Specialties:

Digital music/media entrepreneur, strategic alliances, connector/networker, content strategy, digital distribution, develop new business models and revenue streams, dealmaking, established artists. Working with disruptive technologies & emerging business models in music and entertainment is the common theme of my work. Also over twenty years producing award shows, digital music or media conferences, large scale concerts, celebrity fundraisers, compilations.
