Roger Penycate’s Interests:

LAUGHING DAUGHTER - The Musical After 30 years of writing and a 5 year collaboration with the composer, Rick Jones, the show is now ready for production. Laughing Daughter is a Country Rock/Americana Musical depicting the interwoven lives and loves of three very different couples. Laughing Daughter, a 19 girl old Native American girl, leaves the reservation dreaming of becoming a star in the music industry. Junior Johnson travels the country performing at a variety of gigs, barely making enough to pay for his accommodation, meals and bar bills. The down-home Jolene, a dedicated life-long fan, has a secret and finally decides to declare herself. Whilst trying to help the inconsolable James, the divorced Storm Heart, missing his estranged wife, Shell Flower, comes to terms with being separated from the two people he loves most. The show will premier in Maryland at the appropriately named Indian Head Center for the Arts, on the 3rd September 2009 with a run of 12 nights.
