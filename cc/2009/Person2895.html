Alec East’s Summary

Founder of charitymash.com, specialist in digital media for marketing & brand communications with experience across multiple delivery platforms.

With 14 years experience in interactive and moving image and a background as an award-winning designer,
I now deliver strategies which allow clients to leverage the opportunities of Search, Social Media, Email, mobile web, interactive TV and beyond the browser channels to increase sales, grow long-term customer value and build brand awareness & equity.

My work is featured in design books, I'm an occasional speaker at industry and public events, a blogger, a moderator for social networks and a contributing writer for trade and national press.
