Ray Craighead’s Summary

Fine artist and illustrator using traditional and digital media. Extensive experience doing conceptual and technical illustration, presentation graphics, magazine cover illustration, portraiture, children's illustrations, way-finding maps, data-driven graphics, etc.

Specialist in Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop, Corel Painter, Microsoft PowerPoint, Daz Carrara (3D) and Google SketchUp. Also experienced in Adobe InDesign, DreamWeaver and Flash, Microsoft Excel and other graphic-related software.

College-level teaching experience in graphic design. Enjoy sharing traditional and digital art experience with interested students.

Ray Craighead’s Specialties:

Software expertise includes Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, Microsoft PowerPoint, Corel Painter, Google SketchUp, DAZ Carrara and others. Also produce variable data graphics for specialized VDP projects utilizing Microsoft Excel and Data-Driven Graphic features found in Adobe CS.
