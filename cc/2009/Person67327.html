Michael Gannon’s Summary

Sales and Marketing executive with Medical Equipment, Medical Device and Pharmaceutical companies. Extensive experience in business development, creating revenue growth and delivering corporate value for life sciences businesses. Skilled at creating strategies to increase sales, market share and penetrating new markets. Adept at building effective organizations, selecting talent, setting goals, launching new products and delivering top and bottom lines. Leadership experiences with start-up ventures and large established companies. Practical experience with budgeting, managing profit & loss, developing sales funnels, forecasting sales and providing change leadership. Management career includes developing first and second-line managers and leading nationwide sales teams. Success negotiating, closing and implementing agreements with large national accounts and developing sustainable marketing campaigns.
