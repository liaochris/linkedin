Bryce Johnson’s Summary

I am currently working with the Microsoft SQL UX group on some new software focused on providing information workers with familiar tools that alow them to do powerful data analysis.

As a member of the User Experience Team at Microsoft Dynamics in Redmond Washington I was be part of the team designing future releases of the Dynamics ERP solutions. My focus was to bring the AX Enterprise Web Portal user experience closer to that of the Windows Client while not discarding the best features or interaction patterns from SharePoint and best-of-breed web applications.

As the Director of User Experience Design for Navantis Inc. an award-winning IT professional services firm in Toronto, ON. Organizations like: Microsoft, CIBC, Honda Canada, MSN Sympatico, the Canadian National Institute for the Blind, Magna Entertainment Inc., the City of Hamilton, the Canadian Broadcasting Corporation, the Toronto Stock Exchange, and the Canadian Olympic Commission.
