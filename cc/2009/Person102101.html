Aart van Bezooyen’s Summary

Aart van Bezooyen is a Dutch designer who lives and works in Hamburg. He received a masters degree in industrial design from the Delft University of Technology for originating the Material Explorer, a materials selection support tool for designers.

Material Stories is where he brings together his experience in industrial design and passion for materials. He inspires and enables the best use of materials in order to make design more competitive, creative and sustainable.

Currently, he teaches materials and design at universities in The Netherlands and Germany and supports companies with materials research, concept design and strategic thinking.

Aart van Bezooyen’s Specialties:

materials research, industrial design, strategic consulting, lateral thinking, querdenken, materialselection, innovation, materials trends, material inspiration, branding and identity, workshops, brainstorming
