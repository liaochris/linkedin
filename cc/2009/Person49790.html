Jo Jordan’s Summary

I lead Rooi Limited, a company devoted entirely to encouraging collective action on the internet by groups of like-minded people.

We devise and introduce online communities where people are able to find each other and work together to advance their individual and common purposes.

My prime project in February 2009 is the establishment of http://olney100.ning.com for the town of Olney in Buckinghamshire, England, UK.

Come and have a look and see if you have any mutual interest with us. We are a town of fine foods & wines, antiques & rugs, art galleries and high fashion, classic cars & country walks. Amazing Grace was written here. The Pancake race was invented here. Originally, a lace making town, we know have a thriving IT industry.

Positive psychology, work psychology, social media, coaching.

Leadership training, management skills, team building, project management, conventional HR in competitive organizations (see my cv), university teaching and CPD, world wide network of mavens, connectors and evangelists in work psychology and social media.
