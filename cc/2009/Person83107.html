Eric Wall’s Summary

I am currently President of E.L. Wall and Associates, a media and political consulting firm. We specialize in grassroots organizing, oppositional research, promotion, social media, demographics, political strategy and analysis.

I am also the current party Secretary/Outreach Coordinator/Precinct Development Director for the Bay Co. Florida Republican Executive Committee.

* I have been involved in politics in one way or another since being a member of the Bay County Young Republicans in 1988 after moving to Panama City, Florida. I have supervised volunteers, am an Army veteran and have a variety of political experience.

*I was a member of the Republican Liberty Caucus Florida state executive committee from 2008-2009 and am currently a member of the Republican Liberty Caucus National Committee, I have worked with many groups including Freedom Works, Americans for Prosperity, Florida Taxpayers Union and the James Madison Institute

* I have met with legislators in the Florida House and Senate and the Governor's office both in 2008 and 2009 regarding property taxes and enacting spending caps, including the GOP House and Senate leadership this past session.

*I was a Regional Field Coordinator and precinct leader for the Ron Paul 2008 presidential campaign, with responsibilities in Washington, Jackson, Holmes, Gulf, Calhoun, Liberty, Bay and Wakulla counties that involved a lot of travel during the primary season. I signed a non-disclosure agreement and I will do so again if need be.

I ran for RPOF State Committeeman and received almost 6,000 votes in the GOP primary.

I served as the E-campaign Director for the Bay County Republican Party during the 2008 general election.

I was the Communications Director and Deputy Field Director for Mark Mulligan for Congress (R) 2008 in Congressional District 2, covering 16 counties.

I was elected Bay Co. REC Secretary last December 1st.
