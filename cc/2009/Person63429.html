Mark Laymon’s Summary

Mark Laymon has opened the doors at SNASM! Please register and add your photo/avatar today! http://snasm.com

Mark Laymon is a Search Engine Optimization specialist (SEO), niche portal and web designer. Using sophisticated programming techniques targeting search engines, he drives highly targeted Internet traffic to top quality sites. Prior to founding his businesses, Mark Laymon was in the Restaurant industry. He was a Chef for the Arugula Grille, and a corporate trainer for new proprietors of Carrabba’s Italian Grill franchises, owned by Outback. For Papa John’s, Mark opened multiple stores as a Manager, handling training and Grand Openings, Plus Mark Laymon has been seen in the back of the house at E.R. Bradley’s Saloon, a West Palm Beach waterfront restaurant tradition.

Since then, Mark Laymon has become a successful Florida based SEO specialist targeting “the dark-side of SEO,” otherwise known as user friendly, fully automated websites, and domain cloaking. Mark Laymon has a very broad selection of blogs, websites, and domains under his management. Mark Laymon mostly works with CMS Blogs, (Content Management Systems) which allows the targeting of content to push RSS feeds across many platforms that permit the maximum targeting of demographics.

His love of fishing has led to the creation of several fishing websites. Originally from Minnesota, he is an avid outdoorsman; big game hunting in addition to fishing, which provide the ingredients for his gourmet cooking. Mark Laymon is a graduate of Florida Culinary Institute.
