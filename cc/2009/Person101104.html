Troy Jensen’s Summary

Troy Jensen currently serves as President and Chief Executive Officer of SolVentus Energy, Inc. SolVentus Energy, a new division of Transpacific CleanTech, Inc., is working on creating markets and financial solutions for Renewable Energy, focused primarily on the Southwest US solar sector residential and commercial developments.

Troy Jensen has spent his career in marketing, finance and monetization modeling throughout the digital mediums and technology sector, including Interactive, mobile, and technology platforms. His strong background in business development and sales, as well as expertise in business development, technology and operations, has led to leading the inception and execution of hundreds of sophisticated digital marketing and application offerings built on industry-leading technology platforms for many Fortune 500 clients. Troy has lead both ad agency, online ad network and publisher teams responsible for selling and successfully executing over $450 million dollars in digital advertising campaigns, web site design, development and application solutions, mobile application development and marketing, Social Media initiatives, online content syndication and strategic cross-media integration initiatives. He has also lead, co-founded or consulted with several successful Interactive startups. Troy has spoken at digital technology forums and events, and has contributed to articles and research in relation to the latest happenings in the emerging digital mediums, and early-stage funding and growth strategies for startups.  

When not consumed by all things digital, Troy passion is in the air - he has his private pilots license and is currently in training for his instrument rating. Other hobbies include reading just about anything he can get his hands on, collecting every electronic gadget possible, is a practicing amateur economist/historian, and is an avid Miami Dolphins fan.

Troy Jensen’s Experience

  • President and Chief Executive Officer

    SolVentus Energy, Inc.

    (Renewables & Environment industry)

    April 2009 — Present (4 months)

    Troy serves as President and CEO of SolVentus Energy, Inc., a new division of Transpacific CleanTech, Inc. SolVentus Energy is working on creating markets and financial solutions for Renewable Energy, focused primarily on the Southwest US solar sector residential and commercial developments. SolVentus Energy has been in an Alpha stage for the past 12 months, and is emerging Beta to the public in Mid-May 2009. With private financing secured and a strong team of leading industry players, SolVentus Energy's mission is to help build markets for renewable energy across the Southwest initially, and globally within five years. Our team is excited, as are our strong partners in solar and wind manufacturers, technology innovators, distribution, electrical contractors, Clean Energy finance, residential and commercial developers, utilities, and energy traders. Ultimately, we want Clean Energy adoption to hit the critical mass needed to provide healthy profits for all involved, provide end-users inexpensive energy, dramatically reduce our carbon footprint and create a cleaner world for us all, and spur the beginning of the end of our reliance on foreign sources of energy for the United States, Europe and Asia.

Jensen Digital Media provides digital revenue consultancy services for several high-profile online publishers, entertainment brands, content providers, production studios, online gaming firms, digital ad agencies, online ad networks, as well as a deep expertise in digital content distribution and syndication across across multiple delivery platforms, including online video, mobile and an expertise in social media viral distribution through share-to-social technology.

  • Senior Vice President, Global Sales and Marketing

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    August 2007 — July 2008 (1 year)

    Troy Jensen served as Senior Vice President of Global Sales and Marketing for Optimate Networks, a digital video syndication and distribution network, delivering professionally produced video over the web, mobile and through viral social media applications and widgets. The Optimate content management system ingests video content, transcodes it for the production studios or broadcast networks, and infuses the video files with proprietary meta data that allows Optimate to target video contextually and segment traffic. Jensen's tenure with Optimate saw the network achieve record year-over-year ad sales revenue increases in every quarter. In his first three months with Optimate, ad sales revenue jumped 800% from the preceding quarter. He also assisted with publisher and video entertainment content acquisition efforts content producers, and in early 2008 Jensen lead Optimate in obtaining it’s first major digital content syndication agreement with a major broadcast television network.

  • President and Founder

    January 2007 — August 2007 (8 months)

    Troy served as President and Founder of Edge Interactive Media, Inc., a full-service interactive ad agency specializing in producing online video productions and Interactive marketing advertising campaigns. Edge assembled a team with expertise in navigating the constantly-changing Interactive landscape as mediums merge, video content migrates, and consumer behavior shifts. Edge Interactive excelled at helping advertisers and publishers alike in creating the intimate brand connection so important in building awareness and meeting defined campaign goals. Troy also oversaw online video content syndication strategies and site monetization. As a result of Jensen's long history and expertise in online advertising, Edge was able to form strategic partnerships with several high-profile Interactive technology platforms to solidify an agency technology infrastructure with the ability to provide granular targeting and reporting in real time.
