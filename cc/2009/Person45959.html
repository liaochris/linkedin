Jennifer Cifuentes’s Summary

For most of my life I have been fascinated with what makes people tick and how things work. In my professional career, I’ve used this natural curiosity to help me uncover hidden insights that can be used to create marketing that is effective in meeting the client’s goals and objectives. In addition, I am constantly challenging myself and others to put themselves in the place of the target audience – to always keep in mind the needs and desires of those who are the end user.

I’ve spent most of my career on the creative side, but I’ve always felt that something was missing from my experience as a creative. As a result, I decided to pursue an advanced degree on the strategic side in Integrated Marketing Communications.

My graduate-level educational experience at Roosevelt University has helped place me firmly between strategic and creative thinking and has helped solidify my personal philosophy: “Marketing must go beyond looking great. It has to meet or exceed the client’s expectations, while also respecting the target audience.”

Also, I’m well organized, have great communication skills and a passion for my work.
