Dr. Sheri Rosenthal’s Summary

Sheri is a Master Toltec Teacher trained by don Miguel Ruiz, the author of "The Four Agreements" and has apprenticed with him for 8 years. She was a Podiatrist for 16 years in her pre-Toltec days and the director of a podiatric surgical residency program. From 2000 to 2002 she acted as Executive Director of Sixth Sun Foundation, a Toltec non-profit corporation. There Sheri had the opportunity and privilege to design and run don Miguel Ruiz's power journeys, and co-teach with him at his workshops.

Sheri's passionate about spiritual journeys and owner of Journeys of the Spirit® (www.journeysofthespirit.com) a tour company that specializes in adventures that open the heart and heal the mind. Journeys of the Spirit can design journeys for authors, teachers, and New Thought Churches that are not just trips - but adventures of the soul. We have a wonderful affiliate program for our trips and we pay commissions to those who promote our journeys and refer paid clients. For more info: http://cli.gs/bMRG9a

Sheri is the author of "The Complete Idiot's Guide to Toltec Wisdom," a wonderful book which includes everything you could possibly want to know about everything Toltec! In addition she also has written "Banish Mind Spam: Four Steps for Deprogramming Self-Limiting and Self-Sabotaging Beliefs".

Sheri is devoted to combining in a practical way, the wonderful esoteric teachings from our Toltec traditions. Using her unique background she transforms these teachings into clear concepts peppered with wry humor and lots of love! She delights in bringing forth these teachings and watching people transform before her eyes.
