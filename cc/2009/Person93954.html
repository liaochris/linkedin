Stefan Larsson’s Summary

20 years in international IT business as energetic, driving, enthusiastic and creative manager in various sizes of companies. Computer Aided Design and 3D technology has always been the guiding star. Large professional network and contacts, as well as deep knowledge in the Computer graphic industry together with documented international management and sales skills.

Has been in the professional IT industry since 1987 and started at the beginning of the home computer and Personal computer era with computers like ABC80 from Luxor and later Commodore 64 and Sinclair Spectrum. At the age of 14 took over teaching in computer classes in School for the computer language BASIC. After graduating from 4 year technical Engineer school for Electronics and computers, Military Service at P4 in Skövde and work as an electrical engineer, the entrepreneur career started.
