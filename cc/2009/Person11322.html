Bill Gerba’s Summary

The bulk of my experience and expertise involves using technology to meet and exceed marketing objectives. I'm particularly active in the digital signage (digital out-of-home or "dOOH") space, and have worked on numerous shopper marketing, retail media and digital signage projects in retail, health care, hospitality and other vertical markets.
