Jorge Parra Photography’s Summary

Jorge Parra Photography, based in Miami , but available nation and worldwide, provides still photography and video solutions for the commercial, advertising and editorial markets by using an interesting fusion of personal, commercial and fine art work, with carefully applied technical skills and production values to cater to different fields of the industry, advertising, editorial, marketing, PR and publicity.

This fusion has helped providing services to a varied clientele, ranging from fashion designers, to interior designers, architects,celebrities, to corporate, travel and hospitality. Direct clients, magazines and Advertising Agencies benefits from this accumulated experience and always evolving fusion process, which has resulted in varied portfolios, available online, from our launch pad, The Visual Hub.(www.TheVisualHub.com)

News and updates about projects, ideas and works in progress are available on my PhotoBlog,

http://PureVisualEnergy.com.

Cheers!

Jorge Parra Photography’s Specialties:

The blend of Fashion, Beauty,Fine Art and Editorial backgrounds allow me to run Lifestyle Advertising Campaigns and other VisualProjects, involving complex Productions.

Health & Beauty, Fashion, Architecture and Interior Design, Travel and Tourism/Hospitality photography, and images for the Luxury and Leisure market, with an edgy conceptual touch, have become a signature of my multifaceted work.
