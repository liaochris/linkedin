Jim Thompson’s Summary

Seventeen years of independent consulting experience. Primary focus has been due diligence for investment bankers and legal firms. Secondary focus has been marketing of products and services to the pulp and paper industry.

Founder and Executive Editor of PaperMoney, Nip Impressions, Capital Arguments and The Thompson Private Letter. Read by over 19,000 subscribers worldwide on a regular basis.
