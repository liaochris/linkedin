Janna Kimel’s Summary

My work focuses on helping hi-tech and health care related companies bring new and improved products to market. Qualitative research is conducted and requirements written to inform the next generation of product, service or software.

I have 15+ years of work in soft-goods production and design research with expertise in sensors, wearable technology, medication management, medical products, exercise and physical rehabilitation.

Janna Kimel’s Specialties:

design research, industrial design, concept generation, fieldwork and interviewing to get at the root of unmet needs through research and usability issues, writing requirements,
interaction design, user experience, also textile research and sewn prototyping

Population focus includes elderly, people with a disability, people going through physical rehabilitation and those living with chronic disease.
