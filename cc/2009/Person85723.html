Roger Early’s Summary

I am a Web Marketer/Analyst with over eight years of specialized experience in scientific instrumentation, cathodic electronics and software, semiconductor manufacturing, retail, financial services, online education, and software engineering industries. Dedication to accuracy, presentation, and exceptional customer service has always been paramount along with a superior record of surpassing expectations, deadlines, and organization.

My experience includes the investigation, documentation and implementation of business unit needs and goals for online marketing activities and the establishment/reporting of actionable metrics. I am experienced with SiteCatalyst, HBX, and WebTrends. My project management experience includes multi-site SiteCatalyst implementation and migration projects.

I am accustomed to being at the intersection of development, product management, marketing, and business development and I offer strategic perspectives, sound business judgment, deep analytical capabilities, and a collaborative working style. I possess strong intellectual curiosity, and a passion for achieving practical business impact.
