Pam Kirby’s Summary

I am a residential real estate professional with Bean Group. I am seeking buyer and seller referrals from past clients and other Realtors from across the country.

When with Coldwell Banker, I was recognized as a top producing agent, earning the President's Circle awards in 2005 & 2006 and the Diamond Society in 2004 & 2007. I work both on the listing and buyer sides of transactions and also have experience in marketing new construction and historic renovation projects.
