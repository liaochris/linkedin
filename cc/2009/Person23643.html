Dave Rigotti’s Summary

Dave is a writer, speaker, and Internet marketer. He is the owner of Freezing Hot, a small Internet marketing company that develops measurable marketing campaigns for small and large businesses alike. He’s had internships with some of the most well-known and influential brands in the world, conducting search engine optimization and developing marketing communication strategies. In July, Dave will be joining Microsoft as a Product Manager on Live Search.

Dave recently graduated from The University of Toledo (BBA with Honors, magna cum laude, 2009) after majoring in marketing and entrepreneurship, family and small business. His is currently working on getting his thesis, titled “The Social Media Use of Obama and McCain: The correlation between the use of social media Web sites among different age groups and the influence of such sites on the 2008 presidential election,” published.
