Karen Sieczka’s Summary

Are you a human resources professional, training manager, CEO, or small biz owner trying to figure out how you are going to be able to meet your yearly training goals with a shrinking budget? Karen can help!! Working under short-term training contracts and creating customized training is her specialty.

Visit www.GrowingGreatIdeas.com for more information on how Karen can help you.

Karen Sieczka is a trainer, author, and founder of GrowingGreatIdeas.com. In 2008, she trained nearly 700 participants in a variety of programs including technology, supervisory skills, and customized training workshops as an adjunct instructor at Gulf Coast Community College and Troy University.

Karen is familiar with the struggle many of us encounter when we become entrenched in the 9 to 5 world and lose sight of what really makes us tick. Her own struggle became the inspiration for her training program called Growing Great Ideas: Unleashing Creativity at Work.

The program generates ideas, enthusiasm, and teamwork. It is available now for business and non-profit organizations to save money by boosting ideas,creativity, and enthusiasm.

It can be customized to address particular issues or challenges.

She has finished the first edition of the companion book Growing Great Ideas: Unleashing Creativity at Work. The book is available in print and download formats at LULU.com,

http://www.lulu.com/content/3342025

Need to jumpstart the creative processes at work? Karen can help and is available to speak on "Where's the Next Great Idea?"
