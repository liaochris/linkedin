Jon Harmon’s Summary

Strategic public relations executive driving improved corporate reputation from the inside out.. Brand builder. Media relations pro. Experienced leader.

As Navistar's senior communications executive, I lead a team responsible for employee and dealer communications, media relations and corporate brand for a $16 billion global truck manufacturer.

Previously, I founded Force for Good Communications, a public relations consultancy centered on transparency, honesty, integrity and social responsibility.

During a 23-year run at Ford Motor Company, I progressed through a variety of increasingly responsible positions in employee communications, issues management, media relations, speechwriting, product promotion and communications strategy as practitioner, manager and director.
