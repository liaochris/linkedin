Steve Clark | Wireless Asset Recovery’s Summary

Steve Clark is a business development specialist with decades of experience helping technology companies achieve their goals and objectives.

He now operates as Vice President of Sales at e-Cycle

For the past several years Steve Clark has worked as the vice president of business incubation services for TechColumbus, an organization providing support and resources to accelerate technology-driven economic development in Central Ohio.

Steve Clark was responsible for the growth of dozens of start-up companies, and collaborated with industry, government and academic institutions in the development and implementation of strategic business initiatives.

From 1991 to 1999, Clark was director of sales and marketing for CRM solution provider Wilke/Thornton, Inc., where he was responsible for all call center software application sales and marketing initiatives for major consumer product lines such as Hershey, Coors, Quaker Oats and M&M Mars.

Steve Clark was vice president of sales and marketing for Battelle, an international science and technology company, from 1979 to 1990. He was also a founding executive of Information Dimensions, Inc., a Battelle subsidiary. Clark helped grow Information Dimensions into a leading provider of web-enabled knowledge centers and library management solutions, with annual revenues of $30 million.
