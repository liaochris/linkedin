Silvia Richardson’s Summary

Silvia Richardson: 21st Century Immigrant Living The American Dream

Silvia Richardson was born in the beautiful city of Appenzell, Switzerland surrounded by the Swiss Alps. Appenzell is a town of 5,000 in Eastern Switzerland, less than two hours from Zürich by train. The town lies amid green hills with just enough low-altitude Alps to remind tourists that they're still in Switzerland.
It's most famous for its annual Landesgemeinde, an open-air parliament where citizens vote directly on major issues instead of trusting elected officials to make decisions for them.
She was raised as the middle child amid four brothers. Silvia speaks 3 languages fluently. Silvia began her dream for independence at an early age. She began her career in banking throughout Europe.
Later on she became a Marketing Assistant at DuPont promoting the Teflon/Silverstone Cookware’s.
She fulfilled that childhood dream for independence when she moved to America. She attended Delaware County Community College in Media, Pennsylvania. If that was not difficult enough, she also completed the arduous requirements to gain full American citizenship.
Silvia now lives in the Lonestar State of Texas where she worked as an Administrative Manager. She has a wonderful mentor who took her under his wings and teaches her all about Internet Marketing.
In her “spare” time, she is a devoted mom to her 11 year old son Ryan, cares for lost and stray animals, draws, reads, and writes. Silvia also enjoys working with her hands and that has lead to building miniature dollhouses and crafts with her son. When you ask Silvia's son Ryan what he thinks of his mom you be surprised:
"My mom is a very thoughtful, funny and caring person. She is always happy, likes what I like and she is special to my heart. She is a great mom, very talented and surprises me often."

Silvia worked with My VMTeam, Boresha Coffee, Orovo and BlogTalkRadio and focus on a new direction now.
