Jonathan Cheriff’s Summary

Jonathan Cheriff - Client Strategy, Business Development, Online Marketing & Emerging Technology

Award-winning Interactive Advertising professional and Digital Marketing Entrepreneur with a proven history of landing, developing, and managing marketing programs for Fortune 500 clients. A pioneer in internet and rich media marketing with expertise in new media, strategic planning, creative technology, viral marketing and Social Online Communities. Proven ability to successfully grow and manage business units and bring proprietary products to market. A strategic B2C, B2B-focused innovative marketer responsible for Social Online Community Member registration and retention.

After earning his Degree in Broadcasting & Film with a minor in Marketing from Boston University, he went on to become Vice President of Marketing for Lyons Management Group, the largest Entertainment Company in Boston, Massachusetts.

During the same period he was publisher for Honey Magazine, a lifestyle magazine, and a partner in Permigrin Records out of Boston. There, he purchased and placed media advertising and established various artists in the community.

His experience with interactive advertising started at Identity One, where he served as Marketing Director, helping the company establish its brand and define its markets.

Among the clients he has pitched, landed and managed are Hall Wines, Ledson Vineyards, Juice Shack, Honig Wines, Fosters, Beam, Union Bay, Penguin, Karmaloop, Howe, Exxon Mobil, Universal Studios, MGM, Zebra Cards, Intel Corporation, Microsoft Corporation, Frederick's of Hollywood, Starwood Resorts, Piaggio USA, Arnold Advertising, Tracy Locke, Digitas, Zone Alarms, Websense, Agami, Volkswagen and many more.

Jonathan Cheriff’s Specialties:

Interactive Marketing Consultant and Media Buyer/Sales. New Business Development, Pitches,Sales, and Account Management. Hunter and a Farmer. Everything but fixing the kitchen sink!
