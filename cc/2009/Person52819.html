Jeff Wilson’s Summary

I spent over 20 years in the automotive paint industry in sales and management. I was general manager of a chain of paint stores, an independant sales representative for over 30 manufacturers, and worked with AKZO Noble Sikkens, BASF Corporation, and Sherwin Williams in both sales and management.

My goal was to always be in the industry that I have a passion for, which is computers. I stayed in the industry by being a tech resource for the companies I worked for and selling both custom software and custom built computers as a side business.

The opportunity arose to start my own company and it has grown from there. Networking has had a huge impact on the organization and continues today. Referrals are 100% of the business in both IT work for businesses and web development. You can go to http://www.sbmsystems.com and view the portfolio page to see a sampleing of clients and works.
