Emilie Barta’s Summary

Emilie Barta is an experienced Presenter and Product Demonstrator who has been booked on over 200 trade shows, conventions, press releases, business meetings, and product launches across the US and Canada. Emilie has experience in a broad range of industries and LG, Bosch, Extreme Networks, San Disk, Berlex, Maquet, Promise Technology, Lasermax Roll Systems, and NaylorCMG are just a few of her many repeat clients. They can attest that her effervescent personality and ability to communicate any information as if she handles it every day, makes it possible for her to meld into their team. Companies know that having Emilie as a first impression spells success!

As a Program Host, Emilie has had the opportunity to tell the stories of government dignitaries, sports personalities, business owners, industry experts, and the "average joe." She most recently could be seen as the Host of the Best New Products segment on "Today's Homeowner with Danny Lipford" which is syndicated in over 200 markets. Other experience includes being the Host and Reporter, and sometimes Writer and Associate Producer, of programs for the Association for Manufacturing Technology (IMTS-TV); the City of St. Louis (CITY TV 10); the Department of Veterans' Affairs (VAKN-TV); and the St. Louis NBC affiliate (KSDK-TV). And her Telly Award, honoring the very best cable television programs, proves that she is highly successful at creating warmth and rapport with not only her interview subjects, but also with her audience.

Emilie can also be seen and heard in over 100 corporate videos, industrial films, and radio and TV commercials as the Narrator or in Character Roles. Companies such as Anheuser-Busch, Famous Footwear, Kohl’s, Mc Donald’s, ADP, SSM Health Care, Schnucks Markets, and the University of Utah have all had the opportunity to work with Emilie on a repeated basis because they know that she can give them whatever they ask, and usually on the first take.
