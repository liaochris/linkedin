eric fehrman’s Summary

COMPANY
At Fehrman Tool & Die we design & manufacture high quality plastic injection molds, prototype molds, punch and trim dies, and perform all types of custom machining.

We excel at jobs that are difficult to design and build and we are confident that "no job is too tough". We are known for our superior quality, competitive costs, consistently on-time deliveries, and for being very flexible and easy to work with.

Our portfolio includes clients in the automotive industry, appliances, electronics, medical, consumer products, commercial products, child products, and aerospace.

If you would like to discuss a design or tooling project, please email me at eric@ftdinc.com

PERSONAL
I manage day to day operations and oversee all projects. I am responsible for customer care and sales and project estimating. I oversee engineering and perform part and tool design as well as CAM and communicate with all staff and suppliers.

I am fluent with the following CAD/CAM softwares: Cimatron E & IT, Cadkey, Surfcam, I-DEAS Master Series, & Mastercam.

eric fehrman’s Specialties:

Plastic part & tooling design; complex part modeling & surfacing; CAD/CAM; plastic injection molds; prototype molds; punch & trim dies; custom machining
