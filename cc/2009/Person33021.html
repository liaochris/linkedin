Bernardo Ferdman’s Summary

Bernardo is an accomplished Leadership and Organization Development Consultant with more than 25 years of experience working with diverse groups and organizations to increase individual and collective effectiveness. See http://bernardoferdman.org.

Bernardo Ferdman’s Specialties:

Consulting, education, and assessment in the areas of diversity and inclusion, multicultural and Latino/a leadership, cross-cultural communication, integration of Latino/a employees, Latino/a professional and leadership development, intergroup relations, development and implementation of effective strategic diversity initiatives, leadership development and coaching. See http://bernardoferdman.org for more information.
