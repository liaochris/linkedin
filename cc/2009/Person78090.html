Paul Bryan’s Summary

Senior software solution architect and software project delivery team lead. Proven track record delivering robust enterprise identity, access management, federation, portal, financial and transaction processing software solutions. Over 20 years professional experience in development, deployment and operations of high-performance and secure enterprise software solutions.

Utilizes pragmatic architecture, design and development methodologies to deliver end-to-end enterprise solutions, including: delivery process management, requirements analysis and modeling, systems and application software architecture and design, hands-on software development, systems integration, quality assurance, deployment and knowledge transfer.
