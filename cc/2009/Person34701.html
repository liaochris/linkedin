Chris Miller’s Summary

We help you make smarter hiring and business decisions.

I'm a licensed Private Investigator with over 30 years of experience in employee screening, retail loss prevention, corporate security and background investigations.

My company, FYI Screening, Inc., helps Human Resources and Loss Prevention professionals improve the effectiveness of their employee screening programs, minimize risk and hire smarter.

My team at FYI will help you...

* Hire the best-fit employees
* Make faster hiring decisions
* Build a solid, trusted work force
* Reduce turnover
