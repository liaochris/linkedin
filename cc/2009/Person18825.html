Chris Tulino’s Summary

Professional Project Manager (PMI Certified) with over nine years of experience managing teams, and projects across a variety of industries. Accustomed to mentoring other project managers and working effectively within Project Management Office (PMO) environments. Advanced business analysis skills and deep technical knowledge. Excellent organizational and communication skills.

Management Skills
- Excellent skills working with teams to plan single projects and with project managers to plan complex interdependent projects and programs
- Advanced knowledge of MS Project including techniques for plan development, tracking and reporting
- Use of critical chain project management techniques to manage time and resource buffers, minimize risk and maximize the benefit of task duration variance
- Use of business analysis techniques to elicit true requirements for projects, processes and technology components
- Use of quality assurance, quality control and automated testing techniques to ensure project quality
- Excellent soft skills to motivate and align team members and project stakeholders
- Ability to train and mentor other project managers

Technical Skills
- A rich technical background in current software development methods, tools and techniques
- Experience at every station within the software development life cycle from business analyst to project manager to tier 2 support
- Ability to review technical solutions and participate in the design and development of technical projects
- Excellent ability to speak to and understand technical tasks from system architecture to database design, to object design.
