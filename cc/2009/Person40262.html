Mike Maiorino’s Summary

Mike's directed sales and professional services for leading software vendors and small consulting firms including the top HR consulting firm for Sage Software. Mike's career also served in a number of Sales Executive positions at ADP, Sage Software, Kronos, SSA Global (fka as Infinium), and Art Technology Group. He is a member in Northern Virginia Chapter of SHRM (Society of Human Resource Management) and completed his certification as a PHR (Professional in Human Resources) in December, 2002.

HRMS Solutions has been named Spectrum Business Partner of the Year in 2008, Achieved Sage Software President Club Status in 2007 and 2008 while also achieving Ascentis Business Partner of the Year in 2006, 2007 and 2008. Mike was also recognized as Biltmore Who's Who in 2007 as one of Washington, DC most distinguished members.

Prior to launching HRMS Solutions, Inc, Mike co-founded HR Technology Partners, a HR consulting firm and value added reseller. Mike has also created partnerships with leading workforce management vendors including Spectrum HR, Sage Software, Deltek, Ascentis Corporation, HRSmart, Qqest Software, PeopleStrategy, Mangrove Software, Galaxy Technologies, Visibility Software and SinglePoint, Inc.
