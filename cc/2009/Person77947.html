Paul V Brown’s Summary

Paul has a long and value-added track record as a Technical Recruiter supporting equipment, service and software products for the fabrication of semiconductor chips, flat panels, and thin film solar and crystalline silicon photovoltaic cells, in addition to millimeterwave communications hardware. He has extensive corporate and agency recruiting experience and has a tremendous amount of fun while doing his work. He is a true business partner and strategic consultant to hiring managers and candidates alike while managing the full staffing cycle recruitment process.

Paul V Brown’s Specialties:

Paul's areas of expertise are in Hi-Tech sectors, including Semiconductor, Thin Film Solar and Crystalline Silicon Photovoltaics, Flat Panel Capital Equipment and Fabrication, RF/Millimeterwave Telecom products, Contract Mfg., and Consumer Electronics. Working directly on-site, he is influential while growing solid relationships with hiring managers and the candidates he works with. His influencing skills results in name generation and direct referrals of great candidates for Applied Materials.
