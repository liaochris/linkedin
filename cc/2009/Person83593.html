Richard Dudley’s Summary

Twenty five plus years of experience in international development work primarily with fisheries and other natural resources. Mostly in Indonesia, Oman, and parts of Africa. Current interest in applying systems modeling techniques to international and natural resource management problems. Interested in short or long term consulting contracts in these realms.

Richard Dudley’s Specialties:

Analysis of complex human - natural resource systems for improved natural resource management for sustainable use. Leadership of consulting teams working on natural resource and international development issues.
