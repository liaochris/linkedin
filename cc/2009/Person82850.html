Regis Hadiaris’s Summary

Regis Hadiaris is an award-winning Internet marketer and experienced leader known for unconventional ideas and impressive results.

He has 12 years of experience leading hundreds of successful Internet marketing programs and teams for the financial, education, automotive, tourism, and consumer products industries.

Regis has a track record of achieving and exceeding marketing goals without spending additional money on advertising. Highly respected as a source of advice, Regis has been a standing-room only speaker at events like the eMetrics conference, published several articles on best practices for Internet marketing, and was featured in the book “The Persona Lifecycle – Keeping People in Mind Throughout Product Design.”

Dot Connector, Regis’ blog about leadership, productivity, and being successful at work, is recognized by the 9 Rules blog network as one of the best sources of independent content online. His ideas have received acclaim from companies such as Google and Hewlett Packard, as well as marketing experts like Mark Stevens, the bestselling author of the book, “Your Marketing Sucks.”

Regis Hadiaris’s Specialties:

12 years of experience in leading online marketing projects and teams, with expertise in: direct response marketing, Regis' specific marketing expertise is in search engine marketing, social media, landing page optimization, A/B testing, multi-variable testing, web marketing analytics, online advertising, pay per click advertising, continuous user experience improvement, and piloting new technologies.
