Dale Carlton’s Summary

National Speaker and Consultant, speaking in approximately 20 states per year and to over 50,000 people since 1990. Dale Carlton the Senior Instructor for Carlton Seminars has personal sales of over $100 million in the field of Real Estate. Prior to starting Carlton Seminars, Dale was the Sr. Vice President & Executive Broker for Lindsey & Assoc., Inc., and helped lead the company to over $700 million in sales in 2005.

Dale Carlton who is also an Attorney, is a Senior Instructor with the Council of Residential Specialists, is a certified instructor with the Arkansas State Board of Private Career Education, and teaches with the Graduate of REALTOR® Institute, Arkansas Institute of Real Estate, Lorman Education Services, and Sterling Education Services, Inc. He has taught throughout the nation in areas such as: San Francisco, Corpus Christie, Memphis, Detroit, Lexington, Naples, Las Vegas, Miami, Wichita, Ft. Worth, Colorado Spings, Honolulu the University of Arkansas, the University of Notre Dame and many, many more.

Dale is not only an experienced educator, but he is actively working within the fields that he consults, speaks and educates.

Dale Carlton’s Specialties:

Marketing Expertise, Business Planning, Ethics Training, Business Policy, Real Estate Marketing, Real Estate Investments, Legal Advice, Strength Based Marketing, Wealth Building, 1031 Tax-Deferred Exchanges, Foreclosures, Marketing Budgets, and many more.
