Mark Whiting’s Summary

Mark is a young designer and thinker with experience in consulting for project and business strategy, reducing cultural barriers in Asia-Western collaborations, and providing rich technical insight with a background in prototyping, 3D, research based design and mathematics with associated logic and theory.

Mark is interested in everything, is addicted to learning and implementing ideas and in the past few years has had a growing started trying new languages such as Korean, Mandarin, and French.

Mark is now studying for a Masters in Design Management at KAIST in Korea and after its completion he hopes to start a small design and thinking consulting firm to operate internationally and propagate good design implementation.

Mark Whiting’s Specialties:

Design strategy, design thinking, development strategy, business consulting, ethnographic research, international exposure and communication, Chinese Language (Mandarin), problem solving strategies, coaching, collaborating locally and in distributed groups.

Solidworks, Luxology Modo, Google SketchUp, Rhino 3D, Shake, procedural graphics, software and workflow workshops in CAID, communication and problem disambiguation using information mapping and IBIS with tools such as Compendium.
