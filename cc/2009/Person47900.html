Joseph Matheny’s Summary

Some may say that my career spans varied fields and that is true enough, however I would add that I have a multi-discipline approach that is necessary in the modern age. As Robert Heinlein once said, "Specialization is for insects".

Some of the things I have achieved as an inventor, creator, marketer, product manager, CEO, CTO, writer and artist include: Playing a role in establishing and evangelizing standards and practices such as CD ROM, PDF, DVD, XML, RSS, Podcasting, ARG and digital video. I am the inventor of of several matching and recommendation algorightms. I am a published author of screenplays, white papers, technology, sci-fi, marketing and gaming books.

"Joseph Matheny possesses a very rare combination of creative vision, authentic leadership, and pure guts. If you have a compelling new product opportunity to bring to market, I can't think of a better field general than Joe. " Lex Sisney, CEO ELC Technologies.
