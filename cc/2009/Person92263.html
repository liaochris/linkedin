Simon Lewis’s Summary

Journalism professional for more than a decade, writing, editing and producing work for newspapers, wire services, agencies, magazines and websites in the UK, Ireland and United States.

Has reported on major sporting championships and events across four continents in football, golf, tennis, boxing, track and field, Formula One, horse racing, rugby and cricket and covered sports from baseball to ice yachting.

In addition to sport, has had published travel features, business news, lifestyle, music, fitness and health stories.

Simon Lewis’s Specialties:

Writing, reporting, editorial and media production; website development and project management; business administration and management (Irish Management Institute Diploma – Young Managers Programme for High Potential Managers completed in 2000)
