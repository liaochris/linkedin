Thomas Green’s Summary

Who am I ?

--- Well to be honest I am an absolute nobody, that has the strange yet wonderful ability to make friends in a variety of unusual fields.

I often say that I've dabbled in so many areas of the entertainment industry and related fields for so many years now, that "some people" seem to think I'm a professional.
(Silly people!.)

Of course this ability has often brought people together, and I have seen some wonderful projects and creations come into existence because of that skill.

I feel blessed to have so many friends in so many worlds and I hope that I have made their dreams and lives better. I hope to continue in bringing forth new ideas (both mine and others) into existence, and of course make a good bit of money in the process.

But what I most hope to achieve is to make existence on this rock we call a planet, better for everyone that I happen to meet along the way. After all, Life is way too short and precious to waste it away by not enjoying it all with your friends.
