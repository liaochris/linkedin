Fabian Gonzalez’s Summary

Management consultant in the area of information governance and privacy; and experienced IT auditor. Deep experience in designing, implementing and auditing information systems and information management controls for Fortune 500 clients in the financial services; electronics, software and services; and healthcare industries.

Most recently, I have assisted my clients in performing large scale security and privacy assessments (general, GAPP, and HIPAA), deploying full-scale privacy programs, performing SAS 70 audits, ISO 17799 audits, general IT reviews, and Sarbanes-Oxley compliance assistance services.

Representative clients include: Visa Inc., Ariba, Wind River Systems, Inc., Yahoo!, Google, Varian Medical Systems, and Gap Inc.

Fabian Gonzalez’s Specialties:

Information systems audit, security, and privacy consulting for Fortune 500 clients, Sarbanes-Oxley compliance, Certified Information Systems Auditor (CISA), Certified Information Security Manager (CISM), Certified Information Privacy Professional (CIPP), and a CIPP/Information Technology (CIPP/IT)
