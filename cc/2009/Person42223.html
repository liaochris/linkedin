Gallup’s StrengthsFinder psychological profiling* - My Top 5 Strengths:

1. Strategic – Faced with any given problem, they can quickly spot the relevant patterns, anticipate problems, and create alternative solutions. Problem-solvers.

2. Deliberative – Take serious care in making decisions or choices, and anticipate obstacles well. Serious and mature.

3. Relator – Enjoys close relationships with others, and find deep satisfaction in working hard with friends to achieve a goal. Highly loyal and ethical.

4. Focus – Highly goal-oriented, they first determine priorities and then use these as a compass for all activities. Efficient.
