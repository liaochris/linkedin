Bernard Leong’s Summary

I am currently in the private sector mainly on interactive digital media space, both as an entrepreneur specializing in online social networks (former COO, SENATUS - building & growing the platform) and investor under Thymos Capital LLP on online social networks, mobile and web applications. My experience is mainly in product and business development of online social networks and web-mobile apps.

Formerly an academic and research scientist, specializing in computational biology, theoretical physics, economics and strong interest in public policy. I have published papers in prestigous journals on these fields (ApJ, Physical Review D, Cell).
