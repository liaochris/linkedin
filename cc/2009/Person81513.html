Ragan Kellams, PHR’s Summary

Since 2002 Ragan has been passionate about perfecting the art of the connection - whether connecting a job seeker to an employer or connecting with contacts across the globe through the evolving web.

Social media and emerging technologies provide a new transparency for employers and employees and the barriers once caused by the need for face-time and handshakes are now non-existent as a result. Living and breathing these technologies has provided an invaluable asset as an executive recruiter and human resource professional in the digital and traditional advertising realms.

Ragan's expertise lies at the intersection of talent acquisition, social media strategy for the employer brand and a forward view to generational evolutions and motivators.

To contact directly email ragan@iqinteractive or rkadgirl@hotmail.com.
