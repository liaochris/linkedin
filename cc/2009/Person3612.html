Alex Sysoef’s Summary

An IT pro with several years of experience behind me I have played around with building websites since 1999. Learning in process I have tried and used many popular CMS and blog platforms with Typo3 becoming my personal choice for a CMS when I need to build a professional sites and WordPress as a platform of choice for blogs.

But as I became more knowledgeable with the raw power and customizability of WordPress I have found that it can be so much more then simple blog, as used by many people. WordPress can be easily turned into a fully equipped Web 2.0 portal unmatched by any others.

On pages of my blog I share my personal experience, technical know-how and marketing strategies to earn from your WordPress blog. What I already know as a Webmaster and learn as blogger I share freely and hope that information I provide will help you reach the level of success you are looking for.

Being involved into Internet Marketing since 2005 I always search for ways to combine knowledge and gained experience from several crucial areas and melt it into formula to earn full time income while working at your own leisure.

Web 2.0 Wealth System was a direct result of my knowledge and experience combined into most comprehensive self study video tutorials and complete blog system. Best of the WordPress plugins integrated into custom theme and ready to monetize enables even complete newbies to become bloggers with a professionally looking platform.
