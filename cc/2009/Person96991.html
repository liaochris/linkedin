Taylor Anderson, PE’s Summary

Professional engineer with more than a decade of experience in civil engineering for land development projects in Wisconsin, Georgia and North Carolina. A client oriented leader with strong business development skills and proven ability to provide successful project management.

Service on several community oriented committees, including Gwinnett County's Department of Transportation SPLOST committee that included 8 citizens that assisted the County in directing more than $160 million in tax funding on local transportation projects. Serve on Gwinnett County's Stormwater Technical Advisory Committee, assisting the County in reviewing and writing stormwater management regulations. Other committees include Council for Quality Growth, Gwinnett Parking Lot Ordinance Advisory, and Cape Fear Home Builders Association Land Council.

Presently serve as Chairman of Gwinnett County's Committee writing new stormwater regulations for redevelopment projects. Also serve as a member of Gwinnett Chamber of Commerce Economic Development Council. Appointed by the Gwinnett County Board of Commissioners as the Professional Engineer representative on the Development Advisory Committee.

Taylor Anderson, PE’s Specialties:

Licensed PE in Georgia, North Carolina and South Carolina. Also hold an NCEES record which provides the ability to expedite professional engineering liscensure.

Level II Certified Erosion Control Design Professional in Georgia.

A keen interest and specialty in redevelopment projects, particularly solving complex stormwater issues.
