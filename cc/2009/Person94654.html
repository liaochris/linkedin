Dr Steve Dunjey’s Summary

I am a specialist in Emergency Medicine..awarded my FACEM in 1991, and I have a special interest in teaching, and in Ultrasound ( sub specialty training completed in 2007, Diploma of Diagnostic Ultrasound) I currently work at the 2 largest teaching hospitals in Perth, part time. I also work a day a week at the womens hospital, and have done since 2003, doing acute gynae emergency work in the ED. I trained in WA, but have worked as a specialist at the Royal Brisbane, and also at the King Khaled Hospital, in Jedda, Saudi Arabia.
