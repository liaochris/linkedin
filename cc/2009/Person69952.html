    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    August 1986 — June 2008 (21 years 11 months)

    Since 1986, Monica Doss has served as President & CEO of the Council for Entrepreneurial Development (CED), a non-profit organization founded in 1984 to stimulate the creation and expansion of high-growth entrepreneurial businesses in North Carolina. Doss is responsible for spearheading the direction, operation and overall management of the organization. During her 20-year tenure, CED has grown to become the largest entrepreneurial support organization in the United States, with 4000+ members representing 1100 member companies, and an annual budget of $2 million.
