Elliot Blake’s Summary

Emmy Award-winning Writer and Producer of animation, new media, and comics, including the animated web series “Re\Visioned: Tomb Raider” and “Space Ghost Coast to Coast,” and the comic book story “Mekano Turbo,” featured in the upcoming Image Comics anthology “Popgun, Vol. 4.”

Elliot Blake’s Specialties:

animation producing; budget creation and maintenance; schedule creation; writing; story-editing; voice directing;production supervising
