Steve Thorn’s Summary

Steve Thorn has over 17 years of multimedia design, 3D graphics and web development experience.
His works have been shown in virtual and physical art galleries, his animations have been seen on ESPN, QVC and various local networks. He has developed websites for major corporations, government installations and is currently a Sr. Web Developer for a $24 Billion corporation.
He has created e-learning courses for Microsoft and Cisco certifications, and also Human Factors Engineering and Acquisition training for the Air Force.
His Flash-driven applications have been used by scientists and researchers worldwide.

He is an entrepreneur and freelance contractor that is also a published author of one novel and various short stories and novellas.

He can be contacted via his website: http://www.stevethorn.com

Steve Thorn’s Specialties:

E-learning developer for consumer and government clients, developer of award-winning applications in Flash, ColdFusion and PHP.
