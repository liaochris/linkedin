Peter Bowen’s Summary

Master of Business Administration with 15 Years of Experience in Management, System Administration, and Software Engineering. My teams consistently engineer and implement complicated and difficult projects correctly, on budget, and on time.

Currently, I manage the web hosting business for a large Internet Service Provider.

I manage change well, having guided my teams in four organizations through ownership changes.

I understand the requirements for enterprise level software, having led the cross-functional teams that built the provisioning platform for an e-commerce centric hosting platform, transitioned a Health Care Information System from a simple client/server system to an enterprise class system, and improved the billing process, reporting, and accuracy of a large web hosting provider.

Individually I implemented the advertising engine for the Excite e-commerce offering, maintained a product aggregation/search engine, wrote a perl orb interface, rewrote the perl interface to the XM personal radio, and returned two ceramic studios to profitability.

Perl (OO & mod_perl), C++, SQL, Java, Livewire/JavaScript, UML/OO Methodology, CORBA, MySQL, Oracle, SQL Server, and PICK (UniVerse).
