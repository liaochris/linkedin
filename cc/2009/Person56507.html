Krista Paul’s Summary

TravelFli is an online company that solves problems for frequent flyers. Our core applications help users manage over 150 frequent flyer accounts, search for award availability and book award flights.

Travelfli was part of the 2008 Techstars program (www.techstars.org) in Boulder, Colorado. It a mentorship-driven program that chooses ten companies per summer and provides them with the best access to serial entrepreneurs and venture capitalists in Colorado and across the United States.

Please sign up to learn more about our launch at www.travelfli.com.
