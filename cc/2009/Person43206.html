Jamey Clayberg’s Summary

Breadth is the word I would use to describe my professional experience, I've worked in print design, textiles, point of sale, branding, web design, illustration, music and audio production, gift product design, book design, photography, cinematography, animation and creative consulting.

Breadth is wonderful to me because I get the chance to work (and play) in tons of fresh new things all the time, life and work stays interesting.

My goals are to keep growing, keep learning, to keep moving forward.
