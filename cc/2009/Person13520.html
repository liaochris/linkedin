Brian Gottlieb’s Summary

Brian Gottlieb founded Got-Focus LLC, a full service public opinion research firm. Got-Focus LLC specializes in public policy, politics, corporate image, reputation and crisis management. Mr. Gottlieb has experience developing and conducting successful quantitative and qualitative research strategies to match his clients’ individual needs. Got-Focus LLC works for national, local, political, corporate, and association clients. Got-Focus LLC is a Republican firm, and works closely with Democratic consultants on bipartisan corporate projects.

Mr. Gottlieb has more than 15 years of experience working in and around Capitol Hill, public policy and politics. Serving as a Legislative Assistant and Senior Legislative Assistant to former U.S. Representative Peter G. Torkildsen (R-MA), during the 103rd and 104th Congresses, he was the Congressman’s principal policy advisor on issues ranging from environmental protection to transportation and infrastructure. Since then, Mr. Gottlieb has held positions in Federal Government Relations for both the American Society for Microbiology and the American Chemistry Council (formerly the Chemical Manufacturers Association). Most recently, Mr. Gottlieb was a Vice President with Voter/Consumer Research (V/CR) in Washington, DC.

Mr. Gottlieb received his focus group moderator training from the Riva Training Institute in Bethesda, Maryland. He holds a Master of Arts degree from American University´s School of Public Affairs and a Bachelor of Arts degree from Hobart College. He has lectured at Hobart and William Smith Colleges, and George Washington University.

Brian Gottlieb’s Specialties:

Public opinion research, ballot initiatives, political campaigns, corporate image, issue management and strategic consulting.
