Todd Davies’s Summary

After 20 years of advisory and consulting experience helping leaders to devise and implement major change programs I am now specialising in pushing the boundaries of converging strategy, risk, governance and assurance with a particular emphasis on resilience, strategic risk intelligence and emerging technologies.
