Nick Whitmoyer’s Summary

Nick Whitmoyer is a Senior Consultant and founder of Whitmoyer, a user experience web studio. With over ten years of experience, Nick has had the opportunity to work with clients from a variety of industries and realizes the value in staying ahead of trends, especially when it comes to creating synergy between branding and a dynamic online presence. As a Senior Consultant, Nick focuses on providing online marketing strategies that combine memorable tactics with measurable results. With a passion for design, he begins all projects with a phase of close client collaboration and broad creative exploration, allowing clients to take a strategic time-out.

Whitmoyer has recently completed work for Retirement Living TV, The Motley Fool, Art Directors Club of Metropolitan Washington, Tinsel Cinema, Nclud, DBC*PR, and Fuszion Collaborative.
 
Nick studied graphic design at Bradley Academy for the Visual Arts and has been an enthusiastic member of the design community ever since. He serves as Website Chair and board member of the Art Directors Club of Metropolitan Washington in addition to teaching web design part-time at Boston University Center for Digital Arts and Imaging. Most recently, he has been able to combine his love of typography and web design as Co-Editor of FullBleed, a web magazine for design professionals. Nick can also be found rubbing elbows at various design and technology events nationwide.

CSS, XHTML, PHP, Web Standards, Information Architecture, Wireframes, Web Design, Graphic Design, User Interface Design, User Interface Development, Interactive Design, Online Marketing, Front-end Web Development
