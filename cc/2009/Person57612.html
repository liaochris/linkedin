Lars Kongshem’s Summary

Internet and media executive with more than 15 years of experience in online business strategies, web content development, and online & print publishing. Solution-driven innovator with strong skills in strategic planning, operations management, and relationship building. Award-winning journalist and editor with deep domain expertise in technology issues.

• Built a high-performing team of 15 online content, community, and web production professionals to reinvent and manage 3,000+ marketing web pages for the world’s fourth largest software company.
• Led the website content integration strategy for Symantec's $10.25 billion acquisition of Veritas Software, one of the largest software company mergers in history.
• Developed and executed the editorial strategy for the launch of a national technology magazine at Scholastic Inc.
