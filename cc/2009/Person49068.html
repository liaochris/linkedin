John Head’s Summary

John has worked with Lotus technologies since 1992. He first began with Lotus Ami Pro 2.0. Subsequently, he has done custom development for Lotus SmartSuite, Microsoft Office, OpenOffice.org and Lotus Notes providing custom development, administration, and training for numerous clients. John specializes in and is the recognized resource in the Lotus Business Partner community for automation of desktop applications from within Lotus Notes.

John is talented at transitioning Customer Business Processes into Applications. He is also skilled in resource allocation and project management.

John Head’s Specialties:

Business Application Development, Software Application Architecture, Lotus Notes Client Development, Lotus Notes Web Development, Integration of Microsoft Office and other Microsoft Technologies with Lotus Notes/Domino, Microsoft Office Development, OpenOffice.org Development
