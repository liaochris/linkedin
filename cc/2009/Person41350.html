Vic Gaffney’s Summary

Vic's professional exposure encompasses more than 30 years of experiences, and as such, compiling this summary is somewhat arduous at best and perhaps self serving at worst! So here goes:

☆ Corporate consultant in management, quality assurance, marketing & the development/implementation of advertising strategies. High premium on providing preemptive legal support in English and Hindi. Focus on international & constitutional law, intellectual property, contract drafting, governmental liaison & negotiation, workplace health & safety, quality assurance management systems (TQM) & auditing, etc ...

☆ Education consultant providing vocational guidance, counseling, admissions and visa application services for South Asian students that require study pathways into universities and colleges in countries (especially): Australia, New Zealand, USA, Canada, European Union, England, Scotland, Ireland, and other destinations, etc ...

☆ Employment consultant & broker specializing in placements for professionals (from India) who qualify for "Industry in Demand" sectors abroad. Jobs available include experienced teachers (BEd/MEd), IT professionals (all disciplines), engineers (all disciplines), medical/dental professionals, etc...

☆ Immigration consultant for all types of visas, including H1b, L1, green card, overseas employment, sponsorships, spouse & family reunion, business investment (inbound/outbound), skills category, sports category, etc ...

☆ Advertising & marketing consultant, specializing in campaign management, point of sale (PoS) creation & design, corporate presentations & submissions, business cards, letterheads, logos, emblems, catchy/memorable mottos, creative scriptwriting & editing, print media & multimedia presentations, website design & hosting, etc ...

☆ Legal advocacy, providing advice, case co-ordination, dispute resolution, and specialist consultant in business, international, criminal & family law, etc ...
