Tracey Vincent’s Summary

Postdoctoral fellow at the Technion Institute, investigating metal oxide nanostructures for artifical photosynthesis.

Enthusiastic teacher and researcher - a range of publications and presentations completed - drawing from a broad knowledge base including material science, mechanical engineering and electrical engineering.

Several years industry experience as a microwave (radio-frequency electrical) design engineer. Skills developed and demonstrated during this time: creative and efficient problem solving, project management, and teamworking.
