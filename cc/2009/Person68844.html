Here is more info about who I am:
Based in Provo, Utah, at the foot of the beautiful Wasatch Mountains, Mike Watson Investing is a groundbreaking educational institution and real estate investing company. We currently serve over 5000 students across the country and are experiencing rapid growth despite the challenges of today’s market.
The wealth we achieve with our student body is a result of a proven investing system. This holistic approach to investing creates personal and financial freedom each day as we invest in our own deals and partner with students nationwide. We believe hands-on experience is essential in the education of our students, and have found it indispensable to our mutual prosperity.
Our vision for our students extends far beyond their education. Although partnering with our students on their investments enhances their short-term educational experience, our ultimate goal is to create unlimited,
synergetic prosperity through long-term partnerships. We’re not interested in partnering on one or two deals to help you get started; we’re fully invested in your success because we believe there is no limit to the wealth we can generate as an educated, visionary, and revolutionary investing community.

Our Mission
“To create and liberate partners by instilling in them financial prosperity, balanced lifestyle, career satisfaction, and freedom by providing world-class education and hands-on participation.”
