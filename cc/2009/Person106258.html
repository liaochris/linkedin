Zachary Alexander’s Summary

I founded ebTDesign in 2003 as the first online-media company devoted to P2P Economy success. At ebTDesign, we are dedicated to finding unique solutions for our clients that combine IT Investment leadership, post-Globalization marketing and branded strategic content.

In 2008, I started the Strategic Network for Industry Cluster Empowerment (SNICE) to bring together industry leaders and the top entrepreneur support professionals. Prior to founding ebTDesign and SNICE, I held Enterprise Architect and Senior Technical positions with AOL, Nextel, Neustar and other technology companies.

You can follow me at http://twitter.com/zalexander
