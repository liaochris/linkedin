Chelsea Blacker’s Summary

With over two years of search experience, my industry expertise spans both sides of the pond lending me a unique background that understands both the European and American online audiences.

With my roots firmly planted in SEO, I have expanded into social media and am currently monitoring SM avenues to asses how our clients are perceived online and of course, trying to mold that perception. Conversions, I believe, are the best way to measure search's effectiveness; the challenge is always deciding what will count as a conversion.

While I love a bit of PPC and believe it's a great way to up initial traffic, I advocate using PPC as a small part of a larger online marketing strategy. Conversions most often come form organic results hence SEO should be any organization''s top priority.
