Lionel FAUCHER’s Summary

I created Akamedia as a facilitator for the trade of video content:
- Producers can save costs in the distribution of their audiovisual production worldwide, while increasing their reach and their licensing revenues;
- Broadcasters can search, find and license on demand Premium content from a fast growing group of world class producers, while dramatically reducing their production costs by automating their rights clearing.

Lionel FAUCHER’s Specialties:

Content Distribution: Copyright Monetisation, Audiovisual Content Licensing & Delivery, Automated Rights Clearing, Premium Footage Procurement;
Media Asset Management & Video Distribution Solutions: Indexing, Sorting, Tagging;
Digital Rights Management & Security: Watermarking, Fingerprinting and Tracking.
