Kent Wakeford’s Summary

Over 12 years of entertainment, media and interactive experience, including running a digital media company, co-founding an ad technology and content rating company, leading sales / business development teams, general counsel at leading social media company, and attorney at national law firm. Expertise in structuring and negotiating all aspects of digital media partnerships, venture financing, and acquisition of digital / social media / technology companies.

Executive team member and co-founder of digital media and ad technology companies with lead role developing hundreds of relationships in the digital, social media, and ad technology space with media companies, sports leagues, social networks, search engines, ecommerce sites, ad networks and exchanges, data providers, and behavioral targeting companies.

Completed equity investments and/or warrant issuance in fourteen digital and social media companies. Sourced and negotiated the acquisition of four community-based online media properties. Helped negotiate and facilitate venture financing including angel investments, Series A and Series C investments. Lead legal negotiator for the acquisition of the leading online bookmarking platform.

Kent Wakeford’s Specialties:

Developing digital media strategies and partnerships with emphasis on online advertising technology, content syndication, cross-platform marketing relationships, advertising and ecommerce partnerships, and data acquisition. Sourcing, evaluating and structuring the sale and acquisition of digital media assets and companies.
