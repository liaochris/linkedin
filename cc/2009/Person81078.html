Patrick Wadlington’s Summary

Senior-level psychometrician. Have developed and maintained high stakes, large scale organizational assessments for multiple organizations.

Industrial/organizational psychological researcher. Have researched and implemented applications for various organizational psychological topics such as leadership, personality, selection, job satisfaction.

Industrial/organizational psychological consultant. Have consulted and handled I/O issues of individuals, teams, and organizations.

Education psychological researcher. Have researched and developed instrument to measure learning disability (e.g., dyslexia, discalculia) within children and adults. Have researched teaching styles within teachers.

Patrick Wadlington’s Specialties:

Psychometrics, Large-scale & high stakes organizational test development, Structural equation modeling (SEM), Item Response Theory(IRT), Personality research, Individual difference research, Selection profiling, Item tracking systems, Computer adaptive testing (CAT), Measurement Decision Theory (MDT)
