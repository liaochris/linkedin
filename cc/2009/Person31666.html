  • Owner

    November 2007 — Present (1 year 9 months)

However, friends and workmates encouraged us to make it available on the Internet, so that people, interested in financial market, could track the stock market through their mobile devices, everywhere. Thereafter, we have worked hard to turn M-Stocks App into a great application.
