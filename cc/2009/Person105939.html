Yoel Arnon’s Summary

After ten years as a developer at the Microsoft MSMQ team, I decided to resign and start my own venture.
My first goal is MSMQ related consulting - architecture, programming, support, training - you name it. If MSMQ is a central piece of your architecture, I can help you make the most out of it.
I can also help in the broader scope of enterprise messaging infrastructure - from technologies to the best implementation of business processes.
come visit me at www.msmq.biz

Yoel Arnon’s Specialties:

World class MSMQ (Microsoft Message Queue) expert.
Software engineering - design and implementation. .NET technologies, C++/VB/C#, WCF, Biztalk Server, SQL Server. MBA (Cum Laude) from the Technion, 2003.
