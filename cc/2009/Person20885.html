Craig Stover’s Summary

Painter, printmaker, sculptor. Learn more: www.picturemaker.com.

Additionally, I am the Executive Director of Allens Lane Art Center ~ Philadelphia, PA. The Center serves a wide variety of community constituents with a dance studio, an arts studio, an art gallery, a full community theater, and a very popular summer camp for kids. Learn more: www.allenslane.org.

Craig Stover’s Specialties:

I am a professional artist with an accessible studio and gallery near the Philadelphia Museum of Art that is open by appointment.

A variety of technology skills: web development, digital imaging, technology assessments, recommendations and planning.

I lecture on art history, branding for the arts, and non-profit best practices as well as teach painting, drawing and printmaking.
