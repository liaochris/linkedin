Nicolò Michetti’s Summary

Nicolò is in charge of Digital PR Milan office.

He has specific experience in the development of internet communication plans and web communication strategy. He has an extensive experience in managing web projects, also at an international level, online strategy and integration with offline, from definition and development of business opportunities to the real planning management.

Nicolò is fluent in French and also speaks English and Spanish.

Nicolò Michetti’s Specialties:

Nicolò serves since February 2002 Digital PR, the new media agency fully focused on digital communication and part of the Hill & Knowlton network.
Digital PR has become in the last years a leading agency in Internet Relations services on the Italian and international market.
Among the clients of Digital PR are: Microsoft, Nokia, the Fiat Group, GSK, Danone, ING Direct, L'Oréal, etc.
