Jim Liddle’s Summary

Experienced Sales professional with vast experience in direct enterprise Sales, Channels and Partnerships, specialising in Finance, Online Gambling and Betting, Media and Telco, but also having sold into Retail and Manufacturing industries.

I have contacts, and sold into, many organisations, both in the UK, Europe, New York, and AsiaPac such as: Goldman Sachs, UBS, Deutsche Bank, Bank of America, Calyon, Merrill Lynch, France Telecom, British Telecom, Colt Telecom, Vodafone, IG Index, O2, British American Tobacco, H3G, IG Index etc.

I have lived and worked in both the US (New York and San Francisco) and in Japan during my career and have contacts in both countries as well as a large networks of contacts throughout central europe.

Experience in building organisations from the ground up with several years experience of a combination of MD and Sales Director roles involving Regional, European, and International Management.

I am used to being the first employee for a region and have successfully done this for companies who are located in the USA, India and Israel.

I've been the top salesman in all the organisations I have worked for, exceeding quota both from a personal and regional perspective.

Strong technology professional with technical knowledge and expertise in Grid Computing, DataGrid, HPC, Data Caching, Cloud Computing, JEE, .Net, ESB, SOA, J2ME / MIDP, Iphone, Business Rules and virtualisation.

A true sales product specialist and able to combines sales with pre-sales work as well as offering high value consultancy.

Published Author with many Technology articles published as well as technical authorship of marketing and product related collateral for a variety of companies.

Excellent presentation skills and have presented at various venues, including Java One, CityGrid and specialist Sales conferences.
