Steve Berczuk’s Summary

Expert in agile software development and release management with experience delivering enterprise applications through multiple release cycles. Skilled at leading teams, mentoring team members, developing architecture, design, and requirements, and establishing lightweight software configuration management and release processes to enable organizations to build stable products quickly. Author of Software Configuration Management Patterns: Effective Teamwork, Practical Integration (Addison-Wesley ) I do contract software development and coaching, and consulting on development, design, and SCM Process issues, and have been very successful in helping teams be more effective by through process improvements and technical leadership.
