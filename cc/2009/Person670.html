Richard Abbott’s Summary

Aircraft structures consultant with experience of development and life extension programs for Part 23 and Part 25 composite/metalllic civil and military aircraft.
Worked at senior technical level managing stress, loads, liaison and structural compliance functions. I have contracted and consulted in the UK, Europe, Canada and the USA.
