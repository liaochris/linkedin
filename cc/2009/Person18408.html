Christie Shinn’s Summary

I currently am working freelance with a few projects at the moment, but would love to get a permanent, full-time or part time position in doing what I love.

Currently, I am working on Moddb with Seven-- a Half Life 2 and Red Alert 3: Southern Rising as a concept artist with both mods.

My continuing clients are with: Olive Jane with release paperwork and mailers as well as patterns for their women's clothing line- Retro Resort 2008 as well as assisting them with their website. I also work with List Caddy for graphic design for print packaging as well as original designs to be published ON their products. Currently, I am in the process in Workout On the Go's product design for a fitness show targeted for children in their concept and design department. I am also a Production Assistant with Ethereal Muse for their upcoming game, Encore.

I have done Character Concepts with ArcNovo, Inc. for their upcoming children's animation as well as designed Flash Ad banners for IMS Barter's ad campaign for Forbes.com. I have just completed SON's hip-hop album cover for Kingdom.


I am currently looking for something that will constantly challenge me and to grow bigger and better as an artist and as a person. I know I have the skills to implement it, but I hunger for the knowledge to acquire more. I do not mind if it is a long-term position or a freelance. Every bit helps.

Christie Shinn’s Specialties:

Production art: character concept, environment concept with Photoshop and Illustrator. 3D modeling using 3DS Max. I have also have experience in Flash animation and other forms of classical 2D animation. I have done texturing as well.

Oh yeah, for the admins in you, I also have expertise in Excel and the rest of Microsoft Office as well.

Fashion: Textile and pattern design, line and color swatch sheets, logo, and web graphics work and update.
