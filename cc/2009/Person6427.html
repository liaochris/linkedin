Angus McRae, CEBS’s Summary

Employee benefits consultants who specialize in providing coverage for dynamic, rapid-growth employers. We are happy to work with founders, consultants and venture capitalists as they develop a venture's business plan. We will install an employee benefits package, manage those plans through the hyper-growth stages, and on through the exit of the founders.

Angus McRae, CEBS’s Specialties:

We will serve any industry, but most of our clients are high-tech / engineering companies, physician / medical practices, legal practices and executive search firms.
