  • Coordinating Networking Organizer

    S*E*N*S*A

    (Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    August 2005 — September 2006 (1 year 2 months)

    During this period I prepared and branded this concept and idea, but this idea has been retwisted, refocused and revamped to suit more research than networking needs through iÆREAS.

    S*E*N*S*A means

    [SUSTAINABILITY] * [ENTREPRENEURSHIP] * [NETWORKING] * [SUPPORT] * [ASSOCIATION]

    This association had the intention and purpose to amplify and accelerate existing sustainopreneurial ventures in survival, emerging and developed economies globally in inter-local and multi-local activities. Instead of multiplying the works associated to the association, it was meant to have replications of good practices to be done, with culturally adapted franchises.

    It was intended that the synergy and leverage that came from unifying the existing initiatives should increase the power of the associated processes by coming together and collectively and collaboratively share experiences and energy in a plus-sum game.
