Kent McDonald’s Summary

Accomplished strategic thinker, consulting specialist, business leader, and MBA with 13+ years of experience elevating organizational performance through expert orchestration of operational restructuring and improvements. Reputable for providing leading organizations with cutting-edge IT solutions. Identify areas in need of improvements and introduce tools, training strategies, and operational optimization techniques.
