Chris Butler’s Summary

15+ years of well-rounded, progressive and hands-on experience in Product and Technology Management, Business Management and Marketing.

- Experienced in Carrier Network Management Systems for DSL access, IP Core Ethernet networks, Optical Ethernet, Packet (SIP-based VoIP) and Circuit switched telephony for Carrier networks. Developed electronic collateral for account teams.

- Handled and directed technical questions and customer bid responses from Account teams on all issues pertaining to OSS and NMS including scalable deployment architectures and security.

- Prepared and delivered Technical and Sales training seminars to Sales and Sales Engineering personnel (90+ participants).

- Knowledgeable in scalable, Java 2EE-based multi-server OSS and Network Element Management Solutions, equally knowledgeable in hardware platforms.

- Experienced in working with offshore teams based in Europe, India and China.

- Highly successful in initiating product development outsourcing plans worth approximately US$ 18M per year. Developed selection criteria and led business arrangement negotiations with finalists.

- Experienced in analyzing conceptual business or technology thrusts to develop business cases and product plans.

- Developed incremental and profitable revenue streams through combination of new high-speed Internet access products, geographic expansion (United States) and new channels to market.

- Managed several Products in terms of life cycle – cradle to end of life.

- Holder of two (2) Patent awards.
