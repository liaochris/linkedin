Gerald Deutsch’s Summary

Business Analyst focused on the banking & finance industry with special expertise regarding credit risk management and the regulatory and compliance issues involved as well as the technical background to efficiently communicate requirements and overall system design to ICT departments.

As my main assets I'd personally see my strong Math and ICT background, design experience regarding data models for large DWH type systems and software systems built around these models (i.e. responsible for architecture and design of the Commezbank Basel II RWA calculation engine for their Basel II simulation environment), my solid understanding of CR portfolio models (Credit Risk+, Credit Metrics, KMV) or more general Merton and Vasicek models and 5 years+ of project experience, implementing Basel II AIRB for large multinational (top 20) institutes in Germany & The Netherlands.

Being used to work in international project teams I feel comfortable with communication in "English only" or "English/local mixed" environments and actually do enjoy working with diverse people with different cultural backgrounds
