Rob Benjamin, CGEIT’s Summary

I am a hands-on business and technology consulting practice leader, writer, and coach experienced in multiple industries and business functions.

* Certified in Governance of Enterprise Information Technology

* A published writer and speaker on business and technology alignment, operational and project risks, organizational excellence, and business process management

* A proven record of success in identifying, assessing, prioritizing, and managing risks and improvement opportunities in financial controls, business operations, and techhology projects

I help financial and operational sponsors of strategic technology investments pick those that offer the greatest gains in business value consistent with their tolerance for reasonable risk. I help them manage both value and risks from beginning to end, through

* Due diligence for business cases, vendors, technologies, mergers and acquisitions, and partnerships

* Independent, unbiased assessment of high-risk, high-value technology projects

* Support for strategic business process improvement initiatives
