Vanessa Smith’s Summary

Wellness Coach who believes health is the foundation of success, rather than one aspect of our lives. My mission and passion is to educate, inspire, and empower people to live healthy, successful, and fulfilling lives.

Professional experience includes speaking, writing, resource development, leadership training, life coaching and health education. My specialty is in the combination of health, intuition, and coaching so you can truly let your life bloom.


GOALS:
• To offer an Integrative Wellness Works coaching certificate program specializing in the combination of holistic health education, intuition development, and life coaching.

• Create a non-profit empowering young girls to be leaders and find their unique voice through health, intuition, and coaching.

• Bestselling author of several books and programs on health, coaching, and intuition

• Have wildly successful group coaching programs


HOW I USE MY EXPERTISE:
• Help you establish a foundation of health to increase your quality of life, your ability to think clearly, and your effectiveness at what you do.

• Develop and trust your intuition so you can make the best choice for your regardless of the circumstance.

• Use life coaching to help you learn how to create change you desire and flow with the changes that life brings.


ALSO PASSIONATE ABOUT:
• Supporting local farmers and keeping the integrity of our food systems organic.

• Just about all things green, eco, sustainable

• Clean water and access to it
