Thibault Masson’s Summary

Experienced in internet marketing, I value entrepreneurial spirit and I am curious to hear about new ventures.
After starting in banking (1998), I branched out toward my real passion, i.e. internet marketing. First, I worked in a company selling consumer credit online (1999). Then, I joined BroadVision, a software company specialized in e-commerce platforms and stayed on for 3 years, until 2003.
After this valuable experience, I chose to get back to school and earn my MBA in the US, at the Kellogg School of Management. After an internship at Orbitz in 2004, I came back to France to join ebookers.fr, Orbitz's sister company in Europe, as Marketing Manager.
After 2 successfull years of learning and generating additional sales, I have chosen to launch my own venture, Fashion Fox. This new company is an online marketing consultancy helping fashion companies sell more and market better online.
My immediate goal is to get fashion industry members and online marketing experts to know about Fashion Fox's free newsletter about the impact of online marketing on the fashion industry.
