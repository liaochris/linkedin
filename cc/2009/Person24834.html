Davina Haisell’s Summary

Davina is a proofreader, editor, writer, personal development blogger, and a life coach.

Life coaching pretty much defies description. That’s why I offer the first 30 minutes to clients FREE to connect with the experience, and discover how it could benefit them.

Change won’t happen in a person’s life if they make choices blindly while living on autopilot. You can coast through life with no real direction, or you can choose a direction and reach your destination… faster! That’s life coaching.

Making a conscious decision to change your attitude and become inspired about life will affect other people. Imagine your impact!

I have a background in advertising where I was employed as a proofreader for 20 years. Making copy clear, correct, in context and consistent is my specialty.

I format books and ebooks. I’ve proofed menus for Boston Pizza, advertisements for TELUS and BC Hydro, and packaging for Sun-Rype to name a few.

One colleague says, “Davina understands the bigger picture significance of quality control. Without her dedication and diligence, it wouldn't have been so easy for us to deliver consistent brand experiences for our clients and their customers.”

Launching a personal development blog has satisfied my insatiable love for writing. It has also given me the pleasure of supporting other writers and bloggers who have been thankful for my expert editing and proofreading services.

One client says, “You did an excellent job with my last post. I couldn't believe how many things you caught and how much better the post read after your edits.”

I encourage you to visit my websites or blog, or join me on Twitter at http://twitter.com/DavinaH.

I look forward to connecting with you.
