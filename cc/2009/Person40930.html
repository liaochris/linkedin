Michael Wynn’s Summary

I'm running Icona Inc, where my creative and talented crew crank out an endless array of websites, print materials, tradeshow displays, CMS systems, eComm solutions, sage advice and fresh thinking. We're constantly busy juggling our huge client list with tight deadlines and top notch work, and I like to think we've created a fun, energetic place to work.

Icona occupies a funky studio space in Calgary's East Village, and works with companies around the world, often teaming up with other marketing firms, media houses and talented freelancers.

As the President of Icona, I'm building a committed team of professionals focused on developing cutting edge custom web applications and print design material.

I'm also the owner of Active Image Studios Inc, my freelance design company, and a current partner in HotWings Resources (www.hotwings.ca).

I'm one of the lucky few who absolutely love what they do for a living.

Michael Wynn’s Specialties:

Some general areas would include: creative development, graphic & web design, complex print jobs, site management, marketing plans and creative briefs.
