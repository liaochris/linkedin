Chris Pickering’s Summary

Chris is a business coach for the Art of Business Ltd in Bristol. He has over 30 years experience directing and coaching small businesses.

His early experience was gained as an engineer at Lockheed in USA and later as a Lecturer at Southampton University where he achieved exceptional success attracting over £3m research funding and publishing more than 60 referred papers.

Over the years, he has accumulated considerable experience in delivering projects to business, higher education and the third sector throughout Europe and in the US.

Chris Pickering’s Specialties:

Business Coaching, Management Training, Research & Development, Project Management, Organistational and Information Capital
