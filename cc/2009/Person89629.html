Scott Merrick’s Summary

Former host of the bi-weekly podcast for educators and other interested listeners: Snacks4theBrain! at http://blogs.vanderbilt.edu/s4theb.

Presenter of educational workshops for teachers, including "Web 2.0 for Us," a weeklong hands-on immersion into the world of the Read/Write Web.

Author of Lives, "the pretty good American Novel," at http://lulu.com/scottmerrick and of "Scott Merrick's Songs for Alaska, Featuring the Last Frontier Band" at http://cdbaby.com/scottmerrick1.

ISTE Second Life Docent and Island Manager of the Blogger's Hut and the Podcasting Place, two resource-rich island buildings for sharing and highlighting superlative work in education.

I am a learner and an educator. Everything I do in life proceeds from these two habits of mind.

Scott Merrick’s Specialties:

Second Life networking and content creation, curriculum design and innovation, podcasting, Web publishing, video editing and Web publishing, Interactive Videoconferencing (IVC), guitar and mandolin, parenting
