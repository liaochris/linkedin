Chad Fullerton’s Summary

Hello there! My name is Chad Fullerton an interactive/UI designer in Ontario, Canada. Currently in my final year at McMaster University with a B.A. in Communication Studies & Multimedia. Expected to graduate April 2009. 3.8 GPA

I am a creative, self-motivated designer with experience in all aspects of interactive and user interface design
from concept to delivery. I build user experiences that are engaging, intuitive, and user centric. My design and
communication skills are complemented by my technical background, making me an adaptable collaborator in a
fast paced environment.

Please visit http://www.chadfullerton.com for samples of my work or view my CV at http://www.chadfullerton.com/cv.pdf

Need help with a media project? Looking for some advice? I am always reachable via e-mail and enjoy helping others with their startup projects whenever I find spare time. Feel free to contact me, I believe in an open community and good karma where developers/designers assist each other.
