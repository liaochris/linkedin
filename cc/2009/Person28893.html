Dylan Conroy’s Summary

Dylan Conroy attended UCSB and studied screenwriting while earning a BA in film and new media. Dylan has a certificate of Entertainment PR and screenwriting from UCLA. He is a graduate of the Act One Screenwriters program.

While at UCSB Dylan interned at The Santa Barbara Film Fest and Ivan Reitman's Montecito Pictures.

Dylan's first job was as an account exec with Howard Bragman of BNC. He worked with celebrities such as Stevie Wonder, The Red Hot Chili Peppers, Mark Cronin and Phil Rosenthal.

Dylan then served as Bruce Nash's personal Research Assistant in development. While at Nash he developed numerous reality television and feature film concepts.

Dylan left Nash to pursue his production experience as a Segment Producer on the Profiles TV (Amazing Race) Harpo (The Oprah Winfrey Show) joint production, The Big Give for ABC.

Dylan then joined Chris Usselman as a producer and development consultant on a reality show called The Hearts and Minds project which is currently under consideration at major productions companies and agencies.

Dylan now runs a successful consulting business where he helps clients develop, package, sell and produce reality content as well as manage other creative producers and writers.

Dylan has optioned shows to National Lampoon and Eco Maniacal Productions.

Dylan is a member of entertainment professional development groups including Connecting Reality and Act One. He is also the founder and president of Sigs in Entertainment, a networking group for Sigma Chi's in entertainment.

Dylan is also the Founder and CCO of FlashCity.com a new media company in the arena of celebrity gossip.

Dylan is now and financier @ Strata Productions where he manages a base of equity partners who invest in independent films.

Dylan is the twelfth most connected person in Entertainment on Linked In.
