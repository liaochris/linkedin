Cindy Prosser’s Summary

Cindy-Leigh Prosser is the proprietor of Attitude e-media and a freelance Visual Designer & Information Architect.

Located in Mandurah, Western Australia, Attitude service an array of international and local clientele.

Attitude e-media don't outsource projects. We engage international professionals of the highest caliber for consultation and software. As Chief Designer I hand code my websites, so you can be assured that your project has been custom made from beginning to end. This is demonstrated by the quality of work I produce. The finished product will result in your website having cross browser compatibility, constructed to W3C standards, accessibility tested, search engine friendly with the option of becoming mobile ready (for mobile devices such as phones, PDA's, etc). Attitude e-media value the integrity of the Internet, so we take pride in the way your website is built. That our clients continue to return is testimony to that!

Cindy has moderated & administered e-Business & Web Development forums, and had publications in various forms of media in regards to early web design, web standards, choosing a designer and freelancing.

ENVIRO FRIENDLY
The business sector is a major contributor to the level of greenhouse gas emissions in the atmosphere. However, we're unable to operate our businesses without electricity. So Attitude e-media has decided to invest in better operations that will nullify it's contribution to Global Warming, and our impact on the environment. We have begun this journey by choosing green energy to power our business premises & residences, offsetting our carbon footprint, only using energy efficient equipment & using environmentally responsible products!
