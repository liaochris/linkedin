Giovanni Gallucci’s Summary

Giovanni Gallucci designed, developed and deployed online communities when MySpace was no one's space, has used a Mac since before the “i” anything, and created vertical search engines when Google's creators were still undergrads. Today he is a social media expert, search marketer, public speaker, new media producer, connector & online brand evangelist who provides strategy for new developments in social media, search, online public relations and buzz marketing and functions as a strategic representative to top politicos, internet personalities, government, and forward-thinking companies interested in breaking new ground in social media.

Currently Giovanni is Viewzi.com’s social media ninja and is on the faculties of AdobeTV and xTrain.com providing training in social media, search engine optimization and podcasting. In addition to these ventures, Giovanni produces ViewziTV, posts “almost daily” on his blog TheAgencyBlog.com and provides online training in social media and podcasting at SocialMediaStrategies.TV and SecretsOfPodcasting.TV.

Giovanni volunteers his time to several professional/community organizations like Barcamp, Brookhaven College’s CIS Advisory Board, the Gerson Lehrman Group, DFWIMA (Dallas-Fort Worth Interactive Marketing Association), POD (Podcasters of Dallas), Social Media Club's Dallas Chapter, Search Engine Marketing Professionals Organization (SEMPO), Dallas/Fort Worth Search Engine Marketers (DFWSEM), and the Microsoft Executive Circle.

Past and present work includes top-tier clients like 3Com, American Airlines, The American Cancer Society, The American Heart Association, BMC Software, Brinker International, Bristol Hotels & Resorts, Centex Homes, Compass Bank, Conversis Global, Corel Software, Gateway Computers, Globaltec Solutions, Hilton Hotels, JC Penney, Microsoft Corporation, Moroch Public Relations, NASCAR, Nortel, Sabre Technologies, Samsung Electronics, The Travel Channel and many others.
