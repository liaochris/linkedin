Shawn Shahani’s Summary

I conceptualize, manage and implement campaigns with emphasis on both branding and ROI. My experience has been diverse, creating successful strategies for a wide variety of clients, from web start-ups to multinational corporations. These include creative advertising strategies as well as covert online PR and marketing tactics to drive buzz and word of mouth. I have also crafted offline initiatives, utilizing on-the-ground teams for both business development and guerilla marketing.

In addition, I have developed content, initiated UGC efforts and engaged, built and managed hundreds of thousands within social networks. I am well-versed in search engine optimization and PPC management strategies. I also have traditional PR experience and formal journalism training.

I am also studied in design principles with an emphasis on intent design and architectural theory. I also have expansive and professional experience with graphic design and print layout and editorial.
