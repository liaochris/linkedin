Rebecca A. Nagy’s Summary

A professional member of NSA (National Speaker's Association), I have been a speaker, trainer and workshop leader for more than 20 years. My presentations have been given to a wide variety of audiences including corporations such as Microsoft and Wachovia to Queens Univesityy and The National Association of Catering Executives. My topics all revolve around the Power of Mind and the Law of Attraction and include: Generations in the Workplace, Take Your Soul to Work, Your Power to Co Create, A Passion for the Possible: Co Creating with Power and Purpose (A Mega session which I will be presenting at the National Speaker's Assn.'s National Convention this July, 2007), among others. All of my programs are customized to my clients goals and objectives, so this is just a small list.

I am also a nondenominational and interfaith minister, graduating from Sancta Sophia Seminary in 1996. I will officiate wedding ceremonies and baptisms that are customized and personalized at any location in the Charlotte metro area.

I come out of 20 years in the fashion retail industry where I worked as a DMM, Fashion Director, Training Director, Fashion Merchandiser, and Fashion Designer for some of the largest design companies and retailers in the country including Lord and Taylor NY, Gimbels East and Kmart Corp.

Leaving Corporate America in 1998, I decided to pursue my calling as a spiritual teacher and inspirational speaker. This also led to my being asked to officiate weddings, which now average close to 150 a year! (For more, please see my wedding pages online: http://www.acharlotteweddingminister.com
