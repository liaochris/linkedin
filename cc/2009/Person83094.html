Renzie Baluyut’s Summary

After spending some 15 years in the broadcasting industry, I decided to finally pursue my own personal projects and entrepreneurial endeavors.

By profession, I am a consultant on matters of media & marketing, outsourcing and content development. I'm also in the business of producing and promoting concerts and other live events. I am also a professional blogger.

Currently, I am a Managing Partner for ProFora Media, Inc., and our company is in the business of webinars and virtual events.

In the past, I have been connected with 99.5RT, AdFarm, Mellow Touch/Mellow 94.7, Easycall Philippines, Inc., ePerformax, GlobalSky, Trixan Pty Ltd., and 105.1 Crossover.
