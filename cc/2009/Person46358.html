Jeremy Neideck’s Summary

I am a graduate from Queensland University of Technology’s Performance Studies program and completed my undergraduate degree in 2005 with a minor in Music (Vocal Studies). I am an interdisciplinary artist who has worked as an actor, singer, musician, dancer, composer for theatre, choreographer and production manager and have experience in print and multi-media design as well as the design and manufacture of textile items.

I have also developed and led performance workshops in a number of environments including the PowerKidz program at the Brisbane Powerhouse, Education Queensland and the International Language Arts and Recreation Festival (South Korea) for QUT, LATT, and REM Theatre at both the Chung Ang University, and the National Youth Centre.

Jeremy Neideck’s Specialties:

Performer, musician (piano, concert flute, daegeum and soegeum (large and small Korean bamboo flutes) piano accordion, ukulele, synthesizer and computer music), singer (music theatre, choral, jazz and contemporary rock), composer, sound designer, music director, dancer, choreographer, production manager, print and multi-media design,
