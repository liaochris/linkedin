Douglas Pollei’s Summary

Develop strategic opportunities for growth through relationships with selected vendors, venture capital portfolio companies, and industry networks. Realize importance of maintaining close ongoing connections with thought leaders, trade show groups, and online networks.

Experienced senior manager with venture-held Internet platform company (10 years experience in high technology). Comprehensive leadership experience in target marketing initiatives, digital communications, due diligence, vendor relationships, and strategic planning in diverse B2B and B2C environments. I have also worked on emerging media and IP application projects for numerous clients and have been involved with Internet technologies since 1997. Part of senior management team involved with driving new business development and expansion strategies. Currently leading strategic partnership with Google.

Part of 40 person national advisory board on social media created to expand media literacy, encourage adoption of industry standards, promote ethical practices, and share lessons learned. Co-founder of Social Media Club of Minneapolis, Minnesota with an emphasis on social media in the enterprise.

Looking for network connections to individuals at early stage companies, venture-backed startups, VCs, or PE, that provide customer facing technology solutions for SMBs, Enterprises, and Internet service organizations (collaboration solutions, mobile, new media/marketing).
