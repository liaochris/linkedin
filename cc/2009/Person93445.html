Stephen Pokoj [LION]’s Summary

Healthcare Executive with 28 years of combined industry experience as a Principal Search Consultant at Critical Path Recruiting Solutions, Inc.(CPRS); a Senior Partner in an Executive Search and Placement firm; Partner with big 6 Healthcare IT Consulting firm; IT Project Direction and Management; Healthcare IT Consulting; and Clinical Laboratory Administration; Experience and passion is driven by all facets of executive search; building/managing large consulting practices; and clinical systems implementations.

Currently, Steve is the Principal Search Consultant of Critical Path Recruiting Solutions, Inc.. CPRS delivers successful results to clients by coupling a first hand understanding of the healthcare and IT consulting industries with a custom methodology designed to match the ideal candidate(s) with the needs of the client. The hands-on experience of both arenas provides Steve with the ability to completely understand and satisfy the needs of both the client and candidate. Steve has assembled a team at CPRS that has extensive healthcare IT industry consulting and recruiting roots.

Steve developed these necessary relationships and skills while spending over 10 years as a partner at ACSHCS, formerly Superior Consultant Company. There he was responsible for the Cerner Services vertical, the largest national group of independent Cerner consultants where he staffed 800 projects across 200 clients over a 6 year period. Prior to that Mr. Pokoj served as an IT Project Director and Laboratory Administrator at the Children’s Hospital of Buffalo (part of the Kaleida Health network). Mr. Pokoj also served as a clinical instructor at the State University of New York at Buffalo, where he earned MS and BS degrees in Medical Technology.
