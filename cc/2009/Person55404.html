Ktasha Hardge’s Summary

A member of the National Association of Tax Professionals (www.natptax.com), I am President & CEO of Hardge Connections, LLC, a tax and financial services company founded in 2003.

I am passionate about helping others keep more of what they make. I do more than prepare income tax returns. Through educating and empowering others about how they can apply different tax strategies to their particular situation, bottom line, I help people save a lot of money.

I am inspired by Oprah Winfrey. I appreciate how she diversifies her income and uses her brand to bring in multiple streams of income. I have goals to expand into other outlets and industries. I know that travel is a $7 trillion dollar industry so I decided to become a part of YTB. I think their business model is unmatched. They provide me with an opportunity to earn multiple streams of income and I am looking to work with people who share a similar interest. I use the YTB opportunity to teach people how they can supplement their income, gain tax advantages and benefit in other ways.

My additional skills include Professional Resume Writing, Advanced MS Word and Excel, PowerPoint, Access, Publisher, Business Plan Writing, Small Business Bookkeeping, Payroll Services, Financial Reports, Expert Typing Services, Marketing Assistance, and more.
