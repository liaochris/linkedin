Jinal Shah’s Summary

In her role as a strategist at Electric Artists, Jinal has developed digital and social media strategies for brands such as A&E Television, Le Meridien Hotels, Westin Hotels and skincare start-up, Rx For Brown Skin.

Jinal has been blogging circa 1999 (even before it was called Blogging) An understanding of marketing and developing brands with the aid of new media comes naturally to her. Prior to Electric Artists, she was a strategist at Poke New York and worked on developing e-commerce and digital experience strategies for clients such as Johnson & Johnson, Cynthia Rowley and tableware start-up, Teroforma.

Before moving to New York to work on the agency side, Jinal worked as the Business Development manager at Advanta Bank Corp where she developed and launched, IdeaBlob.com, the first social network ever created by a credit card company.

She enjoys traveling and fiction-writing.
