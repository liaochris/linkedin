Dave Saunders’s Summary

Madison+Main is an award-winning, advertising and new media firm that provides creative marketing solutions for emerging companies, usually small and mid-sized organizations with 15 to 1,500 employees. Clients include A Moment in Time, Big Brothers/Big Sisters, Children Incorporated, The Coleman Institute, Getloaded.com, The Greater Richmond Chamber, Richmond.com, Shore Bank, Uppy’s, Wythe Will Distributing, and many others. The firm is based in Richmond, Virginia, with an additional office in San Diego, California. For more information, please visitwww.madisonmain.com or www.madisonmain.mobi from your mobile device.
