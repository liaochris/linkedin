Asian Correspondent’s Summary

Asian Correspondent is an English-language liberal news and commentary website covering all of the Asia-Pacific region. Launched in October 2009 by the Hybrid News Group and the brainchild of managing director James Craven, Asian Correspondent combines syndicated news content, articles from established bloggers and correspondents from across the region, and user contributions to offer its own brand of online news delivery. The website covers politics, economics, new media, green and society issues, among other subjects.

The site's regular contributors include among others Jeff Ooi, Jeannie Cho Lee, Ahirudin Attan, Randeep Hooda, Peter Scudamore-Smith, Sriram Vadlamani, Edwin Espejo, Paul Denlinger, Andy Jackson, Liau Chuan Yi, Norvin Chan, Atanu Dey, Nathan Schwartzman, Elmer Cagape, Paul Farol, Tonyo Cruz, Danny Arao, James Morgan, Clement Tan, Randall van der Woning, Bala Murali Krishna and Gerry O’Kane.

Asian Correspondent’s Specialties:

Asian Correspondent covers all of the Asia-Pacific region, with particular focus on: China, India, Japan, South Korea, Hong Kong, Malaysia, Indonesia, Thailand, Philippines, Vietnam, Australia, Singapore and Taiwan.
