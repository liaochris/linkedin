Jason Smith’s Summary

My professional passion is using market forces to bring about environmental sustainability. Toward this end I founded LiveNeutral in 2005, one of the first North American carbon offset and climate change education organizations. I did this while completing my MBA in Sustainable Management from Presidio School of Management. There I gained greater exposure to the key business drivers of environmental markets and sustainability. In January 2008, I sold LiveNeutral to ClimateCHECK and joined their team of best-in-class greenhouse gas consultants. ClimateCHECK provides an opportunity for me to participant directly in shaping the North American post-Kyoto carbon and clean technology markets.
