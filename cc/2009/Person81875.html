Morgan Ramsay’s Summary

Morgan Ramsay founded Entertainment Media Council in 2008 and has served as President since its inception. Mr. Ramsay became Chief Executive Officer in September 2008.

Prior to joining Entertainment Media Council, Mr. Ramsay served as President and Chief Executive Officer at Heretic, a defense and entertainment ad agency, from 2002 until ceasing operations in 2009. In addition, he served as President and Chief Executive Officer at DiabloTrader.com, a virtual-assets brokerage, which he sold in 2003.

An industrious volunteer, Mr. Ramsay served as a board member at International Game Developers Association, San Diego, and in leadership roles with many professional organizations.
