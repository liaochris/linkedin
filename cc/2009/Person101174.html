Scot Clausing’s Summary

Enterprise development and architecture implementation for rich internet and web-enabled applications, primarily in Adobe Flex & Java.

Recent projects have been for the Health Care industry including new development and improvement of clinical applications for DaVita. I have had wonderful experiences creating data collection, trending and analysis software for companies such as US Playing Card, Kohl’s & EyeMed and performing data aggregation, analysis and customized reporting for an extensive list of companies including Kroger, Sonoco, Seagram’s, Schwan’s, JTM & Kellogg’s.

Goals moving forward include continued education and advancement as a respected developer in the workplace as well as the community. I enjoy helping others in the areas in which I do well, with hopes that they will do the same for me when I am in need :)
