Jessica Lee’s Summary

I am a savvy, effective HR business partner with a proven track record of results. My background includes a well-rounded portfolio of talent management responsibilities including recruiting, training & development, organizational development, employee relations, benefits administration, employee recognition and HR program and policy development.
