Ankur Dinesh Garg’s Summary

Wirefoot, is a highly Internet and Web Focused Company, with a core aim of delivering value added consulting to its clients. Working for many years in Internet and realizing the potential of this Industry, I have now started to feel it as one of my passion. There are endless possibilities over Internet and we stand to help everyone who wish to target these opportunities. As an Entrepreneur and Founder of Wirefoot, My goal is to create an state of the art consulting firm and work to our best in helping Business leverage Internet to their best.

Prior to Wirefoot I spent few best years of my life with ANMsoft. My association with ANMsoft was since its inception, year 2003. ANMsoft was a 5 membered team when I joined it and we have moved to 250+ now. I changed many roles and responsibilities in ANM. Starting as IT Manager I moved to Business Analysis, Delivery Management, Project Management then finally Business Development in which I took responsibility as Business Manager, Business Head, Head of Operation (Delhi Office) and VP Sales & Marketing (present). I was luck one to be a part of many leading Indian portals, and I got opportunity to perform multiple contribution on all of these.

Prior to ANM I was a student in India's most reputed technology institution, IIT Bombay. I did my courses in Mechanical Engineering and pursued my Masters in Computer Aided Design and Automation, from the same institute. I graduated from IIT Bombay in Year 2004 with Bachelor and Masters Degree in Mechanical Technology.

During my graduation at IIT Bombay, I was involved with Palsoft Infosystems Limited (A Jaipur based CAD CAM solution company) as technology & business consultant and Media Matrix World Wide (A Mumbai based Media House) as technology & business consultant.

As a career goal I wish to value add society in whatever way I can. At the end of days I would prefer to be know for things I have done rather then a person with lot of money.
