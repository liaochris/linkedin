Ajay Burlingham-Böhr’s Summary

Over 26 years experience in IT management within commercial, not-for-profit and education organisations, with over 11 years at Head of Department Level and 3 years at Executive Board level with additional areas of responsibility such as media production and services.

A strong technical and strategic background in the IT industry enhanced with an MBA completed in 2004.

Currently Director of Information Systems and Media Services at Anglia Ruskin University with responsibility for 100 permanent and contract staff and a budget in excess of £5m.

Other current work:

Visiting lecturer in Organizational Politics and Business Relationship Management at Cranfield University School of Management.

Accredited Coach with the Institute of Human Development and currently undertaking some coaching both as an internal coach and with a few external clients.

Accredited MBTI (Myers Briggs Type Indicator) practitioner with OPP for individual personal development and team building.

Chartered Fellow of the British Computer Society and member of BCS Elite.

Founding Chair of the Charities Consortium IT Director's Group.

Winner of the Computer Weekly Best Places to Work in IT Award 2006 whilst Head of IS at the RSPB.

Also undertaking occasional consultancy work and developing and delivering training courses on topics such as Knowledge Management, Strategy Mapping and Balanced Scorecards
