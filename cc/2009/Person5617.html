Andrew Girvan’s Summary

An extremely conﬁdent and creative person with wide experience demonstrating his ability to work in and manage projects. Has proven his ability to contribute effectively to the work of an organisation working in different capacities throughout the performing arts, particularly at the Edinburgh Festival Fringe. Andrew is able to add value to any team, particularly when able to use his deep knowledge of the performing arts to support the role.
