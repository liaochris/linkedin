Bonnie O'Brien, PHR, CTS, CPC, CSP’s Summary

Bonnie O’Brien is a human resource professional and SME in recruiting and hiring. A leader in the industry for over 20 years, she holds the following certifications:
- Professional in Human Resources from SHRM
- Certified Temporary Specialist and
- Certified Personnel Consultant - National Association of Personnel Services
- Certified Staffing Professional - American Staffing Association

Since 1993 she has served as facilitator to the North Jersey Chapter of SHRM for the PHR/SPHR course; taught dozens of workshops and training programs on job analysis; full life cycle recruiting (sourcing to hire); quality talent acquisition; and provided career counseling to job seekers for both private and public organizations.

Her strategic positioning of Placement Professionals (she previously owned two Norrell Staffing offices) has enabled diversification into government contracting via certifications from:
- State of New Jersey WBE (Woman Business Enterprise)
- State of New Jersey SBE (Small Business Enterprise).
- Port Authority of NY & NJ SBE and WBE
- NYC Dept. of Small Business
- WBENC

As a 3 year President of the NJ Staffing Alliance (a statewide trade association) and Chair of the Legislative Committee for over 10 years she testified before State government on staffing issues and appeared as an expert witness in legal cases involving staffing and employment.

An honors graduate of Farleigh Dickinson University (NJ) with a BS degree in Business (Management & Marketing) she also possesses a Paralegal Certification. She has sat on several non-profit organization's boards, received numerous awards from both private and public organizations and has been recognized by Business New Jersey (NJBIZ) magazine in the "Business Leaders Who’s Who" for the past ten years.

Bonnie O'Brien, PHR, CTS, CPC, CSP’s Specialties:

Full Life Cycle Sourcing, Recruiting, Selection; Job and Labor Market Analysis, Design, Jos Seeker Workshops and Job Description development.

Projects: Department of Defense (DoD); Port Authority; former military (engineers, logisticians, armorers); small arms repair and upgrades.

Customer base: diverse international and local companies including: Medical Device, Consumer Electronics, Financial, Government, Military, Insurance, IT.
