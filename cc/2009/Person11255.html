William Brantley’s Summary

Bill Brantley is a researcher, teacher, and trainer in public administration, project management, change management, and nonprofit management. He has been a trainer for over ten years and a college professor for six years. Bill has taught both online courses and traditional college courses at three different universities. He has an MBA, MA in Political Management, and is a certified Project Management Professional. Currently, Bill is finishing a PhD in Public Administration with an emphasis on non-profit management and knowledge management.

William Brantley’s Specialties:

Project Management, Public Administration, Teaching Online, Training, Nonprofit Management, Change Management, and Knowledge Management
