Chris Stennett’s Summary

Chris is an entrepreneurial, adaptable and results-driven business oriented IT professional with over 12 years international experience of high-profile clients and a track record that covers project and pre-sales consultancy together with business development, team leadership and project management services.


His primary areas of expertise include business case & ROI development, solution proposition & demonstration / proof-of-concept development, business & process analysis, systems analysis & functional design, functional architecture, implementation planning & approach, project scoping & pilot support and RFI / RFP handling. He has applied these skills across retail banking, LP&I, consumer credit, government, telecoms and retail sectors; typically in CRM (customer experience / sales & service), straight-through-processing, Internet and Intranet / Extranet, front office (contact centre / branch / sales force), middle office, back office and data warehousing & business intelligence (inc. Executive dashboard / cockpit) domains.

As a business solutions consultant Chris is experienced in engaging with all levels of stakeholders on both business and technical aspects of a solution from pre-sales / product selection, initial conception & feasibility, business case development and scoping through to user acceptance testing, training and deployment support. He is a highly presentable & adaptable consultant, respectful of organisational cultures and adept at fitting in to new environments and forging relationships.

In addition to his business solutions IT consultancy credentials, Chris has had valuable front-line experience running a small business focusing on the development and distribution of own-brand consumer electronics, sourcing primarily from the Far East.

Chris also holds MBA and BEng Hons. Engineering degrees.
