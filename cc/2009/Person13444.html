Brian Cork’s Summary

Brian Patrick Cork's experience spans that of a proud and deeply involved father; successful businessman; professional athlete; volunteer firefighter; and, entrepreneur.

Brian has been a highly successful executive and entrepreneur. He has raised over $500 million in private placement capital leading California in 1990, 1991 and 1993. Brian has led sales teams exceeding 1400, and built and sold several successful businesses. In 2003, Brian was named to London's prestigious "Ol Man Society" for his contributions as a businessman and philanthropist. In 2004 Brian was the G2 economics "Coalition Member of the Year". In both 2007 and 2008 brian cork Human Capital was named to the Inc. 500. Brian has also been selected by Catalyst Magazine as a "Top 50 Entrepreneur" in 2004 and 2005, and a "Top 25 Entrepreneur" in 2007 and 2008. In 2008 Entrepreneur Magazine said that Brian Patrick Cork was one of the top three most influential entrepreneurs in the Southeast.

Brian Cork's interests include the "Prudent Society of Optimistic Gentlemen", a global group business investors. Brian is also the a Founder and Resident Entrepreneur of Ministry Accelerator, focused on developing startup companies that can be self-sufficient and self-sustaining within eighteen months. Brian also Coaches the Fusion U14 Shockers Soccer Team.

Brian Cork is the author of the Blog: The Unsinkable Brian Cork. You can view this Blog at http://www.unsinkablebriancork.com. He is currently writing his first books entitled "This Would Be Jefferson's View", and "The CEO Handbook".
