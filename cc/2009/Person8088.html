Arun Manansingh’s Summary

Career Summary: Business oriented information technology executive with a passion for creating demonstrable business value through technology enablement with over eleven years of IT management experience in international finance, leasing, and banking.   

Expertise: 
1. Considerable expertise in building and managing a global IT department
2. Successful record in development of a strategic IT plan and implementing in a complex, global organization

4. A sincere desire to contribute to the continued growth of a firm

1. To become a senior IT executive where I can contribute to the corporate vision and responsible for leading a group of people to develop innovative ways to build and implement the technology solutions in alignment with the corporate vision.   

2. I would be interested in opportunities in finance at a startup or an established company in either the New York, Stamford or Westchester area. Open to other industries, as well.
