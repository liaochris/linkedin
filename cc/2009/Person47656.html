Jeffrey Jakucyk’s Summary

7 years experience in architecture firms of varying sizes. Focused on custom residential design, additions and remodeling, historic preservation, cabinetry design and detailing, interior finish selections, and electrical and mechanical systems. Developed new documentation methods for Lutron lighting automation systems motorized roller shade installations. Worked with innovative building system designs including new spray-foam insulation systems, ground-source (geothermal) heat pumps, outdoor air economizers, and energy recovery ventilators.

5 years experience in Macintosh office information technology. Managed 20 workstations, 2 servers, OpenDirectory, workgroup administration, calendar sharing, website maintenance, tape backups, large format black & white plotting, and color printing administration and maintenance.

3 years experience with architectural and sports photography. Photographed dozens of sporting events, from high school basketball, wrestling, and swimming/diving to triathlons and bike races. Also photographed several architectural interiors and exteriors for office portfolio use and publication. See the "Photography Website" link for examples.

9 years on and off research on Cincinnati's transit history, focused mainly the streetcar and electric interurban railway system, with additional research on specific local steam railroads and other transportation infrastructure. Research includes photo documentation of extant remains and mapping with GIS and CAD software. See the "Main Website" link for for more information.

Jeffrey Jakucyk’s Specialties:

Custom residential design, additions and remodeling, historic preservation, cabinet design, electric plans, Lutron systems, HVAC, geothermal systems.
