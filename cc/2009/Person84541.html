Robbie Mac Iver, PMP, CSM’s Summary

Robbie Mac Iver brings business and technology teams together to create and implement innovative solutions to complex business problems. By providing practical, sensible, and effective project leadership, Robbie improves both teams and processes to achieve visible, predictable results.

As a Certified Scrum Master and a PMP certified project manager, Robbie is well versed in the values and principles of agile practices as well as more traditional project management methods. As a result he is well suited as an agile coach to assist teams and organizations making the transition to agile practices.

Robbie has Diversified experienced in all phases of the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) from requirements discovery through product deployment and has a proven track record of delivering successful projects by communicating, coordinating, and integrating all aspects of the project.

He has also successfully implemented agile practices outside of the software development domain using Scrum to manage complex business processes.

Robbie is a co-founder of the Houston chapter of the Agile Project Leadership Network (APLN).
