John Allaire’s Summary

My career has been focused on successfully maximizing service, performance and capacity throughout logistics organizations, including distribution center, transportation, manufacturing and retail operations.

Design, implementation and management of Engineered Work Standards (EWS), Warehouse Management System (WMS) and Transportation Management System (TMS) for distribution and logistics networks in grocery retailers and wholesalers throughout the US and Canada including A&P, Albertsons, American Stores, Giant, Raley’s, SUPERVALU, Stop & Shop, Sysco and others.
Design, implementation and maintenance of performance-based (incentive and pay for performance) programs for warehousing, transportation and manufacturing operations.
Methods and Procedures recommendation, justification and implementation, DC facility layout and design for conventional and mechanized applications in a variety of product handling environments.
Development, testing, implementation and training of TMS applications for large and medium sized private fleet operations.
Over 25 years experience dedicated to improving logistics and manufacturing productivity in private industry and DOD environments. Intellectual capital and industry experience in recommending, testing and implementing best of class WMS, TMS and EWS applications for logistics operations.
Successfully installed EWS programs in challenging union environments, including defending system and data integrity in arbitration.
