Pieter Frank de Jong’s Summary

Pieter Frank de Jong is a Dutch Illustrator & Graphic designer living and working in Amsterdam. In 2006, right after finishing his BA in communication-design from Academie Minerva in Groningen he decided to pursue a freelance career. His style incorporates a lot of handdrawn illustrations that get tweaked and edited digitally giving it a strong sense of contemporary craftsmanship.

Pieter Frank works for a diverse international client base on both Illustrative and graphic design projects. Pieter Frank is available for illustration, design and art direction.

Illustration, Design, Art Direction.
