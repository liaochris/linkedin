Beverly Lawrence, MH, C.A.Ht., CSC’s Summary

Alternative healing practice since 1994, originally based in Los Altos, CA and now in Chandler, AZ.

Added acupuncture and related modalities in 2007 at the PHIMA Clinic in Phoenix and in 2008 at the Hu Gi Acupuncture Center in Chandler, AZ.

Expect to add this practice at the Dancing Bear Healing Center in early 2009.
