Brian Phillips’s Summary

I have the keys to New York City. I handle sales and leasing and have access to residential & commercial listings in all of Manhattan, Brooklyn, and Bronx. I have 11 years in the business and I love what I do. I'm in this business because I'm a people person. I enjoy networking, and I take pleasure in finding customers a space they'll enjoy being in. I have a team of associates that speak several languages. I have a very good reputation, and I'm very popular among my peers. I'm also well connected. I belong to several organizations including the Real Estate Board of New York's (REBNY) Education Committee, REBNY's Membership Committee, REBNY's Residential Sales Council, and REBNY's Upper Manhattan Committee. I'm also involved with the my local Chamber of Commerce, my local Community Board, my local democratic club, and have volunteered my time to individuals living with HIV since 2000.

Thinking of listing or relocating anywhere in the world or know someone who is? Allow me to put you in touch with the most successful agents through Prudential's vast national & international relocation and referral network. Prudential refers internally as well as to outside firms. I receive credit by submitting the request to help locate appropriate agents in your area no matter where in the world. Thank you for taking the time to read about me.
