WHAT SOMEONE ELSE SAYS ABOUT ME:

As one of the founders of Brains on Fire, we call her our Courageous President because she is. Robbin deals with tough issues, within and outside our doors with style, determination and brutal honesty – all the while serving as a source of inspiration for her co-Brains on Fire kindred spirits. Robbin is not only an outstanding entrepreneur, but an outstanding leader for her employees, her community and her industry. Since Robbin took on the role of president three years ago, Brains on Fire has increased revenue (and profits) over 40%. Brains on Fire continues to give back by donating over 5% of it’s total revenue per year to not for profit organizations.

Through her guidance, Brains on Fire has been recognized nationally by the Word of Mouth Marketing Association’s WOMMIE Awards, a Gold EFFIE, the ad:tech Awards and Inc. Magazine’s 5000 fastest-growing companies; and globally by the ReBrand 100 Awards, which recognizes the top 100 rebranding successes in the world.

Robbin has recruited highly talented individuals from all over the country to elevate Brains on Fire to a national status as we continue to create solutions for companies in many diverse industries. Locally, she gives her time to the Board of the Greenville CVB and the Board for the Peace Center.
