Dr. Joel Fugleberg’s Summary

Dr. Joel Fugleberg graduated from Association Free Lutheran Bible School (AFLBS) in Plymouth, MN before attending the
University of MN-Morris. There he graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in biology before completing his doctorate training at
Northwestern College of Chiropractic in Bloomington, MN.

Injuries while wrestling for the University of MN-Morris led to Dr. Fugleberg's interest in chiropractic .  He notes that he was impressed with chiropractic's ability to get an athlete back into competition quickly and also promote optimal performance.  His athletic background has led
Dr. Fugleberg to work with the Chaska Hawk wrestling team; he has also provided care for players of the Minnesota Vikings, Minnesota Timberwolves, & Minnesota Vixen. 

Dr. Fugleberg has worked the past 3 years at a busy multi-disciplinary clinic in Chanhassen seeing a variety of patients. “I’ve been blessed to treat newborns to 94 year olds and everyone in between!” remarks Dr. Fugleberg.

As a perpetual learner, Dr. Fugleberg has taken post-graduate educational coursework in various chiropractic techniques and is currently completing coursework to become certified in Wellness Science through the International Chiropractic Association. He is well-versed in corrective chiropractic, nutritional strategies and wellness care.

In his spare time, Dr. Fugleberg enjoys spending time with his wife and daughter, his family and friends, and leading a healthy lifestyle.  He competes in various road races and triathlons recently finishing the Twin Cities marathon. 

Dr. Fugleberg's dream is that every person in the world would have a chiropractor on their healthcare team.  His faith, family values, and commitment to excellence collectively impact his patient care. He emphasizes, "True health is only attained through positive lifestyle choices - I help others who want to be on the path to a healthier life!"
