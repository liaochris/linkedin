Daniel Gross’s Summary

I am currently the Director of IT for Waterford Union High School. I also serve as consulting director for the Southeastern Wisconsin Instructional Network Group (SWING).

In addition to working with K-12 and higher education partners, I also head up the WIRED Partners initiative to get Wisconsin’s Resources in Educational Partnerships: museums, zoos, libraries and other ‘content providers,’ to use today’s communications technology as an effective educational outreach tool.

My background is in both elementary and adult education, and I have been practicing various forms of distance learning since 1976. A creative approach, a zeal for lifelong learning, and my knack for technology come together in programs that ignite learners and produce real ‘technology integration’ skills to take home.

I seek to continue in a leadership position in a highly technical field where I can enhance my skills, redefine cutting edge technology, teach and learn with others, and advance both professionally and financially.

Please inquire about my availability to speak to your group about the future of Educational Communications Technology, and what the classroom of tomorrow might look like. I can also provide customized training to help your executive team present a professional image during electronically assisted meetings such as videoconferences, webconferences, and other digital communication forums.

Daniel Gross’s Specialties:

Videoconferencing of all flavors, especially the application of most all educational communications technologies including webconferencing, streaming, learning management systems and more! More than button pushing, I can help you communicate effectively through technology.
