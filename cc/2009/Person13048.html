Brandy Ellis’s Summary

I have 8 years of sales experience, founded in door to door sales for Kirby Vacuum cleaners and ADT Security Systems. From there, moving into Business Development and Account Management within the recruiting industry. I have been consistently ranked a top performer in cold calling, breaking into new accounts, and forging strong and lasting relationships. I have a blended background of RPO, Executive Search Agency, and Corporate experience that has allowed me to gain in-depth knowledge of the recruiting lifecycle and organizational needs and better align myself as a true strategic staffing partner.

In the past, I have successfully built a contract staffing practice with a top ranked MRI office to include implementing back office procedures, new client development, and full lifecycle candidate management. In each position listed have been tasked with building processes, managing difficult clients and/or situations, achieving difficult quotas and recruiting goals, and have been moved into increasing roles of responsibility in each.

That success has translated into a track record of excellent recruiting performance. For 3 years I have acted as a designated member of the Sourcing Training teams for multiple organizations; championing new Web 2.0 sourcing techniques, AIRS, and cutting edge methodologies to new team members/hires as well as existing team ongoing development.
