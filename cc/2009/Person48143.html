JoAnn Bruhn’s Summary

About me: I have experienced a lot of devastating losses in my lifetime, including the death of my youngest son, Craig, at age 10 (leukemia). Through the process of grief and healing, I have discovered some very wonderful gifts. My purpose has become clear to me: I am here in this lifetime to help people deal with their losses.

I am a compassionate Intuitive. I am very honored to be able to bring important messages to you from those in Spirit. I am recommended by renowned Intuitive, Kathryn Harwig. You can read her recommendation at www.harwig.com. Just click on "Community". Contact me for more information or to schedule an appointment at:joann@sundanceproject.com or send an email to me here

In service, I offer private consultations, classes and workshops designed to teach people better tools for dealing with grief and loss. Visit www.sundanceproject.com for more information.

In other settings I perform the original songs that I compose. The tones and vibrations of this music resonate to inspire comfort and healing. I create songs from a deep place of intuitive awareness once I have an idea of the purpose of the song.

I love people, and it is my honor to be of service. I invite you to visit my website at www.sundanceproject.com
