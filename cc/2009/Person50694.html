Josef Scarantino’s Summary

Josef Scarantino is the Founder & Executive Director of a nonprofit organization called webstartAfrica, based in Washington DC. WebstartAfrica exists to serve and support African technologists and break down the barriers to technology in Africa.

WebstartAfrica is currently focusing on Kenya, Sudan, Uganda, Nigeria, South Africa, & Ghana, but others are being added to the list. WebstartAfrica is in need of Partners located in Africa and Sponsors interested in supporting our work.

If interested, read more at: http://webstartafrica.org
