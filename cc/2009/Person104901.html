Will McInnes’s Summary

My primary role is as Managing Director of NixonMcInnes - http://www.nixonmcinnes.co.uk.

NixonMcInnes is the largest independent social media agency in the UK, creating stronger unions between major international brands and their consumers.

Current clients include: Channel 4, Cisco, Oxfam, Chelsea Football Club, Barclaycard, Coca-Cola, BMW, The Make Your Mark Campaign, The University of Southampton and Co-operative Financial Services.

My responsibilities are guiding the strategic direction of the business, directing a number of our key client relationships, managing the management team and public speaking.

My goals are to grow NixonMcInnes' reputation on the international stage, to grow our client base of visionary marketers within major brands and to be contributing to and making sense of online innovations as they happen - all in a way that has a positive impact on the world we live in.
