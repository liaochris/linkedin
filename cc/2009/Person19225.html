Connie Mayse’s Summary

I am an award-winning communications professional with strong experience in internal communications and human resources. In a nutshell, I am an excellent writer and strong public speaker with hands-on technical expertise, including writing for the web, print, speeches, video, and presentations. My human resources background includes labor relations (including negotiation), a strong benefits background and more.

Accomplishments include
• Complete rewrite of employee handbook, resulting in three communication awards.
• Intranet and internet design, content development and content management.
• Development of employee video library, including video scripting, production, direction and voice-over.
• Creation and implementation of strategic communication plans at both organization and project level.

I am presently seeking a position as an employee communications specialist or web content manager at an organization that values promoting two-way communication with its employees or members. I am willing to relocate for the right position.
