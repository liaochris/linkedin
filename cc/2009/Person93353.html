April Chapman’s Summary

I am a social media consultant, speaker, and workshop facilitator. I work with individuals or companies that would like to leverage social media, teaching them how to take advantage of the opportunities available in the virtual marketplace. I have a passion to education, coach & inspire others to Speak Up for themselves on the social networks!

I speak and offer workshops on the following topics:

• Social Media Applications for Business: How to Use
Facebook, Linked In and Twitter to Grow Your Business
• Virtual Meetings & Events: How to Extend the Reach of
Your Events
• “Yes, it’s Personal!” Understanding and Appreciating
Different Personalities to Create Effective Work
Environments

I have worked within many industries including:

• Meeting & Event Industry
• Real Estate, Corporate and County Levels
• Marketing/PR
• Training & Development
• Direct Sales
• Non-Profit
• Churches

If you would like to explore to determine whether I would be a good fit for your organization, please contact me at 904.589.1234 or April@Speakin-Up.com. I promise that if I don't think we will be a good fit I will be honest with you.
