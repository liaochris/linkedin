Scott Sheaffer’s Summary

Scott R. Sheaffer, CSE, DTM, publishes a sales tips website (SalesTipsByScott.com). He is a contributing writer for other books, blogs and national publications. He has recently published his book "Comatose Management." He lives in DFW (Dallas, Texas) and has worked in sales, sales management and sales training for over 20 years in companies ranging from small enterprises to Fortune 500 companies. The focus of Scott’s industry experience has been with industrial distributors. He is a graduate of Baylor University and a member of ASTD and TMI.

Scott Sheaffer’s Specialties:

Sales training, public speaking and sales management.
