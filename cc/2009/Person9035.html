Aaron Zafran’s Summary

Seasoned software developer/architect with 10+ years of experience, with a focus in ColdFusion development, .NET desktop application development, and database architecture/design (MSSQL 2k5, Oracle 10i). Strengths include maximizing application scalability/efficiency, developing flexible, reusable, organized code, employing innovative and effective web-based UI design (particularly using JS/DHTML/AJAX), and technical documentation. Extensive traditional (non-web-based) programming experience, in numerous languages, including C#/C++ and Visual Basic.

Held currently inactive security clearance (Secret Level / DoD) through ATS, Inc.

Aaron has a lifelong history of musical endeavors, having crafted over 50 original compositions to date, beginning at the age of 13 years and continuing through the present. Trained in both classical and contemporary piano (21 years total), he has participated in numerous solo and ensemble performances in concert throughout his youth under the guidance and instruction of mentors at Loch-Reid Music. His current focus is in electronic music production with future goals of doing so professionally.
