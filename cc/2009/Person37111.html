Jennifer Fisher’s Summary

My passion has always been in 'service' to others. I owned and operated a computer service and repair business on the East Coast of Florida for almost 15 years.

I got involved in Network Marketing not only to create an income for myself, but to help others do the same.

My primary goal is not to have people join me in my business, but to help educate them on the 'behind the scenes' structure of Network Marketing and why there is such a high failure rate. It's not the product that counts as much as it is the structure of the Company itself. Learn 5 simple key components necessary for YOUR success and you will succeed.
