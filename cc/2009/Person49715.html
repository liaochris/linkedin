Business Continuity

Is your organisation fully prepared for a major disaster or severe disruption… or are you hoping for the best, just like almost half of UK business. Even though 40% of business suffered problems last year, and 80% of businesses which suffer a major disaster fail within two years…

Or, do you think that simply backing up your data will protect you? It could, if continuously undertaken to the high standards necessary…but how often do you test the data; or restore the system; or try and buy replacement servers at short notice?

Oakview offers help with: Advising, drafting, preparing, writing, testing and facilitating Business Continuity Planning, all using BS 25999 – the global Business Continuity standard. With our business partners, we can offer you state of the art back-up facilities; and our connections with property companies mean we can identify off-site restoration sites at short notice.
