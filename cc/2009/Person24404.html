David Marshall’s Summary

As a software and systems architect who has been a pioneer in the virtualiation industry since 2000, I've helped design new software solutions that directly address the challenges faced by IT; and I've now started four virtualization specific companies that have each successfully raised venture capital funding. I'm a recognized virtualization subject matter expert who has published four virtualization specific books. I've written numerous magazine articles for publishers such as Virtual-Strategy, TechTarget, Network World and CIO magazine. I've been writing InfoWorld's Virtualization Report column since May of 2006, and I'm the owner and operator of one of the oldest and most successful virtualization news sites, VMBlog.com.

I offer the following:

• Desktop, Server and Application Virtualization expertise
• Ability to help early stage companies execute from idea development to product launch.
• Result oriented professional with experience in product marketing, branding, and public and analyst relations.
• A strong passion for social media and online marketing strategies with new media and Web 2.0 marketing.
• Datacenter infrastructure design and implementation
• Support center management and operations management
• Research and Development to help create and foster new technological ideas
