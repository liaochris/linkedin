Troy Shaw’s Summary

Our Mission

The Diversity Television Network was founded with one thing in mind: helping people. We deliver helpful, educational and resource information to people in need. You will find us in the doctor’s office, laundry mat, barbershop, and State, County, and Federal Facilities. DTV strives to be the people’s resource for stimulating information.

DTV provides quality informational programming to central city and improvised communities around the country. By placing digital screens where the people of these communities must go to seek services, we create an opportunity to educate them on the numerous services available for their benefit.
