Jim Bishop’s Summary

I have over 25 years of business and entrepreneurial experience working as a controller, an analyst, a consultant and as a business owner.

I help my clients with a comprehensive financial plan to help them identify and prioritize their goals. I do not charge for this process because it is too important to skip and to valuable to put a fair value on. The financial plan helps you with the key questions.

1) At what age do you want to retire?
2) What income do you need to retire on?
3) How much capital (savings) do you need to generate that income?
4) How much do you need to put away monthly to achieve that?
5) What rate of return do you need to make it all work?

If the goal is not retirement, then change the work and the date to education or buy a vacation home.
