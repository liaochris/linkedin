Dominic Tong, M.D.’s Summary

Enterprising and accomplished life sciences professional with nearly twenty years of diverse healthcare practice and management experience in the academic, public and private sectors. Medical Affairs and Clinical Research experience in the biopharmaceutical and medical device space with the Johnson & Johnson Family of Companies. Extensive knowledge and reference base in science, internal medicine and diagnostic radiology. Exceptionally adaptive and able to rapidly acquire new skills and responsibilities. Major strengths include relationship-building, creative problem-solving, strategic thinking, cross-function collaboration and development of synergies in the biopharmaceutical and medical device space across multiple disease and therapeutic areas.
