Kat Black’s Summary

iPhone apps now out - see littlewhitebook.net and illumiQ.com

I've got my paws in many digital-content pies. Ideally, I just want to make a living from being a creative, but I seem to spend most of my time doing business-related stuff.

I'm a VJ, artist, author and digital media consultant. Married to Jasper Cook, the other half of VJzoo - we've performed in Spain, Singapore, the UK, France, NZ, New York, San Francisco and around Australia. I also ran the Byte Me! Festival of digital content in Perth, Western Australia in 2007 with speakers from Dreamworks, EA Games, Weta Digital and Weta Workshop as well as local animators and 3D companies with an international reputation.

Kat Black’s Specialties:

VJing, social networking, iPhone and web 2.0 content development, tarot, design, digital collage, casual games, festival co-ordination, business match-making, exhausting people with my bouncy enthusiasm, especially when in an overcaffeinated state (uh... that's most of the time)
