Michael McFadden’s Summary

Entrepreneur by heart, Michael McFadden has channeled his brand strategy, new product innovation, and marketing expertise into a new luxury vacation rental marketing platform. Prior to starting TheSociety.com and VacationHomesForCharity.org Michael completed his MBA at Oxford. At Oxford, in addition to his degree he received a Blue on the golf team.

Before graduate school, Michael worked on the Jose Ole brand as the new products manager and before joining the Jose Ole brand team he worked at the Sterling-Rice Group in Boulder, Colorado.

Michael is a member of the Who's Who of Luxury Real Estate for his work at TheSociety.com. He is also a member of the CMO Council.
