John Grozik’s Summary

The original Digital Docent with more than 30 years of experience with technology enhanced learning, immersive/interactive environments, webinar development, and emerging IT and Media technologies.

Through Digital Campfires, Inc., John is helping authors develop and publish content for e-Readers, now focusing on Amazon's new Kindle2 technologies.

John Grozik’s Specialties:

Digital publishing, Innovative project design and leadership for public and private institutions, customized online learning, webinar development, workshops on innovation and facilitated thinking, economic and workforce development strategies, interactive and educational environments for museums, trade shows, and public spaces, collaborative workspace system design, grant proposal development, creative brainstorming, and product development/marketing.
