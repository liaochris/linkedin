Mark Carbone’s Summary

Mark Carbone is a technology builder. He’s the owner and CEO of XE Corporation, an award-winning enterprise software firm in Orlando, FL. XE Corporation began in 1999 when Mark created the first business management engine for entrepreneurs: XE-700. His company also creates mobile apps for iPhones and Facebook, and creates social media strategies and social networks for companies in a variety of industries, from movie makers to farmers. Mark also serves as CTO for Kingdom Racing, an Indy Car racing team, where his technology helps the team do more to win races while spending less money to get there.

Mark’s faith in God is first in his life along with his beautiful wife, Susan, and their first child on the way. Mark has an MBA from Rollins College, where he also founded the Rollins Business Journal, and served on the alumni board. He is also UCF graduate in Pre-Law, Lifework Leadership graduate, and a current Dream Builder. Mark also speaks to groups about the economy and how to harness technology to prosper in this new normal. Mark was also nominated for the Most Influential Businessman of 2007 by the Orlando Business Journal.

    OUR TECHNOLOGY
    XE's XE-700 Business Management Engine is an all-in-1 software that bolts into organizations with 10 to 10,000 employees that will improve the way they run their company the day they turn it on. XE-700 is part .NET 3.5 and part Open Source.

    WHAT IT DOES
    It gives a CEO and management a real-time window into what their best and worst clients, vendors, and employees are doing to further the success of their company. It does this by managing all parts of a company - marketing, sales, project management, work orders, inventory, scheduling, and people management.
