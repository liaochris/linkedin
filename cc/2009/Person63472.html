Mark McDonnell’s Summary

Currently working freelance for various animation studios and related projects. Specializing in a multitude of crafts ranging from Character Design, Layout Design/Look Development, Graphic Design, to Theme Park Design. Previously an Art Director/Illustrator at Technicolor Creative Services for 6 years servicing many of the studios including: Walt Disney Feature, Walt Disney TVA, Disney Toon Studios, Miramax, Fox, H.I.T. Entertainment, New Line, Mattel, Sony, Legendary Pictures, Jim Henson Company and many more.

Mark McDonnell’s Specialties:

Versatility: Layout Designer, Character Designer, Look Development/BG Designer, Graphic Designer, and an Instructor at various institutions - currently teaching at Walt Disney Television Animation.
