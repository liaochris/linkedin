CJ Yu’s Summary

I currently serve as Manager of Digital Media at Endemol USA, a division of Endemol Group, a world leading content producer and distributor of television and online programming, including such breakout hits as Deal or No Deal, Wipeout, and Big Brother. I joined Endemol after several of experience at various industry leading entertainment and interactive media companies where I served in a variety of key leadership roles in both creative and business development.

My career was jump-started at the early age of 16 by writing published feature articles and film reviews for the Sacramento Bee while still in high school, and at the age of 18, had the opportunity to create a commercial concept for Snapple/Deutsch as part of their $40 million advertising campaign ((http://marketing-case-studies.blogspot.com/2008/04/bottles-personified-campaign.html).

These experiences helped open the door to opportunities at various studios and networks during college, allowing me to quickly land a role as a Producer at E! Entertainment Networks immediately after graduation.

I am an aggressive creative executive with nearly a decade of experience in leadership roles developing unscripted original programming, executing innovative marketing campaigns, and producing cutting-edge content with a strong emphasis in creating multi-platform entertainment experiences for film, TV, digital media, and branded entertainment.

I am interested in exploiting interactive platforms, developing groundbreaking business models and working with like-minded creative partners to produce truly unique monetizable content and revenue-generating digital products. With shortening attention spans and splintering budgets, it is more important than ever to find new ways to more deeply connect with audiences and be louder than the noise that surrounds us in the digital age.
