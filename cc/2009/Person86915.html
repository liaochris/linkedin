Joe Ruggles’s Summary

Our Vision is to offer an affordable solution for Real Estate agents while providing a top level of service to our clients. Part of this vision includes offering real estate agents the opportunity to 'keep more money' in order to provide them with better lives. Our business system provides real estate agents a home for their business where we consider them as family and friends. This Vision is driven by the desire to hold the highest ethics, morals and honesty throughout our lives. Compromise is not an option.

Our Mission is to have all of our clients provide us with a ranking of ELEVEN in satisfaction based on a scale of one to ten. We consider a personal referral as the highest compliment to our service. Our satisfied clients associate our brand with honesty, integrity and loyalty. Interwoven in this mission is the privilege to ‘give back’, we support Habitat for Humanity and St. Jude Children’s Research Hospital.

Our Goal is to experience a better life by building better relationships. We define a relationship as a magnification of life experiences. We are committed to these relationships, our professional business plan and our business system. To be fulfilled by taking massive action and traveling with it on the journey to success.

About Eleven Realty – The Broker team at Eleven Realty exceeds 38 combined years of experience and is an award winning management team.
Joe and Cheryl Ruggles have been awarded the 2008 Atlanta Business Chronicle’s Pacesetter Award as one of the fifty fastest growing companies in Atlanta Georgia. Joe is an Advisory Board Member for the School of Business at Clayton State University and was a finalist for the 2007 Greater North Fulton Small Business Person of the Year, additionally he serves as an officer with Toastmaster’s Club. Joe and Cheryl are available to the Eleven Realty agents when experienced professional assistance is required.
