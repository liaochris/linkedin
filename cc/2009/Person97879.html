Cheyenne Morrison’s Summary

Cheyenne Morrison is a multifaceted renaissance man most commonly known as the owner of Coldwell Banker Morrison's Private Islands. His involvement as a Private Island specialist first started in earnest 9 years ago when a wealthy author he had been friends with many years decided to purchase an island. After 3 years researching and tracking down possible islands worldwide for him, the author failed to buy an island. But all that information led to his involvement in island realty in the year 2000.

Morrison has lived and traveled to islands all over the world most for most of my adult life; and has first hand experience of islands around the world. Because of his success as one of the world's leading private island brokers, people often do not know that he is also an accomplished writer, island travel expert and photographer whose photographs of private islands have been downloaded millions of times around the world.

He is The Islomaniac ™
īl-o-mā'nē-āk' [noun] One with a passion or craze for islands.

He is the Editor of The Islomaniac's Blog, a journal of islomania and his passion for islands all over the world. The webiste also contains Cheyenne's Islomaniacs Store with island movies, books, posters, hundreds of free tropical island wallpapers and even gear for living on an island. The definitive guide to everything you everything you ever wanted to know about islands. Private island resorts, celebrity island owners, islands travel, and islands ephemera.

Morrison fulfilled a life-long dream by living for a year alone in a tiny nipa hut in the Philippines on the magnificent Pinagbuyatan island, pictured on the cover of the Lonely Planet Philippines.
