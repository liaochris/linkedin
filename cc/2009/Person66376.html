Meg Meyer’s Summary

My latest projects include moving from being the "go to" freelancer to being the owner of a thriving Business Support Services & Executive Business Coaching Company (http://www.MegMeyer.com) and finding free or inexpensive spaces in SE Wisconsin in which to hold classes & events for Center of Muse. Both businesses aim to help business owners, creative and fun people learn, grow, and improve. Send me a message to ask how.

I love ethical businesses that care as much about the Earth and the beings in it, as they do their profits. I love helping people do what they love and make money at it. I love that I can do just that, every day, myself.

I hate doing work that doesn't seem interesting or worth the time wasted doing it. I rarely have to take on such trivial tasks, but have the liberty to pass on anything without enough monetary or personal compensation.

These days, I spend my working hours writing, designing blogs, websites and other new media, & coaching creative entrepreneurs.

Oh, and by the way, if you enjoy fruit, veggies, dairy, or beef - you might want to look up what's going on with honeybees. If they go, so does much of the pollination.
