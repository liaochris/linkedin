Chris Fox’s Summary

I am an experienced technical author and embedded systems designer, architect and engineer with many years in a wide variety of disciplines and many successful products completed and patents filed. This includes integrated circuit design, cryptography and cryptographic module design, asset and people tracking, factory floor automation, physical security and access control systems, fuel management and measuring systems, global networked systems, system integration, wireless radio communications, audio recording, acoustic design, and commercial television and radio production.
