Steve Gerencser’s Summary

Finally getting my sea legs after working in an office environment for the last 7 years. I had forgotten how much I enjoyed the freedom of working by myself for myself.

My goals for 2009 include a serious effort at helping bring a level of professionalism to search marketing (SEO) that will be instrumental in elevating the industry from "the web guy" to a profession that is respected for the results it creates.

Coming from a rather unique background of actually having run real world businesses I bring a perspective to the industry that is sorely lacking in so many areas. It is my hope to be able to share as much of what I have learned as possible with the people coming in to the industry with a purely theoretical or development background.
