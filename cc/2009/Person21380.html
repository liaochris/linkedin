Curtis Porter ChFC, CLU’s Summary

Financial Planner with a broad knowledge of comprehensive financial planning including insurance, taxation, retirement plans, investments, and estate planning. FINRA licenses held include series 6,7,63, and 65. Current designations include ChFC, CLU and CFP curriculum certification. Additional designation held as a Philanthropic Development Officer (PDO) as determined by the National Association for Charitable Estate Planners for work in charitable foundation creation. Achieved associates degree in business management from the University of New Hampshire prior to enrolling at The American College for finance. Interested in forging worthwhile and stimulating relationships with other professionals in both the financial industry and other similar complimentary industries.

Featured contributor of various financial topics in multiple media outlets including the business section of the Eagle Tribune of Lawrence, MA reaching over 100,000 readers on Sunday's. Also, a featured panelist on local cable television programs discussing various financial planning matters and a frequent speaker at organizations including senior centers and school districts in and around NH and MA.
