David Muller’s Summary

Globaly experienced CIO, leading corporations in the successful delivery of cost-effective, high-performance Information Technology solutions. Broad multi industry knowledge and out-of-the-box thinking to help organizations deliver lasting value. Deep understanding of IT architecture and the various components and design considerations needed to successfully deploy and manage a business solution. Passionate about data, recognizing that it is the quality, content and relationship of data irrespective of its source that drives value for an organization. Sound experience in developing and implementing IT strategies together with relevant policy, building business support and allowing organizations to respond quickly as conditions change. David believes a successful IT strategy is built on four components:
1. People.
2. Technology.
3. Empowerment of business users and,
4. Consistent delivery of results through repeatable processes.
An accomplished speaker he has regularly presented at Executive and Technology conferences in North America, Hong Kong and Australia.
