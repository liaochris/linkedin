William Zhou’s Summary

William Zhou started off as a script kitty in elementary school when he “hacked” some games. He later taught himself BASIC in Gr. 7 from an old and dusty book found in the public library. Gr. 8 was the year when he dove into the world of web developments. His first site was created using nothing but HTML. As he progressed, he learned many other web languages as well. Soon, he found himself building his own school’s forum and website. Ideas would flow out from his head everyday. Some of which are written down and can be found at his personal blog. As an early adopter, he owns many interesting gadgets. He is also found at many networks on the internet. Ones such as Twitter, Flickr, Facebook, and Qik are his favorites.

Photography is also a big part of his life. Though he has only recently started doing it, he enjoys it very much. His camera, a Nikon D200, has traveled with him to many amazing places in the world. Furthermore, he owns a photoblog where he displays some of those amazing shots.
