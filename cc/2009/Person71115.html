Kami Watson Huyse’s Summary

Kami Watson Huyse, APR, principal of My PR Pro, is a public relations consultant based in Houston, Texas. She is a top-ranked public relations blogger and is known for her ability to use social media tools to forge relationships with key stakeholders.

Additionally, she has an extensive background in crisis communication and reputation management, as well as significant experience working with the media. She has garnered coverage for her clients in media outlets such as the “Washington Post,” the “Wall Street Journal,” the “CBS Early Show” and the “San Antonio Express News,” among others.

Public Relations Planning, Media Relations, Crisis Communication, Reputation Management, Online Communications

    In addition, built a B2B industry publication from the ground up and served as its editor for eight years. With MHI becoming the first client of My PR Pro when Huyse made the move from Washington, D.C. to San Antonio, Texas.
