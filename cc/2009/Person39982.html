Rob Holmes’s Summary

For over a decade, I have been operating the world's first and leading Internet IP enforcement program, which I founded. I pioneered the study of the "virtual crime scene", comprised of three primary types of evidence: physical, digital, and psychological. While most investigators were busy "thinking outside-the-box", I knew all along that there is no box.
