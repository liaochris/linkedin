George Brymer’s Summary

I am the author of Vital Integrities: How Values-Based Leaders Acquire and Preserve Their Credibility, founder and president of All Square, Inc., and the creator of The Leading from the Heart Workshop®. I have nearly three decades of leadership experience, including nineteen years working for a Fortune 500 bank. I lecture on leadership, communications, business ethics, and board governance to corporate, nonprofit, and association audiences around the country.

George Brymer’s Specialties:

The Leading from the Heart Workshop teaches leaders at all levels how to lead with heartfelt principles. This three-day workshop provides a unique opportunity to explore values-based leadership in an intimate, small group setting. By balancing information with experiential exercises that are both practical and memorable, I ensure that the lessons learned will endure long after the workshop ends.
