Dahna Chandler’s Summary

An award-winning former journalist, I'm an Arts & Entertainment Media Relations and Audience Development strategist who helps her clients create raving fans and exponentially raise their profile, ticket sales, event attendance, listeners, viewers, readers, visitors and patrons by crafting the right message to get the right media attention at the right time.

An independent practitioner since 1993, I've specialized in social/online media since 1997 and can work for English-speaking clients located anywhere in the world.

I'm passionate about my clients' work and, an expert at building audiences and attracting fans; I'm passionate about getting them publicity. I couple my media savvy with my outstanding, journalistic skills to develop creative, innovative publicity campaigns that support your overall marketing objectives as well as your immediate need for increased press visibility.

During over a decade of experience as a national journalist, I have established credibility with journalists and their organizations by understanding what they want, how and when.

Accordingly, because I know and honor what publications want for their readers, I only pitch them relevant stories, not spam them with "canned" pitches. By understanding the needs of busy journalists, editors and producers, I connect easily and build rapport with decision-makers in the top-tier print and broadcast press to get your stories press.

In cases where I write news content for you, by employing current press writing practices and crafting the press materials and pitches that provide editors and producers what they demand--interesting, newsy, content relevant to their audience--I can maximize the media attention you receive.

Let me maximize my media savvy, whether within your major US metropolitan area or nationally, to successfully execute major media campaigns on behalf of your organization, client, event or venue, to get you press.

Dahna Chandler’s Specialties:

Publicity writing, media relations and audience development strategy for A&E professionals and organizations in theater, dance, visual art (including museums and public galleries), event venues, music, movies, television, radio, online media, sports and professional speaking as well as agents/publicists and PR firms needing these services for their clients.
