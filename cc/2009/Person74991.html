Norm Page’s Summary

Same Page Capital (”SPC”) is a digital media technology consulting firm. We help start-ups develop the go-to-market strategies and provide the proof-of-concept strategic sales execution that drives initial market traction and business growth.

SPC blends unique skill sets and expertise around business, revenue, channel, and partner development as well as digital media technology marketing, product marketing, PR and media services. The end result … SPC is a powerful ally to any company looking to enter the complex market that is digital media — and thrive.

Our intimate knowledge and connections within the worlds of online publishing, content production, brand marketers, media technology, and interactive agencies allows us to provide our clients a unique opportunity to quickly and cost effectively:

» Develop new business channels that create multiple ways to monetize your business offerings.
» Refine your media technology product or service offerings such that “value” is created across all industry constituents.
» Enable buyers and partners to say Yes to your offering!

We look forward to working with each of you on a project in the near future!

Norm Page’s Specialties:

In media sales since 1991; in online media and technology since 1996; from 1996-2000 was with (2) start-ups as part of founding teams, both of which successfully went public. Specialize in boot-strapping sales organizations through growth stages.

Extensive experience successfully launching and selling media; technology-enabled ad networks; contextualization, optimization, search management, ad management, rich media, video, email, mobile, and ad exchange technology platforms.
