Joanne Meehl - www.TheJobSearchQueen.com’s Summary

Career and job search coach who's helped thousands of professionals market themselves so that they truly know what they want to do, and know how to land it. Background includes outplacement (Lee Hecht Harrison), consultative sales person, engaging speaker. Frequently-published writer with latest book out in April 2009, for new grads: YES, You Can Get That Job! Published in September 2007: The Resume Queen's Job Search Thesaurus and Career Guide.

Unique: ongoing dialog with senior hiring managers, recruiters, and HR about what they look for. Firmly believes in role as advocate who respects clients’ integrity while helping them find their best work, all with a strong sense of urgency.
