Leticia Supple’s Summary

Now managing editor (and partner) of Adelaide-based house, Brascoe Publishing, my expertise is available to those who require the assistance of a communication professional.

I am an experienced editor, and have worked on several doctoral and masters-by-research theses (in psychology, public health, nursing and general practice). I have also worked in various other fields, including IT, communications, corporate documentation, web editing and writing, and so on.

Fiction editing, non-fiction editing or academic editing all present different challenges, and I pride myself on being able to work very well at all levels: from the big picture down to the tiny detail.

Having been a published writer (in fiction and in journalism) I know well what it's like to be edited myself! A background in being on the other side of the desk has certainly been very useful in this regard.

In dealing with writers as their publisher and editor nearly every day, my history as a published writer always comes into play.

The services that my publishing house offers includes:
editing, communication consultancies, self-publishing, publishing, technical and report writing, and much, much more. Please visit our website at http://www.brascoebooks.com.au for further information.
