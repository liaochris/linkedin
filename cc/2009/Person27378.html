Manage all aspects of an IS Department in a CPA firm including staff, budget, network migrations, servers, wireless, VoIP, security, software development, mergers, regulatory compliance, policy creation, performance based metrics, business continuity plans, access control systems, alarms, and paperless office conversion. Pioneer in online training and hybrid courses at Los Medanos College and San Diego City College. Designed courseware for CISSP prep course and other security classes. Consulted as a technical expert for security related books including Microsoft Official Curriculum, and Microsoft certification exams. MCT Ambassador for Microsoft Learning at the SQL 2005 Launch. Served on various advisory committees and as a Subject Matter Expert for various projects.
