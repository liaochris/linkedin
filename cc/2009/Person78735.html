Peter Graves’s Summary

We formed Channel Computing in January 2007, offering services including e-commerce, CMS and web development, SEO, Business databases and reporting systems, analysis and design, e-commerce consultancy, internet marketing.

We have increasinlgy focused on the Internet marketing and Search Engine marketing space, and particularly enjoy achieving the best results from sites that we both design and Market, enabling us to structure things right from the very start of a project.

My personal IT experience stems from 15 years in IT, starting as an Analyst / Programmer and later as a project manager and consultant.

A background in motorcycle racing, engineering, sales and marketing gives me a broad perspective on life and business, and particularly in producing IT solutions that really support the bottom line for a business.

I lived for 5 years in New Zealand where I met Anna and we had our son Sam, we returned to the UK in late 2006.
