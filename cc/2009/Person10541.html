Ben Roberts’s Summary

Ben Roberts is the up-and-coming real estate expert, leader in modern real estate technology, and Social Media hub for the fine people of Northwest Arkansas. He splits his professional time between residential home listing and investment property acquisition and management, recently adding foreclosures and distressed properties to the mix. A full-time professional through up markets and down since 2005, Ben has the experience to tackle every real estate issue. He is establishing the #1 blogsite for real estate in Northwest Arkansas, EXIT Real Estate 540, that provides it’s readers (you) with ‘your personal edge in real estate’. Recently, he has redefined real estate marketing using the proprietary “Home Marketing Upgrade Strategy” for every listing he takes, which he uses to provide the most comprehensive real estate services in Northwest Arkansas. Ben enjoys everything from scuba diving, sailing, and freediving, to tennis, golf, and reading. He is a certified PADI scuba instructor, a gadget aficionado, and can hold his breath for over three minutes when the need arises. He lives in Northwest Arkansas with his wife and infant son.

Ben Roberts’s Specialties:

Multi-Family sales, leasing, and cash-flow analysis. Internet branding social media networking, and identity visibility. Purchase and sales negotiations. Comprehensive property marketing strategies.
