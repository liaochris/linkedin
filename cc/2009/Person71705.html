Nathan Williams’s Summary

Over 14 years experience in digital. Deep understanding of online technologies and their impact upon business and society.

I began my career working for Clarity IBD (later to become Proxicom) developing some of the first instances of web sites for brands such as The CarphoneWarehouse and Railtrack.

In the following years I have worked in a variety of leading digital agencies across a broad range of sectors, developing along the way a deep understanding of how digital affects a business, it’s brand and customers.

I joined The Partners (WPP) in 2008 as Head of Digital, tasked with developing the Digital offering and capability of the company. With a broad understanding of technology and technical implementation, and a detailed knowledge of (User) Experience Design, I’m able to bring a very rounded approach to digital planning for the Brands I work with.

Goals:
• To develop collaborative, long term relationships with the brands I work with.
• To deliver innovation in product & brand experience
• To create experiences that build long-term customer engagement and advocacy of brands.
• Understand the key trends that drive consumer & market behaviour and their impact upon brand experience.
• To have fun
