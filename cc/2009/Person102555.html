Vibhushan Waghmare vibs25@yahoo.co.in’s Summary

Vibhushan has co-founded a product-based technology startup - MQuotient Solutions Ltd. (www.mquotient.net). He is handling the Product Management division for this startup. He has been involved in conceptualizing and building the company from scratch. This startup offers software solutions for marketing challenges faced by the enterprises and help them optimize their marketing functions.
