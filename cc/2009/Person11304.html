Bill Finkelstein’s Summary

I'm the Inventor of Internet Banking. We launched first at Wells Fargo on May 18, 1995 and beat next bank to market by over 6 months.

Experienced, hands on, Sr. executive, consultant and speaker with a proven track record of success, now in semi-retirement.

I am available for selected, high impact speaking opportunities and/or consulting engagements. Contact me for more information (see our web site for my email address).

I am also available for website design and for computer, marine and amateur radio diagnosis and repair. I specialize in services to the cruising community. see: http://williamfinkelstein.com for more information.

My wife, Mary and I we are currently cruising on our Valiant 50 sailing vessel "Raptor Dance"

See http://raptordance.com for my contact information, photos and travel log.
