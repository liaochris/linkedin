Tim Purcell SVR R1’s Summary

My passion in business is to get organisations (people!) working with solutions that compliment their business. With and without technology. In the end its all about the people in your team. I am often used as a management sounding board - be their reality check.

It's not just about finding and develop the processes and tools to best suit any and every situation. It's about bringing the people along on the transformation. Without them your success it typically very limited.

Whether your business is local or international there are significant advantages to putting in place initiatives that work. A series of 2% improvements in a few areas of a business can have a significant impact to the bottom line.

I have developed and utilised this approach working both in corporate and medium/small business across various geographies and roles in a variety of industries:

- management, solution, product and business experience gained in the retail, finance, NFP associations, design, manufacturing, distribution, B2B, government, high tech and telecommunications industries

- development and growth of online & retail businesses

- defined and implemented outcome focused transformations

- managed small and large teams of people

- built significant new business opportunities throughout business career

- lived and worked in Europe and Australasia
