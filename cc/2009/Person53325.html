Karen Tursi’s Summary

CHOP Personal Chef Services provides home meal services, party services and romantic dinners throughout Chicagoland.

Using the best available ingredients, Chef Karen will develop menus to your specific tastes.

Karen Tursi’s Specialties:

HOME MEAL SERVICES
Cooked-from-scratch customized meals for singles, couples and families in the Chicagoland area. Special diets no problem! Low-sodium, low-fat, diabetic, allergies, weight loss, etc.

ROMANTIC DINNERS
Elegant five-course dinner includes table flowers, candles, framed keepsake menu, and artisan chocolates.

SMALL PARTY SERVICES
CHOP will plan, shop, cook and clean up so you can enjoy your guests. Special theme menus available.
