Jay Kingley’s Summary

Jay is the founder and CEO of Kingley Health. Kingley Health is a leading practitioner of transformational medicine integrating lifestyle management; including wellness advising (case management), medical nutrition therapy, behavioral therapy, and physical activity; orthopedic services including chiropractic, physical therapy, clinical massage, acupuncture, and physiatry; and medicine. Kingley Health focuses on the treatment and prevention of chronic lifestyle diseases including obesity and overweight plus related medical co-morbidities. Kingley Health, started in 2004, has served almost 10,000 clients out of 10 locations in NJ and PA. Kingley Health has had over 450 physicians directly refer to its programs.

Prior to starting and running Kingley Health, Jay’s record of accomplishment includes designing, developing, and operating a number of successful businesses, serving as a senior partner with two global consulting firms, and achieving entrepreneurial success. Previously, Jay headed a global venture development group at Zurich Financial Services. Jay started with Zurich as the CEO of Scudder Investment’s new business incubator group.

Prior to Zurich, Jay was a management consultant where he worked in partnership with senior client management on issues of strategy, technology, and finance while building high value practice areas for two international consulting firms. He was a senior partner and member of the Operations Committee at Diamond Management & Technology Consultants where he led the firm’s global financial services practice. He was previously a Vice President with The LEK Partnership, where he was a leader of the firm’s telecommunications practice.

Jay has been a principal in the creation of six start-up or early stage companies. Jay is currently a member of the Board of Directors of Cicero, Inc., a publicly traded company that is a leader in enterprise software for desktop automation and integration.
