Oleg Miroshnichenko (oleg@olmir.com)’s Summary

Oleg Miroshnichenko joined Dalet as project manager in 2006 after more than ten years of significant experience acquired working for international companies such as Insead, Schlumberger and the Metromedia International Group.
Oleg has accumulated valuable skills as a system administrator, software engineer for client-server applications, solution developer and IT project leader.
As a Dalet project manager, Oleg handles a variety of system deployments, from medium-size, integrated production and broadcast platforms for the Greenland Broadcasting Company (KNR), Czech Z1 TV, Ukrainian Citi TV and French Orange Sport/Orange Cinema to a very large-scale radio and TV newsroom for the Danish Broadcasting Corporation (DR) and Russian 1st Channel (ex-ORT).
Oleg holds a Masters degree with honors in theoretical physics from the Russian Slavonic University.
