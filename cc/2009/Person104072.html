Rituraj Borooah’s Summary

I co-founded X-istech in 1999 and we offer web design and development services, and recruitment process outsourcing services (RPO) within a value based delivery framework. As a full service web development house we offer a whole gamut of services:

1) Web 2.0 Graphic Design, Identity/ Logo Design and web Interfaces.
2) HTML / XHTML development: Web 2.0 CSS driven XHTML table less interface development with JavaScript, Ajax, Moo Tools, Jquery etc.
3) Web Programming: We have expertise in both Open Source (Linux, Apache, MySQL, PHP) and Microsoft Technologies (vb.net, c#, sql server 2005, site server, commerce server). Also open source frameworks like Cake PHP and open source systems like Drupal, Word Press and OS Commerce etc.
4) Quality Assurance
5) Website Maintenance.
6) Search Engine Optimization and Marketing. (SEO, SEM)

We have developed and have extensive knowledge and track record in designing and building Word Press Blogs, Ecommerce Stores, CMS driven corporate websites, Social Networking Systems and demo's can be arranged on request.

Under our RPO services we provide full-time dedicated recruiter(s); with all the necessary skills, tools and technologies for US recruitment process. Working like an extension to your team, the dedicated recruiter can support you in:
1) Source Active and passive candidates matching the positions
2) Filter: Resume screening and response handling
3) Technical Check: Preliminary technical interview
4) Interview & Negotiate rates with the consultants as per requirements and submit profiles to your account managers
5) Update and maintain your Applicant Tracking System (ATS)

Our Three Discriminating Factors:
1) Ability to understand the customers' requirements with high level of adaptability.
2) Transparent, process driven and structured development and Project Management.
3) Demonstrable track record, Cost Effective and Reliable.
