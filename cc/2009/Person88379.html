Sandra Possing’s Summary

Sandra received her B.A. in Anthropology from UCLA, where she also minored in Italian after studying abroad in Bologna, Italy. Since then, she has worked in a variety of industries including IT recruiting, financial planning, fitness, and emerging technology.

Currently, Sandra divides her time between teaching boot camp classes and doing personal training sessions, helping social media clients grow their businesses online, bartending for the colorful Marina locals, and blogging for the killer startup, MixMatchMusic.

Sandra Possing’s Specialties:

Social media strategies and implementation, writing, editing/proofreading, competitive analysis, research, communication, health/fitness.
