Michael Wolff’s Summary

With an entrepreneurial and management background in IT, telecoms, and outsourcing, my primary focus since 1995 has been in the development of solutions and infrastructures that

- support professionals to work online from anywhere, either individually or as part of a team;

- enable entrepreneurs to create, promote and operate virtual teams of professionals as sustainable businesses; and

- provide organisations of any size online access to a self-organising contingency workforce.
