Lacy J. Nelson, M.Ed., ACC’s Summary

Career Coach with over 24 years career coaching experience with executive level professionals, entry to mid-level staff, as well as graduate, college, and high school students. Received certification as Associate Certified Coach by the International Coach Federation.

Expertise includes administering and interpreting career assessments including the MBTI and Strong; resume drafting; training job seekers in job search, interview and negotiation strategies; teaching job market, employer and continuing education research skills to all levels of individuals who are in transition or who are undecided about career/life direction.

Work closely with client companies in team/employee development and transition issues. Train companies’ Senior Management and Human Resource Professionals in employee performance and separation procedures. Conduct on-site employee transition counseling. Actively network with Hiring, Human Resources, and Recruiting Managers in identifying job openings and career opportunities.

Certified in the Hogan Assessment Systems to assist companies in Employee Selection and Development.
