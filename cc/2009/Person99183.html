Tim Young’s Summary

Tim Young is the VP of Information Technology at Bright Horizons Family Solutions. He has held technology executive positions; typically in growth roles. He is a council member of CIO Executive Council and has served on the board for Nashua Children’s Learning Center and on a Work and Witness team to Panama. He has insight into leveraging relationship management to synchronize IT with communities to achieve solid business/IT alignment, while placing innovation at the center of IT strategy and vision evolution through servant leadership.

There are forces at play in the landscape around us that call for new innovative organizational and leadership capabilities. The mission is to become as strategically agile as we are operationally efficient to thrive in this increasingly disruptive landscape in this network era. To safeguard margins, we must become streams of innovation and evolve into agile teams that work together in unity with a common belief, mission and vision to impact the community we serve.

Responding quickly is not always enough; we need to know that our company is moving in the right direction. Business velocity in the right direction requires a flexible and agile technology architecture that delivers accurate real time information and enables us to rapidly adapt processes and services to meet evolving business needs.

IT Leaders in the 21st century must evolve!

Tim Young’s Specialties:

Other areas of insight include social networking, information risk and negotiation. He holds a BS in Computer Science from Fitchburg State College and a CIO Pocket MBA from Boston University School of Management. He is Quoted in CIO, Computer World & CRN magazines and has published works on Servant Leadership, IT Innovation and Vendor Relationships. He also is asked to present and teach on these topics in diverse venues.
