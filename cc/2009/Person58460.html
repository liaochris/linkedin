Tom Lehman’s Summary

An experienced, results-oriented business executive with over 25 years experience in the online industry and with technology-based products and services. Frequently working with emerging technologies and new business models, he has a strong ability to see the broader picture and translate new tools into tangible benefits for companies and non-profits and their customers and members.

He left a senior management position to form Lehman Associates in 1992 to help companies and organizations take advantage of networked information and communication. This work shifted from private networks to the Internet shortly thereafter and Lehman was an early leader. He authored an article for a professional journal in 1995 titled "Doing Business on the Internet".

Lehman Associates primary services include business and marketing strategy, Internet business development, marketing and industry research, and executive consulting. Clients include private and public companies, trade and professional associations, and other non-profits. Services to associations and related non-profits is a major part of our business.

An important current focus are consulting services to enable companies and non-profits to understand and take advantage of Web 2.0 collaboration models and technology.

Lehman Associates maintains offices in Minneapolis and Washington, DC.
