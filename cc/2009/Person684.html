Abigail Shaw’s Summary

My strength is my track record: Successful new approaches to business problems created by sudden growth.

Although I have been successful as a consultant, my best work has been done as an executive, managing diverse teams towards clear business goals. One example:

Goal: Create efficient communications platform to support company turnaround’s change management needs
Approach: Designed and lead creation of enterprise internet to support centralized company news channel.Drove and pulled employee traffic to channel, using all tactics, including provocative content, employee contributions, reliable editorial cycles, news standards to trim meaningless executive statements, and signs in the elevators.
Cost/Time Frame: $260,000 (plus internal resources)/six months
Result: Web metrics showing that all employees viewed company news page once every two days, spending an average of three minutes reading the news.
