Hans von Sichart’s Summary

I am currently working with the Voice Technology Group at Cisco in San Jose. Occasionally, I teach at the University of Santa Cruz, Silicon Valley Extension. In the past, I was a designer at firms such as frogdesign, Sapient, Philips Design and Apple. I was lucky to work with very talented people, our work has received international awards and hopefully made quite a few users happy.

- Experience designing Desktop Software, Web 2.0 applications and Consumer Products.
- Demonstrated ability to lead designers and facilitate collaboration with business owners and engineers.
- Deploying a User Centered Design approach that guarantees innovative products that users love.
- Passionate about life-long learning, teaching design and growing individuals.

Hans von Sichart’s Specialties:

- Creative direction for concept development, design, specification, production and implementation.
- Excellent communication and presentation skills including practice and departmental management, people growth, team management and client and vendor relations.
- Expert of User Centered Design, including visual design and interaction design.
