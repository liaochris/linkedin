Helena Casimiro’s Summary

The Racanelli Construction Company, Inc. has at its foundation over five decades of building excellence. Now, in its third generation, the Company's high quality, customer oriented services are a testimony to the personal integrity and experience of its principals.

Through industry advancements and market fluctuations, one thing has remained constant: Racanelli's commitment to the highest standards of construction quality. Today, that commitment is evident in the Company's extensive portfolio of corporate, industrial, healthcare and retail projects.


Modern techniques are used to provide efficient cost estimations and thorough evaluation of subcontractors' bids. Sophisticated computer aided design and engineering systems, materials order tracking systems, project monitoring and reporting programs further promote project integrity and delivery according to original specifications.
