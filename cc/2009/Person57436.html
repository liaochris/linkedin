LaQuitta Jones’s Summary

Hello I am LaQuitta Terrell Jones, president of Terrell Tax & Planning, LLC a bookkeeping, billing and tax preparation services company located in Atlanta, Ga. We specialize in assisting entrepreneurs, contract professionals, small business owners and small to mid-size corporations with accurate record keeping, consistent invoices and timely tax filings.

We have provided exceptional services for the last four years to clients in various industries; Retail Stores, Caterers, Restaurants, Engineers, Designers, Realtor and more.

Our services are mobile and technology driven, this has allowed us to services not only Atlanta but surrounding cities and states, with clients in Florida, Alabama, Tennessee, Louisiana and the Carolina.

Let us get your finances in order and stay that way!
