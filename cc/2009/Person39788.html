Henrik Berggren’s Summary

I´m very passionate about technology and has always been. My interest started growing when I got my first computer, a C64, when I was ten years old. From there it has developed into something great that now is involved is almost everything I do. I like my work to be structured and try to use the strategies of GTD (Getting Things Done) as much as I can. My biggest interests in life are the web, interaction, communication and friends. Patience is not my strongest side but I try to turn it into something positive as often as I can by reflecting on how to make things better, faster and more effective. I have worked a lot with programming and software development but have over the last years shifted focus to business development and strategies in our rapidly changing digital landscape. In my spare time I enjoy fishing, reading and music.
