Vern Westgate’s Summary

I do a lot of copyediting. If you have a book or any other written matter that you want made far better...clear, concise and readable..call me. I do books regularly, 1 or 2 per week, for www.StrategicBookGroup.com.

I think like a systems engineer and project manager as I worked as both.

I'll communicate clearly and concisely to your audience.

I write and edit technical, business and other non-fiction documents. (Some of the resumes I did for folks may have been fiction...I wrote what they told me).

I learned Proposal management at Hughes Aircraft. I learned the STOP technique from Jim Tracey, who orignated it. It's a hands down winner for most proposals.

I've written professionally since 1960 or so. My best gig was writing Apollo Spacecraft Operating Procedures and working daily with the astronauts.

I've written for large companies...North American Aviation, Boeing, Hughes Aircraft, Nissan...and a wide range of mid-size and small businesses.

I'm particularly good at solving productivity, production and quality problems, then documenting the solution so the problem doesn't come up again...

I presented seminars for Fred Pryor Seminars for a year, have spoken to large and small conferences, business groups, companies and church groups.

I developed and delivered a significant number of training programs for technical, management and business skills.

Check out my website: www.vernwrites.com
