Sean Lenihan’s Summary

Xtension Marketing is a Los Angeles-based, turn-key engagement marketing company. Using a virtual business model, Xtension Marketing provides cutting-edge creative, top-notch event management and cost-effective budgeting, to execute a wide range of programs. Based on a proven strategy, our programs provide a measurable return on their investment. This progressive virtual agency model for engagement marketing allows more of the client’s budget to be invested into the activation of a program.

As a turn-key provider of creative services, event strategy, onsite display presence and services, complete event production management and post-event analysis, Xtension Marketing provides the services to assist our clients consumer marketing efforts. With our engagement marketing know-how and proven onsite-strategy planning, your budget can be stretched further to reach more consumers and add to your bottom line.

Formerly operating as Automotive Displays LLC, Xtension Marketing has enjoyed event success with top global brands such Toyota, Lexus, Xbox 360, Aston Martin, and Subway, to name a few.
