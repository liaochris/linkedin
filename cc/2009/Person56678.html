Kris Underwood’s Summary

My work has been published at several magazines including MotherVerse, Mom Writer's Literary Magazine, mamazine.com and Poetry Midwest. Additionally, I've reviewed books for MotherTalk, University of Michigan Press and Da Capo Press.
I currently hold the position of Managing Editor at Mamazina Magazine, formerly Mom Writer's Literary Magazine. I've also taken on the role of mentor at the MotherVerse Workshops (MotherVerse Magazine).
For examples of my writing, please visit My Blog http://krisunderwood.blogspot.com/
