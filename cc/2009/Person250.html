Joe High’s Summary

I am a Financial Coach, a Financial Planner. Our mission is inspiring our clients to achieve their goals through education, communication, and service that exceeds their expectations. Our number one goal: INCREASE YOUR PAYCHECK IN RETIREMENT!

I love helping my clients position themselves to live THEIR dreams! I base the goals you have on your values. We DREAM, we DEFINE GOALS, and then we TRACK! I work with businessowners & executives of fast-growth companies who are genuinely nice people who want to delegate their financial planning, so they can do what they love to do. In the end, it is our goal to help you towards The Ultimate Gift -- planning to create legacies that leave monies to groups that serve your passions!

401k expert; Estate & Retirement Planning expert especially in the areas of "stretch" IRA's. Accredited Asset Management Specialist (AAMS). Financial Planning.
