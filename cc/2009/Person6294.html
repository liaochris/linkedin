Angela Siefer’s Summary

I envision a world in which all members of society have the tools and the resources to use the Internet for the betterment of themselves and their communities. I work toward this goal both in my advocacy efforts for equity access to technology and in my work at Shiny Door.

I understand the challenges of small businesses and nonprofits. I believe integrating online and offline networking can create an immensely strong network; a network you can genuinely use to promote your business.

I assist small business owners, job searchers, membership based nonprofits, and freelancers with understanding the value of social networking. I teach strategic use of carefully chosen social networking tools both in my workshops and in my consulting to help you promote and build your organization/ your business/yourself.

Angela Siefer’s Experience

  • Founder & Social Networking Consultant

    ShinyDoor

    (Internet industry)

    November 2008 — Present (9 months)

    I provide social networking workshops and guidance to the hesitant. I bootstrapped the launch of Shiny Door in 3 weeks, became profitable in 8 weeks and hired an employee in 4 months from inception.

    Clients include a federal agency, a state agency, non-profit organizations, and membership based organizations. I provide some workshops through partnerships with career centers and universities.

    All trainings are conducted in person and include the following:

    • An explanation of the value of a particular online activity and the expected outcome,
    • Recommendations of easy to use tools for professionals
    • Open discussion on personal vs professional social networking, privacy concerns, message control, and social networking strategies,
    • Detailed instructions of how to set up and use the particular tools being recommended, and
    • A minimum of geek speak. I promise!
