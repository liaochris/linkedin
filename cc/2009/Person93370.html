Nigel Spencer’s Summary

Nigel Spencer is a seasoned expert in building rich client software applications. With over 15 years of commercial experience, 7 of those on the .NET platform, he has been involved in designing and coding across all tiers of application development. His current focus has been on building scalable line of business applications using a best practices approach to development with Windows Presentation Foundation (WPF).

As a successful leader of several software teams, from start-up through to delivery and support, Nigel's role has also included many mentoring aspects. From teaching .NET fundamentals to large teams through to providing training for more advanced and emerging software patterns such as MVC and MVVM.

Nigel is passionate about continuous development and keeps abreast of trends in both technology and software engineering practices with a view to how they can be pragmatically applied in building better software. He keeps a running commentary of his thoughts on client application development on his blog at blog.spencen.com.

Nigel Spencer’s Specialties:

.NET, C#, VB.NET, WPF, LINQ, Sync Framework, MbUnit, RhinoMocks, SVN, TFS
