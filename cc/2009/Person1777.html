Adrien Agoado’s Summary

Consumer Connection, Inc. is a national search firm that has been in business since 1982. I joined CCI in 1994 after spending several years with Coopers & Lybrand. We have 10 recruiters and 4 recruiting assistants in my office specializing in different fields within the CPG/consumer products industry. I, personally, focus in brand management/consumer marketing and complete over 25 search assignments yearly (our company typically fills 140). Some of my clients include Reckitt Benckiser, Dannon, Schering Plough, Coty, Alcoa, Starbucks, Cranium, Microsoft and T-Mobile. I work on a national level and my clients are located not just in Seattle, but in New York, New Jersey, Connecticut, Cincinnati, Charlotte, Atlanta, Chicago, Minneapolis, Denver, Los Angeles and San Francisco (just to name a few).
