Fred Destin’s Summary

I am a venture capitalist focusing on early and mid stage investment opportunities. I look for high potential entrepreneurs who want to build great companies to support them in their project.

Venture investing is a long-term business, and so is company creation, therefore I only seek to work with real professionals who are dedicated to building sustainable companies.

I do this job because I get a real kick out of chasing the best opportunities, getting to meet great entrepreneurs and facing the challenges of growth with them.

In a bid to help entrepreneurs get into the minds of VCs, I write an entreneurship focused blog @ www.freddestin.com
