Mohan Tavorath’s Summary

Chief Executive Officer with experience leading all aspects of business and technology strategy, consulting, operations, software product development and information technology management.

Over 19 years of experience driving business results via the strategic application of innovative technology solutions at large Fortune 100 corporations, premier Big 4 consulting firms and innovative and entrepreneurial startups.

Led current firm through a multi-year high-growth technology-driven transformation - recognized by awards in the Inc. 500, Deloitte North America Technology Fast 500 and Software 500.

Held executive and technology leadership positions in both North America and Asia. Graduated with an MBA in Business Strategy from Columbia University in New York and Masters and Bachelors degrees in Computer Science from the University of Pune, India.
