Doug Smith’s Summary

I help people and organizations develop their leadership skills. My speciality is working with supervisors and managers. Using highly interactive training workshops and coaching I help people to develop centered leadership with courage, creativity, clarity and compassion.

Doug Smith’s Specialties:

Leadership training, leadership coaching, and leadership consulting. I blend highly interactive guided discovery training with coaching and fun learning activities from the performing arts to help leaders develop their confidence, expertise, and effectiveness.
