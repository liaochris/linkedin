Leslie Ciborowski’s Summary

Leslie has over 25 years of a multi-faceted background in training, management, sales, and technology. With proven success in managing people, budgets and projects; analyzing and managing business change; assessing, developing and deploying skills improvement programs; a strong background in technology; a passion for working with people; and a solid understanding of business processes, she is able to assist organizations maximize their most important resource: people.

Her strong training background, organizational skills, and exceptional development expertise, augment her extensive sales and marketing abilities.

She is the President and Founder of TrainSmart, a certified woman-owned, full-service training and consulting business, which began in 1994. Leslie formed TrainSmart after successfully establishing a training division for a software development company, a technology training division for a multi-faceted training organization, a successful startup consulting company and 11 years with a national retail chain where her experience was a progression of training needs assessments, instructional design and development, project management and management.

Assessing the needs of an organization or project and then delivering practical leadership development initiatives and marketing these programs effectively throughout an organization are key to her success.
