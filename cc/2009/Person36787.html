Gloria Regalbuto Bentley’s Summary

I am a highly experienced Chief Learning Officer with special expertise in starting-up or re-structuring learning and development functions to increase effectiveness and alignment with the organization's mission and vision.

I have worked in very avante garde talent management settings and am adept at using competency definition and assessment to put an organization's human capital to its most appropriate and best use.

Gloria Regalbuto Bentley’s Specialties:

Management of the Learning Function; Competency Definition and Assessment; Talent Management; Process Design and Analysis; Performance Management; Organizational Design; Leadership Development; Instructional Design (multimedia; web-based; Instructor-led; self-paced, etc.)

    August 2003 — May 2005 (1 year 10 months)

I re-organized and integrated the learning and development function across the corporation’s divisions, geographic locations, people management functions, and with business strategy. The Learning and Development department now provides learning interventions across the value chain, including enhancements and support for broad-based, profit generating customer (distributor) training function. We provided support for world-class manufacturing strategy, new processes and work-practices in marketing, sales and distribution. I designed and implemented a new Competency Acquisition Process (CAP) that aligned with the Performance Management and Strategic People Resourcing process. In addition, I restructured the way the corporation budgets and accounts for learning and development expenses.
