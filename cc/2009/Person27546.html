Donna Talarico’s Summary

I am a Senior Account Manager at Solid Cactus, a total e-commerce solutions company based in Wilkes-Barre, Pa. Solid Cactus was named an Inc. 500 Fastest Growing Companies in America, at #255, and is the 11th Best Place to Work in PA, up from #21 in last year's nod. I also write for Solid Cactus's eBiz Insider, a nationally-distributed e-commerce magazine with a circulation of 16,000 and regularly contribute to the corporate blog.

I earned a BA in Communication Studies from Wilkes University and am pursuing an MA in Creative Writing, also at Wilkes.

I am a contributing writer to several local and national publications.

Previously, I was an admissions and outreach representative for the career school industry and a radio station promotions director.

I have also combined my interest in working with adult learners and writing to self publish a book. In 2005 I released "Kids, Have You Seen My Backpack?" and Other Inspirational Stories on Non-Traditional Students: An Adult Learner Anthology.

In 2007, my compilation "Wilkes University: Off the Record" was released by College Prowler, a national college guidebook publisher, offering student-written guides for over 200 colleges and universities.
