Jeremy Likness’s Summary

I am an entrepreneur at heart. My field of expertise for the past 15 years has been technology, specifically software development, in various industries including insurance, health and wellness, supply chain management, Wi-Fi hotspots, mobile devices, and WLAN management.

I established my professional reputation as a software development expert through my role as development manager at Manhattan Associates in 1996 while working on custom code and enhancements for their flagship PkMS project and overseeing a team of developers. As Director of IT for Dr. Tango (Hispanicare) I was able to practice my Spanish-speaking skills while I grew our technology infrastructure to support a 250% increase in staff and served as architect for the next generation platform for our multilingual diet software built on top of a custom content management engine.

I founded an online health and wellness business in 2004 and was one of the first online fitness trainers to use podcasts to reach a broad audience through online blogs and fitness lessons. I produced and sold several CD sets internationally and published my book, "Lose Fat, Not Faith" which has sold thousands of copies in over a dozen countries around the world.

As development manager and architect for Wandering WiFi, I continue to help us grow and solidify our position as one of the leading wireless technology solution providers in the United States. I oversee the development of our product portfolio that includes HotSpot solutions and AirWatch™, a management console specific to managing enterprise grade wireless networks, mobile devices, and the users that rely on this environment to do their job. AirWatch™ is a Rich Internet Application (RIA) built on Microsoft technologies that automatically monitors wireless networks and mobile devices, providing intelligent monitoring and automatic resolution of the issues that impact mobile users and / or information security.
