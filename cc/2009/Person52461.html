Jurrie Hobers’s Summary

I'm a motivated and self starting professional and find great joy in constantly challenging myself and my team to get better at what we do.

I am passionate about video games and (web)technology in general. I think innovation in its broadest way is the driving force of companies, great and very much practical usable in your day to day work if you just pay attention. I learn every day and enjoy pushing boundaries in standard patterns to find new and better ways that give higher returns.

I have a strong commercial sense and always try to find a model that drives interest of all parties involved.
I find huge inspiration in the person of Seth Godin and his motto: 'Be remarkable or be invisible' which I am using in my daily business.

My goal in life is to constantly grow through experience and use that both in my personal and professional life to make a difference to the things, companies & people I care about.

Quote: "There is no shortcut to sustainable success."
