Laurent Janssens’s Summary

Being the bridge that establishes smart partnership between IT and Business.

What are the expectations of business for the IT department?
Does IT deliver services that are in line with the business strategy?
What is the value of IT?
Is IT a business enabler or a simple utility?
Who takes the IT decisions?

IT Governance has the objective to repsond to all those questions...
For this, IT'ers should be like chameleons, able to understand the business but ITers should be around the table when strategic decisions are taken (like M&A, new products, etc).

IT Governance is not just the "control" part (even that i have interesting experience to share on this matter, please refer to our article in the IT Cutter journal (january 2007) or Isaca Control Journal (June 2007), it is also the directing part....

Our motto: "being an independant and pragmatic partner of CIO for IT optimisation"

We regularly faciliate workshops with IT executives on those hot topics.
