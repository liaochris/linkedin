Eric Christ’s Summary

Implemented a rapid development process at McKesson, which in the first 12 months, delivered 10 new products generating $22 million in sales.
Transformed Product Delivery team at 1stAg from start-up mode to a professional organization by creating a customer relationship process that integrated development, marketing, and customer service.
Grew an internal project team into a strategic business unit that delivered $16 million in revenue for Harbinger in 2000, an increase of 650 percent over 1999.
Developed new Internet-based electronic commerce products that led to the formation of $12 million joint venture between Harbinger and BellSouth.
