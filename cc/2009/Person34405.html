Fred Stein’s Summary

As a consultant I help companies grow their business through partnerships. Since 1998, I have helped Sun and other companies, in Mobile Broadband by:

* Finding new partners;
* Producing proposals to win new deals
* Forging consensus with partners on commercial and technical terms;
* Building joint teams to bring new products to market;
* And managing partner projects to revenue generation.

Proposals ranged from $1M to over $100M for multi-year deals with the following companies:

As a Program Manager, I managed scope, set milestones and delivered on time.
