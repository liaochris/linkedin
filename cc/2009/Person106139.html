Yuriy Gusev’s Summary

• Over 12 years of interdisciplinary research experience in US academic and industry laboratories in the areas of Systems Biology, Bioinformatics, Computational Biology and Cancer Research;
• Biomarker Discovery with Next Generation Sequencing; Molecular Diagnostics; Genomics and Proteomics for Cancer Research
• Over 6 years of Cancer Research experience at the Johns Hopkins School of Medicine.
Over 45 peer-reviewed publications including systems biology of human cancer, microRNA and gene expression profiling, mathematical modeling, computer simulation and statistical analysis in Cancer Research
