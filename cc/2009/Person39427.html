Eric Hemati’s Summary

I have been in sales and sales management since 1994, beginning as a door-to-door bookman to work my way through college. My primary specialty is forming sales teams that can generate revenue in undeveloped territories within a 90-day period. My secondary specialty is mentoring sales managers and salespeople into top-level performers by establishing success habits that they will take with them into any future position.

  • Partner

    The Parish

    (Entertainment industry)

    February 2008 — Present (1 year 6 months)

    The Parish room is one of the most adored live music venues in the Country, by fans & artists alike, housing arguably the best indoor sound in all of Texas. The Parish has physical character, hardwood floors, and a great musical history. It has attracted some of the best acts in the independent music scene, as well as rock legends. The Parish offers live music mostly 7 nights per week, and is non-genre specific.

    The Parish has hosted artists ranging from My Morning Jacket, Grupo Fantasma, Sharon Jones & the Dap Kings, and Black Rebel Motorcycle Club, to Ingird Michaelson, Jens Lekman, She & Him, and The Weakerthans. One of the favorite shows to date was at SXSW 2007, when the Parish hosted the a la carte jam including Tom Morello (Rage Against the Machine), Perry Farrell (Jane’s Addiction), Pete Townshend (Who), Les Claypool (Primus) and Slash (Guns n Roses). For future shows and festival events, check out the Parish calendar.
