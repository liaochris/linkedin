As a successful entrepreneur, international speaker, writer, and university professor, Carmen's collective experience is supported by the strength of a network of industry authorities and opinion leaders. She has served as a partner with CEOs and their executive teams to help solve some of their largest and most complex strategic business and marketing challenges on Latin American Markets.

While working with prominent hospitals and doctors, Carmen launched Carmen Bracamonte & Co., a market research and market intelligence firm with focus on Healthcare and Hispanic Demographics. Carmen looks at clients in the healthcare industry from a variety of vantage points to understand how PR can have the greatest impact. She sets benchmarks at the start of each campaign and measure our success at key milestones.
