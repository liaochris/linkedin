Luigi Grimaldi’s Summary

Luigi participated in the research and planning of one of the first broadcast data transmissions. He registered the Radiotext, Radiosoftware and Telesoftware trademarks, which were very popular in Italy at the onset of the personal and home computers. He collaborated with the Media division of IBM Semea and founded Apis Niger Software in the Publishing production area and RGB-CMYK conversion systems. After the mid 90s, he became CEO of Navità and President of Sinedita, a leading company in newspaper production systems. He has been a part of the Italian Internet as founder of Exibart.com and as CEO of Columbus Net and then became CEO of Unione Editoriale SpA. Since 2003 he has been CEO of Exenia and Exenia Net. Currently he is fully engaged in the launch of his new creatures, we+ workspace, and how enterprise 2.0 evangelizer.
