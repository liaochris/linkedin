Janet (Ferguson) Keeley’s Summary

I have more than 20 years of experience as a writer/editor/manager in corporate marketing communications departments and advertising agencies. I have worked to promote a broad range of products and services -- from chemicals, insulation, phone books and life insurance to computers, professional services, outdoor adventure gear, automotive air bags, industrial slicers, colocation and hosting services. I love a challenge, thrive on learning, and find almost any subject interesting. My expertise covers everything from messaging strategy to newsletters, brochures, advertising copy, Web sites and presentation writing. Call or e-mail me to see how I can help you with your project. SEE WWW.KEELEYCOMMUNICATIONS.COM FOR MORE INFORMATION.
