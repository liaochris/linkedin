Ronald van Barneveld’s Summary

Ronald van Barneveld is a multi-purpose, analytical person and entrepreneur. Ronald is working in the ICT world since 1992 and as a result has a excellent understanding of the ICT related topics, technologies and solutions and how these can assist in achieving better business results.
As an experienced IT architect, Ronald has a substantial broad technical knowledge of the various topics surrounding ICT infrastructures to be able to translate customer (business) demands into flexible, creative and innovative end-to-end solutions.
