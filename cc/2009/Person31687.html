Ernest Smyth’s Summary

Ernest Smyth is a business growth & development expert helping companies increase visibilty while keeping advertising and marketing spend under control. He effectively utilizes social media and market research to increase corporate revenues and expand customer base.

He puts a high premium on turning timely and pertinent advertising and marketing data into actionable direction for business growth based on the buying / usage habits of their best customers.

Ernest is a leader with broad experience managing and streamlining businesses, defining and implementing growth strategies, defining core customers, and identifying effective media to reach those customers. Direct experience with increasing revenues through social media, Internet advertising, traditional advertising, research, marketing, public speaking, management, and team motivation.

Interested in early-stage startups as well as well-positioned companies with significant upside potential.
