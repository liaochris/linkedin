Donata Morandi’s Summary

Donata is the founder of 2Glamour Inc . 2Glamour offers staging and interior decorating services as well as Event Planning and Management.
She moved to Canada from Milan, Italy. A busy mother of four, this dynamo was an intricate part of the design team at 2Glamour.com in Milan, a design firm which specializes in staging, refining, acquiring "hard to find" antiquities and home organization. She was also partner and COO for 9Net Websolutions, a primary Italian webhosting Company that was sold to third parties in 2003.
Upon her arrival into Canada, she spent considerable time decorating her new home and was asked to give advice to her new friends about their homes. Based on the rave reviews she received for her work, Donata was inspired to carry on the 2Glamour tradition and opened a sister company in Toronto, appropriately named 2Glamour Inc.
Donata is a fully Accredited Staging® Professional™ and offers staging, interior decorating, home and office organization.

She is also the Event Coordinator at CIIM Canada, a not for profit organization, where she manages their conferences, galas and other fundraising activities. More information on www.ciim.ca or www.ciimtorontoawards.ca

Her past work background ranges from acting as founder and COO of an internet based company, where she managed a team of 30+ people, to public relations and sales for a primary fashion designer in Italy.

In her personal time, Donata is strongly involved with her community. She is Vice Chair on the school Council and an active member with The ontario Association of Parents in Catholic Education (OAPCE).

She was a member and former Chairperson for Canada for Italian Women World, a networking organization that focuses on bringing Italian women professionals around the world together.

She was on the board of the Toronto Chapter of the Canadian Celiac Association, a charity that provides support for those suffering from celiac disease.
