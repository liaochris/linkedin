Jeffrey Carpenter’s Summary

Jeffrey Carpenter specializes in aiding higher education institutions in all phases of marketing communications. He has successfully facilitated projects to include process evaluation, marketing materials make-over, website redesign and redirection, as well as defining a clear marketing message and strategy.

Mr. Carpenter is also highly involved with higher education retention and mentoring, as well as providing client colleges with access to military students via Vantage Point's military marketing programs.

Using his Project Management skills, Mr. Carpenter is able to lead large and small team projects from inception to delivery, setting up interim goals and benchmarks, and meeting stated deadlines.
