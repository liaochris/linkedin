Eric Gilboord’s Summary

1. The Expert Small Business Calls for Marketing Advice ... That's Easy to Understand

www.EricGilboord.com

Eric is a specialist in marketing small and medium-sized businesses. He is a popular speaker, columnist, and author of many articles and books on moving businesses up to the next level.

He prides himself in his ability to communicate and connect with SMB audiences. Offering them a clear understanding of marketing in a simplified language so any business owner can understand. He removes the mystery of marketing and make it accessible to everyone no matter how little their previous marketing experience may be.

He has worked as a marketing executive for the past 30 years in Canadian and International advertising agencies as well as specialty shops. As a marketing expert, he has solid experience in all aspects of marketing. His work history includes national, retail and business to business advertising as well as direct marketing and promotion, covering various industries. His services have been provided to Ontario Lottery Corporation, Ontario Hydro, Ford, Converse, AT & T, Swiss Chalet, CBS Records, Budget Rent-A-Car and many other companies. For the past 14 years he has worked as a coach and a marketing supplier to hundreds of small & medium-sized businesses.

To learn more about Eric visit www.EricGilboord.com or call 416-686-2466.
