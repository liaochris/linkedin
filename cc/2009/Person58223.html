    In this new project, having designed the framework of this system, there is need to design and begin implementation of a prototype and its deployment in real world. Botnets are networks of infected computers controlled remotely by an intruder in a synchronous, are currently the greatest threat to Internet security.
    Our solution proposed in the previous project is to enhance the collective intelligence to share information of thousands of computers to detect the behavior of Botnets and prevent their actions. This distributed form allows each team to share the little information that allows correlation of the same action on other computers protected.
    The idea, as defined in the final report of the group, proved to be implementable and high scalability. The objectives of the current project are to develop a prototype for testing, design
    best way to distribute it on the market and subsequently achieve an implementation used on the Internet.
