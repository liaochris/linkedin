Bobbin Beam’s Summary

Voiceover industry veteran, Bobbin Beam comes fully loaded with professional sound booth, and is available via ISDN , Source-Connect, and phone patch.

Recent clients: Cortislim, Microsoft, Perfumania, Cisco Systems, LG, Doubletree Hotel Orlando, Expedia.com, American Heart Association, Ramada Inn. FAST Production/turnaround. Broadcast-quality audio files can be delivered globally over the internet via FTP site in your preferred format (.wav, MP3, .aiff, etc.). Where space and bandwidth allow, files can also be emailed. Ground-delivered CD's are an option as well.

Bobbin Beam's wide vocal range is ideal for commercials, in-store announcements, narration, message on hold, voice prompt/IVR , audio books, radio and television sweepers, promos/imaging, video games and animation character styles. Bobbin specializes in technical scripts. Her sound is flexible in a variety of ages and presents a warm, real, conversational, friendly, clear, authoritative, and always-believable delivery. She is highly directable. When you need just the right voice and a professional who will get the job done quickly and accurately, many happy clients have worked with Bobbin Beam.

Bobbin Beam’s Specialties:

Radio production, script writing, marketing, graphic design. A state of the art professional recording studio, isolation booth, complete with ProTools, Neumann TLM193 microphone.
