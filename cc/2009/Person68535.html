Mike Hawkins’s Summary

People and fiscal management with a new business development focus within an agile and fast paced environment. Fast growth period specialist. Thrives on shaping impossible ideas into working commercial business models.

Today Mike is pioneering a marketing and advertising concept that embraces all facets of targeted media and technology. The Pilot launched 3rd April 2009.

In 2006, added retail management to the portfolio operating a small niche business based in Hawthorn Victoria.

Mike previously managed the nationwide sales team and was responsible for marketing, sales and customer service within an Internet Based Software services organisation. Mike has a vast sales and recruitment experience from his lengthy recruitment consultant career with such companies as Adecco and Dunhill (now Robert Walters). Combining his recruitment background with senior executive roles across diverse industries including financial, logistics and FMCG, Mike drove BigRedSky to be the fastest growing Australian provider of e-Recruitment systems.
