Collin Croome’s Summary

I am a multimedia, online-marketing and social media expert with 20 years of experience in the new media industry and creative consultancies.

I specialized in producing and managing large scale online projects and campaigns for international premium brands from various industries, like fashion, lifestyle, luxury and information technology.

I've started and successfully managed my own multimedia design agency for over 13 years. I have a large network of talented creative people in all fields of new media production from concept, design, animation to programming.

Besides I would call myself an Apple expert and long-term Apple fellow with wide knowledge of Apple’s products, services and technologies. From 1991 to 2001 I worked for Apple Germany as a new media evangelist and multimedia marketing specialist.

I am a Green Card holder with ambition to live and work in the US...
