Marko Dugonjić’s Summary

In Croatia and abroad Marko is recognized as an expert on the web standards and standards-based web sites.

By keeping things simple, he is able to build accessible and easy to use web interfaces which are both visually appealing and highly usable.

Marko is nowadays Creative and User Experience director at Creative Nights, a nanoscale web design studio he found in early 2009., working closely with local and international most notable web designers and developers on selected projects.

He is also the author of Typetester, an online application for web designers.
