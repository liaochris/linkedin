Yuval Zukerman’s Summary

My technical experience revolves around my ability to be a jack of all trades. I never scoff at a technology if it gets me closer to the solution and as such I try to attain as much knowledge in as many technologies as possible.

I believe that you have to either teach or learn, but you have to do one or the other or you will stagnate. I am passionate about my work and will go to great length to ensure clients are happy. My strength is in bringing the know-how and drive to envision answers to problems.

My current research focus in on social networks and how marketing entities can leverage them to engage consumers in new ways, building loyalty and fostering business.

The list of clients I helped includes The Coca-Cola Company, eBay, Rentokil-Initial, Belo Media, PC Connection and The Greater Boston Food Bank.
