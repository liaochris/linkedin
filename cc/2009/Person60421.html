Laura Slade’s Summary

I am a Chartered Marketer and Microsoft Certified Application Specialist with extensive communications experience across print, live and electronic media and have a successful product marketing background in hardware and software.
Able to operate well at both strategic and tactical levels, I have experience of managing staff, agencies and significant A&P budgets. Naturally organised and collaborative with good IT skills, I am able to improve marketing systems or processes to best serve the client.
A tenacious, committed and self motivated communications professional, I can be a passionate advocate for the right brand, service or change programme.
