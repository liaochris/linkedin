Steve Zimba’s Summary

Proven track record of success with 21 years experience in the communications and technology industry, developing and growing a variety of businesses. In depth knowledge of the converging communications, entertainment & IT services marketplaces. A visionary, a creator, and a results driven leader with a talent for identifying emerging technology opportunities and transforming them to fully functioning businesses with significant revenues and value.

Business Creation: proven track record identifying new business opportunities and transforming emerging technologies into profitable lines of business - led strategic planning and business development for large, integrated product portfolios and businesses

Financial & Organizational Management: experienced in managing teams and finances of a business, in building complex business cases and securing funding for new products and initiatives – managed large organizations, budgets and P&L for several products/projects
International: significant experience building business in international markets including EMEA, LATAM and APAC – strong connections in and knowledge of these markets in addition to North America
Partner Management: drove several strategic deals - negotiated and managed multi-million dollar contracts with customers, suppliers, and strategic alliance partners
Product Development: managed several large scale development projects - well practiced in program and project management methodologies
