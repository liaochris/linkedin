Miles Bennett’s Summary

I am a web analytics consultant and internet geek. Freely admit it! I like all things Web 2.0 and more importantly in understanding how they affect busineses in both a positive and negative way.

My company TargetStone has helped many of the UK and European retail financial services sites set up and get value from their web analytics solutions. These brands include Barclaycard, Barclays Bank, Mint Credit Card, Royal Bank of Scotland, NatWest, Abbey.com, Lombard Direct, Ulster Bank, Coutts.

I have also built customer acquisition / retention strategies and enhanced the customer experience through customer surveys, analysis and internal focus groups.
