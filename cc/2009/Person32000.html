Eugene Castro’s Summary

Mr. Eugene Castro received his Doctorate of Jurisprudence from New York Law School in June 1985. Eugene Castro’s J.D. came after his scholastic achievement of a Bachelor of Arts degree in Urban Legal Studies from the City College of New York, where Mr. Eugene Castro was the Captain of the Varsity Tennis Team and graduated with a 3.7 grade point average.

Eugene Castro’s scholastic accomplishments have been followed by a series of diverse and innovative business ventures. In 2003, Mr. Eugene Castro opened the Sound Sleep Center, and he served as President of the sleep diagnostics center until December 2007. The Sound Sleep Center, based in Maple Grove, Minnesota, has been credited as the largest independently owned, freestanding sleep diagnostic facility in the Midwest, and Mr. Eugene Castro’s leadership role within the enterprise accounts for no small part of the achievement. Eugene Castro, as President of the Sound Sleep Center, held responsibilities in marketing, supervising, hiring, funding, contract acquisition, and managing insurance affiliations. The Sound Sleep Center, directed by Eugene Castro, is also the notable recipient of full accreditation from the Academy of Sleep Science.
