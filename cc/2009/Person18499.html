  • Board Advisor

    Style Coalition

    (Apparel & Fashion industry)

    2008 — Present (1 year )

    Style Coalition combines two of my passions: fashion and new media. We are supporting serious and qualified online coverage of the fashion world through celebrating and promoting fashion bloggers as recognized and respected members of the media.

    Our mission is simple - to get designers, trendsetters, tastemakers and other in the business of fashion to respect that independent writers/bloggers/journalists are indeed influential and will continue to have a heavy hand in shaping the trends, purchases and ultimately, success, of fashion brands.

    SC seeks to bring the voices of fashion bloggers together and let them be heard.
