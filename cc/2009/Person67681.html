Michael Sacks’s Summary

A dedicated, hard-working, award-winning communications professional who has marketed everything from iris recognition technology to talking stuffed animals that promote family values.

Throughout my 16-year career, I have provided PR and Marketing Counsel to many prestigious law firms and medical professionals throughout the Philadelphia region. My diverse client list has also extended to businesses and organizations in both the corporate and non-profit sectors; including builders, technology companies, staffing agencies, the March of Dimes, and even Mister Softee.

For the past eight years, I have dedicated myself to providing "lemon-aid" for distressed drivers as Director of Communications & Client Services for Kimmel and Silverman, the Nation's oldest and largest lemon law and automotive consumer advocacy firm (perhaps best known as 1-800-LEMON-LAW). Responsibilities include developing public relations and consumer education campaigns; managing the annual marketing budget; and coordinating all print, broadcast, outdoor and internet advertising. Our Firm has been featured in over 500 consumer stories, and I have been honored to work with such media outlets as Good Morning America, The CBS Early Show, Extra, Heloise Radio, Kiplingers, Consumer Reports, and Black Enterprise Magazine.
