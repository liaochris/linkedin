Jessica Lindsay’s Summary

Im an illustrator, colorist and character designer! Ive worked with animation, gaming, media and publishing companies as a freelancer for 2 years now. I also do some work in-house for Pascal Blais studio as a layout colorist, ive worked on two mini wheats commercials as well as a marinela tv spot. I love my work and there's nothing i would like more than to help others realize their projects.

I studied at cegep du Vieux-Montreal, where I completed my first animated short "Uno". I do animation on the side, but I mostly do illustration. I have 3 books that are published up to now and that are listed below, with more in the works.

I master coloring programs such as Photoshop and Painter, and i know a little bit of flash as well.
