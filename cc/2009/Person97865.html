About Us
SIMPLY STATED- THE HELMERS GROUP believes in old school ethics and honesty paired with modern day technology. We believe that while technolgy is a growing part of all current and future business in todays market, it in no way can overcome the simple ideal of good service. We strive to give you nothing but the best of both.

Areas of Expertise
THE HELMERS GROUP services most of the 7 County metro area with an emphasis on the Northern and Western suburbs. Cities included in that area are Maple Grove, Osseo, Plymouth, Medina, Corcoran, Minnetonka, New Hope, Crystal, Brooklyn Park, Rogers, Champlin, Elk River, Albertville, Hanover and our hometown of St.Michael



THE HELMERS GROUP specializes in helping all to reach their home buying and selling goals. We believe that everyone buying or selling a home deserves to feel as though they received truly remarkable service.



THE HELMERS GROUP also services several foreclosure properties each year. Investors can recieve information about upcoming foreclosures well in advance of actually going to market.


Through our knowledge and experience, positive enthusiasm, consistent attention to detail, open and continuous communication and professional creativity together we will create a win/win real estate results!

Let's be honest, moving can be a stressful time. Our goal is to minimize the complications and to actually make the process enjoyable!

We are dedicated to building strong, lasting relationships and seeing that your family is well taken care of. If you are considering buying or selling a home or would just like to have additional information about real estate in your area, please do not hesitate to call or e-mail either Eric or Corey.
