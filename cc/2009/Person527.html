I own the technical pre-sales processes by supporting my sales partners with technical presentations on Lumeta's IPsonar product. IPsonar is a network intelligence tool use to actively baseline large networks and provide information on protocol reach and flow within a Companies network perimeter and test connectivity across network boundaries. IPsonar's primary function is to detect all gateways to the internet and across security boundaries to make sure they are within corporate security policy and discover the true network perimeter including unknown networks within an enterprise. With this tool I deliver post-sales consulting services to our clients via purchased service engagements. I then evaluate the data and prepare and deliver a presentation to the client.
