David is a successful executive recruiter, public speaker, Internet radio show host (Your Brand Radio) and small business supporter. He is deeply involved with his wife's business (www.JSDesigncustom.com) creating custom products for family/social events. David's background includes top production status as a retained executive recruiter. He has interviewed hundreds of executives and managed diverse client engagements. David's client work ranged from venture backed start-ups to some of the best known brands in the world. He advises entrepreneurial ventures and start-up organizations.

David earned his BS Marketing at Metro State College in Denver where he has been awarded 2008 Alumni of the Year - Giving Back. He also was recognized by the school and Oppenheimer Funds for Entrepreneur of the Year - honorable mention.

David is married to Julie who is an entrepreneur in Denver as well. David is on the advisory board for the Center for Innovation out of MSCD and past board president for Camp Fire Rockies.
