Strong product marketing and product management skills with a focus on market and customer requirements and the "big picture" of integration of cross-functional business processes. Proven strength in outbound product marketing and management, including analyst interactions, highly rated presentations at trade shows and customer workshops, definition and execution of product management-driven marketing programs, and executive sponsorship and management of relationships with strategic customers.

Team management, product marketing and management, and consulting.
