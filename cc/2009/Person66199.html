Micah Dubinko’s Summary

Experienced Software and Information Architect, with a history of developing and shipping new products from the ground up.

Experience leading technical editing of an international standard (W3C XForms). O'Reilly author. Over fourteen years of experience in the software industry, designing and shipping multiple major releases of software in use worldwide. Special emphasis on XML technologies including XForms, XSLT, XPath, XQuery, Relax NG, and related technologies. Contributor and originator of multiple open source projects. Special interest in personal information management, mobile, and "web of data" applications.
