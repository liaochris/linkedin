Matt Grayson’s Summary

I'm a web developer who builds user-focused web sites and applications with an emphasis on usability, scalability and open standards.

I believe applications should be intuitive. When users have to think about how to use an application, it hinders them from accomplishing the task at hand.

Since 2001, I have been building rich, interactive, user-friendly sites using standards-based (X)HTML, CSS, Javascript and a variety of back-end technologies. I also have as many years of systems administration experience in multiple environments including Windows, Linux and Mac OS X.

My favorite tools include Python, Django, Ruby, Ruby on Rails, Javascript, Mac OS X and Linux.

I am currently employed full-time, but am always looking for freelance opportunities. Feel free to get in touch.
