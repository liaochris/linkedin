Barbara J Wiggins’s Summary

Barbara Wiggins is the Founder and President of Griffin Valuation Group, Ltd.

Griffin Valuation Group specializes in nationwide Cost Segregation & Property Tax Consulting which can substantially lower corporate annual tax burdens and increase cash flow. We provide Big-4 quality, experience and expertise without the big price.
