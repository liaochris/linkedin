Jeff Corns’s Summary

I am an executive with broad functional area experience, including Business Development, Operations, and Staff Development. With some niche product development background, my primary focus has been on professional services. I have specific interest in geospatial technologies, and how technology can help transform organizations. I am a very passionate person and enjoy serving in an executive role supporting talented leadership with a strong vision.

My professional goal is to help my clients implement innovative solutions to solve their most pressing problems or needs, while helping my staff to be the best leaders possible. I have always believed in bringing exceptional value and a strong work ethic to my clients, my staff, and my company.
