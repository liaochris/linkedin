Scott Spinola’s Summary

Scott is an avid writer, both professionally and personally, and an effective communicator with ten years content management and technical writing experience in varied settings. He excels at simplifying complex procedures and technical information. He also has a growing interest in creative writing, having written several pieces of short fiction and the beginning of a novel. He has excellent proofreading and content management skills, and ten years experience writing technical documentation ranging from process documents to user guides. He brings to any task strong technical competency, a customer focus, creativity, flexibility, attention to detail, strong interpersonal and communications skills, and an ability to learn new skills quickly.

Scott is a detail-oriented professional, has a creative imagination, and is gifted at using words to express new ideas and concepts. He can be called on when a fresh, new way to communicate important information is needed. His keen eye for the big picture helps with strategic planning, identifying possibilities, and finding creative solutions to difficult problems. Scott naturally generates lots of new ideas and ways of doing things and likes to be included in brainstorming sessions or whenever new and different solutions must be found.

Scott's application and technology experience includes advanced knowledge of Adobe FrameMaker, Microsoft Excel, and Microsoft Word, and experience with HTML, CSS, Adobe Captivate, and Adobe RoboHelp. He also has eight years experience with enterprise content management and portal systems such as Vignette, Microsoft Office SharePoint Portal and SharePoint Server, Documentum, and Plumtree Portal, and over 15 years experience with Macintosh and Windows operating systems.
