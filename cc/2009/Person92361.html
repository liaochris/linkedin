Richard Feldman’s Summary

I have over 20 year experience in Marketing and Graphics design. In addition, I have extensive experience in commercial printing, variable data printing and in direct mail production. Over the course of my career I have
successfully developed marketing programs for new business pursuits, client presentations and customer retention projects. My clients included both major and mid sized companies in the Financial, Publishing, Energy, Non-Profit, Pharmaceutical and Insurance industries.

I have excellent leadership, people management and project management skills. I'm considered by my peers to be a:

- Strategic thinker; combining technical expertise with firm commercial objectivity.
- Strong communicator with excellent interpersonal skills.
- Proven mentor and coach with a goal oriented approach.
- Hands on manger when appropriate.
- Self motivated, with a high work ethic.

Now as owner and CEO of Simply Dynamic Creative Services, a full service creative services company, I have joined together a team of professionals to service our clients in all aspects of marketing, printing, direct mail and web development.

With over 75 years of combined experience in direct marketing, print and design and over 20 years of combined experience on the web, we have the expertise to be your integrated creative partner.

Our mission is to be the creative services company most admired for its people, partnerships and performance. We strive to build long-term relationships with each customer as an individual, specializing in the development of result-oriented advertising, promotion and direct marketing designed to surpass our client's expectations.
