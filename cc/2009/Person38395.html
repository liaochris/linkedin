Gus Widmayer’s Summary

Specialist in the treatment and packaging of semiconductors. Consults regularly on placement issues for semiconductors to contract electronic manufacturers. Management exec with three decades of broad experience in all aspects of small business management.

Gus Widmayer’s Specialties:

Semiconductors, device packages, EIA Standards, JEDEC specifications, sourcing, manufacturing, purchasing, quality, accounting, marketing, sales, and financial management. Also some direct experience with real estate and financial consulting.
