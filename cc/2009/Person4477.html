Amber Porter Cox’s Summary

Amber enjoys the stimulating challenge of building a new product or service, growing a business, or pursuing a new business model - both as a leader or part of a team.

As a team lead, she is dedicated, effective, and believes that it should be fun in the process. Amber is a skilled, innovative, problem solver - rooted in a 'can do' personality and quick creativity - she is known for getting the job done. She has excellent communication and management skills, noted in her ability to drive projects to successful completion. Often, her knack for leading change and driving new process development creates the structure needed within chaotic environments.
