Nathan Holman’s Summary

Since 2002, Nathan has provided expert search marketing consulting and optimization tactics, first as an independent consultant, and later as the Senior Search Engine Marketing Strategist of Vizion Interactive. Nathan lives on the bleeding edge of search engine optimization and paid search marketing developments, and brings this expertise to every client strategy session.

Nathan is the SEO braintrust of the company, and is responsible for initial and ongoing training of other SEO specialists. Nathan specializes in competitive landscape analysis, keyword research methods, inbound link building, internal content optimization, and pay per click strategy.

He is a passionate domain investor and web entrepreneur. From CGC (consumer generated content) websites to industry chatter, Nathan's finger is on the pulse of the industry. Years of research, testing, and experimentation have contributed to Nathan's expert search engine optimization strategies.
