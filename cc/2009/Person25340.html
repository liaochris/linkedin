Debby Lloyd’s Summary

Debby Lloyd is a tenured International Executive Search Consultant. As a recruiter working solely in renewable energy, carbon management and sustainability industries, she is also an Honorary Advisor to the Catlin Arctic Survey, participates in judging for the UK Green Business Awards and is a frequent referrer to the Cleantech, start-up and investor community. Her pre-search professional background includes dot.com, commercial sales & manufacturing with legal and entrepreneurial start-up experience.

Debby Lloyd’s Specialties:

Completed mandates include C Level and Management & Technical positions in Wind, Solar, Microgeneration, Sustainable Consulting, Green ICT, Biomass and the Private Foundation/Charity sectors.

Debby Lloyd’s Experience

  • Managing Director

    EcoSearch

    EcoSearch is a consultancy that specialises in building management and technical teams for businesses operating in:

    • Renewable Energy
    • Clean Technology
    • Carbon Management
    • Sustainability

    My organisation has unique insight into the climate change agenda and as a result operates at the heart of business growth and transformation.

    Through our Advisory, Executive Search and Consulting practices we help organisations achieve competitive advantage.

    Simply put we match opportunity with people, products, services and technology.

    Areas of focus in Renewable Energy include specialist desks in Wind, Wave/Tidal, microgeneration, solar etc.

    In Cleantech we focus on technologies for “green agenda” improvements and efficiencies (including carbon footprint reduction), smart metering, cleantech research etc

    EcoSearch recruits specialist management and technical expertise across the complete climate change mitigation and adaptation lifecycle from start up R&D to blue chip and from Government to Private Foundation and NGO.

    EcoSearch have been sponsors at All Energy 07, 08 & 09 and The European Green IT Summit.

    I am an honorary advisor to The Catlin Arctic Survey and have been participating as a judge in the ENDS, Green Business Awards. EcoSearch is also a member of many organisations including EnviroBusiness and is a SEEDA Hub Company.

    As a Director and non-exec director of start up companies operating in the energy and climate change arena I bring indepth understanding of the challenges associated with growing businesses in this space.

    During the course of my work I have been successful in connecting many organisations with cleantech investment community, am familiar with many technologies and my introductions have led to some UK firsts for renewable energy deployment.

    My network of contacts spans more than 35 countries. Please feel free to reach out and connect with me.
