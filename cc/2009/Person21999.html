Dan Carew’s Summary

Dan's lived 23 years in Asia, is fluent in Japanese and runs a Hong Kong-based boutique IT consultancy. Dan is

• An IT Project Management Pro: Proven track record at large international companies for getting IT projects back on track-–and on-time, on-budget delivery. Strengths are straight talk, out-of-the box creative thinking and team leadership, combined with best-practice PMBOK program management skills.

• A Software Development & Product Management Expert: Led teams building large complex software solutions from scratch (e.g., Japanese-language version of broker research database, bespoke products for web portals) and customizations of large software packages (from, e.g., Yahoo!, Fujitsu, TEC Toshiba, SAP, Microsoft Navision). Deep knowledge of development cycle and product life cycle. Advocate of agile development, SOA and SaaS.

• A CIO-at-large: A business person who speaks IT and a technologist who understands business. Led large multi-cultural teams, and managed big budgets. Facile writer and diplomatic communicator. Deep technical knowledge: expert in Asian-language localization; web site and ASP design and development; Web 2.0, enterprise wikis, intranets and KM; banking systems and financial databases; retail back office, e-commerce, POS, merchandising, supply chain and CRM/loyalty; ERP systems and system integration; business process mapping and development; data warehousing and business intelligence; and workflow automation. Extensive experience building business cases with ROIs, business development, doing large deals, vendor/client management, IT governance, and ITIL.

• A Technology Branding and Strategy Guru: Right brain creativity for left brain technologists. Has developed IT strategy for start-ups, and strategic business plans at large companies.

• An indie film maker with a production company (Kanda River Productions), a digital cinema production package built around Red One camera #1304 and a high-def post-production studio.
