Evan Reiser’s Summary

Evan is currently employed as the CEO of GamerNook.com, overseeing business development, sales and engineering.

Prior to GamerNook.com Evan was most recently employed at Capital IQ, a division of Standard and Poor’s. Evan worked as one of four software engineers on the Screening & Analytics team that helped to design and develop the billion dollar Screening Technology used by over 2000 client firms.

Before working at Capital IQ, he worked for Eastman Kodak doing Research and Development in Artificial Intelligence and Multi-Agent Systems; clients included various intelligence agencies in Great Britain as well as the Royal Navy and the Royal Air-force. Evan holds a Bachelors of Science in Computer Systems Engineering from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute where he specialized in Database Systems, Data-mining and Computer System Design.
