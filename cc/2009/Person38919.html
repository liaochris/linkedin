Heather Hartmann (hartmaha@yahoo.com)’s Summary

Heather Hartmann, LLC is an extension of your human resources or recruiting department. Or, if you are a smaller organization and don't have an HR or Recruiting team, we can do that for you. Our model is very flexible, and able to meet any of your Recruiting and HR needs. We've done work for a variety of industries, and all sizes of organizations, both locally and nationally. Most recently, Heather has been on contract recruiting for Hewitt Associates Health Management practice. Prior to that, she has held a long-term assignments with Towers Perrin, Monsanto, Magellan Behavioral Health and Accenture.

Heather has over twenty years of recruiting experience including key positions at Peabody Energy, Accenture, Cap Gemini Ernst and Young, The Federal Reserve Bank of St. Louis and EdwardJones. She has been an Advisor to Staffing.org, and has served as a Region Chair and Area Vice President of the Employment Management Association.

Heather has also spoken at ERE Media conferences, and been published in "On Staffing - Advice & Perspectives" published by Wiley & Sons.
