John Hyde’s Summary

John Hyde is a Senior Vice President of NAPL, a not-for-profit
business management association representing progressive,
leading companies in the graphic communications industry.

NAPL is uniquely positioned to help companies grow, improve
and prosper through unparalleled member resources,
industry-leading research and custom-tailored consulting
services.

As an entrepreneur, John has the unique ability to quickly read a client’s situation when facing a crossroads and lay out the alternative courses of action enabling faster ownership decision-making, with less risk, and with greater confidence. John’s insights are based on his nearly 20 years’ experience helping print and graphic communications companies
become industry leaders.

John is a trusted advisor to company owners and senior executives who are seeking to build long term shareholder value through strategic mergers and acquisitions.

A licensed attorney, John holds a BA from Colgate University and a JD from Tulane Law School. He joined NAPL in May 2004 when the consulting firm he founded in 1990, Rampart Associates, was acquired by NAPL.

Prior to that, John practiced law in the late 1980s at two large law firms known for mergers and acquisitions and debt restructuring.

John Hyde’s Specialties:

John’s subject matter expertise encompasses the areas of business valuation; mergers and acquisitions; sale of business; debt restructuring; financing; partnership agreements; succession planning; and turnaround management.
