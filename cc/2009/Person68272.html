Mihai Crasneanu’s Experience

  • CEO

    Grey Juice Lab

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Online Media industry)

    September 2007 — Present (1 year 11 months)

    Grey Juice Lab is dedicated to helping telecom, cable and mobile operators around the world in defining and structuring their digital content strategy, conceiving their IPTV and VOD offering, negotiating the best and most appropriate content with the majors studios and with the local right owners, successfully implementing and launching the service, and last but not least, accompanying the operator in the day to day operations of the service to ensure good content management, efficient interfaces, profitable pricing and promotions and product innovation. Grey Juice Lab focus is product definition, content negotiation, content business models and marketing, customer adoption and product innovation.

    Current Grey Juice Lab customers are telecom, mobile and cable operators in Western and Eastern Europe, Latin America and Middle East.

  • Partner

    Result

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Management Consulting industry)

    September 2007 — Present (1 year 11 months)

    As a partner at Result (www.result.com), I help companies in the TIME sector (Telecom, Internet, Media, Entertainment) accelerate their international growth. Thanks to its presence in more than 15 countries, Result has created an ecosystem for growth that enables companies to expand faster, more efficiently and more successfully. Result clients include startups such as Piczo and FON, and leading media groups such as Schibsted.

  • Founder & CEO

    Glowria

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Entertainment industry)

    November 2001 — May 2007 (5 years 7 months)

    glowria is the largest independent VOD white label operator in Europe, with more than 5000 VOD titles negotiated with the Major studios (Warner, Sony, Universal, Paramount, etc.), the international providers (BBC, HBO, MTV, Turner, etc.) and local content providers for each country. It operates the Video On Demand service on IPTV (via Set Top Box) and PC (download), such as Neuf Cegetel (#2 ISP in France, 3.5 million subscribers), Fnac (largest French online retailer), Vodafone, Carrefour (4 countries), etc. Glowria is also the largest online DVD rental service (cf Netflix) in France and #2 in Germany, with a library of 25.000 titles.

  • VP of International Deployment, Sales and Marketing

    UOL

    (Privately Held; 1001-5000 employees; Online Media industry)

    June 1999 — March 2001 (1 year 10 months)

    Launched the International branch of the leading Brazilian ISP and portal UOL. Opened 7 countries : US (Miami), Spain (Madrid and Barcelona) and 5 countries in Latin America (Argentina, Mexico, Chile, Venezuela, Colombia). Undertook HR, content and technology deployment. Rolled out the sales and marketing force. JV with local media companies. Positionned UOL the #1 portal and service provider in Latin America after 1.5 years of international launch.

  • Founder & CEO

    MCC One (Argentina)

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Internet industry)

    January 1997 — June 1999 (2 years 6 months)

    I founded MCC One to offer a packaged, customized and ready-to-deploy Webmail/Freemail service, aimed to large ISPs and Telcos and supporting large volumes of users, in a region where almost nobody new at that time about Hotmail. The product/service was a success among regional providers. Telefonica of Argentina massively deployed it and later integrated the service in Terra portal.

  • Project Manager
