Melissa Michaels’s Summary

New Unity Brand Clothing Line:

INPELOTO (INfinite, PEace, LOve and TOgetherness) is my "new unity brand" that makes a statement featuring my distinctive designs and harmonious statements.

Inpeloto LLC comprises of volumes of my poetry, jewelry line called Inpeloto Bella: Jewelry with a Conscience™ that integrates my love of poetry into my designs. Inpeloto Bodywear makes tshirts and hoodies for women and men, along with camisoles and caps. It is sustainable clothing made from 100% combed organic cotton and other eco-friendly fibers. It is Clothing with a Conscience™. This brand uses fair trade principles.

These are clothes with a message which makes bold, conscious and positive proclamations with distinctive, clean and vibrant designs. Invest in humanity, look amazing, and help create global dialogues. It is also a new word that speaks powerful volumes.

10% of the sales proceeds benefits: BookPALs (Performing Artists for Literacy in Schools) and Conservation International. Doing what matters and making a difference is the essence of Inpeloto.

Inpeloto’s mission: To be world relevant through social transformation by implementing infinite peace, love and togetherness for daily existence of conscious action. Love is a verb.
