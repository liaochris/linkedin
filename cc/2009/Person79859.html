Piotr Zakrzewski’s Summary

I am working as an engineer in automotive industry. I have obtained Master in Science and Executive Engineering degree (in 2007) at Ecole Nationale Superieure des Mines de Paris one of the leading French institutions in the domain of executive engineering (often ranked 1st or 2nd in French press).

I am an ambitious and dynamic person. I am always looking for new challenging goals. I do appreciate the satisfaction which follows the realization of these objectives. My mid-term career goal is to get experience working in an international environment. As for my further professional development I aim a project management related position and MBA studies.

Before I became a student at the ENSMP I studied three years at Jagiellonian University in Krakow (Poland). During my studies I’ve not only obtained valuable knowledge and scientific skills but also a capacity for efficient learning and hard work, which I was able to prove by being the only one admitted to the Ecole des Mines from all candidates from my country.

Starting my studies in France was a challenging act of entrepreneurship. The capacities to adapt in new conditions and to work in international groups were crucial for my successful integration.
