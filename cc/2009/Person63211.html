Michael V Thompson’s Summary

Hello. I am the owner of Market Accelerators, LLC and a Duct Tape Marketing Coach.

My company works with select small companies who want to grow their business to the next level. These are typically companies without a marketing strategy or marketing expertise and who are tired of spending resources on marketing that doesn't seem to work.

If you are a business owner and you often say:

"If only I could get in front of more prospects..."
"My website brings very little business"
"I don't know how best to use social media in my business"
"Advertising doesn't work"

Then perhaps we should talk.

I have been a professional marketer for over 20 years and I'm known for my very practical approach and ideas.

I speak regularly at business events on small business marketing topics and I'm both a blogger and podcaster.

SOLID EXPERIENCE:
My experience in marketing has been broad. I've managed marketing in three distinct worldwide businesses within Hewlett Packard and led strategic marketing management at Mattel Toys. I've also worked for a variety of small companies whose names will be less familiar to you. During my career I've launched new products, grown new businesses and helped companies get their marketing back on track.

I have lived and worked in Barcelona, Spain, Singapore and the UK.

You can reach me at (360) 448-7474
