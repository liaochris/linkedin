Aloke Bajpai’s Summary

Active in travel technology for nearly 8 years, in managerial, operational, and technical roles. Have worked closely with GDSs, Travel Technology providers, Aggregators, Cruiselines, Consolidators and Travel Agents.

In 2006, I founded iXiGO with ex-colleagues from Amadeus, to provide "travel 2.0" search to the Indian market. www.iXiGO.com was launched in June 2007 and is now India's #1 and Asia's #2 travel search engine, powering nearly 2% of all online travel in India.

Aloke Bajpai’s Specialties:

Strong understanding of travel B2B & B2C. Relationships with GDSs, Airlines, Aggregators and Travel Technology Providers, and a base in the biggest market for IT talent.
