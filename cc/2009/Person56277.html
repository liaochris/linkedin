Kostandinos Baltzoglou’s Summary

I've been creative since I was a little boy in Detroit
watching Mr. Dressup... now I live in the 'burbs
and watch HGTV. That kid with the bowl
hair cut is alive and well and looking to do great things...
minus the bowl hair cut, of course.

I am self-motivated and extremely detail-oriented with
exceptional verbal and written communication skills. I'm
also "Monk-ish" in my organizational skills. I take
great pride in my work and excel both in working
on my own and in team projects.

My range extends from print to web to social media:
I am well versed in traditional design, such as printed
materials like direct mail pieces, business cards,
stationery, typography, logos, and posters as well
as basic HTML/CSS web sites that focus on design,
navigation, structure, and ease of use for the end user.
I also enjoy the challenge of writing witty, eye-catching copy.
More recently I've also had experience with wayfinding
systems and signage.

A few of my goals are to:
1) Constantly improve my design and technical skills.
2) Learn how to screen print.
3) Work on an official project for the Detroit Tigers.
4) Have an abstract painting in the collection of the Detroit Institute of Arts.
5) Publish a collection of my creative writing.
6) Work on a project for Morrissey.
7) Work on a project for Metric and/or Emily Haines.
8) Work on a project for the Yeah Yeah Yeahs.
9) Work on a project for The Catherine Wheel.
There's always room for more...
