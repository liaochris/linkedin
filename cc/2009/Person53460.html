Karl Scrivener’s Summary

Senior Executive Recruiter with in excess of 14 years sales experience backed within blue chip companies.

Officially regarded on 'Linked In' as one of the most endorsed Recruitment Consultants in the United Kingdom and across the world. This kudos is gained by endorsements and references gained from clients and candidates alike based on my performance and relationships with each.

Accomplished and highly successful sales career which started as an IT Sales Consultant at Hays IT, before moving on to Informatiq Consulting, Vision iT recruitment, IT Executive and most recently Idealpeople Ltd.

My goals are to continue to excel as an IT Sales and recruitment professional through sheer diligence, hard work and networking with like-minded business professionals. My responsibilities in the past have also included heading up teams of Sales Consultants of which I have had managerial responsibilities at all of my past employers.
