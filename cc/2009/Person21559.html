Cynthia Marcotte Stamer’s Summary

Helps organizations and leaders manage people and processes to achieve their business and operational objectives and manage legal and other risks.

Applies in-depth of human resources, insurance, employee benefits, health care, corporate compliance and other legal matters to assist clients to formulate pragmatic operational and risk management strategies taking into account the financial, operational, political, legal and other realities.

Board certified in labor and employment law by the Texas Board of Legal Specialization.

Helps organizations establish and administer effective corporate ethics and compliance, internal controls, external controls, human resources, employee benefits, outsourcing, isnrasnce and other management strategies.

Regularly provides training, counseling, representation to entrepreneurs and established businesses and their founders, officers, and directors on operational and risk management issues including the establishment and administration of effective internal controls and corporate governance, including design and administration of up-the-ladder corporate compliance and risk management strategies under Sarbanes-Oxley and other laws.

Nationally recognized for work, training and publications foremployee benefit plan sponsors, fiduciaries, vendors, and others to design, document, administer and defend cost-effective and legally compliant employee benefit programs that manage legal risks and achieve business objectives.

Her insights quoted in the Wall Street Journal, the BNA Pension and Benefits Reporter, the Dallas Business Journal, the Houston Business Journal, Modern Health Care, Managed Healthcare Executive, Health Leaders, Caring for The Elderly Magazine, For The Record, the Dallas Morning News, Spencer Publications and others.

Ms. Stamer also serves in numerous professional and civil leadership roles, is a popular speaker, and is the author of hundreds of publications.
