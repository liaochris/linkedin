  • Founder / Studio GM / Sr. Producer

    2K Games Austin

    (Public Company; TTWO; Computer Games industry)

    September 2008 — October 2009 (1 year 2 months)

    Pitched and founded a new 2K Games incubation studio in Austin to prototype a MMO adaptation of an existing franchise. I recruited the team, managed the establishment of the studio and set the overall direction of the project, managing relationships with the IP holder and 2K Games corporate. As the company’s only source of MMO expertise, we were chartered with the tasks of educating the company on issues relating to MMO development and market strategy and given the authority to determine how best to adapt the franchise into an MMO.

    In less than eight months of development, our team of ten developers built a working, custom multiplayer client and server prototype from scratch. The prototype included numerous game systems and a rudimentary 3D engine using Gamebryo. The team also developed an iconic visual style for the game which took the IP in a bold new direction that was designed to favor low system requirements and attract players familiar with highly stylized Asian-inspired MMO’s. We drafted whitepapers and provided detailed financial projections, staffing plans and market assessments.
