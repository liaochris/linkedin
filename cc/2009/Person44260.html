Jason Weiss’s Summary

Experience: An accomplished project manager and team leader focused on building HR systems and processes that deliver business results. Professional and academic expertise in design and delivery of creative and effective customized solutions for hiring and developing staff at all levels. Recognized innovator in assessment technology. Extensive experience in leading software design to create selection and development systems for organization-wide application.

Goals: My goal is to improve HR systems to achieve their maximum impact. I enjoy adjusting and standardizing existing processes and integrating them into a strong talent management approach. I enjoy building solutions that make a difference, whether that means bringing in the right tools from outside, or building entirely new tools when the situation warrants it.

Jason Weiss’s Specialties:

• Crafting strategic and execution plans for talent management
• Developing customized hiring and development solutions to meet business goals
• Traditional and technology-based assessment center design, delivery, and rollout
• Competency design and modeling
• Job analysis
• Survey design and analysis
• Training needs analysis and design and facilitation of training and development programs
• Performance management and succession planning
