Marty M. Fahncke’s Summary

Since 1999, I have been President of FawnKey & Associates, a product development, marketing, and consulting company. FawnKey & Associates helps emerging and existing businesses increase revenue, profitability, and customer satisfaction through the use of integrated marketing and sales efforts.
