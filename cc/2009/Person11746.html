Blackford Middleton’s Summary

Academic physician scientist, professor, and administrator for clinical informatics R&D group at Partners HealthCare System, Harvard Medical School, Boston. Experienced in academic and private sector healthcare information technology product design, development, and implementation (eletronic medical records, personal health record software, clinical decision support), and value-based technology assessment of healthcare information technology (HIT). Committed to healthcare transformation with the successful implementation of HIT.

Blackford Middleton’s Specialties:

Clinical Informatics, patient safety and quality management, electronic health records, clinical decision support, patient-centered computing, healthcare information technology assessment
