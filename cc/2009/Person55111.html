Kevin Liebl’s Summary

I am a seasoned, high-technology executive with both domestic and international experience. Internationally, I have launched and managed marketing and sales programs throughout Europe and Latin America. I have been the spokesperson for presenting company strategy to Wall Street; and as CEO of a high-tech startup I both secured capital to fund business expansion and successfully sold the company to a Fortune 1000 corporation.

I received a Bachelor of Science in Information and Computer Science from the University of California at Irvine and a Masters of Business Administration from Pepperdine University. I have been a member of the American Marketing Association, the American Electronics Association, a founding member of the Storage Networking Industry Association, the Fibre Channel Industry Association, the RAID Advisory Board, and the SCSI Trade Association. I am also a noted industry speaker and is well published in both business and industry publications.

My wife Ellen and I have two sons and a daughter and live in Ladera Ranch, CA.
