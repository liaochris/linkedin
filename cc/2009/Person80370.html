Pablo Päster’s Summary

ClimateCHECK is a leading greenhouse gas services company that develops ISO 14064 compliant methodologies for new clean technologies to connect the cleantech and the carbon markets. ClimateCHECK also develops innovative greenhouse gas management and offset platforms for major clients such as the GE/AES joint venture, Greenhouse Gas Services (ghgs.com). As Vice President of Greenhouse Gas Management in ClimateCHECK's San Francisco office, I manage the delivery of technical services, including greenhouse gas emissions inventory, supply chain GHG emissions inventory, "carbon neutral" product evaluations, verification, validation, methodology and protocol development, and generating greenhouse gas assertions for marketing purposes. My most recent clients have included Red Bull North America, a major gas pipeline company, Sherwood Equities, Accenture, UNFCCC, and the Greenhouse Gas Management Institute (of which ClimateCHECK is a co-founder).
