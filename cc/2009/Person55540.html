Kimberly Crew’s Summary

Kimberly Erle Crew continues her 15+ year run as a successful serial entrepreneur developing businesses that achieve outstanding results in crowded verticals. Leveraging her early experiences, Kimberly has managed to create a portfolio of companies that continue to grow even in the face of economic headwinds.

In her role as advisor, Kimberly and Lighthouse Venture Management bring all the resources that contribute to her success into focus for Coachable clients, guiding high potential small business companies to achive market status.

Kimberly’s businesses plow 30% of their results back into the community though such organizations as the not-for-profit, Resources for Human Development and Murex Investments, a Common Good Venture Fund.

Kimberly Crew’s Specialties:

Insightful business assessment with clarification of goals, execution and the resources to implement action plans.
