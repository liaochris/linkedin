Ayelet Baron’s Summary

Ayelet has expertise in social networking, web 2.0 and business strategy. She has extensive global business expertise to drive new growth by managing relationships in a cross-functional fashion and accelerating go-to-market strategies.

Ayelet is currently on the Executive Leadership team in Cisco's Emerging Markets.

Ayelet is on the Board of Directors of Family and Children Services, www.fcservices.org

During her career, she has built leading edge teams, worked in diverse industries and developed a reputation as a leader and a highly creative change agent. Ayelet is an innovative leader in developing and delivering business strategy, planning and operations, with the ability to transform an organization to meet its strategic business goals.

Ayelet is a recognized industry leader and speaker on business strategy, social media and online communities. She recently contributed a chapter on business strategy to a book published by Jossey-Bass, The IABC Handbook of Organizational Communication: A Guide to Internal Communication, Public Relations, Marketing and Leadership.
