Mike Little’s Summary

A veteran creative and passionate student of usability and user experience. Business-centered with a talent for combining innovation with substance and a firmly grounded understanding of the client's design goals. Die-hard follower of web-standards and industry best practices.

Extensive experience building and managing creative teams in profitable print- and web-based organizations. Over 15 years of management experience. Clear vision. Thorough understanding of clients and resources.

Likes mint chip ice cream.

Mike Little’s Specialties:

Art Direction, User Experience Design, Information Architecture Development, Usability Design, User Interface Design, Project Management, Web Design, Creative Department Management, Design Consulting and Print Design. Not to mention my significant skills in Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Quark, Fireworks and Dreamweaver...
