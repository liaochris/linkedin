Duncan Bucknell’s Summary

Duncan is a well known thought leader in intellectual property strategy. His firm’s blog, IP Think Tank is ranked the number one intellectual property blog of all time by Justia.com and his recently launched weekly podcast, which touches on the latest in IP around the globe and with guest appearances from other IP thought leaders, are a regular part of the working week for thousands of people.

Duncan is CEO of Duncan Bucknell Company, an intellectual property strategy firm with clients based all over the world. The firm takes a strategic and global perspective and has assisted clients in about 50 countries since 2005. The firm’s clients range from some of the largest corporate entities in the world down to new and exciting start up entities.

In his spare time, Duncan is a Principal Fellow at the Melbourne Business School, Australia’s leading business school, where he teaches ‘Strategic Management of Intellectual Property’, a globally-focussed course designed to provide executives with insights and some basic tools to practically identify and handle intellectual property issues.

Have a look at the team at Duncan Bucknell Company at www.duncanbucknell.com
