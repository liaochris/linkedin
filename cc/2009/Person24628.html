David Saffir’s Summary

David Saffir is an internationally recognized, award winning portrait, commercial, and fine art photographer and printmaker. He is the author of Mastering Digital Color, and has published a wide range of articles on photography, digital printmaking, color management, new technology evaluations, and more. He lives near Los Angeles, California, in Santa Clarita.

He currently consults for HP, Nikon, Ergooft, and others.

Publication credits include Professional Photographer, Rangefinder, After Capture, HP Pro Photo Blog, and many others. He is the sole author of two books, "Mastering Digital Color", and "The Joy of Discovery"

HIs blog is http://davidsaffir.wordpress.com
Web Site: www.davidsaffir.com

David Saffir’s Specialties:

Photography - high-definition medium format imaging

Consulting - troubleshooting color management and workflow issues, facility design and setup, product development and field testing.
