William Exeter’s Summary

William L. Exeter is the President and Chief Executive Officer for Exeter 1031 Exchange Services, LLC and Exeter Fiduciary Services, LLC and their affiliate companies.

Mr. Exeter has been in the financial services industry since 1980 and entered the 1031 exchange and fiduciary services industries in 1986. He has written and lectured extensively on 1031 exchange and 1033 exchange transactions, on Tenant-In-Common (TIC) Properties as like-kind replacement property solutions and on Title Holding Trusts (Land Trusts.

In addition, Mr. Exeter is the owner and host of The Exeter Group Real Estate Talk Radio Show™, is a frequent guest expert on "The Financial Advisors — Money Talk Radio Show” on San Diego News Radio AM 600 KOGO and on the "Inside Business Radio Show" on Business Talk Radio AM 1000 KCEO San Diego.

Exeter 1031 Exchange Services, LLC is a national institutional Qualified Intermediary that administers all types of 1031 Exchanges in all 50 states.

Exeter Fiduciary Services, LLC is a private fiduciary services provider that administers Title Holding Trusts (Land Trusts), Deferred Sales Trusts, and family living trusts throughout the California market place.

Exeter has offices in San Diego (HQ), Irvine, Ontario, Bakersfield, Fresno, San Francisco, California, as well as Kona, Hawaii, Springfield, New Jersey, and East Northpark, New York.

You can reach our national corporate headquarters office at (619) 615-4210, toll free at (866) 393-8370, or ask any 1031 exchange related question via email at ask@exeterco.com.

You can contact our branch office nearest you by calling (866) 393-8377.

William Exeter’s Specialties:

Our 1031 exchange services include forward, reverse and improvement 1031 exchanges for real and personal property. We administer all types of 1031 exchanges in all 50 states.

Our private fiduciary services include the administration of Title Holding Trusts (Land Trusts), Deferred Sales Trusts, and regular living trusts throughout California.

Our clients include individual, corporate and institutional investors/taxpayers.
