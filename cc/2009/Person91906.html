David Kemper’s Summary

Hello, I am a content analyst and writer interested in digital access. I research, analyse, write, and develop solutions that enable knowledge-based industries to use the Web to improve and enhance access to their vital information assets and to automate and streamline business procedures.

My interests include:

+ Enhancing people's access to information to foster solid decision-making
+ Emerging web technologies
+ Content creation and delivery
+ Communication and collaboration

I want to explore emerging technologies and their impact on information access, discovery and delivery, community and collaboration.

David Kemper’s Specialties:

Research, analysis, oral and written communication, elucidation of complex concepts, vendor liaison, user experience and accessibility, web design and content development. Pushing projects forward. Teamwork.
