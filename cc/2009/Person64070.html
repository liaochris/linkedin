    What is ACE

    Founded in 1987, Advancing Canadian Entrepreneurship (ACE) is a national, not-for-profit organization that is dedicated to teaching and igniting young Canadians to create brighter futures for themselves and their communities. Working in partnership with business and higher education ACE delivers two programs on 49 university and college campuses across Canada; a team based program and an individual based program - Students In Free Enterprise and Student Entrepreneur.

    SIFE Program
    In 2000, ACE partnered with Students In Free Enterprise (SIFE) to operate their team based program in Canada. SIFE is a global, not-for-profit organization that is literally changing the world through highly dedicated student teams on more than 1,800 university campuses in over 40 countries.
