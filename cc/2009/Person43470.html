Janice Cutler’s Summary

Janice Cutler began her education at Siena College in Loudonville, NY. She received a B.S. in Marketing/Management and International Studies with a concentration in German. She graduated in 1990 at which point she worked in the Marketing and Survey Research field with companies such as SavaTree, IBM, and the Research Triangle Institute. Soon her interests turned to Horticulture and she enrolled in a program at North Carolina State University. In November of 1995, Janice began working at Montgomery’s Florist and in the Spring of 1996 became a certified Floral Designer by the Southeast Florida School of Floral Design. In October of 1996 she became the new owner of Montgomery’s Florist and successfully ran the company until 2001 at which point she decided to sell the business and focus on raising her children. In July 2007, Montgomery’s Florist had closed their doors and Janice noticed the space was available. After spending the last seven years raising her two beautiful daughters she decided to return to the career which she loves. By November 2007, our doors reopened as North Raleigh Florist. Her design techniques stay current and her teaching ability allows the rest of the staff to grow and learn. She also takes initiative to send her employees to design workshops and customer service classes. As an active member of the Raleigh Chamber of Commerce as well as a volunteer with the Mount Vernon Middle School, the MeFine Foundation, Muscular Dystrophy Association, Janice is able to support the community and give something back. Janice sets her business apart from other florists with personalization, dedication, and incredible attention to detail. She has a great passion for the floral industry and strongly believes in long-term relationships. Janice strives to help every customer express themselves perfectly for each occasion. She strongly believes in long-term relationships.
