Sayeed Rahman’s Summary

Mr. Sayeedur Rahman brings over 13 years of experience in technical management and development. He is currently working as a Programmer Analyst Senior with Division of Consulting & Project Management for Commonwealth Office of Technology. Kentucky Ranks 4th in 2007 Brown University e-Gov Study, up from 44th in 2002.

Mr. Rahman has strong technical background in Unix System Administration and System Engineering, where he worked in the Main Data Center for Governor's Office for Technology. He also has several years of experience as System Analyst and Senior Programmer Analyst for the Commonwealth of Kentucky. Mr. Rahman is also the founder of Bangladesh Information Technology group called BANGLA-IT http://www.banglait.org. He participated in various national and international conferences in USA, Taiwan, Vietnam, Bangladesh and presented research based papers. Author of several articles/papers published in Newspapers, Magazines and Books http://www.techsparks.com/asia-unplugged.html. Apart from the professional activities, he has some interests in research and education assisted development in Bangladesh.

Mr. Rahman holds a Bachelor degree in Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Bombay, India. He obtained his Masters degree in Engineering from City University of NY (CUNY), City College campus, USA.
