Nabeel Malik’s Summary

With an MBA Degree in International Business and more then ten (10) years of corporate experience, I am currently working as General Manager at Axiom Private Limited. My overall experience revolves around the concept of Service Level Management. During my career I have worked with some of the industrial & educational conglomerates of Pakistan.

Having thorough knowledge of business ethics and practices regarding USA, UK, Malaysia, Middle East & Far East markets I have been involved in devising SLRs/SLTs, defining SLAs along with associated OLAs and Under Pinning Contracts along with their continuous monitoring and review which enables my organization to proactively prevent service failures. Continuous and effective reporting mechanism has been my specialization which enables me to prepare Service Improvement Plans (SIP) for the growth of my organization.
offshoring and multisourcing are few of those focus areas where I engages myself in order to prosper client’s business in an innovative way.
