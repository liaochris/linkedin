Before becoming associated with LTC Financial Partners as an Independent Agent, I spent fifteen years as a personal and business coach, ultimately coaching senior level executives in Fortune 1000 and Fortune 500 companies in breakthrough thinking methodolgies.

I launched a freelance writing and web services business in January 2007 and often write about long-term care. Linda Lee Kaye’s Experience

  • Long Term Care Specialist/Independent Agent

    LTC Financial Partners

    (Partnership; Insurance industry)

    January 2009 — Present (11 months)

    In caring for both my father and my father-in-law over several years, I directly experienced the damaging impact that providing long term care to family members without the benefit of long term care insurance can have on the preservation of financial assets, income and the emotional well-being of survivors. The sudden death of my husband at age 55 further reinforced the importance of being prepared for unexpected health crises and their consequences.

    With long-term care insurance, you gain the peace of mind that you won’t be a burden on your family for your care; you avoid the possibility of depending on our limited and inadequate medical welfare system; you preserve your hard-earned income, assets and most importantly, your lifestyle; and you retain your independence and control over your care. Therefore, I became passionately committed to educating and helping others, especially women, address their biggest and perhaps most important financial risk, the risk of eventually needing long term care.

    Insurers look for certain health risks in women as well as men. While these risks may not limit you now, you could be at greater risk for needing long-term care later. Too many women wait until it’s too late. You can’t buy long-term care insurance after a stroke, a heart attack or once you’ve been diagnosed with Multiple Sclerosis, Parkinson’s Disease or Alzheimer’s, just to name a few conditions that hundreds of American women suffer from. Don’t get caught with an uninsurable health risk.

    As an Independent Broker with LTC Financial Partners (http://www.ltcfp.com), I can provide you with all the information you need to make a sound decision for your future care. Whether online or face-to-face, I will work with you to create the right combination of benefits to accommodate your unique needs as well as your budget.
