Flavia Westerwelle’s Summary

After more than 20 years in sales, marketing and advertising for the building and interior design industry my husband Klaus and I combined our expertise and started our company TransDomo, LLC. Our background enables us to provide comprehensive professional real estate and consulting services to sellers as well as buyers in South Carolina and internationally. We are able to show our buyer clients every property that is on the market in the area of South Carolina and we will make sure that buying or selling your home will be as successful and smooth as possible. Just call us !
Because we are bilingual English/German and offer a extensive business expertise in European countries we can help with your search for a beautiful castle in Germany, a French residence at the Cote d'Azur, a Swiss mountain chalet in the Alps, or with investment, commercial, or industrial properties in bustling European cities.

We provide professional real estate services in
- Residential real estate
- Luxury real estate
- Commercial real estate / Industrial real estate
- International real estate
