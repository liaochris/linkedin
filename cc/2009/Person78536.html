Pete DiSantis, CMC’s Summary

What does PDCA do? We DO profit search and rescue.

We are a management consulting firm focusing on results-oriented process improvement directly and immediately effecting increasing profitability, improving value and empowering employees.

Our Areas of Focus
Business Analysis:
 Project identification, justification, planning, implementation and management.
 Operations audits.

Logistics Support:
 Coordination of transportation, warehousing and distribution operations.
 Route planning.

Industrial Engineering:
 Process improvement or new process development
 Operations planning and control.

Management and Leadership Development:
 Develop, produce and facilitate custom management and leadership workshops.
 Co-active coaching to resolve the most pressing issues.

Our Ideal Client
Any business entity, with little or no engineering or training support; Referred by an existing client or colleague; A management team with aspirations for persistent long-term progress providing prosperity for all stakeholders; Devoted to the continuous improvement of the business through defining goals, personnel empowerment, planning and accountability.

Industries Served
Logistics, Transportation, Distribution, Airline, Banking, Small Businesses, Training and Internet.
