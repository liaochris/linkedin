Laércio Queiroz’s Summary

I'm a solution architect specialized in SAP, BEA and IBM technologies with focus on service-oriented architectures. My main interests lay in application's definition, design and architecture. I have been working in development and application support projects for major companies in Brazil. Currently I'm a senior consultant at BBKO Consulting team, thus doing development supports for external and internal customers.

As SAP Netweaver instructor partner at SAP Brazil, I have written numerous articles and web presentations about various aspects of enterprise technology and participate in several training courses. SAP Netweaver Java and Composition Environment official instructor.

I'm a certified consultant in SAP, IBM and BEA SOA Enterprise Architecture Models, SAP Netweaver Platform, and some other technologies: SAP Enterprise Portal, SCEA(I), SCBCD 5, SCWCD, SCJP, MCP.NET, IBM OOA&D.
