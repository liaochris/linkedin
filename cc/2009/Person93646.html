Salim Sheikh’s Summary

Nominated for “SOA Architect of the Year” as part of Oracle Magazine's 2009 Editor's Choice Awards.
--
An independent consultant, entrepreneur and thought-leader with a refreshing flamboyant and trusted reputation for delivering high-quality advice, strategy, Business/IT solutions across several Industry sectors over the past 13 years.

Enterprise Architect and Strategist delivering consultancy advice, direction and solutions as part of Business & IT alignment strategies.

A well-grounded professional with a strong engineering backed up by an impressive academic record that includes BSc (Hons), MSc & PhD qualifications.

Currently writing a series of books on "Holistic Enterprise Architecture".

Also writing a series of white papers relating to SOA, Business/IT alignment, Governance and IT Strategy for Microsoft, IBM, Oracle, EDS, HP, the Open Group and several Business/IT magazines & journals.
