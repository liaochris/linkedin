Ernesto José Pérez García’s Summary

Ernesto J. Pérez received his Master's degree in Computer Science Engineering from the University of Malaga (Spain) in 2008. He currently works at Avanzada 7, S.L. (Málaga, Spain) as a Software Engineer, developing and leading software projects for customers such as Vodafone and Telefonica.

He also performs research activities in the area of network security, in the University of Malaga.
