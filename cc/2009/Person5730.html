Andrew Macris’s Summary

For the past 14+ years, I've helped marketers meet their goals and look like stars.
First and foremost, I'm a conceptual thinker. Ideas are my business. But not just creative. Good creative without proper targeting and an enticing offer just won't achieve the kind of results you're seeking.

What really makes me do flips is leading a successful marketing program. I know that if I produce for my clients, they'll come back... and they'll recommend me.

Macris Direct is a completely virtual agency, staffed with highly trained and specialized contractors whom I've had a professional relationship for many years. I've gone through the trenches with these folks, and they'll come through for you.

Unlike other agencies who have a love affair with a certain media or pre-packaged solution... generally broadcast media with fat commissions... we recommend the media that will work best for YOUR marketing challenges.

So why pay for italian leather couches and 60-inch flat screen presentation monitors? Save the overhead, and hire Macris Direct. We'll give you better results for less cash.
