Marijn Pannekoek’s Summary

Internet and online marketing is one of my greatest passions. I constantly search the web, and mainly blogs, to stay up to date with the latest news, features, research and technologies on online marketing. Overall I use this information for deciding what is right to fit in the companies online strategy.
Developing this online strategy will be my next focus for the upcoming months. It has my interest to dive deeper into the companywide strategic plan to set the base for the core online activities for Deloitte.com.
I also have a great interest in online marketing for the recruitment department.

Besides my passion for online marketing I have a great interest in developing my compentences. Last year a personal coach guided me in developing my personal effectiveness. I also like to read books like `The 7 habits´ of Stephen R. Covey.
