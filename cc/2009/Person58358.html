Lee Klein’s Summary

Nonrepresentational fine art available for private and corporate art collections executed in oil and mixed media. My work has been exhibited at the Contemporary Art Museum - Saint Louis, Missouri in 2007. I currently have pieces available at the Watergate Gallery in Washington D.C., Lyndsay McCandless Contemporary Gallery in Jackson Hole, Wyoming and a group show in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania curated by Beth Medoway and Colleen Hammond. I will be participating in a fine art exhibition in San Francisco, California on October 28, 2009 curated by Hannah Klein; all proceeds from the sale of my art work will benefit women recently diagnosed with breast cancer.

In the past, my art work has been exhibited at Darnell Fine Art in Santa Fe, New Mexico; Mark Palmer Gallery, Paducah, Kentucky; Hoffman - LaChance Fine Art Gallery, Saint Louis, Missouri; Contemporary Art Gallery at Forest Park in Saint Louis, Missouri; Three Sinks Gallery, Webster Groves, Missouri; Gallery FAB; Saint Louis Missouri; Athena Art Gallery; Omaha Nebraska as well as National Juried Art Exhibitions in Sedona, Arizona; Chicago, Illinois; and New York, New York.



Publications: "Homestead" 2009. W House by architectural designers Nona Yehia and Jefferson Ellinger located in New York City and Jackson, Wyoming.

Corporate Collections include Western Baptist Hospital in Paducah, Kentucky; Folely, Mansfield, Attorneys At Law, Saint Louis, Missouri; Hancock Fabrics in Memphis Tennessee; Gastroenterology Associates in Iowa City, Iowa; Kullmann, Klein & Dioneda, Attorneys At Law; Clayton, Missouri as well as numerous private collectors throughout the world.



Fine art galleries, art consultants, dealers, art museums, curators, universities, corporations, private art collectors, and interior designers may contact me at: artlklein@aol.com.
