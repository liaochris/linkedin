Robin Grant’s Summary

A 14 year veteran of digital marketing, consistently producing effective and award winning work for the world's biggest brands (amongst many others, I'm the proud owner of D&AD, Clio, One Show and NMA Effectiveness awards). I started blogging in '97 and after a stint as Product Development Director at Habbo, I've created conversational marketing campaigns for Eurostar, Shell, GlaxoSmithKline, 3, Canon, STA Travel, Skype and Ford. I'm a founding member of the governing council of WOM UK, the UK's Word of Mouth Marketing Association, a member of the IAB's Social Media Council and a regular conference speaker on social media.

I founded We Are Social to help brands to listen, understand and engage in conversations in social media.

Robin Grant’s Specialties:

strategy, branding, marketing, advertising, conversational marketing, word of mouth, influencer marketing, advocacy programs, social media relations, online PR, buzz, blogs & blogging and most importantly getting things done
