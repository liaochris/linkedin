Tanya Smith, MBA, PHR, CCMC, CLTMC’s Summary

Coach Tanya Smith is a certified career coach specializing in providing faith-based leadership & career management resources. She is founder and lead coach for Design Your Work. Through deep listening techniques, evaluative assessments, and the most current career technologies, Design Your Work helps super busy professionals create or transition into the career divinely designed for them so they can enjoy waking up on Monday morning with a smile!

A self-professed “recovering control addict”, Coach Tanya is committed to continuous self-discovery and encouraging others to rely on spiritual intuition and guidance as the source of all positive solutions. Her personal mission is to influence and encourage others to discover the call that God has on their lives.

Coach Tanya's professional background includes multiple years of experience as a dynamic Human Resources professional for a major corporation, with a designation as a Professional in Human Resources (PHR). Other formal education includes a degree in the field of Psychology and an advanced degree in Business Administration. She is a qualified occupational and Myers-Briggs assessment administrator, and a qualified Leadership Practitioner in Neuro-Linguistic Programming (NLP). Tanya earned her first career coaching certification with Career Coach Academy in 2007 and formalized her calling to work with individuals and groups seeking to find and advance their career calling through Be Intention Career Coaching, now called Design Your Work. In 2008, she completed a professional certification under the Christian track in Leadership & Talent Management Coaching with Leadership Coach Academy.
