Larry Lawfer’s Summary

I have delivered twenty seven years of visual communications for a vast array of corporate and institutional clients. I have won both regional, national and international awards for this work. I have always been active in my community as a volunteer and a leader. I was co-president of the American Society of Media Professionals in New England for a number of years and was approinted to the national board of the ASMP as the Chapter Presidents Representative for two years. I have been active with MITX, BIMA, Social Media Breakfast, Social Media Club of Boston, PodCamp Foundation and a host of philanthropies.
