Dimitri Kalagas’s Summary

After seven years as an Art Director and Designer, Dimitri has developed an international reputation for producing ideas and visual communications that transcend the clutter. A career of creativity spanning an eclectic mix of mediums - including branding, design, editorship, advertising, photography, radio hosting, managing experimental music events and art installations – Dimitri has evolved into directing film, recently releasing his debut music video for Melbourne band TST.

Internationally awarded, Dimitri's work has appeared in various publications including Lifelounge Magazine, Artichoke, Lucky, Desktop, The Block, Communication Arts and Lürzer's Archive as well as a number of websites including QBN, Not Cot, Format, High Snobiety, Hypebeast, Sneaker Freaker, Cpluv, Slam X Hype, The Vine, Complex and Now Now amongst others.

Former and current clients include adidas, adidas Originals, Fosters, Cadbury Schweppes, Fairfax Digital, www.thevine.com.au, Levi's, Jim Beam, Southern Comfort, Chiko, Motorola, Nokia, Ansell Lifestyle, Russian Standard Vodka, www.lifelounge.com, Monster Skate Park, Playboy, Inertia Records, Calvin Klein, Nobody Denim and Reform Clothing.
