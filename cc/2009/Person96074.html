Susan Popoola’s Summary

Susan is a professionally qualified Human Resources professional specialising in Human Resources Transformation (Processes & Information Systems) and Talent Management. Susan focuses on unlocking the potential in individuals and organisations alike by leveraging the power of people and effectively using all of the available resources in order to facilitate strategic growth and development Susan’s experience extends across the private, public and voluntary sectors within the UK and abroad.

She has worked with organisations such as De La Rue, Ove Arup, National Power, Department of Health, Local Authorities, Government Agencies, News International, Penna Group and a number of SME Businesses, NGOs and Charities.

Susan is also the author of "Touching the Heart of Milton Keynes. A Social Perspective" and a Fellow of the Royal Society of Arts

Susan is a pro-active strategic thinker with an eye for detail and a proven ability to work meticulously and calmly. She achieves success by analysing information and situations and adopting a clear communications approach.
