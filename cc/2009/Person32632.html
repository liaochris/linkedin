Jan Simmonds’s Summary

Entrepreneur with over 20 years experience in Media, 12 of which as a top Manager in Music, Film & TV.

Several years ago, I became a proud Dad and chose a lifestyle change, moving to Lymington on the UK's South Coast where I developed several successful local online businesses and community projects and have much enjoyed a more family orientated time. (www.lymington.com)

More recently I have missed the international playing field I used to inhabit and realised my usefullness at home is increasingly limited to paying bills, so with famebook™ & snoozd™ (www.famebook.com & www.snoozd.com) rapidly propelling me back into the limelight, I'm really enjoying the thrill of knowing all my collective experience and extensive contacts are dovetailing into possibly one of the most exciting periods of my life.

Given the reaction to both business models, I should have called them The Pelican Briefs!

famebook™ is a big media play and will be worth a billion dollars or more (or that's the plan) within the next few years and one day will afford me the luxury of writing kids books and not caring if anyone reads them as well as being able to invest in up and coming people like me who derive much more pleasure from giving than taking, so seek wealth to fuel that opportunity.

snoozd™ is a simple model with huge viral potential and is in stealth mode!
