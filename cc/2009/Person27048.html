Diane Kulisek’s Summary

My goal is to provide effective support to individuals and organizations striving to enhance product or service quality.

Diane Kulisek’s Specialties:

Heavily Regulated Industries such as Aerospace and Defense (DCMA, FAA, NASA), FDA-regulated (Medical Device, Pharmaceutical, Nutraceutical, Food, Cosmetic) and, most recently, Nuclear (NRC) and Consumer Product manufacturing; metal fabrication, plastics and assembly operations; Quality Management Systems (ISO, cGMP, QMS), Quality Performance Metrics and Strategic Planning, Corrective and Preventive Action (CAPA), Customer Relations, Supplier Control and Qualification.
