Marco Bardus’s Summary

Born in Udine (Italy), I hold a Master of Arts degree in Corporate Public Relations from the University of Udine in Gorizia (2007). I began my PhD studies in June 2008 at the Institute of Communication and Health, Università della Svizzera Italiana where I also work as a research assistant.

During the last few years I gained expertise in various sectors, such as journalism, PR and media relations, event management, web content management, and in the research field, functioning as project manager and coordinator of various organizations and associations in Italy and abroad.

Among the most important experiences, I worked first as an intern and then as a planner for the Savonlinna Institute for Regional Development and Research, University of Joensuu (Finland), (August 2005 - August 2006) and as press officer for the Province of Gorizia (in 2007). I have also held positions as a research assistant, project manager for several different projects, and consultant for laboratories and departments within the University of Udine. Since July 2005, I have worked as a free lance journalist and I am also an active member of UniFERPI, the students’ branch of the Italian Federation of Public Relations.

Due to my curiosity and need of novelties, I would like to work in the PR industry within big international companies especially in the high-tech, telecommunications, computing, videogaming and energy sectors (Nokia, Google, Samsung, Sony or Apple, just to name a few of my Lovemarks).

I would like to work with people who are willing to share with me their experience, their knowledge and values, exchanging viewpoints and personal opinions.
