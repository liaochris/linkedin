Lisa (Hardman) Dunnavant’s Summary

I'm Lisa, a fearless, seasoned Graphic Designer / Art Director [yes, there's a difference!] with a proven track record and versatile palette. I can take direction, play well with others, work under some crazy tight deadlines AND develop wicked clever ideas! I wanna have some FUN while flexing my creative muscles. My passion is print advertising. I'm constantly striving for aesthetically pleasing [yet thoroughly effective] design. My work includes award-winning designs of corporate collateral materials, ad campaigns, identity, editorial design/re-design, brand strategy, signage/building graphics and more. With a wide range of experience, I’m very versatile and seasoned and (am told) simply a lot of fun to work with! My rate is competitively priced at $100-150/hour and I am anxious to get started...

MY PAST FULL-TIME LIFE: I was an art director for many years in some high-profile Portland and Seattle ad agencies. Went solo in 2000, but still continue to do creative overflow for many. For past 15 years I've served the creative industry as Sr. Art Rep, Gallery Manager, Sr. Graphic Designer, Sr. Art Director to freelance Creative Director. Eventually owning my own design studio, 3SistersDesign. In this time I've had the chance to create award winning work for both small start-ups and worldwide brands. Among the big ones are Nike, Adidas, Cadillac, Dairy Queen, Real Networks, Chase Manhattan, Pfizer, Dr. Marten's, Hewlett-Packard, Epson, Microsoft and more.

MY NEEDS: Coffee. Always Coffee. Oh, and as much as I love the whole freelancing thing (working in my pj's) I also crave some adult conversation and the collaboration amongst other creatives. Would love to partner up! I can always use referrals for clients that need to update their identity and make their marketing materials more effective. Oh and also, I'm a print designer so if one of you amazing web artists want to trade out design services for a new website for moi that would be most awesome.
