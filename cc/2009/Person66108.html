Martin Cunnington’s Summary

Martin joined MRM Worldwide (then Zentropy Partners) in October 2000 from HP (then Compaq) after 10 years’ service in Munich, Germany, where he was responsible for EMEA Internet and Intranet site co-ordination. Martin graduated from the University of East Anglia with a B.Sc. (Hons.) in Computer Studies in 1984. He has more than 20 years IT experience, working with a wide range of platforms, packages and languages in a variety of roles including programming, analysis, systems programming, bulk email and system administration, production, project and quality management.

Martin is a Chartered IT Professional, a PRINCE2 qualified project manager and holds an ISEB Certificate in Software Testing.

Martin Cunnington’s Specialties:

Software project management, software quality assurance, internet, worldwide web, integrated marketing communications, operations, digital production
