Michele Thompkins’s Summary

Protégé is a full service public relations and marketing firm, located in Houston, Texas. Founded in 2005, in that time we have attracted clients like J. Anthony Brown, Mary Griffin, Chris Chew and many more. Protégé has built a reputation for getting our clients noticed.
Public relations are impossible without building relationships. Protégé values our strong relationships with our clients and our media resources. We value our client’s input to find the perfect blend of creativity. Every client is provided with a focused plan to maintain current clients and obtain additional prospects. Establish credibility for your business and conveys expertise with our marketing.
Protégé takes pride in personal consultation with each client. Your project has special needs that we customize to hit your target market and gain exposure. Offering our clients a wide range of services, customized campaigns, we are your one stop for your PR needs.
Michele Thompkins and her team of experts can help your project; small business and band boost sales, credibility and awareness.
As the founder of Protégé, Michele is a public-relations consultant and published writer with over 15 years of extensive experience and an excellent track-record for success. Protégé specializes in getting the national media attention that is needed via targeted public-relations efforts.

Protégé only accepts a limited number of clients. Then, she acts as an extension of
your team, provides the individual attention you deserve and uses her public relations and marketing experience to help your business succeed.
