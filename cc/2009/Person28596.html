Duane Sword’s Summary

Duane Sword has spent the past ten years in various technology ventures, primarily hardware-based platforms for telecommunications test and monitoring. His expertise spans marketing communications, product management and roadmap development. Applications for handheld chemical identification, authentication and screening products for the safety-security-military market, pharmaceutical and consumer health, and a variety of industrial applications where onsite FTIR and Raman spectroscopic instrumentation can save lives, protect communities or increase quality.

In his role as Vice President of Marketing for Ahura Scientific, Mr. Sword is responsible for overall marketing strategy, marketing communications and product management.

Prior to joining Ahura Scientific, Mr. Sword served as Vice President, Product Management for Empirix, a Voice over IP test and monitoring supplier to major service providers and equipment manufacturers.

Mr. Sword also held numerous product management, marketing, and business development roles at Agilent Technologies and Hewlett-Packard throughout his career. He has worked extensively in the telecoms industry from wireline to wireless radio access networks and spent more than ten years in broadband communications.

Recipient of a 2006 "Top 100 in the Voice Industry" recognition and a regular speaker at industry seminars and conferences, Mr. Sword holds a Bachelor of Electrical Engineering degree from the Royal Melbourne Institute of Technology University (RMIT) in Australia.

Duane Sword’s Specialties:

Product Management through Outbound Marketing of hardware-based platforms in a B-B environment in direct sales and channel partner business models.
