Mike Fuller’s Summary

I've been employed in motorsports for 10 years in composites and wind tunnel development program capacities. I've primarily been involved with open-wheel aero programs though not exclusively. I was responsible for the initial visual studies for Panoz CART (DP01) and Superleague (DP09) programs in addition to other unannounced EMT/Panoz projects.

Mike Fuller’s Specialties:

Automotive concept ideation and development with concentrations in aerodynamics and styling. Carbon fiber laminator (pre-preg or wet-lay), pattern and mold maker. Motorsports technology writer (concentrating on the design and development of sportscars). I have one published book and have written for various large circulation print magazines.
