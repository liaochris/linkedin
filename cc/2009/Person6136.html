Andrew Funk’s Summary

Project Engineer for a diverse mechanical product portfolio. Specialist in aircraft systems including environmental control systems, vapor cycle systems, air cycle systems, liquid loop and heat transfer, heat exchanger manufacture, gas turbine engines and auxiliary power systems.

Experience with quality controls, six sigma and globalization initiatives, detailed design and manufacturing processes, root cause and corrective action investigations.

Experienced with both commercial and military product development and testing and certification. Particularly interested in new product development and entrepreneurial endeavors.

Experience leading a diverse team of engineers through a difficult schedule to meet program milestones. I'm currently studying to get my MBA and expand my skillset and experience with market analysis, financial modeling, and entrepreneurship.
