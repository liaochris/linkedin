Dr. Angelo Cacciola Donati’s Summary

To introduce myself, I am Economist, qualified as a licentiate of the University of Parma (Italy) and as Economics Doctor of the University of Fribourg (Switzerland). But I have a lot of different skills, both as a ICT expert and professor and as an artist.
Just after the achievement of my Doctorate I have started working in the ITC sector where I had the opportunity to do significant educational and professional experiences. I have taught ITC to more than 2.000 persons (young people and adult, student, workers and managers) in Spain and Italy. I have conceived and direct four educational projects (Fabulando, New Fabulando, Relate your town, Libera Libertà: la donna nel ‘900) with the partnership of European Commission. From the artistic side, instead, I have written and published numerous (20) fairy-tales in Italian and Spanish.
Recently we are achieve the presentation of FuturistiSiti a new spectacle concerning the Futurism (see: www.futuristisiti.com)
Also, from 1996, I am e-marketing consultant.
