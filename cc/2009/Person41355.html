Indraneel Mukerjee’s Summary

In 1999 March, when I left SAP India as the Marketing Head of the sub-continent, I stopped working and started living!

Today, personally, as well as through iProdigy (http://www.iprodigy.net) I have touched lives of more than 5000 individuals working with about 50 client organizations, in much the same way that I touched my own life. Learning, discovering and unfolding the awesome power within. The whole, complete and perfect Being.

Every moment of my living, as well as of those associated with iProdigy, is driven by the purpose to accompany individuals and enterprises in their journey to discover higher than realized potential for participation and performance and thus consciously evolve into a more meaningful state of existence.

I believe in the living-ness of enterprises and other humanistic systems and passionately pursue the cause of conscious evolution of such entities.
