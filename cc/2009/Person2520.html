Alan Shapiro’s Summary

ALAN SHAPIRO
Chief Creative, Inspiration and Elation Officer
HeadOverHeels Collective

“Make every meeting with a client the highlight of their day”
and "Make every meeting between a brand and their target consumer a head-over-heels, cant-wait-to-see-you-again experience."
Simple thoughts, but ones that have propelled Alan throughout his career and continue each day as he helps clients connect with consumers in passion-inducing ways.

“It’s about careful listening and respectful challenging of our clients and the absolute devotion to their brands that results in exceptional work. And it’s about working tirelessly to understand our client’s consumers better than they do – their passions, their needs and how they liked to be reached.”

Alan's experience spans advertising, integrated marketing and emerging media at Hill Holiday, Grey Advertising and Omnicom Group's Alcone Marketing working on a diverse range of brands including Kool-Aid, Domino's Pizza, Cover Girl, Reebok, John Hancock, Unilever, Pernod Ricard, Heineken, Del Monte, Coty, GE, Hasbro, Nestlé, LG and Smuckers.

Alan realizes that yesterday’s surprises soon become today’s benchmarks, and that his is a job of constant reinvention and reinvigoration.

“In today’s new idea-centric, media-agnostic marketing world, history and where we’ve been don’t count for much. All that matters is where we are going and that we have fun and continue to inspire each other and our clients along the way.”

Alan lives in Briarcliff Manor, NY with his family, an ever-growing menagerie of animals and what is quite possibly the largest collection of carved coconut heads in the world.
