Sharon Kraun’s Summary

I’m a hybrid. My professional career started within the newsroom working as a reporter, assignment editor then producer. Before heading into PR (a logical transition for many ‘retired’ news folks), I jumped into promotions, directing events, public relations and marketing for the Southeast’s largest entertainment weekly newspaper group. A fortuitous lunch led a change in professional direction enabling me to add the business to business experience alongside my consumer and promotional marketing skills.

I’ve been practicing an integrated approach to promoting a business long before the phrase was business chic. I like to approach a challenge looking at all of the pieces of the marketing mix – public relations, advertising, promotions and general marketing to make sure the messaging is unified across all channels.

I excel at helping my clients conceive creative solutions. I don’t just create the ideas. And more importantly, I know how – and actually assist with implementation - backing my words with action.
