Carter Ritchie’s Summary

Carter heads a team of Virginia Realtors serving the Commonwealth. As members of several MLS Systems, the team has the ability to help Sellers market property accross the state while helping Buyers find properties throughout Virginia. The team consists of Carter & Lynley Ritchie and several Buyer's Agents, and a Land & Commercial Specialist. As one of Virginia's leading Real Estate Teams, The Carter Ritchie Team looks forward to sharing their commitment to excellence with you and serving your every Real Estate need.
