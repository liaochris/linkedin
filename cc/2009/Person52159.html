Julia The Transcription Lady’s Summary

I help speakers, authors, coaches and consultants turn their teleseminars and video webinars into products to use in blog posts, article marketing, and ezine articles. I take your audio recording of your teleseminar that is sitting in your desk drawer getting dusty and turn it into cash. To find out more click on http://www.thetranscriptionlady.com

Over 7 years experience in Transcription, Word 2007, Excel 2007, Access 2007, ExpressScribe, Outlook 2007, Abacus Law Management, WordPerfect 12.

I am enrolled at Sanford Brown College in Fenton Missouri majoring Paralegal Studies. I am a Student Ambassador and hold a 3.9 gpa. My short-term goal is to graduate Suma Cum Laude from Sanford Brown this December. My long term goal is to have my brand "The Transcription Lady" be a household name (at least on the internet) and my "Wall of Transcription" a prestigious place for your transcript to be inducted to. I am also an Accredited Legal Secretary for the past 5 years with National Association of Legal Secretaries
