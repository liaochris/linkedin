Molly Gordon’s Summary

14 years life and business coaching

Molly Gordon’s Specialties:

Small business coaching including marketing, promotion, sales, business development and personal growth for entrepreneurs. Focus is on helping accidental entrepreneurs -- people who love their work but have not typically considered themselves as business owners -- to build businesses that fit "just right."
