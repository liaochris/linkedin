Mark Amtower’s Summary

Mark Amtower provides consulting services to small, medium and large government contracting firms in the areas of gaining and maintaining targeted visibility in the government market through ROI-based marketing, sales support, business development support, leveraging social media, strategic introductions for manufacturers and government resellers, targeted PR and press relations.

His radio show, "Amtower Off Center", airs Monday at noon on Federal News Radio (AM 1500) in Washington, DC. It is archived and simulcast on www.FederalNewsRadio.com.

He is the author of Government Marketing Best Practices (5th printing) is recognized as a leading authority on marketing to the government (B2G).

- quoted in over 200 magazines and newspapers, including Entrepreneur (4 times), Forbes, Federal Computer Week (over 40 times), the NY Times, LA Times, Washington Post, Washington Technology, Network World, BtoB, Financial Times, Investor's Business Daily and many others.

- frequent contributor to national business publications, having written more than 150 articles for Catalog Success, DM News, Federal Computer Week, Government Executive, Government Technology Reseller, MC (Marketing Computers), Management Consulting News, Target Marketing, Washington Technology and other business publications.

- popular speaker at various industry events, including the Annual Catalog Conference, Axciom/Direct Media Business Coop, Business Mailer’s Group, Coalition for Government Procurement, Direct Marketing to Business, Federal Channels, Grant Thornton’s CEO Government Roundtable, Hampton Roads American Marketing Association (HRAMA), HighTech Direct, MacWorld Summit, MasterCard Government Financial Summit, MeritDirect B-to-B Coop, Modular Building Institute conference, National Truck Equipment Assn conference, Outlook Conference, SEWP Contract Conference, TechExpo, and his own events.

Amtower is widely known for his dry humor, candor and all black attire.
