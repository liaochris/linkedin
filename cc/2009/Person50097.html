Jonathan M. Soares’s Summary

Jonathan Soares founded Q Products in May 2005 marketing his "Jonathan's Q" brand of gourmet BBQ Sauces, Marinades and Dressings. Mr. Soares single-handily launched the "Jonathan’s Q" brand nationally into more than 5,000 retail locations with a $10k investment in less than 3 years. His company includes a portfolio of developing brands focused in grocery, mass, food service, club and drug store channels of distribution.

As an Advisor/Consultant, Jonathan focuses his efforts on; sales, marketing, brand pioneering, developing strategic partnerships, co-packing relationships, national account development, broker management, viral & social media branding, customer management, sales team management, contract negotiation, product development, in-store-marketing, product merchandising, new product launches, M&A, capital allocation and alternative financing.

Jonathan has been featured in Entrepreneur Magazine, Gourmet News, Live on Fox Business news and ABC News NOW. In 2009 he was honored as one of Fairfield County’s "TOP 40 UNDER 40."
