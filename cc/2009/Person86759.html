Ruben Spruijt’s Summary

Ruben Spruijt works as Solutions Architect at PQR, a major IT company that designs, implements en migrates advanced IT infrastructure and is a Microsoft Gold Certified Patner, Citrix Platinum Solution Advisor, VMware Premier and Consultancy Partner in the Netherlands. In his job, Ruben is primary focussed on Application Delivery, hardware and software virtualisation. He is awarded with the Microsoft Most Value Professional (MVP) 'App-V', Citrix Technology Professional (CTP), VMware xExpert and RES Software Value Professional (RSVP).
