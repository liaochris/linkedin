Dhananjay Parkhe जय् पारखे’s Summary

Mr. Dhananjay Parkhe is a professional with over 34 years of experience in banking, chemicals, fertilizers and express distribution businesses.
He graduated in Agricultural Sciences as an Honours Graduate and did his Masters in Applied Economics.
He is also an alumnus of the Institute of Management Development and Research Pune and he has obtained various online certifications He worked as a Profit centre head for a Dutch Multinational in New Delhi and Mumbai, India.
He has done a stint as an entrepreneur for three years as headhunter, trainer, coach, indenting agent.
From March 2007 Dhananjay has been appointed as Director CSR and Quality and looks after Corporate Social Responsibility, Corporate Sustainability, Business Excellence, Business Strategy, Risk Management, Integrity and Security, Special Projects, Public Affairs.
He was a visiting Professor in Symbiosis Institute of Business Management in Pune, India where he taught Entrepreneurship, business planning and Balanced scorecard. He was a Member of the Governing body of India's premier Management Institute - Indian Institute of Foreign Trade, New Delhi. .
He is a Life Member of the National HRD Network of India, New Delhi Chapter and was a Member of the National Executive committee of Express Industry Council of India.
He represented his company on Federation of Indian chambers of Commerce and Industry FICCI on their Joint Business Councils (European) as a member.
He was a Member of Advisory panel of the Central Board of Film Certification ( Censor board) by the Ministry of Information and broadcasting, Government of India.
He is also a member of the FIEO ( Federation of Indian Exporters Organisation) Services committee and a member of International association of coaches.
He is a member of Bombay Management Association and European Union Chambers of Commerce and Industry, Mumbai, member of European Corporate Governance Institute.
