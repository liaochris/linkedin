Luis Henrique Martinez, Ph.D., CEM, CEA, LEED AP’s Summary

A Project Development, Construction, Energy, and Sustainability executive with over 20 years of experience in the energy, oil, and construction industries; combined with strong educational background in construction management and business administration results in a uniquely effective combination of international business development, project development, consulting, and project management skills.

ABENGOA SOLAR ►www.AbengoaSolar.com◄ develops and applies solar technologies for sustainable development in the infrastructure, environment and energy sectors through the application of Concentrating Solar Power (CSP) and Photovoltaic (PV) technologies.

PERT-CPM, LLC ►www.Pert-Cpm.com◄ a Project Development consulting firm that assists project developers, property owners, investors, and lenders in the energy, oil, utilities, hospitality, industrial, and real estate industries.

CARBON NEUTRAL SOLUTIONS, LLC ►www.CarbonNeutralSolutions.net◄ a sustainability consulting company that provides Energy Audits, Sustainability, Energy Efficiency Solutions and Retrofits to industrial, commercial, and residential clients.

Prior to the above, Mr. Martinez managed the acquisition, construction, completion, operation, and sale of several large developments in Latin America: multiple resorts and hotels in Mexico and Venezuela; power plants in Panama, Peru, El Salvador, and Mexico; and a number of high rise commercial and multifamily construction projects.

Educational background includes a Ph.D. in Construction Management from Purdue University; Certified Energy Manager (CEM) and Certified Energy Auditor (CEA) from the Association of Energy Engineers; and LEEP AP from the US Green Building Council. ►www.luishenriquemartinez.com◄
