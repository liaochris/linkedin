Tina Moore’s Summary

I founded PrimoSites Web Developers in 2001. My goal: to fill in a customer service gap in the web design industry. Before My company existed there were web Design firms who catered to big companies and the price, of the services offered, forced small companies to decline becoming a client. On the other end were template websites that were cost effecient for small business but still not the answer because it forced the small business owner to learn or get left behind in search engine rankings, when you own a small business your time is already sechduled to the maximum. My goal became a reality by offering a cost effective solution to those small businesses that were finding it difficult to get a good web presence. I am a woman business owner, I take pride in my small business and I aspire to see it grow just like other small business owners. Because I am a small business owner, I understand the needs of my clients. Each year my clients tell others who become clients, the growth is so exciting for me to be a part of! When we say, "We make having a great website easy!" We mean it!
Please take the time to look at my web site and let me know if I can help your small business too!
