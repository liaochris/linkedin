Accenture - Regional Managing Partner for Government CRM, Immigration, Justice and Public Safety from 2002-2006 across EMEA, Canada and Latin America.

Accenture - Head of Strategy Consulting and Telecommunications in Eastern Europe from 1997-2000.

Gemini Consulting - 3 years in transformation strategy (Analysis and Design team).
PA Consulting - 5 years strategy across public and private sectors.
HM Foreign and Commonwealth Office - Diplomatic Officer from 1981-89 with experience in regional policy, finance, strategy and IT.
