Tim Pine’s Summary

Commercial insurance broker for one of the largest regional independent insurance brokerages based in California as reported by the Los Angeles Business Journal, Arroyo Insurance Services. As a third generation insurance professional licensed in California and 43 other states I have diverse group of clientele ranging from property owners to international software companies. My sales career has been dedicated to business development starting in internet search and ERP software, which along with my educational back ground in neuroscience, gives me a unique advantage in understanding complex business models and identifying risk.

I believe in partnering with my clients to truly understand their businesses, business philosophy, where they want to grow and how we can position their insurance program to make their future goals a reality while protecting the assets they have built. In the Commercial insurance environment, companies have many choices when it comes to insurance brokers, we are here to show them that we are more than just a broker, we are a partner in you business and your future success.

The depth of my support team at Arroyo Insurance Services and the resources available from the companies we represent allows me great flexibility in handling new risks and obtaining all available credits and discounts for my clients. We work with 35+ a Rated California Admitted Insurance companies and forge relationships with them which help us in our negotiations on behalf of our clients. We also have excellent relationships in the surplus lines markets which at Arroyo, I have been afforded the flexibility to work with clients from a wide range of fields including non-profits, technology and biotech, manufacturing, construction, and the entertainment industry to name a few. Contact me at my site http://www.socalinsurancebroker.com or (800) 401-6764

My Personal Philosophy - Integrity, Honesty and developing a thorough understanding of my clients needs.
