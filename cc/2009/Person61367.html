Magalie Pedrono’s Summary

2008: Master in Management of innovation projects and entrepreneuriat

Initially formed as industrial designer, I don't find interests any more to be working in a domain in which the main purpose is to create objects for mass consumption. That's why, for 2 years already, I have chose to work on social design.
I more especially exercise my creativity so it serve for the development of responsible lifestyles, by proposing alternative systems which make it possible to adopt a reduced and reasoned consumption, for example.
I use now design's tools and strategy in order to attract the consumers towards more responsible behaviors, and to gradually allow them to be autonomous (in their acts, their thought etc.).
I thus consider my work as a help to transition and my main motivation is anchored in the urgency that there is to modify our Western lifestyle massively. I would like to contribute to it by proposing adapted services.

I am therefore interested in all that is sustainable design, social design, slow design and eco-systemic thinking…
