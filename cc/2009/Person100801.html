Travis Stoliker’s Summary

Experienced Marketing Director with a skill for innovation and continual improvement.

I am the Sales & Marketing Director for Liquid Web Inc. My day to day activities include AdWords management, marketing strategy, sales management, creative management, brand identity, website writing, press release writing and new media advertising through Twitter, Facebook and other avenues. At Liquid Web, working with my team, I executed the first Trade Show, completed a company website redesign, the first customer newsletter, hired and formed the first sales department, first Microsoft partnership, hired 90 new employees (120total) and constantly break monthly revenue records since 2007. Liquid Web was recognized by INC Magazine as one of the nations fastest growing companies in 2006 and 2007. I executed two Grand Opening events which received television, radio and newspaper coverage. Governor Granholm, two house representatives, our local politicians and all local media attended these events. Local recognition has tripled, national and global recognition has gained approximately 60% yearly based on Unique page views.

In addition to my marketing activities, I am also a director and business manager focused on continual business improvement and strategic planning. I worked with our support managers and the whole company to reduce our support times (email response average from 2 hours to 10 minutes) and improve our overall support quality. I rewrote the employee handbook, formalized the employee review process and created specialized departments to improve efficiency and consistency.

I worked as a product manager at a global software company. I was responsible for product feature requests, Marketing Requirements Documentation and working with a team of engineers to translate customer feedback into product innovations. To understand our customers, I travel globally to trade shows and other events and engage directly with our customers and partners.
