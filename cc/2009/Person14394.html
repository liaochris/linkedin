Billy Shih’s Summary

I am a specialist in website optimization through multivariate and a/b split testing and an overall proponent of all online marketing, e.g. PPC, search and social media.

Throughout my short professional career I have gained experience in offline and online marketing, importing, information systems, blogging, retail sales, and entrepreneurship.

I have a deep love for the Internet and the power it holds for improving the human experience. My goal is to contribute to the growth and proliferation of the web through providing content and working within the online industry.
