Steve Kaye’s Summary

I facilitate meetings and conduct workshops on leadership skills.

The workshops help leaders accomplish more in three areas:

1) Meetings: Better meetings get results that take your business forward.

2) Presentations: Better presentations inspire people to take action.

3) Communication: Better communication makes everything more efficient.

Since 1992 my workshops have shown leaders modern techniques that are easy to apply. And my meeting facilitation always achieves results that others will support.

I've written three books on effective meetings and one book on leadership, published over 900 hundred blogs and articles, and appeared on radio and TV.

Read my blog for daily tips on how to be a more effective leader. Visit my web site for dozens of articles.
