Jason and Amber Gardner’s Summary

Successfully bringing homebuyers and sellers together in Sherwood Oregon & the greater Portland Metro areas through years of experience, a respected name and proven results!

As one of the most recognizable Realtors making a positive difference in the Sherwood Oregon area, Jason and Amber Gardner stand by their one core philosophy, “Committed to YOUR Success.” The Gardner Team believes the important things in life start at home. Insuring our clients enjoy their experience in the purchase or sale of that home is of paramount importance.

Working primarily by referral, many look to Jason and Amber as the areas preferred real estate experts for those looking to purchase or sell residential homes, luxury estates, land, acreage or wine country properties and new construction real estate. You can see some of these recommendations on our video testimonials at www.RealEstateStories.net or by reviewing our history of performance as leading Realtors and award-winning Top Producers.

With great appreciation for your confidence and trust, we're prepared to represent all your real estate needs and deliver desired results. This will be accomplished through our focus and commitment to exceptional service, providing unmatched value at all times, utilizing our extensive professional network and creating demand for your home through our vigorous print and online home marketing strategies.
