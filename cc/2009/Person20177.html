Colleen Wainwright’s Summary

Basically? I provide marketing focus for overwhelmed self-starters.

Which means? I help people make sense of the bewildering array of options and opportunities that the modern world of marketing and social media presents us all with, and come up with a clean, actionable plan. It's amazing what smart, motivated types can actually get done once they have a map and a good light.

I also do a lot of writing (essays, blog articles, columns), some speaking and a great deal of Horsing Around on the Internet. That's right: I'm a NERD.

Colleen Wainwright’s Specialties:

Marketing, performance (the song-and-dance kind, not the ROI kind), public speaking, punditry.
