Simone Ventola, ENG, MBA, CISM’s Summary

My day to day life in PricewaterhouseCoopers Advisory is spent supporting and managing projects for the improvement of IT organization and effectiveness.

I'm used to contribute and coordinate analysis of the as-is IT environment (from technology, organization and process points of view) in order to detect and communicate gaps that may arise from best practices and/or legal constraints.

The usual follow-up comprises identifying remediation activities and supporting clients in defining the action plan and implementing solutions.

PricewaterhouseCoopers (www.pwc.com) provides industry-focused assurance, tax and advisory services to build public trust and enhance value for its clients and their stakeholders. More than 146.000 people in 150 countries across our network share their thinking, experience and solutions to develop fresh perspectives and practical advice. In Italy about 3000 professional operate from 17 different cities.

“PricewaterhouseCoopers” refers to the network of member firms of PricewaterhouseCoopers International Limited, each of which is a separate and independent legal entity.

An asset I am leveraging on my previous professional experience:
- IT architectures
- Logical security, encryption and digital signature
- IT Security Standards (ISO 27000, PCI/DSS, etc.)
- Project Management
- IT Governance and Organization
- Enterprise-wide Risk Management
- Corporate and IT Strategy
