Kathleen Donahue Aguilar’s Summary

I own the Virtual Arts Law Center, a law firm. The Virtual Arts Law Center provides affordable "virtual" in-house counsel to nonprofit institutions with a focus on arts nonprofits. I help medium to large nonprofits handle day-to-day legal issues while staying within a limited budget. When a nonprofit is not ready to hire a full-time attorney or does not want to pay high fees to outside law firms for routine matters, I can help. With my background as a musician, arts administrator, and corporate lawyer, I have the experience and skills to become a team member and help a nonprofit organization fulfill its mission, reach its business goals, and manage risk.

Kathleen Donahue Aguilar’s Specialties:

Nonprofit law, tax-exempt organizations, contract negotiation, contract law, copyright law and trademark law, computer law, privacy law, employment law, mergers and acquisitions, immigration law, insurance law, litigation.
