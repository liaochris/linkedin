Charles King’s Summary

A former US Army officer specializing in guided missile technology and neuclear weapons deployment. An instructor at the Army's prestigious Air Defense Center at Fort Bliss, Texas. Commanding officer of the first generation Pershing Missile System. Certified as a Surveyor by the US Army.

Founder and organizer of the Mansfield (Ohio) Opportunities Industrialization Center. A job training facility for disadvantaged and "unemployable" persons, placing more than 100 previously disadvantaged persons in industrial jobs per year for the three years. Upon accepting this job, discovered there was no money to pay the salary offered. In addition to setting up the center and designing curriculm, had to go about the community raising funds.
Although many said it couldn't be done, the agency was accepted as a United Way agency in its third year.
The agency continued to serving the community for seventeen years.


Consultant in Organization Design, Development and Structuring and corporate training for more than thirty-five years, working with some of America's foremst organizations: Americhem, Inc.; Babcock & Wilcox, Firestone Tire & Rubber Co. , General Electric, Indiana Bell, Nationwide Insurance, North Carolina Mutual, Ohio Bell, US Department of Personnel, US Environment & Protection Agengy, Goodyear Tire & Rubber; New York Life, The States of North Carolina and Ohio. Wake County, NC Government.

Corporate Executive: Assistant Vice President, Wholesale Sales at Warner Press, Inc. Led a team of seven Regional Salespersons across the United States, stocking christian bookstores with the company's products. In addition there was a staff of eighteen telemarketers taking client orders by phone. Achived double digit sales increases while there.

Currently bringing the numerous experiences from a full career together to offer practical world class mentoring, coaching and training to the people of this country.

Website: charles-king-masterful-living.com

Charles King’s Specialties:
