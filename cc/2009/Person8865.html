Aviram Eisenberg’s Summary

Ignite's unique approach to software outsourcing for ISV's (Independent Software Vendors) in Israel is the brainchild of Ignite Founder and CEO Aviram Eisenberg.

Previous to launching Ignite, Aviram served as the Chief Architect for MIND CTI, a public company traded on the NASDAQ that provides end-to-end billing and mediation solutions for the Telecom industry.

Aviram provided solid leadership for the architecture group, the group that defines the Company's next-generation solutions. He designed and managed software projects for strategic customers like Vodafone and Hutchison, and managed on-going technical relationships with such partners as Oracle, SUN, IBM, HP and others.

As MIND's Chief Architect, Aviram was an integral part of the development and optimization of the hybrid onsite-offshore delivery model initiated by MIND to meet the need for cost-effective R&D in the wake of the 'dot.com' crisis. Aviram oversaw the development of new R&D procedures to support this delivery model, defined and conducted training for R&D personnel and forged effective communications between the Israeli and East-European teams.

Aviram saw the potential benefit inherent in hybrid onsite-offshore delivery for the entire Israeli software industry. After seven years of intensive and successful work, he launched Ignite to make this proven delivery model accessible to all Israeli ISVs.

Aviram holds a B.Sc. degree in Computer Science from the Technion and has served for more than seven years in the Israeli software industry.

Aviram Eisenberg’s Experience

  • CEO

    Ignite Ltd.

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Computer Software industry)

    March 2006 — Present (3 years 5 months)

    Aviram is the Founder and CEO of Ignite, a pure play Software Outsourcing company that provides enterprise-scale software solutions for Independent Software Vendors in Israel.

    Ignite utilizes Clear-Box Software Outsourcing, a unique methodology tailored to meet the specific requirements of ISVs:

    Onsite-offshore delivery model: Ignite's onsite team provides face-to-face project management, requirement analysis, architecture, UI design and integration; the offshore team - design, implementation and testing.

    Milestone based pricing: Ignite’s pricing model reduces the R&D risk to a minimum. Payments are made only after the milestone’s acceptance criteria were approved by the customer.

    Distributed agile development process: Frequent milestones, continious integration, test automation and short delivery cycles keep customers in full control of their projects and open development windows that make changes and adjustments a natural part of the process.
