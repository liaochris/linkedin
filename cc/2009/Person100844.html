Trena Daignault’s Summary

As a permanent resident of this community I am familiar with the area and the housing market. I will provide you with timely service from the initial appointment to the closing table. Should you need assistance with moving, mortgage loans, inspectors, insurance or other aspects of buying or selling a home, I can provide you with professional business partners who I am confident you will be happy with.

I am always seeking to step it up to the next level. This benefits me personally and professionally. It also benefits you because it allows me to be on the edge of new technology, sharpens my skills and keeps me informed and up to date on current issues affecting our industry.
