Matthew Allen’s Summary

Verticalmove, Inc. is a highly respected Headhunting and Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO) service provider that is focused upon building human capital and intellectual assets for Fortune 1000 and emerging growth companies worldwide.

For more than a decade we've represented some of the worlds most prestigious companies such as Johnson & Johnson, Microsoft, State Street Bank, Network Appliance and EMC Corp. We've collaborated with these and many other organizations to identify, design and implement sustainable process and best-practices that standardize and streamline all aspects of attracting, managing and retaining exceptional professional talent.

Our RPO services provide comprehensive and fully customized solutions, which are designed to significantly improve overall talent quality, diversity and time-to-hire, while diminishing risk, costs-per-hire and turnover. Each onsite assignment is supported by an end-to-end, fully integrated, web-based platform that provides “actionable” Reporting & Analytics, HRMS, Applicant Tracking, Career Portal Hosting and Workflow Automation.

We don't advertise on job boards. We don't search on resume boards. We offer our clients a truly unique network of passive individuals that they would likely have NOT had prior. We'd rather avoid putting our clients into overly competitive situations and only deal in passive individuals who are likely happily employed and not announcing themselves to the masses.

We've built relationships with an extensive number of professionals throughout our industry, individual contributors all the way to the 'C' level executives. In fact, we have documented more than 124,685 conversation with more than 22,858 professionals since we started started tracking in 2004.
