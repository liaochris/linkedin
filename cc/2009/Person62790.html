Mariëlla Beukers’s Summary

Wijnkronieken.tekst en redactie is a text and editing bureau with specialization in wine.

- Winemerchants and importers for example can contact me if they need assistance in writing newsletters, content for websites, press releases etc... Contacting me to do this work saves them time, valuable time they can spend directly on their customers!

- I also write for consumer magazines and platforms, e.g. By the Grape.com and Taste!, Gall&Gall's magazine.

- If you are interested in a wine tasting accompanied by an introduction on the history of wine for example, please contact me. The audience at Museum Dorestad was very enthusiastic to hear about wines in the early Middle Ages.

In my professional career, I have build up the following experience.

- Writing and editing texts for newsletters, websites, magazines etc....

- Blogging: first Dutch blogger on wine and wine history (from July 2003-...). See www.wijnkronieken.nl. You can also follow me on Twitter as @Wijnkronieken.

- Product management for books and educational material.

- Editing and publishing, together with communications, taking care of all activities within a small publishing department.

- Member of Quality Team, auditing

- Board member for public relations of Vereniging Oud-Utrecht : organizing volunteers, working with volunteers etc..., editing website.

- (Academic) research: (medieval) history, having resulted in several articles.
