Sally Dagna’s Summary

My bookkeeping experience is somewhat eclectic. I owned my own special event business for many years in Arizona, and as the owner I also took care of all the bookkeeping and tax filings for the business. Later, I provided some account services for a variety of other businesses in Arizona and California.

Currently, I work about half the time in the areas of data entry and payroll, letting my husband whose specialty is taxes handle the larger issues. The other half of my time is spent networking and public relations.

As far as professional goals are concerned, mine include working more closely with the independent film industry in our area.

Sally Dagna’s Specialties:

My expertise with regard to independent film in particular comes from both education and experience. My BFA was earned in Film Production, and I have been a partner in small independent film companies both in Arizona and Washington, so have much more first-hand knowledge about the types of expenses and what they cover with regard to that particular industry than those who have not worked directly in it.

  • Member

    Mill Creek Police Department Citizen Patrol Booster Club

    (Non-Profit; Fund-Raising industry)

    May 2009 — Present (3 months)

    The MCPDCPBC is a non-profit organization currently being formed to support the work of the Mill Creek Police Department's Citizen Patrol through organized fund-raising events and community awareness efforts.

    The Mill Creek Police Department's Citizen Patrol is comprised of area residents who go through a rigorous six-week training program followed by on-going training throughout the year as opportunity arises. The members are not City of Mill Creek employees. They are all either currently working or retired adults from the local area who bring with them a wealth of skill and experience. Several are past law enforcement professionals, while others are current business owners and employees from the greater Seattle community.

    The MCPDCPBC's purpose in raising needed funds for equipment, training, vehicles, and other expenses through this non-profit organization is two-fold: 1) to increase the community's understanding of the vast resources and services being provided to it at little-to-no impact to the City's strained budget, and 2) to garner financial support from the community the MCPDCPBC serves.

    My position within this organization is currently Vice President of the Board of Directors.

    Watch for more information and our website (currently under construction) to come!

  • Co-Owner

    Full Potential LLP

    (Accounting industry)

    2006 — Present (3 years)

    We provide bookkeeping, payroll services, and tax preparation for small business owners both locally and in other states. We maintain the flexibility to provide as much or little support as our clients need. Some (and we encourage this) come to us for QuickBooks training so they can do all their own data entry, then see us every quarter to clean up anything that may need adjusting and generate their quarterly reports. Others call us on an as-needed basis, while still more give us 100% of their bookkeeping work and just drop off or fax us everything as it comes in.

    Independent filmmakers call on us from time to time as projects come up, and we take care of any needs they may have. In some cases they need payroll run, followed by sending out all the W-2's the following January. For others we have done full-blown bookkeeping pre-through-post, payroll, and liaison between the filmmaker and SAG to make sure all the union's requirements are met and reports filed.
