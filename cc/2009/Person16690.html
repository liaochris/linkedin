Alessandra Battochio Casolato’s Summary

CH2A is directed by Alessandra M. Battochio Casolato. She has started working professionally as a trainee at DPZ Propaganda, one of the biggest advertising agencies in Brazil, where she worked in the area of Press Service and Public Relations.
She has integrated groups in the divulgation of clothing brands, and renowned fashion designers such as: Huis Clos, Fause Haten, Walter Rodrigues, Ellus and great fashion shows and events in this sector, such as: Phytoervas Fashion and Morumbi Fashion.
She has worked for Natura Cosméticos (Cosmetics) making events and taking part in the divulgation of the launching of perfumery among other institutional events of the company. Has also participated in the divulgation and in the event of celebration of 10 years of BM&F – Bolsa de Mercadorias e Futuros, in shows at Teatro Municipal de São Paulo (Municipal Theater), with great singers of the Brazilian Pop Music.
She has also worked at Morumbi Shopping, where was the responsible coordinator of the divulgation of other great shopping malls in São Paulo (Shopping Paulista, Shopping Metrópole, Shopping Anália Franco).
She has also worked in the divulgation of clients in the Health and Beauty, such as: Abbott Laboratórios, Bausch & Lomb, Cliníca Mayo de Jacksonville (Florida), and in the area of culture and entertainment with: SESC SP – Serviços Social do Comércio and Wet’n’ Wild.
She has also worked at Memorial da América Latina (Latin America Memorial) as senior artistic cultural producer before creating in the year 2000, CH2A Communications, A company that, nowadays, has in its portfolio long term clients, such as: Centro da Cultura Judaica (since 2000), Tampopo Hair Cutting Team (since 2001), Exponor Brasil and Casa Valduga (since 2003
