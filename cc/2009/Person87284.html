Ryan C Davidson’s Summary

As an interactive designer/developer I work to solve complex design and development problems in interactive environments. I have 3 years experience working with user experience design and have added development to my list of expertise. I have a deep understanding of the business of creative interactive work and can act as a bridge between designers' creative needs, developers' technical needs and business objectives.

At PBJS I also worked heavily with measurable results for creative interactive work, including site traffic and usage, user experience testing and return on investment metrics.

I also have experience writing/editing technical documents including functional specs, documentation and applicaiton wire frames.
