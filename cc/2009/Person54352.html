Kelly Costa’s Summary

I have worked in the field of environmental sustainability and social change for sixteen years. Each project has deepened my knowledge of issues and resources; each position has expanded my range of experience in both nonprofits and social enterprises; each opportunity has honed my research, writing and communication skills.

Now, I’m applying my interest in education and restoration as teacher at Taproot Academy, as member of the Sunset Youth Services board, and as a researcher and consultant to short-term, purpose-driven projects.
