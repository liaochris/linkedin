Becky Dorton-Byers’s Summary

I am a new face in the embroidery business, but I am not new to sewing. I started sewing at age eight when my grandmother taught me to make doll clothes. And, just sewing wasn't enough for me. I soon learned to design, adding embellishments such as applique and hand embroidery. I also learned to crochet and quilt. By seventh grade, my home economics teacher was amazed at the perfection in my sewing projects.

I have always taken great pride in any project I have created, giving a tremendous amount of attention to detail. I promise you this same detailed quality with your embroidered items. It is so exciting to see a design that was created with software and then to see it embroidered on your apparel, home decor, totebag, or baby gift ... just to name a few!

Circle B Designs of Embroidery is operated out of our home in the embroidery shop. You may Order from our Web Site, Brochure, or if you are in my area, stop in and see finished items. At this time, shop is open by appointment only.

I would appreciate the opportunity to earn your business. Of course, your complete satisfaction is always 100% guaranteed.

Give me a call or email to discuss your ideas. I look forward to starting a working relationship with you.
