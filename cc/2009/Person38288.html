Guy-Charles Pelletier’s Summary

Serial entrepreneur with over 15 years of experience in start-ups with an innovative approach that creates value through original and sustainable business models.

I spent the last 10 years developing businesses involved in Internet video, creating the best synergies between technology, digital media/entertainment and Information technology through Live broadcast and Video on Demand via a multi-platform distribution approach.

Enhanced by a creative spin, I bring an extensive business background to develop the best profitable strategies to introduce Internet TV into any global organizations.

Since 2001, I provide Internet TV strategies to help global marketing, communications and PR agencies, telecommunications groups, media and entertainment groups, corporations, foundations and governements.

My goal is to create value for profitable and non-profitable organizations by developing everyday the global online video community.
