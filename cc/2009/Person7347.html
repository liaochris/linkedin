Pascale Cotton’s Summary

Coach training at Coachville (USA), the biggest coach training institute in the world (www.coachville.com):
- Thomas Leonard School of Coaching
- Graduate School of Coaching
- and the various Coachville programs in the areas of e-marketing, corporate coaching, small business coaching, etc.
Coaching practice since 2002.
Founding member of Coachville (2001)
Founding member of the International Association of Coaches (IAC) (2003).

Multiple trainings in many American coaching techniques (Coachville), NLP or Neuro-Linguistic Programming (Master Practitioner) and American marketing and business development methods (particularly e-marketing).

I am based near Geneva, Switzerland, but I coach clients from various countries on five continents in French, English or German. The coaching takes place through the phone and Internet.

Pascale Cotton’s Specialties:

Coaching of
- expats or diplomats (integration, overcoming culture shock, intercultural leadership, multicultural teams, work/life balance, networking, career development)
- "trailing spouses" who want to develop their own career or to create their own "portable business"
- international or mobile solo professionals, entrepreneurs, infopreneurs and small business owners, who want to create and develop their own "portable business" or virtual business using the Internet and e-marketing.
