Todd Thomas’s Summary

President of IMPACT Consulting and Development, LLC and IMPACT Publications, LLC. Both serve to enable change with impact! The focus of my work is showing leaders how to leverage their strengths in a way that brings their lives, and those of their employees, closer to their full potential. I have worked in industry for the last 13 years. Prior to this I was a college professor at North Carolina State University and Indiana University.

Weekly columnist for TheStreet.com and frequent guest on Fox Business News, Neal Cavuto, CNBC and others.
