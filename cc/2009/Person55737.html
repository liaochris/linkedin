Kip Finch’s Summary

Kip Finch has over 19 years of creative experience in the direct marketing industry. As a strategic thinker, with exceptional creative development, production and management skills he has successfully implemented highly profitable creative campaigns in both traditional print media as well as the interactive space. Kip has managed small and large creative teams with a strong track record cross multiple industry verticals. He is an advocate for exceptional user experiences and data-driven creative development. He is passionate about getting results and is financially prudent in all of his endeavors.

Kip Finch’s Specialties:

Creative development, Marketing campaign strategies, SEO and SEM, conceptual development and brainstorms, inspiring creativity, innovative thinker, strong concepts and creative solutions, persuasive leadership skills through thoughtful management.
