M. Evan Parker’s Summary

A licensed Cosmetologist and 28-year veteran of the professional salon industry, M. Evan Parker holds a Master's Degree in Business Administration, with an expansive background in salon management and operations, product and program development, marketing, sales, and education.

Working with leading manufacturers, progressive distributors, and top salons, Evan has developed and launched many of the industry's most innovative and successful products and programs.

Specializing in the support and development of small to mid-sized companies, Evan's unique experience and insight allows for the creation of highly effective programs and systems guaranteed to take your business to the next level.
