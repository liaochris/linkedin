Eric Rose, M.D.’s Summary

I have been involved in clinical informatics since shortly after completing my residency in Family Medicine in 1996. Presently, I am responsible for the Practice Partner EHR, an ambulatory electronic health record. I continue to practice medicine and to teach at the University of Washington.

Eric Rose, M.D.’s Specialties:

I am experienced in the design and implementation of, and training in the use of, electronic health records and related technologies. My particular interests within clinical informatics are in data modeling, interoperability, user interface design, and clinical decision support.
