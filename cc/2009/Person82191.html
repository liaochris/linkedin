Raul Rodriguez Jr., CPA’s Summary

Senior Finance (CPA) and Corporate Development executive with leadership experience in the development and execution of market and growth strategy. Extensive mergers and acquistions (M&A,) divestiture and greenfield development experience in a highly disciplined, strategic investment process. Advisor and business partner to executive and senior business unit management at a dynamic and decentralized Fortune 500 company. Strong finance and accounting foundation with former Big 6 firm.
