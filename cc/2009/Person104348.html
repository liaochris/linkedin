Wesley Piercy’s Summary

With over 10 years of Sales, Advertising and Marketing experience you are hiring a well rounded Real Estate Consultant who can help you get the most out of your Real Estate transaction. Whether it is price, ease, convenience or a unique set of circumstances I will be 100% focused on your needs.

The attention to detail and the emphasis I place on educating clients throughout the transaction have earned me high praise from loyal clients. I provide outstanding client counseling, education and service through the arduous task of buying or selling a home. Many of my clients have found me through the web site and have been pleasantly surprised that one simple contact was all they needed to buy, sell, or get information about Kansas City and surrounding real estate. Call or e-mail me with any questions or services needed and I will be happy to help. My goal is to get RESULTS for you.

Kansas City Metro properties are my main focus, however if you are buying or selling outside of the area I can help locate a great agent anywhere in the world. I specialize in residential homes including single family homes, condos and even multiple unit buildings as well as commercial properties. I work with Buyers, Sellers and Investors, and in cases where your needs are elsewhere I will gladly match you with another experienced REALTOR who specializes in your area of interest. Call me at 913-488-0566 or email me at wesley@wesleypiercy.com today and let me go to work for you.

My Philosophy

"I know first hand buying and selling a home can be very stressful. It is my personal goal to make your real estate transaction as smooth and stress free as possible. My professionalism, knowledge and experience will guide you through, step by step."
