Karl Van der Auwera’s Summary

In the past years we did several migrations and brand new installations of computernetworks. Most of the time based on HP/compaq hardware and Microsoft networkingsoftware. The rest of the time we did networkmanagement, technical writing, networksecurity etc.

For the near future we really want to stay in the segment of the SMB's. We are going to specialize in projects like installations, migrations etc. In the mean while we are working on the monitoring of the networks of our customers because that's the second thing we want to specialize Consultrix in. The third stap is a more in depth knowledge of everything that concerns security, disaster recovery, anti-spam, anti-virus, etc etc etc.
We are already working on that today and we try to offer the best for our customers but we are sure it always can get better.

We are currently setting up DEMO's for our latest product : OfficeByWeb. More information will follow later. But if you are to curious you can always contact me !
