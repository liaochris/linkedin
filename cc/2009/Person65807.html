Mayur Jobanputra’s Summary

Since graduating from SFU with a Business degree in 1996, I have spent the last 10 years working in the IT field. I have gained extensive experience with Technical Services and Support, Technical Writing, QA, Release Management, Web Development, Web server management and much more. I have experience managing networks and software systems large and small: everything from a 10 PC small business networks to large Enterprise class banking servers running Microsoft Datacenter.

Most recently I used VMWare virtualization technology to manage a farm of 50+ virtual servers on a daily basis as part of my responsibility as a Release Engineer for Intrawest. I managed all builds and deployment for QA and Production and UAT environments using NANT, Visual Studio, and Installshield. I also actively played a role in on-site Production releases and ensuring availability of our Intrawest online web sites.

With Full Motion Group, we create graphic and web design providing everything from business cards, logos, to web sites for businesses. We design web sites in ASP, PHP, SQL, Joomla, Wordpress, mySQL, Photoshop, and occassionally Flash. You can visit our site at www.fullmotiongroup.com for more information about us.
