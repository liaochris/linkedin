Roger Harris’s Summary

As Social Media Manager at Capstrat my role is to develop and implement strategies for clients to support their engagement with their audience via the social Web. I work with our Web development and creative teams to ensure seamless integration of existing client assets into social media channels. My social media specialist areas include:
• Assessing online conversations around an organization
• Evaluating and monitoring online reputations
• Marketing and branding online content
• B2C and B2B outreach and communication

For Capstrat clients, I blend social media approaches inside a PR/communications framework to extend client messaging and outreach into Web 2.0 and social channels. I also present to industry and civic groups on social media, beginning with social media basics, through to strategy development and implementation.

Recent presentations include:
• Games People Play: Social Media Dimensions of Online Gaming. Triangle Gaming Conference
• Social Media Panel. Public Relations Student Society of America
• Launching Your Non-profit into Social Media Space. Branding in the Digital Age, Council of Public Relations Firms
• Counting on Social Media. American Institute of Public Certified Accountants
• Mobile: Marketing on the Move. Capstrat (Internal)
• Social Media Trends. Capstrat (Internal)

I help businesses understand the potential of Internet marketing and communications. Recent projects include:
• Developing social media strategy for large and small organizations
• Assisting companies in planning for social media crisis
• Determining appropriate social media metrics for campaign evaluation
• Articulating best practices for a range of social media platforms including Twitter, Facebook and LinkedIn
