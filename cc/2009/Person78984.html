Peter Wilkerson’s Summary

Information architect with considerable experience solving business problems with enterprise search, content management and related information technologies. Specializes in bringing different groups together to solve problem at hand, often acting as an interpreter among the different groups so needs are better understood and met. Particularly interested in methodologies that indicate information architecture's success in meeting business goals, such such as ROI.
