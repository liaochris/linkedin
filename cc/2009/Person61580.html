Malcolm Campbell’s Summary

I am a novelist and freelance writer. My metaphysical adventure novel "The Sun Singer" was published in 2004. My thriller/comedy "Jock Stewart and the Missing Sea of Fire" was released in August 2009.

As a freelancer, I focus on grant writing for nonprofit organizations, especially museums, along with collateral deliverables including press releases, brochure and newsletter copy, research for national register applications, and bylaws and mission statements

In addition to ghost-written web content and trade articles, my articles have appeared in the former Atlanta Journal-Constitution Sunday Magazine, the "Rosicrucian Digest," "Nostalgia Magazine," "The Chronicle of Higher Education," Great Lakes "Bulletin," "Smoking Poet," "Future Earth" and "Nonprofit World." I am a Contributing Writer at north Georgia's "Living Jackson Magazine" and appear in the 2008 "Forever Friends" literary anthology from Mandinam Press.

Malcolm Campbell’s Specialties:

grant writing, mission statements, nonprofit publicity, feature articles, book reviews, web content, press releases
