Steven J. Massicot’s Summary

Motivated, creative and versatile executive experienced in full range of real estate development, management and related disciplines, including Design, Entitlements, Construction, Purchasing, Project Management, Marketing, Property/Asset Management, Acquisitions, Dispositions, and Financing.

• Developed & managed a variety of property types from high profile, large budget, multi-phased, mixed use developments to single-purpose, modest budget developments
• Projects include High-Rise, Mixed-Use, Hotel / Resort, Athletic Facilities / Spas, Office, Industrial, Retail, Single-Family and Multi-Family Residential, Land Development, and Renovations/Adaptive Reuse
• Oversee projects from ground up, accepting full accountability for direction, strategic & financial management.
• Able to step in during any point of the development/asset management process, quickly recognize issues & ensure deadlines, milestones and budgets are met; at the same time, recognizing the value of superior design
• Assembled multi-discipline teams & worked with persons/groups representing diverse interests, directing resources required to execute appropriate business plan.
• Managed joint ventures, investor relations, board approvals - made numerous presentations before corporate boards, public councils, special interest groups
• Negotiated loan workouts, retaining management/leasing agreements; uniquely qualified to provide expertise for distressed real estate investments that require oversight or a more active role in the real estate
• Navigated through tedious entitlement/permitting processes to achieve significant regulatory concessions
