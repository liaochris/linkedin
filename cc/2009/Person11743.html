Nate Ludens’s Summary

Nate Ludens has been growing in the commercial creative industry professionally for 11 years, with the last eight years in Las Vegas, Nevada.

As the founder of Black Diamond Digital Imaging, a unique hybrid creative company in Las Vegas, Nevada, Nate has been specializing in commercial photography and graphic design since April of 2005.

Nate's goals are to continue to contribute to the Las Vegas Creative Community and to teach and learn from other creatives. His memberships have included the AIGA (The Professional Organization for Design), as well as the NAPP (Photoshop Professionals) and the WPPI (Wedding and Portrait Photographers).

Non-profit organizations have been a significant part of Nate's work. In recent years, his design and photographic contributions have benefitted the JDRF (Juvenile Diabetes Research), Lions HealthFirst Foundation, Big Brothers/Big Sisters, as well as firefighters and educational fundraisers, such as the Friends of Tanaka Elementary School in Las Vegas.

In August of 2009, Nate will be teaching Adobe Lightroom 2.0 Seminars at the University of Nevada-Las Vegas.

Nate Ludens’s Specialties:

Nate is a reliable results-guy who is willing to get his hands dirty. He's down to earth and has a wide range of market experience.
