Robin Sax’s Summary

Robin Sax is a Deputy District Attorney for the County of Los Angeles assigned to the Sex Crimes unit, specializing in Child Sexual Assault. She represents the L.A County District Attorney’s office on a multi-disciplinary team at UCLA’s Rape Treatment Center/ Stuart House where she conducts forensic interviews of children victims of sexual assault and prosecutes the perpetrators of these crimes. Prosecutor Sax is an instructor for the Los Angeles Police Department, Los Angeles County Sheriff’s Department, California District Attorney’s Association and a lecturer in UCLA’s Paralegal Program. She is a sought after speaker on child sexual assault and internet safety. She has received many community awards for her work to protect children of sexual abuse and victims of domestic abuse.

Robin authored "The Complete Idiot’s Guide to the Criminal Justice” (May 2009 by Penguin USA), "It Happens Everyday: Inside the Life of a Sex Crimes DA" (Prometheus, April 2009), "Everything Parents Need to Know About Molesters: A Sex Crimes DA Answer Parents 100- Most-Asked Questions" (Prometheus, February 2009) and "Everything I learned About Parenting I Learned In Court" to be published by AMACOM in 2010.
