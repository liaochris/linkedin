Jason Chan’s Summary

Jason is currently a Visual Designer at eBay, designing the next generation e-commerce website, creating a cohesive, streamlined product search experience, driven by an intuitive user interface that's supported by an evolving holistic visual-design system.

Prior to eBay, Jason was an award winning interactive designer at Responsys, a bay-area ad agency that focuses on email-marketing. He has succesfully designed and launched email campaigns for clients like Virgin-America, LEGO, GE, E-LOAN, PETCO, and ZIRH.

Jason is also a freelance interactive designer. Recent works include developing marketing emails and landing pages for Stitcher.com and also re-designing an application for Technesis. Local Non-profit Agencies like PurpleSilk.com and the Hong Kong Consumer Council were also past clients.
