Naomi Simson’s Summary

Naomi has a 20 year background in corporate marketing with companies such as IBM, Ansett and Apple Computer, but she started her own show more than 10 year ago – Naomi is particularly passionate about pleasure. Given that the average life has only 27,610 days – she wonders how many are truly amazing. Naomi is the 2008 winner of the National Telstra Business Womens Award - Innovation.

RedBalloon is changing gifting in Australia & New Zealand forever, so that people get given gifts that make them feel great. "We just want people to have a good time. Simply we package pleasure."

The RedBalloon (amazing gifts for amazing people) was created as a hobby in 2001, yet has been recognised on 2003 - 2008 in the BRW Fast 100 lists RedBalloon was named in the top 10 Best Places to work in Australia in May 2009.

RedBalloon now lists more than 2500 amazing activities across Australia and New Zealand – ranging from learning how to fly a helicopter to belly dancing lessons.

Naomi Simson’s Specialties:

Naomi is a marketer by training, but now specializes in the area of Reward and Recognition programs.

She is a regular speaker on leadership and has written a book 'I want what she's having'.
