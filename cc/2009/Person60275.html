Louis Deering’s Summary

As a senior affiliate manager at Income Access, I oversee strategic planning and day-to-day affiliate operations of large igaming brands, including Centrebet, Devil Fish Gaming, Victor Chandler, Ladbrokes, Bingo.com and other top Income Access clients. This includes managing program positioning with respect to commissions, promotions, marketing materials and other USPs, negotiating high-level deals with leading affiliates and developing communications strategies to grow the brand within the affiliate industry. This also includes taking responsibility for long-term growth and profitability of all of our clients' affiliate programs.

Long-term success for Income Access has always been closely tied to our relationships within the industry. As a senior affiliate manager, I maintain strong relationships with leading affiliates, operators, software suppliers, bloggers, forum moderators and other key influencers across all verticals of the igaming industry. In addition, I take part in the on-going industry dialogues by blogging, participating in forums and attending industry conferences.

Louis Deering’s Specialties:

I make it a priority to stay up-to-date with the latest industry trends and technologies. I'm well-versed in affiliate marketing strategies including SEO, Paid Search, Email Marketing, Geo-targeting, Co-Registration, Social Networking and blogging, as well as RSS, iFrames and other dynamic content. I also continually study the latest marketing and retention strategies from an operator's perspective, including CRM, Branding and Landing Page Conversion Optimization.
