Ian D. Nock’s Summary

As an independent consultant, I offer a wide range of consulting and delivery services. I focus primarily on the Broadband and TV space, particularly with regard to consumer device development and integration, however I also offer a second string in small, medium and large IT systems for SMEs and similar sized organisations.

I also take an active interest in technology, blogging both from my company blog and also through a special interest site focused on mobile tech for the modern roaming worker. I have a deep interest in all Internet services and technologies on the bleeding edge, so you can find me out there finding new stuff all the time.
