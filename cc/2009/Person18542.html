Peter Christo’s Summary

Peter holds a Masters Degree in Entrepreneurship and Innovation from Swinburne University of Technology, along with a Bachelor of Economics & Marketing. He has been a serial entrepreneur since his 20’s and started over 6 businesses including Internet Resource Centre in South Australia (also working in San Francisco USA during the dot com boom), Christo Consulting (IT Business in 1999) X2O Pty Ltd (IT Contracting) and now manages ‘Christo Partners’ a Startup Business Advisory.

Peter is also founder of a number of current start-ups including Podmatcher.com, and Melbournecoffeereview.com. Peter is a public speaker on Entrepreneurship, he has presented to eminent organisations including APEC on online business and entrepreneurship. Peter also contributes his time as an adjunct lecturer at RMIT’s Business School teaching Entrepreneurship to undergraduate students. Peter is a father of two young children, and has travelled extensively through Asia both on business and as a tourist/traveller.
