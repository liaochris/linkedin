Joris Evers’s Summary

Joris is a public relations professional at McAfee, joining the security pure play in early 2007 after a decade as a technology journalist, bringing industry expertise, a strong network and a reporter mindset to the McAfee PR team.

Joris Evers’s Specialties:

competitive public relations, social media, media relations, story creation, PR counseling, editing, writing, PR strategist, blogging, media training, messaging, executive communications

Joris Evers’s Experience

  • Director, Worldwide Public Relations

    McAfee Inc.

    (Public Company; MFE; Computer & Network Security industry)

    May 2007 — Present (2 years 7 months)

    At McAfee, Joris is responsible for:

    - Executive communications: Strengthening the communications platform and increasing visibility for McAfee's senior executive team, including speaking engagements and media relations globally and McAfee's corporate blog.

    - McAfee Labs communications: Using the unique content and expertise of McAfee's researchers worldwide to create compelling media pitches, events, publications and speaking slots at high profile events.

    - Corporate communications: Supporting incoming media inquiries as well as communications tactics around topics including alliances, acquisitions and executive hires. Also managing internal and external publications and supporting mergers and acquisitions.

    - Public Sector communications: Managing a specialized agency for local, state and federal government public relations and public affairs. Driving communications for McAfee's growing public sector business by doing focused outreach to specialized media and influencers.

    - Competitive communications: Getting McAfee's position into news generated by rivals.

  • Sr. Writer

    CNET News.com

    (Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; CNET; Online Media industry)

    May 2005 — May 2007 (2 years 1 month)

Provide guidance to editors, coach writers and collaborate with different individuals and departments on projects. Establish security industry expertise and a network of contacts, including media and analysts. Provide feedback to management on achievement of goals and drive opportunities.

    - Threats sections on News.com and ZDNET.com score very high, driving traffic to both sites.
    - Successfully launched a weekly podcast in collaboration with CNET.com.
    - Finalist for 2006 Online News Association award.
    - Frequent commentator for local, national and international media on technology and security.
