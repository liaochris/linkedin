B. Drew Moynihan’s Summary

Drew Moynihan has a rich and diversified background in business development, strategic planning, marketing, and internet development. He is known as a proactive, solutions-driven, innovative business technology leader, with an eye toward innovation, simplicity, and eco-responsibility. Drew co-founded doCuda inc. in 2008, and as President and COO leads the company’s daily operations, while working with doCuda’s partners to extend consumer reach while achieving shareholder value.

With more than 18 years experience in internet, print, and broadcast industries, Drew‘s vision is focused on instilling the operational philosophy, "client-focused, service-oriented, and team-based". Passionate about technology and its possibilities, he supports open development models that stimulate communication, creativity and innovation across all divisions within an enterprise. As a visionary professional Drew has consistently demonstrated the ability to formulate and implement business development, process improvements, technology deployments and technical training as solutions to business requirements to achieve performance and productivity, while controlling costs.

Drew’s experience spans the range of industries, from start up to fortune 200, with expertise in operations, sales, information technology, and engineering management in SaaS, Sales Engineering, Retail Operations, Data Warehousing, and Print, Internet, and Broadcast Media.

Drew’s educational background is in electrical engineering from A&M University and Business Technology from Texas State University, and maintains ties to Texas State to this day.
