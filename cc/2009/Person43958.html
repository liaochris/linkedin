Jason Felix’s Summary

My goal is to visually create the most compelling and unique visual designs for the entertainment industry.

With 15+ years experience in the entertainment industry, I've utilized a diverse skill set to create everything from concept art, illustrations, 3d animation, 3d models, cover art, storyboards, animatics, and cinematic direction.

Knowing each facet of how to produce a final 3d model has proven invaluable when designing from the ground up. My artistic emphasis is to create conceptual artwork and design original IP's.

I've been fortunate to worked on high profile video games such as StarCraft, Prince of Persia, and Hellgate.

Jason Felix’s Specialties:

Concept art of characters & creatures design, illustrations, cover artist, and designer of original IP's.
