James Boone Dryden’s Summary

I have a substantial knowledge of the editing & publishing world. As well as working in the small press industry for the past four years, I currently hold a position as the managing editor of a regional, multicultural magazine.

My current part-time job is running The JB Dryden Company. We are a small business focusing on professional editing for fiction and non-fiction.
