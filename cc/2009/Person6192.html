Andy Vogel’s Summary

Local Citizen and Social Media Revenue Pioneer.

Andy Vogel has dedicated his career to developing the skills, experience and results required to be a superior strategist, sales leader, and marketing tactician for existing and new businesses seeking to grow revenue online.
