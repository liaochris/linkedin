Kristen Beireis’s Summary

President of Coaches' Marketing Source, where Kristen and her team help elite life changers change more lives with no-sweat marketing. Since 2006, Kristen has been helping coaches and life changers with their marketing. She has assembled a team that is capable of taking projects, running with them, and making executive decisions that will make the marketing more effective in the overall marketing plan. Kristen's coach training through CTI comes in handy when the marketing list gets overwhelming and her clients need help prioritizing and breaking it out into small pieces. Kristen is the holder of all marketing plans, so her clients don't have to waste valuable brain space on remembering the details.

Kristen has always followed her dream careers. She's even decided to leave dreams in pursuit of a happier lifestyle. As a Life Coach, Kristen helps home-based business owners who no longer enjoy the work they've chosen to do. She believes that everyone should love their work, or it's not worth doing. So, she challenges her clients to rekindle (or redesign) their passion and move into a business they love. Creating small steps and honoring the client's passion in their work, an AMAZING journey begins.

Kristen Beireis’s Specialties:

Online business systems creation and implementation. Marketing plan implementation. Running businesses from the heart, with integrity, professionalism and individuality. Many years of following my passion and making a career of it.
