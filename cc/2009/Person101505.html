Bui Tyril’s Summary

Editor, writer, concept developer with 20+ years of professional experience in communication, PR, marketing, advertising, publishing and journalism. Developed and produced numerous publications, presentations, ads, articles, brochures, special reports, websites, newsletters and more.

Publisher and editor-in-chief of Faroe Business Report, the international review of Faroe Islands industry and trade.

Co-authored and co-edited the book "Images of Fishermen: The North Atlantic" by Maria Olsen et al (GlobalOne Press, 2006).

Goals and aims: build trust and credibility, boost sales, results for clients, promote effectively, give users the best experience, increase understanding of issues involved, through innovation, research, hard work and creativity.
