Richard Stephan’s Summary

With a BA in Production Engineering and a Career in the Packing Industry for over 22 years, Richard was CEO of a Leading Firm in charge of over 80 employees. With hands on experience in all areas - particularly in Computing and IT, Richard is now dedicated to bringing his IT skills to his profession as a leading Sales Executive with TonyDavidsHomes.com LLC.

Providing cutting edge innovation with his YouTube Videos to Lead-Generating systems on the Internet, Richard has helped many buyers both locally and Overseas to find their dream home - all online before even visiting the area in person.

With a warm and friendly disposition, Richard is highly respected in the local Community as a long term resident and knowledgeable in all areas of Real Estate from the Seaside Coastlines of beautiful Florida to his hometown Celebration. Utilizing the power of the Internet that has become most Buyers No. 1 source for information about REAL ESTATE and as a certified e-PRO® Professional, Richard positions his sellers´ homes on more than 400 different websites using pictures, virtual tours and videos to promote the homes. Also known locally for his love of hosting Open Houses to showcase his homes, Richard offers his services as both a Buyer´s and Seller´s Agent.
