Guy Tourigny’s Summary

Guy is a Learning Technology SME in IBM's Human Capital Management practice. Guy has extensive experience leading teams in the Human Capital Management practice area, focusing his work primarily with large international clients in regulated industries. His engagement experience includes projects involving a variety of learning solutions including technologies such as Learning Management Systems (LMS), Learning Content Management Systems (LCMS), Virtual Classroom Systems (VCS), and Web-based Training (WBT) Content Design, Development, and Integration.

Internally at IBM, Guy is the designated Learning Technology SME for the IBM Corporation. In this role, Guy provides subject matter expertise in strategies for learning technologies best practices and their practical application. In other noteworthy client engagements Guy has designed the strategic learning solutions to support learning management for compliance requirements. Many of these projects involved skills in research, benchmarking, focus group facilitation, and executive-level presentation.

Guy is also an active participant with standard organizations such as: AICC, SCORM, IEEE, and LETSI.

Guy Tourigny’s Specialties:

eLearning standards (AICC, SCORM, IEEE) on both delivery systems (LMS/LCMS/CDS/CMS/VCS) and content design, development, conversion, and integration. I have recent experience with Saba, SumTotal, SAP LS/LSO, Peoplsoft ELM, Centra, and Interwise.
