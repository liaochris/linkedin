Jean Ann Van Krevelen’s Summary

Jean Ann combines her years as a C-level executive with a deep understanding of online marketing to create solid business-building strategies for our clients. In addition to utilizing a vast array of social media tools, she has a depth of knowledge about each application and its potential role in customer identification and brand building.

Jean Ann has a Master’s in Social Work and uses her 17 years of study of human behavior to help companies identify target markets and communication strategies. She has a strong history of systems thinking and can craft complex, over arching social media strategies that bring together multiple variables for optimal execution.

Recently, Jean Ann has been named “2008 Top 50 Tweeple to Follow” on Twitter. She was also honored to be in the ranks of one of “50 of the Most Powerful and Influential Women in Social Media” and “30 Entrepreneurs to Follow on Twitter”.
