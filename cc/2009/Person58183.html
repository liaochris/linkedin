Chris Denny, MBA’s Summary

Lead Optimize designs and manages fully-integrated multi-part marketing systems for small and medium businesses.

Affordable Professional Marketing: For about the cost of a part-time employee you can have a professional marketing department with a complete arsenal of marketing possibilities such as:

- Press Releases
- Search Engine Optimization
- E-newsletter Management
- Materials & Ad Design
- Online Content Generation
- Sales Letter Writing

Value: Imagine getting your monthly marketing report detailing your marketing activities, results, and expectations - and only have to deal with ONE person for all of your marketing activities.

No contracts. No employees. All your marketing will be fully managed and integrated by your Lead Optimize marketing manager.
