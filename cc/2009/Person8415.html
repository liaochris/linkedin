Asmo Halinen’s Summary

I have a versatile career in the Finnish internet industry since 1995. Among many other things I've been leading the creation of numerous internet content/media businesses in the country and abroad. In total I've been involved (either as a designer, project manager, producer or CEO) in building hundreds of different web sites / web campaigns.

I have a strong entrepreneur background through co-founding Apaja in 2001 and later its gaming spinoff company Apaja Online Entertainment.

Asmo Halinen’s Specialties:

I possess quite a wide understanding of internet, its technologies and design, as well as of the creation of online community services with business acumen. In addition, my core competencies consist of strong HR skills due to the various manager positions now and in the past.
