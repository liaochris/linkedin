Wei Wei Jeang’s Summary

Registered patent attorney with 20+ years of patent law experience in procurement of patents, rendering patent opinions (patentability, invalidity, noninfringement, & clearance), IP due diligence, patent reexaminations, and litigation support.

Familiar technical areas include telecommunications, computer hardware and software, computer networking, and semiconductor processing.

Fluent in Mandarin Chinese and can read and write Chinese.

Wei Wei Jeang’s Specialties:

Patent prosecution and litigation support in telecommunications, computer hardware and software, computer networking, and semiconductor processing technologies.
