Andreas Fankhauser’s Summary

I am a partner at gbf Gerspacher Bühlmann Fankhauser, a Swiss boutique law firm with a special focus on international business law.

My practice is devoted to providing advice and to the resolution of disputes concerning
- aviation and aerospace
- competition and trade
- corporate and commercial matters
- business crime.

Prior to founding my own law firm, I was an associate at a leading business law firm in Zurich, as well as a legal secretary and a judge in the District Court of Zurich.

I have been involved in a number of significant cases, including the DHL/Bashkirian Airlines' mid-air collision in July 2002.

I was admitted to the bar in 2000 and speak German, English and French.

Andreas Fankhauser’s Specialties:

A particular focus in my practice are European-Chinese business relations.
