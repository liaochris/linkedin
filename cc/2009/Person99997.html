Thomas McCarey’s Summary

Thomas McCarey is experienced in all facets of residential real estate - representation, negotiation, marketing, sales and analysis. But the four factors that set Tom apart from other Chicago area real estate professionals (according to his clients) is his ability to listen, his deep knowledge of the market, his tireless work ethic and his capacity to effectively structure workable deals. Tom assists buyers and sellers in the metropolitan market and nearby north and west suburbs with residential real estate transactions. Working on a referral basis, Tom ensures the absolute satisfaction of his clients and ranks among the highest echelon of Chicago realtors. That he succeeds in his stated mission is reflected by the glowing praise he receives from his buying and selling clients, their ongoing referrals of family and friends, and his award-winning recognition by his brokerage and the Chicago Association of Realtors. Enabling Tom's success is his drive to see to that his clients are more than satisfied and his integration of a multi-pronged technological marketing approach.

Thomas McCarey’s Specialties:

Contract negotiation from both buyer and seller platforms, aggressive & professional electronic, online, and hard copy marketing program, intuitive and extensive market awareness, aggressive search engine optimization (for fullest expression of listings to online consumers, and a deep understanding of the current market.
