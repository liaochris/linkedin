Roland Palm’s Summary

Roland Palm is the President of Roland Palm Business Group, Inc. an international management, information technology, web design & IT outsourcing consulting firm located in Panama City.

Since the year 1989 Roland has worked as a technical authority in the database, internet, intranet, networking and e-commerce arena and as a general data processing expert covering a wide range of server technologies and computing applications on Fortune 500 companies, US Government Agencies and the US Air Force @ Howard Air Force Base.

Roland has deployed advanced information technology projects resolving critical issues on existing systems which required innovative solutions in many aspects of the project. He has extensive experience in the development of government initiatives related to strategic investment, collaboration, civil engagement (e-democracy), workforce, privacy, security, interoperability, public records management and libraries, permanent availability and preservation, public access to resources, reduction of the digital divide, distance education, competition, infrastructure, e-commerce development, reduction of redundant and overlapping government agency activities, governance and management of change, policy making, international trade process streamlining and integrated human resources.

Roland is providing consulting services worldwide (English, Spanish). Please feel free to contact him if you have a good development/offshore project or a new idea on hand.

Services: Application Development, Call Center Operations, Customer Relationship Management, Data Center Operations, Data Migration Services, Database Administration, Enterprise Project Management, IT Asset Management, IT Infrastructure, IT Outsourcing, Web Design, Web Development, Web Hosting, e-Commerce, IT Infrastructure.
