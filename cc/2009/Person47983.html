Jamie Kisner’s Summary

I am an entrepreneur, writer, seminar host, adjunct instructor, and community volunteer. I started my own business as a mobile notary signing agent serving the South Florida area in 2007. JMK Notary & Services includes specializations in medical signings, mortgage signings, auto signings, and weddings to name a few. As a certified and background screened member of National Notary Association, I am listed as an expertly trained professional. Additionally, I had the pleasure of working with lenders such as Chase, Bank of America, and Fidelity. The mission I fulfill everyday is to prevent fraud in business transactions.

My goals are to remain competitive in the legal industry, and to expand services consistently. In 2009, the company expanded into commercial inspections for organizations such as LexisNexis; TransUnion; Equifax and more. JMK Notary & Services started a new branch in SEO services, which provides customized packages for clients e-Marketing needs. In addition, I hold a Masters in Business Administration, which I utilize as an instructor with Miami-Dade College.
