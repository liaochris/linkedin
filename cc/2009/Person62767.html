Marie Chandler’s Summary

I have over 20 years as a high level Personal Assistant in large firms such as Deloitte Accountants and Minter Ellison Lawyers in Australia. I have vast experience with the Microsoft suite of products and Adobe Photoshop, with intermediate level of knowledge in Dreamweaver Web Design (ongoing). I am currently learning Adobe Flash, Illustrator and InDesign to increase my service offerings.

With my experience and understanding of how to run an efficient office it is my goal to educate business owners as to the advantage of using a Virtual Assistant. Work can be allocated to me via the internet and business owners can then get out into the marketplace and sell their expertise - not sit in the office doing admin work. We can be partners in making your business more productive and profitable.
