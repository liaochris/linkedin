Oleg Podsechin’s Summary

I run Ionsquare, which is an IT consultancy specializing in helping start-ups and new ventures bridge the £50k-£500k funding gap by providing advice, technology services, strategic partnerships and sales leads.

We are based in Cambridge and I am frequently in London. So, if you're around and have an idea you want to discuss, please drop me a line and let's meet.

I'm very interested in new business ideas that use existing technology in innovative ways and taking these products and services to market. I have been or am working with companies in the following areas:

Mobile media, mobile gaming, mobile search, mobile social networks, mobile content delivery, mobile security, information retrieval and linguistic analysis, web analytics, day trading, financial data management, electronic ticketing and access control, telemetry and wireless sensor networks.

Oleg Podsechin’s Specialties:

Software design, software development, project management, outsourcing, business case development, networking.
