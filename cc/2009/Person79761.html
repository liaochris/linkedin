Pieter-Paul Walraven’s Summary

I graduated on the internationalization of the Chinese Internet industry. For my thesis research I visited numerous Chinese Internet companies and performed over 30 interviews with, among others, analysts, CEO’s, government officials, bloggers, and VCs. Previously I have studied in Hong Kong and traveled extensively through the Asia-Pacific region with a particular focus on Mainland China.
Some articles on my research:
http://www.cibmagazine.com.cn/Features/Infotech.asp?id=985&from_imitation_to_innovation.html

http://orgnets.net/urban_china/walraven


When I am not at work I write for The Next Web a blog that covers start-up news from all over the world (not just the Valley), exciting new technologies and inspiring entrepreneurs (www.TheNExtWeb.com). I am also a contributor at MOBINODE an award winning blog that covers the Internet market news in Asia especially in China (www.MOBINODE.com). Lastly I am one of the authors of CSN Blog a Dutch blog about everything related to corporate social networking (www.CSNBlog.nl).
