Brian Duchek’s Summary

Passionately devotes best practice efforts to creating positive user-focused software experiences at all levels of the product development process from idea conception and architecture design to testing and deployment.

Over seven years of interactive design and development experience spent crafting and iterating web applications and e-commerce software systems. Advanced visual design skills practiced with strong record of usable interfaces, appreciated standards advocacy, and studied accessibility features. Expert web technology experience in (x)HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Ajax, plus strong XML and Java skills in N-tier architecture environments.
