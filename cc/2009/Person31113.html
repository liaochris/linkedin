Eric Hansen’s Summary

I am currently working as the Business Development Manager for Combat Identification (CID) Systems for BAE Systems in Greenlawn, NY. My primary interest is in the area of millimeter wave technology, but I am also interested in other cooperative, non-cooperative and net-centric opportunities in the CID realm.

I am a retired Marine Officer with a wide array of leadership experiences and management roles in large organizations. I have had a broad variety of operational experiences as well as acquistion experience, operational testing and requirements experience. Educated in Industrial and Systems Engineering.

I am interested in sustainable living and solar power as a personal endeavor and am willing and available to answer any questions you may have or partner with interested parties in bringing solar energy into your life or work.
