Massimo Canducci’s Summary

Technology & Innovation Lab Director in Industry and Services Business Unit at Engineering Ingegneria Informatica, Italy.

Currently he leads a team working on all ICT innovation related topics providing excellence services to industry and services market.

Accessibiity Competence Centre Director in Research & Innovation Division of Engineering Ingegneria Informatica, Italy.

He starts working in IT field in 1990, he has held management positions at Engineering Ingegneria Informatica in critical project management, professional open source, IT infrastructure design for large companies and public administration.
He works from years in Human-Computer Interaction, Ergonomics and Accessibility of IT systems and in these fields he attends as keynote speaker in a lot of international meeting and conferences.

He works and take part in a large number of international working groups:

- CNIPA working group that produced technical requirements for Accessibility in Public Administration Law in Italy;
- Ergonomics Commission in UNI (Italian Standard Organization);
- ISO Working Group ISO/TC 159/SC4/WG 5 "Software Ergonomics and Human-Computer Dialogues"
- ISO Working Group ISO-IEC/JTC1/SC35 "Information Technology / User Interface"
- European Commission Working Group (European Commitee for Standardization) CEN/BT/WG185 “eAccessibility”

He published many books and technical articles on most important Italian ICT magazines, also he has many professional certifications.

He worked in several countries: Italy, Belgium, Germany, Canada, United States, Spain, Japan.

Also he is a trainer at Engineering ICT School on Accessibiility and Enterprise 2.0 fields.

Massimo Canducci’s Specialties:

Enterprise 2.0, Ergonomics, Usability, Accessibility, Open Source, Software Engineering, Project Management, Architectural solutions definition
