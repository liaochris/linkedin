Kristie McNealy MD’s Summary

Mom, MD, Writer and New Media Guru with an interest in freelance writing, medical marketing and education, as well as improving family-centered care for preemies in the neonatal intensive care unit setting (NICU). Particular interests include providing support networks and educational resources for parents of premature infants and advocating for prematurity awareness, as well as training health care providers about life in the NICU from the perspective of the family. Currently working as President and Founder of NICU 101, as well as writing and editing a Women's and Children's Health Blog at KristieMcNealy.com.

I welcome connections from like minded professionals!
