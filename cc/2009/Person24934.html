Dawood Marion’s Summary

After graduating high school I studied drawing and watercolor painting at Santa Monica College. Between my classmates and the instructor I was thoroughly challenged to produce some of my best work. In '98 I started freelancing at Rap pages Magazine. I joined on there as an Illustrator, visualizing the main focus of various articles. This was a valuble opportunity for me as I learned about the pressure of meeting short deadlines. In 2005 I did CD Cover art for The Art Of Who Did (Adams Adair) , an L.A. based music group. I went on to do 3 more of the groups CD covers. Later '05 I got a job with MackVision Entertainment, working on designs and illustrations for an independent movie. I did concept design, storyboards and graphic design for a graphic novel to pitch the movie. I Graduated From Westwood College with a degree in Graphic Design June 2006. soon after I landed a job as a Concept Illustrator at Magnadyne Automotive Electronics . In the same month I began studying Production Design at Gnomon School Of Visual Effects.
My goal is to open a school of design Los Angeles that will offer high end art training to under privileged youth.

Dawood Marion’s Specialties:

Character design, Illustration, sculpting, drawing and painting (digital and traditional).
