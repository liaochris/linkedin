Chad Rodvold’s Summary

Professionally trained by the founding father of landscape illumination, nicknamed Mr. Moonlight in Time Magazine. Subsequently became the National Design Consultant for his firm. Designed and maintained well over a thousand installations throughout the country.

Founding Partner of Signature Illumination Designs (SI Designs), a nationwide design-consult-build landscape illumination firm. Principal Landscape Illumination Designer for SI Designs.

Work with architects, landscape architects, interior designers, builders, and developers on creating the relationship with their projects nighttime environments.

Current projects in FL, CO, TX, and the Caribbean

Chad Rodvold’s Specialties:

Our primary work involves fine estates, hotels and resorts, country clubs, and luxury real estate developments.
