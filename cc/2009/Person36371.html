Gina Cuclis’s Summary

I work with businesses and organizations to help them reach their goals by communicating the right messages to the right people. My more than two decades of communications experience includes public relations, marketing, radio and TV news, community organizing and political action. Find testimonials and descriptions of results I achieved for clients, on my website. Also sign up on www.cuclispr.com to receive a free PR tip each month. My blog, North Bay New Media http://blog.cuclispr.com, discusses the changing media landscape in northern California and how organizations are adapting. Follow me on Twitter @ginacuclis.

Gina Cuclis’s Specialties:

Professional services, health, environment, nonprofits, local government agencies, special districts, political action, campaign support.
