Bastien Labelle’s Summary

I'm a 22 yo French guy who has just passed his Master 2 in information systems at Toulouse 1 University. I'm currently living in Toulouse Area, in South of France, but my dream is to work in SF Bay Area or in NYC.

I have skills in XHTML, XML, CSS, Javascript. I program in PHP (I tend to think I'm a PHP rockstar sometimes), but I'm also working with Python and Django, sometimes on top of Google App Engine. I know about some Java servlets & JSP, and I know the basics of C, C++, C#, VB.NET. I'm good at data design, and I know how to use PostgreSQL, MySQL, SQLite, Oracle and MS Access, and I'm currently learning about CouchDB.

I have 1+ year of professional experience, but as a passionate person, I can objectively say that I have kind of more than 1+ year of experience: I play with PHP since I'm 15, and my curiosity has driven me to give a try to Python & Django. By the way, passion is my main motivation. Also, I've been giving classes about Web 2.0 & social media at Toulouse Business School, and I've done some Influencer Marketing at Communit-y.

I'm a Mac addict, and I love programming on Mac OS X, on Coda, Espresso or TextMate. OS X is a great professional environment, and I'd love to work on a Mac everyday!
