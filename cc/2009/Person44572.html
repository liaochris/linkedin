Jay Parekh’s Summary

Jay has over twenty years of executive, strategy, operations and software development experience in the IT industry.

Most recently as Chief Strategy Officer at Oracle and BMC, his responsibilities included corporate strategy, solution architecture, business planning, corporate development (M&A) and strategic marketing. Prior to that he served as Venture Partner, Strategy Consultant and Board Member at multiple companies, and as Founder & CEO of highly successful Silicon Valley start-ups. Before that he held senior management positions at IBM, McDonnell Douglas, British Telecom and Nortel, where he managed global software development, IT operations and services organizations.

He holds Masters in Computer Science from University of Michigan.
