Søren Rose Kjær’s Summary

Søren Rose Kjær is a designer & serial entrepreneur . In 1994 Søren founded In2media (www.in2media.com), today one of Denmark’s leading new media agencies and in 2000 Castit (www.castit.dk), an online casting agency providing models for more than 25% of Danish commercials.
In 2004 Søren Rose Kjær Co-Founded Trunk Archive (www.trunkarchive.com) which is based in New York.
Today Søren Rose Kjær is still active as Board member in In2media, Castit & Trunk Archive and working full time as a designer, his debute is a Desk for Holmris Hansen (www.milk.dk)

Søren was also the driving force behind the project Dunny Show Cph (www.dunnyshowcph.dk); an exhibition in support of the UN’s 2015 Millennium Developmental Goals. Søren Rose Kjær & Nicolai Moltke-Leth completed the Paris Dakar Rally in 2002 and the East African Safari Rally in 2005 (www.rallywithpurpose.com).

Søren Rose Kjær’s Experience

  • Designer

    Søren Rose Studio

    (Design industry)

    September 2008 — Present (11 months)

    In a world where thousands of new products are born everyday, you really have to be able to offer something special. For the last 15 years, I have followed the design scene closely. Still, at the end of the day, my primary measurement has always been the same. It’s a simple question: Do I really want this?
    I designed MILK for myself, not for anyone else. For me, it’s always personal. I needed a desk that was simple but never boring. Smart but not overly sophisticated. I wanted a desk worth falling for, so I created MILK. In the future, I’ll continue to use my intuition and passion to create more great designs that I hope you will enjoy as much as I do.
    My website is schedule to launch around 15th of November with some of my new designs. Please contact me if you have any inquiries.
