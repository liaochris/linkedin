Glen Montgomery’s Summary

My experience in school as well as at Curtis is dual sided. I work both behind the camera in photography and behind the computer screen in editorial. This allows me a very different perspective because I have more power to carry a message through the whole storytelling process. While I am enticed by both of these responsibilities, I find my heart drawn more towards the post production side of media creation. I love putting images together and designing motion graphics. In this last year I have greatly embraced social media and this new web age to reach a wider audience. So many more tools are available to help connect and showcase talents, and I am driven to incorporate more of these in my workflow.
