Bob Dodge’s Summary

My experience, passion and communication style serve my clients well in my practice of assisting organizations in managing Change. Regardless of the scope and type of the initiatives; (mergers, process changes, software projects, six sigma changes, or reorganizations) my clients have appreciated my abilities to help them address resistance to change.

Bob Dodge’s Specialties:

My approach is to focus on the client:
• Recent experience with managing changes
• How changes are being managed today
• What is working well and what could be done better
• The potential business impact of their changes
• The clients’ capacity and capability for changes to their current approach
• Adding value to what they already do
• Current mission-critical changes and opportunities to ensure they are successful
