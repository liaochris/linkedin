Sebastian Muriel’s Summary

Muriel holds a full-time MBA General Management degree from IAE Argentina (Top-One Argentina, Top-Five LatinAmerica) with specialisation in Entrepreneurship at London Business School UK (Top-One Europe, Top-Five Worldwide).
He has more than five years consulting experience working in a leading software company in Argentina providing services to several hi-tech companies in Silicon Valley, Florida, Puerto Rico, Spain and LatinAmerica.
Muriel also dedicated himself on developing interpersonal skills, team motivation and goals achievement skills pursuing an Executive Coach degree from a one-year course and four months practices on Ontological Coaching. He has been ever since helping political and professional leaders to overcome recurrent obstacles an achieve long-awaited remarkable results.

He graduated in Electronics Engineering from the most prestigious Engineering University in Argentina (ITBA) in 1997.
Muriel is fluent in english and spanish and is able to communicate in basic german.
He has been traveling extensively to broaden his mindset to the different cultures and business opportunities: Spain, Germany, Switzerland, Denmark, Monaco, France, UK, Belgium, Italy, Greece, Czech Republic, Netherland, Puerto Rico, US, Nicaragua.

During his years of consulting he has developed analytical skills to understand patterns and execute consequent effective designs and efficient implementations.
During his MBA he has built on these skills to a business perspective reading in courses on business strategy, competition analysis and sustainable advantage development.
Working for start-ups in Silicon Valley, he has exercised and mastered creativity and positive entrepreneurial-tenancy to take over development constraints and unexpected implementation issues with limited resources, structure and information. He has also developed communication skills to instruct and explain to clients about new technologies being implemented in the core of their products.
