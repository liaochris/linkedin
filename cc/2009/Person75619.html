Olivier Amprimo’s Summary

I have a keen interest in building sustainable organizations in a knowledge economy.
This includes a focus on:
- How to successfully facilitate and nurture within an organisation a culture of continuous attention to innovation so that members are up-to-date and make better decisions at their level and help the organisation perform better and improve painlessly.
- How to adapt management's behaviour
- How to rejuvenate organisational processes
