Peter Groen’s Summary

Peter is an experienced manager, advisor and auditor with a background in information sciences complemented with a broad knowledge of and experience in the field of IT governance, Information Management, Operations, IT strategy, Information Security and IT auditing.

He has broadened his business knowledge by finishing an Executive MBA at the Rotterdam School of Management (RSM).

Based on this experience he is now acting as manager of outsourced operations in the banking industry.

His goal is to help organizations bridge and then close the gap between business and IT.

Peter Groen’s Specialties:

Peter's specialities are Operations, IT governance, Information Management, IT management, Business and IT Control and Auditing IT projects (Project Quality Assurance).
