Robert Fransgaard’s Summary

A london based digital art director and information designer who with a decade of experience has successfully developed and enhanced the online presence of his clients. See work examples on www.fransgaard.com

Robert Fransgaard’s Specialties:

Online strategy and user experience management. Digital art direction and conceptual delivery, interactive design, multi-channel digital marketing, Flash, web standards and fully accessibility compliant web design.

  • Senior Designer

  • Freelance Web Designer

    Freelance

    (Online Media industry)

    January 1999 — August 1999 (8 months)

    Dingelink.dk became the home for young Danish mobile phone users from spring 1999 to summer 2004 where it was taken offline.

    During the time it was live it became one of the top ten most visited sites in Denmark. And it was a great portfolio piece to get me start in London.
