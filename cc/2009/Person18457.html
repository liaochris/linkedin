Christine Choquel (née Lintz)’s Summary

After graduating from Ecole Centrale Paris (97), I started my carreer as a manager in charge of the Process and Methods department in a factory of the French metal company Vallourec.
Two years later, I left the industrial sector to work in the IT services industry and joined Pixelpark, first as a technical project manager and then as a project director. After 3 years at Pixelpark, I joined Edifixio where I was an eBusiness project director for more than 2 years.

Then I took a parental leave for 3 years during which I have been performing freelance missions for zSlide.

I am now working as an independant consutant. I perform project management missions in the fields of internet / web 2.0 / media / press which are sectors I know well after working several years in web agencies and IT service providers.
