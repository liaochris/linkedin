Bob Dickinson’s Summary

Serial entrepreneur with a proven track record. Executive management experience in engineering, sales, and marketing. Executive management experience in public and private companies. Significant background in architecture and design of complex security and communications technologies. Over 20 years programming experience (wide variety of platforms and languages). Inventor on US Patent 6,609,196 "E-mail firewall with stored key encryption/decryption".
