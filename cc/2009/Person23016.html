Dara Turransky’s Summary

Founder and Creative Director of 7 Lucky Dogs Creative, a division of Turransky, LLC.

At 7 Lucky Dogs, we assist independent pet businesses increase their bottom line through branding and marketing strategy. I am passionate about the pet industry and enjoy the challenge of assisting business owners market products that are beneficial to the dog, cat and horse communities.

Dara Turransky’s Specialties:

Branding, Brand Identities, Business Papers, Brochures, Direct Marketing, E-commerce Design, Packaging Design, Promotional Materials, Sales Kits, Trade Show Design, and Web Design.
