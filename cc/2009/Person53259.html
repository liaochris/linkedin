Karen Kehoe’s Summary

Enthusiasm, knowledge, energy and my 28 years of real estate are what I'm best known for. 24 Hrs. 7 days Access I'm known for . I believe you should always give more than you receive, and thus I created my logo "The Key To Your Real Estate Success". I've been described as having enthusiasm, great knowledge, and I'm a people-person who genuinely loves her career and those she works with. I believe buying or selling a home should be a fun experience, but of course there are times when pure business enters into it. Many of my past buyers and sellers have become life-long friends, and for that I am very fortunate.

I assist everyone, including those needing rentals... as I believe everyone has a right to share in my knowledge.

I have a team of people (mortgage lenders, attorneys) I work with who share my love and enthusiasm for real estate. They are also available 18 hours a day, 7 days a week. Yes it is dedication, but if you love what you are doing, it's not difficult to be accessible24/7.

I've listed and sold over 2,000 homes.
