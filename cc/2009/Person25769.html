Denise Pellow’s Summary

Speaker and author of articles and educational materials informing parents on Kids, Technology & the Internet. "5 Dangerous Trends Parents Concerning Kids, Technology & the Internet." Article publisher, "Teens, Tweens and Texting," "Teens, Trauma and YouTube," "Top 10 Internet Safety Tips," "Spying vs. Caring - Monitoring Your Kids Online."

Goals are to reach as many parents, teachers, facilitators and anyone who care about kids to inform, educate and bring awareness to the dangers our kids face with today's technology. Book your next speaking engagement today!

Denise Pellow’s Specialties:

Liaison between government, community, schools and parents. Instrumental in changing laws to protect kids online.
