Andrew Peek’s Summary

A few truths I subscribe to...

- Successful business leaders surround themselves with people smarter than they are, challenge the notion that you should never re-invent the wheel, and sacrifice to leave the world in better shape than when they arrived.

- Conversations will continue to happen. Markets will continue to be challenged. People will continue to change their minds - with or without a company's permission. I pride myself on helping remarkable companies take the necessary risks to turn their ideas into best practices.

- Experience is practically currency, and when you consider the bigger picture, is worth far more.
