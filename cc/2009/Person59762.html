Liz Micik’s Summary

Liz has more than 28 years experience successfully planning and executing strategic marketing plans and budgets in a variety of industries, company sizes and types. She's spent the last 10 years learning new ways and keeping up with the changing waves of what marketing works best online as well.

"I love the constant challenge of the Internet. I love the speed of change and the ways you can combine, test, refine and redefine a marketing campaign or a whole business online. It's addictive,"she says. "But I hate the fact that so many people confuse that flexibility with easiness. It's not free and it's not that easy."

She's currently working mostly with small and medium sized businesses who need her in-the-trenches experience and help keeping their websites, newsletters, and blogs updated with fresh and engaging content. She also reaches out from there to draw in the right audience using a customized mix of proactive marketing such as article marketing, social bookmarking and more.

Liz Micik’s Specialties:

Website content creation, design and development using CSS, XHTML, AJAX, online video/audio, some PHP/MySQL, etc., and most importantly, a solid command of everyday English
