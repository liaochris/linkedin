Maurizio Pensato’s Summary

PENSATO+PARTNERS * renewable energy consulting group provides ready-to-construct projects as well as turn key solutions for medium and big size photovoltaic plants (grid) as well as rooftop solar systems in Italy (Apulia, Basilicata, Marche, Abruzzo and Molise).

Since 2006, through shareholdings in Italian project development companies in Apulia, Italy PENSATO+PARTNERS * renewable energy consulting group has successfully developed 22 solar power plants (grid) with a total solar power output of over 21.956 kW.
