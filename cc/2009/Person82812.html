Reene Ferguson’s Summary

I believe that presentation is everything. That's why I love video so much. My goal has been to get people moving toward a better quality of life, so my popular television show, "Access Health & Fitness" has given me a creative platform to connect people to a healthier lifestyle. I have created some educational and inspiring segments over the past years that have changed lives. My ability to develop positive programming has transitioned my production skills into a full-service production company offering individuals and businesses a more powerful way to promote, entertain, inform and sell.

Reene Ferguson’s Specialties:

Skilled in taking a client's idea and turning it into a video presentation that sells what they offer, tells their story and delivers their message in a more memorable way.
