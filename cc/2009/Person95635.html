Sudhanshu Raheja’s Summary

Sudhanshu Raheja is a 26 year old entrepreneur based out of Pune, India.

After completing his engineering from the Army Institute of Technology, he started working with Phoenixgen Systems on backends for stock exchange connectivity. For two years he managed and developed components for markets in Korea, Australia, Hong Kong, Japan, Singapore and India.

A life long yearning to be an entrepreneur finally got the better of him and he started doing freelance projects on web development in 2006. In June 2007, he started Vercingetorix Technologies (vxtindia.com) with a mission to build world class web products in India.

Vercingetorix Technologies is now a 8 people firm with an expertise in web and mobile design and development.

They have worked with a number of web and mobile technologies including iPhone SDK, php, python and javascript, facebook and orkut applications, google gears, adobe air and a number of open source systems for content management, transformation and ecommerce.
