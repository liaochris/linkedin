Ann Oliveri’s Summary

As a marketing consultant and coach, Ann Oliveri helps mission-driven entrepreneurs, organizations, and communities become engines of knowledge and learning, problem-solving and innovation, social change and wealth creation.

Solid record of achievement growing power and influence of nonprofit organizations serving real estate and construction executives, design professionals and financial institutions.

Strengths include collaboration with board and senior management, integration of marketing and communication programs, and strategic new business development.

Ann Oliveri’s Specialties:

Capacity Building: leadership, organizational, and business development.

Brand Building: strategic marketing and communications, integrating local operations worldwide.

Community Building: multisector collaboration for economic, environmental, and social ROI.
