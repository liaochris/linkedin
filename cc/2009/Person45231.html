Jeff Ash’s Summary

Jeff Ash’s Specialties:

Writing, editing, print design, multimedia production (photo, audio, video), social media feeds. Each is a medium, so none is well done.

Jeff Ash’s Experience

  • Assistant online editor, content provider, blogger and tweeter

    Green Bay Press-Gazette

    (Newspapers industry)

    January 2007 — Present (2 years 11 months)

    Updating www.greenbaypressgazette.com and www.packersnews.com and their Twitter feeds and Facebook pages in real time with fresh news and multimedia (photos, audio, video), mostly during the peak user hours of 6 a.m. to 6 p.m. I select content and write and edit material to drive traffic, with frequent revisions. Blogger and primary writer for Out of Bounds, a Green Bay Packers fan-oriented blog at www.packersnews.com, since October 2007. Co-editor of and writer for "Legendary: The Unforgettable Career of Brett Favre," a 2008 book, and for a photo history of the Green Bay Packers, due out in November 2009.

  • Packers photo archivist

    Green Bay Press-Gazette

    (Newspapers industry)

    March 2003 — Present (6 years 9 months)

    Though this is not a formal position, I am the Press-Gazette staff member most familiar with the contents of our archive of historic Green Bay Packers photos dating to the 1940s. I gathered photos from the archive for three books: "Lambeau Field: Green Bay's National Treasure" (2003), "Legendary: The Unforgettable Career of Brett Favre" (2008) and a photo history of the Packers due out in November 2009. I also draw from the archive to create photo galleries for use at www.packersnews.com

  • Blogger and e-mail newsletter writer/publisher

    Self-employed

    (Internet industry)

    August 1994 — Present (15 years 4 months)

    Writing, editing and publishing AM, Then FM, a music blog featuring rock, pop, soul, R&B and country tunes from the 1960s forward, since February 2007. Traffic is 100,000 views per year. (http://amthenfm.wordpress.com)

    Writing, editing and publishing The Midnight Tracker, a music blog featuring rock, pop, soul and R&B album sides from the 1960s to the 1980s, since November 2007. (http://midnighttracker.wordpress.com)

    Writing, editing and publishing a blog on behalf of the D.C. Everest High School Class of 1975, sharing news and notes of interest to alumni and friends, since April 2008. (http://dce75.wordpress.com)

    Wrote and edited the Green Bay Update, a free e-mail newsletter on the Green Bay Packers, self-distributed from August 1994 to 1998, then hosted and distributed by www.southendzone.com from 1998 to August 2006.

  • Assistant metro editor

    Green Bay Press-Gazette

    (Newspapers industry)

After Super Bowl, supervised metro reporters and planned and edited their coverage.

  • Features editor

    (Newspapers industry)

    Responsible for all content of features sections and occasional special sections for Wisconsin's third-largest newspaper. Supervised staff of four reporters and one assistant editor. Features and entertainment pages created by our staff were honored in annual Wisconsin Newspaper Association judging.
