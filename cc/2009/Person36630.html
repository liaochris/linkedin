GKnot Foundation is a Private Foundation that began in 1996 as a Web Resource Innovation Center to provide a Safety Net for Communities in all 50 States that inspired MoveOn.org and many United States grassroots organizations.

GKnot Foundation grew from a need to re-connect the Internet Community of Kids and Adults Concerned that created a dis-connected Community. MacGruff Crime Dog & other Community Watch Dog Organizations weren't enough to fill the void of people going to their next door neighbor & saying hello, finding out if they're okay, providig a community of Well Being & a Social Safety Net.

GKnot Foundation believes & knows for a fact that Earth's Nature is Abundance. A Well Earth, a GKnot Production, will provide guidance to ways Communities can create Self-Sustaining Business Environment that automatically reinforces Well Being.

GKnot Foundation's A Well Earth & Democracy Work aims to:
- Support Native Ancestral Rennaisance in every Community;
- Teach Leadership & Professional Communitication Skills Youth Leadership & Kids need to Communicate Professionally within SixSigma WIN WIN Diplomatic Communications Framework;
- Assist Community Members in creating a Tightly Knit Politically Engaged Financial Secure General Community to Prevent Exploitation.
