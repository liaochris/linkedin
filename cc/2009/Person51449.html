Jeff Powell’s Summary

More than twenty years experience as an Entrepreneur, Business owner, Business consultant- specializing in supporting projects which are difficult being accomplished in time; and Investor in SMEs around the world.

The last few years I have specialized in supporting companies in addressing large complex global challenges facing different industries. I also represent several environmental organizations who have solutions for some of the larger environmental challenges facing us.

I spent the first half of my work life in Europe and has spent the last ten years working primarily in Asia and Europe, which gives me a rather unique perspective in this emerging global economy. I have worked in over 25 countries.

Started Critical Business Solutions in 2006

Critical Business Solutions is a group of consultants with a unique blend of knowledge, insight and skill set. Our talent is in providing an environment that enables smart people to have authentic and powerful discussion that lead to new action, fresh perceptions and extraordinary results.

Still, we assume that our clients know more about their job, their industry and their organization at a much deeper level than we could ever understand.

Our mission, then, is to help teams inquire into the nature of what's in their way, how they could step out of those binds and accomplish what they envision.

Critical Business Solutions primarily focuses on the medical and pharmaceutical sector, as well as energy, environmental and high-tech industries.
