Barrington Polite’s Summary

I am a specialist in disaster preparedness. I am also a first responder with training in first-aid, cpr, evacuation planning and a host of other area's. I am a member of N.F.P.A. (National Fire Protection Association) and C.E.R.T. Community Emergency Response Training. Our company is in conjunction with Homeland Security, and F.E.M.A. (Federal Emergency Management Agengy) and The Mayor of NewYorkCity, Ready NewYork program and it's efforts to get the public ready for disaster's. we educate the public in these efforts and will assist companies, organization's, religious institution's and small businesses with disaster planning, or what we call, Emergency Action Plan (E.A.P.). Be sure to visit us at: www.myspace.com/dieevac where you can see our weekly blog and stay tuned for our events. Our goal is to prepare individual's in the home, at work or play or just sitting around,to have the knowledge and understanding in what it means to be prepared.

We, at Disaster & Immediate Evacuation have build an on going relationship with the senior citizen center community by conducting a series of complimentary presentations on Ready-Preparedness in the Greater NewYork City area and counties. We would like to thank the following Senior Centers and their directors for their cooperation; Marisol Marte, Director of Dyckman Senior center, Michelle Brown Community Service Aide of St. Nicholas Houses Senior center, Keith Mitchell Director of Lincoln East Senior center, Floyd Cohen,LMSW Director of Douglass Houses Senior center, Viola Collins, Director of Martin Luther King Jr. Towers Senior center, and Anna Rodriguez of Jackie Robinson Senior center.

We continue our dedicated service to the public and the business community at large for their continued interest to get themselves prepared for disasters and putting safety and security above profit and prudence.

Barrington Polite’s Specialties:

Our product's, are Personal Emergency Kit's(P.E.K.'s) which are utility bags, They contain items that can enhance or increase your survival in the event of a un-forseen disaster. MRE's(Meals Ready to Eat) emergency food rations, emergency water, waterproof match's, first-aid and a host of other items that make up the kit's. Whatever the need, we have the product for you and the service's are robust and very informative. We cover over 78% in terms of the demand for these kind of services.
