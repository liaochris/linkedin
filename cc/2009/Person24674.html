David "AnjelusX" Slauenwhite’s Summary

Independent Writer, Game Consultant,Designer, Producer, Community Communicator

As an avid gamer since my childhood with the Atari, Coleco and of course Arcades. Not to mention tabletop play with board games and pen and paper role-playing games, I have been passionate about video games and have always sought to be a part of the industry where the magic is made.

While my focus is primarily writing and world concept creation, I have been working towards studying various design disciplines to further both my personal knowledge as well as opportunities with the industry.

Also through my blog http://www.anjelsyndicate.org I have written a body of consisting of commentary and editorials on the industry, design and community approaches.
