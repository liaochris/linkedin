Paulo de Souza Lima’s Summary

I have worked for some of the greatest corporations in Brazil like: Companhia Vale do Rio Doce, Sita do Brasil (today known as Orange), Embratel, Telemar, FIAT, Petrobras, Datasul and Positivo Informática. All of them as Technical Maintenance Engineer (some of them as supervisor). I have worked for medium and small business as well.
At those small business, I have learned to work in resourceless environments, getting as much as possible from available resources, using creativity.

My experiences are in Telecommunications and Information Technology Services, mainly focused in maintenance, infrastructure, servers and network (LAN/MAN/WAN) administration.
