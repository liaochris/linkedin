Remko Lodder’s Summary

Technical Unix Engineer at Snow BV that Specializes in Unix, Networking and Security consultancy and support.

I am an experienced system administrator (since '99), mainly focussed on the *BSD derived systems, but also a fair bit of Linux distributions (Slackware, Redhat, Suse and so on). Since I am heavily involved in OpenSource work I joined the FreeBSD committers group (doc, www, secteam, bugmeister, secteam-secretary, src) to assist with the various tasks that are open.

My focus in my job is networking and security (and ofcourse Unix), I am an experienced Checkpoint administrator, My current task is migrating firewalls from one vendor to the other (checkpoint, juniper, fortigate) and doing network management (various cisco devices, from the 800 series to 3750g switches). Previous tasks included maintaining and doing active change management on various Checkpoint devices, from building VPN's to High Availability clusters, and a lot of cisco devices to operate WAN lines (cisco 2600, 3500, 6500 series), and migrating towards a centralized datacenter.

Beyond that I am also a teacher that transfers my skills to people around me that need it. I am keen on evolving and would like to see everyone benefit from knowledge.
