Michael Bell, GRI’s Summary

Seasoned broker with hundreds of negotiated residential and commercial transactions throughout LA County. Top 3% nationally, working only on referral since 1999. Traits include strong reputation, massive internet marketing, clear and honest communication, comprehensive construction knowledge, ability to train agents in all aspects of real estate, and a proven track record with testimonials gladly provided.

Michael Bell, GRI’s Specialties:

Residential and commercial sales and consulting in LA County. Expertise in negotiations, market data, and strategy, and am also a trainer.
