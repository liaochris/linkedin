Through mental exercises called Mind Power Stretching, Randy has helped thousands achieve the mental edge and rise up to the next level.

What is Golf Mind Power?

Simply put, golf mind power is incorporating the power of your inner swing (or your thoughts) into your outer swing (or the action you take). We are all capable of choosing our thoughts that either keep us statically where we’ve been or move us dynamically where we want to be. If you had the choice to be static or dynamic, what would you choose?
