Paul Smith’s Summary

I'm the Creative Partner at Never Odd or Even LLP, a new media agency specialising in mobile applications, web development and social media marketing.

Outside of Never Odd or Even, I blog for all sorts of people including the Guardian, and recently completed a social media project involving Twitter called Twitchhiker.

I worked for 13 years as a producer and programmer for both commercial radio and the BBC. I also spent five years larking about as a copywriter, winning plenty of awards that now adorn the downstairs toilet.
