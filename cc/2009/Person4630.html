Amit Bapat’s Summary

Currently I'm working as a TME for Software & Services Group in Intel. I'm part of the Open Source Technology Center (OTC) working on Moblin, an Intel sponsored Linux distribution specifically optimized for Intel Atom processors.

Prior to that I was in a start up of New Business Initiatives group in Intel that was creating a platform to deliver value added services to Intel PC clients in service provider network. My focus was customer/supplier engagements in both technical and business domains, business analysis, interface between marketing and engineering, technical documentation, demos.

Prior to that I was working as a TME at Modular Communications Platform Division (MCPD) in Intel, responsible for technical marketing of ATCA Single Board Computers, which are PC servers in bladed architecture, designed specifically for telecommunication market. I served as a single point of technical contact for several ATCA products for tier 1 customers. Responsibilities included customer engagement, product documentation, interface between marketing and engineering.

Before that I worked as a CAD engineer at Intel, developing software for design automation team for about 7 years. Focus was on design automation in areas like circuit simulations, circuit synthesis, circuit optimization, register file synthesis, noise simulation, design database management, tool release methodology, customization of design entry platform.

Prior to Intel I worked in various software companies as software engineer in many areas such as medical claims processing, office automation, multi-byte conversion, database translations, mobile sales information system.
