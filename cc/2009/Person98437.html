Thomas Plessis’s Summary

I started my career at Fun Radio (CHR) in France as radio host & show Producer, then I worked for Radio FG, first Dance CHR radio station in Paris as Program Director and Morning Show Presenter.

After this strong experience at Radio FG I started my own company ( first as independant consultant at Beat It Broadcast )

I worked for several companies such as Radio France International and the French Foreign Office and for Radio Stations ( Full services, AC, CHR formats) in South Africa, Chad, Morocco, Jordan,Tunisia...


After one year of successful activities, Beat it Broadcast became Play 6 ( still in activity) where I produce radio shows, dedicated programs for instore radio stations, and TV programs, producer of audio and video contents (podcasts and videos) for websites & online medias.

In 2006 I created the web company JADETI and launched radiopodcast.com the first on demand talk radio website. JADETI is now part of Lagardere Active, major french international media company , where I am Head of Radio Programs & Internet Development at Lagardere Active Radio International.

Thomas Plessis’s Specialties:

- 12 years experience in Radio industry (France & International) and contents production associated to a strong internet profile, specialized in internet Radio, podcasting, "on demand" music, smart radios and also videos and social medias.

- Sense of Entrepreneurship: Founded a production company (still in activity) and another startup company successfully sold.
