Jorge Pelayo’s Summary

Seasoned International Bilingual Marketing Executive. Vast experience of over 25 years. Successfully performed as General/Country Manager, Marketing and Sales Director and VP for large Multinationals, as well as Agency Managing Director, Entrepreneur and Consultant. Has devised, strategized, managed and executed volume driving and profit building initiatives and campaigns that have:
• Captured market-leading dominance (market share gains of up to 35%) for both new and existing Brands and products.
• Doubled sales in less than a year (up to $92 Million/yr).
• Boosted managed business subsidiaries into their Corporate Top-10 List worldwide in terms of revenue and profits.
• Led start-ups, mature businesses, recently acquired units and stagnant businesses into market leadership and above target volume/ profitability.

Persuasive bilingual communicator. Substantial achievements in Branding, Brand Identity and Communications, overall Business Development, devising and executing strategies later reapplied into the U.S., Europe and South America, and in providing Marketing support to Sales Areas. Has Consulting and Entrepreneurial expertise through self-owned businesses, as well as Teaching experience at the University level.

Interested on opportunities in Canada, including leading companies, early-stage startups as well as companies “on the bubble”.
