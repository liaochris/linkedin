Gabriel Sim’s Summary

Gabriel is a seasoned market researcher with extensive experience across diverse industries, from consumer-related industries (e.g. consumer, retail, and media) to professional services (e.g. financial services) and industrial markets (e.g. chemicals, logistics and manufacturing). He leads, designs, and implements projects to help client understand marketing and business issues by embedding fact-based insights into their business strategy.

Gabriel has 16 years of experience in providing customised research and information services. His areas of expertise include continuous research (both consumer and business research), information searching & retrieval, information & knowledge management, and portal solutions & information architecture. Gabriel is a recognised expert in the design and implementation of successful portal solutions, with a specialisation in Global Intelligence Alliance's Intelligence Plaza (http://www.globalintelligence.com/solutions/plaza/). He has advised numerous companies such as Asia Pacific Breweries, Amex, Cerebos Pacific, DHL, Dow Chemical, Henkel, Hewlett-Packard, Intel, LaFarge, Standard Chartered and Visa.

Gabriel was instrumental in establishing the regional information and knowledge hub at Fusion Consulting (now Global Intelligence Alliance). He also headed the regional knowledge centre at Synovate Business Consulting. His previous roles included managing regional qualitative and quantitative research programmes at British American Tobacco, leading the customer satisfaction research team at ACNielsen Research (Singapore), and shepherding a team of regional business information analysts at Finland-based Esmerk Information Services.

Gabriel holds a MSc (Information Studies) from the Nanyang Technological University and a BSc (Economics) from the University of London. He has also obtained the Certified Professional Marketer (Asia) status, conferred by the Asia Marketing Federation.
