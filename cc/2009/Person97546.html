Terry Hinkle’s Summary

After enjoying a very successful business career in the creative world of advertising and marketing working with the heads of major national and international corporations to help grow their businesses, I created and was host of a popular nationally syndicated radio show: ShadoWorlds. After three years of interviewing many of the top authors, teachers and experts in the world of metaphysics, science, theology, spirituality, motivation, healing, ancient history and the paranormal... I combined this fascinating information with my lifetimes of knowledge to write Plan BE, Secrets of the Divine Feminine (www.gotoplanbe.com) and started on my new path as an author and empowering teacher.

While writing Plan BE, I started listening again to my artistic muse. I was inspired to create all the art for my book, then taught art in the school district where I live and am now finally expressing my natural creative gifts through personal works of Vibrational Art (www.hinkleart.com). I am having fun again.
