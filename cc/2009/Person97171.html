Sanjay Gupta’s Summary

Founder/CEO of Tea Connexions, a tea distribution company that offers gourmet teas beautifully packaged. We accomplish this goal by working with motivated entrepreneurs who promote our products under their own brand names. In addition to gourmet teas, we provide other value added services including training, marketing tools, and e-commerce.

Tea Connexions started in a 200 square foot office as an idea and today is a reality with over 100 distributors selling our products, primarily, in the United States.

In 2009, we set up permanent operations in the United States, Tea Connexions USA and are seeking to set up operations with other partners throughout the world. To learn more about us, visit http://www.teaconnexions.com

I would describe myself as a strategic and tactical executive. I am able to determine a strategy and determine the method to implement the strategy. This is one of my strongest strengths.

The goal of Tea Connexions is to become the "tea connexion", providing our distributors top quality teas we source ourselves, in addition to other established brands.
