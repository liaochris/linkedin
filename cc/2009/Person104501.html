William Saito’s Summary

Award-winning entrepreneur with expertise in ICT and related technologies. Recognized authority and frequent lecturer on IT security issues. Founder and president of InTecur, a Tokyo-based consultancy that helps companies find markets for innovative technologies, identify new markets for existing technologies and adjust technologies for new uses as traditional markets contract, shift and change. Experienced in expanding product focuses from local to cross-border or global applications. Bilingual in English and Japanese. Well-connected with corporate leaders, industry experts and government organizations on both sides of Pacific.
