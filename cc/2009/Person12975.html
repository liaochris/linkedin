Brandon Jennings’s Summary

I'm currently enjoying a career in television production while slowly developing my passion of photography into a viable second career. It's been a real journey over the last several years, with life taking me in many different directions,notably New York City, where I was fortunate enough to sharpen my skills as a broadcast editor and learn from countless numbers of talented and gifted people.
