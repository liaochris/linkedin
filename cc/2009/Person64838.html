Matteo La Rosa’s Summary

After a degree in Economical Statistics Sciences, I worked 9 years in a Publishing House where I was responsible for the international division. In 2000, I spent one whole year in the incubator of ideas and innovation as it was the magazine Web Marketing Tools; I worked then four years as a Key Account in Quadrante, a web agency where I met my current associates.

From 2004 I founded Koinema, a dynamic and very flexible Multimedia Design Studio for online communication based on vanguard and research.

Koinema helps companies to use Internet and the new media (like Videos) in the best way, given the needs of the company itself, and with an eye to the last marketing trends.

+ Effective use of the whole media (internet, video, flash animation through cellulars, etc.)

+ Push consultation service: we do not wait for the needs of our customer, but we try to stimulate them with new ideas and new techniques applications

+ Koinema tries to fuse all the existing media, i.e. a website could be a CD-ROM as well as a good Video presentation and so on...

Web Site: http://www.koinema.com

We have also brand new sport social network for the, online from July 2009:
