Merit Tukiainen’s Summary

After 20 year in the hospitality industry, culminating as the Director of Revenue at the Four Seasons Hotel in Boston I wanted to be my own boss and have some flexibility for my children. Realizing that life as an entrepreneur and a starting up a retail venture, hours spent working would certainly not be less, I nevertheless took the plunge and trusted the universe. I had confidence in my business education and long experience travelling and working in Europe. I have developed an eye for quality, detail, a passion for thoughtful, personalized service, and a keen sense for customer relationship building. My general marketing, networking, and marketing database analysis skills are helping me build Night & Day.

My goal is to develop Night & Day to a solid enterprise that can grow to more stores, be sold, or franchised in the next 3-5 years.

After accomplishing that I wish to set up an independent consulting enterprise in the areas of customer service and process improvement. I believe my experiences have made me an expert in recognizing opportunities for service level improvements, and revenue growth opportunities within the service delivery cycle. I also love and have extensive knowledge in developing SOPs and training to support the service delivery process.
