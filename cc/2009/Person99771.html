Tomaž Orešič’s Summary

Energy utility manager with international experience in energy sales as well as in the energy related legal issues.
From 2007 Group Head of marketing for Central and Western Europe at the London based EFT Group. EFT Group is active in 18 European countries and is the market leader in terms of electricity trading in SE Europe and also developing several investment projects in its own generation capacities.
From 2003 to 2007 Member of the Board of Elektro Maribor d.d., the second biggest electricity distribution company in Slovenia.
Co-Founder of ZORA Biosciences Oy, Espoo, Finland (www.zora.fi).
Founder and the Chairman of the annual international conference on electricity market and distribution ENERGIJA 09 (www.energija09.si), as well as a programme committee member of Transmission & Distribution Europe (www.td-europe.eu), guest lecturer at the Faculty of Economics at the University of Ljubljana, Member of the VaasaETT Advisory Committee (http://www.vaasaett.com/advisory-committee/) and a speaker at several international events, focusing on energy legislation issues, energy market design and electricity supply activities.
From 2009 he is a member of the Court of Honour of the Managers’ Association of Slovenia.
In addition to the listed responsibilities he has been a Chairman or Member of Supervisory Boards of 6 Slovenian companies during the last 10 years.
