Steve Misewicz’s Summary

Stevens Design, Inc. works with sales and marketing managers responsible for generating creative communications that impact the viewer and increase awareness for a wide variety of B2B and B2C companies.

Steve is a creative, compassionate, professional art director with 29 years experience bringing visual impact to print. He has 9 years experience in corporate communications art direction prior to becoming President of Stevens Design, Inc.

Steve Misewicz’s Specialties:

Print Communications background - if it's printable, we've designed for it. MAC/Skills: Extensive in QXP, PS, AI, Acrobat (CS4). Basic in ID, Word, XLS, PP
