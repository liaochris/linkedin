Chad Cooper’s Summary

On the marketing side- I am typically brought in to build marketing & sales strategies for internet properties. I am a firm believer in the "human behind every decision" mentality. I have a proven track record at planning & executing user growth roadmaps that yield millions of users.

As an editor- I follow a personal motto in all of my roles: Keep your user at heart when making your decisions. Without them- you're nowhere.

On the sales side- I only work for products and companies I believe in... As such- they typically sell themselves :-) Armed with that in the back of my mind- the art of the deal becomes (slightly) less stressful. I have a track record of turning companies profitable, too- which is a good thing I hear.

Some people have called me a type-A personality; stuck in a comedians body. This harmony is why I love positions where I am not only at the table for driving marketing/communications/sales- but, also having my hand in the overall user experience- such as assisting in the streamlining of UI's, hardware availability, network security, latency, and- above all CONTENT.

I love planning, executing, and managing highly successful web site launches, including SEM, SEO, and UI protection/development. I identify the users, drive them to the site, and then like being responsible for the "stickiness" that turns them into hardcore users.

I am very easy to work with- and find myself meeting/having great discussion with people throughout the web/IT sphere about the new .com era. I am very proud to be apart of the new internet boom!

Sales is fun, too :-)
People really know when:
A. You know your product inside & out...
B. You believe in & love your product
C. You know precisely how your product or solution can help them get the audience and attention they seek.
