Angela Sweet’s Summary

I have been involved in workforce mobility and rental relocation for over 17 years. I have enjoyed being involved in training and developing an excellent team of touring agents, relocation specialists and sales experts. Currently I am working on development of our destination services products to make our offerings more robust. CORT currently offers area orientations, settling in, housing tours, rental assistance, apartment locating, rental furniture, housewares, and lump sum relocation assistance. We work with contractors, expats, 80% of the Fortune 500 companies, Relocation Service Providers, Destination Service Providers, and small to large companies that need to relocate employees.
