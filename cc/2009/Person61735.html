Manish Miglani’s Summary

As a professional DJ since 1993, my career has taken me from bars and nightclubs to playing some of the biggest nightclubs in North America, touring live act opener for The Advent and running EAR Bookings which represented 33 of the world's most renowned house and techno DJs. Since 1997, I have thrown large scale dance music events in Champaign and Chicago, Illinois and also was behind the Gramaphone Techno Showcases with Josh Werner. As a producer since 2003, I have honed my craft with releases on the Onion's 7" inch sampler and the Default_One Cd which featured Rino Cerrone, The Advent, Steve Rachmad and others. My music covers house, techno, hip hop and even bollywood remixes. I am seeking start-up capital for an idea with branding centered around "Tha Werd".
