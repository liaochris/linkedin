Andrew Comeau’s Summary

My primary experience is in application programming with Microsoft Access and Visual Basic. I have several years of experience in professional development for areas including laboratory testing and human resources. I am also a Microsoft Certified Professional in Windows Application Development with Visual Basic.NET.

My original training was in accounting with an emphasis on computer operations. Over the years, I've pursued a career in I.T. using the experience that I've gained working with PC hardware and software.

In addition to my regular employment, I operate the website Drewslair.com in order to share my software and programming knowledge. The site includes articles and tutorials on relational database concepts and application design with Access. I also answer questions by e-mail from readers who have found the site helpful.

My goal is to continue in my career as a programmer and to expand my current skills to include other languages and technologies. I also plan to continue developing online services such as Drewslair.com to offer greater resources on tools such as VB.Net.
