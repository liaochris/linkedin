Scott Mount’s Summary

I have 18 Years agency experience. My role has evolved with every opportunity from being a traditional designer to a thinking designer/strategist leading digital ideas that compliment a clients’ business challenges. I love the dog & pony shows, getting my hands dirty and collaborating with teams to create something from nothing. I don't like egos, hacked together teams and can’t do attitudes.
