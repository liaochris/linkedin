Dean Meyers’s Summary

Everyone can tell their story visually. I can show you how.

I have been a Web designer and developer since 1995, starting with pharmaceutical and medical marketing sites designed for professionals and consumers to recent projects including Content Management Systems for business, non-profit organizations and educational institutions.

I have also developed programs to "teach you how to fish"--l can teach you how to put visual expression into all of your communication, from reporting to presentations.


In addition to complete fluency in an array of graphic tools including Indesign, Quark, Photoshop, Illustrator and Dreamweaver, technical skills include programming HTML/CSS/PHP/mySQL based sites, creating Flash banners, building blogs and wikis, and keeping up with the latest Web 2.0 developments.

Dean Meyers’s Specialties:

Music composition, sound editing (for multimedia, video, web); and video editing for the Web; multi-cultural marketing (Latin America/Carribean/US); HTML/CSS; Flash; PHP/mySQL (LAMP and IIS6 environments); CSS; Javascript.
