Nathan Egan’s Summary

Nathan Egan is recognized as the creator and definitive authority on Freesourcing. His consultancy, the Freesource Agency, LLC helps organizations leverage free, web-based Social Media + Web 2.0 platforms to drive efficiency through their organization and build better connectivity with their best prospects and customers.

Freesource designs customized workshops that specifically address each client’s unique sales model and target markets. Delivered online or in-person, our scalable freesourcing programs help companies:

> Uncover + Create New Qualified Sales Leads
> Turn Cold Calls into Warm Handshakes
> Create Stronger Customer Relationships
> Build Dynamic Communication Infrastructures
> Deliver Powerful Brand Messaging 24x7
> Maximize Current Resources + Past Successes
