Tony Richardson’s Summary

After trying garbage collection, drumming, and hardware sales, I discovered advertising.

I have created TV commercials, print ads, radio ads, direct response, web copy, sales letters, brochures and even tiny classified ads.

I've worked for Colgate Palmolive, Kimberly-Clark, American Express, Unilever, Nestle, Glaxo SmithKline, Westpac, Qantas, Telstra, and many more brands, in some of Australias best known agencies.

Then 7 years ago I set out on my own ... and I've never been happier.

I love dealing directly with clients and helping solve their marketing problems ... creatively.

Oh, and I've written a book called 'Super TV - How marketers can create better TV advertising for a lot less money." You can down load it for FREE at

http://www.tacticaltv.com.au/free.html

Tony Richardson’s Specialties:

I work with clients through the creative process in a collaborative 'no-fuss' atmosphere. All on time and below budget. Together we strive for original, breakthrough, creative solutions to each brief and problem.

With my professional partners I can help clients with copywriting, art direction, creative direction, design, print production, TV production, radio production, account management, research stimulus and many other creative tasks.
