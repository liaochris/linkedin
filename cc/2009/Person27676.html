Dorothy Nelson’s Summary

Started out as User Interface Developer in the 80s at Bell Communications Research, and through Oracle, Aspect Telecommunications, Intraspect, Webvan, eBay, and finally Google, I've become nearly exclusively a User Interface Designer with some prototyping expertise. Recently, I am leading people and initiatives at Google, including team education and design pattern standardization.

My goal is to positively affect the lives of as many people as I can with the work that I do, making life easier, more fun, and more satisfying.
