Teresa Dentino’s Summary

Teresa Dentino, CEO/Founder of The Financial 411, has just published the first book in a series specifically created for women who want to get financially savvy.

As a new stockbroker 25 years ago, Founder and CEO Teresa Dentino realized that her female clients were underserved by the investment community. Through years of academic research and empirical study, she has developed unique expert standing in the field of financial education for women. Her programs have helped thousands of women take control of their financial future.

Through research and experience, we've learned a lot about the social and psychological barriers that prevent women from getting involved in their finances. Our program addresses those barriers and takes all the intimidation out of the educational process. There's currently nothing like it in the financial world programming.

We have cultivated content delivery methods for women that are years ahead of the curve, and we are uniquely positioned to leverage these methods.

Teresa earned the National Association of Securities Dealers licenses (NASD Series: 7, 3, 63, 65, and 24) in San Francisco in 1985, and achieved the highest designation of Principal. Teresa is also a certified mediator and a Certified Divorce Financial Analyst.

Drawing on her seasoned background as a full-service investment advisor and former NASD Principal, Ms. Dentino specializes in advising women and families without the conflict of asset managment services and investment sales. Through programs like the Financial Summit and the Financial Fire Drill she is often called upon to evaluate overall investment management performance and asset allocations. Consultation and guidance only with no investment sales.
