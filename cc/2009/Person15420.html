Carl Myhill’s Summary

My Career objective is to to design and build excellent user interfaces (applications or web sites) for companies that share my belief in the competitive advantage gained from investing in user-centred design.

I am very keen to use 16 years experience in User Experience Design (UXD) to support organisations doing something about climate change, carbon emissions, sustainability and environmental matters.

Carl Myhill’s Specialties:

Interaction Design based on the Cooper approach, accessible design, ethnography, paper prototyping, end-to-end interaction design, working effectively with programming teams (even doing Agile)
