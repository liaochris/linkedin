Remy Lang’s Summary

My main tasks as Data Storage and TSM Specialist are providing our customers with the hassle-free backup & recovery facilities they need, and providing first-rate handling of incidents, problems and changes.

In addition to being one member of a small specialist team of Data Storage and TSM Specialists, I am responsible for the large scale migration of TSM clients from multiple platforms to a new TSM server park. Furthermore, I am responsible for the life cycle management of the TSM servers and clients, organizing upgrade time lines for the TSM software across ING Netherlands.


Beside my regular work I study Law at the Hogeschool van Amsterdam. This 4-year HBO-Rechten course prepares the student for a job as para legal: the right and left hand of the lawyer.

My interests lie with finding smarter, fairer and leaner solutions for aspects of criminal and private law processes. I am also interested in the psychological aspects of criminal investigation, suspect profiling, the investigative hearings and the determination of punishability and punishment. In addition, I am interested in how integrity, accountability and compliancy (should) affect me as a legal professional.


My main interest as a photographer is social and documentary photography, in particular in Mongolia. Other fields include street photography and event photography.
