Robb Walters’s Summary

E-Commerce & Internet Marketing Director with over 10 years experience helping build a billion-dollar retail website, creating online brands, and maximizing profit from the online channel.

Fortune 50 retail experience, including online from start-up to $1.8B in annual revenues. Accustomed to delivering big results on a limited budget, using ROI focused analysis.

Other previous positions (marketing, operations, accounting, IT and design) have helped to round my professional experience, and help in developing effective cross-functional teams.

Comfortable presenting ideas one-to-one or one-to many, I have spoken at local and regional conferences and universities.

Also, I am an active member of industry organizations, including Shop.org (the web component of the National Retail Federation), a charter member of the Email Marketing Roundtable, member of the DMA and Chair of their Advocacy Committee, the EEC and the Seattle chapter of the DMA, I also attend national and regional conferences focused specifically on internet commerce and marketing.
