Liz Fischbeck’s Summary

Scale, innovation, and efficiency are all words that I am drawn to, which makes online media an appropriate and enjoyable place for me to be. I'm passionate about social networking and web connectivity, which has led me to work at two community-based sites: popular travel site VirtualTourist.com (TripAdvisor family) and social networking giant, Facebook.

I'm a forward thinking contributor who enjoys developing new ideas and processes, and seeing them develop over time. As an Account Manager specifically, I enjoy being on the front line of communication with our advertisers to help maximize ROI and drive product changes.
