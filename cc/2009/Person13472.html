Brian Dote’s Summary

I run a web application development company, Dotestudios (dotestudios.com) as a freelance web developer and recently left Apple in 2008 to work as a senior user interface engineer for a private R&D company here in Honolulu called Archinoetics.

I joined Apple in early 2004 working simultaneously on .Mac (membership based service with over a million members) and iTunes. In my early days at Apple, I was part of the iTunes Europe launch, the first version of Motion (Apple's motion graphics application), and various .Mac services. 

In 2005 I was promoted to lead the .Mac User Interface Engineering team. My team was a key part of many successful .Mac launches. In 2007 I was a key member of the team that wrote some of the first web applications for the iPhone launch. I'm a co-applicant on two patents pending from this work. In the summer of 2007, I led the team in the successful launch of the .Mac Web Gallery. 

Prior to joining Apple in 2004, I worked at Incyte Corporation as the lead web developer. I also worked as a web application developer at Cisco Systems in 2001 and Northpoint Communications in 2000.

 Born in Honolulu, I earned my BA Zoology degree from the University of Hawaii and my MS Information Systems from Hawaii Pacific University. I currently live in Manoa, Hawaii.
