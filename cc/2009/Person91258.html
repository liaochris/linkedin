Sharyn Hutchinson’s Summary

I am an Interior ReDesigner/Home Stager who helps you create beautiful spaces. Whether selling a home, moving in or just looking for a fresh new look, we work with your existing furnishings and accessories to achieve your dream. Our company, ReDesign Concepts, LLC offers a variety of services created to fit any budget.

I am an affiliate member of the Interior Design Society and the International Staging & ReDesign Directory. I also belong to the American Business Women’s Association where I am currently serving as treasurer for the Futura Chapter.

I have also made guest appearances on Talk Radio Shows AM660, AM1190 WAMT, “Your Money, Your Home” and the Creating Your Masterpiece Podcast Show. I am also a published author on www.ezinearticles.com.

Our goal is to help you create the home you desire. Working within your budget, you can have the home you have always wanted. We look forward to helping you achieve this dream. Everyone deserves a beautiful space.
