Ken Peck’s Summary

I have been in the insurance industry since 1998. I have sold tens of thousands of insurance policies with a direct writer as well as an independent agent. In my time in the industry I have managed sales forces for three companies that always produced at a high level. I have a passion for selling and servicing insurance with honesty and integrity. My goal is to make sure that the risk I insure is the correct risk for the correct company with the correct amount of coverage. What ever company I am involved in will make sure to balance protecting the consumer and honoring our underwriters while producing at the highest level of profitability.
