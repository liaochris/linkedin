Aaron Neumann’s Interests:

The past year I've worked PT for Eastside Food Co-op, Northeast Minneapolis' only community-owned, natural-foods grocery store. I lead programs and take courses at Landmark Education, a global leader in the field of personal/professional training and development and one of the largest providers of continuing education. I was the Green Party endorsed candidate for a Minneapolis City Council seat in 2005, and came in 2nd place with 30% of the vote. I consider our effort a victory and am committed to the most livable, just and peaceful Minneapolis possible. A true renaissance man, I'm an artist/musician and create everything from cave paintings to original music. My art is in ancient styles and techniques, I play a variety of instruments, and my current project is a mix of bohemian acoustic indie folk original world-influenced music. Mostly my interests include you! Networking to make positive futures happen is an authentic self-expression for me.
