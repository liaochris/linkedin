Michael Palko’s Summary

I'm a self-starter with a background in project management and training design and delivery. I have a penchant for developing creative traditional classroom and e-Learning courses using a variety of tools. I know the value of applications and web sites that focus on usability and design. I'm currently delving into the role of community manager, constructing learning community using SharePoint and all that the Web 2.0 world has to offer, including podcasts and mLearning. I'm am enthralled by the concepts and power of social media, networks and viral marketing.
