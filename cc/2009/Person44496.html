Jay Gould’s Summary

I've built and sold many startups. Today I'm the CEO of Gamers Media, the first vertical ad network focused on delivering premium blue chip advertisers to the long tail of online gaming.

Prior to Gamers Media I founded one of the first social networks, video sharing sites, and the first viral video widget site. I later became President of Bolt Media and I've invested and raised cash in a few other startups.
