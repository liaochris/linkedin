Image by Interface is a Vancouver, BC based image management company providing digital photography, multimedia and communications services for people, places, events and things. We are photographers for wedding, professional, family, corporate, commercial, editorial, event, model, actor, fashion, glamour, travel, advertising agency and public relations clients worldwide. We are key note speakers on digital and travel photography and global travel adventure topics. We are also frequent cruise ship guest lecturers. Public speaking clients include Holland America Lines, travel industry groups, photo confrences & corporate meetings.

We teach digital & travel photogaphy to beginner, enthusiast and professional photographers, artists and travellers.

Our multimedia presentations will inspire, educate and entertain you.
