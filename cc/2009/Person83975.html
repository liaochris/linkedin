Rick Jessup’s Summary

I'm a veteran of the social media scene as an active blogger since 1995. I live my life online and could very well replace my resume with a Google search. This knowledge and experience provides me the unique opportunity to be a results-focused marketing and branding mind with an aptitude for social media strategy. Outside my core areas of expertise I have extensive international experience, excellent skills with the written word, and the ability to present professionally. I’m a unique thinker, and I love challenging the status quo on tactical execution while remaining true to the brand. I love building relationships, live to create loyalty, and always give the customer something to care about.
