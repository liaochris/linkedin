William Eastman’s Summary

Based upon my 30 years in the consulting and training industry, we have assembled an industry leading library of best practices of fast growth through our open source guild of content providers (thought leaders).

I also serve as a "virtual" executive with our channel partners where we can blend core competences and create an industry defining offer.
