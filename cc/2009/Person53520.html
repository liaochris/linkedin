Karthikeyan Iyer’s Summary

Founder-director of Crafitti Consulting Private Limited, a new venture in the innovation and strategy space. Co-crafting innovation in business and technology contexts using techniques, frameworks and methodologies experimented and developed over several years of grass-roots application and learning.

Innovation Consultant facilitating innovative thinking in business strategy, process efficiency, new solution conceptualization, technology evolution, innovation capability building and intellectual property management including patent strategy.

Lean Consultant with experience in envisaging and implementing Lean Thinking in more than 100 projects in a variety of areas including Telecommunications, Financial Services, Package Implementation, Testing Services, IT Operations and Application Development Frameworks. Responsibilities include devising organizational Lean strategy and roadmap, policy setting, training and mentoring cadre of Lean Facilitators, incorporating new techniques to catalyze Lean Thinking, distilling Lean learning from publications and experiments for broad-basing and organization-wide application.

Author of publications Holistic Value Framework – creating right value streams using TRIZ and other concepts and article Strategic Project Management Priorities in Global Software Development. Author of concept notes Lean in Application Maintenance, Application Development using Design Structure Matrices.

Program Manager interacting with a diverse set of people ranging from engineers to middle, senior and executive management and helping them adopt new management and ideation techniques in the midst of acute project delivery pressures.

Technical Consultant in the Telecommunications domain with experience in developing, testing and architecting solutions for multiple clients.

Karthikeyan Iyer’s Specialties:

Innovation techniques, structured problem definition and articulation, process streamlining and efficiency, value frameworks, ideation, decision-making, intellectual property management, technology evolution landscaping, patent strategy.
