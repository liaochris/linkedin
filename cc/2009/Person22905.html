Dan Schawbel’s Summary

I'm the leading personal branding expert for Gen-Y and the author of the bestselling career book, “Me 2.0: Build a Powerful Brand to Achieve Career Success (Kaplan, April 2009)“. It also made the New York Times summer reading list for job seekers and was one of three recommended social networking books by Shape Magazine. Fast Company calls me a “personal branding force of nature" and The New York Times considers me to be a "personal branding guru."

Recently, BusinessWeek named me as one of twenty people entrepreneurs should follow on twitter, alongside Richard Branson.

My Personal Branding Blog is consistently ranked in the top 20 marketing blogs in the world by AdAge, is the #1 job blog by Careerbuilder in 2008, and has achieved syndication from Forbes, Reuters and Fox Business. I also publish Personal Branding Magazine, am the head judge for the Personal Brand Awards and direct Personal Branding TV. I run personal branding social events and workshops in addition.

I've written articles in major magazines and online resources such as BusinessWeek, BrandWeek Magazine, PRWeek, and Advertising Age. I've been quoted in such outlets as The New York Times, NPR, The Wall Street Journal, Fast Company, ABC News, Boston Globe, and Yahoo! Finance.
