Joslyn Baker’s Summary

SENIOR COMMERCIAL PHOTOGRAPHY & PRODUCTION MANAGEMENT PROFESSIONAL
with several years experience in advertising, marketing, printing and publishing.

COMMERCIAL PHOTOGRAPHY VETERAN
Shooting professionally since 1980. From Fortune 500 companies to local and regional businesses, client roster contains such well-known names as Bennigan's, Hilton Hotels, Safeway, Seiko, Shell Oil, and the Texas Commission on the Arts. Comprehensive knowledge of all stages of the photographic process. Fluent in architectural, food, jewelry, product and catalog photography for promotional use by agencies, service, retail and manufacturing companies. Excellent photographer with a well-developed eye for design. Effective photo producer. Creative, resourceful collaborator with art directors, clients and talent. Recognize the value of the art of persuasion through superlative photography.

PROFICIENT PRINT PRODUCTION MANAGEMENT PROFESSIONAL
Exemplary record of delivering of top-quality printed products on time and within budget. Manage and coordinate all aspects of print production. Portfolio includes collateral, international multi-lingual ad campaigns, trade show exhibits, outdoor, out-of-home, specialty promotional items, packaging, POS, signage, direct mail with fulfillment, specialty printing & binding. Proficient coordinator of multiple, complex projects and personalities in fast-paced environs while maintaining sense of humor and balance. Known for doing “whatever it takes.”
