David Henderson’s Summary

David Henderson is an Internet Marketing and Social Media pioneer. Over the past 14 years he has helped build some of the most innovative, influential and successful Internet marketing companies of our time.

David was an early senior executive for both DoubleClick and MatchLogic helping to pioneer Internet Advertising and Digital Marketing in the mid-1990's. As social media evolved in the early 2000’s David consulted a number of early stage startup companies while collaborating with the attention community developing early attention economy principles and concepts. In 2007 David co-founded Social Media Networks one of the very first social media application and ad networks for open social platforms.

David's latest startup is SocialEyes, a sCRM plafform, that enables brands to manage community relationships and social presence across social platforms.
