Daniel Siden’s Summary

Haberdasherylondon LLP is a new breed of creative group blending traditional new media creative disciplines with new materials and technologies to provide Creative Direction and Development for a wide variety of industries. I founded HaberdasheryLondon LLP with two hyper-talented business partners Mac Cox, and Ben Rigby. Since beginning, we have grown dramatically in size and turnover by keeping freeform design experimentation at the core of our creative process to help providing fresh ideas and bespoke solutions.

Core competencies include:
Industrial & Product Design
Art Installation+Sculpture Design
User Interface Development
Interaction Design
Experience Design
Mechanical, Light Electrical & Optical Engineering
Project Management
2-D, 3-D Modelling
Prototyping
Design for Manufacturing
Process Development
Graphic Design
Moving and still Image Production Design and Direction
Specialist lighting design and Development


With an undergraduate background in Mechanical Engineering, Physics and Industrial Design from Carnegie Mellon University (USA) I worked as a professional Design Engineer for five years before relocating to the UK to study Industrial Design at the Royal College of Art. While at the RCA and since graduating I have received several awards and acclimations for my work including the National Endowment for Science Technology and Art (NESTA).

Daniel Siden’s Specialties:

Industrial Design, Product Design, Interaction Design, Engineering, Graphic Design, Design for Manufacture, Graphic Design, (Branding + Print Coming Soon) Modeling/Protyping, Design for Manufacture, Market Research, Competitive Advantage Analysis.
