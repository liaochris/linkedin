Chad Hayward’s Summary

Chad Hayward is President and Assessment Director for the Hire Insight Group, a firm that provides clients with services in screening and assessing for high-potential job candidates. His firm places a strong emphasis on both research and practice, using only those methods and selection tools that have proven their worth in identifying employee potential.

In 2001, he received his Master’s degree in Industrial/Organizational Psychology from the University of Waterloo, since which time he has consulted for organizations of all sizes, from three-person start-ups to large multinational corporations. This has provided a substantial base of practical experience and a keen understanding of the problems associated with the current state of recruitment and selection.

Hire Insight was established to help companies overcome these problems and to meet the need for an more objective approach to hiring. The result was a series of systematic programs that provide clients with in-depth information on candidate fit, ability and potential, with consideration for the different timelines and budgets required of various types of organizations.

For more information, please visit www.hireinsightgroup.com

Chad is also a:

- Member of the Human Resources Institute of Alberta (HRIA) Editorial Commitee.
- Featured human resources expert on Focus (http://www.focus.com/groups/human-resources/).
- Writer for CanadaOne (http://www.canadaone.com), an online publication that provides advice and resources to small and medium-sized businesses in Canada.

Chad Hayward’s Specialties:

sourcing, assessment, testing, development, coaching, report-writing, feedback, job analysis, assessment center administration, behavior-based interviewing, design of selection systems.
