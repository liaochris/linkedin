Charles Seymour Jr’s Summary

Charles Seymour Jr.
Online Marketing Specialist / Community Builder with Families In Mind

Founder/Work At Home Dad: UltimateWorkAtHomeDads.com
President/CEO: SMO Marketing LLC
Founder/Blogger: RaisingGreatFamilies.com
Producer/Chief Marketing Officer: LizSeymour.com (and her music CD)
Founder/Raconteur: ILearnedItAllFromMyKids.com
Founder/Father: FathersAndTheDaughtersTheyLove.com




Charlie is featured in the new books, "Dream Inc," about 32 Philadelphia-area entrepreneurs and their million dollar secrets to success and "The Ultimate Success Secret" as a top Web 2.0 marketing specialist. He is a marketer with a heart for the arts and education, children, families, and how they all tie together... and has an eye for how to tell their story to captivate other people.

Charlie is no stranger to thinking big. He honed his skills as a business marketer and entrepreneur after earning his MBA from The Wharton School in 1975. He sold $247,368,657.97 as a salesman and marketed more than $1 billion as the Vice President of Marketing for Jackson-Cross Company. His follow-up position as Senior Vice President of International Marketing at the architectural firm, The Hillier Group, further added to his business strengths.

He founded Torn Ticket at Tufts University where he earned his BA in Econ/Music and was a founder of Wharton and the Arts at the University of Pennsylvania. He has also studied Dan Kennedy's Direct Response Marketing and is a Gold Level Member of the Insiders Circle.

He is a frequent speaker and author about The Art of Business and the Business of the Arts. Perhaps you read about him in the National Speakers Associations Speaker Magazine.

His personal portal site is http://CharlesSeymourJr.com.
