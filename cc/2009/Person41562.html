Kirsty Wilson’s Summary

I am a four year trained Primary School teacher and had a career change back in 1992. I have worked within small business for the past 18 years and held positions such as receptionist, QA Officer, Personal Assistant, Executive Assistant, Office Manager and the most senior role as Business Manager with a Tax/Accounting firm.

Interim Business Solutions was established during 2005 when I wished to create a better work-life balance for myself and my family.

Many of the jobs I assist clients with are project based and I'm able to undertake them in my home office. This also provides lots of variety and challenges which I always crave.

My goals are to continue to be a valuable resource for my clients when they require assistance with their business administration.
