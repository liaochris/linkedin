Shannon Thaw’s Summary

Shannon is a Health & Wellness Coach with degrees in Holistic Healthcare and Natural Health (2009).

She successfully integrates her background in herbalism, natural health, and personal and spiritual development to work together with Moms in identifying their unique path to a healthy, holistic and more fulfilling lifestyle.

Along with one-on-one coaching sessions, Shannon teaches the Holistic Living Series, Intuitive Development, and Aromatherapy classes.

Shannon is a member of Holistic Moms Network, a national organization devoted to supporting moms who make healthy choices for their families. She is the founder of Twin Cities Holistic Connections, an online social network of holistic people in the greater Twin Cities area and hosts a radio show of the same name on BlogTalkRadio.com.

When she is not busy doing all of these things, she loves spending time with her husband and two boys, learning new things, reading books and traveling!

Shannon Thaw’s Specialties:

Holistic coaching, herbalism, diet transititioning, meditation, stress reduction, dis-ease prevention.
