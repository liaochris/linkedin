Craig Hepburn’s Summary

Over the past ten years I have developed an exceptional understanding of Internet, Intranet, Multimedia Technologies and how to apply it to maximum effect for business. I am a focused leader, self-starter with the ability to adapt quickly to new environments and the ability to communicate at various levels. I have extensive knowledge of web2.0 technologies and have been involved in developing social media and product strategies for our global brand. I am passionate about innovations, web / mobile technology and human computer interaction. I have headed up many large ecommerce and Intranet projects for Rentokil Initial, BAA and STA Travel over the past ten years. I enjoy pushing the boundaries of what's possible and developing cutting edge technology concepts and seeing these through to fruition.

I have been invited to speak at many travel and technology events over the past couple of years on social media, online travel, web2.0 and more recently virtual worlds and second life where i was involved in concepting and managing the worlds first virtual travel branch. I have extensive knowledge in the Google technology suit and managed to utilise these to develop many project collaboration processes.
