Paul Peters’s Summary

Near to eighteen years working experience of which some twelve years in Information Technology. During these years acted in a variety of roles such as Information Analyst, Project Manager, Management Consultant and Chief Architect.
Broad international experience throughout the European Union, since 1999 engaged in some 50-60 projects in 18 different countries.

Main areas of expertise are Integration, EAI, BPM, SOA, B2B, BPR, ERP, Collaborative Commerce, Strategic Consulting and Change Management.

Innovative generalist, combining pragmatism with a creative urge and passion for excellence with both a bird's eye and detailed view on business context and technological means into enterprise scale solution architectures.
Since eight years focused on design, implementation and management of integration and collaboration based solutions.
