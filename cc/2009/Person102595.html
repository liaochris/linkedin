Bryce Vickmark’s Summary

Some of my professional aspirations as a photographer include covering a campaign for President of the United States and the White House, working regularly for a major news weekly, photographing extended documentary photo essays about domestic and international social issues, shooting more environmental portraits of real people and continuing to be able to feed myself using my camera (without having to hit prey with it).

I invite you to view my work on my website at www.vickmark.com. If you like what you see, please consider linking to my site from yours or your blog. Thanks a lot!

Clients/work has appeared in New Republic Magazine, The New York Times, the World Picture Network, Zuma Press, Sipa Press, People Magazine, Science et Vie (FR), Focus (GE), L’Hebdo (CH), Countdown Magazine, eServer Magazine, Mulit-Family Executive, Builder Magazine, Replacement Contractor Magazine, Developer Magazine, Tennis Magazine, Lacrosse Magazine, Le Monde Newspaper (FR), The Sunday Mirror (UK), Offshore Magazine, Federal Computer Week Magazine, Yale University Alumni Magazine and The Connecticut Law Tribune.

Bryce Vickmark’s Specialties:

I specialize in the following types of people photography. Editorial, environmental portraiture, documentary, political, commercial, sports and street.
