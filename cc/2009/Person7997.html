David Lanphear’s Summary

I'm an art director and graphic designer with signficant experience in daily delivery of print production and graphic communication. These are vital roles to your company's internal and external message, so please add my skills and experience to your team. You'll rely on my unique imagination, seasoned graphic communication experience, and my playful sense of humor; plus my mastery of toolsets Adobe Creative Suite, Font Lab Suite, Maya, storyboarding, polished illustration and audience-capturing delivery, all tempered by my passion to get your projects done right and on-time, even when there are multiple target dates. With me, you'll find the adage is true: You want something done, give it to a busy person. As a director, I recognize that the team comes first, so it's important to drop unnecessary tasks, delay time-wasting grinds, delegate auxiliary work to accomplished teammates, and finish the critical ASAP.

I have a 23-year career strongly rooted in elemental storytelling, ranging through newspaper illustration, award-winning editorial cartooning, comic book illustration, industry-praised comic book production, award-winning digital lettering, best-selling font creation, storyboard art, art directing, and recently, freelance production arts, and talent coordination for many media outlets. I continue to seek situations to employ and improve my storytelling and illustration crafts, and to evangelize on sequential art's power and eloquence.

David Lanphear’s Specialties:

Art direction, design, talent coordination, technical print production, pre-press troubleshooting, expert vector illustration, expert hand-lettering skills, expert font manipulation, expert font creation, expert knowledge of software Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Acrobat, Adobe InDesign, Font Lab Studio, Macromedia Fontographer.
