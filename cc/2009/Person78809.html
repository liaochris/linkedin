Peter Kolb’s Summary

Experienced postdoctoral level research scholar with expertise in fragment-based virtual high-throughput screening and in silico drug design on multiple targets. Author of the computer program DAIM, which is used in fragment-based drug design.

Peter Kolb’s Specialties:

LIE models, programming, computer-aided drug design, chemoinformatics, fragments, fragment generation, DAIM
