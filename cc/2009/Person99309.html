TJ Goulet’s Summary

Sales and marketing professional with 23 years of sales, sales management and business owner experience including owning and selling 7 succesfull companies.

Currently CMO & Co-Founder of Bythezip.com Directory, Inc.

Bythezip.com Directory, Inc. was founded in 2005 and is headquartered in Malvern, PA; with offices in West Chester PA, Princeton NJ and Bethesda MD. We currently serve the Greater Philadelphia Metro, Washington DC Metro and North Jersey areas. Bythezip.com is one of the fastest growing web solutions companies in the Mid-Atlantic Region adding over 2,000 clients in the last 2 years. We currently offer the following products and services through Bythezip.com and BTZWeb.com: Online Directory, Customer Loyalty Programs, Website Design and Development, Search Engine Optimization, Graphic Design, Corporate Identity and Branding, Newsletter/Email Marketing and Website Hosting Services.
