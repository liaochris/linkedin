Ray Heffer’s Summary

I have been working within the IT industry since 1997, including the public sector, government and commercial environments. My extensive experience is backed up by my VMWare VI3 VCP, CPSA and MCSE certifications and the passion I have for technology.

My current role is Technical Infrastructure Manager and previous roles have included Senior Technical Consultant, Data Centre Manager for Civica, and Network Manager for a large housing association.

I would best describe myself as a senior technical specialist, with an excellent approach to team leadership and lead management of technical projects. My main area of expertise is virtualization and server infrastructure (VMware, Linux and Microsoft technologies), including storage solutions (Datacore, EMC and HP).

• Infrastructure management; Team manager and project lead, focused on ITIL v3 methodologies.
• VMware Virtual Infrastructure: Technical lead and award winning success with server consolidation project in VI3. 55% physical server reduction and 37% power consumption saving.
• SAN Solutions: DataCore, EMC and HP storage specialisations. Successful migration from EMC to DataCore including VMware, Exchange and SQL clusters.
• Web hosting solution: Technical lead for infrastructure design of highly resilient government web portal for large data collection application. Benefiting from Zeus ZXTM VA, SQL 2005 cluster, and VMware VI3 virtual infrastructure.
• ISP enterprise technologies: Lead consultancy and technical authority at ISP for Redhat Linux; BIND, LAMP web hosting, Perl scripting. Complimented by Cisco network and firewall support, hosting solutions and customer product design.
• Microsoft technologies: Senior consultant for Microsoft technical solutions. Active Directory, Exchange 2003, ISA 2004 / 2006, SQL, and clustered / NLB solutions.
