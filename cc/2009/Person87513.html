Ryan Schmelz’s Summary

Ryan Schmelz is a dynamic senior executive and consultant with over 20 years of experience. His employment history includes key positions in software and information services, technology, financial services, market research, and legal services organizations.
Mr. Schmelz is a team builder, experienced in leading large and small organizations and varied functional units, including marketing, sales, systems development, finance, operations, administration, human resources, and legal. He has led IT organizations of over 300 and managed operating budgets in excess of $50MM. He has supported organizations with over 15,000 users worldwide and worked with organizations of less than 5 people.
His management consulting experience encompasses strategy, restructuring / M&A, outsourcing, organization design, turnaround management, business process reengineering, information technology management and litigation support / electronic data discovery.
