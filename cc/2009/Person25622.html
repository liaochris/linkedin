Deirdre Spencer’s Summary

Deirdre Spencer’s life-long love of fine art coupled with her interest in computers and media made graphic design a natural career path. Since building her first corporate website in 1996, her web design has won numerous awards, including four prestigious Horizon Interactive Awards since 2005. She enjoys the ever-evolving challenge of fusing great design with innovative web technology. Equally proficient in both print design (from concept to prepress) and web design (from site architecture to XHTML and CSS coding), Deirdre’s impressive portfolio includes work for clients such as the American Iron and Steel Institute, the Oakland International Airport, The Berkshire Museum, Manex Visual Effects, McRoskey Mattress Company and Taproot Foundation.

Deirdre Spencer’s Specialties:

Printed marketing collateral and website design and development: site architecture, wire frames, XHTML, CSS, experience working with Javascript, ASP, PHP, and Drupal; film output, pre-press. Software: Mac OSX and Windows platforms; Photoshop, ImageReady, Freehand, Fireworks, Acrobat, Illustrator, Dreamweaver, HTML editors, InDesign, QuarkXPress, and more.
