Erin Brown Bell’s Summary

Eclectic writer with over a decade of professional experience. Some career highlights include interviewing Michigan Governor Jennifer Granholm, meeting the United States "Drug Czar" John Walters, and hanging out with former Detroit Mayor Dennis Archer.

Working as a writer at FOX 2 News was pretty exciting as well.

I'm most proud of the feature articles I've written for The Oakland Press and some of the ads I've done for HOUR Detroit.
