Dana Rice’s Summary

Currently on sabbatical from Seattle City Light.

Current projects include classes and workshops at the Actorsite in Los Angeles, CA.

Less recent projects include music recording, theatre and movie projects. Played the title role in "Lysistrata" and a featured role as a heavy-metal-clad administrator in "The Day My Parents Became Cool". Playing with the band "Blood & Biscotti". Rehearsing and recording monologues and show tunes for audition material, and singing them with friends. Collaborating with musician friends on tricks of the trade with web services for performers. Assisting neighborhood organizations with community outreach.
