Patrick Hatfield’s Summary

Advertising Senior Art Director with broad experience in traditional and emerging media. My most recent industry focus has been international automotive market retail performance solutions. I also have an extensive range of experience working with the following industries/sectors; retail, healthcare, DoD (department of defense/military), printing, utility, B to B, electronics, consumer products, education, sports, aviation, entertainment, financial, furniture and professional services.

Within BBDO Detroit, PCG, Dziurman Dzign, Marvel Entertainment, etc. I have gained a wide range of experiences, skills and knowledge resulting in a versitle bag of award-winning work. I have managed groups of creative individuals with a key focus on delivering the most creative, impactful, targeted award-winning results. Continually keeping my skills and knowledge honed in most industry standard applications as well as traditional media profeciencies, I am able to be extremely flexable when working with teams of varied skill levels and understanding.

My team building profeciencies and business acumen have allowed me to bring out the highest levels of those that I work with and a therough understanding from the client perspective at the achieveable results.
