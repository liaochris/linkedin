Natan Jacobs’s Summary

Natan Jacobs is a co-founder of Vestis Group and serves as Designated Broker for the firm. During the course of his career, Jacobs has sold a variety of types of transactions including industrial, retail, land, investments, condominium conversions, portfolio sales, and the sale of over 3,500 units of multi-family housing throughout Arizona and Texas, totaling over $200 million. In addition, he has been instrumental in developing and building three residential subdivisions including 42 single family homes. Jacobs holds an Arizona Real Estate Broker’s license. Jacobs lives in Phoenix with his wife, Larel, and their son, Kellan, and is active in multiple professional, civic, and youth organizations.
