Ben Grossman’s Summary

Hi there. I'm a passionate, directed, and fresh advertising, marketing, and social media strategist and enthusiast. I have a wide range of experience working within agencies and for private clients: from pitch, to research, to strategy, to creative executions, and everywhere in between.

I’ve collaborated with a number of marketing and advertising agencies to develop campaign strategy for clients, educate agency staff about my areas of specialty, and review and augment agency practices and potential. I also work privately with a roster of clients to create and coordinate execution of integrated marketing communications plans, review opportunities, train staff, and optimize business viability.

I love what I do. I’m passionate about my work and am committed to a high level of quality, strict attention to detail, and a wider vision for the future.
