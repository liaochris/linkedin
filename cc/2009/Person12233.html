Bob McCarthy’s Summary

An accomplished high tech marketing executive, I have successfully marketed on-demand (SaaS) products, Internet services, and enterprise software for more than 10 years.

I currently specialize in providing marketing and product services to leading SaaS companies, as well as advising business organizations on the selection and deployment of SaaS services.

Previously, I was VP of marketing at Boomerang, a SaaS provider of online marketing services. There I played a key role in transforming the company from its traditional base into a software-as-a-service SaaS) provider. I led product management and product marketing at the company.

Before Boomerang, I led product management and product marketing at a national broadband provider and at a national ISP. In each case, I rolled out new services and products that achieved significant results in the business market.

Earlier, I worked at IBM and led a product marketing team that delivered innovative new products to the midrange market.

My immediate goal is to successfully establish Austin Marketers as a premiere product consulting organization for the software industry.

Bob McCarthy’s Specialties:

Market research, market strategy, new markets, competitive strategy, vendor evaluation, customer segmentation, product assessment, and retention marketing.
