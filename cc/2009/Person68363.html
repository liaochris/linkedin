    Business Approach

    Mike’s approach is to bring together a team of professionals to provide you with the total financial solutions you require to help reach your overall financial objectives.

    Mike believes that successful investing means not only capturing risks that generate expected returns but also reducing the risks that do not. Avoidable risks include holding too few securities, betting on countries or industries, following market predictions, and speculating on "information" from rating services. To all these, diversification is the antidote. It washes away the random fortunes of individual stocks and positions your portfolio to capture the returns of broad economic forces.
