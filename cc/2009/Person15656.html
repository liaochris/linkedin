Carol Cox’s Summary

Follow me on Twitter at http://www.twitter.com/civiclink

I am currently teaching at Full Sail University in Winter Park as the Course Director for the Internet Business Models course as well as the Website Advertising & Design Principles course in the Internet Marketing Bachelor of Science degree program.

I am co-founder of DemsLink.com and past chair of the Seminole County Democratic Party in Florida. My company provides technology, online marketing, and media consulting services to Democratic candidates and organizations. Check out some of our videos at www.demslink.com/videos.asp.

I am also president of InterMedia Solutions, an Internet Marketing and Technology company. I am an experienced database developer and programmer, specializing in Microsoft technologies. I am a Microsoft Certified Technology Specialist in Windows SharePoint Services 3.0 and SharePoint (MOSS) 2007. I have managed and developed projects for such Fortune 500 companies as Office Depot and Lowe's as well as for well-known brands like Popeye's, Church's Chicken, and Cinnabon.

Before moving to Central Florida in 2003, I lived in Atlanta, where I was active in the technology community, often giving presentations at meetings and conferences. I served as an officer of SERIS, the SouthEast Regional Internet Society, as Vice President for Tech Challenges. I was also responsible for the development of Techlanta, a membership and event notification Web site for Atlanta non-profit groups.

Prior to founding InterMedia, I worked in college administration, including implementing financial aid software for a private four-year college. I hold a Master of Arts degree from Emory University, and I graduated Phi Beta Kappa from the University of Florida with a Bachelor of Arts degree. I was born and raised in Palm Beach County, Florida.
