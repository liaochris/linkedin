Marjorie Geiser’s Summary

It is Margie's goal that all health and fitness professionals live their dream of starting and running an amazing business. Many of these people imagine owning their own business, but allow fears, doubts and limiting conversations hold them back.

She is a Registered Dietitian, a Certified Personal Trainer and a Life Coach educated through CTI. She has owned MEG Fitness since June, 1996 and began offering coaching services to other health professionals in 2003. In 2006, she developed MEG Enterprises, Inc, in order to expand her business start-up coaching services. In 2005, she returned to school for an MBA in Entrepreneurship in order to be of even more value to her clients. In 2008, Margie started California Based Publishing in order to publish her book, "Just Jump", which will be released in July, 2008.

Today Margie walks her business clients through a process that helps them discover who they love to work with, what makes them shine above their competition, how to present themselves and their services to others, and then walks them through the intimidating process of creating a business plan, a marketing plan, and how to put it all together for a rewarding business AND life.

Margie also finds that many of her business coaching clients, even though they are health professionals, also need the support of a caring coach to help them achieve health goals, either through improved dietary habits or fitness habits.
