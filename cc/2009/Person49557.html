John Richau’s Summary

HERS rater focused on California's New Solar Homes Partnership and helping solar companies comply with the program through training, testing, and compliance management.

We also perform diagnostic testing and analysis of existing single family homes. Our goal is to help homeowners make the best energy decisions based on objective third-party testing and analysis.

John Richau’s Specialties:

NSHP Compliance and training of sales staff for small to large solar contractors, Home Energy Ratings using the only California Energy Commission approved software: RateTool, Energy Efficient Mortgage certification, California Energy Star Home certification, SCE's California New Homes Program (CANHP) certification, and PG&E's New Solar Homes Partnership Performance method certification.
