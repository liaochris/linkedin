I'm currently working on the following Rio Vista (www.riovista.net) projects:

1) The New Jersey Health Care Center (www.njhcc.com) is an 84,000 sq. ft. active medical facility located at 680 Kinderkamack Road in Oradell, New Jersey. The facility will include medical offices, an ambulatory surgery center, a diagnostic imaging center, and an urgent care center.

4) Rio Vista Realty (www.riovistanj.com) is a full-service real estate brokerage company serving Northern, NJ. Our goal is to provide the highest quality realty services through a diversified team of highly knowledgeable licensed professionals.
