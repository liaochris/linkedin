David Mascarina’s Summary

Over ten years of experience in Web Production, which includes Senior Designer at Pasadena City College and Telepictures Productions. Garnered accolades from Los Angeles County, Pasadena City College and Macromeda (now Adobe).

Currently, Web Designer for the Los Angeles Zoo and Botanical Gardens, Web Producer for California State University, Northridge and Creative Director for DGM Seven.
