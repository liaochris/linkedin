Rick Sheahan’s Summary

STRATEGIC ALLIANCE & REAL ESTATE PROFESSIONAL

I am a continual student, respected instructor, and award-winning licensed Realtor in Kansas and Missouri, 12-year veteran real estate investor, landlord, and property consultant. I work closely with homebuyers, sellers, investors & lenders, as well as mentoring other agents, buying and remodeling houses for retail sale, and helping real estate investors from all over the United States develop their Kansas City portfolios.

I have nearly 25 years invested with major corporations in positions of engineering, project management, computer system design and implementation, accounting, and on merger and acquisition teams.

“Rick leaves nothing to chance. One of the most detail-minded Realtor I've worked with. I think of Rick, when I hear the word professional.”
-Pat Brady, Senior Mortgage Consultant, Capitol Federal Savings, May 27, 2008

I am an active member of many organizations and the 2010 President of SHBC (Small & Home Business Connection). I am passionate about building business through networking and development of strategic partnerships.

Core Advocates Strategic Alliance workshop - The Power of R3 – Reputation x Resources x Relationships = Results. The purpose of networking is to establish relationships. During an R3 workshop, you’ll decide if you are happy with the results you are getting in your line of work and review your past relationships and resources to see what can change to improve your results.
