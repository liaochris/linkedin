    (Public Company; BBC; Broadcast Media industry)

    October 2002 — Present (7 years 2 months)

    Responsible for providing a first class consultative recruitment service to the BBC World Service Spanish & Americas Division and the World Service Trust.

    The Span Am Division covers BBC offices across Latin America, Russia, Brazil, Ukraine etc.

    The BBC World Service Trust is the independent, international charity set up by the BBC. It uses media and communications to reduce poverty and promote human rights, thereby enabling people to build better lives. In any given year the Trust engages and interacts with more than 100 million people - in their local languages - in over 40 countries. It does so by creating innovative media campaigns - on television, radio, online, mobiles and village outreach. Our five focus areas are: Health, Learning and Livelihoods, Governance and Human Rights, Humanitarian Response and Environment.

    The charity is funded by grants from international development donors such as the Department for International Development (DFID), the European Union, UN Agencies and US Foundations.
