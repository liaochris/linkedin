Nick Caruso’s Summary

My main work is to help support public schools by providing training and other expertise to help locally elected boards of education provide the very best education they can. I have worked with over 80% of the boards in Connecticut and have presented on a variety of topics at numerous state and national conferences. I also believe strongly in supporting 21st Century Skills to America's public schools and work diligently in supporting technology use in schools. I also am responsible for supervising our office technology.

Nick Caruso’s Specialties:

I served as a board of education member from 1983-93 and was First Vice-President for CABE, which gave me some involvement with the National School Boards Association. Those experiences help me bring clarity and focus to boards and help them through difficult times.
