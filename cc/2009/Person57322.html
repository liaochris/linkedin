Lance Cooper’s Summary

• For 25 years managed, trained, and coached sales teams, sales managers, and business owners needing greater sales production.

• Installed new systems: people, processes (sales plans, activity management, face-to-face skills), tools, and skills to help small entrepreneurial and billion dollar companies achieve sales goals.

• Coached sales managers and sales teams, over a 6 month or 10 year time span, to adopt new habits and behaviors leading to better time management and increased sales goal achievement

• Worked for six years as an independent sales consultant for Integrity Training Systems and was #1 or #2 in North American sales revenue for sales of The Customer or Integrity Selling.

• Authored "The Greatest Salespeople in the World Sell with CLASS," "Recuit and Coach the Best," and "The Roots of Greatness: Extraordinary Service."
