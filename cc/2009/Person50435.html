Jools Watsham’s Summary

Jools Watsham brings over 16 years of video-game industry experience to Renegade Kid having contributed to the development of 32 published titles, including Moon, Dementium: The Ward, and NBA Jam for major publishers, such as Mastiff, Gamecock Media Group, Namco, Data East, Fisher-Price, and Sunsoft. An experienced Game Director, Art Director, Lead Designer, Lead Artist, and Producer, Watshams' objective is to develop high-quality entertainment products, enjoyed by games players and critics alike.

Jools Watsham’s Specialties:

Game Director, Producer, Artist, Composer, and Audio Designer.
