Lars Fløe Nielsen’s Summary

I work as a technical exec with broad experience competitive analysis, technical market trends, market strategies and technical brand development. I have extensive experience with building and forming strategic alliances with major partners such as Microsoft.

As one of the founders of Sitecore, I have been directly involved in building software, branding it and forming a global reseller and partner chain, growing a company from nothing to a significant WCMS player with global presence.
