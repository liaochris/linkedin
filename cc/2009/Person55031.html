Kevin J. Furst’s Summary

Through out college you could find me in the design studio until the wee hours of the night designing and re-designing projects until I got them just right. At times an occasional trip to the bar was necessary, for inspirational purposes only of course. I would describe myself as an enthusiastic, self-motivated designer and activist.

I focus my personal work on design that advocates awareness and change that leaves the viewer to take action towards making a difference. This "call to action" has inspired thousands and is what originally inspired me to become a designer. My dream job is to work for National Geographic or own my own design studio with a few friends from college. Eventually I will hopefully find myself in the Peace Corps once some of the student loans are paid off.

Some of my work has been internationally published and recognized in a wide variety of blogs, trade magazines and books. I have had the pleasure of working for companies large and small but I am always drawn back to projects that have an impact.

Outside of the "office," I like to spend my time enjoying the outdoors, biking, hiking and exploring with my camera. I also enjoys good coffee, cooking, finding new places to eat, wine tasting and reading. I also enjoy the cold side of the pillow, MSNBC, medium-well steak and the Phillies.
