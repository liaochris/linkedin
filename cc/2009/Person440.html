Aaron Bramley’s Summary

Aaron grew up in Tucson, Arizona where he was a dedicated student and avid musician. He went to New York University and graduated in 2006. While living in Manhattan, Aaron studied politics and linguistics. During the summers he would intern with Ridgewood Associates Public Relations, The Southern Arizona Office of the Governor, or The South Bronx Concerned Citizens.

After college, Aaron moved back to sunny Tucson to work for Ridgewood full time. He continued to develop his communication skills and also received on-the-job training for audio and video production.

In July, 2008, Aaron moved to Austin, TX where he works remotely for Ridgewood.

He held a volunteer position as the Director of Communications for the Austin Museum of Digital Art and an is now in the process of starting a film festival exclusively for nonprofit and grassroots organizations called Lights. Camera. Help.
