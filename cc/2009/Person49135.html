    NET(net) Inc. specializes in Vendor Contract Lifecycle Management and Optimization Services and is recognized as an Industry Leader, providing a full range of technology consulting services to businesses and governmental entities throughout the United States and the world. NET(net) helps companies review, analyze and optimize their core supplier partnerships and empowers them with professional services designed to help reduce costs, improve quality, mitigate risk and strengthen contractual agreements. NET(net) has a proven history of helping clients optimize information technology investments while enhancing customer-supplier relationships and promoting business partnerships.

    As Chief Strategy Officer, John Fisher leads the client services practice area and all strategic initiatives for NET(net), which helps client organizations optimize their technology investments, maximize the effectiveness of strategic technologies and foster decisions that better enable the business of IT.
