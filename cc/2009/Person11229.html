Bill Albing’s Summary

Technical communication specialist with engineering background and over 15 years of experience working alongside developers, engineers, inventors, entrepreneurs. Developed documentation systems, designed web sites and intranet sites, worked with online help systems, authoring tools, and structured data. Exemplary writing skills are also part of the mix. Proponent of social networking as a basis for collaborative communication.

Bill Albing’s Specialties:
