Braun Mincher’s Summary

Still in his early 30’s, Braun Mincher has been a successful entrepreneur for over 19 years in a diverse variety of successful businesses.

A highly driven and motivated individual, he was a self-made millionaire before the age of 25 and able to "retire" a multi-millionaire before 30, despite dropping out of college to grow his first business. His accomplishments have earned him the prestigious Ernst & Young / Inc. Magazine “Entrepreneur of the Year” award and repeat appearances on national TV, including the NBC “Today Show” and with Neil Cavuto on the FOX Business Channel, in addition to frequently being quoted in numerous business publications.

After the sale of his telecom company in 2004 (CTI), Braun became a passionate financial literacy advocate and has worked tirelessly to get basic financial education classes integrated into the high school curriculum and also made a graduation requirement. He has authored an award-winning book on the subject and was the executive producer of a documentary movie exposing the state of financial illiteracy in America.

Braun is an energetic, charismatic and knowledgeable professional public speaker to groups of all sizes on most entrepreneurial and personal finance topics. He is an FAA licensed aircraft pilot (land & sea) who enjoys flying (he has a Cessna 182), boating / wakeboarding, SCUBA diving and traveling both domestically and internationally, recently returning from business trips to South Africa and India. In a previous life, Braun was also a formula racecar driver.

With the recent expiration of his non-compete agreement, Braun's next venture is a return to his telecom roots with the nationwide launch of a new and revolutionary "hosted" business phone system platform, leveraging the power of technology (VoIP) to provide better functionality and cost savings to businesses during these challenging economic times. Stay tuned for details or check out Braun's websites for more information!

  • President / Consultant

    PCG Telecom Group, Inc.

    (Telecommunications industry)

    June 2008 — Present (1 year 6 months)

    PCG is a telecommunications firm that I co-founded in 1996. With the recent expiration of my non-compete agreement, I will be re-taking the helm of PCG in the summer of 2009. Our customer-centric company serves 2 business niches with our unique divisions:

    CONSULTING - Our signature offering is a contingency-based "bill auditing and analysis" service of business telecom bills. We specialize in working with corporations that spend at least $2,500 per month on the combined cost of Local Phone Service, Long Distance, WAN Network (Internet, T1's, MPLS, etc.) and Wireless Cellular Phones. Generally, our SMB clients are big enough to spend significantly on telecom, but not quite big enough to have their own telecom manager on staff -- that's where PCG comes in. Our goal is to help clients do everything related to telecom better, faster and cheaper (e.g. more efficiently) and our unique consulting approach creates an alignment of interest with our valued clients.

    HOSTED PHONE SYSTEMS (PBX) - The days of purchasing, depreciating and maintaining a "premise-based" business phone system are numbered. By creatively leveraging new technology properly (VoIP), we now offer a "hosted" phone system platform for business customers with 2 to 2,000 employees, geographically disbursed anywhere in the country or around the world. Benefits are NO capital investment; just a monthly cost of about $50 per user which includes the phone set, local phone lines and ALL outbound long distance. We think that we have built a better mouse trap (so to speak...) and really enjoy sharing the business case for this new phone system solution with others.
