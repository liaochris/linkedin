Geordie Barker’s Summary

As the Managing Director of my own company (TTEC) and a freelance in the Theatre and Corporate Events Industry, I am skilled in a wide range of areas. Lighting Design, Sound Design, Production, Stage and Technical Management are my main areas of expertise, calling upon my training at the Victorian College of the Arts, and experience in many areas of the industry. With strong technical knowledge and a good planning ability, I am able to call upon industry specialists to work together with artists and the client to achieve a collective vision. My work has included, Fahion Launches, Multi Stage Multi Artist Installations, Theatre Productions, Corporate Functions and Launches, Audio Visual Installations, Weddings and Private Parties, Group Collaborations, and more. I have also worked as a Voice Over Artist. I am always ready to push the boundaries of technology, and enjoy a challenge. I enjoy work in all areas of the industry and i am able to recommend professionals within the industry to assist in the areas of Costume, Design, Workshop, Event Power, and Technology. Please feel free to contact me and have a chat regarding how i can assist you in your next production or event. I am always willing to have a chat regarding new technology or ideas. Regards, Geordie

Lighting Design, Sound Design, Production Management, Event Management, Technical Management and Direction, Event Power, Health and Safety, Rigging, Architectural Lighting, Emergency Lighting, Sound System Design, Permanent Installations, Sound Recording and Mastering, Voice Overs, Theatre, Corporate Events

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Internet industry)

    February 2008 — Present (1 year 6 months)

    MiWiFi was started in March of 2008 by two arts students, Geordie Barker and Edward Dowling. The two were frustrated at the lack of available, affordable wireless internet access in the Melbourne CBD and surrounds.

    After almost two months of research on the technology, the market, the competition and the developing face of the internet, Geordie and Ed ‘bit the bullet’ so to speak and started MiWiFi with the aim to spread affordable, efficient wireless internet throughout Melbourne. Utilizing the new and developing technology of Mesh Networking, Geordie and Ed hope to reach their dream.

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Entertainment industry)

    As Production Designer for the Spring Fashion Launch of leading Melbourne Fashion Design Firm Decjuba, I was responsible for the areas of Lighting and Sound Design, Staging, Stage Management, and Operation. These recordings, recorded both locally and overseas, were an essential part of the atmosphere of the event. The soundscape was mixed along with client selected music, through the main speaker system and the audience response was very positive. The space was lit effectively through the use of up lighting and cool blue industrial colours.
