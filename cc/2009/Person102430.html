Verena Visser Aibel’s Summary

Verena Visser Aibel, 51, a Business & Life Coach, is Founder of Vibrant Visions International LLC, a Global Coaching and Consulting Enterprise, and has over 25 years experience assisting Edgewalkers, Trailblazers, Leaders, Executives, Business Owners/Entrepreneurs, and Individuals Worldwide. Affectionately known as The Top Ten Coach and Writer, Verena's articles have appeared in over 260 publications and have reached an estimated 800,000 subscribers worldwide. Verena is also an Avid Speaker, Workshop and Seminar Facilitator, Teleclasses Leader and Retreat Facilitator. She is a Certified Graduate of Coach University (CTP) and is currently a Professional Certified Coach (PCC) Candidate, a designation awarded by the International Coaching Federation (www.coachfederation.org). Verena is a Communication Studies with Honors Graduate from Pennsylvania State University. She is an active member of the ICF, the New Jersey Professional Coaches Association (www.NJcoaches.org), New Jersey Association of Women Business (www.njawbo.org), (www.NABBW.com), the Wellness Possibilities Network (www.wellnesspossibilities.com), and the Association for Higher Awareness. She is a Rotary Foundation Alumni to eastern Germany, and Founder, Global Women with Vision.

One client said about Verena, “Verena is a spirited, dynamic coach who thinks out of the box and yet is practical and down-to-earth. She listened carefully and seamlessly synthesized my needs, abilities, and gifts, and options. Her holistic process gave me a unique, individual, and gratifying experience.”
