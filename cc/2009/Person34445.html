Doc Kane’s Summary

Roscommon's team of professional writers possess decades of experience in a variety of fields including finance, IT, HR benefits, direct mail, pharma, biotech and FMCG. Whether you're in a crunch and need an extra writer to meet deadlines, or have an ongoing need, we can help with your internal messaging and customer-facing marketing communications goals. When you need a writer with a particular specialty, we're just a phone call away.

If it has words, we do it!

Doc Kane’s Specialties:

We help companies create high-value communications deliverables without the need for additional headcount. These include: marketing communications, technical writing, employee communications, benefit communications, internal communications, trade magazine writing, executive ghostwriting, sales letters, case studies, white papers, integrated marketing communications, direct mail, web content, copywriting and more.
