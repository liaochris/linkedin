Kristo Reinsalu’s Summary

I am a good strategist but at the same time I consider myself hard-working and passionately dedicated to my tasks. Daily I usually follow the principle that "devil is always in details". Regardless of the fact that I like to deal with many questions at the same time, problems will never pile up for me since I can always keep an eye on the big picture. This was an invaluable asset what I acquired while I was working in the Ministry of Economic Affairs and Communications as well as in the Ministry of Finance.

During last 5 years I have had really great honour to be one of the "pioneers" in Estonian Space Community to contribute to accession process to European Space Agency (ESA). It is still ongoing mission which has already offered for me valuable experiences in terms of managing nationwide space projects. Undoubtedly "Estonian Race for Space" will open us unprecedented and numerous (business) opportunities in coming years! Everybody, who is interested needs to stay tuned and eyes opened! Sky is not the limit anymore. Definitely it has`nt been and won`t be limit for me either!
