James Reda’s Summary

James F. Reda & Associates, LLC is located in Times Square in Manhattan. We are very experienced in working with Fortune-1000 companies in meeting their needs for executive compensation consulting services.

Our firm has extensive experience in the area of equity awards, compensation committee advisory services, incentive programs of all kinds and the performance evaluation and goal-setting process. We work with clients in the following industries: financial services, health-care, life-science, technology, retail and manufacturing. In addition, we have substantial experience with private companies.
