Eric Legault’s Summary

Eric has over 13 years experience in the IT industry, and has focused his energies developing solutions based on Microsoft application platforms such as Microsoft Office, SharePoint, SQL Server and Exchange.  Eric is the president of Collaborative Innovations (http://www.collaborativeinnovations.ca/), a Micro ISV and consulting services provider specializing on Microsoft messaging and collaboration solutions.  He holds certifications as a Microsoft Certified Database Administrator (MCDBA) and Microsoft Certified Technology Specialist (MCTS) for Messaging & Collaboration, SharePoint Infrastructure, WSS 3.0 Application Development and MOSS 2007 Application Development.  Eric has also published articles in MSDN, Office Online, Windows IT Pro magazine and various online technology newsletters, edited books on Outlook, SharePoint and Access, and maintains a blog on Outlook programming and SharePoint technologies (http://blogs.officezealot.com/legault). In 2008, Eric briefly took over the Internet’s #1 developer resource site for Outlook - OutlookCode.com (it's now back in Sue's much more capable hands!).  Eric is also engaged with technical communities as a co-founder of the Winnipeg SharePoint User Group and via speaking engagements at conferences around the globe.
