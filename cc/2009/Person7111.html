Antonio Fournier’s Summary

I provide business intelligence and analysis to multi-strategy investment Funds and Hedge Funds based in London, Geneva and New York with in total over $10 billion under management and investing in a wide range of sectors, keeping a very low profile when it comes to clients’ identity and strategy.

I also represent in Spain ESL & Network, a business intelligence consulting firm that provides confidential analysis, networking and intermediation to blue-chip corporations or industry leaders in sectors such as sovereign funds or state-owned multinationals, energy, construction, environment, tourism, consumer goods, media or creative and luxury industries. ESL & Network has offices in Paris, Beijing, Berlin, Brussels, Budapest, Frankfurt, Geneva, London, Madrid, Mexico, Milan, Moscow, New Delhi, New York, Rome, The Hague and Washington.

As a Public Affairs and strategic marketing and communication professional, my areas of expertise are in a wide range of sectors such as energy, construction, shipping, tourism, consumer goods and luxury industries. After leaving the Paris Graduate School of Commerce (ESCP 91) I worked closely for two years with Andree Putman, the worldwide known French designer. In 1994 I started International Communication & Marketing (ICM) and Globally, two strategic marketing consultancies. ICM became Spain’s leading agency in the fashion, luxury and cosmetic businesses, working for more than 8 years with clients such as Procter & Gamble (Vidal Sassoon, Hugo Boss, Pantene, Laura Biagiotti, Giorgio Beverly Hills, Max Factor, etc.), Beiersdorf BDF Nivea, Bourjois, Estee Lauder, Vivienne Westwood, LVMH-Parfums Givenchy, Madrid Fashion Week Pasarela Cibeles, Factory Outlet Stores, The Walt Disney Company, etc.

After 15 years of experience as a top leading executive consultant in strategic management, I was offered to enter Spain and Latin America’s nº1 PR firm as non-executive Vice President and member of its advisory board.
