Scott Schaefer’s Summary

Three-time National Emmy Award winner (BILL NYE THE SCIENCE GUY, PBS/Disney) with over 23 years' experience as a Producer, Writer, Director and Marketing Manager in TV, multimedia, internet and technology.

I currently am seeking freelance opportunities as a Copywriter, Director, Producer or Blogger in comedic, advertising/marketing, corporate or educational.

Nominated for a 2005 Directors Guild Award for Outstanding Directing in a Reality Series for Showtime's "Penn & Teller:Bullsh*t!"

Demo reel and full credits available at http://www.scottso.net

Most recently I've been developing some websites, including a series of local blogs for the south Seattle area: http://www.b-townblog, http://www.whitecenterblog, http://www.tukwilablog.com, http://www.seatacblog and http://www.normandyparkblog.com.

Have also developed http://www.crapolicious.com, which showcases the "absolute worst of the web," and http://www.jokestarter.com, which is aimed at helping writers come up with joke and bit ideas.

In a nutshell, I'm an award-winning experienced TV Writer turned budding internet media mogul!
