Brent Kobayashi’s Summary

With over 20 years experience in the hospitality industry, Brent has a proven track record for delivering business results through strategic planning and operational initiatives.

Combing ten years of hotel operations management and ten years of training and human resources management, Brent has been able to successfully direct programs and initiatives that have assisted hotels in achieving some of the most distinguished awards in the hospitality industry, including the AAA Five Diamond Award and the Mobil Five Star Award.

He has worked for some of the top hotels in the US & Canada, including: Four Seasons Hotel Chicago, The Ritz-Carlton Chicago, Four Seasons Resort Aviara (North San Diego), and The Pan Pacific Hotel Vancouver.
