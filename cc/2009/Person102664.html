A message to Buyer's buying a personal property, Second/Third home buyers and Vacation home buyers:
A word about the "bottom" - Negotiate - Buyers want to wait and wonder if the market has hit "bottom". "Bottom" is a relative term; you only know its bottom when the market quickly rebounds with a vengeance - and the press won't know until it is too late. Even if you should pay a little more than you think you might at a later date, normal appreciation would make the difference irrelevant within a short period of time. Huge inventory + less competition + low mortgage interest rates + sellers willing to negotiate = sweet deals!
