Michael Stout’s Summary

English Language teacher who is occasionally creative, frequently passionate, and always skeptical of professional profiles loaded with adverbs and adjectives.
Experience in collaborative curriculum, syllabus, and test development. Active in the EFL community at the national level with the Japan Association of Language Teaching.
Focused on outreach. Interacting and collaborating with students both inside and outside the classroom. Interacting and collaborating with colleagues through projects including conference organisation, conference presentations, publishing, and materials development.
Fascinated by the possibilities afforded by web 2.0 technology.
Always looking for opportunities to do collaborative research.

Michael Stout’s Specialties:
