George Reed’s Summary

Over 37 years of experience serving the broadcasting and communications tower industries:

 Managing Director for Media Services Group (Radio and TV station brokerage, station valuations, workout asset management - trusts and receiverships, communications tower brokerage, and expert testimony).

 Station owner, Monticello Media in Charlottesville, VA, and MSG Radio (San Juan) with an emphasis on serving the local community with meaningful news, entertainment, information and public service. Monticello Media began operating WCYK-FM, WHTE-FM, WWTJ-FM, WZGN-FM, WKAV-AM, WCHV-AM, and related digital assets in September of 2007.

 Cell tower development and ownership in the southeast (USAntenna).

George@MediaServicesGroup.com

George Reed’s Specialties:

Radio and TV station brokerage. Station valuations. Workout asset management (trusts, receiverships, and CRO's), Station ownership. Cell and broadcast tower brokerage, development and ownership.
