Jonathan Petersen, MBA, CeM’s Summary

Relevant. Engaging. Valuable. Combine these consumer-focused elements throughout your integrated communication messages and you have a winning formula for public relations and marketing success. A lifelong learner, I strategically seek pivotal happenings, vital trends, leadership qualities, important statistics, and insightful articles, matching them with company objectives and products to provide consumers with self-actualizing benefits.

My more than 25 years of award-winning experience: local radio reporter and anchor, national news network editor, voice-over talent, entrepreneur, PR professional, columnist, seminar speaker, general and direct marketer, strategic marketer, and, since 1993, Internet marketer and social media marketer.

I'm the former (founding) religion news editor for UPI Radio Network in Washington, DC, responsible for anchoring daily national newscasts and producing news reports. Prior to UPI, I was news director at local radio stations in Connecticut and Minnesota. I am a professional narrator for published audiobooks. And I speak on the subjects of social media marketing and public relations (eg, to MBA students at Western Michigan University and to corporations).

Books that have shaped my career: Permission Marketing and Purple Cow by Seth Godin, The Tipping Point and Blink by Malcolm Gladwell, and Made to Stick by Chip Heath and Dan Heath.

I'm a certified eMarketer (eMA), a believer in Word Of Mouth marketing, an educator with The Council of Advisors, Gerson Lehrman Group, New York, and a member of the Online News Association (ONA), and the Public Relations Society of America (PRSA).

After 22 years with Zondervan serving in a variety of marketing capacities, my most recent position (Sr. Dir., Corporate & Internet Communications) was eliminated (along with more than 20 others) in a reorganization of the company. I’m believing God wants me somewhere else right now so I'm seeking other opportunities. Do you have one? Let's talk.
