Kenneth Patterson’s Summary

For the past eleven years I was employed at KTEH-TV - PBS for the Silicon Valley - most recently serving as the Broadcast Scheduling Manager, as well as the occasional stints in front of the camera and the odd piece of video editing.

I like to assemble things whether it's a schedule, a video, or a computer. I work well with deadlines of various durations, and am very technology friendly - not shying away from the changes in technology that affects whatever endeavor he is engaged in, in fact he looks forward to the benefits and challenges these changes pose.

Currently I am looking to move into the technology sector, either continuing to work with media or moving into testing and development.
