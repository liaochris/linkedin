Ann Crane’s Summary

Welcome to Meyerhof's Fine Catering, serving the greater Irvine area, Orange County, and beyond since 1970. I am Ann Crane, owner.

Whether it's corporate fine dining in Newport Beach, a special equestrian competition in Coto de Caza, or a spectacular wedding in Anaheim, we create the perfect event just for you!
As full service caterers, we not only prepare and deliver our fabulous foods to Huntington Harbor, Laguna Niguel and Lemon Heights, we provide the experienced serving staff and hardworking chefs to make your event happen. We plan your menu and deliver right to your event site whether you're in Yorba Linda or Ladera Ranch. It truly is that easy! We've already done a site inspection, so our staff works the plan to set the stage for the perfect event. The best part: we clean up afterwards. Your only job is to enjoy your own party.

We also work with our event partners to create the perfect setting:
Rentals—tables, chairs, linens, china, and cutlery
Florals—cut flowers, live plants, trees, and shrubbery
Design—lighting, décor, and color coordination
Beverage service—full bar, sodas, and specialty drinks
Tenting, floor coverings, and dance surfaces
Photography
Videography
Live music
And much, much more

Working in close contact with our clients, we collaborate with you to stage the perfect event.

Meyerhof's is fully licensed and insured, and we strive to be good corporate partners in the most creative possible way.
Please enjoy our website. Tempt your taste buds with some of our great menu items, and feast your eyes on our fun event photos. And when you're ready, contact us and let us know how we can assist you on your next event. We're here for you.

Ann Crane’s Specialties:

We are a full service caterer, and that means we can help you with every aspect of your event.

One thing you need to know about us: we love a challenge! We go into our planning with the mantra that anything is possible! An idea may need tweaking, but with some great brainstorming and creative magic, we work with you to make your special event possible.
