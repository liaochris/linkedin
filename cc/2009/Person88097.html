Sami Niemelä’s Summary

First and foremost I am a designer. Hands down & dirty is how I love my work.

Secondly, I possess a great deal of understanding on design as a strategic asset, and how it relates to various media and environments – including user experience, digital and physical services, brands and devices.

I have been involved in interactive design for the last 12 years, mobile sphere since the first days of web/wap portals, and lived to tell through the dotcom crash.

I believe an individual can and should change the world with his actions and I have a very strong will to also do so. We live in exciting times, and I am thrilled to be alive right here, right now.

Sami Niemelä’s Specialties:

Creative direction, digital design, brand and identity, graphic design, interaction, user experience, service design, touch interaction, interface design.
