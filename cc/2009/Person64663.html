Mat Lemmings’s Summary

A consultant active in the areas of operational and IT systems design, with implementation and support experience in manufacturing, educational, industrial and professional organisations across the UK, Europe and North America.

Having now had twenty one years in the IT industry I have acquired significant project management, technical and training experience and can offer real-world, objective consultancy with no product or manufacturer affiliation to deliver the right solution to clients' needs.

I'm most interested in helping new or expanding businesses define, implement and manage their operational and IT systems and have excellent relationships with other consultants active in areas such as software & web development, product design, marketing and corporate finance, helping me provide a 'one-stop-shop' to business owners.

Mat Lemmings’s Specialties:

Project management, systems analysis and design, LAN, WAN, software development team management, customer support, technical consultancy, Windows Server, Windows XP, Windows Vista, Linux.
