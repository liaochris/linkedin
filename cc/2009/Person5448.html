Andres Barreto’s Summary

Founder and President of PulsoSocial.com, online publication and community that reports the latest on technology, social media and entrepreneurship from Latin America.

Previously, founder Grooveshark.com, an online music sharing community, and Socialatom, a consulting firm for social media marketing and engineering, business development and international marketing that helps U.S. companies reach latin america and viceversa.

My experience is in building new businesses and products of large impact, user experience design, community building, social media marketing, social application development, public relations and social entrepreneurship.

I am a frugal adventure traveler, salsa dancer and aspiring DJ/producer.

Andres Barreto’s Specialties:
