    Gavin Thomas Photo

    (Photography industry)

    September 2007 — Present (2 years 3 months)

    Gavin Thomas is a New York City based freelance Photographer who is best known for his experimental and photojournalistic style. Some of his clients include the Brooklyn Chamber of Commerce, New York Magazine, Rap-Up Magazine, Rochester Democrat & Chronicle's Insider and Live Music Productions. He has shot artists such as 50 Cent, Tony Yayo, Lloyd Banks, T.I., Christina Milian, Kid Sister, Wale, and more!

    In 2008 he was named to the Blackbook Raw 50 book for Emerging photographers. Gavin has also won awards from PDN, Olympus, Lomography, and UnScene New York.

    Whether on the streets, on location, or in the studio his subjects appear comfortable and natural.
