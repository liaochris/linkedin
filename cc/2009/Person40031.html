    The HuMobisten, an art and design duo from Rotterdam, have been around for more than eight years. The HuMobisten (the name is a contraction of ‘humor’ and ‘mind your own business-ism’) are Rufus Ketting and Gyz La Rivière. They call themselves serious pranksters who make art and design that is mostly about themselves and the absurd world around them.
