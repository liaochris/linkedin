Tim O’Neill "Oldcommguy™"’s Summary

I have over 45 years technology experience in data/voice and video networking analysis companies including successful senior roles in Sales, Product Design, Marketing Management, Business Development and Security.
I have proven world wide experience with excellent communications skills and a history of successes in raising corporate and product value while increasing market acceptance through the focused development of products, market penetration and corporate visibility (Branding and Identity)strategies including many industry/lab awards with associated sales strategies. My deep technical knowledge and broad experience in LAN, WAN, ATM, WiFi, IP, VoIP, Video-oIP, Database, and Security for Internet technologies and products coupled with my broad industry awareness, executive experience and work ethics have been keys to my successes. I am a patent holding degreed electrical engineer and helped design the first network data scope (1976) as well as many other successful products in the communications market arena.
- I am also an infrastructiure compliance professional.
- I also was in Law Enforcement for over 10 years and I am volunteering with the Georgia Executive Director of the P.O.S.T. and other agencies on Cybercrimes training and support plus digital crime scene preservation and data recovery.
- I am also a volunteer trainer on internet Safety for Children, Adults and Seniors.
- I am an HTCIA and InfraGard member plus others.
I am known by many as Oldcommguy™!
