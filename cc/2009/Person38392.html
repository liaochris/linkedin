Gwen Shamblin’s Summary

Gwen Shamblin was born and raised in Memphis, TN, with a strong faith and foundational values. Undergraduate degree in Dietetics and Masters Degree in Nutrition with an emphasis in Biochemistry from the University of Tennessee in Knoxville. An Instructor of Foods and Nutrition at the University of Memphis for five years. A very spiritual person with a strong faith in God, Ms. Shamblin founded The Weigh Down Workshop in 1986, to teach these principles to those who were desperately seeking to lose weight permanently. Initially offered through small classes taught by Mrs. Shamblin in a retail setting, this teaching began yielding unprecedented results. Participants were not only losing their weight while eating regular foods, but they were using the same Bible-based principles to turn away from other addictions such as smoking or alcohol abuse. By 1992, churches from all over the country hosted Weigh Down seminars for their church members. Churches began to sign up, and the media began to pay attention. The growth was explosive. By the late 1990s, Weigh Down was internationally known in most Protestant, Catholic, and Evangelical churches. Gwen Shamblin was featured on 20/20, Larry King Live, and The View,Good Housekeeping and Woman’s Day. In 1997, The Weigh Down Diet Book was published by Doubleday, Inc. and became a million-seller as people discovered the secrets to losing weight quickly and permanently while finding a new relationship with God.
In1999, Gwen Shamblin, along with other individuals, founded Remnant Fellowship Church. Remnant is found in over 100 cites worldwide where members hear convicting and encouraging messages. Twice-weekly church services are webcast.
The dream of helping people turn away from the love of food and toward a love of God has turned into a ministry. Gwen Shamblin has been married over 20 years and has 2 grown children who are married and 4 grandchildren who reside in Brentwood, Tennessee.
