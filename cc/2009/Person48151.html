As a commercial real estate agent and executive, I'm committed to your success and I pay close attention to your specific needs and goals -- whether buying or selling. When selling, I promote your property or project as if it were my own. I remain competitive, energized and flexible in rapidly changing real estate markets. I'm proactive in my approach to challenges in the market or industry. I strive to develop and execute a relevant marketing program tailored to your specific needs and always deliver phenomenal results.

I believe in building a motivated team of people for each project when working with buyers/sellers. Building a cohesive team of knowledgeable, skilled and experienced professionals from both the private and public sectors, and who are results oriented, makes all the difference in the success of a project. Similarly, when working with a developer/owner, my goal is to think both strategically and tactically to pull together a powerful cross-functional team of individuals in the industry and government that often consists of engineers, finance, marketing and architects, to name a few. This helps maximize the return on investment for developers and all others concerned with the project.
