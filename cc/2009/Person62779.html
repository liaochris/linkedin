Marie Hoyt-Pariury: Designer’s Summary

An award winning Graphic Designer with a very diverse work experience. Located in Norwood, just south of the city, I work in the Boston area and at home. Work experience includes: collateral, ads, logo design, packaging, posters, print production, as well as, designing the graphics and creating web banners for trade show websites. I am very organized, making certain a job is done within exact specifications, including confirmation on jobs already started before I have worked on them. I learn new software quickly, and I work equally as efficient on both platforms, Mac and PC.

Marie Hoyt-Pariury: Designer’s Specialties:
