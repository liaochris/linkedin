Carol B.C. Honkanen’s Summary

I have about 30 years of Real Estate industry experience, ranging from Mortgage to Real Estate Sales and Investments.

My goal is to help people to achieve their dream of owning a home and to save them money in the process.

I love working with people who appreciate bottom-lines and no-nonsense. If you're an Engineer - you probably appreciate my direct approach and attention to detail and the facts. If you are an Agent, you might be happy to know that I love helping other Agents (regardless of what company you belong) and I give out Referrals ALL the time, all over the U.S..

And, as a matter of fact, if you are an Agent looking for the right fit for YOU - ask me why I am where I am. I have been to at least 10 other offices and can tell you the Pros and Cons of them all. Save yourself some time and aggravation from MY experience. Sometimes you don't find out the "truth" until you join and see if the company can Walk the Talk!

Companies are getting more and more competitive and offering more benefits, etc... Several have a "Profit Sharing" program, etc...

Carol B.C. Honkanen’s Specialties:

My specialties are Luxury and Custom Homes outside Seattle city limits. My favorite cities are Bothell, Edmonds, Everett, Issaquah, Lynnwood and Snohomish.

I also love to work with properties in Kirkland and Redmond and the surrounding areas up through Issaquah.

I pride myself on finding my Clients those "Best Kept Secrets" of Snohomish County!
