Greg Acuna’s Summary

Pala Flicks has been created to produce socially conscious content for television, films, web, and interactive media. Pala, the isle paradise in Aldous Huxley’s book “Island,” is Greg's budding dream. Based in Goa, on the west coast of India, the company plans on reaching out to creative people around the planet in an effort to forge cooperation, artistic vision and hope for our world.

Greg is also a consultant with over 20 years experience creatively managing IT projects. He specializes in organizing startups, strategy planning, and troubleshooting ventures with budget or schedule problems. He has diverse experience in a wide variety of undertakings the most recent of which have been in India. He has used innovation and highly creative thinking to solve complex business and technical issues. Greg has successfully worked:
• Managing large teams on complex projects for software development, Internet startups and creative endeavors.
• Reorganizing over budget and behind schedule groups to put them back on track.
• Advising companies on business strategy including early acceptance and use of the Internet for internal workflow efficiency, customer service and furthering sales opportunities.
• Training staff in both IT and business practices.
• Overseeing, advising and hands-on creative work with new companies on product design, identity creation, economic modeling and marketing tactics.
• Developing, producing and directing film and video projects including an independent feature film, promotional pieces, and creative shorts.

Greg Acuna’s Specialties:

Consulting, outsourcing, business processes, startups, strategy planning, troubleshooting, overseeing creative and technical projects. India, US, Europe. Writing, producing and directing film, video, theater, animation.
