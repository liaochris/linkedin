Plínio Okamoto’s Summary

Award winner creative director with 12 years experience dealing with digital media, Okamoto is a creative group leader - responsible for managing the online and offline creative team of RAPP Brazil. According to the prestigious AdAge´s Agency A-list 2008, RAPP is amongst the greatest Direct Marketing Agencies Worldwide.

Okamoto has extensive experience leading creative teams and received numerous awards in important advertising festivals such as Cannes, London, MSN MMOnline and Gramado. He has developed integrated marketing services for leading brands such as Hewlett Packard Latam, Nokia, Petrobras, Volkswagen, Itaú Bank, Itaucard, Philips, Telefônica, Natura, Pernambucanas, Roche, SKY TV, Citröen, Folha de S.Paulo, McAfee, Microsoft, Organon, AT&T, MSN, Siemens, Fedex among others.

Plinio has lectured at universities and other institutions. This year he was chosen to be one the brazilian judges for the digital category at the prestigious London International Advertising Awards. He has also been part of Gramado International Advertising Awards jury for three consecutive years.

Okamoto is a Miami Ad School/ESPM professor and guest professor at MBA Marketing/USP.

Plínio Okamoto’s Specialties:

CRM, branding, creative concept, out of box thinking, team leadership, regional and global projects, latin america, usability, user experience, community management, social tools, online-offline integration, marketing, user-generated content, online promotions, viral campaigns.
