Sterling Ledet’s Summary

Sterling Ledet is President a chain of Adobe, Quark, Apple and Google authorized training centers offering public classes in the U.S or onsite anywhere in the world (we love to travel).

Subscribe to our Adobe, Quark and Apple training newsletter at http://www.ledet.com/subscribe for a chance to win a free class. 2 given away every month.

Seeks top-notch sales, client care, and training professionals on both a full-time and contract 1099 basis. Recruiting page for W-2 is at http://www.ledet.com/jobs and for contract trainers is at http://www.ledet.com/recruiting

Seeks connections with other industry insiders, industry trade show connections, EO and TEC/Vistage members, interesting business propositions (such as tradeouts for seats in our classes, space for us to offer classes at your business, cross-marketing opportunities, etc.)
