Michael Blonsky’s Summary

Michael is a veteran technologist who has 18+ years of operations, business development, software sales, software architecture, software development and consulting experience with firms like VMware, Grant Thornton and Accenture.

Michael joined the GoingOn team to lead the company's services and support organization and ensure a world-class experience for all of GoingOn's customers around the globe. Michael is a veteran of several Silicon Valley software companies and startups. His experience includes directing the efforts around building out the Virtual Appliances initiative for VMware (NASDAQ: VMW). By developing a successful ISV outreach program, Michael helped define a new category of software solutions. Prior to the last 11 years in the San Francisco Bay Area, Michael spent 8 years in technology consulting with firms like Accenture and Grant Thornton.Michael holds a M.S. in Business and Technology from Johns Hopkins University.

  • VP of Services and Support

    GoingOn

    (Privately Held; Computer Software industry)

    September 2009 — Present (3 months)

    GoingOn is a leading provider of open-source Social Learning solutions. Our mission is rooted in the belief that knowledge is socially constructed and that learning is best achieved through dialog, collaboration and transparent sharing of information. To this end, we seek to extend traditional eLearning systems, by providing the fastest and most affordable way for organizations to deploy social learning communities where participants can connect, contribute and collaborate. Designed for educational and corporate environments, GoingOn communities can be deployed as a stand-alone environment for both online and blended learning, or can be tightly integrated with existing LCMS/LMS systems. GoingOn is a commercial open source software company providing products, services, and technical support on top of a Drupal and Moodle-based software foundation. Our commitment to the open-source model allows us to utilize the efforts and innovation of thousands of developers, while at the same time ensuring that our solution is affordable, non-proprietary and continually evolving to meet best practices. GoingOn is an active contributor to the Open Educational Research (OER) effort and is committed to working with non-profits in the area of global education.

  • VP of US Operations

    earthfaves AG

    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    May 2009 — August 2009 (4 months)

    earthfaves has been in business since 2007, was conceived and bootstrapped by a group of ex-SAP talent that has been quietly building an incredible new platform that adds a useful layer to the social networks that are currently exploding around the globe.

    earthfaves is a platform that allows businesses to effectively build a community of promoters. earthfaves plugs into existing social networks and allows people to recommend their favorite places (restaurants, hotels, taverns, parks, virtually anything) to their network of friends. earthfaves allows people to perform "social searches" and find things to do, places to go, and amazing adventures that their network of trusted friends have favored. This social search delivers banded results that show earthfaves of a person's friends, friends-of-friends, subject matter experts, and then general Google search results. Instead of getting millions of results that were ranked by a third-party, users of earthfaves get trusted search results that leverage the collective intelligence and similar tastes of the people in their social networks.
