Matt Corrion’s Summary

As the owner of Outdoor Design Group, Matt Corrion provides the creative vision and leadership for the firm. He is personally dedicated to each client and is involved in all the key aspects of the firm's projects.

Matt is a licensed landscape architect with over 10 years of experience working in the landscape architecture, land planning, and land development industries in the midwest and western United States. Matt is a friendly and creative professional who takes pride in providing responsive, reliable, and efficient work. He loves to take on new challenges, and has an extensive portfolio of completed projects.

Founded in 2004, in Denver, Colorado, Outdoor Design Group provides consulting, design, and project management services to a variety of private and public clients. Our core services include landscape architecture, land planning & entitlements, site renovation planning & design, construction management, renewable energy consulting, development coordination, and the design of high-end outdoor living spaces and gardens.

Matt Corrion’s Specialties:

site evaluation & analysis, land planning & entitlements, development coordination, site design, project negotiations & plan submittals, landscape design, site renovation design and planning, landscape water use analysis & irrigation strategies, renewable energy consulting, water features, monuments, parks, trails, private gardens & residences, streetscapes, urban design, construction documents, cost estimating & value engineering, project bidding, and construction management.
