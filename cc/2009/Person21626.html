Daan Jansonius’s Summary

I am currently in my final year of a BA Marketing degree. I am specialising in the areas of digital marketing and branding.

My interest in digital marketing was sparked during my placement year at online marketing agency Onetomarket. Here I focussed on creating, managing and optimising search engine advertising campaigns, as well as getting heavily involved in the company's own marketing programs. I also led a research team which was exploring the business opportunities of social media.

My interest in the field of online marketing led me initially to start guest posting on Viralblog.com, which is a leading blog in the field of viral marketing, and later to start my own blog at Daanjansonius.com

You can read more about my thoughts on (online) marketing at:

www.daanjansonius.com
