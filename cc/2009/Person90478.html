Josh Garner’s Summary

As an SEO, I optimize websites owned by small and medium-sized businesses so as to provide higher rankings in the search engines (Google, Yahoo!, MSN, etc.) thus providing a higher visibility online. As higher rankings mean higher visibility, this also induces business growth and earning potential.
