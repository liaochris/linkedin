Gustavo Farfan’s Summary

Gustavo Farfan’s interest in real estate is a passion and his technical expertise stems from both his education and experience.

Farfan has been working in real estate since 1985, having held executive positions with a variety of high-profile projects and brokerage firms in South Florida. From New-Development such as High-Rise Starts to Re-Branding, Managing, Disposition and Consulting.

Farfan specializes in global real estate marketing in the areas of high-rises, luxury waterfront homes, condo-hotels and investment property. His tenure in the industry, coupled with his global network of relationships and Internet expertise applied to marketing make him a relevant force in the South Florida real estate marketplace.

Farfan’s greatest asset is his continuing working relationship with domestic and international Brokers, as well as his in-depth knowledge of the financial and legal aspects to brokering a sale. Farfan is fluent in English, Portuguese and Spanish.
