Stephan Verveen’s Summary

Over the years I have explored a broad range of viewpoints on online media. The organisations I worked for range from startup to multinational, from commercial to not for profit.

The common denominator in all my work is the facilitation of people to fully realize their potential in a networked environment.

I am a sparring partner with a creative and analytical view on business processes and human interaction.
