Courtney Quattrini’s Summary

Courtney Quattrini joined Travers Collins & Company as an Account Manager in July 2008. Courtney has extensive experience delivering targeted intergrated communications programs to a variety of clients including: Jamestown 2007 - America’s 400th Anniversary, the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services, Christopher and Dana Reeve Paralysis Resource Center, Novartis, Smithsonian’s National Portrait Gallery, Acciona Energy North America, Wolf Blass Wines, The American Red Cross, the New York State Podiatric Medical Association, Dunkin' Donuts and Wal-Mart.

Prior to joining Travers Collins & Company, Courtney was a Senior Account Executive with Ruder Finn’s Washington office where she was responsible for the implementation and execution of strategic communication campaigns for a variety of clients in Ruder Finn’s consumer, healthcare, travel & tourism, global issues and arts & culture practice areas.

Before joining Ruder Finn, Courtney was an Assistant Account Executive at Carey O’Donnell Public Relations Group in West Palm Beach, Florida.

Courtney’s media outreach efforts have resulted in a number of outstanding placements with outlets including: The Associated Press, Chicago Sun-Times, The Economist, Newsweek, The New York Times, NPR, Reuters, The Today Show, United Press International, U.S. News & World Report, USA Today, The Washington Post, Woman’s Day, Working Mother Magazine, XM Radio and ABC, CBS, FOX, and NBC affiliates nationwide.

Courtney is a native of Buffalo, New York and has a bachelor’s degree in public communication from the State University of New York College at Buffalo.
