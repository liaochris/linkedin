Nicola Robinsonova’s Summary

Training for my present position includes a career as a market research analyst (Neilsen, BMRB & Ipsos), some time in media sales & active involvement in organising business mixers such as Women in Business (London Chapter). This built upon the academic base of a degree in Culture & Communication from Lancaster University, UK.

I have also enjoyed applying my experience of business planning & marketing to the field of not for profit funding, securing many thousands of pounds for voluntary groups in the Birmingham area, as well as training individuals in the skills of running their voluntary organisations.

My own training has been ongoing & I have formal, post graduate qualifications in market research & training. I aspire to the CIM Diploma - and will complete it when I have more time...
