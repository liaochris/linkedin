Harish Agrawal’s Summary

Harish Agrawal is director at Veda Informatics. In this capacity, he oversees the growth and development of the group and is responsible for strategy, delivery, and business development functions.

Harish brings around 11 years of leadership, strategy, program management, and technology experience to Veda’s clients. Before founding Veda Informatics, he was Program manager for Sapient Corporation a consulting firm and worked with clients from Fortune 500 companies. For Sapient Harish managed and delivered complex engagements for clients like Lucent, Cisco and Verizon working in Global offices of Sapient at New York, San Francisco and India. Prior to that, he was associated with Silverline Technologies as a Technology consultant.

Harish holds a Bachelor’s degree in Computer science from VYWS Engineering College Amravati.
