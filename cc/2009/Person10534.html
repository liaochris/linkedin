Scott Baldwin’s Summary

I work closely with clients and stakeholders leveraging my expertise in information architecture, user interface design, and usability and deliver compelling user-centered interactive projects that are easy-to-use, extend brands online and drive revenue and customer satisfaction.

I'm also an experienced Internet strategist and web analyst and hold an Award of Achievement in Web Analytics from the University of British Columbia and often help clients leverage web analytics to make better decisions as to where to invest in user-centered work and to help demonstrate the value of user experience.

Scott Baldwin’s Specialties:

Information architecture, interaction design, usability, internet marketing, web analytics, web project management, team leadership and channel strategy development.
