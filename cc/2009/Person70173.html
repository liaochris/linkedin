Renato Silvio Mortera’s Summary

I received my MS in Biomedical Engineer on 09th October 2006 and I was admitted to "Ordine degli ingegneri della Provincia di Torino" on 19th April 2007.
Currently I'm a PhD student in Materials Science and Technology at the Politecnico di Torino (Department of Materials Science and Chemical Engineering).
My PhD thesis concerns Mesoporous Silica Nanoparticles for Drug Release in Biomedical Applications. During my second year of PhD I spent six months in US at Iowa State University, Chemistry department.
