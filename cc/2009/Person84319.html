Robert Wallace’s Summary

Duces Tecum, Inc is a full service title insurance agency able to assist the real estate professional in any type of title transaction reguardless of size, complexity or geographic location within the United States (except for Utah, California and Hawaii). In addition to traditional title services it also provides litigation support in the form of expert witness services on equity stripping, title clogging and certification of necessary parties to court actions as well as skip trace, heir and asset location in select markets. International Title Insurance will be considered on a country by country basis.
