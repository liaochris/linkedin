Stephen Olmstead’s Summary

I am an experienced graphic designer with nearly 10 years of field experience. My design capabilities span the print, web, and video fields. I have two degrees in Graphic Design related fields and always enjoy unique and challenging projects.

I exist to glorify the Lord, though I admit, I fail frequently. I am a slave to Christ first, a husband to my best friend second, and an artist third. I rejoice in a reliance that is built upon someone that is so much bigger than myself. The psalmist says it best, "I call as my heart grows faint; lead me to the rock that is higher than I." (Psalm 61:2) I currently work as a production artist/graphic designer at a local, Christian non-profit organization located in Phoenix, Arizona.
