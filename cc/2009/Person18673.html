Christopher Sitek’s Summary

Award winning professional with a 20-year record of success leading investment management initiatives. Currently a Portfolio Manager and Accredited Investment Fiduciary Analyst™ for M&I Investment Management Company. Manage investment portfolios for ultra high net worth and high net worth individuals and families, ERISA plans, endowment and foundation funds.

2006-2008, Principal for Integrities 360. Engaged by private investors and investment fiduciaries to bring clarity to the complexities of the investment management process, improve risk management, and potentially help increase long-term investment performance.

2004-2006, Co-Manager on Investment Committee for the Tactical Allocation Group LLC. (TAG), a global asset allocation manager. Managed custom and model investment portfolios for private investors, ERISA plans, corporate funds, endowment and foundation funds. Received two national awards for investment excellence. Further, as COO and CCO, provided leadership and strategic direction in areas of compliance, finance, operations, and client service.

Previously, spent nearly 15 years with UBS leading several investment initiatives including the Corporate Service Group, a boutique money management operation within UBS (the predecessor to TAG) that delivered global tactical portfolio solutions using mutual funds, institutional managers and hedge funds. Also served as Alternative Investments Coordinator and Liaison to the UBS Alternative Investments Group in NJ.

In brief, accomplished investment management professional with a proven record of excellence in portfolio management, investment consulting, and the global fiduciary practices for managing the investment management process.
