Enza Cristiana Reina’s Summary

At present Enza Reina is providing with her experience as consultant/researcher, some national and international companies and associations (Mercer SA, Space On Sail, Equilibri.net).
From 2006 to July 2008, Enza Reina joined Soges S.p.a - International Operations Division, as project manager being in charge of the daily management and backstopping activities of two EC funded project, implemented in the Middle East region (Lebanon)
Since 2003 Enza Reina published several papers on political analysis on Mediterranean Region, attended as speaker several international meetings and she has been awarded with the grant Love Difference (Inter-mediterranean cultural network).
Starting from 2005 to May 2007 she has been in charge for the Studies and Research Department at Love Difference – Fondazione Pistoletto (Biella, Italy) developing research projects on Art-Enterprise-Society relations on the Mediterranean area.
From 2003 to 2005 she worked as consultants and trainer at the Inter-cultural Centre of the City of Turin managing and delivering educational workshops on Human Rights ,as executive editor for the on-line magazine "BAB", and in charge of the daily press review.
She carried out, as researcher, in the frame of the research project “ MigrAction” leaded by Cespi (Research Centre on International Politics- Rome) the first National Repertory of co-development projects, investigating links between migrations flows and economic development and drafting the first National (Italian) Repertory of co-development projects.
Enza Cristiana Reina is graduated in International Law with a major in Minorities cultural Rights and she holds a Master Degree in Peacekeeping Management attended at the University of Turin.
She attended, also,several post graduated courses concerning PCM, Gender Issues, Communication.
