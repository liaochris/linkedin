Tim Rutherford’s Summary

While sales has played a role within my professional experience, I’ve been considered a “jack of all trades”. I have a background in technology including website design, web marketing, graphic design, multimedia generation, telecommunications, programming and more. I’ve hired, fired, trained, scheduled and supervised individuals. I have a background in operations management, not only as a consultant but as a department manager and small business owner with full P&L responsibilities. As a consultant, and as an employee, I’ve accomplished a fair amount of process improvement by utilizing existing technologies and by streamlining processes. As an MBA, I’ve been exposed to all areas of business from a managerial perspective. On top of all of this is an understanding and appreciation for effective marketing, advertising, PR, and communications—both internally and externally.
