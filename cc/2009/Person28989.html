Earl Barnett’s Summary

Aside from my name being Earl, I’m a machinist by trade, a teacher by calling, and a compulsive student by choice. I’m the son of a truck driver and a nurse. I eat too much. I don’t work out nearly enough. I have an irrational fear of foreign travel and zombies. I have a sleeve tattoo, love hardcore, live a straight-edge life but refuse to call myself such. I’m an easygoing, obsessive-compulsive, passionate, manic man who lives his life as a God-fearer always striving to be something better than i currently am.

I aspire to teach for a living. I have a Bachelor Degree in Theology and am presently pursuing an MTS in the History and Interpretation of Christianity at the Candler School of Theology, Emory University

I am devoting my life to theological studies with the hope of reuniting the hope and life of the church with the knowledge and wisdom of academia (It’s a touch melodramatic, I know).

And I really am terrified of zombies. No, really.
