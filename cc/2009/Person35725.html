Geoff Wood’s Summary

A self-proclaimed “entrepreneur, strategist, and catalyst,” Geoff recently relocated back to Iowa after nearly five years in Indianapolis.

In the past he’s managed a software development business unit, an IT support center, and promoted technology modernization for local governments across the country.

Now, he’s returned to the state where he grew up to undertake a variety of entrepreneurial endeavors including managing Iowa operations for Omaha-based Ideation Ventures, serving as a founding member of Venture 594, LLC, contributing features on Iowa's "creative class" to SiliconPrairieNews.com, and many other exciting opportunities.

He loves to work with entrepreneurs and small businesses who have a vision for their organization but can use operational assistance in getting it to the next step.

Geoff Wood’s Specialties:

Strategic thinking, using social media for problem solving, nascent technology, providing the operations and business perspective to entrepreneurs "big ideas", the application of emerging technologies to traditional organizations, consulting on local government modernization, creating and leading entrepreneurial ventures inside established organizations, Geographic Information Systems
