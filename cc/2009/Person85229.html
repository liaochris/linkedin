Robin Matuk’s Summary

I am an Internet Business Coach who addresses the needs of women entrepreneurs and business owners looking to maximize the power of the Internet to build, manage, systemize, and grow a thriving business. I'm the President and founder of My Digital Coach, a blogger at Creating with Impact and a Community Builder at The Big Idea Community.

The Internet has drastically changed the way entrepreneurs do business and as a result, competition has increased tremendously. I founded My Digital Coach to assist entrepreneurs and business owners to take advantage of these changes. I provide one-on-one coaching, group workshops and a networking community for women entrepreneurs and business owners so they stay ahead of the competition. My services include Digital Makeovers which focus on bringing technology into businesses to make them efficient, systemized, and more profitable. These are just a few of the ways I can assist with the growth of any business, creating a more productive, organized entrepreneur while utilizing the power of the Internet.

I bring over fifteen years experience as a computer trainer and coach and offers a range of highly accessible and easy-to-learn approaches to computer skills workshops and one-on-one coaching sessions. As a Corporate Trainer with Kaiser Permanente, I received the “Above and Beyond” award for developing a formal “Trainer Certification Program” for the Information Technology Training & Consulting Department. My varied and diverse background combines business, technology and the creative arts to provide practical technological applications with a human touch. I have a BA in Fine Arts from Brooklyn College of the City University of New York and additional training in Counseling Psychology, Human Resources Training and Development and Web Design.
