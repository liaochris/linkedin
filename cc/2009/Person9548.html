Barry Foster’s Summary

Barry manages the Forensic Technology Practice for Deloitte in New Zealand and has 20 years Police, NZ Army and Consultant experience. he conducted his first computer based examination in 1992 and has worked extensivly in the areas of e-crime and forensic computing since 1999.

He has held investigative and management roles in New Zealand Army, NZ Police and in Consulting Companies. He has provided evidence for Military and Criminal Courts and is a recognized Expert in the New Zealand Courts. Barry has completed formal training to an expert level in the use of forensic recovery, preservation and analysis software in New Zealand and the United States. He regularly conducts training in electronic crime scene and analysis procedures for law enforcement, academic and special interest groups.

Barry Foster’s Specialties:

Computer Forensic Analysis, Mobile Device Forensics
