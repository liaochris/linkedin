Michelle Carter, LFD, GC-C’s Summary

Unique blend of creative, sales, marketing, and management skills. Strong analytical and planning skill set, combined with the ability to coordinate the efforts of many to meet organizational goals. Adept at assessing client needs and developing solutions in short time period.

Varied and well-rounded experiences including reporting and event planning on tight deadlines, business coaching, marketing, copy writing, promotions and small business management.

Licensed funeral director and certified grief counselor. Founder of The New York Center for Transition, providing counseling services and funeral consulting to individuals and families facing an imminent loss. Also specializes in assisting those recently diagnosed with chronic diseases such as rheumatoid arthritis and fibromyalgia. All services provided in clients' homes.

Extensive journalism background. As a member of Ladies Who Launch, provides marketing, coaching, PR and copy writing expertise to women launching and expanding their business ventures.

Member of and webmistress for the Hendrick Hudson Lions Club, member of Lions International.

Also a Notary Public.

Interested in new business partnerships, academic research, nonprofits, and consulting.

Michelle Carter, LFD, GC-C’s Specialties:
