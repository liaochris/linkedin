Dan Iampieri’s Summary

I am a 2000 graduate of Glenelg High School in Glenelg, Maryland. I grew up in Western Howard County where I had the best times of my life with the best people and friends I've ever known or ever will have. You all know who you are!

Now I'm a Realtor with Weichert,Realtors Caton Properties in Ellicott City, Maryland. I've been in the business since 2003, but if you look at my pictures in my album you'll see a picture of me from over 20 years ago in front of a Real Estate sign. That's because I've been around this business my whole life and my whole family is in it! I started out as a child sitting in Open Houses on Sundays with my Mom, as a teenager putting up signs for agents in my Dad's office, and as a young adult cleaning out dirty old houses for agents in my office before they put the house on the market. I even used to clean the toilets in my office!
