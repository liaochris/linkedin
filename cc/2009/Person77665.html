Paul Lamb’s Summary

I have over 20 years of experience as an entrepreneur, nonprofit executive, consultant, and facilitator. My firm, "Man on a Mission Consulting", is dedicated to management consulting for social change. In particular we focus on developing innovative social enterprise business models and leveraging new technologies to change lives. My clients range from large companies and foundations to grassroots, community based organizations.

I am also an active writer/blogger & trainer. My "mission" is to be a catalyst for high-impact social change and community engagement.

Paul Lamb’s Specialties:

Innovative programs development, project management, facilitation/training, strategic partnership building, public relations & marketing. Particular areas of expertise include emerging technologies and Web 2.0, social enterprise development, fundraising, and nonprofit management.
