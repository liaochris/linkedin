Denise A. Romano’s Summary

Denise is a certified Emotional Intelligence Coach working with Executives and HR Professionals on Leadership, Ethics, Diversity, and Conflict Resolution.

Denise is also the author of The HR Toolkit: An Indispensible Resource for Credible Activists, which will be published in the spring by McGraw Hill.

Her approach to organizational and individual effectiveness is based in open systems theory and application. Denise holds an MA in Organizational Psychology and an EdM in Counseling Psychology both from Columbia University.

Denise is also an HR/OD Professional with more than 12 years of Executive Human Resources and Organizational Development experience. She is currently Director of HR/OD at a NYS Public Benefit Corporation in Downtown Manhattan.

Denise is certfied in the use of Emotional Intelligence assessments (EQi and EQ360) and is also a Certified PREPARE/ENRICH Couples' Counselor.

She has studied Advanced Conflict Resolution and Mediation at Columbia University and has been accepted into NYSDRA's mediation certification program.

Denise has a strong interest in workplace issues around ethics, consistency, compliance, power-sharing, and in promoting psychologically healthy work environments.

Denise supports HR professionals and other Executives to be Credible Activists within their organizations ~ choosing ethical compliance and educating others over non-compliant, inconsistent, and unethical options.

Denise is working on a practical, user-friendly handbook for HR/OD professionals that addresses many common workplace issues and challenges.

    The HR Toolkit: An Indispensible Resource for Being A Credible Activist

    With more than 100 templates, memos, and checklists, there’s nothing this toolkit can’t fix! HR professionals know that addressing issues before they escalate can make the difference in saving the company time and money. The HR Toolkit is the must have handbook if you’re looking to skillfully identify, diplomatically address, and strategically resolve any issue that confronts your organization.

    Including a handy indexed listing of the most common workplace conflicts and solutions for ease-of-reference, expert author Denise A. Romano, MA, EdM provides practical and actionable ways to empower and educate HR professionals and management.

    Biographical note
    Denise A. Romano has an MA in Organizational Psychology and an EdM in Counseling Psychology from Columbia University, as well as an MFA in Writing from Naropa University. She has more than 12 years of experience working in the Human Resources and Organizational Development (HR/OD) field in government, nonprofit, and for-profit companies, including 6 additional years of whistleblower activity. She has built two different HR/OD departments from the ground up.
