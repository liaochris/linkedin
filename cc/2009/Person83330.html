Rhiannon Bowman’s Summary

October 12, 2009

I am a freelance writer and sometimes photographer based in Charlotte, N.C. Currently, I write for several local media outlets on a variety of topics.

Meanwhile, I'm starting to stretch into the national market and have begun work on a non-fiction book with a make-you-laugh title.

It feels good to admit that I'm living my dream.

Best,
Rhi B.
