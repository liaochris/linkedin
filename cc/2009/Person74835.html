Pamela Mayer’s Summary

I am an ENTJ & a serial entrepreneur. An RN & professional musician for 15 years in So. California I've owned Medical Needs Day Cares, Elite Holistic Salon/Spas, Construction Companies, a waterside Restaurant with pool and Tiki Bar service and consulted in Interior Decorating & a Dog Rescue.

My UPSCALE PET BOUTIQUE website, www.noblecanine.com offers the finest designer products, such as Caserta Pets, Hedy Manon, Jo Sherwood, Kwigy Bo, & many others. The site is warm, welcoming with surprises on each page. I offer products for those who spoil & want to extend their sense of style to their darling canine family members. Those distinctive clients who insist on the highest quality and latest trends. I have put together a collection of the most chic designers for pet carriers, artisan feeding bowls, auto booster seats, fashionable dog beds, the most luxurious Spa line & some wonderfully whimsical gifts for you & your pet that will make you smile.


My long range goal is to start a NON-PROFIT organization, acquire land grant, professional volunteers to create a Haven for abused animals & children. The concept is to take "at-risk" children from foster care, abuse, neglect and expose them to animals who need help for the same reasons. They will heal each other. My reward is that I get to watch.
