Ton Koenraad’s Summary

A.L.M. (Ton) Koenraad, MA (Utrecht)

Ton Koenraad works as a teacher educator and ICT-E consultant at Utrecht University of Applied Sciences. He is a registered expert for the European Commission’s Education and Learning programme and contributes to e-learning Europa. He is a member of the ICT-plaform of the former National Bureau for Modern Languages (NaB-MVT) and participates in various networks, SIGs and CoPs.

His activities involve the acquisition, coordination and evaluation of national and EU-projects in the fields of modern languages, teacher education and e-learning.
Specific current interests are: Computer Assisted Language Learning (CALL) related software development and task design, telecollaboration, Interactive Whiteboard research, e-tools for Content Integrated Language Learning (CLIL) and 3D Virtual Worlds for language learning.

His publications and contributions to conferences are available from his personal pages (see websites)

Ton Koenraad’s Specialties:

(International) Technology-based educational project acquisition and management.
Particular expertise on ICTtools for Content Integrated Language Learning (CLIL), materials design and training for pedagogical uses of Interactive Whiteboards and educational applications in 3D Virtual Worlds (Active Worlds, Second Life etc..).
