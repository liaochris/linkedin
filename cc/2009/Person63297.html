Mark Gibbons’s Summary

Residential home sales with RE/MAX Landmark, REALTORS since 1999. Clients include sellers (expired listings, FSBO's, re-sales), buyers (relocation, 1st time buyers), investors, builders, developers, past clients and referrals. Past sales include $150,000 condo's, $500,000 income properties, $1.5 million dollar homes......and everything in between.

My business plan calls for prospecting 5 days a week. I specifically contact expired listings, FSBO's, past clients, my center of incluence and top producing area Realtors. The goal of prospecting is to find highly quaified buyers for my listings, and motivated sellers for my buyers. This "pro-active" approach to selling real estate is the key component to a marketing plan that is designed to sell your house for the MOST amount of money, in the LEAST amount of time. Sitting around and waiting does not get results, and does not get a home sold. Being "pro-active" does!

RE/MAX Landmark, REALTORS has 13 office locations throughout the Greater Boston region. Past and current clients have found this to be a great benefit, allowing for maximum exposure and flexability. The result is "SOLD" signs in over 40 different towns throughout the area over the past 9+ years.

Whether you are buying a home, selling a home, or doing both......My goal is to help you get what you want, when you want it. I can reach my goals by helping you and your family achieve yours. If you or someone you know is looking to buy or sell real estate, give me a call, I'd be happy to help.
