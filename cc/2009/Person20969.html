Michelle Dunn’s Summary

Czar in the credit and debt collection industry, Michelle reigns supreme over every avenue of debt collection. Starting a collection agency, marketing a collection agency, making collection calls and getting paid. Michelle does everything well, she does it meticulously, she’s driven and ambitious and expects us to do as she does. Her core values reflect her quest for perfection. Michelle has a vision coupled with follow through to completion and a go-getter personality. Michelle is attractive and professional balancing out a serious profession. She has brand power with her "Collecting Money" series of books, her Credit & Collections Association and her reputation for helping women.

Michelle Dunn’s Specialties:

I specialize in credit and debt collection, writing credit policies, business plans, marketing plans, writing books and articles and publishing.
