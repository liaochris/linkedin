Matt Glasgow’s Summary

- 10+ years of clinical experience in a variety of medical, surgical and primary care specialties and settings.
- 10+ years of experience in clinical decision support product development and integration (in the United States, New Zealand and Australian markets), including: "info-button" integrated provision of patient specific management and treatment recommendations using patient data from within clinical applications; consulting on data structures and terminologies for single disease order sets in computerized physician order entry systems (CPOE) applications; consulting on multiple co-morbidity based order sets for CPOE applications.
- 10+ years of experience in evidence-based medicine and literature/research.
- 17+ years of experience in the fields of fitness instruction, personal training, sports medicine and injury rehabilitation, at a variety of levels and settings.
- Excellent Information Systems and Information Technology knowledge and experience.
- Knowledge and experience pertaining to health informatics topics and standards: messaging and vocabulary/terminology standards (including HL7, SNOMED, ICD, LOINC), medical/clinical knowledge representation and data modelling, physician referential and workflow applications, and clinical decision-support integration.
- Significant web design experience, including standards-compliant HTML, XHTML, CSS, server-side scripting (PHP, MySQL) and client-side scripting.
