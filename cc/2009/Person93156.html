Sonny Vinberg, AIA’s Summary

Sr. level Architect seeking career with exciting Architectural, A/E/C, or Energy firm. Locations either nationally or internationally,

Languages spoken: Norwegian, Swedish, some French, and Alutiiq.

Experienced leader with 30 years broadly-based experience in architecture, planning, hospitality, and commercial, retail, government, office, industrial, and real estate development. Heavy experience with "front-end" development, "due diligence," real estate and planning and master planning of various project types, architectural project management, production and construction management, and team management. Experience in a large facet of architecture project types, including sustainable design and hospitality design.
