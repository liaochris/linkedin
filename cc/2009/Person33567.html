Frans Lytzen’s Summary

I have 15+ years experience in the software sector, with seven years in the HR Software sector and considerable international experience. I specialise in delivering change and business reorganisation, for the whole business or for specific teams, with a strong focus on improving the bottom line.
My background is a combination of a classic business training, technical expertise and project management, working with and for international companies as well as small local companies.

Frans Lytzen’s Specialties:

- Professional services optimisation - improving efficiency and profitability as well as customer satisfaction
- Offshore software development, in particular moving existing operations offshore
- Agile (Scrum/XP) software development
- Change management
- Innovation and software design
