Mitch Paioff’s Summary

For nearly ten years I have been implementing IBM Cognos 8 Planning solutions for companies all over the U.S. and in Canada. Hundreds of students have completed my accelerated Cognos Planning courses.

Prior to starting my own consulting business in 2001, I was a controller and chief financial officer in the manufacturing, distribution, and construction industries. In those positions, I prepared annual budgets, rolling forecasts, and cash flow projections. Those experiences have given me an excellent foundation for building superior models using IBM Cognos 8 Planning software.

I recently completed the 5-day IBM Cognos TM1 developer course.
