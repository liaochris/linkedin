John Lopata, PMP, Six Sigma Black Belt’s Summary

An Engineering Executive, inventor with 38 patents, Six Sigma Black Belt and certified Project Management Professional (PMP).

Proven track record starting, growing and mentoring technical teams in the United States, Ireland and India.

An intense focus on rapid product development, operations execution and value based product and process optimization using phase gate reviews, Design for Six Sigma (DFSS) and LEAN delivering over $50 million per year new product sales and licensing revenue while reducing costs in excess of $1 million per year through process improvements.

Specific product development, manufacturing implementation and successful commercialization experience targeted at an innovative organization driving continuous improvement to implement the company’s vision, meet or exceed customer requirements and fuel growth.
