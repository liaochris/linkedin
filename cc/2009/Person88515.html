Sanjit Narwekar, PMP’s Summary

Sanjit has a Bachelor’s degree in Production Engineering and has almost 20 years of work experience encompassing I.T. Consulting for Business Intelligence, Mainframe applications, Management Consulting, Project Management and Business Development.

Sanjit has excellent multicultural skills and exposure. He has worked in India, U.K. and South Africa.

A Production Engineer by qualification, Sanjit was involved in writing Financial Proposals for funding new projects in the Engineering/Manufacturing sectors.

As an I.T. Professional, he specialized in maintaining applications for the Retail, Banking and Insurance sectors. As a Business Consultant, Sanjit has worked on Performance Management using Balanced Scorecard and Dashboards in the Internal Revenue, Customs and Banking areas.

Sanjit is 40 years old and currently lives in Pune, India with his wife and two children aged 7 and 4. Sanjit’s father is a retired Air Force officer and his mother is a housewife.

Sanjit has wide ranging personal interests that include Science & Technology, Shooting, Nature, Wildlife, Indian Classical and Rock music, Cricket and Golf. In his spare time Sanjit is an avid trekker, off-roader and golfer.
