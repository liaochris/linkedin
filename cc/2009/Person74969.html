Norman Dijkstra’s Summary

Working for CollectEasy debiteuren (creditmanagement) outsourcing, I combine my ICT skills with debiteurenbeheer by connecting financial administrations to our OnGuard implementation. Automating the debtor and invoice information to CollectEasy, keeping them up to date on a daily basis with no manual intervention.

In charge of the department "Incasso (collection)" I talk to all markets and market areas. For advice feel free to contact me!

Norman Dijkstra’s Specialties:

Connecting to the administration of our customers enables CollectEasy to perform the creditmanagement for those customers in the most efficient manner. Working this way, for a specific customer we have seen the open outstanding drop from 7 to 3.5 million euro's in a few months. Just by keeping tabs on the open invoices, and reminding as soon as payments are due.
We perform the services Creditmanagement (outsourced to our office) and Collection (debiteurenbeheer en incasso)
