Sandra Upchurch’s Summary

It is my hope to provide a cost-effective yet quality ADR experience for all parties in all manner of disputes. Given the current economic stressors facing corporations and governmental entities it is my goal to encourage state and local governments and businesses to reach out to ADR more often in a pre-suit setting and, failing that, very early on in litigation in order to effectively manage the unpredictable costs associated with discovery and trial

Sandra Upchurch’s Specialties:

including but not limited to intergovernmental disputes, litigation with state and/or local government as a party, land use disputes, environmental disputes, personal injury cases and employment matters

Sandra Upchurch’s Experience

  • Mediation Counsel

    Upchurch Watson White and Max Mediation Group

    (Privately Held; Alternative Dispute Resolution industry)

    January 2009 — Present (11 months)

    At UWWM I mediate and facilitate all manner of cases with an emphasis on disputes involving a governmental entity or between governmental entities. I am working to encourage governing bodies to embrace ADR in pre-suit applications and in the context of land use and environmental disputes. I am especially interested in providing cost-effective yet affordable ADR options for parties, governmental or otherwise.
