Alan Stamm’s Summary

Consultant to enterprises involved in technology, finance, real estate, health care, economic development, law, transportation, education, manufacturing, social services and arts/culture.

- Capabilities: Branding, marketing communications, media/blogger relations, speech texts, government relations support, grant applications and event management.

- Tactics: Online content, e-newsletters, social media posts, annual reports, white papers, case studies, professional journal articles, news media commentaries, marketing collateral, best-practices research, SEM-optimized copywriting, advertorials and direct mail.

Alan Stamm’s Specialties:

Create engaging narratives, anticipate media focus, experience working with senior management, fast-turnaround responses to developing issues, journalism skills for presenting messages that are consistent, memorable and high-impact.
