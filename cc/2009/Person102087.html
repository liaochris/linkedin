Valto Loikkanen’s Summary

I like to work with innovative ideas and innovative people in new ventures that have good potential. I'm an owner in several companies and also networked with joint ventures with several other companies.

I want to push good plans into action to get results. Plans are like great ideas, there can be many good ones, but without result oriented execution, those rarely generate any real value.

I have a graphics designer background and close to 15 years of experience in entrepreneurship and general business management, with a good track record for identifying new business opportunities.

My first start-up business VTG, was specialized in mobile phone design covers IE. personalization. Established in 1995 and quickly grew up, to 100 employees with offices in US. Australia and Finland.

Experienced in creating and executing new business plans, developing and managing sales, marketing, operations and personnel at both start-up and established companies in design, IT and telecoms industry, with international work experience in Europe and USA.

As a Business Advisor, I have advised hundreds of new entrepreneurs on starting-up their own businesses.
