Mary Panetta’s Summary

Mary Panetta is a litigation attorney with experience in legal knowledge management, strategic marketing, professional development, and litigation practice support. Mary has significant experience in implementing practice-oriented and business intelligence initiatives to ensure enterprise-wide knowledge-sharing, professional skills development and highly effective client service.

Previously, as Director of Knowledge Management at Akin Gump, Mary provided strategic direction and support to the firm's practice groups in developing resources to facilitate the sharing and reuse of the firm's collective knowledge. She also managed the firm's marketing content, with heavy emphasis on the firm's proposal materials and Web site, and the development of best practices related to these tools.

Prior to joining Akin Gump, Mary practiced law at Morrison & Foerster LLP and Crosby Heafey Roach & May. She received her J.D. in 1997 from UCLA School of Law, where she was an editor of the UCLA Women's Law Journal and participated in Moot Court.
