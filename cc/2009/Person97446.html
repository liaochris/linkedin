Teresa Morrow’s Summary

I have over 15 years experience in the executive administrative profession. In late 2005, I decided to leave the corporate world and join with my husband to handle the daily operations of his floor cleaning business, Morr Services, Inc.

As I was getting more exposure for my husband's business to get online, I was checking for networking groups. One of the first was a women organization that I am very active in still is WECAI - Women's eCommerce Association International.

In 2007, I decided to start my own business to combine my passions of reading, writing and connecting with others. I started my business, Key Business Partners. The business started out as a virtual assistant business and is now specialized to focus on working with authors and writers to help them with online promotion.

I am a native Floridian...(yes, there aren't too many of us out there). I have lived in other cities in Florida(such as Largo, Clearwater and Tampa) however, not lived in any other state. I really do enjoy living in Florida (for the most part). Sometimes the heat can get to be too much and I am not too keen about hurricane season. However, I have had a few opportunities to live other places and haven't left yet.

Teresa Morrow’s Specialties:

I specialize in helping authors and writers promote their books online by managing virtual book blog tours, assisting in social media and blog implementation, article marketing and press release creation and submissions.

I have almost 20 years working with start ups to Fortune 500 companies in different industries such as financial planning, landscape architecture, floor cleaning and property management.
