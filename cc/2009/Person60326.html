Samantha Missingham’s Summary

Sam has worked in publishing for 14 years, starting at Incisive Media working on financial technology publications. She then moved to Centaur where she was marketing manager for a portfolio of titles including New Media Age. She then moved on to become marketing manager for Centaur's portal site www.mad.co.uk. She has provided marketing consultancy expertise to Future Publishing, Skep Media, Mountside Media and an ongoing relationship with Centaur.

She is currently working for Nielsen Business Media providing audience marketing support for The Bookseller magazine and website.

After setting up on her own, she established Lounge Marketing in 2005 to offer a broader base of publishers and event organisers creative and marketing solutions. Her clients reflect some of the largest publishers in the UK.

Lounge Marketing has developed and now forms a collective of experienced professionals - each with their own area of expertise earned in the most well regarded publishing and event companies in the UK.

Through this shared knowledge and experience, Lounge Marketing offers creative, well thought out, intelligent solutions across editorial, marketing, design, web & online development, search engine optimisation and communication.
