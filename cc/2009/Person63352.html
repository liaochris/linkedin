Mark Hodder’s Summary

Professionally my goal is to meet the demands of work while maintaining my integrity, honesty, professionalism and accountability.

I am comfortable stepping up and taking control when needed and just as comfortable in playing the support role to move the agenda forward. I believe a company is only as good as its employees. I look for employers that not only invest in their business but also their most important asset - their employees.

A strong believer in life long learning, I am always up for new challenges and training opportunities that help me grow both personally and professionally.

My strengths lay in my out-of-the-box thinking, creativity and excellent technology skills. I can move seamlessly between conversing with the marketing, accounting and technical departments with my mixture of business and computer education/life experiences.

I have extensive work experience in the public sector of Canada at both the provincial and federal levels as well as work experience in the private sector. This mixture of work experience has allowed me to grow professionally and personally while obtaining a understanding of the challenges that each sector faces.

I have also successfully launched and grown a profitable part-time business under the name Hodder Consulting in 2008. In less then 1 year revenues grew significantly which allowed me to purse running my business full time.

Hodder Consulting provides web design, graphic design, research and IT consulting among other services. You can learn more at www.yourwizard.ca
