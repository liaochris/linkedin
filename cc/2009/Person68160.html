Mick Stanic’s Summary

I have been involved in the new media and digital content industry since 1990.

As a developer, designer, producer, Executive Producer, General Manager and founder with firms that have included Splatt Technologies, VRX, Australian Business Theatre, CapitalPR, Hothouse, Next Online, Singleton OgilvyInteractive, The Podcast Network, Principius and Dreampool Digital, my underlying role has always been to promote the value/experience that interactive and digital mediums provide to all of the stakeholders involved.

In late 2006, I founded Honeysuckle Creek Pty Ltd, which is a company with a diverse range of interests, including both digital and realworld projects.

In mid 2007 I became a Director at EYEfi Pty Ltd who design and provide innovative products and services that have spawned from the recent convergence of IT&T, IP Networking, wireless and CCTV/Security applications.

In early 2009 i formed the first of the businesses owned by Honeysuckle Creek. Upper Yarra Digital Media, which also operates Upper Yarra Web Hosting, was started to provide a variety of services to community groups and small to medium tourism and retails businesses across rural, regional and metropolitan Australia.

Another business, DSS46 Digital, which will service other industries and locations is currently being structured and will launch during 2009.

In early June of 2009, I launched the first of the pure non-digital-specific businesses owned by Honeysuckle Creek called "ExpeditionOz Services", which provides a variety of remote expedition and outdoor services.

I also spend a lot of time talking to the media, speaking at conferences and blogging and I am currently a Board Member of the National Executive of AIMIA and also a member of the Internet Society of Australia.
