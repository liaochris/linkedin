Marc Alt’s Summary

Marc Alt is president of Marc Alt + Partners, a design, research and brand strategy agency dedicated to sustainable innovation. Marc Alt speaks frequently to a wide variety of audiences and companies about green design, sustainability and creating value by aligning corporate strategy with environmental and social benefit.

Marc Alt + Partners provides consulting services, strategy and insight to clients from a diverse range of industry verticals. Recent engagements include BMW/MINI USA, Bloomberg LP, Ogilvy, Butler Shine (BSSP), Liz Claiborne, Lippincott, Consumer Electronics Association of America (CEA), The Green Electronics Council, Neenah Paper and others.

Marc regularly speaks at a variety of international conferences and industry summits focused on innovation. He has lectured at the American Marketing Association, Yale, NYU, AIGA, the PSFK Trends Conference, SINOCES and many other venues on the convergence of green design, marketing and business. He is often asked to provide POV and context on green issues for journalists and is quoted frequently in media.

As an advocate of business transformation, Marc develops environmental conferences, including Greener Gadgets, a conference exploring the challenges, possibilities and trends in the greening of the $170 billion consumer electronics industry.

Marc is active in initiatives that are helping design and product innovation teams share knowledge and accelerate understanding of sustainable design principles. Marc serves on the advisory board of The Designers Accord and was founding co-chair of the AIGA Center for Sustainable Design. Marc previously served on the board of the NY Chapter of AIGA, The Professional Association for Design.
