Laura D Lewis’s Summary

I'm a bridge builder.

I create the means to get from point a to point b in life whether it's finding ways for people to live/work and appreciate each other rather than fear each other in the Middle East. Or creating a wonderful love relationship and fulfilling marriage (Couples Company, Inc), Surviving Unemployment and getting back to work, (Laid Off, Now What?!?) or assisting non-profits and churches raise the funds necessary to support their mission through books and music (Storybook Advent), everything I do combines my passions for art, writing, music, philanthropy, public speaking and media.

Education is also a big part of my work. With a partner we publish a weekly article on life in the Middle East under occupation which has gained him, (we publish under his name) international acclaim and several journalism awards.

Reaching out to members of the peace movement in Israel and the Middle East plays a big part in my personal life. The majority of my free time is spent tearing down misconceptions between Judaism, Islam and Christianity and working with various human rights and faith based groups within each of the faiths. Additionally I work with several organizations taking on extremism and racism in each of the faiths. As a Christian, my work in this area focuses on confronting extremism in Christian church. We've all discovered fanaticism needs to be addressed from within the faith by members of it. We've also discovered all the faiths have their fanatics, most easily recognized by a fusing of faith with politics and military endeavors and the use of scripture out of context to justify actions whether it's the Torah, Talmud, Bible or Koran.

From this work I've gained a reputation among political leaders and decision-makers in the US, Europe and the Middle East as an expert on the history/political situations in the Middle East and on the three faiths underlying each culture.
