Werner Augustin’s Summary

Corporate generalist with focus on international business development, sales and consulting in telecommunications. Familiar with both telecommunication services and equipment industry. 30 years of work experience in telecommunications & datacommunications - from design to production, services, marketing, sales, business development, regulatory affairs, lobbying, international organisations and consulting.

Interest in developing new international contacts for business development and for consulting cooperation in telecommunications, carrier, investment and/or lobbying projects particularily in the area of Central and South-East Europe.

Werner Augustin’s Specialties:

Negotiation of Carrier Services & Interconnection Contracts, Business Development with International Telecoms Carriers in Germany, Czech Republic, Slovakia, Hungary, Slovenia, Croatia, Montenegro, Serbia, Romania, France, Spain and Switzerland, Sales & Purchasing of Telecom Services, from 2003 to 2005 Chairman of the ENPM ( European Telecommunications Network Planning Forum )
