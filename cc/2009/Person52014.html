Judy Schriener’s Summary

Web-online-social media proponent. Longtime journalist covering marketing/advertising/public relations for 5-1/2 years and then design and construction for 16 years. Previously spent a decade as an advertising and marketing practitioner. Now pursuing niche specialty of aging baby boomers and their (our) effect on the job market, design and construction industry, etc. Writing a book to be published by McGraw-Hill later this year (2009) on how to build housing for baby boomers.

Judy Schriener’s Specialties:

Writing, editing, Web sites and navigation, e-commerce, social media, blogs, podcasts, video, baby boomers' effect on design & construction as they continue to age, networking, mentoring.
