Elisa M. Ortiz’s Summary

Elisa M. Ortiz is a dedicated activist and organizer for social change with proven experience developing and leading advocacy and civic engagement campaigns, working with diverse grassroots constituencies, utilizing various social marketing and outreach tools, and training, educating and empowering thousands of people.

Her combination of strategic thinking, experience implementing nationwide advocacy campaigns, leadership skills, and relationship-building savvy are what you need to advance the mission of your organization, respond to the demands of your constituents, and grow your field capacity.
