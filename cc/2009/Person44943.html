Jim Brown’s Summary

An accomplished communications professional (14+ years) with a heavy focus in mobile. Expertise in sales, business development, strategic positioning, marketing, with expertise in planning, developing, launching and managing product/service companies in B2B, B2C and B2B2C markets. In some cases this has included the development and positioning of patent documentation for international filing. Experience includes work in both nimble start-up and corporate environments - areas include mobile content, aggregation, m-payments, mobile directory services and conceptual application development. MSc from Syracuse University in Telecommunications, in addition to University of Toronto, Strategic Leadership program. Interested in providing leadership to help companies get to the next level.

CO-FOUNDER of Mobile Monday Toronto chapter. Go to www.mobilemondaytoronto.com.
