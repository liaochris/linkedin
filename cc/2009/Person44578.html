Jay Philips’s Summary

An experienced Quality Assurance Specialist, specializing in manual and automated test design, defect tracking, development and implementation. Areas of expertise include web based testing, which includes functional, regression, automation and performance testing.

Test Management for multiple projects between various groups. Leadership roles in creation, maintenance and execution of QA activities, testing documentation and processes. An experienced technical trainer, writer and mentor on testing best practices, procedures and tools.

Solid understanding of software development processes, software life cycle, and quality processes and controls. Familiar with testing tools, which support a full software and system life cycle, including requirements tracking, configuration management, defect tracking, test team management, and automated regression test tools.
