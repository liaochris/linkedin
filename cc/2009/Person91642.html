Sherry Hall’s Summary

Marketing home care services by day and creating virtual campuses for universities in Second Life by night/weekends.

Possess solid experience working within Second Life as Project Manager on immersive education content development projects for clients such as Loyola Marymount University/LA, Fielding Graduate University, George Mason University. Adept at identifying and leading teams of programmers, 2D/3D designers and more to meet complex client objectives.

Mid to senior management marketing and business professional in the long term care industry for over 16 years, including Fortune 500 companies in regional and national positions, reporting primarily to executive management. Currently working for a home care company as Business Development Director.

Graduated from the University of Louisville with a B.A.S. in Communications (Public Relations & Advertising) and A.A.S in Interior Design, which included coursework at the J.B. Speed School of Engineering.
