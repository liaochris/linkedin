Ed Linebach’s Summary

Well, after 12 years and 2 months I have resigned from NationJob and accepted an offer to do some contract work for the State of Iowa.

I am currently supervising a work crew of 12 doing flood clean-up.

I have been with NationJob now over 12 years. In that time I have progressed from a fledgling beginning salesman to leading the company in annual sales after only 14 months and have since never been beaten in obtaining "top dog" status in terms of revenue generated.

I am now Director-eBusiness and am responsible for most site content. This includes all affiliate partnerships, advertising agency point of contact, and all media relations.

I have been honored many times by being asked to speak about industry trends and the recruitment industry in general as well as being published in multiple newspaper articles and assorted industry publications.
You will find me in the social networking arena under "jobboardguy".

NationJob is the oldest independently owned job board in existence as well as one of the highest trafficked. NationJob is proud to be celebrating 20 years in the recruitment realm in 2008 and look forward to unveiling many new features.

My long range goal is to continue to keep NationJob in the top site category as well as ensuring it remains one of the industry's elite resources for obtaining QUALITY candidates.
