Terrie S. Wheeler, MBC’s Summary

After spending more than a decade marketing attorneys at four law firms in the Twin Cities area, Terrie founded Professional Services Marketing, Inc. (PSM) in 1996. PSM is dedicated to helping professional services firms and their professionals achieve their marketing goals.

As a Marketing Consultant and Marketing Coach, Terrie has more than 25 years experience providing:
~ Strategic Business Communications Planning
~ Law Practice Marketing at all levels
~~ Individual Marketing Initiatives
~~ Firm-wide and Outsourced Marketing
~~ Regional Office Strategies
~~ Practice Group Planning
~~ Industry Group Marketing

In addition to working as an Attorney Marketing Consultant and Law Practice Marketing Coach, Terrie and the PSM team offer two unique programs to assist individuals in the development of results-oriented marketing strategies:

~~ Your Personal Marketing Coach ~~
Offering one-on-one attention from a personal Marketing Coach. Specializing in Professional Services Coaching to:
~ Lawyers
~ Bankers
~ Accountants
~ Financial Services Professionals and
~ Consultants

~~ www.MarketYourLawPractice.com ~~
A unique web-based tool for lawyers, this website is an affinity partner of bar associations across the US including California, New York, New Jersey, Massachusetts, Florida, Ohio, and Vermont.
Through this exclusive website, subscribers develop and implement a Personal Marketing Plan using hundreds of tools, tips, checklists, and templates.
Unlimited access to a 24/7 Virtual Marketing Coach is included.

Speaking Engagements - Terrie is a frequent presenter on topics related to professional services marketing and has trained for business groups, educational institutions, and professional services firms in Minnesota and throughout the country.

The lawyers at Popham Haik have all landed well and I am happy I was able to spend a couple years of my career there. Plus, the implosion of the firm really provided the catapault for me to launch my new business, Professional Services Marketing, Inc.

  • Director of Marketing

    Maslon, Edelman, Borman & Brand

    (Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Law Practice industry)

    1990 — 1994 (4 years )

    I truly enjoyed the four years I spent as a marketing director at Maslon. It's a stellar firm with great lawyers. The only reason I left was because I was recruited to serve as director of marketing for Popham Haik, at the time the third largest firm in the state with offices around the world. I simply couldn't pass up the opportunity to augment my regional experience with national and international experience.
