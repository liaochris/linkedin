Amanda Piegza’s Summary

My current position is to head Missouri S&T's institutional repository (Scholars' Mine). Although most of the 'basics' are in place (platform, applications for contribution, etc) it is still a work-in-progress as we aim to create a way to have our Federated search (through Ex Libris) search the repository, feed out our metadata to Google Scholar, as well as other endeavors. My other duties include digitizing university publications such as the yearbook, alumni magazine, etc and finding an appropriate platform for that digital content. I am currently teamed up with the Yearbook committee to work on digitizing the RollaMOs.

**Colleagues, feel free to add me, no matter what area of librarianship you're in! However, I will not recommend, nor accept a recommendation from, anyone I haven't worked with personally.***


*Experience in digital archiving and database creation and management and work with a few digital repositories. Platforms: Dspace, Documentum, and ContentDM.

*Advanced copyright knowledge, specifically regarding self-archiving, and have created a database of publisher policies.

*Experience with XHTML, CSS, XML, teaching information literacy classes, and more.

Amanda Piegza’s Specialties:

Digitizing experience and knowledge of best practices; coding HTML; XHTML (CSS); intermediate Spanish language skills; cataloging (DCQ metadata and MARC -spanish and english), experience with SIRSI, OCLC, and database management and navigation; Experience with Dspace and ContentDM; advanced public speaking; adherence to copyright law and policies; grant writing; event planning in a variety of contexts.
