Tyson Kirksey’s Summary

Since 1999, Tyson Kirksey has been creating, developing and marketing websites for clients and customers across the nation. With a bachelor's degree in Interactive Media and a programming and marketing background, Tyson is well-equipped to handle a project from start to finish (although he believes a website is never finished).

After receiving his degree, Tyson accepted a position with Zunch Communications, Inc. as an SEO Specialist and Web Developer. During his two and a half year tenure with Zunch, Tyson quickly emerged as a thought leader and respected project manager, and was rewarded with high profile accounts such as La Quinta, Compass Bank, FASTSIGNS, Experian and many others.

In November 2006, Tyson accepted a position with New Media Gateway, Inc. where he is directly responsible for search marketing campaigns for many high profile clients. Day-to-day tasks include competitive research, PPC campaign management, link development, site architecture and blueprinting, traffic analysis, conversion path optimizations, and client strategy meetings. Tyson is also the web analytics team leader, helping NMG and its clients identify and measure their online ROI. Tyson led NMG's efforts to achieve its status as a Google Analytics Authorized Consultant.

His approach is simple: Be honest and transparent with customers and work hard to demonstrate value for services performed.
