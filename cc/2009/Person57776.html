Laura Lorek’s Summary

I write about the Internet and technology at the San Antonio Express-News.

But I'm also interested in everything going on around the world.

In 2007, I participated in a fascinating fellowship at Columbia University on Globalization. It changed the way I live, my ethics and my world outlook.

In 2006, I went to Taiwan and Singapore with the East-West Center of Honolulu, Hawaii as an Asia-Pacific Journalism Fellow. I hope to travel back to Asia again very soon.
