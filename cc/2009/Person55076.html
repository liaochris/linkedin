Kevin Johansen’s Summary

Call me a social capitalist. I put a lot of energy into insuring the health of the communities I belong to, because if they're not in good shape it's harder on all of us. What this translates into is that I work every day to improve the business ecology, and I'd like to think that I'm talented enough and well enough connected to make a difference. So if you're a 'Keystone' provider of infrastructure, or have a new product or service that you think creates new and sustainable efficiencies for a market, I'd love to talk to you.

Kevin Johansen’s Specialties:

As the EVP BizDev of the BusinessCatapult.com, I work to create new resources for the business development ecology that utilizes 'evidence based entrepreneurship'.

As a Huge Partner I help Colorado emerging growth companies access 'accelerant' funds and to improve their operations & marketplace development strategies.

CMG is a private investment bank and equity buyout firm that specializes in services for the owners of privately held companies with annual revenues from $7-$50 million. They help with financing management buyouts, the sale of companies, corporate finance, subordinated debt, senior debt, institutional and professional equity, mezzanine financing, and financing turnarounds, workouts and restructurings.

  • Founder

    The Entrepreneurial Standards Forum

    (Venture Capital & Private Equity industry)

    November 2004 — March 2009 (4 years 5 months)

    The Enterpreneurial Standards Forum (www.ES2F.org) was started in early 2005 as a part of the DaVinci Institute to serve as an open, non-profit, standards-making body that develops standards that help entrepreneurial ventures and investors to make better decisions. It was spun out as an independent non-profit entity in July 2005.

    Current projects include:

    * Building a directory of business plan templates available on the web.
    * Building a database that can be used by startup companies to benchmark themselves.
    * Developing tools for assessing entrepreneurial teams.
    * Developing a standard that will allow business plans to be audited in the same way as financial statements (using GAPP or ISB) and quality systems (using ISO 9000).
