Brian Vicars’s Summary

IT Talent Scout for the SPR Companies specializing in recruiting Contractors, Contract to Hire and Direct Hire Salaried technical individuals in the Chicago market.

SPR is a family of IT professional services firms that deliver high-value technology solutions and in-demand talent that help organizations achieve critical business objectives and solve even the most complex technology challenges.

SPR has provided hundreds of Fortune 1000 and mid-market companies throughout the Midwest with high-quality services since 1973. Through our family of agile, responsive and service-oriented companies, SPR:

Provides Business Process Management solutions that turn vision into value using Microsoft technologies.
Delivers customized project-based applications & software.
Sources in-demand IT executives and senior technologists for full-time positions.
Enables companies to achieve greater diversity by engaging qualified people with disabilities.

We've built decades-long relationships with clients and consultants who know that we put their needs first. Our team of over 200 consultants is comprised of experienced, insightful technical experts who help clients successfully meet strategic objectives, seamlessly execute projects and maximize their technology investments.
