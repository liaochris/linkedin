Patrick Noonan’s Summary

Professional goal: Creating social value by improving the decision quality of managers and leaders, at all levels, across functional types, in all sectors.

We can accomplish this by fortifying natural, intuitive decision processes with careful, systematic reasoning -- and channeled, constructive creativity -- about:

-- decisions & alternatives
-- risks & uncertainties
-- tradeoffs among objectives
-- common and conflicting goals of other players
-- clear thinking about complexity & information

Coming in 3Q10: "Decision & Information Analysis," my new text on decision making & data analysis, published by McGraw-Hill/Irwin

Patrick Noonan’s Specialties:

Decision & risk analysis; game theory, negotiation & strategic thinking; the psychology of judgment & decision making; data analysis; optimization; spreadsheet analytics; structured problem-solving & communication
