Steven Heath, PMP’s Summary

I am a PMP-certified project manager with 15 years of operational and project management experience. My military and corporate experiences have given me strong leadership skills. I have led small, highly technical teams at the project level and large military departments. I believe in the importance of collaboration and cultivating relationships, regardless of the team size, is vital in achieving an organization’s goals.
