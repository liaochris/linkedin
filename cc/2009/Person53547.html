Karyn Romeis’s Summary

Twenty years as a learning professional. Seventeen of them as a jill-of-all-trades trainer and consultant covering the full spectrum of the learning cycle. More recently making the shift into blended learning solutions design, and then into L&D consultancy. Learning geek, passionately committed to learner empowerment. Deeply aware that learning is about change, and that many are resistant to change. Adept at finding ways to bring people on board.
