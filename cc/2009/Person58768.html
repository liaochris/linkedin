Les Blatt’s Summary

I am an experienced communications and new media professional. My career has included broadcast journalism (as an award-winning writer, editor and producer for both television and radio) and public relations (specializing in online media production and outreach). I have a solid grounding in multimedia and am an evangelist for the use of video, audio, photos and graphics by companies trying to get their message out to the world. I am skilled, and can offer guidance, on effective digital strategies involving blogging, podcasting and vlogging.

My goal is to continue my growth in these and related areas.

Les Blatt’s Specialties:

online production, multimedia, streaming/downloadable video and audio, skilled PowerPoint creation and presentation, public speaking, blogs, podcast creation
