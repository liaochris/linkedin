Chris Fabian, EHF’s Summary

Chris serves as President/CEO at Professional Service Solutions, LLC (PSS). The core focus of PSS is to establish long term service and reverse logistics support relationships that lead to cost effective, world class alternatives for our customers. PSS represents a select group of proven outsource partners that provide expert support services to manufacturers, OEM's, dealers, distributors, and service contract providers. We specialize in all areas of service and reverse logistics including:
 LCD Panel rework, repair and remanufacturing of panels: polarizer & backlight replacement, bonding, etc. of POS & laptop panels, touchscreen displays, etc. < 20".
 BGA/VGA PCB Board and Chassis Level Repair, Rework, and Recycling Services in a world class enviorment.
 DLP Light Engine Recycling for Front & Rear Projection DLP Products
 Customized Extended Warranty Service Plans
 Depot Repair Service (Manufacturers & OEM's)
 World Class Customer Solutions call centers
 Reverse Logistics and end of life support of products including rework, repair, refurbishing, recycling, remanufacturing and disposal.
 National Parts Support including Parts Receipt, Stocking, warehousing, Inventory Control, Pick, Pack and Ship.
 National Field Service Support and Dispatch Services
 Consulting Services for global Manufacturers and Dealers interested in streamlining their existing internal support organizations or require assistance in establishing complete service and RL support services for their products sold in the US marketplace.
 Recuritment and Placement Services for Service and RL Professionals. PSS works closely with many HR professionals, and directly with Manufacturers, Dealers and Affiliates in the Industry, successfully placing people in key positions. Contact PSS If you are looking to fill an open position in your business. We specialize in finding Service and RL Executives to meet your business requirements.
