Chris Wessell’s Summary

I have been in the recruiting business for over five years and, although I've spent a significant portion of that time focused on technology roles, I have recruited for positions ranging from everyday business needs (accountants, salespeople, etc) to the unusual and/or highly specialized (flavor scientists, wind engineers, etc).

Besides direct experience in recruiting I also offer consultative expertise in recruitment process design (including metrics) and applicant tracking system selection.

I am on the Board of Directors of the Capital Region Human Resources Association, the local chapter of Society for Human Resource Management (over 800 members). I received my PHR certification in January of 2009. I am also active in the Capital Region Recruiters' Network and other networking groups.

Outside the world of recruiting, I am the alumni advisor for Tau Kappa Epsilon at UAlbany, president of my fraternity's Alumni Association, and a member of the Board of Directors of the UAlbany Alumni Association.
