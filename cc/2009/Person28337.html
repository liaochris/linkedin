David Rodecker’s Summary

DAVID has lead product development and technology companies for over a decade and has a history of creating unique online services. At RelevantAds, David has created a service that simplifies online marketing for SMBs; servicing more than 1,200 companies in it's first year and nearly 5,000 business listings. David built Local.com which became the fastest growing local search destination hitting 4.4MM unique visitors in 3 months. Prior to Local, David was a founding partner of Mail2World, where he created a multilingual email service that hosts 6.5 million mailboxes. Prior to dotcoms, David was in the financial industry with PIMCO where he deployed an automated bank-to-broker transaction processing system; under his management it exchanged trillions dollars.

David has formed partnerships with Google, Yahoo, Verizon, AT&T, Yellow Pages, TargusInfo, Amacai, ICQ (AOL), iVillage, EsMas and NetIdentity.

David has appeared on CNBC, TechTV and has spoken at conferences including: Inbox Event, ISPCon, Microsoft software training, and has been a guest instructor at Cal State University, Business School.

David graduated Phi Kappa Phi from in Engineering from the University of Southern California and went on to UCLA for his masters degree.
