Ellie Shoja’s Summary

Those who meet me may know that I am friendly and inspirational and that I teach dance lessons. Those who know me, know that I am also a writer in the process of completing a book on dance technique, that I am passionate about art, that I paint murals on occasion, that I can knit up a storm, that I can spend hours on my computer creating websites, flyers, brochure, vector drawings, etc. and that I enjoy brain teasers and puzzles. Those who know me well may know that I know 14 different types of solitaire, that I've never felt boredom, that I am a great teacher and trainer, that I sometimes make jewelry as gifts, and that I have a degree in Fine Arts.

I enjoy wearing many different hats, doing many different things, and continuously learning. The underlying and unifying thread that ties everything I do together is my dedications to pursuing what I am passionate about. And my biggest passion of all, the one that is present in every thing that I have done to this day, and the one that will be with me every step that I take into the future is my Passion for Inspiration.

I have been inspired by life, beauty, and love. It is my deepest and most intimate desire to inspire every person whose path I cross.
