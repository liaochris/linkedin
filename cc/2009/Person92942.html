Steve Nimmons’s Summary

Steve Nimmons is a leading Information Technologist with Fellowships in numerous professional institutions spanning the disciplines of Information Technology, Engineering, Natural History, Antiquaries and the Arts. He is a Freeman of London, an enthusiastic philanthropist and Rotarian with numerous active interests in world affairs.

Known for solving business problems and delivering efficient technology solutions combining innovation, best practice and assured quality. A leading innovator and technologist with pragmatic and sophisticated commercial acumen.

Highly experienced across all major vertical industries and with vendor solutions from IBM (WebSphere, Cognos, Tivoli), Microsoft (BizTalk, .NET, Sharepoint), EMC, Oracle (Fusion), TIBCO, OpenText, SAS, SAP. 17 years experience in Software Engineering and design (C, C++, Java, Prolog, RUP).

Steve Nimmons’s Specialties:

Technology strategy, communication, influencing skills, leadership, consulting and architecture including BPM, SOA, Integration, ECM / Content Management, Security, Business Intelligence and Emerging Technology. Interests also include collaboration technologies, mashups, RFID and cloud computing.

Prince2 Certified Practitioner, TIBCO and IBM certified professional.
