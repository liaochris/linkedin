Lisa Turner’s Summary

With over 20 years of experience in human resources, training and development, and process improvement through Six Sigma and TQM, Lisa has the breadth of knowledge and experience to solve business problems quickly.

Lisa also coaches executives who want to reach the next level of both personal and financial success. She uses logical problem solving techniques with proprietary goal setting strategies ("Six Sigma meets The Secret") so that individuals can achieve lifelong goals while increasing career happiness and balance. This, in turn, provides higher levels of career effectiveness and satisfaction.

www.YourAchievementCoach.com

Lisa Turner’s Specialties:

Personal Coaching, Executive Coaching, Goal Setting, Time Management, Business Productivity. Integrating Six Sigma problem solving with Emotional Intelligence. Human Resources, Process Improvement, Employee Training and Career Guidance. Teleclasses. Business Startups.
