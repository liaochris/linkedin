Ashwin Bhambri’s Summary

Founder, COO at TalentOnCampus – www.talentoncampus.com is the world’s only transnational career commencement portal with a presence in the US, UK, Europe & India. TalentOnCampus is soon to be a truly global IT product that incepted in India. TalentOnCampus is presently incubated out of the IC2 Institute , University of Texas at Austin www.ic2.org

I built this startup from idea to a complete business and have been assisted by a phenomenal Team & CEO – the last 3 years entailed a. building the product, b. building an A-Class Team and c. marketing TalentOnCampus internationally in the process we won the Lockheed Martin Global Innovation Program and the Technium – IB Wales Challenge. Source – www.indiainnovates.in | www.technium.co.uk

Prior to this over 8 years of work experience at large corporations in India & Internationally - Relaince, KotakMahindra, National Bank Financial. Have studied & worked internationally (Canada) for 4 years and have a good understanding of the North American Business Environment
