Graeme Benstead’s Summary

I love the internet and all the ways it has allowed us to communicate with like minded individuals all over the world. People with specific interests are, for the first time ever, able to share their passion with others with no geographical boundaries, it's a wonderful time for sharing, creativity and collaboration.

I have 7 years of experience in the online marketing industry; designing, building and marketing websites, online campaigns and brands whilst coaching businesses and individuals in the use of these technologies.

Social media has already changed the way a lot of us work, my goal is to help everyone gain the means and the knowledge to use these tools to improve their businesses, their understanding and the relationships they have with their customers.
