Jacob Burghardt’s Summary

I specialize in helping product teams to envision powerful, engaging, and productive user experiences for knowledge work. My highly collaborative consulting methods center on understanding, interpreting, and visualizing strategic design challenges in a way that drives decision making, shared vision, and design innovation in product development processes.

I have worked for industry leading clients on diverse application types, including aviation navigation, scientific instrumentation and analysis, product lifecycle management, specialized financial trading and back office operations, creative asset workflow, supply chain management, and online media.

My online e-book, "Working through Screens:100 Ideas for Envisioning Powerful, Engaging, and Productive User Experiences in Knowledge Work," is freely available for download on this page and at www.FlashbulbInteraction.com/WTS.html

SPECIALTIES:

User experience strategy / framework building -- Facilitating the development of clear vision, compelling positioning, and distilled priorities for complex interactive experiences

Product concepting / interaction design -- Visualizing the essential forms, contents, and behaviors of new or iteratively improved onscreen offerings

Workshop facilitation / participatory envisioning processes -- Collaborating closely with teams and stakeholders to sketch desirable, shared and actionable futures

Secondary / trends research -- Providing informative design context and new lenses on potential spaces of design opportunity

Expert user interface evaluation – Identifying opportunities for improvement in current systems and suggesting new directions for focused evolution or inventive exploration

Generative and evaluative usability research – Informing targeted design ideation and refinement with customer data from the field or laboratory
