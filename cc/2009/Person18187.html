Chris Samila’s Summary

Highly dedicated entrepreneur with double undergraduate degree looking to create a career with cutting edge green company.


In early 2007 while attending Arizona State University as an undergraduate I started an event management company with the goal of providing a platform for various stakeholders in the Southwestern U.S. to accelerate their sustainability efforts. This led to the creation of the GreenSummit Expo and Conference which was held in April 2007 and September 2008.

http://www.GreenSummit.net

The 2008 GreenSummit combined multiple traditionally separate events into one powerful and unique gathering.
- 5000+ Attendees
- Green Innovations Expo (120 exhibitors)
- Advancing Sustainability Conference (200 speakers and 100 educational sessions)
- Municipal Green Building Event (18 cities in Phoenix region participated)
- University Innovation Showcases
- Networking platform for emerging green industry leaders
- Eco-Tours of U.S. Green Building Council LEED sites
- Organic Chef Presentations for LOHAS consumer market segment
- Coverage by radio, magazines, and affiliates for every major television network, along with special feature story by Bloomberg TV

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The creation of GreenSummit was a fascinating and highly educational experience. As of winter 2008 I handed off my director position at GreenSummit Inc. to my business partners for future expansion. I am currently looking for an innovative career dealing more in-depth with sustainability in the energy, IT, and or green building sectors. I am specifically interested in demand-side energy management, smart grid infrastructure, and companies in the energy efficiency/renewable energy industry. My desire is to be deeply engaged with a rapidly changing sector of the new green economy.
