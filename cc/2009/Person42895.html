James Girling’s Summary

I work for Grainger West, a leading recruiter to Ernst & Young, PwC and Deloitte. We specialise in financial recruitment services for the majority of the Top 20 practice firms in the UK, as well as a number of government agencies and industry clients. Although our general focus is the UK, we also recruit for a large number of companies around the world and always have a variety of opportunities available.

As well as using our network of skilled auditors, security and finance professionals, we also headhunt to fill specific roles.

Personally, I am always willing to look at CVs of strong candidates as I take a proactive approach to the market and can find opportunities that aren't necessarily advertised.
