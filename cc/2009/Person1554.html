Alejandro Díaz-Caro’s Summary

I am a PhD student at Université Joseph Fourier in the Quantum Computation in Grenoble group which is part of the CAPP team at Laboratoire d'Informatique de Grenoble. I work under the supervision of Pablo Arrighi and I am researching on type theory and quantum logics.

Before comming to Grenoble, I obtained the title of «Licenciatura» in Computer Science (December 21th, 2007) at Universidad Nacional de Rosario, Argentina. It is a five-year degree with thesis, equivalent to a Master degree in the EU system. I also worked as teaching assistant at UNR.

Alejandro Díaz-Caro’s Specialties:

Quantum Functional Programming Languages, Quantum Lambda Calculus, Quantum Type Theory, Quantum Logic
