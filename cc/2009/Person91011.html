Shane Hudson’s Summary

Vision-driven, results-oriented arts, culture and entertainment professional with extensive experience in strategic planning, marketing, communications, media relations, management, fund raising, development, budgeting and finance for non-profit organizations, small businesses and for-profit corporations.

Knowledgeable in the incorporation of traditional and new media tools to deepen relationships, engage and build trust with inclined participants and current patrons. Social media enthusiast, practitioner, presenter and consultant.

Reputation for developing strategic plans of action that focus on long-term goals and short-term tasks.

Expertise in producing special events, promotions and sponsorships and developing business relationships.

Dynamic, creative and flexible team leader with the ability to train and manage both staff and volunteers.

Customer-service focused manager of operations, sales and merchandising.

Excellent written and oral presentation skills, strong analytical abilities, and a commitment to produce positive results.
