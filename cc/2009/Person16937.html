Charlene Li’s Summary

I'm an independent thought leader on social and emerging technologies, with a specific focus on social technologies, Web 2.0, and interactive media, and marketing. I bring to my thinking not only first-hand experience in media, but also strong strategy and business training and experience. I'm not afraid to tell businesses what they should do, but will always do so in a respectful, cooperative manner.

Charlene Li’s Specialties:

Business strategy and market positioning, trend analysis, market sizing, Web site design, email design, media strategies, writing and synthesis of ideas. Coverage topics include: social computing (blogs, RSS, podcasting, and social networking), consumer portals and search, media site design, local media, and online classifieds.
