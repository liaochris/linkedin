Andrea Coppedè’s Summary

I graduated with a diploma in Advanced Studies in Character Animation from AnimationMentor.com. Animation Mentor is an 18-month program taught by Mentors that are seasoned veterans of the Animation industry. I was mentored by Bret Parker (Pixar Animation Studio), Sean Sexton (Dreamworks) and Elliot Bour (Walt Disney Animation) just to name a few.

Below the link to my last Reel, shows some of the highlights of my work that I completed while working toward my diploma in Advanced Studies in Character Animation from AnimationMentor.com. During my time at Animation Mentor I had the opportunity to work with some of the industry’s best known Animators and I am now confident in my skills working in Animation.

www.andreacoppede.it

Before AnimationMentor I attended to a post secondary school in Computer Graphics in Rome, “Istituto Quasar” Design University, a generalist course which covered all the matters of a CG Production, from the Idea up to the final render. During the two years of course I met for the first time the Character Animation and from that moment I decided to follow this inspiration and passion trough Animation Mentor and now with a new experience in this field and hope with your company.

My working experiences are all in the industrial design field, a completely different field respect the animation, but in these years I worked in several team, environment and complex project that helped my to grow up in the Ability to work collaboratively and open to direction.
