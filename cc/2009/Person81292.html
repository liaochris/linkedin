Rachel Daniel’s Summary

Ms. Daniel is CEO of Synergy Marketing Strategy and Research, Inc.a full-service marketing consulting, brand strategy, and research firm. Synergy designs data-driven strategies to help our clients establish, maintain, and maximize trust in their brands. Founded in 2002, the firm boasts a unique blend of traditional scientific rigor in both our qualitative and quantitative research methods and innovation in our organizational theory and marketing strategies.

Synergy has designed marketing research studies (focus groups, ethnographic studies, telephone surveys, and mystery shopping studies) and strategic marketing initiatives (strategic marketing plans, communications plans, diversity initiatives, special projects, and outsourced tactical) for a wide range of sectors, including the financial services industry (Key Bank, National City, Prudential Insurance).

The firm also works with government and quasi-government entities, including the transportation sector (SARTA), municipalities (Cuyahoga County, City of Cleveland, Warrensville City Schools), nonprofit organizations (Akron Urban League, Cleveland Bridge Builders), and universities (Kent State University, Case Western Reserve University). Additionally, Synergy serves health care sector (Akron General Hospital, Life Line Screening, SummaCare), a number of retail organizations (Best Buy, P&G, GOJO), and communications and technology companies (Time Warner Cable, Microsoft Corporation, Alltel Corporation).

Some of Synergy’s longest running clients are advertising agencies. The company’s sweet spot is helping clients in every sector determine better ways to increase customer and employee loyalty. The Synergy team enjoys conducting multi-modal studies of internal and external stakeholder groups; it gives clients an opportunity to establish the most important competitive advantage in business today: TRUST.
