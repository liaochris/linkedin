    Townhall, LLC delivers news, information, and political commentary and analysis through its Web site. It also offers blogs, message boards, online chats, a conservative talk radio hosts section, a calendar of conservative events, activism tools, book and entertainment reviews, newsletters, jobs, and a historical documents archive. The company operates as an interactive community on the Internet to bring Internet users, conservative public policy organizations, congressional staff, and political activists together under an umbrella of news, opinion, and action. It serves as a one-stop mall of ideas in which people congregate to exchange, discuss, and disseminate the news and information from the conservative movement. Townhall was founded in 1991 and is headquartered in Washington, District Of Columbia. As of April 28, 2006, Townhall, LLC is a subsidiary of Salem Communications Corp.
