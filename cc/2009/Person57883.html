Laurence Virtue-Deshaies’s Summary

With a passion for nature and the great outdoors, Laurence has a sense for understanding a clientele. Born in Quebec but having lived numerous years in Vancouver, France and Africa, Laurence is a worldly traveler. She holds a Business Degree from one of Canada's top business schools. After carrying out numerous Marketing management mandates for large and small organizations over the past 5 years, Laurence joined Tremblant Living in 2008 as Vice-President Marketing with the mission to share our unique experience offering with our ever-growing clientele.
