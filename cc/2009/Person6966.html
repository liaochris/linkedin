Anthony Murillo’s Summary

Anthony Murillo has been practicing public relations for almost eight years. After short tenures as an executive at Burditch Marketing Communications (BurditchMC.com) and Atomic PR (AtomicPR.com), he now serves as Managing director and publicist for amPR, a firm he founded in 2003. Anthony has a stellar track record as publicist for the entertainment industry’s top & emerging actors, television personalities, directors, musicians, feature films and professional organizations.

Recent/current clients include Marisa Petroro (Actor), Amanda MacKay (TV personality), Nicole Pulliam (Actor), Lindsay Clubine (TV personality), Candace Kita (Writer, actor and model), Erica Shaffer (TV personality), Kevin Ryan (Actor), Deedee Pfeiffer (Actor), Trista Sutter (Reality TV star), Kelly Nyks (Director), Kathy Christopherson (Actor), Paul Todisco (Director/Producer), Betty McCormick Aggas (Talent Manager) and Alex Katunich former bassist for modern rock band Incubus.

Key industry events including the Opening of the Walt Disney Concert Hall, American Cinematheque Awards - honoring Samuel L. Jackson, American Cinematheque Awards - honoring Nicole Kidman, American Cinematheque Awards - honoring Steve Martin, American Cinematheque Awards - honoring Al Pacino and Grace Restaurant Opening in Los Angeles.

Film festivals including the AFI Dallas International Film Festival, Beverly Hills Film Festival, Seattle International Film Festival, Los Angeles Short Film Festival, Sarasota Film Festival, Garden State Film Festival, Connecticut International Film Festival and Berkshire International Film Festival.

Additional key client work includes/ has included the St. Regis Los Angeles, Four Seasons Resorts, Meadwood Napa Valley, San Ysidro Ranch, IDOL Vodka, E&J Gallo Winery, Rancho Zabaco Winery, Vinturi Wine Aerator, Michael Berman Ltd., LinkedIn.com, Bebo.com, Blurb.com, Hotwire.com, BitTorrent.com, Starbucks, Albertson's, Sony-Ericson, Pioneer and IMAX.
