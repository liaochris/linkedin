Stef Brown’s Summary

After 15 years as a marketer for premier league agencies like Landor Associates, The Partners and Blue Marlin Brand Design, I formed my own business in April 2009. On Pointe Marketing specialises in business development, marketing and communications for the design industry.

No job is too big or small – from writing strategies and plans (quick wins and long term), messaging, case studies, thought leadership, awards entries, event planning, press relations, media training through to copy checking (and much more).

Determinedly results focused, all On Pointe Marketing’s work is geared to build agency reputation and drive the business development pipeline.

Clients include the DBA, Keen as Mustard Marketing, Nude Brand Creation, The Workroom and WARL.

But that's not the only reason why I call it On Pointe Marketing... ask me the other story behind the name some time.
