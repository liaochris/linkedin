Toren Lehrmann’s Summary

Hello, my name is Toren Lehrmann, I have over 3 years experience in the video games industry, in addition to creating renders for architectural visualization, television, and the web. I have contributed artwork on four professional game development projects, as well as two Award Winning Unreal Tournament 2004 Modifications.

Currently, I am an Environment Artist at BioWare, working on the Star Wars MMO team.

I'm interested in meeting new people, so feel free to contact me!

3D Studio Max, VRay, Maya, Mental Ray, Photoshop, 3D Art, Graphics, 3D Modeling, Texturing, Rigging, Lighting, Rendering, Photo Manipulation
