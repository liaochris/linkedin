John Lampe’s Summary

I am a network security professional. I am an expert at creating tools which test networks or applications. I am intimately familiar with most of the major security applications (both closed and open source). I am an expert at black-box penetration testing and network protocol analysis. I am an expert at finding security flaws in all types of systems. I am well versed in Security Policies and Guidelines and have more than 10 years of experience working with Federal Government, State & local government, Fortune 500, and security vendors.

John Lampe’s Specialties:

Network defense, Security Policy, Security infrastructure planning and implementation, merger and acquisition planning and Risk analysis, audit, testing of new security products, development of new security products, management of security professionals, incident response, policy enforcement and compliance.
