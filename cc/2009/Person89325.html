Scott Addyman’s Summary

Energetic Consumer Electronics Sales Executive. Scott is a leader and a mentor of talent who inspires and motivates others by example, through experienced customer relations, and his enthusiasm for work, He is fully charged with a remarkable career launching products and managing key North American Consumer Electronics Retail Accounts including Mass Merchant, Big Box, Specialty Retailers, Etailers, Superstores and Clubs.

Mr. Addyman, possesses an entrepreneurial drive for results and has the ability to find creative solutions and generate revenues in a competitive market environment. Known for being the founder and sales leader of LaserSoft, Inc., the world's first casual games publisher to explode onto the scene on Target, GameStop, Best Buy, and Wal-Mart shelves, as well as, the founding principal of Addyman & Associates an award winning Minneapolis based Video Games and Consumer Electronics Manufacturers Representatives firm, pioneering the launch and growth of todays most recognized and established brands with then emerging retailers such as Target, Best Buy, Costco, Sams, and GameStop.

He has 18+ year sales career delivering multi-million dollar revenue, doubling growth for manufacturers of Consumer Electronics, Video Games & Peripherals, Home Entertainment Systems & Components, Home Theater, Digital Content for Download, Mobile Entertainment Devices, Computer Accessories, PC’s, Internet & Digital Music Devices, Home-Entertainment Controls, Gaming and Wireless Devices, Memory and Consumer Software.

His In-depth mastery of product placement at retail and online, implementing effective product launch-sell thru and end of life strategies with an eye for the top and bottom lines can only come from his experience and track record in penetrating & growing markets while planning and directing organization business processes and procedures.

He is able to reach and drive decisions possessing a "roll up your sleeves" and a "make it happen" attitude.
