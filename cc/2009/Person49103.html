John Elmore’s Summary

Mr. Elmore consults with leading law firms and companies on financial, economic, accounting and technology issues across a variety of industries and types of businesses, with particular emphasis on the valuation and analysis of intellectual property. His experience includes the calculation of damages, drafting expert reports and preparation of deposition and trial testimony in a variety of litigation matters. He has provided testimony as both a technical and an accounting expert.
