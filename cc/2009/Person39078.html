Dwayne Hall’s Summary

Experienced professional with over 21 years in the information technology industry, with a focus on providing technical infrastructure consulting/architecture services to a wide variety of clients. My process usually involves requirements gathering, consideration of alternative solutions, evaluation of solutions, design/testing and implementation of solutions, then revalidation that the solution has successfully addressed the original business problem.
