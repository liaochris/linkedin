Lee Gonzales’s Summary

I inspire success and ensure organizational agility using outstanding leadership, management, team building, planning, problem solving, organization, and communication skills. I couple these skill with business acumen, software engineering, IT architecture, and change management experience and the result is a track record of delivering high value, strategic products and applications. In 1997 I began working in the IT field, since then I have worked as a software tester, system administrator, consultant, developer, technical lead, technical manager and a director. I have worked in teams, lead & managed teams, and managed managers to deliver software applications and products in use by upwards of a couple of million people. I find that by constantly generating a steady stream of options and creative solutions I can keep myself and my teams agile and able to adapt to whatever assignments or projects we are given. I focus on producing high value, strategic applications and products which deliver foundational IT enterprise capabilities that deliver immediate and lasting value.
