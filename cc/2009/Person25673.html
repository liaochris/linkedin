Enoch Chapman’s Summary

Born of Fire and Flames, Enoch (or "The Enoch" as he would later want to be known as) came into this world with the sound of trumpets and heavenly choirs. His education and intellect were honed at an early age when, "The Enoch" crafted a microprocessor and programmed it to sing (digitally tone) Swanee River for a 4th Grade Science Fair. Since then his love of technology and devices grew from a childish facination into a mature exuberance.

"The Enoch" is a Professional Software Developer with 20+ years of experience working in various development languages including: Visual Basic, VB.Net, C#, Java, EJB, Delphi, Delphi.Net, J#, ASP.Net, ASP, PHP, XML, XSLT, XSD, HTML, Javascript, Dreamweaver, Shockwave, Lingo, JSP, Coldfusion, AJAX, SQL Server, MySQL, Oracle, Firebird, SQL, Access, Microsoft Office, etc. With specialties and aptitudes in Database Architecture, Application Framework Design and Multi-phase thinking, Enoch is one of the most sought after Consultants in the Utah Valley Consultancy Market.

His company, Wasatch Dev LLC, has worked with many prestigeous clients including: ModusLink, Propay, IBahn, Team Everest, Agel, Novell, PowerQuest (Symmantec), and many, many others. With the help of his business partner, Bryce Nielsen, Enoch has increased productivity in systems integration, data integrity and processing thoroughput that have saved companies several millions of dollars a year.
