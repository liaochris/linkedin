Jim Dee’s Summary

Began my career in the editorial and production departments of international business and legal publisers (Prentice Hall / Simon & Schuster / Aspen Publishers / Wolters-Kluwer). Worked there full-time from 1993-96, then as a contractor until 2002.

In 1997, I accepted a marketing / BD position within a regional Deloitte office for whom I wrote 100+ large-scale audit, tax, and IT consulting proposals ranging in fees from roughly $50,000 to $4 million.

Since 1993, I'd been involved in electronic publishing (CD-ROMs / web site development, etc). Because of this interest, I took an Internet management position at an international association of environmental scientists. From 1999-2001, I learned to manage larger-scale, database-driven web sites. They soon promoted me to Sr. Manager in charge of all organizational marketing communications and Internet development.

In 2001, I accepted an offer from a regional CPA and consulting firm to lead their marketing and business development functions (my current role).

Additionally, since the mid-1990s, I've been a freelance / contract-based writer, editor, publisher, marketer, web developer, PR consultant, and project manager (averaging $20k or so annually in billable work). I also serve on the board of directors of the Botanic Garden of Western Pennsylvania (currently serving as finance committee chairman during the kick-off of a $30 million capital campaign).
