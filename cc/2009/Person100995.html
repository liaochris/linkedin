Trip O'Dell’s Summary

I'm the senior user experience designer on Microsoft's Windows Live Hotmail team in Mountain View, California.

I work with some of the brightest and most talented designers, researchers, program managers and engineers in the tech business.

I get to work on the future of interpersonal communications for more than half-a-billion users. Its a fantastic gig that requires copious amounts of coffee, a good sense of humor, lots of patience and a generous scoop of humility.
