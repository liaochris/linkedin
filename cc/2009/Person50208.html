Jon Eland’s Summary

Extensively skilled in delivering effective online marketing and functional solutions I am now identifying the products, practices and services that will take the best in online editorial design from print into digital channels.

I have worked developing websites for over a dozen years and have experienced a wide number of sectors and organisation types and sizes - as well as having consulted on a vast array of web based solutions. These projects have included websites, content management solutions, recruitment campaigns, candidate management systems, e-commerce and online magazines.

I have worked in the capacities of client, supplier and sub-contractor and have set up development teams and associated processes, built sites myself, consulted as a freelancer and acted as project/account manager on several projects.

I am always up for a challenge - generally able to see the future and find ways of making it happen; realistically and focussing on identified goals and required outcomes. Engagement and delivering solutions that create effective two-way communications are part of my every day.

Practicing what I preach is great - so by combining my love of photography with a desire to understand how online communities function I have been very active in delivering the successful Leeds group on Flickr.com; hopefully culminating in a one day free photography conference.

The future may be digital but I also understand the history of print and see its role now and in the future. Viva la digital/analogue revolution!
