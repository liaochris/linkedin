Karl Newman’s Summary

A 25 year dedication to Corporate Training has brought Karl Newman and his company, Innoventions, Inc. to the forefront of Corporate Content Development and Rapid e-Learning technology. Karl has been on the Front-Lines of Corporate Education in almost every aspect of the training business.

Karl’s vast experience in Corporate Training and the love of technology based applications, allows him to transform ideas and information in entertaining and engaging training content, or what he refers to as “Traintainment”.

With flexible delivery vehicles and rapid deployment, our programs are designed to stimulate and spark the imagination as well as deepen understanding in Real World Solutions that are extremely valuable to Companies, vital for peak performance, and essential for success.

The design and development of Corporate Universities, combined with the integration into a Learning Management System (LMS) takes these training applications to a web-based... "anytime, anyplace, any-pace" access to e-Learning content and administration. This not only creates internal value to the many Fortune 500 Corporations he has served, but is highly respected by regulated industries where compliance training is essential and accurately documented.

The mission of Innoventions is to be the professional training resource of choice by consistently providing affordable quality training and performance consulting. Our goal is to establish strategic alliance with our Clients by delivering measurable results though excellent performance, and to be able to Exceed Expectations… not just deliver Education.
