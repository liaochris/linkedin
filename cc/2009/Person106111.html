Yuli Ziv’s Summary

Yuli Ziv is a co-founder and EIC of My It Things, a leading user generated fashion magazine and community. She is a founding member of Style Coalition, an alliance of independent online publishers in the fashion vertical. Style Coalition is responsible for the creation of Inside The Tents, the first portal for the coverage of NY Fashion Week in the social media.

Yuli is an avid speaker on the topic of social media as it relates to the fashion industry. She organizes and leads the Fashion 2.0 discussion panels and networking event series in NYC, bringing together fashion and technology worlds.

Yuli Ziv’s Specialties:

Social Media, Creative & Marketing Strategy, Branding and Identity, Art Direction, Web 2.0 Technologies, User Generating Content, Web Design, GUI, Rich Media, SEO, Viral Marketing, Design for Print, Writing, Community Building, Fashion 2.0

Yuli Ziv’s Experience

  • Founding Member

    Style Coalition

    (Online Media industry)

    January 2009 — Present (7 months)

    A coalition of independent online publishers in the fashion and lifestyle vertical committed to building a bottom-up, content-driven alliance whose mission is to both advance professional standards in content creation and increase the effectiveness of advertising messages within this realm.

  • Founder

    Fashion 2.0 Meetup

    (Events Services industry)

    May 2008 — Present (1 year 3 months)

    Fashion 2.0 is a monthly networking event series in NYC sponsored and hosted at the W Hotels, bringing together entrepreneurs, emerging designers and online publishers who are passionate about fashion and technology. Running for more than a year, the group has about 600 active members who gather monthly to network and hear from the industry leaders on how they utilize modern technologies in the fashion space.

  • Co-Founder & Editor-In-Chief

    MyItThings.com

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Online Media industry)

    March 2007 — Present (2 years 5 months)

    My"It"Things is a unique venture focusing on User Generated Content and trends. Started as an online user generated magazine, MyItThings.com is an ultimate community for trendsetters and bloggers, which allows personal expression in forms of opinion sharing, product reviews and recommendations. MyItThings online publishing platform provides exposure to its writers and contributors, as well as a great source for unbiased information for its readers. Yuli Ziv serves as an Editor-In-Chief of the magazine and oversees design, content and marketing strategies.
