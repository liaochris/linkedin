Kate Morris’s Summary

Marketing is my passion, and that covers all kinds. I pride myself in being a balancer, bringing together the new forms of media with the old to reach every potential client possible.

My education is in traditional marketing and advertising with a focus on customer relationship management. The core of my experience and joy in marketing though is in Search Engine Marketing. I am a certified Google AdWords Professional and have been a practicing search engine marketer since 2003. I specialize in web usability, code optimization, and paid placement (PPC).
