    1986 — 2005 (19 years )

    JOAN HARNED
    Joan has lived in Eagle County since 1972 and has been selling real estate with Black Bear Real Estate Co., predecessor of Keller Williams Mountain Properties since 1986. Joan was the 1998 Vail Board of Realtors “Realtor of the Year,” served on the Vail Board of Realtors as a Director for six years and was Chairman of the Vail Board of Realtors for 2003-2004. Joan also was appointed by Governor Owens to be a Director on the Great Outdoors Colorado Trust Fund Board. In 2004, Joan became an owner and Managing Broker for Black Bear Real Estate. In 2005, Joan became Managing Broker & one of three owners of Keller Williams Mountain Properties. She is a graduate of UC at Santa Barbara. After graduation, Joan worked as a flight attendant for a major airline for 20+ years, prior to her real estate career. Joan has been married to Tom Harned for over 39 years, has one son and nine delightful grandchildren. She enjoys running, walking, golfing and snowshoeing in addition to working at least full time.
