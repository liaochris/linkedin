Ajoy Mallik’s Summary

Ajoy Mallik is currently the Global Head, Venture Capital and COIN for TATA Consultancy Services (TCS). COIN is the worldwide external co-innovation effort of TCS.

He brings strong operational & scale-up experience to the co-innovation and venture/private equity efforts of TCS through his roles as advisor and board member to several start-ups, and as head of Sales and Business Development to several others. He is also an individual investor in start-ups and other private asset classes.

His investing experience started in 1998 with Skyblaze Ventures, an early stage venture fund in Silicon Valley, investing in capital efficient companies across the sectors of consumer internet, software, and wireless applications. He also served as the operational and bizdev anchor for SkyBlaze's portfolio of companies.

Ajoy has an MBA in Information Systems and International Business and a B.S, Physics, from the University of Maryland, College Park. His knowledge about the VC/PE industry and cross border investments has made him a regular speaker at various conferences.
