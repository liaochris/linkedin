Dario Passadore’s Summary

International business consultant with extensive background in process improvement, human capital, profit engineering, operational effectiveness, strategic marketing, branding, marcom, direct marketing, business development, and sales. Resourceful problem solver with demonstrated excellent communication and leadership skills. Shown aptitude to coach, mentor and develop direct reports. Fluent in English and Italian with good knowledge of Spanish. Proven creative skills and contagious enthusiasm complete the profile.

Dario Passadore has 12 years of international experience as marketing professional and, most recently, as a successful business consultant. After serving as Category Manager for Carrefour Italy for a few years, he decided to move to the United States to obtain an MBA from the University of Florida. As a result of a very productive internship with Office Depot, he then received a permanent job offer in the corporate marketing department, where he assumed different roles with incremental responsibilities over the years. After a short stint as Director of Strategic Marketing at Wal-Mart Stores in Bentonville, Dario eventually became the head of marketing at Mosaic Sales Solutions, a leading field sales and marketing company in North America. In February 2007, Dario founded DARMIC Consulting, a boutique consulting firm that has provided effective and innovative solutions to clients in the Dallas Metroplex. Dario also collaborated with the George S. May International Company, a full service management consulting firm specialized in addressing the needs of small and mid-sized businesses across North America. As Project Director, he had the opportunity to lead several teams of talented professionals in creating and implementing programs and tools in the areas of waste, management, business operations, finance, and strategy for clients across different industries that generated several million dollars in cost savings.
