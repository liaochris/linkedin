Barbara Talmadge’s Summary

Custom window treatment and soft home furnishings design and manufacturing professional currently seeking new career opportunities and freelance projects involving draperies and soft home furnishings, including design, manufacturing, consulting, educational writing, editing, photography, print and web related projects.

Barbara Talmadge’s Specialties:

Window treatment design, manufacturing, and detailing expert with over 30 years of experience in drapery and custom soft treatment marketing, production, installation, and customer service. Proficient in Dream Draper G4 window treatment and space planning design software program, and currently pursuing additional expertise in interior and product photography, Adobe CS3, InDesign, Illustrator, PhotoShop, prepress, XHTML, and CSS.
