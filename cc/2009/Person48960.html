John Brougher’s Summary

John Brougher serves as New Media Communications Manager for the Democratic Governors Association (DGA) in Washington DC. John's been working with technology and communications for years, and has a passion for technology, new media and marketing. Previously working as a Regional Field Director for Jill Long Thompson's campaign for governor, John has served as a speaker for conferences and panels and emceed numerous events, from fashion shows to cultural events. He has been selected three times by the China Care Foundation to emcee benefit fundraising dinners, raising money to support orphans in China.

John Brougher’s Specialties:

Public speaking, marketing, technology marketing and communications, technology training to broad audiences, student affairs, new media, podcasting, blogging
