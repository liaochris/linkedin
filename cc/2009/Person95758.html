Sumer Sethi’s Summary

Sumer Kumar Sethi is an Indian Radiologist with great academic record having many national and international publications to his name. His other achievements include authoring handbook of Radiology “Review Of Radiology” for medical Students which is a country wide best seller. Special interest in Medical Informatics and Creator Of a web site- http://www.indianradiology.com , www.sumerdoc.blogspot.com one of the premier Radiology Portals worldwide. This site is an online Radiology Magazine, which has been featured in the Times of India, Radiographics, BMJ, Journal of Thoracic Imaging, RT image magazine & International Society of Radiology. Creator of the concept of Radiology Grand Rounds. CEO of Teleradiology Providers, www.teleradproviders.com which is one of the pioneering company in India providing Teleradiology support to small towns around Delhi & in places likel Kabul and Nepal. He is one of the invited national faculties in Indian national conference IRIA 2008 Bangalore for Teleradiology. Currently, working as consultant radiologist in VIMHANS & NMC diagnostics, Delhi one of the leading Neurology referral centres in the city. Course director DAMS, Delhi academy of medical sciences, Non profit society helping medical students prepare for various post graduate examinations. www.damsdelhi.com . Editor in chief for Internet Journal of Radiology
