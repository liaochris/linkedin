Sanjeev Himachali, GPHR’s Summary

Sanjeev Himachali is a HR Professional with extensive experience in domains like Talent Identification, Talent Acquisition, Talent Management, Employee Engagement, Career Planning, HR Research and Compensation and Benefits.
