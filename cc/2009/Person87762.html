Emilio Saez Van Eerd’s Summary

Starting a new job (May 2009) at Jumbo Supermarkten in Veghel as a product/category manager for internet wines.

Before this I worked in Spain as an export manager for Bodegas La Casa de las Vides (D.O. Valencia) , and Bodegas Tintoralba (D.O. Almansa) and exported Spanish wines form many regions to pretty much everywhere.

I used to write about wine, talk about wine, and teach wine at the International "Spanish" Wine School in Alicante.

I guess I just like wine too much, and I am not planning to do anything else. Wine is my life!
