Vincent Peppe’s Summary

Business, technology and IP attorney, experienced as in-house counsel.

• Corporate and IP •

Advise companies and business owners regarding business formation and ownership; mergers/acquisitions; joint ventures; strategic alliances; e-commerce; consumer protection and privacy; corporate policies and procedures; and licensing of trademarks; copyrights; trade secrets; and domain names.

Review, negotiate and draft commercial agreements including product distribution and reseller contracts; vendor and supplier agreements; marketing and affiliate sales agreements; employment, consultant and non-compete agreements; domain name sales agreements; letters of intent; and non-disclosure agreements.

• Media & Entertainment •

Negotiate and draft content license and distribution agreements for broadcast, Internet, mobile, games and consumer electronics platforms. Music; books, feature films; TV shows; sports events; and other content.

Advise independent cable TV channels regarding broadcast carriage agreements, syndication and digital distribution deals.

• Communications & Marketing •

Advise businesses regarding communications with consumers via print, broadcast, Internet and mobile platforms.

Handle compliance with legal standards and industry guidelines for publishing; advertising; marketing; promotions; sponsorships; contests; sweepstakes; events and endorsements. Matters involve DMCA; COPPA; CDA; Telephone Consumer Protection Act; CAN-SPAM; FTC regulations; Lanham Act; right of publicity; defamation; unfair competition; and state consumer protection statutes.

• Dispute Resolution •

Represent clients in litigation, mediation, arbitration and administrative proceedings. Familiar with PTO and UDRP rules. Breach of contract, copyright and trademark infringement and other business disputes.

• Licensed: New York, Connecticut and Tennessee •
