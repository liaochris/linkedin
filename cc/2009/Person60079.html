Loren Hicks’s Summary

I’m a senior businessperson, IT executive and large-scale project manager with proven success in difficult situations. A specialist in getting results quickly while transforming organizations to build capability for continued performance. A relationship builder inside and outside the organization. An innovative thinker always looking for ways to grow business, increase profits, and reduce costs while prudently measuring and managing risk. Recognized as one of Canada’s top project managers.

I’m currently working with a second-stage ecommerce firm to expand their offering, and a well established firm seeking to rationalize their global IT. I am always open to discussing new opportunities, as long as they are challenging. Whether restructuring to meet new realities and goals, recovering a struggling project or organization, getting hard decisions made and implemented, or realizing an innovation to capture new markets, it is the challenge I welcome.

Loren Hicks’s Specialties:

Business knowledge across many verticals - eCommerce, manufacturing, securities, transportation, banking, insurance, leisure, and more. Specialist in project recovery - bringing new thinking and discipline to make faltering initiatives deliver. Solid knowledge of streamlining businesses processes, from marketing through fulfillment. Lots of experience in strategy processes, technology management, and technology organization management. Solid salesperson, and manager and coach of salespeople.
