Shanti Hadioetomo’s Summary

Communication professional with experience in graphic design, event management, marketing, promotions and website maintenance. I'm also a social media enthusiast.

I currently serve as 2009/2010 Association Liaison Chair at Society of Graphic Designers of Canada (GDC/BC), an active member of IABC/BC chapter and Wired Woman Vancouver chapter.

In my spare time, I enjoy watching films, dining out, long walks in downtown Vancouver. In the summer, I like to rollerblade and fly a kite. Buzz me if you're looking for rollerblading buddy or fly a kite together.
