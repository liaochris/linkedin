Joshua Tomeoni’s Summary

Mortgage Planner with extensive experience in both home financing and financial planning. My goal is to help people fit their mortgage in with their overall financial plan. Unlike traditional loan officers, I am a Mortgage Planner. I have found that most people in the mortgage industry treat home loans as simply a commodity. This is a huge mistake for the biggest financial decision most people will ever make!

A mortgage can cost you thousands of dollars if done wrong and save you tens of thousands if done right.

My goals are to be personable and educate my clients on what Mortgage Planning truly is and help them save the most money when buying or refinancing their home. I do this by creating a unique mortgage plan for every client and help show them how to escape hidden fees and charges.
