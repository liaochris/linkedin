Kawania Wooten, CMP’s Summary

Innovative senior-level association executive who excels at competitive strategic planning, program development, convention management, relationship building, and volunteer management. Expertise in developing programmatic initiatives that provide added value, reduce operational costs, streamline internal processes, and standardize association policies and procedures. Vibrant communicator called upon to lead speaking engagements at major annual conferences and leadership meetings.

Kawania’s twenty years of meeting and event planning experience for Washington DC corporations, non-profits, and foundations blend naturally with her years of experience as a skilled designer of wedding cakes and the ‘official’ party planner for friends and family. Her organization, creativity, and flair for the unique led Kawania to her passion, "wedding and special event consulting." In addition, her strong inter-personal skills, keen attention to the smallest details, and the ability to capture her client’s concepts, style and dreamscapes are the hallmark of Kawania’s successful business.

Kawania Wooten, CMP’s Specialties:

Competitive strategic planning, program development, convention management, event planning, wedding planning, meeting planning, relationship building, contract negotiation, alternative dispute resolution, and volunteer management.
