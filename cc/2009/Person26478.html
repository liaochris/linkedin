Diane Downey (Diane@YardFairy.com)’s Summary

As a life long gardener, I love to share my passion for landscapes with my clients to help them create a unique backyard (or front yard) oasis using environmentally sensitive techniques. I bring my background and experience in project management and communications to the process so that my clients landscaping needs are serviced in a timely and smooth manner.

Diane Downey (Diane@YardFairy.com)’s Specialties:

Low water, low maintenance gardens that transform your yard into a space that provides peace and tranquility, while meeting you and your family's needs, and in a way that is environmentally responsible.
