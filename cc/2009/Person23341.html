Franklin Samir Dattein’s Summary

I work as a Software Engineer since year 2000, mainly with Java Platform and Web development.

I am working at HP Computers, in Porto Alegre, Brazil. HP gave me the chance to work in 10+ different projects and acquire experience and maturity.
Currently, my roles are "Technical Leader" and Software Architect in a Remote Printing Management Solution for Window CE. This is a very challenging system that includes the development of a embedded web server, with limited resources, a MVC Framework and a Web Services Framework, basedo on Windows Communication Fondation.

In 2002 I have created the PortalJava.com, one of the biggest Java online communities of Brazil, with more than 35.000 thousand Java developers.
In 2007 I was named the JUG Leader of the RSJUG, the Java Users Group of my State.

In the past I have developed web applications for Credit Card Management, Management of the buildings of the Brazilian government, Data Mining and Marketing tools, Human Resources management and Duty management, also for the government.
My area of Interest are Content Management Systems. I have been developing an Open Source CMS, with several web 2.0 features.

My professional goal is to move to an English speaking country, to learn a new culture, enhance my english speaking skills and work with Content Management Systems.
