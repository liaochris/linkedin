Rosmalia Hardman’s Summary

An experienced marketer, hospitality start-up specialist, professional speaker and workshop leader for business seminars and conferences on the topic of travel management, sales, marketing and brand since 1995. With experience spanning over 30 years in the travel and tourism industry, Rosmalia is an expert in the area of marketing innovation, corporate branding, integrated marketing and communications, converged media solutions, consumer insights, customer care, business acquisition, account management, revenue optimization and balanced scorecard.

Rosmalia has held senior leadership positions in Jumeirah, W Hotels, Mandarin Oriental, Rosenbluth International, The Fullerton, Pan Pacific, and Melco China Resorts. After spending four years in the airline industry, she began her hospitality career in 1981 as a member of the pre-opening team with the Dynasty Singapore (now Singapore Marriott). In 1986, she took on marketing and sales responsibilities.

An experienced and highly passionate marketing and branding professional with proven skills in leading change across the organization, shaping the company's public profile and building new capabilities within the marketing department and across the company. A specialist in hospitality start-ups, Rosmalia’s pre-opening portfolios include luxury hotels, villas, restaurants & bars, marina & yacht club, golf & country club, sports club, beach club, ski resort, conference and convention facilities.

Highly skilled in pre-opening hotel and integrated resort operations, strategic planning, re-structuring, re-aligning and re-designing of sales, marketing and business operations, revenue management, travel management, event management & planning, and business development.

Rosmalia Hardman’s Specialties:

Hotel Pre-opening & Launch; Business Development & Acquisition; Account Management; Strategic Planning & Management; Change Management; People Development & Training; Revenue Management; Corporate Branding; Brand Positioning; Integrated Marketing and Communications; Marketing Innovation; Loyalty Program Management; Media Art Direction; Converged Media Platforms; Social Marketing.
