Ronda Densford’s Summary

I specialize in residential real estate. I particularly enjoy working with first time buyers and as of late, have become very active in the short sale market. I have listed several short sale properties and would be happy to help you through the process of selling your home as a short sale. I would also be more than happy to walk you through the process of buying a home as a short sale. The process is long, but well worth the time and effort it takes! I am a Certified Short Sale Specialist.

I have a very strong support group of mortgage brokers, bankers, appraisers, Home Inspectors and other real estate pros to help make the purchase process smooth and easy.

Buying and selling real estate doesn't HAVE to be so stressful! Call me today to discuss your Real Estate needs.
