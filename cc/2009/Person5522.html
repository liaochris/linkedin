Andrew Breen’s Summary

An experienced technology entrepreneur and leader ranging from startup software product and service companies to heading product development groups within larger organizations, I have driven the formation and growth of several businesses through successful launch and expansion. My deep product and business strategy experience combined with my professional and academic background in software engineering offers me a unique ability to fully understand the problem space. I can bridge the often challenging gap between product/design teams and engineering, and have developed agile and efficient product management processes that facilitate efficient execution on product vision but which are not too rigid to stymie innovation.

Formerly, as Director of Palm’s Services Product Marketing Group, I led the formation and strategic direction of Palm’s new wireless data services business. This included responsibility for product marketing and development, sales and market strategy, and infrastructure vendor evaluation and negotiation. The business grew from concept to one of the top three initiatives at Palm within one year.

I leverage my experience and knowledge of business strategy advising investors looking at the technology and consumer products industries. I was the key industry advisor to Harbinger Capital on their $100m investment in Openwave (OPWV) where I engaged the Board and management with a turnaround strategy to refocus and trim products and return capital to investors.

Currently, I am advising several companies including being actively involved with Talk2Us. Talk2Us makes communications relevant with a next-generation voice and text solution providing control and context lacking in today's systems. For early stage businesses, I help with product development across mobile/telecom and Internet services including setting the vision, identifying customers and partners, and executing on product development.
