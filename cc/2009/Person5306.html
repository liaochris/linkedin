About my Current Job
As International Medical Leader I work in the Medical Marketing Team of one of the key Biotech products of the Company in the Basel Headquarter.
My mission is to deliver excellence in medical knowledge of the product with a focus on Inflammatory Chronic Arthritis, Manage Key Opinion Leaders' Network, provide insight and develop Clinical Studies, support the marketing strategies and the positioning messages in a consistent way with a good balance between science and business, but always aiming the best for the patients with severe diseases.
My keywords are Mobility, Flexibility, Accountability, Competence, and above all "DO-IT-NOW".
I strongly believe that Medical Affairs must have a creative attitude, thus I feel very confortable with medical marketing activities and strategic developement.
