Tony Kremer’s Summary

Since 1990, my company KINETIC PRODUCTIONS INC. has been providing Field Production Services (ENG/EFP) to a wide variety of National and International clients (NBC Sports, CBS News, ESPN, MTV, Golf Channel, ABC, EuroNews, BBC, CNN, RTL Germany).

In addition, I've been fortunate to Freelance for many of these same Broadcast clients, while also performing Location Sound Services for Film & HD clients (commercial, corporate & documentary).
