Scott Wild’s Summary

I am an outgoing team player that sees more potential in others than they see in themselves.

I love to create, entertain, educate and inspire with my products, services and skills. I am a perpetual student of life. I am willing to share my God-given abilities and talents with others.

My life motto (as learned from my mentor, Bob Proctor) is "Think. Believe. Action. Results." What you think (most often) eventually becomes what you believe. Your beliefs drive your actions. Your actions determine your results. Those result reinforce what you think and the cycle starts all over again. This the very essence of how people and the Universe work together. The key is that you can positively change this at any time of any given day. It all starts with a decision.

I am a risk-taker who knows that my goals and dreams are already headed in my direction. I believe if you have a crystal-clear vision of where you want to go, the "how" doesn't matter.

The brick walls that life puts in front of you are not intended to stop you, but to give you an opportunity to prove just how bad you really want it.

I have worked in many different industries from the small mom & pop shops to a national radio network as a show producer. Each job and experience I have ever had is just one more tool that I carry in my toolbox. I live by my grandfather's words "Can-do is easily carried around".
