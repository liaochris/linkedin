Rachel Kokosenski, M.Ed.’s Summary

With a background deeply rooted in the love of learning inspired by my parents in my own childhood, I believe that educational travel is vital to the learning and development of children. Through combining common practices, hands-on knowledge, and cultural enrichment early in life as part of growth process, I am devoted to helping parents plant the seed of the love of learning early in their children. Using the specially designed research and planning tools of Travel 2 Educate, I strive to construct an easy-to-use and easy to access information platform for home school parents.

Please visit http://www.travel2educate.com to learn more.
