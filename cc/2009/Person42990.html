James Maas’s Summary

I've been totally immersed in the whole concept of the Internet, and taken by the idea of communicating with the entire world, cheaply and efficiently, since first hearing it discussed at Berkley back in the early 90's. It's been said that if I couldn't find a way to get paid for working on the web, I'd make the money elsewhere and do the web thing for free. Fortunately, that hasn't happened, too often ;-) and I've managed to be involved, much of the time profitably, since 1991.
