Victor Currie’s Summary

From the official company bio:

Victor Currie’s directing and producing work has taken him around the globe, and also spanned most every media. Partnered with long-time collaborator Scott Robinson in Robinson Currie Productions, Currie is actively involved in development of film, television, and new media projects, and consults to various companies on marketing and new media.

As a producer, Currie has overseen projects for Fox Television Studios, A&E Networks, satellite broadcasters, syndicators, and more. He has directed award-winning commercials in both the USA and Europe, multi-camera live and live-to-tape broadcasts, and corporate marketing media. He has anchored and hosted both live and taped broadcasts, hosted a weekly live radio call-in show in Los Angeles, and reported for both television and radio. He has also consulted in the creation of several satellite and Internet broadcast services.

He was a founding partner of television production company M Creative Group, CEO of production services provider CRT Media, President of Venture Media Productions and commercial/corporate production company Broadcorp Productions, as well as Executive Producer of the Video Information Network satellite television information service. In a more traditional business role, he also spent two years as Chief Operating Officer of Beverly Hills-based business training and personal development company Life Motivations, Inc.

He is a Broadcast Journalism graduate of the University of Southern California, and also admits to a few years as a Universal Studios tour guide. He lives in Newport Beach, California with his wife Erica Watson-Currie, PhD and son Nick Charles, and is the president for 2008-2009 of the Rotary Club of Newport-Balboa.

Victor Currie’s Specialties:

Directing, producing, writing, hosting/anchoring, voiceover, editing & motion graphics design, creative consulting. Experienced in producing both within the major studio/network system and in the independent world.
