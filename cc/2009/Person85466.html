Rob Woollen’s Summary

Member of the Institute of Training and Occupational Learning and the
Guild of Holistic Therapists.
My current goal is the Ironman UK race in August 2009.

Professionally, my mission is to make wellness a norm in the workplace. There are so many ways in which we can do things with wellness in mind- often at little or no cost. This way we can build a productive and motivated workforce to take us round the corner and into the upturn after recession.

Rob Woollen’s Experience

  • Corporate Wellness Manager

    Rightway Complete Wellness Solutions

    (Privately Held; Higher Education industry)

    July 2007 — Present (2 years 1 month)

    As a young, vibrant company, Rightway is helping UK businesses to be more productive, healthy and energised through health and fitness education, motivation and stress management and wellness initiatives. We help companies to understand how their working practices and culture can affect their employees, resulting in happier, more motivated staff that want to work for them. My job is to plan these initiatives and devise effective, efficient implementation to give the client satisfaction and results. With a supplier network numbering over 300, we can reach the whole of the UK.

    In addition to offering wellness interventions, we offer workplace wellbieng audits to find out what is right - and what is not - about your current wellness provision. We also train workplace health champions, offering the RSPH Level 2 Award in Understanding Health Improvement.

    Clients range from PLC's to architecture practises and local government offices to universities.
