Steve Andrews’s Summary

Steve Andrews is a Microsoft Team System MVP and INETA speaker, and has been working as a developer for more than 9 years. During this time, he has designed and developed applications in such widely varying areas as trust accounting, medical information management, supply chain management, and retail systems. Most recently, he has been immersed in Visual Studio Team System. Steve is very active in the developer community, and is a frequent speaker, author and trainer. Steve is also an MCP, ICSOO, Speaker Liaison for the Philly .NET User Group, and all around .NET fanatic.
