Jenny Dibble’s Summary

I'm experienced in developing and managing e-marketing programs from the ground up; identifying and capitalizing on new media opportunities; and integrating lifecycle marketing to drive revenue, profits, and growth.

I thoroughly enjoy conducting training and one-on-one consultation on the internet marketing space, helping small business owners capitalize on the tremendous potential for business growth through online mediums.

With my company, SearchMarketMe, we're helping small business owners make more money through online marketing initiatives, such as search engine optimization, paid search, email marketing, blogging, online PR, and social media initiatives.

We specialize in working with small to medium sized service businesses, with a specialty in lead generation to drive sales.

Check out my blog: http://blog.searchmarketme.com

Jenny Dibble’s Specialties:

I focus on a holistic internet marketing approach, utilizing all aspects of online marketing to ensure no stone is left unturned. These tactics include search engine marketing, including search engine optimization (SEO) and pay per click as well as advanced email marketing, website analytics and usability testing, online PR and other new media avenues. The bottom line? It's not all about SEO!
