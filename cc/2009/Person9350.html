Jim Owens’s Summary

Design for web and print using flash, dreamweaver, flex and InDesign. Illustration (technical and otherwise) using non-digital methods, illustrator, painter, photoshop, autocad, sketchup and zbrush. Photography using film, conventional digital and HDRI methods and macro, medium focal-length and telephoto lenses. Typography and typographic design. Hand coding with HTML, CSS, MXML and AS 3.0. Use of PHP, mySQL and AJAX with dreamweaver and in pre-constructed applications such as wordpress and joomla. Research, planning, copywriting and editing.

Jim Owens’s Specialties:

Turnkey design and implementation of all aspects of small and medium-sized web sites and print projects. Technical graphics. Extraction-based industries. Nature, science and/or travel based work.
