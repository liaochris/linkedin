Kristopher Patel’s Summary

A set of fresh eyes is what I bring to the table.

Small businesses have a great opportunity in using Internet Advertising and Marketing to connect on a new level with their customers, clients, and partners. It is an effective and efficient use of resources to garner a desired brand image, sales numbers, and end-user engagement.
