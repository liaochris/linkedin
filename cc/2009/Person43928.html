Jason Dick’s Summary

I am a skilled fund development professional. I have worked within corporate giving, granting, major gifts, and annual fund and grassroots fundraising. My experience has been within education, health care and social services in both the USA and Canada.

I am fervent about the community and social change. And I am passionate about non-profits securing funding for their mission. I am in regular contact with many business and non-profits globally. Feel free to check out my blog for information on how to start your own fundraising plan: www.ASmallChange.net.

I would love to connect if you are:
-a non-profit with questions about fund development
-a business looking for a non-profit to partner with or need help creating a giving plan
-an individual looking to help non-profits either through financial donations, networking, or creating and evaluating business models

Jason Dick’s Specialties:
