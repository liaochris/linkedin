Iben Rodriguez’s Summary

Bio2009: Iben Rodriguez is an Infrastructure Consulting Professional with over 20 years experience working in complex IT environments. Iben has an extensive knowledge of VMware-specific environments having spent 2 years working for VMware/EMC in various roles. Iben has delivered complex projects for international pharmaceutical and semiconductor companies, ecommerce companies and large city governments. Iben is considered one of the foremost industry experts in Enterprise Virtualization security and infrastructure design.
Eco-Virtualization Evangelist - Helping save our planet by promoting virtualization of security appliances for our enterprise data centers.

Contributor to ESX Server Security Benchmark: http://www.cisecurity.org/tools2/vm/CIS_VM_Benchmark_v1.0.pdf

"Hands-On" IT Manager responsible for Telecom, Local and Wide Area Networks, Email/Exchange system, Web Servers, VPN, Firewalls, Source Control system (Perforce), Development servers and laptops etc. as well as helping establish best-practices for high availability and security. Responsible for managing the External Applications Hosting Data Center.

Security Manager at VMware. Worked with EMC Headquarters. VCP 2006.

ESX 3 and Virtual Center 2, Storage Area Networks, Ethernets, Firewalls, Wireless.

Customer Application Manager supporting end users at a large biotech traveling to remote areas for distributed client server application. Oracle forms, SQL*net on Windows NT, Macintosh, Solaris in an FDA validated environment.

4 Years in US Air Force as Telecommunications Equipment (teletype, crypto, punched paper tape) Technician.

Volunteer for 1988 Seoul Olympics, Santa Clara Swim Club (SCSC), and Peninsula Bible Chuch, Cupertino (PBCC).
