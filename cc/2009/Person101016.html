Trish Beach’s Summary

Having owned several small businesses, Trish Beach knows what it is like to compete with big chain stores. That's why she initially joined as a member of Your Local City, a marketing, network and advertising company that s based here in Austin TX. They helped her home-based business be able to play on a level playing field. And beyond!

Now as Regional Ambassador for Your Local City, Trish will show you how you too can effectively and affordably compete with "The Big Dogs" and come out ahead of the game. She is a wealth of information for you and your growing business as a Certified Niche Network Marketing Professional. As your Referral Source, you seldom see her without her Palm Treo overflowing of contact information. From knowing who to have you contact when you have a question or business need to referring clients to you to keep your business successful, she is ready.

One of Trish's favorite part of her job is facilitating and helping start up new networking groups in the area. She is currently Facilitator of YLC’s Leander/Cedar Park Networking Group which meets every Wednesday morning in Leander and Co-Facilitator of YLC’s Christian Business Networking Group which meets every Tuesday morning at Hill Country Bible Church on Hwy 620. Stay tuned for information on her new groups in Marble Falls, Pflugerville, Hutto, Lakeway, Dallas, Pennsylvania, West Virginia, California, Arizona, and more!

Trish is also looking for team members to help her promote local businesses. For information on how you can start a fun and easy home-based business, contact her today,

As owner and Creative Facilitator of Gabby's Crafts Focus Board Workshops, Trish can also show you how the use of a Focus Board (aka Vision Board, Goal Board, Treasure Map etc) can bring your marketing goals to life. Learn more at http://www.yourlocalcity.com/directory/Gabbys-Crafts-Focus-Boards.html
