  • KIK project

    Noordelijke Hogeschool Leeuwarden

    (Educational Institution; Higher Education industry)

    February 2008 — July 2008 (6 months)

    Together with the KIK foundation, we thought about creative ways to alter the image of problematic youth in the northern region of the Netherlands. Often these young people have real dreams and want to be given the opportunity to better themselves. If only companies believed them a bit more, they too could make the world a better place with their talent and ambitions. We as a team of students were asked to develop a plan to better the image of these people with multimedia.
    We did our best and came with a marketing plan that consists of a three phased plan involving a viral campaign, a community website and concepts of real meetings between different groups of people (business, youth, public).
