Romain Boisseron’s Summary

I studied at first in Montpellier a city in south of France, in "classe preparatoire", which is a long drill lasting between two and three years to prepare students in France to several national contests to enter engineer schools. This system is quite unique in the world, where engineer school could be understood as University specialized in the industry and company field whereas common university in France would be more dedicated to research and teaching.

I finally could enter the engineer school of my choice, the "institut d'informatique d'entreprise" which is, to my mind, one of the best computer related school in France.
