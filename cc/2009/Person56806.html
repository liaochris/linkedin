Karl Thompson’s Summary

Senior Designer.

17+ years experience in the design industry - designing and producing user interfaces (UI/UX), websites, identities/logos, multimedia, illustrations, print work, presentations, digital photography, signs, interiors etc. The complete package.

I can undertake the design of a companies identity, websites, software etc. whatever is required.

This can typically involve:
1. Creating a company logo and product logo(s).
2. Creating a corporate style guide to be used any person or company that produces any future work for the company.
3. Creating a new company website - design user interface, build and maintain the site (or advise on a suitable CMS solution to enable the client to update).
4. Design and produce company stationary, business cards, letterheads etc.
5. Design and produce product/services brochures.
6. Produce a template for corporate presentations.
7. Produce any required illustration work.
8. Produce any required displays or signage designs (or advise on signage).
9. Shoot any required digital photography (products, locations etc).
10. Consultancy - maintain relations with the client and train staff (when required) or train-in a replacement "in-house" designer, to continue any ongoing work in the style and quality of my work (using the corp style guide).

I always aim to be professional and courteous, and complete my work with "more than expected" results.

Karl Thompson’s Specialties:

User Interface / User Experience Design, Web Design with HTML, CSS and Flash, Print / Graphic Design, Illustration, Digital Photography. Client liaison and consultation. Ideation. Full creative services solutions. Vast experience and knowledge.
