Robert Ford’s Summary

A pragmatic visionary with deep, hands-on experience of start-up, turnaround and growth environments, seeking an executive leadership position with a dynamic organization that is determined to leverage Information Technology to improve how they connect, communicate, collaborate, compete and win.

An accomplished entrepreneur and executive leader (CEO/CTO/CIO), my track record is characterized by well-diversified information technology, business development, communications and general management experience. I have successfully led multiple integration, consolidation and transformational activities, and a consistent theme throughout my career has been identifying, assimilating, and operationalizing technologies that positively change the way that business is done.

I’m currently considering new opportunities to put my skills and experience to work in an environment that inspires passion. In particular, I'm seeking the following:

- Senior Executive position (CEO, COO, President) in a well-funded, emerging growth company / startup.

- Senior Executive position (CIO / CTO) in an established mid-sized company that is looking to transform the way that it does business, and how it connects with its stakeholders (shareholders, employees, partners, customers, community)
