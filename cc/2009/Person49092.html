John Wilson’s Summary

John has worked within the capital markets environment both as a practitioner and consultant for over 17 years. He has held board level positions with major financial institutions and has been proud to have acted as an advisor to many of the industry's leading names. He ably fuses experience in markets, processes and technology to produce innovative solutions to business challenges and opportunities. A strong innovator and leader of change, John relishes the opportunity to problem solve and marry "technology solutions" with problems. His enthusiasm and keen entrepreneurial spirit generates momentum in any project or business setting.

Career Coverage
• Change Management – Leading the design and implementation of large-scale business process and technology change programmes
• Corporate Strategy - Strategic planning and business/product development in capital market firms
• Line management – functional, financial, risk, regulatory, commercial and general management roles
• Experience with buy-side, sell-side, custody, clearing and exchanges, able to offer broad and deep subject matter expertise of front, middle and back office from both a business and technology perspective
• Significant asset classes exposure to equities, bonds, ETD and OTC derivatives and FX/MM

John Wilson’s Specialties:

Derivatives, Equities, Fixed Income, FX, Treasury, Web 2.0 technologies, Workflow, Process Design, Integration methods and technologies, Large Programmes of change, Corporate Development, Change Management, Transformation, Programme Management, Operations, Trading, Clearing, Settlement, Process Automation, M&A due dilligence, post M&A integration
