Loni Carr’s Summary

The Colonial Group is an executive search and recruiting firm that has assisted companies of all sizes in the recruitment and selection of top talent from around the nation. Every assignment managed by our firm includes the involvement of a principal experienced in helping clients build and strengthen high performance management teams.

The Colonial Group’s recruiting resources are second to none. Services include video conference interviewing, public records background checks, behavioral assessments, referencing, resignation counseling, offer acceptance assurance, partial to turn-key relocation programs.

Loni joins our team with over 8 years in Human Resources and Operations Management. With a background in recruitment, employee relations, and customer service, she understands the role that quality hiring plays in an organization's success. Loni's style is to get to know her candidates' likes and dislikes and she especially enjoys the aspect of enabling them to attain their career goals. Loni has her BS in Business Administration from Old Dominion University.
