Stuart Oliver’s Summary

Feel free to connect with me info@stuart-oliver.com

Entrepreneur, Technology COO, Operations Director & Interim Manager

An experienced technology operations executive and interim programme manager who has successfully built start-ups in the media, publishing and real estate sectors.
He has a proven track record and extensive experience in shaping consultancy operations for the successful delivery of solutions on high-level strategic assignments. His clients have included major financial services businesses, retail organisations and City institutions.

Stuart’s leadership roles have typically encompassed building an all-important network of clients, partners and influencers.

Stuart has a passion for technology, risk and innovation and has demonstrated a capability for managing change in both start-up and mature environments. He has considerable expertise in developing and championing business technology benefits and has managed multi-million pound budgets and teams of up to sixty staff.

He is a member of the IEEE, Top Interim and is a member of Global Scot, an exclusive group comprising 900 influential senior business leaders. Stuart is an experienced keynote speaker, most recently presenting to over 120 businesses in Scotland at an event arranged by the Scottish Government.

Stuart has a passion for helping SME’s to grow sustainable businesses and his ‘get things done’ attitude is well known across his peers and in the industry.

Described as a ‘problem solver with passion’, Stuart excels at approaching situations with enthusiasm and having worked for global brands across the world though to start-up enterprises, Stuart takes adapting to different cultures and working environments in his stride.

Stuart can provide expert advice on a number of areas including starting and running a small business, IT interim management, technology for business, document management and business process automation.

Stuart Oliver’s Specialties:

I an a proponent of fractional working and the use of virtual teams. I have worked very successfully with others using this model that offers reduced client cost and far swifter reaction times to client demands.
