Norbert Romeijn’s Summary

Norbert Romeijn is a customer focused, motivated and energetic Supply Chain Manager with passion and 20 years of experience in 6 commercial industries, ie. upstream oil & gas industry, service, reverse & repair logistics/SCM in the high-tech EMS industry, electronics & fiber optics, chemicals & plastics, food & perfume ingredients as well as logistics services at DHL Exel.

He has demonstrated skills and qualities in supply chain management (SCM) concerning strategic sourcing, purchasing & contract management, demand management, planning, customer service, warehousing & logistics as well as reverse/service/repair logistics, project mgt, people management, biz controls & compliance as well as change management.

Norbert has successfully changed supply chain processes and organizations in large and international companies.

Norbert has built and implemented strategies for supply chain, sourcing/procurement and demand management. As MT member he has supply chain related P&L and balance sheet responsibilities.

Under his direction, inventory levels & lead times have been reduced, customer delivery & supplier performance and warehouse accuracy levels have been increased

He is a coaching leader, who is results oriented and who can lead large teams of employees as well as individual specialists.

Through having worked closely together with Operations, Quality, EH&S, Finance, IT and HR management, he has gained a high level of affinity, insight and engagement in these working areas.

Norbert is experienced in improving supply chains to Eastern Europe, Russia, Caspian and West Africa.

He has been member of the SAP implementation steering committee and has gained experience with mergers & acquisitions in the field of supply chain management.

He manages stress and is a strong communicator to all levels within the organization
