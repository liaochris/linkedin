Hans Veldman’s Summary

I am Owner & Founder of Hans Veldman Consultancy Solutions. Main business is CRM advising, implementations and support. Further competences Business Advizing & Analyzing and Interim and Project Management.

I have experience in starting up a new company and leadership. For SCAR Dynamics I was the Competence Manager of CRM. My responsibilities were setting up the Dynamics CRM cell, finding new business and people. In 2008 I have worked starting up iRelate as a new CRM company, so business development and sales.

I have worked in the CRM world since 2005 as a CRM Business Consultant. I'm also a Certified Dynamics CRM and MS Professional Consultant and owner of the Microsoft Dynamics CRM Blog (www.microsoftdynamicscrm.blogspot.com)

Since 2005 I have done several Microsoft Dynamics CRM implementations in version 1.2, 3.0 and 4.0 for small/medium companies. My roles during these implementations are mainly Technical and Functional Consultancy and Project Manager. Furthermore I'm giving Sales demos and Microsoft Dynamics CRM courses at all levels.

History
I started working in a restaurant as Manager. During this period I have gained experiences in leadership, running several restaurants, financial aspects and human resource.

My career in the Consultancy started on the Technical Microsoft Domain (Operating Systems, Architecture and Infrastructure). I have worked since 2005 till end 2007 for Atos Origin as a Business Consultant for Microsoft Dynamics CRM and Business Consultancy for the finance industry. During my time at Atos Origin I was also the Expertise Group Leader for Microsoft Dynamics CRM. I have written several proposals for CRM implementations, project planning’s, hour estimations, making/giving demos and giving training for CRM.

Furthermore I have gained experiences in the finance sector for mostly ABN AMRO and ING. I have international banking experiences for Due Diligence, Client Management Information and Management Reporting.
