John Sheridan’s Summary

John is Managing Partner and co-owner of Social Media 404 Inc., and also President of blindSpot Business Consulting Inc. He has over 25 years experience and specializes in creating innovative and proven best practice solutions driven by business requirements.

Mr. Sheridan has provided leadership and expertise on a number of key assignments involving organizational structure and performance reviews, business process and technology reviews, strategic planning, and implementation of architectures, systems and services through performance improvement delivery projects in a wide variety of complex environments.

Mr. Sheridan is also a member of the User Experience Network (UXnet.org) Board of Directors. UXnet is a California-based non-profit organization comprised of hundreds of volunteers around the world, who are experts in the field of improving the quality of experience a person has when interacting with a specific design. This can range from a specific artifact, such as a cup, toy or website, up to larger, integrated experiences such as a museum or an airport.

John is an experienced business and technology consultant, and assists clients with the use of Social and New Media methods and technologies to help clients improve customer relations and increase internal and partner effectiveness. In particular, John is pioneering the discipline of Community Experience (CX), and acts as a Social Architect to assist organizations with strategies to create highly effective online interaction.

He has conducted assignments across North America and Europe with telecommunications, natural resources, financial, public sector, with volunteer organizations, health, education, utilities, and internet-based businesses.
