Jason Haag’s Summary

Jason Haag's professional interest and background is in learning technology. He is currently employed by Stanley Associates, Inc. as Systems Engineering and Technical Analysis (SETA) support contractor for the Joint Advanced Distributed Learning (ADL) Co-lab. He is an ADL Sharable Content Object Model (SCORM) technical working group participant, member of the Defense Advanced Distributed Learning Action Team (DADLAT), IEEE Computer Society, and IEEE Learning Technology Standards Committee.
