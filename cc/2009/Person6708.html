Anne Nicolai’s Summary

Relationship development, innovation and writing skills are at the core of my career path. Before joining Best Buy in May 2005, I ran entrepreneurial ventures including 20 years of marketing/communications consulting and speechwriting for retail and other clients. Over the years, my client list included Target Corporation (Target Stores, Marshall Field's, Mervyn's, Dayton's, Dayton Hudson Foundation, Target Foundation, AMC), Jamba Juice, M&I Marshall & Ilsley Bank, National City Bank, Morton's of Chicago, Select Comfort Corporation, Faegre & Benson, Hormel Foods, 3M, General Mills, National Association of Child Care Resource & Referral Agencies (NACCRA), Thermo King, University of Minnesota Continuing Education, Minneapolis Public Schools Transportation Services. From 1989 to 1993, I worked at two PR firms and taught public relations writing courses at University of St. Thomas in St. Paul, Minnesota. From 2001 to 2003, I was co-CEO of a U.S.-China venture bringing entertainment and fashion merchandise to armchair travelers in 10 U.S. states.

Anne Nicolai’s Specialties:

Business development, relationship development, strategic planning, contract negotiation, sales, writing/editing, marketing, communications, sponsorship development, program development.
