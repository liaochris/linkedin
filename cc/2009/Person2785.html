Alberto Ciaramella’s Summary

I do presently manage Intellisemantic, a high tech start up company, which I founded in 2005. The company was hosted in the Incubatore Imprese Innovative del Politecnico di Torino and is focused to develop web2.0 and semantic applications for the enterprises for simplifying the collection and access of intranet and internet documents for portals, content management and intelligence. Before that I was long time a researcher at CSELT (now Telecom Italia Labs) in speech and language technologies, where I also supervised a software development team for integrating speech technologies in different kinds of applications. In my professional activity I published more than 30 technical and research paper and autored or coautored 4 patents. I partecipated to different european research projects and international standardisation bodies.
