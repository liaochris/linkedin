Monica M. Schultz’s Summary

Monica's core competency is making complexity understandable and--more importantly--adoptable. She understands complex concepts in science and operations, then relates them to both the mission statement and bottom line by making smart, informed recommendations; outlining strategies and tactics, and connecting people and information to ensure that everyone is focused on achieving a common goal—on time and in budget.
