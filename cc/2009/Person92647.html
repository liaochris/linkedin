Niki Browning’s Summary

I currently hold the position of Art Director for Mundania press where I oversee a small group of cover artists. My job includes one on one contact and negotiation with authors, scheduling and task assignments. In my role as Primary Cover Artist I design and layout covers for Mundania's high profile authors such as Piers Anthony, Anne Logston, Don Callander and Louise Cooper.

I have been working in the art industry for the past four years doing cover art. I work primarily in digital and am proficient in Photoshop, Poser, Vue, Carrara and Painter. I would like to continue to further my career in the freelance artist field and am always looking for new clients.

Niki Browning’s Specialties:

Art Direction, Cover layout and design, Digital content creation, task assignment, scheduling, basic management skills.
