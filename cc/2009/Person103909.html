Wayne Breitbarth’s Summary

I am proud Owner and President of M&M Office Interiors a full service interiors provider with products and services including commercial office furniture, raised floors, moveable walls, carpeting, interior design, space planning, project management, installation and delivery of office furniture. We are the exclusive distributor of Haworth office furniture and architectural interiors products (raised floors-TecCrete, and moveable walls) in southeastern Wisconsin. Haworth is one of the largest manufacturers of office furniture in the world. We have been providing office furniture solutions to the southeastern Wisconsin marketplace for almost 50 years. We have the area’s largest office furniture showroom with over 11,000 square feet of display area so that when you bring your team in to “check it out” you can really “test drive” products in a very unique environment. We are located at W233 N2833 Roundy Circle West, Suite 100 Pewaukee, WI 53072.
******************************************************************
I am also very passionate about helping train people on the business social networking platform Linkedin. I have trained thousands of people for FREE, on how to effectively use Linkedin offering a class called “Linkedin Speed Training”. You can contact me if you would like to attend one of my upcoming classes. I also have a weekly Linkedin Tips & Helps that is being received by thousands of subscribers also for FREE, let me know if you want to be on that list.
*******************************************************************
I am also a CPA (Certified Public Accountant) and am very active in several charitable organizations and believe strongly that we all have an obligation to give back to the needy and underserved in the greater Milwaukee area. I have recently started a once-a-month lunch mentor program for business professionals who want to help an MPS high school student with increasing their “social capital”, consider joining us?
