Sally Pudlo Chavez’s Summary

I believe that effective communication with the client and design team is the foundation for the functional basis for all interior design decisions.

I believe that it is my job to help people feel comfortable in their space and enjoy their environment through the use of color, texture, scale and materials.

I believe that it is important that every project integrate with its surroundings and the design should derive from the project needs and context.

And finally I believe that innovative and fresh ideas can develop from creative uses of ordinary and standard materials.

Sally Pudlo Chavez’s Experience

  • Senior Interior Designer

    Aviar Design

    (Architecture & Planning industry)

    October 2006 — Present (3 years 2 months)

    Sally Chavez joined Aviar Design in 2006 as senior interior designer. She has assisted Linda Mracek in directing the design team and developing new business markets. As a project management leader, she is able to calculate fees, write contracts, schedule workload and manage the team effort with attention to design continuity, details and documentation. She develops training criteria and programs for in-house staff. Sally is proficient and experienced on many types of software including AutoCAD (2D and 3D), Microsoft Office Suite, and Adobe Photoshop. Sally maintains good customer relationships with past and present clients, as well as favorable relationships with manufacturers, vendors and suppliers.
