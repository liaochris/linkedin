Paula Constantino’s Summary

Passion: Using technology to inspire, enlighten and empower at-risk women worldwide with practical, "how to" information and lifetime skills via digital audio information modules (AIM) -providing life skills and information to enhance lives of women... build self esteem, confidence and motivation; enhance communication skills; foster entrepreneurship; & so much more. In emerging economies, groups or individuals can listen & learn at time from anywhere in the world - even without any electricity using a handheld audio player.

NOW is the time for Change - with information that is easy to access, understand and pass on to others - WE CAN CHANGE the World!

As an attorney, I combined my analytical, creative and technology skills with my motivation and team-building skills - to create a web-based resource - women helping women achieve more success with less stress globally through a team of Mentors & others at WomesSupportTeam.com. & FindACoach Online.com.

Embracing my true passion, I founded the1000 Women Can Change the World.
