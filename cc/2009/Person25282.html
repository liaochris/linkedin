Debbi DiMaggio 510-414-6777’s Summary

Debbi DiMaggio is to real estate as Joe DiMaggio is to baseball: a charismatic winner with an exuberant style all her own. (The world-renowned baseball player happens to be Debbi’s third cousin.) Debbi’s reputation has spread from her native Piedmont throughout the Bay Area as a real estate professional in a class by herself. Her buyers love her tireless efforts to find them the right home and negotiate the best terms in any market, and her sellers couldn’t imagine the process without her circumspect management of the entire process. While Debbi makes her work appear effortless and even fun (a DiMaggio flair), this is by careful design, and the result of years of experience and hard work.


For the past fifteen years, Debbi has specialized in helping her clients relocate from across the globe, over the Bridge, and just around the corner. With this depth of experience comes an intuition that enables her to usher her clients into environments and neighborhoods that almost seem designed for their specific needs and wishes. Debbi’s reputation has resulted in a client list that reads like the credits of a Hollywood blockbuster, including such celebrities as Macauley Culkin, Julianne Moore, Sally Field, Hugh Grant, Linda Fiorentino, Ted Dansen, Tom Arnold and Jason Richardson. But whether you are a film star, professional athlete, CEO, teacher, firefighter or pastry chef, as Debbi’s client, you are always the celebrity.

Like her famous relative, Debbi DiMaggio discovered early what she does best, and then developed that talent to a high professional degree, making her delighted clients the real winners.

Please contact Debbi at 510.414.6777 or debbi@debbidimaggio.com to assist you with all of your real estate needs. She looks forward to your call.
