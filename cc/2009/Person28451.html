David Burns’s Summary

A test engineer looking for creative solutions to future problems, and an eye out for new uses for older technology. Test engineers typically are not locked into the traditional purpose of technology, since its imperative to be intimately with every function. Expert at isolating hard ware and software failures, determining root cause, modification of testing procedures to catch escapes, and meeting business units quality and quantity levels through out a products lifecycle. Engineer focused on the Semi-Conductor industry with expertise in all parts of a product lifecycle. My history as a Computer and Electrical Engineer has been for both midsize and large companies. Strengths include a hardworking, practical approach to responsibilities while looking forward to creative ways of improving my environment.
