Wayne Horkan’s Summary

Wayne Horkan FBCS CITP, FIoD, SMIEEE, MIET, is a highly talented, successful and experienced Technologist and Business Leader with an extensive career in IT / IS over more than seventeen years specialising in Architecture and the composition, delivery, and implementation of ICT systems. Intelligent, enthusiastic, objective, and passionate, especially about technology and the people who enable the benefits it can bring.

Current role is Chief Technology Officer (CTO) for the UK and Ireland at Sun Microsystems Ltd. (Sun), a globally renowned Technology provider and significant contributor to the Internet age. As Sun's leading technologist for the UK, I represent the UK and Ireland Technical Community and provide Technology and Architectural leadership to the Sun UK and Ireland executive team, across Sun's largest customers and partners, and to Sun’s consultancy and delivery organisations.

Outside of my work at Sun I am a Non-Executive Director at Street UK, a charity involved in tackling Social and Financial exclusion. I also enjoy socialising in a number of membership organisations, with a focus upon; Professionalism in IT, Enterprise Architecture, Directorial Excellence, and Social Entrepreneurship.
