Ming Chan’s Summary

As CEO/CTO of The1stMovement, Ming Chan brings a passion and dedication to Interactive technology rarely seen in the industry today. His Emmy® award-winning experience leading emerging technology assignments for Fortune 500 companies such as Apple, Cisco, Disney, Sun Microsystems and many others led him to start the award-winning digital communication agency in 2006. With offices in California and Colorado, The1stMovement focuses on using emerging technology creatively to build digital communications for some of the world’s most renowned brands including Adobe, AT&T, Comcast Entertainment Group and more.

Ming started his career as a software engineer in Silicon Valley developing video chat and compression programs. He then joined Design Reactor, a leading integrated digital marketing agency in 2002 and led their Flash design/development efforts. His work included web sites, games, and applications for clients such as 20th Century Fox, AMD, Hewlett-Packard, Intel, PayPal, Universal Pictures and more. His work has been recognized by numerous awards and publications including the Emmy® Technical Excellence Award, FWA awards, KeyArts Awards, ADDY, Omni awards and Horizon Interactive awards. He is also very active in both the local and national Interactive community and currently serves as a mentor in the American Film Institute's Digital Content Lab.
