Nolan Keenan’s Summary

Nolan Keenan is a highly experienced PMP and Prince2 foundation certified strategic Project/Program practitioner with experience across both infrastructure and application environments. Operating confidently at CXO level and combining a strong business and IT focus he has successfully managed and delivered a number of large complex initiatives for international blue chip corporations across the banking & telecommunications sectors throughout EMEA and Asia-Pacific.

With an Honours degree in Systems Analysis Nolan’s business management experience spans 18 years of which 12 years has been in Project & Program Management Consultancy. Exceptional communication and leadership skills and a strong delivery focus contribute towards his continued success.

Nolan Keenan’s Specialties:

Program Management, Project Management, Portfolio Management, Strategic Program Planning, Project/Program Recoveries, Data Centre relocations, implementations and upgrades, Network Infrastructure implementations and upgrades, Organisational Change Management, Operational Risk Management systems, Credit Risk Management systems
