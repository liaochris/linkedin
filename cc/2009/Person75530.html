Olga Brunner, MS CMC’s Summary

Olga Brunner has a proven track record of dealing with complex fragile elder healthcare issues and getting results.

Olga is a Certified Geriatric Care Manager who does what long distance families cannot, be there at a moment’s notice.

“As a senior care advocate, my goal is to help clients attain high quality care and services using a medical and social model.”

Olga's consulting firm, A Good Daughter, works with a wide range of long distance families who frequently make significant decisions based upon our work with their frail elders in South East Florida.

Our clients typically cite the comprehensiveness of our assessments, along with our analytical ability, integrity, and objectivity as being instrumental to their parent's ability to live safely as they age-in-place.

Olga Brunner, MS CMC’s Specialties:

Senior level professional with direct business and senior market experience including: Healthcare Management; Elder Care Management and startup; Imaging Management and startup; Medicare Compliance training for hospitals and healthcare corporations; Department of Elder Affairs Approved ALF trainer; Entrepreneur currently running two businesses with leadership position in Marketing, Education, and Community Relations; trained Nursing Home Administrator and Activities Director.
