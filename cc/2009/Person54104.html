Michael Keenan, Esq.’s Summary

Elder law practitioner in Glastonbury, Connecticut. I have been practicing law for over 11 years and my practice focuses exclusively on the areas of elder law, estate planning and probate. I provide clients and their families with peace-of-mind by addressing any legal issues regarding Medicaid ("Title 19") planning and the Medicaid application process. I prepare appropriate estate planning documents for my clients such as wills, trusts, powers of attorney, advanced health care directives and special needs trust planning for disabled family members. I also address all matters in Connecticut's Probate Courts, such as conservatorships and administering decedent estates.
