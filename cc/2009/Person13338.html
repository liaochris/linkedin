Brett Trout’s Summary

With over fourteen years experience as a registered Patent Attorney, Brett Trout assists clients with all types of patent, trademark and copyright registration and protection. From obtaining patents, to negotiating software agreements, to litigating intellectual property and internet issues, Mr. Trout seeks practical and effective solutions for both individuals and corporations. Mr. Trout is also the author of three books on Internet law, including his latest - Cyber Law: A Legal Arsenal For Online Business.

Brett Trout’s Specialties:

Patents, trademarks, copyrights, licensing, software, internet and all types of intellectual property and information technology solutions.
