Alexander Bosika’s Summary

Working for and working with proven/successful teams is the great imperative. You may possess great talent. You may have the knowledge. But you need leadership and agile organized teams which support your efforts to execute with laser-focused precision. Without this basic platform, great products are never created.

Are you in a rut? Read Seth Godin's "The Dip" for a quick primer on "opportunity cost". Don't accept the status quo. Don't settle for less. ALWAYS think BIG.
