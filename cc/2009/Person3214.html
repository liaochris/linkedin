Alexandre Douzet’s Summary

Alexandre Douzet is Co-Founder and President of TheLadders.com. In this role, Douzet is responsible for global business operations, product development, and helping to lead the company’s ongoing global expansion.
As executive vice president for the past two years, Douzet was responsible for driving the significant growth of the company’s core job search business unit and leading European expansion with the launch of TheLadders.co.uk. He also oversaw the launch of the executive resume service, the company’s first new product line beyond traditional job search. Prior to serving as executive vice president, Douzet served as vice president of marketing at TheLadders.com, overseeing and cultivating the company’s branding and customer acquisition efforts. Over the past 5 years, Douzet grew TheLadders.com subscription base from zero to over two million members.
Prior to TheLadders.com, Douzet was director of online marketing at HotJobs.com. Before joining HotJobs.com, Douzet worked at BMG Direct, the world’s largest music club, where he helped develop customer acquisition programs. Douzet also has experience in the catalog, fund raising and financial services industries. He was an instructor of Direct Marketing, Financial Planning and Analysis at New York University, and was a Marketing Analyst for Air France.
Douzet holds an MBA from INSEAD; a Master's in Direct Marketing from New York University, where he received the "Lester Wunderman" Award; and a Bachelor's in Business Administration from the University of Lyon III in France. He is fluent in French and English, and is conversational in Spanish.
