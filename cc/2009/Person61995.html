Marc Duchesne’s Summary

I am doing Fiber Optics and Telecoms since 25 years. Thanks to the Mac and the Internet, I am doing business as unusual since 15 years. Customer Satisfaction is my motto.
I have had the chance to begin my career at the very beginning of Optical Communications. I had the chance to work for a large american company before, during, and a little (too short) while after the Dotcom' Era. I have had the chance to learn Web 2.0 and Social Media before it became mainstream. Since 2 years, I am a lucky guy: I work daily on the reality of FTTH Fiber-To-The-Home and Broadband matters at large.

That is why I am focusing now on everything Sustainable Development: from truly innovative applications and usages of Broadband to Communities networks, from CO2 Footprint management to CSR Corporate Social Responsibility reporting, from you as professional to you as a person.

I would like to share this experience with you. To make your life better. Let's talk.
