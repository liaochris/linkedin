Walter Flaat’s Summary

The human side of technology. That, in one short sentence is the focus of my professional live. Most people would agree that ICT is here to stay, and that it is an ever increasing influence on or lives... but why then, is ICT it seen as some kind of external entity that has nothing to do with 'real' people?

I started my carreer as webmaster for the TV guides published by state television (just about the time I started to shave). After that I had the good fortune to be able to start my own company, which turned out to be a succes, with some major international clients. My parents came into service; the company was incorporated, and the company was able to sustain the family for about six years.

I realize to be extremely privileged to have had all these experiences at a young age and I do believe I have tried to gain all lessons that could be learned from them. My continual passion is to improve myself, be a better professional, and a better person in general.
