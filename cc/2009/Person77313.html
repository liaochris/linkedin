Patty Gray’s Summary

Ms. Gray has been a writer, editor, public affairs professional, corporate communications executive, interior designer, fundraiser, and internet entrepreneur. Alice Cosmetics is her first internet startup company.

A detailed look at the creation of the Alice Cosmetics brand can be viewed in the "Biz Blog" series of blogs by small business owners appearing each week on the Logoworks/Hewlett-Packard Small Business Website. The web address for this blog is http://smallbusiness.logoworks.com/bizblog/category/alice-cosmetics.

More about Alice Cosmetics can be found in "All Things Mineral Makeup" (www.squidoo.com/alicecosmetics) and "Story of a Startup" (http://makeupstartup.blogspot.com).

On-line retail sales, social network marketing, business writing, and expert copy editing.
