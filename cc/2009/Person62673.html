ABOUT MARIA
Maria Marsala entered the business world at 14 and hit Wall Street 3 years later. She climbed the corporate ladder without killing herself on that glass ceiling, exhibiting fierce determination and keen insights to became a bond trader and department manager at a time when women executives were a rare commodity.

She is a business strategist, coach, speaker, and author appearing on the radio, TV, and publications as Money magazine, NAFE, The Street, Women’s Day, The Bottom Line, and PC Magazine.
