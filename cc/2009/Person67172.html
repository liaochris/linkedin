Michael Buechele’s Summary

Since the early 90s I've worked in publishing, both print and online. Before the Tech Bubble burst, I joined DoubleClick in NYC’s Silicon Alley to learn about adserving from the best in the industry. Later, I went on to help some of the biggest and best publishers get even better; companies like Cartoon Network, Brightcove, Marvel Comics, National Public Broadcasting, CBS Radio, and NBC Universal. In 2007, I started 11|15 Media after seeing the tremendous opportunities in Performance Marketing, publishing and to have a platform to teach leadership. I’m currently writing a book on Motivation and Leadership.
