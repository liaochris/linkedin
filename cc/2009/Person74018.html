Nick Perry’s Summary

My primary responsibly is website traffic growth. I accomplish this by developing and overseeing new content strategies as well as tools and features that allow our readers to interact with the site. I manage our editorial staff and our video production unit.

In addition, I continue to write a blog that I began in 2004. The blog, which focuses on the stock market, serves as a test bed for new ideas and keeps me actively engaged with our reader base. It has also become a major source of traffic growth for our site.

My market comments have been printed in The Wall Street Journal, USA Today, MarketWatch, Dow Jones News Wire, the Associated Press, Reuters, and Investor's Business Daily.
