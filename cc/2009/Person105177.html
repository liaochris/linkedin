Wolf Gugler’s Summary

Executive search and recruitment industry expertise since 1985. HR Graduate. Focused on the home improvement industry since year one; asked to write human resources related articles and speak to large and small groups alike in this field. Broadened North American reach by opening a U.S. office in 2000; Professional staff and researchers located in Canada and the U.S. Also have a network of affiliates in place in most major Canadian cities. Gulger, Guler, Googler

Wolf Gugler’s Specialties:

Mid-Senior-level recruitment for home improvement, hardware and housewares industries; Retailers and Vendors e.g. Home Depot, Lowe's, Rust-Oleum, ITW. Categories include paint/decor, building materials, electrical, building materials. Titles; GM, VP Sales, Marketing Director, National Accounts Manager, HR Manager. Also consult to other non-related industries including oilfield, municipal, manufacturing. Always provide the same in-depth consultative and results-oriented approach we're noted for.
