Jessica Wallin’s Summary

My name is Jessica P. Wallin, and I am an entertainment blogger. I run my own celebrity website: Gravy and Biscuits (gravyandbiscuits.com). Past and present credits include celebrity sites Backseat Cuddler (backseatcuddler.com), Hollywood Crap (hollywoodcrap.com), and Gabby Babble (gabbybabble.com). I have also written American Idol news and interviews for Idol Mania (idol-mania.com/american-idol-fan), and music reviews for a Chattanooga weekly alternative called Enigma Magazine and cdreviews.com.
