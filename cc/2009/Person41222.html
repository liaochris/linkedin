Imad Naffa, P.E., C.B.O., CASp’s Summary

Founder, President and Sr. Engineer, NAFFA International, Fresno, CA, 2001- Present

International (100+ countries) online resources and content provider for Building, Fire, M-P-E, Structural and Accessibility Codes.

Bringing building code expertise and information to the masses and deciphering of the complex building codes.

Provider of expedited complete plan review (plan check) and building code consulting services to governmental agencies and international designers and applicants; building, fire code and ADA, ADAAG consulting services for all types of projects and complex/hazardous occupancies nationwide. California Certified Access Specialist (CASp) reviews. NAFFA utilizes Netsuite, Intuit QuickBase, Groupee and Google Apps in running operations and dispensing building, fire and accessibility/ADA code resources via the internet.

VP and Sr. Engineer, CMA, Fresno, 1988-2001.

Interm Deputy City Engineer, City of Sanger, Sanger, California, 1993- 1995. Various onsite and residential subdivisions site improvement assignments.

Interm Plan Check Engineer, City of Salinas, 1989-1993.

Sr. Engineer, CMA, Fresno, 1983-88.
