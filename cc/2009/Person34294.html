Frederick [FN] Noronha’s Summary

Journalist for two decades. Based in Goa, on the west coast of India, but regularly travels mainaly to cover the southern and western regions of this vast country (and beyond). Focusses on writing on Free Software, Open Source, ICT-for-development, IT and related issues. Also specialises in book reviews, blogs, alternative communications, radio issues, and writing about documentary film in India. Has written extensively on Goa, and the media in INdia.

Frederick [FN] Noronha’s Specialties:

An India-based journalist and photographer. Focuses on alternate issues, development, communications, ICT (see www.bytesforall.org), and themes listed above.
