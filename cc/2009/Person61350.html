Mae Lon Ding’s Summary

For over 20 years, employers have retained me to assist them to attract, retain, and motivate talented employees by designing employee compensation, incentive, and performance management systems that support the business strategy.

Expert witness in litigation for issues involving employee compensation, overtime, employee performance, employment discrimination, or wrongful termination.

For more information about my consulting, expert witness, and speaking services go to web site at http://personnelsystems.com

Mae Lon Ding’s Specialties:

I help middle market private and public companies, family owned businesses, and nonprofits. I design competitive and motivating salary, bonus, commission, phantom stock, long term incentives, equity compensation, and performance appraisal plans. I have worked with organizations in almost every industry including hi tech, biotech, financial services, professional services, real estate, cocnstruction, wholesale, retail, media, manufacturing, food, agriculture, utilities, etc.
