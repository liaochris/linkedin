Susan Reece’s Summary

Susan Reece led human resources and corporate strategic planning functions as a c-suite executive for over ten years. Susan’s international management experience provide insights into the opportunities and challenges that executives experience in the global market.

She has worked with organizations at all phases of the corporate lifecycle, from start- ups to mature companies, and in varied industries including distribution, consumer goods, manufacturing, pharmaceutical, and medical device. Susan consults in the areas of business strategy, organization development and executive development.

Susan Reece’s Specialties:

Susan Reece is a licensed industrial/organizational psychologist with a PhD from the University of Georgia. During her career, Dr. Susan Reece has been one of a select few within her organization to be sponsored for executive business education at the London Business School, Duke University and the University of North Carolina. Visit her website for more information.
