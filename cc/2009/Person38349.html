Gregory Bell’s Summary

Broad understanding of author relations, publishing processes, and development and marketing of print titles and online products. Currently handles authored treatises and practice guides on litigation subjects and is also a member of a team managing content development and taxonomies on Westlaw Business for legal publisher West, part of information solutions provider Thomson Reuters. Previously practiced law representing institutions of higher education, local and state governmental entities, and closely-held companies in labor and employment, corporate, education, and administrative law matters. Co-owner of business with his wife Dianna S. Leach, focusing on author and editorial services, project management, business blogging and social media consulting.

Gregory Bell’s Specialties:

Author relations, writing, editing, use of legal and other databases, publishing, electronic publishing systems, legal publishing, blog and social media consulting (including virtual book tours).
