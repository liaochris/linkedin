Molly Mednikow’s Summary

-Founder/Director of AMazon Community Animal Rescue, Education & Safety, June 2004 - Present. Resided in Peru's Amazon Region June 2004 - January 2008. Created first "Humane Society" in region, although NGO encompasses much more: public health, education, welfare, international volunteers, massive spay/neuter clinics for strays throughout Amazon region. Disaster relief organization in September 2007, following Earthquake. I currently operate Amazon CARES from the USA. Peru staff = 8, which includes 2 licensed veterinarians. ModernVet Clinic and large no-kill shelter in nearby jungle area. Awards include Doris Day Animal League "Kindred Spirit Award", 2006 and "Charities in Focus Photography Contest" Honorable Mention, 2007. Mission: To create a humane, healthy environment for all living beings in Peru's Amazon Region.

-CEO of Mednikow Jewelers Atlanta, January 1994 - May 1995. Named one of Top 25 Independent Jewelers in USA by Lustre (trade mag). Located in Phipps Plaza in Buckhead. High end items, exclusive brands & items created in our own shop (located at original Memphis store (since 1891). Sold business to family in 2005. Retail Entrepreneur of the Year 2006 by Buckhead Business Association, Award for Philanthropy, Crohn's & Colitis Foundation of TN and GA.

-Marketing Director of Georgia State University Exec,MBA Program, January 1992 - December 1994, Recruited Executives through receptions, ad creation & placement, phone contact. Edited a new 24 page color brochure.

-Congressional Liaison for National Jewish Coalition, 1990. Direct contact with Members of Congress and high ranking staffers through breakfast events and inidvidual meetings.
