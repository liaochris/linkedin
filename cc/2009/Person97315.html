Taymar Johnson’s Summary

Pittsburgh born Tee-Jay aka Slim, claims he's not a rapper but a businessman. A hustler in the truest essence he profited $300 a month from a lemonade stand and newspaper route at the age of 9!

His electrifying, humorous live performances have built a cult following from Pittsburgh to San Diego. He opened for DMC of Run-DMC and shared the stage with: Supernatural, LMNO, Wiz-Khalifa, The Visionaries, LA Symphony, Pigeon John, 4th Avenue Jones, JPB Inc, Tee-Jay has sold out the Underground Movement

He recorded the critically acclaimed Testimony and made headlines in Pittsburgh’s Mugzine, SD Music Matters, UK -based Hip-Hop Kingz co-signed for Tee-Jay’s music, and he's been featured in the Pittsburgh Post Gazette, Pittsburgh Tribune Review, San Diego Union Tribune, and North County Times,
