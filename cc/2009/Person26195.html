Devin Blanks’s Summary

Equipped with several years of experience at some of the foremost respected companies in the professional recruiting industry, I recently set out to incorporate my expertise, vast networks, and strong understanding of recruiting and staffing into the form of an organization that goes above and beyond the standard operations of most agencies of its kind. I have a very affirmed passion for what I do, as well as a strong belief and vested interest in surfacing outstanding opportunities for top candidates, and supplying solid companies with the very best professionals for the job.
Over the years, I've learned that whether they're small start-ups or large corporations, companies want to grow their organizations the right way. I find that companies really only want 2 things from their recruiter/staffing provider--quality candidates and great service. My goal, and the goal of my company, is to provide the overall best of the very best in recruiting and staffing! And, we're not just another ordinary staffing firm. Instead, we take a very consultative approach, and serve as a complete strategic, professional search and placement solution.
