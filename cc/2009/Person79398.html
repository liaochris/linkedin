Phil Tadros’s Summary

2001 : changed the zoning on a 105 unit building to open up a 4,000 square foot multimedia performing arts cafe set in a 1920's hotel lobby and ballroom called the "Chase" cafe located at 7301 N Sheridan, Chicago

2002 : developed & sold mobile store located at 518 Main Street Evanston, IL

2003 : developed mobile phone store located at 1409 Lunt, Chicago
