Luca Deriu’s Summary

Company administrator, technical writer, author, supervisor and videogame animation teacher. Always focused on 3D art, I worked first as Designer for big companies, then as freelance and now, I own my studio (PlaySys) with 11 people.
At PlaySys we work to produce high quality 3D images and animations, using Autodesk's softwares that helps us to do the best.
I worked as author for Hoepli's "3ds Max Design e Architettura" manual for the Italian market and I'm debugging some code of PlaySys videogame project. I work as producer, magazine supervisor and sometime as musician.
