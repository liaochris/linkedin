Dave deBronkart’s Summary

Insightful, analytical communicator, mapping what customers need onto what technology can do. Fast learner, experienced requirements analyst, product launcher. Career concentration in data-driven graphic arts workflows with highly usable UIs; recent exposure to converting legacy business processes to an object-oriented .Net / SQL Server 2000 environment including XML.

Dave deBronkart’s Specialties:
