Vernon Lewis’s Summary

I have applied my tech skills as a Spaceflight Operations Analyst at the Caltech Jet Propulsion Laboratory ( JPL ) for NASA in Pasadena for over 10 years. This work was primarily in the telecommunications division for the Deep Space Network where I worked on Voyager 1 & 2, Pioneer, Helios, Magellan, Viking, & Topex Poseidon, & Galileo projects. Also as Director of Administrative Computing at Scripps College in Claremont, CA, and as MIS Director for Fairplex in Pomona, Ca. I have also worked as a systems manager for Science Application International, Corp. at the National Training Center - Fort Irwin, CA, and for a number of independent contracts including General Dynamics, Kodak, Bell & Howell, Honeywell, & Singer corporations. I have been a liason and special projects manager with the Los Angeles County Fair, City of Pomona, JPL, Pomona Unified School District, San Gabriel Valley Economic Partnership, the Inland Empire Economic Council and several other organizations. Recently I have taught at Chaffey College and the City of Chino in addition to Upland Music School & Rancho Academy of Music.

Vernon Lewis’s Specialties:

Designing, configuring, and managing systems of different sizes and applications. Developing and instructing with operations and training materials. Assisting with needs requirements and configuration control. Integrated circuit design & manufacturing, and some network engineering.
