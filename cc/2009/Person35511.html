Geary Davis, L.Ac.’s Summary

I am an acupuncturist whose main focus is sustainable health, using acupuncture, microcurrent and colored light acupuncture, as well as other techniques, to treat many conditions. Microcurrent and colored light acupuncture are new technologies which synthesize the best of the best in leading-edge alternative healthcare. This is a significant improvement over previous technologies and is highly effective for facial rejuvenation, pain relief, and meridian balancing.

I also incorporate the use of syntonic phototherapy; mapping functional visual fields and treating with color through the eyes. This is a proven, effective healing tool after traumatic brain injury, as well as for learning and behavioral problems, and other issues.

My focus on sustainable health helps people to realize and remember how to heal themselves naturally and cultivate themselves with strength and flexibility to successfully weather their challenges, physically, emotionally, mentally, and spiritually. As we tend to our own garden (taking care of our Self), we find that we can thrive in any situation that arises.

I am a passionate voice for sustainable health! I am a practitioner, student, author, motivational speaker, inventor, entrepreneur, and philosopher. I also have experience as a terminally ill patient, a recovering patient, and a recovered patient – and the understanding of how I found myself in each category and what I did to move beyond those limitations as a spiritual being! These experiences have dramatically impacted me and I bring them with me to every interaction. I enjoy what I do and am grateful for the many opportunities to continue learning and sharing what I have learned to benefit others.

I host and facilitate a Thursday Evening Group, discussing spiritual topics.

Please contact me at gearydavis@gmail.com for information regarding a session - individual, couple, group, or workshop, or to request information on the topic of our next weekly group meeting.
