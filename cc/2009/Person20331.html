Constantine Roussos [linkedin@ music.us]’s Experience

  • Founder & CEO

    .MUSIC (dotMusic) Top-Level-Domain Name Extension

    (Music industry)

    January 2004 — Present (5 years 11 months)

    .music is the web domain extension representing the global music community, giving music entities a unique identity online. Not only does it increase a brand's visibility on the net, .music removes second-guessing & confusion by immediately associating a brand’s line of business with a memorable identity: music.

    Why .MUSIC?

    • .music is self-explanatory, memorable and enhances brand recognition
    • Increases brand visibility online
    • Dedicated domain branding vs. subdomains e.g Nokia.music > music.nokia.com
    • Enables market targeting & segmentation of your brand's music-related activities
    • Improves search ranking (SEO), marketing campaigns & conversion rates
    • Minimizes clicks to get to specific information
    • Enables direct-to-browser search (as opposed to only relying on search engines)
    • Protect and control your brand/intellectual capital/trademark/copyright
    • Gives opportunity to offer customized domains to affiliates/vendors/partners
