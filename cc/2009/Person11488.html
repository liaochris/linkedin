Bill Black’s Summary

I am fortunate enough to have contributed to some epic AAA titles, a few of note:

•Lineage II The Chaotic Chronicles
•Neverwinter Nights
•World of Warcraft The Burning Crusade
•Mech Warrior II

•Delta Force Black Hawk Down

Great sound doesn't sell games, yet audio is half the experience, great dialog, music and SFX can go a long way to enhance the immersive experience of a game.

I have 16 years of experience in the video game industry. I have made noise for PC, MMORGs,Social Networking Games PS1, PS2, XBox, Wii, iPhone, Facebook, Flash and Macintosh Games.
