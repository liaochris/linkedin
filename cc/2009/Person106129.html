Yura Liamin’s Summary

What is Lux?


We are an award-winning team of expert photojournalists with artistic flare, impressive professionalism and a passion for telling stories through the art of photography. Our work has been recognized nationally and internationally and has been presented in numerous publications throughout the world.

Lux Photo Studios presents wedding photography with a special blend of photojournalism, fine art and a touch of fashion. We specialize in creating works of enduring beauty that capture all of the passion and lavishness that your special day has to offer. We present exceptional photography, outstanding videos, and beautiful keepsake albums of images that will continue to convey the story of your special day for years to come. Our talented team of photographers, album designers, video editors and Lux customer care representatives will work with you to create a photo and video documentation of your wedding you’ll want to show off to everyone you know!

Exceptional photography doesn’t have to be exceptionally expensive. We deliver high-quality work at a fair price. Our prices are straightforward and our clients save on bundled packages. All of our packages include high-resolution digital negatives of all of your photos and raw footage of your videos. We offer a wide-selection of pre-packaged solutions, ranging from basic to full-service coverage with countless options and always-impeccable service.

There are no second chances at capturing once-in-a-lifetime moments. Trust your photographer, go with Lux!
