Nick Broom’s Summary

Entrepreneur and left-brain creative thinker.
I am passionate about customer service, communications, branding and design. With 15 years practical experience in creating creative, customer-focused marketing projects and campaigns in a wide range of businesses, I understand that new media opportunities need to be integrated into an existing and comprehensive strategy to ensure appropriate tactics are delivered in a consistent and measurable format.

Through my consultancy work I help businesses comprehend the opportunities for their brands presented through social media and how best to use these routes of communication to listen, understand and engage with their audience, without the hype and buzzwords that are so common in this space.

I started Preview in 1994, and PVLUK in 2000;now one of the best known names in the vehicle markings sector and built 2 teams of talented individuals in disparate industries where customer focus and targeted marketing approaches have been key to developing our client base and building our brands.

Early in 2008 I involved myself in Go4Fresh - we're set to launch a revolution in the provision of fresh, healthy foods to the workplace - Its a start-up with extensive ambitions - see the website for more!!

I'm also hands on with CIM Sussex, as vice chair on the committee, which is a great and practical challenge whilst helping me continue to hone my marketing skills.

I fill my spare time with sporting activities and a love of motor racing; if its cold, then I may be found snowboarding, if its hot, then I'll be surfing, somewhere in between and I'll be running, cycling or swimming, competitively if possible!

Nick Broom’s Specialties:

core skills (both me and my companies) in: business and marketing strategy, customer service, social media and insight, branding / identity, purposeful creative design and internet development, marketing communications. Oh, and high visibility vehicle graphics and markings for the emergency services and highways sector!
