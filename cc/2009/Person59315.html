Lindy B. Batdorf’s Summary

Every shred of meaningful experience I have reflects some facet of human to human communication and the importance of respect and relationship.

So...the heart and soul of all I do has to do with:
The Art and Heart of Communication.

My goal is to:
--help businesses succeed by holding workshops and seminars that encourage respect and enhance communication
--write books and articles that help us laugh, think or feel to a deeper degree
--assist others in performance and presentation skills (I especially enjoy helping reluctant speakers build confidence)

Lindy B. Batdorf’s Specialties:

The Art of Heart Communication, Communications Coach, Motivational Keynote Speaker, Facilitator, Writer’s Workshop leader, Inspirational & Church Retreat Speaker, Author, Freelance Writer, Freelance Producer

Lindy B. Batdorf’s Experience

  • Speaker ~ Communication/Performance Coach

    Batdorf Communications

    (Public Relations and Communications industry)

    May 2002 — Present (7 years 7 months)

    The Art & Heart of Communication is based on the importance of common sense, respect and understanding.

    Whether at work, at home, in the marketplace, the neighborhood, at church, other organizations or even in line at the post office -- every person is significant to the function of the whole.

    In short, people matter. They matter, because whether we like it or not, we are thrown into interactions every day.

    Communication is the glue and/or electric fence that holds or hinders these relationships and interactions.

    As a speaker, writer and teacher, I offer tools to enhance, build or rebuild communication for individuals, families, groups and organizations based on that big three: respect, understanding and common sense.
