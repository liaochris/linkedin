Bill Tyson’s Summary

Seasoned direct marketer, business developer, strategic planner and leader with more than 24 years of combined domestic and international experience. Proven track record in impacting corporate bottom line through skillful orchestration and execution of business plans focused on sales, marketing and account management functions by targeting diverse markets through multiple channels. Strategic cultivator of profitable partnerships, joint ventures and alliances with a successful background serving in an instrumental role in new product and service innovations serving financial services companies.
