Kim Sherrell’s Summary

About

Kim Sherrell is a filmmaker, educator, and independent media producer. She is currently working on projects including a sitcom and a couple of tech startups. An authority on multimedia design, cultural theory, and contemporary art, Kim is the founder of #Artwalk on Twitter.

Kim Sherrell’s Specialties:

Areas of expertise include theory and praxis of contemporary visual arts, visual communication/graphic design, digital filmmaking, project management, content curation/collaboration and community building.

Fluent with tools such as XHTML/CSS, Apple's Final Cut Studio, and the Adobe Creative Suite.
