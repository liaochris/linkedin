Nimesh Shah’s Summary

I have worked with Indian FMCG companies in the field of sales and marketing straight after B-School. All these years in these companies have exposed me to several facets of corporate life - Charting business roadmaps, Team building, Communication development and Financial controls.

I realised that now was the right time to charter out on my own and dawn an entrepreneur's hat. This led to the start up of Windchimes communications - a social media specializing agency. The key objective being understanding the brand / corporate objective and suitably advicing and implementing social media strategy for the same
