Anne Carr’s Summary

Bolt Public Relations (http://www.boltpr.com) is a full-service public relations, copywriting, event planning, SMS marketing and social networking agency with offices in North San Diego County and Orange County, California. We work with organizations across a variety of industries, including technology, education, business, biotechnology, sports, health and fitness, non-profit, travel and hospitality, beauty, fashion and entertainment.

Bolt is on a mission to create a profitable buzz and positive for clients by leveraging our relationships with the media, social networks, community officials, consumers, non-profit organizations, other businesses and local professional associations. We create prolific exposure for brands, build credibility for companies, and drive traffic to businesses through revenue-generating public relations and social media management strategies. Simply put, if no one knows a business exists, does it?

Bolt PR delivers cost-effective, strategic and revenue-generating results. We tap into every available medium to communicate your message to your target audiences, whether they're watching the news, listening to the radio, checking a text message, reading the newspaper or a magazine, Googling, or attending an event.

Feel free to contact me anytime at anne@boltpr.com or via Twitter at http://twitter.com/boltbuzz108 to learn more about how Bolt PR can supercharge your business.

With a B.A. in English/Creative Writing (Nonfiction), I am a natural story-finder, -writer and -teller. I view life through a creative lens and problem-solving mindset to produce revenue-generating results for businesses. Through my experience working with media on- and offling, I have become passionate about the influence of social media, integrated with public relations, to create exposure and tell a story.

Recently, I have been asked to speak and teach seminars about using social media for businesses or for job seekers.
