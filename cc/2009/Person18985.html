Chuck Sacco’s Summary

Chuck leads PhindMe Mobile, whose technology automates the design and generation of mobile web applications. Combined with text message marketing, social networking and mobile search, PhindMe’s mobile web apps provide businesses with a powerful set of digital marketing processes needed to reach and engage with an increasingly mobile consumer.

Chuck's career includes a track-record of launching products and growing businesses in the fields of enterprise software, customer relationship management and mobile applications.

PhindMe Mobile is Chuck’s second startup, previously launching an enterprise technology consulting business. He grew the consulting and client development operations of that self-funded startup before the business was sold to what is now a public software company.

Just prior to PhindMe, Chuck led SoftBrands’ Evolution division in the Americas where he successfully launched new products including an innovative Business Process Management solution for mid-sized businesses. SoftBrands is an int'l ERP, CRM, and BPM company in the manufacturing, distribution, and hospitality industries.

Chuck’s has specific expertise in selling and implementing technology platforms with small and mid-sized companies, helping them improve their bottom line through the effective use of technology. He has also led the setup of national and international partner programs, helping local companies leverage local market expertise.

With an MBA in technology management from Drexel University and a Beta Gamma Sigma honoree, Chuck has authored articles and speeches on mobile marketing and business process management.

He recently presented at Mobile Monday Mid-Atlantic, the NJ Technology Council Venture Conference, Search Marketing Expo Local/Mobile, and the Philadelphia-based Innovator's Forum, discussing how businesses can use mobile technologies to enhance their marketing.

Chuck also chairs the Drexel LeBow College of Business Alumni Council.
