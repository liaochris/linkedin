    We give you good advice on marketing. We don’t put lipstick on pigs. And we don’t like that expression. Because it implies that your offering stinks and lipstick can make even a pig look good.

    Instead we give you advice to help you define what business you are in, where you want to take it and how to get there. Then we roll up our sleeves and help you execute.

    Yes, we put our muscle where our advice is.

    And that’s not “just marketing”. It is the stuff that matters because: Marketing is the soul of a well run business.

    Have you ever pondered on questions such as: What markets to go after. How to listen to your customers. What products and services to offer. How to position, price and promote them. How to distribute them.

    If you have, then you’re doing marketing. Marketing, properly defined. And it’s not about pretty brochures and clever taglines.
