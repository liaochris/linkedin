Business Overview

One of the ways Ideal Scents helps you to create financial freedom is by network marketing. Starting a home based business is a solution for many who are looking for time and money freedom. I am a distributor for a $1.5 billion “Premier Wellness Company”, called New Vision International. These wellness products have been proven time and again to make people feel better, look better, have more energy and they are definitely at the top of the charts for healthy aging.

Ideal Scents collaborates with James L. Geiger, MD, the oilMD, Promoting Wellness Through Nature with essential oils.

Ideal Scents is connected to a virtual media company called VM Direct which brings three of todays hottest industries, streaming video, social networking and referral marketing into one successful business opportunity. Its flagship product is Hello World a web based digital media solution where you can manage all of your digital content, including music, photos, video and more. You can send and receive video emails, webcast live, podcast with up to 3 people at a time.

Build yourself another multiple source of income, be a digital entrepreneur.
