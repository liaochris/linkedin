Gahlord Dewald’s Summary

I work with businesses to develop strategic creative assets and relevant content. I have over 10 years experience designing websites, producing animation and conducting branding/identity work for entrepreneurial organizations of all sizes. I specialize in understanding what site visitors like and helping clients make more of it.

I'm a frequent speaker on web development, analytics and marketing issues for business audiences. Most recently including Blogworld in Las Vegas, Inman Connect in San Francisco, Blogger Connect in NYC, the Women's Small Business Development Program in Burlington, Vermont and the Vermont 3.0 Tech Jam and Conference also in Burlington. I also write a weekly column for Inman News on technology and marketing in lead generation businesses.

Outside of work, I am active in Burlington's cultural life as arranger and musical director for a puppet opera company, college-level animation teacher, and bassist in a wide variety of situations ranging from community orchestras to improvisatory rock ensembles.

I am most motivated by projects which allow me to make use of data and information to assemble meaning and make things people like.

Gahlord Dewald’s Specialties:

Web analytics, content development, design, explaining complicated topics in ways that help decision-making and action.
