Mark Castellano’s Summary

Mark Castellano is Founder and President of MAC Green Energy, a solar energy integrator, who represents a world-class power producer that installs, operates, and finances photovoltaic systems. Mark's clients include large commercial, educational, and industrial enterprises. He manages relationships between client and principal from site analysis to system switch-on.

Mark has over twenty years of experience in sales, marketing, support, and development of products and services in the telecommunications, financial, semiconductors, and energy sectors. His past employers include distinguished companies as AT&T Bell Laboratories, Merrill Lynch, and Texas Instruments.

Mark's technical background and broad experience enables him to connect with customers at all levels, understand their needs, and come up with creative solutions to close business.

If you own or manage a commercial, industrial, educational, or government establishment and would like to lower your electrical costs without capital investment, contact Mark. A small investment of your time could yield significant financial savings.
