Sven Laepple’s Summary

Sven's passion is to create, to invent, improve and grow.

He has 15+ yrs. relevant professional experience in Internet, Telecommunication, Consulting, IT Management and Software Development. He graduated in International Business Administration at the University of Mannheim, Germany with majors in International Management and Marketing, Organisation and American Studies.

Sven worked as manager for global or multinational corporations like the Vodafone Group, Deutsche Telekom and T-Systems.

He also served as managing director and executive board member in the creative and consulting industry. He co-founded and listed the interactive integrator BLUE C and worked for the interactive agency I-D Media.

As consultant, he did among else innovative projects for Deutsche Telekom, T-Mobile International, Bank Austria, Martin Dawes Group, EdP, Siemens Nixdorf, Air France.

Sven is a global player, having worked and lived in various countries. He speaks fluent english, french, german and spanish and visits regularly the US.

While being able to see the big picture, as few do, he is also strong in implementation due to his broad professional experience. He utilises a proven set of consulting tools and techniques to reach pre-defined goals within time, and within budget. Excellent social skills ensure wide acceptance across organisational levels and cultural divides.

Sven is interested getting involved in innovative projects. If required, he is supported by a large international network of professionals from various backgrounds.
