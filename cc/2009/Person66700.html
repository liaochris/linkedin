Tom Mahoney’s Summary

Founder and Director of Merchant911, LLC, a Delaware Limited Liability Company dedicated to prevention of credit card fraud from the Merchant and cardholder perspective.

Partner in CNP Associates, LLC and co-developer of an e-learning course on chargeback prevention for on-line merchants.

Over 26 years in computer Network Management.

Advanced level Toastmaster available for credit card fraud prevention speaking engagements.

Familiar with all building trades, having worked in construction and property management.

Tom Mahoney’s Experience

  • Founder - Director

    Merchant911, LLC

    (Internet industry)

    January 2001 — Present (8 years 7 months)

    Merchant911.org is a group of over 3700 merchants united to protect themselves against on-line credit card fraud. Membership is restricted to on-line merchants and there is no charge to become a member. Members have access to searchable databases containing over 1/4 million fraud fighting records, two mailing lists for fraud alerts and fraud related discussions, and a forum for the discussion of any matters concerning on-line merchants.

    In addition to E-Commerce Merchants, Merchant 911’s members include fraud prevention executives from Visa, Apple Computer, ChaseBank, BankCard USA, PayPal and dozens of other transaction processors, acquirers, issuers, and law enforcement who all pool information geared to fraud prevention.

    Merchant 911 has been featured in the on the CBS Evening News, National Public Radio and on Japanese network television. We have also collaborated on articles appearing in the Wall Street Journal, ComputerWorld, CnetNews, and numerous other trade journals.
