J.D. Hillen’s Summary

As a designer, I have a responsibility not only to create visually stimulating web applications and graphics, but push the medium beyond its current limits. My objective is to create seamless integration of visual elements and programmed logic. The practice of web standards for image optimization, back end coding, and Actionscript is something that is an absolute necessity in my designs.

I pride myself on my knowledge of design standards for both Flash and other HTML based projects. Having the experience to working with multiple clients to achieve goals has enhanced my communication skills. Handling multiple projects that have rigid deadlines and many intricate details is also not a problem. My key to juggling many tasks is organization. Staying organized plays a large role in my creative process. I also enjoy problem-solving and the opportunity to learn and grow as a designer with each project. The reason I became a designer is to produce fluid works that inspire the user.
