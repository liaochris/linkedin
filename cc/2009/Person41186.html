Jason Witenstein-Weaver’s Summary

I have a voracious appetite for creating the best of class experiences. It's my philosophy that life is an expression of sequential experiences so each one we have, share and remember becomes a part of who we are good, bad, or indifferent. I've become both obsessed with enhancing every experience that I can. We've only begun to scratch the surface of interactive innovation and my continuing challenge is to better understand, communicate, design and create to that end.
