Kris Coppieters’s Summary

Translating between propellerheads and artists, or how to avoid software project failure, developer burn-out and user discontent.

Many software projects fail because of non-technical aspects - 'want' vs. 'need', non-communication between stakeholders, feature creep, tool selection, lack of fun,...

Kris has a proven track record in world-wide mentoring, coaching, training and co-development services from a left brain/right brain perspective - teach how to gain and balance technical skills as well as people skills.

Talk to Kris if you do software development around the DTP/printing/prepress industry (e.g. QuarkXPress, Adobe products,...) - his custom InDesign SDK training/QuarkXPress XTension development training comes highly recommended.

If you want to make off-the-shelf software work better and smoother, talk to Kris - he can help you extend it, integrate it, and train developers and users about its efficient use.

Contact Kris (kris@rorohiko.com) for coaching and mentoring your development team.
