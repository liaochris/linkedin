Chris Liversidge’s Summary

With a background in web design built working in London & Madrid before moving to Edinburgh, I've been involved with computing and the internet from the get go. Three years at bigmouthmedia subsequently developed a passion for online marketing and the power of the internet to solve traditional business problems.

While at bigmouthmedia, we developed truly leading-edge web marketing solutions for many of the biggest brands in the world. Search Engine Optimisation (SEO) is a particular speciality and I remain heavily involved in evangelising for the evolution of this new industry into a respected, accountable, and genuinely revolutionary process which delivers outstanding return on investment.

Web development and web analytic tools are at the heart of QueryClick, founded in early 2008, where our sole objective is to deliver the most effective online business solution for our clients. Years of experience have shown that no two companies have the same objectives and needs online, and to truly deliver the best solution a multi-disciplinary ability is essential. As a result, at QueryClick we work with experts in design, web development, brand creation, widget development, online marketing, usability, mobile platforms and analytics to deliver websites capable of delivering - and measuring! - the best ROI & brand exposure possible for their owners.

Developing boutique solutions for our client base allows our team to stay ahead of the game online, while I find being part of such a team to be a great privilege.
