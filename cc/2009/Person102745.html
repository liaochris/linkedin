Vidar Brekke’s Summary

I am an experienced leader and entrepreneurial marketing strategist & product development executive with a focus on what’s next and how to get there first.

I co-founded “Social Intent” with a vision for the mobile and social web and the unique opportunities it presented for brands to extend themselves into the lives of customers.

I’m equally skilled in laying out long-term strategic plans at the C-level as I am executing them. In my career I have managed teams across practically every marketing discipline from traditional branding to interactive marketing, web development, SEO/SEM, bleeding edge social media technologies and mobile app development.

I have a knack for ideation and concept development and I pride myself on being able to simultaneously think like a business-executive, technologist, interaction designer, marketer and customer.

I am also the host of New York OpenCoffee: http://www.meetup.com/NYopenCoffee/
