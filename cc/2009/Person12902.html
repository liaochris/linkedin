Richard van den Boogaard’s Summary

Independent consultant in branded channels.

I advise brands (profit and non-profit) and media organisations in strategy and development of Branded Channels on a broad range of external platforms: online, mobile and through interactive television.

Thinking outside-the-site is key in this approach.

Gone are the days that your site is the most important thing since sliced bread. Your users have found their favorite spots and will only convert to your site if you really have something to offer. Having a content syndication strategy on multiple platforms will jointly add attention to your propositions.

For more information, see http://www.brandedchannels.com
