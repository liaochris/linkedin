Thomas Hatfield’s Summary

In today’s linked world, collaborative networking is becoming more than a buzz word. Connections across geopolitical frontiers, disciplines, information depots are critical to every business entity. My focus is in counseling business leaders and entrepreneurs in how to coalesce the many contacts and resources they have into an efficient business asset. ~20 years of experience in finding and connecting information to business activities. Licensed attorney in CT, CA, USPTO

Goal: Guide and structure business strategy to take optimal advantage of a collaborative networked world facing high levels of volatility, uncertainty, complexity, and ambiguity.
