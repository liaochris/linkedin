Alan Morrison’s Summary

Strategic advice, conceptual thinking and problem solving on the future of enterprise IT. Big picture grasp of enterprise software, technology manufacturing and services industries. Web technology and semantics a recent focus. Previous background in wireless semiconductors and systems, including five years as a wireless semiconductor market analyst.

Project leadership and management ability with internationally-based, cross-functional enterprise groups.

Strong written and visual communications abilities, and public speaking experience for both lay and technical audiences.

Alan Morrison’s Specialties:

IT strategy and trends analysis, particularly in enterprise software, wireless/RF, semiconductors, fixed networking, and optoelectronics. Advice on IT trend implications for C-suite executives and their direct reports.
