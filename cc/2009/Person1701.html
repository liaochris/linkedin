Adrian Lee’s Summary

Highly experienced industry and software consultant specialising in Transportation Management applications covering network design and modelling, tactical transportation procurement, operational transportation planning and execution, visibility and freight settlement processes.

Adrian Lee’s Specialties:

15 years of consulting experience in a number of industry verticals (3PL, CPG / Retail, Manufacturing, Hi-Tech) with leading edge clients (Tesco, MISC, Toll Logistics, Elkem, DHL) using a variety of Transportation Management / SCM systems including Paragon, Manugistics, G-Log (GC3 / OTM) and i2.
