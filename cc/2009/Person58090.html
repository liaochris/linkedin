Kelly Chang Rickert’s Summary

I am the founder of the Law Offices of Kelly Chang, A Professional Law Corporation. As an attorney with a real estate broker's license, my practice focuses on Family Law and Real Estate Law. Our practice is devoted to helping those who need us where it matters most - at home. Whatever situation you are in, it is unique and important, and we treat every case that way. We do not take your trust for granted. All calls, without exception, are returned within 24 hours.

I specialize in Family Law including Divorce, Annulment, Spousal Support, Child Support; Modification, Child Custody and Visitation, Prenuptial and Postnuptial Agreements, Adoptions, Property Division; Restraining Orders; Real Estate Law; Family Law Mediation.
