Marion E. Gold’s Summary

Marion provides a full range of editorial services including Writing, Ghost Writing and Editing, development of quality Press Kits, Press Releases, Editorials, Books and more. An experienced writer and marketing communications expert, her articles and editorials appear in marketing trade journals, and national newspapers and magazines - including Advertising Age, Chicago Sun-Times, Chicago Tribune, Arizona Republic and the Business Journal of Phoenix. She is author of the "Personal Publicity Planner: A Guide to Marketing YOU," and "TOP COPS: Profiles of Women in Command." "TOP COPS" was endorsed by former Governors George Ryan (Illinois), George Pataki (New York), and Janet Napolitano, U.S. Secretary of Homeland Secretary and former Governor of Arizona. Marion was featured in Working Woman magazine and included by Today's Chicago Woman in its list of 100 Women Making a Difference. She is a past president of the Illinois Woman’s Press Association and current vice president of Arizona Press Women.

"My writing career began in health care, as a journalist for two pharmaceutical/pharmacy publications, a group of multi-specialty physician newspapers, and then as an Associate Editor for the Journal of the American Medical Association's Medical News Department. Later at the AMA, I was director of Direct Mail Marketing/List Management. I moved deeper into marketing, as EVP and General Manager of a medical communications agency (part of the OMNICOM group of agencies), where I spent 13 years writing, editing and managing the publication of medical education materials - developing CME programs & sales training materials in all medical specialties, including nursing and pharmacy. In 1994, I turned to authoring books, articles and editorials about women in the workforce, ghostwriting, public speaking, and developing visibility plans and press kits for authors, entrepreneurs and business owners, and nonprofit organizations."

To learn more visit www.mariongold.com.

Marion E. Gold’s Specialties:

Comprehensive editorial services provided to individuals, corporations, corporate executives, publications and organizations, medical education institutions, and ad agencies - including all aspects of publishing, writing, ghost writing and editing for professional and consumer audiences. Marketing and public relations for entrepreneurs, book authors, small businesses and non-profits, "Personal Publicity" and other speciality press kit development, "Marketing YOU" workshops and presentations.
