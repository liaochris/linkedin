Ved Sen’s Summary

ThinkPLANK has worked with leading broadcasters such as MTV to implement technology driven business change, including global technology platform rollout, process changes, metrics definition and creation of technology strategies.

We continue to work with with Media, Technology, Telecom, Content, Marketing, Internet companies and industry bodies, helping to make business sense of Digital Media Convergence - addressing both threats and opportunities to create, refine and implement strategies for success.

As technology going into the Media / Convergence segment changes dramatically with more digitization, network and IP based content going to time and place shifted/ mobile consumers, the business models, processes and strategy for the industry will need to adapt to ensure success, in an increatingly digital and converged world.

With 13 years of experience in the software applications industry, working largely with publishing/ broadcast/ internet businesses, I understand Technology and Creative processes and my core skills include Strategy and Process Analysis & Definition, spanning business and technology.

Ved Sen’s Specialties:

Channel Management System, Digital Media Workflows, Business Change Initiatives, Digital Asset Management, process redesign and automation, interactive television, gaming, new media applications, broadcast, publishing, web based applications and enterprise solutions for Media/ Entertainment businesses.
