Matt Hewitson MAHRI [LION] 2500+ Connections’s Summary

Matt joined Harrier having held senior consultant and business manager positions with an international recruitment firm in Perth and London. He comes from a generalist HR background and is an experienced HR recruiter.

Matt has established Harrier’s HR Talent Pool which specialises in the placement of HR professionals across Australia in consulting, contracting and permanent opportunities. With an emphasis on candidate care and relationship management, combined with his specialist market knowledge, Matt has an outstanding track record of recruiting HR professionals in Australia and overseas.

He spent two years in the UK and led numerous recruitment campaigns for HR professionals spanning the UK and Europe. Across Europe, he concentrated on senior level HR appointments, assisting in the establishment of HR shared service centres for multinational outsourcing companies.

As a member of AHRI, Matt's passion to increase the professionalism of the HR community has led him to convene an AHRI specialist forum titled "HR Business Partnering".

Matt also holds a Bachelor of Psychology degree.
