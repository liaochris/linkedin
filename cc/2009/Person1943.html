Liz Jenkins’s Summary

After owning and managing several businesses, and teaching special education for many years, I realized that my strengths are in organizing and project management. As a Professional Organizer, I assist my clients with reducing chaos in their lives, creating a sense of order and helping them find their stuff when they need it.
As a Home Stager, I make homes sell faster and for more money by taking a tired home and turning it into a showcase. My team will do everything from paint selection, furniture re-arranging and accessorizing . . . all the way to analyzing repairs and maintenance needs. I then implement the plan to make sure that not only does the home show wonderfully, it stays sold after the home inspection.
As a Project Manager, I work with home owners to get projects done such as kitchen renovations, painting, flooring, lighting, organizing and more. My planning, on site management, scheduling and follow up with contractors as well as the ability to relieve the home owner of worry and hassle can save both time and money.
You can always find me at www.afreshspace.com!
