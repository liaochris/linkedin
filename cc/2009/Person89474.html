Scott Garsed-Donnelly’s Summary

New media technologist with 22 years strategic design, development, and management experience, including management of teams undertaking large-scale interactive, web, software user interface, and print projects. Outgoing, iterative, self-empowering management style. Technology-focused university-level teaching experience. Advanced degree in arts-based information management, focusing on creative technology.

My charge? Harnessing technology to make work and life easier, and applying interactive technologies and user-centered design to create meaningful interactions between businesses and their customers.

Scott Garsed-Donnelly’s Specialties:

Traditional, multimedia, and interaction design; user experience research, planning, and design; design team building and management.
