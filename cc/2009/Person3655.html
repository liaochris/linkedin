Alexys Fairfield’s Summary

Life is the epic novel in which Alexys Fairfield eloquently writes. She describes herself as, "A passionate word ninja, and life novelist reconditioning the human condition and rewriting life's drama with love."

Sensing a need for people to feel a connection with themselves and with each other, her first blog, Unraveling The Spiritual Mystique became an instant hit, garnering visitors from over 150 countries within the first four months.

Writing what she sees, hears and feels through her acute observation, she touches on everything that makes us tick.

"I highly recommend this site to anyone who has a yearning to learn about themselves. Alexys writes with a pen made of an artist. She wields her keyboard like a paint brush and uses our screens as her canvas. My screen is always open to her paintings and I hope others see what I see. Brilliant, soul touching words that give you cause to Think!" - Darrel - Things I Know About

Whether it's finding yourself through posts such as, The Atom Of Soul; Amazing Humans; Make Your Brian An Assault Weapon, Human Factory Of Souls; or The Shell, you will find yourself lovingly sprawled on the pages crafted by this soulful writer.

Not only is Alexys Fairfield the author of three blogs, but she has also interviewed various celebrity superstars in the field of music, art and film. Her work has been published in various local and national publications. She has also written film scripts.

"All writers are the eyes and ears of the world - the pulse of life that is tenderly measured with our fingers. As we painstakingly transform blank pages into stories, we constantly shift perceptions, ideas, thoughts and feelings. It's about embracing the darkness and creating light," Alexys explains.

Alexys Fairfield has exclusively created the web content of Thespis.net and her work can also be found on IMDb, Squidoo, Ezine, Blogger, and more.
