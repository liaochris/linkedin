David Kalman’s Experience

  • Product Manager / ePublishing

    Minnick Web Services

    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    February 2009 — Present (6 months)

    Responsible for new biz dev for MWS's eBook product, which offers an innovative way to deliver content to readers while producing 100s of leads for sponsors.

    An MWS eBook is a package of editorial content--text and other media--presented in a Flash-based reader. Inside, the eBook incl. Audience Mgmt w/registration & data collection; StatReport for tracking & reporting; eProofer for collaboration; and hosting on eBookHost.com.

    Typically, a Publisher sells an eBook sponsorship on a topic of interest to readers. The Publisher pulls articles from the archive or commissions new ones, and delivers files to MWS. In about 2 weeks, MWS produces an eBook, which the Publisher then markets via Web, email, and print. Other than the marketing, the eBook is turn-key, w/low overhead and zero involvement from your IT or Web dept.

    MWS has produced 500+ eBooks for its clientele of print/online publishers. Over the past yr the eBooks have avg'd 1,038 registrants w/55% opt-in. A tremendous value.
