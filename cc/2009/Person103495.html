Steve Peters’s Summary

Founder and Chief Experience Architect at No Mimes Media, LLC. Formerly Experience Design Director for 42 Entertainment, recent projects include The Threshold (Cisco), Six Minutes to Midnight (Watchmen feature film), Stop the International (The International feature film), Project Abraham (Sony/Insomniac's Resistance 2), Why So Serious (The Dark Knight feature film), Year Zero (Nine Inch Nails), Vanishing Point (Microsoft/AMD Vista Release), Dead Man's Tale (Pirates of the Caribbean Dead Man's Chest feature film), and Last Call Poker (Activision's GUN).

Prior to 42 Entertainment, work included development of an Alternate Reality Game reality television pilot, and Project MU (2003), which was an independent Alternate Reality Game set in the universe of The Matrix. In 2002, founded and was Lead Editor for the Alternate Reality Gaming Network (www.argn.com).

Previous to working as an ARG designer, was President and CEO of Vital Interactive Media (founded 1987). Projects there included music and sound design for television, theater, independent artists, and computer games. Clients included Microsoft, Universal Television, SpikeTV and Nash Entertainment.
