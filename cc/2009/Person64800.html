Matthew Digesti’s Summary

The Digesti Law Firm advises its clients on a wide variety of business law strategies in both the transactional and litigation context. We combine our experience in business, economics and the law to advise clients on issues including formation, operation, employment, intellectual property, business sales and purchases, partnerships, joint ventures and strategic alliances. The Digesti Law Firm is becoming a pioneer in e-commerce legal representation as it continues to draft, negotiate and close such e-commerce partnership and joint venture agreements encompassing merchandising manufacture fulfillment, merchandising distribution fulfillment, online sponsorship, VIP ticketing, and direct social networking between artists, athletes and fans. He has worked closely with attorneys and management for entertainers and athletes such as Kobe Bryant, the Game, Back Street Boys, Boys to Men, Paul Oakenfold, Thalia, Third Eye Blind, Eric Byrnes, Chris Daughtry, and T-Pain, to name a few.

In today's digital age, intellectual property protection has become paramount. The Digesti Law Firm handles all aspects of intellectual property protection, transaction and litigation. Our clients include established corporations and visionary entrepreneurs in the social networking, e-commerce, web site design, videography, music, athletic, advertising and real estate industries.

The Digesti Law Firm leverages its experience in these areas to shoulder the legal aspects of each client's business while freeing valuable time for the client to focus on what it does best--business.

Matthew Digesti’s Specialties:

Corporate formation advisement, corporate formation and ongoing operations in the Internet, music, athletic and real estate industries; corporate governing document drafting; e-commerce contracts; social information management; merchandise agreements; intellectual property protection, intellectual property licensing; video and music production and distribution; endorsements; record deal contracts and deal memorandums.
