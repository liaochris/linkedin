David Donhoff’s Summary

David Donhoff is the founding principal at No Bull Financial, LLC, an international financial advisory and leverage planning firm headquartered in Kirkland, Washington, and Leverage Planners International™, a global network of affiliate firms. In 2007 Donhoff brought the first Tradable Forex Mortgage (TFM) fee-based equity-reclamation model to the U.S., integrating managed real estate debt, cross-currency hedging, and global market management to re-convert real estate debt into equity tax-free. Today David leads the forefront of alternative structured personal financial strategies. For over 25 years Donhoff has been guiding clients to achieve personal freedom and financial independence. As a featured contributor in national publications such as The Motley Fool, That Home Site, Guns and Whiskey, Global Economic Trend Analysis, and other prominent economic journals and financial BLOGs, Donhoff is a recognized advisor, advocate, and author. David swept away his CPA Joanne 10 years ago, and today she oversees the counting of the beans while David helps his clients MAKE and KEEP their beans from their Lake Washington vista home just a rain-soaked coffee-cup-walk from the NBF executive offices in the financial district of Kirkland, Washington.

David Donhoff’s Specialties:

Residential and Commercial Real Estate finance, business finance, credit analysis and improvement, asset protection, estate planning, retirement income development and planning, safe indexing, leveraged investment development, construction development, land development, condominium conversion, business benefits planning, succession planning.
