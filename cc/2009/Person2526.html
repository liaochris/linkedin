Alan Alexander’s Summary

Ten year goal was to get an MBA from The Citadel, The Military College of South Carolina.

Alan Alexander was a Real Estate Executive and a licensed Realtor based out of the Historic, Greater Charleston, SC, USA. He is joining Weichert Realty and looks forward to serving clients worldwide. He has access to a worldwide network of Real Estate Agents serving the Relocation and Referral market. His services add nothing to the cost of the transaction and can help by being accountable for finding responsible, competent, and conscientious professionals. Investors are welcome. Commercial properties also fall into his network.

He was looking for clients who are buying or selling property nationwide, whether they are moving across town, or around the globe. Company relocations are also sought, whether opening a new location or moving to a new Headquarters.

The Charleston, South Carolina area is a wonderful place to live and work. It is the home of the International Arts Festival, Spoleto. It has many world-class golf courses, including those on Kiawah Island, Seabrook Island, Edisto Island, Isle of Palms, and Wild Dunes. It is home to the Family Circle Cup WTA tournament every year and is only 1.5 hour drive to Hilton Head Island. Myrtle Beach is also 1.5 hours away.

Charleston is also good for business; with an International Airport, a deep channel port (serving PANAMAX vessels), and the genesis of Interstate 26 along which is 1 hour to Interstate 95. With this nexus of transportation, it has attracted many new industries, and facilitated South Carolina’s landing the BMW North American manufacturing plant. For the second consecutive year, the state of South Carolina has been named America’s Most Business-Friendly State, based on an independent study by Pollina.

South Carolina's emergence as a global business hub combined with its rich "Old-South" heritage allows you to benefit from an eclectic and diverse mix of cultures.
