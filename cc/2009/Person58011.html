Laurie Meisel’s Summary

A seasoned Web Producer with over 10 years of experience in production, editing, newsletter development, ad delivery and search engine optimization.

Laurie has been online since she got her first modem in '94 and hasn't looked back since. From the days of BBS, Laurie has had a constant web presence. During the 90s she was an active newsgroup participant, maintaining a number of personal & newsgroup related web sites, doing biz with people all over the world.

In '98, she joined what was then the McGraw-Hill Construction Information Group and took over production of ArchitecturalRecord.com, ENR.com, Design-Build.com, as well as Construction.com and the sites for the McGraw-Hill Construction Regional publications. Eventually, the sites grew & MyHouse was added as well as GreenSource.com.

Today, Laurie primarily works on ArchitecturalRecord.com which won a Jesse H. Neal Award for Best Web Site as well as being named to BtoB Media Business Magazine's list of 10 Great Media Web Sites.

In addition, Laurie has also pursued her love of photography primarily concentrating on professional sports and events. Her photos have been featured in Engineering News-Record, Design-Build Magazine, ArchitecturalRecord.com, Construction.com, as well as a number of basketball related sites.

Laurie believes in giving back to the community & has volunteered her photography, web skills, voice, & time whenever she can. If you are a non-profit organization based in Manhattan & are looking for an event photographer, Laurie will consider your request.

lauriemeisel@gmail.com

Also known as the "Queen of Online Bargain Shopping," colleagues, as well as friends and family know to turn to her first before making purchases. 99% of the time she can find it for less from a reputable dealer. Laurie was an early adopter of online shopping and has made purchases from 100s of different vendors. Look for her e-book in the Spring 2009.
