Shireen Smith’s Summary

Intellectual Property Solicitor with substantial background in commercial and technology law, having established own central London law firm Azrights. The firm employs a core team of experts and retains a number of consultants and professionals from other disciplines (such as Chartered Patent Attorneys) who are brought into projects as necessary. In this way high quality specialist skills are offered to clients at affordable prices.
The firm is particuarly focused on brand management work, which includes trade mark and domain registrations and litigation. As well as dealing with passing off, counterfeit goods, and trade mark infringement disputes, Azrights handles franchising matters. The firm also advises on copyright, designs, and patents, and is currently busy dealing with the legal issues arising from the internet, such as social networking, privacy, defamation and marketing laws.

Shireen Smith’s Specialties:

Internet and Technology law, with an emphasis on brand management, particularly issues arising from web 2.0 and the changing nature of networking and social communications. Recent experience includes a counterfeit goods dispute, and a franchising dispute
