Mo McKinstry’s Summary

M & M Group designs and provides customized presentations for your organization such as enhancing leadership skills, resume writing, business networking and public speaking.

Mo McKinstry is a Professional speaker, workshop leader, and consultant for leadership. She inspires those who can achieve to make the most of their situation, as well as make motivated those who need inspiration. The ultimate goal is to create a high performing organizations in which all can reach their full potential.

She has spoken at various career fairs, business conventions and youth summits in California, Georgia, Maryland, Washington, DC and many more. Mo strongly believes in training the youth today to be better leaders tomorrow is her passion. She is a motivator of sorts and will cause you to see yourself and others differently, so that you will be able to value the difference.
