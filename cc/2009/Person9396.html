Barbara Hastings-Asatourian’s Summary

Barbara qualified in nursing midwifery and health visiting before entering higher education as a Senior Lecturer in Public Health.

She now develops and authors unique sex education materials and provides sexual health information and resources such as Contraception the Board Game, Contraception the Computer Game and Safer Sex:An Interactive Learning Resource on Video and DVD.

Barbara also works extensively in the voluntary sector and is a volunteer for Glossop Women's Aid, a mentor for Business in the Prince's Trust, and one of the directors of Manchester Diamonds Cheerleading Squad (MDCS) CIC.. She is also a SPARK Ambassador for Women's Enterprise.

Her aims?
To have an impact on sexually transmitted infections and unwanted pregnancies amongst teenagers worldwide.
To promote assertiveness and negotiation skills for healthy relationships, including identifying early signs of abuse, especially in young people.
To build confidence and team spirit amongst young people through the MDCS.
To promote business opportunities for women through the Ambassadors Programme and Prince's Trust Business Programme.

Conference and Seminar Presentations to promote aims (see above)
