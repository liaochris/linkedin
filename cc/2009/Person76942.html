Michael McLaughlin’s Summary

McLaughlin IP Pte. Ltd. offers filing services to help clients obtain intellectual property rights in both Singapore and in countries throughout the world.

Additionally, we provide advisory & advocacy, consultancy and training services.

McLaughlin IP Pte. Ltd. was formed in 2008 in Singapore. Although we offer services in all fields of intellectual property, we have a particular expertise in the field of patent law and practice. Although we are, for now at least, a small firm, we have top-firm expertise and experience, and the level of service we provide to clients is perhaps second-to-none in Singapore.
