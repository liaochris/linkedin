Ashley Routson’s Summary

Ashley is a multi-faceted food & beverage professional and enthusiast. She has experience working in multiple realms of the food, beverage & hospitality industry as well as had experience working in marketing, advertising and public relations.

Ashley is currently pursuing certification as a Certified Specialist of Wine and aspires to one day join the Court of Master Sommeliers. She is also a beer connoisseur and currently authors "The Beer Wench" blog.

Ashley also prides herself in being an early adopter and avid user of Social Media. It is her goal and prerogative to combine her passion for the restaurant, food & beverage industry with her love and obsession for social media and the Internet.
