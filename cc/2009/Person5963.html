Andrew Arnold’s Summary

I'm a British national who has lived and worked in Denmark since 1996. Since starting as a local newspaper journalist in 1987, I've worked at a string of international trade magazines in the UK before becoming a correspondent for AFX News in Denmark. I've also worked for The Financial Times and The Economist Intelligence Unit. I worked as a Public Relations Consultant at Carlsberg, Communications Manager for Hempel, and Account Director at Hill & Knowlton's Copenhagen office. I ran my own company for a while, but now I'm working at Eye for Image

Andrew Arnold’s Specialties:

International Public Relations, Trade PR, internal branding, internal communications, copywriting, language policies, tone of voice, journalism
