Mike Zadorozniak’s Summary

Hi my name is Michael Zadorozniak and I've made a career out of working with businesses between $5m to $100m to help them become "GO TO" brands.

In just over ten years REDelicious has established itself as one of the country’s leading management, marketing & business development consultancies.

We work with Retailers, Manufactures, Importers and Professional Services companies to make more sales and more profits by showing them what they're doing wrong and then by teaching them what to do right. Think of it this way - we SEED companies with ripe IDEAS, NURTURE then just right, to finally HARVEST more sales and profits.

* Honours Dip ED in Media and Business Communications Arts
* Working in both multi-national advertising firms and independent creative ‘hot shops’.

Our ethos that ‘nothing happens in business until something gets sold’ is a truism that drives us. We have been featured in trade and business journals and case studied by Monash University Business School.

Manufacturing, Service, Fashion, Telecommunications, Franchise, Retail, Supermarket, Automotive, Food & FMCG

• Expertise in sales and managment process, systems, marketing, training, business development
