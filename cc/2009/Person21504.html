Music instructor at Gwinnett School of Music (Grayson, GA Location): beginning guitar, beginning & advance piano, vocal training, and songwriting
Performing musician for various events: Weddings, Restaurants, Festivals, Parties, Songwriters in the Rounds
Recording Artist: 3 albums available on iTunes & 1 at CD Baby
Currently working with Farmington Public School in TeenSL training instructors how to broadcast, setup audio equipment, & teach their students how to broadcast their original compositions.
Because of the use of Internet Broadcasting and the online program, Second Life(tm), I have been afforded the opportunities to take my virtual performances live to the majority of the places listed above across the US.

The fusing of virtual reality with the real world and bringing people together is what I am all about. I hope to be able to provide these kinds of services to other musicians and bring quality entertainment to companies that are willing to step outside the box and open up their minds to something new and innovative.
