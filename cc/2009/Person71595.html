Nathan Baron’s Summary

I started sailing in offshore yachts at the age of eleven and have sailed almost 10,000 nautical miles on the Great Lakes. I am a past Shark Canadian Champion and have placed in the top ten in two World championships (3rd in 2005, 9th in 2006). Other notable results: 1st offshore division at CORK Sail Week, 2nd at the Ontario Match Racing Championships, and 4th in the Freeman Cup.

My goal is to compete in and win the 2009 Mini Transat, a single handed yacht race across the atlantic ocean that is sailed in an extreme ocean racing dinghy called the Open 6.5 or Mini.

The team is seeking a corporate partnership that will benefit both sides through internal and external communications opportunities, in exchange for the funds needed for the team to reach its goal of getting to the start line prepared to win and take on this huge challenge.
