Tim Patterson’s Summary

25+ years behind the microphone in various radio stations and capacities: on-air, production, morning show host, commercial voice over artist

7+ years VP of Sales and Marketing at Interpretive Exhibits in Salem, Oregon; handling tradeshow marketing and booth creation for clients throughout the western US.

Writer for untold years, including a bi-weekly column on tradeshow marketing at http://nwmeetingsmag.com; recently published as a columnist in the Statesman Journal's Inside Business section (http://statesmanjournal.com/insidebiz), and as a guest columnist for Portland Business Journal.

Recently released two e-books: "Twittering Your Way to Tradeshow Success" and "101 Rules of Tradeshow Marketing" available at http://tradeshowguyblog.com.

Podcaster under my on-air professional name of Tim 'Gonzo' Gordon at http://CommunicationSteroids.com with Roger Pike. Podcast and e-mail newsletter published weekly; currently have a number of private and public speaking and training appearances on the calendar.
