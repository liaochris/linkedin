Josh Long’s Summary

I am an experienced and educated computer technologist with 10 years of professional experience, including management positions.

I currently work as a computer Network Specialist for a large organization, supporting several dozen sites in the largest geographic county in the contiguous United States.

During my career, I have often been hand-picked by Directors of Information Technology to work with them on a variety of projects.

I am interested in full-time work in the field of information security or virus and malware analysis.
