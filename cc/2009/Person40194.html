Howard Pearson’s Summary

I am an experienced load and performance tester with a background in infrastructure design and administration. I have been a load and performance tester for the last five years in various industries including: Law, Insurance, business intelligence, construction and real estate, government, telecoms, and transport.

Over the years I have been involved in all areas of the performance testing life-cycle, having been responsible for:

-Requirements definition and analysis
-Test planning and design
-Test harness design and implementation
-Test script design and creation
-Scenario design
-Test execution
-Results analysis and reporting

Primarily working with LoadRunner I have experience of the following protocols:

-Web
-Citrix
-RTE
-COM
-SAP

I am also experienced with Mercury Deep Diagnostics for .NET, OpenSTA, Quality Centre/Test Director. Additionally I have experience of working with various Windows server technologies (Windows NT, 2000, 2003, Microsoft SQL, Exchange etc) as well as other enterprise systems (Citrix, SAP, Notes etc).

In addition I hold the following qualifications:

-Mercury Certified Product Consultant in LoadRunner 7.6
-ISEB Foundation in Software Testing
-Various Microsoft certifications in NT4
-Certified Lotus Professional (Notes 4.6)
-City & Guilds – C Programming
