Bradly Sax’s Summary

SHG Consulting|Management is a boutique-sized services firm focused on understanding the local, regional, national and global trends, dynamics and daily evolutions of the lodging industry. We utilize our comprehensive knowledge of the industry in providing operational, asset positioning, business development and marketing consultation and training services throughout the eastern United States and Canada. Specialties include; increasing market penetration in the electronic distribution channels; asset management and advisory; hotel management including receivership appointments and management under receivership; the development and implementation of operational process, procedure and policy; sales team development; and executive level training all with a focus on growing revenues.

Bradly Sax’s Interests:

Lodging businesses are management/sales intensive entities requiring a specialized base of knowledge and expertise. SHG Consulting provides owners, financial institutions and institutional investor clients with the knowledge and expertise in all aspects of lodging operations and sales. Lodging related businesses are one of the most complex and challenging forms of property management and ownership. At SHG Consulting, we take an active role in maximizing owner investment. Whether through ownership representation; short-term management agreements; or for limited term consulting projects, we are equipped to provide expert guidance and assistance. SHG provides support for your hospitality operation using a consultive approach for the times when your organization may need some additional assistance, an outside perspective or staff training and development.
