John Kaldeway’s Summary

I like working with entrepreneurial types who get marketing, and understand it as an investment into themselves and their company. It's not about art. It's about success : Think like an investor.

An artist at heart, a promoter in spirit, I've always been drawn to the 'big idea' that effectively communicates. Exploring ways to help create the spark that swings push to pull is fascinating : Think like a marketer.

Marketing is about getting into the end-customer's head, and understanding what they will be drawn to, what they will relate to, and what they need : Think like a customer : Never stop learning.
