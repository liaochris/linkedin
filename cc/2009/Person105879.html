Yihong Ding’s Summary

Active thinker, researcher, and blogger on World Wide Web, particular on Web 2.0 and Semantic Web. Authored a unique model of Web evolution. Well known blogger on Web technology at the frontier of Web industry and Web academy. Primary author of Thinking Space, contributing author for several tier-1 Web technology sites such as Internet Evolution, Semantic Focus, and ZDNet. Professional developer on such as Java, C/C++, RDF, and OWL. His primary research includes semantic annotation, data extraction, ontology generation and manipulation, and World Wide Web evolution.
