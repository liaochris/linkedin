Peter Bodifée’s Summary

Peter Bodifee is the president of Bodifee Consulting Group Inc., a firm specializing in helping you leverage information technology for dramatic success and maximum profitability.

Peter has been a consultant since 2005 and a former software developer, data center manager, network specialist, system engineer, and IT architect. He has worked for and with profit and non-profit organizations such as ESA, Bosch, General Electric Plastic, Ericsson, Fluke, EMC, Ernst & Young, Essent, KPMG, and KLM Airlines, as well as lesser-known medium-sized organizations such as Amarantis Education Group.

In the past 23 years, his openness to new challenges lead him to work on projects in eight countries with people from 15 countries. He has lived in five different countries on three continents, which has given him a taste of the multicultural and truly global workplace. As a result, he now has a command of three languages: English, German, and Dutch. He has worked on both the demand and the supply side of the table. As no other, he can relate to business-to-business issues in the IT domain.


Keywords experience (in alphabetical order): architect, coach, client facing, consultative selling, data centers, design, development, disaster recovery, helicopter view, high availability, human machine interfacing, implementation, integration, IT infrastructure technology, networks, networked storage, operating systems, performance analysis, project management, team player, testing, security, storage management, strategy, virtualization.

Contact: peter@bodifeeconsulting.com

Peter Bodifée’s Specialties:

Disaster resilient data center solutions, (Re)building IT infrastructure after mergers and acquisitions, infrastructure standardisation and policies.
