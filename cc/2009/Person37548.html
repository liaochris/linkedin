Greg Giesen’s Summary

Greg Giesen is president of Greg Giesen & Associates, a management development company specializing in conflict resolution, leadership development, teambuilding and motivational speaking.

Greg is also a graduate school professor at the University of Denver and teaches Group & Team Dynamics and Presentation Skills. He is the author of four books on Creating Authenticity, including his latest, Ask Dr. Mac: Take the Journey to Authentic Leadership. Ask Dr. Mac recently won the bronze medal for best business fable in 2008.

In addition, Greg facilitates the award-winning workshop, Leading From Within (www.leadingfromwithin.net), an intensive, three-day leadership development workshop designed to help participants achieve optimal performance as a leader in both their personal and professional lives.

Greg also co-hosts the Ask Dr. Mac talk radio show on www.latalkradio.com, each Thursday at 5pm PST.

For more information about Greg Giesen and his company, go to www.GregGiesenAssociates.com or www.AskDrMac.com. Follow Greg on Twitter (twitter.com/askdrmac).
