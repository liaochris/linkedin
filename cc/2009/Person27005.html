DJ Waldow’s Summary

My life path has allowed me to see the Michigan Wolverines win the National Championship during my senior year of college, live in Chicago while working as a consultant, spend almost a year in Kansas City trying to get a startup off the ground (2000 was not the best year to be a dotcom), substitute teach while working for a non-profit in Clearwater Beach, FL, teach 8th grade American History for 2 years in Rochester, NY after earning my Masters in Education and advise clients on email marketing best practices at Bronto Software in Durham, NC. I've loved every minute of it. Every day continues to get better.

DJ Waldow’s Specialties:

I'm a blog contributor on the Email Experience Blog (http://blog.emailexperience.org/) and the Bronto Blog (http://blog.bronto.com/) and am an active member in the Email Marketer's Club (http://b2bemailmarketing.ning.com/).
