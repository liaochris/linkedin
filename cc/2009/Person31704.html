Ernst-Jan Pfauth’s Summary

Ernst-Jan Pfauth (23) is a blogger and journalist, who previously worked in New York to cover news at the United Nations. He's currently blogging at Dutch quality newspaper nrc.next and co-organized an international bloggers conference in Amsterdam on October 24, 2008. Before writing for nrc.next, he was the editor in chief of The Next Web, an international Web 2.0 blog.

Ok, enough for third person now. I'm regularly speaking about blogging and journalism. You can book me via The Next Speaker: http://thenextspeaker.com/sprekers/ernst-jan-pfauth/
