Rodolfo Aular-Hernández’s Summary

Rodolfo has over 12 years of experience in information systems development projects, application support and database designs to meet business needs in Oil and Gas Distribution Industries and Telecommunications. He is currently completing a Master’s degree in Project Management and working as PM consultant at PMC Project Management Centre Inc. As the Integration Products Manager at PMC, he is presently working as PM methodology implantation consultant for Bell Mobility and as Project Manager for the Ministry of Immigration and Cultural Communities.

At IBM Venezuela, for the CANTV SW Factory Project, he first coordinated the WEB and C/S development teams and then he performed the Operation Manager role.

Before that, he used to work as a systems engineer, for PDVSA (the Venezuelan state oil company) and as a development project leader for Intesa (an ancient SAIC subsidiary based in Caracas). His role was mainly oriented to provide IT solutions for gas distribution business on client/server platform, integrating GIS technology resources (geographic information systems), WEB technology projects, data modeling, process analysis, SW installation, configuration and maintenance.

In 1999, he was on a one year assignment by INTESA, to work for SAIC UK (Aberdeen) in Geoscience Team, handling system and server faults and requests, as well as correcting and enhancing Unix scripts for several Geo-applications.
