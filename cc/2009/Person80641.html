Jim Cox’s Summary

HR360 Full suite of web based offerings.

HR24 24hr Service

Priority HR was founded as true Co-Employer that provides Human Resource management and administration for small and medium sized businesses in Greater Grand Rapids.
Our strict focus was designed around serving only our Greater Grand Rapids market. Today it’s grown into a full HR outsourced provider that is still strictly serving Grand Rapids still a true Co-Employer yet with the industtries most advanced technichal abilties and complexity to implement Advancved Captives in the State of Michigan . Quite simply, we act as an "off-site" or "outsourced" HR Department or assist in as little or as much as the business desires.
Payroll was traditionally the first function sought after. As the compliance, tax, safety, benefits and workers compensation issues became more challenging, equal importance of these were assigned by small business.
During the last decade Health Care Cost for small businesses have skyrocketed, this along with a demand for increased efficiencies and a growing disparity of how small businesses use office technology has lead to relationships with a wide variety of industries insistent upon reducing the cost of these. We all expect 100% accuracy and simplicity. The need for Simplicity, Speed and Accuracy lead to the creation of a full suite of Web-Based, self service options. Giving employees 24/7 access to benefits, forms and answers to a host of issues will lighten your load. When you compare the cost of your benefits and insurances, exactly as you currently have or enhanced, you’ll not only see efficiencies and savings, but transparency of everything we do!
We invite you to walk down a well worn path and take a look at the possibilities. Time is precious and we respect that. You can simply request a customized online demonstration that can be accommodated at anytime.
