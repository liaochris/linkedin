Angel Medinilla, CSM’s Summary

12+ years working experience in the ICT market, five of them in AUNA (second biggest Telco operator in Spain) as a Project Manager and Systems Engineer; two of them in Iberbanda (WiMax operator) as a Project Manager. Relevant experience in Research&Development, including several EC funded projects, and advanced Internet services development.

Started my own Project Management Consulting firm two years ago. Today, Proyectalis is considered the leading Agile consulting and coaching company in Spain, offering Scrum training and implementation to a wide spectrum of software, telco and internet local and worlwide companies.
