B. M. Shahrier Majumder, PMP’s Summary

I am a talented and accomplished IT Management professional, with extensive background in Project Management and Process Improvement. Proven ability and experience to direct technical and business teams, and manage software development and process improvement projects. Adept at directing resources to work for projects from inception to completion.

My main strength is that I can take up any responsibility and can carry them out to the best of my ability. I am a problem solving and solution oriented professional. Around 8 years of my job experiences in different software development companies I have worked in Project Management, Requirement Analysis, Operations Management, HR Recruiting, Software Quality Assurance and Process Improvement functions.

Experience includes software development project management for financial accounts management, human resource management, garments company management, educational institute management; process improvement project to comply with SEI CMMI, ISO 9001; COTS product implementation project (Microsoft Project Server, Share Point Portal); conducting training on Project Management, Software Engineering and Quality Assurance functional areas.

I have working experience with Microsoft Project Professional & Project Server, Microsoft SharePoint Portal, Microsoft Visio, UML; having knowledge about Microsoft Solution Framework (MSF), Microsoft Visual Studio Team System (VSTS) & Team Foundation Server (TFS), PCMM and Six Sigma.

I have participated in the 20+ international level trainings & workshops related to Project Management, Software Engineering, Quality Assurance, Internal Quality Audit, CMMI and International Business Development.
