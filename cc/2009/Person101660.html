Fabrizio Ulisse’s Summary

After a couple of years as aspiring journalist, in 1991 I began working for tv network Videomusic as segment producer and writer. During this period I began developing my video production and management skills, and I started exploring the web as emerging new media.
In 1996 I joined a small new media agency in Tuscany, as web project manager . In 1999 I launched my own company (SAKE'), and until 2002 I worked with firms like Telecom, Finsiel, Trenitalia, Banca d'Italia, Ford, as information/media architect and project manager. In 2002 I began working as teacher (Universita' di Milano, Elis School), and as free-lance consultant, then I joined DOL where I started an internet tv division which developed the first political party webtv, dsonline.tv, and a series of internet tv and web 2.0 projects.
