Joy Montgomery’s Summary

Joy Montgomery is founder and principal of Structural Integrity. For more than 20 years she has developed effective business systems for information technology and manufacturing. She is a popular speaker on systems, processes and continuous improvement.

Joy is all about ensuring that you get a significant return on your investment (ROI) and demonstrating that a solid structure increases profits right away. Joy’s expertise is in building the base, the business system.

Joy particularly enjoys working with early stage and stealth mode startups. She optimizes and develops formal flows, procedures and organizational interfaces for them that have the integrity demanded by Sarbanes Oxley and ISO 9000 – and does it fast and affordably.

For returning Veterans, Joy is the founder of ReBoot Camp. ReBoot Camp is an early stage and not deliberately stealth mode effort to help returning Veterans reconnect to Family, Community, and Career. Our returning Veterans are part of the solution to America's economic recovery and should not be underemployed.
