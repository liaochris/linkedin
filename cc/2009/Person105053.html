Casey Dawes’s Summary

Prior to beginning her coaching training in 2003, Casey worked in multiple careers ranging from being a stage-hand at an Actor’s Equity theater in New England to running a multi-million dollar software users group all over the world.

Casey has personally consulted for organizations such as AT&T, Verizon, Principal Financial and UPS, helping them understand and organize their customer data to create more business opportunities for these large corporations.

She has been the “closer” for sales of consulting services to these organizations and created and launched a training program at IBM for their database sales people.

Now working with women small business owners, Casey has helped many reach their financial and operational goals through consulting, coaching and education.

Casey lives on the Monterey Bay south of San Francisco with her husband, Ken. They are owned by two cats – Mongo and Hera.
