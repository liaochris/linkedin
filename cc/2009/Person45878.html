Jen McCabe’s Summary

Primary objective: Sparking healthcare change using social media and n=1 consumer/patient advocacy. Jen has covered healthcare/hospital innovation @Health Management Rx, @TheWorldHealthCareBlog, @DisruptiveWomeninHealthcare, @Nexthealth.NL, @TheHealthCareBlog, and @Healthcentral.com.

Using Twitter, Jen aligns health/medical 'tweets' with research, contacts, and companies they need to get things done. Follow @jensmccabe.

A co-founder of the NextHealth collaborative (NL), Jen works to improve global care policy, planning, and delivery.

As former Speaker Liaison for Health 2.0 USA, she coached star players in the Health 2.0/HIT sphere.

Jen served as Associate Administrator at Christ House, performing ops, HR, safety, & admin/mgmt functions for the DC-area 33-bed nonprofit medical facility.

Jen cut her teeth as Analyst & Director during start-up days at The Hanover Research Council (www.hanoverresearch.com), sourcing, scheduling and closing her first meeting as a multiyear membership and cultivating HRCs early development in the hospital/healthcare vertical.

Prior to joining Hanover, Jen developed PM skills via total responsibility (budget & content) for production of a bimonthly magazine, Cast Polymer Connection, at the American Composite Manufacturers' Association.

Ms. McCabe formerly served as Vice Chair, Board of Directors, for Baltimore-based MEDBANK of Maryland, Inc. (www.medbankmd.org), an NFP providing free prescription medications to 35,000 residents via pharmaceutical firms' patient assistance programs (PAPs). She was elected unanimously to the BOD at age 26, her first such appointment.

A Paul H. Nitze Scholar, she received her B.A. in Literature, with a minor in Women, Gender, & Sexuality studies, magna cum laude, from St. Mary's College of Maryland (www.smcm.edu). Her thesis addressed "Experiential Poetry Readings: Catharsis for Author and Audience."

Jen McCabe’s Specialties:

Catalyst. Experiential analyst. Serial connector of healthcare innovators. Semantic web/HIT researcher, whiteboarding evolution of eHealth, mHealth.

Star analyst, using quant/qual methodologies to extrapolate goal definition & assessment.

Internal & external evangelist passionate about illuminating new initiatives, especially within hospital/healthcare & nonprofit strata.

"Finger on the pulse" managing of soft assets and targeted plans for program design, expansion, & implementation.
