Dr. Dwayne Jakes, CMC’s Summary

Dr. Dwayne D. Jakes. Is a native of Columbus, Georgia. I am a Certified Management Consultant (CMC) (R), Institute Of Management Consultants, USA, Washington, DC,

Area Chairman for the Graduate College of Business and Management at the University of Phoenix, Columbus, Georgia Campus. I Facilitate over 70 courses at the University of Phoenix Campus and Online. I have published several magazine articles for Consulting to Business,Top Consultant and Top Executive.

Founder and International Managing Director of Dwayne D. Jakes & Associates Management Systems, LLC. My web address is www.ddjakes.com. GOOGLE or America Online, my key words are "Start-Up Business Coach", Author and Publisher of “C2B” Consulting to Business an Electronic Magazine (The Forum for Consultants by Consultants Worldwide). I have over 20 years of Management experience and 19 years of Certified Management Consulting exp.

As International Managing Director, I am responsible for designing strategic, tactical, operations, fiscal, human capital, and business development for the firm. Work directly with c-level executives to determine cost, staffing resources for projects or enterprise-wide technology initiatives.

Combines progressive management consulting, project management, and business development experience with proven expertise in start-up, re-organizing, and directing consulting practices and working with Fortune 100 client accounts.
