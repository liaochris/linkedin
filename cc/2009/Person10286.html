Benjamin Lichtenwalner’s Summary

Benjamin Lichtenwalner has a broad background in business technology, including experience in support, programming, architecture, analysis, management and leadership roles. Lichtenwalner has implemented solutions or overseen the rapid growth of technology services at companies including Inc. 500, Fortune 500 and non-profit organizations. Benjamin is also a strong proponent of Servant-Leadership and works to educate others on the benefits of humble leadership through service first.

Mr. Lichtenwalner holds an M.B.A. with a concentration in Corporate Entrepreneurship from Lehigh University and a B.S. in Management Science and Information Systems from the Smeal College of Business at The Pennsylvania State University.

Regardless of his professional success, Mr. Lichtenwalner understands that it's not about him. He has been blessed with incredible teams and a wonderful network of family and friends whose support has been key in his continued success.

Beyond his professional interests, Benjamin also enjoys visiting with family, reading (The Good Book, business and technology publications) and riding his motorcycle. He spends most of his time between Westchester County, New York and Holland, MI with his wife and son.
