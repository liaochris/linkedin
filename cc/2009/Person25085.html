Don Parris’s Summary

Free/Open Source Software advocate and journalist, Security officer, ordained minister. Author of "Penguin in the Pew". Designed church management database back-end for MySQL and PostgreSQL. My phenomenal track record as a journalist includes moving up from new journalist to Editor-in-Chief in 6 months and leading LXer.com through strong growth over the course of the ensuing year. My ability to lead sustained growth and build community gave me the confidence to launch Blue GNU, a website focused more narrowly on the Free Software movement. Blue GNU, now in its 2nd quarter, is showing strong growth.
