Roshan Shah’s Summary

Roshan Shah, a Canadian Citizen and a Serial Entrepreneur is a strong advocate of Open Source Software. Roshan has delivered number of lectures on benefits of using Open Source to various Universities in India. He speaks regularly on Drupal and Ruby on Rails.

Roshan is CEO and founder of Gloscon Solutions Pvt Limited, a leading Open Source company which specializes in Drupal Development, Ruby on Rails Development and 24x7 Server Monitoring and Performance Tuning services for high traffic Drupal and Ruby on Rails sites.

As a Serial Entrepreneur, Roshan is known to build high performance teams and next generation leaders. Gloscon Solutions Pvt Limited fully owns Gujarat Hosting Pvt Limited ( a 24x7 Web Hosting and Infrastructure/Application Monitoring company) and OpenKick Technologies( Open Source Training Company) and regularly mentors the executive teams of both these subsidiaries.

Roshan now focuses most on Ruby on Rails with the third spin off - Rails House which provides Ruby on Rails Development services to global customers.

Before starting Gloscon Solutions Pvt Limited, Roshan spend 11 years in various roles working with clients and companies like IBM, HP, JP Morgan, Macmillan Computer Publishing, Peerflix, Speedware, Satyam as well as Larsen and Toubro Infotech. Roshan was also a Director in NetVision Web Technologies Pvt Limited - a company providing Java Training in Gujarat, India which later went Public.

Roshan Shah holds Bachelors Degree in Computer Engineering from DDIT, Advanced Diploma in Enterprise Management from AIMA, Diploma in Business Finance from ICFAI and is an Entrepreneurship Graduate from SEEDS, Canada.

Roshan is also Sun Certified Java Programmer and a Certified Project Manager.

Roshan is also Architecting and Designing various Web 2.0 properties for Indian markets using Drupal and Ruby on Rails as the development frameworks.
