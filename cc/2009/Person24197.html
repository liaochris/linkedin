David Hobbie’s Summary

My mission is to enhance search, knowledge sharing, and collaboration among the litigators at my firm. I bring to bear a strong understanding of the theory of knowledge management and social collaborative technology as well as my experience as a practicing litigator.

Published articles include:

"Legal Matter Management," in "Knowledge Management: More Than the Sum of Its Parts," ILTA White Paper, June 2009

"Enterprise 2.0 at Goodwin Procter," KMPro Journal, V. 6 No. 1, p. 14.

Speaking engagements include:

"Litigation Matter Management: Gathering and Reporting," (panel), August 27, 2009, International Legal Technology Association Conference, Washington DC

"Enterprise 2.0: What It Is and Why You Should Care," (extended session), August 25, 2009, International Legal Technology Association Conference, Washington, DC

"Expanding Search", October 2008, ARK Conference, Chicago, IL (only known KM presentation to include solo Bach)

"Extending Corporate KM? Litigation Knowledge Management" (panel), October 2008, ARK Conference, Chicago, IL,

"Wikis in Action," , October 2008, to an international knowledge management peer group, Boston, MA

"Matter Intelligence: Leveraging Information Over the Matter Life-Cycle," August 25, 2008, International Legal Technology Association Conference, Dallas, TX

"Social Tagging in the Enterprise: Different Drivers, Different Implementations," June 25, 2008, Boston KM Forum, Waltham, MA
