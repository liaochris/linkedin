Dundar Sahin’s Summary

Freelance consultant offering solutions in the arena of Social Compliance
Health & Safety, BCM and Emergency Management.

• Establishment of Social Accountability Management Systems, focused on how the management systems standard in SA8000 can improve the workplaces’ overall social performance and compliance with codes, and tools and systems most effective for monitoring and ensuring that subcontractors also maintain safe and decent working conditions.

• Establishment of Health and Safety Management Systems, focused on Health and Safety consulting to ensure adherence to specific industry standards, to provide training, and to perform program review and audits in the following areas:

• Safety Program Audit & Review
• Emergency Preparedness Plan Audit & Review
• Documents Related Review
• Accident/Incident Investigation, Review

• Establishment of Emergency Management Systems, focused on providing various Corporate Preparedness Planning services that facilitate the development and delivery of comprehensive emergency management services. Using site data and graphics, information is made readily available to corporates, school, hospital, industry and government response officials to aid in making strategic and tactical decisions for real-time incidents such as a chemical release, fire, or workplace incident.
