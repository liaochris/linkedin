Zemira Z. Jones’s Summary

Veteran broadcaster with 32 years experience and covering a wide range of broadcast platforms including radio, at the station and network level, television, satellite radio and new media. Jones has managed CHR, Classic Rock, Alternative Rock Active Rock, Modern Rock, Urban Contemporary, Urban AC, Jazz, Country, News/Talk, Radio for Kid's 6-11, and Sports formats across the country.

• Radio Ink Magazine named Zemira one of America’s “ 8 BEST MANAGERS IN RADIO” nationally. Radio and Records Magazine also voted Zemira as one of the TOP MANAGERS IN RADIO and his radio station WLS as News/Talk Station of the year.

• From 2004-2008 Zemira was Vice President of Operations for Radio One Inc. the nation’s 7th largest radio company with operational oversight for all 71 owned radio stations

• 1995-2004 President and General Manager ABC Radio/Chicago including News-Talk 890 WLS , Alternative Rocker WZZN-FM The Zone, Radio Disney 1300 WRDZ-AM and ESPN’s, ESPN 1000 WMVP-AM. He launched the flagship station that started the ESPN nationwide syndication. which is now over 300 radio stations nation wide.

• 1992-1995 General Sales Manager of the legendary Washington D.C. station WMAL-AM. Zemira first became a General Manager in 1982 with United Broadcasting Company with WRQC-FM, in Cleveland Ohio.

• Jones’ Radio career started in 1976 in Baltimore with United Broadcasting Company’s WLPL-FM/WSID-AM and then WXYV-FM owned by Schering-Plough Broadcasting. Jones’ reputation as a strong seller elevated him to Baltimore’s number one rated television station WJZ-TV owned by Westinghouse broadcasting and worked with the yet undiscovered local talk host" Oprah Winfrey for three years.

• Served on Board of directors of Reach Media, parent company of The Tom Joyner Morning Show. The Illinois Broadcasters Assoc., Chicagoland Chamber of Commerce, Streetwise Newspaper, Volunteers of America of Illinois, Associated Press Advisory Board, and Silver Cross Hospital.
