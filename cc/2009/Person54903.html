Kevin Aires’s Summary

Digital Experience Pioneer | Innovator | Community Builder

Kevin is Director - Digital Experience for George P Johnson UK Ltd. GPJ is the worlds premier experience marketing company and is a leader in integrating online and live experiences to create a whole new marketing paradigm we call OnLIVE.

Kevin was previously Events Manager for IBM’s Virtual Business Center in the online world of 'Second Life', and Guildmaster of IBM’s Virtual Universe Community of over 6000 IBMer’s who are exploring virtual worlds for business.

Kevin regularly presents on virtual worlds and has even hosted his own live virtual chat show! He regularly blogs on virtual worlds and web2.0 and assisted IBM UK with their web2.0 and client community building efforts.

Kevin has over eleven years professional experience working for blue chip companies (Shell, Exxon, IBM), and is generally valued for his proven ability to generate and promote creative strategic solutions to business issues and holds two IBM multimedia patents.
