Susan Hailstones’s Summary

I am a LAMP, Perl & PHP Recruitment Specialist. My portfolio of client’s ranges from start up businesses to large digital giants. More recently I have worked with a few clients using a combination of LAMP, WAMP & some Java.

Networking is at the heart of my business. Most of my candidates can not be found on the job boards & many of my clients were referred to me.

I differentiate myself from other agencies in many ways-all of which save time, money and hassle!

Some of our unique selling points are;

* Part of the Adecco group-CP is the largest Agency in the UK
* Financially secure,stable & reliable-CP has been in business for 37 years.
* E-Services, 350 + tech tests, bespoke testing available, psychometric testing, salary benchmarking, MI, competitor analysis & online training
* Dedicated account management
* Vertical market specialists-who understand your business, your market sector & the technologies that you use
* Competency-based selection with fully briefed, qualified & recommended shortlist of CV’s

My business is about value not volume. I believe the recruitment process is a 2 way street and I will work with my Clients to establish exactly what they are looking for and with my candidates to ensure that they are prepared & ready for every interview they attend.

Whether you are a company looking for high calibre candidates or a Job seeker looking for a high calibre company; Please email: susanhailstones@computerpeople.co.uk or call me on 0207 440 2095
