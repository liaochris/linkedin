Amit Avasthi’s Summary

An MBA in HR, Amit has wide experience working with organizations of all sizes, and industries on their HR issues, including HR Technology adoption.
Having worked in roles ranging from R&D, Product Management, Business Development and solution implementation, Amit can help you gain insights into both HR issues and their technology fallout and thereby can help in identifying the best solution for customer needs.

Having worked with the largest of large and complex cases, Amit comes equipped with knowledge of structuring, working and aligning on large deals as well as creating more mindshare.

Having been responsible for directing all business development activity for the Human Capital Management suite of products across India in his past assignments,.has worked on Business plan creation and market assessment for all new HCM products.
Amit has also contributed to thought leadership with various presentations on different forums, articles on Human Capital Management and contributes regularly through his blog.
