Russ Christ’s Summary

With 15 years of writing, editing, and media relations experience, we specialize in creating effective media relations and community outreach programs for mid-size, growth oriented businesses. We help our clients identify newsworthy story angles and work strategically to align the story with the most appropriate media outlet/target audience.

Ambitious, creative and organized, I have demonstrated high-level success in public relations, client management and relationship building. Abilities include client and staff management, adapting quickly to and juggling the needs of clients and media, and writing concise, powerful releases and pitches.

Have a proven ability to increase brand awareness about and sales for clients. This is accomplished by securing consistently favorable coverage in international, national and regional consumer and trade outlets. I ensure team members do so as well.

Client servicing functions include:

• Developing and executing PR plans
• Crafting news releases and ghost-written articles
• Phone and e-mail feature-story pitching
• Client reporting
• Preparing electronic and print media kits
• Completing national media e- and postal mailings
• Organizing and participating in media tours
• Managing client image databases
• Researching and helping manage client media lists
• 15 years experience in PR, journalism and consumer marketing
• Experience developing and implementing PR plans
• Proficiency extracting information from subjects and writing news releases
• Elevated understanding of and passion for the golf, resort, and travel industries
• The ability to interact successfully with media, clients, colleagues and business people
• Computer proficiency with Microsoft Office, Powerpoint, Photoshop, scanning and basic graphics programs

Russ Christ’s Specialties:

Specialize in golf-specific writing and media outreach; 11-year member of the Golf Writers Association of America; co-founder of the Southwest Golf Media Association;

In addition to media relations, our services include all aspects of strategic public relations: from brand positioning, media relations, to leveraging advertising, building community relations and securing partnerships. Our focus is to effectively communicate your message and connect your business with key audiences.
