Gina Capone’s Summary

Currently the community manager/leader for the JDRF type 1 social website for people and families with diabetes and also an expert patient blogger at mydiabetescentral.com.

I also have experience in the design field, over 10 years under my belt this year. My speciality? Designing weekly, monthly and e-pdf publications. My design style: Clean. I believe that less is more and by using cool type treatments, interesting colors and eye catching graphics you can achieve the wow factor, without making the page overbearing for the reader.

I am available for a full-time or freelance opportunity in the design or healthcare industry.
