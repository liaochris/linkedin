Shannon Burns’s Summary

Victory Solutions LLC’s proprietary technology and services provide Republican and conservative clients with a competitive advantage in political campaigns, party building and issue advocacy. The technology and services dramatically improve the effectiveness and accountability of political grassroots operations. They were proven to be highly effective during the Ohio gubernatorial primary campaign of Ken Blackwell and with the Ohio Republican Party in 2006. Victory VoIP is a proprietary phone system and call management service that nearly triples the call output of a traditional volunteer phone bank. The Victory Center is a Web-based grassroots operations management system that exponentially increases the impact of grassroots operations on the outcome of elections and the legislative process.
