Marcello Valentini’s Summary

A strong curiosity and a very wide set of interests (varying from quantic-physics to psychology and to astrology) have been leading me to continuosly try to increase both my technical and my social knowledge, and to find relations between them.

Having an INTP (Introverted iNtuitive Thinking Perceiving) personality type, I love to use my knowledge and my understanding in order to solve any kind of problems (both technical and non-technical ones).

A Diploma in Computer Science, a Laurea degree (MEng equivalent) in Electronic Engineering and a further Master in Telecommunications, give me a technical knowledge that covers all aspects of the Information & Communication Technology (ICT) field. In the last years I have also attended Management, Economics and Business Development training courses.

Currently, I am looking for a new job in the ICT field that could offer me a combination of dynamic activities and frequent travels, management responsibilities and positive interactions with other people. I am also evaluating different entrepreneurship opportunities, in order to make up an ICT company by my own based on one of my personal interests (mainly related to mobile business and innovation, wearable technologies, context awareness).

Marcello Valentini’s Specialties:

My technical and social background help me being a natural technical leader, having job/teams organization capabilities and a strong focus on targets reaching. I like public speaking and every kind of situation involving contacts with other people.

My technical skills are mainly related to software design in C/C++, Java, web design in PHP and ASP, hardware design in VHDL, system integration, analysis and evaluation of new technologies and technical solutions.
