Tamela Rich, MBA’s Summary

I GHOSTWRITE for subject matter experts and professionals. Output ranges from newsletters and executive summaries to opinion pieces and blogs.

I write CONTENT and COPY that align with strategic business goals.

I provide CAN-SPAM-compliant newsletter services, from copy and custom templates through turnkey mailing and robust analytics.

I write business plans, white papers, case studies, presentations and funding proposals, all of which require analytical and communications expertise.

The meat and potatoes of business writing persuades through the use of information and analysis. Problem is, most people who understand numbers are not gifted communicators. I hone my narrative skills with magazine articles in Charlotte Magazine.
