Ted DeCorte, Benefits Consultant’s Summary

Ted DeCorte has 19 years experience in the healthcare/ health insurance industry, and is currently director of product development/sales (dental, HSA, HRA) for Sierra Healthcare Options, a UnitedHealthcare company, in Las Vegas, NV. He also develops web properties including mylasvegasnow.com, eclecticbuzz.com, christinedecorte.com, smartwomeninvest.com, as well as others.

Ted has a varied background in business, government and education, serving in operations for Nevada Pacific Dental, First National Bank of NV, in marketing/sales for UnitedHealthcare, Sierra Health & Life, NPD,in fundraising for the Jerry Lewis Telethon & Muscular Dystrophy Association, as a college history instructor and high school teacher and administrator, as district director for Congressman James H. Bilbray (D-NV) and as a writer & consultant. His writings have been featured in publications for conservation, business, chambers of commerce, consumers and seniors.

In high school and in college he moonlighted as a percussionist for a singing group that included at various times: Lynda Carter (“Wonder Woman”) and Dianne Kay (“Eight Is Enough” / ”1941”). In 1992, Ted peered into a empty bag of potato chips as the “dad” in a humorous “Vegas Chips” advertising campaign that proclaimed “You Gotta Hold ‘em.”

Ted is active in the community as an assistant scout leader, a church board member, a board member and legislative liaison for Clark County Health Underwriters Association, a member of Nevada State and National Associations of Health Underwriters, a board member for Center for Creative Therapeutic Arts and Scoliosis Association of Clark County, a member of Small Business Development Center committee. He has also served as chair for Las Vegas Impact Lecture Series, Congressional Award committee, Southern Nevada Job Fair, as a member of Henderson Rotary, and on Clark County Democratic Central Committee.
Ted networks www.linkedin.com., www.plaxo.com, and www.facebook.com.
