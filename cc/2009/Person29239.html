Edwin Ebreo’s Summary

EDWIN C. EBREO has over 14 years experience in human resource management and training and is currently working as President and General Manager of ExeQserve Corporation (pronounced as eksekyuserv) an HR Solutions company engaged in Executive Search, Training and Human Resource Consulting. He works concurrently as HR Manager of CheQ Systems and as HR Consultant for a number of SME's and Cooperatives in the Philippines. His last position was as HR Director of Athena E-Services, a business process outsourcing company. He has occupied senior HR and training positions in PET Plans Group, SPI Technologies, Inc. and Philippine Seven Corp. (7-11 Food Stores). He also worked as HR Consultant for a number of IT, BPO companies and a Cooperative with National presence.

He has conducted numerous training programs on teambuilding, leadership, basic supervision, performance management, customer service, visioning, problem solving and decision-making, trainer’s training, and labor management cooperation, among others, for his companies and other companies. He also wrote a number of articles as a career advisor for MyTrabaho.Com, a web-based recruitment and career service company.
