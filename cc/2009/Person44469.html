Jay Durgan’s Summary

Outhink Media provides services and software to connect, inform and inspire a fast growing community of independent video producers and other new media artists. Through website properties like ourmedia.org, SpinXpress.com and other efforts, Outhink Media hosts a friendly gathering place for participants to share ideas, collaborate and market their skills.

Outhink Media is part social networking, part producer peer mentoring and part project work flow integrator for a uniquely creative group of individuals whether they are new media producers, editors, recording artists, videographers or any other involved contributors that love making and working in the progressive media space.

Helping creative new media artists produce compelling content and generate profit through collaborative efforts from anywhere in the world at anytime is what Outhink Media is all about.

Raiden Holdings, San Francisco focuses on investing in and working with technology companies that provide solutions to fixed and mobile platforms benefiting media, sport, entertainment and informatics.

I have exceptional experience and extensive knowledge: 1) building businesses; 2) developing and exploiting brands; 3) generating new revenue sources for established products; 4) identifying new technologies that represent valid business opportunities; and 5) in many countries, economies, political circumstances and other unique climates.
