Phill Cross’s Summary

As an Internet-savvy real estate professional in the Greater Baltimore Maryland Metropolitan Area, I've had the opportunity to help many homebuyers find their ideal homes and many sellers obtain top dollar for their homes. Buying or selling a home is one of the largest financial transactions that most people ever undertake, so I will help you through every step of the process.

The cornerstone to my success is first-class, friendly, and effective personal service. I coordinate with you to make certain you are getting the best possible service, with the least amount of stress, time or effort on your part. My goal is to make your home buying or selling experience as easy and enjoyable as possible.

I maintain a "State of the Art" website with links to informative and useful information. I gather up-to-the-minute reports on finance and market trends allowing me to peform the best possible job for Buyers and Sellers alike. I strive daily to keep up with the latest in High Technology, including "the Net", while not losing sight of 'Old Fashioned' personal service with my clients.
