Evan Rudowski’s Summary

Two decades of international management operating experience in the online media industry on two continents.

SubHub provides a managed platform that makes it easy and affordable for content creators, owners and publishers to build and manage revenue-generating content websites.

Atlantic Leap helps U.S. internet companies to enter and expand into Europe.

Previously I was managing director of Excite UK and Excite Europe, a subsidiary of Excite@Home. Before that I spent two years with Excite.com in Silicon Valley. In the late 1980s and early 1990s I worked in New York with News Corporation and the Tribune Company's Newsday on some of their early online initiatives.

Evan Rudowski’s Specialties:

Online content publishing and revenue generation via subscription, advertising and e-commerce.

European market entry strategy and execution for American internet companies. Substantial pan-European management and operating experience.
