Barbara Safani’s Summary

As a career strategist and resume writer, I specialize in managing job search campaigns for six-figure professionals and executives who know where they want to go, but need assistance uncovering the most efficient path.

I have over twelve years of experience in career management, recruiting, and executive coaching. I have appeared as a career expert on CNN, ABC, FOX, and ROBtv and my career advice has been published in The New York Times, The Wall Street Journal, The Washington Post, Fortune Magazine, Yahoo HotJobs, AOL, and MSNBC online. I am a career adviser for CareerBuilder and a triple-certified resume writer, a distinction only a handful of writers worldwide have achieved. I am the author of Happy About My Resume: 50 Tips for Building a Better Document to Secure a Brighter Future and my work has been published in more than two dozen career-relevant books. In addition, I am a regular contributor to The Ladders, Kennedy Executive Agent, and Career Hub, a top-25 HR blog. My coaching and writing credentials include:

Certified Career Master (CCM)
Certified Expert Resume Writer (CERW)
Nationally Certified Resume Writer (NCRW)
Certified Professional Resume Writer (CPRW)

Barbara Safani’s Specialties:

job search strategy, networking, interviewing, and salary negotiation coaching, on-line identity management, corporate outplacement, career research, and career marketing collateral (resumes, bios, marketing letters, marketing plans, on-line profiles).
