Robert Bryant’s Summary

Draftnik since 1988 and owner of NFL Draft Dog since 2004. NFL Draft Dog is a scouting service which manages NFL Draft Dog.com and the weblog "The NFL Draft Site". What is a Draftnik you ask? Someone that covers the NFL Draft year round and that is what we do at NFL Draft Dog. We scout NFL Draft prospects by analyzing hours upon hours of game film and have multiple contacts within the industry including current and former NFL Scouts, coaches, current and former players, experienced sports writers and other Draftniks.
