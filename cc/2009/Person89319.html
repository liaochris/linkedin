Scott Baldwin’s Summary

I have been working in the commercial real estate industry since the early 90’s. The bulk of my experience has been in land sales, but I also sell and lease a bit of office, retail, and investment real estate. I work with my three brothers and we currently own two companies: a commercial and residential real estate firm and a marketing/advertising firm. Our companies collectively have over 50 employees and independent contractors. The companies have very strong internet advertising and marketing presences, which are a significant value-add to our clients. We have truly harnessed the internet as a solid marketing vehicle for our real estate endeavors; and, our web design services provide a unique opportunity for real estate related clients.

I'm also a former Marine and police officer. I left the Indianapolis Metropolitan Police Department before official retirement, but I am currently volunteering as a detective in my local county’s Special Task Team. My experience at IMPD ranged from Field Training Officer, Narcotics Detective, Homicide Detective - Unit Supervisor, Uniform Sergeant, to Metro Drug Task Force. The military and investigative background has been very useful to me as a real estate broker from an organizational, communications, and task oriented perspective.
