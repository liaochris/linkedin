Christophe Hamerling’s Summary

I am Service Oriented Architecture R&D engineer at eBM WebSourcing, the French SOA open-source editor.
My research and development activities are mainly around the PEtALS Distributed Entreprise Service Bus project (http://petals.ow2.org). This open-source SOA runtime is based on an ambitious and agile architecture. PEtALS is the core module for other open-source products we develop at eBM WebSourcing and for European and French Research projects we are involved in.
As core commiter of several OW2 projects (PEtALS ESB, Dragon SOA Governance, easyWSDL), my main domains of interest are Web Services, SOA, open-source, distributed computing, Open Source and all things around Java stuff.

    SOA Research Engineer and PEtALS ESB product manager.

    More on the project website : http://petals.ow2.org

    I am currently working on the SOA4All research project. SOA4ALL is an European research project which will help to realize a a world where billions of parties are exposing and consuming services via advanced Web technology.
    My main roles in this project is to participate to the architecture definition and reference implementation of the Service Bus. This work will be based on the PEtALS ESB project and on research product which will drive us to a highly distributed service bus at the web scale.

    More on the prject website : http://www.soa4all.eu
