Ron Herron’s Summary

A successful creative advertising and marketing manager with proven experience in all aspects of advertising and graphic design, production, planning and analysis. Currently pursuing project management and consulting opportunities involving direct client and creative contact that put my extensive expertise to good use.

Ron Herron’s Specialties:

Expert high-end studio creative & production management; skilled counsel on broadcast production cost/media placement competitive analysis; social networking for brand awareness, excellent copywriting skills; direct & mentor creative personnel; conduct supplier negotiations; review creative content and resolve production issues; provide expert evaluation of creative processes to every level of an organization. Proficient in PhotoShop, InDesign, Quark, DreamWeaver, MS Word, PowerPoint and Excel.

Ron Herron’s Experience

  • Business Process Manager, Marketing Operations

    General Motors

    (Public Company; GM; Automotive industry)

    2004 — 2008 (4 years )

    Analyzed advertising production efficiency for print and broadcast media; identified and implemented improved tracking methods; provided research and initial planning for GM as an early adopter of the interactive Ad-ID process for tracking and reporting on all production and media spending.

    Working with the AAAA and ANA trade organizations, advised on ad efficiency to GM brand team senior management, their ad agencies and GM Planworks. Developed reporting guidelines, advised on the formation of new business processes, and communicated process requirements to all levels.

  • Manager, Graphics & Photo Operations

    General Motors

    (Public Company; GM; Automotive industry)

    1994 — 2004 (10 years )

    Managed business processes for graphic and interactive design, publishing, printing and photo services in the Detroit region. Established first in-house interactive design team to work on the integration of sound design principles and technical web expertise. Brought under-achieving section from a $900K annual deficit to profitability within 18 months.

    Based on these positive results, was given Process Manager responsibility for the $22-million-plus graphic and interactive design, print and photo services operations in Southeast Michigan, which included facilities and personnel in Detroit, Warren, Pontiac, Milford and Grand Blanc.

  • Operations Manager

    GM Public Relations

    (Public Company; GM; Automotive industry)

    1980 — 1994 (14 years )

    Managed the design and production of SEC-regulated financial documents with important public relations use, such as the GM Annual Report. Also produced other publications targeting special constituencies, overseeing design and final production/distribution.

    Wrote and edited award-winning articles for GM publications. Designed and produced materials for GM special events such as the GM Cancer Foundation. Wrote, designed and produced public relations materials used during GM's sponsorship of the World Cup.
