Dr. Anthony W. Tanona’s Summary

If you are having difficulty with your child or adolescent, or are looking to improve your family relationships, I am here to help. I specialize in the treatment and psychological assessment of children and adolescents. I also have extensive experience providing family and couples therapy.

I would be honored to work with you in an attempt to enhance interactions within your family, improve communication and understanding in your relationships, and decrease disruptive behaviors and conflict. Additionally, if your child is struggling academically or their school is requesting an evaluation, I provide Psychological Testing to effectively assess the problem and provide practical recommendations. I am on many insurance plans, and offer a sliding fee scale to meet your individual financial situation. In addition, I offer morning, daytime, and evening hours Monday through Saturday.

I have extensive experience providing psychotherapy to children, adolescents, and families. I am a warm and caring therapist who will work with you and your loved ones to help you achieve your goals. Visit me at http://www.DrTanona.com or contact me at Delta Psychological Group at 305-933-5733 for a free initial telephone consultation or to schedule an appointment.
