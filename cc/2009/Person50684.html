Josef Assad’s Summary

Strategic consultant with substantial IT experience. Special focus on open source technology and consultation around open culture strategy. Industry experience includes bilateral and multilateral development agencies (UNDP, Italian Cooperation, etc.) as well as the public sector (Ministries of Higher Education and of Social Affairs, etc) in addition to private sector (having been involved in open source strategic work with HP, EFG Hermes to bring VC funding for open source to the Middle East).

Broader consultation experience includes development of national long-range strategy for the Egyptian ceramic sector as part of an EU funded study.

Josef Assad’s Specialties:

Industry-level strategy development, enterprise strategic management consultancy, open source (from grass roots to public/private enterprise-class). Ceramic tile industry, production/sales/profitability planning.
