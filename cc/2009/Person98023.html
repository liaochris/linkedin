Billy Martinez’s Summary

An Introduction;

Often corporations search for candidates relying almost exclusively on the surface value of the resume, a simple list of accomplishments in the plain vernacular of business. But they only serve to scratch the surface. They can’t convey energy, enthusiasm, adaptability, resourcefulness or the ability to roll with the punches. They can’t tell you how well someone will react in moments of pressure or crisis.

They cannot tell you about a persons ambition.

So let me introduce myself properly.

I am hardworking, reliable, engaging and affable. I work well with others and am still able to operate independently of direct supervision without overstepping the limits of my mandate. I look forward to challenges and I excel under pressure. I’m looking for an organization with a clear vision, a focus on its mission with a clear understanding of the value of training and industry standards. A professional development environment that will allow me to flex my skills and exercise my ability to learn new technologies and methodology.

I am looking for something challenging enough to keep me searching for answers. If your organization can meet that challenge, I would welcome the opportunity to review my experience in greater detail.
