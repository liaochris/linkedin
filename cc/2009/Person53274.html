Karen Lumholt’s Summary

Journalist, educated in Aarhus (Danmarks Journalisthøjskole) and University of Copenhagen. From 1994 journalist and radiohost at Danmarks Radio (radio, politics and debate). From 2000 editor and journalist at Forbrugerinformationen og Forbrugerstyrelsen. From 2004 owner/partner in Lumholt & Stahlschmidt Kommunikation doing storytelling, public relations, information services and human resource development.
Before: Teacher in history, religion and drama, educated in Zahles Seminarium.

Karen Lumholt’s Specialties:

Appreciative Inquiry. Storylistening, storytelling, rolemodels. Brand development. Communication strategy. Public relations.
