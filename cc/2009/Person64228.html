Marty Barnhart’s Summary

Business operations is a specialty that can help a company grow and a business leader focus better. It has been my strength to "do the details" for businesses which frees the CEO or Board of Directors to show their strengths. It is a privilege to serve as a virtual Business Operations Officer for companies through Steady Growth Enterprises.

Over the past 25 years I have enjoyed the opportunity to work in County government, to oversee five non-profits and serve with gifted leaders. Through great training and experience I have learned how to lead with wisdom. My goal is to provide well for my family and be a trusted advisor and servant leader.  I want to use my gifts to grow, connect and activate people.

Marty Barnhart’s Specialties:

Business Operations Officer, Consultant for Profit and Non Profit, Communicator, Staff Mentor and expert in growing businesses.
