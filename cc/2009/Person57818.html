Laura Salasco’s Summary

Experienced board level executive who helps companies to achieve significant growth by designing, planning and executing new strategies and major implementation projects, in a direct leadership role or as advisor to management.

Highly reputed Business Excellence leader and renowned EFQM Award assessor. Track record of building high-performance teams and creating effective partnerships across different cultures, business sizes and management styles.

A visionary who created new consumer-facing venture capital-backed businesses, and provided thought leadership in environments where chaotic new technologies needed to be commercially evaluated and driven for shareholder benefit.

An English-Italian bilingual with 20 years’ international management experience, has led multi-site cross-functional European projects, delivering on time, in full, below budget and exceeding customer expectations.
