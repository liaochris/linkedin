Matt Lee’s Summary

Bingo Theory Inc. is an interactive consulting and services company, bridging the gap between ideas, people and technology. Matt Lee and Erin Rauk bring a combined 30-year focus on the best of what marketing and technology has to offer. The Bingo Theory team is a unique blend of strategy and big-picture thinking while applying tactical elements to every client engagement. Erin and Matt have experience from both the traditional and interactive marketing worlds that help create seamless brand messaging. They have worked with, and in, both start-ups and Fortune 500 companies, including Target, General Mills, and 3M.
