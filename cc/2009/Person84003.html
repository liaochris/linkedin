Rick Morgan’s Summary

As an independent consultant and adviser, I have worked on advancing the success of the Independent Agency System for over 25 years. I owned my own agency in Boulder, Colorado, was co-founder, and served as publisher and editor of the TAAR (The Automated Agency Report). I am the originator of “Transactional Filing”, co-developer of Silver Plume, served as Sr. Vice President of ConfirmNet and Sr. Vice President of Marketing for Applied Systems. Today, much of my focus is helping my clients create and implement a social web strategy.

I am currently serving as Chair of the IIABA Agents Council for Technology (ACT) Web 2.0 workgroup and Director At-Large for IIABNY

Life isn't all business.

* I'm an adventurer. I love to travel, hike, mountain bike, camp, go on backpacking trips, and travel across the United States with JoAnne in our RV.
* I'm an artist. That means I know what shade of green the lichen on the trees is.
* I'm a grandpa. I have 8 grandkids, several of whom enjoy beating me at Wii bowling and tennis.
* I'm a golfer. I can finally keep some of my drives in the fairway.
* I'm a technology geek. If it were up to me, I would have named my grandkids Eudora, Mozilla, and Dotcom.
* I'm a lucky man. I live in Broomfield, Colo. with my wife JoAnne. She was my high school sweetheart.
