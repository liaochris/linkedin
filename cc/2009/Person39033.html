Hayley Every’s Summary

More than 8 years experience in establishing and overseeing research projects related to the development and analysis of new materials. Responsibilities have included preparation of research proposals, for both government funding and industrial partners, mentoring students (MSc and PhD levels) as well as conducting independent research.

Hayley Every’s Specialties:

Materials science, and in particular the structure-property relationships of materials. Experience with analytical techniques including NMR spectroscopy, impedance spectroscopy, x-ray spectroscopy and thermal analysis.
