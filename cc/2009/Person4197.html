John J. Alquist’s Summary

Owner/Operator: Alquist Enterprises, 1987 to the present. Business consulting, network marketing, professional speaking (with emphasis on the rise fo self-employment and Internet business proficiency) professional writing and business consulting.

1972-1987: Bank Marketing Executive. Vice President & Marketing Director, BarclaysAmerican (owned by the Barclays Group); Vice President, Market Planning, Wells Fargo Bank; Advertising Manager, Peoples Trust of New Jersey.

Goals: to encourage and assist people become self-employed, returning us back to the historical normalcy of 80% self-employment. This is being done by the operation of an AMSOIL network as well as consulting, writing and speaking.

John and his wife own, operate and are building a national AMSOIL distribution team. We cover 31 states and seek more growth.

John is a professional speaker, business consultant, and business writer.

John and his wife also have a Water4Gas business as well as am OptumHealthAllies business--both online.

John J. Alquist’s Specialties:

Business consulting, professional speaking, strategic planning meeting consulting and facilitation, network marketing, consumer and business marketing. Also a prolific business writer--business plans, newsletters, promotion material, PR news releases.
