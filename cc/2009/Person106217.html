Yvette Dubel’s Summary

I am in the simplest sense a " clear creative mind PLUS" for your sustainability. I specialize in delivering creativity in the context of multilateral solutions.

It is a challenge to describe holistic solutions because so much depends on the context.

I work with a small team to develop projects with multifunctional attached event series that engage growing niche markets. More than buzz marketing we develop a program that will keep the right people paying attention.
I am especially interested in collaborations to explore Quality Attention.

Are you looking for alternatives to the "hit or miss" of mass marketing?

This approach is more cost effective over the duration of your relationship with the marketplace than traditional advertising and delivers unparalleled meaning at the individual level.

With a focused approach you benefit from my ability to see beyond borders to give managers and business owners comparative advantages for earned value. Finally, see connections that are often not obvious but vital to getting to the heart of a matter on time and within budget.
