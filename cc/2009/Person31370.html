Peter Ericson’s Summary

Today businesses need to tackle not only the mission of their website but also to compel visitors to land there. Blogging, video, social media and seo put companies on the path to gaining customers trust and pointing them towards their offerings.

Peter is the founder and President of The Complete Website, LLC which focuses on building custom WordPress solutions. His team builds corporate websites, custom web-apps, blogs, online magazines, and leverages seo and social media to achieve results.
