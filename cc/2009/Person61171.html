Maarten Van Vulpen’s Summary

We create advanced webapplications for publishers. The platform is a stable release, which contains all the needs for modern publishers. The platform is used for portals, digital magazines, sending of massmail and events. Clients like SDU, Kluwer, Beta Publishers, VNU, myBusinessMedia, Elektor Media and Ilse Media do use our services and application.

Our main focus is on publishers. Next to these customers, we also work for financial institutions and some other 'outside our core branche'-companies.

We do not only build these applications. Publishers can consult us for strategic advice. Together we will start from the business model to the launch of the website. We have capacities for all parts of the way from scratch to launch.
