Cesar Aya’s Summary

After spending 9 years within the Sales & Marketing area in the IT industry in Latin America, Cesar decided to start the MBA programme at Oxford University. From the first moment after arriving in Oxford, Cesar refocused his career to Strategic Planning and Futures Thinking (Scenarios). He worked on a Strategic Consulting Project on Scenarios for a private reinsurance company, under the James Martin Institute for Science and Civilization supervision, during July and August 2008.

He currently advises a European Agency on the implementation of Scenarios and is involved in Scenario projects with Longevity and Pension risks, Global Sustainable Development and Global Financial Development and Regulation.

Cesar Aya’s Specialties:

Scenarios for improving the understanding of the future, contesting organisations´ strategy and dealing with complex problems. Project management, business development, client management, distribution channels, and multi-country operations.
