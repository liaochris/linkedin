Lori Chance’s Summary

I love writing. Whether it's helping a first-time author get their thoughts into book, or writing my own life lessons in a way that inspires, empowers and connects people, writing is one of my passions in life.

I'm also truly loving the opportunity to offer affordable life coaching opportunities for women to not just achieve their goals but realize their dreams. Having experienced coaching, I know the value and amazing results it can provide and enjoy giving that gift to others.

My book, Who Am I? How to Answer the Single Most Important Question You'll Ever Ask, combines these two passions and is NOW AVAILABLE! It's a self-coaching book for women who want that something 'more' out of life but don't know where to start. Here is what others are already saying about it:

**Awareness of values is the key to unlock our hidden potential. Lori Chance has created easy-to-follow steps to identify our values and a how-to guide to use them in our everyday lives. I highly recommend her book to those individuals seeking clarity and direction. ~Rev. Christine Green, Spiritual Mentor.

** Who Am I is concise, empathetic, human, and practical. This isn't someone talking down to us from their million dollar pedestal. Lori and all her examples are real people. The best part was the values exercises. I couldn't help printing them out so I could work on them the next day in my morning journaling. ~ Barb Rees, author and speaker for Dreams Inspirations Seminars.

**The premise of the book is a very important one. I recommend her book for anyone who has been struggling with their life plan. Besides practical exercises, the book also offers stories of real people facing a variety of difficult situations that are easy to relate to. ~ Mia Neuse, licensed acupuncturist and mom
