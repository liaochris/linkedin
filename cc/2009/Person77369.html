Paula Hendricks’s Summary

Author, writer, publisher, book designer, book producer, book shepherd / coach. Paula Hendricks has authored several books and made significant contributions to more than eighteen. Cinnabar Bridge Publishing is the publishing arm of her communications business.

Strong marketing background: Management positions in major New York City ad agencies; author, founder of an artists’ owned art gallery, photojournalist, working artist, eCommerce pioneer in New Mexico.

Experienced project manager, writer, b/w photographer with strong marketing background helps sustainable businesses and experts with their book projects. Our particular interests are cities, the built environment, energy and water.

Other Publishing credits: More than 100 editorial contributions to magazines, web sites, and newspapers.

Workshop credits: Paula facilitates workshops on publishing, writing, and networking. She consistently gets rave reviews from attendees and sponsors.
