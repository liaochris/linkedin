Eb Schmidt’s Summary

productivity21 is providing consulting, coaching and training services to corporations in the US and Germany for increased productivity. To accomplish this goal we are using one of the following tool sets:

1. The 10 step process of Expense Reduction Analysts to find extra profit by reducing non-core business expenditures,
2. Lean/6Sigma tools for continuous improvement processes and
3. The Mission Control® Productivity system with its potential to double individual productivity.

In short we can help you

1. save money,
2. reduce waste, improve quality and increase production and
3. experience power, freedom and peace of mind in the face of everything you have to do and handle.
