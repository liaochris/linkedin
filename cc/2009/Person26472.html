Diane C. Hill’s Summary

Korean Community Service Center, grant proposals for JS Free Clinic, CYITC in DC, No.Va. Community Foundation, prep for CFRE certification.

As a creative, resourceful marketing & development professional by training, I have a history of creating good value in the business sector incl. leadership of product development teams & alliances in ICT (Information Comms Tech) -- plus my nonprofit career & community involvement. What I most enjoy is using my marketing & partnering talents to help solve social problems & create social value. I’m an active member of AFP (Assn. I'm committed to using my "helping spirit" to improve "quality of life" community issues, now in excellent health after a year+ hiatus beating hepatitis. I’d love the opportunity to make a difference at a global, national or community-based social enterprise. My work at Inova Health System to help save patients’ lives in our community, as Sr. Dev. Target job titles: Manager, Officer , Director, Assoc. for: Resource development, corporate or community relations/partnerships, community engagement, cause marketing, leadership giving, grantsmanship.

What is a mission of “improving quality of life community issues”? It's the important work serving needs such as:
- Community/public health or other health related
- Literacy, incl. financial, information, medical
- Nonprofit mgmt & leadership
- Community needs related, incl. empowering those living with disadvantages, housing, workforce development, civic engagement, youth service learning, elderly/aging.
