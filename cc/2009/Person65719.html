Max Lenderman’s Summary

Max Lenderman is Executive Creative Director at GMR Marketing, the largest experiential marketing company in North America, where his work has won numerous industry recognitions, the latest including the 2006 & 2007 Pro Award, the 2006 MAA Worldwide Globes Award, the 2009 Effie Award and the 2007, 2008 and 2009 Ex Award.

Max’s book called "Experience the Message: How Experiential Marketing is Changing the Brand World" was a Canadian Business Book of the Year Finalist in 2006. It was translated into Spanish in 2008.

His latest book called “Brand New World: How Oligarchs, Paupers and Pirates Are Changing Business” is published by HarperCollins.

Max wrote a column in Strategy Magazine called “Notes from the Underground,” and is a sought-after public speaker on the subjects of strategic branding, experiential marketing and global business trends.

Max is a founding board member of the International Experiential Marketing Association (IXMA).

He lives in Chicago, but spends most of his time in airports and on airplanes. His blog is found at experiencethemessage.com.
