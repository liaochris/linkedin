Nicolai Andler’s Summary

Nicolai (39) is an experienced high perfpormance business coach. Since 1999, he has been involved in coaching, management training, large business transformation projects, classic management consulting and IT projects.

Nicolai draws on a wide varitety of coaching tools and models to best respond to the individuals needs. He is flexible and resourceful which enables a variety of transactional or transformational goals and personal development activities to be achieved. He does not believe that one-size fits all. Nicolai is not ‘fluffy’and has an ability to create an environment that encourages clients to focus on their goals rather than get caught up in the noise around a person or situation.

Nicolai is equipped with a combined Masters in Chemical Engineering/ Masters in Commerce and a French MBA.

He is the author of a bestselling book on management tools – www.consulting-handbook.com and is currently writing a book on leadership, coaching and EQ tools.

Originally a management and IT consultant, Nicolai is able to see both strategic and operational issues quickly and clearly, thereby avoiding unnecessary time wastage on getting him up to speed.

Nicolai has founded the Ignite Group and runs his own coaching practice (the Life Coaching Centre) in Cape Town since 2004.
He was involved in leadership development programs and organisational coaching including executive coaching, Peer Learning Groups, personal development and Emotional Intelligence.
