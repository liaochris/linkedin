Our value proposition includes
a) Monitoring brands on social media to gain key consumer insights and thereby drive engagement with the customers
b) Suggesting customer engagement strategies and technologies that would enable the brand to engage with the stakeholders
c) Driving Engagement Campaigns for specific product offerings on social media

Specifically my profile includes
a) Conducting Social Media Audits for brands/products/industries

e) Servicing of clients
