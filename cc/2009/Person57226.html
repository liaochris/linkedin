  • Co-Founder

    Patent Holder / Research Engineer. Next generation residential and commercial electrical delivery.   • Co-Founder

    POW-R Consortium, LLC

    (Computer Software industry)

    2000 — Present (9 years)

    Chairman at POW-R Consortium, the world's leading data reduction software for audio CD production. Currently licensed by Apple, Digidesign-Avid, Sonic, Cakewalk-Sonar, Sequoia-Samplitude, Merging-Pyramix, Ableton, etc.. POW-R processing now used on over 100 million audio CDs shipped annually.

    (Music industry)

World leader in premium microphone preamplifier technology - over 40,000 channels in use by today's top artists, studios, orchestras, labs, and film scoring stages. Incorporated 1994. Received Charter B-Corporation status 2008 (www.bcorporation.net).

  • Start Up
