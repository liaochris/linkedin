Barbara French’s Summary

Barbara French is an expert on influencer relations, including identifying top influencers and engaging with them by building mutually beneficial relationships, creating influencer programs and transitioning from internally driven to influencer-led marketing.

She is co-founder and managing editor of Tekrati, the world’s most popular source for news and directories on the high tech industry analysts. Tekrati websites serve more than a quarter million unique visitors each year.

In addition to her role at Tekrati, Barbara is a consultant at Influencer50, a services company specializing in influencer identification and engagement.

She also sits on the editorial advisory board of Business Trends Quarterly magazine. She is based near San Francisco.
