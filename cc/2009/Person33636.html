Ford Myers, Career Expert’s Summary

Ford's new book was recently published by John Wiley & Sons. It is entitled, "Get The Job You Want, Even When No One's Hiring." To order and access free bonuses, please visit: www.GetTheJobBook.com.

***********************************************************************

Ford R. Myers is President of Career Potential, LLC. He and his team work with individuals who are in career transition through one-on-one career coaching services. These programs help clients take charge of their careers, create the work they love, and earn what they deserve!

Career Potential also works with companies that are committed to attracting, developing and retaining premium talent, for lasting marketplace success.

Ford has worked at three of the nation’s largest career services firms. His articles and interviews have appeared in many national publications, including the Wall Street Journal, New York Times, Washington Post, US News & World Report, and Fortune. In addition, Ford has conducted presentations at numerous associations, companies, conferences and universities.

Ford has been a frequent guest on television and radio networks, such as NBC-TV, CBS-TV, Fox-TV, Comcast-TV, ABC Radio Network, National Public Radio, and Clear Channel Radio Network. He is also author of "The Ultimate Career Guide," a comprehensive manual for career management and job search.

Contact Ford via telephone at 1-888-967-5762.
