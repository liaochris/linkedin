Stephen Hodde’s Summary

Music and sound design for interactive entertainment and advertising, games, film, & television.

• Sound Design
Creation of unique sound effect assets: interface/HUD, foley, environmental ambience, gore, fighting and impact, weapons, creatures, underwater, and imaging effects.

• Music
Scoring to picture, orchestration, mixing, background/ambient loops, electronic textures, and adaptive music production.

• Sound Integration
Heavy collaboration with programmers for developing creative interactive audio implementation methods. Extensive quality assurance testing and audio-related issue resolution.
