Seth Goldstein’s Summary

Since 1998, I have designed Web sites for companies of varying sizes. Having used Macromedia Dreamweaver, Flash and Adobe Photoshop CS for my clients’ sites, I have a firm grasp of the programs and what they can do. I also have used (X)HTML, CSS. I am familiar with PHP, MySQL, Javascript and Open Source Software.

As the technology on the Internet continues to evolve, so do my goals for Goldstein Media. In addition to Web Design, we have decided to branch into Search Engine Optimization and Social Media Consulting. Though these are new forays into different avenues of Internet marketing, the goal for Goldstein Media is to provide a complete package for our clients, so that they can get the most out of their investment on the Internet.

Currently my Website and Blog rank #1 in both the Local search and organic results in Google for the key phrase: "Philadelphia Social Media Marketing" and #1 in Local and #4 in Organic for the key phrase: "Social Media Marketing Philadelphia."

I pride myself on the work that I produce and the contacts I make.
