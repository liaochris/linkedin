Sam Palazzolo, CPLP, PCC’s Experience

  • President & Chief Influence Officer

    Pathos Leadership Group

    (Management Consulting industry)

    March 2005 — Present (4 years 9 months)

    Sam is President and Chief Influence Officer at Pathos Leadership Group, where a client said that we're the home of the influential edge! We deliver this "influential edge" through our Business & Executive Coaching, Leadership Development programs and our Speaking engagements. In 2008, after interviewing 3,000+ business leaders, Sam had his first book published. Titled “The Influential Leader" it identified the 10 most critical skills that these leaders possessed which they perceived allowed them to succeed, and more importantly without them they would certainly fail.

    Sam has also authored "Influential Sales" (2009) which is a more in-depth look at one of the most important skills identified in "The Influential Leader" and "Communicate with Influence" (Due out 2010), identified as the foundation for all success! His leadership articles have also been published in 100+ magazines.

    Since 2005, he has been fortunate to serve clients such as Allstate, AT&T, Bayer Health Care, BMW, California Highway Patrol (CHiP’s!), Department of Defense, Federal Bureau of Investigation, FedEx, Fred Pryor / CareerTrack, Ford, GEICO, General Motors, IKEA, Lincoln National Life Insurance, Pepsi Bottling Company, the PGA, Toyota, United States Postal Service, Verizon, Chevron Energy Corporation, Isle of Capri Casino (I donated any fees back to them!) and UPS Supply Chain Solutions.
