Hans Wendland’s Summary

I am currently living my dreams at Six Red Marbles as the Creative Master of Emerging Technologies (also known as a Director of Digital Strategy).

In the last year I have had the pleasure of working at Monster Worldwide as the User Experience Director for the Employer division, and recently completed work on the Playyoo project at Imaginventure in Lugano Switzerland.

I have strong skills in multimedia design, and extensive experience in project management, methodology, development of multi-channel products and services including Web and Mobile programs, high-level client contact, team-building, and highest-quality delivery.

In my career I have focused on User Experience design, building user-centric interactive products and services for a variety of digital channels. I have built and led multi-disciplinary product development groups, and I have led projects from strategic workshop through design, development and deployment phases for web-based offerings to Fortune 500 companies. I contributed to building User Experience Groups for international consulting firms including Accenture and Cambridge Technology Partners both in the US and in Europe.

I started the Multimedia Program at Harvard University for the Extension School where I taught a Graduate level multimedia development course for several years, as well as delivered a number of professional multimedia summer programs.

My experience in project management includes specific expertise with projects requiring strong interactive design and multi-disciplinary development teams.

    Renowned User Experience Director joins Six Red Marbles' Research and Development Group

    August 24, 2009 - Cambridge, MA - Six Red Marbles announced today the addition of Hans Wendland as Director of Digital Strategy. Wendland will report to President Sarah White and be part of the R&D/Innovation team. The team's focus is on analyzing the educational marketplace and technological trends to seek out ways that potential solutions can create opportunities for Six Red Marbles and its clients.

    "An artist by spirit, an educator by nature and a technology guru by trade, Hans' creativity and insight into both the logic and art of technology go hand in hand with Six Red Marbles' philosophy," White said. "We are very excited to have him on board."

    "Six Red Marbles is exploring new and original methods and working with both old and new clients," CEO Jacques Driscoll said. "With Hans bringing his passion and modern thinking to those clients, the possibilities are limitless. He is fond of saying that his four drives are art, music, technology and the streets. I look forward to what those drives, combined with Hans' solid understanding of what our clients need, can accomplish."

    "The most successful digital products are usable, useful and engaging. In the end, the success or failure of any digital offering is determined by the end-users," Wendland said. "With Six Red Marbles' extensive experience and exclusive focus on education coupled with creativity and forward thinking, I know that I am working with the team that is going to make a difference."

    Hans can be contacted at h.wendland@sixredmarbles.com
