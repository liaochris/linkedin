Soren Detering’s Summary

Seasoned SAP Logistics Platinum consultant with 15 years experience in SAP implementation projects. Expert in SAP Sales, Service and Warranty Management processes.

Detering Consulting is SAP certified "Powered by Netweaver" for XI and Portals, has implemented several RFC, BAPI, IDOC, EDI, Rosettanet RNIF interfaces to SD, Service and Warranty, and is strong in Business Analytics.

Proven ability to work in a project team environment and to deliver according to customer’s requirements. Strong business consulting experience, delivered multiple full life cycle projects successfully. Management of large offshore developments.

Solid track record of delivering projects on time and under budget.
