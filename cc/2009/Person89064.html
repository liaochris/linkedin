Sam Beamond’s Summary

Sam is a proven marketing professional demonstrating bottom line achievements using both online and print solutions. Demonstrated tactical ability with web, print and interactive marketing concepts both internationally and domestically from concept to implementation. Ability to not only strategize, and organize marketing concepts across the board, but the ability to implement them hands on.

In the early stages of his career Sam worked in print design providing graphical direction for direct mail pieces. While a print/graphic designer at work, he was an avid web "student" by night... In 2001, he brought his web development skills into a professional environment and served as Online Designer for a Legal Education company.

While pursuing additional contracts (see www.beamondcreative.com), Sam currently serves as Marcom Manager at a Printing Equipment Manufacturer in the Twin Cities area. His role requires him to wear multiple "hats" daily, that draw on his continuing expertise in all aspects of marketing. Brochure development, web design, eNewsletters, online marketing and sales support materials could all be in a days work for Sam.

Today, my focus is marketing through web-based mediums and staying in step with fast developing internet technologies. More and more of my daily tasks are about leveraging the internet to increase sales, generate leads and build customer retention.
