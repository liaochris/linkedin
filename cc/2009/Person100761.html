Travis Egan’s Summary

The mortgage markets are in a shambles; nobody can get financed anymore. Does it help to have an experienced loan professional? 818 families have thought so over the past 15 years. I have helped them and funded just over 1600 loans during that time. I am driven to help you.

If you're a veteran, I strive to help you. As a veteran myself I am committed to seeing you achieve the dream of home ownership. You will have the option of a zero down payment loan and competitive rates. Combine that with a savvy real estate agent's negotiation skills and you might just get your dream home for VERY little money out of your pocket.

Thinking of buying your first home? I will use government-insured programs like FHA, USDA, or WHEDA to minimize the cost out of pocket and secure you a great rate at a payment we KNOW you can afford.

Strong analytical skills, combined with market savvy help you get the proper financing through conventional or portfolio loans.

REALTORs feel comfortable referring their best buyers to me because I get the job done. It’s not just that when I say a loan will close, we deliver, but my team stays in constant communication with you, your buyers, and the listing agent to ensure a smooth transaction. "No surprises" is a tough mantra in a mortgage market that surprises us weekly. I will deliver with my commitment to speed.

Call me at (262) 728-4203 to schedule a consultation.

I love this job - you will see it when you talk to me.
