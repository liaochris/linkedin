Simon Higgins’s Summary

Simon is an IT&T professional who has worked in in-house IT management roles as well as for leading vendors in the IT industry since 1990 in England, Europe, the US and Asia Pacific.

During this period, he has gained a strong skill set encompassing the deployment and management of virtualisation from the leading vendors – VMware, Citrix Systems and Microsoft.

In brief his skills encompass virtualisation, X86 management tools and application delivery solutions from leading vendors such as Citrix Systems, VMware and Microsoft covering the deployment and management of X86 desktops, application delivery and application virtualisation, virtualisation of X86 datacentre workloads and virtual desktop infrastructure to help customers reduce their capital, operating expenditure and complexity of their IT infrastructures.

Simon Higgins’s Specialties:

Virtualisation, Solution Selling, Product Sales /Solution Evangelism, desktop management technologies covering both traditional and virtualised desktops and competitive and market analysis.

Business Development across Government, Financial services, Commercial and Education verticals through technology thought leadership with both customers and business partners in the infrastructure space.
