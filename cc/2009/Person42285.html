Jackie Kuehl (Maluska)’s Summary

Marketing Executive (M.B.A.) who believes in working with integrity, enthusiasm, and respect for a client's unique goals. Passionate about marketing and passionate about helping grow successful businesses.

Kuehl Marketing assists clients in two ways: 1) Acting as the marketing function for companies without a marketing resource and 2) Acting as an additional marketing manager for companies with a marketing department, but short on resources.

As a marketing contractor and consultant Kuehl Marketing will achieve your objectives, overcome barriers and move your organization forward. We can make your marketing work for you.

The Kuehl Marketing approach begins with relevancy first. Relevancy to your objectives and relvancy with your customers. Jackie's approach provides smart new perspectives, a customer focus, marketing best practices, and simply "get's the job done". She ensures strategy comes before tactics to align marketing plans and programs which deliver results.

Our clients think of us as an extension of their company working in their best interest. We will only recommend and implement programs that are relevant to your business.
