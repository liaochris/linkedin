Tammy Munson’s Summary

Tammy Munson will proudly admit that she will never be the perfect military wife. It’s not in her blood and she thinks how boring it would be to be perfect all the time . Her life cannot be equaled to a Lifetime television show! She don’t have the cleanliest house on the post, but she tries her best. Her family is fed, happy and healthy. What more can you ask for right??

So who is Tammy??
She is a Conservative, Army wife ,has been married to her husband for 10 years, mama (to 2 kids and 2 dogs), blogger, writer, social media project manager and technology geek.

Tammy has been a work at home mom since 1998 and full time since 2001. She mostly worked as website designer, graphic designer and social media project manager but her true passion is the military life and supporting the “whole” military family. (service member, kids, spouse, etc)

Tammy is always involved in some project for the work at home mom, small business community or the military spouse. She has been organizing twice-per-year virtual expos for the work at home community since 2006. Additionally, you can find her blogging about her military life at Army Household6 as well as several other military spouse-focused blogs as an contributing blogger. e.

Additionally, she serves as the Colorado Installation Ambassador for Military.com, Technical Coordinator for ParentsZone.org, and Military channel Editor for BlissfullyDomestic.com

When not working on an online project, Tammy can be found working out on her Wii Fit, mountain biking around Colorado Springs, volunteering at the kids’ school or working with her husband’s unit at Fort Carson.

If you would like to hire Tammy, you can reach her at 719-313-1924 or email at tammy.munson@gmail.com
