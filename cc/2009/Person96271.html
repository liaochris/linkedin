Swamy Swarna’s Summary

After my graduation, I joined the 1-year Training Programme of Atomic Energy Establishment Trombay Training School, Bombay (now Mumbai). I joined the Testing and Inspection Section of Atomic Fuels Division, Bhabha Atomic Research Centre, Trombay, Bombay on 1st August 1967 and was engaged in testing and inspection of uranium ingots and rods, and wall thickness monitoring of admiralty brass tubes using ultrasonic resonance thickness testing. In March 1968, I was transfered to Coolant Tube Processing Facility of Rajasthan Atomic Power Project, Kota and was involved in the incoming inspection, process quality control and final inspection of Zircaloy 2 coolant tubes for the RAPP I and II reactors under construction.

In November 1971, I was transfered to Nuclear Fuel Complex, Hyderabad. I was involved in the testing and inspection of zircaloy tubes (fuel tubes, Calandria Tubes, Coolant Tubes etc.), stainless steel tubes for nuclear and chemical industries, Stainless Steel plates and pressure vessels, in-service inspection of pressure vessels etc. I was also involved in training and certification of personnel, documentation, quality surveillance, Third Party inspection, vendor evaluation and vendor development, manpower recruitment etc.
