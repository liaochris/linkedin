Jim HAYSOM’s Summary

9+ years of Sales / Business Development and Account Management experience within the online marketing and advertising industry, with prestigious client wins including national retailers and global brands.

(1999) I became a dotcom entrepreneur and set up an advertising company called Pub2Club which specialised in the bar and nightclub industry along the south coast of the UK.

(2002) My experience with new media took me to London where I worked for a leading search engine marketing and internet intelligence agency, Webgravity, which was later acquired by dealgroupmedia.

(2004) I was actively involved with the successful launch and growth of the world's largest comparison shopping search engine / price comparison website, Shopzilla (formerly BizRate.com) into the UK and Europe.

(2007) Shopzilla became the UK's 2nd largest comparison shopping website in March 2007 (comScore). Joined Ministry of Sound to head up the digital advertising sales for websites Ministryofsound.com and HedKandi.com.

(2008) Joined the advertising team at eBay UK to develop new strategic partnerships.
