Danny Nathan’s Summary

Danny is a visual storyteller, a weaver of words, and a brand initiator. His fascination with impressions and monikers, branding and strategy, as well as all forms of design, shaped a diverse education. After completing a B.A. with dual majors in marketing and acting at Washington University in St. Louis, Danny went on to earn his M.A. in advertising from the renowned Texas Creative Program at the University of Texas in Austin.

Bolstered by a strong marketing background and a keen eye for design, exposure to the theories of media-neutral advertising and creativity opened Danny’s eyes to possibilities beyond “traditional” branding. He immersed himself in the digital landscape, studying and participating in the current internet trends which are now synonymous with contemporary culture.

After serving as a founding writer, and later Editor, for Beyond Madison Avenue (ranked one of Ad Age’s top 70 media and marketing blogs) as well as contributing regularly to JoshSpear.com, a premiere trend-spotting and cool hunting blog, Danny launched the *AmongMany Network in June of 2008.

This growing collection of online media provides a place for like-minded people to gather and discuss the ever-evolving landscape of technology, creativity, and communications.
