Mark Berthelemy’s Summary

eLearning and Internet generalist with a primary & secondary teaching background. I have worked in the commercial learning & development sector since 2000: leading teams of online mentors, undertaking product development & management, consultancy & training on elearning software, and designing learning solutions to meet specific commercial and public sector needs.

Able to work closely with clients to understand their needs and drivers, and then to create realistic learning and communication solutions.
