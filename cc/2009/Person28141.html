Andres Armeda’s Summary

Andres Armeda is the Founder of Armeda Consulting and has over 14 years of military and private sector experience. He has held executive management roles as well as being an enterprise-level technical engineer.

Andres is an energetic and results-oriented internet consultant, creative designer, and executive with global business experience. Accomplished at both people and project management, with skills to develop and maintain a broad vision, Andres cultivates excellent relationships and communicates effectively across an organization to build focused and productive teams. Demonstrated ability to conceptualize and deliver complex technology solutions. Fluent in Spanish.
