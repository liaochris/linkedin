Jacob Høedt Larsen’s Summary

I believe that to be succesful you need to be visible and relate to your key stakeholders.
This is true whether you are a person or a business.

I love to help people and small businesses achieve this success through being visible
and distinct within their market place and relate to the right stakeholders
in a consistent and constant fashion.

I work with all types of stakeholders: Employees, the media, the public, investors, clients and
customers. The purpose is always the same: Tell the right story to the right people.

I carry this out in my daily job, I write about in books and blogs and I preach it in talks
and seminars.

My values: Giver's gain, passion

My passions: Connecting people, adventure, communication
