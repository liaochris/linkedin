Chris Maguire’s Summary

Starting off my career as an IT specialist offering 2nd and 3rd line support across the UK I soon moved from the actual computing side of things into the design and consultation of Telecommunication networks. Working all over the world I moved with my company at the time (Alcatel) to Virginia in the USA.

After living there for 5 years and after a few different jobs (due to redundancies), I moved back to the UK to follow my dreams and passions in life and to take my existing website Altsounds.com from just a music related website into, a website of great honesty and musical opinion with a fully fledged multimedia studio at it's core - screamadelica.co.uk. I would consider myself a multimedia specialist with high levels of skill in Music, Design, Film, Photography, Writing [creative and technical] and Art. I love all creative mediums and utilize them all at some point in any given week. I love the fact that one week I might be recording and producing an album, and the next I may be filming a music video, then doing a website and Myspace design and then an album cover. Don't let that fool you into thinking I am a jack of all trades and specialist of none. I specialize in each entity and provide a high level of creativity, originality, class and service in everything I do. If you need any form of creativity for your business, I am your man!

I am always up for consulting and freelancing my skills to others. I have managed to single handedly design, build and grow Altsounds.com over five years, to the site it is today with no monetary backing [but the help of my VP AJ]. The coding, the design, the SEO, the user experience - everything you see is done by me - apart from the content, which is done by the team with help from me! If you need anything whatsoever just let me know, I am willing to travel and willing to work all over the world.
