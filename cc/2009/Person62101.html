Marc Fonteijn’s Summary

I've have a strong background in new media, online and mobile. Currently applying my knowledge in a right-brain oriented environment where the focus lies on people instead of technology. I'm driven by the impact of technology on our society and the changes in social attitude that are associated in our continuously more connected world. I'm constantly trying to get a grasp on what the rules of our evolving society are.

We're moving to a world in which creativity becomes equally valuable as logic. Not only in business but also in the way we try solve social challenges. Design won't be a skill that it used to make cool things, it already is skill that will make the difference between success and failure.

I enjoy public speaking in business context as well as in educational settings. Especially on topics like design innovation, the power of co-creation and living in a connected society.

Life motto: "failure is when you stop trying"

Marc Fonteijn’s Specialties:

Service design, design driven innovation, new business development, concept creation, qualitative user research, strategic scenario development

  • Co-founder

    31Volts

    (Design industry)

    January 2007 — Present (2 years 7 months)

    31Volts is a Service Design & Innovation agency. We help companies to obtain a competitive advantage through the creation of better service (experiences). Our expertise lies in design driven innovation and user-centered value creation.

    We combine design-thinking, business strategy and new media expertise to co-create the next generation service concepts. Services that are driven by a deeper understanding in people and their lifes.

    We work in the private sector -insurance, transportation, FMCG- as well as the public sector -healthcare, local government, education-.
