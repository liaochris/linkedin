Jim Miller’s Summary

I wrapped up my thirty year career with the Tennessee Wildlife Resources Agency, and started a new part-time career with American Eagle Airlines in Chattanooga.

I have taken up golf in my retirement, and highly recommend it. I still enjoy fishing, birding, hiking, and camping, but being on the links with my buddies is just the best. We usually have 4 or 5 foursomes, and it just doesn't get any better than that!

If anyone needs to complete a foursome, give me a shout. If AA flies there, I can be there on short notice.

    American Eagle

    (Public Company; 5001-10,000 employees; AMR; Airlines/Aviation industry)

    November 2006 — Present (2 years 9 months)

    Work part-time at American Eagle Airlines in Chattanooga, TN as a station agent. A station agent does about everything but fly the plane. We check in passengers, handle their bags, marshal the plane, and of course audit the paperwork. I really enjoy the physical nature of the job, and we have a great team to work with in Chattanooga. We have many frequent fliers, and our passengers are just the best!
