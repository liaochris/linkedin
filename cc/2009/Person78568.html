Pete Machalek’s Summary

I am a consulting professional skill-builder helping individuals and groups present themselves with increased influential impact, whether they're speaking to one or a thousand people.

Drawing on our backgrounds as filmmakers, my partners and I work with professionals exactly the same way we work with actors, helping them 'win' in their real-life scenes.

Working with individuals and teams alike, we help our clients find the strategy, the approach and the language to inspire others to decisive action.

We offer training in the areas of Stage Presence and Story Structure to help professionals deliver more effective presentations, lead teams with clearer vision and greater inspiration, and generate stronger, more produtive relationships with prospects and clients.
