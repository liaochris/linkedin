Rod McKinnis’s Summary

From selling lemonade on the Southside of Chicago at the age of 7 to leading a multi-billion dollar sales force by the age of 30, I've had a passion for sales and helping others my entire life.

Sustainable high performance sales results is the riddle most orgnizations cannot seem to solve - we have...

As a former Global Fortune 400 Executive, my team and I grew a $40B business unit 50% in just 18 months. The experience was very rewarding and enabled us to start The McKinnis Consulting Group (TMCG - www.SalesisSimple.com) a Sales Growth consulting firm specializing in accelerating desired sales results.

We now partner with sales organizations throughout the globe to teach our proven, customizable sales system. Bottomline - we recruit, train and develop sales professionals to achieve the desired results of the organization using a proven process. We generate consistent sustainable outcomes of:

- Increased Revenue
- Reduced Costs
- Higher Shareholder Value
- Improve Customer and Employee Satisfaction

Our sales process is proven and easily deployed. Our client list ranges from INC 1000 to Fortune 500 companies, it also includes NASCAR, Toshiba and the US Government.

We aim to assist more sales teams with:
- Accelerated sales growth
- Sustainable sales results
- Improving the personal performance of its sales reps

My team and I have won numerous sales awards throughout my professional career yet my highest achievement is being married to my college sweetheart for 16 years and being a father of our two boys.

If your organization needs better sales results, quickly give us 20 minutes of your time. You'll be glad you did!
