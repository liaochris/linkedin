Eric-Paul Lecluse’s Summary

As an experienced freelance Flash developer/software engineer I'm specialized in ActionScript 3.0 for websites, web applications and web based games. I have a love for design, motion graphics and "the little things that matter". Throw in a strong enthusiasm for new developments in the web and Flash community (think agile project methods, 3D visuals, audio synthesis, etc) and you have a sense of what I'm about.

I'm available for contract work, check my website for contact details. The three languages I speak and write fluently are: Dutch, English and German. So feel free to connect!
