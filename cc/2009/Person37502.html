Greg Chokas, PMP’s Summary

Began career as a field engineer working with one of the first computer graphic systems designed by GE for NASA space shuttle simulations. Relocated to Europe to manage European divisions for two years. Held increasing responsible positions resulting in a program management position at Media 100.

The following years included program, project and product management positions for the delivery of new software products, service products, and alliance management. India project was part of a humanitarian effort for a non-profit organization.
