George Lenard’s Summary

23 years' experience concentrating exclusively on representation of employers in all aspects of labor and employment law.

Owner and editor of George's Employment Blawg, a popular blog presenting workplace news & views on subjects including labor & employment law and human resources since May 2003.

Legal researcher and writer for corporate clients of LRN, the Legal Knowledge Company since 2004.

Frequent speaker and writer on labor and employment law topics.

Seek additional opportunities to provide employers and other attorneys with advice, representation before agencies and courts, employment contract drafting and review, employee handbook and policy preparation and review, and legal research and writing. Open to alternative billing arrangements.

George Lenard’s Specialties:

Labor and employment law representing employers.

* All aspects of employment discrimination, including harassment and disability discrimination.

* Noncompete agreements and trade secrets.

* Labor law and arbitration under National Labor Relations Act.

* Legal research and writing.

* Workplace issues relating to blogging, social networking, and Web 2.0.
