Zahid H Javali’s Summary

I have been a journalist for the last 13 years and done just about everything under the publication sun. From working in a weekly tabloid to a monthly magazine to a fortnightly tourist guide to a quarterly investment journal to a daily newspaper, it's been one long joyride.

After being the City Editor of MiD DAY, a daily tabloid in Bangalore, I now run Write Wing Media that is into custom publishing coffeetable books, magazines, brochures, newsletters, and syndicating content.

That aside, I also freelance as editor/writer/photographer for publications in India, US and Singapore. I am Consulting Editor for Travel & Shop, a fortnightly tourist guide in Bangalore besides working for The Elite Post and Workforce Management, America's award-winning magazine read by CEOs and CFOs the world over. Not to mention, SilverKris, the inflight magazine of Singapore Airlines.

I also regularly contribute to an American portal on digital photography and computers besides being a regular movie columnist for the in-flight magazine of Paramount Airways besides being on the advisory board of a newly-launched magazine on the South Indian film industry. I also contribute technology/gadgets pieces to The Man magazine (Malayala Manorama Group) and Mint, the business newspaper of The Wall Street Journal.

That apart, I do CEO profiles of tech companies for LIVE IT, a tech magazine brought out by the Delhi Press Group (Caravan, Alive, Women's Era), and do some travel writing for India Today Travel Plus.

If you have some interesting projects, feel free to mail me at zahidjavali[at]gmail[dot]com.
