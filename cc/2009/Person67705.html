Michael Griffith’s Summary

All I want to do is work with cool people and build cool stuff.

Brands live in the minds of the beholder and are shaped through interaction. I build brand experiences. I translate, shape, and grow brand in the digital space.

I’ve been designing new media for 19 years but still suffer night terrors spawned from the 16 color VGA palette. Being digital since 1990, my understanding of Information architecture, usability, acessability, and compliance sets me apart from traditional creative directors. I help clients understand how a digital brand strategy can extend their brand experience.

When not actively destroying traditional television and print advertising, I teach others how to do it. I have taught both the design and development of new media at the college level and continue to speak at professional organizations and conferences.

My favorite color is the very web unsafe DFE9D0.

Michael Griffith’s Specialties:

Industry experience includes Consumer Package Goods, Travel & Leisure, Telecom, Financial, OTC Pharma and B2B.
Media experience includes sites, micro sites, social media, digital media, E-mail, computer based training, marketing CDs, kiosks, trade show media, hand held devices and apps.
