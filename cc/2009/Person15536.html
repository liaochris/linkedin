Carlos Richer cricher@richernet.com’s Summary

About Carlos Richer

After 21 years with Absormex, where he used to be General Director, Mr. Richer retired in 2005 to start his own company dedicated to provide consulting services to the disposable diaper industry. He is 51 years old and an expert in the field of manufacturing disposable absorbent products, with 25 years of experience. On his Website you will find useful links related to the disposable diaper industry, as well as links to websites of most of the manufacturers in the world.


Mr. Richer's skills

Mr. Richer is very familiar with diaper requirements, production equipment selection, intellectual property issues and improving diaper performance. He is also an expert at analyzing products and patents to find weak and strong points and make recommendations for changes in the product design or to avoid infringement. Mr. Richer has experience with diaper factory economic feasibility studies and new plant layouts. He has also helped insurance companies and financial institutions with machine appraisals and with damaged warehouses claims. He has helped several independent inventors to write better patents and also helped them find potential clients. He speaks fluent Spanish and English, he also has working knowledge of Portuguese and Italian.
