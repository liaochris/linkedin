Bert DuMars’s Summary

Results-charged, 20+ year career is distinguished by achievements in driving high-impact E-Commerce marketing initiatives that have generated outstanding returns. Rapidly analyze, study and understand opportunities, competitive landscapes, and industry ecosystems, and quickly visualize the big picture, execute decisions, and move initiatives forward. Career highlights:

• Increased functionality, customer access, sales and transactions of two major web sites by orchestrating web site redesigns.
• Generated $10 million in revenues (2003) with the successful launch of 19 global web stores.
• Drove $25+ million in incremental annual revenues with the development of new technical support/channel services.
• Spearheaded an innovative start-up web site that set the standard for gourmet coffee and food products.
• Developed the www.trendmicro.com site, which was ranked as one of the Top 100 Classic Sites by PC Magazine in February 2002 and March 2003.
• Grew FY01 $40 million run rate by an additional $10 million with the development of 70 new managed collocation services (SAN, NAS, Backup), including data storage and managed application services.
• Interviewed 100+ times by the media including the Wall Street Journal, Associated Press, USA Today, and NPR.
