Michel Kestemont’s Summary

Michel Kestemont, a Belgian, who has more than 25 years of experience in the music business in Brazil, the USA and Europe (Brussels and Paris).

He graduated from Belmont University and has worked in three continents (North America, Europe, and South America). He has worked for both record and music publishing companies and held senior positions with Polygram, BMG and EMI.

Michel is always looking for new challenges in any field. His most important strength is to be able to learn fast and understand how to solve problems wether it is by an hand on attitude or lead the specialist(s) needed.

The diverse positions in different cultures and work conditions forced Michel to have a strong ability to adapt and be fast in surrounding himself with the right people.

Michel Kestemont’s Specialties:

Music Publishing, Copyright Law, Royalty Auditing from any source, Royalty Administration and set up, Marketing of music, Acquisition of catalogues, contract negotiation in the music industry.
