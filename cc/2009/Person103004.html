Vincent Tucker’s Summary

“As the next American Feel Good Story in the making”, Vincent “Young Heartbreak” Tucker can be described in three words: Charismatic, Focused and Ambitious. After one Google search, you’ll quickly agree that Mr. Tucker is one of the up-and-coming forces in the entertainment business today. In addition to being a rising music & entertainment executive, Vincent is a promoter, event host, radio personality, entrepreneur & philanthropist.

The real-life story of Vincent “Young Heartbreak” Tucker is a heartwarming and inspirational one, making him a unique role model for at-risk youth everywhere. ‘Coming from the bottom’, Mr. Tucker’s story includes surviving a deadly house fire that claimed the lives of three (Grandparents & 7 year old sister), premature deaths of childhood friends, dealing with his parents’ drug abuse plus surviving abandonment at the age of 13. Instead of turning to the streets, Mr. Tucker has been using those dark moments in his life to fuel his rise to the top of the music and entertainment industry.

Now at the age of 25, the founder of CWC Entertainment Group, L.L.C., oversees one of the south’s emerging urban music entertainment companies, encompassing a broad range of businesses including recording, artist management, public relations, event promotions and cd duplication. He’s currently the host of online syndicated program, The Vincent Tucker Radio Show. Mr. Tucker recently completed a non-partisan voter registration campaign entitled Register To Speak Out!™, created to register 100,000 new young voters for the 2008 United States Presidential election.
