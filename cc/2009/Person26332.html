Daniel González Gasull’s Summary

Daniel studied Electrical and Computer Engeneering at the University of Seville in Spain. He developed web and mobile applications using J2EE MVC frameworks, SQL and XML for several years in Seville and Barcelona. He joined a 100-person team for Accenture Technology Solutions' Dublin office working on a large Java project.

Daniel recently moved to Los Angeles, where he started agile development with Python after realizing its substantial benefits over Java — less code, less bugs and less development time.

Daniel founded DjanSoft in 2008. Daniel is fluent in English, Spanish and Catalan.
