Michael Tepp’s Summary

I have a degree in computer science, am a certified IT project manager, Sun certified Java programmer, and certified Scrum master. I have more than 15 years of experience in the development of electronic trading systems and automated trading applications. I have worked on software projects for basket trading, single stock trading, derivatives, portfolio management, PNL calculations, index arbitrage, and more.
