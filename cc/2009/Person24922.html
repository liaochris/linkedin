Dawn Nocera’s Summary

Personal & Professional Development Training, Coaching, Speaking

Why do you need a LifeCoach to speak at your event or to conduct training with your team?

Imagine this...
A year from now, you are at the same place you are today. Same results, same attitude, same ol' same ol'...and getting a little old.

What if you could...
* Hire one of the best life coaches around to teach your team how to use their most valualbe asset... their minds!

* Motivate your team to elliminate the unneccessary activity in their life and focus on the one thing that will make a difference in their results.

* Inspire your best people to achieve more than ever before!

* Educate your team as to how to build relationships with ease.

Now, imagine...
A year from now, your team outdoing last year's numbers and creating an environment that attracts and retains others who are the best in their field, creates an endless stream of referrals, and is having the time of their lives!

Allow me to be your *Secret* Weapon to Success!

The seminars and corporate training that I provide through LifeSuccess Consulting blend the timeless personal development and success teachings of Andrew Carnegie, Napoleon Hill, Earl Nightingale, James Allen, and the like, with the modern day teachings of Bob Proctor, Wayne Dyer and John Maxwell.

What are some of the signs you need a personal life coach?

* You're a rock star in your industry and you have lost your mojo!
* Your life looks great on the outside, but on the inside something is missing.
* You seem to have hit a ceiling in our career, relationships, wealth, or health.
* There is a big gap between where you are and where you want to be.
* You've been thinking about it for a while and now is the perfect time to invest in yourself!

Life is good! Shouldn't it be for you and your team as well!

P.S. Don't buy the hype. Refuse to participate in any recession.
