Damon Hart-Davis’s Summary

Experienced in engineering mission-critical systems in many areas including investment/merchant and retail/Internet banking.
Also a keen provider of "pro bono" services such as the Gallery (http://gallery.hd.org/) with many millions of unique visitors, NTP, etc, etc.
My aim is always to create reliable and usable systems with fun technology!
I have a BSc(Hons) in AI&CS and an MSc in CS from Edinburgh University and am brewing up to starting a part-time PhD...

Java "Expert" in Sun's Java Community Process in a couple of areas.
