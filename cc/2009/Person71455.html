    Career Transition Support Group

    (Consumer Services industry)

    January 1989 — Present (21 years )

    The Career Transition Support Group (CTSG), founded in 1989, provides career change guidance and support to those finding themselves between jobs, or to those contemplating making a career change. The schedule of instruction is a 10-12 week repeating cycle of key topics designed to give attendees a good overview of the career change process.

    No matter where you enter the schedule, you may complete the program simply by continuing to attend until you have completed all topics. You are free to pick and choose from topics presented, but we recommend you try to attend them all for maximum benefit to you.

    All those who provide support are unpaid volunteers who receive no remuneration whatsoever. There is no cost whatsoever to you to be a part of the group.

    Meetings are held at 7:00 pm Mondays, subject to variances caused by holidays. A schedule is published prior to each quarter.
