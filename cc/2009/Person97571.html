Terry Rondberg’s Summary

The president of the World Chiropractic Alliance, Dr. Terry Rondberg is widely regarded as one of the chiropractic profession’s leading figures. For decades now, he has shown unparalleled commitment to the chiropractic profession, bringing exceptional fervor and youthful vigor to his many endeavors and contributions.
After receiving his Doctor of Chiropractic (DC) from Logan College of Chiropractic, Dr. Rondberg began publishing The Chiropractic Journal, responding to the needs and demands of the time. What was missing from the current selection of periodicals was a professionally edited source for news and features for chiropractic practitioners and professionals. The Chiropractic Journal stepped in to fill that void. At the time of its inception, in 1986, The Chiropractic Journal was the first of its kind: The first publication to openly advocate subluxation—based chiropractic—while simultaneously maintaining high standards of journalism. The Journal employed experienced journalists to offer valuable, thought-provoking articles in the wellness field. Decades later, Dr. Terry Rondberg continues to publish the Journal. Its mission has not changed since its inception. The Journal continues to exist as a source of accurate and up-to-date reporting on current issues and events, an open forum for opinions from chiropractic practitioners and professionals, and an advocate for sublaxion-based chiropractic and chiropractors, while adhering to strict standards of journalistic integrity.
In addition to publishing the Journal—which continues to be an authoritative reference for chiropractic practitioners and professionals —Dr. Terry Rondberg has written a number of best-selling books on the subject of chiropractic. Years of experience have made Dr. Terry Rondberg a highly coveted speaker at chiropractic events, panels, and discussions. He has even made appearances on television shows and radio programs.
