Chris Hyzy’s Summary

My experience in the Real Estate Industry started several years ago as I began working as a Leasing Agent for an Apartment Community in Dallas. As time went on, I was promoted to a Marketing Director, overseeing the DFW Metroplex, and onto running several Apartment Communities at once.

I moved away from the Apartment Industry to focus on my Real Estate Career. My focus is now on Real Estate Property Management, while working with Foreclosure Investments, and Rental Properties.

My goal has always been to provide all of my Clients, Investors and Owners with the most professional service available. I strive for nothing but the best, and my work shows for that. No one is ever treated as "just a number" in my book, each person has their own unique needs that are addressed and listened to by someone who truly has a vested interest.

I oversee Residential Property Management, Rental Listings, Apartment Locating, Foreclosure/Investments, Multi-Family and much more!
