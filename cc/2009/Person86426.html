Ross Clevens, MD’s Interests:

Ross A. Clevens, MD, FACS is a Board Certified Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgeon. Dr. Clevens completed his undergraduate training at Yale University, medical school/internship at Harvard Medical School and residency/fellowship at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. Our practice specializes in facial plastic surgery including facelift, rhinoplasty, eyelid surgery, laser surgery, skin resurfacing procedures and skin cancer reconstructive surgery including Mohs surgery.
