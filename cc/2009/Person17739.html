Chris Crafford’s Summary

I am a successful technologist and executive with over 29 years of software architecture, development, and executive management experience, adding value while delivering. I have successfully led the design and delivery of multiple enterprise class applications as well as SAAS services working in several startups and corporate web services. I excel at leading the architecture of products as well as evangelizing a concept or product in the corporation, with partners, customers and the market in general. Very experienced setting up and managing offshore teams and Agile SDLC methodologies. I am able to work with a broad range of technologies including SOA, applying EA and am very familiar with business issues in print, compliance, email archiving, grid computing, security, cryptography, e-commerce, multi-media, search and large scale database implementation. Chris is a co-founder of Austin Inventors and Entrepreneurs Association.
