David Smith’s Summary

A combined career over thirty four years, seventeen years in City based Merchant Banks and over seventeen in the Military, has provided a highly developed, professional working knowledge of the Telecommunications environment, Local and Wide Area Networking.

Experience has been gained operating within strict institutional guidelines as well as in field, workshop and teaching environments, both within the UK and abroad. Initially trained through a formal apprenticeship, subsequently developing specialist expertise.

Responsibilities have encompassed all aspects relating to the management, planning, maintenance and repair and installation of communications centres and networks, both local and wide area, employing an extensive range of sophisticated systems, equipment and software.

Also responsible for ensuring availability of spares and materials, and maintaining effective liaison at all levels.
This experience has cultivated strong management, communicative and organisational skills.

These attributes coupled with extensive hands on technical knowledge are particularly suited to the leadership and management of any operational Network team.
