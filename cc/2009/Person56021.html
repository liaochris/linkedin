Klokie Grossfeld’s Summary

Daniel “Klokie” Grossfeld is an entrepreneur, senior developer and information architect with 13 years of professional experience engineering complex database-driven websites, e-commerce, content management systems, and standards-based user interfaces for premier international clients. Comfortable developing early-stage start-ups, Klokie is a founding partner of four successful international enterprises within the past decade. With experience managing agile development teams in New York, London, Paris, and Barcelona, Klokie is currently based in Stockholm, Sweden.
