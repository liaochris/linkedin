Brad Wellman’s Summary

I have substantial experience in social media monitoring, strategy development and analysis having worked with clients such as UnitedHealth Group, Nestle Nutrition, Smiths Medical, Halo Innovations, and Vote Yes Minnesota to name a few. I played a crucial role in the passing of the Clean Water, Land and Legacy Amendment during the 2008 election by managing Vote Yes Minnesota’s social media campaign, and my attention to detail has ensured meeting various project deadlines, time and time again. I also manage Spyder Trap’s social media operations as a primary contributor to the agency’s blog, and as the lead person behind their presence in social media environments.

I am a Strategic Communications major from the School of Journalism and Mass Communication at the University of Minnesota – Twin Cities, and a member of the Minnesota Interactive Marketing Association (MIMA). In my free time, I enjoy playing and officiating ice hockey, golfing, snowboarding, and blogging.
