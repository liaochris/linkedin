Barb Dybwad’s Summary

Barb is the Director of Production at Crowd Fusion, a new web publishing company solving the pain points of publishers at scale, where she has a hand in editorial direction and brand building as well as database and platform development. She is also the co-Editor of Obsessable.com, the first Crowd Fusion site, covering personal technology for gadget enthusiasts and non-experts alike.

Formerly, Barb was the Producer of Engadget and Joystiq networks at Weblogs, Inc., an AOL company. She founded the popular World of Warcraft blog WoW Insider, MMO destination Massively.com, and the PC news and downloads site BigDownload.com. She is also the former Editor-in-chief of the Social Software Weblog, Senior Editor of Engadget, and has spoken and written on multiple topics in the fields of social media, technology, gaming and virtual worlds.
