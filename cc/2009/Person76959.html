Pat Holkesvig’s Summary

Homes In Stockton, Inc. is a state-of-the-art, full-service real estate marketing company specializing in REOs and loss mitigation in California's Central Valley. The broker is Werner Properties Inc. For over 25 years Werners has filled the void between the burdensome corporate real estate office and the too-small-for-full-service independent broker.

We have earned a reputation for excellence and are committed to providing exceptional service. We are your eyes and ears in our market. Our dedicated real estate professionals are self-motivated, highly organized, team players that utilize the latest in technology to maximize effectiveness. Our job is to sell your assets in the shortest period of time for the highest possible price. Highlights of our administrative background includes:

• Completed Default School's REO Best Practices and Advanced Evaluations courses
• List REOs for Countrywide, Wells Fargo, IndyMac and Old Republic Default Services
• Set-up on REOTRANS, RES.NET, REOWORLD, REALTrans, AssignREO and many others
• Committed to maintain homes which help preserve neighborhoods
• Right-on BPOs
• Obtain top dollar offers
• Relationships with tradesman for rekeying, cleanup, construction & landscaping
• Financial wherewithal to cover monthly REO expenses
• Utilization of technology to maximize efficiency and response times
• Structured paperless transaction coordination including electronic signatures
• Staff proficiency in Microsoft Word, Excel and PowerPoint
