Philip Nealey’s Summary

Accomplished entrepreneurial professional with experience in all aspects of running a business including: management, marketing and sales, financial and project management skills. Experience working with business professionals in all levels of management. Possesses an exceptional ability to perceive synergies between people, technologies and companies.
