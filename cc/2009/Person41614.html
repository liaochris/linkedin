Suzee Ebeling PCC, PMC’s Summary

A current project involves supporting coaches to pass ICF and/or Coach U certification on their first try. I work with brand new coaches who are just getting into the field as well as "old school" coaches who remember Thomas Leonard and took their training before the core competencies were included in the curriculum.

Services for coaches include one-to-one mentor coaching, an ongoing Core Competency Mentoring Group (for certification or CCEU's) and a Client Success Group I host the Group Coaching SIG (a special interest group open to the public) The SIG meets by teleconference on the 1st Thursday of every month at 3pm eastern. To receive monthly Group SIG announcements, send a blank email to: Group_Coaching_SIG-On@news.webvalence.com
I am also an ACC assessor for the ICF and have formed a Coach Credentialing SIG which meets the 1st Friday of the Month at 12 Noon Eastern. You can sign up here: http://groups.yahoo.com/group/CoachCredentialingSIG

Services for "civilians" (folks who aren't involved in the coaching industry} include individual and group life coaching for highly intuitive individuals who have a big vision of what is possible for people and the world.
