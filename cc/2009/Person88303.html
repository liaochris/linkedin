Sanders McConnell’s Summary

A graduate of Presbyterian College in South Carolina, Sanders earned a Bachelor of Science degree in business. He is currently President of My HSA Rewards, a loyalty company that provides cash rewards to individuals with Health Savings Accounts. He is also owner of Simple2communicate, an audio and web Conferencing Company in Atlanta. Sanders’ background includes experience with ADP TotalSource, a Fortune 500 company as well as other entrepreneurial endeavors. He became involved in the healthcare industry when he started and grew a medical equipment and supply company’s sales territory in Atlanta. He has further experience with healthcare- related issues, having worked in the employee leasing industry where his responsibilities included helping small companies with employee benefits. He also launched a corporate wellness business with two partners. With his work experience in healthcare and benefits as a backdrop, Sanders is familiar with the issues that business owners face when tackling the health insurance needs of their employees. Sanders has acquired the invaluable, hands-on knowledge of what it takes to run a small business. In addition, Sanders has the expertise in Health Savings Accounts that will provide the necessary tools to help make My HSA Rewards successful. Sanders has been a committee member of the American Heart Association Golf Tournament that has been held annually in Atlanta for the past five years.
