Stuart Migdon’s Summary

In addition to publishing my first book, "Jesus Take the Wheel: 7 Keys to a Transformed Life With God," I am a successful Christian businessman and currently serving as elder and biblical counselor at Calvary Chapel Old Bridge in New Jersey. My growing understanding of my own need to let Jesus take the wheel of my life has equipped me for the many situations I face in ministry today. In every area, I challenge those I encounter to begin living a transformed life by letting Jesus have His rightful place behind the wheel of their lives. In my book, readers are given an easy and practical plan to follow each day that will teach them what they need to do in order to begin letting Jesus be the One in the driver's seat. Please visit my website for more information!
