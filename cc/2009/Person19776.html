Christopher Jones’s Summary

Christopher M. Jones is the Principal of Keystone Financial Planning, a Registered Investment Advisor in Pennsylvania. Mr. Jones provides comprehensive, fee-only financial planning and asset management services to clients nationwide.

Prior to starting the firm, Mr. Jones worked as a financial planner for two other fee-only financial planning and asset management firms. Before entering the financial planning field, Mr. Jones was a management consultant for Monitor Company, a top-tier strategy consulting firm with global headquarters in Cambridge, MA.

Mr. Jones is a CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER practitioner. He completed the CFP Professional Education Program from the College of Financial Planning. Mr. Jones graduated (summa cum laude) from Brigham Young University with a B.S. in Economics and a minor in Business Management.

Christopher Jones’s Specialties:

asset management, fee-only financial planning, goal setting, cash flow planning, tax planning, education funding, retirement planning, insurance needs analysis, employment contract negotations for non-profit and for-profit executives
