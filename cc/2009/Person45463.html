Jeff Morris’s Summary

I started building and repairing PCs in 1991. Since then, I've worked for seven companies in PC repair/support, or software development. I've amassed extensive debugging and troubleshooting experience, which I've used to support users, develop solutions, and train individuals and groups.

The goals of this company are to provide the residential and BtoB market with affordable, dependable PC support by a trained professional who will stand behind his work, and to help people understand how to better protect themselves from the common pitfalls of computer use.
