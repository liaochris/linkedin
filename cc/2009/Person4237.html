Russ Alman’s Summary

* 15 years of experience as a Macintosh computer support specialist in Eugene, Oregon; Sarasota, Florida; and Olympia, Washington. My specialty is implementing customer management and marketing systems for small businesses using Macintosh hardware and software, featuring Marketcircle Daylite (www.marketcircle.com). I also have previous experience building databases using ProVue Panorama and Filemaker Pro. I am a certified member of the Apple Consultants Network, and a certified trainer for Marketcircle Daylite.

* College-level teaching experience in mass communications, business information systems and technology, and Macintosh applications, marketing systems and operating systems.

• Extensive entrepreneurial experience:

- In college, to help pay for expenses, offered professional word processing services
- Started computer support business soon after finishing graduate school.
- Developed a unique marketing product called Keep$ake Cards, a line of high-quality, die-cut laminated business cards.
- Founded the South Sound Barter Alliance, an independent barter exchange organization in the South Puget Sound region.
- Became an independent distributor for SendOutCards greeting card service (www.givegreetingcards.com)

My goal is the continued professional transition from providing Macintosh computer support to offering unique, referral and relationship marketing tools for small businesses, independent sales professionals, and entrepreneurs. By leveraging my combination of technology experience, teaching and training experience, and communications and marketing skills, I can help these types of professionals develop powerful referral networks to grow their business and customer base without heavy reliance on expensive mass marketing.
