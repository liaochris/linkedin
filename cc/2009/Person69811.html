Mohammed Alzubi’s Summary

Mohammed Alzubi is a director at the technology investment group of Dubai Silicon Oasis Authority (DSOA), previously Mohammed was a managing partner at Arab Seed Ventures, a venture capital fund focusing on seed stage investments in the Arab region & Silicon Valley. Mohammed was also the founder and CEO of Sulaf Solutions, an IT solutions provider based in Silicon Valley. Mohammed has over 13 years of experience in the network infrastructure and Internet spaces and has worked for a wide range of customers, including Cisco System, Wilson Sonsini, Sun Microsystems, American Express, Excite@Home, and has lived through the Internet boom and bust.His engagements spanned four continents, and six countries.
Mohammed is an Angel investor, an advisor to VCs on business and technical matters, and an advisor to many entrepreneurs.

Mohammed is a member of Sand Hill Angels -an Angel investment group based in Silicon Valley, an Advisory Board member of LinkSV, Advisory Board member of kyube.com, a founding Board member of Techwadi, an advisor with the non-profit Pacific Community Venture, and a member of the Gerson Lehrman Group Tech, Media & Telecom Council. Mohammed has a BS in Electrical Engineering from Brigham Young University and holds various technical certifications including CCIE, MCSE, CCSP, and MCDBA
