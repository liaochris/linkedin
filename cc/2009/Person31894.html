Esther Lim’s Summary

Over twelve years experience in developing and delivering successful web strategies, integrated marketing, interactive advertising, and branding campaigns incorporating emerging, new and social media. Esther focuses on understanding client industries, competitive landscapes, and interactive behaviors to develop engagement strategies that improve lead-generating, awareness building and audience retention activities.

Prior to starting her own company, Esther worked as a principal at Crimson Consulting's Interactive Practice where she conceived, developed and executed on Social and New Media entertainment based experiential marketing programs for F500-1000 companies such as Cisco, Sun, Seagate, and Fujitsu.

Esther was also VP of Client Services and Strategy for e-Storm International, an international marketing agency where she was in charge of creating advertising strategies for clients as well as building out new product offerings, work flow processes, organization and infrastructure. Previous clients include: ABC, Capital One Health Care Finance, Disney & Co., McKesson, Bank of America, Union Bank of California, Hitachi, Reuters, RCA, Eastman Kodak, NBC, Verio/NTT Communications, Verizon, Puma and American Suzuki Motor Corporation.

Esther holds a Bachelor's Degree in French Literature from San Francisco State University, graduating magna cum laude.
