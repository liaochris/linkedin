Labor Relations
Successfully spearheaded several collective bargaining negotiations. Oversaw relations with six collective bargaining units. Built close relationships with the bargaining units without failing to meet the needs and goals of management. Administered grievance and arbitration procedures for over 3,500 employees. Reduced turn-over by 60%. Reduced workers compensation claims by more than $1,000,000 a year.

Analysis and Execution
Exceptional abilities to identify problem areas in communication, policy administration and employee relations. Through careful analysis many points of friction can be addressed and relations issues can be avoided. Finding what the issues are is only the beginning, without insightful implementation the problem is only addressed for the moment, not prevented in the future. The most obvious cause is, often, only a symptom.

Team Building through Diversity
Bringing people of diverse backgrounds together builds a team which performs better due to increased collective insight. Often, all diversity means to a company is that they need to avoid showing discrimination. By developing relationships with recruiters, employment agencies and universities in different geographic locations, we enlarge the pool of applicants and, by extension, the diversity therein. A diverse workforce builds a better workplace and a better world.
