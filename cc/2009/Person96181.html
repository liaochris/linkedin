Suzanne Yada’s Summary

* I am a social media strategist and steering committee member at The Public Press, a nonprofit noncommercial news outlet in San Francisco. I help in many aspects of the organization, including devising a plan for social media integration, helping with business and strategic planning, blogging, and designing marketing and fundraising materials.

* I have assisted the Society of Professional Journalists NorCal chapter in putting on events, designing marketing materials and using social media to gather comments from the public.

* I am a copy editor for SHiFT, a San Jose State University-based magazine distributed to social entrepreneurs in the Silicon Valley.

* I was the managing editor for the Global Media Initiative, a group of 20 SJSU students who traveled to London and Paris in March 2009 to gain international reporting experience.

* I blog for San Jose Metblogs, a network of hyperlocal blogs.

* I am an active Twitterer and have been named one of the top 10 journalists on Twitter by prominent blogger Mark Luckie at 10,000 Words.

* I have worked as a copy editor and designer for three years at the Visalia Times-Delta, a daily newspaper in Central California. That job also included uploading to the Web, managing online comments and editing a videocast for about two months.

* I was editor-in-chief of The Campus, the weekly newspaper for College of the Sequoias, as well as The Truth Xposed, a Visalia-based youth magazine dedicated to giving at-risk youth a creative outlet.

* I was a volunteer for a nonprofit low-power radio station in Visalia and hosted, scripted and edited my own radio show.

* I was a proofreader and pre-press operator for three years at a print shop in Visalia.
