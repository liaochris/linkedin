Brian Sooy’s Summary

25+ years in graphic design and visual communications.
Principal Designer: Altered Ego Fonts

Sooy + Co. is a branding and visual communications firm that creates brands that inspire. We enable business, education and nonprofit organizations to grow, increase market share, build community and raise awareness.

Our clients succeed by differentiating their brand in their audience’s minds and hearts. We empower our clients to define their uniqueness through positioning, strategy, design and marketing that connects with people in print, social, interactive and web media.
