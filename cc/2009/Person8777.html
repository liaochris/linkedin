Autumn Conley’s Summary

My goal as a professional copyeditor is simply to use my writing, editing, proofreading, and administrative skills to help you streamline your book manuscript, academic paper, fiction or nonfiction, newsletter, sales materials, or web text – whatever you have written, I would love to read it! I can use my experience in writing and with grammar and style to make your ideas shine for your readers and help you say what you want to say in a creative, organized way that maintains your style and tone while eliminating common errors that detract from your work. No matter what you’ve written or how much work it needs, I can help you polish that manuscript of any length in any genre quickly, conveniently, inexpensively, and with a personal touch!

As a freelance writer and book reviewer who has been published often over the past few decades, I am also eager to take on writing assignments in a variety of fields and genres, or to provide written book reviews for other writers. If you need something written, let me know!
