Tim Frick’s Summary

I am the owner of a Chicago-based media design firm with extensive experience on projects from small to large for clients ranging in size from Fortune 100 companies to one-person startups. Design and user experience drive all that we do at Mightybytes, be it a quick and simple one-day turnaround design comp or a complex, year-long interactive educational software title or video production project. I have also authored an interactive process guide titled 'Managing Interactive Media Projects' from Delmar Cengage Learning and regularly present at conferences, seminars and workshops on various aspects of design and media production.

Tim Frick’s Specialties:

Mightybytes creates rich media experiences using design, motion, video, sound, music, animation and interactivity to tell our clients' stories and brand their products.
