Isabelle Camps-Campins’s Summary

An articulate, dedicated, youthful architect with a solid ten years professional experience in Architecture, both in office management and site environments. Bilingual in French and English, with a solid comprehension of Spanish, welcomes the opportunity to be a dependable, resourceful and dynamic member of any team. Adept in overcoming design or site hurdles, with an ability to deliver effective, innovative and lateral-thinking solutions, within specified deadlines. Has an astute, personable good-humored disposition, performing well in often highly demanding situations.

Isabelle Camps-Campins’s Specialties:

Strong in clear visual and graphic communication, with a skillful ability to use modern drawing software packages, which compliments a wide range of traditional draughting skills. Has had extensive experience dealing with Clients, Quantity Surveyors, Contractors, Sub-Conractors and Suppliers, Landscapists and Interior Designers, with a sound understanding of running a small architectural firm.
