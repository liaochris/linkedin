Leonardo Falaschini’s Summary

Leonardo Falaschini started producing Visual Arts for games in 1995, before being a gradute of Escuela Superior Martín Malharro of Visual Arts in Mar del Plata, Argentina, as Professional Illustrator. He then produced visual art for 2D flash webgames and later 3D assets for real-time 3D webgames and participated developing social networks strategies. He served as Lead Artist on several proyects and started to perform as Scrum Master and Game Designer. He's also a part time comic artist and writer.

Leonardo Falaschini’s Specialties:

Leading Art Production Process, Concept Art, 2D vector, 2D Prerendered and realtime 3D graphics, Illustration
