Joshua Slavitt’s Summary

My practice covers all aspects of intellectual property law including:

-Counseling start-up and established companies in the development and implementation of intellectual property strategies

-Preparing and prosecuting patent and trademark applications

-Conducting IP due diligence reviews for venture capital and private equity investors

-Providing patentability, validity, and freedom to operate opinions

-Litigating IP infringment disputes at the trial and appellate levels.

Joshua Slavitt’s Specialties:

Intellectual property strategy development and implementation; patent and trademark prosecution; IP due diligence and opinions; and intellectual property litigation
