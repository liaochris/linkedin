Alan Underkofler’s Summary

Why I’m on LinkedIn:

• To network with other professionals so I can offer the best referrals to my clients who need services and products I do not provide.

• To attract clients who will benefit from what I have to offer.

To that end I offer the following:

• I provide consulting services to help sales professionals and companies with their Customer Relationship Management needs.

• I help review current keep-in-touch and Relationship Marketing systems.

• I show clients how to implement simple tools to increase referrals, increase sales, and retain more clients and customers

What I’m looking for on LinkedIn:

• I’m looking for partners to distribute my products and services – I can add value to their product offering and expand their reach with their clients.

• I’m looking to consult with Law Firms, Insurance Agencies, CPA Firms, Financial Planning firms, etc., who have a book of business and are looking to leverage those relationships into profitable streams of referral business.
