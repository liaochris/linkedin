Harry Tucker’s Summary

An award winning project manager and technology leader, including project manager / technology team lead for the Bank of New York’s Workstation project, winner of the 1994 Windows World Open “Financial Application of the Year” award.

Co founder and CTO of an award winning, New York-based software company specializing in a web-based human capital optimization (scheduling) product.

A proven team leader and team contributor with fine-tuned analytical, organizational, and strategic planning skills.

Demonstrated ability to revive endangered projects through effective communication and expectation realignment. .

Strong written and oral communication skills.

Proven ability to bridge the gap between technology and business units by enabling effective communication between both units.

Experience with all levels of tactical and strategic business development from both sides of equation; producer and supplier of services.

Extremely well versed in all levels of IT application development, including architecture and design of complex, enterprise-wide applications.

Seasoned instructor for team building, technology and process and methodology classes.

Strong proponent for effective management of clients and teams through proper communication and expectation management.

Sits on the board of directors for several not-for-profit charities in Canada.

Founder of charity-based international chess tournament, donating the proceeds to children’s charities.

Motivational speaker, lecturer, writer and Internet radio host.

Founder: Microsoft Personal Empowerment Group, a group dedicated to the development of personal and professional excellence

Proven industry experience in the following verticals: Financial Services, Property and Casualty Insurance, Auto Insurance, Retail, Call Centers, Human Capital Optimization, Government and Consultant Services
