Sandee(Sandra) Lewin Nye’s Summary

Creative Photographer and Graphic Designer able to take a project through the creative process from concept to completion. Extensive educational foundation, and varied work experience in Photography/Fine Art/Graphic Design. Creative vision reliable; results oriented with strong analytical, organizational, multi-tasking, and problem solving abilities with excellent customer service and interpersonal skills.

Photographs published in various magazines, books, on CD's, Individual and collaborative fine art photographic exhibits in various galleries and studios. You will find my photographic images on a variety of musician, poet and theater production websites.

Project and Event Production experience. I have been the Artist Curator of 4 of my Fine Art Photography Exhibits. Produced 3 Sandee's Sunday Blues Jam & Photography Exhibits. Extensive experience designing logos, letterheads, business cards as well as creating newspaper/magazine ads, brochures, business forms, CD covers, flyers, promo packages and media kits, menus, newsletters, POP material, web banners, for clients in various organization.

Additional experience in writing press releases; as a teacher/trainer; event production; community arts.
