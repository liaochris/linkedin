Griff Potter’s Summary

I am an experienced news media manager. I have experience in all linear broadcast and new media platforms, and years of creative programming experience in various news and information channels and sites. My specialties include broadcast management, editorial and production, I love to build and lead teams, create and grow new brands and products. This is a great time to be in the content creation business.

Griff Potter’s Experience

  • News Director, WPSD-TV

    Paxton Media Group

    (Broadcast Media industry)

    August 2004 — October 2008 (4 years 3 months)

    Managed information gathering and presentation for WPSD-TV, KBSI-TV, and WPSDLocal6.com. My strategies led WPSD to #1 in ratings in key time periods and demographics. This position involved intense engagement/support for a staff of 50. Developed sophisticated multi-platform digital journalism workflow which included putting more journalists in the newsroom.   • News Director, WQAD-TV

    New York Times Broadcast Group

    (Broadcast Media industry)

    October 1997 — January 2003 (5 years 4 months)

The result was an average 3-4 million page views per month and the site receiving the Edward R.Murrow Award for Best Website. Also developed and nurtured innovative Newspaper Convergence with Dispatch/Rock Island Argus Newspaper group which included sharing of video resources, reporting staffs and leadership in community service projects.
