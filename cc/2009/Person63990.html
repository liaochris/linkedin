Martin Brown’s Summary

Over 30 years experience in built environment, construction and facilities management related professions, in the UK and Worldwide. Former project manager and business improvement director, now independent and focused on leading and supporting improvements within the built environment.

Presenter, facilitator, coach, educator and blogger on:

Sustainability
Collaborative working
Futures
Virtual Worlds
Web2.0
Complexity and Connectivity
Benchmarking
Community of Practices
Excellence
Continuous Improvement

Also within my portfolio approach :

Constructing the Future: project manager (Elevate regeneration)

be2camp: co founder, where web2.0 and the built environment meet

Evolution-IP, consultants providing strategic advice and support

Founding Fellow, The Benchmark Institute

Constructing Excellence: Chair of Lancashire Best Practice Club and Collaborative Working Champion

My main business area is in leading and supporting improvements across the built environment, which at the moment tend to be environment, carbon and social issues, however my portfolio approach encompasses a wide range of organisational improvement with a client base that covers multi-national organisations to small micro businesses.

Since 1994, I have been involved in leading and supporting improvements in construction and facilities management at national and regional levels through organisations such as European Forum for Teamwork, DBF, Constructing Excellence and Center for Facilities Management.

As a resident of Second Life, I explore the future of virtual environments as an aid to improving usability of built environments. I am currently working on a number of projects that link real life learning with the second life environment.

I have more than a passing interest in connectivity - from application of complexity and chaos theory, to Web2.0 to wiki and blogs to connectivity with nature.
