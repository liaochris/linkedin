Simon Jones’s Summary

Internationally experienced transitions director throughout the UK, Europe and Australia. Strong proactive financial leadership (CPA) of start-up, high growth, business recovery, turnaround, M&A, restructuring, recapitalisation and refinancing transitions. Varied sectors including manufacturing and FMCG, retail, leisure, travel, tourism, technology and public sectors.

Niche, and passion, is hands-on leadership with clients at Board level to deliver business recovery and corporate transaction strategies and projects.

Commercial business reviews of strategic, cultural, governance, financial, project and technical aspects with recovery readiness assessments.

Practical global business technology delivery of ERP and Reporting solutions to assure business decision making.

Simon Jones’s Specialties:

Saving and augmenting corporate value in times of high duress.

Focusing directors and executives with reality checks and psychological and situational insights.

Diligent cash and profitability leadership.

Key to current times: insight, consistency, efficacy, prudence and governance.

Ideal clients are with c£20m-£250m turnover.
