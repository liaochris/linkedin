Michelle Dumas’s Summary

Founded in 1996 by Michelle Dumas, Distinctive Documents is one of the Internet's top professional resume writing, personal branding, and career marketing firms. As one of only a handful of dually certified professional resume writers (CPRW and NCRW), with cutting-edge training as a Career Coach and Personal Branding Strategist, and as a Credentialed Career Master, Job & Career Transition Coach, and a Certified Employment Interview Professional Michelle Dumas is an authority in the industry and one of the leading executive resume writers in the U.S.

Michelle is the author of "Secrets of a Successful Job Search" and "101 Before-and-After Resume Examples." Her work has also been featured in ten top-selling books, including "Executive Job Search for $100,000 to $1 Million+ Jobs," "Resume Winners from the Pros," "Expert Resumes from Computer and Web Jobs," "Expert Resumes for Teachers and Educators," "Expert Resumes for Manufacturing Professionals," and "America's Top Resumes for America's Top Jobs." Her articles on job search techniques are featured on many sites throughout the Internet and in "CareerXroads 2002 and 2003". Michelle has been quoted in the media on resume writing topics and also serves as a "resume expert" for ExecuNet, the Internet's premier career center for the $100K+ executive.

As an active member of the National Resume Writers' Association, the Professional Association of Resume Writers/Career Coaches, and the Career Masters Institute, Michelle stays abreast of ever-evolving trends in the employment field. She is a former member of the NRWA Regional Board and also served from 1998-2004 on the NRWA Certification Commission and 2006-present on the NRWA Certification Commission Advisory Board, training and setting quality standards for resume writers nationwide.

Michelle continues to work very selectively and exclusively with a small number of top-tier clients through www.100kCareerMarketing.com
