James Joyner’s Summary

I have been Managing Editor of the Atlantic Council of the United States, a foreign policy think tank, since September 2007. I oversaw the revamping of their online presence and established and edit their public policy blog, New Atlanticist. Pageviews have increased 35-fold since my taking over.

I founded outsidethebeltway.com in January 2003 and have built it into one of the most respected right-of-center weblogs. More recently, I have expanded the OTB Media brand to include several niche blogs.

I've written dozens of articles for publications including Tech Central Station, TCS Daily, Reason, Legal Affairs, Human Events, The New Individualist, and The Washington Examiner.

I have a PhD in political science and taught at the university level for nine years, was Managing Editor of Strategic Insights, and am a combat veteran of Desert Storm.

A more detailed bio, including a list of publications, is available at http://jamesjoyner.com/bio.html.
