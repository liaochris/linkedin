Jason Sowell’s Summary

I'm a native Floridian and have been in ministry for 10 years as a student pastor and church planter. Most recently, I was the Pastor of Media Outreach & NexGen Ministries, and a teaching pastor at Relevant Church - an innovative, college-age/young adult focused church in Ybor City, FL. I worked as the Creative Marketing Consultant for More2Life, an abstinence education program in Pinellas County, where I created the popular ad campaign, “Red Letter Rebels.”

I currently write student ministry resources, inspirational columns for several publications, speak to students and adults at camps and events all over the United States, and am launching a non-profit organization called Current.

I am passionate about global affairs concerning people that are less fortunate and in need of humanitarian and spiritual aid, and have contributed to many global campaigns and organizations over the years. I want to make a personal impact by reaching college students and leading them around the world, partnering with affective organizations, to engage these issues.

In my dream world, I live on the beach in Southern California and am a pro surfer. In real life, I live in the city of Tampa where I never actually surf.
