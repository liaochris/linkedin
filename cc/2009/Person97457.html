Teresha Aird’s Summary

I have been marketing and promoting websites since 1997, optimising websites, link building and providing online marketing consultancy from conception through to promotion for nearly 1000 clients in South Africa and the UK between 1997 to 2003.

In 2003 I went to work for Fubra as their Network Content Manager and then joined my husband in setting up and running our own company, InfoActive Media Ltd in the UK. In 2007 we set up the Australian branch (unfortunately it's not all laptops on the beach sipping chardonnay - we usually end up working in the office well into the night due to working in both Australian and UK timezones!).

I have in the past been heavily involved with online directories and databases, having joined Dmoz in its infancy, building up the South African section and later the UK Business and Economy and Travel and Tourism sections. I moderated the Directories section on Search Engine Forums ("JimWorld" as it was originally known) for a time and was quite active in providing advice and consultancy on various online promotion forums and lists.
