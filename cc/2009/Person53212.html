Karen Eastman Bigos-www.trghome.com’s Summary

People actually call me the same way they would a 411 service, when it comes to Millburn and Short Hills. I have been here all my life and I know every street, cross street, odd and even numbers, and often who has lived in the house for the last 50+ years. I know all the businesses, many landlords and have been selling real estate for over a quarter century. I went to Millburn High School and ran 3 of my high school reunions.

It is no accident that I have negotiated the sale of over 600 homes or compiled a sales volume of over $600 million. The harder I work, the luckier I get and my business continues to grow from personal referrals from satisfied clients, the many groups with whom I network, and even from strangers who have heard about me over the last quarter century.

My business partner Daniel Cannizzo, who has worked with me since 2000, is another fellow lifelong resident and graduate of Millburn High. He, along with my husband Mark who is a Broker and an attorney, all agree that a boutique has a place in this community and the surrounding towns. By focusing on Total Transaction Service, we have distinguished ourselves as leaders in our industry in less than four years of business.
