David Anthony (danthony@21ventures.net)’s Summary

The markets for technology innovation have changed dramatically over the past decade creating numerous forces that precipitate a new direction for venture capital strategy.

21Ventures is a Cleantech Virtual Incubator focusing on the technology and innovations that will dominate the 21st century. Since its founding in 2003, 21Ventures has provided seed, growth and bridge capital to more than 40 companies across the globe. The firm's expertise in Cleantech has directed investment across the spectrum of wind, solar, energy storage, efficiency and agricultural technology. The firm occasionally invests in other market sectors as opportunities and synergies arise.
With over $250 million under management, we are constantly seeking out new opportunities for investment and return; therefore, we work overtime to shift quickly and strategically into new opportunities.

    Clean Power Technogies, Inc.

    (Venture Capital & Private Equity industry)

    2009 — Present (less than a year)

    Clean Power Technologies aims to significantly reduce emissions from transport and utilities by introducing our steam hybrid system.

    Our steam hybrid system is based on our proprietary technology Clean Energy Storage And Recovery (CESAR). CESAR is able to recover otherwise wasted heat from combustion engine exhaust and cooling systems which is then utilised to generate high-pressure superheated steam. This energy is converted to rotational power by expansion through a vapour engine to drive auxiliary equipment or to supplement a vehicle's main power source.

    We can reduce fuel usage with an accompanying reduction in emissions.

  • Co-investor, Board Member

    Advanced Telemetry, LLC

    (Renewables & Environment industry)

    2008 — Present (1 year)

    Corporate Overview
    Founded in September 2007, Advanced Telemetry is enabling energy and resource conservation through its proprietary EcoView line of products. EcoView is available under the Advanced Telemetry brand as well as being white-labeled for certain distribution partners.

    The EcoView product line embodies Advanced Telemetry's corporate belief that change in energy wasting consumption habits can only occur if the results of these habits are visible to energy users in real time. As such, the EcoView product line enables users to observe and take immediate action to address excessive resource consumption. Advanced Telemetry greatly supports efforts by federal, state, and local governments to bring energy efficiency and conservation incentives and devices to the forefront of the dialogue regarding global warming and resource conservation. The EcoView product line is utility and meter agnostic and does not require the participation of utilities to achieve full functionality.

    The Company is headquartered in San Diego, California and venture backed by 21Ventures (www.21ventures.net).

    BioPetroClean

    (Environmental Services industry)

    2007 — Present (2 years)

    BioPetroClean is a cleantech company that specializes in the bioremediation of industrial wastewaters. Our breakthrough technology, Automated Chemostat Treatment (ACT), is a solution that offers significant improvements over other existing processes on the market. ACT can be applied to a wide variety of sites (including but not limited to oil refineries, terminals, drilling sites, marine ports, power stations, contaminated resevoirs, storage tanks, etc).

    It has been field-proven at numerous sites, and has been shown to bring down contamination levels to well below goverment discharge levels. The output is virtually sludge-free, and can be returned directly to the environment. It is cost-effective, easy to implement, and carries very low running costs. It can be fitted into existing infrastructures, leaving a much smaller on-site footprint.

    BPC offers anything from one-time solutions to full implementations that process continuous streams of industrial waste water.
