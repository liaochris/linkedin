Marc Gratacos’s Summary

Currently I work as consultant at the International Swaps and Derivatives Association, Inc. (ISDA). ISDA is the global trade association representing participants in the privately negotiated derivatives industry, a business covering swaps and options across all asset classes (interest rate, currency, commodity and energy, credit and equity). At ISDA, I am helping to develop FpML, the industry-standard XML vocabulary for complex financial products. I participate in all the working groups, putting together proposals, giving technical support, editing the specifications, and chairing the FpML Business Process Working Group. In addition, I work consulting and giving training courses in FpML and developing a variety of projects in the OTC derivatives industry.

Marc Gratacos’s Specialties:

Consulting, derivatives, integration, messaging, XML vocabularies, model-driven technologies, data modeling, business processes.
