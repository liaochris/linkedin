Berta Daniels’s Summary

For two decades, Berta A. Daniels has found the soul of diverse subjects, from scorching, to mouth-watering, to ethereal.

A native of the Boston area, Berta attended the Art Institute of Boston, where she majored in photography and sculpture. She works as a lifestyle photographer for magazines, books and other publications. Whether on location or in the studio, Berta focuses on creating unique images for the for the music and restaurant industries, nightlife, travel and fashion, as well as corporate and family portraiture.

Berta Daniels’s Specialties:

Food and resturant photography, public relations, portraiture, live concert and music industry photography, travel photography, annual reports, corporate portraits, headshots, CD cover art, fine art

Photoshop, digital work flow, studio lighting, shooting on location
