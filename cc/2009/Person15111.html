Camilla Rendal Nielsen’s Summary

With more than 12 years experience in Intellectual Property Rights, I assist companies and universities with identifying inventions, assessing invention disclosures for patentability and marketability and draft and prosecute patent applicatins. I furthermore train reseachers, engineers, and professionals in Intellectual Property Rights to raise the awareness on this subject. I further assist companies in drawing up and implementing Intellectual Property Strategies and in Portfolio Management.

I have a scientific background in applied physics and electronics, and I am a qualified European Patent Attorney having worked as a consultant and as an in-house patent attorney.
