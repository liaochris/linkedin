Bo Buchanan’s Summary

I am a REALTOR in the greater Far Western Suburbs of Chicago. I cover Oswego-Aurora-Naperville-Plainfield and surrounding areas. I also run Blue60.com - a directory of resources for real estate professionals. My passion has always been in the media and technology and now I am able to combine those interests with the world of Real Estate.

When I first began working in Real Estate, I saw the boom occuring in and around the Oswego area. I chose to work with Kettley Realtors because I admired the honesty and dedication of the company founder, Ed Kettley. I continue to enjoy working for Kettley Realtors and I am an avid proponent of Real Estate as an investment vehicle (my first home was a four flat in Chicago!).

Shortly after becoming a Realtor I saw a need for some sort of resource to help Real Estate Professionals find tools, professionals and resources to run their business. I founded Blue60 in 2003 out of my own desire to find real estate tools on the internet. If you are a Real Estate Professional looking for resources to help you with marketing, lead generation, websites or anything else - check out the Blue60.com directory.


REAL ESTATE COMMUNITIES
----------------------------------------------
As a REALTOR I help people buy and sell Real Estate in Oswego, Montgomery, Yorkville, Bristol, Naperville, Plainfield, Aurora, North Aurora, Plano, Sandwich, Sugar Grove, Lisle, Batavia, Geneva, St. Charles, Warrenville, West Chicago and Wheaton

MEDIA & TECHNOLOGY
-------------------------------------
I have over 10 years experience in Radio, TV, Internet & Print Media. I am constantly looking for Real Estate Resources on the internet to share with Real Estate Professionals at Blue60.com. I maintain 2 blogs - one for Real Estate Professionals (Blue60Blog.com) and one for anyone interested in buying/selling real estate(IllinoisHouseHunter.com).

Bo Buchanan’s Specialties:

I specialize in residential real estate sales (single family homes, townhomes, codndos), Multi-Unit Properties (Small Apartment Buildings), Bank Owned Foreclosures or REO's, and helping homeowners avoid foreclosure through Short Sales - selling a home even when you owe more than it can be sold for.
