Ralph Jannelli’s Summary

I understand the challenges we are all facing this year and realize the need to more effectively manage expenses. Marketing is one area where you must make dollars spent work harder than ever. Now is NOT the time to reduce your investment, but to be smarter and more strategic. Because, let’s be honest, if your not marketing to your prospects, someone else is!
If you advertise when everyone else stops marketing, your message is more likely to be noticed due to fewer ads in the market and your business is more likely to be remembered when everyone starts advertising again. It’s common sense, but yet during every recession, one of the first things companies do is pull back on their marketing and advertising.
During the current economic downturn, you have an incredible opportunity to INCREASE SALES and BUILD MARKETSHARE. But don’t take my word for it…I can show you proof.
Promotional Marketing is THE most cost effective method to get your message out, I can help you design and execute a plan to get the kind of ROI you are looking for from your marketing investment.
