Carol Nix’s Summary

I have owned NIXdesign, a full service design + interactive marketing firm, for over 18 years. NIXdesign was an early integrated marketing communications pioneer in the Triangle region – we adopted web/interactive services early in the game and are consistently ranked as one of the Triangle’s Top 25 Interactive Media firms. We have worked with hundreds of start-ups and companies in a wide range of industries including: Quintiles, NetIQ, SAS/Dataflux, The Body Shop, Oenophilia, NC Medical Board, Nomaco, The Freelon Group architects, Hatteras Networks, Biologics and Burt's Bees and Perfumes Isabell NYC.

With a changing economy, I revised the firm's business model and accepted a position at French West Vaughan, one of the nation’s largest independent public relations firms, as the Director of Interactive Media. There I worked with many Fortune 500 companies such as Astroturf, Wrangler, Fossil, Coke, and CIAA and branded and developed the agency’s first interactive services center, the e•MERGING Media Lab. I was then promoted to SVP of Marketing Services and ran both the interactive and creative divisions. Following the agency's merger with another company, I returned full-time to NIXdesign where I work with agencies and companies to provide a full range of services including branding, web/interactive design, marketing strategy, management, and new business development. I often work on-site with clients as a marketing consultant and I also provide advertising/design/PR agencies with growth strategy, creative direction, management and new business opportunities.
