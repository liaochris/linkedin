Diane Crompton’s Summary

Career Consultant with over 15 years combined experience in career transition services, recruiting, education and consulting. Experienced in coaching professionals at all levels from a wide range of functional and industry backgrounds with varying career objectives. Additional areas of expertise in project management, client relations and customized curriculum design. Advocate for face-to-face and online networking to promote career transition and effective career management. Co-author with Ellen Sautter of "Seven Days to Online Networking: Make Connections to Advance Your Career and Business Quickly (JIST, 2008)."

Experience includes project management of several career centers and coordination of large scale national projects. Known for inspired and thoughtful approach to coaching, training and project coordination.

Email: diane.crompton at right.com

Diane Crompton’s Specialties:

Group facilitation, outplacement training, project management, job lead generation, recruiting, resume writing, online networking, social networking, LinkedIn, education, coordination, bilingual instruction, curriculum design, assessment interpretation, instructional design principles, client relations, Train the Trainer
