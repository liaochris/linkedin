Sankarshan Mukhopadhyay’s Summary

I have been actively participating in Free and Open Source Software for close to 12 years now in various roles of a Systems Engineer, Developer, Consultant, Team Lead, Sales Engineer and Project Manager.

I have both participated and lead distributed as well as virtual teams tasked with delivery of products. I have experience in managing projects of sufficient complexity and modularity. Such projects include both product innovations as well as sustaining engineering of existing services and products.

I have experience of creating, delivering and managing Products and Projects in the area of development and consumption of Free and Open Source Software tools.

I have expertise in speaking about Free and Open Source Software, Open Standards and Public Commons.

I have a functional knowledge in the following organizational units and processes:

+ Systems Engineering
+ Sales Engineering
+ Services Delivery
+ Program Management
+ Partner Management

I have been a Marketing Contact for GNOME as well as OpenOffice.org in India for around 5 years now. I also managed the Public Relations and Community Outreach efforts of the Ankur Bangla Project. I am the Joint Lead for the Bengali (India) locale for OpenOffice.org, Firefox and Thunderbird Native Language efforts. I am a Fedora Ambassador and a member of the Fedora Marketing Team and part of the Fedora Project.

I aim to work with teams innovating in Products or Services space with an emphasis on building Community of Usage around the innovation.
