Rosalie Petralia’s Summary

Florida State University MLIS student, graduating August 2009. My interest in information studies revolves around maximizing the power of existing technologies to make peoples' lives easier. Improving the information literacy of Brevard County's students is one personal goal, empowering those students to make waves is another.

Information is also the focus of my primary work as a technical writer for Harris Stratex Networks, where I write online user guides for NetBoss network management applications. In my spare time I am Senior Copy Editor and sometime reviews writer for the internationally known Ink 19 magazine.

Technology, digital efficiency, books, travel, and music are my diversions; squirrels are my aversion.

Writing, editing, online help, public speaking, teaching
