Fabio De Gregorio’s Summary

My field of expertise is clinical research. While studying for taking my PhD (Ocular Patho-physiology), I spent ten years in the Ocular Physio-Pharmacology Department of “La Sapienza” University of Rome, where I had the opportunity to learn how developing ophthalmic drugs, taking part in preclinical studies on animal models and phase II-III clinical trials. Successively, since I started to work in pharmaceutical industry, I have planned, designed and coordinated several phase II and III clinical trials. Recently, I expanded my interests and experience in pharmacovigilance, acting as the Qualified Person for Pharmacovigilance for the company where I have been working. Therefore, as my professional background is a mixture of direct clinical practice as an ophthalmologist and experience in pharmaceutical industry, I have the skills to cope with any clinical affair in a peculiarly comprehensive way.
