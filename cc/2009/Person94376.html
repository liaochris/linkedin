Stephen Howard’s Summary

I entered into the telecom industry in 2003 as an independent agent selling AT&T services to businesses. I then moved to eGIX where I have been since 2004.

Telecom is an industry that is rapidly changing. I enjoy and understand it. I have helped many companies over the years save money and implement new technology. My key focus is to solve communication problems.

It is very apparent that every business is different and we have put together a comphrehensive product line that allows us to assist no matter the stage or cycle of a business.

Stephen Howard’s Specialties:

eGIX as a company has been a leader in technology and being a leader allowed us to catch the eye of Cincinnati Bell who purchased our company in 1st quarter of 2008.

With the ability to deliver integrated voice & data circuits, SIP trunks, hosted IP PBX systems and fix mobile convergence(allows cell phones to be extension of your office phone system) we provide the latest technology.
