Kyle Orland’s Summary

Writing about video games for anyone who will pay me. I have been published in a wide variety of outlets including Electonic Gaming Monthly, Paste Magazine, The Escapist, Next Gen, Gamasutra, and National Public Radio. I'm the author of Wiley's Wii for Dummies, published in Sept. 2008, and The Video Game Style Guide and Reference Manual, published in August 2007.

Kyle Orland’s Specialties:

Video games and video game culture
