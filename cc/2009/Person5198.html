Andrea Malloni, SPHR’s Summary

A creative and results driven HR generalist with more than 10 years of experience in a number of diverse industries including new media, IT outsourcing and higher education. Strategic partnership with varied stakeholders in the development and delivery of HR services (including direct consultation with executive leadership).

Adept at researching, building and implementing HR tools and resources. Hands-on delivery of day-to-day aspects of HR including employee relations, recruitment, coaching, leadership development, performance management, compensation, benefits administration and new program and policy development.

A proven contributor that values metrics and results.
