Ravi "HeyRavi.com"’s Summary

Ravi tours the country performing original music, conducting guitar clinics, and lecturing on crucial issues facing the music industry. The singer-songwriter-guitarist has performed on Letterman, Leno, Saturday Night Live, Today Show, Good Morning America, etc., and at the most prestigious venues including Madison Square Garden, Louisiana Superdome, and a White House Christmas party. Ravi's autobiographical book about touring as the guitarist of the top-selling band in the world was published by Simon & Schuster.

With professional endorsements & business savvy, Ravi has released CDs and toured extensively for signings, product clinics, and performances—both solo and with his band. He writes and performs a unique twist on "pop" music, combining funky grooves with smart songwriting. His music and musicianship have garnered rave reviews, comparing him to Eric Clapton, Steve Winwood, Duane Allman, Peter Frampton, and others.

In addition, Ravi is the founder of www.ArtisticIntegrity.org and www.CultureofIntegrity.org. These Web sites combined with his frequent lectures and articles on "Integrity" have positioned Ravi as one of the most outspoken advocates on the subject in the music industry.

He recently broadened his horizons (literally) by entering the Aviation Industry as a consultant, and of course, pilot. He premiered his seminar "Making General Aviation Sexy" at the industry's celebrated event, "Sun 'n' Fun" in Lakeland Fl, and will present that and other seminars around the nation including at the country's largest aviation show, "AirVenture" in Oshkosh WI. In addition, he writes for AOPA's Flight Training magazine.

Ravi, of Indian and German descent (and grand nephew of India’s first Prime Minister, Nehru), was born in Washington, DC. He grew up in a "Wall Street" family (his two older brothers and father work in finance), but Ravi traveled down a very different road.

Ravi "HeyRavi.com"’s Specialties:

Guitarist, Singer, Songwriter. Licensed pilot. Lecturer, Writer, Consultant, and Clinician in the Music and Aviation Industries. Recording studio owner and engineer/producer. Also, proficient in Desktop Publishing (MS Office Suite), Graphics and Web Design (Photoshop, Dreamweaver), Accounting (Quickbooks, Quicken), and other software applications.
