Ignacio Gallardo’s Summary

My professional goal is to help the students and alumni of the University of California identify and fulfill their career expectations. At Career Services, I serve as the bridge between their college experience and employment or graduate school, helping them apply what they have learned. By providing comprehensive resources, programs, and counseling on career development, internships, employment, and graduate school, I assist students and alumni to make career decisions, connect with employers, and attain their life goals.
