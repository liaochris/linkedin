Jos Konincks’s Summary

Since 2006 I am active as accredited independent company adviser and B2B broker for companies. Also I can be hired as interim professional for typical insurance & risk control projects.

My goals:
As Adviser & B2B broker help with the support of an international group of accredited advisers companies in subjects like: Business plans, Buying and selling of company resources such as buildings and equipment, Cost reduction utilities, Exit route for owners or company transfer, Export and Import, Innovation, Investments and Risk management.
Because of this international cooperation I ensure form and level on which support is provided connect to the specific needs, wishes and objectives of companies.

As interim insurance professional help with my expertise insurance companies, brokers and insurance departments of companies in solving specific projects.

My background is 30+ years experience in the insurance world. I worked as underwriter & insurance broker. My activities were in the field of risk management & insurances, especially aimed at the dangers related to operational management, material & image damage. I participated in project groups for insurance and prevention related subjects. On request gave presentations to interested groups.

For further information I invite you to visit the company website www.k-c-s.eu

Jos Konincks’s Specialties:

Independent analyse, advice, implementation and control of Business, Risk and Insurance Management solutions. Broker in offers and demands in businesses of my clients.
