Nicolò Wojewoda’s Summary

I consider myself an analyst and manager, quite often a leader, with significant experience in the field. What I'm really interested in and what I've really tried to do so far is involving young people in the decision-making processes related to higher education policies: I truly believe youth should be given the right to shape the society they'll live in and their kids will grow in. My particular focus, as you might have guessed by my background, is on engineering education.

Nicolò Wojewoda’s Specialties:

Engineering education policies, student involvement in higher education, European policies
