Ash Donaldson’s Summary

I've been practising, studying and promoting all aspects of human-centred design for more than 14 years.

Highlights include becoming:
- The first federal court expert witness in human-computer interaction for Australia
- The representative Australian expert, developing human-centred design International standards for ISO
- The Chair of the Australian national human-computer interaction conference, OZCHI
- The inaugural World Usability Day leader for Sydney
- A TEDster www.ted.com

Ash Donaldson’s Specialties:

Human factors, human-computer interaction, user experience, information architecture, usability, activity theory, accessibility, strategy, web standards, anthropology, social media
