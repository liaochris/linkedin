Merrily Orsini’s Summary

The corecubed family of services helps companies tell their stories in a way that helps sell more product and/or service. With monthly marketing communications programs including print and interactive media, clients see results. From custom campaigns with corecubed to innovative products at MarketHomeCare.com, clients can choose which corecubed service fits them best.

Founder, Merrily Orsini, MSSW, is an award-winning entrepreneur with a background that combines skills in technology and people. She was a pioneer in the geriatric care managed home care business model starting in 1981, and now is again ahead of the curve with her collaborative integrated strategic marketing communications business providing marketing, design and PR.

Read about Merrily's speaking engagements and her home care blog at www.merrilyorsini.com.
