Wulfrano Moreno’s Summary

I am a certified SAP Technology Architect with extensive experience in the SAP Netweaver Platform and SOA. In my four years with SAP I have been involved in a large number of projects, both internal and external with Local and International costumers as well.

With the knowledge gained in different roles throughout all phases of a project I am providing valuable assistance to customer projects in an advisory role during the design and realization phases. Customers also benefit from my quality solution reviews and problem solving skills.

Before joining SAP I mainly work helping Mexican and Latin American customers to create corporate, branding and commercial websites; implementing custom development solutions that often involved the integration of legacy systems using several web technologies.

With fifteen years of experience, I have special interest in new technologies and development techniques like SOA, Complex Event Processing, AJAX, POJOs, Java 5, Mashups, Don't Repeat Yourself frameworks and Rich Internet Applications among others.

I consider myself a technophile with excellent self-training skills, a passion for knowledge and a big curiosity for how things work in general, that's why my areas of interest cover not just Engineering but Economics, Psychology, Physics and Philosophy as well.
