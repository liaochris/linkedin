Shankar Subramanian . CPBA’s Summary

Shankar Subramanian is an experienced trainer with more than 17 years of experience in the service industry and corporate training. He is the Founder and Principal Consultant of Ninedots . Ninedots specializes in Innovation consulting and training on creativity and Lateral thinking.

Shankar is on the advisory committee of various organizations and NGO’s and them resolve their business concerns.
Shankar is a Certified Instructor of de Bono’s Six Thinking Hats and Lateral Thinking . Shankar is a passionate trainer and his training programs have a lasting impact on participants who attend his sessions. Shankar’s programs are interactive with a balanced mix of humor and Shankar is an avid photographer and has exhibited his pictures in various galleries in India . Shankar believes that by helping individuals realize their potential and equipping them with critical business skills he contributes to the overall success of the Organization
