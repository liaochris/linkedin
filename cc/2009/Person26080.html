Desigan Chinniah’s Summary

The enthusiastic, sociable and self motivated, Desigan Chinniah, is an experienced User Experience and Front-End Development Consultant, presently plying his trade for Gumtree.com.

With over 5 years of experience with leading internet properties (Ask Jeeves, Ask.com, Skype, BBC World Service, eBay and Gumtree.com), he enjoys producing, designing and developing, cutting-edge content for web interfaces and applications. He is passionate about web standards, accessibility, innovation and a positive user experience using user-centred design methodologies.

He is also actively involved with the London social media/technology scene, regularly attending meet ups, geek dinners, events and conferences, as well as most recently organising BarCampLondon5, held at eBay UK's Richmind campus.
