Paul Kruitz’s Summary

I am a young dutch game programmer interested in graphics/shaders and game play. Ever since the Atari 2600 playing video games has been one of my biggest passions. When I was 16 years of age, I realised that I wanted to become a video games programmer. Nowadays I still enjoy playing video games, but I enjoy creating them even more. The idea of having people playing something that I have made gives me great satisfaction. That is the reason why I am attending a Msc. Games Programming in Hull.
I can speak and write 3 languages: Dutch (native), English (Near Native), French(conversational).
