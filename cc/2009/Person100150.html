Tom Stone’s Summary

I am a Visual Artist well versed in an array of creative skills.

Sculpting background:
I have worked with sculpting media such as oil-based clay, wood, and cast metals. I welded my own armatures for the clay sculptures and created my own molds for the cast metals. I have shown my sculptures in various galleries in the Northeastern USA and on my portfolio website.

Digital background:
Creating abstract experimental animations with a painterly appearance.
Freelance: visual effects and rotoscoping for short and independent feature length films. Working with clients in NY and NC.
Freelance: website designs, flash animations.

Tom Stone’s Specialties:

Software skills: Maya, Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects, Final Cut Pro, Premiere, Flash, Dreamweaver, Camtasia, GIMP.

Artistic skills: Sculpting, Ceramics, Drawing, Web Animation, Web Design, 3D Modeling, Broadcast Animation, Compositing, Rotoscoping, Storyboarding, Animatics.

Business skills: Quickly adapts to new positions, self-driven, quick follow-through, time-management, trustworthy, and excellent communication skills.
