Gregory Audetto’s Summary

Gregory is Commercial / Business Development Director of Renova Power. Renova Power reduces the environmental impact and the energy costs of Italian firms, by supplying solutions for buildings and for sustainable mobility.
Gregory’s previous employments have been in the Automotive sector where, among other responsibilities, he has managed an international car racing series during a 4 year period.

Gregory Audetto’s Experience

  • Commercial & Business Development Director

    Renova Power S.r.l.

    (Oil & Energy industry)

    December 2007 — Present (2 years )

    Renova Power reduces the environmental impact and the energy costs of Italian firms, by supplying solutions for buildings and for sustainable mobility. In particular, in order to supply integrated solutions for green buildings, Renova Power has established a partnership with a real estate company that has over 100 years of history in the construction sector. This partnership led Renova Power to enter into an alliance of high-profile companies mainly operating in engineering, architecture and construction. The member companies of the alliance collaborate on a daily basis and create synergies in order to provide integrated solutions to each other’s clients. Each member company, including Renova Power, has an office in the same building which has been studied to maximise interaction.

    Renova Power’s 3 areas of specialization are the following:

    ENERGY: Renova Power installs turnkey solar photovoltaic systems, supplies electricity at competitive prices and provides energy efficiency solutions. In particular, for solar photovoltaic systems, Renova Power supplies roof-top turnkey solar photovoltaic systems comprised of engineering, installation and obtaining of government subsidies.

    GREEN BUILDINGS: Renova Power supplies building materials, construction solutions and integrated systems that increase building’s efficiency and reduce their environmental impact. Renova Power´s green building solutions decrease cost of ownership, improve employees’ working conditions and reduces absenteeism.. For the every solution Renova Power offers, the company has as a deep understanding of the product and a longterm partnership with the company supplying it.

    SUSTAINABLE MOBILIY: Renova Power provides solutions to car washes, gas stations and car rentals in order to reduce the energy costs and environmental impact of both their activity and their clients.
