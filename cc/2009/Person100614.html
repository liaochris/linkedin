Tracey Stuckey’s Summary

I am an expert in the field of early childhood education and certified by the National Board for Professional Teaching standards, the highest certification a teacher can obtain. I have taught kindergarten for 15 years, facilitated workshops for teachers to improve their practice, designed learning cadres to connect college education majors to our schools and worked diligently with preschools and daycare centers to improve curriculum and play opportunities for young children.

My mission through Wiggle Giggle Learn is to change the face of education in America by empowering parents of children ages birth to five with the secrets of how early learning happens. Wiggle Giggle Learn teaches parents how to maximize brain development during their child's most critical early years of development.
