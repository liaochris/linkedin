  • Marketing Manager

    Mandalay Sports Entertainment / Dayton Dragons Baseball

    (Privately Held; Sports industry)

    2004 — 2004 (less than a year)

    Served as an intern and then marketing manager in 1999, organizing efforts to introduce the new team to the Miami Valley community. Returned as marketing manager for the 2004 season, handling promotions for the following corporate accounts: Anthem BCBS, Beazer Homes, Heidelberg Distributing, Kettering Medical Center Network, Microsoft Xbox, and Shell / True North Energy. Spotlight promotions included the Anthem BCBS "Home Run for Life" campaign and Anheuser Busch "Around the World Tour."

    Mandalay Sports Entertainment is a subsidiary of Mandalay Entertainment Group (http://www.mandalay.com/). MSE controls the Dayton Dragons, a minor league affiliate of the Cincinnati Reds, and owners include Peter Guber, Magic Johnson, and Archie Griffin. The Dragons sold out every single seat for every single game over seven seasons, unprecedented for minor league baseball. Street & Smith named the Dragons the most outstanding minor league organization in both 2003 and 2004.
