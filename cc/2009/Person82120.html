Rick Arritola’s Summary

Rick Arritola is an Executive Search Consultant specializing in Global Supply Chain/Trade Compliance Management talent acquisition, based in Atlanta. Rick has been in the Executive Search business since 1993, and has spent his recruiting career based in Miami, FL; Lima,Peru; and since 1998 in Atlanta, GA. His reputation for providing exceptional talent, and delivering them to a hire, is second to none.

Rick has the knowledge and ability to quickly ascertain the qualifications of his candidates. This allows him to provide qualified, proven, results-oriented professionals for his clients review in a timely manner.

Another asset Rick brings to the table is having spent a year recruiting in South America. This has allowed him to put his Global Trade experience and expertise to the test, both abroad, and domestically. Perhaps that’s why many of the world's largest global companies (manufacturers, retailers, distributors, and 3PLs) request his services. Rick’s significant knowledge and experience makes him a unique addition to any hiring organization's staffing resources.

Rick is an active member in the Council of Supply Chain Management Professionals, Society of Human Resources Management, International Society of Logistics Engineers, Warehousing Education & Research Council, and the Association of American Exporters & Importers. Rick has been published in “The Wall Street Journal”, and stays up to date on all the latest industry trends.
