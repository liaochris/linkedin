Gail Kent, MFA, ABC’s Summary

You want to be famous? Get local, regional or national publicity? Effectively market your company to improve your bottom line? We've done it in spades.

We help small business owners make a living while living their Amerian dream by digging out, shaping and promoting their unique stories. We use online and traditional media and marketing techniques to generate buzz for businesses and non-profits.

For businesses in Hampton Roads, we provide a FREE service to connect Hampton Roads reporters with businesses who want publicity at www.HRMediaConnectionz.com. If you live in Hampton Roads and want FREE publicity, be sure to check it out and join! But we work for clients not only in Hampton Roads, but across the country!

Our firm specializes in non-profits and small businesses, helping them manage their reputations, gain visibility and reach their marketing goals.

We provide a full range of services through our strategic alliances with networking partners. Services include communications planning, project management, publicity, branding, advertising, direct mail marketing, copywriting, editing, graphics, development of print materials, web site development, article writing for internet marketing, photography, media training, event planning and more.

As managing director, I leverage 30+ years of experience in nearly every aspect of communications, marketing and public relations for my clients. I earned the accredited business communicator designation (ABC) from the International Association of Business Communicators and have won more than 350 local, state and national awards for my communications work.

But we don't work for awards. We work for our clients. Check out my recommendations and see for yourself. Then email me and let's set up a free half-hour phone consultation to get some ideas going for you.
