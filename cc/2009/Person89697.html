Scott Rodgers’s Summary

You could think of me as an "enabler" (the good kind) that enables organizations to take advantage of the web to meet their objectives -- to go from where they are today, to where they want to go, via the web.

As media, advertising and the web experienced significant change over the past 8 years, I have helped local media navigate the evolving space and capitalize on the online opportunities. It involved bridging traditional media with Interactive media, creating and growing online properties, enhancing audience engagement, solutions for advertisers, and watching trends for relevance to an evolving media and advertising landscape. My online media career spans local radio, television and newspaper, the local and corporate levels, and technology, operations, product, marketing and sales roles. Many of the principles used by online media, along with my in-depth and varied perspective, can also be applied to any organization's web presence.

Are you fully leveraging the web to drive results for your organization? I can help...

* Review your existing web presence and objectives
* Develop a web strategy plan for your organization
* Execute the plan -- I either provide that service, recommend someone who can, or train your team to be self-sufficient online.

In a nutshell, it is about releasing your potential online, expanding your online footprint, integrating the web with your operation, and driving results.
