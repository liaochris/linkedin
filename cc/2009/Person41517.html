Julie Salisbury’s Experience

  • Founder and Owner

    InspireABook

    (Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    February 2006 — Present (3 years 6 months)

    InspireABook(tm) is the simplest book-building system in the world. It was developed as a modular program presented as 8 classes which step you through the proces of turning your writing or content into a book. It is ideal for entrepreneurs who have plenty of content on their business but feel overwhelmed. The unique InspireABook workbook is presented in a stylish leather briefcase which contains templates and worksheets which you step through with the assistance of 8 teleseminar classes. Alternatively, Julie encourages her students to attend one of her winter retreats and combine a vacation with quality time to escape and write their book with personal guidance.

  • Outreach Director Co Founder

    Licadho Canada

    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    November 2005 — February 2007 (1 year 4 months)

    Co-founder of this grass roots division of Licadho.org the most respected human rights organization in Cambodia. The President and Co-Founder Lee Robinson is permanently based in Phnom Penn, Cambodia and is responsible for 700 families under threat for the last 2 years of an illegal land grab for property development. The emergency response team she leads is on 24 hour call and she responds with white faces and armed with camera's. She has made numerous short documentaries used for evidence and advocacy. My role is still liaison in Canada and emergency fund-raising usually to pay medical bills for women and children harmed by illegal forced evictions. The grass roots organization was formed to give access to the village and provide more imaginative solutions to the basic human right of community and home. Please take the time to view the short documentaries on the website.
