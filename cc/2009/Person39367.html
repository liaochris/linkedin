Helen Todd’s Summary

My current home is at the Wommie winning firm theKbuzz, specializing in social media and word of mouth marketing. As the Director of Buzz, I can help anyone successfully enter and engage in the social media space.

My insatiable curiosity has lead me to study in London, research marketing and tourism in Thailand and volunteer in Beijing for the 2008 Olympics.

In December of 2008, I graduated with a Masters degree in Integrated Marketing Communication from Emerson College in Boston, MA. I received a BA in Marketing, cum laude, from Xavier University in Cincinnati, OH, prior to relocating to Boston.

I want to contribute my passion for marketing and endless wonderment of the world through my work.

Helen Todd’s Specialties:

Social Media, Facebook, Twitter, YouTube, Integrated Marketing Communications, Emerging Media in Marketing, SEO and SEM, Destination Marketing and Sustainable Travel interest me.
