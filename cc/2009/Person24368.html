David Kaye’s Summary

Having lived and worked in Asia, North America and Africa; I have a deep understanding of ICT, international business, emerging markets and diverse cultural practices. My 15+ years experience in IT operations, software development and technical sales combined with an international network of trusted colleagues, form the basis of my ability to succeed in any business environment. The continuous thread of my career is my passion, energy and dedication to managing my team’s potential and its influence on company performance. I’m ultimately loyal and put incredible effort into building relationship.

I have consistently held executive level positions and pride myself on my ability to lead large teams and to quickly learn new systems and create strategy to solve complex problems. My career highlights include heading technology at Kalahari.net, Southern Africa’s leading e-commerce provider, running corporate global infrastructure to support Critical Path’s offering to 60 million users and working as technical director for Ogilvy Interactive 360° brand strategy offering.

Since returning to the U.S. with my family, my objective is to find a permanent leadership role in delivering international eCommerce and software projects that embody a creative approach to user experience.
