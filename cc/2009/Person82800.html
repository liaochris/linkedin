Reed Kathrein’s Summary

REED R. KATHREIN works to protect the rights of individuals and institiutions though legal advocacy in investor, antitrust and consumer class actions. He has represented institutions, pension funds, investors and consumers in class and representative actions against corporations who commit securities or consumer fraud, unfair business practices, or antitrust or environmental violations. Mr. Kathrein is a partner in the class action law firm of Hagens Berman Sobel & Shapiro LLP for whom he opened their San Francisco office in March 2007. Previously he was a partner in Lerach Coughlin Stoia Geller Rudman & Robbins LLP. He opened the San Francisco office of the predecessor firm, Milberg Weiss Bershad Hynes & Lerach LLP, in 1994.

Reed Kathrein’s Honors:
