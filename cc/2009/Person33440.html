Flavia Paluello’s Summary

Three and half years as responsible for the elaboration and spreading of press releases and external/ internal communication for Brazilian customers and multinationals. Experience one and half year as Responsible Senior of Public Relations for the University FEI (Fundação Educacional Inaciana), one of the most recognized universities of management and engineering it Brazil. Beyond taking care in simultaneous of customers as SMARTtech, Magneti Marelli and SAE Brazil (Society of Automobile Engineers).
As public relations Júnior I have experience of two years as responsible for the elaboration of press releases and press kits of two North American companies: Rockwell Automation of Brazil and Tecflux/Swagelok and for the elaboration of the internal communication of companies as Repsol YPF - Division Lubricative, Lojas Marisa and The Client Cards.
I also did works as free lancer for the pilot of Brazilian StockCar Beto Giorgi and some translations for Porsche magazine in Brazil.
