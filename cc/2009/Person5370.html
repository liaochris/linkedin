André Faria Gomes’s Summary

André Faria Gomes is a software architect, he works for Bluesoft Consultoria in São Paulo, Brazil. He is finishing his graduation in Information Systems at FIAP, he is a member of the SouJava (Java User Group) and a InfoQ Brazil editor.
