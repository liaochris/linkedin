Everett Westcott’s Summary

My background includes thirty-five years of executive experience including “C” level responsibilities with two start-up companies, Aquidneck Data Corporation and The Freedom Group, that were respectively sold to Eastman Kodak and American Retirement Corporation. I also served as a Corporate Internal Auditor for General Dynamics Corporation and Chief Corporate Accountant for Sanders Associates, Inc, now a subsidiary of Lockheed Martin Corporation.

I am currently the CEO of EMW Senior Living Group as well as a founding Managing Partner in The Westcott Edwards Group, both national Management Consulting Companies. These companies are comprised of accomplished senior executives dedicated to helping clients meet and exceed their strategic objectives. The firm is owned and managed by its partners and joins with its clients to solve challenging business issues, exploit opportunities and find new profitable venues for growth.

As CFO/SVP Operations, I played a significant part in helping a young company become nationally successful in the business of designing, constructing, marketing and managing senior living centers. As a Partner, in the capacity of CFO/Senior VP of Operations, I was able to utilize his financial and managerial abilities to help the Freedom Group go from a start-up, to one that ultimately managed 1,700 employees and accumulated $300 million in assets.

During the early – mid eighties, I was CFO and a Director of Aquidneck Data Corporation in Newport, RI. During his employment, Inc. Magazine twice ranked this “high-tech” company as one of the fifty best-managed companies in the nation.
