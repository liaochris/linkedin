Jose Bernardo Gonzalez’s Summary

*Principal Broker/ President of Tu Casa Real Estate. A Real Estate office with end to end bilingual services and a clientele that primarily comes from Mexico and South America.
*Recently completed CIPS (Certified International Property Specialist) designation requirements.
*Member of International Business Council Advisory Board for the Oregon Associations of Realtors.
*Member of Business Issues Committee for Oregon Associations of Realtors.
*Co Taught "Doing Business in Mexico" course for OAR in Portland.
*Speaker for "Tapping into the International Market Both Locally and Abroad" panel presentation for the OAR in Eugene.
*Wrote article for Oregon Realtor Magazine "What Does International Real Estate Mean to You?.
*Currently broadcasting a weekly radio show named "Como Comprar Tu Casa en Mexico" (How to Buy Your Home in Mexico) on a Spanish station.
*Hosting Seminars on "How to Buy Your House in Mexico".

**President/ Partner of Tu Casa Trust International in Mexico City. A Real Estate office dedicted to developing and maintaining relationships with our Mexican developers.
