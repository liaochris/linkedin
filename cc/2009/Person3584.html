Alex Schwartz’s Summary

I'm a game developer/designer and 3D game artist with a passion for innovation, games, and that sweet spot where they coexist. I try to span the gap of programmer/artist and distribute my time among art creation, scripting, and designing games. I've worked on multiple project teams to complete some small interesting games. I also dabble in web design and have been juggling (knives/clubs/balls/torches) for 12 years. I recently graduated from WPI with an Interactive Media and Game Development degree and am currently rocking the Boston game dev scene like its 1999.
