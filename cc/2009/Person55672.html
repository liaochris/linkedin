Kim Seeling Smith’s Summary

Whether you are looking for a "Sea Change" (major life shift) or a "Tree Change" (minor adjustment in one or more areas) Kim Seeling Smith, the self-proclaimed "Queen of Reinvention" can help. Kim has reinvented herself professionally with 4 separate, distinct and successful careers, personally by marrying later in life, having the world's most amicable divorce and now having a blast dating again at 48, and physically by losing 50 lbs / 22 kg's and reshaping her body through yoga.

She now combines all of her experience in the "art of reinvention" to help individuals reinvent themselves professional and personally for extraordinary results.

Kim is also passionate about sales as a profession and provides sales training and coaching, specifically for people in professional services environments who have never been trained in sales but are now responsible for developing business for their firm.

Kim has been a top Recruitment Consultant and Sales Manager in 6 markets and 3 countries. She is also a consistently successful Entrepreneur, a recovering Accountant, part-time Property Investor and dedicated yoga practitioner.

Kim is currently a certified:
Neuro-Strategist
NLP Practitioner
Time Line Therapist
Hypnotherapist
Extended DISC Facilitator

Kim is also completing a Certificate in Strategic Intervention which is co-sponsored by the Robbins-Madanes Center for Strategic Intervention and the Council For Human Rights of Children of The University of San Francisco Center for Child and Family Development.

Kim has held several leadership roles within the community as well including 2 Chairmanships. She is an active member of the Sydney CBD Rotary Club and a Senior Leader for Robbins Research International.
