Ian Berry CSP FAIM’s Summary

I inspire, influence, and encourage people from all walks of life to do today what others will only be thinking about tomorrow.

In the business world I work with leaders and managers to increase the percentage of their employees fully engaged in their work as well as to deliver value to all their stakeholders that they demand, desire, and feel they deserve.

I am an Instigator and have relationships with many colleagues who are Implementers and thereby am able to work in the short and long term with clients wishing to build and grow businesses that are good for people, our planet, and for profit.

My passion is helping people to do good and therefore do better in their lives and businesses.

If you
believe and are acting on your belief that doing good is good for business,
believe doing good is good for business, you are just looking for ways to take action, or
you want to believe doing good is good for business,
then you are potentially my client.

I have unique expertise in performance partnering with people to turn

a) Vision into a compelling story
b) Mission into a cause beyond profit
c) Values into virtues

and how to

d) Guarantee the effectiveness of performance management systems (i.e. recruitment, induction, engagement, appreciation, accountability, retention, and succession),

I also find the experts most appropriate for you to work with you to build a profitable and sustainable business.

I am often referred to as a visionary contrarian.

I am open to building business relationships of high value and mutual reward with anyone passionate about corporate social responsibility/sustainability/doing good.
