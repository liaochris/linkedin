Valadi K Jagannathan’s Summary

Jags design has been working on several areas of Design, related to communication and Internet areas. Started as a product design outfit, We have worked on Furniture to consumer durable product ideas. We worked on applications research of Sheeting based raw materials and point of purchase systems for a few clients. Of late we have been working with Web design, Animation, print design and multi media interface areas. We had a stint in Packaging, Retail and promotional designs, Point of purchase and dispensers, Gifting and Novelty solutions, sculpting services to name a few.
