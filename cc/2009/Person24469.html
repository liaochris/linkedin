David Mullen’s Summary

My goal is to help connect brands with their key customers in meaningful ways. That includes using a blend of traditional and non-traditional marketing channels to create integrated communications efforts that present clear, consistent, compelling messages to customers.

I believe a marketing agency should work to become indispensable to the brands it represents. It should serve as trusted counsel and constantly bring new, game-changing ideas to the table.
