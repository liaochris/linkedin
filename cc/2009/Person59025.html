Gary Wallis’s Summary

Liberty Opportunity Group works with insurance and financial services organizations handling retained and contingency search assignments throughout the United States. We are dedicated to identifying, evaluating, recruiting and delivering the highest-caliber Insurance and Financial Services professionals. We're talking about Insurance and Financial Services executives who can step into any position and begin contributing to your company immediately. Whether you need to find a high caliber CEO or to build an entire team virtually overnight; Liberty Opportunity Group is the answer to your Insurance and Financial Services executive search needs. We connect time-strapped employers with talented candidates.


Liberty Opportunity Group delivers talented human capital that is not currently seeking new opportunities because they are too busy succeeding in their current positions. Over the years, we have acquired an expansive understanding of the dynamics involved with attracting quality executives. We understand your need to generate revenue through senior-level professionals, and we know how to identify key leadership characteristics in such individuals before we show them to your door.
