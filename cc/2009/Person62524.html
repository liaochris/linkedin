Marcus Price’s Summary

I am Managing Principal of Agilier Ltd. Agilier is the agile business change consultancy to help you identify and solve the business issues that are stopping you reach your full potential. Our Agile Business Change approach delivers a prompt, controlled and manageable change process ensuring we deliver business value early and often.

Our services are bespoke, tailored to provide you with a solution for change, a solution for integration and a solution for the future - with particular expertise in business process change, IT integration and IT project turnaround.

We deliver the advice, support and development you need to the highest standards. We work with Private and Public Sectors, including Central Government, Defence and Financial bodies, who remain confident in our ability to deliver an exacting, focused and expert solution that provides the greatest possible return on investment.

Agilier’s internal processes and procedures are certified to IS09001:2000 standard. This award assures that we meet the highest independent standards and ensures that we are able offer our customers the most efficient service delivery.

Please visit www.agilier.com/contact to keep in touch via our free monthly email newsletter.

Agile methods (Lean, DSDM, Scrum, XP), Joint Application Development (JAD), workshop facilitation, requirements gathering, stakeholder alignment, consensus building, process mapping, gap analysis, planning, risk management, reporting, budgeting, change management, supplier management, test strategies.
