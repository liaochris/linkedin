Judy Irving’s Summary

Strong and inspiring leader and business owner who bring 20 plus years of experinence to assist you in business development, building empowered and effective teams and being a distinctive leader. Exceptional communication skills which combined with universal principles and personal insights enables Irving to assist her clients in shifting perspectives. Different perspectives = different choices = different results. Her Transformative Coaching uses both coaching skills, structured business systems and proven models to take your and your company to the next level.

Hands-on leader with experience in motivating and engaging employees to full potential and effectiveness. Direct, yet compassionate, Irving walks her talk and sets a high standard as a role model in her industry. She says, " I love working with men and women who are up to something, ready to play a bigger game and realize that today's world, takes strong leaders, tomorrow's will take even stronger."
