Corinne McKay’s Summary

I provide high-quality French to English translation services, mainly in the fields of law, corporate communications and international development. Some of my recent projects include:
-French to English translation of a contract for health center funding in Rwanda
-French to English translation of an energy company's sustainable development charter
-French to English translation of sales and technical documents for a hemodialysis water purifier
-French to English translation of software licensing agreements.

My clients include law firms, international development agencies, public health institutes and translation companies. I also wrote the book "How to Succeed as a Freelance Translator," ranked among Amazon.com's top 100 Job and Career Guides. I am the current President of the Colorado Translators Association and a past Administrator of the American Translators Association's French Language Division.
