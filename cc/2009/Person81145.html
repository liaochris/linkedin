About Me....

I am a technology strategist and business leader possessing tactician chops to back it up... and the frequent flier miles to show for it. My professional life is marked with senior roles at companies where I straddle the line of development, operations, and rewarding travel to both close business and revisit my customers.

It's been a great ride so far.

Most recently, I was CTO of Digitel and traveled all over the US with a strong focus in the Southeast. Previously, I was CTO for NeoNova (an Azure Capital and Bridgescale Partners portfolio company) which took me to various trade events and RLEC customers all over the rural US. My years consulting at Scient (formerly iXL) gave me the chance to work both domestically and internationally with Fortune 500 companies and .com's.

Yes, I get around. (see my TripIt schedule for details)

Presently, I am based in Research Triangle Park, North Carolina in the East and Missoula, Montana in the West.

As you might imagine, I am also actively adding new clients for 2010 that benefit from my expertise and strategic technology services.

My typical roles are as follows: Interim CEO, Interim CIO, Interim CTO, Thought Leader, Technical PM, Strategy Consultant, Strategist, Evangelist, Technical Advisor

My ideal settings are as follows: Strategic leadership role in emerging technology or telecom oriented entrepreneurial setting. Location and travel terms are fully negotiable. Rate card available upon request.

Feel free to contact me via InMail or through introduction (preferred) and all opportunities are considered on a case by case basis.

Please visit my website to learn more.
