Steven Groves’s Summary

Really enjoy sharing my experiences in developing innovative programs for companies wanting to leverage social media.

As the Chief Evangelist and Co-Founder of TheSocialmediaBible.com, I am always looking out for the latest / greatest in social media and ANY conversation on the ROI and benefits.

Examples include -
* Strategies for using social media to connect to customers, prospects and stakeholders
* Development and deployment of blogs, videoblogs, microblogs, podcasts and social networks for niche business
* Co-founder of WorldWebinarNetwork.com and instrumental in getting the network from zero to over 100 members in three weeks
* Corporate Fundraising Chair for American Cancer Society Relay for Life for Second Life 2008-2009

Active in the entrepreneurial scene in Arizona -
* Committee Chair - Arizona Entrepreneurship Conference 2006, 2007 & 2008
* Guest Presenter on Marketing - Kauffman FastTrac Training in Phoenix, & Gilbert 2006, 2007 & 2008
* Guest Presenter on Marketing at ASU / Barretts Honors College 2006 & 2007
* InvestSouthwest Presentation Mentoring Team Member 2007

My real passion is working on projects to identify, develop and execute programs to create revenue streams, structure successful business development efforts, and develop finance-able business projects.

I've worked with many small to medium businesses and provided business development, marketing and sales consulting in most of my engagements.
