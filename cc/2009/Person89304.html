Scot Duke’s Summary

My Mission is to use my 32 years of business operations management experience in mentoring business people to accomplishing their goals.

I have a true passion for the game of golf. I also have a tremendous desire to help the golf industry improve by showing more business people how productive GOLF can be. My goal is to show how golf can be used as a powerful business tool.

My mantra is to "Keep It Simple to Get Things Done!". I am inspired by the "Big Ideas" and the people that dream them.

I choose to ONLY work with honorable individuals who believe in making a buck while making a difference.

UPDATE: I am expanding my efforts to include Remodeling Consulting service. My new division of IBGS will help homeowners make an informed decision on remodeling.
