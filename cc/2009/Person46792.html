Jessica Mellott is a teen pop/rock singer, songwriter and actress . The 2006 Los Angeles Music Awards "Pop Female Singer/Songwriter of the Year" began her vocal instruction when she was only five years old. Her training includes classical music, jazz, musical theater and contemporary pop.

Jessica's compelling voice, songwriting skills and magnetic personality have attracted tremendous attention. Her song "Life Grabs You" is featured in an Indie film. On Myspace.com Jessica received so many song plays she was selected to be in a book about Myspace. Jessica received 850,000+ plays when she was highlighted at Zorpia.com. At Myxer.com her ringtone was downloaded 6,000+ times in the 24 hours it was spotlighted. She is currently featured on Snapvine.com (5 million+ users) and other websites.

Jessica is also an accomplished actress who boasts both starring and supporting roles in shows ranging from "The Secret Garden" to "Working". In 2005, Jessica earned a certificate in Musical Theater from the respected New York Arts Camp Stagedoor Manor.

Jessica volunteers her vocal talents frequently to help the community. Her performances have ranged from entertaining the armed forces to shows that have helped raise thousands of dollars for the public school system.

Currently Jessica attends Berklee College of Music, gigs solo and with the Jessica Mellott Band and collaborates on music projects with music professionals all over the world.

Whether she is singing a gripping ballad or high energy song, Jessica captivates the crowd and has gained a world wide fan base. Get the latest news, listen to her music and view more photos at her websites.
