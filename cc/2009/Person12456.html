Kim Shanahan’s Summary

Welcome! Since 1999 Book Bouquet has been bringing the joys of good books and beautiful baskets to the nation.

Book Bouquet's founder and president, Kim Shanahan, conceived of Book Bouquet when she wanted to cheer up a sick friend in the hospital.

Books were just right to relieve her friend's boredom, but with her then two year old son at her side the thought of going to a bookstore, gift store and post office had Kim wishing for an easier and faster way! The lightbulb went off and Book Bouquet was born.

Our very first gift basket was the Boredom Buster get well gift. It is still one of our most popular gifts.

We continue to create unusual gifts featuring great books. Book Bouquet now offers gifts for all occasions, and fulfills our vision of beautiful ways to send books for gifts.

From our bestselling Baby Gift Baskets and Kids Boredom Busters to our distinct Business Gifts, you'll find the right gift at Book Bouquet for everyone on your shopping list.
