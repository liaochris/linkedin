Dave Saunders’s Summary

A results driven, decisive and self-starting visionary leader with domestic and international management experience in finance and operations in environments including: manufacturing, technology, consumer products, professional services, managed services and software.

Accomplished executive with proven success in building talented teams, business transformation and problem solving and a proven track record of driving profitable growth and profit enhancement. A strong business partner to general management, sales, marketing and operations – having partnered with and delivered results across all management functions to drive profitable growth and market expansion, create business alliances, establish and scale operations and reduce costs. Passionate about aligning teams, processes, information and analytics with strategy and embedding finance teams into business operations to provide leadership and independent perspective, guide decisions and create value.

An individual balanced in business, leadership and management expertise with strong technical competencies and educational credentials. Thrives in dynamic, fast paced entrepreneurial environments with the ability to think in multiple dimensions, operate at a macro or micro level at will and bring direction and leadership to complex and ambiguous business issues.
