Charlene (Charly) Leetham’s Summary

I have over 20 Years experience in IT, ranging from Field Service to Contract & Business Management. I also have extensive Business Management experience having owned my own businesses for the past 6 years.

I am a natural educator and have experience in training and coaching others to overcome their technology challenges.

As the Training Manager for Tuggeranong Dog Training Club (Canberra, ACT) for several years, I managed the team that wrote and implemented the Instructor Training Manual, conducted Instructor Training Courses and oversaw the quality of the training provided.

Today, using my unique blend of business and technical skills, I provide services to solopreneurs and small business who wish to sell or promote their products and services online. I provide a full range of services from Small Business Coaching, to Website Design, Product Creation and Packaging, Marketing and Traffic Generation, I have the skills and resources to help you.

I specialize in using Wordpress as a content management system - whilst I realize there are other platforms available that perform similar functions, I have found the flexibility and the wide range of support available for Wordpress to be second to none.

You can receive my weekly newsletter, where I share my experiences and advice on how you can improve your online presence by visiting http://askcharlyleetham.com/ and completing the form in the top right hand corner.
