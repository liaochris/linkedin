Steve Tennant’s Summary

I help software and Internet company CEOs grow by understanding market needs and creating solutions that address them better than anyone else. I work in person with companies in the San Francisco Bay Area, Silicon Valley, Oakland and virtually with companies around the world.

Get my newsletter with tips to grow your company at www.tennantconsulting.com/newsletter.html

Follow on Twitter at www.twitter.com/stevetennant

I work with companies at all stages of development, from startups to Fortune 50. For startups, I have a roadmap to take the company from concept to customer, including raising venture capital. For larger companies, we help them manage product, marketing and alliance operations. For more information, visit www.tennantconsulting.com.

Founded the PeopleSoft Alumni Network, a corporate alumni group of 3700+ former PeopleSoft employees (www.psftalumni.net).

Frequent new product and entrepreneurship speaker in the Bay Area, including UC Berkeley's Lester Center for Entrepreneurship, Stanford's Continuing Studies program, Product Management Camp, Startup Camp, and Technology Venture's Entrepreneurship Training Program. Work quoted or featured in the Wall Street Journal, Entrepreneur Magazine, The Industry Standard, Business Week Online, CIO Magazine, all the major Bay Area papers and many trade and industry publications.
