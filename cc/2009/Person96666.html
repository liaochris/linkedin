D.J. Smith’s Experience

    HealthCampNash

    (Hospital & Health Care industry)

    January 2009 — Present (11 months)

    HealthCampNash is a free, user-generated “unconference” whose goal is to explore, understand, and affect the changing world of healthcare – from shifting methodologies to the implications of Health 2.0 and everything in between. HealthCampNash brings together a diverse group of people to share ideas and challenge one another to make positive change happen in the healthcare industry.

    HealthCampNash is a spin-off of the highly successful BarCamp
