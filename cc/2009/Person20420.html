Corey Lavinsky’s Summary

I have recently accepted a position as Senior Analyst at PIRA Energy Group. These are very exciting times for the energy industry and I am glad to be on board with the premier consulting firm specializing in global energy market analysis.

I was previously the CEO of Growthink Research, a prominent venture capital research firm. Growthink Research provides leads, competitive intelligence, custom reporting, and dealflow to entrepreneurs looking for capital, venture capital firms, and investment banks. We also license our extensive online content.

If you are a professional in the biofuels or VC/PE industries, or an entrepreneur looking for funding, please feel free to email me at corey@growthinkresearch.com.

Corey Lavinsky’s Specialties:

(1) Providing research and analysis on the biofuels industry, including weekly, monthly and special reports on ethanol, biodiesel, global mandates, pending legislation, activity in developing countries, new technologies, reduction in greenhouse gases (GHG), etc.

(2) Working with entrepreneurs looking to raise venture capital.

(3) Working with venture capital firms, private equity firms, corporate investors, and investment bankers looking for quality dealflow.

Corey Lavinsky’s Experience

  • Sr. Analyst, Biofuels

    PIRA Energy Group

    (Think Tanks industry)

    April 2009 — Present (4 months)

    PIRA Energy Group, founded in 1976, is an international energy consulting firm specializing in global energy market analysis and intelligence. PIRA's Global Biofuels Service provides an in-depth analysis of the commercial markets for bioethanol in the U.S. and Brazil, which account for about 90% of global bioethanol production. Other countries/regions with active bioethanol programs are also covered, including China, Europe, Canada, Colombia, Argentina, South Africa, India, Thailand, Indonesia, Australia and New Zealand.

    Further, the service covers in detail the biodiesel industry in Western Europe — which accounts for 80% of global biodiesel production — and in the U.S. Regular analysis of other countries that have active biodiesel programs, such as China, Brazil, Poland, Bulgaria, Slovenia, South Africa, Indonesia, Malaysia, South Korea, Singapore, the Philippines and India, is also provided.

    June 2000 — Present (9 years 2 months)

Companies seeking capital, venture capital firms, investment bankers, and professional service providers rely on Growthink Research to raise capital, identify and secure new clients, and gain competitive intelligence.
