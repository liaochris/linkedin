Lu-Ann Fuentes’s Summary

As Editorial and Communication Services Director at Ogilvy Public Relations, Lu-Ann develops communication strategies to drive design of information/media campaigns that achieve client objectives.
 
She has been providing editorial services (involving marketing support, account management , internal and external communication) for two decades—spanning different environments from corporate affairs to advocacy/non-profit, from journalism and media production to public relations work.
 
Before joining Ogilvy, her last two professional engagements were: as Editorial Director generating print and online content for a U.S.-based publishing company, and as Corporate Communication Officer at San Miguel Corporation, the largest publicly listed food, beverage and packaging company in the Philippines. For the latter, she logged in seven years of providing publicity support and account servicing for SMC’s various business units—San Miguel Beer, San Miguel Purefoods Corporation, San Miguel Packaging Products, and Ginebra San Miguel, Inc. This included producing SMC’s monthly corporate publication (which won three Anvil Awards of Excellence during her term as editor-in-chief) as a group-wide communication tool, extending the reach of corporate and brand image-building.

She holds a Bachelor of Arts in Communication, major in Journalism, from the University of the Philippines, Diliman.
