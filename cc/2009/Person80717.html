Ms. Treyce DGabriel’s Summary

I have over 22 years of experience in both Forensic Handwriting Analysis and Handwriting Formation Therapy. I have been featured on radio & television talk shows and in written media. I am an internationally recognized trainer for agencies such as the Intl Assn Law Enfmt Intel Analysts, many Correctional agencies, Great Lakes Gang Investigators Assn and more. I also offer online classes, books and services for individuals, corporations, and agencies.

I am a published author of "Answers!", "Written Violence - the Personality Behind the Pen", "The Only HWA Book You Will Ever Need", "Case Files", & more. My background experience includes working within law enforcement genres including as a correctional offcr, academy instructor, FTO, P.I., Fugitive Recovery Agent & field supervisor for a national investigative agency. I have worked on many high profile cases including cases for Streetcats Int'l, 4theKids, Lost Child Netwk, Missing/Exploited Children & Missing Kin.

I have worked on employee screening, school safety, violence potential, psychological autopsies, progression of suicide, profiles of Scott Peterson, Michael Jackson, etc. I have solved forgeries, identified authors of anonymous notes, uncovered lies, solved employee theft, and worked international murder & kidnapping cases. Other cases include telling a woman her husband could brutally murder her & a year later - he did; another when I warned that a sheriff deputy was very dangerous & could kill someone - 3 years later he shot his girlfriend in the head; another when I stated a Homicide Investigator should not be trusted around children - a year later he was arrested for 2 counts of Child Battery.
