Alejandro Pisanty’s Summary

Well-versed in matters related to IT, the Internet, and the Information Society, with cross-layer knowledge and experience. Alejandro Pisanty brings together technology, policy, strategy, and education at the local, national and international level. A good public speaker and a team player

Experience:
Trustee of the Internet Society (ISOC) and head of ISOC Mexico
Director at the Internet Corporation for Assigned Names and Numbers, ICANN
CIO of the National University of Mexico (Universidad Nacional Autonoma de Mexico), UNAM
Head of Open and Distance Education, UNAM
Have managed supercomputing, Internet services, data and voice networks, education, training, a systems unit, Web-based services (portals, pages, etc.), e-learning, videoconferencing.

Alejandro Pisanty’s Specialties:
