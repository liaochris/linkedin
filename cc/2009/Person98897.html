Tim Kassouf’s Summary

I'm currently the Marketing Manager for G.1440.

G.1440 provides IT Solutions that enable our customers to achieve their business goals. With a focus on serving the IT needs of any sized business, G.1440 provides both top-notch IT talent AND top-tier IT & web consulting solutions.

G.1440 Staffing Services provide talent on a staffing, contract-to-hire, or full-time employment model, and the G.1440 Consulting Services team of IT Design and Development professionals work side-by-side with clients to produce customized Web-based business applications and effective Web presence.

With expertise that spans the Java and Microsoft .Net worlds, G.1440 will craft the right IT solution for each and every customer's unique needs. Combining the capabilities of our established teams of IT consultants while utilizing IT "free agents" (via our Staffing Services Division) creates the right team to get the job done at the right price.

IT exists for one reason: to enhance a business' capability to achieve their stated business goals.

G.1440 means winning in business.
