Alan Kravitz’s Summary

As a copywriter who specializes in helping socially conscious organizations, I know that making the world a better place is a tall order. Many of the organizations I work with have writing/communications staffs, yet they still have more writing projects than they can handle. That's where I come in. The biggest "headaches" I'm asked to solve include writing direct mail, as well as website copy and web articles. Through my editing services, I also help clients gain a fresh perspective on the effectiveness of existing print and/or web copy.

Alan Kravitz’s Specialties:

The organizations I work with have a wide range of writing needs. I help them with: web writing, direct mail campaigns, brochure copy, newsletters (both email and print), annual reports, white papers, articles for media publications and speechwriting.
