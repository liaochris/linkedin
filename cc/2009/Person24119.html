David Gaines’s Summary

Musician with over thirty years of experience as a classically-trained composer, arranger, performer, and music journalist.

I've worked with the Embassy of Afghanistan in Washington, Afghan pop music legend Ehsan Aman, Slovak Radio Symphony Orchestra, conductors Kirk Trevor and Vit Micka, Stamford Young Artists Philharmonic, Bulgarian Esperanto Choir, Master Singers of Virginia, Peabody Wind Ensemble, and the Moravian Philharmonic Orchestra. Commercial classical recordings available on the MMC Recordings and Verda Stelo Music labels.

I've also conducted interviews with Ian Anderson and Martin Barre of Jethro Tull, Daryl Stuermer of Genesis, Michael Dunford of Renaissance, and pianist Liz Story resulting in feature stories published in the Washington Times and other Washington, D.C. area periodicals.

David Gaines’s Specialties:

Music for concert band, wind ensemble, and symphony orchestra; chamber music for low brass instruments & unusual instrumental combinations; electronic music.

Multi-keyboardist with experience in rock, pop, wedding, ska, and regaae bands.

Karaoke KJ specializing in providing karaoke at private parties and events.
