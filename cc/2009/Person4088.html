Allison Douglas’s Summary

With a diverse 20-year career in all aspects of
customs brokerage, and import export solutions,
Allison Douglas has proven to be a skilled
executive, trained professional, a detailed
trouble-shooter and turnaround expert. She
has worked in various aspects of the industry,
including tarrif classification, customs documentation,
and release procedures,programs for non-resident importers, as
well as developing and training her client’s staff with the program
process. Her specialty is working with US based companies to expand
and streamline their business into the Canadian Market.
Allison currently holds her Certified Customs Specialist Designation
(CCS) and previously was a Qualified Customs broker with the Canadian
Society of Customs Brokers.
