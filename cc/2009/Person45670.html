Jeff Ruday’s Summary

I'm driven to use technology to enhance personal and team productivity.

At the Hoop Dreams Scholarship Fund I developed and implemented systems around traditional business software and other innovative tools, including web based applications, to automate repetitive tasks, increase accuracy, enhance attention to detail and streamline the planning, production and operation of special events.

In today's online environment it is no longer enough to expect people to come to your homepage or blog. Today it's about the distribution of information through social networks to create a conversation, not a monologue.

SUMMARY

Self-motivated, detail oriented and flexible business professional with 10 years of non profit finance and operations experience, and over 30 years of special event planning, management and production experience.

PROFESSIONAL OBJECTIVE

To obtain a full time position or consulting assignment that will allow me to use my organizational and technology skills to enhance team collaboration and performance, and streamline the planning and production of special events.

SUMMARY OF QUALIFICATIONS

Skilled special event professional with extensive experience managing large, outdoor, public events that include the closing of public streets and commercial parking areas.

Assisted with the planning, development and launch, in the 1990s, of Hoop-It-Up, a national grass roots sporting event associated with the NBA and NBC Sports, and produced in over 50 U. S. cities and overseas.

Assisted with the development of a Washington, DC non profit from an all volunteer effort to a 2 million dollar organization. Provided finance, administration, HR and IT support for a team of ten.

For 10 years organized and produced one of Washington DC's best known events, the Hoop Dreams Scholarship Fund 3-on-3 Basketball Tournament and Community Festival.
