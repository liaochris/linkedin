Teresa Quilty’s Summary

I am a leadership development professional with particular strengths in development strategy, program design, interpersonal communication, coaching and facilitation. My passion is helping individuals and organizations succeed by building on their inherent greatness. My interest is in contributing my strategic, development and creative skills in a progressive, collaborative environment that supports continual learning and growth.

As a seasoned professional with a background in education, non-profit, consulting and corporate sectors, I joined Cameco Corporation in 2006, where I now lead the company’s leadership development strategy, programs and services. Cameco is a leading global supplier of uranium to produce clean air energy.

I hold a graduate certificate in Evidence Based Coaching from Fielding Graduate University, an MBA with a Specialization in Leadership from Royal Roads University, as well as undergraduate degrees in Arts and Education from the University of Western Ontario. I am a Certified Management Consultant, a member of International Coach Federation, an advanced Toastmaster and am certified in EQ in Action and MBTI assessment instruments.

A mother of three, I find meaning and joy in my family, practicing art, spirituality and creative living, learning, and active connection with nature.
