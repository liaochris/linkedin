Talmadge Zipperer’s Summary

I served a total of 6 years in the Military as a combat engineer. During my time in the service I was activated twice to include a deployment overseas. In 2005, I served as team leader of the lead vehicle as we conducted convoy operations missions in Iraq. During the final few months of our deployment I served as the control center NCOIC at Abu Ghraib prison.

After returning in 2006, I worked to finish my degree in Public Relations and Marketing at Northern Kentucky University. I then accepted a full time position at an advertising firm in which I had completed an internship prior to graduation. In 2007 I received my first opportunity to work in recruiting. After a few years of valuable experience in the traditional recruiting arena I came into contact with RecruitMilitary.

I was very fortunate to connect with RecruitMilitary. Our organization has provided me the opportunity use the knowledge I gained in the recruiting industry and apply it to my passion for helping fellow Veterans. I now find myself in a position to help Veterans get connected with opportunities that I know through my own personal experiences, that they have unquestionably earned.

Talmadge Zipperer’s Specialties:

Helping organizations across all industries connect to men and women whom possess military backgrounds through Professional Services, Online Databases, Electronic Marketing Tools, Opportunity Expos, Advertising (Print and Electronic) and social networking.
