Jim O'Neill’s Summary

30 years experience in broadcast operations, management, syndication, on-air and creative production. Created nationally syndicated progam targeting the trucking industry which led to the development of the first online recruitment service for professional drivers, 1-800-DRIVERS.

Extensive background in traffic management center operations, 511 deployments, data fusion, traffic monitoring, gathering and dissemination technologies.

Over the years I have developed an extremely strong creative network with limitless production capabilities. Today, I am using my skills & experience and drawing on that network when needed for the benefit of my clients...and having a blast.

Jim O'Neill’s Specialties:

Crafting creative solutions to marketing, advertising and branding challenges.

Broad base of hands on business experience in broadcasting, syndication, program and product development and transportation operations.
