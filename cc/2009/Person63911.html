Marta Strickland’s Summary

Marta is a digital enthusiast from birth and grew up on the dunes of Lake Michigan before joining big city life at Columbia College Chicago. She graduated with a BA in Film and Interactive Multimedia. Her previous work experience includes freelance, teaching, and four years as an Interactive Strategist / Producer at Q LTD in Ann Arbor, a strategic design consultancy.

Marta joined Organic in May 2007 and has been working for strategy for Chrysler, Dodge, and Geek Squad. Her specialties include social media strategy and mapping emerging web trends. She frequently voices her mind on the Organic blog, ThreeMinds.

During her free time, she enjoys travel, cooking, wine, and filmmaking with her husband and family.
