Shawn Callahan’s Summary

Management consultant who has been designing and implementing organisational change initiatives for the past 15 years. Specific skills and interest in anthropological techniques such as the collection and interpretation of workplace stories.

Was a core member of the Cynefin Centre, an international group that applies complexity principles to tackle intractable organisation issues.

Both a researcher and hands-on practitioner who enjoys spanning the divide between consulting and academia.

Recent projects include issues of trust, aging workforce, narrative representation, knowledge strategy, lessons learned, communities of practice, knowledge mapping and intellectual capital management.

Co-founder of the ActKM, the largest community of practice for public sector knowledge managers. Co-founder of Worldwide Story Work, a community of practice for story practitioners.

Shawn Callahan’s Specialties:

knowledge strategy, change management, narrative techniques, collaboration, communities of practice, expertise location, change management, culture change
