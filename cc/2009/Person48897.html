John Aylward’s Summary

A self-motivated and results orientated client-facing consultant with excellent communication and presentation skills. I have a wide range of experience in architecting and developing web-based applications based around both relational and free-text database systems.
I have particular experience in creating document, knowledge and information management solutions as well as e-business technologies and methodologies. Roles have been across the full project life-cycle, encompassing client liaison, project definition, creation and deployment phases.
Having originally trained as a lawyer and being a qualified pensions professional together with project management, consultancy and sales roles in my background, I feel I am particularly qualified to understand all the commercial aspects of implementing information management solutions.
