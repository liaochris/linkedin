Debra Beach’s Summary

My work in education and the visual communications industry has spanned over 20 years. During that time I directed, managed and executed a number of projects for companies and organizations including: Design, illustration, web development, photography & videoography, education & training, and the development of marketing approaches. This entailed directing, managing, designing and executing projects including signage, publications, logos, outdoor, and web. I have managed a Technology Enhanced Classroom Support Program at a university, currently direct the instructional design at Tiffin University, and continue as an instructional design and multimedia consultant for Flugga-Beach LLC

My research interests include ubiquitous computing, or the use of portable devices that can be used for education anytime or anywhere the learner goes. I have a passion for creating learning environments and tools that meet learners varied learning styles.

I enjoy researching and writing about a variety of subjects related to education, technology, creativity, and design on my blog, e-Debra: An Online Resource.
