Matthew Dorchester, DC, CCSP, LLC’s Summary

Matthew Dorchester, DC, CCSP, LLC has been caring for Scottsdale and Phoenix residents for over 20 years. The practice is known for treating a variety of conditions that include the neck, shoulder, chest, arm, back, hands, feet, knees, and hips. The facility's doctors treat the cause of symptoms related to hand numbness, foot numbness, back pain, headaches, weight gain, sleeplessness, lethargy, and decreased sex drive. We care for acute injuries like strain, and sprain. We treat injuries casued by yoga, bicycles, motorcycles, sports, automobiles, and work. Our history includes the treatment of professional athletes asscociated with PRCA, NFL, NHL, NBA, ATP, and AVP. Bio - Identical Hormone Replacement Therapy (BHRT) for men and women is the fastest growing department. Testosterone therapy for both men and women is very popular and in demand.
