Wendy Ackland’s Summary

With more than 10 years experience in professional marketing practice, my background is varied and crosses several industries.

Most notably, I have been employed as Marketing Strategist with News Limited's The Cairns Post (where I discovered my passion for analytics), and was first introduced to media as Sales and Promotions Coordinator for the TEN Network, based in Cairns.

I have gained extensive public relations and community experience in the Not-For-Profit and Health sectors, and managed the sales process for the Cairns port of Australian air Express (a national logistics company).

I now own and operate BurntToast® Marketing, an agency that specialises in marketing strategy and data interpretation for small business. The business was founded in 2007 and provides tailored and professional marketing services to a range of industries.
