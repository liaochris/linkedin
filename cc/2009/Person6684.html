Anne Lackey’s Summary

Anne was born in Georgia, but has lived up and down the east coast. She has a background in technology sales, marketing & public relations. Her last position was as Vice President—Sales for a local technology company which she left to pursue real estate full time.

Anne’s role is to provide Investing and Property Management solutions for our customers across the nation and manages the day to day operations of Solid Source Property Management which includes working with investors and Tenants to provide winning solutions for all parties.

Anne was selected for the Atlanta Board of REALTORS® Leadership Development Program—2007.

Anne & Her Husband Mark do a lot together -

Both Mark and Anne are licensed Real Estate Agents and are seasoned Real Estate Investors. They personally have over 20 investment properties throughout Georgia, Florida, & Alabama. They are active in their church, the community and real estate investment groups like Georgia Real Estate Investor Association (GaREIA), North Metro REiA, and IRCA. They co-lead geographic subgroup from 2004—2006 and still are active in that group to continually gain knowledge of trends and to learn from other successful investors.

They speak approximately 80 times a year to other investment groups and assists other Investors in building their portfolio wisely through acquisition of rental real estate. They co-hosted “The Real Estate Investing Hour” every Saturday morning for two years, providing information about real estate trends and education. You can listen to their shows at http://www.TheREiHour.com.

Anne & Mark also taught Real Estate Investment classes at Barney Fletcher Realty U for investors wanting to learn more about how to get started.
