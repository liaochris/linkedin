Kathleen Nickerson, PhD’s Summary

Dr. Kathy Nickerson, a relationship expert who helps couples reconnect and repair their marriages, has been quoted in Woman’s World, Forbes Woman, Woman’s Day, Glamour UK, and the LA Times....and that’s just last week.

Dr. Nickerson is a founding member of FeelBetterNetwork.com and—along with her passion for helping people—brings more than 20 years professional mental health counseling experience. As a licensed clinical psychologist, she has an extensive background in various areas including anxiety, addiction, domestic violence, and depression, but today she focuses mainly on relationship counseling through her California-based private practice and globally on FeelBetterNetwork.com. She also maintains a year-round schedule speaking at more than 150 conferences and broadcast media outlets, and has authored or co-authored more than 75 professional articles and books including:

•The Everything Book – Defiant Kids
•Speaking Up: How to Help Children Who Live In Abusive Homes
•Save The Date: A Curriculum for Safe & Healthy Teen Dating
•Be Your Own Shrink: 10 Steps to Keep You Happy and Healthy
•Let's Stay Together: The Essential Guide for Strengthening & Repairing Your Relationships

As an accomplished writer with a passion for teaching couples how to strengthen and repair their relationships, Kathy frequently contributes to national and internal publications. Dr. Kathy's work, comments, and insights have been featured in the following magazines and websites:

In addition to working in private practice, Kathy teaches courses for mental health professionals at the California Institute for Professional Continuing Education.

Kathleen Nickerson, PhD’s Honors:

As an expert counselor, I love to teach people how to feel more confident, worry less, and be happier. In recent years, I have had the privilege of speaking at more than 100 local and national conferences and training programs. I have also been a featured guest on numerous local radio and television programs. In addition to speaking, I enjoy writing and have written over 45 professional publications, including three books on living your best life.

I am a licensed clinical psychologist (PSY 20446) who does local counseling (within California) and coaching (outside of California, by phone and internet). I received both my doctorate (PhD) and masters (MS) degree in psychology from Capella University in Minneapolis. My undergraduate education (BS) was in chemistry and physics at UC Irvine.

When I am not working, I enjoy spending time with my husband and my bulldog, Piglet. I love to walk on our beautiful beaches, paint watercolor landscapes, read, write, and watch old movies.
