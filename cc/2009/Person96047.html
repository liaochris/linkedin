Susan Morgan’s Summary

Creative, organized, research-loving writer with 23 years professional writing experience — everything from get-results direct mail pieces and thorough technical manuals to eye-catching marketing brochures and sharply worded editorial. Recently I've been doing a good deal of online work, and have started my own blog at http://smm110861.wordpress.com/ to capture and make accessible some of that wide-ranging and always original content.

You should also know that I enjoy the challenge of research, put time, attention and effort into all my projects (large or small), have an eye for detail and deliver content that is on target, on time and ready to use. I take pride in my work, and if we get the chance to work together you'll see this for yourself, first hand. Of course, I'm happy to provide professional references if you like.

You'll find a good deal of my work at http://www.Writing.Com/authors/smm110861. Keep checking back too, I'm adding more all the time!

Beyond writing to pay the rent, a lifetime love of fiction has produced 2 novels (Out of the Ordinary published in 2007), 4 short stories (an award winner in 2004, another in 2008) and a number of tart (and politically incorrect) opinion pieces and blog posts.
