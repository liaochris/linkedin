Lorna Lardizabal Dietz’s Summary

“Living My Life As An Experiment”

May 26, 2009

Starting at 10 years old, Lorna Lardizabal Dietz learned entrepreneurship skills at her family’s home bakeshop — Sally’s Home Bake Shop — in Cebu City, Philippines, giving her a “domestic goddess” status in baking. Her B.S. Hotel & Restaurant Administration degree from the University of the Philippines proved to be a worthy stepping stone to opportunities in teaching, consulting, public speaking, and creative writing. Lorna’s stint as the department head of the B.S. Hotel & Restaurant Administration program at Southwestern University for 4-1/2 years, right after college, prepared her for leadership and operational qualities needed to manage detail-oriented activities.

A management, marketing, and sales background in specialty retail, furniture leasing, corporate relocation, staging homes for sale, insurance, credit and financial solutions, real estate and mortgage marketing, special event and workshop facilitation, and advertising & circulation in a mainstream business journal and business development in ethnic media publications (Filipinas Magazine, KabariNews.com, and Amritsar Times and World Sikh News for the Filipino, Indonesian, and Punjabi American communities) make Lorna a valuable networking and strategic business consulting resource.

Although she regrets giving up her Securities Series 7 license and her California insurance producer's licenses in property & casualty, life & health, and long-term care, Lorna currently works on marketing assignments for April Insurance Agency (ThinkApril.com). She is currently training to be a certified Clear Credit Exchange consultant and a seminar speaker in credit empowerment so she can support April Financial, Inc. and Intero Real Estate San Francisco Sunset’s services, among others.

Learn more about Lorna Dietz at her websites (where she houses her blogs) at www.RadiantView.com, DiabetesLifeNotes.blogspot.com, FilipinaImages.com, and NaFFAAR8.com.
