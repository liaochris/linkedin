Ian Truscott’s Summary

A driven software company leader with over a decade of enterprise software experience; as a CTO, in product marketing, product development, sales and consulting - from starting my career as a computer operator and UNIX administrator.

During this time I have taken various products to market, engaging with a broad range of organisations (including McDonalds, Diageo, AstraZeneca, WWE and Glaxo) and large central government departments while living and working in the USA and UK.

A strong web content management pedigree, having focused on web technologies for the majority of the last ten years, working with some of the major vendors and pioneers in this area.
