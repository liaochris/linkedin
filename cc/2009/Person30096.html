Elizabeth Barron’s Summary

I am an administrator with the richness of knowledge and experience needed to effectively manage an organization beyond being sustainable, into thriving. Prior to holding an accountant/ tax preparation/ auditing position at a public accounting firm, I had been a business valuation/litigation support (bv/ls) services specialist and the professional development coordinator in the same CPA firm. I have essentially managed mini-startups in the areas of professional development management and in the bv/ls niche, remaining highly flexible in order to always most effectively serve the firm and its changing needs.

With every position I have held, I have focused on developing the strong managerial, relationship development and financial and accounting skills necessary for practical, effective and forward-looking leadership. I am deeply committed to consciously contributing to the greater common good, and seek employment that supports that focus.

My passions include:

Effective leadership in consciousness: developing holistic, practical approaches to what is beneficial and profitable for *all* stakeholders.

Professional Development --> meshing life and learning.

Supporting the mutualism of businesses and "non-profits" as the strength of our global social economy.

Grant writing; Community Benefit Sector (Non-profit) development.

Project development and management.

Elizabeth Barron’s Specialties:

Working with Non-Profits on Grant Writing and Development.

Internal team and client communications.

Development, implementation and management of life-learning/ professional development programs.
