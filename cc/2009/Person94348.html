Stephen Fernandez’s Summary

Following his graduation from University Stephen spent 10 years working within the telecommunications and internet fileds much of this time was spent in a Pan-European capacity. More recently following a move to Spain, Stephen has been working for a Marketing Services and Digital Signage company based in Madrid.

Stephen Fernandez’s Specialties:

Key player in major strategic activities. Managing withdrawals from unprofitable markets, including disposal of assets, sale of on-going business units, involving regulatory, contractual and staffing issues.
Rationalised and integrated product set of over 300 services (from acquisitions) down to a profitable 45.
Launched a single Pan-European Internet Access service across 12 countries and 15 autonomous networks (from acquisitions)

Stephen Fernandez’s Experience

  • Product Director

    John Ryan

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    2005 — 2008 (3 years)

    Due to his experience in Strategic Business Development and Business Transformation, Stephen was recruited to develop and implement the strategy needed to move a principally entrepreneurial company onto the next phase of its development, instigating the business practices needed to support its growth into a multinational organisation. Stephen introduced the rigours of product management and a bid review process into the organisation including controls for change management, product development, and support. Over the last year he has managed the launch of a new business model and new channels to Market for continental Europe using VAR partners including companies such as Telefonica, SCC, HP, Reuters and T-systems. As part of this he also developed and launched a new Digital Signage brand in the EMEIA market place, within the first year the brand recognition went from zero to 6th worldwide.

    Stephen formalised and designed the customer service processes and also drove a program to standardise the internal IT structure of the company.
