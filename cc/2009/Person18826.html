Chris Turley’s Summary

Nearly twenty years of design and construction experience creating projects that not only responds to the technical goals of a project but also creates a wonderful place to live and work. We eliminate the obstacles that help our clients fulfill the motto: make room for Your life.

Chris Turley’s Specialties:

Residential projects. Speaker for the American Institute of Architects and the Historic Chicago Bungalow Association. Kitchen and bath designer. Green and Healthy House design as well as promoter of the Not So Big House concept. We work with home owners that want to turn their most valuable asset into something that is not only a great investment but a wonderful place to live their lives, guiding them to good ecological, green design concepts.
