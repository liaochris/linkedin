Carsten Brüggmann’s Summary

I am a passionate and experienced Sound Designer for computer games as well as movies, at the moment working at Replay Studios in Hamburg on a new title, using Unreal3 technology after finishing "Velvet Assassin" as well as another licensed title.

I finished the audio engineer degree at the SAE college in Hamburg/Germany to gain more knowledge about professional music and audio production in a studio environment. (including recording, mixing, editing, session procedures as well as the whole music track production cycle)
