Ahmed Edwards’s Summary

I have comprehensive marketing and technological understanding of several industries including: Digital Out-Of -Home (DOOH) Advertising, Wireless Telecommunications, Alternative Medicine, Biotechnology and Health Care. Experience promoting and marketing in these industries gives me a universal understanding of the human condition and what works in triggering a positive response to marketing campaigns.

I recently developed a concept, IDIS (Integrated Digital Informational Signage), which seeks to blend public safety information and commercial advertising in the digital media format. IDIS is the main focus of Lavads LLC (www.lavads.net).
