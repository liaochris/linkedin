Mary A. C. Fallon’s Summary

Writer and video producer with a 15-year track record helping technology company senior executives change perceptions, held by external audiences and internal stakeholders, to improve image, processes and sales.

Content-developer with history of unraveling complex issues and processes into easy-to-understand stories.

Creator of an award-winning documentary and articles with foundation of 17 years as a journalist for print, radio, TV and Web.

Change agent who successfully partnered with senior executives to execute major shifts in how their operations marketed products and communicated with the media, customers and employees.

Documentary filmmaker passionate about creating positive change.

Mary A. C. Fallon’s Specialties:

Video editing and production, writing about how people use technology and create break-throughs, managing communications teams and projects, breaking news.
