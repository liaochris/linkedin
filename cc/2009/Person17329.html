Cheryl Good’s Summary

Our office has been a leader in the executive search world for almost 10 years, and consistently recognized as one of the top performing offices within the 1,100-office network. This office has achieved “President Club” for performance in the top 10% of all offices.

I try to understand the objective of my client as well as the objective of each candidate I work with. I expect all placements to be long term career moves, and approach the process with this in mind. I believe that networking and communication are the keys to finding the impact player my clients’ desire.

I have almost 15 years of outside sales experience to bring to the table. I know what it takes to be successful in sales coming from that background and can identify Impact Players that will fit your team model.


I focus on Sales, Marketing, Management (Sales up to VP Level), and clinical positions in the Medical and Health Care industry. I only work with companies with a strong reputation and the highest integrity. I work with candidates to understand their career objectives and work to match their objectives with a great company opportunity.

I screen with my clients exact needs in mind. This way I do not waste the time and money of my client, the candidate, and my firm. I primarily conduct all searches from networking. I will make every effort to understand the goals of both the candidate and hiring manager to assure a positive fit in the placement.

I would like the opportunity to speak with you about our services to determine if you have a need where we may be of assistance. Please feel free to contact me with any questions you may have.

Call me at (404)221-1021 (ext. 1194) or send an email to cheri@mriatl.com
