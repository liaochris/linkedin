Mark Buckley’s Summary

After law school, I was a general practice attorney with emphasis in estate planning, liability law, and consumer bankruptcy. I later received my Series 7, Series 66, and insurance licenses to become an estate planning strategist for a high net-worth advisory team in Providence. In 2008 I returned to private practice specializing in Chapter 7 bankruptcy representation.
