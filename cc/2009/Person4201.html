Alrik Koudenburg’s Summary

Alrik Koudenburg (1976) is a curious, analytical, resourceful freelance design director based in Amsterdam. Working for both clients directly and design agencies around the globe, he is responsible for concept and strategy. He enjoys creating first drafts and launching ideas to develop an initiative and works across the many different disciplines of design [interior + retail / events + experiences / product design / interactive media / graphic design + corporate communication / photography / freework]. Once the concepts are approved, he often directs a team of creatives to execute it.

Alrik is committed to creating exciting and refreshing brand experiences that go beyond what clients request, and deliver what they really need. Alrik’s strongest traits are his passion, his critical eye and his ability to key into opportunities for client’s products and services, based on his broad knowledge of trends, fashions and how they fit with consumers and clients. Diversity and exciting, new challenges are what drives him, whether it’s creating his own tree-tent, unique (retail) objects, a fashion show for Nike, a lobby bar for Heineken International, teaching or contributing in brainstorming sessions. It makes his work feel like play.

Alrik studied Graphic Design in Rotterdam, Fine-art in Sweden and Industrial Design at the Design Academy in Eindhoven. He has traveled extensively and lived and worked abroad in cities like London / New York / Buenos Aires and Sydney. This has broadened his life experience and enhanced his ability to approach tasks in different ways.

Alrik Koudenburg’s Specialties:

Multi-disciplinairy design work, ranching from: interior design, retail objects / windows / stores, product design, event /experiences, photography, graphic design, corporate communication + strategy, guerilla marketing, interactive media, books + magazines...and teaching / attending brainstorms. Furthermore Alrik is a speaker at YourFullCircle, giving a series of workshops about future creation, living the life one loves.
