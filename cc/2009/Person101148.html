Truman J. Hedding’s Summary

Internet exec with broad experience in all aspects of internet marketing. Direct experience with search engine optimization, affiliate marketing, pay per click management & site conversion optimization.

Truman Hedding is the VP of Internet Marketing for Boulevards New Media, Inc., where he directs all aspects of search engine marketing for Boulevards and its affiliate marketing division.

Prior to joining Boulevards in 2006, Truman held managerial positions at various public and private search marketing firms, with an expertise spanning geotargeted search, evaluative analytics and content creation and optimization. As a recognized expert in the search marketing community, Truman has consulted for Fortune 1000 companies and small businesses across the nation, and has been quoted in such notable publications as the Los Angeles Times and Business Week.
