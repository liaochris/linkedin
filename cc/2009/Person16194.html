Charles Bradley’s Summary

Technology Project Manager currently pursuing certification as a Clinical Research Professional and co-founder of Johnson, Bradley & Associates, Inc.. JB&A Inc specializes in providing turnaround services to troubled companies in today's economically depressed market.

I am a former US Army Officer with myriad professional experiences gleaned throughout a diverse military career. These experiences ran the gamut from combat arms organizations to participation on US diplomatic missions to the Soviet Union.

During my military career, I earned a Masters degree in Information Systems Technology from Johns Hopkins University and have applied the knowledge and skills I have gained in a host of areas to include information systems security, information security, networking, database management, and hardware/software project management.

I am currently the systems administrator for the Tampa Office of Disability Adjudication and Review for the Social Security Administration. My duties include network management, database maintenance, and the operation of the CCTV hearing rooms in Tampa and at a remote site in Clearwater. I also work with a partner, Mr. Stanley Johnson, as one of the principals of Johnson, Bradley & Associates, Inc., a business management consultancy.
