Steve Higgins’s Summary

Steve is an energetic, internationally recognised expert in Middle East and International business with a specific interest in investment, telecommunications, media, recruitment, sustainability and CleanTech.

SJH International Limited is a UK and Dubai based consultancy company that helps businesses accelerate their growth in the MENA region.


Key management skills and related experience include:

Business Start-Up & Development: Proven track record of successfully managing high growth start-up’s and MBO. Creating and implementing strategy; driving innovation; building, developing, leading and motivating teams. Building and developing businesses: high value sales and contract negotiations; building and developing long term business relationships; developing ‘buy-in’ and customer loyalty.

Strategic & operational business management: Main Board experience. Creating and successfully implementing corporate strategy. Forming organisational, product and technology strategies at national and international business levels.

System and Product Development experience: Extensive experience gained over 20 years. Highly experienced in the effective implementation of new technologies and technology strategy.

International business experience: Proven and verified abilities in initiating and developing international business & joint ventures. Skilled in the facilitation and development of international business relationships and strategic partnerships.

Steve Higgins’s Specialties:

Investment, telecommunications, international business development and partnership facilitation, expert in international business specifically Dubai, UAE and Middle East MENA region.
