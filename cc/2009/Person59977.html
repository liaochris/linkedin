Lola Audu’s Summary

Successful Real Estate Broker in Grand Rapids Michigan. Certified as a Residential Specialist, E-Pro Designee and Graduate Real Estate Institute (GRI) Lola's passion is directed towards creating a wholistic approach to training & developing professionals & individuals.

The body, mind and spirit need to operate in balance and harmony to create success in life and business. To facilitate this process, Lola has develped a 1-2 year Mentor Training apprentice program for real estate professionals. Lola has written and taught a variety of professional and spiritual courses to organizations and church groups.

The real estate practice of Audu Real Estate is characterized by attention to detail, commitment to results and compassion for the well-being of our clients.
