Jakob Kjøller’s Summary

Hi my name is Jakob Kjøller and I am a visonary creative designer, specialized in digital medias.

I love making designs that make sense daily to a lot of people.

I am educated as graphic and digital designer (master degree) at the Danish School of Design 1998-2002.
I have studied at the School of Architecture (bachelor degree) at the Royal Danish Academy of Art 1995-1998.

I live in Copenhagen West and and I can be found on our old sailing boat in Christianshavn in the summertime.
