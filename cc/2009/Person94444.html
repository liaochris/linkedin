Stephen Page’s Summary

I have been practising in family law since 1985, admitted in 1987 and accredited as a family law specialist since 1996.

I have experience in all types of family law: property settlement, children, child support, child maintenance, spousal maintenance, binding financial agreements, cohabitation agreements, separation agreements, domestic violence, child abduction, adoption, Hague convention matters, international disputes, LGBT issues.

I am on the panel of independent children's lawyers.

I have spoken at local, national and international conferences and to various media about domestic violence, family law and LGBT issues.

My email address is spage@harringtonfamilylawyers.com
