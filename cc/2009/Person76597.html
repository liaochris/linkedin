Shrihari Pandit’s Summary

Shrihari Pandit has been President & CEO since founding Stealth Communications in 1995. Prior to Stealth, In the early 1990s, Shrihari was a network securities consultant to various software and telecom companies which include MCI, Sprint and Sun Microsystems. Shrihari was also an independent consultant to various U.S. Government Agencies such as NASA and National Infrastructure Protection Center (NIPC), now part of the Department of Homeland Security. In the past ten years, Shrihari has developed Stealth into New York City's largest Internet gateway. Stealth maintains a number of successful lines of services across the telecom industry including the Voice Peering Fabric, which is the world's largest marketplace for telephony services.

Shrihari Pandit’s Specialties:

IPv4, IPv6, Voice Over IP (VoIP), ENUM, SRV, Peering, Optical Switching, Ultra-high speed networking and communication protocols, Ethernet Transport & Tunnelling technologies, WiFi, Wireless, Interconnection of networks, Software Engineering, Router/Switch architectures.

Metro Markets: New York City, Chicago, Atlanta, Miami and London.
