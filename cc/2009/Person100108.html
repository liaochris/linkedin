Thomas Rue’s Summary

Over 20 years as a professional counselor and educator in public and non-profit settings; have taught research methodology, humanistic psychology, and introduction to self-management for three different undergraduate colleges; contributing editor for a community newspaper, 1985 to 2000; original articles published in national magazines.

Thomas Rue’s Specialties:

Mental health and chemical dependency counseling; research; writing; marketing; web development; consulting; organizing and community action.
