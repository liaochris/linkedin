Misty Bachmann’s Summary

Misty Bachmann is a Recruiting Manager with Reach Recruits. With over 9 years of direct recruiting experience, she is recognized for her expertise in Senior Management, Executive Level Recruiting and Healthcare. Prior to joining Reach Recruits, Misty held various positions from National Recruiter to Human Resource Manager. She brings her vast knowledge and business background with an emphasis in Human Resource.

Misty is passionate about helping professionals advance in their careers as well as fulfilling her clients’ staffing needs. She brings an energetic, positive attitude that enables her to provide the top level of performance expected by candidates and companies
