Mitchell Leeman’s Summary

For the past twenty years, or so, I have been building successful teams consisting of varied back grounds. Always working for "the man" so to speak. Well now I have become "the man" and provide my services for clients within the Health Care and Allied Health Care Industries.

My knowledge base and intuitive people skills provide me with the tools required to fully understand my clients requirements and to find the appropriate candidates.

In the past I have generally shied away from recruiters due to a perceived lack of integrity. I have vowed to myself that I will NEVER be that person. I will find only the best qualified candidates for an opportunity, even if my odds of making a placement are diminished by the lack of volume.

Integrity is the only possession one has that travels with them into the great unknown. I plan to have mine well remembered.
