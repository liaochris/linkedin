Robert Di Marco’s Summary

Robert Di Marco Managing Partner of GSA ENERGY. Mr. Di Marco has overseen the Company since its inception, managing day to day operations, customer acquisitions, joint ventures, contracts negotiations and compliance issues. He supervises all negotiations within GSA and builds a well versed management team.

Mr. Di Marco was the founder of Global Services Advisory, which began in 2002 f/k/a Knight Consulting Group, an international sourcing and consulting company. He has served as CEO at GSA, steering the Company to constant growth and innovation in international markets and industrial commodities. Since 2002, Mr. Di Marco has grown GSA from a start-up consulting and product sourcing business, to a global facilitator of physical commodities, all while positioning the company as the next leader in acquisition and distribution of energy products and soft commodities focusing in 2009 on launching GSA Energy LLC. Robert’s vision and business acumen have allowed a 20 year entrepreneurial career initially as a commodity trader with Univest, trading metals and then Currencies. His career then advanced to the equity markets, international finance & banking, technology and global sourcing/import-export. Robert’s leadership, vision and determination have enabled him to seize market opportunities, trends and leverage them quickly in good economies and bad. While President of Viking Investment/IHC Holdings, was instrumental in the precedent setting reorganization of Standard Oil of Delaware into American Resources and the Acquisition and Merger of Southern Gas, including acquisitions of Gulf Platforms and Natural Gas Pipelines in North America, setting reorganization trends throughout the energy sector. Robert's tenacity and willingness to address tough issues head on has earned him respect and high regard. In 1999, this resolve awarded him the Turner Entrepreneur of the Year Award and was also voted in 2000 as one of the top Up and Coming CEOs in the south east.

    GSA Energy, LLC is quality assured, privately owned, boutique oil trading firm, with its primary market operations in Dallas Texas, and secondary market operations in Palm Beach, Florida. GSA was formed by the merger of two established trading houses and currently has over 20 years of energy trading experience on its desk. GSA Energy's forte is primarily middle distillates. GSAE deals in the trading market through a network of well-established and long term relationships with clients that remain paramount to our philosophy and success. We are the worldwide exclusive mandate for Petroleum Oil Alliance.

  • CEO

    Global Services Advisory

    (International Trade and Development industry)

    January 2002 — Present (7 years 7 months)

    Global Services and Advisory (GSA) formally Knight Consulting, is a global buy/sell facilitator that utilizes its worldwide network of management and intellectual capital as a means to offer complex strategies and solutions to solve the sourcing, import/export and procurement problems of its clients. Currently with representatives in Beijing, Palm Beach, Montreal, Montevideo, Panama City, New York, London, and Miami, GSA serves as the connecting agent for its global collaborative on six of the seven continents sourcing strategies and products in its three divisions that range from, GSA ENERGY - oil, and refined petroleum products, GSA TRADING precious metals, precious stones, etc. and GSA CAPITAL - financial and consulting (MTNs, Financial Instruments such as BGs MTNs, etc.). GSA’s ingenuity and foresight has positioned itself to take advantage of the globalization of world markets and be a conduit to its clients.
