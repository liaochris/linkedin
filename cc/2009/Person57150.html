Kyra Cavanaugh’s Summary

Kyra Cavanaugh is the president and founder of Life Meets Work, a consultancy and online community that helps companies and individuals expand opportunities for flexible work.

We offer speaking, workshops, consulting engagements, online resources and placement services to make it easier for companies to implement flexible workplaces and individuals to find flexible work.

Prior to launching Life Meets Work, Kyra worked in the consumer packaged goods industry in roles that included: consultant, marketing professional, project manager, and business development director.

Throughout her career, she has developed innovative strategies and solutions for companies such as: Quaker, Nestle, Keebler, General Mills, Ocean Spray and Dominick's Finer Foods, as well as the Canadian Government.

Kyra Cavanaugh’s Specialties:

Change management, strategic planning, project management, managing virtual teams, implementing flexible workplaces.
