Kent Ninomiya’s Summary

Veteran journalist with more than 22 years experience as a television news anchor, reporter, and managing editor. Professional writer and editor of more than 1000 articles. Media and broadcast talent consultant. Topic editor, moderator, and frequent contributor on the subjects of Asia/Pacific Rim, mass media, consumer issues, financial issues, martial arts, and world travel.
