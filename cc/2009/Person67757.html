Michael Tarrant’s Summary

Electronic Medical Record Configuration Engineer with outpatient and inpatient documentation development experience.

My current work is used in the clinics of the Seattle Cancer Care Alliance by a wide range of clinicians and providers.

My past work has been used in over 250,000 inpatient encounters at the University of Washington Medical Center. I am honored to have been a part of so many patient's care.

I am interested in applying my skills to the following areas:
* Rules development (MLMs, Arden syntax)
* CPOE (Computerized Physician Order Entry) design and build
* Documentation across the electronic health record
* Open source EMR/EHR applications for public health and use in developing countries
* EMR/EHR records migration and continuity over time
* Data analysis
