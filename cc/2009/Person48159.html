Jo-Anne Peck’s Summary

Co-founder of Preservation Resource, Inc., Ms. Peck is a historic preservation professional with over 12 years experience in preservation construction, materials conservation, compatible design, and research. She is responsible for historic building condition assessments, measured drawings, rehabilitation design, construction drawings and specifications, Design Guidelines, research, historic resource surveys and National and Local Register Nominations. Her strengths lie in her considerable construction and architecture experience coupled with her preservation education.

Recently the company has expanded its offerings to include hard to find products for owners of historic Florida homes at HistoricShed.com. Items offered include custom designed outbuildings, traditional screens doors, wood window screens, foundation lattice and garden gates. All products are designed with historically authentic details in addition to meeting Florida's climate challenges.

  • Owner

    Preservation Resource, Inc.

    (Architecture & Planning industry)

    January 2000 — Present (9 years 11 months)

    Preservation Resource, Inc. was founded in January of 2000 from the merger of a historic preservation design firm and a construction company specializing in historic building renovations. The result is a full-service historic preservation consulting company with unmatched expertise in the research and sympathetic planning for the rehabilitation, restoration and related new construction for historic buildings. The company prides itself on its comprehensive knowledge of typical construction types, styles and materials common to Florida, as well as accepted preservation theory and practice. The company has been fortunate to participate in a variety of project types from initial planning through construction. Clients have included the Florida Department of Transportation, South Florida Water Management District, various municipalities, engineering firms, cultural management companies and numerous private clients.
