Chuck Miro - Realtor’s Summary

♦ Realtor, Specializing in Residential Real Estate
♦ Location: Northern Virginia
♦ Member, Long & Foster Executive's Club
♦ Top-Producing Agent

"Knowledge, Experience, Integrity…and Friendly, Professional Service!"

When selling a home, an experienced and professional real estate agent can be an indispensable resource. I can give you up-to-date information regarding prices, financing, terms and conditions of competing properties, and information about the housing market in general. I will market your property to other real estate agents as well as to the public, and will know the best way to go about doing so. I can also help you objectively evaluate buyer proposals, and then, finally, help you close the sale.

When buying a home, having the assistance of an experience and professional real estate agent can be equally as important. I have access to resources for assisting you in your home search not available elsewhere. I can also provide you objective information about each property, and give you advice based on years of experience. And I will help you negotiate and also steer you through the closing process to make sure everything goes smoothly.

As I continue to build my business through referrals, I hope you won’t mind me asking for your support on a regular basis; your support through your thoughts and referrals means so much to my business. So I ask now that you keep me in mind should you hear of folks looking to buy or sell a home, in Virginia or Maryland, please let me know! I will make certain they receive the absolute best care and service.

If you would like a free market analysis, please call me at 571-243-7511 or email me at chuck.miro@longandfoster.com.

Thank you!

TO REALTORS: Reciprocal referral proposals from other licensed real estate professionals are always welcome!
