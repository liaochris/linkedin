Sheryl Torr-Brown PhD’s Summary

What I do:

Connecting across disciplines and creating innovative environments is my passion.

-Developing science collaborations between industry and academia
-Basic scientist with subject matter expertise in medicine and biological science, pharmaceutical R&D, cardiovascular disease, diabetes, diseases of the elderly, retinal diseases, neuro-pharmacology, psychology of disease experience, oncology, personalized medicine
-Translating complex science and clinical information into accessible information for peers, non-experts and the public
-Building communities and connecting ideas, people, concepts
- Empathic market research
-Applying knowledge management techniques
-Envisioning the future through foresight intelligence, including scenario planning
-Identifying trends and emerging opportunities

Quick bio:
I'm a Brit who has been in the States for 20 after coming over for a post-doc in '89. I have spent almost 20 years in the pharmaceutical industry in a variety of positions including Head of Thrombosis Research for Searle, Director of Knowledge Management and Strategy for Monsanto Corporate Division, and Head of Science and Technology for Pfizer Drug Safety Evaluation.

I am a member of the Scientific Advisory Boards for the Personalized Medicine journal and a UK- based IT company.I am also currently serving on the board of a local Food Center and working with the center to end hunger through building community resilience in the county of New London.
