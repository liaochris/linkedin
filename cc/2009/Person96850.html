Tracy Aquilla, PhD’s Summary

Registered Patent Attorney providing a complete spectrum of services in intellectual property law, including counseling, opinions, procurement, licensing, conveyance and enforcement of patents, trademarks and copyrights worldwide. Our professional relationships with experienced patent and trademark attorneys on six continents enable us to provide global intellectual property legal services for a diverse client base. The firm's clients are distributed throughout the United States and Asia and include large multinational corporations, small companies and start-ups, and individual inventors in all sectors, from high-tech to high-fashion.

Tracy Aquilla, PhD’s Specialties:

Biotechnology, genetic engineering, pharmaceutical, biomedical and chemical technologies, human and veterinary medicine and medical devices, energy systems, mechanical and automotive engineering, construction tools and equipment, optics, holography and three-dimensional displays, computer hardware and software systems.
