Erik Visser’s Summary

After years of working experience with offline media in the creative sector I engaged full throttle in online. I was instantly struck by this very lively and broad medium that like no other offline medium interconnects and interacts with all other forms of communication and life itself. Loving the challenges and opportunities that come with a medium that is still growing up.

Combining my knowledge, experience, conceptual and strategical thinking I have the skill to combine the (emerging) opportunities on the net with the wishes, goals and needs of people and organizations.
By listening closely and interacting intensely, the client becomes more aware of their needs, their place within society and the needs of their market and the customers within.

From this solid starting point the stategy will emerge that wil show the short and long term opportunities. And how the online presence should take shape. Creating, together with other experts and technical parties, the process of authentic dialogue with customers, the market and beyond.

We as people have the opportunity to build upon the beauty that is and shape the future for the better.
