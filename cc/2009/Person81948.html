Randolph Alamo’s Summary

Mr. Alamo's successful career highlights invaluable experience in the areas of business operations and associated advisory. Said background is applied to start-up operations, new projects and existing operations in need of keen insight and leadership.

In short, Mr. Alamo's career experience allows him to contribute on behalf of his charge, a strong understanding of challenges specific to the nuances of highly competitive and regulated metropolitan markets.

Career Highlights

* As a Commodity Manager for Merrill Lynch - led the RFx and negotiation of a $125 Million Facilities Management contract encompassing Merrill Lynch facilities throughout Canada, Latin America and the U.S.A., resulting in major strategic change and financial gain for the company.

* As Regional Facilities Manager for CB Richard Ellis - re-engineered the real estate portfolio of the former Fleet Bank Corporation, resulting in major savings while improving client services and processes.

* As Vice President for Thermax Construction - planned and developed this successful NYC start-up, resulting in the substantial valuation and sale of the business, after establishing substantial B to B relations.
