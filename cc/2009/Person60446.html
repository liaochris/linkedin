Lisa Trank’s Summary

Currently owner of One Purpose PR & Communications, as well as the part-time Marketing Specialist for PEI, I've served as Director of Communications for Naropa University, Director of Marketing for the Boulder Jewish Community Center during key times of growth and brand indentity and have been lead public relations and marketing/communications specialist for a wide variety of independent and sustainable projects, including the films: "What the Bleep Do We Know?," "Peaceful Warrior," "The Real Dirt on Farmer John" and "The 11th Hour."

My goal is to engage with socially responsible and sustainable organizations, businesses and individuals who want to take their company, product or work to another area. I'm well-versed in non-mainstream approaches to messaging and media relations, but also maintain a very strong list of media relationships in traditional outlets.

Lisa Trank’s Specialties:

Comprehensive media and public relations strategic planning and implementation, grassroots marketing initiatives, transformational entertainment promotions, entrepreneurial marketing support, editing and copywriting.
