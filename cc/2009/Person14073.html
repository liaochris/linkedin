Bruce McDougall’s Summary

I have been in the data processing field for more than 30 years, with over 10 years as an independent consultant. I have worked for large corporations (Prudential Insurance, Beneficial Finance and Litton Industries) where I worked in the Computer Operations and Technical Support Departments and also for a medium sized company (New Jersey Educational Computer Network) where I was the Software Director. I also had long term consulting engagements at Nabisco and Hoechst Celanese. My background has always been in the service area, either internal or external. When working for Litton and NJECN, I worked with many of our external clients that ranged in size from a sole proprietorship to the Department of Defense. I have dealt with companies in many industries as well as governmental agencies and educational institutions.
