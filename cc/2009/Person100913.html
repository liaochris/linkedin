Trevor Turnbull’s Summary

As social media has started to dominate the traditional and online news headlines, I have been focused on how I can monitor these trends and figure out ways to implement social media strategies for the companies I work with. Because of this, I have found myself constantly researching the latest trends in social media as they relate to marketing, advertising, business and sports teams.

My background in hockey, combined with my experience as a business owner and account executive in the online marketing industry have particularly fueled my passion to work with sports teams. I believe there is a tremendous opportunity for sports teams to embrace social media to build their fan base and retain those fans through the highs and lows.

As we all know, we are in some tough economic times right now. By engaging in the conversation with hard core fans, season ticket holders and casual fans, sports teams will be able to realize the viral power of social media.....and at a much cheaper cost than traditional one-way marketing. At the end of the day, the professional sports business is still just that……….a business. So, because of this, I spend a lot of my time figuring out ways to help sports leagues and teams monitor, measure and implement strategies to maximize their return on investment (ROI) and return on engagement (ROE) from social media marketing.
