Norm Fisher’s Summary

We offer specialized service to buyers and sellers of residential real estate in the city of Saskatoon and the greater Saskatoon area. We are interested in connecting with people who are seeking professional representation for a residential real estate transaction in urban and suburban areas of greater Saskatoon. As residential specialists we do not accept assignments in commercial real estate, farms, land or property management.

We are also interested in expanding our network of real estate professionals around the globe. We're particularly interested in associating with other professionals who leverage technology in their day to day business and understand how their clients benefit from it.

Norm Fisher’s Specialties:

Buyer specialists.
Seller specialists.
Internet marketing for residential real estate on a vast network of real estate websites.
Professional presentation of residential properties utilizing great photographs and descriptive text.
Use of electronic communication (email, SMS, blog, web-based service tools) to keep our clients informed on their transaction and the Saskatoon real estate market in general.
