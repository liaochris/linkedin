Amanda Theiler’s Summary

Between May 2008-May 2009 I interned at the Iona Group in Morton, IL as a video production intern where I assisted on video shoots, edited videos, and helped in pre-production by creating shot lists. Videos worked on included a promotional video for Pioneer, a commercial for Schwan's which was broadcast on NBC during The Bachelor, several training videos for Caterpillar, as well as other projects.

For web and graphic design, I have worked for AU North America, the North American branch of a French Insurance company creating their website and a mailer for them.

I completed my Bachelor of Science in Multimedia at Bradley University in May 2009 and moved to New York City to pursue a career primarily in video and motion graphics. I am currently accepting freelance work in video, motion graphics, graphic design, and web design.
