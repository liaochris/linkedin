Anirudh Bhalotia’s Summary

With 5 years of 3D Character animation experience involving lots of challenges both creatively and technically, I can straightaway contribute immensely to your company's production.

Having worked in some of India’s best Animation studios such as MUV STUDIOS, Chennai, CREST ANIMATION STUDIOS LTD., Mumbai and working on internationally acclaimed projects has given me tremendous international exposure which can be an asset to your ever growing organization. I have been professionally trained on the technical aspect of MAYA and at the same time my work in the above mentioned organizations has given me the creative edge by learning about the intricacies of the creative side of animation. I had also been selected for attending acting workshop which has given me a whole new dimension to character animation.

I was also given the opportunity and have the experience of being a Student Volunteer for the very first Siggraph Asia, Conference and Exhibition which was held in Singapore and was also chosen as One of the Top-Ten Student Volunteers who worked extra ordinarily during the Conference-Exhibition.

I am also a student at Animation Mentor - Online Character Animation School, to further add to my skills and understanding of being an Animator. I believe an Animator should never cease to learn, every day should be a learning experience for any Animator. This is the only way to make sure you are always on top of your skills and constantly re-inventing yourself.
