Criss Ittermann’s Summary

Eclectic Tech commits to inspire, empower & train people while providing continuing support to ensure their success.

Criss is a unique and brilliant thinker, able to mingle logic with creativity, and she balances the strengths and weaknesses of her clients. She helps widen narrow perspectives, elicit commitment to forward progression on projects, and clarifies the obscure. Criss works with clients from a wide variety of businesses to build their business through defining clear objectives, leveraging personal and community strengths to have positive outcomes, and pinpointing weaknesses or obstacles so they can be managed or removed from the picture.

In addition to business coaching, Eclectic Tech specializes in building brochure websites and web application systems for clients. Criss is both graphic designer and web developer.

brainstorming, group facilitation, workshop facilitation, defining business vision & mission; graphic design with Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign; web applications including PmWiki, WordPress, ZenCart, etc.; programming with PHP, DHTML/XHTML, CSS, MySQL;
