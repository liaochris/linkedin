Viktor Prochazka’s Summary

I currently live in Prague, Czech Republic. I work as a mass marketer in a company selling electricity - Prazska energetika, a.s. ( PRE ).

Before that I worked for HUDYsport a.s. (distributor of The North Face in CZ at the time) as a start-up manager. I pulled together Prague's The North Face store A to Z - getting the spot, negotiating, marketing, HR etc.

Before starting with HUDYsport and The North Face I was a core member of the creative studio Spoon s.r.o. - and it was great. Unfortunately Spoon studio didn't hack it in the world of business so it's only great memories now.

I lead a life of a husband, father, worker, freelancer and a blogger.
