Laura Bergells’s Summary

I help growing businesses develop winning online marketing plans. And I often pitch in and help deploy some of the tactics I recommend -- especially when folks don't have the time or resources to tackle it themselves.

My two blogs keep readers informed of what's happening in the world of internet marketing and presentation. And I also maintain a website that offers free video how-to's for the do-it-yourself crowd.

Highly intuitive, I'm quick to understand your business goals, and deliver creative thinking, strategic planning, training, and wonderful words that help you sell.

Based in Grand Rapids, Michigan, I serve clients all over the world.
