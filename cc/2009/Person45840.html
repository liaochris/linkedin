Nipa Shah’s Summary

We are licensors (similar to franchisors) of Internet Marketing Solutions. You may buy an Internet Marketing License which allows you to become your own internet marketing company owner. As a licensee you:

1) benefit from a low investment (lower than buying any franchise or existing business)
2) can leverage a ready infrastructure of trained low-cost, "MANAGED" resources
3) receive a website and other collateral
4) do not pay any royalties
5) do not have any geographic limitations to doing business

If you are a marketing company owner, web development company, or graphic designer, you may own a marketing agency license which will allow you to utilize our infrastructure, resources, and online marketing expertise to deliver web solutions for your clients.

The Marketing Biz in a Box TM has been created by Jenesys Group, LLC, an online marketing company.

Jenesys Group, LLC has expertise in implementing Everything Web TM solutions, including social media marketing strategy planning, social media marketing implementation, search engine optimization,paid marketing, and basic to complex web solutions.

Nipa, the founder of Jenesys Group, LLC is also the founder of the Michigan India Chamber of Commerce (www.miicc.com) which was launched in 2007. The MIICC was started to foster networking and facilitating business with and within Asian Indians in Michigan.

Nipa has spent 18 years at General Motors Corporation in various leadership roles. Her experiences include leading a technology group supporting the sales & marketing organization, leading the procurement of technology services upwards of $650M, negotiating contracts worth millions of dollars, and conducting business process re-engineering activities to create efficiency at GM.

Nipa Shah’s Specialties:

Creating entrepreneurs. We believe that entrepreneurship should not be difficult. We help entrepreneurs launch online marketing companies to provide solutions to consumers focusing on improving traffic, branding, & visibility through search engine and social media marketing activities.
