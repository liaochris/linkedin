Jason Garey’s Summary

Jason has worked in publishing for six years, and recently joined MEDIAmerica in 2007 after relocating to Oregon from Memphis, Tennessee. He has worked in the sales management role for both newspapers and magazines getting his start in 2001 with the Chattanooga Times Free Press. His resume also includes Memphis Business Journal, Oregon Home, and Oregon Business magazine. His responsibility for MEDIAmerica includes sales team management, sales promotions, strategy, event sponsorships, high impact advertising, as well as develops, implements, and manages the company's advertising strategy, both from a business, sales and technical perspective. It is his job to develop budgets and projections for advertising projects. He spends time negotiating agreements with outside sales representatives, managing the development of sales materials, including promotional sales materials and media kits, and is responsible for managing the advertising sales representatives.
