Jonathan Kahn’s Summary

Clients usually involve me if they are seeking creative solutions with an innovative edge, as I'm relied to wield a flexible yet practical approach to complex technology for avante-garde problem-solving, technical alignment and cutting-edge architecture fused with business strategy.

Continuously aiming to hone the ability to capture the clients' enterprise goals and frame their initiatives using applied technology architecture, I usually look to enterprise middleware for its ability to deliver on the promise of integration and composite functionality, with cost reduction over the implementation lifecycle.

Many progressive companies are seeking to integrate in this market - capitalizing on the availability of top IT talent at more reasonable costs, yet are challenged with developing the right roadmaps for smooth implementation.

Having been involved with delivering IT business strategy and delivery guidance at the Director and C-levels of various retail and pharmaceutical clients amongst others. I've also helped companies codify and optimize key business process using Architecture and Business Modeling tools.

At Oracle, I've worked to deepen my exposure whilst enjoying the ability to draw on deeply skilled SME peers, who join me in leading solutions delivery for clients in their care, and due to their deep technical skills -- help bring innovation and value to our joint clients.

Recently, I've been certified as a Value Selling Practitioner and I strive now to also incorporate these discovery and delivery methods while adding value to new and ongoing client relationships, which helps ensure strong business value-driven, solutions in all our accounts.

Please contact me if you want to discuss a particular new project or experience that involves enterprise architecture and strategy as well as business development within the software industry or IT industry. If I can't help, I may know someone who can...

Jonathan Kahn’s Specialties:
