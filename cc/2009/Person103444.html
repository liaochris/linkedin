April Chapple’s Summary

Author: 86 Girls Indoor Volleyball Tips: How To Gain Confidence On The Volleyball Court and How To Stop Serving Like A Wimp. Available for licensing/customization by volleyball companies and sporting good associations these downloadable e-book and e-booklet tips were written specifically for the 3,000,000+ female high school volleyball player audience and are distributed and sold at volleyballvoices.com.


Senior Proofreader/Copywriter: Proofed printed pieces and advertising collateral, Web sites and magazine copy for content, grammar, readability and typographical errors for all MGM MIRAGE properties at MGM MIRAGE Advertising. Primary accounts were Bellagio, City Center, MGM Grand, Mandalay Bay, Monte Carlo, and The Mirage.

Columnist: Three-year "Five Quick Tips" columnist for the American Volleyball Coaches Association's industry magazine, "Coaching Volleyball".

Volleyball Journalist: Regular contributor to the Las Vegas Sports Magazine, Active.com frequent contributor, author of online content and numerous articles on coaching volleyball.

Creator, Visionary, Entrepreneur: Volleyball Voices.com is a volleyball news hub where female players find inspiration, information and volleyball heroes.

Professional Photographer: Italy's major media outlets and regular contributor to Italy's three volleyball magazines

Professional Volleyball Player: USA National Team, FIVB, AVP, CBVA, FIPAV
