Zachary Cobb’s Summary

Zachary is co founder of the Ideas and Angles consulting group that works with businesses to form and implement social media strategies custom fit to their marketing goals. Focusing on creative content creation, technical implementation, and industry research.

In addition to his work in the social media space Zachary is a freelance photographer that has shot US Senators, pro sporting events and national print campaigns. Zachary has a strength in composition and building relationships with clients that go beyond the shutter. He has experience in different styles of photography including portraiture, photojournalism, architecture, commercial, and fine art. Photography website and portfolio management including experience with online print sales and required business practices related to being self employed.
