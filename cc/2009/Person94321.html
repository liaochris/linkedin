Stephen Cotterell’s Summary

I am part of a talented, experienced and creative team of people (or as Seth Godin would say, a Tribe) called Executive Matters Limited. We specialise in bridging communication gaps in business.

In our experience, quality communication is at the heart of business excellence and therefore essential in maximising perfromance and results.

We remove common and fundamental barriers to elevated performance by resourcing our clients to inspire, engage, connect and motivate themselves and others, in order to release untapped potential to achieve business and personal excellence.

Why is this important? What does it mean? How do we bridge the gaps? How do you rate communication within your organisation? What insights into communication would you like to see and experience? So many questions that we would like to discuss with you. Maybe you have some very specific questions of your own that require practical and sustainable answers from us?

Our credentials cover working with international financial services, consulting, pharmaceutical, satellite technology and luxury goods brands. We are practical people who are keen to share our skills with you in a practical way that delivers flexibility, results and happier business relationships.
