Ric Holland’s Summary

2009 - Appointed to Global Evangelist Manager. Author of Art of Making Marks published by IdN Publications. Author of the blog Art of Wa.com (www.artofwa.com) Project Leader for Wacom's global web experience redesign. Writer/Editor for corporate communications from Japanese HQ and recently won a CEO's award for successfully communicating Wacom's reputation in global professional digital content creation markets. (DCC)

2006 - Joined Wacom Australia to enhance new business development by leveraging his expert knowledge and relationships in Professional Graphics Industries, Tertiary Education Markets, Business and Enterprise Customers helping to grow Wacom Australia's market share in the ANZ region.

2003 - 2005 - Ric Holland focused his energy on building alliance partnerships and to develop business activities in the latest wave of emerging digital media markets. Contracted by major brands in digital interactive media, music production, digital publishing, broadcasting and content creation. He has helped find alternate revenue sources, cross-media business models and creative concepts for many digital interactive productions.

Industry Liaison Mentor at LAMP - AFTRS and Digital Media Lecturer at UTS, UNSW CoFA, Billy Blue Design School and CGC. Co founder or the '4 Day Thing' design education series.

His background as a Creative Director and Innovator over the past 25 years in emerging new fields of design and digital media has given him unique insights and forward thinking when considering new conceptual design models, alternate market opportunities, audience behavior, creative and technology innovation.

Ric Holland’s Specialties:

Creative Director, Art Director, Graphic Designer, Artist, Digital Media Producer, Brand Strategist, Digital Media Lecturer, Industry Liason Specialist, Business Development, Sales & Marketing, Technology Evangelist, Team building.
