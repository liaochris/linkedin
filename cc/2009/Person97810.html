Domenico Tassone’s Summary

OVERVIEW: A seasoned Internet marketing professional with a high-energy evangelical approach; offer both a broad and deep online media background and often work to accelerate online business; fuse hands-on skill with strategic marketing, operational know-how and financial insight.
