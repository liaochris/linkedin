Tom Muyllaert’s Summary

Personal mission statement: Listen. Learn. & Leverage value.

I have 10 years of experience in sales & marketing and had the opportunity to get involved in the commercial start-up of a spin-off company of the VUB back in 1997(ELSYCA - www.elsyca.com).
My sales profile is more of a hunter having experience in (international) business development. Since a couple of years now, I am enjoying my passion being the internet & online marketing in specific.

I used to work as senior SEO consultant at iProspect Belgium, part of Aegis Media Belgium since January 2008 (before part Belgacom Skynet). Within Aegis, iProspect is part of the Isobar network, the digital media BU. Currently I hold a position as Sales Consultant @ The Reference, a leading Belgian web agency.
