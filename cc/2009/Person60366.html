Duncan Isaksen-Loxton’s Summary

Developing a taste for computers at an early age (apparently one of my first spoken words!) I gained a taste for multimedia development at school, and went on to gain an A-Level in Computing backed up by Electronics and Physics.

I was in the first year to complete Multimedia Computing - one of the first courses of its kind in the world at a university level - a degree in all facets of the internet. From low level protocols such as http, smtp, imap and gsm in mobile networks, programming languages for the internet to databases and web design, animation and human computer interfaces, the course gave me an in depth of knowledge not found from a lot of other computing degrees.

Now proficient in a range of technologies and languages I can advise on the suitability of solutions for the job at hand and tailor that solution for the best results.

Currently learning about new and developing rich media platforms such as Macromedia's Flex, and the Javascript and Web Services technique known as AJAX, I am looking to expand my knowledge and push the boundaries of applications delivered over the internet.
