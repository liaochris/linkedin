Aerial M. Ellis’s Summary

Emerging among the brightest in the industry, Aerial has worked in variety of capacities in media and entertainment. A gifted speaker and strategist, she nurtured an entrepreneurial spirit to fully engage in a career as a public relations consultant.

She has aligned client partnerships with Sony Music, Olay Skincare, National Basketball Association, Black Entertainment Television and Burger King among others.

She has also positioned media coverage in outlets including CNN, CBS, TV One, USA Today, NY Times, Ebony, Essence, Entrepreneur, Yahoo News, Sister 2 Sister, Upscale, Nashville Lifestyles and more.

Recently, Aerial was selected as a winner of the PR News 15-to-Watch Award and as one of Nashville's Top 30 Under 30.

Prior to launching Urbane Imagery - a public relations consultancy that provides tailored marketing and media relations to entrepreneurs, small businesses and non profit organizations - Aerial worked in promotions and marketing at Cumulus Media.

Later, she served as Operations Manager for WTST, Tennessee State University Campus Radio and led the station to win the 2005 College Radio Station of the Year Award. She returned to serve in the university's Office of Media Relations.
