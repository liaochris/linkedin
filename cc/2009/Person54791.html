Ken Warren’s Summary

Difficult clients are part-and-parcel of working in the education, health and welfare sectors.

When you add challenging co-workers into the mix, then your team can face some tough communication issues.

Ken Warren is Australia's leading speaker on Dealing with Aggressive, Demanding and Unmotivated People.

With his engaging, interactive and positive approach, Ken has helped thousands of education, health and welfare professionals learn how to turn difficult people around and bring out their best.

Ken regularly features in the media and professional journals for his practical & down-to-earth strategies.

His approach focuses on seeing the best in people and taking advantage of an individual’s strengths. He teaches what the research and his experience have found to make the most difference.

With 24 years hands-on experience working with challenging clients – couples and co-workers in conflict, people with drug-related and mental health problems, and aggressive and violent individuals - Ken is no ivory-tower theorist. His practical strategies work in the field.

Ken is a warm, entertaining and thought-provoking speaker, and has achieved Professional Member status in the National Speakers' Association of Australia.

Ken tailors his presentations to meet the needs of each audience. Here are just a few of the topics he can present at your next conference, association meeting or in-house training day.
