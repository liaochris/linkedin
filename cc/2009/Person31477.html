Erik Flippo’s Summary

I'm a graphic designer with more than 17 years of professional experience working in-house, for ad agencies and design firms, and on my own. In 2006 I started Peavine Design Inc., providing web, print and user-interface design services to advertising agencies and private clients. After two years in North Carolina and 11 years in Northern and Southern California, I returned to Reno, Nevada in 2001.

Erik Flippo’s Specialties:

graphic design, web design and production, branding and identity, naming
