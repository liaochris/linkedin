Michele Ruppert’s Summary

I love to help people feel great! One way of doing this is through massage and bodywork. Another way is through education. I do both through your appointments and by giving public talks and demonstrations. The intention of my practice is to help people realize that they don't need to be in pain all day, everyday. When I help people release pain and get them back to their natural body alignment, their mental outlook on life changes and they are able to move more freely in their bodies. Because of these facilitated changes, people are able to do anything they want to do!

PLUS with the ongoing war efforts since early 2000 along with continuous, intense deployments worldwide by military, veteran, and other personnel supporting government operations AND with the barriers these people and their families are experiencing in receiving adequate healthcare services when they return to the United States, there is an urgent need to help heal and reintegrate these people back into society.

SO I am working to bring therapeutic and medicinal massage therapy-bodywork practices into the government/military healthcare-rehabilitation facilities for stress and pain prevention. If you want to help and learn more about it, please visit http://home.earthlink.net/~micheleruppert/id18.html to help support my project!
