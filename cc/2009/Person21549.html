Cynthia McKenna MEd, MA, MDiv, LPC, NCC’s Summary

Does your life look good on the outside but inside you feel hurt and sad?

Do you find yourself thinking, “I ought to be happy” but instead you feel stressed and overwhelmed?

I can help.

Some people keep pain inside because they don’t want others to know they are hurting. Sometimes they just don’t know how to make the pain go away.

I can help you heal pain and sadness, and reduce your stress and anxiety. Here are some examples of things you might want to work on in therapy:

•Anxiety
•Depression
•Food Issues
•Relationships
•LGBT Issues
•Women’s Issues

My office is in the beautiful Texas Hill Country. I offer a private, confidential, and safe setting for our work together. I invite you to call my office to schedule an appointment or get more information.

I look forward to hearing from you.
