Jeff Adkins’s Summary

With over 20 years of executive search, staffing, consulting, and management experience, my firm (www.theadkinsgroup.com) has provided nationwide executive search and recruitment consulting services to a number of organizations, large and small, from start-ups to Fortune 100’s. I have successfully completed numerous searches at the executive-level and below, and have provided consulting support (in the areas of staffing strategy, recruitment, and retention) to clients such as Dell, Sprint, Varian Medical Devices, Intel, Sun, Cisco, Microsoft, AMD, Spansion, Mentor Graphics, Tellabs, Analog Devices, Amphenol, and Motorola/Freescale, to name a few.

I am entering my 14th year in the executive search/recruiting arena--I have an extensive background and track record in business and management. This experience has provided me with excellent insight into the organizational needs of The Adkins Group clients. Before founding The Adkins Group, I was a Senior Partner of an Austin, Texas-based, nationwide executive search firm, and, before that, was a top-performing Executive Search Consultant at a technology-focused search firm, also based in Austin.

I earned my B.S. degree in Business Administration from Southern Illinois University, and a Masters Certificate in Human Resource Management from St. Edwards University. I am a Certified Professional in Human Resources (PHR).

The success of our organization is directly related to our ability to recognize and address the issues that are affecting our clients today. The Adkins Group's mission (and mine) is to provide world-class recruitment solutions that positively impact our client companies’ bottom-lines. We aim to achieve excellence by performing above and beyond expectations--with consistency, honesty, and integrity.

Jeff Adkins’s Specialties:

Executive Search; Targeted Research; Recruiting Process Model Development/Developing a Recruiting Plan; Identifying Sources of Qualified Candidates/Target List Development; Candidate Selection Model Development/Screening Candidates; Attracting Potential Candidates, Selling the Organization; Implementing Strategies to Keep Your Top Employees; Align Staffing and Retention to the Core Culture of the Organization; Training and Management (train and manage in-house recruiters).
