Anthony Lopez-Vito’s Summary

My company, Another Cup of Coffee, is a London-based company developing web sites for start-ups. If you need a website but have a limited budget, please get in touch as I'm sure we can help you.

What I do well is jump into a technical mess and fix it quickly using the most appropriate tools available. The trouble can be related to software, infrastructure or managing technical people; it doesn't matter. That's because I happen to be good at analysing information and breaking down a complex problem into easily solvable components. Having said that, I'm pretty rubbish at coming up with those ground-breaking ideas, but I can take a good idea and improve on it. When encountering something unfamiliar, I rely on my team to help point the way forward.

Large corporations with rigid practices may not find me a suitable fit; I deliver the best results when given freedom to use my own creativity. In the past, I've worked with a systems integrator; world-leading mobile operators; banks and a $100bn Japanese trading house. It was fun while it lasted but wasn't for me.

I love small companies and start-ups. I'm a co-founder of ARC, a London-based outsourcing consultancy. I've also served as a trustee of the Centre for Filipinos, a UK-based charity, and co-founded Phil-UK, a not-for-profit community group.

Anthony Lopez-Vito’s Specialties:
