Dave Lougheed’s Summary

I am a seasoned interactive executive specializing in web interaction design and usability. I have assembled and mentored some of the industry's most talented user experience professionals including information architects, creative directors, usability specialists, designers, copywriters, and front-end web developers for over 13 years in multiple North American cities. I've delivered work for some great clients including Nike, Visa, AOL, MSN, BMW, Time-Warner, Office Max, Kraft Foods, Pfizer, Sony, Starbucks, Nintendo, and many others.

I am very passionate about experience design, social media, mobile platforms, and transformational leadership. I have delivered speaking engagements on leadership and technology for the University of Toronto, International Academy of Design, Seneca College, Centennial College, Registered Graphic Designers, and other local organizations.
