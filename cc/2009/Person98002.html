Theo Reichgelt’s Summary

Creative IT consultant, manager and communicator. Many years of experience in document process automation. EDP certified by Xplor international. I help organizations to improve their document and communication processes using modern software technology, my experience and a practical approach.

Currently working on business development on the Caribbean islands for my branch on Curacao, Netherlands Antilles. Bringing new technology and affordable solutions to formally paper driven organizations and managing the change process.

Some companies I worked for in the past are Ohra, Delta Lloyd, VGZ, ASR insurancegroup, PGGM, De Telegraaf, Fortis, de Belastingdienst, SNS Bank, Barclay Card and Girobank.

Also creative graphic designer, marketeer and new media specialist. I run my advertising agency in my spare time and help organizations with all kinds of graphic work, websites, concepts and marketing strategies. I also advice on the use of new media and communication concepts.
