Terry Hassett’s Summary

I provide professional services to residential real estate clients for real estate located in Northern Colorado. I have successfully sold real estate in Northern Colorado for over 30 years. Having taught real estate courses, as well as training and managing other brokers, I am knowledgeable in many areas of real estate including, residential re-sale and investment properties, new construction and project marketing, land and acreages, corporate relocation, REO/foreclosure properties, short sale negotiations and Sec. 1031 tax deferred exchanges. My expertise lies in negotiating real estate contracts, marketing, and Colorado real estate practice. I work to bring a unique level of knowledge and experience to each transaction. My focus is paying special care and attention to achieving desired results for my clients and helping them achieve their individual goals.

Terry Hassett’s Specialties:

Residential Re-Sale, Residential New Construction, Builder/Project Representation, Residential Investments, 1031 Exchanges, Corporate Relocation, REO and Foreclosure Properties, Short Sale Negotiations, land and acreages, and other property sales.
