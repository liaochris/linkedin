Steven Fisher’s Summary

Steve is three-time entrepreneur and is currently Managing Director of Ground Effect Media an online, experiential and word of mouth marketing firm. He has worked in the entertainment and technology industries for the last 16 years and is widely recognized for his views and insights in the areas of Interactive Strategy, Digital Marketing and Social Media. Steve brings a history of successfully designing and launching word of mouth and digital marketing campaigns for the Entertainment, Consumer Products and Technology Industries.

He has written several e-books on the subjects of User Experience, Social Media, Innovation, Online Marketing, and Small Business Management. My current blogs are at StevesBrain.com and SolutionsArePower.com. Currently, he is doing research for his first book, “Innovation Rebels”, which identifies trends and profiles those who are “Innovation Rebels”, is planned for a December 2009 release.

On a personal note, I am an avid pilot, musician and photographer.
