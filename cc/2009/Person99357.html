Theresa Hummel-Krallinger’s Summary

I am a well-respected corporate trainer and performance consultant, but it’s my work as a humorist and motivational speaker that sets me apart. I am a frequently requested speaker on topics of employee morale, professional development, performance improvement and workplace communications. My experience as a professional stand-up comedian enables me to add fun and humor to my consulting and training work. I have over 20 years business experience and have served in a number of leadership roles in a Fortune 100 Company.
