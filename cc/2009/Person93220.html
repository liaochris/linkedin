    Mediamätning i Skandinavien AB (MMS)

    (Information Services industry)

    September 2006 — June 2007 (10 months)

    At MMS, I was leading the media research project "Moving Images 2007" ("Rörliga bilder 2007").

    Description
    The ways of consuming “moving images” have increased dramatically during the recent years to a point where we can talk of an explosion of consumer possibilities. This development, exposing both new possibilities and threats, literally affects every single person working within the moving images industry. In order to to make the right decisions based on high quality information and statistics, Moving Images 2007 has been produced.

    The report from MMS gives a new perspective on moving images by looking at how and how much moving images are consumed on various platforms in Sweden. The report has been sold to a great number of media companies in the nordic countries. I've also held more than 15 talks on the topic in different contexts.The results of the 138 page-report has also been frequently quoted in the Swedish media.
