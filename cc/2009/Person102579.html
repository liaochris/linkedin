Vicki Donlan’s Summary

Vicki Donlan is a business broker with VR Business Brokers in Hingham, MA. Her extensive experience working with small businesses, growing and selling her own, and having a far-reaching network cause her to be a resource every buyer and seller needs.
She is President of Vicki Donlan, an independent consulting firm assisting entrepreneurs to grow their businesses and develop an exit plan.
She has co-founded or founded three women's organizations for the purpose of providing a place for women in business to network.In 1998, she founded, and published, Women's Business, the first and only newspaper giving visibility and ego to women in business in MA, NH and RI, with a controlled circulation of 25,000. In November 2004, sold the newspaper to the Boston Herald and continued to published it to honor a three year contract. In October 2007, published my first book HER TURN Why It's Time For Women To Lead in America (Praeger Publishers), a compilation of my over twenty years of experience as a businesswoman advocating for women in business nationally with 70 interviews from leading male and female thoughtleaders throughout the United States. She consults with businessowners on exit strategies and works with business owners to sell their business, while continuing to speak on the importance of women's leadership at this critical time in American history.

Vicki Donlan’s Specialties:

Specializing on working with buyers and sellers of businesses to reach their dream in owning and exiting business at the right time for the right dollar amount. Consults on business plans, marketing plans and exit strategies and on women's leadership retention and growth at corporations.
