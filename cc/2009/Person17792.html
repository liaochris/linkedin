Chris Peterson’s Summary

Through my career I have held positions which have required me to model, texture, animate, light, and render passes for various projects. This has greatly increased my knowledge and skill as a generalist. I have proven that I work well as part of a team and also independently by effectly doing what needs to be done to get the project completed, on time, and to the quality that everyone expects. Finally, I have been able to streamline several pipelines by utilizing several of my Lscripts, MEL scripts, and Eyeon Fusion scripts.

My ability to problem solve and create tools can be especially valuable to any production.
