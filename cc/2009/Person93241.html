Geoff Taylor’s Summary

Primary teaching and then fulltime ordained ministry for two decades in Western Sydney has been the fodder for soulsupply.com . This website ministry has grown from the countless things that God has taught me.

Influence is by far the best goal to have in life, and it is my goal. The wider the influence the greater the personal impact that can remain on lives touched, and lives changed. Lives changed in this generation will then sow well into subsequent generations. My goal fully realised is for unseen generational impact until Jesus returns.

SoulSupply.com was begun to bring the much needed influence of Jesus' encouragement across this world. ALL people need encouraging, many need it daily. Hence my goal is to flood this globe with the Christian daily encouraging influence of Soul Snacks, all free from soulsupply.com

Thru SoulSupply.com it is also my intention to defend the God of the Cross to those who would listen, that He does not wish suffering upon anyone and He is not the source of malevolence in this world. He just gets unfairly blamed too often, it must hurt Him ever so deeply. God's core is to care - I want the world to know this.
