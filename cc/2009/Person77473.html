Paul Chubbuck’s Summary

I have a Masters in Counseling and am a certified Somatic Experiencing Practitioner (S.E.P),with additional training in Somatic Trauma Resolution (STR), and Polarity Therapy, also body-centered healing approaches. My 30-some year professional and personal growth path — seeking and finding solutions to my own challenges — forms the authentic foundation for what I offer to others. Many kind and wise teachers have generously helped me on my path. As I came to feel increasing success, contentment and fulfillment in my life, I felt called give back — to assist others.

I am compassionate and skilled at helping my clients rediscover joy and excitement in their natural healing abilities.

Contact me to help you decide if Somatic Experiencing® and I can help you become the successful and happy person you want to be.

I practice in Fort Collins, Colorado, and do phone sessions almost anywhere. Email me your questions or to set up an appointment or call me at: 970-493-2958.

Paul Chubbuck’s Specialties:

People who wish to gain a greater sense of trust in their own inner guidance will benefit from this powerful self-awareness work. My work is also particularly successful with helping people to heal from a challenging past, including abuse, loss, or trauma. Often when one feels stuck and unable to achieve the personal or career success they desire, there is something like this in their past. I am very skilled and successful at helping with that.
