Shahood Alam’s Summary

Shahood Alam holds a Certificate in Islamic Finance from CIMA-UK {2009}, a Post-Graduate Diploma in Islamic Banking & Finance from Sheikh Taqi Usmani's CIE-Karachi {2007}, Certified Information System Auditor from ISACA-USA {2005}, MS (Systems Engineering) from QAU-Islamabad {1996}, BE (Computer Systems) from NED-Karachi {1994}, Bachelor of Commerce from KU-Karachi {1991} and over 15 years of diverse professional experience.

Shahood is an Islamic Banker at DIB managing Cash Management. He joined DIB after 4 years of Corporate banking exposure with HSBC. Prior to banking, he worked for 6 years with Aga Khan University Hospital in different capacities. He has worked for almost a year at Philips for their ERP implementation. Earlier, he has more than 3 years exposure of working with Big5 consulting firms like Ferguson (PwC) and Sidat Hyder (Andersen).

He has taught IT-related subjects for more than 3 years at various leading institutes like CBM, HIIT, AKU, COMSATS, JUW and Aligarh Institute mostly as visiting faculty. Throughout his career, he remained involved with leading professional bodies like IEEE, ISACA and CIMA.
