Steve Brown’s Summary

Steve Brown is a professional performer with nearly 20 years of experience in theater and the variety arts. From full-scale theatre work to street performing; festivals to malls, Steve has entertained audiences of all ages in 17 different countries.

He is also experienced in product development (more than 20 different toys and accessories brought from concept to market), brand management (revival of the Duncan Yo-Yo brand), event marketing (managed and coordinated product release tours in half a dozen countries), copy writing, packaging layout, and art direction.

Steve also makes the best grilled cheese sandwiches in the Western Hemisphere.
