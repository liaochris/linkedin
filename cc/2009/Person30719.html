Enrico Giubertoni’s Summary

Enrico Giubertoni is deeply specialized in eCommerce Marketing and Communication techniques.

European Citizen, Blogger (www.buzzes.eu), he writes paper about Marketing applied to Web 2.0, he is Trainer in eCommerce Marketing, Blog Marketing, and Web 2.0 fields.

He has developped Marketing strategies for eCommerce Startups.

With a strong knowledge of B2C eCommerce MTK/Communication strategies, he is now Master Professor and Trainer at Acme about Marketing and Communication in Web 2.0 and Social Media era
