Claudio Teixeira’s Summary

A Sun Certified Architect (SCEA) experienced in creating solutions in high heterogenic and legacy integration environments. An experienced Team Leader with in-depth knowledge of Unified Process and Agile methodologies such as SCRUM and Extreme Programming, delivering successful projects in a variety of programming languages and environments.
