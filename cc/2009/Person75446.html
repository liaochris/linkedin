Rob O'Keefe’s Summary

Rob is the Chief Technology Officer of Arc Aspicio, improving mission performance through business process and technology.

Arc Aspicio is a management consulting and information technology company that specializes in homeland security. Our services include strategic planning, business architecture, strategic communications, mission/technology alignment, information technology, program management, and market research. Arc Aspicio is building a community that promotes collaboration, continuous learning, and innovation to improve homeland security.

Arc Aspicio is an award winning Great Place to Work!

Our awards and honors include:

U.S. Chamber of Commerce Small Business Blue Ribbon Award 2007, 2008, and 2009

Helios Apollo Award Finalist 2007, 2008, and 2009

Mentioned in Washingtonian Great Places to Work 2007

Lead multi-year, multi-project deployments at major organizations to successfully deploy new applications and business functions.
