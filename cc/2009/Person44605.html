Jay Stillman’s Summary

S-Tech Personnel are specialists in providing manpower and consultancy solutions within the Automotive, Aerospace, Marine, MRO and Oil and Gas industries. We have a vast range of high profile clients which covers all aspects of technical and engineering remits.

S-Tech Personnel is a young dynamic recruitment consultancy for the Technical and Engineering industry sectors. Our senior management team are qualified, time served engineers, which enables us to provide a quick and precise response to any technical recruitment brief.

The company adopts a ‘partnership' approach to ensure a detailed understanding of our clients objectives and requirements before recommending the most effective solution from a extensive portfolio of manpower and consultancy services. Used individually or in combination, these solutions is the basis of S-Tech Personnel acting as a continuous business partner to our clients operations.

Utilising our state of the art recruitment IT systems and comprehensive range of solutions, which when partnered with our vast database of candidates, S-Tech Personnel has the ability to deliver a quick response.

S-Tech Personnel has a passion for customer service to our clients and candidates, along with an appreciation for our environment. The company recognise that as a recruitment consultancy it has an input into the lives of our employees, candidates, clients and the community. Our continuous dedication to act ethically as a company is reflected in our social and environmental responsibilities. S-Tech Personnel is a corporate member of the Recruitment and Employment Confederation (REC), is a Tree 100 Company and a Carbon Conscious Company through the Carbon Managers Trust.
