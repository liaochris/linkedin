    Corporate Health & Wellness Walking Program

    The bottom line is that getting people moving saves organizations about $900/person/year in direct medical costs. Less absenteeism due to unmanaged stress and illness. Less health costs. Less Workers’ Compensation and disability claim costs and happier employees.

    By implementing a WalkingSpree wellness program, you’ll not only help your employees RETAIN and REGAIN their health, you’ll also be earning a healthy ROI. Several studies discovered that businesses can earn back from $3 to $8 for each dollar spent on wellness.

    WalkingSpree.com provides a web-connected 10,000 steps wellness program with the Omron USB Pedometer. WalkingSpree has developed a web-based interface for activity, food consumption and body tracking to deliver a dynamic online wellness program using a Web 2.0 technology platform.
