Artur Uzieblo’s Summary

23 years of experience developing Products and Solutions for Operations Support Systems (OSS). Experience in all major OSS areas: Inventory, Order Management, Service Activation, Service Management, Assurance, Network Management. Customer facing, leading roles with Tier-1 companies.
