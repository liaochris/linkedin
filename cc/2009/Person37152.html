Giuseppe Paterno'’s Summary

Experienced architect on networking, security and Linux (wireless, application security, OS security,...). Great experience on investigating root causes of technical problems. Deep market knowledge. Multiplatform OS knowledge (Linux, Solaris, AIX, MacOS, Windows, AS/400, MVS). Excellent expert on networking standards and developing new ones. Interest in all computer issues, mainly in the above areas. Good attitude to research and development projects. Part of the opensource community (ILS, Sikurezza, IEEE,...). Cisco CCNP, Red Hat Certified Security Specialist (RHCSS) and other certifications. Currently writes articles, publications and books, most notably: "security in wireless LANs" and "single sign-on with kerberos and LDAP".
