Dr. Mario Barrett, PhD’s Summary

The Barrett Center for Leadership Development, LLC (TM) is a web-based consulting firm that provides leadership and organizational development training in the form of workshops/seminars, coaching, and speaking engagements to individuals and organizations. The Barrett Center for Leadership Development, LLC (TM) is on the cutting edge in terms of leadership and organizational development.

Our Mission: To help clients develop their leadership from the inside-out.

Our Vision: Uplift the human condition by teaching individuals and organizations how to lead their existence from the inside-out.

Our Creed: Help develop those who want to develop themselves.

Dr. Mario Barrett, PhD’s Specialties:

Personal Leadership and Organizational Development, Organizational Change Consulting, Coaching, and Public Speaking
