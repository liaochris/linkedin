Timothy Banks’s Summary

Professional designer equipped to handle high-end design and creative management. Complete knowledge of the programs necessary to visually communicate: Photoshop, Illustrator, Flash, InDesign and even Quark. Interactive designer who can handle front end design and CSS/Java/XHTML code architecture. No problem working in a Mac or PC environment. Accomplished illustrator able to provide an extra level of service for a design or layout.

Timothy Banks’s Specialties:

Design, Corporate Identity, Layout, Branding, Web, Marketing Design, Expert Vector Skills, Adobe CS4 Expert, Quark Warrior, Digital Illustrator, Traditional Illustrator (Acrylic, Pen and Ink, Watercolor, Sketch).
