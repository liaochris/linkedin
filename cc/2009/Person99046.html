Tim Seitz’s Summary

I'm currently writing a book called 52 Pick Me Ups, about 52 people in my personal business network. Each of these people gives great advice on building a business, sales, customer service or just the right way to treat people.

Networking (not just online) is the way to do business in the 21st century! I'm so glad I haven't had to make a cold call or e-mail sales pitch in over six months, but my business has increased 300%!

I love Mind Movies, I made this one in June and my life has already started changing!

http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oVyVb2IvD_M

By watching this Mind Movie before I go to sleep and right after I wake up, I focus on my goals and give thanks for my blessings. By sharing this video, I inspire, entertain and reach out to network with great people

If you would like to make a Mind Movie, I'll be glad to help!

I've been changing the meaning of merchant services! First off, I don't work for one company, I work for my merchants. That means the customer is ALWAYS right!

Second, I think it means more than credit card processing and payment services, it means making sure your clients are making the most of their business. One of the best ways any business can succeed is to join BNI (see www.bni.com or talk to me about finding a chapter near you).

For the past 3 years I have been speaking to landlords, property managers, software designers, banks, lawyers and renters to develop the best system for collecting the rent on time every time. In January 2008 we launched Automatic Lease, a GREEN solution to getting the rent on time!

I look forward to creating new relationships with landlords, property managers, home owner associations and people interested in automating payments and helping the planet.
