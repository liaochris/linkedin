Jim Bartek’s Summary

Jim's career has been a path of highly custom and cutting edge solutions related to the visualization of information, particularly related to the life sciences.

These solutions have included everything from building one of the largest stereoscopic tiled display walls (imagine 24 projectors making one 12'x7' 3D image you interact with) at the Purdue Envision Center, to determining the most effective way to visually explain complex life sciences concepts to critical audiences.
