Jennifer Lam’s Summary

Jennifer is an über organised digital producer with over 5 years experience in the online media industry. With a strong creative background and solid technical knowledge, she is results-driven and passionate about interactive advertising and production processes.

She is particularly interested in process documentation, business, client & team communication and producing world-class creative.

Between the hours of 10pm and 3am on a weekday and on weekends, Jennifer is also a food blogger, an entrepreneur, author, photographer, wanna-be dressmaker, emoticons collector, shopaholic and an avid golfer.
