Randy Astle’s Summary

My desire is to work on preexisting or new television shows--for preschoolers, older children, tweens, and teens--as a freelancer, staff writer, or story editor.

I've had multiple premises accepted by Handy Manny on Playhouse Disney and am currently working on new shows with Believe Animation in Green Bay and Francis Fitzpatrick, creator of Jakers! The Adventures of Piggley Winks, in Dublin. I have additional screenwriting, film editing, and production experience in productions for adults.

I have nine sample screenplays--Angelina Ballerina, The Backyardigans, Charlie and Lola, Elmo's World, Peep and the Big Wide World, Phineas and Ferb, True Jackson VP, The Wonder Pets, and WordGirl--as well as a full curriculum vitae with production experience at www.randyastle.com.
