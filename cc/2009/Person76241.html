Owen Geronimo’s Summary

Owen Geronimo serves as an Entrepreneur, Social Media/ SEO Marketing Consultant at RGG Global Business Networks and is located in San Francisco, CA. Owen founded RGG Global Business Networks in 2006. Additionally, Owen serves as a Real Estate Adviser at RGG Global Estates, and does International Real Estate Sales at World Properties For Sale (Current) and does Import/Export at RGG Global Enterprises (Current).

He serves on the boards of The Tree Light Foundation, a moderator of the following professional chambers: SF Professional Men’s Group, SF Bay Area Tech Entrepreneurs and California Real Estate Investors Educational Group and the founder of The Real Estate List network.

Owen attended San Francisco State University where he studied Marketing. He also attended Wharton UOP and Trump University where he studied business.

Owen’s core expertise includes 1) Loan Origination 2) Mortgage Financing 3) Social Media, Search Engine Optimization, Search Marketing 4) Public Relations/ Event Promotions and 5) Customer Relations Management.

In his personal time, Owen enjoys playing tennis, curating arts, producing music events, listening to music, reading comic books and business books, cooking, driving to Napa, travel, investing in real estate, researching and studying the global economy. He’s also an active online activist, a blogger an accomplished abstract painter and a digital photographer.
