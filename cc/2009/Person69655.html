Michelle McManus’s Summary

Online Marketing Strategist and Tactician for the global logistics company, OHL with more than 10 years in marketing and 9 in the logistics and b-to-b space.

Looking to increase awareness of our services and company in order to build meaningful relationships that help businesses large and small become more productive, gain much needed visibility to their supply chain and increase customer satisfaction through the partnership that OHL offers.

My ability to adapt, understand and imagine possibilities for communicating via new media marketing channels has allowed OHL to demonstrate thought leadership and transparency while building community in advance of many of our counterparts and industry leaders.

Accolades:
I've written articles on blogging which have been published in 3PL Executive.

Speaking engagements for Belmont University on the subject of blogging, Transportation Marketing and Communications Association on the subjects of SEO, Social Media and Podcasting, and Linkedin.

Founded the Middle Tennessee Blogger and Podcaster's Group as well as This Is Smyrna, TN community website.
