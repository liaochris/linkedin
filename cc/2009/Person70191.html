  • Retail & Wholesale Assistant

    Horrocks Ltd.

    (Retail industry)

    October 2003 — September 2008 (5 years )

    During my time at Horrocks I worked both part-time and full-time and had various responsibilities. The focus is on high quality, preferably local produce.

    - Attending local produce fairs
    - Product placement and stock rotation
    - Handling customer complaints
    - Carrying out deliveries

    - Opening and closing the premises
    - Assisting with marketing activities

    I enjoyed my time at Horrocks and as well as gaining knowledge about the retail industry, I also learnt key skills like customer interaction and teamwork.

Morys Ireland’s Interests:

My main pastime is fencing, I fence at a number of clubs in both Hampshire and West Sussex and compete at as many tournaments around the country as I can. In 2005 I was one of four founder members of Chichester Community Fencing Club and was instrumental in acquiring a £9,000 Lottery Grant used to purchase equipment. I trained to become a coach and have been coaching at the club ever since. In 2008 we decided to incorporate the club into a 'community interest company' to ensure it remains a not-for-profit organisation. So far I have served as both the secretary and a director of the company.
