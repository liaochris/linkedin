Bob Vance AUDIOBOB’s Specialties:

Bob Vance is an audio post editor/sound designer/producer by trade but it was his 20 years in radio that gives him the experience of a seasoned voice over artist. Bob has added his voice to countless ad campaigns. Recently Bob has found favor in the industrial world with his matter-of-fact delivery for Exxon Mobil, Baker-Hughes, OSHA and B.J Services. He’s even found his way into the meetings and events circle by being the pre recorded in-house announcer for award ceremonies around the world.
