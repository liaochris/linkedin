Steph Savill MBA FCIM FInstTT’s Summary

A professional Chartered Marketer, Fellow of the Chartered Institute of Marketing and a professional Strategic Marketer with experience in the automotive, leisure and travel industries.

I run FOXY Lady Drivers Club, a UK motoring support network for women drivers and FOXY Choice where we promote the best and female friendly garages and dealerships to women.

I am passionate about the need for better marketing communications in the motor industry. For example, motorists need to know how to choose the best and safest businesses for garage services such as MOT's, car servicing and repairs.

I want to see independent and down to earth information about this to actively encourage customer choice and healthy competition between independent and franchised businesses. There is a place at the table for both as long as we match the right customer and her expectations, using creative communications.

I think the motor industry should be using the recession as a time for strategic planning. There are many sales opportunities that are being missed because businesses are doing nothing for themselves. For example, there is a yawning female service gap called mistrust and to bridge it the industry and individual businesses need to understand what their target female audience REALLY wants...

Get the aftersales services working and the new car sales will follow, providing the trust is there.

Few understand the female customer in her many different guises, despite the evident value of her business. That is quite a challenge in such a male oriented industry
