Taylor Allis’s Summary

Business executive with more than fifteen years international experience in Information Technology (IT), storage, and technology services industries. Ability to analyze business and customer trends, develop technology business cases, recommend investment strategies, and position and launch new products and services. Previously responsible for strategic, marketing, and operational organizations at Sun Microsystems, StorageTek, Exabyte (now Tandberg Data), Galileo, a Hewlett-Packard distributor, and multiple IT start-ups. Publisher of multiple IT articles and whitepapers. Storage Management blogger for TechTarget’s IT Knowledge Exchange, a site that receives over 90,000 IT visitors a month.
