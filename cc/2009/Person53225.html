Karen Commins’s Summary

I'm a voice-over actor who collaborates with my clients so that your communication can be delivered -- and remembered -- in this frenzied world.

As a voice-over artist, I produce audio recordings of:

* commercials
* narrations for corporate, Internet and televised videos
* podcasts
* e-learning modules
* audiobooks

Past projects have included commercials, narrations, audiobooks, games and phone systems. Credits and clients include Pepsi Cola, ADP, Popeyes Chicken and Biscuits, Delta Airlines, Electronic Arts, BlueCielo ECM, Verizon Wireless, Georgia Department of Transportation, Turner South and NorthStar Audio Books.

My custom-built, soundproof studio is outfitted with industry-standard equipment found in top recording facilities:

Neumann TLM 103 microphone
Focusrite Platinum Voicemaster pre-amp/proc.
Macbook Pro
Pro Tools LE
Telos Zephyr ISDN Transceiver
Panasonic 3700 DAT recorder

You can see pictures of my stunning studio at this page on my web site:

http://www.AVOICEAboveTheCrowd.com/tour.shtml

I can quickly record your script and provide it to you in your choice of file formats, either on-line or on CD or DAT. Many clients receive the final product within hours.

I am available on short notice to record for you in any location throughout the metro Atlanta, GA area. I am a non-union voice talent and can be booked either freelance or through one of my agents. Depending on the length and nature of the project, I also am willing and able to travel to other cities.


I invite you to follow me on Twitter: @KarenCommins
