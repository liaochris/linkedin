Lance Wicks’s Summary

I run enVirtua, a niche consulting business that assists organisations to understand and implement virtualisation, cloud computing and data centre automation.

Coming from a support background in the financial sector originally I have worked for a wide variety of organisations, ranging from small businesses to FTSE 100 firms.

I see my specialty as being able to see the practical benefits of a technology and then applying it to a business requirement.

Outside of "work" I am heavily involved in the sport and martial art of Judo. And am proud to have been a keynote lecturer (twice now) at University of Bath and at the BJA National Performance Centre, where I have spoken on coaching Digital Natives.

I Blog about Judo at www.judocoach.com and also run other Judo websites.
