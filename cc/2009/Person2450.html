Adrian Kosmaczewski, MSc IT - LION’s Summary

I have been involved in the design, development and maintenance of software since 1996, in Argentina, Switzerland, USA and the UK, as a Software Developer and Architect, specializing since 2002 in Open Source technologies, coupled with good knowledge of the Apple Cocoa and Microsoft .NET frameworks.

My goal is to provide the best possible solution to my clients, taking into account their environment, objectives and possibilities, using the best tool for each situation. I have been analyzing situations and providing solutions for over 10 years. I have excellent architectural skills, being able to design and integrate large systems in different environments, coaching teams through projects and providing guidance for quality issues.
