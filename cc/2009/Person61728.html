Senior Management Professional with 13 years of IT Solutions & Services experience in roles such as Chief Executive Officer, Business Head, Business Development Manager, Account Manager, Partner Manager, Presale, Sales Support, Global Delivery Manager, Project Manager & Consultant for European, U.S & Asian Customers. Verticals: Media, Mobile, Retail, Manufacturing, eGovernance & Retail Banking. Horizontals: ECM, BPM, BI, SOA, Enterprise Collaboration, eCommerce, KM & CRM.

Head the European and North American Businesses for Vishwak apart from Directorial responsibilities in Vishwak Solutions Limited, U.K. Goal is to grow Vishwak's European and North American Businesses and establish Vishwak as a Global Player. Part of the Vishwak Group Corporate Management. Established & manage VSL, U.K.

Previous role at VSPL, headed the Enterprise Solutions SBU owning Revenue Targets, Partner Management, Business Strategy and verticals such as Media, Retail, eGovernance & Professional Services Group. Established Media, Retail Verticals & Professional Services Group.

Successfully managed application development projects of various sizes, product development, migration projects, support operations & services portfolio.

Prior to my experience with SISL, worked with Sonata Software Limited where I began my IT career.

Hold a Diploma in Business Management from ICFAI after graduation from BITS, Pilani. A PMP, PM@Siemens, certified Internal Auditor & Six Sigma Green Belt Professional.
