Stefano Epifani’s Summary

Stefano Epifani is professor of “interactive communication” in the faculty of communication sciences at the University of Rome “La Sapienza”. He has more than 10 years of experience in e-business process management gained working with some of the most important Italian IT company. Actually, he’s managing director of Info Solutions, a consulting firm which works in the development of e-learning and knowledge management project. He is e-Learning & Knowledge Management responsible of the “Istituto Superiore per lo sviluppo del terzo settore” (IST), founding associate of the Institute for the Study of the History and Economic Doctrine (ISSDE), associate of the Italian Society for the Advancement of Science (SIPS).
