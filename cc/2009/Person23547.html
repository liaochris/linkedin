Dave Levinson’s Summary

Cranial Tap, Inc. develops cutting edge solutions geared towards helping businesses, academia and organizations realize the potential of virtual world environments. Our technologies serve as a platform for global collaboration, sharing of ideas, conducting of research, telework, embedded learning / training systems and branding. Visual 3D environments support these goals in ways never before possible.

Want to learn more about making your business more productive? Reach out to:
info [at] CranialTap.com.

Dave Levinson’s Specialties:

Online entertainment and video game professional with more than 20 years of experience. Responsible for launching nearly 100 consumer products ranging from online video games to VoIP user interfaces. Author of six worldwide patents in the virtual world space. Inventor of the successful AOL SuperBuddy 3D characters. Co-founder and former Chair of the Orlando Chapter of SIGGRAPH. Dave is also an author, artist, inventor and visionary. He often speaks on topics related to the virtual world space.
