Heather Whaling’s Summary

An award-winning, seasoned communicator, Heather fuses strategic thinking, strong writing skills and creativity to deliver public relations and marketing results that help clients achieve their business goals.

She advises businesses and non-profits about Web 2.0's influence on online and offline communication -- and the value of incorporating emerging media strategies with traditional approaches. Not one to just "talk the talk," Heather maintains a presence on multiple social networks, manages the agency's blog and maintains her own personal site.

Heather delivers public relations and marketing services in a variety of industries, including professional services (e.g., law and accounting firms), healthcare, nonprofits, real estate/development, software/technology, education and public affairs. Her clients have been featured in TechCrunch, Fortune Small Business, Entrepreneur Magazine, the New York Times, Wall Street Journal, Associated Press, Washington Post, New York Daily News and the Miami Herald, as well as regional and trade publications.

Heather Whaling’s Specialties:

Strategic planning; media relations; interactive marketing and social media; branding; crisis communication; message development and deployment; public affairs and grassroots organizing; event planning; technical, news and feature writing; and public speaking.
