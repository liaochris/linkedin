Felix Faassen’s Summary

My experience covers software development, internet technology and security. My specialties are to do with system integration, software development. I am a creative person and can be of value as an architect, consultant or designer. My power lies within the combination of drive, a deep knowledge of ICT and a good set of communicational skills. I learn fast and I like to not only apply this knowledge, but also share this with others.
