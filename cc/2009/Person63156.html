Mark Anthony’s Summary

My talent is balancing a grasp of details and a focus on results. Over 20 years experience and a rare combination of technical, business, communication, and people skills contribute to my capacity to identify and align client business and technology needs.

I am at my best when designing, communicating, and facilitating solutions. My success often lies in my ability to help stakeholders see things in new ways and from new perspectives. I am trained in both quantitative and qualitative research methods, and I am driven by a passion for user-centered design and usability.

In addition to multiple IBM and HP certifications and a long history of IT experience, I also have practical business and management. I love all things geeky, but I'm more into the big picture and strategy than I am the guy who sits and codes for days on end. I prefer to work on the analysis, design, writing, and usability ends of the project, and I thrive when I can experience a variety of new challenges and projects. I prefer to work with people than to hide from them.
