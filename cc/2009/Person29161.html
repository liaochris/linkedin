Ed and Cherie Fields’s Summary

Projects Unlimited, Inc., an Association Management Company (AMC) based in Birmingham, Alabama. As an AMC, we provide operational support and leadership to member and donor-based nonprofit associations including professional, trade and civic organizations.

We specialize in the full service management of nonprofit associations and our services include administrative management, financial management, strategic planning, membership development, communications, and meeting planning, among other services.

Our slogan is "relax, it's handled" because our clients can relax knowing that the affairs of the associations they care about are being handled with excellence when we are working for them.
