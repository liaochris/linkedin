Ginger Galloway Reichl’s Summary

Pinstripe Marketing is a full-service agency with a focus on professional services firms, including information technology, law, finance, healthcare, engineering, architecture and more.

We are fortunate to have a team with extensive experience planning and executing strategic marketing campaigns for a broad range of clients – from small start-ups to established firms and Fortune 500 companies. We offer comprehensive agency services such as brand development, copywriting, advertising, collateral material, web design, e-newsletters, blogs, podcasts, media buying, public relations, and event planning.

We are dedicated to providing exceptional service and appreciate each of our clients. If we aren't the best fit for you, we will recommend an agency that may better meet your needs. We can be found on the web at www.pinstripemarketing.com
