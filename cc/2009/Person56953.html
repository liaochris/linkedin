Kurt Swanke’s Summary

Kurt brings over 20 years of experience designing product management, marketing & demand creation strategies for various technology companies. His background includes numerous vertical markets and encompasses subjects from start-ups to Fortune International rated corporations.

Kurt recently embarked on an ambitious campaign to update and improve the entire Acsys Interactive product suite based on client feedback & industry best practices. He has already made great strides in bolstering Acsys' reputation as a world-class provider of full service interactive solutions.

Over his career, Kurt has held several senior management-level product & marketing positions, including those at CT TyMetrix, a Wolters Kluwer business. At CT TyMetrix, Kurt led the product management & marketing teams for this progressive ASP.net software provider, architecting deployment of resources to drive growth of sales by 89%. Balancing a strategic vision with pragmatic, results-driven deliverables, Kurt implemented several pilot initiatives, including a client advisory board and market research campaigns that revolved around customer satisfaction, prospect assessment & product development surveys. He also designed the company's award-winning websites in the US and Europe in addition to the company extranet and intranet.

Prior to his time at CT TyMetrix, Kurt held positions of increasing authority at Konica Minolta & Gerber Scientific Products. At Konica Minolta, Kurt managed all software & hardware product lines for an $800-million division & created various launch programs to support the efforts of an international, 2,000-person sales force. That role followed his success at Gerber Scientific, where he devised & executed a marketing plan for a fledgling group that saw a subsequent ten-fold increase in sales revenue.

Kurt holds both a B.S. degree in Marketing and an International M.B.A degree from Central Connecticut State University.
