Karthik Mani’s Summary

Profile
15 years experience in leading cross-functional teams of technical, functional and business focused members across product management, product marketing, business development and product development.

b) Strong command of product marketing techniques from conception to end-of-life; led launches of 7 new products.
c) Proven leadership in change management and transformation of more than 200 people team, located in both India and the US, through high growth as well as tough market conditions.

e) Skilled in developing and managing processes to balance roadmaps between customer requests and market requirements.
f) Extensive background in process design and implementation of demand planning, supply chain planning, factory planning and inter-enterprise collaboration solutions combined with in-depth knowledge of supply chain dynamics in multiple industries.
