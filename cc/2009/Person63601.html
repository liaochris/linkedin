Mark Roah’s Experience

  • SVP, Business Development

    OCTANE360

    (Internet industry)

    May 2006 — Present (3 years 3 months)

    OCTANE360 is an emerging technology-driven Internet marketing firm focused on revolutionizing the way businesses capture targeted traffic online. OCTANE360 powers its business via its proprietary inbound marketing platform, OCTANE.

    OCTANE is a one-stop inbound marketing technology that allows anyone from a small business owner to the most savvy of online marketers to turn their online presence into a revenue and growth center. Today OCTANE powers thousands of websites owned by a growing list of publishers, bloggers, online marketers, national advertisers and small businesses. OCTANE fuels their clients’ businesses by enabling them to capture highly sought after organic search engine ranking, targeted leads, and focused consumers. The company is headquartered in Los Angeles, CA.
