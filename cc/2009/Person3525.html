Alex Moore’s Summary

Alex is an entrepreneur. He is the co-founder and Managing Director of the list sharing and social recommendation site snagsta.com. Before this Alex worked as head of Digital Business Development at digitaltmw (the digital arm of TMW - Tullo Marshall Warren). From 1997 to the beginning of 2006, Alex was the Managing Director of award winning digital marketing company Web Guru Asia (a company he co-founded with Jeff Zweig).

Alex Moore’s Specialties:

Managing company profitability and growth, business development, financial management, product development, branding and positioning, systems and operations management.
