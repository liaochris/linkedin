Chris Magnani’s Summary

Talented in a wide variety of disciplines, I have successfully leveraged my skills and experience for companies to provide services in Software Quality Assurance testing, website and online publishing testing, graphic communications, user support, and interactive media design. At Electronic Arts, I tested online sales systems and a newly produced promotional system.
