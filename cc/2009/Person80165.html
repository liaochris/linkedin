Joseph Pobereskin’s Summary

Based in the Chicago metro area, my commercial & editorial assignments have taken me around the world & have led to publication in more than fifty countries. I studied photography at the Fashion Institute of Technology in New York, and for eleven years thereafter I apprenticed with some of New York's finest photographers, acquiring expertise in various disciplines.

While my photographic interests cover a broad spectrum, I specialize in Location Photography for top-level Advertising and Corporate clients including: AIG, AT&T, American Airlines, American Express, Ameritech, Bank of America, Boston Acoustics, Cantor Fitzgerald, Cessna, Columbia Pictures/Sony, Comsat, Continental Airlines, Criimi Mae, Discovery Channel, Walt Disney Imagineering, DuMaurier/International Tobacco Ltd, Emirates Airline, ExxonMobil, Florida Power & Light, Foldcraft, Hewlett Packard, International Gold Corp., Kodak, Lincoln Center For The Performing Arts, Lucent Technologies, Marriott Hotels, Mazda, Mellon Bank, Meredith Corp., Miramax, Motorola, NASA, NBC Television, NYC & Co/New York Convention & Visitors Bureau, The New York Times, Nikon, Northern Telecom, Northwest Airlines, Olympus, Orbitz, Rockefeller University, Samsung, Schieffelin & Somerset, Siemens, Sprint, Steelcase, Stewart's Root Beer, Taco Bell, Tokyo Electric Power Company, Trump Organization, Unisys, UPS, US Airways, US Postal Service, Verizon, The Wall Street Journal, Warner Brothers, WTZA-TV, Yahoo!.

Editorial clients include: American Archaeology, American Heritage, Attache, BBC, Better Homes & Gardens, The Boston Globe, Buyside, Forbes, GEO (Germany), Illume (Japan), Invention & Technology, Meridian, Mutual Funds Magazine, The New York Times, Newsweek, Orbit, ProximaViagem (Brazil), Realtor, Research Magazine, Retail Interiors (UK), Travel & Leisure, Travel Holiday, Vanidades.

Joseph Pobereskin’s Specialties:

Advertising, Annual Reports, Capability Brochures, Corporate Magazines, Editorial, Posters. Distillation of complex marketing concepts into a single image through application of professional photographic, staging, lighting and digital imaging techniques. Direction of models and talent, casting, location scouting and editing. Arrangement of intricate logistics, estimating, bidding and cost control; invoicing, supervision of accounting and record keeping, and other management functions.
