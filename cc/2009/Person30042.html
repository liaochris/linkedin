Elisabeth McMillan’s Summary

Equestrian business consultant and the owner/editor of www.EquestrianProfessional.com, a website that provides career support to professional equestrians. Keynote speaker at equestrian organization meetings around the U.S.

Owned & operated a successful Hunter/Jumper barn -20+ years, including international horse sales, and an onsite tack store. Also spent time riding cutting horses, breaking race horses and have dabbled in Dressage and Reining.
