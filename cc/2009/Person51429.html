Joseph Majewski’s Summary

JPM Construction Consultants, Inc. is a full service consulting, estimating, and project management firm based in Spring Lake New Jersey and serving the construction industry nation wide.

JPM is able to provide complete certified cost estimating services (all levels, budget to definitive) performed by certified professional estimators. Our cost control, certified construction inspections, certified project management and scheduling services are utilized in project management and claims preparation and evaluation. We are experienced in all construction disciplines from site work to instrumentation and provide construction management services for projects of varying scope and size. These services are preformed on an as required basis and are geared to support architects, attorneys, owners and contractors in the commercial, institutional, industrial and utility fields.

JPM Construction Consultants can furnish computer-generated estimates, schedules (P3, CPM or Gant), budget forecasts and percentage completions.

We are recognized by the A.S.P.E. (American Society of Professional Estimators), A.A.C.E. (American Association of Cost Engineers), C.S.I. (Construction Specifications Institute), A.A.A. (American Arbitration Association), A.C.I. (Association of Construction Inspectors) and the N.J.N.S.P.E. (New Jersey National Society of Professional Engineers).

If you would like more information regarding the services we can provide, please visit our website at http://jpmconstructionconsultants.com
