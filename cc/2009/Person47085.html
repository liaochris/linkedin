Josh Beil’s Summary

I have a market research background in technology, with a focus on PC/server shipments, web hosting, data centers, and outsourced and managed services.

I applied this market knowledge in my first real entrepeneurial effort, co-founding a wireless ISP, Skywave Broadband, in my hometown on Honolulu. As a member of the Board of Directors and strategic advisor, I hope to make Skywave as successful as possible through new customer acquisition and strategic alliances.

I was most recently Director of Offer Management at Level 3 Communications, in the Social Media & Technology Content Markets Group where I focus on social networks, video aggregators, virtual worlds, SaaS, and mobile entertainment.

Josh Beil’s Specialties:

- Domain expertise in wireless broadband, Web hosting, managed services, utility computing, online gaming and virtual worlds.
- Market research, analysis, authoring reports, presenting.
- Communicating with press and providing quotes.
- Design and management of competitive intelligence Web sites.
