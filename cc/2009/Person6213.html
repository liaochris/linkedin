Aneesh Mathai’s Summary

13 years experience in software development for Real Time Embedded Systems. Most of my experience (10 years) is on Automotive Driver Information Systems such as Instrument Clusters and Infotainment Systems. Began my career in a small startup, then an Indian MNC, US MNC and now with a European MNC. Worked on products for major global automotive OEMs.

Aneesh Mathai’s Specialties:

Started my career with writing software in assembly language, later moved to C, and then C++ (OOPS). In the same line, I started with 8bit Microchip PIC microcontroller and now working on 64bit MIPS processor. Vehicle Networking is one of my areas of interest. International experience in USA, Germany and China besides India.
