Andrew Paterson’s Summary

Andrew Paterson is a European web-entrepreneur, father, friend, triathlete, citizen-networker and eco-enthusiast. Andrew is the Founder - President of Laneo; created in 2007, Laneo is a global initiative that connects people, brands and environmental groups to clean up the planet. The Andrew Paterson Foundation, established in 2008, helps get underway social, humanitarian and environmental initiatives that, without our help, could not start, launch, deliver or grow. Andrew is currently working on two other web-based projects; one centered on collaborative social aid and the other, an online educational game for very young children that are scheduled to launch in 2010. He also maintains 3 Blogs and runs 2 monthly networking groups.

Prior to his entrepreneurship, Andrew held successful Senior Management positions at Hertz, Shell and Compass Group specializing in Customer facing operations. He has taught at INSEAD Executive Education and HEC and is a frequent speaker at Business Schools and Internet Conferences on the impact of new technology on global issues.

Additionally, Andrew is an accomplished athlete and confirmed outdoor fanatic, having participated in more than 250 triathlons throughout the world, 15 of which were Ironman. Today he concentrates his spare time on Trail Running, Rock Climbing and Surfing because of his present workload. Other hobbies outside sports include, Folk Guitar, Organic Food, Alternative and Renewable Energy, Indigenous Peoples and Writing and Animating Children's Stories. He currently lives in Southern France with his wife and young son.

Andrew Paterson’s Specialties:

Customer Relationship Management (CRM), Sales Force Automation (SFA), Collaborative, Community & Hosted Services (Web 2.0), Business Process Reengineering (BPR), Enterprise Incentive Management (EIM), Balanced Scorecard Management (BSC), Corporate Social Responsiblity (CSR)
