Soheill Deriss’s Summary

I'm a driven creative with great eye for the big picture and details who likes to add a special artistic touch to productions where it fits. Although I love the production work, I find that the creative process, idea / concept, of a production is as equal important, and that's where I like to give input and come up with great ideas to the table.
