Angela Hill’s Summary

Since 1992, I have worked in the fields of design, marketing and branding in St. Louis, San Francisco and San Diego. In 2004, I started my firm Incitrio and am the president and creative director.

We work with innovative, growing companies looking to take their positioning and revenue to the next level.

Our services include:
branding - strategy and analysis
design - identity, collateral, catalogs, annual reports, tradeshow, packaging, etc.
web - design & programming, e-commerce, intranet, customized wordpress, SEO, PPC, html newsletters, social media campaigns, etc.
advertising - print: magazines, billboards, etc.

Angela Hill’s Specialties:

branding, brand strategy, brand analysis, graphic design, advertising, marketing, networking, identity, catalog, annual reports, marketing collateral, tradeshow, promotion, tradeshow displays, packaging, point of purchase displays, photo art direction, ad copy, e-commerce, SEO, PPC, electronic marketing, html newsletters
