Devin Holmes’s Summary

I have over 15 years of technology experience in a variety of roles at start-ups and large corporations building innovative business-driven technology-savvy organizations. I have led software development and professional services teams globally as well as held executive management team positions at YUDU Media, The New York Times Company and OneSoft Corporation. Most recently, I was engaged as a consultant at Cisco Systems to help IT and Marketing executives in a $1Billion initiative. Leveraging both my fast-paced startup experience and a mature organizational focus, I understand how to bridge the gap between technology and business while delivering measurable top line and bottom line results.

I have two MBA’s and a BS in Engineering from top global educational institutions, have dual Italian and US citizenship and am fluent in English and Italian.
