David Hope’s Summary

As of December 2008 Vice President & Regional Managing Director for Lawson Software Asia Pacific & Japan having earlier been responsible for Lawson's business in Asia since April 2006. Prior to Lawson held the position of Regional Vice President for Intentia's Asia operations since 2001 joining Intentia from IBM where I was the Asia Pacific Director of Software alliances based in Singapore. Prior to Lawson, Intentia & IBM I worked with a subsidiary of Alcatel in Asia as a regional marketing director and before joining Alcatel I worked for 6.5 years with the Japanese trading house ITOCHU based in Sydney & Tokyo. I have lived and worked in Asia for over 26 years.

David Hope’s Specialties:

Asia Pacific, sales, marketing, business development, new market entry, manufacturing & distribution focus. Specialised focus and experience in building businesses in the Asia pacific region. Experience includes working in the software industry, IT, Manufacturing and minerals and metals commodities trading industries.

David Hope’s Experience

  • Vice President & Regional Managing Director, Asia Pacific

    Lawson Software

    (Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; LWSN; Computer Software industry)

    April 2006 — Present (3 years 8 months)

    In April 2006, Lawson Software and Intentia merged to form the new Lawson which is now the third largest publically listed ERP software company globally. Lawson delivers software and implementation services to 4,000 customer sites in manufacturing, distribution, retail and services industries across 40 countries. In Asia Pacific Lawson has offices in Australia, New Zealand, Japan, China, Hong Kong, Taiwan, Singapore, Malaysia, a JV in Thailand and business partners covering the rest of the markets across the Asia Pacific region.

    Lawson's Financials, Human Capital Management, Supply Chain Management, Business Intelligence, Asset Management and industry-specific solutions help customers streamline processes and enhance their business performance. Lawson's aim is to radically simplify the process of deploying and using its applications and help customers reduce costs while increasing their flexibility.

  • Vice President, Intentia Asia Pacific Pte Ltd (now Lawson Software)

    Intentia

    (Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; Computer Software industry)

    October 2001 — April 2006 (4 years 7 months)

    Intentia was a specialised software company in the ERP & supply chain market focused on providing solutions to mid sized companies who specialise in manufacturing and distribution across a range of specialised industry market segments. Prior to Intentia's merger with Lawson the company was one of Europe's largest software companies. In my role with Intentia I managed the regional AP business through a mix of both direct and channel partners across the AP region.
