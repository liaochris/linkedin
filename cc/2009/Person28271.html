James Hogg’s Summary

James P Hogg is a PhD Candidate in Education in the field of Instructional Technology with a cognate focus in Hospitality. He is also an Adjunct Faculty member at the Rosen College of Hospitality Management at the University of Central Florida. He graduated from The University of South Carolina in 1992 with a BS degree in Hospitality Management and 1994 with a Masters Degree in Hospitality Management with a focus in Strategic Management. He has spent 18 years in hospitality operations management with various companies including 8 years with The Walt Disney Company. He has been a General Manager, Training Manager, Operations Manager, Duty Manager and a Corporate Troubleshooter. His experience is in Restaurants, Hotels, Events, Resorts, and Theme Parks.
