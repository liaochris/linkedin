Christy Fournier’s Summary

Christy Fournier became a Realtor® in 2002. The Fourniers have lived in Northwest Arkansas since 1996. Now they continue to enjoy the constantly changing and growing area that Northwest Arkansas has become.

In March 2006, Christy received her Arkansas Real Estate Brokers license. She is a member of the National Realtors association, the Arkansas Board of Realtors and the Rogers Board of Realtors. Other professional organizations that Christy belongs to are the Business Women's Association, Women in Networking, and served on the Eureka Springs Parks & Recreation Commission as Secretary from 2002 - 2003. Christy served two terms as Treasurer for the Rogers Toastmasters club as well as Secretary to the Board of the newly founded Northwest Arkansas ALS Association where she now serves as Vice President, President Elect. She is also an alumnus of the Louisiana Lions Club Camp for children with disabilities.
In addition to English, Christy is fluent in American Sign Language & French. If you asked Christy why she became and continues to be a Realtor, she would say for the enrichment and fulfillment of her life and of course, for the fun!
Her mission is to provide superior customer and client services by dedicating herself to a higher standard of professionalism; striving continually for higher education and a better quality of life for herself as well as others around her. She will focus all of her energy and resources with uncompromising honesty and integrity toward meeting the needs of her clients!
If you are looking for Knowledge, Experience, Professionalism, Integrity, and Personal Service in a REALTOR®, Christy Fournier possesses all these qualities.
