Steve Goodman’s Summary

I'm a co-founder of SquareFeet, a smarter way to find and rent apartments.

I enjoy ideas, improving ideas, and making ideas reality. To wit, I'm an ideas man. Learning and growing are what I do best.

I’ve been in the web development field for over 10 years, and I have worked on a wide range of projects, from data exploration/visualization to inventory tracking to expert systems.

I was a co-founder of CfDev.com (now Zrinity) back in 1999, one of the first companies building sophisticated ColdFusion components, such as the very popular ActivEdit WYSIWYG editor.

At the Immune Tolerance Network, I helped develop a specimen management system which allowed an increase of over 300% in specimen throughput from 2003 to 2006. I also developed an RNA specimen quality analysis tool, in conjunction with ITN core laboratory management staff.

I spent a two years as an independent IT consultant, which was a very personal, gratifying endeavor. I helped SmartLogic Solutions streamline their project management processes, and establish strong relationships with clients and business partners.
