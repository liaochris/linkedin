Rachel Pradhan’s Summary

Indra Successful Coach Marketing is Sister Company to Blaze Interactive, an award-wining web design and interactive company in Orange County, CA. With Indra Successful Coach Marketing we help life and business coaches easily create custom, easy-to-edit websites. We also provide our coaching clients with blogs, enewsletters, podcasts and online video.

Our ongoing support includes Content Management Systems (so our clients don't have to call for small changes), monthly maintenance at low rates, per project and hourly rates.
