Rafael Van Dyke’s Summary

Rafael is a seasoned professional with over 15 years of experience in IT, which began as an instructor – teaching introductory and advanced courses in Windows, Microsoft Office, networking, and other software. Since then, Rafael has also taught ASP.NET courses, and has mentored many junior and intermediate developers in several capacities.

Rafael has spent the past 10 years developing solutions for companies with Microsoft technologies and 5 years of experience working with.NET. This experience also includes designing and maintaining SQL Server databases. He has a solid understanding of object oriented and component-based methodologies, as well as n-tier Client/Server.

Rafael is a MCP in Web Applications using Visual Studio (C#), and is working on becoming a Microsoft Certified Professional Developer.
