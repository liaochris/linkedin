Danny Devriendt’s Summary

After six years working as a journalist for radio, TV and print, I started working as a communications and PR consultant. I have over 10 years of experience in international b-to-b and b-to-c communications in Technology and Corporate environments.

I conducted over 280 international media training sessions, and a plethora of speaker- and presentation skill training sessions, mostly on top-management level.
Organising and managing public relations, events and conference content management, internal/external communication plans, and business centered communications are amongst my key capabilities.
I am also a dab hand in finding creative audio-visual support for events and key-notes.
As a communication 3.0 specialist, I integrate the classic ways of communicating with the exciting new possibilities of interactive media. Blogs, podcasts and online communities are my online habitat. www.heliade.net
