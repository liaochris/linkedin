Anne Carey’s Summary

A meeting planner for over ten years, Anne Carey has managed meetings from 10 to 2900 attendees. A certified meeting professional with a BS in Hospitality Management -- and a concentration in Meetings and Events -- from Roosevelt University, she lived in Spain for two years and has a BA in Spanish from Tulane University. For eight years "Carey" (her nickname) executed a 4-day annual conference and trade show with 2900 attendees, 250 booths, 160 speakers, 84 concurrent sessions and 20 workshops.

She specializes in the management of association meetings, including budget design and financial management; site selection; contract negotiation; event marketing and brochure production; speaker coordination; registration management; conference logistics; pre-planning and on-site management of A/V, catering and registration staff; post event financial reporting; attendee surveys; customer service; exhibit hall management; coordination and supervision of 3rd party suppliers and service providers.

Anne Carey’s Specialties:

Negotiating meeting contracts, speaker liaison, supplier management, event marketing, budgeting, site selection, onsite management, exhibition management
