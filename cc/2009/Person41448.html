Ingrid Kopp’s Summary

I have been working in independent film for the past 10 years. Currently I run Shooting People in the US which allows me to indulge in my love for both film and technology.

Documentaries make me happy.

I am passionate about living and participating in a vibrant creative community (ideally with healthcare for all!)

Despite all this I sometimes dream about raising goats in the middle of nowhere. But this feeling usually passes.
