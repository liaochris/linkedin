Stephen Cobb’s Summary

As a successful serial entrepreneur in several verticals I accumulated expertise as an evangelist for new products and product positioning. I am now striving to apply that expertise at Monetate, a startup focused on improving online experiences through innovative post-click strategies.

I enjoy educating new markets to speed adoption of game-changing technologies. I'm also a subject matter expert in information security and privacy strategy, known for developing predictive risk and threat models, increasing awareness of security and privacy issues through education and articulation, and creating innovative, profit-enhancing solutions to security and privacy problems.

Currently engaged in social media and new media research--blogs, Twitter, soc nets, and such like--particularly with respect to new economic and socio-political challenges. Also thinking about business continuity, risk displacement, trust erosion and social impact of security breaches.

Stephen Cobb’s Specialties:

Analyzing opportunities for innovative technology to bring increased efficiency and reduced risk to markets and systems. Evangelizing products that address those opportunities and educating markets to embrace new solutions. Analyzing and predicting risks to security and privacy of data; establishing standards of due care; education about, and articulation of, security and privacy challenges at all levels; pioneering of innovative security and privacy solutions.
