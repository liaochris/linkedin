Rob Toker’s Summary

As the leader of the SCC Americas Technology Solutions Group, Robert Toker is focused on identifying how emerging technologies can be commercially deployed into a range of renewable energy solutions. Rob brings to this role broad experience in developing deep, convergent technology businesses, often at the intersection of private enterprise and public policy.

Technology is the coin of the realm in both supply side and demand side management clean energy projects. The application of appropriate technologies that reduce risk and increase returns is central to our mission and to the realization of positive outcomes for our firm and our project partners.
