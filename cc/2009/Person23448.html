Dave Edwards’s Summary

Corporate executive responsible for ensuring the success of our Engineering Services Division's (ESD) customers, employees, and stakeholders. Contributes to various advisory boards as well as invited speaker for academic and membership organizations.

Professional Goal: to create and sustain a systems engineering products and services organization preferred by both customers and employees.

ESD provides professional systems engineering products and services to government, healthcare and transportation market sectors.

Specializing in Precision and High Reliability Computer, Electronics and Electromechanical Systems for

Maritime: Surface, Subsurface, Small Marine Craft, Antonymous/Unmanned Craft - (Navigation, Communication, Emissions Monitoring, I/T and Radar)

Rail: Geographical Information Systems (GIS), Control, Communication and Health Monitoring Systems

Healthcare: I/T and Biomedical systems, and devices

Types of support services provided: Hardware and Software Systems Design, Technical Services, Field Engineering, Electronic Repair, Systems Installation and Support, System Integration and Production Services Technical Training, Technical Writing, Logistics and Material Support Services, Integrated Logistics Support (ILS), , Programmatic, Acquisition, and Administrative Support
