Dave McClure’s Summary

I'm a geek, former developer & entrepreneur, startup investor, tech blogger, marketing nerd, and overall fan of entrepreneurship. I've lived in Silicon Valley for 20 years, and loved every minute of it.

I currently run a seed-stage investment program for Founders Fund called FF Angel, which makes $50K-250K investments in startups raising <$1M in capital. I also run the fbFund Incubator program which makes $25-100K investments in Facebook-related startups.

Overall, i advise & invest in geeks & entrepreneurs who create great new consumer and small business internet startups. In particular, i focus on helping startups improve their product development & marketing process, by emphasizing the use of simple, actionable metrics to drive startup success.
