James Thomas’s Summary

I have a formal education in Physics/Astrophysics from the University of California at Irvine with a strong emphasis on laboratory methods and instrumentation. I have also acted as project manager on a variety of smaller aerospace/defense contracts. I have worked in a variety of laboratory settings and continue to develop my knowledge of scientific & technology development. My core technical experience is in applied plasma physics, space physics and technology, optics and lasers, data acquisition & analysis, and computational methods. I conduct private research on space exploration and development with a focus on the utilization of space resources and human settlement of the nearby space environment.

I've also worked on the preparation of a considerable number of aerospace/defense related contracts with a higher than average industry win rate. Sponsoring agencies include Air Force Research Labs (AFRL), Missile Defense Agency (MDA), DARPA, NAVY, ARMY Tacom, and NASA.

I am also a web media developer and Search Engine Optimization (SEO) consultant with experience in XHTML/CSS/PHP and SEO/SEM/Analytics methodologies.

My current interests include the emergence of the new space industry, human settlement, and space commerce. My focus is on the development of the near-Earth system, ISRU, accessing and using space resources, and human habitats.
