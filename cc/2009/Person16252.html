Carlos Cardona’s Summary

On the forefront of Macintosh computer and server technology for the last fifteen years, I am an Apple Certified Technical Coordinator (OS X servers), and Apple Certified Help Desk Specialist. I have set up and managed Apple Xserves, Xraids, Apple Xsan (for video), and many Mac OS X Servers. I was one of the first New Yorkers to setup Mac OS X Server (in 2000 at Columbia Univ.), and before that taught Appleshare Server at NYC schools.

I create custom OSX builds and packages with The Casper Suite (the leading Enterprise Management suite of applications, which includes Imaging/Patching, Mac & PC Inventory, Management Policies, SOX reporting, etc.) I have setup creative video workflows with Final Cut Server, print workflows with VersionCue Server, and setup and managed AFP, SMB, NFS, FTP, SU Servers, Portable Home Directories, Netboot, Netinstall, Web and other server technologies, as well as large Backup Systems (NetVault certified). I’ve built FileMaker databases, company intranets, managed web projects.

As a former Production Artist with an electronics background, I am uniquely qualified to support designers and production staff, audio and video users, indeed users of all types. I was mentored by one of the top Mac consultants in New York, and am a supporting member of MetroMac. Please see my Linkedin profile at http://www.linkedin.com/in/ccardona for more information.

Design firms, Agencies, Publishers, Video Editors-all creatives can benefit from better tools and systems than the ones they typically use. Photoshop, InDesign, and Final Cut Pro are quality tools, but not everyone in the company needs them in order to join in the creative process. A “parallel workflow”, and having the correct server products, (like Final Cut Server, Version Cue, etc.) can increase productivity tremendously.
