Robert Imbriale’s Summary

Robert provides a unique perspective on creating a successful business. His backgroud includes have been online since 1984, having served as Director of Marketing for 3 large direct marketing firms before leaving the corporate world in 1995 to go out on his own. He has since written a best-selling book, "Motivational Marketing" and has spoken at hundreds of live events, delivered countless teleseminars, web casts, and has worked with more than 3,000 people one-on-one.

His work combines the best of many worlds including marketing, advertising, psychology, motivation, and much more which creates his very unique, and highly profitable approach to business. His clients can best be described as "raving fans" and with good reason. Robert comes from the heart and tells it like it is in a way that's refreshingly powerful.

Robert is also host of "The Motivational Marketing Show" a weekly motivational radio show available on iTunes and on the web.
