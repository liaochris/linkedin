David Spinks’s Summary

I am passionate about studying the growth of social media and emerging technologies to help businesses communicate and engage with their communities. I write at my personal blog at www.davidspinks.com and occasionally write at other professional's blogs and Mashable.com

With such unfamiliar concepts comes both success and failure. By discussing our experiences and ideas through social media, we become catalysts for the spread of knowledge and can develop new, creative concepts into relevant applications for businesses and individuals. I learn something new every day from innovative leaders that share their ideas and experiences while gradually learning from my own.

I aim to develop a career, and pursue my goal of becoming a thought leader, in social media/marketing and emerging technologies.
