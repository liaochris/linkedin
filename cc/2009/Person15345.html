Carla Nix’s Summary

Advanced Toastmaster with strong skills and experience in leadership. Obtained numerous awards and credentials in public speaking through Toastmasters International.

Currently using public speaking skills in a professional manner as a motivational speaker. I enjoy speaking to youth groups and ministries and women's groups and ministries; but am equally comfortable leading and conducting leadership trainings in ministry as well as in the corporate arena.
