Mattis Manzel’s Summary

I developed an entirely decentralized and community-based system of mutual information about wikis to be exchanged among wikis. Every wiki dynamically defines its wiki-neighbors and puts out a wiki-net-feed about this information. The wiki-net server computes the wiki-net-feeds and informs communities about being watched. The wiki-net is software independent and applies to all wikis.
I also started several wiki-hives.
