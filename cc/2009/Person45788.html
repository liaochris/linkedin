Jocelyn Eikenburg’s Summary

As writers who know China, we can offer a variety of services that run from copywriting to China consultation to writing about China. Our experience in China and facility with language means you’ll always get a fresh perspective and just the message you need.

Make sure every word counts with our writing services. We do online and offline projects, including ad copy, articles, e-mail copy, marketing collateral, and websites. Unlike most writers who studied Humanities, Jocelyn has a science degree, providing the research skills and logical thinking to drive great projects. While we do a variety of marketing materials, we love helping with websites, and have a flair for creating user-friendly content.

We provide high-quality Chinese translation services -- Jocelyn is fluent in Mandarin Chinese, and her husband is a Chinese national. We’re available to consult one-on-one about Chinese culture, or train your staff in cultural etiquette and behavior in China. Journalists can also call on us to be sources for news stories on China.
