Joseph Deignan’s Summary

Joseph Deignan drives OptiEnergy, the only large-scale integrated Solar PV Developer and EPC player in Bulgaria, Czech republic, Macedonia and Slovakia: emerging renewable power economies blessed by an essential combination of essential sunshine and bankable incentives for Solar PV.

OptiEnergy thrives by being the absolute best in the region in each of in 3 distinct theaters: (1) Technical/EPC, (2) Financials and (3) Compliance/Regulatory and orchestrating these to deliver world-class solutions to demanding global utilities with world-class project management.

That this 'Trinity' can synchronize so efficiently to execute all aspects of large-scale Solar PV projects in Central and South East Europe is a tribute to flawless workflow and world-class organization.
That leading global Utility players choose OptiEnergy for rollout of their Emerging Market PV portfolio is further approval.

Joseph has unrivalled contacts in all theaters of the energy domain: Government/Institutional, Financial, Commercial, EU, US, etc. His intimate familiarity with all aspects (legal, financial, structural, and technological, etc) of large scale project development from conception through delivery is unmatched in this emerging sector.

Joseph is a Solar Energy Consultant to Guidepoint Global Capital, Reuters, Standard & Poor, a member of the Society of Industry Leaders, a member of the McKinsey Quarterly Executive Panel, an educator with the Gerson Lehrman Group as well as a frequent conference chair.

Prior to his solar epiphany, Joseph founded Opticentre, the world’s largest dedicated provider of Pre-Press /DTP and Technical Publishing/Documentation solutions to demanding global product shippers such as Toyota, DELL, Kodak and many more demanding global players. Based in Plovdiv, Bulgaria with a sales offices in Boston, this business employs 45 documentation engineers today.
