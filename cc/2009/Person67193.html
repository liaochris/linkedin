Mike Carsten’s Summary

Mike Carsten is a graphic designer from New York City. He graduated from Pratt Institute in Brooklyn, and is also an Adobe Certified Expert in Illustrator. Mike is a participating member of the AIGA, Type Directors Club and Art Directors Club. He provides a broad range of services from book and poster design to exhibition graphics and wayfinding signage systems. Mike has also taken a special interest in focusing on branding and environmental graphic design.

Mike Carsten’s Specialties:

Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, Filemaker Pro, AutoCAD.
Corporate Identity, wayfinding signage, ADA signage, book design, package design, information design.
