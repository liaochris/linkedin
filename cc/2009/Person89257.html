Paul Martin Schwartz’s Summary

Paul Martin Schwartz is a seasoned business developer and Internet technologist in the IT and Telecommunications industries. During the last ten years Paul has gained in-depth knowledge in planning, deployment and operation of fixed and wireless broadband access services. He is a specialist in Fiber-to-the-Home (FTTH) and held a management position in the Silicon Valley based FTTH project, HomeFiber Inc. His track record includes infrastructure design, business planning, product development as well delivery of services and content.
From his various positions, Paul has management experience from running product development and network roll-out projects. He has a solid foundation in the disciplines of planning a project, maintaining a budget and delivering according to plan.
His first-hand experience from the Service Provider space and his knowledge of value creating content services has enabled him to work as a consultant, assisting leading Danish utility companies to build Open Access FTTH networks. His professional strengths lie in his focus on the customer. Paul is a self-starter and enjoys working in a dynamic and entrepreneurial environment.
