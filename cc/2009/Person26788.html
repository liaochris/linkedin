Dino Eliadis’s Summary

A senior-level business professional with over 25 years of leadership experience and MBA credentials, Dino Eliadis has dedicated his career to creating and leading high performance teams and organizations. His experience spans many different industries including:

• Manufacturing
• Technology
• Business Services
• Construction
• Non-Profits
• Aerospace

He has held positions both in corporate and consulting engagements as:

• General Manager
• Business Development Director
• Director of Operation
• Director of Sales & Marketing
• IT Director
• Project Executive

Today Dino focuses on assisting small to medium businesses in improving their revenue engine’s performance. Through DE, Inc. he has developed proprietary tools to rapidly assess a company’s marketing, sales, and operations to provide useful, high-impact information in an easy-to-implement format, resulting in increased productivity and improved revenue growth that leads to maximized profits.

Dino Eliadis’s Specialties:
