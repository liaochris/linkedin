Elizabeth Grace Filby’s Summary

I have evidence of a key flaw in DoH "facts" and statistics about Cervarix HPV vaccine side effects - understated by 1-2 decimal places and omitting common ones e.g. arthralgia - joint pains. This serious omission was not communicated to PCTs or GPs or the public when I pointed it out Oct 08.

Key messages: good news about superbugs; dangers of toxins in orthodox vaccines.

* co-authoring a scientific review paper on phage therapy;
* scoop: in 1961 SPL phage was flown specially from New York to London to treat Elizabeth Taylor's staphylococcal pneumonia.
* historical research scoop: Reigate's top secret military bunker was Battle HQ in WW2: Winston Churchill; Montgomery; Eisenhower; Canadian troops. Illustrated talk on 15 November 2009 hosted by Churchill Association Surrey & West Sussex.
*Churchill Report: The Health Value of Bacteriophages - 20 pages FOC.
*Educational video for RAFT scientific research charity on skin cancer/melanoma.

Professional experience includes collaboration on Masters degrees and psychological research presented at BPS conference; Director of magazine and book publishing companies; Ed. Publishing Consultant; Consultant for Hampshire and Isle of Wight Strategic Health Authority; BBC educational radio; interviews on radio and national magazines; editor of 'Discovering Reigate Priory - the place and the people' by historian A.J.Ward; revisions of Judicial Studies Board's "Equal Treatment Bench Book"; public speaking to govt departments e.g. Home Office, Inland Revenue, Health and Safety teams, PCTs, NERC; House of Lords meeting on Dignity at Work bill; creator of Personal Risk Assessment form; Mentoring ppt.; research for ESF-funded project on Barriers to Employment; 'A Stress Management Blueprint for the Future in Education' - 2000; 'MRSA - Healing Scents and Common Sense' - 2005. Solo Designs For Wellbeing exhibition 2003; artwork in UK galleries inc. for Sense Scotland - Helen Keller International.
