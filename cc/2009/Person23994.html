David Dilling’s Summary

Director for the European, Middle Eastern and African (EMEA) territories of a leading American software company in the design, publishing and printing sectors- otherwise know as Graphic Communications. Dealing with all aspects of running and growing a software business through the channel. Planed, implemented and started Markzware's European office in the late 90's and continue to manage and work at that facility.

Building, maintaining and advancing effect sales channels for both boxed as well as ESD or electronic applications (Apps, Plug-ins and XTensions) and services. Technical as well as traditional sales experience in software and electronics.

Looking for distributors, resellers, consultants and trainers who understand quality assurance or preflighting as it is called, as well as file format conversion of proprietary formats (Adobe InDesign, QuarkXPress, Microsoft Publisher, etc.) within a larger creative design, printing and publishing sector. With a particular interest in those embracing the Internet and digital marketing opportunities. Using communities such as Twitter, Linkedin, FaceBook in conjunction with blogging, Google Ad Word campaigns and YouTube video marketing to help grow the traditional software distribution channel.

Personal goal: To do what is right.

David Dilling’s Specialties:

Business management, building sales and VAR channels, channel incentives, customer service, international marketing and branding, social networking, internet marketing, viral marketing, video editing and production. Twitter, YouTube, Vimeo, Facebook, blogging and more. Bridging the traditional box channel with the electronic, digital channel.
