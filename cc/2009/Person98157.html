Wintress Odom’s Summary

The Writers For Hire (TWFH) is a Houston-based writing firm. We are one of the only firms in the nation to offer writing as our sole service offering. Unlike a freelancer, we have the power of a unique team approach and our combined skills encompass almost all writing styles and genres. Unlike a marketing agency, our copywriting service is a primary and not a secondary focus. This unique position: between freelancer and agency makes us the perfect choice for companies or individuals who seek more stability and skills than those of a freelancer, but who still want one-on-one attention and a writing specialty. Visit our portfolio at http://www.thewritersforhire.com/portfolio.html.
