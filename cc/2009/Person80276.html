Mary Pope-Handy’s Summary

I am a full-time, second generation REALTOR® since early 1993 (my Mother, Pat Pope, was a local REALTOR® from 1956 until her death in 1996)

I have an extensive real state education - including eight college level courses in real estate. Designations & certifications include: ABR (Accredited Buyer's Representative), CRS (Certified Residential Specialist), SRES (Seniors Real Estate Specialist), ePro (Internet Marketing Certification), CNHS (Certified New Homes Specialist), and ASP (Accredited Staging Professional).

Each year I attend conferences, workshops and courses to better my professional skills and knowledge.

In Dec 2004 I completed a book, Get The Best Deal When Selling Your Home In Silicon Valley (co-authored with Ken Deshaies).

- Aug 1, 2007: Winner of the Project Blogger Contest, sponsored by Inman News and Active Rain. Together with my mentor, Frances Flynn Thorsen, we excelled throughout a 14 week realty blogging competition and were able to assign the $5000 winnings to the charity of our choice, CARE. Being dedicated to cutting edge resources in real estate and having a half dozen websites, blogging looked like "the next great thing" in realty marketing - so I jumped in with both feet. It was a tremendous success in all respects!

- July 23, 2008: Speaker at Inman's Bloggers Connect in San Francisco. I was on a panel moderated by Jay Thompson, together with Linda Davis, Teresa Boardman, and Laurie Manny. Our topic was "Case Study: How Four Bloggers Are Closing Sales." We discussed what strategies worked to help us generate business and make sales from our blogs.
