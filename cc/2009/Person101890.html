Jeremy Tromans (Utility Warehouse Distributor)’s Summary

Following 10 years in the Royal Navy (Medical branch), I was employed within the UK Pharmaceutical Industry (Johnson & Johnson), starting out in a variety of Medical Sales roles and thereafter within Product Management.

Since 1999, I have been self-employed, initially forming a specialist pharmaceutical recruitment agency, which was quickly evolved to operate as an online recruitment portal (www.allaboutmedicalsales.com). Since this time, my interests have moved towards the fast developing Internet sector, with a particular interest in Internet Marketing.

Further information on Internet Marketing / Net Media Marketing Ltd:
www.NetMediaMarketing.co.uk / 0121 270 6407

Alongside the above, there has also been an active interest in professional network marketing and in particular, the ongoing development of an independent distribution network in partnership with Telecom plus Plc (The Utility Warehouse).

Further information on The Utility Warehouse:
www.BusinessOpportunityPlus.co.uk
