Ton Zijlstra’s Summary

Consultancy, Research, Technology, Speaking

Experience in organisational level KM initiatives, with a focus on the non-technological side. Do have large experience and knowledge of technology though.

Currently focussing on individual-centered KM approaches: self directed learning, the role of the individual in networks and communities, personal knowledge management, and knowlege worker effectivity, dealing with information abundance and information strategies.

I regularly speak on conferences and do workshops about these themes, and enjoy doing so.

Ton Zijlstra’s Specialties:

social software, dealing with information overload, communities of practice, system dynamics, innovation, complexity theory, social networks, learning, self directed learning, ict-tools, process design, quality assurance, knowledge worker effectivity.
