    (Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Banking industry)

    April 1987 — May 1991 (4 years 2 months)

    Born in Palermo on January 15,1943, is an Italian artist (singer, musician, poet and painter). He started his musical career at only 16 by winning an important competion at Mondello (Palermo) named the "Capannina d'oro" as a song sing writer. He graduated with honors from the University of Palermo, Faculty of Economics,and he has worked for over thirty years as a bank officer.Barely aged 44, he achieved in 1987 the position of deputy general manager of an important bank in Sicily. As a marketing expert, he published in 1976 "Il marketing nell'azienda bancaria",traceable at the Biblioteca Nazionale Centrale,Florence.He is a chartered interpreter (English and French) with the Civil and Penal Court of Palermo, Italy. He is also a well known astrologer (Member of AFA - American Federation of Astrologers and Founder of The Italian Center of Astrology for Sicily). He has published poems, tales and aphorisms (http://www.aphorism.it/giovanni_mascellaro/)
