Jeanette Gonzalez’s Summary

Jeanette Gonzalez is an award-winning producer with over fifteen years of experience in the film & television commercial industry. She is also a Certified Personal & Life Coach. Most recently, Jeanette has added Publisher & Editor to her repertoire. She is pleased to announce the first book to be edited by her, and published by her company, JG Productions: "You Know What To Do," written by Arnie M. Gonzalez.

Coach. Filmmaker. Publisher. In each of these arenas, Jeanette offers her creativity, a keen intuition and high level communication skills. While her work may seem diverse, she weaves it all together with a compelling common thread: Jeanette’s fervent belief in the human potential – which makes her a powerful agent for change.
