Steve Mun’s Summary

Silicon Valley Realtor Group with the premier Real Estate Firm in the Bay Area. National Association of Realtors Certified with extensive training and experience in the areas of integrated online marketing (e-PRO), in addition to traditional marketing to better leverage the internet for the benefit of our clients.

Also is Quality Service GOLD Certified (QSC), a certification which provides our business practices follow specific procedures and standards of practice for the benefit of consumers. We take the job of providing excellent customer service to our clients seriously. We are among the elite 1-2% of real estate professionals who are willing to have their customer satisfaction ratings record made publicly available. Most agents are not willing to subscribe to the high standards set by QSC that we have chosen to follow.

Who also as a Certified Distress Property Expert (CDPE) is a Short Sale Specialist who is intimately aware of various methods and procedures to help homeowners avoid foreclosures gained through a combination of thorough training and extensive field experience. We have been highly successful in helping distressed sellers with their foreclosure problems. Talk to us so we can determine if we can help you out of your difficult situation.

Providing Quality Service that you deserve.
