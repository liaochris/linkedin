Peter Carlsen’s Summary

Peter Carlsen is entrepreneur and businessman with over 25 years experience in the Hospitality Sector. He currently serves as CEO for Athentics Ltd. He has a strong focus on next generation IDS distribution, channel management, Web 2.0 user experience and social media in hospitality.

Prior to joining Booking Booster in 2005, Peter worked in a variety of business development positions in corporate and small/mid-sized firms, but always keeping the focus on hospitality.
