Keith Erwood’s Summary

Keith Erwood, Principal of Dynamic Network Technologies, is the go-to expert on the subject of disaster preparedness, business continuity, and enterprise resiliency as readers of his popular blog, Disasterpreparednessblog.com, will attest. Using All-Hazards modeling, Keith’s brand of risk management services offers complete and holistic plans designed not only to address disaster recovery theoretically but also to actively assist clients during an event. His hands-on view toward disaster management arises from over 13 years’ experience as an EMT for the New York City Fire Department. Coupled with his IT background in data recovery and backup, Keith offers unparalleled personalized services that are quickly shaping the standards, best practices and education of business continuity professionals, emergency managers and business executives across the country.

His clients have included hospitals and the healthcare industry, providing continuity audits and planning as well as surge hospital planning, and large retail businesses where Keith set up off-site disaster recovery solutions to maintain critical payment systems. Medium-sized businesses benefit from his business continuity planning, disaster recovery planning and off-site secure data backups. For small businesses, Keith offers low-cost planning options to assist with continuity planning. Many businesses have found added value in Keith’s resiliency planning, able to focus on their core functions and maintain their business vision while mitigating exposure to disasters and protect their brand.

In addition to his blog, Keith regularly speaks on his areas of expertise in risk management and contributes articles to many publications. An active member of the Association of Contingency Planners, Keith currently serves as his Chapter President.. Keith is also currently working on a non-profit foundation to help small and mid-sized businesses after a disaster.
