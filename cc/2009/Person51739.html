Joe Suma’s Summary

Consultant with VMware’s Professional Services Organization. I have 10 years of experience in Information Technology both in the private and public sectors managing and designing server based solutions. I am very involved within the VMware community in South Florida. In April 2006 I started and championed the South Florida VMware Users Group (VMUG) until August of 2008, before joining VMware as a consultant. I enjoy meeting new people and discussing technologies such as those which revolve around server virtualization and how to manage it.
