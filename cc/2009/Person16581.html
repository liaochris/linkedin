Cesar Marcondes’s Summary

* 3 years of experience in research and development of new Internet protocols, especially on the area of new transport protocols (worked directly on the design, implementation and extensive evaluation of TCPW-BBE (PFLDnet06) and TCP Probe (Global Internet 05)).
* 3 additional years background on multimedia and voice over IP protocols (SIP/H.323) while working at Brazilian Research Backbone (RNP2/Brazil) on the deployment of a national-scale “multi-signaling” voice over IP network over a QoS provisioned backbone network. http://www.voip.nce.ufrj.br/index_en.html.
* Extensive programming experience: from user-land applications, simulation software and kernel implementation (Linux, FreeBSD, OpenSolaris). http://www.cs.ucla.edu/~cesar/code/.
* Significant hands-on expertise on a variety of hardware and software platforms.
