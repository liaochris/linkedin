Shirley Marie Bradby’s Summary

"English_For_You" my freelance education management and translation service offers professional and enthusiastic assistance to individuals, schools, companies and organizations that need an experienced Information Technology savvy American English Mother Tongue teacher to help them learn or improve their English language skills.

With the advent of information technology and the Internet my capabilities as an ESL teacher and translator have been further enhanced and empowered. Translating and delivering documents and projects by email has never been easier and my ESL lessons are now based on multimedia didactic materials that offer the learner more and better opportunities to see and hear English being used in various contexts. Acquired English language skills can now be immediately applied and used by the student /client to reach their academic, professional or personal objectives.


I am also presently experimenting with a new system called "Edutainment" which combines "education" with "entertainment" to increase interest in language learning and knowledge retention! Guess what? It works! (smile)
