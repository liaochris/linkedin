Alan Fryer’s Summary

Overview
14 years of experience in the medical device industry with a track record of strong leadership skills combined with a solid technical foundation in process, software and systems engineering.

Skills
Experience doing both line and project management working with co-located, distributed and cross-cultural teams.
Experience working with contractors and managing outsourced development activities.
Strong capacity for considering the “Big Picture” and employing Systems Engineering concepts.
Keen interest in understanding the problem domain and the needs of the user that is critical in building the right product.
Experience working in highly process intensive environment for Class III medical devices.
Strong Objected Oriented and Programming background with knowledge of SW design methodologies, UML, Design Patterns.
Experience in multiple languages: Pascal, C, C++, Java (J++), C#, VB.NET, Perl, Assembly Language (68000, 6502, 68HC11). Also some Ruby, Visual Basic, JavaScript, VBScript, XML, Powershell.
10 + Years of MS Windows experience, also some experience on Unix systems. Win32 programming, DotNet Programming, COM programming using ATL and DotNet. Experienced in multi-threaded environment.
Embedded software experience in highly constrained safety critical real-time
systems.
Career emphasis has been on Software/system design from a technical side but I have some experience doing digital design, digital signal processing, micro-controller board design, hardware/software trouble-shooting, etc. Experience writing VHDL and at least reading verilog.
Management training in Leadership, Team Building, Coaching, Accounting, Operations Research, Project Management, Facilitation and Negotiation.
