Bertil Hatt’s Summary

I am taking three years to understand the success of social services (messaging, telephone, night-clubs, contact-based sites, etc.) based on the structure of the underlying network. I isolated new elements, offered new elements to forecast a diffusion from them and suggested new approach to competition.

I am trying to isolate what are the evolution at stake because of digital technology.

Used to be an Energy consultant, I am still thinking of what to do next: use my results to help launches, go back to consulting, try setting up a company, teach Internet economics; any advise is welcome.
