Tina Santillo’s Summary

earths-journey.com is an online Eco-Art Gallery dedicated to showing artists and crafters who have the talent to "repurpose" and "reuse" items that we throw out daily. These unique pieces of art and functional art are all hand made by talented Eco-Artists all for the purpose of helping to save our environment through Artistic Beauty! Sit back, relax and be INSPIRED BY GREEN!

Launched in September, 2008 - we've already attracted some wonderful artists to the site, and by late 2009, we are confident that our name will be synoymous with the words Eco-Artists
