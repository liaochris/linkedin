Brian Smith’s Summary

I am a marketing consultant and branding champion in Omaha, NE. I work to help businesses and not-for-profit groups figure out how to be effective in their marketing.

Basically, I make sense of all the stuff that drives you crazy about marketing. I create and execute strategic marketing plans. It makes me happy and makes my clients happy, so it can't be all that bad.

My background includes work in start-up groups, financial businesses, retail, wellness, food, not-for-profit and other organizations. Clients have included Omaha Performing Arts, University of Nebraska and Nebraska State Recycling Association.
