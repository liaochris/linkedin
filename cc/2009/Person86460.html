Ross McDonald’s Summary

Corporate finance for clean energy projects and clean technology ventures. Expansion capital, M&A advisory. Extensive international network.

Interested in speaking with developers, entrepreneurs, equity investors and debt providers within solar, wind, biomass/WTE and biofuels.

Ross writes for multiple publications, including Cleantech Investor magazine and the Association of Corporate Growth's Mergers & Acquisitions Journal.
