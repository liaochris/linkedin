Annabelle Reitman’s Summary

A career management consultant/coach with more than 30 years's experience specializing in a) resume development taregeting clients' individualid professional stories, and b) short-term coaching for clients in transitions and changes. She is an established writer and author in the career and talent management arenas, writing for both print & web sites.

Conduct workshops/presentations on career transititons/changes, job search, mentoring, storytelling and resumes, multigeneration workforce/communications among the 4 generations, and using storytelling as a tool for the hiring/on-boarding process. Annabelle is a career subject matter expert consultant for ASTD with her last assignment as outside consultant for the January 2009 Infoline issue, “Tune Up Your Resume.”
