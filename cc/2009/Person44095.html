Jason Munson’s Summary

Jason Munson is an online product executive with 12 years of experience creating web strategy roadmaps and successfully managing tactical implementations for fortune 100 companies both as a business owner (AOL, LLC) and as a strategy consultant (Siteworx).

Jason has launched more than a dozen international and global products and he speaks four languages – fluently.

Jason also has significant experience managing cross-functional teams of business, marketing, and technology experts, as well as, experience overseeing agency relationships and managing sizeable budgets.

Currently, Jason manages Business Development and Account Management for Siteworx; and is the principal strategist for many of Siteworx most prestigious clients.
