Rory Vaden, MBA’s Summary

Rory Vaden is a Life Performance Strategist, author, and speaker, who has been featured on Oprah radio. He relates profound truths coupled with humorous anecdotes empowering professionals to conquer their fears and take immediate action in their businesses and their lives.

Consulting with organizations, companies and individuals on leveraging self-discipline to create extraordinary performance, his personal coaching clients include individuals such as Chad Goldwasser, the former #1 Keller Williams Real Estate Agent Worldwide.

Rory's insights on overcoming procrastination and personal setbacks have been shared in media around the world. As the Co-Founder of a multi-million dollar training company, Success Starts Now!, his pragmatic advice has been field tested by thousands of aspiring professionals from all different industries.

A living example of his own principles, Rory’s self-discipline has led him to attain Magna Cum Laude degrees in Management, Accounting, Leadership and an MBA from the University of Denver. He is also a first degree black belt in Shao-Lin Kung Fu and a former record breaking ultra producer for the world’s oldest direct sales network marketing organization, The Southwestern Company.

In 2007, Rory became The World Champion of Public Speaking first runner-up for Toastmasters International out of 25,000 entries worldwide. He is the author of the book No Laughs to Know Laughs: How to be Funny to Make More Money, and the audio series, The Audience is NOT in their Underwear: How to Craft Truly Compelling Presentations and Deliver them Like a Champ.

His groundbreaking book Take The Stairs – 9 Critical Strategies from Improving your Self-Discipline is coming soon.

Today in addition to being an author, speaker, and entrepreneur, Rory is leading a rapidly growing worldwide social movement called the Take The Stairs World Tour in which he is raising money for charity by climbing the 10 tallest buildings in the world.

Rory Vaden, MBA’s Specialties:

Everyone has dreams. Most people don't hit them. Renowned expert, author, and speaker, Rory Vaden, says that the difference between people who get what they want out of life and those that don't get life the way they want it can be summed up in one word: Discipline. Discipline is the ability to get oneself to do things they know they should do but don't feel like doing.
