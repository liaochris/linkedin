David Anthold’s Summary

Over the past 8 years, I have learned how the Facilities contribute & support the bottom line of the company. In addtion, I have aligned myself with key individuals in the furniture industry to support our business needs.

Moving forward, I plan to move into a more workplace strategy role combining my passion for research, people, culture, and leadership. This role will allow me to study the workplace and evaluate areas of improvement that contribute to the attraction/retention of the workforce while planning for the next generation knowledge worker.
