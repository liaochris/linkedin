Roberto Tavano’s Summary

Roberto Tavano is a seasoned international business professional. He is currently a Partner with Unisys Corporation, where he has been busy over the past few years creating, leading and developing the company Security programmes across Europe and beyond, with specific focus on the inception and development of paradigm-shifting and economically large global offerings. In this capacity, he has helped positioning Unisys as a major player in the global security space of border control and aviation security, conjugating enhanced security with passenger convenience. He is leading the development of innovative value propositions based on biometrics that are being proposed to airports and airlines worldwide, targeting passenger facilitation and enhanced security with a comprehensive, holistic approach.
He joined Unisys from Capgemini, where he held the position of VP Public Sector. In a previous life he was a Partner with Ernst & Young, leading e-Business and Supply Chain in Southern Europe, and an entrepreneur that owned and ran a private consulting business, in Italy and the UK, serving regional and international clients across Europe and the UK.
He’s a regular guest speaker at international conferences, sought for interviews by media, and has published several white papers and articles.

Roberto’s rich and motley professional experience is particularly appreciated in the inception, proposal and development of original, innovative business ideas based on a truly holistic business vision and the ability to leverage innovative, paradigm shifting technologies, particularly when applied to complex cross-industry ecosystems.

He is a Physics graduate from the University of Trieste, Italy.
