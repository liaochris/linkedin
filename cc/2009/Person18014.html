Chris Luger’s Summary

Worked as a Graphc Designer/Prepress technician at a couple different organizations.

I was promoted to prepress manager. I would oversee all of the prepress operations over three facilities. Implemented color management systems, and frontend prepress software workflows, as well as quality control procedures.

Recently, took on the responsibility of Senior Tech Services Specialist for the midwest for a large Software Company based in Denver, CO.

I'm looking to really make a difference. To understand customers needs, as well as understand the technology and how I can provide a real world solution. I feel that I'm enthusiastic and really believe that I can use these together to have both the customer and my business succeed.

  • Senior Technical Services Specialist

    Quark, Inc.

  • Prepress Manager

    Mackay Envelope Company

    (Privately Held; 501-1000 employees; Printing industry)

    January 1998 — September 2005 (7 years 9 months)

    Oversee all prepress operations.  Performed software and hardware support for the prepress department in a mixed Mac and PC network, across multiple locations.  Being proficient in OS X, using QuarkXPress, Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop, and other applications, I design, create, preflight, edit and prep files for both litho and flexo printing.  I check all  customer supplied artwork as well as create artwork for a variety of customers.  I have experience using Artwork Systems' Nexus prepress workflow.  I output digital color proofs including DuPont Cromalin b2 contract proofs and HP Color Lasers.  I confirm line screen, angle, distortion, and emulsion.  We can then keep an entire digital workflow by then generating 1-bit Tiffs that are sent to a Esko-Graphics Spark flexo plate setter.
