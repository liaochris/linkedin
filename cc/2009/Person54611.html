Ken Mc Neill’s Summary

I am a Software Architect and Technology Engineer with a proven track record of creative problem solving applied to complex, distributed systems and applications.

My range of experience encompasses different fields and sectors, from embedded real-time to high-level, web-based e-business.

As a strong communicator and a technology advocate, I have published technical articles on subjects such as software modeling (please see websites, below).

I am fascinated by 3D technologies, having built and demonstrated prototype systems for companies such as IBM. I am the creator of J3DWorkbench--a powerful desktop tool for building 3D scenes in a rich-client environment (please see websites, below).

Linux-savvy, and a fan of Ubuntu, I am an active member of the Open Source Community, contributing code and support to several projects, including Java3D and Art of Illusion.

I am fully bilingual-French, and I like working in dynamic, international environments.
