John D. Darer’s Summary

John D. Darer provides financial consulting services and settlement solutions in conjunction with the settlement and resolution of all types of claims and litigation. Settlement services include comprehensive settlement planning, mediation/negotiation strategies, structured settlement annuity placement and diversification strategy, implementation of alternative periodic payment and litigation recovery management solutions . Regardless of where you stand, by utilizing us as your centralized information resource you can save time and energy.

Related services include life insurance, joint and survivor (second to die) life insurance, disability insurance, long term care and Medigap coverage

Holds insurance licenses in States of AL, AZ, CA, CO, CT, DE, FL, GA, IL, IN, KS, KY, LA, MA, MD, ME, MI, MN, MO, MS, NC, ND, NE, NH, NJ, NY, NV, OH, PA, SC, TX, RI, VA, VT, WA, WI, WV. and the District of Columbia .
