Dava Behrens’s Summary

Real estate transactions are more often than not a result life events; some of which are happy, some less so. Whether buying a first home, experiencing a corporate relocation, moving up or downsizing, buying or selling, my goal is help you get the most out of those events that are joyful or help you navigate the rough seas.

As your full service real estate professional, my efforts are geared toward making your transaction best fit your needs. I will give you the attention, information and advice you need to make informed decisions. Many years in real estate, administration and management have taught me to be organized, detail oriented, a creative thinker and an excellent negotiator.

There is so much more to what I do that driving around in my car, touring houses or sticking a sign in the front yard and hoping for the best…

My mission is to:

• ADVOCATE on your behalf when negotiating terms and conditions of a sale

• PROTECT you by ensuring compliance with current disclosure requirements

• COMMUNICATE with you regularly regarding the progress of your transaction and in the manner you choose

• PERFORM in accordance with The Coldwell Banker Service Guarantee

• Be actively INVOLVED throughout the transaction

• ADDRESS every detail ensuring a successful close
