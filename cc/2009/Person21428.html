Codru Vrabie’s Summary

- representing civil society organizations on the National Integrity Council, a representative body overseeing the activity of the Integrity Agency;
- currently working at the Romanian Institute of Training, in a project on institutional transparency of European funds' disbursement in Romania;
- trainer & consultant on matters related to good administration, (new) public management, anti-corruption, legislation drafting and institution building;
- 10 years working experience in advocacy NGOs in Romania, as well as other advocacy NGOs in South-Eastern Europe;
- interested in comparative legislative systems and processes, public participation in decision-making and policy-making, free access to information, and conflict of interest prevention

Codru Vrabie’s Specialties:

- Program and Project Assessment, Management and Evaluation; Project Writing and Budgeting/Fundraising;
- Strategic Analysis and Planning; Human Resources Analysis and Management;
- Legal Analysis, Assessment, and Legislation Drafting; Regulatory Impact Assessment;
- Public Administration Legal Framework & Management; Public and Government Relations, Advocacy;
- Teaching/Training and Public Speaking Experience; News and Academic Editing/Proofreading

Codru Vrabie’s Experience

  • member, CSO representative

    National Integrity Council

    (Government Administration industry)

    July 2007 — Present (2 years 1 month)

    3-year mandate representing civil society organizations on the National Integrity Council, a representative body overseeing the activity of the National Integrity Agency, an autonomous administrative authority in charge of verifying and sanctioning conflicts of interests & unjustified wealth [details available at http://www.integritate.eu/ ]

  • trainer/consultant

    Romanian Institute of Training (IRT)

    (Government Administration industry)

    May 2005 — Present (4 years 3 months)

    - currently coordinating a project on the institutional transparency of EU funds' disbursement in Romania--based on desk research and on-site monitoring, we intend to identify good or bad practices, and edit a how-to guide to the benefit of the management and implementation authorities in charge of EU funds in Romania;
    - since Aug 2008, returned to IRT as trainer on various topics, from good administration to advocacy;
    - until Sept 2007, managed and provided specific training and technical assistance/evaluation projects for public servants and public administration units on matters related to strategic development and planning, budget and human resource planning, access to information and transparency of decision-making, public procurement and public-private partnerships, streamlining bureaucratic processes, avoiding and preventing conflict of interests

  • board member

    Transparency International--Romanian Chapter (TI-RO)

    (Non-Profit; 1-10 employees; Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    February 2002 — Present (7 years 6 months)

    - contributed in developing integrity policies for public administration, judiciary and business, including legal assessment of access to information, transparency of decision-making processes, public procurement procedures, preparedness of public servants, and general anti-fraud/anti-corruption policies
    - until May 2005, coordinated the Chapter's government and public affairs, managed technical assistance to governmental units on the fine-tuning of the National Anti-corruption Strategy and the ratification of the UN Convention against Corruption, planned the strategic development of the Chapter and its Advocacy & Legal Advice Center
    - currently serving a second term on the Board of Directors--elected July 2005, resigned Sept. 2006; reelected Feb. 2007-2010

  • advisor to the Speaker of the House

    Romanian Parliament/Chamber of Deputies

    (Government Relations industry)

    October 2007 — July 2008 (10 months)

    - prepared a procedure for public consultation processes related to priority items on the upcoming EU agenda

    - helped out with everything that I could, in full accordance with my status as beneficiary of the Romanian Government Scholarship

  • intern
