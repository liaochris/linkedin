Cheryl Smithem’s Summary

Cheryl Van Landingham Smithem’s career encompasses a variety of marketing, public relations and advertising areas, including account management, public relations and marketing for diverse industries. From tourism, hospitality, fine dining and restaurants, homebuilding, continuing care retirement communities, interior design to a wide variety of the arts and arts management.

She has served as Vice President, Sales and Marketing for Summerville Homes, LLC a regional volume homebuilding company in the southeastern United States and is the Connection Maven for a number of clients in Charleston, SC. Her primary expertise is the development of marketing and public relations plans. She has expertise in developing intriguing promotions to drive short term sales as well as expertise in providing research to assist with the evaluation brands, products and position. She has in-depth knowledge of the following markets Charlotte, NC; Greenville, Columbia, Charleston, Myrtle Beach, Aiken, SC; and the Gulf coast of Florida.
