Stephanie Lloyd’s Summary

Stephanie is Strategist-in-Chief, Radiant Veracity, in Atlanta, Georgia, at the intersection of Talent Strategies + Social Media.

She is a National Careers + Workplace Columnist for Examiner, a division of Clarity Media Group owned by one of the largest media investment companies in the world. There she authors a highly-regarded career and job search advice column for 110 major markets nationwide. Additionally, Stephanie is a contributor to RecruitingBloggers.com, HelpIWasLaidOff.com, YouNeedAJob.org, and the TalentNetLive blog.

With more than 15 years of experience in corporate recruiting and executive search, Stephanie works with hiring managers, HR executives, business owners, and recruiting firms on recruitment and retention strategy including how to better utilize social media for talent acquisition and employee communication.

Additionally, she helps individuals by providing guidance on subjects ranging from the job search to personal branding and career advancement strategies.

Stephanie is named in 150+ Experts on Twitter ALL Job Seekers MUST Follow by Career Rocketeer and she has been featured on Gravity Free Radio as well as High Velocity Radio discussing Social Media + The Workplace. She currently serves as President and webmaster for the Women’s Finance Exchange.

Like her grandmother, Stephanie delivers a healthy dose of candor and serves it with a smile.

If you are an individual in need of assistance with your job search or if your company has recruiting or social media training needs you can contact Stephanie by email at stephanie (at) radiantveracity (dot) com and you can find her on Twitter at http://twitter.com/StephanieALloyd.
