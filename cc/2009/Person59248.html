Linda Trignano’s Summary

As a Leadership & Career Coach using DiSC insight work assessments, I focus on giving managers and teams the tools to understand their communication style & needs to foster outstanding working relationships. My consulting & coaching services focus on developing and implementing training solutions for businesses and individuals looking to enhance their career management skills to standout in todays' competitive environment.

Recognized as a results-oriented business partner turning work performance concepts into results that positively affect your bottom line. Effective in collaborating with senior management to drive improved organizational solutions. Committed to consistently delivering quality client service. Expertise includes experience in human resources, business development, coaching and sales.

I coach managers on improved leadership skills, organizational change, team building and conflict resolution for measurable business results. I utilize the DiSC work insight assessments to drive organizational changes as well as work with managers to set up HR processes to ensure compliance with best HR practices.

I am interested in opportunities to work with individuals and teams who are looking to raise their level of performance.

Contact me at trignano@hrperformancesolutions.com for additional information and discussion as to how I can assist you and to discuss speaking opportunities for seminars, lunch & learn workshops, etc.

I have over 16 years experience at AT&T in the areas of sales, Human Resources and business development working as a manager with AT&T's Worldnet ISP service as well as direct sales experience selling to small to mid-size businesses.

I am a frequent speaker at local job seekers' groups and coach in-transition job seekers to "ace the interview" and other critical skills to assist them in quickly landing their next career opportunity.
