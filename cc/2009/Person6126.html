Andrew Wright’s Summary

I am the Vice President of Lehigh Valley Acura Honda Hyundai and Suzuki. We are working everyday to use the power of the internet to sell more cars, satisfy customers and exchange ideas. The internet is changing the way business is conducted. We have embraced that philosophy and continue to expand our presence on the internet while maintaining our commitment to process improvement. However, we still believe in the importance of interacting with our customers in person to deliver the purchasing and service experience they demand. We are creating a network of customers and car enthusiasts who see value in being able to converse directly with their auto retailer to exchange thoughts, suggestions and ideas.
