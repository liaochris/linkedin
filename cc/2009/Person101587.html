Udder Hope’s Summary

This profile is for my cow in the 2007 CowParade. I had been involved with Cowparade the last time they grazed in West Hartford (2003) and I thought it would be so much fun to have a cow but I couldn't justify the expense at the time. I decided to dedicate the cow as a public honor to my Mother and include the cow as part of Breast Cancer Awareness (BCA) month in October, donating a portion of my monthly proceeds to BCA.

I started my business in October of 1995 and I've grown it consistently through a focus on customer service, client relationships, and commitment to the community.

This year, I am going to award a $1000 scholarship to the person who answers this question best: "What does Udder Hope mean to you?"

I can tell you what it means to me:
"I have had some significant challenges in my life over the past 10-15 years. What helped me through them was HOPE.I don't believe you can sit around and hope for something to happen-you definitely have to be your own best advocate and have action. But I feel without hope it is hard to move forward or to take that action."
