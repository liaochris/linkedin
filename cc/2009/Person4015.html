KC Allen’s Summary

I spent nearly two decades in radio before making the move into video. My company was formed in 2001 and since then we have experienced production work in many aspects of film and video. We've produced corporate videos for Fortune 200 companies, state agencies, county resources and local retailers. I've worked on film projects both short and feature length as well as a great deal of content designed for web distribution for clients on state and regional levels. From celebrities to the "everyman", our style of directing puts the on-camera talent at ease. We have developed strategic partnerships that can expand your success with any marketing solution.

KC Allen’s Specialties:

Non-profits and fund-raising projects, commercials and narratives are our forte. I shoot in many platforms, but prefer the Sony CineAlta line. We use the Sony XDcam PDW350L and the XDCam PMW-EX1 series of tapeless HD cameras. We also use top of the line sound equipment as well. We edit on the industry preferred Final Cut platform and are experts in encoding top quality HD video for the web. Our scripting is exceptional for productions as short as a TV commercial or as long as a feature film.
