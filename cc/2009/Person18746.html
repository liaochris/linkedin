Christopher Robin Nash’s Summary

Christopher Robin Nash has over 13 years experience as a Sales Professional and Recruiting Specialist within the Technology, Renewable Energy and Marketing services industries. Chris earned his Bachelor’s Degree in Advertising from Oklahoma State University. Prior to joining GreenFoot Technologies, He was a Staffing Program Manager for ITRON, the leading technology provider to the global energy and water industries. Before ITRON, Chris was a Recruiting Manager at OSI Consulting and at the same time was elected Vice President of Avision inc., he has held positions with 2 Fortune five-hundred companies; Dell Computer Corporation and Comcast.

Experientially, Chris lends a vast knowledge of the Technology Services industry and is highly capable of building technical teams, managing the Recruiting process, and delivering project deliverables within tight deadlines.

Chris has also managed Technical Support and Sales Teams. Chris created the largest Dell VAR in the Four Corners Region that did one million dollars in revenue his first year.

Chris was an Advertising account executive for Comcast where he Maintained and established accounts in the Four Corners Region. He created and presented advertising campaigns to fit the lifestyles and demographics that these local and national accounts wanted to reach.

In his spare time, Chris enjoys volunteering his time and experiences to helping others. He also enjoys music, the outdoors, reading and spending time with his family.

Contract Negotiation, Research, Recruiting, Sales, and building long mutually beneficial business relationships.
