Timothy Bachman’s Summary

Timothy Bachman, President of Bachman ID, provides the vision and strategic planning for implementing design innovation into "prepared" retail and workplace environments. He helps clients visualize ideas, create process, and inspire change using creative design solutions within organizations. Tim works with industry leaders to design new and exciting integrated retail and workplace environments that provide exceptional experiences for customers and staff.

Tim has 30 years of experience in planning and design for merchandising, brand development, visual communications, custom fixture and interactive multimedia communications. Tim has worked with financial, manufacturing, corporate, specialty retail, communications, and healthcare clients.

Since 1981, he has worked with clients such as Ameritrade, AMF Bowling, Arclight (Cardinal Health), Bell Canada, Borden Foods, Canadian Imperial Bank of Commerce, Citibank of Argentina, Citicorp, Cole National-The Eyeworks, Cord Camera, CoreStates Bank, Fidelity Investments, First National Bank of Chicago, (BankOne), Fuller O’Brien Paint, General Electric, Herman Miller, HSBC Group, KI Inc., Mervyn’s, Mini Mart, Monsanto, Nationwide Insurance, Norwest Bank, O'Bleness Hospital, Ohio Health, Pearle Vision Centers, Sears of Mexico, Steelcase Inc., The Original Cookie Co., Westernbank- Puerto Rico, Volkswagen/Audi- South Africa, Well Pet (PetCo), Wells Fargo, Wendy’s International and Western Canada Lottery Corporation.

Tim's goals are to create integrated environments and communication that inspire users and visitors. His goal is to position design as a strategic activity in his client's culture. Building a culture of design allows innovation to flourish. Innovation is a necessary activity for any major enterprise that wishes to remain profitable.
