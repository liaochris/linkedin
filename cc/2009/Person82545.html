Rebecca Murphey’s Summary

I craft smart, standards-based front-end architectures for clients big and small. I emphasize modularization, CSS and JavaScript abstraction, and minimal HTTP overhead. I specialize in creating templating systems and jQuery-based frameworks for web applications. If you need a developer who can get your site’s front-end architecture under control, you’ve come to the right place.
