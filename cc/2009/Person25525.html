Dee Cornwall’s Summary

Procure all interior construction items for new store and remodel projects for the Bloomingdale's and Macy's Divisions.
Develop, maintain and rebid National Account Programs to acquire the best price, quality, service and on-time delivery for all requirements. Rollout requirements (same items to many stores at one time) need to be coordinated throughout the year as required.
Plan and be proactive for new projects going forward, learn from issues/problems in the past, partner as team with all aspects of the store planning team to ensure our objective will be achievable.
Purchasing must coordinate at the beginning with all parties of the projects and be the last to leave when the material arrives and final inspections are passed.

Negotiation, National Account Programs, Bid Programs and/or Projects, Installation Supervision, Purchasing, Sourcing, Punchlist Process, Specification Verification, and Approving Manufacturing Process.
