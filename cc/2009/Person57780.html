Laura Lynn Burke, EA’s Summary

Laura Burke, EA specializes in tax resolution. She loves working with clients, assisting with tax reduction, and penalty/interest abatement, as well as payment plans. She specializes in tax planning and preparation, timing is everything. "What the wise do in the beginning, fools do in the end." Plan ahead for your business, make sure you are tax compliant while keeping your hard earned dollars in your pocket! With over 18 years in the financial industry Burke brings a wealth of knowledge to you!
In reviewing your tax options she will also check to see if your mortgage rates are as low as they could be. She offers a full professional service consultation.
