Ronald van Rossum’s Summary

Change agent (veranderregisseur). I work using the organisation objectives as a starting point with a team at the realisation of these objectives. I apply the necessary situational and contextually interventions and techniques to reach the aimed results. In co-makership and co-creation. In cooperation with people. I try to motivate, enthuse, and coach the people around me in order to help them into a win-win situation reaching both personal and organisational objectives.

Greatest successes I reached in environments of business / organisation development or renewal. My creativity and enthusiasm, coupled with my structured manner of working and thinking, I use to lead an organisation to successful organisation renewal. After this renewal I transfer responsibilities to managers in order to professionalise further.

I am driven by integrity, honesty and openness and bases upon those core values I approach people and situations with a large amount energy and enthusiasm. I am sensitive and communicate easily at all levels within organisations. I use my personality and my natural curiosity to deepen myself in people and processes. Quickly resulting in understanding the culture and situation the organisation is in. I am analytically and conceptually strong and have a drive to results. Last but not least I work seriously where humour and pleasure plays an important role to create a good work climate, without losing sight on the results to be reached.
