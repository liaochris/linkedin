Marc Steinlin’s Summary

Marc Steinlin has a Master degree from the University of Zurich in Sociology and International Relations. In addition he has completed a post graduate course in development studies of the Swiss Federal Institute for Technology (ETH Zurich). He has been working with Helvetas, Swiss Association for International Cooperation, for
more than eight years in their head office in Zurich as well as in Mali. During these years, he assumed responsibilities as Knowledge Manager of Helvetas, designing and implementing an organisation-wide knowledge Management initiative. Furthermore he has specialised in human rights issues, project management, change management and facilitation. In 2006, Marc Steinlin has set up his own consultancy enterprise.
