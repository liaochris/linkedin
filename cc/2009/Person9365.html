Barbara Penn Atkins’s Summary

• President of the Penna Group, LLC. Author of "70 is the New 40" book, Speaker and Trained Retirement Coach and Certified Retirement Facilitator. Specializing in pre-retirement readiness, re-careering and post retirement coaching. Offers individual and group coaching services to adults who desire to take a proactive approach to find answers to 'what's next' after retirement.
• Over 20 years as an entrepreneur/business owner of a multi-million dollar data processing firm with over 185 full-time employees; and co-owner of an executive search company
• Published in Money Magazine, Black Enterprise, The Washington Post, Detroit News and Detroit Free Press. Recipient of the prestigious ATHENA award, SBA and numerous other awards for her business acumen and leadership.

• Attended Michigan State University, Wayne State University and University of Phoenix.
