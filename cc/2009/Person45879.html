Jennifer Meyer’s Summary

Jennifer is the CEO of www.webemarketing.com and has over nine years of Internet Marketing and database marketing expertise. Her strategic, analytical, leadership and interpersonal skills make her a valuable asset to any project. She has a Finance degree from the Carlson School of Management and an MBA in ecommerce from Jones International University.

Jennifer has an expertise in Strategic Internet Marketing consulting with a diverse background in startup companies, agencies and corporate environments. She has experience in search engine optimization, paid search advertising, email marketing, integrated marketing strategy development, business analysis methodology, branding integration, process development, vendor selection and implementation.

Throughout her career, she developed excellent project management skills and has been responsible for simultaneously managing the design, development and implementation of online marketing campaigns across 20+ national and international brands. She has a proven ability to collaboratively partner with internal and external cross-functional teams to accomplish strategic goals.

Jennifer has a very analytical background with experience in web analytics, database marketing, loyalty marketing and market research. She has been involved with projects such as customer lifetime value, customer profiling, segmentation, grow share, acquisition, retention, and RFM analysis. She has a comprehensive technical background and experience with several Internet Marketing solutions and database marketing software.
