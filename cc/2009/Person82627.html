Michael Richter’s Summary

After 6 years of professional sales with Canon USA and Novartis Pharmaceuticals, it was time to make a transition. I needed to create a situation that allowed me to utilize my marketing and advertising education and marry it with my sales and customers service experience and make it my own. I have been extremely fortunate because very few people can create that combination and truly love what they do.

I have built a strong referral network that has allowed me to continue to grow my business. My goals when working with each client are not simply to help buy or sell a home, but to become an intricate part of their personal network. I become a part of their investment strategy and work as a partnership to help build their personal wealth with strong real estate investments. It's these partnerships that keep me going strong in both weak and strong real estate markets because there is always opportunity in any market.
