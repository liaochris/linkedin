Craig Opperman’s Summary

I am a partner in the Intellectual Property Practice of a large multinational law firm and bring almost two decades of U.S., European and Asian legal and corporate executive experience to bear on designing and building conception-to-commercialization intellectual property portfolios and strategies for clients. Education wise, I am a Stanford Business School SEP alumnus, hold Law and Engineering degrees, and am a graduate of the South African Naval Academy. I am also admitted to practice in California and South Africa as well as before the U.S. and South African Patent Offices.
