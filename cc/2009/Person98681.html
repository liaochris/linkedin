Tiffany Ng’s Summary

Dynamic and team player. Tiffany is someone who enjoys working under suitable amount of pressure and is quickly adaptable in a fast-paced environment. Comfortable in communicating with all levels and able to build a strong rapport with people due to her curious nature. She is a self-starter with a proactive and flexible approach. She is also excellent in organizational skills and has strong attention to detail.

Equipped with technical background from school and a strong interest in technology enables her to quickly adapt to the different recruitment needs. She is also able to relate well with technical professionals due to the common interest and background.

I am responsible for sourcing and qualifying candidates. The cycle includes sourcing (direct/referral/coldcall), candidate screening (onsite/phone), job pitching, reference check, salary proposal and working closely with hiring managers. My forte is Technical Recruitment and especially within Computer Game & Hi-Tech industries.
