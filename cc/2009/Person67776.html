Michael Trust, MPA, SPHR-CA’s Summary

Human Resources: Highly accomplished human resources professional with varied industry experience in both small (under $25 million) and multi-billion dollar organizations (Fortune 250-500 type generally), working as an employee or as a contractor. Experience has been in both union and non-union organizations. Areas of expertise include recruitment/staffing at all levels from CxO down; employee relations, policy & procedure, benefits, compensation, payroll, and HRIS.

I have a proven track record of saving organizations money in the Human Resources function, while increasing efficiency, significantly upgrading service, and increasing staff professionalism.

Master's degree in management from a top five school. Senior Professional in Human Resources (SPHR) and Senior Professional in Human Resources - California (SPHR-CA) certified.

Coaching: I have helped many people reevaluate their career and professional goals, and then put plans in place to achieve those goals, helping them to exceed their own expectations. I have an extensive background in Human Resources, as well as a background in Real Estate, and having successfully worked for others and successfully run my own businesses, I bring a different perspective to the conversation. I am an action-oriented coach who derives great satisfaction from seeing clients achieve at their full potential. As a coach, I abide by the International Coach Federation's (ICF) Code of Ethics.

I also have assisted many people with resume development and interview preparation that has resulted in a high percentage of interviews.

Michael Trust, MPA, SPHR-CA’s Specialties:

Coaching: career and professional. Helping you achieve your goals and exceed your own expectations by following your life passions. Expert resume development and interview preparation.
