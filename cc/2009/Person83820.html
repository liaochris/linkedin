Richard Guy’s Summary

I have been selling and practicing the “Science of Better” for more than 15 years. The Science of Better is another name for operations research (O.R.), which is the discipline of applying advanced analytical methods to help make better decisions. O.R. uses techniques such as mathematical modeling and the art of algorithm development combined with intelligent data processing capabilities of computer science to analyze complex situations. The advanced optimization technology results give executives the power to make more effective decisions and build more productive systems.
