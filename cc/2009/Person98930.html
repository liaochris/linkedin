Tim Malone, MCSE’s Summary

Veteran IT Manager experienced in Aviation, Manufacturing, Publishing, Distribution and Retail Sales. Microsoft Certified Network Engineer (MCSE). It doesn't matter what the industry is - the concept is the same - I am passionate about providing excellent technical support and network administration. I have been employed most of my career as the only computer dude in small to medium businesses with less than 200 employees.

What I do:
Provide internal company IT support services while directing work of external network engineers. Maintain all servers, network equipment and computers, responsible for LAN & WAN uptime. Administer department budget, purchase and install all computers, phone and FAX equipment. Support business systems, install software, develop and maintain company website and intranet.
