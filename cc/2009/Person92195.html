Simone Poutnik’s Summary

Born and grown up in Germany, studied Communication Systems for International Relations in Italy and now nomading between Brussels, London, Greece, Germany...

Apart from being co-founder of the Hub Brussels, I do process design and facilitation work for social innovation in English, German and Italian.

My inquiry is all about how to innovate naturally. How can we design spaces, initiatives, communities etc. by building on what is authentic, meaningful and sustainable, and letting go of what is not serving?

I am working with the U-process http://www.presencing.com/ and I am a practitioner of the art of hosting meaningful conversations and initiatives http://www.artofhosting.org/.

I have been working for CommunityIntelligence http://community-intelligence.com, the European Commission and the European Social Fund on supporting Communities of Practice, e.g. CoP on Partnership in the ESF http://partnership.esflive.eu/, for Siemens and WWF.

I am also a guardian of Axladitsa Avatakia http://www.axladitsa.org, a home place, an olive farm and a place of collective inquiry, learning and retreat - a place for Living Wholeness in Greece.

In the past I worked as a membership and project coordinator for EABIS http://www.eabis.org, as a facilitator of experiential workshops at the eco village PantaRei http://www.pantarei-cea.it/ and in the restaurant sector.

I love story telling, creative writing, dance and other forms of body work, meditation, cooking, being in nature and learning from natural systems.

My learning goals for this year are to get a basic understanding of biomimicry, improve my french and start speaking a little bit of dutch. I'd also like to do a course in graphic facilitation.

Simone Poutnik’s Specialties:

participatory process design and facilitation (in English, German and Italian), incubating and cultivating communities of practice, project and event management, online facilitation, stakeholder mapping, community building, harvesting (creative documentation and pattern seeking)
