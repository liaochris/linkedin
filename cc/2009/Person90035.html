Sean Harry’s Summary

As a career coach, I show people how to discover their talents and effectively market themselves. Most of my clients find positions in half the average time and are able to negotiate for a higher salary. But more importantly, they find engaging work, increased job satisfaction, and environments that fit their personalities.

Companies that I work with get happier, more productive employees and experience significant reductions in turnover. I welcome introductions to individuals who have had successful careers but are considering a career transition.

QUALIFICATIONS:
Over 15 years of successful responsibility and expertise in:
• Training and Development
• Organizing people and processes
• Recruiting and developing high impact teams
• Planning and implementing strategic objectives.
• Recruiting and Hiring

If you are trying to figure out "what you want to be when you grow up", OR if you need soft skills training for your key leaders, give me a call and let's see how I can help!

Recent speaking engagements include: I Take the Lead (Keynote Speaker for Quarterly Event); Association of Bridal Consultants (Keynote Speaker); Project Management Institute, Portland Chapter (Workshop Leader), ASTD National Leadership Conference in Washington DC (Workshop Co-Leader), Beaverton/Hillsboro Chamber Expo (Speaker).
