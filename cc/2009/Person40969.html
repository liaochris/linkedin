John Sileo - Identity Theft Speaker’s Summary

Identity Theft Speaker, John Sileo, had his identity stolen out of his corporation and used to commit a series of crimes, including $300,000 worth of digital embezzlement. While the data thief operated behind the safety of John’s identity, John and his business were held legally and financially responsible for the felonies committed. Ultimately, identity theft destroyed John’s corporation, but John chose to fight back.

Emerging from this crisis, John became America’s leading identity theft speaker and identity theft expert. His experiences from victim to victor led him to write the critically acclaimed, award-winning book, Stolen Lives: Identity Theft Prevention Made Simple, which was recently chosen as the #1 Business Book at the EVVY Awards.

Weaving his identity theft speeches together with cutting-edge research, disarming humor and hilarious audience interaction, John inspires every one of us to emotionally grasp and intellectually comprehend the profitability of privacy.

His satisfied repeat clients include the Department of Defense, FDIC, Blue Cross Blue Shield, Pfizer, the Federal Reserve Bank, Lincoln Financial Group, AARP, and hundreds of others. Identity Theft Speaker John Sileo is regularly featured on NBC, ABC, Fox and Money Matters Today.

John is not only an identity theft speaker, but President of The Sileo Group. In addition to providing identity theft speeches, identity theft seminars and identity theft workshops, the Sileo Group is an idea-lab focused on guiding organizations to proactively protect their profitable data.

Prior to becoming an identity theft speaker, John graduated from Harvard University with honors and started four successful businesses.
