Sam Azer’s Summary

Sam started working in the computing world as a teenager in the late 70's. For over 25 years Sam has provided services to small and large corporations, leading Enterprise Development teams as well as R&D teams, producing both hardware and software solutions. He has experience in network architecture, server security, disaster planning and recovery, database design and optimization, C/C++ software development (systems and applications) for Linux, Windows and the Web.
