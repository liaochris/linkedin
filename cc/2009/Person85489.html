Roberto "Roby" Perissin’s Summary

Swiss and Italian citizenships
Languages : Italian, English, French, Spanish

Roby started his professional career in the Electronic Data Processing (EDP) field in 1980 in Milan with a known EDP consulting company, stationed at Beckman Instruments Italy in Milan, then hired by Beckman Instruments he was moved to France for 2 years then Spain (6 months) and then to the US (Orange County, Ca) for 7 years (with Sensormedics Corp., a spin-off of Beckman) where he covered the roles of International Marketing Coordinator, then Sales manager for Latin America and the Far East, followed by the position of Marketing Manager for Sensormedics' Pulmonary Function and Sleep Diagnostics product lines.

Upon his return to Italy Roby has been managing his family' company, Sensormedics Italia (exclusive distributor in Italy for Sensormedics, Jaeger, Micromedical, Tanita, Bodymedia, CNSystems, etc), which has achieved the leading market position in Italy in the Pulmonary Function, Cardiopulmonary Exercise testing and Nutritional Diagnostic markets as well as "best distributor" worldwide for several suppliers for many years in a row.

Roby, using his long standing reputation, knowledge and experience in the international medical markets, also runs since year 2000 his Medical Marketing/advertising firm "Proby", along with friend and art director Edo Passarella.
The agency caters to several International companies assisting in launching new products, sales rep training, new product development, advertising and marketing campaigns, etc.
Proby can analyze a medical company' ' organization and fix, improve or optimize the sales and marketing structure, support, comunication, strategies.

He has and still does lecture internationally in 4 languages on pulmonary function, cardiopulmonary exercise testing, respiratory mechanics, sleep, metabolic measurements, etc as well as sales techniques, motivation strategies, marketing, advertising, etc.
