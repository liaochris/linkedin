Heather Carine’s Summary

Heather is a Freelance Business Researcher with her own business, Carine Research, based in Adelaide, South Australia. She also serves as the Information Services Manager at Hunt & Hunt, a national law firm.

Heather established Carine Research to work with businesses that need well-researched information for strategic decisions or advice.

Her research is relied on for business development proposals and tenders, client development initiatives and legal advice. She also enjoys writing and presenting on business research skills and knowledge management.

Over the last ten years Heather has worked as a knowledge and marketing specialist at national law firm, Hunt & Hunt and researcher at Goldman Sachs in London.

Heather holds a Masters Degree in Business (Information Technology) from RMIT University.

Business research, online research, business development, knowledge management
