Susan Guarneri’s Summary

"Empowering best-fit job and career transitions with smart insights, courage, and heartfelt support"

1. More than 23 years' experience in the careers industry, providing outplacement, career consulting, job search coaching, resume writing, corporate training and management development to individuals and groups, including Fortune 500 companies.

2. National Certified Career Counselor, Certified Online Identity Management Strategist, Certified Personal Branding Strategist, Certified Career Management Coach, and Master Resume Writer LIfetime Achievement Award (Career Directors International).

3. Co-author of Job Search Bloopers: Every Mistake You Can Make on the Road to Career Suicide and How to Avoid Them (CareerPress, August 2008).

4. Using proven career assessment tools (both right-brain and left-brain oriented) and career counseling/coaching to:
- develop clear and compelling career choices and pathways that "fit" the individual and enable career satisfaction,
- unearth personal branding differentiators and develop compelling online identity and self-marketing communications, and
- facilitate managerial and leadership development of soft skills, such as coaching, employee motivation, team building and improved communications.

5. Seeking individuals committed to career growth and organizations committed to improved performance and employee motivation.
