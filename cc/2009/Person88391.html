Andy Ellis’s Summary

My goal is to live to see an economicaly,politicaly, and socially just world. This of course is no simple task and requires work on a lot of fronts, but first and foremost it requires the creation of systems that allow communities to lead their own efforts to grow, heal, and revitalize themselves. A benchmark along the way to the larger goal is the establishment of social enterprises, which can make enough money to inact their social acts without having to succumb to the will of granters...thats what I am working on now.

My professional experience is a 3 part combination of argument and debate coaching,communication technology, and non-profit program management.
