Ryan Marshall’s Summary

My career started at Calvary Chapel MultiMedia, but it didn't really take off until I was brought on board to assist the design and development of BOOMj.com's social network while the company was still in the very early stages of start-up. I consider myself incredibly lucky to have caught such a break, but it has been a very richly rewarding experience.

At Beyond Commerce, after getting the first version of BOOMj.com online, I assisted and oversaw multiple revisions and improvements to the website while it grew to 1 million unique visitors per month. With BOOMj.com stable and generating repeat visits, Beyond Commerce set me on to assisting with the design & development of their e-commerce website, Shop.BOOMj.com. This site was also a great success for Beyond Commerce and ultimately led to the inception of their brand-new i-SUPPLY division.

The rest, as they say, is history. I currently consult and freelance for a variety of clients all over Southern California and across the USA, tackling projects as small as a photographer's website, to some as big as a full-featured social network for supermodels.
