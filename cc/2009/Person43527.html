Jan Malloch’s Summary

I am an inspirational author and speaker, with special emphasis on producing digital training materials and courses in positive thinking and achievement for the global market.

I trained as a Master Practitioner of NLP and Clinical Hypnotherapist, which has given me loads of techniques and tools to help people achieve their full potential.

My book "The Positive Achiever - 11 Essentials to Achieve the Success You Deserve" is available now Amazon.com. Please see my reading list below!!

I moved with my mother and sister from New York State to Scotland when I was 10 years old. Getting used to pre-decimal currency and accents that were often difficult to understand, my sister and I certainly had our work cut out!

I went onto university but was out after a year - I didn't know I was expected to study as well as have a good time!

After 10 years in the Civil Service, I started a family. I was a stay-at-home mum for 7 years and ran a part-time dressmaking business to keep me sane.

I returned to office work in 1995 to recruit computer assemblers for IBM. By then, PCs had replaced pens and paper. I didn't have a clue about how to switch on a PC, never mind how to use one!

I started a Maths degree through the OU which meant buying a second-hand PC. Within a year, I had upgraded that PC, and built another two. I was determined to get a job in IT.

At the time, I was 42, a female wanting to get into a traditionally male-dominated career, and I had no work experience in this field at all. Because the desire was so strong, I was not going to allow any of these obstacles to get in my way. Within 4 months, I was taken on as a trainee data networks engineer.

I have now left the world of IT to help others to achieve what they want in life.

If you truly desire to make positive changes in your life, then it will happen.
