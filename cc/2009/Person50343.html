Jonathan Pape, MBA’s Summary

Jonathan currently works as an Digital Marketing Search Specialist for Meijer, a large privately owned retailer in the midwest with over 187 stores. Jon oversees the Search Engine Marketing (SEM) and Search Engine Optimization (SEO) for Meijer.com, a large department store web site.

Meijer is listed by Forbes as 29th most Reputable Company in US - http://tinyurl.com/lfxpcb

Jon recently completed his MBA in marketing and international business from Western Michigan University (WMU).

Jon has experience managing search engine marketing (SEM), email, affiliate, search engine optimization (SEO), contextual advertising, behavioral marketing, peripheral sites, and online video.

Feel free to contact Jonathan through http://jon-pape.com.

Advertising Credentials
• Jon has developed and maintained multiple search engine accounts (using Google's My Client Center (MCC)) and created product advertising strategy using multiple search engines including Google AdWords, Yahoo Overature, MSN, Enhance, and LookSmart.
• Managed an advertising budget of 1 million dollars a month.
• Hired to restructure an existing AdWord campaign, Jon reduced the companies spending by 20% and increased their sales 5%, which equaled over $40,000 a month in total savings.
• Jon was responsible for building a dynamic AdWord system to create, update and organize URL’s based on direct product sales, location, and activity.
• Maintained a Return on Advertising Spending (ROAS) of nearly 300%.
• Jon has achieved the Google Professional Adword Certification and Yahoo Ambassador Certification.
• Jon has completed the Perry Marshall Sales consultation courses.
