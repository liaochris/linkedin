Bill Thomas’s Summary

I am an accomplished business development, marketing and IT consulting professional with deep domain knowledge of the Internet, enterprise content management [ECM] and the enterprise IT marketplace. I am a strategic thinking corporate-entrepreneur who is adept at quickly translating product features into revenue generating value propositions while also defining the business centric responsibilities required to deliver the product/service. My domain expertise in marketing, BD and IT afford me the ability to lead organizations and projects cross-functionally.

My real claim to fame is the ideation that put the plastic cap at the end of a shoelace; touted in many Hollywood movies, now known as the fruglebinder. ;-)

As a consultant and key employee I have helped companies define their critical business challenges; extensive experience in both Fortune50 and SMB, my experience at both the senior management and director level have allowed for ownership of programs representing SBU's in both the front of the store [marketing & sales] as well as the back of the store [IT].

I am a results oriented self-starter who takes pleasure in team building and the creation of top performing brands; through practical observation of client motivations I continuously offer meaningful, profitable problem solving solutions. I work well in complex, changing environments; I enjoy understanding and resolving business and cross cultural issues. I understand the importance of creating relationships that result in an environment of trust and professional friendship - these values allow everyone to succeed.
