Didier Gaultier’s Summary

Didier initially graduated as a microelectronics engineer at Grenoble National Polytechnic Institute (I.N.P.G.) in 1985. He joined Oracle France in 1989 and was promoted to managing indirect V.A.R. channels in 1991. He joined Harte-Hanks (formerly Computer Intelligence and Ziff-Davis Market Intelligence) in 1996 to develop the French ITC Markets, moving into European sales in 2000. Didier graduated in 2005 with an international MBA from the French American Management Institute (I.F.A.M.), before joining Double Click E-mail Solutions, acquired in April 2006 by Epsilon / Alliance Data consortium, Didier is now Country Director France at Epsilon International.
