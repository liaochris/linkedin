Dana Lookadoo’s Summary

Dana Lookadoo is a Search Marketing Optimizer specializing in Conversational SEO, the art and science of ranking in search engines, engaging customers to build relationships, and helping website visitors find what they need. Conversational SEO maximizes the inter-relationship of Search Engine Optimization and Social Media Marketing.

*******
Dana Lookadoo founded Pixel Position in 2003 and is rebranding Pixel Position to offer hosting-related services only. A new venture to help and train those who are doing SEO on their own is under development.

Dana's passions include:

* Search Engine Optimization & Copywriting
* Social Media Marketing & Engagement
* Landing Page Optimization & Usability
* Information Architecture & Strategic Planning
* Web Development and Graphic Design
* Technical Training & Curriculum Development

******
Dana began a career in computing in 1984 and in the Internet in 1996. Internet marketing provided the medium to merge interests in people, writing & technology.

Education:
B.S. (magna cum laude) in Telecommunications, Multimedia and Applied Computing, minor in Instructional Technology

A.A. in Business Administration

Studied Virtual Reality Modeling Language at the Naval Postgraduate School - focus on visual application for instructional design.
