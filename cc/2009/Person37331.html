Valerie A. Nelson’s Summary

Grant Consulting Services specializes in assisting nonprofit organizations to meet their goals and objectives through comprehensive grant development services. These services include:

• Full project development
• Comprehensive funding searches
• Professional grant proposal writing services

Project Development:

We can assist your organization in planning, implementing and evaluating your current and new projects. This process can help you clarify goals, objectives and activities through a comprehensive plan that is tailor made for your unique organization and populations served.

Comprehensive funding searches:

Did you ever wonder if there are other avenues of funding out there for your projects? There probably are. We have access to over 71,000 grant making agencies in the United States, including the Federal Government. We receive weekly updates to new and continuing funding opportunities. Let us find the perfect funding match for you!

Professional grant proposal writing services:

Fundraising is one of the most significant tasks done in order to ensure the continuation of your program or research. Yet many organizations lack the resources, experience or skills to complete the application process. You no longer have to submit the same proposal to foundations or agencies that have different requirements. We will write a proposal that is tailor made for your program. Let our experience work for you. We have a great success rate and understanding of what the grant-makers are looking for in a proposal. We can save you time and potential headaches while helping you to become fully funded.

Valerie A. Nelson’s Specialties:

Grant development for nonprofit organizations. Creating processes and procedures for fundraising, writing business plans, annual reports, sales letters, marketing materials and articles on varied topics.
