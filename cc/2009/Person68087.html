Michelle Tripp’s Summary

With the benefit of strategic and tactical marketing plan development, a brand repositioning specialty, and years of concepting creative campaigns, Michelle merges strategy and creative with powerful outcomes. One of Michelle’s hallmarks is identifying core issues and synthesizing business challenges into a series of actionable and innovative solutions. Her business insight is an asset to clients looking for a clear plan of action and impactful execution.

Michelle’s marketing experience includes consulting clients in industries such as banking and finance, hospitals and healthcare, higher education, wireless, pharma, energy, technology, sports and events, government agencies, media, luxury brands, and consumer services. Michelle has leveraged her marketing experience into a brand positioning specialty. In 1999, Michelle began developing the foundation for the architecture of a proprietary core value branding methodology that has now been executed in more than a dozen industries.

Beginning her career as a dual creative Writer and Art Director, Michelle has been responsible for the concept and creation of hundreds of print projects and more than 30 award-winning TV commercials including the role of Director on nearly a dozen spots. For her combined print and broadcast work, Michelle has received numerous creative and marketing awards and Best of Shows, including 16 Tellys, an IECF Astrolabium (Japan), The Gold CASE Award, New York Globals, and ADDYs.

As a social media strategist, Michelle is an adept analyst, avid new media researcher, creative technologist, and early adopter. Her experience includes developing site structure, content, and social media strategies for marketing integration, including projects for national brands.
