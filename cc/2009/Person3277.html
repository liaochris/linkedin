Alexander Bermudez’s Summary

Alexander Bermudez’s ten years of experience involved Commercial Leasing, strategic Portfolio Management and the brokering of Investment Properties. Over the last 5 years, Alexander Bermudez has focused his efforts on identifying and purchasing Investment Properties, for his private clients.

Alexander Bermudez founded Classic Apartments in 2000. He personally oversaw the acquisition of various real estate investments, maximized returns through good management and aggressive redevelopment, fulfilling Classic Apartments’ guiding principles, of providing quality affordable rental housing, in the Los Angeles area.

Alexander Bermudez joined The House of Realty in the spring of 2004, where he specialized in the analysis and acquisition of Commercial Real Estate Investments. Further providing a more complete service to The House of Realty and its clients.

More recently Alexander Bermudez has leveraged the knowledge and experience that Classic Apartments has gained over the years as a Commercial Landlord, to provide property management services to other property owners. The strategic partnerships Classic Apartments has developed with local architects, designers and contractors, have proven to be invaluable to the long-term growth of its clients’ Real Estate assets.

As an active member of the community, Alexander Bermudez founded the Invisible Minority Project. The goal is to humanize the men, women and children who are living on the streets through photographic documentation, shining hope and humanity through their eyes, captured through the lens of a camera. An ongoing slide show can be viewed at InvisibleMinority.org.
