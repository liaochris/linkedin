Paul Cripps’s Summary

With over 10 years experience in the media industries, I recently left my position heading up a small team at volume to join a small start up known as Nine Four http://www.ninefour.co.uk.

My work involves all aspects form budgeting and costing jobs, project management, scheduling, design and lead development of projects.

One of our greatest strengths at nine four is a true understanding of design along with a good grounding in databases and CMS. Some agencies deem this cross over as bad and it’s frowned upon. But our clients would dis-agree. You only need to take a glimpse at our folio to see we truly 'get' user interface design.
