Jason Crist’s Summary

I am a Flash Programmer who has been working with Interactive Application Development for over a decade. I began with motion graphics and animation for television but the left side of my brain took over and was drawn to programming. I've been hacking Flash since version 4 and don't expect to stop any time soon.

I'm interested in all kinds of Interactive Application Development, I've dabbled in Silverlight, AJAX, WPF, Java and a number of server-side things but my focus continues to be the Flash Platform. I believe that it's important to specialize to be the best you can be but to ignore other areas completely is to refuse to grow.

I have been in a number of leadership roles and large groups but I find that I usually work best either solo or with small groups of self-motivated people with very clear roles.

I enjoy what I do and I believe that I'm pretty good at it. But it is also important to me to carefully balance work, self improvement, family and fun. To many of any makes for a lop-sided life.
