Andrew Thorn, PhD’s Summary

Andrew Thorn - I am very interested in getting to know the best you!
andrew@andrewthorn.com - 760-559-3548 Direct

"At some point competency becomes a non-issue. It is expected of a leader and even the most ordinary leaders usually possess it in abundance.

At issue then is a person’s behavior, and how effectively people around them respond to their leadership efforts. It is the area of growth each of us must conquer on a continual basis. No one is exempt!" Andrew Thorn

Behavioral Executive Coach with substantial experience in Fortune 500 companies. Focus on c-level executives and their teams. Personal coach to two of the top six executive coaches as ranked by Forbes Magazine. Strong focus on identifying self-deception and self-deceptive behavior and creating an awareness of your personal impact on the organization and others.

Expertise in work/life balance initiatives and planning for the second half of life. Managing the transition from ambition to meaning. Focus on becoming vs achieving. Designing and integrated work/life plans focused on personal and professional aspirations. Managing work/life conflict. Focus on living in the present moment.

Organization effectiveness. Creating dynamic and sustainable culture growth. Building an inclusive work force. Managing uses and abuses of organizational power. Creating a culture of accountability. Pioneer of the peer coaching methodologies for organizations. Development of Leader Behaviors. Understanding Individual value. Creating a learning organization. Creating learning community behaviors.

Team evolution; the next level of team building. Established teams don't need to be built, they need to evolve and develop next level competencies and behaviors. Team coaching. Feedforward
