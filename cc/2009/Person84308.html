Bob Payne’s Summary

30+ years of university teaching experience, developing, delivering and evaluating undergraduate and graduate courses in recreation parks and tourism.

Developing and executing educational field trips to Africa, Central America and Europe. International travel to Africa, Southeast Asia, Australia and New Zealand, Caribbean and Europe.

Consultant to Parks Canada, Makivik Corporation, Moose River.James Bay Coalition and Forests for Tomorrow.
