Jon Reese’s Summary

I am an accomplished Dallas-based print and interactive media designer. I have successfully managed creative teams and produced award-winning creative for advertising and communication agencies that include RBMM (The Richards Group), DDB (DDB-Needham) and TM (Temerlin-McClain).

I have designed for high-profile clients that include American Airlines, Pepsi Co., The U.S. Air Force, Nokia, Hershey’s Chocolate, Hampton Inn and Suites, Motel 6, Best Western, Texas Instruments, Chili’s (Brinker International), Intel and Epson, just to name a few.

www.jonreese.com

Jon Reese’s Specialties:

Concept, design and production of Brand Identity (including logos, corporate style, and business collateral), E-Commerce and Flash-intensive Websites, Online Marketing and Advertising (including Search Engine Optimization and rich banner advertising), Unique ULTRA-HIGH-RESPONSE Direct Mail techniques, and Kiosk, DVD and CD promotions and applications.
