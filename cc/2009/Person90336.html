Sergi Mora, CPCC coach@sergimora.com’s Summary

Since 2003 Sergi has enabled many entrepreneurs perceive their businesses from new empowering perspectives while connecting them to the right sources of money. As a member of the International Coaching Federation, Sergi specializes in the young company that's struggling with the transition from startup to smooth, well-run operation. You at that stage will need a new level of thinking, courage & accountability to take your company to the Next Level.

Sergi's experience in the Business Angels and Venture Capital sector provides him with clear insight on how to find winning business proposals. According to his experience, the 3 most important factors that any venture needs to achieve superior success are:

1) A competent, committed and credible team aligned to the same Great Vision.
2) Have an Investor as a partner that provides them with sound strategic advice.
3) Have an experienced coach who pushes them out of their comfort zone while keeping them accountable and focused on the right track.

He is building an international community of people who are committed to their personal growth and who want to give back to their communities. More precisely, the community is formed by mentor baby boomers and accreditted coaches that are willing to empower entrepreneurs for sustainable growth.

Sergi Mora, CPCC coach@sergimora.com’s Specialties:

1. Coaching Entrepreneurs and pushing them to their Next level of Performance and Leadership.
2. Training Business Angels on how to coach their talented entrepreneurs and get Superior Results.
3. Training 50+ year old executives that want to give back to their communities through coaching and mentoring Entrepreneurs
