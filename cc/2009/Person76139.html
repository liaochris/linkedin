Oliver Southgate’s Summary

Oliver is an experienced and well respected innovator in the online world, working with technologies and concepts that range from Enterprise Content Management and Service Oriented Architecture through to Enterprise 2.0, Web 2.0 and Social Media.

Having come from a development background, Oliver has learned all things web related through having worked with some of the largest companies in the world. Oliver has over ten years experience within these companies covering numerous projects involving design, development, consultation and integration of information based solutions.

Oliver is a highly motivated and enthusiastic individual who enjoys learning and understanding online methods and problems in order to become an expert. He also possess strong analytical and facilitation skills, this combined with his motivation and enthusiasm for the web makes him a great asset and has given him successful track record in providing imaginative solutions to meet both project and business objectives.
