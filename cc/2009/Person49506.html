John Holland’s Summary

Business focussed IT Relationship Manager and Change Manager, skilled in bridging the gap between technology and business to lead teams through organisational and personal changes to successful conclusions. I manage the demand side of IT, working at the interface of business and technology to deliver value through technology operatons and change programmes.

As a Relationship Manager and Change Manager, I focus on the people and teams impacted by organisational change. With coaching, clear communications and direction, transformations can be made in people's lives and business. This supports clients, colleagues and partners to build the success they desire.

I have delivered programmes of change including process and technology transformations. Some examples include the deployment of a CRM system and associated process changes within a regulated environment, introduction of a document management systems as part of a paperless office initiative and the relocation of people and systems during a multi-site office move.

I have a particular interest in helping people overcome challenges in regulated environments. Analysing and understanding the risks combined with coaching and creativity can lead to true innovation in managing within the regulatory controls.
