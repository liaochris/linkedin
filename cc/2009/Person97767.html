Steve Holt’s Summary

An award-winning, convergence-minded journalist and marketing copywriter with experience writing for direct mail, the Web, and daily newspapers. Journalistic expertise includes subjects like sports and fitness, religious trends, current events, travel, interesting people, and more, for Web and print media.

Reporting, writing, direct mail, Web copywriting, copy editing, page layout and design, research, interviewing, Web publishing, and new media.
