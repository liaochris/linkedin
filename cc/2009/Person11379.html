Bill Loguidice’s Summary

Bill Loguidice is a professional writer, multimedia professional and businessman skilled in Technical and Creative Writing, Technology, Creative Development, Procurement, Project Management, Operations, Supervision, Analysis, and Process Engineering.

After obtaining a Bachelor's degree in Communications from Rider University, Mr. Loguidice has been a long-time business, technology, staffing and creative professional. He has contributed to various business, entertainment, and medical periodicals, including U.S. 1, Video Game Collector, and Surgical Rounds, writing and developing ideas on a broad range of topics. A videogame and personal computer collector since before it was trendy, Bill is the co-founder and Managing Director for the online publication, Armchair Arcade, one of PC Magazine's Top 100 Websites for 2005. Bill is also the co-founder of Myth Core, a creative development company.

As a top videogame and computer historian and collector, Bill personally owns and maintains more than 300 different systems from the 1970s to the present day, including a large volume of associated software, accessories, and literature. It is from these great resources and his sincere passion for the topic that Bill is often called upon to provide subject matter expertise to both public and private media interests, including the Las Vegas Review-Journal and the Orlando Sentinel. He is also co-author on a book from Focal Press/Elsevier, "Vintage Games: An Insider Look at the History of Grand Theft Auto, Super Mario and the Most Influential Games of All Time", and other upcoming books and projects, including a book focusing on interviews with videogame professionals and a feature film documentary on videogames.
