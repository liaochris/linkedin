Tristam Sparks’s Summary

Tristam Sparks designs and produces websites, on and offline motion graphics, short films, identity systems and information experience visualisations for both print and interactive media.

His undergraduate degree in Computer Graphic Design from the Wanganui School of Design in New Zealand enabled a diversified and expansive, award winning portfolio that intersects many professional experiences and projects.

This experience culminated in obtaining a Masters in Arts from Interaction Design Institute Ivrea, Italy where his thesis project explored ideas regarding participatory and communal communications tools related to democratic systems in governance.

At present, Tristam is based in London, England and following up on personal projects and explorations whilst exploring contract consultancy and freelance opportunities.
