Bethany Bovard’s Summary

For the past 10 years, I've been fortunate enough to work with hundreds of educators in K12 and higher education settings. During that time, I've explored, researched, and practiced teaching with technology in f2f and online settings with high school students, k12 teachers, graduate students, and faculty. I have had meaningful and engaging encounters with many dedicated professionals and students - encounters that have profoundly shaped my understanding of the impact of technology in educational settings.
