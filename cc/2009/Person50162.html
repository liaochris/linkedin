Jon Brouchoud’s Summary

Designer and virtual world evangelist, particularly interested in architecture and the built environment, augmented design collaboration, and virtual building design.

In addition to providing virtual design services, my goal is to bring realtime, multi-user, virtual worlds to mainstream AEC-industry adoption by providing resources, consultation, collaboration tools, training, community organization, and virtual project spaces.

Jon Brouchoud’s Specialties:

Architectural design, virtual world design, writing, community management.

    Studio Wikitecture's central aim is to apply an open-source paradigm to the design and production of architecture and urban planning. In much the same way Wikipedia enables a loose, self-organizing network of contributors to collaborate on content creation, SW has been experimenting with ways to bring together a diverse and geographically disperse community of individuals to create an architecturally noteworthy design that, in the end, is more than the sum of its parts.
