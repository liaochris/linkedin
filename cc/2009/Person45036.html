JD (Jean-Dominique) Vandenberghe’s Summary

I always love to work on projects that mix technical and artistic issues. That’s what DVD creation (and now Blu-ray) is about. I soon discovered that broadcast motion design, print design, web design offer the same challenges.
My goal, since May 2007, is to implement the Escape Lab recipe in Los Angeles with Escape Lab, Inc.
