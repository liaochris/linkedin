Sander D. Morijn’s Summary

Sander D. Morijn is an experienced manager with many years experience in managing HR and recruitment teams in an international and competitive setting. He has also many years hands-on executive search, consultancy and research experience, a broad international network and was responsible for countless successful executive assignments and projects with well-known multinationals and niche companies all over the world.

In February 2009, Sander D. Morijn joined Penske Logistics as HR Manager Talent & Development.

For more information about Penske Logistics please check: www.penskelogistics.com.
