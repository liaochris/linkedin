Caressa Flannery’s Summary

I have more than 15 years experience in advertising and marketing and over 10 in web development/sales. I have worked for ad agencies and small multimedia development shops as well as high end developers with high profile clientele. I have unique experience of being on both sides of web development, both as sales/business development and web construction/site architecture. I have a solid reputation in assisting businesses with their marketing efforts including Internet marketing.

More recently passions have surfaced in health and wellness and anti-aging. I work with health professionals to implement nutraMetrix into their practices. nutraMetrix is a comprehensive wellness system that offers isotonic nutraceuticals, low glycemic weight management through the Transitions Lifestyle System and DNA screening analysis. I work with them to achieve their financial goals while assisting their patients with a complementary approach to health. I enjoy being a weight management coach through Transitions and watching individuals implement lifestyle changes that help them shed pounds of body fat!!!

Additionally, I truly believe in the power of multiple streams of income. I have been blessed to find a residual income system that has caused my own income to soar while helping to assist others in achieving their dreams/goals.

I enjoy sharing my experiences with field leaders who want to further their careers so becoming a Certified Web Trainer and sharing is a passion.

I am a Past President of the Greater Frederick Advertising Federation (GFAF), past ADDY Award chairperson for many, many years and a Past President of the Frederick Jaycees (junior chamber) where I was honored with many accolades.

Personally, I am a busy single mom to a wonderful teenager and enjoy time with friends and family, traveling and real estate investing.
