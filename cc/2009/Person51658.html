Jonathan ("Jono") Shuster’s Summary

In a nutshell, I'm passionate about helping people who have innovative and creative ways of solving complex problems -- especially entrepreneurs who want to realize their potential by starting or growing their companies.

As an entrepreneur, executive, and manager, I've gained a solid understanding of the full range of factors that must come together to build and grow a successful business. My background is in technology commercialization, both in industry and academia, primarily fixing business processes then applying technologies to automate those processes.

As an entrepreneur, it's often hard to tell what you're doing right and what you're doing wrong. That's why we started the Entrepreneurial Standards Forum, to help entrepreneurs learn where to focus their energies based on the hard experience of companies that have been down the same path and succeeded.

The Entrepreneurial Standards Forum's Benchmark Survey is a key technology behind the Angel Capital Summit (AngelCapitalSummit.org), held in Denver each fall. If you're an entrepreneur seeking investment capital, or an early stage investor seeking quality deal flow, you'll want to attend this.
