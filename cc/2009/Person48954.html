    CEO for all programs & services of the Chamber and serves on the Board of Directors. The South Metro Denver Chamber is a full - service (20 staff) regional Chamber of Commerce representing business predominately in 4 counties (Arapahoe, Denver, Douglas & Jefferson County) and in 16 cities (Aurora, Castle Pines, Castle Rock, Centennial, Cherry Hills, Columbine Valley, Denver, Englewood, Glendale, Greenwood Village, Highlands Ranch, Lakewood, Littleton, Lone Tree, Parker, & Sheridan) in South Metro Denver , Major Chamber divisions : Small Business Development Center, Economic Development Group, Events & Visitors Bureau, and Public Policy. The Chamber consists of 1600 businesses with over 100,000 employees with 70 programs, services & events each year. There is a reason our website is www.bestchamber.com - Our members and their leadership to build a vibrant business community, a healthy economy providing for a wonderful quality of life for the communities we serve...join us!

  • President

    Arapahoe County Water & Wastewater Authority

    (Government Agency; 11-50 employees; Utilities industry)

    January 1999 — January 2003 (4 years 1 month)

    Arapahoe County Water and Wastewater Authority (ACWWA) is a political subdivision formed in 1988 by an agreement between Arapahoe County, and the Arapahoe Water and Sanitation District (AWSD) for the purpose of developing water resources, systems and facilities, wastewater collection and treatment facilities for the ACWWA service area. The Authority serves an area of more than eight square miles in the southeastern Denver metropolitan area, approximately 10 miles from downtown Denver. Visit www.arapahoewater.org for more information.
