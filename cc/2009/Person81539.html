Rahel Anne Bailie’s Summary

Development of content strategies for organizations that want to manage their content as an asset with corporate value.Core areas are content strategies for business impact:
* component content management - increasing accuracy and efficiency of technical information - multi-channel publishing, re-use of technical documentation across publication types and to other departments such as customer support
* content convergence - looking at combining and distributing multiples content types to align with business goals
* performance improvement - increasing effectiveness of online transactions, creating customer conversations and authentic relationships

Rahel Anne Bailie’s Specialties:

Content strategies, content management consulting (business process re-engineering, content modelling, XMetaL training, content structuring, tools and delivery recommendations), user experience consulting (usability assessments, testing, information architecture), and content development (documentation, Web content, help and embedded assistance)
