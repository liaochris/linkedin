Channing Rollo’s Summary

Channing Rollo is a strategic marketing, planning and research professional. She currently serves as Lead Business Specialist, Marketing & Communication, for Florida Power & Light Company (FPL), the rate-regulated electric utility subsidiary of FPL Group, Inc., and one of the largest investor-owned electric utilities in the nation. Prior to joining FPL, she served as Business Identification Director and Head of Marketing, North America, for international management consulting firm Proudfoot Consulting. In October 2008, she was recognized by Consulting Magazine with a Women Leaders in Consulting Award – the Future Leader Award.

Prior to joining Proudfoot as Marketing Research Manager in 2006, she served as Chief Operating Officer of The Maverick Group, a Nashville-based strategic marketing and advertising agency supporting regional and national clients.

Rollo joined The Maverick Group in June 2004 following four years of service at ClientLogic Corporation, an international business process outsourcing provider (now named Sitel following a merger). There she served as the Manager of Business Intelligence and Manager of Strategic Marketing.

Rollo's compositions have appeared in numerous technology and service trade publications, including Merger Mogul, Internet World, ZDNet, MSN Tech & Gadgets, DM News, Customer Service Management Magazine, Direct Marketing Magazine, Call Center E-Journal, FierceEnterprise, SearchCRM and Warehousing Management Magazine. As a corporate spokesperson for ClientLogic, her industry and client commentary appeared in InformationWeek, CRM Magazine, Outsourcing Journal, DM News, Customer Inter@ction Solutions and Call Center Magazine.

Rollo began her work at ClientLogic as an e-business analyst following her service at Tropicana Products, Inc. and two Washington, D.C. think tanks.
