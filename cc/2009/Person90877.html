Shai Gutner’s Summary

TaKaDu provides a software solution which reduces water leakage levels by detecting leaks in water networks, using an analytical method. The solution takes advantage of the continuous meter readings available in water distribution systems, and is based on mathematical models and advanced algorithms. Our solution does not require any intervention in the network operation or any equipment installation.
