Alex Iglecia (connect@iglecia.com)’s Summary

Alex has eight years of experience in body-mind coaching, training, teaching and innovating focusing on the intersection of conscious movement, transformative practices, yoga, inquiry and effective action.

Alex has developed visual designs focusing on effective presentation and clear communication and has led workshops utilizing innovative and traditional body-mind practices to facilitate the emergence of personal and collective solutions.

Goals:
One one hand...to support and effecting radical and nourishing change in individuals through embodied experience, training, and inquiry.

On the other hand...to present and facilitate at leading conferences and client engagements, focusing on processes in which solutions to wide-ranging problems emerge naturally, efficiently and contain a call to action.
