Dan Hushon’s Summary

Dan leads the techical architecture, cross-EMC alignment, and execution of EMC's grid and utility computing strategy. Joining EMC in July of 2006, Dan has been engaged at integrating information centric grid activities into a commerical platform the Information Grid, and realizing an information grid focussed on Business Information processing and specifically Customer Data Integration. This platform, is designed to offer a pre-integrated solution, designed for high-scale, high-throughput, information processing; leveraging SOA techniques, information analytics and PetaByte scale information management capabilities. Dan's specific interests include massive scale distributed computing/operating environments, distributed fault tolerant environments, and Web 2.0 / social collaborative computing.

Prior to joining EMC, Dan spent 2 years as the CT for Sun's Sun Grid program, a pay as you go, multi-tenant isolated network of computing resources linked by a network operating system, and a year as Chief Technologist for the US Sales Organization. While at Sun, Dan had been involved in a variety of Enterprise Technology Insertion projects in the telecommunications, defense, financial and manufacturing vertical markets. These projects had leveraged distributed service based architectures to integrate legacy systems,while creating a loosely coupled environment to facilitate service re-use and provide architecture resiliency to constantly changing demand/load as well as service composition. Dan's consulting experience ranged a broad set of Sun technologies that include the J2EE, J2ME, Jini Technology, Jxta, JavaCard, Edge, N1 and Blade services and deploying these technologies to a variety of enterprise infrastructure targets. The specific target markets have included wireless application and Internet Service Providers (WASP/WISP), Virtual Network Service Providers (VNSP), as well as broadband, digital television and traditional enterprise customers.

Dan Hushon’s Specialties:

Dan has been involved in both internal product development and customer facing field orgs. Dan's technical background lies in massively distributed service oriented architectures - either traditional n-tier with CORBA/J2EE/Web services or through peer oriented grid environments with SGE, Jini and Jxta technologies. Dan has been very involved in Grid stds orgs, and was named a fellow in Media Grid. Dan has also managed a $15M P&L and grown several practices in emerging areas to critical mass.
