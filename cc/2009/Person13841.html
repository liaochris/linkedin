Bridgette Dipboye’s Summary

I have been working with RadSciences Group for the past 5 years (since Oct 2004). I began working in marketing, research and business development; then transitioned into working in human resources and recruitment/ staffing. In January of 2008, I received my PHR (Professional in Human Resources) Certification.

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

RadSciences Group specializes in providing organizational development programs and recruitment services to hospitals throughout the country. Unlike the traditional “headhunter”, we personally meet all of our clients so we understand who we are dealing with, and what we are selling. We believe that if you are going to pay us to work for you, we should be more than just a voice over the phone and commit to the due diligence that is necessary to build a foundation of trust.

We have several contracts we can offer our clients and can tailor a recruitment partnership that compliments your existing team of HR professionals. Whether it is a full-service approach for hard to fill positions, or simple assessments that identify a candidates behaviors, we can make it work.
