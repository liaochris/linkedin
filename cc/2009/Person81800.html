Karthik Raman’s Summary

My primary research interests are in the area of Systems Biology, particularly pathway modelling and in the analysis of complex networks. I have experience in constraint-based modelling of biochemical networks, comparative phylogenetic analysis of genomes and comparative metabolic network analysis.

I am particularly interested in the application of complex network science to various disciplines, in and outside biology.

I look forward to a career of research in an industry or academic setting.
