Jasper Blake’s Summary

I am honoured to have been asked to manage the social media for a great company with a great product - the UOWHO bracelet.

If someone asked you to think about doing a single kind thing, most of us would try, some of us would forget and some would just not get to it. The UOWHO™ bracelet is a simple, tangible reminder. If someone did a kind thing for us and gave us the UOWHO™ bracelet and asked us to pass it on, we would be more likely to follow through.

The bracelets can be 'branded' with your name, logo or website address - so for non-profits looking to raise funds or spread awareness of their cause - or companies looking gain good PR and awareness, what better way than by encouraging random acts of kindness.

Blogger for Social Smart
Social-smart is a web-based Dashboard collaboration tool which integrates search, & email marketing and mobile technologies with multi-channel social networking, for use either in-house or under license by an agency or consultant. Social-smart also provides a social media campaign strategy checklist and consultancy services. Blogging for Social-smart keeps me in touch with all the latest social media thinking and practice.
