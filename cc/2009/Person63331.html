Mark Hennick’s Summary

Current, detailed and enthusiastic about graphic design, Mark Hennick lives and breathes corporate identity. Throughout the last few decades, Mark has teamed with various clients to develop effective visual business tools. Using a strong sense of typography and color theory, Mark interprets your directions and gives you design choices that will “wow” you. His goal is to give you the presence you want and to project the message you need.
