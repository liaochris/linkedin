Richard Barwick’s Summary

I co-founded Creativitea to try and do something different, my business partner and I wanted to help clients combine great design with functionality on both the digital and traditional platforms without loosing site of our creative work and vision.

TEA is now a melting pot of artistic talents from a wide variety of backgrounds including graf, illustration, fashion, graphic design and digital. These are individual talents, united by the simple idea of forming a design agency producing work it can take pride in.

Our agency is now based in Guildford, Surrey and are working on an eclectic range of products and clients.
