Among the several customers: CAD.Lab SpA (BO) and Think 3 (USA), Regione Emilia Romagna (BO), Parco del Taro (PR), Bormioli Vetri (PR), Istituto Case Popolari (SO), Actify (USA), Guareschi (PR), Bocchi BRD Design (PR), Studi Delfi (PR), and many others.

    CAD.Lab

    (Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Computer Software industry)

    May 1989 — September 1991 (2 years 5 months)

  • Researcher (grant)

    CNR

    (Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; Research industry)

    March 1988 — March 1989 (1 year 1 month)

    I developed one of the first 2D and 3D parametric CAD systems based on a data model called HDAG (Hierarchical Direct Acyclic Graph). The HDAG and the CAD systems based on it have been described in several scientific publications.

  • Researcher (grant)
