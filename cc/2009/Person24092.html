David Fideler’s Summary

I help companies communicate the real value they offer and, as a strategic copywriter, craft marketing messages with clarity and focus.

By paying special attention to the real value you offer and the pressing needs of your target audience, my expertise will help accelerate sales and boost the return on your marketing investment.

My other area of specialization is writing and designing high-value lead generating and lead nurturing tools like white papers, case studies, sales letters, and the like, mainly for B2B companies. (I’m also the founder of the “Content Marketing” and “B2B Lead Nurturing” groups here on LinkedIn.)

Interested in learning more? Visit my websites and feel free to contact me at any time.
