Saren Sakurai’s Summary

With 15 years of experience in the digital space, I have been developing and executing integrated marketing strategies for both small and multinational brands in the US and Asian markets.

I currently lead the Kraft Foods account for AKQA, including the well known CPG brands Philadelphia Cream Cheese, Kraft Singles, Velveeta, DiGiorno, and California Pizza Kitchen

Prior to AKQA I have worked with Nike Asia Pacific; Nike US; Sony Pictures (Home Entertainment, Columbia Pictures, Sony Mobile); Team Red Bull; CBS Mobile, Kyocera Wireless, Jenny Craig, The Venetian Hotel and Casino, and FearNet.com.

Recent projects have included awareness and reach, trial driving, and digital engagement campaigns; strong elements of Social Media; Mobile applications and SMS extentions; experiencial websites; and integrated partnership programs.

Saren Sakurai’s Specialties:

Integrated Marketing and Brand Strategy leadership; Branded Entertainment; iPhone Applications; Branded Social Media; Mobile and Viral Marketing; Digital Content Development; Youth Branding; Client Service, Relationship Management, Account Management; and Business Development.
