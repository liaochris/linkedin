Tyler Rockwell’s Summary

I started my games art career while still in school at the Academy of Art by interning at EA in Redwood Shores back in 2001. Since then I have had the opportunity to add my name to 8 EA games of varying genres and art styles. My latest project, The Godfather 2, was both challenging and rewarding offering me experiences as a Lead Artist overseeing in house artists and several outsourced artists at EA Shanghai.

I have a traditional art background and I'm still trying to find the time outside of crunches to enjoy that part of my creativity.

I'm currently looking to continue my work experience as an environment artist while channeling my career and learning towards more lead and management experiences.

I'm happily married to an absolutely awesome girl and maintain a small menagerie of animals at home.

Tyler Rockwell’s Specialties:

Environment Lead, modeling, texturing, assembly, and working with outsourcing groups.
