Murlidhar Naidu’s Summary

Around six years of experience in IT/ITES and manufacturing industry. Experience in Pre Sales, and Busines Analysis, Business Development, Requirement Consulting, Solution Designing, Project Management, Client Interaction, Client Relationship Management.

Currently working as Business and Pre Sales Analyst at Rishabh Software Ltd. Completely responsible for managing the Business Analysis of the largest Telecom Account at Rishabh Software Ltd. The responsibilities include client relationship management, business analysis, requirement gathering, project management in the area of OSS and BSS for Telecom domain.

Have been very active in Technology unconferences, meets and events. Technology enthusiast. One of the co-founders of Rich Internet Applicaiton and Rich User Interface internet society. Dabbled with consulting small organisations and startups.
