Mark Bradford’s Summary

Ten years leading commercial interactive software strategy, design and development for various industries. Experience includes conception product management, production and support of custom solutions and productized brand management software for major CPGs, pharmaceuticals and financial services. Seasoned by "previous life" as a business attorney and persuasive communication background.

Aspiring entrepreneur. ChirpUp is a modern interactive marketing agency that helps business be heard online. We blend proven marketing methods with current technologies and social media. Our techniques are based on a five part formula for brand building and communication. Our ideas are premised on individuality, authenticity and social media as a democratizing marketing force.
