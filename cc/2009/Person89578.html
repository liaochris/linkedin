Scott Kuehl is Vice President of Marketing and Sales for Nor-Son, Inc., a privately-held, award-winning construction services company.

Before joining Nor-Son, Scott founded BrandResolve, LLC, a brand consulting firm specializing in advising entrepreneurial executives and SMB owners.

Prior to that he was President and Executive Creative Director of the business-to-business marketing agency, Schemer Kuehl, Inc. Over a 10-year period, he founded, built and sold this Top 100 US Agency (BtoB Magazine/Forbes-sponsored) and Twin Cities Top 25 Advertising Agency (The Business Journals).

Scott graduated magna cum laude with a bachelor of fine arts degree from Mankato State University. He is also the recipient of the 2005 Minnesota State University - Mankato, Distinguished Young Alumni Award. (formerly Mankato State University.)
