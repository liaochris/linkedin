Kristi Daeda’s Summary

I work with mid- to senior-level professionals that are looking for a job to create a simple but powerful personal marketing plan in order to get a better job, faster, with less grunt work.

I also work with entrepreneurs to create a personalized, high-impact brand that will open doors to partnerships, press and new clients.

Kristi Daeda’s Specialties:

career coaching & consulting, personal branding, business branding, communications strategy, social media, internal communications, marketing, employee engagement, corporate culture, strategic planning, web technology.

Kristi Daeda’s Experience

  • Success Coach

    Twin Sparks Consulting, Inc.

    (Public Relations and Communications industry)

    January 2009 — Present (1 year )

    I partner with job seekers and entrepreneurs in their pursuit of success in three key areas:
    ~Mission: Clarifying why you do what you do and how your business fulfills your purpose
    ~Message: Creating powerful, relevant and authentic branding
    ~Media: Developing a comprehensive strategy to communicate key messages, incorporating both traditional and new media.

    I am also a frequent public speaker on career, job search, professionalism, communications, and social media topics.

  • Creator/Writer

    Career Adventure

    (Internet industry)

    January 2009 — Present (1 year )

    Career Adventure provides insight, advice and strategies for professionals as they chart a course to inspiring work. The site garners thousands of page views weekly. Career Kick Start, the newsletter associated with the site, is published weekly. Newsletter subscribers receive free access to teleseminars, ebooks and other resources to advance their careers. Career-related articles have also appeared on a range of other career and business blogs. Most recently, I was honored to be named an Adjunct Lecturer at Brand Camp U, a blog and conference devoted to personal branding. Career Adventure can be found at http://www.kristidaeda.com.
