Annmarie Hanlon’s Summary

BACKGROUND
Professionally qualified Marketing Consultant with Evonomie. Author of QUICK WIN MARKETING and Co-Author, with Joanna Akins, Of QUICK WIN DIGITAL MARKETING published by Oak Tree Press.

HOW WE CAN HELP YOUR COMPANY
Over the last 20 years I have worked with many companies
• Develop marketing strategies, create practical action plans
• Evaluate projects, carry out formative and summative evaluations
• Market research into new markets
• Deliver marketing workshops

Clients include business to business, public sector & high profile organisations from the consumer market; international membership organisations, Government depts, European manufacturers.

IF YOU ARE LOOKING FOR MARKETING TRAINING

WE RUN WEBINARS & WORKSHOPS
Check the EVENTS section on our website

GROUP / CONFERENCE ORGANISERS, COLLEGE OR UNIVERSITY
I deliver a series of workshops for small groups (10 – 30) with a hands-on focus, delegates create an action plan to take away, e.g:
• Quick Win Marketing: generate faster results for your business.
• Digital Marketing: social networking (Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn) & blogging.
• Marketing Strategy / Marketing Planning
• Social Networking Strategies

For students, I have delivered one off lectures e.g. marketing research, marketing planning, marketing strategy.

For larger groups I am happy to join conference panels for questions on digital marketing, marketing for SMEs, marketing planning, marketing research, quick win marketing, marketing on a shoestring.

My home and office is in Lichfield, Staffordshire, near Birmingham. We have good road, rail and airport links.
