Paige Hall’s Summary

Before founding AboutFace™ in 1995 (formerly The Secret Shopper Company), Paige Hall started the secret shopping program at Boar's Head Provision Company. She then moved on to become the Director of Training for the Hartsfield-Atlanta Airport's parking operation. There she was able to improve customer service 16% within 6 months.

AboutFace has helped measure and improve the performance of millions of sales and service personnel, increasing revenue at these companies almost 1 billion dollars over the last 13 years. AboutFace has earned 6.2 times more awards than any other company in the industry. Hall has been voted industry leader 5 years running by the NCPMS. These accolades are flattering, but Hall is most proud of the day-to-day efforts of the staff at AboutFace. Recent nominations include: Business woman of hte Year, Rising Star, Excel Awards, Inc. 5,000, etc.

Standards are high, and the company is staunchly committed to fresh, innovative and clever ideas that drive topline revenue.

Hall is a popular public speaker who is often called upon to share her expertise in customer experience innovation and strategy, employee development, frontline performance improvement, training and marketing research (specifically secret shopping and ground intelligence). Her style is evident in her creative 'Customer Experience Vigilante' blog. Take a look: www.customerexperiencevigilante.com.
