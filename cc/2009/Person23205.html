Darren Saul’s Summary

Carnegie Hill is a small team of consultants with over 9 years experience in IT Recruitment - both Contract and Permanent. We pride ourselves on the quality of work and flexibility of solutions we provide.

Our Recruitment Service consists of End to End Recruitment or Partial Recruitment. We specialise in 2 key niche IT areas and hence are able to draw on a very extensive network of quality candidates in our particular areas of speciality - FAST.

Our client portfolio is across many different sectors and we generally work either exclusively or as part of a small number of recruiters. We have enjoyed very successful long term relationships with all our clients.
