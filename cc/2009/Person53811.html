Kathy Jenkins’s Summary

Professional organizer who will help you design an organizing system for any room of your home. Recommends systems and products to remove the clutter your home and home office space. Special interest in working with children to teach them how to organize and help them become better students by developing study habits and time management. Operates a successful online retail store, Organized A to Z.com (http://www.organizedatoz.com) specializing in organizing products used by other professional organizers and featuring stylish solutions for an organized lifestyle. Also provides personal assistant services to help manage and complete your "to-do" list, run errands, plan meals, shop, and pay bills.

Kathy Jenkins’s Specialties:

Household organization,personal assistant services, organizing in the schools; organizing kitchens, bedrooms, bathrooms, kids' rooms, playrooms, family rooms, garages, basements, attics; home office organization with flling & tickler systems, paper management, work flow, & time management; teaching children to organize, determining thinking & learning styles, organization at home & school, study habits & time management
