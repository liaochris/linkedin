Apostolos Koutropoulos’s Summary

Hello, I'm a Mac! ... well, not really, but I am a Mac User.

I love gaining new knowledge, technology, managing, planning, analyzing, and new gadgetry.

I've spent time commenting and writing about new technologies that come out - I can see myself doing this full time (at least for a while)

Even though I enjoy coding - here and there - just for fun, I can't really see myself as a 9-5 developer. I enjoy learning new languages (human or machine), and making my computer do something new, cool or useful.

I enjoy advising fellow students on what classes to take that may coincide with their personal and professional interests. I also enjoy helping people discover new library resources that can help them in their academic and life pursuits.

I would like to continue to learn new things as time goes by. I am passively thinking of pursuing a PhD, programs that hit that 'sweet spot' (in terms of my interests) seem just out of reach.
