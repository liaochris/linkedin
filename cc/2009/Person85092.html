Robert Wickman’s Summary

Robert Wickman is a learning and development professional with a passion for lifelong learning. He draws on his experience as a manager, facilitator, and trusted advisor to educate people on the skills they need to be successful as executive leaders, mid-level managers, and frontline employees. Organizations have benefited with improved communication skills and trust, reduced conflict, and higher employee engagement. In all his work, Robert’s goal is to further both knowledge and understanding so that individuals may be more effective in both their work and personal lives. His mission is that people would know their potential in life and live their dreams.

Known as a clear communicator with big, forward thinking ideas and exceptional problem-solving skills, his sessions are marked by depth of content, practical suggestions to apply learned concepts, and a gentle sense of humor. Robert’s insight into human behavior and his experience as an instructor enable him to help people and organizations break through the barriers that prevent them from achieving performance goals.

Obsessed about leadership effectiveness, organizational change, and creating cultures of collaboration, Robert’s areas of expertise include: employee engagement, managerial productivity, conflict resolution, and human capital strategy, making him a natural fit for organizations looking for programs that deliver usable tools. He has worked with organizations of all sizes.

Robert Wickman’s Specialties:

Employee Engagement, Managerial Productivity, Team/Council Facilitation, Conflict Resolution, and Human Capital Strategy
