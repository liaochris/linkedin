Pete Stevens PSpictures’s Summary

Freelance photographer with nearly 25 years' experience, 19 of which working for local newspaper groups...a very challenging and rewarding experience - sometimes covering up to a dozen assignments a day - all interesting in their own way.

Including (but not limited to): Local and national political campaigns, (M.Thatcher), illustrating the plight of Romanian orphans, advertising features, every sport you could shake a stick at, golden weddings, actors/theatre, musicians, flower shows, train crashes, local schools coverage and regular visits to maternity units to photograph new-borns. The journey continues...

'Variety' really is the spice of life.

After achieving promotions, experiencing numerous newspaper group mergers and relocation I decided to take the plunge and become a full time freelance photographer in November 2005 leaving job security behind me to devote my time to photography with Creative Empathy.

Going freelance was the scariest, yet best thing I ever did!

I now spend quality time on each assignment, listening so as to produce the best results. I help to promote businesses, individuals, charities, illustrate goods and services for corporate clients and SMEs as well as capture those special moments we all appreciate. I love my job.

My clients value the time and effort that I take in discussing their needs well before my camera comes out of its bag. It's my tried and tested formula. If you know what you want to convey - I am listening.

Being able to provide a creative, professional, yet personal service is truly an enjoyable passion of mine whether it is to record, illustrate, amuse or provoke - I'm an experienced eye for what you need - in the best possible way.

I just don't do paparazi (hope I spelt that incorrectly) - I have a life and can sleep peacefully at night !
