Karen Highland’s Summary

After many years as a teacher, including full-time, part-time and tutoring, I got my real estate license and joined my husband in the business. He has been an agent for 16 years, and we are building a Team, The Highland Real Estate Group.

My role on the Team is website management, Blogging, and contact management. I'm an avid blogger, managing several blogs and usually writing every day of the week. I enjoy sharing real estate information and concepts with Frederick buyers and sellers.

I share the office management with Chris and the interaction with buyers and sellers. He is a great negotiator and handles the initial sale or buy, and I handle contract to close issues.

Together, we make one great realtor!
