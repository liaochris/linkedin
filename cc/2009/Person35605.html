Gene Leo’s Summary

I am currently a Lotus Field Technical Sales Specialist in the Pacific Southwest. My subject matter expertise is in Lotus Connections, Lotus Notes/Domino and Lotus Sametime. Prior to joining the Sales organization, I spent 8 years in our Lotus lab services organization. In that capacity, I architected large WW Domino infrastructures as well as architected and executed migration strategies from other messaging system including, but not exclusively Microsoft Exchange to Lotus Domino. I have over 15 years of IT experience.
