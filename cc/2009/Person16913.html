Charee Klimek’s Summary

As Managing Partner and Principal Brand Strategist for Vocii, Charee draws on more than 16 years experience researching and articulating employer brands, implementing internal and external communication strategies and being a trusted resource for her clients. She is a true partner, advisor and change agent; having managed more than $200 million in client budgets throughout her career and extending value well past the numbers. She has lead the partnerships and fostered brands of premier organizations including UnitedHealth Group, sanofi-aventis, T-Mobile, CB Richard Ellis, 3Com, Wyeth Pharmaceuticals, Hospira, Motorola, and Discover Financial Services to name a few. Her career portfolio includes work with businesses that have workforces ranging from 50 employees to 75,000+ global team members.

After a fulfilling career in both the private and corporate sectors, Charee left the agency world to pursue her dream of providing her expertise to medium and small-sized businesses that simply can't afford the big agency overhead. Her vision is to offer a unique branding solution that taps the potential of a company's two most important assets: employees and customers. Her values are grounded in the belief that success is based on respect for people, integrity, teamwork, dedication and a long-term commitment to shared goals and objectives.

When you partner with Charee, you can be certain you are collaborating with someone who is dedicated to the highest level of service, has a solid track record of doing what’s right for her clients, ensuring the highest return on your investment.

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    March 2009 — Present (9 months)

    Empower the voices that shape your brand™.

    Vocii™, from the Latin root vōc meaning “voice, vocalize or bring to call, symbolizes the social expression of the individual and the collective voice of your business community; representing the sum of all people who have had an experience with and influence over your organizational brand.

    An independent brand consultancy, Vocii focuses on the voice of your company culture by interacting with and observing your employees, managers and leadership. We uncover who you truly are and what you aspire to be.

    We listen to the voices of real-life customers to understand the value and authenticity in your promise, your products and your services.

    We work with you to develop a communication road map that will act as your compass for brand as business strategy and the process of aligning what you say with what you do.

    We partner with your leadership to train them on how to think differently about communication and decision making that affect cultural health.

    Together we define the behaviors that are required to make a positive impact on the employee and customer experience to deliver on your brand promise.

    Ultimately, Vocii is here to help you ease the transition from one-way communication to participation in your communities; moving your business forward by empowering the potential that exists in your employees and customers – the voices that shape your brand.
