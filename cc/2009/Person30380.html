Catrice M. Jackson’s Summary

Catrice M. Jackson, M.S., LMHP and LPC is a Motivational Speaker, Experiential Trainer and Life Coach and is the CEO of Catriceology Enterprises. Catrice challenges people to turn off cruise control, begin to face their fears and create lives full of passion and purpose.

Catriceology is Catrice’s unique concoction and her perspective on life. She delivers engaging messages that are real, bold, compassionate and honest, and creates an environment where people are comfortable sharing and engaging. She is successful through conveying an authentic and transparent aura that generates trust and integrity.

Catrice’s messages and workshops are informative, fresh, innovative and dynamic. She believes in giving participants more than what they ask for and leaving them emotionally moved, provoked to think critically and ultimately participants will experience an “A ha” moment leading to change.
