Kevin Perera’s Summary

• Banking technology architect and strategist specializing in the solution deployment approaches across various technology platforms, as a competitive differentiator for challenging businesses.

• Extensive experience in planning, managing and executing engagements involving audiences from both the business and technology communities, and spanning product, partner, client and prospect organizations.

• Focused on delivering practical strategies, methodologies and solutions in practical yet innovative ways to match an organization's business strategy, goals and culture.
