João Ventura’s Summary

I have been very active in the European Aerospace Industry for the last decade. I started in Avionics projects in a company initially called RTSN that changed it's name to Skysoft. After a brief stint to finish my Master's course in real-time systems, I found a project that I liked so much that I decided to move to Germany to pursue it. The experience was very rewarding, and I made several new friends, but unfortunately it was very brief.

In my new company, Critical Software, I started to dedicate myself more to space projects and eventually became a fixed-term contractor for the European Space Agency, in Darmstadt (Germany Again). The experience gained there enabled me to start a new Job in Spain, in GMV, designing and implementing the satellite control system for some of the world's major players in the telecom satellite industry.
