Jennifer Griffin Chesney’s Summary

Creative, results-driven leader with over twelve years of expertise in product portfolio management of financial services software. Leads strategic planning and execution of innovative business technology products with specialties in products for Institutional Securities clients (Investment Banking, Equities, Fixed Income, Prime Brokerage, Private Wealth), financial social community Web 2.0 products for consumer clients, and streaming collaborative portfolio management tools for retail brokers and clients. Background includes managing multi-million dollar technology budgets, leading complex projects with global teams of 20-75 employees and consultants, and implementing progressive outsourcing, expense tracking, and client profitability models to optimize spend on clients and reduce operational costs.

In addition to her experience as a creator of financial services technologies, Jennifer is also an accomplished opera singer and writer. She is a Canadian Permanent Resident and a US Citizen.
