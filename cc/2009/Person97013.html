Cindy Pond’s Summary

Taylor & Pond is a web design and interactive agency that integrates the latest in technology, social media presence and metrics. Based in San Diego with a significant Los Angeles presence, our experienced in-house project managers, programmers, social media experts and interface designers custom create technology solutions and branding to meet your company's needs. We are hands on and consistently identify client opportunities, present innovative ideas, and formulate an effective strategy for our Clients.

Taylor & Pond incorporates website design, branding, strategy, architecture and organization services, SEM visibility in online communities, social and online media implementations, and rapid, responsive maintenance services.

Clients Include: Herbalife International, Creative Nail Design, Noxzema Shaving, Old Spice Shaving, Universal Group, Good Feet, Novatel Wireless, LavitaRx, Childrens Primary Care Medical Group, Bbeaute and many more.

Branding, innovative ideas, data capture, unique online marketing, GUI, social media plans and strategic design bring success to each and every project.
