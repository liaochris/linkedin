Julie Wade’s Summary

Ms. Wade is a litigation support and marketing consultant with over 25 years experience in the legal profession having worked on complex litigation cases for Fortune 500 companies at national law firms. She received her paralegal certificate from the University of North Texas and obtained two certifications in electronic discovery from Kroll OnTrack. Ms. Wade is a member of the Paralegal Division of the State Bar of Texas and serves on the Publications Committee. She is a published author and presenter on the topics of legal technology and electronic discovery.

Julie Wade’s Specialties:

Litigation Support and Marketing Consultant for legal technology companies and law firms dealing with electronic discovery issues.
