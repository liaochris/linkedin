Harriette Yahr’s Summary

Lots of experience in all aspects of filmmaking - from writing to directing, editing, shooting, producing. Comfortable, confident, creative--I am always open to inspiring projects both as a filmmaker (writer/director), journalist and film & writing consultant, including screenplay feedback and editing of varied writing projects.

I also have shot, produced, edited, and worked in film sales/distribution. My main website, www.yahrfilms.com, has extensive bio including awards, grants, projects. I also teach screenwriting and filmmaking workshops in Miami/South Florida and Traveling Workshops. More on that at MiamiFilmWorkshops.com.
