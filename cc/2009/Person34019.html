Frank Bisono’s Summary

Currently a User Experience Designer at Microsoft with the Management & Administration Experience (MAX) group. Prior to working at Microsoft, I concentrated on running a New Media and IT consulting / design firm for over ten years, and held high-level positions in sales, creative and technical roles at several telecom, internet & dot com companies.

I am incredibly passionate about combining design with technology, and absolutely LOVE what I do and the technologies that I work with on a daily basis. I am an active blogger and advocate for the user when it comes to creating beautiful, yet functional user experiences.
