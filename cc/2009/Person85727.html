Roger Glovsky’s Summary

For over 20 years, Roger Glovsky has provided legal counsel to traditional and high-tech companies in the areas of business formation and operation, financing (private placement and venture capital), employment and employment compensation (including equity incentive compensation), sales and distribution agreements, software and technology licensing, Internet-related issues, and mergers and acquisitions. Mr. Glovsky is an entrepreneurial attorney who has worked as outside counsel at two major Boston law firms and as in-house counsel for two venture-backed software companies.

Roger Glovsky’s Specialties:

business formation (e.g., incorporation, LLC formation, partnerships), allocation of equity, financing, venture capital, mergers and acquisitions, technology and software licensing, sales agreements, customer contracts, OEM and distribution agreements, equipment and office leases, and corporate administration
