Helen (Lewis) Garabedian’s Summary

Itsy Bitsy Yoga® is a development-based yoga program for babies, toddlers, and preschool-aged children. This unique program includes over 125 yoga poses, songs, and developmental activities created by Helen Garabedian. In 1999, this Boston-based company was established and presently boasts more than 500 class locations in 12 countries. Helen Garabedian’s 1st book, Itsy Bitsy Yoga: Poses to Help Your Baby Sleep Longer, Digest Better and Grow Stronger (Fireside/Simon & Schuster, 2004) is a perennial best-seller and a top ten ‘sleep’ book in the children’s health category on Amazon.com. Pottery Barn Kids is one of the many fine retail outlets where the Itsy Bitsy Yoga book is sold.

Last winter Helen begun the release her award-winning Itsy Bitsy Yoga DVD line (winners of The Parent’s Choice Foundation and The National Center for Parenting Seal of Approval, 2008.) Helen has partnered with ‘i Play Baby Wear’ to produce an IBY-branded organic line of baby yoga clothing and yoga mats, sold nationwide at Nordstrom and many Whole Foods locations too. Helen’s 2nd book, Itsy Bitsy Yoga for Toddlers and Preschoolers: 8-Minute Routines to Help Your Child Grow Smarter, Be Happier, and Behave Better (DeCapo Lifelong/Perseus Books) was released this past spring and is poised to revolutionize the way parents deal with tantrums, sleep problems, and ensures an active, healthy lifestyle for their young.

Helen Garabedian is a seasoned spokesperson and media professional. She has shared her persona, influence, and expertise with companies such as The First Years, organic infant food companies, Lululemon, Dr. Harvey Karp’s Happiest Baby Educator program, and The Greenhouse Spa. Helen is interviewed regularly by TV networks and the print media. Helen and Itsy Bitsy Yoga have been featured on CNN, MSNBC, CBS News, Fox, NBC, ABC, BBC Radio, and in People, Parenting, Parents, Child, Yoga Journal, Fortune Small Business, Oxygen, the AP Newswire, USA Today, and many more!
