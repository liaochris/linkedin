Tyler Kongslie’s Summary

Tyler is seeking freelance/full-time employment in entertainment ad design in the Portland and Los Angeles areas. Interested particularly in poster key art design.

Tyler is an Art Director with more than five years professional experience in traditional advertising design for regional and national clients. In the spring of 2007, he started his own freelance business and moved to New York City where he freelanced at a variety of clients including Bravo Network, SpotCo and The Ironworks. During that time he also sought education and experience in the commercial photography industry by volunteering at three major studios for hands-on experience. (Sun Studios, Splashlight Studios, and Sun West Studios).

Presently, Tyler is living in Portland, OR and continues his freelance graphic design business. He is volunteering his time and skills to local filmmaker, Michael Bartlett as Graphic Designer responsible for designing a promotional key art poster and flash-based website to attract financing for the film's summer production.

Tyler graduated from the University of Oregon’s School of Journalism and Communication in 2001.
