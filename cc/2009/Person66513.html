Melih Cılga’s Summary

For over two years, I worked in Millward Brown Istanbul office, as a Business Planning Consultant for Brand and Media Strategies.
As a member of our Client Services team, I am responsible for providing two kinds of consultancy:

1) Recommendations on how to improve advertisements' creative strategies and their actionable creative ideas.

2) Competition analysis reports for several brand categories, consumer insights and helpful knowledge integration solutions for "senior level" marketing executives.

In terms of formal education, I am a computer engineer graduated from I.T.U. in 1989, however, I positioned my career route in advertising, brand strategy and then business planning consultancy.

A market research based consultancy firm, a digital / web design company and three advertising agencies were the venues of my career history.
