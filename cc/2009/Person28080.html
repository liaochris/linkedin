Dr. Carole G. Moskovitz’s Summary

Dr.Carole Moskovitz is a highly skilled consultant in the field of communications, voice and performance. Dr. Moskovitz, is an accomplished entertainer, voice and performance coach, spiritual counselor and has been a professional entertainer for over 30 years.Clients range from entertainment to corporate to lay professionals in voice, voiceover, performance, communication effectiveness, spiritual counseling and mediation consulting.

She specializes in empowering clients to use the full range of their voice, healing voice-related traumas, while rediscovering their natural voice, sound, style and the joy of speaking, singing, performing, and communicating... in all areas of their lives.

Drawing on her skills in traditional education and over 35 years in spiritual/intuitive education and training, Dr. Moskovitz draws upon a wide range of traditional and creative modalities in working with her clients: sound, toning, expanding vocal range, breath work, communication skills, authentic movement, inner listening/dialogue/rhythm, creative arts, writing, spontaneous singing, song-writing and more.

Utilizing these skills herself as a performer, she has leant her voiceover talents to local business and television spots and her singing has touched the hearts of many, from Town Hall in New York, clubs and private work in Philadelphia and New Jersey to selected venues and private work in the L.A. area...and is known best for her love of romantic ballads.
