Chris Reid’s Summary

Chris Reid is the founder of Phoenix Designs, an Internet marketing firm based in Baltimore, Maryland. He is also a partner in a local information technology company that offers open source solutions to businesses.

Phoenix Designs provides an expansive range of Internet marketing services. Some of these include search engine optimization (SEO), Pay Per Click (PPC) campaign management and PR services.

We also offer search engine marketing (SEM) friendly website development. We predominately develop on the open source CMS platform Joomla. All of our eCommerce sites are powered by Magento.

Chris Reid’s Specialties:

SEO, Internet marketing, PPC management, Website design, Joomla CMS website development, Magento eCommerce website development.
