Roisin Markham’s Experience

  • Consultant | Facilitator | Artist | Creativity Coach

    Creative Dynamix

    (Management Consulting industry)

    June 2007 — Present (2 years 7 months)

    Success through Innovation | Creativity | Action

    Roisin Markham combines her creative savvy, business acumen and strategic vision with her passion for people. Enabling individuals and organisations to set their objectives or goals and follow them through to achieve their own success.

    Creative Dynamix offers SME's, Networks and Not for Profit organisations consultancy on
    - creativity in the work place to enhance revenue and productivity,
    - business development strategies and reviews,
    - communications within teams and to clients
    - developing individual career satisfaction

    Roisin’s approach mixes facilitation, active listening, lateral thinking, solution generation, change making, project management and results.

    As a Creativity Coach Roisin works with individuals and groups to mentor and facilitate:
    - accessing the creative process
    - managing change through creativity
    - positive personal development
    - dynamic insights for personal journeys

  • Artist

    Ballygarrett Art Studio

    (Arts and Crafts industry)

    November 2004 — Present (5 years 2 months)

    Roisin Markham’s arts practice includes personal art works and projects, workshops and unusual events for individuals and groups (not for profit organisations, businesses, networks).

    Ballygarrett Art Studio offers paintings, photography, fine art prints and handmade textiles for sale and rent. Some work can be viewed online, see links below. Certain collections are not published online and can be seen exclusively by arrangement.
    Roisin Markham has an excellent range of art work and prices. Corporate rates are available contact Ballygarrett Art Studio for more information. Irish companies can avail of tax breaks if purchasing art to enhance their office environment see www.revenue.ie.

    Roisin’s work will brighten any office and also makes an excellent corporate or special gift.

    She is an advocate of creative arts for all. As an artist and aspiring social entrepreneur Roisin continues to explore ways and bring opportunity and access to art and creative experiences for communities.

    She plans to build on recent successful projects with the Arts Department and the Community and Enterprise Department in Wexford County Council in bringing events to Gorey and North Wexford.
