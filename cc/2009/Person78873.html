Peter Osiecki’s Summary

Originally trained as a photographer, I planned to work in the Hospitality Industry to supplement my income and ended up finding a home for 25 years. It was a great ride, with positions ranging from line level to director level. The people in this business are great and they are warriors!

I moved away from the restaurant and hotel business 18 months ago to open my own photography business. My work spans weddings and shooting for small business websites, including Inn's and B&B's. I have recently been published in Ladie's Home Journal, March 2008, and other newspapers.

My photography also has an artistic resonance that is offered in archival B&W and Color prints, matted and framed, and available for sale at the New Hampshire Antique Coop in Milford, NH (www.nhntiquecoop.com).
