Jennifer Wallace’s Summary

I am meta. I <3 patterns. I have a love/hate relationship with computers. They are tools. Humans first invented tools, and now tools reinvent humanity. I'd like to be in on that, and make it better for humans.
Why I am an IA: To be happy, while working, I form humanly navigable Juis-Louis Borgeian paths through the digital maze-garden world of today’s realm of hypermedia and hypertext in which we live today. To be happy, while resting, I am formed by the user-friendly and trustable paths I find in my memory and memories during my “present tense”, which I hope will lead me to further and more happiness.

Jennifer Wallace’s Specialties:

I love to reverse engineer any problem, statement, or thought, to get to the root of the problem/answer synchronously, in a tool agnostic manner and methodology.
