Tapio Hakanen’s Summary

Sound designer and award winning electronic music producer from Finland. 15 years of experience in music production, remixing, audio branding, sound design for films, TV-ads, computer games, animations, multimedia and theater plays.

Past and current clients include Battery, Disney, Elisa, Finnish National Theater, Grey, Kiasma, Lowe, Microsoft, MTV, Nokia, Sony Music, Sulake, Taivas, Universal Music and Warner Music to name a few.

Tapio Hakanen’s Specialties:

Electronic music, sound & music design, user interface sound and audio branding.
