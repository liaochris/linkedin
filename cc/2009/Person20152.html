Kirsti A. Dyer MD, MS, FT’s Summary

Physician, Professional Health Educator, Professor, Lecturer, Author, Online Educator, Medical Writer and Editor. Focus has been in three main areas: grief, loss, bereavement and trauma issues in regards to medical care; end-of-life care and hospice care; lifestyle medicine, in particular health, wellness and nutrition. Emphasis in the last several has been on health education in college settings in person and online.

Kirsti A. Dyer MD, MS, FT’s Specialties:

Internal Medicine; Thanatology: Death, Dying and Bereavement; Grief and Loss; Coping with Life Challenge; NICU Parent Grief; Traumatic Loss, PTSD: End-of-Life and Hospice Care; Health, Wellness and Nutrition; Lifestyle Medicine; Distance Education; Online Learning; Medical Writing and Editing
