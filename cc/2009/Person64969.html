Matthew da Silva’s Summary

Matthew dreams of supporting himself by writing high-quality, fascinating feature stories that are ethically written. He writes on a range of topics of global interest. He currently publishes with Anthill magazine.

Matthew also has extensive experience in document production from desktop publishing, publication design and layout, to writing and editing. He worked full-time as a technical writer from 2003 to 2009 and is now looking to complement earnings from freelance journalism by taking short-term contracts for technical writing.

Matthew da Silva’s Specialties:

Feature stories in investigative mode, including longer-format, in-depth features.

Technical writing for software, including content management systems, student administration systems, and automation and control systems.
