Philip O'Keefe, PE’s Summary

As a licensed Professional Engineer, Mr. O'Keefe's mechanical and electrical engineering experience spans over 27 years in the design, testing, and operation of:

* Medical Devices
* Electric Power Tools
* Gasoline Engine Powered Tools
* Hand Tools
* Electrical Appliances
* Electronic Devices
* Electric Utility Power Plants
* Manufacturing Processes and Equipment
* Process Control Systems and Safety Interlocks
* Railroads

Since 2000, Mr. O'Keefe has been providing engineering expert witness services and litigation support to attorneys and insurance professionals all over the United States. He has been retained to provide opinions to support the positions of plaintiffs and defendants. He is experienced at providing expert witness testimony in depositions and in the courtroom.

As a certified instructor in private industry, Mr. O'Keefe has years of experience teaching technical subject matter to diverse audiences. As an engineering expert witness, he uses this experience to capture and retain the listener's attention. He aims to be understood, whatever the technical proficiency level.

Visit Mr. O'Keefe's website at www.engineeringexpert.net to learn more about his background and experience, or call 612-756-3273. Skype users can call: pokeefe6963.

Philip O'Keefe, PE’s Specialties:
