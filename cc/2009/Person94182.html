Stephanie Burks’s Summary

I started working with Oracle while employed at IU as an Unix System Administrator. Working with various researchers I installed and configured multiple instances in support of their research. I currently support 11 Oracle instances. I regularly create new schemas, use RMAN for backups, install updates, add additional packages at the request of my customers and address performance issues. Additionally, I perform the various Unix tasks associated with locating, creating, and mounting filesystems to be allocated to Oracle.

I recently modified scripts that create MySQL 5.06 servers for our Researchers allowing them access on their own port and the ability to start and stop their servers and create backups at will.

I thoroughly enjoy working with people to help them accomplish their goals. I view myself as a customer support person regardless of my position. I believe in sharing knowledge with others so that they may become stronger themselves.

I also really enjoy working with hardware. I have great troubleshooting skills from years of working at Sun and wish I could do a bit more of that. My favorite system to support has been the Sun E10K. It was a complex system to administer and I was able to use all areas of my expertise in doing so.
