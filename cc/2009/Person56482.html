Kristian Grove Møller’s Summary

Danish Art Director and multidisciplinary designer Kristian Grove Møller (nickname kRiSs cRéoL).

Since 2003 kRiSs has used his strong conceptual skills and fresh and idea driven approach to design on many projects expanding across various media and has won numerous high profile awards in the process.

"I have a true passion for what I do. Always pushing myself to evolve as a designer and conceptual thinker, coming up with new original ideas trying not to settle for the 'trendy' or the 'norm'."

Kristian Grove Møller’s Specialties:

Art Direction, graphic design, illustration, print, film, motion graphics, animation, concept development, branding and identity, interactive web sites.
