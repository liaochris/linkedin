George Wright’s Summary

A proactive, adaptable and conscientious client facing Senior Consulting Architect, George comes with 25+ years of international experience in IT and software engineering with a focus on the defence, finance, telecommunication and geospatial client industries where he has delivered full life cycle business and technical projects with a strong focus on governance, design authority and return on investment

George consistently works to the highest professional standards, drawing on wide ranging technical and business expertise and contacts network in working with customer and program delivery teams towards the production of robust high quality solutions to business problems. He is recognized for his strategic and innovative thinking and has a track record of success.

With a strong passion for team building, knowledge sharing and mentoring the growth of individual team members, George sees a competent core team as a key fundamental of effective solution delivery. To achieve this, he promotes clear and concise communication and partnership between the team members, stakeholders and vendors, thereby ensuring that all participants have an understanding of project/program deliverable and business benefits in line with design authority guidelines. He is an articulate and diplomatic communicator with strong coaching skills.

He is currently working with IBM on one of their largest IT infrastructure projects worldwide. In this project George has a key leadership role in performance engineering and governance, leading a team of 15 architects and with responsibility for the performance management and capacity planning for BHP Billiton’s global IT infrastructure across all solution program streams.
