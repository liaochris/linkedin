Giles Rhys Jones’s Summary

“One of the freshest and most perceptive thinkers I have ever worked with.” August 29, 2007
Rory Sutherland, Vice Chairman, Ogilvy. Chairman IPA


The proliferation of technology has fundamentally changed how consumers and brands interact, and not only through digital channels.

The digital consumer is just a fallicy, they are just consumers, and they are digital.

Consequently interactive thinking needs to be at the heart of marketing strategy and interactive delivery at the heart of marketing delivery.

I am passionate about connecting brands and customers to drive business growth and how agencies need to structure to better deliver this.

I have done this at Saatchi & Saatchi helping to set up Vision, Agency.com setting up their Digital Marketing Group, founding AgencyRepublic and driving the digital agency at Ogilvy.

Presentations, training and work has taken me to Australia, South Africa, America and much of Europe.
