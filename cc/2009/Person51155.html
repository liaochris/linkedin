Joshua Mack’s Summary

Digital product creator and strategist with over 15 years working at major media companies and start-ups creating excellent products and online businesses in matrixed environments.

*Evolving brands online
*Producing compelling editorial and marketing offerings
*Translating ideas into products, tools, and marketing campaigns

*Pushing the envelope in meaningful ways with an eye towards measurable results
