Mark James, CPC’s Summary

Mark James is a Certified Personnel Consultant and the President and Founder of Hire Consulting Services, LLC in San Diego, CA. He has been providing career coaching and executive recruiting services for over 19 years.

Previous to starting HCS, he served as Senior Vice President of Recruiting at The Angus Group, the oldest and one the most respected retained search firms in Cincinnati, OH. In 2002, Mark formed an ongoing strategic partnership with Centennial, Inc., another respected Cincinnati search firm, where he created and developed a very successful career transition coaching practice for senior executives and professionals.

During his early career spanning over 15 years, he held several sales and marketing management positions, including 7 years with the Agfa Photo Division of Bayer Corporation, in addition to Management Recruiters, Polaroid Corporation, Pepsi-Cola Bottling Group, The Gallo Winery and United Parcel Service.

For the last six years, he has been the host and facilitator of the monthly ExecuNet Networking Meetings for executives in career transition in Cincinnati, San Diego and Irvine, CA. He is also an online moderator and facilitator for ExecuNet's Networking Forum and the Consulting Executive Roundtable.

Mr. James has served on the Board of Directors for the San Diego Professional Coaches Alliance for the last 3 years. He has a successful blog, Career Catalyst and been published in several news articles, books and newsletters, conducted numerous seminars, career workshops and public speaking events.
