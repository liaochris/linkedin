Alessia Canori’s Summary

After a degree in Science of Communication, I had a collaboration with Antea cross media agency, as web writer junior for institutional and turistical website, with the responsibility of ideating and creating web contents.

Then I moved in Gimacom (Rome), where I was responsible for marketing and communication, including promoting the image of the firm
through web and others media and then creating and updating web content for both the Italian and the English versions of institutional website (www.gimacom.com).

At the moment, I'm working in Linfa, a society leader in creating and developing
e-learning, e-communication and e-information products for a number of high-profile clients including ALITALIA, IlSole24ORE, LUISS, BNL and Coop.
I was responsible for different aspects of the production process: storyboard creation; product debug; realization of didactic material and instruction manual attached to products; website updating through the support of content management system.
