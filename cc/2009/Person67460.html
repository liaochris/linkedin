Michael Knight’s Summary

For most people, deciding to buy or sell a home is the single largest financial decision that they will ever make. The real estate professional that the client selects to represent them will play a vital role in whether or not the client is able to successfully negotiate and close their transaction on their terms. The client, as well as all of the parties involved, relies heavily on the real estate professional’s experience and advice. With this large responsibility being placed on the real estate professional it is imperative that the professional be knowledgeable and capable of properly handling the task.

I have worked hard to ensure that I am the most qualified professional to represent you. I have invested heavily in my education and career by earning four college degrees, three professional designations, and a Real Estate Broker’s License. By educating myself about the market and the industry I am able to effectively and efficiently represent my clients and their interests. In addition, I am a Realtor® which means that I belong to the National Association of Realtors® and subscribe to a Code of Ethics designed to protect the public. I am committed to my clients and my profession and look forward to providing you with exceptional service now and in the future.
