Maurice Brooks Sr’s Summary

As a practical, interactive, solution-focused therapist, Maurice Brooks, Sr.’s treatment approach is to provide support and practical feedback to help clients resolve current problems and long-standing patterns. With sensitivity and compassion, he helps clients find solutions to a wide range of life challenges and psychological issues.

Blending conventional and alternative approaches, Maurice Brooks, Sr. draws on a variety of styles and techniques to incorporate what will be most helpful for each client. He strategically works with each client to help them build on their strengths to identify and achieve life goals.

His practice provides a number of services, including executive group therapy; individual coaching sessions; hypnotherapy for subjects such as smoking cessation and weight loss; and empowering group seminars.
