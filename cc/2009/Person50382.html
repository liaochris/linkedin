Jonathan Spinney’s Summary

15 years combined experience working with location technologies, GIS & geospatial services, geodata, wireless location network infrastructure, and GPS handsets, and in a wide range of roles as an industry analyst, product manager, industry solutions manager, marketing director, business development VP, and academic.

Most recently I was Vice President of Business Development at Sonim, a manufacturer of rugged GPS-enabled mobile phones where I worked on carrier, retail, & B2B sales, partner branding, grassroots global-market insertion, enterprise and consumer LBS applications design, and supplier agreements. Prior to joining Sonim, I was Developer Marketing Director at Openwave positioning a carrier-managed mobile location web service delivery platform in the B2B developer ecosystem with Sprint, and deploying mobile Browser, MMS, and AJAX device software solutions with Kyocera USA, LGE, Motorola, Sagem, Samsung, and Qualcomm. Before that, I had an Industry Solutions Manager role at ESRI, where I built a large developer-partner network serving industry verticals and consumer subsegments jointly with wireless carrier sales teams from Sprint, NEXTEL, and Bell Mobility. In 2004 I won ESRIs Award in Innovation. Before ESRI, I deployed the first wireless e911 location-based products at AT&T, Sprint, T-Mobile USA, Telus, and Bell while at LBS pioneer SignalSoft. These systems still route half of all the 911 emergency services calls in the U.S. and Canada today. I started my wireless work as a Senior Analyst in Washington D.C. with research & consulting firm The Strategis Group where I built, managed, and sold a wireless-market segmentation GIS product that tracked FCC spectrum auction activity and coverage build-outs. My tech career began at M7 Visual Intelligence, an angel-backed remote sensing and LIDAR innovator funded by Crown Castle International.
