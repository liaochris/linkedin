Kathy O'Reilly’s Summary

Produced nationally-syndicated TV talkshows and magazine format TV news and entertainment programming for 15 years before moving over to the Internet in 1999. Developed and directed all PR, Social Media Strategies, Corporate Communications and Media Relations for one of the pioneering Internet brands. Currently manage traditional Media and Analyst relations and participate in Social Media strategies and outreach for the leading global online career and recruitment web site.

*Manage Traditional PR and Social Media for product announcements and outreach to boost brand awareness and drive traffic.
*Develop and maintain all media relationships (broadcast, print, online, bloggers) with business, trade and consumer leads.
*Manage social media messaging and networking.
*Provide content leads for company Blog.
*Direct/staff media interviews.
*Work with product leads to determine news angles.
*Manage media training sessions, speaking and interview prep for company spokespeople.
*Research, Develop and Execute new Global Speaker's Bureau.
*Write press releases and media alerts.
*Develop and manage pitches for media and analysts.
*Manage external relationships with analyst and research firms including Forrester, Jupiter, Gartner, comScore and Nielsen NetRatings.
*Manage outside PR firm.
*Develop ongoing speaking calendar for spokespeople, identifying top-tier conferences for senior management and product leads and identify spokespeople within organization.
