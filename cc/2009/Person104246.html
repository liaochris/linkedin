Wendy Flanagan wflanagan@murraymedia.com’s Summary

Murray (http://www.murraymedia.com) is a leading design and technology studio that provides agency services such as strategic marketing, creative design, and Web-based technology for mid-sized to large Fortune-level clients in the NY/NJ metro area. We design marketing and communication solutions that enable companies to effectively: improve sales performance, promote consistent marketing, and maximize employee efficiency. Murray talent includes: marketing consulting, project management, information architecture, copywriting, Web design, and Web programming and support. We are certified in Ektron and Sitecore Content Management Systems (CMS).

As one of three principals at Murray, I have spent almost half my lifetime immersed in this think-tank entrepreneurial environment. I have been a change advocate at ADT Security Services, Novartis, Wyndham Hotels, Verizon Wireless, and SimplexGrinnell.

I have served on the Board for the NJ/IABC (http://www.njiabc.com), where I acted as President in 2005. In 2005, I coordinated the NJ MarCom Council (http://www.njmarcom.org) for joint networking events for the non-profit professional groups in NJ. Events welcome increasing numbers of attendees (430+).

I served on the board of the NJ AMA; and act as moderator on the B2B Special Interest Group forum for the American Marketing Association (http://www.marketingpower.com). I am a member of MENG (http://www.mengonline.com) and serve on the board of NJAdClub (http://www.njadclub.org).

I have presented to audiences up to 150 people on a variety of topics, including Integrated Marketing Communications and Brand Building Through Technology.

My firm has won many awards for design http://www.murraymedia.com/awards.aspx and I have personally been honored by NJ Biz as one of NJ's 50 Top Women in Business; and inducted to the Advertising Hall of Fame by NJAdClub.
