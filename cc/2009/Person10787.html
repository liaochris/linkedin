Bert Schiettecatte’s Summary

I have been a user of professional audio equipment from early on and have always been interested in using technology to give people the ability to create.

After getting my MA/Music, Science and Technology degree (Stanford University), I returned to Belgium to found PERCUSSA.

Our goal is to design revolutionary products and technology for the professional audio and electronic musical instrument industry.

Bert Schiettecatte’s Specialties:

Complete design and development cycle for pro-audio / electronic musical instrument products.
