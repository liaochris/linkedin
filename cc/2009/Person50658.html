Jose A. Carmona’s Summary

I have been teaching both Spanish and English as a Second Language for 24 years at community colleges and universities in New Jersey and Florida. These are some of my accomplishments:

*managed/chaired language and English as a Second Language programs
*increased enrollment in every program managed some by over 100 percent.
*written and published five books including textbooks of ESL and Spanish, poetry and a professional book
*consulted for various businesses and educational entities
*founded and managed several businesses
*developed and taught online classes for four colleges
*published over 80 poems in magazines and journals
*presented over 100 times at conferences and invited engagements
*received numerous educational awards
