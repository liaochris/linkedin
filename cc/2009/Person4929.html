Ana Leonor Torrano Grecco Borges’s Summary

Ana Leonor is a speech language pathologist with a big heart for kids and a mind for business! This is a result of many years of clinical experience allied to tools given by a MBA in projects. She has considerable experience working for both public and private sectors. Is proud of been responsible for many special projects like “Home Care for Technology-Dependent Children”, among others. In addition, from later 2005 and beginning of 2008 Ana Leonor have lived in the U.S. where she studied English and improved her personal skills. In returning to Brazil, assists voluntarily a non-profit which takes care of children with cancer, reorganizing its projects and finding sponsors for them.
