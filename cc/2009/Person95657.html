Sue Burke’s Summary

Began paid career in 1972 as high school columnist in local newspaper. Significant experience in small and medium-sized publications and as a freelance writer and editor. Work involved graphic design and photography. Additional experience in English teaching, public relations, and fundraising.

Became full-time freelancer in January 1993. Published work includes magazine and newspaper articles, essays, fiction and translations.

High level written and spoken Spanish.

Permission to work in Spain and U.S.
