Raymond Doran’s Summary

An experienced, motivated and detailed Software Engineer with a complete end-to-end understanding of the software development process. Over 12 years of professional experience in software development, technology support, and graphic arts. Software development skills include Rich Internet Applications (Ajax, Flex, AIR) and Java based frameworks, as well as traditional development with SQL, ORACLE, JAVA (J2EE), JSP, PHP, EJB, Hibernate, Spring, Struts, C++, HTML, XML, BlueMartini. I am an experienced in all areas of Object Oriented Application Design (OOAD) using the Unified Modeling Language (UML) and Design Patterns. I also have experience managing small development teams. The past eight years have been mainly focused on the Java family of technologies with a strong emphasis on distributed applications over the internet or corporate intranet. The ability to speak comfortably in large groups, one on one with clients, or mentoring peers is a skill that I have been able to acquire as well.
