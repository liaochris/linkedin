Jerry Weinstock’s Summary

Jerry Weinstock, President and CEO, has the energy and vision of a true entrepreneur. His 20+ years’ experience includes a broad mix of business development, sales and sales management, hardware and software technical competency, and marketing know how.

Jerry has successfully launched three companies based on new economy principles.

• In 1996 Jerry was one of the first to develop a for-profit HTML based e-mail newsletter targeting a niche marketplace.

• At the height of the dot-com craze, he sold a permission-based e-mail newsletter business (AutoIdeas.com) for a niche market for the rate of $33 per name – a rate that was previously unthinkable.

• Jerry has been recognized for his professional contributions and served on the Board of Directors of the American Marketing Association - Kansas City Chapter as the President and VP of Interactive Services from 2001 through 2005.

• In 2008 he became the President of the Kansas City Chapter of the International Association of Microsoft Certified Partners, an international organization. Jerry was one of the founding members of the chapter.

• He has spoken on the subjects of e-mail marketing, technology integration for small business and fighting spam to professional associations, including marketing, security, and building construction associations, and at technology industry trade shows.

• In 2002 Jerry became the first and ultimately only Kansas resident to successfully file 12 lawsuits against bulk mailers that spammed iBizInitiatives with unsolicited mail. To chronicle the successful lawsuits and educate other people, he created the Web site – KansasNoSpam.com.

• In 2004 he formed the Kansas City Chief Marketing Officers Group which is based on exchanging ideas and driving results. The KCCMO has now gone web 3.0 and is a Linkedin group.

Jerry Weinstock’s Experience

  • President & CEO

    CRM Innovation LLC

    (Computer Software industry)

    May 2008 — Present (1 year 3 months)

    CRM Innovation LLC is a Microsoft Gold Certified. We develop add-ins for Microsoft Dynamics CRM 4.0.

    Our latest product - Web2CRM - is a visual form developer that allows CRM users to create a form for their website for any corresponding Dynamics CRM 4.0 entity. The visual form designer reads the metadata from the CRM database and creates a facsimile of the CRM form in the design tool. Using Microsoft’s Silverlight 2.0 software with a drag and drop environment allows the CRM user to visually create the form without any programming required. Web2CRM is a Software Plus Services (S++S) application.

    InLine Help provides guidance to a CRM user when they are filling out CRM forms by allowing the creation of custom tooltip messages that are displayed on mouseovers of labels or controls.

    AutoNumber is an add-on product that can create a unique, easy to read reference number for records in the CRM system.
