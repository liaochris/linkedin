  • Web author and publisher

    The Art of Patient Care

    (Medical Practice industry)

    January 2009 — Present (7 months)

    At age 60, after 33 years of practice in Internal Medicine, I have started to organize and write down my thoughts and experiences related to the art of caring for patients. I am hopeful that these pages will be useful to younger members of the profession - those in the formative years - as well as to older health care professionals wishing to "renew their vows" so to speak.

  • Hospitalist

    Sentara Virginia Beach General Hospital

    (Medical Practice industry)

    January 2003 — Present (6 years 7 months)

    I am currently a member of a 7 person hospitalist group taking care of hospitalized patients with a variety of serious medical illnesses such as congestive heart failure, pneumonia, asthma, serious infections, strokes, etc. I did a combination of office and hospital work for about 27 years, although now I have limited my practice to hospital work only.

  • Model Railroader

    Building Your Model Railroad

    (Arts and Crafts industry)

    1990 — Present (19 years)

    I have been working on model railroad layouts for the past 19 years in my spare time. I am currently working on my fourth layout - an N-scale freelanced railroad based in the Appalachians around 1948 when steam engines were still doing most of the work. Recently, I've taken a break from my layout to develop a website containing all the information, tools and techniques needed to build a model railroad. (http://www.building-your-model-railroad.com)
