Adam Ducker’s Summary

I am a 26 year old graduate of Computer Science with a minor in Digital Graphics. I have 4 years professional experience with Web Development. I currently live in Newark, DE and work as an independent contractor for companies on both the East and West coasts.

My current workload includes cleaning up and refactoring CSS on existing company pages as well as converting brand new comps and wire frame designs into functioning XHTML, CSS and JavaScript powered pages.
