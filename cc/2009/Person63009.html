Marjolijn Kamphuis’s Summary

Marjolijn Kamphuis is Producer Digital Media at MTV Networks in Amsterdam, coordinating online projects for all channels including Nickelodeon, TMF, MTV and Comedy Central. Projects range from branded content microsites for advertisers, to interactive web- or mobile portals for featured TV shows or channels.

In the hours that remain after a full-time job at MTV, Marjolijn writes for popular Marketing- & Tech Blog DutchCowboys.nl. She recently launched it's spin-off site: DutchCowgirls.nl; A blog on trends, marketing, media and gadgets written by female (Digital) Media professionals.

She watches movies, a lot. Listens to music, a lot. Would like to start a cool-brand one day and write a book. Not necessarily about the cool brand. Is also the founder of viral marketing stunt givemeaneos.nl, and spoke at the Online Strategic Marketing Masterclass @Nyenrode University fall 08 and 07. You can book her as a speaker on trends and Digital Media via TheNextSpeaker.com
