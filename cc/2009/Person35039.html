Max Garkavtsev [max @ qarea.com]’s Summary

I am the Founder of an independent software testing company BugHuntress QA Lab and a software development company QArea. It is my core business and main passion for the last 7 years.

We build QArea as a Test-Driven Company. What does it mean?

QArea provides software R&D services (Java, .NET, C++, PHP). Using the term "Test-Driven company" we emphasize that independent QA/QC control is used not only as services for customers but first of all as an INTERNAL QArea procedure. In other words:
- EVERY software development project in QArea starts and finishes in BugHuntress QA Lab.

Our SQA engineers are not assimilated among developers - BugHuntress QA Lab is completely independent. BugHuntress task is to let QArea stakeholders to know the truth about our projects at any phase of the lifecycle. No ungrounded optimism, no dominance of a project manager’s opinion - just impartial and thorough information about the project progress.

That is why I say that Quality is the essence of QArea name, QArea business and QArea production processes.

The customers can enjoy BugHuntress services as well. In addition to independent software testing, BugHuntress QA Lab offers services to mitigate inherent risks of software development outsourcing. In this way we make your outsourcing initiatives more cost-effective. Really, an IT project failure is much cheaper to prevent rather than to fix.

BTW, such an independent QA/QC control of outsourcing projects is especially significant in times of a recession when each dollar you paid should work for you at 100%.

Some time ago we put together a list of advantages of independent software testing. The list turned out really large! You can see it at http://www.bughuntress.com/software-testing-services/independent-software-testing-benefits.html.

I travel a lot; I’m an active networker and a member of many international business clubs and communities. So, feel free to contact me [max at qarea.com] and join my network.
