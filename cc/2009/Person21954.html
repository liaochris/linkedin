Dana Shultz’s Summary

Business-savvy licensing and intellectual property lawyer with in-depth knowledge of law, business and technology. By developing close working relationships with clients based on a positive attitude and rock-solid dependability, I help them achieve favorable outcomes in contract negotiations and other legal matters.

Most clients are small technology companies, though I work with large, publicly-held corporations, as well. Whatever their size, I bring more than a decade of in house legal experience at such companies as Lexis-Nexis and Visa, where I was VP and Legal Counsel.

JD, Boalt Hall (University of California at Berkeley)
AB, Communication Sciences, University of Michigan

Dana Shultz’s Specialties:

Advising clients on a wide variety of legal issues, such as corporation / LLC formation, intellectual property strategy, employment, compensation, corporate law, litigation management and regulatory compliance.

Drafting, negotiating and interpreting a broad range of agreements, including complex licensing, open source, systems integration, software development, internet application services, nondisclosure, leases, financing, executive compensation and more.
