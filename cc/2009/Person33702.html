David Fox’s Summary

I have had to change areas of professional specialization several times during the course of my career. I consider myself a life-long learner, able to become a quick-study on new subjects of interest.

My past positions include public forester, environmental consultant, and consulting forester. My professional expertise over the years has included urban forestry/horticulture, plant taxonomy, wetland delineation and vegetation cover type mapping, CAD mapping, urban tree inventory, database and software design, computer network management/help desk, and GPS/GIS mapping.

I have a strong interest in tropical forestry/agroforestry and urban agriculture as these subjects relate to poverty relief - interests that are not satisfied by my current employment. To "scratch that itch" I travel on periodic volunteer trips where I can use my expertise to help educate people while learning about their life and culture.

Experienced in broadcast radio including on-air and audio spot production. Experienced public speaker.
