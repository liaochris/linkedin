Tom E. Turner’s Summary

In 1975 I was admitted to MIT and moved to Cambridge, Massachusetts. I remained in the Boston-area for more than 30 years, providing mostly hardware and software support, programming and teaching assistance at Harvard, MIT, and other organizations. As a volunteer I helped the public access tv and free + open source software communities . Recently I have been spending more time in Port Washington, NY to help reorganize the Twin Pines Food Cooperative & Charitable Thrift.
