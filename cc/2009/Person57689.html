Crystal Kauffman’s Summary

My name is Crystal Kauffman (Pen name: Imogen Rayne). I am a Freelance Copywriter, Published Author and Poet, and Songwriter with eight years of experience. In addition, I am the Founder of the Laugh at Cancer Organization Inc., which is a non-profit organization. My mission is to help families facing cancer touch in anyway and create a Global of community for those, who have been touched by cancer…directly and indirectly!

You can find out a little more about me and LACO, by visiting my website listed in my signature. In my personal life, I am blessed three beautiful children and a handsome stepson. My hubby and I have been married two years, but we dated nine years. Thankfully, I am celebrating my sixth year anniversary of being a cancer survivor. The aggressive treatment left me disabled and struggling with health issues, which has made it even harder for me to find gainful employment.

Eight years ago, I was a high school drop out, but I wanted to earn an education. Since then, I worked hard to succeed in high school adult program, because I wanted to change my life. Additionally, I completed two freelance writing programs and I have earned my Associate Degree in Business Arts. Now, I am pursuing a Bachelor’s in Communications, which will allow me to pursue my professional career goals.

When I created this organization five years ago, I never realized how many families were hurting and need of hope and encouragement to handle cancer’s touch. Currently, I have over 500+ articles, which has been published on the internet so far. I have begun writing two novels, which are about finished. Personally, I love to dabble in every kind of writing, so I wear many types of caps in my life. However, my true passion is helping others touched by cancer.
