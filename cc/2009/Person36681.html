Glen Hawkins’s Summary

I merged my 10-year old graphic design business (Hawkins Creative Services, Inc.) with 451 Marketing of Boston, MA. As Creative Director I manage the creative team of designers copywriters and developers to create award winning creative solutions to our clients' marketing challenges.

451 Marketing (www.451marketing.com) - The Leader in New Media Communications™ – is one of the premier lead generation agencies in the country that utilizes cutting-edge communication techniques on the web. The firm’s areas of expertise include Online Lead Generation, Social Media Marketing, Public Relations, Search Engine Marketing, and Web 2.0 Design and Implementation.

Glen Hawkins’s Specialties:

I lead the creative team in developing print, corporate identity, advertising, event graphics and Web 2.0 design.
