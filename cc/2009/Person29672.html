Eric Gavaldo’s Summary

During all my career, my activities have been focusing on Testing and Automation with as constant objective of improving the quality of the products I was working on. I've been acting on different areas as different as:
- networking software/hardware (network access servers, routers, VPN etc.),
- legacy software (distributed applications)
- embedded software (mobile phones, smart cards, setup boxes etc.)
- and internet (WebTV).

I'm now acting on the other side ;) ! trying to build products of the highest quality as possible.

On my free time, I'm also the owner/developer of XQual XStudio (http://www.xqual.com), a free test management tool available on Windows, Linux and MacOSX platforms.

I see myself as being determined and goal oriented, self-evaluative and sometimes critical of my own performance.
Given objective, I'm able to develop my own plan, manage my time and focus my effort on a daily basis to reach these goals. I would also describe myself as cheerful, sociable and enthusiastic.
