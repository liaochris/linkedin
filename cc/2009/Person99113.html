Tim Shepherd’s Summary

I have worked as a high-tech industry analyst since 2006, researching and analysing mobile technology markets - specifically around the smart phone and location based services markets. However, my role has also led me to follow trends in other mobile devices such as MIDs and the wider mobile phone market.
I have experience in writing analytical reports, assessing qualitative and quantitative trends, collecting and collating shipment tracking data, forecasting and working on confidential consultancy projects and bespoke work for a broad range of technology focused clients. I am also used to presenting to clients, participating in webcasts and interviewing, talking with or advising senior personnel within technology companies on trends and strategies.

Tim Shepherd’s Specialties:

The smart phone market, GPS mobile navigation and location based services (LBS)
