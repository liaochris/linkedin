Matt Buchan’s Summary

Matt is an MBA student at the Michael G. Foster School of Business, University of Washington. With an academic focus on finance, strategy, and management, Matt is also involved outside of class as an officer in the MBA Finance Society and as member of the Entrepreneurship Network, NetImpact, and the MBA Ambassadors clubs.

Prior to business school, Matt worked for several years in the actuarial consulting field for Watson Wyatt Worldwide where he developed strong skills in project management, communication, and analysis.

Matt plans to combine his rigorous quantitative training with a focus on current business issues and trends to pursue a career in corporate finance. He is currently completing a summer internship in supply chain finance with Amazon.com.
