Lynne Jarman-Johnson’s Summary

"When I need a 'jolt of energy,' I contact Lynne and she pours her energy into me for a full cup of productivity!" Brenda Moore, Urban League.

"Lynne's energy and enthusiasm for your goals is infectious. Her presentations are thought provoking, researched, and exclusive to her audiences." Vickie Smith, Lake Michigan Credit Union.

Lynne Jarman-Johnson is the President/Owner of Jarman-Johnson Communication. Recognized professionally in the National Speakers Association, she helps businesses learn & implement effective communication tools to transform their workplace...”The JjWay!”

She is respected for her personalization and her innate ability to ensure audiences feel connected and “a part” of the learning every step of the way.
