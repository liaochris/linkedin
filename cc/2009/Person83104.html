RepoExpress Home Tour’s Summary

AS SEEN ON CHANNEL 8 AND 10. ALSO FEATURED IN THE SAN DIEGO UNION TRIBUNE

Coshow Real Estate Group introduces the REPO EXPRESS, offering ONE STOP SHOPPING for homebuyers and investors interested in bargain priced foreclosed property in the Metro San Diego areas of Downtown, Hillcrest, North Park and Normal Heights. Each of our tours is tailored by property type, depending on your interest: Single Family, Condominiums and Investment properties.

We currently have limited availability to join our REPO Express foreclosure bus tours, so please call our office to start the reservation process.

REPO Express tours will showcase 10-12 of the properties on each tour. There will be lenders and other real estate professionals on site to answer your questions. Visit www.RepoExpressTour.com for more information. Each tour has limited availability, call the REPO Express Hotline @ 619-294-3221 or email info@repoexpresstour.com to reserve a seat on the REPO Express!

Coshow Real Estate Group is a full service real estate brokerage located in Hillcrest and serves the Metro San Diego communities. The Coshow Group is made up of a diverse group of real estate professionals with specialized knowledge in Residential, Investment and Commercial properties. Visit www.coshowrealestategroup.com for more information.
