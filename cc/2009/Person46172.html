Jen Sickels’s Summary

Hi everyone.

Well, I moved to Cleveland from Athens in June, and have been working with customers at Renewal by Andersen since July.

Back up a little.

I graduated in June 2008 from Ohio University's E.W. Scripps School of Journalism with a bachelor's in online journalism. I stuck around and worked at The Athens Messenger/Vinton County Courier as a copy editor until the end of May 2009. That position included editing stories and photographs as well as paginating my little fingers off. (I understand and sort of cherish the irony in working as a paginator with a degree in online journalism.)

As a freelancer, I'm primarily interested in editing copy, and I love paginating (brochures, fliers -- you name it). I also would love to break into photography and gain experience in writing copy. Feel free to send me a message if I can be of help to your publication.

My strengths: I'm an editor in every sense of the word, I'm level-headed, and I'm a quick learner.

I have references and my work at the ready if you're interested.

Jen Sickels’s Specialties:

Macromedia, Adobe Suite, AP style, selecting and editing photos/video/audio, paginating (mostly with Quark), being courteous, content management systems, delivering flowers.
