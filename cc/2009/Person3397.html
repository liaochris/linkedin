Alex Heberling’s Summary

I'm primarily a storyteller who just happens to be able to capture people well on paper. I am an independent webcomic artist with plans to go full-time with my projects within 5 years. I manage the production, publishing, and merchandising of my work, which caters to many different age groups and backgrounds.

I graduated from Ohio State University in 2008 with a BFA in Art & Technology.

Alex Heberling’s Specialties:

Digital imaging and manipulation with Adobe Photoshop, character design and portraiture, design consultation, customer service, and web design.
