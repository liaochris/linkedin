J. Michael Lewis’s Summary

As a director, Justin started producing short content under the banner Tempered Zealot Productions to better understand several aspects of filmmaking processes, and to hone his directing abilities.

Justin has produced seven (7) short films to date. A Thousand Words: Bloodline, co-written and co-directed with Jerod Brennen, won Best Horror Short at The Indie Gathering (2007) and Best Music at Frightmares Film Festival (2007).

Justin is currently producing an independent horror short, A Thousand Words: Revelation. The script was written by Jerod Brennen, production will begin in August 2008 and post-production should wrap in late 2008 early 2009.

Justin is continuing to develop feature-length and short content. Tempered Zealot Productions anticipates wrapping production on an independent feature, Dream Thirteen, and producing another short film in 2008. Justin is actively seeking partnerships with professional production companies interested in producing and distributing a feature horror trilogy.
