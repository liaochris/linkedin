Daniel Davis’s Summary

I have a rich work history in multiple settings and conditions. I spent 7 years in aviation as a Airframe and Powerplant Mechanic, 7 years in the US Army as a Behavioral Science Specialist and the past 13 years in Nursing Informatics. My down time is spent as the Media Ministries Team Leader of a large church. We create multimedia presentations for use in-house, stream video on the Internet live and record DVD's of the service.

Working in these varied settings with multiple systems has given me a unique skill set from which to address issues and find solutions. Regardless if the solution demands a hardware solution, software solution, or a little of both - I am equipped to identify the appropriate solution and implement it at minimal cost.
