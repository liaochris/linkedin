Shaun Milne’s Summary

Media consultant examining how trends and strategy can be developed and applied practically in both digital and print environments.

Substantial journalistic experience gained from roles as reporter, sub-editor, news editor and assistant editor on various national UK newspapers.

Former co-owner and director of an award winning newspaper and magazine publishing company specialising in digital page turn publications.

Provide advice to other companies on improvements in working practice, strategies and development of core business.

Motivated self-starter with generous contacts books and hands-on approach to developing lasting relationships with key stakeholders.

Highly organized with a proven ability to work across a range of media platforms including National Press, consumer publishing, digital magazines and online.

Experience of working within hostile environments overseas including problem solving, risk assessment, liaising and content delivery.

Additional experience of business management, forward planning, editorial conferences, staff training, conflict resolutions, crisis management, ambassadorial roles, confident writing, editing and commissioning skills, staff development, budgets and dealing with both legal and compliance issues.

Pro-active high level relationships across media, business and political communities.

Elected as Fellow to the Royal Society for the encouragement of Arts, Manufactures and Commerce (RSA) in 2008.

Active blogger for a number of sites, seasoned ghost-writer for newspaper columns.

Keen advocate of educating next generation of journalists on how to marry traditional newspaper skills with new media.

Shaun Milne’s Specialties:

Digital newspaper and magazine production, reporting skils, news management, media law, newspaper production, contract negotiations, copywriting, newsgathering, strategy, online news, web development, training.
