Samuel Linares’s Summary

Samuel Linares is IT Manager and Information Security Knowledge Office Manager with Grupo Intermark. With +13 years of security, system integration and project management experience. He leads and has created all the security services offered by Grupo Intermark, and before in Tecnocom, one of the top 3 IT companies in Spain, implementing a lot of security solutions in customers, including compliance audits, penetration tests, ethical hacking, firewalls and IPSs deployments or security network designs. He holds various certifications including ISACA CISA, CISM & CGEIT, (ISC)2 CISSP, GIAC Systems and Network Auditor (GSNA), GIAC Assessing Wireless Networks (GAWN), Google Hacking & Defense (SSP-GHD), BSI (British Standards Institution) BS 25999 & BS 7799 Lead Auditor, NetAsq CNE & CNE, Juniper JNCIA-FW, Checkpoint CCSE & CCSA, Sun SCNA & SCSA, among others and is the author of InfoSecMan Blog. Samuel holds a B.S. in Computer Science from the Universidad de Oviedo and is University Specialist in Data Protection by the Colegio Universitario Escorial Maria Cristina. He is author of IS2ME Methodology. (You can obtain more information about Samuel in his personal web page: InfoSecMan)
