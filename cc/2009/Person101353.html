Tony White (twhite@themilitaryedge.com)’s Summary

I have over 16 years of military recruiting experience with the Air Force Reserve Command and 15 years of managerial, supervisory, project management, and event planning experience.

Presently I am the Program Director for The Military Edge. We are a veteran owned and operated company that places its focus on getting veterans hired in corporate america. We host job fairs across the country and invite you to participate with us. Our job fairs are open to the general public as well.
Consider attending one, two or all of our job fairs in 2009.
