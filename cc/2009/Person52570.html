Justin Fuller’s Summary

G2 provides professional telecommunications Contract Negotiation services to Fortune 500 and Global 100 companies across all industries. Our clients are some of the most well-known names in business with complex networks and telecom infrastructures spanning across the Americas, Europe and Asia Pacific. We single-focus our business in this niche area because it requires the highest degree of expertise, intellectual capital and market resources in order to consistently perform on the leading-edge of the market. G2 is the only privately-held, negotiation-only firm in the U.S. that process and utilizes our unique and superior resources of Insider Knowledge and Benchmark Intelligence in order to deliver a truly world-class contract for every business we represent.
