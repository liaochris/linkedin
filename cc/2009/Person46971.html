James Fowler’s Summary

I provide design and implementation (C++ and Python) expertise for distributed system development, with an emphasis on performance, reliability, and integration of third-party (commercial and open source) resources. I am currently developing tools for performance monitoring and fault isolation in parallel (distributed, multi-threaded) systems.

I've worked with development teams at Morgan Stanley, CSFB, Goldman Sachs, E*Trade, BellSouth (AT&T), Verizon, Telcordia, Microsoft, and many others companies in the financial, telecom, and ISV markets.

While I am not a LION, I am open to LinkedIn invitations, especially if you are actively involved with systems using C++ or Python. I won't automatically accept anyone, but I'll never report your first invitation as abusive (spam/"Don't Know"). Email: james@openseaconsulting.com
