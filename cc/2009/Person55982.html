Klaus Kaasgaard’s Summary

- Accomplished executive with proven track record in research, design and product strategy. Expertise in translating customer insights into actionable business intelligence, including marketing requirements, product requirements, and user experience models.

- Several years experience in leading and motivating research and design teams of up to 50+ people. Possess leadership skills to articulate visions and the business cases behind them, create both team- and executive buy-in and excitement, solve complex personnel issues and ensure effective and timely execution.

- Internationally renowned leader in the field of user experience and human-computer interaction.

- 15+ years experience in software design and research across multiple domains, including web, mobile, enterprise software, and medical informatics.

- 2000 Book on “Software Design and Usability” was reviewed in the SIGCHI bulletin (http://www.acm.org/sigchi/bulletin/2001.6/novdec01.pdf).
