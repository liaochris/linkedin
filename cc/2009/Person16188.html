Carole Barklow’s Summary

I am a senior-level systems/software engineer in the Aerospace/Defense industry.

My background includes extensive experience as a senior systems engineer, flight systems engineer, software engineer and manager, programmer, analyst, and scientist. I also held positions as technical and science writer, editor, and research associate.

Currently I am a GPS Senior Systems Engineer at SAIC in the Ground Segment OCS IPT verifying requirements and performing risk management. Recently I supported the Space Based Infrared Satellite System (SBIRS) as a senior software/systems engineer and test lead at Lockheed Martin. In recent roles with SPARTA, Inc., I supported multiple contracts for the Army and for NASA Dryden Flight Research Center, including Future Combat Systems and X-43 (Hyper-X). With additional documented success in leading the test software development effort for the Hyper-X project, which earned recognition in the Guinness Book of World Records, I will certainly be an asset to any team.
