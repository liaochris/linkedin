Most people think of branding as how they present themselves and their company. I take branding beyond simply the message into something that produces tangible results. By integrating branding into all areas of business – Sales, Marketing, and Operations, you create a dynamic value proposition that comes alive, gets leads and generates revenue.

Brand Iron is a leading branding and marketing consulting and implementation firm in the Rocky Mountain region. Applying its three-phase, seven-step proprietary system to Forging Brands, Driving Revenue®, Brand Iron helps companies build business and improve bottom line results.

Brand Iron strengthens marketplace position by analyzing and improving sales, marketing and operations process holistically. Brand Iron's various service offerings include strategic planning, graphic design, public relations, advertising and direct marketing. Additional information can be found using our website link.

In addition to working with clients, I love participating in community activities and public speaking to educate others on the power and importance of branding and business development. I enjoy sharing my enthusiasm for what can be achieved when branding and strategy are aligned.
