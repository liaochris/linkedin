John Mangor - Baurley’s Summary

I have been working in animation since 1996. In the past 15 years, I've been fortunate enough to have worked in all types of the animation industry... from feature films, video games, tv/commercials, music videos, independent, motion rides and web. I have worked in a large span of animation styles as well as experience in modeling, texturing and even the experience of running my own company for the last few years. My animation company Kranioclast Productions specializes in FX for film/tv, music videos, web design and animation, and multimedia using a variety of tools.

I continue to challenge myself to grow, to learn and to excel at my craft, leaving behind "my best" at bringing the imagination to life whether it be with a crew or solo.
