Mike Fitzgerald’s Summary

I specialize in the Technology market in and around the Manhattan area. Our work covers a variety of industries and corporate environments. We have a history of successfully finding and representing the following types of candidates: Software Engineers, Project Managers, Interactive Developers, Information Architect/User Experience Designers, Director/VP/CTO's, Systems Analyst.

Most of our work focuses on the application and systems level, more so than the infrastructure side of the house. While we have built strong partnerships with some of the better known companies in the city, we also represent a lot of small to mid-size, less publicized companies offering dynamic work environment, close-knit cultures, and lifestyle flexiblity.

I am always interested in speaking with qualified technologists, and I prefer to talk with people who are not "actively" investigating the market. The ideal time to learn about your next career opportunity is when you're not concerned about your current one.

Please reach out to me if you're interested in speaking.
