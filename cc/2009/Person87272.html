Ryan Petersen’s Summary

Ryan Petersen has over 9 years of experience in enterprise software sales and IT project management. Ryan has worked closely with many Fortune 500 and Federal government customers on a broad range of implementations including; application performance management, network management, data warehousing, business intelligence, predictive analysis, high-availability, storage management and disaster recovery to name a few.

Ryan currently sells best-in-class solutions to address: application performance management; network engineering; capacity planning; and network configuration audit & compliance to large enterprise accounts in the Southeast for OPNET Technologies. Ryan earned his BS in Aeronautical Science from Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University in '96 and his Masters in Business Administration from Oklahoma City University in 1998. Ryan has also served in the U.S. Air Force and Air National Guard.
