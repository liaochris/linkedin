Lisa B. Marshall’s Summary

www.lisabmarshall.com - Lisa B. Marshall
www.thepublicspeaker.tv - The Public Speaker

Specializing in communication skills for business and science professionals.

Described as passionate, highly interactive, and energetic, communication specialist, Lisa B. Marshall, works with organizations to help professionals to be persuasive, engaging, and memorable every time they communicate. She is host and creator of The Public Speaker: Quick and Dirty Tips for Improving Your Communication Skills. Free, fun, and practical, this show is part of Macmillan's QD Network and debuted at #3 in the iTunes Top 100.

From public speaking to conflict management to podcasting, Lisa delivers large and small group seminars, keynotes, and private consulting using an evidence-based approach. Sample institutional customers include: University of Pennsylvania, Genentech, Inc., Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, Locus Pharmaceuticals, Stanford University and Merck & Co. Clients also include individual professionals (at all levels) for private, one-one-one assistance.

Organizations and leaders need more than good ideas to get ahead. Just as important, is the ability to communicate ideas in a captivating, concise and compelling manner.
