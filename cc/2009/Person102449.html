Robert Koller, CAIA’s Summary

Robert Koller is a lawyer at Clifford Chance based in Frankfurt am Main / Germany. Before he worked in the financial services department of a top Spanish law firm and in the funds department of the biggest Gibraltar law firm. Previously, he gained also experience in the diplomatic field, having worked at the Austrian Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Robert has expertise in funds (retail, private and particularly hedge funds) as well as capital markets (including bonds, derivatives and ISDA documentation), project and asset finance. Robert also has experience in corporate and corporate finance work, including take-overs, private placements, Public Private Partnerships, compliance and M&A. He was involved in establishing electronic trading platforms for clients and has worked also in Mexico, France, Austria and the UK.

Educated in Austria, Robert graduated an Austrian and a Spanish law degree. He is qualified as a Spanish lawyer and is admitted to Bar Association in Frankfurt as a European Lawyer. He is a Certified Alternative Investment Analyst and fluent in German, Spanish, English and French.
