Doug Everitt’s Summary

Serving up Mac OS solutions for Creative Professionals in Denver, Boulder, and around Colorado since 1989. Nearly all new clients come through referrals from satisfied customers.

Co-founder and Host of the Rocky Mountain West Conference (g rockies) on The WELL (well.com) 1994-1998. Social networking online since the 1980s, before it was called that.

Personal interests include politics, baseball, music, lesser-known ethnic restaurants of Denver, and food & dining (in general). Proud fan of the Colorado Rockies baseball team; I've cheered them live at Coors Field since it opened in 1995. Baseball is the most important thing that doesn't matter, in case you don't know that.

Doug Everitt’s Specialties:

Mac OS system support, cross-platform network integration, wireless network security, equipment purchase recommendation, migration to new systems, Mac OS X Server support, general IT consulting, and making software "play nicely" together.
