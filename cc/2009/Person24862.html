Davor Maričić’s Summary

I established Institut za elektroničko poslovanje in september 2008., with commercial projects targeted to market in Q3/2009.

Specialties:
Web/IT business development, Management and organisation process development, Public relations, Corporate communication, Marketing/Brand development, consulting

My goal:
To sharpen my expertise in PR/Communications and Marketing as well as corporate business and finance knowledge.

In March 2005. I organised Communications/PR at KING ICT (M SAN Grupa, www.MSANGRUPA.com), as Head of Communications.
At that time we develop web-system 5CMS further and applied it to all regional web portals, so in January 2006. I started as Head of Web services in M SAN Grupa (KING ICT HR/BiH/SRB, KimTec SRB/BiH/CG/SLO, Pakom MK, BAKS Grupa, Elektromagic BiH, EMSC...).
We further enhanced the system so more than 60 portals of group member companies uses this system, for corporate or brand web sites (www.VIVAX.com, www.MSGW.info, www.MS-START.com, www.SAMSUNG-HR.com).

09/2004. at KING ICT I started a portal www.ODLIKASI.hr as Editor in Chief, a joint project with Ministry of Science, to develop advanced web-system.

Late 1999. as Testing Lab Chief at IT/Computer magazines VIDI and PC Play, in Jan. 2001. I became Editor in Chief.
For more than 3 years I was responsible for media operations, organisation and daily production.

During 1997-1998. I advanced as Head of Marketing at Aromara, Zagreb.

During 1997-98. I was Web-media producer on Croatian National TV (HTV).

In the 1990`s during study (Chemistry engineering @ FKIT, Fakultet kemijskog inženjerstva i tehnologije, Zagreb) involved in music/video industry, I owe a music recording studio and did some of Croatia`s music stars. I hosted radio shows on Radio Velika Gorica (1992-95) and Otvoreni Radio (1996-98).
I was assistant at various technical seminars in FKIT faculty (Sveučilište u Zagrebu, 1992-96), as well on Media Seminar at Faculty of Journalism (1995-98.).
