Michelle Cullison’s Experience

  • Web Consultant, New Media Speaker

    Daystar Digital Development, Inc

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    October 2000 — Present (8 years 10 months)

    Since 1993, Michelle Cullison has seen websites evolve from online brochures to powerful business tools. Michelle’s 14 years of experience in website programming and owning her own business makes her a natural at explaining the latest Web 2.0 technology to businesses. “Web 2.0 is really about building trust online and it happens just like it does at local networking events,” says Cullison. “Business owners need to get in front of their audience and stay in contact to forge real relationships and convert them into business.” Cullison applies web technology to handle business processes and establish a presence online. Her passion is to educate business leaders in the greater New Orleans area on how to use this technology to their advantage.
