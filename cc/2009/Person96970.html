Tausha P. Major, MBA’s Experience

  • Chair of Professional Development Committee

    Philadelphia Association of Paralegals

    (Legal Services industry)

    February 2009 — Present (10 months)

    This committee was founded in 2000 and has established contact with paralegal programs, colleges, law firms and legal placement agencies to promote the paralegal profession. The committee sends paralegals to area schools, colleges, and paralegal programs to speak about the paralegal profession. The committee also focuses on key issues facing the profession, such as educational opportunities, salary issues, the PACE exam offered by NFPA, and promoting better utilization and development of paralegals. The committee organizes several events throughout the year, including the annual Student Forum and the Joan Weldon Scholarship award.

    Tausha is currently designing a comprehensive mentorship program linking working paralegals with paralegal students and new entrants into the legal industry. Relationships with area colleges, universities and trade schools have been enhanced to create a cohesive program and, to bring her vision into reality.

  • Owner

    The Administrative Link

    (Legal Services industry)

    July 2008 — Present (1 year 5 months)

    Tausha P. Major is described as an out-of-the-box overachiever in the pursuit of excellence! She has 20 years experience in the legal industry. She earned a paralegal certificate in 1989. She continued her education and earned a B.S. in Business Communications from Chestnut Hill College and an MBA in Management from Eastern University. Her specialty areas include personal injury, medical malpractice, wrongful death and product liability. She has extensive experience in public speaking, management, event facilitation and professional training & development. Tausha also served as the systems administrator for a law firm.

    Tausha is passionate about promoting continuing education, training and professional development. She is a regular guest speaker and lecturer at colleges, seminars, churches and other venues.
    She truly is your Administrative Link!
