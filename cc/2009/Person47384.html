Jim Harris’s Summary

I am an independent consultant, speaker, writer and blogger with over 15 years of professional services and application development experience in data quality (DQ), data integration, data warehousing (DW), business intelligence (BI), customer data integration (CDI), and master data management (MDM).

I have worked with Global 500 companies in finance, brokerage, banking, insurance, healthcare, pharmaceuticals, manufacturing, retail, telecommunications, and utilities.

I have a long history with the product that is now known as IBM InfoSphere QualityStage, for which I was named a 2009-2010 IBM Information Champion for InfoSphere.

Additionally, I have some experience with Informatica Data Quality and DataFlux dfPower Studio. I am a regular contributor to the DataFlux Community of Experts.

I am on the Expert Panel and a Contributing Editor to Data Quality Pro, which is the leading data quality online magazine and community resource dedicated to data quality professionals.

I am a member of the International Association for Information and Data Quality (IAIDQ) and the Iowa Chapter of Data Management Association International (DAMA).

I have strong skills in client engagement, negotiation, technical translation, presentation, training, and mentoring.
