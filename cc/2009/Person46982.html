James F. Robinson’s Summary

Filmmaker and visionary new media innovator and speaker with strong international experience and relationships. Futurist and media trend-spotter. A proven leader in getting results and innovating new forms of media and bringing new creative services to market. Founded CinemaElecrtic Inc. and built a mobile distribution network in more than 50 countries before taking the company public. Extensive experience in motion pictures and television, wrote, produced &, directed the award-winning independent motion picture, STILL BREATHING which was distributed by October Films (now Focus features) domestically and Paramount Pictures, Universal and Disney internationally. Created TEXT-ME TV for client Active Loop, a live broadcast Participation TV project in association with CW Network affiliates. Professional speaker and author.
