Roberto Garavaglia’s Summary

STRATEGIC CONSULTANCY

Successful track record in Electronic Payment Systems business, for more than two decades I'm committing on the conception of new business models and innovative products/services. Thanks to my own long term vision I’ve been able to achieve excellent goals with wide margins ahead of the market and anticipating competition.

In several companies leading in e-Payments arena, I’ve led complex projects in Italy and around the Europe ranging in SEPA, e-Commerce, Mobile Payments, Cross Border Acquiring, Revolving Credit and Loyalty Schemas.

Experienced in market analysis and great expert in European regulatory for SEPA (PSD, e-Money Directive, etc.), since 2003, I'm working as free lance Senior Management Consultant, offering a strategic consultancy for banking institute and new-comers who are interesting to compete in the Retail Payment Services market as Payment Institutions, EMIs, Card Issuers/Acquirers or processors.

In 2007 I've conceived the "PAGAMENTI 2.0" concept, evolution of a Payment Services along the social-economic scenarios of the emerging architecture of participation, defining the role of the "Payment Services Consumer".

e/m-PAYMENTS EVANGELIST

Scientific adviser for e/m-Payments, I also act as chairman and/or keynote speaker at conference/seminar/workshop and as independent journalist and active columnist for magazines focused on new payments business.

In March 2008, I've created and launched CloseToPay (www.closetopay.com), with to now its average 130-150 hits per day, it’s the unique Italian blog committed on the
e/m-Payments knowledge transfer, pursuing the goal to bridge the gap between user and providers of Electronic Payment Systems /Services.
