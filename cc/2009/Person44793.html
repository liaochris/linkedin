Jake Carey-Rand’s Summary

I am the best resource possible in leveraging technology and people to improve the business process, flow and profitability. My background includes international business ventures, IT and telecommunications planning and sales, partner development and executive recruiting. This helps to create a unique perspective and approach in order to do what I do best; I make it easier for my clients to concentrate on their business' core competency.

My clients, partners and references call on me for many reasons. I collect, review and analyze their telecom services. I analyze procedures and billing, vendor/client communication and other business issues which are affected by technology. I will also present a more comprehensive approach to make ongoing management of the business easier.

Fundamentally it comes down to one thing: Transitioning your technology from a cost center to a profit center and getting the right knowledge and information to help you when you need it most. This is done through effective and efficient partner and vendor management in addition to ongoing communication.

I am always interested in early stage opportunities in international trade and technology/Internet ventures.
