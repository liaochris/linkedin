Barry Adams’s Summary

I'm an experienced specialist on all things web, both internet and intranet. I have broad experience with content management systems, web development projects, online marketing, search engines, social media, email marketing, and consulting.

Aside from my daily job managing a wide array of online marketing activities for Seopa's insurance comparison websites Quotezone.co.uk and CompareNI.com, I also write for SearchCowboys.com, Europe's leading blog for search marketing professionals.
