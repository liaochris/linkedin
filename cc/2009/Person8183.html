A. Sean Gellatly’s Summary

ENERSUL is an international technology based company working in hand with oil and gas companies which have need to develop and input mechanical scrubbing practices for the extraction of premium sulphur from sour gas.
Our ability to bring a premium product to market, as well as the offering of varried forming systems and varriable levels of associated costs keeps us the world leader in our field.
We are the world's largest sulphur producing company.

My position is logistics based, working from within western Canada on behalf of many international Enersul locations as well as multiple foreign companies and governments.
My direct involvement includes; Warehouse Control, International and Domestic Shipping/Receiving (air, sea and ground), Staff Supervision... etc.

Although we are dominant outside of North America, we also have strong holdings in both Alberta and BC.
