Richard Hames’s Experience

  • Distinguished Professor and Director

    Asian Foresight Institute

    (Research industry)

    July 2008 — Present (1 year 6 months)

    The Asian Foresight Institute is a research and advisory body located at Dhurakij Pundit University in Bangkok with research links to other universities in Melbourne, Singapore, Taipei and Dubai. The Institute is a successor to the Australian Foresight Institute which was founded by Professor Richard Slaughter at Swinburne University. The AFI undertakes both commissioned and proprietary research into global futures across a range of literacies and interests. We also give strategic advice to governments, international NGOs and corporations (from explicitly non-western perspectives) concerning their ongoing viability.
