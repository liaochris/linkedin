Piermarco Rosa’s Summary

-From October 1991 to October 1995 he wrote on ConsoleMania and The Games Machine.
-From March 1996 to March 1997 he wrote on PC Professionale (Mondadori Informatica).
-From november 1996 to july 1998 he wrote on Il Mio Computer.
-Since July 1997 he contributes to the daily newspaper Il Secolo XIX, mainly in the cultural area.
-From March 1998 to March 1999 he worked as marketing analyst to Software&CO.
-From January 2000 to February 2001 he worked as Information Technology Director at PrimoCanale, the greatest Ligurian tv network, planning and realizing the first italian web tv site, turned into G8 official tv web site.
-From March 2000 to April 2001 he was Executive Manager of software channel Gamestorm of Internet portal CiaoWeb (FIAT Group).
-From March 2001 to March 2002 he was Information Technology Director at Iso Sistemi, manager of a team for creating Internet portals.
-During 2002 he started a collaboration with University of Genova, Faculty of Engineering (Multi-medial Electronics laboratory), planning the game for palmOS computers VEgame, produced in association with MIT to Science Exhibition in Venice.
-From October 2002 to July 2003 he collaborated with Soloinrete as director of electronic magazine GameXpert.
-Since October 2005 he collaborates with University of Genoa, Faculty of Engineering developing educational games on next generation tv platforms.
-From April 2007 to March 2008 he worked as Producer (Project Manager) at Gameloft, leading international developer and publisher of videogames for mobile phones and consoles, for which he has directed and produced "Pro Moto Racing", world distributed and titled in Italy as "Loris Capirossi Pro Moto Racing".
-Since June 2007 he teaches Videogame Design in the European Institute of Design in Rome.
-During 2008 he taught Electronics and Digital Systems in Genoa.
-Since July 2008 he is the Executive Director of the Master in Videogame Design at European Institute of Design in Rome.
