Gary Bamberger’s Summary

Professional and personal leadership coach with a proven track record of working with people to identify what inspires and motivates them, develop their vision and goals, then provide support in making the necessary changes in their lives.

IT and Project Management Leader successful in designing and optimizing technologies to drive business performance. Extensive expertise in the financial services industry, specifically banking and consumer lending. Strong track record of leading matrixed, cross-functional and remote teams ensuring collaboration as a focused unit to achieve aggressive business goals. Particularly effective at directing the evolution of technical and non-technical solutions from concept through implementation, constantly managing the needs of the stakeholders, the team and the project. Successful in intense and demanding environments experiencing rapid change through internal growth, acquisition and revitalization. Decisive, solutions-focused with top notch presentation skills. Certified ScrumMaster and Certified Scrum Practitioner with proven ability to successfully transform organizations to deliver projects using Agile, Lean and Scrum practices within the context of existing organizations. Effectively develops and delivers training materials and workshops.
