Janet Kostielney’s Summary

JK Enterprises works closely with tourism, economic development, literacy, downtown revitalization and youth development non-profit organizations for municipalities. Other clients include engineers, architects, contractors, distributors, as well as education, entertainment and healthcare providers.

For all our clients, we produce and distribute press releases, newsletters, and blast emails. We identify potential grants, submit grant proposals and administrating grants received by our clients. JK Enterprises also creates and manages websites, produces multi-media presentations and marketing collateral. We have proven success in creating and implementing fundraising projects, and producing photo and video archives.

Jan Kostielney, owner, is a published author of non-fiction books as well as articles on websites, in magazines and newspapers. She is an accomplished photographer whose photos are used on many websites, in professional brochures and magazines as well as educational textbooks.
