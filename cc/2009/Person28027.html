Jacqui Swinburne’s Summary

Dragonstorm Records is a family run business,affiliated to EMI over 40 years ago and was owned by the father of the current owner Jacquiline. Jack Henry, who was signed to EMI with the Joe Loss Orchestra as well as his own Band `Ace Of Clubs.`Due to Jack Henry' death the label became dormant and was resurrected by Jacquiline in 2003 and is going from strength to strength! It is run by Jacquiline and her music producer son Ed.
The label is very commercial, specialising in Urban, RnB, Pop, Rock.

Jacqui also owns several other companies including: A music booking and artist development agency Stone Music Group22 which has some great bands signed to it.

A recently launched metal-Rock label & Management company, which specialises in the genres of heavy rock and metal bands.

A Book publishing house, `Dragonstorm Publishing` who has some great very talented children's authors signed, who are going through the process of publishing each book, as well as writing her own children's series `The Fairies With Attitude` with six books in the series which is also part of a Anti Bullying Campaign.

A fashion label `Ryofu` `Timeless Expressions` which includes trendy collections for all ages with a specific collection for women over the age of 40 yrs.

A beauty - Fashion and Lifestyle Magazine, with interviews, features, beauty and fashion tips plus lots more.


They are all progressing very well, it's constant hard work, with few hours to sleep but well worth it. I have a great team who I collaborate with and who work for me. Thanks to everyone who help me to make each one of my companies a success.
