Stephen Pierzchala’s Summary

In a decade-long career, I have developed a strong understanding of, and desire to share, the core concepts that drive Web performance and affect online business results.

I actively promote these concepts in my work at the leading Web performance measurement firm, and through the own experimental distributed measurement test-bed. I also actively discuss Web performance concepts and comment online new trends and ideas on my blog, Newest Industry.

I actively following trends and concepts that shape modern online businesses, including: Online branding, Reputation Management, Authority development, Productivity, Information management, and IT-Business relationships.

Stephen Pierzchala’s Specialties:

Web performance analysis, continuous improvement, best practices analysis, author, presenter, HTTP, HTML, TCP/IP, Network diagnosis, Web 2.0, consulting, evangelist, Twitter, FriendFeed, Social Media
