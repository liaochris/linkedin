Mauricio Longo’s Summary

Skilled generalist with extensive experience in product management and IT infrastructure management as well as strategy planning, software development and solutions architecture.

Solutions Architect with wide experience in designing corporate system and data architectures, working with large development teams.

Published author of over a dozen technical books on software development, various versions of Windows and more recently social media and personal and corporate marketing.

Marketing and strategy consultant to companies in the software and Internet industries.

Associate member and member of the committee of the Firebird Foundation and Global Member of the Internet Society.

Speaker at several international technical conferences such as Open Source Database Conference 2005 (Frankfurt), International Firebird Conference 2005 and 2006 (Prague), several editions of Fenasoft and COMDEX South America and Firebird Developer's Day. Presenter on several Webinars about Web development and Web 2.0.
