David Barron’s Summary

Senior executive with two decades of experience providing strategic planning, marketing and business development expertise for industry leading companies, such as Apple Computer, The Walt Disney Company, Microsoft, SBC Communications and Motorola as well as a number of small start ups. Ability to blend a strong strategic orientation with significant tactical experience to achieve successful results.

Involved in the launch of numerous products and technologies, evaluated & modeled a number of new business opportunities, developed product licensing and distribution strategies for products and services and negotiated contracts for technology licenses and product distribution agreements. Managed the creation, operations and/or promotional activities of a number of strategic alliances, joint ventures and industry associations.

Featured speaker and panelist for a number of industry events, including The Digital Media Summit and Digital Hollywood. Years of experience as a presenter in Microsoft’s and Apple Computer’s Executive Briefing Centers. Highly skilled in explaining complex technologies to non-technical audiences.
