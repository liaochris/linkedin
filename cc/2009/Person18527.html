Chris Tingom (chris@tornadodesign.com)’s Summary

Chris is a talented and business-minded technologist with a flare for design. He is personable and contributes business ideas for the betterment of the project.

No two clients require the same solution and Chris is particularly adept at understanding and developing the underlying purpose of each of his client’s needs to effectively realize their goals. Communicating those goals in design and function are what Chris does best.

Chris has been designing web sites for over 12 years, and has designed web sites for Arizona Highways, Dial Corp, Desert Sportsman, ECOtality, Viddler, and many other companies and organizations. A Scottsdale native, he keeps a personal list of places to see in Arizona at www.placestoseeinarizona.com.

Chris brings vision and thoughtful advice to any discussion about site planning or social media design. With years of experience devoted to expanding the reach of people and products online, he is able to share ideas about how to integrate new technologies and design concepts.

Throughout the process of each project, Chris is in constant communication with the client, making sure that the client’s requirements are fully understood and implemented step by step. Effective communication is the vehicle of efficiency in Chris' strategy. Chris is highly motivated to see his clients receive the end product that they desire.

Chris founded Tornado Design in 1998.

Chris is the lead product designer behind Minuteglass.
