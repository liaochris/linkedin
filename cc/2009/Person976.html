A C Powell’s Summary

Amir Clayton “A C” Powell has, over the past ten (10) years, been very successful in transforming under-performing business units into high-producing models of operating excellence. An entrepreneurial and strategic visionary in command of a clear sense of purpose, Powell has proven himself capable of both managing rapid growth and setting a tone of urgency during periods of declining revenue. Additionally, Powell has shown himself to be a leader skilled at translating conceptual models into specific business objectives and building high-performance executive teams to implement competitive strategies.

A C Powell’s Specialties:

Budgeting & Capital Utilization; Government Relations & Legislative Analysis; Corporate Direction / Vision & Strategic Planning; Revenue Development / Fundraising; Cost Reduction / Containment & Profit / Surplus Optimization; and Reorganization & Turnaround Management
