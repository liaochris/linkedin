David Norris’s Summary

If you think up some great ideas, if your friend sells those ideas, and you've got a finance guy to sort out the funding, I'm your guy to get it done. Delivery, execution, building the factory, that's me. More than just a production line manager, I'll bring creative thinking to the table and build a great team around product strategy, production and business operations.

I stated my career in travel (tour operating), then moved online in 2000. Since then I've worked in fast growing companies in travel & leisure.

Recently I've turned my hand to the hospitaility industry. At Livebookings Network, we're building the world's biggest distribution network for online restaurant reservations. It's fun, fast and challenging.

David Norris’s Experience

  • Chief Operating Officer

    Livebookings

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Internet industry)

    November 2007 — Present (1 year 9 months)

    Livebookings is owned by Livebookings Holdings Ltd. Livebookings Holdings Ltd is an e-business specialising in transaction-based booking and information services. The company is owned and run by a team of individuals who share a considerable amount of experience in building and leading international e-commerce and IT companies, as well as a solid background in working within the restaurant industry. Based in London, with offices in Hamburg, Stockholm and Madrid, and operating in 10 countries the company's core activity is to deliver online marketing services to restaurants by enabling online bookings, both through the restaurant's own website and through a global distribution network of partners. In February 2008, the company secured second round funding of £6.5m from Balderton Capital.
