Joe Pezzillo’s Summary

I know Reid from when we both worked at Apple's eWorld, he was doing content work and I was in the R&D lab in Boulder.

After eWorld, I did a variety of consulting projects with folks I worked with there, then started a dot-com doing Internet Radio in mid-1996 which rode the wave until early 2001 when the money ran out.

I took time off, traveled the world, did some mercenary management work in California for a more successful internet radio dot com that eventually got acquired, and then came home.

I've been doing subscription Internet Radio with Real Networks ( see musicforcubicles.com ) and working on a new software technology for advanced data mining.

My goals are to have fun and enjoy life,

See you there!
