Erinn Figg’s Summary

Respectfully resigned from my position as Director of Public Relations at Canyon Ranch to assist family with a (now-resolved) health crisis and pursue consulting opportunities. During my almost three years with the renowned, award-winning health resort/spa company, my responsibilities focused on directing public relations activities for all Canyon Ranch properties and initiatives, including

-- Canyon Ranch destination resorts in Tucson, Ariz., and Lenox, Mass.;

-- Canyon Ranch SpaClubs at The Venetian/Palazzo, at Gaylord Palms resort in Orlando, and on the luxury oceanliner QM2;

-- Canyon Ranch licensed products, including Canyon Ranch Your Transformation skincare, launching May 2008 at Saks Fifth Avenue and saks.com;

-- Canyon Ranch Living communities in Miami Beach and other soon-to-be-revealed locations;

-- Canyon Ranch corporate, satellite and partner PR firms.
