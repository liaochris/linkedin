* * * * *
JAN ZLOTNICK, New York City, is strategtic-creative director of The Zlotnick Group, a customer-insight and marketing-advertising agency in the Tribeca neighborhood of downtown Manhttan. Jan worked at global and regional ad agencies before opening his branding and creative media communications shop. His agency's ReThink-RingTrue ethos and work ethic help clients build authentic brands through counter-intuitive thinking and solutions.

Zlotnick's portfolio includes: healthcare, hotel, travel+tourism, fashion, real estate, packaged goods, beauty-skin care/shaving, publishing, the legal industry, and more...with clients/brands such as Schick, I Love NY, Ilford Photo, Time Hotel, AmeriHealth, Paul Hastings Legal Counsel, NY Presbyterian, BMW Motorcycles, Guess, Hackensack Univ. Medical Center, Corcoran Real Estate, Heartland Brewery, Timberline, Blue Cross Blue Shield, Consumer Reports WebWatch, and the Anti-Counterfeiting Association...
http://www.janzlotnick.com
