Melanie Benson Strick’s Summary

Success Connections is a coaching company dedicated to helping small business owners get out of overwhelm and skyrocket their results.

Our clients average a 172% growth while learning to focus on their high payoff activities and leverage their time so they can experience more freedom and sanity.

For entrepreneurs who are overwhelmed, we offer the Get Out of Overwhelm audio CD at http://www.successconnections.com/overwhelm.

For entrepreneurs who want to grow their business quickly while enjoying more freedom, we suggest discovering how to delegate to virtual contractors with The Power of the Virtual Team E-Book at http://www.thepowerofthevirtualteam.com.

We also offer more advanced training and mentoring with programs like Virtual Team Building TeleBootcamp, Fast-Track to a 6 & 7 Figure Lifestyle Business as well as masterminds retreats.

In addition to these cutting-edge coaching programs, Melanie speaks across the nation and appears regularly in publications such as the LA Times and Woman's Day. Our "Get Out of Overwhelm" and "The Entrepreneur's Secret Weapon" talks can be heard on many radio stations.
