Gaea Honeycutt’s Summary

A research, analysis, and communications consultant with experience in a range of fields. More information available at http://www.honeycuttconsulting.us and at http://www.weirdingword.com.

Gaea Honeycutt’s Specialties:

Clients: nonprofits, associations, authors and small to mid-size businesses. Services: qualitative research, analysis and program evaluation; copyediting and freelance writing; and publication, collateral and web design. Fields: child and family welfare, violence prevention and domestic violence, cultural competency in healthcare, and other areas of social policy.
