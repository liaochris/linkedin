Jayne Furlong’s Summary

Since 1989, Majestic Mortgage Corporation has been
servicing customers in Rhode Island, Massachusetts,
Connecticut and most recently in New York. Our
Founder and President, Jayne E. Furlong, has been
licensed in Real Estate since 1986 and has been
specializing in mortgage lending since 1987.

Jayne Furlong’s Specialties:

Jayne's goal is to provide customers with the best possible rates,
lowest closing costs and superior personalized service available.
It is for this reason, that over 90% of her business comes from
personal referrals and repeat customers.
