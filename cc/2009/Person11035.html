Bill Greeves’s Summary

Looking for more ways to provide affordable technology solutions at the local government level that will help us reduce manual and labor intensive process with automation and provide easier, more effective ways to improve citizen service and collaborative development of policy and process.

Specifically, I am very much involved in the development of direct communication between citizens and government using Web2.0 concepts and technologies.
