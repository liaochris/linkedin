Ivan Versluis’s Summary

Ivan Versluis is working as Global IT Engineering Manager at Almatis, a global leader in the development, manufacture and supply of premium specialty alumina products. With nearly 1000 employees worldwide Almatis operates nine production facilities worldwide and serves customers around the world. Within the Global IT team Ivan is responsible for the change part of the IT Infrastructure. In 2000 Ivan finished his system administrator traineeship and since than working for Almatis where he focuses on delivering efficient solutions in the IT infrastructure.

Ivan's expertise are networking, virtualization, windows & linux operating systems and web development. The certification he holds include MCP, MCSE Security, MCSE, CCNA and Security+.

Ivan lives in Dordrecht, the Netherlands. In his spare time he enjoys in reading books, sports, digital photography and learning about new technologies. Ivan maintains a public blog where he shares his ideas, tutorials and helps the internet community by posting solutions on the problems he solves.

Ivan Versluis’s Specialties:

- Nine years of experience in designing, installing, configuring, and troubleshooting network systems
- Experience in engineering Windows server- and client platforms
- Experience in engineering LAN, WAN and firewall infrastructures
- Experience in working and managing external IT outsourcing providers
- Experience and knowhow for file-server-, network-, user- and data migrations
- Designing, planning and migrating physical server infrastructure into hypervisor environments
