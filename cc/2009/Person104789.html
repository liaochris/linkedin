Bill Webster’s Summary

For more than 25 years, Bill has developed and managed state-of-the-art technology, marketing, media and training solutions for a number of Fortune 500 Companies like AT&T, General Motors, Hewlett Packard, Lucent, American Express, AVAYA, Aetna US Healthcare, Bristol Myers Squibb -- including such services as marketing & media strategy as well as a wide range of technology, and training solutions.

His extensive technology, creative, and media management experience has taken him around the globe managing and developing a wide range of technology and marketing projects for many business sectors, including such areas as Finance, Healthcare, Environmental Concerns, Educational and Distance Learning, Personal Development, Entertainment, Supply Chain Managment, and Non-Profit Fundraising and Development. Companies such as, Roche, Abbott Labs, Equitable Insurance, Pitney Bowes, Johnson and Johnson, Chrysler Corp, NEC, Choice Hotels, FedEx, Junior Achievement, United Way and PETA have utilized his services.

Bill currently serves as Managing Director of LeoTechnoSoft and it's sister company mea|SOLUTIONS, Global Software Solutions Providers with offices in United States, India, Canada, and the UK. In this role he oversees the development and deployment of Web-based Business Applications and Web-based Marketing Solutions for the finance, healthcare, retail, entertainment, and manufacturing sectors. With extensive experience in outsourcing strategy, and the management of offshore development teams, Bill speaks, trains, writes and consults on off-shore management, web-based marketing, software development methodologies, business strategy, organizational infrastructure, product/business viability, and technology investment strategies.
