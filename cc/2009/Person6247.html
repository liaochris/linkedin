Angela DeFinis’s Summary

Summary:

• Over 25 years as a corporate trainer and consultant receiving the highest accolades, evaluations and customer ratings. Created powerful long term alliances with Fortune 1000 clients. Emphasis on performance improvement and behavior change to support the communication success of the enterprise.

• Founder and CEO, DeFinis Communications, Inc. Successful entrepreneur and dedicated small business owner. Empower company vision, sustain quality, deliver training and consulting services and oversee all aspects of the business including sales, marketing, program development, staff training, and customer relationship management. DFCI offers a suite of seven dynamic and content rich programs for speakers at all levels. We hire highly experience trainers who deliver our programs with elegance and power.

• Over 15 years specializing in Professional Public Speaking, Presentation Skills Training and Executive Speech Coaching. Trained thousands of professionals and employees in all industries. Dedicated subject matter expert, passionate educator and eager to support the development of competence and confidence in others.

• Author and Keynote Speaker helping presenters come alive! Angela speaks on all aspects of Professional Public Speaking and offers programs on powerful delivery, message development and PowerPoint creation. Published chapter in the book, Roadmap to Success along with Steven Covey and Ken Blanchard.

• 7 Years working in the performing arts as an actress, National Movement Specialist and arts administrator for local, state and national arts organizations including The Maine State Commission on the Arts and Humanities and the National Endowment for the Arts.
