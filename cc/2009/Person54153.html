Keith Bogen, SPHR’s Summary

As a Business Professional with Human Capital Enrichment Management expertise, I have delivered HR Director, Manager and Consultant services to organizations of all sizes for the past 15 years. My networking groups and career development skills have also been of tremendous support to thousands across the United States and internationally and the circle of those for whom I make connections grows enormously each year.

Areas of expertise that have greatly benefitted companies and clients include:

Network Development
Change Management
Employee Relations
Talent Development
Process Improvement
Behavioral and Legal Analysis
Performance Management

I also have particular strengths in mergers & acquisitions, benefits, compensation, recruitment and career development.

My educational background includes dual Master's degrees in Human Resource Management and Labor & Industrial Relations, as well as certifications in Law and Six Sigma. I share my Senior Professional in Human Resources (SPHR) certification with those my efforts support.

Keith Bogen, SPHR’s Specialties:

Organizational development, change management, mergers and acquisitions, compensation and benefits, recruitment
