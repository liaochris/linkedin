Kerry Layton’s Summary

I have over 23 years of experience in Design and Communications. I currently work with organizations and corporations to develop brand communications. I am a hands on designer that can work independently or lead teams to accomplish large tasks successfully.

I want to continue to be challenged to create the best possible solutions to complex problems that, in-turn, produce measurable results.
