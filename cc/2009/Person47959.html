Jacob Hunter’s Summary

Founding a successful business in environmental consulting, I have developed a broad skill-set and reputation of integrity and success in the insurance restoration industry.

I have performed over 400 environmental assessments for commercial and residential projects. I have managed over 600 water loss mitigation, asbestos abatement, and mold remediation projects professionally, ranging in size from a moldy studio apartment to an entire flooded university building.
