Simon Gianoutsos’s Summary

In my career I have worked in a variety of roles including Business Analysis, Development, Testing, Deployment, Ongoing Support, Project Management, Solution Architecture and Enterprise Architecture.

Most recently I have been involved with large Enterprise & Solution Architecture activities which have had no shortage of challenges.

I am also extremely passionate about Online and Social Media technologies, and have been a technical Subject Matter Expert in this space for a number of years.

My current career objective is to obtain a senior management / CIO role within a large corporate organisation that fully utilises and builds upon my technical, innovative and personal skills and rewards me accordingly.

Simon Gianoutsos’s Specialties:

Enterprise Architecture, Solution Architecture with a focus on Consumer Mass Market, Online, Service Delivery, Telecom Directories and Broadband.
