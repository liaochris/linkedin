F J Bering’s Summary

I am a highly motivated individual with valuable Systems experience focused on implementing practices, procedures and systems that increase organizational functionality.

Recently, I designed an enterprise Information Architecture in conjunction with a roll-out of WebSphere portal and the associated web content management system. I lead the company's business units through a paradigm shift from the current IS/IT-centered content model to one based upon business needs and requirements. In the recent past, I implemented Lotus Sametime within the enterprise, instituting instant messaging and web conferencing functionality within the organization .

In other positions, I have lead the effort to build and design corporate Intranets and internet presences. Other various part-time consulting opportunities have allowed me to help smaller organizations build an effective web presence.
