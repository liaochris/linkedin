Kevin Ainsworth’s Summary

Partner in Ainsworth Maguire (AM) a PR business based in Bury, Greater Manchester, UK.

I believe that everyone has a story to tell. Some people want a continuing dialogue with their stakeholders, others just want to put out messages intermittently. It is my job to help people do this efficiently and professionally.

With my business partner, Adrian Maguire we run two businesses. AM is our original business and this serves business-to-business clients in England's North West. Most AM clients are in construction,engineering and technology and require continuing programmes that support their marketing objectives.

CLICKintoPR.com is our second business. This is a pay-as-you-go project based service delivered over the web. The service is for customers who have a more intermittent need for publicity. Clients are drawn ffrom many occupations and are more geographically diverse than with AM.
