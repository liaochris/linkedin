Gidons Designs’s Summary

With 15 years of experience, we are still learning something new everyday with in the Art and Design industry.
Being able to keep up with latest trends enables Gidon's Designs to keep advancing in the skills we have to offer through the Art and Design services we provide. Our aim is to provide unique and bespoke Art and Design though varies media, including 3D and Animation, which will give the opportunity for the client to incorperate what ever piece of Art or Design that would be unique for their marketing, advertising and branding.
We are also looking to build more of an awareness in the kind of media the client chooses whether it be for advertising, branding or marketing. By being for more creative and open minded, this opens the doors to more unique and bespoke Art and Design.

Gidons Designs’s Specialties:

Being able to transform a piece of Art or Design through painting, drawing and airbrushing, to a print, whether it be for a Wallpaper covering, large display, advertising or branding. Your bespoke piece of Art or Design can be applied to a variety of materials, including Wood, Glass, Stone, Metal and varies fabrics. We can also create and recreate almost any art and design digitally using the latest software including 3D visuals.
