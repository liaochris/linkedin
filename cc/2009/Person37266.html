Graham Hills’s Summary

I am a data driven online marketing professional with 7+ years experience in the travel industry having worked for Tourism Western Australia, OctopusTravel (a Travelport brand) and now Yahoo! South East Asia. I am currently based in Singapore.

I am passionate about the online space and embrace online trends. I have strong skills and experience in online marketing strategy, online campaign management, search engine marketing, SEO and analytics. I strive to increase conversion and overall return on advertising spend through a proactive approach to campaign management and strong attention to analytics.

I have advanced knowledge of Hitwise and Omniture Site Catalyst and am proficient with other analytics packages like WebTrends, Google Analytics and NetRatings SiteCensus.

I am a motivated team player with a strong desire to constantly challenge myself and others around me.

Graham Hills’s Specialties:

search engine optimisation, search engine marketing, online campaign management, email marketing, online performance analysis and benchmarking, web analytics, web measurement strategy, online product management, content strategy, online marketing strategy.

Additionally I speak Bahasa Indonesia.
