Heather Rankin’s Summary

My name is Heather Rankin and I am a full time Real Estate Agent in the Lake Powell area, an avid blogger and a professional musician with the band Sage.

The Lake Powell Real Estate blog is about Lake Powell real estate as well as national real estate news and other reflections on our area! You can search all of the Lake Powell Real Estate listings for homes and properties for sale as well as business opportunities by clicking on “search Lake Powell mls”.

Currently I hold two real estate licenses, one in Arizona and one in Utah. My Utah Brokerage is ERA Utah Properties and my Arizona Brokerage is Burton Brokerage. I am very honored and excited to work with both of them. Recently I have been added on the Indigo Ridge team at Lake Powell and am looking forward to participating in the project.

I moved to the Lake Powell area 17 years ago and love it here. Hopefully you will find something helpful or enlightening in my blog. If you have any real estate questions - give me a call 928-691-6444
