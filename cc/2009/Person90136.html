Sean Kelly’s Summary

I have been in Corporate Recruiting since 1999 after several years spent learning the business in the agency arena with such firms as Snelling and Snelling, Robert Half International and Ballantyne Computer Services Inc.

My Corporate Recruiting experience has been with Sun Micro systems, Cisco Systems, CSpeed Corporation, eBay Inc, Symantec Corporation and currently Adobe Systems Inc.

I am currently responsible for the support of the Inside Sales, Demand Generation, Professional Services, Consulting and Pre sales teams here in San Francisco and through out the rest of the Country.

While at Sun Microsystems I supported the Java Marketing organization

While at Cisco I supported the Western region of the Enterprise Sales team including all of the pre sales functions with in that region.

While at CSpeed my charter was the support of the entire organization along with one other Recruiter. This was a start up and as such, we worked on everything.

While at eBay I supported the Legal, Trust and Safety, HR and Global Billing organizations.

While at Symantec I worked with the Pre Sales or SE team as well as some Mid Market and Inside Sales functions.

Sean Kelly’s Specialties:

Having supported a variety of teams and organizations through out my career, my speciality would have to be in the Sales arena.
