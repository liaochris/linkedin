Susan Baird’s Summary

Add up the minutes and you'll find that you spend the majority of your day communicating: email, meetings, phone calls, conversations, etc. When it really matters, are you confident that your message hits the mark?

If you've always wanted to polish those communication skills, we can enhance your confidence, help you embrace your personal communication style, and show you a variety of strategies to make your communication relevant, clear, and engaging. Whether it's writing, speaking, networking, or creating an authentic online presence, we can help.

And if you'd rather outsource those writing tasks so you can focus your energy on other projects, we are happy to comply. We work hard to understand your company, your communication goals, and the impression you want to make, and then we translate your ideas into words that resonate with your target market. We specialize in marketing collateral and website content, and enjoy the challenge of taking something complicated and transforming it into a message your market understands.

Clear, engaging, and relevant communication is the most important indicator of future success. Let us help you reach your goals.
