Michelle Braun’s Summary

A hypnotherapist focused on providing individualized or client-centered therapy for improving the quality of life. Specializing in pain management, health issues, Post Traumatic Stress Syndrome (especially in regards to military and other victims of trauma), fears, phobias, hypno-anasthesia, pre- and post- medical procedures, hand writing analysis and dream therapy. Interested in advising schools and corporations on the use of self-hypnosis for stress reduction and personal self-improvement and advising human resource departments using hand writing analysis. Available to speak to any group on the wonders of the subconscious and how by one utilizing its powers can create great change in a person's life. Primary interest centered on the individual client.
