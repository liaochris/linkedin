Dr. Indu Arora’s Summary

Dr. Indu Arora is a highly accomplished international speaker, acclaimed Yoga Therapist, Master Teacher, Healer and Author with more than 27000 hours of experience.

She is a regular presenter at National & International Level Conferences.
Dr. Arora is an expert in the field of Therapeutic Yoga, Medicine Herbalism, prefers to apply a holistic approach to heal her clients through a combination of Yoga, Diverse Meditation techniques, Bach Flower remedies, Acupressure, Reflexology, Magnet Therapy, Naturopathy, Reiki, ESP, Herbal/ Kitchen remedies, Mantra and Mudras.

Dr. Indu Arora wrote her first book on yoga in year 2005 (Yoga: Ancient Heritage Tomorrows vision). She has also launched many of Yoga DVD, Audio CD / Tapes and a MP3 since 2005

A board member of the World Movement of Yoga, is a regular columnist for “Tathaastu” (Spiritual, Mental and Physical Well being Magazine) www.tathaastumag.com and “Yoga Chicago” (An Illinois based Yoga Magazine) www.yogachicago.com

Recently Dr. Indu Arora has been honored by the Mayor and Chief of Police for contributing to the community resource center of Woodridge, IL.
A recipient of “Hall of Fame” award by the NRI welfare society was also chosen as “Dupage Woman You should Know” by Dupage Woman Magazine and is an International Advisor to World Yoga Council.

Dr. Arora founded “Yogsadhna” (www.yogsadhna.com) in year 2000, to offer inter-disciplinary approach to the ancient practice of healing through yoga, meditation and alternate healing modalities.
She is also associated with Helping Hand Rehabilitation center in Chicago as a Wellness Coach and Yoga Therapist for adult with physical and mental handicap.

A true Yogini by Karma has a Doctorate in Alternate Medicine (MD), a Medicinal Herbalism degree (BMHS), and a diploma in Naturopathy (ND) and Magnet Therapy. Awarded with Yoga Shiromani and Yoga Bhaskar has a Grand Parenting E-500 T-500 experienced registered yoga teacher certificate from Yoga Alliance
