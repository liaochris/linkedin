Rick Littrell’s Summary

Rick Littrell is President/CMO of Magicomm LLC. A marketing solutions company focused on developing innovative strategy, creative and technical implementations of high value, web enabled marketing solutions. Magicomm’s content management-based web solutions, variable video, vdp, personalized url’s, cross media direct marketing implementations, and social media strategies puts Magicomm on the leading edge for providing innovative and measurable results to our clients.

Magicomm won the 2006 PODi Best Practices Award for Relevant Marketing and was winner (honorable mention) of a 2005 Printing Innovation with Xerox Imaging award (PIXi). These awards are multinational competitions created to recognize innovation and excellence in digital printing applications.


Rick is a dedicated student of marketing communications trends. He enjoys using emerging communications technologies for business development opportunities, brand awareness expansion, and start-up situations.
Working with advanced technology products for over 30 years, Rick has extensive experience in international marketing, sales, and business development. Previously, he was Vice President, North American Field Operations for Polaroid Graphic Imaging System LLC. Prior to that, he was Senior Worldwide Product Line Manager for Computer-to-Plate Output Systems for Agfa Corporation. Other positions held were in prepress production, applications development, and product development for leading vendors and print service providers within the graphics industry.
Additionally, he has published extensively in North American trade journals and is internationally known as an entertaining and insightful presenter on a wide range of marketing and technology topics.
He takes pride in being active within the Graphic Communications industry. He is a member of IPA – The Association of Graphic Solutions Providers, American Marketing Association, and Printing Industry of New England (PINE).

Rick Littrell’s Specialties:

Innovative marketing strategy development, Imaging workflow development, cross-media marketing campaigns, social media marketing, integrated marketing technology, Product launches, Event promotion and management, automated document production systems
