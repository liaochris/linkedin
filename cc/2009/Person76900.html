Robert S.C. Kirschner’s Summary

As president and publisher of Passport Publications & Media Corporation, I established the firm in 1989, to help organizations and businesses promote and establish brand awareness through custom publishing and relationship marketing solutions.

With a passion for people and publishing, I accept the challenge of representing clients at their best, and strive to redefine quality custom publishing. I emphasize standards of excellence, to perform at the highest level of service, quality and attention, given to all aspects of our product, customers and the community.

To date, we have published over 300 magazines.
