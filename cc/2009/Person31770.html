Erwin van Dijck’s Summary

Video is everywhere. From intranet, digital TV, instore communications, peer-to-peer networks to internet viewing. It is rapidly gaining momentum and importance. Video is a high-impact way of communicating with your audience. Efficient, visually attractive and effective.
