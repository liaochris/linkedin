Justin Clarke’s Summary

Director and Co-Founder of Gotham Digital Science Ltd (a subsidiary of Gotham Digital Science LLC, based in New York). Security consultant with extensive international Big 4 risk management, security consulting and testing experience. Based in the United Kingdom, with previous experience in the United States and New Zealand.

Lead author/technical editor of "SQL Injection Attacks and Defenses" - published May 2009 by Syngress - http://www.elsevierdirect.com/product.jsp?isbn=9781597494243, co-author of "Network Security Tools" - published April 2005 by O'Reilly - http://www.oreilly.com/catalog/networkst, contributor to "Network Security Assessment, 2nd Edition" - http://www.oreilly.com/catalog/9780596510305, as well as a speaker at various security conferences and events such as Black Hat, EuSecWest, OWASP, OSCON, RSA and SANS. Currently Chapter leader of the OWASP London chapter.

Currently hold the CISSP, CISM, CISA, and CEH certifications.

Justin Clarke’s Specialties:

Application penetration testing, application source code review, application architecture review, development process security re-engineering, security training, network/infrastructure attack and penetration, developing network security tools, security controls and process review, security project management, information systems risk management.
