Rita Bonucchi’s Summary

I have been working for a long time as Marketing Manager. In 1993 I decided to run my own company, wanting to build a company following my dreams and vision, without constraints:
commitment and freedom together, looking always for the smartest way to implement a strategy, hoping to work better, not longer or harder, in a no paper company.
Other consultants are not competitors, but maybe customers, or opinion leader, or suppliers: you never know. Knowledge is something to exchange and share.
This is why I am the owner of Bonucchi & Associati.
