Danny Ho’s Summary

I am a doctoral candidate in Information Systems, with expected completion at end of summer 2009.

I design and evaluate information systems and work processes to enhance computerized collaboration, accessibility to information, and data-driven workflow in healthcare and telecommunications applications. I have co-authored 4 journal papers, 7 conference proceedings, and given 6 talks at human factors and medical informatics venues. Publications can be found on my CV website.
