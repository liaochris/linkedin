Vicente Aguilar’s Summary

Senior Linux systems engineer with a strong background in software analysis and development, project and team management, and computer security.
Currently I work at Optenet as the Technical Manager in all the projects for our main customer, Telefónica.
Before this, I have worked for more than five years at the IT company of an editorial group, with 16 newspapers all around the country and more than 20 other related companies, managing all their technologies needs from the internal network to the e-mail servers, DNS, FTP, web servers backing their Digital Editions, etc. Lately have been doing SEO and web2.0 related stuff, too.
Besides, I've been developing and administering for the past three years the e-Commerce site of a comic-book store: www.valquirias.com.
