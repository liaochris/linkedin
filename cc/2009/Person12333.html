Boe Parrish 7,000+ "INVITES WELCOME"’s Summary

Managing sales people in two states and multiple locations, for US Sprint Communications Company, I was spending a significant amount of time assisting my team with personal issues, in order to help them produce more effectively on the job. This season of sales management is where the genesis for Corporate Care was born.

I combined my passion for business, and my history for serving others while in ministry, and formed Corporate Care, Inc. in 1987. My dream was the blending of biblical wisdom and marketplace excellence. We began encouraging the lives of employees, while impacting bottom line results. We have been able to add hundreds of thousands of dollars to the bottom line of many of our clients.

Significant financial results, 14% higher productivity, 21% reduced absenteeism, and improving employee morale have become the benchmarks, of our innovative 21st Century employee assistance program. Our on-site, highly relational, proactive, and highly utilized program is totally transforming employee care across the nation. Visit us at www.corporatecareok.com and begin receiving corporate care, and moving beyond compliance only!
