John Vlastelica’s Summary

We don't recruit.
We help companies recruit better.

Since 2002, we've helped organization across North America improve their in-house recruiting capabilities.

We've been hired by all different sized companies in high-tech, retail, bio-tech, healthcare, financial services, and government sectors to help ensure they're recruiting and hiring the right people, that they're managing their recruiting resources effectively, and that their recruiters, interviewers, and hiring managers are productive and focused on what matters most.

We help with talent acquisition...
-> Strategies, plans, and org models
-> Processes
-> Systems and technology (ATS/TMS, CRM)
-> Metrics and reports
-> Marketing/Employment Branding
-> Tools, guides and templates
-> Custom training for recruiting managers, recruiters, hiring managers, and interviewers

Custom Training Programs Include:
-> Interviewing Training for Hiring Managers/Interviewers
-> Recruiter Client Management / SLA Training
-> Recruiting Strategy and Metrics Training
-> Recruiting Leadership Training
-> Sourcing, Screening, and Selling Training for Recruiters

John Vlastelica is a top-rated, repeat speaker at regional and national recruiting conferences (including ERE, AIRS, Kennedy, and SHRM's Staffing Management conferences), an instructor for the Strategic Talent Acquisition Roundtable Leadership Academies, author of work published in popular white papers and 2 books on staffing best practices, and 4-term President of SHRM's Staffing Management Association of Seattle.

Learn more at http://www.RecruitingToolbox.com

John Vlastelica’s Specialties:

Building and growing a company's in-house recruiting capabilities. Candidate generation strategy and ROI analysis. Candidate assessment and recruiting process improvement. Recruiter, interviewer, and hiring manager training. Employment branding.
