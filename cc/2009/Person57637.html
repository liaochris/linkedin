LaShawn Brown’s Summary

One in a Billion Consulting is a woman owned, State of Texas Certified Historically Underutilized Business that focuses on team building and inclusion activities. We help people who work together, work together better.

Globally, we provide interactive, upbeat, on-site professional development to a multitude of clients. Our “Everybody Matters” diversity appreciation workshop has received 100% positive evaluations from its participants.

Outcomes include better communication skills, plus a greater understanding of self and others. Fostering improved human relationships ultimately creates an environment of productivity and increased revenue enabling participants and their companies to reach new heights.
