Christine Bell’s Summary

Learning and Development Professional and Chartered Member of the CIPD with over 20 years of experience working in the fields of training and learning.
I provide consultancy services to a range of clients in the public, private and not for profit sectors. I design be-spoke interventions for each of my clients using real situations and real materials so that the learning is relevant and makes an impact on the organisation’s achievement of its goals and values.
The topics I cover include: engaging and motivating employees, facilitating effective meetings, project leadership, customer service, emotionally intelligent leadership, innovation and entrepreneur skills. My relaxed and informal style creates a productive working atmosphere.
I am based in Yorkshire and have worked across the UK, Germany, Netherlands and Italy.

Christine Bell’s Specialties:

Group coaching. Action Learning. Appreciative Inquiry. Future Vision. LVT. Open Space. World Café. Facilitation to achieve change. Training the Trainer. Working with international companies with employees from a variety of cultural backgrounds. Writing learning materials. Career coaching.
