Ryan Atkinson’s Summary

An experienced marketing professional who saw the opportunity to make life easier for marketing managers everywhere.

My years in brand management taught me simple and effective solutions to the challenges of the profession. I realized that I could start a company that helps other marketing professionals achieve more, work less and get the most out of their budgets.

Now I work with a network of freelance creative professionals from around the country to provide solutions to growing businesses. I provide full-service consulting, planning, strategy and project coordination. I pull together the right people to achieve the best results. I always respect the outlines of projects as defined by my clients. I have the business know-how to be effective, and the creative experience to propel any company to success.

My business is fast becoming known throughout the region for our expertise in sustainable marketing. We help clients reduce environmental impact while achieving effective promotions. I firmly believe that businesses can be an asset to the community environment, and I enjoy being a part of that ongoing process.
