Sylvain MARET’s Summary

In 1991, after obtaining an msc in computer science from Geneva Engineering School, Sylvain Maret joined Swiss TV in Geneva. Sylvain Maret helped the Swiss TV to build a new network for delivering innovative and reliable solutions such as a new digital newsroom. Sylvain Maret also built the first Internet architecture for Swiss TV (1992). He was in charge of Internet Security (Firewall, Unix Hardening, DNS,Reverse Proxy, SSL and Strong Authentication).

In 1996 Mr Maret was hired as Security Engineer by Dimension Data (formely Datelec), the largest IT security company for Switzerland. For 5 years he built a security team for the Switzerland market. Sylvain was in charge of deployment, training, product management and security consulting. During this time Sylvain Maret was a PKI & Cryptography teacher, a Checkpoint CCSI Instructor and Ethical Hacker expert. In 1997, Sylvain Maret patented the first generation of SSL Reverse Proxy WAF supporting Strong Perimeter Authentication (SecurID, S/Key, PKI and OCSP support): a product named "Secure Gate"

In 2001, Sylvain Maret founded e-Xpert Solutions SA in Geneva. During 8 years Sylvain was the CTO. He led e-Xpert Solutions for new technology business development mainly in Strong Authentication, Web Security, Web SSO, PKI, biometry and SOA Security. Sylvain Maret was mainly involved in new technology deployment, Proof of Concept and Consulting.

As a result, in 2009, Sylvain Maret founded a new consulting firm (MARET Consulting) with a sole aim: to help companies to deal with manage security technology mainly for Digital Identity Security, Mobile Security, Web Security, Technical Audit.

Sylvain writes regularly in Technical papers and he is a Lecturer at Geneva University and a leading Security expert for University of Applied Sciences at Yverdon.

Public presentations held at OSSIR, Clusis, University of Geneva and Yverdon, Webcast at BrightTALK

Now: Technology Evangelist for OpenID and Strong Authentication
