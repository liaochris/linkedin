Ron Jones’s Summary

Ron is an Internet marketing consultant, trainer, columnist and owner of Symetri Internet Marketing, which provides strategic consulting and Internet marketing solutions for companies like Sealy Inc., Broyhill Furniture, Kimberly Clark Healthcare, Wachovia, Shaw Industries, and Kayser Roth since 1998.

Ron is actively involved in the SEM community and speaks regularly at conferences and seminars. He also conducts training workshops for Social Media Marketing, Search Engine Optimization, Paid Search Advertising and Web Analytics.

Ron also serves on the Board of Directors for SEMPO (Search Engine Marketing Professional Organization), and co-chairs the SEMPO Institute. Ron is also one of the authors for the SEMPO Institute Insiders Guide and Advanced courses.

Ron is a columnist for Search Engine Watch and writes the SEM.edu and SEM 101 columns. Sign up at http://searchenginewatch.com/3627704

Follow me on Twitter: twitter.com/ron_jones
