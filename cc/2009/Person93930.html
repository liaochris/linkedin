Stefania Lintonbon’s Summary

Stefania’s background in acting, from children’s theatre through to modern drama and Shakespeare makes her a uniquely flexible voice talent. She has a very distinctive, bright, warm, educated, easy on the ears, versatile voice specialising – but not limited to – animation, audiobooks, computer games, commercials, corporate work, narration, real estate/travel tour videos, talking toys, training materials, and radio drama.

A native of the USA who now lives in England, Stefania is comfortable voicing your project with either an American or British accent.

This professional voice artist is focused, friendly, flexible, versatile, creative, positive and takes direction well -- a skilled, trained, experienced actress.

Stefania is also available for Phone patch via Skype - if you'd like to direct while she records your project.

Voicovers are deliverable via formats such as MP3, WAVE, services such as YouSendIt, or CD (International Signed for delivery).

Please have a look at the website where you'll find a variety of demos in different genres. Custom demo requests are welcomed.

Stefania Lintonbon’s Specialties:

I speak intermediate-level Japanese. Dialects include General American, American Southern, New York, California, British RP. Character acting is my speciality, but I can happily do straight corporate voices as well!
