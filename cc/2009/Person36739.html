Glenn Swan’s Summary

I Specialize in finding the highest caliber people in the areas of market research, advertising and marketing. I've been told that I'm a "different" kind of recruiter. While I look for and still require tangible cues, I dig deeper to get to know how a candidate can help to add value to your organization. The soft skills are sometimes more important than the hard. Acquisition of great candidates is important, but retention of great employees is critical.
