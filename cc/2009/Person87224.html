Robin Whitson’s Summary

You will find that it is my unique ability to identify key sales and marketing opportunities and capitalize on them that has ensured my success. Couple that with my business acumen and you will see I have "it" and would love to use my energy, enthusiasm, creativity, technology experience, exceptional management skills, and financial background to work for you.

Strategic.Input.Ideation.Relator.Adapability

I enjoy working with small and medium size businesses on their marketing efforts and business plans. One of my areas of expertise is the design and implementations of web sites and e-marketing efforts including e-commerce and CRM implementations.

For over two decades, I helped manufacturers sell package goods and beverages to consumers through various retail sales channels including grocery, convenience stores, discount and drug stores.  I grew brands and market share regionally for national companies like Kraft General Foods, American Home Products, Mars, Tyson, Johnson & Johnson, Kodak, and Carnation.

My experience in the food brokerage business offered me the opportunity to work closely with product managers and advertising agencies like Grey and Saatchi & Saatchi.  I spearheaded the initial sales and marketing efforts including test markets for brands like Crystal Light, Kool-Aid box drinks, Ranch Style Bean line extensions, Contadina Fresh Pasta, and Pedigree dry dog food. I have been successful in developing strategic, results-oriented marketing brand strategies as well as local and regional campaigns for consumer products.

After the sale of my family-owned business, I became a Registered Investment Advisor and wealth manager working with affluent business people. Recently, I married and moved to San Antonio.  Finding it difficult to provide my Dallas/Fort Worth clients with the level of service they deserved, I sold my practice to my partner and am currently a consultant.
