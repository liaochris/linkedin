Walter De Brouwer’s Summary

Time ‘serial entrepeneur’, The Sunday Times 'collateral thinker',Financial Times ‘Ubernerd’. Involved in 2 international IPO’s (Stepstone [LSE: sso], Eunet-Qwest [nyse:Q]). Shareholder in long-distance carriers in two continents (Eunet, Qwest, Global Crossing); founder of a computer magazine publisher, taken over by VNU.
Founding father of Europe’s first “thinkubator” (Starlab) and the concept of “Deep Future” research. Now Fork Capital, Venture Capital; London-based Latitude Resources plc. CEO Pajamanation NV/SA
