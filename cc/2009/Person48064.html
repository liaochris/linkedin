Johan Martin’s Summary

Web application development providing open solutions built using open source software and based on open standards. We build secure extranets for our clients giving their clients "anytime secure access" to personalized information. Our sites are built using PHP with a variety of database backends including MySQL, Postgresql and MS-SQL Server. We strive to make our sites w3c compliant and accessible.
Looking for more financial service providers (private equity, hedge funds, etc.) that would like to provide their clients with secure "anytime access" to their information.
