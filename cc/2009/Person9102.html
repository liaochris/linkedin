Bob Bartels’s Summary

b2 ideas is a marketing communications firm specializing in the design and implementation of strategic public and media relations programs. From full-scale campaigns to short-term projects, b2 ideas has the experience and capabilities to help you meet and exceed your goals. We have experience in serving the marine, outdoors, do-it-yourself, and corporate categories of Public Relations work.

Bob Bartels’s Specialties:

Equally important, is the attention to detail and the highly targeted communication techniques the agency provides to clients.
