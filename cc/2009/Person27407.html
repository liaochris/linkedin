Jiri Donat, Ph.D.’s Summary

Multilingual internationally experienced business leader and visionary management consultant focused on telco, IT and other converging industry sectors.
20+ years’ experience.
Opinion former - columns and analyses in business and IT journals.
Frequent Chairman and speaker on conferences; teacher of MBA courses.
Author of three books (“E-business For Managers” - Grada 2000, a sci-fi novel "Stab in the back on the information superhighway" - Grada 2000 and "The ABCs of Entrepreneurship In The Web 2.0 Era" - Alfa 2007).

Jiri's life-time passion is creating visions and business scenarios for commercial sectors that are impacted by discontinuous innovation caused by information and communication technologies.
