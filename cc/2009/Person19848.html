Paul Litwack’s Summary

Paul Litwack is the Capability Improvement Coach®. He created and led the Executive Coaching practice of the International Coach Academy (Australia) and is Director of the Institute for Professional Advancement (Canada).

He has professionally served on a number of significant Association and Organizational Boards - currently as Membership Portfolio Chair for the International Coach Federation/ICF Toronto Chapter (one of ICF's top 5 chapters worldwide).

Since 1976, his thought-provoking Keynotes, Professional Coaching, TeleClasses and custom Training programs continue to help leaders to uplevel their personal, professional and business capabilities. References and Blog at www.the-coach.com

Coach Paul's real world experience comes from years of successful projects from the 'back room' to the Boardroom. He's an active community leader too (example: 1 week after 9/11 he volunteered at GROUND ZERO )
