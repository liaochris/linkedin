Summer Hagy’s Summary

I have been with Rasmussen College since April 2006 starting in National Online Admissions. In October 2006 I moved into the Career Services Advisor role on the Brooklyn Park campus. My primary role here is to place our current students and especially our graduates in careers related to their field of study. I am always on the lookout for new community connections as well as those employers looking to hire qualified, dedicated employees.

Summer Hagy’s Specialties:

Rasmussen College specializes in Bachelors, AAS Degrees, Certificates and Diplomas in various fields including Business, Accounting, Office Managment, Allied Health, Early Childhood Education, Information Management/IT, Digital Design, Nursing and Criminal Justice. Rasmussen College is proud to now also offer a wide variety of Masters Programs through our Partner colleges. To learn more visit us at www.rasmussen.edu
