Ajita Perera’s Summary

Ajita Perera is a marketing and communications professional with expertise in journalism, writing, public relations, press relations, event planning, web publishing & content management and marketing events. She is a highly accomplished, multifaceted professional with demonstrable success working with international teams in a global offshore model. She is talented in initiating and delivering creative and timely results on innovative projects. Ajita also had an extremely successful career in television as an anchor, host, interviewer, news reporter and producer.
