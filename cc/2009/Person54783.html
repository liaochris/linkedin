Kent Speakman’s Summary

I am the Director of an interactive agency that helps companies achieve their objectives through first seeking to understand, creating a strategic plan, and building out online solutions. I also work on a variety of film and television projects with my entertainment consulting company I started in the late 90's. Both are very complimentary positions to each other, especially now TV & Web are emerging platforms.

With a decade's experience in strategic marketing and communications combined with a hand on background in operations and management I am working within my skill sets and passions at Rare Method. I am really happy to be leading our business development team and contributing to the strategy team at our agency. I also help with our Public relations and special events initiatives.

It is key to understand a customer and create an experience that delivers a strong ROI - meeting their objectives. We do this through collaboration with our clients, benchmarking, competitive analysis and strategic planning. I love this part of the job, I get to work with clients in multiple industries and learn the interworking of their organizations. It is my primary role to engage new clients on both national and international levels and lead this collaboration to formulate a plan. I have been lucky enough to have worked with a wide range of clients in the Entertainment, Finance, Tourism & Hospitality, Aviation, Insurance, Luxury, Yachting, Energy/Oil & Gas and Non profit sectors.

Furthermore, I have had the pleasure of working with some of the biggest companies in the entertainment industry, which is an area I am extremely passionate about. I am also currently working on producing a full length feature 3d film as well as 2 television series.
