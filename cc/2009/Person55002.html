Kevin Dykes’s Summary

My name is Kevin Dykes and I am an entrepreneur, web strategist, startup advisor and 'new marketing'/social web consultant... an American living in Hamburg, Germany. I've been involved in building web companies as a co-founder, contributor or consultant since 1996 - providing the glue between the technical software product development & the business/revenue model to accelerate growth.

Currently I am Co-Founder of @Vibe Media Venture Accelerator, with locations in Austin, TX and Hamburg, Germany - focusing on market development and business model innovation for web companies. I also consult with company leaders on web & social media strategy.
