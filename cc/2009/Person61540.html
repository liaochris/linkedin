Gaelene Adams Wood’s Summary

Gaelene Adams Wood is the Founding Director of Making Magic Happen Ltd and it all started as a result of rewriting her Will. The Executor of the Will asked what she would do with her estate if her husband died before her - a poignant question as he had been diagnosed with cancer. She started thinking about a "dream organisation" to help people make their dreams come true. The more information she provided to the Executor, the more he wanted to know to ensure her wishes would be correctly carried out. Before long she had the outline of a business plan and realised "Why wait until I'm dead?"

Our vision is to assist as many people as possible to experience the joy and the power of realizing at least one of their dreams in their lifetime. We recognize that when people can access all the resources they need - emotionally, mentally, physically, spiritually, financially – they can achieve their dreams and make a far more powerful contribution to society.

Our vision includes a Foundation to support people who have experienced sexual abuse (and are not able to afford our services) to assist them to unlock the ball and chain of sexual abuse and achieve their dreams.

Our aim is a million dreams come true by 2020.

Gaelene Adams Wood’s Specialties:

Overcome personal adversity - for 20 years she has been healing childhood sexual abuse, living with the long term dis-ease Endometriosis and resulting infertility, and in 2002 her husband was diagnosed with Cancer.

In business since 18 in event mgmt, fundraising, admin and sales in banking, telecoms, marketing, real estate, forestry, cosmetics and floristry. She has worked for corporates, SME's, govt depts, educational institutions, franchises and an international aid agency.
