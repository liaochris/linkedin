Don Meador’s Summary

As Director of Sales & Marketing for the Landmark Hotel Group, I oversee the sales and marketing efforts of the hotels within our company's portfolio, which currently includes The Crowne Plaza Virginia Beach-Norfolk, The Candlewood Suites Virginia Beach-Norfolk, The Howard Johnson Virginia Beach Oceanfront and The Hilton Garden Inn and Suffolk Conference Center in Virginia, the Candlewood Suites Richmond Airport, and The Comfort Inn Oceanfront North in Kill Devil Hills, and Comfort Inn Oceanfront South in Nags Head, North Carolina.

In addition, I blog at "Get A Room!" where I offer advice to business and leisure travelers, group and meeting planners, tour operators and travel agents. Feel free to visit at http://getahotelroom.blogspot.com.

I'm also a guest blogger for the American Bus Association at http://www.buses.org/blog.
