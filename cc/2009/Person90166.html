Sean Sweet’s Summary

Full cycle recruitment of high volume, quick turnaround as well as niche skill set professionals. Recent focus has been on SAP and finance consultants & managers for public services projects at Big 5 management and technology consulting firms.

Strong process ownership: Candidate sourcing, offer negotiation, onboarding (including immigration and relocation transactions), retention and referral generation.

Background also includes successes in agency recruiting, human resources leadership and municipal program management.

---
Sean gets it. He understands the urgency and impact on business of open requisitions. He gets the politics and the urging and subtlety often required of corporate recruiters. He knows and excels at the process and reporting. He exceeds the sophistication candidates expect and the candor stakeholders require.

Sean Sweet’s Specialties:

ERP / IT & finance recruitment. At BearingPoint, Sean was the sole finance recruiter for Public Sector Solutions (target SAP modules: FI, FM, GM, PSCD - many candidates have CA or CGA designations). He also ran 2 Controller searches for a Canadian practice and several accounting/finance business analyst searches for teams in Alberta and the US Midwest.
