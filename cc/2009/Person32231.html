Emiel van Rooijen’s Summary

I am a senior application engineer/ architect with over 10 years experience in the field. For the larger part of my career I have designed and developed financial applications and integrations in the banking sector.

My objectives are:
- To deliver subject matter expertise in the area of finance by drawing from my 7+ years experience with financial/ banking applications
- To provide my experience with a broad range of topics such as web/ desktop application development, security, architecture, information systems and infrastructure
- To deliver the highest of quality solutions by drawing from my experience with a broad range of technologies such as the ones of Oracle, Microsoft, Sun and IBM
