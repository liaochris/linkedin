Robert Hewitt’s Summary

After graduation I worked for a prominent South American ad agency based in Sao Paulo, Brazil. Returning home to Cleveland, I then worked in the Creative Studio of American Greetings before moving to New York.

I've since created my own literary, social network, operating as its Managing Member and worked as Lead Designer for Takkle before accepting a position as Senior Lead Designer for Rave Mobile Safety (formerly Rave Wireless).

Robert Hewitt’s Specialties:

Web, print and UI design: proficient with Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Keynote, Microsoft Office, Quark, Freehand MX, and HTML code; fluent in Portuguese
