Simon Hill’s Summary

Internet-product development practitioner, consultant, and manager for 12 years; for over forty websites and mobile applications; for Fortune 1000 companies and start ups.

Developing the Online Organization: My work has included developing successful consulting, design, and engagement-management services and business-development strategies for the Internet design-and-development industry. Hired, trained, and managed teams of project managers, business analysts, information architects/UI designers, and graphic designers. I have also developed a specialty in software project-management beyond Agile, and in rescuing large, troubled software projects.

Innovation in Methods, Processes, & Tools: Developed industry-leading methodologies and tools for planning, designing, prototyping, managing, and testing websites, online software, and mobile applications, in use in several Internet design firms, including the mobile-web consumer-products R&D unit of Vodafone. I pioneered some early uses of UML for large-scale web-software business-modeling in the late 1990s in the UK.

Design Innovation: I am also an accomplished "value-centric" product manager, "user-centered" user-interface (UI) designer, information architect, and business analyst, and have performed innovative and award-winning work for clients in over twenty-five industries.

In recognition of my work, I was awarded a Denver Business Journal 2008 Top 40 Under Forty 2008 award, and am an occasional guest columnist and speaker at national and local conferences on strategy for web business, process methodologies, and user-interface design. I have conducted many workshops for clients on all aspects of planning, innovation, design, and development of websites and mobile applications.

Simon Hill’s Specialties:

Web & mobile application design, development, & management; information architecture; web-technology strategy; business analysis; user-interface & interaction design, usability & human-factor assessments of new media

Business planning & launching, stakeholder & market analysis, engagement/account management, project management, organizational structure & design, virtual collaboration tools, process improvement for online-product R&D, marketing, sales, & support
