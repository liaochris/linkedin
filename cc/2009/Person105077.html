Wolfgang Jaegel’s Summary

As Founder & Managing Director of Admax Plus, Wolfgang drives the company’s vision and is responsible for building the company’s brand with strategic marketing initiatives and client acquisition.

He also oversees Admax Plus’ high-profile clients’ marketing campaigns to ensure they are balanced with strategy, best practices and results.

Since 1996, Wolfgang has specialized in pure and applied research related to search engine optimization (SEO), web analytics and internet marketing.

He is a renowned expert on these topics, recognized for his published articles, his frequent speaking engagements, as well as his prestigious consulting and coaching programs.

Wolfgang thrives in entrepreneurial environments, where fast-paced change and continually evolving technology is the norm.

In the workplace, Wolfgang is the type of "leader" that is not afraid to roll up his sleeves and get involved with the team at all levels. A key characteristic of Wolfgang’s leadership style is that he does not ask people to do a task that he would not be prepared to do himself.
