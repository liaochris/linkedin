Traci Suppa’s Summary

WordScapes…Crafting messages through the creative use of words.

Corporate / B2B Writing Services --
Traci provides enthusiastic and professional writing and editing services for the public and private sectors. She produces experienced and knowledgeable writing on a wide range of topics, including the tourism, hospitality, retail and “lifestyle” industries, parenting, design, politics and more.

In the past year alone, Traci has worked with a wide variety of clients across an intriguing array of business categories, including a “green” chef, a mayoral candidate, a bridal gown designer, a trade show management company, a toy store owner, a furniture maker, and the former U.S. Olympic Committee coaching director.

Past projects include press releases, press kits, web site copy, e-newsletters, blogs, podcast scripts, media pitch letters, brochures, reports, business proposals,
ad copy, catalog copy, travel supplement copy, speech and talking points, conference and trade show manuals and other marketing materials.

Advertorial assignments with bylines have appeared in The Crafts Report, Giftware News, Gourmet Retailer, Hudson Valley Magazine, and Tableware Today Magazine.

Editorial Writing / Journalism --
Traci is also a published writer, with bylines and ghostwritten credits in magazines, newspapers, and web sites, including the Hudson Valley Guide, Hudson Valley News, Lighthouse Digest, Poughkeepsie Journal, TQM Magazine, Westchester County Travel Guide, Westchester Family Magazine, EscapeMaker.com, Offbeattravel.com, 52PerfectDays.com, NewParent.com, OffbeatTravel.com, RootandSprout.com, Travels.com,Travelforkids.com, TravelSavvyMom.com and TravelMuse.com.

Traci Suppa’s Specialties:

Traci enjoys juggling her avocations of parenting, writing and traveling. Growing up on assignment in the Middle East created a ceaseless cultural curiosity that permeates her writing. A 17-year professional career in tourism destination marketing provided a strong background knowledge of the travel industry. Travel is her passion, but when time or finances don't permit, gardening, decorating, cooking and other creative outlets fill the void. And she has been known to fill a scrapbook or two.
