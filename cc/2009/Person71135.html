Myrtle Beach SEO - Jan Chilton’s Summary

I once took a test to see what line of work I would be best suited for, and was told that I had a special talent for pattern recognition. It was suggested that I get into some sort of 'spy' work, puzzle solving, or even deciphering Egyptian hieroglyphics.

Instead, I ended up training myself to build websites. I am rather obsessive when I set out to do something, and this was no exception.

This was in 1999 when the internet was still young, and Yahoo ruled cyberspace. Fast-forward about 4 years and I was sitting in an office reading about the search engines and what made a website show up for a particular term. I had found my puzzle to solve, and I'm still doing it.

I've worked with real estate agents and resort management companies all over, and have a large group of friends and associates from the east coast to the west coast, as can be seen by my references on here. I pretty much eat, sleep and breathe the search engines, and real estate is a passion, although I've yet to be wealthy enough to invest in it. My hero is Donald Trump, and my greatest wish is to someday be able to help him market one of his properties...:-)

My specialty is generating leads. I do this with a combination of website optimization, blogs, classifieds, and the social sites. You will hear that content is king, but you have to know what to do with that content to make it work for you..

You can find me on My Space and Facebook as "LUVDAVY" if you want to know about me personally. Lots of pictures, MOVIE STARS...LOL. and stuff about the Monkees, web marketing, and even politics.
