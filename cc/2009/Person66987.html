Maria Hebda’s Summary

GOAL:

To position CertifiedResumeWriters.com and CertifiedCareerCoaches.com as key resources for serious job seekers and individuals who want more than just another job, but want to take their career to the next level.

To continue serving colleagues and providing them with an affordable marketing vehicle that offers vast opportunities to strategically market and promote their career services as well as industry expertise to potential clients, growing their bottom line.

OBJECTIVE:

Threefold: Connect individuals seeking professional resume writing or career coaching services with certified professionals; provide vast opportunities for writers and coaches to demonstrate their expertise; and to educate the public about the resume writing and career coaching industry by providing credential and organizational resources.

EXPERIENCE:

Maria Hebda is a Certified Career Management Coach (CCMC) and Certified Professional Resume Writer (CPRW). Over eight years of first-hand knowledge and experience creating and building a valuable marketing vehicle strategically designed to produce vast client leads for credentialed resume writers and career coaches.

Identified the need to inform and educate the public about credentialed career professionals and the "careers industry" while providing a key resource to easily locate the career experts.

CertifiedResumeWriters.com and CertifiedCareerCoaches.com are the first and only exclusive websites supporting diverse certified career professionals!
