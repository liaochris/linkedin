Ben Yandell 7100+’s Summary

Yandell and Company is a cash commodity brokerage organization. We act as an intermediary facilitating transactions in commodities such as gold bullion, rough cut diamonds, fuels, and crude oil. Our job is to match sellers with buyers by taking prices, procedures, quantities, and delivery point into account. We are not a mandate for any buyer or seller. Instead we focus on finding the right deal for the right buyer or seller at the right time.

Our primary focus is on gold, diamonds and fuels. However, we do have strong relationships with sellers of copper cathodes, iron ore, urea, used rails, HMS 1 % 2 and several other commodities.
