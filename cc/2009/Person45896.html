Jenna Taylor’s Summary

Responsible for scoping and delivering all client projects, driving new business, and aiding in planning and managing all marketing activity.

More than ten years experience of web design and usability, in particular web interaction, user interaction, usability and information architecture.

Managed web projects for many government and educational bodies, as well as commercial businesses across the finance, telecommunications and travel sectors. Small local business to large Fortune 500 companies.

"I believe forward thinking web development companies recognize the need for a usability specialist who can work closely with their clients to co-ordinate and manage their projects. I am lucky enough to have the ability to translate complex client briefs and design ideas into compelling websites that really deliver against user requirements - as well as simply enjoying team play atmosphere!"

~Jenna Taylor ( Maiden name: Eskeland )
