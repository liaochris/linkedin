Nancy Pav’s Summary

Nancy has been a licensed real estate agent for a over a dozen years helping clients all over the Northern Virginia region with both resale homes and new construction. She is a consistent top producer, having attained some of the highest honors within Long & Foster and the local board of REALTORS®. She is attentive, thorough, and will be there with you every step of the way. It's these very same qualities she brings to her position as Assistant Branch Manager of the Ashburn Office. As a buyer or a seller, you can trust Nancy to deliver the results you expect. It's why her past clients will tell you, "You Gotta Have Pav!"
