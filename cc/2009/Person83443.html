Ricardo Sanchez’s Summary

I am an experienced Internet and Games executive with a strong background in launching new products and businesses and am currently looking for a new position.

Many of my past roles have involved improving the performance and quality of existing businesses and driving new growth and usage. I have experience on both the operations and creative sides of the fence. I've managed large teams in advertising operations, production, product management, design, video production and editorial. I have also had a hands on role in the development of PC and online games, as well as editorial and video content.

I was a founding member of IGN Entertainment and a member of the launch team for Turner Broadcasting's GameTap.

I can be reached at rick@bugeyedmonster.com
