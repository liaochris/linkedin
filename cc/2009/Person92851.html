Jeffrey Hamilton Smith’s Summary

• Graphic designer, brander, lecturer, author -with over 25 years experience. Proponent of the gestalt principles of design. Advance-level education in cognitive and behavioral psychology, design theory, sensory branding and brand empathy development. Specializing in corporate, lifestyle, and fashion industries with overseas translatory product placement acumen.

• Particular interest in brand participatory behavior. Rabid interest in working with proactive businesses who embrace change; Forward-thinking B2B and B2C entrepreneurs who can fuse the association between transparency, authenticity and integrity with the healthy promulgation of brand development.

Graphic Design, Experience Design, Experiential Marketing, Product Design, Furniture Design, Branding, Sensory Branding, Advertising.

  • Principal

    Smith Design Works

    (Graphic Design industry)

    March 1989 — Present (20 years 5 months)

    As SDW passed its 20 year anniversary, we continue to approach each project with due diligence in the fields of history, psychology, sociology, graphic design, branding, literary and design theory. In fact, SDW is much more than a traditional design studio. We are visual engineers, aesthetic psychologists, and market sociologists extrapolating logic out of chaos, intrigue out of placidity, persuasion out of apathy and communication out of info-bombardment.
    Our solutions do not rest in catering to petty whims which pacify the simplest of consumer tastes, but rather that which enthralls users to the extent of a yearning involvement. We realize that in order to nurture a market's prospective desire to belong to a brand, thereby becoming willing participants, one must provide imagery and messaging which covertly seduces with underlying educational and social merit.

    For these reasons, SDW was presented the "American Design Consortium's '"Seattle's Top Studio Award" for 2007.'
