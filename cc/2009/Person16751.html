Chad Stewart’s Summary

Chad Stewart is 26 years old & has spent the past 21 of those years right here in Lake City, FL, building a thorough understanding of the local marketplace, real estate & people.

He graduated Florida Real Estate Institute in 2001 as a licensed salesperson and in 2007 received his Florida Real Estate Brokers license and is active as a Broker Associate with Westfield Realty Group.

His background includes six years of business administration, management & marketing, including the position of general manager of Westfield Investment Group, a real estate development company. He has excellent knowledge & experience with the latest technology for business & real estate use.

Chad Stewart is known for his dependability, promptness, and honesty, and is committed to treating his customers with that same level of service.

Chad Stewart’s Specialties:

Commercial Real Estate Sales & Leasing, Residential Real Estate Sales, Land & Acreage Sales, Graphics Design, Accounting - Bookkeeping, Marketing & Advertising.
