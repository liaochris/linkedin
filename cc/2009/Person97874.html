Bob Mortensen’s Summary

Graphic Designer for the Print, Web and Environment media markets for over 7 years in the greater Seattle area.

Print designer creating targeted marketing material for clients based on their company culture, target audience and specific industry.

Web designer combining targeted, conceptual design techniques with current internet technologies including; HTML, CSS, Flash, SEO (search engine optimization) and PHP.
