Tennyson E. Stead’s Summary

While developing and producing my own content in film and interactive media, I work as a freelance screenwriter and as a production executive. My background ranges from theater to the sciences, and I love telling human stories in utterly fantastic settings.

With the sale of two films I helped put into production, I'll be seeing the money to begin production on my debut feature, a strange little comedy called "Sam Bailey". In the meantime, I'm focused on selling a handful of spec scripts and on broadening my network and understanding of the business.

Tennyson E. Stead’s Specialties:

Screenwriting, directing, and producing are my focus, and other areas where I have a lot of experience include sales, investing, film market trends, contracts, writing, and management.

Tennyson E. Stead’s Experience

  • Owner

    8 Sided Interactive

    (Media Production industry)

    January 2008 — Present (1 year 7 months)

    How we see the world and how we interact with it is becoming more and more passive. Our toys have started playing with us. Telling stories for mass consumption, even in a metaphorical or satirical vein, is not going to address that truth appropriately. New media, demanding new levels and methods of audience interactivity, is the only way to cultivate an active audience. If I do my job right, the fun and the spectacle in these worlds will challenge us to embrace our own accountability - both now and down the road, as we develop these worlds into the future.

    I'm bringing back playtime, because that's how we learn to live.

  • Owner

    8 Sided Films

    (Motion Pictures and Film industry)

    September 2006 — Present (2 years 11 months)

    For a film to realize its full creative and financial potential, every element of the film's development should reflect the overall goals of the project. From the work of developing the script and securing the financing to eventual distribution, a consensus on what the film is intended to accomplish insures that everyone is working to solve the same problems. Great films are made by teams of talented people united by a common intent.

    8 Sided Films represents my stewardship over those stories born from intent too multifaceted or unique for studio production, and my commitment to honoring that intent from page to screen and beyond.
