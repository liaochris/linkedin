Matt Jessop, CIR, ACIR’s Summary

I am a senior business analyst and project manager with 12+ years experience of running large and complex IT and business projects within both the public and private sectors. I am Prince2 qualified and can demonstrate a strong track record of successful projects encompassing; business process consulting, system implementations, business recovery and change management.

My core experience is in the delivery and management of SaaS and similar online solutions, and I have a great deal of expertise in the use and integration of web based solutions and Social Media into a day to day business context. I have been invited to speak on such topics at a number of high profile industry events and am an avid user and advocate of new media as a tool for realising tangible business benefit.

I consider myself to be a results orientated achiever with excellent analysis, communication and man management skills. I have extensive public sector experience having run many projects within central government, for organisations such as the House of Commons, local government for a large number of local authorities, and the private sector for many leading utilities and other large corporates. My principle domain experience is recruitment, payroll, HR and related software solutions.

My track record includes;
- Project Manager and Lead Analyst for system implementations at 11 local authorities
- Project Manager and Lead Analyst for system implementations at a range of leading corporates including HP, EDF, British Nuclear Group, Transco, Centrica and Williams Lea
- Business Analyst for House of Commons.
- Project Manager, Business Analyst and Solution Architect for a number of SaaS solution providers.
- Business Analyst for a number of leading RPO and Staffing companies including Capita, Manpower, Pertemps, TMP, Eden Brown, Carlisle Staffing etc.

Matt Jessop, CIR, ACIR’s Specialties:

Project Management, Change Management, Business Process Consulting, Business Analysis, Solution Design
