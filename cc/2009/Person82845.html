Régis Barondeau’s Summary

Through my professional experience, I have collaborated with a variety of companies as a consultant as well as a manager. I’m currently completing a Master’s degree in Project Management. My thesis question is "How wikis can help us deal with complexity in project management?". I’m also actively involved in TikiWiki, one of the top five open source wiki.

Those three, professional, academic and IT dimensions enable me to fully exploit my skills. Their combination has shaped my added value and has facilitated the expansion of my network as well as an ongoing exchange of knowledge.
