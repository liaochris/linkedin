Meri Aaron Walker’s Summary

I'm "The Virtual Meeting Coach." I love helping business people learn to use the right virtual meeting tools and other social media to connect with clients, customers, and friends so you can cut costs, build relationships and revenues, and improve operational efficiencies in this tough economy.

I am a co-author of "Teamwork is An Individual Skill: Getting Your Work Done When Sharing Responsibility" (Berret Kohler, 2001). I spent nearly two decades as a close advisor to senior management teams in real estate, healthcare, long-term care, and technology companies in Austin, TX, helping leaders tackle and surmount the challenges of collaboration in an expanding global economy.

A popular speaker, I regularly share my insights with business and government leaders and at universities and work confidently with creative teams and business leaders across North America and Europe.

After relocating to southern Oregon late in 2006, I have become a social media evangelist, helping people use free social media and virtual meeting tools to strengthen local and distance relationships.

I blog as "The Virtual Meeting Coach" (http://virtualmeetingcoach.com) and train clients both face-to-face and online through my new company, Virtual Meeting Startup.

My popular short course, "21 Sure-Fire Ways To Build Trust Working With Others Online," is available free at http://VirtualMeetingStartup.com.

Meri Aaron Walker’s Specialties:

Business communication, relationship development, business management, online learning, online meeting management, instructional design for adult learning, Web 2.0 strategies for small business and education, application of natural strengths metrics to individual and team performance improvement.
