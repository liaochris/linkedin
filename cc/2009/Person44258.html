Jason Webb’s Summary

One year Human Resource Management (Business Administration) to enhance team leadership and project management skills. Graduated 'The Academy of Design' with the top average in my section with a solid base in many of the industry standard software packages. Enrolled at 'George Brown College' for Graphic Design to gain a better understanding of the principles and elements of design. The natural evolution of my career led me to 'Seneca College' for Print Pre-press Digital Workflow to give me a rounded skill set in the digital field. I have been actively engaged in new media for the past 6 years and have also graduated with honors at Seneca College for 2D Effects Compositing for Film and Broadcast. Worked for three years at Cuppa Coffee Animation, Video Compositing Department, on the hit Disney kids’ TV program, Jo-Jo's Circus. Wrapped up three productions (Superman Returns, Harry Potter & The Order of the Pheonix, and Beowulf) at IMAX as Lead Rotoscope & Keyer Supervisor for a team of 25. Performed as Visual Effects Supervisor with PLPMedia in Toronto on superheroes online TV series called Team Epic. Recently finished Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince, once again as Lead Rotoscope & Keyer Supervisor at IMAX on a team of 25. Teaching experience also includes Motion Graphics at Ontario College of Art & Design and Web Design at Georgian College in Owen Sound.

Jason Webb’s Specialties:

Visual Effects, Compositing & Editing, CMS Web Deisgn for B2B & B2C.
