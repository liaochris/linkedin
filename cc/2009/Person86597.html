Roy Rumaner’s Summary

Over twenty five years experience in software development. For the past 17 years I have specialized in Lotus Notes Development and Administration. I have worked in all versions of Lotus Notes from R2 through R8.5.1. I am also a Certified Lotus Notes Professional (CLP) and an IBM Business Partner.

I have worked for numerous companies around the country, both on-site and remotely on either a full time basis or through consulting arrangements.

Roy Rumaner’s Specialties:

Experienced working for a variety of organizations including, but not limited to, home building, health care, insurance, automotive parts manufacturing, packaging and shipping, defense contractors, printing, electronics manufacturing, banking and consulting.
