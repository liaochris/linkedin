James Ellis’s Summary

Geek-turned-manager, a web warrior with an MBA, who lives in the space between ROI and RSS, databases and design. As both a manager and strategist, I see the big picture and the details, using iterative advancements and bold vision to achieve transformational goals: increasing traffic at one site by 500% in three years or increasing engagement by 20% in one year at another. I am always looking for new challenges, especially in Chicago.

James Ellis’s Specialties:

Strategic Planning, Project Management, Web Marketing and Promotion, Brand Development and Repositioning, Web Analytics, Social Networking/Emergent Web Technology, Vendor Relationship Management, Staff Development and Motivation, Presentations and Training, Information and Workflow Design and Metaphor Creation.
