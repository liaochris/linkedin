Stephen X. Arthur’s Summary

Thirty years experience. See a wide range of writing samples at www.sxa-portfolio.com.

Note: Script writing services draw on ten years of experience authoring six feature screenplays, eight years directing independent short films, and four years as a professional video writer. Taught screenwriting at the Institute for Communication Arts. Three options to produce were sold to film producers in Los Angeles. Award-winning short films have screened in numerous film festivals worldwide over 36 years. Nominated for Best Director, Animated Production, at the BC Film and Television Industry Leo Awards, 2000, for "Transfigured" (NFB production).

Revisable manuals, science writing, life science, cognitive science, geology, medical devices, biomedical imaging, microscopy, engineering for bulk handling, mining, electrical power & energy, health care, health informatics, bioinformatics, patents, scientific software, narration, animation & VFX, CGI, sound design, audio engineering, digital photography, screenplay format and story editing.
