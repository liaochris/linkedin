Robert Corcoran’s Summary

I am founder of LAMBDA TD Software, Inc. and co-inventor of our flagship product- the LCE Rosebud Automation System. We have the first patented, web-based Smart Home Automation and universal remote control system harnessed into a single user interface device. The LAMBDA LCE Rosebud is a Master Command Center for your personal universe. Our systems are sold through construction developers, residential homebuilders, Home Owner Associations (HOAs), and eventually through home improvement superstores such as Home Depot and Lowe's. Our customers are the actual property and home owners. We are the first Smart Home System that can connect a private dwelling into a community and then to the rest of the world. Our LAMBDA shield logo represents a symbol of unity for the private citizen as we move forward towards a GREENER and ever-changing universe.
