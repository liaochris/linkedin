Brian Lewandowski’s Summary

Currently I am seeking work that utilizes my skills as a 20 Year Professional in Creative Development, Design, Broadcast, Digital Media & Writing.

In the role of a Senior Interactive Producer and Designer, I have completed prior work requiring knowledge of budgets, technical skills, design work and more for major corporations and design groups (NASCAR RacePoints, TripAdvisor, Genuity, GM, Nextel, Mullen, MCI-Worldcom). I am well-versed in audio, graphics, video production, writing and design. I have overseen Creative Direction on those projects (and additional ones) and advised staffs of up to 15 - 30 people.

I was also the Project Manager for all of the Southeast and Caribbean for Bay Networks (Nortel). I've written technical documentation, marketing documentation and more. I spent years in television production with the likes of AMC, IFC, Bravo and ESPN.


I have performed as a freelance Writer and Editor for the likes of Mullen, OLN and others, many elements and past experiences that make up my background are being used to drive home those two key elements.

In addition to my daily musings at bglewandowski.wordpress.com, I have served as a Neighborhood Editor for the Not For Tourists Guide to Boston and just released my first book (Slop and Swill from a Festering Mind ISBN: 1-4116-0357-5). This daily humor site and related interview site (www.fiveforthefamous.com) receive multitudes of visitors.
