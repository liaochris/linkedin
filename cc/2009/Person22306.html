Daniel N Erasmus <<daniel@dnerasmus.com>>’s Summary

Daniel handles assignments for multi-national corporations world-wide in assisting them overcome any tax controversies, starting at the commencement of a Tax Authority audit. He and his tax S.W.A.T team have completed assignments in Budapest, Hungary, and Bucharest, Romania, with the next assignment set for Italy. He and his team have other engagements pending in South Africa, and 9 other countries in Europe - including Italy, Russia, Ukraine, France, UK, Netherlands, Germany and Switzerland.

The principles in his book 7 Habitual Tax Mistakes are being followed - and work!

See www.taxriskmanagement.com for more information

He also involves himself in restructuring businesses internationally to improve profitability AND advising clients on national and international tax planning and tax controversy issues, into Africa from Europe, USA and Asia, and from Africa to Europe, USA and Asia. Focus on an overall strategy and implementation of a plan of action to minimize tax risk, including any form of tax exposure in a plan or a controversy.
Web site www.dnerasmus.com and published textbook by Lexis Nexis on the '7 Habitual Tax Mistakes' gives examples of work and case studies.
GOOGLE '7 Habitual Tax Mistakes'.
Email daniel@dnerasmus.com for more information.
