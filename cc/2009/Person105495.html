Xavier Burguera Dominguez’s Summary

Bachelors degree in Computer Science, CISA, CISM, CISSP, MBCI, CHFI, TOGAF 8, ISO/IEC 27001 Lead Auditor, ITIL v3 Foundation Certificate.

14 years experience in consultancy and information systems development, 11 of which were spent in consultancy and information security projects.

I began my career in MAPS Informática Industrial participating in the development of an industrial inspection system by means of Computer Vision. I then worked at The Catalan Institute of Technology designing systems based on Neuronal Networks and Data Mining. I had the position of technical director at Xpert. Later I worked as a security consultant and PKI technical manager at DaVinci Consulting Tecnológico and ADD Distribuciones Informáticas respectively.

For more than 7 years, I have further developed my career working at T-Systems Iberia, a company in which I have been responsible for different consultancy and system integration projects.

Nowadays I reconcile my professional activity at T-Systems with Vice Dean External Services cargo at Official College of Computer Science Engineers of Catalonia (COEIC).
