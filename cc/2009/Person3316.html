Alex Dakoulas’s Summary

I have a strong interest in design in relation to youth culture, film, fashion. Design can be a form of expression, and that is what I find myself drawn to.

I also have a growing emphasis on the ideas behind projects, especially as as starting point for inspiration, and a way to communicate a message.

I am very driven on personal projects, and I try to have that flow into my professional work as well. I enjoy creating things for people to see, and relate to, and hope I can continue with that.
