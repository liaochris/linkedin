jamie tallerico’s Summary

An outgoing, motivated, creative guy who is up for any new challenge. I have an understanding of very many different fields of design and pride myself on that. I work well in group environments and love getting people excited about the tasks at hand. I want to keep growing and learning as designer. Because you can always learn and never know too much.

jamie tallerico’s Specialties:

I have my own screen-printing set up at home. I can design a woven shirt, fleece, jackets and pants. I can do the tech pack that goes with it. I can design a website and do most of the coding (All HTML-little PHP-little Flash). I am good with color. Can incorporate many applique and embroidery techniques into garments. I love to illustrate type. I love pen and ink. I am good with branding, packaging and painting.
Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, Quark. Dreamweaver, Flash, Fireworks.
