Cynthia Mallard’s Summary

Cynthia has worked in the marketing communications industry for over 20 years. Her background includes owning and working in a family print shop, art director at a corporate healthcare communications company, is an award-winning artist and product creator. She is sole-proprietor of a graphic design studio www.cynergiestudio.com, which provides design and illustration services, and co-owns www.prismatek.com, a company that provides software and services for the printing industry. Her talents include print and UI design, marketing, client services, SaaS (software as a service) application development, and web 2.0 design and management. Cynthia enjoys incorporating new and progressive ideas that will enhance and promote the goals and objectives of her customers.
