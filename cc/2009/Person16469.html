Celia R. Dyer, D.M.D.’s Summary

Founder and Executive Producer at TechDrawl.com, a video blog covering technology startups and emerging companies fresh from the South. I produce video content for internet broadcasts including scripting, filming, and post-production and write news stories on technology events and entrepreneurs for TechDrawl.

A founder at StartupChicks.net, an organization founded by technology entrepreneur Jennifer Bonnett of eTour.com fame whose mission is networking and mentoring women technology entrepreneurs.

Licensed general dentist by profession, formerly specializing in general and cosmetic restorative procedures including dental implant services. Owned Tuxedo Dental, P.C. in the Buckhead community of Atlanta, GA.
