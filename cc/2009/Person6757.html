Ann Grove’s Summary

I can be reached at 717.927.6168 for a free consultation, or you can follow me on Twitter: @AnnGrove.

As president of Logical Writing Solutions, Inc., I serve as the lead consultant for a technical and business communication firm. Often, Logical is hired to write materials for a new product or a new release or version of an existing product. We write guides or manuals, or develop eLearning or instructor-led training solutions. Founded in 2000, LWS also can help you prepare for SOX audits, spruce up that tired web page, or launch your next product. I often serve as lead consultant.

I have many volunteer passions. I serve as president of the midstate Pennsylvania chapter of the Society for Technical Communication. Also, in the last year, I've been helping job hunters on an informal basis to understand how to develop a personal brand, an effort that earned me a nomination as 2009 Technology Educator of the Year for the Technology Council of Central Pennsylvania.
