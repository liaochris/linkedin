Ronald Garner’s Summary

Ron Garner has over 11 years experience in Investor Relations, assisting small companies with corporate communications and finance. His journey into the field began at a venture capital company were he was quickly placed in charge of sales training. When another VC acquired that firm he was tapped for voice over work for the company, which considered his melodious delivery "an important asset". Later, one of his sales trainees recommended Mr. Garner for a position in Investor relations. A natural in public relations, he became a client favorite for both his way with investors as well as his writing.

Today, via multi-media, Mr. Garner serves as financial news reporter, press release writer, publisher, producer, interviewer -- "the voice of AudioStocks.com"

Ronald Garner’s Specialties:

Director of Communications for multi-media communications specialist, AudioStocks.com, Ron Garner is an expert in the fields of Marketing Communications, Investor Relations and Corporate Communications.
