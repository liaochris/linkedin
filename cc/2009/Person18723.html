Christopher Parr’s Summary

Thinks big picture and consistency across marketing channels; Internet, catalog, retail, TV and print. Focuses on the customer experience, comprehensive brand strategy, CRM, driving retail and web traffic -- and ROI. Innovator in the social media marketing space, with blogs, Facebook, Twitter, YouTube, social networking and communities.

After working in the TV, film and theatre industry, Christopher Parr founded Parr Interactive in 1995. As Marketing & Creative Director of the award-winning interactive, branding and e-marketing agency, he developed marketing strategies for many clients. His projects have been featured in numerous publications, including USA Today, Wired, Ad Age and Entertainment Weekly, and case studies featured in Clear Blogging from Apress, Web Sites That Work from Adobe Press and HTML Unleashed from Macmillan Publishing.

He was also a VP, Marketing/Creative Director for a technology and entertainment e-commerce company, working with Warner Bros. Pub., BMG, AOL, leading the marketing team, branding and PR. Christopher also financed and co-owned an internet e-commerce startup, featuring luxury home & garden goods.

Currently, Christopher manages global brand communications, brand ID and guidelines, CRM, interactive, media and planning for Sub-Zero and Wolf, maker of luxury kitchen appliances. From product launches to e-commerce, he also manages B2C, B2B touch points and all things internet; social media marketing, interactive, promotions, media, video, branding, e-newsletters, blogging & podcasts -- and developing campaigns with HGTV, New York Times, Food Network, Oprah and Epicurious. For Sub-Zero, Christopher recently launched an integrated campaign for Sub-Zero Wine Storage, featuring print ads, brochures, e-newsletters, online banners and a new wine blog at subzerowine.com, including wine events at Four Seasons, the Ritz-Carlton, with magazine support from Conde Nast Traveler, Wine Spectator and Food & Wine.
