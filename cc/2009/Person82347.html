Ray Trygstad’s Summary

As Director of Information Technology of the IIT Center for Professional Development, I am responsible for managing staff and assets including all computer laboratories, faculty and staff computer assets, UNIX/Linux/Windows/Solaris servers, networks, and multiple Web sites on two university campuses.
As Associate Director for IIT Information Technology and Management Degree Programs, designed and manage curriculum for Information Technology & Management undergraduate and graduate degrees; advise undergraduate and graduate students enrolled in the programs. Design, write and manage curriculum and courses in Web Development, Information Security Management, Operating System Virtualization for IIT Center for Professional Development. Evaluate and hire instructors for courses. Wrote and taught Web design courses for Stuart Graduate School of Business. Member, Illinois Institute of Technology Undergraduate Studies Committee. Served as a member of the Association of Internet Professionals Certification Accreditation Council.
As Manager of Client Services for university Computing and Network Services, I was responsible for managing staff and assets including all computer laboratories, Novell networks, Helpdesk services, and the university Web site.
Subject Matter Expert for CompTIA iNet+ certification.
Served as a charter member of the Council of the World Wide Web Virtual Library.
