Ben Ang’s Summary

Formally in the Singapore Police Force for the past 5 years due to national service. Ben began his career as a amateur private trader in 2004. He specialized in derivatives trading mainly in stock options initially and has since traded many other instruments such as equities, spot forex over the past 3 years.

Ben discovered his passion in the stock market and seeks to continue to improve his trading skills. He has attended numerous investment and trading seminars, read lots of books on trading and investing. During the process, he also founded theinvestorportal.com and benakm.com where he shares his knowledge with an ever growing audience from around the world. He is an accredited platinum writer for ezinearticles.com and approved marketplace writer for helium.com.

Ben recently left his former job in January 2009 to focus on developing his business full-time. He recently joined Matthew James International and became the operations manager while still continuing to build up his business. Life has became more fulfilling and meaningful since then. Ben is determined and passionate, and driven to pursue his goals in life.
