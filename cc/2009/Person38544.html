Hal Moore’s Summary

Bolton Consulting specializes in placing Senior level finance/accounting professionals into interim and contract positions. We place CFO's, Controllers, Finance Managers, Consultants, and Directors on short and long term contracts.

The Bolton Group was founded in 1997 as a specialty niche search firm, focusing solely in the areas of accounting and finance. While our focus has remained solidy grounded in uncovering elite accounting and finance professionals, we have grown aggressively to serve the entire Southeastern region of the United States in other areas, such as IT, insurance, and banking.

Over the last five years, The Bolton Group has opened 12 new offices in addition to Atlanta Corporate including Baltimore, Charlotte, Delaware Valley, Jacksonville, Miami, Northern Virginia, Orlando, Raleigh-Durham, Richmond, Tampa, Washington D.C., and Winston-Salem.

I am an Executive Recruiter covering the Unted States, dealing specifically with Accounting, Finance, Audit, Tax, and IT professionals. I specialize in assisting Big 4 and public accounting professionals as they migrate into new career opportunities.

I also work with IT professionals dealing with software development, database design and administration, ERP Implementations, and networking.
