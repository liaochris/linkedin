Tom Tuohy’s Summary

I have been a practicing attorney for 25 years and represented over 14,000 individuals while providing representation and educational seminars for the employees of the City of Chicago, Chicago Police Department, Secretary of State of Illinois, Chicago Transit Authority, Metra, and members of International Brotherhood of Electrical Workers,US Steelworkers and Fraternal Order of Police.

In 1989 I founded the non- profit international children’s organization, Dreams for Kids. Creating social change through youth leadership programs and sports and activities program for children with disabilities, the organization brings youth together to serve the local and world communities.

In 2007, I chose to assume full time resonsibilites with Dreams for Kids in order to direct its global expansion and recruit an army of goodwill ambassadors who bring leadership and change to the world.

    Our programs include:

    Holiday for Hope: The largest event in the world for homeless and underprivileged children.
