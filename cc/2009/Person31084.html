Eric Egler’s Summary

I direct NER’s engineering and software group. NER, Inc. has three core areas of focus: Engineering and Software; Automation and Controls; and Materials Test and Inspection. I originally joined NER in 2002 and was head of sales and recruiting. I have a unique background with education and experience in both electronics technology and business (sales, business development, management). My expertise comes from 17 years of working with industries such as aerospace/defense, industrial equipment, test equipment, medical technology and telecommunications. I started my career in the US Air Force and then graduated Magna Cum Laude with a Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration from Cardinal Stritch University.

I work with the engineering management of high-tech product manufacturers across the US to provide engineering resources for contract or project needs and direct hire. I thoroughly understand the electrical, mechanical, and software product development process and the organization of engineering project teams. That means my customers receive quality engineers with the right skills and background to make an immediate impact. Visit http://www.nerinc.com for more information.
