Gareth Davies’s Summary

Gareth is a Director in consulting, focussing on the Oil and Gas industry in the Gulf. Currently living and working in Kuwait.

Prior to this Gareth worked for Deloitte UK, before which he founded and ran two companies focusing on Salesforce.com software technology, implementation and marketing strategy. He has an MBA from London Business School prior to which held various senior sales, marketing, operations and technology development positions in Schlumberger, while the company was charged with providing web solutions, portal implementations and outsourced data mangement services for oil exploration companies.

His work in the UK, Norway, Azerbaijan and Indonesia enabled him to experience negotiation in multi-cultural settings, the science of major account management and the art of deal making.
