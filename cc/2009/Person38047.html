Guilherme Jaccoud’s Summary

Guilherme is a 29 years old graphic designer and music producer from São Paulo, Brazil.

As a designer, he has designed a variety of interactive experiences over the past 10 years for major brands in Brazil. He worked at international agencies such as Wunderman, Rapp Collins and Euro RSCG.

As a musician, he has been involved with diferents music styles, from House to Trance and from Techno to Minimal, and released music under the name of diferents artists, such SYNTHATiX, OVERLOAD and MULTIPLY. His music has been released in compilations in several countries such Brazil, Portugal, Germany, Sweden and Israel.

Today Guilherme teaches AUDIO POST and MUSIC CREATION at the UME Institute of Electronic Music. He is also working on a new music project called THE COLOUR METHOD, a sophisticated blend of minimalist music, deep beats and colorful melodies... releases are coming soon!

Guilherme Jaccoud’s Specialties:

Art Direction, Graphic Design, Vectors, Typography, Web Design, Interactive Video, Online Communities, Semantic Web, Music Production, Music Arrangement and Music Mastering.
