Katie Carter’s Summary

The skill set that I draw from is as wide-ranging as my clientele. I work with several different market segments: venture-backed tech and life science clients, equity fund service firms, pre-revenue and publicly-traded companies, national automobile dealerships, as well as a middle market group.

I specialize in consulting on a company's treasury operations, analyzing current account and fee structures, suggest services that will make a difference in daily efficiency and ease, and commit to the ongoing relationship-building practices that come along with forging a long-term alliance.

I have a tenacious drive to ensure the satisfaction of our customers and I take great pride in the fact that I work for a bank that actually cares about clients no matter how big or how small.
