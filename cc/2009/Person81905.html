Randall Caudle’s Summary

Immigration attorney for individuals, families and businesses.

I represent companies in corporate immigration compliance and in securing visas and U.S. Legal Permanent Residence for their employees.

I also represent couples in foreign national marriage to U.S. Citizen cases and fiance(e) visas.

I represent athletes, musicians, computer and internet professionals, architects, teachers, professors, researchers, nurses, social workers, attorneys, graphic artists, small businesses, mid-size businesses, large businesses, and families and individuals from around the world.

I represent foreign nationals in removal/deportation proceedings in immigration court, before the Board of Immigration Appeals (BIA) and before Federal Circuit Courts of Appeal.

I am an expert in I-601 waivers of inadmissibility and in foreign nurse immigration issues.

I have prior experience as the in-house immigration attorney for a Silicon Valley high-tech company.
