Steven Hall’s Summary

My passions, both in creative and opera, revolve around people, communication and relationships.

With the advent of emerging social media technologies like Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, and FriendFeed, I see the avenues of disconnected communication slowly regaining the lost element of *relating* to one another in the process, driving me to a growing deeper knowledge and skill set for the industry. I routinely work in all areas of the Adobe Creative Suite with print, web, audio & video.

I am a results-driven creative currently affiliated with the dallas summer musicals, majestic theatre, The Uptown Church, Booker T. Washington High Advisory Board and others, caring for print, web, creative and consulting needs. I'm equally comfortable in front of an audience teaching / performing, and in front my computer designing.

I'm also a member of The Dallas Opera company working as both a soloist and chorister; and an elected union Governor through 2011 with A.G.M.A.

Steven Hall’s Specialties:

Operatically, I am in Spinto Tenor fach. Vocationally, I specialize in creative, web & branding as well as consulting in emerging social media technologies.
