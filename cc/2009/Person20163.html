CommercialLeads .net’s Summary

Commercialleads.net (http://www.commercialleads.net ) provides lead generation, marketing and advertising tools for the Commercial Mortgage and Real Estate industry.

Commercial Mortgage Re-Finance and Commercial Loan Modification requests are high due to a few key elements. 400 billion in loans coming due, A slow market, limited financing options, conduits virtually shut down and Low rates. The timing couldn't be better for brokers and lenders who have sources of funds for Income Producing Property, Commercial Loan Modification and Distressed Property deals.

CommercialLeads.net (http://www.commercialleads.net ) offers both commercial refinance / purchase opportunities as well as commercial loan modification requests to industry professionals on an exclusive basis.

Through CommercialLeads.net's (http://www.commercialleads.net ) Commercial Mortgage Leads program you can obtain quality deals of all types referred from Commercial Realtors, Title Reps, Appraisers and 16 other segments on behalf of current or past clients who they are trying to help in these difficult times.
