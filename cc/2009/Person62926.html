Marion Conway’s Summary

Marion Conway is an independent consultant working exclusively in the nonprofit sector. She has 30 years of corporate experience working for a Fortune 100 company and she has been consulting in the nonprofit sector for the last six years. Marion is a popular workshop presenter and board retreat facilitator. Marion's areas of expertise include:

-Strategic Planning

-Leadership and Board Development

-Understanding Technology for Nonprofits

-Facilitation

-Keynote Speaker

Whether working on a major strategic planning project, facilitating a board retreat, or providing ongoing executive coaching, Marion offers personalized service tailored to your needs and budget.

If you would like to discuss a project with Marion, or get more information about her consulting services visit her website, her blog or send an email message.
