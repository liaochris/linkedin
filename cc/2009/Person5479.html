Andrew Kerr’s Summary

Delivering the Citrix Delivery Center vision to customers moving away from their current static Data Center. The Citrix Delivery Center optimizes and deliveries Desktops and Applications from the core of the Citrix Delivery Center to users wherever, whenever and to any device (PC,Thin Client or Mobile Device). The core of the Citrix Delivery Center is a fluid and dynamic virtual/physical server environment powered by Citrix's XenServer. With the key components of the CDC being; Citrix's XenApp for Windows based applications, Citrix's XenDesktop for the delivery of desktops and Citrix's NetScaler appliances for Web application delivery.

The Citrix Delivery Center core value is; simplification, centralization and optimization of IT organizations.
