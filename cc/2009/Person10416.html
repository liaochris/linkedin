Ben Lavender’s Summary

Visionary thinker. Inventor of BBC iPlayer. Great evangelist, motivator and presenter of new ideas. Industry recognised expert in pan-platform on-demand secure media distribution. Results driven product manager with proven track record, over 10 years, of leading complex technical projects over aggressive timescales, managing tight and £2M+ budgets for BBC, Five, MTV & Walt Disney.

Product Development. Media distribution. DRM. Internet Geekness.
