Oscar Corral’s Summary

I started my career as a print reporter, and have now become accustomed to shooting, editing and producing radio and video content for our website, and our partner radio station, WLRN.

In October of 2008, I left the Herald to launch Explica, a multimedia production and consulting company. We want to help companies and startups harness the explosive growth and interest in video.
