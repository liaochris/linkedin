Daryl Auclair’s Summary

Most recently I have been studying Computer Science at the University of California San Diego. I have performed a case study analysis of approaches to Object-Oriented Design in C, C++, and Java to recommend which approaches are best suited to solve programming problems. Seven projects were analyzed which implemented a stack-based calculator that has evolved to utilize binary trees, circular linked lists, and hash tables. The efficiency of each data structure was analyzed after each project iteration.

I have over 10 years experience as a web and graphic designer. I started as a self-taught HTML novice and have progressed over the years to become a successful developer. I specialize in the areas of online business creation and the deployment of Rich Internet Applications.

As a well rounded developer, I have gained extensive experience in User Interface Design, Search Engine Optimization and online marketing. I hope to expand my knowledge and skills in business and become a successful entrepreneur.
