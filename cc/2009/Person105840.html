Yermo Lamers’s Summary

I am a serial entrepreneur and senior software developer/architect who tends to take on roles that bridge the gap between technology and business.

I've founded three companies and have been involved in various capacities in launching a number of others.

On the technical side, I have over 20 years of experience designing and developing complex software systems across multiple platforms in a number of languages. These systems have ranged in complexity from simple web applications all the way up to distributed military command and control systems.

At DTLink Software, I am currently developing http://collabInvest.com, an instant social investing community which combines Windows desktop investing software, instant messaging/XMPP, and real-time collaboration integrated with a Web 2.0 social networking site.

At Nuts and Bolts Interactive, I am currently developing http://formvista.com, an internet business suite and platform that tightly integrates a custom developed CRM backend, a website goal analytics and reporting system, and a WYSIWYG browser based preview/publish content management front end all to automate portions of the sales funnel and other business processes.

I am looking for networking opportunities to develop complementary relationships for both businesses.
