Jacquelyn Meadows’s Summary

A medical practice is a business plain and simple! Moreover, businesses can only flourish when the foundation is built on proven business practices, solid and consistent procedures, shared information, and accountability. All aspects of financial and patient data are vital in the strategic planning of any business. It is impossible to manage what you cannot measure. Like any business, you must have clear assessable and well-defined information to support life-changing and profitable business decisions.

Successful medical practices must in-touch with the increasing trend to process work and communicate via electronic channels as the healthcare system is rapidly moving toward the evolution of using only electronic methods. Also, it is vital to stay on top of the ever-changing and ever forward moving technological opportunities that is offered and will continue to be offered electronically throughout the entire healthcare system. The 21st Century will see a surge in systematic techniques of electronic formats, which is driven by two main factors:
1. Potential cost savings
2. Having access to real-time information
