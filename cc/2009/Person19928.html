Court Peterson’s Summary

Our Inland Northwest Real Estate Team goes the extra mile for the Real Estate Home Buyer and Seller. Your property is unique to you and therefore to us. From bare land to houses in town to the mansion overlooking Lake Coeur d'Alene, all clients needs and wants are important and we take them into full consideration to help make your Real Estate Transaction as pleasant as possible.

When you are thinking about a move - Near or Far, remember to use a Realtor that not only knows the area you are moving to AND loves the process of helping you to find your new home. Court and Michele not only know our Coeur d'Alene and Spokane area. We also live to help people find their new homes. We know our neighborhoods and really enjoy showing people the ins and outs of each and every one of them. Contact Court or Michele for up to date information.

When you expect results, we are your Real Estate Team!
