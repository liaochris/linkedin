Andrew Mayoras’s Summary

I am an attorney specializing in probate litigation, with a secondary emphasis on commercial litigation, business planning and some real estate and personal injury.

With my wife and co-author, I've recently written a book called Trial & Heirs: Famous Fortune Fights! to help people learn from celebrity errors to protect their heirs. It's scheduled release date is November 15, 2009.

I maintain an active blog called The Probate Lawyer Blog, through which I write about famous fortune fights. You can visit my blog at www.probatelawyerblog.com.

I have authorized several articles to provide an overview of the various legal issues arising in probate litigation cases, including challenging Durable Power of Attorneys, Guardianship Disputes, Theft and Exploitation of Assets, and Challenges Involving Joint Assets, Wills and Trusts. You can read them here: http://www.probatelitigationmi.com/CM/Custom/What-Happens-When-The-Family-Cant-Get-Along.asp

I help families with difficult cases involving wills, trusts, estate, guardianships, conservatorships, assets disputes, joint accounts, insurance issues, annuity problems, elder abuse, exploitation, undue influence, beneficiary rights, fiduciary abuses, trust and estate administration, and all other types of probate issues and legal problems relating to seniors and their families.

I am an active twitter user; you can view my twitter profile at http://twitter.com/probateblogger.

Andrew Mayoras’s Specialties:

writing about true celebrity probate and estate stories, and practicing law in the areas of: probate litigation, commercial litigation, real estate, business planning, personal injury, guardianships and conservatorships, will and trust contests, decedent estates, assets disputes, family court fights
