Paul Krause’s Summary

Multi-disciplinary art director (print, online, identity, logo design, and some more stuff besides) for Mayo Clinic. Using a broad range of design skills I direct and design a variety of projects such as brochures, book covers, books, annual reports, logos, corporate identity, advertising and marketing materials, catalogs, displays, booths, interactive demos and web sites.
