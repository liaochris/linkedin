Rosario Piazzese’s Summary

Rosario has begun his consulting experience in ICT consulting firms in 1994, with Microsoft Certified Partners.
He joined IDC in 2001 as Manager in charge of the Management & Technology Practice of IDC EMEA Consulting.
He joined Key Partners in 2004 as Manager. In 2006, after the acquisition, Rosario entered in the Deloitte network as Senior Manager.
He's now Associate Partner in The Innovation Group, working on strategic topics of IT directly related with business needs, supporting CEO and CIO.
He took place to ICT Governance, Business Process Reengineering and analysis and organisational design projects; he is now involved as manager in project dealing with definition of ICT Governance function’s role in banking and financial industry.
