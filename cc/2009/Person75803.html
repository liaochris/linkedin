David Couper’s Summary

Training Development Consultant and Career Coach with twenty years experience.

David Couper has twenty years experience developing peoples’ talents and coaching executives and managers in transition. His career successes range from coaching managers in Tokyo to supporting producers in Hollywood.

As a training leader working on training, organizational development, and change management projects, he was responsible for teams of up to 25 people and has been accountable for budgets of up to $1.5M. He has designed for virtual classrooms, online training, satellite television, video, and for blended solutions. He has also been awarded two awards for training projects. David is a certified master trainer in Leadership.

He is a joint US/UK citizen and has lived in Asia and Europe.

David Couper’s Specialties:

career development, career transition, coaching, leadership, team-building, change management, diversity, culture shifts, conflict resolution, communication.
