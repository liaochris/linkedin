Ramon Kolb’s Summary

A telecommunications professional with many years of experience driving value-added products, solutions, and deliveries to mobile handset manufacturers and mobile operators. Proven track record in relationship management, product negotiation, and deliverable management that resulted in realization of multi-million dollars of revenue and sales/uptake of new products by new and existing customers.

Ample experience in the telecommunications industry, both on the network side (large footprint, operator premises based products) and on the device side (small footprint, embedded products).

Skills include:
- driving design wins with mobile handset manufacturers and carriers
- development of product strategy and positioning
- business development
- roadmap development
- program management
- product delivery management for extremely complex products with multiple interdependent partnerships
- partnership management for joint deliveries to major customers
- technical sales and marketing to major and minor international network operators
- UI, UE, and telephony (voice) interface design for handset and network based products
- product/program management for large scale (multi-million-$) projects for demanding customers
- technically savvy in mobile and landline network technology and topology
- deep technical understanding of low footprint (mobile device) requirements
- able to define and implement technical solutions for business problems
- multi lingual and internationally oriented: English, Spanish, Dutch, German, French

Citizen of The Netherlands. US Permanent Resident. Based near Boston, MA.
