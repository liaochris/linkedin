Jerry Lore’s Summary

Technology Evangelist with an extensive background in all aspects of the computer networks, social media ie. facebook, twitter, myspace, Web 2.0. Worked a good deal on management consulting, E-commerce solutions, and information management for various industries. Currently developing marketing & business opportunities that are strategic and capable of working pro actively with multiple teams to achieve results.

Responsible for driving management & turnkey solutions to clients and offer services such as helping Executive Directors make informed technology decisions and assist in implementation and support for all solutions that my companies create and recommend.

They can range from VoIP, server migration, multi-platform architecture, (CMS) content management system, DB management, web 2.0, SEO/SEM, E-commerce solutions, non-traditional/guerrilla marketing campaigns, branding, web design and development from inception to completion.

Proven leadership, communication, negotiation, and analytical skills. Decisive, solutions-focused and results-oriented, with an emphasis on maximizing operating efficiencies and growing revenue and margins, for both start-ups and established enterprises.

Have taught at a few colleges/universities in NY/NJ area. Currently teaching at the Fashion Institute of Technology in the Advertising and Marketing Communications department.

Working currently for the City of Hoboken responsible for launching the new website: http://www.hobokennj.org and implemented Word Press for the backend.

High level communication and presentation skills.

Broad-based experience in all facets of Urban Street Design, especially in Street Culture and advertising and marketing.
