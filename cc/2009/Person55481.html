Stephanie Kifowit’s Summary

Governmental Experience: Stephanie Kifowit has been Alderman of the Third Ward for 5 years. She was first elected in 2003; and recently was re-elected by 72% of the vote in 2007. She is a member of the American Society of Public Administrators, Illinois Municipal League, and the Illinois City/County Management Association.

Financial Experience: Stephanie has worked for six years in the banking and financial industry. She managed portfolios for clients as a registered financial advisor for Morgan Stanley, and as a private banking associate for Bank One (now JP Morgan).

Formal Education: Stephanie is an alumna of Northern Illinois University. She received her undergraduate degree in Political Science and her Masters Degree in Public Administration with an emphasis in Governmental Finance and Strategic Planning.

Military Service: Proudly serving her country in the United States Marine Corps on active duty for four years, Stephanie Kifowit was stationed at Camp Pendleton, CA and Okinawa, Japan. She is a life-long member of the Women Marine Association, and the Marine Corps League. In addition, she is a member of the American Legion.

Stephanie Kifowit and her husband Steve have two children, Sabrina age 10 and Samuel age 6. The family is made complete with Steve's daughter Alexandria and her son William age 2.

While Steve is a life-long resident and graduate of East Aurora High School, the family formally made their home in Aurora 12 years ago. Their children attend public schools in District 131.

Stephanie Kifowit is in her third year as a Substitute Teacher in East Aurora School District 131, in addition to being a Girl Scout Troop leader for over 3 years.

Stephanie Kifowit’s Specialties:

Combining a distinguished career in the financial industry with decorated military service and experience in local government, Stephanie Kifowit offers a unique blend of a commitment to public service coupled with strong leadership and an education tailor-made for the challenges of the City of Aurora.
