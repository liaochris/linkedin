Elisa Haake’s Summary

WHY USE LANDAJOB?
Here are some of the reasons we (and so many of our clients) feel we are the best choice for your staffing needs:

Integrity: As recruiters, our reputation is everything. We adhere to strict guidelines regarding confidentiality of all the information shared with us by the hiring companies and our candidates. We place the needs of our clients and our candidates above the needs of our company and hold all our employees to the highest business ethical standards.

Value: Hiring is an expensive proposition. The hours spent in advertising, resume review, interviewing and making offers to potential new hires are hours that could be better spent running (and growing) your business. Using our proprietary database of more than 3,000 candidates, our network of industry pros and candidate referrals to weed out the chaff and deliver pre-screened, pre-qualified candidates targeted specifically to your requirements and your budget.

Flexibility: Imagine being able to properly staff for all the peaks and valleys of your work cycles—having enough staff to get through the busy stages, or covering vacations, sick days, or leaves. Imagine having the flexibility to add or reduce staff without the costs and hassles of taxes, 1099s, wage and hour audits, unemployment rate increases and the negative impact on employee morale when staff reductions have to be made. Imagine Landajob. One phone call and help is on the way. Think of it as just-in-time staffing or, hassle-free hiring.

Compliance: Don’t know the federal regulations for temporary employees? Don’t worry. We do, and we follow all federal, state and local regulations to protect both our clients and candidates. We handle all federal, state and local payroll tax withholdings, pay all employment taxes, and maintain workers’ compensation and general liability policies for all our temporary associates.
