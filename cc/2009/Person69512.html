Michael Kleinheinz’s Summary

I am currently the President and CEO of United Brandworks, LLC., charged with developing a global network of brand engineers, designers of all stripes, and consultants in all branding disciplines. Prior to founding United Brandworks, I worked in a variety of management and individual contributor positions at companies including J3 Motorsport, PZ Race Components, Peter Skop Industries, EPX Carbon Performance Bicycles and Maxxis Tires/Cheng Shin Rubber. I continue to maintain a thriving consulting practice focused on niche sport branding, as well as tech-based management.

I am a 1988 graduate of University of Georgia, earning a Bachelors degree in Marketing Education.

Michael Kleinheinz’s Specialties:

Brand Creation and Management, Sales Management, Advertising/Media, Sponsorship Development, International (10 years experience), Product Development, Team Development
