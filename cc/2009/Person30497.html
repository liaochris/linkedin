Emily Palmer’s Summary

After earning my degree from Cal Poly in Graphic Communication I joined Consolidated Graphics, one of the largest and most technologically advanced printing companies in North America. As an Associate in the Leadership Development Program for CGX I am getting the opportunity for hands-on experience in almost every job in a typical printing plant, from press operator to estimator to management.

My current role has give me expertise in finding complete print marketing solutions to save clients time and money by providing innovative ways to better execute print marketing campaigns and collateral fulfillment. I am responsible for coordinating with the client, agency, and printing company to coordinate marketing projects.

Emily Palmer’s Specialties:

Printer and agency communication, Project Coordination, FSC (Forest Stewardship Council) certification and green printing initiatives, product packaging, CGXSolutions Storefront technology, Lean manufacturing process management, print planning and estimating for web and sheetfed offset printing, basic graphic design skills.
