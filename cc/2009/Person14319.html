Bryan Strawser’s Summary

Security & technology professional with more than fifteen years of front line, field, and corporate leadership experience. Recognized industry leader in Innovation, Security Technology, and Assets Protection (Loss Prevention). I'm fascinated by the intersection between technology, behavior, communication, collaboration, and social media - and how we can capitalize on that strategically.

Currently, I lead the Innovation Team for Target's Assets Protection group. In this role, my team is responsible for the development of creative strategies and new technologies that support Target's teams worldwide.
