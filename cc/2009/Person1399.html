Walter Adamson’s Summary

CEO of NewleaseG2M focused on the delivery end of digital transformation to web-centric marketing and lead generation, in association with Mural Consulting. Specialising in transforming businesses into successful SaaS operations where Mural has conducted over 400 assignments.

NewleaseG2M is also the exclusive partner in Australia for the Social Media Academy.

Walter Adamson is also the founder and principal of Digital Investor (www.digitalinvestor.com.au). DI primarily provides business advice to owners and investors in IT and telco-related service companies with a client base in wireless, mobile, IT and sometimes more generally with larger firms in regard to their IT strategy. DI also has certain holdings and equity entitlements as a result of cash investment and/or earned positions in a number of ventures.

He holds an MS (by thesis) in Comp Sci, has been awarded 6 Australian patents, and attended the Advanced Management Program at the Mt Eliza Business School. Walter was a founder of the IS Auditor’s Association in Australia. He is Advisor to the ICT Strategy Board of the Government of Victoria, Australia.

Community: Awarded “Friend for Life” by Berry Street Refuge and Community Centre, for contributions over a 25-year period.

"We hired Walter to complete a technology audit, but he gave us much more -- he has the ability to identify business opportunities that benefit all sides, creating networks of customers, suppliers, investors and intermediaries. His breadth of knowledge and willingness to push himself beyond the brief are tremendously valuable." Zenon Pasieczny, Director, Saphet Capital Management (www.saphet.com)

Walter Adamson’s Specialties:

Developing Business Models & Customer Value Propositions, especially in relation to IT/telco Industry Trends. Planning & Executing Business Strategy and Partnering. Business Portfolio Redesign. Corporate-IT Alignment. Business Models for SI Firms. Strategic Sourcing Analysis. Reseller-Vendor Partnering Strategies. Identifying & Building International Partnerships. IT Intellectual Property Due Diligence for Investors and Acquirers. Technology Commercialization Process Audits.
