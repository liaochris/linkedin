Jeff Moss’s Summary

Jeff Moss has over sixteen years of business and technical consulting experience focused primarily on Oracle technologies. Jeff specializes in enhancing IS productivity, performance and value through his extensive experience and versatility.

This has been through the best practices implementation of a range of Oracle technologies including the Oracle Database Server; development, analytical, ETL and BI tools and performance management applications.

Jeff has successfully applied a combination of analysis, consulting, technology, design and implementation skills for blue-chip European and US based clients including EOn (Powergen), Millward Brown (WPP), Vodafone, MBNA, Halifax Building Society, Chemical Bank and NCM Credit Insurance.

Jeff Moss’s Specialties:

Data Warehousing, SQL, Oracle technologies and VLDB.
