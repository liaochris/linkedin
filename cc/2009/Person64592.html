Mathias Lin | 林马’s Summary

Mathias Lin, born 1978 in Hamburg/Germany, is founder and managing director of SYSVISION Limited (Hong Kong) as well as Vice President, Products & Technologies at SYSVISION USA, Inc.

Mathias has 11+ years of professional experience in IT and has been consulting clients ranging from startups to Fortune 500 corporations. He is an enthusiastic proponent about technology and its impact on business and productivity.

In 01/2004 he joined SYSVISION GmbH which he served as managing director until 12/2005. The company works for national and international customers such as Lufthansa, Bayer, China Telecom, Unilever, Malteser Hilfsdienst, Benchpark, Dräger, META Group, Philips, Atos Origin. Core business is IT consulting and customized ERP- and ebusiness software solutions.
In 12/2005 he established the Hong Kong branch SYSVISION Limited.

His personal interest lies in healthcare IT and how open source and open knowledge concepts and projects can contribute to the healthcare systems in developing and emerging countries.
