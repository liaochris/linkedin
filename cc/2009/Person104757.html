Bill Thompson’s Summary

Communications professional working primarily in print for the last 25 years. Focused on providing clients with high end collateral services. As a Print Production Manager for a design studio, created digital asset library for international motor sports client. Worked with creative team to coordinate the development of marketing materials, maximizing impact while minimizing costs. Strong budget experience working with multiple vendors, projecting and tracking costs on both a project and annual basis.
As bridge between creative, client and production, responsibilities included everything from account management through copy writing and press okays.
