Gina Callender’s Summary

I joined Con Edison in 2000 as part of the Management Intern Program. I currently work on the Public Service Commission (PSC) Management Audit Team where my functions include analyzing trends, preparing and submitting sensitive communications, communicating trends and status to executives, scheduling interviews conducted by the PSC and an independent consultant for the Company, coordinating and running preparation and debriefing sessions for interviewees and note takers.

Prior to this assignment, I worked in other management positions with increasing responsibility including: Field Operations Planner in Queens Gas Distribution Services where I managed a staff of over 40 employees, both union and management, in scheduled projects and inspections, as well as emergency gas incidents; Engineering Supervisor in Manhattan Energy Services where I interacted with internal and external customers to ensure completion of PSC mandates, and supervised designers responsible for engineering new and existing customer electric loads and services; Energy Analyst in Energy Management, where I developed daily and weekly trading plans and purchased and sold wholesale energy in the commodities market in accordance with those plans; and Chief Construction Inspector in Manhattan Construction Management where she was responsible for both union-titled construction inspectors and contractors performing final restoration.

I have a Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering from Manhattan College in Riverdale, NY and an MBA from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute with a concentration in Management. In my spare time, I enjoys travelling, snowboarding and running my small art business, Karmic Henna, at local craft shows and festivals.
