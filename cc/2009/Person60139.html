Lori White’s Summary

Lori Elizabeth White is an award-winning online marketing manager and social media strategist with a focus on community building, blog outreach and interactive copy writing. A graduate with honors from the University of Pennsylvania, Lori has consulted with international and national public relations firms and advertising and marketing agencies, working with clients that range from St. Louis based startup MealChime.com to national brands Bacardi USA, Papa Johns and AT&T. Her insights into the intersection of the real world and social media (as well as her resume) can be found at her personal website, http://iamlori.com.

And now for the first person:

I work with clients to build their conversations, shape stories and create communities. Fluent in all things internet and interactive, I aim to further serve clients by combining on-point copywriting and creative concepting, sharp project management and insightful and inteligent information design.

I care about my work and I care about the people I am ultimately working for - consumers like me and you. In short - I care.
