John Paget’s Summary

Independent producer and director of feature-length documentary films for Television, DVD and limited theatrical release.

I also do contract / freelance work as a director, shooter, editor and director of photography. I have worked hard at my craft to develop a real aesthetic style in my shooting and editing - and I am a very experienced field producer having shot projects in Haiti, Cuba, Dominican Republic, Belize, South Africa, Russia, Thailand, China, Mexico, as well as nearly all 50 states of the US.

John Paget’s Specialties:

cinematography, editing, video for web, advocacy and marketing, documentary film, television production, DVD design and publishing, website design and creation.
