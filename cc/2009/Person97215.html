Robert Stein’s Summary

My professional experience consists of several years doing both Mechanical & Controls design for the automated machine industry using Autodesk Products. After I left the machine design industry I moved into consulting for the Autodesk products where I spent most of my time working with AutoCAD Electrical, managing software development projects with an multi geographical development team, and working with clients on process improvements.

Now I am working with ThyssenKrupp System Engineering as a Software Development Engineer mainly focussing on maintaining existing customization to the Autodesk software the company uses, as well as writing new features as needed.

Robert Stein’s Specialties:

Years of Electrical and Mechanical design experience, I have worked with the special machine industry for over 12 years now.
