Bridget Gailey’s Summary

bg | creative is a boutique design and marketing studio, born of the 14-year career of Bridget Gailey. I combine an inherent understanding of psychology and style with a knack for making a daunting task seem easy. Driven by a desire to make the world both stunning and smart, I make full use of both my left and right brain.

bg | creative offers a comprehensive array of services, taking on projects big and small, with flawless execution from start to finish. Our goal is to make your life easier while making you shine. We go above and beyond every time, and work hard to make things fun. We listen, advise, and manage projects for businesses and non-profits of all sizes. We can also be a valuable resource for agencies and in-house departments who need a hand.
