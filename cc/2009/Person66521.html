Melinda Gipson’s Summary

Melinda Gipson is a pioneering expert in profitable interactive publishing and advertising strategies. Among her many recent projects, she developed a local shopping search engine and associated SEM business model, along with the marketing and sales strategy necessary to achieve success; she successfully negotiated traditional publisher relationships with both Yahoo! and Google; and she has a highly respected track record for putting technology innovators together with first-mover Web publishers for successful partnerships and collaborations.

She currently seeks:
* a permanent position that will make maximum use of her abilities to generate immediate revenue and to position your company/organization for strategic growth over the long haul, or
* consulting opportunities in interactive business development, preferably in media (content or advertising); writing, editing or analyst assignments (white papers and market studies welcome); or marketing and PR assignments.

Melinda Gipson’s Specialties:

Online advertising & publishing maven, business development expert, "New Media Pioneer," PR, semantic Web (personalization), content distribution, marketing and local online ad sales strategy and execution. Blog publishing and advertising innovations at newzmaven.com. Have spoken internationally on "being digital" and how to create successful technology partnerships that can refresh your corporate DNA.

Melinda Gipson’s Experience

  • VP Business Development

    Grouptivity

    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    January 2009 — Present (7 months)

    Grouptivity turns email sharing into social media for affiliated publishers. It's unique Share+ application generates user filtered content that can be ranked using its patented ShareRank(tm) algorithm. ShareRanking(tm) offers users the opportunity to interact with others about content that has been demonstrated -- by virtue of being repeatedly shared -- to be worthy of attention. Publishers working with Grouptivity include Glam, Hearst, ACN, CNHI, Times-Shamrock, Detroit Newspaper Partnership (Gannett and Media News Group Interactive), and others.

  • Electronic Media Director

    NAA

    (Non-Profit; 201-500 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    February 1995 — October 2006 (11 years 9 months)

While at the NAA, she developed the NAA's first Web site, NAA.org, then a host of supporting sites: NewsVoyager.com, AdConnections.org, The Digital Edge (www.digitaledge.org), and the industry's first wireless index, the Local News Gateway (LNGate.com). She helped the industry develop business strategies to make their Internet operations more profitable, and created pathways for innovative technology companies to forge partnerships with leading publishers. She remains a regular contributor to Presstime Magazine and the Newspaper industry's New Media Federation.
