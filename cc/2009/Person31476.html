Erik Fabian’s Summary

I strategize interactive experiences for cultural, educational and marketing contexts. I provide ideation services, facilitate brainstorms, and teach groups to use creative, collaborative processes. I help small, creative driven projects establish sustainable business practices and marketing focus.

My art practice currently explores concepts of value in inflationary economic environments. I look at the cultural, emotional and aesthetic aspects of inflation as well as the financial.

Erik Fabian’s Specialties:

Advanced understanding in topics including brainstorming, critical feedback methodologies; individual and group innovation strategies; event, workshop and game design strategies; the history and production of contemporary fine art and performance; and precious metals investment and monetary inflation. Service Design, Experience Design, Experiential Marketing, Event Design
