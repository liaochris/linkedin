Charlene Chua’s Summary

Charlene is an award-winning illustrator based in Toronto, Canada. She works primarily in Adobe Illustratorand Photoshop. Her work has appeared in magazines, books, advertisements, and websites.

Her portfolio can be viewed online at: http://www.charlenechua.com
