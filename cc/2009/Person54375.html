Kelly Hobkirk’s Summary

I have held a variety of senior level positions in design, marketing and advertising during a 23 year career. For the past 16 years, Train of Thought has been my passion and constant proving ground for graphic design, copywriting, websites, strategic development, advertising, and a full range of marketing communications. My goal on every project is to truly understand client needs and their target market, then strategically write and design the best possible marketing communications to meet their objectives. I take great pride in producing superior creative that meets or exceeds my clients' objectives every time.

I thoroughly enjoy teaching clients about what I do because when they are involved in the process, they can really appreciate the exceptionally high return on investment that they get. After working with me, clients consistently tell me that they can see how all of the elements of their corporate identity, brand, and marketing communications work together to form an incredibly strong whole. These efforts shape their employee and customer experiences and truly guide their perception of the company and its products.

Our work is empowers companies and helps stimulate sales. I personally get very excited when I see my clients succeed.

Kelly Hobkirk’s Specialties:

I specialize in corporate identity, brand identity, brand messaging, new product development and launch, illustration, website design, advertising, and marketing communications. I provide a productive environment wherein projects are thoroughly managed, from strategy to creative, and from precision execution to launch.

I believe in an integrated approach to advertising and graphic design because it results in stronger brands every time.
