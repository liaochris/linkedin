Marigo Raftopoulos’s Experience

  • Owner & Principal Strategist

    Strategic Games Lab (New start-up)

    (Computer Games industry)

    November 2008 — Present (1 year 1 month)

    We develop business solutions for your organisation’s strategic learning & development needs through game-based learning. Game based learning can assist organisations in exploring management and leadership challenges including the learning and application of new skills and ideas among their staff and stakeholders

    Learning is about engaging people to learn new skills, ideas and practices in a safe-fail environment. And a games-approach to learning places emphasis on engagement. People learn best when engaged in fun, interesting, experiential learning experiences.

    The motto of the StrategicGamesLab is “Business is Child’s Play” because we believe that people learn and strategise better through engaging, creative environments that invite them to think outside the square.

  • Owner & Principal Consultant

    Strategic Essentials Management Consultants

    (Management Consulting industry)

    1994 — Present (15 years )

    I've been working as a management consultant for over 20 years. I spent the early part of my career in the large international firms and now I know better. Square pegs (the consultancy's formula answer) do not fit into round holes (the client's perceived problem). Today I work together with my clients to help them make sense of their challenges, renew their thinking and to inspire them into pursuing lifelong learning and adventure. These are the foundations of innovation and business and personal achievement.

    Our core competencies are in strategic business analysis, public policy optimisation, organisational development, leadership and entrepreneurship development.

    Where appropriate we apply the best of the tried and tested traditional business methodologies to our projects along side more innovative, contemporary and leading edge thinking, approaches and tools.
