Frans van de Pol’s Summary

More than 20 years experience in the Aviation and Aerospace industry in various international leadership roles; Manufacturing/Final Assembly, Engineering, Product Support, MRO (Aircraft and Components), Aircraft Conversions and Completions, Continued Airworthiness and Training.

Excellent social and communication skills with focus on achieving business objectives and motivating and building teams.
