Dominique Foucart’s Summary

I help organizations and people in conflicts to resolve it and thanks to this resolution, to improve their relationships for the longer term.

I do this through mediating the resolution between the parties, as a chartered mediator under the belgian law.

I do this also in councelling and training people on collaborative negociation.

I do it also by preparing contractual agreements with parties in order to prevent the conflicts.

You can link your network to mine by contacting me on dominique@interactes.be
I am also member of: ecademy, viadeo, xing
