Rachel Cotton-Smith’s Summary

I create work for clients from Private Collections to artwork for Professional and Corporate use.

I can offer professional illustrations, fine art paintings, and murals as well as the digital design work necessary for your business to flourish.

As an Illustrative Designer, I begin all designs with drawings of the intended plan to bring a sense of individuality to my projects. I have experience with developing logos, marketing materials, collateral materials, and websites. As a printing professional for several years, I have the necessary knowledge to ensure that the final project will be ready for print greatly reducing the amount of money the client will have to pay additional for pre-press issues. With a background in illustration, painting, and photography, I am capable of completing a project without having to outsource to other professionals, again saving clients a great deal of money.

Rachel Cotton-Smith’s Specialties:

Pre-press experience; Full color and Spot color printing; Proficient with Adobe InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop, and Dreamweaver; Wordpress Savvy, CSS, strong drawing skills, painting, experience in Marketing
