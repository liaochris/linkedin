Renée Barrett’s Summary

Renée Barrett, aka AAARenée, is a professional services marketing and business development professional with over 11 years experience using technology and change management to advance the initiatives of two AM LAW 100 law firms, a not-for-profit organization, and her clients. An accomplished relationship builder with partners, general counsels, retired judges, corporate officers, decision makers, and support staff who embraces technology and strategic changes for optimal results. Well versed in the professional services culture, law firm operations, and global business environment. A diplomatic, client-oriented, and dedicated team player with a track record for raising visibility and increasing participation in business development related activities.

She appreciates that the practice of professional services is at its core a relationship driven business. As such, professionals constantly need to assess their key relationships, strategies, and profitability. Using awareness, action, and accountability she works with clients to advance their initiatives.

AAARenée is known as an advisor, coach, and trainer that instead of accepting “can’t” moves forward by asking “how?” When retained, she works tirelessly to boost her clients’ results. Her familiarity with the intricacies of professional services firms empowers her to make a powerful contribution.
