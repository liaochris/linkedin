They also realize that their children are so “American” that they would be hard pressed to cope with just being sent to Nigeria to live with relatives there, go to school and learn the culture. These parents saw the need for a International school in Nigeria that would meet the educational needs of their children while at the same time introducing them to Nigerian culture in a setting
