Sandra Gulland’s Summary

I have published four novels.

The Josephine B. trilogy, based on the life of Josephine Bonaparte, has been published in 15 countries and in 13 languages. It has sold over a million worldwide. The titles are: The Many Lives & Secret Sorrows of Josephine B.; Tales of Passion, Tales of Woe; The Last Great Dance on Earth.

My newest novel is MISTRESS OF THE SUN, is based on the life of Louise de la Vallière, mistress of the Sun King. It is now out in Canada, and will be published in the U.S. in June.

(Please see my website for details: www.sandragulland.com.)
