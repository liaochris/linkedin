George Lin’s Summary

I have worked as an Art Director/User Experience Expert in both full time positions and as an independent freelancer. I have knowledge and experience working with user-centered design principles, PLUS, experience managing successful creative teams in large scale projects. My professional skills draw on 8 years of previous experience as an Art Director, and Lead Designer working with top fortune 500 companies such as Yahoo!, Visa and eBay/PayPal.
