Peter Hartung’s Experience

  • Art dealer, owner

    Comicart.dk

    (Privately Held; Myself Only; Arts and Crafts industry)

    March 2006 — Present (3 years 5 months)

    Comicart.dk is the first, the largest - and the only! - danish online shop, trading and selling international original comic art. We are situated near Copenhagen, and we love comics and comic art. We specialize in Danish comic art and American comic strip art from the Golden Age of comics. We have a large selection of art by more than 160 artists. This is the place to find illustration art, fantasy art, erotic art, rare books and much more.

  • Owner

    Hartung Press

    (Privately Held; Myself Only; Public Relations and Communications industry)

    August 2001 — Present (8 years)

    Hartung Press specializes in high quality, professional journalism and public relation work. We produce a weekly section about the real estate market for Danish newspaper Berlingske Tidende - BusinessEjendomme. The owner, Peter Hartung, is a freelance journalist and communication advisor who specializes in a variety of areas. To mention a few - architecture, property investment, construction, leadership, popular culture, agriculture, art - including comics and original comic art, popular music and much more.

  • Journalist

    Berlingske Tidende

    (Newspapers industry)

    August 2002 — December 2008 (6 years 5 months)

    Berlingske Tidende is the oldest newspaper in the world that still lives! Founded in 1749 by Ernst Henrich Berling, still in existence; with a circulation of about 125,000 copies on weekdays, it is also one of the "big three" broadsheet-quality newspapers in Denmark. In 2006 Berlingske Tidende, along with the rest of Berlingske Officin and Orkla Media, was sold to the British investment group Mecom Group.

    Peter Hartung works as a freelance journalist and editor of the weekly section, Berlingske Tidende BusinessEjendomme. Here you can read all about the Danish and the international real estate markets, news about investment and architecture. Peter has written the complete section since 2002 - it publishes with the newspaper every wednesday.

    Latest: After more than six years Berlingske Tidende has decided not to prolong the freelance contract with Peter Hartung at the end of 2008. This will allow Peter to work for other companies and media in the real estate and construction world.

  • Journalist

    Den Liberale Presse

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Newspapers industry)

    January 1989 — June 2001 (12 years 6 months)

    During my almost 13 years employed at Danish news agency Den Liberale Presse (Liberal Press), I was working for up to 21 Danish regional daily newspapers. My position was leading news journalist and reporter, and I was for more than 12 years a member of the press club at the parliament (Folketingets Presseloge), and covered a variety of areas - politics, economy, finance, business, agriculture, popular music and rock, litterature, comics and culture. I was a member of the company's board for several years, chosen by my colleagues.
