Eric Lent’s Summary

Eric Lent is a proven veteran in general management and a senior marketing executive with a successful history of visionary and strategic leadership building great brands, driving growth and achieving financial goals. Eric has delivered results across a range of corporate cultures at consumer products powerhouses, including The Hershey Company, Eastman Kodak, SC Johnson and Saatchi & Saatchi for iconic brands such as Hershey’s, Reese’s, Kodak, British Airways, Ivory, J&B Scotch and Glade.
