Michael Hall, PMP’s Summary

Michael has over 28 years of global experience in design, delivery, and sustainment of application systems and enabling technical architectures. He has served in a variety of roles in management and in technology, across a wide spectrum of business environments including Retail, Consumer Goods, and Manufacturing. In addition to his technical and organizational skills, Michael is a certified Project Management Professional, and is experienced in implementing project management office (PMO) organizations and processes.
