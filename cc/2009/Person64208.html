Martin Varallo’s Summary

I am a trained Fine Artist, a painter, printmaker and sculptor (BA hons).

Creativity is essential to me.

I have performed world wide for over 18 years at the worlds top festivals and venues. Created circus acts, comedy routines and theatre shows.

I also write and direct for solo, double acts and Circus Companies.

I give specialised workshops in physical comedy, creating comic routines, playfulness, improvisation and trust building.

During my performing career I have continued to draw, create and use digital media. My specialities are Flash design and interaction, CSS, Illustration and graphic design.

I am trained in Falsh, CSS and javascript.

http://twitter.com/YouTheBiscuit
