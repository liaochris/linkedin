Tamara Heater’s Summary

I have worked in health care for over 30 years, and desire to re-enter the sales arena in medical device, DME, capital equipment or disposables. My experience includes working with York Medical Supply, the number one supplier of disposables, surgical supplies, DME purchase and rental, and general medical supplies to hospitals, physicians, nursing homes, home health agencies and home care patients, through out the heartland, now known as American Home Patient. In addition, through the consultative account executive position at Partsbase.com, I was able to work with global aerospace and defense companies utilizing a parts listing service to minimize inventory or downtime. I was also responsible to educate companies such as PEI Helicopters or Sikorsky or Boeing regarding the use of government procurement information including part numbers and technical drawings. I very much enjoyed the work, and desire a consultative selling position to engage and combine my experience in sales, education, acute health care supervision and and transport practice.

If finding a self starter is important to you needs, I also worked as a freelance, contract media representative in sponsorship and ad sales working with the worlds largest ad agencies for media buys, representing sales for pro/celebrity golf tournaments, such as Bing Crosby, or ad sales for Gaylord Entertainments' Z-Music Television, or spotlight feature print stories for Flying Magazine.

I am currently working toward my Bachelor's degree, although, not having one, never stopped me from working with great companies. Many of the companies I worked with were simultaneous employment, and overlap in time. I have stepped out of my comfort zone to do work that interested me to broaden my knowledge of the world.

I am also, happily, the mother of two grown boys Stuart, who is a student in his third year of college and Ryan who served our Armed Forces, serving in the Army, and is now home pursuing his education.
