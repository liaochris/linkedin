Eric Frankel’s Summary

Hotel Selection Services is a national site selection company, that at no cost, assists groups in finding hotel venues for any events they will have. We find hotels that meet the criteria for your availability, rate and space needs. We excel at finding the right fit and make sure that your event is setup with the hotel of your choice. Rather than you contacting a multitude of people to obtain information on their hotels, you would provide us with your specifications and we would locate hotels that meet your needs. The hotels would send the proposals or agreements to secure space. You would contract with your choice of properties. We have the ability to offer you different proposals so you can choose the best fit. There is no charge for our services to your organization. The hotel that you contract with will pay us for our services. We work with any groups that have a need for 15 or more rooms per night.
