Roger Rowett’s Summary

I have worked extensively within the care and education sectors, both as a provider, and within regulation and inspection. I now offer a range of consultancy and training services that focus on self evaluation, Appreciative Inquiry, and customer feedback. These are delivered as part of Taith Ltd - Taith is Welsh for 'journey'.

Taith Ltd is committed to providing services that result in tangible outcomes. We offer a professional, personalised and friendly service at a realistic rate. These are a few quotes from recent clients'

'Very pleased, with the service provided, it was delivered on time, to budget and met all senior manager's expectations.'

'The methodology conducted in relation to gathering evidence was most thorough and systematic. The production of the report based on the evidence gathered was also very impressive. Roger was able to build up excellent working relationships with key personnel and delivered the overview of the report at two management meetings in a concise, interesting and informative way.'

'This was a most valuable service as it enabled a very complicated and potentially time consuming investigation to be worked through very quickly - essentially based on Roger's considerable experience of investigating complex complaints.'

'Very informative and thought provoking. Can use information given straight away to improve the service to clients.'
