Stefanie Zizzo’s Summary

Stefanie is a career coach, life coach, speaker & workshop facilitator.

She works with individuals & groups seeking to:

Stretch out of their comfort zones to reach their potential
Learn how to strike a balance between career & work
Re-ignite their passion for their work & life
Strengthen their work relationships
Transition their work and/or life, creating their "best fit"

Stefanie offers interactive seminars to organizations and professional associations on such topics as:

Taking Ownership of Your Career
Getting Noticed, Known and Connected
Just Do It: Life is Too Short to Let Fear Stop You
Embracing Change
For a full list go to http://www.stefaniezizzo.com/presentations.html.

She is also the author of The Journey From Comfort to Possibility: A Workbook of Self-Discovery and Personal Transformation. Copies are available through www.stefaniezizzo.com/Workbook.

Contact her at stefanie@stefaniezizzo.com or 919-744-9722
