Laura Briere’s Summary

We are currently seeking a graphic designer that can produce a wide array of creative deliverables and also with internet project production. Personality is vital, as this job is supposed to be fun! The right candidate will have an outgoing, creative spirit, and really enjoy their work.

DAILY RESPONSIBILITIES

Your responsibilities will center on creative output for many projects, such as developing logos, websites, brochures, business cards, and more. You can expect that every day will be different than the last, with different projects, priorities and processes.

Projects may involve: working from sketches, creating something from scratch in Photoshop, modifying someone else’s art, repairing damaged files, creating art in Illustrator, assembling collateral in InDesign, editing HTML, writing CSS, and pretty much anything. The position requires a lot of the “jack of all trades” type of mentality with regard to creativity, with a can-do attitude.

SPECIFIC DETAILS

• Full time, Monday through Friday 9 AM to 6 PM, with 1 Hour for Lunch
• Office is located in Worcester, near Downtown, Route 20 and the Mass Pike, free parking
• Benefits available

REQUIREMENTS

• Have expert proficiency in Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and Indesign
• Thorough understanding of CSS, PHP, HTML, and WordPress
• Knowledge of the print industry and print production specifications
• Bachelors of Arts desired, but not required
• Minimum 5 Years Experience – exceptions will be made for truly compelling portfolios

TO APPLY

Submit the following articles to laura@vision-advertising.com, with the subject “Graphic Design Position”:

• Resume
• Cover Letter
• 20 of the 25 portfolio samples requested:
• 5 logos
• 5 web designs
• 5 web development projects (may be the same as ii)
• 5 advertisement/billboard samples
• 5 promotional material samples
