Stefanie (Moylan) Dozier’s Summary

Stefanie is a MARKETING & EVENT PLANNING professional with over 10 yrs of experience. She is successful in providing MARKETING, GRAPHIC DESIGN & EVENT PLANNING services both as employee or contractor for local businesses, as well as large corporations and nonprofits. She has MANAGED EMPLOYEES with staff up to 40 employees for over 15 yrs. in office, hospitality & retail enviroments.

Stefanie was honored as "Businessperson of the Year in 2001" by the Streamwood Chamber of Commerce & was voted one of "Chicago's Best Wedding Planners 2007" by CitySearch.

Stefanie is accomplished in the areas of MARKETING COMMUNICATIONS & DESIGN. She is responsible for corporate marketing & strategic plans, as well as reports & projection budgeting and has been recognized for her ability to use resources effectively and efficiently. She is experienced in the grassroots of identity planning, branding & product placement as well as tech-savvy social marketing such as Twitter, LinkedIn & Facebook. She is proficient in programs such as Adobe CreativeSuite and Quark, as well as MS Word, Excel & PowerPoint. She design's eye-catching media advertising including: display ads, catalogs, PowerPoint presentations, brochures, newsletters, postcards, reports & websites. Stefanie is skillful in photography, public relations writing & advertising: press releases, advertorials & other media writing & design.

As an EVENT PLANNER, she has managed corporate & nonprofit events, weddings, tradeshows, & private affairs , since 1999. Her experience includes: Theme Development, Service and Site Acquisition & Negotiation, Vendor Relations, Budget & Timeline Planning, Event Marketing, Stationery, Party Favors, Menu Planning, Décor & Floral Design.

Stefanie has volunteered on, or chaired, many committees. She also served on the Board of Directors for the Streamwood Chamber of Commerce and in 2004, the SCC honored her with an award for "Distinguished & Devoted Service".
