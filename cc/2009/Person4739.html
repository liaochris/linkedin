Amrita Makhni’s Summary

I have over eight years’ experience in identifying & sourcing professionals for clients in New Zealand, India, UK & USA, I have extensive exposure to process oriented methodologies. Besides account management experience, I have recruited for positions as an Internal (in-house) Recruiter, RPO Recruitment Consultant & in Recruitment Agencies.

In my career, I have effectively contributed to -

- Develop a recruiting strategy that continually replenishes the candidate pipeline
- Develop and support strategy including electronic systems and social media (Web 2.0)
- Develop collateral marketing materials for Web 2.0 channels
- Develop the Referral and Key Strategic Hire programs for clients
- Develop and maintain company-wide standards and procedures for recruiting all levels
- Develop training material for individuals in recruitment (including Hiring Managers)

At present, I am focused on optimising the use of Employee Referral Programs as a candidate sourcing channel. I am constantly engaging with people to be effective in my strategy and of course, still learning as I go along.....

Besides ERPs, I assist professionals to create profiles that ensure optimum visibility and presentation of their skills and experience in their field. Companies usually utilise this service to assist their employees when applying for roles internally. Individuals utilise this service to enhance their chances of obtaining interviews in a tight market.

I am keen to meet people from all walks of life and if I can be of assistance to you in any way, please feel free to connect. :)
