Mark Edwards’s Summary

Currently programming CBS Radio's highly successful KEZK and KYKY (Y98) in St. Louis. KEZK was named Rado And Records Large Market Adult Contemporary Station Of The Year in September of 2008. Both stations have had significant ratings increases in the last year, with KEZK again #1 among all listerers 12+ and both stations ranking #1 or #2 in all key female demographic cells. Also programming several online streams and HD channels. Over two decades as successful radio Program Director and Product Manager Extensive experience in brand building and marketing broadcast and online properties. Award winner for radio programming and innovative direct marketing programs.

Mark Edwards’s Specialties:

Focusing broadcast properties to super-serve target audience, innovative online and e-mail marketing and promotion, talent management and development, rebranding and building new brands in traditional and new media, developing target driven strategies, well known visionary with plans and theories about broadcasting and new media's role in peoples' lives both short and long term.
