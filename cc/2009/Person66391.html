Mike Henderson’s Summary

Creative Copywriter, Podcast Producer, Photographer, Videographer, Editor, Account Executive, and Project Manager with experience in brand development; content production, publication and distribution; social media community creation, participation and management; sales and business development; account and project management; events coordination and execution.

In the past year I have developed skills, relationships and credibility in all of the above fields. I have gained respect in both the company and the community as a reliable, creative person who can communicate and execute on ideas and concepts both independently and as a team.

Copywriting and personal brand-defined blogging specialist. I write creative non-fiction articles on the outdoors and community interests. I'm increasingly involved in community outreach and development projects like the Reno Bike Project and the open space and trail advocacy group The Poedunks (www.poedunk.com). Roving dirtbagism: I clean up good, but don't let it fool you.

Please check my blog if you're interested in the specifics. It's all there, unfiltered, and possibly unpleasant, but it's what I'm all about.

I live about a 90% car-free lifestyle: I ride my bike. I get other people to ride bikes. helmet-head is the new Faux-hawk! Common sense dictates that I wear a helmet, so some days I get lucky and some I don't. I'm not into casinos, so this is the only gambling I do.

-M
