Roberta Alvarenga’s Summary

Executive Director of Projects, Strategic Planning, Creation new business, Management of online and social media projects.

Since 2002, publishes articles concerning the coverage of the Brazilian and Latin American scenario to write about the relationship between technology, culture & science, for magazines and portals: Tropic - Ideas from North to South, NY Arts Magazine, International Arts Fairs, TPM, New School, Webinsider, W / News, A1 releases, among others.
Gave many lectures and classes in the national territory on the digital culture and new forms of digital communication: CAMPUS PARTY BRASIL, MBA-IGROUP, FGV-SP, SENAC-SP, SOS TV, PUC-SP, IED, UNISAL, TECEDUCTION, IBC, Interchange, IQPC , CASA DE CULTURA GAFANHOTO, FBIZ Agency, ALMAPBBDO, INFO APRIL 2007 ...
In the international context gave lectures in several countries like Colombia, France, Holland and USA.
Member of Pacific Rim New Media Summit (PRNMS) Education Working Group in the discussion and publication of studies about the impact of new communication technologies in education. ISEA / San Jose: A Global Festival of Art on the Edge. Ca. USA
