Gio Carino’s Summary

A license and experienced Design+Build Architect utilizing and advocating Building Information Modelling / Management. Mr. Carino has worked on projects from small residentials to high-rise developments; continuously and consistently adding wider project experience scope as his career progresses. He is an advocate of BIM and is continuously collaborating with industry professionals for the advancement and further proliferation of BIM in the Philippines. Mr. Carino has experiences in BIM implementation and also offers this service in the form of consultancy work as well as BIM Remote Production Support (BIM-RPS). His experience includes working with industry professionals in the USA, UAE, EU, Australia, Canada, Singapore, Hongkong and the Philippines.

Mr. Carino also has direct extensive experience in off-shore/on-shore Outsourced / Workshare services in the Contact Center, CAD production and 3D-Visualization sector from early-stage start-ups to multi-tiered operations; from finance analysis to marketing and business development to employee relations and motivation.

Mr. Carino is a member of the United Architects of the Philippines (UAP), Marikina Valley Chapter, the Don Bosco Technical College Mandaluyong Alumni Association (86-90), National Association of Mapua Alumnus (NAMA), and the Revit User Group Philippines.

Platforms used: AutoCAD, Revit Architecture/MEP/Structure, 3DMax, SketchUp (w/ Vray), Adobe Photoshop, GIMPshop, OpenOffice, ProgeCAD Smart.
