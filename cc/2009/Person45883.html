Jenna Brenn’s Summary

I have worked in web design and development for the past 9 years. I believe that a good website is a popular website! Websites should be built to allow for the very best search engine optimization while showcasing the business' brand and advertising their products. Too many websites are built to either one extreme or the other - either to be search engine friendly, or to provide a sleek design and high-end experience to visitors without being available to search engines. My websites are created to provide the best of both worlds - to provide a professional, custom design with a user-friendly interface and take every necessary step to help that website reach the top of the search engine rankings.

I began my career working for some of the top advertising agencies in Kansas and have since provided my services to businesses in south-central Kansas through my own business – Art of Jen Web Design and Development. I have also served as a part-time faculty member at Friends University teaching Interactive Multimedia Production. More recently I have given presentations on doing business online to small business professionals through the Kansas Small Business Development Center, Commerce Bank, and the South-Central Kansas Economic Development District. You can view my Powerpoint documents from these presentations via that Slide Share feature on LinkedIn.
