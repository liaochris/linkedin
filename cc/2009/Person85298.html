Rob Kleeger’s Summary

I have lead several start-up entrepreneurial ventures in various technology-based industries over the years, three (3) which led the venture to be acquired. I pride myself as a well-seasoned strategic sales, marketing, and business development executive that has gained hands on experience in building and transforming businesses through strategic and tactical growth.

Presently, I focus in engaging and developing early stage complex technical digital forensic investigation services for law firms, all size corporations, corporate IT and/or security executives, human resource professionals, and other litigation support professionals.

In addition, I regularly provide non-technical lectures to law firms and litigation attorneys and their staff through “lunch & learns”. Notably, Mr. Kleeger was selected to participate on an e-Discovery "Virtual Advisory Board" for The Institute for the Advancement of the American Legal System (IAALS). He is a faculty member of Lawline.com, a former faculty member of the NJ Superior Court Judicial College of the Administrative Office, a panelist at Rutgers–Newark Law School symposium on "E-Discovery and Its Impact on Corporate Governance and Litigation", presented on behalf of Half Moon CLE Seminars E-Discovery: Is Your Business Ready To Navigate Through The Minefield?, The Garden State Council SHRM Annual Conference & Expo“ HR on Trial”, New Jersey Defense Association “Strategies to Protect Employers from Claims Arising from Computer Misuse and Abuse and the Use of Digital Forensics as a Discovery Tool“, 2008 Spring Conference for ITA (Information Technology Alliance), featured on WNBC/NBC 4 News Channel, and many other conferences and seminars around the Metro area and nationally. In addition, he is an author and co-author of several articles relating to ESI and e-discovery practices.

Rob Kleeger’s Specialties:

Matters involving data theft and crimes facilitated by computer such as Data Theft, Restricted Covenant, Non-Compete, Sexual Harassment, Discrimination, Theft of IP, Commercial Litigation, Embezzlement, Bankruptcy, Class Actions, Toxic Tort, SEC/Securities, matrimonial related matters, criminal, and many more..
I regularly advise both inhouse corporate counsel, general counsel, outside counsel in both Federal and State litigation matters.
