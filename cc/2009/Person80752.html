Eric Ellis’s Summary

Web design/development manager and entrepreneur focused on user-centered best practices and turn-key project management.

In the last two years:

1. Planned, organized, and constructed web-based tools that cement design best practices for the largest online banking service in the country.

2. Redesigned a corporate website for a national non-profit company, implementing a content management system that has saved the organization thousands of dollars in web management services annually.

3. Developed an online community aimed at high-school-aged youth leaders.

4. Partnered with visual designers in the creation of a nationwide brand.
