Anton Stén’s Summary

I am a positive minded guy in my late twenties. I support Liverpool FC, love music and enjoy Apple products. I am happily married and a have a wonderful dog named Meta. I love French things.

I have nearly 10 years of experience in Art Direction (see below) and my strengths are strategy & concepts, design work ranging from corporate sites to web2.0 to fashion/lifestyle, trends in digital media and utilization of digital media.

Anton Stén’s Specialties:

Concepts, ideas, graphic design, trends in new media, art direction, new media, web, fashion/lifestyle brands.
