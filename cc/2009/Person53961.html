Kavit Haria’s Summary

I'm interested in internet entrepreneurship and development.

Since 2004, I've been a web-author and music business consultant with InsiderMusicBusiness.com. I also write a weekly newsletter to over 10,000 musicians worldwide. My most popular internet-based training program is the Musicians Mastermind - teaching you how to get more exposure for your music, sell more of it and get more gigs.

Since early 2008, I set up my latest venture, Continuity Code, that works with savvy individuals, guiding them to start and run profitable internet-based businesses that fuel their lifestyles, passions and desires. We run intensive 3-day training events and have home study programs.
