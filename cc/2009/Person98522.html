Thom Poole’s Summary

I have over 21 years international marketing management experience, with the last 17 years having been involved in e-marketing. A Chartered Marketer and Fellow of the CIM, I have written a book called '<a href="http://www.lulu.com/content/2371624">Play It By Trust</a>' which has now been published, and I write a monthly column for the Daily Telegraph Business Club.

I am a member of the committees of the Surrey branch of the CIM, and the South East Region.

I have recently become involved in the accessibility of websites, involving the proof of compliance of the recent Disability Discrimination Act having developed a quality standard mark under the brand of <a href="http://www,dda-audit.co.uk">DDA-Audit.co.uk</a>.

I am also a regular speaker on the circuit, presenting at various trade shows and seminars.

My clients have included the Microsoft, London School of Business & Finance, Infoline - conference organisers, Sky Banners - aerial banner towing company, British Airways, EdExcel, Saudi Airlines, etc.
