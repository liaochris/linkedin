Kevin O'Neil’s Summary

I am an Effects Animator in 2D. Worked at most of the major studios, WB, Disney Feature, Disney TV, Warner Classics. Experienced in 2D special effects. I started out in character animation at Don Bluth Studios in Ireland.

I've taken the last few years off for personal and family reasons. Been busy with independent projects, one being a 3D short with other animation professionals from Disney and Dreamworks. The other projects are book related.

My goal is to keep working in this great business and keep abreast of new technology and develop new projects.

Kevin O'Neil’s Specialties:

Specializing in 2D effects animation. Experience in Maya, modeling and animating.
