Sandy Jones-Kaminski’s Summary

A senior exec recently introduced me in an email by saying, “Sandy (Jones-Kaminski) is a Strategic Business Development & Marketing Sherpa who puts the letter 'E' in Entrepreneur & 'N' in Networking."

Of course I liked that intro, but I'm proud to say that I'm also a recently published author (look for me on Amazon) and hands-on consultant offering proven experience as an opportunity generator capable of constructing and selling solutions to solve strategic challenges. Basically, I have experience developing new business from scratch and one could say that I have a keen eye for opportunity.

People I have worked with comment that I am data-driven, persistent, persuasive and professional. They have also noted that I have a fairly unique combination of experience in strategy development, account management, sales, marketing, and operations areas of public and privately held companies.

I'm known for employing a proactive, consultative approach geared towards long-term relationship building and being adept at structuring large strategic deals. My additional strengths include partner & channel management, revenue creation, client retention, online marketing, social media, PR, contract negotiations and human resources.

I'm looking for opportunities where my entrepreneurial nature and extensive biz dev, networking, partnership & strategic marketing expertise will enable me to support aggressive business goals.
