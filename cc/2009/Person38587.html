Hamish Thompson’s Summary

WINNER, OUTSTANDING SMALL PR CONSULTANCY OF THE YEAR, CHARTERED INSTITUTE OF PUBLIC RELATIONS PRIDE AWARDS

SHORTLISTED, CHARTERED INSTITUTE OF PUBLIC RELATIONS PRESIDENT'S GRAND PRIX 2009

My business, Twelve Thirty Eight, is an award-winning PR, reputation and communications consultancy, more details on which are available at www.1238kmh.com.

Our team comprises some of the UK's most experienced in-house communicators and journalists from national broadcast and print media.

We offer a service for businesses and public sector organisations - from start-ups to FTSE 100 businesses - that is designed to support their reputational objectives.

Clients include Vision Express, Reevoo.com, Sainsbury's, John Lewis, PC World, Richer Sounds, Disney Store, Magister Artis Capital, The TechGuys, Screwfix, RoadTour and other leading brands.

Unlike most agencies, we take an holistic view of the media and avoid the artificial distinctions between consumer, corporate and financial PR. In our view, the role of public relations is to support an organisation's multi-faceted objectives with a clear unifying message that resonates with all audiences.

Our communications programmes explain organisational purpose, raise customer awareness, spur customer action and build reputation.

We have a fundamentally different business model that draws heavily on social media as a tool to engage with journalists and clients and avoids the expensive trappings and overheads of central London offices. Our announcements typically deliver very high returns on investment and our contact books are the best in the business.
