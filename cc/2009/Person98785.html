Tim Cimbura’s Summary

Tim Cimbura is an expert technology business consultant and professional speaker and entertainer. He builds powerful and easy-to-use custom FileMaker databases and web solutions that make people more productive and give companies a competitive edge. As a speaker, Tim inspires and motivates individuals with a message about being a "Child at Heart". He shares key principles that we can learn from children that help us to live more successful and vibrant lives as adults while masterfully weaving entertainment into his message. http://www.childatheart.com

As a teen, Tim was recognized as a real life "whiz kid" when he developed educational software and licensed complex computer code to other developers. Today, Tim's advanced technology is used in fortune 100 companies and small businesses. Tim has owned and operated his consulting business Cimbura.com for over two decades. He has lead successful technology startups and had several of his unique ideas published in magazines such as FileMaker Advisor, MacUser, and MacWorld.
