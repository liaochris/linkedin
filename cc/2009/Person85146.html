Rob Goss’s Summary

Full-time, freelance writer and editor primarily covering travel, lifestyle and society/social issues in Japan, although I also write on expat issues and business. Published in approximately 35 outlets around the world in media that includes national and international consumer magazines, newspapers, guidebooks, custom publications, and the Web.

Notable recent clients: Lexus Magazine, South China Morning Post, J-Select, eFinancialCareers, iNTOUCH, Insight Guides, Northstar Travel Media, TripSketch, MediaTransAsia, and The National.

My editorial work has covered Japan-related non-fiction books, newspaper copy editing, and a stint as editor of Japan's most well-known bilingual (Eng-Jp) magazine.

For clippings and a fuller biography, or to get in touch about possible assignments and rates, please visit www.tokyofreelance.com.
