Renée Tymn’s Summary

I offer a quality of work culled from experience in blockbuster film and award-winning commercial campaigns. I have collaborated with directors in Hollywood and around the globe providing set supervision, effects compositing, and color treatment. My fast-pace and keen eye for detail has enabled me to deliver finals on Flame/Inferno for a long list of Post Houses notorious for their demanding standards including Digital Domain, Sony Imageworks, and Hy*drau"lx.

Over fifteen years of compositing and effects experience, combined with enthusiasm and a dynamic personality set me apart from my peers.
