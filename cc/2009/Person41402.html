Informex USA’s Summary

Informex brings together serious buyers and sellers of chemicals, biologics, chemical technologies and related services. It is the global meeting place to efficiently showcase as well as learn about the capabilities of high-value, high-quality fine, specialty and custom chemical manufacturers, from pilot to commercial scale. It’s where all the elements come together.
