Benjamin Geyerhahn’s Summary

Hudson TGP is a lobbying, communications and public political consulting firm. It represents a range clients, from non-profits focused on changing the way we educate our children to innovative technology companies with new ways to provide local, state and federal benefits to the nation's working poor. Hudson provides comprehensive support, developing political and communications strategy at the outset, and carrying the process through to execution.

Hudson also works with candidates for elected office, for profit and not-for-profit organizations. Current clients included Snyder for DA, State Senator Craig Johnson, Head Count, the Small Business Majority and Democrats for Education Reform.
