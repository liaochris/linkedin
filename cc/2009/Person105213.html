Tim Wood’s Summary

If you are like most business owners you view your banker as a necessary evil. Someone to clear your deposits and pay your checks. Occasionally they are there to lend you money. My customers don't view me that way. I want to understand your business not just so that I can Bank it but so that I can help you grow it. I spend a lot of time talking to members of the community in various groups and events and I take pride in being able to connect your business with customers and partners. Try putting your banker on your team I think you will find a whole new way of doing business.

In my 17 years in the Financial Services Industry, I have worked with two of the top 10 Financial Service Companies in the United States and their predecessors; I have handled all types of accounts including deposits, loans, and investment accounts for most types of Small Businesses and Individuals. Let me put that experience to work for you.

OBA Bank is 148 years young and as one of the oldest continuously operating Banks we are strong and solid. OBA Bank was always known for its customer service. Ask our competitors once a client is with OBA Bank they don't willingly leave. We now bring the customer service skills that we have honed over the years with a strong balance sheet to work for you and your business. While many institutions in the Montgomery County area are reducing lines of credit for their business customers or are not renewing loans that make sense we has money to lend and want to work with businesses that understand and appreciate what a relationship with a local community bank can bring.

If you feel that your Bank benefits more from your relationship than you do please call me and lets talk about how I can help you.
