David Cohen’s Summary

David S. Cohen, Esq. is the Director of Legal Affairs & Risk Management for Angels Baseball LP in Anaheim, California. David drafts and negotiates legal terms of contracts with sponsors, vendors, unions, employees, and Suiteholders. He oversees organizational wide workers compensation and insurance issues. He also conducts medical screening of potential draftees and free agent players. He is involved with all aspects of stadium guest claims from prevention and incidence through litigation. David also provides legal assistance to LAA 1, LLC, the operator of AM830 KLAA in Los Angeles, California.

Prior to joining the Angels, David spent 9 years working as an Athletic Trainer in the Baltimore Orioles, Boston Red Sox, and Montreal Expos organizations. He is licensed to practice law in California and is a National Athletic Trainers Association Certified Athletic Trainer. Cohen was honored by his peers in the Professional Baseball Athletic Trainers Society as the "2001 Minor League Baseball Athletic Trainer of the Year".

He earned a Bachelors Degree in Exercise Physiology and Masters Degree in Community Health Promotion from West Virginia University School of Medicine in Morgantown, West Virginia. Cohen also holds a Juris Doctor from California Western School of Law in San Diego, California. He is also a graduate of the Major League Baseball Scouting Bureau Scout Development Program.

David has been involved in the Tort, Trial, and Insurance Practice Section of the American Bar Association (TIPS) for four years. He served two years as the Student Liaison to the TIPS Council. He is involved in task forces, general committees, and standing committees. He Chairs the TIPS Self Insurer and Risk Managers Committee and Task Force on Outreach to Law Students. David is a member of the State Bar of California, National Athletic Trainers Association, ABA Forum on Entertainment & Sports Industries, Sports Lawyers Association, and Association of Corporate Council.
