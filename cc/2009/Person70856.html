Murray Edwards’s Summary

A freelance photojournalist for over 15 years traveling extensively throughout the Southern United States.

Specializing in photographing and writing about the Southern way of life, travel locations, events and nature.

Provided articles and images of the storm damage of hurricane Katrina along the Mississippi Gulf Coast, tornado damage in Alabama, Georgia.

Have provided articles and images for the use in text books, magazines, area newspapers.

A regular contributor to several regional and national publications and news agencies.

Available for assignment work in the Southern Region of the United States.
