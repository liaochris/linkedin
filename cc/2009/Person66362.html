Meghan Kiernan’s Summary

Meghan has over 13 years of experience with Seattle-based startup companies and a broad and rich background in business development, online media, advertising, and marketing.  Currently, Meghan is founder of Vigor Digital Consulting which specializes in online marketing, advertising, social media and SEO projects. Prior to starting Vigor, Meghan was Vice President of Business Development and Advertising for travel startup, Yapta.com. In this role, Meghan led the way to several strategic partnerships as well as developing and managing a profitable online advertising business for Yapta. Prior to Yapta, Meghan was Director of Marketing and Business Development at Jobster.com, where she also created their first self-service online advertising platform for recruiters and hiring managers.  Prior to Jobster, Meghan was Director of Online Media and Business Development at HouseValues.com.  At HouseValues, along with managing an online media buying operation and team, Meghan initiated and closed a major distribution deal with MSNBC.com.  Meghan got started in online advertising back in 1998 with digital marketing agency Avenue A (aQuantive), where she helped to develop ad serving technology and worked with clients such as Uproar, Eddie Bauer and Microsoft. Meghan has a BA from Seattle Pacific University where she graduated Summa Cum Laude in 1997. She is very active in the community and currently sits on the board for PAWS (Progressive Animal Welfare Society) and works part time as a Master of Ceremonies with Allegria Auctions.

Meghan Kiernan’s Specialties:

Online advertising, strategic and business development relationships, online consumer experience, acquisition marketing, retention marketing, media buying, marketing strategy and execution, web design and creative development, direct response campaigns and branding campaigns.
