  • Co-Founder

    Survian Technologies LLC

    (Environmental Services industry)

    December 2008 — Present (1 year )

    A service-based start-up venture seeking to provide Wind Energy companies with a reliable cost-effective means of surveying wind resource areas for local avian and bat wildlife.

    Current offerings are extremely cost- and labor-intensive, and the resulting data is plagued with observer-bias, and prone to high levels of statistical variability. This leads to poor turbine placement and significant reductions in wind farm efficiency, due to prolonged periods of turbine inactivity.

    Survian Tech. LLC intends to solve this problem by offering a comprehensive, digitally automated surveillance system coupling novel object segmentation algorithms and intelligent surveillance software with high-definition image acquisition hardware.
