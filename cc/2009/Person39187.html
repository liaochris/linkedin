HeatherO O'Sullivan Canney’s Summary

With over 15 years experience as an entrepreneur and in sales and marketing, I am passionate about helping others create their own success by building their life and business around their purpose and passion!
By helping people discover their purpose and passion and define their mission & message, I can empower people to connect with who they are and connect that with "what they do". This is the fuel for their energy, and ultimate success! "Showing entrepreneurs how to create a clear message, focus on a mission of making a difference, and use the power of social media to deliver that message, empowers them to not only create a profitable business, but a business that inspires and fulfills them AND serves others!"

"Social entrepreneurship and authenticity aren't just buzz words, they are a way of being!"

It is my goal to make a significant difference in the world by helping young entrepreneurs (read: age or "stage") "connect the dots" between who they are, what they are here for, who they are here to serve and how to connect with and serve others, so that they can live happy, successful and fulfilling lives beyond their wildest dreams!

I am a passionate believer in the power of social networking to enRICH your life and business! Contact me, or visit my website to find out how you can transform YOUR world with the power of connecting with others!
