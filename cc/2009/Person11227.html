Bill Aiken’s Summary

Consultant and Executive with over 14 years of finance and performance management experience. Have worked for Fortune 500 companies, small businesses, and a major consulting firm. Founded Abacist Group in 2007.

Prior to Abacist, Bill served as a Finance Director for PepsiCo/Frito-Lay. PepsiCo in 2007 was ranked #3 for Best Companies for Leaders by Chief Executive magazine -- behind only GE and Proctor and Gamble and was also ranked the #1 best managed Food & Beverage company by Forbes magazine.
