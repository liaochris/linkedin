  • Owner

    Russellware, Inc.

    (Computer Software industry)

    September 1998 — Present (11 years 3 months)

    Russellware, Inc. founder Katherine Russell is a FileMaker Certified Developer in 7, 8, 9, and 10 specializing in initial project design, complete production, and adaptation of existing systems to new business goals.
