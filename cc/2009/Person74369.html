Niels Helles Borgen’s Summary

After completing business school in Denmark I held a two year apprenticeship with a Danish auditing and financial services company. Subsequently in 1995 I joined A.P. Moller Maersk as a International Shipping trainee and have up to now worked in 9 countries world wide within both the liner shipping and logistics sides of thee APMM business.
After heading the Maersk Logistics activities in the Eastern Meditteranean Area for four years I decided to pursue my lifelong dream of running my own business.

I am currently pursuing this through searching for an operating platform within the Nordic or Baltic region.
