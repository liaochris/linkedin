George Jucan, MSc PMP MPM’s Summary

George Jucan is an experienced Project Manager Professional (PMP) with 20 years of technical and management experience in complex environments, both in public and private sector. He is well known in the project management community as a successful IT project management consultant, speaker at public events, trainer and author of high-impact project management articles.

Project management consulting:
Combining his extensive PM & SDLC methodological expertise with a proven process quality approach and a formal approach to services delivery, George provides consulting services in the following areas:
- Project Management (hands-on)
- Project Management Methodology
- Project Management Office
- IT strategy and business alignment
- Processes reengineering and optimization
- Organizational Project Management Maturity Model (OPM3)

Developing project management standards:
George is currently Vice-Chair of the Canadian Advisory Committee for the International Organization for Standardization (ISO/TC236) participating in definition of ISO 21500 for project management. Past leadership activities in PMI Standards Committees include:
· Chapter 10 Team Lead, PMBOK® Guide 4th Edition
· Core Team, Project Manager Competency Development Framework - 2nd Edition
· Chapters 5&10 Team Lead, Government Extension to the PMBOK® Guide - 3rd Edition

Other Accomplishments:
· Speaker on project management topics: PMI Lakeshore 2009, ProjectWorld 2009, PMI MTC 2008, PMXPO 2008, DRC 2008, PMI GTISLIG 2008 & 2007, ProjectWorld 2006.
· Author of articles on project management, methodologies, security and data warehousing, published on specialized venues (Gantthead, Oracle Technology Network, ITtoolbox, The Data Administration News-letter, Journal of Conceptual Modelling).
· Trainer of project management courses, including PMP preparation through National Project Management and PMI Southern Ontario Chapter.
· Interviewed for Oracle Magazine (Sept 04, May 06, July 06).

George Jucan, MSc PMP MPM’s Specialties:

Project Management Methodologies, PM competencies and career development, IT & IS Project Manager, Project Management training, ITIL/ITSM, Six Sigma, business intelligence, data warehousing
