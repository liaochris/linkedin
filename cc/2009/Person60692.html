Ludwig Mantay’s Summary

Ludwig at Leesburg Toyota, is a 21-year Navy veteran. Ludwig has been recognized as a Master Salesman in car sales and trucks in the Northern Virginia district since 2008 by Toyota and was awarded as a top-trainer for the northern Virginia district in 2008 by Central Atlantic Toyota.

Ludwig at Leesburg Toyota/ Scion has served buyers and owners in surrounding communities including Leesburg, Ashburn, Purceville, Lincoln, Herndon, Warrenton, Winchester, Waterford, Front Royal, Alexandria, Fairfax, Manassas, Arlington and Alexandria Virginia. Additionally, the Bethesda, Germantown, Poolsville, and the greater Frederick, Maryland area are also served by Ludwig at Leesburg Toyota/ Scion.

Leesburg Toyota has a large inventory of vehicles, including the Toyota 4Runner, Avalon, Camry, Camry Hybrid, Camry Solara, Corolla, FJ Cruiser, Highlander, Highlander Hybrid, Land Cruiser, Matrix, Prius, RAV4, Sequoia, Sienna, Tacoma, Tundra or Yaris and other used cars, trucks and SUVs. Leesburg Scion offers the Scion xA, xB, xD, tC.

Ludwig's customers have rated him with an over 99% customer satisfaction rating based on surveys conducted by Toyota since 2006. Ludwig organizes many of Leesburg Toyota's charity drives. Sheparding the 07/08 Leesburg Toyota Toys for Tots drive, Ludwig, with the help and support of his general manager Mike McNicolas, helped garner Leesburg Toyota the distinction of being the largest Loudoun donator in 2007and 2008. A member of the Business Network International, Loudoun Business Alliance Chapter, and the Loudoun County Chamber of Commerce, Ludwig gives back to his community by also being involved in raising money with Leesburg Toyota for Loudoun Youth Fest, and ROCK (Recreation Outreach to Community Kids).
