Ray Huang’s Summary

Ray Huang is currently project manager and design engineer in the environmental services & cleantech sector, where he is involved in the design, construction and commissioning of innovative & novel biological and physio-chemical process systems for air, water and soil pollution removal, and for energy recovery & re-use. He has five years of experience in medical & biotechnology, having previously served in HQ SAF Medical Corps as Special Projects Coordinator in CBRD, and is co-author to several peer-reviewed research publications in the Journals of Industrial Chemistry & Engineering Research, Biomaterials Science and Membrane Science. His areas of expertise are in stimuli-responsive composite materials (1 patent application), bio-catalysis and viral-based biological control. He holds a M.A.Sc in Bioprocess Engineering, B.Eng.Mgmt in Process Systems Engineering, summa cum laude, from McMaster University, and a LL.B(Hons) in Environmental & IP Law from the University of London.
