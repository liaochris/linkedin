Glen J. Cooper’s Summary

Business coach, business broker and mergers & acquisition intermediary. Expanding services of Maine Business Brokers, which he co-founded in 1981, to now offer business coaching, turnaround management services, workout assistance and pre-bankruptcy consulting to distressed businesses and business owners.

Also Vice President and Broker Associate of New Hampshire Business Sales, a business brokerage and commercial real estate firm in Portsmouth and Meredith, New Hampshire.

Speaker, writer, teacher, and workshop leader in the field of business brokerage and general business. He combines the style of a motivational speaker with a street-smart view of business buying and selling basics, business valuation, and succession planning for experts and professionals, as well as general audiences, in all time formats.

Teaches courses and workshops for the International Business Brokers Association (IBBA) in professional practice management, professional use of social media, business appraisal and many other topics. Leads many regional and local workshops on similar topics.

Glen J. Cooper’s Specialties:

Business Coach, Speaker, Writer, Teacher, Certified Business Intermediary (CBI), Certified Business Appraiser (CBA), Business Valuator Accredited for Litigation (BVAL), Founding member and Fellow of the International Business Brokers Association (IBBA), and Member, Institute of Business Appraisers (IBA).
