Jason Schklar’s Summary

Experienced game producer, designer, and user experience consultant. My goal is to partner with creative and technically savvy people to create fun, immersive, and awe-inspiring user experiences.

I bring to the table a strong background in game development, user experience research, and a commitment to iterative design. I thrive in an environment where honesty, hard work, and a sense of humor are valued.

Specific areas of design and user experience expertise include:

- Initial experience
- Discoverability and usability of core game play mechanics and UI
- Overall game enjoyability and playability
- Designing learn-as-you-play tutorial modes that help broaden your audience without alienating your core market

Producering expertise includes:

- Mentoring and supervising game production staff
- Providing due diligence review of schedule and milestone requirements
- Improving internal and external communication processes

Game Titles Shipped:

- Rise of Nations (usability guy and tutorial co-designer)
- Rise of Nations: Thrones & Patriots (producer & usability guy)
- Rise of Legends (producer, usability guy, and tutorial co-designer)
- Crimson Skies: High Road to Revenge (usability guy and tutorial co-designer)
- Jade Empire (usability guy)
- Catan for XBLA (producer, usability guy, and tutorial co-designer)
- Ninja Blade (external producer & provided design direction on core combat, controls, and tutorial)

Social Computing Feature Work:

- Amazon.com community contribution pipeline: Customer Ratings & Reviews, Video Uploads, Tags,
Product Comparisons, Profile Creation
- Amazon.com powered SNS applications on Facebook & LinkedIn
