Dr. Marcus Ettinger’s Summary

I established AdvancedHealing.Com - A Holistic Clinic in 1992 with the goal of becoming a world class "Medical Detective". From that date I have helped thousands of clients from all across America and abroad achieve renewed health through the use of advanced clinical nutrition, state of the art diagnostics, chiropractic and what I like to call, "re-education."

My philosophy and approach is to look at each client as a unique individual and address their health concerns from a holistic viewpoint. Acknowledging that a health challenge exists, setting a realistic goal for a return to optimal wellness and a thorough commitment to achieving this goal, is all that is needed to begin realizing true health.

I believe that prescription drugs, in general, only activate or suppress chemical reactions within the body. Prescription drugs do not rebuild or regenerate, tissue or function, they merely treat a named symptom. It makes sense then, that in order to experience a durable resolution to a particular body challenge, treating just the symptom is of no long term benefit.

An accurate and thorough assessment of a clients current state of health, combined with an exact plan to regain optimal wellness and vitality, is exactly what we do best. It truly is possible, to realize your true innate potential without the use of potentially harmful medications or surgeries.

Dr. Marcus Ettinger’s Specialties:

As a primary health-care practice we have seen clients with a wide variety of health challenges. From weight, cholesterol, blood sugar and blood pressure dysregulation to more complicated issues such as autoimmune, arthritis, allergy, endocrine, pathogens, and toxicity.
