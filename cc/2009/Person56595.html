Kristina Ross’s Summary

Since 1998, I have focused exclusively on the user-centered design of complex application interfaces for desktop, mobile devices, and the web.

I am familiar with GUI conventions across numerous platforms, and have worked with both enterprise and consumer products across a broad variety of verticals.

I enjoy working with cross-functional, agile teams, am fluent with standard authoring tools, and am familiar with contemporary code standards and development libraries such as YUI and JQuery.

I am looking for opportunities to create useful and attractive software for consumer or enterprise products and services.

Kristina Ross’s Specialties:

* Needs, task and work flow analysis, ethnographic research, and usage profiles research

* Information design and information architecture

* Interface wire frame design, flow diagrams

* Icon and visual interface design

* Developmental prototyping via standard code libraries and tools

* User-focused design process development, team-building, mentoring
