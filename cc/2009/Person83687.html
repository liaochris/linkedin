Richard Nacht, Esq.’s Summary

Richard Nacht is the founder and CEO of Respond Media. Richard has twenty-five years in the marketing, finance and technology development industries. Richard has focused his talents on creating online strategies aimed at producing technologies that benefit corporations, organizations, consumers and other communities of interest.

Richard is also the CEO of Career Connection Network, a SaaS technology provider which leverages the social networking phenomenon to enable corporate customers to build relationships with and recruit top tier talent, both active and passive. CCN partners with F1000 brands to build niche career online communities where like minded professionals can communicate, collaborate and learn how to advance their careers and enjoy a sense of community. CCN’s corporate sponsors then access and mine these communities for their staffing and recruiting needs.

Richard has been honored as a New Jersey Governor’s Cup for Entrepreneurial Excellence finalist and is a member of the Inc. 500 list of America’s Fastest Growing Private Companies. He is a member of the Bar of the United States Supreme Court.

Richard is the author of Recruiting with Social Media, forthcoming from John Wiley & Sons, winter 2008 and the co-author of Realty Blogging: Build your Brand and Outsmart Your Competition.

Richard is an Adjunct Professor in the Graduate Programs, Department of Leadership and Human Capital Management, New York University. He is a Senior Research Fellow and Chairman of the Education Committee of the Society for New Communications Research, and is the Technology Chair and on the Board of Directors of the Marketing Executives Networking Group (MENG).

Richard holds a B.A. from the University of Pennsylvania, a Juris Doctor (J.D.) from the New York Law School, an Executive MBA degree from the New York University Stern School of Business and is a current doctoral candidate at the International School for Management in Paris, France.
