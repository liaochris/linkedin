Victor M. Visocchi’s Summary

With offices from coast-to-coast, Schooley Mitchell Telecom Consultants is the largest independent telecommunications consulting company in North America. Our primary service includes a thorough review of existing telecommunications systems for businesses of all sizes in all industries. We present detailed, customized recommendations about ways to reduce costs while maintaining or improving service, and any approved changes will be implemented and managed by Schooley Mitchell. Fees are then based on a portion of realized savings, creating a no-risk cost management option for North American businesses.
