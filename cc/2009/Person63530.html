Mark O'Neill’s Summary

I am British, 34 years old and now living in Würzburg, Germany. In the past, I have worked in industries including the Scottish civil service (mainly the Education Department & Justice Department), banking, insurance, tourism, sales, journalism, call centers, marketing and publishing. I have been writing for 20 years and have been published in many publications. I have also done work in Scottish youth radio as a presenter & reporter.

I attended journalism school in England from 1992-1993 and so I am proficient in journalism skills including shorthand, media law, touch-typing & investigative journalism. As part of my journalism training, I worked at various British newspapers and news wire agencies having stories published and gaining valuable work experience. Highlights included making a television programme with the BBC & appearing on BBC radio.

I am now a freelance writer and managing / publishing editor at MakeUseOf.com. I also write an internet / technology blog at BetterThanTherapy.net and I am an occasional guest blogger on GeeksAreSexy.net .

You can see an almost-full list of my published articles by going to http://www.markoneill.org/writingarchive.html . I regret I cannot show all my work due to legal agreements with clients and / or the legal copyrights of the work being sold to the client(s).

I am also the former consultant & copywriter for an American e-commerce software company based in Indianapolis, USA. My duties there involved writing their user manuals & blog and moderating their user forum. I also developed marketing materials, promoted the company in Europe and helped to translate their software into German.
