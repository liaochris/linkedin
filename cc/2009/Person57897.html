I moved to Nashville in April 08 from Brookyln, NY. Am currently teaching part-time at the Art Institute of Tennessee and loving it! I am also accepting freelance clients. Currently doing work for an auction house here and a variety of clients for a marketing business out of North Carolina. Would like to expand my roster of clients when it is a good fit.

Lauren Vandervelde’s Specialties:
