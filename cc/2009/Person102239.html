Varun Parwal, FRM’s Summary

Armed with the FRM and the Indian CFA certifications, I've over 40 months of experience in the equity research industry. Out of this, I've spent over 36 months covering U.S. stocks across sectors such as Defence (Boeing), Automotives (Ford & GM), Media (Time Warner), Electronics (Sony), Retailers (Wal-Mart & Home Depot), Beverages (Coca-Cola & PepsiCo) and Biotech & Pharma. Of the sectors covered, my core competency is in the U.S. Biotech & Pharma sector, wherein I've tracked a universe of 26 companies for a period of over 30 months. I also have experience of performing research on stocks across various sectors in the MENA region, West Africa and India.
