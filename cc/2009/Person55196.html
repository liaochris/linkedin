Kevin Quinley, CPCU, ARM’s Summary

I assist life science clients in addressing risk management challenges arising from their product liability exposures. Additionally, I serve as a resource in risk selection, analysis and claims management for life science risks. The latter include medical device companies, pharmaceutical firms, biotech entities, clinical trial companies and contract research organizations.

Through writing and speaking activities in the risk management arena, I develop “deliverables” and content to assist client and differentiate Berkley Life Sciences as a premier provider of financial protection geared to life science entities.
