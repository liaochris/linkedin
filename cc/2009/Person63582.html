Mark Ranford’s Summary

Mark Ranford is the founder and Managing Director of Stratagility. Mark graduated with a First Class Honours degree in Mathematics and Computing, as well as a Masters in Knowledge Systems. His experience spans over 20 years in a broad range of roles including: strategy management, knowledge management, project management, product management & marketing, account management, business & partnership development.

Mark’s last post before founding Stratagility was at Accellion where he helped build a whole new line of business for the company in the Internet Backup area. This Backup service acquired two of the worlds largest PC manufacturers as its resellers during marks tenure and represented Accellion’s dominant source of revenue.

Mark's endeavour in creating a Backup service encapsulates his approach to strategy. Envisioning and then moulding the future to ones own advantage. Repeatedly innovating to maintain and increase value.

At heart, Marks nature has always been entrepreneurial. He thinks of himself best as an “Imagineer”. His inspirations include: James Lovelock, Alfred Whitehead, David Bohm, Gary Hamel, JJ Krishnamurti & John Storrs-Hall.

With Stratagility, Mark is building a business that helps others repeatedly innovate to create new business & value, a capability that he terms “Strategic Agility”. Stratagility offers solutions & services in Strategy, Innovation and Knowledge Management.
