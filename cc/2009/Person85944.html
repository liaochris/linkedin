    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    April 2009 — Present (8 months)

    The purpose of METRO is to keep members informed on current Information Systems training topics and to offer members an opportunity to share insights and ideas. Members consist of companies interested in starting or improving their Information Systems training programs.

    Member representatives are responsible for all or part of the Information Systems education function. They range from full-time I.S. training coordinators to part-time in-house instructors to managers with strong training interests. Guests are welcome to attend.

    Benefits of joining this organization are to provide members with valuable resources, such as the following necessities.

    Exchange information about the latest teaching techniques, methods, and practices that can be used within companies today.


    Meet and network with other educators/trainers in the area who may or may not have an emphasis in Information Systems and find out what they are doing and how they are doing it.


    Contact persons who may be using an idea that would aid others in the group.


    Exchange information about potential training resources or options.
