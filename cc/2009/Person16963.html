Charles Burleigh’s Summary

Data Governance is key to business success, especially in these tough economic times. Businesses are generating larger volumes of data, yet much of that data is not accurate and therefor not actionable. Without the ability to generate accurate business intelligence a business will struggle.

I love data! I'm passionate about data integration and data migration. Recently my passion has moved over towards data profiling, data quality and data governance.

My ideal position is with a company that is serious about data governance which will be able to utilize my skills and experience in data integration and allow my employer to achieve its data management goals.

I am an Expert Content Contributor and Blogger on CIOZone.com, http://www.ciozone.com/go2/?TheDataIntegrator, where I blog about data integration and data governance issues.
