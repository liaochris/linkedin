    (Think Tanks industry)

  • Owner and CEO

    OTS Management Pty Ltd

    (Management Consulting industry)

    July 2004 — Present (5 years 5 months)

    Created this strategic consulting company specialising in consulting to SME's, providing strategic and business planning, vision and mission statement quantification into performance measures, profit improvement.

    Products include "SMART Marketing", a 6 half-day workshop/coaching producing short, sharp marketing plan; "The ZEST Factor", a 12 step business diagnostics program to improve business performance; "TEAM!", a 1 or 2 day workshop to develop teamwork in the workplace; "Ignition!", a workshop process to start up a project oriented team.

    Clients have included Richard Noble (real estate management and development), Kimberley Land Council, Goolarri Media (radio, TV and multimedia organisation), Knight Piesold (engineering), Western Australian and Commonwealth government departments.
