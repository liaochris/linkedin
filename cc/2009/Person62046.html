Marcelly Suhali’s Summary

I work for Walton International Group, a Canadian Asset Management Firm, with over 30 yrs of establishment years and a group AUM of CAD $8.8 bn. It has Global Operations and we manage both retail, private and institutional clients.

For over 30 years, Walton has helped all of our clients to diversify their portfolios into something that has little to no relation with the speculative markets, called Pre-Development Land Investing Opportunities.

This asset class, albeit a bit slow and illiquid, it is also a non-volatile, non leveraged, hard asset - backed product which has never lost a single cent when held to maturity. Even during downturns like now.

Walton's Land Banking Audited track record is a whopping average ROI of 28.98% p.a. (* As audited by PWC in 2009)

Although we still belong to a rather untapped market for now. I believe, through strategic awareness and education, more and more individuals and institutions will benefit from diversifying into a niche asset class like PDLI.
