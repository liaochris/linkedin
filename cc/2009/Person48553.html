Joel Ungar’s Summary

Hello, my name is Joel Ungar and since 1984 I’ve helped hundreds of businesses meet their financial assurance and tax needs.

One complaint I often hear is how a business can never get a straight answer on what their professional services will cost. That’s one reason why I don’t bill by the hour – I give you a fixed fee upfront. Phone calls and emails are included in the fee – you don’t need to worry about the “meter running.”

What makes us different? Our Firm is focused on specific industries:

• SEC Audits – we currently are the #53 SEC auditor (by number of SEC clients) and expect to be in the Top 25 within the next year. We have clients throughout the United States (most of our SEC clients are not in the Detroit area).

• Franchisors and franchisees – we have over ten years experience in the industry and are members of the International Franchise Association.

• Restaurant groups – we understand restaurant financial statements and can help you.

Our fees are fair and reasonable, and our service is outstanding.

If the idea of working with a CPA that tells you the fee up front, has broad experience in SEC audits, franchising, restaurants and other industries appeals to you, please contact me.

Regards,

Joel M. Ungar, CPA, CFE
