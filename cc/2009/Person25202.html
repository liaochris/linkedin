Dean Lindsay .com’s Summary

Recognized as a ‘Sales-and-networking guru’ by the Dallas Business Journal, and spotlighted as an OUTSTANDING SPEAKER by the International Association of Speakers Bureaus, Dean Lindsay delivers killer keynotes, breakouts, and boot camps designed to empowering progress in sales, service, and workplace performance.

Dean is a guest lecturer to the UCLA and University of Dallas MBA programs as well as the International Call Management Institute.

Dean's book Cracking the Networking CODE: 4 Steps to Priceless Business Relationships (World Gumbo Publishing -www.worldgumbo.com) is recommended reading by the United Professional Sales Association and Profit magazine. It is presently being translated into Korean.

The book has been endorsed by a who's who of business leaders and performance experts including Ken Blanchard - author of The One Minute Manager, Brian Tracy and Frank Bracken, the former President and COO of Haggar Clothing Co.

Dean is a featured contributor to Executive Travel and Executive Excellence magazines as well as the nationally distributed audio publication Selling Power Live hosted by Jeffrey Gitomer. He is also the head writer and editor of the widely read e-mail-based newsletter, The Progress Report (Subscribe at www.ProgressAgents.com).

A cum laude graduate of the University of North Texas, Dean presently serves on the Executive Advisory Board for UNT’s Department of Marketing and Logistics and on the Board of Directors for the UNT Alumni Association.
