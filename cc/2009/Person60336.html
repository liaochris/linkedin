G.J Bajaj’s Summary

G.J.Bajaj is a graduate in chemistry, from DAV College of arts and science, solapur and post graduate in ‘oils and paints’ from VJTI, Mumbai. He possesses vast experience in innumerable and diversified faculties of life.

Running an ultramodern edible oil refinery, or wholesale distribution of textile goods, investment in stocks or distribution of electronic goods, are the different type of portfolios he has managed.

He was the resident director of solvent extraction company, processing 50,000 MT of oilcakes per annum, right from purchasing of the land to the commissioning of the plant. He is a promoter director of an ultramodern hospital, Solapur kidney care and research centre pvt. ltd.

Mr. Bajaj owns horticultural farm of amla fruit (Indian gooseberry). It is so beautifully designed and landscaped that thousands of nature-loving people arrange their picnics and programs in his garden.

He has written several articles, on various subjects, in the magazines and newspapers. His articles on 'Vitamin C in amla (Indian gooseberry)' have been greatly appreciated by the visitors of his website,http://www.kanhaiyaamla.com from all over the world.

He strongly believes that,

'Humanity','Humor','Hobby' and 'Hard earned money',
produce love, joy,contentment and wealth respectively.

These core virtues and values surface at every page of the book,'LOVE BEGETS WEALTH'.
