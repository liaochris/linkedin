Andrew Neville’s Summary

Neville & Rowe develops and supports investment management software for financial businesses.

With our unique knowledge of both the investment business and software solutions we naturally bridge the gap between business requirements and technology, and being an independent company ensures that we recommend and implement the best solutions for you.

Formed by professionals with a wealth of experience and expertise gained through 25 years in the commercial sphere, the aim of the company is to maximise business performance.

With the ethos, 'Empower your Information’, Neville & Rowe use the unique association of financial knowledge and software development skills to deliver inspired, efficient and affordable solutions to even the most complex business requirements. With true dedication to customer care, the Neville & Rowe team are capable, pro-active and approachable, offering a transparent service, with no hidden extras.
