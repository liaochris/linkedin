Jill Konrath’s Summary

Jill Konrath helps sellers crack into corporate accounts, speed up their sales cycle & win big contracts. She's the author of Selling to Big Companies - Fortune magazine "must read," Amazon Top 25 sales book for 3+ years & Gold Medalist at 2008 Sales Book Awards.

Additionally, she's a/an:

• Internationally-recognized B2B sales strategist & sales trainer.
• Speaker at sales conferences, kickoff meetings and industry events.
• Writer/publisher of popular e-newsletter and industry-leading blog.
• Founder of the Sales SheBang conference for savvy saleswomen.
• Passionate about turning the sales force into a competitive advantage.

To bring Jill in to speak at your sales meeting or to learn more about selling to big companies, email jill@sellingtobigcompanies.com
