Craig Kanalley’s Summary

I'm a hard-worker and self-starter who pays close attention to detail. I'm passionate about journalism and believe strongly in its important role in society, especially in today's digital world.

I'm particularly interested in the real-time Web and its effect on journalism which I studied closely while completing a Masters degree in journalism (online focus) from DePaul University.

Besides a strong interest in journalistic applications of social media, especially Twitter, I've also developed a passion for SEO, tracking search trends and breaking news.
