Karen Andrews’s Summary

Shine Sales Solutions was established to provide advice, guidance and tools that assist business owners to understand the sales process and develop the skills required to grow their business.

Director of Shine Sales Solutions, Karen Andrews has over 10 years experience in managing new business development campaigns for companies. She holds a Bachelor of Business Studies in Business Management & Human Resource Development. She is passionate about making the sales process easier, more efficient and most importantly, more successful.

Karen Andrews’s Specialties:

We work with your organisation to increase sales quickly & easily. There are so many ways of making sales & marketing your business, it is simply a matter of knowing what is right for your business.

We integrate tools such as Twitter, LinkedIn, Facebook, Website & Email Marketing & show you how to generate sales, market your business & boost your brand.

We also work with Corporate's to engage & sell to the SME market.
