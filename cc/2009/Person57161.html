Kari Ziegler’s Summary

Kari brings over 12 years of software experience to Level Studios (Formally Web Associates), with the last 6 years focused on creating and developing Company-wide quality processes, practices and teams in companies that varied from small privately owned start-ups to publicly owned Fortune 500 companies. In 1996, Kari was a founding member of the first AOL Instant Messenger QA team, where she started her practice of tracking test plans, tests and metrics in a database environment. From 2000-2006, Kari built, managed and directed teams for both US and Globally based companies such as CMGi, Student Advantage, GTECH, and TransUnion / TrueCredit. Kari's experience in financial, service based, entertainment and content management software has proven priceless to the companies she has worked for in the past.
