Abby Goldsmith’s Summary

Animator with professional experience in 3D Studio Max, Maya, Flash, Adobe Photoshop, Pro Motion and pixel art, personal films. I've also trained animators and led the art team on several games. Eight years of game production experience for GBA, Nintendo DS, mobile, PC, Gamecube, X-Box, PS2, and other platforms.

Abby Goldsmith’s Specialties:

3D and 2D character animation. I also do low poly 3D modeling, texturing, and character rigging. I can design 2D sprites, menu and UI/HUD elements; I also draw and have experience managing art teams. Other skills include Flash MX animation and HTML web design.
