Carrington Malin’s Summary

Carrington Malin is founder and managing director of Spot On Public Relations, one of the Middle East's leading public relations, integrated communications and marketing firms (established in 1994). As a consultant, he has a broad range of communications and marketing experience with firms such as Cisco Systems, Cleveland Clinic, Disney, France Telecom, IBM, Intel, Microsoft, Motorola & a variety of government organisations and NGOs across the Arab world.
