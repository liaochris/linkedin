Tim Swearingen’s Summary

Tim is a Training Specialist for Direct Alliance Corporation, prior to joining the team at DAC, he had found success in the building component industry.

In his spare time he is an accomplished public speaker, an award winning leader, an amateur photographer, a former stand up comic (trust me…he is a much better trainer than he ever was as a comic), an avid Boston Red Sox fan and the “poster child” for having some of the finest golf equipment money can buy (and yet he still struggles to break 95).

Though originally from Maine, Tim lived in Indianapolis, Indiana for several years. In 2000, he finally came to his senses and, realizing that you don’t have to shovel sunshine, he moved to sunny (and very warm) Chandler, Arizona. No, he doesn’t miss the change of seasons or shoveling snow…

Tim Swearingen’s Specialties:

Instructional design, adult learning theory application development, small & large group facilitation, structural component design, fabrication and repair, purchasing, process and system development, customer relations, human resources and staff management.
