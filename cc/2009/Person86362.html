Rosemary Rein’s Summary

HRD Consultant, International Author, Speaker & Certified Professional Business & Life Coach. Rosemary is author of "Go Wild! Survival Skills for Business and Life", published by Fred Pryor /Career Track Training, as well as a new business anthology "Blueprint for Success" with Dr. Stephen Covey and Ken Blanchard. Rosemary is a frequent speaker at global conferences on business ideation and leadership and last year presented in North and Latin America, Europe and South Africa. She also welcomes and plans and facilitates executive teams like the YPO (Young President's Organization) for strategic immersion and team building at her leadership center based in Tropical Costa Rica.

Through a global business faculty, Rosemary provides expert facilitators for meetings, events and conferences.

In her trademarked retreats, seminars and personal coaching programs delivered around the world, Rosemary's focus is on dramatically improving Customer Service, Leadership, Teamwork and Creativity via a Learning Safari Experience. "Go Wild! Go GREAT! The Journey From Now to WOW!TM

Now to WOW!TM Safaris have been hosted for such organizations as Procter & Gamble, Humana Healthcare, International Women in Coffee, The YPO and The U.S. Military.


-To continue to bring best practices to Corporate, Governmental and Non-Profit Teams through the delivery of inspired training and learning safaris that produce measurable results and revolutionary change!

-Bring out Passion, Service, and Creativity in work teams via inspired training, coaching and expert meeting facilitation.

- To connect and collaborate with world class facilitators, business leaders and community advocates in the design and delivery of head and heart connections that positively changes the way we think, do business, care for each other and care for our planet.
