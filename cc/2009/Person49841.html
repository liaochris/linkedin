Jonah Keegan’s Summary

Launching websites and web products. Direct experience with social savings (consumer) and ad-tech. Practice involves identifying customer pain points, creating use cases for stakeholder buy-in and establishing solutions, mapping development (specs) and developing marketing opportunities.

At Freshman Fund this consisted of focus groups, surveys and one-on-one user tests; getting stakeholders into a room and agreeing on the solution, writing a detailed spec, taking developer feedback and refining specs through to product launch, and coordinating with PR and email marketing for user messaging and external marketing.
