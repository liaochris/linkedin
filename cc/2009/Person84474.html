Ryan Stancl’s Summary

Long:
Game designer and published author with over 2 years of industry experience, a B.A. in English, and a Game Design degree.

• Lead designer / writer on 2-part educational RPG series for the DS and 2 casual games for the PC and Mac

• Game designer / writer on upcoming game based on a popular Cartoon Network franchise

• Created systems and mechanics for an RPG, a casual dash game, and a trivia / board game hybrid

• Wrote & maintained 4 full-length design docs, 10 shorter design docs, several pitches, dialogue & cutscenes

• Scripted levels for PC dash game, boards for PC trivia game, and dialogue for RPG using in-house toolsets

• Created 1 full-conversion game in UT – Seas of Europa – 2 board games, 2 level mods, and 2 Flash games

• Designed 8 user-interface systems, prototyping two and implementing several others

• Twice-published author on Gamasutra – 3-part article titled Katamari Damacy – A Critique & a postmortem

• Graduated from secondary schools with Honors; won 2 game design awards at VFS; won 1 ELAN for Seas

• Worked 10 years in professional team environments
