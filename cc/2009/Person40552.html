Harold van de Kamp’s Summary

Harold van de Kamp has more than 5 years of experience in the IT as a consultant at a diversity of organizations. He has a broad knowledge of the complete Microsoft platform, where most business solutions he realized, are based on SharePoint, Office and Microsoft .NET.

Harold is a communicative and involved consultant who learns fast and likes to undertake challenges. His social and loyal character makes him an excellent team player and coach who likes to fill the gaps between man and machine.
