Timothy Berman’s Summary

Experienced in troubleshooting, research, documentation, and breaking down information from a technical aspect. This was accomplished in a technical support position with WDS Global. Specific tasks included initial diagnostics on Blackberry Handheld devices, PDA/Smartphone devices. Whether it is guiding a customer through initial setup of their new handheld's, or to provide basic troubleshooting to resolve email configuration issues. Other calls dealt with more advanced trouble shooting steps (Blackberry JVM Errors) and devotions to specific research and possible resolutions to either assist the customer with getting their devices working again, or going over possible replacement options if all troubleshooting steps were exhausted.

In one specific situation, a customer had called in with a Blackberry JVM error. Knowledge Based article search on WDS Global's intranet database did not have any information on proper troubleshooting steps. Neither did Blackberry Knowledge base search produced any articles on step-by-step troubleshooting. Because of this, detailed notations of all troubleshooting steps were documented in the Remedy Ticket field, various knowledge based articles were consulted, researched, and with the assistance of the customer, a final resolution was found that provided the customer with a working handheld device.

Once the call was completed, a knowledge based article was written with the documented research, current articles that were consulted referenced, submitted to supervisor, technical support leads, and a proposal to include this as a knowledge base article for other representatives to refer to if a similiar call came through.
