John Hessburg’s Summary

At U.S. DIVE TRAVEL, we are wholesalers of tropical island vacations for scuba divers, snorkeling enthusiasts & folks who love beach basking. With a careful eye on safety, savings & comfort, we broker & plan dream vacations for clients who share our keen desire for ocean adventures.

During 18 wonderful years of wholesaling tropical trips, we've been blessed to serve thousands of good people. Our vacation planners -- all veteran world travelers -- cater to clients who place high value on reef preservation. That's why we represent only top-quality resorts & yachts that have proven their respect for vulnerable ocean eco-systems, by making life-positive choices that protect corals & critters before cash flow.

Our prime goal at U.S. DIVE TRAVEL is continually to refine our customer service, research efficiency & vacation package details. Also we hope, for many more years, to explore & develop new island resort destinations & add new diving yachts -- across the South Pacific & Caribbean regions.

The tropical learning curve never sags. It keeps climbing, year after exciting year, as we find creative, client-centered ways to manage challenges from El Nino reef impacts, global recession & airline pricing shifts.

Life near the sea, & under the sea, is good beyond measure. This big blue planet is alive & well, friends! After more than 44 in-depth inspection tours of tropical island groups around the world, we speak from direct experience & personal delight in the ocean realms.

Hope we can share that island delight with you, as well. Thanks for taking a moment to visit U.S. DIVE TRAVEL ....

John Hessburg’s Experience

  • Founder, General Mgr

    U.S. DIVE TRAVEL

    (Leisure, Travel & Tourism industry)

    March 1991 — Present (18 years 9 months)

    U.S. DIVE TRAVEL NETWORK was founded in March 1991 -- in Seattle WA, USA -- by John Hessburg & Susan Hessburg. Then the reservations office was moved to Minneapolis-St. Paul MN in June 1999, where the Network HQ operates today. Our website at www.usdivetravel.com is the largest & longest-running resource for scuba diving vacations & snorkeling vacations on the Internet, with more than 777,000 words of text + 1,776 copyrighted photos of tropical island getaways in more than 30 countries. After nearly 2 decades of reef assessment trips to the South Pacific & the Caribbean, our areas of special expertise include the Fiji Islands, the Hawaiian Islands, Belize, Honduras & the Dutch Antilles. As wholesalers of tropical adventure vacations, we continue to rep most major diving live-aboard yachts worldwide -- including the Aggressor Fleet, Explorer Ventures Fleet, the Peter Hughes Dancer Fleet, Mike Ball Dive Expeditions, Ocean Hunters in Palau, Nai'a in Fiji, Bilikiki in the Solomons & Odyssey in Truk Lagoon.

  • Staff Writer, Investigative Reporter

    Seattle Post-Intelligencer

    (Privately Held; Newspapers industry)

    August 1982 — March 1991 (8 years 8 months)

    • Staff writer, investigative reporter for Seattle Post-Intelligencer. (August 1982 to March 1991) Morning daily circulation 200,000. Covered fast-breaking news, general assignment features, several in-depth probes, plus features on the Latino community & mountaineering adventures. In one 7-month investigation in 1988, wrote 25 stories showing how lax Forest Service management fostered widespread log theft & fraud in Pacific Northwest timber industry. Stories sparked a congressional probe. Forest Service forced to reform national timber accountability policies after new federal laws were passed.

    • Freelance correspondent for Reuters -- the British news agency. (May 1983 to May 1991). As principal stringer in Western Washington, filed several spot-news stories per month. Many stories received national, some international, wire-service distribution, including features on the 1984 NCAA Final Four basketball tournament in Seattle, the July 1990 Goodwill Games in Seattle, the Mount Hood mountaineering disaster of May 1986.

    • Freelance contributor to Summit, Climbing & Pacific Northwest magazines (November 1984 to October 1988) Was associate editor at Summit, America's oldest mountaineering magazine. Two cover stories in Pacific Northwest: “Women Who Climb” & “Younger Men, Older Women.”

    • Freelance contributor to Mpls. St. Paul, Twin Cities & Twin Cities Reader magazines. (October 1981 to August 1982) In September 1982, published investigation in Mpls. St. Paul magazine that showed how inept fiscal planning led to the political collapse of Governor Al Quie's administration.

John Hessburg’s Education

  • University of Minnesota-Twin Cities

    B.A. Summa cum Laude, Phi Beta Kappa , Journalism, Spanish/Portuguese, Poetry , 1973 — 1977

    John Hessburg preceded his college career with one year's study abroad, as a Rotary International Scholar in Argentina, from July 1970 to June 1971. He then worked as a trail builder in the Cascade Mountains for the WA State DNR for one year. Studied freshman year at Grinnell College in Iowa, then later enrolled at the U of Minnesota, Mpls campus. Audited courses for one year at U of M's Med School: physiology of human color vision + chronobiology. One year's training in Journalism Law. Graduated with 3 concurrent majors -- main focus Print Journalism -- from the University of Minnesota's School of Journalism in spring 1977.

    Fluent in Spanish + Portuguese. Conversant in German.

    Activities and Societies:
    Varsity track & cross-country at Grinnell College (Iowa) during freshman year; then transferred to U of M; walk-on / track team University of Minnesota sophomore year; medical writer & special edition editor at Minnesota Daily, circ. 40,000, junior & senior years.
