Mike Hurst’s Summary

HJA Fire and Security was formed in 1992 and specializes, as the name suggests, in recruitment in the Fire and Security industry. It operates primarily in the UK but also internationally.

Sectors covered include:
Electronic Security (CCTV, Access Control, Intruder etc.)
Fire Systems ( Detection, Suppression and Protection)
Physical Security (Gate Automation, Locking, Perimeter etc.)
Manned Security
Corporate Security (Security Management, Risk, Business Continuity etc.)

Mike Hurst’s Specialties:

Roles recruited for include Sales and Marketing, Engineering and Technical disciplines; Management and Senior Management, Corporate Security / Security Management.

Depending upon the nature of the role, I work either on a retained search or on a contingency search basis.
