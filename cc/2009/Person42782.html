James M. Bucki’s Summary

Mr. Bucki is currently the Director of the Academic Computer Technology Programs at Genesee Community College in Batavia, New York. He has over 17 years of corporate experience in planning, managing, designing and implementing information technology and telecommunication systems. As an administrator and instructor he brings real-world knowledge to the student’s classroom experience. He also serves the college and local community as a member of several technology advisory boards and committees.

He has previously worked in the consulting industry and has had a wide range of experiences across many business types. His experiences include banking, health care, manufacturing and publishing.

Mr. Bucki has a Masters Degree from the State University of New York at Buffalo and received his Bachelor of Science (Cum Laude) from Syracuse University. He is certified by Cisco Systems® to teach the networking and technology curriculum at Genesee Community College.
