Horst Walther’s Summary

education

He completed his studies in chemistry, oriental studies , computer science and economics at the university of Hamburg with the diploma in chemistry with a diploma thesis from physical chemistry. He prepared a doctoral thesis on a subject from theoretical chemistry and was awarded with excellence to become a doctor rer. nat. Additional subjects of his doctoral examination were computer science and technical chemistry.

experience

Since 1975 Horst Walther operates in the area of information technology. From 1984 on he was successfully acting as a Management Consultant in the business areas automotive, chemistry, pharmaceutics, shipping and banks, insurances and other financial institutions. Recently he focused on insurance companies, banks and the automotive sector.

special skills

Horst Walther has acquired the following special professional skills ...

Identity Management - Process design, Systems evaluation and implementation.
Automated assignment of User privileges, evaluation and implementation of User-Provisioning-Systems,
Implementation of software quality managment
Project management, certified as a project management professional (PMP) by the PMI
Management of off shore-Software development projects
Evaluation and selection of policy management systems for life insurance contracts, "Riester-Pension" and Pensions funds

Dr. Horst Walther is a member of the PMI (Project Management Institute), the GI (Gesellschaft für Informatik), the DASMA (Deutschsprachige Anwendergruppe für Software-Metrik and Aufwandschätzung e.V.) and active contributor to the Arbeitskreis Security a chapter of the initiative of Hamburg's Senate Hamburg@work

Horst Walther’s Specialties:

Dr. Horst Walther, PMP is the president of the SiG Software Integration GmbH in Hamburg. He works as a Management Consultant since more than 20 years. He focuses his expertise on Audits and analysis of the potential of IT-shops and enterprises in the IT-business, Development and assessment of IT-strategies and the implementation of selected strategic actions of information management, like security management, project management and identity management.
