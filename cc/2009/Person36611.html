Jiri Mocicka’s Summary

I am a creative Senior Media Designer specialising in UI and graphic design for digital media, with a passion for professional design, typography and technology. I focus on clean coding methods and pixel-perfect layouts. I acted as a front-end designer for more than 6 years, during which my experience grew alongside the developing market technologies and demands. I have a good understanding of XHTML, CSS, XML, PHP, JS, AS3, jQuery at the presentation level of code, and also gained experience in the application of interaction techniques including personals, goal-based design, support for accessibility and interaction design patterns.

My goal for the future is to continue applying these skills and techniques to create new and exciting user experiences in an environment that fosters innovation and creativity. I enjoy expanding my skills to meet the requirements of the project or position.

Over the years I've been privileged to have worked directly or under contract with international clients such as SGI, Nokia, Samsung, Eastman, Hasbro, DC, Marvel, Warner Brothers, Arsenal FC, Pars Saint German FC, Sao Paulo FC, Jack Denials, Audi, Bath Spa, and Czech subdivision of Eva Jiricna Architecture Limited – EJAL.
