John Trivedi’s Summary

John Trivedi is the Owner, Group Cruise Organizer, and Corporate Event Planner at World Travelers United, LLC. His goal is to create special group cruise programs for reunions, affinity groups, corporate events, and pre and post cruises by providing the utmost value based on your needs, interests, and desires.

John Trivedi specializes in 3 cruise lines: Carnival, Norwegian, and Royal Caribbean and 4 regions: The Caribbean, Bahamas, Mexican Riviera, and Alaska.

John Trivedi’s Specialties:

John Trivedi works with large groups of people who are looking to create a special event onboard a cruise ship for their employees and members including pre and post cruises - primarily businesses who hold annual events at various land-based locations and are looking for a more cost-effective strategy and a more fulfilling and fun experience. John takes care of all the details in planning the event from start to finish taking the workload off event planners and businesses/associations.
