Richard Gale’s Summary

What I love is taking a piece of content, and looking at how to best market it, monetise it and how it can work in a cross platform world. How do the different business models of broadcast work in today’s cross platform environment, and how are they changing? How are traditional linear broadcast brands adapting to the world of VOD, where the content brand is more important than the channel brand – and how does this shift power from brand owner to producer?

I’ve been working in this field at a senior level for years now and every year new questions and challenges arise. You can never stay still and that is the most exciting part of working in a multi-platform, global, content led role!

Richard Gale’s Specialties:

Broadcast Marketing & Product Development, Linear, VOD, Mobile TV, IPTV, Home Entertainment, Global Business Development, Revenue Generation, Leadership, Marketing & Commercial Strategy Development, Cross Platform Convergence Strategies, Subscription and Premium Product Sales, Team Building, Market Research. Just don’t ask me anything technical!!
