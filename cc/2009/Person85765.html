Roger Overall’s Summary

Our goals are to give our clients:

- Better marketing material due to striking images.
- More sales due to enticing photographs of their services/products.
- A higher profile due to attention-grabbing pictures.
- Guaranteed first-rate images because they are dealing with a professional.

I've photographed assignments around the world, and my work has appeared in magazines, promotional material, books, advertisements and annual reports as far away as China and Fiji.

I'm a member of the Irish Professional Photographer's Association and have won numerous gold and silver crests in its photography judgings. I have also won 2 Fujifilm Professional Photography Merit Awards, the 2008 IPPA/Royal & Sun Alliance Landscape Image Award, and the 2009 IPPA/Royal & Sun Alliance Best Reportage Wedding Portfolio

Roger Overall’s Specialties:

Food, Interiors, Architecture, Worldwide Corporate Location Shoots (Personnel, Products and Operations), Also shoot Documentary Weddings
