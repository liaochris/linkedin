Mark Yolton’s Summary

Currently: Senior Vice President of the SAP Community Network at the largest and most successful business application software company in the world.

Responsible for the SAP Developer Network (SDN) -- the ecosystem of developers, enterprise architects, sys admins, and (SI and ISV) partners that surrounds and extends the core SAP environment. SDN is online at http://sdn.sap.com/

Also responsible for the Business Process Expert (BPX) community at SAP. See the early formation of this community of business analysts, solution consultants, and similar roles at http://bpx.sap.com (It's free to join/register).

Together, these virtual communities exceed one million members, are extremely active, growing fast, influential, and are examples of Web 2.0 and social networking best practices for the enterprise (Enterprise 2.0).

Also responsible for community events including SAP TechEd (four major events on three continents) and SAP Tech Tour (50 events on six continents). Together, these events attract ~30,000 community members to connect, collaborate, and co-innovate with SAP.

Previously: Senior Director of Oracle Web Marketing, with responsibility for Oracle.com (Ocom) - the public portal, Oracle Technology Network (OTN) - the developer portal, Oracle Partner Network (OPN) - the partner portal, and MyOracle (MyO) - the employee portal.

Previously: Led PeopleSoft.com and core aspects of its customer and partner portals. Vice President of Marketing and Strategy for Sun Microsystems. Senior Director of eBusiness at Sun. Regional Director of Marketing Services at Unisys Corporation. (...among other positions).

Work experience includes the high tech (SAP, Oracle, PeopleSoft, Sun, Unisys) and financial services (Prudential) industries, from east coast (Philadelphia and Northern N.J.) and west coast (San Francisco and Silicon Valley) locations, with global responsibility.
