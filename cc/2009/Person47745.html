John Kivit’s Summary

Founder and shareholder of online market research agency Multiscope. John Kivit has been a pioneer in the field of online market research since 1996 and has a special interest and knowledge of marketing, media, ICT & entertainment. Multiscope was founded in 1993 and specializes in online market research & surveys, software for online market research and website audience measurement. Multiscope owns a consumer panel with 135.000 members, a B2B panel with over 40.000 members and several specialty consumer panels. Moreover the company owns proprietary software for online market research (Socratos) and its own website audience measurement technology (Visiscan).
