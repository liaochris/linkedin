Enterhealth Recovery’s Summary

Enterhealth is a company whose mission is to more effectively address the disease of alcohol and drug addiction by continually investing in the most advanced educational tools and leading treatment resources. Enterhealth’s Life Recovery Center is a living example of that dedication.

Situated on 43 acres just north of the Dallas / Fort Worth metropolitan area, Enterhealth’s Life Recovery Center is a privately owned, premier adult alcohol and drug addiction treatment center designed to maximize the recovery process. Our comprehensive program offers clients a personalized treatment approach with 24-hour intake, screening and evaluation services.
Enterhealth’s Life Recovery Center’s comfortable, spacious setting offers private and semi-private rooms wrapped in an emotionally and physically safe atmosphere.The integration of therapeutic activities, along with an extensive menu of amenities, aid in stress reduction and help facilitate the healing process.
These elements, incorporated with a personalized treatment approach and ongoing support for life care, offers clients the ability to learn, understand and put into practice the coping skills necessary for long-term sobriety.
