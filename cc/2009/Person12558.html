Bo Gilbert, MIRM’s Summary

Bo Gilbert, Internet Marketing Consultant, has over 15 years of experience working in the new home construction industry. For the last 11 years, Bo has been assisting homebuilders, developers and new home sales professionals market their homes and new home communities on the Internet.

Bo started a new company, BotheWebguy, in 2009 to help small businesses get more from the Internet assisting with existing websites, blogs, and social media marketing

Bo continue to consult with select clients of mRELEVANCE ( a join venture with Mitch Levinson Consulting, MLC New Home Marketing, and Flammer Realtions),

In past employment, Bo served as a Regional Sales Manager at HomeBuilder.com, the new homes division of Homestore, Inc.known now as MOVE.

Bo is a Member of the Institute of Residential Marketing (MIRM) through the National Association of Home Builders.

Bo graduated from North Carolina State University and holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Civil Engineering with a Construction Option. He currently resides in Atlanta, GA and is an active member of the Greater Atlanta Home Builders Association as well as the Atlanta Sales and Marketing Council.
