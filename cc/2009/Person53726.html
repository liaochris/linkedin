Kathleen Harper’s Summary

Kathleen is an e-Learning specialist from a background as a graphic designer with years of print and web experience. She is currently developing interactive e-learning content using Flash, Photoshop, Adobe Acrobat and web-based learning content management systems.

An award-winning designer, Kathleen has brought print and online projects from initial concept through delivery and deployment. Her idea of the ideal job is a collaborative environment where she can work on project that are inspirational, informative, and educational, in a forward-thinking, ethical, and thoughtful culture with plenty of room for creative exploration. That's where she performs best, with her 15+ years of design experience and arts interests.

An added bonus for those who might be thinking about contacting Kathleen: She has a second love, writing, and has been able to apply her talents in various ways, editing, copywriting, and fiction writing, throughout her design career.

Kathleen Harper’s Specialties:

Interactive design for the web and electronic documents; Section 508 (ADA) compliance; instructional design and development; Publication design and print project management; Consulting/Contracting; Branding and identity; Art direction.
