Lisa Paitz Spindler’s Summary

I've been working in graphic design for 10 years, and 8 of those have been on the web, developing for a Washington, DC business aviation trade association on 11 of its affiliated web sites. I have also designed the user interface for a portal-based web application being developed by a Simi Valley, CA mortgage processing company. Currently, I'm managing web marketing efforts for a company that develops enterprise software and services for specialty pharmacies.

I've taken projects from concept sketches and graphic treatments to working wireframes and completed products, both on my own and supervising others. All of my online projects adhere to web standards for accessibility and repurposing of content for a variety of media. I have a solid understanding of how branding rules cross media and influence the design of everything from products and information design to typography and color aesthetics.
