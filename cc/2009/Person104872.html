William Wei’s Summary

William is a dynamic executive with over 15 years of successful achievements in strategic partnerships, new business development and product marketing. William has extensive experiences in building enterprise mission-critical system for financial institutes, telecom industry and Internet companies. With entrepreneurial and international background, William founded KC Software, Inc. to provide technology and information consulting services for Fortune 500 companies. While at KC Software, William also developed two subscription-based CRM (Customer Relationship Management) products for small and medium size businesses and for the health care industry. At United States Postal Services, one of KC Software’s clients, William redesigned and implemented the very large-scale nationwide call center management SOA (Service-Oriented Architecture) system, which made it possible for U.S. Postal Services to integrate on demand services throughout the web and IVR(Interactive Voice Response). With proven track record of managing and leading technology teams, William built teams by coaching and inspiring people to maximize the individual’s and the whole team’s potential. William is also the founder of a few web 2.0 sites that are constantly pushing the web innovation envelopes. Currently, William is currently focusing on mobile software development (iPhone, Android, etc) in the greater China area.
