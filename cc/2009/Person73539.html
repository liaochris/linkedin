Vicki Kolovou’s Summary

My initial studies were in the field of Food Technology and I worked for over 16years for a Greek technical Wood Industry magazine- changing between many positions in the company, including the Graphic Design & DTP department. Meanwhile I started my degree in IT & Computing at the UK Open University and upon completion decided to embark on my own business. Apart from CEO I am also one of the Web developers of the firm, that now employees 6 people.

netWire specialises in web development and Internet promotion solutions combined with graphic design. I believe that our strong point is the trust we build with our customers giving them solutions that vary from websites to promoting their product outside the Internet.

netWire has also produced its own Internet product called StreetGuides : Online Shopping Street Guides, with www.ermou.gr its flagship. ermou.gr has been online for about a year (since Oct.'08) and has managed to become an important guide on finding your away around the shops in the major commercial road of Athens. In the future more StreetGuides will be developed.
