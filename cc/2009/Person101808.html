Toby Morning’s Summary

I am a Start Up addict living in the northern California. I am a web designer as well as an internet consultant who can be hired on an hourly basis through my firm, Blue Monitor.

I have deep "hands on" vertical experience in media, technology, and angel investing.

I have worked on projects in digital rights management for media, consumer electronics, and retail.


In 1995 I founded a music distribution company, I was the first west coast distributor for Rawkus Records in 1997, and was the first vinyl distributor to sell battle tools, and music to Pro Sound Stage and Lighting, the largest DJ catalog service in the US.

In 1998 I founded the DJ Union in Santa Cruz, in 1999 I joined Destiny Media Technologies. In 2002 I started and sold a small wireless ISP in LA, in 2003 I started a BPO company from India named Blue Monitor.

In 2005 I Co -Founded a software company which has developed a web services platform for company to quickly, and cost effectively built application around digital media.

I have been an investor in set top box makers, clothing company's, Raves, and Hip Hop concerts in my younger years.

Currently I am working on a Co-Working space for "Media Centric" starts ups in downtown San Jose in the broadband corridor (access to affordable broadband is essential), and working with lots of start ups in Africa and India.
