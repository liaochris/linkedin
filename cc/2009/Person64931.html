Matthew Bate’s Summary

Currently, I am involved in technology–as the developer of a cloud computing platform–and as a trainer and coach specialising in personal productivity and ongoing development.

I teach people how to do more in less time and with dramatically reduced stress levels. My systems of workflow, productivity and project management can take ideas from concept to completion and help you to move forward.

My goal is to help people to be more efficient in what they do by teaching them something of benefit to the rest of their lives.

This is the culmination of the knowledge I gained while working in technology both for others and for myself. I have experience of technology product design, software architecture, marketing and project management. I have co-lead a successful start-up and intend to do so again in the near future.

Matthew Bate’s Specialties:

My personal areas of expertise include technology design, productivity coaching, life coaching, workflow management, project management, team management, leadership, product design, software architecture, systems analysis, systems thinking, offshore development, outsourcing, marketing and business consulting.

    While still technically employed by EDL and acting as a Director and advisor, I no longer have any day-to-day responsibilities. It is likely that in the near future, EDL will once again seek launch opportunities for its product.
