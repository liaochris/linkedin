Pam Claughton’s Summary

Five years ago, after nearly ten years with a large Boston recruiting firm, I opened Custom Search Group, Inc. in Plymouth, MA. My focus has been on senior level marketing and technology searches from mid to Sr. VP and C level.

We have recently expanded and moved into new office space on Main St. in downtown Plymouth, MA. As part of our expanded offerings, we are excited to provide temporary and/or contract staffing services throughout Massachusetts for administrative as well as technology positions.

Within the technology space, I have a particular interest in search engine technology, mobile applications, e-commerce and other cutting edge web 2.0 focused companies. My clients range from exciting, VC funded start-ups to established market leaders, some of whom use me exclusively.

Marketing/Communications, Product/Project Management, and Software Engineers/Solutions Architects are my most common searches and where I have the deepest networks.

If you are thinking of engaging in a search, and would like to hear about opportunities that might match with your search objectives, please feel free to forward your resume to me in confidence.
