James Long’s Summary

Games Producer with over 16 years’ experience across development and publishing, including Senior Producer positions at ZOO Digital Publishing and Tuna Technologies, Head of Core Technology for Infogrames Sheffield and Lead Programmer on the critically acclaimed first two instalments of “Broken Sword” at Revolution Software. Involved in production, team management, programming and support for dozens of published titles across multiple platforms.

James Long’s Specialties:

Staff management & mentoring; project management; product evaluation & design; troubleshooting; QA & compliance testing; concept & certification submissions; creating manuals; outsourcing; liasing with publishing, development, product support, localisation, recording, QA & certification teams; working with multiple platforms (Wii, DS, PSP, Xbox/360, PC, PS2, J2ME mobile, GameCube, GBA, Playstation, Dreamcast, Amiga, Atari ST...)
