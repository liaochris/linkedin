Amy Uhrich’s Summary

Over the course of my life I have been interested primarily in artistic and literary endeavors. I am a diligent, meticulous individual with a strong creative bent, and while I make a good employee, I thrive on my strong independent streak. My greatest interest lies in self-sufficiency, and promoting and utilizing my personal and professional skills. You will find a detailed representation of these skills below, and I encourage you to contact me if I can assist you in any way.
