Quentin Brown’s Summary

I have been an Internet Marketer for the past 10 Years selling my own products and services online. This has been a great experience and allowed me to live in Thailand for 2 years from 2006 to 2008 teaching orphans and young people how to build websites, develop media and general computer work.

All the students are now placed in jobs or running their own small businesses or charity work.

I hope to go back in march 2010 and will keep working via the Internet.

Our latest project is helping small business to optimize their online presence using inbound Marketing techniques. This is the process of optimizing business websites and developing content and distributing across the Internet to draw customers and prospects to your website so you can start a dialog.

It has been working very well and is reasonable easy to set up and provides long term exposure compared to the traditional short term advertising and marketing strategies.
