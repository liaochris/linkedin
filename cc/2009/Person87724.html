Gena Riede’s Summary

As a full time well seasoned Real Estate Broker I focus on the Sacramento County area, El Dorado Hills, Roseville and Granite Bay with an emphasis on residential homes, luxury home market, gated communities, the active adult communities and investment properties.

I thoroughly enjoy what I do and the people that I meet along the way.

In this age of transparency, it is my goal to make sure that anyone seeking information about Real Estate get the straight scoop in order to make educated decisions that are best for them. That is my goal and one that I work hard to share.

As an excellent negotiator...I can't help but emphasize what my previous clients have always remarked..."I always want to make sure that you are on my side and not on the other side." I pride myself on providing the best service to my clients before, during and after a transaction.
