Karen Talavera’s Summary

Now Follow Synchronicity Marketing on Twitter!
http://twitter.com/SyncMarketing

Karen Talavera,President of Synchronicity Marketing, is a nationally-recognized email and integrated marketing expert, speaker, author and trainer.

Karen employs a unique approach to helping clients apply classical direct marketing principles to emerging digital marketing channels like email, mobile messaging, and Web 2.0. With a particular specialty in email marketing, she has taught legal compliance, best pratices, and tactical application of email to literally thousands of marketing professionals through the Direct Marketing Association and others. Karen also provides custom email marketing education, coaching, and workshops through in-house seminars for major corporations such as ServiceMaster, Kaiser Permanente, AAA, Applied Materials, and Texas Instruments.

If you or your company needs help building, sustaining and expanding customer relationships through an integration of traditional and new media, or requires strategic email marketing expertise, compliance and best practices education Synchronicity Marketing would like to work with you. Karen not only helps companies through the growing pains of new media adoption, but moreover teaches them how to successfully connect both online and offline marketing channels to create a whole greater than the sum of its parts.

It can be done, are you ready to try?
