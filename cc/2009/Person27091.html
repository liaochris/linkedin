  • President and CEO

    (Maritime industry)

    April 2003 — January 2007 (3 years 10 months)

    Teekay Shipping (Australia) is the largest owner and operator of ships in Australia. As Managing Director I was fully responsible for 800 employees, 16 ships, 10 tugs, with gross annual revenue of over $100 million AUD.

    The primary objective and job purpose of the position of Managing Director, Australia is to ensure that the Company fleet operates efficiently in a safe and environmentally sound manner and that it provides a competitive, reliable shipping service in the carriage of crude oil and finished products. Teekay Australia is a profit centre and as such was supported by growing business in ship management and marine services.
