Rui Fontoura’s Summary

My professional goal is to help companies moving from paper based document exchange type of communication with trading partners to fully automated Electronic Documents Exchange processes.
The problem with Electronic Documents Exchange has always been the “Integration Gap” . It is too difficult and it takes too much time to integrate Electronic Documents with the existing ERP’s
At SOFTLIMITS we build software tools to bridge the integration gap, and we provide a Service called Netdocs that links business partners and facilitates the exchange of Electronic Documents like Electronic Invoices with digital signatures and legal value.
