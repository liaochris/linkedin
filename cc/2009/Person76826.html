Parul Shah’s Summary

With more than 10 years of media and marketing experience, Parul Shah co-founded Knee Deep Marketing in 2007.

Passionate about the technology space, she held a range of PR and marketing positions for startups like Garden.com and Imandi.com, and most recently, for Microsoft.

During her six years at Microsoft, Parul launched several consumer-facing products including MSN 8, Microsoft Office OneNote, Microsoft Office Outlook Live and Windows Live Family Safety. Her experience includes everything from product management and planning to developing results-driven go to market strategies.

Most recently, Parul was the group manager for MSN.com where she drove marketing strategy and campaign execution for the MSN homepage and portal properties including Sports, News, Travel, Lifestyle and Health and Fitness.

Prior to entering the consumer technology space, Parul spent several years in the television broadcasting business first producing programming and then moving into sales and management.

She holds an M.A. in Broadcast Communications from San Francisco State University and a B.A. in Mass Communications from the University of California at Berkeley. She lives in Bellevue, Washington with her husband and two children.
