William Kucmierowski’s Summary

Being able to boast over eleven years in the world of professional wrestling is few and far between. Not many individuals can handle the physical aspect, yet the mental and business "pieces of the puzzle" as well. I have been a force to be reckoned with inside the sport of kings being a fan favorite in numerous promotions around the country. Throughout my years, I have been the focus of countless forms of media and have accomplished many impressive feats. As a professional wrestling trainer and mentor, I have guided well over sixty-five wrestling hopefuls' careers; with a few who have actually received contracts or developmental deals to the WWE (World Wrestling Entertainment). I have a professional sales and marketing background that spans over fifteen years including entrepreneurial, corporate, and privately owned business'. I like to think of myself as an innovator who is capable of anything I put my mind to!

Over the last decade… Brimstone has laid a “path of destruction” throughout the world of professional wrestling that is without a shadow of a doubt been more than impressive! He has dominated almost every form of media, and has personally carved the road to success for young hopefuls by training well over 65 students via his former school CMPW [Critical Mass Pro Wrestling], PWR [Pro Wrestling Revolution], and FTW Wrestling. Brimstone is a founder of the stable Critical Mass, and the CoD [Carnival of Destruction]. Re-live some vintage favorites and current classics in this trip down memory lane.
