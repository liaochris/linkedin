Bryan Castillo’s Summary

Choice Title, Inc. has been owned and managed by Sheila Bonnette since 1989. We currently have 6 offices and employ an average of 30-35 employees year round. All employees are trained and qualified to provide superior customer services, both before and after the sale. Flexibility is the main focus of Choice Title, as we believe that our clients should not be overly inconvenienced during the stressful period of buying or selling their homes.

Our closing process is unique in that each department is specialized in a specific area, offering to our clients an excellent quality of service, skill and knowledge. When your contract to Sell or Purchase has been received by Choice Title, a file is opened and all parties are contacted to verify the contract information. The title is then researched in the public records of the appropriate parish and all problems such as liens, judgements and mortgages are cleared before the closing takes place. In addition to the utilization of the latest computer technology and internet packaging, our personnel work diligently to insure a smooth, worry-free closing for all parties concerned.

We will schedule your closing at a time that is most convenient for you, with late hour and weekend appointments available. Our personnel will review all closing figures, insurance coverages and special conditions prior to the closing appointment, in order to address any questions or concerns before you arrive.
