Brent Francese’s Summary

I am a highly experienced, skilled and passionate designer in the field of architecture seeking to widen my knowledge of architecture to further a career into a practicing architect.

I began my career in the field of construction and progressed from a small office environment into larger office experiences. I have worked on small and large residential projects, commercial developments, retail developments and mixed use buildings.

I also build and design blogs, websites and logos, record music and write often in my spare moments.

Brent Francese’s Specialties:

Residential design, mixed use residential design with a strong background in construction and construction systems.

Strong CAD background in Revit 2008, AutoCAD, SketchUp and Vectorworks.

Strong graphic design background in Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Pagemaker.

Strong musical background in Garageband and Ableton Live, live software sampling, drums and percussion.
