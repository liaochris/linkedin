Eric Peltier, a.k.a. the doormouse, is an entrepreneur and marketing innovator with a proven track record, and an extensive scholastic background, in the creation and execution of marketing initiatives in both new and traditional media in order to effectively reach target audiences. He is considered a respected and award winning music industry professional with more than ten years background in marketing; experience in promotion, publicity, project management, entertainment programming, analysis and communication with an extensive online and traditional portfolio.
