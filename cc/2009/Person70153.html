Morris Packer’s Summary

Morris Packer
Mobile Services
THE BONNIER GROUP

Morris Packer, born 1962 i New York and living in Europe since 1968, continues to cross borders. With more than 15 years within the media industry he has closely followed the evolution from desk top publishing to the mobile web (Morris still hates the fact that he was invited by his best friend - who had a scholarship at Cern - to the very first conference about the world wide web but turned it down for a really dumb reason). With a slightly Faustian relationship to technology he loves print and always has a new gadget to show. Since April Fools Day 2004 he runs the department of Bonnier Mobile Services that provides know-how and technology to all brands within The Bonnier Group.

Update: As of the 1st of April 2009 Morris is stationed at TV4 and Expressens joint venture MOBILAB
