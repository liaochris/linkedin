Dear Friend,

A great mind said:

YOU WANT TO BE CONSIDERED THE ONLY ONE DOING WHAT YOU DO

The above has been a guiding principle throughout my professional career.

I was fortunate enough to work with think-tanks from organizations like Harvard Business School, London School of Economics, The Economist, INSEAD, University of Oxford, Chartered Institute of Marketing and many more.

In the last couple of years, I focused mainly on delivering Business Intelligence to Global and local Fortune500 companies in the CEE Region. I was instrumental in organizing over 300 different conferences, round-tables, seminars and workshops, which gathered more than 8,000 people in total.
