Emily Chardell’s Summary

Emily is the president and founder of SFI, Ltd., a marketing research consulting firm. SFI is dedicated to providing clients with actionable research implications to assist in making strategic marketing decisions. The firm conducts both qualitative and quantitative research projects.

Emily specializes in conducting qualitative research studies, including in-person and online focus groups and individual interviews, for a wide range of clients in both consumer and business product and service industries. She brings a unique combination of strengths and skills to her clients, blending extensive marketing experience and perspective plus superior moderating and interviewing ability. She is recognized as having an innate sensitivity to respondents, and an exceptional ability to gain their confidence and engage them in productive discussions.

Key Strengths:
• Extensive marketing background
• Superior moderating & interviewing skills
• Wide range of experience across a broad spectrum of categories and issues
• Utilization of an extensive array of research methodologies and techniques
• Experience with many and diverse targets
• Experience on “both sides of the mirror”
• Ability to quickly digest and comprehend client business situations and marketing research needs
