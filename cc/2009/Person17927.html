Chris Gould’s Summary

Chris Gould is the Global Strategic Sourcing Leader for Hewitt Associates. Hewitt Associates for the last 5 years has been recognized by FORTUNE as one of America's most admired companies and is considered the leader in the HR BPO industry. Mr. Gould has been in HR consulting since 1996 and is considered an industry expert in social media/networking recruiting. He is a requested speaker on topics related to social networking and his work has resulted in press interest including The Wall Street Journal and the SHRM HR Magazine. He is also the founder of the Kansas City Online Community, an online community with over 3200 members. He lives in Overland Park, KS with his wife and 3 children.
