Joseph Keyser’s Summary

For more than 25 years, I have provided a public face and (hopefully) entertaining voice for a wide range of "green issues," whether as a newspaper columnist (more than 10 years as the "GreenMan"), cable television host ("The GreenMan Show" - greenmanshow.com), media relations specialist, magazine and newsletter editor, or spokesperson for national non-profit organizations and state and local government agencies. I have more than 75 on-air television and radio interviews to my credit (many as "America's Compost King"), approximately 150 publications, scores of conference presentations (domestic and international), more than 500 workshops and public presentations and demonstrations, and during all of these years, have also maintained a strong, consistent presence on boards of trustees and advisory boards and committees, and have served numerous roles with volunteer organizations.
