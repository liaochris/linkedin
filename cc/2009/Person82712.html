Rebekah Ford’s Summary

I have a lot of experience in consulting, informing, structuring, designing and building accessible, functional websites and a lot of experience in structuring sound via the internet from my time at BBC Radio (now Audio) & Music Interactive. from leading a very strong team of developers between 2001 and 2006, I have extensive people and project management experience and training. My team and I were responsible for the technical client-side code of the following BBC websites: Radios 1-5live, 6music, 1xtra, Asian Network, BBC7 Music, The Proms, the launch of the Radio Player (now part of the iPlayer) as well as a number of pan-BBC project affects on the department.

I started my career as a runner in a commercial art studio, in the 80s (before the Internet!) delivering and liaising with advertising creatives on storyboards and above the line campaign mock ups. I then worked as a paste up artist for Deadline comic where I also spent time as a Tank Girl lookalike for the magazine, having to shave my head with a Bic razor on several occasions and appearing on the cover of the April 1990 issue! I also enjoyed a fruitful career as a make-up artist during the 90s beautifying models, actors, musicians for photo and video shoots, before changing tack and teaching myself how to build websites.


I also DJ hip hop, old school, funk, soul and disco.

I spent 5 months living in Lisbon in 2006, where I endeavoured to improve my Portuguese language skills as well as discovering more about the country and its people. I have since, been back more times than I can count and considers it my second home
