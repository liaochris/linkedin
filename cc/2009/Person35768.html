George Grattan’s Summary

I offer a unique combination of strategic, marketing, communications, public relations, managerial, and analytical skills honed in the development, environmental, non-profit and academic sectors. I am an experienced writer and a creative colleague used to developing messages and managing outreach efforts to a wide range of internal and external audiences. I am mission-driven, adaptable, inventive, and committed to creating and maintaining positive working relationships.

George Grattan’s Specialties:

Designing and implementing communications campaigns to donors, volunteers, partners, and the general public across a range of media; public speaking; editing and writing for a wide range of audiences, including writing for the web, grant writing, and editing scientific language for the general reader; developing strategic communications partnerships with external stakeholders.
