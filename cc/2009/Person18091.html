Christopher Burbridge’s Summary

I have worked as a freelance graphic designer, a copy editor, a writer, and—for fourteen years—as a computer programmer for the software industry, developing online help systems, websites, relational databases, and custom text processing tools.

In 2005, I started an independent web design and development company, geared toward helping small businesses and startups create a successful presence on the web.

In 2008, I decided to focus exclusively on serving the needs of "web newbies" -- the large body of people who (a) need a website; (b) don't exactly know how to go about getting what they need, and making a success out of it.

I've written The Web Newbie's Success Guide: A 4-Step Process for Achieving Your Internet Goal (free as a download at http://www.RealSimpleSuccess.com), to address the unmet needs of this population.
