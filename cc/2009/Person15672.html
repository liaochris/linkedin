Carole Mahoney’s Summary

The largest part of my experience is as a web marketing manager which required detail oriented analysis skills as well influential writing experience for ad campaigns, online content or copy.

I have largely worked with B2B companies, but I am looking to expand into more B2C as well.The independent streak in me is highly motivated and creativity drives my campaign performance, but it is the focus to detail and ability to see the larger goals that help me to be a productive manager and bring teams together. My love of challenges is a great match with outstanding problem solving and team leadership skills.

I have been described as a hybrid of left brain and right brain.

It has been my professional experience and it is my belief that a learning organization who understands a team environment and recognizes what each person brings to the table has the best chance for growth in a changing marketplace.

One of my personal goals is to identify these organizations and help them to reach their full potential. Currently I am seeking forward thinking companies and individuals that have an appreciation for the value of these concepts and putting them to practice.
