Sarah Lamansky’s Summary

Internet Marketing is one of the most cost effective ways of directly advertising your business...24/7. My job is to help both local and national based businesses get found by creating and executing a complete online strategy. From keyword selection and implementation, website architecture analysis, to devising a link marketing campaign - it's my job to find a balance between form and function so that clients see a return on investment.
