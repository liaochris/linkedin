Antoine Borg’s Summary

I am a highly motivated software architect with 15 years of experience architecting and developing integration projects in the UK, Malta and now Belgium. I have worked on projects in the financial, telephony, marketing and manufacturing sectors both in private industry sector as well as with the public sector.

With a history of designing and documenting internal and external processes and procedures, I have progressed through my career by utilising my communicational and analytical skills to great effect. I am a creative thinker who works hard at improving the processes and systems that I work with to further enhance my, and my colleagues, working environment.

Antoine Borg’s Specialties:

Software architecture, Pattern analysis, Software design, Lecturing, Public speaking, Mule consultancy, SOA consultancy
