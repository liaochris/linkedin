Patricia, C. Scholl-Thompson’s Summary

After spending over 20 years in the European IT-industry watching the rise and fall of many so called "big players" alongside with this ever changing marketplace I have decided to brake up my tents and move on to something more earthly, something that always triggered my inspiration. HORSES :-)

In 2004 I founded HORSELITE Products with the goal to make a difference. Ever since all the efforts at HORSELITE Products are directed towards 100% sustainability within an industry that so far did not care much about environmental issues.

100% sustainability takes 200% motivation and 150% of neverending effort in convincing people that working with and for HORSELITE is the right thing to do.

My days now are not less stressful, but wholesome, because at the end of each day I can look at myself in the mirror knowing I have done my share to work towards a better tomorrow.
