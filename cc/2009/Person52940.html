Jeffrey Miller’s Summary

Internationally-recognized self-protection & personal development consultant focused on providing programs for individuals, groups, and corporate clients. Specializing in general self-protection, womens' self-protection issues, workplace violence defensive tactics training, stress management and life purpose coaching services. My goal is to teach my clients time-tested and proven techniques, strategies, and methods for creating the life they've always dreamed of living, and the skills necessary for protecting that life from anything that might threaten it.

Jeffrey Miller’s Specialties:

Physical security, self-defense and street survival training for security and law enforcement personnel, workplace violence defensive tactics trainer, combat handgun defensive tactics trainer, wilderness survival instructor, leadership and team-building program instructor, life coach, executive life coach, meditation teacher

Jeffrey Miller’s Experience

  • Owner, Consultant

    Warrior Concepts International, Inc.

    (Health, Wellness and Fitness industry)

    October 2004 — Present (4 years 10 months)

    Warrior Concepts has 2 main divisions. The first is the training facility located at 362 Market St., Sunbury, Pa. Here, we provide training programs, seminars, and workshops for private, organizational, and business clients. The second facet of WCI is it's consulting division which provides on-site consulting, program development and training, and presentations for groups, organizations, departments, and corporate clients in the areas of self-defense, workplace violence, stress management, life purpose development, life and executive coaching, as-well-as management, leadership, and team-building programs.

  • Owner & Lead Investigator

    Private investigation, evidence procurement, surveilance, and background verification services to private, legal, and corporate clients. Also provided personal escort (bodyguard) services to private and high-profile clients such as several Tibetan teachers while touring the US (as our State Dept refused to offer protection for fear of upsetting our new friends, the Chinese Gov't.)

  • Owner & Chief Instructor

    Miller's Martial Arts

    (Health, Wellness and Fitness industry)

    November 1989 — August 1999 (9 years 10 months)

    Self-Protection & personal development training for men, women, and children. Focusing on the ancient martial art of Ninjutsu and its practical, real-world uses for modern students in the Western-world.

    After a review of the markets wants and needs, and a realization that I was not offering the same product or services as that of most other martial arts schools, changed name to Warrior Concepts International, Inc, in October of 2004.

  • Military Policeman, Investigator, Unit Self-defense & survival instructor, etc.

    US Army

    (Military industry)

    June 1981 — January 1988 (6 years 8 months)

    Primary military occupational specialty (MOS) was 95B, military policeman. Also executed duties including: criminal investigator, SWAT team member, physical security specialist, military police desk radio and criminal computer operator, and undercover black market and drug suppression agent. Also served on the security team protecting the commanding general during the operation in Grenada, served as a bodyguard for a former Secretary of Defense, several dignitaries, and several members of the Dallas Cowboy Cheerleaders (while on tour with the USO in South Korea). In addition, developed and taught self-defense and wilderness survival programs to military police units on Ft. Bragg, NC and Schloss Kaserne, Butzbach, West Germany.
