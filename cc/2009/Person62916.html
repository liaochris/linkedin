Mario Martinez II’s Summary

Mario Martinez II is Chief Executive Officer & Co-Founder of 360 Vantage and is responsible for the overall vision, strategy, and execution of the business.

Having over 13 years of enterprise class experience with multi-billion dollar International and U.S. public pharmaceutical companies, Martinez brings with him sales & marketing technology management expertise ranging from end-user and infrastructure technology operations & support to customer relationship management and business intelligence solutions. He has served on both the vendor and client sides and has been responsible for operating environments & projects with over 500 sales representatives.

Today, with his diverse background, business experience, leadership, and proven track record of success, he is focused on leading the 360 Vantage team to achieve the mission and build a first class technology solutions company where clients, employees, and shareholders will enjoy great success.

Mario Martinez II’s Specialties:

Technology systems management within the pharmaceutical vertical including SFA, CRM, BI systems, mobile solutions including hardware and software, data center operations, project management, operations management, training, negotiations, and leadership.
