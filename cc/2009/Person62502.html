Marcus Cake’s Summary

Marcus Cake is also Head of Research for Newport Capital. Newport Capital is an Australian technology investment banking & corporate advisory firm established in 1989. For further information, please see http://www.newportcapital.com.au .

MarcusCake.com builds online social networks for financial markets and economic development. Let's accelerate the transition to Web 4.0 and transform the world? Humanity has a 50% chance of survival beyond 2100. We need to build the Web 3.0 online social, industry and political networks on the critical path to Web 4.0 and pull in the next stage of economic development, environmental sustainability, awareness, life, work and global governance. We have three projects to transform financial markets. We will show you how to build the others! What contribution will you make? For further information, please refer to http://www.marcuscake.com .
