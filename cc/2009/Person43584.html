Jan Thomas, PhD, CSP’s Summary

Occupational safety and health professional with doctorate and over 30 years of experience. Career has included significant work with Federal OSHA, Virginia OSHA (VOSH), a large urban university and over 15 years of consultation with public and private sector employers.
This background includes regulatory compliance, accident and fatality investigations, development of policy and procedures, program management and extensive experience in education, training, research, technical writing, forensic consultation and expert witness testimony.
Hazard auditing has been performed in all sectors of work including heavy construction, homebuilding, manufacturing and shipyard employment.
Certified Safety Professional (#14267).
Authorized by OSHA to teach the 10- and 30-hour Construction and General Industry Outreach courses.

Jan Thomas, PhD, CSP’s Specialties:

Construction Safety Subject Matter Expert, Safety Auditing, Machine Guarding, OSHA/VOSH Compliance and Abatement, Employee Safety Training and public speaking.
