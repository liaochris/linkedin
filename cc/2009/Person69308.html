Chris Mitchell’s Summary

I am a Washington, DC-based patent attorney providing legal services to clients throughout the world. I have experience serving a broad spectrum of clients, including publicly and privately held companies, law firms, non-profit organizations, and individuals. I focus my practice in all areas of intellectual property law, with an emphasis on client counseling, intellectual property procurement, licensing, and litigation support. My patent prosecution experience is primarily in business methods, computer software, and computer hardware. My training is in the computer science and business fields. Prior to starting this practice, Mr. Mitchell served in the patent practice of one of Northeast Ohio’s largest law firms, and later with a top 15 U.S. patent firm located in Washington, DC (source: Top Patent Firms 2008, Intellectual Property Today). He has worked in the intellectual property field since 2001.
