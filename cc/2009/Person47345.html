Jim Erb’s Summary

My business relationships are built on integrity, professionalism and a shared pursuit of realized potential. Every day my clients make choices to protect their families and their businesses, and I strive over the long term to provide the information and direction that lends focus to their future. — Jim Erb

Financial success doesn't happen by chance. It has much more to do with choice. Transforming aspirations into actuality takes acumen, hard work, discipline and strategic financial planning.

To help clients achieve their objectives, my firm works to serve as a friend and an advocate. Life’s complexities leave little room for "standard" approaches, so we use a four-step approach in working with our clients:

1) Discovering your values, attitudes and preferences concerning your financial assets.
2) Clarifying your vision for the future.
3) Connecting your vision, values and goals with the planning process.
4) Implementing an action plan that seeks to produce your desired results.

Our planning process is unique – we start at a different plane than other advisors. We start above the planning horizon and focus on helping you clarify your Mission, Vision, Values and Goals.

Then we focus on the issues below the horizon – Strategies, Tactics and Tools.

By staying above the horizon and gaining clarity around your goals, Jim can help you make wise choices about the solutions below the planning horizon.

Working in the best interests of our clients is our business, and we take this role very seriously.

Whether you're concerned for your own planning needs, your family or your business, we can help you find the right answers.
