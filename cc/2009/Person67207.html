Michael Cheek’s Summary

Good designers, technical developers, creative artists, organized project managers or effective communicators can be found easily.

But how many web designers understand how a visitor will read a site? How many artists comprehend technical tasks, can explain requirements to the developers, and manage a detailed project from start to launch and well beyond? How many creative directors bring an imaginative vision that’s well outside the proverbial box but grounded enough to plot a roadmap to success?

With Michael Cheek, art and technology merge effortlessly; exemplary communication skills intersect with extreme creativity.

Michael Cheek offers…
… marketing prowess with knowledge in branding and corporate identity development, strategic planning, positioning, research analysis, advertising, public relations and promotion.
… years of graphic design in all media with particular aptitudes in logo development, branding and identity.
… technical know-how for websites including site mapping, logical infrastructure, navigation design, usability, wireframes, storyboarding and search-engine optimization.
… more-than-enough management experience including team building, project management and budgetary processes.

Michael Cheek’s Specialties:

Online strategy, online marketing and communication, branding and identity, creativity, team-building, management.
