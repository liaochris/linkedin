Michael Litman’s Summary

Michael Litman is the Social Media Strategist for Consolidated PR working in their Digital practice. He is passionate about educating the company and it’s clients on best practices on the social web and engagement with online media.

Recent campaigns include working with MTV Staying Alive and Spotify, blogger relations for HSBC, amplifying film launches digitally for Disney, online reputation management for KLM, a graduate employment campaign for the Department of Business Innovation and Skills (BIS), and a youth-focussed health programme for the Scottish Government.

Previous experience includes award-winning campaigns for clients including Smirnoff, Marmite, Bulleit Bourbon, Westfield London, ShoZu and Big Yellow Storage.

Active on the London PR / social media scene attending events and networking which has immense value in developing professional connections, existing friendships and knowledge growth.
