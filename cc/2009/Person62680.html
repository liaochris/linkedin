Maria Montano’s Summary

I am currently the Web Master for Fine Print Imaging, a Colorado Fine Art Printing Company. We specialize in creating digital fine art prints for proffesional artists and photographers.

We are currently working on Search Engine Optimization, and creating a more vital internet presence.

Fine Print is also dedicated to the conservation movement. Having been involved in conservation related issues for over 20 years, we are proud to support artists and photographers who are using their talents for grass roots conservation causes.

Digital Photography, Community Action/Planning, Dreamweaver, Photoshop CS3, Website Development and Design
