Laurie Sheppard’s Summary

Master Certified Coach with focus on positive change, provides individuals and groups life coaching and career strategizing services. Speciality in mid-level professional women entrepreneurs, women managers and women returning to the workforce.

Additional services include Positive Thinking Way process to quickly eliminate negative patterns, beliefs and emotions to improve behaviors, performance and goals' achievement.

Author of two books, professional speaker, outside consultant trainer for Thinkx, innovation and problem resolution company.

Visit http://www.creatingatwill.com/blog to view our Change-maker's Blog and post your comments. See our homepage sidebar to sign up for our free Women Entrepreneurs' Newsletter.

We also have articles and other free resources are also available.

  • Project Manager for Los Angeles program

    The Youth at Risk Foundation

    (Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    June 1986 — November 1986 (6 months)

    Took on a short-term management position, hired by the Board of Directors, to organize and launch this training program. I enrolled over thirty volunteers. This included a doctor and nurse to give pre-course exams to all volunteers and and be on course for this 4-day event in the mountains. The LA inner city youth selected, participated in this outward-bound style ropes course and class work. I and all volunteers did the ropes course as well, aimed at team building, leadership practice development, etc.

    I assisted in the parent-student interviews to help select the candidates to participate in this program. While at the course I managed onsite activities and problem-solved. I stayed briefly following the training to help connect trainees with their year-long adult mentors.

    There is no center in Los Angeles now, but the Phoenix office is the original center that still exists and does great work for these inner-city youth who learn to reclaim themselves and their lives.
