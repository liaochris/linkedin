Greg Thorson’s Summary

Most of my experience has been working at the point where creativity and technology meet.

My current full-time position is as a Technical Producer in ABC TV Networks's Sales and Marketing Division. I execute enhancements to, and maintain, http://stagepass.abc.go.com/. This is ABC's website for the advertising community.

Previously, with eSchool online, I have eight years of experience as a Web Producer, developing complex multimedia products for high-profile textbook publishers and school districts. In this position, I have coordinated the efforts of departments across the company, and have taken ownership of all products from initial conceptualization to final delivery. One product I oversaw, "Teaching Algebra to All Students," was a CODiE finalist earlier this year.

In addition, I have extensive experience with building dynamic websites with PHP, AJAX, and Flash, both as a consultant and working independently.
