Robyn McCamy’s Summary

Robyn began her career in the hospitality industry 14 years ago with a well known Nashville restaurant. She quickly worked her way through the ranks to Director of Special Events for Mere Bulles and Director of Marketing for Southern Restaurant Group. She brings more than ten years of restaurant operations, marketing and public relations experience.

Robyn joined Destination Nashville in 2004. Robyn’s ability to engage in collaborative inspiration with clients and her reputation for flawless execution continues to win her many admirers. Along with experience on the communications and membership committees, Robyn is the Vice President of Education for the Tennessee Chapter of Meeting Professionals International. She is also extremely involved with the industry leader in Destination Management, Global Event Partners. With enthusiasm to spare and a passion for what she calls the “memory-making business,” Robyn thrives on working directly with her clients to craft every detail of their program with a goal to create memorable experiences for each attendee.

When Robyn is not working, she is spending time with her nine year old daughter, Rachel
