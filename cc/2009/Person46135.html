Jenny Pittam’s Summary

As the marketing manager for the zac team, a medium-sized real estate company, I embrace my daily job of increasing community awareness of our company and generating as much potential business as possible for our agents. My marketing focuses on lead generation (resulting in listings and sales), which is born from a myriad of efforts, including managing our websites, producing all internal and external marketing materials, advertising, marketing campaigns, working with my boss to develop and evaluate current and future plans, and more. I enjoy organizing and working with others and have the pleasure of hiring and managing our small marketing team.

In the future, I would like to utilize my expertise in marketing, my leadership experience in the Army, and my passion for the outdoors and preservation of our planet's resources to find a job with a company that works to support and promote environmental issues. For now, during my off-time, I research and pursue green issues on my blog www.OurGreenAtlanta.com with a focus on local Atlanta and Decatur issues.
