Torstein D. Kongshem’s Summary

Torstein is an experienced Engagement manager working 17 years with large national and international corporations of banking and finance, utilities and risk management.

He held the position of Assistant General Manager for IT projects and systems on international cash management in one of the largest financial institutions in the Nordic region.

He held the position of Chief of Projects in an IT consultancy company that delivered solutions and services to the power market in northern Europe.

He has lead a team of software specialists, onshore and offshore, in the migration of more than a dozen legacy systems’ data into one, for the world’s largest risk management company.

Torstein has extensive experience in procuring and managing offshore consultants for the purpose of achieving the best balance between capability, capacity and price. He is also very experienced in contract negotations.

Torstein is a Bachelor of Computer Science from the University of Oslo, and has an economics degree from the Norwegian School of Business Administration, NHH.

He is a results-oriented and goal-directed manager. He has a great ability to motivate people to get things done, and cooperates easily with people from different environments and cultures.
