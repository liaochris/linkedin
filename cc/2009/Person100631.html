Tracy Cavanaugh’s Summary

Tracy Cavanaugh CMPS, CMP

Tracy Cavanaugh, founder of The CS Advisory Group at AFFG, has committed herself to creating a unique process for her mortgage clients based on expert advice, accountability and long term relationships. With a passionate focus on helping to elevate the level of professionalism in her industry,Tracy believes that if she is helping her borrowers to create the largest debt in their life, then she has a responsibility to help them manage that debt and to serve as a resource for them throughout the life of their loan. Dedicated to her client’s success, Tracy’s process is based on a collaborative approach where she works with her client’s other professional advisors to ensure transparency and maximum client benefit. Her team approach has redefined the level of confidence that her client’s experience.

Tracy graduated cum laude from Kean University and has earned the designations of both Certified Mortgage Planning Specialist (CMPS) and Certified Mortgage Planner (CMP). She holds her New Jersey Mortgage Banking License for both first and second mortgages and is a licensed California Real Estate Salesperson. She is a principal of America's First Funding Group and is a member of the Certified Mortgage Planning Specialist Institute and the Beverly Hills Chamber of Commerce.
