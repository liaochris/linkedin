Steve Levin’s Summary

Steve Levin is an angel investor, advisor, entrepreneur and start-up executive. He has founded and developed successful businesses across a broad spectrum of products and services. Steve has also helped some of the worlds largest companies develop and launch new business ventures in marketing, software and business services.

Steve is currently the CEO of Acara Global, an investment and innovation accelerator that creates new platforms for breakthrough products and customer experiences.
