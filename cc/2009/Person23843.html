David Boardman’s Summary

David Boardman is designer of services and interactions at MIT Mobile Experience Lab. His research is focused on the wide intersection merging urban environment and social relations with technology. His skills in interaction design are merged with a user-centered approach based on social sciences and semiotics. He received a BA in Media & Communication Studies in Torino and a MA degree in Interaction Design at Domus Academy / Interaction Design Institute in Milan. His arts and design projects were exhibited in Venice, Santiago de Chile, Amsterdam, Sidney, La Habana, Milan and Florence.

David Boardman’s Specialties:

Design Analysis & Research; Futurecasting & Scenarios; Concept development; Prototyping; UX; Visual design & Communication
