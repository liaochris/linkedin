Mindy J. Selinger’s Summary

***Note:*** I welcome your invitation but *Please* read "Contact Settings" at the bottom before extending an invitation...Thank you!
*********************
In 2002 Mindy started compiling the San Diego Networking Guide, as a tool for her coaching clients who wanted to attend networking events.

In July of 2008 she started working on creating regional Networking Guides across the country, launching NetworkingEventFinders.com in October, 2008.

With that expansion, she is in the process of writing a book on the networking skills she has been teaching for a number of years. The target publishing date is December, 2009
