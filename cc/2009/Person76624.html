Perry Anne Norton’s Summary

Creation of compelling audio for use as a unique and cutting-edge marketing or training tool. PanRight utilizes over 20 years of voiceover narration, production, digital editing, and music experience, consulting with businesses locally and internationally on creative uses of audio for increased exposure and retention rates. Founder speaks at teleconferences and is a freelance writer on voiceover production. PanRight provides a one-stop, inexpensive solution for talent, studio, engineer and editor; no location is an obstacle with digital audio technology. Podcasts, TV and radio ads, audio newsletters, audio books, tutorials, voice prompts, educational and children's media, archival material, web promotion, English for speakers of other languages; character voices and dialects. Production done in state-of-the-art studio. Custom demos upon request. Same-day delivery available for many projects via email, FTP or overnight CD; file formats include MP3, AIFF and WAV.
