Steven Salter’s Summary

Senior level Executive Recruiter and operational leader with over seventeen years international business experience; thirteen of which have been within the global executive recruitment industry.

Detailed industry experience in Management Consultancy, Systems Integration, Technology Advisory, and Global Outsourcing sectors; accomplished in identifying, winning, and leading campaigns covering executive mapping and name generation, retained search and selection, contingency search, advertised selection, and interim management.

In addition has extensive experience in the creation of resource and headcount strategies, creation of large-scale sourcing strategies, auditing existing resourcing and recruitment strategies, designing and facilitating assessment centres, Identifying and measuring capabilities, competencies and behaviours; competency based interviewing, and utilizing psychometric based tools for management development, succession planning and recruitment decision making.
