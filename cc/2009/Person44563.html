Jayne Leighton Herd’s Summary

Jayne Leighton Herd founded Chez-Vous in 2005 with Laurent Stadelmann (http://www.linkedin.com/in/laurentstadelmann).

Combining skills, experiences and passions, they created a vision for Chez-Vous to offer services to find the right property (home search & relocation), style it (interior design & original art) and maintain it (plumbing, heating & electrical services).

Jayne has a creative background in property styling, art and design, and a corporate background in marketing communications, advertising and design.

Creative expression is essential to Jayne! And she has had the advantage of combining creativity with solid business experience. She began her career in marketing and is experienced in many aspects including advertising, branding, and graphic design. And as a professionally-qualified (CIM) marketer, she knows how best to market and sell a product/service.

In tandem with her corporate career, Jayne continued to develop her love of painting and interior styling. Now a talented, professional artist, Jayne enjoys being commissioned to create paintings for private and business clients. She frequently exhibits her work and her latest paintings are on her online art galleries: www.art-chez-vous.co.uk.

Jayne had been styling her own properties for several years, when she became a resource for family, friends and then acquaintances. As requests for her help with property styling increased, she realised how beneficial her skills could be to others.

Growing frustrated by company politics, Jayne realised that she wanted to run her own businesses and concentrate her working life on her passions for art, design and property, using these to benefit others.

So now she combines her marketing, advertising and design experience with her property expertise, style and artistic flair to help clients to make the most of and enjoy their homes.

Jayne trained in property styling with expert, Ann Maurice, The House Doctor™, and has studied interior design.
