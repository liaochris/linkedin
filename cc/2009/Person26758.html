Dina Henry Scott, PMP’s Summary

A creative and innovative Business Professional with extensive people, process, project and change management experience in a variety of environments. Proven ability to bring cross-functional teams to a high level of performance through a collaborative approach. Thirteen (13) years of experience and progressive responsibility in health care and computer manufacturing industries. Cross-divisional experience in project leadership including acquisitions, major technology initiatives, business process re-engineering programs and new product launches. Focuses on achieving value by using innovative and fresh approaches to business problems. Believes balance between building effective processes, developing strong team talent and efficient project execution leads to business success. Develops and instructs on project leadership topics via mentoring sessions, classes, workshops and podcasts.
