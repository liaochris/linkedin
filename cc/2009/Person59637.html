David Gibson’s Summary

David Gibson is a technology product manager specializing in changing education. He develops products and business models to move education into the information wealth system. He has a passion for applying computer technology to business and customer problems with over fourteen years experience in the education marketplace. David currently serves as Group Product Manager for User Certification at Microsoft where his team is focused on Microsoft Office certification, entry-level academic certifications, and accreditation of certification.
