Patrick Martin’s Summary

**Patrick looking for Business Angels / Investors looking to make investments for a minimum $1,000,000+ plus Australian Dollars in projects with clear exit strategies and strong and competent teams**

**Please do not contact me with employment offers / no recruiters. Interested solely in board roles, equity participation, or investment opportunities**

Currently Patrick is pursuing postgraduate coursework at the University of Sydney, focusing on Entrepreneurship, Commerce, and International Business in his Masters degree.

He is actively engaged as a Director at the Sydney University Young Entrepreneurs Society, playing a key role in helping the organization grow and expand its reach in its ability to motivate more students and young people to become entrepreneurs.

He is also a judge on the Sydney Uni Apprentice and the annual university-wide Genesis business plan competition, evaluating many pitches and investment proposals.

An aspiring entrepreneur at heart, he is passionate about new business opportunities and concepts that are global in nature, scalable, and which have strong execution viability.

Patrick is fluent in English, German, Spanish and Portuguese. While a child and teen, Patrick grew up in Germany, Brazil, Mexico, and the Dominican Republic, and later called the United States and Australia home. He is passionate about meeting and interacting with people from diverse cultures and backgrounds.

Contact Patrick to discuss business proposals or partnership opportunities in early-stage or growth ventures, referrals to one of his contacts, or simply to get some good travel tips!
