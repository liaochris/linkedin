Lindsey Karberg’s Summary

Lindsey Karberg is an established public relations (PR) and marketing professional for one of Phoenix Business Journal’s top ranked Valley PR firms, Amendola Communications, named “Best-of-the-Best” national small PR agency in 2007 and 2008 by PRSourceCode’s prestigious “Top Tech Communicators” study. Lindsey is a social media guru, and the bulk of her industry experience includes healthcare, healthcare IT, medical aesthetics, pharma/biotech and franchise systems.

Outside of her career, Lindsey spends her free time offering needed services to local art programs through the Business Volunteers for the Arts. As a proponent of educational and social initiatives, Lindsey has also worked oversees with the South African Counsel for the Prevention of Alcoholism & Drug Dependency and has actively supported nutrition-education outreach offered by the American Diabetes Association. In her free time, Lindsey is a salsa dancing fiend and an avid runner who annually participates in P.F. Chang’s Rock ‘n’ Roll marathon to benefit The Leukemia & Lymphoma Society.

Lindsey attended Arizona State University (ASU) where she received a B.A. in Political Science with a focus in International Studies and a Minor in English Linguistics. She continues educational advancement through graduate courses at ASU, as well as professional development opportunities offered by the Public Relations Society of America.
