Trevor Byrne’s Summary

Customer-focused, hands-on manager with a track record of creating and driving change while reducing costs and increasing productivity and profitability. Able to generate high value from IT through strategic planning, staffing, and project management. History of successful partnering with business executives in the development and deployment of high quality, cost-effective, mission critical IT solutions to support corporate objectives.
