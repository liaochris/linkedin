Harold Dimpel’s Summary

• founder of mHITs and responsible for leading the mHITs commercialisation process.

• entrepreneur with a successful track record in the establishment and commercialisation of a range of business ventures for over 18 years.

• experience in the Information and Communication Technologies (“ICT”) industry across a broad range of sectors including mobile data communications, mobile content service provisioning, application development, Internet Service Provision, Television Broadcasting, GPS telematics, location based technologies and fibre optic telecommunications systems.

Specific Experience:

• founder and director of mymobilephone Pty Ltd - one of Australia’s first mobile content download portals. The website was the first in Australia to allow content purchases by SMS text message and the first mobile portal to sell Java game downloads in Australia. The company has won numerous business awards and award nominations.

• director and major shareholder Goldweb Communications Pty Ltd, one of the oldest and longest continually operating Internet Service providers in the ACT region. Led an investment and a subsequent successful trade sale of the company.

• founding partner of Maxitrak Pty Ltd and led the development and commercialisation of leading edge GSM based generation an automatic vehicle location based technology platform. The technology is used in a range of high security applications across Australia including the armored vehicle fleets and mission critical applications.

• connected professionally in local business circles, community groups and organisations and have presented at a number of events and conferences including MEGA (www.mega.org.au)
