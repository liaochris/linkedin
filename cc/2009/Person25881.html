Dennis Purvine’s Summary

I am an experienced business executive and consultant with more than 30 years in the trenches. I have seen the inside of 100's of companies, both large and small, across multiple industries. Currently, I am focused on small businesses, specializing in business operations, management and finance. My goal is to help my clients address the internal roadblocks that inhibit growth and build a sound operational foundation that positions them to make the leap to the next level of business success. In addition to specific guidance on how to run a business efficiently and effectively, I help my clients take a step back and see the business forest for the trees. As an outside advisor and executive peer, I can also help my clients identify potential issues, spot trends and see where the business is heading in the future, then redirect it when necessary. Essentially, I am a Chief Operations Officer (COO) or Chief Financial Officer (CFO) for hire for small businesses who need this skill, but not on a full time basis.
