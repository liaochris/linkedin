Bill Wittur’s Summary

I'm an Internet Marketing Expert, with 13+ years experience in the industry. I have experience with media buying and planning, strategic marketing and ecommerce activities and writing for blogs and other media. I have had hands on experience with most tools related to online advertising, with "speaking knowledge" of ad trafficking systems such as DoubleClick's DART, 24/7's OAS and OpenAds and cursory understanding of production requirements / guidelines. Translation: I can work with both marketers and traffickers.

I have a thorough knowledge of SEM tools (with a focus on Google, MSN & Yahoo) and a core focus of my business is to help clients and agencies appreciate the importance of effective search bid management. Today, many people leave this to the search companies, which I feel is a grave mistake.

I live in London, Ontario, but my company has clients throughout Ontario and North America. I hope to add European clients because I'm usually up at 4AM and eventually want to move there.
