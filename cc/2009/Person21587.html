Cyril Onwubiko’s Summary

Dr. Cyril is a Security Professional with substantial R&D, consulting and practical experience in attack analysis, data fusion, computer network defence, intelligence and security informatics. Cyril is SC cleared and a CESG CLAS consultant. He is the author of the book "Security Framework for Attack Detection in Computer Networks"

He has broadened R&D and hands-on experiences in various aspects of internetworking, information security and information assurance. He has published numerous peer-reviewed articles on computer and network security at different journals and conferences (see: http://www.research-series.com/cyril).

Cyril Onwubiko’s Specialties:

Computer Network Security, Information Security, Network Forensics, Security Information and Event Management (SIEM) Solutions, Threat Analysis, Vulnerability Assessment & Risk Management.
