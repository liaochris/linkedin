Karen Tkaczyk’s Summary

Freelance translator from French into English and Spanish into English, specializing in chemistry. Translation of texts relating to the chemical industry, including intellectual property, and most often in the areas of pharmaceutical and cosmetic chemistry.
2009-2011 President of the Nevada Interpreters and Translators Association

Translation, French, Spanish, British English, US English, Chemistry, Pharmaceuticals, Cosmetics, Biotech, Patents, Intellectual Property, Patent Litigation, Freelance work, NITA
