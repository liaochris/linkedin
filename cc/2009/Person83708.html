Richard Randall’s Summary

Business and managment consultant and senior executive who has had full P&L responsibility for a $125 million manufacturing business with plants in the U.S. and Europe and a global sales, marketing and distribution network. Experience includes strategic planning and leading execution at all levels of the organization. Recognized for effective cross-functional team building, breaking down organizational silos and cultural barriers, and for leadership using world-class management processes. Led international management teams recognized with ITT’s Chaiman's Award for Growth, ITT's Gold Ring of Quality for operational excellence, and ITT's Engineered for Life Award for new product development. Personally received ITT’s Gold Ring of Quality individual award. In 2006 founded New Level Advisors, a business and management consulting practice. Focused on strategic planning, operational excellence, change management, executive coaching and team building. Regional markets in and around South Central Pennsylvania include York, PA; Hanover, PA; Lancaster, PA; Harrisburg, PA;Reading, PA; Philadelphia, PA and Baltimore, MD. Seeking consulting referrals and advisory board opportunities with a focus on privately held companies or small divisions of public companies.

Richard Randall’s Specialties:

Value-maximizing strategic planning grounded in the principles of value-based management. Quote to cash operational excellence including sales and operations planning, lean six sigma and aggressive cash management. Leadership and change management. Innovative new product development, launch and product portfolio management.
