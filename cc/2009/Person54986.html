Kevin Dobbs’s Summary

Experienced technology executive. Nearly 20 years of success managing sales, marketing, business development, mergers and acquisitions, product marketing and operational functions for both public and start-up companies.

Highly knowledgeable regarding SaaS [Software as a Service], on-demand business models as well as enterprise-class software applications for talent management, HCM, CRM, Financials and ERP.

Adept at establishing new market categories as well as raising capital and working with venture capitalists and private equity firms.
