Daniele Florio’s Summary

Configuration Manager and VAS Services Integration Manager for WIND TELECOMUNICAZIONI S.p.A.

I'm currently working in "VAS & Messaging Application Management" group that performs integration and performance tests for VAS WIND systems. I am the reference for all external and content providers that use WIND to send free and charged SMS and MMS through delivery WIND platforms.

In particular, I follow integration flow with external providers and I am the configuration manager for WIND VAS services like Sanremo, Miss Italia, and more other formats TV and for the donation events like TELETHON and other ONLUS associations initiatives.

For hobby I am particularly interested in user centred design methodologies, processes and tools, infact I'm started my experience like PERL programmer.

Always interested in innovative, technology advanced, cutting edges projects in Italy or outside.
