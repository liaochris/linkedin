Nicky Jameson’s Summary

I specialize in writing direct response website copy and content so that websites convert more visitors into leads and/or sales. I have 15 years experience of marketing, and writing in the technology, real estate, publishing, financial services and logistics industries.

I write persuasive intelligent online marketing copy - primarily for Business to business companies - to make their web sites more effective and helping clients stay profitable and successful.

What clients like about me is that I "get it" when it comes to their business. What I don't know I make it my job to research. I help them speak to their prospects directly - and that makes for powerful copy.

I'm the author of the 19 "New" Rules of Social Media Copywriting, a special report touching on what marketers need to know to generate leads using Social Media Marketing.

I blog on Social Media Marketing, focusing on how businesses can use it strategically. I also blog about copywriting, technology and business success in general. I actually have two blogs, so I know a tidy bit about blogging.
Find out more about me on my website and my blog.
