Jennifer Lee’s Summary

Awaken your inner muse with Artizen Coaching. Certified life coach, artist, writer and yogini, Jennifer Lee, reconnects professional and entrepreneurial women to their passions and purpose through creativity and coaching. Using her expressive arts envisioning approach, she blends creative elements like visualization, drawing, collage and book arts with her coaching, workshops and innovative products to help clients tap into their greatest potential and live their most authentic, full life.

She is the creator of the Right-Brain Business Plan and the Unfolding Your Life Vision Kit.

As an independent consultant/trainer/leadership development coach, Jennifer has co-designed and co-facilitated a year-long leadership program for a Chicago-based non-profit serving adults with mental illness. She has led MBTI team building sessions. She has also led the Ladies Who Launch Incubator intensive workshop in Oakland.

Valuing the mind/body/spirit connection, Jennifer became a certified Ashtanga yoga instructor through San Francisco's It's Yoga Studio. She has been a guest lecturer/workshop leader for the "Manifesting Your Dreams" portion of It’s Yoga’s globally recognized teacher training program.

Jennifer is a featured coach in the book "The Girl's Guide to Kicking Your Career into Gear" and a featured artist in "The Vision Board: The Secret to an Extraordinary Life."
