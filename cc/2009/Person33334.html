Kimberly Douglas, SPHR’s Summary

I have a passion for helping team leaders bring out the best in those who work for them. This means making sure everyone knows and is committed to where we are going as a team - and they know how they need to work together to get there.

Clients at organizations such as AT&T, Coca-Cola, Home Depot, United Way, UPS, and even the US Marine Corps hire me to facilitate or to speak – to dramatically improve their business performance.

Wiley just published my first book, The Firefly Effect - a guidebook for managers with key insights and tools for building teams that work creatively and cooperatively together, produce tangible results, and make employees proud to be a part of something bigger and more meaningful than just 'work'.

I believe in giving back to my community - and fulfill on that promise by providing pro bono consulting and facilitation services to such organizations as Leadership Atlanta, the Partnership Against Domestic Violence, and The Westminster Schools.

Kimberly Douglas, SPHR’s Specialties:

* Team Effectiveness - establish trust, resolve conflict, increase innovation and productivity

* Strategic Planning and Execution - create compelling plans that get done

* Creative Problem-Solving - proven tools for tackling tough business challenges

* Communicate with Impact - in person, on a team, in public

* Conduct Assessments (Herrmann Brain Dominance Instrument and MBTI) to learn how each member of the team thinks, reacts under pressure, makes decisions, and communicates
