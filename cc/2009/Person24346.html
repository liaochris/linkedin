David Leech, PMP, ITIL’s Summary

Experienced Project / Program Manager with over ten years of delivering high value, high visibility projects and programs to Fortune 500 Companies. Have occupied senior organizational roles, both as Global Manager of the Project Management Team, and as Lead Program Manager to key client program initiatives. Prior to Project Management, spent five years working on an Investment Bank trading floor, as technical support and then middle office operations (supporting traders, middle office, financial and developers).

Project and program management experience extends to data center migrations, large scale application rollouts, virtualization, automation, security upgrades, monitoring, capacity planning, application and infrastructure integration and trading floor buildouts.

These roles have required the direction of multiple teams from within a matrix management organization or from external vendors, and working closely with the client throughout the project lifecycle from business case development, ROI and scope definition to delivery, handover to operations, development of KPIs, and lessons learned.

Possess excellent interpersonal and communication skills, a goal-oriented, diligent and resourceful Program Manager with a solid understanding of business practices, project management, negotiation and people skills.

Non financial industry.
