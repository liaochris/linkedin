Norm Rice’s Summary

As your real estate agent, you always remain my priority. Just what does that mean? To me it is simple - You deserve open & honest communication, experience, professionalism, but above all someone who takes you seriously as a buyer or seller. Isn't respect a big part of all that?

I can tell you all day that I know the neighborhoods, schools, market conditions, zoning regulations and what it takes to make the deal go through, but that is all secondary. Being an active local chamber of commerce member, or a member of civic service clubs such as the Lions & Kiwanis indeed keep me well in tune with our community and can be of value to you, howver, still in the end, Quality service means putting you first. It takes a full-time commitment - anything less and you'd be short changed. As an agent, I'm able to "work the market" for you, keeping up-to-date with new listings and conditions as they impact the market.

It would be my honor to serve as your real estate agent. Let's get together to talk about your home buying and/or selling plans.

Call me directly at (503) 348-0158 (if you are local), 1-800-339-0980 (if you are out the local area) or send me an e-mail at NormRice@mac.com We'll set-up a time to meet that is easy and convenient for you.
