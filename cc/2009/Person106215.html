Yvette Agostini’s Summary

In this span of my professional life I'm working on both sides of my experiences. After some years focused mainly in the Information Security and IT business, I'm now also active again on the renewables energies.

Information Security professional experience and goals:
I gained a substantial experience on information risk analysis and organizational security through a range of industry: financial, TLC, travel&leisures, military.
Actually my technical skills are Unix OS systems administration and integration, IT incident management tools and techniques (including digital forensic), ethical hacking (especially focused on attack strategies and results summarizations), systems and software documentation.
I evaluate offers regarding Information security projects taking place in Italy or Europe.
I would be very pleased to approach the field of financial computer forensics.

Renewable Energies professional experience and goals:
I have worked on a number of evaluations of technical and project issues of existing or planned renewable energies plants, in order to produce due diligences to be used in the evaluation of investments on these activities.
Main technical focus is on SCADA systems for renewable energies and wind turbine control systems, but I have an overall good knowledge of the whole wind and photovoltaic energy conversion process and of the Balance Of Plant issues for wind farms and PV fields.
My goal is to expand my knowledge of the renewable energies industry, mainly wind and photovoltaic applications, through due diligence and consultancy activity.

Yvette Agostini’s Specialties:

Renewable Energies: assessments and evaluation of wind plant and PV fields design and build .
Wind turbines industry assessment and evaluation.
SCADA and control systems for renewable energies.
Project management.

Information Security: Risk assessment, incident handling, digital forensics, penetration testing. Unix/Linux sysadmining, network administration, firewalling, ids technologies, log analysis, wifi security.
