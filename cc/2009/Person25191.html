Dean Guadagni’s Summary

With a business development career spanning over 20 years, my goal now is to be the driving force behind companies, entrepreneurs, and job seekers in their quest to deliver their value message to their strategically targeted audience through the use of Web 2.0 social media sites and blogging.

A member of the content syndication service, Blogburst.com, I author a nationally recognized business self help blog, as well as co-author our Inner Architect blog. I have had over 140 articles published by mass media agencies Reuters.com, FoxBusiness.com, Nielsen.com, Chicago Sun-Times, Computer Shopper, and Ziff Davis Enterprise. Prior to joining Inner Architect, I worked with management consulting firm Domus Consulting Group, helping real estate brokerages and affiliates, develop technology marketing strategies in real estate.

I am a frequent public speaker and workshop facilitator on such topics as Web 2.0 and social media strategies, job search, blogging, and networking.
