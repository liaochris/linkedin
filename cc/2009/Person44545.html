Jayme Peta’s Summary

I'm a professional psychotherapist with more than 10 years experience working with LGBTQ youth. I wish to expand my practice in the Boulder/Denver area and help LGBTQ youth with issues around identity, depression, self-esteem, increasing strength and resilience, substance use, friends & relationships, school stress and more. I also have significant experience working with grief and loss. I take a client-centered approach that incorporates other techniques to help clients increase feelings of strength, well-being and confidence.

Jayme Peta’s Specialties:

Grief and loss; sexual minority youth, training for clinicians on working with LGBTQ clients, eating mindfully, mindful and transpersonal approach to mental health; client centered mental health.
