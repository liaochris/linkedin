Joe Laurino’s Summary

Award-winning game designer and developer. Has been designing and developing games as a hobby for over 10 years, and has been working professionally since 2006. In the past, has worked as a game designer for one of the biggest mobile companies in the world, Gameloft. Has a broad skill-set encompassing most areas of game design, development, and production.

Has managed creative teams in lead designer roles and has acted as a Producer and Associate Producer in various independent and commercial projects.

Currently working as Arkadium's Game Producer and Audio Director.

Joe Laurino’s Specialties:

Game design, game writing, storytelling, character design and development, mission and scenario development, gameplay mechanic balancing, art and music direction, project and production management.

  • Founder, CEO, Lead Designer

    HyperActive Productions LTD

    (Non-Profit; 1-10 employees; Computer Games industry)

    September 1996 — September 2006 (10 years 1 month)

    Founded in September of 1996 by Joe Laurino and Bryan Rodriguez, HyperActive Productions was a non-profit, just-for-fun game development team consisting of a few like-minded individuals who wanted to get into the game industry someday in various roles. Joe released games like JetPack Justice and Infection and HP developed a reasonably large fan base, all things considering. When college life called, the company was put to rest so that Joe and his comrades could focus on the bigger picture.
