Peter Guthrie’s Summary

I am a freelance visualisation artist based in London. I studied Architecture at Edinburgh College of Art from 1995-2000, then worked for a number of Architect's Practices including RMJM Ltd. and Richard Murphy Architects in Scotland. I now specialize in 3D graphic arts and work freelance producing visuals for a wide range of project types.
