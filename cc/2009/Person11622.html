Gil Gerretsen’s Summary

Gil Gerretsen is a marketing revolutionary! He formed BizTrek in 1994 after nearly 20 years as a specialist in turning around financially troubled businesses. During that experience he developed, tested and refined 12 Marketing Triggers that ALWAYS produce more customers for small businesses.

At the same time, he recognized that the only way for a business owner to achieve sustained success and economic freedom was for them to define and master their own marketing efforts, rather than rely on outside consultants or ad agencies.

He also recognized that business owners had few places where they could turn to become proficient in the real world of marketing a small business.

This insight led to the formation of BizTrek. By providing seminars about the 12 Triggers and mastermind-style Marketing Guilds for mentoring, BizTrek helps small business owners gain competitive advantage to reach their full business capacity and potential.

BizTrek's goal is to introduce it's programs across the U.S. and Canada through a network of local mentors.
