Kimberley Borgens’s Summary

Kimberley Borgens is powerful business consultant, dynamic coach and inspiring mentor. She has been an entrepreneur for more than 20 years as a successful business owner. She has more than 8 years experience as a direct selling consultant and more than 6 years experience as a compassionate and powerful coach.

Kimberley has coached and mentored key leaders in many direct selling and network marketing businesses supporting them in creating significant results. She has produced and facilitated with a leading character development company and has worked with over 5000 people in improving their life skills.

Kimberley is knowledgeable, compassionate and has networking power. Her mission in life is to inspire, empower and educate entrepreneurs to step though their fears, improve their life style and reach their dreams.
