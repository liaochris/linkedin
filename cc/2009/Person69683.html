Maria Montoya’s Summary

As a real estate professional, I work with each of my clients individually, taking the time to understand their unique needs and lifestyles.

I work with a large network of cooperative and interactive associates, and have forged relationships with real estate agents at various companies across the city, state, and country. These connections provide me with the ability to expose my listed properties to a wider audience, and in turn, allow me to expose my client buyers to a larger range of available properties, often before they are even officially listed for sale.

My background in sales and marketing, along with years of negotiating purchase offers, are skills that have served my clients well – whether they have been buyers or sellers. Nearly 50% of my business comes from referrals by past clients.

It's my mission to treat clients as I'd want to be treated, and to provide them with an exceptional level of service. My goal is to retain my clients for life, and the strategy to make that happen is to provide top notch service at every level.

If the time is approaching for you to consider hiring a real estate professional to help you buy or sell your home or investment property, I ask you to seriously consider working with me. We work together, we win together.
