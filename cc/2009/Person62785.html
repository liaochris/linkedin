Marie Lally’s Summary

Hello! I am Marie Lally and I am a Realtor with O'Brien Realty in Southern Maryland. I am honest, hardworking, responsive and professional. I am dedicated to providing a high level of service to my clients. I enjoy people and love being a Realtor!

I am a Southern Maryland resident, having moved here from the Philadelphia suburbs (Bucks County) in 1989. I am proud to say that St. Mary's County is my home...I love what our great region offers! I love the birds and wildlife, I love the beautiful water and, most of all, I love the people in Southern Maryland!

Southern Maryland is the geographic area in which I have expertise. In addition to having a great knowlege of this beautiful area, I enjoy working everyone I meet but especially enjoy working with first-time homebuyers and sellers! I have a background in customer service and always strive to maintain responsive, friendly, professional service to my clients. I take whatever time is needed with first-time homebuyers to make their purchase enjoyable, memorable and well informed. I am patient. For sellers, I provide innovative marketing strategies, open communication, and a promise to market their property as intensely as I would market my own. I have degrees and many years of experience in both statistics and economics...I understand the unique market conditions we are experiencing. I am hard working and know that my persistence, my innovative strategies and unique ideas will provide my sellers with the reassurance they need concerning the marketing of their homes.

Marie Lally’s Specialties:

New construction will always be my passion. I was Vice President of a construction company for many years. I have achieved success in new home sales, having worked as a community sales manager for a SOMD builder. Currently, I own and operate a support company for builders and provide QA services and customer service/warranty services. My builder experience includes services provided to K&P Builders, Centex Homes, K.Hovnanian, Patriot Homes (Lennar), Rocky Gorge Homes, Thomas Homes and others.
