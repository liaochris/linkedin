Brandon Satrom’s Summary

Technology visionary with ten years of experience delivering software, solutions, architecture and strategy to organizations of all sizes. Experienced in enterprise-scale architectures and distributed systems running on the Microsoft platform, specifically SharePoint, C#, BizTalk, Windows Workflow Foundation, Windows Communication Foundation and SQL Server.
