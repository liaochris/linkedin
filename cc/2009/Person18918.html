Chu Chiang’s Summary

I am a trained graphic, UI, UX, visual and interaction designer with more than 5 years of agency experience. I have been primarily focused on web applications and interface design projects. I strongly believe in user experience design, it is what gives products its tactile feel and offers a physical point of engagement for users. Ideally, I would like a position working on mobile UX design; I strongly believe that this is quickly becoming the most tangible computing platform for the non-computing population. Great products can change the world, but great design will let the world use your products.
