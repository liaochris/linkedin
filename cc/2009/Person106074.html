Shelina Virani’s Summary

YRisk Consultancy and Training, Corp is small women owned business established in 2001 with the purpose to provide excellence in solutions that will enable institutions and clients to better manage risk and create efficiencies in processes by offering risk management advisory, training and end to end enterprise business development, technology solutions and services,

Our research team members perform in-depth analysis and provide resources that can assist our clients to make informative decisions..

Our consulting team members listen and identify the gaps in business performance and provide effective solutions that are customized to meet our clients specifications.

Our recruiting teams evaluate each candidates knowledge, experience and conduct an extensive background check prior to placing individuals on assignments and projects.

Our training instructors develop content which is specific to their expertise and knowledge and deliver it to participants in engaging informative workshops and seminars that teach best practices and standards.

Our mentorship programs and networking opportunities allow individuals to keep well informed about the ever-changing industry and seek guidance as needed with a database of information which is available but not limited to white papers, articles and case studies to further enhance knowlege or business best practices.

Shelina Virani’s Specialties:

YRisk Consultancy specializes in business development, technology, risk management with special focus on credit, fraud and operations, deal structuring, negotiations and syndication in Global, Capital and Secondary Markets.

YRisk Training specializes in risk management and end to end enterprise Commerical and Residential Lending operational processes and best practices.
