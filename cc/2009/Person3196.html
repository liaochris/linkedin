Alexandra C. Potora’s Summary

If I happen to have a supplementary half an hour in my day, I will put it to excellent use by finding myself a new bonnet to wear.

I was told countless times by countless people that the best thing in the life of a business career is to find that one good “something” that you enjoy doing, and learning how to do it best. In a theoretically new and improved pop civilization, where the attention span levels are one notch to none, maybe my hypothesis on the uncertainty of the above sentence will improve the near subsidized level of our innocent self importance.

My life has taken me places I know I will never stopover again. I have witnessed and experienced circumstances, life and people in my 24 years of age that most will never experience in a lifetime. I am bigheaded to say that there is not one good “something” that I am good at, but proudly say that I am the best at virtually everything that I choose to do.
Whoopi Goldberg said on 60 minutes “It never occurs to me that there are things that I can't do”, and I couldn’t relate to anything else more so than this quote.

I was the Romanian pop idol when, at the age of 18 I made the conscious choice of facing life head on and start chasing after the song of my heart. When others are fearful of moving from Oregon to Connecticut, I have crossed an Ocean in my pursuit of happiness and the American Dream.
My business endeavors have gained me knowledge in Processing/Underwriting, Sales, Management, Event Coordinating, Marketing and Public Relations just to name a few. I was the Account Executive, the Life and Health Insurance Agent, the Recruiting Specialist, the Director of Communications, the Singer, the Writer, the Painter and the Model.
Now that I have conquered the fear of rejection, the American Dream is simply the beginning – keep an eye out for me, as I will not stop till I reach the new dream of Global Domination.
