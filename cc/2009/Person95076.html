Steve Wylie’s Summary

Steve has been working with video and IT for several years, and currently works as a content producer and technical director on large scale corporate events throughout Australia.

Steve has a strong passion for video and new media formats, and enjoys promoting and utilizing related technologies.

Steve uses vision systems including Dataton Watchout, Barco Encore, Vista Systems Spyder and Montage, Christie Digital projectors, and works with production software including Adobe After Effects, Flash, Apple Motion, Final Cut Pro, Keynote, and Microsoft PowerPoint.
