Michael Cordova’s Summary

For most of my career I have been designing and building mission critical hardware, networking and custom database software systems. This includes building systems for NORAD (the primary US Air Force Command Center) and most of the biggest semiconductor (Intel, AMD, Fairchild Semiconductor, Lucent Technologies, Applied Materials, Microchip, and more) and pharmaceutical/pharmacological (Pfizer, Immunex, Dupont, Johnson & Johnson, Bristol-Myers Squibb, and more) companies in the world .

I'm an Electrical Engineer and I have developed software in more than 45 languages.

Since the advent of the Internet I have been obtaining #1 search engine rankings for the most important keywords of customer websites. This led to being asked to be a founder in the Internet marketing/direct marketing company Mercury Leads, Inc.

Mercury Leads was founded in April 2006 and since then we have succeeded in creating powerful online and offline marketing plans for our customers and seeing them through to successful completion.

The synergy between online and offline marketing has proven to be a huge factor in separating Mercury Leads from our competition. Increasing sales is still the goal.

Michael Cordova’s Specialties:

Software Design and Development, Web Design and Development, Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Internet Marketing, Database Design and Development - in short, design of systems and websites that automate our customers processes and bring in more qualified leads and business for them.
