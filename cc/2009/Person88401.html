Sandro Alberti’s Summary

Extensive academic and design-industry experience (14+ years). Continually involved in academic contexts (in conjunction to professional/industry pursuits), as instructor, academic consultant, invited juror. Very interested in the building of knowledge both inside and out of ‘classroom walls’, inciting students to ‘push the envelope’, attend various events/ lectures, and develop continued, lifelong learning. Leveraging the Web to share design theory, calendar events, essays, thoughts, projects, portfolios online. Experience spans crossborder conditions, between Mexico and USA; engaging multicultural creativity. Experience supervising/ managing staffs, since 1992. Highly-focused on Information Technology (Web design + interactive media). Engaged in coding and production of information technology, as well as research, time management, and client-team interaction. Committed to community promotion of the Arts and Information Technology

Sandro Alberti’s Specialties:

Digital media (design + code development; XHTML, CSS, AJaX, interactive Flash, PHP, Web video, analytics, mobile applications), CMS (Drupal, Wordpress, Expression Engine), architecture, fashion design, graphic design; Certified Paypal Developer (see: www.paypal.com/Certification/dc_directory.html#California)
