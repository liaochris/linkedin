Michael Erney’s Summary

My wide knowledge of the recruiting industry enables me to quickly understand the challenges of my clients, thereby helping them solve problems, develop alternatives, and find creative ways to promote their businesses.

I have had tremendous success placing C Level Executives, VPs, Directors, GMs, Controllers, Accountants, Senior Engineers, Land Developers, HR Professionals, and other key employees. I have placed candidates successfully at over 100 clients nationwide.

I earned my Bachelor's Degree in Finance from John Carroll University. After spending 2 years as a Financial Consultant, I decided to pursue my current career in the field of human capital management.
