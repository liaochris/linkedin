Erik Giberti’s Summary

Focused on providing small businesses quality web based applications that allow growth in their market. From simple data acquisition and analysis tools to CRM systems, web content management to e-commerce. Effective use of the internet is something that small businesses often leave to the owner of a company. The time they spend following industry trends is time spent not focusing on their core businesses. Technology consulting provides them with a way to continue to use the internet to their advantage without loosing sight of what’s critical in their daily activities.
