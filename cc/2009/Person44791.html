John Cannata’s Summary

Homeowners - Whether you are searching for a first home, moving up, looking into refinancing, or just have questions regarding mortgage financing, I'll help find the mortgage solution to meet your financial needs. My commitment to my clients throughout the process will be to ensure that you are well represented and informed at all times. Prior to closing the loan, we will go through the numbers to ensure we are on the same page and the rate/fees are what we have discussed. Reviewing the numbers prior to closing will ensure there aren't any mistakes and also will avoid any confusion at the closing table. No surprises when you work with me. For mortgage products, terms, and other helpful information - visit my mortgage website.

Real Estate Agents - Partnership is the key to success in this business. I work closely with Real Estate Agents so that we have a united front to our customer. No 'he said she said' when working with our company. Each week you are updated with the status of your files so that you are always involved and have the latest and greatest information. When we work together, your customers are a part of my service 'after the sale'. This service provides constant contact monthly through a Newsletter, Birthdays, and Anniversaries, just to name a few. By keeping in contact with our customers, we build loyalty which then translates to repeat business and word-of-mouth referrals. Do not continue to send business to a Loan Officer or Banker that is just waiting around for your business. Get into a partnership with a Loan Officer that helps you create loyal clients and a constant flow of referrals. A Loan Officer that helps you with your marketing plan to stir up new business from neighbors, businesses, and strangers that we turn into friends. To find out more about me and the services I provide, visit my Real Estate Agent Marketing Website
