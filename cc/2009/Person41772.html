Isaac Hernandez’s Summary

I love people, I love to play and I love taking pictures. Put these together and you get intimate, playful photographs with my personal twist. For the last 20 years I have been taking pictures for hundreds of different titles around the globe: from magazines and newspapers to music albums and books.

I have also been editor at Mercury Press International, a media agency, and a volunteer art and photography teacher at a public school. I am working to bring my experience into the advertising market.
