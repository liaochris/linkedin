Marvin Smith’s Summary

Provide the Microsoft’s Entertainment & Devices Division staffing team with mission critical talent by identifying and cultivating relationships with target talent community. Talent community development strategies include competitive intelligence, e-sourcing/recruiting, online recruiting, social networking, and talent database acquisition.

Our business mission is to be at the forefront our Microsoft’s Connected Entertainment initiative by delivering digital lifestyle experiences in collaboration and integration that are designed for a community of users. The Entertainment & Devices is comprised of our distinct businesses: Music (Zune & the Zune Marketplace), Gaming (Causal Game Platforms, Xbox Live, Windows Vista Games, & Xbox 360), Video (Microsoft TV, eHome & Windows Media Center), Communication (mobile & the mobile communications platform), and Productivity & Extended Consumer Experiences (PC Hardware, Windows Automotive, Windows Embedded, & MacOffice).
