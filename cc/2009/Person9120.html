Babette Wagenvoort’s Summary

As a visual artist & illustrator I spend most of my time drawing, writing or doing commissioned work - to view my work & read the latest news please visit my website:
http://www.babettewagenvoort.com

I'm a tutor at the KABK in The Hague where I teach 'drawing' & 'image concept' to BA Graphic Design students. In recent years I have also taught illustration and drawing to both fine art and illustration students (MA and BA level).

Since January 2008 I am curator of Volkskrant/oog - an online project of one of the main Dutch newspapers. http://www.volkskrant.nl/oog
‘Oog’ (Eye) is an online platform where artist respond in image and/or sound to news and topical events.
