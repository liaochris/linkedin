Michael Loban’s Summary

Over the past five years, I have directed a number of marketing campaigns and worked with dozens of organizations to help them establish online presence, build and manage their brand and establish long lasting relationships with customers.

My primary responsibility is to develop and guide the execution of social strategies.​ I help companies identify and define opportunities for social engagement.​ Areas include, but are not limited to: research and analysis; community development; engagement plans; content and dialogue plans; and technology implementation.​ I provide strategic consulting around social efforts across the enterprise that will grow the credibility and relevance of the Client’s business.​

Additionally, I often conduct seminars and classes on social media and using viral marketing to help people find jobs in today's challenging times.

Michael Loban’s Specialties:

Social media strategy, online community building, viral marketing and online brand building.
