Sergio Espeja’s Summary

Founder and CEO of verycocinar.com a WebTV about gastronomy. He is a Ruby on Rails enthusiast, has been working with web technologies since 1999 and has a good artificial intelligence background.

He gave conference talks at RailsConf Europe 2007 and at Conferencia Rails Hispana 2006 and 2007.
Sergio has created some open source projects such as "Bayesian Networks for Ruby", "General Genetic Algorithms for Ruby" and "MetaRails", among others.

He also runs a blog in Spanish about Rails at spejman.blogspot.com and shows its open source contributions in English at spejman-on-rails.blogspot.com.
