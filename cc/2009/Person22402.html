Dan Wright’s Summary

Dan Wright is an open-source developer with extensive experience using object-oriented design methodologies to build database-driven e-commerce web applications.

Dan's primary language of choice is Perl and has contributed to the Comprehensive Perl Archive Network's (CPAN) repository of open-source code both in the way of bug-fixes and new code.

Dan is also experienced as a conference organizer, having run several 150+ person conferences for different non-profit organizations. The most notable of which will be the Yet Another Perl Conference's (YAPC) 10th anniversary event at Carnegie Mellon University in 2009.

Additionally, Dan has a strong dedication to giving back to the community. As a volunteer in the Boy Scouts of America, Dan has provided technical and leadership services on the Council, Area, and Regional (Northeast United States) level.
