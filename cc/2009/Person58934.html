Lex Jansen’s Summary

Hands-on IT professional with almost 20 years of experience in the pharmaceutical industry. Excellent technical skills in SAS programming and systems integration. Special interests in SAS application development and implementing CDISC. Proven experience in development and implementation of standards in a global pharmaceutical environment. Expert in integrating SAS with other leading technologies such as PDF, XML, Microsoft Office, to support electronic submissions.
Experienced with working as a liaison between Bio-Statistics and IT Departments in a global setting.

Member of the editorial board of the Pharmaceutical Programming Journal (http://www.maney.co.uk/journals/pha)

Member of CDISC XML Technologies Team (formerly known as the ODM team)
Member of CDISC Integrated Safety Data Pilot Project team
DIA member

Lex Jansen’s Specialties:

SAS, CDISC, SDTM, define.xml, XML, PDF, clinical data standards, statistical programming, data analysis, data- and system validation
