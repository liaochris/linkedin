Steve Loos’s Summary

We are a full service real estate appraisal firm, and work in the Central Coast area of California: Santa Clara, San Benito, Monterey and Santa Cruz counties.

Our staff includes State Certified General Appraisers with FHA approval. We also are experienced in FHA 203k and FNMA HomeStyle rehab projects.

We have all the latest technology, but always remember that service is best provided in person.

Appraisals for attorneys, government agencies, private investors and lenders.
Valuation services for taxation, estate, arbitration, easement, condemnation and right of way.
Properties include single and multi family residential including subdivision, commerical and industrial properties, and ranch and farm land.
