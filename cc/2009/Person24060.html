David Patterson’s Summary

Expertise and patents in computational chemistry, similarity metrics in chemistry (recently extended into -omics), and pattern recognition. Multidimensional data exploration and optimization is a central theme. Interactive data mining, algorithm development, cheminformatics and bioinformatics.

David Patterson’s Specialties:

QSAR, virtual libraries of chemicals, visualization of high dimensional systems, application of similarity to design drug discovery processes. Data mining, algorithms, recursive optimization processes.
