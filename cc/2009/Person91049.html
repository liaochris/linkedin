Shane Sheibani’s Summary

"Cosmetic Surgery is a wonderful specialty in which I can combine my passion for science of medicine and surgery with my artistic abilities. Having done thousands of Cosmetic Surgeries of face, nose, breasts, body and skin, including some of the most challenging Advanced, Revisional, Corrective and Reconstructive Cosmetic Surgeries, I truly appreciate my specialty because I can be creative, enjoy doing what I am good at, while helping others. I love learning and growing. I am constantly reading, attending conferences and seminars in addition to speaking with other leaders in the field of cosmetic surgery in order to perfect my techniques. My goal is always to adhere to the highest standard of care in cosmetic surgery while creating naturally attractive cosmetic surgery results for my patients.

I enjoy being challenged, and Revisional or Corrective, Reconstructive Cosmetic Surgeries are some of the most challenging procedures a Cosmetic Surgeon can perform.

These surgeries demand the surgeon's utmost in creativity and excellence of surgical knowledge and skill combined with patience and attention to detail.

I specialize in these types of surgeries in addition to General Natural Cosmetic Surgeries of face, nose, breasts, body and skin. I feel humbled and truly appreciate the confidence that my patients continuously show me by referring their family and friends to our practice. It truly excites me when a patient is referred to me who is seeking my best. It is great to know that I am able to enjoy myself doing what I love to do while being of benefit to my patients by sharing my gifts."
