Awnya Boam’s Summary

I have been with Story Time Felts as an Independent Consultant for 4.5 years now. I love the products and believe in the concept of imagination. I find that far too often parents are getting children toys that will entertain them and may have some educational content too, but kids learn the most and the best with toys that encourage imagination. To put it another way "The more a toy does, the less a child learns". Kids need to manipulate things with their hands and use their minds to fully learn and discover their imagination.

I have loved the business that I am able to have becuase of Story Time Felts. It is a great tax break and I love feeling that I am doing something to contribute to the family budget at the same time as being able to stay home with my 3 kids.

I love the people that I am able to meet and work with as well. I have made some great friends that I never would have met if I had not been in this business.

I have big goals for my business in the future. I want to be one of the highest selling consultants in the company and have a large team under me. I want to establish myself as a leader and a teacher in the Direct Sales Industry.
