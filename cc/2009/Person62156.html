Marcia Nieuwenhuis’s Education

  • Universiteit van Amsterdam

    Master , Political Science , 2003 — 2006

    Nieuwenhuis obtained a Master Degree in Political Science. Special areas of her expertise include International Relations and Multicultural Democracy. She extensively researched the effect of internet forums for ethnic minorities (such as the frequently visited Maroc.nl) on political participation.

  • Hogeschool van Utrecht

    Bachelor , Journalism , 1998 — 2002

    Nieuwenhuis obtained a Bachelor Degree in Journalism (specialised in TV and written media). During her studies in journalism she also followed the international masterclass Europe in the World, taught at Hogeschool Utrecht, the Danish School of Journalism and Aarhus University in Denmark.

  • Aarhus Universitet

    Masterclass , Europe in the World , 2002

    The international masterclass 'Europe in the World' is taught at Hogeschool Utrecht, the Danish School of Journalism and Aarhus University in Denmark. This programme is focused at the political, economical and historical background of Europe.
