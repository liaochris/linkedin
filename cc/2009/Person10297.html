Benjamin Boudreau’s Summary

Benjamin Boudreau can't draw to save his life – bad for him but good for you.

At an early age, the crayons were pushed aside and writing became his passion, quickly winning his first competition at age six. Benjamin has since gone on to appear as a national top five finalist for CBC Radio's Canada Writes and as the valedictorian upon completion of his Bachelor of Public Relations at Mount Saint Vincent University.

By publishing the running narrative in his head to a personal blog, Benjamin’s daily life quickly developed a following, proving that he truly can make any material exciting and engaging.

Backed with project coordination and strategic communications experience, Benjamin’s words have been spoken by executive directors, corporate vice presidents and a minister of the federal government. Among other initiatives, his writing supported a change in federal legislation, a multimillion-dollar funding announcement, and national awareness strategies.

He may be a failed artist but as a celebrated wordsmith he is ready for any challenge.
