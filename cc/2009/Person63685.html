Mark Taylor’s Summary

A results-oriented executive with a 35-year history as an accomplished CEO and corporate manager, Mark’s extensive leadership and operational experience offers a diversity of relevant business building skills and perspective to Chief Executives and business owners. Mark thrives on coaching people who run companies to positively transform their businesses during times of turmoil.

As a industry expert consulting thousands of organizations, including Unisys, Ford, General Electric, the United States Postal Service, DHL, and Standard Register Corporation, Mark is considered to be the nation’s authority on increasing profit and productivity in shipping operations. He has been featured as an industry expert on ABC News and in the New York Times, and is the author of Computerized Shipping Systems: Increasing Profit & Productivity Through Technology. Taylor has been named a Distinguished Logistics Professional (DLP) by the American Society of Transportation & Logistics in recognition of his 30 year, career-long contributions to the field of logistics.

Taylor is in demand as a speaker at major industry events, including PARCEL, Document Strategy Forum, the National Conference on Operations & Fulfillment, the National Postal Forum, the Council of Logistics Management, Mailcom, and the Parcel Logistics Expo. He was selected by Vistage International to join their elite corps of professional speakers to CEOs and senior executives.

He writes a monthly columns for PARCEL & Mailing Systems magazines, and is a frequent contributor to numerous industry publications, including Operations and Fulfillment, MAIL, and Frontline Solutions magazines.
