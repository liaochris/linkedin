Nick Halverson’s Summary

Over four years of real estate development, sales/property management and strategic planning in Costa Rica. I am the largest shareholder of a development 10 minutes from the beach in the Southern region of Costa Rica.

I run a real estate company with properties in Costa Rica, Belize, Panama, Bahamas, Nicaragua and Dominican Republic.

I also assist my father with his consulting business - D. Halverson & Associates where we work with some the country's largest and most successful agriculutural coops in the United States.

Previously, I spent ten years in international business development, marketing, and a small stint in sell side mergers and acquisitions.

I have worked closely with companies such as Wal-Mart, Castrol (BP), Kellogg's, Fujifilm and Yamaha in developing and managing marketing and sponsorship programs for them.

Nick Halverson’s Specialties:

Improving my spoken Spanish skills.
