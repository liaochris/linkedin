Jo Caudron’s Summary

My goal is to help our customers and partners to find their place in the constantly changing world of new media.

- Founder of Dear Media, a consulting company specialized in (digital) media innovation
- Co-founder and CEO of ONE Agency, the innovative full-service interactive agency
- Co-founder of tvAgency, the first interactive TV production company in Belgium
- Co-founder of Nousom (2004), an "idea breeding" company
- Co-founder of theOriginals (2003), an internet advisory company
- Co-founder and CEO of xCA (2001-2003), an innovative multi-device product company
- Co-founder of Webware (1996), a portal and content company with portals like Webbel and IBS
- Chairman of The Reference (1993-2003)
- CEO, Sales & Marketing manager of The Reference (1993-2000)
- Co-founder of The Reference (1993)
