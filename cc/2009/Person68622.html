Mikel J. Wisler’s Summary

I am an independent filmmaker and freelance videographer in Boston, Massachusetts. I have shot, edited, and produced a variety of projects, and I am always looking for new possibilities. I have directed TV commercials, worked crew on commercial shoots, edited Telly Award winning projects, have been a crew member on two feature films, and I have written, produced, and directed several of my own short films. I am also a screenwriter with several short and feature scripts under my belt. I am very interested in eventually directing my own feature films, but feel it is very important to gain plenty of experience and connect with the right people first. I am currently seeking funding for my first feature film.

Mikel J. Wisler’s Specialties:

Director, Writer, Editor, Videographer, Digital Cinematographer, Producer, Assistant Director, Grip.

    (Motion Pictures and Film industry)

    December 2008 — Present (8 months)

    (Motion Pictures and Film industry)

    July 2006 — March 2007 (9 months)
