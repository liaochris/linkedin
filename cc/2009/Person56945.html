Kurt Mitchell’s Summary

Artist; illustrator; designer; concept creator; comic book author and artist.

Currently working on authoring and drawing graphic novels and childrens books as part of developing larger IPs (Intellectual Properties).Comic books are currently available through www.comixpress.com. Childrens books are available as manuscripts; illustrations are in process...

Currently teaching storyboarding and Game Development at Moraine Valley Community College.

Freelance Illustrator working for clients such as: Carus Publishing, The Chicago Reader, The Catalyst, The Chicago Tribune, Crain's Chicago Business, The ABA Press, The Museum of Science and Industry and many more...

Kurt Mitchell’s Specialties:

Digital concept realization and imagery using PhotoShop and 3DStudioMax;
pencil and paper; pen and ink; watercolor

  • Childrens Book Author/ Writer/Illustrator

    Self-employed

    (Self-Employed; Graphic Design industry)

    May 2006 — Present (3 years 8 months)

    I'm currently working on a series of books using anthropomorphic animals in reworkings of cultural classics. The manuscripts use rhyming quatrains and slightly elevated vocabulary coupled with richly rendered illustrations.

    Current titles:

    "Armadillo Buckaroo" -the story of a day in the life of a cowboy starring a bronco bustin' armadillo.

    "The Ballad of Tex Rex" -a slight retelling of "High Noon".

    "Tyranno De Bergerac" -a take on "Cyrano De Bergerac", with a "proboscically endowed" dinosaur in the title role.

    "Oh, Hindenblimp..." - a minor retelling of the last flight of the Hindenburg with environmental overtones.
