Ryan Weaver’s Summary

I was born with three irrepressible urges: to write, to explore and to ask questions. I enjoy work that allows me to manage people, deadlines and the quality of my final product. My interests tend to center on travel, music, art, the environment, and food writing, which has become my main area of expertise (but don't call me a "foodie" -- I love SpaghettiO's as much as the next millenial kid). I'm always looking for ways to expand my horizons, collaborate with creative and inspiring people, and learn new skills.
