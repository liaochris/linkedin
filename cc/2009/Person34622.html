Gary Broussard’s Summary

Gary P. Broussard has held leadership roles in nonprofits ranging from department head to directorships to the executive level. Most recently as Vice President for Institutional Advancement at St. Gregory's University - a wonderful Catholic university in Oklahoma.

Through these experiences, he learned countless lessons about how to work effectively with others, about how to build and run a successful organization.

At GPB Associates, LLC, we provide educational institutions and other nonprofits with customized services to help optimize funding activities and operational effectiveness.

Gary Broussard’s Specialties:

Expertise in nonprofit board education & training, nonprofit management, annual fund re-tooling, establishing planned giving programs, and capital campaigns. seminar and workshop training, online webinar training and consulting
