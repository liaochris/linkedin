Paul Reynolds’s Summary

FableVision’s mission is to innovate using media, storytelling and technology to reach, support and inspire ALL learners - both in the creation of original products and co-development with select partners.

Helped found FableVision - an award-winning children’s media company metro Boston - in 1996 with my twin brother Peter H. Reynolds - who also enjoys a thriving children's book publishing career (http://www.peterhreynolds.com.)

FableVision enjoys partnerships and co-development relationships with a wide range of museums, publishers, and broadcasters, including Jim Henson Productions, Corporation for Public Broadcasting, MIT Education Arcade, CAST, Houghton-Mifflin, Research Institute for Learning & Development, National Academy of Sciences, Learning.com, Baltimore Children’s Museum, the W5 in Ireland, PBS, TSP/Scholastic, Carnegie Hall, and NOGGIN.

FableVision's award-winning K-8 educational software includes the BrainCogs: Test-Taking Survival Kit and Stationery Studio: Customized Stationery for Young Writers, which was recently named Scholastic Instructor Magazine’s TEACHER RAVES 2004 “Best Products” For more information about FableVision, please explore www.fablevision.com - or read a recent feature from The Boston Globe at http://www.fablevision.com/new/globe_11-12.pdf.
