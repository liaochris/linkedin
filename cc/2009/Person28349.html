Petri I. Salonen’s Summary

With a strong academic and professional background, my personal mission is to work with technology companies, both European and US and help these organizations to globalize their operations.

Today, TELLUS servers clients on all three continents (Europe, US and Latin America and Asia). TELLUS provides services in multiple areas such as:

- Business development with lead generation and marketing
- Distribution of technology products to US and Latin Americas
- Strategy development for technology companies within different areas such as
o Setting development strategy
o Setting marketing and sales strategy
o Setting market segmentation strategy

TELLUS has exposure to Asian markets and with its network of partners, TELLUS can provide help in design services, software development and other software product development related activities.

TELLUS Technology solutions provides consulting services in enterprise data warehousing, business intelligence and customer relationship management solutions. TELLUS helps its enterprise clients to set its technology strategy, align this with overall business strategy to fuel growth.
