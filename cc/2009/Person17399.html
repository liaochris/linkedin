Ches Martin’s Summary

I hunger for progressive work with agile, dynamic teams in media and communications. In particular: standards-based web application development and high-end video post-production -- fields where technology and creativity come together in a fresh product and environment.

I have a well-rounded background in online and analog media, and am continually driven by a passion for learning. I have architected and maintained scalable, fault-tolerant web application clusters, integrated shared storage solutions for post facilities, given product demonstrations, trained and supported users, edited and color-corrected short films, and helped engineer a thing or two.

I'm a geek with people skills. I understand content industries and the needs of media professionals, and can help to improve the efficiency of both.
