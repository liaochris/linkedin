Cyril Simone’s Summary

Launched a new firm focused on the use of science in social networking, analysis, design and community growth, also business development, strategy and project management. Our approach to social media ranges from the mathematics of network science to the social psychology of human interactions. By taking this approach to social technology we can ensure a solution that is based in objective measures not subjective guesses.

Developed a new marketing strategy and process using social media to increase web traffic, revenue and engage people in a more meaningful way. Focused on web metrics and analysis of trending information.

Managed organization team for a restructuring and outsourcing effort for one of the business sectors with an estimate $37 Million in savings.
Prior: CIO Corporate Office News Corporation 2004 to 2006
Focus: Managed all aspects of Information Technology from the Data Center to the web.
