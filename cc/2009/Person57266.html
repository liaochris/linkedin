Lakshman Pillai’s Summary

Have 18 years of experience in Personal, Community and Enterprise Knowledge Management (KM), Learning Management and Information Technology as an expert consultant, visionary leader, evangelist and entrepreneur. My Knowledge Management thought leadership started with a paper presentation in 1988 and full-time journey started in 1997. Authored my first KM article in 1998 that was published in a Singapore magazine. Presenting in many KM conferences since 1998.

After gaining valuable experience, I founded Lpcube - a focused Knowledge Management Company. Am the chief architect of True Knowledge Management and Organizational Learning Management strategy and system called TrueKM. Currently leading a team of talented people to build and market a world class personal and enterprise knowledge management solution and to transform my KM vision into a reality. Our complete end-to-end KM includes Enterprise KM, Personal KM, Community KM, Project KM, Support KM, innovative Social Networking Platform (Enterprise 2.0/Web 2.0) for collaborative learning, True KM Maturity Model, KM consulting methodology and smart learning programs.

I provide high value KM consulting for knowledge enterprises in various industries to drive organizational learning and personal competency management for harnessing collective intelligence.

Enterprise Knowledge Management consulting model (RapidKMi) that I developed addresses people, processes, best practices, platforms (intelligent knowledge systems) and change management.

In IT, I have gained experience in project management, product development, product management, consulting, training and support.

Am a strong believer of system thinking for building high-performance workforce through enterprise competency management, facilitating collaborative culture and workplace, establishing learning network for effective learning and integrating business functions towards nurturing collective intelligence and maximizing operational excellence.
