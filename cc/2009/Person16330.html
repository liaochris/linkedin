Claudio Del Tufo’s Summary

Claudio Del Tufo is an IT professional with 15+ years of experience and a diverse industry background. He is really passionate about Object Oriented Software Engineering and very experienced with the Analysis (OOA), Design (UML) and Development (JAVA, C++) of complex software systems. He is also accomplished in Operational Research, Mathematical Modeling and Mixed Integer Programming (MIP). Claudio has facilitated and managed projects in US and Europe with roles ranging from Technical Leader to IT Director. He is a published author, keen technical reviewer, University lecturer fluent in both English and Italian.
