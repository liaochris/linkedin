Bart-Jan Hindriks’s Experience

  • Projectleider Burgerzakensysteem (mGBA 2.0)

    Ministerie van BZK

    (Government Agency; Government Administration industry)

    May 2009 — Present (8 months)

    De modernisering van de Gemeentelijke Basisadministratie Persoonsgegevens is nodig voor de ontwikkeling van de GBA tot dé basisregistratie voor persoonsgegevens in het landelijke stelsel van basisregistraties.

    Dit betekent dat alle geautoriseerde instanties 24 uur per dag 'on-line' kunnen beschikken over actuele en betrouwbare persoonsgegevens, dat er een gestandaardiseerde en moderne uitwisseling van berichten plaatsvindt en dat de kwaliteit van de GBA doorlopend wordt gecontroleerd en verbeterd.

    Het programma mGBA 2.0 is ondergebracht bij het Ministerie van Binnenlandse Zaken en Koninkrijksrelaties. Stichting ICTU ondersteunt het programma bij de uitvoering.

  • Programme Manager e-Inspecties

    ICTU

    (Government Agency; Government Administration industry)

    July 2006 — March 2009 (2 years 9 months)

    The e-Inspecties programme gives an irreversible impulse to the cooperation between inspectorates by smart use of ICT for a smaller and better working government.

    The team has grown to approx. 35 people (architects, project management, general staff, software development and maintenance), reporting directly to the portfolio manager ICT of the Council of State Inspectorates (NL: Inspectieraad).

    Main vision is the use joint systems: central components (85-90% generic) and tailor made software (approx. 10%-15% specific). In 2007 and 2008 the team has implemented Digital Dossiers (Transportation and Fireworks) and Joint Inspection Rooms (Hazardous Companies, Chemical Companies and Childcare). More systems and implementations will follow in 2009 and onwards.

    Funded in 2006-2007 by the Ministries of Internal (BZK) and Economic (EZ) Affairs jointly with the State Inspectorates. In 2008 the Dutch government has granted 24,5 mln euro for the continuation of the e-Inspection programme.

  • Programme Manager NIM-BRZO

    ICTU

    (Government Agency; 201-500 employees; Government Administration industry)

    June 2006 — July 2007 (1 year 2 months)

    After the desastrous results of an explosion of a small chemical plant in Seveso (It.), EU regulations make inspections work closely together on safety inspections of companies storing a certain amount of explosive materials. Dutch regulation, BRZO (www.brzo99.nl) appoints approx. 400 BRZO companies to be inspected jointly by the Labor Inspection, Fire Department and local authorities. An improved New Inspection Methodology (NIM) has been developed.

    The NIM-BRZO programme (8 pers.) has developed and implemented the Joint Inspection Room, a virtual collaboration environment supporting the NIM in which inspectors jointly observe, write their findings and draw conclusions. At the end of this process a ready to be sent End-Report will be generated.

    The system is in operation since september 2007. Because of it's flexibility it will be used in the e-Inspections programma. Therefore these programmes have been merged on juli 1, 2007.

  • Deputy Programme Manager Burgerservicenummer

    ICTU

    (Government Agency; 201-500 employees; Government Administration industry)

    May 2004 — June 2006 (2 years 2 months)

    Project manager for the technical components supporting the Civil Service Number (CSN, Dutch: Burgerservicenummer) system to be introduced in late 2006. The CSN will be assigned by the government to all individuals that have a relationship with the Dutch government (i.e. all people living in Holland). This number can then be used for the identification of individuals.

    The project is executed by a team of approx. 35 persons divided in 5 projects with a total project budget of EUR 15 mln.

    My main responsability was the development of the Civil Service Number Management System (Beheervoorziening BSN), the central service for verification and assignment of numbers.

    For more information, check: http://www.programmabsn.nl
