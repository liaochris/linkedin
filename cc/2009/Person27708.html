Laura Marino’s Summary

I founded www.dottorlex.it to give a professional service both to people needing advice about legal matters and professionals who want to improve their visibility on the web. My core business is the providing of legal content, especially about labour.
I have been working for a large publisher specialized in legal content and I still work as writer. My last book is "Maternità. Le norme per i genitori che lavorano", Buffetti, 2009.
Currently I am publishing monthly a series of short and practical books (available as ebooks or print-on-demand on www.lulu.com), they are called infobussola.

Laura Marino’s Specialties:

competenza tecnico-editoriali per pubblicazioni on line e off line, competenze nell'ambito delle discipline giuridiche connesse al mondo del lavoro, conoscenza approfondita del mercato editoriale per i liberi professionisti del settore giuridico-economico e del target di riferimento.
