Randall Goya’s Summary

I develop Web 2.0 (I prefer "Web 2.x") community, social publishing, ecommerce and rich media web sites. In 2004, I identified Drupal as a useful tool, and today I build about 80% of my projects with it. I have a talented network of experts, my "team" includes graphic, Flash/Flex and 3d designers, writers, marketers, User Experience experts, and programmers who can handle any kind of project. As a developer and project manager, I maintain the highest standards of best-practices and usability, with consideration for extensibility and SEO/SEM. I worked in the film postproduction industry for 10 years on major feature films http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0333025/ - I believe that is a very similar environment to web production. I suffer from "pforp" http://netsperience.org/content/blog/what-pforp which may annoy some people but it ensures clarity and usability. (pforp = "Passion FOR Precision") I have been developing web sites since 1996, beginning with a free GeoCities site, where I explored the possibilites of DHTML/JavaScript. The site won awards, and my code was featured on the front page of JavaScript.com.
