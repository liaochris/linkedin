Steve Lodin’s Summary

Results-oriented IT executive with international work experience in large global companies. Highly motivated leader with excellent coaching, facilitation and mentoring skills. Currently engaging with customers to identify and solve business problems related to IT security and risk management issues. Previously specialized in medical device security and healthcare IT security. Multi-faceted IT career – internal IT leadership, customer-facing IT vendor, and IT consultant. Multi-industry career – technology, healthcare/medical devices, accounting, and automotive manufacturing. Strong oral and written communication skills with many public speaking engagements and published works. Graduate degree in computer science with focus in computer security. Early career path was electrical engineering, software engineering and microprocessor system development.

Steve Lodin’s Specialties:

Executive-level IT strategy decision-making, IT Infrastructure (Networking, UNIX, Linux, Windows, OSX, etc...), IT Security, Medical Device Security. I am a CISSP since 1998.
