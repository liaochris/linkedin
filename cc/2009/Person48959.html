John Briggs’s Summary

20 years of product management experience. Proven ability to build and lead teams of product managers responsible for all aspects of creation, development, implementation, and ongoing management of consumer-facing applications.

I am also advising a few early stage start-ups on how to structure their product development teams, role definition within those teams, and how to streamline their product development process. Past clients include StubHub, Danoo, BoredAt and Maria Health.
