Randal Leeb-du Toit’s Summary

VISION, OVERSIGHT & OPERATIONAL EXPERIENCE

I am a serial entrepreneur, who cut his teeth as an intrapreneur within one of the world's largest business to business publishing houses a decade ago. A lawyer by training, I've built up an early stage high tech venture portfolio across information technology, mobile, biotech and nanotech and run the largest incubation operation in the southern hemisphere, raising and deploying over half a billion dollars to date.

I've lived and worked in Africa, Europe, Australia and the United States and have had experience in start ups and multinationals.

My goal is to continue making a positive difference by leveraging my skill array and network.
