Patrick Galla’s Summary

Has worked with KY and IN employers since 1984. Has consulted with them for the purposes of establishing employee benefits of good value. Business has been built on caring customer service.
Since 1997 has contracted with 5 of the nation's largest insurance carriers doing employee benefit communications. Conducted seminars for 1000's of employees who work for Fortune 500 companies.
Has newsletter that reaches 900 contract enrollers across the country with access to additional 1700. Current projects include building a website for purposes of linking employers, consultants, carriers, and enrollers.

Patrick Galla’s Specialties:

Communicates benefits through formal PowerPoint presentations. Since 2004 has trained contract enrollers on presentation skills and has co-authored a guide. Has conducted seminars at large groups including: Ralston-Purina, Delta Air, Continental Tire, Nationwide, City of Alexandria, Aventis, Lucent, Citigroup, Drexel Univ, MCI, UPS, Omni Hotels, GAP, State of New Mex, State of Maryland, Spectrum Health, Questar, Wolfe Creek Nuclear, Perdue, Amer Board of Int Med, Charles Schwab.
