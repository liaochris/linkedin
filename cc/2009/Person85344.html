Rob Metzgar’s Summary

A power user of Photoshop and Illustrator, proficiency in Flash, Dreamweaver, Image-ready, and a variety of many other graphic programs. Thorough understanding of web design tools and techniques, including HTML, graphic optimization, animation techniques, mood boards, storyboarding, and mental modeling. Strong team and client leadership, and the ability to multi-task and manage my workflow in a project time critical environment. Facilitated and established process and communication about design with over 20 of the worlds largest brands at 6 different locations on both the west and east coast and Europe throughout Time Warner.

Rob Metzgar’s Specialties:

User Interface Design / Graphic Design - Usability Testing - High/ Low fidelity prototyping, Human Information Processing - Field Studies / Contextual Inquiry - Web-based Applications - Heuristic Evaluation - Task Analysis - Experimental Design and Methodology - Accessibility - Use case scenarios - Concept modeling.
