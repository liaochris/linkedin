Richard Chen’s Summary

I am a software architect in Emerging Markets Platform Group of Intel where I am responsible for software product definition and architecture design of emerging market platform products like Classmate PC. Prior to joining Intel, I was an IT expat in Singapore for six years, where I led to accomplish several multi-million projects that include intelligent remote monitoring and control system for industry machine and sensor networks, and a total asset management portal that has been used by many large property companies all over the world, and a unified communications system for enterprise-level multi-modal collaboration. I was also a research scholar in National University of Singapore and Kent Ridge Digital Lab working in distance learning, course management system and networking research areas.

My goals are:

- To assume technical solution architect or CTOship role
