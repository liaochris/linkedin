John Davis’s Summary

I am focusing on, and I am fully committed to, full-time support of the SendOutCards business I'm building. I have assisted many people to become senders of cards. I love working with people who want to improve their relationships with friends, family and/or customers. My best clients include successful authors, ministers and other church personnel, higher education institutions, real estate brokers and agents and business owners that are committed to network marketing or enticing referrals from those they serve.

As a manager with SendOutCards, I also assist new Distributors and Retail Affiliates to be successful entrepreneurs. I continue assisting my team as they promote to evermore rewarding advancements by organizing events and helping team members to grow their organization.

In addition, I have been an active community volunteer from age eighteen to the present. During the past twenty-five years I have been a leader at the First Unitarian Universalist Church of San Diego in many capacities, including President of the Board of Trustees. I co-founded the Unitarian Universalist Men’s Fellowship and served on the continental Steering Council for the Unitarian Universalist Men’s Network. I also served the San Diego Memorial Society as a volunteer, including a year as its President. The Society promotes simple, dignified, inexpensive cremations and burials as a non-profit, non-sectarian, service organization.

John Davis’s Specialties:

Generating referrals, creating heartfelt communications that build strong bonds with people you care about, and teaching entrepreneurs to generate explosive growth of their business. I also assist people who want to earn $300 to $900 a month working no more than three to six hours a month.
