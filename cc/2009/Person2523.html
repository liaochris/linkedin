Alana Fairchild’s Summary

Alana Fairchild is a professional spiritual director, providing spiritual mentoring, psychotherapy and intuitive spiritual guidance for those seeking to grow in consciousness and requiring spiritual support and professional supervision, particularly when engaging in positions of professional and spiritual leadership. Apart from her private consultations, she operates an online spiritual resource centre at www.alanafairchild.com and also lectures widely on living a spiritually-conscious life that focuses on contribution to humanity.

Alana Fairchild’s Specialties:

Alana's expertise is in intuitive assessment, the nature of the spiritual path for the modern spiritual seeker, particularly those in leadership positions, working with addictions and emotional healing through spiritual psychotherapy.
