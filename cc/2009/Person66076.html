Michael Coleman’s Summary

I am the Product Manager for Adobe After Effects, the industry standard software for creating motion graphics and visual effects. Prior to my current position, I was the lead user experience designer for Adobe Production Premium, a suite of software products for creating digital video and audio.

I am passionate about creating engaging products that focus on the most important opportunities and customer needs. In addition to software product management, my domain expertise includes designing and producing visual effects and motion graphics for film & video.

Michael Coleman’s Specialties:

More than a decade of experience making software products. Experience includes product management, product design, user interface design and user experience design. Projects include web applications and world-class desktop applications such as Adobe After Effects & Adobe Production Studio (a suite of Adobe programs that includes After Effects, Premiere Pro, Encore and Audition).
