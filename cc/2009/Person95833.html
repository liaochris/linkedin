    XPLANE

    (Privately Held; Design industry)

    June 2009 — Present (2 months)

    Founded in 1993 by Dave Gray, XPLANE is an information design consultancy that drives results for many of the world’s leading corporations by clarifying complex business issues through visual collaboration.

    With an innovative visual, multidisciplinary methodology, XPLANE is unique because we apply left and right-brained approaches to develop fresh solutions to your most challenging problems. Our approach accelerates understanding, which drives actions and results to help our customers. Companies have hired XPLANE to motivate employees, drive sales, convince decision makers and improve processes.

    We draw from a diverse set of backgrounds to do this. We combine information designers, journalists, comic book artists, management consultants, software developers, trainers, technologists and a diversity of other expertise in order to look at challenges in new ways and provide fresh ideas and insights.

    Today XPLANE is proud to serve 40 of the FORTUNE 500 companies around the globe. Headquartered in Portland, Oregon, XPLANE also operates offices in St. Louis and Madrid, facilitating 24/7 service for clients.

  • Owner/Principal

    BrightSpot InfoDesign

    (Information Services industry)

    May 2007 — Present (2 years 3 months)

    I am currently the owner of an information design firm called BrightSpot. The services I offer include: Meeting Design and Support, Publication Design and Development, and Information Design and Production. I partner with clients to help them visualize complex information for their audiences. This can range from graphically recording any group process to designing and producing communication pieces, teaching tools, systems maps, etc. My goal is to support the public and private sectors by making mental models explicit and clear and therefore allowing organizations to take smart action. Together, my clients and I turn dense, complex, or even dull information into visually-pleasing, coherent and memorable tools for the people who need to interpret and use it.
