Linda Chong’s Summary

A dynamic, experienced and meticulous professional who has more than 13 years of extensive IT experience on management, consulting, evangelism, architecture design/review, pre-sales engagement and systems integration in the areas of SOA, Web 2.0, e-business/e-commerce and enterprise application integration (EAI).

In my current role at Microsoft Consulting Services, my portfolio includes project management and presales engagement, focusing primarily on application development opportunities and engagements such as .NET consulting, solution architecture, SOA, SQL Server/BizTalk/SharePoint/CRM consulting, etc.

In my previous role at Microsoft as the Architect Evangelist, I assumed the lead role in driving thought leadership and platform adoption in SOA, Software+Services, Web 2.0 and other emerging technologies to strategic enterprise customers and partners on the Microsoft platform. I also represented Microsoft in SiTF SOA Chapter as a member of the executive committee team to drive the adoption of SOA in Singapore.

Prior to Microsoft, I worked at IBM Business Consulting Services for more than 9 years. Back then, I implemented multi-million mega projects that span across the entire SDLC from conducting requirement/assessment study, mapping of client’s business processes/requirements to IT architecture/solutions, designing, development to deployment. I had led teams of more than 100 people (including enterprise customers, offshore development centres, business partners under my leadership) in delivering mainframe migration, turnkey and enterprise wide projects.
