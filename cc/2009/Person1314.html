Adam McGreggor’s Summary

First and foremost, I'm an opinionated blunt generalist.

Dabbling in a variety of things, mainly somehow involving IT management, or analysis.

IT wise, I'm a *nixer & windows bloke, although I tend to preferring dealing with OpenSource: mainly due to my hatred of wizards/the idiotization of the industry.

I will do Windows (and, at a push, Netware) consultancy, management, analyses, second-opinions, infrastructure rejigs, negotiations, strategy, migrations & upgrades, procurement, advice, and external consultancy.

Mainly Debian & FreeBSD, these days, with a bit of MacOS for good measure, I'm Old-Skool windows, from 3.0 to XP on the Workstation (I don't rate "Vista"), LanMan, NT, Server 2k/2003. Also Exchange, SQL (MySQL, PostGres, and MS), Exim, Apache...

Elsewhere, I'm (mainly) a Commercial Analyst, Policy Draftsman, Negotiator, Strategic Thinker/Directionalist.

Very broad knowledge-base into areas outside my 'specialism(s)', opinionated, confident.
