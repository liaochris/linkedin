DJ Crash’s Summary

=====

Crash is a legendary DJ. His career has spanned 20 years. He has worked venues in numerous states and foreign countries. Crash's clients have included Hugh Hefner, Vanity Fair, Vogue, Sony, and NBC. Crash has been a member of N.A.C.E., A.D.J.A. and, I.S.E.S. Crash has performed at more than 3000 shows, clubs and special events. He has a unique ability to read crowds and cater to specific group needs.

Crash is elegant, discreet, professional, prompt and has a warm personality. Groups interested in entertainment services should contact Crash directly.

Please take a moment to review these pages. DJ rates vary from $400 up to several thousand.

I am looking to work with creative, open-minded, fair, fun people !!!
