Jonathan Deiss’s Summary

My professional experience is primarily with ad agencies including most recently Dailey & Associates, Chiat Day/Tequila, Tribal DDB, and Trailer Park. Currently, I am Creative Director and owner of my own company Tasty Design Treats.

As an Art Director/Multimedia Developer, I've worked on marketing / brand / interactive / advertising campaigns for Sony, ConocoPhillips, Best Western, Callaway Golf, MacGregor Golf, Nissan, Harman International, ConAgra Foods.

As a Creative Director I have overseen accounts for Activision, Clearwire, Wells Fargo, Epson, ESPN, Warner Brothers, Disney, Kia and Sony to name a few.
