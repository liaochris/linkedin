Chandragupta Borkotoky’s Summary

Chandragupta Borkotoky is working as a Software Engineer with Sloka Telecom, Bangalore. Mr Chandragupta Borkotoky is also the incumbent Chairman of the ACM Chapter, A.E.C. He is an organizer of HeadStart and ACM Compute event which primarily provides a dynamic platform to the technology startups to showcase their technical edges.

He has published research papers in international journals such as ACM New York, IJCSA etc His papers are published in numerous conference proceedings such as eIT2007, Pioneer2k7, ICAC2008 etc. He enjoys tremendous experience in the fields of prototype software development in the domain of Brain Computer Interface.

Mr Borkotoky has been conferred one International, three National and one State Level Awards for his contributions in the field of novel research in human computer interaction. He is filing the patent for his invention of a PTS system for user authentication in October.
