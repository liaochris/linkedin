Jesse Albert’s Summary

Jesse Albert has been involved in interactive marketing and strategy for over a decade working in both corporate and entertainment digital media.

He has created innovative digital strategy and multi-platform (transmedia) solutions on behalf of world leading artists, brands and intellectual properties.

He often collaborates with (and in his current position as an agent, represents) thought leaders and best of breed to create activations that distill cutting edge theory into mass consumer adoption.

He is equally comfortable with entertainment creatives and silicon valley technologists and adept at bridging the gap between the two.

As an agent at ICM, Albert covers new media opportunities across a broad spectrum of entertainment business practices, including the sale of original IP, music, publishing, branded entertainment, corporate consulting and venture capital on behalf of the agency and it’s clients for one of the world’s largest and most respected talent agencies.
