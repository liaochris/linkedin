Paige Beal’s Summary

Assistant Professor in the Point Park University School of Business, and owner of MoxieMMC (marketing management consultancy) drawing on 20 plus years successfully managing media properties including internet, cable, radio, and print.

Proven business capability in marketing and advertising is supported by an MBA from the University of Pittsburgh Katz Graduate School of Business. Graduate of Leadership Pittsburgh program, previous board member of the Pittsburgh Advertising Federation and the Pittsburgh Radio and TV Club (a past president as well as winner of “Best Media Professional” and “Best Internet Professional” and Art Stein Award recipient).

Owner of Pittsburgh Alpacas - a small farm breeding champion peruvian alpacas.

Paige Beal’s Specialties:

Advertising, marketing, marketing and advertising research, internet and emerging technology advertising and marketing, media marketing and management.
