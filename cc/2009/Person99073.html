Tim Pendry’s Summary

TPPR is a private consulting business supporting sovereign states, educational institutions, corporations, high net worth individuals, law firms and NGOs with media consultancy, political analysis, public affairs and litigation support - www.tppr.co.uk

Pendry White Partnership is a strategic corporate marketing and business communications consultancy committed to the international professional services sector and to the UK private business sector - www.pendrywhite.com

The Pendry White Whiteboard Blog is at http://whiteboard.pendrywhite.biz

A new enterprise in specialist web media is under development and seeking support and funding. Further information and access to its secure web site can be supplied on application and on signature of an NDA

I act as Chairman and as a senior professional adviser at both TPPR and Pendry White, which is a subsidiary of TPPR with a 35% minority shareholder. Both companies are committed to personal service. Pendry White acts as agency service provider to TPPR. I am Managing Director and majority shareholder of the new enterprise which has an independent Chairman and I am a Director of The Middle East Association, the premier trade association for UK businesses operaring in the Middle East.

TPPR has special strengths in the analysis of Eurasian and Middle Eastern politics and has acted in support of private sector and sovereign interests in both war on terror and regional peace process issues.

My goals in this context are a) to refer and service business appropriate to TPPR and Pendry White, b) to ensure management succession at Pendry White in the long term (experienced individuals or associates should contact me) and c) to assess the viability of the new media enterprise under current economic conditions, ensure a high standard of editorial content and commercial direction and raise appropriate levels of funding in due course.
