Rebecca Herold, CIPP, CISSP, CISM, CISA’s Summary

Rebecca Herold, CISSP, CIPP, CISM, CISA, FLMI, "The Privacy Professor" tm, has over 17 years of information privacy and security experience and is currently the owner and principal for Rebecca Herold, LLC. Rebecca is also an author and instructor, currently an Adjunct Professor for the Norwich University Master of Science in Information Assurance Program. Prior to this, Rebecca was VP – Privacy Services and Chief Privacy Officer for DelCreo, Inc. Rebecca additionally has written chapters in many books covering information security and privacy and regulatory requirements. Rebecca has written hundreds of articles and has also authored a monthly privacy column in the CSI Alert newsletter since January 2001. Rebecca’s books include:

* The Privacy Papers
* Managing an Information Security and Privacy Awareness and Training Program
* The Privacy Management Toolkit
* The Definitive Guide to Security Inside the Perimeter
* The Business Executive Practical Guides to Compliance and Security Risks book series
* IT Compliance: The Essentials Series Volume I
* IT Compliance: The Essentials Series Volume II
* Improving IT Service Support Through ITIL
* The Practical Guide to HIPAA Privacy and Security Compliance
* Say What You Do
* The Encyclopedia of Information Assurance (2009)
* (Title as yet not established for ITIL V3 book) (Summer 2008)

Rebecca creates the quarterly multi-media information security & privacy awareness subscription resource, "Protecting Information." Rebecca also creates an ongoing series of info sec & privacy training modules.

Rebecca has a B.S. in Math and Computer Science and an M.A. in Computer Science and Education. Rebecca is a longtime member of ISACA, Infragard and IAPP. Rebecca can be reached at rebeccaherold@rebeccaherold.com, 515.996.2199 or 515.491.1564

Rebecca Herold, CIPP, CISSP, CISM, CISA’s Specialties:

Global privacy & security governance programs; data protection laws; privacy & security standards; supporting policies, procedures; data flow analysis; privacy & security program maturity models; incident & breach response; vendor security & privacy program review; security and privacy awareness communications, activities, events & training content development, evaluation and delivery; information security & privacy risk asssessment; privacy impact assessments; virtual privacy officer
