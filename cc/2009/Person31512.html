Erik MacKinnon’s Summary

My recent venture is Gravytrain, a marketing firm which in essence provides two main services for our clients:

1) Sales Lead Generation: We leverage our skills in marketing and advertising to find individuals in your business area that need your products and services. We then connect you or your sales team with those individuals for you to convert and close the sale.

2) Online / Internet Presence Management: We handle website design and management, customer marketing, search engine optimization, online advertising campaigns, copy and content generation and more to ensure that whenever someone wants to find your business online, they find it, and your current customers are always reminded that you are there.

Contact me here on LinkedIn or at http://gravytrain.ca if you'd like to know more.

--

Prior to founding Gravytrain, I worked with Vancouver's MindField Group - a Recruitment Process Outsourcing firm that is dedicated to helping their clients make the correct hire each and every time.

Visit http://rethinkrecruiting.com to check them out.

--

Before MindField Group, I spent 5 years working with the Entrepreneurs' Organization - http://www.eonetwork.org. EO is a peer-networking organization of over 7,000 entrepreneurs across the globe. No hired guns, no marketing guys, and no solicitation of any kind.

Of my 5 years there, the most recent 2 were managing the Global Student Entrepreneur Awards program.

The GSEA is a program that recognizes undergraduate student entrepreneurs from around the world for their amazing ability to juggle a full-time courseload with growing a company. Visit the GSEA online at http://www.gsea.org.

--

Prior to EO, I had a number of business ventures including a concert promotion company called Velocity Entertainment, and my "dot-com" Dustcloud Media, which was founded in 1999.
