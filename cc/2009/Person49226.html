John Horrigan’s Summary

Experienced Program Manager with diversified exposure in manufacturing, new product development, business operations, supply chain management and quality assurance with demonstrated success in on-time, on-budget delivery, team leadership and mentoring, resource allocation, market analysis, risk mitigation, process improvement and regulatory compliance.

John Horrigan’s Specialties:

A talented project manager and motivator with polished presentation skills and a sharp mathematical mind, John keeps his teams focused by driving the schedule through the timely closure of actions and deliverables while identifying and eliminating trojan horses. He achieves this through shared knowledge, process refinement, meticulous program reviews and real-time communication. John's mission is to service his customer before someone else does. His mantra is "consistency breeds excellence".
