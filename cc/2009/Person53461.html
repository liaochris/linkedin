Karl Smith’s Summary

Director of Digital who fully understands digital - strategy, business analysis, solutions, architecture and online marketing able to do the job as well as talk about it. I am an expert partner for outsourced digital strategy, end-to-end definition services and project management.

I am based between London and Edinburgh and am of French/Irish heritage making cross cultural thinking a fact of life, I also have strong links with the USA.

I work as a specialist digital consultant and am involved in strategic, web, software and e-commerce projects with clients whose main focus is increasing transaction frequency and accuracy through the provision of context focused and responsive user systems. I strongly believe that 'one size does not fit all' and am able to track change and innovate to assist my clients in extending their current reach and effectiveness.

digital architecture, solutions architect, usability, accessibility, information architecture (+non-english), interaction design, certified ScrumMaster, agile XP, agile User Stories, HCI research, research design, eye-tracking, heuristics, web analytics, pathway analytics, client management, vendor management, business analysis, process mapping, project resourcing, product development, product management, SEO, SEM, functional specification, technical specification, research reports
