Here is my response to a fellow Linkedin member
who wrote:

"Saw your post and went to your profile to learn more about you. I went to your website and was surprised it was [re: internet marketing] when you are listed as having your own law practice for the last 20 plus years. I was interested to find out what type of law you practice etc."

My reply ~

"My Law practice is advising clients on hiring attorneys (here in the SF Bay area) as I am acquainted with many of the top practitioners in a variety of practice areas.

I find creating and expanding my marketing and coaching business more gratifying than practicing law at this point in my life. I enjoy teaching relationship marketing and attraction marketing for small business. I find working with professionals launching new careers especially rewarding.

Like myself, many attorneys and other professionals seek second careers around the 20-year mark... Thanks for writing. Please keep in touch.

Peace and Prosperity,

susie"
