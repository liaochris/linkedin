Norman Brook’s Summary

Norman Brook originally qualified as a secondary school teacher before returning to study sports coaching. He started his professional career in sport in 1982 when he was appointed as a British National Athletics Coach.

Norman has a wide experience of sport developed through his work with UK Athletics, the Sports Council for Northern Ireland, the British Triathlon Federation and as a sports consutant. Norman has held senior leadership positions in sport and has extensive experience of sports coaching, high performance sport, sports development, sports governance, sports management, and sports marketing.

Norman has authored four books on sports training.

Norman is currently the owner of Brook Sport and Leisure Consultants.

Norman Brook’s Specialties:

Strategic, business and operational planning; Resource mobilisation; Marketing, media, commercial and sponsorship strategies; Sport and social development programmes; Leadership development and modern organisational governance; Sports tourism linked to development initiatives; Event and project management; Skills development programmes; High performance sport from talent identification through to medal winning success; Project monitoring and evaluation.
