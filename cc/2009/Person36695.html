Glenna Wiseman’s Summary

I am a seasoned executive with a long standing passion for the application of business principles to “green” goals. I believe strongly that the energy sector offers one of the greatest opportunities for transformation to a cooler, more sustainable planet.

My post as VP of marketing at the HelioGroup affords me vision on energy efficiency, renewable energy (solar photovoltaics in particular) and the financing challenges inherent in the clean tech sector. The HelioGroup consists of three firms with an integrated approach to the green energy industry: HelioPower, founded in 2001 and a leader in engineered solar photovoltaic installations; HelioEMS, energy management solutions and Helio Micro Utility, a developer/owner of solar generation systems. URL’s: www.HelioPower.com; www.HelioEMS.com and www.HeliomU.com.

Solar Industry Senior Marketing, PR & Web Executive
Dynamic strategist whose passion, backed by sharp skills, has created successful campaigns, new companies and growth for existing firms

Solar industry experienced professional with proven business building skills in range of industry sectors, across all size company structures: multi-national Fortune 100 to “on the napkin” start up! Skilled strategist having created and executed business plan objectives. Experience working in collaborative environment to enter public IPO markets. Excellent team motivation and leadership abilities. Superb communications, interpersonal and presentation skills.
