Charlie Davidson’s Summary

Although a born and bred Highlander, Charlie has recently joined the Sitekit team down in Oxfordshire helping to look after some of the companies key accounts. Before Sitekit, he spent four years at the University of Stirling getting his BA (Hon’s) in International Business, before carrying out a yearlong business internship with digital research institute, Distance Lab. He is passionate about making things happen, questioning the tried and tested, and helping his clients maximise their web potential.
