Steven Puma’s Summary

I've recently graduated with an MBA in Sustainable Management from the Presidio School of Management, and my interests are in the areas of emerging technology, mass collaboration, strategic planning, sustainable business and Outcome-Driven Innovation (ODI) customer research and product development.

My prior background includes over 10 years of experience in the IT field, focused on automated software deployment and standardized desktops with Microsoft operating systems.
