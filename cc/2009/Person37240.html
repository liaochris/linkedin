Graham Cobb’s Summary

I am Director of Service Delivery Marketing for Telcordia. I am responsible for product marketing for the Service Delivery Solutions business unit including the Real Time Charging and Policy solution provided to Telcordia's major mobile customers such as Tata, Oi, Orange and Swisscom Mobile.

I have 25 years technical and management experience in designing, developing, implementing and marketing telecommunications solutions for many fixed and mobile network operators. I have experience of both the business and technical aspects of advanced value added services, real-time charging and mobile marketing. In addition, I have architected, designed, developed, implemented or managed all aspects of data and voice communications solutions including network infrastructure, network management and network intelligence.

I started my career developing data networking products for Digital Equipment (now HP) before moving into voice telecommunications about fifteen years ago. Before joining Telcordia in 1999, I was IN Business Development Manager for Stratus Computers where I provided solutions to operators including Orange UK and Omnitel Pronto Italia (now Vodafone).
