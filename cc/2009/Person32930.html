Felicia C. Sullivan’s Summary

Felicia C. Sullivan is a New York-based author, online marketing professional and project manager. She is the author of the memoir The Sky Isn't Visible from Here (Algonquin Books, 2008), Sr. Online Marketing Manager at HarperCollins, the founder of the critically acclaimed literary journal Small Spiral Notebook, producer of a podcast radio show, and curator of a literary reading series. Sullivan has over a decade of experience launching, marketing, and managing online properties for boutique firms to multi-million dollar corporations.

Sullivan launched a successful luxury goods e-tailer (The Collection, LLC), and was a Sr. Project Manager at multi-million dollar companies (Time Warner Cable, luxlook.com - bought out by eluxury.com). She also has a background in finance (Morgan Stanley, Chase) and bakes in her spare time.

o Strategic and forward thinking: able to digest information flow and rapid changes in the marketplace to plan and executive creative marketing campaigns
o Works well under pressure, prioritizes, balances multiple responsibilities and multiple personalities/management styles
o Vigilant PR specialist/promoter: from aggressive blog outreach to innovative event planning, to building integral third-party relationships, unique pr strategies are always paramount
o Able to take risks to build results
o Passionate about the creative but has a strong business sense
o Strong business acumen & professionalism, excellent presentation skills w/solid communication skills.
o Can digest complexity and deliver simplicity.

Her motto: If I haven't already done it, trust me, I will.
