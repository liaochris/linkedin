Samuel Zarbock’s Summary

I'd like to obtain ordered results from apparently chaotic data.
While manipulating, classifying and clustering numbers or words, I set large amounts of data to be presented to users in both usable and interactive way.
What I actually look for in IT projects I'm working on, is a high
quality interaction between the user and the information.

I've been working for 2,5 years exclusively on texts, implementing chatterbots and dialogic AI systems. After this experience I dicovered myself deeply interested into textual analysis of large corpora. This is what I am currently exploring in private projects.
