Abhay Padgaonkar’s Summary

Abhay Padgaonkar is the founder and President of Innovative Solutions Consulting, LLC. He is an award-winning management consultant, author, and speaker.

Abhay advises marquee clients such as American Express on improving organizational and individual performance by “making the complex simple and making the simple work.” Abhay is a two-time winner of an “Excellence in Practice” honor by ASTD for his innovative work.

He writes the ‘Smart Manager’ column for Modern Pharmaceuticals magazine and has authored and published popular articles on a variety of topics such as leadership, strategy, customer loyalty, innovation, employee engagement, change management, sales effectiveness, marketing, communications, project management, training, demographics, and diversity in reputable print and online publications, including Performance Improvement, Marketing Profs, Leadership Excellence, Chain Store Age, HR.com, Wise Marketer, Express Pharma, Business Knowhow, CEO Refresher, Rain Today, Top-Consultant, Projects@Work, Training Magazine, and The Business Journal. His articles have been translated into many languages around the world.

He has been a speaker for audiences across the country representing various organizations as well as internationally.

Abhay regularly shares his views on today's management challenges with reporters and journalists from publications including, The Wall Street Journal, HR Magazine, MSNBC.com, SHRM Online, The Business Journal, The London Free Press, The Journal News, The Leading Edge, and Sales and Marketing Management Magazine for a feature article on NBC's Emmy-winning show “The Office.”

He has been a member of Institute of Management Consultants (IMC), Society for Human Resource Management (SHRM), and The Indus Entrepreneurs (TiE).

Abhay has earned an M.B.A. in Finance and MIS from Rice University, M.S. in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Kentucky, and B. Tech. from Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay.
