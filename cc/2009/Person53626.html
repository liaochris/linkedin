Mary "Katie" LaFrance’s Summary

I work with small business owners, solo entrepreneurs and other busy professionals to maximize their internet presence from a marketing standpoint. I set up shopping carts, contact capture tools, auto responders, email blasts and more.

I will help you to create a strategic social media and internet marketing plan for your unique needs and then assist you with implementing that plan. Setting up and assistance with maintaining your social media/network accounts is not only time consuming but requires a good working knowledge of how to best leverage the different social media platforms.

I have worked with some of the biggest names in marketing and coaching - let me share that knowledge and expertise with you. Partner with me now to learn why I have messages from my clients' clients DAILY that say: "Thank you for your follow up. The most frustrating thing is the coaches who have a big practice but terrible administrative support. This is refreshing. :-)" - MC, 11/2008.
