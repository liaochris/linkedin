Jackie Gagarin Curry’s Summary

User experience specialist who helps companies identify UI (user interface) related requirements, create detailed product specifications, and test usability in order to create more useful and usable products, reduce development costs, and decrease time to market.

I have worked on a variety of projects since I started in the industry in 1995. These include e-commerce software, award-winning security software, banking software and consumer web sites.

Jackie Gagarin Curry’s Specialties:

- Concept design (UCD is my methodology)
- Prototyping (sketches , wireframe, blueprint, etc)
- Usability testing (traditional or eye-tracking)
- Strategy (product, service & market opportunity)

Also skilled at brainstorming and collaboration across competencies and departments, developing clear product requirements specifying features, functionality and use cases in order to plan products that best serve customer needs.
