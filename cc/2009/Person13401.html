Brian Brady’s Summary

The mortgage markets are in a shambles; nobody can get financed anymore. Does it help to have an experienced loan originator with a financial planning background? 736 families have thought so over the past 13 years. I've helped them and funded just under 1700 loans during that time. I'd love to help you.

If you're a veteran, we thrive on helping you. You'll have the option of a zero down payment loan and competitive rates. Combine that with a savvy real estate agent's negotiation skills and you might just get your dream home for VERY little money out of your pocket.

Thinking of buying your first home? I"ll use government-insured programs like FHA, CalSTRS, and CalHFA to minimize the cost out of pocket and secure you a great rate at a payment we KNOW you can afford.

More established customers and real estate investors tap my financial planning experience when they look for a loan solution. Strong analytical skills, combined with market savvy help you get the proper financing through conventional or portfolio loans.

REALTORs all over the country feel comfortable referring their best buyers to me because I get the job done. My team of three stays in constant communication with you, your buyers, and the listing agent to ensure a smooth transaction. "No surprises" is a tough mantra in a mortgage market that surprises us weekly. We'll deliver with our commitment to speed.

Call me at (858)-777-9751 to schedule a consultation.

I love this job- you'll see it when you talk to me.
