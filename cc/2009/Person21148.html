Chuck Russom’s Summary

Award winning game audio veteran with over 10 years of industry experience. I’ve worked in-house for Activision, EA, Sony Computer Entertainment, and New World Computing. I have been freelance since 2008.

Worked on top game franchises such as God of War, Call Of Duty, Medal of Honor, James Bond, and Heroes of Might and Magic.

I’ve served as Audio Director, Sound Designer, Consultant, Sound Effects Recordist, Dialog Supervisor, Music Supervisor, Sound Implementer, you name it, I’ve done it!

Extensive experience in coordinating and conducting field recording shoots (guns, explosions, animals, etc). I've served as a sound designer on dozens of titles, with heavy focus on weapons and creature design. Also highly versed in the art/skill of implementation. I've led several large audio teams and worked on titles with budgets large, small, and huge.
