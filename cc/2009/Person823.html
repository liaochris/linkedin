Ab Kuijer’s Summary

JuniorSenior is a one-stop shop with the best specialists in-house. Brand strategists, researchers, creative directors, copywriters, event planners, Flash 10 designers and PR-pro’s; they all working jointly together for you! That makes JuniorSenior a solution driven, creative provider that enables you to reach your marketing targets and even more. From strategy to execution; JuniorSenior always delivers within the budget and beyond your expectations.

Our playground is Europe; from East to West and we are delighted to be working for clients such as Unilever, Johnson & Johnson, Chupa Chups and Ubisoft. They chose us because we are specialised in communication with young people (children, students and starters).
