Greg Balanko-Dickson’s Summary

Types of Business Owners Greg Works With

I work with successful businessmen who have owned a business for 10+ years, have employees, and sell to other businesses. They are ready to make the transition from business owner to CEO. Eager to learn how to make their business work for them instead of them working for it.

Three Biggest Challenges Faced by Greg's Clients

Creating a self-managed, self-directed corporate culture that liberates them from the tyranny of a growing, successful business that is dependent on their energy and personal day-to-day involvement.

Developing the structure, systems, and tools to manage day-to-day operations with or without the owners' presence.

Knowing how to convert existing products and services into self-renewing profit centers that help the business achieve critical mass.

What Clients Can Expect to Experience

Confidence knowing they have the tools, people, and framework in place to operate the business with ease.

Assurance knowing that their values and corporate culture will safely pilot the business throughout its life cycle.

Knowledge that they have a meaningful succession plan for themselves and key employees.

A realistic and achievable plan to achieve financial freedom and a lifestyle of their choice.

Areas of Specialty

Outcome focused business planning, lifestyle design, and financial blueprints that liberate you from the tyranny of a growing and successful business. Whether you want to achieve better life balance, improve the customer service experience, increase effectiveness of marketing & sales, attract amazing employees, or develop a strategic business plan to achieve critical mass and financial independence; you will find the coaching, support, resources, tools, and strategies to convert your vision into reality.

Greg Balanko-Dickson’s Specialties:
