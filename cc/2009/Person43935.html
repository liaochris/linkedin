Jason Drakeford’s Summary

Hi! Right now I am Art Director at BBDO and in my spare time freelance in motion graphics, design and illustration. Most recently I've been filming in HD, learning new post production techniques and streamlining the process of creating a quality video piece in a short amount of time without skimping on the concept or quality. To view case studies of my most recent work go to www.jasondrakeford.com
