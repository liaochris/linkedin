Pere Rosales’s Summary

Traditional interruptive advertising, branding and marketing isn't working anymore. Now the consumers have the power, you can't interrupt them, but they can interrupt you...

People are not targets anymore, people are communities, and they expect to be treated with care and respect. Today the only way to achieve customer loyalty and return business is to adapt your marketing model to engage people. Believe that the world needs, not more communication, but better communication.

Ability to understand consumers in the digital space and to use that understanding to help client’s identify opportunities and meet business needs on-line.

Experience in the following areas:

• Strategy – designing solutions that meet clients’ digital business objectives.

• Customer experience – in depth qualitative and quantitative understanding of consumers behaviour on-line.

• Research services – in depth market knowledge of digital developments and their implications on existing business models.

• Market intelligence – competitive landscape reviews and on-going sector monitoring.

• Knowledge share – sector briefings for senior executive and non-digital teams.

• Solution management – providing programme and project management resource to help deliver digital change.

Experience with business processes in the digital area and a good understanding of how digital technology supports business

Experience of senior management in a consultancy practice including charging/billing processes, marketing/ business development.

Strong people management skills & experience.
