Abhishek Desai’s Summary

I have worked with Patni Computers (www.patni.com) as a Software Developer in 2003 and then worked with Systems Plus Pvt. Ltd. (www.spluspl.com) as a Software Consultant.

Not satisfied with the jobs, I started DIGICorp Information Systems Pvt. Ltd. with 3 of my class mates in 2004. From then we have taken it to new heights every year and are having client base in US, Europe and Middle East now.

We are also involved in creating our own web based concepts called www.SignInStyle.com, www.PetPooja.com and www.Rivals4Ever.com. Many more concepts are coming very soon.

Our goal is to make this world a better place by leveraging Technology and Internet. and we want to build a great company known for its work culture and client satisfaction.
