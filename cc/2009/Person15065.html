Julie Hopkins’s Summary

I have been photographing people, places and things since 1997. I was a professional ballet dancer for thirteen years and my dance career that spanned the globe, along with a degree in art history, and a passion for art, helped to develop my discerning eye for beauty. I am a member in good standing of Association for Wedding Professionals International, Advertising Photographers of America, Hollywood Chamber of Commerce and Le Tip (business networking group). I also hold a certificate in photography from Santa Monica College. I am published in Dance Magazine, Pointe magazine, Back Stage (west) and exhibit my dance imagery in local shows.

Julie Hopkins’s Specialties:

Capturing the precise moment of gesture in both portraiture and dance.
