Noel Duan’s Summary

I am a high school senior from Silicon Valley (barely 18!) with great interest in exploring my interests in entrepreneurship, writing, journalism, publishing, fashion, public relations, online media, marketing, blogging, and communication.

I am eager to learn everything and anything at my young age -- my enthusiasm for learning and exploring has propelled me to a lot of amazing ventures, from living in New York City independently at age 17 to intern for Seventeen Magazine to opening Miss Couturable (http://www.misscouturable.com), my beloved teen lifestyle/fashion blog.

There are so many open doors in this world -- and I hope I won't go wrong, whichever door I choose!

Don't hesitate to email me at noel at misscouturable dot com.
