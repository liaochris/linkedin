Frans Dekker’s Summary

BACKGROUND & APPROACH:  

Throughout his career, Mr. Dekker has developed a proven track record in delivering Strategic Change, Development, & Performance Improvement results in (Program) Management, Consultancy, Training, & Coaching roles in Consumer Electronics, Office Equipment, Medical Equipment, Financial Services, Automotive, Telecom, Government, and Healthcare.

  While working towards achieving strategic objectives, his approach is both people and results-oriented, and incorporates the development of job related and interpersonal skills, as well as the improvement of team dynamics.   SERVICE OFFERING:

He supports clients in becoming more effective and efficient in defining and getting the results they want, and has a proven track record of adding value to organizations in the following areas:

  1). Leading the development & delivery of strategic programs.

 4). Increasing (inter)personal effectiveness of people in leadership positions (incl. program & project managers), as well as of people in customer facing roles.
