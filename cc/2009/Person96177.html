Makeup Artist Suzanne Patterson’s Summary

Suzanne's company, Creative Artistry & FX, is based in Washington DC, and provides an extensive range of professional makeup services, special makeup effects, and hair styling for film, video/television and print. Her decades of experience includes movies, television series and specials, documentaries, commercial ad campaigns, and network televised sports. Various productions in which she has been the makeup department head have won multiple major industry awards, such as the Emmy, Telly, Cine Eagle, Aegis, Omni Intermedia, Communicator, and International Davey Awards.

She has worked with and designed makeup for some of Hollywood's biggest stars such as Arnold Schwarzenegger, Melanie Griffith, Don Johnson, Jennifer Love Hewitt, Magic Johnson, Tim Allen, Charlie Sheen, Queen Latifah, and numerous other numerous celebrities, professional athletes, and high profile clients.

Suzanne is regarded as a national expert on (HD) High Definition makeup techniques, and a sought after makeup artist for television and video productions to perform her services in this format. Her makeup and hair grooming expertise frequently serves National Politicians, corporate executives, and famous personalities in and around the nation's Capital.

Suzanne is a veteran magazine feature article author with many articles written and published in several magazines. Her previous editorial experience includes Associate Editor of 1st Hold Magazine, Airbrush Editor of Face Painting International Magazine, and West Coast Contributing Editor for FilmCrew Magazine. She continues to write makeup and skin care articles exclusively featured on her websites and for syndication.

For more information and to see my celebrity makeup photos and client list visit www.creativeartistryfx.com
