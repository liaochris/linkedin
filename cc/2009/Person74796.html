Nicholas D. Nakao’s Summary

My experience comes from multiple years of work in the business, various seminars and classes, and plenty of happy clients. I work with specialty programs like the "Hercules Affordable Housing Program" and also provide my own Public Informative Seminars for everyone to help educate the community. Call me today for information on special home buyer programs. I work alongside several assistance programs and would love to share them with you.

I am constantly searching for ways to better improve myself for my clients and students. I have been trained in some of the most prestigious real estate programs such as the “By Referral Only” program and "Level 3 Performance Training". I am now also certified for the "Monthly Mortgage Assistance Program (MMAP)". I have attended several seminars and schools to reach higher levels of knowledge and a higher grade of provided service, and to this day, I am still learning more, for you!

I now offer the "Talking House" service to all of my listings free of charge and offer rentals of the service to anyone selling their home on their own. Ask me how and what it can do for you or check out my "Real Estate" page! It is proven!

My success comes from people who have given me the chance to provide a service that is above and beyond what you may expect from other insurance, real estate, and loan professionals. Referrals are the “lifeblood” of my business and I greatly appreciate any words of approval. I strongly believe that there is no such thing as a “closed deal". I service everyone as a client for life!
