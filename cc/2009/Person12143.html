Bob Corporaal’s Summary

As a freelance senior visual & interaction designer I work with clients to develop ideas and concepts into fullgrown interactive experiences. In my work I take an integral approach, balancing the different ingredients and stakeholders. Asking the difficult questions and challenging existing ideas when needed. My focus is always on creating great user experiences keeping in mind that usability and brand experience are essentially one.

What makes me sets me apart is that I combine 7+ years of (interaction) design experience with a technical background (aerospace engineering). This allows me to work directly with both the marketing/client and development end of a project, often forming the bridge between both ends.

Above all I'm professional and curious: I enjoy working in multidisciplinary teams, combining knowledge from different fields, learning new things and exploring new ideas.

Feel free to contact me for more information, my portfolio or concerning projects.
