Eric Barker’s Summary

Seasoned media professional adept at achievement of bottom line impact through hands-on guidance of creative content design and brand messaging. Tenured career in Hollywood includes management and development of multi-medium entertainment solutions (film, video games, animation) for toptier companies like Disney, Sony and 20th Century Fox.
