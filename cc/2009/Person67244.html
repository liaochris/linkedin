    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; DIS; Entertainment industry)

    1981 — 1993 (12 years)

    Supervised Systems Programmers in the installation and maintenance of the Walt Disney Company corporate data communications network which consisted of seven Unisys DCPs, a Stratus 220, a Stratus 75, three Unisys 2200 mainframes, five Unisys 90/80 mainframes, three IBM AS/400s, and an IBM 3090.
