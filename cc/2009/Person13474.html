Brian Driscoll’s Summary

Currently I am an Application Developer SoftAssist, Inc., an award-winning E-Learning software development company located in the Philadelphia, PA area. In this position I have worked in a team environment to produce web-based training products and line of business applications using primarily my expertise in Flash (ActionScript 2.0), JavaScript, C# .NET, WCF, Silverlight 2.0/3.0, Flash Interactive Server 3.0, SQL Server 2005, and IIS 6.0.
