Nancy Clemens’s Summary

I can provide your company/group with leadership and facilitation to develop the common vision, value proposition, and integrated action plan required to transition great technology into a profitable business. I have a proven track record of aligning corporate values, product development, and target-market requirements in a way that builds strong brand and corporate identity, and creates a dedicated (and often fanatical) user-base.

Having launched several successful companies, I have strong management-level expertise and possess the flexibility, understanding, resources and systems-thinking required to successfully grow an evolving technology company to the next level.

My experience spans critical components of a developing company, including strategic planning, roadmap development, idea/concept development, branding/user-centered design, product definition, project management, business development, guerilla marketing, and market analysis. My background in areas including stereoscopic 3D, visualization, human-computer interaction, social media and 3D games—coupled with my obsession with leading-edge communication technologies—allows me to apply my knowledge across a variety of technology fields.   

One of my great strengths is that I enjoy "humanizing" technology, making it accessible and easily understandable, whether the intended audience be your target market, potential partners, or investors. I will lead your team in creating a strategic and tactical plan that clearly conveys your value proposition through all aspects of your business, in ways that will deeply engage your audience—translating directly into increased revenues.   

Send me an email or give me a call now, and let’s discuss ways that your organization can benefit from my experience and capabilities!
