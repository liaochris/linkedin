Giuseppe Lugano’s Summary

Five years of experience in the telecommunication industry. Eight years of experience in international organizations (Erasmus Student Network, Tsemppi and Maailma Tutuksi). My professional goal is to contribute to the development of a human-centric European Ubiquitous Network Society through high-quality research conducted in the public or private sector as consultant or project leader.

My current research is focusing on building a conceptual framework for design of mobile social software applications (MoSoSo) supporting social capital.

Giuseppe Lugano’s Specialties:

My expertise is in predicting societal trends, observing and investigating utilization of ICT in society through three dimensions of user experience: local/global, real/virtual, individual/collective.
