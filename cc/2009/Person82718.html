Rebel Brown’s Summary

I've consulted for over twenty years (wow), focused on creating go-to-market strategy, market launches and fund raising. I also have significant sales and channel experience. My clients say I'm a closet geek. I probably am. I dig technology & complex products.

I've seen a lot, thanks to diverse client experience. I intuitively know what works. I also know what doesn't work. I've learned from my own mistakes, and from those I've seen along the way.

• For the last 10 years I’ve specialized in turnarounds/start-arounds. Companies with great value in IP, customer bases and more.They've hit some kind of wall and must reinvent to survive. There's so much opportunity in these businesses - I appreciate the chance to help them soar again!

• I have significant experience working with start-ups & early stage clients.I love helping young companies get off the ground.The key is to focus in areas with short time-to-profitable revenue & strong differentiation. I’ve helped 30+ start-ups in company & product strategy, business development/M&A'/channels and market launches.

• I also help companies who are humming along enjoying their flight.There's always another opportunity just ahead. I help these companies find and capture that next updraft, and the next.

• This is the 3rd economic down and up I’ve experienced as a consultant. I have a good handle on how to define and create actionable strategies to drive short term profitable revenue. That's exactly what companies need to do now. Plan for the up turn. Reinventing themselves.

• My clients tell me I’m especially skilled at identifying opportunities and then creating a strategy to quickly and profitably capture them. You can read about my clients, their own words, on my website.

* Prior to starting my consulting business in the late 1980's, I sold PCM mainframes and software to the Fortune 500, primarily in the financial services and aerospace industry.
