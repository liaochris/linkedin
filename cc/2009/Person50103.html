Jonathan Hedrick’s Summary

My most recent experience includes a tenure as Branch Manager of the local Express Employment Professionals franchise in the New River Valley of Southwest Virginia. I was both a successful Staffing Consultant, Branch Manager, and Business Development Manager with Express from early 2006 to late 2008. Working with the then franchise owner, Joe Farmer, and former collegues Mike McCoy, Rylee Rhodes and Maggie Davis, we successfully serviced our clients by finding qualified, motivated individuals for various positions.

Before my tenure with Express I was a prominent student leader that successfully completed two Bachelors degrees at Christopher Newport University.

My goals include:
I am currently seeking gainful employment with long term growth that rewards experience and appreciates dedication with an emphasis on finacial and professional awards for educated, professional individuals. In the future I would like to pursue opening my own business.

I am a Dedicated and focused professional who prioritizies and completes multiple tasks to achieve project goals. I am both Independent and self-motivated, with excellent research, writing, interviewing and interpersonal skills; able to grow positive relationships with clients and colleagues at all organizational levels.

I am a Highly organized and detailed-oriented team player motivated to succeed at profitable and rewarding career in a positive business environment. I have valuable experience in all aspects of workforce selection and human resource functions; performance management, employee relations, resource planning, recruitment, selection, termination and career specific training.

I am an experienced communicater that effectively has tangible success in interpersonal, managerial and in public speaking orientated situations.
