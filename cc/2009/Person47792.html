Jeremy Lattimore’s Summary

Spending the time to analyze and understand the business problem allows for proper business solution to be designed. The architecture is then created to support the business solution. From the solution design and the architecture creation a proper piece of software can then be created to solve the issue.

By utilizing this mindset and over 10 years experience in the solutions development arena, Jeremy is able to provide solutions to complex issues. It is always very important to focus on understanding business and technology. Understanding business allows you to outline a solution, the technology allows you to create and implement the solution. It is very important to balance the two.

From the business aspect, Jeremy has sought education to build the base for this by attaining his MBA in 2004. He has also attained an experience base in many industries over his career including:
• Insurance
• Financial Services
• Education
• Non-Profit
• Real Estate
• Commercial Mortgage

From the technology aspect Jeremy holds as Bachelor of Science in Computer Information Systems and is a Microsoft Certified Solution Developer. Jeremy has spent a significant portion of his career as a consultant in the areas of web application development, Windows application development, multi-tier (n-tier) development, and database development.

He has worked in many technologies throughout his career. Some of the technologies/languages that he is versed in include:
• VB.Net
• C#
• T-SQL
• JavaScript
• Web Services
• SQL Server Integration Services (SSIS)
• SQL Server 2000 - 2005

With over 5 years of experience in lead and management roles he has been able to experience projects from both a developer and management roles. This allows him to see a project from a big picture standpoint with the ability to take into account business and management needs.
