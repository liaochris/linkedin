Peter Van Osta’s Summary

- An MD and biochemist with more than 10 years of experience in organizing and delivering process and technology innovation in the pharmaceutical industry (enabling technologies and processes) and in healthcare (eHealth).
- Capable of bridging the gap between business, science and technology (ICT)
- Solving problems in a creative way, with the ability to create solutions for complex business and scientific challenges.
- Organizational project capability development, using industry standards like CMMi and PMBOK
- Developing advanced enabling technologies, such as High Content Screening (HCS) and high-end digital image analysis.

Languages: Dutch, English, German and basic French
