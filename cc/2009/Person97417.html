Terence Eden’s Summary

A creative, technically minded Mobile Internet manager.

I have always had a passionate interest in technology and the way it shapes our societies and ourselves. Having become an expert in the field of mobile portals and the relationship they have with their users, I am keen to apply that knowledge to see how we can shape all our services to better fit our customers' diverse needs.

I am an experienced and confident presenter. I represent Vodafone by speaking at Mobile Monday London and MobileCampBrighton amongst other internal and external conferences.
As a trained actor, I am comfortable interacting with all audiences.

I enjoy organising sponsorship for events such as BarCampLondon6 and other Social Media Events.
I find them invaluable sources of unfiltered feedback from users and non-users.

As a 6-Sigma Green Belt, I take great pride in optimising procedures and outputs of my team. I firmly believe in the use of statistical analysis to investigate our problems and predict our future needs.

I enjoy attending formal Focus Group and participating in Vodafone UK's user communities. I love learning from our customers.

I'm experienced in turning fluffy marketing thoughts into solid, dependable requirements - and turning scary HTTP error codes into pretty, easy to understand graphs.

Interested in making things better.
