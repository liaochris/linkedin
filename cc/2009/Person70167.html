Morten Meyer’s Summary

A highly experienced electrical engineer, superintendent and project manager with more than 22 years experience from the shipping industry. Broad experience in Marine Operation and Ship Management together with good Administration skills. Proficient in all areas relating to technical operation of ships, ranging from electrical maintenance and design to project management and drydocking. Electrical project manager for several multi-million dollar ship conversion projects. Proven supervisory skills and can work as part of a team or using own initiative to reach goals. Knowledge and hands-on experience from most marine technical systems including diesel electric propulsion and high voltage power plants.
