José Almeida’s Summary

José is a senior IT Consultant, specializing in highly-distributed solution architectures in complex IT environments and challenging business scenarios. Over the years, José has worked with customers in various industries, including Financial Services, Public Sector and TelCo, designing and implementing software architectures in various technological areas, from Knowledge and Information Management to LOB Applications, CRM and Middleware solutions.

As an experienced consultant, José also works with senior-level IT Management on Enterprise Architecture, helping IT organizations to realize value from their IT investments and achieve greater business alignment.
José is also a regular speaker at public and internal Microsoft events, focusing on the software development lifecycle and how the software industry can help organizations achieve better results.

José has a special interest in agile software development techniques, having co-authored a book on agile software development, titled "Desenvolvimento Orientado por Objectos - Domain-Driven Design, Testes Unitários e Refactoring" (Object Oriented Development - Domain-Driven Design, Unit Testing and Refactoring)
ISBN: 989-615-013-3
