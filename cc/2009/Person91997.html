John Gheur’s Summary

John brings a hands-on creative strategic background with leadership and vision in establishing companies' unique brands within the marketplace. John has become an expert in advertising & interactive entertainment creative marketing. With strong creative, strategic and production skills specialized in youth entertainment marketing, John has the experience to handle fortune 500 company brands hands on. He has excellent client services skills in building relationships and managing expectations for a broad range of client types and award winning campaigns.

From 1999-2004 at DNA Studio, John was instrumental in collaborating and developing DNA Studio from a small boutique agency into the Top 50 Agencies in the world. As Executive Creative Director, John oversaw DNA Studio's creative department. He managed new and in-progress projects from concept to new technology research, to design and marketing initiatives. He has managed countless high-profile accounts in the film, music and consumer-product industries, such as Universal, Sony, Warner Brothers, Coca-Cola, Anheuser Busch, McDonalds, HP, Cunard Cruise lines, and Boost Mobile to name a few.

Prior to 1999, a past history in advertising and design and entertainment creative John also worked with Paramount Television as Director of Client Services. As Director, He oversaw all of Paramount TV's marketing creative in on-air, outdoor, and print medias. Prior to entering in the entertainment space John worked in print production design, immerging internet technologies, imaging and photography with Think New Ideas (formerlly the Mednik Group)

With over 17 years of creative experience, John was trained at the acclaimed Otis/Parsons School of Art/Design and brings to his work his incomparable skills as a creative leader, photographer, artist and designer. Brought up all over the world, he also provides an invaluable global perspective to any project he takes on.

John Gheur’s Specialties:

Interactive, business development, strategy, creative concepting, production management, new media, development, and an incomparable global perspective.
