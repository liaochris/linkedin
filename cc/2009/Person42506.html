Jade De Leon’s Summary

I am a Sydney, Australia based, nationally published, professional photographer with over 13 years working commercial and lifestyle portrait photography experience. My specialty is people.

Additionally, I am a certified by New York University as an organizational and executive coach. I often offer my personal coaching services on a pro-bono basis to help those who really need it and want to make change, mainly those who are unemployed and those who suffer from panic attacks and depression.

Very involved in community service.

Jade De Leon’s Specialties:

Digital photography, portraits, corporate and executive photos, general photography needs. New York University certified organizational and executive coach. Coaching, brainstorming, troubleshooting, business and personal development, project management, business planning, strategy, and development planning. Fluent in Spanish. Senior First Aid Certified by Australian Red Cross.
