Marco Beglia’s Summary

Project Financing.
International Project Management.
International Affairs - International Trade and Development.

A graduate in International Affairs and Economics, I have experience in international affairs and some field experience (Qatar, Lebanon, Egypt). Currently a Project Financing Assistant, I support the Financial and Commercial Departments in elaborating and analyzing the project cash flow and finding financing tools. I also provide support the internationalization of the company drafting MoUs, LoIs, Business Plans and participating to the negotiations with the potential partners.

In 2008 I have been anInternational Project Assistant at FIARD managing and coordinating portfolios, with daily contacts with partners from EU countries, Middle-East and North-Africa.

In 2007, I have been an intern at the International Trade Centre - UNCTAD/WTO based in Geneva, Switzerland. Among the main tasks carried out: Economic & Financial Data collection & elaboration, Research (Finance for Trade), Press Abstract writing and dissemination. Support to the Finance for Trade Programme: support to the design of structured financial tools for SMEs, feasibility and pre-feasibility studies, context analysis (finance and economics).

Of Italian origin and trilingual in Italian, English, French and working knowledge of Spanish, I am able to develop and maintain effective work relationships with different nationals and cultures while maintaining sensitivity and respect for diversity. I possess the ability to identify strategic issues and solve them with my interpersonal skills, delivering quick and effective solutions to complex problems.
