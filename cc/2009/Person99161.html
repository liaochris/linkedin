Tim Vermeir’s Summary

I am a member of the Brussels bar (advocaat/avocat) specialised in utilities law (energy, water, electronic communication), public procurement and general public law.

The Legal500, edition 2009, states the following about me:

"Tim Vermeir has moved from Loyens & Loeff to Publius where clients include Wingas GmbH, carving out a key niche in EU energy law, as an alternative to larger practices in dealing with regulators."

In 2007 and in 2008 I was named one out of three Belgian lawyers to be recommended in the Expert Guide - Energy and Natural Resource Lawyers. In the Legal 500 I am described as "[knowing] the utilities market (energy, water) in the Flemish Region very well" (edition 2008), "[having] experience of regulatory implications of major projects, having advised on the construction of a direct gas pipeline in Antwerp" and being "an impressive advocate with good connections and insights, especially in the energy sector." (edition 2007)

Tim Vermeir’s Specialties:

Energy Law, Utilities Law, Administrative Law, Public Procurement Law
