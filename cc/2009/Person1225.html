Adam Gordon’s Summary

Every company tells a story...don't it?

Or, if it doesn't it should. Stories connect with people; we have evolved to learn from, remember, and be touched by stories. Telling a company's story well makes the difference between flourishing and mediocrity; being preferred versus identifiable; being sought versus available.

I am an award-winning story teller. In business speak this means I am a senior marketer with a strong technical background a experience in both B2B and B2C environments.

When all of your marketing supports your story in a way that is outcome-oriented, it all comes together and sings to people. Every campaign, every piece of material, every web page, every online element drives your audiences through their sales cycles.

Better ROI? Sure. And, more than that, greater efficiency, enhanced memorability, and--let's face it--more fun.

Adam Gordon’s Specialties:

Outcome-oriented marketing. Totally focused on achieving one goal: yours.

MarketWise. A unique market analysis tool for understanding and growing markets. Now your marketing is based on knowledge and surety, not intuition.

Strategy. Knowing what story to tell and to whom.

Writing and Messaging. Get the story right, readable, and concise. Your content is you. It needs to be more than just comprehensible, it needs to sing to your audiences. It needs to tell your story.
