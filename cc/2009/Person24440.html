David Mezzapelle’s Summary

David's Professional Experience:

Founded GoliathJobs Inc., a web-based employment service for students & alumni. The goal for GoliathJobs is to bring a unique, powerful method of recruiting to employers across all industries & demographics.

The goal for JobsOver50.com is the same except it focuses on the hiring needs of Baby Boomers & Retirees.

Prior to GoliathJobs, David founded Goliath Technology in 1990, a data center procurement & integration company and a global leader in IT distribution.

While at Goliath Technology David created the Goliath ABA Program (Academic Business Advancement). This program included staffing of college students & recent graduates for jobs/internships throughout North America. This program yielded significant buzz and intensified the company’s growth at a rapid pace.

David sold the company in 2007 and has focused his efforts on GoliathJobs.

David Mezzapelle’s Specialties:

Marketing, Promotion, Branding & Identity, Finance, Recruitment/HR, Development, Communications, Buzz, Academics, Generational Demographics (Baby Boomers, Retirees, Generation X, Y, Z), etc.

    GoliathJobs.com

    (Human Resources industry)

    January 2006 — Present (3 years 7 months)

    GoliathJobs.com - The #1 Job Site on the Web.

    GoliathJobs is a free web-based employment service for students & alumni. We connect job seekers to employers via schools & alma maters. GoliathJobs creates partnerships with schools throughout North America which serve as liaisons. This model delivers a powerful career service edge to schools at no charge (and lifelong career services), a competitive edge to job seekers and high-quality results to employers. 100% Spam-Free.

    JobsOver50.com is a free web-based employment service for Baby Boomers & Retirees built on the same platform.

    We believe that employment starts with education regardless of age, experience or educational level. We also believe that connecting job seekers to employers through verifiable intermediaries, such as schools & alma maters, is the optimum way to secure a job or find an employee.

    For more information about our concept, testimonials, recent press, etc please visit http://goliathjobs.com/pages/about/
