Leslie Allison Seei’s Summary

For the past 20 years, Leslie has worked in the creative fields of promotion, graphic arts and package design. Clients included Quaker Oats, Perdue Farms, Tootisie Roll and El Pollo Loco Restaurants.

In November of 2006, she joined her husband at Robust Promotions after 18 months of collaboration between leading experts in all areas vital to the development, execution and implementation of promotional gaming and incentive marketing programs for restaurant chains as well as other merchants.

"Four Walls Marketing" promotions reward loyal partronage. Customers also tell others when they win, generating word-of-mouth advertising. In addition, these promotions give your employees valuable face time with your customers and promote goodwill. Your customers will have a very postive perception of your business.

Other uses for the game cards include charity events, employee incentive programs and cross-promotions.

Leslie Allison Seei’s Specialties:

Specializing in customized Instant-Win Bounce-back promotions for the restaurant industry and other merchants. Our turnkey service provides consulting, legal support, state registration, filing fees, graphic design, high-security printing and shipping as well as ongoing marketing analysis reporting and promotional support materials - all at an appealing price that makes it accessible to the mid-size markets.

    The principle goal of N.I.S.A. is to save healthy, good natured Samoyeds from unnecessary euthanasia. N.I.S.A.provides counseling on behavior, grooming, general health, obedience classes, and more to any "Proud Samoyed owner".

    Unfortunately, puppies of all breeds are purchased on impulse and often end up in shelters or dumped on the street. Samoyeds are no exception, especially at Christmas time. Frequently advertised as the "Christmas Puppy", and unfortunate for the breed, too many individuals leap in without thought of commitment to the purchase of a Samoyed puppy.

    Heartworm treatments, spay and neuter plus all vaccination and grooming expenses are covered by N.I.S.A.

    Many Sammie owners have joined N.I.S.A. efforts but the need for foster homes and permanent families to adopt, continues to grow. If you have room in your heart and your home to share time and love with a Samoyed, contact them today!
