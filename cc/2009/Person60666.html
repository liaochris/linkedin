Lucy Brownlee’s Summary

I have 25 years experience working in the pre-press and graphic design industry. My expertise includes Print Media Advertising, Point of Purchase Display and Signage, Product Packaging, Digital Design, Logo Branding Creation, Screen Print and Textile Sublimation. My business includes Auto Cad Design for Architectural Signage and manufacturing.

My experience, strength and passion, as a fine artist, allowed me to enjoy a long and successful career in the Graphic Arts field. My desire in continued education, development and experimentation in the field of Graphic Arts, lead me to Innoventions, Inc. I joined the team and currently hold the position of Senior Production Artist, where I am involved in the creation of pre-production graphics, illustrations and audio, for video, web, e-Learning and flash projects.

I enjoy the growing industry of Graphic and Fine Arts and participating in its evolution into Web 2.0 and beyond.
