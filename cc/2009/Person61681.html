Francesco Mandelli’s Summary

Francesco Mandelli is currently an Independent Insurance Professional providing consulting services in Property and Casualty, Marine Insurance, Builders Risk, Commercial Crime and Fidelity as well as Surety Bonding. Previously was Underwriting Manager at Royal & SunAlliance Group from 2006 to 2008 and Branch Manager from 2004 to 2006. At Royal & SunAlliance, he managed a team of over 50 underwriters, distributed in the whole Italy and was responsible for business development and improvement of profitability and sustainability where he also worked as Business Development Manager from 2002 to 2004 and Casualty Manager from 2000 to 2001. He was Casualty and Development Manager at AGF from 1993 to 1999. With an experience of more than 20 years in the Insurance Industry in Italy having previously served in several positions in companies like Johnson & Higgins, Duomo Assicurazioni and MEIE Assicuratrice from 1985 to 1993.

His goals are:

- Doing an analysis of insurance needs.
- Marketing insurance coverages suited to clients operations.
- Selecting the best company or companies for insurance programs.
- Providing with a competitively priced proposal.
- Working hard to get fair, prompt settlement of the claims.
- Periodically doing reviews of insurance programs.
- Always looking for ways to save money on the protection of business needs of the clients.

Commercial and Personal Package Policies (property and liability including products liability)
