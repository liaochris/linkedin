Donato Diorio’s Summary

As the Founder and CEO of Broadlook Technologies I am primarily responsible for the business logic behind the products, leading the business process architecture and forming relationships with partner companies to further the acceptance of Broadlook products and services. I am deeply involved in our sale processes and enjoy a hands-on approach with many of our clients.

Recently, I received the recruiting industry blog of the year for my blog, www.iDonato.com. My blog focuses on the "what keeps me up at night" in regards to how technology, process and philosophy effect sales and recruitment.

In the last year, I have focused on expanding the original vision behind Broadlook from a software tools company (focusing on Internet research), into a company with three intersecting business units. Today, Broadlook's three business units are software tools, data provider to data resellers, and data correction services.

Any of my LinkedIN connections are welcome to a free 14 day trial of our award-winning research tool: Broadlook Diver. (normally only 7 day trial). Invite me to your network to get your trial (yes, one of my uuber cool sales reps will contact you).

BroadLook products include: Profiler, Diver, Eclipse, Contact Capture, Market Mapper, Job Pulse, News Pulse, BroadMail and ContentHound.

Broadlook's new data correction service is called "Ellis" (named after Ellis Island from my Italian roots) is focused on "Bringing your data into the new world". There is no other data augmentation service that can take advantage of real-time information.

In addition to Broadlook, I am passionately interested in Alternative Energy initiatives and welcome connects from people that are looking to change the world with technology.
