Christophe Veltsos aka Dr. InfoSec™’s Summary

Chris Veltsos is a faculty member in the Department of Information Systems & Technology at Minnesota State University, Mankato. He regularly teaches Information Security and Information Warfare classes as well as oversees the department's Internship program.

Chris is the President of PrudentSecurity LLC, an information security and privacy consulting company based in Mankato, MN. Chris is also the Chief Blogging Officer at DrInfoSec.com, a security blog featuring news, commentary, and interesting quotes. In addition to his faculty and consulting work, Chris is President of the Mankato Chapter of the Information Systems Security Association (ISSA).

In 2008, Chris spent six months as a member of the Editorial Board of the SANS NewsBites, a semi-weekly information security electronic newsletter reaching over 180,000 readers. His comments have appeared in 27 of the 43 NewsBites issues he was associated with.

Training attended: ISO 27001 Lead Auditor training (2009)
