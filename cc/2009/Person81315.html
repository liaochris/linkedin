Rachel Kaplan’s Summary

I believe in uniting people with technology that increases the enjoyment of life, whether it be social, utility, organization, charity or luxury. I believe in giving people what they want when they want it and coming up with ways to get rid of everything else they don't. I have some weird ideal if democratizing the Web and hope to be a part of something that represents the same ideal.

I specialize in technology and application marketing, organic marketing and building communities. I also have a keen eye towards usability and can tell if someone will think your stuff sucks or rocks their socks off.

I don't believe in throwing a bunch of stuff at people they might not want to see, so I am an odd ball marketer in that sense. :-)
