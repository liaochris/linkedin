Keith Carter [kcarter@stoneshare.com]’s Summary

Keith is a Professional Services Executive with proven expertise and a consistent track record of forming and growing services teams focused on the ICT sector. His previous assignments have allowed him to gain significant experience creating consulting services and business solutions tailored to deliver measurable, positive change.

A well-known expert in his field, Keith sits on the boards of the Canadian Advanced Technology Alliance and the Information and Communications Technology Council.

Keith is also a member of the U.S.-based Human Capital Institute, holds a bachelor's degree from the University of Western Ontario, and is a member of The Leadership Forum associated with Ottawa University's Executive MBA program.

When not occupied with business responsibilities, Keith takes his passion to the track as a nationally certified instructor for the Porsche Club of America.
