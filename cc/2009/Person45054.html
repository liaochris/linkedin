  • Mixed Media Visual Artist

    Jean Bourque

    (Public Company; ARMHY; Semiconductors industry)

    January 1995 — Present (14 years 11 months)

    The artwork of Jean Bourque has been shown nationally and internationally. Bourque art is in collections in the Netherlands, Germany, England and in many states in the US as well as Mexico and Canada. Her artwork often features bright lively canvases with large strokes ranging from a minimalist approach to a layered look.

    A true mixed media artist, her work is known for its large scale unique look at the world with many layers and multiple mediums on various materials. Her abstract paintings usually have pastels and or charcoal as well as acrylics or oil paint. Many of them have bits and pieces from other paintings, interesting textures created using various tools, papers, fabrics, found objects and mediums. Give her a few unrelated items and next thing you know they will work together and be something unique. She states she loves assemblage and fiberarts as much as painting. She has restored and recreated fine furnishings as well as one of a kind book arts known as Altered books. She did a 10 ft Steel Palm Tree with an op art design and has studied with many international artists including over 10 years with Ilona Royce Smithken of NYC. She has trained in Feng Shui and Faux Finishing as well as graphic and web design. Bourque states; "From the delicate to the sturdy, I love the creative process."
