David DuPre’s Summary

I am a Performance Engineer with experience in application development, IT systems operations, IT support, disaster recovery planning, configuration management, and service desk operations.

As a Performance Engineering Consultant I have experience leading project teams, executing projects, and teaching our customers in a classroom setting. Many engagements require one-on-one training either on site or remotely using web enabled meetings.

More than 9 years experience helping customers’ answer questions like these:
Will these new servers improve performance of our applications?
How long will these servers continue to meet the SLA of our growing business?
What part of the application contributes the most to end-user response time?
Where are the bottlenecks in my distributed application?
Will relocating the datacenter improve our Website performance?

To answer these kinds of questions I used discrete event simulations tools from HyPerformix. However, tools are not enough. The methodology or process of building a model of an application is critical, and I helped develop, use, and teach it to many of our customers.

This methodology includes an application design review, building a data collection plan tailored to the environment, collecting the data, analyzing the results, and finally building a simulation model.

Some of the companies I have worked with are in these industries:
3 wireless telephone companies, 6 commercial banks, 5 insurance companies, one foreign government, retail companies, manufacturers, and 2 consulting companies.

David DuPre’s Specialties:

Minimizing risk with performance engineering, cutting IT costs with simulation modeling, and improving application design with performance budgeting.
