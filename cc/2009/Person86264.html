Ron Stewart’s Summary

Professional Experience:

A veteran of 2 startups (GetItNext and marketmagnet), I have over fourteen years of professional experience in technical project management, staff development, process definition, testing, business development, financial management and executive leadership.

I have extensive consulting experience in providing leadership, strategy, and vision to enterprise wide business solutions in the areas of Web 2.0, Internet Applications, Systems Integration, Solution Architecture, Information Strategies, Strategic Planning and Outsourcing in a variety of different industries. I've successfully led and managed multiple engagements and been responsible for account and client relationship management for numerous clients.

My technical project management experience focuses on bridging the communications gap between business and technology executives due to strong understanding of both business and technology issues.

I hold BA in Economics from the University of Alberta and a MBA from Queen's University in Ontario.

Goals:

As an entrepeneur, Ron's provides senior management experience to provide leadership for business and technology issues. To make a strong contribution to the growth and profitability of the firm and professional development of its people. To continue my personal and professional growth.
