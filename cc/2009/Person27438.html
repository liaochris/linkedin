Don Crawley’s Summary

I am an expert on I.T. people; I understand them from a technical perspective and from a human-to-human perspective.

15 years experience as a writer, speaker, trainer, and consultant in the areas of Cisco, Microsoft, and Linux desktops/servers/networks. 25 years experience in automation and technology. Certifications include MCSE, CCNA, and Linux+. Member: Washington Technology Industry Association, Toastmasters. Experienced in the areas of technical training, business skills training, instructional design. Today, I work with I.T. professionals to enhance their work, lives, and careers.

Don Crawley’s Specialties:

Customer service training for I.T. people, network design, administration, and security training. Cisco router configuration, management, and training, Cisco ASA firewall configuration, management and training, Linux configuration, management, and training, Joomla website design, management, and training, communications, and negotiation training for IT professionals.
