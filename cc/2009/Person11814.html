Blake Johnson’s Summary

I would love to be a compositor/motion graphics artist in a creative, fun, inspiring environment.

I keep up a filmmaking blog at http://animivirtus.com/blog that focuses on low-budget DIY filmmaking, bringing together helpful resources and showcasing such works for filmmakers with similar means.

I was awarded Best & Outstanding Student awards in two separate high school filmmaking classes. I directed, produced and co-wrote a feature film title LATENT(CY) in my senior year of high school, after which I pursued education in Visual Effects.

Throughout high school I taught myself filmmaking, graphic and web design, read as much and as often as I could get my hands on an internet connection, and built my own websites, camera rigs and writing graphic design tutorials for my personal websites.
