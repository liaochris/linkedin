Sri Susanti’s Summary

My career spans for 5 years as a process engineer in petrochemical plant and engineering company. On my tracks, I have been assigned to involve in several ammonia projects. My responsibility covered generating P&ID, hydraulic study /calculation, line sizing, line list, tie-in list, pressure profile, restriction orifice sizing calculation, process datasheet for equipment and instrumentation, nozzle/connection summary, etc.

I'm also familiar with process and system engineering software, such as Hysys, Aspen, HTRI, and Smart Plant P&ID.
