Gabriel Kent’s Summary

Gabriel Kent is a technologist with an inborn technolust. He loves making music, travel and good food. For more than a decade he has worked in a variety of technology related fields such as intelligent media presentation, systems automation, consumer electronics, video games, Internet marketing and MPEG-4 systems. He has given talks on MPEG-4 Systems authoring, Open Source Education and Grid (a.k.a. Cloud) Computing. His primary focus since 2004 has been Information Retrieval and Internet marketing. Gabriel is the inventor of Search-based Contextual Routing (SbCR) and is currently a partner in an Internet marketing consulting firm he co-founded in early 2009.
