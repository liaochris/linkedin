Paul Cohen’s Summary

Paul Cohen is the Chief Executive Officer of Trinston Diversified, LLC. and a freshman at Yale University.

Paul Cohen was the class of 2009 valedictorian from Vineland High School. He served as the Future Business Leaders of America (FBLA) 2008-09 National Eastern Region Vice President, responsible for 43,000 students in nineteen states and territories. FBLA is the world’s largest non-profit student business organization that builds synergies between business and education through leadership opportunities, competitive events, community service, and innovative career development programs. Additionally, Paul directed New Jersey FBLA in 2008-09 as the State President. Prior to that, Paul served as the 2007-08 Eastern Region International Relations Director, 2007-08 National President's Technology Assistant, and 2007-08 New Jersey FBLA State Webmaster.

Paul has a passion for business and technology, and Trinston Diversified is the fusion of his dual interests. Founded in 2003, Trinston Diversified has been providing unique technology solutions for over forty-five clients. The internet investments and concept development side of Trinston Diversified's operations was started in August 2007 as a complement to the company's existing services.

With a passion for learning, Paul draws on his interests in business, technology, linguistics, international relations, philosophy, law, German language, and cognitive science. Paul is also heavily involved in the anti-genocide movement, and he has used his experiences with the anti-genocide constituency to serve as a guest speaker for the New Jersey Commission on Holocaust Education.
