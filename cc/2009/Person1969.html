Amy Gahran’s Summary

I help organizations and individuals (especially news organizations, journalism schools, and journalists) achieve their goals through effective, creative use of online, social, and mobile media.

My background is in journalism and editorial management -- but my skill set has advance far beyond writing stories and managing reporters. I keep abreast of the latest online, social, and mobile tools and services. I spot opportunities and pitfalls, manage experiments, and craft successful strategies.

I can show you how to constructively participate in the public conversation in ways that will engage and energize you, engage your community, leverage your strengths, and build your business. More importantly, I can help you understand how participating in the public conversation can help your reach and expand your goals.

Services include strategic consulting, seminars, one-on-one coaching, weblogs, writing, editing, technology assessment, and project management.
