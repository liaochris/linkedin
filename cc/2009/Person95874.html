Jennifer Ott’s Summary

Jennifer Ott’s writing career began years ago and has been supported by her job in the fashion industry. During that time she has written countless screenplays and three fiction novels. Two of her screenplays, The Tourist and Truth and Craziness were previous semi-finalist at the Austin Screenwriting competition. And her short script, “Space Trash,” was a 2006 quarterfinalist Write Brothers contest. “Ooh Baby Compound Me,” is a testimony to years of sacrifice trying to fulfill dreams while maintaining a swinging lifestyle in the fashion world. It came at a price, and for Jennifer Ott, the price was credit card debt. Today she lives to write about it.

Professional photographer with prints for sales at FineArtAmerica.com, ArtistRising.com, Art.Com, AllPosters.com.

Apparel and stationary product for sales at cafepress.com and zazzle.com
