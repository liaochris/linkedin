Kendall Messner’s Summary

Kendall sets the highest standards of client service for the firm and provides leadership and execution on all top-level placements. His vision and commitment to excellence have provided the platform for Bluespeed Technology’s service model.

Kendall’s extensive understanding of recruiting processes and in-depth knowledge of the senior level consulting world with a special emphasis in Life Sciences and Healthcare make him immediately effective in achieving success in the business that he conducts. He also recognizes the importance of partnering with his clients to fulfill their needs. Clients often turn to him for advice and guidance on complex recruiting issues beyond those that come up in searches. His commitment to his clients has earned him an outstanding and well-deserved reputation in the executive search world.

Equally important, Kendall also serves as the primary source of information, advice and guidance for candidates through the often complex interviewing process. He will tell the candidate exactly what the feedback from the client is throughout the interviewing experience. The relationships he develops continue beyond successful placements. Kendall prides himself on following-up – in fact, relationships with many of the hiring managers within Bluespeed's client company base started on the candidate side of the recruiting equation.

Prior to Bluespeed, Kendall spent several years in the “Boom Days” of recruiting in the late 90’s as a senior contributor at a larger firm. Before this, he successfully completed a five-year stint as a full time commercial and corporate speaker at industry events, trade shows, charity events and the North American stand-up comedy circuit. He has been quoted as an authority in the Wall Street Journal, as well as numerous other publications. Further, he authors quarterly newsletters in the Managed Markets and Business Consulting space.
