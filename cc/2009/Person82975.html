Renae Bolton’s Summary

Markeing 4 Realtors is a full-service company focusing on marketing assistance. I have been a VA full-time since 1998 and have worked specifically with real estate professionals starting in 2003. I am a certified Professional Real Estate Virtual Assistants through the REVA Network (http://www.revanetwork.com/) and am listed as a PREVA on IREAA (http://www.ireaa.com/), I am also the Chairperson of the Lead Management Guild through the REVA Network (www.revanetwork.com/news.php).

It is important for me to let you know that I am highly invested in the team approach to virtual assisting. Working alone is not an efficient business practice and always causes problems. As a result, over the years, I've developed a team of associates who work closely with me and my clients. They have all signed non-disclosure agreements and we trust them implicitly with our client details. This allows us to not only be able to provide services to my clients that may not be in my primary skillset, but I am also able to take on a much larger work load than a single VA can handle. It also affords me the opportunity to take vacations and have family time when necessary without leaving my clients in the lurch while I'm away. It is a win-win situation for all involved.
