Paul M. Heller, Esq.’s Summary

A SF Bay Area Institution for 25+ years, Heller Immigration Law Group, “HILG”, http://greencard1.com, is one of the premiere immigration law firms now headquartered in Silicon Valley (Palo Alto), CA. We obtain status for non citizens, incl: investor visas, h1b, 'national interest' and eb1 'extraordinary aliens', perm labor certification under 'fast-track' provisions, adj. of status, and the myriad of other types of visas. Clients include: multinationals, to large and small corporations, to Silicon Valley startups, outstanding and preeminent scientists, post-docs - and other researchers and academics, many of whom often wish to self-petition their employment-based cases. We also handle marriage, VAWA, and fiancé/e cases, and I-751 petitions to remove 'conditional resident status'. We provide comprehensive and individualized attention to each and every matter.

Paul M. Heller, Esq.’s Specialties:

Employment-based Immigration Law & PERM labor certification (1981-present). Green cards and working permits for non-immigrants. We serve both the 'start-up' and VC community, as well as business, corporate, and individual clients. Corporate 'multi case' discounts available.

We will match any existing arrangement in price.

Finally, we can arrange 'General Counsel' services for Startups - as well as 'Of Counsel' services to law firms who want to establish an immigration arm.
