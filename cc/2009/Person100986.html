Triona Guidry’s Summary

Freelance computer consultant and writer with nearly twenty years of IT experience, specializing in small business and home computer users. Skills include tech support for PC (Windows) and Mac, plus server technologies like Windows Server, Apple XServe, Novell Netware, UNIX and more. Other skills include computer networking, security, firewalls, disaster recovery, web design and more. My writing experience consists of everything from novels to articles, essays, brochures, newsletters, press releases, web site copy and blogs. Available to meet your computer needs, including on-site tech support in northwest Chicago suburbs. Interested in writing opportunities, particularly involving computers and the Internet. Available as a speaker or trainer on computer topics.
