David A. Chapa’s Summary

Co-Author, “Implementing Backup and Recovery: The Readiness Guide for the Enterprise” published by Wiley Technology Publishing. Recognized worldwide as an authority on the subject of DR, Backup and Recovery.

Over 20 years industry experience holding Senior level Technical/Sales positions with Cheyenne Software, Unisys, OpenVision, NSI Software, DataStaff and Quantum (formerly ADIC). Currently Director, Data Protection Solutions at NetApp.

Prior to joining NetApp, managed a thriving Data Protection Consulting Services organization in the Chicago, IL area focused in Storage Management and Data Availability. Architected several data protection solutions from medium to large companies.

Speaker at several industry events including VERITAS Vision, CA World, CIO Forum as well as served as panelist on various discussions related to disaster recovery, business resumption and data protection strategies.

David A. Chapa’s Specialties:

Strength in identifying & formulating strategies for technology vision, evangelism, acquisition, integration and implementation.

Dedicated leadership in key technology areas including Enterprise Backup and Recovery, Disaster Recovery, Business Continuity and Archive Solutions.

Combine strategic planning and team development expertise with strong technical qualifications to present comprehensive, compound solutions to achieve responsive long term objectives for growth and profitability.
