Robert L Pritchett’s Summary

I volunteer time to the SEEnetwork effort as Treasurer and board member and am currently in the running for a new "day" job.

I was managing web content and updates at the Pure Energy System wiki website - http://www.peswiki.com as a member of the New Energy Congress - and full-time employee of PES Network, Inc. until March 14, 2009. http://www.pureenergysystems.com/

I run the Solar Energy Consultants Group on LinkedIn.

I have established the "Alternative Energy User Group" as a non-profit organization, where folks can come and learn more about Alternative Energy Systems each month.

I've project-managed various telecom/computer network systems from inception to completion for businesses, school districts, cities and state institutions.

I currently manage a fantastic team of around 15 people internationally, who review books, games, hardware, software and training apps for the Macintosh environment. Together, we publish a magazine once a month and I participated in the Mac ReviewCast Podcast each month for 1 year. We also look at and work with new computer-based technologies as they are released and experiment with them through our network and website at http://www.maccompanion.com
