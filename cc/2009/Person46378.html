Jeremy Rudy’s Summary

I'm a student, entrepreneur and passionate activist for my generation.

My company is Kyros, a web-based social learning platform for college students and professors, launching in fall 2009.

I also work as an independent contractor for event sales & marketing through my other company, Inspire Speaking LLC.

On the side, I like speaking about life management, entrepreneurship, future trends, and the Millennial Generation. I've spoken to groups as large as 600 of students at Arizona State University and Toni & Guy Academy, educators, and teens with groups including the Future Business Leaders of America.

I'm also a full-time Honors Digital Media and Business student at Arizona State University. During my freshman year, I started the Executive Luncheon Series, which continues to bring local business leaders to speak with students 10 times per year. I also served as the Executive Vice President for the Business School Council, and was Pat Tillman Scholar. Right now, I'm doing my honors thesis on technology in higher-education.

I'm a big fan of: Buckminster Fuller, Elon Musk, Steve Jobs and Peter Diamendes. I love talking about trends and ideas, playing music, traveling, and jumping out of airplanes.

Jeremy Rudy’s Specialties:

Business development, managing software development, social/web media, technology in higher-ed, live-event sales and marketing.
