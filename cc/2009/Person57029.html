Kyle Aulerich’s Summary

Kyle Aulerich is Chief Operating Officer and Co-Founder of 360 Vantage where he is responsible for overall company operations. Kyle is focused on strategic and tactical management as well as the design, operation, and improvement of 360 Vantage solutions and services.

Kyle also has responsibility for developing and communicating 360 Vantage’s vision and strategy through positioning, branding, advertising, and associated growth initiatives.

Customer-and partner-focused, Kyle believes in setting strategic direction, establishing clear business objectives and ensuring that these objectives are met. The ability to be flexible and adjust priorities quickly to accommodate changing customer requirements is central to Kyle’s management style, and the success of his organization.

Kyle holds a B.S. in Information Technology from the University of Phoenix, Arizona.

Kyle has honorably served in Operation Iraqi Freedom as a Sergeant in the United States Marine Corps.
