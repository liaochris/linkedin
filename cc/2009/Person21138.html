Shaun Guevarra’s Summary

Founder and Owner of the Capital River Services, LLC company, a startup company aiming to make a big splash. My background is in business and marketing and now opening the market by bringing a love of the river, a strong vision and a determination to make the difference needed here in DC's transportation industry.

Addressing the main concerns of area residents, A.R.T’s design is a transportation system to help alleviate the increasing delays caused by overcrowded highways and address an appropriate level of sensitivity into the environmental impact of a river transportation system. It is through partnerships with other green companies in manufacturing, propulsion and mass transit connections that the vision of a new Potomac River becomes closer to reality. This will allow commuters and tourists alike to see more of the city in an enchanting way without the stress of traffic and pollution.

American River Taxi, a service of Capital River Services, LLC, seeks to serve the infrastructure and economic development of the Potomac River waterfront by providing a green solution water taxi service that will encourage water transportation as a common extension to the commuting and transport solutions of the Washington Metropolitan Area.
