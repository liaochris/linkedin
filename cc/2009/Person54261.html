Keith Shirley (K.Shirley at Shercom.com)’s Summary

Having worked with IT in numerous forms for over 20 years I'm know the benefits that can be gained as well as the headaches it can cause.

My personal aim is to help people get the most out of their interaction with technology and support them in their learning process. My business background helps ensure that I can help people focus on what is really needed and how changes will be reflected in profits.

I have almost 10 years experience running my own project management and IT consultancy company and I was on the board of directors of a multinational telecoms company based in Germany. I am very good at working in a team environment but I have the drive needed to be able to push projects forward and achieve results.

I love putting people in contact with each other - if you wish to link up with me then please make sure to include a description of you and your business. Help me to help you by giving me the information I need to pass good customers your way.
