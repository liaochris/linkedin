Freddie Daniells’s Summary

I have recently taken a new leadership role at Toastmasters International, a non-profit organization that helps people develops public speaking, communication and leadership skills. I am responsible for the success and development of the organisation across London. We currently have around 1,000 members in London.

I also give speeches both for love and money and coach people through speeches and presentations.

Finally, I help businesses with business plans and marketing activities. I am currently acting as a marketing consultant to a portfolio risk management software business, helping them revamp their marketing activity.

Prior to this, I served as CEO of a marketing technology consulting firm. We helped large organizations to improve processes within their marketing operations. We also advised on technology solutions to help those process improvements. We were a small but highly regarded specialist in Marketing Resource Management software.

Prior to this, I was the co-CEO of an early stage venture capital business. We injected both money and expertise into early stage enterprise software and Internet businesses.

My original background is in Institutional Equity Sales, working for EF Hutton, Prudential Bache, Drexel Burnham Lambert and finally for Cowen & Co. I have worked in US and European equities, specialising in the high growth industries of technology and healthcare. I left as a Director of SG Cowen.
