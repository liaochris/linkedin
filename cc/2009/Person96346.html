Michael Swiszcz’s Summary

Michael is a Director with the Phoenix Office of RSM McGladrey.

Michael has over 1,000 1st degree connections and 9+mill in his network.

Currently, Michael serves at the President of the Phoenix CEO-CFO Group. This Networking Group is the Hottest Ticket in the Phoenix Metro Valley. The Group is attracting CEO, CFO, COO, CIO and other C-Level Executives to its meetings. Are you connected?

Michael is an active member of the Phoenix CEO-CFO Group and the Institute of Management Accountants (IMA). He is a member of the Board of Directors of the AZ Valley of the Sun Chapter of the IMA has served two terms as the President of the 500+ member IMA chapter. Michael is also an Active Member of the Urban Land Institute (ULI), Construction Financial Management Association (CFMA), Financial Executives International (FEI) Arizona Chapter and the Association for Corporate Growth (ACG)-Arizona Chapter.

With a Bachelors degree in Finance from Bentley University (College) and active affiliations with organizations dedicated to the accounting and finance professions, Michael brings with him a knowledge of the profession and a vast network of resources in the marketplace.
