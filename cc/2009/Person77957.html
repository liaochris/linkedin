More than 20 years of professional experience as writer, consultant, editor.

Former Director of Corporate Communications for Right Management Consultants, Inc--global HR consulting firm based in Philadelphia (seven years)

Seeking challenging editorial projects of any length. Significant experience with longer projects, including books and book-length documents.

Also, very experienced in survey design and research project management; can analyze data and create reports for range of purposes.
