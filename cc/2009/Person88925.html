Satha Arumanayagam’s Summary

Satha is a subject matter expert on Finance and IT business solutions.

During her 20+ years experience Satha has held leadership positions in Finance Management, Solutions Consulting, Sales Leadership, Project Leadership and Practice Leadership with HCL Technologies, SVA Global, Microsoft, Epicor and others.

As a Finance and IT Business professional Satha is passionate about continuing to add tangible value to businesses in the following areas:

 business and practice leadership

 leading end to end evaluation and selection of ERP, CRM, and Business Analytics solutions

 working with C level executives to improve ROI ( average of 20%) on Investments in IT Solutions and Services

 working with senior management teams to Rationalise IT Spend and Optimise Returns on Vendor Contracts

 direct solutions sales in the corporate sector, SMEs, and government (federal and state government) agencies

 managing IT Projects up to AUD 5 million and Delivering Team Revenue quota up to AUD 25 million

 specialist experience in working with federal and state government agencies and the not for profit sector

 sound knowledge of current project management and service delivery practices


Satha is:
an expert columnist to the Microsoft Dynamics Live Community;
a fellow of the Chartered Institute of Management Accountants (CIMA), UK;
and
a member of the Australian Society of Certified Practicing Accountants

Satha lives in Sydney, Australia with her lovely partner Rob, their pet dogs Pip and Emma and a tank full of colourful fish.
