Al Stevens’s Summary

A highly experienced web and user experience designer. Passionate about web usability and standards, I want to design the web of the future today and I want to use only the best tools.

I take a collaborative and iterative approach and will take advantage of SEO, analytics and other metrics to focus the design effort exactly where customers need it – helping them achieve their goals.
