Superior Lamp Inc.: Company Profile and History

From the time that Superior Lamp Inc. was founded in 1977, they have been the premier supplier of lighting products in the industry. Superior Lamp Inc. has been and continues to be an industry leader in product reliability and innovation with a focus on environmentally safe and responsible products and marketing methods. Superior Lamp Inc. is located in Mount Laurel, NJ, just a few miles east of Philadelphia and their distribution hub serves thousands of clients all over North America.

Superior Lamp Inc.’s heavy-duty lighting products appeal to a wide range of commercial, industrial, and municipal clients for their advanced design, innovation, energy efficiency, significant savings over time from lower utility bills and extraordinary longevity, and lower maintenance costs. On average, Superior Lamp Inc.’s lighting products last 18 times longer than standard lighting and their heavy-duty construction ensures consistent performance and reliability year after year. Superior Lamp Inc.’s extensive line of lighting products includes:

 Superior Lamp Inc. Incandescent Lighting
 Superior Lamp Inc. Fluorescent Tube Lights
 Superior Lamp Inc. Compact Fluorescent Bulbs
 Superior Lamp Inc. Metal Halide Lighting
 Superior Lamp Inc. Lumi-Twist Lamps
 Superior Lamp Inc. Halogen Par Lamps
 Superior Lamp Inc. Mercury Vapor Lighting
 Superior Lamp Inc. High-Pressure Sodium Lighting
 Superior Lamp Inc. 30-Year Lighting Ballasts
 And a Huge Selection of Unusual and Hard-to-Find Lighting Solutions in a Wide Range of Different Sizes and Shapes

From the very beginning, Superior Lamp Inc.’s lighting products have set the industry benchmark for performance, construction, and uncompromising standards for reliability.
