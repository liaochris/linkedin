Patricia Hirsch, MCC, MBA’s Summary

Patricia Hirsch is a Master Certified Coach, credentialed via the International Coach Federation (ICF), and an Executive Personal Coach with "Design Your Life Coaching." Advanced certified in ontological learning (the study of 'being' human), Patricia expertly coaches clients to stand back and take an objective birds eye view of how they operate such that they get the results that show up in their life.

Patricia says, "It is my job to work with clients willing to shift their way-of-being, or perspective, based on the consistent results they are getting that are "not working" to results that will work.

Oftentimes we operate in our lives from old conversations and decisions that we made up when we were very young, or that we actually were "born into." In other words, conversations that were already existing in our families and communities, among other environments, and that we automatically adopted as if they were our own; like a fish to water, we don't notice they aren't our own.

Until we are able to shift our perspective to "observing how we are observing" our own life, there is no way that we can take thoughtful actions to produce desired results.

Often we only change our actions, thinking and hoping that alone will offer us different results.

"Doing the same thing over and over again, thinking we will arrive at different results." That sounds suspiciously like a definition of insanity."

For an in-depth overview of her professional experience and what led her to be a professional coach, visit www.designyourlifecoaching.com

Patricia Hirsch, MCC, MBA’s Specialties:

A Master Certified Coach who coaches groups or individuals nationwide to business leaders, management teams, independent clients via phone or in person.
