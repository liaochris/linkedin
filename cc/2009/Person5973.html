Andy Beaupre’s Summary

For three decades, Andy has built memorable branding auras for business-to-business, consumer and clean technology companies.

His highly customized communications programs help companies achieve strategic endgame objectives. For example, more than 30 Beaupre clients have been acquired in lucrative M&A deals and IPOs valued at over $5 billion.

Beaupre is owned by Brodeur/Omnicom Group, Inc. (NYSE:OMC), the leading global communications holding company.
