Mariano Petit de Murat’s Summary

Professional muralist with 25 years experience. Speaks Spanish and English fluidly.
Broad experience in all aspects of arts, graphic design and muralism.
Interested in private homes decoration as well as companies and public spaces. Project Manager in a wide variety of arts and design applications.
Particularly interested digital art and 3D images creation using Photoshop, Poser, 3D studio max and similar software.
Always interested in muralism and digital art promotion projects, as well as close interaction with digital artists from around the globe.

Resume,

Mariano Petit de Murat was born in Mexico City. He begins his career as a professional artist in 1985. His work is exhibited in various galleries and artistic events like Hall Des Artistes, Center of the image, Museum of the Chopo, Itatti Gallery, Misrachi Gallery, The Panaderia Gallery and the National Center of the Arts among others.

In the year of 2000 he moves to the island of Cozumel where he begins to experiment as a digital artist, exhibiting his work online in MODA Museum of Digital Arts, MOCA Museum of Computer Art, Art Majeur, Deviant Art, Renderosity, Runtime DNA and many other sites where digital art is promoted. He has become an experienced digital artist, considered refined and creative among his fellow digital artists.

Also in 2000, he opens a muralism and interior-exterior decoration business with notable success called Cozumel Murals. It has become the most important muralism services provider in the Mexican Caribbean with over 200 murals painted over the last 8 years.

At Art Sublime (personal site), visitors can appreciate the best of his work. Today, as general director of MODA Museum of Digital Art he has acomplished one of his most desired goals, to promote digital art and to offer talented artists a space for their work to be apreciated in a pleasant atmosphere.
