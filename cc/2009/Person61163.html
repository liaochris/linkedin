Maarten Elout’s Summary

People are infinitely more capable and far more amazing than they think. I love creating an environment and facilitating processes that bring that out in them!

I have been guiding people in workshops and ceremonies for a decade now and always aim to hold open a door between most people's everyday reality of restriction, fear and limitation, and a reality of endless possibility, acceptance, joy and well-being.

My intention is to serve the people and the highest possible good, forsaking any personal agenda or need and allowing myself to gift whatever is needed in the most fun, interactive, practical and real, down to earth way to achieve long lasting positive results in people's lives.
