Bob Trewyn, ABR, e-Pro’s Summary

As an eight year veteran of the real estate industry, I continue to add valuable training in areas of importance to better serve my clients needs. In 2008, I sat for, and passed the Tennessee Residential General Contractors exam as well as the Tennessee General Contractors Business Law exam. In doing so, I am now able to offer my clients valuable insight into the complex issues relating to new home construction and the management of their construction projects.
