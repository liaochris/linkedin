Joseph C. Gioconda’s Summary

Joseph Gioconda is a partner in the global law firm DLA Piper LLP (US). He is responsible for the Trademark Litigation practice in that Firm's New York office. He focuses his practice on litigating and counseling in the areas of anticounterfeiting, trademarks and trade dress, domain name protection, copyrights, design patents, and false advertising.

Mr. Gioconda represents a wide range of leading multinational clients and has protected some of the most famous and valuable brands in the world. He has expertise in diverse business sectors including fashion and luxury goods, apparel, footwear, personal electronics and computers, pharmaceuticals, household consumer products, art, entertainment, media and e-commerce.

He has successfully litigated cases in many federal and state courts throughout the U.S. His cases have involved temporary restraining orders, preliminary injunction hearings, jury trials and appeals.

Mr. Gioconda has lectured at Yale Law School on a variety of intellectual property topics and has been widely quoted in the legal, business, and general press on intellectual property issues. He is a frequent commentator on CNN, NBC and MSNBC. Journalist Tim Phillips profiled him in the recent book "Knockoff: The Deadly Trade in Counterfeit Goods."

Mr. Gioconda's experience drafting legislation for the Senate Judiciary Committee has given him valuable insight into this area of law. Moreover, his experience at the U.S. Attorney's Office in New York has given him exposure to working closely with federal and local government officials and law enforcement agencies to achieve the seizure of articles that infringe his clients' rights. Mr. Gioconda has trained hundreds of U.S. Customs agents to strengthen the nation’s borders against the tide of illegal imports.

To serve his community, Mr. Gioconda acted as Special Counsel to the King's County District Attorney's office in successfully defending a jury's verdict of armed robbery on appeal.

Joseph C. Gioconda’s Specialties:

Anticounterfeiting, Trademark Litigation and Enforcement, Copyright Litigation, Trade Dress Litigation, Unfair Competition, Entertainment and Media Law and Counseling. With more than 3,600 lawyers located in 25 countries and 64 offices throughout Asia, Europe, the Middle East and the U.S., DLA Piper is now the largest law firm in the world. These vast resources enable Mr. Gioconda to assist clients with their legal needs around the globe.
