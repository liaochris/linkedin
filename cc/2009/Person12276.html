Bob Sarni’s Experience

  • Agile Organizational Coach/Certified Scrum Coach (CSC) and Trainer (CST)

    EMC Consulting

    (Public Company; EMC; Information Technology and Services industry)

    March 2009 — Present (5 months)

    Bob Sarni is an Agile Organizational Change Coach for EMC Consulting Life Science Practice. He is focused on helping clients realize maximum value on their investments by leveraging Agile methods for product development. Bob’s expertise is in guiding enterprise transition and implementation of Agile’s values and principles.

    Bob is an Agile thought leader, coach, trainer and practitioner who is certified by the Scrum Alliance as a Certified ScrumMaster (CSM), a Certified Scrum Product Owner (CSPO), a Certified Scrum Practitioner (CSP), a Certified Scrum Coach (CSC) and a Certified Scrum Trainer (CST). Bob was a member of the 2008 spring (Chicago) and fall (Stockholm) Scrum Gathering program review committees, as well as a member of the Scrum Alliance committees that developed the new Certified Scrum Coach certification program and the new certification test for CSM and CSPOs. Currently he is a stage reviewer for the distributed development stage at Agile 2009. Additionally, Bob is a reviewer of CSP and CSC applications for the Scrum Alliance.
