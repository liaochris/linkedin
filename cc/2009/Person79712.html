Pierre Polycarpe’s Summary

Passionate about Talent Management and the SaaS market, I currently work for Taleo as the Pre-Sales Engineer for France and Southern Europe markets.

I love winning early and bringing "New Business" and "New names" to Taleo by delivering stunning presentations and demonstrations in front of a large audience of HR, IT and Executive people. I have no secret: I work hard to be a process, product & domain expert as well as trusted advisor, and I love my job!

I am a result driven Sales professional. Since I have joined Taleo, I have closed more than 20 major transactions, most of them among CAC40 from which: Veolia, Renault, Alstom, Air France, PSA, , Eiffage, Banque Populaire, ….

Outside of the traditional Pre-sales responsibilities, I play a key role in the marketing and localization of our product strategy for the French and European markets. My background as a Consultant and Entrepreneur is certainly the main reason for my thirst of new ideas and new ways of marketing our products and doing business.

I have also been instrumental in the creation of an eco-system of partners and consulting firms to grow our influence on the market. Our demonstrated track record of success as a team is certainly my best advocate of the value I try to bring. Want to know more? Ask any people I have been working with! I hope they will speak for me.

I often am a speaker in tradeshows and events (Congrès HR, Solution RH, RH 2010, Petit-déjeuners, Webcast) and a point of contact for journalist and analyst interviews to promote our brand and our new products.

My personal productivity strategic weapon in 4 words: “Home office & Team Work”. I am also a Talent Management Blogger, but as you will see, not a full time one!
