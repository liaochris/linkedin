Kristin Gumper’s Summary

With her background in both the non-profit and for-profit sectors, Kristin Gumper, senior account executive, brings valuable insight to Standing Partnership.

Kristin currently provides crisis communications, strategic employee communications and national and trade media relations for a variety of clients including The Doe Run Company, the Dishman Group, Solutia, CARBOGEN AMCIS AG and Delaware North Companies. Kristin's project management and copywriting efforts have helped land clients numerous accolades, including several APEX Awards for Publication Excellence, Silver Quill and Bronze Quill awards from the International Association of Business Communicators, as well as a 2005 TAM award from the Business Marketing Association in St. Louis.

Prior to joining Standing Partnership, Kristin served as an international and regional communications contractor for Anheuser-Busch Companies, Inc. While at Anheuser-Busch, Kristin provided communications support for a variety of events and projects, including the South African Budweiser rollout, American Red Cross canned water donations (due to hurricanes), Tom Joyner Sky Shows, and Clydesdale Camera Days at breweries nationwide.

Prior to her work at Anheuser-Busch, Kristin worked for the National Multiple Sclerosis Society, where she provided extensive media relations and writing support for various programs, including the National MS Walk, MS 150 and 2002 MS Sports Celebrity Awards Dinner.

Outside of work, Kristin is most proud of the time she donates to homeless animals as a puppy foster parent for Heartland Weim Rescue, Stray Rescue of St. Louis, and the Humane Society of Missouri. Kristin also serves as secretary of the Maryland Heights Parks & Recreation Advisory Committee, and is a member of the Missouri Parks and Recreation Association. Kristin enjoys learning about Missouri wines and honing her equestrian skills in her spare time.
