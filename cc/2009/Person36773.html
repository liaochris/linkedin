Karol Brown’s Summary

Global Justice Law Group provides high quality legal services for family and employment based immigration to the United States. Our firm offers extraordinary customer services to families and businesses seeking visas to the US. We use online technology to allow our clients to request services, provide information, review the status of their case, and have their questions answered. Our attorneys provide professional, intelligent, well-informed answers and assistance throughout the immigration process.

Karol Brown’s Specialties:

Family based immigration, employment based immigration, H1B visas, L1A visas, L1B visas, TN NAFTA visas, O1 Aliens of Extraordinary Ability, permanent residency, green card, immigrant and nonimmigrant visa petitions, asylum, VAWA, naturalization, and citizenship
