Richard Hirsh’s Summary

Certified in the comprehensive practice of industrial hygiene. Global Industrial Hygiene and Safety Manager for Stiefel Laboratories as well as EHS Manager for the Stiefel West Coast R&D site. Responsible for global H&S policies, baseline standards, training, technical assistance, API exposure assessments, GHS MSDS management, and all EHS site activities. Previously at Rohm and Haas Company in the Global EHS and Sustainable Development department. Responsible for IH technical assistance to over 130 locations worldwide, training, developing corporate EHS policies and standards, conducting EHS compliance audits. Author of the company’s global Ergonomics Program standard. Past- President of the Northern California Section of the American Industrial Hygiene Association.
