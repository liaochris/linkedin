Brandon Dennis’s Summary

I am an award winning machinima author, with a YouTube following of 25 million. Machinima is film set in video game worlds, and it is a new and innovative way to market a game. I am the creative force behind Oxhorn Brand Movies, which caught the attention of WeGame.com, where I have worked as the Creative Director for the past year and a half. I edit video using the Adobe Creative Suite, and have mastered Adobe After Effects and Adobe Premiere Pro. I write, sing, act, record and edit my own songs and scripts, and produce my own audio using Audacity. I am a jack-of-all trades when it comes to animated film production, which made me valuable to WeGame as a “studio-of-one”.

Proficient with industry standard motion graphics and visual effects software.

Creative visually, verbally and on paper.

Amiable personality / works well with others.

Relishes responsibility / willingness to tackle any project.

Community builder / company ambassador.

Hard worker / project finisher.

Communicates well with a team.

Experience directing a team of animators and musicians.

Versatile / master of numerous skill-sets

4 years experience with animated gaming film production.
