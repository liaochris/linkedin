Philippe Bertaud’s Summary

• Guitarist-Performer and Recording Artist, I regularly perform in Europe, the USA, Canada and Latin America (Mexico,Brazil, Chile, Salvador...), I have appeared at venues such as Carnegie Hall in New York, International Guitar Festival in Round Top, World Guitarist Series in CA, the Montreal Jazz Festival, Euro-San Francisco, Winter Festival in Brazil, CultFusion, Festival des Tulipes and the Edmonton Festival as well as many others, and recorded 4 different CD's and a masterclass DVD about Villa Lobos that will be distributed by Carl Fisher in January. I represent the Alhambra Guitars and also other companies such as D’Addario and Planet Waves.

Philippe Bertaud’s Experience

  • Endorser

    D'Addario and Planet Waves

    (Music industry)

    November 2008 — Present (1 year 1 month)

    D'Addario is a manufacturer of musical instrument strings, primarily guitar strings, currently headquartered in Farmingdale, Long Island, New York. It is a family-owned-and-operated business that is one of the largest string manufacturers in the world, not only producing several lines of strings under their own brand names, but also making OEM strings for other musical instrument companies. They also produce and distribute other musical accessories under the companies Planet Waves (guitar cables, straps, polish and other accessories), Evans (drumheads) and Rico (reeds for woodwind instruments.)

  • Endorser

    Alhambra Guitar Company

    (Arts and Crafts industry)

    June 2007 — Present (2 years 6 months)

    Manufacturas Alhambra was founded in 1965 from an original small guitar workshop located in the town of Almura de Alcoy in Spain.

    From the very beginning it was always our policy to combine the highest standard of craftmanship with the latest advancements in technology to produce the finest quality instruments.
    We have given special priority to our research department where all ideas and projects are developed. Manned by highly qualified staff, it has enabled us to produce the top models for our range which today are considered second to none.

    Finally, with the coordination of our production director and our specialized craftsman, the results of these investigations are applied to our basic guitars. Although modern technology is an important part of our production technique, all our instruments are built in the traditional Spanish way.
    The reward for our endeavours is the acknowledgement that Alhambra guitars enjoy a deservedly worldwide reputation for outstanding quality.

  • Director of Summer Guitar Academy

    Round Top International Institute

    (Fine Art industry)

    September 2006 — Present (3 years 3 months)

    The Guitar Academy at Round Top offers one week of intensive training for young talented guitarists (18 years of age and over) under the tutelage of distinguished international faculty. Daily classes focus on issues of technique, interpretation, music theory and score analysis, problem solving, sight-reading, improvisation and ensemble playing and performance. The format of each day is a mixture of group activity, master class and ensemble rehearsal with a group performance at the end of the week. Solo performances are scheduled every night.

    The International Festival-Institute offers to invited participants a full scholarship for tuition through the James Dick Foundation for the Performing Arts. Room and board are included.
