Chad Allen’s Summary

"Things should be made as simple as possible, but not any simpler."
-- Albert Einstein

Chad Allen is an interactive designer and developer currently residing in beautiful Salt Lake City, Utah. Many of the other ‘web guys’ focus exclusively on the creative side or the technical side, but I pride myself on excelling at both. I specialize in simple, clean, professional, and creative website designs and web development that makes your business not only look better on the web but ultimatley increase your bottom line.

Design is more than just choosing colors, typefaces, shapes, and images. I believe that great website design is in the details. I believe that design must support the functionality, and vice versa. Good website design results in a positive experience for your customers. I create user experiences that reflect positivley on my clients brand and company.

+ Concepting and storyboarding ideas based around thematic messages

+ Designing interactive websites and applications from rough to polish

+ Developing visual aspects of client brands

+ Maintaining an ongoing understanding of design trends

+ Versatile UI designer & web technologist with 8 years experience in user-interface design, user-experience design, prototyping and front-end development.

+ Extensive involvement in high-traffic web development cycles and projects

+ Strong background in cross-browser/-platform development, W3C web standards, CSS development, as well as optimization and accessibility.

+ Excels in agile development environments.
