Emrys Roberts’s Summary

My passion when I was young was always art and creativity and remains to this day. When I eventually made it to college, my focus evolved after having an inspiring professor who introduced me to the world of video and film, and realizing I had a real talent with the art form. I've been in corporate video for about 4 to 5 years now, and I also have my own business that I produce my short films, documentaries, and graphic design.

My main areas of expertise have been Motion Graphics Design, Video Editing and Videography, but I have been finding a renewed passion for building my cinematography experience and quality. I've also been pursuing my passion for storytelling in film and in motion media.
