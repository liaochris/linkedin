Tammy Mathias’s Summary

My core values and goals include meeting new people, assisting others with succeeding in meeting their business goals - both off-line (via events and meeting face-to-face). I am considered to be among the Top Networkers and Business Matchmaking Experts in Colorado.

I am the past Director Colorado Event Operations and Host for Integrated Alliances, where I planned and executed their networking events and LinkedIn Workshops. My responsibilities included scouting and negotiating venues, food, lighting, entertainment, set-up of exhibitors, planning the events and coaching volunteers on the event.

Experience included registration operations in Denver and Boulder. These events attracted 100-500 people and 10-20 exhibitors each month depending on the type of event and time of season. I coached business owners, sponsors and exhibitors and also planned and produced successful trade-shows.

I speak at events engaging other business owners and business professionals. I promote the benefits of LinkedIn Hands-on Workshops and Webinars.

Why I am here in LinkedIn:

♦ I am seeking partners, sponsors, exhibitors and attendees for my Business Matchmaking Services and networking events at Dynamic’N’Ovations. I am also seeking individuals that wish to learn more about how to use LinkedIn for business purposes and career development.

♦ I am networking with people to build my network and help others. I believe that the true benefits of networking happen “down the road” so I am looking to network (and connect) with a large number of people and turn them into quality connections so that all of my contacts will reap the benefits now and in the future.
