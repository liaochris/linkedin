Liz Argall’s Summary

To sum it up in one word, eclectic. I like to go in to a job, throw myself into it as hard as I can, live it, breathe it... and then have a nice nap and a lie down before the next gig. I think people are amazing and love working in areas where I can help people's wonderful qualities manifest... from running adult education workshops, to developing and supporting volunteers, helping people connect to services that can help them, sharing a story or cultural experience, being a good boss or board member.

Goal, to tell stories that entertain and make people think.

Liz Argall’s Specialties:

Story telling across a range of media, communication, liaison, adult education, consulting, writing articles, building empowerment, engagement and grass roots activism, writing and acquitting grants.
