Jerry Long’s Summary

“Providing Professional Representation & Consultation to Buyers & Sellers of Real Estate since 1986.”
I have operated Jerry Long Real Estate Group continuously since 1986, except for a “time out” as Broker and C.O.O. at Coldwell Banker Griffith & Blair. I belong to both the Lawrence Board of REALTORS® Multiple Listing Service, and the Topeka Area Association of REALTORS® (TAAR) and it’s MLS. At TAAR, I have served as chairperson of many committees, and also as a Director and President of the association and its MLS in 2004, currently serving as vice-chairman of the Professional Standards Committee. I am a Director of the Kansas Association of REALTORS® and am a member of the National Association of REALTORS®. I hold the ABR, CRB, CRS, GRI & SRES Professional Designations, and am licensed as a Real Estate Broker in the states of Kansas and Colorado.
