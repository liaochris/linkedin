Collin Starkweather’s Summary

Economist at leading Chicago-based economics consultancy with specialties in M&A, including antitrust and related regulatory issues, and financial analysis, including securities fraud and economic analysis in support of various litigation issues. Reputation for delivering timely results and innovative analysis with the highest level of professional integrity. Exceptionally strong numerical modeling background, including experience with both econometrics and numerical optimization. Intermediate Mandarin speaking, reading, and writing.

In addition to work in economics and numerical modeling, my work has included extensive software engineering and architecture, database management (DBA), and systems administration experience with large-scale Unix and Linux deployments as well as management of IT teams and software product development.

Notable Achievements:
- A former top-performing Economist at Lexecon (now CompassLexecon)
- Quickly promoted at The Active Network to lead the engineering team through incredibly rapid growth
- Awarded the highest honor conferred on foreign experts by Yunnan Province
- Maintained a flourishing independent software engineering consulting practice through the worst of the dot-com bust

Years of Experience:
- 5 years experience managing teams of research analysts and software engineers
- 9 years experience in numerical optimization, econometrics, and algorithm design
- 9 years software engineering and architecture experience
- 2 years teaching experience at the university level
