Roger Lee’s Summary

For over two decades I have served as a national political and direct mail consultant for progressive and democratic candidates, organizations, causes and ballot measures. Based in Washington DC I also have offices in Southern California. I offer my consulting services to a limited number of clients allowing me to provide direct hands-on service.

Throughout my career, I have only selected progressive and democratic candidates and causes. Often my candidates were considered underdogs, but viable. Usually outspent by the opposition, I developed new strategies to squeeze the maximum value out of limited campaign resources.

My new techniques worked, and I have won 50 of the 55 major campaigns I have directed. These strategies have been refined by time and technology, and they form the cornerstone of the services I offer my clients today.

In addition to the races have managed, I have served as the direct mail consultant to an abundant number of other candidates and organizations. In this capacity I have received numerous awards for direct mail campaigns as well as from progressive organizations.

My clients have included Clinton/Gore (National Direct Mail Consultant) 1992), Democratic National Committee (DNC), Democratic Congressional Campaign Committee (DCCC), Democratic Legislative Campaign Committee (DLCC), Dean for America Campaign, NAACP, NARAL Pro-Choice America, Amnesty International, Emily’s List “Women Vote!” Project, as well as for State Democratic Parties and State Coordinated Campaigns, State Legislative Campaign Committees, Candidates and Ballot Measures throughout the country from Alaska to Missouri to Pennsylvania.
