Iain MacCall’s Summary

A business focused programme and project manager who has gained over 20 years of experience in service architecture, design, and implementation while meeting business challenges in several sectors. Developed a pragmatic approach to delivery by working for end-users, service providers and consultancies across sectors like financial services, media, telecoms, government, retail and manufacturing.

  • Programme Manager

    Reuters

    (Public Company; RTR.L; Financial Services industry)

    November 2004 — December 2008 (4 years 2 months)

    Programme Manager for one of the world’s largest infrastructure transformation projects, moved Reuters from fragmented local provision of networks for product distribution to 18,000 customer sites in 130 countries, to using one outsourced provider for distribution on a common global network. Shaped programme into 8 interdependent streams eg establishing new business processes and systems for ordering, fulfilling and supporting Reuters products; developing and launching IP-enabled versions of legacy products; connecting and launching 60 products on the new network; training frontline staff and setting new commercial policy, Led the programme so (a) saving $73 Million pa from a $410 Million pa spend on telecommunications service while maintaining operational service levels to Reuters demanding finance sector customers (b) centralising key support roles and (c) building a team delivering significant organisational change and additional financial benefits by reducing Reuters data centre foot print from 220 datacentres to 30.
