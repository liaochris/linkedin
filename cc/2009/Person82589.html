Terri Hayley’s Summary

Terri Hayley's background is in new home sales. She was the #1 Sales Counselor for one of the Top 20 Builders in the Nation. Terri negotiated the new home transaction between the buyer and seller in 100's of transactions and really excels at getting you what you want at the best price possible. So, it only makes sense that she decided in 2008 to join her husband, Robert, at the # 1 real estate sales offices in Dallas - Keller Williams Preston Road office where we closed $850 million in sales last year alone.

My husband, Robert Hayley, and I work as a team. He does more of the commercial side, but works with me on the residential side of things, too. Ask us about our "sell your home in 39 days or sell it for free" home seller program. Also, if you are interested in buying a home, we guarantee to save you a minimum of $5,000 on the purchase of your home. How is it different working with us? You will be hard pressed to find a Realtor team who will listen more meticulously to your needs, try harder to fulfill them, genuinely care more about you and your family's happiness, who are native Texans with a broad scope deep knowledge of the North Texas area, and will negotiate on your behalf with whatever level of ferocity the situation requires to get your needs met. If you are interested in buying or selling a home in the Dallas, TX area, please give me a ring.
