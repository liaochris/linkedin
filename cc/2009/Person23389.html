Dave Boulter’s Summary

My experience includes sales, marketing and service delivery to Financial, Higher Education and Research, Oil and Gas, Manufacturing and Media markets. Responsibilities have included management of the Customer Support Centre, Logistics, Service Sales, Professional Services, Systems Engineering and Field Service Delivery teams. Expertise also includes product management and strategic planning functions.

I am adept at building and maintaining strong teams, building new business, and providing leadership and direction through implementation of strategies. I have been noted as a respected leader with the ability to focus and motivate teams to achieve company and individual objectives. I have developed communication and problem solving skills with the ability to resolve conflicts and achieve improved business performance. I believe I possess significant energy and enthusiasm and enjoy a challenging environment.

I am looking for a senior management role requiring a strategic business approach and strong leadership skills to create significant value for an organisation. I enjoy achieving success through team based involvement. I hope that my broad industry experience enhanced by MBA studies will ensure continued success in roles of substantial challenge.
