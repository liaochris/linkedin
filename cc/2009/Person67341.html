Michael Gilboe’s Summary

I produce and record a variety of projects for many different clients. I love working with different types of projects, and am good with explaining the various parts of the process to clients. In addition, I am very familiar with new media on the internet, and can assist my clients with achieving the visibility they desire on their projects.

  • Songwriter and Composer

    Michael Gilboe (Self-employed)

    (Self-Employed; Myself Only; Music industry)

    February 1989 — Present (20 years 6 months)

I am currently writing music and lyrics for an internet musical, "Cupid".

  • Artistic Director

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Performing Arts industry)
