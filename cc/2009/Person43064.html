James Piggott’s Summary

A freelance artist focusing on a combination of arts and technology. A nerd in the extreme, he's learned how computers, technology, and the internet can be used in combination with artistic skill to produce a unique style. He has a store of knowledge on social networking and internet communication, running a Blog for almost 3 years, and a website for almost 8 years. Recently, he's expanded his website count from one to several (an exact count can't be made at this point, as there are more coming) and expanded from a simple Blog to connecting tendrils throughout the internet's social web services.
