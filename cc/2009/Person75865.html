Onno L.J. de Vries’s Summary

My Mission:
Motivate people to be liberated from their own fear and be powerful beyond measure.

My own History:
Onno is owner of The Gordian Knot a privately held company active in the field of Business Engineering, Excecutive Management & Consulting Services.

Onno L.J. de Vries was co- founder and Director at Atrivision a limited liability company formed in 2007 providing dedicated Learning Process Solutions.

Prior to Atrivision Onno joint Siennax in February 2004. As an Account Director he was responsible for global business development of several major accounts, with a focus on Learning Process Outsourcing.

Prior to joining Siennax Learning Services
Onno was co-founder and Director of The Smartvalley a limited liability company formed in 2002 providing tailor made E-learningsolutions.

From 2000 to 2002 Onno hold functions as Managing Director within The Vision Web a limited liability company providing Consulting Services in the field of IT.

From 1983 to 2000 Onno was employed in several functions by Getronics lately as Managing Director responsible for a Business Unit providing IT-services.

Onno holds degrees in Business Administration and Engineering, and is Member of The Royal Institute of Engineers (KIVI NIRA), the Association of Organization Consultants and the International Association of Outsourcing Professionals (IAOP).
