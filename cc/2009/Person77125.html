Patrick Hoesly’s Summary

I’m an architectural illustrator creating 2D & 3D presentation images for designers to visualize and sell their designs. I’m experienced producing computer models, renderings, animations, graphic designs, and marketing materials.

My greatest strength is my architectural background as it allows me to communicate effectively with architects and designers. I can read CD drawings, specifications, sketches, and understand the architectural language. I completely understand an architect’s schedule and the need for things to be done correctly and on time.

I'm also the author and Illustrator of two watercolor books:
Words in Watercolor. ISBN: 978-0-557-08819-5
Quotes in Watercolor. ISBN: 978-0-615-32153-0
go to www.zooboing.com to download free images from the book!

3D Software applications including: 3D Studio Max • SketchUp • V-Ray • Mental Ray • Piranesi • E-On Vue • Cinema 4D • Artlantis • Archivision • Revit • AutoCAD • Architectural Desktop

2D Software applications including: Adobe Photoshop • Illustrator • InDesign • Acrobat • Premiere • Audacity • Microsoft Word • Excel • Access • FrontPage • Outlook • Powerpoint.
