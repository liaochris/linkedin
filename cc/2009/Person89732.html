BACKGROUND
I've traveled Alaska filming in just about every condition possible, and know what it means to get the shot and tell the story. A mountaineering and rock climbing background has conditioned me to put safety first by understanding the environment and surroundings.

EXPERIENCE OVERVIEW

Alaska Public Radio: KSKA 91.1 FM: Relief DJ, Editor
Last Frontier Studios: Studio Sound Engineer

ABOUT ME
A fifth generation Alaskan and adventure outdoor filmmaker. Founder of Alaska HDTV and Perfect Blend Media, which are based in downtown Anchorage, Alaska. Family history in Alaska has inspired the pioneer spirit throughout my life, from mountaineering, to the 1992 Olympic trials, to following my passion for Alaska wildlife, ecology, and study of Herpetology. I have more than 10 years in digital media production, search marketing, and website development with clients including universities, state offices and small innovative businesses.
