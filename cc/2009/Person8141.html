Alejandro Core’s Summary

Alejandro Saenz-Core works as a Renewables/Low Carbon Technologies Project Manager for Mott MacDonald, a firm expert on engineering and consulting in global infrastructure projects, developing different GHG offset projects from Landfill-gas-to-energy projects to Carbon Capture and Storage ones.

Previously he used to work as a Senior Business Developer for AgCert International, a firm expert on Green-House Gas offset projects.

He has evaluated Carbon Offset Opportunities and Emission Reduction Projects originated in USA, Ukraine, Russia, China, India, South Africa, Botswana, Kenya, Iran, Mexico, Brazil, Colombia, Peru, Honduras, Panama, Chile, Argentina and Uruguay.

Additionally, he has worked extensively in Strategy & Change Consulting as an Energy & Utilities Expert for firms, such as Navigant Consulting Inc., Wood MacKenzie Ltd and CERA. Prepared, analyzed, and delivered corporate strategy & transformation studies and solutions for clients working for Oil and Gas Major Players; such as Qatar Petroleum, Pemex, Total, BG, Repsol and Petrobras.

He has worked on several engagements combining finance, strategy and market analysis, including technical and financial modeling for energy companies on gas and power projects and initiatives. He provides expertise in regulatory policy, strategic business planning, market analysis, commercial structuring and negotiations, and the business development and financing of power generation, transmission and distribution; natural gas transmission and storage, LNG infrastructure throughout Latin America, Western Europe, North Africa and Australia.

In recent years, he has managed and supported comprehensive market evaluations in the energy and financial industries to global energy developers and investors.

Additionally, Mr. Saenz-Core is fluent in French, English, Portuguese and Spanish. He also got an MBA, a Masters Degree in Energy Systems and an Electrical Engineer Degree.

Alejandro Core’s Specialties:

Strategic planning, business & project development, solutions builder, contracts administration, commercial operations and marketing of energy projects, products and services.
Sizing carbon offset opportunitites, according existing UNFCCC's methodologies.
Simulation models and Excel Modeling.
