Cynthia Armistead’s Summary

I'm a business, technical, and general writer with a wealth of experience in diverse industries. My background, which includes insurance, medical, banking, retail, internet, telecom, accounting, networking, and musical knowledge as well as technical expertise, gives me an advantage in identifying business requirements and working with management and administrative personnel as an analyst or project manager.
