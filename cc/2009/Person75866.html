Onno Hamburger’s Summary

I have been training and coaching people for more than 10 years. I have developed my intuition and talent in such a way that I am able to help you to get to the core fast.

In a friendly but razor sharp approach I help you to differentiate between bullshit and the real thing. This facilitates you to make big steps in your personal development.

I use different methods while coaching and training, always trying to stay in close contact with your world. I have a preference for down-to-earth, evidence based interventions.

My main goal is to help you to increase your sustainable happiness at work. My definition of happiness@work is a combination of pleasure, meaning and satisfaction at work.

I think most people prefer happiness. Apart from that research shows that happy employees are more effective, innovative, productive and less vulnerable to stress than unhappy employees.

Onno is owner and group manager of several LinkedIn groups:

The "Happiness@work" group. The largest LinkedIn group on positive psychology and happiness(@work) in the world. The happiness@work group is an international group of selected professionals working with positive psychology and happiness in the workplace using evidence based interventions.

The "Positive Psychology Coaching network". An international network of positive psychology coaches sharing information and experiences on coaching clients using positive psychology interventions.

"Positieve Psychologen netwerk". Netwerk of Dutch psychologist that are interested in and work with positive psychology.
