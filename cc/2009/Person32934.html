Felicia Sullivan’s Summary

My passion is to help groups connect their missions for change to those who will make the mission reality.

I use the power of communication, media, and information technologies for community development, civic engagement and political action.. With an MA in Media Studies and 16 years of community-based practice, I have special expertise working with community media & technology centers as well as grassroots organizing and arts-based groups. I love working with individuals and organizations committed to growth, learning, building capacity, and change.

My particular strengths are in communications planning, web design and development (including web 2.0), media & technology education and technology planning, I also have expertise in professional development support, board development (including group facilitation), and program evaluation.

Modern communication, media and information technologies are powerful. Let me help you leverage them to be better meet the needs of those you serve.
