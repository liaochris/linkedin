Chris Lowe’s Summary

My professional experience to this point has helped me to discover that I do not wish to focus entirely on print or digital areas of design, but to continue to explore all areas of design.

Collaboration is very important to me, so the ability to work with other driven professionals is essential to a great work environment. I am interested in exploring the areas of user interface design and contribute my design skills to the design of products and applications.

Chris Lowe’s Specialties:
