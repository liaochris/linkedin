Gavin Little’s Summary

Echolab is an award winning sound design studio setup in 2001 by Gavin Little, specialising in creative sound for TV, Film & Interactive on worldwide projects for...Guinness, Coke, Heineken, O2, MTV, Kelloggs, Sony etc.

In 2005 Heineken 'MusicHeads' TV Commercial won the ICAD Award for Best Sound Design.

Current Projects:
2 MTVNHD Commercials. DIrected by KORB, Lithuania.
FUSE TV show opener Directed by Thornberg & Forester, NY.
New Under Armour Live-Action spot, Directed by Shilo, NY.
BlackBerry sponsorship stings for Fast Forward TV Show. Directed by Royale, LA. Agency: Leo Burnett, UK.

Recent Projects:
Sound Design on 2 commercials for Solpadeine/Solpasinus with Ogilvy, Dublin.
Sound Design for 'Colossus' a 5 minute art installation film by Paki Smith on exhibition at the Douglas Hyde Gallery, Dublin.
Creating the Sound Design on an epic 45sec cgi spot for Saatchi & Saatchi, Dubai.
Sound Design for a short film Directed by Vinny Morales (Kultnation) Mexico City.
Music Score & Sound Design on 2 TV Commercials for Under Armour, Directed by Shilo, NY.
Music Composition for Swatch Watch TV Commercial with Transistor Studios, NY.
Sony Style | Comcast Labs - Music Score & Sound Design for an in-store visual exhibit directed by Tronic Studio, NY.
Music & Sound Design for Cellular South TV Commercial - Agency Y&R, NY. Directed by Shilo, NY.

Echolab has also designed sound for short films including...
'The Silent City' directed by Oscar Nominated Director Ruairi Robinson starring Cillian Murphy (Batman/28 Days Later).
Sound Design on Nick Ryan's short film 'The German' starring Toby Kebbell (Control/Dead Man's Shoes).
'A Lonely Sky' directed by Nick Ryan and starring Kier Dullea (2001:A Space Odyssey)
'God's Kitchen' directed by Paki Smith - selected for competition at The 2003 Venice Film Festival. Echolab was nominated for TFTA Awards for Best Music for TV/Film.

Gavin Little’s Specialties:

Creative Sound Design & Music Composition.
