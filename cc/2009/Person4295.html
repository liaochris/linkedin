Alexander Lyadov’s Summary

I am passionate about discovering the true potential of innovative ideas, talented people, entrepreneurial ventures and original brands. Every person and every idea has its own Mission.zip file installed from on high. After observing very different people in various social settings I am more than ever convinced that following one’s own way and experiencing the act of creation in harmony with our values is what make us happy. No fame, money or power can be a substitute for inner drive and joy to be oneself.

All life events happen with good purpose, but often only later we appreciate their meaning and value. My education combined analytical chemistry, business administration, marketing, advertising and investments. Professionally I have 12+ years of comprehensive asset management experience - both tangible (money) and intangible (ideas, brands and human capital). Diverse stimulus is the best food for creative synthesis, right?

Currently I am researching the role of Trust and Reputation in uncovering group effort potential. The roots are in leading teams of incredibly talented people towards bold goals and having to fight every day with what I call “Post-soviet anti-group mentality”.

My strong belief is that the greatest things on Earth happen thanks to interdependent effort of people driven by one common goal and sharing similar set of fundamental values. This is equally true for innovative start-up venture, student association, non-profit team or the entire nation. Historically our countries were always lacking social capital, but now this might become a core bottleneck for future growth (fueled by innovation, not oil exploitation). If true, then any improvement in social capital accumulation would bring enormous rewards for you and me and the society at large.

Sounds idealistic? It’s not, as these are questions of existence as creative human being and not as a destructive weed. So they have quite practical meaning to me.
