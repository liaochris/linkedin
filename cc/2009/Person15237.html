Maurizio Caporali’s Summary

Maurizio Caporali is Product Manager and Interaction Designer at Softec spa and he teaches and research at the University of Siena IDA Design Laboratory (www.discom.unisi.it/ida/).
He has been working as a research assistant at the University of Siena Multimedia Media Lab, as a Interaction Designer at University of Applied Science western Switzerland, he worked at Arsnova, Digital Arts & Sciences (www.arsnovasiena.org), and Infoblu - Autostrade SpA (www.infoblu.it), as technology and product innovation analyst and Web designer.
Maurizio Caporali is information technology, services consultant and service designer for mobile devices business. His research interests include physical computing, Web 2.0 services, social computing, interaction design and mobile services.
He holds a Masters Degree in Mathematics at University of Siena, specialization in computer science, and a Ph.D. in Information and Communication Technologies at Electronic Engineering Department of University of Florence, Italy (http://tsi.det.unifi.it) (April 2007), with a focus on computer-supported cooperative work, social computing, collaborative knowledge-based environments.

Interaction Design, Digital MockUp, Mobile user experience design, interface design, Service Oriented Architecture, Web Service, XHTML, CSS, PHP
