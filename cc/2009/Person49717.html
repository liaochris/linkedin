John Walker’s Summary

I am currently available to provide hands-on services to organizations and individuals in the following three areas:

1. Recruitment - I am currently available to provide retained, contingent or direct sourcing recruiting services. I employ a wide range of tactics to find qualified candidates including using the latest tools like Blogs, Social Networking, Twitter, Facebook and Linkedin.

2. Online Advertising & Marketing - I am also available to provide online advertising and marketing services. I have a strong background in digital capabilities including social media marketing, SEM, viral campaigns, online publicity, mobile, video, Pay-Per-Click Advertising (PPC) and Search Engine Marketing (SEM) and Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

3. Career Coaching - I have over 15 years in recruiting that has given me a special insight into what makes job seekers successful in their quest to finding meaningful in employment. I advice on resume and cover letter writing, interviewing techniques and the mechanics behind using online tools to find a job.

I cover everything from Personal Branding, Social Networking, Social Media and all the latest online tools. If you ever wanted to know how to use Twitter, Facebook and Linkedin to find your next job then we should talk.

My overall objective to combine and apply all of my experience in a comprehensive way that provides a solid foundation to creating solutions for today's most difficult business challenges in Recruitment, Personal Branding, Advertising and Marketing.

I currently volunteer at the California Center for Sustainable Energy where I advise and assist their marketing team in using Social Media and online marketing campaigns that drive traffic and create aware in the community about their programs and events.
