Lenore Wilkas, gri, sres, rcs-d’s Summary

This California native likes nothing better than to assist people in buying and selling their property. The San Francisco Peninsula is one of the nicest places on this earth and we are so lucky to live here! That's why I like to share this devotion to my area with others. I live and work on the Peninsula. Real Estate is an exciting arena for us with a healthy economy and vibrant real estate market. I'd like to help you in your next real estate transaction.

• I am here to negotiate for you so you get the best price for your property.
• I am here to show you our communities and help you select the right one for you.
• I am here as a resource both during and after your home purchase.
• I am here to counsel you, support you and help you.
• I am here to assist you whether you are buying your first home or moving up to a larger one.

Real Estate is a service business and that is what I provide. Prudential Fine Homes International is a worldwide high end real estate company and we are here, in downtown Burlingame, ready to help you find your new home or sell your current one and move to the next one.
