Suhit Anantula, MBA, PRINCE2’s Summary

Suhit is a Strategic Planning Officer at the Dept. for Families and Communities (DFC) with the State government of South Australia.

Suhit co-founded TechNovus Corp with the mission to evangelize cloud computing & SaaS in the Oceania region.

Suhit recently founded the Adelaide Green Drinks network.

Suhit worked as an "Environmental Project Officer" at DFC. He is an MBA graduate from the International Graduate School of Business in Australia.

Suhit is a contributing author on Micro-Finance to the book WorldChanging.

Suhit has co-founded www.bpodigest.com, India's first interactive vortal on the outsourcing industry which was launched by the CM of AP, YS Raja Shekara Reddy. It caters exclusively to the outsourcing community providing news, information, knowledge, blogs, forums and jobs.

Suhit’s worked as a Project Manager with Deeshaa Ventures (www.deeshaa.com) involved working with the RISC core team members in helping formulate the business strategies and evangelizing the idea and has also created a community of interested members (~350) though an online community.

Suhit has been part of the core group behind the Tsunami Help website (htt://tsunamihelp.blogspot.com) which provided critical information in the Dec, 2004 Tsunami and was covered by major newspapers magazines all around the world.

Prior to joining Deeshaa he worked as a Senior Analyst at ADP India, a company that focuses on providing mission-critical software and Business Process Outsourcing services to Investment Banks.

Suhit has a very strong interest in sustainability, environment, economics, technology and development and maintains a weblog chronicling this interest at www.worldisgreen.com. The weblog was nominated as the “Best IndiBlog of the Year” for 2004.

Suhit is a featured writer for Oh! My News International where he writes on various topics.

He lives with his wife, Bindu Madhavi, in Adelaide, Australia.
