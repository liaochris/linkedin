Dawn Bonner’s Summary

www.livinglifeboomerstyle.com
www.livinglifeboomerstyle.mobi

This is an upscale lifestyle magazine combined with online shopping about the lifestyles that baby boomer's have created. Departments include travel, movies, 'Ask Ernie' is a column written by the Dear Abby of addiction, Ernie Hooker, music, fashion, and fun.

After two years of planning the magazine is now up and running with several different columns, 98% is all original content.

Attending college for a double major in journalism and photography, plus bringing together 14+ years in the salon industry, and years of working with people in marketing campaigns, customer service, business consulting, and interior decorating have all added up to a fun, upbeat, entertaining, interesting, and unique magazine.

Dawn Bonner’s Specialties:
