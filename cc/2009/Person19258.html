Chris Kenworthy’s Summary

I am a web developer and online marketer. I have 12+ years of experience developing and marketing web applications, web sites, and online communities.

In 2001 I founded Dream.In.Code, a programming and web development community. Dream.In.Code is the leading web site for programming help with over 155,000 members and 1.2 million page views/month.

I have a degree in Web Technology and experience developing web sites in PHP and ColdFusion. I have built and optimized MSSQL and MySQL databases as well as configured and optimized IIS and Apache web servers for high traffic web environments.

I am Google AdWords and Google Analytics certified and have managed hundreds of ad campaigns. I am passionate about web analytics, A/B and multivariate testing, and optimization. I am experienced in on-site monetization, email marketing, search engine optimization, and search engine marketing.

I am available on a limited basis as a consultant for optimization, SEO, email marketing, and AdWords projects.
