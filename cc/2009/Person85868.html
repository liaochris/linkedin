Roland Graham’s Summary

Passionate advocate for homeowners and business owners for the adoption of technologies that fit their specific lifestyle and functional needs.

This advocacy is extended into the HDHomes.tv network where we produce and air episodes showcasing people's engagement with technology and the specific products that state-of-the-art manufacturers are producing. We further extend our online video platform out to other organizations who have a technology story to tell and a specific audience to reach.

Additionally, this advocacy extends into the events that we produce. Creating environments that foster high value interaction between end-users, service providers, manufacturers, project mangers, engineers, and specifiers.

Direct and candid communication is critical to the successful implementation of technology to fit specific end-user needs. It is to this end that our events strive to deliver value to all participants.
