Dana Deskiewicz’s Summary

I thoroughly enjoy the creative industry. While the challenges are similar across any medium - to translate client initiatives to a target audience - the journey from concept to completion on any given project is varied, challenging and exciting. For over a decade, I have initiated and created solutions for campaigns both online (sites, rich media, social networks, iPhone) and offline (print, broadcast, outdoor)

I am an expert in developing innovative creative strategies and solutions specializing in online and digital. I have integrated, cross-media campaign experience with print, broadcast and other offline media to create unique experiences for any project. My experience ranges from brands to entertainment, meeting the needs of such clients as HBO, CBS, vitaminwater, Barnes & Noble, Match.com, Ask.com, Disney and others with success.

Being an ardent follower and enthusiast of new media and emerging technologies, I am always in the know of current and future trends. This, along with my diverse experience, allows me to provide a unique solution through a combination of strategy, design and technology to meet any project goal.

Other responsibilities include:
• Recruitment and management of team members
• Development and implementation of creative process and project budgets
• Cross departmental communication and collaboration
• Strategy on new business development and pitches
• Vendor relationships
