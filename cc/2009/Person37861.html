Greg Pitkoff’s Summary

Experienced corporate, consumer and B2B communicator combining:

- corporate communications and executive publicity for 1-800-FLOWERS.COM

- specialty in children's product and services marketing, including programs for HearthSong, Magic Cabin and The Little Gym
