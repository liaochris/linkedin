Blair Wright’s Summary

I have large scale enterprise infrastructure experience from working in some of the world’s cutting edge IT companies.

I have worked for IBM (Global Support), WaMu (Washington Mutual), Microsoft (Passport & MMS), Kindred Healthcare (Corporate Data Center) and the Walt Disney Internet Group just to name a few Fortune 500 companies I that I have worked with as a Systems Engineer / Consultant

Some of my professional experiences include designing and deploying a secure 5 domain Active Directory forest and integrating the forest with 4 other AD forests across 5 data centers throughout the US. I then migrated all the Walt Disney Internet group properties to the new PCI compliant “secure” Active Directory forest. Other WDIG achievements include assisting in the design and deployment of critical DisneyMobile.com (MVNO) backend component services that were critical for the launch of the global MVNO service.

I also helped launch a new Microsoft property MMS (Microsoft Managed Solutions) where I was part of a small “Tier III Ops” team that designed, deployed and managed “in-house” large scale Active Directory backend solutions for global corporations.

Another position I had the honor of being a part of was Microsoft MSN passport. There I provided Tier III secure operations support (SecOpsIE) for MSN Passport (Windows Live ID). I worked incident response for 700+ load balanced IIS servers that provided login and SSL authentication for the primary MSN authentication portal for all Microsoft properties.

If my experience sounds like it may be a good fit with your organization, please contact me and I would be happy to send you my resume.

Thank you for your consideration.
