Andy Settle’s Summary

Andy Settle is a consultant in the IT industry with a wealth of experience working in both large and small organisations. Andy has the skill set and breadth of knowledge needed by operations deploying or maintaining information systems.

With a background in systems and network integration, software development and operations, he has the end-to-end process knowledge enabling him to step into most stages within the life cycle of most IT systems.

Andy has been responsible for the production and delivery of security solutions and policies for numerous clients. Taking responsibility for such things as security audits and assessments for planning and deployment of capacity and business continuity tasks. He has also headed up proactive security uplifts as well as incident responses and operating system hardening as and when necessary.

Andy is a copy editor for The Perl Review (www.theperlreview.com).

Andy Settle’s Specialties:

• A broad and mature understanding of Information Security (INFOSEC).

• A broad knowledge of the Unix family of operating systems including Solaris, AIX, HP/UX, BSD and Linux.

• A broad knowledge of scripting and programming languages such as Perl, C, C++, Java, Ruby, Pascal, TCL/TK, SH, Visual Basic and JavaScript.

• Network programming, Unix programming, Vulnerability Assessments, Penetration testing, Web & Database security.
