  • Owner

    Allan Burch Illustration (Self-employed)

    (Self-Employed; Myself Only; Graphic Design industry)

    January 1994 — Present (15 years 7 months)

    I am a portrait artist and illustrator, creating images for editorial, publishing, advertising, and institutional clients.

    My illustrations are rooted in realism with a personal raw energy and flowing movement. Concepts employ subtlety and use the components of the painting -- value, light, texture, color, and cropping -- to help tell the story. They work great for any project that has a need for contemporary visuals that are straightforward, accessible to a wide audience, and display a personal edge. They also work perfectly for any and all portrait needs.
