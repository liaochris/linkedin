Kate Dickman’s Summary

During the last 2 years of college, Kate served as an apprentice of sorts, learning the basics of internet marketing while expanding her knowledge on the subject every day. Eventually she obtained clients of her own and has been working as a freelancer (around full time work) ever since.

After returning from a philanthropic trip to Ghana, Africa, Kate left her passion for TV & Film behind and fully began pursuing a career in digital marketing. Although she enjoys SEM as a whole, social media is her primary interest.

She has a Bachelor of Science in Communication Arts with a Minor in Marketing from St. John's University.
