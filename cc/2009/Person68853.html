Mike Wikan’s Summary

I have been in the production of Video Games for 16 years now, and in the Art field for years before that.

I have Designed Games (Video and otherwise), led Production Teams, designed Camouflage for Soviet tanks in Simulators, designed Toy Spaceships for several companies, crafted art for the tourist industry in Florida, won awards at many game shows, dove off the coast of Puerto Rico and the Lesser Antilles, stalked the wily Largemouth Bass in the swamps of Canoochie creek, hired attorneys for intellectual property issues, stood on the battlefield at Yorktown where Cornwallis surrendered to Washington and lived in many, many places in my life.

I bring a wide range of professional and personal experiences to bear on any project I deal with.

Mike Wikan’s Specialties:

I have worn many hats in my years as a Game Designer. I have written scripts, designed Pitch Documents, interviewed Candidates,crafted games from whole cloth, mixed and edited Audio, Directed Projects, dealt with many licensed products, and sold Millions and Millions and Millions of games.
