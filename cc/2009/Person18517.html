Christine Stoddard’s Summary

I am a professional writer (www.associatedcontent.com/christinestoddard) with interest and experience in other fields of the arts, mainly acting (www.modelmayhem.com/christinestoddard), drawing (http://forgetfairytales.blogspot.com), and collage (http://christineandcollage.blogspot.com). Personally, I refer to myself as an "interdisciplinary wordsmith" because I constantly strive to find new ways to integrate language into film/video, stage productions, illustration, and mixed media. I try to find connections between art forms rather than creating harsh and superficial divisions. Publicly and professionally, however, I refer to myself as a "writer and interdisciplinary artist" because it is less ambiguous of a term.

Right now, I investing my time and effort in a wide range of creative endeavors and academic work. My freelance projects range but mostly involve writing (from non-fiction to fiction in a variety of genres and forms) but I also act and sell my comics and collages. Outside of creating my own art, I try to help other young artists, which is why I established the Neo-Indie Arts Festival (http://neoindieartsfestival.blogspot.com).

Christine Stoddard’s Specialties:

Writing skits and dialogues; acting; summarizing content; writing display advertising; editing in English, Spanish, and basic French and Italian; digital photography (familiarity with Nikon digital cameras and knowledge of Photoshop); resume writing; fashion writing; knowledge of AP style; writing and editing poetry; freelance work; online business; teen marketing; work-from-home; artistic/alternative modeling; literature critique; formatting documents, organizing art festivals
