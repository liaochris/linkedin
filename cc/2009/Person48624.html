Joe M. Turner’s Summary

Joe M. Turner is a professional speaker and corporate entertainer based in Atlanta, Georgia. He creates customized magic, mentalism and illusion presentations for meetings and events in the United States and Canada. His one-man show ran for two years at the Grand Hyatt Atlanta and now tours other hotels and theaters across America.

By adapting his performances to emphasize his clients' key messages, Mr. Turner has developed a niche as "America's Corporate Conjuror." He has also served as a magic consultant, writer and director for television, theatre, film and promotional projects. Mr. Turner has entertained prominent elected officials, sports figures, corporate leaders and other celebrities. He has performed and been interviewed in numerous television, radio and print appearances, including appearances on Good Morning America, Nightline, and CNN Headline News.

Joe is a repeat performer at renowned entertainment venues, including regular appearances at the world-famous Magic Castle in Hollywood, California. Joe is one of an elite group of performers who is certified to perform in all three of their showrooms – close-up, parlor, and stage. For you, this means that Joe delivers a proven combination of world-class skills and multi-venue versatility to meet your specific needs.

Joe combines seven years of corporate experience in training, business communication, human performance development and change management initiatives at Fortune 100 clients with his extensive theatre experience and magical talents to create customized magical presentations for his clients.

As a speaker, some of his keynote topics include "Memory Mojo!" which focuses on mnemonic memory training for personal productivity, and "The MAGIC of Change," a basic change management toolkit for emerging organizational leaders.

For details, visit http://www.turnermagic.com and http://www.turnertalks.com
