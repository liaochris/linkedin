Damnish Kumar’s Summary

BookMyKhana.com - Online Food Court

As Internet is gaining popularity, online food ordering system is becoming an essential part of any restaurant's marketing arsenal. In mid 2008, a brilliant idea blossomed and after thorough market research & analysis of the infinite prospects lying on this particular segment of food business, the decision was made to kick start this endeavour.
After 9 months of relentless hard work, finally BookMyKhana.com was launched on March 31, 2009. It chose Delhi & NCR as its operating field, as this region has the highest per capita income in India, a booming working hub with excellent Infrastructural facilities & having an average monthly budget of Rs. 2000 on outside food only. Very quickly, it jumped up the ranking charts. Only in three month’s time from its inception, it moved from rank 1 crore on alexa to 61000 ranking worldwide. With a minimum of 500 users visiting daily & having 400+ Restaurants in line, BookMyKhana.com today stands as India’s No.1 Online Food Order Portal.

Special Features making it a success:

1. Free and easy use
2. Efficient online ordering process
3. Elimination of phone order misunderstandings due to communication barriers
4. Saves time for customers, restaurants and merchants
5. Price & quality comparison feature among restaurants
6. Saves customer’s favourite orders and restaurants for easy re-ordering
7. Increased access to special discounts and promotions for the online customers
8. Increased exposure for restaurants and merchants to the marketplace

BookMyKhana.com, a revolution in online food ordering system is treading new grounds of innovation & cultivates a culture which encourages excellence with ground breaking measures to excel. This spirit to stand alone in the competition & determination coupled with a dedicated work force will surely promise a brighter future for BookMyKhana.com.
