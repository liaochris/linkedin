Grant Hogarth’s Summary

I am an experienced professional communicator, information developer, and team leader, with a proven ability to effectively communicate both business and technical knowledge to diverse audiences. I create award-winning documents (both technical and non-technical) and products for companies of all sizes, from startups to Fortune 500 companies. I bring a wide range of knowledge and experience to my work, and the ability to see the immediate details and place them in a larger context.

My experience includes 10+ years managing both creative and technical personnel, and in two positions increased revenues by 105% ($0.7M), and 45% ($0.11M). I have a strong knowledge of the disciplines supporting product development, (configuration management, change control, engineering, QA, localization, etc.), as well as a working knowledge of databases, spreadsheets, and project-management tools such as MS Project. I have a history of successfully employing risk/mitigation analysis to identify potential problems and opportunities.

My skills include effective team leadership, clear writing, strong (and sensitive) editing, information architecture, document architecture, publication design, usability, desktop publishing, conceptual illustration, and writing for the Web.
