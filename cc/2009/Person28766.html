Dustin Williams’s Summary

Dustin started life as a developer, which influences his design. He draws on keen understanding, experience, seeing, and confidence to design with clean lines and strong user focus.

Dustin’s many specialties all have “user experience” at their core. They include Branding, Interaction Design, User Interface Design, Website Design, Website Development, Graphic Design and Usability. His other skills also include: User Interface Prototyping, Storyboards and Wireframing Scenarios, Information Architecture, Mobile Device Interaction and Social Device Interaction.

Dustin has done work for many Fortune 500 companies, often employing user research to focus on usability. One of those companies being Microsoft. He helped the Pioneer Studios (PMX) team out with various Interaction Design and User Interface work.

He’s comfortable with CSS/HTML Development, PHP, and Flash among many other tools, including almost everything that starts with “Adobe.”
