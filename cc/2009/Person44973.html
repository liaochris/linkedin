Jose Davi Furlan’s Summary

Managing Partner of Procesix and Vice-president of ABPMP Brazil - Association of Business Process Management (BPM) Professionals Chapter Brazil. Executive consultant, strategist, lecturer and instructor specialized in process improvement (BPM), quality and reference models (CMMI etc), and strategic planning with a wide experience and outstanding accomplishments in business and information technology related areas. He holds a Master in Business Administration at Catholic University of São Paulo (PUC-SP), a bachelor in Mathematics at the same university and a professional master's degree in Information Engineering and Project Management at Ernst & Young, Cleveland, USA. Researcher and author of seven books being a precursor of these themes for Portuguese-speaking countries: "Object Modeling through UML", Makron Books, 1998 (best-seller); "Business Modeling", Makron Books, 1997; "Executive Information Systems", Makron Books, 1994; "Information Reengineering", Makron Books, 1994; "Information Technology Megatrends", Makron Books, 1993; "How to Elaborate and Implement the Strategic Information Systems Planning", Makron Books, 1991; "Information Engineering", McGraw-Hill, 1988 (best-seller). Besides an author, he has been a technical reviewer of Information Technology and business administration related publications for Makron Books and Pearson Education. As a lecturer, he has given speeches in congresses, panels, debates and seminars on process improvement (BPM, CMMI), strategic thinking and software engineering throughout Brazil and abroad. In the academic field, he has been teaching at renowned post-graduate courses in São Paulo, Brazil, for many years on the topics of his expertise. He has an important international experience in Latin America, USA and Europe and holds an English certificate from University of Cambridge (England). Besides English, he is fluent in Portuguese and Spanish

Jose Davi Furlan’s Specialties:
