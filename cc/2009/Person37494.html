Greg Bray’s Summary

Greg Bray, President of Blue Tangerine Solutions, is a technical expert in all aspects of small business technology, including technology strategy, office network support, disaster recovery planning, and Web site development. Bray often refers to himself as a “translator”, as he bridges the gap between the different languages of business and technology.

Blue Tangerine Solutions, formerly known as eCalton, is an IT Services company that specializes in Internet Web site development and small business network management. The company helps its clients maximize the business benefits of Internet technology, as well as simplify network management and maintenance. In 2007, Blue Tangerine Solutions initiated its “Ask the Experts” Webinar series as an effort to give back to the industry by bringing busineses and industry experts together to share their knowledge in an easy and cost effective way.

Before owning the company, Bray was the Vice President of Operations for eCalton. Bray purchased the company in August 2006 and subsequently renamed it to Blue Tangerine Solutions.

Prior to joining eCalton in 1999, Bray was a Lead Web Developer for Research Triangle Institute in North Carolina. He has a MBA, with an emphasis in Management Information Systems and eCommerce, from North Carolina State University and a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science from Brigham Young University.
