Rob Gonzalez’s Summary

Primary technical contact responsible for providing consultative, proactive and/or reactive support to Cisco accounts. Depending on the Service Contract the individual may be responsible for network planning, design, implementation, operation and optimization (PDIOO). This may include remote or on-site: infrastructure design, implementation planning, deployment support, software strategy, performance engineering and optimization, knowledge transfer and mentoring, proof-of-concept testing, network auditing/sizing, pre-staging, pre-configuration, deployment planning and acceptance of Cisco product in execution or advisory/assistance or support models. Gives technical guidance to customers and internal parties as required. May act as technical project manager in projects.

Rob Gonzalez’s Specialties:

Technical evaluation, Subject Matter Expert, Protocol Translation, Standards based testing using GR-253-CORE, GR-499-CORE, IEEE 802.3.

Physical layer design and troubleshooting: DS1 through OC-192 TDM expert, SONET, Electrical and Optical Transport, DWDM.

Layer 2 design and troubleshooting: Ethernet LAN, WAN, Frame Relay, ATM, Metro Ethernet, Gigabit Ethernet, 10G Ethernet, X.25.

Layer 3 design and troubleshooting: IP, Routing protocols, OSPF, BGP, EIGRP, IS-IS.
