Kerwin Hack’s Summary

I have had over 27 years in the Financial Services industry working mainly in the IT area. I became an IT director with my main focus on the delivering change and people development. I ran Service Management, Application Support, Risk, Audit, Resourcing, Reporting, Communications and other activities within IT, delivering significant change in each of those areas. In particular, I have managed relocations, integrations, offshoring, outsourcing, cost reduction, redundancy programmes and talent management programmes. My interest in helping other people has been further developed through my training as a Co-Active Coach, for which I am now certified. I have set up my own business, delivering coaching, training and interim management. I am also working as an associate for Fairplace, providing outplacement support and career management.

Kerwin Hack’s Specialties:

Outplacement, office closure, relocation, offshoring, outsourcing, union negotiation, management development, talent management, redundancies and programme management.
