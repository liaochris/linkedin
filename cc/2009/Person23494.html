Dave Hein’s Summary

Based on his extended success in building and managing technology and service businesses throughout his career, Dave Hein has founded Hein Capital to provide his experience, drive and motivation to the executive leaders of today's leading early-stage companies.

With a specific focus on clear definition of the business activities that generate revenue and build early companies into long-term successful enterprises, Mr. Hein helps these early-stage entrepreneurs to rapidly build technology, while defining and placing top-level executives to drive early adoption of the services and grow revenue at an accelerated pace.

Building businesses activities that attract the right investors is key to the continued success of small business today. Hein Capital helps to put the best companies on the fast-track to profitability and growth through investment capital.

Dave Hein’s Specialties:

A leader focused on execution, Mr. Hein has developed and guided organizations of all sizes in the areas of Business Development, branding, identity creation, mergers and acquisition, contract negotiation, sales and business leadership coaching and funding. Success throughout his career is defined by the growth and expansion of top-level teams focused on the execution of aggressive and unique business propositions.
