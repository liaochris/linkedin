Kathleen McGuire, D.M.A.’s Summary

Conductor specializing in larger forces, including orchestras, 200+ voice choirs, and opera. Also a published arranger and composer. Conducting experience includes almost 30 years, working in Australia, UK, and USA with professional, community, high school, and college organizations. Career highlights include conducting at Carnegie Hall, Kennedy Center, Sydney Opera House, and Soldier Field.

Kathleen McGuire, D.M.A.’s Specialties:

Strong diplomat able to bring together large, disparate groups of people who possess divergent views. Community leader and social justice advocate with a record of longevity. Additional skills include: producer (CD, DVD); public speaker (live audiences; radio and television); writer (media releases; monthly columns; grant applications); instrumentalist (bass & classical guitar; trumpet; clarinet; percussion); vocalist (soprano & alto)
