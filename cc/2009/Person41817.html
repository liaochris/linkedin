Kathy Stanavitch’s Summary

"All I can strive to be is the absolute best possible version of myself."
I can help you with every aspect of buying or selling your home because I’m experienced, and because I am a professional, and most of all because I care.................

For the past 20 years I have strived to make the process of buying or selling Real Estate in New Jersey less stressful and complicated. There are many elements of a smooth transaction.

I dedicated the first years of my career to the First Time Homebuyer the mortgage interest rate was in the high teens and the market crashed. I received first hand experience from the sellers' who could not sell their homes due to market conditions. We had to find buyers', it took almost 10 years for that market to come, to the market we have experienced for at least 8 years, a Sellers' market. But, however the beginning of 2006, brought another turn, we are officially in a Buyers' market, as of the present time.

Agents with strong negotiating skills, were in demand, I furthered my career by continuing to receive designations that would make me stand out in such a fast paced, quick selling, as a result made it an extremely pressured market for buyers to make decision. I was with Prudential New Jersey Properties until 2006, I left to go further and become a RE/MAX BROKER ASSOCIATE.

I am still RE/MAX Journey,as we all know the economy and the "subprime" is still with us. I am still a Buyers' Agent, and will be there to see this through................It will turn around I promise..........I will be here for you.

My new goal is to continue to list and sell real estate in New Jersey and to take advantage of my Instructor's License and teach Real Estate someday soon there will be another turn and we will be up-to it................

Kathy Stanavitch’s Specialties:
