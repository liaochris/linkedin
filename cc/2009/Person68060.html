Michelle Menna’s Summary

A resident of Carthay Square, Michelle specializes in residential properties on the Westside, with emphasis on Beverlywood and adjacent, the Carthay neighborhoods, Pico-Robertson, Beverly Grove and Culver City. Michelle has over 12 years of marketing experience to guide the V.I.P. Group in marketing your home to potential buyers. An innovative thinker and experienced negotiator, Michelle will create the most effective marketing tools to promote and sell your home at the highest price possible!

A Friend at your Side whether you Sell or Buy, Michelle invests in her clients and builds relationships for the long-term.

Michelle is married to Doron, with whom she has three children and a beautiful Siberian cat. A native Southern Californian, Michelle grew up in the city of Upland, graduated high school, then attended U.C. San Diego, graduating with a B.A. in Communications/Visual Arts. Michelle prides herself on a thorough understanding of the Southern California marketplace. Michelle is a fluent Hebrew speaker.
