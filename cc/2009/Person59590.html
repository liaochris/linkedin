Lisa Sneddon’s Summary

Finding the best senior care options for families and seniors in the Chicagoland area. We offer this as a free service to the families/senior.

Help to identify the options that best fit their care needs, geographical area and budget. We then recommend, set up, and tour the best two options with them.

Our team of 5 has over 67 combined years of experience in senior living and senior care.
