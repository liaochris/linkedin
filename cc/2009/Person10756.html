Bert Mahoney’s Summary

I show organizations how to increase their relevance in the lives of people using web-based communications such as websites, blogs, email newsletters, and web-based marketing techniques.

I have over 14 years experience developing web-based communications, 18 years experience as a graphic designer in publishing and corporate environments, and over 4 years as a business owner.

I blend art, science, and business.

I build online tools that allow you to communicate your message to people who want it and need it.

I blog, I design, and I think about things at http://www.berchman.com

I twitter at http://twitter.com/berchman

My background is in fine arts, information science, and the Internet. I mix my passion for emerging technology with my ability to build web-based communications that have purpose, defined goals, and are measurable.

I am always looking to make connections and facilitate them. I welcome new partnerships, new ideas, and love new challenges.
