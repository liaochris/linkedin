Sanjay Samani’s Summary

Developer of Differencia, a data comparison and diff application for Mac OS X. Differencia can be used to perform bank reconciliations, compare Excel files and diff files from different applications. It can perform text file comparisons even if the file and data formats do not match, making it unique amongst file diff tools. Simply put, Differencia can compare apples to oranges.

Independent Mac Software Vendor with strong business experience with business formation, eCommerce, marketing and product life-cycle. Developer in Cocoa, Cocoa Touch (iPhone), Objective-C, databases.

Project Manager in Investment Banking IT. Broad range of banking business knowledge including derivatives, risk management, trading, finance and back office. Broad, strong technical experience particularly (but not exclusively) with Sybase, Unix and Summit.
