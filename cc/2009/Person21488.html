Neill Kramer’s Summary

Neill has over 15 years of experience in developing new user experiences:
- Developed a unique interface for instant messaging and online shopping as founder of Bunnybot Inc.
- Developed the user interface for a financial trading system for Xigo, Inc. (formerly BrokerBot, Inc.).
- Developed experimental interfaces for desktop and web photo software for Pictra, Inc.
- Developed the UI for a "Powerpoint for kids" desktop application, Kid's Studio, winner of the 1994 Codie Award for Best School Productivity/Creativity Program.
- Also responsible for product definitions, marketing, (including lifecycle management), product requirements, feature tracking, delivery schedules, product collaterals, competitive analysis and tracking, product presentations, and product positioning.
- Managed small teams of software developers.

Neill Kramer’s Specialties:

Neill keeps the novice user in mind throughout all aspects of a product's design.
