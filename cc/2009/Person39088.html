  • Owner . Cheif Organiser

    Pro DJ Show

    (Non-Profit; 1-10 employees; Entertainment industry)

    March 2007 — March 2007 (1 month)

    The Pro DJ Show was held on March 25th 2007 at Lancaster University.
    This was a first for UK DJ shows in the fact it had split exhibition halls and separate seminar rooms for educational purposes.

    No other independant DJ show had done this before and once again has paved the way forward for other shows to copy this role.

    The Pro DJ Show was created to be a Bi-annual show so that the correct content can be found and arranged without the need to rush vendors into places to fill an annual show.

    The Pro DJ Show was also the very first show to be held anywhere in the world where 3 exclusive competing products came together under one roof, these were of course the Numark, Cortex and Denon Digital DJ Controllers. This was the very fist opportunity for show visitors to rate these products side-by-side.

    DJ shows organised under the HeadlineDJ banner have consistantly provided many "Firsts" within the industry and will continue to provide ground breaking events in the future.
