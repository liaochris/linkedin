Aishwarya Damodar’s Summary

Professional with more than a decade experience in delivering leading edge business solutions in areas of Information Technology spread over Media, Entertainment, & Technology industry and over 4 years experience in Human Resource.

My area of fore expertise is Project Management Operations, System Analysis, Resource Management, Strategy and Planning. My strengths are analytical reasoning & managerial skills with strong adaptability towards managing team dynamics and passion for meeting timelines.

Having had the exposure to multi-dimensional project planning and operations at Nimbus Communications Ltd. & STAR India Pvt. Ltd., I understand the demands and needs of setting up a New Media venture along with the brand expectations from the internet community. My assignments of covering online the Cricket World Cup 2007 and other International Matches gave me the wide exposure to LIVE streaming, Video on Demand, CDN, Ad Serving, DRM & CRM implementation and operations. The added learning of covering online various reality shows like KBC–Season 3, Star Voice of India-Season 1 and managing several dotcoms & WAP site has helped me understand the true potential of New Media.

I am drawn to projects that are stimulating, challenging and require logical analysis or abstract thinking. I can add value from a start-up venture stage till the project execution at all levels.
