Henrik Ahlen’s Summary

My specialities:
- Business development in interactive media services and online video with a focus on revenue models.
- Management of development projects and international research.
- Creating cost-efficient video concepts.
- Creating documented, structured and effective workgroup methods using inexpensive online tools.

Seeking consultancy assignments in projects involving digital media and online video. Project management, business development, video production and writing scripts, reports and articles.

International assignments welcome, I have a a large international business network and extensive experience of international production and cooperation.

My roots are in film and television. I have worked as a cameraman and editor for national television news/documentaries and feature films, as well as a producer of corporate films. From that I became interested in interactive multimedia production. I founded Ahead MultiMedia AB i Stockholm, Sweden in 1988. It became Sweden's leading professional multimedia production house, growing to 40 employees by 1995 when it was acquired by Bonnier Publishing Group.
I had a great time in Los Angeles, California in 1999-2000 when I was vice-president for Internet development at a start-up and responsible for an internet-based training portal.
After that I worked with e-learning research as Studio Manager at the Interactive Institute in Stockholm 2000-2001.

Henrik Ahlen’s Specialties:

I am an experienced business developer and project manager, media producer and key account manager. Bootstrapping specialist; finding cost-efficient solutions for start-ups.
Broad experience in interactive media production, telecom solutions, business development and international networking. I am always updated on interactive media business trends and the latest technology.

My blog on digital media: http://alfabravo.com (in English)
