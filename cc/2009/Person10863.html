beth g sanders’s Summary

I’m a freelance graphic designer, write, editor and social media geek in west Tennessee. My husband and I have two girls, one in high school, one in college.

I particularly love working with churches and Christian ministries and organizations, many of whom do not have the resources to staff a full-services communications department.

My vision is to use my God-given passion and gift for writing, design, social media and communications to help them accomplish their mission and establish a strong presence in the community.

I'm involved in the Memphis Tech Community with Social Media Expedition, BarCamp Memphis and SocialCamp Memphis.
