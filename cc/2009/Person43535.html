Janna Rust’s Summary

All of her life, Janna Rust has been passionate about equipping and empowering others to achieve their fullest potential. People working with her find that her energy, experience and sense of humor make change not only achievable but also enjoyable.

Janna is founder of Purposeful Partnerships LLC, where she is a professional leadership coach and corporate trainer, specializing in helping her clients develop a lifestyle of authentic leadership.

She holds the Certified Life Coach credential from the Institute of Life Coach Training, and is a Certified Behavior, Values and TriMetrix Analyst through Target Training International.

Janna has her MBA from Rockhurst University, her Bachelor of Science in Accounting from the University of Missouri, Columbia and is a former CPA and Corporate Controller with an 18 year in Corporate America, including some time in the financial services industry.
