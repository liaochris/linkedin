Lindsay Durango’s Summary

I was a page designer at The Roanoke Times for several years, with experience laying out news, sports and features broadsheet pages, and entertainment tab pages.

Focus on sharing information by way of very visual, easily consumable design.

Emphasis on applying new techniques and styles for presentation.

Now I'm carrying those skills into my position as Senior Design with Metro Pulse in Knoxville, TN.
