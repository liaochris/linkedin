Richard Nantel’s Summary

Richard Nantel is a senior level executive with Brandon Hall Research, a fast-growing American research firm. Having joined the company in 2000 as an analyst, he has quickly climbed the ranks; attaining his current position of CEO in 2007.

Richard Nantel’s background as a technologist has provided him with the ability to solve business problems through the smart use of technology using streamlined, well-defined methodologies. He has extensive experience working with virtual teams; leveraging collaborative technologies that allow skilled people to work together regardless of where in the world they are located.

Richard Nantel specializes in getting things done. He’s unrepentantly action-oriented. The world is filled with people who say "we should do..." and stand back hoping others will bring the idea to fruition. Richard Nantel believes the execution of good strategy is the key to success. For any problem, there’s a solution. Once we discover that solution, we have a duty to ensure the solution is implemented.

Prior to attaining his current position of CEO of Brandon Hall Research, Richard Nantel has for more than 20 years managed, designed and developed large-scale information technology projects. He has extensive skills in strategy, operations, management, marketing, Web- and client/server-based application development, social media, programming/authoring, graphic interface design, and development of procedural and quality assurance methodologies. He has strong organizational skills and has helped insure the successful completion of many large projects for important clients including the Canadian Department of National Defence, SUN Microsystems, Cirque du Soleil and Pfizer, among others.
