Beth Serata Miller, ABC’s Summary

I grow orchids and businesses through attentive care, creative strategies and delivery (I sing show tunes to my orchids and bake cookies for my grandkids), and pit-bull determination that gets the job done.

For over 30 years - with a gray hair for every project, I've guided clients into creating business clarity by targeting the right audiences with the right messages.

My approach to integrated marketing communications (IMC) is the same as my approach the care and feeding of my bromeliads, plumerias and orchids. So I've found a way to mix my passion for plants with my love of communications, and have become the Communication Gardener.

Applying the right light, soil, temperature, fertilizers, and watching out for pests keeps plants thriving and blooming. Similarly, integrated marketing communications requires a clear plan for brand messages, knowing the right audience, and the best ways to reach the masses.

I have the green thumb...and it works for plants and for business to business (B2B) communication strategy. Check it out - it really is green, and it brings in the green.
