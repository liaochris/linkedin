Faith Rivera’s Summary

My goal is to support the transformation of others through groovy conscious music and community building workshops/presentations!

I seek to connect with organizations, companies, individuals that can use my music to empower thousands, if not millions, to live the most fulfilling & successful life ever through different mediums:
**Custom-made songs or themed compilation CDs to support product launch
**Custom music for website, film, multi-media presentations
**Collaboration & creation of products/services using music to uplift wide audiences

I am also open to having sponsorship for my band & I & our multi-media show in traveling to bring this message of hope & inspiration, connection & community to others around the nation & the globe. Think of us as your own positive mobile rockin' "billboard" to not only promote you, but to promote the idea of us all shining our brightest light in service to all!

I travel internationally sharing my inspirational music at conferences, concerts, church services & community gatherings. I support others through empowering presentations as a guest speaker as well as workshops on singing & songwriting. Communities like Unity of St. Petersburg to the St. Croix Landmarks Society, have used my workshops as a way to reach out to the greater community. In my workshops, we build groups made up of diverse folks from their community, then showcase them later that weekend as part of my concert! It has been and powerful way to unite people & build community through the power of music.
