Sandy Zohari’s Summary

I graduated from playing with computers to playing with databases and soon developed a passion for the intricacies of database design and functionality. I have over twenty five years of experience creating, designing, and implementing custom database applications, plus a proven ability to translate customer needs into highly effective business solutions.

For most of my career, I worked in the business departments of either Fortune-500 companies or non-profit organizations, where I was able to utilize my analytical and technical skills to make the greatest impact on staff performance and production. My current clients are diverse group of small to medium size businesses and non-profit organizations. I am passionate about learning about new business needs and how I can increase productivity with a custom designed database application.

Another passion of mine is designing natural gemstone jewelry. In 2009, Sage-Thyme Jewelry and Design became a reality. Eventually I plan to begin selling online in addition to vending. My jewelry can also be found in several shops throughout the Finger Lakes region.
