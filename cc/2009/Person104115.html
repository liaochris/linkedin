I have 13 years experience in online marketing, web development and ecommerce. I'm interested in making contacts in ecommerce, web development, technology, online advertising, direct marketing, and business in general.

In 2007 I joined ecommerce development and marketing firm Timberline Interactive to manage client services and perform search-engine optimization, cost-per-click advertising, Google Analytics, A/B testing, email marketing, online PR, etc.

Clients include Lake Champlain Chocolates, Vermont Bicycle Touring, Garrett Wade, Gorton's Fresh Seafood, Dakin Farms, Dinn Trophy, Liberty Orchards, King Arthur Flour, Danforth Pewter, and American Meadows.

For six years prior to Timberline, I managed the websites of the Vermont Teddy Bear Company, PajamaGram and Calyx Flowers. The sites transacted over $42 million a year, and during peak holidays, 2,000 orders per hour.

I've worked with Yahoo! Store, Demandware, ePages, InterShop, TI Commerce, CommerceV3 and BroadVision ecommerce platforms. I enjoy comparing notes with other merchants using these and other platforms. We're also now working with the Ning social-network platform.

Before moving to the web in 1995, I worked in book and magazine publishing.

Tom Funk’s Specialties:
