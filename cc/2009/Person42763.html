James Bazen’s Summary

- Engineering/Producing: Engineer, produce and master artists and projects of all types including two 2008 Wammie (Washington Area Music Association) nominated CD's.

- Music Composition/Arranging: Composed music for PBS documentary “The Government Girls of World War II”. Published original arrangements for big band and combo. Arrange all types of music including jazz, classical and pop for bands, classical ensembles and small groups.

- Music Performance: Leader, woodwinds player, composer and arranger for various bands and ensembles including The James Bazen Big Band. Played woodwinds on many CD projects including five as a leader. Because I read music and improvise well I also do jazz and classical session work for Omega Studios in Rockville, MD. Other ensembles include The Greensboro Symphony Orchestra, Chicago Symphonic Wind Ensemble and various pit orchestras.

- Music Education: Clinician at Georgetown University; taught private sax/flute/clarinet lessons.
