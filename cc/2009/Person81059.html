Patrick van der Spank’s Summary

When I come in, ICT organizations WILL deliver services according to their customers or organizations requirements.

My secret recipe is simple: Listen carefully to all involved parties, make sure everybody understands the situation viewed from all sides and be creative in finding the optimal solution.

Building bridges in complex environments is what I do best, I do that by first creating clarity on all levels, developing vision together, obtaining support and commitment on all levels and then it's time for real action: don't try but just DO it.

Policies, rules and procedures are necessary, just always remember why you introduced them and constantly challenge / improve them.
