David Grajal’s Experience

  • Service Manager

    MACH

    (Privately Held; Telecommunications industry)

    February 2009 — Present (6 months)

    We are in charge of MACH SMS Service, i.e. the provisioning of the SMS HUB Service which is based on the initiative of the GSMA, namely the OC Project. We have brought together MACH excellence in clearing, and expertise of the sister company End2End in SMS business to bring forward MACH SMS, which offers wide coverage, connectivity, complete reporting (hub and bilateral traffic), data and financial clearing all in one service.
    For example, the MACH SMS Hub Solution, as central part of the MACH SMS Solution, will enable network operators to send and receive SMS to/from multiple global destinations, rapidly establishing inter-working relationships by using just one network configuration, one testing process, one contract and just one financial relation.
