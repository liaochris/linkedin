Emeric VIGIER’s Summary

Working as a Software engineer at ST-Ericsson in Le Mans, France, I handle performance issues on a dual-OS mobile platform running Linux kernel. It goes through test plans, shell scripts, test execution, result presentation and interpretation. I upgrade and support a monitoring tool giving CPU usage in real-time.

I here managed a 18 member team, conducted weekly meetings, improve my communication skills through press conferences and partnership negotiations, and handled an annual budget of 12'000€.

My long-term goal is to evolve in an international environment as a project leader.
