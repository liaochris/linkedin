Ryan Stoner’s Summary

Strategist working with large and start-up companies to uncover and develop opportunities that can help businesses better engage with consumers. Specialties include early-stage strategic development and creating marketing plans for brands in entertainment and new media platforms, consisting of identifying and developing brand positioning and growth opportunities, messaging, online advertising and social media strategies. My passion is applying my extensive experience across multiple categories and customer segments to solve highly complex business and communications problems. Interested in early-stage startups that will support long-term business growth.

Ryan Stoner’s Experience

  • Director of Strategy

    Omelet

    (Privately Held; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    December 2006 — Present (2 years 8 months)

    Omelet's guiding philosophy is that all business problems deserve and are best served by solutions derived from solid strategy and are borne out through the most appropriate media of expression. On a more practical level, that involves looking at the business problem, whether it is a business idea, brand, artist, movie or product, and working collaboratively to challenge conventions and create the best solution to solve the problem.

    Omelet and its staff have extensive experience in helping both large and start-up companies define clear market positioning, forward thinking strategic vision, and creative execution of that vision. We are always looking to partner or work with clients who genuinely want to innovate, grow and pioneer new ways of thinking or generate new revenue opportunities.

    Extensive experience blending entertainment and strategic marketing to create unique marketing formats to interact with viewers in new ways.
