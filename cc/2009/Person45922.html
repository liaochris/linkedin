Jennie Kermode’s Summary

A skilled and incisive writer and editor with over ten years' experience, I am the person to contact when you need high quality work completed to tight deadlines. I have an extensive publication history and have worked on everything from novels to business courses. I can provide you with the kind of material that really gets readers coming back for more. And you don't need to take my word for it - I have a long list of satisfied clients willing to testify that I can get results.

I believe that a professional approach and a good education are crucial to success in this industry. That's why I have a Master's Degree in English (with honours) and I'm working my way towards a further degree in Sociology and Research Methods, expecting to graduate next year. I am a member of the National Union of Journalists and I am well connected within media circles, so I am able to draw on all the latest techniques designed to make my work - and yours - a success.

Based in a fully fitted home office, I have access to both Windows and Linux based computers, a scanner, printer and graphic input software. I regularly work in partnership with a local photographer and can supply high quality images to your specifications, along with my work, for an additional fee.
