Brian Johns’s Summary

Experienced Ohio workers' compensation hearing officer (administrative law judge) with a broad and transferable skill set. High premium in delivering quality work product while meeting deadlines. Extensive experience in dealing with injured workers and their employers in highly contested workers' compensation hearings. Excellent analytical and writing skill set transferable to non legal occupations.

Voracious reader of magazines, newspapers, and books with a premium on awareness of current world, national, and local events and issues.
