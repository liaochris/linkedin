Calvin Jones’s Summary

I'm a freelance writer, journalist, blogger, online marketer and author with a background in marine biology, IT and internet systems. I'm passionate about helping people to connect with each other more effectively in print, on the web and through social media.

I'm the co-author of "Understanding Digital Marketing", a book that demystifies digital marketing and makes it accessible to a mainstream business and marketing audience.

To keep things varied I also write regularly for regional, national and international publications on subjects ranging from wildlife and the environment, through parenting to technology, the Internet, social media and digital marketing.

My ultimate goal is to write fiction for a living... which will take a while, but I'm working on it!
