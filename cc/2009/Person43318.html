Janae Jaynes-Learned’s Summary

Brokers for Charity is a socially responsible real estate company donating to 501(c)(3) nonprofit organizations through quality commercial and residential real estate referrals nationwide.

Before you buy or sell real estate use Brokers for Charity to receive quality real estate referrals and support your cause by designating where our donation is given.

If you are a nonprofit organization, let your supporters know that if they use Brokers for Charity you can receive needed donations.

We are always open to adding quality real estate professionals to take care of our socially responsible referrals.
