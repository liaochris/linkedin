Chin-Hock (C.H.) Low’s Summary

I have been involved in at least 7 startup/early stage companies since 1983. I have launched or was involved in the launching of over 60 major software products/projects/business lines. My main experience is web-based technology oriented products/companies.

I founded Orbius in 2007 to provide an affordable, social website or online community platform for small-medium busineses and organizations(non-profits, PTAs, alumni, church, golf clubs, etc) to interact and communicate their members more efficiently online. A Website on Orbius is easy to set up in days, and maintained without IT help. Broad suite of powerful interactive features can be configured and changed at any time.
