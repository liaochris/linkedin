Josh Tynjala’s Summary

With over five years experience developing products targeting Flash Player, Josh Tynjala offers a wealth of knowledge gained from working with many technologies in Adobe's Flash Platform. His experience includes the development of customer-facing rich Internet applications, integration of Flash Platform technologies with native OS applications, the development of two separate charting/graphing frameworks, along with the design and development of many other reusable UI controls and data visualization components for Flash and Flex.

Currently, Josh provides consulting services targeting both Flex and general Flash development needs. Additionally, he recently founded Bowler Hat Games, an indie game company.

Josh Tynjala’s Specialties:

Rich Internet Applications (RIAs), Flash, Flex, ActionScript 3, Adobe technologies, custom components, data visualization and charting, HTML/JS/CSS, web standards, open source software
