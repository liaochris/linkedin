Roger Linder’s Summary

I've been a professional computer programmer and datacomm specialist for over 30 years, working primarily in state government.

However, I also have done extensive outside work in web development and applications for a number of non-profit organizations and personal interests. Most notably, I am the creator and webmaster for The Todd Rundgren Connection, fan web site for the musician Todd Rundgren, which enjoys a large worldwide audience.

In addition, I am a volunteer musician and have an active interest in composition and performance.
