Matt Mansfield’s Summary

An accomplished news editor and leader, with experience in strategic planning and change management, I'm an associate professor for Northwestern University and serve as the co-director of Medill's Washington graduate program.

Major strengths include visual journalism and multimedia skill, as well as business development, new knowledge creation, and project management. I have expertise in leading through times of great industry upheaval and managing significant budgets, having had direct oversight of a multi-million dollar news operation at the San Jose Mercury News.

I'm also a partner in the design and specialty training firm MG Redesign, with skills in newspapers, magazines and corporate communications.

And I am the president of the international Society for News Design: Our mission is to enhance communication around the world through excellence in visual journalism.

Matt Mansfield’s Specialties:

A well-known expert, instructor and mentor on news management, cultural communication, conversational + social media, design and graphics use, and photojournalism.
