Adam Streets’s Summary

Adam Streets is the Web Marketing Manager for TEKsystems, the leading IT staffing and services company. TEKsystems' capabilities span a wide range of services from: technical staff augmentation, direct placement services, to full management of IT projects and comprehensive workforce management solutions. With over 25 years of experience, TEKsystems delivers the best IT talent, is an experienced IT outsourcing partner, and provides careers in the IT industry.

With over 90 office locations throughout North America and Europe, TEKsystems delivers IT capabilities, providing customers with a range of flexible engagement models.

Adam began his career at Allegis Group, Inc in July of 2007 as a Web Project Manager and transitioned full-time to TEKsystems in October 2009.

As the Web Marketing Manager, Adam is integrated into TEKsystems' marketing team, where he works with various service groups, subject matter experts and upper management to create user and SEO compliant content, design upgrades, website re-designs, branding campaigns, online advertising initiatives and social media strategies. He is also responsible for creating online marketing plans that tie into corporate initiatives across all of TEKsystems' verticals.

The web entities currently under TEKsystems include:
• TEKsystems;
• TEKsystems Canada, Germany, Netherlands, England and Ireland;
• TEKsystems Education Services;
• Asset Management Resources;
• TEKsystems Careers; and,
• TEKsystems Virtual Support Community.

In addition to working at TEKsystems, Adam is also the CFO for his own company, the Maren Consulting Group, LLC. The Maren Consulting Group is a provider of professional online marketing services. Whose goal is to help your business reach its fullest potential by complementing your offline marketing efforts with a full suite of online marketing services.
