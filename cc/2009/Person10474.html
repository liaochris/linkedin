Ben Obank’s Summary

GX is the first German, Spanish and Chinese language magazine with an editorial focus on betting, casinos, poker, strategies, sports and lifestyle.
GX has been developed especially for men between 18 and 40
years old who want more than just a gamble. For the
first time experienced gamblers, beginners and anyone
with an interest in gambling has a magazine to allow
them to get more from their favourite pastimes. GX is
designed to entertain and inform with news and features
on the latest gambling trends, tips and tricks.

There is now a large audience of young Germans and Spanish with an interest in gambling and only one magazine to provide them with information on this rapidly growing
new leisure activity. The magazine has a commitment to be a platform for delivering the latest information on the newest products and services in the gambling world as well as being a forum for enthusiasts.
