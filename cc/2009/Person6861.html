Anshuman Tiwari’s Summary

I am an experienced Change and Quality Management professional with a track record in managing large Change and Business Excellence programs. I currently head the Business Excellence program at Infosys Technologies Limited (Infosys). The program has CEO and Board level sponsorship. As part of this massive change management program my team has identified and driven org-wide beneficial change in management practices and business results.

Prior to Infosys, I was Principal Consultant and Business Leader with Qimpro Consultants, Mumbai. At Qimpro I provided quality and change consulting services (over 700 mandays) to a variety of companies – some of them winning national and international awards. I also co-developed the International Quality Maturity Model (IQMM). As an experienced trainer I have trained over 1000 industry professional in quality concepts including Business Excellence and Strategic Six Sigma.

I am a certified Manager of Quality/OE (ASQ), Certified Quality Engineer, and Six Sigma Black Belt. I have also been a trainer for examiners of the IMC Ramakrishna Bajaj National Quality Award and served as a lead examiner and steering committee member of the award.

I have played a leadership role with the India Chapter for American Society for Quality (ASQ).

Anshuman Tiwari’s Specialties:

Working with all levels of management to make change and quality improvement a reality
- Conceptualizing and driving large beneficial change programs
- Developing and deploying Business Excellence criteria/models across large organizations
- Total Quality Management and Strategic Six Sigma.
