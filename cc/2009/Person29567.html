Ed Whiting’s Summary

Owner and Director of 8media - Internet Media Investment and Consultancy Company

CEO and Founder of Travelbeen - social search engine for travel.

Until recently I was the eCommerce director of UK based company Comtec, a leading technology supplier to the leisure travel sector. Holding a board position and responsible for developing and implementing Comtec’s e-commerce services. I am currently exploring several new opportunities in the eCommerce and travel technology arena.

I have worked in e-commerce and travel technology since 1995 and hold a proven track record of launching digital products and services, including Bargainholidays.com, Aloud.com, A2bTravel.com, Holiday.co.uk, Comtec’s ViewdataPlus, WebSell and http://www.travel.co.uk.

The WebSell product range is used by over 300 U.K. travel companies. Key customers have included Thomascook.com, Travelcare.co.uk, Expedia.co.uk, Opodo.co.uk, Lastminute.com, First Choice’s Holiday Hypermarket and The First Resort.

Previous positions include Product Development Manager at EMAP Online, Product Director and Managing Director at EMAP Digital Travel, E-commerce Director at Holiday Express and Product and Marketing Director at Comtec.
