Leah Humphries’s Summary

Leah E. Humphries-Kljunich
President •Apple Creative Group •Leah Elizabeth Jewelry •My Heart Ties

Apple Creative Group a graphic design/marketing and Print brokering firm. Humphries is the lead designer with over 17 years experience in the
business of marketing and design.

My Heart Ties is a web based company and was created as the result of my battle with Crohn’s Disease and subsequent ostomy surgery in 1992.
My Heart Ties is reaching ostomates here in the United States as well as abroad. MHT additionally receives referrals from prominent hospitals like the Cleveland Clinic as well as Hollister
My Heart Ties hold a patent.

Leah Elizabeth Jewelry is hand- made, semi-precious gemstone jewelry line created Leah was invited to participate in an exclusive jewelry trunk show At the famed Bloomingdales in Manhattan in January of 07 and found he line to be highly successful. She currently sells on her web site at leahleizabethjewelry.com as well as J.Rake.com and local art galleries.Leah creates each piece of jewelry by hand in studio.

Humphries earned a BA from Mercyhurst College, Erie Pa in Communication Art in 1990
