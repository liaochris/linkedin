David Drucker’s Summary

Technology Visionary, with nearly 20 years experience creating user interfaces for business, medical and scientific applications, general public, and financial organizations. Have consistently created best-of-industry designs for clients and employers. My goal is is to make beautiful things, that work for people with the minimum of training or documentation. If they aren't thinking about the software, web site or device, but instead, the task at hand, then I've succeeded.

I relocated to Vancouver, British Columbia from Boston, Massachusetts in 2005. After work in a startup, and then with IBM on a large software project, I've taken the position of VP of Creative Services with BusinessLogic, as head of their new creative brand: Escape Velocity, a division of BusinessLogic that creates innovative and large web sites for customers in North America and beyond.

David Drucker’s Specialties:

Multimedia, Business Software, User Interface Design, Software and Web Prototypes, Information Architecture, Information Design, Screen Design for software and Devices (Medical, Consumer, etc.). Management of teams creating complex user interactions that employ both server-side and client-side elements toward the end of a great user experience.
