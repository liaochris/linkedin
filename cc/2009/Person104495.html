Vic Desotelle’s Summary

Vic Desotelle is owner and senior facilitator with the firms DiscoveryFuel.com (previously inKNOWvate.com) and Colabs.us, which are onsite and online centers for collaborative innovation and group development. Vic's charter is to enable 'sustainable learning' within enterprise emphasizing the concept known as "WHOLE SYSTEM INNOVATION".

By combining principles for sustainable development with frameworks for collaborative learning, Vic is supporting the design of sustainable technologies, businesses, and communities that work for a 21st-century, globally-conscious society.

Vic's Silicon Valley background in Electrical Engineering Design and Business Technology Incubator Management provides a baseline for rational thinking and technical development, while his work with nonlinear, organic systems provides a deeper understanding of innovation within community and enterprise and its relationship to economic, ecological, and social needs - known as the 'triple bottom line'.

Vic emphasizes the need for evolved thinking and compassion within enterprise to understand how individuals and organizations can both serve and profit within an emerging global market. To this end, Vic has committed his future to shaping new frameworks that help to align organizations with greater planetary awareness and cultural values so that healthy, more whole communities can be created and sustained.
