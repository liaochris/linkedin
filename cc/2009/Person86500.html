Lee Roth’s Summary

A renowned celebrity red carpet photographer based in Hollywood, California, Lee Roth has worked many of Hollywood's most prestigious celebrity events including; the Academy Awards®, Primetime Emmy Awards, numerous movie premieres and charity events. Syndicated worldwide, Lee's photos appear in print and on television around the globe.

Primarily serving Southern California and the Las Vegas area, Lee and his Roth Stock Digital Media offer location shoots anywhere in the world. Have cameras, will travel!

Lee's work has appeared in People Magazine, Us Weekly, In Touch, Glamour, Cosmopolitan, Harper's Bazaar and many other fine publications. Past clients include the Hollywood Chamber of Commerce, The Magic Johnson Foundation, Hard Rock Hotel & Casino, actor/comedian Sinbad and HBO.

Lee Roth’s Specialties:

Photography for Live Events, Celebrity and Corporate Headshots, Talent Portfolios and Private Glamour Sessions
