Phil Gerbyshak’s Summary

I work with organizations to create a plan to share and maximize their authentic brand with the world, and social media is my tool of choice. It's really not about the tools; it's all about the conversations you participate in and the connections you create and cultivate.

I also work with people to unleash the greatness inside them, and am able to do that through connecting people to the ideas, people, and information they need to take it to the next level.

At the Make It Great! Institute, I do professional speaking, half day seminars, webinars, social media maximizing and identity/branding for organizations and their people looking to take their life and their brand to the next level.

Contact me today to learn how I can help YOU make it great!
