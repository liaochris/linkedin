Karen McGrane’s Summary

I am a senior partner at Bond Art + Science, a web consultancy, and formerly VP and National Lead for User Experience at Avenue A/Razorﬁsh. I work to evangelize the user-centered design process by providing information architecture consulting services to clients, conducting user research and usability tests, and mentoring a talented and diverse staff of user experience designers. I'm a specialist in working with publishing clients to help extend their brands and generate advertising revenue online, Clients have included The New York Times, Fast Company, The Atlantic, Encyclopedia Britannica, The Disney Internet Group, and Condé Nast.
