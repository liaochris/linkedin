Robert Morsch’s Summary

Robert Morsch has a broad international experience in different Senior Management and Board positions within the B2B and B2C market sectors. Robert is experienced in negotiations, post merger activities, the start-up of new companies, restructuring and change programs (Business- and Technology-Transformation and -Alignment) and the development and launch of new products and services. He is result driven, very pragmatic and analytic and has the power to solve problems. He is very communicative and able to work at Board and Top-management level, also within complex and political environments. He is a team player with clear leadership capabilities. He is skilled to coach members of the Board, managers, employees and teams. He knows the CEO and CIO-agenda.
As a graduated industrial engineer (1988), Robert has worked since 1983 for 14 years in business with a focus on Global Marketing & Sales, Product Development, Transformation in Operations/Manufacturing and Professional Services within Buhrmann Tetterode (trading), ATAG Holding (manufacturing), Internatio Muller (trading & manufacturing) and VNU Exhibitions Europe. Robert has been with Capgemini Consulting since 1998, working for different market sectors: Life Sciences, Finance & Insurance, Telco & Media, Professional Services, Utilities, (Heavy) Manufacturing, etc. He has held lead consulting roles in large Business & Technology Transformation programs with e.g. Casema (cable company), Orange (mobile operator), Creyfs, Randstad (temp agencies), EWR, Essent (utilities), Nokia Global, ABN AMRO (banking), Achmea/Eureco (insurance), Organon, Schering Plough (both life sciences), BDO (accountancy) and BCD Travel (B2B travel industry).

Robert Morsch’s Specialties:

Business- & Information Strategy, Business- & Technology-Transformation and Alignment (IT-Transformation Consultancy, certified Level 2), Program and Engagement Management (EM, certified Level 2), IT-Governance, IT-Sourcing, Post-merger Integration, Change Management, Business Case & Benefit Tracking, Strategy formulation and execution, Marketing & Sales, Product Development and Operations Excellence.
