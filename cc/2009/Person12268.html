Bob Rhubart’s Summary

Recognized expertise in the use of Social Media in a business context, with extensive experience in the use of blogs, wikis, podcasts, and social networks in inbound marketing and online community management.

Proven skill at creating copy that is lively, sharp, and engaging -- writing that gets attention. Demonstrated ability to absorb complex technical information and translate it into easily understood copy for any audience in any medium.
