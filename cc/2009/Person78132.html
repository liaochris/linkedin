Caron - Jane Lyon’s Summary

Social Media - Second Life - Online Social Networks
Passionate about live performance and digital futures.

PCM’s target audiences are creatively minded businesses looking to embrace change for a sustainable digital future. Hearing about social media platforms hitting the main steam like Twitter and Facebook businesses are eager to explore the potential of incorporating a new media marketing strategy into their business. PCM provides the knowledge for a sustainable digital presence, confidence and understanding to articulate the features you desire to incorporate in a web presence and provides a team of experts to collaborate with your company to retain the personal essence of social media with the benefit of support and professional guidance.

A considered strategy with SMART aims, objectives, guidelines and targets is essential to achieving success with Social Media.

PCM creative works closely with creative practitioners, small businesses and organisations integrating, developing and optimising Web 2.0, Social Media, Social Networking and online community facilities linking them back in to a website to produce cost effective dynamic Internet solutions. I collaborate with each client to provide a package focused on ROI to turn web statistics and databases in to thriving online communities.
