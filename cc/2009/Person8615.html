Steve Lombardi’s Summary

Steve has worked as a lawyer for over twenty-seven years. Since 1981 he has tried civil cases mostly in the Iowa state court system but also in California, Montana and Minnesota.

Since 1996 he has been the owner of commercial real estate. With the addition of a commercial real estate company, Summit Real Estate Investments, they together manage 60,000 square feet of medical, retail and residential space. Summit Real Estate's goal to make a return on the investment in the same way Steve climbs mountains, they make money one step at a time.

Steve Lombardi’s Specialties:

As a law firm we specialize in the area of civil litigation, personal injury and business related. We don't take every case but only those with large damages and/or those with a statement to be made.
