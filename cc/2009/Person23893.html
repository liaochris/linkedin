David Carleton’s Summary

I provide these services to my clients:

+ Social Media Marketing
I show companies how they can use the power of social websites to build their brand, get more referrals and increase their sales by integrating social media marketing into their current marketing plan.

+ Strategic Planning And Meeting Facilitator
My role is to facilitate the actual strategic planning session and keep the discussions relevant and the team focused and on task.

+ Introducing New Products
From packaging to brochures and copywriting to advertising concepts and design, I have the experience and track record to make your new product introduction a success.

+ Selecting and Developing Niche Markets and Channels of Distribution
Whether you sell commercial, industrial, or consumer products and services I can help you find and develop the right channels & markets for your company.

+ Brand/Company Image
I help you clearly define what your brand image is now and then help you change it, promote it or enhance it.

+ Lead Generation
I teach guerrilla marketing techniques that will make your prospects stand up and ask you for information about "what" to buy, not "if" they should buy.

http://www.GetLeadsASAP.com

+ Advertising and Media Strategy
I teach you how to increase your qualified leads and advertising ROI regardless of the advertising media you use.

+ Internet Marketing & Website Strategy
I bring you up to speed on the latest and most effective on-line tactics to make the most of your website and internet marketing strategies.

+ Ongoing Communication - Follow Up Marketing
I can show you how you can make your customers and clients happier and you more money.

+ Referral Marketing
Word-of-mouth is not Referral Marketing because it is not a system a cannot be controlled. I teach you how to implement a variety of systematic referral programs that continually generate a flood of referrals into your business.

http://www.GetReferralsASAP.com
