Todd Zalk’s Summary

I am a "Make it Happen Now" kind of leader. My goal is to master "Results Through Relationships!" Everything important in a profitable business is done through relationships. Strong long-term relationships come from establishing trust. I believe I can build trust instantly and then it grows from there. (Covey talks about this concept in his book "Speed of Trust.") I like to ask questions and then listen to the unique needs of my customers. When people trust me they seek my expert advice with regard to their business and especially their financial and banking needs resulting in very satisfied customers. Again, it is all about "Results Through Relationships!"
