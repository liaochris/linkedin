david coxon’s Summary

One of the great things about working in IT in the North East, especially at a place like BALTIC, is that I get the opportunity to meet some great people and to work with so many fantastic organisations.

Over the past 2 years it has been my pleasure to work with Codeworks Connect, the Supermondaysand Bytenight teams, the IT directors forum, the Arts council, the AV festival and the GNAF digital subgroup.

I have also enjoyed the opportunity to write for the Codeworks Technotes Column in the journal, and for Bdaily.

david coxon’s Specialties:

When it comes to IT, I see myself as a good all rounder covering all aspects of ICT from networking to telecoms desktop and server support, but also working with point of sales, AV, video, web design/development and social media.

If I had to say what my specialisms were I would tend to say that that I favour strategic design and implementation, working with images and video files and networks.
