Amanda Cey’s Summary

Amanda brings ten years of experience working in both the corporate and private sectors in the areas of public relations, marketing and sales. Prior to forming ABCey Events in 2007, she headed the marketing and advertising division for Vessel, a luxury nightclub in downtown San Francisco’s Union Square, before becoming their private and corporate events manager.

Over the course of her career, she has managed private and corporate events for many leading Bay Area, Non-Profit and International companies including Reply.com, Epitomics, Michael Moore, Adobe, Charles Schwab, Admiral Insurance, Macy’s, Belkin, The Study Group, and Next-Aid.

Amanda holds a Bachelors Degree in Psychology from the University of California Santa Cruz.

For more information on private and corporate events, please contact Amanda at Amanda@abcey.com.
