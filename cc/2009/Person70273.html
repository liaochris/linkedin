    moxxe \’mak-se\ n. [fr. Moxie, a trademark for a soft drink] (1930) 1: ENERGY, PEP 2: COURAGE, DETERMINATION 3: KNOW-HOW, EXPERTISE

    Moxxe focuses on public relations, marketing, branding & special events for those who wish to target consumers with specific products or services geared to affect how time and money is spent. By understanding the mode of living of an individual or group – behaviors, habits, attitudes, tastes, moral standards & economic level – Moxxe can identify target demographics for each of its clients to create programs to bridge the gap between consumers and how they interact within their worlds. By finding the right mix of integrated strategies and the means of credibly communicating messages, Moxxe offers clients a sound knowledge of all their options; the savvy to make their message meaningful to those who count; and the focus to creatively take them from concept to results.
