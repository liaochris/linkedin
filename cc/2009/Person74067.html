Nick Tooley’s Summary

Idealpeople exists to align industry specialists with client expectations. We're recruitment experts in the Communications, e-Business, IT and New Media sectors in the UK and internationally - and we're committed to keeping our status as the most effective player in our market.

We're dedicated to superseding the outdated practices of traditional recruitment agencies and to exceeding the expectations of both our clients and candidates. We consistently achieve our goals. We provide a truly responsive, consultative approach that avoids the unethical 'hard sell' practices of other agencies, ensuring 'right first time' matches and an enjoyable, positive experience for both clients and candidates.

Idealpeople is committed to building and maintaining a long-term business that attracts and rewards only the most highly-motivated, ethical and professional consultants - people who will consistently deliver for you.
