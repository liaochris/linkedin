Nick Clement’s Summary

A creative multi-discipline Associate Creative Director specialising in art direction and graphic design for digital media, stupidly passionate about good design, typography and technology.

- Creative Art Direction
- Graphic Design
- Interactive/Interaction Design
- User Interface & Web Design
- Digital Advertising
- Branding
- Strategy
- Production

Over the years I've been privileged to have worked with international clients such as Sony Computer Entertainment, PlayStation, BSkyB, Diageo, Orange, Nokia, T-Mobile, Virgin, P&G, Eurostar, Adidas, Sony Music, Teletext, Sony Pictures, glue London, Anorak Digital and of late Razorfish, Profero, Fjord and BBC amongst many others.

Nick Clement’s Specialties:

Creative thought, Innovation, Digital, Graphic Design, Interactive Design, GUI/Interface Design, Rich Media Design, Web Design, Information Architecture, Iconography, Typographic Design, Illustration, Photography, Art Direction.
