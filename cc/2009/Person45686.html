Jeff Sheard’s Summary

At CyberCoach we work with our clients to understand their business goals, and then help to deliver better business results by up-skilling their team.

Specifically, CyberCoach delivers killer workshops focussed on Selling, Negotiation, Presenting Persuasively & handling the Media. We also consult in these fields, and incorporate Web 2.0 technologies such as Video, Web Conferencing, Social Networking, Virtual Communities & User Generated Content. This drives long term application of skills learned.

In addition, we have an awesome conference product called "Nature of Success" based on extreme big wave surfing - we've partnered with an ex-pro surfer and high performance sports coach on this one. This delivers a unique, tailored and high impact element to your next conference or off-site event.

Our solutions are a customized blend of both face-to-face and web-enabled learning & development programs & consulting assignments.

Our goal is to assist our clients to :
- maximize the productivity of their people.
- improve the persuasive impact of their people.
- drive competitive differentiation in the market via their people.
- grow new business & successfully target new markets.
- reduce costs by leveraging Web 2.0 technologies to better communicate and collaborate with, and influence, their stakeholders.
