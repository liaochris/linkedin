Rene Vreeswijk’s Summary

I'm a recruiter in agribusiness and food. I will be a sparring partner to young professionals and also professionals as well.

At Agrifoodcareer personal contact is of paramount importance. To put it briefly: we work quickly and efficiently. Within two days of your enquiry we will present you in concrete terms with a clear proposal of what we can offer you. This could be finding a suitable position or filling vacant positions. We are an intermediary for personnel, both starters and people with experience, with a professional and intellectual capacity ranging from Bachelor to Masters.
