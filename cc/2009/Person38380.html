Gwen Croft’s Summary

Gwen Croft, founder and president of Croft Communications, combines the unique blend of New York City sophistication with the personal southern touch to provide everything from a simple web site to establish your web presence, to a sophisticated web application to meet special needs.

With over 20 years experience, Croft Communications incorporated in Manhattan in 1999.

Services include:

* Web site design and development
* Web site evaluation and redesign
* Web site marketing and promotion
* Project consultation
* Creative arts and graphics design
* Domain name registration
* Search engine optimization
* Web site hosting
* Extra support to help your regular staff

Gwen Croft’s Specialties:
