Robert Morrow’s Summary

In my career, I have created a variety of projects including newspaper inserts, weekly print ads, dimensional direct mail pieces, magazines, trade shows and exhibits, TV and radio.

Many of my programs have been recognized by industry and advertising associations for their creative and/or business success. I am a dedicated professional whose accomplishments include:

• An advertising program for a new healthcare facility branded across multiple service lines which was recognized with two ADDYs from the Ad Club of the Triangle.

• A comprehensive ad campaign including television, print and radio for a major tractor manufacturer that was recognized with a "Best of National AgriMarketing Association" award for marketing communications

• An awareness campaign for a laboratory equipment manufacturer, that used a toucan as its mascot. The program was credited with increased sales, and earned a spot in the Creativity Annual
