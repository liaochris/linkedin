Christopher Dwight’s Summary

Global leader in rapidly expanding Master Data field.

I am currently the director of field strategy for Oracle Master Data Management (MDM). Based in Austin, Texas, I am responsible for driving the success of Oracle's master data management portfolio throughout North America. In this role, I engage with both partners and customers to develop strategies around information governance and master data management. With more than twelve years experience in the master data field, I have helped numerous Global 2000 companies worldwide overcome significant master data challenges to create business value.

I come to Oracle as a result of their acquisition of Hyperion Solutions. At Hyperion, I was based in Paris, France as the director of MDM Business Development for the Europe, Middle East and Africa region. Prior to Hyperion, I was a founding member of Razza Solutions, the Texas based software start-up that pioneered financial and analytical master data management technology. I played a critical role within the Razza organization to guide the company to software start-up success.

I seek to continue to derive business value from Oracle's Master Data Management portfolio and to play an active role in defining the evolving master data market-space.

Christopher Dwight’s Specialties:

Enterprise master data best practices, master data market space and various vendor offerings, deep understanding of business needs and processes impacted by master data quality, building customer understanding and vision for solution, successfully communicating the business value of improved master data management processes, guiding customers to successful master data management solutions.
