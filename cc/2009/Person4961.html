Anand Narayanaswamy’s Summary

Anand Narayanaswamy, a Microsoft Most Valuable Professional (MVP), works as chief technical editor for ASPAlliance.com based in Trivandrum, India. He is one of the members of the prestigious Community-Credit.com Hall of Fame club and is also the member of ASPAlliance Advisory Board. He has published several articles related to .NET for various websites and is the author of Community Server Quickly (http://www.packtpub.com/community-server/book) published by Packt Publishing. He runs http://www.learnxpress.com and http://www.dotnetalbum.com. Anand blogs at http://www.visualanand.net and can be reached at visualanand@gmail.com. He also twitter daily at http://twitter.com/anandnmvp.
