Lowell Smith’s Summary

I am founder and President of InspiraFS, Inc. (Inspira), a firm the provides recordkeeping services for Individual Retirement Accounts (IRAs). The Inspira white label solution enables financial services organizations such as payroll providers, 401(k) plan recordkeepers, and banks to eliminate a cost center while enhancing or establishing an IRA product resulting in additional profits. The IRA accounts are the client's, and the website, call center and statements are branded to its unique IRA program.

The goal of Inspira is to address the needs of financial organizations and the growing numbers of IRA customers as this type of retirement plan become the most predominant in the industy. Through this IRA-centric solution, we are able to specifically address the income replacement and estate planning concerns of individuals preparing for retirement.

Lowell Smith’s Specialties:

My expertise involves evaluating, researching and creating products and services that meet the needs of my clients and ultimately their clients.

Management consulting, retirement plan compliance, product development, technology utilization and business analysis are my major areas of person expertise.
