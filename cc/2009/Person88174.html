Sam Powell - Professional Realtor®’s Summary

Sam has been in the real estate industry since 2002 and has primarily focused on her clients within the LGBT community. Sam's main goal is to provide all of her clients with the services and information needed to be confident in their decision making when making purchase or selling a property.

Sam is NOT your typical real estate agent. She is 100% mobile/wireless which allows her to be in touch with her clients at all times. Sam is known for her casual work style, her strong commitment to her clients and their needs, and her intense level of education/mentorship that she brings to her buyers, sellers, investors.

If you're not working with a professional Realtor, consider interviewing Sam!
