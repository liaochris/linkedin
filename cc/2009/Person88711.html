Sarah Hutton’s Summary

I am currently the Communications Manager at Camp Broadway, where I launched the Camp Broadway Alumni Association in order to reconnect with our 10,000 alumni. I am involved with all marketing, promotional, and public relations efforts and handle all social media for the company.

Previously, I interned a digital marketing agency DX Street, primarily working on Lifetime TV and the NY Liberty accounts, as well as in the social media department at Schneider Associates, a Boston based PR firm, where I assisted will all social media accounts. I also interned at Nets Basketball in the Event and Entertainment Marketing department. There I assisted with the planning and presentation of events and helped with dance team appearances. In addition to my internships I have done consulting work for a few agencies in Boston and NY including boutique agency, Refine & Focus.

Professionally I have worked in an office setting for many years, starting with insurance brokerage firms NIA Group, LLC and Corporate Synergies Group, Inc. where I assisted the sales force in preparation for appointments, helped produce spreadsheets for the underwriters, and performed clerical duties.

I am a recent cum laude graduate of Emerson College with a B.S. in Marketing Communications and a minor in Management and Leadership. At Emerson, I was a national member of the Public Relations Student Society of America (PRSSA) and served as President for the Emerson College chapter, as well as an AE for The Emerson Group, PRSSA's student run consulting firm. I was also a member of the Street Team at Emerson's radio station WERS.
