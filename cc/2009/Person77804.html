Paul Parkinson’s Summary

Pre-sales technical consultant and embedded software engineer with 18+ years experience, and in-depth knowledge of Aerospace & Defence industry sectors, with current UK Security Clearances.

Professional Interests include Information Security (InfoSec), Integrated Modular Avionics (IMA) and Intelligence Surveillance & Target Acquisition Reconnaissance (ISTAR) systems.

Author of numerous technical papers & published articles, see:
http://homepage.ntlworld.com/paul.j.parkinson/publications.html

Paul Parkinson’s Specialties:

* Secure operating systems (VxWorks MILS 2.0), Common Criteria
* Safety-critical systems (Def Stan 00-56, DO-178B, ARINC 653)
* Embedded and real-time software (VxWorks 5.x / 6.x / 653);
* Web design (HTML, XML, RSS);
* Device driver development (Solaris / UNIX, LynxOS RTOS)
* VMEbus
* PowerPC architectures
* Digital Signal Processors (TI C40, Analog Devices SHARC)
