Michael Greer’s Summary

[PLEASE NOTE: I have a more complete profile & info on Facebook at: http://profile.to/michael_greer ]

Project Management (PM) author and trainer whose mission is to help new project managers become more effective. Through books, workshops, and public speaking appearances, seeks to demystify the field of project management (PM) and make it accessible to newcomers. Spent many years as an instructional designer developing custom training and performance support solutions for all sorts of clients and content areas (sales, mgt, technical).
