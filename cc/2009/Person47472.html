Jimmy Dean’s Summary

Decades of experience in successful media development, deployment and management. Broadcast (radio/TV/cable), print (magazine) and new-media.

Community builder. Multimedia generalist. Publishing specialist.

Jimmy Dean’s Experience

  • Managing Partner and Publisher

    HARBOR STYLE Magazine and Sun Coast Magazine Group, LLC

    (Partnership; 1-10 employees; Publishing industry)

    January 2007 — Present (2 years 7 months)

    HARBOR STYLE Magazine is a new community publication for the Greater Charlotte Harbor area of Florida's Gulf coast. With offices in the historic riverfront village of Punta Gorda, the magazine celebrates the lives, heritage, and future of the people who call this magnificent part of paradise "home".

  • President

    Jimmy Dean, P.A. dba OMG (Original Media Guru Enterprises) (Sole Proprietorship)

    (Sole Proprietorship; 1-10 employees; Management Consulting industry)

    January 2006 — Present (3 years 7 months)

    Consulting services for magazines and general corporate communication structure. Multimedia disciplined. Voice and spokesperson talent for corporate and commercial production. On screen talent for "VOTING MATTERS", a 30 minute video-tutorial produced in Sarasota, used to train poll workers in every precinct in 6 states to be more sensitive to disabled voters. See www.votingmatters.com

  • Executive Publisher

    SARASOTA Magazine, CurtCo Publishing, LLC

    (Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Publishing industry)

    October 2004 — December 2005 (1 year 3 months)

    Managed the area's leading lifestyle/city magazine group, SARASOTA Magazine division, www.curtco.com

  • President & Group Publisher

    Gulfshore Media

    (Partnership; 51-200 employees; Publishing industry)

    April 1983 — October 2004 (21 years 7 months)

    Co-founded and oversaw daily operations for this multi-title magazine publishing firm, with offices in Sarasota, Naples, Orlando and Jacksonville. Group publisher for more than 20 different titles in the city/lifestyle genre. Sold company to CurtCo Media, www.curtco.com, publisher of Robb Report and Worth Magazines.

  • On-air Talent to Management

    various broadcast outlets in North Carolina

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Broadcast Media industry)

    June 1972 — March 1983 (10 years 10 months)

    Media career began as radio DJ in Burlington, NC at age 12, with Program Director positions at WTOB in Winston-Salem, WZOO in Asheboro and eventually 50,000 watt WPTF in Raleigh, NC. At age 29 moved from broadcasting to pursue career in publishing in Florida.
