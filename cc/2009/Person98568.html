Antoinette Bartsch-Newhouse’s Summary

Three Peas Co – urban chic apparel and accessories to compliment your lifestyle. What started as a quest to find chic hair bows for a daughter has quickly re-ignited the passion to create and style. Founder and lead designer Antoinette Augustina Newhouse recalls designing her outfits before the first day of school when she was a little girl (drawings are still trying to be found).

The hair bow quest started in January 2008 and with the help of a friend, the first bows were created. The friend has since left and the official launch of the www.threepeasco.com was in June 2008. A few local retailers quickly picked up the line in their stores and the website was born.

Three Peas Co. finished 2008 by introducing the line at different events throughout Southern California to rave reviews and excited customers who quickly become loyal followers. 2009 has started off with an explosion. Requests are pouring in from mom blogs, fashion sites and boutiques.
