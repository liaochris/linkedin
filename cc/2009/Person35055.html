Garrett Droege’s Summary

I provide insurance solutions for businesses and individuals. . I am particularly focused on risk management for growth oriented companies of all sizes. Provide all lines of insurance, including: Property, Casualty, Professional (E&O, D&O, EPLI, etc.), Workers Compensation, Group Benefits, HR/Payroll Management, Bonds, etc. My territory includes the entire southeast, with an area focus on Charlotte and Lake Norman.

Garrett is a graduate of The Hartford School of Insurance and The University of North Carolina at Wilmington. He has attained the designations of Construction Risk & Insurance Specialist (CRIS) from the International Risk Management Institute and Commercial Lines Insurance Specialist (CLCS) from The National Underwriter.

He is currently working towards becoming a Certified Insurance Counselor (CIC), a Certified Risk Manager (CRM), and Registered Professional Liability Underwriter (RPLU).

Garrett uses a unique process called The Ideal Client Transformation to help his clients become more attractive to the insurance marketplace and improve their bottom line.

Garrett Droege’s Specialties:

Technology and Cyber-Risk, Life Sciences, Medical Malpractice, Religious Institutions, Construction Industry, Home/Condo Owners Associations are primary focuses. However, I have markets available for all small commercial and middle market accounts.

Personal Lines expertise: high-end homeowners, luxury auto, personal umbrella, yachts/boats, etc.
