Andy Murray’s Summary

About me:

I am a director of Outperform, a registered consultant (PPM, PRINCE2) and was the lead author for PRINCE2 2009. I have helped implement PRINCE2 in numerous organisation across 15 countries (Europe, Asia, North America, Africa, Middle East).

Of particular interest for me is the area of project/programme assurance. I've helped organisations implement audit/assurance schemes and update their project management processes to comply with governance legislation (e.g. Sarbanes-Oxley). In 2004 I started the Chartered Director programme with the IOD as a means to link good practice project/programme governance with corporate governance requirements. I'm pleased to report that in October 2006 I gained Chartered Director status.

I have been using maturity models as a consulting aid for some 8+ years and find them an invaluable tool to help diagnose the root cause of problems to help formulate cost-effective improvement solutions.

In 2007 I was appointed by OGC to be the Lead Author for the PRINCE2 refresh. The revised method (PRINCE2 2009) will be launched on 16th June 2009.

I manage the PRINCE2 group on LinkedIn. http://www.linkedin.com/groupRegistration?gid=40653

About Outperform:

Outperform is an Accredited Consulting Organisation (ACO) assessed in accordance with BS EN 45011 and licensed to consult in the use of OGC best practice products: PRINCE2 (projects), MSP (programmes), M_o_R (risk), P3O (project, programme and portfolio offices), P2MM/P3M3 (maturity models). Outperform is a corporate member of the Association for Project Management, a founding member of the Middle East PPM community of practice and is ISO 9001 certified.

Outperform has an ongoing commitment to the development of ‘best practice’ as evident in providing authors for various best practice publications, white-papers and vodcasts.

More information including white-papers, newsletters and vodcasts can be found on the Outperform website www.outperform.co.uk
