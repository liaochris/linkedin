Sherri Fowler’s Summary

Contact me to receive our biweekly newsletter, which offers marketing tips and technology information. Blog - MyFaithfulAssistant.wordpress.com; Email - Sherri@MyFaithfulAssistant.com; Direct - 740.412.2311.

My Faithful Assistant, founded by Sherri Fowler, is a Virtual Assistant company. My Faithful Assistant (MFA) provides administrative and marketing services to clients nationwide. It doesn’t matter where clients are located because MFA can provide services to clients via phone, email, fax and online…virtually.

There are many reasons why hiring MFA can be a sound business decision. One reason is because MFA is an independent contractor, not an employee, and you can hire MFA on an as-needed basis. Therefore, you won’t have the expense that goes along with hiring a full-time employee. With MFA, you are only charged for the materials and services you need, when you need them, or for as long as you need them.

Besides keeping your overhead low, delegating your non-revenue, mundane, time-consuming tasks to us will open up the opportunity for you to focus on the things that will generate revenue and grow your business. You should be in the leadership role of building your business, not the position of an office assistant.

If you’ve never used a Virtual Assistant (VA) before, we suggest delegating one project on your “to do” list to us. We realize in order for your business to grow, you are not going to be able to perform each task or required duty within your business. That’s why our completing one project for you will give us an opportunity to prove our dependability, service and cost-effectiveness to you, which then opens up the door for a trusting, long lasting relationship…one project at a time.

It doesn’t matter if you are a veteran business owner or brand new in business, you can benefit from using MFA. Whether you need help getting started, help maintaining, or help on a continual basis…we would love the opportunity to earn your business.
