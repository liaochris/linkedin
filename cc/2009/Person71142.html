Melody Ng’s Summary

Prior to joining IBM, I held various roles in software support, testing, and information development in different technology companies, including Telus and NCR.

I have been working in the Information Management brand of Software Group in IBM since 2003, holding positions in various teams such as DB2 Strategic Linux Technologies and Integration, DB2-SAP Integration Center, and DB2 Competitive Linux Technologies and Enablement.

Currently, I'm Manager of the Data Management Emerging Partnerships and Technologies team. Our team focuses on the enablement and integration of various IBM Data Management technologies with business partners, delivering value to the end customers and create more opportunities in the emerging markets for DB2, and other data management products.

My goal is to continue to develop professionally in the IT industry and to take on different challenges that will add to my life experience broaden my personal and professional networks.
