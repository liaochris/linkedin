Timeshare Coach’s Summary

I have been in Direct Sales since 1992. Dedicated to Sales and Sales Training. My belief is the more you learn the more you earn and in 2005 after taking the Napoleon Hill Science of Success course, I decided to create The Timeshare Coach and dedicate my work to share Sales and Motivational Tips to everyone involved in the Selling of Timeshare in a professional and ethical way.

What I share can be used in all forms of direct selling not just Timeshare.

The word BLAME does not exist. There is always a sale. They sell you or you sell them, someone is selling. With belief in your product and the right attitude you will succeed.....FACT!!!!

Author of the book The Timeshare Coach, Change your Thoughts, Sell more Timeshare.

My website www.thetimesharecoach.com provides free weekly sales tips and is a great resource for sales teams and a good tool for managers to use in sales meetings.

Certified Instructor Science of Success Course Napoleon Hill Foundation.

Inspirational and Motivational Sales Seminars

THE TIMESHARE COACH LIFE PURPOSE To Inspire and give Belief to Timeshare Sales Professionals to Sell More Timeshare, Love what they do and have a Fun, Successful and highly Profitable Timeshare Career... FACT!!!
