Norberto Meijome’s Summary

I have more than 10 years of real world experience working on high volume Internet products and services. I have managed the global systems and infrastructure for Kazaa, one of the largest online products at the time, in a highly litigious and rapidly evolving environment; headed the engineering team at AOL Australia, and managed technical product development of cutting edge network technology for Cisco's carrier grade network devices.

I have extensive international working experience and have worked with distributed development teams in Australia, USA, The Netherlands and Israel. I have managed the technical relationship with partners and service providers from all around the world, as well as consulted with industry bodies and government agencies.

I love and live the internet, and strive always to find the best solution that addresses the business' needs. I want to have new, interesting challenges to solve, as well as getting more involved in distributed systems and cloud computing.
