Kurt Reinke’s Summary

♦Kurt Reinke: Professional Trainer, Coach and Consultant
♦LOCATION: Kansas City
♦INVITE me to connect at kurt.reinke@engroupresources.com

Kurt Reinke works with professionals who are stalled, frustrated and not moving forward, both professionally and personally, to help you discover your strengths and talents, so that you can create opportunities and live up to your potential.

► Pacific Accord – Investment in Excellence® Training. Are you stagnant and find that something is blocking you from achieving your goal? This powerful program helps you discover what’s holding you back, while providing easy-to-implement tools to change your attitudes and beliefs, translating to greater results and higher performance.

► Salezworks – 9 Steps to Productive Prospecting Training. Are you a non-selling professional who cringes at the thought of having to “sell”? In this program you’ll learn how to build referral partnerships that impact sales and, in turn, your success within your company.

► EN Group Resources - Coaching. As you look at yourself, do you find it hard to discover and embrace your special skills and talents? You’re not alone. Many of us need others to help us see our gifts and capitalize on our potential. As a coach, Kurt works with you to engage the powerful force within you.

► EN Group Resources – Training Development. Is it your mission to share your knowledge to help others succeed? Do you find it easy to share your concepts through writing or speaking but wish they could be turned into programs that would make a real difference? Kurt Reinke has a unique talent to help you translate your knowledge into a training program that engages the participant to teach them how to turn concept into action.

Kurt Reinke formed EN Group Resources to facilitate the process of continuous improvement. EN Group Resources is an authentic, progressive, effective company, dedicated to helping you capture your potential and fulfill your dreams.
