Luke Walker’s Summary

After being in radio for 7 years I am now back in school working towards a law degree. With a wide variety of different legal careers out there I am excited to learn about all of them. Currently I find myself most interested in Labor Law, defending workers rights, International Law and learning about ways to be involved in the legislative process. Whenever I get a chance I still jump on the air and you can hear me now and then on WTMX-Chicago. Follow me on twitter.com/lukewalker for updates.
