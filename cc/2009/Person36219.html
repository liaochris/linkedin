Gianni Varrica’s Summary

Freelance music composer, MIDI programmer and sound designer for TV, videogames, web sites and other interactive media, with more than six years of experience.

Session musician, arranger, keyboard and piano player, digital piano and synthesizer demonstrator; music instructor (modern piano, theory, music technology) since 2001.

Given the nature of my work, I've been able to learn how to manage my working time depending on the projects assigned to me. Therefore, I guarantee maximum flexibility and reliability.

Gianni Varrica’s Specialties:

Competent with hardware and software professional electronic instruments such as synthesizers, workstations and samplers.
Proficient with music composition, sound design and notation software (Steinberg Cubase, Sonar, Reason, Finale, Max/MSP, CSound).
