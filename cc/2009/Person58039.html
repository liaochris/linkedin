LaVaughn Henry, Ph.D.’s Summary

Accomplished risk management executive with 15+ years of diverse experience in the fields of housing economics, federal regulation and consulting, and government affairs. Strong leader with an exceptional history of delivering results based on adaptive and creative insight, analytical rigor, and knowledge of the policymaking environment. Demonstrated strengths include:
• In-depth knowledge of federal policy, institutions, and conditions relating to the U.S. housing and mortgage markets • Excellent public speaker on issues related to the economy, housing, and mortgage markets • Significant experience in providing counsel to senior executives and federal officials

LaVaughn Henry, Ph.D.’s Specialties:
