Zinta Aistars (zintaaistars@yahoo.com)’s Summary

Zinta is Publications Editor at Spectrum Health in Grand Rapids. She analyzes developments in specific health care fields to determine need for revisions, corrections, and changes in previously published materials, and development of new material; determines specific or general subject material to be developed for publication; is responsible for patient education brochures and other Grand Rapids hospitals' publications; implements recommendations to improve operational efficiency and new services for areas of responsibility; and contributes to Spectrum Health Today Magazine, Focus on Nursing, SH News, annual reports, and various other electronic and print health care publications.

Zinta is a widely published freelance writer and editor. She is the author of three books, and her articles and poetry have appeared in countless publications. Zinta has served as a reviewer on the Arts Council of Greater Kalamazoo grants panel in 2008 and again in 2009.
