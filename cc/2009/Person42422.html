Jacob Shama’s Summary

Leader, entrepreneurial, with 20 years of experience in the fields of communications and information technology,
with 12 years of experience in executive-level management.
Holds DSc (Doctor of Sciences) in Electrical Engineering.

Prior to Mintigo was VP Products and VP Operations at modu mobile, the modular mobile handset company.
Held variety of managerial and leading research positions at the IDF (Israel Defense Forces), his most recent position was head of an elite R&D Technology Center.

Received his Doctorial D.Sc. degree from George Washington University and both his M.Sc. and B.Sc. with honors from Tel-Aviv University.

Jacob Shama’s Specialties:

Executive Management, Mobile Communications, Information Technology, Signal processing, Data analysis and processing, Algorithm research and Patterns recognition.
