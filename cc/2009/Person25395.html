Deborah Corn’s Summary

…WHAT I DO...
Develop, implement and manage agency Production and work flow processes. Train and supervise Traffic and Production departments. Primary contact for agency new business, creative presentations, launches, branding, estimates and studio requests. Coordinate, supervise and schedule daily in-house work flow for interactive, print, DM and collateral and digital assignments. Schedule studio and digital work. Hire Creative freelancers, Traffic, Production and support staff as necessary. Organize and develop agency creative schedules and manage workload of Creative Department and Studio. Provide sizing and spec information from SRDS, or vendor source to in-house personnel. Track projects from creative development through final proof. Maintain agency job jackets and presentation materials. Negotiate and estimate with vendors for print and collateral assignments including art buying and issue purchase orders. Procure and manage fulfillment of DM and Direct Response programs including contract negotiation, storage, postage or delivery method, inventory and list management. Reconcile vendor invoices and keep projects within approved budgets. Conceive and execute collateral print materials including on site approvals and press checks. Maintain and oversee continuity of production quality, color and content of ad campaigns and collateral materials. Coordinate and oversee color corrections and retouching with agency, vendor and photographers. Apply solid organization, communication and problem solving skills. Multi-task all projects in an effective and efficient manner.

Deborah Corn’s Specialties:

I have vast experience in all aspects of Production from concept to delivery including Direct Mail & Direct Response Programs, collateral, outdoor, POP, ads, event planning & management, trade shows, interactive/digital, agency self-promotion, catalogs & annual reports, as well as out of the box & guerrilla marketing projects. My understanding of graphics & design, photography & retouching, as well as color & paper, have been integral in many Advertising award honors throughout my career.
