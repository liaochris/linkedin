Chris Reinhard’s Summary

Chris Reinhard has been a copywriter and social media strategist for Motive: A Hybrid Agency since 2005. His experience in the experiential, online and social media marketing arenas places him in a unique group of young marketers capable of satisfying creative and executional needs for a variety of clients. Since joining Motive, Chris has helped coordinate interactive social media programs for Qdoba Mexican Grill, Native Eyewear and Good Times Burgers & Frozen Custard, and aided in the execution of nontraditional programs for Pepsi, Mountain Dew and Vehix.com. Chris has been awarded a Denver 50 Award (as part of the Motive team) for his work on Mountain Dew’s ‘Dew Report’ branded entertainment series, and a 2009 SxSW Viewer’s Choice nomination for online writing.

Before joining Motive, Chris acted as a sampling coordinator for PepsiCo, aiding in the orchestration of several programs surrounding both the pre-launch and launch efforts for Pepsi Blue and Pepsi Twist. In 2004 and 2005, Chris sat on the Board of Directors for Boulder Freeride, the nation’s largest collegiate club. Additionally, in 2001 Chris founded PunkDiscovery.com, an online music journal for which he acted as CEO, head articles editor, columnist and head webmaster until 2006. In 2007 and 2008, Chris participated on the advisory board for Zuzingo, a social media company based in Boulder, Colorado.

Chris has completed lead acting roles in three short films: 'Delivery' by Eli Berg, 'Incall' by Andrew Litvak, and 'Young Adults' by Cassandra Moore. His film experience continues to increase as a part of Motive's digital creative team.

Chris is a staff writer for OneRiot Blog, a finalist for the 2009 SxSW Viewers Choice Award.

In April 2009, Chris spoke at the nation's largest Ignite event in Boulder, Colorado.

Chris operates a personal blog, Reverie Machine, located at http://www.chrisreinhard.com.
