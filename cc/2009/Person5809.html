Andrew Rowe’s Summary

Serial entrepreneur with over 25 years of experience building and leading successful ventures in the technology, manufacturing, healthcare and business service sectors. Experience includes a VC-backed start-up through IPO, a leveraged buy-out and turnaround of a large industrial company, bootstrapping two professional services firms, investment and/or leadership roles in several start-ups.

My career includes 15+ years of international management experience, with overseas assignments in Europe, Latin America and Asia, and extensive travel to over 70 countries. I'm bilingual in Spanish, fluent in French and Portuguese, and speak some Italian.

Active in start-ups as a board member, and provide board-level leadership to community service organizations.

Andrew Rowe’s Specialties:

Corporate leadership, general management, sales & marketing management, channel development and global business management. Domain expertise includes technology, manufacturing, heavy industry, healthcare and business services.
