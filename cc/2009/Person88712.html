Sarah Perez’s Summary

I had worked in I.T. since 2001, initially as a network engineer, then as a Systems Administrator for La-Z-Boy of Tampa Bay. When the La-Z-Boy franchise was sold by the owners, I took a position as an I.T. Engineer for Florida Bank Group, the holding company for several community bank subsidiaries across Florida, including the Bank of St. Petersburg here in the Bay area.

In February 2008, I left the full-time position at the bank to become a professional blogger. I now blog for Microsoft's Channel 10 (on10.net) , Read Write Web (readwriteweb.com), as well as my own site (sarahintampa.com). I have also recently launched a small tech blog network, Grand Effect (grandeffect.com).
