Beverly Garland’s Summary

I am a sixteen-year veteran of the computer game industry, with credits on seven shipped games, and experience as a lead and manager on teams as large as 75 people. My recent positions have entailed art production management, associate producer tasks, and leading cross-functional teams under the Agile model of production as a Scrum Master.

Core values for my work:
1. To help myself and others think and operate in new and better ways so that we can accomplish great things while retaining a high quality of life
2. To incorporate fun, variety, inspiration and creativity in everything I do
3. To encourage cooperation ("power with") over command-and-control ("power over") as a better model for building a team

Beverly Garland’s Specialties:

Group facilitation, Agile/Scrum software development, Career coaching, Writing and editing, Research, Art direction, Photoshop, and Production management

Technical skills: Microsoft Excel, Microsoft Project, Adobe Photoshop CS, MoveableType Pro, Wordpress, Adobe Premiere, Adobe AfterEffects, HTML, Microsoft Outlook Professional, and Microsoft Power Point.
