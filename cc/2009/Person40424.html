  • Tutor, Travel Writing Course

    National Union of Journalists

    (Non-Profit; 11-50 employees; Online Media industry)

    January 2003 — Present (6 years 7 months)

    Course duration: One full day workshop

    Cost: £80 per person (lunch included) NUJ Member rate, non-member rate £160.00

    Who should attend:

    No formal qualifications, but journalists with writing experience.

    Content

    The course is more a nuts and bolts course rather than a 'How to Write Travel Articles' course. Hugh only takes on people who can write already as he teaches how to break into travel using skills journalists have already.

    The Course covers:

    Researching and writing the successful travel article
    The expanding guide book market
    Travel photography
    Dealing with the travel trade
    Members interested in attending this course should contact Angela Austin, Assistant Organiser, to register email angelaa@nuj.org.uk or tel: 0141 248 6648
    http://www.nujscotland.com/in-class-courses/travel-writing
