Gina Petrello-Pray (LION)’s Summary

Gina brings over 18 years of experience in the human capital industry. She has high recognition and has won numerous awards for her sales growth in helping companies find top talent. She is recognized as a talent scout for companies ranging from small and mid-sized firms to Fortune 500 companies. Including some of the most recognized and respected names in the Printed & Flexible Packaging, Label, Film, Adhesives, and Paper industry. Her company specializes in placing Top Level Management, Sales Professionals, Engineers and Product Development Positions. As well as Temporary project hires that require top level human capital specialist.

Her company also has special resume services in helping candidates create and design resumes that get results and interviews. In addition we focus on leveraging people's virtual presence, via online networks, LinkedIn, blogs, social network sites, and virtual communities that get candidates presence noticed.

Gina’s ability to forge strong personal client relationships, deliver unparalleled value-added services, and understand how industry organizations function, is the foundation of her success. She is dedicated to providing the highest level of personal service, quality, and integrity to each client and is recognized as one of the industry’s leading search consultants. Gina has earned a reputation for placing exceptional candidates and building top producing management, sales, and marketing teams for her clients.

Gina has been widely regarded as an observer of trends affecting the recruiting business and human capital management. Her opinions have been published in leading industry publications such as Labels & Labeling, Flexo Market News and Flexible Packaging Magazine. She has also earned numerous speaking engagements at industry-related conferences including Labelexpo and CMM, and is a member of IOPP.
Gina holds a Bachelor Degree in Business Management and Finance from Kent State University
