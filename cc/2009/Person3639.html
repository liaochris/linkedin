Alex Wallerstedt’s Summary

An award winning graphic designer and production artist with an eye for clean, elegant and meaningful design, Alex is known for developing creative solutions for business and marketing needs. Working closely with clients, he strives for innovative and strategic design.

Alex has experience in all forms of printed media as well as a familiarity with web/electronic media. His experience, talent, customer service and willingness to learn keep his clients coming back.
