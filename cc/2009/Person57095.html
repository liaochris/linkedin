Kyle Michel Sullivan’s Summary

Okay, picture this -- you're a director or producer; you got a script that ain't quite right or an idea that needs to be brought to life but you ain't no writer; or you just want somebody who can freshen things up and bring in a new perspective. Well look ye not farther, mine own compatriots -- I be the guy.

I'm an award-winning writer with 29 screenplays under his belt, of which he's sold 2, optioned 4 and done 9 works for hire as well as writing, producing and directing several industrial and training videos. I've also written plays and viral videos, and am a published author of erotica and fiction (5 books, total). For more information on my award-winners, check out --

http://www.moviebytes.com/ws/WinningScripts.cfm?keyword=Kyle+Michel+Sullivan

(References and samples upon request.)

My focus is on character and action, which to me transcends genre. But to be upfront, I DO have a weakness -- scatological comedy. I can do SLAPSTICK, HUMOR and WIT (I have written one script that aims for all three), but a Farrelly Brother or Judd Apatow I ain't.

So...if you gots the need for a writer with speed who's down with the creed...or prefer someone of a more literary bent to massage your story into a fine wine, bring it on. I have no problem taking no credit, so long as I get paid. And an NDA is also NBD.

The next step -- making contact. As in one of the e-mail addresses below.

kmswrites@mac.com
kyle@kmscb.com

Let's bring joy to the situation, kiddles. Life's too short to do anything less.
