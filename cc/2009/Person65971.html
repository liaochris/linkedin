McCain Inc.’s Experience

  • Industry Leader in Traffic, Transit and Parking Solutions

    McCain Inc.

    (Privately Held; Transportation/Trucking/Railroad industry)

    1987 — Present (22 years )

    McCain has been providing traffic related solutions to business and municipalities for over 25 years. McCain's products and services are widely trusted across the US and around the world to safely guide millions of motorist from point A to point B through the use of traffic, transit, parking and freeway/incident management solutions.

  • Parking Guidance System

    McCain Inc.

    (Privately Held; Transportation/Trucking/Railroad industry)

    1987 — Present (22 years )

    McCain's parking guidance solution is a combination of intelligent software and hardware that efficiently manages the occupancy of any size parking facility.

    • Provides accurate and reliable real-time space counts by monitoring each individual parking bay
    • Maximizes your parking structure occupancy and revenue by allowing 100% use of all parking spaces
    • Guides drivers to available parking through highly visible exterior and interior signs, and individual LED bay sensors
    • Minimizes driver stress
    • Reduces pollution and carbon emissions

  • Transit Management Solutions

    McCain Inc.

    (Transportation/Trucking/Railroad industry)

    1987 — Present (22 years )

    McCain’s transit solution allows city residents real-time access to transit schedules from their mobile phones and allows agencies to effortlessly manage their entire fleet to enhance performance and on-time arrivals.

    This advanced solution uses a robust real-time vehicle location technique, which combines GPS and dead-reckoning technologies, guaranteeing high-accuracy location of the fleet in all operating conditions. Whether there is traffic, weather conditions, or unforeseen events, agencies will have the means to keep their fleet operating on time.
