  • Metadata Technician

    Wake Forest University

    (Educational Institution; 1001-5000 employees; Higher Education industry)

  • Center for Primary Research and Training Fellow

    UCLA

    (Educational Institution; 10,001 or more employees; Research industry)

    September 2007 — June 2008 (10 months)

    Processing, description, and finding aids for two manuscripts collections, including approximately 25 linear feet of personal papers as well as intellectual organization and metadata for over 1000 popular culture items including phonograph records, stereographs, advertisements, and pulp serials. Created EAD-compatible (Encoded Archival Description) finding aids using DACS (Describing Archives: A Content Standard). Created and managed relational databases using Microsoft Access.

  • Library and Archives Graduate Associate, Creative Resource Center

    The Walt Disney Company

    (Public Company; DIS; Information Technology and Services industry)

    June 2007 — May 2008 (1 year )

    Reference using trend services, character art, model sheets and other visual resources for the Creative Resource Center and Archives of the Consumer Products Division of the Walt Disney Company. Cataloged over 3000 items and managed circulation using Sirsi Workflows and OCLC Worldcat. Archival processing and description of product idea archive spanning 7 years. Created and managed idea database using Documentum-based server.

  • From Interns to Library Leaders (FILL) Intern

    (Libraries industry)

    September 2007 — November 2007 (3 months)

    Adult reference and archival consultation for local history collection of over 70 linear feet. Archival processing and description of personal papers of 1 linear foot. Created archival processing manual which has been implemented for local history docents. Edited local history collection development policy and accessions policy.

  • Teaching Assistant
