Ellyssa Kroski’s Summary

I'm a freelance information consultant, writer, editor, lecturer, librarian, and conference speaker. I've written about Web technologies for major publications including Choice, School Library Journal, and ALA’s Library Technology Reports. My book Web 2.0 for Librarians and Information Professionals was published in February 2008. I'm currently developing a ten-book technology series for Neal-Schuman publishers, teaching at four universities, and speaking across the country. Please check out my website for my upcoming events and publications.
