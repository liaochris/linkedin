Patrick Petit’s Summary

Senior architect and software engineer with over 20 years of experience in Unix, Java and C languages programming, and Internet engineering.

I am interested in advanced developments and innovative solutions prototyping. Focused more recently on projects involving the design and implementation of Web Access Management and Federation solutions based on open-source software and technologies mostly originating from Sun Microsystems including; Glassfish, OpenESB, OpenDS, and OpenSSO, as well as other open-source products.

Currently working on finalizing my MBA at GGSB. My master thesis objective is to deep dive into analyzing the business opportunities and impediments of cloud computing from the point of view of small and medium businesses.

Patrick Petit’s Specialties:

C, J2SE, J2EE, Virtualization, System Management and Monitoring, Web Access Management, Federation, Identity Management, Unix-like OSs (Solaris, Linux, Mac OSX).
