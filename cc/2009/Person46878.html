Joey Trotz’s Summary

Extensive strategic, editorial, technical, program management and product development experience across a wide range of business disciplines, including web services, digital and linear advertising, interactive television and others across Turner Broadcasting Systems web sites and networks including CNN, SI.com, PGATour.com, CNNMoney.com, CartoonNetwork, TBS and TNT. VP-level operations and project management duties for SI.com, the partner web site for Sports Illustrated magazine. Editorial roles managing special event coverage for the SI website. Other roles integrating newspapers and new media, and my first career as a news photographer working in Boston with the Associated Press and later for newspapers around Georgia.

Joey Trotz’s Specialties:

Strategic technology vision and planning. Product and program management for interactive media, focusing on integration of broadcast and print with internet, interactive television and mobile products. Advertising technology, video ad serving, digital operations, interactive television.
