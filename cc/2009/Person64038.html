Martin de Wit’s Summary

DCom economist with proven research and consultancy track record in the field of natural resources and the environment. Focus on measurement and management of public and private socio-ecological risks, costs and benefits at government, business and household level. Interested in providing services to support management of complex and dynamic socio-ecological systems. Global outreach, especially when relevant to Africa's developmental challenge.

Martin de Wit’s Specialties:

Natural resource and environmental policy development, extended cost benefit analysis, valuation of the environment, systems dynamics modelling, environmental policy instruments, natural resource assessment, small business management
