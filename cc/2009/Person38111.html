Guillem Recolons Argenter’s Summary

I have been working in Marketing and Advertising for almost 30 years. I have had the privilege to work for the greats like BBDO, JWT, Ogilvy and Saatchi & Saatchi, and I have been Managing Director of Altraforma, a local agency in Barcelona. Currently I am an independent consultant, and a founding member of Lateral Consulting and TVLowCost Spain.

    TVLowCost

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    September 2007 — Present (2 years 3 months)

    TVLowCost was first launched in Paris in October 2004.

    100% independent, it belongs to its managers, a rather unusual situation in the advertising industry where 85% of the ad agencies belong to 5 global groups !

    It's immediate success has been tremendous and hundreds of companies have expressed high interest in this revolutionary approach. TVLC Paris already handles 80 clients and has produced over 500 TV commercials since then.

    Due to this huge impact on the market, the company decided rapidly to become international and to cover all the key markets with its revolutionary approach of TV advertising. Currently, the group is represented by 12 agencies, including Spain (establised 2007), all over the world and employs a little more than 200 people.

    Its philosophy can be summarized by this simple phrase : offer to the advertisers all over the world to GET MORE FOR LESS !
