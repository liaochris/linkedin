Woody Nash’s Summary

At Boomerang Systems, a U.S.-based manufacturer of automated parking and self-storage systems, I direct all marketing and business development activities for the company. Our products help real estate developers, car dealers, municipalities save money by using their real estate more effectively to meet their parking and self-storage needs.

While at Exodus Capital Advisors (http://www.exoduscap.com) I advised business owners of companies up to $250MM looking to maximize the cash flow and exit value of their company by discovering latent opportunities for revenue growth and cost reduction.

At Administaff (http://www.administaff.com) I sold a suite of HR-related business services (payroll, benefits, recruiting, training, etc...) to companies of 6 - 1,000 employees. I qualified for Chairman's Club exceeding my quota by over two and a half times.

I have built two companies which provided me with rich experience in all facets of business. The first was footwear company, N.E.O.S Performance Overshoes, (http://www.overshoe.com) which I sold for a profit after 5 years, and the second, KYS, a web-based training company for retailers to better educate their sales staff about the products they sold. These two start-ups gave me extensive hands on experience with overseas product development, manufacturing, importing, web application development, finance, sales and marketing.

I am the recipient of numerous business awards including the SBA Young Entrepreneur of the Year and Gold Medal Winer for Consumer Product Design in the Business Week IDEA Design Awards. I have raised $3.5M in angel and venture capital investments through private placements.
