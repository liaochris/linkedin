Carl Kutsmode’s Summary

Passionate recruiting solutions consultant with over 15 years experience as a Talent Acquisition Management Consultant including 8 years as owner and founder of a corporate recruitment solutions consulting & outsourcing firm. Industry pioneer in leveraging the Internet and "outside the box" strategies with technology to streamline corporate recruiting processes and build robust, quality talent pipelines for ongoing current and future hiring needs.

Expertise in assessing US and Global current state talent acquisition practices and aligning internal corporate talent acquisition capabilities with expansion related talent needs. Over 10 years selling, implementing and overseeing the successful delivery of (RPO) outsourced recruiting and talent acquisition consulting solutions for companies ranging from startup to Fortune 1000 global companies to help them cost effectively achieve growth related talent acquisition goals.

Carl Kutsmode’s Specialties:

I specialize in working with growing companies to align their talent acquisition goals with their strategic growth or new market expansion goals. My typical client has internal challenges managing their own hiring process during unexpected hiring spikes or in support of ongoing, high volume recruitment needs resulting from growth, expansion, M&A or market rebound etc. Industries include: Pharmaceutical, Biotech, Consumer Products, Hospitality, Healthcare, Mfg and financial services
