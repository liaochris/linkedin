Cheryl Perkins’s Summary

I specialize in helping the person who is disorganized AND unhealthy. It is not just about the act of putting things away. It has a lot to do with what's going on in our brains. The concept behind this is who would think that organization would be the key to losing weight? If your kitchen is organized, you are much more inclined to reach for healthy food items. The state of one's home IS the state of one's mind...and stomach!

I help organize people w/ a range of residential disorganizational challenges from A - Z, from mild clutter to packrats and hoarders.
