Gordon A. Rogers’s Summary

Gordon established a business (Meliora Systems, Inc. 1988-1998) that created one of the pioneering products in the corporate e-learning & talent management field (Ingenium). He guided the growth of the company from a start-up to IPO through a merger with Paul Allen's company, Asymetrix Learning Systems (ASYM). This exit event yielded a 12X return for initial investors in the business. See http://bit.ly/gi9hQ.

After leaving ASYM/CLKS/SUMT in 2000, he spent the next several years investing in and serving on the advisory boards of various tech startups in the e-learning and online coaching sectors. He now focuses primarily on helping early stage ventures in the education and virtual world sectors raise capital and create strategic partnerships.
He is a member of Atlanta Technology Angels (www.angelatlanta.com) and Angel Lounge (http://www.startuplounge.com/tag/angellounge/), and the Atlanta CEO Council. He is currently Managing Director of Vernon Bridge Ventures, an early stage capital/advisory firm.

He has extensive experience serving on boards of non-profit organizations (YMCA of Greater Atlanta, Interfaith Outreach Home, Teacher Support Network, The Lionheart School).
