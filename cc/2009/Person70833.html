Murat Ersoy’s Summary

BIOGRAPHY:
On behalf of The Cabinet, the Ministry of Education honored Murat Ersoy in 2005 as “Student of the Year” of the Netherlands, of all 73 universities in the country. This annual occasion is organized by Scienceguide, Lsvb, and the Iso. The reason for his national recognition is that he was active in so many different fields with impact. For instance, he excelled in leading diverse groups that organized many activities and demonstrated resolve in the Dutch community. European level championships in sports, founding successful organizations, writing columns for newspapers, his unconditional willingness to help out others, and performing as an outstanding student complete his profile. Besides, two years before his reception, he was elected as “Most Promising Bicultural Student” of his University.

Murat holds a Bachelor of Commerce degree in Marketing from The Hague University, NL and is a candidate for Master of Arts in International Business (MBA) at Webster University, who provided him a special dedicated scholarship together with The Hague University, because of his international character and involvement. Born in The Hague and being a Dutch citizen with the Caucasian/Turkish heritage of his parents, he has experience of work in Dubai, and has done Masters studies in EU, the US and Shanghai (P.R.China), as well as speaking 8 languages; Dutch/ Turkish/ Northwest-Chinese/ English/ North-Caucasian/ Kazakh/ and basic Russian, and German.

Mr. Ersoy is the founder of MultiPotentials, and a member of the Grand Council of “Brotherhood of young Entrepreneurs” Infect. Breaking through conventions and inexhaustible searching for solutions is his motto. Alongside building the MultiPotentials-movement he is accountable for business development, project strategy, stabilizer, motive force, administrator, and impossibilities solver. Speaker on conferences and symposia regarding: bottom line marketing, high-potentials, biculturalism, and innovation matters.
