Rick Breemer’s Summary

Over the last 8 years I have specialised in Livelink software (OScript) development for Open Text's flagship product Livelink Enterprise Server.

My main quality is using my broad experience, technical excellence, commitment to quality, customer focus and excellent listening skills to understand my client's Enterprise Content Management needs and to provide the best solution.

My daily work includes the following responsibilites:
• Design and realise new solutions for Livelink (Livelink modules developed in OScript);
• Be the technical lead for a team of developers. The developers are based in the Netherlands, India and the Philippines;
• Audit and participate in project tenders;
• Audit Livelink software/customisations;
• Engage in pre-sales activities;
• Give technical Livelink trainings;
• Advise in Livelink related matters in any area.
