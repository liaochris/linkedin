William Christopher’s Summary

Environmental Analyst/Engineer - performing qualitative and quantitative assessments of the environmental impacts of manufactured products and services from cradle to grave using life cycle impact assessment (LCIA) and life cycle screening methods.

Professional experience in product design and development, materials and manufacturing, environmental standards development and certification.

Academic research on the environmental impacts of industry, agriculture and institutions.

Mr. Christopher is a Mechanical Engineer with 10 years product design and manufacturing experience and an M.S. in Environmental Management. In his role at SCS Mr. Christopher conducts environmental evaluations of manufactured goods, material content audits and life cycle impact assessments. Mr. Christopher has experience in web-based database development projects and has recently developed an extensive online product registry for green school building products.
