Gloria Feldt’s Summary

Author of three books: New York Times best seller "Send Yourself Roses", in collaboration with actress Kathleen Turner; previously "The War on Choice" and "Behind Every Choice Is a Story". Blogger at www.gloriafeldt.com/heartfeldt-politics-blog/. Commentary published in the New York Times, Elle, Salon, MS, USA Today, Women's e-News, Alternet, etc. Keynote speaker, media commentator on women's lives, public and reproductive health, media, politics, and visionary leadership. Leading activist and expert on these issues from where the personal and political meet. 30 years in reproductive health/rights/justice, 9 as national president and CEO of Planned Parenthood Federation of America, the world's largest reproductive health care provider and advocate.

Visit my website www.GloriaFeldt.com for full bio and clips.

Gloria Feldt’s Specialties:

I have spoken at numerous national organization, university, and political venues and have apeared on most major news and public affairs shows. People magazine called me "the voice of experience" because of my personal story. I was a teen mom who rose to lead Planned Parenthood Federation of America. Now my voice is independent as an impassioned and inspirational keynote speaker, writer, and media commentator on women's lives, health, media, politics, and visionary leadership.
