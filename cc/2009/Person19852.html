Coach Rick Kolster-CBC’s Summary

With over 25 years of business experience , ranging from restaurant hospitality services to industrial sales and manufacturing, we have taken this experience, both corporate and entreprenuerial and coach individuals towards a more effective and fulfilled life. Coach Rick is also the co-author of the bestselling book, Selling for Geniuses. We utilize a supportive, non judgemental style when we work with C- level to the "soloprenuer" to identify the things in their life that they want to achieve and work to set direction and goals to achieve these things. We are considered experts in facilitating communications, interpersonal skills and motivation, as well as leadership and sales development for all levels of an organization. Having worked with organizations in every corner of the US and Canada, we have been well received by organizations of all sizes and people of vastly different levels of responsibilities. Our goal is the help each individual identify their professional and personal purpose and live a goal directed life to achieve this purpose. We can be reached at 817-748-7425 or info@mypotentialplus.com
