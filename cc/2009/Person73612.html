Valentine Makhouleen’s Summary

A Toronto-based interactive art director with a decade of experience developing engaging new media.

Over the years, I have had the privilege to partner with many non-profit and corporate brands including WorldVision, Canadian Ape Alliance, Frontier College, IBM, Bell Canada, DHL, American Express, Olivieri, Cogeco, Timex, J.M. Smucker’s, Kraft and Cisco; as well as several major advertising agencies including Ogilvy and Cossette. Going forward, my aim is to work with organizations whose mission is to help shape an ethical sustainable future for the world.

Valentine Makhouleen’s Specialties:

Traditional and new media, art direction, 360° integrated advertising and marketing, social networking, web development, motion, photography, 3D and illustration.
