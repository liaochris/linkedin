David J McClelland’s Summary

Dave has worked in technology and design fields in the service of communication and learning for over 10 years. An education and experience in industrial design informs his approach to solutions for business and government.

He has deep experience integrating third-party and custom content into learning management systems via SCORM. Much of his efforts are focused on programming and designing rich media content and developing tools to support external content development by non-programmers.
