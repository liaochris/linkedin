Terri McGhee’s Summary

Terri is a proven self starter with over eleven years of sales experience within the retail, film and commercial print markets. In addition, she has proven marketing and business management experience as the owner of her own Booking Agency. Terri was a professional model for Christian Dior as well as other well known designers. She competed in National Talent & Beauty Competitions for over eight years and won several National Titles. She has experience as a Methodist Lay Speaker. She is active in community volunteer work. Terri has had a music ministry for over 20 years and is currently singing on a Praise & Worship Team. Terri is a team player with strong people skills and strong sales ability. She has a positive attitude, and can solve problems easily. Terri’s strength is that she can handle situations with difficult people and she gets along well with anyone.
