Aaron Lewis’s Summary

Broker Associate, REO Team Leader for The Lewis Team at Prudential California Realty in the Turlock, CA office. Specializing in representing sellers, buyers, investors, and institutional REO sellers of residential real estate in the Central Valley of California, including Stanislaus, Merced, and San Joaquin counties. Heavy experience in REO, having managed/sold over 180 REO properties within the last year, averaging 75+ properties under management at a time.
