Derek Britton’s Summary

Strong track record of meeting time-to-market, functional, lead-generation and 8-figure revenue targets. Key personal contribution on notable seven-figure projects working with Global 2000 accounts. Direct experience managing key client implementation projects.

Proven success as a product and project management leader, helping shape corporate strategy and vision, and implementing and managing associated roadmap and products; backed by relevant industry-leading professional training and strong natural interpersonal skills.
