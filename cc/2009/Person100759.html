Travis Drouin’s Summary

With over fifteen years in public accounting and private industry, I'm currently an audit partner in one of Massachusetts' largest non-national public accounting firms. I provide audit and assurance services to my privately-held clients, while also providing a number of finance and accounting consulting services to my SEC registered clients. Through my colleagues at MFA, I also provide my clients with access to staffing and information technology solutions, and wealth planning services.
