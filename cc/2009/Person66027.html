Diane McHutchison, PMP, SSGB’s Summary

I am the Project Manager or Analysis Manager that you need. I have the analysis and project management expertise that will meet your needs and give you a Manager who will hit the ground running. I have strong facilitation skills, and have been an instructor for many years, both in the corporate and the volunteer sectors. I have dealt with many different audiences, from CEOs to the shop floor. Recognizing the importance of improving processes, especially in these economic times, I am currently enrolled in a Six Sigma Black Belt Certification program to enhance and refine the skills I already have as a Six Sigma Green Belt.

In my current position, I am the Senior Project Manager overseeing the implementation of a customizable COTS program into multiple speciality divisions of a major hospital. These divisions ranged from a very small practice consisting of a few physicians and therapists, to a large and busy surgical division.

I have experience in both the pharmaceutical and medical device industries, so rigid quality controls and best practices are no stranger to me. I have supported a development lifecycle process, and contributed to its refinement. My first love is requirements analysis, but those skills make me a better manager, since nearly anything we do requires the critical thinking skills of a good analyst.

Additionally, I am an experienced small business owner, having been CEO/COO of an S-Corporation selling equipment and supplies to emergency responders. After selling that business, but needing a use for free time, I started designing and selling hand crafted jewelry.

MBA. PMP. SSGB.
