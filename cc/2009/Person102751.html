George Robbins’s Summary

I have been in the production arts for over 20 years and have enjoyed every minute. It is not often we get the chance to do the things we love for pay, but that is exactly what I do. Taking a project from load in to load out is, at least for me, a fantastic journey of expression. From designing a large video production to directing a television commercial or building a beautiful corporate set, I have found many ways to fulfill my need to be a part of bringing new ideas to life.

I would love to offer my art, my skills and my experience to you and your company. If, after your examination of my qualifications, you feel I may be the type of person your company could use please feel free to contact me at anytime.

Thank you in advance for your consideration of my qualifications.
