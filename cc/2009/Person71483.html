Natalie Carlson’s Summary

I am an independent graphic and web designer, creature of the night, and a hardened and shameless Tori Amos fan. My freelance design services are available for print and web media. My goal and focus as a designer is to present meaningful information in an aesthetic, inventive, and accessible format.

I have 10 years of experience and collegiate study in the fields of art, graphic design, and digital media. I have been taking-on professional print and web-design jobs since 2004. My design services (Natalie Carlson Designs) were established as a self-owned business in 2007. Academically, I am currently finishing up my undergraduate work in Art History and Digital Media. I plan to continue my education at San Jose State University in the Fall, and ultimately achieve an BFA/MFA in Graphic Design.

Natalie Carlson’s Specialties:

Macintosh OSX+, Windows XP and Vista, InDesign, Photoshop, Illustrator, XHTML, PHP, CSS, customizing Javascript (pure-CSS solutions preferred)
