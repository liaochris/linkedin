Scott Andrews’s Summary

Business management and sales process expert. A master at getting in the door and building strong alliance relationships. Previous experience includes a Summit Club/President's Club history of success with IBM, Data General, EMC2, DecisionOne, and more. I've worked with the largest technology, technology service & support, and vacation industry firms. I've directly, or through my firms, provided solutions for Blue Shield of California, EDS, HP, IBM, LSI Logic, Safeway, Tandem (now HP), the State of California, and many more organizations.

I strive to make a difference through helping business leaders to empower their employees through advanced (yet simple) structure and management processes and therefore create more success while having fun doing it.

I am also the founder and board member of SLOJAZZFEST.ORG - bringing a world-class jazz festival to San Luis Obispo.
