Liz Bittar’s Summary

Liz Bittar is a consultant, facilitator and training designer, fluent in English, Spanish, German, Italian and Portuguese. Lived in England, Switzerland, Agentina, United States and Brazil.

In charge of a consulting firm since 1997, Liz Bittar & Associados, a group of associates who share one passion: developing people. With specialists in different areas, the company provides training and coaching in 3 different languages - English, Spanish and Portuguese.

Liz Bittar is also founder and Managing Director at Secretárias Virtuais, (Virtual Assistants), a company that provides virtual offices, meeting and training rooms, with full assistance in English, Spanish and Portuguese.
