VISTA BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT

(1) Business and Sales Performance
My company, Vista Business Development, provides consulting and training services helping large companies, predominantly in the technology and IS sector, to compete more effectively in the global market through enhanced deal management and sales practices.

In 2008 I have worked with Fujitsu Siemens Computers (FSC), Microsoft, Thales Group, Atos Origin, Capita and Aviva to improve their sales performance.

I am a highly experienced Account Manager and Sales Professional with nearly 20 years experience working for major multinational organisations (Capgemini, Atos, Schlumberger) in senior Account Management, Business Development, Consulting and Sales Training roles.

(2) PROFESSIONAL REPRESENTATION in ECUADOR
Vista Business Development, offers a consultancy service (using hourly or daily rates) for companies requiring some form of representation in Ecuador. We provide the perfect combination of extensive knowledge of doing business in Ecuador together with significant experience of large international companies.
Typical assignments we offer are:-
-Investigate/qualify business opportunities
-Finding & vetting business partners/suppliers/contractors
-Market & Sector analysis
-Attend meetings on your behalf (pre sale, sale or post sale)
-Manage your local partners/suppliers/contractors
-Personnel services/management for your locally based staff



Vista Business Development is based in the UK and Ecuador, providing coverage across Europe and the Americas in English and Spanish.
