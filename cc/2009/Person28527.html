Dario Susanj’s Summary

A former journalist, an editor, and a book author, specialised in covering ICT. Almost 20 years of experience in writing about computer technology and telecommunications, with 1700+ articles, 8 books published so far, and another one in production.

Since Oct 2007, PR account manager specialising in PR for technology companies. See below for the list of accounts I have worked on.

  • ICT journalist

    Self-employed

    (Self-Employed; Media Production industry)

    May 1988 — September 2007 (19 years 5 months)

    I have written about Information Technology and Communications for various magazines and newspapers. Starting way back when I was just 14, I wrote for magazines such as Svet kompjutera and Moj mikro, reviewing games. Further along, I was the news editor at the Croatian edition of the BYTE magazine and at Computerworld Croatia. I also wrote for daily newspapers such as the Večernji list and Vjesnik, business magazines such as Banka, Poslovni dnevnik, Eurotrend, and Business.hr (which I am still presently writing for occasionally), and ICT magazines such as PC Chip, Moj Moby, Bug, Mreza, and the Croatian edition of T3. Some of my work in the more general media has included the regular section on useful websites in the Croatia Airlines' in-flight magazine Croatia and a regular section with computer tips and useful websites in the teen magazine Teen.

  • editor-in-chief

    PC Chip Magazine

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Media Production industry)

    September 2003 — July 2007 (3 years 11 months)

    My responsibilities at the PC Chip Magazine included much more than just being editor-in-chief. I wrote numerous articles, reviews, and columns for both the PC Chip magazine and Moj Moby, a telecommunications magazine. PC Chip is the second best-selling, but perhaps easily even the most influential Croatian ICT magazine within the industry. Published each month on 200+ pages, it frequently goes much further and deeper than any of the competing magazines, covering areas such as the business IT, trends and technologies, application of ICT in various industries, and so on. Frequent interviews, cover stories and special reports have made this magazine unique in Croatia.
