Marc Nathan’s Experience

  • Mentor

    Capital Factory

    (Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    January 2009 — Present (7 months)

    Capital Factory (http://www.capitalfactory.com) is a seed stage mentoring program for startups that provides a small amount of seed capital and weekly mentoring sessions by entrepreneurs who have founded successful companies. Startup companies apply to participate in our 10 week summer program intended to get a startup pointed in the right direction with a clear path to cash flow.

    (Non-Profit; 11-50 employees; Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    July 2007 — Present (2 years 1 month)

    The Director of Development assists Technology-Related Startup and Early Growth Stage Companies in all aspects of Business Strategy, Capital Raising, Product Development, Customer Acquisition, Marketing and Public Relations, Partnership Management in and around the Greater Houston, Texas Region.

  • Marketing Chairman

    Houston Angel Network

    (Non-Profit; 51-200 employees; Venture Capital & Private Equity industry)

    January 2006 — Present (3 years 7 months)

    The Houston Angel Network is a non-profit organization that provides its members a forum in which to efficiently evaluate promising early-stage investment opportunities. Its membership consists of active and SEC-accredited angel investors who share the goal of making informed, collaborative investments in promising early stage Texas-based companies.

    HAN was founded in late 2001 and is the largest and most active angel network in Texas. In 2005, HAN members have invested approximately $6.5M in 15 deals.
