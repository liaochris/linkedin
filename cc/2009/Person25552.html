Deepak Ravindran’s Summary

Having started my career as an Entrepreneur even from my school days,i founded Swades Solutions an offshore Web design/SEO firm way back on 2005.
After joining college for Computer science,Deepak along with 3 other youngsters founded Innoz Technologies,a privately incorporated firm located at Technopark,Kerala.
Innoz is the leading startup on mobile & web-based services in India.
Innoz has created a truly interoperable and accessible ‘knowledge-information’ service, SMSGYAN that is available across an impressive group of operators including Airtel,BSNL,MTNL,Aircel,Vodafone,Idea,TATA etc.. Innoz is one among the #16 ‘Top Companies’ to participate in the finals of DFJ-CISCO Global Business Plan Competition held on June 2009. Based in Technopark Campus, Kerala Innoz Tech. is an iAccelerator backed company.
