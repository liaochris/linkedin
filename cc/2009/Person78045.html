Martin Pazzani’s Summary

A seasoned global business executive with a 30-year tenure in management, brand building, integrated marketing and strategic development that spans the ad agency, corporate, music, and film worlds. Have done business on six continents and held senior positions in major corporations and agencies alike, including Foote Cone & Belding, the Interpublic Group, DDB Needham, Bally Total Fitness, Heublein (now Diageo), and Elias Arts.

Currently CEO of Trailer Central / The Golden Trailer Awards, the Oscars of the movie trailer business, where they are evolving this former trade show into a year-round, multi-platform, consumer entertainment property with tv, web, and in-theater channels.

Board Member of Advertising Production Resources Co (APRCO), Blue Maze Entertainment, Sir Groovy Music, and Therability Fitness.

Former Fortune 500 Chief Marketing Officer, ex-Advertising Agency Senior management, and ex-Chairman/CEO/President of the world's most awarded music agency.

Expertise in branding, integrated marketing, audio identity, advertising, tv, film, branded entertainment. Global clientèle and A-list clients of the biggest and best advertising agencies, corporate marketers, tv and film companies, and branding firms.

Published author and thought leader on music, branding, marketing, advertising, and strategy.

Speaker at TED 2008 Conference in Monterey, CA on "The Power of Music"

Speaker, panelist, seminar leader around the world (Cannes, Dubai, London, Paris, Johannesburg, India, Brazil, Argentina, Mexico, Tokyo, Hong Kong, Sydney, Auckland, etc.) on competitive strategy, music, audio identity, and marketing.

The 146th human being to stand on the summit of the tallest mountain in Antarctica, Mt. Vinson. Climbed Matterhorn and Mont Blanc 4 times each.
