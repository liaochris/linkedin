Jeremy Quinn’s Summary

A highly skilled IT professional, with a wide range of experience in web technologies both on the client and the server.

Specialises in applying open source and open standards to projects and excels at conceptualising problems at all levels of abstraction.

Has a strong track record of successful collaboration, equally he can independently take a project from initial ideas through to implementation.

Jeremy Quinn’s Specialties:

AJAX, Apache (Ant, Cocoon, HTTPd, Lucene, OJB, Maven), CMS, CSS, Dojo, DTD, Hibernate, HTML, J2EE, Java, JavaScript, JMS, JSON, JSONP, LDAP, Spring, SQL, STX, W3C Standards, WebDAV, XML (numerous languages), XSD, XSLT
