Jane Ahlering’s Summary

Through both her professional experience and education, Jane has functioned across sectors and in multifaceted roles including marketing communications, project and campaign management, corporate responsibility, and research.

Jane returned to graduate school after nearly three years as a marketing associate in a professional services environment. In May 2009, she completed her joint MSc/MA degree in Global Communications at London School of Economics and University of Southern California. She has a specific interest in, and completed her graduate thesis on, corporate identity and social responsibility communications.

Jane Ahlering’s Specialties:

Internal and external communications; corporate social responsibility; international marketing and brand strategy; change management; social media.
