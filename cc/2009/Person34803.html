Gabriel Mesquida’s Summary

Engineer and economist, project manager with wide experience in airports, from design to commissioning. Focused on a wide variety of subjects and always willing to learn new things.

A major achievement is to have successfully commissioned, the 17th of June 2009, the biggest airport passenger terminal building in Europe (544.000 sqm.) in the framework of a €5.100 million airport enlargement project. Everything worked :)
