Vikas Shah’s Summary

Vikas believes in creating systems and processes for delivering consistent results rather than administering ‘short term’ steroid shots to boost performance. He is a focused professional and likes to work in an environment laden with challenges and ambiguities; managing status quo bores him.

He is a well rounded professional and understand various aspects of business including finance, operations, distribution, marketing, HR, SCM etc. Vikas drives an open and transparent system of administration. He is committed to any assignment that he undertakes. He tenaciously pursues these to logical fruition. His team clamours (both within and outside the organization) for an opportunity to work with him (given his strong mentoring and coaching skills).

Vikas Shah’s Specialties:

Vikas spent 10 years in Tata Teleservices Ltd. (TTSL) i.e. part of the telecom industry. He worked in various capacities including Rural Telephony, Public Telephony, Quality, Collections, and Sales. In his last role Vikas managed following business lines Rural, Pay Telephony, PoS and Calling Cards. Prior to TTSL, he worked for a consuting firm called PSi. At PSi he worked for some of the major Fortune – 500 companies. The incumbent contributed in the areas of research, consulting and sourcing.
