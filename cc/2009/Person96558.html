Tracy Currie’s Summary

Experience Director and CEO.

Founder and Executive Director of Sirius Telecommunications Ltd, taking an active role in various positions throughout the company’s development including: CEO, Mergers & Acquisitions, General Management, Operational management, Change management and Project management.

Subsequently held CEO roles in Phoneware; Phone Control and Zamro International largely to restructure and refocus the business.

Ten years experience as a company director.
