Mike Szabo’s Summary

l'm a designer and digital artist with extensive experience in creating online, print, and broadcast identity and content for advertising and media companies.

Mike Szabo’s Specialties:

Assembly of photographs, artwork and typography concepts for high-profile brands and advertising campaigns.

Creation of layered images with persons, skin, makeup, clothing, hair, objects, environments, textures, interfaces.

Color-correction, optimization, and finalization of high-resolution art for use in print, web, game, and broadcast media.

Technical expertise to install, maintain, support, instruct and operate all design studio hardware and software.
