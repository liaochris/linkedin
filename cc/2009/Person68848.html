Mike Weiner’s Summary

In my capacity as President/CEO of PreConstruction Catalysts, Inc, (PCC Funding), I facilitate transactions between principal sellers and buyers of bank paper: Bank Guarantees, Medium Term Notes, etc in the Trillions of dollars. Profits from these deals fund humanitarian and environmental projects, developments and enterprises.

I work with licensed trade platform managers and principal partners who deal with the Top 20 world banks, hedge funds, international banks, and other funding resources.

PCC Funding is bringing the principals with the projects and the money together, and acts as a catalyst to create viable projects for humanitarian and environmental benefit.

Background and Experience:

An effective communicator of ideas and concepts, my 35+ years of experience in broadcasting, marketing, advertising, management and business leadership, in addition to the "School of Hard Knocks" experiences, help me engage people across all channels, from C-level to z-level, to create synergy and teamwork which results in accomplishment of a objective, strategy and mission. My style is to bring people together with their talents, assets and skills, then facilitate them into a direction and let them work their magic.

I am responsible for business development and sustaining excellent client relationships with leaders in their respective fields, such as Booz Allen Hamilton, Lockheed Martin, Northrop Grumman, Waste Management, WESTAT, Bozzuto, Lincoln Properties, and other clients.

Brought together a team of several experts in a collegial setting from the advertising, marketing, public relations and electronic communications media


My personal mission is to leave the world better a better place than I found it, through leadership, authenticity, integrity and strength.PCC Funding is a vehicle through which this will be done.
