Shyam Balani’s Summary

BizIT Consultants, Inc. (www.bizitconsultants.com) was founded by Shyam K Balani, in response to a growing demand from US Corporate Treasuries and Banks for Platinum Level, SAP Treasury and Banking (SAP FSCM) consultants, with an in depth knowledge of Treasury processes and Financial instruments.

To date, Shyam has managed over 14 SAP Treasury projects globally, over the last 12 years.

Our brand name clients have appreciated our excellent services levels in SAP Treasury and this has lead us to further expand our services across all SAP Functional modules and IT infrastructure services.

We now also have a team of Platinum Level consultants based out of Singapore and India to address your business requirements in a more cost effective service model.

Our consultants develop state of the art, customized solutions without disregarding your key business operations and growth objectives in mind.
