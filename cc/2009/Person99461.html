Todd Nilson’s Summary

Finding the right Information Technology professional who meets your specific technical requirements and company's work environment is tough. Top companies engage my services because I am relentless, because my research skills are excellent, and because I understand and build strong talent networks both virtually and in the real world.

My specialty is finding and hiring information technology executives and senior level practitioners, including CIO's, CTO's, VP's of development or PMO, directors, managers, and architects. I manage a great team and personally oversee the searches we undertake.

If you are seeking new career options in Information Technology and would like me to review your resume or discuss your search, you can reach me by calling (414) 312-4233.

Learn about current career opportunities at: http://tinyurl.com/currentjobs

My clients include the Fortune 1000 with an emphasis in the Midwest; however, I have been engaged on top level searches nationally. My clients' industries include technology, manufacturing, health care, and financial services.

I am experienced at social media employment branding, application tracking system (ATS) selection and implementation, Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO), and hiring process auditing with an emphasis on candidate experience.

Todd Nilson’s Specialties:

Information Technology hiring, recruiting strategy, passive candidate search techniques, behavioral interviewing, new media, social networking, job networking, outplacement counseling, effective resume and cover letter writing, applicant tracking system (ATS) selection, training, research, writing, public speaking, hiring process improvement
