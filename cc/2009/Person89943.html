Sean Alvarez’s Summary

Sean Alvarez is an entertainment professional with over 10 years of experience in the motion picture and television industries. He has been contracted as a supervising producer, line producer, production manager, and production accountant in his career thus far.

Through the course of his career Sean has had the opportunity to work alongside some of the most prominent figures in film and television entertainment. His extensive background includes experience in a wide range of formats including studio live action films, CGI animated features, independent films, television movies of the week, television game shows, and network television unscripted docudramas, and reality competitions. His work has taken him across the nation and abroad successfully leading numerous projects from concept to delivery.

His solid foundation in the area of production finance prepared Sean for the rigorous demands of production management. He has consistently demonstrated a remarkable ability to create, implement and oversee any project size from cable, to network, to the big screen.

Sean Alvarez has a proven track record creating feasible, cost-effective, creatively driven production models and he has consistently delivered them through all phases of the production process.

Sean Alvarez’s Specialties:

Project management, format development, casting, budgeting, cost reporting, contract negotiation, infrastructure planning/implementation, location logistics, strategic alliances, international negotiations, union negotiations
