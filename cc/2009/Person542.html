Aaron Miller’s Summary

Creating, planning, and executing contests, events, concerts, live broadcasts, web based promotions, and marketing plans for 102.3 the Max, a Hot AC radio station in Louisville, KY. Emceeing events and public speaking. Representing 102.3 The Max at community events. Writing content for the morning show (including most of the original parody songs with contributions from Tommy Lee), writing content for the website (www.themaxfm.com).

Additionally, I write a daily blog for Velocity Weekly, a publication of the Courier-Journal. In the past I have been the TV columnist and a freelance features writer for Velocity.
