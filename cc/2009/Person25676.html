In addition to this, I have helped--in my capacity as independent consultant-- global corporations investigate opportunities in India and to better understand this market, guiding them about the India specific strategies --particularly on entry related and regulatory environment in Telecom and Technology domain, business intelligence, identifying suitable business partners.
