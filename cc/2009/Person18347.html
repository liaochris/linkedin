Christian Meldgaard’s Summary

My skills are primarily within business development, entrepreneurship, branding and strategy. I strive to be innovative and thinking out of the box in a relevant business context.

My competencies stretch widely. Earlier in my carrier I was appointed to start up a company – and made break-even within a year, I’ve been a key-account manager, I have participated in the development of major marketing campaigns and been Head of LoweFriends Interactive in Copenhagen (The Danish branch of the advertising agency Lowe Worldwide/Draft). I have been deeply involved in the book project; Retailization.com. Today I am a counsellor at Copenhagen Business School and Partner + Strategic Director at Airbourne (the media neutral strategic planning company based on co-creation).
