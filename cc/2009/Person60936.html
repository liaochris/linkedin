Tsur (Tsuf) Pelly’s Summary

* I am a Pro Geographical photographer. Long ago I realized that my photography work can be significant for increasing the awareness and sensibility of people around the world to the strong connection between humanity and our home planet Earth, and to the global ecological problems that we so responsible for them as members of the modern world.

* As a diving instructor I realized that all nature system elements must affect each other as one big organism, so no part can escape from the over-all planet eco-problem fate. I decided to specialize in U.W. Photography and started to document the fascinating Under Water World and also, unfortunately, his eco wounds.

* I work as a professional photographer for Nature and Geographical magazines and own a large and diverse digital photo images repository from Israel, the Red Sea and around the planet. (You're welcome to visit my website: www.lupapa.com).

* From 1995 till today most of My Geo photography activities devoted for illuminate this "Mankind-Nature" connection topic. I established and participated in several self and group photo-art exhibitions and Photo contests which impart me with a few rewords. I give lectures together with audio-visual performances about Mankind-Nature connection, together with Geo Photography and U/W photography workshops.

* I am also a Theatre Director and a Drama teacher-instructor (BFA-Special distinction) that developed a special technique to use as a Director with my actors-performers and with any group in my drama workshops (See above in Tsuf's Drama Workshops: My Method).

* The connection between my specialities is a Multi-Disciplinary method I developed and named: " The Semiotic-Reaction Technic" for Explore our Human-behavior that lead to a special drama actions workshops, that can help us to understand and experiencing better (or: "to remind our Body again") the magical connection between Art, Nature & Reality-life actions.
