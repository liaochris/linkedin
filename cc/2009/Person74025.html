Nick Reddin’s Summary

My Bio:

Nick has been with Manpower since 2001, working in multiple states including California and Illinois. He has over 11 years of experience in the employment services industry; the majority of his background is in working with Fortune 500 companies to achieve their workflow goals. Nick specializes in change management and driving operational efficiencies. He is an expert on the changing world of work and consults with many companies in helping them prepare for coming changes.

Nick's experience includes overseeing full-cycle recruiting, sales, and efficiency planning and consulting. His experience is varied between Technology, Financial, Call Center, Administrative, and Industrial environments. He is a sought after speaker at academic and company events as well as being interviewed on T.V and in newspapers. He is a frequent contributing writer to the Des Moines Business Record, JobDig, Veritas Magazine and Iowa ABI BizLogs and print magazine. Nick was also chosen as JobDig’s 2008/2009 career tip guru.

Nick lives in Urbandale, Iowa. He has five children and has been married for 18 years to his wife, Dana. He has served as a Youth and Children’s Pastor for 13 years and is an advocate for helping children and youth achieve their best and focus on their future. He works with organizations such as Junior Achievement, Iowa Association of Business and Industry (ABI), IASP, SHRM, Quad Cities Development Group, San Diego Workforce Development, serves on multiple boards, and is an on-call consultant to the Department of Labor and Iowa Workforce Development. Nick is also a guest host on 1040am WHO radio in Des Moines where he discusses the changing world of work.


You can keep up with Nick's writings at www.WorkExposed.com and www.WorkExposedBlog.com
