Glenda Myles’s Summary

Creating new programs, solving problems, and producing great results is what motivates me. I'm honest, direct, and believe in doing the hard work necessary to be successful. As an accomplished marketing professional; I'm equally competent building strategic marketing plans as I am with executing on those plans.

I love new technologies and its application in the corporate world. But I don't believe one size fits all. And I use my years of experience to achieve success through program development and evaluation, project management, strategic planning, market research, and facilitation.
