Chris Atwood’s Summary

I'm a project manager and a managing partner in CC Communications, a start-up marketing communications firm with an emphasis in design and interactive media. We work with accounts in the Austin and Houston metropolitan areas, and specialize working with new clients who are interested in developing a creative, identifiable brand, and then publicizing it through the Web and other media with a cohesive marketing/advertising communications plan.

I graduated from Texas Tech University with a B.A. in Public Relations with a minor in political science. In the past I have experienced writing for a newspaper with a daily readership of about 20,000 (Daily Toreador at Texas Tech University), creating concepts for multi-faceted campaigns to include not just the public/media relations perspective, but the advertising, marketing and promotions, and computer science/Web site design/structure consultation.
