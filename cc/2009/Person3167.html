Alexander S. White’s Summary

I am a recent graduate of Northwestern University. I am living in Boulder, CO. I am almost 23.

I ran a student group at Northwestern with a budget over $330,000 and over 50 members. I ran concert production for Grammy-winning acts including Flight of the Conchords, Kanye West and Wilco. I worked at the largest ad agency in Chicago on the Kraft account. Before that I spent two summers interning at Universal Records in New York City and Chicago. In high school I worked at Ithaca's own College Town Bagels. I have started two companies.

I burn through recommended novels and books on psychology, economics, business management, science, current events, and history. I am addicted to Google Reader. I love movies that make me think, music that gets stuck in my head, personal productivity & efficiency hacks, the last block of a long run, really talking to people, and constant self-improvement.
