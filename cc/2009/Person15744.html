Carol King’s Summary

Carol King is a multi-disciplined senior graphic designer/art director with over 7 years of experience in both print, branding and web design. Carol has worked on projects in a variety of industries from corporate to luxury/entertainment to non-profit and financial.

With a diversified background working in house for several top Fortune 500 companies to working independently as a freelance designer, Carol brings a wide range of knowledge, background and experience to her design work.

Carol King’s Specialties:

Branding, print design, brand identity, web design, flash banners, logos, marketing collateral, brochures, catalogs, packaging, package design, account management, print production, print procurement, corporate identity, rebranding, marketing collateral
