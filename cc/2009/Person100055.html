Tommy Toy’s Summary

My core areas of expertise are: STRATEGY, BUSINESS PLANNING and VENTURE CAPITAL

Nearly 30 years broad industry, business and consulting experience. Manufacturing, retail/whole, distribution, indoor tanning, professional services, advertising agency, TV and radio broadcasting, software, wireless and Internet, including the latest in Web 2.0 technologies.

Creative and strategic marketing guru. An expert at developing business and marketing strategies and tactics for both startups and existing businesses. I bring focus, organization and strong problem-solving and entrepreneurial skills when I work with entrepreneurs and business owners.

I specialize in working with embryonic and early stage businesses because they need the most help getting organized, developing a successful business model, business structure, strategic and tactical plan so that you can grow into a successful business enterprise that can effectively compete in the marketplace.

I advise entrepreneurs and business owners with their decision-making: on strategy, marketing, finances, operations, manufacturing, distribution, technology, organization serve as their venture capital advisor and facilitator

I am a generalist with extensive private industry and business consulting experience in many industries, so chances are very good that I understand your industry space and can provide valuable advice and assistance with the business issues and challenges you face every day.

I serve as your advisor and mentor and coach so that you are ready to meet with potential investors, avoid common pitfalls and can confidently pitch your deal or venture. I will also review and evaluate all term sheets, contracts and agreements and with you, make recommendations and assist you in final negotiations to insure a successful funding.
