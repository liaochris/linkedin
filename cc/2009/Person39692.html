Heather Hollick’s Summary

An IT leader and project director who excels at helping organizations challenged by significant transformations, including major projects, outsourcing, or general restructuring. Sweet spot in taming large and unruly projects with a keen ability to focus on leadership-oriented project issues that often prevent project managers from executing their plans successfully. Great at facilitating cultural changes and building engaged teams.

• More than 20 years experience in leadership positions ranging from IT to human resources to start-up operations.
• Able to build new new teams in the face of rapidly adjusting project priorities.
• Built and led multiple teams in IT, service delivery and end-user support.
• Established and delivered project portfolio management for multiple large enterprise organizations.
• Successfully delivered large-scale, multi-country, multi-site projects.
• Influence and accountability at COO and CIO levels.
• Effective at leveraging outsourcing partners to deliver increasing levels of service.
• A keen ability to influence across the organization.
• Accomplished presenter and speaker.
• Mentor and coach for many high potential people.
