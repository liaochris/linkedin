Sara Agee’s Summary

Let me be your personal search agent in your career plan! If you are an IT Professional, I'd love to hear about your career goals & become a passive source of info on job opportunities that fit the bill.

Benefits of networking with a recruiter, like me:
1. Gain access to jobs that aren't advertised.
2. Receive resume editing & interview coaching.
3. Get real feedback after every interview.
4. Enter into a vast network of Professionals & Hiring Managers in your industry.
5. Describe your dream job once, & never be bothered to look for a job again. I'll contact you when such a position comes available.
6. All of this is absolute FREE to you! I'm paid by my clients when they decide to hire a candidate whom I represent.


How to help me help you:
1. Be prepared. Have an updated version of your resume & invest some thought into what your career goals are & the timing you have in mind for making a move.
2. Be upfront. Clearly communicate your expectations on the types of positions, companies, salary ranges, etc. We take our direction from you & only inform you about opportunities that fit your description.
3. Be honest. Our job is not to screen you out! Our job is to find the perfect position for your experience, strengths & career goals.


If you'd like to explore the possibility of working together, please email me at sagee@cornerofficeconsultants.com. I look forward to making your acquaintance!
