Boris Epstein’s Summary

I am the Founder and CEO of BINC - a Professional Search Firm specializing in the placement of software professionals throughout the US (www.bincsearch.com).

It is our daily goal to help great companies connect with great people. Our clients include some of the top software companies within the world of enterprise software, web 2.0, business intelligence, digital entertainment, digital media, search, storage and gaming. Our candidates are graduates from top National and International Universities and have experience building, testing, implementing and selling the world's best known and most successful software products in the world.

If you work within the software industry and are either looking for great people or are looking for a great company, call me. The BINC Search team will always do everything in our power to help.
