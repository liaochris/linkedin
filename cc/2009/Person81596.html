Rainbowdharma International’s Summary

Thirty years of licensed counseling serving the mentally ill and developmentally disabled. Introduced to Buddhism in 1971. Two years of retreat followed by ten years of study with the abbot of the Dalai Lama's personal monastery. Accomplished teacher, musician, dramatist, author, lecturer, and filmmaker. Travel includes Thailand, Cambodia, Canada, Mexico, Netherlands, France, England, Sweden, and Estonia. Entertainment industry friendly. Competence and confidentiality always.
