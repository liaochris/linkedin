Tom Connors’s Summary

Ours is a demanding craft. One that places a premium on interaction and mutual understanding. One that requires the strictest attention to detail, superior knowledge of technology and design, and the commitment and ability to respond to everyday factors, such as budgets and deadlines, which so crucially affect the job at hand.

For almost three decades, we have helped clients communicate thoughts and dreams through precision model making. Our models and prototypes reflect the profession's highest standards. We work with a variety of materials - rigid or flexible, clear or painted - to produce visually and functionally exceptional models.

Tom Connors’s Specialties:

Visual CNC is experienced at machining: ABS, ABS-FR, Polycarbonate, GF-PC, Acetal, Delrin AF, Nylon, GF Nylon, Ultem, GF Ultem, PPS, Ryton, Rynite, Radel, Acrylic, Teflon, PBT, Noryl, GF Noryl, PEEK, PVC, Semitron, Polystyrene, Phenolic, Torlon, Polysulfone, Vespel, HDPE, PP, Xenoy, Xylex, Pattern Plank, Ren, Foam, Aluminum, Brass, Wood, and if you have any other special resins, please ask.
