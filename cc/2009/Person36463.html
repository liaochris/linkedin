Giorgio Buccilli’s Summary

I’m a consultant for EnginSoft, the Italy based engineering software and consulting company with subsidiaries in Europe and United States as Business Manager. EnginSoft grows its international operations on a sustained basis by entering new engineering markets, like the evolutionary design optimization. Our portfolio includes computer-aided engineering software and consultancy.

In 1995, after graduating I got my first real job as research fellow at Worgas, the inventor of the low emission V-shaped flame. While I was working there, I brought into the company computer aided engineering tools and methods and was awarded of patents from the US Patent and Trademark Office for low emissions domestic burners.

On early 2000, I moved to Fluent Inc. the market leading editor of fluid-dynamic software, to build sales and marketing team in Italy and to grow the business. I served Fluent (who latery became Ansys in 2006) until 2007 as Sales&Marketing Manager, increasing revenues from .5M to 4M in five years. Early in 2007 I started my own small business: mathVALUE, before starting my collaboration with EnginSoft.

I graduated in 1995 with a Master of Science in Aeronautical Engineering from the University ‘La Sapienza’ of Rome (Italy), and put a new arrow in my quiver with an executive MBA at the Politecnico di Milano in 2004.

I live in Trento with my wife, and play piano for tolerant friends, sometimes.
