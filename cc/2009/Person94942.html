Steven Knapp’s Summary

Have a high-technology product? Looking for smarter customers? We can help establish your technology as the prevailing technology. We help you to better define your product for target markets, create compelling product collateral, demonstrate the key product benefits, and teach your customers and your sales channel.

The Prevailing Technology team engages at any step of the development process. We help define, document, develop, and deploy your high-technology product or service. Specialties include FPGA design, CAE software, and embedded system applications.

Steven Knapp’s Specialties:

technical marketing, technical writing, embedded development, digital system design, product definition, FPGA, system on chip
