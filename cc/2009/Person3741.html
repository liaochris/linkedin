Alice Elliott’s Summary

By rebranding as a Visual Marketer, my new business Design Your Marketing will endeavour to educate businesses into becoming more attractive to their customers.

It's not just about how they look to the outside world (logo, corporate colours, website, literature, etc) but what kind of message you are saying, how to make your words as well as your images and layout more visual, how to encourage a response to your promotions and once your customers have crossed your threshold, how to convert them into sales and how to maintain a long term business relationship with them for the future.
