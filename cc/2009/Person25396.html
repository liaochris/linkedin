Deborah Dachis Gold’s Summary

DDG Creative is the umbrella company for the award-winning creative projects of senior-level writer and creative director, Deborah Dachis Gold. Ms. Dachis Gold’s background includes inspired content creation and direction, conceptual copywriting, and creative management of cross-functional teams. DDG is at her best when creating the story behind high level conceptual design for premium brands with a passion for embracing innovation.

Ms. Dachis Gold has been writing for the Web since 1996 and is a member of The One Club, AIGA, Ad Fed and MIMA. She is currently accepting projects directly from clients and from traditional and interactive agencies throughout North America.

Deborah Dachis Gold’s Specialties:

Branded storytelling/copywriting and the creation of original content including Webisodics, online comic series, scripting for short films and character creation and development. Creative Direction. Social Media savvy.
