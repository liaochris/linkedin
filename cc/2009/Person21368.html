Curtis Doll’s Summary

Curt had a poem entitled ‘The Lark Ascending’, published in the 2002 International Library of Poetry Anthology which was titled The Solace of Night. It was published by Watermark Press, One Poetry Plaza, Owings Mills, MD. He has been published in Stained Glass Quarterly Magazine on a set of six monumental stained glass windows he designed for Tifereth Israel in Columbus, OH using the Six Orders of the Mishna as the theme. He has also designed more than 115 crystal beveled glass clusters for MIKA International in Buffalo, NY. His most recent endeavor is partnering with MIKA International in the creation and marketing of a new line of beveled clusters. Curt designed all 53 in this line titled the 300 Series. The main sales tool they will be using is a book designed by Curt of over 553 design applications of these beveled glass clusters within door, side-lite and transom stained glass panels. Curt is currently involved in Selling, Marketing, Overseeing Business Finance and Procurement of Materials, Maintaining Corporate and Business Web Sites & Fulfilling Design Contracts for CurtisGraphics Corporation.

Curtis Doll’s Specialties:

Curt's expertise is in graphic design, fine art photography and architectural stained glass design for corporate, liturgical and residential clients, producing paintings in various mediums including pastel, oil, acrylic, gouache and watercolor as well as etching, wood cut, linoleum block and giclée fine art printmaking.
