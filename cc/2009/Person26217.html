Devious Diva’s Summary

I have worked as an actor in theatre, film and television for many years. I have also directed many theatre projects including youth programmes, special needs projects and adult education.

I am also singer with the band Bad Mathematics. We do regular gigs and are about to release a live album.

I recently had a photographic exhibition of my images of the Roma settlements that I have been covering on my blog for three years.

I am also working on a European Union funded project on the veil/hijab. My main work is with photography and video on this particular project that will culminate in a exhibition/installation in April 2009.
