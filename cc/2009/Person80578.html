Prescott Hill’s Summary

I'm an independent professional doodler, clocking over 28 years in graphic design for print, with 8 years as a Designer/Art Director at Hasbro, Inc.

I specialize in digital graphic design and illustration with a focus on the children's market for Retail, Toy, Publishing, and Entertainment Industries.

I'm Mac based and my capabilities include: logo/ID design, character design, 2D illustration, 3D illustration, graphic direction for packaging, conceptual development, managing creatives, and story boarding.

Portfolio online at www.prescotthill.com

Prescott Hill’s Specialties:

Though most of my graphic design experience is print based, I also have experience in web/interactive design, set/display design, product development, and video production.
