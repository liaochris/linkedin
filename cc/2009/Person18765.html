Chris Sisson’s Summary

Stints in Fine Art, Illustration, and Graphic Design culminated in graduate work at NIU and an MFA in Painting, leading me to Higher Education as my ultimate career path. Outside of education I continue to take on illustration work, commissioned assignments, graphic design projects, and gallery exhibitions.

Chris Sisson’s Specialties:

In Higher Education I have a range of teaching experience specifically related to my background in Graphic Design, Illustration, and Fine Art. Course topics include but are not limited to: Drawing, 2D/3D, Software, Art History, Conceptual Thinking, Advertising, Type, Art Direction, Storyboarding, Illustration, Pro Practices, & Portfolio. I have written curriculum at levels ranging from 100-400 for both the online and traditional classroom formats and am an experienced team leader in this area.
