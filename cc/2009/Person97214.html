Rodney Cheung’s Summary

Rodney Cheung is currently the Professional Services Manager at acrodex. He has been leading teams and consulting with over 17 years of experience primarily working with large and complex environments in both the service provider and large enterprise space. He specializes in Technical Architecture and Deployment, Service Development and Strategy using technical leading edge & cost-effective solutions to optimize business objectives through multiservice (data / voice / video) infrastructure, Microsoft SharePoint portals and workflows, Virtualization (VMware & Hyper-V), PerformancePoint Business Intelligence services, and Storage solutions.

Rodney has proven to be a leader at accomplishing client and company strategies with his current team of consultants in delivering customer project objectives. Rodney leads his team to complete these objectives while simultaneously fulfilling the personal needs of the employee as well as the needs of the consulting organization. Prior to acrodex, Rodney has managed a team of 35 consultants in marchFIRST, a professional services organization reaching over $7 million annual revenue for his group and demonstrated an ability to deliver on commitments to both company and clients.

His proven ability to combine his in-depth technical expertise in combination with his personal and communication skills and understanding of business requirements is one of his primary critical success factors with both strategic and tactical initiatives.

Rodney holds a Bachelors of Applied Science degree in Systems Design Engineering from the University of Waterloo. He is a Cisco Certified Internetwork Expert (CCIE) and Cisco Certified Design Professional (CCDP) and licenced Professional Engineer (P.Eng)
