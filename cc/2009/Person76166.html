Otis Collier’s Summary

My name is Otis Collier and I am a Personal Branding Coach. I help career minded individuals, business owners and entrepreneurs become famous in their field of interest. This often results in clients experiencing better job offers, higher salaries, increased sales and paid speaking engagements.

This is achieved through the implementation of viral internet marketing campaigns and the utilization of advance Web 2.0 technologies. Each client is taught how to create, build and protect their personal brand. The end result is a satisfied client who has become a highly recognized authority figure.

Otis Collier’s Specialties:

personal brand marketing, career counseling, performance coaching, salary negotiations, job hunting seminars, public speaking, internet marketing, employment selection, sourcing, interviewing, cold calling, networking and diversity recruiting.
