Diana L. Ford’s Summary

Diana L. Ford is a Choreographer/Dancer/Media Entrepreneur, and she works in association DLF Productions & Company. She is a high school graduate from the School for the Creative & Performing Arts. She has a bachelor’s degree in Communications–Radio/Television from Northern Kentucky University. As a former employee of N.K.U., Diana has more than 10 years of media experience from working in the I.T.– Media Production & Distribution Division, where she was a Media Coordinator. Ms. Ford has a wide range of Theater & Media experience, both onstage & offstage, including working as a Production Assistant with Channel 12/WKRC, & performing in shows with the Cincinnati Children’s Theater. She also choreographed 1 version of the musical “100 Years of Divas in American Music…” & performed in this production within various capacities. She has danced in multiple Rhythm & Motion demonstrations throughout Cincinnati under the direction of Heather Britt. In addition,Diana teaches her JUST DANCE Class at the Contemporary Dance Theater (CDT) & Urban Active Fitness.

Ms. Ford choreographed the thought provoking 20 min. piece called “FREE AT LAST?!?” & Part 2 of that called “LAND OF CONFUSION…” which included different forms of edited audio,music & video. “FREE AT LAST?!?” premiered in the Performance & Time Arts Series (PTA) at CDT in March 2006, & “LAND OF CONFUSION…” debuted at The Madisonville Arts Center (M.A.C.) in May 2008.“The Good, The Bad & The Evil–Angels vs. Demons” is just a continuation of these previous pieces of work, & it recently premiered at CDT in the PTA,and continued running through the 2009 Cincinnati Fringe Festival on Gabriel’s Corner. All of these performances are connected,& they have their own significant poignant themes. Furthermore, Diana directed,produced & choreographed her own dance,poetry & music production in 2007 called *The Woman Within (Letting Go…)*for the 20/20 Festival. She has a very broad singing & acting background,and she writes as well.
