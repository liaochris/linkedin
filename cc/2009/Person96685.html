About THT Design:

THT Design is a website design and development company based in Portland Oregon. We love creating attractive, custom sites with simple, clean code. Whether you are an entrepreneur taking a leap into a new business, or you are looking to refresh your business's look, we cover the gamut of sites for your varying needs. Learn more about us: http://thtdesign.net


Why Choose THT Design:

Our focus is on ease. We setup easy-to-use sites that require simple maintenance and are a fun...not a chore. Learn more about why you might like working with us: http://thtdesign.net


About Tamara Hill-Tanquist:

In 2005, Tamara Hill-Tanquist founded THT Design. She has taught web design and programming at Pacific University and currently serves as the Green Team Committee Director for AIGA Portland. The Green Team Committee is researching graphic design-related sustainability issues. Tamara studied fine art and web design at the University of Oregon and graduated with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Multimedia Design. In her spare time, Tamara enjoys hiking, photography and throwing a Frisbee with her husband, Andy.
