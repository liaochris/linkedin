Margo Pinkerton’s Summary

Arnie Zann and Margo Pinkerton are a seasoned, award-winning photographer team with many years of experience making beautiful images for clients worldwide. Photography is their life and their passion, and they have been privileged to work with many wonderful people in Fortune 100 and 500 corporations as well as editorial clients.

Their track record of solving photography location challenges along with their knack of putting their subjects and clients at ease allows everyone to have as much fun on a photography assignment as they do.

Zann and Pinkerton have photographed everything from presidential campaigns to manufacturing, from top-secret aviation technology to wine collections, from high-tech and bio-tech to elegant portraits, and just about everything in between.

Margo Pinkerton’s Specialties:

Photographers Zann and Pinkerton are known for their magic of light that is eye-catching, a necessity for those who want to call attention to their company and services. Their experience includes photography in the following areas: CEO portraits, environmental portraits, aviation, high-tech, bio-tech (including clean rooms), corporate, R&D and manufacturing, medical, industrial, travel, architecture, energy, and even a jazz singer's CD cover.
