Scott Wolfe’s Summary

Construction attorney in Louisiana and Washington states, raised in a family of general contractors. I focus my practice on assisting contractors and those in the construction industry with contract negotiation, contract drafting, liens, construction disputes, construction claims and more. We also represent individuals in construction accidents or in defects claims against builders.

Named as a Leader in Law by New Orleans City Business Magazine in 2006, 2007 and 2008.
