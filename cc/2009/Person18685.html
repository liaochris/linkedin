Chris Brown’s Summary

Interstrat, an Australia-based firm, and Gallagher Management, a US-based firm, merged in September 2007. The new firm is named MarketCulture Strategies and has offices in Sydney, San Francisco, Boston, and Paris.

The combination of these two firms will provide corporate clients with a global solution for increasing profitability by benchmarking and enhancing their marketing skill sets and corporate culture.

MarketCulture Strategies is performing cutting-edge research on what makes successful companies successful. In scores of studies we have found companies that are consistently successful approach the market differently than laggards. We have also found that the traits found in successful firms can be measured, benchmarked, and improved to create a competitive advantage for our clients. We call these traits The DNA of Profitable Companies™.

Contact me. I am happy to share our research that has revealed The DNA of Profitable Companies

Experienced Sales and Marketing Executive and Practioner with extensive experience in the Technology and Telecommunication Industries.
Experienced in-house customised performance improvement program faciliator and educator in the area of marketing, sales and associated functions.
