    (Privately Held; Real Estate industry)

    December 2003 — Present (6 years )

    Since founding Bean Group in late 2003 the company has grown to become the largest independent real estate brokerage in New Hampshire, As of September 2009 we have eight offices and over 175 REALTORS, brokers and employees. Our newest office is located in Franklin, NH.

    Our success is the result of marrying professional, motivated real estate sales people with the best website and technology platform in a great brokerage environment that agents are enthusiastic about being a part of.

    FOR REALTORS IN NH, ME & VT:

    Real estate sales professionals don't have to "be high-tech" to be successful Bean Group agents. Allow us to augment your real estate sales expertise with our technical expertise and watch your sales grow- for a confidential discussion contact me directly or visit our careers section at beangroup.com.

    FOR NH, ME & VT BROKERAGE FIRMS & BROKER / OWNERS:

    Bean Group is looking to expand our brokerage operations in New Hampshire and we are actively seeking potential real estate brokerage merger or acquisition candidates. Ideal merger or acquisition candidates are fundamentally well run independent brokerage operations that are struggling with the technological upheavals that are driving the real estate brokerage industry today. Interested parties can feel free to contact me here or through the Bean Group website. All inquiries are in confidence and discretion is assured.
