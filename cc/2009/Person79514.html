Phil Trumbo’s Summary

An experienced creative visualist in film, TV and interactive media; Trumbo has provided creative and artistic direction to nearly one hundred high-profile games, with retail sales of over a billion dollars. He guided the creative and artistic development of Amaze Entertainment from startup to it’s merger with Foundation 9 Entertainment, creating the world’s largest independent game developer.

Many of his of his notable successes come from adapting prestigious film and television properties into highly successful games, including- Pirates of the Caribbean™, Chronicles of Narnia™, Lord of the Rings™, Harry Potter ™”, Shrek™, “Spiderman™, The Sims™, Star Wars™, Lego™, Finding Nemo™, “X-Men™ and Ice Age™. He has been instrumental in recruiting top creative talent as well as creating opportunities for gifted entry-level artists.

Phil won an Emmy Award for directing the opening title sequence for CBS's Pee Wee's Playhouse. He's also served as director, designer and animator for clients, including- Electronic Arts, Activision Inc., Buena Vista Games, Vivendi Universal Games,NBC, ABC, CBS, Proctor and Gamble, General Foods, Ralston Purina, General Mills, Nickelodeon and MTV.
