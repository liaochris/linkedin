Nichola Burton’s Summary

As the founder of The Pushworth Group, the largest Entertainment Service Company in Queensland, I am responsible for the Service, Art and Education of all infrastructure, operations, systems and programs. I am the author and trainer of all Pushworth Service Packages for Hospitality, Corporate, Government, Private, and Retail, Community and Industry clients. The business of Performance is expression, freedom and creation. The performance of Business is order, system and form. As a qualified Front Line Manager and a Certified Trainer, I educate, faciliate and integrate a profitable synthesis of these two for every enterprise in a sustainable holistic responsibile fashion.

In my new enterprise, How Good Is This!, I provide personal and professional services to Individuals and Businesses in the facilitation of programs, seminars and one on one consultation sessions.
