Matt Haikin’s Summary

I am an IT/web-strategist with nearly 30 years experience across all sectors, in large and small organisations, and spanning a range of subjects.including Diversity, Online Recruitment, Regeneration, Employment, Vocational Training, E-Learning, Social Networks/Online Communities, Web Development and many more.

I also run London's only diversity job board (Fuse Jobs - http://www.FuseJobs.co.uk), specialising junior roles, casual work and traineeships.

Always interested in discussing possible opportunities - personal freelance work or business partnership suggestions for Fuse Jobs.
