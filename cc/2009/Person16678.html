Chad Griffeth, AIF’s Summary

Co-Founder of Actium LLC and key architect in design of 401(k)/403(b) employee account management (BeManaged) and investment advice (BeAdvised) solutions. Operating as Fiduciary Adviser (PPA), our flagship program, BeManaged, provides participants professional, institutional account management, 4-6 personalized updates per year, and a Personal Annual Review to demonstrate performance, all on a fee-for-service basis. Providing participants the "answer" they want via free 401(k) consultations has been found to be a clear, independent solution for paternalistic companies concerned about their employees' asset allocation and ability to manage risk appropriately. Have been quoted recently by Marketwatch.com and a panelist speaker at the '09 Fiduciary360 and CFDD national conferences regarding the delivery of prudent, fiduciary participant advice.

Chad Griffeth, AIF’s Specialties:

401(k), ERISA, PPA, 401(k) account management and investment advice provisions/services, independent fiduciaries, investment fiduciaries, investor behavior.
