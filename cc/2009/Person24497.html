David Norman’s Summary

I have an overwhelming desire to see church done in a new way that will reach those who have never been reached before. This has been a resonating passion of mine and a theme in all my service. I feel called to serve a church that shares this passion with any or all of my talents and experience, including leading worship, preaching and creating Bible series and studies, connecting with youth and adults, and even web design. I trust God to lead me to this church and am excited at His plans for my future.
