Stuart Edeal’s Summary

I love the quote from a management book I just read: "Never stop learning, develop strong relationships and embrace change; this is a field that is constantly changing."

I am a 'jack of all trades - master of none', and I am fine with that. It allows me the flexibility to feel comfortable with any situation and the ability know when I don't have the answer and the ambition to find it!

When all is said and done, I'm a gadget guy. I established my professional reputation as a software support engineer in the 90s. Now I am involved in many fields: technology consultant, business analyst, entrepreneur, writer and solutions architect.
