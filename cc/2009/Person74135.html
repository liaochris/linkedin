Nicola Mattina’s Summary

I am a management consultant with a strong experience in the field of corporate communication and marketing. I had the opportunity to delve into several specialties: public relations, internal communication, branding, online communication.
In the last years, I specialized in the creation and government of social network e I actively study the impact of social media (blog, social network, wiki, etc.) on people, companies and institutions.
I write about these topics in my blog and on Nova 24 (Il Sole 24 Ore) and I am invited to conferences and seminars as speaker and teacher.

Nicola Mattina’s Specialties:

Management consulting, corporate communication, public relations, branding and identity, network weaving, social media governance, social software, user experience, web design.
