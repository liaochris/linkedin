Gopi Sangha’s Summary

I'm an independent individual who has a visionary perspective of pop-culture & entertainment in the 21st century, equipped with the know-how and skill sets to pragmatically realize the business potential of an ever changing, and growingly complex globalized culture. Creativity, research, know-how & hard work have allowed me to continuously inspire, innovate, and push the expectations from myself, my employers & my clients. Between non-profit organizing for Hip Hop Congress, founding & creating the HEAT Music Festival, weekly nightlife promotions, artist development & running a thriving music blog, I have learned and developed a very broad set of skills, experience, & networking necessary for successful entertainment related business ventures & opportunities.

  • Founder & Producer, HEAT Festival

    University of California, Riverside

    (Educational Institution; Higher Education industry)

    October 2006 — June 2008 (1 year 9 months)

    Founded and produced the HEAT Festival at U.C. Riverside in its monumental first two year's of operation. I led a team which brainstormed the event, prepared budgets, booked talent, designed, marketed, produced & executed the festival which attracted 6,000 guests in its first year and 10,500 in its second year.

    In its second year the HEAT Festival featured 3 concurrent stages, two of which under massive festival tenting, and a lineup composing of: 311, Mos Def, Moby, Girl Talk, The Clipse, Circa Survive, Lies In Disguise, Afrobots, Monte Negro, Aloe Blacc, DJ Low B, L.A. Riots, Villians, & DJ Morsy.

    The festival has gone on to become the most successful event in U.C. Riverside's history, widely noted throughout college-entertainment industry & has quickly been adopted as U.C. Riverside's premier annual tradition.
