Damon Di Placido’s Summary

Established in 1920, Colliers Lanard & Axilbund is the oldest full-service commercial real estate provider in the tri-state area. For over 88 years, we have applied our knowledge and expertise to deliver comprehensive solutions to clients who buy, sell, lease, develop and invest in commercial real estate.

Today, we proudly offer a full range of services in retail, office, industrial & investment brokerage, property & asset management, construction, finance & appraisal, and consulting, non profit & corporate solutions. Our experienced professionals possess an extensive knowledge of the region and the industries in which they specialize. This knowledge is enhanced by our in-house team of experts in market research, law, finance, technology and marketing. And, thanks to our partnership with Colliers International, our clients also benefit from the added resources of a global organization.

Our presence is global. Our regional history is unparalleled. And, as a result of exciting new technology, global communication, and original marketing strategies, Colliers Lanard & Axilbund continues to be an innovator in the commercial real estate marketplace.
