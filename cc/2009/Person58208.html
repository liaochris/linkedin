    U.S. Green Real Estate Council

    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    May 2008 — Present (1 year 3 months)

    I am a Director of the U.S Green Real Estate Council (USGREC). USGREC is a voluntary real estate professional membership organization dedicated to educating and certifying its members to promote green, sustainable and energy efficient building practices for new and retrofitted construction of commercial and residential buildings in the United States to reduce the effects of global warming.

    (Non-Profit; 1-10 employees; Renewables & Environment industry)

    June 2006 — Present (3 years 2 months)

    I am the President & Executive Director of GreenMotion, a non-profit organization, which I founded in 2006. Committed to my vision to promote sustainable environmental development, I became an architect of an expedition that will lead me across the United States and Canada, in an alternative fueled vehicle in May 2009. I will be working with a highly experienced Advisory Board, leaders from key community sectors, international organizations and governments to create new renewable energy strategies. I am engaged in an unprecedented educational campaign to communicate the urgent and global need and best practices for renewable energies worldwide.
