Edward Williams’s Summary

Cold Closing- the art of making the connection; is an innovative sales methodology to increase sales performance, motivation and results through sales development efforts such as cold calling, email marketing and social media networking.

The purpose of Cold Closing is to bring about a shift from traditional negative thinking related to cold calling and instrusive sales development practices.

Our training method/ process focuses on leveraging these essential components:
- communication model
- active listening and critical questioning
- personal belief and faith
- persistent/ consistent action habit with right expectation

These principles are not unique because effective communication skills are taught through many sales training and process resources. However, Cold Closing accomplishes distinguishable results due to a primary focus on attitude enrichment; then mechanics and skills development.

Ultimatey, Cold Closing is a product of much wisdom and practice developed by great men and women who have paved a path that I can only hope to appropriately represent and follow.
