Jean Lloyd’s Summary

My job at Solid Cactus is to get the most from our clients' Pay Per Click accounts across Google, Yahoo and MSN. I analyze the client's site and then work with them to find the keywords that will draw qualified traffic to their site. I enjoy crafting ad text that captures the reader's attention and draws them in to our clients' sites to learn more.

I was a writing major in college and I enjoy the challenge that writing PPC ads creates. Tech writing is different than other kinds of writing. It's more concise than creative. I love the challenge and have been able to develop several successful campaigns for clients in industries as diverse as jewelry, children's clothing & cell phone accessories.

Solid Cactus hired me as a tech writer right out of college. I was doing all the setup but none of the managing, which was the logical next step. Now I communicate directly with clients, managing their accounts and helping them take their online advertising up to the next level. I also get to work with my team on their challenges and help shape the future of the PPC department. It's exciting coming in to work every day. There's always a new challenge to overcome with PPC!
