Tom Wambeke’s Summary

I’m a project manager in innovative educational technology. I'm specialized in giving guidance and consultancy in methodology for flexible individual and competence based learning projects in adult education and vocational training. This consists of:
- instructional design & methodology
- instructive and user-friendly multimedia for adult learners in distance education
- e-learning, blended learning and workplace learning.

My professional interests focus as well on Web2.0 developments, social software, digital didactics, life long learning, knowledge management, learning standards, computer supported collaborative learning, distance education and in-service teacher training.

Tom Wambeke’s Specialties:

Currently working with Zope/Plone, Moodle and Joomla (CMS/LCMS) but have experience with other systems as well (Blackboard, Drupal). Learning objects are produced with ExE-learning, Adobe Captivate, Camtasia and Articulate. Communication and collaboration is done by a wide arrange of social software (web2.0). For any other aspect of instructional design () we have either off the shelf tools or look for integration with the LCMS we are using.
