Will Christiansen’s Summary

I am currently an Animator at Bungie in Kirkland, WA, before that I was an Animator at Multiverse in Mountain View, CA, working on in-game animations (cycles, idles, player actions, etc).

Before that I worked at Factor 5 LLC in San Rafael, CA where I animated dragons and knights for Lair's Downloadable Content; I also provided animation for some un-announced projects on the PS3 and Wii consoles.
