  • Founder

    SeeZam S.A

    (Internet industry)

    March 2009 — Present (9 months)

    www.SeeZam.com is an online safe facility offering the same capabilities as a social network. SeeZam is an efficient way of centralising your personal and confidential information, which you can then classify into 3 categories, each offering different levels of security:
    - Public : you can share certain information with everyone
    - Confidential : access is restricted to certain named persons – Premium Account
    - Secret : information can only be accessed by 2 trusted persons in the event of your death – Optimum Account
    If the worst comes to pass, SeeZam offers a secure online solution allowing your loved ones access to your instructions without having to hunt down documents or guess your last wishes.
