Annette Huygens-Tholen’s Summary

As a Sydney 2000 Olympian, Annette knows what it takes to excel and to achieve goals. She also understands the difficulties in transitioning in life beyond sport. It's not easy without guidance and new direction. After studying the mindset of champions and gaining qualification as a Master Results Coach and NLP Practitioner. Annette now realises she can achieve so much more and is going for GOLD in life. She is determined to help others tap into their unlimited potential through one-on-one personal coaching, group workshops, teleseminars, books and e-books.

Annette specialises in developing the mindset of a champion beyond the sports field. She helps clients discover their inner resources, access positive emotional states and create empowering beliefs to set them on their path towards their desired goals and dreams.

Annette has now joined one of the leading transformational and training organisations, Christopher Howard Companies, to teach anyone and everyone the tools and techniques that can help them live the life of their dreams.

She shares her time between Australia and a new home base in Maui, Hawaii.

She will motivate and inspire you to be your best.
