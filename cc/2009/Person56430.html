Krishanna Spencer’s Summary

A creative force to be reckoned with an eclectic array of talents and abilities, my ventures include writing, fine art and craft,tech, social media and community. Currently, my passion for connecting like-minded creative people and communities is indulged as the community evangelist of Active Gray Matter and Gallery Geek and Artist-In-Residence at 12 12 Gallery. When I'm not geeking around on Creative Wonk, I share my innate affinity to create by blending words, imagery, soul, and life into works of art and jewelry at krishanna | dot | com.
