Jeri Denniston’s Summary

Do you and your team have a clear vision of where your business is headed and how they contribute to that? I am a strategic marketing and social media strategist who helps clients apply a holistic approach to planning, marketing, and problem solving, ensuring everyone on the team understands their role in supporting organizational goals.

Social media networking provides a powerful system to promote your business, create visibility and generate response. These tools are critical components of a 21st Century strategic marketing plan that supports the organization's overall strategic objectives.

My more than 20 years of experience spans all facets of marketing and management, including social media networking, cause-related marketing, product and market development, business and strategic planning, public relations, advertising, creative services, and community relations.

Jeri Denniston’s Experience

  • Partner in Strategic Marketing & Management

    Haines Centre for Strategic Management

    (Management Consulting industry)

    December 2006 — Present (2 years 8 months)

    Strategic Thinking, Marketing, Leadership and Change Management training using Systems Thinking.

    We are interpretors and translators of best practices research in strategic thinking, planning and management.

    Systems Thinking is about thinking, planning and acting strategically on a daily basis to achieve your desired results. You start with your future desired outcomes and work backwards to identify the strategies necessary to close the gap from today to that future vision. This is different from analytical thinking (how most US people are taught) which starts with today and tries to problem-solve solutions, with no roadmap of where you're headed, just the elimination of the problem.

  • Partner / Social Media Strategist

    Denner Group International

    (Management Consulting industry)

    October 2005 — Present (3 years 10 months)

    As the world of marketing has changed, I have begun integrating social networking into my marketing strategies, using the latest Web 2.0 tools such as Twitter, LinkedIn, Facebook, FriendFeed, and more.

    I am passionate about helping business owners and their teams integrate sound marketing strategies as part of their overall strategic planning process. Using Systems Thinking as the foundation for the marketing and planning process, is a key factor in setting yourself apart from your competition.

    If you look at your business as a whole system comprised of many components that work together to achieve a common goal, you’ll see that marketing is one of those systems. For the business to function successfully and smoothly, everyone must be on the same page in terms of how they support the organizational strategies, marketing included.
