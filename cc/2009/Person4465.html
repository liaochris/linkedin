Amber Leonard-Dixon’s Summary

Amber M. Dixon is the Recruiting and Marketing Manager for Intermountain Financial Group/MassMutual. She has over 10 years of experience in the Marketing/PR arena with an eclectic background. Her experience ranges from an Account Representative with a Public Relations firm to a Marketing Manager with a High-Tech firm. Amber has been with the Agency going on 7 years.

Amber assists all MassMutual financial professionals and the Agency in the Utah area with their marketing and public relations initiatives and also is the manager of recruiting to bring on new talent for this powerful career.

MassMutual, a fortune 100 company, offers unique professional opportunities for individuals interested in a career that brings tremendous personal satisfaction, independence and financial rewards. In addition, you'll be provided with customized training, comprehensive support and a competitive benefits package.

We are committed to your personal and professional development. Whether you're looking for new avenues to expand your existing product line, seeking to balance work and your personal life, or looking to maximize your career potential, we invite you to explore the exciting range of national career opportunities.
