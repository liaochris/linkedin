Judi Lowenthal’s Summary

I have been providing professional mobile notary services to the Community throughout Orange County since 2003, for private parties, wealth management and private equity firms, corporations, law firms, banks, escrow and title companies, high profile executives, celebrities, and for those who may be bed-ridden, sick, or just too busy to get away (or behind bars!). Services are provided where most convenient for the signers, i.e., offices, homes, hospitals, senior centers, jails, hotels, coffeehouses, and even tanning salons, horse stables, and a bowling alley lounge, to name a few. My motto is “whenever and wherever I am needed” and I truly am available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, as well as responding to last-minute calls.

My background includes many years as a legal assistant and executive assistant. With my extensive administrative skills, and a passion for working with and helping people of all walks of life, I realized this would be a business I would really enjoy and could be successful at.

I have also mentored many other notaries to help them have success in the notary business, offering help over the phone, via e-mail, as well as taking new notaries on signings with me so they can see first-hand how to conduct a signing, and feel more comfortable going out on their own. Yes, they are my competition; however, I really enjoy helping and teaching others and seeing people succeed in their own business, too.

Judi Lowenthal’s Specialties:

Notary Public commissioned to perform general notarizations of signatures on all documents as legally allowed for estate planning and adoptions, Deeds, Powers of Attorney, letters, agreements, insurance and real estate documents.

Certified Loan Signing Agent, assisting Borrowers signing loan documents.
