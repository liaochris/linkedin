Eric De Carufel’s Summary

Eric De Carufel is a MVP C# and a .NET solution architect for Orckestra in Montreal. He is a specialist in application development with Miscrodoft .NET framework. He has more than fifteen years of experience in software design in various domain as transportation, food store, insurance, engineering, finance and electronic business.
