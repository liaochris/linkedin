Nick Tapalansky’s Summary

My first published project, AWAKENING, is currently being released bi-monthly by Archaia Studios Press and has been critically well received. I have numerous other projects in various stages of development currently being placed with publishers.

As a writer and editor, I'm seeking to meet and work with as many publishers, editors, and creators as possible to best place my projects and facilitate growth in these fields.
