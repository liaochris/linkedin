Hueina Su’s Summary

Hueina@BeyondHorizonCoaching.com
Open Networking Accepted

Hueina Su, MS, BSN, CEC, The Nurturer's Coach™, is a Coach, Keynote Speaker, Author, and Founder of Beyond Horizon Coaching, specializing in coaching & training medical professionals and other women "Nurturers". Through private coaching, keynotes and workshops, Hueina provides powerful solutions for Stress Management, Life Balance, and Intensive Self-Care. She has 20+ years of experience in nursing, counseling, coaching, training, nonprofit management, small business and sales.


KEYNOTE SPEAKING

Hueina is a keynote speaker for government agencies, nonprofit, medical, nursing & other professional associations and conferences. Topics focus on stress management, work life balance, caring for the caregivers, and women's empowerment.

Video demo at www.NJNurseSpeaker.com


BOOK

"Intensive Care for the Nurturer's Soul: 7 Keys to Nurture Yourself While Caring for Others" is an interactive guidebook for women nurturers who would like to create more balance, joy and time for themselves. Terri Levine, The Guru of Coaching and best-selling author calls it "a must read for anyone who is in the role of caregiver."

FREE chapters at www.IntensiveSelfCareBook.com


CREDENTIALS & TRAINING

Certified by iPEC Coaching, one of world's foremost ICF accredited coaching schools. Master of Science in Family Studies, Bachelor of Science in Nursing.

Other training: Landmark Forum, Landmark Self Expression and Leadership Program (SELP), iPEC Life Potentials Training, Guided Imagery, Therapeutic Essential Oils (Aromatherapy).
