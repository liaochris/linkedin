Marjorie Price’s Summary

Psychiatric and community health nurse clinician, certified hypnotherapist, Reiki Master/Teacher and certified medium who extensively and originally practices hands-on healing, alternative and western medicine, grief counseling, women's empowerment and spirituality, recovery and regression therapies, and offers a uniquely integrative strategy for a development and empowerment health philosophy. Services include but are not limited to energetic healing, past-life regression, Reiki, hypnotherapy, therapeutic journaling, intuitive and spiritual development, shamanic and archetypal healing, dream work, meditation, relaxation, visualization, conflict resolution and goal achievement. Additional specialties include related workshop and small group intensives including women's self-healing and spirituality groups, holistic and new leadership skills training for health care providers. Individual, couples, groups, classes, and related products for empowerment, development, healing and achievement.
