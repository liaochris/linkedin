Dave Barger’s Summary

Website Design Memphis / Strategy
In the 13th year of developing and executing Web strategies critical to our clients' organizational goals. Previously served consumer product development and improvement through Reliability Engineering and Voice of the Customer type Marketing practices.

Social Media Marketing / Social Networking:
It's a great pleasure to share the tools being leveraged by citizens and consumers, and to train business people about Social Media Marketing. To this end, LunaWeb has organized the Social Media Breakfast - Memphis to foster Social Media and Networking in the economic sphere. Our 9th Breakfast will be held December 3rd. Ask me about it.

BarCamp Memphis:
With LaunchMemphis, awesome Planners and sponsors, we just experienced BarCampMemphis with nearly 100 people on November 8th. It was a dream come true in community building and 2.0 geekery. Visit www.BarCampMemphis.com for info, photos, and other related media.

The economy is increasingly becoming a conversational community and we are driven to engage Memphis in the leading waves of this new paradigm. I am available for speaking engagements.

LaunchMemphis:
As a founding member of LaunchMemphis I enjoy being a part of an enthusiastic group assembled to ignite and foster entrepreneurship in Memphis.
