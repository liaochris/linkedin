Ignacio Paredes’s Summary

Ignacio Paredes has a M.S. in Computer Science and works as security consultant for Grupo Intermark. Since 1999 he has been involved in different projects related to information security for important enterprises mainly from the telecommunications field. He is an expert in the design and deployment of technical and administrative security solutions, incluiding topics such as applications security, secure network design or business continuity planning.
