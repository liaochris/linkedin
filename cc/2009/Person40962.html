Ken Smith’s Summary

Ken Smith uses the Internet, imaging technology, clear writing, journalistic savvy, public relations experience and webmaster and online database skills to build communities of interest among diverse populations of stakeholders. He chooses which media to use based on the needs of the sender, the key or priority message, and the preferences and technology capabilities of the receiver.
