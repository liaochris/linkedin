Peter Stinson’s Summary

An accomplished trainer, writer, teacher, and coach, I currently serve as an organizational performance consultant with the United States Coast Guard. Assigned to the Hampton Roads' based Atlantic Area, my primary focus is on the implementation of the Criteria for Performance Excellence, the Coast Guard's leadership and management framework based on the internationally recognized Baldrige Criteria. I work with leaders at all levels of the Coast Guard to assist them in implementing processes and systems which support the Criteria.
