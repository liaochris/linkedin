Tony Baumer’s Summary

In 1989 I started BD Movers. We handle household, office and commercial moves. Local to NJ, Interstate and International.

The Bullet Points:
Firm Pricing, No casual labor. That means no surprises on moving day and no scary people in your house/office

Our trademark is "Faith can move mountains, for everything else there's BD Movers" (tm)

I am open to all connections in the NJ/Philly/NY area tony@bdmovers.com

Tony Baumer’s Specialties:

Senior Moves-We handle all needs from move/pack/cleaning and work with elder lawyers, professional organizers, etc.

Office Moving with innovative systems-Crate and dolly system to greatly speed up moving.

International Moves-We work with shippers to find the best options. We work with relocation companies to provide local labor and professional container pack and load. Survey and inventory service available.
