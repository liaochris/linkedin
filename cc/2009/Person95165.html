Steve Shah’s Summary

- Marketing and product strategy executive focused on enterprise-class technology.

- 17 years of industry experience primarily in startup to mid-sized organizations.

- Co-founder of a telephony startup differentiating with machine learning technology.

- Principal of a strategic marketing consultancy with S&P 500 clientele.

- Prominent role in the Application Delivery Controller (ADC, L4-7 switching) market having been a member of the core teams at Array Networks (IPO) and NetScaler (acquired by Citrix, $320M).

- Product strategy / product management leadership at NetScaler helped grow revenues from $10M to $100M and move the product from niche to leadership in the Gartner MQ.

- Grew multiple products to leadership positions in revenue and vision.

- Significant market expertise in Application Delivery Controllers (ADC, L4-7 switching), Network Security (SSL-VPN, Application Firewalls, AAA), Linux/Open Source, and IVR.

- Strong knack for connecting business value to technology details. Often pulled into significant accounts to present product vision.

- Deep background in network software development, operating systems, and IT. Very credible in business and technical contexts to both customers and engineering.

- Significant product expertise in application networking (network/server load balancing, web acceleration), enterprise security (firewalls, VPN, IDS/IDP, application firewall, AAA), Linux/Open Source, and systems and network administration.

- Author of multiple books on Linux systems administration with over 70,000 copies sold.

Steve currently serves on the advisory board of MiraLink, StrangeLoop Networks, and Fiji Systems.

Steve Shah’s Specialties:

Consulting, CTO, Product Vision, Product Strategy, Product management, Product marketing, Strategic Planning, M&A, Datacenter Networking, Content Networking (L4-7 switching, Server Load Balancing [SLB], SSL, Compression, Caching), Application and Network Security, SSL-VPN, Linux, Open Source, and systems/network architecture
