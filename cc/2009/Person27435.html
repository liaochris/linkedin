Don Coorough’s Summary

1976/77 - Murakami Wolf Films: Production Assistant; "The Extraordinary Adventures of the Mouse and His Child," "Puff the Magic Dragon" and commercials.

1978/79 - Lisberger Studios: Production Manager; "Animalympics."

1979/80 - Spungbuggy Works: Head of Animation; commercials, also directed mattes for 1980 titles to "Magnum PI."

1981 - Kinney Vallas: Production Manager: Final Checker; commercials and "Stanley the Ugly Duckling."

1979/1981 and 1983/1987 - wrote songs for and performed in bands on "new wave" club circuit. Song "They Died Young" recorded by The Tooners, available on iTunes.

1989 - obtained paralegal certificate.

1990 - Flagstaff, AZ - paralegal in Criminal, Personal Injury, Family, Business, and Bankruptcy.

1996 - Baer Animation: Production Manager; "Rodeo" for The Fremont Street Experience, educational CD Rom "Connie and Bonnie's Birthday Blast Off", TV special "Annabelle's Wish", and commercials; Animation Director for 1998 Discovery Channel Eco-Challenge.

1999/2000 - Log Home Design Ideas: freelance writer; articles for magazine. Triscari Productions: ghost writer; pilot teleplay and series proposal of a sitcom titled "Dumbels."

2006/2007 - Alliance for the Unification of Mankind (AUM) Publishing Company: wrote, edited, self-published and distributed three editions of chapbook, "A Critical Mass and Other Poems."

2007 - Poem "Parched Lips" published 10/07 by edificeWRECKED.

2008 - Two poems published 3/1/08 by Blood Moon Rising Magazine. Eight poems published 3/3/08 by Eleventh Transmission. Five poems published 6/18/08 by Blood Moon Rising Magazine.

2009 - Cartier Street Review, published two poems on 2/1/09. Two articles and four poems published by TucsonHomeless.org in June and July of 2009. Article "Combating Extremism in Pakistan and Afghanistan" published in July of 2009 by The Glasgow Review. One poem and two articles published by Osprey Journal on 8/14/09.

Don Coorough’s Specialties:

Poet for 40 years, 15 years in animation - managing all aspects of production and post-production (with experience assisting in pre-production as well), 27 years in music (performed in bands and solo in clubs and concerts as lead guitarist, rhythm guitarist, singer/songwriter/guitarist, and bass player), 27 years as songwriter/composer ("They Died Young" recorded by The Tooners on their CD "Rocktasia" and available on iTunes), 10 years in law as a paralegal, 8 years as a freelance writer.
