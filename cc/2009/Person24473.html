David Music’s Summary

As a Director on the NKF Consulting team, I have responsibilities to support our many services across all business types. Our approach is to look at and understand real estate and operational issues from three perspectives: location, function and on-going management. Without understanding the processes that drive real estate needs it is impossible to actually develop an effective portfolio strategy.

Data/Business Intelligence – Use QlikView to develop processes and applications to create visibility to disparate data sets across organizations. Develop, maintain and update custom demo applications for use in broad applications and industry silos (Sourcing/Procurement, Healthcare, Law Firms, Supply Chain, Real Estate, Government).

Location Optimization – Evaluate client criteria for facility locations and align that with local qualities to identify zones of opportunity to relocate or expand operations. Use industry standard data sets to evaluate demographics, psychographics and geographical features.

Supply Chain/Logistics – Perform Supply Chain evaluations for large clients. Evaluate inventory positions, model transportation costs for location optimization (using LogicNet), study labor management practices and recommend strategies, design and budget warehouse layouts for implementation.

Workplace Strategies – Evaluate and improve office work functions through the use of various analytical tools including Activity Based Costing, Lean and Six Sigma. Work with a team of architects and designers to develop comprehensive strategy.

Portfolio Strategies – Work as part of a team to integrate business process and space efficiency practices. Combine and standardize broad processes across a portfolio to increase utilization and reduce costs.

Sales Support – Present at outside seminars, developed and maintained a company blog, develop client presentations.
