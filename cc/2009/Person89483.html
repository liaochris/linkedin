Scott Golas’s Summary

Scott Golas leads the workforce development, administration, real estate and several business development efforts at Centro. Scott joined Centro after Booz Allen Hamilton, where he focused on strategy and organizational design with technology clients. While at Booz Allen, Scott worked closely with clients who wanted to transform their organizations and their industries by applying strategic technology and operations changes.

Prior to joining Booz Allen, Scott was a Managing Consultant for PA Consulting. Before joining PA Consulting, he was V.P. & Global Outsourcing Director with NelsonHall, a business process research firm. he provided consulting to both the service provider and buy-side communities.

Prior to joining NelsonHall, Scott was the Chief Strategy Officer and Portfolio Executive with Aon Consulting, based in Chicago. In this role, he developed operations, sales and marketing strategies; and developed teams comprising Aon’s practice areas and select technology and solutions providers. He had built a similar, strategic team during his four years at PricewaterhouseCoopers, where he was the HRM (Human Resources Management) consulting practice leader for PWC’s West Region.

Scott was selected as one of the HRO Today Magazine “Superstars of HR Outsourcing,” and Human Resource Executive Magazine “Rising Stars of HR,” and was a PricewaterhouseCoopers HR Innovation Award finalist. His BS is in Manufacturing Engineering & Technology from Purdue University, and his MS is in Organization Development from the Loyola University, Chicago.
