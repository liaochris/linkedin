Janet Powers’s Summary

The Diva Toolbox is a new Internet-based community at www.divatoolbox.com. Diva Toolbox serves women ages 35-65 with news, commentary, advice, humor, and community exchange. As the name implies, the Diva Toolbox is a practical resource for women that is both aspirational and inspirational. The Diva Toolbox women are engaged in multiple vocations and lead busy, engaged lives in the workplace, as mothers, daughters, sisters, wives, grandmothers, girlfriends and as community members.

The mission of the Diva Toolbox is to empower, educate, and entertain women. We seek to provide insight into issues that affect women, to inspire women to achieve, to educate women in both practical and intellectual subjects, to offer a forum for discussion, and to create a supportive and nurturing community. The Diva Toolbox presents timely articles, engaging stories, powerful testimonials, and wise words from members of our community. We offer a forum for discussion, and an opportunity for differing viewpoints.

The Diva Toolbox will attract an audience of women decision makers from across the globe. Every day, the Diva Toolbox empowers women with the information and expertise they need to do just about anything! Our visitors run businesses, households, influence others and make buying decisions.
