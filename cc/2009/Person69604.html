Maria Loumioti’s Summary

Maria Loumioti, is a graduate of Business Administration at Athens University of Economics and Business and she was evaluated among top 1% of cohort. She has won three awards in national managerial student competitions and has participated in a number of national and international conferences of EU, AIESEC and AEGEE. She was an active member/volunteer in AIESEC, dedicated in global communication and integration. In 2005, an international team of students and she had initialized a personal development business venture in Singapore. She was accepted as the only Greek student on scholarship in the KEN Practitioner Course, regarding knowledge innovation economy, created J100 youth innovation/ entrepreneurship/ research platform, a projected presented in UN World Summit (Oman 2006). Maria has worked as a financial and business analyst in Procter &Gamble and Microsoft. The most unique experience so far was her being an Olympic and Paralympic volunteer in the Games of Athens 2004. Apart from that, she likes traveling around the world and she has traveled alone through China. She is fluent in English and German and has basic knowledge of Japanese.
