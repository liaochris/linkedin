Geoff Peters’s Summary

My role for Compassion International is to vision and direct marketing efforts tied to global poverty intervention programs (i.e. Aids/HIV, Malaria, Global Food Crisis, etc.) Over the years, I've conducted workshops and training events on strategic marketing and community/media relations for thousands of non-profit leaders across the country.

In addition to my work with non-profits, I've worked with government agencies and corporations like Disney to structure and assist their community relations efforts. I also serve as adjunct faculty at Colorado Technical University in the areas of Marketing & E-Commerce.
