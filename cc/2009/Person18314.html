Christian Forthomme’s Summary

Global change leader, entrepreneur. educator, sustainability consultant and connector for innovative global organizations. Key contributor to the success of leadership development, culture change and sustainability initiatives for clients in North America and Europe. Built highly effective multi-cultural teams able to surpass business goals. Launched two successful innovative global consulting firms. Created and directed a pioneering MBA program in Organizational Leadership. Has been connecting innovative organizations in Europe and North America for the past 18 years. Clients include Genentech, the Gates Foundation, Odwalla, J&J, Groupe Yves Rocher, France Telecom, Bayer, BNP-Paribas or Nature et Decouvertes.
