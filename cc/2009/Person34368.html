Fred Nikgohar’s Summary

Experienced hands on CEO with a wide range of experience in everything from real estate finance to consumer electronics to robotics and automation. Currently I'm running RoboDynamics, a Robotic Telepresence (RTP) company I founded in September 2003. At RoboDynamics we enable remote workers to enjoy the immersive experience of being at the office without physically being there by using a robot as an avatar.
