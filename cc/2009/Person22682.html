Dan Kehrley’s Summary

YFM Media can help you sell, educate, motivate and entertain audiences through powerful videos, CD-ROM's, DVD's, and the World Wide Web. Our projects focus on integrating business strategy and new media to help companies communicate with their customers, employees and stockholders.
