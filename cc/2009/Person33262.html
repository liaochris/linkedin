Brian Crewe’s Summary

Originally from Minneapolis, Mn., Brian is a graduate of the University of Southern California's School of Cinematic Arts. Producing credits include DANCE OF THE DEAD, which was released by Sam Rami's Ghost House Underground through Lions Gate Films, and the wb.com horror series BLOOD CELL.

Brian also produced and edited the soon to be released feature films FATHER VS. SON, a comedy directed by Joe Ballarini and GOLDEN EARRINGS, a thriller from writer / director Marion Kerr.

His screenplay for the drama SARA'S SONG, co-written with Natalie Plant, was chosen as an official selection at the Beverly Hills Film Festival. Directing credits include the award winning short film LEARNING TO FLY, Sony's crackle.com spotlight series THE RANT N’ RAVE PROJECT, and the short film CHOICE, which was featured on THE HUFFINGTON POST.

For more information about Brian and other projects in development at Film Crewe Productions visit www.FilmCrewe.com
