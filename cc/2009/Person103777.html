Bill Andrews’s Summary

Strong Program Manager with the ability to take command of complex and contentious situations and develop tactical solutions while maintaining the integrity of the overall strategic direction of both the technical aspects of IT and the business needs of the company.

29 years IT experience, with 21 years as manager in charge of teams of various sizes from small coding teams to Program Management for global launches (e.g. involving 150+ personnel spanning 44 applications simultaneously).

Broad vision and understanding of the role of IT in a company's profitability with the ability to effectively communicate and manage needs of all facets of the organization from Executive Management to End User.

Experienced in the business of running an IT organization including vendor relationship management, outsourcing, IT customer relationship management, budget and financial management, technology assessment, and employee mentoring.

Extensive business analysis background with experience in manufacturing, product development, customer service, marketing and sales, and retailer systems.
