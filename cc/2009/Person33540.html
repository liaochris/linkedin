Flo Schell, EdM’s Summary

Flo Schell enjoyed a sixteen year career with Sylvan Learning Systems,Inc, culminating as Vice President of Franchise Development.

In 2000, while Flo was VP of Sales, Sylvan Learning Centers was named the #1 Franchise in North America and doubled its locations from 300 to 750+.

Flo was named a "Franchise Superstar of the Year" by Franchise Update magazine and was featured in Success magazine and The Wall Street Journal for her "relationship approach" to selling and business.

Her sales secrets are available now in her new book, Stop Selling...Start Clicking! (MP Press, 2006)

Now, as a Certified Business Coach and Founder of Franchise Coaching Systems, a company dedicated to creating sales solutions for franchise companies and franchisees, Flo offers specialized and customized coaching and consulting services in the areas of sales, marketing, and system growth for franchisees and franchise sales teams.

Her clients include: College Hunks Hauling Junk, International Center for Entrepreuneurial Development (ICED), Huntington Learning Centers, Learning RX, Drama Kids International, Paralegal Services USA, McGruff Safe Kids TID, Foster's Grille, Knowledge Points, Fun Bus, HouseMasters and Carpet Network among others.

Flo's new book, available at www.stopsellingstartclicking.com is being used by franchise companies worldwide as a training tool for franchisees.

Keynotes include: US Coast Guard Academy, Knowledge Points Annual Conference, FunBus Annual Conference, Foster's Grille 1st Annual Conference, and College Hunks Hauling Junk.

Speaking Engagements include: International Franchise Association Conference, Capital Area Franchise Association, New Jersey Women's Business Owners, New Jersey Professional Coaches Association, and New York Chapter International Coaches Association.

Flo is a member of the IFA (International Franchise Association) Supplier Forum.

Flo's newest offering is the No1 Mentoring Agency.
