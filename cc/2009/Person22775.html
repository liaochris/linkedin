Dannie Jost’s Summary

My focus in policy formulation is based on fundamental aspects of sustainability and evolution within the social and technological contexts. My broader scientific interests focus on the relationship between humanity and technology.

As an executive leadership consultant I worked closely with individuals who have inspired me in the articulation and formulation of my own core interests that have all to do with relationships and interactions. I look at most of the systems and environments that I encounter and study through the lens of complex adaptive systems.

Dannie Jost’s Specialties:

Strategy, negotiation and application of dynamic complex adaptive systems.
