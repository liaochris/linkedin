Kathryn Darden’s Summary

I have many years experience in sales and marketing, beginning when I was in college. I honed those skills and became proficient in promoting and publicizing Christian events and entertainment. Recognizing the need for a better outlet for publicizing Christian events in Nashville, I developed the Koinonia Concert Calendar in 1987 which became Christian Activities Publications in 1992,

As a writer and journalist, I have written articles for many newspapers and magazines across the country. I have also had poetry published in numerous anthologies and journals through the years.

In 1990, I began compiling tips about concert promotions for churches and other promoters on a tight budget which I published in a small, 8-page booklet. In 2006 I served as an author's rep for a Nashville publishing company. Taking my many years experience in sales, marketing, and publicity, and combining it with my years as a concert promoter and author's rep, I have just written a book for people trying to promote their own self-published book on a tight budget.

I am available for conventions and seminars as a speaker on book promotions.
