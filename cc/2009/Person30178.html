Ellen de Vries’s Summary

Just me:
I run DIY copywriting workshops, write scripts for elearning, run copywriting mentoring sessions and brand language development workshops.

The branding business:
I'm also the founder of The Copy House in Brighton. We work with all sorts of companies from local start-ups to multinationals to develop their brand language online and offline, write and edit the copy for communications and marketing, as well as giving our clients support for all their business writing tasks.
