A Brewster Smythe’s Summary

Professional freelance writer and fundraiser, specializing in sustainable marketing and fund development for sustainable products, services and implementation of green business practices, prepared to generate writing, marketing and event development projects for the individual and small business.

My mission is to create a local, eco-friendly web communications source that will help to advance the cause of the individual businessperson and non-profit organization, while becoming environmentally aware.
