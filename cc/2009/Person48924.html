John Bergdoll, Sustainable Design Strategist’s Summary

As a sustainable design strategist, John builds valued reputations for organizations creating positive environmental outcomes for social and economic objectives by developing sustainable design options, sharing information, and raising awareness and understanding.

Articulate, personable, creative and analytical, John works to identify, communicate and implement sustainable strategies, initiatives and programs in ways that build collaboration and foster change, be more profitable, high performing, efficient, and healthy.

John's Graduate Certificate in Sustainable Design from Boston Architectural College is uniquely combined with 21 years experience as the president and creative director of Creative Solutions, Inc., a firm that connects strategy and integrated marketing to solve communication problems and improve profitability.
