  • Head of eBusiness

    Discount Tire Company

    (Privately Held; Retail industry)

    July 1990 — February 2004 (13 years 8 months)

I was responsible for creating the company’s online strategies and explaining those plans to the retail operations group. More frequently, I adapted those plans to meet the needs of the stores —- the lifeblood of the company. Other responsibilities included P & L, vendor negotiation, metrics analysis, marketing and advertising.
