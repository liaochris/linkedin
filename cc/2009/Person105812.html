Yaniv De Ridder’s Summary

Yaniv is based in the Benelux region and has worked with Flash since Flash 4. He has been developing Rich Internet Applications since 2002, using Flash MX, and was an early adopter of Flex, having used it since version 1.0.
Yaniv has a strong multimedia background from sound editing to video post production and in the other hand have strong development skills from multimedia development using Flash media products to .NET enterprise web application development. This combination of knowledge is a good mix for Design Lead Innovation in the RIA world.
