    1. If it's not fun, I'll pass

    What's the right digital strategy for your organization? Do you need a Facebook page or Twitter account? How do you take advantage of Google's services to help grow your business? What are the steps to create the best website that is visually appealing AND functional at the same time?

  • Founder

    FindandGoSeek.net

    (Online Media industry)

    May 2007 — Present (2 years 7 months)

    FindandGoSeek.net is all about family fun. Whether you are looking for something to do this weekend, need a summer camp recommendation, or just looking for that perfect sledding hill, FindandGoSeek is your best online resource. Started in the northern Vermont area in and around Burlington, it's an easy-to-use website that provides over 4,000 detailed descriptions of what to do and what's going on in northern Vermont. Better yet, users can add their reviews and experiences to share with others--already 500 have been posted. Now, the nationally recognized and award-winning website is expanding to neighborhoods in your neck of the woods.
