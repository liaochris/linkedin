  • Thawte WOT Notary

    Thawte Web of Trust

    (Security and Investigations industry)

    April 2009 — Present (4 months)

    The thawte Web of Trust is a new community-driven trust model similar to the original PGP web of trust. Click here to find out more about The Web of Trust.

    If you live in an area where there are no Web of Trust notaries, but really want to join the Web of Trust - you now can! We will recognize CPA's, Practising Attorneys and Bank Managers as Trusted Third Parties to certify your identity.

    When you first enroll in the thawte Personal Certification System your identity is not trusted. You are not able to put your name into any certificates - instead your name appears as "thawte Freemail Member". To get your name into the certificate you need to find some Web of Trust Notaries in your area using the Notary Map. Each of these notaries will be able to give you a number of Trust Points when you appear in person with your necessary identifying documentation. The more experienced the notary, the more Trust Points she can assign to you. Notaries can assign between 10 and 35 points. Once you have 50 Trust Points you become trusted, and you can put your name in your certs.

    For more GOTO: https://www.thawte.com/cgi/personal/wot/contents.exe
