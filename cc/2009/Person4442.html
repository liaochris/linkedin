Albert Mavashev’s Summary

I currently manage development and technology strategy at Nastel Technologies -- focusing on monitoring complex, composite applications based on Service Oriented Architecture (SOA). My early background has been in developing products around enterprise application integration technologies such as messaging, brokers, java, j2ee and others. Today my focus is around business transaction performance (BTP) and best practices around SOA, CEP (Complex Event Processing), enterprise service bus (ESB) and Business Activity Monitoring (BAM).
