Mike Mitchell’s Summary

As the principal of Panometric LLC, I specialize in designing and utilizing wireless measurement and location technology. As a technologist with an extremely broad range of hardware and software design expertise, an MBA in IT, and startup experience, I cross the technical and business divide to bring the capabilities of automatic measurement and location to benefit enterprises. I have a comprehensive understanding of how these new technologies can and cannot be used. I also assist manufacturers in reaching new markets through targeted product development, and integrators with deep understanding of the technologies.
