Jim Meredith’s Summary

Between a business and its customers is a portfolio of property. Aligned and designed in the right way, it is the source of profit.

But in a constrained economy, place and space move from assets to liabilities. Misalignments cause loss of position and opportunity.

MEREDITH Strategy & Design reconciles the lexicon of place-based engagements between business intention and customer satisfaction. Businesses articulate strategy designs effectively. Responding design strategies deliver measurably.

Used in transformation initiatives for companies, non-profits, institutions, and communities, the approach is increasingly attractive to those positioning now for sustainable competitive leadership.

Jim Meredith’s Specialties:

Activating the power of place between function and interaction, I uncover and make extraordinary connections capturing the value of investments in architecture, workplace and brand.

I use collaborative engagements to generate creative concepts enhancing---
* Organizational design and its supporting architecture
* Interaction and the power of engagement
* Place-based innovation
* Environmental articulation of brand value
* The design of compelling experience
