Nancy Shonka Padberg’s Summary

CEO of Navigate Boomer Media, LLC an online rep firm for 100 baby boomer websites, blogs, social networking communities and streaming radio. We also provide online research services to advertisers and marketers from our broad pool of active baby boomers.

Founder/Publisher, Best Boomer Towns, LLC. A website that provides key data in 21 U.S. towns for relocating Baby Boomers. Original content from columnists and bloggers. www.bestbooomertowns.com

President, Navigate Marketing, Public Relations & SEO Firm in Santa Monica, May 2005. Former Fortune 500 Executive, earned MBA and energetic executive that delivers results. Co-author of leadership book available on Amazon.com, "The American Dream in a Kilt."

Vice President at The Phelps Group in Santa Monica for seven years. Key clients included PETCO, Tahiti Tourisme, Pepperdine University, LA Fitness and The Fairmont Hotels & Resorts. Managed and led clients in marketing and communications objectives, coached account team while focusing on increasing revenues, creating processes, contract negotiations, team leader, recruiting key talent, streamlining communications and analyzing potential acquisition targets. Average agency annual growth 18%. (PR, Promotion, Advertising, Partnerships, Strategy, Negotiations, Revenue Generation)

Director for two years at Adlink creating strategic partnerships for networks and clients. Examples include Pillsbury and The Food Network, Hyundai and E! Entertainment and SeaWorld and Discovery's Shark Week. Increased revenue 48% through creative programs.

Account Manager for seven years at The Los Angeles Times negotiating multi-million dollar contracts. Responsible for $38 million revenue in various categories of retail, entertainment and fashion. Increased revenues on average of 12% annually.
