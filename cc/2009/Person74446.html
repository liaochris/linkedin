Nikhil M. Anand’s Summary

I graduated from Gnomon in 2006. I've always been interested in the art side of technicalities which is what lead me to being a Creature TD/Artist for both game & film.

Past 3 years I've had the opportunity to work on rigging creatures, vehicles & environments. In the past 9 months I've been setting up muscle rigs for human characters in Maya for use with Motion Builder(mo-cap), working on Cloth simulation setups(n-Cloth) for a plethora of costumes & creating hairstyles for the characters in an undisclosed Hi-def film project with Plastic Wax, Australia. I recently completed work on an exciting creature film, rigging monsters complete with Maya Muscle for a US based production.

I'm right now setting up creature rigs & costumes for an animated short film. I'll also be doing the liquid simulation effects for this short along side the cloth & hair setups.

Feel free to get in touch. I'm always on the look-out for new & exciting opportunities. Its a small world..!

Nikhil M. Anand’s Specialties:

Character Rigging & Dynamics, Animation Pipeline Development using Autodesk Maya in N-Cloth, Hair & Muscle modules, MEL, Motion Builder, character setup in 3ds Max (exploring).
