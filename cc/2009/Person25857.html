Dennis Wagner’s Summary

I am on a continual search for business owners who are striving to meet their full potential. Like a sports coach, I work with them on a 1-2-1 basis to build the business that will enable them to achieve their dreams. I push harder than they push themselves and keep them focused on the goals we are trying to achieve. Sometimes it means being that unreasonable friend - the one that will tell you like it is. We work on all areas of the business - sales, marketing, operations, team recruiting and development, and finances - and we have a lot of fun.

Dennis Wagner’s Specialties:

Business Coaching, Team development, Sales and Marketing strategies for proven growth, Customer Service
