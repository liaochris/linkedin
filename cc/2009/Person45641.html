Jeffrey Ten’s Summary

Jeffrey brings 28 years of international senior management marketing and sales experience from the consumer products industry. He has led both global sales forces and marketing divisions for Fortune 100 companies. Jeffrey has launched brands such as Hugo Boss, Giorgio Beverly Hills, Estee Lauder, and Calvin Klein. in over 50 countries. Having had direct P&L responsibility for up to $420 million in sales, Jeffrey has a consistent record of developing top- performing business units.

Additionally, he has managed global sale forces, marketing teams as well as coordinated distribution
and sourcing from Latin America, Europe and the Pacific Rim. Jeffrey’s management experience is broad, ranging from start-up and turnaround situations, to mature, established enterprises. He has experience in developing distribution channels from prestige department store, specialty retail, mass retail, travel retail and direct sales through DRTV and e-commerce. Jeffrey also has a strong background in building global brands by tailoring to local market needs while maintaining global brand equity. Jeffrey also has experience as licensor and licensee in developing both fashion and cosmetic brands.

Mr. Ten’s work experience includes President, ck Calvin Klein beauty Senior Vice President Marketing and Sales International, Markwins International, a 500 million dollar cosmetic and accessory manufacturer with offices in the USA and manufacturing in Shen Zhen China, Senior Vice President International Sales and Marketing Del Labs Inc, Vice President Consumer Products, Hambrecht and Quist Asia Pacific, Vice President Sales, Procter and Gamble, Vice President Sales and Marketing, Giorgio Beverly Hills a division of Avon Products, Director Business Development, Estee Lauder International, Americas/Asia Pacific., International Director Monet a division of General Mills, International Director Elizabeth Arden.
