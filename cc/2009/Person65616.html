Mauro Palacios’s Summary

Brazilian Art Director based in São Paulo.

Bachelor in 'Marketing and Advertising', I have been working on the Internet, advertising and design fields since 2001, and have already developed and leaded interactive creative projects for some of the top multi-international companies in Brazil, such as Nestlé, Johnson & Johnson, The Coca-Cola Company, Adidas, Pfizer, Roche, Nissan, Fiat, Kia, BenQ-Siemens, TIM, Citibank, Diageo, Inbev, EDS, Accor Hotels, Terra and Unilever.

Nowadays, I run my own studio, leading web creative projects.
