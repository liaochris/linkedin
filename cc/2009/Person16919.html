Charissa Cowart’s Summary

Charissa Cowart is a media personality and trendspotter, who specializes in revealing the latest fashion trends from around the world, with the end goal of creating a global community of trendspotters.

She has a passion for fashion and networking with others on a level that allows her to get to know them and understand their passions.

She was born and raised in Mobile, Alabama and holds a bachelor degree in Broadcast Journalism from Samford University in Birmingham, Alabama.

Charissa is a member of Phi Mu. She was crowned Miss Samford University 2006 and spent 4 years competing in the Miss America Organization.

  • Fashion Trend Spotting

    Blogger

    (Apparel & Fashion industry)

    November 2008 — Present (1 year 2 months)

    Let me explain a little about what Fashion Trend Spotting is. If you haven’t guessed by now, it is basically what the title states – spotting trends in today’s fashion industry.

    Why is the site different?

    However, I define “today’s fashion industry” different than most would. To me, today’s fashion industry is YOU and ME. Take a look at what you are wearing right now. It might be jeans and a t-shirt, a snappy casual outfit, a cocktail dress or just pajamas, but either way you are wearing it for a reason. Maybe it matches the event you are heading to, maybe you just think it’s cute, or maybe it’s super comfy.

    No matter what, you have a reason for what you wear everyday. My point is, you don’t have to wear an outfit just because you saw it in the latest issue of Vogue or InStyle. While there is absolutely nothing wrong with those fashion trends, there is also nothing wrong with your natural instinct on what YOU like.
