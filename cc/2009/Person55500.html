Killian Tobin’s Summary

I have a great deal of practical experience performing infrastructure master planning for municipal agencies. I received my M.S. in Computer Science to broaden my potential contribution in both fields. My background allows me to provide unique insight on specialized software applications for the Civil Engineering field.

I now work for MWH Soft, the leading provider of master planning software to major municipalities and their consultants. Since joining MWH Soft, I have been afforded numerous opportunities to provide input on the development of our software. I enjoy the continual challenge of reaching out to new clients and assisting our existing users.

My other interests include: data visualization/demographic research, designing web applications; information design; being a weather geek; exploring sustainability issues; and sailing.
