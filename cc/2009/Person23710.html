  • Assistant Organizer

    Blue Venture Community

    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    June 2008 — Present (1 year 2 months)

    Blue Venture Community (BVC) is a private community for Columbia students, alumni and employees interested in all aspects of entrepreneurship. The group welcomes members from any school, sector or industry function (e.g., entrepreneur, VC, lawyer, etc).

    BVC seeks to foster entrepreneurship by facilitating communication, providing access to resources and creating community. Through BVC members meet every few weeks to participate in an array of events. Event formats include demo events, idea incubation, educational panels, speaker events, and happy hours.

    Individuals must possess a Columbia email address to join.

    This group is a privately operated group not affiliated with Columbia University.
