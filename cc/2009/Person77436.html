Paul Biggs’s Summary

Paul Biggs is a seasoned internet marketer who focuses on generating not only brand awareness and website traffic, but more importantly qualified leads and solid ROI.

He has spent most of his career in client services with management consulting and online advertising agencies such as Anvil Media (Portland), Deloitte Consulting (Boston), and Lubin Lawrence (New York). More recently, Paul traded breadth for depth, and dove into the industry side of things with Jive Software. There, he spearheads online lead generation for their enterprise-class collaboration and community software -- Jive SBS. Paul does this by creating and managing integrated marketing programs that span search engine marketing (SEO & PPC), social media outreach, email campaigns, blogging, media buying, and live webcasts.

Paul Biggs’s Specialties:

online marketing, lead generation, live webcasts/webinars, search engine marketing (SEM), search engine optimization (SEO), pay-per-click advertising (PPC), media buying, email campaigns, web analytics, conversion rate optimization (Salesforce, Eloqua, Google Website Optimizer), social media optimization, online PR, HTML/CSS/Javascript, blogging, business development, strategic partnerships, entrepreneurship
