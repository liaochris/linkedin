Bill Russell’s Summary

Bill Russell creates images and design that enhance the printed word. As an accomplished illustrator, visual journalist and web designer, he has contributed his creative expertise and picture-making to a multitude of companies, forums and online media throughout the United States and Canada. His voracious appetite for artistic expression keeps Bill busy painting abstract collages in his San Rafael studio. When Bill is not busy creating art and design, he enjoys spending time with his family and their beloved Chihuahua, Peso.

Bill Russell’s Specialties:

illustration, web design, visual journalism, writing and teaching.

  • Visual Journalist

    Profile Reportage

    (Design industry)

    April 2005 — Present (4 years 9 months)

    Bill specializes in an illustration style he calls "profile reportage", an on-site, sketchbook-style series of drawings and text. Through this approach BIll brings people, situations and events to life.

    Some clients include Chow.com where he draws various dishes being prepared for a bi-weekly, on-line feature called The Basics. For TODO magazine he drew cab drivers in sitiu. He illustrated small business owners for the San Francisco Chamber of Commerce monthly newsletter. Bill created over 80 illustrated profiles of unique Bay area people for a Saturday feature called the Bay Folk Sketchbook in the San Francisco Chronicle.

    His recent Civil War Days portfolio illustrates the characters that perform a live reenactment of the Civil War. His process is showcased in a video at http://billustration.com/prorep/civilwarvideo.html

    Bill is seeking a few corporate clients to produce visual profiles on an on-going basis.
