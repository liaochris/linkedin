Elsa Bartley’s Summary

Passionate about great design and fascinated by the web; the social implications of how we connect with each other through technology. After working as a professional web designer, I sought the challenge of complex (human-computer) interactions that interface design held, specialising in usability and accessibility, without sacrificing aesthetics. My ongoing interest in the evolution of the web and the cultural shifts it affects; exploring the new business opportunities that it creates.

Elsa Bartley’s Specialties:

User centred design, usability, accessibility without sacrificing aesthetics; taking as much pride in getting the underlying code right as the graphics and the design. Managing business stakeholders, creating innovative solutions, prototyping and presenting new ideas. XHTML, CSS, WPF & Expression Blend, Javascript, Adobe CS4.
