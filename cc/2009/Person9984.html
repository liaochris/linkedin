Bob Beaty’s Summary

My goals are simple: work with the best clients, craft their message and drive public awareness through electronic and print mediums. My ultimate job would be to work with a strong hi- or biotech entity and use my skills to help said company realize its full potential. The niche I fill is to provide investors and readers unbiased information in a form that is unique to the genre. As one of the first writers to provide original content to the Internet over 12 years ago, my style resonates both with readers and clients. My work has increased the shareholder bases of many dozens of companies by making the message clear, concise and compelling. Coupled with a keen understanding of how to distribute and market that message, my services have been consistently sought out by small and microcap companies as well as some of the best portals on the Internet.

Bob Beaty’s Specialties:

My greatest strength is to take complex information and distill it to a form that is both approachable and informative. If the assignment doesn't interest me once completed, what interest would it be to the reader? In the Smallcap space, particularly hi-tech and bio-tech, information must be presented in actionable form; either for investors, customers or within the company itself-- a skill I've honed after writing 1000's of articles, press releases, investor fact sheets and speeches.
