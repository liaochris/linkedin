Keanen Wold’s Summary

I started my career as a developer in PowerBuilder, moved to JAVA and have had my share of .net, PHP, VB, C++, etc through the years.

In 2005 I took a detour and was leading an effort to implement smartphones and ToughBooks within Northwest Airlines for the mechanic strike. I really enjoy mobile technologies, it's a great passion for me.

I moved back to software technologies when I took a position in the NWA middleware team to become a Portal Architect. This role has morphed into overseeing the integration of Portal, ECM and eforms tools at NWA and most recently collaboration.

I am currently working with Delta for our merger efforts on integrating into a single collaboration integrated platform.
