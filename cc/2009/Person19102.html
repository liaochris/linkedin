Cindy Pavlinac’s Experience

  • Resident Studio Artist

    Art Works Downtown

    (Fine Art industry)

    1997 — Present (12 years )

    I am a founding artist at Art Works Downtown, placing my fine art photography in the stream of possibility. Framed photo subjects explore prehistoric sites, archaeoastronomy, labyrinths, classic cars, aerospace, dance, tech fairs, and Ren Faires. I teach individual photography in my studio, host interns, and photograph artwork for artists. Besides framed fine art photography and digital photo composites, my studio contains fused glass and assemblage. Also on hand are books, music CDs, post cards, cards, and small art gifts featuring my images. I am an independent multimedia producer, content provider, and creative consultant. My image library provides editorial, rights managed stock for publication, web, AV production. Hours by appointment and chance.

  • Producer

    Merlin Approved Productions

    (Photography industry)

    1985 — Present (24 years )

    We create event specific multimedia presentations and installation extravaganzas with original photography and music for conferences, art galleries, labyrinth walks, literary events, and private functions. Titles include Celtic Journey, Mystical Britain, Southwest Spirit, Labyrinths of Spirit and Grace. We weave stunning photography with transformative music to share travelers’ tales from the Road Trip of Life.

  • Owner/ Operator

    Sacred Land Photography

    (Fine Art industry)

    1984 — Present (25 years )

    I specialize in images which convey the beauty, mystery, and power of place. I work on location throughout Europe and North America photographing ancient sanctuaries and modern expressions of the sacred. Since my first photo exhibit in 1974 my fine art photography has been collected, won awards, and appeared in over 800 publications. I produce multimedia presentations featuring historic and prehistoric sites, rock art and labyrinths. I am an independent multimedia producer, content provider, and creative consultant. My Labyrinths of Spirit and Grace Image Library and photography of historic and prehistoric sites provides editorial, rights managed stock photos for publication, print, web, and AV production. I also design book covers and CD artwork for authors and musicians.

    I’ve been visiting special places since Apollo whispered to me in Delphi, Greece in 1977, charging me to bridge the ancient with the modern. I aim to inspire people through images, words, music, and performance towards a hope filled future.

  • Vice President

    California Writers Club, Marin Branch

    (Writing and Editing industry)

    June 2007 — November 2009 (2 years 6 months)

    The California Writers Club is the nation's oldest professional club for writers, founded in 1909 by San Francisco Bay Area writers, including Jack London. We are a nonprofit education corporation with seventeen branches and 1100 members throughout California. Our organization is dedicated to educating writers of all levels and disciplines in the craft of writing and in the marketing of their work. Our branches hold regular meetings with informative speakers and opportunities for networking with fellow writers and publishing industry specialists.

    I am one of the founding members of the Marin Branch, chartered in 1999. We meet the 4th Sunday at Book Passage in Corte Madera, drawing writers from all over the Bay Area. Our meetings have featured authors, publishers, agents, lawyers, bloggers, printers, marketers, and other writing professionals. Our branch has a critique group registry and plans several writing outings and informal gatherings during the year. We're here to inspire, inform, encourage, network, and remind writers to have fun. Join us and improve your writing and build your career.
    Marin Branch: http://www.cwcmarinwriters.com
    California Writers Club: http://www.calwriters.org

  • Pro Bono Labyrinth Photographer

    Grace Cathedral/Quest/Veriditas

    (Photography industry)

    1990 — 2008 (18 years )

    Professional documentation of the birth of the worldwide Labyrinth Movement at its epicenter, the Chartres Labyrinths installed in Grace Cathedral, San Francisco. Photographed monthly walks, concerts, 24 hour New Years vigils, weekend pilgrimages, overnight Dream Quests, Friday noon Peace Walks, Grace After Hours, Veriditas events, School of the Sacred Feminine. Provided press photos, created Gift Shop post cards, supplied countless images for publicity, bulletins, videos, fund raising. Produced multimedia presentations with hundreds of photos, created labyrinth walk installations projecting images onto 30 foot silk veils for labyrinth walks. Published photos appeared in national newspapers, worldwide websites, Time Magazine’s Pictures of the Year. I walked with the Grace Cathedral labyrinths from their first bold painting on canvas through the installation of the wool tapestry rug and outside terrazzo terrace to the celebration of the magnificent cut stone floor installation.
