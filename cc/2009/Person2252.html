Mark Caldwell’s Summary

With more than 20 years of hands-on database application design and development experience, combined with a degree in Business Administration along with experience owning and operating a business, Mark is in the unusual position of being able to work well on BOTH the technology and business sides within companies.

Having spent a dozen years as an independent contractor, Mark developed a high professional standard for quality of work and development process. He infuses his teams with these principles, and empowers them with key tools throughout the Software Development Life Cycle.

Throughout his career, Mark has had a passion for working with people in order to create something of great value, regardless of whether the project itself was big or small. Mark has been successful in the Financial Services, Design / Construction, and Government sectors.

Mark Caldwell’s Specialties:

Team leadership, business management, training and coaching, clear communications, SQL Server Development and DBA, ASP.NET with C#.
