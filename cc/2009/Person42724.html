Jameka Whitten’s Summary

Jameka S. Whitten founded JSW Media Group in May 2006 and under her leadership the company has already managed to garner a number of accolades in a relatively short period of operation—quite an impressive accomplishment. Ms. Whitten has an extensive background in journalism, communications, business and marketing, which enables her to effectively utilize all of her talents at JSW Media Group as well as in her previous role as the Executive Creative Director for SOTAC (State of the Arts & Culture) Magazine, http://www.sotacmag.com, a full color glossy publication based out of Charlotte, NC. Ms. Whitten holds a bachelor's in Communication Studies from Northwestern University and a Master's in Organizational Management from University of Phoenix.
