Jo Furber MREC DipRP’s Summary

- MREC
- 1st Class (Hons) Degree
- Experienced Recruiter in the areas of Geoscience, Oil & Gas & Construction
- Global Recruitment coverage

WRS are an independant recruitment consultancy established by experienced recruiters and specialising in the recruitment of Oil & Gas, Mining and Construction professionals worldwide.

Our team of dedicated consultants have a combined experience of over 50 years and relationships within the International marketplace that ensure that our candidates have access to some of the very best roles available in the industry today.

I personally have a background of growing up with the Oil & Gas Industry, with time spent living in Denmark and Noway following my father through his career Project Managing the design of Rigs in the North Sea.

I spent nearly 4 years developing my trade as a specialist recruiter for the Construction Industry in Scotland before deciding to make the move to my current position to set up the Oil & Gas division of WRS with a particular focus on Geoscience which has also developed itself into Mining Geoscience.

I am an Individual member of REC (The UK governing body of recruitment) and as such provide a service at the highest possible standards and adhearing to the REC code of ethics.
