Brian Richardson’s Summary

Brian has 30 years experience in the live stage production industry as Talent, Crew, Writing, Directing, and Creative Brainstorming for events. In the video production industry, Brian has 10 years experience as Talent, Crew, Writing, Directing and Creative Brainstorming for productions.

Brian is also a professional actor and has filmed roles in three motion pictures to be released in 2009: American Virgin (aka Virgin on Bourbon Street, starring Rob Schneider), Deadheads, and The Man Who Collected Food.

Before receiving his Masters of Arts in Clinical Psychology from Wheaton College, Brian received his Bachelor of Arts in Psychology from Michigan State University.

During his collegiate experience, Brian was an active musician and vocalist, actor and artist. Brian studied Studio Art at the Collegiate level for 4 years and was a student of Industrial Design at the College for Creative Studies (formerly Center for Creative Studies) for 1 year. Brian was one of only 4 students in his entire class to receive an A level rating in the Automotive Design class at the school, which at the time was ranked 3rd in the world for Automotive Design.

After over 10 years as a volunteer leader, and a professional leader, in the Christian Church, Brian became an ordained pastor in October of 2006 through the Church Ministry Association.

Emeth Productions exists to give people the best product they can possibly receive in return for the money they spend, and to give people an excellently memorable experience along the way, where the process of how the final product is created is just as important as the final product itself.
