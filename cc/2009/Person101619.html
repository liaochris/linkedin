Dr Ugyen Dorji’s Summary

Since year 1999 I have practiced traditional, herbal and alopathic medicine, serving the Bhutanese community in Thimphu for some two and a half years, and in various countries throughout Europe for another two years, before returning to India and continuing my service in various charitable and philanthropic activities.

My travels in Europe took me to Norway, Germany, Sweden, Denmark, Finland and Spain. My duties included delivering lectures at various universities, medical colleges, preparing tradition herbal medicines, delivering botanical recognition courses, seasonal diet management courses, treating patients, conducting basic home-remedy preparation courses (preventative medicine).

My current work with the Chedug Charitable Foundation is founded on the belief in the principles of the Hippocratic Oath, serving the community to the best of my ability, and avoiding causing harm to others.

We provide medical camps, free medicines, free medical procedures, with an emphasis on child & mother health care. We also provide reproduction health care services, HIV awareness and prevention services, and we have a strong platform for anti-child labour and exploitation/traffic of the underaged.
