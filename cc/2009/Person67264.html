Michael DiMella’s Summary

I am Managing Partner of Charlesgate Realty Group, a leader in the real estate brokerage business in Boston, MA. I have successfully guided the company to five straight years of positive revenue growth since inception (even through a "down" real estate market). As a recognized expert on the Boston and national real estate market, I have been frequently quoted in publications such as The Wall Street Journal, The Boston Globe, The Boston Business Journal, and Banker and Tradesman. In addition, I author the popular Boston real estate blog, "23 Neighborhoods".
