Daniele Mattioli’s Summary

I am an italian photographer based in Shanghai . I work as Editorial, Corporate and Portrait photographer . My Work has been published in different magazine worldwide including Time Asia, New York Times, Vanity Fair Germany, Focus Magazine, Merian , D Repubblica,Panorama, Corriere Magazine, Geo Japan, Daily Telegraph, El Mundo magazine (Spain) and many others. I am profesionally focusing more in Portraits and Corporate work lately, clients including Nestle’ , Stanford Univercity, Lenzing , GTZ, PPG. etc. . My work has been shown in different venues and galleries including Leica Gallery in Solms (Germany), Reportage Festival in Sydney, Visa Pour l’Image in Perpignan, Italian Cultural Office in Shanghai, Villa Amoretti in Turin (Italy), Anzenberger Gallery in Vienna. The new web site www.danielemattioli.com is a showcase of my commercial work which display also a link to my latest tear sheets. Apart from working commercially i also continue my personal project and writing my personal blog on photography. Please also see the slideshow below which display my portrait and corporate portfolio.
