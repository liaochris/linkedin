Vickie Nagy’s Summary

I pride myself on providing the highest level of service mixed with a strong skill dynamic overlaying a strong technology background. Through my past corporate positions, I moved numerous times and learned first-hand the skill disparity between Realtors. My focus is on delivering a high level of service customized to meet and exceed the specific needs of each client.

As an expert in my previous field, I developed a passion for professional development to be the very best she could be. Moving forward, within my first year of becoming a Realtor®, I achieved the prestigious National Association of Realtors designations of GRI and CRS. Less than 5% of Realtor’s in the country have both these designations, and very few achieve that within their first year of business.

Assisting home buyers and sellers throughout Contra Costa and Alameda Counties from the Danville office of Keller Williams Realty. In addition, I am an Accredited Home Staging Professional and share the knowledge of how to professionally stage your home and position it in its best light.
