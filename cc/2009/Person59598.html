Lisa Tulchin’s Summary

I am both an experienced training specialist who creates a dynamic classroom and a seasoned instructional designer who develops curricula and classes and easy to follow instructional materials and job aids. I get very high reviews when I train because I can work successfully with people who have differing skill levels, from beginner to advanced. My training materials also gain stellar reviews, because my goal is to create a practical document or online tutorials that focuses on real world skills rather than the fancy bells and whistles.

In my working life I have gained solid project management skills as well as familiarity with writing and editing techniques that can easily cross disciplines. My most recent full time job focused on software as a service, but I have worked with academics, lawyers, bankers, and public policy makers.

I'm a problem solver at heart, and this translates into helping people find skills they may not have known they had, or helping them refine what they know.

Lisa Tulchin’s Specialties:

technical training, performance-based training, coaching, creating documentation, creating online training (Capitave, Camtasia, Screen Mimic, iShowU), instructional design, developing a project plan, change management, adapting to new technology and being able to break processes down to pass on knowledge to others.
