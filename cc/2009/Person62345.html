Marco Menco’s Summary

I'm Modeler and Texture Artist at Image Engine with recent experience in working on Neil Blomkamp "District 9". I've been working as a full time artist and as an independent illustrator, modeler and animator. I have extensive background in the digital/traditional arts including character design, rapid prototyping, 3d modeling for real time. I'll always be working hard to build and make creatures alive!
