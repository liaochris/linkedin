Robert Goldasich’s Summary

I am a Trainer/Instructional Designer with extensive experience in the development, facilitation and delivery of training programs. I also have experience managing Training Teams and Departments.

I can take the most technical of subjects, break them down into easily digestible components, and assemble them conceptually to others in a way they can easily absorb. I'm proficient in the creation of curriculum and courses intended for a variety of delivery methods, including instructor-led, blended learning, training videos as well as computer and web based learning modules. I am skilled at generating and implementing learning programs that support both business and financial objectives.
