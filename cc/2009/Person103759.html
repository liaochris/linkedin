Walt Jaschek’s Summary

Walt Jaschek is a writer, marketing consultant and brand storyteller whose experience is measured in decades, whose creative awards are measured by the cubic foot and whose satisfied clients are measured by size of corner offices.

Walt distills complex marketing messages into memorable, highly readable stories. He's done just that recently for:

•A&E Television. Wrote a national radio campaign promoting "Gene Simmons Family Jewels" for this network, with rock star Gene Simmons and wife Shannon Tweed performing Walt's scripts.

• Grey Goose Vodka. Wrote a small book for inclusion in retail packages, explaining for the first time the processes behind "The World's Best Tasting Vodka."

• Parkside Financial. Co-created identity and marketing materials for this bank, including the positioning line "Take the Parkside Path," the URL parksidepath.com, and other integrated expressions of that metaphor.

• The Crescent. Positioned these million-dollar condominiums as "The Shape That Defines Your Future" and co-created a series of very high-end marketing materials based on that theme.

• SSM Heart Institute. Created for the Maring Weissman agency and their client the positioning line "Closer to your heart," and helped bring that line to life in a series of print ads and commercials.

If we peer backwards into Walt's career, we also see high-profile concepts and award-winning executions for CBS-TV, 20th Century Fox, Volvo, McDonald's and many others.

But let's instead look forward. How can Walt help tell YOUR story?

The answer begins with a phone call.
