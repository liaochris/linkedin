Fred Raguillat, PMP’s Summary

Fred Raguillat is a Senior Project Manager in the WPLC Support Organisation. Fred has two passions: the first one is innovation. In this domain he has participated in programmes such as ExtremeBlue as Project mentor, and in the TAP programme as Project sponsor/participant. Fred has also been an active inventor, and is an expert in the IBM Patent process. He acts as a mentor to newly formed Patent teams and he is an evaluator on the RTIM Patent Review board.
His second passion is Social Computing, in Jan 08 Fred joined the BlueIQ ambassador community in order to share further his passion and knowledge of social software as an Internal IBM evangelist. BlueIQs ambassadors are experts who help IBM individual employees, teams and communities with using IBM social software. They volunteer their time and talent, to energize and enable every IBM employee in order for him/her to benefit from using IBM social software.

Fred Raguillat, PMP’s Specialties:

Prior Joining IBM, Fred has worked in several management positions within Technical Support environment. Fred's background is SWE, he is PMP® certified, holds a BA in Business Studies, along with other diplomas in Management, Software Engineering & Computer Sciences fields.
