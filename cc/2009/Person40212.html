Mike Howatson’s Summary

Creative Direction is the most nebulous of roles... and easily the most satisfying. I've found that the secret is not to dictate, but rally. No one person ever knows the complete answer to a digital experience solution, but an engaged, motivated, happy team of smart, creative people will nail it in short order. Wrangling a team of such people, motivating them, cheering them, being wowed by them, and most importantly, learning WITH them is the heart of the Creative Direction role as far as I'm concerned.

I am basically a huge fan of leading a internet/online/experience solution/user experience/design and development team to create great work, grow and evolve. There is nothing more fulfilling to me than to do excellent work for an interesting and challenging client objective, while enthusiastically coaching the members of the team to reach their next plateau... to extend beyond their comfort zone... to grow and succeed.
Delivery of outstanding, innovative customer experience solutions can only be achieved when intelligent, talented, motivated people come together with a common, focused goal... to lead and encourage these successes is easily my main motivator.
The best part of enjoying what I do, is to do it with an outgoing, positive, and humour-based approach... creative development and problem solving are without question the best way to expend your personal energy, and to do it with a great team is impossible to beat.
This is what I do, and this is what motivates me.

I am what I do... how lucky can you get?
