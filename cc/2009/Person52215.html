Julie Lapalme’s Summary

Bilingual, autonomous self-starter who also enjoys the benefits and challenges of working as part of a team. Experience in working on large scale projects from start-up and managing them over time. Possess a strong sense of design which carries through both print and web projects. Art background has developed creative outlook, critical mind and respect of process.
