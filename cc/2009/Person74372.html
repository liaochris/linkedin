Niels Leidecker’s Summary

My recent experiences are all within project management, primarily within development and implementation of IT solutions.

Having worked in international projects I have a solid understanding of managing multi cultural projects and a deep practical know-how regarding the challanges that comes with working with different nationalities. Both from a technical and business process point of view.

Apart from IT projects, I have worked for quit some time with business optimization projects and management support.

During my work as project manager, I have developed an interest for innovation management, change management and human ressources management.

My goal is to keep combining my understanding for IT solutions and business requirements in order to develop and implement solutions for automizing and optimizing business, from a technical aswel as a business perspective.
