Dirk J. Bal CISA CBRM’s Summary

Dirk Johannes Bal is a business executive with vast experience as IT-manager in a multinational company. Responsible for managing and setting up of IT services, including off-shoring services out of Brazil.
Has developed exceptional interpersonal skills, business development acumen, gravitas, bearing & presence.

Furthermore he, as a free-lance and interim manager, has developed up-to-date skills and experience in managing SoX oriented EDP-audits, IT compliance and Business Risk projects and in setting up Business Continuity Planning.

He is a certified IT-auditor (CISA) and certified Business Resilience Manager (CBRM), which certifications and knowledge are recent and are being kept up-to-date. Also proficient in several process oriented methodologies and frameworks, such as COBIT, ISO38500, ISO27001, ITIL, Balanced Score Card and PST (Process Survey Tool)..

Specialized in IT GRC-management (Governance, Risk and Compliance) and Business Risk Management, he has the ability to articulate value at tactical and strategic level, identifying improvements in business processes resulting in reduced costs and enhanced level of control.

Dirk Johannes Bal is Dutch with Brazilian citizenship.
