Rahul Gaur’s Summary

Soft to Hard, Virtual to Real, Slow-paced to High-paced, Usual to Unusual, Routine to New, Regular to Challenging -- that in brief encapsulates my professional experience so far.

Having spent close to 2 years in Software Industry, learning about the same, living the 'dream' to become a software engineer and coming out with bug-free products, with ample perks and 'cool' life, I decided to CHANGE the course of my life.

I pursued my MBA from IIFT Delhi and decided to work in an industry, far removed from the Software industry. And here I am. Working in one of the finest Manufacturing organisations of the world -- Tata Steel, in Marketing & Sales. And boy oh boy, am I loving it! The constant change, unusual challenging situations, business scenarios, is making for a rich experience so far.

My current role includes taking care of the Precision Tubes business in North India, with Automobile OEMs as my major clients. In the role of a Customer Account Manager, I'm the bread-earner for Tata Steel, playing my part in improving the bottomline.

Goals:

My current goals are to immerse myself fully in the business I am in, learning the nitty-gritties of the same, clearing all my business fundamentals so as to achieve the long-term goal to drive the business independently for my company, in one of the major geographies.
