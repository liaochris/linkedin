Willem Karssenberg’s Summary

Willem Karssenberg is working for the Drenthe College in the Netherlands.
Drenthe College is a regional educational institute (ROC) where young people and adults can take courses in intermediate vocational education and adult education.
Started as a teacher more than 25 years ago, the last 10 years Willem works as e-advisor and innovator within the IT department of the College. He is involved in the implementation of e-Learning initiatives and supports the educational departments in all aspects where Internet Technology is playing a role.
Willem also has a well read "edu"-weblog, called "Trendmatcher between IT and Education". Messages are mainly about all kind of (web2.0) developments on the internet and the way they can be used in Education.
