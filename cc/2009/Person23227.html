Darren Woolley’s Summary

TrinityP3 is an independent marketing management consulting company that provides marketers and advertisers with benchmarks, training and advice on how to maximise the value of their advertising and marketing budget by providing cost benchmarks, industry best practice knowledge, training and independent third party advice.

With offices in Sydney, Melbourne, Hong Kong and Singapore we can help our clients find the right supplier, negotiate the ideal contract, benchmark the current costs or assess their current processes across Australasia including China, SE Asia, Australia and New Zealand.

Darren Woolley’s Specialties:

Training, process auditing, cost benchmarking and assessments in: Media Planning & Buying, TV and print production, Direct and Interactive marketing, Public relations, Agency selection and remuneration.
