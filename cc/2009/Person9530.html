Barry Burnett’s Summary

Barry Burnett, GRI, RECS, SRES, has been a Full Service Realtor for 36 years and has conducted business in 5 states, from residential sales and development to industrial and commercial sales and development, rehabbing, repositioning, investor coordination, and all points between. Burnett has taught real estate on the university level over 31 years in several states, and an acknowledged Expert Witness in the LA and Southern California Court System.

Barry Burnett has personally closed over 4,000 real estate transactions for his clients and partners. His motto is: "I only want the right to serve you for another 24 years-- then you're on your own!" He figures 60 years of client care should then earn him the right to a fishing trip or two. In the meantime, expect Top Expertise and Personal Service!

Barry Burnett is a Leader in his profession. He was a Director for the California Association of Realtors, Past President and Director of the Burbank Association of Realtors, and is past California State Chairman of the Legislative Committee, vigorously defending Private Property Rights on a State and National level on behalf of over 225,000 Realtors and their clients in California. He also serves on many Boards for the community and charities, notably the Burbank Chamber of Commerce and Providence-Saint Joseph Hospital Foundation Leadership Committee. Barry was named Spotlight Citizen of the Year 2005 by the City of Burbank and the Colony Theatre for his service to the community at large; the Robert T. Andersen Man of the Year National Award for Christians in Government by Alpha Gamma Omega Fraternity, 1995; and Kiwanian of the Year, 1989.

Barry Burnett believes YOUR business is Your Business! And discretion and care in protecting your fiduciary position is paramount to him and his associates. Serving the 4th generation of his earliest clients, he provides unquestioned integrity and utmost honesty in his dealings, for "life is short, so keep it clean and right".

Barry Burnett’s Specialties:

Brokerage, Commercial Real Estate and Management, Portfolio Analysis, Exchange Structuring, Multi-Family Property Investment & Management, Office and Industrial Sales, Consulting, Partnerships, Residential Sales and Leasing, Repositioning Investors and Properties, Rehabbing.
