Ann-Marie Abbott’s Summary

Dynamic Senior Recruiter with extensive background with transitional turn-around and high-growth organizations. Results-oriented, creative and flexible approach to HR Recruitment, directed toward strategic planning, accountability, problem solving, and teamwork.

Utilizing a customer focused, collaborative business approach, Ann-Marie Abbott has a proven track record of meeting her client’s most challenging hiring needs. In the global market, her experience and dedication to making excellent placements has been proven time and again.

Since Ann-Marie established her career initially as an in-house Human Resource Recruiter, her practice always includes an initial workforce analysis to develop a successful recruitment strategy while maintaining a focus on employee retention as well as sourcing and recruitment. Understanding the high risks, costs and customer satisfaction associated with high turn-over rates, Ann-Marie strives to assist her managers and candidates thru the interview process to insure the best match for all.

Ann-Marie is a trained AIRS Internet Recruiter and has extensive training in Selection Interviewing, Advanced Behavioral Interviewing, Myers-Briggs, Human Dynamics along with extensive technology applications. Ann-Marie is also a member of SHRM (Society of Human Resource Management) and NAHCR (National Association Health Care Recruiters).
