Kshitij Consultants’s Summary

Kshitij is a budding consultancy based out of Hyderabad and working for various clients in IT/ITES, Telecom, Construction/Real Estate, retail etc. Our focus is to make sure that “Skills meet Success” and we strive for it. We provide end-to-end placement services to product and services sector.

We would use this platform to let you know about various job openings, discuss some issues about hiring, putting open ended questions and trying to help you and our clients. We would also try to put some questions and training material that will help some of you to prepare along with some insights of ’How the hiring process goes?’

We are always looking forward to extend our services to various domains not limited to any particular but extending it to other domains. We will use this platform to fulfill this objective too to the extent possible.

We are currently operating in collaboration with another Hyderabad based Consulting firm called VRK Solutions. We work\ed with some of very good companies like Symphony Services, Real Page, Ramp, High Radius, Secureyes, infra-totem, GMR - Roads, Cushman & Walkfield etc.

If you are looking for a change\position, please let us know at hr@kshitijconsultants.com or raghavshruti@gmail.com

Thanks and have a good time.
