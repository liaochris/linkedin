Deborah Leeson’s Summary

Currently I'm teaching Video two days a week at a Charter High School.
I have a Bachelors degree in Music Education and a Masters in Instructional Technology. I taught music education for thirty years in the Texas public school system.
I've performed with the Houston Grand Opera, and played supporting roles in Oliver, The Secret Garden and Grease.

I would love to work with a professional crew in creating a documentary.
