About John Martin
--------------------------

A longtime resident of Houston Texas, John Martin is an active participant in the Houston networking community. He spends what little free time he has following his passion as a wine, food and spirits enthusiast.

Here's what he's doing when he's not at work:

(1) Foodie.
John is the chief organizer for "Dining Out Club / Houston," an informal club that meets once or more each month at Houston area restaurants. This active group currently counts more than 200 Houston area foodies as members.

Go here to join up: http://diningout.meetup.com/568/

(2) Wine Geek.
When it comes to wine John is a scholar, a teacher and a student. John recently passed the Certified Specailist of Wine (CSW) exam. John's group "Houston Wine Geeks" meets on Wednesdays at a wine bar somewhere in the Houston area.

Go here to join up with the Houston Wine Geeks: http://wine.meetup.com/564/

(3) Cocktailian.
While Houston is no hotbed for the revival of the American cocktail culture, John is doing his part. He's an active spirits educator and is the organizer of Houston's first and only cocktail/spirits enthusiast group, the "Drinking Out Club."

Go here for more information: www.drinkingoutclub.com.

(4) Wine, Spirits and Food Blogger.
John is blogging at off-focus.com, so catch up with him there.
