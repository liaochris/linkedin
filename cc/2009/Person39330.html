Helen Baxter’s Summary

I'm Managing Directrix of Mohawk Media, and produce animated science & technology pods for the g33k Show. I am a writer, keynote speaker and have been covering digital media for over 10 years on radio & TV.

In 2008 I joined the X|Media|Lab think tank, and was an international mentor at the XMedia Labs in Wellington (Commercialising Ideas) & Melbourne (DIY TV).

I am available for keynote speeches, writing, radio, TV & digital media consultancy.

Helen Baxter’s Specialties:

community, virtual worlds, animation, diy tv, podcasting, online radio, drupal, open source software
