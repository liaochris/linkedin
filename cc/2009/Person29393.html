Edrian Thomidis’s Summary

As a Graphic Designer, Edrian Thomidis has been designing web sites since 1995 for clients such as Pepsi, Discovery Channel, Sony, CSFB, Microsoft, Colonial Penn, Baptist Hospital, Rheem, Autonation, and other high profile clients.

Her passion has always been painting and illustration. As the owner of Tenengo Design, Inc since 2000, Edrian takes advantage of her outstanding creative integrity and broad creativity and experience to produce high quality work which ranges from web sites, logos and brochures to paintings, and illustrations as well as special occasions card design.

Edrian's training and background is in Fine Arts and Graphic Design. She received a BFA in Graphic Design with concentration in Illustration from the University of Florida in 1997 where she graduated with honors.

When Edrian is not at her studio, she loves spending time with her husband Theo and kids: Gabi and Alex. She also enjoys exercising and reading.

Questions about work, rates, projects and art are always welcome.

Thanks for visiting!

Edrian Thomidis’s Specialties:

She enjoys illustrating for children and families. Her work is described as playful and colorful.
Her paintings and illustrations are done on traditional media with acrylic paints on various surfaces.
Her digital work is done using programs such as Illustrator, Photoshop, Dreamweaver, Fireworks, Quark Xpress, Visio, Microsoft Office, and other design, web related software.
