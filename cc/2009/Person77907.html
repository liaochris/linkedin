Paul T. Barham’s Summary

High technology lead plus individual contributor with successful achievements and experience in designing, developing and delivering large-scale software products and systems as well as professional technical services. Results driven entrepreneur with skills and strong experience in program management, product specification, software development, strategic development and partnerships, new business development, and research and development (R&D) management. Highly qualified leader, team builder and mentor with a software development background and demonstrated abilities functioning in a fast-paced and highly-independent environment managing diverse virtual and distributed teams with strength in communicating across groups, divisions and to management.

- Over 21 years defining/specifying, developing, managing and delivering complex packaged and service-based software solutions with 5 years of university teaching, 4 years of U.S. Government R&D simulation software development, and 12 years of applied industry experience designing and developing large software systems using object-oriented (mostly C++), cross-platform approaches.

- Over 15 years managing, leading and mentoring software, hardware and system engineering teams including distributed and virtual teams and contractors. I, along with my teams, designed, developed and fielded complex industry-leading , PC-based, integrated hardware/software training systems (featured on CNN and Discovery Channel for being novel and bleeding-edge). Experience with agile methodologies and full understanding of the software development life cycle (SDLC).

- Over 13 years R&D and program management experience, operating with little or no management direction and oversight

- Over 8 years serving as group program manager leading and directing up to 4 other PMs to manage all Government programs for a corporate group/division including overall budget accountability, hiring, dismissing, training and mentoring.
