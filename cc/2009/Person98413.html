Thomas Martinuzzo’s Summary

Univalor is the commercialization arm of Université de Montréal and most of its affiliated institutions.

Within Univalor, I manage a portfolio of innovative technologies arising mainly from École Polytechnique de Montreal (Engineering School), Université de Montreal and HEC Montreal (Business School).

Always very interested in connecting with new companies or entrepreneurs willing to extend their competitiveness or seeking their next venture.
