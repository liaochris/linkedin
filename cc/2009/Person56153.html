Koen Vervloesem’s Summary

I'm a freelance journalist with a broad technical and scientific background, writing in Dutch and English.

My specialties are IT, science and mathematics, about which I'm writing for Belgian, Dutch and international magazines and websites. This varies from news items, interviews and columns to background stories, technical articles and tutorials.

You can find more information on my website: http://www.vervloesem.eu/journalist/

Koen Vervloesem’s Specialties:

* Information technology: open source software, open standards, operating systems (Linux, BSD, Solaris, Mac OS X, Windows), embedded systems, computer security, web development, semantic web, programming (Java, Ruby), XML, geographical information systems;

* Computer science: artificial intelligence, algorithms, cryptography;

* Mathematics: number theory, discrete mathematics, applied mathematics, fractals, cellular automata, logic, history and philosophy of mathematics.
