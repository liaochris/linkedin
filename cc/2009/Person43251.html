Jamie Bloomquist’s Summary

An interactive design professional who has worked on the Web since its inception. I have benefited creatively and professionally from diverse environments that range from an interactive agency, a 10,000 member corporation to a freelance professional. I am able to lead and manage a project through all phases of planning, design and development.

Jamie Bloomquist’s Specialties:

My strengths include: branding and identity, graphic and interactive design, art direction, site planning, user experience design, XHTML/CSS, concept development, flash design, motion design, print design, photography, audio mixing, and illustration.

I also bring project and client management skills, which include: proposals, timeline, budget and project management.
