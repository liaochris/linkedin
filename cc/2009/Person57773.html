Laura Levitan’s Summary

Laura has held executive posts with Ogilvy, Clarion Marketing, Omnicom, Iceland Spring, Revlon, Zyman & Keller Fay.

A graduate of Ohio U, she resides in Roswell, GA.

Mr. Youth helps brands navigate through a constantly evolving media landscape to conceive strategies & deliver campaigns that engage a new breed of consumer. Since 2002, we have been immersed in the youth market partnering with leading brands to help them reach & impact the most elusive & rapidly changing consumers.

Now, Millennials are beginning to turn 30 and are starting families of their own.

As our clients continue to look to us to help them understand this new consumer, one we like to call Consumer 2.0, we realized that our brand needed some growing up as well. The launch of our new branding will further our focus of creating strategies and campaigns that help our clients reach this evolved consumer. Whether brands aim to reach teens, young adults or even Millennial Moms, we will continue to deliver fresh ideas deeply rooted in grown up thinking.

Explore our brand spankin' new site to discover what we believe in and see how the modern media mix of word of mouth, social interactive, & experiential marketing is working for some of the world's best brands. www.mryouth.com

We were recently listed to the prestigious Inc 500 List of the nation's fastest-growing private companies & is a Top 20 agency on the PROMO 100.

RepNation was developed from a market need to reach this increasingly elusive demographic, & is now thriving as our clients have seen tremendous results. RepNation has developed several offerings, all backed by our proprietary technology platform RepWare, that allow marketers to reach their consumers via on & off line peer to peer, WOM communications.

RepNation campaigns allow for customized, localized & personalized messaging that not only reaches this nearly unreachable demographic but does so impactfully & in a highly trackable manner. www.repnation.com
