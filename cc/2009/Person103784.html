Charles Wankel’s Summary

The best-selling of my recent books (all on Amazon) include the following:

INNOVATIVE APPROACHES TO GLOBAL SUSTAINABILITY (Palgrave Macmillan, 2008) and GLOBAL SUSTAINABILITY INITIATIVES: NEW MODELS AND NEW APPROACHES (IAP, 2008) with reports on creative ideas succeeding for companies around the world in their movement to green management.

With articles on corporate, online, and traditional universities approaching the need for ongoing development and education is UNIVERSITY AND CORPORATE INNOVATIONS IN LIFELONG LEARNING (2008).

Ideas from many leading experts on doing financially well by doing good are in ALLEVIATING POVERTY THROUGH BUSINESS STRATEGY (Palgrave Macmillan, 2008) and INNOVATIVE APPROACHES TO REDUCING POVERTY (2007).

100 chapters viewing each of the important areas of management through the cutting-edge lens 21ST CENTURY MANAGEMENT (SAGE, 2008).

Competency-based, practice-driven, ethical decision-making acumen developing NEW VISIONS OF GRADUATE MANAGEMENT EDUCATION (2007).

I incorporate LinkedIn robustly into student exercises and also am teaching using three-dimensional augmented realities such as the Second Life virtual world, which the Harvard Business Review (February 2008, p. 30) listed as business breakthrough of 2008.

I am currently heading up the writing a management textbook Management through Collaboration: Teaming in a Networked World (Routledge, 2010) with 900+ authors in 90 nations. Put into Google the terms CHRONICLE and WANKEL for a report on it.
