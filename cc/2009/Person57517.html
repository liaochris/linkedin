Larry Edwards’s Summary

Larry Edwards is an award-winning journalist and covered San Diego business, politics and the America’s Cup for more than 20 years.

Prior to joining Cook & Schmid, he served as business editor for San Diego Magazine. Previously, he edited The T Sector magazine, The Log Newspapers and Maritime Quarterly. He also worked as a staff writer for the San Diego Business Journal and as a stringer for the Associated Press.

In the mid-’90s, Larry was a principal in Events Online, a website development firm that co-produced the official America’s Cup ’95 website. In 1999-2000, he covered the 2000 America’s Cup in Auckland, New Zealand, for the event’s official website.

He is the author of two books, including the “Official Netscape Internet Business Starter Kit,” published by Netscape Press.

Larry’s news and feature articles have appeared in national and international publications, including 48° North, Alaska Airlines, eBusiness Advisor, Grand Prix Sailor Weekly, Internet and Java Advisor, Los Angeles Times, New Zealand Herald, Sailing World, San Diego Log, San Diego Magazine, San Diego Metropolitan, San Diego Reader, San Diego Union-Tribune, Soundings, Yachting and Yachting World.

His awards include Best of Show from the San Diego Press Club in 1994, 1997, 2004 and 2005.
