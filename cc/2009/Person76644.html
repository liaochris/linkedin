Paola Di Maio’s Summary

I monitor and research the evolution and development of new technologies in relation to operational intelligence, and provide senior and strategic advice to top level CIOs worldwide in operations, learning, information sectors. I publish highly targeted briefings, sector updates and executive reports focusing on process transformation, innovation, system of systems engineering, management and design. I provide consulting services in relation to operations and project management I organize, manage and run technical programs for researchers and managers and I follow closely developments in integrated systems design, web science, knowledge and content management systems.
I develop conceptual and semantic models, and ad hoc methodologies for ontology engineering . A former science and technology correspondent for major European publications, expert Adviser for Science and Engineering Panels of the European Commission, I have early career experience in publishing and copyright administration and management.
