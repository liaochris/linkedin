Jean-Marie Babonneau’s Summary

THE STORY

Better_World_Inc. was the title of a short story written by Jean-Marie Babonneau in Iceland in 2000. It was an irony piece about the delirious and humorous finding of a secret recipe for mass-producing top models by a genetic research company.


THE MAN

Copenhagen- & Paris-based photographer, artistic director, film director, vj, art-teacher, stop-frame animator, performer, born in France in 1971, Jean-Marie Babonneau works under the pseudonyme & company name Better_World_Inc. dedicating his visions & crafts to art projects & visual performances.


MULTI-CULTURAL INSPIRATION

Since his childhood, Jean-Marie has enjoyed finding inspiration and new perspectives in other cultures (in Germany 1975-1980, Iceland 1999-2002, Denmark 2003-today).
