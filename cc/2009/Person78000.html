Pavithra Ratnakar’s Summary

Entrepreneur with an engineering background and a belief that infrastructure growth in a country should be sufficiently supported by skills and knowledge required to go and grow with it.

India’s tremendous growth in the telecommunications and networking field has created the requirement for Quality Telecom education and training at both University and Enterprise levels.

LIDO Telecom Pvt. Ltd. has been created to serve India as well as to support LIDO clients in Asia, Africa and the Middle East , providing Telecom Training Solutions to Enterprises, Training Centres and Academic Institutions.
