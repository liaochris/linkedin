Michael Darius’s Summary

With a missionary spirit and background in comparative media studies, Michael has led numerous startups into success. For twelve years he has enjoyed working with world class teams at Sony, 3Com and Apple to create breakthrough product experiences that we have grown to know and love such as the iTunes Music Store, .Mac and the iLife suite.

Michael Darius’s Specialties:

High touch design, Design Research, iPhone & Mobile Application Development, Ideation, Concept & Prototype development for Rich Internet Applications & Rich Media Widgets, Next Generation Icon design, Interaction design, Storyboarding, Digital Storytelling, Interactive Quicktime Authoring, Platform Strategy, Interface Engineering, Agile Development
