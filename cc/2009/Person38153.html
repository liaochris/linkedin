Shaun Gummere’s Summary

I'm a senior design manager and educator with ten plus years of experience in web strategy, information architecture, graphic/interface design and execution. I am very good at synthesizing information, managing processes to reach consensus, performing research to validate an approach, and then executing on it to get the job done.

Shaun Gummere’s Specialties:

Strategic online communication, web design (interface design, information architecture, web standards based XHTML/CSS, usability/user-centered design, accessibility), online marketing, email marketing, search engine optimization, management of small, high-performing teams, instruction in web and graphic design (higher education)
