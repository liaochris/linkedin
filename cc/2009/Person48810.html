Johanna Moore’s Summary

Over twenty years of experience working as a scene painter, faux finisher, gilder, restorer of gilded objects, art handler, gallery owner. Pinhole Photographer. Fine Art Painter. Broad experience working with contemporary artists designing presentations for exhibits. Continue to research the history of gilding, gilding techniques, history of frame makers in America and in Maine in particular. Independent research in American Art History. Strive to master my craft and to improve my skills in wood carving and frame design.

Johanna Moore’s Specialties:

design, collaboration with interior designers, architects, artists, collectors, ability to draw on knowledge for design sensitivity to historic or contemporary settings, appraisals, collections management. Examination and condition reports for art collections. Can link collectors with conservation professionals and/or decorators suitable for the client.
