Ann Clifford’s Summary

Safari Solutions is an Indianapolis based HR consulting firm providing recruitment process outsourcing services to meet the specific hiring needs of small and mid-size companies. Over 250 business owners have benefited from the expertise of a Safari Solutions recruiting specialist for their hiring initiatives.

Ann Clifford’s Specialties:

Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO): Recruiting strategy, sourcing and screening candidates, writing job ads/postings, interviewing, background reference checks, job profiles, behavioral assessments, skills testing, job descriptions, offer negotiation, career sites, interviewer training, employee development
