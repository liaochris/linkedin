Ed Nailor’s Summary

Since my early teen years I have played with technology. I played with and owned personal computers before they were personal!

Today, I run an online website design service with a focus on getting found in the search engines and generating leads. Married with my mortgage and real estate experience, our primary target client is the mortgage and real estate professionals looking to stand out in their industry... however, we will work with a variety of businesses, including churches.

As our business is based online, we have no regional territory and can service anyone within the United States.

The goal is continued growth that competes with the "big boys" all the while surpassing them in results, service and ROI.
