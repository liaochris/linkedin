Beth M. Anderson’s Summary

Entered the computer industry in 1981 as a programmer. Have held many positions over the years, including both hardware and technical support, documentation, Project management in a Unix environment, IT manager. Opened first company in 1997. Currently running Plum Creek Associates, my web design firm for small businesses, OrgTrack.com, my web development firm for membership based organizations, and heading up the BNIConnect Project, the future for BNI online. Also Executive Producer of Get Connected events.
