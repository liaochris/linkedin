AJ : Indigo World’s Summary

Indigo World is a new book being written specifically to identify, center and awaken Indigo perceptions. Written by AJ, futurist and medium, this book brings together first hand accounts of individuals with outstanding abilities and details how Indigos will guide us through the great changes that are ahead for the earth and all humanity.

Indigo World details Indigo's extraordinary compassion for others, and their frustration at Society's conventional norms. Here, Indigos in their own words help readers to identify exactly who they are and why they are here, but more importantly, Indigo World will serve as a guide book to both identify the role of Indigo and awaken our perceptions as to our shared future reality.

Early reviews suggest that this book will redefine the way we look at Indigos and revolutionize our view of the future.

AJ is currently seeking a publisher for this, the first of four books in the Indigo World series.

AJ : Indigo World’s Specialties:

AJ is Editor, Social Media for IndigoWorld.com; a futurist and medium. He has written and produced for television news and lectures on metaphysics and personal development. He is writing the book 'Indigo World' to awaken our perceptions and enable further insight and ability to embrace the positive changes ahead for humanity.
