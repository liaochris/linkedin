Michael Brette,J.D.’s Summary

I provide structured financing for public companies with PIPE's, non-recourse stock loans and non-toxic Equity Lines of Credit. I also purchase aged debt from public companies, insiders and non-affiliates and pay 100 cents on the dollar. In addition, I assist insiders and non-affiliates with monetizing their stock positions without selling. I prepare all private placement offering documents to allow companies to legally raise capital from accredited investors in compliance with State and Federal security laws. In addition, I arrange presentations with investors qualified to make an investment.

I have published two books, Asset Protection Planning and Raising Capital For Your Business (Amazon.com). I host my own business talk radio show, Wall Street News Hour and co-produce a national financial television show, Let's Talk Stock. I have appeared on more than 200 television and radio shows discussing various business and financial topics. I have my Juris Doctor (Law Degree) for 30 years. I am listed in Who's Who in the World and Who's Who in Industry & Finance.

I advise clients on a wide variety of transactional business matters from start-up counseling, venture capital, reverse mergers, licensing agreements, capital formation, raising capital for public companies, joint ventures, hedge funds, private equity, corporate formations, public offerings and monetizing intellectual property.

I have invested millions of dollars in emerging growth companies and assisted in raising millions more.

In addition, I assist public companies with adding more liquidity to their stock from market makers and providing retail support for their stock. Visit www.fastpitchnetworking.com profile for disclaimers on public company representation, IR/PR fees & compensation. Our website above.
