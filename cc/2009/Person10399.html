Ben Keene’s Summary

Spent 10 years working in adventure/ volunteer/ gap year industry. Educating myself about sustainable living, fairly traded tourism and digital media.

Tribewanted has given me a fantastic insight into cross-cultural community building, social networks, media and enterprise.
