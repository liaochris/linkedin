David R Meyer - Denver DiSC Specialist’s Summary

Why I’m on LinkedIn:

To be successful today, businesses need to get the very most from their employees. Study after study has shown that engaged employees produce more work, show more creativity, and take better care of their customers.
The bottom line? An improved bottom line for your company.
At ECI Learning Systems LLC we work with you to create an environment of employee engagement by aligning your corporate culture, your management techniques, and your employee expectations. When these three concepts align an energy is created in your company that inspires your employees so that they want to come to work and strive to do their very best every day.
How do we do this?
Using the latest technologies, we will work with you and your team to identify the strengths and challenges of your organization and then create a customized development plan to supercharge your organization.
We will implement this plan throughout your organization through a process of Education of new concepts and techniques, Communication of these concepts throughout your organization, and finally Integration so that the concepts are embedded in your organization for long term success.

What I’m looking for on LinkedIn:

• I’m looking for contacts with friends, new and old, who understand my passion for excellence and want to bring it to their organizations.

• I’m looking for Power Partners, who want to assist their clients and our clients in achieving more by working together toward a common goal.
