Christi Borden’s Summary

Roaming to the far reaches of the globe has inspired me and energized my approach as one of the area’s leading real estate professionals. Whether my clients are across town or across the globe, I bring sheer enthusiasm, relevant experience and a broader perspective to their home purchase or sale. I keep you informed throughout the process and works closely with you to take the stress and worry out of your transaction, allowing you to focus on the more important aspects of your move.

If you’re looking to buy, sell, invest or relocate to or from the Katy and West Houston areas or perhaps an international destination in the future, let me help you reach your next destination successfully. Get A Charge Out of Real Estate by achieving your goals.
