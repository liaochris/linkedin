Nancy Karp’s Summary

I am a Realtor specializing in residential real estate on Chicago's North Shore. Building on my experience as a business executive with more than 25 years successful experience in marketing, sales, and client service, I represent buyers and sellers including relocations, first-time buyers, luxury properties, new construction, short sales, pre-foreclosures, and much more.

If you want to learn how to sell your home for the best possible price in the least amount of time, please give me a call. Or, if you know someone that is thinking about buying or selling a property on the North Shore, contact me as soon as possible.

To see all residential properties for sale on the North Shore go to www.NancyKarp.com.
