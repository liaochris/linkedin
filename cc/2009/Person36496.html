Giovanni Calabrese’s Summary

Custom Creations has been in the theming industry for the past 18 years. We first started out in the retail industry creating themed environments and props for companies such as Macy's Thanksgiving Day Parade, Sears, and other large department stores. We then started creating products for various media companies such as Saatchi & Saatchi, which quickly moved us to the movie and tv industry, and then crossed over to IAAPA, where we have just completed our 10th year! We still cover a wide range of industries that has a need for our services.

Goals?... Design and create the best theming and Mini golf course construction to our abilities. We are not afraid to take on new challenges and put all of our selves into our work!

Giovanni Calabrese’s Specialties:

over sized sculptures, black light mini golf design and fabrication, black light laser mazes, foam sculpture, indoor / outdoor 3-D sculpted signs.
