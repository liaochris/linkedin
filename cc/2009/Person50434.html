Julia Wolf’s Summary

Hello !

I am young and dynamic, ideas buzzing around my head while being very professional and organised.

My school training includes both traditional university and art school, allowing me to combine creativity, art and general culture. I have mainly evolved through experience and relations, through trainings and free lance jobs and that makes me quite versatile. I like to know what's happening around me, that's why even if my original expertise is graphic design I came to learn webdesign, icon design, photo or Google Adwords.

I draw, sew and write for pleasure on top of my free lance jobs, which leaves my imagination wander, stimulation my ideas and making my universe richer.

I lay my creativity at your service, creating in order to fit your needs in the best possible way. I can handle your graphic communication as a whole, from your logo to your website, your business cards or your flyers, insuring you with a single contact for everything, and with real homogeneity throughout your various communication means.

Don't hesitate to contact me for more information or for an estimate, I can guarantee your communication will be one of a kind !
