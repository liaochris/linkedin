Gary Hoke, MBA, PMP’s Summary

Gary currently serves as Director, Enterprise Applications at INC Research. INC Research is a therapeutically focused global contract research organization (CRO) that provides pharmaceutical and biotechnology companies unmatched drug development expertise. Gary provides strategic, tactical and operational direction to develop world-class software systems that address business needs and provide sustainable competitive advantages.

Gary holds an MBA from North Carolina State University with concentrations in Financial and Services Management. Gary also holds a B.S. degree in both Computer and Electrical Engineering and a Masters of Engineering degree from North Carolina State University. He is a Microsoft Certified Professional (MCP) and holds the Microsoft Certified Technology Specialist (MCTS) certification for SQL Server 2005. Gary is PMP certified in Project Management by Project Management Institute (PMI).
