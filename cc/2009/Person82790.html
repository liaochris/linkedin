Susan Coghlan’s Summary

I help small businesses and busy professionals learn about and use Social Media to strengthen their brands and grow their profit margins.

Social Media can overwhelm small business owners and busy professionals. For me, it is a fascinating way to connect with a target market. I help small businesses and busy professionals better understand how Social Media marketing works. I help them create an individualized Power -Branded Profile™ to clearly define their own unique and powerful brand and to use on Social Networking sites like Twitter, Facebook, and LinkedIn.

I have 13+ years of experience writing and editing for industries such as amusement, staffing, telecommunications and accounting. I can write for various audiences and demographics like "Empty Nesters" or "married people without children." I can help you identify your target audience and write copy that targets them.

My strong background in Marketing Communications combined with an in-depth knowledge of and interest in Social Media platforms and trends provides a unique ability to plan and execute a Social Media Strategy for small businesses and busy professionals. I can help you transition your traditional marketing efforts to a successful Social Media campaign which will ultimately save you time and money.

My business blogging experience includes writing for a Canadian property management company in Whistler, British Columbia to write SEO (Search Engine Optimization) copy to help boost their Google ranking in preparation for the 2010 Winter Olympics.

Other clients include MomCentral.com who partnered with Feld Entertainment to promote the 2009 Ringling Bros. and Barnum & Baily Circus through Social Media and a client in Hungary who needed business blogging posts for very specific demographics like Young and Trendy, Kid-Obsessed, and Home Comfort.

Blogging for business and helping you understand and use Social Media to grow your business is my passion.
