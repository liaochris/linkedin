    This installation work includes physical installation of radios, antennas and Transmission lines, electronic tune-up, recording performance data, establishing the communications link with the control center.

I was responsible for coordinating installation activities at multiple sites simultaneously. My job location was Manaus, Amazonas, Brazil.
