Cindy Hillsey’s Summary

I love working with women in, and on, their businesses. As a Coach I get to do just that! I really enjoy coaching 1:1 other Virtual Assistant's, Coaches, & Solopreneur's. My clients come to me when they are either new in business or have been in business a year or so and need coaching around setting up systems, processes, and procedures which will allow them to be more efficient in their business. It's always gratifying to me when my client achieves the results they wanted.

I've spent my entire working life working in small businesses. My undergrad degree is in Management. Can you tell I love business?! I really do! Small businesses are the backbone of the business world, and I enjoy helping those who want to make their business a success.

I currently volunteer at the local SCORE office as a counselor specializing in online businesses. I've been a volunteer with SCORE for about 4 years now. It's fun to see all the new ideas people have for opening their own businesses.
