Kira Garfagnoli’s Summary

I am very interested in the activity of designing in different fields. I have extensive layout and design experience and enjoy working on precise, detailed projects. In addition, I possess over 10 years of experience in desktop publishing and web design programs. I am a well organized person, I work well with diverse cultures at all levels and I feel fine working in team or even by myself.
My work experience in Graphic Design have enables me to gain an understanding of design principles, printing process and various computer software. I am fluent both in Italian and English, I’m half Finnish and I think that my cultural background can contribute to developing designs for different costumers.
I really would like to find a company who believe in the power of communicate and that will give me the chance to express myself in a positive environment. I don’t mind about the position, I feel I can be an art director as well as a graphic designer.
