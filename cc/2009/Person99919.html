Tom Horsey’s Summary

I have been involved in advertising since 1996, with a speciality in mobile marketing since 2001 when I set up Froggie to run the launch of mi-logo, the first mobile content brand to launch in the Spanish market, as well as the market leader for over a year from launch date. In 2006 I set up Publicidad Interactiva as one of the world's first boutique mobile internet advertising agencies, and in 2007 I bought into Creafi Communications a fast growing internet advertising network and advertising brokerage.
