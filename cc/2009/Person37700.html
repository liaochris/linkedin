Gregory Mancuso - Photographer’s Summary

Are you someone who needs to visually show the human warmth of your humans? Looking for a photographer who can create lifestyle imagery and environmental portraits that reveal the natural warmth and personality of your people within impeccably designed and lit compositions? Hey, that’s me--I can help you out!

For over 20 years, I’ve been creating captivating pictures for corporate, advertising, and editorial clients. Using sensitivity, respect and heaping helpings of humor, I’m able to relax and gain the trust of people, capturing their humanity and essence within impactful photos. Clients say I have an innate talent for making those involved in the shoot, willing co-conspirators of the creative process, resulting in everyone contributing to the pleasant task of making striking imagery.

I’ve worked for many of our finest magazines including Time, Business Week, Forbes, People, Us, TV Guide, Woman's World, First for Women, ESPN Magazine, American Banker, Architectural Digest, Sunset and LA Times. I’ve also provided high quality and innovative images for many Fortune 500 companies including Pepsi, Anheuser Busch, Wells Fargo, Bank of America, Federal Express, Toyota, RAND, Dove, Motorola, Verizon, Chevron, Clorox and Macy's.

I’ve also been hired by many agencies and firms such as Hill and Knowlton, Edelman, Weber Shandwick, Ketchum, Fleishman-Hillard, Rogers Group, Burson Marsteller, Cerrell Associates, CKPR, Manning Selvage and Lee, GMR Marketing, Perry Com, Landis Design and Sharp Design.

Please take a moment to view a sample portfolio below or at my website http://www.GregoryMancuso.com. Thanks.
