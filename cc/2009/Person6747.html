Ann Evanston’s Summary

Ann Evanston, MA has developed clients worldwide for over a decade. Teaching techniques of the warrior, clients learn to create “pull” to attract customer loyalty, channel intrinsic motivators, create buy-in and ultimately, obtain results. Ann is certified Development Dimensions International, Behavioral Technology Interviewing, Leadership Pipeline and a faculty of the American Management Association. To learn more about her innovative warrior programs that turn survivors into thrivers email her at ann@zenaenterprises.com.

Capture imagination. Capture power. Capture success. Within every person there is a warrior. Their power is strong, powerful and captivating. They are able to draw people in so they WANT to be a part. Are you capturing all you can as a business owner?

Ann M. Evanston, M.A., has over 20 years speaking, and consulting experience. With an extensive background in developing new leaders from corporate America to those starting their own business, she has developed programs and presented seminars to diverse populations and workforces.

Ann’s speaking career began in seventh grade where she participated in Toastmasters and competed in Oratory. She has always dreamed of being a speaker and trainer! Ann has spoken in front of audiences ranging from as small as ten to as large as five thousand. Ann had the honor of speaking in front of 10,000 people when she was the MC for the Push for Excellence Program at the Tacoma Dome in 1985.

Ann Evanston holds a Master’s degree in Psychology/Training and Organizational Development from Antioch University Seattle and an undergraduate degree in Sociology from Whitman College. Her social psychology degrees have made her a master in the web 2.0 social networking arena. Now she is able to teach others how to connect and attract what you need whether in person or online.

If you are confused and overwhelmed buy all the ways to "network" your business, let Ann help!

Ann Evanston’s Specialties:

Leadership development, productivity and attitude training and coaching for individuals and teams. Specialize in coaching all passages in leadership to increase overall effectiveness. Staff of high qualified trainers that specialize in communications, supervision and productivity training.
