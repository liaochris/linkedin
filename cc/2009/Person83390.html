Riaz Ahmed’s Summary

I have worked for LBi, the leading international full service digital agency network, for nearly ten years. I specialise in CMS, search, ecommerce, b2c/e/b, and portal solutions built on Microsoft technologies.

I started my career at LBi (actually iXL/Scient/SBI/Framfab!) as an Application Developer designing and developing customer solution’s in ASP.NET, SQL Server, SharePoint, Commerce Server, BizTalk and Content Management Server.

I have developed technology solutions for a number of high profile clients such as: Sony, SABMiller, Electronic Arts, Virgin, NHS, Morgan Stanley, Lloyds, UBS, Sara Lee and Philip Morris International, Standard Chartered, Syngenta, Adecco and Orange.

For the last four years I have been focusing on SharePoint technology consulting and managing the LBi London Microsoft Solutions group which is now nearly 30 strong.
