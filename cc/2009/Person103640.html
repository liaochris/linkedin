Wagner Paula’s Summary

Over nine years of experience in the digital space, working for brands such as Johnson&Johnson, Henkel, Coca-Cola, American Express, Honda, and Nokia. He has worked extensively on both environments; the creative and developmental side of the digital industry and is fluent in bridging the gap between both disciplines. As Interactive Developer he helps to define the interactive strategy of all projects overseeing the project development. His focus on emerging media has driven him to explore multiple channels from physical installation environments, to motion graphics, web and mobile development.
