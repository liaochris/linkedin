Dr. Mommy -’s Summary

For as long as I can remember I wanted to help people and so it was only natural for me to pursue a health related profession. I went to many of years of schooling before I decided which route to take and then decided to pursue Chiropractic.

The field of Chiropractic was perfect for me and my beliefs...while in Chiropractic school I met my soul-mate who is also a Chiropractor today:) After finishing my career we decided to have a family and then after much thought and prayers decided it would be best for me to home school our children. I have been homeschooling for over 10 years and love every minute of it.. I continue to be a Health/Wellness Advocate and am a speaker and writer on the subject.

I am a freelance writer/editor have been writing for as long as I can remember. Because of my l love of writing I started a writing business called Dr. Mommy Writes, where I offer services in editing, formatting Ebooks and Special Reports as well as PLR rewrites.

Dr. Mommy LLC is a company that encompasses, my love of health, nutrition, writing, and most importantly the importance of family and faith...My latest project is called Reserved Only..
It is a program that every parent should consider obtaining to teach their children the importance of the 3R's - Respect, Responsibility and Renewal.

Also included in my company is a mentoring program. More specifically a program for parents. As parents we experience many struggles in raising our children and unfortunately were never given the manual...but with guidance and reassurance and our faith, we can instill in our children the morals and values to make them into responsible young adults. My services will be available soon and will also be offering tele-classes, watch for those coming soon!

My mission: Helping You to help your family.
