John Muchow’s Summary

I have been involved in software development since 1988, the year I received my Master's Degree in Computer Science. I am the author of the book Core J2ME Technology, a best selling book in the category of mobile application development.

My work has involved collaboration with many well known publications & businesses, with a handful listed here:

* Dr. Dobbs / O’Reilly / PC Magazine / IBM developerWorks
* Forum Nokia / Sony Ericsson / Symbian / Nextel / Siemens
* Borland / Motorola / Sun Microsystems

I am the author of two popular blogs: http://iPhoneDeveloperTips.com and http://MacDeveloperTips.com

John Muchow’s Specialties:

iPhone programming, iPhone consulting, iPhone application design
