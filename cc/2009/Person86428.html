Ross Cooney’s Summary

Ross Cooney is the Managing Director and was one of the founding members of Rozmic Wireless, the developers of the emailcloud email spam protection service.

Ross has been at the forefront of spam and virus research for more than a decade. Working in partnership with some of the industry greats such as Trend Micro, Command Antivirus, Sophos and Beginfinite.

Ross has particular expertise in Linux software development and Cloud Computing. He runs the Gateshead & Newcastle-upon-Tyne Amazon Web Service user group and helps to manage the SuperMondays events every month in Newcastle University.

Ross is also a member of the Advisory Board of Codeworks Connect, the trade association for digital businesses in the North East of England. Codeworks Connect has around 300 trade members in the IT secotr: http://www.josephswan.org.uk

Ross also acts as a Community Governor of Joseph Swan School in Low Fell, Gateshead. The school has around 1300 students aged 11-18. http://www.josephswan.org.uk

Ross writes a blog on the IT, Cloud Computing and email market called Spouting Shite: http://www.spoutingshite.com

In the past he has been nominated by the Irish Internet Association for a Net Visionary Award in the Internet Entrepreneur category and listed as a business “role model” by the Discover Your Potential campaign in Newcastle-upon-Tyne and an entrepreneur in the IfWeCanYouCan.co.uk campaign. More recently emailcloud has been nominated for an award in the North East Business Awards.

Ross Cooney’s Specialties:

Ross builds technical solutions using the best open source and commercial Internet infrastructure products available.
