Jason Clement’s Summary

Jason is the Director of Search for Wieden + Kennedy, one of the largest independently owned advertising agencies. Jason is a nine year industry veteran and well versed in large site optimization strategies, advanced auction buying methodologies and non-traditional search marketing applications.

After starting his career with a short stint at a pure-play search agency in 1999, Jason's found a new home helping to develop successful search marketing outfits for media and creative agencies. He's already successfully integrated company acquisitions and lead cross-agency teams to deliver on client demands for two of the largest agency holding companies.

Jason's currently managing a group of search professionals in New York and overseeing strategy for campaigns globally. In spite of spending most of his time on conference calls and airline flights, he still claims to live with his three cats, two dogs and tolerant wife in Brooklyn, New York.
