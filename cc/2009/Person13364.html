Brian Adams’s Summary

Mr. Brian Adams is the owner of the successful wedding photography company, Brian Adams PhotoGraphics, Inc., based where he lives and works in Winter Park, Florida. Mr. Adams has been running his own business since 2002 and has captured hundreds of weddings all over the United States and the world. On average, he shoots 30 to 40 weddings a year and specializes in using natural or available light, which lends a touch of photojournalism and portraiture to his photographs. Brian Adams prides himself on capturing realistic, celebratory moments, and his philosophy regarding his work can be summed up in a single thought: “the best photographs are those that capture human emotions in their natural form.”

Outside of his home state, Brian Adams has photographed local and destination weddings in such diverse locales as The Bahamas, Boston, Massachusetts, Fairbanks, Alaska, Las Vegas, Nevada, New Haven, Connecticut, New York, Vail, Colorado, and Washington, D.C. In Florida, Adams has shot in such major metropolitan areas as St. Petersburg, Tampa, Miami, Jacksonville, Pensacola, Key West and Orlando. His work has been featured on several high-profile shows, including Oprah, Good Morning America, and Larry King Live. Additionally, Mr. Adams is in good standing with prominent photography associations, including PPA (Professional Photographers of America) and the WPPI (Wedding and Portrait Photographers International).

Before beginning his career in photography, Mr. Adams was a student at the University of Tampa, where he graduated in 1999 with a Bachelor of Science degree. While at the University of Tampa, Brian also took part in athletics as part of the varsity swimming and crew teams.

Brian Adams’s Specialties:

Brian Adams has been photographing weddings nonstop for the past six years and currently shoots more than thirty weddings per year.
