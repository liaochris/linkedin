Steve Craig’s Summary

Dynamic and result-oriented individual with eight years of training, management and technical recruiting experience. Focused in the placement of IT Directors, Managers, Architects, Software Engineers, Database Developers/Administrators, Network/System/Security Engineers, Quality Assurance Engineers, Technical Program/Product/Project Managers and Business Analysts.

Digital Prospectors specializes in locating and placing highly qualified professionals in direct hire permanent, contract and contract-to-hire positions nationwide. Our organization has averaged 47% growth in sales year over year, with last years revenues reaching more than $12 million.

Since its founding DPC has developed relationships with over 200 clients in 37 states.

Steve Craig’s Specialties:

I provide my clients with a nation wide network of contract, temp-to-perm and permanent recruiting solutions. I coordinate and conduct full life cycle recruiting from searching for qualified candidates, reviewing submitted resumes, screening candidates, scheduling interviews, conducting interviews, checking references, and tracking candidates and position status.
