Scott Gingold’s Summary

Scott gets results!

Clients trust him implicitly and without question.

Before starting Successful Strategies Company in 1993, Scott worked for many large corporations and held many titles. His specialty was finding new, creative ways to boost sales and turn red ink into black ink.

In 1994 before the Internet became the Internet we know today, Scott launched powefeedback.com to to measure the effectiveness of business websites. Powerfeedback has grown consistently since then. Today is full-service market intelligence firm offering quantitative, qualitative and consulting services both domestically and internationally.

Scott and his experienced team can quickly diagnose business challenges, analyze the findings, and prescribe strategies to cure the issues uncovered through the research. Under Scott's leadership, Powerfeedback can serve as either the general practitioner to evaluate the health of your business - and, upon your request we can perform the surgery needed to remove any negative conditions or situations.

Scott's latest venture, ConfidentialCounselor.com is drawing rave reviews from small businesses and start-up's who need expert assistance at reasonable and affordable rates.

In addition, Scott is a highly sought out public speaker and business commentator.
