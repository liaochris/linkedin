Technical Product Manager * Application/Solution Architect

Dynamic, innovative IT leader with over 10 years of success in software design, architecture, development, and the delivery of production software solutions with profit margins up to 50+%. Excel at leading technical teams to deliver solutions for a wide range of companies including Fortune 100 leaders such as Ingram Micro and Wal-Mart and other large companies such as SP Richards, Buy.com, and Acer.
