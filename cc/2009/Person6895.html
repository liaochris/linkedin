Anthony Bockerstette’s Summary

Anthony Bockerstette
Director, Retail Management


Anthony Bockerstette has been in the Retail and Sales Industry in numerous capacities as a Manager, Corporate Trainer, and Recruiter for 17 years.

Anthony started his Retail career as a Manager in St. Louis, MO with Merry Go Round Enterprises winning a number of Sales and Management awards. After his time with MGRE, Anthony quickly became an asset to Edison Brothers Stores in a Training and Development position, based in Washington, DC.

In 1998, Anthony was recruited to Indian Motorcycle to facilitate the start up of a new dealership in the St. Louis area. As Sales Manager, Anthony's dealership quickly grew to become the 4th largest volume dealership in the country. With a true passion for the retail industry coupled with his background in training and development, Anthony leveraged his skills and transitioned to become a recruiter in 2000.

With his years of hands-on knowledge, he is a specialist recruiter in the Retail and Sales Industry. Anthony's targeted searches include those who excel in all fields of the Retail and Sales Industry. With his numerous industry contacts, Anthony partners with the top retailers in the country evaluating and isolating the best candidates for both field and corporate positions.

Anthony has built strong relationships with companies such as: Aldi Foods, Bed Bath and Beyond, Adidas, Nike, Under Armour, Michael Stars, James Perse, Toys R Us, VF , Columbia Sportswear, and Yankee Candle
