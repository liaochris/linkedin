Michele Studer, MBA’s Summary

I am a Business Strategy Execution Specialist with a focus on creating value through M&A and post-merger integration. Dedicated to dramatically increasing economic profit, I am adept at developing customer-focused, innovative growth and expense reduction strategies, translating conceptual strategy models into executable plans, and building high-performance teams using positive motivation to instill a passion for quality results.
