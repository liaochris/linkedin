Jean-Yves Besle’s Summary

Professional executive coach with substantial hands-on experience as executive and coach in world-class high-tech companies (aerospace, avionics, software houses, telecommunications).

Worked 25 years managing worldwide corporate technology-based projects and developing human assets.

My job is to help executives align their mission to their personal values whilst alleviating their psychological distress so that they are in a position to steer their career, to contribute efficiently to their company's success and to foster leadership.
