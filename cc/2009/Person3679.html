Alfonso (Alfy) Nunez’s Summary

Alfonso Nunez is an HR professional with a wealth of experience managing in-house and outsourced HR and talent maangement teams for some of the world’s leading companies. Prior to joining RPO Group, Alfy was the Director of Programmes for Capital Consulting, one of Europe’s largest and most innovative HR process outsourcing specialist companies.

During his time at Capital, Alfy’s achievements included managing the recruitment outsourcing rollout, implementation and change management for Dell, KPMG, AOL in the UK, D&B (formerly Dun & Bradstreet) throughout Europe and advising non-outsourcing clients such as Vodafone, Smith & Nephew and government organisations on specific areas of improvement in talent management strategies. The primary focus of his responsibilities was to ensure that all client implementations delivered significant cost reduction, improved employer branding and reputation, higher quality talent, reduction in time to hire and enabled clients’ people strategies to be realised.

Prior to joining Capital Consulting, Alfy’s background focused on managing in-house teams within the hardware, software and systems integration sectors
