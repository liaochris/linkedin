Stephen Gale’s Summary

Stephen Gale is an independent consultant providing business evaluation and due diligence services to financial institutions, and skilled counsel and assistance to businesses that are preparing for a funding event. Stephen received his MBA from the USC Marshall School of Business and has held executive positions with profit and loss and strategic development responsibility in both the Electronics and Software industries. During 2001, he formed Stephen Gale & Associates, a business development partnership.

Stephen works extensively with regional and community banks, performing over 500 field examinations and financial/operational audits to support commercial lending in the last four years. In addition, he has performed acquisition integration planning, implementation and post-change assessments in the banking industry.

Stephen also works with a small number of socially responsible, early stage companies who have grown to the point that they are preparing for a funding event. Typical clients have completed two or more rounds of venture financing and they are seeking to improve their operations and financial performance to qualify for a strategic investment, mezzanine financing or traditional bank financing, including asset based lending.

Sustainable business practices produce cost savings and profit enhancements that can mean significantly better performance over the long run vis-à-vis competitors. Stephen helps a committed management team bring such high performance and corresponding access to financial resources.

Stephen Gale’s Specialties:

Preparation to Obtain Financing Without Diluting Ownership, Maximizing Value in an Asset or Business Acqusition, Sustainabilty Audit, Operational / Financial Performance Review, Knowledge Based Strategic Planning, Organizational Development, Executive Coaching
