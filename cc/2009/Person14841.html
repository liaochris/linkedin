Chris Anderson’s Summary

Chris combines experience in not-for-profit campaigning with internet and artificial intelligence skills. He has acted as first campaigns officer, then web editor, for Animal Aid - the UK's largest animal rights organisation. He now works freelance for not-for-profit organisations, but continues to work closely with Animal Aid.

He sees his role as keeping at the cutting edge of technological developments, and leveraging new technologies to help not-for-profits campaign effectively.

Chris Anderson’s Specialties:

project management, online/offline activism, web design & development, social media, event organisation
