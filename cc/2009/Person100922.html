Trey Champagne’s Summary

Pelican Energy Consultants is an engineering firm that focuses on oil & gas companies, pipeline companies, and refineries/petrochemical companies. I have been working in our sales/marketing department since March 2007. We assist our customers in numerous ways, ranging from permitting, procurement, drafting, design, project management, and civil, mechanical, and electrical engineering services.

My childhood friend started the company two weeks before Katrina in 2005, and successfully grown the company regionally. Our goals are to continue to grow the company to have a national presence, and to eventually have an international presence. In 2005, the company started with four individuals, and we now have just under 100 employees and have offices in New Orleans, La; Houston, TX; and Dallas, TX. Our headquarters are in my hometown of Covington, LA.

My previous experience has mostly composed of sales in the oil and gas industry, with a short stint from 2005 - 2007, in the paper/pulp industry. I have sold cranes, pumps, and project management services before joining Pelican Energy in '07.
