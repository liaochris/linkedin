Mansi Bhatia’s Summary

In less than a decade, I have worked as a writer/editor and corporate communications specialist, freelanced for magazines, newspapers and a website, interned at a TV station, and graduated with a master's degree in journalism. Not only have I written and edited articles catering to varied audiences (teens, educators, students, medical professionals and the general community) and drafted press releases, I have also created promotional materials and headed outreach and marketing efforts for a non-profit agency. I am greatly involved in strategic online social networking and highly interested in exploring its uses, potential and impact on industry.

In addition to my full time job at San Jose State University, I enjoy being an editorial consultant, providing MBA applicants writing and editing support from conceptualization of essays through final review.
