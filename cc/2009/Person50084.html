Jonathan Abrams’s Summary

I am an Apple Certified Support Professional and audio engineer with a primary focus on plant maintenance and integration. Most recently, I had a User Report published in TV Technology on Nutmeg's use of the Avenue Signal Integration System by Ensemble Designs. Other past publications include an article for the November 2007 issue of Broadcast Engineering, and being interviewed for the main News article in the June 2007 issue of Post Magazine; also adapted for Digital Content Producer and Yamaha's Commercial Audio website, as well as an article on surround sound in the October 2006 issue of Post Magazine.

My past audio engineering work includes: Sound editorial for "Red", a Time Will Tell and Panman/Soljan production, recording the commentary of Aaron Eckhardt for the DVD release of "Thank You For Smoking", ADR recording for Sean Reilly's "Amy's Rules" (independent), and post mixing for Erica Lutzker's "Orbiting Pluto" (independent).
