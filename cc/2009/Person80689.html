D. Jeffrey Wright’s Summary

Think Tank (Policy Institute) Strategist, Project Architect, and Business Developer with a wide variety of skills and experience in everything from auto mechanics and carpentry to working on climate change and coordinating disaster relief programs for churches following national calamities.

Worked with a great number of industries and classified development campaigns for nonprofits, religious institutions, private corporations, governmental agencies, political campaigns, universities and with individuals.

My goals are evolutionary inasmuch as, they are changing as I go forward in life. I am always open to new opportunities where I can demonstrate innovation, sustainability, social reform, community advancement, and implement cooperative principles.

Currently living in Venezuela, working on a number of popular projects involving social reform, rural development, and economic advancement of marginalized and rural populations in Venezuela and neighboring countries including soon Guyana through the Pan-American Foundation.

See more about me in an referenced article at the English Wikipedia, by searching on "Col. David J. Wright" or "D. Jeffrey Wright".
