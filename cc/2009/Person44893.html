João Craveiro’s Summary

João Craveiro has a B.Sc. in Informatics Engineering (2007) from the University of Lisbon, Portugal, and is currently pursuing a M.Sc. in Informatics Engineering (specialization area: Systems Architecture and Computer Networks) in the same institution. His thesis work concerns the integration of generic operating systems, alongside real-time operating systems, in an architecture observing robust temporal and spatial partitioning. He has been involved in the national project DARIO, and in the ESA-sponsored international consortium projects AIR and AIR-II. Research interests include: real-time, embedded systems, Linux, RTEMS, and temporal and spatial partitioning. He is a student member of IEEE.

João Craveiro’s Specialties:

Real-time systems, embedded systems, real-time operating systems, temporal and spatial segregation. Linux, RTAI, RTEMS. Programming, modelling and other languages/platforms: Java, C, C#, PHP, SQL/MySQL, Haskell, Prolog, bash script, assembly, HTML/XHTML, CSS, JavaScript, XML/XSLT, Appia. Web content accessibility to visually-impaired users (concept and pratical application).
