Jerry Posner’s Summary

Got motivation?

Since 1993, giving lectures and workshops for guests at Canyon Ranch in the Berkshires, including: Time and Life Management Toolbox, Practicing the Practical Power of Gratitude, Ways to Create "Miracles" in Your Life, Ways to Make "The Butterfly Effect" Work For You.

Since 1998, providing custom-designed customer service, professional development, leadership and team building programs for Tio Juan's Margaritas Restaurants in Maine, New Hampshire, Connecticut and Massachusetts.

Workshops, seminars and keynotes for a wide variety of client businesses -- including hotels, banks, restaurants, retail, health care, real estate, non-profits, financial consultants, sales, manufacturing and membership organizations.

Clients have included: FDIC (Boston & NYC); National Endowment for the Arts; GE Plastics; Brunswick Corp.; W.E. Aubuchon Co.; Prime Outlets, Lee MA; Berkshire Life Insurance; Berkshire Healthcare Systems; Interprint; Old Sturbridge Village; Hancock Shaker Village; Peabody Hotel Group; and Crowne Plaza Hotel, Pittsfield MA (designated trainer).

Also, still producing and voicing the occasional radio commercial.

Jerry Posner’s Specialties:

Customer care resulting in customer & brand loyalty, communication & leadership skills -- practical tools, techniques & strategies to facilitate positive change. Motivational entertainment promoting expanded awareness of behavior, habits, choices -- ways to increase the likelihoods of positive outcomes at work, in relationships, and in other areas of life.

Please visit: www.jerryposner.com for more.
