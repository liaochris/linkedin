SERVICES AND DEDICATIONS

SENIORS REAL ESTATE SPECIALIST: Seniors Real Estate Specialists help seniors make wise decisions about selling the family home, buying rental property, managing the capital gains and estate tax implications of owning real estate, obtaining a reverse mortgage, moving to a senior community, and other issues. By earning the SRES designation, Debbie has the knowledge and expertise to help you in all these ways.

FINE HOMES AND ESTATES: The Century 21 Fine Homes and Estates program criteria have been enhanced to accomodate the unique needs of buying and selling luxury homes, including specialty advertising, negotiation, and contracts. Only offices and agents who have met the strict requirements of the designation are permitted to use the Fine Homes and Estates specialty marketing systems. The benefits are passed directly to you.

NEW HOMES CONSTRUCTION: Certification in the Century 21 New Homes Construction program ensures complete understanding of the keys necessary to produce good results in new homes sales. New housing trends can be identified, the approval and building process can be explained, and a superior understanding of building materials and techniques translates into a homebuilding process that proceeds smoothly, without surprises.

DEBBIE GILBERT is dedicated to improving your real estate success in these and many other ways.
