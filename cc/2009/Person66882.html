Mateus Folque’s Summary

Six years of experience working with SAP in major consulting firms. I’ve worked five years at Deloitte and started my own business recently (Winger Consulting). At Winger I’ve acquired international experience, working in countries such as United Kingdom, France, China and Slovakia, along with customers ranging from Capgemini, Saint-Gobain and SAP, to IBM.

I have full-cycle project experience working with Business Warehouse (BI), Controlling (CO) and Strategic Enterprise Management (SEM) solutions, having developed several of these projects during my collaboration at Deloitte.

I have solid financial background, namely in controlling, where I possess professional experience in Business Reporting (2 full-cycle projects), Budgeting and Planning (4 full-cycle projects), Activity Based Costing (1 full-cycle project), Product Costing (2 full-cycle project), Profitability Analysis (2 full-cycle projects), Balanced Scorecards (2 full-cycle projects) and Value Driver Trees (1 full-cycle project).

I hold national and international experience conducting professional training to post graduation students (Overgest/ISCTE), consulting professionals (IBM, Novabase), Directors (Media Capital, Brisa and EPAL) and end users (Saint-Gobain and Compal, among others). I have been SAP Academy Trainer in United Kingdom, China and Portugal for BI and SEM courses.

I have intensive focus on my customers business and value drivers, as I’m always aiming to provide an excellent service to customers.

I undertook a major in Controlling at Overgest/ISCTE, and graduated in Management and Information Systems at the same University.

Mateus Folque’s Specialties:
