Lary Holland’s Summary

Much of my professional experience focuses on technology, broadcasting, redistribution across various platforms. Network design, network administration, server deployment, internet radio technologies, and telephony. For over a decade I have worked with key technologies, server platforms, transport/routing systems, and software design. Coaching management/small business development as well as non-profits.

I work to reduce the effects of harmful governmental policies that are either wasteful or intrude into the protected liberty of parenting, property, and privacy. I continue to work and build my knowledge of software and hardware technologies.

Some of my goals are to tie in various cross-sections of society to effectuate change in key areas. Using technology to further social movements and political movements that align with less restrictive government and more responsible individualism. Yes, I am an idealist but more importantly a realist.
