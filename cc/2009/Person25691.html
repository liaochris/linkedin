Derek Mart’s Summary

I have roughly fourteen years of Professional Experience working with Computer Software, Hardware, Networks, and Security, including network installation and repairs, UNIX/Linux server administration, Windows workstation and some server administration, desktop support services, and systems deployment, troubleshooting, and repair.
