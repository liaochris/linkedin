    (Public Company; 201-500 employees; Electrical/Electronic Manufacturing industry)

    January 1983 — April 2002 (19 years 4 months)

    Produced over 50 million Incandescent and over 15 million Fluorescent lamps annually.
    I became Export sales Manager and worked together with Novena Marketing Pty, Ltd. to develop our own Brand and started our Export Business. (Travelled to Philippines, Hong Kong, Thailand and Singapore)
    I also was for some years Quality Manager, in this position also involved with Standards comity and integrated this position in the end with my position as Production Manager.

  • Department Manager

    (Public Company; 501-1000 employees; Electrical/Electronic Manufacturing industry)

    1977 — 1982 (5 years)

    Production of Special Fluorescent Lights.

Starting with some 280 Employees but being able to reduce this with higher outputs to some 220 employees over the time I was responsible.
