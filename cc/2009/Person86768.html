Richard Stang’s Summary

Richard Stang co-founded Business Innovation Group to help companies realize rapid business growth by Mastering the Innovation Process.

Richard founded NewDay Power to leverage his 19 years experience in the energy project finance and project development industry in both the private and public sector.

As Managing Director, Richard has over 18 years professional experience in technology and product commercialization; pilot program and beta test development and execution; national and international strategic sales and business development; channel partner creation, development and implementation of fully integrated marketing plans; project management; and strategic planning for companies ranging in size from start-ups to international organizations.

Richard Stang’s Specialties:

Richard has leveraged his training and experience as an energy and business law attorney in finding strategic solutions to new and complex business situations. Richard has developed a sharp, analytical skill set and disciplined process to hone proprietary technology, product commercialization, and financing models, visualize unique business opportunities and relationships, and formulate, and execute upon, strategic marketing plans and business development arrangements and partnerships.
