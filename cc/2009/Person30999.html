Eric Barron’s Summary

After a brief flirtation with Law School, my life took an unexpected right turn into the mortgage/real estate business. During this eventful 15 year period, I was very fortunate to be introduced to what many people may term their, "true calling".

For me, it was a love of teaching and motivating people to make the most of their natural abilities. Combining these two passions, I spent much of my 30's mastering the art of connecting with people. This lead me to launch ericbarronlive, which has now evolved into TAP - The Ability Project.

Come take a peek at what we're all about at, theabilityproject.com. We are open to any and all networking opportunities and suggestions on collaboration.
