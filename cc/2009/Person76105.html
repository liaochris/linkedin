Oscar O'Connor’s Summary

Oscar O’Connor, Chartered Engineer and Chartered IT Professional, is an experienced programme director and senior manager with direct experience of more than 12 industries/business sectors.

Oscar is an accredited PRINCE2 and MSP Practitioner and a professional member of both the British Computer Society and the Business Continuity Institute. Oscar is a regular public speaker on business continuity and related issues.

Oscar is also a Trustee of The Web of Hope, a charity providing information and educational resources in the field of sustainable development.
