Christopher Little’s Summary

Award-winning business veteran accomplished in every aspect of measurable strategic and tactical marketing.

Expertise includes: creating and pegging company and product brand awareness; advertising and collateral concept, development and deployment; competition and marketplace investigation and analysis; PR campaign development and execution; product definition and competitive positioning, pricing models; trade show concepts and management; sales support; RFP/RFQ submissions; market research; design of superior direct mail and telemarketing programs.

I've done end-to-end marketing: I have managed the soup-to-nuts realities of start-ups but I have also successfully worked at large companies with large staffs, external, global PR firms, multi-million dollar promotions and more.

Everything gets measured and evaluated (I'm the kind of manager who, when someone is trying to convince me and says there are "well, there are lots of examples", will ask the person to "name six"). I also love intelligent push back (i.e., let's confirm assumptions by bringing them out).

My philosophy on strategic vision: if it resonates, you'll eventually hear it echoed back to you. In other words, if it has traction, you will find people adopting it without realizing it was invented.

My philosophy on expenses: Keep all marketing as humanly measurable as possible; for example, the dollar and time cost of a lead through every step (from contact to conversion to closure).

I thrive on new developments and challenges.
