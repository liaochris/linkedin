Kreg Kukor’s Summary

Kreg Kukor has 20 + years in Global quality management, his career spans Asia, Mexico, Canada, India, and USA, through many diverse industries. Kreg started his quality career in Canada working through numerous positions in a variety of multi national corporations. He has held positions within the local ASQ chapter as education and certification chairman.. Kreg has written articles for Tracks magazine, has been featured by Managing Automation for supplier quality systems and recognized as a contributor to the latest ASQ published certified quality engineer handbook regarding web based quality information systems.  Over the past 3 years Kreg has spoken on national stages regarding global executive business metrics, Global web based quality information systems and is pioneering roll though put yield in supplier chain risk management. He has been a guest speaker to AMR Research, Mesa, IQS and SME conferences. Kreg has been responsible for the implementation and certification of ISO 9001:2000 / TS16949, ISO14001, Q12002 in over 15 manufacturing locations.
