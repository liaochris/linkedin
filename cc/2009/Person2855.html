Sally Albright’s Summary

A 15-year veteran of Capitol Hill politics, Sally Albright is a freelance public affairs and marketing consultant in Washington, DC. During the 2008 election cycle, Ms. Albright traveled the country with the National Advance Team for Hillary Clinton’s Presidential bid, and most recently served as Field Director for the successful “Red to Blue” campaign of Bobby Bright for Congress.

Currently, Albright is overseeing several online marketing campaigns. The challenges are rewarding, but Albright wants to return to politics. She is actively seeking a policy position on Capitol Hill.

Prior to opening her own business, Ms. Albright was Vice President of the Federal Government Relations Practice at McGuire Woods Consulting. She has also represented the National Association of Regulatory Utility Commissioners (NARUC) as Director of Congressional and Public Relations. On Capitol Hill, Albright served as Senior Legislative Assistant and Committee Liaison to Representative Earl F. Hilliard (D-AL), handling a variety of issue areas and special projects.

Born and raised in Alabama, Sally Albright holds a B.A. in Communications from Hollins University and has completed graduate coursework in Public Policy at Georgetown University and at the GS Graduate School.
