Jérôme Delacroix’s Summary

After writing the first French book about wikis ("Les wikis") and creating a blog about cooperation best practices and technologies (www.cooperatique.com), I have dedided to come back to the world of consultancy through the implementation of cooperative approaches and tools.

Cooperatique is now a company providing change management services dealing with social capital assessment and fostering cooperative management. We select and deploy the best tools for your organization, train your people and coach them in order to create / develop a cooperative team spirit aiming at reaching your business goals.
