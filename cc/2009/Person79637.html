Pierce Mattie’s Summary

As the leader in lifestyle public relations, Pierce Mattie PR specializes in developing publicity campaigns for beauty, cosmetic, fashion, home furnishing, and health conscious clients utilizing a fusion of public relations, viral marketing, cross promotions, exciting events, product placement, and web site publicity. With offices in Los Angeles and New York Pierce Mattie PR is strategically entrenched in two of the largest media and entertainment centers in the world to do one thing: to reach people for our clients. Pierce Mattie publicizes the labels of emerging fashion/beauty brands as well as established fashion designers. The management of Pierce Mattie PR and its team of Brand Advocates have potent connections with consumer and trade fashion/beauty/luxury, accessories, style, home decor and market editors at top magazines, newspapers, blogging communities and web sites worldwide. Our Los Angeles office has substantial collaborative relationships with top talk show producers and celebrity management firms.
