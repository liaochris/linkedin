Charles Wildbank’s Summary

Contemporary fine art paintings and murals on international scale, residential and commercial including hotel industry

Charles Wildbank’s Specialties:

Murals, large format archival printing, art design, photography and stage design

Charles Wildbank’s Experience

  • Artist

    Wildbank Fine Art

    (Fine Art industry)

    April 1989 — Present (20 years 4 months)

    Long Island's east end is wine country and a colony for notable artists spanning the North Fork, Shelter Island and the Hamptons. Wildbank's studio is situated near the shores of Peconic Bay and is open to public by appointment.

  • Mural Artist

    Cunard Line

    (Public Company; Myself Only; Architecture & Planning industry)

    September 2001 — January 2004 (2 years 5 months)

    Commissioned by Cunard Lines to paint two murals depicting coastal scenes of America and England, 18 feet tall, for January 2004 inauguration of Queen Mary II. Murals are located in aft deck 3 next to grand stairwells.
