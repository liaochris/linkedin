Frances Smith, Ed.D.,CVE’s Summary

Involved with VCU Centers for Teaching Excellence Faculty Learning Community - Exploring the relevance of technology in teaching and learning and social networking among students.

Over 20+ years in instructional and assistive technology - providing direct service to students with disabilities, staff development training to educators, serving on statewide advisory boards.
