  • Co-Founder

    LabCoat Innovations

    (Privately Held; Pharmaceuticals industry)

    January 2007 — February 2008 (1 year 2 months)

    In a medical environment increasingly influenced by technology, clinical research was behind. Sure, clinical research organization (CROs) had electronic data capture (EDC) solutions, but what opportunity did regular physicians have at their own research interests?

    The reality is that most private practice physicians have at least a casual interest in clinical research. They do not have tools to help them in this endeavor however. I developed an online tool at www.clinicresearch.com where physicians can obtain information from their patients for clinical research purposes.

    I architected and developed the solution using Ruby on Rails. Utilizing several Ruby on Rails security features, the application is HIPPA and 22 CFR 11 compliant. I was also responsible for hosting and managing the application's uptime.

  • Clinical Research Coordinator

    Oregon Brain and Spine Institute

    (Hospital & Health Care industry)

    April 2004 — December 2006 (2 years 9 months)

    I began as a medical assistant, but was quickly promoted to a billing specialist and finally was tapped to start the research department of this six physician neurosurgical practice.

    I manged the day to day operations, study development and contract negotiation to ensure the mission of the practice was carried out in each project we undertook.

    I also developed a custom software solution for the practice to manage the day to to day operations of enrolling patients in its clinical trials. Written mostly in PHP, it helped make the operations of the department much more efficient and attract new sponsors for research projects.
