Sheri Ann Bell-Rehwoldt’s Summary

Raised a military brat, I have lived in many parts of America as well as Germany. I love travel, the arts, great food, and meeting people.

Brand new to the Salt Lake City area, I am interested in establishing a broad network of cool people who are high on Utah.

I'm the author/co-author of 10 books, eight for juvenile market. I'm currently working on two middle-grade novels.

Too, I am experienced in corporate communications, media and blogger relations, event planning, and trade show coordination.

My specialties: interviewing, writing, and building relationships. On my interview wish list: Richard Branson, Prince Charles, and Dame Edna. Yes, I'm crazy for Brits. ;-)

Beyond securing freelance writing and editing projects, I am interested in full-time employment in public relations or marketing in the tourism, hospitality, media, or healthcare arenas.

A 2009 goal: explore the world of documentary making.
