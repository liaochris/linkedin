Mike McGuff’s Summary

Mike McGuff is an award winning multimedia producer. Recently he took home two Lone Star Emmy Awards and took first and second place in the Texas Associated Press Broadcasters special online content category in 2009 for abc13.com. Previously he was an Emmy nominated television news producer and reporter. He also is one of the top bloggers in Houston at mikemcguff.com.
