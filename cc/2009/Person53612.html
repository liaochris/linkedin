Kate Farber’s Experience

  • Director, Interactive Solutions

    RF|Binder Partners

    (Privately Held; Public Relations and Communications industry)

    December 2006 — Present (3 years )

    Kate Farber serves as the Director of Interactive Solutions developing social media communications programs for RF|Binder clients spanning a range of industries - from food & beverage to consumer products to financial services.

    During her time at RF|Binder, Kate has worked with account teams and clients on a range of exciting social media and Internet-based programs. Examples include planning a two-day mommy blogger conference hosted at a company’s headquarters, counseling multiple clients on their Twitter and Facebook strategies, managing the creation of corporate microsites, developing an interactive wine and food pairing wheel and assisting with a virtual wine tasting event for eight winemakers and 30 prominent wine bloggers.

  • Interactive Account Director

    Special Ops Media

    (Privately Held; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    September 2004 — December 2006 (2 years 4 months)
