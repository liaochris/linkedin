Jeff Edwards’s Summary

Management consultant with two decades of experience in the IT industry. Began career as a software developer and managed teams through all phases of software development. The most recent nine years have been spent in management consulting. Background is fairly evenly divided between work in the private and public sectors.

Enjoy performing organizational assessments to identify opportunities for improvement, improving operations through process redesign, and defining business strategy. While implementing technology may be the answer, I like to explore changes in organizational structure, business processes, and policies first. Changes in these areas of a business are oftentimes less expensive and easier to implement than a technical solution.
