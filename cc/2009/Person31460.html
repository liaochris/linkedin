Erik Burckle’s Summary

Recognized as an innovative and seasoned Senior Marketing Executive with a successful background in strategic/operational marketing, product and business development seeking a position where more than 19 years of successful progressive experience in all phases of Strategic Planning, Market Research and Business Intelligence can be utilized to the benefit of an organization.
