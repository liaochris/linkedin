Todd Montgomery’s Summary

I work in organizations with leaders and their teams to improve performance by developing adaptable leadership and better interpersonal dynamics.

I help people to achieve these things with the Integral Growth model, which promotes:
a) better emotional self-management,
b) knowledge of one’s own strengths and how to best apply them, and
c) ways to combine the strengths of everyone in the team to improve overall performance.

**INTEGRAL GROWTH - the Integral Growth model is a practical business tool for personal and professional development. It combines over 20 years of research in neuroscience, leadership and management theory, psychology, anthropology and many other fields of human behaviour.

This holistic framework takes into account people's rational and emotional responses at multiple levels of human activity. In business terms, it is a powerful and practical tool for change, yet can be easily understood and applied at all levels of the organization, "from the boardroom to the boiler room".

**CONSULTANCY - toddmontgomery consultancy helps clients to enhance their leadership and management capabilities, particularly as relates to communications challenges and business changes - including organizations that are either actively involved with (or currently considering) a merger or acquisition, a reorganization, a culture change, new CEO, multinational/cross-cultural business ventures, or similar large-scale change.

**PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT - developing and delivering programs to help people learn to better manage patterns of reactive behaviour and integrate their diverse talents and particular strengths, so they can do more of what they're best at doing.

For more information visit Todd's blog: www.tmconsultancy.blogspot.com
