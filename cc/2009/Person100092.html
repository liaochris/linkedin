Tom Pryor’s Summary

Web and Social Networking UI/UX Design & Development

I build and design web solutions with a particular focus on producing pixel accurate, user friendly, beautiful experiences that people want to share. 

Having worked with the web since it's 'early' day's in 1995 I have acquired a deep understanding of it's architecture, both on the front and back-ends that allows me to produce high quality, standards based, multi-platform, web applications and sites that exceed expectations, are delivered on time & under budget, and are EASY to use and BEAUTIFUL.

I prefer to develop on the front-end but I also have extensive knowledge of the web back-end with over 10 years experience developing applications and systems for it, that enables to create efficient front-ends quickly, from complex wizard based forms to redundant, fault tolerant, self healing web systems for mission critical public and private facing applications, such as eCommerce, marketing and sales campaign support systems.
