Kelly Gardner’s Summary

Professional, emotionally intelligent and strategy based problem solver with significant management and international experience. I thrive in leadership roles and enjoy collaborating with clients, and senior management. I consider myself to be extremely tech savvy who instinctively looks for technological ways and means to overcome obstacles.

I have been with GGP for 5 years. I started as an Intern through the Prodigy Program and shortly after was hired on as an Assistant GM. For two years I worked through a development rotation program at a number of centers in Utah. While at these centers, I worked in all aspects of management, i.e. forecasting, redevelopment, leasing, operations and marketing.

Currently I am the General Manager at the Three Rivers Mall in Kelso, WA. Managing a 47 acre site anchored by Target, Safeway, two hotels, Regal Cinema, and approx 250,000 SF of enclosed mall anchored with JC Penney, Macy's and Sears. My main area of focus at this center is cultivating our business development program and working to improve the visual standards of the center while controlling expenses and capital projects.

I am also very active in the community being a member of the Kelso/Longiew Chamber of Commerce, Kelso Rotary and sit on the Board of Directors for the Cowlitz County Habitat for Humanity.

I enjoy leading and working with my mall team as well as the various business owners and managers in the mall and in the community. I am attending Portland State University as an MBA student with a concentration in International Business and Real Estate.
