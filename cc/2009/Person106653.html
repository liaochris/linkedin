Zoe Sands’s Summary

An experienced Digital Marketing Manager since 1997 with PRINCE2 practitioner status, during this period I have successfully launched many new online initiatives for Cisco, Dialogic, the Chartered Institute of Marketing (CIM) and Hyundai, including content managed and e-commerce based websites. I've experienced International exposure running projects across Europe, US, APAC. My solid project management and business process modelling along with my online marketing skills enable me to manage and deliver projects on time, on budget and on brand.

My approach is to create an environment where those around me share my passion for the Internet and the opportunities it presents. Sharing my knowledge, championing and communicating the benefits of Web 2.0 capabilities enhances both the user experience and offers additional online communication channels for organisations I work for.

Zoe Sands’s Specialties:

PRINCE2 Practitioner - project management. Emarketing - management and creation of email and SMS campaigns. Search Engine Optimisation (SEO) and Search Engine Marketing (SEM) campaigns. Website creation and development and the introduction of content mangement systems (CMS) including workflow management. Web 2.0 - creation of blogs, RSS feeds, podcasts, chat online (messenger applications) and Twitter. Mobile websites, Mobile Marketing and Mobile SEM. Web Analytics and Visual Identity creation.
