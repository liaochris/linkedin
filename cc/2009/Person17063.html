Charles Silk’s Summary

During his time as a student at New York University College of
Dentistry, Charles Silk, D.D.S., took time away from studying in the
field of his chosen profession and literally took to the field while
playing football. Silk also played basketball while in college and
enjoys skiing.

Dr. Silk’s love of skiing has taken him to Switzerland and New Zealand
to explore the slopes. Silk likes to ski at Hunter Mountain in his home
state of New York and sometimes attends skiing shows to offer support by
donating funds to the Hunter Mountain Ski Team, which is based in
upstate New York.

In addition to being a professional prosthodontist, a dentist who
specializes in cosmetic and restorative work, Dr. Silk is a former
professional basketball player, having shot hoops as a member of the
Macabiah Basketball Team and competing internationally. Charles Silk’s
other athletic hobbies include biking and swimming.

His dedication to health is also exemplified by his pro bono work. Dr.
Charles Silk helps raise funds for breast cancer research as well as
participates in breast cancer awareness campaigns. Silk also donates his
time and services at the health center at New York University.

Right now, Charles Silk, D.D.S. is a prosthodontist at Park South
Dental, 200 Central Park South, Suite 214, New York, NY 10019. Silk’s
patients come to him for help with dental implants, crowns, dentures,
porcelain veneers, as well as other cosmetic or reconstructive corrections.

Dr. Charles Silk serves as president of New York Academy of Cosmetic
Dentistry and is also affiliated with First District Dental Society,
American Dental Association, American College of Prosthodontists and the
Academy of Dental Facial Esthetics, and American Academy of Cosmetic
Dentistry.

Dr. Silk graduated from New York University College of Dentistry in 1983
with his Doctor of Dental Surgery in tow.
