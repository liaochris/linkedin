Ytzik Aranov’s Summary

Mr. Aranov’s career covers executive and leadership roles as well as Corporate Transformation & operational restructuring for startup, private and public corporations. He has extensive experience in Mergers & Acquisitions (Search, Due Diligence, Integration, and Finance), Investment / Merchant Banking, strategic planning, corporate finance, Business Development, management consulting, Branding, Globalization, and Sales & Marketing.

His career also covers executive IT/MIS strategy & implementation, eBusiness leadership, definition and design of business & technology solutions, E-Commerce/Web technology integration, Social Media/Social Community, Branding, Online Marketing, and Project Office / PMO implementation in numerous industries.

He has taken a number of companies public and has advised/consulted to and restructured Global 1000 and Fortune 100 companies and others in the following industries:

• Energy (Oil & Gas, Alternative Energy, Clean Tech)
• Information Technology (SW/HW, Web, eCommerce, Social Media/Communities, Business Intelligence, Telecomm)
• Manufacturing, Logistics & Distribution
• Retail (Consumer Goods, Food, Electronics, Luxury)
• Financial Services (Insurance, Banking, Investment Banking, Capital Markets)
• Health Care (Managed Care, Hospitals)
• Media & Entertainment
• Life Sciences (Pharmaceuticals, Nanotech, Biotech)

He has been a founding entrepreneur and executive of numerous startups in technology, healthcare, Retail, manufacturing, logistics, Web-Internet, Social Media, etc., as well as a Not-For-Profit organization.
