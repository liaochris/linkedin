PAOLA FIORE’s Summary

Sustainability Management & Communications Specialist. Founder of ETICA@AMBIENTE® Sustainability Management & Communications Consulting.

Graduated in 1999 in Law and Economics with a specialisation in Environmental Law and Management under the supervision of Emilio Gerelli, Public Finance and Financial Law Emeritus Professor at the Italian University of the Studies of Pavia.

Honours degree in 2003 in BA (Hons) Documentary Photography, the internationally recognized course established in late '70s by Magnum Photo Welsh photographer David Hurn, at the Newport School of Art, Media and Design of the British University of Wales College Newport.

MSc in 2000 in Environmental Law and Management, and MSc in 2007 in Business and Corporate Communication; continuing multiple training diploma courses, seminars, workshops and high annual attendance records at conferences and lectures on sustainability management and communications issues, CSR policies and strategies.

With 10 years of experience in sustainability management, communications and coaching activities, worked as independent professional with B2B Private Companies, Public Administrations, Universities, Non profit Organizations and Media Industry in Italy and UK.

Innate passion for documenting, reporting, travelling, understanding different culture and making sense of the importance of Science and Nature continuously support personal life and work activities, inspiring unique creativity and visioning.

Strongly committed to work on sustainability management, communications and coaching programmes, and CSR plans to empower responsible companies creating innovative sustainable business solutions, and increse the bottom line measuring the economical, environmental and social results, and constantly monitoring impacts locally and globally.
