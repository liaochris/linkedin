Julie Gomoll’s Summary

LaunchPad Coworking + Cafe has been in the works since spring of 2007. It's a beautifully design space in downtown Ausint. We rent individual work ares and meeting rooms. The cafe is open to the public.

We're 8 weeks from opening, but this economic mess has hit us hard. Several investors have backed out and the banks are being very tight-fisted. I'm actively looking for investors and corporate sponsors.

Founder and CEO of Go Media, a graphic design firm in Austin. After nine years, sold Go Media to Excite in 1996. Ran communities for Excite for three years. Post-Excite took on various social media gigs, including VPchat, a subscription based Internet chat service, which is still in operation.

Julie Gomoll’s Experience

  • CEO

    LaunchPad Coworking

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Consumer Services industry)

    October 2007 — Present (1 year 10 months)

    LaunchPad Coworking is Austin’s first coworking café. Founded on the concept that when people share knowledge great ideas naturally take off, LaunchPad Coworking offers inspiring workspaces designed to foster productivity, creativity and collaboration.

    Our thoughtfully detailed workspaces, meeting rooms, and conference rooms are available by the hour with discounts for bulk-hour purchases. Each space can be outfitted with a variety of peripherals ready to connect to the customer’s own computer. Here, people can work independently or collaboratively, and free themselves from long-term leases and cramped corporate offices. The comfortable atmosphere at LaunchPad is enhanced by our café featuring specialty coffee drinks, beer, wine and light, healthy fare.

    LaunchPad Coworking will be open in spring, 2008

    August 1996 — January 2000 (3 years 6 months)

  • Founder, CEO

    Go Media

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Graphic Design industry)

    September 1987 — August 1996 (9 years)

    Sold Go Media to Excite in 1996.
