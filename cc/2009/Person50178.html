Jon Cline’s Summary

Profile: Salesforce.com/Web Marketing Consultant

Jon Cline has a proven formula for helping businesses grow and prosper on the Web. Drawing on 10-plus years of experience in Relationship Management and Web Marketing, Cline helps firms thrive in the competitive, constantly changing landscape of Internet Marketing.

Cline demystifies the inner workings of Web Marketing. He brings tremendous value to companies by helping them create and implement new strategies to grow their Web businesses and take advantage of emerging trends.

Cline’s expertise includes:
- Salesforce.com Administration
- Web Solution planning & implementation,
- Internet marketing goal setting and measurement,
- Web Marketing services, including:
--- • Search Engine optimization & marketing (SEO/SEM) - to help firms attract new customers and grow revenue,
--- • Performance tracking - enabling companies to determine return on investment (ROI) and indentify new business opportunities.

Cline tailors services for each client, delivering results to match customer goals.

Peter Cohan, now Principal, The Second Derivative, was employed at another firm when he sought Cline's consulting. "His specific recommendations were truly actionable; we started making changes and enjoying the rewards the very next morning."

France.com owner J.N. Frydman hired Cline in 2003. He says: Cline "has a fantastic ability to deliver on time, and in our experience he always delivered beyond our expectations...."

Cline writes and speaks for corporate audiences on best practices in Web marketing and business development. He also facilitates workshops and Webinars for clients. Some examples:
