Phaedra Boinodiris’s Summary

Recognized by the IGDA as one of the top 100 women in the gaming industry for my work founding WomenGamers.com,
I am now currently a Serious Games Producer @ IBM. I am developing a series of games for technical training, marketing, and extending brand reach and just launched the second game in the INNOV8 game series teaching Business Process Management and Services Oriented Architecure in a Sim-style game.

My first 3D serious game for academic use is a BPM simulation tool built on an FPS engine. I launched IBM's very first Serious Games Day in RTP, NC Feb 2009 which is now an international roadshow.

I have been an entrepreneur for 10 years first with Atlantis Internet Technologies, an IBM business partner focusing on WebSphere technologies and then with WomenGamers.Com (WG). WG is a C corp started 10 years ago with my sister and is now a popular women's gaming portal. We launched the first scholarship for women to attain degrees in game design and development within the United States through SMU, launching over a dozen bright young women into careers with companies like EA, Acclaim, and Activision.
