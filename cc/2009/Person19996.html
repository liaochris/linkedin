Colin Crawford’s Summary

My key interest is the integration of print, online and digital distribution and how technology changes the way audiences and advertisers respond to quality content no matter in which form it is distributed - online, print, mobile or at conferences, seminars and expositions. In this interactive, digital world, media companies have to put audience interests first and provide services and solutions that keeps those audiences engaged and participating.

I believe that the online industry needs to greatly improve transparency in its measurement systems - we need to be able to put in place metrics that measure the intensity of our audience's engagement with various forms of content.

The role of media networks, ad exchanges, demand generation programs plus the developing opportunities in video and mobile will ensure an exciting and vibrant future for all of us in the ever changing media industry
