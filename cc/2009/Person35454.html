Gianfranco Chicco’s Summary

During most of 2008 I’ve been on a sabbatical in Japan. I have also collaborated in the organization of iSummit 2008 international congress in Sapporo and with a creative Japanese company helping them penetrate the European market. Meanwhile I finished the project work of my executive MBA and attended Japanese language school and several photography workshops.

During the last 10 years I’ve been involved in very different projects that shared some common elements: international development and opening of new markets; working under pressure in high performance teams to reach ambitious objectives; understanding of business dynamics and promoting innovation; navigating successfully in a technological environment.

At HSM Italy I’ve been Business Manager and Marketing Director. I wasresponsible of the startup of the company in Italy, operations and marketing & communication activities. HSM organizes the World Business Forum, an event for the business community which brings together management gurus like Jack Welch, Bill Clinton, Michael Porter, Kevin Roberts, Philip Kotler, Malcolm Gladwel, William Ury and Tom Peters. This and other events take place in major hubs like New York City, Milan, Madrid, Mexico DF, Sao Paulo, and Buenos Aires.

I consolidated my experience in event management and marketing in several countries and I share part of it in my blog www.conferencebasics.com.

Before I’ve been involved in other projects including logistics & engineering feasibility studies, internet product development and financial analysis.

In my free time I’m an off/online freelance writer for magazines and blogs and have my own at www.gchicco.com. I occasionally do photography work too.

In the future I’d like to explore the “green” industry. Sustainability will be a strong driver for creating new products and services that not only will make the World a better place, but also offers a lot of opportunities for profitable business.

Gianfranco Chicco’s Specialties:

- Project management: development of new projects in several disciplines and countries, from the business plan to operations
-Marketing & Communication: using traditional and innovative resources, from printed media to web 2.0. Fluent in several languages
-Event design and management: from the design of an “experience” to the realization of it
-Company start-up and international development
- Business writing, blogging and photography
