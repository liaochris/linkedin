Byron Aulick’s Summary

Byron B. Aulick, CDIA+, Project+, PDI+
President and co-owner of DataVault in MA. Serving the imaging industry for over 24 years in many capacities including sales, solutions expert, system implementations, trainer and consultant. Mr. Aulick has trained thousands of students from all over the world and continues to offer training internationally. He has successfully run a scanning services bureau since 1996 and currently consults with business owners implementing scanning bureau services of their own. Mr. Aulick has authored DataVault’s training materials including; ECM Sales training, CDIA+, Needs Assessment, PDI+ and Document Imaging-101 courses. Byron is a subject matter expert to CompTIA and has been training since 1985. Mr. Aulick is a very thorough and passionate teacher, known globally for his excellence in instruction and his high quality training materials that yield highly successful results.
