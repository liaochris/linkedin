Joseph Crawford’s Summary

I started out with the internet in 1994 when i bought my first computer with my own money. I started getting into web development in 1997 by viewing the source code for HTML and then picking up ASP. About a year after using ASP I found PHP and decided to make the switch.

I have been using PHP since 1998. I started to really get involved with web development while creating and managing my own websites. I hit the books hard and started reading everything I could find on website development. I decided not to focus on anything design related but to go for the back-end development.

My library has grown over the years and I have learned so much from all of the dedicated under-paid authors who have written the books.

In 2005 I started up my own company Codebowl Solutions, Inc. While it was somewhat successful I decided to grab a full-time position. I have been working from home since 2005 and I feel that I can be more productive while working at home.
