Jos Schuurmans’s Summary

After six years at Nokia's global communications team, I decided to re-boot my own firm with a new name and a new business proposition.

All transactions - either privately, in busines, or in the public domain and the media - are the result of relationships, built on conversations between people. Cluetail Ltd. - an hommage to the Cluetrain Manifesto and the Long Tail - is an integrated communications services company which helps its customers enhance the quality of their conversations, connections and relationships.

Building on our experience in journalism, organizational communications, and participatory intra- and Internet technologies, Cluetail develops processes and tools which maximize the reach, impact, visiblity and measurability of our customers' communications efforts.

Jos Schuurmans’s Specialties:

Cluetail's consultancy services include current- and desired-state analyses and roadmapping, process and tool development and implementation.

Cluetail's operational support services include technical support, content and channel management, content creation, media monitoring, business intelligence, training, team building, coaching and mediation.
