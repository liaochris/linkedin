Krista Haimovitch’s Summary

I have a Masters degree in counseling psychology and am a certified hypnotherapist.

My labor of love at the moment is TAP -- the Travel Awareness Project -- conceived to inspire & educate a global network of "conscious travelers" who travel with the explicit purpose of promoting awakened states of consciousness in themselves, and others around the world. A book and website on this subject are in the works.

15 years previous experience as a freelance writer, editor and filmmaker now supports innovative coaching techniques designed to unlock creativity and promote forward motion towards growth-oriented, calculated risk-taking for clients.

Happier, more productive choices that align inner experience with both daily and once-in-a-lifetime journeys are our birthright. Leave your comfort zone! A personalized blend of hypnosis techniques, creative unblocking, brainstorming and motivational dialogues will lead to new enthusiasm, vitality and curiosity. Which leads to...???

Krista Haimovitch’s Specialties:
