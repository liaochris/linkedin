Ashley Kingsley’s Summary

Ashley E. Kingsley thrives on connecting people. She is on the cutting edge of Web 2.0/Social Media and brings years of community relations, marketing and business development expertise to her clients.

She has consulted with both non-profit and for-profit organizations, including the University of California San Diego, ColoradoBizBuzz.com, Downtown Denver News, KNRC Radio, Colorado AIDS Project, the Matthew Shepard Foundation, LoDo Music Festival, and Special Olympics Colorado.

In 2001 Ashley created and wrote ‘SoLo in LoDo’ a monthly column featured in the Downtown Denver News. The column has been recognized on WB2 News, Savvy Singles Magazine and continues, eight years later, to get published by online entities as well as an anthology published in 2006.

MAKING A DIFFERENCE

In May of 2005 Ashley founded ASHLEY’S LIST, an e-newsletter designed to encourage people to support small business. The list has grown substantially and has garnered several community partnerships, including This Week in Denver, The Colorado Connector, and more.

In 2007, Ashley founded TWICE IS NICE, a group dedicated to recycling gently used children’s clothing, toys, books and gear in effort to cut down on waste and draw the community together in sharing.

Ashley has volunteered for Coloradoan’s Working Together to Prevent HIV/AIDS, the San Diego Women’s Homeless Shelter. She is currently serves on the Board of Advisers for the Grady Grossman School located in Cambodia to assist in the growth and awareness efforts. She is also on the Steering Committee for AJ Clemmons campaign for CU Regency.

Viral and Grassroots Marketing Campaigns, Social Networking, Business Development, Writer, Blogging, Public Speaker, Company Start Up, Sales, Event Planning, Strategic Partnerships, Networking.
