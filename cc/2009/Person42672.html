Jakolien Sok’s Summary

12 years of international experience in HRM and Recruitment. I've held HR management positions at Seatrade, a global shipping company and the European headquarters of the US multinational Avery Dennison (Fortune 500).

Dutch as native language and a near native command of the English language both verbal and in writing.

Fully up to speed with all current regulations and laws in the HR profession. Business Partner at both management and employee level. At both companies the HR departments needed to be reorganized as well as partly created from scratch. In both instances I achieved these goals and more. Vast experience with Expats, Unions, CLA’s and Works Councils.

Founder of Sokrates Recruitment, an Executive Search and Consulting firm focused primarily on senior HR, Sales and Communication Professionals.
