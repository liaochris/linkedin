Paul Myers’s Summary

Adept business leader, highly accomplished academic professor and driven practitioner with diverse expertise necessary to help executives and managers better understand themselves and their organizations in order to achieve exceptional performance.

Proven leader and teacher focused on developing organizational tools for individuals and teams to excel across multiple industries.

Hands-on management consulting experience with Fortune 100 companies, small start-up companies and non-profit organizations.

Focus on helping managers build organizations that align business, operations, and IT strategies and foster employee commitment and excellence.

Skilled case study discussion leader. Research and teaching include corporate governance, leadership, social entrepreneurship, sustainability, knowledge management, and technology & operations management. Also interested in business school curriculum and policy development and implementation.
