Antonio Lieto’s Summary

Graduated (BA) with honours in Logics and a M.A. in Communications at University of Salerno (with full marks).

My main interests regard Artificial Intelligence (Information Retrivial, Text & Data Mining, Knowledge Represenation Systems, Decision Support Systems) and the study and the analysis of decision making processes.

I'm chief of the editorial board of AeA Review (an online review).

I'm President (november 2006) of Feedback, an universitary project about the start-up simulation of an on line communication enterprise.

I'm a blogger about marketing, communication, new media and technology themes since January 2007.

Since September 2007 I'm author of the survey "Artificial Intelligence" on Comunitàzione.it and I'm member of the editorial board.

Since November 2007 I partecipate, as author, at Nòva Lab Il Sole 24 Ore and I write about research, innovation, artificial intelligence, nformation&computer science, new media, decision making, knowledge management, creativity and sensemaking themes.

More informations about me are available on my personal web page: http://www.antoniolieto.blogspot.com

Mail: alieto@unisa.it
