Stephanie Himel-Nelson’s Summary

Attorney with expertise in government contract, construction, contract and administrative law, as well as extensive writing experience. I own my own company, The Legal Writers Group, which provides legal research and writing services to law firms on a contract basis.

I am a Contributing Editor and the Photography Editor for MOMocrats.com; a contributing writer for D.C. Metro Moms blog; and the owner of Lawyer Mama Omnimedia, LLC, a social media consulting group. My op-ed and political writing has been published in the Washington Post online, Raising Kaine, and the Huffington Post. Some of my essays have also been syndicated by the McClatchy Group, appearing in dozens of online and print publications across the country.

I am also a full-time volunteer, serving as the Director of New Media for Blue Star Families, Inc., a non-partisan, non-profit organization dedicated to supporting and empowering military families.

I am also a professional photographer specializing in documentary style, natural light photography for LightHearted Photography.
