Carmina Pérez’s Summary

Carmina Pérez is a writer, speaker and digital media marketing consultant. She is a veteran of the internet, television and financial services industries, and a social media expert. Her blog, Mogulette-in-the-making, which she created in Feb. 2007, has been featured in the New York Times twice and focuses on tools and resources for businesses. In addition to writing and consulting, Carmina regularly presents workshops on emerging Web 2.0 technologies.

Carmina started her career in financial services, where she spent 14 years in sales and marketing as a Vice President at Lehman Brothers. She then transitioned into broadcast television and worked as an editorial producer at CNN Financial News for 6 1/2 years. That's where she honed her skills in news gathering, writing and video production. From there she moved on to the internet, writing articles on personal finance for AOL Latino and later editing their welcome page and news channel. After about a year she gained enough confidence in HTML programming, web infrastructure, content and page layout to strike out on her own.

In her current consulting practice, Carmina works closely with selected web developers and designers to ensure her clients’ sites are set up to emphasize social interaction and community. She then helps create content strategies designed to attract top quality traffic to the website. This is accomplished by tactical editing of text, video, and images as well as monitoring and maintaining the clients’ presence in the various social networks. This also involves reviewing the site’s analytics and making the most of developing traffic trends. Since she began consulting in Sept. 2007, Carmina has helped over 25 clients with social media marketing and blog set-ups, and has presented more than 35 workshops on web 2.0 technologies for business.
