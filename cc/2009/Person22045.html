Anthony Mok’s Summary

I've 15 years of working experience in creating excellence, change & transformation in organisations. I'm the founder & Principal Consultant of the MINDEF Innovation & Transformation Office (2001-07). I was instrumental in creating her Innovation Framework & Pipeline, & Jumpstart; the change management programme that brought creativity, innovation & entrepreneurship to the Ministry of Defence & Singapore Armed Forces (http://xxiaohao.blogspot.com/search/label/Credentials). These initiatives have attracted the interest of the National Library Board, Prime Minister Office, SPRING Singapore, Civil Service College, & ChervonTexaco.

Through the years, I have developed competencies and skills in creating conversations and interventions for change, like the MINDEF iGame, iThink (http://www.innovationtools.com/PDF/Mass_Ideation_Process.pdf) & iDive. I’ve designed consultation & facilitation tools & processes, & delivered these as organisational interventions to obtain intended outcomes.

I hold a Bachelor Degree in Business Administration (Operational Management & Logistics) with the National University of Singapore and Master Degree in Education (Leadership, Policy & Change) with the Monash University. Currently, I’m working on my PhD with the University of Western Australia. Besides these, I hold the Graduate Diplomas & Diplomas with the Institute of Management Consultant (Singapore), Chartered Institute of Marketing (UK), Association of Business Executives (UK), Institute of Administrative Management (UK). I'm a member of the International Association of Facilitators and a fully certified trainer by the Institute of Adult Learning.

Some of my clients include Roundtable of Sustainable Palm Oil, Ministry of Defence, National Health Care Group, Ministry of Education, Ministry of Law, Land and Transport Authority of Singapore, National Community Leadership Institute, & Gillette Asia Pacific.
