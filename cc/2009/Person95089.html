Stephen Ostrom’s Summary

A dedicated and innovative Network Support Specialist with extensive experience gained through progressive networking positions. Creates solutions to difficult problems, dedicated to customer requirements by providing the successful results required to satisfy client needs and generate new business.

Experience in diverse data and voice networking roles, involved in all phases of data network operations and implementation.
Expertise in the design, procurement, installation, acceptance testing, removal, monitoring and repair of telecommunication circuits, systems and networks as well as conditioned analog voice and data lines, as well as switched voice systems.

Most recently managed the network of Rolls Royce Canada and participated in the installation and design of network moves and upgrades.
Second level support for all EDS Canada clients and internal services and networks.
Previous clients have included the Bank of Canada, the Ontario Government and Canada Post.
