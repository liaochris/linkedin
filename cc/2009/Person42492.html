Jacqui Chew’s Summary

Seasoned marketing and business development professional with experience leveraging online and offline programs to help brands, non-profits and media companies connect with their audiences. Successful track record developing and implementing multi-channel marketing and business development programs for enterprise software, Internet, information security and consumer products and services companies. Experienced in working with cross-functional teams to achieve organizational goals.

Author of articles on social media for iMedia Connection, Chiefmarketer and Association Publishing and Atlanta Business magazines, and has provided expert commentary to business publications including DemandGen Report, Newspaper & Technology and DM News.

Consulting clients include Chupa Chups USA, eBay, IBM e-Business Services, RealEstate.com, SSL International and ThePort Network.
