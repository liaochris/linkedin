Robert Green’s Summary

I am an experienced Systems, Test, Requirements and Automation Engineer with a degree in Aerospace Engineering from Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University. I have extensive experience in requirements elicitation, authoring and maintenance. I have over 10 years of experience providing problem solving solutions, ranging from e-commerce web applications to weapon systems. I have worked for a variety of companies, including Lockheed Martin, Deloitte and Touche and PRICE Systems, where I am currently the Director of Product Development. My responsibilities at PRICE range from managing a team of Systems Engineers and Software Engineers, managing the direction of all PRICE Systems’ solutions, acting in the technical sales support role, while still contributing to the day-to-day production of software. I have assisted various organizations with hands-on, practical process improvement initiatives, which lead to my passion and knowledge of what happens when theory meets the real world.
