John Hadley’s Summary

I help job seekers frustrated with their search. I also work with professionals struggling to increase their visibility and influence at work or in the market. My goal is to empower my clients with the marketing skills that unlock the hidden job market, and land them great opportunities throughout their career.

I offer a number of valuable resources on my website at www.JHACareers.com. I also publish a monthly Career Tips newsletter. To check out the contents of past issues, visit www.JHACareers.com/Newsletter.htm.

John Hadley’s Specialties:

Coaching on Career Search strategy, Crafting a Killer Resume, Creating Cover Letters, Developing Compelling Accomplishment Stories, Networking and Marketing Pitches, Interview Skills and Salary Negotiation, Career Growth, Business Development and Marketing, and Relationship Building.
