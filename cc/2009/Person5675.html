Andrew Kable’s Summary

I am managing director of H2Otalent, Australia's leading recruitment agency focusing exclusively on the high-growth water infrastructure sector. We have a global reach, and are always interested in hearing from water sector talent anywhere in the world.

Also please get in touch if you have a track record in recruitment for the water sector.

Andrew Kable’s Specialties:

Executive Search, recruitment, contractor management and employer branding for water & wastewater industry businesses in the Asia Pacific. Career management and job search support for water industry professionals.
