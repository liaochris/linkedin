Kim Moldofsky’s Summary

I've been a factory worker and a social worker; a dolphin trainer and a corporate trainer. After taking the plunge and becoming a potty trainer and stay-at-home mom, my career was in the toilet for a few years.

Now, in addition to blogging around the momoshpere and Tweeting with roughly 2,000 followers, I help large companies stay abreast of trends and connect with online mom influencers.

As a freelance writer and professional blogger, I've contributed to BabyCenter.com's popular Momformation blog, Chicago Parent magazine, corporate newsletters, and other publications.
