David Biernbaum’s Summary

Consumer packaged goods professional in the field of marketing, retail sales, broker management, and business development. Far reaching background and resilient career with major national brands, niche and licensed products, private label, and many types of specialty brands.

History of building approaches that constantly result in success, equity, and rapid development in retail sales.

Highlights (Partial)

 Leadership highly instrumental at Zooth, Inc. during its most propelling growth years, leading to the profitable sales of the brand to Gillette Co, then Procter and Gamble.

 Helped develop Vi-Jon Laboratories to be a major force in personal care products at retail, ultimately leading to the highly profitable corporate merger with Perrigo, Inc.

 At the Gillette Company and Oral-B Laboratories, led sales forces through high-impact maneuvers often shattering record sales and breaking competition.

 At Beecham Products (GSK) led the initial U.S. test launches of Aquafresh Toothpaste, a major global brand.

 As an independent contractor, has launched national brands, directed national sales, and assisted in all facets of marketing, operations, and business reform; while elevating value and equity for entrepreneurial CPG companies including:

INDUSTRY LEADERSHIP:

 BRAINTRUST panelist on RetailWire.Com, the very popular and distinguished retail blog read daily by many CPG Retail Executives worldwide.

 Frequent Contributor and Columnists on CPG Marketing Topics: Mass Market Retailer, Chain Drug Review, PL Buyer, ECRM Expo, Etc.

 Trade Associations Leadership Committees including currently NACDS Successful Selling and Product to Market initiatives; and several past and current initiatives with ECRM and GMDC.

 Frequent Instructor at American Marketing Association (AMA), and past instructor and speaker at International Institute of Marketing (IIR); and sponsored events with Information Resources (IRI.)
