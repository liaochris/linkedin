Sanford Cohen’s Summary

A graduate of the Ringling College of Art and Design, I founded Design Marketing Group, Inc in 1992. I manage all aspects of the company's daily activities, from new client generation and account service, to design and production.

Sanford Cohen’s Specialties:

Design Marketing Group, Inc. is a well-established full service marketing communications firm servicing client needs both locally and nationally for the past 16 years. DMG offers highly creative targeted solutions for all marketing and communications needs including trade show booth design and show management, identity and branding, public relations, strategic marketing and web and print design.
