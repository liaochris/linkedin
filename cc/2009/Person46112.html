With over 18 years in the Silicon Valley and 16 years specifically in B2B Marketing, my background includes a successful and proven track record at large established companies as well as several start-ups.

My philosophy: The marketing team's goal isn't to push a product down the prospect's throat, but instead to keep wafting about in the prospect's line of vision through a wide variety of media (webinars, seminars, white papers, press mentions, direct mail, web, email, etc.) until a prospect finally raises their hand, asking for help with an immediate particular need.

Jenny Coupe’s Specialties:

Outbound marketing champion: online/web marketing, demand creation, lead generation/nurturing, (drip marketing), search engine optimization and marketing, social media marketing, web production and management, direct marketing, event management, public/analyst relations, partner/channel/reseller marketing, and branding/positioning/messaging.
