Antoinette Follett’s Summary

Bentley-Hall, Inc. is a contract publishing firm that delivers communication and marketing solutions for corporate and professional clients. Our aim is to become an extension of your marketing, and advertising departments, leveraging more than 20 years experience producing materials that enhance brand awareness, increase sales, and educate dealers and consumers. Clients from diverse industries—including Buick, Roland, Carnival Cruise Lines, and Yamaha.
