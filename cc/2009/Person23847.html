David A.Boley II MS,RN’s Summary

Decorated U.S. Navy Veteran with over 10 years experience in diverse health care settings and 6 years in business management, entrepreneurship and consulting.

Motivational Executive and award-winning, visionary leader accomplished in managing teams, running highly efficient meetings, and stoking creative brainstorming sessions. Yes!

Published Registered Nurse dedicated to the creation of novel teaching methods and therapeutic approaches to complex disease states through nursing science.
