Heather Fraser’s Summary

I am passionate about empowering business owners and executives to fall back in love with their companies while boosting financial profits and increasing personal fulfillment.

I am implementing The Growth Coach’s signature group coaching process, unique Strategic Mindset® system, one-on-one coaching and other related services to help business owners, executives and managers examine the current status of both their business, career and personal lives and develop a road map to help them achieve their goals.

Our coaching programs are effective because clients experience growth, success and shifts as they implement key strategies.

I am a speaker on entrepreneurship leadership, management development, life balance and success strategies.
