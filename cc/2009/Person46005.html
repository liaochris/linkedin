Jennifer Devlin’s Summary

Women's ministry leader, international speaker and published author with over a decade of experience ministering to the needs of women. Regional coordinator in volunteer position of women's ministry organization, overseeing 35 distinct groups (2003-2005). Author of two traditionally published books, "Life Principles for Christ-like Living" with AMG Publishers in 2006, and "Verses We Know by Heart" with Randall House in 2008. Contributor to numerous compilation works, as well as writing credits with the Huntsville Times Life Points column and InTouch Magazine Bible study articles, and CBN.com. Speaking experience at local, regional, national and international venues; from small group encouragement to auditorium seating. Mission team leader experience as well, with mission involvement in the Americas and Africa. Interested in speaking to women's groups and churches worldwide, as well as providing ministry training and teaching seminars. Currently working on upcoming book concepts.

Jennifer Devlin’s Specialties:

Accomplished author, presentation development (custom and within theme), co-editor of compilation work, non-fiction writing, public speaking, women's ministry training, leadership training, conference planning and keynote presentations, powerpoint and other media presentations, mentoring and training, marketing material development, internet marketing and publicity, training small group leaders, community networking.
