Arline Wall’s Summary

C-level executive with global supervisory roles in the development and marketing of billion dollar consumer brands at international Fortune 500 companies. Expertise in turn-key product management of packaged goods with award-winning success. Proficient in all phases of product development from conception, product design, sourcing, testing, packaging, production, and planning. Adept at creating new products and revitalizing mature brands. Skilled in marketing strategies including brand identity, pricing, promotion, and distribution. Demonstrated success in building licensed partnerships and promotional programs. Unique background on both the manufacturing and retail sides of the supply chain. Category management experience in accessories, crafts, gift, home décor, seasonal, sporting goods, stationery, tabletop and toys. Distribution experience in book, convenience, drug, grocery, mass and specialty channels. Proven portfolio of developing new businesses, executing strategy, improving sales, and increasing profits.

As Senior Vice President, Product Development & Marketing at Russ Berrie, I developed and marketed over 1500 global products annually. As RUSS Corporate Communications chief, I increased consumer impressions from less than 1 million to over 100 million annually.

As Global Brand Director at Toys ‘R’ Us, I oversaw product marketing/management of $1.8B Core Division for this $11B Fortune 500 Company and the world’s leading toy retailer. I conceived the strategy for and executed exclusive marketing partnership programs with national brands including Animal Planet, Disney/Pixar, Home Depot and others. I received the Toys ‘R’ Us President’s Award for “performance beyond expectations”.

As Vice President, Marketing at Hasbro, I oversaw the U.S. market introduction of the enduring classic game Trivial Pursuit which broke all sales records with 88 million units in 26 countries.
