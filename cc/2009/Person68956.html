Miles Burke’s Summary

My first book was published late last year, The Principles of Successful Freelancing, published by SitePoint. You can read more about the book over at http://www.principlesofsuccessfulfreelancing.com

I'm also co-editor of the very popular email newsletter, SitePoint Tribune, more info at http://www.sitepoint.com/newsletter/archives?id=2

During the day, I am Managing Director of award winning website design and development company, Bam Creative.

I am Chairperson of the Australian Web Industry Association, an organisation dedicated to improving the web industry in Australia.

I am the recipient of the young entrepreneurship award, '40 under 40' by Business News, and appeared in the 2008 edition of 'Whos Who in Western Australia'.

I am also a proud father to three children, and dedicated husband.
