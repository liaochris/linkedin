Peter Groom [peter@petergroom.com]’s Summary

A commercially focused, senior manager with significant interpersonal, project, and operational experience gained with a number of major high technology internationals.

- Familiar with all aspects of both project and development life cycles, with very strong emphasis on service delivery.
- Can manage change across the entire organization, using influencing and communications skills.
- Able to prioritise demand for IT services while promoting a climate of continuous improvement.
