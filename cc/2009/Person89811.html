Scott Whigham’s Summary

I founded LearnItFirst.com as a way to provide high-quality Instructor-Led Training to "regular people". I've been an instructor for nearly a decade in classes where people pay several thousand dollars a week to learn tips and tricks. The problem always has been that small businesses and regular people could not afford the money for these classes and, as such, were unable to get the same benefits. LearnItFirst.com is my way of helping small businesses and regular people gain access to the world's best instructors and learn topics that matter: SQL Server, Exchange Server, Windows Server, SharePoint, .NET, and more.
