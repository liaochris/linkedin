Mark Robertson’s Summary

Mark Robertson is a veteran search engine marketing professional of over 8 years with expertise, and the founder of a popular website, ReelSEO. ReelSEO.com is the first-of-its-kind website for the online video marketing space and provides valuable tips, news, interviews, conference coverage, commentary, and other resources about the online video industry.

As a passionate and experienced search engine marketing professional, Mark is a popular speaker and thought-leader within the media market and search industries. Mark has had the honor of speaking and moderating several industry conference sessions in addition to having conducted numerous seminars nationwide on the power of search engine marketing for local business. Mark has also consulted with several online video and internet marketing companies looking to expand their product range.

Previously, Mark was the Director of Search for Freedom Interactive, the interactive division of Freedom Communications, Inc., a privately owned media company composed of more than 100 newspaper, magazine, broadcast television, and interactive properties throughout the nation. Mark was responsible for overseeing Freedom Communications’ national search and local search lines of business which are critical to Freedom's revenue and audience growth strategies. Specific products under Mark's area of expertise included internal site search, vertical search, contextual advertising, directories, and Freedom's advertiser product offerings for paid search marketing, local search, organic search engine optimization, and video search marketing. In addition to responsibilities of running these lines of business, Mark also provided strategic and tactical guidance in regards to search engine optimization and search engine marketing efforts for Freedom's interactive content.
