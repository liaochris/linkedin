    Fraud Busters, Inc.

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Information Services industry)

    June 1984 — November 1989 (5 years 6 months)

    Fraud Busters, Inc. provided proactive fraud prevention reviews & front-end verifications of the abilities of individuals or companies to perform as stipulated in business, financial or employment agreements. Through proprietary systems, Fraud Busters provided cost effective, verified & analyzed data to assist in the decision making processes of business executives.

    Fraud Busters' products & services were specifically aimed towards helping business operations which were either going through transitions, expanding, or which were vulnerable to loss through fraudulent activity. These categories encompass the financial community, private and public companies, firms seeking capital from outside sources, commercial insurance firms, & corporations & individuals involved in acquisitions, mergers, joint ventures, or in risk-taking operations.
