Beth Bijou’s Summary

I am a user experience evangelist with top-tier web design expertise with process-driven projects. I specialize in creating high-performance, user-based designs that directly impact customer satisfaction, company growth and profitability. My background in the IT industry, user experience design, visual interaction design, website design, usability, and SEO/SEM are a definite asset to any company with an online presence or browser-based applications.

• Drives the design of clean, efficient, useful application interfaces from conceptualization to implementation

• Translates user requirements and complex business processes into highly usable, highly interactive Web applications

• Constructs diagrams, mockups, and prototypes to effectively communicate your designs to internal and external stakeholders

• Works as usability specialist to conduct usability tests, site visits and other user research to inform and validate your designs

• Collaborates with branding and graphics specialists to support the visual style of corporation

• Conceive, document, and evangelize user interface guidelines and design patterns

Having run my own business I know what it takes first-hand to do an excellent job. My enthusiasm and energy enable me to obtain consistent results. I am confident and approachable. You will find me truly dedicated to customer satisfaction and completely loyal. I know how essential ‘team spirit’ is and am successful at motivating and leading staff.

Visit my website www.bijoumind.com for additional information about my skill set, experience, client list and project descriptions. I look forward to speaking with you to further discuss how we may help each other be successful.
