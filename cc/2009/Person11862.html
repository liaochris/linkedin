B. Leigh Hogan’s Summary

I have over ten thousand hours of grammar instruction and writing practice between my education and my personal correspondences. I love my monthly dose of copy editing from LSB, and my Title List continues to grow.

I am constantly learning new skills. Currently, I am learning html. The psychology of sales and advertisements remains a fascination. Anything new interests me.

  • Copy Editor

    Liquid Silver Books

    (Privately Held; Publishing industry)

    January 2009 — Present (1 year )

    First, I examine copy for prescriptive grammar, stilted language, concrete details, psychological plausibility, continuity, repetition of rare words or common phrases, and factual accuracy. Although I mentally diagram each sentence, I accept subjective grammar to maintain the tone of the work. If needed, I send the copy to author for revisions. Then, I line edit to ensure clarity, logic, and flow. I assess the necessity of gerunds, adverbs, and passive verbs. I ensure that any and only material related to one subject is in a single paragraph. Next, I run grammar check. At the author’s discretion, I may add the keywords, tagline, mini-blurb, and blurb. Finally, I return the copy in a timely manner.
