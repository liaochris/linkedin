Edward Mage’s Summary

Over the past seven years I've had many wonderful opportunities to nurture my love for customer satisfaction, refine my chameleon like communication & technical skills all while developing a through method for analytical problem solving and project management. I believe strongly in attention to detail, proven customer service skills, and an ability to learn from, and quickly adapt to a variety of situations.

These experiences would prove useful during my time as Project Coordinator for a Philadelphia non-profit. Assisting in the development and implementation of the scheduled IT support system helped me understand the importance of organization, time management and a passion for bridging the information gap between clients and service providers.

As an Associate Coordinator for DHL, I assisted team supervisors in scheduling, preparing employee reports as well as facilitating portions of new hire training.

My duties at the Pennsylvania Department of Revenue included providing internal support to state employees utilizing SMS, Active Directory, and Tivoli backup and recovery tools. As a part of a small technical assistance center, we were the single point of contact for the creation and resolution of a variety of end user and server issues.

Currently I provide web hosting and technology consultation services for for clients of Exjinn Support Services, and periodically serve as a help desk analyst on a temporary or contract basis.I am also currently pursuing an Associates Degree with a focus on Nursing Informatics, with expectations to graduate in 2010.
