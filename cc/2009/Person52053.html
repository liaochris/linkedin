Jules Ruis’s Summary

Jules Ruis's highly qualified competence is to coach people in Interaction Management based on the Fractal Approach: basic awareness, business awareness and global awareness.

Jules Ruis’s Specialties:

Management consultancy, Training Interactie Management (TIM), cooperation within Triple Helix (i.e. education, industry and government), fractal geometry, building professional networks, Brainport South-East Netherlands.
