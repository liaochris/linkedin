Henk van der Duim’s Summary

Over 15 years of project-, interim management experience combined with the urge to create music and write poems & lyrics. A wonderful source of inspiration. Very thankful for the gift of being able to look at things from a different perspective.

2005: the birth of ON-Mix Media, a digital label. Currently hosting over 45 artists and more than 100 releases. I've released 13 albums/ep and 2 videos.

In a few words I'm a serial entrepreneur. I've founded/co-founded several companies and have sat in leadership in many start-ups in need of the entrepreneur spirit, drive and focus.
