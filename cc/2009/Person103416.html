Noel Huelsenbeck’s Summary

15 year tracked record as an entrepreneur and consultant within the telecommunications and wireless industry. Driven to help corporations get control and visibility of telecom and wireless expenses as well as help them procure "Best of Breed" service's from the major telecom and wireless carriers.

Broad experience with legacy and Next Gen voice and data services such as MPLS, frame relay, T-1's, DS-3's, Optical Carrier, Metro-Ethernet, VoIP and SIP as well as traditional legacy local, long distance, calling cards and conferencing services.

Focused on Telecom and Wireless Expense Management Software and creating methodologies to lower costs and get control of telecom and the ever growing wireless expense. http://www.vocio.com

Noel Huelsenbeck’s Experience

  • President

    Vocio - Telecom Expense Managment

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Telecommunications industry)

    May 2001 — Present (8 years 3 months)

    Vocio Inc. is the premier provider of Telecom and Wireless Expense Management Software to Mid Market Enterprises. Our web based application V-Vision allows all paper, CD-ROM, web, XML and EDI invoices to be processed timely and automatically detects billing errors and flags them for review by telecom or financial analysts. The application also provides an up to date accurate inventory of all services so that no payments are made for former employees or closed locations.

    All invoices are audited and inventoried and reports can be automatically e-mailed to CIO’s, CFO’s, VP’s, Directors, Managers or Analysts on any broad or granular metric relating to spend or inventory. Vocio’s track record has been to lower telecom and wireless spend on average 20% or more within 90 days.

    Vocio also has a carrier services division to help clients procure and provision telecom and wireless services any where in the world. Vocio was founded in 2001 and is headquartered in San Diego.
