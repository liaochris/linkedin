  • Singer, Composer, Producer

    Full Frontal Music

    (Entertainment industry)

    1997 — 1999 (2 years)

    Wrote, performed and co-produced one-man show of Monologues and Songs "(Clowns, Goddesses & Tough Guys.") Subject dealt with growing up, having heroes, and moving on.
    Beginning a coffeehouse, the show developed at The Blue Rider Theatre in Chicago, then at Live Bait Theatre in Chicago. Originally a solo work with a stage, a piano and me, it grew into having a live, bluesy rock band to perform the songs. We recorded the show's songs, ("Clowns, Goddesses & Tough Guys"), then recorded and released a 2d CD, ("peachy"). The band featured Shawn Wallace, Blair Holmes and Scott Bennett. Scott has gone on to record and tour with Brian Wilson. The show itself was co-produced with David Flatley, and directed by Jeremy Wechsler.
