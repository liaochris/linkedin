Jaroslav Cír’s Summary

I live in London and in Prague. I work for Unilever as a global Consumer & Market Insight Manager for Rexona.

I believe that collaboration among creative individuals is the future of insight generation and marketing. Connecting people and brands, and working with people to generate new ideas and solve problems is what excites me about my job.

Jaroslav Cír’s Specialties:

• Research in Central and Eastern Europe
