Oliver Starr’s Summary

Experienced Entrepreneur and Executive with extensive experience in business development, community building and social media, blogging, mobile technology including substantial experience with mobile applications, marketing copy writing from advertising and promotional materials to technical documents as well as blog posts and feature articles, business strategy particularly for early stage ventures, and sourcing of capital from private and institutional investors.

I also have experience in network administration and hardware management from purchasing to deployment and maintenance. Previous work includes product development, biological and biochemical research as well as genomic database sales experience.

In addition I have blogged professionally for the past 5 years (not only independently but also for two private and one public company as well as for the TechCrunch Network) Finally I worked for nine months at a venture fund in Orange County, CA.

I have been the successful founder of several start-ups (both acquired) as well as more than 15 years high level corporate consulting with exceptional results and references.

I'm currently looking for a new challenge in technology or green-tech where I can truly make a difference. Ideally a forward facing role that might involve community development, evangelism, blogging and business development or strategy. I thrive in entrepreneurial organizations that put a premium on passion, creativity and an unstoppable work ethic. My perfect position is one in which I can have a direct impact upon the prospects of the company and a role in developing the organization's culture as well as its public persona. I especially enjoy working with truly innovative technologies and very smart people. I would also be very excited to once again work for a venture fund, especially one focused on sustainable technologies and/or disruptive innovations in biological sciences, mobile applications or medical devices.
