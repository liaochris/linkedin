Shaun Connolly’s Summary

Goal:
Entrepreneurial software leader looking to leverage proven business strategy, software development, product management and marketing expertise to grow or transform early stage or midsize software companies into successful market leaders.

Professional Summary:
* 20 years as a software executive, business strategist, product development, and technical marketing professional

* Well-respected for a strong combination of development and marketing experience that’s rooted in a deep understanding of the complete lifecycle of bringing individual products and entire product suites to market.

* Proven ability to build software business valuations, target companies for acquisition, position companies for acquisition, and handle the challenges inherent in corporate integrations (people, process, and business)

* Rare blend of hands-on technical expertise and keen business sense that provides the ability to talk in-depth on both technical and business issues.

* Focused on inspiring people to be passionate about teamwork, execution, and respect for employees, customers, partners, and community

* Skilled at operating in a fast-paced business environment, solving complex problems, making decisions quickly, wearing several hats, and constantly exuding a “whatever it takes to get the job done” positive attitude.
