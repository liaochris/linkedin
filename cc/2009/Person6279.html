Angela Ploetz’s Summary

POSH Space stands for Productive Office - Stylish Home and was created with the concept that people desire to live in a space that appeals to them visually, however; you can not enjoy your space if it is full with clutter.

My goal is to help people create a space that supports them, mentally, physically, and aesthetically. When you are free from the chaos you have the capacity to create more of what you want.

In additional to traditional home organizing services, POSH Space offers three specialized service areas: Home Paper Organization, Bridging The Gaps-Organizing For Super Parents, and Unpacked In Three Days-Moving & Relocation Services.

My goal is to continue growing my business and offerings to a national level.

Angela Ploetz’s Specialties:

1. Home Paper & Filing. In an ocean of paper we help people gain control with a system that will change the way you think about paper.
2. Moving & Relocation Specialists. We help executives get their homes unpacked and in place in a few short days taking the stress out of moving.
3. Home Sanity-Bridging the Gaps for Super Parents.
We help busy parents find how their homes are not functioning efficiently by evaluating four core areas to determine how the function of the home can be improved.
