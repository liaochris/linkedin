Charlene Bowden’s Summary

CSB Management Group was established in 2006. The President & CEO has an extensive background in administration management and consulting. A decade of experience has formed CSB Management Group, an agency recognized for providing its clients comprehensive management and consulting services in the areas of artist and career development. CSB Management Group is guided by a mission to provide professional services designed to inspire others to take active steps toward their goal.

CSB Management Group provides full management services. We are the movers, motivators and communicators that work with everyone who has a stake in the artist's career. The objective is to make sure everyone is effectively and passionately pulling together to make the artist shine.

CSB Management Group offers limited management services to artists who need periodic consultation sessions. Services will be structured to fit the needs of the artist and the manager. This service lays a foundation for artists during the early stages of their career development. Limited management services are also provided for corporate, small and non-profit businesses.

Services include:

* Goal setting
* Planning
* Corporate structure
* Assessments
* Legal and financial advisement
* Marketing plan
* Publicity/promotional materials
* Image consulting
* Production
* Press Kit development
* Music Industry Workshops
