Bob Smits’s Summary

I have been managing in several industries in different positions for more than 30 years ( interim management, programme management, executive management in television, business reorganisation management, involved in a lot of innovational developments) Since 1979 also busy working in the ICT industry mostly regarding innovative projects and programs for the media and multimedia. The last fifteen years I'm primarily working in the internet and multimedia industry (as project manager, consultant and interim manager).

I like using my experience as a management coach. Very experienced in developments in the new media industry and cross media business solutions, internet services development and e-commerce.
