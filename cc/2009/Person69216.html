Miroslav Kostic’s Summary

I'm an illustrator, animator and graphic designer from Novi Sad, Serbia.

After finished high school for graphic design as technician of graphic design and propaganda, I applied at the Academy of Art in Novi Sad, at graphic communications department.

During my studying I had worked for few Serbian publishers doing mostly children illustration and utility design.

Today I'm finishing my first animated movie, illustrating books, design flash online games, storyboarding and etc.
