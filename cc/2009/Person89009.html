Saurav Das’s Summary

I have a little more than 12 years of diversified experience in the areas of pooled-campus recruitment, business development, branding, media relations among other activities.

Quite a multi-tasking guy, I have spent the last seven years of my career with academic institutions and led teams to recruit more than 10,000 students not just from the institutions I worked for but also from those in and around Mumbai, Navi Mumbai, Thane and neighbouring districts. I did initiate and implement some of the largest joint-campus drives in the region for multi-national IT as well as non-IT companies along with my team.

Of late, I am focusing on starting some ventures in the field of education like a primary school for the economically backward with corporate and individual sponsorship; and a venture for free career counselling (mentoring) using the social networking sites for young engineering and management graduates.
