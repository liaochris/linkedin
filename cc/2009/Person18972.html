Chuck Palm’s Summary

Chuck began his career by serving his country in the Communications corps. of the United States Army. During a tour of duty that included living overseas, Chuck's responsibilities including training his team members on technology that was several years ahead of anything available commercially.

Upon being honorably discharged from the Army, Chuck sought out a challenge in the computer industry and quickly became a Certified Computer Network Engineer and Wireless Networking specialist.

Chuck's list of clients include companies such as IBM, Price Waterhouse Coopers and the Orlando magic.

Chuck has continued to be a trailblazer and specialist in new technologies having started two companies before he turned 35.

He is often asked to speak on a variety of technology topics at tradeshows and organizations including groups like Gartner, ASCII, and McAfee.

In 2007 Chuck started a new venture called IPN, the Internet Podcasting Network and has already produced countless Podcasts for clients as well as IPN.

The rapidly growing company produces and manages podcast series such as "Podcasting for Your Business" and has created shows for fortune 500 companies as well as small startups.

Both his name and IPN are often mentioned in iTunes and several other podcast directories.

Chuck lives in Tampa with his wife and 2 children, and is the author of the book "What the Heck is Podcasting?" due out Summer of 2008.

For more information on how to contact Chuck Palm for speaking engagements or his seminar series, visit IPN at http://ipnetcast.com
