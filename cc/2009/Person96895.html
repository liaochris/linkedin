Tarek Mohamed’s Experience

  • CIO and acting COO

    PROMIS Solutions AG

    (Public Company; 11-50 employees; Computer Software industry)

    May 2008 — Present (1 year 8 months)

    Tarek Mohamed has earned degrees in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science. He has twenty-five years of experience in IT, with more than 15 years in Business Process Analysis, Modelling and Implementation. Tarek was responsible for the success of many high value projects in Utilities, Finance, Insurance, Manufacturing and Commerce. His can-do attitude, paired with his understanding of client needs and his in-depth knowledge of state-of-the-art IT makes him uniquely qualified to bridge the gap between Business and IT.

    After planning and implementing Workflow Systems, he was Casewise's Partner for Germany and the Middle East. In 2005, he started with Proforma, were he left his post as Director Services & Support EMEA to join PROMIS GmbH.

    Very recently, Tarek has become one of the co-founders and shareholders of PROMIS Solutions AG, where he has taken the roles of CIO and acting COO and is the responsible executive for the delivery of PROMIS Solutions high quality services.

  • CTO

    PROMIS Software & Services GmbH

    (Computer Software industry)

    November 2007 — Present (2 years 2 months)

    Tarek is also still CTO of the PROMIS Group, which is now supporting the AG and serves as a ressource pool.

    In this role, Tarek has combined several IT and Service related responsibilities, namely
    - Software Development
    - Management of Professional Services
    - Management of IT Infrastructure

    Software Development
    Tarek's main task was to create a new, holistic Enterprise Architecture Tool reflecting the knowledge of PROMIS Staff regarding the existing and future market demand.
    Based on his experience as Software Developer and Product Manager, Tarek started by creating the high level concept and architecture for the desired tool, which he broke down to the level of function descriptions required for development.
    He managed the selection process to find a development team, including the creation and issuing of RFIs and RFPs, a comparison matrix to allow for an informed decision, and the review and valuation of the final proposals.
    Tarek completed this task by providing the board with the decision for a development partnership and the accompanying cost- and development-plan.
    This has been the core basis for PROMIS' business plan.

    Management of Professional Services
    Based on his personal experience in this area - Tarek has been managing service, support and training in many employments since 1996 - Tarek also was responsible for PROMIS' service offerings and their execution. Amongst other things, he developed specific EA workshops and trainings based on his experience as Manager / Director Services and Support at Proforma and Metastorm.

    Management of IT Infrastructure
    As the technical officer at PROMIS, Tarek also provided the necessary IT and communication infrastructure necessary for a company, whose requirements for flexibility and 24/7 access had to meet clear budget limitations.
