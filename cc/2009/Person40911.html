Iceman Baldy’s Summary

Iceman Baldy is principal of the Iceman Baldy Group, which consists of the Black Internet Marketing Forum, BlackWepreneur Network and his weblog at Iceman Baldy.com. He has interests in technology, entrepreneurship, and personal development.
