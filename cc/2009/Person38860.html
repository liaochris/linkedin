Philip Edward Alexy’s Summary

I have over 14 years of experience in both major film and television post-production projects, including "Charlotte's Web", "Star Wars, Episode One", "Casper", "Flubber", "Angela Anaconda" and "Stargate SG-1". I have worked in both large and small studio facilities and have seen all aspects of CG animation, from bidding, pre-production, team building, production crunches, and post-production PR.

Philip Edward Alexy’s Specialties:

Advanced experience in Maya[animation, modeling], Softimage 3|D[animation, modeling], XSI 4.2[animation, modeling].
Intermediate experience in Lightwave[animation, modeling], 3DStudio Max & Character Studio[animation], Houdini[animation], Photoshop, Adobe Premiere. Boujou[tracking].
Team supervision, project management.
