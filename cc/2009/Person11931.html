Trisha Hadley’s Summary

BlueFire Multimedia is a Michigan-based creative services provider for mostly small and mid-sized companies, both locally and across the globe. From print to web, we help companies identify and craft their message into effective visual marketing.

We believe that your business and/or product image is vital. This is true whether you’re a Fortune 500 or a small start-up. Creating a cohesive brand to deliver the message is as important as a well-developed business model. We are interested in working with clients who share this value and who are dedicated to making their business grow.
