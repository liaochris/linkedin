    May 2009 — Present (3 months)

    Formed by PRSA Houston's board in 2001 with an initial seed grant, PRFH has grown its scholarship funds through silent auctions, a professional development fundraiser last November and individual donations. Chapter members and guests will have an upcoming opportunity to contribute to PRFH through its silent auction held in conjunction with this year's Excalibur Awards on June 9.

    PRFH was formed to support educational excellence by helping prepare high school and college students for careers in public relations and communication. The group will award student scholarships and distribute funds to educational programs that support public relations and communication.

  • ETC - Communications Committee Chairwoman

    Houston Technology Center

    (Public Relations and Communications industry)

    January 2009 — Present (7 months)

    Building a successful technology company requires more than just an entrepreneurial spirit; it requires a business environment that fosters growth and innovation.

    Houston is home to a number of resources that make it easy for you to do just that - from specialized associations to major tech-friendly corporations. So how do you take advantage of these resources?

    The answer is simple: Houston Technology Center's Emerging Technology Council (ETC). At the ETC, we specialize in helping technology companies tap into everything Houston has to offer. Think of us as your technology "concierge" - a group that provides single-point access to the organizationsand institutions here to help you succeed.

    There is a variety of networking and learning programs and materials designed to assist you in generating new business opportunities and producing innovative technologies.
