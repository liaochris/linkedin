Piero Refolo’s Summary

*** Professional:
He begins his professional experience in 1997 in worldwide automation leader ABB, taking part in relevant projects where he is involved in the development of systems and software for control and automation of industrial plants. He participates actively at start-up phase of several plants.
In September 1999 he decides to face a new professional challenge, beginning an experience in a start-up company named Info Solution (at that time 4 workers). He becomes co-owner of this firm in 2000.
At the beginning he takes part in projects in aero-space area. Meanwhile, he begins taking account management activities. He participates at every evolution phase of the firm (up to 80 workers, 3 sites), doing various activities and playing several roles required for the business growth. Thus he acquires “on field” management skills, deeply enhanced on theoretical side by a two - years Executive Master of Business Administration (EMBA) at MIP.

In 2007 he gives way Info Solution attracted by a new professional challenge, co-founding AERMATICA SpA.
