Brent Sapp, MBA’s Summary

Business/organizational problem solving, innovation, and implementing ideas are my "fastballs". I thrive on discovery and developing new strategies that can produce quantifiable results for companies, non-profit organizations and individuals.

My expertise and results in branding and business development have enabled companies to reach new audiences, position their products and services as market leaders, and quickly build rapport with current and future customers.

I've increased sales 800% my first 18 months as EVP, I secured a state wide contract that generated 900k for a start-up, I created a unique Value Proposition for a 1.5 billion dollar distributorship in a mature market, I've generated over 100% EBITDA on marketing campaigns, etc. I've also written a book and training series that is used domestically and internationally (thanks to a great publisher).

My experience spans several industries – I dive into new fields, ask the crucial (Freakonomics) questions, identify the "hidden truth", and discover new alternatives to produce results. I resemble the individual Daniel Pink describes in his book "A Whole New Mind" - I create novelty and nuance not routine, I craft emotion into facts and figures, and I create "stories" that differentiate more than design.

I'm passionate about making difference here and around the world. I'm also a writer who strives to translate complex concepts into simple stories for others to enjoy.
