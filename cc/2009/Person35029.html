Gareth Young’s Summary

CPA and Chartered Accountant (UK), former M&A principal, running my own business since 2006 and finding lots of new things to do. In addition to helping businesses obtain millions of dollars of training-related state income tax credits and delivering extensive training and strategic consulting to a major CPA firm, I have performed start-up CFO, strategic consulting, information memorandum and other financial services.

I enjoy a balance of using my expertise to help others and taking on new, sometimes scary challenges, and I hope that I will continue to be amazed at the opportunities that present themselves when I approach situations with an open mind.

I have also been writing novels since 2003, and am soon to put out my first (The Grace of Guilt); I expect a second to follow within the year. My 2008 accomplishments are: hiking up Half Dome; seeing Elvis Costello at the Ryman; watching the Kirov Ring Cycle at the Met; and being ordained a Zen Priest.

Gareth Young’s Experience

  • Sales and Delivery

    Alpharesults

    (Accounting industry)

    2006 — Present (3 years)

    Helps businesses of all sizes to identify, quantify, document, and file for retraining tax credits that can be substantial, in some instances resulting in a refund of 50% of Georgia state income tax paid within the last three years (if combined with the jobs tax credit, in which Alpharesults also specializes, the taxpayer can sometimes realize a credit of the full amount of income tax paid/payable). Typically our clients are:

    * Unaware of these tax credits
    * Frustrated by the complexity of meeting the requirements of the credits
    * Overwhelmed by the resources and costs required to file for these credits

    We maximize the tax credits while minimizing the risk, resources, and costs associated with obtaining them. In tough economic times, this can be of enormous value to clients.
