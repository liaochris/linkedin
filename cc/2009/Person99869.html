SOFTWARE SALES, MARKETING & OPERATIONS EXECUTIVE

A resourceful business professional who has developed a core set of tools and processes that have repeatedly grown shareholder value in diverse industries including: wireless communications, financial services as well as Internet commerce / exchange and Infrastructure software solutions

• As President - responsible for building the OEM & VAR channels and the support infrastructure while concurrently developing the Marketing and Public Relations strategy necessary to establish the company as the leader in each of its chosen markets in the western hemisphere
• As EVP Sales, Marketing & Operations - designed the strategy and navigated the company from a retail orientation to an OEM focus which resulted in software margins increasing from 70% in 2004 to a profitable 91% in 2005 and rising to over 99% in 2006
