Thomas Shaw’s Summary

Currently consulting and operating as an Interim Marketing Leader to Fortune 500 and growth oriented clients, Tom recently has been called to advise Accenture and their clients as one of their Financial Services Marketing experts. Recently, Tom stepped in at CreditReport.com as CMO to help align their marketing platform for growth over the next 18 months, planning budgets, resources, vendors and agencies with immediate results.

Tom was previously the Senior Vice President (SVP), Marketing, at H & R Block/Option One Mortgage managing the marketing function for the mortgage organization and supporting its 1000+ national sales team.

In early 2007, he left a (5 year) senior marketing role with New Century Financial, Home123 Mortgage, and New Century Mortgage, where he managed a $100+ Million P & L for Retail, Builder and Wholesale Marketing driving the direct marketing, direct to consumer business (B2C) and business-to-business (B2B) marketing, supporting a 2000+ sales team.

Retail Responsibilities included: Direct Marketing, Agency Management, branding activities, lead generation, research, business development, advertising, collateral production and multimedia services. For company’s wholesale arm, at which he was responsible for a host of revenue-driving, business-to-business marketing programs.

Before joining New Century, Tom brings veteran experience from the advertising agency world and has held marketing leadership positions for a variety of companies, including FirstPoint Energy Corp and Shopzilla.com (formerly BizRate.com).

Tom has a highly rated marketing blog - http://www.MarketingExecutive.biz
Recognized as one of the Most Influential Marketing Blogs and Podcasts by Ogilvy and Mather Advertising
