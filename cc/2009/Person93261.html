Donna Gayles, CIR’s Summary

Synopsis of Recruitment Experience
--AIRS Certified, dynamic and result-oriented Senior Recruiter with 11 years of experience; full life cycle recruiting, sourcing and staffing (recruit on a national, multi-location level).
--Responsible, loyal, reliable and independent worker with high level of enthusiasm and creativity.
--Successfully partnered with and consulted to hiring Managers, Directors and VPs on job specifications and development of effective recruitment strategies.
--Educated managers about hiring procedures, approval processes, & exceptions. Enforced HRS Staffing policies; responded to all customer inquiries with sense of urgency.
--Valued as an expert in sourcing the “passive” candidates; including cold calling and researching.
--Develop sourcing strategies and solutions to support company recruiting goals.
--Consistently surpassed recruitment goals with in the first three month of employment.
--Extensive experience with multiple Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS): PeopleSoft e-Recruit, RecruitMax, Taleo, Brassring, PeopleClick, Hiredesk and iCIMS.
--HR responsibilities have included compensation analysis, salary negotiations, new hire orientation, employee relations, training, benefits, payroll, terminations and exit interviews.
--Supported Human Resources efforts in 90% retention of candidates.
