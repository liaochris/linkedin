Neil Gibbons MBA’s Summary

Executive Director on an interim management basis of Henley Management College's open enrollment programs, encompassing responsibility for 5'500 program members on MBA, DBA and post-experience MSc programs. Henley is the UK's longest established business school, with an outstanding track record and heritage, currently ranked within the top 10 world-wide in two of The Economist's Top 10 MBA rankings. Currently a member of the executive team concluding a merger with University of Reading's Business School to create a top 5 European business school in revenue terms within fiscal 2008-9. Leading business development and investment into emerging fast-growth Asian and African markets.
