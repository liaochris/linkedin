Rob Cram’s Summary

Currently woking part-time as a event manager for a major international hotel chain, as well as an executive managing distributor with Essante Worldwide.

A UNIQUE EXPERIENCE>>>> Upon doing some product research, I kept coming across a really unique one of a kind product and company time and time again that offered an opportunity that looked way to good to be true....

I had never done this type of thing called network marketing, so I decided to give it a shot, with very little investment to lose if it didn't work out for me. I got involved with this network marketing company called Essante Worlwide (formerly - For Ultimate Nutrition (FUN) Unlimited) in May of 2007.

Never being involved in this type of program, i was a little lost for the first month or so. Upon using the training and tools provided by the company as suggested by my mentor and friend, I quickly began to accelerate and learn what network marketing is about.

Now, months into my venture with Essante Worldwide, while working on the business about 8-10 hours a week I have grown my success team of associate distributors worldwide and I had no network marketing experience.

When I joined Essante Worldwide when they were called FUN Unlimited, they were only distributing in the USA and Canada.

I had set a personal goal for myself once I grasped a hold of the network marketing concept ... it was to make this company worldwide... I decided to expand the market with a new target... International... I have done it!!
