Kyle Mosley’s Summary

KAM provides each client with the Knowledge of the industry, the Aptitude of an experienced recruiter, and then Meets client needs by representing the top talent in your industry.

We enable our clients to build productive, cohesive and diverse teams. KAM conducts Detailed Screening and Reference Checks of submitted candidates, Interview Debriefing & Feedback, Assists in Offer Negotiations, Salary Surveys, Benefit Reviews, Criminal & Initial Drug Screening*.

Kyle Mosley’s Specialties:

KAM Consulting Services is a professional recruiting and staffing firm located in the Houston, TX area. We excel at placing professionals in Engineering, Manufacturing, Bio-Pharm, R &D/Scientific, Human Resources, Accounting, Management, Legal and other related fields.

We perform contingency, retained, and contract (temp) search services for employers nationwide.
