John Farrall, CFA’s Summary

Investment professional since 1993 specializing in equity investments as a buy-side analyst in mutual fund, private client, and hedge fund arenas. Responsible for investments decisions within the Health Care sector since 1995 - current equity investments of ~$2 billion AUM across ~100 securities, mostly above $1 Bn in market capitalization.

Derivatives experience dates back to 1997 in listed options with various hedge funds, LLC partnerships, and a brief experience trading on the floor of the CBOE. Now responsible for all listed derivatives strategies across all sectors - notional of over ~$500MM is actively being hedged with ~$5.2 billion in AUM impacted by a listed contract.

Also was part of the team responsible for development of our structured products capabilities ~ $575MM in notional issued over the past three years.

Interested in innovation, creating a better client experience through financial engineering, developing more attractive asymmetric-return investments, leading organizational change and open networking collaborations.

Students or recent graduates are welcome to invite me to connect, call, or email me for informational interviews and/or help with beginning a career in investment management.
