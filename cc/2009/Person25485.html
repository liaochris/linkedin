Deb Titus’s Summary

Deb Titus joined the training and development industry as a trainer with Dale Carnegie in 1984. She delivers a full library of focused competency builder workshops as well as four core programs including The Dale Carnegie® Course in Effective Leadership and Communication, Leadership Training for Managers, High Impact Presentations and the Sales Advantage. She continues to reach out to Women in Business as well as the Young Professional Networks throughout New Hampshire. Deb possesses an acute intuitive people sense with an unwavering commitment to improving the performance of people and organizations.

In 1992 she pursued her interests in organizational development consulting with a focus on human capital management strategies including competency model development, online competency assessments and systems that link training to career planning, succession planning, performance management and business growth. Deb was a weekly talk radio guest to discuss day-to-day leadership, management and workforce issues.

Back with Dale Carnegie since late 2004, she continues to be sought out by business leaders to assist them in building a more competent workforce, compelled to perform at levels that generate outstanding results. Through a blended and tailored approach, they administer online assessment tools, consulting and training to develop individuals, teams and organizations to perform at levels that make them leaders in their respective industries. While she is a member of a global operation with global reach, her focus is on the local touch in New Hampshire.

Originally from New York, she moved to Derry, NH in 1986 where she and her husband lived for 21 years. They now live in an Auburn, NH 1908 farmhouse. She is a proud parent of transitioning adults, Geoff (1988) and Sarah (1990). She enjoys most sports and the outdoors. Four-legged family members include a dog and two horses.

Deb Titus’s Specialties:

Organization Development Consulting, Team Member Engagement, Competency Assessments, Leadership Effectiveness, Sales Effectiveness, Customer Service Focus, Process Improvement, Communication/Presentation Effectiveness. Three Distinct Deliver Methods: Onsite Corporate Solutions - Public Time-Spaced Training Programs - Public 1, 2 and 3-day Seminars.
