Stephen Gilfus’s Summary

Steve is a serial entrepreneur, business leader, and noted software designer and inventor. He has been noted as being instrumental to the development of the eLearning market space and making Blackboard a leading provider of e-Learning software and services, He has presented and keynoted several leading global industry conferences and has lectured on teaching and learning on the Internet and the growing online learning marketplace at hundred’s of academic institutions within the US, Canada, UK, Europe, Australia, and Latin America.

His expertise managing fast growth Internet software companies coupled with his entrepreneurial passion has been critical to Blackboard's success and ability to go from start-up to a public company, in 2004 (Nasdaq: BBBB). The company enjoys over $250 million in annual revenues, with ~950 employees and over a $1B market cap with Blackboard's flagship products being used by millions of students across more than 3,600 institutions in 70 countries. In 1998, Gilfus co-founded CourseInfo LLC, which merged to form Blackboard Inc. (at http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Courseinfo)

He is currently General Partner of Gilfus Ventures llc. (with investments in WeddingWire.com, UpsideOver.com and several other internet start-ups), Chairman of the Board of Upside Over Inc., (an internet specialty retailer), and Chairman and CEO of Event Innovation Inc.

Gilfus holds a B.S degree from Cornell University where he was an academic scholar. He is a member of the Northern Virginia Technology Council, Advisory Board Member of Entrepreneurship @ Cornell, and founder of the Cornell Entrepreneur Organization.
