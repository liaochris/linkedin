Andrew Knott’s Summary

I have about 12 years experience developing web applications for a diverse range of clients including BBC, Channel 4, WWF, Jamie Oliver, the Art Gallery of New South Wales, Alexander McQueen, Yahoo! UK and Orange UK.

I am an enthusiastic technology evangelist and a proponent of the importance of user (human) experience in all facets of life. I consider myself a technical craftsperson and I am a great believer in the potential of people to do amazing things given the right tools, good information and productive partnerships.

Recently my professional focus has been on developing with Ruby and I have developed a number of large scale applications using 'Rails' for Channel 4, Orange, Charlotte Church, Camron UK and others.

I am also currently developing custom mobile applications for the iPhone and iPod Touch.

Andrew Knott’s Specialties:

Technical Direction, User Experience, IA, Ruby on Rails, Cocoa, Object Orientated Design, Agile Development, Physical Computing, iPhone applications.
