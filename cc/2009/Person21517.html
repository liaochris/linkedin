Cyndi Wiley’s Summary

My work has been seen in the March 2003 issue of PRINT magazine, the July 2007 issue of Curve magazine, the February 2005 issue of Esquire magazine, the July 2005 issue of Fortune magazine, the documentary movie The Asphalt Gospel and The Rachael Ray Show.

Cyndi Wiley’s Specialties:

Armed with a MacBook Pro, I have expert-level software skills using Adobe Creative Suite (CS3). I am proficient with both Macintosh and Windows systems, QuarkXPress, Dreamweaver, Flash, Word and PowerPoint.

I also have exceptional communication skills and can diffuse a difficult situation with humor and creative problem solving.

Cyndi Wiley’s Experience

  • HWIC (Head Woman in Charge)

    Studio3b Creative

    (Graphic Design industry)

    October 2000 — Present (8 years 10 months)

    I started Studio3b as a side business to my day job as a creative director. Over the past seven years, I have built a loyal customer base and have transitioned Studio3b into my full-time passion. Studio3b is a small, but agile company. I consult on how best to utilize professional designers, how to incorporate social media into your business plan and can actually perform design work in addition to consulting. Tying all the pieces together forms good branding for you and your company.

    I love to work with individuals and organizations that work to better human existence.

    I design packaging, websites, logos, business cards, promo materials and coordinate printing. I also screen print, paint, draw, own a commercial 15-needle embroidery machine and coordinate promotional products for numerous companies in St. Louis, Missouri, and Des Moines, Iowa.

  • Creative Director

    (Pharmaceuticals industry)

    April 2001 — July 2007 (6 years 4 months)

Concepted a retail sales program that increased revenue by over 200% during its first year, by researching ways to increase customer retention. Developed long-term relationships with vendors and clients, handled multiple projects including product launches that required full branding, package design, magalog design, advertising, trade show booth design, web design, coordination of photographers, programmers and managing staff.
