Richard Martin’s Summary

Provides supervision to staff throughout the Virginia Department of Social Services in the achievement of the department's mission, goals, and objectives through research, analysis, evaluation, and recommendations concerning state legislation, regulations, and program operations. Coordinates the activities of the State Board of Social Services and the Child Day-Care Council and serves as the department's primary staff to both bodies.

Richard Martin’s Specialties:

legislative research and analysis, regulatory writing, intergovernmental relations, public relations, meeting planning and coordination

Richard Martin’s Experience

    Virginia Department of Social Services

    (Government Administration industry)

    September 1994 — Present (15 years 3 months)

    Serves as the department’s liaison to the State Board of Social Services and the Child Day-Care Council. Coordinates and manages the department’s legislative responsibilities by:
    • Supervising the development and approval of the agency’s legislative package;
    • Reviewing and analyzing legislation to determine impact on the department; and
    • Managing the department’s legislative monitoring and tracking function;

    • Serving as the agency’s Regulatory Coordinator; and

    Coordinates the department’s support of the State Board of Social Services and the Child Day-Care Council.

    (Public Company; 51-200 employees; Broadcast Media industry)

    September 1971 — November 1974 (3 years 3 months)
