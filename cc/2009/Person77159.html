Patrick Matte’s Summary

As the Interactive Director, Patrick leads the design of user experiences that bring BLITZ’s interactive campaigns to life. Patrick also heads the development and integration of the BLITZ Fabric™, a proprietary framework for Adobe Flash™ built specifically for interactive brand experiences that require an intensive application approach, infrastructure and rich features.

Patrick brings a vast range of experience to BLITZ with professional aptitude using Flash architectures, Papervision 3D, programming and has led the development of video delivery and integration of sound, animation and motion graphics. While maintaining a high standard for coding practices at BLITZ, Patrick also actively organizes internal education sessions and seminars.

Previously, Patrick worked as a flash developer at Sidlee (formerly Diesel Marketing) and Nurun. His portfolio boasts award-winning interactive work for Adobe, MGM Grand and The Royal Canadian Mint. Patrick has also spoken on expert panels at various industry events including FITC, Adobe MAX, and the LA Flash User Group.

Patrick studied 3D Animation with the UQAM Design Faculty in Montreal and both Graphic Design and Architecture at Cegep du Vieux Montreal.

Patrick Matte’s Specialties:

Falsh, Actionscript 3.0, Papervision3D, components and framework development, motion graphics 2D and 3D.
