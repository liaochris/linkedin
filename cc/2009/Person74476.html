Nikki Reagan’s Summary

Hi! I’m Nikki Reagan, specializing in social media, public relations and website design in Bullhead City, AZ! I also design and print marketing collateral such as business cards, postcards, brochures, flyers, banners, etc. If you need something to promote your business, I can provide it! And, if it’s something that I cannot provide, I can tell you who can.

“Promote, promote, promote” is my mantra and I work with several businesses in the area on getting the “most bang for their buck” as far as marketing and advertising.

The rules of marketing are changing quickly and most business owners do not realize the true power of the internet. My husband and I also run The Computer Doctor, and as a small business owner, I know first hand the cost of marketing and the frustration of not seeing results from ads running in various publications.

What I do goes beyond traditional printed advertising. I can help your business grow and reach more people by combining a variety of marketing techniques to compliment your existing advertising and expand your market reach.
