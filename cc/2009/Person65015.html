Matthew Harrison’s Summary

B2B International is a global business-to-business market research and market intelligence firm. We specialize in answering the most difficult questions facing your business through a combination of market research and consultancy expertise.

Our aim is to provide strategic direction and tactical recommendations to our clients, whatever and wherever their business. Whether you are considering entering a new or emerging market, wish to segment your b-to-b audience, are seeking to reinvigorate your brand, or are evaluating acquisition targets, B2B International can provide you with a tailor-made solution.

Our b-to-b experience covers every industry sector and is truly global in nature. Our European headquarters in Manchester, England, our North American headquarters in New York, USA, our Asian consultancy in Beijing, China, and our affiliate office in Moscow, Russia are staffed with high-level consultants as well as on-the-ground data collection capabilities. Our expertise covers the key Western economies as well as all of Asia-Pacific, Eastern Europe, Middle-East and Africa.
