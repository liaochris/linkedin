Michael Coffey’s Summary

Mike has extensive professional experience creating new business ventures, developing strategies and execution models to capitalize on business ‘turn-arounds’, and developing capital sources. During the technology wave of the 90s, Mike served as a member of the Board of Directors, and Vice President of a professional services firm in the technology arena. In 2001, Mike leveraged his sales skills and business acumen to develop new business ventures within the real estate industry. Mike has been instrumental in launching two private equity ventures which own and operate Real Estate Brokerage firms in six US states.

Michael Coffey’s Specialties:

My personal strengths are, strategy development, operational excellence, sales and sales management, capital acquisition, recruitment, coaching and development.
