Abby Berendt Lavoi’s Summary

I produce for TV, Internet, and Publishing industries. My work spans the realms of broadcast commercials and promos, short form documentaries, online videos, websites, marketing collateral and online promotional campaigns.

I work independently and with teams of people to create and write successful marketing and branding campaigns. Creating innovative promotions and working quickly and efficiently to keep on deadline is my forte. I’m known for thinking outside the box and maintaining the highest level of quality work. I always give great attention to detail and have written copy that has exceeded clients’ expectations.

I have many years of coordinating and producing experience and have managed numerous projects from start to finish. My background includes writing, producing, and editing television promos and short documentaries, website management and development, including video and content updates, site fixes, traffic reports, and revisions. I have extensive knowledge with social media platforms and have run various third party ad campaigns (BlogAds, Google AdWords, and Facebook Ads, etc). I’ve designed and directed the production of online promotions, including eblasts, newsletters, banner ads, microsites, blogs, and social networks. I have experience creating and maintaining online relationships, including identity development and online self-promotion.
