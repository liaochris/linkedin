Ryan Yee’s Summary

A career professional in the field of training, performance support, and instructional design with over 20 years experience in the field of training and performance support. Taught instructional design at the graduate level and worked in a variety of industries, including government, high-tech, retail, medical, and utilities.

Over 20 years of direct experience as a training consultant, instructional designer, performance support technologist, and stand-up trainer.

Received a Masters Degree in Education, Instructional Technologies, and has taught instructional design at the graduate level as a lecturer.

Currently responsible for directing the training and maintaining the operational performance of over 1,000 screeners at San Francisco International Airport.

Specializes in needs assessment, instructional design, and post-training evaluation using a variety of educational research methods, including both qualitative and quantitative data analyses.

Recognized for outstanding instructional design and learning presentation in the printed medium, including scripted leader’s guides, creative job aids, and effective user manuals.

Expert professional blogger with expertise in helping others to create and develop an online presence on the Internet integrating text with video, audio (podcasts), and pictures.

Ryan Yee’s Specialties:

Recognized expert in print-based instructional design and training, leverages existing Internet technologies (blogging, streaming video, podcasts, search engines) to find innovative ways to support operational performance and corporate communication.
