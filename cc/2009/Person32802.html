Frank Capria’s Summary

Over a decade of experience in television post production as an editor, designer, and manager, gave me a deep understanding of what it takes to compete in the fast-changing media environment. Since 2000 my primary focus has been interactive technologies and application development - launching successful Kingpin Interactive and Xprove. During a 2007 sabbatical I briefly returned to editing as a producer/editor for ABC's NASCAR in Primetime.

Currently I am a Senior Product Designer for editors at Avid Technology, the industry leader in professional post production tools.

Frank Capria’s Specialties:

The breadth of my experience - from Emmy Award winning designer to technology entrepreneur has given me a deep understanding of the challenges facing content owners and creators in the 21st century.
