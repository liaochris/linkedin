Eric Torkia, MASc’s Summary

OBJECTIVE
To provide support and expertise to world class organizations implementing collaborative best practices and processes.

PROFILE
Since 1999, Eric Torkia MASc has worked with organizations spanning multiple industries and sectors, most notably – Professional Services, Manufacturing/Distribution and Public Sector organizations. He has practical
experience as a consultant and as a project manager with organizations implementing technological and strategic change, Enterprise Relationship Management (ERM) best practices as well as collaborative processes.

He has equally assisted service organizations in launching new products, marketing professional services, developing new value propositions or business models.

Mr. Torkia’s academic background includes a Master’s degree in information systems management from the University of Québec in Montreal as well as a BBA in international marketing and management from Northwood University Florida.
