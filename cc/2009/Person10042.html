Becky Wright’s Summary

Female Songwriter of the Year '08 (Agape Fest), 2009 Top 5 Finalist, Female Songwriter & Female Vocalist of the Year. 2008 Radio single, "Call Me Loved" released to 2,000 radio stations, from upcoming (2009) "Heart of Adoption" album.
Professional Singer/Songwriter and Recording Artist (since 1991, signed with Lamon Records in 2006, www.lamonrecords.com); album released June 2006. Previous single (song, "Billy's Train") is playing worldwide, on over 600 stations in USA, Australia, Cananda, and beyond. Private Music Teacher and contract educator in private & public schools. BSEd in Early Childhood Education from Northeastern State University in Oklahoma, taught elementary music for several years. Former editor of "The Reader's Bible", published in Oklahoma and distributed worldwide. Teach Songwriting Seminars to public and private groups, give Christian concerts and speak to church and civic groups, at conferences, revivals, Christian schools, women's groups, youth groups. Many songs published in Nashville, Atlanta, and now my own company. Other artists have cut (recorded) my songs, as well, and am currently working on Christian and country artists' projects. Member of ASCAP (Writer & Publisher), GMA (Gospel Music Association), CCMA (Christian Country Music Association), and NSAI (Nashville Songwriter. Featured Songwriter on www.christiansongwriters.org. Currently booking my Summer & Fall 2008 Concert/Speaking Itinerary. Frequent guest judge for Oklahoma pageants.

Becky Wright’s Specialties:

Songwriting, graphic design, piano/keyboards, recording, demos, voice/performance/piano lessons, public speaking, PR, writing for press publications, photography, commercial jingle-writing. Motivational Speaker for colleges/universities, judge talent competitions/pageants. Highly skilled in editing, grammer, radio promo/commercials, advertising. Member of the Oklahoma Arts Council, regular contributor to several regional newspapers.
