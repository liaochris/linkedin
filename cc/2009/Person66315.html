Megan Fitzgerald’s Summary

I'm an expat career and personal branding coach and founder of Career By Choice. I help expats and international development pros worldwide use their personal brand to build a career or business that fits who they are, what they stand for and their international lifestyle.

Having built a career & business that supports my choice of living and working abroad, I'm passionate about using my extensive global experience and training and a creative, 2.0 approach to help my clients do the same. Committed to the pursuit of authenticity and excellence, I work with my clients to clarify their goals and personal brand, create compelling brand communication tools on/offline, build a distinctive online identity with social media, grow a strong network and apply strategies that increase their success and impact in the world.

With 15+ years experience in professional and business development on 5 continents my credentials include:
• Certified Personal Branding Strategist - 1 of the first 100 worldwide
• Certified Career Coach and Executive Coach
• Certified Online Identity Strategist – 1 of the first 35 worldwide
• Masters Degree in Multimedia Communications

I work best with curious, passionate, forward-thinking professionals and entrepreneurs that crave more clarity around what makes them unique and stand out. They see their career or business as a vehicle for making a difference in people's lives. Committed to their long-term success, they're eager and open to applying new strategies & 2.0 technologies required to thrive in today's global marketplace.

Named one of the "top 50 personal branding consultants worth working with", I've shared my expertise with global audiences, been featured in Fortune Small Business Magazine, on CNN.com and in expat publications such as Career in Your Suitcase and Italy Magazine.

When not working I enjoy travel, film and a caffè macchiato to start my day.
