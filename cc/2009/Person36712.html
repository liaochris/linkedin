Glenn Garnes’s Summary

Relationship Marketing Center:

I am the founder of the Relationship Marketing Center where we teach business professionals how to save themselves time energy and money by making relationships the centerpiece of their marketing strategy. We provide technology tools and training on how take business networking more seriously, and how to generate more business through referrals and relationships with other business professionals.

Author:

I am the author of "Let's NOT Be Partners, Things You Must Do Before you Tie the Knot". www.LetsNotBePartners.com This book teaches you exactly what you need to know before you go into business with anyone.

I also lecture on the subject of partners selection and structuring working relationships with partners,and I teach partnership coaching to other business professionals as well as serving as a partner coach myself.

Professional Speaker:

I speak on a variety of business topics including business networking, relationship marketing, collaborative marketing, partnering, strategic alliances, and joint venturing, social media marketing, and systematic relationship building.

Relationship Marketing Consulting:

I work with a very small number of marketing clients as a relationship marketing consultant. I help them development and implement marketing plans centered on relationship marketing, collaborative marketing, business networking and the adoption of automated systems that facilitate high touch connections with customers and referral sources.
