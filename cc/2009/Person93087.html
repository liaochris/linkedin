John Clark’s Summary

During my 18+ years in the IT industry, I have worked for companies ranging in size from just a few staff to international corporations with thousands of employees. As a result, I have built up considerable knowledge and experience to both articulate and deliver IT solutions tailored to any company size and their particular needs. I am also a rare find in the IT industry, a techie that can actually communicate successfully with "normal" people. I'm also a strong believer that IT is a business tool, and do not subscribe to "technology for technology's sake".

To date, my career has encompassed IT management, Project Management, IT support services, Collaboration Solutions, Windows-based infrastructure, desktop & laptop deployment, website development, project lifecycle consultancy and software engineering. I have experience across several industries, including Retail, Pharmaceuticals, IT Services, Telecomms, Government and Defence.
