Kelly Lenihan’s Summary

Do you need a website that reflects your vision but need help focusing your intentions?

Is your website languishing from lack of attention and could use a little sprucing up?

Are you ready to work smarter not harder and have your website bring your customers to you?

My goal is to help small business owners translate their business vision into eloquent, elegant, easy-to-navigate websites. When you collaborate with me, I take the time to understand your business operations and needs so that together we can develop a successful online presence that's graphically pleasing, serves a purpose, and accomplishes your goals. I even provide ongoing website maintenance to continue giving your website the extra special attention it deserves.

I do all of this because you have more important things to do - like run your business.
