Jack Hallahan’s Summary

Jack brings to MobiTV twenty years marketing insight, offering senior level brand development expertise. Mixing traditional and new media experience in website development, advertising and integrated marketing, Jack has helped build brands including TiVo, Apple, Mattel, CNNSI, and Chevron.
As Vice President, Advertising and Brand Partnerships for MobiTV, he is focused on creating a diverse client portfolio in Retail, Consumer Goods, Automotive, and Entertainment.
Prior experience as Managing Director Wirestone and Vice President at AKQA Advertising includes multi-channel marketing for Visa USA, LucasArts, Macy’s, Nike, and Williams-Sonoma.
Jack developed his advertising sales career in New York helping launch Men’s Health Magazine after managing Fortune 500 clients for Sports Illustrated. Advertising, media and marketing strategy form the foundation of his agency experience at Chiat/Day and Young & Rubicam, where he began his career on Madison Avenue after graduating from Villanova University. Jack lives in Orinda, California, with his wife Lisa and their three children.
