Todd Bottorff’s Summary

Turner (turnerpublishing.com) is an independent publisher of fine books sold through Borders, Waldenbooks, Barnes & Noble, Amazon and in great independent booksellers across the country. Turner has the following imprints: Turner, our flagship imprint that focuses on topics of local interest, including our Historic Photos series, Iroquois Press is our fiction imprint, Trade Paper Press that publishes non-fiction paperback books, and Ramsey & Todd, our children's book imprint.
