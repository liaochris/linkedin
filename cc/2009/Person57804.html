Laura Pasquini’s Summary

Sharing, collaborating and engaging is the best way to learn. My passion lies in student development, emerging technologies, program planning and utilizing resources to enhance training and learning.

I specialize in academic research and development focused on the holistic principle of the learner in the following areas:

-academic advising
-study skills & learning strategies
-academic support: tutoring & supplemental instruction
-career exploration & research
-employment search & application
-professional & graduate school applications

At present I am interested in emerging technologies that best support blended learning pedagogy for education and training. I have experience with research, design and implementation of various online tools including:

-podcasting & vodcasting development
-online social networking, photo sharing & bookmarking
-personal web creation, i.e. blogs & effective web writing
-wiki creation for education & project planning
-website usability: content audit, usability & visual design

I have created & facilitated workshops, seminars and training modules on the above uses of technology and how to effectively implement these resources for students, staff, instructors & external partners.

I am available for consulting, speaking engagements, workshop development & commissioned projects. Be sure to connect with me here: http://techknowtools.wordpress.com/
