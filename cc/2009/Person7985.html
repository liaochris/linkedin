Jessica Watson’s Summary

My name is Jessica Watson and I am dedicated to bridging the gap between business and creativity. The website concept, www.artisticescape.com, is symbolic of breaking away from the norm and establishing oneself through superior, high quality graphic design. My services include several unique promotional materials, such as logos, identity packages, brochures, media kits, web design and much more.

You work hard at what you do, let me handle the designing.

Jessica Watson’s Specialties:
