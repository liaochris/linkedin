Samuele Camatari’s Summary

The name Jusan originates from Japan and signifies the number 13. A number that for centuries has always brought fortune. Like success luck is not something that we are born with it’s not found in our DNA. However we at Jusan believe that unlike luck a formula for success can be created. The roots of our success are based on experience, quality and trust; these core principles we never forget when working with our clients.

A small core team decided to embark on this challenge, driven with the passion and determination to succeed in all of our projects. In a short time we have transformed into a true Network, continuing to experience strong growth that every day brings together innovative creation and professionalism.

A team that focuses on achieving its goals, working hard to ensure each milestone is surpassed, assuring that we will arrive to you with the complete product in hand.
