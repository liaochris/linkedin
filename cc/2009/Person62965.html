About NewPage Corporation
Headquartered in Miamisburg, Ohio, NewPage Corporation is the largest coated paper manufacturer in North America, based on production capacity, with $4.4 billion in net sales for the year ended December 31, 2008.

The NewPage Corporation has already set a course for leadership in the printing paper industry. In 2009, NewPage was named "Top 10 Places to Work" for the deployment of Lean Six Sigma and honored as the “Most Successful Lean Six Sigma Start-Up Program” by iSixSigma Magazine. In 2008, Fortune magazine named us one of "America's Most Admired" in the paper manufacturing industry.

NewPage owns paper mills in Kentucky, Maine, Maryland, Michigan, Minnesota, Wisconsin and Nova Scotia, Canada. NewPage employs approximately 8,000 skilled professionals in various locations across the United States and Canada. Savvy, enthusiastic and creative, our employees are dedicated to making NewPage the best printing paper business in North America.

NewPage produces paper that is used for everything from high-end corporate collateral and direct mail to newspapers, magazines and catalogs. Our customers include some of the most recognized names in their industries.
