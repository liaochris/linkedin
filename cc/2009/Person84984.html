Roberto Venturini’s Summary

Marketing and Communication Expert.

Roberto Venturini worked for more than 15 years in the advertising business, as Account Director in charge of international projects and clients such as a Apple Computer, Microsoft, Pepsi Cola, Gillette, in Saatchi & Saatchi, BBDO and McCann.

Was among the pioneers of Internet Marketing in Italy, starting in 1994 as COO of Cyberio (BBDO Italy interactive) and later in McCann Interactive. In 2000 set up its own consulting business focused on internet marketing and business support, in Italy and later in Spain (www.venturini.biz).

Since January 2008 is Digital Strategic Planner in ARC Leo Burnett Milano.

Roberto has been teaching Marketing, Advertising and eMarketing for institutions such as Sole 24 ore (leading Italian financial newspaper), Università Cattolica, Telecom Italia and others since 1998, and has worked as Visiting Professor, teaching Marketing and Internet Marketing at the European University for Management Studies in Barcelona (2003-2007), IED Milano

As a Journalist, has published more than 300 articles on Advertising, e-marketing and Internet Business on Web Marketing Tools, Media Key, ADV, Apogeo on line, Panorama and Espresso.

Roberto co-authored the book “Internet Content Management”.

If you understand Italian, you might want to have a look at my Marketing, Branding, Design and Internet Marketing blog: http://robertoventurini.blogspot.com/
