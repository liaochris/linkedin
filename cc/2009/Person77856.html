Paul Rouke’s Summary

Commercially experienced user experience designer and information architect following almost 6 years as the senior web and user experience designer at the UK's largest home shopping business, Littlewoods Shop Direct. 2 years team management experience, alongside project management duties. 5 years experience designing and improving conversion and drop-out rates for key shopping pages for websites which generate 6 and 7 figure yearly demand. Involvenment in a variety of user testing programmes to truly understand how a user navigates shopping sites and areas which cause the most frustration or confusion. 2 years experience working part-time, and now full-time, for PRWD, a full service professional design business. Primary goal is to continually grow the design and consultancy agency, both in staff and number and size of UK clients, both through strong referrals and online and offline marketing, whilst continually keeping pace with the ever evolving online channel.

Paul Rouke’s Specialties:

usability, interaction design, user experience, information architecture, increasing e-commerce conversion rates, project management, business critical e-commerce page design, checkout process analysis and design, e-commerce site critiques, email marketing, affiliate marketing (static and rich media)
