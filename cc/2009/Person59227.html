Linda M. Orlando’s Summary

Linda M. Orlando has enjoyed a progressive, 20+ year career in the information technology field while engaged in diverse industries. She launched her career as a Programmer, and advanced to higher levels of responsibility with roles including Project Manager, PC Technician, Technology Services Delivery Manager, and her current role as Chief Information Technology Officer / Director of Global Technology. She has consistently demonstrated technology prowess in hands-on technology roles and as a Senior IT Executive with global responsibilities. Her career reflects progressive technology and senior executive roles in financial services, investment banking, broker/dealer, e-commerce, and publishing industries.
