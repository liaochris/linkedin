Jason Butchko’s Summary

Results-driven leader with extensive operations, project management, and program management experience. Multi-industry, international track record including scientific instruments, consumer products, metal/plastic injection molding, and government contracts. Direct experience launching multiple new products and implementing Lean/Six Sigma Manufacturing practices at manufacturing sites in the US and EU. I drive a culture of continuous improvement to attain operational and financial excellence.

Recently led M&A efforts for a small group of private equity investors at AMS/Westco. Completed two acquisitions and integrations in 14 months -- more than doubling the revenue base of the company holdings and diversified the portfolio into strategic growth markets. Exceeded ROIC and cash flow targets within 6 months.

Six years B2B environmental/industrial instrumentation – launched multiple new products (spectroscopy, colorimetry, discrete analysis, electrochemistry, FIA, TOC); led Lachat Instruments (Loveland, CO) and Radiometer Analytical (Lyon, FR) to a 3x market growth rate.

Participated, led, and/or championed 45+ domestic and international kaizen events in both office and production environments. Championed DFSS and DMAIC process to reduce/eliminate scrap and production waste.
