Bob Dolan’s Summary

If you are looking for a buyer for your business, or looking for an acquisition as incremental sales growth, or a stand-alone business, you have found someone who is passionate about what he does, gets results as well as a straight talker and to the point. There are several reasons Bob can help.

- Bob only works with five to eight mid-market/lower mid-market clients at a time which allows him to be responsive, focused, and connected with clients at all times.
- Bob does not use multi-listings with other brokers but rather a closed network of professional referring sources of over 400 consultants for a pool of potential buyers, looking for businesses with EBITDA/SDE of $250,000 and/or gross sales of more than $1,000,000.
- By not using a the multi-listing network, it allows Bob to keep control of confidential information which is a foremost concern.
- Bob is a specialist in the printing/graphics trade, manufacturing, medical practices, medical billing, as well as business services.
- From Bob’s extensive graphics and marketing background and available tools, he is able to package a business and present it for sale to command the highest reasonable value.
- Bob’s core management background has been in operations, sales, and marketing with a particular skill of listening very carefully and being able to find the middle ground of both sides of the transaction to consummate the sale.
- Size matters. As a Boutique Brokerage/Intermediary business, Bob promises that you will get the owner regardless of whether the deal is large or small. You will get the utmost attention. Clients notice our “entrepreneurial spirit”.

Bob Dolan’s Specialties:

The printing trades, manufacturing, medical practices, medical billing services, medical products, medical IT, business brokerage, M&A as well as being a intermediary and negotiator.
