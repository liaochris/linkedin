Robert Caldwell’s Summary

Located in Midlothian, Va., artist Robert L Caldwell is well known for his highly detailed, realistic drawings and paintings. With camera and sketchbook always in tow, Robert draws inspiration from familiar items. He searches for elements that are often overlooked but that provide opportunities for compelling compositions, particularly where people and nature intersect. His work has been shown nationally and can be seen at Berkley Gallery in Warrenton, Va., Lovetts Gallery in Tulsa, Okla., and The Judy Newcomb Gallery in Richmond, Va. For more information about Robert and his work, call 804.639.7604 or visit www.rlcaldwell.com.

Robert Caldwell’s Specialties:

Traditional realistic oil paintings and graphite drawings of wildlife in theirs and ours habitats.
