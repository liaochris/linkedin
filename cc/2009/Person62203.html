Marcio Chalegre Coimbra’s Summary

Career in government relations focused in the development, defense and implementation of political agenda and corporate image regarding communications strategies and public affairs in different countries.

Professional experience in political parties and political campaigns, also in federal government and private institutions of advocacy, lobbying, public and corporate affairs, political intelligence and government relations in leading positions.

Experience in NGO´s (foundations and institutes) and public policy training institutions in fundraising, strategic planning, and nonprofit management. Coordination of Social Entrepreneurship initiatives with innovative solutions and concrete results.

Strong analytical capacity and understanding of the international political scenario and nationals mechanisms of policymaking. Able to monitor, analyze information and anticipate political facts.

Familiarity with Internet tools and professionally as chief-editor of a website of political analysis since 2003.

Academic career as lecturer during four years, with a book published, having IOB-Thomson (USA) as editor.

Bachelor degree in law, approved by the Brazilian Bar Association as an attorney since 2000. MBA in Economic Law from FGV, post-graduate studies in International Law at UFRGS, Master in Political Action at the Universidad Rey Juan Carlos in Spain, with full scholarship and improvement course at Harvard Law School.

International professional experience in Spain, Austria and USA for a year and a half. English and Spanish fluent. Basic knowledge of German and French.

Leadership, sense of initiative and entrepreneurial outlook.
