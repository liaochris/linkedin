Amy Applebaum’s Summary

THE COMPANY

Bootcamp for Your Mind® is a company committed to helping people, all over the world, achieve an amazing life.

It is a place to make your goals a reality. If you say you want more money, you’re going to create it. If you say you want to improve a relationship, you’ll start improving it immediately.

Bootcamp for Your Mind is not about talking, it’s about doing. No excuses, no blaming - just achieving!

Currently, Bootcamp for Your Mind offers Programs for individuals as well as Groups. Additional products, including books and Tele-Bootcamps will be released in 2009. I am currently in development of a reality show based on my process.

My immediate goals are to launch Totally Fabulous Females Community and spread the word about Group Bootcamp!

Totally Fabulous Females Community is about becoming the Woman you were born to be. You don’t have to be there yet but here is where you will get motivated and inspired to step outside of your comfort zone and take your life to the next level. It’s about sharing and growing. Ultimately, this group is about fun, creativity and action!!

Our first event will be in Los Angeles
on Saturday May 9, 2009. This is a must
attend so Save the Date! Email me at amy@bootcampforyourmind.com for more information.

Group Bootcamp is a 12 Week Coaching Program that will change your life! For more information visit www.bootcampforyourmind.com.
