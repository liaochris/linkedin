Tatsuya Nakagawa’s Summary

Tatsuya Nakagawa is President and CEO of Atomica Creative Group, a specialized strategic product marketing firm based in Vancouver Canada. He studied at California’s University of San Francisco and is a graduate of Canada’s University of British Columbia with a degree in human kinetics. He has assisted Bridgestone, Hitachi, Sato Pharmaceutical and other global companies in their early stage deployments and has been involved in many successful product launches in North America, Europe and Asia in several industries.

Atomica Creative Group is a specialized strategic product marketing firm. Through leading edge insight and research, sound strategic planning and effective project management, Atomica helps companies achieve greater success in bringing new products to market and in improving their existing businesses. Our clients engage our experts on an advisory, consultative or collaborative basis to support their product marketing initiatives.

Please contact me if you have any questions or if I can be of assistance to you in any way.
