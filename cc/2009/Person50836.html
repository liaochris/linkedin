Joseph Maggiore’s Summary

Born in MA and raised in South FL, I made AZ my home in 1995 when I moved to be closer to family and to attend ASU. After successfully founding, managing and selling my own automotive performance business, as well as being an integral part of a team that ambitiously achieved more than 500% sales growth in just under 14 months as a .com Operations Director, I set my sights on the Real Estate market in early 2006. A career in Real Estate and Development has always been in my blood- it has been my family’s business for 3 generations!

I completed my Real Estate schooling at the Arizona School of Real Estate and Business in Scottsdale in early 2006. I am a licensed REALTOR, and have extensive personal and professional experience with residential and commercial transactions, property management, investment properties as well as corporate and personal relocations. I pride myself on applying my own blend of humor, tenacity, enthusiasm, smart thinking, and dedication when working for my precious clients, and always look forward to finding creative ways to work around “challenging” new situations. I have worked all over the Valley, developing an exceptional reputation with clients, vendors, and fellow agents.

I am a proud member of The Scottsdale Area Association of REALTORS (SAAR), The Arizona Association of REALTORS (AAR) and The National Association of REALTORS (NAR). Also, I have completed additional education in Residential Property Management and am a Realty Executives Relocation Specialist.

Married in 2003 to my high school sweetheart, I currently reside in Northeast Phoenix with my wife and our son, born in early 2007. I am the Board President of my community’s Homeowner’s Association, an avid racquetball player, and will forever be an auto enthusiast.
