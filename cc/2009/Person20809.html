Craig Hodgkins’s Summary

I offer a variety of interactive, creative and presentational workshops, and serve as a show writer, content developer, event host and conference speaker for companies and organizations throughout the United States. I have several voice-over credits to my name, and have been known to perform an occasional live song parody or two.

After my sixteen year career as a writer, speaker and event host for The Walt Disney Company, I worked in advertising and for Mariners Church in Irvine, CA. Currently, in addition to my writing, speaking and consulting work, I serve as Pastor of Content Development for Saddleback Church, a 20,000+ member congregation in Lake Forest, CA. led by Rick Warren.

Craig Hodgkins’s Specialties:

Show writing and programming, script writing, live event and workshop hosting, conference speaking and video production. And don't forget weddings and funerals.
