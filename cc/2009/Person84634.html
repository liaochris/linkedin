Robert Hugh Campbell’s Summary

As the owner of Smojoe, I craft interesting content to weave inexpensive social nets around client goods and services. My firm provides cost effective 'white hat' marketing solutions for small to mid size businesses looking to increase their website's social relevance and thereby boost their online traffic and conversion; my practice builds content rich backlinks for greater keyword penetration and increased Google page rank.

After three years of steady work at LifeCapture Interactive, I've developed some signature social media marketing techniques and pioneered new platforms for branded content - I've been hard at work promoting almost all of LifeCapture's client websites and their growth metrics evidence my success.

In the spring of 2008, Rob Campbell founded Smojoe to do three types of social media: article marketing, blogvertising and discussion forum marketing.

Between November 07and June 08, Rob Campbell created highly effective discussion forum marketing practices to increase the Google page rank of eight different Alliance Atlantis movie trailer micro pages as Lead Content Producer.

Robert Hugh Campbell’s Specialties:

Social media maven, web 2.0 user rewarded community designer, multi-platform brand storyteller, discussion forum marketing wizard, and page rank builder.
