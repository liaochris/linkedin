After 10 successful years recruiting top talent as a Corporate Recruiter, the entrepreneurial bug bit me and I started my own company, Sandler Careers; we assist business services organizations nationwide in locating their next TOP performer !! As an Associate Member of NAPEO, our focus is primarily in the PEO, HRO, ASO, HCM and Payroll Services industries. Sandler Careers' recruiters are AIRS CIR certified (Certified Internet Recruiter) and have the unique talent beyond most to locate those hard-to find candidates who may, or may not, be in search of their next opportunity. Our passion for both the tactical and strategic areas of recruiting have made us successful .. and OUR goal is to make your next long term career placement, whether we represent you as a client or as a candidate.
