Kirby Winfield’s Summary

Since 1996, my experience has been in pre/during/post-IPO marketing communications planning, corporate and product branding, online advertising monetization, post-acquisition organizational restructuring, strategic business planning and financial forecasting, product definition and development, and online design development for industry-leading organizations.

My goals are to help early- and mid-stage ventures succeed online, and to create products that fill unmet needs for businesses or consumers.
