Stephanie L. Gross’s Summary

Work: Substantial proficiency in cutting-edge internet and Web technology. Self-starter and self-motivated. Substantial experience in public service librarianship and database searching in academic libraries. Board-certified school media specialist. Currently position: First librarian at Yeshiva University directly responsible for electronic reserves. Create and maintain collection of over 8,000 reserve documents and 500 course pages for most schools of Yeshiva University, including graduate and Hebrew studies, using SirsiDynix's Docutek system.

Professional service: Advisement/mentoring both library students and new librarians.

Always interested in issues of professional development and sharing of knowledge.
