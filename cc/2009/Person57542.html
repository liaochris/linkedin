Larry Marshall’s Summary

MTSI (Marshall Technology Solutions, Inc.) specializes in ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) and CRM (Customer Relationship Management) software applications for small to medium sized businesses around the world. We currently manage products such as Sage MAS 90, Sage MAS 200, Sage MAS 500, Sage BusinessWorks, ACT by Sage CRM, and Sage SalesLogix CRM. We also manage SYSPRO ERP and CRM applications. MTSI has offices around the United States and have been in this industry since 1990.
