Janet Slack’s Summary

I have been a life coach for six years and found it to be one of the most powerful, meaningful ways to make change that there is. Life Adventure Coaching is the company that I founded to help people make lasting change in their lives. When we look forward to every day and whatever adventure that it may bring, we can be more happy, accomplish more and feel powerful.

Because I feel so strongly about the value of coaching and because many coaches struggle with how to start a coaching business, I specialize in helping coaches and other solopreneurs to be more knowledgeable about the business world. I have an e-book, articles, blog and weekly newsletter devoted to the steps it takes for a coach to be an entrepreneur.

If you are interested in learning more about developing a business as a coach, therapist, consultant or other type of solo practitioner, you may be interested in subscribing to my free weekly newsletter, Biz Tips for Coaches at http://www.biztipsforcoaches.com.

Articles on business development, being an entrepreneur, coaching and the coaching business can be found on my blog at http://www.biztipsforcoaches.com/blog/.

Janet Slack’s Specialties:

Professional coaching for those wanting a balanced and challenging life filled with adventure. Business development for coaches and solopreneurs. Therapist with a limited private practice and specialties in couples counseling, trauma and women's issues.
