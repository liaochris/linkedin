Leslee Masoero’s Summary

This year I started an ecommerce business. From my years of experience in the work place and community efforts, I felt I had what it took to start an online business in an area I love..outdoor decor. My goal is to grow this business so that I am able to supplement my current bookkeeping earnings. My earnings are critical to paying for the college costs of my three children.

I am a bookkeeper for several clients. I am a Certified QuickBooks Pro Advisor and also have experience with Quicken, Microsoft Excel & Online Banking. This is currently my primary means of income. My accounting experience has been invaluable in the business development of my internet business.

I also have diverse leadership experience from many years of community service. Some of these responsibilities including PTA President, Romania Mission Fundraising Chairperson, Marching Band State Competition Committee Chairperson, and other various responsibilites in my church and school communities.
