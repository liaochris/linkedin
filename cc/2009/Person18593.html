  • External Relations

    WSC-SD

    (Environmental Services industry)

    January 2009 — Present (7 months)

    The World Student Community for Sustainable Development is a multi-disciplinary network of motivated students with the ability to think and act both locally and globally, and who share the vision to make a difference. Our purpose is to be a leading international student organization that carries out meaningful projects which result in positive and enduring changes that improve lives and communities around the world.

    In the position of External Relations Officer I am responsible for developing & maintaining a corporate identity for the WSC-SD, keeping contact with external organizations in the field of sustainable development and student involvement, carrying out common projects, and helping out to promote the WSC-SD in relevant networks.

  • Chair

    Morgen

    (Environmental Services industry)

    September 2008 — Present (11 months)

    Morgen (Tomorrow) is the Dutch students network for sustainable development.

    Morgen works with & for students in higher education and aims to inspire students to integrate values of sustainable development and environment, ethical lifestyle in their personal life and in the choices they make regarding their study & professional career.

    Morgen does this by organizing events, workshops, and conferences, by carrying out comparative research on university policies, by supporting local environmental platforms and by making & employing partnerships with other organisations in the field.

    Morgen is a nationally-based organisation with a global scope of topics.
