Gerard Blokdijk, Service Management, ITIL’s Summary

“YOU’RE MISSING OUT IF YOU HAVEN’T SEEN GERARD PRESENT. His presentations are packed with first hand experience and awesome systems to use right away. You’ll get more value than you thought possible.”


Gerard Blokdijk is one of the industry's most innovative ITSM and business visionaries. As CEO and owner of The Art of Service, he was instrumental in building a company that consisted of a suitcase and a laptop to a list of A-Clients, a team of A-players, and is a Coveted Employer to Work For, growing the company from 0 to multi million in 4 years, with no debt or outside shareholders. He continues to excel at what he knows best and loves most: helping people build exceptional companies.

For over 20 years Gerard has been coaching, speaking to, or helping Managers build companies. He has authored over 35 books on Management strategies. He is one of his country's most innovative business leaders and is a leading force behind one of the fastest growing businesses of the decade, The Art of Service. He is widely regarded as an expert in growing IT Service Management organizations, their teams and their cultures.

The Art of Service provides service management consulting and ITIL accredited education services with service and systems management professionals in Asia Pacific. The company has served over 800 clients and 20,000 students in 33 countries. A recognized leader in the international service management community, The Art of Service’s management team has been involved with practical application of service management concepts and practices since 1989 and has been instrumental in the development of ITIL since its inception.
