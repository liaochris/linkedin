Karl Ortenburg’s Summary

Karl Ortenburg manages Advanced Marketing on the Internet (AMI) Ltd, an online business development and consultancy firm, specializing in social media - working with Internet marketing experts from North America, Europe and Asia. AMI focuses on cost-effective Internet strategies that small to medium businesses can apply to grow their web businesses.


Karl’s mission is to support people to succeed - in building, managing and growing their Internet business.


Karl originally from Coburg, Southern Germany, left Bertelsmann AG to come to attend a Sloan M.Sc at London Business School. He founded AMI in 1996 and worked with companies in the US, Canada, the UK, Germany, Switzerland, Spain, India, and Brazil (e.g. Bertelsmann, Deutsche Bank, Random House).


Karl enjoys music, sports and travel. He is married and has two sons. His personal motto is: "How can I add value today?"
