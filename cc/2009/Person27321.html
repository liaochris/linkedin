Dominic Vaccaro’s Summary

My interest in computers pre-dates today's modern Internet and began back in the early 80's with my love for the old Atari and Coleco video game systems. I wanted to learn more and my thirst for knowledge brought me to the purchase of my first Atari 800 computer system. My passion for computers grew when I added a modem and eventually ran one of the most popular Atari 8-bit BBS systems in the US, known as Titan. I eventually wrote my own BBS software and later traded up my Atari computer system for a PC platform with the advent of DOS and Windows.
