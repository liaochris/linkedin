Sachin Agarwal’s Summary

Sachin Agarwal is a senior research scientist at Deutsche Telekom AG, Laboratories, a joint collaboration between Deutsche Telekom, A.G. and the Technical University of Berlin. He received his Ph.D. and M.S. degrees from Boston University's Networking and Information Systems Laboratory in the department of Electrical and Computer Engineering in 2005 and 2002 respectively. He has interned at Microsoft Research in Redmond in the Communications and Collaboration Systems group and worked for a technology start-up in the Boston area.
