Karen Schaffner’s Summary

I have more than 30 years publishing experience, nearly all involving the retail market. In the course of my career, I have received numerous honors, including the most prestigious award presented in business - to - business publishing, American Business Media’s Crain Award for career excellence.
My current business, Corporate Image Consulting, leverages my knowledge of the market, the publications and my client focus to provide public relations, marketing and custom publishing solutions in the retail design, hospitality design and contract design markets. Clients include the Association for Retail Environments, Chute Gerdeman Retail and idX Corporation.

Publishing, sales and editorial management, event management, public relations, marketing.
