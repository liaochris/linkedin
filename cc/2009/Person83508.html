Aziz Grieser’s Summary

As a senior marketer and web-savvy entrepreneur, professionally trained while attending the Two-Year MBA Program at Babson College, the top-rated MBA program for entrepreneurship, I live for the market's validation.

What can be learned from your available past sales and product or service data, and how can it be used to create new value for all stakeholders?

This is a marketer's "scientific method", and it guided my own data-driven analysis that I tailored over the past six years to add value to my clients, sometimes occurring at critical moments during the earliest development of a company.

Along the way, I developed a deep understanding of several industries that my clients were using web-based applications to profit from. Some of my specialty areas include:
- commerce
- advertising
- payments
- search
- gaming
- social networking.

The insight that I gained while consulting enabled me to identify an incredible opportunity and found my own company, BuyPlayWin.com (BPW). BPW creates a novel and fun experience that extends online shopping into the realm of entertainment. Due for release in 2010, BPW was selected out of 400 applications to receive investment and attend the prestigious TechStars.org incubator program in Boulder, CO.

I am accustomed to solving problems and making difficult decisions, largely due to the vibrant personal network that I have invited to get to know me better. Filled with highly competent professionals, mentors, and friends, I frequently request input from those in my network with relevant expertise. These traits are characteristic of start-up veterans.

If you think there might be a good reason for us to connect, please do not hesitate to contact me.


Sincerely,

Aziz Grieser
