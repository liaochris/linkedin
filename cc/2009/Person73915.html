Nick Drake-Knight’s Summary

Nick Drake-Knight has been consulting and speaking for more than 20 years. In the 1990s, he lectured in management, before developing his consulting skills in Europe and the FSU. He returned to work in the UK as a senior business adviser for the DTI funded Business Link network, with a special interest in sales and service environments.

Nick is a board director of the UK’s leading mystery shopping company, Performance in People Ltd., (www.performanceinpeople.co.uk) providing sales and customer service consultancy to many of the UK’s (and the world’s) top retail, financial services and automotive brands.

NDK Group (www.ndk-group.com) is Nick’s personal consulting business, providing the platform for his public speaking interests and the source of his popular books.

He is an authority on the use of video and telephone mystery shopping materials to coach effective sales & customer service technique. He has helped many global brand names to implement his unique Continue & Begin™ coaching model to create and sustain sales & customer service excellence.

Nick has advised on the development of sales & customer service excellence at Mercedes-Benz (UK), Honda (UK), VW (UK), Halifax-Bank of Scotland (HBOS), B&Q, Argos and Homebase (Home Retail Group), O2, The Carphone Warehouse, and many other world-class brands. He continues to work with many of the UK’s leading B2C businesses, developing sales processes and customer service excellence.

Nick Drake-Knight is author of a number of popular business books including:

BOOMERANG! Coach Your Team To Be The Best And See Customers Come Back Time After Time! 254pp, Pollinger, 2007, ISBN 978-1-905665-51-8

MEERKAT SELLING: Be The Best In Big Ticket Retail Selling, 206pp, Pollinger, 2008, ISBN 978-1-905665-70-9

SALES HYPNOSIS: The Structure & Use of Hypnotic Phenomena & Indirect Suggestion in Sales', 224pp, NDK Group, 2004, ISBN 0-9546744-0-5

All are available via www.ndk-group.com.
