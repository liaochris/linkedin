Jeremiah Alexander’s Summary

Jeremiah is an Entrepreneur, specialising in New Media and Digital Technologies. He is currently the Owner and Director of Ideonic Limited, a Serious Games development studio in the North East of England. He has a background in computer games development, in particular programming.

He has a keen interest in the application of new technologies for learning and is the North East (UK) Coordinator for Angils, the European Serious Games Trade Association. He is actively involved in encouraging entrepreneurship in young people and is an ambassador for Young Enterprise and Make your Mark. Additional he is also a business mentor for the NES (New Entrepreneur Scholarship) programme.
