Jan Mundo’s Summary

I work in several domains:

As a copyeditor and writer, I edit and write educational, training, and health-related materials.

As a Somatic Coach, I help clients achieve health, happiness, and meaning through in-depth, transformational coaching, bodywork, and self-care practices.

I specialize in headache and migraine relief and prevention, using my unique, effective hands-on therapy and body-mind coaching program. Lifelong headache sufferers get over their cycles of chronic pain without drugs or side effects.

I offer private sessions, classes, workshops, lectures, seminars, teleclasses and professional training programs.

Jan Mundo’s Specialties:

Copyediting and writing for education, training, and medical.
Somatic Coaching and bodywork, headaches and migraines, stress reduction, body-mind therapies and self-care practices.
