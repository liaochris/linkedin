Daniel Nunes’s Summary

I have a special relationship with the information technology and computers industry. I remember the first PC I used in 1987: it was a XEROX with CPM/M operating system and BASIC programming language. I also remember here the first PC with MS-DOS that I used (it was a Zenith), the DBMS dBASE II and III, Lotus 1-2-3 spreadsheet, Windows 3.1, the first time I used the Internet with a dial-up modem with a stunning velocity 9.6 kbit/s. Great times!

I also have special skills for accounting and financial reporting and analyses. I like the accounting world with it´s E=mc2 equation: Assets = Liabilities + (Shareholders or Owners equity). The double-entry bookkeeping system is for me one of the great inventions of human kind.

After 10 years since I began to work, I managed to work in a dream company: MuchBeta. Here I found a internet company where I am the CFO.
