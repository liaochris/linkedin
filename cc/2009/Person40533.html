Ted Husted’s Summary

Twenty-four years experience building business applications, and nine years experience building enterprise web applications on multiple platforms. Six years experience with test-first and agile development practices. Five years experience designing and conducting training courses for web developers. Four years experience integrating Ajax into web applications.

Longtime member of the Apache Struts group and co-founder of the Apache Commons. Regular speaker at ApacheCon US, as well as to various user groups, including NEJUG. Consulted with teams throughout the United States, including CitiGroup, Nationwide Insurance, and Pepsi Bottling Group. Books include Struts in Action, JUnit in Action, and Professional JSP Site Design.

My professional goals include making the best use of the latest technologies by helping people help themselves.

Ted Husted’s Specialties:

Business analysis, web development, quality assurance, training, database architecture, application architecture, infrastructure analysis. Mentoring, knowledge transfer, documentation, customer support, agile development, unit testing.

Technical specialties: Java/J2EE, JSP, JDBC, SQL, XML, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JUnit, NUnit, Selenium, C#, .NET, Ajax (including Atlas, Dojo, and YUI), iMIS AMS, Ektron CMS.
