Tim Brunelle’s Summary

I'm a writer and creative director. And a drummer. I'm keen on solving business problems with more than words and images.

I grew up in Minneapolis. Got a degree in Jazz. Stumbled into advertising in 1993. Lived on the east coast from 1995-2006. Learned a lot about how ad agencies work (and don't). Got heavily involved in interactive.

The highlight was nine years spent helping lead and evolve the Volkswagen "Drivers wanted." campaign at Arnold Worldwide, including helping launch the New Beetle Convertible, Touareg and Phaeton as well as numerous digital initiatives, including turbonium.com, RadioVW.com and four redesigns of vw.com.

Moved back to Minneapolis in 2006 to help lead Carmichael Lynch into a new era. Now I'm a part of creating a new advertising entity: Hello Viking. We vigorously seek to solve business and marketing problems with words, images and technology. Hello Viking provides strategic and creative consulting and production services to Marketers & Brands; Product Development, Investors & Start-ups; Ad Agencies; PR & Design Firms; Commercial/Video Production Houses and Media Companies & Publishers.

We believe a new kind of advertising has come online, even in traditional venues, based on mutually beneficial inter-action, relevant data and respect. We believe every aspect of a corporation communicates now—from billing to human resources, shipping to governance—and the world is talking about it all. The question isn’t if but when your brand will join the conversation. We seek to practice and implement marketing with innovative partners who agree the consumer is in control.

Let’s listen. Let’s talk. Let’s build and maintain conversation that improves awareness, motivates conversion and energizes loyalty.

Tim Brunelle’s Specialties:

Strategic brand consulting, marketing and media planning, advertising development (creative, production), social media consulting, stewardship and optimization, interactive design and production, intellectual property and application development
