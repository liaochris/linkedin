Lorana Price’s Summary

Holy Cow Branding is a woman-owned business located in Cary, North Carolina. Holy Cow Provides a full spectrum of internal communication and marketing services to mid to large-sized businesses in a variety of industries.

With a strong focus on strategy and a devotion to doing what is best for the client, Holy Cow has become an award-winning agency agency with an ever-expanding client roster.

We are comprised of a talented team of marketing communication, brand strategy and graphic design marketers.
