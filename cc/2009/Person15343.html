Carla Mills works as an executive team member to develop, execute and monitor the results of company strategy with the goal of achieving the full profit potential of a company. I understand the operations of the company and develop the finance group to operate strategically to maximize cash flow, instill discipline on expenses and harvest the leverage that happens when you grow revenues faster than expenses.

WHY I’M HERE ON LINKEDIN:
I believe that staying connected to your social and business network is an asset both personally and professionally. I view my network as a resource for professional advice, business opportunities and sourcing talent in new opportunities.

I offer the following:
♦ Finance Department Start-Up and Transformation. Do you have a growing business that is outpacing the talent in your finance/accounting staff? I have the experience to successfully build talent and processes from the ground up. I work with the business to design processes that are responsive to specific business needs while maintaining a strong environment of internal controls.
♦ Financial and Management Reporting. Are you getting the numbers you need to run your business effectively? I create informative financial statements by intelligently designing the right building blocks needed to produce actionable information. You will be able to understand the underlying factors that produce the business and financial results.
♦ Treasury and Cash Management. Do you have enough cash to meet the demands of your business? I monitor cash like a hawk. Cash is the lifeblood of a business and I establish and maintain banking relationships, optimize your cost of capital and take advantage of payment terms to ensure the cash is there when needed.
