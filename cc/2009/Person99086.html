Tim Pulice’s Summary

Focused and self-reliant writer/editor/project manager who produces original editorial, promotional, and social media content. Particularly skilled as an interviewer, whether subject is an author, musician, actor, CEO, etc. Ideal position also would incorporate talents as a broadcaster. Focused on business, technology, arts & entertainment, politics, and education, main topics of "Bits & Bytes," an online column that ran in Crain's Detroit Business in 2006 and 2007, and currently in personal blog (see link above), "'The Pulice Report." Highly proficient with such Web-based collaborative tools as Google Docs, Zoho Writer, Highrise, and Writeboard. Maintains the highest professional standards.

Tim Pulice’s Specialties:

Retail marketing, social media, online research, branding and identity, content development and management, media/vendor relations, fundraising.
