Rob Mead’s Summary

I’m a freelance journalist, copywriter and editor with over 17 years' experience producing content for a wide range of consumer print and online publications.

I'm professional, enthusiastic and highly motivated. I'm currently looking for freelance or permanent editorial, PR and copywriting opportunities.

I currently work with a wide range of consumer and business publications, writing original news, review and feature pieces for TechRadar, Grand Designs Magazine, MacFormat, Macworld and iGizmo, as well as B2B titles Business Matters and The Works.

My most recent assignment has been to work with a London PR agency, producing high quality press releases for a major blue chip company's 2009 marketing campaign.
