Navin Quadros’s Summary

With over a decade of IT experience spanning Consulting, Technology, and Outsourcing, Navin currently enables companies to effectively harness on the power of technology to achieve business excellence.

Academically, Navin has a Masters degree in Marketing Management and a Bachelors degree in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering from the University of Mumbai. He is also a Cisco Certified Network Associate [CCNA].

In addition to his work, Navin is also a speaker at various Technology Forums. He most recently spoke at PCQuest InfraSummit 2009, where he shared insights about how linking IT with business goals is the primary way for CIOs and business leaders to survive and thrive in the recession.

Navin Quadros’s Specialties:

Navin's core technical expertise is predominantly focussed on IT Infrastructure and its incorporation into Organizations to enhance the value of their business.

His ability to dynamically interface with clients at various levels and enable them to maximize their potential through innovative business and technology solutions is his main strength.
