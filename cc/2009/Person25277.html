Deb Barrett’s Summary

As an award winning designer and Principal in Window Dressings, Deb Barrett has built a reputation for cutting edge couture treatments and soft furnishings. In addition to Window Dressings, she is an accomplished speaker, editor and author. Deb regularly speaks at industry and association events a variety of design, business and trend topics. Barrett’s regular column, Trendspotting appears in Vision magazine and her new book, It’s All in the Details launches early 2009. As a trend strategist, she travels the world to develop design intelligence and interior forecasts. As a market intelligence consultant, Deb regularly works with design industry partners, consumers and professionals to impart trend insights through presentations, white papers and products.

Deb Barrett’s Specialties:

During her successful tenure in design, Deb has had a broad and varied background and experience that is a rare mix in the industry. Her fascination with what’s around the corner and the constant search for inspiration has led her to her current roles as consultant, speaker and design blogger. She hopes to continue to inspire other design pros and her peers by sharing her knowledge and experience about her two passions- couture window coverings and trendspotting.
