Lili Goleniewski’s Summary

Ms. Lillian Goleniewski is Founder and President of the LIDO Organization Inc. Founded in 1984, LIDO is a leading provider of telecommunications education, information, and advisory services. Lili speaks world-wide about trends in communications technology and the importance of knowledge in this constantly changing field. Lili is also the CEO of LIDO Telecom Pvt. Ltd. (LTPL), formed in 2008, this LIDO venture is dedicated to establishment of telecom education centers and e-training facilities serving the vast needs for expert education throughout India, the Asia-Pacific region, the Middle East, and Africa.
Lili is the author of the bestselling Telecommunications Essentials 2nd Edition: The Complete Global Source for Communications Fundamentals, Data Networking and the Internet, Next-Generation Networks, and Wireless Communications (Addison Wesley, October 2006, ISBN 10: 0321427610). Her globally acclaimed LIDO Telecommunications Essentials® seminar series have served over 50,000 professionals and students worldwide since 1984. She is also the creator of the LIDO Telecommunications Essentials® eLearning series, offering over 34 hours of a rich media, multi-dimensional, flexible on-demand learning experience.
Lili has served as Advisory Board Member for numerous industry conferences, technical committees and awards programs, in the US as well as abroad, and has also been an instructor at San Francisco State University. She is currently serving as an Advisory Board member of CompTIA. LIDO further serves those seeking telecom knowledge through LIDO Telecom WebCentral®, and the LIDO Telecom Essentials Learning Center (www.telecomwebcentral.com).
Lili graduated Phi Beta Kappa and Summa Cum Laude from Wayne State University in Detroit, Michigan. She holds a B.A. in Psychology and has completed numerous post-graduate studies in ICT as well as psychology. Lili was the recipient of a NSF Award to conduct research in the area of human perception and information processing
