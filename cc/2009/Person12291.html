Robert E. Stewart, Sr.’s Summary

Bob Stewart has led the development of enterprise scale rich Internet applications for high-profile media consumer sites including Adobe, Boeing, BT, CBS, CNET, Disney, Facebook, Fidelity, Gomez, Hyundai, Intuit, Kodak, NFL, Ribbit, SearchMe, Times Mirror and other public and stealthy Web 2.0 startups.

Stewart served as Chief Technology Officer of Motorola's $1B Internet and Networking Group where he earned Motorola's highest information technology technical ladder rank of Strategic Adviser Technical Analyst.

Bob regularly performs technology due diligence for a number of venture capital firms in North America and Europe where he often acts as interim Chief Technology Officer for select portfolio companies. Bob has also been involved, both as an investment adviser and a transition manager, in substantial M&A activity including a $220M merger with BellSouth and a $290M acquisition from Compaq.

Bob also earned global recognition as Chief Technology Officer for CMGI portfolio companies Planet Direct, CMGI Solutions, Zip2 and MyWay.com.
