Jeff Crites’s Summary

I am a Digital Communication, Marketing and Innovation strategist with deep experience (15 years) in traditional and online media, specializing in community management, business development, social/online media, internal and external 'open innovation' (consumer, employee, community feedback & crowdsourcing). At the heart of all of it is the way communication is evolving.

My full time current position, Online Community Manager at Army.mil (contractor w/ security clearance), involves managing an online community of Army public affairs professionals around the world. I help them use a web-based content management platform to share the Army story via Army.mil (4-7 million unique visits monthly). My job also entails product development, business development, social media and general online communication strategy, and interacting with a wide mix of civilians and Soldiers, up to the highest levels (staff for Secretary of the Army).

I'm passionate about 'Open Innovation' and Collaboration 2.0. Formerly Director, North America at Fellowforce.com, an Open Innovation portal and platform: duties included online marketing, community managment, Sales, PR, project management, branding, product development.

My consultancy, Brick and Click, focuses on digital communication, marketing and innovation for SME, media (radio in particular) and government. Services include: Social Media marketing, E-mail Newsletters, Consumer & Employee feedback solutions.

I blog at BrickandClick.com, and am immersed in social media each day on Twitter (@BrickandClick), Facebook, and here on Linkedin.

My radio career included:
Operations Director/News Anchor, Bill Bennett's Morning in America
Operations Manager/Program Director (3 XM Radio Channels)
Award winning Creative Director (Commercial Writer/Producer)
Podcast Developer/Producer (TownHall.com)
Network Radio News Anchor/Editor (SRN News)
