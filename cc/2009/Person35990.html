Gerry Rubendall’s Summary

I launched GR Design in 2002 after buying out my former business partner. GR turned into something I never expected it to become. I started out creating art & I ended up creating a promotional marketing company.

Determination, rolling with the punches, and help from a lot of good peopel let me grow GR so it was able to double in sales each year. Then in 2006 I merged the company with CMC so we would both be able to bring more to the table for all of our clietns.

After working with CMC for 3 years, my love of working with non-profits and helping small business got the better of me so I moved to the Burleson Area Chamber of Commerce.

In addition to helping companies with their strategic marketing, one of the greatest joys of my life today is the opportunity to work with new entrepreneurs - teaching them how to avoid the mistakes I made along the way.
