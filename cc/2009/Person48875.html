John Allen Dees, Jr’s Summary

15 years experience in sales and marketing. Sales experience has consisted of selling print media, direct mail, and Internet advertising; developing, managing & renting databases. Managed a printing brokerage including the coordination and use of in-house and outsource mail fulfillment services. Demographic research and market analysis and developed marketing solutions for niche businesses. Work has included the sale of consulting contracts to school districts for the management of E-rate funding. Spent over 10 years as the sales and marketing director for a national publishing and advertising company. My key responsibilities and achievements included maintaining a consistent and growing client base made up of Fortune 500 companies as well as small-to-medium businesses that I assisted in the marketing of products and services to high school and college coaches and professional sports teams. In addition I developed new markets for the two national publications and marketing database companies. I was awarded “Client First, Satisfaction Award” in 2008 while working for Statewide Business Brokers, a brokerage company, selling small-to-medium sized businesses in the states of Alabama, Georgia and Louisiana. In summary, I have been successful doing direct sales, phone sales and writing sales copy for direct mail promotions and Internet marketing.
Currently I am the East Coast & South East Account Executive for SmartWave Technologies a wireless centric consulting engineering firm specializing in the design, build and deployment of wireless networks and the applications these networks support.
