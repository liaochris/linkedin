Glenn Hernandez’s Summary

Specializing in motion graphics, visual effects, 3D animation, keying, compositing, motion tracking, particles, print design and multimedia.

A native of NYC, Glenn graduated from New York Institute of Technology with a degree in Computer Science and became involved with computer graphics when he started working for the New York Mets. Setting out on his own, and Having both artistic and technical skills, has given him the opportunity to work with various media and with a broad range of clients.

Through the years he has acquired and mastered a broad range of skills in web development, print/video and interactive advertising, motion graphics, visual effects and 3D animation. His past work includes everything from commercials, corporate presentations, in-game live graphics, DVD creation and interactive media.

His list of clients include, NY Mets, Nickelodeon, A&E, ADM Productions and M2 Post. Indirect clients include, MLB, Yankees, NY Giants, NY Jets, NY Rangers, NY Nets, NJ Devils, US Tennis Open, Walmart, Canon, Sam's Club, Motorola, Goodyear, Weight Watchers,

Glenn Hernandez’s Specialties:

Glenn has 12 years of professional experience in various applications, including but not limited to: After Effects CS4, Cinema 4D & Xpresso, PFHoe Pro, Mocha and Motor, Final Cut Studio 2, Final Cut Pro, DVD Studio Pro, Compressor, Photoshop CS4, Inscriber® G3 On-Air Graphics Systems, Keynote, Flash, Dreamweaver, Illustrator, PowerPoint, Quark, Indesign. Some experience with Shake, Nuke, Fusion, RealFlow and Maya
