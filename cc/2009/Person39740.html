Hilary Johnson’s Summary

Hilary spend a good portion of her life working in various office and administrative positions before rediscovering her creative side when working with a friend who brought her into the field of of handcrafted jewelry. Hilary has been designing her own line since June 2005.

Her long-time interest in rocks and geology has become a passion. When she isn't creating or marketing, she is reading rock and gemstone encyclopedias and geological field books. She now looks at every creek bed and road cut with excitement and anticipation.

Currently her pieces are available on Etsy at http://hiljohn,etsy,com and she also sells directly through local network groups, office and home shows.

Her work is available for charitable donation for silent auctions.

Visit her blog at http://57designstudio.blogspot.com.
Facebook profile is http://www.facebook.com/home.php#/profile.php?id=1018899439.
Follow her on Twitter http://twitter.com/HilaryJ.
