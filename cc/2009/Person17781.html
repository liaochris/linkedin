Chris Durr’s Summary

Copywriter, Reporter, Editor, Proofreader, Videographer, Multimedia Specialist

Several years of reporting, writing, editing, and managing for a college newspaper and embarking on a one-of-a-kind year-long multimedia internship led me along the path to freelance reporting for a metropolitan daily.

Taking on another internship at a cutting-edge Web and multi-media design firm allowed me to learn a plethora of skills in a creative and challenging field.

Skills include:

- Ease of use with Adobe, Microsoft, and other software.
- Shooting still photos and video
- Writing in a variety of voices, tones, and styles: from technical and formal to relaxed and witty
- Original and thoughtful idea creation
