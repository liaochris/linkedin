Lennart Sandelin’s Summary

My whole working life has had one common denominator:
I have always been working with people.
From being a hospital orderly at a ward for long-term patients, cashier at former Åhléns Livs, telephone interviewer at SIFO (SIFO Research and Consulting)
to being a salesman the last 20 years. Up till 1998 within advertising,
when I changed trade to the IT-business and became a Sales Representative/Recruiter of System developers (IT consultants), at a company called Quaint AB.

I stayed there for five years and then went back to Media Sales (TM) for three years at Nordic Netproducts AB (former Ekonomisk Litteratur AB).

From September 2006 thru October 2007 I were back in the Consultancy Trade,
as a Marketing Coordinator at Momentus Management Consulting AB.

With my newly acquired knowledge about Process Structured Thinking and organized way to work, I were once again back in the Media Business, as a Project Manager/WebSales at Hexanova Media Group, ready to turn this knowledge into practice.

After close to a year at Hexanova I came in contact with a company specializing in producing cost effective web sites for the SME-/SMB-segment of the market and as it turned out, they gave me an offer to start up their InsideSales Dept in Stockholm.
I accepted their offer and became their Inside Sales Manager towards said market.

Unfortunately my time there were brief, as the global Financial crisis struck their clients hard - why I left rather promptly and now work as a Sales Rep within the MediaPlant Group; first at the leading CD- and DVD- (incl Blu-Ray) manufacturing company in the Nordic region, MediaPlant AB, and now since June 2009 at the Wholesales company Harry Bergfelt AB, also within the MediaPlant Group.

Lennart Sandelin’s Specialties:

Internet, Web, Sales, Inspirer, "Door Opener", "People Person", Public Procurements Coordinator, TM, TeleSales, Specialist at making the "impossible" business meeting come true!
