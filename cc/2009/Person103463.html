Dan Von Kohorn’s Summary

Portfolio manager for Commodities fund; investment committee for two equity funds. Financial technician with experience in bonds, equities, commodities, asset allocation, and building analytics for each. Focus on quantitative analysis: relative value, timeseries, yield/premium curves, fundamental valuation, statistical arbitrage, and efficient frontier modeling. Also enjoy assisting creative technology companies through financing and strategic partnerships.

Dan Von Kohorn’s Specialties:

Equity and commodity trading, web-based application development, timeseries forecasting, yield curve analytics, fundamental valuation, efficient frontier modeling.
