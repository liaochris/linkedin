Bob MacNeil’s Summary

Illustrator/ Graphic Designer with over 16 years experience. Works can be found in video game and animation productions as well as static imagery for print and packaged consumer goods.

Bob MacNeil’s Specialties:

I am traditionally trained as a painter/ illustrator, but presently use digital media exclusively in my work. I am well versed in Adobe's Photoshop and Illustrator and have a tendency for my digital work to appear painted by media such as acrylics or watercolor. I also have a strong sense and understanding of design and how to apply it intelligently to boost the aesthetic value of a given project.
