Jade Bunker Gonzáles’s Summary

Design is problem solving. I love the double challenge of creating a solution that is both functional and beautiful.

My work extends across media, including print, screen and identity branding. I am seeking a work environment that will continually challenge me with a strong work/play ethic and tough standards that yields creative thinking and smart design concepts. I am a doer and a practical perfectionist with a keen eye for kerning and leading, texture and color, and the budget and deadlines. My toolbox includes strong conceptual, design, typography, photography, drawing/painting/cutting, writing and communication skills.
