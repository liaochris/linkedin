Kyung Min’s Summary

Kyung Min is a progressive-minded individual, with a diverse background in all the modern communication arts, who wishes to help expedite change and make a positive impact on society. Throughout his higher education training, Mr. Min has fully embraced the latest technologies and all the opportunities they have to offer.

He learned how to use the latest digital audio technologies and work in the evolving music industry from his education at Berklee College of Music and his work in professional studios and with artists and film directors. He believes that music is only part of a comprehensive visual marketing concept, which is vital to creating an image, a message, or a brand. To that end, he has worked full time at Digidesign as Content Manager of Web Marketing, and currently he is further developing his skills in new media arts at Academy of Art University, as well as working working full time as Creative Director of Elative Marketing and Director of Marketing for Addo Label.

Mr. Min hopes that all of the progress that he eagerly embraces and works towards also benefits society on a universal scale. Environmental issues are of special interest and concern to him, so he helped create Addo Label, an all-digital record label with the hopes of influencing the record industry to not only further embrace digital distribution and new media marketing channels, but also address environmental issues affected by the business. In addition, at Digidesign, Mr. Min was a representative of the Marketing department for the internal Environmental Management Team, which is devoted to ensuring that the various activities and practices of Digidesign help to preserve a sustainable planet.
