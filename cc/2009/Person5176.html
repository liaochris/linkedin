Andrea Gold (www.goldstars.com)’s Summary

President and owner of international speakers bureau for 20+ years.

Expert consultant on matching resources (speakers, trainers, facilitators, entertainers) to the groups' needs, for corporations, associations and government agencies worldwide.

Catalyst for solutions for both business and personal life potential. Adventurous and innvovative mindset, embracing constant self-improvement and prompting others to live life fully.

Co-author of "The Business of Successful Speaking" (www.goldstars.com/products/008.html). Also 3 values education stories for children, former newspaper reporter & photographer, freelance writer.

Additional work experience: Direct marketing, editing and technical editing, physical labor, and retail sales.

Active member in International Association of Speakers Bureaus (IASB), Meeting Professionals International (ISB), Meetings Community (MeCo).
