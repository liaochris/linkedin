Kurt Rampton’s Summary

Senior industrial designer at BOLTgroup, a Charlotte design consultancy.

I handle research, conceptualization, and design development for new products with high complexity and scope. My role is to generate ideas and solutions to benefit the user's experience and the client's bottom line. I specialize in human-factors, aesthetic, mechanical, and sustainability challenges. I work closely with the diverse and talented team at BOLTgroup to realize innovation in products and delight in users.

Kurt Rampton’s Specialties:

Human factors, form development, design for sustainability, user experience, brand impression, product innovation
