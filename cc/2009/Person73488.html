Nestor Marquez’s Summary

I have developed a great deal of experience across many industries both at client and consulting and agency side.

I have worked for multinational corporations such as E.I Du Pont de Nemours & Co., Accenture, WPP/Young & Rubican/Wunderman, DDB as well to smaller initiatives like Argentine Post, Response Logic and Manifesto DDB. I have Argentine and Italian (EU) citizenship and immigrant papers for Mexico.

Nestor Marquez’s Specialties:

Argentine with experience of working in Brazil (2 yrs) and Mexico (the last 5 yrs.) and many industries: Communications (Wunderman, Y&R, Manifesto DDB), Consulting (Accenture), D&B (Diageo, Cerveceria Cuahutemoc) Automotive (Ford, VW), FMCG (Coca Cola, Bimbo, Danone), Retail (Disco), Telecos (Telefonica, Movistar), Software and Computers (Dell, Nokia, Microsoft) and Banking (Banamex, Citibank).
