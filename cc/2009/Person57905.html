Lauren Milligan’s Summary

ResuMAYDAY staffs a team of expert resume writers from a variety of backgrounds. We offer expert resume development, interview skills coaching and other services that will help launch and execute a professional and effective job search. Each resume is carefully crafted to promote our clients in a manner that completely supports their career goals. This means that all of our work is customized; we never use resume templates.

For our corporate clients, we offer affordable and top-notch OUTPLACEMENT services for up to 50 employees at a time. We have redefined traditional outplacement. Outdated services that don't provide any value to your employees have been eliminated, while other services have been enhanced. Our main goal is to provide everything that your employees need to compete in today's aggressive employment market.

Business owners - your clients want to know who you are! Do your online and print marketing materials include your Professional Bio? A compelling and dynamic bio is the easiest way to build trust and rapport with your clients.

Job seekers are not the only ones who need interview skills. Have you ever hired the perfect person, only to regret it a few weeks later? Our Interviewer Skills Program is the perfect solution. Don't make expensive hiring mistakes ever again! Let's face it; no one has really ever been 'trained' to conduct an interview - until now! We'll discuss the most modern interview scenarios and the right vs. wrong things that managers do. Call for more details.
