Remco Oostelaar’s Summary

Remco Oostelaar has got a broad experience in testing and project management especially in the financial and products domain. In the previous years he has gained a wide experience as a test coordinator around off-shore and outsourcing of test activities.

Every project has got its own characteristics, which means there is no standard solution to achieve the customer business goal. In collaboration with the customer and his team Remco will deliver the best strategy. To realize the project case he strongly acts on quality and transparency business activities.

In his work he is guiding and result-oriented and is strongly connected with the task and his team. On one hand he works on long term business objectives and on the other hand he takes up rapidly operational challenges within the project.

Remco Oostelaar’s Specialties:

Functional acceptance testing, Implementation Planning, Integration Testing, IT Management, ITIL, Mercury Performance Testing Tools, Performance Stress Test Case Development, PRINCE2, System Testing, Test Strategy, Testing, Testing Methods, Testing Tools, User Acceptance Testing, Windows Client Systems
