Rob Field’s Summary

I'm Rob Field, a professional visual effects artist living and working in Utah.

Some of my work includes a short film selected for the Sundance Film Festival, ABC's Golden Globe nominated series "Pushing Daisies", a Disney feature film, numerous indie films, and television work for the Discovery Channel and the History Channel. Return clients of mine include the US Navy and The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.

I am primarily a compositor with work experience in both Nuke and AfterEffects. I am also a matchmover and tracker, currently working in PFTrack, SynthEyes, Monet, and Mocha. I also have years of experience delivering 3D and CGI work in Maya and will frequently choose to render through Cinema4D.

Please visit my website to view my demo reel or contact me regarding your project.
