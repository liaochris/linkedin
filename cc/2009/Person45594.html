Jeffrey K. Radt’s Summary

I'm the President of Kingmaker International, Inc., one of North America's premier Executive Search Firms that is committed to improving the quality of the recruiting industry as well as the quality of the recruiting experience one client and candidate at a time. By addressing key challenges faced during this process I hope to demonstrate that there is a better way of doing things.

I possess 11 years of experience and a strong track record of success in both Sales/Marketing and Recruiting with a portfolio of national and international clients. My philosophy is simple - - foster integrity and trust by personally managing the recruiting process from the start to the start date. Most of all, I strive to be passionate about every single aspect of my work.

Why Recruiting? Because I believe there isn't another industry and/or line of work that can provide me with as much opportunity to create positive change than this one. What we do as Recruiters has the ability to not only change lives it can change companies, industries, and even local economies. Throughout our careers in this industry, that potential will always remain. There is always a chance to do more, to do it better, to effect change, and to produce positive results.

I am humbled to be in a position such as this. I would be honored to be in a position to help you in any way that I can.
