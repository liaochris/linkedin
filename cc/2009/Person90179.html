Sean Wing’s Summary

Sean Wing is just your everyday, run in the mill, type of guy, other than a few distinct features which drive him to achieve the utmost success in every area of his life and his work. Sean Wing has a determination to succeed in life, using his God given talents to excel in his profession of business. As a serial entrepreneur, Sean has been starting businesses of all size, since he was nine. From creating his own car washing business, hiring local kids to do the work while Sean managed the administration to major ventures in online networking, Sean has managed to learn and enjoy the taste of success, and even more, that of failure. As Thomas Watson, the founder of IBM stated, “If you want to double your rate of success, you must quadruple your rate of failure.” This, Sean has found, is as true as the sky is blue. Although young in many aspects, Sean has managed to spring into action in both mind and soul to produce effect, quick, and satisfying results, proving his capability in major situation, involving both personal and business life. Still waiting for his million dollar ventures, which are hopefully at the verge, Sean Wing will continue to proceed in the realm of business, and with the help of his dear friends, find success, adventure, and a life.
