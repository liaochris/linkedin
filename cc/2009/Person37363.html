Grant Plowman’s Summary

We work with subject matter experts to help share their expertise. When words alone are not sufficient we create video, animations, audio, and interactive exercises to supplement your material. Whatever the topic, who ever the audience: we help to make the message effective and the results memorable and measurable.

Experience..
We have more that 15 years experience in creating all forms of educational experiences that change lives and inspire learners. Our clients include universities, state and local government, corporate and non profit.

We also publish……
If asked, we publish all support materials including books, workbooks, DVD, CD, and or Internet based delivery. We create packages that fit your audience and desired delivery method. We provide the level of support you desire from instructional design, writing, editing, e-commerce, to web based course delivery, on screen talent, voice over, forums, chat, webinars, testing, quizzing, surveys, forms, to technical support, etc. We have our own video and audio studio as well as full print, bind, and publish facilities in house.


Full Featured on-line school support…
We are also developing a fully integrated on-line school so that subject matter experts may offer their content in a variety of formats including both synchronous and asynchronous modes.

In short you provide expertise, and we provide everything else to deliver your message. Let’s chat and discuss your expertise, your market, and how to make your knowledge available to a larger audience.

Just tell me a good time to call and the number I should use.

I look forward to discussing your passion and what you wish to share with others.
