David Thomson’s Summary

Currently the Chief Creative Officer of Momentum Design Lab, a premier user experience design company located in San Francisco, California. With years of experience, our team members have worked with various successful start-ups and large-scale enterprises to produce graphics and interactive interfaces for web-based products. Because we have worked with companies in industries ranging from software and hardware to social networking and non-profit we are confident our team can help you strategize and successfully take your product to market.

We have worked with companies across industries including: software, hardware, financial services, retail, healthcare, manufacturing, legal, entertainment, media, human resources, social networking, mobile, television, logistics, and non-profit.

Over the three years we have successfully helped the following companies bring their projects to market: Intel, Orange Labs, MobiTV, SuccessFactors, Rapt, Knewco, Adeptra, California Association of Realtors, Barnes and Noble, Ariba, CleverMachine, Biz360, IAC, Involver, Rocketseed, Opposing Views, Marketbright, Databasics, BondDesk, Openwave, VINTek, GoFish, VaultStreet, Polimetrix, SkyData Systems, Atlantis Computing, SelectLeaders, Overstock, Fair Isaac, Conklin Intracom, HedgeOP, Moderati, Walmart, Tript, Club Spaces, LinkStorm, VantageILM, RoadRunner Online, Cerego Japan, SuccessMetrics, VFlyer, Concurro, and NeoClarus.

During the past two years, eight of seventy of our customers have raised over a combined $175 million in venture funding and $660 million in public offerings since working with us to improve or develop their product.
