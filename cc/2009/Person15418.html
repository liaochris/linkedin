Carl Moss’s Summary

An experienced independent consultant and project manager, for over 20 years I have helped financial organisations select, implement and optimise their systems. I have experience of managing multiple concurrent projects through all stages of the systems development life cycle using various structured methodologies. Strongly business and delivery-focused, I have a good track record of productive liaison between stakeholders and IT teams, as well as effective 3rd party vendor contract negotiation and management. Recently focused on Client Reporting, IT Controls, Governance and Compliance (Sarbanes-Oxley), eBusiness/Web applications, CRM and Investment Performance Attribution. I am a team player and enjoy mentoring client staff. Currently non-exec Director and co-owner (with wife, Julia) of Polestar Pilates UK Ltd., where I have been working on website development (using Adobe Dreamweaver CS4) and the implementation of GoldMine Special Edition CRM.

Carl Moss’s Specialties:

System selection and implementation projects, client reporting and communications, CRM, performance measurement and attribution, SOX verification, website re-freshes.
