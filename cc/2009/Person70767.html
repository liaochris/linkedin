Madanu Ujjwal Kumar’s Summary

Madanu Ujjwal Kumar is the founder and CEO of MakeLogic.

Madanu helped the company build an active working partnership with Microsoft at the time when Windows Mobile was in its beta stage. MakeLogic developed MicroGraphs a charting solution for this new and powerful platform.

Microsoft conducted a case study on MakeLogic and MicroGraphs. Please see : http://download.microsoft.com/documents/customerevidence
/5967_MAKELOGIC_dotnet_Compact_Framework_Final.DOC

Madanu then took the company to provide work flow solutions to field force automation using mobile devices. MakeLogic developed a custom solution to a news paper company in New York. The solution was used by the company to help the truck drivers distribute tabloids to various retailers around the city.

MakeLogic developed an efficient Business Process Management (BPM) solution which helped the news paper company reduce the confusion and save time in reaching their customers. The solution was much appreciated.

Madanu built a robust 'offshore development' model for MakeLogic based on strong methodologies and regular customer interactions. MakeLogic involves the customer at every stage of solution development.

Good and responsible corporate behaviour is helping the company gain new customers and retain the old ones.

MakeLogic contributes significant amount of time and effort to open source solutions. Products developed by MakeLogic are used by *THOUSANDS* of individuals and *HUNDREDS* of enterprises around the world.

Madanu has *BIGGER* plans for the company and wants to reach them by generating value for customers, employees and investors.

Madanu believes that this inter-dependence of employees, customers and investors with good ethical values can create a win-win situation for every one involved.
