TEAM-BUILDING AND TRAINING: (15 years) Facilitate enterprise teams to adapt SharePoint to existing IT infrastructure and policies. INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT PROJECT MANAGEMENT: (27 years) Design, monitor and evaluate development projects in IT, knowledge management, and economic growth in developing countries. Supervised staff in U.S. and overseas, and coordinated multinational projects. Retired 2006 as a Foreign Service Officer with USAID.
