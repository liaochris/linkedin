Ziv Elul’s Summary

I'm the Co-Founder and Co-CEO at innerActive Smart Media.
innerActive has created a world where mobile content is free for everyone.
A leading provider of rich media advertising solutions, innerActive is the first to create advertising-funded entertainment channels. innerActive offers a complete solution for content and advertising, with contextual, dynamic adverts, embedded in the mobile content – be it Games, Video, Instant Messaging or other WAP applications. innerActive works in partnership with mobile operators, off- deck portals, advertisers and content providers, enabling them to offer free entertainment content to their customers.
