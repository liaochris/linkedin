Stephanie Fierman’s Summary

Nearly 20 years experience combining branding, messaging and communications with expertise in data-rich, measurable customer acquisition, retention and growth-driven businesses in multiple industries.

Proven track record of innovation and successful brand-building for growing ventures as well as Fortune 50 organizations. Core expertise in mobilizing and motivating diverse, often decentralized product organizations and finance, PMO, customer service, operations and technology teams in the U.S. and abroad.

Stephanie Fierman’s Specialties:

* Leveraging a full spectrum of tools incl. research, branded content, social media, search and online brand management.
* Defining a strategy and motivating, coaching and organizing colleagues across an organization to incorporate and execute against it.
*Integrating offline and online marketing, service and analytics to deliver meaningful information to all sales and customer touchpoints.
* Creating a reputation of thought leadership based on a company's true "voice" and point of view.
