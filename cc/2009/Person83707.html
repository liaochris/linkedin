Richard Purvis’s Summary

My Background:
For the best part of a Decade I have Worked for, Sold, Marketed and Consulted for Job boards, Recruitment Consultancies, Blue Chips, SME's and Advertising Agencies.

In every case it has involved online recruitment. I decided to set up Crunch to fill what I still see as the skill gap, between the opportunity OLR offers and the knowledge to realise that potential.

Our core focus is on providing companies with a way to monitor every penny spent on recruiting. We provide a Methodology and an Auditing service that allows you to make real judgments about your suppliers (media or otherwise) based on performance.

My Mission Statement is:
“Crunch Digital Media Consultancy is a business focused on providing expert support and advice to companies and individuals looking to recruit via the internet. We provide outsourced solutions and knowledge covering every aspect of candidate attraction via a digital source”
In simple terms we plan, buy and supply online/digital media for recruitment ad agencies, recruitment consultants and HR department.

Why outsource to Crunch?
• Enhance capabilities in key competitive areas – Win pitches and retain clients on your strength in online
• Partner with experts to increase innovation – Crunch are leaders in new thinking for recruitment advertising and web 2.0
• Increase the ability to focus on core competencies – let your teams do what they do best
• Move from fixed costs to variable costs – digital experts are expensive to employ full time
• Reduce costs – no planning time, no time wasted negotiating media, Crunch already have the best rates in the market
• Speed time to market – With a combined 30 years experience buying and selling digital media we can recommend, plan and buy faster than anyone else.
• Improve business performance – Using online will increase your profit and improve your client’s response rates at the same time!!!
