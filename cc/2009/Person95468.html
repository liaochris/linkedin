Stuart Foster’s Summary

Stuart Foster was born in Maine, raised in Maine, and went to school in Maine where he majored in English at Bates College. Stuart started off working as a Marketing Specialist for BCC Research and soon realized that social media would be his meal ticket to internet fame and glory (the Tay Zonday of Marketing). Addicted to Bolt, Facebook, and other Social Media since their inception, he has taken this enthusiasm for new media to make connections, blog market, and explore other marketing efforts within those realms. He enjoys massive quantities of Diet Coke/caffeine, Boston sports, running (slowly). Major problem: Stuart has a tendency to lose jackets.
