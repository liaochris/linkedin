Donald L. ("Skip") Conover’s Summary

India and the Middle East combined represent the largest economic opportunity of the next quarter century. I have been developing international businesses as an executive and entrepreneur since 1979, and I have been developing relationships in Saudi Arabia (63% of the GCC funds) since 2002.

Link to me on LinkedIn using: dlconover@msn.com

In the period 1998-2006, I founded and led CBay Systems, Ltd. to become the largest in a 1,500 company healthcare services industry. Starting from zero in 1998, my colleagues and I built a company, which now grosses over $400 million annually, and went public on the London Stock Exchange (AIM: CBAY) in June 2007. On August 6, 2008, CBAY purchased 69.5% of MedQuist, the largest company in its industry, for US$285 million. My non-compete with CBay expired at midnight on September 30, 2008.
