Jon Samsel’s Summary

Jon has a 17 year background in Internet business strategy and e-commerce. Jon is a blogger, published author, and speaker. Currently, Jon focuses on social-search marketing, website optimization, content publishing & syndication, as well as paid online marketing campaigns.

Jon co-authored five books, one of which is available for free in a cool, 3D PDF format by following this link:

Jon Samsel’s Specialties:

Online marketing, microsite development, landing page optimization, social media marketing, SEO, SEM, UGC, Twitter applications, mash-ups, user-centric design, blogging, writing, public speaking, mobile interface design, and e-commerce integration.
