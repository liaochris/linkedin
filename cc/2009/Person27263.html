Dolf Muccillo’s Summary

Senior Technical Recruiter at Cardinal Solutions Group.

* Involved in all phases of recruitment lifecycle from sourcing to offer and rejection processes.

* Sourcing and attracting a wide range of IT professionals from college graduates to senior level resources via traditional and Internet-based methods.

* Targeting of passive candidates via LinkedIn, Facebook, MySpace, employee referral bonus programs, local user groups and creative job postings.

* Evaluating large numbers of resumes and applications. Prescreening qualified candidates by phone. Determining if screened candidates should proceed to technical interviews.

* Scheduling and coordinating face to face interviews and other requisite interactions with hiring managers. Promoting the idea of “Recruiting as an opportunity, not a chore.”

* Spearheaded initiative to raise employee referral bonus leading to a significant increase in total referrals. Evangelized the use of LinkedIn and integrated it with Cardinal’s employee referral bonus program.

* Creation of custom feedback forms with Adobe LiveCycle Designer.
