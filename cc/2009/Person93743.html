Stacy Williams’s Summary

Stacy Williams’ extensive marketing experience laid the foundation for the creation of Prominent Placement, a full-service, award-winning Atlanta search marketing firm, in 2001. An expert on internet marketing solutions, Stacy is sought after nationwide as a regular speaker on search engine marketing, and is frequently published on the subject. As President of Prominent Placement, Stacy oversees the daily operations of the company, assuring that campaigns are managed with the highest quality and proficiency.

Prominent Placement leads the search engine marketing industry with its extensive marketing background, as well as attention to each and every client, educating companies on the value of effective, strategic search engine marketing in their overall marketing agenda.

Prior to her establishment of Prominent Placement, Stacy’s career spanned both coasts, serving at advertising agencies in both Los Angeles and Atlanta. Most recently, she spent nine years at the ad agency Kilgannon.

A graduate of the University of California at Berkeley's Haas School, she received a B.S. in Business Administration, with an emphasis in Marketing. Stacy also received an M.A. in Communications Management from the University of Southern California's Annenberg School. She was a finalist for the Georgia Woman Entrepreneur of the Year Award for 2006.

Stacy Williams’s Specialties:

Strategic search engine marketing, including search engine optimization, pay-per-click campaigns (paid listings), link-building, optimized press releases, local search marketing and Internet Yellow Pages, search engine reputation management, web analytics and reporting.
