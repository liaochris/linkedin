Fred Schlomka’s Summary

For over twenty-five years Fred Schlomka has been the chief executive of several companies and non-profit organizations on both sides of the Atlantic including American Caribbean Builders in Florida and Mosaic Communities in Israel. In 2003 he was awarded a Social Entrepreneur Fellowship from the Echoing Green Foundation in New York. He has also lectured on Palestine/Israel issues in Europe and the USA, and his articles have been published widely.

Mr. Schlomka is currently building a new social enterprise, ToursInEnglish.com, and consults with businesses and non-profit organizations (NGOs) in the Palestine/Israel arena.

Fred Schlomka’s Experience

  • Owner

    www.ToursInEnglish.com

    (Leisure, Travel & Tourism industry)

    November 2007 — Present (1 year 9 months)

    Tours in English is a social enterprise providing alternative tours that are informative and analytical, covering the culture, history, and political geography of Israel/Palestine/West Bank. The wide range of itineraries visit areas that embody the history and current status of the conflict between Israelis and Palestinians, and between Israel's Jewish and Arab citizens.

    The tours are part of the emerging sustainable economy and benefit Palestinian organizations, villagers, and tour guides. The information and analysis provided on the tours are based on human rights considerations, the rights of self-determination, and the rights of all peoples to live in safety and political freedom.

  • Partner

    North Bridge Investments

    (Partnership; 1-10 employees; Investment Management industry)

    November 2006 — Present (2 years 9 months)

    NBI is a unique, Israeli-registered company operating at the Palestinian/Israeli interface, providing economic development and management services, and facilitating investment projects in Israel and Palestine. The company combines a social investment approach with a strategy of matching Palestinian, Israeli and international capital with profitable projects appropriate to the equitable integration of the Palestinian and Israeli economies.

    The Principals of NBI are Sam Bahour and Fred Schlomka. They combine a background of business innovation, social entrepreneurship, and multinational experience. Mr. Bahour and Mr. Schlomka have family roots in Israel and Palestine, and returned from the USA to assist in the development of the country’s economy and civic society. Both men, a Palestinian and an Israeli, share a vision of a just and democratic society, and have developed their partnership to facilitate meaningful business relationships across the Palestinian/Israeli divide.
