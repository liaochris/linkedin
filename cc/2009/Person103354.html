Vladimir Neykov’s Summary

Vlad is 3D Artist and Computer Games Professional. Along with creating strong visuals, he is also interested in the humanity behind games in the areas of social simulations and serious games.
Vlad earned his Master of Fine Arts degree in Interactive Design and Game Development at the Savannah College of Art and Design. He has previously earned a Bachelor of Arts degree in Computer Art and an Associate degree in Multimedia.
