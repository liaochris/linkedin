Eric Bostrom’s Summary

ASD has been in business since 1986 servicing IT related projects nationwide. We have performed hundreds of projects for companies such as Allstate Insurance, Career Education, FedEx Ground, Education Management, HondaJet, Intergraph, McKesson and Unisys, just to name a few. In 2008 we managed projects in over 2200 zip codes nationwide.

ASD is a four time Inc. 5,000 company, representing the fastest growing private companies in the U.S.A. We employ approximately 35 Associates and over 900 AASDIs (Authorized ASD Installers). We are debt free, cash flow positive and financially sound.

Our projects range from cabling buildings with fiber optic and category 6 cable, designing and installing VoIP networks, wireless networks, digital signage, rollouts, access control and CCTV to setting up PC's and network swithes. If it has an IP address, we install it.

The majority of our clients have a relationship with ASD that averages 8 years! That says a lot about the type of service we provide.

Check us out at www.asd-usa.com or drop me a line for additional information.
