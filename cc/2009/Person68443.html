Mike Cuppett’s Summary

"Delivering Business Solutions through Technology Alignment"

Mr. Cuppett is a 20+ year industry veteran specializing in delivering business enabling technology solutions. His track record reveals strong leadership, focused team development and personalized staff mentoring resulting in exceptionally effective and goal-driven teams. His broad experience covers many core disciplines including:

Operations – supporting the 24x7x365 availability of critical applications and databases, including an e-commerce environment generating $100M+ in revenue.

Security – implementing controls and policies to meet SOX, PCI-DSS, disaster recovery/business continuity, internal and external audits and forensic processing governance compliance.

Infrastructure – designing highly-available, excellent performing system architectures driven in response to the business’s strategy and demand.

Quality – instituting change management processes for packaging and delivering software releases, including testing and monitoring requirements. Performing as Test Director for large scale application releases concerning U.S. Army applications used in the European theater of operations.

Mr. Cuppett has successfully led teams responsible for distributed systems, database and data warehouse operations, application development, security, data center operations, e-commerce environments, desktop technologies, networks and telecommunications. Mr. Cuppett’s continual focus on delivering solutions based on the needs of the business while improving the customer’s experience has proven to be a powerful and successful strategy. Mr. Cuppett holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Management and Computer Information Systems and an Associate of Science degree in Computer Studies. As a lifelong learner, Mr. Cuppett is currently pursuing dual graduate degrees focused on Financial Management and Information Systems and the CISSP security certification.
