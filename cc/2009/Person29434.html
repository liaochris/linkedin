Eduard Blacquière’s Summary

Besides the work I am doing from within my positions, I am actively reading and writing about the search industry. As a graduate student I conducted an extensive market research for the search engine marketing market in the Netherlands.

My research has been adapted with the industry and is considered a to be the first quality benchmark of the search industry in the Netherlands.

That gave me the opportunity to speak at some events. For instance I was a speaker at the Affiliate Dag in 2008, a large affiliate marketing event in the Netherlands.

In 2009 the Search Engine Strategies conference came to the Netherlands for the first time. I was honoured with opening the event with a presentation about an update of the market research I did.
