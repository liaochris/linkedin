Joel Abraham’s Summary

For over 16 years, I have developed innovative and cost effective niche technical talent pipelines. Throughout these 16 years, I’ve been working with IT & Recruiting departments to improve their ability to identify and acquire critical-to-success niche talent. I have proven success hitting critical objectives, niche resource deadlines, and reducing cost to hit ROI goals.

My business & industry experience ranges from either being the “tip of the spear” for early technology adopters, enhancing technical capabilities, to finding the missing niche IT skill sets to hit your project time lines.

My talent acquisition experience makes it easy to fit into how my clients source, recruit and onboard their resources. I understand the full recruitment & business cycle, which is reflected in the flexible and customizable services. (targeted recruitment, diversity, and enhancing MVS programs’ niche capabilities meet clients’ business needs).

My success has been my ability to build, sustain and grow long term relationships and a proactive network. I have read thousands of resumes, and interviewed almost as many candidates. In my profile, you will see information that I hope helps anyone looking for work or is scared of the uncertainty of the current job market. (See Docs)

My passion is business & technology. I do understand the current economic climate, most companies want to hire, but do not have the budget to do so. I am looking for an opportunity to understand your background, technologies your company is using, and what you look for in a valued resource. In return I promise to provide fair prices, honest feedback, sense of urgency, qualified resources and competitive market information.

Candidates: I am willing to help in any way I can, whether it be providing tips, or sharing my network. Before you reach out to me, understand that there are a lot of job seekers who need help, in order to receive my support you will have to put the effort in.
