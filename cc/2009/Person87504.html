Pastor Ryan Rose’s Summary

Awesome Flash Video Below
http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QRmz03i0jo8

First and foremost, Ryan is a dedicated Christian who loves Jesus Christ with all of his heart.

Ryan graduated from the University of the Ozarks, with honors in May of 2007. While there, Ryan was involved in all forms of media available; he was a producer for KUOZ Channel 6 News the University’s news station that was broadcast to the campus, community, and surrounding areas, a radio show host on KUOZ 100.5, a reporter for the Mountain Eagle Newspaper, a photographer for the “Aerie” Ozarks yearbook, and a play-by play and color commentator for the University’s Men’s and Women’s Basketball and Soccer games as well as a paid PA Announcer.

Ryan is the Media Pastor at Butterfield Assembly of God Church in Van Buren, AR.

Ryan is the Director of all the Multi-Media activities for the church. From the audio and video production during services to editing mission trip documentaries, Ryan also trains and oversees a media team of approximately 20 Media Ministry Members. We are hoping to more than double our media team in the next few months.
