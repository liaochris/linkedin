Nancy Mann Jackson’s Summary

An award-winning freelance writer, my work appears in a variety of magazines and web sites for consumer, trade and custom audiences.

Recently I've written for consumer publications including Entrepreneur.com, Working Mother, Relish, Hobby Farm Home, Ode, Bankrate.com, Preservation, and American Heritage.

I also contribute to a number of custom publications including Hammock Publishing's American Spirit (client: Daughters of the American Revolutioni), MyBusiness (client: National Federation of Independent Business), and The Magazine Group's Higher Ed Tech (client: CDW Corporation).

My work also appears in trade publications. I contribute monthly departments to EXPO and bi-monthly departments to Credit Union Business. Other trade magazine credits include Convene, Incentives & Meetings International, Motivation Strategies, American City & County, Waste Age, Pizza Today and South Central Construction.

Nancy Mann Jackson’s Specialties:

I write about a wide variety of topics, but specialties include small business, parenting, food, sustainability, health and travel.

My work spans several types of media including consumer magazines, trade magazines, custom magazines, web writing and corporate communications.
