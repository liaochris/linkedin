kiji mccafferty’s Summary

Designer and Illustrator, strong background in both corporate and boutique design environments, ability to provide both creative and technical skill sets to a variety of clients and markets.

As an illustrator I am particularly adept at a wide range of styles and mediums. With such a diverse aesthetic background its not surprising the range of media its been applied to: graphic apparel, repeat patterns, outdoor ad campaigns, online, editorial and animation.

As an artist, Mr. Kiji has studied traditional Tibetan Thangka Painting in Nepal which he has used as the basis for his approach in his ongoing large scale mural work, most recently for the Electric Windows event in Beacon New York and 2007's Miami Art Basel.

kiji mccafferty’s Specialties:

Brand and Identity development, Graphic Design, Illustration, 2-D and 3-D Drafting, Design Management and coordination of vendors and web developers, production design techniques and production sourcing network.
