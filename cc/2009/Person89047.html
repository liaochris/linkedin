Sayan Ghosh’s Summary

Sayan is working as a Manager with Deloitte and Touche Consulting India. He takes up a techno-functional role with responsibilities spanning across Project delivery, development/design/architecture, Practice Development, Business Development and Knowledge Management. He has exposure to a number of technologies like ASP.NET, Microsoft .NET, Winforms, SQL Server, C#, Microsoft Office+VSTA+VSTO solutions, ASP, VB, VB.Net, VSTF, XSL/XML, JavaScript, CSS, HTML/DHTML, Crystal Reports, Oracle Warehouse Builder, Oracle database, PL/SQL etc. He holds interests in domains of Enterprise Architecture, Architecture frameworks, Technology Strategy Advisory, Cloud Computing and Enterprise SOA.

Earlier he was working as a Software Development Engineer with Application Services Management group, Microsoft IT, India.He acted as a Development Lead/Point of Contact for an 8 member team. Working on Design, Architecture and Development. Handling build schedules, feature allocations and communication with others teams. Drove several initiatives to upgrade the legacy code, implement multi-layered architecture, security/performance standards compliance, database upgrade, build solid documentation base of existing features.Worked on implementing a number of standard processes to align to the organizational directives, like VSTF adoption, Scrum, SDLC, Code review process etc. Handled the role of business analyst for some time for key features. Participated in the interview/selection/ramping up of vendor resources for the project from key partner organizations.

Previously, he was working as a Consultant in the Business Intelligence group in Systems Consultancy and technical projects in Technology Advisory Services group, PwC India, with a specific focus on Data Warehousing and Microsoft Technology based Web-Development. He worked in domains like Tax services, International Assignment Services, Telecom, Real Estate, Legal Partnerships and HR Outsourcing.
