Chris Flaherty’s Summary

Describing my role, my focus is project development, requirements capture, formulation and integration of the project team. As well, I follow this through to project delivery and stakeholder management, including decision-making issues. Key Programme Management skill areas:
- Requirements analysis and project integration.
- Business case development and analysis.
- Cost/benefit analysis.
- Managing programmes.
I am a PRINCE 2 practitioner in project management.
I am an Associate Member (U.K. Association for Project Management); and a Registered Project Manager (Australian Institute of Project Management).

RISK & VULNERABILITY ANALYSIS
Since 2000 I have also established an international career in vulnerability and resilience analysis. I am a commentator on security, defense and counter terrorism research, its application, and project management.
I have pioneered concepts such as 3D Tactics, Fragmentation and Weaponisation of Buildings. I have developed a counter terrorism building vulnerability analysis for mass gathering commercial, industrial and shopping areas. As well, I give critical infrastructure protection policy advice.
I frequently publish in this topic and regularly speak at conferences. In the past I was a writer for the Australian Security Magazine. I comment on the evolving critical infrastructure legislative environment from a commercial view.

Chris Flaherty’s Specialties:

1. Programme/Project Manager, Requirements Analysis and project integration.
2. Vulnerability and resilience analysis.
