Cedar Lee’s Summary

I've been a painter all my life. I studied art at Goucher College in Baltimore, MD, graduating in 2005 with highest honors. I sold my first painting at age 12 and now have work in private collections throughout the world.
I've worked as an art instructor at Anne Arundel Community College, and I'm involved in the Baltimore, MD art scene. My work has been featured in solo and group exhibitions in galleries, arts centers, and alternative venues.
I am currently represented by art galleries in several U.S. states. I operate my art studio from home, where I paint vivid, dramatic landscapes, colorful flowers, and portraits by commission.
In addition to painting, I'm also skilled in jewelry design, photography and pottery.
My primary goals are to continue growing my business, my art skills, and my collector base.
