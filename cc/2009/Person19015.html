Anton Chuvakin’s Summary

Dr Anton Chuvakin, GCIH, GCFA (http://www.chuvakin.org) is a recognized security expert and book author.

A frequent conference speaker, he also represents a company at various security meetings and standard organizations. He is an author of a book "Security Warrior" (http://www.securitywarrior.org) and a contributor to "Know Your Enemy II", "Information Security Management Handbook", "Critical Threads 2006", “Hacker’s Challenge 3”, "PCI Compliance," "OSSEC Host-Based Intrusion Detection Guide" - others are in the making. :-)

Anton also published numerous papers on a broad range of security subjects, covering intrusion detection, incident response, log management, correlation, forensics, vulnerability management, honeypots and many others. In his spare time he maintains his security portal http://www.info-secure.org and a few blogs, including http://www.securitywarrior.org.

Anton Chuvakin’s Specialties:

Computer forensics, intrusion detection, security information management, log analysis, log correlation, log management, security standards - CVSS, OVAL, AVDL, CEE, OSSTMM, incident response, Unix and Linux security, honeypots, honeynets, security policy and management, writing on security, future security technologies
