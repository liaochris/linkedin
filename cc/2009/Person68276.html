Mihai Coman’s Summary

Experience

Close to 10 years of real world, hands on experience designing & developing a wide array of software solutions ranging from real-time embedded systems & Windows kernel drivers to Internet centric ERP, CMS & distributed enterprise systems.

I never believed in catching/keeping up with tehnology & IT trends - they're too slow; there's a long track of way ahead of their time solutions I worked on to prove this.

While it may come as a complete surprise, I'm still a bit behind with web trends so no web 9.0 applications here. Buzzwords are not my thing but I can deal just fine with the technology behind them when needed to deliver real solutions to real problems.

Started H&C Consulting in late 2007. H&C's goal is to support innovators, leaders, managers and visionaries in their endeavors to succeed by helping them make intelligent decisions about online strategy, communications and brand.

Far from being a departure from my software development 'roots', H&C still provides software development services while leveraging our comprehensive web/online media know-how as part of our 'complete client experience'.
