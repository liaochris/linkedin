Mollye Barrett’s Summary

Mollye Barrett is a business development executive with a focus on content management and technical communication consulting. She develops strategies for optimizing content, improving the authoring environment and planning for business continuity. Mollye presents vendor-neutral content management options that focus on business case, ROI, content reuse, document analysis, information modeling and architecture, workflow analysis, translation, single-source writing, file format handling and document conversions.

Mollye believes relationships are greatest form of influence. She is a working leader that guides both strategically and tactically. Enjoys working with diverse, dynamic teams and maintaining a focus on emerging technologies.

Mollye Barrett’s Specialties:

business development, XML content management, technical publication management, technical communication, technical writer, editor, communication strategy development, business continuity planning, audience analysis, process owner, single source publishing and authoring, foreign language translation process, offshore production, document conversion
