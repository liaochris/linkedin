Bob Jeffers’s Summary

Bob Jeffers, Founder & CEO Allen-Jeffers Associates, Inc.
I am seeking to connect with those involved directly or indirectly in the medical device or pharmaceutical industries. Feel free to contact me.

Unique for most recruiters in the industry, we have hands-on experience in both the medical device and pharmaceutical industries. I personally have held positions as Superintendent of Manufacturing, Production Manager, Plant Manager as well as executive positions in both Production Control and Inventory Control. I have a technical undergrad and have had prior experience as a microbiologist and quality control chemist in my early years upon graduation.

Industry-wide experience and contacts have assisted my firm to be successful over the long haul. Few firms have had the longevity and success that we have enjoyed since 1978. We treat our candidates as the professionals they are. We treat our clients with the respect and sense of urgency they expect with an eye to service.

All contacts are kept completely confidential. Please feel free to contact me if you think that we can be of any assistance in your career or broadening your business contacts.
