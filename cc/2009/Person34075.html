Frank Warren’s Summary

Frank Warren graduated from Boston College in 2007 with a B.A. in Economics. Shortly following graduation, Frank began working as a staff accountant at La Jolla Bank, a privately held savings association based out of Rancho Santa Fe, CA.

Frank applied his analytical skills to solve complex problems for La Jolla Bank. These included daily balancing of all La Jolla Bank branches and generating macro-level reports to be used by the Controller as well as the Chief Financial Officer. Frank lead a team with the goal to increase the technological products offered by La Jolla Bank, specifically working with Remote Deposit Capture.

Frank is currently an MBA candidate at Pepperdine's Graziadio School of Business and Management. In the future Frank hopes to continue his career in finance in either the fields of banking or technology.
