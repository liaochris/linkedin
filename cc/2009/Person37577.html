Greg Hollingsworth’s Summary

I have been working in Marketing for coming up on three years with extensive knowledge of social media/marketing (although I am far from an expert). My focus is primarily in direct online marketing with as much social media marketing thrown in as I can swing. I have copious experience running online advertising campaigns (as well as finding interesting and unique ways to modify them in bulk).

My goals are very simple, to do the very best job I can wherever I happen to be and to provide my family with everything needed to enjoy a good life here in the heartland of America.

In addition to my professional experience, I run the blog Devil In The Details and write a weekly column for How to Split an Atom.
