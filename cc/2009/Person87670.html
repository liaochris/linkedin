Sabine Kurjo McNeill’s Summary

I studied mathematics and programming and had my first job as a systems analyst at CERN, the European Centre for Nuclear Research in Geneva.

After a serious car accident I was told that my pain was 'psychological'. Hence I studied psychology and ended up organising international conferences and seminars.

By chance I came across an article on prime numbers which triggered my thinking into what has become my life's work: to develop web portals based on my prototype software, which open the gates to hardware and measuring devices in harmony with light, nature and life.

Sabine Kurjo McNeill’s Specialties:

New software methods for analysing digital images, layering multi-dimensional data of any complex system and 'measuring on-screen'.
