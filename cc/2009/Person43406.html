Janet Barclay’s Summary

I established Organized Assistant in order to share my natural organizing abilities and the many computer and administrative skills I have gained throughout my life.

My career has taken me into offices across several sectors, including Manufacturing, Financial Services, Social Services, and the Ontario Government. Committed to lifelong learning, I continually upgrade my skills through self-study and by participating in seminars and conferences. I hold a Bachelor of Arts degree with Specialized Honours in French and an Advanced Career Consultant Certificate, and am certified as a Master Virtual Assistant.

Janet Barclay’s Specialties:

Specialize in supporting professional organizers, career coaches and resume writers with the design of websites, newsletters, flyers, and other marketing materials; proofreading and editing articles, ebooks and website content; Internet research to support book or article writing, business or marketing plans, and other projects; and development of PowerPoint presentations and training materials.
