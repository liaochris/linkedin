Jim Risner’s Summary

Votigo, Inc. is the leading social media technology and service provider enabling businesses to better engage and communicate with their customers through the use of user-generated video & photo contests, branded social networks, and social network applications.

We help Fortune 500 companies, advertising agencies, PR firms, publishers, media companies and brands including Victoria’s Secret PINK, KOHL's, Sharpie, Gardenburger, ConocoPhillips, Pandora, and The Motley Fool navigate the social web and exceed their marketing goals through our suite of social media solutions.
