  • President

    Accelerate Inc.

    (Privately Held; Management Consulting industry)

    2006 — Present (3 years)

    Accelerate is a management consulting company that helps our clients accelerate their business performance by providing business development expertise - primarily in the pursuit, capture, and proposal phases.

    Accelerate accomplishes this via our consultative approach, operational experience, approach, internal policies, and our unique ability to provide nationally-respected leaders / hands-on experts (Accelerators).

    Our experience base as a company is extensive, and in addition, our Accelerators are selected for their extensive accomplishments prior to joining Accelerate - we select the best from among a very large talent base, and work hard to provide our clients with their right fit. A typical Accelerator is a former executive with business unit, business development, and program management experience, 30 years experience, possessing advanced degrees, and above all - significant real-world accomplishments.

    Accelerate serves Fortune 500 companies, as well as medium, small, and start-up companies.

    Our Public Sector expertise includes:
    -> Companies bidding to US federal, state, and local agencies
    -> US firms bidding to international agencies
    -> International entities bidding to US agencies

    Our Private Sector expertise includes:
    -> Tier 1 / Tier 2 business development between companies
    -> Teaming
    -> Selling to large companies
