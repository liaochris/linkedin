Kellie Powell’s Summary

Kellie Powell is a playwright, poet, writer, and theatre artist. She has written more than twenty plays, many of which are available for purchase from These Aren't My Shoes Productions (notmyshoes.net). Her plays have been produced by Illinois State University Free Stage, the Penny Dreadful Players, Studio Z, Hinman Production Company, and Asphalt Jungle.

Her poetry has been published in ESC! Magazine, Word Riot, Pens on Fire, Flask and Pen, Euphemism, Ugly Poets, Beautiful Poems, Poetry Cemetery, and Brome & Beyond. Her non-fiction credits include the Feminist Review, After Ellen, the Daily Vidette, Liberal Candy, and Associated Content.

When Kellie isn't creating theatre or some other form of art, she's usually ranting about politics. She is the Executive Production Director for These Aren't My Shoes Productions and she maintains The Monologue Database.
