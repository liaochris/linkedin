Rich Cruz’s Summary

Behold! Signs is a full-service sign company owned and operated by Rich Cruz and Lisa Banos-Cruz who are each seasoned artists, educators, and marketing specialists!

Our vision: Driven by an innate need to help others, our vision is to be the ultimate visual communications resource for small businesses, schools, universities, churches, and non-profit organizations, helping them communicate their message to their target audience through creative, artful, and oftentimes whimsical graphics.

We are NOT the mom-and-pop sign shop around the corner that simply clerks your orders. We specialize in creative, consultative, and collaborative service utilizing years of results-driven experience and education in the graphics and marketing realm to aid our clients in achieving their vision. If you want to be "taken care of", come and see us... We promise outstanding service, delivery when you need it, and superior quality meeting or exceeding your expectations each and every time!

We serve education institutions, non-profit organizations, retail stores, wholesale outfits, warehouse facilities, vehicle fleets, arts organizations, museums, and event planners. We provide: indoor/outdoor signs, illuminated/non-illuminated signs, full-color large-format prints, banners, real estate signs, retail signs, trade show exhibits, vehicle graphics, and a huge gamut of other sign solutions.

Over 30 years combined experience in the sign making and graphic design industry, over 10 years in sales and marketing, and a career built upon consulting and education allows Behold! Signs to stand out from the run-of-the-mill sign shops. We are a full-service sign COMPANY, with Big Business power and Small Business personality!

Behold! Your Vision Realized in Full Color!

Remember: A business with no sign, is a sign of no business!
