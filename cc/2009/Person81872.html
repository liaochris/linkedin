Michael Dale’s Summary

I am currently the business owner of Rampage Consulting, a technology consulting, web site design and development business. I was previously employed by Anoka-Metro Regional Treatment Center (MN Dept. of Human Services) as a Systems Analyst Unit Supervisor. Due to budget cuts within the Department of Human Services, management positions were eliminated which included my position. My responsibilities included duties involved with managing and maintaining all aspects of the Information Technology Department with a strong emphasis on Intranet Development, departmental management, security and training. Prior to this, I was employed by Precision Associates, Inc., a manufacturer of specialized O-Rings and Seals. In all, I have approximately 15 years of experience in the Information Technology field. I am a self-taught, self-starter with an affinity towards resourcefulness and creativity. I am committed to providing the best possible service in a collaborative environment.
