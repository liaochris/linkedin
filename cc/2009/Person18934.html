Chuck Clark’s Experience

  • Technologist

    Pulse Energy

    (Privately Held; Renewables & Environment industry)

    January 2009 — Present (11 months)

    Pulse Energy Management Software communicates with a large number of building automation and metering systems to collect, archive, analyze and communicate energy information. The system helps building owners and operators quickly identify and resolve inefficient energy use, helps occupants learn how their buildings perform and ultimately helps to save money and reduce emissions.

    From a technical perspective this entails integrating with existing meters and building automation systems to collect and send real-time meter data to the archival and analytics engine we've written. Looking at past building performance and taking into consideration existing factors such as temperature, time of year and wind speed and direction we are able to predict how a building should be performing and alert operators when certain thresholds of comfort or energy consumption are surpassed. We can visualize the historical data and predictions in many different ways for different audiences including building occupants, facility managers and executives focused on sustainability and reducing energy costs.
