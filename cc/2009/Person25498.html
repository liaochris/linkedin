Bob DeCecco, CPA’s Summary

I am known as a passionate, driven and competitive entrepreneur with skills as diverse as my experiences. I am driven by a deep desire to be known as the subject matter expert of whatever business or industry I am involved in. A strong work ethic keeps me on the cutting edge and provides the opportunity to make a positive impact in my life and the lives of others around me.

Prior to founding theTRAFFICplan, I acquired a Mortgage Bank which was about to enter into bankrutpcy; Aclarian Mortgage. After facilitating a turn-around and growing operations, Aclarian was acquired by a publicly traded REIT ~ Opteum Inc. (formerly known as OPX: NYSE), now known as Opteum Mortgage.

Before that, I held the position of CFO for a BPM software company, Q-Link Tech. Co-lead an effort to raise $8mm in VC financing sustaining company efforts until an eventual sale to Adobe Systems for $20mm; returning all capital invested with a deserving ROI to investors and founders.

As a CPA with PricewaterhouseCoopers I have participated in the following IPOs and Secondary Market Offerings:

- Microfinancial Leasing (MFI: NYSE)
- Switchboard.com (SWBD: No longer listed)
- Affiliated Managers Group (AMG: NYSE); secondary offering

In early 2004, I helped co-found an Alliance within our regional business community leading to the early stages of a new Chamber of Commerce (www.lwrba.org)

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Internet industry)

    July 2007 — Present (2 years 1 month)

    Typed-In is an internet media company focused on helping businesses of all size increase sales by taking advantage of the shift in users approach from print media to local online search. Through the acquisition and development of its geo-targeted domain network, these domains, once acquired by Typed-In, are subsequently monetized by bridging the advertiser to the local consumer.

    A geo-targeted domain name answers the question of what (product/service) & where (location); for example, consumers searching on the internet for a particular product or service in their local market may type the following in their browser; LAWYERPORTLAND.COM, DIVORCEATTORNEYBOSTON.COM, PLUMBERLASVEGAS.COM, DOCTORSANFRANCISCO.COM, etc.

    Consumers searching locally are a very high quality lead for a business, typically converting 250% more often than conventional leads for a fraction of the cost of traditional advertising.
