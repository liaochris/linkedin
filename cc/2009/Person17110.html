Charles Kelly’s Summary

I am a hard working, experienced technology professional with over 20 years of experience in corporate and government / intelligence environments. I've been a leader in the technology professionals community with a strong network of colleagues and friends. I have vast experience in building and growing technical communities and programs.

In Washington, DC, I was a Microsoft Regional Director, I started and lead the 33,000 member NT*Pro (Association of Windows NT Professionals) and I worked as a Computer Systems Analyst at the National Science Foundation.
