Corsano Otino’s Summary

Otino Corsano describes his visual art practice as neo-conceptualist, new genre work. A graduate of Otis College of Art (Los Angeles), Corsano approaches painting as a trope. His art writings and artist interviews are published in ArtUS magazine and can also be found online at artpost.info. Corsano has taught at the Univesity of Toronto and the Ontario College of Art

Corsano Otino’s Specialties:

New Genres, Conceptual Art, Installation, Painting tropes, New Media, Video, Artist Interviews, Storyboards, Art Writing, Art Criticism.

Corsano Otino’s Interests:
