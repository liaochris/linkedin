Michael Szpilzinger’s Summary

As my career has grown, I have realized that my calling has always been to help people. It is what drives me and fuels my passion.

I love meeting people and connecting on a deep level. I aspire to be a great connector and have joined the efforts of FrumNetwork to work towards that goal.

I have a strong background in technology solutions and training. I attribute my success to my sincerity and my ability to deconstruct complex things into more simple ones for the benefit of others.

I have a passion for effective communication and conversation.

My goal is to reach out to businesses through my blog and PR service site, to help them serve their customers in a meaningful and engaging way, keeping them coming back for more.

My wife/partner, Dena, is a professional graphic designer with 10 years of experience. Together, we provide a rich set of materials and expertise to help small businesses communicate with their markets.

I see myself being a valuable asset to businesses who want a more human side to their marketing and create an engaging brand.

I believe in building online communities and have started building one myself (MyNachas.com).
