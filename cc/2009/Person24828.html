David Ziembicki Jr.’s Summary

Deep technical expertise in many of Microsoft’s infrastructure technologies in the areas of virtualization, security, high availability, storage, directory services, database services, collaboration services, and service oriented architecture (SOA).

An infrastructure architect with over 10 years experience in large architecture, design, and migration projects at Federal agencies and Fortune 500 companies.

Broad knowledge of Microsoft’s strategies and directions across a multitude of areas including datacenter design, dynamic systems, cloud computing, Software+Services, and service oriented architecture.

Recognized Microsoft expert in datacenter virtualization and consolidation and a key contributor to Microsoft’s worldwide server virtualization offerings.

Founding member of the Microsoft Certified Architect program, the highest level of certification offered by Microsoft.
