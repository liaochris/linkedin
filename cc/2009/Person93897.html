  • Strategic Planner

    Duval Guillaume

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    August 2003 — November 2008 (5 years 4 months)

    From August 2003, my collaboration with Duval Guillaume got a more structural form. I started for half a year in the agency of Duval Guillaume Brussels, moved to Duval Guillaume Dialog, and little by little started to work as an integrated planner, over the edges of different disciplines like e-, coprorate, advertising, dm and PR. Finally I arrived at the integrated agency DG Antwerp.

    My job has always been including two essential steps in 'the creative work before the creative work':
    In the first place, the focus was always the big picture: what's the big brand idea? what makes it relevant for the people?
    Meaning-making was the key.
    After that we asked ourselves 'how will we tell the people and engage them?
    Here, channel planning was key.

    I worked for various brands in various industries such as Belgacom, CenterParcs, Dexia, Axa, Herta, Douwe Egberts, Senseo, Steelforce, LeasePlan, Miele, Weight Watchers, Triodos, Vredeseilanden Carlsberg and many more
