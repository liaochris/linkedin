Deb Walsh’s Summary

I have spent my career helping to build brands and audiences, develop artists, and generate sales and attention for a wide range of people, products and events. My current and primary focus is working with creative organizations, artist, filmmakers, musicians and arts organizations. My professional background includes positions at a leading national public relations agency, multiple consultative relationships with regional arts and entertainment organizations, and at a national non-profit organization. At root, I am an idea generator and a connector who believes that when we are passionate about who we work with and the ideas we are advancing, great things happen.

You can get a look at how and what I think about over at my blog, http://www.onecoolfreeidea.com

Deb Walsh’s Specialties:

Brand development, event production, publicity, strategy for people and projects in the entertainment industry, music, film, arts and arts organizations.
