John Mazzara’s Summary

I own three companies that are vertically integrated in the areas of financial services.

I have successfully sold real estate since 1986. I sell residential and commercial properties and I am affiliated with RE/MAX Associates Plus as a broker associate. I have been an owner and manager of rental real estate since 1986.

I became a CFP-Certified Financial Planner in 1989 and built upon that education with my other degrees and certifications. I have been securities registered since 1993. My broker dealer is Multi-Financial Securities Corporation, which is owned by ING. Neither Multi Financial Securities Corporation nor ING have any affiliation with any of the other services I provide. I am series 7, 63, and 65 registered. All securities are through Multi-Financial Securities Corporation, member FINRA/SIPC, 7701 France Ave S, Suite 200, Edina, MN 55435, (952) 470-1660. Mutual funds, annuities, stocks and other investments available through Multi-Financial Securities are not insured by the FINRA, SIPC nor are they obligations of or guaranteed by Multi-Financial Securities or any other affiliated entity. Such investments will fluctuate in value and are subject to investment risks including loss of some or all of the principal. Financial Planning Associates and Venture Development Inc. and RE/MAX Associates Plus are not affiliated with Multi-Financial Securities Corporation.

In 1995 I started Venture Development. We are Minnesota based mortgage broker. We like to say "we are Minnesota's Premier Mortgage Broker". My wife Patti runs the company with me and is our Vice President.

I have the following financial designations: CFP CLU CHFC CEBS MBA MS. While this sounds like a lot of alphabet soup, it represents a lot of hours devoted to becoming better at what I do. My goal is to be the best, by becoming more educated I am able to add more value to my clients.

By being client focused and helping others succeed, I have become successful.
