Neil Walker’s Summary

An accomplished, results-driven programme manager and project manager with over 20 years experience in driving change and delivering business benefit within high pressure, commercial environments, specifically banking and financial services.

Skilled at directing and executing complex, multi-disciplinary programmes and projects. With a proven track record for delivering change programme success, ranging from strategic initiatives through to the implementation of advanced process improvements and enterprise-wide solutions.

An outstanding relationship builder. Operated credibly at executive level and effectively manage diverse stakeholder groups and third parties. With an inherent ability to manage and lead successful, cohesive teams to drive forward business transformation and deliver value.

BACKGROUND:

Neil worked primarily in the Financial Services sector (23 years). 10 years with NatWest Bank followed by diverse technical enablement and management roles with retail banks, investment banks and other financial services firms.

At NatWest, he worked for 6 years across retail and corporate business groups (achieving rapid progression as a select member of the Bank’s Management Development Programme). Head hunted into the Bank’s IT Group in a consulting role (as an “IT business partner working with business stakeholders to shape the demand for effective banking solutions”. A role that has featured throughout his career).

Neil is actively searching for challenging interim management opportunities with a great company.
