Michael Lara’s Summary

A native of Newark, New Jersey, Michael Lara, is a people-centric leader in Specialty Advertising, with a unique, military-leadership background. After graduating from Bloomfield High School, Michael joined the United States Army. While there, he earned highly-respected medals and was awarded the rank of Staff Sergeant. Later assigned to the “War on Drugs Campaign” He served as the leader for a drug-suppression team, he worked on covert missions for the 3rd Region Criminal Investigation Detachment Command in Germany and received several U.S. and foreign awards for his law enforcement and leadership excellence.
While leading troops through combat zones in the Gulf, Somalia, and Haiti, Michael and his squad were awarded the Army Expeditionary Medal which at the time had not been awarded to soldiers since the Vietnam era. Subsequently, he was chosen to lead a special protective combat team ensuring the security of the U.S. Army Liaison assigned by Washington, D.C. and the Pentagon during Operation Uphold Democracy. After receiving the “Silver Star Medal for Bravery” from the American Police Hall of Fame,Michael was assigned as an instructor of Close Quarters Battle (CQB) Tactics. Here, he trained S.W.A.T teams from all over the country in preparation for the 1996 Olympics in Atlanta, Georgia. Michael then worked in civilian law enforcement as a Deputy Sheriff and Police Officer. And after graduating from Alabama State Troopers “Night Hawks” S.W.A.T. School, he was awarded the coveted, “Night Hawks” designation and became the Special Weapons and Tactics (SWAT) Training Officer for his department.
As the President of Brandango Incorporated, Michael currently uses his discipline and strategy experience to help business customers implement an effective promotional marketing strategy using promotional products,and strategic solutions they need quickly and cost-effectively. He continues to enjoy his philanthropic approach with his company, and remains humble at heart.
