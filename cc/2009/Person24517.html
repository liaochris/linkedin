Eric Davidove’s Summary

I've spent my entire professional career inventing learning programmes that have delivered great business benefits, and leading teams to develop and deliver them. I introduced social learning to BT and helped create Dare2Share, an award winning and very successful platform for learning, knowledge management and collaboration that has grown to become a standard offering for Accenture. I am dedicated to helping business leaders reduce the time to competence for their critical workforces, extract more value from informal learning and learning technologies, drive efficient learning operations, and enable more “user generated” content development.

My core competencies include business case development, project and programme management, business and systems integration, process design, organizational change, learning and development, social media, operational management, commercial and vendor management, measurement and evaluation, and change management.

I have worked across many industries and geographies, with diverse teams, with proven results.

I worked for Accenture from May 1987 until June 2009. I had been a Senior Executive since 1998.

Eric Davidove’s Specialties:

Extracting value from informal learning, deploying social media, improving learning operations, and aligning learning to business value.
