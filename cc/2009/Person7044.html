    January 2007 — November 2008 (1 year 11 months)

    My job at ProduWeb is quite wide.
    In the first place I meet customers - existing or potential ones - and find out with them what are their needs in terms of e-business, e-marketing and e-commerce.
    Then, I set up offers for them, which I hope meet their needs. When contracts are signed, I'm in charge of the project's management.
