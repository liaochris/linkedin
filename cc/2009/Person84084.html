Ric McLaughlin’s Summary

Senior technology manager with a history of leading dynamic teams that deliver successful projects creating effective websites. Proven successful in simultaneously executing strategic plans and proactively improving operations. Expert at conceiving and delivering technical solutions to complex problems using refined consulting skills – initiated 17 successful website projects. Masterful at leveraging shrink-wrap software development techniques, certified technical skills and real world technical know-how to build robust software – architected 7 websites with 3 achieving 99.99% uptime. Skilled at building dynamic web development teams – directed 6 web development teams that each delivered 4 or more successful projects.

Publisher of http://www.websitehoncho.com - a “how to” site for professional website managers.
