Dwayne Alexander’s Summary

The Alexander Companies are communications & media specialists. Our clients benefit from our full service and integrated approach to traditional & online PR.

1. Alexander Communications
2. Goals Inc.

Alexander Communications is an established and fast growing full service PR company.

Alexander Communications has a specialist Online PR capability- and now operates in the space where the confluence of seo, sem, traditional pr, social networking & other web 2.0 innovations lives.

What is Online PR?
• Create spikes of visibility and builds up brand equity
• Fosters debate, conversation creates “word of mouth” buzz
• Generates advocacy and builds reputation
• Makes it easier for journalists researching stories to find you
• Expands company branding footprint on the web
• Contributes to proactive search results and reputation management
• Helps fuel the sales pipeline

Goals Inc. is a media owner with established content deals with major NZ media owners, specialises in technology and web related change management projects, interviews goalgetters through Livemygoals.com, the nzherald.co.nz business blog called "Inspired People"

The purpose of the content is to share insights into how to create action around your important personal and business goals. We do this through our featured interviews with business leaders and other goalgetters.

A former CEO of one of APN News & Media Ltd’s online companies, Dwayne introduced the Online component into the consultancy to help current Alexander Communications clients & other New Zealand companies manage their online profile & reputation.

Dwayne has 18 years experience in media and marketing & a post graduate Honors Degree in Marketing (cum laude) and is also a director of Goals Inc. Ltd.

Married with two daughters, Dwayne spends his downtime kayaking,playing the guitar & building a library of inspirational business and life stories.
