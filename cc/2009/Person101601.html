Uel J. Readioff’s Summary

As a self-motivated and highly organised team player, my focus is always to see each project I undertake to an expected end. My career has been in Retail Sales and selling with over 25 years in Retail management.

I am passionate about my customers, how you sell and market yourselves to them, how your image and brand is perceived by your end users. I focus my skills around helping people improve their Sales and Selling skills, improving their Staff Training, maximise their Staffs personal Development. Delivering Customer Service training that is on the shop floor working in live environments with your team when trade is busy, not class orientated taking your staff away from crucial selling opportunities, giving your staff real experiences rather than mock scenarios.

I make no excuse for my energy, passion and enthusiasm for this either.

Our key focus is to generate Sales & Marketing leads and constantly improving Sales.
