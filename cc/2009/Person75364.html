Aad (Adrian) Offerman’s Summary

    Adrian Offerman (1969) has been working as an ICT specialist journalist, technology writer, magazine maker and New Media specialist in the Dutch ICT market for over ten years now. He was the editor-in-chief of Chief Information Officer, Linux News, Mnet, Windows & Netwerken, and Computer-Telefonie, and was a contributing editor for CIO, Supply Chain Magazine, C-sharp, NetworkWorld, and Network Computing.
    At this moment he is a contributing editor for Computable, CRN, InfoWorld.nl, TechWorld.nl, Energy Magazine, and Noodzaak.

    Adrian has a Master's Degree in Computer Engineering from Delft University of Technology, where he specialized in Computer Architecture and Digital Technique. His thesis project resulted in three international scientific publications.
    Next year he hopes to obtain a Master's Degree in Psychology from Leiden University, where he specializes in theoretical and clinical psychology.
