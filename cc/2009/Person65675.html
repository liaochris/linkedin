Max Guernsey’s Summary

"Software exists to make business happen; not the other way around."

I am a business-minded software development professional with over a decade of experience in software development, design, testing, analysis, and deployment. I have demonstrated strong leadership skills time and again.

My focus is on building strong software development teams that:
* Have little waste in their software development process
* Deliver quickly while preserving sustainability
* Make products of superlative quality
* Innovate while focusing on narrowing the gap between business and technology

I am an expert in:
* TDD (unit-test driven development)
* ATDD (acceptance-test driven development)
* Design Patterns/OOP/OOD
* Agile middle-tier development
* Agile database development
* Scrum

I am experienced in:
* Agile front-end development
* Lean Thinking
* Agile analysis and planning

I am a published technical author and an aspiring fiction writer.
