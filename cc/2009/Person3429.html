Alexis Lamonja’s Summary

I am a consultant with over ten years of experience working with networks on various platforms. I have proven my skills by performing mandates in the architecture, analysis, migration and evolution of networks. I have long experience implementing and administering network security structures.
I had mandates in the public and private sectors, working within environments with several network operating systems, on both servers and workstations. I am able to work with wide and complex network architectures.
My knowledge of scripting allows me to automate network tasks efficiently, and that in a very large environment. I have excellent experience with Windows 2003 migrations, and I am also adept at implementing development and quality-control environments on this platform.
