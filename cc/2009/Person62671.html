Maria Lucia Uribe Torres’s Summary

I am a highly motivated social development practitioner with experience working on ethics education and interfaith learning. Nine years of international experience working on leadership development for children and young people. Experience working on the development of educational materials based on competencies and ethical values that can foster a culture of peace and the ability in children to learn how to live together. Able to develop trainings and materials on cultural understanding and interfaith learning for children, young people and adults.

I also have experience in fundraising, social research, project development and partnership building with government, non-government and private sectors.

I see myself working on the development of new learning and educational methodologies for children and young people and implementing new models of quality education mainly in developing countries.
