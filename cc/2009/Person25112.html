Danielle Delgado’s Summary

Having transitioned out of a career as an actress, I am seeking more freelance projects for my burgeoning photographic pursuits, as I narrow down more precisely just what kind of business I'd like to be manifesting by the end of 2009. I am also interested in more assignments requiring some travel.
