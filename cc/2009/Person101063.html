Trond Bugge’s Summary

A Master in Marketing graduate, strategic thinker, engaging in person, persuasive in writing with a demonstrated proficiency of unstructured business development.

Experienced entrepreneur with new leadership practice, solid background from investor relations, and fresh visions on where the Mobile Internet and Advertising Industry is heading.

Pre-millennium Futurist with four years of Board / CEO level facilitation of Scenario-based Strategy and Market Innovation processses.

Sold out to focus on the Mobile Entertainment industry February 2001.
