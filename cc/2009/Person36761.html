Lynn Johnson’s Summary

Lynn is a creative entrepreneur, theater artist, teacher & facilitator with almost 20 years experience creating original theater with children, youth and adults of all ages.

Lynn has worked as an actor, director, teacher, administrator, trainer, and organizational consultant to youth and arts organizations throughout the United States.

In 2006, Lynn, along with her partner in life and business, Allison Kenny, established Glitter & Razz Productions, a premium provider of collaborative, dramatic, and magical birthday parties and learning events for creative kids.

Lynn Johnson’s Specialties:

Small business management, arts education programming & curriculum design, dynamic training and teaching, non-profit capacity building, diversity consulting, leadership and staff coaching, project management, public speaking, reflective listening, and strong writing

Lynn Johnson’s Experience

  • Co-Director

    OutLook Theater Project

    (Performing Arts industry)

    August 2007 — Present (2 years 4 months)

    I am the co-founder/director (along with Rebecca Schultz) of OutLook Theater Project, a newly formed, ensemble-based company that creates original performance about relevant issues from a queer perspective. We collaborate with a diversity of communities to make work that questions the norm, transforms audiences into participants, and inspires personal and social change. OLTP achieves its mission through a blend of professional theater and community-based projects. We draw from improvisational and physical theater, Theatre of the Oppressed, and other techniques to create works that address social issues and empower participants and audiences.
