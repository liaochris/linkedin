Teresa Dominici’s Summary

Born in Tanzania (East Africa), by both Italian parents, lived in Tanzania and Italy and currently I reside in San Diego, California.

My interest in art and drawing talent started as far back as a child, and I became seriously involved year 2006. I work primarily in acrylic paints and occasionally with oil and pastel..

I am a self-taught artist and a writer, and I have commissioned work. Creativity is my passion, whether I be sketching and painting, writing or creating websites.

I get my inspiration from places I have been through my travels all over the world from subjects from the many photographs and drawings taken through my journey.

I am Interested in new business opportunities either through my artwork or office, networking with art museum and art gallery, artists and art collectors,to exhibits local and internationally.

I am fluent in Italian, English, Swahili and basic Spanish.

I look forward in connecting with you.
