Kathleen DeLuca, CSEP, CMP’s Summary

I have been producing special events, meetings and conference for more than 25 years. Prior to launching Events Plus, Inc., I held positions with The Franklin Institute Science Museum, Occidental Chemical Corporation, and Rainbow Balloons, where I produced hot air balloon festivals.

I have a diverse business background in corporate and non-profit organizations along with two industry designations.
I serve on two industry boards and, with the assistance of a dedicated and talented staff, continue to expand Events Plus, Inc services.

In August 2008, I launched Fido's Festival USA™ a two day dog centric festival, which is trademarked and will expand in various locations throughout the United States.

My commitment to service, attention to detail, and reputation for excellence drives me to strive for the best and most innovative, cost effective results for every client.
