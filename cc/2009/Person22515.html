I am currently seeking full-time employment as consultant, analyst, or project manager in the environmental, analytical chemistry or air quality fields.

Environmental engineer and scientist, multi-disciplinary project manager, with rich expertise in fate and transport of pollutants, analytical chemistry, and environmental technologies. Detail oriented, applying analytical and leadership skills to serve clients needs.

Certified: ISO-17025 Lead Assessor (2008), ISO-17025 Technical Assessor (2004), EIT - Fundamentals of Engineering (1994)

* Directing and training teams to work independently, think critically, and produce quality results.
* Extensive expertise in developing and implementing methods, QA/QC, validation, sampling, monitoring, and advanced models.

* Academic lecturer and author of articles; 20 years expertise in presentations, performance & TV.
* US citizen, native English speaker. Excellent writing, communication, interpersonal skills.
