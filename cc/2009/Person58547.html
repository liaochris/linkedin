Tom Watson III’s Summary

I have been a student in both the Fine Arts and Graphic Design fields of study, completing a Bachelor’s degree from The University of Dayton in Graphic Design. I have also earned an Associate’s degree from Sinclair College in Graphic Design.

I have had a variety of experiences outside of the classroom.
I have been published in a number of independent magazines, organized and participated in group art exhibits, and I have been the official website photographer for a Dayton live music venue/night club, as well as other interesting accomplishments.

My post-college work has included identity design for charitable organizations. I designed all the publicity materials for a number of benefit events, and have done various other freelance work. Because of these and many other life experiences, I am a well-rounded individual who can be an asset to your company. I will also benefit by joining your company, where I will gain further valuable experience.
