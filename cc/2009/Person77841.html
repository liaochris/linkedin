Paul Ringer’s Summary

Providing unique high value Management Training & Business Consultancy for organisations who need to grow sustainably and organically through recession conditions.

We implement proven business processes that enable our customers to make the seemingly impossible possible. We help our customers to improve their performance, to push the envelope and to make their stretch targets.

Using our Processes our blue chip customers have time and again been able to cut their CAPEX and OPEX while producing a series of step changes in their individual, team and organisational productivity and their performance.

Whether doubling managers' productivity, building high performance teams, building efficient integrated teams, agreeing budgets, unlocking new markets and new customers, step changing our customers' bid win rates and margins, cutting the cost of bidding, securing complex bids, specifying solutions, improving the quality of project delivery, speeding and smoothing project delivery, reviewing projects, doing process improvement, turning businesses and business units around, developing organisational or project strategy and business improvement plans, implementing internal change projects and changing organisational culture and behaviours our customers find that our Processes are invaluable.

Our unique Processes include our:

5-Minute Process
CDN Consulting Process
Implementing Internal Projects Process
Campaign Development & Management
Persuasive Presentations
Business Writing

In April 2009 the Chief Information Officer at the Ministry of Justice said that using our "5-Minute Process puts you in the top 1% of people" in business today.

The Memory Maps Company was founded in 1986 as Wootton Technical Services by Anthony (Tony) Ringer. The Company then became WTS and then The Memory Maps Company (Maidenhead) and The Memory Maps Company (Marlow). These companies linked to become The Memory Maps Company Ltd in 2001.
