Alex Fielding’s Summary

Alex Fielding has spent his career innovating in the areas of advanced research and development of networking and communications products. His passion for green tech, security, and data centers has spanned senior level positions at Power Assure, Ripcord, Apple, TVDC, Wheels of Zeus, Cisco, DEC, and Cadence.

Alex serves on the board of directors of PharmaCapture and CAWEW. He is a technology advisor to TED Med.

He also is a member of ARIN, and active contributor to the IETF, and a contributing member of two NIST working groups on SCAP. Alex is a prior member of MENSA. He is a founding member of Singularity University at NASA Ames Research Center. Alex is also a contributor and co-author on several patent applications.
