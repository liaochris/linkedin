RONALD COOK’s Summary

Firm Overview
►Our Firm’s attorneys have significant experience in handling a wide range of federal and state civil and criminal matters.
►Our Firm emphasizes dispute resolution as an alternative to costly, time-consuming litigation. Where there is no alternative, however, the Firm is prepared to go to trial.
►Our firm offers experienced, dedicated and affordable legal services.
►In today's fast paced environment, you need a law firm that can handle all of your legal needs effectively, efficiently and economically.
►Our Firm currently has three offices. The Suffolk County office is located in Smithtown. The Nassau County office is located in Garden City. The Manhattan office is located in mid-town on 5th Avenue.

Firm philosophy
►Our Firm strives to provide the highest quality legal services to its clients with individualized attention. The Firm’s attorneys are available for any need our clients may have at virtually any time. Unlike most attorneys, the Firm’s attorneys provide clients with our home and cell phone numbers for around-the-clock access.
►Our Firm offers its business clients an integrated approach to their legal representation, combining the practices of litigation and business transactions within a single, full-service Firm to meet the day-to-day needs of emerging, middle-market, and large private and public companies.
►Our Firm’s attorneys have been trained through previous work with nationwide law firms. Our attorneys are also entrepreneurs who have forged out on their own to provide clients with a level of attention not found in larger law firms.
►This approach enables the attorney who is most familiar with a client’s business to provide consistent, timely and cost-effective representation across a broad spectrum of legal needs, while also providing access to the expertise necessary to advise clients in complex personal or business transactions.
