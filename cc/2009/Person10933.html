Mike Hunter’s Summary

betterlanguages.com Ltd. is one of the fastest growing translation agencies in the UK. We are widening our client base, and constantly improving our service. The customer experience is particularly important to us. We always use professional translators and interpreters of relevant specialism. We are backed by industry leading computer assisted translation tools, which support the work of the translators, ensuring consistent terminology. Clients include Mothercare, New Look Retailers, and US company Zak Designs.

Mike Hunter’s Specialties:

We translate in all major specialisms including website translation and localisation, legal translation, label and packaging translation, translation of instructions and user guides, business document translation.
