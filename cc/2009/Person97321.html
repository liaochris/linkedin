Teemu Kivikangas’s Summary

I am an experienced game designer, art director, illustrator and film-maker working on cutting-edge media design. I hold an MA (New Media) for University of Art and Design Helsinki. A decade of work experience in the new media industry - with wide range of different positions and responsibilities - has given me a broad view of the possibilities of the digital media and a strong skillset to utilize in my work.

My design clients include prestigious brands like Nokia, Williams and Pernod-Ricard, fashion houses (e.g. Marja Kurki), media companies (e.g. SanomaWSOY), advertising agencies and a number of the most innovative players in the Finnish game industry (e.g. Digital Chocolate and Mr.Goodliving).

As a freelancer, I most enjoy projects where I can work in a team, in a highly collaborative fashion with my clients.

Teemu Kivikangas’s Specialties:

Game design specialties: massively multi-player games, adverticing and games, character design, script-writing and dramaturgy, storytelling in games.

Game graphics specialties: art direction, concept graphics, character design, 2D-game graphics, mobile games, visual identities.

Film-making specialties: Motion graphics, script-writing and dramaturgy, documentary film.

Other specialties: Language skills (Finnish, English, Swedish, Spanish, Portuguese and basic Japanese and German)
