Robin Zachary’s Summary

A dynamic career art directing fashion, lifestyle, beauty and still life photography for all types of visual media. I devise concepts and produce creative solutions within budget, on time and with all logistics carefully orchestrated. I am a results-oriented team leader with expertise in assembling creative teams or merging seamlessly with an already established team.

Robin Zachary’s Specialties:

Concept development for creating visual imagery for luxury items, fashion, beauty, spa, lingerie, and men's fashion with a specialization in weddings (fashion and reception). Art direction, prop styling, craft project development, and set design; initiating partnerships with major hotels, resorts and spas (both US and abroad) for mutually beneficial exposure in national magazines; project management and logistics for location shoots including location scouting, itinerary and budget preparation.
