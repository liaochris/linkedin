Bert Verdonck + Genius Shortcuts’s Summary

Bert Verdonck is an outstanding coach, extraordinary lifehacker and a creative mind. He also contributes by connecting people, writing blogs, presenting a radio show, giving lectures and sharing knowledge. He lives up to his personal motto: Enjoy every day!

Bert is also the first Wealth Dynamics Master Practitioner in the Benelux, and specialises in helping entrepreneurs. He was personally trained by Roger Hamilton and his team in Indonesia.

Bert is the founder of Genius Shortcuts (www.geniusshortcuts.com). Buy his audio CD now!

Bert has created his personal coaching programme: Create, Connect & Contribute. Bert coaches people, especially entrepreneurs, who want to live the life of their dreams. He helps them to step out of their daily pattern and inspires them to find their true passion(s), not only by giving a framework and tools, but also by providing practical (business) ideas and connections to realise them!

Bert Verdonck has a background in biometrics (fingerprint, iris scan and facial recognition) and in online communication and services. Bert studied Commercial Engineering at the University of Antwerp, Belgium.

More info about Bert Verdonck is available at www.bertverdonck.com
