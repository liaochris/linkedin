Robert Grawet’s Summary

General Manager of .advanced Method, the world's leading digital signage software company. .advancedMethod is a subsidiary of EIKI Digital Systems, Inc., a company with roots going back 55 years, that is headquarted in Osaka, Japan. EIKI has offices in all parts of the world.

After joining .advancedMethod in February 08, was responsible for finding a company to acquire us to give us the proper infrastructure/funding to complete our business plan. Sold company in July 08, 6 months after taking this assignment. Value of acquisition - $3.2Million.

Since the acquisition in July 08, has responsibility for execution of our business plan including rollout of a new software product and building distribution for worldwide sales.

As of March 09, .aM has a North American distributor/dealer channel and have just begun to set up our international distribution.

Formerly Director of Interactive Digital Signage and Manager OEM/Special Projects at SMART Technologies, Inc. the world's leading source for interactive electronic whiteboards.

Previously served as President/CEO of Da-Lite Screen Co. subsidiary;Visual Structures, a manufacturer of custom video walls. Largest project;the display for the Tokyo Stock Exchange.

Co-Inventor of 'Instant Expert' - a combination digital signage system and instant connect video conference call to an 'expert' or 'translator' so that customers can have personal access to the world of information on your product.

See information on Instant Expert at:
http://instantexpertbyam.blogspot.com/

Primary experience as Senior Management Executive successful at building and leading a world-class team. Strong business acumen with the ability to execute wide range of sales and marketing strategies to establish market presence and increase revenues and profitability. A speaker at numerous Strategy Institute events including Chairmanship of one. Excellent at building sales channels and integrating sales and marketing organizations.

Robert Grawet’s Specialties:

Strategic partnership development, Infrastructure design and execution; Business Development of new products, sales channels, strategies; Executive and Sales Management. Public Speaking relating to Digital Signage.
