James McKim’s Summary

Most recently I led the information technology group at an internationally known non-profit organization. Prior to that, I was a co-founder of a management consulting firm specialized in the strategic use of information through both non-human and human means. My previous years experience ranges from coding for a small software development house to managing client/server services for Digital Equipment Corp.

Believing in participating in win-win situations, I seek opportunities to play a leadership role work in an organization where I can put to use my extensive experience providing strategies and systems which ensure the best use of human and information technology capital making sure the right information is available to the right people at the right time to drive the organization's success.
