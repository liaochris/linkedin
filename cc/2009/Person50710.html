Jose Maldonado’s Summary

I've been in many diverse positions, and would like to use that experience to produce projects with the hope that they will help people to appreciate and enjoy their lives more or perhaps make them think a little bit more about the world we are a part of.

I feel that my eclectic background in art, video/animation production, design, administration, education, promotions, planning and management have equipped me to become a unique and well rounded producer with many advantages, tools and connections at my disposal.
