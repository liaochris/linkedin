Doug Kyle’s Summary

Doug brings a background in Project Management, Business Analysis and Quality Assurance to business solutions roles primarily focused around software solutions, workshop facilitation, and process re-engineering.

Projects have included senior roles managing the development of the commercial applications OnCore (www.ddytech.com) and Digital Time Capture (www.DigitalTimeCapture.com). Custom solutions designed for a competitive edge including point of purchase retails systems, inventory management and project costing applications. Process re-engineering projects have included project management office (PMO) implementations, project and software development lifecycle, bid management process and IT C-SOX work flow.

Doug has numerous partnerships with application providers and solutions developers to ensure you get the best solution. Doug is currently pursuing his Certified Management Consulting certification.
