Patricia Truter’s Summary

A versatile, manager and consultant with >15 years of drug delivery and product development experience from idea, preclinical development to clinical trials and commercialization. Strengths include the ability to build effective teams by identifying and acquiring skills needed and motivating technical experts to address corporate strategy by the development of scalable intellectual property while addressing the FDA requirements for approval.

A passionate team player, able to keep abreast of technical challenges within the biotechnology and pharmaceutical industries with a hands on approach and the unique ability to tighten development timelines by identifying go/no go decisions within a very short time frame. Able to translate corporate objectives into manageable individual goals and motivate a team to become sensitive and aggressive towards tight deadlines and schedules.
