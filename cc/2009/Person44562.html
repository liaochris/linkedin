Jayne Juvan’s Summary

Jayne E. Juvan, Esq., an attorney with the Private Equity Practice Group and Health Care Practice Group at Benesch Friedlander Coplan & Aronoff LLP, concentrates her practice principally in the areas of mergers, acquisitions and divestitures, corporate governance and regulatory compliance. While she represents clients in a wide range of industries, she has broad experience representing private equity funds with health care investments and regularly advises both publicly and privately held health care companies such as retail and institutional pharmacies, retail medical clinics, hospitals and health systems, long term care providers and ancillary service providers on compliance with fraud and abuse laws, state licensure laws, HIPAA, and Medicare and Medicaid reimbursement laws.

In addition to her practice, Ms. Juvan maintains a nationally recognized blog on corporate and health care law, available at www.juvanshealthlawupdate.com. The blog highlights legal developments at the intersection of health care and private equity.

Ms. Juvan is a frequent lecturer on the topics of leadership, career management, community service and the law. She has also been quoted or featured in several regional and national publications, including Inside Business Magazine, Crain's Cleveland Business, Poetic Justice, RFID Update, Computerworld, Pharmaceutical Commerce, LSC Updates, Chief Security Officer Magazine, CIO World News, IG Living and Healthcare IT News / MobileHealthWatch.com.

Ms. Juvan dedicates a significant amount of her time to pro bono assistance and is very active in the community. She is admitted to practice law in the State of Ohio.
