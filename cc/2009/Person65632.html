Maury Giles’s Summary

Entrepreneurially-minded executive with a passion for helping marketers succeed with truly customer-centric solutions. Bring 15 years of marketing research, ROI analytics, and communication strategy expertise for consumer brand, political, non-profit, and B2B clients across numerous categories. Thrive on finding new ways to (1) understand the "why" and the "how" people make decisions, and (2) help marketers genuinely connect with people using that insight.

Maury Giles’s Specialties:

Marketing communications planning, marketing research (ethnographic, qualitative, survey research, etc.), branding, marketing ROI and simulation techniques, media planning/optimization, group facilitation/strategic planning
