Darin Warren’s Summary

Darin has been working on internet solutions since 1997 designing and developing for clients such as: LDS Church, MTV2, Mandalay Resort Group, IDTes, Sandman Studios, Skullcandy, Candesa, PSNet, The Entity, NapkinJournal.com, SunrisePeak, IPINA and countless others across the United States and Africa.

Darin was the Director of Multimedia at WorldVuer.com a online community communications company creating video email and web video conferencing solutions, managed a design and development team for Excite@Home and has ran Dwarren Design which is his own Web Design/Developement Company.

Experienced designer and developer on the following applications and enviroments: Flash, Flex, Photoshop, Illustrator, Encore, After Effects, Video Compression, Javascript, PHP, ASP, Red5 (Media Server), Flash Media Server, Apache, MySql, Window OS and Mac OS.

List of clients are available at www.dwarren.com
