Stephen Hart’s Summary

I see my job as a vocation and my philosophy for that vocation is very simple - I want to help people ~ and to do that I have to create results.

This means that whether as a public speaker, corporate trainer or career coach when I am tasked with presenting material it is my job to see that it gets to the delegates or audience in a format that they can understand, relate to and benefit from.

I delight in getting my delegates or audiences to challenge their views on the world and themselves.

In a nutshell I help people to think differently.

Stephen Hart’s Specialties:

Extensive experience at training recruitment and sales professionals of all levels.

Also good experience at management and leadership training.

Experienced with personal development programmes focusing on career choices, confidence, motivation and self belief changes.

A creative and articulate public speaker particularly good at stimulating an audience to see things from a fresh perspective.
