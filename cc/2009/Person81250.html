Mark Rabinowitz’s Summary

Film industry consultant with expertise in the founding and operation of international and regional film festivals as well as new media enterprises. Currently in acquisitions at CINELAN, a new documentary film syndication company.

Have attended more than 125 festivals since 1993, including Cannes, Sundance, Berlin, Rotterdam and Toronto.

13 years of online and print journalism experience for national and international publications. Co-founder and former co-editor in chief of a major international independent film news service.

Industry Liaison for the Hamptons International Film Festival. Also programmed for that festival and others.

Co-produced award-winning 35mm feature film.

Mark Rabinowitz’s Specialties:

Online and print journalism; documentary and narrative film distribution and production; film festival programming, industry relations and administration.

Web site organization consultant, expert in low-cost website startups.
