Javier Alejandro Santillán’s Summary

I have got more than ten (10) years of experience in several informatics aspects. I'm experienced in Networking, Implementation of Desktop Applications, Technical Support, Bakcoffice and trainer for internal and external customers.
Since more than three years and a half I'm focused in SQA as a Tester and as a Leader of Testing, taken part in several projects under both, CMMI methodologies and Agile.
My experience is for leadership of software quality and testing teams, including deployment of procedures, practices and standard documentation, CMMI-2 and 3 compatible.
I also worked as a software tester for functional and integration testing projects, under Java, Ajax, Lotus Notes, C#.Net, ASP and other technologies.
I'm interested in the transcendental aspects of the work teams, as professionals, persons and teammates. My main goal is the excellence in software development processes, having products/services as a result of transformation in actual models and paradigms.
