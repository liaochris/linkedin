  • IT Architect

As the Information Security Officer, I developed a comprehensive ISO 17799-based Information Security Policy Manual and managed the HIPAA Security compliance program.

  • Principal Engineer

  • Director, Information Technology

    NewFields, Inc.

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Environmental Services industry)

    As Director of Information Technology, I managed vendor relationships, selected technologies, managed corporate networks, updated the public web site, and set up two new offices for this environmental consulting firm. As a chemical engineer, I provided combustion engineering consulting services for a large industrial client.

  • Principal Engineer

    Foster Wheeler Environmental

    (Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; Environmental Services industry)

    October 1989 — May 1998 (8 years 8 months)

    As a registered professional Chemical Engineer in the States of California and Washington, I provided engineering consulting services for a wide variety of combustion, chemical, nuclear, and environmental applications. I published about a dozen papers, presented at technical conferences, and I contributed to chapters in books such as the Kirk-Othmer Encyclopedia of Chemical Technology. I also served as the Engineering Manager in the Sacramento Office, supervising up to 10 engineering consultants.

  • Nuclear Engineer

    Puget Sound Naval Shipyard

    (Government Agency; 10,001 or more employees; Military industry)

    July 1984 — September 1989 (5 years 3 months)

    I worked in the Radiological Technical Division at the Puget Sound Naval Shipyard as a Nuclear Engineer, focusing primarily on radioactive decontamination technologies and operations. I took a 200-hour course in applied health physics (how to work safely with radioactivity) at Oak Ridge Associated University, and I took a 200-hour Navy course on the internals of the S5W reactor plant. I also wrote policies, procedures, standards, and guidelines in the form of Nuclear Power Manuals and Nuclear Power Engineering Instructions.
