Simon Gibbs’s Summary

I started my professional career building reporting solutions in MS Access, Excel, PL/SQL and T-SQL for an MVNO though my primary experience has been building and maintaining well structured ASP.NET applications, debugging web controls and pages, as well as working directly in XHTML, CSS and Javascript. Lately, I have returned to data processing with queues and web services in the telecommunications sector.

I am able to criticise and refine a specification by understanding both the user's and the business point of view, and also keeping in mind the operational support of a system. I'm seeking to increase my experience in leadership situations and have been working recently as senior developer in a small team.
