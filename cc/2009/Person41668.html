Ipswitch File Transfer’s Summary

Ipswitch File Transfer builds software to securely move your valuable data. The Ipswitch MOVEit® and WS_FTP® secure and managed file transfer solutions deliver the security and governance necessary to help enable compliance for our more than 40 million global users – including the majority of Fortune 1000 enterprises and government agencies. These organizations trust Ipswitch File Transfer solutions to secure, manage, automate and streamline their critical and highly-sensitive file transfers and data workflows. We invite you to learn more at www.IpswitchFT.com or to contact us at www.IpswitchFT.com/contact.

Ipswitch File Transfer’s Experience

  • Secure and Managed File Transfer Solutions

    Ipswitch, Inc.

    (Privately Held; Computer Software industry)

    January 1992 — Present (17 years 11 months)

    Ipswitch File Transfer (FT) is responsible for developing, selling and supporting the company’s well-known lines of secure and managed file transfer software -- WS_FTP Server, WS_FTP Professional, WS_FTP Home, MOVEit Central, and MOVEit DMZ. These products provide a comprehensive set of solutions used around the world by tens of thousands of small to medium and enterprise-level companies, government agencies and educational institutions – as well as by millions of individuals.

    The FT division has approximately 75 employees at it's US offices in Lexington, Massachusetts, Madison, Wisconsin and Augusta, Georgia, as well as in the United Kingdom and Europe. The division’s products are sold worldwide via e-commerce, a direct sales staff, and through distributors, resellers and OEMs.
