Dmitry Nozhnin’s Summary

Despite having a MSc in Physics (nucleai & elementary particles, all that invisible stuff) computer games always were my hobby, passion, addiction and my way to make a living.

Over 10 years I've tried hats of QA tester, level designer, producer and lead designer, manager and project lead. My focus is shifting from production towards business development and strategic thinking, boosted by studying an MBA.

Online games are my real passion. I am going to combine this passion with my knowledge of games business and creative game designer skills to start up a small online games company and drive it through the competitive storms.

Entrepreneur's life is challenging. And I really love to be challenged.
