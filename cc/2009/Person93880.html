  • Company Director

    Darker Limited

    (Consumer Goods industry)

    November 2003 — March 2007 (3 years 5 months)

    Darker Ltd. was a company created by myself and a business partner. The Glass Block Outlet (a subsidiary of Darker Ltd.) website was developed and built by myself and sells glass blocks and glass block related products online. We were the first glass blocks online store to calculate the materials required to build your glass blocks design. The online store was created using Actinic Catalog and integrated with a WorldPay merchant account. Although Actinic was a useful program, it still required me to develop my own JavaScript and PHP to create the glass block DIY installation pack systems.

    Glass Block Outlet is number one in many search engines for related industry key phrases such as 'Glass Blocks', 'Glass Bricks', 'Glass Block' and 'Glass Brick'. This is due to the search engine optimisation I have implemented since the start of the project.

    Glass Block Outlet is listed on the BBC website under Garden Invaders Top Ten Products.
