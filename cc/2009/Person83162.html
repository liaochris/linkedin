Chris Rettstatt’s Summary

I'm a published children's book author with 3+ years in publishing and 10+ years as a specialist in youth-focused virtual community and online marketing.

Expertise:

* Developed transmedia entertainment properties for kids and teens
* Online marketing techniques and practices (SEO, WOM, grassroots, viral)
* COPPA, CARU, children's privacy and safety
* Built and managed teams as large as 30, including designers, editors, web developers, and online community staff
* Extensive social media user and strategist
* Tons of hands-on experience, from building and maintaining drupal communities to creating and running marketing campaigns

Examples:

* Created epic fantasy property called Kaimira, including five-book deal with Candlewick Press and Walker Books & "360-degree development deal" with BBC Worldwide
* Managing Director, Interactive for a branch of Thomson Corporation
* Developed MMOG story bibles for a Toronto-based nonprofit
* Contributed a chapter on "kids and digital ownership" for Settlers of the New Virtual Worlds
* Developed online safety e-learning modules for schoolteachers
* Regular conference speaker on "kids online" topics
* Performed online community consulting for Fox Kids Online
* Editor for the Online Directory Project
* Online privacy (COPPA) consulting for Back Yard Jungle, funded by the Corporation for Public Broadcasting
* Director, summer computer camp for kids
* Published "kids online" articles
* Judge, Digital Communities category, Prix Ars prize
* Judge, Fantasy / Science Fiction category, 2007 Cybils award
