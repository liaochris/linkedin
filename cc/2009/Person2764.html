Albert McGovern’s Summary

Committed Engineering professional with a diverse technical and industry background who thinks and plans strategically, yet acts tactically with a sense of urgency. A hands-on leader with integrity who builds strong teams by recognizing and focusing on individual strengths and achieving results by creating team commitments. Utilizing excellent communications skills, I empower and enable team members by leading through example, edification, empathetic listening and mentoring.

Aggressively progressed through the Design Engineering career continuum to acquire sufficient hands-on experience at each level resulting in promotions to greater responsibility throughout my career. Possess strong knowledge of electronics packaging techniques, and material types and manufacturing processes for plastic and metallic part design, including rapid prototyping and analysis techniques and tools.
