Dan Meneely’s Summary

I've had an interesting 15 Years designing for print, interactive and web, along with a touch of 3D and broadcast work in the early years of my career. I'm primarily focused on print design and working my way back into web and interactive design. I have a painting background as well, and occasionally sell my work through galleries and online.

I am extremely loyal to any employer who brings me on board. I bring a high level of commitment, creativity, talent and design experience to my role. I'm also very dead-line conscious and goal oriented.

Dan Meneely’s Specialties:

Graphic design, layout design, web design, logo design, illustration, character design, photo retouch, digital art, painting and drawing. And problem solving in general.
