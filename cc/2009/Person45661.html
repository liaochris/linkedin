Jeff Roach’s Summary

With more than 15 years in advertising and marketing communications, Jeff has launched products, developed brands, influenced opinion, and won share. He's one of the most well-rounded marketers in North America with deep experience in Advertising, Digital, Broadcast, Experiential, Lifestyle, Entertainment, Retail and PR. With a career start as an art director, Jeff went on to become a copywriter, creative director, account director and strategic planner with a career highlight that includes a CASSIE award for advertising effectiveness for his role in a youth tobacco cessation campaign. Jeff has worked on hundreds of brands in virtually every category including BlackBerry, Bubblicious, Coors, Coty, Durex, Energizer, Fisher-Price, Fujifilm, MasterCard, Mattel, Microsoft, MINI, MTV, Nokia, Procter & Gamble, PUMA and Ubisoft. Jeff has been quoted in the Globe & Mail, has presented at Understanding Youth and Internet World conferences, and is a Communications and Advertising Accredited Professional. Jeff launched Glitteration to meet today's need for a nimble, new-model communications firm, engineered from the ground-up to service the future of business.

Jeff's experience is rich, previously as the VP, Marketing Strategy of the Y Syndicate, which includes Youthography, Riot Brand Experiences and District Lifestyle Marketing; VP, Client Services with experiential youth marketing firm MASEV Communications; and as Co-Creative Director of marketing communications agency JWA Group.

Jeff splits his time between offices in Orange County and Toronto.
