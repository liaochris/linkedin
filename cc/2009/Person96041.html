Susan Mason’s Summary

Susan heads up the interactive division of Ross Culbert & Lavery. She has over 10 years experience with website project management which includes establishing creative direction, design development, production and pricing. She oversees all aspects of RCL’s most demanding web projects and works closely with team members (strategic analyst, creative, technology and client) to ensure smooth development processes. She manages projects that vary in size and complexity and assures that they are delivered at the highest standard, on time and on budget.

Her resume of web sites includes those for Debevoise & Plimpton LLP, Morgan & Finnegan LLP, Metronexus, Lehman Brothers (Shareholders’ Annual), Omni Financial, Miramax Films, American Express (Intranet), ARCO, Kelley Drye & Warren LLP, Metlife’s Defined Benefit Services and the award-winning Proskauer Rose LLP site. With the breadth of her background, she has the unique ability to span both creative and technical worlds and design work that extends easily to different mediums. She is involved in all phases of creative and design development.

Susan Mason’s Specialties:

Susan extends business strategies to the web through the development of tangible visual documents in the form of the creative brief, sitemaps, and wireframes. She brings together both functionality and aesthetic to translate marketing goals and business initiatives to the visual plane. Susan's broad depth of online, video and print experience helps provide branding and strategy continuity across diverse mediums.
