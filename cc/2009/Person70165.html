Morten Gade’s Summary

I am head of communication at Bysted A/S, one of the major communication consultancies at Bysted. I have a background as a communication consultant, and mainly focus and strategy, campaigns and digital media.

I work for a number of different clients, including WWF Verdensnaturfonden, Ledernes Hovedorganisation, Kunstakademiets Arkitektskole, Dansk Design Center and Egmont.

Additionaly, I teach at The IT-University in Copenhagen, where I am external lecturer (ekstern lektor), focusing on digital strategy and social software (web 2.0 / social media)

Morten Gade’s Specialties:

Communication strategy, web strategy, social media, business development, campaigns, proces consulting, user experience, experience communication (events etc.)
