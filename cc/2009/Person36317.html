Gil Heiman’s Summary

Community Director at Clarizen (On-demand Project Collaboration solutions), acting as the users’ advocate and liaison between the user community and Clarizen internal members. My main role is managing the inside sales, support and account management teams and facilitating the tools and content to make sure Clarizen end-users’ voice is heard internally.

Prior to Clarizen, worked at an early stage Swiss P2P software company called Collanos, as the Director of Product Management and Community.
Over the last 10 years have been focusing on community and product management activities, representing the customer/user at internet start-ups and large software companies.

Entrepreneur in spirit, always seeking innovative solutions to create a better user experience. Thrive in smaller, team oriented environments and enjoy taking upon myself formidable challenges which I pursue with determination.

MBA and MS-MIS from Boston University and a B.A in Japanese from Hebrew University, Jerusalem.

Gil Heiman’s Specialties:

Product Management, Product Marketing, Competitive Analysis, Team Collaboration, Customer Advocacy, User Experience, Community, Inside Sales.
