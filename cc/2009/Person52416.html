Jung Yeo’s Summary

Project management and consulting professional responsible for practice management, business development, and solution delivery in the SAP CRM practice. Vast experience in planning, configuration, development, and management of SAP implementation projects at commercial and public sector clients since 1998 and SAP CRM since 2000.

Prior to joining TPC, Jung was a Senior Technology Architect and Senior Manager at BearingPoint, Inc. Prior to joining BearingPoint, Jung launched his consulting career at SAP America, first with the CRM Solution Center in 2000, and subsequently with CRM Consulting. Many of Jung’s clients are profiled in SAP customer success stories and ROI case studies. Jung started his career as a Process Development Scientist and Utility Operator in a Biotechnology start-up plant with Bayer Pharmaceuticals and later joined the SAP project at Bayer Consumer Care and Pharmaceuticals Divisions as an ABAP developer.
