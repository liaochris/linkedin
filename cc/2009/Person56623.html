Kristin Miltner’s Summary

I love composing music and designing sound effects and soundscapes for games. My goal is to work in an imaginative and active environment with other artists and designers to collaboratively create fun, intuitive and inventive games.

I have creative and diverse abilities in sound design, music creation, and audio
implementation. Excellent interaction with support staff, technicians, and producers.

Games:

Sims Carnival: Defense Against Monsters, Pinball!, Fractured Earth, Dungeons with Dragons, Sunday Sunday Sunday!, Crate Pusher, Castle Escape.

Sims: Sims 3, Sim Animals, MySims Kingdom, Sims 2 expansion packs.

Didj: Neopets, Included title: Jetpack Heroes, Super Chicks, Didj baserom library.

Leapster: Sponge Bob Square Pants Saves the Day, Fosters Home for Imaginary Friends, Bratz, Letters on the Loose, Pixar Studios Cars
Supercharged, Pixar Studios The Incredibles, Leapster Arcade Games Word Chasers, Letterpillar, Number Raiders, Creature
Create, LMax Game Catalog, Leapster baserom library.

Fly and Fly 2: Fly Gen 2 Memorizer, Music Studio Pro, Spanish Translator Pro, Fly baserom library.

Awards:
• Game Audio Network Guild Award: Leapster Sponge Bob Square Pants Saves the Day
• 2006 Parents' Choice Foundation, Parents' Choice Approved Award: Little Leaps


• Created baserom instrument sets and custom instruments to exceed original design goals. Exploited a limited palette of instruments and create dynamic background for small-footprint projects.
• Implemented interactive music engine for the Leapster Platform. Worked with programmers to make interactive music games using midi with embedded control tracks that trigger sfx on user input.

Kristin Miltner’s Specialties:

• Principle contributor to core audio development of Leapster, Didj, and Fly Gen2, including the formation and implementation of their internal sound effect libraries, user-interface sound sets, and startup logos (Leapster/LMax/Leapster TV).
• Developed audio protocols for all non-interactive sequences (cut scenes).
