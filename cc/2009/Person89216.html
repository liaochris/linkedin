Warner Schmidt’s Summary

The majority of my experience comes from my late high school and early college years where I spend much time fixing, troubleshooting, and building computers for friends, family, and various other aquaintances.

For Masterson Company, I build and maintain workstations, monitor and install security systems, and plan and implement networking within the company. I also create manuals or quick reference guides for various systems within the company.

I also have a wide variety of leadership experience, most of which was provided to me by the Boy Scouts of America. I am proud to say that I am an Eagle Scout, an achievement that less than 3% of Scouts ever achieve. I have done many volunteer acts through BSA including Scouting For Food, Park Clean Ups, Eagle Scout Service Projects, and most importantly to me, the National Youth Leadership Training (NYLT) Course hosted by Milwaukee County Council. I have been with NYLT for 6 years including the year I went through training.

I have experience in computer hardware and software, communications, networking, wireless networking, DVR and subsystems management, Microsoft Operating Systems from 95 through Windows 7, the Microsoft Office Suite including Word, Excel, Publisher, FrontPage, PowerPoint, OneNote, and more, java programming, website design and programming, printers, cameras, and more.
