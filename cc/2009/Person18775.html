Christopher Thompson’s Summary

Beginning in 1994 I started in the business as a private consultant for start-up companies gearing advertising campaigns to enhance new product ideas. Creating an entire campaign from nothing essentially, from initial conceptuals, through hand drawn samples, to digital based media and the finished product, I developed whatever was required while supporting the client with any relevant research and market data. More recently, I was the Creative Director at I.A.R, an advertising firm based in the Southern United States, that catered to the needs of national and international clients. Conceptualizing classic print media and online designs while traveling extensively provided me with enormous challenges and amazing rewards. Whether perfecting a campaign for the luxury market or working with photographers on location, the advertising and design industries have given me great fulfillment.

Most of my time was spent in the New York office and across such countries as France, Germany, Italy, and Japan. Sadly, I was working on the set of a commercial when I.A.R.'s founder passed away. This prompted me to seek other opportunities.

With an ironic twist of fate, what began as a small software development firm, has grown into a stalwart company, bringing unexpected joys of its own. Branching out into Imagining and Bioinformatics has opened up avenues of creative ingenuity across multiple industries allowing me to tap into both the Beauty of Design and the Rewards of Business.

Currently, I divide my time between TriLateral Systems and other ventures. As a personal challenge, I do my very best to work on one Philanthropic project each day.
