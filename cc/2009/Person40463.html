Hugo Raposo, PMP®, Certified Architect’s Summary

Mr. Hugo Raposo has 15 years of experience in Information Technology, Solution Architectures and integration Architectures. He is recognized for his contributions and deep commitments in Enterprise Architectures and Architectural Solutions for Banking, Telecom, Health, Oil & Gas verticals and other emerging industries. Mr. Hugo Raposo has a strong strategic vision for new businesses and a wide international experience in reduction of costs, self-sustainable growth and operational managerial methods.

Mr. Hugo Raposo is a certified Project Manager Professional (PMP) and a Senior Enterprise Architect certified by Sun Microsystems, IBM and BEA. In this connection, he is specialized in designing and implementation of SOA and BPM solutions based on Sun Microsystems, IBM, BEA and Oracle infrastructure. Moreover, he has a deep experience in establishing and managing technical sales, designing technical proposals, standardizing reference architectures, leading and overseeing solution implementation and finally, providing responsive and quality technical assistance to account teams as a subject matter expert (SME).

Mr. Hugo Raposo is an appreciated teacher and has a broad and deep knowledge of Rational concepts (UML and RUP), SOA, EAI, J2EE, BPM, PMBOK, CMMI, Function Points analysis and RDBMS.
