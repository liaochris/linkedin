Joseph Brutto’s Summary

This includes not only work with various platforms (Linux, AIX, Windows, IBM iSeries), programming languages (C/C++, Java, Perl, Python, Ruby, etc.) but also within the business areas of the realm.

My career has focused on solving the difficult problems of integration regardless of the specific industry in which they dwell. My qualifications and experience have grown from being a simple programmer to being a generally well-rounded architect, engineer and active business contributor.

My affinity towards research, software in general, constant reading, etc. lends itself to not only furthering my current skills and expanding my skill sets, but also towards my future goals. I've been rewarded throughout my career for this as well as for my overall dedication to my work.
