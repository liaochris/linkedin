Jo Mack MIRP, Cert.RP’s Summary

How time flies! I have been working in the recruitment industry now for almost 18 years, and in HSE (Health, Safety and Environmental) recruitment for the last 4 of those years. My company, New Level Recruitment, specialises in HSE Recruitment to all industry sectors and across all grades, from entry level to Director. We pride ourselves on providing an open, honest and ethical recruitment service to our clients and candidates - call us to find out for yourself!

In addition to my work at New Level Recruitment I am proud to be a Regional Director of TEAM (The Employment Agents Movement), a networking association of independent recruitment agencies.
