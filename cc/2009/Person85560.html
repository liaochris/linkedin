Roderick L. Evans’s Summary

Roderick L. Evans has been active in the Christian community as a minister, author, and publisher for over 12 years. He has written numerous books, teaching resources, and other devotional materials for the Christian community. Roderick L. Evans is the founder of Kingdom Builders International Ministries: A ministry dedicated to promoting maturity, unity, and holiness in the Body of Christ.

He is the founder and president of Berea Bible Institute located in Kenya, East Africa. He is also the founder of Kingdom Builders Ministerial Alliance located in various parts of the world. In addition, he is the president of Kingdom Builders Publishing and Kingdom Builders Productions.

He and his family (wife - Letitia and their son - Seth) reside in Elizabeth City, North Carolina where he continues his extensive speaking and writing career.
