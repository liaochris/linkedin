Edward Cates’s Summary

Marketing executive with a wealth of breadth and expertise within the field. Previously contributed to the development of national multicultural marketing strategy to deliver commercial and private bank client relationships through event sponsorships.

Unique C-level experience providing premier event & meeting management support to the top 10-15% of executives at Bank of America. Event execution ranges from client entertainment to senior management meetings with the top 600 global executives within the bank.

Later transitioned to manage all marketing, communications and national relationships with supplier diversity advocacy organizations.
