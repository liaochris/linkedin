Matthias Zeitler’s Summary

Matthias is an expert in web technologies, offshore outsourcing, global content lifecycles, translation process technology and enterprise software product management.

After leading a captive offshore development organization in Russia he is a strong believer in offshore and outsourcing as a strategic business advantage. He is driving to create great products and solutions that excite customers.

He has been driving the development of cutting edge products and solutions at various positions with globalization industry leaders like the STAR Group, TRADOS and SDL International. Since globalization is always integrated into other functions and systems, he has a lot of experience with partners and 3rd party technologies like Documentum, Interwoven, Astoria, Arbortext/PTC, Alchemy, Systran and many other commercial or internal front- and backend systems.

He has worked in different countries where he gained a solid understanding of international business culture and local nuances.

To advance his knowledge and to strengthen his network he is currently attending the MBA program at Santa Clara University with a focus on leadership and entrepreneurship.

He also founded and developed several smaller projects and businesses in the social media space. His Biotrend application on Facebook has grown to 45,000 users and is still growing.
