Sherry Michaels’s Summary

My company provides training and documentation content faster than industry standard. We create materials in all media that can be reused and repurposed whenever possible. We have a team of over 25 professionals whom include in their expertise e-learning, web site content and rich media design and development as well as live instruction delivery and development. We manage training development projects so that they beat deadlines, are under budget, and make our clients successful in meeting their business goals.

Sherry Michaels’s Specialties:

Mission critical applications, compliance, regulatory, sales, new hire employee, technical training in all forms (including ERP), director-level support and employee level performance improvement. Our industry expertise ranges from telecomm to insurance and/or healthcare, but we have a very strong background in electronics and electronics distribution. Our latest projects have included training for reservations for the travel industry and call center training for pharmaceuticals.
