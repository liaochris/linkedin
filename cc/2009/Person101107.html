Troy King’s Summary

As a Master Certified Coach (MCC) with over 15 years in Leadership Development and Coaching, Troy brings a wealth of experience to every individual and corporate client, as well as a commitment to being on the leading edge of 'Coaching for Sustainable Results'.

As CEO of Corporate Symphony International and a Master Certifiied Coach (MCC) the International Coach Federation's highest designation, Troy has been part of award winning Coaching and Leadership programs throughout North America, including the prestigious Prism Award on 3 separate occasions with 3 different clients.

Troy is based in Vancouver but works with clients throughout North America and through Corporate Symphony International leads a team of world class Coaching Partners and dozens of Affiliate Coaches with global coverage on large coaching projects.

Troy's clients include individual leaders committed to taking their leadership to the next level and global organizations committed to world class coaching and leadership development programs.

Troy's corporate client list includes: Rogers Communications / Wireless, ADP, Bell Canada, Cisco Systems, AT&T, Avaya, Espial, Freybe, Honeywell, Kotai Financial, Netcelerate, McKesson, Price Waterhouse Coopers (PWC), Siemens, Vale Inco and Videotron, as well as a number of local and global non profits that he provides pro bono coaching for.

Troy has extensive coaching, leadership training and talent development experience in a number of industries, including telecommunications, technology, logistics, accounting, investments, engineering and business services.

Troy King’s Specialties:

1. Triple Bottom Line Impact (Business Results, Leadership Capability, and Sustainability)
2. Coaching ROI (individual and corporate)
3. Stretch objectives - if it's 'impossible' it just takes a little longer or more likely it takes some 'outside the box thinking'.
4. Leadership Legacy - Who are you committed to being as a Leader and what difference are you committed to making in the world?!
