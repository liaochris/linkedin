Gianmarco Carnovale’s Summary

Early visionary and theorist of the mobile revolution of personal computing.

Continuous learner, committed to apply both formal and experiential learning for the benefit of my own personal growth. Convinced that new ideas come by application of fantasy and lateral thinking to every day's experience.

Experienced start-up builder with background of six newcos incorporated and developed in last 8 years; defined and launched trade activities, mobile products, web services, connected software; new projects in web 2.0 and publishing field currently under development. I'm a technology scouter, business developer, strategist on market development and introduction of new products/services, investing where usability drive product differentiation, adoption, and use.

Many goals in building strong and durable alliances, identifying customer needs and creating new market channels.

Gianmarco Carnovale’s Specialties:

Most phases of a start-up management from pre-launch: creation of business model; initial business planning; company incorporation; building and management of strategic alliances; market's positioning and competitivity analisys; commercial entrance on italian ICT, telco, new media/content distribution and trade markets; creation of innovative offering of product/services for specific targets; team building.
