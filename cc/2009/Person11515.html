Billy Ramirez’s Summary

Billy joined Plimus as Marketing Manager in June 2009.

From May 2006 to November 2008, Billy Ramirez was a Partner Operations Specialist at MyWaves. Prior to this, he served as Marketing Associate and Content Programmer.

Previously, he was the Assistant Editor at DRUM! Magazine where he interviewed industry professionals and wrote monthly articles ranging from 300 to 3,000 words.

He graduated from Santa Clara University with a degree in Communication in 2003.
