Annika Hipple’s Summary

I'm a freelance writer and photographer specializing in travel, environmental issues, philanthropy, and international development.

As a journalist, I contribute to a variety of magazines, newspapers, and online publications. Topics I am particularly interested in include: responsible travel, unique and emerging destinations, nature/wildlife/ecotourism, sustainable and community-based development, conservation, climate change, green living, philanthropy, ethnic issues, preservation of cultural heritage, authentic cultural interactions, outdoor recreation, and soft adventure. In addition, I write regularly about the meetings and events industry and also enjoy writing profiles of interesting people.

As a copywriter, I work with businesses and organizations who want to stand out in a crowd. Effective communication is about connecting with people in a way that resonates with their needs, concerns, and interests. At its heart, it's about telling a good story. Through articles, website content, brochures, reports, and other written products I help you inform, engage, and inspire your target audience. Simply put, I find the words to show the world who you are and how you are different. My clients include travel, hospitality, and outdoor recreation companies; cultural organizations; nonprofit organizations working on global issues; and businesses that are striving to be sustainable and socially responsible.

I am also experienced at working with non-native English speakers who want to reach a broader audience. My multilingual skills enable me to work easily with clients from different linguistic backgrounds. I can smooth out stylistic issues in existing English text or create new English content from materials in Swedish, Spanish, French, German, Italian, or Portuguese.
