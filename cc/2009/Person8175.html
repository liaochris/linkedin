Jeff Sliger’s Summary

Over 20 years as a business owner. While trying to find help getting my company to list higher on the Natural or Organic Search Engine Results I found there was a need for this service to business people who like myself spent thousands of dollars every month trying to get online customers. I found a lot of companies that would claim to get better Search Engine Results for my website. They would take my money and work on one element like Keywords, or Doorway Pages, Keyword Saturation, Submitting my Website to supposedly thousands of online Directories and things of that nature. After spending an obscene amount of money my search results did not improve and many times suffered. Frustrated, I began studying to find out how to do it myself. This business is a direct result of my search for this service. We offer a balanced attack on the combined elements that major Search Engines use to determine Search Engine Results. These techniques are not Black Hat practices that could get your business banned from search results altogether. Rather these are the techniques that comply with and are recommended by Search Engines to establish your websites relevance when a query is made about a product or service you offer.

Jeff Sliger’s Specialties:

Online Business Promotion. Improving your business visibility through better natural Search Engine Results. We offer the total package from Web page design or redesign, to optimizing for higher search ranking and off site promotion. We work to promote your online presence so you can focus on running your business.
