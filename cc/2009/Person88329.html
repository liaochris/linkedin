Sandip is presently Manager in KPMG’s IT Advisory Services in India. Sandip participates actively in many global initiatives within the IPBR (Information Protection and Business Resilience) practice of KPMG. Apart from developing service offering around SIEM, he has contributed significantly in developing content / strategy for IT Security Framework and Software Asset Management service offerings.
