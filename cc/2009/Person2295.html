Ajit Singh’s Summary

Hi, I am Ajit Singh. I am one of the founder members of Host Analytics India Operations since May’2001. In 2003, I was inducted on the Board of Directors on Host Analytics India entity. During this, I wore multiple hats ranging from identifying product functionalities, designing application, managing projects, administrations etc.

At Host Analytics, my key work areas are application functionality, relational database / OLAP multidimensional database design and UI design related to Corporate Performance Management (CPM) industry. This typically includes Budgeting, Forecasting, Financial / OLAP Reporting, Balanced Scorecards and Dashboard.

Right now, I am focusing on Analysis Services 2005 OLAP cube optimization, MDX query optimization and Reporting Services integration.

Prior, I have worked for nearly 5 years in various aspects of business like, steel wire products production, chemical vessels manufacturing and quality control/assurance, materials planning & procurement, and credit investment research at a big investment firm in India. By education, I am a Production Engineer and a qualified Chartered Financial Analyst from India.

I stay with my wife, Shweta, at Hyderabad, India.
