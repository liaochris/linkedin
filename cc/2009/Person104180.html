Elliott Augustine’s Summary

I have had the privilege of working with some of the brightest talents at WebXperts.com. Our team of web experts help clients realize their Internet visions on a daily basis. Whether the task is to create a custom intranet that makes a corporation work more efficiently, build a website for an upstart company, or create a web portal that will be used by millions - working to help build the Internet has been my goal and my companies driving mission.

WebXperts has worked with Grammy award winning music producers, Fortune 500 companies, Level 1 trauma centers, and media conglomerates to solve some of their most mission critical needs. We have consistently delivered for over 10 years. The great thing about working on such large scale internet projects is that we get to bring so much experience to small and mid size businesses and service them as they grow. We have always put a strong emphasis on knowledgeable customer service.

My personal computer experience pre-dates the Internet and web design but most of my professional experience revolves around working with the client, managing a team of programmers, or rolling up my sleeves and programming myself. I love to program in .NET and Unix based languages. I never cease to be amazed by the Internet and I truly love what I do.
