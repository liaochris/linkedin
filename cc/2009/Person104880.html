Willie Carter’s Summary

A seasoned operations/quality professional with a record of success in discrete, continuous and batch manufacturing. Experienced in creating customer value for manufacturing and service industries. Accomplished at eliminating waste in all its forms, time, cost, and materials in manufacturing, administrative and management processes.

Some 38 years experience in operations and quality improvements. Interested in helping small to Fortune 1000 companies create customer value by reducing costs, increasing process efficiency, improving quality and increasing the participatory power of their people.

Certifed Lean Sensei, Certified Manger of Quality and Organizational Excellence, and Certified ISO 9000 Lead Assessor. Adjunct Instructor at Roosevelt University teaching Statistics, Operations Management, and Executive Leadership to BA/MBA students. Author of the book "Process Improvement for Administrative Departments."

Willie Carter’s Specialties:

Providing solutions for creating customer value utilizing Lean thinking, cross-functional process mapping/cycle time reduction, quality management systems, and team problem solving/teambuilding.
