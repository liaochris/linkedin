Cardinal Sleep Disorder Centers of America’s Summary

If you're one of the 60 million Americans who have a sleep disorder, your life can be affected in many ways. You may experience poor work and school performance to mood problems, lack of energy and the inability to concentrate. And that's just for starters.

If left untreated, some sleep disorders pose serious threats to your health. They can increase your risk for high blood pressure, heart disease and stroke as well as damage your immune system. Knowing all of this, isn't it time you put your sleep problems to rest?

Here at Cardinal Sleep Disorder Centers of America, that's exactly what we do. Our team of highly qualified sleep disorder professionals can evaluate and treat the full range of sleep disorders.

Our sleep experts include board-certified sleep physicians, and registered technologists. Together, they offer you and your loved ones over 100 years of combined experience.

But we offer you more than credentials and experience. We also offer you the personalized care and compassion you deserve. From your very first visit, you'll be treated like a valued friend. And you'll feel confident knowing that Cardinal is trusted by other doctors, their patients and their families.
