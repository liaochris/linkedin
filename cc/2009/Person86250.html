Ronald Rothhaas’s Summary

I am a marketplace minister "masquerading" as an arborist and business owner with a wide range of industry experience.

Arbor Doctor LLC seeks to serve people ethically and with integrity. We specialize in complete tree and landscape care, consulting, plant health care, and diagnostics.

I am working toward becoming a registered consulting arborist and would like to continue to develop my consulting practice, building relationships with professionals, especially those in the legal and insurance communities.
