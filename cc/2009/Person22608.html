Daniel Vink’s Summary

I have been a management (now senior) consultant since March 2006, all of which at Deloitte. I have participated in projects in quite differentiated industries (aviation, tourism railway, construction, comercial realestate, oil, among others) and concerning diverse competencies. However, my main focus since joining the company has been the transport, with projects in railway and several airlines (mostly flag carriers).

With over two years experience in the airline industry, I have acquired a comprehensive knowledge of the core and IT processes, arquitecture and tendencies of this sector.

I also have extensive knowledge and experience in corporate and function transformation (namely human resources, finance and accounting, sourcing and procurement).

As a consultant, I have had the opportunity to develop constructive thinking, logical information structuring and analysis, and out-of-the-box thinking.
