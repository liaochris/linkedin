Richard Collin’s Summary

Recognized as one of the European leader and expert in the e-transformation of organization, collaborative work , enterprise 2.0, social networking and KM domains, Richard D. Collin is an authority within the business community and IT people which support the change toward knowledge based and social networked organization along the appropriate usage of relevant 2.0 technologies.

Using vision to frame action and leadership to shape actionable vision, Richard D. Collin performs in understanding and managing complexity of the business, e-transformation of organization and KM, through large projects, new forms of organization or strategic consulting, support and coaching. While mastering technologies such as web 2.0, and in dealing with its conceptual and creative capabilities, and in promoting effective actions, he anticipates and he is acting to achieve and to monitor planned results. The global multinational environment and senior management people he is strategically and excellent to cope with, helped him overtime to establish and to consolidate a unique network of companies, institutions and individuals to renew and to maintain its knowledge while strengthening its expertise and entrepreneurship capabilities.
Beyond his practice, Richard D. Collin is heading the Enterprise 2.0 Institute, holding the Chaire "Collective efficiency, collaborative working environment & networked organiszation" at Grenoble Ecole de Management together with the Centre for Knowledge & Intangible Economy"
