Atul Karmarkar’s Summary

BU Head, Blogger, Technology buff, Investor

Have been a part of the service & hospitality industry for most of my working life, handling functions related to Operations, Sales & Marketing, Technology and HR, leading and managing teams with extremely diverse backgrounds, abilities, thoughts and cultures.
