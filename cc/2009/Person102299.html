Victoria Bracewell Lewis’s Summary

Originally, a traditional graphic designer and art director, my career took a left turn in 1988 when I was first introduced to AOL. The new world order that the Internet and eBusiness offered changed both my professional, as well as personal life. As they say, there was no turning back. From that point, work and to some extent even the personal life, focused on eBusiness/eCommerce and interactive marketing for the online retailing and entertainment sectors in the USA, Europe and emerging markets in Central Europe and Asia.
