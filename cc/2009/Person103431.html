Russell Volckmann III’s Summary

Deutsch, 日本語 : OK

strategic thinker, storyteller, positioner, articulator, creator

I build successful brands and solid brand experiences and I love what I do. I have helped entrepreneurs, small companies, Fortune 500 companies, and global companies tell their stories through successful brand, visual identity, marketing advertising, and social strategies.

In the past 17 years, I have held director and executive advisory level roles for brand and marketing strategy on both client and agency sides, and enjoyed every single one of those roles. Frequently invited to speak at universities. Equally expert as Creative Director, Executive Producer, Account Director or other brand/ marketing/ creative strategy.

Russell’s goals include building a great team of visionary & savvy brand heroes to deliver innovative strategy, design, and message solutions that win customer loyalty. From strategic marketing & brand development, interactive experience, designing creative, to leading & mentoring the team—I offer a full range of creative & strategic service skills to help companies grow & prosper.

Creative successes include marketing, advertising and branding for such organizations as Landor Associates, FCB San Francisco, L'ORÉAL Japan, AT&T Japan, the San Francisco Asian Art Museum, Ziff-Davis Publishing/ Smart Business Magazine, ZDNET, TechTV, Hewlett-Packard, Palm, Rainforest Action Network, the Grateful Dead, Pink Floyd, the Brady Bunch, Fetzer, Fujitsu Japan, Ikonic Interactive, Red Dot Interactive, N.E.T., Geffen Records, Bank of America, Charles Schwab, Sun Microsystems, E*Trade, Cylink, KM Disney, Inc., Emirates Airlines, McDonald’s, CNN Headline News, more...
