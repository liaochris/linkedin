mario boon’s Summary

For 10 years now I have been writing and drawing picture books, board books, children's books and comicbooks for multiple publishers and packagers in Belgium and abroad.
I also have drawn hundreds of illustrations for schoolbooks, newspapers, advertising campaigns, stationery, websites and more.

My work has been published in Belgium, Holland, Poland, Russia, Japan, Australia, Brazil and the USA by De Eenhoorn, Standaard Uitgeverij, Balloon Books, C4Ci, Image Comics, IDW. I have won a couple of awards for my cartoons and picture books.

mario boon’s Specialties:

I specialise in drawing and designing books geared towards toddlers, preschool and first grade kids.
