Martin Burns’s Summary

Adaptable, creative, focused recruiter. I tend to stay on the bleeding edge of best practices and technologies that impact recruiting, but I'm old school enough to pound the phone (you know - it's that thing on your desk with all the buttons & numbers that occasionally beeps at you). I'm metrics driven, as well as intuitive in my approaches. Fairly well right-left brain balanced.

Currently, I'm focused on finding top talent for ZoomInfo, a fast growing, highly disruptive business information company outside of Boston.

When I'm bored - which I loathe - I put on a couple of different hats:
-Maintain our @ZoomInfo Twitter alias as well as my semi-pro @recruitermoe alias
-Blog at recruiter.wordpress.com (recruiting trends, and advice for job seekers)
-Created and maintain our onboarding strategy
-Write chunks of the copy for our About site
-Oversee recruitment product marketing (newsletter, setting up webinars on recruiting products, conference strategy, thought leadership, customer advisory board, etc)
-Expansion design and implementation (designed 50% physical growth in our headquarters, negotiated with vendors, oversaw contractors, selected and negotiated pricing on furniture, actively - i.e., swung a hammer, turned a screwdriver - involved with physical construction and employee move)

Outside of ZoomInfo, I'm involved with the recruiting community. I contribute to a variety of recruiting oriented thought leadership groups, provide beta testing and product feedback to new recruiting technology companies, attend national trade shows, and speak at industry events.

Martin Burns’s Specialties:

Social media search & optimization, name sourcing, relationship building, employment branding, cheeky recruitment ads, career-site design, SEO/ SEM. Industries I know well are: internet search, finance, actuarial, education, publishing, bio & pharma. Bit of a grab bag. I've worked both sides of the agency desk, as well corporate recruiting, and had a stints as an entrepreneur & in publishing. It's all been interesting.
