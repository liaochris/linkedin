Peter Korchnak’s Summary

I'm a sustainable marketer, blogger, and speaker.

My company, Semiosis Communications, is a sustainable marketing consultancy helping socially responsible organizations optimize their triple-bottom line.

On my Sustainable Marketing Blog, I explore the intersection of marketing, sustainability, community, and social media.
