Lucio Dias Ribeiro’s Summary

Online marketing is my passion. I love what I do. For others work is a daily grind. For me it is a passion. I am constantly evolving my personal knowledge and love learning about new online technologies and trends.
Indeed, in my spare time I run my own blog to ensure my knowledge remains current - www.marketingeasy.net. In June 2008 I was recognized as one of the 30 most influential online marketing bloggers in Australia and in 2009 again.

In 2009 I was voted the 6th World's most trusted SEO/SEM
professional by Internet Business Magazine and one of the top Internet Marketing professionals by Marketing Today website www.marketingtoday.com/

I am a strategically minded Online Marketing professional who works effectively as a liaison between management and implementation disciplines.

With more than 15 years Marketing experience including Advertising agencies Ammiratis Puris Lintas, McCann-Erickson, Lowe, and Sun MRM, I worked with global (Unilever, GM, Microsoft, AMRO Bank and more) and local brands in Australia and Brazil.

In 2008 I openned my own Startup company - The Online Circle where I share my online marketing passion with Jeff Richardson, and share the philosophy of seeing the world through the clients eye’s and avoiding Internet clichés and jargon.
