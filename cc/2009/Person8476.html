Charles Harris’s Summary

As of January 2008, I have elected to change careers with the blessing and support of my wife to follow my dream to become a full-time football coach. As with any drastic career change, I and my family have made some concessions. However, it is our hope that in two years time I will be able to step-up and run my own program.

To help supplement my income, I will continue to consult at a part-time level for local businesses and government agencies. Please visit www.CharlesHarris.net for more information.

Prior to 2008, I was the CEO of Astrixs, LLC.; originally founded in 1994 as a business development and technology consulting firm. I had successfully built a client base that spanned the global community with more than 30 clients with a cumulative annual budget of a quarter million dollars to manage projects.

Currently I am the Offensive and Defensive Lines coach for Ravenna High School, a coach for Ravenna Youth Football League and coach at the Edge Sports Performance Academy. I am also the Coaching Director for the Ravenna Youth Football League and have been elected as a three year trustee on the Ravenna GridIron Board.
