Scott Bane’s Summary

I have had specific experience on a large church staff in and around Christian education.
• Developing a day care center
• Administrating a Christian school
• Writing and teaching curriculum for an adult
During that time, I came to see the value of building networks of learners and connecting people through educational content. My life in eLearning began to emerge.

I came to Sevenstar Academy because I could see that shared vision for connecting learners through online courses. My specific input is in the areas of design and delivery and as the Learning Management System (LMS) expert. The best educational content is of no use if it cannot be effectively accessed because of poor design and delivery.

I have specific experience working with Desire 2 Learn LMS, Moodle and most recently ANGEL LMS. I also have experience consulting with schools and colleges about implementing online solutions to their offerings. Specifically, developing earned income strategies around online learning.

Scott Bane’s Specialties:

Getting started in online learning, Instructional Design, ANGEL LMS training, New student orientation, Online teacher training, How to build layers of interaction into online courses.
