Stacia Kelly’s Summary

Balanced.

On the one hand, I work with clients to help get their minds and bodies in line with their goals. Those goals being everything from fitness and nutrition to healing and health. The stress of the IT industry pushed me to a PhD in Holistic Health and I gain pleasure from helping others heal and learn to balance. That means teaching children the joy and focus of yoga and martial arts, to helping adults learn to relax down and visualize their goals, whether they be healing for my stroke victims, better health for my smoking and weight loss clients, or better athletic performances for my athletes.

On the other, I am a proposal and MS Word Diva. My sales support teams term, not mine. *grins* I believe we all have some sort of OCD in our lives, and mine, is documents! I love working with RFPs and teams who want to respond to win. But I'm picky. I expect the relationship to be there and for the team to be willing to write to the best of their ability. In a sales support role, I work on things like RFPs, technical white papers, to website designs and marketing materials. It spans the writing realm.

If you look closely you will see the high impact stress of one life lead to the need for a balanced outlook in another, and they have perfectly blended to allow me to help in two realms. I can manage a proposal with low impact and high results, and I can work with the toughest clients helping them REALLY calm down, focus, and achieve the goals they want and need to achieve.

Stacia Kelly’s Specialties:

Electic: proposals, writing in all realms, health, fitness, meditation, yoga, marital arts, marketing, web design and really listening to the wants, needs and desires of a client in ANY realm to translate that into help, health and sales.
