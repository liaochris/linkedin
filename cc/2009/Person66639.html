Melvin Rivera’s Summary

Originally starting in print, Melvin Rivera has been developing websites for an ever growing list of clients that include Sony Pictures, MGM, Warner Bros., FOX and Universal.

Over the span of 12 years he has played the role of creative director, designer, programmer, project manager and even founded a small boutique design company. He has a keen interest in technology and codes neatly by hand css, actionscript, php, javascript and objective-c.

Actively seeking opportunities.

Melvin Rivera’s Specialties:

design: color, composition and typography. code: php, javascript, actionscript, wordpress, open apis, social web, iphone development
