Rob Emms’s Summary

I am an experienced and successful online business professional, always looking for a new challenge. I have worked with small and large businesses, startups, self-employed product distributors and folks from all walks of life. I love to get involved with online startups in need of a multi tasking, energetic, well connected, optimistic, problem solver with a “can do” attitude and a nose for online success. I enjoy life and have a fun outlook whilst enjoying the finer things in life. I bring with me a network of specialists in SEO, SEM, RSS, WEB 2.0, Affiliate Marketing, Online Marketing, Web Analytics, Web Monitoring, Web Testing, Web Development, Web Design and IT.

Rob Emms’s Specialties:
