Ellerton Whitney, MBA’s Summary

A well-rounded, Adwords-certified search engine marketing professional, Ellerton has performed search engine marketing in numerous verticals, including health care, debt management, B2C/B2B tech, education, and many others. He has managed projects with clients ranging from the Fortune 50 all the way down to small business.

Ellerton is an expert in data analysis, fully understand search engine marketing's value chain. He uses his expertise in Excel amongst other tools to execute the proper implementation of complex paid search campaigns involving multi-variate testing, ad creative testing, and different keyword mixes across multiple search engines. He understands what kind of search strategy it requires to break through the clutter.

In addition to SEM, Ellerton has experience search engine optimizing content for a number of web properties, including Motor Trend & Automobile Mag. As an avid car enthusiast, he orchestrated social media marketing campaigns utilizing Web 2.0 sites such as Reddit, Digg, Yahoo! Answers, and bulletin boards to populate links and build traffic.

Ellerton earned his M.B.A. in Marketing from SDSU, specializing in Branding, Market Research, and Consumer Behavior, amongst other subjects. He also earned his B.A. from UCSD.
