John Cave’s Experience

  • Sustainable Materials Manager

    EH Smith (Builders Merchants) Ltd

    (Privately Held; Construction industry)

    April 2009 — Present (9 months)

    As part of my Strategic Development role, I was asked to look into the market for Sustainable Building materials. After 18 months of research with numerous Architects, Contractors, Manufacturers, Industry Organisations and Academics we have launched our Sustainable Building Materials offer.

  • Chairman for FORCE (Forum for Constructing Excellence) in Coventry and Warwickshire

    Constructing Excellence

    (Construction industry)

    March 2009 — Present (10 months)

    FORCE, the Forum for Constructing Excellence, was formed following a period of consultation which started in late 2000, between local construction leaders and the local Learning and Skills Council.

    The proposal was that a Forum was needed, to not only address serious issues regarding skills and labour shortage, but also to take forward a spirit of change in the industry, a spirit that would embody the philosophy of the Government’s report, Rethinking Construction.

    Since 2000, FORCE has evolved into the local Best Practice and Constructing Excellence Club, and is now encompassed within the Constructing Excellence movement nationally, as well as the West Midlands Centre for Constructing Excellence regionally.

    Membership is open to all stakeholders in the construction industry – from training providers and colleges, through suppliers, contractors and consultants, to property agents, clients and end users.

    The Forum’s objectives galvanise our Industry players to try to work together to improve our Industry through sharing best practice, developing business through networking, and encourage business development through opportunity sharing and training.

  • Strategic Business Development

    EH Smith (Builders Merchants) Ltd

    (Privately Held; Construction industry)

    March 2006 — Present (3 years 10 months)

    Founded in 1922, EH Smith is now one of the largest privately owned building materials suppliers to the construction industry in the UK with a turnover of almost £110m (2007/8)

    We have been involved in major building projects for almost a century and have an established pedigree that demonstrates our continued commitment to meeting the aesthetic, technical and commercial requirements of specifiers employed on multi-million pound contracts throughout the UK.

    My focus is 'Strategic Business Development.' In plain english, I look for opportunities and then develop a strategy to make them happen. I do a lot of work with Masterplan Architects, City Planning and Regeneration teams and large building contractors.

    One of those Strategic Developments is the development of a range of materials to meet the needs of Clients, Architects, Specifiers and Contractors when constructing 'Sustainable Buildings.' EH Smith Sustainable Products is the result of a collaboration between the maufacturers, architects, clients and contractors that we work with to ensure that we have a relevant and diverse choice of products.

    The range is in stock from September 2009 and covers wall construction, roofing, timber, insulation, airtightness and moisture management, landscaping, renewable technology, solar lighting, drainage and water conservation and underfloor heating so please contact me for further details.
