Vandy Massey’s Summary

Founder and CEO of MSA Interactive Ltd (relaunched as Engauge in 2008). Specialists in 360 degree feedback, online performance tools, appraisals and employee engagement.

We work with companies who want to engage their people. We help them to improve engagement levels and collective leadership in order to develop a sustainable culture that allows employees to shape the future of their companies.

For me, the key is in creating exactly the right solution for each organisation. We measure the competencies critical to the organisation’s success, provide support for HR and training departments and make the process of personal development easier for all participants - discovering those ‘Aha!’ moments of insight in the results.

Our professional reputation is based on successfully creating and delivering assessment solutions. We have become the trusted advisor to many of the world's most respected companies in the area of performance measurement.

Vandy Massey’s Specialties:

Employee engagement, performance assessments, 360 degree feedback, appraisals, employee opinion measurements, team performance assessment, competency frameworks, ability profiles
