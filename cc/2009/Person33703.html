Becky Fox’s Summary

Have you tried losing weight and keeping it off with little success?

Fox Fitness is passionate about helping busy professionals and stay-at-home women reach their fitness goals, increase their energy levels, and reduce their stress by creating a customized workout and nutrition plan that can be done at your home, office, or the park. Each program is based on the clients individual goals and needs, and thus no two workouts are ever the same. Having a professional come to you eliminates wasted time driving to the gym and overly crowded workout areas.

Fox Fitness has been featured on the WBIR style show, the Knoxville News Sentinel, the Farragut Press, and the Knoxville Business Journal. I write a various articles for the Knoxville News Sentinel Fit Knoxville Blog, Diva Guide, Power Systems, Isuckatgolf.com, and Magazine Blu.

Don’t live in Knoxville? No problem, Fox Fitness also offer online and phone based personal training programs to fit your needs wherever you may be. We will work with you to create a workout customized to your needs. Our goal is to motivate you, help you enjoy your workouts, and teach new and healthy lifestyle habits.

In addition, to training, Fox Fitness has partnered up with HardKnox Bootcamp to bring you an outdoor fitness bootcamp class designed to get you in the best shape of your life. Various locations and class times available!

In addition to running a business, I am also an Optimum Nutrition and American Bodybuilding figure competitor and fitness model. I have been on the covers of Power Systems catalogs and Magazine Blu. I am looking to book shoots for fitness and commercial work. I'm also helping to promote the East Tennessee Sports Expo Figure and Bodybuilding Show that will be held in October 2009.
