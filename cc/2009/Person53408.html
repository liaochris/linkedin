Karla (With a K) Edward’s Summary

An Internet and multimedia professional. KarlaWithaK.net provides Internet marketing and computer education for classroom, one-on-one tutoring or small group environments. Tutoring in English, Grammar/Phonics and Social Studies is also available.

Operates ProClubGear.com, an online distributor of Pro Club brand urban wear, is a subsidiary of karlawithak.net.

As an Interviewer/Host/Emcee, Karla With a K, provides compelling, entertaining, humorous conversation, commentary and meaningful discussion on American History, Politics, Public Education, Health, Relationships and the media.

Karla With a K, is a member of the Screen Actors Guild and the American Federation of Radio and Televison Artists where she served six years as Announcer Rep. on the L. A. Local Board of Directors.
