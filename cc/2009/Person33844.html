Trevor Hart’s Summary

Trevor has been involved in franchising for over 15 years. Originally as a marketing executive for a national franchise chain and then moving onto franchise development and recruitment. Trevor setup the international franchise website www.franchiseek.com in 1999 and went onto publish Franchise Focus magazine and a range of franchising e-books.

Trevor launched the Franchisee Association in August 2008 and is actively involved to promoting franchising globally.
