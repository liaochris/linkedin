Dino Baskovic’s Summary

Corporate public relations manager for a global, multi-billion dollar direct sales conglomerate.

Principal of a technology-savvy, multidisciplinary agency serving the needs of corporate PR professionals, public relations firms and their clients.

Holds over a decade of experience providing strategic counsel, online methodologies and program management. Meshes well with grounded, ambitious goal-seekers that appreciate innovative approaches to creative communications and marketing.

Former graduate web design professor at Lawrence Technological University in Southfield, Michigan.
