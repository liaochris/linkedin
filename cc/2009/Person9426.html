Barbara Rozgonyi’s Summary

About Barbara Rozgonyi . . . edgy, grounded, prophetic

If you’re on a quest for more media attention, better search engine visibility, closer customer communications or a greater following as a subject matter expert, you’re in the right place.

I’m Barbara Rozgonyi, founder of CoryWest Media, LLC and conversation host at wiredPRworks.

I’d like to help you manage your image, promote your products and tell your story.

Here's how:
- analyze and transform your marketing with strategic marketing/PR consulting services designed to attract your optimal community
- craft, create and distribute social media news/press releases to expand your reach online and generate attention
- design blog and online newsroom architectures to differentiate your brand and connect you with your community
- deliver public speaking programs and workshops that advance your audience's knowledge with actionable skills

Every effort is designed to help my readers, customers or audience members become better communicators and storytellers. Your success is my professional trail in life.
