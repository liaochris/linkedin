Sergio Bravo’s Summary

In 1989, Smith Smith & Smith [aka S3] began as a small advertising and design office catering to the booming surf industry. Companies like Gotcha, Rollerblade, Surfing Magazine, Volleyball Magazine and other action sports companies, benefited from S3's vast surf culture experience. Soon after, S3 became involved in the bicycle, motorcycle and automotive industries and has maintained a presence in these categories for the last 18 years. The experience gained in these fields is not only professional but comes from an enthusiast level as well. “Our staff here at S3 has always had some level of personal involvement on an enthusiast level,” says agency Founder and Creative Director Sergio Bravo. ”This serves as an invaluable resource for us and our clients.” says Bravo
S3 has created successful advertising and/or marketing materials for Suzuki, Kawasaki, Toyota, GM/Pontiac, Suomy Helmets, Motorcycle Industry Council, MOMO Racing, GT Bicycles, Troy Lee Designs, Devinci Cycles and Marzocchi Suspension just to name a few.
Smith Smith & Smith is located in Hermosa Beach, California. S3 services include advertising, brand strategy, design, identity, packaging, POP, interactive, web, merchandise design and public relations. For more information visit >www.smith3x.com< or call 310.379.1799.

Sergio Bravo’s Specialties:
