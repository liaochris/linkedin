Sean Yeomans’s Summary

I've had an affiliation to computers and their uses since the early 1980's my first experience/exposure being a TRS-80, and Apple II. It wasn't until 1994 that I decided to treat my hobby as a career, whereupon I earned a Diploma of Multimedia Design and Communications at the Applied Multimedia Training Centre in Winnipeg.

Since 1995, I have been a: Digital Image editor, Interactive desktop/web app developer, 3D modeler, programmer and scripter for web, site designer and information architect, teacher and consultant.

My goals are to continually maintain and improve my knowledge and experience in the field in order to continue as a an IT Professional, and thereby continue to be paid doing what I otherwise do for fun.
