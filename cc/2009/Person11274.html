Bill Champitto’s Summary

Bill is currently a the Executive Director for Creative Boston Design Studio, a boutique firm focused on Creative Direction, Identity Profile and Design Services in eMedia, Print and Multi-Media for small to medium sized companies. In this role, Bill has also support the founding of Leader Networks with partner Vanessa DiMauro focusing on helping companies leverage social network and virtual community practices for business development and customer relations. Bill Champitto graduated from Berklee College of Music in Boston with a Bachelors Degree in Music Performance in 1984, and also holds an Associated Degree for Business Management from SUNY.

Bill entered the technology field in 1984 as a designer of interfaces for on-line business applications at both W.R. Grace and Investors Bank and Trust. He moved into consulting at Keane, Inc. and became the Managing Director of their e-Services business unit as it grew from $56M to $231M between 1996 and 1999. Bill moved to become Managing Director at Primix Solutions, an internet based consulting firm helping clients translate their brand to the web for extending sales and marketing channels, knowledge management development and focused communities.

In 2002 Bill became a founder member of Maxiom Consulting Group, supporting the business transformation of emerging life sciences bringing new products to market. In 2005 Bill founded Creative Boston Design Studios, LLC. As the Executive Creative Director, Bill helps companies create an environment that brings their image and brand to life through corporate identity framework development, directing and designing graphics for marketing and training materials, websites, seminars and tradeshows, and designs for executive offices and conference rooms.
