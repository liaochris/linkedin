Rachel Pastirik’s Summary

Rachel Pastirik is a web designer and developer, or as she puts it, a "web architect." Much like the famous architect Frank Lloyd Wright, Pastirik combines aesthetics and science to create web and application experiences that are both pleasing to see and to interact with.

Since 1998, Pastirik's firm has architected more than 100 stable and secure web sites in your neighborhood and around the globe. Some of the more entertaining and challenging projects include:

* A political campaign in the Republic of El Salvador
* A daily newspaper serving 19 counties in Virginia
* An Austrian woodworking machinery distributor
* A foundation headed by tennis pro Venus Williams
* An event for the Marine Corps League
* Projects for universities, small and medium size businesses, and consultants

In 2003, Pastirik received a B.S. in the Media field, with a Web concentration, and a minor in Information Technology from Radford University. During this time she spent three years directing and leading a team of forty students in the production of Radford University's weekly online magazine. She also taught classes in the Computer Science Department, and chaired the Student Media Committee - all while working towards her own degree.

As a community activist, sponsor, and educator Rachel Pastirik is not shy when she steps up to the plate to participate in community activities. Led by her own courage, loyalty, and sense of duty to help people, Pastirik leads with integrity vision, and an unstoppable belief that "helping each other really makes a difference."

Pastirik's consulting firm, Netdrafter, serves as an outlet for her creative and scientific interests. Pastirik's mission is to provide attractive, high-value, web services that offer affordable big company technology for anyone who wants to have a web presence. Netdrafted companies just can't say enough about how happy they are with Netdrafter.
