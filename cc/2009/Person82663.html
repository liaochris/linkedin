Rebecca Corliss’s Summary

I am a member of the HubSpot marketing team, specializing in creative content development. I also manage HubSpot's events and sponsorships, and I am a co-producer of HubSpot TV.

My quirky talent includes creating music videos. The first music video I made for HubSpot received 40,000 views in its first week.

To view it, visit: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4-lGe5MnBlY

In my free time, I sing and perform with my a cappella singing ensemble called Common Sound. I founded the group in the summer of 2008. I also write for RebeccaCorliss.com, a blog that celebrates social media and music.
