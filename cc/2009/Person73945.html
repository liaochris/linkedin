Nick Gregory’s Summary

Nick is the CEO and founder of Market Accelerator (htp://www.marketxl.co.uk), a leading UK IT marketing agency specialising in market strategy, resources development and campaign execution services to build our client's market presence and sales pipeline.

Prior to this, Nick was a co-founder and Sales and Marketing Director of Portico Software, a manufacturing intelligence software company, and has also enjoyed successful roles as Vice President of Mediasurface Plc and UK Marketing Director for Oracle, as well as other sales and marketing management roles for technology companies.

Nick is an experienced IT executive with a total of 20 years IT industry experience (16 of these in sales and marketing).

Nick Gregory’s Specialties:

Nick's portfolio or skills include Strategic Marketing, Market Positioning and Strategy, Market Campaign development and execution, Marketing resources development, Competitive Analysis, Business Development and Start Ups.
