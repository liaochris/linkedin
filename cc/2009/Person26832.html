Matthew Warren’s Summary

I'm the founder & CEO of Radar Multimedia, an award-winning multi-disciplinary corporate communications firm that specializes in the development and production of multimedia projects worldwide. We help our clients discover new and creative ways to tell their story using audio, video, web, print and live events. Radar was founded in 2004 and within three years became a million dollar company.

I've produced projects in more than twelve countries including the Philippines, Uganda and Peru. Some of Radar's recent projects were for Walmart, DIRECTV, Ubisoft, Universal Studios Hollywood, JAFRA Cosmetics International, Public Storage and the SDPA.

As a Writer-Director-Producer:

I co-wrote, co-produced and directed "The KREW" (a children's DVD starring Candace Cameron Bure); I co-produced "Stop & Think" with Francis Chan; I directed two award-winning short films for Interthinx: "FSI" in 2007 and "Fraud Angels" in 2008 and recently directed "The Invitation" for ForgottenStories.org. I'm a co-producer at Skip1.org. My other films include "The Cylinder" "Tumbleweed" "Jack'd" and several webisodes for CarCrazyCentral.com. I'm Matthew Warren (III) on IMDb.com.
