Rhea Perry’s Summary

While home educating my 7 children, I've developed a unique educational philosophy that has been called Entrepreneurial Education. We focus on encouraging students to be self-educating and to create home businesses that leverage the power of the Internet.

I've been called the Community Queen because I encourage entrepreneurs of all ages to network with each other online and at professional conferences. Our first Yahoo! Group became famous when it produced an annual international home business conference.

My team and I coach students to develop a mindset for success, seek specific vision for their lives, get specialized education and align themselves with successful mentors in particular areas.

Our goal is to help America become financially free, one family at a time.
