rick whitworth’s Summary

Rick is the founder and started Merged Media in 2003. He graduated with a degree in economics from Principia College, and brings a strong professional background in accounting and information systems. Rick worked at NRT, Coldwell Banker's parent company, for nine years, starting out in accounting and working his way up to the position of Systems Analyst within the Information Systems department.

His skills include HTML, JavaScript, SQL, Mysql, Access, JSP, ASP, PHP, Red Hat Linux Web Server setup and configuration, data base management, DNS, e-mail setup, and much more.

Rick also helps run MorSystems.com, a family owned business that hosts mortgage websites for thousands of clients, as VP/Technical Director.
