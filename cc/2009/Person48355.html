Joe Castillo’s Summary

Joe has spent 21 years in advertising, founder of the Advertising Library, a full service agency in Knoxville, TN. His personal accomplishments include a series of fine art, pen and ink illustrations authoring a book and numerous paintings.

Joe Castillo is now traveling and inspiring people with live presenttions of his "SandStory" artwork.
