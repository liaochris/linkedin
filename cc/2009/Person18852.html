Chris Veness’s Summary

Chris Veness is something of an information junkie. He loves designing, managing and manipulating information.

Much of today’s world is drowning in data. Chris loves turning dumb data into intelligent information.

Information is so pervasive, this can take him in many directions. Largely, but not always, it’s websites – medieval Welsh manuscripts to management reporting, air traffic management to sports coaching.

Chris Veness’s Specialties:

MySQL, MS SQL Server, Oracle, Postgres, MS-Access; PHP, ASP, ColdFusion; JavaScript; XML, XSLT. Geodesy / mapping. Information design, usability, UI design.
