Dan Steiner’s Summary

Helping small business owners and entrepreneurs manage the financial side of their businesses. Focus on providing part-time bookkeeping and accounting services at affordable rates, typically replacing the higher cost of a CPA.

I have worked in public accounting and held high level corporate controller and CFO positions. After identifying a need in the market, I decided to go out on my own and start my own company. I really enjoy working on the "front lines" with owners and seeing the direct results of my efforts.

I want Steiner Business Solutions to be known as the best available resource for small business owners.
