Steve Ganz’s Summary

If you are interested in knowing about Publishing Industry opportunities, please send me an invitation to connect to sganz@uspublishingjobs.com.

Personnel Associates, Inc. has specialized in the placement of people in the publishing industry for over 20 years. The firm was founded by Marge Crawford and Ed Wells in 1982. Marge was a former English teacher who became familiar with the industry through using textbooks, produced by what are now our clients.

Steve Ganz has 18 years of solid experience in printing, publishing, advertising and marketing for Fortune 1000 companies, as well as sales and computer expertise.

Steve Ganz’s Specialties:

Assisting educational publishing companies such as Prentice Hall, McGraw-Hill, Elsevier, Sage Publishing, Taylor & Francis, Delmar Learning, Course Technology and many others in finding the quality people they need to develop, market, produce and sell their products.
