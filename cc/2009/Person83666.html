Richard MacKinnon’s Summary

I have been working as an Occupational Psychologist in the UK for the past 10 years, working as both an in-house and external consultant, in public and private sector environments.

I am a senior consultant with Talent Q (www.talentqgroup.com), specialising in online assessment solutions and talent management consulting.

Inspired and developed by Roger Holdsworth, Talent Q offers leading edge online psychometric assessment and a new way of applying psychometrics in talent management.

Traditionally many organisations have used assessment in a piecemeal way, with little connection between assessment for recruitment, development, teambuilding and identifying potential. Talent Q changes this through an innovative system mapped across the talent lifecycle.

We deliver tailored solutions combining our online technology, blended learning and consulting capabilities.
