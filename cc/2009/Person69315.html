Mitchell Fox’s Summary

Management strategy consultant with experience serving clients in the USA, Latin America, Europe, Australia, and the Middle East. Professional experience in the automotive, biotechnology, pharmaceuticals, telecommunications, financial services, and medical devices industries, as well as the social and public sectors.

Near-term career objectives include gaining strategy and operating experience in venture-backed consumer businesses (internet, services, and cleantech) as well as investment experience in venture capital. In the future, I hope to build entrepreneurial startup businesses as a founder or business partner.
