When Trust Matters ...

A precondition for success in leadership: identification of (in-)formal key figures, facilitating top-level contacts leading to trustworthy relationships between executives as well as information sharing for equitable and sustainable agreements.

Since 1990s I am engaged in supporting top-level executives from German Government, NATO, EU, industry and banking to implement goals in international relations and business. I was assigned as a trainer for intercultural communication for special forces, police, intelligence officers, MPs and government executives to improve continually handling and understanding of local culture, politics, business and decision making processes. Occasionally I do lectures at Universities and publications to share my experiences with scholars.

Based on my previous jobs I can provide you for successful business and politics in transition countries and crisis or (post-)conflict areas a large network of established contacts with key-figures and service providers (attorneys, PR, media, logistic, real estate, translation, human resources and security). They are experts with long time living experiences in relevant countries or competent native experts.

Working on the interface of cultures in transition countries, crisis and conflict areas depends highly on professional competences in intercultural communication, change, knowledge and conflict-/crisis management I am specialized in
