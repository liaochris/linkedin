Angela Risner’s Summary

Angela has worked in the corporate training world for over 10 years now. She has developed her own classes and materials for many of those years, which enabled her to tailor her training to each customer's individual needs.

Angela has developed such classes as Listening Skills, Getting Things Done (based on David Allen's landmark book), Personal Career Development, and many others. She has facilitated such classes as Emotional Intelligence, Communications Skills, Customer Service, Time Management, and Coaching.

She is a member of the ASTD and is working on obtaining certification through Langevin during 2009. Her unique combination of a legal education with years of experience as a trainer allows Angela to work confidently with many levels of employees and management.

Instructional Design, Facilitation Techniques, Interpersonal Skills, Computer Skills
