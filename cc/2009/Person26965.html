Dj Heckes’s Summary

Look up the word “go-getter” in the dictionary and you might as well see a picture of D.J. Heckes, owner of EXHIB-IT! Tradeshow Marketing Experts.
While raising three children, D.J. started selling exhibits out of her home for Seattle, Washington-based Exhibit Design. Before long, D.J. was able to purchase the New Mexico and Texas market territories from her employer and establish her own company. “I wanted to make a difference in my children’s lives,” she says, “and also use my ambition and drive to make a difference in my own life and that of my clients.” Now, EXHIB-IT! is in its 9th successful business year.
CEO/Owner of EXHIB-IT!, a GSA Certified, woman-owned small business headquartered in Albuquerque, NM. EXHIB-IT! was founded and organized by DJ Heckes, for the express purpose of providing quality products with exhibit marketing and design services to federal agencies and commercial enterprises.

Dj Heckes’s Specialties:

EXHIB-IT! Tradeshow Marketing experts is a trade show marketing firm that specializes in displays, design, graphics, and client services related to exhibiting like rentals, training, storage and I & D.

Dj Heckes’s Interests:

Born and raised in North Carolina, DJ is goal-oriented and strives to succeed both personally and professionally. She is a wife and mother of three children, holds an Associate Degree in PreLaw and a BA in Health Care Management. She is also a Hot Air Balloon Pilot and a Competitive Soccer Player.
