Mary Kay Hopkins’s Summary

Over 30 years in residential real estate brokerage. Owned Mary Kay Hopkins, Inc. for 25 years, a Lake Charles area real estate firm with significant relocation related business. Strengths include utilzing technology to the maximum, especially real estate internet marketing, introducing new marketing concepts, negotiating, managing, and training.

Certified by the Louisiana Real Estate Commission as a broker and educator.

Mary Kay Hopkins, LLC represents buyers or sellers, but does not engage in disclosed dual agency.

The Mary Kay Hopkins Resoucre Center assists individuals, firms, and associations with building and/or maintaining their websites.

Mary Kay Hopkins’s Specialties:

Utilization of real estate technology, training, build and maintain websites, real estate buyer or seller representation.
