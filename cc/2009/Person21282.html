Charlie Terry’s Summary

Turnaround and go-to-market executive with hands-on experience running both public and private companies, integrating acquisitions, managing and driving sales channels, re-positioning company and products, designing and executing go-to-market plans and implementing strategic alliances.

+President of MarketResearch.com: doubled revenue to $40+ via acquisition.

+CEO of COMTEX News Network: turnaround had never been profitable with stock at $0.10. Took to $5.00+, 5 straight profitable years, 6x revenue growth
