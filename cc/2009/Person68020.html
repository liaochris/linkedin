Michelle Fischer’s Summary

Inspired by helping people to achieve their best, both personally and professionally, Michelle joined Creative Leadership as co-Director and Talent Management Consultant in December 2007, following a merger with her previous business New Horizon Associates. Prior to this she worked within Resourcing and Recruitment at a variety of complex Professional Services, agency and outsourced organisations including Portman Building Society, Fidelity Investments, PwC and LV.

Michelle is BPS level 2 qualified, an NLP Master Practitioner and holds a Certificate in Coaching from the Coaching Academy. She is a member of The ANLP, NLP Dorset, the British Psychological Society, and Business Exchange Wessex. She is also a volunteer mentor for the Prince’s Trust.
