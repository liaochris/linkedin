David Green’s Summary

I established NetGreen Consulting, Inc., an Internet marketing and consulting company, in the Hilton Head, SC, area in 2005. We provide ecommerce-capable "self-service" websites for small to medium sized businesses and non-profit organizations, as well as Common Criteria Certification (CCC) and FIPS 140-2 Validation consulting and project management services. I am also developing a network of independent contractors to work with me in expanding a product brokerage and Internet marketing business implementing a franchise type of business system.

I have provided websites for a growing clientele in Hilton Head/Bluffton, SC, Savannah, GA, and other areas. I specialize in setting up small to medium businesses and non-profit organizations with ecommerce-capable websites that they can maintain and update themselves, add unlimited pages or catalog items, and make changes any time they want. Toll-free technical support as well as SEO services and my personal customer training are both available.

I have led three successful CCC evaluation projects for major networking vendors, written a number of white papers and implementation guides, provided editorial reviews for two networking books, supported an NMS application platform, trained engineers as a Master Certified Novell Instructor, and designed, installed, and supported enterprise networks since 1986. I'm currently working on a FIPS 140-2 validation project for a high-def videoconferencing system that implements encryption of audio and video streams.
