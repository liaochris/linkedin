Mark Montgomery’s Summary

Mark Montgomery brings his years of experience as a teacher, professor, and college administrator to help his clients find the best college match--and then get in.

Montgomery Educational Consulting is a full-service educational planning company that can help students at all levels of academic performance to find the colleges that suit their abilities and aspirations.

We work with high achievers, late bloomers, athletes, actors, and students with learning differences.

Mark Montgomery’s Specialties:

College counseling, admissions consulting, higher education administration, curriculum development, instructional design, project planning, creation of foreign exchange programs, teaching, education policy, textbook analysis.
