Eric Obalil's Summary

Eric Obalil is a Financial Consultant* & Wealth Manager* with Alexander Financial Services.

We work with clients who value their free time away from work, who are concerned about having a secure financial future for their family and who understand the importance of leaving behind a legacy for their loved ones.

Our advisors work WITH you, to cooperatively develop a strategy and execute a plan to reach your financial and lifestyle goals. This allows you to enjoy peace of mind that your financial plan is what you want it to be, not what someone tells you it should be.

At Alexander Financial Services, we believe, in an automated world, there is no substitute for genuine human relationships. Our clients' experience and well being will always be our #1 priority.

We accomplish this through our desire to understand your unique situation, develop a plan tailored to you specifically and effectively communicate progress, so that you will always know how we are working for you.

By working with Alexander Financial, you will have confidence in the work we do and our efforts to understand your concerns and reach your goals. We pledge to work every day to become your most trusted advisor.
