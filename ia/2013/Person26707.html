Connie Cornelius' Summary

Sales managers know that nothing motivates and excites like the anticipation of travel! By outsourcing your incentive travel programs to me, organizations save time and money by letting a professional manage the logistics. We specialize in meeting and events at sea, so the next time you would like to add some WOW to your event, call us at Affinity Group Tours & Travel.

Other organizations using our travel planning services include:
~ Professional & Educational Associations
~ Trade Organizations
~ Charitable & Fraternal Organizations
~ Museum Membership Groups
~ Religious, Gender and Ethnic Groups

In addition to developing customized tour itineraries for private groups, I provide consulting services to tourist destinations. I am passionate about sustainability and helping communities retain their “sense of place”. I have extensive experience in marketing management and have worked with tourism destinations and travel suppliers in more than 30 countries. In 2004, I earned a Master in Tourism Administration - Destination Management to better assist communities and attractions being threatened by over use.

Coupled with my solid research skills, I can help travel and tourism officials develop a balance between nature and tourism.
Organizations interested in my consulting services include:
~ Convention & Visitor Bureaus
~ Economic development agencies
~ Tour attractions
~ State and local tourism organizations
~ Businesses in the travel and tourism industry

Specialties

Travel services including cruise, air, hotel, guides, and customized planning for groups of 10 or more. I also provide consulting services for sustainable tourism development, destination assessment, tourism research, marketing strategies, visitor satisfaction, and attraction evaluation.
