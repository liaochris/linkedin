Jennifer Nunnally, DC's Summary

I organized the Chiropractic Healthcare team for the CONCACAF 2007 Gold Cup, including 19 DC's, 2 ATC's, and 1 PT, as well as 21 interns and 12 LMT's. I served as a resident on the medical commission at the 2006 Central American Games in Cartegena, Colombia. I served as the Official Chiropractor for the Fort Wayne Freedom Professional Indoor Football Team and The Semi Pro Football Teams Wolfpack and Classic City Crush for the last four years.

I am a board member and nurse file auditor for Home Care Services, a large home health service provider.

I serve as a provider for Spectracell, Diagnostechs, ELISA/ACT, Doctors Data, and other laboratories my clinic focuses on putting "health" back into healthcare.

***With a history in corporate employee benefits prior to becoming a healthcare provider, I am determined to redefine Corporate Wellness in this emergent market.***

Specialties: I have earned my doctorate in Chiropractic and hold a dual B.S in Anatomy and Health & Wellness. I am certified in Graston Technique TM, and use Kinesiotape (not certified). I received my CCCN (Certification in Clinical Neurology) in 2006, and am currently completing the CCSP (Certified Chiropractic Sports Physician) post graduate program. I have also completed (but not certified) 50 hours of post graduate work with the Carrick Institute in Neurobehavioral Disorders: ADHD.
