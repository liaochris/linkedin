Ed Buclatin's Summary

Senior Navy Officer with 25 years of military service and coach of award-winning collaborative teams. Sixteen years of high-profile corporate communication, public affairs, online communication, creative multimedia management, and crisis communication experience, working with US government interagencies and international organizations. Promoted rapidly through a series of increasingly complex positions. Outstanding leadership credentials. Proven team leader and innovator who excels at creating winning strategies, vision and products. Key inner-circle advisor to the CEO and board members. Results-driven, entrepreneurial thinker with ethical / character-based management style.

Specialties: An innovative enterprise communication leader, with extensive background in communication strategic planning, integrated marketing, crisis communications, online communications (web sites, social media, cloud computing and mobile technology applications), media relations, special events planning, internal communications and community outreach.
