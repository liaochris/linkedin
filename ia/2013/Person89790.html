Maurice Overholt's Summary

Creative, hard-working and charismatic leader with a heart for God and excellent brainstorming, analytical and musical skills. Possesses 11 years' experience in leading worship and music ministry, including eight years full-time at a multi-generational and multi-stylistic church of over 1,400 and the past year at a seeker focused progressive church of 700. Excels not only as a keyboardist, worship leader and pastor, but also as a multi-stylistic composer, arranger and loop writer.

Goals:
As an educator my objective is to equip people to enrich their own lives and the lives of others through the arts.

As a music pastor by objective is to enable people to worship from the heart while training effective worship leaders.

Specialties

worship program vision and direction; musical program vision and direction; choral and orchestral conducting; worship service leadership solo and from the piano; rock band direction, orchestration, and composition; classical composition from orchestra to small ensembles; pop/rock/jazz vocals; classical vocals; vocal health; personal and corporate pastoring
