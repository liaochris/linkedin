Gwen Peake's Summary

I am the program analyst for Compass Care, the pediatric palliative care program at UCSF Benioff Children’s Hospital. Compass Care helps children live as well as possible for as long as possible and provides support to families coping with their child’s serious illness or death. I bring to this position my strong background in administration, sensitive communications, event planning and the ability to manage many different projects and responsibilities simultaneously.
