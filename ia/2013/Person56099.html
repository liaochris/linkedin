Brad Thayer's Summary

Celebrating over 30 years experience in supply chain management, including leading the most "unique" multi-temp and multi-licensed distribution company on the West Coast USA!

DSW Distribution Centers, Inc. is a well known 40-year old World-Class 3PL specializing in; "boutique" cold-chain logistics services, "extreme" value-added services, e-commerce fulfillment, functional & alcoholic beverages, pharmaceuticals, bio-tech, dairy, organic & kosher, raw materials, packaging, CPG's and general consumer house-hold products.

Brad's daily objectives are to make someone laugh, exceed business targets and make time for family and friends.

Specialties: Business development, supply-chain management, logistics, product branding, networking and enjoying every minute of it!!
