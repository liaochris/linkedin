Jamie Robertson's Summary

Jamie Robertson here ☺ my career path looks like this:

Go to university ✓
Graduate ✓
Get a Job ✓
Grow and Develop ✓
Set up in Business ✓
Enjoy every minute of it ✓

Fortunately the job I got straight after university was in sales and I was thrown in at the deep end. And of course the only thing to do was to learn to swim. Taking full responsibility for my development then got me thinking big and it wasn’t long before I set up my first company.

During the heights of the economic crisis I helped thousands of Jobseekers find work by providing support and training and teaching them how they could find work. In particular I introduced many professionals to social media and how it could be used for job hunting and online reputational management. During this time I learnt NLP which sparked an interest in personal/professional development.

I then set up my first business helping small UK businesses with various online and offline marketing tasks.

One of my main goals is to empower employees by making them aware of their legal entailment. In order to overcome the many barriers I use my expertise in online marketing to make the law as accessible and user friendly as I can.

My other main objective is to help coaches, therapists; soloprenuers and entrepreneurs develop and grow their businesses. I primary do this by helping them get more clients and market their services online through Get That Lead and also through the Coach Support Service.

I am happy to help entrepreneurs, soloprenuers and owners of start-up businesses. If you think I can help then please ask.

I accept connection requests from coaches, therapists, trainers, consultants, entrepreneurs and soloprenuers.

Invite me to connect or email ✉ me at jamie@workitout.uk.com.

Specialties: websites for soloprenuers, trainers, consultants ande coaches
