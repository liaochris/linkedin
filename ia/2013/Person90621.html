MdME Lawyers | Private Notary's Summary

MdME is a leading Macau full service law firm, which has built a strong reputation for providing its Clients with high-quality, innovative and practical legal advice, following international standards of responsiveness and efficiency.

Since its foundation, MdME and its team have been involved in the largest developments, transactions and infrastructure projects launched in Macau, representing the key-players in such projects.

Our Client portfolio includes some of the largest corporations operating in Macau, in such diverse areas as banking, financial services, construction, infrastructure, gaming, retail, real estate, intellectual property, IT and telecommunications.
