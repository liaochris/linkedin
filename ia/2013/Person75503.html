Kieran O'Hea's Summary

I am Chief Digital Officer of the city of Brisbane where I lead the development and implementation of a strategy aimed at growing the city's digital economy by increasing digital uptake among businesses, supporting digital start-ups and improving the public's online experience of Brisbane - www.digitalbrisbane.com.au/strategy

I have twenty years experience in digital strategy development at corporate, public sector and municipal level in Ireland and abroad. My work in the area of Digital Capability Frameworks has been recognised by the European Commission's Digital Agenda for Europe and has been published by the Innovation Value Institute, where I am a Senior Honorary Research Fellow.

I previously published an autobiographical blog (or "blography") called Digitigm and through my LinkedIn group I campaigned actively for the development of an Irish National Digital Strategy.

Specialties: Digital transformation, digital capability, online strategy, digital marketing and web services delivery, project management, e-marketing, e-commerce, social media
