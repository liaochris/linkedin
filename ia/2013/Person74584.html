Ken Wissner's Summary

I have a dual role with CoreElement, focused on both our client’s and our own business. My primary responsibilities involve developing and maintaining productive client relationships, as well as administration for our company including implementing emerging technology, training and processes.

My background and training is not customary in the creative field. The majority of my career was spent in the manufacturing world, culminating as owner of a regional manufacturers representative firm. I’ve had the good fortune to represent some of worlds leading manufacturers, from standard cataloged products to custom and engineered equipment. I think strategically and embrace accountability as well as the absolute need for return on investment.

I spent my childhood in Michigan and raised my family in Cleveland, Ohio. Except for a few years on the West Coast, most of my career has been based in the Great Lakes and Midwest region, although I have enjoyed the opportunity to work with people and manufacturers throughout the United States, Canada and Europe.
