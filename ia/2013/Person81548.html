Lisa Holton's Summary

As a soloist and with talented partners in video and design, I develop online, print and visual content for corporations, universities and publications.  My roots are in business journalism – I’m a former Chicago Sun-Times Business Editor and reporter and magazine editor for Thomson Reuters/SourceMedia. I understand organizations and their needs, and I enjoy telling a good story.

I launched The Lisa Company in 1998 and co-founded Really Simple Video in 2009. I've worked with global corporations, consultants, universities and nonprofits as a writer, editor and project manager in an increasingly digital space. I'm grounded in print, but active in video, general web content and social media on all assignments. I'm a busy author, ghostwriter and magazine writer with regular bylines in U.S. business publications and custom media. At Really Simple Video, I'm project manager and co-producer, focusing on story development, research, writing and client relations.

Whether I'm working for executives, communications officers or top editors, I produce work that's always readable, persuasive and audience-specific. Please check my websites for additional background and work samples:
