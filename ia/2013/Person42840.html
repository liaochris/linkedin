Ernest de Bruin's Summary

I am an enthusiastic and passionate career, life and performance coach with 36 years of corporate background whose values of trust, honesty and concern for my clients drive me to facilitate the best possible outcomes for them. A caring coach with a proven ability to built rapport with clients, good listening skills and an empathetic approach.

I facilitate a process to help clients unlock their full potential, enrich their lives, develop goals to support their purpose in life, develop career development plans, enhance career performance and plan and manage their careers.

My value add to your organisation is being your partner in maximizing the work performance of your important and expensive management and specialist resources by helping them to identify and remove the interferences impacting their potential for excellent performance and leadership.

I achieve this by using effective coaching and feedback methodologies to help them identify these interferences and understand the impact thereof on their performance.
