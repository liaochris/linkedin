Jessica L. Kincaid's Summary

In her free time, she is a founding director of Issues Concerning Adolescents of Nevada, a non-profit that works to keep middle school and high school students involved in activities and out of trouble. In 2008 she also founded and continues to run the Winchester Community Center’s Encore voice program and is always ready to burst into song.
