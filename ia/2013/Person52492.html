Helena Achia's Summary

Helena Achia has over 10 years experience within the design industry. Most recently she has founded Achia Design, providing freelance Industrial Design and Consultative Services. The focus is on design for public spaces and experiences.

Moving forward Helena is interested in projects where user experience, artistry, brand messaging and spatial design collide.
