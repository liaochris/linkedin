Mary Lyman's Summary

Owner of MedConRx, Heber City, Utah. I am the current owner and consultant for a healthcare consulting company that assists physician offices in becoming more profitable, efficient and decrease the practices risk. We have developed a method to analyze and help practices benchmark good data and processes. We can successfully review internal practice process and help develop process improvement along with benchmark tools to help practices operate better. Because of the operational and managerial experience I have, I have the ability to successfully evaluate practices from all aspects of management. MedConRx has developed a network of companies and entities that will facilitate most of the consulting needs of a physician practice. Medical billing analysis, E/M coding auditing, compliance programs, physician strategic planning, operational overview and HR consulting is available. We work with existing physician groups and new physician start ups assisting with Performas and business plans as well as consulting services for new start up organizations.

A unique value that I add to the physician practice consulting is the dual education and experience associated with both a nursing degree and a business degree. I can successfully understand both the clinical needs and the business needs of a practice.As I have traveled throughout the United States working with physician offices, the topic of training has always come up. There is a real need for both process and customer service training in physician offices. As a result, I have recently developed virtual training and certification programs for Medical Office Managers and Customer Service Representatives. These programs are based upon the years of consulting and the many topics that I have had requests for training. Marketing for these products will begin in July, 2011.
