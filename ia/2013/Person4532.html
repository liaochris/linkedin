Alicia Nachman's Summary

Understanding users is at the center of everything I do. That's how I design meaningful products and services that are useful, usable, and delightful. I take a holistic approach to design in order to craft a cohesive and thoughtful user experience. In addition to UX, I like to have my hands on everything from strategic product planning, internal process improvements, documentation & communication, and project management. I have a no-nonsense, 'question everything' approach to software development, and I know how to balance the big picture while juggling the details and still releasing products. I thrive on driven, multi-disciplinary teams. A certified Scrum Master, I am fluent in Agile methodologies, and I bring past experience in the software development industry ranging from quality assurance and release management to programming and developer support.

Specialties

user-centered design, qualitative research methods, user testing, wireframing, information architecture, interaction design, usability, software development process, Agile
