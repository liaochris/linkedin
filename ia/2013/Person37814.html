Drew McLellan's Summary

Drew McLellan is a 25-year marketing veteran who helps clients create authentic love affairs with their customers. He also helps other agency owners learn how to take their business to the next level.

He loves to create "a ha" moments for his clients, peers and audiences across the land through vivid story telling, Italian heritage inspired hand gestures and the occasional tipping of a sacred cow.

Drew is also one of the world’s top marketing and branding blogger, according to AdAge’s Top 150 index.

He’s written the book 99.3 Random Acts of Marketing, co-editing the Age of Conversation series of books and in 1995, he launched his own firm McLellan Marketing Group.

Recently he has appeared in the New York Times, Entrepreneur Magazine, Business Week and Fortune’s Small Business. The Wall Street Journal calls him one of 10 bloggers that every entrepreneur should read.

Specialties

Branding, marketing strategy, copywriting, public speaking (on topics of marketing, branding and social media), blogging, toss a mean fastball, and building relationships with clients so they never leave.
