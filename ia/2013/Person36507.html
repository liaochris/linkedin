Dominnique Stefansson's Summary

QUEEN OF KINGS

Specialist in | Brands | Campaign & Project Management

Queen of Kings provides independent support, strategy and implementation for Brands and Brand campaign/projects launching or expanding in the UK and overseas. People don't just buy products, they want to buy into IDEAS. Whether you are a start up brand or an established competitor we will find the gap in the market and full fill it with strong, stand-out IDEAS and SOLUTIONS, bringing your concepts to life. Our expertise are in FMCG and LUXURY brands and falls into two main areas; Consultancy and Creative Marketing/ Project Management. Once we have discussed your ideas confidentially and in detail we then propose a tailored plan of action to suit your project, taking into account your budget and timescale.

FOUNDER

With extensive years experience in marketing and specialising in the cosmetics industry, Dominnique's passion for cosmetics started with business development and marketing for O'Boticario a Brazilian multinational with 2800 stores globally. Further senior management roles followed, including Marketing Manager and later General Manager for O'Boticario South Africa, followed by Brand Manager for Maybelline NY Loreal Paris, London and since then has worked with successful beauty brands locally and as far as Iceland .
Since setting up Queen of Kings, Dominnique has worked on a number of diverse marketing and brand development projects ranging form established organisations to start ups across international borders. With her flexible and professional approach and a proven track record of creating unique, innovative campaigns and strategies, Dominnique has a broad range of both creative and commercial skills and works with a team of industry experts such as graphics designers, Media agencies, PR, photographers and sales distributors across markets from South Africa, Brasil, London and Iceland.
