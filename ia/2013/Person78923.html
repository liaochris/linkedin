Laura Schlafly| Speaker| Career Coach| Author's Summary

LOOK TO LAURA, A CREATIVE CATALYST FOR CAREER CHANGE, ENCORE CAREER COACH - Laura@CareerChoicesWithLaura.com

(Get blog posts at: http://www.careerchoiceswithlaura.com/blog)
(Sign up for weekly empowering Inspirations at http://www.CareerChoicesWithLaura.com)

Customized Coaching Programs for:

* MID-LIFE CAREER CHANGERS & JOB SEEKERS- you're preparing for a company or industry change, or perhaps to exit corporate life altogether and start your own business.

* ENCORE CAREER SEEKERS - you are age 40 to 70 and want a fulfilling life after leaving the corporate world while planning to continue to stay engaged with work and local, national and world issues beyond the traditional "retirement age". You might even choose to set up your own business. Whatever path you choose, your intent is to NOT retire as your parents have done!

Laura's variety of hands-on experience in marketing, advertising services, field sales, business start up & turn around are collectively applied in supporting her clients with their desire for career change. She specializes in working with professionals in the age range of 40-70 providing support in their career transition, including group workshops tailored to informing and motivating these professionals to develop a "portfolio life" starting at age 40.

You can find out more specifics on how Laura can assist you in career choice and change by contacting her at: Laura@CareerChoicesWithLaura.com.

Specialties: Public Speaking, Career Workshops, Midlife Career Transitions, Career Change, Encore Careers, Self Discovery, Career Assessments, Interview coaching; LinkedIn training; Personal Branding; Twitter for job search training, Salary Negotiations, Transferable Skills, Creating and Relating, Working in Retirement, Retirement Jobs, Meaningful Careers, Writer, Blogger
