Rick Mark's Summary

I have practiced IT services at varying levels over the years and currently serve as a Strategic IT Advisor and an outsourced CTO. With an emphasis in Information Management services and information security for more than 20 years, I have specialized in most all facets of both growing and established companies IT operations and infrastructures. I have architected, integrated, and implemented large corporate and wide-area network and security infrastructure solutions, for mid-market enterprises, for most all of my professional experience. I have also overseen and implemented SingerLewak’s virtualization technologies practice, including its cloud services and support offering, with emphasis on daily operations models as well as Business Continuity Planning.

I am also an avid automotive enthusiast. With a past in automotive customization, high performance and racing applications, show quality audio and video work, custom paint and auto body design, and for the last decade and a half, an automotive advisor, product inventor and solutions consultant. Specializing in high end, exotic automobiles, the classics and today's Japanese performance cars, not to mention luxury automobiles.

Automotive consulting based on visionary product development, project management and automotive business consulting on operations and profitability. Though the automotive part of my life has taken a back seat in general, I still spend some late nights and weekends working on the hobby I truly love.

I'm a family man who married his high school sweetheart more than 20 years ago. I have two daughters who I am so very proud of.

A truly lucky person, who enjoys life every day, and enjoys what I do every day.
