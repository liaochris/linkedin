Christa Ross' Summary

A few things about me...

I have a lot of fun working with buyers and sellers, helping them move in or move on. But I also know it's a huge responsibility to be entrusted with your biggest investment, and I take that responsibility seriously.

I use my 15 years of experience in advertising and technology to put a creative mix of marketing ideas to work for my clients. I combine that with simple, good customer service - like returning phone calls quickly, paying attention to details, keeping my promises and being worthy of your trust.

I am a full time real estate agent with RE/MAX Select Realty. One of the facts I am most proud of is that RE/MAX Select sells more homes per agent than anyone else in Western PA. Why? Because have a great team of smart, dedicated agents who work hard for their clients. I am proud to be one of them.

I've lived in Pittsburgh most of my life (except for a few years in Ohio and D.C.). I grew up in the north of Pittsburgh, moved to the west, south, and now the east. It's great experience that I use to help my clients figure out the best neighborhood for their next home.

I have a particular affinity for green and energy efficient homes. I am an Ecosociate™, a GREEN Designee and a RE/MAX Green Specialist. Partly, my interest comes from the fact that I spend a lot of time outside, riding my bike and skiing, and I want to protect our environment so it will be a healthy place for my two kids - Jonathan and Nina. I also know that these homes are quality built, beautiful and better for your family, health and wallet.

I don't just sell real estate; I invest in and own a number of properties too. That means I can help you achieve your goals, whether it is a comfortable place to call home or an investment for the future.

Now you know a bit about me, it's your turn! Give me a call at 724-779-1437 or drop me an email and let me know how I can help you! Or visit me online at www.bestpittsburghhomes.com

Christa Ross' Experience

REALTOR

RE/MAX Select Realty

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Real Estate industry

January 2009 – Present (4 years 8 months)

By specializing in green and energy-efficient real estate I am able to combine my profession with my passion. As an avid cyclist, mountain biker, skier and mom, I believe that protecting our environment and reducing the impact we have on the planet is of utmost importance. I also believe that we can achieve that while saving money, living better and being comfortable. Choosing to live green has benefits that range from lowering your bills to lowering your emissions - and regardless of which is more important to you, they are both good results.

As more individuals look for healthier, cheaper, more comfortable, more sustainable options, it is my job to help them achieve their "green" goals. That is the reason I chose to become a certified Ecosociate™ by EcoBroker® International and a RE/MAX Green Specialist. Ecosociates™ complete a comprehensive energy and environmental curriculum on topics such as energy-efficiency insulation, heating and cooling and appliances; indoor air quality; solar technologies; environmental and health issues; and green building programs.

My experience goes beyond green, with over a decade of professional marketing experience under my belt and an expertise in technology tools for real estate, I provide my sellers with marketing skills and service that are unparalleled in the industry. I work to differentiate your home, package it to meet a buyer's needs and use both traditional and innovative tools to reach buyer's who are looking for their next home.

I am proud to be a member of the RE/MAX Select Realty team and I look forward to helping you buy or sell your next home. Call me at 724-933-6300 x214 or send me an email to christaross@remax.net. Or visit my website at www.greenhomespgh.com for more information.
