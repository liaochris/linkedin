Henry Jawhary's Summary

Henry is a seasoned technologist with over a decade of extensive experience in enterprise level software development, web application development, and web architecture. In the last 14 years, Henry managed numerous multimillion dollar technology projects for corporations across California and Nevada. Henry served as Program Manager for the Department of Defense where he led initiatives and technology projects to enhance the defense supply chain.

Henry previously held executive level IT positions at various well known corporations throughout California. He is highly regarded as an exceptional leader with a natural talent for technology, development, and solution delivery.

Henry is also a leading industry figure and writer in Online Reputation Management, Social Media Management, and Content Management via SearchRespect.com.

In 2008-2009, Henry attended the MBA program at the prestigious UCLA Anderson School of Management. Currently, Henry resides in Irvine, California, where he is CEO of Signing Stream Inc., a leading nationwide lender services company servicing Chase, Bank of America, Citibank, and major lenders across the US.

Specialties: Software Project Management; Enterprise, Web, and Software Application Architecture and Development; E-Commerce; Business Process Management; Supply Chain Management; Business Intelligence; IT Governance.
