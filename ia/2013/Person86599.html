Mark Frietch's Summary

Bridging Social Media Marketing and Talent Acquisition

Mark Frietch is a Social Media Coach, Talent Acquisition Consultant and Career Consultant specializing in candidate identification and development using both traditional and non-traditional means (Social Media). He has over eight years of recruiting experience in the financial services industry. His searches have ranged from administrative, sales, operations, technology positions to C-level positions. His ability to identify and develop relationships has earned him accolades from leaders and peers alike.

In his spare time, Mark serves as a speaker to various organizations providing outreach to those who are currently in a career transition environment. His presentations on the effective use of Linkedin and other social media sites to network and build relationships has allowed him to educate job seekers on how to get noticed in a challenging job market.

Most recently, Mark has been published in an eBook entitled "Ignite Your Passion". In this book, Mark outlines steps to using your passion to direct your career development using social media.
