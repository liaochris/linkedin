Chris McGraw's Summary

Management consultant and executive with nearly 20 years of experience in strategy, operations performance improvement and cost reduction. Chris worked for nearly a decade at top consulting firms A.T. Kearney, PRTM, and AlixPartners, where he rose to the level of Principal and Vice President, before establishing his own practice, McGraw and Company, in 2008.

With particularly deep expertise is supply chain and procurement, Chris has implemented more than $200M in savings at F-500 clients across a breadth of industries, including automotive and aerospace, energy and oil & gas, food manufacturing and distribution, and retail.

Chris's project experience includes Strategic Sourcing, Business Process Reengineering, Distribution Network Rationalization, Organizational Design, Strategy, and M&A Due Diligence. He has outstanding program management skills, having led teams of more than 80 people.

Prior to consulting, Chris held P&L responsibility at a $120M retailer, where he led a business turnaround and increased sales by 30% annually. He has exceptional international experience with 5 years living and working overseas and fluent Spanish.

Chris earned an MBA in Supply Chain Management and Finance at Michigan State, where he was an Eli Broad scholar (a full-ride merit scholarship). He holds a bachelor's degree from Vanderbilt.

Clients:
BP, Caesars Entertainment, Chrysler, Ford, GM, Kellogg’s, Lafarge, Magna, National Grid, Office Max, Pfizer, Pitney-Bowes, Rich’s, Sears, Spirit Aerosystems (ex-Boeing), State Farm, Sunoco, and US Foods.
