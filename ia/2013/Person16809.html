Brenda Priest's Summary

Your Door & More helps you share the joy and excitement of owning a home by providing personalized classic pen-and-ink illustrations of your door, front porch or home. These high-end, detailed illustrations are ideal for use as framed artwork, customized moving announcements, or holiday greeting cards. The artist, Brenda Priest, has been illustrating with pen-and-ink for more than 12 years and has recently been commissioned to illustrate work for the New Hope Valley Railway, The Apex Historical Society, The Mims House in Holly Springs, and Holly Grove Elementary School.

Specialties

Pen & Ink Illustration, home sketches and drawing with pen & ink.

Brenda Priest's Experience

Pen & Ink Illustrator/Artist

Your Door & More

Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Fine Art industry

October 2007 – Present (6 years 3 months)

I am an amazing creative artist that specializes in pen & ink illustrations, of homes, buildings and businesses. I work from photographs to create one-of-a-kind sketches of where you live or have lived. These drawings are great for loved ones, wedding presents, housewarming gifts, or milestone birthdays.

I have illustrated alma maters, honeymoon cottages, churches, front doors, porches, mansions, historic homes and childhood homes. The possibilities are endless! Contact me for portfolio!

These sketches can be matted and framed to create 'home portraits' or turned into fantastic note cards for a new home owner or unique Christmas cards.

I personally love donations to silent auctions or other charitable endeavors. Please contact me for more information.
