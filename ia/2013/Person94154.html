Mike Ferry's Summary

Overview:

I founded The Mike Ferry Organization which is the Leading Real Estate Coaching and Real Estate Training Company in North America. I've been involved in Real Estate Sales and Real Estate Management for more than forty years, earning an unmatched reputation for success built on a foundation of hard work, dedication to my personal goals, and an unwavering commitment to my clients and their success.

Real Estate and Early Career:

I began my career with Nightingale-Conant where I was quickly promoted to the position of National Training Director responsible for the sales activities of 1,300 people. I then made the decision to move into the residential Real Estate field as a Real Estate salesperson. After a career of selling homes and earning the honor of being one of the top agents in the state of California, I decided the time was right to launch my own company.

Real Estate Coaching/Real Estate Training Business:

Four decades later, The Mike Ferry Organization is a $50,000,000 company with tens of thousands of clients. Our phenomenal natural ability to teach and mentor the original techniques of The Mike Ferry System has helped countless agents achieve and exceed their personal and business goals.

I'm recognized by the National Association of Realtors® as one of the most influential people in the entire Real Estate profession. As the foremost expert on Real Estate sales techniques, Real Estate training and motivation, my company and I lead hundreds of seminars and retreats every year and have the industry's leading Real Estate Coaching programs. Real Estate agents, mortgage professionals, title representatives, and business executives alike have increased their production and profit because of the skills and mindset they have learned through the Mike Ferry systems.

“The business is great because the results agents get from working with us are exceptional. Agents want to know how to sell more homes in less time and that is exactly what we teach them.”
