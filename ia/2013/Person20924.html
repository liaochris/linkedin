Catherin Bakaloff's Summary

Hi, my name is Catherin Marie Bakaloff and I have been doing Graphic Design for the past 5 years. I’ve recently graduated from DMAC with the highest of honors, Summa Cum Laude, and was also a member of the DMAC Honor Society. In January of 2013, I submitted my artwork to the Addy Competition for the very first time, and won a Silver Addy Award. My talents include knowledge of the Adobe Creative Suite CS3-CS6, and my professional experience includes the rebranding of several different start-up companies. Graphic Design is what I want to do with my life simply because it makes me happy. A characteristic that distinguishes me from others is that I’m truly dedicated and determined to accomplish what comes my way. I have the ability and mind-set to create anything from scratch, and I pride myself on really trying to develop images that haven’t been done, seen or created before. Being able to complete a project above and beyond expectation, while meeting every specification and deadline, truly defines me as a Graphic Designer.
