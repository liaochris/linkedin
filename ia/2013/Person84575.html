Mandy Boyle's Summary

Mandy Boyle is a writer, marketer, and all-around nice girl with a passion for storytelling.

When she’s not optimizing websites and making things search-friendly, she’s an active blogger. She slings posts in a few places, including Search Engine People, a search engine community blog, and on NewFrugality.com, where she’s the Editor in Chief while learning to do more with less.
