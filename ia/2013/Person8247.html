Ann Bedford-Flood's Summary

I started Alliance Personnel in 1997 after a brief exposure to the Recruiting world. In January 2009, we changed our name to ProSource Staffing. I am passionate about the Staffing Industry. It is gives me great pleasure to be able to assist professionals in their search for new opportunities. Everyday is a new day and no two days ever seem to be the same. This is such a great opportunity to help others.

Specialties

We place Administrative, Accounting, Finance, Legal, Engineers and various other clerical positions.
