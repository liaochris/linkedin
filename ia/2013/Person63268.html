John E. Stith's Summary

I am on LinkedIn to broaden and maintain my network and to locate opportunities.

My primary business is writing (author of eight novels, several screenplays, and numerous short stores and articles). I have done extensive website development (from the very simple to the very powerful) but I've sold that business.

My broad skills in the computer field have a foundation of 20+ years spent developing a range of software projects, many with a project management role. I have worked for software companies in the Colorado Springs area, including Kaman Sciences Corp. (and then ITT). I’m now an independent contractor.

My novels (first published by Ace Books and Tor Books) are Reckoning Infinity, Reunion on Neverend, Manhattan Transfer, Redshift Rendezvous, Deep Quarry, Death Tolls, Memory Blank, and Scapescope.

Experienced with Drupal, Open Source, Ecommerce.

Specialties: Drupal, Open Source, Content Management Systems (CMS), Website Development, JavaScript, JQuery, PHP, CSS, (X)HTML, MySQL, ASP, MS SQL Server

Software Project Team Lead

ITT Industries

Public Company; 5001-10,000 employees; ITT; Industrial Automation industry

2001 – 2003 (2 years)

Coordinate technical and schedule efforts for a software development team designing, developing, integrating, testing, and documenting ground control segment of the Wideband Gapfiller Satellite for US Space Command and Army Space Command (a SATCOM system). ITT’s portion includes developing software in C++ and Java to control baseband units, cryptos, and modems. The effort includes a human-computer interface (HCI), server component on prime/redundant pair of Windows 2000 Servers, a multi-terabyte RAID, and Oracle database. In addition to our development responsibilities, we are also the system integrator. Member of SEPG. Stith’s 2002 CMM efforts resulted in winning the Ring of Quality Award and the ITT President’s Award.
