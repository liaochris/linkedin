Harold Li's Summary

Harold Li works with organizations to develop and execute public relations strategies that build relationships and deliver value, with an emphasis on incorporating digital media from the ground up.

Harold has worked public relations and digital marketing in New York, Hong Kong, Taipei, and Shanghai with clients such as HP, Qualcomm, Hong Kong Disneyland, Colgate-Palmolive, Johnson & Johnson, Peninsula Hotels, and Four Seasons Hotels & Resorts. He has presented on digital marketing at conferences such as Social Media Week and uses his work in social media to justify spending all his time on Twitter and Facebook. Follow him on Twitter at @waphle.
