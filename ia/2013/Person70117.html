J. Thomas Black's Summary

Mr. Black was licensed to practice law in Texas by the Supreme Court of Texas in 1982. He became Board Certified in Consumer Bankruptcy Law by the Texas Board of Legal Specialization in 1989, and he was recertified in 1994, 1999, 2004, and 2009. Mr. Black is also licensed to practice in the 5th Circuit Court of Appeals, and the U.S. Tax Court.

Specialties: Consumer bankruptcy, Chapter 7, Chapter 13, Stop Foreclosure, Stop Repossession, Stop IRS and Debt Collection Lawsuit defense.

Mr. Black also assists clients with debt settlements and debt negotiation, as well as litigation against mortgage servicers, banks and other consumer lenders for violations of consumer protection laws, primarily in U.S. Bankruptcy Courts and U.S. District Courts.
