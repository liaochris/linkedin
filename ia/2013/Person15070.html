Dr. Sarah Layton CMC, FIMC's Summary

OUR VISION: Every client we work with increases their revenue and reduces cost when they implement the strategy created. They become grounded in the principles of Value Innovation and know how to move into the future creating large markets with sustainable growth.

Our Purpose: To INSPIRE and PREPARE ORGANIZATIONS to SUCCEED in the GLOBAL ECONOMY

DRAMATIC IMPACT:
Helped a global company identify over a million dollars in costs spent on services their customers didn't care about.

Helped a large machinery manufacturer restructure the company from being an international company to being a global company which positioned them to sell successfully to their competitor.

Helped the CEO of a private company regain control after maverick employees tried to sabotage the health of the company for personal gain.

Helped a major Ivy League medical school hospital reduce their customer complaints by 45%.
