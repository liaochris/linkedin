Ambrane India Pvt. Ltd.'s Summary

From outstanding innovation in portable handheld technology, Ambrane India Pvt. Ltd. has been providing superior technology at a cypher of the cost. We produce number of products, ranging from Mini Laptops to Wireless Mouse, all competitively priced and emerged for day to day life of a common man. Ambrane India has grown to be one of the leaders in Technology & IT Products such as Computer Peripherals, Tablets and Mobility Devices in India today. Led by its experienced management team with over years of experience in the consumer electronic business, Ambrane India has grown the distribution of its products throughout India in both online and offline sales.

While product distribution and sales have grown, the company has achieved the trust of thousands of its customers and providing remarkable support with pick & drop facility from their place and that is the key reason for our success in the Tablet Industry. The business segment covers a portfolio of 2G and 3G Tablets with Calling and Non-Calling facilities, Multimedia Speakers, Laptop, Wi-Fi Router, Wireless Mouse, Wireless Keyboard, Power Bank (Pocket UPS), Smart TV Sticks to name just a few.

Ambrane India is working out hard to bring Tablets, into consumer’s everyday lives with innovative leading edge technology that minimizes the gap between the users and speedily growing technology in the present market, with user-friendly interfaces. The company is entirely devoted to shape an unparalleled tune up by focusing on Innovation in every Product, Support Services and Circulation.
