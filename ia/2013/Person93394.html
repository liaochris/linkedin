Michelle Brenner's Summary

I am currently enjoying a stay at Sony Pictures Imageworks, where I have been given the opportunity to grow in both technical expertise and business savvy. I am the consistent leader in department efficiencies, constantly finding ways to save money and processing time. I am always on the lookout on how to do more with less by creating new scripts and pipelines. When presented with a frustrated client, I always investigate to the heart of the problem and will often present multiple solutions to give them the best results with limited resources. I try to have both a fast, short term fix and a plan for robust, long term solution. I have prepared and presented various reports and statistical analysis to clearly communicate ongoing problems.

As a manager, I have received high marks from both my employees and supervisors. They have highlighted my ability to lead without micromanaging and create an atmosphere conducive to learning and growth. My supervisors have remarked on my passion and due diligence, citing how I often go above and beyond my job description to provide the best customer service.

I love watching movies. There is nothing like the feeling of a great movie transporting you to a different place, whether its a an epic superbattle or an intimate portrait of a crumbling relationship. Being a part of the industry gives me a great sense of satisfaction. I like knowing that I am a part of something much bigger than myself; that I am creating a work in tandem with hundreds of others over thousands of hours.

In my free time I also enjoy playing indie games, reading genre novels and watching every non-procedural tv show I can find.
