Justin Bidwell's Summary

Bidwell & Associates, LLC, specializes in the staffing of engineers, designers/drafters, technicians, and technical writers on both permanent and contract bases within the Twin Cities metro area and throughout the Midwest.
Our corporate clients range across industries, from Fortune 500 companies to entrepreneurial start-ups. Areas of specialty include: manufacturing/production, HVAC, A&E firms, industrial and heavy equipment, medical device, electro-mechanical, automation, and packaging.

We pride ourselves on quick responses to our clients and candidates to make the transition from company to company--or the acquisition by a company--as smooth as possible.
