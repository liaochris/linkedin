Bernard M. Curtis, EdD's Publications

  • The Affirmation Principle

    • Xlibris
    • November 19, 2012
    Authors: Bernard M. Curtis, EdD

    The Affirmation Principle advances the proposition that organizations can bring out the best in people and achieve extraordinary success by understanding how to lead and care for the human spirit. It begins by presenting the business case for the importance of organizational leaders to understand the benefits of valuing its people. The book challenges leaders to have the courage to change and to build stronger leader-follower relationships. The book focuses on the human-affirming leadership model and sound humane principles in working with employees. It then moves on to show how leaders' value to the organization can be measured, how they can become more accountable, and what they can do to bring out the best in people. This book provides new tools to help leaders who are serious about their development take their leadership thinking and skill to the next level.
