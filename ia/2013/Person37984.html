Jill Bjerke, BS, DC, CAPS's Summary

Dr. Jill Bjerke is the founder and president of Cut the Clutter Company, LLC and its division, Aging in Place with Grace. She is a thought leader, entrepreneur and change agent, known for encouraging solution seeking and positive results.

Cut the Clutter Co. focuses on customized solutions and services for those wishing to downsize, eliminate clutter and create clean and simple living spaces. Dr. Bjerke is known for her creativity, intense work ethic and her ability to derive resolutions to highly complex problems and issues. Her clients appreciate her staff’s attention to detail, compassion and patience in working through highly emotionally charged decisions. Her personal life and business reflect the saying, "People don't care how much you know until they know how much you care."

As a Certified Aging in Place Specialist (CAPS), she created Aging in Place with Grace as a source of information and services for those wanting to determine if staying in their own home as they age is the right decision for them. She has written a home assessment software program currently in development that will provide builders, remodelers, occupational and physical therapists and senior service providers with a tool for determining the changes a residence needs to make it “age in place” friendly and safe. She is a published, international author, providing crucial, timely information at a time when it is needed most and recently accepted a gubernatorial appointment as Commissioner to the Iowa Department on Aging.

Dr. Bjerke’s background as a health care physician, business executive and community leader has shaped the way downsizing and aging in place is perceived. A highly sought after speaker,her presentations are known for offering a unique, witty and refreshing view of creating clean and simple spaces and forming a safe and enjoyable way to age in place. She actually began blogging in 1995 for Chiropractic Online Today and hasn't stopped writing since.

Specialties

Research, organizing, project management, business development, solution and resolution seeking, negotiating, public speaking and presentations, highly developed communication skills, software development.
