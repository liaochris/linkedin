Comics for Kids Instructor

Meltdown Comics

July 2011 – Present (2 years 3 months) Greater Los Angeles Area

Work with children on creating and developing characters/stories, and specifically telling a story sequentially. Students are welcome and encouraged to bring in favorite books, comic books, or graphic novels to discuss and gain ideas and inspiration from. The same goes for referencing books, toys, and other items they can find inspiration from within the Meltdown comics store. Students work towards creating a sequential story, possibly ranging in length from one to eight pages depending on the individual child's preference and progress. Both writing and drawing are involved, with pencil, pens, pen and ink, and markers being some of the supplies used.
