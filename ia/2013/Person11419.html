Bacilio Mendez II's Summary

I currently serve as Diversity Chair of the Legal Division of the Special Libraries Association (SLA) and have recently been named the 2013 National Lawyers Guild (NLG) Legal Worker of the Year. I have previously served as: the Law & Technology Fellow of LeGaL - the LGBT Bar Association of Greater New York; a 2012 NLG W. Haywood Burns Memorial Fellow for Social & Economic Justice; the Kings County Supreme Court of the New York State Unified Court System's 2010 Nathan R. Sobel Law Library Fellow; Diversity Chair of the New York Chapter of SLA; Co-Convener of the LGBT Issues Caucus of SLA; and art director of "Law Lines" (the quarterly publication of the Law Library Association of Greater New York [a chapter of the American Association of Law Libraries]). I am a Beta Phi Mu graduate of Pratt Institute's School of Information & Library Science, as well an Oberlin College alum.
