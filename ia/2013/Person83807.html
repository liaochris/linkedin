Lynn Torre's Summary

Change is the one thing you can count on in life, and it usually involves money.

Lynn Torre is a unique financial advisor: She recognizes that the way clients think about money (their money mindset) impacts the way they save, spend, and invest it.

Lynn helps clients navigate financial transitions in life such as:

- retirement
- job change
- divorce
- death of spouse
- just need help

She uses the proprietary WISDOM Way to Financial Freedom™ to take a comprehensive look at every area of clients' finances. Using this approach, clients gain insight and learn effective behaviors – putting them on the path to financial freedom.

An outgrowth of her financial planning process is "Money Success from the Inside Out!" This new program helps people claim their personal power and create a NEW relationship with money. It guides exploration of their thoughts and behaviors about money - and how their money mindset is holding them back. It's a wake-up call that lets them reclaim their personal power and create a NEW relationship with money. Only with this insight can they can live the life they truly desire.

Specialties: Fee-based financial planning, investment advisory, and insurance services, retirement and financial planning, post-divorce planning and money mindset education
