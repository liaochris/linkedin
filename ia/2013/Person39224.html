Roger Adams, Ph.D., CISSN's Summary

Dr. Adams believes that no one has the same nutritional needs. Therefore, he customizes nutrition programs to fit the individual needs of his clientele. Dr. Adams works one-on-one with clients as they strive to meet their personal nutrition and fitness goals. In addition to individual instruction, Dr. Adams also holds group nutrition education sessions and classes. Dr. Adams also speaks at business seminars, health fairs, charity organizations and other groups that desire more information on the ever-changing field of nutrition.


Topics of experience and specialized training include:

Weight loss and weight management
Nutrition throughout the disease state
Obesity, cardiovascular disease, diabetes, cancer
Eating disorders and disordered eating behaviors
Drug and nutrient interactions
Basic and advanced nutrient metabolism
Nutritional assessment
Nutrition throughout the entire life cycle
Infant and adolescent nutrition
Nutrition for teens and young adults
Adult and geriatric nutrition
Pre- and post-natal nutrition
Nutrition during pregnancy
Reproductive nutrition
Nutrition and exercise
Dietary supplements
Sport-specific and performance nutrition

Specialties

Weight loss counseling; Sports performance nutrition; Personalized diet plans; Recipes that will help clients reach their goals and suit their lifestyles; Corporate wellness programs, including assessments; Personalized fitness plans
