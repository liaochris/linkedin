Aziza Iqbal's Projects

  • Brownbook Magazine: QMA

    • December 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Aziza Iqbal

    For Brownbook Magazine's collaboration with Qatar Museums Authority, I was commissioned to create a set of patterns inspired by traditional Bedouin weaves for the cover of each of the 8 issues.

  • Brownpaperbag

    • February 2009 to April 2010
    Team Members: Aziza Iqbal

    Editorial illustrations for the Bombay-based weekly guide – each concept integrates the featured topic with their iconic branding: a brown paper bag.

  • All Sports Magazine

    • February 2007 to June 2007
    Team Members: Aziza Iqbal

    Design direction of the sports publication, from grid systems, typography, infographics, to the style guidelines and layout for the three launch issues.

  • Vox Magazine: Issue 3, Vodafone Qatar

    • March 2011 to June 2011
    Team Members: Aziza Iqbal

    Redesign and art direction for the third issue of Vodafone Qatar's bilingual lifestyle magazine, with all content curated locally in Doha. The primary task and challenge was to maintain the highly energetic visual language established by the first two issues, and assert the Vodafone brand, but to improve readability and content organisation in both English and Arabic.

  • Favini Papers, Italy

    • July 2005 to September 2005
    Team Members: Aziza Iqbal

    A collection of cards designed to showcase the first range of Favini Papers launched in India using a variety of print techniques.

  • Kyoorius Designyatra 2006

    • July 2006 to October 2006
    Team Members: Aziza Iqbal

    Designyatra Goa ‘06:
    Branding and merchandising for the first-of-its-kind, 3-day design conference and production management for Kyoorius Design Magazine Issue 01.
