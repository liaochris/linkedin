Bruce Greene's Summary

Experienced in the full spectrum of the software development process: concept and specification, coding and testing, help systems, deployment and customer support. I design, write, debug, or improve software that runs in the most demanding environments where problems can result in injuries or huge financial loss - the manufacturing of automobiles, aircraft, integrated circuits, clothing, and structural steel.

Skilled at verbal, written, and presentation communications; explaining highly technical issues to managers and enabling them to make informed decisions. Electrical engineering degree with a Master of Science in closed-loop controls.

Specialties

Mission-critical software for industrial and laboratory automation... done right the first time! Windows PC development (C#, Visual C++, VB.NET), Windows Presentation Foundation, motion control (PMAC, Galil), and robotics (Adept, Denso, Staubli).
