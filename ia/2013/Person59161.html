Jason Kaufman's Summary

Authoritative culture critic and reporter with strong writing and research skills and more than two decades in the field. In-depth knowledge of fine arts and the culture sector, particularly art history, museums, architecture, photography, policy issues, the art market, collecting, and art world leaders. Work has appeared in The Washington Post, The New York Times, The Wall Street Journal, The Los Angeles Times and publications, including The Art Newspaper, for which he long served as Chief U.S. Correspondent. Contributor to exhibition catalogues for the Whitney Museum, Beyeler Foundation, Morgan Library, Central Academy of Fine Arts in Beijing, and MASSMOCA (forthcoming).

Specialties: Highly informed writer, consultant, teacher, and lecturer on museums and exhibitions, art criticism, and arts policy in the U.S. and abroad.

Welcomes opportunities to write, consult, edit and lecture on museums, exhibitions, artists, contemporary art, architecture, and arts policy. Extensive experience working internationally.
