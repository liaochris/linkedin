"The League" is essentially a venture capital firm for non-profits. We raise money and then give it to non-profits who are building new programs, support them through the first years of the new program with trained volunteers (our members) and then help them launch the new program to the community.

      I have been Community VP on the Board of the JLPA•MP and led a group of 15-20 women and 6 community project areas within the League. We taught parents the importance of reading aloud to their children, helped families be more fit, provided guidance and support to foster kids and their foster parents, mentored kids at the East Palo Alto Charter School, reviewed and sponsored legislation in Sacramento, granted $35,000 to local non-profits and more!
