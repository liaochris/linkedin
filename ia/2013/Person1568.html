Adam Parnes' Summary

Innovative senior corporate communications professional with over 13 years of internal and external communications experience. Proven communicator in all aspects of HR communications, employee engagement, employment brand, content management and comfortable and skilled in working with all levels of management.

Lead large scale organizational development and change initiatives from planning through execution with an emphasis on Human Resources programs and employee engagement.

Developed and executed all aspects of HR and internal communications.

Defined employee engagement survey requirements to replace previous vendor, managed RFP process, implemented survey, administered manager training and action planning.

Proposed and implemented Yammer, an enterprise social networking service, to provide an online public forum for all employees to share information, stay informed and collaborate.

Managed the redesign and implementation of new intranet content management systems.

Managed the successful awards nomination process to highlight the association as a great place to work: Computerworld’s Great Places to Work in IT, AARP Best Places to Work for Employees Over 50, Northern Virginia Family Service CARE Award and Washingtonian’s Great Places to Work list.

Drafted and stewarded the approval of a new social media policy to comply with latest National Labor Relations Board’s guidance.

Specialties: corporate communications, internal communications, employee engagement, content management, social media, Yammer, brand management, change management, professional speaking
