Brian Mayer, MBA's Summary

Customer Experience Architect | Technophile | Social Media Native | Change Instigator

I help small businesses and non-profits solve their marketing, technology, and customer service challenges with creative, customer-focused solutions. I'm available for both short and long-term contracts or as a full-time Marketing Manager, Customer Experience Director (or similar) in a service, technology, non-profit, or retail industry.

As an experienced Customer Experience Strategist & Manager (12+yrs), I design processes and manage the business functions that define and deliver the customer's experience. Each of these functions plays a different, yet equally significant role in determining the customer's overall experience and in turn has significant short and long-term impact on both revenue and net income.
