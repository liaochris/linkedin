Jeff Thompson's Organizations

  • Oklahoma Recruiters Network - OKRN

    Founder
    • February 2013 to Present

    We are a non-profit professional association that offers education, assistance, discussion and camaraderie for recruiters in Oklahoma. The Oklahoma Recruiters Network or OKRN is modeled and based on the success of DFW Texas Recruiters Network or DFWTRN that has hundreds of members and holds monthly luncheons.

    Just like DFWTRN, The Oklahoma Recruiters Network was formed for the purpose of sharing and networking with technical / non-technical recruiters, head hunters and human resource professionals. We strive in educating our members, assisting with staffing difficult positions, offering tips to reduce cost-of-hire, developing professional skills, and promote camaraderie and professional discussions.

    As a member of OKRN we hold a monthly luncheon or meeting and strive to attract informative speakers, technology partners, resource providers, and top-notch recruiting talent.
