Connie Bennett, CHHC, CPC, ACC's Experience

USA Self Employed

December 2001 – Present (11 years 9 months)

I motivate people to let go of their sugar and carb addiction and create smart habits so they can easily shed weight, boost energy, rev up their libido, be happier, concentrate better, increase productivity, and more.

To that end, I write books -- Beyond Sugar Shock (Hay House) and Sugar Shock! (from Berkley Books). I also give talks for corporations, health events and empowerment conferences; write articles, essays, columns and blog posts; and my labor of love-- hosting the Gab With the Gurus Show (http://www.GabWithTheGurus.com).

For Gab with the Gurus, I interview celebrities, thought leaders, bestselling authors and renowned personalities, who inspire, provoke, educate and entertain you. Listen live Wednesdays at 9 am PDT at Blog Talk Radio -- www.GabWithTheGurus.com -- or tune in later, at your convenience. Guests have included: Dr. Mehmet Oz, Suzanne Somers, Lisa Rinna, Marilu Henner, Barbara DeAngelis, Dr. Mark Hyman, Jack LaLanne, "FlyLady" Marla Cilley, Law of Attraction experts, fitness trainers and many more.

In addition, I'm a Sugar Freedom Coach, life coach and certified holistic health coach.

Currently, I'm:
(1) Open to speaking invitations from around the country and world to help people kick sugar so they can get a life that rocks;
(2) Available for freelance writing gigs;
(3) Taking new private and group coaching clients
(4) Seeking volunteers and interns to help with blogging, social media, building my community on SparkPeople group
(5) Open to ideas for my blogs, http://www.SugarShockBlog.com as well as my blogs on the Huffington Post and Psychology Today
