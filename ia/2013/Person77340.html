By building this platform ZamKor became one of the most recognizable educational e-learning content providers in Poland (in Physics, ZamKor's specialty).   • SBGrid Content Management System

    Team Members: Krzysztof Satola

    My responsibilities: Software Architecture, Design and Development.

SBGrid brings together more than 160 like-minded laboratories all in the business of discovering what biological molecules look like and how they work. SBGrid eliminates the need for a dedicated software technician in large structural biology groups and it enables smaller, less specialized groups to create sustainable structural biology research programs. The CMS was built in Perl and HTML/CSS supported by MySQL database.
