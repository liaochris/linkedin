Helen Hodgson's Summary

I have over sixteen years of experience as a Massage Therapist, and have worked for many prestigious spas and salons in Beverly Hills CA. After years of practicing solo, in 2000 I began a network of stellar massage therapists and other spa staff through my own Mobile Spa Business, Serve the Goddess. I also run U Knead Massage, a corporate chair massage service, and my latest venture is How to Create a Mobile Spa Business, which aims to inspire and support others interested in starting their own mobile spa globally.
Gifted with both an ability to intrinsically understand my clients’ needs and a strong affinity for helping others, I have a diverse background that can be used to meet your immediate and long-term needs. I am an excellent multi-tasker with a very organized mind who can effectively work independently as well as on a team. I have impeccable communication skills and I have a passion for volunteer work as witnessed by my current role as California State Coordinator of the Emergency Response Massage International Team.
