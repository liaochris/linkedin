Jonathan Vokes' Summary

Jonathan is a leading Payment Card professional with over 15 years IT experience in Financial Institutions. Jonathan is an industry expert in Mobile Payments, Contactless and EMV.

Jonathan is currently Head of mPowa working to launch an all in one mobile payment solution based on Bluetooth EMV. Previously Jonathan was Technology Director Worldpay Zinc and launched WorldPay Zinc in June 2013.

Jonathan has an impressive track record as a leading payment consultant, which includes mobile payments, Europe’s first contactless solution and the world’s first EMV Chip and PIN implementation. Enjoying a high profile in the UK and Europe Jonathan has represented the UK on ISO20022, EPAS and other industry bodies and was on the EMVCo Board of Advisors.

Jonathan also provides general IT consultancy including due diligence for new acquisitions.

Skills
• Setting up internal mobile payment start-up technology business.
• Enterprise architecture for card, mobile and payment systems.
• Key technology influencer for global card & payment standards.
• Key PoS technical consultancy to blue chip clients and industry bodies.
• Authorship of industry technical specifications.
• Payment processing platform consultancy.
