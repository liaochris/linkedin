Christopher Coleman's Summary

Pronounced dead at birth, Christopher Coleman went 15 minutes without oxygen to his brain, the doctors diagnosed Christopher with Cerebral Palsy. This condition was supposed to prevent him from moving, talking, or even thinking for himself. Today, I speak to hundreds who come to hear my words of encouragement and inspiration.

As America's Unconfined Life Coach, Motivational Speaker and Author, I travel the world sharing principles with individuals teaching them how to live an "Unconfined Life". Through life coaching, motivational speaking, written literature, TV, radio and social media, I am able to guide individuals to their personal, physical, spiritual, professional and financial freedom.

I believe that you are living an Unconfined Life when circumstances around you or within you do not prevent you from loving others (despite their possible differences), finding a healthy balance, living out your faith, pursuing your dreams, and investing in your future.

I was created to motivate, encourage, and challenge individuals to live a life beyond where they’re living today. Join me in this movement --- as we challenge the World to live an UNCONFINED LIFE.

www.christophercoleman.net

Specialties: Motivational Speaking, Team Dynamics Consulting, Life-Coaching, Author and Contributing Writer.
