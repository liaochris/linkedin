Josh Dolin's Summary

Josh Dolin is the author of The Web Guru Guide™, a book released in 2010 and highly regarded by entrepreneurs and business executives. He is the CEO and founder of Tempo Creative, an inbound marketing firm that has been providing ROI-based results for over 700 clients around the globe for over a decade.

Josh was born in Chicago in 1975. He's had a passion for writing and entrepreneurship since a very early age. Dolin holds a degree from the prestigious English and Writers' Workshop program at The University of Iowa, where he graduated in 1997.

After graduation, Dolin worked in international sales and marketing for a retail advertising firm in Chicago prior to founding Tempo Creative. He is actively pursuing global expansion for multiple companies. In September of 2011 he accompanied Arizona's Governor Jan Brewer along with the Arizona Technology Council on a trade delegation to China.

He currently resides in Cave Creek, Arizona with his wife, son, and two dogs. When not writing or working with clients, Josh can be found traveling the world, playing guitar, and contemplating his next business venture.
