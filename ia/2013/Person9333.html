Ara Abrahamian, CRPC®, CMFC®'s Summary

Ara Abrahamian began his career in the financial services industry in 2003 as a Financial Advisor with Ameriprise Financial. He soon earned the Advanced Financial Advisor title. With Ameriprise, Ara regularly advised other advisors in portfolio and asset management. With Divergent Planning, Ara oversees the firms asset management and portfolio programs.

Ara attended the University of Maryland where he earned a B.S. degree in Marketing and International Business. Ara holds various licenses and designations including the CMFC® designation and performs duties of a Chartered Mutual Fund Counselor which includes analyzing mutual fund managers, prospectuses and fees.

Ara is very passionate about his Armenian heritage and is willing to talk for hours about his several trips to Armenia. He is very active in the Armenian community and regularly attends Soorp Khatch Armenian Apostolic Church.

In his free time, Ara enjoys traveling and food. Another of Ara's passions outside of work is basketball. You can often find him playing several times a week. While he has no chance at ever making it in the NBA, the dream will not fade! Ara’s latest adventure is CrossFit. He really enjoys the challenge and pushing himself to the max.

In 2011, Ara co-founded "Matt vs Ara", a fundraising program with monthly athletic events

Ara is registered to sell securities to US Residents only in the states of AL, CA, CO, DC, DE, FL, KY, MD, NC, NY, PA, SC, VA and VT.

Securities and Investment Advisory Services offered through H. Beck Inc., Member FINRA, SIPC. H. Beck Inc. and Divergent Planning are not affiliated.
