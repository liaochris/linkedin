Gloria Feldt's Summary

It's time for women to Take The Lead! I co-founded a nonprofit organization of that name with the bold mission to prepare, develop, inspire, and propel women to take their fair and equal share of leadership roles across all sectors by 2025. Want to be part of this movement, take our learning programs, or attend our events? Contact me directly and follow our LinkedIn page Take The Lead Women.

I'm an inspiring keynote speaker, author, teacher, and commentator on leadership and women's relationship with power. My expertise comes from frontline experience as a CEO and women's advocate. Contact me to discuss your group's needs for a customized speech, workshop, or webinar.

My latest book, No Excuses: 9 Ways Women Can Change How We Think About Power, is a national and amazon bestseller. It cracks the code that can help women embrace their power to reach parity from the boardroom to the bedroom for good--their own and the good of society.

Previous books include the New York Times best seller "Send Yourself Roses", co-written with actress Kathleen Turner; "The War on Choice" and "Behind Every Choice Is a Story".

People magazine called me "the voice of experience" because of my personal story as a teen mom and high school dropout from rural Texas who rose to lead Planned Parenthood Federation of America, the world's largest reproductive health and advocacy organization. Vanity Fair named me to its Top 200 Women Legends, Leaders, and Trailblazers.

I blog on my website (www.GloriaFeldt.com) www.GloriaFeldt.com/heartfeldt-blog. My commentary has been published in the New York Times,Daily beast, Washington Post, Elle, Salon, MS, USA Today, Women's e-News, Alternet, Politico, and many more. I've also appeared on most all major TV public affairs and news programs.

Visit my website www.GloriaFeldt.com for full bio and clips. Connect on Facebook and twitter @GloriaFeldt too. Take The Lead's website is http://www.taketheleadwomen.com and we're on all social media as well.

Specialties

Speaker: inspirational keynote speaker with a compelling message and practical leadership "Power Tools," for corporate leadership, women's civic groups, national professional organizations, universities, and political venues; media commentator; expert on visionary and authentic leadership, women's lives, health, media, politics, and relationship with power.
