David Steward's Summary

Leader in brand-building and turnaround across multiple platforms, including digital content, e-commerce, direct marketing, magazines, television and books. Presently CEO and President of ShermansTravel Media a leading online and mobile publisher of top travel deals and vacation destination advice. Previously President / COO of 20x200 the leading art commerce site offering limited edition prints at affordable prices and Advisor to InMarkit which powers personalized, authentic word of mouth marketing for retailers and brands.

Also previously CEO of F+W Media, a $200M private-equity backed niche consumer media company. Earlier in my career I led a dramatic turnaround of a $200M direct marketer, and was instrumental in building/ redeveloping three of America’s top media brands: People Magazine, Martha Stewart Living and TV Guide. My leadership, analytical, financial and creative skills propelled these brands into profitable new directions.

Specialties: Digital Business Creation and Growth, Brand Building, Turnaround, Creating multiple media platform companies, Integrated / Multi Channel Marketing, e-commerce, Direct Marketing, Social Commerce, Mobile Applications
