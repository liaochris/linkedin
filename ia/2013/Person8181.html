Anna Lau, CPA, MBA's Summary

I am a finance professional with strong business intelligence experience. With degrees are in accounting and finance and after years of crunching numbers for CEO's and CFO's, I asked "What are they doing with the data?" and proceeded to get an MBA in strategy for the answer. I love advising senior executives at small and mid-sized companies, streamlining processes and teaching. I'm also a techie geek.

I reside in Silicon Valley and keep an active CPA license in my home state of Florida. I love to learn and teach and completed 112 hours of continuing education although only 40 were required last year. Thank goodness for ebooks and my Windows tablet!
