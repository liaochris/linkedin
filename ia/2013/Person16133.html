Bradley Keyes' Summary

Brad is the Managing Broker and founder of HK Brokerage, Inc. Founded in 2011, HK Brokerage is a full service residential and commercial real estate brokerage. In addition to brokerage, Brad is an attorney at the Law Office of Martin Horwitz, located in Beverly Hills. His practice centers on real estate, engaging in both litigation and transactional work. He represents developers, syndicators, investors, and funds in a wide range of matters including acquisitions, dispositions, syndications, fund formations, development, leasing, financing, and foreclosures. His clients range from individual investors, corporations, and institutions across all asset classes of real estate, including residential, multi-family, retail, office, industrial, and hospitality.

Raised in Los Angeles, a graduate of UCLA, and a graduate of Loyola Law School Los Angeles, Brad is a multi-talented professional equipped to meet every challenge of today's real estate market. A downtown resident since 2008 and a former Westsider, Brad's enthusiasm for sales, detail-oriented approach, and legal background make him the right choice for the seasoned real estate investor and the first time home buyer alike.

Brad's understanding of the fluctuating market and familiarity with the Los Angeles area gives his clients the most current and comprehensive information on which to draw when making important decisions. Skilled in both marketing and promotion, Brad creates marketing plans specific to each client's needs, ensuring that each home stands out amongst the competition and commands top dollar. If you seek to buy a home, Brad's depth of experience and thoughtful counsel afforded throughout the process make for the finest possible representation in the industry.

Brad's approach to client service exemplifies integrity and an unwavering commitment to his clients' complete satisfaction. If you are interested in professional representation please feel free to contact Brad.
