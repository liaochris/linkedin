Matt Jones' Summary

Matt has over 20 years of professional experience and has successfully delivered numerous network, internet and security based products to the market. This has been achieved through inspiring the teams he has led to focus on value and delivery both technically and commercially. These teams have been based in the UK, US, Japan, Europe, Moldova, India, Canada and South Africa.

He is a driven and realistic leader who is unafraid to challenge dogma and create resolving strategies for technical, management and business problems. He is articulate and collaborative in his approach to work and is able to balance broad inputs with a decisiveness and openness that has enabled him to get the most from the business units he has led.

Matt is approachable, transparent and a passionate facilitator, catalyst and advocate for the delivery of both tactical and strategic goals.

Specialties: Proven and successful manager of technical teams. Outsourced/offshore service management. Extensive line management. Delivery enablement in software product and SaaS contexts. Strategic planning expertise.
