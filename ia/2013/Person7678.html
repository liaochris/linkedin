Angela Davis-Morris' Summary

I am the president and managing attorney of Davis-Morris Law Firm, P.A., a law firm whose practice is limited to the representation of disabled individuals seeking their social security disability benefits and bankruptcy law. I have been helping the disabled obtain their Social Security Disability Benefits for almost 20 years. My firm has helped thousands of individuals in our community who have been stricken with serious illness, disease and other physical and mental problems that keep them from working. Our bankruptcy practice evolved via the need of our disabled clients who were over-burdened with medical bills. We currently have offices in Clinton and Hattiesburg, Mississippi. I enjoy my practice and feel my biggest achievements are related to individually assisting my clients in obtaining the disability benefits they deserve or helping them resolve their debt problems. I love what I do and I care about the people I serve.

    • Healthy Cells of the Pinebelt
    • June 1, 2010
    Authors: Angela Davis-Morris

    Medical proof is required to properly document any Social Security disability case. This article discusses the need for good, detailed medical records in order to prove such a case.

  • Breast Cancer - A Disability?

    • Healthy Cells Magazine
    • October 1, 2010
    Authors: Angela Davis-Morris

    Breast cancer often does not meet the requirements of Social Security disability rules in that the most serious effects do not generally last a year in duration. This article discusses instances when breast cancer can be the basis of a Social Security disability finding and what one can do to help provide the best proof of limitations to a presiding judge.
