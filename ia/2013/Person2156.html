A Edward Major's Summary

Worked for 4 years at Cahill Gordon & Reindel, primarily in the litigation and public finance areas.
For the past 21 years, I have managed a baseball team of inner city youth, ages 16-20 in Brooklyn, NY, mentoring players, assisted their applying to university and obtaining athletic and other scholarships, as well as jobs.
My pro bono work includes successfully arbitrating a large construction contract for a Church in the Bronx, New York and serving as Vice President and a Director of the Command and General Staff College Foundation.
I am also an active volunteer in West Point and Army War College affairs.
Formerly Co-President of The Bronx High School of Science Parents' Association.

My goal is to further expand the firm's work in the area of fraud assessment and prevention, National Defense law, and utilize my practice in England and Wales. I wish to expand my international commercial real estate practice.
As my firm is a boutique, I offer concentrated and personal service.

Specialties: The firm's specialty is, and has been for all of its 74 years, real property, both commercial and residential. I work with government agencies, both Federal and State, and am capable at acquiring understanding of their procedures and requirements.
