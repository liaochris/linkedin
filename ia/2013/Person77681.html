Kyle Austin's Summary

Throughout his career Kyle has provided public relations, marketing communications, social media, digital advertising and business development counsel for global brands, high-growth start-ups and non-profits, including: Sony, eHarmony, Zaarly, RunKeeper, ByteLight, SinglePlatform, giftiki, ChoiceStream and One Laptop per Child.

Kyle, and his teams, have been successful in creating, executing and managing integrated marketing communication’s campaigns which have elevated brand awareness, increased sales and achieved desired market positioning for his clients.

He was an integral part of the team handling all external communications for One Laptop per Child and was instrumental in outlining and executing communications strategy as part of the 2007 “Give One, Get One” campaign; which earned a Silver Anvil Award from the PRSA and the 2008 IPRA / United Nations Grand Award. The campaign raised $35 million, enabling the delivery of more than 100,000 XO laptops to children across the globe

In addition, in 2009 he teamed with Sony to successfully launch its new line of Sony Readers. Kyle supervised a team that assisted Sony's Digital Reading Division with executing a strategic marketing communications plan for the launch, which resulted in 6.2 billion media impressions and increased sales of Sony Readers by four-times.

Kyle has built strong relationships with leading business, technology and consumer media, securing secured top-tier print, digital and broadcast coverage for his clients with outlets including: The Today Show, The New York Times, CNN, CNBC, The Wall Street Journal, BusinessWeek, Forbes, Fortune, The Financial Times, Reuters, AP, IBD, NPR, BBC and the USA Today.

Specialties: public relations, marketing, strategic communications consulting, business development, media relations, digital media, digital advertising, social media planning and execution, messaging, marketing communications, thought leadership development,
