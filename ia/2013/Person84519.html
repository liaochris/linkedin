Malthe Sigurdsson's Summary

As a creative director, art director and designer, I have altogether 18 years experience in strategic and visual brand building and product development on a world wide scale as well as developing and managing teams working on user experience, interaction design, creative design and I know how to build effective value propositions and consumer messages. In short: How stuff works, looks and talks.

Specialties: Product and brand definitions and propositions, Identity design, User Experience design, Effective messaging to consumers, Strategic brand development.

Started as a freelance Lead Designer and moved on to Designer Manager and Creative Director.

Numerous awards and recognition for Skype brand and websites, among these 3rd place behind only Google and Apple in a 2005 vote by Brand Channel.
