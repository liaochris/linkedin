Al Duncan's Publications

  • My Success Journal For Young People

    • Al Duncan Publishing LLC
    • 2008
    Authors: Al Duncan

    This is no ordinary journal. It is a well known fact that if you want to be successful you should write down your goals and how you plan to accomplish them. And now young people have a special place to do it…in their personal success journal.

    My Success Journal For Young People is full of empowering Duncan Nuggets® for Your Noggin--articles, tips, quotes, and engaging exercises on:

    Understanding the REAL definition of success
    Goal getting
    Overcoming failure
    Conquering your fears
    Building self-confidence and self-discipline
    Dealing with haters and doubters
    Developing your strengths and managing weaknesses
    Creating a strong personal brand
    How to avoid the potential pitfalls of social media
    How to be more successful NOW.
    …and more!

    My Success Journal is the perfect fit for any young person or youth program. It can be easily added to your curriculum because there is plenty of space for young people to take notes and write down their ideas. They can even keep pictures of their dreams in My Success Journal and the short articles and fun exercises will enhance any principles that you are currently teaching them.

  • My Career Journal: A Young Person's Guide To Having An Awesome Career

    • Al Duncan Publishing, LLC
    • 2013
    Authors: Al Duncan

    This is no ordinary journal. It’s packed with Duncan Nuggets® and real-world advice on career success. While you are having fun using it, you will:

    Build a Competitive Advantage that will get you hired.
    Discover how to build an awesome career just for you.
    Get easy exercises and action plans for developing the Top 10 Most Desirable Skills in the job market.
    Learn 21st Century Interviewing Strategies.
    Avoid the Top 10 Red Flags for applications, cover letters, resumes, and interviews.
    Understand why you might want to explore a career in STEM or Advanced Manufacturing.
    Master the soft skills needed for success in the 21st Century.
    Avoid the most common career-threatening mistakes young people make.
    Strengthen your Personal Brand and protect your Digital Reputation.
    Be a rock star during your first 90 days on the job.
    Learn how to set yourself up for a gainful employment.
    Get real world answers from CEO’s, hiring mangers, and career center directors.

    Developed from Al Duncan’s evidence-based soft skills seminars and workshops for young people,
    My Career Journal is the perfect fit for any career readiness program for teens and young adults.

    Overcome self-doubt
