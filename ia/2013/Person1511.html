Adam Leibler's Summary

Expert in bridging the gap between the fields of performance improvement, learning, and technology. I apply the classic, traditional, and new learning development methodologies to the realm of elearning, yielding measurable workforce performance improvement and return on investment.

Highly organized, creative, and detail-oriented instructional designer and manager with over twenty-four years of experience; specializing in direct application of learning methodologies to elearning course development, evaluation, and human performance improvement, including the use of the (s)ADDIE Model, Agile ISD, Kirkpatrick’s Four Levels of Evaluation, and Bloom’s Taxonomy of Learning; Mager Institute/CEP trained in Criterion Referenced Instruction and Instructional Module Development.
