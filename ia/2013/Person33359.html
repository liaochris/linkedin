Debbie Ducic's Summary

**DEBBIE DUCIC: Lifestyle Entrepreneur | Unconventional Lifestyle Content Publisher | Multi Media Maven | GutZy Life Promoter and Coach |
**INDUSTRIES: House Sitting / Pet Sitting | Membership & Information Publishing | Travel & Lifestyles | Internet Marketing | Women in Business/NonProfits | Motivational Speaking | Fashion & Resort Retail
**LOCATION: Australia | Blogosphere | Global Virtual World
**INVITE: Home Owners and Pet Owners who travel to join our matching service with House Sitters / Pet Sitters. Always open to Connecting with People who Care, Take Action and LOVE helping others.

We attract, motivate, coach and develop Gutzy Women (and Brave Men) as a way of life. It’s a belief system. It’s a new innovative and creative way of living and working together……Of dreaming together...Of manifesting together.

Our reason for being here is to establish our credibility with other members as ethical, qualified, experienced, talented, action oriented, and caring human beings who are passionate about helping others create more abundance and freedom in their lives. We believe that by divesting of "stuff" and focusing on what is really important in life, new opportunities, relationships and lifestyles open up.

It is our belief that new technologies and the world wide web is the key to opening up more possibilities for EVERYBODY, but the key is to get back to the essentials of life and relationship to other people, to the earth and to businesses that truly serve.

Specialties

Our specialty is uniting traveling home owners and their beloved pets with house sitters / pet sitters in all parts of the world. We do it by matching them through a cutting edge membership website

What makes us unique is our personal and professional experience with actual housesitting experience, owning a construction and property management business for many years and a decade of creating and running online businesses.
