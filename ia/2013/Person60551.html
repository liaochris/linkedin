Jeanine Wheeler's Summary

During her thirteen-plus years as a real estate consultant, Jeanine has developed a genuine passion for helping home buyers and sellers achieve their real estate dreams. Whether she’s working with first time buyers or luxury home owners looking to sell, Jeanine offers unparalleled dedication and specialized attention to each client.

100% of Jeanine wheeler’s business is generated through referrals. Proving that being represented by an agent whose knowledge and commitment goes above and beyond is an invaluable gift to give and have. Jeanine is committed to delivering ‘all inclusive’ real estate services and provides assistance every step of the way, from negotiating to closings to giving contractor recommendations. In addition, Jeanine has been a Chicago resident for fourteen years and can offer expert insight on virtually every neighborhood in the city.

She is very active within the Chicago real estate community; Jeanine is a member of the Chicago association of Realtors’, women’s council of Realtors’ and serves on the professional standards committee of the Chicago association of Realtors’.

Prior to joining @properties, Jeanine excelled in the corporate health/fitness industry where she honed her ability to assess her clients’ wants and needs and assisted them in achieving their goals. Later, Jeanine successfully applied this talent to real estate when she began her career with a prominent Chicago relocation company.

When she’s not helping clients, Jeanine enjoys yoga, photography, travel, biking and exploring Chicago's newest restaurants. Jeanine has completed a 105-mile bike ride through death valley and the Canadian rockies on behalf of the juvenile diabetes research foundation. Recently Jeanine completed her first half iron man triathlon.
