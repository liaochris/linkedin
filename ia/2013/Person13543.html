Bert Meijers' Summary

I work freelance as an independent Advisor and Project Manager on cutting edge projects to propel social innovation and impact investments.

For several years I've been actively involved in the field of Social Enterprise and Impact Investing through doing business development for a leading impact investing firm in Amsterdam and by producing events such as Compostmodern and the SOCAP Conferences in San Francisco.

With Berlin as my homebase I initiate and work internationally on exciting projects.
In my work I combine the creativity of design thinking with the entrepreneurial approach of business for social impact.
