Leticia Barr's Summary

I'm a DC Metro area dwelling mom with a background in classroom technology integration and school administration who uses everything I learned from teaching children, teachers, and principals as a professional blogger, columnist, social media maven, and consultant who advises about family internet safety and security issues.

I am passionate about working with companies who are dedicated to protecting kids both in real life and in the ever-changing virtual worlds they inhabit when they sit down at their computers or access the internet through mobile devices.

I also like using my teaching background to educate parents about topics related to online safety and help bloggers in their personal professional development.

Specialties

technology, education, family internet safety and security, social media consulting, freelance writing, professional blogging, brand relations, blogger relations
