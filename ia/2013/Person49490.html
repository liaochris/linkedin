Goran Lozo's Summary

Training specialist, instructional designer, consultant, and speaker with extensive international experience and familiarity with multiple cultural protocols.
Areas of Expertise: Instructional Systems Design, Leadership Development, Conflict Resolution, Professional Performance, Personal Improvement, Team Management, Human Resources, Customer/Client Service, Employee Wellness Management, Physical Fitness Training Methodology.

Goran Lozo is a recognized professional coach, corporate trainer, consultant, and an entertaining motivational speaker who specializes in topics related to personal improvement, business productivity, health, and fitness. Every Thursday morning at 7:45 Goran hosts the Tough Love with Goran segment on the Regular Guys Show, Atlanta’s Rock Station 100.5 (www.regularguys.com)
In different professional capacities, Goran has traveled the world and visited, worked, and/or professionally competed in 39 different countries.
Goran earned his coaching degree from the Faculty of Sport and Physical Education, University of Belgrade, and has completed his major requirements for a degree in Workforce Education and Development at Southern Illinois University.
Goran is a retired professional athlete, and a national and international karate champion. He currently holds a 6th Degree Black Belt in Shotokan Karate. He is a former member of Special Forces Units of the Yugoslavian Army and a retired Executive Protection Agent for politicians and corporate executives in the former Republic of Yugoslavia.
In 1992, Goran’s life was the subject of Samurai and Mad Saga, a documentary movie produced by TV Belgrade.
He also has been the subject of many interviews and news articles in a variety of newspapers and magazines. In addition, Goran is the author of multiple publications and articles.
Goran resides in Atlanta, GA. His wife Gordana is a software engineer and their daughter Sara is one accomplished, confident, and self sufficient individual.

    The author Goran Lozo has been a successful active athlete for many years.   • Goran Lozo's Workout Logbook and Journal

    • Vincha Publishing, Atlanta, GA, USA
    • December 2011
    Authors: Goran Lozo

    Most published workout logbooks are designed for only one sport, usually running. This Workout Logbook and Journal is much better choice for every recreational and professional athlete because this logbook is designed to keep track of workouts in many different sports and fitness activities. When you set up your personal fitness goal, keeping the track of all workouts will help you stay focused and motivated. When you record your workouts in your personal logbook you can easily summarize what you did in the previous week, month, or a year and based on collected information you can create or adjust your training plan for the next week, month, or a year. In addition to practical value, this logbook will become one of your greatest memories. When you revisit your old workout logbooks after several years, you will see it not as a logbook, but as a testament to your character, dedication, and persistence. Throughout his successful professional career author Goran Lozo has kept records of his workouts. The information and statistics in his workout logbooks were the foundation for developing and executing training plans for upcoming competitions.
