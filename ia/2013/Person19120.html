Craig Johnson's Summary

"Genius is one percent inspiration and ninety-nine percent perspiration." - Thomas Edison

Since mankind has been on this earth we have been creators. We build. We engineer. We craft. We construct. We design. It's who we are. I believe every single person was created for something LARGER THAN LIFE! (And if they don't know that that is, I want to help them figure it out.) Everyone deserves to spend their life doing the things that they love to do.

I am inspired by the future and what could be. I love to inspire others with a compelling vision of what the future could bring. I take great satisfaction in being busy and productive. Faced with any given scenario, I believe I can quickly spot the relevant patterns and issues and I love to create alternative ways to proceed. I focus on strengths and minimize weaknesses as a way to stimulate personal and group excellence. My passion is to transform something strong into something superb. I make things happen by turning thoughts into action and tend to be very impatient.

Why should we work together?
1) I love meeting new people and will assist in creating a positive and productive working environment.
2) I am a quick learner. I love to turn "What if" into "What is".
3) I will always do the right thing for the client.
4) We will have a blast!
