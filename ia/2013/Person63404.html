John F. Renner's Experience

New Jersey Lawyer - Work Injury, Federal Criminal Defense and Probate

John F. Renner, P.C. Attorney at Law

January 1994 – Present (20 years) Marlton, NJ 08053

Mr. Renner has served the South Jersey community as a lawyer in private practice since January of 1994 focusing on protecting the rights of injured workers and representing individuals accused of crimes in before both the Superior Court of New Jersey and the United States District Court for the District of New Jersey and the United States District Court for the Eastern District of Pennsylvania.

Mr. Renner also actively represents the personal representative of decedent's estates throughout South Jersey in handling the final affairs of loved ones who have passed both with and without a will. Mr. Renner also has been court appointed in various counties to protect the rights of alleged incompetents who face the possibility of a determination of incompetency.
