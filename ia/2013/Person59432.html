Jason Pinto's Projects

  • CBG Benefits: Corporate Website

    • November 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Jason Pinto

    Upon joining the team at CBG Benefits, one of my initial undertakings was to completely revamp the corporate website. Our main goals were:

    - To build a new look and feel for the website: We wanted one that was vibrant, but that also reflected our position as a leader in the world of Employee Benefits and Insurance.

    - To turn the site into an interactive experience for readers: We wanted to do this by setting up a Blog that would be updated multiple times a week, developing online calculators, creating sign-up forms for a variety of resources, and more.

    - To integrate social media into our website

    - To offer a responsive experience for website visitors that were coming from a mobile device such as a smartphone or tablet.

    - To turn the website into a lead-generating machine for our sales reps and producers.

    In early December 2012, we launched the completely redesigned CBGBenefits.com! We will continually be making improvements to this site in terms of content and design --- but we are quite happy to have moved this important project forward so quickly!
