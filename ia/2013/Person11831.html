    • December 2, 2012
    Authors: Barış Geçer, Michal Snoch, Aleksandra Cepinska, Daniel Arias Mutis

    Purpose of this paper is presentation of results of conducted experiment which was implementation of automated song genre recognition system which is a text classification problem.
