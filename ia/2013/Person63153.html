Jessica Phan's Summary

Embraced in the creative and innovative Silicon Valley vortex for the last 10 years, Jessica’s passion lies in discovering and creating various design solutions to improve the world and the interaction of the digital space. With a background in Design Studies and Graphic Design, Jessica is a design evangelist in the marriage of form and function to create delightful, yet meaningful human-centered experiences. Aside from sketching, prototyping and pushing pixels, Jessica enjoys reading Fast Company, watching TED Talks, browsing Cute Overload, sharing quotes on life and love, reflecting in nature, and connecting with other passionate, curious-minded individuals.
