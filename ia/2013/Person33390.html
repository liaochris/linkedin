Debbie Melnikoff, MS Career Counseling's Summary

After pursuing a long and successful career in corporate Human Resources, I began to wonder if what I was doing professionally was fulfilling my life. The answer was ‘no’.

After undergoing a liberating process of self-discovery and better understanding my strengths and passions, I discovered that helping others build satisfying careers was both rewarding and allowed me to share my knowledge of the job search process, gleaned from over 20 years in talent acquisition. After all, I had been working on the other side of the ‘hiring desk’ and I knew that the knowledge gained from that experience could certainly help others successfully navigate the job search process as well as develop career management tools to last a lifetime.

This dream came to life in 2009 when I launched Third Wave Career Coaching. As a Certified Career Coach with a Masters in Career Counseling, I assist both corporate and individual clients in the areas of: Job search strategy; life purpose; corporate career navigation; career branding; social media job search strategy; resume development; effective interviewing skills and salary negotiation.

I became a Certified Social Media Career Strategist through The Academies in July 2012. I love helping my clients leverage the power of LinkedIn, Facebook and Twitter to market themselves effectively and develop career management strategies to last a lifetime.

My clients would tell you that I coach on intuition which is one of my strengths. “Debbie is an outstanding and intuitive career coach. She understood what I was trying to accomplish and provided the tools I needed to navigate the interview process and land the perfect position.”

My corporate clients who have struggled with navigating their careers within a company have shared with me that my experience “across industries as a Human Resources professional” helped them work more effectively with “peers and managers” resulting in finding the best fit without having to change employers.
