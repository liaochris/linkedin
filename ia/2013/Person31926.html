David Collier's Summary

David Collier founded Tilles EMC to provide ethically grounded business advice to small and medium sized business owners. Providing services including business strategy, marketing, sustainable sourcing, project management and employee engagement, David is a firm believer that there is no need to over-complicate issues and likes to act far more than he likes to talk. His method, the basis for the Tilles portfolio is to understand the individual needs of the client and then enable them to create for themselves a better and more productive sustainable business environment.

David has business experience crossing 3 decades opening his first limited company, a small export firm, when he had just turned 20. Before leading Tilles, David's business career had crossed 5 continents and spanned international trade & investment, travel, hospitality, publishing, furniture, retail, import / export and events.

With personal experience in leading companies from such diverse fields as travel and publishing, David's career has led him to accumulate a cross sector field of knowledge in the international business environment. David has worked in the field in conflict zones, alongside the UN, NGO's and Government Embassies whilst delivering both critical services and support for grass roots growth projects.

David returned to university education in his 40's as part of his desire to stay in tune with best practice in his fields of expertise, and is currently pursuing further academic study in the areas of Sustainability, Governance and Business Ethics. Never one to sit idly, David is always willing to discuss new ventures and partnerships, is glad to lend a hand to new green business ideas, help his local community and can be contacted either through the @tillescentral Twitter page or through David's profile on Linked in.

Specialties

David's main business interest now lies in business impact on society and the environment.
