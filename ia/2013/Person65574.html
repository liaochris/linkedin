Joel Wilson's Summary

We live in a time where technology and culture are constantly shifting.

The tools to make a difference are becoming more accessible all the time.

I am passionate about equipping people with these tools, encouraging them to take risks, and helping them make a difference.

Some of my favorite resources are:
Seth Godin's Startup School (podcast)
I can hardly recommend this 15 part series enough. It is a chance to listen in on Seth's interactions with a small group of entrepreneurs over the course of a 3 day intensive workshop. I have listened to it several times and consider it an important education in itself.
http://www.earwolf.com/show/startup-school/

On Writing Well (Book by William Zinsser)
This book changed the way I write. It calls us to write with clarity. A wonderful side-effect is that it challenges us to also think more clearly.

Rework (Book by Jason Fried and David Heinemeier Hansson)
These guys from 37Signals are at the forefront of rethinking what it means to run a successful business. They have some great thoughts on what it means to be in the new world of work.

“If given a choice between investing in someone who has read REWORK or has an MBA,
I’m investing in REWORK every time. A must read for every entrepreneur.” - Mark Cuban
