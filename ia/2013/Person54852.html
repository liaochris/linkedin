I.J. Ikavalko's Experience

Executive Director

IICC Nanofund

December 2010 – Present (2 years 11 months)

The IICC Nanofund™ (IICCNF) manages the nanoscience, nanotechnology consulting and investment portfolio of IICC, the Houston-based merchant bank founded in 1991 by former Shell Oil pension fund head, Leonard Chaikind. IICCNF is linked to academic and commercial nanotechnology organizations. The company's mission is to provide funding and consulting services, mindful of its balance of ethical (nano-bio, green projects), scientific and investment objectives. The venture was founded to provide superior investor return and a commitment to support 21st-C. nanoscience from laboratory to the markets.

President

MultiMate

October 1981 – July 1984 (2 years 10 months)

President / co-founder of the firm that, with a half dozen brilliant young talents and several hundred thousand dollars from an insurance company, developed the leading word processing application of the time in Assembler (!).
