Henry Loewendahl's Summary

Dr Henry Loewendahl has been a pioneer in expanding our knowledge of FDI, competitiveness, and investment promotion.

His landmark publications on investment promotion - “A Framework for Investment Promotion” (published by UNCTAD) and “Handbook for Investment Promotion” (published by Columbia University) - are widely considered the most authoritative guides on how to attract FDI.

After a successful consulting career in PwC and IBM-Plant Location International, Henry co-founded fDi Intelligence, ICA Incentives, Loco Software, and WAVTEQ, all of which are recognized leaders in their respective areas of FDI.

Henry has advised over 100 investment promotion organizations in over 50 countries on how to attract FDI. He has also advised many of the world’s largest companies on their site selection. Henry has been an expert to UNCTAD and the World Bank on FDI and works closely with Vale Columbia Center on Sustainable Development.

Henry is regularly invited to speak and run workshops at intergovernmental and industry conferences on FDI, including by AIM, APEC, ASEAN, UNCTAD, and WAIPA.

He was awarded a Volkswagen scholarship for a PhD in FDI, which was subsequently published as book called “Bargaining with Multinationals” by Palgrave, and he also has a Masters degree in International Political Economy and a BA in Economic and Politics. Henry is co-editor of The fDi Report, published by Financial Times Ltd.

Henry is co-located in Turkey and Canada.

Specialties

Corporate location strategy; FDI trends and location competitiveness; Investment promotion strategy; Investment promotion organization; Marketing locations and cities for inward investment; Investor targeting and lead generation; Investment tools and software; Websites and Mobile Apps for investment promotion; Key note speaker; and Training workshops
