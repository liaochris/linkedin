Georgios Gatos' Summary

I love startups and technology oriented companies. I am an expert in narrowing the gap between a concept idea and its targeted market, by working on business elements and/or fundraising issues.

I consulted many ICT related companies in the past and now I joined one. Being part of incrediblue allows me to express myself and put into action all the advise that helped others in the past.

More "hands on" rather consulting these days :)

I began my career in the Center for Entrepreneurship and Innovation at the University of Florida. Over the years I worked as a consultant of small and medium size innovative companies responsible for business planning and fundraising and later worked as an investment analyst and advisor of Venture Capital firms.

FuIT was my personal consulting brand, through which I offered the necessary advice to startups and other innovative companies, before and during their fundraising process.

Nowadays incrediblue gets 101% of my time, but if there is something really interesting that fits incrediblue's goals or requires free consultation (good karma), I don't mind having a coffee or exchange a couple of emails to discuss.

I actively participate in groups fostering entrepreneurship and innovation, business plan competitions and venture academies, curate the Open Coffee Thessaloniki meetings and belong to the TEDxThessaloniki team.
