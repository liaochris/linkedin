David Kalman's Summary

Senior management professional in media and marketing, working for 30 years at the intersection of business and technology.

Now, I'm launching a social commerce venture with a group of talented co-founders.

In my career, I've headed multi-disciplinary teams of up to 47, run as many as 11 profit centers at-a-time, served as editor-in-chief of two prominent business technology magazines, sold ads and managed sales teams, written two-and-a-half technology books and hundreds of articles and white papers, spoken in front of audiences of up to 5,000, and launched major publications, Web sites, and events.

In my spare time, I produce/write the BrandMagnet content marketing blog and I help publishers with product strategies, organizational development, sales, and marketing. I also help other types of organizations with content marketing, i.e., branded publications and events.
