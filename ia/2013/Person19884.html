Carla Cross' Experience

President

Carla Cross Coaching

January 2006 – Present (7 years 8 months)

A real estate-specific coaching company utilizing proven real estate productivity/profitability tools. CC Coaching has three distinct programs: One-on-one for achieving agents, one-on-one for owners/managers, and a new very affordable production/acceleration program for agents which integrates and coordinates certified 'master CC coaches' in their offices with our professional CC coaches. This is an entirely new approach, because it teamifies all those invested in the success of the agent: the agent himself, the manager, and our CC professional real estate coaches.

Carla Cross Seminars, Inc.

Her resources are endorsed and recommended by Realtor professional organizations CRS and CRB. Carla's resources are more than 'tips': They are complete operational, sales, and management systems.
