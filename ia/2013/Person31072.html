Dave Franckowiak's Summary

Senior Financial Executive with a successful track record of leading accounting and finance teams in publicly traded, private equity owned and privately held organizations. Financial leadership experience includes positions of Chief Financial Officer (CFO) with small and mid-size companies with strong focus on controls and governance, as well as Director level assignments with very large firms. Industry background includes professional services, distribution, building products, telecommunications and biotechnology. BS and Masters in Accounting, Certified Public Accountant (CPA) with Big Four public accounting and public company experience, including Sarbanes-Oxley.

Specialties

Finance, accounting, operations, team-based continuous improvement, internal controls, leadership development, debt financing, investor relations, private-equity, strategic planning performance metrics, mergers and acqusitions, cash management, troubled company work-out, distribution, manufacturing, professional services, telecommunications and biotechnology.
