Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Utilities industry

September 2012 – Present (1 year 2 months) Luik

Lampiris is the fastest growing energy supplier of Belgium. A the logical alternative to the incumbent suppliers, they need to push the market. My role is to ensure the company innovates on all levels, from marketing over service design to corporate culture.
My work is mainly project based, where I also try to install scalable systems and processes that will survive the project itself. Typical projects have a 3 year (thus strategic) vision and a short term (<6 month) initial execution (proof of concept / minimum viable product).
I work as a freelance consultant and report to the CEO and everyone else who is interested.

Koen Delvaux's Projects

  • Social Media Integration

    • July 2011 to April 2012
    Team Members: Koen Delvaux, Tom Vanbelle, Adriaan Reynders, Luc Galoppin

    Together with Koen Delvaux & Luc Galoppin from Medemerkers (www.medemerkers.be), we developed a new social media strategy for Media Markt Belgium. Instead of just creating 1 Facebook page, we decided to create a new role in every outlet: the community manager. Every store is now responsible for their own Facebook community. I took care of the implementation of the project and guided the community managers in the process: how to handle customer complaints, engage with fans, push promotions without boring or pushing your fans, etc. We also developed a 'Contest' application so every community manager can legally manage contests using Facebook.
