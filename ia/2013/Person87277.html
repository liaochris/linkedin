Marla Cummins' Summary

Guiding and Supporting Adults with ADHD (and similar challenges) to Reach their Goals

• Are your frustrated because you know that you are not reaching your potential?
• And, if you have ADHD, you think your ADHD symptoms are getting in your way.
• Then know that there is support to help you minimize your challenges and capitalize on your strengths to reach your goals.

I am fiercely committed to guiding adults with ADHD to implement successful, proven ways of minimizing the challenges of ADD while accentuating your strengths so that you can achieve your goals and dreams.

If you are looking for a proven professional to help you optimize your ability to follow through and complete your most important activities and streamline your personal and professional tasks, you have come to the right place.

I will help you adopt the skills and confidence you need to be successful.

Please explore my website, www.marlacummins.com, for more information about me and my business, Cummins Coaching and Training.
