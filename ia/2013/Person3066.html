Alan Brown's Summary

Graduating in 2004 with an HND in Graphic Design and a further Advanced Diploma in Creative Arts.

Employed as a Graphic / Web designer straight from leaving education, I now have 7 years of experience in many areas of Graphic Design including web, print, identity, animation and 3D art.

The general all round Graphic Design experience served as a great platform to progress. Keen to master one area of my career, I spent the last 4 years focusing mainly on web design and build, deciding in 2010 to specialise in Digital Design by moving to a successful Glasgow based agency.

Currently working on digital design and front end development for clients such as Russell, Scottish Power and The University of Glasgow.

Specialties

- Creating interesting concepts from design briefs.
- High standard web visuals.
- A broad understanding of web technologies.
- Animation including Flash, jQuery and Video.
