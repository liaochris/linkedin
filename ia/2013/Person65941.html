Johannes Brodwall's Projects

  • Statnett: LARM

    • August 2010 to Present
    Team Members: Johannes Brodwall, Anders Karlsen, Anders Gisnås, Frode Carlsen, Mats Thoresen Augustson, Margrethe Sæther Johannessen, Øystein Urdahl Hardeng, Kim A. Martinsen, Trude Martinsen, Karl Ove Vika Sørensen, Pål Berg, Tore Skobba, Espen Wahlstrøm, Stein-Ole Gabrielsen, Eivind Mølster, Ram Yoga

    Role: Lead solution architect (technical and business requirements)

    Statnett is the Transmission System Operator (TSO) for the Norwegian electricity grid. Among their responsibilities, they are tasked with ensuring that the minute-to-minute production of electricity matches the actual usage. This is done though procuring various types of reserve electricity capacity.

    The LARM project modernizes and extends the current solution for acquiring, using and reporting on these reserves as well as many aspects of real-time reporting on the status of the electricity grid.

    The project replaces a large existing system in stepwise releases. The new system has a simple architecture that reduces the cost of ownership as well as the cost of deployment and operations. It is build in Java with a automatically provisioned Swing front-end which communicates over HTTP with a Jetty-based server on top of an Oracle database.
