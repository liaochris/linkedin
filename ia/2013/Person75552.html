Founder and CEO at Danes.tv

Danes.tv

June 2011 – Present (2 years 3 months) Berkeley, ca

Danes.tv is the first and only film-focused website devoted entirely to the Danish experience in America, from the 200,000 first generation immigrants currently living in the States, to those 1,500,000 Danish-Americans who can trace their ancestral roots directly back to Denmark. Through video vignettes and multiple interviews, we capture their hopes and dreams as well as their frustrations and disappointments, and in so doing, explore how Danes forge their true self-identity in their new and complex homeland.
