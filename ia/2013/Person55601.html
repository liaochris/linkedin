Tiffany McDonald's Summary

Nearly 70% of consumers go to the Internet before making a decision to purchase a good or service. My passion is to make sure when you go to the Internet, your business is what your customers find first. From search engine optimization to paid ads to business directory submission, I can increase your business' online visibility. You want to make sure when someone is searching online, they find you or else you are giving business to your competitors.

Aside from being addicted to social media, I love data and statistics. I believe that is why I love internet marketing so much. The metrics are readily available and I'm able to gauge whether or not a campaign is working. Of course, social media is not quite as intuitive but it sure is fun!
