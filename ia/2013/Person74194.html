Kenneth Hoffman's Summary

Ken Hoffman has more than 25 years of hands on experience in the restaurant & hospitality industry holding positions from dishwasher to C level positions. Ken is the Founder and CEO of International Restaurant & Hospitality Consulting Group based in Orlando Florida. International Restaurant & Hospitality Consulting Group has helped multiple companies increase revenue and profits from individual owners to the fortune 5. Ken is a prolific platform speaker who speaks frequently at industry functions, universities and corporate events. Featured in training and business operation textbook, news articles and videos.

Specialties

International Restaurant, Retail, Hotel & Hospitality Industry Advisor and Consultant, -Revenue and Profit Enhancement Programs Efficiency, Employee Training, Leadership Development, Revenue Management Programs,Guest Experince Programs
