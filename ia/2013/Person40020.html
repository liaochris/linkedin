Ethel Gondwe's Summary

I am an entrepreneur and artist. As the proud owner of BEFFTA award winning company Effycom Productions, and 2 time BEFFTA nominated pageant Queen Of Zambia UK; I am definitely not one to shy away from hard work. My talents also include singing , presenting, acting, and modelling. I am determined to succeed in everything I put my efforts to; as a self confessed workaholic, I strives for perfection, and I am always willing to help others achieve their goals. I am currently moulding and training the next round of contestants for Queen of Zambia UK pageant, I work as an artist agent with Indemand UK Agency and I work independently with various record labels, artists and individuals looking to brand their start-up business. There is no challenge too tough for me to take on. Being immersed in an industry that requires a lot of thick skin, i make sure that I maintain great poise, a positive attitude whilst still commanding the respect from those i work with.

I have maintained my focus mainly on the Entertainment industry by working with various record labels such as Baby Girl Music and Just Bounce Records. I quickly moved up the ladder due to my savvy work ethic and infectious personality. This gave me the platform to liaise with the real movers and shakers of the entertainment industry and thus provided me with the ground work required to push start my company, cleverly derived from my nick name "effy" Effycom Productions.

Effycom Productions, was established in 2008, I took advantage of my artistic skills by making Effycom Productions a branding and marketing company, and I have since worked alongside some great individuals and establishments like MTV, BBC1xtra, KISS100 etc. This has elevated me to a position where others seeking knowledge about the industry and how to generaly start a business, come to me for advice on their career options and general motivation on how to cope within the industry and how to further their careers.

Specialties

Artist negotiations, brand Identity, Artist grooming, Artist Representation, Multimedia, business consultancy, Image and Styling
