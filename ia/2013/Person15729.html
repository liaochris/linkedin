Bonny Katzman's Summary

BIO:
In 1996, Bonny established BK Design, an independent studio specializing in event branding and the design of unique 3/Dimensional invitations and accessories for any celebration or event. Bonny’s recent work for Denis Leary, Dan Brown (author of The DaVinci Code) and the CEO of Hallmark Entertainment has garnered international creative awards. Corporate clients include Harvard Business School, Hewlett-Packard, Ropes & Gray, Bank of America, Rosie’s Place, Fidelity, HBO, Pfizer, Long Wharf Marriott, Rapid7, American Institutes for Research, The Red Cross, Opera Boston, Fuller Craft Museum and many private clients. Bonny has a BFA in graphic design from Rhode Island School of Design.

Bonny has over 25 years of experience in the creative industry. She has created corporate identity programs and Annual Reports for a wide range of Fortune 500 clients, including AT&T, Mastercard, Liz Claiborne, Fidelity Investments and Tyco, and has held senior roles at leading advertising, marketing, design and public relations firms in NYC, including Hill & Knowlton, Bessen, Tully & Lee and The Graphic Expression.

Articles include Exhibitors Magazine, Special Events Magazine, The Boston Globe, Meeting and Conventions Magazine, RISD Views, Be My Guest by Rena Kirdar Sinda, and A Passion for Parties by David Tutera.
