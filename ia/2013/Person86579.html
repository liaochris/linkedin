Mark Fenner's Summary

High-performing companies recognize that leadership is action ... backed by conviction and knowledge. It is the the ultimate deal breaker when it comes to growing organizations. Without solid leadership companies squander millions of dollars and impact the lives of those that could have been great employees. Company owners are just now beginning to understand that leadership is a teachable, principle-based skill.

My career is the culmination of over two decades of selling, hiring and training with start-ups to Fortune 500 companies. Focusing on the right people, the right relationships and using the best technologies to enhance growth have proven 300% increases to the bottom-line.

Now, as a John Maxwell Certified Speaker, Coach and Trainer, my workshops, seminars, lectures and coaching allow me to share these proven leadership methods. From one-on-one coaching to audience participation, these workshops are designed to improve the person and the leader.
