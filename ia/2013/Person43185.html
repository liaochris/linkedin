Ethan Lieber's Summary

Mobile and web app focused Product Manager, Product Owner, and Agile Project Manager with extensive offshore management experience. Offer a track record of leading global teams to deliver increased profit, intuitive user experience, and long term business solutions.

Product Manager who increased revenue streams through a social and fitness application for a $650MM annual weight loss company. Agile project management of teams spread from Argentina, San Diego, Detroit, Los Angeles, and Salt Lake City to rapidly deliver features into the market.

Built project teams offshore around defined processes and clear roadmaps. Led a Belizean development team of 10 members alongside an internet marketing team of 40 members to build and utilize a suite of SEO software applications, including a ranking tool and operations CRM.

User experience and mobile design specialist who cofounded a mobile POS startup for both concessions ordering in stadiums and menu ordering in restaurants. Designed CMS system to rapidly release customized mobile ordering applications for restaurants.

Graduated UC Berkeley with a Bachelors in Economics. A focus and determination on designing sleek, intuitive user experiences. A founder of multiple businesses and a manager of international offices, I've developed a well rounded skill set that includes operational process design, sales, management of multiple departments, and agile project management.
