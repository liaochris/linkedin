Mayank Agrawal's Publications

  • A method for converting text flow based review comments to floating review comments and exporting them to Adobe® PDF for reviewers’ reference during an iterative PDF based review process

    • The IP.com Prior Art Database
    • February 23, 2011
    Authors: Mayank Agrawal

    This invention explains a method for converting inline text comments applied on a document through a text editing application to Acrobat®’s floating comments which are then exported to PDF. This also allows a reviewer to receive author’s response on each of his/her comments thus allowing for a communication between the author and the reviewer.
