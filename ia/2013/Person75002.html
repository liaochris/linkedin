Kevin Kopp's Summary

15+ years of community service as a Farmers Insurance Agent. I specialize in the unique issues confronting property owners living near the gulf coast. I have extensive experience with the Texas Windstorm Insurance Association programs as well as the National Flood Insurance programs. I am licensed for the state of Texas in the areas of: Property and Casualty Insurance, (business and personal) Life Insurance, Health Insurance, and Workers Compensation Insurance.
My goal is to earn the reputaion of being a respected resource of competent advice on insurance and risk management issues as well as, to provide a level of service that exceeds our clients expectations.

Specialties

I have a CIC professional desgination. (Certified Insurance Counselor) I specialize in Home insurance, Windstorm insurance, Flood insurance, Small Business insurance, Workers Compensation insurance, Auto insurance, Life insurance and Income tax preparation for individuals and sole proprietors.
