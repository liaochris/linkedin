Martin Mayne's Summary

You are in litigation and have an overwhelming amount of relevant information in email and other electronic media. I will help you get that relevant infomation ready for trial.

As a member of Sedona WG1* and with a broad knowledge base of discovery issues as they relate to the identification, preservation, collection and processing of relevant electronically stored information, together we will get you through the meet and confer process, IT interviews, 30(b)(6) depositions and the rest of the discovery process including review and document productions.

On very short notice, I can put together a flexible and scalable team of litigation experts to work on your case. We will function like a seamless extension of your law firm or legal department.

*The Sedona Conference, Working Group 1, focuses on Electronic Document Retention and Production
