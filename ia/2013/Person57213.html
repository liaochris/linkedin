NetPivotal was born out of 7 years of experience in servicing the web hosting industry. My online marketing experience allowed us to position the netpivotal brand into what, at the time, was the fastest growing segment of the web hosting industry.

Often demanding and always challenging, the development of a web hosting business really allowed me to experience the world from an ISPs perspective.

After 4 years, I sold the business to the management team who have gone onto really develop the services into a highly sustainable SME focused business, operating in both the UK and United States.

Overall the experience has enabled me to increase our marketing offerings within my core business (www.serchen.com) to better facilitate our ever expanding technology focused client base
