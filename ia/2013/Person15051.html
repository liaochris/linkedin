Jay Joya's Summary

I have been working in the art and crafts business for three decades. I used to own a custom sign company, and then got involved importing art to the United States, and finally for the past ten years, I have been enjoying life with my hobby as a multi-faced artist and working with real photographers and artists as their picture framer.

My services are offered as wholesale online and at my local store in San Rafael, California. I have made it easy for the artists to present, preserve and promote their art.

I love what I am doing, and this is the type of job I like do even after I retire.

Specialties

Craftsman - will not say no to any difficult job ahead of me.
