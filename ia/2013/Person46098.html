Fredrik Lyreskog's Summary

Business oriented consultant in Stockholm, with 8 years of professional experience in media, IT and communications industry.
Working with Agile Project Management at Creuna, one of the Top Interactive Agencies in Europe.
Advising multi billion dollar companies, including one of the world's 100 most innovative companies, according to Forbes.

I have managed large scale multi-site platforms, with 500 corporate websites in 70 countries all together. Work tasks includes Scrum/Agile work flows, outsourcing management, marketing automation, cross Nordic campaign coordination, data measurement and analysis, staffing, budgeting, planning, and drinking lots of coffee.

I'm CMS independent, with experience in EPiServer, WordPress, Drupal, Joomla, and Umbraco.

I've won a few awards, authored an e-book, and published hundreds of articles and reports on marketing, web and business.

Fredrik Lyreskog's Projects

  • New digital platform for the news agency TT

    • May 2011 to October 2011
    Team Members: Fredrik Lyreskog

    TT (Tidningarnas Telegrambyrå), the leading news agency in the nordics, thought it was time for a major redesign and rethink of their digital approach.
    We was analyzing their needs and opportunities, as well as working with their visual identity, customer service requirements and business objectives.

    We have tried to find a balance between guiding information and sales messages. TT has a rich history and is daily producing fantastic rich media such as photos, graphics and video - elements we wanted to give room and highlight. The news slider "TT Live" shows clearly that at TT, big things happens around the clock.

    The end result was a modern, rich media corporate website built with open source technology.

  • Intangible Artifacts - Swedens greatest crowdsourced magazine

    • August 2010 to August 2010
    Team Members: Fredrik Lyreskog

    Intangible Artifacts was a very unique magazine experiment in collaboration and creativity.
    We pushed the limits of social media interaction and mass contribution by crowdsourcing 100% of the content creation.
    We applied new methods and work flows making it a total transparent production.
    Creative Commons licenses solved our 0 euro editorial budget, and of course we gave back by giving the magazine away for free - and letting a digital version spread without boundaries.

    Intangible Artifacts went a masterpiece in strategic place branding - peoples relation to their homes and environments, digital conversations, innovations, knowledge sharing and the meetings between people in their every day life.

    The release party? In the same spirit, we launched and celebrated together with over 400 web experts, geeks, journalists and media gurus at Sweden Social Web Camp (SSWC) - Swedens first and largest unconference.

  • Creation of an industry-leading editorial platform for Fastighetsnytt

    • September 2011 to February 2012
    Team Members: Fredrik Lyreskog

    We created a new editorial platform for Fastighetsnytt.se - the leading magazine in the swedish real estate business.

    Our mission was to build an industry-leading editorial site that gives the readers both overview and depth through the news flow. Fastighetsnytt's various external sister services did also have to become a natural part of the new digital platform.
    Other important pieces of the puzzle was a complete advertising system and maximum spread through social media and search engines.

    We took the complete responsibility for initial specifications, analysis of technical requirements (over 5000 articles were fetched out from their old databases and imported), strategic business objectives, web design, information architecture, and content strategic matters.
    My role was project manager, digital strategist, art director, and account manager.

  • LOOA Newsletter

    • October 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Fredrik Lyreskog, Peter Svenonius, Fredrik Colling, Evelina Ander, Sverker Wadstein, Martin Krajewski, Agnieszka Baltyn

    A swedish monthly newsletter in marketing, sales and business development, reaching heavy decision-makers all across Sweden. Covers topics such as digital ROI methods, lead-generation, cases, guides and the latest trends.

  • Matloopen
