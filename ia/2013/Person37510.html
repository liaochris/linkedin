Alan Hart's Summary

Working full time in startup business Plumis, following an entrepreneurially-directed MBA at Imperial College.

Previous experience includes 11 years in science/technology/innovation consultancy, inventing and developing new products and uncovering consumer needs to drive that innovation. My focus for several years was fast-moving consumer packaged goods.

Strong, pragmatic problem solving skills on a technical and commercial level. Excellent communication skills – written, verbal, presentation. Highly capable in both team-based and individual working. Reputation for positive attitude, systematic yet creative thinking, commitment to deadlines.

Specialties

My MBA included two studies of innovation activities within large firms: one examining informal networks in a software/electronics giant, the other seeking to understand a lack of consumer-focused innovation in the large online travel agents.

In consulting, my recent experience includes technology scouting and roadmapping, and innovation audits across large consumer goods companies. I also peer-reviewed all internal venture projects at Scientific Generics for several years.
