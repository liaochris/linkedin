Bob Phillips' Summary

I have been a full-time Realtor in South Orange County, in Southern California, since 1976 - over 37 years! I am affiliated with Realty ONE Group, in the heart of South Orange County, California, the community that I serve.
I am well versed in today's distressed situations, from loan modification consulting, to succesfully short selling houses since the early 1990's. I hold both the CDPE and the SFR distressed property assistance designations.
I also have experience in every "normal" type of residential real estate, from condos to multi-acre estates, from bare land to multi-unit income properties, and to working well with both landlords and tenants.
You can search the Southern California MLS from my website, http://BobPhillips.net
My Facebook fan page is http://www.facebook.com/BobPhillips.Realtor
My California BRE license # is 00581357

Specialties: Short sales. Normal sales. Working equally well with buyers, sellers, landlords and tenants.
