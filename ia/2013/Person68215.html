Jordan Day's Summary

I am a talented software developer who loves to write code. Programming is not only my career, but my passion and hobby as well.

I currently work at Mayville Engineering Company (MEC), a manufacturing company located in Mayville, WI, as a Software Developer. My main focus is on software used by the shipping department. Most of our systems are programmed for the .NET framework in C#, ASP.NET, and Visual Basic. I utilize these languages as well as JavaScript and PLSQL on a daily basis.

I also enjoy tinkering with the Linux operating system. This hobby led to a promotion at MEC and the addition of Linux System Administrator to my title. I manage a production and test server, both running Red Hat Enterprise Linux and four separate Oracle databases in addition to my programming responsibilities.

When I am not programming business applications or managing the Linux servers at work, I enjoy writing agent-based models. Emergence of seemingly complex behavior from simple rules fascinates me. I also enjoy studying new programming languages and tend to have a knack for picking them up quickly.

I am always looking for new challenges to overcome and ways to evolve my skillset.
