Melanie McLane's Summary

As a 33 year veteran of the industry, I have experienced up markets, down markets, and sideways markets. Because so much of my career has been devoted to education and training, I have tremendous experience and the ability to share my experience with others. I write many of my own courses, and can custom write a course for your needs. I am CDEI certified and also write onlne courses.

Specialties

Real estate brokerage, real estate appraisal, real estate sales; special knowledge of the REALTOR Code of Ethics, USPAP, and Pennsylvania license law.
