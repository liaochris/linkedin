Fred Doleac's Summary

Founder of Virtual Homes in 2000 which offered a complete Internet marketing solution for the real estate and mortgage industries. The company provided lead generation, lead notification, lead management, training and support.

Proprietary tools and systems included the Virtual Homes network of virtual community web sites(900+ web sites), VH LeadAlert (lead notification system) and VH LeadCentre (CRM solution).

Company transitioned from RE/MAX franchises to Virtual Homes Real Estate in June of 2009 as a regional independent operating in Massachusetts, New Hampshire, Maine, Rhode Island and Connecticut. Virtual Homes Inc. operated these real estate franchises utilizing company business processes and technology.

Established Praxius Technology as a division of Virtual Homes, Inc. as a provider of technology tools, systems and training for the real estate, title and lending industries. Primary product is the PraxiusPress content management system powered by Worpress.

I have 36 years experience as a real estate agent, 24 as a real estate Broker/Owner and previously owned two RE/MAX franchises (Amherst, NH and Boston, MA).

Specialties

NH Real estate brokerage, New Hampshire real estate agent, short sales, foreclosures, relocation, new construction, land development, recruiting, SEO specialist, CRM, training, real estate coaching. New Hampshire, Rhode Island, Connecticut, Maine and Massachusetts broker licenses.
