Martin Punchev's Summary

I’m a freelance 3D artist specializing in character and creature creation. I worked at Masthead Studios as a Technical Art Director and a 3D character Artist. Prior to working at Masthead I worked as a freelance 3d modeler for Advertising. I live in Sofia, Bulgaria, play lots of games and work hard to look , listen and learn from everything I can.

Specialties

character and hardsurface modeling, high-res sculpting, very precise in-game models, technical art direction for games, deep understanding of what a healthy art pipeline looks like, tech skills, tools development, strong passion for mentoring and leadership.
