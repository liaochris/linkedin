Jacqui Chew's Summary

CEO at iFusion Marketing, a fractional CMO consultancy that helps high growth potential clients grow. Seasoned digital strategist with hands-on experience in Lean marketing and product launches.

Well-versed in SaaS, media and internet-enabled businesses and their business models. Successful track record developing integrated marketing plans to support product and company launches, brand identity development, community building through social channels and b2b lead nurturing programs.

Industry focus: enterprise software on demand, consumer Internet, e-commerce, information security and console and online gaming.

Author of articles on social media for iMedia Connection, Chiefmarketer and Association Publishing and Atlanta Business magazines, and has provided expert commentary to business publications including DemandGen Report, Newspaper & Technology and DM News.

Brands: Abundant Closet, Chupa Chups USA, eBay, FashionAde.com, IBM e-Business Services, Invistics, KontrolFreek, Nanolumens, RealEstate.com, SSL International, Talent Sprocket, ThePort Network and Venture Atlanta.
