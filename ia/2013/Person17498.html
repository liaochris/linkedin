Brian Kenyon's Summary

I am a Project Manager at ICS and a Freelance Drupal website consultant and Online Marketing Strategist. I am looking for challenging projects that will allow me to grow my network with creative and talented people, improve on my IA/UX website production, mobile application development, and db architecture skills, and solve online marketing challenges with effective usability, SEO, PPC, SEM, and social media marketing.

I am passionate about producing creative websites and marketing solutions that connect people online and meet business objectives. I bring vision, creativity, enthusiasm, team spirit, and hard work to help production teams reach their goals and grow their influence. Through effective discovery and design, compelling content, and efficient implementation I produce great websites and grow online market share through strategic online marketing efforts.

I have a hands-on experience creating wireframes, gathering requirements, and leading teams to drive the production of websites while tracking project success through standard project tracking, website analytics, and user behavior analysis.

Previously I was an Interactive Producer, at iFactory and produced http://huntsman.upenn.edu, and http://vistahigherlearning.com/.
Prior to that I was an interactive Producer for Cramer Productions a Digital Marketing and Event Solutions agency. Through this experience I have acted as an interactive producer for websites like the award winning and 2008 MITX Finalist www.LittleLeagueCoach.org, www.MacworldExpo.com, www.EforAllExpo.com, and www.LiveAutographs.com.

After graduation I was the CTO at a startup Niche Concepts LLC focusing on research and development of wearable mobile digital video solutions. I also directed the JonesCAM Helmet Camera systems branding, online marketing programs, and e-commerce website development with OScommerce.

I graduated Cum Laude from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute in 2000 majoring in Electronic Media Arts and Communications.
