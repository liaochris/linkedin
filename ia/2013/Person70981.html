Douglas Gardner's Summary

I am a senior executive with a track record of success in product marketing and strategy, business development, and business operations in digital media, social media, and the wireless industry. Currently I am operating my consulting company, Jump Market Strategies LLC, where the American Red Cross National Headquarters and Neustar are two of my key customers. Connect with me to see how I can help you succeed too.

At the American Red Cross National Headquarters I am leveraging my experience to shape the Red Cross mobile strategy. Our strategy enabled the Red Cross to launch the “Text Haiti to 90999” campaign in response to the catastrophic earthquake in Haiti. The marketing campaign across traditional media, online, social media (Twitter and Facebook), and mobile reached a new generation of philanthropists who donated over $32.5 million through the mobile channel shattering all prior records for mobile charitable giving. I’m honored to be part of this amazing team.

Few people have touched as many facets of Internet content, social media, and wireless as I have. These categories are converging faster than ever before. I am thrilled that I have played a significant role in this evolution of digital media.

Key areas of expertise include the following:
• P&L management experience up to $180 million annual revenue
• Digital media product strategy and marketing (Internet, social media, wireless)
• Business development and sales operations
• International experience in Canada, Western Europe, and India

Specialties

Digital Media Executive, Digital Media and Wireless, Product Marketing, Business Development, Product Strategy, NPO and commercial
