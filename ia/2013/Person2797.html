Amanda Selin's Summary

I am a User Experience Designer in Milwaukee, WI. I make sure that systems, interfaces, graphics, and copy are intuitive to the user and communicate effectively. I love what I do because I get to focus on the details so that the user doesn't have to.

I am interested in building connections and experience in the field of ux, web/interactive design, graphic design, information architecture, marketing/advertising, architecture, branding, and communications.
