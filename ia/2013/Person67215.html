John Waters' Summary

We have spent the last 25 years working with, and for, small and medium sized businesses, across a wide range of sectors all over Ireland. Because at some stage we have seen it, and dealt with it, we understand the needs and problems of these businesses, we have developed a particular expertise in the SME area. Our clients can get on with driving their business forward with the assurance that their Accountancy, Payroll and Taxation needs are being taken care of.

We continually invest in the latest financial technology allowing us to deliver an efficient, accurate and cost effective Accountancy and Payroll service that suits each clients needs. We will monitor those needs to make sure you are getting the right level of service from us at all times.

Our dedicated and experienced staff guide our clients through what can sometimes be a most difficult business necessity, in a down- to- earth personal manner avoiding technical jargon where possible
