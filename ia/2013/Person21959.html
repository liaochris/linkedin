Channing Boucher's Summary

A Lifestyle Investment
This place is incredible and Crested Butte is an amazing town to call home. It's a dream come true for me to be here, watch my kids grow and to be a part of such a beautiful community! Seems like only yesterday, but I first hit town 20 years ago after graduating from the University of Washington. I came to test the area’s legendary mountain bike trails and backcountry ski slopes. I left town in 1994 and after a successful run in the software industry, I returned to Crested Butte to fulfill my dream.

Today, I consider myself very fortunate to be in the real estate business in Crested Butte. Working with people that share a similar affinity for this place gives me tremendous satisfaction. I like to think I specialize in helping folks with the process of investing in their Crested Butte lifestyles. The real estate transaction is only part of it.

We all have our reasons why we first ventured to Crested Butte. Perhaps those same reasons are why some of us choose to stay and call this place home. What are your reasons for learning about Crested Butte? Are you looking to make a lifestyle investment? I welcome the opportunity to help you with the process.

Specialties

My focus is providing unparalleled levels of service. To the benefit of my clients, I implement a leading-edge real estate marketing system, leveraging my distinct advantage with Internet and related electronic based advertising.
