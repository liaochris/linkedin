Brian Vertrees' Summary

When the time came to decide on an emphasis in college, marketing was my choice because it involved a blend of creativity, analytics, sociology and psychology. I realized I had a genuine interest in learning why people make the choices they do; from the brands they buy to the services they use. So it goes without saying that my first job in market research was the perfect way to launch into a marketing / sales career. I haven’t looked back. My passion for understanding what makes us all tick as a consumer continues to grow. The best ideas stem from our own adventures with products, brands and services, and so as the journey of life continues I keep an open eye and ear for those memorable experiences that make a difference.
