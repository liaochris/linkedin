Kevin Flores' Summary

With nearly 20 years of experience in technology, I have led diverse teams of project managers, programmers, creative designers, content and courseware developers, QA testers and IT engineers. I have worked with large corporations and startups on product, brand, marketing and consumer experiences.

I'm driven by purposeful design and smart engineering. I strive to know a little something about everything. To that end, I follow the latest advances in technology, interaction design, and social media. I make time to experiment with as many web and mobile apps, CMS and publishing platforms and gadgets as I can.

I've often held a role that crosses over Product and Project Management. I work closely with designers and participate in UI and UX design, creating wireframes, mockups and action flows. On the technical side, I work with engineers to produce architecture diagrams, functional specifications and API documentation. I advocate the value of test-driven development, analytics and agile methodologies. I know what it takes to design, build, deploy and maintain great software.

I consider myself quite fortunate to have worked on a broad range of projects with budgets from "bootstrapped" to multi-million dollar. This has provided the experience to identify the best development strategy, the right team mix and appropriate technologies for a particular solution. I consider myself to be a very strong and detailed communicator with stakeholders, designers and programmers.

My biggest strength is adaptability; I don't shy away from a challenge and am dedicated to problem solving and creating memorable user experiences. I believe in shared vision and revel in team success.
