  • LEIP Forward! Gaining EI for Today's Leader

    • Sweet Dream Publishing
    • June 3, 2011
    Authors: Lauran Star MAOI

    LEIP Forward! examines the top 16 Leadership Emotional Intelligence Proficiencies, including what they look like for women and how women can improve them with practical and proven tips for success.

    Mastering the Leadership Emotional Intelligence Proficiencies will help today’s leaders maneuver around tomorrows challenges. Organizations want their leaders to possess a specific set of emotional intelligence skills to help foster and grow the internal relationships strong companies are built upon. Organizations where the leaders have strong LEIP demonstrate improved overall job satisfaction, retention, motivation, and performance in their employees year after year. It fosters relationships within the corporate structures that allow you to lead and change the organization’s environment and atmosphere.

This journal uses LEIP (Leadership Emotional Intelligence Profile) as the foundation to leadership skills. The first section of the journal is geared for 5-10 minute writing sessions where you will identify the situation – current LEIP state- the cause – the reaction – results and how to improve.
