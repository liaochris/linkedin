Marcin Marczak's Summary

Marcin Marczak co-founded The Wireless Income on the purpose of creating and teaching home based business seekers online passive income ideas and how to make money blogging. The true meaning of The Wireless Income is “Earn and live anywhere you love as long as you have the online connection”.

As how to make money blogging is all about driving quality traffic to your blog, Marcin’s main method of achieving results is through Facebook Marketing Strategy and Youtube Marketing.

Marcin began his Wireless Income journey with his wife in 2009 from London. They have travelled to Maldives, Malaysia and other countries around the region providing and sharing values and teachings of how to make money blogging and other online passive income ideas. He is currently based in Malaysia.

Specialties

How To Make Money Blogging, Facebook Marketing, Youtube Marketing, Published Author, and Coaching People How To Create Their Passive Income Streams Online.
