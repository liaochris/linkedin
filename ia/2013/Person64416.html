Jayda Klingerman's Summary

In my role with Sky Recruiting, I work as a National Executive Recruiter, specializing in the world of electrical distribution and manufacturing. As a recruiter, I afford busy professionals the opportunity to explore career opportunities in a safe and confidential way. I have successfully "matched" industry professionals with career opportunities in inside & outside sales, operations & branch management, lighting, automation, procurement, quotations, and a selection of specialty positions in both industrial and commercial markets across the country. The clients I recruit for are all within the electrical industry, and include local/independent establishments, regional/national/global companies, and both private & public Fortune 500 corporations.

My recruitment style is that of a "match-maker" and consultant, for both the client and for the candidate. I help businesses grow, and assist motivated professionals grow in their careers.

Specialties: Executive National Recruiter, Specializing in the Electrical Industry (Electrical Distribution, Manufacturing, Industrial Automation); Active Listening & Communication; Sourcing/Screening/Interviewing Job Candidates; Professional Development/Coaching (Resume Writing, Targeted Job Responses, Interview Skills, Job-search Strategy); Cold-Calling; Consultative/Solution Sales; Marketing/Business Development; Business Consulting; Social Networking/Internet Savvy; Willing to Learn; Open to New Ideas & able to Think Outside of the Box.
