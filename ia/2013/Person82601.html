Lory Rosenberg's Summary

Lory D.Rosenberg is the founder and CEO of IDEAS (Immigration Defense & Expert Advocacy Solutions) Consultation and Coaching LLC, where Empowering Excellence and Entrepreneurship is her mission and her promise.

Lory is an award-winning, internationally recognized immigration defense lawyer and sought-after legal consultant and trainer, who works with high-minded immigration lawyers and professionals who want to achieve authentic success in their law practices and businesses.

Lory guides solo and small firm lawyers to brilliantly analyze, persuasively write and effectively resolve complex immigration law issues and client cases. She provides lawyer to lawyer case evaluation, strategy setting, research, writing, mentoring, and comprehensive policy, practice and professional support. She offers authoritative expert opinion testimony in criminal defense and immigration matters, and accepts co-counsel representation in select appellate proceedings.

Lory coaches lawyers, leaders and entrepreneurs alike to breakthrough the performance, productivity, and profit obstacles that stress them out and hold them back. She teaches her clients to make critical mindset shifts, take effective action to increase their visibility and reach ideal clients, and get paid what they’re worth. Lory's authentic success programs and podcasts allow lawyers to unleash their potential, provide winning representation, leverage their services and expertise, and create phenomenal prosperity and fulfillment in their lives and businesses.

Lory's professional goal is to empower excellence and entrepreneurship among lawyers and leaders, and advance the cause of peace, justice and human rights through holistic lawyering. Lory's passion is to share her insights and inspiration teaching others to adopt a positive mindset, realize their full potential, and achieve authentic success and enrichment in their business and personal lives.

Specialties

Immigration consequences of conviction, asylum, removal defense, waivers, investors, adjustment of status, right to counsel, statutory interpretation, administrative appeals, judicial review, due process, & legislative reform. Expert witness, trainer and mentor.

Certified coaching in branding, self-worth, heart-centered marketing systems, performance and productivity, as well as stress, trauma, and grief. Individual client programs, teleseminars, workshops.

Lory Rosenberg's Experience

Owner/CEO

IDEAS Consultation and Coaching LLC

January 2005 – Present (8 years 9 months) Maryland, DC Metro

IDEAS (Immigration Law and Expert Advocacy Solutions) Consultation and Coaching LLC provides hands-on, cutting-edge legal consultation, analysis, writing, mentoring and training for attorneys, judges, advocates, and professionals seeking assistance and collaboration on thorny immigration issues. Lory's focus is on complex motions, hearings, BIA appeals & circuit court review of removal hearings, as well as the consequences of crminal convictions, waivers and asylum applications. Lory's work encompasses enforcement issues; detention, removal, convictions of fraud, moral turpitude, controlled substances, domestic violence and aggravated felony offenses; and admissibility, immigrant visas, adjustment of status, naturalization, and immigration reform.

IDEAS offers consultations involving case and issue analysis, strategy setting, research, and "next-steps" collaboration. Lory will prepare a one-time evaluation of a legal issue, an opinion letter, expert affidavit, issue memo, talking points, or write a brief for one of your cases. She also can develop personally-designed workshops on critical topics, create a package of training sessions for your team, or provide long-term individual legal mentoring and business coaching programs.

IDEAS' authentic success coaching supports high minded lawyers, leaders and entrepreneurs, helping you to breakthrough the performance, productivity and profit challenges that cause stress, frustration, and burnout, so you can maximize your potential and purpose, unleash your creativity, leverage your expertise, and increase your prosperity and fulfillment. Mentoring and coaching programs include positive mindset transformation with practical actions for branding, client attraction, visibility marketing, charging and getting paid what you're worth, resolving money blocks, handling conflict, and increasing self-esteem to elevate your performance and enrich your life.
