Founder, CEO

RIVS

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Staffing and Recruiting industry

May 2011 – Present (2 years 4 months) Chicago, IL

Companies like Charles Schwab, Merck, Vanguard Health, Farmers Insurance, CareerBuilder, and Monster.com hire RIVS to provide them with a platform to digitally interview job candidates.

RIVS is a patented revolutionary online software that uses automated voice and video interviews to both improve time-to-hire by up 90% and allow for discovery of high quality candidates sooner.

A traditional process to find quality candidates might involve posting a job online, having candidates email their resume and cover letter, then setting up a short telephone interview to qualify candidates (that happens to work for both the candidate and interviewer). This process is laborious, error-prone, and extremely time-consuming, often taking several weeks.

Using our web-based technology, recruiters can set up automated written, voice, and video interviews, schedule and record live voice, video, and in-person interviews, rate each candidate at each stage, and ultimately find the best person(s) for the position.Recruiters can realize hundreds of pre-qualified candidates in 24-48 hours from the job posting.

Features:
-Automated written, voice, and video interviews that can all be performed through a browser.
-auto-schedule and record live voice, video (coming soon), and in-person interviews
-create pre-qualifying written questions/answers
-view and manage all resumes, cover letters, and other applications documents in one place
-integrate with Linkedin
-download all job files, or store them with us....and more
