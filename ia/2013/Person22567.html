Chen Li's Experience

Founder & Owner

Herbal Regenesis

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Health, Wellness and Fitness industry

1986 – Present (27 years) Hermosa Beach, CA

Herbal Regenesis was established in 1986 by Master Chen Li, who is trained in Eastern herbal food supplement philosophy and has been practicing the art of alternative and holistic health her entire life. She has always been interested in the study of herbs and their power where balance and harmony are concerned, and most of her knowledge stemmed from her father, the late Dr. Chen Sin. As the seventh generation in a family of traditional Chinese herbalists, she has attained the knowledge and wisdom that grant her the title of Master Herbalist in Eastern Herbal Food Supplementation as well as natural food nutrition.

Our family has been researching and developing these formulations to cleanse, nourish and balance the various organs of the body, thereby promoting a holistic view of what it means to truly live healthy. It is a long standing Chinese tradition to pass on family knowledge and wisdom from one generation to the next. Master Chen Li discovered the invaluable power of herbal formulations from a holistic standpoint, as did all of the master herbalists before her. She has over 30 years of this specialized experience and continues to research and harness the power of herbal solutions to this day. Through her practice of living healthy in mind and body, she continues to be the growing force and face of Herbal Regenesis.
