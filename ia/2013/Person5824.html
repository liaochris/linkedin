Amr Din's Summary

Hi there!

I am a graphic designer and 2D artist interested with working in video games.

Currently working as a UI artist at the Ubisoft UK studio: Reflections on "Just Dance 4" (Kinect & PS3)


Since I can remember I have always had a fascination with fine art, graphic design and an obsession with the front end of video games and how they are presented as an art form

I started making graphics in Flash and Photoshop when I started high school, while at school I started making mini-games and since then my love for playing games and art was combined which led me on to study the Computer Arts course at the the Institute of Arts, Media and Computer Games Abertay.

My skills are focused in 2D art and graphic design in games. This includes creating in-game art (UI, icons, concept art, motion graphics and textures) and marketing/promo material (websites, advertisements, presentations).

A major project I worked as part of Dare to Be Digital was creating the Kinect game, “Joust!” which is currently nominated for a BAFTA 2012 ‘’Ones to Watch’’ award.

I was part of a small development team with 5 other students from all over Europe, the ‘’Digital Knights”, we are representing the ITU of Copenhagen. As the sole artist my job was to create the visual style, in-game graphics, UI and promo material for “Joust!”.

My previous experience includes: working with Disney Interactive Studios as a 2D artist with a student brief to create a game,working on a 2-year project as a graphic designer and general artist to create a brand for the award-winning bar-company The Molly House. As well as being commissioned by the Gallery of Modern Art (Glasgow) to create digital works for previous exhibitions.


I have a keen interest in bridging the gap between fine art, visual quality and video games. As well as continuing development for multiple game projects including a Kinect game.

It is my personal objective in creating and being responsible for high quality visual elements in games.

Specialties

Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Flash & After Effects, 2D Art, Graphic Design, Web Design (understanding of HTML/CSS + Actionscript 2.0) , Illustration, User Interface Art, Logo Design, Branding... and making stuff look good!
