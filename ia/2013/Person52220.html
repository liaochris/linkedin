Heather Frey's Summary

Owner and President of an innovative business concept matching Personal Trainers to their ideal clients and clients to their ideal trainer. Not only do potential clients get to match to their ideal trainer, but the Trainers, Coaches, Instructors, and Fitness Professionals *also* match to their ideal clients... a match both ways.

Using my Figure Competition experience, personal training experience and 15 years in the graphic design and marketing field has allowed me to use my knowledge of health and fitness to create what people need...the RIGHT FIT of information and motivation for their life.

I have used my on-line presence to help motivate and educate people into the life and body they need and have become the Fitness expert for the Ricki Lake Show Community on both Twitter and Facebook. I'll be launching the Change Challenge with the show in 2013.

I also enjoy my role as the fitness specialist for NBC6 Miami's new show, Mom's of Miami, covering all topics that relating to Mom's and life with kids.

Specialties: Fitness Strategist and Life Motivationalist, Speaker, Spokesperson, Artist, Personal Trainer, Graphic Designer, Fitness Model, Figure Competitor

President and Founder

Our goal is to make SmashFit the premiere web spot for all things fitness, with our focus being on matching...finding exactly what's right for the client AND trainer. We will be honing our marketing to hit our target audiences who are diversified fitness trainers, as well as people who want to train and get in shape, which is the entire general public.

Creator of the Change Challenge

SmashFit.com

October 2012 – Present (1 year 2 months)

The Change Challenge is different from other fitness challenges in that it takes the focus away from the scale and puts the emphasis on what it is you need to DO to reach your goals. The focus is on making small, manageable, maintainable changes and when you make these changes week to week, your body will have no choice BUT to change.

It will be launched nationally with The Ricki Lake Show in 2013.
