Lori Ballen's Summary

Lori Ballen of Keller Williams Realty Las Vegas Real Estate creates Dynamic Team of Las Vegas Real Estate Agents offering Buyer Services and Seller Services including Las Vegas Short Sales. Lori Ballen has put together an experienced and professional team of Las Vegas Real Estate Agents in Nevada. Each agent offers a specialty Real Estate service such as: Las Vegas Short Sales, Military Buyers,First Time Home Buyers, Active Adult Living Communities, New Constructions and so on. Lori has taken her years of Las Vegas real Estate experience and assembled the team based on services needed in the Nevada Real Estate Community. One of these services is listing homes and negotiating short sales with the bank. Nevada Short Sales are still on the rise, and experienced short sale agents are valued. Lori has Richard Ballen as lead listing specialist and short sale agent. Mr. Ballen has 4 years of extensive short sale training and hands on experience making him a top listing agent in the area. "Many Las Vegas mortgages now exceed the fair market value of the home." says Richard "Negative equity has become an increasing problem for the nation, but is excessive in Las Vegas. With the prices of Las Vegas homes decreasing, and the interest rates on previous subprime loans increasing, many Las Vegas home sellers have no choice but to negotiate with the bank to take less (also known as a “short”) on the sale of the property. The seller proves a hardship to the bankwhich could include divorce, loss of income, health issues and other various situations proving the monthly mortgage payments are impossible for the homeowner to pay. This is where we come in and help save them from foreclosure." Lori's award winning team has offices at Keller Williams Realty Las Vegas on Durango.
