Lorenzo Franceschi Bicchierai's Summary

I'm a reporter at Mashable's New York headquarters, where I cover cybersecurity, tech policy, privacy and surveillance, hackers, drones, and, more in general, the intersection of technology and civil liberties. Before Mashable, I was an intern at Wired.com, where he wrote for Danger Room, and Threat Level. A recent graduate of Columbia's Graduate School of Journalism I'm also a Law graduate at University of Barcelona.
