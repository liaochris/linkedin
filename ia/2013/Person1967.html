Adrian Frank's Summary

I love to write.

Writing is my profession, but it is also one of my passions. Others are music, motorcycles and reading about nearly any subject.

I draw on a wide variety of experiences when I write. I grew up in Central Texas. I worked at various industrial and retail jobs, including driving a fork lift, driving a soft drink route truck, and working in fast food, before joining the Navy.

I retired from the Navy after 20 years. I worked as an electronics technician and reactor operator on nuclear submarines, with tours along as an instructor along the way. During that time, I traveled extensively. I visited Hawaii, Puerto Rico and several other Caribbean islands, Japan, Hong Kong, Italy and Portugal.

I also draw on the creative writing training and knowledge that I gained while earning Bachelor of Liberal Arts and Master of Business Administration degrees.

Unlike many students, I actually enjoyed researching and writing papers. Business and finance are among my favorite topics to write about, and I have done much research and reading on these subjects since completing my degree.

Other favorite topics include pets, health and wellness issues, information technology and legal issues.

Here are some of my thoughts on writing. I feel that every article deserves the best of my ability, whether it is 100 words or 1000. If I accept an article to write, it will be written from that perspective.

I will always write the best article that I can based on the instructions given. Since instructions can never be perfect, sometimes what I write won't be exactly what you wanted. I want your article to be just right for its intended purpose.

If you like an article, but would like changes in the tone, style or content, I want to make those changes. If I write an article for you and there is anything I can do to improve it, I want to know.

My goal is to give every client the best possible article.

More information can be found at my website, www.adrianfrank.com
