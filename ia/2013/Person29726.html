★ Danielle Navas-Brandt MBA ★'s Summary

Do you want your company to be an industry leader? Manage your Brand on Purpose!

Some people believe now is the time to play it safe. We believe now is the time to stand out. We’re brandfabric, the creative brand strategy agency. We work for clients who aren’t afraid to showcase their personality. Are you?

Your products and/or services are fairly easy to copy.

What you do is easiest to copy.

How you do it requires a little more effort.

But the unique personality of your company and the reason why you do what you do are impossible to copy.

Personality and purpose is exactly why we do what we do at brandfabric. These two drivers are key to your sustainability as a company. They are the reasons others want to do business with you.

We help you find the answers the most fundamental questions to build your brand:
• Who are you?
• Why should I care?
• Why should I believe you?

Your company is unique and should find the values that make up her DNA. Often it is not clear what your company's identity is and how this relates to your image (client perception and reputation).

By digging into the character and personality of your brand it becomes clear what shared values live within. Those values need to be translated into brand values. Using the BRANDSTAR method we get to the core of your brand.
