Matthew Shadbolt's Summary

Creative interactive marketing professional with over 15 years experience.

I am currently the Director of Interactive Product and Marketing for The Corcoran Group, Manhattan's largest residential real estate brokerage. I am responsible for all of Corcoran's interactive output and advertising.

Prior to joining Corcoran in 2006, I gained substantial interactive marketing and advertising experience in the electronic retail and music industry areas, both in Europe and The United States.

My work has been published across numerous worldwide outlets, including The New York Times, The Guardian (UK), Mashable.com, Forbes, The Huffington Post, Crains and Billboard Magazine. My work is regularly published in Real Estate industry publications such as The Real Deal, Curbed, Inman News and the Real Estate section of The New York Times.

I am currently a member of the Hitwise Online Competitive Analysis and Analytics Advisory Board.

I am currently focused on Corcoran's mobile and social media growth and advertising strategy.

Specialties: * Online advertising campaign management, setup and analysis
* New interactive platform product development and navigation
* Interactive user experience & education
* Community building and online audience growth
* Budget management across multiple vendors and advertisers
* Social media for business (especially real estate)
* Mobile development (iPhone and Android)
