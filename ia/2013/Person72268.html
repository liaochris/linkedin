Karie Kaufmann's Summary

Karie Kaufmann has a proven track record of success in both the small business and corporate worlds. Karie first began working in small business 15 years ago, playing various roles including marketing, client relations, and process improvement. After transitioning to marketing and public relations at the corporate level, Karie then went on to manage the largest accounts for a high tech company, where she secured over $1,000,000 in revenue within 6 months. Her practical experience coupled with a MBA from University of the Pacific has made her an invaluable resource for small to medium-sized businesses.

With four years of experience in business and leadership coaching, Karie has helped over 150 business owners to reach their professional and personal goals. She is particularly skilled at coaching companies in the areas of sales, marketing strategies, training, systems development, and profitable growth. She has helped business owners grow from an “owner wears all hats” approach to development of a fully functioning and profitable team, allowing the business owner to focus on the areas that he or she enjoys.

As a recognized leader in the coaching community, Karie is often called upon to provide training and mentoring programs, as well as to serve on advisory boards that support both the coaching community and its business clients. She is committed and passionate about helping business owners reach their maximum potential.

Karie is active in her community, having served on the board of Kids4Tech, a nonprofit organization providing training and computers to underprivileged youth. She has also volunteered extensively at Boys & Girls Clubs of America, and was responsible for the development of a $25,000 corporate scholarship fund. She has been honored by the University of the Pacific as an outstanding alumnus, and is also a Certified Toastmaster.

Specialties

Business coaching, marketing, business systems, team development, project management, lead generation systems, leadership training, client relations
