Chuck Schuele's Summary

I recently retired from a successful career of more than 30 years in Information Technology, working in areas from Computer Operations, Programming, Systems Analysis and Project Management to CIO for a large WI Insurance Company.

My wife Susi and I left the corporate environment to start a new company called My Virtual Project. Combining our talents and years of IT experience, we focus on addressing the needs of small and medium sized businesses with WordPress Website Design, Graphic Design, Logos, Marketing Materials, Search Engine Optimization and Social Media Marketing.

Specialties

MVP provides Web and Graphic Design to showcase your brand whether in print or online. We are Specialists in Social Media Marketing designing custom marketing campaigns using Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, YouTube, Foursquare, as well as other sites, with the latest tools and techniques to help you strengthen your brand, drive more traffic to your door and grow your business. We taught Social Media for Business at a local community college and I am also a professional speaker on Social Media.
