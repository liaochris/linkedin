Christine Robinson's Summary

Talented Professional Writer and Editor with 15+ years of content development experience: writing, editing, proofreading and graphically enhancing materials for news reports, display advertisements, business content, technical documents, blogs, and creative publications. Adept at quickly discerning requirements of audience and modulating writer's voice accordingly. Spirited, but intensely focused individual comfortable in the solitary atmosphere of creativity or the thriving environment of team collaboration on a deadline.

* Capable of developing deadline-driven documents that clearly and accurately convey complex information, deliver messaging to a specific target audience, and generate desired feedback and response rate.

* Sample works selected for publication in two "how to" books used by employment industry pros and careerists: Expert Resume for Health Care Careers and No-Nonsense Cover Letters.

* Prolific writer with a confident voice; self-published author of Beside the Quiet Voices, a creative non-fiction about organ donation, loss, grief, and recovery. Author of two Wordpress blogs.

* Career progression that includes mentoring and training junior writers. Helped launch career of fellow resume writer--Imprint Resumes (Slidell, LA)--now managing a growing business.

* Proficient in MS Word. Quickly adaptable to new technologies and applications, as needed.

Specialties

Versatility, news reporting, content editing, blogging, self-publishing, content creation (for web and print), creative writing, resume writing, proofreading, messaging, coaching, marketing.
