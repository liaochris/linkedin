Beth Terry's Summary

Beth Terry, CSP, is an international speaker, author and columnist. She has spoken to hundreds of thousands of people in 6 countries since 1989. She was President of Pacific Rim Seminars in Hawaii for 14 years before opening Beth Terry Seminars, Inc. in Phoenix in 2002.

Beth brings with her a rich variety of Executive Level experience in Real Estate, Construction, Tourism, Insurance, and Retail. In Hawaii she was Executive Producer and Host of a TV show called "Making Your Life Work" and was a frequent contributor to three major newspapers in Honolulu. Beth is past President of the National Speakers Association (NSA) in Arizona and was Chair of both the Arizona emerging speakers program, and the National Academy for Professional Speaking for NSA.

She is the author of three books. Her first, the inspirational collection "Walking in a Crowd of Angels" was distributed to the families of the 9/11 attack on the Pentagon. Her second book is a humorous collection of wit and wisdom on life called "101 Ways to Make Your Life Easier." Her third book is Thinking About Thinking, a book that explores what Beth calls The Kaleidoscope Effect™: "Nothing is as it seems and everything has another side to it." This book covers concepts of Critical Thinking, Confirmation Bias, Normalcy Bias, and the Black Swan Theory. The Dept of Defense has already purchased more than 500 of these books for distribution.

In 2006, Beth was presented with the highest earned designation from the National Speakers Association: the Certified Speaking Professional (CSP). To date, less than 10% of speakers out of thousands internationally have received that designation.
