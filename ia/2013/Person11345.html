Aziz Peregrino-Brimah's Summary

My name is Aziz Peregrino-Brimah — but most people just call me "Zee." I am a New Media Designer & Developer, which basically means I do cool stuff with graphics, build websites, video, audio and stuff like that. But more importantly, according to my wife and kids I am a trophy husband and a #1 dad! :)

For the past 10 years I have used my design talents to create identity and popularize brands. My creative signature includes award-winning recording artists and producers (Grammy, Stellar, Dove, etc.), NAACP Image Award recipients, movie stars, TV and radio personalities, professional athletes, bestselling authors, motivational speakers, non-profit organizations, businesses and more.

I am an En Sound Trailblazer Award recipient, Stellar Award nominee, Black Gospel Promo honoree, Certified Dreamweaver Developer, 4-year Team Macromedia Member, and a 9-years-combined Prerelease Program participant for Macromedia and Adobe. The broader spectrum of my expertise consists of branding, identity, graphic design, photo retouching and compositing, motion graphics, web design and development, content management, web standards and usability, SEO, social media, and online marketing. While I display proficiency in these areas, it is my passion and love for design that fuels my creativity.

I've had the opportunity to share my knowledge on a few occasions. In 2004 I was fortunate to be one of 16 guest speakers who presented at the 5th Annual TODCON (The Other Developer Conference; sponsored by Macromedia, and then Adobe after their acquisition of Macromedia) in Orlando, FL. TODCON was a 2-day convention dedicated to the learning of Dreamweaver and the Macromedia product suite. My presentation was titled Working with Graphic Designers. The last TODCON was held in 2008.

Other notable speaking engagements include the 22nd Biannual International Bobby Jones Gospel Industry Retreat in (2008 - Hollywood, FL) and the 10th Annual Flavor Fest (2010 - Tampa, FL).

Specialties

branding, identity, graphic design, photo retouching and compositing, motion graphics, web design and development, content management, web standards and usability, SEO, social media, online marketing
