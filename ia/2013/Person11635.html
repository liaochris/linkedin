Yuri Baranov's Summary

Prior to founding Postindustria in late 2006 and other companies that followed it such as Nimble, Semantria, Lifelike Apps, Audioo and Giggster, my professional experience included various functional and executive positions in software marketing & sales.

I focus on nurturing and launching new companies and products ranging from disruptive Social CRM tools (Nimble) to state-of-art semantic APIs (Semantria) to best-of-breed mobile apps (Lifelike Apps) to new-gen recruiting tools (Giggster).

Since November 2006 and through December 2012 I have participated in founding and launching 6 companies, all of them - profitable, groundbreaking, and promising.

In December 2012, I moved on and currently work on new product ideas that excite me.

Usually I act as a software entrepreneur and informally bear a title of Get S**t Done Officer.

My goal is to create a healthy environment for smart and creative people (comrades!) who share a common purpose and make the world better through continuous changes leading to evolutionary breakthroughs. I admire the philosophy of "kaizen" and Kazuo Inamori's views and ideas.

Throughout my career my roles have morphed between C-level responsibilities - key relationships, business development, striking deals, strategic decision making to operational, project and product management roles.

Specialties:

- Building Efficient Engineering Teams
- Detecting Opportunities And Executing With Limited Resources
- Managing And Planning Marketing Activities
- Search Engine Marketing (SEM) Campaigns - Paid & Organic
- Affiliate Program Management
- Online Advertising (Media Buyer's Role)
- Online Direct Sales and Marketing
- Product Management - Wireframing, PRDs, Scoping, Evaluation

Nimble, then codenamed Nudzi - a name quickly rejected because to me it had a connotation to Nazi - was conceived by the greatest CRM evangelist of all time Jon Ferrara and started taking shape after our meeting in May 2009 which lead us into a joint venture and the official start of development in August 2009.

I played an instrumental role in assembling the product delivery team to work in a highly agile environment with constantly changing business requirements and evolving vision.

The result of this enormous effort is a unique product that re-defines what CRM is and makes it turn around people, not sales forecasts or sales pipelines.

Nimble is a Web Based SaaS platform that helps small businesses attract and retain customers by enabling their team to work more effectively together and stay top of mind with their prospects and customers.

Nimble was designed to enable small business to attain the same benefits larger companies have achieved with their CRM strategies without the associated costs and complexities. Nimble CRM combines the power of relationship management, unified conversations/communications, social media tools, and team Collaboration under one roof.

Nimble keeps you and your team organized and allows more effective communications between your company and its contacts from one centralized SaaS platform regardless of what they are using: Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, IM, text message or email.
