Medea Palandjian's Summary

Medea Palandjian- Medea hails from an extensive career in real estate, with experience in condominium sales and new development projects. She has also developed several of her own properties in the area and is also a long time investor. She is a resident of the town's historic district, residing in one of the famed Charles Brigham homes. One of her many passions is in following the market daily and helping buyers and sellers to realize their own dreams. As she frequently states: "I love real estate in general, it flows in my veins!" This passion for the business is the driving force to her success.

Specialties

Market analysis, helping first time buyers, experienced buyers, strong negotiation skills, construction knowledge.
