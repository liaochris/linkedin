InsTIL Service Desk's Summary

InsTIL Service Desk is a software product offering designed to address the day to day IT challenges faced by most organizations today . InsTIL enables organizations to:

• Improve internal IT service levels and productivity
• Deliver best practices & standardization
• Align IT for delivery of value to business

InsTIL has pre-integrated modules such as....

• IT Asset Management
• Incident Management
• Device Monitoring
• Software Distribution

and more, that allow you to choose the functionality that your organization needs.

InsTIL is developed by Precision Group, headquartered in Chennai, India. Precision offers end to end IT solutions to businesses, for designing, implementing and managing IT infrastructure.
