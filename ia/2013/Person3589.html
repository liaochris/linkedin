Alessandra Divizia's Summary

As a freelance illustrator I offer a wide variety of talents. Although I consider my unique style of illustrating and working as a storyboard artist my top strengths,
I’m also an accomplished graphic designer and art director. I’ve worked for
a range of publishing companies, advertising agencies as well as working
one-on-one with directors. I worked with advertising and production agencies for clients including Sony, Gillette, L’Oreal, Mitsubishi, Lab Series, Sephora and Dannon Oikos.

I have an immense passion for visual storytelling, drawing and painting in the
worlds of fashion, beauty and fantasy – inspired by my life in Rome, Milan and Cape Town. I’m now in New York City where I am ready to explore new worlds of beauty, design and inspired imagination.
