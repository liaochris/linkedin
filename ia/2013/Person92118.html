Mike Boehmer, APR's Summary

* Media Manager for largest health system in Ohio, responsible for social media and digital communication strategy, media relations and web/Intranet strategy and content.

* Proven track record helping organizations achieve strategic goals.

* Recognized in Cincinnati, the state of Ohio and elsewhere as an innovator and leader.

* Possess the ability to see the big picture, yet ensure the details get carried out.

* Always learning (and striving to implement) the latest in public relations and marketing.

* Learned a great deal about social media marketing (and marketing in general) while helping my wife with her book From Incurable to Incredible: Cancer Survivors Who Beat the Odds.

Specialties: Some of my areas of expertise are:
* Social media and digital strategy.
* Media relations.
* Strategic public relations.
* Project leadership.
* Writing, editing.
* Intranet and Web site content and management.
* Community presentations.
* Event planning.
* Internal communication and change management.
* Issue management.

Long history of effective writing. Expanding expertise in visual (video, photo, infographics).. Manage contracts with vendors, such as photographers, printers and graphic artists.
