  • Floor Randomizer

    • March 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Jeremiah Frick

    We recently decided to put hardwood floors in our home and we got random width flooring (3, 4, and 5" planks). The theory is that this looks more like traditional hardwoods, which would have made full use of the tree, but you don't want the floor to look like there's a pattern to it; it needs to look random. So, I created a simple application in C# that did two things:

    1. Created a random pattern based on the widths and square footage supplied.
    2. Presented a report indicating how much of each type of flooring was used so you could get a sense of the balance of the selection.

    My father considered this incredibly geeky but it served its purpose.
