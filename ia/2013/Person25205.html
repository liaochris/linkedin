Cindy Kibbe's Summary

Writing is more than just what I do, it’s who I am. It’s why I get up in the morning. It’s why I’m here.

Writing is my passion.

I love to solve problems creatively – the obvious answer might not be so obvious.

I am also extremely adept at multi-tasking and working under tight deadlines – when a person’s life depends on my answer, there’s no time to guess.

I enjoy exploring new audiences for my writing through a solid fundamental understanding of social media, Web design and graphics tools, including Adobe Creative Suite and basic HTML.

I like to make MS Office applications like Word and Excel jump through hoops.

Other facets of my professional life include experience as an IT project manager, corporate team leader and quality management coordinator for the health care industry. I also spent many years working closely with patients, serving as part of their healthcare team as a registered medical technologist.

Some of the strongest professional values I possess include:
*Rapport-Building & Interviewing
*Writing & Editing
*Web & Social Media Publishing
*Data Analysis & Research
*Delivering Deadline Driven Results
*High Volume Multi-Tasking
*Project Oversight
*Team Leadership
*Training & Coaching

My experience history is rich and varied, but writing is my passion. What’s yours?
