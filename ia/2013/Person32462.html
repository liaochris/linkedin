David Reid, CRPC®'s Summary

David Reid is an independent financial advisor affiliated with LPL Financial. David Reid’s investment and financial services practice offers busy individuals and couples unbiased investment and retirement planning advice.

He acts as a “personal financial and retirement trainer”, working with his clients to better realize their retirement dreams and investment goals. David works with families, couples, and single head-of-household families looking to get ahead financially in the Charlotte, Ballantyne, Matthews, and Pineville NC areas. He helps clients understand and simplify the saving and investing process, and functions as a key member of their team working to find solutions to life problems.

David is a recognized specialist in retirement planning, having earned his Chartered Retirement Planning Counselor designation, CRPC®, in 2008.

David was born in Scotland, which helps shape his investment practice as he brings his Scottish heritage of thrift and hard work to benefit each and every client. David partners with his clients and operates under a philosophy that he is “in it with you” - he supplies the plan and the process, his clients supply the goals and wishes, and together they drive for success.


Securities and Financial Planning offered through LPL Financial. A Registered Investment Adviser. LPL Financial Member FINRA / SIPC. For a list of states in which David is registered to do business, please visit: http://www.DavidReidFinancial.com.
