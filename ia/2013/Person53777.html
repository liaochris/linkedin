Howard M. Schrier's Summary

Hello my name is Howard M Schrier. I am a Certified Public Accountant with over twenty years experience in the accounting and finance industry. My roles included manager of an international public accounting firm, controller of private equity funds and Chief Financial Officer of a national company. I have substantially improved the growth and operating results of numerous companies by instituting and coordinating strategic planning with financial systems, technology and cash flow management.

I have evaluated operational activities for working capital improvements, and assisted in acquisitions and restructuring. I have negotiated the sale of companies and worked with lending institutions to raise working capital. I have consulting experience in real estate, retail, medical and professional service firms. I also have experience in start up companies and internal restructurings, including implementation of internal controls.

Member in the New York State Society of Certified Public Accountants, the New Jersey State Society of Certified Public Accountants, the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants, the Association of Certified Fraud Examiners and the Institute of Management Accountants.

Member of the Board of Directors for the The World Adhesion Foundation

Specialties

Real Estate, professional industries, retail/wholesale, business start-ups, business growth, venture and private equity capital raising, finance and accounting department restructuring, CFO/Controller outsourcing and forensic accounting.
