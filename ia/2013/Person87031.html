Mark Scott's Summary

Business and technical communication isn’t everyone’s cup of tea. But it’s Mark Scott's double espresso.

He has a real taste for it. Loves the process of making it. And gets a buzz out of seeing the end product.

A freelance writer and editor, Mark Scott works with all sorts of documentation and online content for sales and marketing, internal communications, projects and operations.

Mark Scott brings:
• business analysis skills to capture and develop the content you need
• consulting and communication skills to facilitate the process
• writing and editing skills to produce well-structured, clear and consistent copy
• creative ability to make material engaging and visually appealing.

He knows how to tell a story, no matter how technical the content.

What’s more, he knows how to craft content that supports and reinforces your business’s vision, values and brand.

Mark Scott's experience crosses multiple functions in businesses small to large, in industries such as banking and financial services, state and federal governments, transport, education, media and IT.

Visit Mark Scott's website: TheDocumentalist.com.au

Specialties (in no particular order):
• Business and technical reports; white papers and briefing papers
• Web content and intranet content
• Case studies, brochures, capability statements and other marketing content
• Project documents
• Policies, processes and plans; operations manuals and handbooks
• Procurement documents (RFPs, RFTs, etc.) and bid responses (proposals and tenders)
• Templates for Word, PowerPoint, Visio and Excel
