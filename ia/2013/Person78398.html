Larry Bratcher's Summary

Larry Bratcher, founder of Bratcher Sports and Entertainment, LLC, played basketball professionally for 10 years. After graduating from Fayetteville State University, Larry played in the CBA, IBA, and USBL in the United States and in professional leagues in Bahrain, Belgium, Canada, China, Colombia, Germany, Iceland, Jordan, Kuwait, Mexico, New Zealand, Romania, Saudi Arabia and Syria.

Larry founded the Bratcher Sports and Entertainment Professional Basketball Camp to provide a solid foundation, enrichment and longevity tools for players interested in a professional basketball career and current professional basketball players.

Specialties

Basketball skill development, professional and youth basketball camps, student-athlete and professional athlete mentorship
