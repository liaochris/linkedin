Mike Luckham's Summary

A focused, technical, and personable Software professional with over 25 years developing and managing IT and technical projects, many of them as a freelance contractor. Expertise in Software Engineering/Development of .NET and Embedded Software using many languages, object-oriented design (OOP/OOD), and computer platforms.

I am a focused, personable, persistent, and detail-oriented software professional able to analyze, review, determine and implement the most optimal solutions to meet the technical needs of an engineering project. Having successfully designed software for various companies, I have an in-depth understanding of operating system concepts, communication protocols, databases, websites and web handlers, electronic schematics and concepts, sensor interfacing, stepper motor systems, Visual Studio and cross-development tool chains, application installers, and code writing. I enjoy working as part of a high-functioning team, regularly solve difficult problems, developing code libraries, drivers, communication protocols, tools, and core architectural concepts employed by the programming team. Additionally, I have been a Project Engineer, a Technical Lead for a group of Software/Hardware Engineers and a Team Lead for two Developer Teams building a minicomputer-based Telex exchange and a PC-based remote monitoring system.
