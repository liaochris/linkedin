Marcela Gomez's Summary

Marcela Gómez founded Hispanic Marketing Group in 2002. Today, the firm is the leading full service bilingual and bicultural marketing and communications agency in Middle Tennessee, and provides strategic counsel and services to consumer, corporate, nonprofit and other clients.

A native of Bogotá, Colombia, Marcela has worked with consumer, corporate, nonprofit and business-to-business clients in industries, such a banking and finance, food, healthcare, public utilities, transportation, education, government and the arts. She plays a key role in the development of marketing, communications and grassroots campaigns, from concepting and copywriting to production.

Marcela is highly regarded as an expert in connecting clients with Latino consumers and frequently presents to business, civic and education groups. The Nashville Business Journal and The Nashville Post have recognized her for her insight, expertise and leadership as both a successful business owner and a strategic counselor.

An active and involved member of the community, Marcela has served on the boards of organizations such as the Nashville Area Chamber of Commerce, the YWCA, the Tennessee Latin American Chamber of Commerce, the Center for Nonprofit Management, Conexión Americas, the Oasis Center, CABLE and others.

Marcela earned a degree in marketing, advertising and television production from Universidad Jorge Tadeo Lozano in Bogotá. She has lived in Nashville since 1994.
