Carrie Corcoran, CTSM's Summary

Carrie Corcoran is a savvy Social Marketer. She helps businesses leverage the power of social media channels like LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook and YouTube. She develops, implements and manages campaigns for them. She is a "think outside the box marketer". She combines her passion for social media marketing with traditional marketing strategies to help business brand themselves and grow. She loves all things marketing and social media and excels at connecting people and ideas. The bottom line is she cares about people and she cares about the bottom line.

She is a Certified Trade Show Marketer (CTSM). She spent over 10 years in the trade show, meeting and events industry working with clients such as Sun Microsystems and the Department of Health and Human Services. She is a pro at managing all aspects of events and meetings, concept to completion.

She has a thirst for knowledge and loves multitasking as she has done for many startup companies. She can bring order to chaos.
