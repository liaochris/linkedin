Mark Gonzales' Summary

I held my first executive position in 2002 as Vice President of New Haven, Inc., a company which owned and operated residential care facilities for the elderly in Southern CA. I helped lead the company to expand to four locations increasing annual gross revenue to the 7 digit mark. While I held my position as VP I also opened my own real estate sales practice in Los Angeles. Within 3 years of opening I was awarded the 30 Under 30 award from Realtor Magazine in 2009 for being an innovator in recognizing the significance of social networking for building client relationships and utilizing it for real estate sales and marketing.

Having received the award gave me a platform to give back to the real estate professional community. In 2009 I was appointed as the Vice Chairman of the YPN Southland Chapter where I applied my knowledge of social media to recruit social network influencers in the local real estate scene. Together we grew our committee to the largest and most recognizable at the Southland Regional Assocation of Realtors. I served on the committee as Chairman following that year.

As my involvement in real estate and leadership increased opportunities became more evident. My belief and utilization of technology has allowed me to continue selling real estate nationally internationally. I do this through The Mark G Group, my team of top producing agent partners who uphold the highest standard in client service. This has allowed me to expand my business portfolio to other industries. In 2010, I formed M&M Advanced Healthcare, Inc. based in Houston, TX which acquires home healthcare companies and implements management systems and structures backed by an executive team which has over 40 years of combined experience in home healthcare agency operations which I sold in 2013 to pursue my ultimate passion; developing wonderful relationships with wonderful people and helping them reach their real estate goals.
