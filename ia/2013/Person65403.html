Career Achievements:

Studio Head for Bethesda Softworks; managed development of platinum selling franchises -- Pirates of the Caribbean, Sea Dogs, IHRA Drag Racing, and Burnout

Produced and developed more than 20 platinum-selling games, including Duke Nukem, Unreal, Major League Baseball, Anno 1602, Doom, Bomberman Live, and Pirates of the Caribbean

Developed more than 20 top-selling mobile games across iPhone, Android and feature phone platforms - including Tetris, Bomberman, Bonk, Pac-Man for Prizes, and Potty Racers

Teaches social game design at The Games Academy in San Francisco. Key areas of focus are core compulsion loops, virality, and monetization of social games

Directed the development of more than two hundred online games at AddictingGames.com that generated 1,600 years of engagement in 2009

Produced Bomberman Live and resurrected the Bomberman franchise for Hudson; garnered awards for Game of the Year and Multiplayer Game of the Year from Microsoft at GDC 2007

Founded Kozmo Games and created hit games Ice Cream Tycoon and War Chess for the casual game markets
