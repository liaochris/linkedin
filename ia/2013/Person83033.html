Lucas Shaffer's Summary

Since 2010, Lucas Shaffer has led a team of professionals at @StandAndStretch to develop and manage interactive marketing strategies using web, social and mobile technologies to increase the affinity between brands and their customers.

Lucas completed his Master's in Applied Computer Science at Columbus State Univ. on December 2010 while working 40 hours a week in a major eCommerce driven environment. After several years in this atmosphere, he was able to learn first-hand how companies made millions of dollars by utilizing the web and watched as the business culture shifted into todays market.

In 2010, as a newlywed, with a baby on the way and new found passion for online community building, Lucas decided to leave the corporate eco-system and bring 'Big Brand' mentality to the businesses and organizations in our area. Thus, Stand And Stretch was born.

Then everything began to change. Columbus Georgia's demand for online solutions was apparent. The areas first technology company geared at online marketing initiatives began allowing smart, innovative business owners to lean heavily on Stand And Stretch to cut traditional marketing spending and focus on reaching the consumer directly; qualifying the leads along the way. In a down economy and IT vacuum, our perseverance for success is allowing us to build long-term relationships with local and national companies. We are not only experiencing continued growth, but we are also bringing external cash flow into the area.

It is often that we create the 'first' of many online initiatives that add something never seen before to our clients.

Along with being an early adopter in social media and its uses for online marketing in Columbus Georgia, Lucas is also a dedicated blogger who often writes about giving the insider perspective for businesses, fatherhood, a love for food, social media, entrepreneurship and leadership. His blogs have been syndicated on SocialMediaToday.com which reaches millions of viewers a month.

Specialties

Entrepreneurship, Social Networking, Programming, Blogging, Client Side Interface Design, Service Oriented Architecture, jQuery Implementations, Flash Development, Graphic Design, AJAX/.NET Framework Development, PHP Environment, SQL/MySQL Server Management, Digital Media Marketing, Brand and Identity Awareness, Authority Cultivation, Social Media Outreach and Integration, Inbound Marketing Professional, SEO, content management and I consider myself of the Notepad Elite!
