Corey Walker's Summary

I am a marketing consultant with sixteen years of experience in the marketing communications field. I hold a B.S. in Marketing from CSU - Sacramento, and have worked in corporate marketing for The Money Store, CampusEngine.com, VSP & Eyefinity. I have specialized experience in small business, e-commerce, managed healthcare, optometry, real estate and finance.

Presently, I am the President of The Marketing Specialist, helping businesses increase their bottom line through targeted, effective marketing solutions. This includes creating custom marketing collateral, social media, web content, printing, mailing and developing comprehensive marketing communications plans. I love what I do, and I love helping fellow entrepreneurs make their dreams a reality!

Specialties: Copywriting, graphic design, marketing communications planning, printing assistance, direct mail, e-mail marketing, media buying, social media, online content
