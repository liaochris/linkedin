Dennis Mark's Summary

Dennis Mark is an award-winning creative professional with more than 16 years of experience in advertising and marketing. He possesses the type of leadership, energy, and vision that consistently yields exceptional results. Dennis has a proven track record of success in designing for a variety of industries, including sports, healthcare, pharmaceutical, technology, financial services, education, retail, hospitality, and media.

Dennis is one of the most versatile and dedicated creative professionals you will find anywhere. He is cool under fire and has an unrelenting desire to get the job done—and done right. Dennis is also the consummate team player who inspires others to succeed. His master's degree in fine arts and a bachelor's degree in business provides a unique combination of talent and expertise. Finally, stir in a heaping sense of humor, bake at 98.6°, and you get someone who can add tremendous flavor to your marketing recipe.
