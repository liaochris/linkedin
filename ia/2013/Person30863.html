Darryl Petters' Summary

An executive and consultant with more than 25 years of experience in senior management, most recently in project management, bulk materials handling and shipping. Skilled negotiator successful with executives and government officials in domestic and foreign markets. Lived and worked in a number of overseas locations. Extensive knowledge of steel and metals manufacturing, processing and distribution with detailed experience in the development of logistics infrastructures.
