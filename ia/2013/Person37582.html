Ben Benjamin's Summary

Ben Benjamin is a recognized authority on orthopedic assessment and treatment techniques using massage therapy. His contributions to the massage field are extensive and include professional publications, teaching, clinical work, consulting, and participation in the greater massage community. He is the author of Listen to your pain, The Ethics of Touch, Exercise Without Injury & Are You Tense? He has also been a contributing author and publisher to several other books. After 47 years of developing his own techniques, Dr Ben Benjamin continues to provide massage therapists the advanced education they would need for assessing & treating musculo-scletal pain through The Benjamin Institute. He is currently a member of the AMTA, ABMP & The Bodywork Education Project and has been a regular featured author in publications such as Massage & Bodywork, Massage Today and The Massage Therapy Journal.

The Benjamin Institute is now focusing on transforming it’s informative, hands-on approach to education into accessible and affordable webinars, which can be taken from the convenience of a home or office. Currently Dr Benjamin is focusing on orthopedic assessment & treatment techniques. He continues to teach live workshops on Orthopedic Assessment & Treatment Techniques, Aaron Mattes’ Active Isolated Stretching & Strengthening and Ethics.

Specialties

Webinars, Workshops, Publications & Videos on Orthopedic Massage, Active Isolated Stretching & Strengthening, Assessment & Treatment of Low Back, Neck, Shoulder, Knee Pain & more....
