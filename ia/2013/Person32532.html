David Santy's Summary

Wordpress web designer and site manager specializing in projects for small businesses as well as individuals. I've served manufacturers of home decor, clothing manufacturers, authors, musicians and more.

My goal is to help small businesses become successful online and to simplify complicated technology for them by offering affordable management services. I'm always happy to assist with any size project or even occasional maintenance tasks.

I also have worked seven years in IT, maintenance, merchandising and sales for small businesses in retail hardware.

Specialties: Web Design, Wordpress, Customer Service, computers, retail systems management, reset merchandising, retail sales, semi-skilled labor (electrical, plumbing, network installation).
