Kevin Tumlinson's Summary

I have decades of experience turning my hobbies into professions.

I make things, and then I use those things to convince people to buy other things.

Mostly I make words, and then jam those words together in an effort to inform, educate, entertain, enlighten, and enrage.

On occasion I jam pixels together to form hard bits of brain candy, like logos and websites and guys in Yeti costumes. That's a thing.

I make cameras do funny things, like capture images of people, places, and things in ways that might be considered artsy or informative or useful for telling someone's story.

Mostly I make the world a little better than how I found it, by going out of my way to add value for the people I meet along the way.

And while I'm at it, I try to make a few folks laugh. It's kind of a hobby.

Above all, I glorify God in all I say and think and do and create and produce. That's also a thing. Makes for a handy bit of eternal prosperity. You should give it a shot.

Specialties: Think User Experience (UX) and Information Architecture (IA). Think Writing and Marketing. Think creativity and innovation. Think books, articles, blog entries, ad copy. Think photography, graphic design, video & film, and social media. Think about jokes that use the word “pants.” Now you’re thinking about me, and the kind of value I bring to the table. You’re my kind of thinker. We should hang out some time.
