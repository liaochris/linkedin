Carl David's Summary

Born and raised in this business which was founded circa 1910 by my Grandfather. Studies in both Art History and Business in college as they are both utilized in our industry. One is not mutually exclusive of the other. My father, Samuel David, one of the great art dealers of all time, was the best teacher anyone could ever have had.

Now in our fourth generation, it is our goal to carry on the David name and the traditions of excellence and integrity upon which our business was founded.

Specialties

American and European 18th -20th century paintings, drawings & watercolors.

Consultation, brokerage, investment counsel and representation for the private, corporate and institutional client

We are a 4th generation family owned and operated Art Gallery in the Rittenhouse Square area of Philadelphia, dealing primarily in original 17th-20th century European and American paintings, watercolors, drawings and Sculpture. Our scope of interest encompasses European Old Master through Modern Master works including French Impressionism and Post Impressionism and American from Colonial period to present with heavy emphasis on Impressionism and Hudson River School and Modernism.
