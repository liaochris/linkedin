DV Forensic's Summary

As a professional forensic engineering firm, Design Visionaries can perform a number of scientific analyses and can appear in court as your engineering expert witness. Our highly skilled engineering team has the experience needed to analyze and investigate crash accidents, injuries, patents, and products. We utilize tried-and-true methods of forensic engineering, including reverse engineering, fault and failure analysis, investigation of materials and components, examination of witness statements and other court documents. A captivating, sophisticated animation can help win your legal case. We specialize in legal animation of accidents, injuries, patents, and products.

Specialties

Accident Reconstruction, Animation, Expert Witness, and Forensic Engineering.
