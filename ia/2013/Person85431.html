Marcus Straub's Summary

As a coach and consultant, I have worked with thousands of people from every walk of life and business, and have experienced great success in helping individuals, teams and organizations reach higher levels of happiness and success.

My goal is to assist as many people as I possible in realizing, embracing and utilizing their potential on a consistent basis for greater happiness and success, in both life and business.

I have published a book, "Is It Fun Being You?' and I have produced a 21-day CD program, "Unleash Your Greatest Life Now!". Both of my products will ignite your personal development!

I offer a FREE 30-minute consultation that allows us to explore your situation, and for you to discover the power of effective coaching.

Marcus Straub's Publications

  • Is It Fun Being You? - The Journey To Lasting Happiness

    • Marcus Straub
    • September 2010
    Authors: Marcus Straub

    Is It Fun Being You?, lays out a simple four-step process that exposes the reader to the power they have to create lasting happiness in their life. As you gain control of your thoughts, actions and words, you are positioned to choose the feelings you want, and therefore the level of happiness you desire. You can get off of the emotional roller coaster, and by doing so, you will enjoy a much more fulfilling life.

  • Unleash Your Greatest Life Now!

    • Self
    • February 2008
    Authors: Marcus Straub

    A 21-Day CD Program, accompanied by en empowerment journal, that takes you through the three most important areas of your life - your relationship with yourself, others and finances - and shows you how to put your relationships on the fast track to greater happiness and success.
