Linda Mateos' Projects

  • LensCrafters "How You See It"

    Team Members: Linda Mateos, Molly Christian, Kaitlin Strovink, Kaitlin Maud, Joshua Bean, Joe Morgan, Jon Grassis

    How I See It is a digital experience from LensCrafters that leverages Facebook connect to show people all their eyes have seen, impressing on them the power of human sight and the importance of caring for their eyes. “Remember when you first saw her?” the voiceover asks as you see a picture of you and your wife from three years ago. With smart versioning and search queries, the experience captures every individual, whether they’re married or not, gay or straight, have a lot of photos or very few—and draws them into the experience seamlessly in a way that feels almost magical.

    In the end, people are left with a custom Facebook cover photo, filled with images from their life, that can be shared via Facebook. And a new, distinctly personal connection to a brand previously known for their semi-annual sales and 1-hour service.

  • SunglassHut.com Redesign

    Team Members: Linda Mateos, Allison Sarno, Michelle Melino, David Sutton, Joe Morgan

    For Sunglass Hut, the world's leading authority on premium sun-wear, we designed and launched enhancements to their ecommerce site. Improvements to the site have included global and faceted navigation, homepage, product catalog, suggestive search, editiorial features and customer care area.

  • Rugby Make Your Own

    Team Members: Linda Mateos, Kyle Outlaw, Tom Mascardo

    The Rugby Make Your Own iPhone app allows users to design and purchase their own Rugby garment. Users can submit their creation to a gallery, email it to a friend, or post it to their Facebook profile. We also designed an accompanying 42" multi-touch, interactive window that is displayed in Rugby stores in New York and San Francisco.

  • ESPN.com Redesign

    Team Members: Linda Mateos, Reggie Wirjadi, Michael Betts, Marisa Gallagher

    For ESPN, we did a redesign of the overall site, including changes to navigation, page layouts and modules. We also made recommendations to optimize the membership registration and Insider subscription flows.
