Dorothy Betts' Summary

Dorothy Betts has twenty five years experience working with teams and individuals, working in creative and manufacturing organisations, also professional sport. She is a 'values driven influencer' believing in integrity & fairness; a natural leader committed to the development of people & using the right measures to deliver service improvement, revenue generation & cost savings; a strategic thinker with diverse project management experience in production, change management; also in lean intervention - always with a focus on the customer.

In the Jerry Rhodes TIP Colours Dorothy comes out balanced green and red, both hard and soft - an inspiring changer!

"I believe in collaboration and involvement, not authority and control - people thrive when they feel involved and valued; when they enjoy their work and when their opinion counts".

As a Coach, Dorothy Betts believes passionately that all people have the potential to make positive changes and works with them, challenging them to gain confidence, overcome limiting beliefs and encouraging action".

Whether it is a Management Team or Individual, Dorothy Betts works with a down-to-earth approach to developing people, aligning human resource management with sales, machinery and material management. People make the difference working through other resources, resulting in increased productivity, increased flexibility with reduced waste and reduced downtime.
