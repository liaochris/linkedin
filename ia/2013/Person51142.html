Greg Wildman's Summary

As founder and CEO of OVC, INC. LLC, Greg Wildman has distinguished him self a leader in the legal marketing industry. Since 1999, Greg has worked with over 700 law firms and has developed over 300 legal websites, using his in-depth knowledge of internet marketing strategies for law firms to become successful on the internet.

Having developed important partnerships with HG.org, NOLO.com, AVVO.com, Lawyer.com and APEX Chat. Greg and OVC can offer complete marketing programs for lawyers and law firms across the country.

Since its inception in 2008, OVC has been able to provide law firms with the same valuable marketing tools at a reasonable price. With the help of Online Video Concepts, law firms have gained more internet exposure with:

Custom Website Development
Reputation Management
Search Engine Optimization
Social Marketing
Video Production
Content Writing
Offsite Optimization

Greg’s philosophy is the more sites you have linking to your website, the more traffic that will be driven to your page: The end result being higher conversion rates with new client representation.

In conjunction with Google, Greg Wildman has placed small to mid-size law firms all over the state of Illinois and throughout the country, at the top of the page in search engine results.

Greg Wildman is a proud corporate sponsor of the DuPage County Bar Association and also helps to sponsor the New Lawyer Happy Hour with the New Lawyer Committee.

To learn more about our internet marketing services and how we can help you achieve results, feel free to call us at (312) 953-3826 or email greg@onlinevideoconcepts.com.
