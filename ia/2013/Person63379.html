Jason Flores' Summary

Hello,

My name is Jason Flores, Sr. Art Director and Visual Storyteller with 10 years experience collaborating and developing brand campaigns from the ground up. I love to express stories and develop a voice for a product or to help evolve a brands' equity in the marketplace. My areas of focus are seasonal trend development, concept prototyping, brand architecture, illustration, motion graphics, advertising, packaging, with web design and some interactivity asset design.

To usher in 2013, I earned a certificate in Motion Graphics at the Bay Area Video Coalition.
I was able to ramp up my technical training with advanced AfterEffects courses covering motion tracking and expressions. Cinema 4D was included also and will be featured in my motion graphics reel upon completion. There was an introduction to Flash and I also gained insight into the brave new world of HTML 5. My website designs are finished as well with an officially branded registered trademark artmovement.com® animated in HTML 5.

The websites are broken up according to trade, but feel to browse them all!
