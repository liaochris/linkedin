Anita Crawford Clark's Summary

Anita Crawford Clark is the founder of Abundant Life Success Academy, Sound Mind Social Media & Mrs. Clark's Social Media Classroom for Children's and YA Writers. She is an author, inspirational and keynote speaker, social media consultant, seminar and workshop leader, and representative with IMPaX WORLD health and nutrition company. Her ability to take complex information and simplify it, along with her inspirational and passionate approach, has made her a sought after motivational and keynote speaker, social media trainer and consultant, mentor and coach to authors, speakers and entrepreneurs.

Anita is a contributing author to the Bestselling book Align, Expand and Succeed: Shifting the Paradigm of Entrepreneurial Success. She is also the author of an upcoming children’s book.

During her seventeen year stint as a Christian school teacher and vice-principal, Anita developed and implemented sports programs, curriculum and her favorite, a band program where she taught concert, jazz and orchestra for the last four years of her teaching career.

What has this woman on fire for life? Knowing her mission, having a purpose, backing it with faith and passion and living it with joy. Anita just goes out an lives the most amazing life possible. She never lets her age, gender, race or past hinder her.

Anita is the mother of three girls ages 3 years,21 years and 23 years old. She enjoys playing the drums for her church praise band, and even took boxing lessons to lose the extra pounds and get fit after having her last child at age 42. Born and raised in Sacramento, California, and a graduate of the University of California, Davis, Anita enjoys time with her family, friends and the community at large.
