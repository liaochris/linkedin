Photographer Services:

It is very true that a picture tells a story just as well as a large amount of descriptive text (aka; a picture is worth a thousand words). When considering such text the overall story however, it can also be said that there are some people who are great writers, good writers and terrible writers.

This is equally as true when considering pictures – some are great, some good and many are terrible. The question here is what makes a great picture versus a terrible picture. In response to many companies asking this exact question, Alberta Industrial Photography has created a great solution to this very frustrating and sometimes very complex process.

Through the process of an image audit, we will determine what images you are currently using to promote a product, what images you are not using and what images you need. Perhaps, you are unknowingly using terrible pictures to promote your product. Allow us to solve this very common dilemma!
