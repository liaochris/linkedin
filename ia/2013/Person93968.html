Mike Batton's Summary

I am a current CTO and former three time CIO with a diverse IT professional experience of over 25 years that includes: project management; systems architecture; development and support management; software quality assurance and testing; engagement management; vendor management; proposal, negotiation and contract activities; and, product lifecycle management.

Much of my focus over the last 9 years has been helping other companies & CIOs in establishing improvements in IT direction, key projects and operations. Interlaced with this has been direct management of IT in corporate turnaround scenarios.

Specialties:
Leading corporate IT organizations (from $12 to $300+ million budgets).
Delivering business value through technology.
Making change happen and leading through transitions.
Understanding of credit/debit card industry and technology.
Mobile payment solutions & implications
Understanding of ARM industry and technology.
Turnaround of IT organization performance.
Managing large-scale development programs of $100+ million.
