Cristina Oliver's Summary

I am a graphic and moving image designer based in London, creating efficient solution for companies. I am currently working for PremierPr as a motion designer in the heart of Soho. My work encompasses a range of disciplines from graphic and motion design to illustration and filmmaking, combining them to find the best way to communicate messages.
I've created branding for companies, video presentations, motion graphic videos, short films, communication design and print.
I have normally been involved in projects from concept stage to completion and delivery.

If the work includes music, that's happiness for me.
