Annette Di Bello Kelly, CPA, CFP®'s Summary

I work with small business owners and individuals in the areas of: Investment and Portfolio Advisory and Management, Financial Planning, Tax Preparation and Planning, Accounting, Finance & CFO functions.

My goal is to help the small business owner and individuals navigate the maze of financial and economic news to better prepare and prosper during good times, and survive downturns in the economy. My expertise as a CPA and CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER means that I am always seeking out the lowest cost, tax advantaged, best quality and most appropriate investment advisory possible. My moto is to always place the client's interests above my own.

I am a Registered Investment Adviser (RIA), and a fee only financial planner. I do not receive any commissions or referral fees of any kind. Furthermore, I am not licensed to sell and I do not sell or benefit from the sale of any financial or life insurance products. I am not affiliated with any one broker and/or their proprietary investments. My advice is objective and unbiased as I formulate financial and tax planning goals to meet the client's best interest. What differentiates me from other "Financial Advisors" is that I'm also a CPA. I'm a boutique firm, and because of my background as a CPA, I have an increased focus on taxes and their impact in all investment and financial planning decisions. Also, this makes my firm a "one stop" operation; everything from tax preparation & planning, accounting all the way to financial and investment advisory.

I have over 25 years of progressive accounting, tax, financial planning, investing and business management experience.

Specialties: Investment Management, Financial Planning, Tax Preparation and Planning, Accounting & Finance, CFO functions.
