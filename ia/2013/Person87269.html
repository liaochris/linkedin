Mark Ziebold's Summary

Attorney with extensive experience in the following practice areas: Estate Planning (from fundamental planning with revocable living trusts to tax planning for the high net worth individual), Asset Protection, Probate, Taxation, Corporations, and Partnerships.

The majority of my business is helping high net worth clients reduce estate taxes. In addition to my Orange County clients, I have clients from San Diego to San Francisco in California and in the Las Vegas/Henderson area. I am licensed to practice law in California, Nevada, Arizona, Texas, the federal central district court in California, and the federal Tax Court.

I am also acting as a private fiduciary with the Caritas Group, an organization of private fiduciaries that administer trusts and estates from a Christian worldview. Look at the organization's website to learn more (www.Caritastrust.com).

I earned my Certified Wealth Preservation Planner designation (CWPP) and Certified Asset Protection Planner designation (CAPP) from the Wealth Preservation Institute in July of 2007. I was designated as a Certified Specialist in Estate Planning, Trust, and Probate Law by the California State Bar in 2010 and a Certified Specialist in Taxation Law by the California State Bar in 2012. Less than 100 attorneys in the state have both designations from the Board of Legal Specialization.

If you are a Chapman Law Alumni or a Baylor Alumni in Southern California, feel free to invite me to your network. I am also happy to meet and join the networks of other professionals that may benefit my clients or that have clients that need tax and estate planning.

Specialties

Estate Tax planning (Installment Sales to Defective Grantor Trusts, Grantor Retained Annuity Trusts, Charitable Trusts, QPRTs, Family Limited Partnerships, etc.), Estate and Gift Tax Returns, Asset Protection strategies, Corporate and Partnership formation and operation, Trust Administration, Probate, planning with Insurance Agents and Providers on Insurance Agreements for Clients, and creating and operating tax exempt entities (Private Foundations, Public Charities).
