Jennifer Sheahan's Summary

People ask me why I do what I do?

It is because I feel like I’m cutting through the noise, the misinformation and the distraction to get the quickest possible path to a good result.

It has a lot to do with the fact that I’m not one of those people who does the same thing that everybody else does. I like to do things the most efficient way. I don’t just do things and don’t just accept things. I always wanted to do something different.

I don’t like fluff,nonsense or waffling. Call it what you want. Just get straight to it!What’s going to work? Is it going to work? How can we make it work? How fast can we make it work?

I believe I have that formula. More to the point, I do have that formula because I’ve been doing it for six years. I have that formula and my core work is in delivering that formula in an easily digestible, useful manner to the widest audience possible.

One more thing that keeps coming to mind is that I feel a personal frustration when people are misinformed or misled or overcharged or whatever for nonsense that isn’t going to deliver a result. I feel like I’m righting that wrong.

By working in this way it has led to many fantastic projects with the likes of Tony Robbins. We worked with Frank Kern for a really long time & James Schramko to this day. Eben Pagan and Ryan Deiss as well have also used our skill sets. All those people, we’ve worked with along with their teams, helping them develop their Facebook Ad strategies and scaling up their campaigns.

In 2012 I had a very special time working on Barack Obama’s re-election campaign. We were one of the social media agencies that were hired to design the campaign Facebook Ads. Targeted at a specific demographic, we sent traffic to promote the election campaign. We also worked with Democrats Abroad which is a US political party and helped them grow their subscriber base to reach Americans who live abroad and need to vote.

It has been a blast and long may it continue!
