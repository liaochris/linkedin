Abendschein, Lindsey's Summary

Designer, photographer, martial artist, bicyclist, and all-around student of life. My goal is to experience as much of what life has to offer as possible, whether it’s experimenting with new recipes, going on a dive with my husband, playing with new design techniques, or just going for a quick photo walk. For the day job, I am fortunate enough to be working for the wonderful non-profit organization for the performing arts, First Stage, where I work with an awesome group of people and I have continued to stretch my creative wings. I love taking on new challenges, because really, life is what you make of it, and I intend to make of it as much as possible!

Current activities: Fencing, biking, Comedy Sportz, with aims for more volunteering at the Bike Collective and Discovery World.

Specialties: Creative Suite in both Macintosh and Windows platforms (I know, an artist who likes working with non-Macs) with expertise in Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop and InDesign.

Received high-level training in Macromedia Flash (8) with some experience creating web pages using Dreamweaver, and Adobe GoLive. More recent training in HTML and CSS, and 3D animation.

Experience with both film and digital photography on a personal and professional level (see portfolio for some examples of my photo work).
