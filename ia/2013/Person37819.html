Drew Miller's Experience

Owner

DeSelby Productions, Inc.

June 1991 – Present (22 years 4 months) Minneapolis Minnesota

Incorporated in 1991, DeSelby Productions, Inc. represents Drew Miller's work as a technology consultant / "digital coach," web and print designer, disc manufacturer, and the Omnium record label.

Omnium Design is a portfolio site showcasing recent projects in web development and print design.

Omnium Recordings is an independent record label presenting world music with a contemporary aspect. 50+ releases to date, distributed nationally on CD via Amazon and digitally on Apple’s iTunes Music Store. The label’s catalog has been built primarily from North American licensing deals with artists and record labels from England, Sweden, Italy, Indonesia, Gambia, and the USA. Current releases include music from Boiled In Lead, Kari Tauring, Felonious Bosch and the Reptile Palace Orchestra.
