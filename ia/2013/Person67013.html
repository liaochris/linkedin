John Sattler, CPC's Summary

I am the Principal of a Executive Career Services firm, providing a full array of personalized career solutions for executives. (www.jesattlermanagement.net)

I love helping my clients in all aspects of the Job Search Process. When I'm writing a new Executive Resume, Conducting a Coaching Session or sourcing New Opportunities for an Executive Client, my energy and passion soar and I become completely absorbed in what I'm doing. I am very fortunate...and my clients benefit greatly from this.

This is a fascinating business. My background in executive recruiting, writing, and sales management provides a rock-solid platform from which to create and deliver great career services. In addition, I have a network of experts and top-tier professionals with whom I regularly consult.
