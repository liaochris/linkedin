Scott Henderson's Summary

Scott Henderson has been working in the IT industry since graduating from the University of Alberta in 1989. He started his career as a software developer, then moved into product management, project management, test lead and business analysis roles. Since 2000, Scott has been working as a consultant, first for Fujtisu Consulting (February 2000 to July 2008), then independently (Henderson Solutions Inc.)

Having worked in such a variety of roles gives Scott a unique perspective on project teams. His experience as a test lead has allowed him to see where business analysts typically fail, making him a better business analyst. His experience as a project manager has taught him the importance of proper planning, and doing whatever is required to meet deadlines. His experience as a developer has given him a sense of what information is important to developers, which again improves his abilities as a business analyst.

Scott works well in any team, because of his flexible and focused approach to solving problems and achieving goals. Scott develops strong relationships with his teammates and his clients, and these enable him to work well with others when trying to resolve issues and move projects forward. Scott has experience in both waterfall and agile methodologies.

Scott is currently contracted with the City of Edmonton until December 31, 2013.

Specialties: Scott is a Project Manager and Senior Business Analyst, specializing in IT development projects. Scott's experience includes government organizations (Federal Government of Canada, Alberta Government, City of Edmonton), NGOs (Workers' Compensation Board), utilities (TELUS and EPCOR), and private corporations (Sony, Lockheed Martin, Newbridge Networks, Focus Corporation).
