Christopher Behnke's Summary

Meet Christopher Behnke:

As an experienced executive, entrepreneur, & expert in media, branding & technology, Chris might sound like a geek on paper. However, when you meet him in person, he emits a powerful energy & enthusiasm that are contagious. Chris’s talent for business showed itself at a very early age when he co-founded Avalon Technology Group, a small technology company in Portland Oregon, at the age of 19. Just two years after marrying his high school sweetheart Alice, he helped develop a web-based “thin-client” technology that paved the way for venture funded startup Hyperlinq Technologies, his second major business venture. As the VP of IT for Rentrak Corporation from 2001 to 2011 chris led the successful launch of every major new product developed for the company.

Chris & Alice now have four children, if you live in their neighborhood, you’ll see that this is one very on-the-go family! Chris often trains for marathons and races with his family in tow. He sees his mission as doing whatever is necessary to ensure his family’s power and effectiveness. Chris is passionate about giving 110 percent to EVERYTHING in life and purposefully casting CONTAGIOUS VISION. He currently serves on the board of M25 Ventures, Delta Ministries, & is the COO of Choose Growth.

Specialties: New Business Development, Executive Coaching, Business Optimization, Technology, Video & Branding, Web Site Design, Information Technology, Networking Technology, Contracts, Negotiation, Public Speaking, Leadership, Management, Software Development, Writing, Videography
