Dave Ratcliffe's Summary

I am a Solaris Systems Admin with cross experience in SCO, Red Hat and other versions of Unix.

I am a 10+ year veteran in the IT industry. I have worked in a highly secured government environment requiring strict protocols for system interconnections, and been responsible for securing and administering Solaris enterprise class servers to DoD specifications and monitoring them for compliance. I have experience with HBSS, and STIGing machines. I have also had extensive experience with manually viewing server logs for inconsistencies, and was one of only 3 people at NSA DISA Mechanicsburg with SABER (Secure Access Baseline for the Enterprise) Certification. I also hold a CompTIA Secure+ certification, and a DoD SECRET clearance. My experience includes the Healthcare Industry, designing, building, maintaining and securing a WAN spread over 32 remote medical sites in 22 counties, and connecting them to a central medical records database enterprise server system in a HIPAA compliant network. I have secured networks using VPN and Checkpoint Firewall, and have experience securing email MTAs, and MUAs, and deploying anti-virus solutions on multiple platforms. I have worked with Rippletech Logcaster (Log monitoring/Alert system), and Retina Network Security Scanner.

I also have fairly extensive experience in training, I have dealt with one-to-one and one-to many training situations, and been responsible for explaining technical concepts to people with little, if any, technical background. I have designed and implemented training for tape storage and systems hardware tier 2 call center agents for SUN Microsystems.
