I’m a technical marketer with a highly cross-functional skill set from past experience not found in many others. I consistently deliver results that have a positive impact on the bottom line. I have specialized training and experience in lean / agile business methodologies: specifically Scrum and Kaizen. I implement lean business principles into sales and marketing departments. I know how to turn-around sales and marketing programs that aren't producing results. I bridge that void by letting the data & facts guide the way.

Marketing Operations and cloud integrations are a couple of my core strengths, because they require a deep appreciation for data-centric concepts and systems theory. Marketing Operations is the confluence of Marketing Automation, CRM, Content Management Systems, and web development. This can be described as Technical Marketing, Marketing IT, Business IT, or Sales IT. The objective is to work with three distinct departments productively and to create synergies. Marketing Operations has the challenge to do more work with less headcount. I manage Marketing Operations / Demand Generation as a profit-center by focusing on sales conversions, not on leads. Lead generation is only a small part of my overall process.

I thrive in cloud / hybrid integration by leveraging these technologies to create value for stakeholders.

Key metrics: Revenue - I've impacted and shown Marketing's influence on revenue well into the hundreds of millions, SEO - wherever I've worked, I've grown unique visitors by 60% and above.
