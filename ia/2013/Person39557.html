Ed Henderson's Summary

Born and raised in Scotland, Ed is an experienced human being, with a love for designing, building, making and breaking things online.

Not afraid to get his hands dirty and his feet wet, he is open to new technologies, as long as they are useful and/or fun.

Ed has a real degree in Computer Science, has run his own business, worked freelance, been employed and acted as a Consultant. He is now employed as a Senior Software Engineer for POPSUGAR in San Francisco, California, USA.

He has a vast experience of all aspects of the industry, from software development to responsive websites to apps to social media. He has also been involved in reviewing and writing a number of books.

Ed thrives on coming up with fresh ideas. Making a difference and turning one of these ideas into a useful, working 'thing' are what floats Ed’s boat.

Away from the crazy world of the Web, Ed has ran the Edinburgh Marathon and abselied down a lighthouse, raising thousands of pounds for charity. He captained his local Scottish rugby team for 3 seasons, winning the championship as top try scorer, and leading his team to the final of a national cup competition.

You may not know that Ed is the Dad from Jack Draws Anything, and winner of the prestigious .net Magazine, Social Media Campaign of the Year 2011 award.

Ed lives in Walnut Creek, California, USA, with the rest of Team Hendo; his amazing wife Rose and awesome sidekicks, Jack, Toby & Noah.

Ed likes cake, bacon, cider and talking about himself in the third person.
