Jessica Riggs' Summary

A 3D artist with experience dating from 1998, I started with Bryce 3 and Poser 3 and have also used Truespace 3-6, ZBrush, Cinema 4, Hexagon 2, Daz Studio 2, and Cararra 7. From November 2007 through to early 2008, I worked under contract for DAZ3D in the QA department. I was involved in testing all programs and add-ons that came out at that time.

I specialize in textures and materials but am working more on meshes presently. I sold a texture set for the Daz Charger horse online, made many Bryce materials and ended up selling them as a set set to DAZ in 2006 that currently are included with the the Bryce content, and I have made various textures for Poser and Daz figures over the years that are available free from the Fignations site. My only other works of note is a combination figure made in Poser and cr2 editor of the poser eagle and lion into a Gryphon and my own Cartoon Pony figure which is still a work in progress.

Under the name Amethyst Gears in Second Life I sell various items made with prims and sculpts, using mainly my own textures and some sculpt of my own making in Hexagon 2.5. I am currently working on a mesh horse avatar for Second Life with possibly a port to Cloud Party. Model made in Hexagon 2.5, rigging in Blender 2.65. Current progress can be viewed here: http://www.flickr.com/photos/36016815@N04/sets/72157632218065192/with/10823133305/
