David Plummer's Summary

Emmy winning Television Producer, Writer and On-Air Contributor, with a strong background in live and taped television production, national syndication, social media, talent booking and management. Experience in creating pilots and launching new shows.

David Plummer's Experience

Producer/Contributor - Windy City Live

WLS ABC 7 Chicago

February 2011 – Present (2 years 10 months) Chicago, IL

Part of the launch team for this Emmy winning live morning talk show.

Produced segments with in-studio guests Vince Vaughn, Octavia Spencer, Martha Stewart, Will Ferrell, Zach Galifianakis, Jimmy Kimmel, Jeff Garlin, Chris Pine, Wendy Williams, Sam Worthington, Ed Burns, Barry Manilow, Gerard Butler, Dan Aykroyd, Kevin Hart, Seann William Scott, Jason Biggs, Nicholas Sparks, The Wayans Brothers, Kal Penn, John Cho, and more.

Produced an episode, complete with musical numbers, with special guest Rosie O'Donnell.

Produced a theme show on "Bullying," which won Windy City Live its first Emmy Award.

Produced a two-part special on racism called, "Facing Race: The Color Divide."

Produced Windy City Live's February sweeps campaign in 2013 called the "Search for a Star," which was WCL's biggest contest to date. This series of segments was similar to "American Idol," with the overall winner taking home a brand new Mazda 6.

Appears on-air weekly as a contributor, with "Plummer's Picks," highlighting the best in DVD/Blu-Ray & On-Demand movie titles.

Has personally interviewed such stars as Robert Downey Jr., Gwyneth Paltrow, Don Cheadle, Charlize Theron, Edward Norton, Steven Spielberg, Sir Ben Kingsley, Kelsey Grammer, Colin Firth and more.

Executive Producer, At the Movies

Disney ABC Television Group

Public Company; 5001-10,000 employees; DIS; Entertainment industry

2008 – 2010 (2 years) Chicago, IL

Executive produced weekly, nationally syndicated movie review program created by film critics Gene Siskel and Roger Ebert and distributed by Disney-ABC Domestic Television.
• Oversaw all aspects of the show including all editorial content, features, promos, budget and talk topics.
• Worked closely with more than 200 station affiliates nationwide.
• Maintained relationships with major movie studios ensuring timely reviews on the telecast.
• Produced more than 600 episodes of TV’s highest rated movie review program.
• Led nationwide search for new hosts, by conducting auditions with talent from across the country.
• Coached on-air talent.
• Tracked national and local market ratings, to assess market share and assure proper contracted placement.
• Revised graphics and participated in set re-design by working closely with scenic design contractors and graphic artists.
• Increased web traffic by more than 100% between 2009 and 2010, by implementing social media as promotional tools.
• Successfully launched on-line contests for www.atthemoviestv.com.
