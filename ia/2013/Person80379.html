Leslie G. Ungar's Summary

Established in 1998, Electric Impulse Communications, Inc. has become a pre-eminent source for coaching, consulting and confidence building in the executive and business worlds. Communication Coach Leslie G. Ungar, is on a mission to make companies and individuals realize their untapped potential and set them on course to achieve that top-notch performance level which is, electrifying.

As you will hear Leslie often say, the 21st century is a different business environment than just a few years ago. Leaders in both national organizations and emerging small businesses need to leverage every opportunity to be more visible. Electric Impulse Communications, Inc. works with companies in diverse markets: healthcare, manufacturing, environmental, hospitality, real estate, homebuilding, legal, media, and consulting to supercharge their communication and leadership performance.

Leslie hosts the Time/Warner TV show Forum 360 in addition to often being quoted in regional and national media.

When you work with Electric Impulse, specifically what accelerates your progress is Leslie’s gift to call upon her diverse background to find the right solution at the right time for the specific client.

Leslie G. Ungar's Publications

  • Herbie's Hints

    • Brooklyn Works
    • December 6, 2012
    Authors: Leslie G. Ungar

    It was not until after my dad died that I remembered all of the verbal jewels he had shared with me. My mom always called him Herbie. I started remembering one "Herbie Hint" at a time. I gathered from the reaches of my memory the tips he had bestowed upon me over the years. Then I applied each "hint" to a current day situation or challenge. What would Herbie say or do? The Herbie's Hints section of my monthly newsletter became the most popular section. I took those hints and added, edited and massaged them into a pocket manual for you to benefit from advice from my dad, which in many ways is advice from all dads.

  • 100 Tips in 100 Days:100 Tips to Communicate Your Competitive Edge

    • Just My Best Publishing
    • November 4, 2010
    Authors: Leslie G. Ungar

    100 Tips in 100 Days:100 easy to follow tips to help you identify your true value, communicate your value, and protect your value in your everyday life.

    In this updated, concise, yet powerful edition, Leslie provides advice that applies to everyday life, the business world and professional speaking. Tips include: how to open with a wow, what parts of a speech you need to memorize, how to choose your seat at a meeting, when to practice, and how to own the podium. This is the only book of its kind, written by a member of the National Speaking Association. 100 Tips in 100 Days is an easy to use trip-tik to help you navigate the journey to use your voice to get results, move your agenda forward, and communicate your competitive edge.
