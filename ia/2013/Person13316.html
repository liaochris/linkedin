Ben Stoddard's Summary

Purely self-trained, I first started designing levels for a game called Lode Runner when I was 5 or 6 years old. Since then, I've designed content for a handful of games across various genres including Neverwinter Nights 1 and 2, Elder Scrolls Morrowind and Oblivion, Half Life 2, Call of Duty 4, Portal, Far Cry 2, and recently Left 4 Dead.

Being hungry for knowledge, and eager to improve my own skills, I actively deconstruct and analyze games from different genres to determine what makes them work and how they result in "fun".

I'm currently pursuing a career in Level Design in the videogame industry.
