Dan Gershenson's Summary

Lots of people can post on a social media channel but don't understand brand strategy.
Lots of people can strategize but don't know how to create the compelling content that powers it.

I bring the best combination of both worlds, drawing upon 18 years of agency and management experience in writing, brand development, creative direction and client service.

How? 3 ways.

1) I write on behalf of B2B marketers and agencies that never have enough time, internal support staff or both to create great online content. Blogs. eNewsletters. Website pages. Social media channel posts. Essentially anything that can be written online. I do 100% of the topic research, writing, curating, distributing and progress reporting. All the while, you concentrate on your other day-to-day business.

2) There's more to it than content production. There's having the perspective to understand how it all fits into the big picture of your overall brand - and that's where my background as a Creative Director can help. I have performed over 100 in-depth Brand Audits that uncover the greatest insights and recommend the best approach via a marketer's playbook for at least the next 6 months.

3) I mentor small business owners and clients of agencies during 2-4 hour sessions on social media so they can learn how to do more of it themselves. I also use plain English over tech speak as much as possible.

Whether you're a small business under 100 people or a small agency, let's talk further to see if there's a great fit for your next project or ongoing guidance.
