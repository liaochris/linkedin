Grape Digital's Summary

We are an agency with social at its heart.

We help brands to not only find their digital voice but to use it to talk to the right people at the right time in the right places.

Grape Digital began in 2009 when a couple of experienced marketing professionals identified that social media was the future in marketing. Grape started working on building amazing strategies and campaigns for brands on social networks years before the rest.

Our offices are just a hop, a skip and a jump from Old Street roundabout and there’s always a brew waiting for any nearby thirsty travelers so give us a digital or actual shout, we answer both!

Grape Digital is part of the award winning Diffusion Media Group that has been established in the Social and Viral marketing sectors for over 10 years. Founded in early 2009 to fill the huge need and demand for an agency to specialise and be expert in Social Media, we are an international collective of social media mad marketing professionals. Working globally for some of the worlds biggest brands, we advise and implement strategy within an extremely fast moving industry. We are not simply a few people brought together from other disciplines within an agency to meet clients demands for social media strategy; we only do social and we do it the best.
