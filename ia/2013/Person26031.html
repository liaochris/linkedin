Jerome Braggs' Summary

Bio-
Jerome Braggs is a Spiritual Life Coach, Author, Inspirational Speaker and Conscious Business Mentor.

Jerome lived a life of shame, chaos, pain, crisis, trauma and drama until it all came crashing down in 2004 in an emergency room hospital bed where he came face to face with a life-threatening disease that the doctors gave him only 7 DAYS to live from. Determined to do whatever it takes to not only survive but to thrive, Jerome committed himself to a deep, personal spiritual journey in order to learn the truth of who we really are, what are true potential is and how we truly create in our lives.

Through this journey, Jerome learned the spiritual principles, personal tools and mindset patterns that allowed him to heal his body, transform his life and defy all the odds.

Today, as a spiritual life coach and teacher, Jerome teaches from the lessons he learned along his journey to help individuals on a spiritual path transform their lives and step into a thriving life based in spiritual principle and filled with happiness, health and prosperity so that they achieve their highest dreams and love the lives they live.

Jerome is a dynamic speaker with an extraordinary story of the power of the human spirit and succeeding against all odds. He shares this story worldwide through his keynotes, seminars and workshops.

His first book, “I Am Enough: A Healing Journey into Worthiness, Self-Love and Trust," is set to release Winter 2013 which will detail the life lessons and spiritual truths he learned along his personal healing journey that allowed him to awaken and leave behind a life of pain for a life of peace, love and embodied worthiness.

In July 2012, Jerome received the Edmond Business Times Magazines "Top 20 Under 40" Award.

Learn more at http://jeromebraggs.com

"Raise your consciousness and raise your life. It happens no other way."
