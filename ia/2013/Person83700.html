I have been named one of the world's top 21 network marketers in John Milton Fogg's latest book, The Greatest Networkers in the World.

ENTREPRENEUR
A little over ten years ago, I was dead broke, living in a small apartment and working 60 plus hours a week. Then, at the age of 50, I re-invented myself. It’s now been over 10 years and through hard worked and LOTS of personal growth, I’m a self-made millionaire and for the first time since childhood, I awakened my dreams and began creating my future.
I vowed to spend the rest of my life teaching others how to change their destiny as well. Now I have an amazing team of individuals, and we’re all building our futures together.
