Jonathon Alsop's Summary

JONATHON ALSOP is a Boston-based wine writer covering wine, food and travel since 1988, and the founder and executive director of the Boston Wine School. He is author of the nationally syndicated wine column "In Vino Veritas" as well as numerous articles for Vintage Magazine, Frequent Flyer, Newport Life, New England Wine Gazette, Mobil Travel Guides, Fodor's Travel Guides, Boston Globe, and others. Jonathon wrote Fodor's Dining Guide to Boston, Cape Cod, and The Islands from 1994-99. His best-known essays like "The Too-Perfect Hostess" and "Raise High The Wine Bar, Oenophile!" subject the wine world to the eccentric scrutiny of his unique critical personality.

Jonathon Alsop's Experience

Executive Director

Boston Wine School

2003 – Present (10 years)

The Boston Wine School was founded by wine writer and educator Jonathon Alsop as a "snob-free zone" where wine lovers at all levels of experience can come to taste, explore, and learn about wines. Each semester, the school offers a range of instuction from introductory classes like "Wine 101" and "Understanding Wine Grapes" to more in-depth wine experiences like "Wine & Cheese Pairing" and "Great Wine Regions of Europe." The Boston Wine School also hosts wine tasting receptions for up to 45 guests and multi-course wine dinners for 30.

Wine Writer

In Vino Veritas By Jonathon Alsop

1989 – Present (24 years)

JONATHON ALSOP is a Boston-based wine writer covering wine, food and travel since 1989. He is author of the nationally syndicated wine column "In Vino Veritas" as well as numerous articles for Vintage Magazine, Frequent Flyer, La Vie Claire, Beverage Business Magazine, New England Wine Gazette, Boston Globe, and many others. He wrote Fodor's Dining Guide to Boston, Cape Cod, and The Islands from 1994-99.

Jonathon's wine writing subjects the wine world to the eccentric scrutiny of his unique critical personality. His wine column, "In Vino Veritas," is an eccentric, iconoclastic look at the world of wine today, syndicated weekly to publications around the world. Greatest hits include "Raise High The Wine Bar, Oenophile!", "The Too-Perfect Hostess," and "Veni, Vidi, Bibi: I Came, I Saw, I Drank."
