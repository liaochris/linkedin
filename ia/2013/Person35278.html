Diane Kimbrough's Summary

Diane Kimbrough is a live performance coach and speaker. She works in both the entertainment industry and corporate world with clients who want to create a more commanding presence.

Diane’s unique, energetic approach teaches entertainers and business professionals how to engage their audience. She helps them convey their message with a dynamic visual presence and delivery style while bringing out the individual’s authentic personality.

With over 25 years of experience in the entertainment industry, Diane is one of the most in-demand live performance coaches in the country, with clientele ranging from emerging artists to top selling recording acts. Executives seek out Diane’s powerful coaching techniques to achieve a more compelling presence in the business world.
