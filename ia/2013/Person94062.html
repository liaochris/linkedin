Mike Cooper's Summary

Mike helps high tech organizations get the basics right when it comes to managing and focusing their work, leading their people, and hiring and developing talent. In his many years’ experience of project, program and line management of both internally facing and externally facing teams, he has proven himself able to move into challenging situations, determine the requirements, problems, or opportunities for improvement, and identify and execute positive changes.

Mike supports the project management profession, being an active participant in the Project Management Institute (PMI). He was a founding board member of PMI’s Consulting Community of Practice, and has delivered many presentations & training courses nationally.

Specialties

IT and engineering management, project and program management, methodology development and training
