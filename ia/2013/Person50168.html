Gregory Hoole's Summary

Over the past twelve years, Greg has held several development and project management positions where he has designed, developed, implemented and supported numerous online software applications. His ability to bring the development and management aspects of a project together combined with his frequent exposure to full life cycle development projects make him a rare breed in the software development industry.

Greg has a background in computer science and has worked for several well known organizations including Toms Shoes, Stussy, Reebok, Harvard Business School Publishing, the Christian Science Publishing Society, the Defense National Stockpile Center and the Financial Crimes Enforcement Network. His skill set includes programming in Magento, Zend Framework, PHP, XML, Classic ASP, JavaScript, VBScript, CSS and HTML, database development and design using MySQL and SQL Server, and a vast understanding of e-commerce applications, content management systems (CMS), disparate systems integration, open source technologies, object oriented programming and PHP development frameworks.
