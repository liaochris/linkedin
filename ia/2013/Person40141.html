Eileen Pardini, M.A., Educator * Earthkeeper's Summary

I teach professional development courses at John F. Kennedy University in the Schools of Holistic Studies and Professional Psychology: Effective Communication, Teaching Practicum, and Exploring Professional Identity.

I also facilitate my own workshops and retreats centered around the art and practice of reverence for self, others, nature and source. I support individuals in deepening their connection with the world around them through meaningful practices of self-reflection, transformative art, and devotional reciprocity.

Committed to nurturing the wisdom and consciousness needed for quality relationships and a sustainable Earth,I bring creativity, intuitive skills, sensitivity, compassion and vision to my work and relationships.

I am an experienced trainer and group facilitator and have worked extensively with business professionals, educators, school administrators, volunteers, students, and non-profits.

Individuals invest in my workshops and retreats to nurture enduring, meaningful relationships with themselves, others, and the world around them.
