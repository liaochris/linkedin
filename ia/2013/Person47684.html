Genevieve Gelinas' Summary

Geneviève serves as Webmaster at SOQUIJ and is located in Montreal, QC.

At present, Geneviève, is a member of the Quebec Bar and the Legal IT organizing committee, as well as team captain of Équipe SOQUIJ (MS Bike Tour). She also serves on the board of the Little Elves Foundation.

Geneviève attended Université de Montréal where she earned a B.A. in History in 1993 and an LL.B. (Bachelor of Law) in 1994. She had previousy earned her DEC in Human sciences from Collège Jean-de-Brébeuf (1988).

Geneviève enjoys good food, cycling, birdwatching, travelling and community work with the Little Elves Foundation (http://littleelves.org). You can sponsor her 2013 MS Bike Tour ride at http://ggelinas.net/velotour.

Spécializations : Geneviève's core expertise includes website maintenance and accessibility, as well as email marketing and analytics.
