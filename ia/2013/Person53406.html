Holiday Johnson's Summary

As she has for over 35 years, yoga pioneer Holiday Johnson offers personal instruction for all ages & abilities, including monthly workshops & annual retreats to Kauai & south France.

Trained by international master teachers, w/a classical Iyengar-style Hatha yoga foundation, Holiday’s unique style emphasizes individual need over conforming to systems. Holiday advocates for her students, helping them reach a harmonious balance of spirit, mind, & body.

Holiday’s Yoga Path

CENTERING
Meeting Her Self through Yoga
Holiday first sought physical relief, but quickly found her whole life enhanced by yoga. She became a passionate advocate, determined to share the gift of yoga w/everyone.

EXPANDING
Widening the Path for Others
Through learning & teaching, Holiday established a yoga presence in businesses & gyms in Portland. Holiday’s enthusiasm led to the expansion of PSU’s program to 100s of students, and she boldly initiated classes in community programs, and in corporations like Nike & Mentor Graphics.

When the demand for yoga was too large, Holiday held the first-ever teacher training in Oregon & helped develop standards for yoga certification in Portland. She then opened Holiday’s Health & Fitness Yoga Center for fifteen years.

EMPOWERING
Reaching Out to Teens & Teachers of Teens
2012 marked the 20th anniversary of the nationally recognized non profit Standing on Your Own Two Feet™, which serves teens & teachers of teens. Holiday learned to access her own inner wisdom through yoga while facing challenges of childhood & early motherhood while still a teen.

The curriculum emphasizes fitness w/awareness, personal development, self-esteem, & building life-long health habits.

INTEGRATING
Practicing at Home & Abroad
A grandmother of 11, Holiday has shifted focus from directing an entire center to offering personal yoga training, retreats, & workshops; developing a teen program; and teaching through the Hawthorne Wellness Center.

Specialties

Student of Thich Nhat Hanh, Zen Buddhst Monk, poet & peace activist.
