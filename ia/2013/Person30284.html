    Authors: Danny Shacham, Gennadi Lembersky,

    Morphological analysis and disambiguation are crucial stages in a variety of natural lan-
    guage processing applications, especially when languages with complex morphology are
    concerned. We present a system which disambiguates the output of a morphological an-

    them under linguistically-motivated constraints. We explore several approaches to clas-

    corpus. Our best result, over 84% accuracy, compares favorably with the state of the art
