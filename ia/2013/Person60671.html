Jean Whaley Williams' Summary

With over 10 years experience in the entertainment/media industry, I've established a proven track record of clear accomplishments in identifying, pursuing and successfully closing complex, multi-million dollar enforcement actions involving the country's largest MSOs. In order to drive better sustainable results, I have built trust and long-term relationships with key employees in the Licensing Division of the U.S. Copyright Office and have created a powerful and compelling "brand" among cable and satellite providers and their counsel, developing a reputation both for fairness and accuracy.

Specialties

My professional background includes both Europe (France) and Asia (Japan), and I am comfortable operating at the crossroads of the quantitative/analytical and the verbal/creative. I am equally at home in the more "fixed" world of regulatory affairs and the more "free wheeling" world of the performing arts.

Thrived on turning around projects that presented technical and management challenges. Achieved maximum impact by creatively employing all available resources. Leveraged exceptional writing and analytical skills to prepare effective advocacy materials such as background papers and talking points.
