Dan Portik's Summary

30 years of professional inside sales, field sales, sales management and development of successful corporate marketing/branding campaigns. As owner of the Jamestown Creative Group for 23 years, I have acquired extensive experience in all aspects of operations and management of a small marketing/advertising agency. Over the years, my company has specialized in effective high end graphic design, web design, commercial photography and video production for companies in the Cleveland Ohio area and beyond. I have had the opportunity to work with some of the largest companies in the world to help develop creative marketing tools and campaigns that have garnered successful results.

Currently, I head up the Cleveland divisions of GenEra Strategies a nationally based integrated marketing firm and GenAge Marketing which focuses primarily on helping customers successfully market their product or service to the boomer and senior demographic. In addition, I have developed a local marketing group called Budget Marcom Solutions. This group is designed to work with local small to medium size companies looking so save money on advertising projects by unitizing my network of small agencies and freelancers.

Specialties: Proficient in operating and managing a Graphic Design, Web Design, Commercial Photography and Commercial Video Production Company. Extensive experience with body language analysis, professional negotiating and successful closing techniques. 30 years as studio musician, songwriter, jingle writer and studio engineer. Software knowledge: Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Movie Maker, Publisher,QuickBooks Pro, CS4, Final Cut Pro, Protools, Filemaker Pro, Constant Contact. Work in both PC/Mac environment.
