Josie Thomson's Summary

Josie Thomson is a multi-award winning and internationally acclaimed certified master coach, life-style author and is well sought after on the global speaking circuit.

Josie is Managing Director at Accelerated Excellence and has been a senior associate, coach, trainer, assessor and mentor with NeuroLeadership Group (formerly Results Coaching Systems) for over a decade. Josie is an organisational change expert and has trained hundreds of coaches in the Asia Pacific region, and coached thousands of people around the globe.

Josie is passionate about unleashing the full force of people's potential, particularly aligning actions and words with truth, integrity and results. Josie coaches leaders to achieve sustainable change and breakthrough personal and business outcomes. She is also committed to the development of coaches and the coaching industry as a profession.

Josie has completed studies in Business, and in the Neuroscience of Leadership, and facilitates the Neuroleadership Institute local interest group in Brisbane. Josie is a leading coach in AsiaPacific on change leadership.

Having survived cancer twice, most recently a brain tumor, Josephine is devoted to sharing the learnings from her experiences for the improvement in the lives of others. She supports people in overcoming their fears and stressors; encouraging them to connect with life and powerfully express their true and authentic selves.
