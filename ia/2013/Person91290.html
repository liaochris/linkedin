Melissa Motz, CFP®, CPA's Summary

Since 1983, Motz Wealth Management has met client needs in a timely and caring way through attention to detail, a friendly, personal approach, and an understanding of complex financial, tax, and economic issues. We offer a full range of financial planning and wealth management services, specializing in financial planning for women in transition. At Motz Wealth Management, we use comprehensive analysis and personalized recommendations to help our clients take control of their financial lives.

Melissa Motz, CFP®, CPA, MST, is the founder and president of Motz Wealth Management. She has more than 25 years experience in investment management, financial planning, and tax planning.

Specialties

Motz Wealth Management offers clients assistance with financial planning, investment management, retirement planning, estate planning, tax planning, divorce financial planning, and more.
