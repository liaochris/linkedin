Carey Schillig's Summary

Carey Schillig Hair Care is a "One-Woman" hair salon, offering haircuts, hair color, and highlights for both men and women in the Corridor.

Proud to be in North Liberty, Iowa renting my own studio out of the '965 Salon & Spa' commercial building (located right off of HWY 965). Thanks to the hair-cutting guidance and teachings from my Uncle Hal Valentine in Los Angeles, CA. Carey Schillig Hair Care specializes in:

- Long hair
- Curly hair
- Men's hair
- Colored hair

Carey Schillig Hair Care also prefers to use hair products that are sulfate-free, paraben-free, and gluten-free. Her product choice is called Surface Hair Health Art (surfacehair.com). All of these hair products smell amazing and all of the bottles can be recycled. Good for your hair, and good for the earth.

Celebrating six successful years of helping to provide fabulous hair care throughout the Corridor. Schedule your hair appointment today! 319-651-5558 www.careyschillighaircare.com
