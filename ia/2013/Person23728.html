Chris Aarons' Summary

Chris Aarons is a seasoned marketing professional who is breaking ground at the intersection where PR, word-of-mouth, content marketing, demand generation and social media all meet. He is the co-author of the book "Social Media Judo – The essential guide to mastering social media and delivering real results for your brand." In addition, he facilitated many group programs for clients, spoken at industry group events and teaches and serves as a guest lecturer on Social Media and Word-of-mouth Marketing at the University of Texas at Austin.

An innovator with more than seventeen years experience, he has created and implemented powerhouse programs in a variety of professional settings. From international PR agencies to Fortune 500 companies, Aarons is the quintessential "go-to" guy in strategic marketing that requires the highest level of creativity and knowledge. In 2005, he launched a successful social media program at AMD, which quickly defined best practices in the world of social media and influencer relations.

Aarons co-founded Ivy Worldwide, formerly BuzzCorps, Inc., a hands-on word-of-mouth, social media and content marketing consultancy. While at Ivy, he received top honors from the Word of Mouth Marketing Association (WOMMA) for their “31 Days of the Dragon” campaign. Since then, it has been become one of the most recognized and copied social media campaigns ever. In addition, Chris has won Summit International’s Emerging Media Award for Effectiveness, HP’s Circle Award for Worldwide Marketing Excellence and were finalist for ad:tech’s Limelight Award for Best Social Media Marketing campaign as well as Promo Magazine’s Pro Award for Best Campaign on a Budget, among others.

Aarons has successfully tackled challenges for industry-leading companies including Adobe, AMD, Dell, HP, LG, Microsoft, Philips and others.
