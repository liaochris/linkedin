Donna Marie Thompson, PhD's Summary

Small Business Profitability Specialist

Increased profits and cut costs for scores of small business in professional services firms, including lawyers, consultants, advisors, and coaches. How many times have you tried the same thing over and over with limited results? If you need more time and more money, then you need a more analytical approach. What is your ROI? Do you know your costs? Do you know where your time goes each week? Do you know where the hidden profits are in your business?

Get the results you expect through Profit Assessments, Cost Assessments, Business Potential Assessments, Energy Leadership Index Assessments, and other powerful and practical tools. Your customized action plan will quickly get you what you want from your business. Business owners have more time, more money, and more energy to focus on a few key things to dramatically increase profits - the gold that is already in the small business.

Core products and services include on-line training courses, profit strategy development, webinars, teleseminars, mastermind groups, group coaching, custom assessments, and membership options for premium content.

Acclaimed public speaking (both keynote and break out) at conferences, workshops, business meetings, retreats, breakfasts, luncheons, and on cruse ships.

Certifications: MBA in Marketing, PhD in International Business, Certified Public Accountant, Master Practitioner of Energy Leadership Index, Certified Professional Coach, Certified NLP Practitioner.

Co-author of best- seller "Bouncing Back: Thriving in Changing Times" featuring Brian Tracy, David Riklan, Wayne Dyer, and John Assaraf.

Specialties

Performance management, ROI analysis, productivity management, time management, profitability analysis. You can increase profits, cut costs, eliminate waste, increase free time, and increase success. Optimize results, increase productivity, focus on competitive advantage, analyze cost structure. Value each hour of your time at its highest level.

Conducting powerful assessments for lawyers, consultants, entrepreneurs, marketers, coaches, and other small business owners.
