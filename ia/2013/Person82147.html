Melinda Roberts' Summary

I provide consulting services in the areas of GPS and wireless positioning and location-based apps and services. I am interested in technical advisory roles for building product roadmaps and bringing new products to market. I provide advice on technology and solutions for location-aware mobile and social networking apps. I also review patent portfolios and provide technical advice to legal teams for competitive and infringement analysis. I have a keen interest in working with product management, marketing, legal and engineering teams.

Thorough knowledge of and hands-on experience developing assisted GPS, network-only and hybrid positioning technologies for location-based apps and services. Key contributor to positioning technology development on Qualcomm's location server and gpsOne solution. Experience in reviewing patent portfolios and providing technical advice to legal teams for competitive and infringement analysis. Extensive experience in algorithmic software development, computational math, modeling and statistical analysis. Thorough knowledge of product development lifecycle from initial concept and user requirements to software design, development, testing and release. Strong analytical and conceptual skills with ability to interpret and summarize large amounts of information into concise reports for senior management. Accomplished writer of technical memos and specifications. Dynamic team player experienced in working with offsite teams, third party vendors and customers.

Specialties

GPS, assisted GPS (A-GPS), cellular network positioning (AFLT/OTDOA/Cell ID), WiFi positioning (RSSI fingerprinting/trilateration), hybrid positioning, crowdsourced databases, location-aware apps, location-based services (LBS), navigation, wide area and global GPS reference networks, extended ephemeris, software design, software development, C, C++, Matlab, mathematical algorithms, modeling, statistics, numerical analysis, probability theory, data analysis

Melinda Roberts' Experience

Founder and Principal Consultant

Locatiom

2009 – Present (4 years)

As Principal Consultant at Locatiom, I provide technical advisory services in the areas of GPS and wireless positioning and location-based services. My services include providing advice on technology and solutions for location-aware mobile and social networking apps. I also review patent portfolios and provide technical advice to legal teams for competitive and infringement analysis.

Senior Software Engineer

Qualcomm

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; QCOM; Semiconductors industry

2003 – 2008 (5 years)

I was a senior engineer in Qualcomm’s location technology group. I developed assisted GPS, network-only and hybrid positioning technologies for the gpsOne® solution that delivers immediate, precise position information to mobile devices. More than 500 million mobile phones worldwide use gpsOne chipsets. I was a lead engineer for positioning technology development on the location server, and a key contributor to several prominent features including gpsOneXTRA™ Assistance that improves startup times for any location-aware device with network connectivity.

Software Engineer

Beeline Technologies (acquired by Hemisphere GPS)

2000 – 2002 (2 years)

I designed and developed precision navigation systems with a tightly coupled GPS/INS. This was real-time embedded system development using C and C++. Beeline Technologies was a startup building technology for autonomous vehicles in the agriculture, mining, construction and military verticals. It was later acquired by Hemisphere GPS.
