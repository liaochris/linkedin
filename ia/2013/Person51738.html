Harnish Jani's Summary

Innovation Strategist and User Experience Designer passionately helping organizations innovate and transform their businesses, brands and internal cultures to offer user- empowering experience solutions, services and create meaningful change in society by leveraging design as an empathetic, strategic and creative catalyst.

Thriving on Cultural Empathy + Strategic Thinking + Design Creativity

Eclectic experience across fields of healthcare, consumer electronics, consumer packaged goods, biotechnology, manufacturing, information technology, sporting goods, industrial equipment, housewares, civic works, emerging markets and education.

Led research expeditions in 9 countries across North America, Europe and Asia.

Interested in Design of Business and Business of Design.


Competencies:

Architecting holistic user experiences:
Passionate designer of empathetic experience solutions focusing on emotional user connections. Ability to think through complex problems in a holistic way to craft creative solutions connecting people, offerings, brands and businesses.

Establishing and leading practices:
Competent change agent with intrapreneurial spirit adept at establishing and scaling practices. Champion the value that strategic design can bring to an organization, build systems and foster culture within divergent groups.

Executing strategic design and innovation ventures:
Experienced project lead of innovation ventures, strategic design projects, global research expeditions, client accounts, organization transformation campaigns, co-creation workshops and educational series.

Enabling and empowering global teams:
Multidisciplinary and multicultural team leader who is a strong advocate for cross-disciplinary teams. Results oriented manager able to define, direct and execute projects while facilitating and coaching teams.

Specialties

Innovation strategy, user experience and service design, human-centered interaction design focusing on emotional connection, industrial design, business innovation ventures, ecosystem modeling, platform co-creation, experience design methods, frameworks and tools, brand management, design language development, ethnographic and cultural studies, Powerpoint defying presentation, graphics and interaction animations, story boarding, infographics, workshop facilitation and ideation.
