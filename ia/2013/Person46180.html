Joe Donofrio's Summary

Family-owned and operated with the utmost attention to customer care. For corporate events, business meetings, company outings, weddings, birthdays or private parties. Your guests will relax and unwind as they enjoy our outstanding friendly service and scrumptious catering. Whether you book the 120 person capacity Free Spirit or the smaller, cozy Gypsy Spirit (2-49 people), you'll enjoy Chicago's most comfortable and inviting yachts. We also offer Chicago River tours.

Joe had worked for his parents, Rich and Judy Borrowdale, in their family-owned business, the original Free Spirit Yacht Cruises, founded in 1983. A captain for eighteen years on their two luxury yachts, Captain Joe is an expert navigator who’s at his happiest when he’s on Lake Michigan.

Captain Joe and and his wife are now the new owner/operators of Free Spirit Yacht Cruises.
