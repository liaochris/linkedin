Dr Michael Leahy's Summary

My aim is to enjoy life to the full and for me this must include a rewarding professional life. I make a living doing what I love: traveling, writing, making TV programmes, and communicating what I´ve learned to young people through my educational and motivational presentations. I work with people I like and trust. My aims? To inspire others to achieve their aspirations, and bring about changes for the good of others.

My television shows include 'Bite Me' (National Geographic Channel / Travel Channel), 'Invasion of the Bodysnatchers', 'RoughScience', 'Pandemics', 'Horizon', 'Is Oral Sex Safe' and 'Labrats' (BBC), 'Invasion of the Bodyscratchers' (BSkyB).

Drawing from personal experience (both during my professional career and personal adventures) I write regular articles and columns about keeping healthy and avoiding threats, both common and unusual, when travelling by motorcycle in remote or exotic locations.

TV presenter, writer, educational and public speaker

Dr Mike Leahy

July 1999 – Present (14 years 1 month) Oxford, United Kingdom

Having worked as a television presenter and writer of television series for the past decade I now enjoy working within education and business giving lively, and sometimes graphic presentations. At schools and Universities I focus on the media, how it works - and sometimes fails, global health, parasitology and self experimentation. In business I specialise in the evidence and facts (rather than the bullshit) behind ´Self Motivation´.
