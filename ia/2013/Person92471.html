Michael Hiles' Summary

Are you a business owner or C-level executive who wants to grow your sales and profitability while also streamlining your business operations? Discover how I can help with my strategic business development, marketing and information management solutions. I will help you create a peak performance organization -- and milk that cash flow cow for all the profitability it's worth.

Business lifecycles are shorter and shorter. Products and services live and die within much shorter spans of time. Windows of market opportunity only exist for those who can develop successful systems on the front end, and the most efficient method of value proposition delivery on the back.

I help business owners and equity capital investors mitigate the risk of capital investment by developing a model-driven approach to business growth. I accomplish this by helping executive management identify and define market opportunities, map business process management of operations to the delivery of their value proposition, and architect the information management roadmap to support the business process.

Ultimately, this will allow you to sell more stuff at a much higher profit margin.... which is never a bad thing.
