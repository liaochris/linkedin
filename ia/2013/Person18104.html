Brooke Davis' Summary

As a real estate professional in Seattle, WA, I have in depth local knowledge and can provide you with the information you need on home buying, home selling, financing and owning real estate in Seattle, WA. Whether you are new to the market or an experienced investor, I have the expertise, proven track record, and resources to help you achieve your real estate goals.

I love people and communicating. I am a Realtor because I really enjoy what I do. I gain great satisfaction out of helping people whether they are buying their first home or selling their second. Often buying or selling a home is accompanied by many changes in life and you can be sure that I will make every transaction as smooth as possible for our clients.

What you get working with me is a very personalized service. You will always be able to reach me or I will return your call in a timely matter with the most accurate information available. I am a highly principled person and I conduct my business with the highest ethics.

As the real estate market continually changes, I am always learning and adapting to new ideas. When I started my real estate career I focused primarily on first time home buyers. Although I currently work with just as many sellers as buyers, I always enjoy the excitement and energy that goes along with your first purchase.

Starting in 2008, I began listing and selling short sales as they have become a growing part of our market. The purchase of the short sale may appeal to first time buyers, investors and second time buyers. It is very important with short sales to have an agent who is experienced and knows what to look for throughout the transaction.
