I'll help you Land Your Next Great Gig™!

Along with writing knock out resumes and other documents, I help them with strategic online positioning so they'll be found by recruiters and hiring decision makers, most of whom search online to source and assess top talent.

Why is branding so important in executive job search?

- The branding process helps you understand your ROI value and what differentiates you from your competition.

- The branding process helps you clearly communicate your value and good fit qualities when you network and interview.

- Branding helps you stand out above your competition in your resume and other career marketing materials.

- Branding generates chemistry for who you are, how you make things happen, and what you offer that no one else does.

Meg Guiseppi, MRW, CMRW, CPRW, CPBS's Experience

CEO, Executive Resume Writer | Branding | LinkedIn Profile Writer | Online Presence Strategist

Executive Career Brand

Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Professional Training & Coaching industry

2009 – Present (4 years)

I believe everyone deserves to work their passion.

By helping my clients identify and communicate their key strengths, personal attributes, drivers and true passions — the things that jazz them in their professional and personal lives — my personal branding process propels them toward jobs that meet their financial and career fulfillment needs.

I work one-on-one with clients, through a highly-individualized process, to define their personal and executive brand, and craft interview-generating documents ― elite resumes, career biographies, cover letters, and collateral documents.

Then, together we transform these materials into a strong online presence with LinkedIn, Twitter and other social networks and social media.

CEO, Executive Resume Writer | Executive Branding Strategist

Executive Resume Branding

2008 – Present (5 years)

Continuing in my mission with Resumes Plus LLC — helping executives define and communicate their brand for job search — I started my Executive Resume Branding blog, and began focusing my practice solely on c-level and senior-level executives, and rising stars poised to move into the top tier.

My first blog was designed to be a hub of constantly updated free information and resources for the c-level and senior-level executive job seeker.

To meet the needs of my clients in the ever-changing digital age of executive job search, I also began collaborating with clients to develop their online presence through LinkedIn, Twitter, and various other social media.

I LOVE the way my business has progressed and transformed over the years. I feel fortunate to be in the careers industry, helping people land the jobs they deserve and work their passion.

CEO, Executive Resume Writer

Resumes Plus LLC

1993 – Present (20 years)

I once again became an entrepreneur with the launch of Resumes Plus, a virtual secretarial/office support business, after a brief stint as the business manager for a similar local business – an off-site secretarial support service.

My focus quickly moved from typing correspondence and reports for small businesses, to resume writing and other career services, with a client base of mostly mid- to senior-level executives in a wide variety of industries and disciplines.

In the early days, before the Internet and social media changed everything about job search, the resume was the thing. Along with networking, just about the only item in an executive’s job search toolkit was a resume . . . and maybe a biography.

Branding came into play for me from the beginning, before there was a name for it. Long before Tom Peters coined the term “personal branding”, my mission was to differentiate my clients’ promise of value to their target employers and position them as a good fit for the jobs they were seeking ― cornerstones of branding.

Resume Writer and Job Search Strategist (pro bono)

(non-profit) Project Self-Sufficiency of Sussex County NJ

1990 – Present (23 years)

A grassroots non-profit agency providing comprehensive services to lift impoverished, marginalized women and children out of the cycle of poverty.

I first joined PSS as the Administrative Assistant to the Executive Director, then became a volunteer when I moved on in my career and founded Resumes Plus LLC.

This organization is very close to my heart. I feel fortunate to have been able to offer support to this vital helping hand in my community.

After contributing thousands of hours of fundraising leadership and administrative support, I was deeply honored to receive the agency’s prestigious “Mosaic Award” for outstanding contributions to PSS participants.

Founder/President

Organic Garden Center

1983 – 1993 (10 years)

Organic growers of exotic herbs and vegetable plants.

How did running a garden center prepare me to market executive job seekers? As the General Manager, I handled all the advertising and promotional efforts for the business.

Conceptualizing and writing new ad copy on a weekly basis fortified my precision writing skills. I quickly became deft at coming up with neat, compelling turns of phrases to market the unique products we were selling.

Writing career marketing communications requires the same kind of tight, compelling, and to-the-point writing.
