Julie Colbrese, CPCC, PCC's Summary

As a certified, co-active coach, Julie maintains a private coaching practice, Hot Coffee Coaching, where she combines coaching skills with creative problem solving tools developed through 20 years in the creative services industry.

Entwined with her coaching practice, Julie is a Group Chair for Vistage International, the world’s leading chief executive organization. As a Chair, Julie facilitates monthly group meetings of business owners/leaders and serves as an executive coach to each group member. In addition, Julie serves as an in-market mentor, trainer and speaker for new and tenured Vistage chairs.

Specialties

Executive Coaching, Small Business Coaching, Life Coaching, Creativity Coaching, Workshop Facilitation, Creative Problem Solving, Advertising & Marketing
