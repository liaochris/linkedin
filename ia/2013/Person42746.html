Erin Ellison's Summary

I'm a graphic designer with over ten years of experience creating and executing marketing strategies. I help passionate entrepreneurs brand and promote themselves through logo, print collateral, and web design. I strive to create designs that are fresh, innovative and and in turn my clients attract their target market and are more successful.

Specialties

I specialize in branding and corporate identity, logo development, print design, brochures, annual reports, catalogs, advertising design, invitations, announcements, personal branding, digital photography, and web site design. Outstanding project management, client and vendor communication and ability to work with clients time frame, budget and individual needs.
