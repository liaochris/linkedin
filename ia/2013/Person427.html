Founder & CEO

Switchbridge, LLC

October 2012 – Present (11 months) Westminster, MD

Switchbridge is a patent-pending healthcare data analytics tool that solves two distinct problems for healthplan sponsors that are otherwise insoluble. First, data fragmentation and clinical data leakage occur when new carriers, vendors or wellness programs are changed or added to a healthplan. For example, when an employer changes carriers, the new carrier has to re-discover from incurred claims those patients who would likely benefit from medical management support. Switchbridge allows for “medical management transition” so engaged members experience no disruptions and no unnecessary costs.
Second, the Switchbridge technology eliminates the threat of technological obsolescence enabling the end-to-end integration and concurrent use of various analytics tools as they become available.
