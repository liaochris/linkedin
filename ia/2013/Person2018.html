Adrian Ott's Summary

I serve as a catalyst for Relentless Visionaries.

My clients consistently say that I find the nuggets in the noise - I frame things in new ways that create "aha" moments. But for me and my team at Exponential Edge, we don't stop there. We help our clients to take advantage of the new opportunity.

Our clients are leaders on a quest for innovative approaches that take their business to the next level. Relentless Visionaries think big and need big ideas. They seek the latest market and customer trends and want practical strategies to apply the latest technology to stay ahead of the curve.

That's where I come in. Relentless Visionaries are extremely intelligent yet they know that there's too much information and disruption out there to go it alone. They also know they cannot rely exclusively on what their internal team tells them. I serve as catalyst for fresh ideas and approaches.

My work with Exponential Edge Is about helping Relentless Visionaries to achieve market leadership. We help executives to map the confluence of trends and technology across industries, delve deeply into customer behaviors and apply that knowledge to develop something entirely new. Our work includes:

Exponential Insights (TM)
* deriving deep-dive insights from social media
* defining true market differentiation for your offering
* taking advantage of industry disruption opportunities
* building game-changing strategic partnerships


Exponential Influence (TM)
* research-powered thought leadership programs
* behavior-changing sales and channel enablement
* social and neuroscience-driven customer engagement

Relentless visionaries don't just do their job - they leave a legacy. We help them get there.

Are you a Relentless Visionary?
