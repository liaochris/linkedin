Sam Armstrong's Projects

  • Flash Frozen

    • September 2012 to June 2013
    Team Members: Sam Armstrong, Keith Catalano, Connor VanDyke, Josh Herbert, Hassan Javaid, Chase Meyers, Grace Zwiefelhofer, Brandy Boothby, Joe Blumburg, Ryan Klauer, Reece Blaiser, Cody Jernander

    Flash Frozen is a Surival Horror Game developed for PC on the Unity Engine. In it, the player must escape from a flooding and freezing cruise ship while managing their body heat in order to survive.

    The game was developed by the University of Wisconsin Stout Game Design & Development Program's Senior 3D Game Design Class.


    The team responsible for its production include: Keith Catalano, Josh Herbert, Sam Armstrong, Hassan Javaid, Chase Meyers, Grace Zwiefelhofer, Brandy Boothby, Joe Blumberg, Ryan Klauer, Reece Blaiser, Cody Jernander & Connor VanDyke under the guidance of Dr. Diane Christie & Dave Beck.
