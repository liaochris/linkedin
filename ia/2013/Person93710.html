Mickey Ross' Summary

I am the owner and principal of The Ross Art Group based in New York City, a company primarily devoted to the sale of Original Vintage Posters from the 1880's to the present. The company is affectionately known as " my hobby on steroids".
During my early career in the textile industry I was involved in the creation and sales of graphic designs for the garment manufacturers who were my clients. During my frequent business trips to Europe I began to collect Original Vintage Posters to hang in my company offices. That collection grew in quality and quantity rather quickly. A few years later, an opportunity arose for me to sell my textile business and start a new career. What to do was the big question?
I found myself intrigued with the idea of selling posters to the design and wholesale markets. The Ross Art Group began life in Westport , Connecticut when I opened up a full Retail Gallery and Custom Framing location . That also served as a destination for our design clients as well as a warehouse to hold our growing inventory.
The focus of the business was, and continues to be, the sale of the finest quality Original Vintage Posters and Custom Framing , offering a diversified inventory for beginners up through and including collectors and institutions. The members of the organization work diligently to achieve the highest level of client satisfaction which has resulted in a client base of over 2000. We have been in business for over 16 years and were one of the first Poster Dealers to offer our products on the Internet. We believe that if a client is well treated form our first contact onward then they will remain loyal and want to do business with us again.
We relocated to Manhattan over five years ago to our beautiful Gallery space in Midtown at 532 Madison Avenue. Our clients can visit in person and see the full array of our Collection,
Please contact me with any and all questions that you may have in the field of Original Vintage Posters.

Mickey Ross' Experience

President and Owner

The Ross Art Group Inc.

1995 – Present (18 years) New York , NY

Owner and President of The Ross Art Group Inc. located in New York City. One of the finest galleries specializing in Original Vintage Posters, Fine Art, and Custom Framing. Mickey concentrates on all acquisition and merchandise presentation for the firm. The company is a diversified source of art that serves both the wholesale and retail markets. The client base is both domestic and international in scope. Mickey is a recognized expert in his field and has appeared on various television and radio programs. The company also operates a Vintage Poster Gallery in Bloomingdale's Lexington Avenue store in New York City.

President

Rosstex Fabrics Inc.

1984 – 1995 (11 years) New York, NY

Rosstex Fabrics Inc was a diversified domestic producer of fashion fabrics. The company was founded by Mickey Ross and grew to $22 million in sales and a staff of 25. From a startup to a well respected and highly profitable company in less than 5, Mickey was the leader that directed the design and marketing. The firm designed, sold, and produced fabrics for the fashion forward women's apparel markets. Sales were primarily in North America but did include an International Division.

Vice President - CEO of Operations

Renrob Fabrics Inc.

1981 – 1984 (3 years) New York , NY

Renrob Fabrics was a textile converter catering to the New York based garment manufacturing industry. Mickey directed all of the purchasing, production, and logistics of the company. Having started as an employee he acquired a partnership in this business prior to moving on.
