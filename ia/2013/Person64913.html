In-Spirited, LLC provides SKILLED ACCOMPANIMENT through professional coaching, spiritual direction, consulting, training and presentations, retreats, mentoring. Dr. Joanne Cahoon supports individuals and groups in paying attention to core truths and values so that they may be ever better embodied/enacted in life and work. Joanne is especially interested in serving those involved in human services, ministry, education, formation, coaching, creativity and the arts. She celebrates creativity, mission, the Spirit, and forward movement - counting it a privilege to accompany individuals and groups.
