George Dunn's Summary

Mr. George J. Dunn, Founder and President Of CRE8, is a worldwide recognized consultant, speaker, instructor, contributing editor, and author on Business Process Innovation and Improvement; Paperless technologies (e.g. Capture, Electronic Forms, ICR/OCR, Imaging, Electronic Document Management, Electronic Content Management, Electronic Signatures, Workflow, Retention Disposition, E-Discovery and COLD); and Complex Computer System Replacement Planning.

Mr. Dunn has over twenty five years of experience in the advanced technology and process improvement industry. He has served in leading roles for organization such as CRE8 Incorporated, GTE Corporation, Wang Labs, ASA International and Peat Marwick.

Mr. Dunn is a: Certified Lean/Six Sigma Expert, Certified Business Process Re-engineer, ECM Consultant, EDMS Consultant, Workflow Consultant, Management Consultant, EDP Auditor, and former CPA. He has served as an advisor to the Worldwide Workflow Standards Board for AIIM.
