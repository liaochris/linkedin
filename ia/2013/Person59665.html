Javier Agüera Reneses' Summary

-> Reach me: javier[at]aguera.es

Member of Sandbox Network
World Economic Forum Global Shapers (Madrid Hub)

Aged 20, I’m a young entrepreneur living in Madrid, Spain.
Since very young I have always had a passion for starting up my own initiatives, and shaping the world around me to the extent my age and personal life allowed me. I would define myself as a “permanent brainstormer”, as I’m constantly coming up with new ideas, and I love finding creative solutions to the problems I face in my daily life (which often end up becoming new projects). I like to do some sport when time lets me, especially tennis, which I love. Besides, DJing and discovering exotic cultures are other of my passions.

Having found 4 start-up projects so far, the most notable one was incorporated 3.5 years ago and named Geeksphone, which was the first European company to launch and Android-based smartphone to the market; now expanding into new potential markets and verticals with innovative mobile solutions.

As an entrepreneur, i'm used to do "a bit of everything", but I'm specially keen on the fields of user experience, product cycle mgmt., and in general, finding creative solutions to the day-to-day issues that challenge any start-up company.

I'm always open to any project or potential collaboration!

Javier Agüera Reneses' Experience

Chief Technology Officer / Co-Founder

Geeks'Phone S.L.

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Telecommunications industry

December 2008 – Present (4 years 5 months) Madrid Area, Spain

Geeksphone is one of the few hardware start-ups in Spain. In our 4-year history, we have pioneered mobile industry by becoming the first European brand to launch an Android-based smartphone in 2009, and recently by launching the first Firefox-OS based devices worldwide, aimed towards developers, in collaboration with Mozilla Foundation and Telefonica Digital.

Despite having launched successfully 4 products, we are small company in this industry. This allows us to take essential decisions in record time, drive agile innovation, and deliver optimized solutions with the end user in mind as a priority.

As an "OpenSource" inspired company, both of us and our clients benefit from crowdsourcing and synergy with major independent companies that share points of view with us.
