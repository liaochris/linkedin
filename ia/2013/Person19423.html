Calvin Fleming's Summary

More than 15 years of experience leading PR and marketing outreach on behalf of major consumer brands, start-up companies, cause-related marketing initiatives and government public education campaigns. Based on his wealth of experience, he’s able to bring together diverse teams to help clients meet their specific goals – collaborating with Spanish and multilingual media experts, social media and web developers, event planners, video production companies and other specialists.

His eclectic portfolio of experience also encompasses overseeing and executing campaigns for national and local nonprofits aimed at raising awareness or funding on issues such as HIV/AIDS, breast cancer and marriage equality – to name a few.

Before opening goodpr., Calvin held senior management positions at leading Los Angeles public relations agencies, a national nonprofit and a charity event production company. Calvin graduated from the University of Southern California's School of Journalism with a B.A. degree in Public Relations.

• Strategically pitched and managed several national and international media outreach campaigns that included analysts, trade, consumer and business press coverage.

• Diverse project management experience includes planning and executing PR campaigns for large-scale events (AIDSRides, Avon Breast Cancer walks, General Motor's Gay Day at the Auto Show), entertainment and corporate functions, industry conferences and political fundraisers.

• Successfully organized campaign fundraisers on behalf of a statewide ballot measure. Campaign raised $2.9 million during the 15-week campaign cycle.

Specialties

Areas of interest and expertise include social media, emergency preparedness, HIV/AIDS, breast cancer, LGBT legislative issues and programs that encourage community volunteerism and philanthropy.
