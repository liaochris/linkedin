Leonard Pak's Experience

User Experience Designer

The Climate Corporation

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Computer Software industry

January 2013 – Present (9 months)

I'm primarily doing interaction design and information architecture oriented work, with some occasional user research, and minor visual/front-end work.

I create wireframes, architecture maps, task flows, user research documentation, prototypes, and other deliverables. The prototypes have ranged in fidelity depending on the project, from simple paper prototypes/storyboards to ultra-high fidelity prototypes complete with animations, loading/selected states, complex programming logic, etc.

My work has included redesigns of existing products and completely new products from scratch, on both mobile and web. I work closely with the rest of the design team, product managers, engineers, and marketing to create pleasant, user-friendly designs under the varying circumstances and constraints of a fast-paced, agile startup environment.

Our products and target users groups fall under specific niches that require understanding of much unique background knowledge — it's been a challenging yet fulfilling ride to build that solid picture of who our users are.
