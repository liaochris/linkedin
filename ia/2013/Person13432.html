Bernadette Hendron's Summary

Breakthrough Recruitment Ltd, operating as an employment agency, is a dynamic Recruitment Solutions Company specialising in the Retail Sector. We offer an innovative and forward-thinking approach to recruitment.

Our core values are honesty, commitment, effective communication and most importantly, delivering our promise. We offer a highly professional service to both our clients and candidates, providing great career opportunities for candidates and delivering the “right fit” for our clients.

Bernadette Hendron, Managing Director has 20 years’ experience in the Recruitment and Human Resources industry. She combines an excellent knowledge of the local market with a proven track record in identifying the highest quality candidates – the “promise to deliver” is paramount to Bernadette’s well established reputation.

Breakthrough is committed to building strong relationships with everyone that we work with. We have an in-depth knowledge of the Retail sector and a team of professionals that will quickly establish the company as one of the leading recruitment solution providers in the marketplace.

In an ever changing industry/market, Breakthrough recognises the importance of always being prepared but also being flexible in approach. We view this as a positive so that we can continue to grow with our clients whilst delivering cost-effective, accountable and highly professional services.

With this in mind we offer integrated and tailored solutions designed to increase the efficiency of your recruitment processes and personalised terms and conditions to meet your specific needs.
