Jim Rafferty's Summary

Executive Recruiter who specializes in the IT Consulting, Consulting Services, Financial Services, Speciality Chemical and Energy and Utility Industries. Our firm has extensive experience in sourcing and placing high level candidates within these industries at the Sr.VP, VP, Director and VP Business Development levels. Our difference is we bridge the gap between client and candidates as we partner with both for a successful resolution to the clients needs and to enhance the career of candidates.

Market Shapers recently concluded an agreement with Careerminds to offer Outplacement Services to small and mid-sized business as well as individuals.

Market Shapers has updated their website to include a unique database in which candidates may post their resumes thus providing more career exposure for our candidates.

Specialties

Successfully sourced and placed Executive level candidates within our specialized industries, primarily due to our rigorous marketing, interviewing, requirements/qualifications skills methodology.
