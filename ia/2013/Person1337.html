Founder

Louisiana Design A Thon

October 2012 – Present (10 months) Louisiana

The idea for Louisiana Design-A-Thon was borrowed from the now popular Hackathons. I had the idea several years ago, but back then, I didn't know the names and faces of the 'night owls' of Baton Rouge's best Web Design and Marketing firms. My unquenchable thirst for knowledge always leads me to seek out like-minded people in my area and industry. Since then, with the help in the rising popularity of social networks (mostly LinkedIn), I can now see the proverbial men (or women) behind the curtain (Wizard of Oz reference). Over the years I've been involved in several groups and organizations whose membership generally includes professionals in the Web Design or creative industries. However, I never felt like I was giving back enough through my involvement. All of the people I've met and friends I made over the years had one thing in common. They love helping others and the communities they call home. Unfortunately, most of them aren't sure how to get involved in their community. And so Louisiana Design-A-Thon was born.
