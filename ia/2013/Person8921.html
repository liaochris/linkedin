Antone Johnson's Summary

Lawyer, advisor and executive with 15+ years experience advising entrepreneurs, investors, lean startups and growth companies in the full range of legal matters. Trained at top-tier Wall Street and Silicon Valley law firms, followed by in-house roles at market leaders in social media, online dating, digital media and entertainment content, ranging from $100 million to $1 billion in annual revenue. Drawing on 5+ years experience as in-house counsel and executive at pioneers MySpace and eHarmony, I offer clients unparalleled expertise in legal and business issues unique to social media/Web 2.0. With offices in San Francisco and Santa Monica, we are well positioned to bridge the gap between Silicon Valley and Hollywood business and legal cultures.

Specialties

Business formation and structuring, lean startups, social media, user-generated content, corporate and business development transactions, Internet advertising and marketing, angel investor/seed financing, venture capital financing, M&A, joint ventures, IPOs, stock option plans, employment and executive compensation matters, litigation management, legal department management, government affairs, risk and crisis management

Like our clients, we are focused on innovation, speed to market, and value. As startups seek partners to help them launch, cultural fit and a shared vision for success is imperative. Our goal is to provide high value to businesses who require that a small investment in legal services generate a large return.
