Jeff DeChamplain's Experience

Elegant Homes Director

Real Living Lifestyles

November 2009 – Present (4 years 3 months) North County San Diego

My passion is delivering a first-class experience for our clients. I'm an active, full time Real Estate Advisor with Real Living Lifestyles in North County San Diego.

For the past 11 years I have assisted well over 165 families purchase & sell residential real estate all over San Diego. When asked where do I specialize; my response is "in my people"... I work with buyers, sellers & investors.

My approach is similar to a consultant; ask questions and listen intently. Our buyer & listing consultations reveal the true purpose and vision of our clients.

I also have a great reputation of partnering with my clients to market & sell residential homes. We offer property evaluations with incredible accuracy. After researching the market I offer our clients choices on selling the home (as-is) or with property improvements. We sell most of our homes within 97% of list price.

As one of my clients described in a testimonial, "Lose the desperation, worry and days on market. Jeff is a solid professional that takes great care, listens and delivers."

We also speacilize in assisting probate attorneys with servicing thier clients.

One of our probate attorneys, Nick Tonner, had the following to say: "If you need a home sold, listen to Jeff. He'll listen to you, he knows what he's doing and he'll work with you to get your home sold. I've trusted him with my client. Trust him to serve you well."


Licensed as a short sale specialists, I have partnered with Short Sales Experts to deliver results to our sellers who are in need.
