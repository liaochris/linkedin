J.D. Wade's Summary

Microsoft SharePoint consultant who assists companies with their architecture, design, deployment, configuration and support for their SharePoint platforms.

My experience with SharePoint starts with the MS Technology Adopters Program (TAP) for SharePoint 2007 in 2005. As well, I have 10 years experience with the Exchange Server products starting with Exchange 5.0 all the way through to Exchange 2003.

I live in St. Louis, Missouri, USA, more specifically in Ballwin and have lived here for over 20 years now. I live here with my wife and our two cats.

I am active in community theatre. I love watching and talking about movies. I have been described as “quirky” and have the alias “KB Man” given to me by others.

I love being a SharePoint consultant and plan to continue consulting for years to come. I have worked for Horizons for years and love working for them. I don't plan on changing that any time in the near future either. I am treated well, pretty much have complete freedom and get to do some fantastic work.

I enjoy speaking on different topics regularly and have been very happy when I have helped do technical editing for different projects.
