Libby Lowe's Summary

I'm a storyteller.

In my role at EHS, I use my journalism background to lead our communications efforts and create compelling content—marketing materials, member communications, web copy, tweets and more.

As a columnist for EcoSalon, I write about issues related to women in the workplace and the world.

I have more than a decade of experience writing and editing for national magazines and websites. As a communications professional, I have experience building brands, developing marketing strategies and creating communications materials that connect with people.

Specialties: Writing, editing, content strategy, marketing, communications, social media strategy and management, blogging, outreach campaigns, building community, writing for low health literacy/low literacy populations, connecting like-minded people and organizations, team leadership, project management
