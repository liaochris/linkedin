Product Manager

CycloMedia Technology B.V.

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Information Technology and Services industry

November 2007 – November 2012 (5 years 1 month)

As product manager of the end user application portfolio I proposed and managed a new application platform based on industry standards and with innovative functionality. It has become the default and primary end user application, supporting the company’s ambition to be the leader in situation awareness based on imagery, entering new industries, and realizing international growth.

GlobeSpotter provides access to 360° panoramic photos (Cycloramas) and aerial imagery. The software and data (over a petabyte of data volume) are available from CycloMedia’s hosting infrastructure (Software as a Service). Data and functionality are authorized to users based on provided credentials. Using an Application Programming Interface (API) GlobeSpotter functionality is integrated into various (webbased and desktop) software solutions (GIS/CAD/taxation/asset management). Advanced functionality enable users to search, navigate, accurately geocode any visible object, take measurements and compare recent and historical situations. Clients are also able to overlay existing (geo)data on top of the imagery, improving the data accessibility and effectiveness.

CycloMedia specializes in the large-scale and systematic visualization of environments using 360° panoramic images (cycloramas). Due to, by CycloMedia developed recording- and process technology, large areas are photographed and entered in an online database. From each recording location, orientation and time are registered, which makes versatile applications possible, such as 3D measurements and modeling. CycloMedia is active in countries throughout Europe and Middle East and has clients in various industries such as central government, local government, real estate, insurance, financial institutions, utilities and construction.
