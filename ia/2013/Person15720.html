Bonnie Weiss, BCC's Summary

Pathways for Life Transformational Workshop Series for Women is an amazing way to transform your life. Discover the life you were meant to live and turn your troubles into treasures. Realize the greatness within yourself through guided self-exploration and Claim your unique path in life!
Reconnect with your highest values, deepest passions and greatest visions by joining Bonnie in this workshop series. Contact Bonnie for more information on the availability of this group or individual transfomational experience. .

Bonnie is a successful Board Certified Life and Spiritual Coach and her passion is helping others transform their lives. Bonnie helps others experience emotional healing, fulfillment and joy.

Bonnie also helps others create a vision for their life that will propel them into successful careers and lives. Without vision we have no compass to guide us and we drift through life. Bonnie can help you set your compass and navigate your success. She offers individual and group coaching as well as workshops and presentations.


Bonnie's long term experience in medical sales provides her with an extraordinary affinity with business leaders looking to develop a stronger presence in their market. She provides one-on-one coaching for professionals, helping them achieve greater success in a shorter time and group workshops on Navigating Goals, Moving through Change and Transition.

Specialties: Visioning and Goal Setting, Stop Stressing and Start Selling, Life and Career Coaching, Transition Coaching, Sales Coaching, Spiritual Coaching, Sales Coaching for Entrepreneurs, Facilitation, Workshops and Presentations
