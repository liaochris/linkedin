Don Hochler, Esq.'s Summary

Over the past thirty one years, my concentration has been in the areas of Commercial Litigation, Real Estate, Corporate Work and Collection Matters. I represent various institutions and businesses in commercial and consumer collections from prelitigation strategy through judgment enforcement and ultimate collection of outstanding debts. In addition, I represent health care providers in all aspects of their practices including business formation, medical practice sale and acquisition, maintenance, HIPAA compliance, collections and day-to-day business affairs.

Since 1982, concentration has been in the areas of Commercial Litigation, Real Estate and Corporate Work. Representation of various institutions and businesses in collections from prelitigation strategy through judgment enforcement.

Since 1987, engaged as the Village Prosecutor for Roslyn Village handling Village Ordinance, Zoning and Traffic infractions.

Mr. Hochler earned his B.A. cum laude from Queens College and his J.D. from St. John’s University. He is licensed to practice in New York before the State and Federal Courts, is a member of the Nassau County Bar Association and is an Arbitrator adjudicating civil cases before the Nassau District Court.
