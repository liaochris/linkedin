Mark Barnes' Summary

I have been composing music for many years and have the knack of bringing out a deep emotional impact on the listener. I strictly Play KORG Keyboards (my favorite). My goals over the next year or two will be to put a complete tour and concert together, highlighting not only my music but that of other wonderful musicians where the audience can come "See The Music". I am also available for TV, Movie and Stage scores, songs and compositions.
