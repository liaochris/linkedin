Anna DiTommaso's Summary

If your business has a "why," I can help you find the "how."

I specialize in helping service based companies transition into their second stage of growth with a strong and trustworthy image. Together, we'll position your company for maturation while preserving the values it was founded on. We'll do this through identity development, print design, web design, and social media strategy. Your business has already proven to be valuable and sustainable. If you're ready for your company image to match its success and potential, let's get in touch.

What makes me different?

My first concern is business. I am a results driven, goal oriented designer who is focused on guiding the target audience towards a desired response. If you know "why" you're doing something, I can help you with the "how."

I'm deeply invested in the companies I work with, and take the time to understand their rich history and ambitious visions for the future. My job is to help your company share its value with the world, attract the right types of customers, and motivate them to take action.


I would like to connect with business owners and professionals, executives, and fellow marketing leaders wanting to grow and strengthen their company's success and drive bottom-line results with creative marketing strategies & solutions. I will also provide valuable contacts and company/industry insights.
