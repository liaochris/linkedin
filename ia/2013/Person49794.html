Grant Allen's Experience

Senior Vice President

ABB Technology Ventures

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; abb; Electrical/Electronic Manufacturing industry

November 2012 – Present (11 months) Washington, DC

ABB Technology Ventures (ATV) is the venture capital arm of ABB, Inc. (NYSE: ABB), a global leader in power and automation technologies. Headquartered in Zurich, with offices in Silicon Valley and Washington, DC, ATV invests in high potential energy technology companies that can benefit from ABB’s engineering resources and global market access. Investing since 2010, ATV has deployed nearly $150 million.

Learn more at http://www.abb.com/ventures

Board Member

Scotrenewables Tidal Power Ltd

Public Company; 11-50 employees; Renewables & Environment industry

January 2013 – Present (9 months) United Kingdom

Scotrenewables Tidal Power Ltd (SRTP) is the world leader in the development of floating tidal stream and run-of-river turbines. SRTP's tidal turbine is an award-winning, patented hydrokinetic energy converter designed to transfer energy from tidal streams and river currents where they are strongest – closest to the surface.

ATV led an approx. $12M USD investment in SRTP in early 2013.
http://www.marine-renewable-energy.com/tidal-stream/abb-leads-12-million-investment-in-tidal-power-leader-scotrenewables-1403

Board Observer

Industrial Defender

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Computer & Network Security industry

January 2012 – Present (1 year 9 months) Foxborough, MA

Industrial Defender is the global leader in automation system management and industrial control systems (ICS) cybersecurity. With deep domain expertise and a singular focus on ICS/SCADA, Industrial Defender products enable customers to ensure the availability and reliability of their key industrial processes. Over 400 companies in 25 countries rely on Industrial Defender technology to monitor, manage and protect their operations and processes.
