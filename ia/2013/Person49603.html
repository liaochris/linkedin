Denise Galvez's Summary

After 15 years of working on both the agency and client-side of marketing, I decided to focus my energy on helping small to mid-size companies grow and compete by creating strategies that are exclusively driven by results. My business model is unique in that I offer big results without the big price tag. No one campaign is the same because no one client is alike. My goal is to build a name and brand that is synonymous with great service and even better results. All companies need a marketing plan in place regardless of their size or budget. Go To Marketing can create a plan that is right for you, whether we work together on implementation, or hire an outside agency. Past and present clients include Bodaclick.com, IMG/Sony Ericsson Tennis Tournament, LivingWell Lady, FitSource Fitness Club for Women, FitSource Training Solutions, VH Pharmacy, Mami and Me, A Customs Brokerage, Michell Consulting Group, Cogent Construction, Martinez Sordo Law, Merge Studios, and Generation-N.com. Marketing to go and made your way!

Denise Galvez's Experience

Owner

Go To Marketing, inc

Public Company; 1-10 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry

April 2006 – Present (7 years 9 months)

Go To Marketing was born in 2006 to fill a void in the PR and marketing industry. While working in retail marketing, I was approached by many colleagues and businesses to help implement marketing programs for their companies. For most of them, budgets did not allow for a full service agency or a full time marketing employee. Go To Marketing provides an alternative to the typical marketing dynamic. We call it outsourcing a high-level Marketing Director at a fraction of the price. All relationships are built around the needs of the particular client. We work directly with the owners to create a campaign that generates results. Whether they are looking to build their sales pipeline or simply brand their company through PR, all efforts are tailor-made. This flexibility and partnership with our clients is what has made us a success.
