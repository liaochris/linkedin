David Abel's Summary

Hello. My name is David Abel. I am a Wedding Celebrant. I provide bespoke Wedding ceremonies in UK and USA, that are a unique alternative to Church or Registry Office. I also train Independent Celebrants (www.foic.org.uk)

Thank you for reading my profile. If you would like any free advice or a quick chat about any 'ceremony challenges' you may have, then please call me on 02082 421842.

If you've any questions, please don't hesitate to contact me: david@uk-celebrant.co.uk

Whether an intimate setting or a black tie affair, I work hard to exceed expectations and create a memorable and personal ceremony that will be completely unique. A ceremony that will touch the hearts of the Bride and Groom, their family and guests, making it truly meaningful.

Creative Ceremonies fill an unmet need by creating individual personalized wedding ceremonies that honour, respect, and incorporate traditions of all faiths and cultures. Inside or outdoors - any location!

As I meet with and get to know couples, I create personalized ceremonies for:-

► Interfaith and interracial Weddings
► Non-denominational weddings
► Civil Marriages
► Civil Partnerships - (same sex ceremonies)
► Renewal of vow ceremonies

I am passionate about every ceremony performed. Solutions provided for venues that are not licensed for weddings or partnerships. Such challenges I will overcome!

Many couples say I far exceeded their expectations.

I can, and will, make it work for you, so that you have the perfect location to share with your family & friends.

I am vested with the authority to marry couples in Florida and California. Your marriage is also recognised by UK Gov't. Marry at ANY location in either State. Sunshine guaranteed!

I have joined Linkedin to reach couples who really want to make their ceremony more meaningful than could ever be imagined. 'I love what I do, and do what I love'.

Outside of work I enjoy my family & motorhome travel in UK & Europe. Carribean Cruises also.
