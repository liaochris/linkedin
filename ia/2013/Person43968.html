Laura Cooksey's Summary

Professional Educator:

Creator of the highly successful "Proven Strategies for Treating Acne" Training DVD. This is a comprehensive system for skincare professionals to successfully treat their acne clients. This set of DVDs gives you precise and detailed instructions on how to:

Assess your client's type of acne and skin
Test your clients’ skin so you can give them the right home care products
Treat your clients’ skin with the most effective acne treatments
And most importantly, counsel your clients on their home-care protocol.
