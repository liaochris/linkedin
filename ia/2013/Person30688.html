Darlene Mack, MA, SPHR's Summary

Senior Human Resources Consultant and Executive Coach with HR Outsourcing firm established in 1991. Darlene has 25+ years in HR and Coaching - MA, Clinical Psychology; SPHR in Human Resources; CMP in Coaching.
