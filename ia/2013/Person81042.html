Linda Jenkinson's Summary

My interest in web content writing springs from a background in sales, a love of words, and a fascination with the miracle of growth.

I have been a parent who helped children grow into adults and a gardener who helped seeds become flowers. Now, I have the extreme privilege of helping small businesses grow into regional, national, and even global enterprises.

Since 1999, I have specialized in freelance writing services and web design for Internet start-ups and small businesses.

Specialties

editorial services, press releases, web content, articles, interviews, tutorials, and newsletters.
