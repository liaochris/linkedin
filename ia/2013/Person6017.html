Amy Cox's Summary

Amy specializes in marketing and graphic design and uses her talents to develop and promote an individual voice for each client. She believes in sustaining strong designer-client relationships and strives to exceed their creative expectations.

“It took me quite a while, working in several different fields, before I finally dabbled in 2 areas that had always fascinated me... MARKETING + DESIGN. That’s where I find myself today, and now I can finally say that I love what I do!

I specialize in marketing and design for small businesses on limited budgets. As a designer, I strive to create out-of-the-box designs, to push the envelope, and welcome new challenges with enthusiasm.”

Amy attended Baylor University in Waco, Texas where she earned her Bachelor of Science degree in Fashion Merchandising with a minor in Business Administration in 2003. In December 2009, she earned her Masters of Business Administration degree with an emphasis in Marketing from St. Louis University. In addition to being a freelance graphic designer, Amy has had multiple years of experience as Creative Director for a full-service marketing firm.

Amy grew up in Wentzville, Missouri and now resides in Spokane, Washington with her husband. In their free time, the active couple likes to travel the world and enjoy the beauty of the outdoors. Their goal is to visit every U.S. National Park in their lifetime.
