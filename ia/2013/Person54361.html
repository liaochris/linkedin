Ian Dale's Summary

I am a creative thinker with an eye for quality and detail. I love to help my clients communicate powerfully in our increasingly visual culture.

As an illustrator, I apply timeless artistic principles and the latest digital tools to create meaningful pictures and appealing characters. I have an affinity for images that wrestle thoughtfully with concepts, as well as sweeter, fun stuff for kids.

As a visual designer, I help clients engage and inspire their audiences, crafting effective solutions for the web and for print. To each project I bring strengths in conceptual development and communication, with attention to user experience and detail.

If you're looking to add visual strength to a project, I'd love to hear from you. Contact me anytime or check out my illustration or design work to see if it's the right fit.

For portfolio samples, please visit http://www.iandale.net
