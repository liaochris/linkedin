Antoine Valot's Summary

I leverage my deep insight of business, design, usability and code to create visionary, innovative experiences that empower and delight.

Working for startups as well as enterprise customers, I radically apply new approaches to mobile, social, and web business to enable new markets, new customers, new opportunities.
