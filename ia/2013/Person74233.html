Ken Hubbard is the Founder of Team Robinson Technical Recruiting, LLC. After serving for 12 years in a technology-reliant military role, he joined a well-known leader in the full-service staffing industry. In 2004 Ken was awarded the title of Certified Staffing Professional through the ASA’s credentialing program.

Ken offers 30 years of practical hands-on technology-based business and recruiting experience; he helps companies locate employees with records of success and contribution.
