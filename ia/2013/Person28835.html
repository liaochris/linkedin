Dallas Diggs' Summary

Dallas Diggs is founding Principal of DiggsGroup, LLC, experts in rapid improvement of business unit revenue and profit performance and related organization structure and processes.

DiggsGroup methods are grounded in market facing, strategic principles of classic brand management as cultivated by Procter & Gamble.

We help business unit leaders realize accelerated results based on brisk analysis, design and implementation of market facing strategies.

Dallas has over 25 years of business building success with consumer, B2B, technology, and non-profit brands. Accomplishments include category and brand improvements for such enduring names as Kraft, Johnson & Johnson, AquaFresh, Coca-Cola and Sprint. His corporate experience includes senior leadership roles in marketing, sales and operations for Fortune 100 and mid size firms along with significant consulting engagements. Dallas’ work reflects long held passions for insights, analysis, strategy, logic and, importantly, results.

Among his business building accomplishments are:
• Repositioning Sprint Business as the best choice for high speed data
• Revitalizing Aqua Fresh toothpaste by outsmarting larger competitors
• Restructuring beverage sales force planning to improve Coca-Cola revenues
• Uncovering insights to create and launch the nation’s first family wireless plan

Dallas received his MBA from Wharton and a BS with honors and Phi Beta Kappa from Allegheny College. He serves on the boards of the Dallas Leadership Foundation and the Positive Coaching Alliance.

Specialties: Strategy at corporate, revenue center, function levels • Consulting/Speaking/Workshops • Innovation • Marketing Leadership: Brand Management • Positioning • Market Segmentation • New Product Development and Launch • Advertising • Direct Marketing • New Channel Development • Strategic Marketing Partnerships • Customer Acquisition and Retention • Competitive Analysis • Market Research/Customer Insights
