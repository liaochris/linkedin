Kathleen Nagro's Summary

I have been licensed in real estate since 1993. I currently hold two real estate Broker licenses in the states of Florida and Connecticut. I worked in real estate sales in the greater Ridgefield, Fairfield County,CT area until 8/05, when my family relocated to Jupiter, Palm Beach County, FL. I now hold my real estate license with Lang Realty, an independent real estate firm with a superb reputation in this area. This is the exceptional brokerage dedicated to customer service above and beyond what is expected. Lang Realty is also committed to innovative technology and strong marketing. It's a client focused organization with the highest regard for ethics and professional standards.

My business has always been based on a relationship business model. I provide intelligent, professional real estate services with a focus on superb customer service. I believe in educating my clientele to thoroughly understand the current real estate market to enable them to make effective, financially sound real estate decisions.

ABR - Authorized Buyers Representative
