ILDIKÓ TÓTH's Publications

  • Dinamikus WAP-oldal tervezése és megvalósítása PHP és Oracle alkalmazásával

    • Gikof Journal
    • June 2003
    Authors: ILDIKÓ TÓTH

    Dinamikus WAP-oldal tervezése és megvalósítása PHP és Oracle alkalmazásával (Planning and developing of dynamic WAP pages )

    In the early age of GSM the price of WAPservice was as high as luxury regarding the connection fees. Nowadays the flat rate or the GPRS solutions makes possible to use the WAP. Besides the static WAP pages, – thanks to the new features of JAVA and PHP – the dynamic contain-based services play more and more significant role, and that is why our project aims to make a practical dynamic WAP application. The paper reviews the technical background of the dynamic WAP-service and its current practice. As a practical result the project realizes railway-ticket checking systems which can ask ticket information stored in the database, and make a list according to the search. The PHP and the Oracle based application can be a part of a complex system in which the passengers are able to buy their tickets using their mobile phone, and/or the ticket inspector has a direct possibility to check the payment of the fare.
