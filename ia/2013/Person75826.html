Kimon Angelides' Summary

The founder of EosHealth, Inc., DiabetesAmerica, Inc., MAB Generation, Inc., and BioNexus Ventures, Kimon Angelides, PhD, MBA (Kim) has consistently pushed the frontiers of health care delivery. He founded EosHealth in late 2008 and serves as its CEO, leading the company in enabling individuals, corporate employees, and health care providers to gain access to information and medical programs online, 24 hours a day, seven days a week.

Prior to EosHealth, Kim founded DiabetesAmerica in 2004. There, he oversaw the opening and operation of a network of diabetes care centers. The facilities provided a range of diagnostic tools, on-site laboratories and testing, and personalized diabetes treatment for a single co-pay. Under Kim’s leadership, DiabetesAmerica grew from one employee to more than 200 in four years. The company now serves as a model for single-service medical care for employers, health plans, and benefit consultants. EosHealth runs in a similar way, offering remote medical care with a comprehensive care and virtual medical home model.

Kim also founded the MAB Generation, which served as a platform for the research and development of monoclonal antibody therapies in response to cancer and infectious diseases. The first of more than 15 patents Kim has secured came out of his tenure with the company. Roughly half of his patents apply to monoclonal antibody therapies, drug development processes, and optical screening techniques. The others govern mobile and medical device applications.

Kim has served as a Fellow of the American College of Healthcare Executives, has sat on the National Advisory Boards of the NIH and the National MS Society, and he currently serves on grant review committees for the NIH.

He is fluent in English, French, and Greek, he also speaks Russian as the result of overseas training.
