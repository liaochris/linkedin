Kris Cavanaugh's Summary

I am an ICF Certified Coach, DiSC Facilitator, Speaker and Author. My background includes over 20 years of experience training and mentoring individuals. My company, Shift, specializes in helping individuals and companies produce more consistent results and increase their effectiveness so their businesses, organizations, departments, and personal lives thrive.

ADVANTAGES OF INVESTING IN A COACHING PROGRAM:
- Identifying skills and capabilities and learning how to use them to the best of your ability.
- Shifting your perspective on important issues.
- Inspiring vision and learning to think outside the box.
- Providing new options and new perspectives.
- Feeling in control of your job and your life by overcoming internal and external obstacles hindering your ambitions.
- Changing your mindset that can be holding back your professional ambitions.
- Clarifying what really matters in your life.
- Learning reliable processes for setting, pursuing and achieving professional and/or personal goals.
