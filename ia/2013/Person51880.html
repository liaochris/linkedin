Harry van der Veen's Summary

Motto: "A day without laughter is a day wasted."

* Ambassador of the city Skellefteå

* Bachelor of Science (BsC) of Multimedia (Communication, Multimedia & Design)

* Shareholder, co-founder, board member & CEO of NUITEQ®, an award-winning interactive multitouch software development company & the creator of Snowflake Suite.

Snowflake is globally the most widely adopted, off the shelf multitouch software solution for interactive displays, supporting touch enabled tablets, AIOs, laptops, large format displays & video walls.

NUITEQ® is a Red Herring 100 Global award winning, venture capital funded software technology company that has also been awarded the 10th Most Promising Tech Startup in the World by Innovate!100.

With its headquarters in Sweden and offices in India and Singapore, its at the forefront of a rapidly growing market for interactive surface solutions.

NUITEQ® provides emerging user interface experiences in the field of Human Computer Interaction (HCI).

The company's vision is to improve the way people interact with technology, through the mission of making interfaces between users and technology more natural and engaging.

It has clients in over 60 countries and partners in over 30 including OEMs, System Integrators (SIs), VARs, resellers, media companies, Fortune 100 companies and government organisations.

Companies & institutes using NUITEQ's multitouch software solutions include 3M, Lenovo, Credit Agricole, Swedbank, ICA, IKEA, Best Buy, Blackberry, Harvard & Stanford University, Google, Microsoft, IBM, Lexus, NASA, NEC, Nokia, Sharp, Panasonic, Porsche, Ferrari, O2, Shell, Total, Toyota and other well known global brands.

The main market verticals that NUITEQ® is active in are entertainment, consumer electronics, education, events/exhibitions, retail & interactive advertisement/digital signage.

Harry's driving force is to make NUITEQ's multi-touch solutions globally available.

http://www.nuiteq.com and http://www.snowflakesuite.com

  • Awarded 10th Most Promising Tech Startup In The World Innovate!2010

    Guidewire Group / Innovate!100
    • December 2010

    Produced by Guidewire Group, Innovate!2010, a global program to identify and promote up-and-coming technology and communications startups from around the world, announced the Innovate!100, the list of outstanding and fast moving startup companies from around the world.

    The List was developed after more than 30 Pitch Slam events on five continents over the past 10 months.

    The Innovate!2010 Summit brought the finalists from these Pitch Slam events together in a three-day program of workshops for technology startup founders focusing on critical skills required for promoting their companies to audiences of potential investors and business partners. On the third day of the Summit, December 2, sixteen companies pitched their businesses in the Ultimate Pitch Slam event for placement on the list, which was determined by a combination of the company's G/SCORE assessment, stage and pace of growth, and business potential.

    The G/SCORE measures a company along seven factors: overall concept, market opportunity, competitive risk, product development, business development, team, and business model. In each factor, the judges compare the company to key milestones that represent achievement in business building. Using the G/SCORE data, combined with other information about the companies, Guidewire Group ranked the startups who participated at each event. The top company at each Pitch Slam event automatically made the Innovate!100 List. Then, the organization selected the top-scoring companies, overall, and then the top five companies in each of the growth stage tiers (prototype, beta, product complete, revenue). This week, 16 of the top 25 companies honed their pitches at the Guidewire Group Studio G workshop in Zaragoza, Spain, to jockey for their final placement on the Innovate!100 List.

    The jury consisted of seasoned entrepreneurship mentors, advisors and angel investors from amongst others Silicon Valley.

    Worldwide 700 companies subscribed to the Innovate!2010. program.
