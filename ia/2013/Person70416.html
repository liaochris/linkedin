Judy Pokras' Summary

Have written hard news and features on a wide range of topics for The New York Times and many other publications, both on- and offline. Have edited articles and columns for magazines, newspapers, newsletters, manuals and books. Have proofread for publishing, marketing and law firms. Am the founder and editor of the 12-year-old online magazine RawFoodsNewsMagazine.com, which has recently been redesigned and features a breaking news blog. Have written and edited several raw vegan recipe books, and am the author of the novel, Artist Girl's Cambridge Daze. Have written columns on healthy recipes, green lifestyle, women's issues. Have written, produced and directed comedy videos. Am available for assignments in any of the above.

Specialties: Healthful lifestyle, organic food, raw vegan recipes, nutrition, green and sustainable topics, arts and culture, automobile safety technology, guest posts, small business, comedy, DIY
