Aaron M. Renn's Summary

I am a consultant who provides services in the technology, business strategy, and urban affairs/public policy arenas. I have nearly 20 years of experience in the field in both consulting and staff roles, and spent the majority of my career at Accenture, where I was a partner.

I also founded and run Telestrian, LLC, a provider of unique data analysis about cities, counties, regions and states. This provides power tool like functionality to professional users at not much more than a free tool price.

I am a leading national writer on urban affairs and have been a policy advisor to senior leaders in several public and private sector organizations in this space. Among my endeavors in this area, my plan for public transit in Chicago won first prize in a global innovation competition sponsored by the Chicagoland Chamber of Commerce.
