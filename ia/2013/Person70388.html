Judy Hamann's Summary

For 20+ years I've focused on developing and implementing professional services marketing programs for various organizations throughout the mid-Atlantic region. Client types have included engineers/architects, accountants, interior designers, mortgage lenders, financial advisors, various retail operations, restaurants, churches, schools and other not-for-profit organizations.

Marketing focus is on harnessing the power of target marketing, developing a strong client maintenance program, and creating the right mix of message and medium.

Specialties

• Creating and promoting a strategic brand identity and voice.
• Identifying untapped opportunities - what could be done.
• Reversing poor performance from traditional marketing (advertising) initiatives.
• Maximizing the use of and results from website and online marketing efforts.
• Become part of your team doing as much or as little as is
desired.
