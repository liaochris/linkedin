Mary Webster's Experience

Freelance Writer

Mary Webster LLC

June 2007 – Present (6 years 4 months)

In 2007, I launched my freelance writing career. Since then, I have not only added to my roster of category and client experience, but also expanded upon the kinds of services I offer.

I have been asked to draw upon my Journalism background to write feature-length articles, advertorials, and other specialty pieces. The interviewing, researching, and writing process involved in bringing an article to life is something I absolutely love. I've also helped perform research, planning, strategy, and positioning projects for a variety of clients. This, too, is a function which both delights and challenges me.

The flexibility of my business model enables me to work with people in a variety of ways:

1. I can partner with agencies to become a temporary face of their client team (pitching, concepting, and even presenting to their clients) or simply working behind the scenes when there is more work than their staff can cover.
2. I can partner with other freelance professionals to perform everything from one-time creative projects to full-blown pitches and integrated marketing efforts.
3. I can also work directly with a client's in-house creative services team or marketing department.

Copywriter

Mullen

Public Company; 501-1000 employees; IPG; Marketing and Advertising industry

February 2007 – June 2007 (5 months)

At Mullen, I gained additional experience in the Financial Services category, among others, writing radio, print, and outdoor campaigns for Wachovia and Wachovia Investors Services.

I also had the opportunity to work on a positioning assignment for Walgreens, which laid the groundwork for the positioning exploratories I've embarked on as a freelancer.

Copywriter

Trone

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry

November 2000 – February 2007 (6 years 4 months)

I was afforded many wonderful opportunities to learn and grow as a writer, as a member of a creative team, and as a holistic and strategic thinker while at Trone. I wrote for all kinds of media, including everything from print, radio, and tv to websites, mobile billboards, and even a children's coloring book. I became a valuable and dependable member of the team throughout all stages of the process, from pitch to production.

During my 6+ years at Trone, I gained valuable and in-depth experience working on a variety of brands, including adidas kids, Healthtex, Quick-Step Flooring, Century Furniture, Jefferson-Pilot Financial, Duke Comprehensive Cancer Center, Novartis Animal Health, Jacobsen, Syngenta, and Remington.
