Favorite Clients: High-achievers, medical professionals, IT and entrepreneurs, service professionals. Topics: creating personal fulfillment, healthy personal and professional relationships along with professional success.

S = Spiritual

Who uses S.M.A.R.T. Counseling and Coaching?

(1) You are married or co-habitating. You want to set a solid healthy foundation for an enduring, flourishing relationship that lasts forever.

(2) You are dating or engaged. You are excited because your dream just might be coming true! And at the same time, you are scared because you don’t want to repeat old patterns. You are smart enough to know that you may not be objective because of all the happy hormones fogging your rational ability. Your friends can’t be objective enough either. What if you ignore important red flags this time? You want an expert to give you feedback. You want to set a healthy foundation so your relationship flourishes forever. Or you want to get clear that this is not the right relationship for you.

(3) You are single and you want to transform self-defeating patterns. You want to develop a flourishing relationship with your Authentic Self before you get deeply involved in an intimate relationship. You want to grow spiritually.

(4) You want to find and heal the emotional root of disease and/or pain.

(5) You want to develop your ability to be a conscious creator so you can create a truly flourishing life.
