Betty H. Williams' Summary

Helping executives, managers, and career-oriented professionals identify and market their unique value is my passion!

I love it when a client says “Wow! I AM valuable and good at what I do. This résumé really highlights my unique talents. I feel so much more confident about my job search than I did before.” There are so many talented professionals out there who do not know how to market themselves in their résumés. And why should they? Just because you are a great CEO, COO, CIO, CMO, or senior-level sales manager, that doesn’t mean you know how to market yourself.

What qualifies me to write résumés? I have 17+ years of experience, strong writing and interviewing skills, and comprehensive knowledge of the job search. I know what questions to ask, what to include and what to delete, how to “brand” my clients in terms of their unique value, and how to combine all the pieces in a way that markets my client at his or her best. And I enjoy the challenge of making that happen!

I hold résumé writing certifications from both the National Résumé Writers Association (NRWA) and the Professional Association of Résumé Writers and Career Coaches (PARWCC) as well as the Certified Employment Interview Consultant (CEIC) certification from Career Directors International (CDI). My work has been published in several Jist Works publications, and I have led a workshop for other professional résumé writers. For over five years I served on the Certification Committee for NRWA’s Nationally Certified Résumé Writer certification. I was recently interviewed on a local CBS affiliate about résumé writing.

Specialties

Who are my clients? I have written powerful résumés and cover letters for a wide variety of executives and career-minded professionals across multiple industries and disciplines. Clients include CEOs, CFOs, COOs, CIOs, Association Executives, Chiefs of Police, City Managers, County Supervisors, Marketing & Sales Executives, Financial Services Professionals, Human Resource Directors, Attorneys, Production Managers, Purchasing Managers, and many others.
