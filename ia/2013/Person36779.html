DonnaLyn Giegerich MBA CIC RYT's Summary

DonnaLyn Giegerich MBA CIC RYT is an integrated business spokesleader. Serial entrepreneur, leadership trainer & professional speaker, DonnaLyn is passionate about empowering corporate teams, businessowners and peopleleaders worldwide. Content & partial client list at www.donnalyn.org.

Her work in the corporate, convention & conference space has taken her from California to Cornell on topics ranging from leadership resilience to work/life integration to professional presence & business building skills. The intersection of her business acumen, life experience, wellness study and zest for living are evident in her work. She coaches & consults by referral.

Co founder of Heritage Benefits Group LLC and a producer partner at Couch Braunsdorf Insurance, she has provided professional planning expertise for decades. She is an award winning businessowner, economics and finance adj. professor, mid life model and a Mrs New Jersey pageant winner for rare cancer awareness. Her wide ranging work has been written about in several books along with wide national and local media credits. She's been interviewed on NBC, News12, front page news for a Gannet paper and has won the yoga model search for the renowned Kripalu Yoga Spring 2013 cover to empower others. She is a virtual wellness columnist for a national health advocacy & blogger/advocate.

Her business and community leadership have been awarded by the Rothman Institute of Entrepreneurial Studies at FDU, National Association of Women Businessowners, Eastern Mon Area Chamber of Commerce, The Community Affairs Resource Center and NYC's Fresh Air Fund to name a few.

She is a business consultant for the Small Business Development Center(SBA), has a myriad of advanced insurance & real estate industry designations and travels often to present on business/wellness points of interest. DonnaLyn is studied in raw food veganism, a trained domestic violence task force member and seasoned Yoga Alliance instructor/national presenter.
