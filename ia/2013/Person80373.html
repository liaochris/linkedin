Leslie Ebersole's Summary

Passion and dedication are the watchwords in my real estate practice.

I use a 3-part valuation method to determine the best possible price to buy or sell a home. Pricing a home to compete in the market is an art and a science, and I spend hours on sifting through the data to determine the right price to list.

Each home we list has a unique marketing plan developed to make the home compete against the other choices on the market. I won’t list a house without professional staging and I’m now incorporating virtual staging into our program. We use the best professional photographers available to make sure that every photo on the internet makes the home look it’s very best.

My listings are featured with premier presentation on the nation’s leading search sites: Realtor.com, Zillow.com and Trulia.com. High-end luxury home listings are presented on the specialty websites like Luxuryportfolio.com and Regents.com.

One of my specialties is working with relocation clients. I am certified with many of the nations largest relocation companies including SIRVA, Weichert and Odyssey.

Before turning to real estate I worked in technology marketing for ten years. As Director of Marketing for Andersen Software I launched product lines in multiple countries and worked with professionals from a wide variety of cultures.

My interest in real estate came through working for a custom home builder as a project manager.

I’m a dedicated blogger and write about the activities, events, and places that make each community unique – and make the Fox Valley an exceptional place to live.

Specialties

Pricing, negotiating, marketing, social media, internet marketing, client relations.
