Chef Donald Crutch's Summary

Chefs Against Cancer believe we should not only take care of chefs but let's expand the care to teach our communities about healthy cooking. For years chefs provide GREAT meals to customers and friends.
We would like to bring Chefs together once a year to the "Master Chefs Care" to learn new recipes, fellowship with chefs and find out what's the NEW in the kitchen from each other!! We support our fellow chefs that are fighting cancer and their immediate family. Let's make sure we support each other till the last dish.

We look forward to all restaurants that employ chefs, and food suppliers to sponsor Chefs Against Cancer events!!!

Mission Statement:
Men supporting the fight against cancer.

Our Vision:
For every man to wear a Pink or Fuchsia Tie (bow tie) nationwide to raise Awareness, Educate and empower our communities about cancer.

Our Goal:
Raise funds towards opening our centers nationwide for our Signature programs and to provide education and resources for Men and Women in order to gain knowledge and Information about prevention, early detection, treatment options, and support groups.

Chef Donald Crutch's Projects

  • Bowl a Strike for a Cure

    • January 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Chef Donald Crutch, D.Louis Crutch, Lisa Crutch

    Bowl a Strike for a Cure believes we should support bowlers in their fight against cancer one strike at a time and teach our young kids about a healthy lifestyle and bowling.

    We would like to bring bowlers together once a year to "Bowlers Care" to learn about cancer prevention, early detection, treatment, fellowship with bowlers from other areas and find out what's the NEW on the Lane and in the Bowling Shops from each other!! We support our fellow bowlers that are fighting cancer and their immediate family. Let's make sure we support each other till the last strike.
