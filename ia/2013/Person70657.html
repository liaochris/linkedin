Julie A. Haverly Anderson's Summary

I believe that it makes good sense to aggressively pursue assets that are rightfully owed to you. It makes equally good sense to utilize the power of a law firm, knowing your lawyer is licensed and has the resources that you can rely upon.

I am the founding member of Anderson Law, LLC. We are a full service Missouri and Kansas Creditor Rights law firm. I have background in real estate, landlord - tenant law, foreclosure, bankruptcy, collection services and probate.

I am licensed in Kansas, Missouri, Nebraska, the Federal District of Kansas, and the Eastern and Western Districts of Missouri.

Judge Kathleen A. Forsyth was appointed Circuit Judge, Division 19, by Governor Bob Holden on August 1, 2003. She is currently serving on the Executive Committee and Board of the Association of Women Lawyers of Greater Kansas City. She is also a member of the National Association of Probate Judges.
