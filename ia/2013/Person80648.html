Lia Ghilardi's Summary

I trained as urban sociologist in Italy and then studied in the UK at City University and The Bartlett School of Planning. My professional interests are for cities and urban development through the use of culture and cultural activities. I have a diploma from the De Bono Creative Techniques School and use creativity and creative thinking in addressing urban problems.
