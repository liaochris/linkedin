Frank Gerratana's Summary

I help engineers and scientists navigate the (sometimes bewildering) intellectual property systems of the United States and of other countries. My core practice is obtaining patents and counseling clients on developing intellectual property portfolios.

I work with several entrepreneurs, startups, and other new business ventures, as well as large companies.

Some of the technologies I focus on are networks, security, cryptography, operating systems, distributed systems, digital systems, microprocessor systems, Internet applications, robotics, medical devices, and consumer products.
