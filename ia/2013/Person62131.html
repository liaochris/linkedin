Jenny Sligh's Summary

Spaces Interior Design & Lavender Hill Events are both design driven and project/detail management focused businesses...
Whether in my role as an Interior Designer or as a Wedding Planner, I strive to give each client an experience and an end product that is uniquely theirs; reflecting their needs, styles and values.

Wedding Planning:
At Lavender Hill Weddings + Events we specialize in creating a wedding day that is unique and personalized to you. From partnering with you to create the wedding of your dreams to coordinating a flawless event, we pride ourselves in organizing and preparing every detail just the way you want it. In doing so, we save you time, effort and money. All that’s left to do is relax and enjoy a day that is uniquely yours!
Like Lavender Hill Events on Facebook: www.facebook.com/LavenderHillEvents

Interior Design:
Spaces Interior Design is a Madison based full service interior design firm offering creative and thoughtful designs for any room in your home or business. Each project is balanced with beauty, function and personalized style. Spaces Interior Design focuses on developing client relationships based upon trust, an exchange of ideas, and value; honoring each individual’s tastes, time and budget resulting in stylish, classic and timeless interior design.
Like Spaces Interior Design on Facebook: www.facebook.com/WisconsinInteriorDesign
