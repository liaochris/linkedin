Sahila ChangeBringer's Summary

My business provides services in:
1: business development - idea, product & service research, planning, implementation. Start-up or existing business restructuring. Accountability partnering;
2: individual, team, organizational coaching & counseling;
3: marketing consultancy/coaching;
4: advertising, public relations, corporate communications, print management & publishing consultancy;
5: writing, editing, proofing, publishing (print & web) - journalism, copy writing, technical, training materials;
6: presentation & broadcast media - seminars, workshops, keynote addresses, facilitation, moderation, media training, voice overs, narration, commentary, MCing;
7: research, development & delivery of education & training projects;
8: research & policy formulation;
9: project & change management;
10: event management; and
11: fund raising & donor/sponsorship management.


I have more than 20 years' experience to management level in these areas of expertise in New Zealand and Australia. I have worked both as an employee and as owner of a consultancy business.

I usually provide my skills on a contract/project or retainer basis, depending on the needs of the client. Where longer, more substantial projects require, I am open to hiring into an organization as an employee.

Please see my website - www.metamind-creative.com - for a link to my resumes, references and portfolio. Please see www.metamind-spirit.com for information about modalities and techniques I use when I provide personal, team and organizational change services.

Specialties

I use both logic (deductive) and intuitive (inductive) reasoning to 'see the greater picture' and the underlying issue or imbalance in structures or relationships.

I see patterns; how things fit and flow together and where they don't - patterns that block the development and effective functioning of a business, project, product, idea or team within an organisation. When you uncover the pattern, the best solution always presents itself - I see the solutions and know how to implement them.
