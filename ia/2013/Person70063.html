John Surdakowski's Summary

Web designer & developer for both desktop and mobile devices. I believe in crafting digital experiences that balance beauty and utility, on any device that successfully connects brands to their audience.

Hello, I'm John. A New York freelance web designer and owner of Avex Designs, a small web design studio located on Madison Ave in NYC. I've been designing and developing digital experiences for start-ups and advertising agencies since 2002. Since then I've worked on a ton of great projects. Some of my specialties include: websites, blogs, mobile sites, custom WordPress themes, social media solutions, iPhone & iPad apps, branding, UI & UX design and rich media campaigns.

Over the years I've held various positions at some major companies, but in 2010 I decided to go out on my own. During that time I've had the opportunity to work with some awesome brands, including: Google, USANetwork, Cinemax, HBO, UBISoft, Sony Music, Bank of America and Glassnote Records. I have also consulted for companies such as: JWT, Havas, Campfire, Monster.com, Estee Lauder, BBDO, NBC, MRM Worldwide, Nickelodeon, MTV Networks and many other New York based companies.

In 2012 I realized Avex Designs needed some room to grow. So, I decided to set up shop at the WeWork Building located at 261 Madison Ave. in New York. Even though I have a shinny new office, with a kitchen (and a beer keg), I still love to work one-on-one with my clients, only taking on a few select projects at a time. Occasionally, I collaborate with other designers and developers, depending on the size of the project. Avex is small by design, but can quickly scale up to meet your project's needs.
