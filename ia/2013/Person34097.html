Denise Michaels' Summary

I mentor professionals to write how-to and personal growth books. In my six month, small group book mentoring program you start from where you're at - to completing your book. The program also covers dealing with editors, publishing options and how to promote and market your book. For 7.5-years I worked exclusively in the seminar and speaking business as a trainer and coach for NY Times #1 bestselling author, Robert G. Allen. I was mentored personally by another author with six NY Times bestsellers.

My focus is on helping you write a how-to or personal growth book that will position you as an expert, leverage your income and help bring you more clients, speaking opportunities, workshop attendees, etc. It starts by focusing on your niche ideal reader before you write the first word.

"Testosterone-free Marketing" which is my own book did quite well and became a business bestseller. It sold in 15 countries. The success of my book was due to the way the book was written to promise something no book had before - so it has a uniqueness. Also, I built up a platform of fans for the belt, resulting in a tidal wave of buyers when the book was released.

As a result I raised my private fees for marketing mentoring four times in the last four years. I get many opportunities for speaking and promoting my book on radio, TV and through other media. It's all about leverage.

During that time, I was a Marketing Mentor, author, speaker and trainer. I still have several loyal marketing mentoring clients. I mentored over 1,800 women and men to succeed in their small businesses.

Over the last two decades I've worked in advertising, sales, marketing research, strategic planning and public relations. Always marketing, always selling and always helping small business owners on a shoestring budget.

I have a BAA degree from Central Michigan University with two majors in Business-Marketing and Journalism-Advertising.

Specialties

Helping you write a "how-to" or "personal growth" book that will help you position yourself as an expert and leverage your business income. I'm also an editor and a ghost writer.
