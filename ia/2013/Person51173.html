Gytis Repečka's Publications

  • Level-based Algorithm for Rendering Hierarchies in Nested Structure Documents

    • Kaunas University of Technology
    • April 27, 2011
    Authors: Gytis Repečka

    Structured systems, such as web sites, e-shops and various catalogues are organized in hierarchical method which determines sorting and classification of elements according to their relationships with other elements positioned in higher or lower hierarchical level. Implementing hierarchical structure in systems which require minimum database queries and processing time can be a challenging task. This article describes level-based algorithm aiming to achieve those requirements for rendering hierarchies in nested structure documents, such as XML or particular markup language XHTML.
