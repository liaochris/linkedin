Paul Hunt's Experience

Events Manager

Waldorf on London

January 2013 – October 2013 (10 months) Canberra Area, Australia

I researched and co-ordinated event opportunities for the Waldorf on London Restaurant and Bar in Canberra.

We successfully put on three live music, dinner and dance shows in the restaurant space.

I undertook all aspects of this production in partnership with the operations director. Amongst other things, I was involved with the marketing, public relations, ticketing, and accounting of the events.

As part of our marketing plan, I maintained our social media presence and pursued various print and web advertising options. I attempted ways to measure and value the different marketing avenues to see what mix best reached our target audience; it was a trial-and-error operation.

---

Another aspect of my role was to help build the restaurant business by looking at ways to alter the service systems, as well as make adjustments to the overall atmosphere in the space. I was most focussed on improving the dinner service by developing relationships with different organisations to hold regular events in the restaurant. We also maintained a good relationship with the Canberra Theatre Centre.

Paul Hunt's Projects

  • Friday Afternoon Sessions

    • April 2013 to October 2013
    Team Members: Paul Hunt

    We continue to provide a successful live music event every Friday afternoon from 4.30pm until 7.30pm to coincide with our regular Happy Hour (from 3pm until 7pm Monday - Friday).

    We work with local jazz musicians that rotate on a regular basis in duos and trios to perform each week. This provides a relaxed background to those wanting an after-work drink or pre-theatre meal.

    We see the number of regular guests increasing, and a growing number of those discovering the Waldorf on London as an option for a good time to unwind with colleagues.

    The growth of this Friday evening event is due in some part to our other projects of expanding the Waldorf on London's social media presence, and changing the interior design to create a more inviting space to spend time in.

  • Local Business Partnerships

    • May 2013 to October 2013
    Team Members: Paul Hunt

    I looked at opportunities to partner with other tourism and hospitality services in Canberra to better promote the Waldorf on London venue.

    Due to our convenient location to some of Canberra's major cultural, business, and government areas, it is useful to try and better serve these sectors with their food and beverage requirements.

    I developed a positive and mutually beneficial relationship with the Canberra Theatre Centre to provide a complete hospitality experience to their patrons. We offer a generous pre-theatre dinner package to theatre-goers; and look to expand this offering in to the future.

  • Vegas Nights

    • February 2013 to June 2013
    Team Members: Paul Hunt

    The Waldorf on London presented a monthly dinner and dance show in the restaurant space earlier in 2013.

    The experiment in using the Waldorf on London Restaurant and Bar space as a combined food/beverage service venue with live music and performances was a way for us to explore the limits of how guests could be served in a different way than general dinner or function service. It translated well in how we serve those who come to the restaurant before a show at the Canberra Theatre or Convention Centre.

    It was also a way to try different marketing channels to promote the Waldorf on London brand. We certainly noticed an increase in engagement with the website and social media pages, which was a positive outcome.
