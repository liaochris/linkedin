Allison Peacock's Summary

Executive-level business development expert with demonstrated success designing creative strategies to achieve organizational goals. Passionate about wellness, integrative medicine, neuroscience and improving the delivery of cutting-edge patient centered care, I have well-proven abilities in leadership and all forms of business communications, as well as more than a decade of construction and design of commercial spaces.

In addition to maintaining several consulting contracts, I’ve spent the past year repurposing my career on my passion for improving healthcare. I'm especially interested in the clinical implications of the latest neuroscience discoveries on the dynamic nature of the healing process, as well as the effect of physical environment on the healing process.

Activities included targeted research on the healthcare industry and on issues of patient advocacy and health literacy, as well as specific study on topics of personal interest in neuroscience. Highlights include:

• Audited an online course for medical and counseling professionals, Narrative and the Brain, taught by neuropsychiatrist Lewis Mehl-Medrona, MD, PhD.

• Participated in a Harvard Medical School Continuing Education course on leadership through publishing, briefly presented my patient story as a spinal cord injury survivor to an audience of medical professionals

• Studied the work of neurosurgeon Allan Hamilton, MD on the brain function of both people and horses, and how bonds between the equine and human species can facilitate healing.

• Presented on the topic of managing productivity and creativity with mindful awareness at Spark of Genius Bootcamp, held at Texas State University Small Business Development Center

Armed and ready for my next big challenge professionally, I seek to leverage decades of proven abilities in strategic communications, media production and design, leadership, and innovative approaches on behalf of a dynamic organization in the medical or healthcare space.

• Build resilience in times of challenge or transformation
• More easily navigate organizational change
• Create the mindset and conditions to thrive in a fast-paced, increasingly technology-driven world
• Create an inspired family, organization, or workplace that supports true human development
