Jacqueline Rudd's Summary

I suppose it all started when we took the crazy steps of uprooting our growing family from the UK in search of an adventure. We first crossed the pond to the USA where I rekindled my love for sewing but after 5yrs we were missing Europe and returned to settle in a small french village not far from the Mediterranean Sea with all the challenges that go with creating a home and learning a new language.

My teenage family were now ready to leave home to start their own lives and I found there was more time for me. I discovered the beauty of living a simpler life in France and was inspired by the abundance of vintage textiles, lace and collectibles which surrounded me......and so my crafting life was born!

Jacqueline Rudd's Experience

Creating French Lace Curtains & Cafe Curtains using Vintage Textiles and Hand Dyeing

Hatched in France

April 2010 – Present (3 years 7 months) Narbonne, France

I am the owner and curator of Hatched in France, www.hatchedinfrance.com, where we stock vintage lace curtains, coloured and handmade cafe curtains for every room in your house. They haven't quite crossed back over into mainstream décor and to many minds will still be the preserve of nosey neighbours. However, my small french collection manages to dispel any images of suburban snoopers. I source new, previously loved and vintage textiles from here in southern rural France and then create 'one of kind' sets and panels. I am passionate about providing a personal, high quality of service and a professional standard of workmanship.

Browse my ephemera to create your own French inspired gifts and my collectibles to find vintage household items to enhance your home.

Developing an Online Social Craft Community who love to Swap Handmade, Vintage and Craft Supplies

Swap Workshop

August 2010 – Present (3 years 3 months) swapworkshop.com

I am the founder of swapworkshop.com a free online social craft network which allows crafters and hobbyists from all over the world to come together and swap their handmade and vintage items using our unique credit system. They accumulate and earn credits by other members choosing their items and by participating in community events. They then use their credits to swap for any item they wish. Yes anything! No need to agree a trade with a partner, here. Learn the easy steps of how to swap once you've joined us!
