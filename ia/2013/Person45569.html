What we do
‘Francis Vermeulen Growing leadership’ coaches companies and organisations on their way to growth and success. Our coaching and training programmes help people to become more conscious of their thoughts, and act more consciously, which strengthens the necessary links in the chain of success. By stimulating personal development we help people to come to the insights needed to become the best of what they can be.

Our goal
We want to provide our customers and course members with the knowledge and means that will help them to achieve a personal breakthrough in a short period of time. We want to teach everyone we guide or help to believe in the possibilities and power of their own dreams again.
We help our clients and course members to find the right balance which enables them more than ever to find opportunities in all aspects of life.

We serve…
People, companies and organisations who have a passion for growth and success.

We are there for…
Anyone who is looking for added value in life.

Our beliefs and our values
We believe that anyone has the possibility to increase the quality of his life. We believe in the capacities of every person. We believe in continuous growth as a key to success. We believe in proactive and innovative entrepreneurship out of authenticity with respect for everyone involved in the process. We believe selfless service is a basic ingredient for the recipe of success.


The courses ‘Tools for leadership’ , ‘Rivers towards happiness’, ‘Create your business’, ‘Master your mind’, ‘The impact of balance’ help the participant to quickly and efficiently achieve the changes he had in mind. You can also become a member of the ‘Coaching Club’.
In other words, our personal and goal-oriented courses will help you to become the best of what you can be.

    • Shanti Aum
    • December 3, 2009
    Authors: Francis Vermeulen

    In ‘Self-awareness in a tailor-made suit’ author Francis Vermeulen describes how we all receive lessons in our lives, and how the way we react to them will define the difference between what we are and what we can be. A better knowledge of how we
    think and who we are will allow us to open the door to a new and
    more successful future.
    Everyone of us has a source of inner power, a river that can lead us to the sea of success and fulfilment. There are no limits to what we can achieve in our personal and professional life. ‘Self-awareness in a tailor-made suit’ offers a manual with guidelines for people who want to follow their very own river to a well-balanced life bristling with renewed energy.
    Let the experiences of the author inspire you, let the rivers play the foremost role in your personal and professional life. Use this book as a guide, wake up the very best in you and create joy and satisfaction for yourself and the people near and dear to you.
