Gail Keller's Summary

Macaroni Kid JoCo is the only hyper-local family fun site for Johnson County, KS. We are part of the national Macaroni Kid media brand. Our free family resource provides an online calendar of what to do with kids, highlights of upcoming family events, articles, contests, parenting resource guides, online business directory and more.

If your business wants to reach Johnson County moms, we can help! We market online via our website and our social media channels and in person at live events and networking functions throughout JoCo. We have no overhead costs to pass on to our clients. Our advertisers do not need to pay for print and physical distribution.

How have you marketed your business? Is there brand awareness in the community? Have you gotten the ROI for your print advertising, coupons, door hangers and direct mail campaigns? What's worked? What hasn't? Where do you network? How can we help you? Who can I introduce you to?

I ask a lot of questions because I want to get to know you and your business. I am a relationship marketer and my mission is to help your business succeed!

I would love to get your message in front of decision making moms with our affordable and effective online advertising and complimentary monthly reporting. If I cannot help you, I will not advertise your business.
