Derek Caldwell's Summary

I help small & medium businesses succeed online by giving them access to the best internet marketing tools & services in a very simple and effective package based pricing structure via BizSpokes.com. I realized through experience with many businesses that most small & medium business owners know they need some sort of internet & online presence in today's world. The problem is that they don't want to have to become a expert on everything just to make progress. BizSpokes.com solves this issue with our all-in-one pricing packages.

My companies primary focus is in the manufacturing/machine shop industry (due to current & historical success in this area) but I'm open for other niches if needed. My perfect fit is a small to medium CNC Machine Shop or Metal Supplier who is looking to increase their brand exposure and get more clients. I have done this with great success and continue to do so with many of my current clients. However, as mentioned above, I'm open to other industry niches as well.


Although I do own BizSpokes.com it is a business that is operated by other outsourced developers, project managers & internet marketing experts. I have worked diligently to be able to have a successful "side" business (BizSpokes.com) while at the same time continuing to be a effective consultant in the corporate world as well.

In addition to running my business, BizSpokes.com, I have been a consultant concentrating in areas of Business Analysis, Custom Reporting, Business Intelligence Dashboards, Technical Writing, Marketing Research, Prospect Conversion & Custom SaaS Developments.
