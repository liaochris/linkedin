What is the Aboriginal Circle - The Aboriginal Circle is a communications company with a focus on promoting and creating awareness of programs, goods, services, events, careers, news, arts and entertainment opportunities to Aboriginal people throughout Alberta and across Canada.

We work with Aboriginal and mainstream businesses, educational and health institutions, government and non-profit organizations to develop and implement strategies, marketing material and approaches to get their information to diverse Aboriginal citizens and other interested clients. We reach out to Aboriginal people wherever they maybe including on-reserve, urban, rural and northern communities.

Our primary tool to engage our audiences is through our website www.aboriginalcircle.com which is a multi-faceted website that allows us and our clients to communicate directly too many diverse people wherever they have an internet connection. We have worked with our web developers to create a user-friendly website that allows users to quickly find information about our clients and what they are promoting to Aboriginal peoples.

Since launching our website in July 4, 2010 we have had over 1.5 million people visit our website and are attracting roughly 200,000 visitors per month and this is constantly growing. Our clients include small to medium businesses, post-secondary institutions, service delivery agencies and large multi-national corporations. Thousands of people visit our website regularly to get relevant information available to Aboriginal people in a variety of various domains and interest areas. Our current market is Alberta based businesses and organizations and national organizations and businesses that offer services in Alberta.
