Sylvie Daumal's Publications

  • Design d'expérience utilisateur - Principes et méthodes

    • Eyrolles
    • July 12, 2012
    Authors: Sylvie Daumal

    Design d'expérience utilisateur - Principes et méthodes defines the field of user experience and describes its core principles and methods (strategy, user research, ideation, iteration). It is richly illustrated with examples from renowned designers, making it a useful reference on documents and artifacts for novices, decisionmakers and experienced pros. As the first French-language book dedicated to UX Design, its goal is to advance understanding of the practice within the digital industry and beyond, in France.
