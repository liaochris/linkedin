Denice Beggs' Summary

Together with my husband we have been buying and selling Real Estate on Sanibel/ Captiva Islands since 1986. We have owned residential property on both islands also including a commercial property on Captiva from 1991-2002. I have worked for the City of Sanibel in our early years on the island in the Recreation Dept. We have also operated retail businesses on Captiva in the commercial center we owned.

Prior to our move here we were business people in Illinois where we bought and sold Real Estate for personal and investment purposes. We owned and operated residential rentals. We also were Supermarket owners in the Grocery Business in our community.
