Andrew Golaszewski's Summary

I am currently a UX designer at The Nerdery, where I plan and conduct research to uncover optimal user experience design solutions for clients in any industry. I then take the insights derived from that research and produce information architecture and design documentation so projects can be fully and accurately defined for project team members and clients alike. Prior to starting this position, I received a Master's degree in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) at Georgia Tech, with a concentration in Digital Media.

My current goals are to continue honing my interaction and information design skills via user-centered design and research methods, and to design systems that will make information more accessible and comprehensible for people, thereby making their daily lives easier.

I spent the first six years of my career as a copywriter, during which I had the opportunity to represent several national and global brands within an array of industries, and I first discovered the true importance of getting to know your audience.

Specialties

user experience design, interaction design, information visualization, information architecture, content strategy, copywriting
