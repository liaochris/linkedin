Lori Bouchie's Summary

I would like to take the time to introduce myself and let you know a little bit about me…

I am a life long resident of beautiful, seaside Gloucester, MA and a Marion Court College graduate, in Swampscott, MA. After graduating college I wanted to travel throughout the country and began my career as a flight attendant for US AIRWAYS. It was a very exciting job and I enjoyed seeing the country along with meeting many wonderful people along the way, however, I realized I wanted to pursue my other dreams. The dream of becoming a successful Realtor, and raising a family. In between my flying schedule I enrolled in a real estate course at night and began selling real estate while still flying throughout the U.S. Within a short time I made the difficult decision to leave the airline after 10 years of service to commit myself fulltime to my passion of selling real estate.
I now have the excitement of meeting new people while helping them buy the home of their dreams or selling a house in order to make a move!
As a Mom, with two small children, a large extended family, and a thriving business, I realize that FAMILY is very important and time management is KEY! I have over 9 years of experience negotiating the sale or purchase of homes throughout Gloucester, Rockport, Beverly, Ipswich, Peabody, Salem, Newbury, and Haverhill and beyond. I am here to help negotiate on your behalf, as well as coordinate schedules, handle details, and follow up to assure your complete success and satisfaction. If you are interested in working with a realtor who cares about You and Your family’s needs, not the almighty dollar, then I am the Realtor for you! I am successful, because I CARE! I am committed to providing superior service to my clients throughout the Cape Ann area.
Thank you, and I hope to work with you in the future!
