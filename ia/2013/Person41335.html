Emily Worden's Summary

I'm focused, dedicated and obsessed with business. I love to start businesses, advise businesses, read about business, think about business, analyze business decisions ... it's all fun for me. My favorite part about business is focusing on the customer - Who are they? Why do they buy? What motivates them? What are their needs? How can you blow their minds and make them tell all their friends about your business?

I also love marketing and studying topics on communication, persuasion, social media and customer psychology. Organizational behavior is another hot topic, and I can't get enough about leadership.
I'll never stop in the pursuit of my dreams and helping other people achieve success.
