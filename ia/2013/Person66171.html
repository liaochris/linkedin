John Carstens' Summary

I direct creative. This creative sometimes looks like an advertising campaign, sometimes feels like a memorable experience, and sometimes sounds like a Spanglish-speaking dog.

As a creative director and copywriter, I've won new business, awards and the occasional smattering of polite applause.

Should you feel compelled to Google "John Carstens" (as I do often), this John Carstens is not to be confused with the Los Angeles-based folk singer or Wharton professor who moonlights as a gimp.

Specialties

Writing ads featuring dusty vehicles, talking animals and agitated businesspeople. Leading cross-discipline teams to blow brand campaigns into millions of connected pieces. Endowing technology with human purpose, while saying things like “mobile penetration” with a straight face.

Started in LA as co-ECD of the West Coast, then moved back to Chicago to help build this office from a UX and research hub into a multi-channel creative juggernaut. My SapientNitro story is about bringing brand storytelling to technology and business strategy, to make all of it more human, meaningful and ridiculously awesome.

It's a simple plan: Lead the design vision for a more inspiring new office. Recruit the city's best digital talent. Foster a culture where people combine their diverse superpowers for good rather than evil. Voila! Work gets better, market takes notice, world feels incrementally more dominated.
