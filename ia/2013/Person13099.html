Ben Jen's Summary

Ben Jen obtained his Bachelor’s of Science in Business Management from Bay State College in Boston, MA, and is actively pursuing his Executive Masters in Business Administration (EMBA) from Northeastern University in Boston, MA. Mr. Jen also holds industry certifications including Microsoft's Certified Desktop Support Technician (MCDST), Certified Systems Engineer (MCSE), CompTIA's A+, Security+, Dell's Certified Systems Engineer (DCSE), Faronics' Certified Sales Professional (FCSP), VMware's Certified Professional (VCP), VMware’s Certified Sales Professional (VSP), Cisco's Certified Entry Network Technician (CCENT), and the EMT Basic (EMT-B). Mr. Jen has volunteer experience in several charity walks in Boston, assisting with the development of a Chamber of Commerce within Bedminster, NJ, and various volunteer positions at hospitals within Central New Jersey. Mr. Jen has over 9-years of experience as an entrepreneur and business owner with the founding of Ben Jen Online, LLC, in 2003 and several other ventures in the telecommunications, technology consulting, debt collections, enterprise software application, healthcare, and cyber security industries.

Specialties: business management, IT administration, IT consulting, assisting with business startups, startup management, enterprise sales
