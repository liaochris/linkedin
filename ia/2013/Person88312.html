John Masson's Summary

I have a high level of experience working with creative and marketing professionals. My experience across multiple industries gives me the edge when partnering with organizational and corporate marketing teams on design, marketing and strategic communications projects. I also work with individuals and small businesses by helping to equip them with a visual brand image in the marketplace.

I maintain great working relationships with my clients, as well as the craftspeople who produce the projects that I've worked hard to design. My various design positions have often involved print procurement and I have had the good fortune to partner with some great companies in the Lawrence/Kansas City area.

Specialties: I create and direct original design concepts. Skilled in hunting and gathering logistical information as well as collaborating with clients and creative teams in order to ensure project success.

Portfolio: logo design, marketing collateral, catalog design, direct mail, annual report design, photo illustration, graphic illustration, sign systems, POS display, special event invitations, ad concept & design, banner design, Flash ads and web content, art and photo direction.
