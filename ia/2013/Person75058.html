Kevin McNoldy's Summary

Kevin McNoldy has been obsessed with music from birth, which led first to his education at Berklee College of Music, then to his career as an independent music producer, recording engineer, mixing engineer, and mastering engineer, with multi-platinum record sales and chart success in a wide variety of genres (rock, pop, dance, electronic, folk, and bluegrass).

Over his twenty year career, Kevin has built, owned, and operated major recording studios in Nashville, Los Angeles, Florida, and Virginia. His current studio in North Carolina, Cphonic, is a mixing and mastering suite designed as a complete online service for music projects from independent artists and record labels.

Kevin is also an accomplished songwriter and multi-instrumentalist, with hundreds of songwriting and performance credits on albums he's produced, jingles, films, and TV.

As a lifelong collector of vintage and modern recording equipment, Kevin has handled pro audio equipment sales, implementation, and studio design for high-end recording studio clients. He has run one of the most successful auction services for studio sales and liquidations. And, with his extensive experience in building and running studios, he is in demand as a consultant for recording studio management and organizational teams.

Recently, he's added "record label and music publishing company owner" to his resume, as well as web design, online marketing services, and SEO for recording artists and pro audio equipment / software companies. But no matter what the title, it all leads back to Kevin's first love: music.
