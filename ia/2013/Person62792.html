Jerry Sullivan's Summary

I specialize in residential and commercial real estate on the Emerald Coast of Northwest Florida ( DESTIN ). I became active in Real Estate in 1988, after completing Real Estate Sales I, II, III in Atlanta, I began buying and selling, managing my own property. Throughout the years I have been involved with Design, Construction, Renovation, Restoration, Sales and Leasing of Residential and Commercial Property. I became a licensed Real Estate Agent in the state of Florida in 2009 when I moved to Destin full time.
