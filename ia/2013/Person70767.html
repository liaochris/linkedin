Julie Smith's Summary

Julie Smith is a trained Professional Life Coach and Leadership Coach. Through coaching she partners with individuals and groups to help them build their vision and reach their goals with Clarity, Focus, and Action.

Her clients include purpose-driven professionals, entrepreneurs, active and emerging leaders in both organization and ministry.

Julie’s true passion lies in helping others to stretch and grow personally, professionally, and as leaders.
An experienced marketplace leader, Julie brings excellence, integrity, and purpose to everything that she does.

For more than twelve years Julie worked in the corporate world in Human Resources management, and training and development. She’s worked on the teams of numerous multi-million-dollar business coaches and speakers, leading teams and managing marketing efforts. She’s devoted more than ten years as an entrepreneur and business owner. Most recently, she’s worked with entrepreneurs and nonprofits in marketing consulting and coaching, as well as web design. Julie also serves on staff at Professional Christian Coaching Institute as marketing director.

Having established and grown her own successful business for more than ten years, and working closely with entrepreneurs from start-up to million-dollar growth, Julie understands their challenges and opportunities. She understands the importance of vision, of purpose, and of passion in order to conceive, build, and grow a sustainable business, movement or initiative.

Specialties: coaching, leadership, leadership development, personal development, marketing, Internet marketing, Internet marketing strategies, online marketing strategy, marketing your business, small business marketing, social media strategy, web design, WordPress, social media branding, entrepreneurship, building a list of potential clients, generating website traffic, consulting, teleseminar planning, developing information products, online product launches, group coaching and membership programs
