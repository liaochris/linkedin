Joy Silha's Summary

I have been a professional qualitative researcher and strategic planner for 20+ years. I am an experienced explorer and an advocate for people, facilitating focus groups, in-depth interviews,conducting ethnographic research and online research. I help companies understand the real lives of people in order to create better products, services and marketing strategies.

I believe that qualitative research is more art than science! This research can challenge common thinking and provide creative solutions, but it is a tool and cannot be a substitute for intuitive understanding and inventiveness. I am a very curious person and I like to "dig deep". I provide a space where people are brought into an experience and can express themselves in an authentic way.

I live in Santa Fe, the City Different, after many years in LA and NY. I approach my work with sensitivity, creativity and tons of energy!

I also bring my skills in facilitation, leadership, my laser-like listening and strategic insight to help Women, Entrepreneurs and Executives step into a leadership role in their life and work. In my Equine Experience, I help people focus, communicate and lead more effectively. In these Leadership Workshops, I use Horses as co-facilitators. This is called Equine Assisted Learning.

I am an accomplished horsewoman, having owned horses for over 20 years and studied Natural Horsemanship with Ray Hunt. I am so excited about training my new mare, with Leslie Hammel Turk's guidance, who is a protege of Ray's.
