Allen Mireles' Summary

I am a strategist with an affinity for technology. I live at the intersection of social media and traditional marketing and public relations.

  I am wordsmith, talker, listener and thinker. I am a member of the inaugural Dell Customer Advisory Panel and have been named "One of the Top 25 Very Intelligent Businesswomen to Follow on Twitter" and called one of the “20 Social Media Divas You Should Be Following”. I’m passionate about how social media tools are being used to help reach business and organizational objectives...and how traditional marketing and public relations are evolving to embrace new technology.

My professional background includes agency work in advertising and public relations and on-camera work in television and on-air work in radio. I am experienced fundraiser and have directed major NW Ohio fundraising initiatives for non-profits and educational institutions.

I am a proficient trainer and presenter and have spoken at numerous conferences including the first Exploring Social Media Business Summit with Jason Falls, The NW Ohio Prevention Conference, the Ohio Trucking Association’s Annual Conference, Social Media Breakfast Toledo, PRSA, PRSSA, University of Toledo, Bowling Green State University and Lourdes College.

  I combine my years of experience developing marketing strategies with my in depth understanding of social media and deliver comprehensive solutions to my clients. If you are interested in learning more about how I can help you with strategy, program implementation, training or public speaking, send me an email at info@allenmireles.com or give me a call at 419.740.1262.
