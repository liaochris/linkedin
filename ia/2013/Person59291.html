Jason Kilp's Summary

Jason Kilp, Principal and Creative Director of Bold Array LLC, a Southern California interactive agency. Bold Array gets to the very root of their clients' needs, utilizing the appropriate design and technologies to procure a solution that is both visually striking and strategic in its execution. Their focus is on desktop and mobile website design, web application development, and enterprise integration solutions.

Being a web and graphic designer with over a decade of experience, Jason has helped to produce nationally and internationally recognized work for Goodwill of Orange County, Lazy Dog Cafe, Envelopments, the Simms Restaurant Group, and a number of others.

Specialties

Web Design and Development, Mobile Web Optimization, Brand Identity Creation, Search Engine Optimization, and Online Marketing.
