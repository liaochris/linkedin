Mark Cruth, PMP, CSM's Summary

I am a savvy, insightful, technical, and energetic Project Manager passionate for developing programs and projects in the Manufacturing and eCommerce industries. I am well-versed with coordinating work across multiple teams in order to exceed leadership expectations. I pride myself with having the business acumen and technical expertise necessary to easily transition between leading requirement discussions with Product Managers, to having technical design conversations with Senior Software Architects. I have built my career around understanding complex problems, developing creative solutions, turning risks into opportunities, and creating a work environment where people feel valued for their contributions.

My Core Competencies include...

Effective Communicator: I can easily translate technical issues into language that can be understood by both technical and non-technical people.

Excellent Collaborator: Reaching across the table to bring Product Owners and Development Teams together to solve a problem is one of my specialties.

Customer Oriented: My passion for improving the user experience of products drives me to ensure that customer satisfaction is a key performance metric on all products I support.

Team Builder: I have been recognized on multiple occasions for devising new methods that increase team productivity and morale in a fast, ever changing environment.

Technically Savvy: I strive to learn and understand the technology used by all products I support in order to identify better solutions to technical problems.
