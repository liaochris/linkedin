David Mazeau's Summary

I'm a compositor artist in motion pictures and tv.
My mission is to integrate live-action, 3D and digital effects elements between them to create the final shot.

My main production experience is with full 3D movie and tv animation, although I touched advertising.
My experience gave me a deep understanding of photography and film, and how interactions of light, lenses and film emulsions make up the image.

Large compositing-wise, I'm also confortable in node based and other compositing systems

My own is getting a huge experience in live-action Visuals FX.

Specialties:
compositing systems (Digital Fusion, Shake, After Effects...)
editing systems (AVID, Premiere, Vegas...)
tracking & rotoscopy systems (PfTrack, PFMatchit, Silhouette, Mocha, Syntheye, Matchmove, Camera Tracker, VooCat)
3D softwares (Softimage, Lightwave, 3D's Max, MODO.)
3D render (Arnold, Maxwell, Mental Ray, FPrime)
