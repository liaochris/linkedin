Jonathan Pollinger's Summary

I help businesses with social media in the following areas:

+ Social media marketing
+ Business development
+ Customer services
+ Employee engagement

I'm based in the Gloucestershire town of Cheltenham although I have clients as far afield as Perth, Australia, yet to visit! and Croatia which I visited earlier this year.

I deliver advice in a variety of ways including:

+ One-to-one social media consultancy
+ Workshops: Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Google+, Pinterest
+ Bespoke in-house social media workshops
+ Documented social media strategies
+ Professional social media speaking

Give me a call on 01242 639023 or email me at jonathan.pollinger@intranetfuture.com or You can find out more about on my website at http://www.intranetfuture.com where you can also sign up for my free fortnightly social media newsletter.

I run events in Cheltenham that bring people together for socialising and co-working. On the last Tuesday each month you can enjoy social dining at wearediningclub. On the last Wednesday of the month at wearesocialclub you can enjoy a short talk on social media and face-to-face networking. Every Friday morning at Coffee and Co you can co-work and network.

I also publish JPFestival.com, which provides previews, analysis, tips and reviews on Jump Racing.

Specialties: Social media consultancy, social media strategy, social media workshops, social media training, social media measurement, social media monitoring, Facebook consultancy, Twitter consultancy, Twitter training, Foursquare consultancy, LinkedIn training, social video consultancy, Google+ training, Pinterest consultancy, YouTube, Instagram and Vine consultancy, events, horse racing website.
