Diane Hudson Burns' Summary

Career Change Management Coach & Trainer with a strong record of writing career change, military conversion, and Federal government résumés with successful results for career transition and position placement—empowering clients with tools to navigate the employment market. Quoted as a career expert in the Career Builder section of major national newspapers including the Baltimore Sun and Los Angeles Times, and the Daily Dayton News on varied career topics.

Consultant, career coach, and résumé writer for the Resume Place (www.resume.place.com) a full-service federal resume and KSA writing and coaching service and outplacement company. Specialize in senior level compositions for Senior Executive Service clients as well as GS-12 to GS-15 clients.

Nationally published author, international conference speaker, and train the trainer – respected as one of the industry’s leading career coaches, two-time award-winning professional résumé writer, and an authority for Military Transitions and Federal Résumé & Application Procedures.

Lived and traveled abroad for 12 years; keynote speaker spoke to varied military audiences (in Germany, the Netherlands, USA, and Japan) regarding careers and motivational topics.

Specialties: Resumes and Career Coaching for Executive-level Subject Matter Experts in science, business, engineering, IT, military, politics, intelligence, logistics, security, medicine, academia, and others. Maintain a high level of confidentiality. Speak at women's retreats, job fairs, outplacement, military training, and others.
