Cheryl Dooley's Summary

Professional real estate designations I hold include GRI, CRS, CRB, ABR, member of ERC. I'm also a member of the Minnesoata Association of Realtors and the Regional Multiple Listing Service of Minnesota. My real estate career began when I was 19 years old. In 1985 I started Hometown Realty with one other agent. My goal when I started the company was to have a few good agents that did a real good job representing buyers and sellers. My strength is in helping buyers and sellers reach their goals when buying and selling real estate. Our company now has 7 agents, 2 licensed staff, 2 part time staff. Our efforts are concentrated on what we can do to best serve our clients and customers. Our goal has always been to be the most respected real estate company in Hutchinson. I've served as past president of the Hutchinson Chamber of Commerce,been a former recipient of "Business Person of the Year", and currently enjoy giving back to our community through the BGB's (Babe's Giving Back) a group of women who do fund raising events.

Specialties

Years of experience bringing buyers and sellers together in all kinds of markets, negotiating skills, marketing skills with proven results, many years of real estate knowledge in Hutchinson and the surrounding area.
