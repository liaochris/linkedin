Genevieve Bos' Summary

Genevieve is a successful technology entrepreneur, technology board member, professional speaker and innovation thought leader. She is the co-founder and CEO of IdeaString™ - a SaaS platform that delivers the world’s first socialized innovation and collaboration platform for the enterprise. IdeaString empowers businesses by turning their most powerful assets - employees, customers, vendors and alliances into innovators to deliver high-value breakthrough ideas. We enable intelligent, mass scale digital curation of knowledge, insights, ideas and experience to transform and optimize business units, support product development, drive strategy and simplify an organization across the enterprise. Customers are mainly F1000 companies.

Genevieve serves as an Advisory Board member for a group of highly innovative technology companies - BLiNQ media, TripLingo, MLC media and DueProps supporting their strategy, business development and customer acquisition strategies.

She was also Co-Founder and Publisher of PINK magazine - launched in June 2005. Editorial is "Fortune meets Oprah". Successfully sold the company five years later. Over 15 years in the software and technology industry as a co-founder or senior leader. One of the founders of ZapMedia and InfoGraphix, both Atlanta-based technology companies. Director of PCS mid market running two business units at Radiant Systems(sold to NCR).

On the Atlanta EO board - eonetwork.org; WITI board Atlanta - Women in Technology International, BDN - Board of Directors Network - whose goal is to get more women on public company boards;

Genevieve’s insights are based on a multi-national career that spans over a decade delivering results in such diverse cultures as the US, Brazil, across Europe, the Middle East, South Africa and Korea. Genevieve’s mission in all her business endeavors is to help companies elevate their performance and effectiveness while delivering bottom-line results in an ever-increasing global world.
