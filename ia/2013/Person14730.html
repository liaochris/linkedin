The Coalition for Innovative Development, Education, and Action (IDEAco) is an education- and development-focused nonprofit organization working to create and inspire new ways of learning and building community around the world.

Through educational programs, workshops, and experiences that apply design thinking to social good, we encourage people to make a positive impact in their communities and lives while creating solutions to our society’s key challenges and opportunities.

IDEAco also operates “flagship” education and community initiatives which are designed to be demonstrations of what the organization’s methods can create, and are laboratories that continually test, improve, and expand upon the concepts central to IDEAco’s work.
