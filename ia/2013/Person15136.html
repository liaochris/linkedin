Brandon Melchior's Summary

Portfolio: www.suburbian.net

Brandon is the User Experience Manager for R&D Ventures at The New York Times Company, leading the UX/UI design for Ricochet, a content marketing platform. Brandon is the Product Owner, managing a team of front-end developers in an agile environment. He designs marketing materials, sales presentations and attend sales meetings.

Prior to working with R&D Ventures, Brandon was Creative Director in Marketing and AdLab, an ad innovation group, at the NYTimes. He has a diverse design background, which includes designing consumer-facing products for web, mobile and TV, plus future interactive media concept demos and broadcast animation packages.

Ricochet (http://bit.ly/JasISy) is a self-service platform that lets Brands attach their display advertising to a specific article, from which Ricochet generates a unique URL that can be shared through the Brand's earned and owned media channels — wherever the Ricochet link goes, the brand's ads will follow. It's highly-targeted, socially-driven content marketing.

Specialties: Product design and strategy, UI design, UX design, social media strategy and analytics, Art Direction, web, mobile, IPTV/set-top box, multi-platform interactions
