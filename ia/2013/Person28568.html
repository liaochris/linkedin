Cynthia Popper's Summary

I'm a seasoned copywriter and editor with nearly a decade of professional writing experience.
Over the years I've written for television, radio, retail, manufacturing, and technology. Article and social media work includes clients in the travel, food & drink, beauty, and lifestyle industries.

Whether I'm working with great creative teams or a solo business flier, seeing the full picture with the right words is what I'm about.

I love clear, declarative messaging.

When cute works, it really works. When it doesn't, it's not cute.

I believe in text pruning, early and often.

I'm a grammar snob... and I'm okay with that.

Perfect grammar isn't everything and doesn't belong everywhere if it interrupts the message.

Knowing the audience is everything.
