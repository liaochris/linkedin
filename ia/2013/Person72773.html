Katherine Randall's Summary

Katalyst Public Relations is a full service public relations and social media agency that works with clients in the lifestyle, hospitality, new media and technology space.

We create customized campaigns and implement them in a creative and strategic way. Our tactics include a well-balanced mix of public relations, social media, digital publicity, blogger relations, event planning and promotion, community relations, marketing and reputation management.

We are buzz-makers and social communicators that specialize in what we love: new media and tech, restaurants and hotels, food and wine, travel and leisure, design and home decor, fitness and beauty, and accessories and retail.

Katalyst PR was established upon three driving principles: personal service, value and RESULTS. As a boutique-sized communications agency, Katalyst is able to deliver big-agency results within any budget, and guarantees that clients receive hands-on attention from an experienced PR professional every step of the way.

Simply put our goal is to reach the right audience with the right story…your story!

Specialties

public relations, publicity, social media strategies, marketing, event planning, event promotion, event marketing, blogger relations, online publicity, digital public relations.
