James "Jim" Cheatham's Summary

I have over twenty-five years of experience in both the public and private sector. Ten years where spent with a Fortune 500 company handling continuity planning, risk management, emergency and safety programs, loss prevention, and management training. The rest has been spent in Public Safety where I have had a broad ranging career that has taken me in leadership roles to Federally declared disasters in several States as well as a declared National Special Security Event. I have earned certifications in areas of emergency management, hazardous materials, homeland security, public safety, and am a national certified instructor. I have had the opportunity to be an author and or contributor to emergency operations plans for city, county, and business organizations as well as risk assessment plans for designated homeland security sites. I have experience in analyzing individuals, organizations, and objectives, developing strategies, and implementing these for successful outcomes.

Specialties: Consulting, Organizational Development, Strategic Planning, Hazard Analysis, Research, Grants,
Policy, Curriculum, Training, and Education
