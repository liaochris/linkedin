Bruce Matez's Summary

Statistics show that over 98% of all divorces in New Jersey settle before trial. Bruce’s focus and goal is to help his clients reach fair and equitable settlments, and avoid the anxiey, stress and financial burden of litigation through the court. He takes the time to educate his clients on the law, to explain the range of possible resolutions of issues, the likely best and worst case scenarios, and works toward an amicable resolution of the issues based upon his client’s informed, rational and reasonable decision as well as best interests. Bruce strives toward striking a healthy balance between effective negotiation and strong advocacy. When necessary and appropriate, he uses his knowledge, experience and strong advocacy skills in litigation.

Having practiced family law for over 24 years, and being a divorced father himself, Bruce understands the emotional dynamics of divorce and all family law issues, and has a keen awareness of the interplay between the emotional, legal, and financial issues in divorce and other family law disputes. His ability to effectively communicate with his clients, empathize with them, listen to them,provide personal service to them, and represent their interests with knowlegde and skill, has earned him the respect and admiration of his colleagues and clients, as well as their gratitude.

Bruce also serves as a mediator for parties seeking to resolve their family law issues in a neutral, non-adversarial, and non-litigious setting, thereby avoiding litigation. He is now practicing Collaborative Divorce as well; another alternative to litigation. He has been chosen by colleagues and appointed by local judges to serve as a mediator, binding arbitrator, parenting coordinator in order to help them resolve various family law issues.
