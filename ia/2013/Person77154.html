Kristin Jensen's Summary

Hi, I'm Kristin Jensen. I am currently an August 2014 candidate for an MFA Creative Writing degree in Creative Non-Fiction. I am also a Class of 2013 student-athlete graduate of Northwestern University's Medill School of Journalism, where I completed my Bachelor of Journalism degree with a 3.5/4.0 GPA.

As a student-athlete, I earned NFCA All-America Scholar-Athlete and Academic All-Big Ten honors as the starting centerfielder on the Northwestern Wildcats Women’s Softball team. For the past four years, I have been a member of Northwestern University Athletes in Action where I plan and lead weekly meetings for an on-campus group of Christian student-athletes.

Samples of my published work can be viewed at http://www.kristinscharkey.com and I can be contacted at jensenkristinlee@gmail.com.

Specialties: Writing, Reporting, Editing, Video Production, Audio Production, Social Media, FinalCut Pro, Audacity, Soundslides Plus, Microsoft Office, Spanish Language
