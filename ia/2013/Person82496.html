Lori Jo Vest's Summary

People fascinate me. Happy people, loud people, creative people, smart people, even angry people...any kind of people. I love them. Every single one.

I've spent my 20+ year career engaging people and creating strong relationships with customers. As a business-to-business sales rep, and later as managing director of a teleproduction studio for 10+ years, my biggest joy has come from the relationships I've built with customers.

I've written a book (with my lovely co-author Marilyn Suttle) about how to connect with customers so that they become loyal fans. It's titled: "Who's Your Gladys? How to Turn Even the Most Difficult Customer into Your Biggest Fan" and it's currently in print in both English and Chinese.

I've parlayed these interests and skills into customer service and sales speaking and consulting. And since I'm a social media fanatic, I also have a gig as a writer of social content for an advertising agency.

Need help getting customers to keep coming back? Getting more customers? How about engaging customers on social channels? I would be thrilled to help!
