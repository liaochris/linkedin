Mark Boroyan's Summary

-Now taking freelance clients so feel free to contact me with inquiries-

I am a cross-disciplined experience and communication designer. Regardless of role, I perform as a hybrid strategist, designer, researcher, and creative director. I have an extensive and diverse background in studio fine art, graphic and interaction design, film and television production, audio and visual media, and honest manual labor. I understand how things work, and I aspire to create content and products that will enhance peoples lives.

Over the years I have been a different person to different people: a Guide at the Grand Canyon, a Photographer, a Stone Mason, a Landscaper, a Graphic Designer, a Carpenter, a Set Builder, a Director, a Videographer, a Film Producer, a Camera Operator, a Video Editor, a Sculptor, a Printmaker, a Beer Slinger, a Boom Operator, an Interaction Designer, and more.

My experiences have assembled me into a strategic and creative thinker. I excel in collaborative environments with beautifully insane individuals.


Specialties: X-Ray vision ,heuristics, user research, experience design, strategy, creative direction, product strategy, interaction design, ethnography, advertising
