Mark Berndt's Summary

Mark Berndt is a commercial and fine art photographer, director and teacher. His environmental portraiture, documentary photography and films celebrate people where they live and work. Currently teaching photography at the Kansas City Art Institute, the Julia Dean Photo Workshops and in private consultation, he is also a former instructor at Art Center College of Design in Pasadena, CA.
