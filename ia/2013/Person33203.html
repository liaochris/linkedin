Dean Allen's Summary

I am a writer located in Greenville, South Carolina. My last book, RATTLESNAKE REVOLUTION, is a lively history of the Tea Party movement. I am currently working on two more non-fiction books, GODS, ALIENS, OR ATLANTIS?, and SUICIDE OF THE REPUBLICAN PARTY.

I have worked with small business owners, the self employed, and high net worth individuals. I have designed innovative strategies to reduce taxation while still being in full compliance with the law. I have also provided structures, strategies and services related to asset protection.

Specialties: Since 2007 I have managed numerous political campaigns, almost always for challengers in Republican primaries.The one exception was my efforts in the re-election campaign of state Senator David Thomas.

I have also worked as a free lance writer for business communications and non-profit organizations, including several Tea Parties.

My final specialty is thirty years experience, assisting clients in the use of international business structures and financial institutions. I have excellent connections in six countries. I am particularly well connected in the Commonwealth of Dominica where I have extensive contacts in government, banking and business.
