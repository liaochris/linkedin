Emily Brennan's Summary

I’m Emily Brennan and I work with new media.

With over five years of experience working professionally in communications, I've successfully boosted the social media and web presence of multiple Ivy League institutions, produced a radio show, coordinated a wide variety of events, and consulted on web strategy and graphic design.

Recently, I completed an interdisciplinary Masters focused on new media, community life, copyright, and cinema studies. Through my education, I’ve found that the knowledge of differing mediums for narratives and storytelling enhances all forms of communication in a community.

  • #PennLaw2016 Instagram Scavenger Hunt

    • August 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Emily Brennan

    To roll out the new Instagram account and encourage social media sharing among 1Ls, I planned a social media scavenger hunt along with orientation leaders. Students had to tag their photos with #PennLaw2016, team name, and clue depicted. I built out a RebelMouse page to pull all the photos together in real time, which allowed for easier scoring and other teams to see where students were exploring campus.

  • #PennLaw13 Graduation Campaign

    • April 2013 to May 2013
    Team Members: Emily Brennan

    To promote the #PennLaw13 class hashtag for graduation, I stopped by their field day celebration to ask students what their favorite Penn Law memory was. The result was increased levels of engagement on Facebook, promoted awareness of the class hashtag for graduation day, and the most popular photo album to date.

    After graduation day, I compiled all the relevant social media posts into Storify to showcase the many facets and emotions of graduation day. Of a class comprised of approximately 200 students, the story received 523 clicks within the first week of going live. Students reached out to say how nice it was to be able to look back on the day.
