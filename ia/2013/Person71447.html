John McCabe, IDSA's Summary

I am a mixture of interesting experiences collected from many years of effectively leading client-sponsored higher-education design studios, understanding and applying adaptive design-build methods and practices, and strategically researching and measuring the social impact of emerging technologies. I have combined many of these passions to design products and educate students that make a positive difference on a global level.

  In addition to leading multiple high-profile design studios per term, being the SCAD IDSA Faculty Advisor, and the Coordinator of Marine Design, I still find the time to serve as the Executive Director of Fulcrum Collaborative, a strategy and product design firm based out of Savannah, GA.

Specialties

disrupting, confusing, searching, finding, solving, smiling

John McCabe, IDSA's Projects

  • AT&T: Product Ecosystem and Brand Development (Sponsored Project @ SCAD)

    • 2012 to Present
    Team Members: John McCabe, IDSA

    The multidisciplinary team of students worked with AT&T and a NY Design firm, Concept101, on a collaboration to research a selected demographic, create appropriate brand attributes, and design product ecosystems around social and technological trends. The final full scale models were on on display at the AT&T booth at the 2013 International Consumer Electronics Show in Las Vegas.

  • Freescale Semiconductors: Home Hub (Sponsored Project @ SCAD)

    • 2011 to Present
    Team Members: John McCabe, IDSA

    My students worked in collaboration with Freescale Semiconductors and Technology Analytics. They began the term by researching demographics, technology trends, social trends, and industry monopolies. The students created cultural probes that were used locate and collect the deepest details of daily activities. This information was affinitized and used to influence the design of lifestyle-enhancing, communication portals for the home.

  • Playpower: Aqua Recreation (Sponsored Project @ SCAD)

    • 2007 to Present
    Team Members: John McCabe, IDSA

    Playpower is the world’s largest, fully integrated manufacturer of playground and recreational equipment. They asked my students to work with one of their sister companies, Aqua Recreation, to produce concepts for the next generation of modular multi-level play structures. The studio examined current and future trends in water based recreational parks. Throughout this complete process the students were in constant contact with designers, marketing, and engineers from Aqua Recreation. Many of the student’s work were of such high quality that Playpower provided them with internship positions.

  • Gulfstream Aerospace: Main Cabin Seating (Sponsored Project @ SCAD)

    • 2007 to Present
    Team Members: John McCabe, IDSA

    The Gulfstream Aerospace Corporation worked with my students to reevaluate what the appropriate mixture of luxury, technology, materials, form and anthropometrics would be needed to effectively redesign the main cabin seating of the G650 prior to it's announcement. The students worked together in groups to create full scale examples of the designs and present them to Gulfstream.

  • deciBel: AT&T sponsored project

    • September 2012 to Present
    Team Members: John McCabe, IDSA, Zachary Holcomb, Jeremy Dennis, Dean Loock, Ryan Holloway, Jacob Fine, Alessandra Corona, Sebastian Garcia

    Our design team was given 10 weeks to essentially reinvent AT&T's image perceived by the young professional demographic (18-24) by designing a family of 'lifestyle' compatible products that were loud, exciting and had a whole lot of personality!
