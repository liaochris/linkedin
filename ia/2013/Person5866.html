Stefano Marrone's Summary

I am an Italian globetrotter, currently based in London, UK/
I love to tell stories visually, no matter what medium I am using/
I strongly believe in being flexible and am open to the new challenges and potential that new technologies can offer/
without forgetting the good old pencil and strong personal communication skills/

BIO
After attending NABA in Milan, graduating with a BA in Media Design and Multimedia Arts, I worked as an event photographer, motion graphics artist and video editor for various advertising and event agencies. This was in conjunction with private clients in Italy and North America.
I graduated from the Classical Animation course at Vancouver Film School and got a Goldsmiths MA in Creative and Cultural Entrepreneurship in London. Currently I run a small animation studio -Nucco Brain- in Shoreditch,London working in the motion graphics, visual development and animation field.

Specialties: classical animation, cutout animation, flash animation, motion graphics, storyboarding, illustration, visual storytelling, compositing, management of creative teams, character and background design, visual development, graphic facilitation.
