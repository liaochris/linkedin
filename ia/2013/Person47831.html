George Aitken's Summary

I have experience with all aspects of estate and business succession planning, and estate and trust administration. I provide counsel in matters concerning federal and state income taxation and tax-exempt organizations. My practice spans all aspects of the transmission and preservation of family wealth for business owners, executives, professionals and persons with complex planning matters. I have assisted many clients with integrating their personal estate plans with their business succession plans to address each client’s unique concerns, which often includes their philanthropic objectives.

My experience in matters concerning federal and state income taxation encompasses the taxation of trusts and estates, individuals, partnerships, limited liability companies and corporations as well as representing clients before federal and local taxing authorities in tax disputes.

I also counsel tax-exempt organizations with complex issues of federal tax law and provide counsel with all phases of charitable planning in which I represent charities as well as individual donors.

With regard to estate and trust administration, I have personal experience serving as trustee of trust funds and as personal representative (executor) of decedents' estates. In addition, I counsel individuals and institutional clients with estate and trust administration matters and assist with the estate, gift and income taxation issues that are frequently encountered. I assist clients with disputes regarding estate and trust administration, will contests, trust reformations and other fiduciary matters.

Specialties: I limit my practice to wills, trusts, estates, probate, closely-held businesses & taxation.
