Jon Boley's Summary

I am passionate about improving audio quality. I enjoy using my understanding of auditory perception (and the underlying neurophysiology) to create new audio algorithms. Through a process I like to call "perception based optimization," these algorithms can improve audio quality across a variety of adverse conditions... often while simultaneously reducing computational requirements.

Specialties

• Psychoacoustics (experiments & models)
• Auditory Neurophysiology
• Algorithm R&D
