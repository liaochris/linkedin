John Masnica's Summary

I help businesses and their owners become more profitable by providing legal and consulting services, with an emphasis in business and real estate transactions. Specifically, I assist them with matters involving business sales, asset protection, corporation and LLC formation, employment issues, real estate transactions, contracts, independent contractor, vendor and service agreements, business planning, structuring, formation and dissolution, licensing, strategic management, intellectual property, succession planning and civil litigation.

I also help families feel more secure and plan for their future with wills, trusts and other forms of estate planning.

I am a San Diego native and I now reside in Mission Valley with my wife and son. I grew up in Escondido, where I attended Orange Glen High School. I graduated from the University of San Diego with a degree in business administration and finance and received my law degree with honors from Thomas Jefferson School of Law.
