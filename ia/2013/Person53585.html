Honey Crenshaw's Summary

Honey came to real estate in search of an entrepreneurial challenge. Now in her 13th year as a REALTOR she is: Experienced, Creative, Resourceful, Knowledgeable about current market trends, and Committed to clients’ best interests.
Prior to becoming a successful real estate professional, Honey was a Hotel Operations Manager. Her passion for Interior Design leads her directly to a career in Real Estate. She can help her buyers see each "house" as a home. Many areas of expertise allows her to offer clients, whether buyers or sellers, an entire package of services. The sales experience, coupled with extensive real estate knowledge, ensures your real estate needs will be carried out professionally, efficiently and personally.
Honey takes her Real Estate Consultant role seriously. Educating clients’ as they sort through their goals and needs in buying or selling property. Always eager to explain every step of the process and consult with each client on how current market conditions affect them.
Making it a point to be aware of financial situations and priorities of both buyers and sellers. This enables her to develop a plan for individuals based on needs. In-depth knowledge, insight, and understanding of Louisville and it's surrounding areas will provide you with a strong foundation from which to begin the process of buying, selling, or investing.
Having always had an instinct for understanding the special needs of buyers and sellers. An expert at building relationships and using the latest technology to serve clients, Her production has consistently grown each year. With focused customer service to each member of her growing pool of clients and referrals.
What makes Honey different is her business background and she knows how to put a transaction together no matter how complicated it may be. Having a very strong work ethic and being available for her clients 24/7 puts her clients ahead of the competition.
