Jerry Van Oort's Summary

Our company, Russell Associates is a training, testing, and inspection content and software system’s company, specializing in helping companies like yours in the food, agribusiness, and general industries, grow and realize a positive return on their investment. Our approach can result in better trained employees, improved training, testing, and inspection processes, greater efficiencies, reduced risk, lower costs, and improved ROI. We've been doing it for over 30 years!


We work with growing companies: aligning business strategies with learning objectives, customizing learning solutions to fill client business needs, and designing and developing interactive e-learning and blended learning programs that improve the effectiveness of training and testing and the efficiency of training and testing delivery.

inspecitTRAC, an automated inspection software designed for inspections, audits and observations, is delivered on a tablet computer that mobilizes the process of data collection and evaluation for application in a wide range of businesses and industries.

Achievement based incentive programs are used to influence behaviors and motivate participants.

Russell Associates works with companies that want to improve training and testing effectiveness (knowledge and information transfer) and efficiency of delivery (reduce lost production hours and travel time). Russell Associates has the technology based tools, expertise, and experience to improve our client's ROI from training.

Russell Associates web based tools are easily implemented and scalable for growing companies.

Russell Associates’ clients have experienced up to 81% time savings using Computer-Based Interactive Training (CBIT) compared to traditional, instructor-led training. Time and cost savings of 80% when using inspectiTRAC to automate inspections, observations, and audits data collection and transfer/

Specialties

Aligning business strategies with learning objectives; elearning; learning management system; customizing learning solutions to client business needs; interactive elearning, blended learning, and classroom delivery design and development; testing and certification; competency based learning management, web based training, surveys; needs assessments; HRD performance management;developing and implementing of performance improvement programs; automation of inspections, audits, observations.
