Lisa Dube Forman's Summary

There is a vast difference between real estate agents--we are not all alike. I provide a valuable service; you are not just sold a property. I am an Independent Buyer Broker who represents Buyers, nor do I work in a realty office that is beholden to Sellers. Why use an agent who lists homes for sale in a realty office? Expert Buyer Broker representation can make all the difference in the purchase of real property. My unique services guide clients through the various stages of home buying. These include research and review, preparation of offers, negotiations, inspections, occasional re-negotiations, sales contracts, and coordination of real estate closings. Should you choose to utilize my services, I will provide you with properties that meet your budget and demands. Your interests are my interests.

Since 1995, I have served our resort mountain community of Lake Placid, New York as a full-time real estate broker. I have proven success in real estate brokerage and have enjoyed solid, repetitive referral business. There is no greater compliment that I can think of than referrals.

I have always striven to remain educated at the highest levels and have actively involved myself in the real estate community. My participation in the Adirondack region's Real Estate Board was both lengthy and involved. As a member of the Northern Adirondack Board of REALTORS® I served as Education Chair, MLS Chairperson, Director, Advisor to the President, Secretary, Vice-President and two terms as President. As President, I set forth and achieved several significant and profitable association milestones of which I am extremely proud. It took a great deal of hard work, long hours and team effort but the end goal was worth it. This is my work ethic, this is what I bring to the table for you.

Specialties: Buyer Broker Representation in the Adirondacks. Specializing in luxury homes, new construction, and condominiums. I am a ePRO Accredited Buyer Representative!
