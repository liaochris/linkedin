Josue Motta's Summary

I've been working with Design and Marketing since 1996. I started out designing for print right when it was about to go digital. I did a lot of branding, illustration, typography and photo enhancement work.

I later moved on to layout for magazines, planing, marketing and designing for Expo. And also some web design and front end programing.

After 12+ years i needed some new inspiration and i applied to Hyper Island and was planing to become a Digital Creative / Project Manager. I liked Motion Graphics more and have been freelancing as an animator since 2011.
