Geeta Nayyar's Summary

Physician executive, public speaker, author, social media leader with special expertise in healthcare innovation, mobile and cloud Health IT solutions. Extensive experience in clinical medicine, expertise in health technologies, health policy, medical communications and public health. Committed to improving efficiencies in health care enterprises, leveraging the power of health care information technology, and bolstering the reach and effectiveness of health promotion and disease awareness programs.

• Extensive clinical medicine background, practicing clinician & academic faculty (board-certified in Internal Medicine)

• A deep understanding of clinical information systems and technology’s role in medicine

• Experience directly supporting marketing and sales in clinical liaison medical officer position for multi-million dollar Health IT services firm

• Successfully launched HIT start up organization within a fortune 50 company; serving as effective thought leader and public spokesperson

• Connector of a variety of key constituents including clinicians / physicians, executive leadership, direct reports, peers, board members, and community leaders

• Physician executive of senior management team, providing strategic clinical input for health systems, providers, payers, government and industry. Particular emphasis on clinical transformation through health information technology

• Excellent verbal, written communication and presentation skills with ability to communicate business and medical issues in an easy to understand manner

• Articulate in translating health care issues to a wide range of stakeholders including physicians, patients, providers and the lay public

• Inspiring leader, strong team player and mentor and coach in the development of people and organizations to achieve breakthrough results
