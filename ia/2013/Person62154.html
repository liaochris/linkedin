Jennifer Vranes' Summary

I am a professional artist, best known for my vibrant and textured paintings of Aspen and Birch Forests. In my own unique style, I load thick paint onto the canvas, causing the white bark and black knobs of the tree trunks to protrude 3-Dimensionally.

My work can be found in top Galleries throughout the United States, and in more than 800 corporate, public, and private collections worldwide.

I especially enjoy working with clients and designers on custom projects. I have extensive experience working with Interior Designers, painting commissions in any size or color palette to suit their client's needs.

I am interested in forging partnerships with Interior Design Firms who specialize in the Hospitality, Corporate, and Private sectors.

Specialties

I love working with Galleries and Designers to create custom Fine Art for their clients.
