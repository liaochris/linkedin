Lucy Curtis' Summary

“Here’s what I believe about mortgages . . . .

For each person, there’s a right one and several wrong ones.
Choose the wrong one, and you’ll pay out more money than you need to. And maybe not just once, but year after year.

OK, you say. So how do I choose the right one?

Here’s my straightforward answer. Pick the brains of someone who’s been through what you’re going through; who’s felt the nervousness of committing to the major outlay of home purchase; and who has the dedication and professional back-up resources to guide you securely through the maze to the right, most appropriate arrangement.

That someone could be me.

I live in Bragg Creek with my daughter, son and architect husband. He designed our home and I can affirm that I lived and breathed the whole building process and came out – eventually! – smiling. I work with Dominion Lending Centres Westcor – a major financial institution with access to the best products and lending rates from across Canada.

And whether you’re . . .
- purchasing your first or next home
- investing in property or buying a second/vacation home
- refinancing to improve cash flow or reduce debt
- checking options for mortgage renewal

. . . you’re assured of my complete dedication to your case. Not only because you need the best service, but also I need to earn the referrals that come with providing it!
If you feel having a word could be helpful – no fuss, no commitment – please get in touch. I look forward to learning what you need.”


"Not just any mortgage...the right mortgage"
Lucy Curtis. On your side.
