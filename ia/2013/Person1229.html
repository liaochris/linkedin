Aaron Curtis, MSBA's Summary

Web applications development manager with a MSBA and 15+ years of experience developing and leading teams for a number of Fortune 500 companies. Chemistry and balance has been key with the teams that I have worked with. It has required working with each developer involved on the project and knowing what makes them drive in order to be fluid. Knowing when to assert and when to get out of the way has been instrumental. I take pride in knowing that I've successfully been involved with a number of agile (iterative) development projects over the years and consider myself an adaptable coach. The most important take away has been that a team approach is most productive. I'm a results oriented professional and work closely with the business to ensure projects meet scope.

Specialties: Agile Methodologies, ASP.NET 1.0-4.5, C#.NET, MVC 4.0, Razor, MVVM, jQuery, JavaScript, AJAX, MSSQL, T-SQL, DTS\SSIS\SSRS, CSS, XML, HTML, Entity Framework 4.1, various source control platforms, as well as full SDLC. Project Management/Leadership.
