Jennifer Nunn-Iwai's Summary

A seasoned Graphic Designer in the Print Publishing/layouts, Entertainment/Theatrical, Wedding/Invitations, Branding and Fashion Industry. With over 8 years of experience, it has enabled me to gain skills in becoming a quick learner, multi-tasking and being very detail-oriented. As well as, all the other necessary skills needed to help get through a project without a hitch.

With a love for style, it was only natural to start a second career as a Fashion/Wardrobe Stylist. With over 3 years of solid styling experience, I've already worked with award winning Celebrity/Editorial Fashion Stylists, Fashion Houses and Photographers. I've also worked at a Fashion House as a Assistant Public Relations/Sample Coordinator. I have experiences in managing all samples (pulling/ organizing/ packing/ returning). On top of communicating with Clients, Stylists, Fashion PR Houses and messengers. Also I've done fittings, dressings, model castings and worked at Fashion Shows. Even though I may not have the years of experience, I believe my other work experience makes up for it.

Please contact me if you'd like to see my full resume and portfolio for a possible position within your company. Thank you for your time!
