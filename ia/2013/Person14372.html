Bill Lydon's Summary

Experienced industrial, process control, and building automation professional with technology and business experience ranging from corporate experience, cofounder & president of a startup company and as an industry consultant. My current thoughts on industrial automation trends: http://automationtrends.com/

Active in the automation industry with articles appearing regularly in Automation.com and InTech magazine ( ISA.org/InTech ) providing insights and industry analysis.
