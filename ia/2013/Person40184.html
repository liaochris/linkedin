Elizabeth Bauman's Summary

Hi, my name is Liz!

At heart, I am a writer. My love of writing has translated to a career in marketing where I spend each day choosing the right words for everything from tweets to books.

With a sharp eye for detail, I create compelling copy, blog posts, e-Newsletters, press releases, and marketing materials for distribution in print and online.

I'm a powerhouse brand ambassador, passionate about finding and building engaged online communities. With more than five years writing, editing, and communications experience, I clearly identify, not just an existing audience, but the best way to talk to them.

In the future, I don't know where I'll be, but I know that I will be writing.

Specialties: - Content creation and direction (marketing copy, web content and blog posts, and more)
- Copy and developmental editing
- Social media management and online community organization
