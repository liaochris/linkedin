Ben Halsall's Summary

My passion is design and education and in my day-to-day work with my company Valley Media Canada I look for solutions that empower my clients to take control of their online marketing tools. I love chatting design and I love to train people so that they don't need to rely on designers for the day-to-day running of their business. Employing technologies such as Wordpress, Facebook, Twitter and Shopify makes this dream possible.

I am also a freelance software instructor for Apple & Adobe software for The Training Company in Gatineau, Ottawa, Toronto & Montréal.

I occasionally teach as a part-time Professor in NewMedia & Publication Design at Cégep Heritage College and in the Media Art & Design department at Algonquin College.

Before moving to Canada I was course leader for Broadcast Media Technologies at Leeds Metropolitan University in the UK. I am also an Apple Certified Trainer Final Cut Pro 7 & X and an Adobe Certified Expert in Adobe Photoshop.

Please get in touch if you would like to work with me. My e-mail is benhalsall@hotmail.com , you can also connect with me here on LinkedIn or on Twitter @benhalsall .

Specialties: Design, Video Editing, Lateral Thinking, Pitching, Teaching, Education, Training, Team Leader, Creative Thinking, Software Training, Collaboration
