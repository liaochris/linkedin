Maykel Nunes' Summary

My work experience includes nine years working at advertising agencies, working with different
kinds of clients and creating varied projects such as branding, editorial design, promotional design, advertising and electoral campaigns. This gave me the ability to improve my problem solving skills, to work under pressure and rise to creative challenges under tight deadlines in a fast-paced environment. I am a highly motivated worker, who breathes design 24/7 with a passion to learn and progress; a positive team player with a critical eye who is always attentive to small details.
