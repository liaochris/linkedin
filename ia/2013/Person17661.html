Brian Duggan's Summary

I help companies Sponsor or Produce Events for maximum thought leadership, business development and content marketing benefit.

I serve the needs of two distinct groups:

1. I develop event-based programs that enable media and online properties (and others) to support advertisers and sponsors in new and innovative ways, and to profit from delivering this enhanced value

2. I help leading corporations achieve their thought leadership and business development goals through effective sponsorship and content marketing programs

My 25 year track record includes developing and implementing Sponsorship and Alliance programs that serve businesses in areas as diverse as live events, social media, business & technology, personal health and sustainability.

Developing and selling high ticket “intangible” programs that deliver tangible results has become a specialty for me, as has helping clients generate real value from something that starts with just an inspired idea.

I would love to connect directly and to explore further how I can be of support to your firm.
