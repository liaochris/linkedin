Social Media Director

Ryan Meili Campaign for Sask NDP Leader

July 2012 – March 2013 (9 months) Saskatchewan, Canada

Working in close coordination with the campaign's senior management team and reporting to the Communications Director, I oversaw all aspects of the social media strategy for a provincial NDP Leadership Candidate.

Our social media team, which grew to include over 80 people by the end of the campaign, was hugely successful and innovative in a variety of ways. We ended the race with more than double the number of Facebook "Likes" of any of the other three candidates, we ran an effective "Likes to Loves" campaign to encourage people who Liked our Facebook page to take the next step to further involvement. We were the only campaign to livestream our launch announcement, the only campaign (and first in Saskatchewan politics) to hold a Reddit AMA chat session, the only campaign to develop iPhone and Android apps. We utilized a variety of tools including Tout, Storify and YouTube to supplement our main focus on promoting our campaign on Facebook and Twitter.

When I volunteered for the Meili campaign during a previous leadership race in 2009, I brought forward the idea for an online "money bomb". This innovative fundraiser - another first for Canadian politics - brought in fully 15% of the total money raised in that campaign.
