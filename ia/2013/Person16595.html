Brandt Tierney's Summary

For over ten years I have been involved with over a hundred EDI projects. I provide daily customer assistance, project design/implementation, and travel on site to teach. I have become a jack of many trades, where I believe the trick to performing an operation is to know the many ways and benefits prior to implementation. In order to follow my belief, I must stay current and practice regularly, and am a true geek at heart.

I also stay current at my current position, where I run into various EDI projects, and rarely have two that are the same. For the last seven years the new HIPAA X12 standards have become a priority and with that all associated linking/combining of legacy formats, systems, and databases.

Whenever possible I take as many moonlighting options to broaden my horizons, which have included web development, PDF generation, graphics, and enhanced office apps (VBA programming and 2007 XML), and hardware repair/maintenance/upgrade) to name a few.

My hope is to create job security by working for multiple entities on a consistent basis. I believe I am an asset as my experience allows for quick solution and turn around time. Please feel free to contact for any project or permanent needs.
