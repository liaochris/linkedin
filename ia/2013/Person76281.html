Kevin Burns' Summary

After nine years as a business executive, Kevin returned to the private practice of law. Admitted to practice in Connecticut and New York, he provides advice to small business owners and investors with regard to all possible issues from creation of business entities, operating problems, sale and purchase of business assets and ownership succession. Kevin has nearly 35 years of experience working for the interests of small business owners and managers. He also has significant experience in related legal areas such as real estate, creditor/debtor rights, estates and trusts and commercial litigation. Taken together he is well qualified to provide personal legal services promptly and cost efficiently.

Kevin L. Burns formerly lead the intellectual property management business of Palisades Media's various independent film distribution companies. They presented two discrete lines of films; one focused on movies that appeal to the connoisseur of art house cinema and the other on young male viewers of horror and action films with an East Asian pedigree.

In his role, Kevin worked on and with the financing model that aided the marketing and promotions of numerous independent movies released theatrically in art-house movie theaters. Those experiences lead to a belief in owning the rights as the real critical step in managing the value in films and the web of intellectual property rights that they include.

In 2008, Kevin took charge of the acquisition of the intellectual property rights of major independent film distribution companies and added hundreds of movie titles to the Palisades library.

Palisades managed its film distribution functions by exploiting the rights in the movies through numerous channels, including traditional theatrical releases, home video in all its various permutations, broadcast and cable television, Internet distribution and digital distribution.
 

Specialties

Legal representation with a focus on the problems of small business owners and managers. Business formation, choice of entity, contracts, real estate, estate planning, wealth preservation. Kevin also maintains close relationships with legal specialists and can make sure you have the right fit to help resolve your legal problem.

Acquisition and management of intellectual property rights in independent feature films.
