Lonnie Rodgers' Summary

With his combination of knowledge and experience, Lonnie Rodgers will be a powerful ally when it comes to buying or selling your home. As a residential realtor in Calgary since 1984, Lonnie has had the joy of helping hundreds of men, women, and families buy or sell a house, earning a reputation for patience and client-centered service along the way.

Lonnie's philosophy as a Realtor could easily be summed up in one simple phrase: "Treat every buyer or seller the same way you would want to be treated." Although it sometimes means taking a slower approach, and doing a lot more follow-up after the home has been sold, he firmly believes it's the only way to ensure that a purchase goes smoothly for everyone.

His clients seem to agree. That simple idea has helped him generate more than 90% of his new contracts through referrals, word of mouth, and repeat business – including several second generation buyers (the children of past clients coming back to work with Lonnie again).

With more than 60 years spent in Calgary, Lonnie knows the market, properties, and neighbourhoods as well as any one person could, and he backs it all up with a desire to ensure that you are as happy as you possibly could be with the purchase or sale of your home.

To find out more about Lonnie, and to see his current listings, you can visit him online at: http://lonnierodgersrealestate.com
