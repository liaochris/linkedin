Malinda Alahakoon's Summary

Malinda Alahakoon is an active tech journalist who shares his expertise with the global community as a writer/presenter and maintains an up-to-date, credible source of information by the name of SiliconStation.com. He is also serving in the academic world as a lecturer with a unique and innovative teaching style having in mind the objective of lighting a bulb for the future.

Being a competent communicator, he possesses natural skills in written/verbal communication as well as an inborn talent to build up positive relationships among people.

In addition, he is a Consulting IT Professional who gives an insight to complex business problems with a unique style of his own which has paved way for many companies to implement long term IT strategies and get in to the path of booming profit. He is a guerrilla marketer who constantly opts for challenges and experiments where he can make the impossible, possible.

Malinda is an adrenalin junkie in his own terms. Apart from the outdoor challenging activities, he is passionate about share trading and number crunching. When not dealing with numbers, he is an artist. He sings, composes and produces music, click frames with his own accent. Black coffee and his guitar are what get him going.
