Holly Wittenberg's Summary

Holly is an entrepreneur, stylist, art lover, wife and mother. She loves sharing the beautiful things she appreciates with others. Her love of fashion started while watching her grandmother design and sew glamorous dresses. Holly fell in love with textured fabrics, unique pieces and the beauty of pulling them all together. Her grandmother's wise words of “pretty is as pretty does” continuously echoes in her mind and serves as one of her guiding principles.

Holly provides full “lifestyling” - personal styling and branding and shopping. Her clients include entrepreneurs, professionals and socialites who understand the importance of making the right first impression and appreciate an expert's advice. They describe her as innovative, stylish, loving and generous. Some consider her an artist using her clients and their homes as her canvas. Holly feeds her passion of helping people feel confident, empowered and successful by striving for perfection on every project.

Specialties

Personal Styling, Fashion Styling, Image Management, Interior Styling, Interior Decorating, Fund Raising, Full Lifecycle Recruiting, Executive Search
