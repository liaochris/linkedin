Kathleen Smith's Summary

ClearedJobs.Net is a veteran owned company focused on providing support to cleared facilities employers in the defense and intelligence communities. We support professionals with security clearances find their next opportunity by providing career support that is geared toward individuals with security clearances.

Our Cleared Job Board has one of the highest percentage of security cleared professionals with TS/SCI, TS/SCI CI Poly and TS/SCI Full Scopes in the industry. No matter if you are looking for JAVA engineer, cybersecurity professional or executive admin assistant, we have the candidates for your security cleared positions. Our Cleared Job Fairs have consistently been rated as the best in the industry providing a high quality environment for both cleared facilities employers and security cleared professionals.

One goal that we exceed at is customer service. On a daily basis we hear from both cleared facilities employers and professionals with security clearances that we provide them with the tools and services they need and that we are always there to provide help.

As part of a veteran owned company, I have enjoyed the connection we have with the military community and relish the opportunity to create opportunities to support our men and women in uniform as they transition into the workforce. We actively participate in the Transition Classes provided at the local bases and at various agencies specifically on how Social Media can support a career search.

Finally I actively support the local DC area recruiting community by being on the board of recruitDC to plan and implement networking and continuous education events for recruiters.
Furthermore I support AFCEA NOVA"s Military Government Transition Program, AFCEA Central Maryland's Women in Intelligence Group and present at many local conferences for government contractors.
