Audrey Friedman RN, CLNC's Summary

With over 28 years nursing experience in a variety of health care settings including oncology and critical care, I provide individualized medical legal case review, medical and nursing resources and education for attorneys, insurance firms and healthcare facilities.

My medical-legal specialties: Litigation support: medical research, education, chronologies, merit reviews. Community Health Education: Living Wills, Health Organizers, Navigating the Medical System; Nursing Coaching/Mentoring.

I also am the owner and developer of www.NursingWitAndWisdom.com, a very unique membership site designed for nurses and nursing students. Our site is designed with weekly themes and is focused on education, clinical and precepting challenges, nurses in the news and inspirational stories designed to inspire. Our program provides monthly programming, Action sheets for each theme presentation, teleconference networking and presentations and opportunities for networking and coaching. Our goal is to "Inspire and Be Inspired" nurses and nursing students!
