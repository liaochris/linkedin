Marlene Stocks' Summary

Personal Philosophy: "We must always go the second mile. When we go the first mile, we simply do what is required of us. It is when we go the second mile that excellence is achieved and minor miracles happen." Deborah McGriff

Professional Summary: Marlene Stocks is an award-winning entrepreneur with proven expertise in senior move management, software training, non-profit program management and German language instruction.

She founded Word Information Services, Inc., a consulting practice that offered personalized software training in 1983. The firm’s client base included international corporations, small to medium sized companies, school districts, and government agencies.

In January 2008, she combined her consulting expertise, talent for project management, outstanding organizational skills, nurturing personality and ability to relate and work with seniors to create Senior Transition Services, Inc.. As a certified senior move manager (cSMM), she helps her clients downsize and declutter their homes and relocate to retirement communities where they begin a new chapter in their lives. Senior Transition Services was recently awarded the "Circle of Service" designation by the National Association of Senior Move Managers (NASMM). This special designation is awarded to senior move management companies attaining an elevated level of experience and expertise.

Specialties: Marlene’s personal goal is to work with her clients to help achieve their downsizing and organizing objectives. She does this by listening and understanding her client’s needs, thereby developing synergy with them. This rapport then allows her to launch a strategy that pro-actively sets realistic expectations, identifies and evaluates alternatives, establishes parameters, defines deliverables, communicates timelines, implements the required services and monitors post-completion satisfaction.

Marlene Stocks' Experience

President

Senior Transition Services

January 2008 – Present (5 years 11 months)

As a certified senior move manager, I offer my clients the personalized downsizing, decluttering and relocation services they need with the compassion, respect, patience and dignity they deserve. By managing every detail of the senior's move to a retirement community and/or coordinating the estate liquidation, I provide my clients and their families "peace of mind" so they don't become too stressed and overwhelmed with a process that may be unfamiliar to them. To date, we have proudly served over 250 clients.

I manage a staff of ten and utilize the services of other professionals and charities that assist seniors. Senior Transition Services serves Philadelphia, Bucks, Chester, Delaware and Montgomery counties as well as South/Central New Jersey.
