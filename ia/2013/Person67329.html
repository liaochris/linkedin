May 2013 – Present (7 months) Greater Seattle Area

The Conference Reporter provides instant, onsite reporting and video interviews about and throughout your event. It's like having your own online conference channel featuring your messages, your leaders and your attendees. Instant information results in instant engagement; your participants will remember what’s important. We partner with you, offering an easy to adopt, no hassle, turnkey approach to build excitement and connections at your conference.

Inform and Involve Your Attendees
We enhance conference experiences where attendees are involved in active learning in ways they understand and currently use. With our customized approach your participants learn more, network enthusiastically, and remain motivated longer. To bring you more value, we highlight your sponsors and showcase your vendors. We echo your conference messaging, conduct interviews and real time reports.

How We Do This
We amplify buzz in a captivating way, creating a positive experience now and for the future. We generate 1 minute video segments from your conference to:

• Highlight important events, so attendees can get the most out of their schedule
• Provide real time pictures and videos of your event to participant's mobile devices
• Cultivate interest and exploration of new information and products
• Add value to their experience with conference tips
• Help you build an online community for your event
• Produce an online media library for future events

We create, publish and share content through relevant communication channels that align with your conference objectives by:

• Partnering with the conference planner team to identify gaps in channels
• Engage the attendees on these channels
• Filling the channels with controlled, relevant, continuous and engaging content

The Conference Reporter takes your message and enhances it; your attendees won't just be aware of it, they will understand it, interact with it, and share it with others.

The Meeting Maximizer (Sole Proprietorship)

Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Events Services industry

August 1996 – Present (17 years 4 months) Greater Seattle Area

Meetings are notoriously “not awesome.” The Meeting Maximizer has a scientific process to stop these evil, money gobbling meetings. Maximize your Meeting R.O.I. The best meetings include these 3 steps:
--------------------------------1) Eliminate Distractions
--------------------------------2) Streamline Agendas
--------------------------------3) Create Interaction

---MAXIMIZE your meeting and conference. No matter where you are in the planning process, the Meeting Maximizer can help eliminate the common problems and reduce the risk of the meeting failure.

---ANALYZE your current plan to diagnose potential pitfalls, fire hazards and liabilities. Prescribe solutions, prevention and back up plans.

---X FACTOR - In my experience with meetings, events, speaking, people can have great ideas, but not everyone can execute them. We will build an action plan to achieve your goals.

---IMPLEMENT - We will create implementations plans with detailed timelines and procedures to streamline efforts.

---MANAGE - We can manage the process, by helping you prioritize the most important tasks.

---INNOVATE - With all of the new technology and changes in our lifestyle, conferences must change too. Doing the conference the way it’s always been done is costly and unappealing. In order to capture the majority of the attendees’s attention, it takes an innovative strategy.

---ZONE - You want to be in the “good zone” where everything aligned. We want to create the “zone” for the attendees, presenters, business partners and sponsors.

---EXPERIENCE – When the experience is valuable and memorable, you have maximized the event. The participants will use word of mouth to market your organization and will drive excitement and attendance for next year.

We use Meeting Maximizer Methodology to produce efficient, productive meetings.
