Fred Pirone's Summary

As a visual and photographic artist, I am interested in fine art, art direction and consulting, concept development, the construction of visual messages and strategic communications, documentaries, and performance.

As an archaeologist, I am interested in the archaeological sciences, Mediterranean archaeology, island archaeology, the Neolithic and Early Bronze Age, pottery, ancient languages, symbology, constructing historical and prehistorical narratives, and digging for the truth about our past.

As a lawyer, I am interested in real estate law, intellectual and cultural property law, bankruptcy and creditors' rights, construction law, contracts and transactional law, mediation and arbitration (alternative dispute resolution) and constitutional law.

Over all I have three primary goals and they are:

1) to become financially independent and to own 100% of my time;

2) to have the body of a Spartan and the mind of an Athenian; and

3) to become immortal

Specialties

As an Artist: Photography and Video, Fine Art, Art Direction, Art Consulting, Art Brokering, Gallery & Exhibitions, Constructing Visual Messages, Concept Development, Documentary Work & Creation, Theatrical & Radio Performance

As an Archaeologist: Archaeological Sciences, Cultural Resource Management, Mediterranean Archaeology

As Lawyer: Real Estate, Bankruptcy, Creditors' Rights, Intellectual Property, Cultural Property, Contracts, Mediation, and Arbitration
