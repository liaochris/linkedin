Freedom Debt Relief's Summary

Who We Are
Freedom Debt Relief is a group of about 600 employees strong and growing – known internally as “The Freedom Family”. We’re energetic, smart & compassionate – but most importantly we’re all about helping our clients through tough financial times. We were founded in 2002 by Stanford Business School graduates, Andrew Housser and Bradford Stroh, and since then we have built the best management team around.

What We Do
Our mission is to help people improve their financial well-being. This means helping clients resolve their debt, educating consumers, conducting consultations, and recommending appropriate viable solutions. Our core services offer an alternative to bankruptcy, credit counseling, and debt consolidation. We approach the business from the human perspective and work for the consumer exclusively (not the banks or other agendas). We become your number one advocate, helping you re-establish solid financial footing.

Responsible Leadership
Freedom Debt Relief is committed to elevating the standards of our industry. We are accredited and founding members of the American Fair Credit Association (AFCC), the leading debt settlement and fair credit advocacy association in the nation.

Specialties: Consumer Debt Negotiation & Settlement, Consumer Advocacy, Credit Counseling, Bankruptcy Alternatives
