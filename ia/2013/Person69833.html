Jeff Rives,SHRM's Summary

Chapel Hill Solutions are experts in global search for the recruiting industry, focusing on many industries: Healthcare, Pharmaceuticals, Biotechnology, Clinical Research, Medical Devices, Apparel/Textile Mfg, Off Shore Sourcing, Financial Services, Quality Management and Information Technology (IT).

Our recruitment consultants are specialized to provide unprecedented individual quality service to both client and candidate. We assist in the total recruitment needs within the Healthcare and Life Sciences Industry including all phases and business areas within the Pharmaceutical, Bio Tech, Clinical Research and Medical Device and Healthcare. We know where to find the people you need to run your business – people who are not usually seeking employment or reading job postings. Our professional expertise in working for both candidate and client insures all parties are satisfied.

We have an in-depth understanding of what our clients look for in candidates and what candidates look for in future employers. Our clients appreciate our personal approach and expertise in matching the right person with the right job, and rely on us to present them with impact players – the most talented candidates who can make a difference for their organizations. We know who they are, their unique skills and abilities, and their personal goals. We are dedicated to placing top-quality talent at all levels.Attracting and hiring top-quality employees is essential in taking your organization to the next level.

Chapel Hill Solutions is dedicated to finding top talent to help take you there. We approach potential candidates regarding your company's opportunity based on your unique needs, priorities, and expectations. We offer a complete portfolio of staffing tools to answer a wide range of needs: Testing, Assessment, Contract Staffing, Project Management, Relocation Services, Background Screening, access to Compensation Surveys, and Industry Intelligence
