Kyle McGowan's Summary

I'm a highly motivated and creative self-starter with 13+ years experience in web site layout, design, and programming for projects ranging from basic informational sites to complex internet-based solutions. I have a successful track record of conveying customer vision through web application development, and front and back-end user-interface design. I am a detail-oriented multi-tasker comfortable managing multiple deadlines both in and out of group settings, while easily adapting to changing technologies and priorities.
