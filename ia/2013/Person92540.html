Michael Johnson's Summary

I have nearly 15 years’ compassionate service experience as certified professional guardian and provider of direct legal services in indigent adult guardianship cases. I serve guardians and adults with severe or profound intellectual disability residing in Medicaid intermediate care or nursing facilities with specialized services. I also have experience in citizen advocacy at the state and federal levels, including access to justice issues in this unique area.

I am now pursuing a research L.L.M. (master of laws) in guardianship law. My thesis focuses on an individual's best interests in the context of profound intellectual disability. This academic research builds on my experience by adding scholarship and teaching or training as an additional focus.

I am interested in new opportunities for employment or consulting while continuing scholarly research and teaching. Other legal experience includes real estate, administrative law, litigation, and appeals. I am licensed for Washington State, Western District of Washington, Ninth Circuit Court of Appeals, and Federal Circuit.
