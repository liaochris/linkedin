Juan Carlos Pagan's Summary

Juan Carlos Pagan is a Designer, Typographer, and Art Director living in NYC. In 2006 he graduated from Parsons School of Design with a B.F.A in Communication Design. A few years later in 2011 he graduated as part of the inaugural class of Type@Cooper, the Postgraduate Typeface Design certification program at Cooper Union. Employed previously at G2 Worldwide, and MTV, Juan is currently the Head of Design at DDB New York and a Partner at the Brooklyn based design studio and type foundry Pagan & Sharp.
