Janis Luedke's Summary

Interior designer and artist working on healing the planet room by room! Creating warm and inviting environments that reduce stress and promote healing, through the use of color, texture, light, art, environmentally friendly and non-toxic materials. Twenty-five years experience in all aspects of interior project planning, design, project management and art consulting for healthcare, commercial, residential applications.

Specialties: Color consulting, interior finishes, furniture, art, environmentally friendly materials.

Sustainable interior design, art and color consulting for commercial, healthcare, senior living and residential applications. Focus on listening to the client and designing the environment to reflect their sensibilties and needs.

Artist

JALARTS

February 2008 – Present (5 years 8 months)

Vivid and colorful nature-oriented paintings in acrylic and watercolor, impressionistic to abstract in style. Strongly influenced by Southwestern landscape, space, light and color.
