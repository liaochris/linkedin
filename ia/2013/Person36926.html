Don Willbanks' Summary

Electrical Enginer (PE) specializing in the design and implementation of PLC-based industrial control systems. Primary experience is in aluminum can/end manufacturing facillities and natural gas pipeline facillities. Primary experience is Allen-Bradley PLC's (ControlLogix, MicroLogix, PLC5, and PLC2), but have additional experience with Unitronics, Mitsubishi, Modicon, TI, and Siemens PLC's. Also, have experience with RSView, Indusoft, Intellution, and Wondware software.

Specialties

PLC system hardware and software design. Electrical installation and construction management. Commissioning and start-up. 480 VAC power distribution. Electrical installation packages for bid and installation.
