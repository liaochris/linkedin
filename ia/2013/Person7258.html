Andrew Saunders' Summary

CAA Integrated Marketing and Content Solutions Executive with diverse entertainment, media, technology and brand marketing knowledge, experience and relationships. Work at the intersection of all entertainment verticals, media platforms and brand marketing disciplines. Founding member of the CAA Brand Coverage Division, which sits at the center of CAA. Work with brands and their agency partners to strategize, develop, distribute and activate custom integrated marketing and content solutions across all media platforms to drive engagement and sales. Provide entertainment marketplace intelligence and direct access to all of CAA's clients and properties (Talent, Production, Digital, Television, Film, Music, Sports, Games, Fashion, Events, Lifestyle, Licensing, Startups and International). Work with traditional and emerging media companies across all platforms to develop custom integrated marketing and content solutions for advertisers (broadcast, cable, digital, social, mobile, experiential, radio, print, direct and OOH). Develop cross-platform strategies for CAA’s clients to build and extend their personal brands across all relevant media channels and into new business sectors. Have consulted for over 200 companies and developed premium solutions and multimillion dollar strategic partnerships for global leaders including Microsoft, P&G, NBCUniversal, Ford, AOL, Intel, Toyota, Chrysler, Cisco, Target, Visa, YouTube Google, Clear Channel, Apple, Disney, Pepsi, Facebook, Mazda, Spin Media, Pernod Ricard, Sony, Yahoo! and Funny or Die. Having worked closely for years with many of the leading domestic and global advertising, media, digital, PR, social and experiential agencies, I have an in-depth understanding of how to leverage partners to generate new revenue streams and optimize for success. Current and previous partners include TBWA\Chiat\Day, 72andSunny, Ogilvy, Deutsch, Saatchi, Team One, Digitas, Carat, OMD, MEC, Initiative, Zenith, Fleishman Hillard and Ketchum.
