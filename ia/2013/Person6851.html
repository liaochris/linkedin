Andrew Danas' Summary

Grove, Jaskiewicz and Cobert, LLP
Washington, D.C.

I represent clients located throughout the United States, as well as clients in foreign countries who do business in the United States and abroad. Our clients range from “Fortune 100” corporations to individuals, trade associations, cooperatives, partnerships and small companies. My firm prides itself as being a “Washington, D.C. Law Firm with a Global Reach.”

My principal areas of practice are corporate and general business; transportation and shipping; mergers and acquisitions; Customs and homeland security; trademarks; international transactions; labor and employment; competition and antitrust; bankruptcy; and litigation and alternative dispute resolution.

I regularly represent client interests before Departments and Agencies of the U.S. Federal and State Governments and, on occasion, before foreign government regulatory agencies both in Asia and Europe.

I litigate commercial and regulatory issues as both lead counsel and special counsel in various courts, as well as in U.S. and foreign arbitration forums. I also represent client interests before the United States Congress and have testified before Congress on issues involving international trade and transportation and competition law issues. I am a frequent speaker at legal and industry events and publish legal articles and columns on a regular basis.
