Ellen Allard's Summary

ELLEN ALLARD is a multi-award winning children’s Recording Artist, Composer, Performer & Early Childhood Music Specialist, working in secular & Jewish music and Education. A former Elementary School Vocal Music teacher and Early Childhood classroom & music teacher, she began her piano studies at age 5, studied flute through high school, & learned to play acoustic guitar in college. She plays banjo, accordion, ukulele, autoharp, recorder, and spoons!

Her CD: Children’s Web award winner Sing it! Say it! Stamp it! Sway it! vol. 1, Parent’s Guide to Children’s Media award winner Sing it! Say it! Stamp it! Sway it! vol. 2, Children’s Web award winner Sing it! Say it! Stamp it! Sway it! vol. 3, Raise the Children, the Parents’ Choice Rec. and Children’s Web award winner Sing Shalom: Songs for the Jewish Holidays, the Parents’ Choice Approved award winner and Children’s Award winner Good Kid, Bring The Sabbath Home, Pizza Pizzazz and Little Taste of Torah. She published 3 manuals as part of the Sing it! Say it! Stamp it! Sway it! series.

One of the most influential Jewish musicians and educators in Jewish music, Ellen presents family concerts, Tot & Family Shabbat Jewish worship services, keynotes & teacher workshops, & is enthusiastically committed to helping kids & families build community through song.

Widely recognized for her appeal to all ages, her songs have become the gold standard for engaging children & adults, & helping them strengthen their Jewish identities. Ellen has a unique, charismatic, charming, & heartwarming way of inviting people to raise their voice in song. When she sings with kids, she knows exactly how to engage and hold their attention, sometimes goofy, sometimes serious, always meaningful. Ellen weaves together powerful, moving and spiritual moments that speak to the hearts, minds, and souls of adult audiences.

Ellen is a graduate of Boston University (Bachelor of Music) and Arcadia University (Master of Early Childhood Education).
