Jay Baldauf's Summary

Arch Apple Financial Services prides itself on spending time with clients and building long term relationships instead of the quick sale. We get all of our new clients through advertising and referrals from existing clients. We do not telemarket or cold call. Chances are you contacted us first due your need for information or insurance. We believe in sending information first, answering your questions and educating you about all your options before selling anything. You will generally get several contacts from us after you first contact us. These inquiries will be to make sure you got the information you requested, that you fully understand what you got, and to answer questions about the information and your particular situation.

After you become a client and your situation changes or the insurance carrier changes rates or coverage we will be there to help you understand the changes and what your options are. Most times we will contact you first to let you know something is changing. We will assist you make any changes necessary and compare your options to help you make an educated decision.

We also try to keep in contact with all of our clients throughout the year. We do this by sending everyone a birthday and Christmas card. This is just a little added service that makes many of our clients very happy, especially the children.
