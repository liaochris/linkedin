Micha Dearen's Summary

I have 18 years of experience in the industry working as a Recruiter for Apartment Management Companies owners, National Management Companies, Third-Party Managements Companies and REIT’s.

I have exclusive contracts with some of the largest management companies in the US because of my proven track record of success in being able to deliver the best candidates in a very short period of time.

My success has come from being able to find specific candidates that are unique in their skill set, qualified and will get the job done. I provide guaranteed results and at a lower price than other recruiters!

Apartment Management Companies Will Come To Me With The Following PROBLEMS:

♦ CHALLENGING PROPERTIES: “I have a very tough portfolio with low end properties that are heavily crime ridden and I need someone to supervise those, hire managers for them, train those managers and make sure they are profitable.”

♦ UNQUALIFIED CANDIDATES: “I’m having trouble finding qualified candidates to manage our properties.”

♦ LOW OCCUPANCY: “I need a manager that will ensure that occupancy rates always stay above 95% and maintain strong resident relations.”

♦ NEW CONSTRUCTION LEASE UP: “I need a new construction lease up manager that can live on site and lease up the asset within the shortest amount of time possible with qualified residents.”

If you are having any of the problems mentioned above, we can provide a solution for you!
