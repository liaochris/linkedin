Audrey Gaffney's Summary

As Interior Architects & Designers you expect us to be professional, dynamic, highly trained & motivated but we also pride ourselves in being ambitious & personal.... aiming to make your project more unique than you expect!
Our mission is to ensure that we deliver the best design service & advice, as you, our client are our best sales people.

No project is never the same so our designs are alway unique & we promise to delivery within your needs & requirements.
With combined experience of over 30 yrs experience in commercial design, we pride ourselves on being at the forefront of the design world, changing & evolving with the times we live in.

We listen to our clients needs, interpretate their wishes & create a unique space for each that will enhance the experience of the end user.

We are committed to providing a consistent & exceptional level of service to each client. As a result, the energy we put into each individual project leads us to create exciting & innovative design solutions that our clients & we here at Be Design are very proud of.

Whether your project is a large hotel project or a hip-hop-happening niteclub, a country cafe or a retail boutique shop in a busy city centre, we know we can show you options that will suit your pocket as much as it will your brief.

Our specialty is to do what we say...that includes working within budgets agreed!!

Let us create a project that is uniquely yours....Be Design.

Specialties

Commercial Interior Design, Interior Architecture, Graphic Design & Re-Branding, Furniture Design, Lighting Design (inc. low energy), Landscape Design, Project Management and Site-Supervision.

Audrey Gaffney's Experience

Owner/Interior Architect

Be Design

July 2008 – Present (5 years 2 months)

Be Design was formed in 2008 in response to consumer demand for professional interior architecture and design services that works in today environment.

Having quickly achieved outstanding results and a reputation for creating "fabulous" interiors for our existing clients, we are determined to achieve the same, and where possilbe higher standards for yourself.

Be Design offer their clients a complete service package, with having the ability to bring a design project from the pre-planning stage through concept to completion.
Our services range from interior architecture, planning permission, interior design, project management and bespoke lighting and furniture. We also specialise in Re-Branding and Led Low-Energy Light controlling.

Talk to us about any project, we are happy to provide a free consultancy meeting to discuss your details.

Senior Interior Designer

Project Architects

Architectural Practice including projects in hotel, office and retail sectors.
Designed and managed interiors in Mahonpoint Shopping Centre, Cork.
From planning issues and building requirements to design and specifying, some of the areas included all Public Toilets, Food Hall, Customer Service Desk, Management Suite, Drop-In Centre, Escalators and Lifts, and all internal floor tiling, signage and wall covering.
Detailed drawings and specification sheets were prepared, scheme boards agreed with client, and managing of fit-out on site.
