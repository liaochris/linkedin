Avi Urban's Summary

Buying or selling a home in the fast-paced Silicon Valley presents a unique set of challenges - and it takes a Realtor with a unique set of skills and experience to help navigate these complex waters.

Are you thinking of buying or selling a home in Silicon Valley? STOP!!

No one likes to hear the words ‘I TOLD YOU SO.’ You’ve heard that hindsight is 20/20. We’re here to change your paradigm. Foresight is 20/20. Contact us for a new mindset.

Avi and his team offer this invitation. Come and join us at one of our complimentary, information-packed seminars, where you’ll get the information and tools you need to help you make savvy real estate decisions.

You have questions and Avi, our experienced real estate consultant, has answers!
The first step is to reserve YOUR seat. (Can’t make it? Avi is happy to meet with you for a one-on-one consultation.)

In Avi’s own words, here’s the story of how a high-tech engineer and entrepreneur found success helping clients reach their real estate goals….

“I arrived in Silicon Valley in 1985 and worked as an engineer and founder of three high-tech startups. During that time I also found success buying and selling my own homes and a few investment properties. Before long I realized that my love of real estate was more than a hobby, and since 2005, it has been my full time career.

All of my clients benefit from the same skills that contributed to my success in high tech — I’m an energetic self-starter, capable of complex analysis and focused execution and an ‘out-of-the-box’ creative thinker.

Don’t just take my word for it. Read right here what my clients are saying about me behind my back.

My unbridled passion for real estate led me to develop my series of free monthly seminars that focus on home buying and selling and real estate investing. My goal is to help each of my clients become an intelligent consumer with lifelong real estate success.”
