twitNERD

April 2009 – Present (4 years 9 months) http://twitnerd.com

A just for fun project using the twitter API.

You can view everyone that follows you that you do not follow back, and you have the option to follow all of them or select those you wish to follow. You also see everyone you requested to follow that has not confirmed your request yet (private accounts).

You can also see everyone you follow that does not follow you back. Those that are unlikely to follow you back (they follow less than 50% of the number of followers they have) will be shown in red. You can unfollow everyone, or just select those you wish to unfollow.

There is also a follow by keyword feature, you can enter a keyword and it will list the last 100 people who have tweeted that keyword. The same as if you searched on twitter, same results but you have the option follow some or all of those people, unless you already follow them.

For those that do the #ff or #followfriday there is a tool for that too. It lists everyone who retweets you or mentions you, and organizes it a nice list of tweets, all you have to do is push the "tweet" button next to the tweet.

Your twitnerd profile now shows you who recently followed or unfollowed you, and lets you send them a tweet (a thanks or a boo hoo).

Soon I will be creating an automation service to allow some of the features to be done automatically on a daily basis. Effortless twitter management :)
