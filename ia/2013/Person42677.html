Erik Therkildsen's Summary

I've been an entrepreneur at heart for over 10 years. I've started 2 businesses within the past two years. But life is about choices and getting back up after you've been knocked down. Being an entrepreneur can be scary! But, if you surround yourself with the right people you can be successful. I've been there and have seen success and failure.

I have developed myself to be a lifelong entrepreneur and business owner with an overriding goal of helping other businesses achieve their goals by leveraging my business experience. Having the ability to share experiences and knowledge with like-minded individuals is a phenomenal experience.

Over the years I have developed a background in all scopes of business, from running a successful farm to achieving a lifelong goal of becoming a full-time firefighter/paramedic. During this time I developed the passion for business and entrepreneurship. I believe it’s more enduring to come from the bottom and make it to the top. As I sit on top as a proud business owner, it gives me great pride to look at what I have achieved.

My clients can always rest assure they are being helped by a dedicated business owner. Looking forward to working with many more businesses and entrepreneurs. Together we can achieve our goals!
