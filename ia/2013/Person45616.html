Frank Agin's Summary

Every entrepreneur, every sales representative, and every profession aspires to be something more than they are. They long to be more widely known. They hope to be more highly regarded in the business community. And, they want greater financial security.

To achieve this, they need more referrals, better contacts and improved information. All of these things come from a consistent practice of effective professional networking.

Through AmSpirit Business Connections, my books and speaking programs, I am dedicated to helping small business types realize these aspirations by empowering them to become more proficient in their networking endeavors.

For more information on me or the books I have written, visit www.frankagin.com. To better explore how I (or AmSpirit Business Connections) can help you become more successful, please visit www.amspirit.com or e-mail me at frankagin@amspirit.com

Frank Agin's Publications

  • Foundational Networking: Building Know, Like and Trust To Create A Lifetime of Extraordinary Success

    • Book - 418 Press
    • October 23, 2008
    Authors: Frank Agin

    Forget about honing those networking skills (they are of little value). Abandon the notion of accumulating spiffy networking techniques (they are a waste of time). You can only achieve long-term networking success through a consistent practice of Foundational Networking.

    Foundational Networking is the process of consciously building in your relationships the quintessential networking elements of know, like and trust by Improving Your Presence (the impression that you are optimistic, confident and approachable ... Enhancing Your Altruism (the notion that you are predisposed to contributing to others) ... Bolstering Your Integrity (the perception that you are honorable, trustworthy and reliable). Foundational Networking will revolutionize how you network.
