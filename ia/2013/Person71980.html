Kara Ronin's Summary

Kara Ronin is the founder of EXECUTIVE IMPRESSIONS. Using her unique portfolio of expertise, she guides professionals, students, and entrepreneurs around the world through international business situations with confidence and class.

Australian born, she spent most of the last 10 years living in Japan, some time in New York, and is now based in France. Previously, Kara worked in the finance industry for high-profile corporate organisations including Deloitte Touche Tohmatsu, Tokyo, in the Financial Services and International Tax Group. She also has significant experience in marketing and teaching related roles in both Australia and Japan. Wanting to make the most of her international profile, she added French and Japanese to her native English.

EXECUTIVE IMPRESSIONS is an international business etiquette consultancy for professionals, students and entrepreneurs who want to feel more comfortable and confident in international business situations, and ultimately stand out.

Kara works with companies, universities, or one-on-one with professionals, entrepreneurs and students.

Her signature programmes are based on the principles of etiquette, designed for a modern life. Some of these programmes are:
◆ Executive First Impressions
◆ Network with Power
◆ Interview With Results
◆ Dine With Class
◆ Working Across Cultures

Kara is available for:
◆ Speaking engagements
◆ Corporate training
◆ University training
◆ One-on-one coaching (in-person or virtual)
