Jon Cofer's Summary

Video has been a huge interest of mine since before high school, when I would shoot silly sketches and movies with my friends.

I have come a long way since then.

I have a wide variety of experience in live production. Directing, technical directing, music, audio mixing, graphics, statistics, camera, and so on. This experience has come from work with Penn State Athletics, classes & the TV station at Clarion University, the Binghamton Mets, and my current position with the New Jersey Devils.

I also am very familiar with post-production. From editing with Adobe Premiere & Final Cut Pro, to animating with After Effects, to graphic design in Photoshop and Illustrator. I even have the Adobe CS5 suite installed on my personal laptop...because you never know when inspiration will hit.

I am always looking to better my skills and gain experience in all areas of the field, not just when it's required. That's what doing what you love for a living is about.

Specialties:
--Adobe Creative Suite 5, Final Cut Pro, Zaxwerks Pro Animator
--Venus 1500 & 7000 software, Edirol PR-80 RealTime Video Presenter, Crossfire Click Effects
--Directing, Technical Directing, Music, Camera
