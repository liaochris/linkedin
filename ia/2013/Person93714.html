Mickey Troccoli's Summary

Mickey has been working in the futures industry since 1997.

He started his career like many successful traders before him; in the S&P 500 pit at the Chicago Mercantile Exchange. Rising through the ranks over the years, Mickey has amassed a wealth of experience and has worked for some of the worlds premier futures brokerage houses.

Now he chooses to share his knowledge with investors all over
the world who wish to become the best possible trader that they
can be.

Specialties

Day trading the S&P and Mini S&P 500 Futures Contracts, Training and Mentorship for traders of all skill levels.

Mickey Troccoli's Experience

Global Sales Director

Trading Advantage

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Research industry

May 2008 – Present (5 years 7 months) Greater Chicago Area

Trading Advantage, LLC™ strives to arm the everyday trader with the same insider knowledge and trading potential as the professional floor trader. We custom mentorship programs designed specifically to challenge your trading capabilities and improve your trading performance.

The Secrets of Traders mentorship programs are self-paced; allowing you to learn as little or as much as you like. This is beneficial, as every trader learns material differently & at a pace they are comfortable with.

We use a live real-time trading "Virtual Trading Room". Our virtual trading room is moderated by real traders who take live trades on a daily basis. This allows our 'Secrets' traders to experience the best (and most powerful) method to learn how to trade; from first-hand experience. The virtual trading room also enables Secrets members to exchange stories with one another, which helps to create a positive, helpful community by which everyone benefits and wins.
