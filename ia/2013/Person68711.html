My mission is to help clients expand in their reach and profitability. I thrive on being given the challenge of developing bold out of the box approaches to reach an outcome. I'm passionate about relationships and building sustainable systems.

Here's my short bio -

I began as a Process Engineer and Process Management Consultant 15 years ago working with a wide range of Fortune 500 companies including Pfizer, JPMorgan, GE, and AIG. I developed an expertise in building reproduce-able systems.

Since 2003 I've immersed myself in all aspects of Internet Marketing, after founding an event planning company called Metrofly with the purpose of creating unique "fun-raisers" for various charities in New York as well as connecting people together for philanthropic causes.

I've since used that systems methodology to empower entrepreneurs to simplify and streamline all facets of their life and business so that they can have their dream lifestyle. Peer Success Circles was born in 2005 to provide that and has grown Internationally.

In 2008, I began Process to Profit to meet the needs of growing entrepreneurs, small and mid-size businesses delivering a unique whole-istic approach to systematize all facets of Online Marketing including Product Launches, Lead Generation and Joint Ventures.
