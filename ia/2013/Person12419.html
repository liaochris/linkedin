Benoit Duguay's Publications

  • Consommation et nouvelles technologies, Au monde de l'hyper

    • Éditions Liber
    • November 1, 2009
    Authors: Benoit Duguay

    «This book is not merely an historical record of different technologies. It aims to outline the benefits of technology as much as the abuses it can lead to, to demonstrate its close relationships with consumption and to explain why hyperconsumption and hypertechnicization of our world go hand in hand. » (B. D.)

  • Consommation et luxe, La vie de l'excès et de l'illusion

    • Éditions Liber
    • November 1, 2007
    Authors: Benoit Duguay

    «What is luxury? What are our expectations towards luxury products and services? How is luxury marketing practiced? Are there behaviours that can counterbalance and thwart hyperconsumption? These are some of the questions I intend to answer in this book. I hope to contribute in making people reflect about consumption and influence our buying habits. Saying this, I do not merely have the general population in mind, but also and especially businesses and marketers who are engaged in logic of escalation the risk of which they do not want to see. » (B. D.)

  • Consommation et image de soi, Dis-moi ce que tu achètes...

    • Éditions Liber
    • November 1, 2005
    Authors: Benoit Duguay

    «Consumption is a habit difficult to break because it is closely linked to self-image. For some, it even becomes compensatory, a way to enhance self-esteem, to project a more positive image. » Contrary to what would have us believe marketing theoreticians, needs are not innate. In addition to the notion of need, this book proposes a concept, expectations, to study what the different actors want in relation to various objects: consumption, tourism, heritage, etc.
