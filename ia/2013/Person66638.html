John Mangia's Summary

Senior Digital Compositor and Lighting artist with experience integrating live action and CG elements for Film, Commercials and Television. Extensive experience using Nuke, Shake, Maya and proprietary tools. Experience using Smoke to lead and finish commercial projects.
