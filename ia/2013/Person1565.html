Adam Palmiter's Summary

Adam Palmiter strives to excel in any job environment or industry he finds himself in. His experience now includes high end sales experience in commercial and residential real estate, business management, accounting and budgeting, business consultation, strategic planning, marketing, and computer programming and web design. He enjoys assessing current business tactics and then setting goals for improvement of profit and productivity through the adjustment, improvement, and solidification of marketing, technology, and business practices.
His future goals are always to expand his business. He loves sales, and the flexibility it allows, but would also love to do more with executive business consulting, as many well established company owners use Adam as a sounding board for strategies and marketing.

Adam Palmiter's Experience

Sales Agent

Palmiter Realty Group

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Real Estate industry

May 2004 – Present (9 years 8 months)

Adam Palmiter has lived in the Mount Snow area since he moved from Alaska in 1985 and has as intimate knowledge of the region. He graduated from Colgate University in the Spring of 2003 and started a web/graphic design company, Cookie Design Co. After long days in front of the computer screen working with pixels instead of people, Adam decided to give the family business a try. Much to his own surprise he loved it and has successfully applied his background in marketing and computer technology to the everchanging landscape of real estate. He currently resides in Wilmington and stays busy with 5 Men's League sports year round. Adam is a member of the local, state and National Association of Realtors. 2007 and 2010 Agent of the Year. 2010 and 2011 #1 Volume Agent for all of Mount Snow and Southern Vermont.
