Les Tokar's Summary

My passion is solid state drives and has been since the first SanDisk public consumer release in 2007. From there, SSDs literally showed up at my door, unexpectedly and from manufacturers worldwide. At one point, a new hobby grew into a very short-lived blog and then came The SSD Review, a technology website that provides SSD specific education, news and reviews.

Some earlier accolades include the fortune of publishing one of the first consumer SSD reviews on the internet, the starting of an SSD discussion in 07 that has received millions of hits to date and remains to be the largest discussion of its kind on the internet as well as participation in many 'behind the scene' activities that has helped form the SSD presence in storage today.

Today, The SSD Review is home to resource guides on, not only such things as MS Vista and Windows 7 Optimization, but also, our SSD Optimization Guide has met with unexpected success and is viewed by thousands daily.

On arrival, today’s consumer is met right off with our SSD Beginner’s Guide which has several articles, leading them through an understanding of SSDs as well as enabling them to make effective and confident purchase choices. From there they can read our SSD reviews to help in their choice of SSD, follow through with purchase and even fine tune their system with our Optimization Guides.

Business and enterprise needs have also been very well met by our website and we are proud to acknowledge that there are companies that would trust no other with their storage hardware. Having compiled extensive reviews for such companies as Intel, Toshiba, SanDisk, Samsung, Hitachi, LSI, Micron, SMART Storage Systems, and countless others, we just might be the industry leader in enterprise storage reviews.

“The key to this business is personal relationships.” – Dicky Fox.
