Jean Seguin's Summary

I have one of the top 5% most viewed @LinkedIn profiles for 2012. I owe this to http://socialtrendz.ca/ !!
Vancouver real estate expert, Realtor and well-known local former restaurateur Jean Séguin gained his real estate acumen from the ground up. Mr. Séguin dove into the world of real estate in Vancouver in 2001 as an investor. The year 2004 brought even more success his way when he became the owner/operator at The famous Smoking Dog Bistro. This neighborhood French bistro has been a staple in lower Kitsilano for 15 years and Jean has always been proud of the quality product that he offered his customers and local patrons. In November of 2010 Jean sold his booming restaurant to return to the world of real estate, this time as a Realtor.
To what does Jean Séguin attribute his smashing success? Simply stated, his experience and love for building relationships with customers while hosting his own restaurant has aided him in gaining the ability to assist clients in determining what they want, and taking fast action to achieve their goals when searching through Vancouver homes for sale. Fatherhood has not hurt either, and has helped Mr. Séguin to realize and understand what the prestigious Vancouver family desires when sifting through Vancouver luxury real estate. His in-depth understanding of the local economic climate does not hurt his clients either. He learned teamwork while playing hockey in Northern Ontario, and is seen as a valuable asset in this capacity by RE/MAX Crest Realty (Westside). Jean is known for giving 150% at every turn, selling his clients homes with exceptional speed for top dollar, and assisting investors in finding the perfect property with a high return on investment in the Vancouver luxury properties market.
