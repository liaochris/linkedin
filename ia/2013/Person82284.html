Tom Hebert's Summary

With 30 years of commitment to the industry Long Pond Auto Body has been proud to serve the Rochester Community with professional auto body and collision repair service.

No matter what year and model vehicle you own, you can expect nothing less than the best results from our professional team. From a small ding or dent repair, touching up a chip or scratch, you can expect great service and quality workmanship.

Our shop is equipped with state of the art frame machines, paint booths and color match professionals to assure you get your vehicle in the same of not better condition it was prior to your accident.

We will explain in full detail how your vehicle will be repaired. After contacting your insurance company, call on us and we will take care of you from there. We know how tough it is to understand what our customers are entitled to so we will help you get through the process of making your insurance claim. After all, we work for you!

As a consumer the NYS Law protects your rights. So don't let your insurance company tell you that you cannot use certain collision shops just because it is not on their list. It is your right to have your vehicle repaired at the shop of your choice.

Today, Tom and the staff devote all of the business hours to make sure they do the best possible job our customers could ask for. If you are ever in the area feel free to stop in and let us show you why we are one of the most respected and trusted auto body collision shops around. The coffee is always on...stop in!
