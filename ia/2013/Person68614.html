Joseph Bione's Summary

I’ve joined LinkedIn to assist the community with advice, guidance and contacts in the areas of business development, organizational development, turnaround management, business process re-engineering, operations, and P&L management.

Projects we can discuss:

Driving companies' performance from good to great; attaining liquidity events,
Building shareholder equity or turning underperforming businesses into powerful and profitable enterprises
Implementing ambitious initiatives that drive effective change, create new efficiencies and increase top and bottom-line performance

Topics of interest include building upon strengths of sales, marketing, operations and financial teams to establish teamwork and a shared vision. Leading successful organizational and process re-engineering campaigns in environments where results and growth are paramount.

Please feel free to reach out to me directly via LinkedIn mail or send me an email at bione@whitehallgroupllc.com.
