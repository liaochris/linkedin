luke berman's Experience

Project Manager

Energise Barnet

Nonprofit; 1-10 employees; Environmental Services industry

September 2012 – Present (1 year 2 months) Barnet, London, UK

Energise Barnet (EB) is a community-led project to reduce energy wastage in homes and buildings, eradicate fuel poverty and make renewable energy more affordable in London's largest Borough.

Energise Barnet CIC, a social enterprise, has been established to deliver the project.

Working in partnership with Barnet Council, NHS Barnet, community organisations, Carillion Energy Services and others, EB provides a single point of contact for information, assessments, funding and installation of energy saving measures and renewable energy systems that enable property owners and tenants to save and make energy and money.

My roles include:
Community engagement:
Building relationships with health professionals, local authority, community organisations,
charities and businesses.
Managing & recruiting volunteers:
Creating 'Community Energy Champions' that can directly help their local community, not only
with advice, but with actual practical help that can really make a difference; this could be as
simple as knowing how to read your gas/electricity bill correctly.
Delivering training:
Teaching health professionals how to identify patients in fuel poverty, teaching community
organisations how to reduce their energy bills, save money and invest in renewable energy to
create a new income stream.
Organising events.:
Promotional events within the community all about how you can make your home warmer. Meet
the buyer events with Carillion and local home improvement businesses on how they can
generate work from the governments new Green Deal scheme.
