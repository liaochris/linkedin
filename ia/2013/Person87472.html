Marten Schröder's Summary

My experiences are mainly in the field of performance improvement, following a long career in ICT.
Performance improvement in the widest meaning. From personal growth to organisational changes. Performance improvent is everywhere.
The binding factor: behaviour!

To be able to find a bottleneck in any system (be it a team, a project, a department or an individua;), and to elevate that constraint with a 'human touch' with respect to change management is my kick in life!

One of the things I really love to do is enable people to improve their business/ process/themselves. Using workshops, training, coaching, Reiki, massage, intervision and therapeutic sessions I guide people and organisations to become aware.

Inspiring People to Improve !

"Verbeteren is mensenwerk"
