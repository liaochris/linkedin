Christine Cossu's Summary

15+ years of award-winning advertising creative and production experience, working for the in-house marketing and advertising creative teams at Discovery Networks, Sony Music and several post-production boutiques in New York and the DC area- writing and producing broadcast, web, radio and print ads, concept development and presentation, integrated brand campaigns, PSAs, EPKs, program packaging, sizzle reels, music videos, documentaries, social and viral videos.

Collaborates well with strategy and media teams to develop integrated ad campaigns, working fluidly within RFPs and creative briefs to generate unique and creative solutions, pitching/presenting, and executing flawless production of the final deliverables across all media platforms.

Specializes in client relations, film, video and audio production and post-production, branding/brand development, branded entertainment, and supervising graphic design & animation for advertising.

Strength in juggling large and small-scale projects from script to screen across multiple creative and production teams, consistently delivering on time and on budget.
