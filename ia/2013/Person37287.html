Douglas Collins' Summary

[Scottsdale]
BroadcastAZ: HD Live Shots, HD Live Broadcast, HD Streaming, Network Remote Show Capable, Virtual Sets, Video News Releases, Satellite Media Tours

Full HD Production Facility – Two Interior Studios/One Exterior Studio

[Phoenix - Reno - Lake Tahoe] DM Productions LLC: Strategic Marketing, Go Public Transactions, Branding, Social Media, Dynamic Web Design and Online Digital Media Design

BroadcastAZ provides live shot services to markets including breaking news, financial industries, public company communication with financial analysts, annual meetings and shareholder media advisories, along with satellite media tours. Virtual set design provides high production value to webinars, streaming training sessions and commercials.

BroadcastAZ has recently completed construction of our Studio B – a 1200 sq. ft. network-style environment that is capable of live broadcasts of remote network programs in the Scottsdale/Metro Phoenix area. It is fully equipped with the TriCaster 855 Professional Switcher, live graphic production, up to eight HD cameras, microphones, wireless IFBs, green screen, green room, catering services and more. We have also opened our Exterior Pool/Patio Studio for the season – details can be found on our website at BroadcastAZ.com under The Latest News.

Where HD video is the medium of choice, BroadcastAZ boasts a fully-equipped broadcast center, capable of televising live High Definition 1080i, Anamorphic High Definition or Standard Definition signals via a dedicated high speed video pipeline to the Azzurro HD Hub in New York City. The signal is then available to more than 200 major networks including ABC, CBS, NBC, Fox, CNN, ESPN and global networks as well. Studio A is for live shots or ‘talking head’ broadcasts while Studio B is a fully equipped, 1200 sq.ft. HD studio for up to 8 HD camera shoots with the TriCaster 855 Professional Switcher, the RoboPrompter teleprompter and green screen.
