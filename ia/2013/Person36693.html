Donald Macnab-Stark's Summary

I am a screenwriter, script doctor and consultant living in Essex, England, specializing in high concept comedies and thrillers.

I have consulted with hundreds of writers, producers and directors in the past five years, helping them to improve and develop their scripts.

I have written over fifty feature scripts, including:

· Dangerous Days – True-life World War II thriller. Producers include Michael Deeley (Blade Runner; The Italian Job; The Deerhunter) and David Wicht (The Last House on the Left; Starship Troopers 3). http://www.dangerous-days.com/

· Rabid – I co-wrote Planet Four Films latest feature film, Rabid, a thriller filmed in Michigan (Director Brian Lawrence). Also a co-writer on Lawrence’s next feature, Politics of Street Crime.

· Blood Curse – Horror script Blood Curse, written with Norwegian producer Martin Molstead has been purchased by Talking Lens Productions. A second script written with Martin, The Virgin Snatchers, has been purchased by Soma Pictures, while a third, The Rig, has been optioned by Starfront Pictures.

· Act Like You Love Me & Hope for Love: Two scripts written for indie producer Dan Garcia. Hope for Love shot in New Orleans in early 2012, while production on wedding comedy, Act Like You Love Me recently wrapped in Malibu.


Production: I am currently producing several films through my production company, Donroy Films:

As well as developing horror film Scarehouse with Canadian partner Rhys Trenhaile:
