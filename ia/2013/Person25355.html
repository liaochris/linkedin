Clare Ultimo's Summary

Interdisciplinary Design professional, with over 20 years experience.

With Clare Ultimo Inc.I began exploring green and socially conscious design in late 80s. I also work as a independent communications consultant for a wide variety of corporate and not-for-profit clients.

My longstanding personal passion and study of "alternative" thinking and healing has led to an expertise in all things beyond the mainstream.
