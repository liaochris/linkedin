Jessica Sabbah's Summary

I have a strong passion for journalism - both online and print. I currently work as a multimedia artist at the Rockford Register Star. I'm a previous Scripps Howard Foundation Wire multimedia fellow. I've held internships at the Washington Post, the Daily Herald and Suburban Life Publications. I'm a 2011 graduate of Northern Illinois University, where I double majored in journalism and media studies communications with a minor in Spanish. While at NIU, I worked at the daily college student newspaper, the Northern Star, in a variety of positions including managing editor and online editor.

Specialties

Multimedia production, web producing, writing, copy editing, design and social media.
