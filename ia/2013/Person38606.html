Duane Craig's Summary

I'm reporting and writing on technology, construction, finance, food, and agriculture, spanning both print and online. That includes more than 80 trade articles in print, well over 1,500 blog posts and more than 50 articles online and in print with publishers such as the Washington Post, Tech Republic, BYTE, Tech Target and A New Domain. I'm also copywriting for leading brands in the food sector, technical writing for an application developer in the AEC sector, newsletter and special event writing/editing/designing in the finance sector, and press release writing for a range of industries. In the fiction genre I've been writing short stories and am currently working on a science fiction/fantasy novel. I'm a contributor to the UpWorld blog and have two blogs in the construction sector.
