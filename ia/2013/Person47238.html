Gary Thomas' Summary

For more than 30 years, I have helped businesses realize the full potential of their products by applying creative approaches to product, solutions, channel and direct marketing. At the heart of my skills is a passion for creating competitively-differentiated sales and marketing strategies.

My experience includes Marketing Manager at Extreme Networks, Marketing Director at Teneros, Marketing Manager at Cisco Systems, Product Marketing Director at Nortel Networks and Bay Networks, Solutions Marketing Manager at SynOptics Communications, Group Manager for Network Products at AT&T Bell Laboratories and Field Support Engineering at Verizon.

I have been responsible for sales readiness and marketing to the Fortune 1000 for product lines that generated in excess of US$1.4 billion and have developed new channels through direct marketing, cross-selling, selective partner recruiting and applying Internet technologies.

My networking experience includes Gigabit Ethernet, Fast Ethernet, Ethernet, ATM, FDDI; routers, switches, appliances, hubs, wireless, and network management.

My security experience includes firewalls, IP-SEC, URL filters, VPN, SSL VPN, NAC, Cisco IOS Intrusion Prevention System (IPS). My Voice over IP (VoIP) experience includes: Functions (PBX, IVR), Media (PoE, VoBB), and Gateways (SIP, H.323, PSTN/IP).

My wireless experience includes WiFi, 802.11b/g/n, 2G/3G/GSM, and Cisco Land Mobile Radio (LMR).

For more information, please visit http://thomasland.org/resume where you can download HTML, DOC or PDF versions of my resume.
