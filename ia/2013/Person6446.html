Andrea Ford's Summary

"Authenticity always." That's been the motto for my career. This mantra helped me tap into the true personality of a brand as a writer. Now, I find myself doing less writing and more teaching and training. i find tremendous personal satisfaction in helping organizations scale, one concise sentence at a time.

Turns out that when you love what you do, it shows. Read through my recommendations and you’ll find a vibrant, hard-working creative professional who’s just as passionate about the details as she is about strategy. I'd love to strike it rich by bottling my energy and selling it to others, but until that happens, I'll just continue being true to myself and finding the fun in life's nooks and crannies.

Specialties: brand voice, brand strategy, strategic branding, authentic branding, content strategy, web content, website writing, content management systems, information architecture, websites, user experience, social media, corporate training, instructional design, e-learning
