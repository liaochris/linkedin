Mary Mansey's Projects

  • SIAC Marseilles, France

    • February 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Mary Mansey

    The 13th edition of the SIAC will take place from the 8th to the 11th March 2013 at the “Parc Chanot” in the hall “Palais des Evénements” (ex Hall 1). The year 2013 is particularly important since Marseilles as the Capital of European Culture will be a centre of international interest.

  • Faure Museum Solo Exhibition - October - December 2013

    • May 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Mary Mansey

    The Faure Museum is pleased to present the first solo exhibition in France of works by French artist Mary Mansey. The exhibition features more than 30 major works made during the last decade.

    The Faure Museum is an art museum situated at Aix les Bains in France in the department of Savoie. It was founded in 1949 and comprised initially artworks from the private collection of Doctor Jean Faure (1862–1942), bequeathed to the city. The Faure Museum possesses the second collection in France of works by Rodin and the second collection of impressionist paintings of France.
