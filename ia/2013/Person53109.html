Hilary Weber's Summary

Hilary Weber is passionate about startups. Her mission is to enable promising entrepreneurs, co-founders and startup teams to develop their leadership abilities and collaboration skills so that their new companies will grow and thrive, which in turn fuels the community at large. She comes from a long line of entrepreneurs that goes back (literally) hundreds of years.

Hilary is a Berkeley-Haas MBA, seasoned business leader, entrepreneur, leadership consultant and experienced professional coach, facilitator, trainer and speaker. She is the founder of Opportu Startup Leadership Consulting, a nexus organization of skilled professionals that provides leadership development/training, organizational design/development consulting and customized coaching at the individual, co-founder, team and organizational levels.

Prior to founding Opportu, Hilary pioneered social media and digital marketing strategy and ran departments in both small and large enterprises across industries, mainly in healthcare, information management/technology and publishing. In those roles, her own leadership approach encompassed being a mentor, coach and trainer to hundreds of colleagues, direct reports and senior executives, and she also hired multiple coaches and leadership consultants for her own teams over the years. She has presented at many national conferences and other venues, has written multiple print and online articles; additionally, Hilary is now writing her first book on leadership.

Hilary is an adjunct coach at Berkeley-Haas, a Venture Advisor at Skydeck Accelerator and an Advisor for Astia. She co-founded Social Enterprise Talks (SET), a monthly event in San Francisco. She lives in San Francisco with her husband and youngest daughter; the oldest is at Stanford.
