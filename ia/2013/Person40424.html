Elene Schou Straumsheim's Summary

I have worked within the health sector for over three decades; the majority of these running my own clinic as a private physiotherapist.During this time I have interviewed and made assessments of over 10 000 persons. My speciality is motivational techniques and stress relief programs in addition to regular rehabilitation procedures.

I started Zodiac Coaching in 2008 after graduating as a ACC (Accredited Certified Coach) from The Coaches Training Institute.

My coaching is based on personality tests of the client, so that we ensure a scientific approach to what kind of change is possible for the coachee.

In 2010 I was certified by Det Norske Veritas after completing an extensive course at Oslo Business School in "Psychological Testing & Recruitment". Following this certification, I started the recruitment and headhunting agency Rekruttering Innlandet AS.

Rekruttering Innlandet AS offers executive coaching with implementation of lasting and productive change for the individual and the organization.

We manage recruitment processes using evidence based methods and use extensive data bases in our search for the best candidates. We also offer tests designed for career counselling for individuals and team development processes. Our test provider Cut-e is one of the worlds largest online test providers and offer a large number of both ability tests and personal profile tests based on work competency skills. For further information take a look at:
http://www.cut-e.no/

Elene hjalp meg å få oversikt over mulighetene, konsekvenser av valg og nye veier. Hun hjalp meg til å bli trygg på de valgene jeg hadde tatt og skulle ta. Elene er flink til å se hva som er problemet og gripe fatt i det og hun var veldig dyktig til å motivere meg til å gjøre noe med det. Hun lærte meg også å se tydligere hva som var viktig for meg og hva som ikke er så viktig. Jeg er henne evig takknemelig for at hun lærte meg å se meg selv tydligere og hvilke ressurser jeg har.”
