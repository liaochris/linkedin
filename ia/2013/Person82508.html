Lorillia Brown-Phillips' Summary

Lorillia Brown-Phillips is an impactful Financial Literacy Educator, Empowerment Speaker and Published Author.

You can find Lorillia providing her expertise regularly on Your Black World and Black Life Coaches.

Lorillia regularly provides personal finance content on her blog at YourMoneyMentor.com

Lorillia's mission and goal is to eradicate Financial Iliteracy, by equipping individuals with the learning skills necessary to increase their Financial IQ. She advocates that you cannot build wealth if you have not mastered the beginning fundamentals of personal finance.

Lorillia is also the author of "Jump Start you Credit: How to Negotiate and Settle your Debts in 10 Step"
