Jeremy Heid's Summary

Big Creek Productions is a video production company based in Northern Colorado. It's our goal to provide high quality video services at an affordable rate to both businesses and organizations. I also work as a freelance director of photography and producer. I've worked on shows that have aired on networks such as NBC Sports, The Outdoor Channel, Sportsman Channel, Versus Network, RFD-TV, HRTV and more.

In 2012 I have already worked on some fantastic shows from Hawaii all the way to the Bahamas and all stops in between. I've been honored to work with illustrious personalities such as Tom Brokaw, Yvon Chouinard of Patagonia Clothing Company, actors Michael Keaton and Jeff Foxworthy and many other great people who's names you'd never recognize.

I've had the great honor of filming the Hawaiian paniolos (cowboys) who have mastered their craft for over 200 years on the side of 13,796 foot tall Mauna Kea Volcano. I literally filmed in awe as incomparable fly fishing guides propelled their skiffs past sting rays and sharks in search of bonefish on the neverending saltwater flats of the Bahamas. I have watched the sun rise and set in no less than a dozen states and over two great oceans already this year and it's only August.

What else does 2012 have in store? I know for sure I will be making trips to places like Oregon, Nevada, California, Iowa, Nebraska, Kansas, Wyoming, Arkansas, Saskatchewan and British Columbia and of course the best place of all . . . my home state of Colorado. This career has been a gift and I intend to treasure it every moment I can, no matter where I find myself next!

Specialties

Filming hunting, fishing, travel, expedition and adventure television. I am also handy at Editing, Post Production, and Conceptualizing Projects
