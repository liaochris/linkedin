Melissa Marro's Summary

Melissa Marro is one of the nation’s leading experts in Home Staging. Moving to Florida in 2011, Marro sold her award winning company, First Impressions Home Staging. With a strong business focus on Marketing, Social Media, and Search Engine Optimization, Marro is an ideal Listing Agent.

Marro’s Charleston based home staging company was nationally known and recognized for its ability to significantly decrease days on market for customers, typically 75% less DOM, as well as ensure sale prices typically 2-6% higher than the statistical neighborhood averages. Targeted home staging and a strong internet presence helped leverage customers’ products and brands. Marro’s Home Staging company was used by individual homeowners, local home builders, and national home builders to help them routinely beat market statistics. Eight of the top ten top agents of the largest real estate company, Carolina one Real Estate, used Marro’s company for their listings.

Transforming the skills from her home staging business, Marro utilizes the best marketing presentation combined with a strong online presence to help sell listings quickly, for top dollar, while also helping develop branding and PR for builder customers.
