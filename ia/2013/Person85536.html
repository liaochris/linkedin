Margaret Croom's Summary

WHAT I DO NOW
* Fullstack Marketer; Communication Strategist specializing in B2C/B2B solutions focusing on web technologies, data integration, data analysis, business intelligence, mobile marketing and B2C/B2B digital marketing.

* Created a niche marketing tool called Nosey Parker and continue to propagate permission-based marketing to a specific type of female shopper. I produce guidebooks/online weeklies/blogs/social media/apps to promote top notch local shopping to women. WOM and connective conversations in fashion, beauty and lifestyle. My market is medium size cities that draw shoppers because of their location within their state and tourist traffic. Currently my markets are Oklahoma City and the Inland NW.

I also provide virtual counsel to businesses that need to have a daily online presence. I analyze their audience and create a plan that provides ROI. I have written a proprietary social media package that includes a copywriting service and response marketing tools.

Specialties: Strategic Planning, Integrated Marketing, Permission Marketing, Virtual Counsel, Social Media, Competitive Intelligence, Web-based/Enterprise Consulting, Creative, Branding and Identity, Planning and Project Management; Information Management; OAO, SEO, SEM; Content Management

WHAT I DID BEFORE
