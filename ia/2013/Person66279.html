John Drexler, SPHR's Experience

Director, HR/OD Consultant

Ronin Venture Partners Inc.

1997 – Present (16 years)

I am the founder, managing director, and lead consultant of Ronin Venture Partners, a San Francisco/ Silicon Valley management consulting firm specializing in Human Resources, Organizational Development, Leadership Development, Executive Coaching, Business Development, and Retained Search. Referrals from current clients and colleagues are my primary source of new clients.

My practice includes frequent back-filling of HR positions that are open due to company growth, joint venture, spin-off, special projects, new headcount or sudden resignation, or have incumbents out on maternity or other leave. I have temporarily filled, part-time or full-time, HR positions at the Generalist, Manager, Director and VP levels.

Over the course of my twenty-plus years of business experience, I have developed a deep and wide portfolio of skills and experience in all areas of Human Resources, including recruiting, staffing, engagement and retention, performance management, compensation, incentives, benefits, HRIS, metrics/ analytics, employee relations, employment law, and training and development. I have successfully functioned in the 'solo HR' role, as an individual contributor/ team player in larger organizations, as a manager of teams of a dozen or more employees, and as the senior-most HR leader at companies (part of C-suite executive team).

Similarly, I have considerable skills and experience in Organizational Development and Leadership Development, including executive sounding board, improving management team effectiveness, conducting organizational assessments and needs analyses, key employee coaching and development, goals/ roles/ processes focus and alignment, strategic planning, intrapreneurship/ entrepreneurship (including coaching teams writing business plans), ghostwriting executive communications, meeting planning and facilitation, conflict resolution, and planning, implementing and sustaining organizational change (renewal, restructuring, and M&A).
