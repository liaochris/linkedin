Jay Daugherty's Projects

  • NAA University

    • September 2009 to Present
    Team Members: Jay Daugherty, Nathan Staton, ryan wagner, Matthew Taflan, Westin Lehman, Ed Terrell, Scott Scerri

    NAA University is a supplemental training program for newly licensed life insurance agents. The original program contained six training modules covering a range of topics including life insurance sales training, product training, and business management. Information was delivered in online text, online video, print text, and DVD form, with a timed exam based on the information presented. Results consistently showed that students who completed the supplemental training program saw significant increases in the amount of insurance business they were able to produce. Enhancements in 2013 allowed the program to offer smaller individual courses at a lower cost of entry, and is being re-structured to more closely resemble an actual university, with required courses and electives being offered at multiple experience levels.
