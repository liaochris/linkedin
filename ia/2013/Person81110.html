LINDA SPRY's Experience

Interior Designer

Linda Spry Interior Design

February 1998 – Present (15 years 8 months) Sarasota, Florida Area

Linda Spry has been involved with the interior design profession for most of her life. At present, she has offices in Sarasota, Florida and Hendersonville-Asheville, North Carolina, as well as working throughout the United States on various projects.
As a member of the American Society of Interior Designers (ASID), Allied, Linda applies her decorative talents and strong management skills to create a premier design business.
“Linda Spry Design Interiors works with clients from every walk of life and from any geographical location. I believe in an open line of communication, and I also compile detailed customer files and have ongoing conversations with my clients with clear goals in mind. From our very first consultation to its completion, your project is as important to me as it is to you. Please contact me with any questions you might have concerning a project that I can help you with.”
