Cliff Feightner's Summary

Cliff Feightner is currently Principal Publisher at FlaAuthor Publishing, LLC.

Since his retirement from his I.T. career, Cliff has written two books. His first was “Lynn’s Story” – a story documenting his wife Lynn’s courageous losing battle with Renal Cell Carcinoma. His second is “Views from Sandhausen – Experiences from a Foreign Service Assignment”. This funny, informative and engaging story spans their three years assignment in Germany. “Views” for Kindle is available now. http://tiny.cc/c64zt . Other formats, including hardcove will be available during Spring 2011.

Cliff was a Senior Information Technology and Program Management Professional with a Fortune 100 company, and a Certified Computing Professional whose career has primarily focused on Professional Project and Program Management in domestic and international settings.
Until last year, he was married (43 years) to his lovely wife Lynn. They have two children and four grandchildren and he lives in Homosassa, Florida. His work has been published in Europe and in the United States. He is a Certified Computing Professional (CCP), a member of the Project Management Institute, MENSA and Intertel.

He enjoys reading, writing, walking, fishing, boating, bicycling, and all manner of Technology pursuits.
