Eric Schlesinger's Summary

Highly driven Senior Sales Management Professional that understand the importance of Solutions Selling, SaaS and how technology plays an important role in allowing business' to achieve new levels. Eric has been involved with the Automotive industry since he was 16 years old and started as a lot attendant. Eric has held almost every position in the dealership world including a Director level position in an 8 store dealer group.

Eric was with izmocars from the infancy. He quickly became a leader. Eric became the first Regional Director and eventually took on the roll of National Director of Sales. Eric was instrumental in the sales success of the company. Eric was the first Account Executive to achieve $1 Million in sales in a year. As a regional Director, Eric continued to deliver and the company recognized 73% of its sales from the Eastern Region. Eric took this same success to his position of National Director of Sales.

Most Recently, Eric has been the Head of Sales for ActivEngage, inc. In this position, Eric has taken the company to new sales levels through hard work, great strategy and Excellence in Execution. In 2011 ActivEngage was named the #121 fastest growing private company in America by Inc 500 and was awarded the Hire Power Award for promoting and hiring American workers. Eric has been influential in forging relationships with General Motors, South East Toyota, Volkswagen, Audi USA, Chrysler Group World Wide, Hendrick Auto Group, Penske Auto Group, AutoByTel, and Van Tuyl to name a few. This growth has continued through 2012 and helped AE achieve new levels.

Specialties: Solutions Selling, SaaS, Contract Negotiations, Sales Strategy, Pipeline Management, Organizational Leadership, Territory Growth and Development, High-Impact Sales Presentations
