Mark Waldo's Summary

I am an intuitive and creative user experience leader and strategist with over 15 years of experience. I have an unusual ability to move easily between business strategy and user experience design. By spanning both, I am able to understand businesses at their highest level, identify solutions and then direct the user experience design and process design necessary to execute on those solutions. My skills have helped me serve startups, agencies, and large corporations across a wide range of industries.

Additionally I've mentored with national leaders in user experience design and base my work on a solid foundation of research and empathy for the end user/audience/customer.

My clients have also benefited from my senior level experience in business development, team leadership, process design, teaching innovation, collaboration and design thinking.
