Ben Gross' Summary

I have worked on both operational and research sides of infrastructure in enterprise, academic, government, and startup environments.

My PhD is in Library and Information Science from the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC). My dissertation focused on the intersection of identity management, security, and user experience and how social, technical, and policy domains influence and shape behavior.

I am a researcher at the Highlands Group and the online editor for Messaging News where I write the "On Message" column.

Specialties

Enterprise infrastructure, security, identity management, user experience, and messaging systems.
