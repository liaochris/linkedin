Ingrid Lezar's Summary

I hold a BA in Visual Communication Design and a Cambridge-certified CELTA. My filter on the world picks up on the relationships between pictures and words, the difference between an en and an em dash, and lots of other language-related titbits.

Specialties: Research. Communication. Illustration, design, and working with Photoshop and InDesign. Writing and editing. Teaching English as a foreign language.
