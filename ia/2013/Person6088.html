Amy Wolff, C.I.D.'s Summary

Amy Wolff Interiors offers interior design and decorating solutions with the results you dream about...coming home to an inviting, functional space that enhances your lifestyle…a place that feels good to you…where you look forward to spending your precious personal time.

Is this you? Busy professionals often don’t have the time, desire, or skill to do an interior design project themselves. They are seeking an easy-to-work-with professional who will take care of their interior design project with integrity, creativity, and sensitivity to their needs.

Amy provides expertise in all aspects of an interior design or decorating project on a flat fee basis – whether you simply need help with the planning, or with selections for a whole house remodeling project, or anything in between.

• Interior Design and Decorating – Single consultations to full interior re-decorating
• Custom Window Treatments & Bedding Ensembles – Gorgeous fabric and decorative treatments
• Kitchen & Bath Remodeling – Design/selection of all components, collaborate with contractors
• Color Scheme & Materials Planning – Paint color selection, wall treatments, flooring selections
• Room Arranging/Space Planning – What goes where and how to maximize functionality
• Home Furnishings, Accessories & Art Sourcing – Custom-made items to “off the floor”
• Outdoor “Room” Decorating – Just as important as your indoor spaces

Phone and email inquiries are welcome!
