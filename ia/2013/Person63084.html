Jessica Kasevich, MSW LCSW's Summary

I am Jessica Kasevich and I am a Psychotherapist. My private practice JK Therapy helps people manage life’s challenges and shows you the way through many issues that are the normal complements of urban life and personal ambition.

Most of us would love to move forward in certain areas of our life but, let’s face it, sometimes we lose our direction, get stuck or thrown off-track. New experiences and unexpected situations pop up— some good and some not so good. THAT'S LIFE. How we handle them is what makes us who we are and, often, leads to real challenges. There is no shame in admitting “I have no idea how to do this” when you’ve never done it before.

I created JK Therapy to provide a safe, comfortable environment and an empathetic ear to help you with both the day-to-day issues and deeply rooted challenges that keep you from experiencing the happiness that is within you. Working together you can overcome what seems overwhelming and make the connection to your best self. I will help you map your life’s route and guide you through the milestones.

I have a Master’s Degree in Clinical Social Work from NYU and years of experience helping people from middle adolescence through retirement age who are dealing with life’s milestones, experiencing undue stress, or facing relationship issues.

I offer interactive, psychodynamic therapy and short-term therapy to individuals, families, and couples in person, on the phone, or via SKYPE.

"I look forward to helping you achieve your goals and remember: "All the Happiness you ever find lies in you."

Contact me today at JK Therapy,
to find the best route to your true self and
get your life back on track!
