Shannon Alter, CPM, CDEI's Summary

Shannon Alter has been a business leader in the real estate management arena for over 25 years, and has held executive leadership positions in the office, retail and hotel property sectors. She has created and developed all aspects of ground-up property and asset management, and her expertise includes strategies for winning new clients, building teams and developing new business. She is a results-driven thought leader who views every point of customer contact as a new opportunity to build rapport and relationships.

Shannon is a national instructor for both IREM and the American Management Association and has presented throughout the U.S, and in Russia, Ukraine, China, The Republic of Georgia and South Korea.

Her column, “Marketing Solutions” appears monthly in the Journal of Property Management. She is a contributor to SmartBrief on Leadership and The Orange County Register and is the author of two books. She is a Board member for California State University, Fullerton’s Real Estate & Land Use Institute and also serves on the Board for Laurel House, a home for teenage girls in crisis.

Shannon is a graduate of the University of Southern California.
