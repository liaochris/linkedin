Aarni Heiskanen's Summary

I worked for architectural design companies for 15 years, and started as an Internet entrepreneur in 1998. In the 2000s I've been developing online services for A/E/C companies at AE Partners.

I'm also a partner at Thinking Business, a company that develops online tools for project and service portfolio management.

I'm a business book author, blogger, and podcaster. My rewarded blog is AEC Business (aec-business.com), and the podcast with the same title is on iTunes.

Specialties: Service strategies and innovation, service development, customer needs analysis, professional services, SaaS development, AEC industry, real estate, project portfolio management
