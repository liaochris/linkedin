Mackenzie Blair's Summary

Moving is one of the most stressful and biggest decisions anyone will ever make. If you are considering moving, you might be wondering:

► What can a Real Estate Agent really do for me?
► What kind of house will I get for my money in the Houston area?
► How negotiable are the sales prices?
► What is the best amount to put down?
► How long will this process take?

As a Houston Realtor, my full-time job is to help both buyers and sellers have a smooth and successful transaction. This means I will track down those homes that meet your specific criteria and take care of negotiations, representing your best interests in tough transactions without ruffling any feathers. Dealing with contracts on a regular basis, I know which conditions should be used or safely removed to make sure the contract offers you complete protection, whether you're buying or selling your home.

In a market that as big as Houston, having someone on your side who knows the market and has the right tools can make all the difference. It is my honor to be your trusted and recommended guide. A 4th generation Texan, I have deep roots in the community and my finger on the pulse of the fast growing and changing Houston market.

My expansive knowledge of the industry has been gained though relocating corporate clients for over 5 years, specializing in historic homes, townhomes and condos in the Inner Loop area, such as The Heights and Montrose. I enjoy living and officing so close to Houston's best attractions, historical landmarks, scenic running and biking trails, and the finest downtown restaurants.

I would love the opportunity to show you around!

Customer Service is the foundation of my work ethic. Doing the best work for each and every client always comes ahead of my commission. I strongly believe in being a good steward of real estate with a moral and honest approach.
