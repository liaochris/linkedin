Amanda Joy Ravenhill's Summary

I am a creative and passionate businesswoman dedicated to stabilizing the climate by promoting sustainable business solutions.

I am the founder of The Biochar Association, which promotes the growth of the biochar industry, a carbon negative, renewable fuel source and soil amendment.

I also teach systems thinking and the principles of sustainable business to promising MBA candidates who are focused on using business as a positive, scalable force for change.

I am deeply inspired by the life of Buckminster Fuller and his vision of "Universe". I share his goal to "make the world work for 100% of humanity in the shortest possible time through spontaneous cooperation without ecological offense or disadvantage to anyone".

Specialties: sustainable business, biochar, graphic recording, biomimcry, impact investing, financing for SMEs in emerging markets, community organizing, GIS, meeting facilitation, design thinking, full-cost accounting, ecological economics, microfranchising, base of the pyramid, micro-gasification, cookstoves,
