John Eades' Summary

While searching for the best way to sell, I discovered Solution Selling as the perfect methodology to align with today's buyer. Solution Selling is a great way to engage new or existing customers, WHEN USED PROPERLY!

I have studied everything about SELLING effectively in today's environment and I wouldn't sell without knowledge of the following:

80% of Buyers start the sales process WITHOUT a sales person's involvement

57% of the purchasing decision is made because the engagement with a sales representative (conversations are critical)

Social Selling is critical to getting back in front of the customer and influencing their thinking and it's here to stay

A Defined Buyer Aligned Sales Process (a definition of what a good sale looks like) is the most repeated way for an organization to sell efficiently and effectively and its how customers want to buy

Typically the clients I work with are...

SALES LEADERSHIP that want to drive measurable and sustainable organic revenue growth within their existing sales team. The focus being on a 30, 60, 90 day results oriented approach by measuring specific success metrics.

LEARNING AND DEVELOPMENT that would like their sales force to improve their sales skills, learn how to sell solutions, use social media to fill the pipeline, or improve presentation techniques in front of CXO's.

SALES PROFESSIONALS that want to improve their overall skill set to improve their pipeline, drive more revenue, and make numbers.
