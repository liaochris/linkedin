Christopher Beaux's Summary

With over thirty years of construction and residential design experience, Christopher Beaux designs and builds quality homes that are nothing less than spectacular. From the basic single-family homes to the upper-end custom homes as well as multi-family developments, Chris has constructed a full range of residential housing with the highest level of finish. Regardless of style or budget, Chris has earned a reputation for innovative design and quality construction. Working closely with clients and their lending institutions, Chris understands the need to complete projects in a timely manner while staying within financial guidelines.

Christopher Beaux has designed over 3000 single-family, multi-family and vacation homes for contractors and homeowners alike. His skill as a designer has been featured in several publications including Professional Builder magazine. Although most of his business career has been mostly spent in Idaho, Chris has designed homes for clients in Alaska, Oregon, Colorado, California, Montana, Washington and Canada.

Chris's latest project is bidjobber.com. Bidjobber.com represents a breakthrough for General Contractors, Sub-Contractors, Suppliers, Service Providers and Homeowners; giving them a powerful set of tools to facilitate the bid-management process, find new work, get material quotes and ensure the best possible service for the best possible price.

“Bidjobber.com essentially combines the elements of several bid sites, networking sites, and construction classifieds into one easy-to-use online program – thus streamlining the bid process, saving time and money, and reducing the need for printed materials.”
