Karen Tiede's Summary

An artist with a business background, I teach Marketing with Pinterest to business owners and marketing managers. Live classes in the Raleigh area, internet classes globally, and Pinterest done-for-you services for businesses that don't see how to make Pinterest work in their market, as well as support for websites that need help converting the traffic you get from Pinterest. (See more at www.red-tuxedo.com)

I am a production weaver of rugs made from locally sourced, 100% recycled clothing and other textiles. I have been marketing rugs and other art through fairs and shows. When I put my art into Pinterest, my web traffic (and better, total time-on-site) exploded, and I began teaching other business owners how to benefit from the traffic Pinterest can send their way.

Art commissions: If you have a pile of souvenir t shirts you'll never wear but don't want to discard, contact me to discuss turning them into a bedside rug your feet will love. I can return a completed rug, or a DIY kit. Similarly, rugs can be created to coordinate with existing color stories, using both your scraps and my stash, as required. (see more at www.rugsfromrags.com)

Outside of my professional work, I'm also a (hula) hoop dance instructor. I've been known to carry hoops to some classes for an interesting study break. In warm months, you can find me at street fairs and festivals in the area. Look for the giant hula hoops and happy people! (See more at www.karentiede.com)
