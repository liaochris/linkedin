Margy Sweeney's Summary

Business communication - PR, marketing, thought leadership development and writing of all kinds - is critical to business growth, and it's what I do. I specialize in three verticals: commercial real estate, financial services and sustainability. In these fields, I bring strong ideas to life through clear communication, strategic thinking and strong writing – in-print, on-line and on-screen.

I lead an 8-person team of senior business content creators, and leverage their experience and my own for our clients. We know our subject matter inside and out, and can partner with you to connect your ideas with clients and prospects.

Specialties

PR, ghostwriting, marketing strategy, web copywriting, clear business communication for all channels. Sectors: sustainability/green, economic development, workplace trends, commercial real estate, and financial services
