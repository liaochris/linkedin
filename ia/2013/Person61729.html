Jennie Y. Chen's Summary

I've been called the Jane Goodall of behaviorists (open to interpretation) or the mash up of psychology meets technology. I'm a strong believer that specialization is for insects. Humans are generalists, and we can excel in many areas. I'm also a skills, experience, and hobby collector.

I like analyzing data, building relationships (online and offline), creating unique experiences, studying culture and behavior across communities, and creating web content. If you have a question, I'll probably answer it with data.

Specialties:
• Social Media Enthusiast and Content Creator
• Creative idea and unique experience strategist and executor
• College Lecturer
• Capillary and Venous Blood Collection, DNA Sample Specialist, and radioimmunoassay of hormones
• Calibrated Peer Review System (CPR) - trained on the CPR system implemented to manage writing assignments and integrate evaluation practice in large classes
• Proficient with the statistical packages: SPSS, SAS, LISREL, AMOS, Hierarchical Linear Modeling (HLM), SQL, R, and Sawtooth
• Understanding and experience with C++, Javascript,and CSS
