Adam Garone's Summary

A pretty mixed assortment of employment starting out with service as an officer in the Australian Army, then with the Australian special forces where I learned and refined the art of leadership and intricacies of operations management.

Then followed a series of roles developing, managing and marketing emerging web and mobile technologies where I developed a passion for the smart application of technology to create highly profitable and innovative business models.

Then my brother challenged me to grow a moustache in 2003 for the first ever Movember. That moment changed my life, it inspired me and the other founders to establish Movember as a men's health campaign, a foundation and a global movement. I'm proud to say that moment has also changed the lives of hundreds of thousands of men from across the world.

MO ON!

Specialties: Global business development; marketing and branding; public relations; collaborations and partnership development, developing and inspiring grassroots movements, thought leadership surrounding emerging technologies; social media marketing.

2004 – Present (9 years) Venice, California

People often ask me: So what do you do for the other 11 months of the year.....?

To run a global charitable organization that raised $140 Million (USD) in 2012 in 21 countries requires year round focus, trust me!

As Global CEO I'm leading our passionate team spread across six offices, driving innovation to keep each campaign fresh, fun and relevant, driving efficiencies to minimize our cost base, working with our beneficiary partners to ensure the funds we raise go to programs that will achieve our vision of having an everlasting impact on the face of men's health. I'm working on which countries we expand into, looking at the risks the organization faces and how we navigate around them.

I'm travelling a lot, promoting Movember and men's health.

Each Movember you'll find me growing a moustache.
