David Mc Connon's Summary

Visual creativity has been my main activity since childhood. Originally trained in engineering, I switched to creative media in the late 90’s armed with a portfolio of artwork and no formal training/qualifications. Since then I have qualified with B.A. (Ord) in Communications & Creative Multimedia 2009 and B.A. (Hons) Video & Film Production in 2010. My experience in video and multimedia production includes graphics, animation, interactivity and DVD authoring.

Highly creative, I am particularly strong at concepts and ideation, creating imaginative yet practical solutions for promotional video and short film. Strong concepts and engaging content are my trademark. As a scriptwriter, I have written several prize-winning short films and have been twice short-listed for the RTÉ/FilmBase Short Script Awards. Videos include projects for the Enterprise Board, European Commission, Irish Wildlife Trust and NASA.

My interest in photography, cinema and nature compliment my main activities in video and multimedia, as well as keeping my active imagination busy and healthy!
