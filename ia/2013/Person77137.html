Kristin Nelson's Summary

I have a passion for marketing.

I began my marketing journey by dressing up as the Great Root Bear and standing road-side, waving customers into my family's A&W restaurant. Real grass roots.

I campaigned for my mom as she was in the Wis. State Assembly. We were in every parade in Waupaca County, went door-to-door with literature and yard signs and hosted numerous fundraisers for 10 years. (I even met my husband at one of our fundraisers!)

Now-a-days, I will go to any networking event I can get my hands on. I love to talk to people and hear their stories. I want to help small to large businesses grow through innovative and strategic marketing ideas.

Specialties

I have a specialized expertise in social media and how businesses can use it to grow their brand and their business.
