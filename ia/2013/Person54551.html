Owner and Podiatrist

Haro Podiatry Centers

January 2001 – Present (12 years 8 months) Clifton NJ

Dr. Haro is a board certified podiatrist. He has been practicing podiatric medicine in his two offices located in Clifton and Jersey City NJ since 2001. The company vision is to help his patients walk, live and play without pain in their feet. At Haro Podiatry, we speak English, Spanish and Arabic!

Chief Resident

Beach Medical Center

January 1998 – December 2000 (3 years) New York

His post graduate training included two years at Long Beach Medical Center, New York. He rose to become Chief Resident and received The Resident of The Year award upon graduation in 2000. He spent his last year of training at Maimonides Medical Center, Brooklyn with emphasis on surgical training and diabetic foot care.
