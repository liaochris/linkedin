Maribel Adame's Summary

I am currently a Relationships Manager at the World Economic Forum in the department of Global Health. My interests are in health and technology, e-health, medical devices, medical imaging and applied research. I have worked extensively in Product Management and Clinical Education in the areas of radiology, cardiology and radiotherapy. I have experience working within hospital (clinical and research) environments with medical professionals and Key Opinion Leaders, and leading cross-functional teams (sales, marketing, production, quality assurance and accounting). Other areas of expertise are strategic and operational marketing, business development, sales support, teaching, market research and global health challenges. I like to see my work as a bridge between unmet needs and technology/business solutions, using creativity and out-of-the-box thinking.

Specialties: Medical Imaging, Product Management, Healthcare, Education, Marketing, Business Development,
Languages: Spanish, English, French, Dutch
