Chitral Naik's Experience

Team Leader and Sr. Development Engineer

Reaction Design

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Computer Software industry

Technical responsibilities and contributions:
• Prediction of fuel effects on performance and emissions of advanced engines using accurate fuel models in CFD simulation. The engines include HCCI, diesel, and dual-fuel or RCCI engines.
• Developed cutting-edge predictive detailed kinetic mechanisms for gasoline, diesel, jet fuel, and alternative fuels to be used by automotive, turbine, and chemical industries
• Innovated ways to formulate multi-component surrogate blends for predicting emissions

Managerial responsibilities and contributions:
