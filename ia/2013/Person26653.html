Conrad Weaver | Video Producer's Summary

An award-winning video producer, Conrad Weaver is the President and CEO of Conjostudios, LLC, a video production company based out of Frederick, MD.

Conrad's work has been recognized nationally by the Telly Awards with a recent Silver Telly award for his "Wheat Harvest Movie" promo video, and a Bronze Telly for his "Frederick Rescue Mission Food Services" video. He has previously won Telly Awards for two other projects, one featuring a school in Guatemala.

"I work hard to establish a reputation for high quality video production and stellar customer service. And I love helping clients tell their story though compelling video narratives! Video plays a huge role in any successful company's marketing strategy; that's where I come in... I work with marketing and PR teams to craft compelling stories that will promote their brand or image.

I'm currently working a documentary film called "The Great American Wheat Harvest" telling the story about the American Wheat Harvest and how food moves from the field to the table. Visit www.greatamericanwheatharvest.com for more info.
