Adam Humphreys' Summary

I’m an open minded person that likes to tinker and experiment with things, whether that be art, electronics, or otherwise. I’m also a serious animation buff and am always looking for animation I enjoy, whether old, new, 2D, 3D, local, or foreign. I’ve worked with Cinema 4D and Photoshop for years developing several skills and techniques for modeling and texturing. As a contrast, I’ve also learned and done much in the way of renewable energy, repairing electrical appliances, and recently, microcontrollers. Firstly, however, I’m a Christian. This isn’t to say I’m one to openly force truth onto anyone because it’s written, “If it is possible, as much as depends on you, live peaceably with all men,” Romans 12:18. I’m also fairly receptive to constructive criticism on my work despite being somewhat reluctant to give criticism.

Concerning creativity and career objectives, sure it would be nice to be a director or an engineer, but I really enjoy planning and executing. It’s not so much as reaching a defined goal, but rather striving in general and solving problems to reach a goal. Even more assuring is knowing how to prevent future problems and streamline workflow. As other objectives go, I’d like to live without dependence on the electrical grid in a very economical home (with a garage to actually park a vehicle).
