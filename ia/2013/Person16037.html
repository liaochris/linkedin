Brad Cooper's Summary

Brad Cooper is a nationally recognized Trusted Advisor
in Farmington, Missouri. He has helped thousands
of families and businesses nationwide, while
compiling over 20 years of financial experience.
His unique blend of customer care, financial
expertise and experience is the driving force
behind his great success.

Brad is a recognized leader in the financial
industry. He has helped a diverse range of
consumers with his “hands on” style" by guiding
them through the financial process. Brad has taken
a special interest in financial education and
helping families protect their assets for a secure
retirement.

Throughout Brad’s extensive financial services
career, he has won numerous awards and received
accolades for his commitment to excellence. He has
earned the respect and admiration of his peers.

A resident of St. Francois County in Missouri,
Brad has had the spotlight on KTJJ 98 FM radio
monthly as an expert and is the host of the weekly
“Financial Answers For Todays Economy" Radio Show
on KREI 800 AM. Brad has also written numerous
articles on the subject of finance and has
contributed numerous financial related articles
published in the Daily Journal.

Brad is a member of the Farmington Chambers of
Commerce in Farmington, Missouri. He is a past
president and member of the Lions Club, a member
of the Masonic Lodge #154, has recently served as
the President for the Mineral Area College
Foundation Board and currently serves as a board
member. In addition, Brad has developed speeches
and workshops tailored for multiple audiences on
the topics of money and credit education.
Brad is originally from Fredericktown, MO and is
married to Shelly Cooper of Park Hills, MO and is
blessed with two daughters, Grace and Maggie Cooper.

Specialties: Financial Education, Fixed Indexed Annuities,
Wealth Management, Insurance, Refinancing and
Purchases, Financial Seminars, and Speaking.
