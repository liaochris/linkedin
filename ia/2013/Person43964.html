Christopher Brady's Summary

Chris is an industry leading Executive Search Consultant with expertise in finding Digital and Technology Talent. He is sought after by start ups, ad agencies, corporate technology / marketing teams, and consulting companies that want to grow their organization by hiring the best talent in the marketplace in the least amount of time. Chris has been featured in Advertising Age and other trade magazines as an expert in recruiting "hard to find" talent.

"Finding the skill set is the easier part, it's working with the business and finding out what they really need and how much they can pay...... that's the challenge"


About Hire Recruitment:

We recruit nationally in Digital Media/Technology/Consulting spaces like: Client Leadership, Design / Development, Project Management, Analytics & Insights, Creative, Software Development, Network & Application Security, Network Engineering, etc

Our Digital Practice area focuses on helping elite Digital Agencies find talent. Positions include: Senior Designer, Art Director, Creative Director, User Experience, Information Architecture, Account, Project Management, Digital Strategy - Social | Mobile, etc.

Contact me at chris(at)hirerecruitment.com for more information.
