Mendhi Audlin's Summary

I am the founder and Spiritual Leader for SpiritualityToday.TV, a ministry in Fort Worth, TX bringing a contemporary spiritual message to the mainstream.

The author of “What If It All Goes RIGHT?” I have spoken to audiences in 43 states and 4 countries about the power of positive thinking. Using a process called "What If Upping," I work with visionary leaders to discover innovative possibilities for moving forward. When used as part of a Mastermind Community, the process has even greater impact, accelerating collaborative relationship-building, and promoting healthy cultures.

I served as the pioneering Spiritual Leader of Unity Church of Wimberley for three years. In 2007, I was invited to launch my concept for an online spiritual radio network, Unity Online Radio. I hosted the network’s first program, The Leading Edge, and grew the network to more than 100,000 listeners in over 60 countries.

At the same time, I worked as a producer for “Jesus and the Awakening to God-Consciousness,” a PBS documentary featuring Deepak Chopra and Rev. Wendy Craig Purcell. I also hosted and produced Unity’s special online video production of “An Evening with Eckhart Tolle.”

I am a former president of the Global Peace Project & author of “Let It Begin with Me: 21 Voices of the New Peace Movement.”

STRENGTHS: Connectedness. Learner. Maximizer. Futuristic. Intellection.

MYERS-BRIGGS: ENFP
Extroversion, Intuition, Feeling, Perceiving
ENFP: "Warm, empathetic, responsive and responsible. Highly attuned to the emotions, needs & motivations of others. Find potential in everyone, want to help others fulfill their potential. May act as catalyst for individual and group growth. Loyal, responsive to praise & criticism. Sociable, facilitate others in a group, and provide inspiring leadership."

Specialties: public speaking, facilitation, mastermind processes, workshops, keynotes, leadership, spirituality, innovation, creativity, vision, purpose-driven community-building
