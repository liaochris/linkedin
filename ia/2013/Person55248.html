The mission of Turda's Tourist Information Center (affectionately known by locals as the CIT) is to revive a sense of local pride in and offer information to tourists about Turda's remarkable tourist attractions: including the vast Turda Gorges, the breathtaking Turda Salt Mine (one of Europe's largest), the remains of the Vth Macedonian Roman legionary camp, an unusual medieval to 19th century town center, over 30 churches of 12+ denominations* and interesting hotels which serve as a useful staging point for the splendours of the nearby Apuseni Mountains.
* It was in Turda in 1568 ad that Europe's first decree of religious freedom was signed.
