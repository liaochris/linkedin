Mike Fraietta's Summary

I've been professionally knee-deep in the social media and social business game as a professional for the last five years serving as a Chief Listener and Community Manager at two successful software companies, as Enterprise Community Manager for one of the world's largest media companies and as an ongoing consultant/project specialist. My experiences living in five countries on four continents and working over 50 types of unique jobs has enabled me to adapt and change ahead of society.
