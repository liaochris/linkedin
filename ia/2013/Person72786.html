Katherine Winter's Summary

Professional Bio:
Katherine Winter has always had an unyielding passion for computer games going back to a very young age. It is no wonder that she chose a career as a software engineer.

Finishing high school a year early, Katherine made the leap to pursue her dream by matriculating at Full Sail University at age 17, one of its youngest students.

Katherine jumped in with both feet by quickly earning a reputation of not only being one of its hardest working students, but also for volunteering on projects that other students would be apprehensive about taking on. Loving a challenge, she always strives for perfection, backed up with a strong code of ethics and a motto of, “Do it right the first time”.

It did not take long for Katherine to become the go to person when others had problems or questions. Her peers knew that she would provide not only the answer but also frequently a more effective way of solving the issue.

With an incomparable work ethic and a passion for perfection, Katherine will become one of Full Sail University’s youngest alumni in July 2010 with a Bachelor of Science in Game Development, and one of the top students in her class.
