Kevin S. Ryan's Summary

I am a digital storyteller perfectly suited for today’s networked world. A journalist by training, I have a unique blend of media and marketing expertise, and a profound understanding of the importance of authentic storytelling in business success. My work is focused on cutting through the hype of social media to help clients understand the who, what and where of their digital marketing. Whether it’s building the social programs for Barnes & Noble or defining the robust interactions on March Madness Live, I have an eye for what customers want, what companies need, and how to use social media to grow business.

Specialties: Social media marketing, social media strategy, digital strategy, community management, editorial leadership, social media training, workshop facilitator, and analyst.
