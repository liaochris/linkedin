Marcell Marias' Summary

Multi-faceted electronic media designer and producer working with video, graphics, music, sound and sculpture to enhance digital and physical environs. Concerned with our Planet Earth and with fostering effective communication and understanding among it's inhabitants.

Musician working in the electronic music genre with experience in soundtrack composition, sound design, mixing, editing and mastering. Involved in two-Grammy-nominated recordings. Interested in surround sound production and performance, having performed several multi-channel performances at Seattle Art Museum.

Video Producer with professional experience including HD camera operation, lighting, video editing, motion graphics, DVD production, compression and web video integration.

Web Designer with graphical user interface design expertise, digital photography and video implementation, and using simple, elegant and functional design.

VJ performances utilizing live, interactive visualization and video sampling. Projection of visualizations on custom-built screens.

Artist working with light, sound, video and sculpture to enhance personal and public environments. Use of sustainable materials including recycled, re-used, surplus, and natural, coupled with energy-efficient illumination.

Specialties

Sound Design, Electronic Music Composition, Soundtrack Composition, Surround Sound Performance, Sustainable Sculpture Design and Fabrication, Live VJ Performance, Inventor of the Vortexya Video Kaleidoscope
