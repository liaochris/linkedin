Beverly Ferguson's Summary

A broad backround in the fine arts, the study of color theory and an avid interest in sewing has prepared me for the excellent service I provide for my clients in custom window treatments and home furnishings. I stirve to make every indvidual's experience meaningful by listening carefully to their wants and needs. I then formulate a personalized design which encompasses their tastes in a workable and solid plan. I help people by creating warm and welcoming environments which are their personal sanctuaries...very important work! Continuing education is important and I make space several times a year for professional level instruction.

Specialties

Color is my expertise, I use color on a sophisticated level to create exactly the mood and feeling the client desires while attaining an appealing aesthetic.

Master Gilding Instructor

November 2004 – Present (9 years)

Since this is one portion of a highly concentrated 3-day curriculum, and since most of the attendees have no prior experience with gilding, it was a challenge to develop the unit to encompass the complicated process and to distill it unto a level which could be grasped quickly and concisely.
