Kate Culligan's Summary

CAREER COACHING for the "Career Confused"
I am a "career turnaround" coach specializing in supporting clients during key turning points in their careers. Whether you're ready to find a more challenging position or you've been downsized -I utilize my huge network of contacts to put you in touch with working professionals. I also support clients to develop their careers at their current companies. This might include learning how to deal with a difficult political situation or how to "rebrand" yourself to increase your visibility.

INDIVIDUAL SERVICES
I provide structure and accountability for clients who are in transition or for those who need support in moving ahead within their current organizations. I coach professionals by helping them to identify their talents, motivations and goals in order to develop strategic action plans. I customize my coaching so each client feels understood, heard and supported.

CORPORATE SERVICES
My work with corporations includes executive coaching, outplacement, speaking, and training. Many organizations, such as Oppenheimer Funds, hire me to create an internal career development program that increases employee retention and engagement. I also work with both the human resources department and transitioning employees during and after a downsizing. I provide group seminars and individual coaching to clarify what the next step is for each of them.

I speak frequently on current topics like generational dynamics within the office, corporate or organizational culture, negotiating office politics productively, and creating engaged work environments by recognizing individual employee niches and work styles.



ABOUT ME
As president of Kate Culligan, Channel 9 News and the Denver Post have covered me several times. These media moments are great examples of my philosophy in action. See the clips, articles, and more about me at my website, www.kateculligan.com.

Specialties

Career coaching, career counseling, outplacement services, training, personal branding, pre-retirement coaching, executive coaching, career assessments, Myers Briggs, Strong Interest Inventory, Highlands, interview preparation, salary negotiation, resume review and creation, glass ceiling issues, emotional intelligence, change management, negotiating office politics, generational coaching, team building, leadership, entrepreneur coaching, networking, small business coaching, speaking
