Joy Resor's Summary

I'm a former newsletter editor and trainer in a corporate setting. As an entrepreneur with my start up company, I spread joy in many ways. I guide clients one-on-one in spiritual direction as they discover their truth within. I facilitate a local Circle of Joy to celebrate and expand joy through journaling prompts and sharing. Satellite participants receive this class through e-mail and snail mail, enjoying their at-home process. Through my website, visitors request a free e-book that brings readers peace and joy, and they also receive a subscription for Go In Joy!, a weekly e-zine. My goals include growing Joy on Your Shoulders TM to spread lots of joy through words, images, and products, and to give part-time work to local seamstresses, helping their individual incomes while boosting the local economy. Batik radiant creations they create with positive messages are available to purchase.

Customers love wearing them, hanging them on a wall or over the edge of a painting or mirror, and displaying them on altars. Each creation is hand sewn locally in the mountains of Western North Carolina.

Patient Volunteer

Transylvania Regional Hospital Hospice

May 2010 – May 2013 (3 years 1 month)

When there's a Hospice patient who would benefit by being a match with me as a volunteer, the volunteer coordinator contacts me to see if I'm available to work with a new patient. If I am, the Hospice social worker takes me to meet the new individual. We connect and see how that person would like to use me--to sit and visit, to take them on errands, to do their shopping, etc., and we schedule my return. Generally, I volunteer with each new patient 2-4 hours a week.
