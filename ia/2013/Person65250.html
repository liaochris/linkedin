Joe Cilea's Summary

As a former collegiate athlete, I know what it means when you have aches and pain that impact your life. We can help!

My team of specialists can help speed your recovery from injuries and provide the appropriate treatments for chronic or new conditions.

If you are suffering from headaches, neck, back or shoulder problems, our multi-disciplinary center of care has the appropriate treatment to make you feel whole again.

In addition to providing physical therapy, interventional pain management, rehabilitative and acupuncture services, I am a regular speaker on topics ranging from Nutrition to Injury Prevention and Wellness.
