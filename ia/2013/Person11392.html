Babette E. Bensoussan's Summary

Babette is a professionally accredited coach, with over 20 years of business experience including Executive Coaching, Mentoring and Training with executives from Australian and Global Fortune 500 Companies, Government Departments, and small to medium businesses. She is a Master Energy Leadership Coach, accredited with the International Coaching Federation and qualified in Myer-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI).

She is also the Managing Director of The MindShifts Group, which specialises in competitive intelligence, strategic planning and strategic marketing projects in the Australasian region.

Babette is as SCIP Fellow and a SCIP Meritorious Award Winner – internationally, the highest award and recognition in the field of Competitive Intelligence. She has taught Competitive Intelligence in undergraduate business and MBA programs both in Australia and overseas. She is currently an Adjunct Professor at the University of Technology Sydney, School of Business.

She has had published numerous articles on strategic planning, competitive intelligence, and strategic marketing and is an invited speaker and guest lecturer both domestically and internationally. She is the co-author of “Strategic and Competitive Analysis”, published by Prentice Hall and released in April 2003. A second book "Business and Competitive Analysis" was released in 2007. Currently both books are in the top 1% of best selling books at Amazon.com and are ranked the number one best selling CI books. They have been translated into multiple languages. A slightly different third book "Analysis Without Paralysis" was released in June 2008 and their fourth "The Financial Times Guide to Analysis for Managers" was released in late 2009. A second edition of Analysis without Paralysis is due for release end October 2012.
