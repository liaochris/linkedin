Kevin Jones' Summary

Consultant, Speaker, Video Enthusiast

My goal is to no less than change the world by helping companies upgrade the way they work. We do this by innovating the way they manage their companies.

The business landscape is changing on many levels. As I advise and consult Fortune 500 companies and SMBs, we work together to simplify operations and increase communication & collaboration so employees can concentrate on those things which are of the most value. Often this involves internal collaborative tools for performance improvement, communication and learning.

I frequently speak at conferences as a keynote and in the general sessions, give webinars, train groups on the topics of Innovation Management, Failure, and using collaborative technologies.

At vinJonesVideos.com I create videos that simply explain difficult concepts in fun and memorable ways. The goal is to help people reevaluate the way they work.

Specialties: Management Innovation, Enterprise 2.0, social media, social networking, performance improvement, learning, training, collaboration, communication

  • 15 Ways to Fail at Social Business

    • Self
    Authors: Kevin Jones

    There are many books, webinars and conferences which show companies how to succeed at social business. Yet what works for one organization may not work for another. This may explain one reason why failures are more common than successes when implementing social technologies within organizations.

    15 Ways To Fail at Social Business uncovers the hidden stories of Enterprise 2.0 failures. Using real examples, Kevin D. Jones shows the top ways others have tried and failed and how to avoid their fate. The lessons of failure give insights into how to be successful by avoiding the mistakes of others.

    Not wanting to repeat the failures that so many have fallen into, those who are implementing collaborative technologies within their organizations will want to learn the valuable insights contained in this book.
