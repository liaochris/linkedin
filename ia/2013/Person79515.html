Leah Grant's Summary

Leah Grant, an International Coach Federation Master Certified Coach, has worked for the last thirteen years with over 900 sole proprietors and small business owners to increase their marketing skills, self-confidence and income. She is an expert in assisting people to get their new businesses off the ground.

As the founder of Leah Grant Enterprises, LLC, Leah shares her business wisdom through training products and coaching.

Since 2001, Leah has taught thousands of coaches all over the world as a Master Leader for CoachU. She serves as a Faculty Mentor and also assisted with updating CoachU’s curriculum by writing the New Coach Basics module. Leah served as the Community Coach for CoachVille’s First Year as a Coach Community. Her coaching practice consists of one-on-one clients.

Leah is a Certified Genos Emotional Intelligence Practitioner and is trained in interpreting the DISC and PIAV assessments.

While a student at Coach U, Leah founded and ran the CoachU West Los Angeles Chapter. She was a founding member of the International Coach Federation’s Orange County Chapter. As an active chapter member, she wore many hats beginning with Program Coordinator and culminating with President.

Leah brings a varied and unique background to her coaching. She has:
--owned a marketing consulting firm
--served as the Vice President of Marketing for a web design and hosting company
--was the Human Relations Manager for a business-to-business Internet company
--worked as a Marketing Manager for a direct mail marketing company
--been a contributing writer for San Jose Magazine
--served as a Lifestyle Counselor for a physician
--made money as a freelance graphic designer

Leah earned her B.A. in Communications at Trinity University in San Antonio, Texas. She is also a Master Certified Graduate of CoachU and has her Professional Mentor Coach certification.
