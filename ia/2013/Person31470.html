President & Owner

Bachman Builders, Inc.

June 1996 – Present (17 years 3 months) Moon Township, PA 15108

Responsible for all aspects of owning and running a custom home building business from sales and marketing, construction supervision, loss-prevention, quality control, contract legality, closings, lot selection, inspections, housing design, warranty maintenance, and structural integrity.

Actively train and mentor employees new to the construction field, giving each employee the tools necessary to learn and grow into supervisory and management roles.

Faithfully lead a weekly staff meeting with employees to ensure all tasks are being accomplished and instil vision, oversight and encouragement.

Work closely with all housing and plan realtors, ensuring they receive necessary and correct information regarding lots, spec homes and potential plans of interest.

Manage all sales activity including initial meetings with all clients to assess their building needs and desires.

Creatively draw pre-architectural home building plans for potential clients.

Estimate and quote all jobs, soliciting best prices from various vendors.

Establish and maintain a good rapport with local vendors, real agents, and building code officials in several counties.

Meet and comply with building inspectors ensuring permits and building requirements, as well as codes, are adhered to and fulfilled.

Schedule project dates with subcontractors and clients and ensured safety, quality control, structural integrity, design and implantation from housing inception to closing and warranty.

Walk each customer through selection process of all major item purchases to ensure product and design integrity.

Foster continued professional growth through membership in National Association of Home Builders as well as the Builders Association of Metropolitan Pittsburgh (BAMP)

Participate in all BAMP Festival of Homes events since 1998.
