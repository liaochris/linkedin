Abhishek Shah's Summary

Abhishek is a serial eCommerce entrepreneur and digital enthusiast immensely passionate on the power of internet as a game changer.

Abhishek is the founder & CEO of Fetise.com - India's leading online fashion outlet for Men and earlier founded DealsAndYou.com the Groupon of India - with successful exit to Germany based Group Buying Global AG which runs leading group buying websites across four continents
