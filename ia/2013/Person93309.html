Michele "Mikey" Kelly's Summary

For the past twenty-five years I have been in media. I was the first women hired by ABC in an executive position, wrote a column for San Francisco Magazine, helped build two other magazines, as well as being an on-air talent for Viacom. Seventeen years ago, my partner Ed Fogelman (former EVP and CFO of Landor for 20 years) and I created the Kelly Fogelman Group Brand activation and Reputation Development Firm. As Chief Marketing and Chief Creative Officer, I originate highly innovative programs which deliver customer acquisition and substantial media placement. Placing clients everywhere from the Wall Street Journal to Germany's Der Spiegel... Magazines from People to Popular Science and Newspapers from the New York Times to Times of London. In respect to TV I have placed clients from CNN to BBC and Telemundo ...online from the Huffington Post to All Things Digital and CNET.

I develop strategic programs that include booking, high level speaking engagement and conferences, developing influencers support, creating compelling releases and events which globally result in hundreds of thousand of media impressions.. In addition I curate solid partnerships (Robert Mondavi Winery, Levi's, Armani, Macy's,eBay) and work with celebrities to promote products (Anjelica Huston, Jon Bon Jovi, Rosario Dawson, Gwen Stefani). Oh! Did I forget that my firm does great product launches too!

Representing such amazing clients as Silicon Valley legend Heidi Roizen, Intelius, Moon Express (one of 3 NASA Contracts to the moon), Rabobank (3rd largest bank in the world), BioArts (Dog cloning ), Groasis Waterboxx ( won Popular Science Award for Best Invention), Autodesk's Deep Flight Aviator,Inter • Continental Hotels, Levi's, Vidal Sassoon, Jimmy Choo, Ex'pressionNew Media College, Silicon Valley Space Center etc. speaks volumes about my unique insight into how to create consumer and press interest.
