Jeanne Hurlbert PhD's Summary

HOW I CAN HELP YOU
We're changing the face of market research by helping you connect meaningfully with your customers or prospects, create the products they want, and increase conversions. We help businesses from the ver smallest to $100-million companies. We offer our "Turn Surveys Into CashTM system for small businesses, custom quizzes to automate a conversation with prospects that will skyrocket conversions, satisfaction surveys, "done for you" surveys that deploy on your website or Facebook page, custom surveys, data analysis, industry research/whitepapers, social media/Facebook marketing, customer segmentation, and more.

ABOUT DR. JEANNE HURLBERT
Expert in survey research, social networks, and social media. Clients include The Tony Robbins Companies, Tatum, Panaroma Orthopedics, SuperSmartHealth, Inc. 500 entrepreneur Ali International, John Assaraf, Newport Trial Group, Joe Polish, Andy Jenkins, Traffic Geyser, and three New York Times bestselling authors. Worked as a consultant on projects with the US Army Corps of Engineers, including the IPET after Hurricane Katrina.

Served on the faculty of Louisiana State University for more than 20 years, where Dr. Hurlbert taught hundreds of social science students and marketing students how to read people’s minds with surveys and directed the Louisiana Population Data Center Survey Research Lab. Used survey research to study how individuals’ social networks affect such things as job-finding, job satisfaction, access to social support, and health. Research has been funded by such agencies as the National Science Foundation and published in top social science and health journals. Served on the editorial board of numerous social science journals, including The Journal of Health and Social Behavior.
