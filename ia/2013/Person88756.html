Matt Grest's Summary

An experienced senior IT professional and leader, with a broad and mature cross functional skill-set and a track record of transforming IT departments.

Currently working at Sky Sports, and responsible for end-to-end project delivery (analysis, design, project management, development, testing) and service delivery (platform architecture and support, service desk) for products and services across Sky Sports' web and mobile platforms, and content syndication.

In February 2013 I completed a 3 year tenure at Yorkshire Building Society, working as part of the IT Leadership Team; responsible for leading a large software development, testing & technical solutions team (150+ people). Responsible for the technical delivery of large-scale financial merger and acquisition programmes, projects and small-change, including core systems, web, IVR & branch systems.

Previous positions include Head of Development at NHS Choices (Capita) where I was responsible for technical delivery of all change on the NHS flagship website - http://nhs.uk. The site is one of the highest profile websites in the UK & is the largest SharePoint publishing platform in Europe.

I was part of the core management team that took Plusnet (UK Internet Service Provider) from a small Sheffield business in 1999, through exponential growth to an eventual £67m acquisition by BT in January 2007, via a flotation on AIM in 2004. I played a lead role in the design and delivery of the UK's most sophisticated ISP provisioning and CRM platform, and innovative product development.

I have a technical background with a broad knowledge of software and platform technologies. I have successfully managed multi-million pound project & departmental teams on a range of internal & customer facing projects using waterfall & agile methodologies, on a global level.

Successfully lead & directed the delivery of offshore transformations at Plusnet, 3T Productions (part of the RM group), LocallyCompared and Yorkshire Building Society.
