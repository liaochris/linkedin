Lee Thurburn's Summary

NetOffer is online CRM software that helps businesses build online communities and automate their digital marketing. We have spent several years designing and building a database system that combines intuitive operation with highly sophisticated flexibility. The system uses WordPress as the primary CMS interface and adds tremendous functionality that is not available anywhere else.

My passion is helping business owners find new ways to grow their businesses and increase their revenues. It is amazing to me how many people have a business but do not have a well organized database of customers and prospects. The NetOffer CRM Platform is perfectly situated to help. We offer advanced technology that usually has a high price tag coupled with a user interface that is incredibly intuitive and easy to use.

The centerpiece of our system is the ability to create marketing campaigns that are focused on targeted segments of your database. Once created, these campaigns will run on auto-pilot until the targeted recipient takes an action that triggers a notice to you that they are ready to move to a different stage in the purchase cycle.
