David Blumentals' Summary

Every day, around the world, we help achieve better I.T. outcomes. This may be using Microsoft CRM/xRM, SharePoint, Web Content or other eCommerce tools. The underlying theme is applying great software design, services, quality & innovation to what are often common business & user problems.

Quality, experience & expertise is at the heart of everything we do. The tiniest detail, the tools we use, the testing we complete to ensure that our solutions never disappoint. Creating software solutions that users love is brought to life by the passion our global team has for creating great customer experiences.

With over 20 years’ experience working with global organizations we view Web 2.0 as an opportunity to get closer to raving fans & clients, better understanding their needs, empowering them to influence strategy & future direction. This, in turn, opens the door to improved services, enhanced loyalty & better advocacy.

Despite the rise of the Internet, globalization & economic downturns the fundamentals of doing business in the 21st century remain unchanged: Managing & strengthening relationships is still a fundamental strategy for any organization – whether in the Legal, Accounting, Finance, Engineering, IT or Professional Sports arenas. It’s the methods & tools for enhancing those relationships that are evolving.

The solutions we deliver are transforming the way organizations market, develop new business & sign new deals – as well as the role that clients & customers play in influencing products, services & strategies.

We understand the vast & emerging opportunities the Web presents for business – marketing, business development & ongoing services. We are embracing Web 2.0 to bring greater agility & ease of use. We leverage social computing to build global & regional communities – throughout Asia Pacific, North America, UK & Europe. We are also creating new Web 2.0-based applications that provide a collaborative, interactive foundation for long-term success.
