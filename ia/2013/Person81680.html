Lisa Rosser's Summary

Lisa Rosser takes the mystery out of finding and recruiting military veterans for corporations, government agencies, higher education institutions and franchisors.

A consultant, author, speaker, and workshop leader, her clients include a number of Fortune 500 companies, including many already recognized by G.I. Jobs magazine as “Top 100 Military-Friendly" employers.

Lisa wrote the book on hiring military! First published in 2007, "The Value of a Veteran(TM): The Guide for Human Resource Professionals to Regarding, Recruiting, and Retaining Military Veterans " continues to elicit praise from within the HR and diversity communities for its detailed overview of the elements of effective veteran hiring programs.

The knowledge that went into that book derived from Lisa’s 22 years of active and reserve military service, coupled with 16+ years of Human Resources consulting and staffing experience. She brings unique insight to the military’s culture, processes and procedures and integrates it into topics civilian HR professionals address daily: how to find the best diverse talent with limited recruiting staff and budgets; complying with OFCCP/EEO requirements; and use of social media tactics.

She is frequently quoted as an expert in veteran employment issues. She has been featured in HR Magazine and Inside Higher Ed and heard at industry HR, diversity and disability conferences such as ERE, SHRM, CUPA-HR, HERC, ILG and USBLN.

For more information on her web seminars, workshops, and twice-monthly "Ask the Military Hiring Expert" sessions, please visit www.TheValueOfaVeteran.com.

Military Friendly Employers Advisory Board Committee Member

Victory Media

Privately Held; Myself Only; Publishing industry

May 2010 – Present (3 years 8 months)

In 2003 Victory Media brought the notion of hiring military talent to a new level by conducting a data-driven survey of the Fortune 1000’s military recruitment practices and publishing the first Military Friendly Employers® list. The rankings of the Top 100 Military Friendly Employers® are based on a survey of over 100 questions which assesses a company's long-term commitment to hiring former military, recruiting, and hiring efforts and results, policies for Reserve/Guard members called to active duty, and the presence of special recruitment military programs.

In 2010 Victory Media established an independent volunteer advisory board to determine the survey methodology and survey questions and weighting. The advisory board does not review or see any survey applications. All surveys are independently reviewed and scored by Ernst & Young.
