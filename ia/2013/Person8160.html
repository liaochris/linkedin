Anna F. Miller's Summary

Anna F. Miller is a documentary-style freelance video editor and producer. She’s fascinated by and appreciative of the risks small business owners take. Her talents lie in the process of extracting the unique stories of a brand’s culture and philosophy, in addition to promoting a product or service. She believes authentic storytelling is the best kind of marketing.

Anna was raised in Chicago before attending the University of North Carolina, Asheville where she studied video and interactive design. She has been a professional full-time editor since 2004, working first in Asheville and then Chicago before heading to Phoenix in 2008. It’s not hard to tap into the entrepreneurial energy of Phoenix, so it was natural that she began capturing and promoting the stories of people doing new and interesting things. Phoenix became the impetus to pursue the documentary path Anna always dreamed of -- she is currently in production on a film about a group of social entrepreneurs.

Anna prefers working with companies who care about their impact on the community and many of her clients are start-ups. Whether she’s editing at a commercial production company or creating videos for her own clients, she brings her distinctive documentary storytelling style and vision into any project, large or small.
