Anne Ganguzza's Summary

Anne is a full time Voice over artist, coach, and producer and owner of Annespeak.

A professional voice actor located in Orange County, CA, Anne offers a full range of voiceover services at highly competitive rates and will work with your budget to help you promote your business to new levels of excellence. Voiceover services include but not limited to: Narration, Corporate and Industrial Narration, Documentary Narratives, E-learning and online courses, Medical Narration, Telephone and Voicemail greetings, IVR and on hold messaging, Internet/Web Presentations, Power Point Presentations, Infomercials, PSA's, Radio/TV Commercials, Audio Books, Station Imaging, Tag Lines, Political Spots, And many more!

She is a current member of the World Voices Organization, the Society of Accredited Voice Over Artists, and Board Member at Large of the LA/Orange County chapter of Media Communications Association-international (MCA-I.).

She also has many years experience as a Network and IT Project Manager and possess a broad range of hands-on experience directing and integrating cutting edge networking and information systems technologies within new and existing large scale educational and government network environments.

Anne is represented by DeSanti Talent Agency and The Rock Agency. For the San Diego and Orange County CA area, please contact Artist Management Agency at http://www.artistmanagementagency.com or (619) 233-6655.
