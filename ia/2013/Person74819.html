Kevin Conroy Smith's Summary

I'm an entrepreneur from Chicago who opened up my first store front business at age 22. Through my experiences I have had the opportunity to develop and build extremely profitable small businesses through hiring, training, managing and directing employees.

My passion is to never stop learning from every person I meet and use that information to help guide my employees and community to a brighter future. My work and projects reflect my commitment to building profitable and long lasting businesses that make a positive impact on the world.

Most importantly, I build businesses that I enjoy. If it's not fun you're not doing it right.
