Brooke Archer's Summary

About us

Archer Executive search uses a cross section of methodologies to achieve maximum talent resourcing solutions such as market/talent mapping of the appropriate markets through high level research, national print advertising, internet advertising, linked in, database search and tapping into extensive national networks. Archer Executive Search offers retained search solutions across a range of industry sectors. We specialise in Senior Executive Positions as well as highly technical roles nationally.
As a boutique search provider Archer Executive Search has the ability and flexibility to provide tailored solutions at an affordable cost to our clients in order to represent our clients brand and value proposition in the most effective way to result in premium talent sourcing and appointment.

Brooke Archer's Experience

Managing Director

Archer Executive Search

May 2013 – Present (4 months) Australia

Brooke Archer is the founder and Managing Director of Archer Executive Search. Prior to Establishing Archer Executive Search Brooke worked with National Search and Selection Company, Boston Kennedy where she was the National Manager of Executive Search covering executive appointments across a range of industry sectors.

Brooke throughout her career has been dedicated to ensuring the best candidates are presented for each position through thorough market assessment and provides a high quality end to end search process; be it locally or nationally.   Before being appointed as National Manager of Executive Search with Boston Kennedy, Brooke took on the task of establishing Boston Kennedy's South Australian office in July 2011. Her results were exemplary due to her focus on customer service and innovation. Brooke was nominated as the South Australian finalist for the 'Young Professional of the Year' award in 2012 for the RCSA (Recruitment and Consulting Services Association, Australia and New Zealand which recognised her contribution to increasing professionalism and innovation within the recruitment industry.

Brooke has also worked with a Global Recruitment company as a specialist and manager in executive search and selection.   Brooke is committed that her services remain exclusive and ethical and partners with a limited number of top-tier and mid-tier companies in each industry sector nationally – in many cases just one. This enables her to share industry knowledge and sector expertise across borders, enabling a high level of support to clients with innovative and highly tailored services anywhere in the country.   Brooke aims to provide research and resourcing solutions for organisations across the globe with a specific emphasis on hard-to-find, scarce skilled technical and executive candidates. Brooke provides an end to end service within the executive search space to deliver a world class recruitment service.
