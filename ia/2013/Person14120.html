Youth Worker

Southend Youth and Connexions Service

November 2002 – October 2003 (1 year)

Worked on four projects: Health Magazine. FutureZone. CHIL

Health Magazine: Providing support to young people who are designing a health magazine for other young people in Southend. Our role was to help the young people to see the issues that not only they were facing but also those of their peers. To help them research it, then to communicate it appropriately, finally to design it.

Future Zone: Developing a programme to support “Looked After” young people in FutureZone, a homework support group. In this session we helped the young people with whatever subjects they came to us with. It was either support with their everyday homework or ongoing assignments. Our purpose was not to teach them but to help them find the answers themselves. Many of the young people would have learning difficulties and so we would often use creative approaches to find the solutions. More often or not taking interests they had and applied them to the subject they were looking at.

CHIL: Working alongside Young Carers to provide a place of refuge from their very difficult lives. A place to play, develop and share their mutual experiences. In both CHIL and Future Zone we encountered young people who displayed a variety of EBD’s and had to support and challenge their behaviour as well as ensuring the safety of other young people at the centre.

Band night: Worked alongside young people who had interest in setting up a Band. I took them through a whole process of starting, rehearsing, understanding band dynamics, developing a sound, recording, publicity and playing live. At the end of the process all the different band were able to put on a live gig together.
