Angelo D'Amico's Summary

TO BE ONE OF A KIND

Angelo's wish is to be viewed as one of a kind. As someone you can trust and rely on. As someone who makes your real estate experience, whether buying or selling, a smooth and enjoyable one. Ultimately he would like to have you as a client for life. As your real estate agent he will do his very best to ensure he is deserving of that consideration.

Angelo successfully followed his father, NHL Hall of Famer John D'Amico, into a career in the National Hockey League as an on-ice official for six years. His motivation and work ethic helped him to reach the pinnacle of the officiating profession, and he continues to maintain his relationships with some of the best teams, coaches and executives in the hockey world.

In 2007, Angelo spearheaded the founding of a charitable endeavor to raise awareness for the fight against blood cancers, Hustle For A Cure - The John D'Amico Fund, in honor of his late father. With his unique approach to the fund, Angelo has engendered support across Canada, USA and Europe from both the hockey and business elite.

Specializing in the luxury real estate market in the city of Mississauga and Oakville, negotiation, customer and public relations, 'One of a Kind' service and SUCCESS!!!
