Lee Tomlinson's Summary

Hello. I’m Lee, a recent cancer survivor, medical consultant, customer care expert and “perpetual patient”. Fact is, I am alive today because of the medical brilliance of many Doctors, nurses and administrators that have saved my life more than once.

On June 23rd, 2012, my world was forever changed when I was diagnosed with Stage 3 throat cancer. During a grueling year of treatment, I experienced “care” that was far too often, demeaning, inhumane and insensitive. Sadly, that frequent lack of “care”, sent me into a depression that led me to seriously consider suicide.

My studies since then revealed that my experience was not unique. To the contrary. Instead, my poor “care” mirrors a multi-decade decline in patient satisfaction with “how” medical practitioners care for their patients as they treat them. Studies conclusively show that insensitive care can have a hugely negative impact on patients’ healing. Furthermore, with the advent of HCAHPS, that lack of care will have profoundly negative financial repercussions for individuals and institutions unwilling to upgrade their patient care.

As a result, it’s now my mission to apply all my professional skills and personal experience to put the “CARE” back into health-care and insure that patients get the compassion and empathy they deserve.

And, in doing so, help medical practitioners reap enormous benefits that include, generating more revenue, being better healers, and living happier, healthier lives.

What we offer is not an ethical discussion about whether being more compassionate is the “right thing to do” or not. Rather, we offer the practical knowledge, processes, and procedures that when applied to a medical setting, will improve HCAHP scores, patient outcomes, the lives of doctors, nurses and other medical practitioners – and as a result -- generate more revenue.

We guarantee it.
