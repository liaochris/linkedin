John Sheldon II's Summary

John Sheldon founded SolarReady® Certified because of the need he saw in the residential solar industry for quality, viable, sales leads. Through years of experience profitably selling and installing turnkey residential solar installations across the country, John has developed a proprietary method of identifying customers who fit a specific list of over 20 criteria for a profitable, SolarReady® solar power solution.

At SolarReady® Certified, we're working to help solar installers grow their penetration of the solar market with a better way of bringing solar power to the millions of people who will benefit from having it.
