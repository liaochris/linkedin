John Payne's Summary

Happy to see my interview on designing for mhealth in Forbes: http://goo.gl/7kcDv

I'm currently a principal at Moment, a digital product design firm in New York City. My current work explores the positive contribution that mobile technologies and experience design can make to healthcare and education with a focus on empowerment of patients and learners.

Educated at Auburn University and Institute of Design at IIT (the New Bauhaus), I have guest lectured on design at Pratt and SCAD and taught graduate and undergraduate courses in design methodology at Parsons and NYU. I frequently participate in industry and corporate events as speaker and organizer.

I'm on the organizing committee of EPIC (The Ethnographic Praxis in Industry Conference), an event I co-chaired in 2012 at Savannah College of Art & Design.
