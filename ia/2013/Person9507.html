Seamless Mobile Health

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Hospital & Health Care industry

Seamless Mobile Health builds technology to prevent hospital readmissions, a problem which is up to 75% preventable and costs Canadian and U.S. hospitals $27 billion each year. Our flagship product is a mobile platform which empowers patients to monitor for complications after surgery and directs patients to the appropriate care faster.

Co-Founder of Let's Talk Health

LetsTalkHealth.ca

January 2013 – Present (8 months) University of Toronto

Let’s Talk Health is a health advocacy group formed by students at the University of Toronto concerned about student mental health. We’re looking to formulate an action plan to improve campus mental health at UofT, which means addressing stigma, service accessibility, and promoting health and most importantly structural change (both academic and environmental).

We host a bi-annual Town Hall bringing students, faculty and administrators together to discuss student mental health challenges.
