Glenda O'Neal's Summary

I am an independent grants consultant @ Glenda O'Neal LLC assisting education, health care, government, public, and not for profit organizations with foundation and government grant programs.

I am the author of "Let's Write a Grant," an instructional book on grant writing, and founder of Grant-Writing-Proposals.com, a website for grant writers.

I instruct online grant writing courses for Purdue University Calumet, Loma Linda University, University of Missouri St. Louis, and CD Publications Federal and Foundation Assistance Monitor.

I have been helping organizations raise millions of dollars in government and foundation grants since 1978.

I am recognized especially for a 100% success rate planning and writing federal grants. My consultation has resulted with applications ranking first in the nation and tallying perfect scores.

Foundations have used my proposals as writing guides for their applicants.

I have developed writing, program development, and reporting skills as a direct result of administering social service programs.

My experience includes working as a program officer reviewing, managing, and auditing federal grants.

I have served on committees to make grants for organizations seeking Older Americans Act funds and for Dreyers Grand Ice Cream and United Way.

I am a presenter for Thompson Publishing Group’s audio conferences on federal grant topics and serve on their Grants Editorial Advisory Board.

I instructed grant writing courses at the University of Missouri- St. Louis Nonprofit Management and Leadership Program for 20 years.

I have also instructed programs and presented webinars for organizations including San Jose State University in California; the Foundation Center and CompassPoint in San Francisco; AFI Systems GrantNavigator; Association of Fundraising Professionals, and for other professional and governmental agencies.

Specialties

Foundation and federal grant writing; proposal reviews; writing books and articles
