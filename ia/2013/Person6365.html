Andrea L. Bordenca's Summary

I am the CEO at DESCO Medical, an independent medical equipment service & asset management organization. Before contracting with DESCO, some of our customers had challenges with the time and energy associated with lack of contract consolidation. Many have made multiple calls to multiple service vendors, which takes up a lot of time and coordination. DESCO provides one phone number to call.

Others have expressed previous challenges around timeliness of vendor response, lack of monitoring cost & repair provided by vendors & staffing frustration when systems continuously breakdown with ineffective resolution. We serve to make lives easier so you can focus on what matters most: patient safety and care.

In May 2013, DESCO Medical partnered with Med One Capital. DESCO now provides rental and leasing options for your capital medical equipment in addition to our in house resident programs and field support.

DESCO evaluates a process each quarter to increase the value we are delivering to our customer. We are working to be as lean as possible to deliver the most effective and efficient service. We are constantly setting metrics and analyzing our efforts to ensure a constant state of motion in the direction of progress with an overall goal of making customer service worldwide as exemplary as it ought to be.

I am a student of somatic education & ontological coaching via The Institute for Generative Leadership & The Newfield Network. I am a student of Next Level, Inc,and serves on the Western MA and MA state Advisory Boards for the March of Dimes.

I reside in MA with my husband Chris Bordenca (bordenca.com), & 3 boys, Jesse, Seany & William.
