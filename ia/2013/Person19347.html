Project Director of Fashion Campus NYC & Fashion Draft NYC

Parsons The New School for Design

Educational Institution; 1001-5000 employees; Higher Education industry

April 2011 – August 2012 (1 year 5 months) New York City

Fashion Campus NYC and Fashion Draft NYC are two of six fashion industry-focused initiatives under NYC Mayor Michael R. Bloomberg’s Fashion NYC 2020 program, which is intended to build on the city’s competitive advantages and enhance its status as a global fashion capital.
Fashion Campus NYC, which launched as an annual event in Summer 2011, is intended to inspire talented students from around the world to pursue careers on the business side of New York City's fashion industry. I led the committee that developed and planned the two-day event for 200 college students. Programming included panel discussions, networking opportunities, career services sessions and a keynote address by Derek Lam in 2011 and Chris Benz in 2012.
Fashion Draft NYC, which launched in 2012, is a fashion industry first. The program recruited top graduating college seniors for full-time career-track positions at top NYC fashion firms like Saks Fifth Avenue, Gilt, Bloomingdale’s, Theory, Macy’s and LF USA. I led the team that developed the rigorous application and selection process, handled the student’s travel and accommodations, and planned the keynote address, a series of company tours and networking opportunities.
