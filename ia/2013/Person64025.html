Jim Boyle's Summary

Working with Point.io to mobilize the Enterprise. Point.io is a Backend-as-a-Service (BaaS) platform and application-programming interface (API) – designed to enable enterprises to access and share even their most sensitive information with absolute security. With custom mobile applications built on the Point.io platform, employees, business and channel partners can now access and share real time information across enterprise boundaries on any device regardless of where the content is stored.

You can reach me at jboyle@point.io

Founded ALL BALL! a small basketball services company. Started in 2003 and sold in 2008.
