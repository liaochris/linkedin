Founder and President of the Board of Directors

Charity for Debt Non Profit 501(c)(3)

May 2009 – Present (4 years 6 months) Norman, Oklahoma

Charity for Debt is a crowd funding system for top quality charitable staffing projects, paid for through student loan debt reduction and tax deductible donations from individuals and organizations.

"Projects" can be funded for as little as $5, which is tax deductible, we have our 501c3 status approved. Projects range in size and scope and determined by the Nonprofit organization needing the professional staffing support.

Most projects are in the $500 range. As soon as a project begins, all of those who helped crowd fund that, usually local, project - will then get the money deducted from their account and a letter for 501c3 tax credit will be sent out automatically.

The staffers will then begin the project, reporting back to all those who donated on his/her/their behalf. The staffers will post updates on a daily basis and donors can have the option to receive these updates in their Social Media news, Email, or a number of other Internet savvy ways. This truly engages the donor to be a part of their contribution.

Once the project is complete, a final outcome proofsheet will be created and sent to all that is involved. This proofsheet will be archived on Charity for Debt for all to see, forever. This content generation will provide enormous SEO advantages for search engine marketing strategies.

Donors can search for projects by many categories like nonprofit sector, location, or profession. The donor can then view individual project pages and decide to add funds in various increments to any project.

The Donor truly can be a part of the experience. The Nonprofit obtains a professional consultant to staff projects or tasks as needed for free. The staffer gets their student loan reduced directly for hourly, tracked and verified time. The Donor then gets the tax deduction.
