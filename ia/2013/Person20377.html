Caroline Miller's Summary

I am an Applied Positive Psychologist and accredited performance coach who works with groups and individuals in the fields of motivation and goal accomplishment. My training is unique and highly specialized to help people enhance their well-being while also identifying and going after valued goals. To that end, I am the author of "Creating Your Best Life: The Ultimate Life List Guide" (Sterling 2009) that shows readers the connection between happiness and goals, and how to accomplish meaningful goals with the science of goal setting and Positive Psychology research. The book has sold out repeatedly both in hardback and paperback, has been translated into multiple languages, and is also used as a textbook. My coaching includes master level classes on goal setting and the application of Positive Psychology principles.

In addition to coaching, I keynote and speak at conferences and gatherings around the world about how to set goals and create the optimal conditions for success from an evidence-based perspective, along with leadership development, flourishing in the workplace, and the top findings on happiness and change . I also teach Positive Psychology and Coaching at the University of Texas School of Management, and have also taught at the NYU School of Continuing and Professional Studies. I wrote a chapter on Positive Psychology and its application to coaching that was published in a French Positive Psychology textbook in 2011 ("Traite de psychologie positive").

I've been a fixture on television and radio for twenty years because of my six books, and was the "happiness expert" on Washington, DC television for two years, as well as the first Positive Psychologist on satellite radio (XM Channel 155). I'm considered an authority on goal accomplishment and its connection to well-being, and have been interviewed by dozens of newspapers, magazines, television and radio shows around the world. A full list of media can be found at my site www,carolinemiller.com.

Specialties

Motivational speaking, teaching and coaching for motivated and energetic clients who want to take ownership of their lives and leave behind a life legacy of which they are proud.
