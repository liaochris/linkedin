Andrew Morton's Summary

Marketing and Public Relations Executive who’s led organizations to unprecedented levels of brand awareness through innovative and interactive strategic communications practices in traditional and new media.

After serving 20-years as an Army Officer (Infantry and Public Affairs) I'm now honored to serve as a social and digital media account executive for Purple Strategies, a one-of-a-kind full-service PR and advertising firm in Alexandria, VA.

Working with a wide-range of clients across the public and private sector I remain passionate about what I believe are the pillars of successful digital campaigns-

- Sharing an organization’s story by developing content that’s both real and relevant.
- Executing a community-management strategy that is responsive and sustainable.
- Creating strategic growth that enables an organization to shape its brand internally and externally.
- Training Executives and PR Managers on strategy, best practices and cultural intricacies of Social Media.

I believe the leadership opportunities of my military career afforded me both the practical insight and vision necessary to lead organizations through even the toughest of communications challenges.

Specialties: public relations executive, marketing, account and brand management, social media, web development, media marketing and relations, strategic communications, advertising, TV and digital production, market analysis, speech writing, media training, event management.
