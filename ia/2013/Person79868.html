Stephen Negron's Summary

Stephen Negron is the President of LegalTech Consulting, Inc. His firm serves hundreds of small businesses in Brooklyn and Manhattan with their COMPUTER FORENSIC, SECURITY and TECH SUPPORT needs. LegalTech Consulting specializes in helping Attorneys, Accountants and Doctors save money, increase productivity, and eliminate unplanned downtime through effective IT support and planning. Their highly personalized services are dedicated to these company's special needs since they require a higher level of security.

He is legally considered an EXPERT in the field of Information Technology in Federal court and State courts of New York, New Jersey and Connecticut, and has testified in Civil, Bankruptcy and Criminal trials in those venues. In his more than 30 years of experience, he has become an expert in LITIGATION SUPPORT and has garnered the accolades of many attorneys and numerous Community Service Awards.

A graduate of Columbia University, he is a founding member of the Senate IT Committee and chairs the Manhattanville Development IT Sub-Committee overseeing technology in the $9 billion project.

He has co-authored numerous publications, and has written and produced technology stories for ABC News where he won an Emmy Award, and was responsible for the Network’s financial and security systems.
