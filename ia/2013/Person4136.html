Alexandros Kogkas' Projects

  • Educational trip to U.S - Visit at U.C Berkeley & Stanford University

    • April 2012 to April 2012
    Team Members: Alexandros Kogkas, Charalampos Michailidis, Konstantinos Moustidis, Savvas Gkantonas, Philip Protogeridis, Antonis Manousis

    Participated in an educational trip, organised by members of the Greek students communities in Berkeley (Hestia), Stanford (Hell.A.S) and M.I.T ( H.S.A of M.I.T) in cooperation with Corallia, under COSINE scholarship.

    Selected among the Greek Universities students, this trip aimed to provide to a limited group of 17 students, a real experience of the academic environment in the U.S.

    Attending courses, lectures and presentations in Berkeley, Stanford and Silicon Valley, the programme aimed to provide the students with an essential experience in order to try and improve the Greek academic environment.
