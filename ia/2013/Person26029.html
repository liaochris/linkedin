☞ WHY

Every day of every week, 100's of small businesses close their doors. Many of these closures could have been avoided if ONLY the business owner had gotten help. Help exists, does it mean letting go of your ego - well yes! does it mean your failing as a business owner - well NO! it does show a level of business maturity that is a requirement if you want to start run build and grow a successful business!

This is WHY we exist. To help save as many businesses as we possibly can!


☞ HOW

We use a four step coaching process with our clients.

☞ First, we work together to get clarity on what you want your business to provide for you in relation to your Income, Lifestyle, wealth and Equity.It is much easier to attract something new into your life when you are clear about what you really want. We have some powerful exercises that will help you quickly gain clarity.

☞ Next, using the ILWE plan we have just developed, we create a strategic plan to act as a roadmap for your business based on your vision and designed to provide alignment within your business. This is the core of what will drive your every day decisions in your business.

☞ Building on that forward momentum, I support you in creating your quarterly and weekly goals that are designed to drive your business. Your quarterly and weekly goals includes specific action steps in two primary areas: eliminating the things that pull you away from the “value” activities in your business and no longer serve you and creating new habits and behavior that keep you focused on the top priorities.

☞ Finally, as your coach, I keep you focused, motivated and accountable as you implement your plan. As you know, change isn’t always easy. Setbacks can occur. Limiting beliefs may surface. Through our coaching you will discover how to overcome any challenges that may arise, enabling you to continue steadily forward as you create a business that is built to serve you and not the other way.
