Evenson Construction Sand and Gravel WI's Summary

Evenson Construction Company Inc. was started in 1957 as a family owned business providing sand and gravel products and excavating services. Our main activity has been to process and deliver sand, stone, washed aggregate, and crushed stone products. Evenson has expanded to numerous pit and quarry locations throughout Manitowoc, Calumet, Sheboygan, Fond du Lac, Washington, Jefferson, and Rock counties.

Evenson Construction has equipment and materials to complete any industrial, commercial, or residential project.

Evenson Construction has ongoing wholesale and retail production of aggregate from pits and quarries owned or leased. We provide aggregate to the surrounding area including the following counties: Manitowoc, Sheboygan, Calumet, Fond du Lac, Washington, and Jefferson.

Evenson Construction will custom crush and/or wash material to meet your needs. In the past and with a lead time, Evenson has produced material for contractors or local counties or townships for large scale projects they need exacting specifications for.

Services Provided:
1. Custom Crushing and/or Washing
2. Rental Trucking
3. Snowplowing
4. Excavating Services:
Building Pads, Excavate Manure Pits, Driveways, Roadways, Recycled Asphalt Driveways, Roads, Feed Pads, Demolition Projects, General Site Prep, Ponds, Subdivisions, Parking Lots, and Ditches.

Aggregates Provided:
Stone Products: 1 ½” Washed Stone, ¾” Washed Stone, ½” Washed Stone, 3/8” Washed Pea Stone, 3”-6” Round Stone, ¾” Clear Stone, Breaker Run, 3” Minus, 1 ¼” Crushed Stone, ¾” Crushed Stone, Screenings

Sand Products: Washed Concrete Sand, Mound Sand, Mason Sand, Farmers Sand, Screened Sand, Washout Sand, Backfill Sand, Road Sand, Sandfill

Other Products: Pit/Bank Run, Concrete Gravel, ¾” Road Gravel, Large Landscape Rocks, Tailings
