Dean Ashley's Summary

I have 17 years industry experience, and, as an artist, have worked on over 11 AAA titles for Sony Computer Entertainment Europe. Highlights include being part of the WipEout, MotorStorm and the Formula One franchises and having experience working with a number of consoles (PS2, PSP, PS3, PSVita ,Ps4 and Xbox One).

During my career, I have worked most disciplines including vehicles, environments and general assets, a few examples of which can be found on my website. I am currently working on completing and uploading non work related examples.

I have excellent high and low polygon modelling skills including a good knowledge of materials, shaders and rendering. I also have a wealth of knowledge and experience of game pipelines, as most of the projects I have worked on involved taking an asset from the concept and design phase, right through to exporting the game data, and getting it to run in the engine.

I am self motivated, intelligent and enthusiastic with the ability to motivate others. I have a true passion for games (understatement!), great attention to detail, and a desire to create outstanding entertainment experiences. I am a good communicator and a great team-player, I maintain good relationships with other team members and I always ensure high quality work is delivered on time achieving all set targets and deadlines. I can work to any art style required.

I also have experience guiding and mentoring other artists, training in Maya, 3DSMax and Photoshop.
