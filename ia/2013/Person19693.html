Tia Lilly's Summary

I am a Realtor on Cape Cod who specializes in Waterfront and Beach Homes, assisting people who want to purchase or sell a vacation home on the Cape. Fortunately, I have had the pleasure of growing up on the Cape, so I know it very well. The beaches are amazing, with over 100 beaches to choose from, along with the 15 towns that comprise Cape Cod, offering much versatility in deciding where to reside.
All 15 towns are wonderful and unique in their own way. The ocean waters are very different depending on which town you are in or “what side of the Cape” you are on - North or South. For example, the North side of the Cape is where famous Route 6A runs from the Sagamore Bridge to the lower part of the Cape. This is the what we refer to as the “bay side". Then there is the South side of the Cape where Route 28 crosses towns, working its way from the bridges through the towns into the lower cape. This is what we refer to as the “ocean side". One side may be more appealing depending on where your interests lie and what you enjoy about the ocean.
This is one of the areas where my expertise is very helpful to my clients. I like to get to know what my client's real estate goals are, as it better helps me to assist them in obtaining their goals. I feel it is important to be there for my clients throughout the whole process, which includes the beginning stage or the "thinking about” stage, to long after their purchase or sale. It is a process which can move faster for some and slower for others, but for me it is about what is best for my client. I always say “I am not going anywhere, so whenever you're ready.” I am very grateful for my clientele, as I have had the opportunity to work with such amazing, successful, interesting, nice people for whom I feel fortunate to know every day!
