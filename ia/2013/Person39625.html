Edmar Mendizabal's Summary

As Director of Business Development for the leading online server and cloud services provider for the games industry (we are Electronic Arts' primary server provider for Battlefield 3 worldwide) I bring my game development experience from my past work as Producer for THQ's Kaos Studios (Frontlines, 2008 & Homefront, 2011) to GlobalGameServers.com (MISi3D, LLC).

With business offices in Rotterdam (The Netherlands) and Los Angeles (United States), and thousands of servers across the globe, we have a rich history of working on projects of all sizes and needs. If you've played AAA games over the last decade with any online component, it is very likely you have played on our servers.

My goal is to increase the reach of our services while continuing to maintain the lowest per server cost in the industry, which is largely attributed to no overhead on sales reps and growth through customer referrals. My experience as a game developer further reinforces our commitment to offer developers a dedicated server team that only serves the games industry, which sets us far apart from all competitors. We know the distinct challenges of each phase of game development and our team works alongside yours through every milestone.
