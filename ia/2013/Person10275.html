Ashley Adams' Summary

With 20 years of legal experience and education, Ashley Adams has garnered recognition, respect and expertise in the legal community of Arizona. Certified by the State Bar of Arizona and honored with awards from the Phoenix Business Journal, and Cambridge’s Who’s Who in America, Ms. Adams is an active lecturer, featured key-note speaker across the country, as well as an Adjunct Professor at Arizona State University Sandra Day O’Connor College of Law.

PERSONAL ATTENTION

Unlike in large firms in which many cases are handed off to a junior lawyer or paralegal, Ms Adams is a seasoned managing partner and provides personal attention and aggressive representation.

Notably winning difficult cases by thorough investigation, hard work and diligent preparation, Ashley Adams prides herself on the relationships built with each client and the successful outcomes that are achieved through direct representation.

PROVEN TRACK RECORD

Whether you are faced with government or civil charges, early and creative trial strategy designed and executed by a proven law firm can help achieve the most ideal result. Ashley D. Adams PLC has a well established record and a skill set that can make a difference, whether defending white-collar crime charges, government investigations, fraud charges, corporate litigation or Health Care Fraud.

Ashley D. Adams PLC focuses exclusively obtaining dismissals of criminal charges and claims in the early stages of a criminal case or investigation and winning cases at trial. This defense model differs from many law firms because Ashley D. Adams accepts a select number of cases each year, and dedicated to each of them a cohesive team of legal experts, investigators and outside consultants. The team is led by the firm’s principal partner Ashley Adams, who founded the firm with the goal of attaining excellence in the field of criminal defense.
