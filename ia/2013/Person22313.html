Charlie Zeleny's Summary

Charlie Zeleny is a Virtuoso Drum Solo Artist and a New York City Session Drummer, Music Director and Producer most popular for his work playing duo with Jordan Rudess from Dream Theater and his drum duet work with Terry Bozzio for The Drum Channel.

He also gained notoriety playing a nonstop 8 minute Drum Solo called "Drumageddon" up an entire Brooklyn Building in one continuous take. Charlie has toured 39 US states, 19 countries and is on 70+ album releases and was an original member of virtuoso progressive metal bands Blotted Science and Behold the Arctopus.

Charlie has been also been able to perform with such acts as The Great Kat, Joe Lynn Turner, Kristin Chenoweth, Warner Bros. artist Outasight & XV and has opened for such acts as The Blue Oyster Cult, Akon, Between the Buried and Me & Bon Jovi in Puerto Rico.

Charlie has been seen & heard on MTV, VH1, E!, XM & Sirius Satellite Radio, Oxygen TV, The View, AOL Sessions, The CBS Morning Show, WPIX, NFL Network, on Tone Maker DJ Ringtone Creator featuring Method Man & Ceelo Green and on various major label projects on Metal Blade, Hydrahead, Sensory, Sony Music & Warner Bros. Records like hiphop artist XV (rapper) & Outasight.

In live performances, he as played such venues as Irving Plaza, Lincoln Center, Iridium Jazz Club, and a New York Jets half time show with the Broadway act Rock of Ages at New Meadowlands Stadium (formerly Giants Stadium) for 82,500 fans.

He has also played The Bowery Ballroom, Six Flags Mainstage, Avatar Recording Studios, Sony Music Studios, Highline Ballroom, Cove City Sound Studios, BB Kings Jazz & Blues Club and The Knitting Factory NYC & LA among many others. He's performed at and has been a featured artist at endorser signings at the NAMM Show in California and has played radio station Z100's Zootopia Concert at the Izod Arena.

Charlie Z is featured as a member of the Sick Drummer Magazine "Hall of Fame" and also in Blender Magazine as "One of the Best Drummers Of All Time".

Specialties

Contact Charlie Z through his Official Website www.CharlieZeleny.com for info on how to hire him for gigs, tours and recordings both on location and remotely at one of his two Protools studios. Charlie has a full Protools rig and FTP server to remotely record drum tracks for artists anywhere in the world. He is also available for live gigs and touring worldwide.
