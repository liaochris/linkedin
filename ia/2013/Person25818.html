David Meikle's Summary

David Meikle is the Conservative Councillor for Pollokshields Ward on Glasgow City Council.

David lives on the southside of Glasgow. David attended local state schools and graduated from Glasgow University in 2005 with an honours degree in politics.

Before entering politics David worked in customer service and audit. During his second term as a councillor David worked with Yuva Consultants as a consultant specialising in online marketing.

David is a moderate Conservative who believes in the free market, lower taxes and the Union.

Specialties: Local government
