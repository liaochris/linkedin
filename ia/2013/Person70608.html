Juliano Dasilva's Summary

I'm Juliano (Moreira) Dasilva, Co-Founder and Product Designer of Cympel. We are a nimble internet start-up focused on solving an interesting problem with advertising online. The experience of online ads is cumbersome and unpleasant with many redirects misleading customers from their intent to purchase.
We're developing transactional ads that are responsive and incredibly engaging and run on top our e-commerce platform.

Prior to Cympel, I spent seven years working as UI/UX designer for Best Buy, one of the largest electronic retailers in the U.S. with millions of customers daily, focused on e-commerce and user interaction. With a strong coding experience, I was responsible for designing key campaigns and web applications to the business ecosystem being used by over 200 million people across the globe, by transforming data and business requirements into relevant and meaningful web experiences. Additionally, I was involved in partnerships with brands such as Google, Bose, Epson, Samsung, and LG, helping them create a solution that reflected Best Buy's vision online.

As a designer, I'm constantly observing how people interact with digital products, their reactions and what makes a great user experience. My job as a designer is to enchant users through their actions — swiping, clicking, seeing — providing them with a remarkable satisfaction across platforms. With the rapid adoption of mobile devices, I've been fully immersed in the implementation of responsive (rwd) techniques on the web, catering to a greater selection of devices, from tablets to televisions, and everything in between. I'm constantly testing and iterating responsive design experiences.

In May 2012, I became co-organizer of the Minnesota Web Design Professionals, the largest web design community in Minnesota with 900+ active members. Our objective is to bring people together to make the web a better place.
