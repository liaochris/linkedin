Martijn de Vrij's Summary

More freedom
CINX is a network organisation of entrepreneurs. Entrepreneurs who are involved with their customers and each other. Together they are building a strong brand and obtaining a rightful place on the market. Through a strong brand they add more value to customers and each other. That provides the quality to succeed and forms the basis of entrepreneurial independence . Independence to work together, network together, share knowledge, explore new markets and to start new partnerships.

More transparency
CINX is completely transparant to both customers and partners. Transparancy in rates and low margins and transparant in quality control. CINX does business directly with managers from large corporations. That saves money for the entrepreneur ànd the customer, who will have a very committed professional to do the job.

More content
CINX-professionals have typical entrepreneurial quailities like perseverance, involvement and sense of responsibility. We aim high at CINX. That is why extended reference checks are part of our recruitment process. Quality is key for us. This way clients will know for sure that the CINX professional will get the required results, but will also fit into their organisational culture.

More value
The heart of CINX is formed by independent (contractor) professionals who are exclusively connected to CINX. They always remain entrepreneurs and will never lose their individuality or independence. Through an innovative franchise formula they make use of marketing materials and sales support, share knowledge, join (social) events and many other benefits offered by the CINX network. The objective of CINX is the objective of its partners: successful entrepreneurship!
