Zupe .'s Summary

Living the true Gemini split personality; Zupe is a Composer, Musician, and Producer, with strengths in Jazz/Fusion and World music, suitable for film and television, commercial, and corporate soundtrack work. ALSO a highly acclaimed musical entertainer. Zupe's one-man show continues to delight audiences across the north-atlantic region, performing the timeless hits of the swing era. But sometimes (remember that Gemini split personality?), Zupe performs a throw-back show from the 80's, which has proven just as successful!

Specialties: Keyboards, Vocals, and audio production.
