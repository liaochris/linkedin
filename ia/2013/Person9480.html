Arian Noordzij's Summary

After my school i joined Imaje as service engineer.
Imaje is a company specialized in industrial coding solutions.
Until 2007 I had different job functions within Imaje like; Helpdesk, Customer and International trainer, PreSales-Projects and finally Service manager.

In 2007 I started at Tradepack as Project Manager. After a couple of months I joined Alserco as Technical Manager. Alserco was a service related company in coding.

March 2009 I made the transfer to (a totally new business for me) Taxameter located in Amsterdam to start in the Project team and after 4 months I was asked to make a job transfer to Teamleader Field Service which I did.

After more than two successful years at TMC I decided to go back in the Inkjet business at the company Markem-Imaje to join the team of PreSales & Service with focus (for me) on software, communication and continuous inkjet systems.

Specialties

Communication (Serial, ethernet, I/O), Service related (high level) solution based thinking.
