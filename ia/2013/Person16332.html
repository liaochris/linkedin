Dr. Carolanne Roach's Summary

I help children and adults overcome visual problems that interfere with learning, reading, computer work, and other daily activities.

www.brainandeyeconnection.com / 405-703-3163

I help patients through a specific type of optometry designed to retrain the brain how to use vision more effectively.

Common Symptoms Related to Vision that I treat:

Double vision
Headaches with near work
Words run together when reading
Falling asleep when reading
Seeing worse at the end of the day
Skipping/repeating lines when reading
Avoiding near work/reading
Omiting small words when reading
Reading comprehension down
Holding reading material too close
Trouble keeping attention on reading
Poor eye/hand coordination (i.e. handwriting)

For more symptoms check out our FREE vision quiz at www.brainandeyeconnection.com/vision_quiz.htm

I have also added low vision and vision rehabilitation services to my skill set and currently perform these services with NewView Oklahoma

www.newviewoklahoma.org
