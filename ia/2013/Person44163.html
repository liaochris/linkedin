Ludmila Adams' Summary

* Ludmila Adams is a Computer Aided Design Instructor, who is teaching Illustrator classes and Photoshop classes online, in person and on your computer. She has developed step by step training videos for that have been used by fashion design students as well as seasoned apparel professionals.

* Her teaching experience ranges from private to corporate classes, to teaching in person at Los Angeles trade technical college and for the FIDM alumni association in person and online

* Her fashion design experience includes years of lingerie design, being a cad artist for trend forecasting services as well as a textile and graphic designer for various apparel companies.

* Since creating www.FashionChalkboard.com, Ludmila strives to provide excellence in video training for fashion designers, crafters and many other creative individuals
