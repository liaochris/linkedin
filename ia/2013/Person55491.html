Gina Mims' Summary

Inspiring People to Love Your Business!

Gina Mims – Owner Inspiro Brands, Inc.
Inspiro is the Brand Strategy and Graphic Design Firm for the Restaurant, Hospitality & Lifestyle Industries and Inspiro’s Leader/Founder, Gina Mims, has developed the brand identity for over 100 dining destinations. She is the Vice President and Co-Creator of the OC Restaurant Association where she leads the branding and design of all marketing materials for countywide “foodie” events, including Orange County Restaurant Week and The Golden Foodie Awards.

Gina serves Les Dames d’Escoffier Los Angeles/Orange County as the Communications Chair, has taught graphic design at Chapman University and is an Advisory Board member for AIGA (the professional association of design). She presents “Branding The Experience” and “Define then Design” to restaurant, marketing and design groups nationally to guide leaders and marketers to create “WOW’ brand for their customers.

SERVICES OFFERED - We Specialize in Branding Business To Consumer Companies

• Start Up Consultation: “Define Then Design™ Brand Strategy Plan”

• Makeover Consultation: “The 20 Step Brand Touch Point Evaluation”

• Brand Design Development:
Logos, Menus, Marketing Collateral, Advertising, Website Navigation, Packaging, Promotional Materials Investor & Branding Books

• Business Strategy & Project Coordination:
We work as a team with many resources – interior designers, architects, event planners, photographers, food consultants, PR firms, signage manufacturers, etc. We are able to make business recommendations for many services or work directly with vendors of your choosing.

Our goal is to work as part of the entire team from the start to create the complete branded customer experience.
