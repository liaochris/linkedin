Diana L. May, SPHR, CPBA, CPVA's Summary

Certified professional with over 20 years experience in the areas of HR leadership, employee relations, development, and strategic workforce planning. I have worked closely with organizations on talent assessment, performance coaching for individuals and teams, conflict resolution, improving organizational communication, and leadership development with an emphasis on increasing emotional and social intelligence skills.

Specialties include evaluating and addressing organizational concerns through customizing programs to achieve bottom line results. These results are achieved by leveraging highly validated assessment tools, training and coaching protocols to help organizations select, integrate, develop, and retain top performers.
