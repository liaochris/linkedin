Jaclyn Gault's Summary

I have planning experience working for and with public agencies and private companies including the City of Seattle, the City of Bainbridge Island, WSDOT, CH2M Hill, and Heery International.

I have a Masters in Urban Planning from the University of Washington with a Certificate in Urban Design, as well as a BA in Urban Studies from San Francisco State University.
