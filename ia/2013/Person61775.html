Jennifer Cifuentes' Summary

I am one of those people that LIVE to WORK, not the other way around.

For most of my life I have been fascinated with what makes people tick and how things work. In my professional career, I’ve used this natural curiosity to help me uncover hidden insights that can be used to create marketing that is effective in meeting an organization's goals and objectives.

Throughout my experience as a designer and a marketer, I've learned that I have a gift for story telling. All great marketing tells a story that connects and compels, right?

Now I spend my day focusing on writing and publishing my own stories, through my various works of fiction, and writing the stories of others through the eMagazines of One Hundred Fountains.

My first ePublication, Atlanta Cheer Magazine, launched in March 2012. In the first two hours of launch, we had more than 4,000 downloads. Before the first month's end, we had over 30,000 downloads and that number continues to grow. Check it out at AtlantaCheer.com.
