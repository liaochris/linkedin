Megy Karydes' Summary

Highly motivated and results-oriented professional with exceptional writing and marketing communications skills. More than 20 years experience in major areas of profit and non-profit management, magazine feature article writing, marketing and communications, public relations, business-to-business and business-to-consumer strategic marketing and communications, business and promotional writing, direct mail campaign strategy and execution, promotional campaign development, marketing plan and strategy development, budget planning, event planning and advertising.

Currently serve as President of Karydes Consulting, a boutique marketing communications agency focused on delivering results to our clients as well as providing quality, well-written articles on sustainability, travel, health, fair trade and retail to both consumer and b2b media outlets. To learn more, visit www.KarydesConsulting.com.

Specialties: Writing, marketing, communications, publicity, media relations, Web site copy, copywriting, freelance magazine and newspaper writing and reporting
