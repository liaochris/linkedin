  • Ишь ты, Масленица!

    • January 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Albert Poghosyan, Hayk Geghamyan, Aren Apikyan, Hayk Sahakyants, Edgar Mkrtchyan

    История о том, как жадный помещик обложил крестьян налогами за землю, за воду и даже за снег. Богач забирает у стариков последний горшок с маслом, а мальчик — внук стариков, едет за богачом в его поместье и хитростью возвращает старикам их добро.

    Приложение состоит из 3-х разделов:

    1. «Почитай мне» - озвучено профессиональными актерами.
    2. «Читаю сам» – без озвучивания.
    3. «Играть и шалить» – игры с героями.

    Пять нтересных и веселых игр:
    1. Масленица. Помоги бабушке испечь блины.
    2. Склад. Помоги сторожихе расставить предметы по своим местам.
    3. Погоня. Помоги мальчику сбежать от богача.
    4. Снежки. Кидай снежками в богача и остальных.
    5. Ночной стрелок. Помешай злой собаке.

    Оригинальные иллюстрации и озвучивание профессиональными актерами делают книжку максимально похожей на известный одноименный мультфильм.

  • Kids at Home

    • November 2011 to February 2012
    Team Members: Albert Poghosyan, Hayk Geghamyan, Aren Apikyan

    Kids at Home is an educational application for kids. It tells about a boy and a girl who live in a beautiful town house with their pets. The game revolves around the things in their house (objects) and all the fun (actions) they have together.

    Game basics:
    - The app is designed to teach the 99 most essential words used at home.
    - The words are separated into two groups, objects and actions, and are spread out throughout the house in five different rooms.
    - There are sub-levels, such as the fridge, wardrobe, commode and kids’ table, which each are filled with additional objects. To see them you have to touch the object.
    - In each room, you can play with the main characters (boy, girl, duck, piggy, dog, cat) by touching and moving them.
    - Each object can be singled out with audio in correct English pronunciation.
    - Navigation is easy and intuitive, so kids can get the hang of it all by themselves.

  • MyFamily HD

    • July 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Albert Poghosyan, Aren Apikyan, Hayk Geghamyan, Mariam Ohanyan, Edgar Mkrtchyan, Aram Rián

    Finally an app that brings family bonding, essential parenting lessons and sharing with relatives all in one place!

    How Your Family Benefits
    - Imagine giving your kids a video game where their parents are the characters they interact with!
    - Have your kids interact virtually with their relatives and stay engaged with family life while touching up house rules and manners.
    - Watch your kids be fully engaged in our games and family life while giving you a hand with essential parenting lessons.
    - Experience a truly human touch when you boost your little gamer's morale, with your own personalized voice recorded encouragement from you or your spouse.

    Getting Started is EASY
    - Upload a photo of each family member's face and they become a character in the game instantly.
    - Upload three facial expressions: happy, neutral, and sad and your kids get instant feedback on their performance. The game displays your character's "happy face" when they succeed, and your "sad face" when they aren't doing so well.
    - Once you have set up the app with your facial expressions and voices, the game is set up with an intuitive touch that toddlers will pick up with little difficulty.
    - Simply show them the app, and cherish your toddler’s priceless moments bonding and learning as they play.

    Bring Your Family Closer Together
    - Add grandma to the mix as you video capture the game and send her an e-mail or better yet upload your child's game to youtube for all your relatives to see!
    - Share family portraits directly from the game via Facebook and Twitter.

    Our free game revolves around dining and table manners with many updates to come. Try it out and give us your comments and feedback.
