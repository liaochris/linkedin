Alec Morgana's Summary

Alec Morgana began his work at a very young age, writing children’s stories and producing videos as a hobby. His areas of interest have since then expanded to realizing the full potential of the internet and social media. He creates mobile applications for the iPhone, iPod touch and iPad as well as manages several of his own websites. His entrepreneurial endeavours to date have reached over 1.2 million people worldwide. In 2012, Alec was named one of South Delta's Top 30 Under 30 and one of Vancouver's Top 24 Under 24. He is known as a tech enthusiast, Apple product lover and passionate entrepreneur. Alec’s areas of expertise include websites, social media marketing, mobile app development and video production.

When Alec isn’t managing his own businesses, he enjoys contributing his talents and vision to other businesses and organizations. He is always open to meeting people and exploring new ideas. Contact Alec for business as well as speaking opportunities.
