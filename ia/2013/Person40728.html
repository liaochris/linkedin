Elizabeth Monier-Williams' Projects

  • WaveCheck on Indiegogo

    • October 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Elizabeth Monier-Williams, Gabi Gomes, Brad Breininger, Marko Zonta, Fazila Seker, Garus Booth, Kyle Kargov

    WaveCheck is a painless, non-surgical clinical technique for women with breast cancer. It combines traditional ultrasound with new software to detect response to chemotherapy within weeks, not months.

    When we learned of this amazing new technology, we were thrilled to be able to name it, brand it, and produce a video to support it — and get the message out. It’s a special thing when creativity meets technology and has a real impact on people’s lives. Cancer has touched many of us, so any type of advancement in the field is exciting and gives people hope.

    To make WaveCheck available to women everywhere as fast as possible, MaRS Innovation is using Indiegogo, a crowdfunding platform, to help bring the technology to more hospitals for testing.

    If you’d like to support WaveCheck and help get it into more hospitals, visit Indiegogo: http://bit.ly/1bgkHvy
