Daniel da Rocha's Summary

Young architect eager to collaborate in projects ranging from urban planning to product design. Large experience with architectural competitions, high-pressure situations and team-work. Main interest lies in exploring new possibilities of algorithmic/computational driven design strategies. Currently developing research in the field of generative scripting, and teaching this subject at the Bauhaus-Dessau Master Degree program.
