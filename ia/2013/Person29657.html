Daniella Matutes' Summary

I am an artist who is also a passionate advocate for the user of systems. I love solving tricky system design problems employing User Centered-Design methodologies and techniques- creating meaningful experiences.

Clients come to me with enthusiasm and great ideas. Even though they may be asking for a tangible- website, blog, marketing campaign, social media strategy, email newsletter- really, I am solving their problem through artistic service. It is empowering for people to see a concept take shape, their ideas come to life and their organization grow and I feel honored to be a part of this process.

So, what do I *really* do? I design & build awesome user experiences & communities using fresh ideas, rich visuals and compelling content that is: Measureable, Accessible, Sustainable & Profitable.

I dove into website design and development in 1996 and have created several online businesses, e-learning campuses and learning management systems and built branding and marketing solutions for major corporations, small businesses and large nonprofits. My portfolio is diverse, both stylistically and organizationally.

I bring a rare blend of high-level artistic ability, strategy and technical expertise. I can write, design and structure content, and also create award winning visuals that greatly enhance user experiences. I really enjoy applying my organizational knowledge and user-centered design techniques to solve problems- combining what the users want with what the organization needs.
