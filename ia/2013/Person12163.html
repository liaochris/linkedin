Jason Boyce's Summary

I am a highly experienced visual artist with a passion for crafting media and messages that matter.

With experience in every visual medium from the web, print and magazine, to theatre screens and television screens, I have worked with a diverse variety of clients in San Francisco and across the US, in Great Britain, and El Salvador to craft their message and deliver it in the most impressive way possible.

My web experience is extensive. I have experience building and managing digital video accounts, distributing films on the internet and using analytics to measure the activity of the online communities enjoying them. I have also designed and built websites, logos, and social media accounts for a number of clients, including restaurants, musicians and professionals.

My print portfolio includes posters for films like Into the Current, guides for school curriculums, DVD covers, advertising for magazine and film festival guides, event flyers, buttons of all sizes, postcards, restaurant menus, and logos for restaurants, bands, films and businesses.

I have thorough experience in film from story crafting to script, storyboards to shooting, and from editing to sound mix to special effects. I have directed commercials, film and video game trailers, shot documentaries and music videos, edited and crafted special effects for clients like Microsoft, eBay, the Food Network and the San Francisco Giants.

In addition, as a teacher, I have worked with students as young as 7, and old enough that it would be impolite to ask. I’ve worked with groups that are 9-12, 16-24, and 55+. My classes on the essentials of film production and media literacy have taught me to communicate ideas effectively and concisely for people of wildly different backgrounds, so that we can work more closely together to create something better.

Specialties: Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere, After Effects, InDesign and Dreamweaver, Apple Final Cut Pro, Motion and Compressor, and Digidesign ProTools.
