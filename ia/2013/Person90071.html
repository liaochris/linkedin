Max Marty's Summary

Goal: To increase the pace of technological innovation and global human progress via radical but practical value creation.

Driving vision: The best innovations are those which fuel other innovation in a virtuous cycle of increasing prosperity. Technologies such as the use of electricity, efficient agriculture, clean running water, the information revolution, and ubiquitous computing, have all served as platforms to catapult us into the next era. Blueseed now stands on the shoulders of these giants of the past seeking to provide the next shoulder for innovation to stand upon. My goal is to make that vision a reality.
