Mathew Georghiou's Summary

CEO, entrepreneur, engineer, author. Designer and publisher of educational games, simulations, and social networks. Gamification and business consultant.

MEDIASPARK.com is producer and publisher of GoVenture - one of the most widely used lines of educational games and simulations in education. We have over a decade of experience creating industry innovations: www.MediaSpark.com/innovation

We also develop custom games, simulations, and social networks for other companies. Our current R&D efforts include the GoVenture Health gamified ebook, a number of mobile apps www.MediaSpark.com/apps and the GoVentureWorld.com MMORPG, which was identified as a top 50 startup in the world.

AT BRANDMETAL.com, we transform your brand message or logo into a premium metal sign, award, or work of art. Add a touch of sophistication to your desk, office, home, or building. Give a meaningful gift to a client or partner.
