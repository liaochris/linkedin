Lewis Myers' Experience

President and Owner

Right at Home in Maryland

September 2012 – Present (1 year 2 months) Rockville, Maryland

I own the Right at Home franchise covering Montgomery, Prince George's and Anne Arundel counties in Maryland. We provide home care and assistance through trained, insured and bonded caregivers who provide a variety of care services for the older folks who need extra help and adults with disabilities. We specialize in Fall Risk Mitigation, Dementia Care, Parkinson's Care, Care for Adults with Disabilities and Transition Care. Our mission is to improve the quality of life for those we serve.
