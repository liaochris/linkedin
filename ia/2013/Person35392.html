Richard C. "Dick" Price's Summary

I have practiced family law for over 35 years in Tarrant County, Texas. I have been a Board Certified Specialist in Family Law since 1984.

I am a big advocate of Collaborative Law and was one of the first group of attorneys trained in Collaborative Law in Tarrant County. I have been a leader in Collaborative Law here since 2000. Currently, I am on the Advisory Board of the Collaborative Law Institute of Texas; I was on the Board of Trustees for 7 years. Over the years, I have been a speaker for a variety of groups, including the State Bar of Texas, about Collaborative Law, family law and law office management issues.

I am also a Mediator for people going through divorces or having other Family Law issues. I work as a neutral third party to help people reach agreeable solutions outside of court.
