JP Stonestreet's Summary

Thinking of building a website or starting a web-based business?

In 1995, I taught myself how to program and there's been no looking back. I've been a serial Internet entrepreneur and web consultant for over 17 years. I've built dozens of websites for large and small companies, and I've created several of my own.

In 2003, I founded PromoteWare, which was a modular web development platform used to build client websites. In 2005, I founded QuoteCatcher.com, which was a business-to-business lead generation company. In 2008, I sold both websites to QuinStreet (QNST) in Foster City, CA.

Since then, I've been a volunteer at SCORE, counseling dozens of people who have the dream of starting their own business. This experience prompted me to write "The Web Startup Roadmap: Navigate Your Way to a Successful Online Business" as a way to share the painful and pleasant experiences that led to the successful sale of my web startup.

My goal is to help as many people as I can achieve their entrepreneurial dream so they can experience the same fulfillment and lifestyle that I have. With the right guidance and a lot of perseverance, anyone can do it. Will you be next?

Specialties: Automation, Start-ups, Acquisition-Transition management, Customer Service and Operations Managment, Business Strategy, Idea Generation and Concept Development, Creating Software to Solve Real-World Problems, Online Marketing and Search Engine Optimization Strategy
