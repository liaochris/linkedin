Tom Berarducci's Summary

Product Development Executive with deep knowledge in digital imaging technology, application software, and hardware devices. In-depth experience in all aspects of engineering management. Thought leader with recognized ability to innovate, make rapid, effective decisions under pressure, and solve complex problems in simple ways.

Successfully created and launched numerous software and hardware products in the mass-market consumer space. Recognized ability to develop and maintain strong relationships with global organizations. Strategic vision and leadership. Experienced at business and IP negotiations and analysis, complex team management, operations, and design. Standards participation and leadership. Internationally-experienced speaker and writer. Apple iOS application developer.

Accomplishments include:

• Created and launched cloud-based wireless gas detection & monitoring service for RAE Systems.

• Led the team that developed the world's first USB digital video camera, the Kodak DVC 300. This was the only Kodak digital camera ever designed and manufactured in the US.

• Led the team that created Kodak Easyshare software, a desktop photo management application with an installed base at its peak of over 40 million, second only to Adobe Photoshop and Microsoft Windows.

• Founding Board Member of SD-Association.

• Instrumental in the formation of such standards as USB, UPnP, and ISO-15740,

• Holder of nine U.S. patents.

Specialties: New product development and design; iOS, application software, embedded hardware, strategic planning, general management, operations, digital Imaging, architecture, and standards.
