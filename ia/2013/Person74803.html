Kevin C. Johnson's Summary

When you call in to this firm, you will speak with me. You will have your questions answered by an attorney and no one else and I will never have a paralegal call you back or answer your question. Also, I never bill my clients for phone calls or emails.

I understand that filing bankruptcy, being charged with a crime, getting a DUI, or filing for divorce can be terrifying and nerve wracking. I understand you want a straight and correct legal answer to your questions. That is why I make myself available to you. I will return you phone calls within 24 hours and I answer my email daily. You are my client and I will do everything to make sure your case goes smoothly. You are paying good money to get help, so you should have access to your attorney. I feel the same way. You pay for an attorney, you should get an attorney.

I have an after-hours emergency line for those who have been arrested. Call (586) 439-4297, extension 702. It will ring to wherever I am and I will answer.

I am a licensed attorney in the State of Michigan and admitted to the United States District Court for the Eastern District of Michigan.

I am looking to network with real estate agents, accountants, financial planners, and mortgage brokers.

Visit my bankruptcy site at www.johnsonwilk.net and receive my FREE e-Book on bankruptcy "Straight Talk About Bankruptcy: A Consumer Guide".

Visit my divorce/family law site at www.metrodetroitdivorcelawyer.com and receive my FREE e-Book "Divorce in Michigan: What you Need to Know".
