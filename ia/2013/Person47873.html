George Dearing's Projects

  • Food Shift Austin

    • September 2011 to Present
    Team Members: George Dearing, Brandi Clark Burton, Janis Bookout, Susan Meredith, Morgan Whitney, Thomas Heatherly, Mark McKim, Roger Chan, Ada Ryland, James Jolly clark, Dana F.

    A city-wide initiative to develop the culture and infrastructure for directing food to its highest and best use.   • USGBC Central Texas Green Building Resources Directory

    • March 2013 to Present
    Team Members: George Dearing, Effie Weaver, LEED AP BD+C, Pam Losefsky, Morgan Stinson, PE, LEED AP, Michael Sweeney, Michael Myers, Michael Wong, CPSM, LEED AP, Ada Ryland, Edgar Farrera

    The purpose of the USGBC Central Texas Green Building Resources Directory is to make commercial green building easier in Central and South Texas.

    The Directory is designed as a multi-faceted tool for commercial green building for new construction as well as operations & maintenance.
    It includes:
    - A company directory searchable by green building strategy, specification division, operations and maintenance budget line item, and keyword
    - A direct connection to commercial incentive programs offered by cities in the region (coming soon!)
    - A materials exchange that will enable people and projects to share surplus or reusable materials and improve efforts for sustainable sourcing (coming soon!)
    - An information hub and idea exchange for building-related trade organizations (coming soon!)

    We recently completed building the directory are excited to begin the process of taking on the early adopter listers. The directory will be exclusive in the sense that companies must meet certain criteria to be accepted as listers—they must have green building experience, must have properly trained people on staff, and must recognize sustainability as a core value throughout their business.
