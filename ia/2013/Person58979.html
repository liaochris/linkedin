Jason Adolf's Summary

Jason Adolf has over a decade of progressive experience in the field of Business Process Management (BPM). He is currently the Director of Process and Performance Management Practice at Serco Inc., and provider of professional, technology and management services focused on the Federal government. Jason overseas the Company’s involvement with Business Process Automation, collaboration technologies, and practice, change and talent management. In this position, Jason currently works with both the defense and civilian sides of the Federal government, including Department of Veterans Affairs, General Services Administration (GSA), Food and Drug Administration (FDA), Naval Special Warfare Group, Joint Staff, Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services and Department of Justice.

Prior to joining Serco in 2010, Jason was the Business Process Management Practice Lead at SRA International. Jason was responsible for standing up the BPM business practice within the Company’s Technology Solutions sector, and led the business from non-existent in 2007 to over 21 practitioners in 2010. He has also held positions at Headhunter.net, Shoedini.com, and the Association for Interactive Media. Jason holds a Bachelors degree from George Washington University.

Jason also serves as the Event Chair for the American Diabetes Association National Capital Tour de Cure. Since 2010, Jason has helped raise over $2.1M for diabetes research and the ride now ranks 7th out of 85 Tour de Cure rides across the United States.
