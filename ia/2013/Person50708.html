Lic. Gabriel Simonet's Summary

Gabriel Simonet - Innovation, Marketing and Strategic Consulting.

With a professional background in Marketing, Business and Technology, Gabriel is an engineering renegade who followed his vocation until he graduated in Advertising & Communications, later becoming a Post Graduated in Marketing. Yes, he's a mutant, half creative, half analytical and half funny.

During his career, Gabriel worked for international Clients, Companies, Entrepreneurs and Internet Startups, helping them develop strategies, improve products, achieve objectives, market brands and -most importantly- make money.

Panda Software, Turner Broadcasting Systems INC, Schneider Electric, The Coca Cola Company, TNU, Correo Nacional, ANTEL, CNCS, Rara Avis, Intendencia Municipal de Montevideo, Minics.com, Imprima3d.com, SalonLine.com.br, KillerStartups.com, HealthCare.com, IdeaFixa.com, CMedia.es, Startups.com Network, LiveClicker, HotSkoop.com, Wamba.com, Chic Parisien, UsingMiles.com, GeneXus, Bacardi, IADB, TEDx Montevideo, Havaianas & Plan Ceibal, are among the Brands, Companies, Sites and Projects he worked for.

Gabriel is a curious and creative guy, an innovation junkie, a generalist ninja, a cross-referencer, a challenging manager and an accomplished focaccia bread baker.

When nobody is looking, he writes stories and composes music and lyrics (he's both a published author and broadcasted producer), in a more-than-a-hobby but less-than-a-job kind of way.

He strongly believes in Human-Centered Design Process as an approach for Business and Products Innovation (he's an IDEO follower), as much as he is convinced that -like Siegel and Gale say-:

"Simple is Smart".
