Jodie O'Rourke's Summary

I'm an experienced Interactive Application Developer, technical author and team leader. My development expertise covers application development for mobile devices (iOS and Android) and browser run-times (Flash and Silverlight). I enjoy leading teams and developing dynamic and engaging experiences for a variety of devices, whilst maintaining high quality code, performance and standards conformity.

My roles have provided me with the opportunity to produce high-profile interactive applications for: Egg, Prudential, Citigroup, mobile applications for Picsel Technologies, and most recently browser, Internet television and mobile applications for blinkbox and Clubcard TV.

I have been an Adobe Community Professional (ACP) for 4 successive years.

Specialties: Leading the development of rich and engaging client-side applications for iOS, Android, Silverlight and Flash, as well as browser presentation layers with built with HTML, CSS & JavaScript.

Improving the efficiency and workflow of client-side development teams by implementing framework/component focused practices, agile & BDD methodologies, continuous integration and automated deployment processes.

Strengths: Objective-C | C# | Java | JavaScript | AS3.0 | MXML | XAML | HTML | CSS | Interaction design
