Allison Arthur's Summary

Impactful marketing isn't just a checklist of tasks. It's not just press releases, blogs, social media posts or event planning. It's about big ideas that resonate with target audiences and engage them with your brand. It's creative, yet focused thinking.

An experienced Marketing Coordinator with successful marketing campaigns behind me, I understand the fundamentals but am always looking to challenge myself and learn more.

I approach my daily tasks not as not a means to an end but as strategic ways to engineer business solutions and build brands. Every day I transition from social media manager to event planner to content developer and more, but I always carve out time to think strategically and brainstorm new ideas.

I love meeting new people. If you'd like to discuss my experiences, toss a few marketing ideas back and forth or just talk Star Wars, feel free to reach out to me anytime.

Specialties: writing, creative development, marketing strategy, social media management, content development, communications, newsletter management, digital advertising, search engine optimization, editing, team collaboration.
