Andy Bell's Summary

I am an experienced television/ film producer having created, produced and arranged the finance for a number of high profile international TV specials for ITV, BBC, PBS, et al, featuring some of the world's biggest names in classical music from Kiri Te Kanawa, Jessye Norman, Roberto Alagna and Jose Carreras. I produced the one of the highest rated South Bank Shows ever, on the Bee Gees, Keppel Road, which was sold in over 50 countries.

At present I am raising the finance for the BFi developed Cavegirl the Movie, a tween movie, recently announced at the Berlinale film market by sales agent Arclight Films set to be filmed in South Africa. Cavegirl is adapted from the hit CBBC series.

In 2006 I innovated the World Series of Backgammon® brand and TV series, a major international big money tour which attracted 100s of players and massive publicity and was subsequently acquired by French casino Groupe Partouche. Eurosport carried several series broadcasted in around 50 countries.

I also have a background in music videos and new media and have been profiled as a 'trailblaizer' in New Media Age (during the first dotcom boom!) for DinnerParty.tv, an improvised interactive online TV show which was "Commended" for 'Best Use of New Media' by the Broadcast Awards and coveraged extensively in the press including featured in the Financial Times.

I am currently developing a new business for a specialist digital channel.

Specialties: Film, TV producer (documentary, childrens/family drama, music and arts), online gaming
