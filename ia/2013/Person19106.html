Brandon Wood's Summary

Certified Commercial Investment Manager | CCIM | Utah, Real Estate Specialist | Salt Lake City, Real Estate Investments | Real Estate Investment Consultant | Real Estate Investment Broker | Utah, Commercial Real Estate, Investment Broker | Industrial | Office | Retail | Investment | Land

CCIM, Commercial Real Estate Broker for Newmark Grubb Acres specializing in Utah Commercial Investment Opportunities, Utah Real Estate and bringing investors together to maximize real estate investment portfolios.

For over a decade, Brandon Wood has been helping his clients maximize returns in their investment real estate portfolios. Focusing on a clients overall portfolio needs and objectives, Brandon consults with his clients to give them the tools to make the best real estate decisions. He is skilled at learning and understanding his client’s criteria and performing based on that criteria.

Brandon is a principal of Newmark Grubb Acres and Executive Vice President within the Investment Division. Since joining Newmark, he has been one of the top-producing agents in the company and the state on an annual basis, finishing as the top Newmark agent for multiple years. Brandon has demonstrated his extraordinary ability to help his clients succeed in the acquisition and disposition of investment properties and portfolios, completing over 500 transactions totaling hundreds of millions of dollars.

Brandon’s clients attest to the fact that he will always put his client's interests first. He tells clients not to buy and sell properties far more often than he makes a buy recommendation. It is critical that an investor knows that the advice they are receiving is genuine. It is this focus on candidness that forges trusting long-term relationships.
