Kenneth Lewis' Summary

Kenneth Lewis' Experience

President, Consultant, Chief Strategist

Clearinghouse Capital Partners

January 2011 – Present (3 years) Vallejo, CA

Clearinghouse Capital Partners is a minority owned business enterprise created with the goal of preserving and stabilizing faith based organizations with troubled assets while assisting banks with maximizing returns on those assets through loss mitigation services and consultation services thereby becoming a liaison between the lenders and the faith based organizations. Our mission is to "Save one Church at a time".
