Bo Allen's Summary

I've been doing web design and development since 1999.

It all started with working on an associate's degree in graphic design and multimedia from Westwood College in Denver. Not really "web developer" stuff there, but it's funny how one thing leads to another! I learned a lot about art, multimedia, and print design... but not so much about web design and development. We had a cheesy HTML class, but it got me interested.

The next thing you know, I'm up til 3 A.M. every night, fiddling with JavaScript, Flash, and HTML. The Photoshop training in college gave me a nice web design boost as well.

You might notice a "break" in my employment history right after Peak Business Media. Well, my dad had a major stroke. I wanted to go to Arkansas (where my parents lived at the time) and spend time with him. It was definitely good for the soul. I spent a lot of time teaching myself about computers and the internet. I gained a lot of skill in web design and development, building and fixing computers, and even internet marketing and SEO. A lot has changed since then, but I've been keeping up.

Well, living in Arkansas... the ticks, chiggers, cottonmouths, tree scorpions, giant spiders, scary townsfolk, rabid possums, and down-wind chicken house smells eventually made me decide to leave.

When I got back to Denver, that's when I started working at FedEx Office (formerly Kinko's). It sucked.

A year and a half later, my friend told me about a web design position open at the company he worked for (Inquisicorp). I went to the interview, did a test (which was to skin Zen Cart), and got hired!

I've been doing great there ever since! I started as a web designer, and within two years got promoted to web developer. I'm definitely more of a developer than a designer!

So there it is, the past ~10 years of my career. I enjoy what I do, and continue to learn and grow.
