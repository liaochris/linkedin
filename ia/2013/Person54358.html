X2 (UK) Ltd are an independent 4PL transport provider owned entirely by its management team, supplying logistics services for manufacturers, retailers and other transport and Logistics companies.

We provide managed transport services for retailers, FMCG manufacturers and logistics companies.

Our services include full load, part load and palletised transport from any location in the UK nationwide, as well as regular services to and from the continent.

We work with a wide range of transport partners providing tremendous flexibility and cost advantage for our customers by working to create efficiency by reducing empty running and utilising complimentary returning vehicles, reducing both carbon emissions and congestion.
