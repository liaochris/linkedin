Charles Beckwith's Summary

Charles Beckwith is a commercial artist with experience across radio, television, filmmaking, publishing, photography, theater, technology development, and fiction writing.

He is available as a producer, director, photographer, project manager, or consultant for all manner of media and creative technology projects.

Since 2008 he has been the editor and publisher of modaCYCLE, a unique independent web magazine focused on reporting about passions, innovation, craftsmanship, intelligence, and other examples of excellence in the fashion industry.

    Team Members: Charles Beckwith

    Curation is the future for fashion, both in the online media and online retail sectors (which have become almost indistinguishable), and understanding curation, despite the many questions it raises, is the key to moving forward progressively in the fashion and luxury goods industries.

  • Trade Shows Blog Post

    • March 2012 to April 2012
    Team Members: Charles Beckwith

    Fashion trade shows are an integral part of the business, but they are frustrating for many participants. There have been many changes for the New York garment trade shows in the last few years, but they are overwhelmingly the same thing they were 10 years ago or even 100 years ago. Change seems to be in the wind though, as operators and participants find them less and less efficient in the digital age, and it's going to be interesting to observe what shape they take next.
