Michelle Caldwell Thompson's Summary

Michelle Caldwell Thompson is the Principal Broker and Owner of CTI Real Estate.

CTI Real Estate is a privately owned real estate services firm. We are licensed in Maryland and Virginia. CTI Real Estate Professionals are positioned to assist clients throughout Metro DC. The firm's service area extends from just North of Richmond, Virginia, throughout the Greater Fredericksburg, Virginia Region to just South of Baltimore, Maryland.

Nearly 50 dedicated professionals at CTI deliver on the promise of an exceptional real estate experience through teamwork based on the principles of diversity, respect, trust, communication, accountability and service.

Michelle Caldwell Thompson has been the Principal Broker of CTI Real Estate since 2004 and is responsible for all corporate strategic initiatives, overseeing the direction of the entire company and managing its team of real estate professionals. A seasoned executive, Michelle Thompson has led CTI’s drive to engage the marketplace and gain market share, while re-defining corporate transparency.

From the early 1990s, Michelle Caldwell Thompson has held a variety of leadership positions in the real estate, corporate relocation and property management industries.
