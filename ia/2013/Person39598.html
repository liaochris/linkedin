Ran Mullins' Experience

Co-CEO

Globili

June 2011 – Present (2 years 2 months) Global

Globili is a global mobile marketing platform for government and business. Globili is a communication platform that turns static content (e.g., signs, documents, ads, packages, etc.) into dynamic and timely information that can be understood in over 50 languages. The content owner and user gain insight by using Globili.

Consumers (End Users)

Globili is used by people who like to travel but don't always know the native language of the place they are visiting. They can text or they understand how to scan a QR code, but they don't want to necessarily download or install a new app and with Globili they don't have to. These people want a convenient and easy to use solution to make them more comfortable and confident with navigating and exploring foreign countries. From travel options to reading restaurant menus Globili provides them that peace of mind for free.

Businesses, Municipalities and Non-Profit Organizations (Clients)

Of course airlines, hotels and resorts and transportation entities are a prime target for Globili, but we believe that the tourism organizations in countries around the world want to be welcoming and offer their visitors the most pleasant stay possible. Globili provides clients with a simple dashboard to create and translate messages into over 50 languages, upload images, add marketing pages, track and organize signs, documents, ads and packaging (CPG you know who you are). Just think of the data. Globili provides all of this starting at $10 per month.
