William Cody Bateman's Summary

William “Cody” Bateman ~ is a Christian author, commentator and adventurer of sorts.

He has traveled extensively throughout his beloved United States of America speaking, encouraging and advocating on behalf of abused children, broken families, fatherhood and veterans issues via national awareness efforts such as:

Trek4Vets™ – a recently completed bicycle tour across America advocating for homeless veterans covering more than 5,000 miles thru 20 states from the Atlantic to the Pacific Oceans in 2013.

Hike4Fathers™ – an effort requiring his hiking more than 1,000 miles along the eastern seaboard advocating for fatherhood and the importance men play in their families, church and national direction in 2008.

Hike4Kids™ – an effort requiring his hiking more than 1,500 miles along the Appalachian Trail advocating for abused and abandoned children – specifically, aged-out youths discarded from Florida’s foster care system in 2002.

As a writer and commentator, his inspirational and motivational articles are read worldwide and has been featured in various medias such as USA Today, Miami Sun-Sentintel, LiveStrong, Chicago Sun-Times and more. He also has made guest appearances before large corporations such as Carnival and Royal Caribbean Cruise Lines, Aviation Technologies and Ocean Bank of Florida. William has had the privilege of sharing his stories of overcoming adversity before various radio and television audiences such as; NPR, Univision and Telemundo Television Networks, TruNews Christian News Radio and Dr. D. James Kennedy’s “Mid-Day Connection” radio program.

Some of his current projects are, “The Last American Patriot” and “Faith in the Fire”- a collection of true-life stories derived from his recently completed 20-state, 5,000 mile bicycle tour across the USA as well as a bible teaching series on his favorite book of the Bible – “Revelation.”

William is currently booking speaking engagements throughout the states at men's conferences, churches and the such.
