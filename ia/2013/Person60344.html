Jeff Daspit's Summary

With companies struggling today to identify top talent in a highly competitive market, we at Fluid Intelligence offer a customizable RPO solution. Think of us as an on-call extension to your human resources or recruiting department. As needed, we assist in pipe-lining candidates via internal and external resume databases, direct sourcing, networking, and social media. We can run a full desk or just pipeline candidates into your applicant tracking system. On the administrative side, we create and maintain job postings, schedule interviews, and conduct independent and thorough reference checks. Whatever works best to support your business and your group...we are truly here to help!

Specialties:
Technical and Professional Recruiting/Screening (Entry Level to C-Level), Research & Sourcing, ATS System Selection, Implementation, Customization and Training, Vendor Selection and Management, Process Improvement and more.

Happily serving all markets in the U.S.
