John Darrin's Summary

I have had two novels published, and a third to be released in September. My first novel won a Best First Novel award along with a publishing contract, and the second was picked up by the 2008 Independent Publisher of the Year and sold out its first printing. I have written hundreds of articles for on-line and published media on topics ranging from sales- and business-related thought-leadership to memoirs to travelogues. I also write very effective marketing and sales materials.

Like many professionals, I stumbled into a sales career after coming up through field operations work in the nuclear industry safety/decontamination/decommissioning/waste-management business. Some fun, huh? Since then, I've had a long and successful history of sales at all levels in the high-tech sector, especially in products and services to the nuclear industry and the post-9/11 radiological emergency response markets, but also in sales automation and customer relationship management software products to B2B sales organizations in all industries.

I am an entrepreneur with several successful start-ups (and a failure to balance things and provide valuable lessons). I have been a senior manager in high-tech small- and mid-sized businesses. I have been an independent consultant for fifteen years. It pays the bills and I am challenged every day, and I enjoy it.
