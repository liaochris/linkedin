Judith Latcha's Summary

We create the "FUN" in FUNctionality!
We put the "PROD" on PRODuctivity!

We are the Design Doctors…
We can fix what ails your space…

From color consultations, to space planning - commercial & residential, to buying new furniture, wall, window or floor treatments, to complete redesigns and construction build-outs.

We are design professionals who assist you in the design, specifications, bidding, ordering and implementation/installation of plans and furnishings. We do it all!

As a degreed interior designer and office furniture dealer with over 18 years in business, I am known as the "Design Doctor" for my ability in solving space/design problems, the implementation of ergonomics and ADA compliance, strategizing with clients for their present and future needs and creating healthy, productive and fun places to work and live.

Let us take your project from concept to completion or work at any point in between with my team of professional designers and contractors.

Specialties

Infinite Possibilities!
Residential: Colors, window treatments, adaptive spaces, kitchens, baths, build-outs & add-ons.
Commercial: Your work chair is as important as your mattress. Ergonomics, chair "fittings" and teche` tool usage. Ask for a free chair sitting.
