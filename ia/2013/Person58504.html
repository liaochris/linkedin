Jan Kornmann's Summary

Jan trains and consults for workplace drug and alcohol testing programs & service agents. She partners with established clients with high level of quality with service as there top priority. Her goal is to help all companies meet their goals of a safe work environment by having drug free employees, training service agents proper rules & regulations and procedures to do their job effectively, and to inform organizations about the drug and alcohol testing industry.
The Workplace Drug & Alcohol program consists of: employee education, reasonable suspicion supervisory training, DER (Designated Employer Representative) training, and policy and procedure guidance to the companies, facilitation of: lab services, MRO, collection sites, random program, SAP, and consultation. Assist companies in Drug Free Workplace program to strengthen company's commitment to employee’s well-being.
Collector training is according to USDOT CFR 49 Part 40. Collectors of urine drug testing are certified to highest industry standards for urine drug and alternative drug testing (oral fluids, hair, sweat, and point of collection of oral fluids and urine for drugs of abuse). Jan is a Master Trainer for Lifeloc Technologies Evidential Breath Testers for collectors as Breath Alcohol Technician's (BAT) and Certified Lifeloc Operator Calibration Technician's as well as Provisional instructors in their companies.
Specialties: Workplace drug and alcohol testing compliance training and consulting.
Authorized Distributor & Master trainer for Lifeloc Technologies EBT's.
