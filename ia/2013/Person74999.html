Kevin Knutson's Summary

At Management Partners, we specialize in helping government leaders improve their operations. We identify opportunities and best practices that will increase organizational effectiveness and efficiency, helping jurisdictions provide better service at a lower cost.

During our 18 years of service, we have earned a national reputation by delivering quality, actionable work products to our clients. The work we do is not an academic exercise; it is grounded in the real world of customer service and accomplishment in the public sector. The firm is staffed with 50 professionals who are experienced public service managers as well as qualified management consultants.

Before joining the firm, I was a local government administrator for twenty-one years and had the opportunity to design and implement budgeting systems, strategic planning, communications, and municipal service delivery. Areas where I have specialized knowledge include strategic planning systems, fund budgeting, financial planning, process management, performance measurement, emergency management, strategic communications planning, public relations, media relations, social media, issue and crisis communications, public outreach, branding, and design.

In addition, I have experience in collective bargaining, economic development, redevelopment, park development, fire services, police services, intergovernmental shared services, legislative affairs, housing, Community Development Block Grants, and homelessness issues.

Throughout my career I've been an advocate for open and transparent government, public participation in government, and the use of process management tools to provide the most efficient and effective services. In fact, while at the City of Coral Springs, I helped develop a process improvement and performance measurement system and was the lead author on award applications for the first local government to win the Malcolm Baldrige National Quality Award.
