Andrea Shapiro's Publications

  • Creating Contagious Commitment: Applying the Tipping Point to Organizational Change

    • Strategy Perspective
    • November 2010
    Authors: Andrea Shapiro, Pat Zigarmi, Foreword

    Creating Contagious Commitment is essential to anyone interested in implementing an organizational change. The book expands on the diverse resources in the Tipping Point computer simulation, which is based on lessons from public health, systems thinking, and organizational theory. Creating Contagious Commitment simultaneously provides a solid foundation and helps the reader to think out-of-the-box in order to implement contagious, sustainable change.
