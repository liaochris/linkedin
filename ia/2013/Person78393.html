Larry Best's Summary

Larry Best is a results-oriented leader who deals with complex business challenges within competitive environments. As a management consultant and community member, he helps others navigate ambiguous conditions and identify opportunities for development.

At age 43, Larry transitioned from a twenty-year career in performance management at a major U.S. media corporation and now advises North American firms in ways of organizational performance optimization. His functional areas of expertise are in sales and marketing. Comfortably relating to frontline employees as well as CEOs, he builds relationships marked by trust and honesty.

Performance improvement is always challenging work because it requires change. Whether dealing with established corporate cultures or enthusiastic start-ups, Larry has broad experience leading initiatives that achieve real results. His mission is to integrate people with plans to achieve common purposes.

As director of sales operations, he led disparate sales organizations from merger uncertainty to a common performance structure including, territory and compensation design, quota allocation, and performance management. His achievements as marketing director were a result of strategic focus on customer acquisition and profitable growth within targeted segments.

Although there are many excellent management consultants, Larry hopes his approach is distinguished by uniquely collaborative processes that produce results. Faced with competitively adverse conditions, he has fostered meaningful improvements through respectful negotiation of mutual interests. Larry believes in order to achieve sustainability, the manner in which performance is optimized is as important as the initial results.

Specialties

Sales Effectiveness: strategy, force structure/sizing, territory design, quota allocation, performance management, compensation and rewards

Marketing Strategy: segmentation, pricing, channel management, and consumer demand

Negotiation: B2B, union, competitors, and institutional

Project Management: On time and on budget, meeting quality expectations
