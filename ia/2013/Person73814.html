Kelli Bragg's Summary

GOALS:
I am an Information Professional seeking a position as an Information Literacy (Teen and Adult Services), Metadata, or Cataloging specialist.

SERVICES - How I can help you:
I have experience in providing reference services to all ages, as well as creating and hosting Teen and Adult programming events.

Also, if you have a digital image repository you'd like organized, I can help. If you need User Instruction training (one-on one or group settings), I can help you. If you are looking for someone who thoroughly enjoys research and wants to serve customer by responding to their inquiries with reliable and relevant information, I can help you.

I produce 508-compliant training videos using Camtasia and also write Continuing Education-, Library-, & Accessibility-focused articles for the bi-monthly WA state library e-newsletter, as well as serving on the WLA's Marketing & Communications Committee.

SKILLS:
Research, User Instruction, Content Curation, Metadata Management, Abstraction, Cataloging.

I love working with people and am passionate about User Instruction, Outreach, Continuing Education, Metadata Applications & Best Practices, Information Technology, Digital Libraries, Knowledge Management, and Accessibility for people with disabilities.

My dream job would be one that combines any of these topics, although I know I would excel at any job consisting of any one of them.

My passion for all the above makes me an exceptional Information Professional: I enjoy organizing, describing, and disseminating information!

EXPERIENCE:
* Experienced in researching, analyzing, and abstracting information
* Well-versed in automated library systems (ILS: Polaris and CARL) from both staff and customer standpoints
* Comfortable providing User Instruction to various audiences
* Work very well in team situations (management style = Transformational Leadership)
* Adept at learning and utilizing various databases, software programs, PACs, and RDBMSs

Specialties

image repository organization, metadata application, database administration, user instruction, mentoring, microsoft office specialist, networking, public service, problem solving, abstracting lengthy information, digitizing images, neogeography, spatial literacy, GIS, public speaking
