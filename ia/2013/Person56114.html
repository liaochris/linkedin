Jeff Fisher's Summary

A serial entrepreneur who enjoys helping others achieve their dreams. My consulting firm specializes in helping local business owners create & maintain their web presence.

Since 1994, I have been helping entrepreneurs and business owners improve their businesses and marketing strategies. In 2009, I decided to focus my efforts on the emerging internet marketing for local businesses. My overriding passion is to help other business owners achieve marketing success on the web.

LocalWebTrafficSolutions.com and LocalChildCareMarketing.com are the vehicles that I use to do that.

I am fascinated by the internet. I ceaselessly study what is required for local business owners to succeed on the internet, and then puts it into action in my own business. Today my consulting is provided as a part of my local internet marketing agency.

It has been said that in a one hour consulting session I will provide more implementable ideas than most small business owners can accomplish in a year.

Clients report that they value Jeff’s candid assessment of issues and his inventive ways of solving business problems. He applies his business experience and insights to design creative approaches for businesses to succeed offline and online.

Specialties

Internet Marketing, Local Internet marketing, Business management, operations management, human resources and other aspects of owning a company with $5M in sales and 100 plus employees. Real Estate investing and creative financing.
