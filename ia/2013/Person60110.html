John Mustin's Summary

As Founder and CEO of Wasabi Rabbit, Mustin envisioned the concept of the digital platform as the delivery vehicle for comprehensive online marketing experiences to complement traditional efforts. He has been instrumental in fueling the agency's reputation for innovation, brand development and performance marketing. Focused on guiding the company’s strategy, Mustin leads business development while managing strategic partnerships and key customer relationships. He has been acknowledged for his ability to deliver meaningful brand and campaign experiences to global clients such as Marketwired, Guardian Life Insurance, Ernst & Young, Deutsche Bank, Fidelity Investments, Sysomos, Lending Tree, L.L. Bean, Subway, and more. Mustin brings this talent, proficiency and leadership to Wasabi Rabbit.

John’s professional background is comprised of a formidable cross section of advertising, brand marketing, digital creative and management spanning 23 years. His track record of creating and managing global and domestic brand development and integrated campaigns includes his tenure at GlobalWorks Group LLC, MMB NY, Harte-Hanks and Lotus Interworks - and spans multiple sectors including social media, retail, automotive, financial services, insurance, healthcare and telecommunications. In his previous role as Partner and Chief Marketing Office at GlobalWorks, he delivered consistent year-over-year revenue and profitability growth while managing the Brand Planning, Account Services, Consumer Research and Media Services practices.

A Captain in the US Navy reserve and a veteran of Operations ENDURING FREEDOM and IRAQI FREEDOM, John was awarded the Meritorious Service Medal for his service during those conflicts.

A United States Naval Academy graduate, John also holds an MBA (cum laude) in Finance & Management from the F. W. Olin Graduate School of Business at Babson College, and an MS in Operations Research from the Naval Postgraduate School.
