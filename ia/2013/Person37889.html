Gregory Marsella's Summary

Merging Knowledge of Neurophysiology with Advanced Technological Solutions!

Not all TMS treatment centers are created equal. Dr. Gregory Marsella has been treating people in South Florida with TMS since 2008. He has had success treating the following conditions: Depression, Bipolar Mania and Depression, Post Partum Depression, Depression in Pregnancy, Anxiety, PTSD, Addictions, OCD, Tourette's SynDrome, Insomnia, Hallucinations in Schizophrenia, Fibromyalgia, Chronic Pain, Stroke Rehabilitation, and Tinnitus.

The discoveries currently being made by TMS researchers are exciting and offer great promise for psychiatric patients. Dr. Marsella stays up-to-date on these new, cutting-edge applications of TMS.
