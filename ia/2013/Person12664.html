Paul Brooks' Experience

Founder

Twile

October 2012 – Present (1 year 3 months) Doncaster, United Kingdom

Twile is a secure, web-based platform for families to record their memories through photos, videos, sounds and milestones. Rather than posting photos of their children on Facebook - where security and privacy are a concern and not everyone will find them interesting – parents can add to a private timeline that is shared only with close friends and family.

The timeline can be scrolled endlessly back into the past or forward into the future, so users can add in future events (holidays, birthdays, weddings) as well as recent photos or significant events from their past. Users can zoom out to view many years at once or zoom in to see just one day.

They can share their timelines with family and friends who can help to build a single version of events by pooling together all photos, videos and comments in one place. But because Twile is a closed network, users will know exactly who can see their content - a person’s stories won’t be visible to anyone outside of the group they choose and can’t be shared further without their permission.
