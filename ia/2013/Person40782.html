Warren Elkin's Summary

Warren Elkin, Senior Advisor at Norhill Wealth Strategies, got into the “Safe Money Business” as a child. Growing up with modest means, he started doing odd jobs around the neighborhood, and at the age of eight bought his first stock with his earnings. After witnessing his family getting taken advantage of and deceived by advisors in three piece suits, he vowed it would never happen to him. Over the years, he has committed himself to helping the “working people”.

At Norhill Wealth Strategies, Elkin is committed to providing strong, supportive and informed financial planning to seniors. Focusing on enhancing and preserving the wealth of his clients and their estates, he helps seniors to achieve financial security, independence and most of all, peace of mind. With over three decades of experience, he has served as a consultant to top accounting and law firms nationwide and has helped a multitude of seniors with their estate, retirement, and health care planning. In addition to his exemplary care, he has received an A+ every year since 1977 from the Better Business Bureau, has met all current continuing education requirements to provide the highest level of information from the State Department of Insurance, and has an exceptional record of business ethics.

Warren Elkin holds a Master’s Degree in Business Administration for Syracuse University and is a consultant to the top accounting and law firms throughout Los Angeles. One of only 5,000 Certified Senior Advisors in the United States, he has successfully completed a rigorous training program covering all aspects of financial planning for seniors – from estate and retirement planning to specific health care issues. He was also selected by the National Register’s Who’s Who as one of the top financial planners Southern California.
