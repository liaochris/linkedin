Erik Kabik's Summary

Photography, Graphic Design, Advertising, Marketing, Public Relations and Sales are skills that Erik Kabik has combined throughout his career and his multiple entrepreneurial ventures dating back to 1990.

Working as a professional photographer since 1990, Erik is one of the longest running event, celebrity, architectural & concert shooters in Las Vegas today. His eye for live music and capturing iconic public figures has landed his photos on the pages of magazines such as Rolling Stone, Maxim, Esquire and People. Erik's Photo clients include Universal Records, Live Nation, AEG, Hard Rock Hotel and MGM Mirage. Erik's photos are represented by multiple international photo agencies giving his imagery a global reach to markets all over the world and giving his clients some of the best possible media exposure. His work can be seen at http://www.erikkabik.com and can be found internationally on a daily basis in entertainment, news, sports, music and architecture magazines, web sites and newspapers.

Erik founded and had been operating Las Vegas Stock llc., the worlds largest gaming resort & lifestyle image photography agency, since 1998. For over 10 years Erik worked side by side with the leading Casino Properties and Advertising Agencies in the North America and Europe helping them plan, select, license, create and execute images into their advertising materials.

Erik Kabik combines his experiences to give his clients a broad range of services beyond photography. Having worked as both a photographer and operator of an international photo agency Erik offers his clients the reach and syndication required to get their images where it matters most after the shutter has snapped.
