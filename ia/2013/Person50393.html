Greg Roeder's Summary

Greg Roeder is an experienced real estate broker who specializes in providing real estate brokerage services across the Northern Colorado region.

Greg's commercial brokerage experience includes representing Northern Colorado tenants such as Alphagraphics, Safesite, Cambria Suites, 3 Margaritas, Dazbog Coffee, Pure Barre Fitness, The Spice and Tea Exchange, Mama Said Sew, Hair Tree Salon, TLC Payroll, Legacy Financial Group, Computer Doctors, and Colorado Fine Woodworks.

Greg has also brokered the sale of 25+ million in residential real estate across Northern Colorado.

Greg’s education achievements include a Bachelor’s Degree in Marketing from the University of Iowa and Master’s Degree in Real Estate Finance from The University of Denver. Greg was a two-time winner of the Academic Achievement Award while at the University of Denver and finished with a 3.92 GPA. Greg is also a CCIM Candidate and member of the International Council of Shopping Centers.
