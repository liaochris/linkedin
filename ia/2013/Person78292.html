Lani Lorenz Fry, MBA's Summary

As Manager of Global Brand Strategy, I pioneered the first globally aligned brand strategy that propelled John Deere to “Best 100 Global Brands,” earning a current brand valuation of $4.85B.

Selected for the John Deere leadership track, I've been promoted through 8 positions and serve as the Brand Experience champion, defining the gameplan that aligns the brand promise across all stakeholder touch points—employee, customer, and dealer—and promoting the brand as a valuable asset and catalyst for profitable growth. Some of my career achievements include:

* Advised key Indian joint venture on brand/brand management and expanded leadership awareness on brand portfolio management; currently on track to become a new function within Branding.

* Raised awareness of need for critical brand considerations during Business Development. Secured brand point-of-view inclusion in JV/MA planning as well as on-boarding of new Business Development staff.

* Collaborated with regional/country leadership in India, China, and Asia to identify opportunities to build a strong brand experience. Introduced program that cultivated local future leaders to promote program.

* Updated global brand architecture standards and facilitated brand and business strategy alignment to sustain aggressive growth in markets holding the best opportunities.

* Built managerial strengths and bolstered team performance by completing the distinguished Coaching Mastery program.

Specialties: Global Brand Development * Strategic Planning * Marketing Initiatives * Brand Standards * Brand Best Practices * Stakeholder Engagement * Concept Marketing * Research & Analysis * Brand Positioning * Innovation & Creativity * Project Managment * Change Management * Thought Leadership * Cross-functional Collaboration * Digital Platforms * Advertising & Promotions * Product Launches * Integrated Communications
