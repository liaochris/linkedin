Mario Guertin, Painting in Partnership, Inc's Summary

Mario has 24 years experience as a Painting Contractor for both house painting and commercial painting. We help customers create living or office environments that express and nurture them, through our expertise with painting and decorating and our unique Color and Concept Consultation.
Mario has also written numerous articles in national trade magazines on topics ranging from craftsmanship development, project management, business systems for small companies, team building and leadership. Mario is the author of a blog on painting with over 120 articles.

Mario has been involved as a volunteer leader of the Painting and Decorating Contractors of America. (PDCA) since 1990 and is the founding President of the PDCA Craftsmanship Forum, a group dedicated to the development of national house painting standards.

Mario gives lectures on topics relating to craftsmanship and the aspects of their work as house painting contractors to local and national audiences. He is the author of a presentation on "The History of Paint in America" and delivers it to various professional groups in the U.S. He is also the owner of a vast collection of paint memorabilia and is a recognized "custodian" of the painting industry's past.

Specialties: Interior Painting and Decorating, Exterior Painting and Decorating, Color Consultation, Decorative Finishing, Faux Finishing, Historical Restoration, Historical Colors,
Wallpapering, Murals, Green Solutions, Deck Finishing, Cabinet Refinishing, Wood Door Restoration,
Concrete Finishing, Window Restoration
Clients include residential homeowners, historical properties, country clubs, doctors offices, financial firms, law offices, salons/spas
