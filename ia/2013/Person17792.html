Brian Tomkins' Summary

CEO of a Chicago based Marketing & PR amplification firm specializing in celebrity-driven corporate event promotions & securing sponorships across the U.S. Also CEO of online marketing firm that provides web design, and on-going integrated social media campaigns. Brian has been a frequent speaker and media guest where he discusses the latest trends in media as they apply to business, politics and events.
During a brief stint in San Diego, Tomkins was a regular TV contributor on KUSI’s “Good Morning San Diego,” hosted the radio program “The Social Media Nation” and was a regular on KCBQ radio. In Chicago, Brian has been regularly published in the Herald News Business Ledger and has recently appeared on CBS Chicago. In 2010 he was featured in the book, “Go Givers Sell More” co-authored by best-selling authors Bob Burg and John David Mann. He has successfully helped authors, businesses, entertainers, organizations, and politicians maximize the effectiveness of their media campaigns across the country. He received his B.A. from Lindenwood University and his M.A. from Governor’s State University.
