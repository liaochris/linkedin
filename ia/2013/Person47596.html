Linda Decker's Summary

Geo Earth Mapping provides cost-effective, innovative GIS/Mapping solutions to businesses, government sectors, non-profit organizations and also offers services in Graphic Design.

With over 20 years mapping and graphics experience, 10 years involved with GIS (Geographic Information Systems), GEM creates and delivers projects to publishing quality with attention to detail and accuracy, satisfaction guaranteed with follow-up service.

Geo Earth Mapping is a member of the Greater Vernon Chamber of Commerce.

Maps & Data for Tenure Licence Submissions,

Street and Route Maps,
