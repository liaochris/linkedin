Gina Lujan's Summary

I love people, entrepreneurship, and innovation. I build communities and make things.
Currently, I am working on community building at Hacker Lab (hacker and maker space) in Sacramento as well as other cities. I believe community innovation and educational spaces can be a huge catalyst and economic driver for our economy. Plus I just love cool technology!

My current projects are working on creating a health insurance program for startups nationally and bringing affordable broadband to startups in Sacramento.

Other things I am into are being an Ambassador for Angel Hack, Chapter Leader for Hackers & Founders Sacramento and Curator for Startup Genome.
