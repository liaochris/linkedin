Krystal Georgiou's Summary

My love primarily lies in developing characters and their stories, and I've helped with the creation and pitching of several original series by contributing designs and story ideas.

Two of my short films, "Best Present Ever", and "Shiny!", were finalists in the Nickelodeon animation festival 2009, and went on to be broadcast on NickToons US. "Best Present Ever" has had a number of other achievements, including being nominated for a Welsh Bafta and being broadcast on CBBC.
