Ingo Boltz's Summary

I'm a technology consultant and have been managing technical projects since 2000. I also run a small web development business for non-profit clients.

Electronic voting and biometric voter registration systems have been a focus of mine since 2005; I have been working in electoral observation missions and technical assistance projects of organizations such as the Carter Center, the OAS, UNDP and OSCE/ODIHR.

I have also applied technology to help causes such as the fight against HIV/AIDS in South Africa, campaigning for freedom of access to information in Argentina, and combating climate change in the Maldives. I currently employ both new technology and new processes for Greenpeace worldwide.

Specialties

All applications of information technology.

Open source software implementations, tools to collaborate more effectively on the web (CMS, Email campaigns, Online Donations, Salesforce.com, etc).

Solid understanding of scientific, economic and social issues surrounding climate change.
