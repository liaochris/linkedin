Bryan Patrick's Summary

I am a husband and father taking one step at a time. My purpose is to connect people with the resources they need to move forward in life by helping them find and fulfill their passion and purpose.

This purpose is primarily fulfilled at First Baptist Church of Laurel, Maryland, where I serve as the Minister of Worship and Creative Arts. I also enjoy meeting with individuals and groups in order to help them find their next steps.

First Baptist Church of Laurel, Maryland is a multi-cultural church located in the heart of the Baltimore/Washington Corridor.

As the Minister of Worship and Creative Arts, my primary task is to plan for, implement, and oversee the weekend worship services. In doing so I work directly with choirs and praise teams, as well as other worship leaders to fulfill this task.

Aside from the Worship Ministry, I work with the Pastoral Staff and ket leaders to provide strategic thinking to overall church development. This includes the formation of a new leadership development strategy to include mentoring, coaching, and group and individual development of church leaders.
