Ahli Moore's Summary

Ahli Moore is a Human Performance expert with over 15 years of consulting and organizational development experience. Since 1993, Ahli has worked with senior executives, training organizations, and human resource departments to increase employee performance and organization efficiency across a multitude of industries. He has consulted for several Fortune 500 companies to develop customized performance improvement programs. Ahli founded X-Factor Solutions after spending over a decade with Accenture, the world's premier management and technology consulting firm and over 3 years as President/COO of Giant Leap Consulting. A graduate of Florida State University, Ahli holds a Master's of Science degree in Communications with a concentration in Information Technologies. Ahli serves on numerous boards including the position of Chairman on the FSU College of Communications Leadership Board.
