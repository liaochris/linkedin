Charlie F. Moran's Summary

I manage web projects for Advertising Age, and my main focus is on building awesome things. Most recently, I worked on our iPad app, making sure we provided the best user experience around consuming our content and that we had the right specs and production processes to get it there. I'm really proud of our work here and encourage anyone interested to download it from the App Store.

In the past, I've led projects like our Super Bowl video web apps, a homepage redesign, content aggregation, our YouTube Channel leaderboard and many more interactive projects, quite often doing a lot of the design and development myself. I'm an analytics junkie, and my intimacy with the depths of Google Analytics has led to many fun and surprising insights on our users and how they like to consume content on all of our platforms.

Specialties: analytics, web project management, web production, content creation, javascript, HTML/CSS, Google Analytics, SEO, Ruby, Ruby on Rails, PHP, social media optimization
