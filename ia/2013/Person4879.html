Allen R. Phillips' Summary

Creative professional with 10+ years experience in Interior Design and Architecture plus a track record of successes in
Advertising Design and Strategic Marketing. Expert in business development, production management and client service.

“Great Design [Well Executed]” describes Allen's philosophy toward the creative, manufacturing and installation process. Indeed, to realize a successful design, execution must be done well.

LEFT-BRAIN SERVICES FOR RIGHT_BRAIN PEOPLE
Throughout his career Allen has practiced SixSigma, Total Quality Management (TQM) and Continuous Process Improvement (CIP). These tools assured successful delivery of product to his advertising clients and now allow him to do the same for interior design and architecture clients. Allen believes that in design, as in marketing, one must strive to find innovative solutions to challenges and opportunities placed before them.

DESIGN EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE
Allen earned a degree in Advertising Design from the Art Institute of Houston in 1984 and over the next two decades built a career which included design direction, strategic marketing, brand management and business development delivering international-agency, multi-media start-up, and supplier-side (consumer and B2B) experience. As a founder of his own San Francisco based firm, and a leader in numerous others, he served the Nation’s top clients including Nike, HP, Intuit, Wells Fargo Bank, Johnson & Johnson, Mrs. Fields, Odwalla and a host of others.

In 2009, Allen left the Advertising and Marketing field to pursue a new career in Interior Design and Architecture studying at Parsons School of Constructed Environments and apprenticing with Eric Hilton Ltd, New York.

Allen is a student member of ASID and supporter of Art for Aids.
