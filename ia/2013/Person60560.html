Jean-Luc Bourdon, CPA/PFS's Summary

As a CPA specializing in wealth management, I help clients understand their financial options and purse them to best reach their life goals.

This often involves crafting strategies to:
- fund, protect and sustain an enjoyable lifestyle
- pay less taxes
- help family (pay for education, assist parents,...)
- build a legacy

In the process, I am privileged to have formed many long-term business friendships.

Thank you, but I cannot accept endorsements, recommendations or testimonials on LinkedIn due to investment advisory compliance rules.
