CalVet Connect Representative

CalVet Connect - Americorps

January 2012 – January 2013 (1 year 1 month) San Diego County

CalVet Connect, formerly known as Operation Welcome Home, is in its second year of service. It's an AmeriCorps program run through the California Department of Veterans Affairs, and is a service position targeting the needs of California's recently returning veterans, as well as veterans in general. For a lot of returning veterans, the AmeriCorps members will be the veterans first point of contact for providing awareness and connectivity to a variety of benefits and services that veterans may be entitled to; however, unaware of how or where to go in order to receive these benefits. AmeriCorps members will provide individual support and coordination of benefits to veterans. AmeriCorps members will be at member site locations throughout the State of California where we will assist veterans with referrals to Health care, college fee waiver, mental health services, eligibility and enrollment services, as well as helping them make appointments and understand their benefits. In addition to the service hours performed at the site locations, the members also participate in outreach and engagement opportunities as well as occasional team meetings and trainings. I am excited to join this organization and I look forward to meeting and helping the CalVeteran in the next 2 years. If you know of a veteran in need, please refer them to myself or have them go to CalVet Connect website to enroll and start the process toward their earned benefits. http://bit.ly/wPQKKh
