Jan Roseleip's Summary

My goal is to assist each client with their real estate needs. I help buyers in the process of step-by-step planning to help achieve their dream of owning a home. And for those first-time homebuyers, I provide the educational piece to manage the maze and connect the client with programs and lenders that might be available to them.

Accredited Buyers Representative Designation.

As an Accredited Staging Professional I can help sellers to position their home in today's market. My consultation takes into account all aspect of staging a home, from curbside to inside.
