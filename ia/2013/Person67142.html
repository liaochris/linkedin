John Child's Summary

With over 20 years of marketing experience, I generate positive results to the companies I work with.

The greatest value that I bring to the table is not just a strategic mindset and marketing creativity -- but the ability to actually dig in to develop and produce the ideas I help create.

I am an accomplished and professional marketing executive who can also design, write copy, deliver presentations, crunch numbers and deliver results.
