Bill Bartmann's Summary

Bill Bartmann is the CEO at CFS II and a vocal advocate in the fight to reform the debt collection industry in America.

Through the website, www.stopthesecriminals.com, Bill Bartmann is working with state and federal legislators, attorneys general, and other influential people across the country to reform the debt collection industry from intimidation and threats, to one of dignity and respect. He started CFS II in order to provide a debt-collection solution that's fair, ethical, and effective.

Bill is circulating a petition (found at www.stopthesecriminals.com) to bring attention to the widespread nature of abuse by debt collectors, and to urge radical reform of the current debt-collection rules.

Bill is a Contributer to Entrepreneur magazine,Huffington Post and Christian Science Monitor
