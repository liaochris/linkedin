Lee Sanders' Summary

I am a composer for films, television shows, video games and online media. My goal is to contribute to the telling of great stories with friends and colleagues—adding to and enhancing the artistry of the project.

Specialties

Combining electronic and orchestral components to achieve a fresh, aggressive sound; generating heroic amounts of music in little to no time; stretching budgets beyond the laws of physics and economics.
