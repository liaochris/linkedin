José Ancer's Summary

Early-Stage Corporate and Venture Capital Attorney in Austin. My practice focuses on representing Texas-based technology entrepreneurs and emerging growth companies, primarily for formations, angel and venture capital financings, acquisitions, initial public offerings, and general issues relating to equity plan administration and corporate governance.

I also run a blog (Silicon Hills Lawyer) for early-stage entrepreneurs to educate themselves on legal and finance issues without handing over scarce capital.
