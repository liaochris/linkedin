Susanne Lakin's Summary

I have been writing fiction for over twenty years and coach and assist new writers with their books and submission materials. My specialties are in the role as: editor, book editor, copyeditor, proofreader, writing coach. I help writers develop and polish their projects, and my aim is to gain more regular clients in more repetitive editing jobs in the book publishing industry. I work with both fiction and nonfiction authors--genres include fantasy, suspense, contemporary, literary, poetry, theological studies, autobiographies--and much more!

Book editing, Copy Editing, Copyediting, Writing Coach, Proofreader, Critique Services, Manuscript Critiques, Developmental Editing, Novel Writing, Novel Critiques

Specialties: I specialize in copyediting (electronic) Web content, books, fiction, nonfiction, and do developmental and substantive editing, basic copyediting and proofreading, and mentoring of new writers.I can provide excellent references. I make it a point to respect the voice and style of my clients, and offer many suggestions on improving their writing and how to get published and/or acquire an agent.

Book editing, Copy Editing, Copyediting, Writing Coach, Critique Services, Manuscript Critiques, Developmental Editing, Book Writing Consultant, Novelist, Novel Coach
