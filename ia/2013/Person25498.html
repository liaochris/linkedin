CLAUS CIBILS's Summary

NTSC
Never The Same Color...

Claus is a special guy, not only because of his professional achievements, but because he is full of energy, full of desire, full of surprises and most important full of life.

He has being around for more than 15 years, working, playing, experimenting and learning from different faces of the audiovisual scene, as a result he is a highly skilled, uniquely versed and results-oriented Creative Producer and Director, becoming a very different kind of profile.

Television broadcast, commercial spots, Vj, video installations, events, internet, music label, teaching, short films, video art, creativity, direction and production have being his play ground, his weapons, his gear all them combined with a Punk basic philosophy, DIY (Do It Yourself).

This background demonstrates knowledge both tactically and strategically in effectively navigating through design, motion graphics, 3D teams, films crews, artists, art directors and technicians to successfully deliver quality, award-winning projects on schedule and within budget.

The combination of a creative mind, highly trained director and a production design talent, give NTSC the know how to give a twist to any production, reducing costs, time and effort, resulting in more profitable projects, with out loosing quality or concept.
