Brad Milner's Summary

Milner-Villa Consulting (MVC) is led by Mr. Brad Milner, President, and based in Ventura, California. MVC provides environmental, engineering, and operations consulting services to public and private clients in California including water, waste water, recycled water, storm water, water conservation, and hazardous materials projects. Current and past clients include large and small private corporations, oil and gas industry, public agencies such as US Department of Defense, County of Ventura, and dozens of cities and utility districts.
