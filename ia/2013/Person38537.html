Pamela Adams' Summary

Learn Know Understand is a division of DSPP LLC that services small business in business success consulting. Learn more on our website http://www.learnknowunderstand.com

Greenwich GWEN is a informal women's networking group of women professionals in the NY, NJ and CT areas. Greenwich GWEN provides a facebook page, a meetup page, and other venues on which to place business information about products and services.

D.S.P.P. LLC specializes in consulting and training services for Fortune 100 and 500 corporations that have invested in ERP tools. Our senior consultants have over 10 years multi-national, multi configuration project experience using SAP® R/3® software.

We supply Project Development and Management skills for SAP® application implementations
We provide Expert level configuration
We train end users and technical personnel

We also conduct needs assessment for small to medium sized business who may not be using technological tools to support their human capital. We analyze business processes and provide recommendations to support strategic planning in the areas of marketing, operations and finance.
