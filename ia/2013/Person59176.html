Jason Sutherland's Summary

Jason Sutherland has always had a passion for creative art and at a young age was able to explore his creative ability. Jason attended Collins College where he studied Graphic Design and minored in 3D Animation. He received his B.A. in Visual Communications with an emphasis in Multi-Media and became certified as an SGI Level 2 3D Animator.

Jason worked at Best Western International as a Multi-Media Specialist while completing his undergrad. This opportunity expanded his working knowledge of corporate communication and branding standards. Although Jason eventually left Best Western, it was a great chance for him to learn the corporate structure, e-commerce & application work-flow, and dynamic presentation design.

After graduating, Jason worked for and organization called The Body Sculpting Center, a leading West Coast Cosmetic Surgery Center that harnessed communication design and referral marketing along side a robust website. As a Marketing Manager, Jason was also the Webmaster while being responsible for the tracking of all external marketing efforts.

Jason went on to work at other companies where the focus was primarily web graphic design and interactive communication. He then decided to start his own hyper-local web based community, Peninsula Shops. Creating his own company has opened up doors to be sensible and logical in creative endeavors for clients all over the San Francisco Peninsula. The interactive application that Jason conceptualized and designed to promote Peninsula Shops, gained both local and national media attention respectively through KPIX CBS 5 and INC. Magazine.

Currently Jason Sutherland is employed as a Web & Marketing Director for Baynetwork, Inc. in Menlo Park, CA while focusing on his M.F.A. in Web Design and New Media at the Academy of Art University. As he continues to refine his skill-set, Jason is committed to human centered and innovative communication design that simplifies life.
