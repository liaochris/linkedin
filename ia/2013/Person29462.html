Daniel Croke's Summary

Experienced real estate investor since 1997. My experience has consisted of wholesaling, rehabbing, managing residential homes and apartments. I have invested in land, single family homes, multi family and tenant in common investments throughout United States. Experienced sales professional in the 1031 intermediary business as well as raising 1031 investment capital for tenant in common syndications. I also hold a California real estate license.

Our company is always seeking new bank sources for REO inventory. We are also seeking outside investor capital to purchase larger REO pools. Our goal is to help the investor community build wealth by providing under valued real estate as well as allow more individuals obtain the american dream through affordable home ownership.

Specialties

Real Estate Investing, 1031 Real Estate Exchanges, property acquisition and disposition, property renovations, marketing, tenant in common investments, and private placements.

Your Creative Property Source for Matching Buyers and Sellers.We are a full-service real estate solutions provider. We provide home sellers and buyers of real estate with legal and ethical alternatives to traditional bank financing. We understand there is an excessive inventory of homes on the market because of the lack of reasonable financing solutions. We structure creative strategies to meet current challenges of sellers who cannot sell but need to sell, with buyers who cannot buy but want to buy. Depending upon the seller’s situation we can use various strategies to solve their problem (perhaps using a combination of methods such as a land trust, wrap around mortgage/seller financing, lease purchase to name a few) We structure the deal in a way that significantly lowers the risk for both parties. We then sell or match them with qualified Rent To Own buyers who cannot qualify using traditional bank financing. We seek to provide a win win solution and keep the american dream alive.
