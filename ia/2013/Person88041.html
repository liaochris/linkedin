Mary Covell's Summary

Currently, I am the Managing Broker for Hunter Realty in Virginia Beach, Virginia. I have over 12 years' experience working in real estate.

By combining the latest marketing techniques with a genuine concern for my clients, I solve real estate needs and assist clients in every phase of the transaction.

I have successfully negotiated and closed numerous REO and Short Sales within the last five years. I have worked with PNC Mortgage, Midland Mortgage, Wells Fargo, Bank of America, Citi Financial and American Home Mortgage.

I have represented buyers and sellers in the sales of VA, HUD and Bank-Owned properties, as well as taken on property preservation tasks. I particularly like to work with investors and first time home buyers. However, all aspects of real estate interest me.

In addition to working with clients, I am a Real Estate Investor. If you have any questions about investing or if you have any properties for sale then let's talk!!! I buy residential real estate in any condition.

My goal is to use my expertise to help home buyers and sellers achieve their real estate goals. I like to be involved in transactions where everyone wins!!!
