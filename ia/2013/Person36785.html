Donnamarie Needle's Summary

In 2013 Donnamarie Needle joined WEICHERT, REALTORS® — New Colony with a rich background in finance, process management and relationship development. With over 20-years of business development and sales experience, Donnamarie and understands the importance of having a thorough and consistent follow-up process in order to build relationships and enjoy success.

As the owner of DKN Consulting, Donnamarie partners with financial professionals to implement and maintain custom follow-up and follow-though systems to help them to open more doors, connect, engage, and build more meaningful and profitable business relationships.

As the owner of Time Saving Concepts, she advised sales and service professionals in developing and implementing day-to-day processes to streamline, coordinate and improve productivity.

Prior to founding these companies, Donnamarie spent 20 years in leadership roles at Chevy Chase Bank.

In every role, Donnamarie has been committed to providing clients a level of customer service that is unsurpassed, and in teaching others to do the same.

“I’m passionate about working with people to help them reach their goals and achieve success. In this case, the goal is buying or selling a home – or both. Throughout my career, my philosophy has always been to provide the ultimate in service, efficiency, and productivity. At WEICHERT, that translates to simplifying a sometimes complicated process by guiding clients through each step of their journey. As a solopreneur for the past 7 years, I’m really looking forward to the collaboration, excitement and productivity that result from working with a team like New Colony.”

Donnamarie believes "the fortune really and truly is in the follow-up," and applies her experience of guiding other professionals in this area to assisting clients who are buying, selling or renting property.
