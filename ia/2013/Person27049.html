Corey Weathers, MEd.'s Summary

Corey is the Principal of Catalyst 2030: A Social Purpose Corporation, which advocates for, develops and showcases high-performance and resource efficient communities. Catalyst focuses on guiding financially and socially responsible actions while providing communities the strategies, resources and tools to measurably improve building and infrastructure operations related to energy, water, waste, and transportation impacts. Excelling as a "big picture" thinker and facilitator, Corey brings a wealth of consulting, operations management and business leadership experience to his work. Incredibly well connected throughout the country, especially the Pacific Northwest, Corey is often referred to as the scotch tape that brings business, utilities, cities, and community stakeholders together to collaborate and innovate. Corey also holds a Bachelor of Science degree from the University of Oregon and a Master of Science Education degree from the University of Washington. His passion for high-performing and resource efficient teams, programs, buildings and communities motivates this impact-driven leader.

Regional connections welcome: corey(at)catalyst2030(dot)com
