Dominique Busso's Summary

The 3 years I spent in San Francisco changed me: I was a Software Engineer, I became an Entrepreneur.

In the last 15 years, I developed in-depth knowledge and skills in:
- digital technologies (IT, e-learning, video games, e-marketing & digital distribution)
- software and game design and production, including freemium game design
- agile business & people management,
- startup and funding process for innovative technology projects

I love to create and grow teams of motivated talents.

But most of all, after 20 years in the new digital technologies business, I know why I go to work every morning:
- to create video games that help people feel better
- to find co-workers happy to work together and happy in their lives
- to have a positive impact on the local and global economic and social environment.

Specialties: New digital technologies, video games, tablets & mobile game business, freemium business model, learning tools, Edutainment, business creation and startup management.

Dominique Busso's Experience

Founder & CEO

HappyBlueFish

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Computer Games industry

2009 – Present (4 years) Saint Malo (France)

Happy Blue Fish is full-player in the Kids & Casual digital video-games market. We design, produce and publish games on tablets and smartphones, dedicated to the improvement of people life through gaming. It's Learning Games for everybody, from kids to families to seniors.

Our editorial strategy: easy-to-play addictive games with strong story-telling and learning values.

Happy Blue Fish is working on its own Learning Games targeting the international markets through digital distribution, with 2 segments : "Kids Gaming" and "Casual Gaming".

Our titles
Kids Gaming: The Deskplorers, Minuscule Kids Edition
Casual Gaming: Okinawa Game, Feng Shui Game, Minuscule (freemium edition)

We also produce games for third-party clients: Hachette, France Télévision, Learnenjoy, etc...

We are opened to publishing and co-production opportunities with license owners targeting the family and Kids segments.
