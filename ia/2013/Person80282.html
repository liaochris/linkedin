MHWB is a massive open online course (MOOC) that provides experiential learning for thousands of passionate students from around the world. Participants will receive lectures from renowned experts on global health challenges and mobile health entrepreneurship. The course is collaborative and community-forming through small-group assignments and the culminating Health Innovation Challenge, where winners can receive cash prizes or seed financing for their project.
