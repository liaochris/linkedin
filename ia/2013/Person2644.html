James Ainsworth's Summary

I have a foundation of academic and professional training in Psychology and Journalism and combine this with a keen eye for what works when it comes to online marketing techniques and establishing innovative best practice.

I have worked in publishing and currently apply my award-winning skills to the social media and content marketing of a leading marketing technology vendor.

My understanding of the B2B comms space is key to identifying opportunities and delivering results from social media and content that can be tied back to lead generation and brand awareness targets, that's not to say that I wouldn't relish a B2C challenge along the way.

I have a strategic mindset when it comes to adapting marketing plans to the nuances of various social media platforms.

I strongly believe in proving the value of social media as a touch-point across multiple aspects of business and not a bolt-on stand alone function.

I see the value in data driven business decisions and not in spur of the moment flights of fancy.
