Lisa Marie Dias' Summary

LisaMarie Dias helps you optimize your digital presence to achieve your business goals.

With a wide range of artistic, business and teaching experience, she works with business owners to create effective online marketing plans, traditional and online marketing materials and offers social media training, implementation and ongoing maintenance where needed. She also specializes in E-newsletter start-up on Constant Contact.

Whether you are just getting started and need a patient introduction or you have an established presence that you would like to optimize, she can help you move forward.

She offers webinars, group workshops and one-on-one sessions demystifying Social Media and helping people create effective strategies for using LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook, free directories and other online tools to grow their businesses.

For those struggling to maintain their online presence, she can create editorial calendars, help build engaged online communities, mine your existing website and archives for relevant content and create effective content and posting plans.

Once set-up, she can show you how to maintain an effective online social media presence in about 15 minutes a day!

LisaMarie is a Constant Contact Solution Provider and Certified Constant Contact Trainer. She publishes her own monthly e-newsletter filled with practical tips and advice to help YOU optimize YOUR online presence and is a columnist for the Alternative Press Social Media Success column. http://thealternativepress.com/columns/social-media-success/articles/one-size-fits-all-solutions-for-social-media

Her articles have been published in both 'Tweeting for Business' and LinkedIn for Business', prominent industry publications.

Visit her website www.LisaMarieDiasDesigns.com to join the mailing list, read her blog and see the calendar page for her upcoming webinars, workshops and speaking engagements. Follow her on Twitter, @LisaMarieDias and reach her via email at LMD@LisaMarieDiasDesigns.com
