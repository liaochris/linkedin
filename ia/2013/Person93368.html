Micheline Laberge, ASID, LEED AP,ID+C's Summary

My design philosophy is to enhance the client’s interests, with the expertise in space planning, color, and style is brought to all my projects. “I want the client’s home or business to be unique and comfortable. An interior that describes their personality and taste.” With an intuitive ability to listen to clients and interpret their style into a unique interiors, is what sets my designs apart. Whether it is designing contemporary to traditional, or old world opulence to tropical elegance, a passion for details and style reflects the client’s initial desires.

Thru the years of experience and education I have become well adversed in construction and interior architecture to take projects from conception to installation. Technical aspects of a project, such as lighting, millwork, and interior details where it involves current technology, equipment and electronics or Green and Environmental concerns, are familiar issues. The experience to address these factors is what contributes to the design of my beautiful interiors and makes them aesthetically pleasing and functional as well. I have also been very instrumental in designing fabrics, wall covering, furniture lines, and carpets for may of projects with major manufacturers.
