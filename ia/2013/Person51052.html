Guy Iaccarino's Summary

Seasoned small-business executive with extensive technology, fundraising, and operations experience. Utilizes strong communication, planning, collaboration, and analysis skills to engage others, set goals, implement solutions, and monitor results. Actively pursues commitment to life-long learning by continually expanding personal skill sets and supporting same for others.

Desire to make a difference is fueled by three young children at home, the principle of personal responsibility, and a belief that success overall only matters if we don’t leave others behind.

Specialties: Information Technology, Fundraising, Data Management, Business Process Analysis, Training, Writing & Editing
