Michael Teys' Summary

Strata and community title law has been the focus of my professional life since 1985. I was a founding partner at Teys McMahon Legal and moved to Sydney in 2001.

I ventured into commercial life from 2004 to 2010 buying strata management busnesses managing over 28 000 apartments in NSW,QLD,VIC and WA while maintaining a law firm at the same time.I exited my commercial interests in 2010 returning to full time legal practice at Teys Lawyers. I like to think that my time in strata management has made me a better strata lawyer. I bought the assets of Andreones Strata Lawyers in March 2010 and since then have put together a great team of lawyers and support people with whom I love working.

We practice exclusively in the area of strata and community title law.Our work involves representing owners and owners corporations or bodies corporate as they are know in Queensland.We accept instructions directly and indirectly via strata or body corporate managers.To avoid conflicts, particularly in our building defects areas, we do not accept instructions from developers.

Our practice groups include building defects, strata community disputes, managements rights assignments and renegotiations, by-laws/rules and levy/fee collection.

My professional goal is to build Teys Lawyers into the first Australian-wide boutique strata law firm. Presently we are working in NSW,VIC,QLD and ACT. We use the latest cloud based non legal project management software by 37 Signals to allow us to work nationally.We have been entirely in the clouds since 2010.

I write a weekly blog, tweet daily, publish four newsletters a week and appear on TV each week (Property Success with Margaret Lomas,SKY Business Channel 602 Saturdays from 9 am ). I am also a regular guest on Your Money Your Call on the same station. I like public speaking and training strata managers and owners on new ways to active better results for their communities.

Specialties

Strata and community title law, owners corporation and body corporate law, management rights assignments and disputes, and strata community disputes
