Lisa Dreissig's Summary

Lisa Dreissig is an accomplished interior designer serving New Jersey, New York and Connecticut. After graduating from The Fashion Institute of Technology, Lisa began her career with New York City start-up, Curated. Lisa managed several residential and hospitality projects, including a residence published in Interior Design magazine. Lisa later brought her talents to award-winning architecture firm S. Russell Groves, located in New York City. During her time with S. Russell Groves, Lisa further refined her skills and expanded her network while managing several upscale projects. Currently, Lisa is focused on adding new residential and commercial projects as well as consulting for local interior design and architecture firms. With Lisa’s top-tier education, strong work experience and an extensive network of vendor and contractor contacts, Lisa is well adept at providing a full suite of interior design services from conception through installation. Becoming intimately familiar with each project allows Lisa to successfully create unique environments that reflect the personality and style of each client. With a gravitation toward modern design fused with vintage and industrial elements, Lisa’s spaces are both sophisticated and inspiring.

Specialties

Residential and Hospitality interiors | Custom furniture and millwork design | Furniture and finish selection | Color schemes | Space layouts | Real estate staging / model apartments | Interior styling.
