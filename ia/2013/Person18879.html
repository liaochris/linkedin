Linda Cobb's Summary

A highly accomplished Creative/Art Director with expertise in publication, packaging, identity and print design. Linda has created award-winning projects for the world’s top entertainment companies as well as small businesses.

She has excellent leadership and mentoring capabilities — building and managing teams for client project work.

Senior Art Director

Capitol Records

Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; EMI; Music industry

Additional responsibilities include art department personnel management/mentoring, inter-department relations and project management, institutional advertising.

Art Director

Warner Bros. Records

Public Company; 501-1000 employees; Music industry

January 1987 – January 1998 (11 years 1 month)

Created packaging, advertising and merchandising for such frontline artists as Madonna, Chris Isaak, k.d. lang, Bette Midler and Adam Sandler.

Responsible for all creative on the projects including: concepting the album art, determining the image/styling of the artist, art directing all photo shoots, contracting illustrators for artwork, generating design and bringing it in on time and on budget. Additional responsibilities include art department personnel management/mentoring, inter-department relations and project management, institutional advertising and creative budgeting.

Elected to be part of a team to streamline costs in the creation of art, color separations and printing. Without affecting the final product, changing vendors or eliminating personnel we were able to trim $1.2 million from our annual financial expenditures.
