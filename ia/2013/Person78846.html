Laura-Leigh Wood, GRI,ABR,SFR,CDPE's Summary

I specialize in helping buyers and sellers invest in real estate in the Greater Orlando area including: Winter Springs, Winter Park, Oviedo, Longwood, Casselberry and surrounding areas.

I have over 15 years experience in the real estate industry. Currently as a real estate sales associate with Exit Real Estate Results; I bring special tools and solutions that exceed the expectations of my clients. I have served as a board member for the Educational Committee for the Orlando Regional Realtors, NAR, FAR as well as a member of our local Mid Florida Regional, Orlando Regional Realty Association, ORRA, and Designations include, but not limited to: GRI, ABR, SFR, and CDPE. I serve as the Chairman for the The Code Board Enforcement Board for The City of Winter Springs.

If you are considering listing your property in the Greater Orlando area, ask me about our latest internet marketing systems. I would love the opportunity to earn your business and look forward to hearing from you.

I am blessed to be a fifth generation Florida Native and to have lived in our beautiful Central Florida all my life. I have over 10 years experience as a commodity broker specializing in various markets. I have two very special sons and we enjoy deep sea fishing, sporting events, movies, theatre, and hanging out at the beach together. We also enjoy helping those less fortunate with various mission opportunities with our community church.
