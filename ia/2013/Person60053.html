John Barron's Summary

I have been developing and optimizing the user experience for e-commerce innovators for more than 16 years — including B2C, B2B and business management applications.
My experience across web and software design and development includes more than five years of conversion optimization, web analytics and multivariate testing. I also have 12 years of leadership, team development and process-development experience; and 10 years of business strategy and product management experience.

I am a proactive manager with hands-on experience in all stages of web and software product design and development, from customer research and requirements gathering, interaction design and visual design, to prototyping, production, analytics, and optimization. I don't just manage; I get things done.

I have managed in-house design, front-end development, product management, and optimization teams as well as contractors, offshore teams and third-party vendors for both small and large companies. I have built innovative, cohesive user experience teams that can collaborate and communicate effectively with engineers, business stakeholders and customers.

Currently, I am the director of user experience and optimization at Row 44, a leading inflight Wi-Fi and entertainment provider working with major airlines throughout the world.

Prior to joining Row 44, I worked for nine years at VEGAS.com, where I held numerous leadership roles guiding the company’s product management, user experience, and conversion optimization initiatives.

Prior to VEGAS.com, I held lead positions in user-interface design at an early B2B procurement software company, PurchasePro.
