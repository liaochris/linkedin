JOHN Y. LAFFERTY, CFO, MBA, CPA's Summary

CFO-Pro founder and principal John Lafferty is an interim CFO that helps CEOs of small to medium-sized companies ($5 million to $30 million in annual revenues) achieve business and financial objectives. John helps clients:
• Increase cash flow through sophisticated cash flow analysis
• Grow their business by developing and implementing strategies identified through business planning analysis
• Develop exit strategies – either via sale or transfer to other family members
• Simplify the processes through which financial statements are generated
• Understand the financial statements, and what the numbers mean for their business
John’s interim CFO clients are successful because John possesses the skills and experience of a seasoned CEO who also thinks like a business owner. When he begins working as an interim CFO, whether it is with an entrepreneur at a funded start-up company or the leader of an established firm, John makes sure he understands the business financial performance metrics and financial ratios that drive the business and are critical to the business situation. Once John understands them, he ensures the CEO understands them as well. This process facilitates executive decision-making and inspires trust. The process John employs as an interim CFO includes:
• A rigorous examination of financial statements
• A frank and thorough discussion with the CEO of the numbers and what they mean during which John communicates what the CEO needs to hear (even if it is not what he/she wants to hear)
• Based on those numbers, development and implementation of customized business and financial strategies designed to meet the company’s objectives
• Monitoring of the financial statements to ensure goals are being met, or to develop alternative strategies

John has helped dozens of small and medium-sized businesses throughout his career. He inspires such confidence that his clients look to him as a trusted advisor well beyond the scope of the interim CFO engagement.

Specialties

CFO-Pro provides the principles and strategies necessary to mitigate the unpredictable turbulence and challenges of business today, including how to:
• Develop your financial strategy
• Build long-term financial models
• Increase cash flow
• Grow profit margins
• Recommend growth initiatives
• Prepare financial statements
• Determine a successful exit strategy
• Advise executives on economic/financial trends
• Plan for the future with what-if scenarios
• Prepare business for future sale
