Greg Hyer's Summary

Welcome,

I'm originally from Rhode Island and have lived in a number of different places along the East Coast including Florida, Virginia, and Maryland. My wife decided to settle down in the Johnston County town of Four Oaks, about 20 minutes south of Raleigh, North Carolina. My wife and I have been married for more than a decade and have five children.

I take professional networking seriously. I'm a giver rather than a taker. I look at networking relationships as an opportunity to know what someone can do for someone I may know. I encourage professionals that I meet to examine their networking strategies and show them that putting others before themselves will get them much further.

Spiritual capitalism is a belief of mine. Integrity and commitment are just as important to a business as efficiency and profitability. It's important to take into consideration how the decisions of a business affect the world at a macro and micro level. The mentality in business of the survival of the fittest is over.

I'm a Certified Email Marketer by MarketingExperiments. Developing email marketing and social media strategies and creating alignment to achieve the desired results are a passion of mine. I enjoy working with others to attract new business and connecting with customers at a level that business has a hard time achieving.

Being part of a startup that's mission includes building sales enablement tools that promote sales and marketing alignment, is exciting. I get to be part of an amazing story about how we enable teams to unlock the value of the content and align it to a specific engagement.

In my opinion, a good marketer needs at least these three fundamental skills:
1. To have the ability to see things from multiple perspectives
2. Be able to tell a story
3. Be a great tester.

I'm still working on being able to tell a story.
