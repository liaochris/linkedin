Darla Wigginton's Summary

Over 20 years' experience developing presentations, eLearning trainings and PowerPoint presentations for companies in a broad range of industries. Previously on staff at Anderson Consulting in New York as well as CNET Networks (now CBS Interactive) and JP Morgan in San Francisco.

As owner of eVision-Design, a multimedia studio, clients have included Cisco, Accenture, Safeway, CBS Interactive, Bersin & Associates, SiriusDecisions, Clarity Consultants, Tech Prose, Banana Republic, The Gap, Williams-Sonoma, Barclays Global Investors, iShares, Symantec, Stone Yamashita, Grey Advertising, BARC, IDEO, Shaklee, Sapient, Peppercom, PMI, JPMorgan, Towers Perrin, Salomon Smith Barney, and First Boston.

Holds a B.A. in English Literature from U.C. Berkeley and was a professional singer (performed with San Francisco Opera and San Francisco Symphony as well as regionally across the United States).

Specialties: Creative Suite; Premiere; Camtasia Studio; After Effects; Captivate; Flash; Voiceover; Video Shooting & Production; Articulate Presenter; Storyline; Adobe Presenter; PowerPoint.
