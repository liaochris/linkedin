Connie McKnight's Summary

I am passionate about serving others. As a networking coach, my focus is to help others become the best they can be. Helping to build leaders is based on respect. Since I choose carefully who I work with and teach others to do the same, it is easy to be respectful of my colleagues. I have 14 years in the network marketing industry and my focus has been to learn from the best and most respected in the industry.

My past experience of 12 years, assisting in training student leaders, prepared me for my current passion.

The way to communicate has changed dramatically in the last decade. Because of social media, we can build a lasting relationship with people all over the world, without leaving our home. Therefore, my focus is teaching others to build their businesses using social media.
