John Weir CHESP, REH's Projects

  • Lean for hard floor care

    • February 2011 to Present
    Team Members: John Weir CHESP, REH, Mary Santos, Victor T, Manuel S, Kurt Lang

    Goals:
    1. Reduce costs, both labor and materials for hard surface floor care.
    2. Do not reduce patient satisfaction
    3. Maintain an acceptable appearance

    This project has been very successful with the elimination of the usual appearance variation between freshly waxed and embedded soil, needing to be stripped and refinished.

    VCT floor appearance is now consistent on a day to day basis with a low luster clean look. Environmental Service Technicians like the smooth easy to mop surfaces. With no finish to scratch we have eliminated the cost of stripper, finish, and most of the usual labor hours needed to maintain floors.

    I have applied this process to a 55,000 sq foot skilled nursing facility and two units in an acute care hospital.

    Patient satisfaction has been maintained. Staff satisfaction has increased. Appearance of floors is much more consistent day to day, week to week, month to month.

    Email me for more information
