Jeremy Weinglass' Summary

I have over 20 years of experience playing the piano professionally for weddings, corporate events, galas, VIP receptions, charity fundraisers, holiday parties, anniversaries, birthdays, you name it. I maintain a wide-ranging repertoire of original music, classical, jazz, standards, musicals, pop, rock, and holiday music while keeping current with the latest hits. While I usually play solo, I can also create ensembles with a host of other musicians or singers.

I am a composer primarily for the piano but have written for rock bands, jazz combos, string ensembles and orchestras. I classify much of my original music as melodic, modern classical. My personal favorite of my achievements thus far is the live music and dance production I created called ODYSSEY. ODYSSEY is an hour long show featuring 7 dancers, a violinist and myself playing piano. (The link to watch the trailer is below.)

My goals include performing ODYSSEY again possibly on an international tour, playing the piano for big audiences on stage in concert halls, composing for film, TV, or libraries and expanding my entertainment business to contract myself and my hand-picked network of outstanding musicians for events in Southern California and beyond.

Specialties

composing music, sight reading, accompanying, orchestral composition, music theory, producing musical events, teaching, speaking, finding talented musicians, conducting.
