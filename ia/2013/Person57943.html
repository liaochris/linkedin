Jamie Burke's Summary

Regarded as one of Europe's leading authorities on Digital Business (Web & Mobile) I've established myself as a pioneer in creating, developing and leading the commercial exploitation of emergent and disruptive business models.

As an Entrepreneur I have taken new ideas of my own and others from concept to fast-growth multi-million pound businesses on multiple occasions. My latest start-up being RetailerSaver.com which is set to disrupt the FMCG Wholesaling Marketplace.

I come with the benefit of having direct applied learning across almost all key sectors for both start-ups and large Blue-Chip organisations including; Honda Europe, Tesco, Mastercard, Nestle, Kraft, Microsoft, Hermes Logistics, Universal, easy Group and more. As a result I have a sensitive understanding of the implications of change on people that it is expected to empower.

Over my career have worked across all sides of the 'digital fence' including:

- Heading up digital teams and departments within agencies such as LBi, WPP, Cossette Group
- The creation of digital Depts., often from scratch, within client-side organisations
- Launching my own multi-million pound EMEA-wide consultancy firm 90:10 Group Ltd which had offices in over 8 countries
- Managed the development of Software using multiple onshore and offshore teams in parrallel
- Led the funding of new ventures through angel seed money to VC stages

In this time I have co-developed a frame-work for business in a real-time and Connected Age called Open Business which will be published as a flagship book in Feb 2014 by Palgrave Macmillan. I take its principles around what you can do with openness to become more innovative and ultimately more competitive to everything I do.

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Internet industry

September 2012 – Present (1 year) High Wycombe, Buckinghamshire

RetailerSaver.com is a powerful new channel that connects brands directly to engage and influence the long-tail of unaffiliated convenience / impulse and licensed retailers.

Leveraging the latest in group buying technology we can push volume and time-sensitive deals out speculatively to members and collect pre-orders to then serve through a fully delivered solution.

With deals come the ability to influence point-of-sale and directly engage the consumer at that critical transactional moment in-store.

This can be used for clearance stock, seasonal promotions, new product launches or targeted customer acquisition of well profiled members in an efficient and scalable way.

The platform allows brands to develop an unmediated relationship with the community to establish a two way flow to gain insights and a deeper understanding of the convenience channel including for npd testing.
