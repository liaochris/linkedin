Marco Lazzeri's Summary

Marco Lazzeri is the technical lead at Linqia and responsible for the systems infrastructure. Marco joined Linqia at the time the company was founded in 2007. With over 11 years of experience developing web technologies, Marco has worked on projects from large enterprises to start-ups.

Marco is currently designing the architecture of Linqia's core advertising platform, focusing on reliability, performance and scalability for all critical components.

Prior to Linqia, Marco worked for 4 years as a consultant at Wind Telecomunicazioni S.p.A., Italy's third largest telecommunication company. At Wind, Marco designed and supervised the development of their analysis and reporting systems, ranging from the company's KPIs to the performance analysis of their systems infrastructure.

Amongst other projects, Marco built the first management systems and social network for the Italian Society of Medical Radiology, connecting thousands of doctors and providing technological infrastructure to their annual national conference. As an entrepreneur he also successfully developed an integrated online/offline booking and management system for hotels.

Specialties

System architecture designing, scalability, end to end web development, strategic business support, project management.

Technologies: Ruby, JavaScript, Clojure, MVC frameworks, cloud based solutions, relational databases, queueing systems, HTML5 & CSS.
