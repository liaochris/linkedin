Luca Mezzalira's Summary

I’m an italian Adobe Certified Expert and Instructor on Flash, Flash Lite and Flex with AIR.
I’m CEO and Software Architect on Inside a Bit a new company based in Padova and Vicenza that is focused on development of Flash Platform and PHP / Jquery / HTML 5 projects.
For 6 years I was owner of Mart3 | the Flash Platform Company.
We are focused in RIAs, Desktop Applications, Rapid Prototyping, Mobile applications and Embedded systems.

In January 2010 Mart3 became Adobe Authorized Training Center.

I'm Adobe Italy consultant for Flash Platform.

In 2011 I became Adobe Community Professional.

I write for national and international technical magazines.

I'm speaker for national and international conferences or community events like Flash Camp, Scotch on the Rocks, 360 Flex, Pycon and so on.

In 2013 I organize an itinerant event in 6 different cities in Italy called "Having fun with Adobe AIR" where people learn how to develop mobile applications for iOS, Android and BlackBerry with Adobe AIR (more information at http://www.havingfunwithadobeair.com/.

Specialties: Flash, Flash Lite, Flex, AIR, Flash Catalyst, Robotlegs, SWIZ, Mate, MVC, OOP, Design Patterns, Starling, Feathers, Multiscreen Development, Mobile Development, Corona SDK, Haxe and NodeJS
