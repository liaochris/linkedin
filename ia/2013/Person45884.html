Frans van der Meijde's Summary

Hello, my name is Frans van der Meijde. I am a 24 year old Master student in Marketing Communications at the University of Twente. Currently I am writing my master thesis about channel choice in online customer support, and to what extent online service channels, like Twitter, Facebook and e-mail might affect customer satisfaction and service loyalty. I hope to graduate in January 2014.

Next to this, I am a part-time internet marketeer at MoneyBird. During this job I am involved in copywriting, SEO, conversion optimisation, brand positioning and other strategic activities. Besides this I am freelance available for webdesign and online marketing projects. While performing my online marketing activities, I strive to use the internet in a most efficient way to inform, persuade, involve and serve customers.

During my Bachelor in Communication & Multimedia Design at The Hague University of Applied Sciences I developed myself into a multipurpose Internet Marketing expert. In the major part I have done several projects in the field of Online Marketing, like E-Mail marketing, Findability, Search Engine Optimization, Usability and Interaction Design. The minor part of my study I spent on different webdevelopment and webdesign projects.
