Chris Strouth's Summary

Art, Music, Culture Marketing and in my spare time helping companies to suck less.

When it comes down to it, I am a producer. Producing is really just solving a series of problems and answering questions-sometimes ones that haven’t been asked yet.

To me producing is a creative prospect, I get involved in every part of the project from conception to distribution. On the vast majority of projects that I have worked on , I have been the chief creative as well so I tend to see both sides of the coin at the same time. When it’s someone else’s project then it becomes about facilitating the core idea, refining it to its sharpest edge. No matter what the medium the core principles are the same: Discover the core of the idea, refine it, sharpen it, give it the best frame, and don’t let yourself get in the way of the message.


Primary goal: To be part of a creative Wrecking Crew making exciting and impactful work that pushes the boundaries without ever feeling like it does. That or to own France...or at least a small portion of it's southern part.

Specialties

Problem solving, producing, multilingual. I speak media in all of its dialects: film, tv, music, words, and pictures. Oh yeah, and that internet thing all the kids are so hyped up on nowadays.
