Jolenta Averill's Summary

After two dozen years working in IT and financial services in three continents, I returned to my hometown of Madison Wisconsin in late 2003 to pursue my dream of starting a real estate brokerage firm. Having personally relocated dozens of times, I understand first-hand the many challenges of moving, be it across the street or across the globe. I hoped that with my passion for technology and excellence in customer service I could make a real difference helping people achieve their real estate goals. After five years as a top-producing Realtor at Restaino Bunbury & Associates and with the testimonials of my many valued clients to back me, I am proud to say that I launched my own brokerage firm, Lake & City Homes Realty, in January 2009.

As a full-time Realtor since 2004, I have purchased and listed an unusually wide variety of property types including residential single-family homes, condominiums, income properties, investment property, luxury homes, lake homes, commercial buildings and more. I'm experienced with both first-time and seasoned home buyers, investors, buyer agency, exclusive seller agency, 1031 Exchanges, land contracts, options to purchase, and purchases involving FHA, VA and WHEDA loans. My ability to generate results for my clients by looking at each set of circumstances with energy and a fresh perspective is one of the ways I feel I add value to the process. My understanding and commitment to investing in the best technology to benefit my clients is another way that I'm helping shape the industry. With my extensive background in client service and keen interest in building referral-based business, I'm committed to ensuring my clients experience smooth and hassle-free transactions, regardless how challenging the circumstances.

Specialties

Residential Single-Family Homes, Condominiums, Income Properties, Investment Property, Commercial Buildings, Buyer Agency, Seller Agency, 1031 Exchanges, Land Contracts, Options to Purchase, FHA loans, VA loans, WHEDA loans, purchases involving coordinating the sale of other property, sales involving properties that need updating, sales involving properties that require professional staging, sales involving extensive web marketing.
