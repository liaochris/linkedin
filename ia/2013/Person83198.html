Lucy Westhead's Summary

An exciting and up and coming top-liner. Over an extremely short period of time Lucy has managed to raise her songwriting profile and is regularly credited by her standout melodies, catchy lyrics and fabulous sounding vocals.

Lucy’s songwriting and performance style has enabled her to perform at many prestigious venues such as: The Royal Albert Hall, Glastonbury, Café de Paris, The Bedford, The Regal Rooms and Taubertal Festival.

Lucy is currently writing with many different songwriters across the UK, LA and Norway, generating a diverse portfolio of songs to pitch globally.

Working with multi talented drummer Troy Miller (Amy Winehouse, Mark Ronson) in his West London studio, she has recorded her brilliant self-written debut EP ‘Need something’.

Lucy is always on the look out to work with other talented songwriters and producers for commercial projects.
