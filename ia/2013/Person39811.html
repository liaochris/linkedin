Ellen K Dunnigan's Summary

Long term care advisory for several years, involved in case management, clinical program development, strategic development, business development, design and implementation of programs and product lines. Now CEO of ACG. Our team provides the following:
-- Professional education of those involved in the care of people diagnosed with Alzheimer's or a related dementia -- including staff caregivers, nurses, operational leaders, general practitioners.
--Consultation with families and development of community-wide resources for families living with dementia
--Implementation of new product lines in healthcare (from pro forma development and demographic studies to start up and proof), sustainability studies and re-engagement, as well as federal and state demonstration projects. We know what projects are available, in which states, and how to access them.

Specialties

Person-centered (person-directed) approaches, adult day programs, dementia/Alzheimer's care, clinical program development, demonstration projects.
