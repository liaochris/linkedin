Diana Dahlberg's Summary

Since 1994 I have been the co-owner of 1 MONTH REALTY with my husband Ron. Since 1998 I have been the top producing agent in all of Kenosha County for single family homes. I specialize in the residential market selling first time homebuyers and up to the executive level of homes. My background includes a variety of things including: public speaking, sales management of 32 sales associates while the Director for Wee Win Toys, also managed and operated my own computer/secretarial service for 18 years; was instrumental in the start-up of a K-12 Christian School in Milwaukee (my son was in the first graduating class), I've been the General Manager of a Christian Radio Station, and involved in a variety of ministries. My secretarial and administrative skills are well established. I am self motivated and very energetic. I'm faithful to those I serve. I am a member of the Professional Standards Committee and have sat in on a number of arbitrations.

Specialties: Contract negotiation, buying, selling, renting, hiring and firing, managing, etc.
