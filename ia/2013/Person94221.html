Mike Hayward's Summary

A British Insurance Awards Risk Management Category 2013 Winner.

A financial services marketing management professional focused on devising strategy and delivering marketing campaigns to achieve business targets within budget.

Management and development of B2B relationships across multiple brands.

Building relationships with customers to maintain loyalty and encourage proactive risk management. The "Hands off our Church Roofs" campaign using PR, online and direct mail has reduced church theft of metal claims by 60% and won a British Insurance Award in July 2013.

Selection, management and performance monitoring of marketing suppliers.

Specialties: Customer retention and loyalty, complaint management, outbound telemarketing, marketing databases, direct response advertising, direct mail, DRTV, affinity marketing, wedding insurance, church insurance, risk management, service recovery

Mike Hayward's Projects

  • Hands off our Church Roofs

    • January 2012 to July 2013
    Team Members: Mike Hayward, Paul Playford

    The campaign provided a much-needed breakthrough in tackling both crime and rising claims costs in metal theft. In the 12 months since its official launch in March 2012, Ecclesiastical successfully installed more than 450 roof alarms in Anglican churches across England, Scotland and Wales. The number of related insurance claims fell by 64% from 2600 in 2011 to 930 in 2012 and the cost of insurance claims in 2012 fell by about 60% year on year.
