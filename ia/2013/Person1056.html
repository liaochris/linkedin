Gayle Naftaly's Summary

GAYLE NAFTALY

Gayle Naftaly has been the owner and operator of access.office since 1998 providing virtual executive assistants, multicultural marketing, public relations and is considered a premier alliance connector – one who identifies groups, individuals and opportunities to form powerful alliances that will positively affect her clients’ businesses going forward. Partnering with gifted and talented professionals, business expanded in many directions.

July, 2013, a new division is launching and Gayle is having an official Grand Opening inviting clients, friends, family, neighbors and local business people from around the West Orange, NJ area.

Gayle is the exec of a women's leadership movement, where business and the coolest women you know collide, about to launch in Essex County, NJ. She is the Director of Communications for the Independent Business Women’s Circle, the Marketing Coordinator of Asian American Cultural Festival, is a member of Adrian’s Network, and the West Orange Chamber of Commerce.

Specialties: Networking as a lifelong skill. Success in networking is immeasurable. Strategic alliance connecting is a powerful tool and connections made are huge!
