Joan Pagano's Summary

Joan is a fitness expert, author and former personal trainer to Jacqueline Onassis and Caroline Kennedy. She has specialized in strength training for women since 1988 and is an authority on the benefits of exercise for women's health issues such as breast cancer, menopause and osteoporosis as well as strength training through the decades.

Joan is the best-selling author of top fitness titles including Strength Training for Women (2004); Strength Training Deck (2006); 8 Weeks to a Younger Body (2007);15-Minute Total Body Workout + DVD (2008); and 15-Minute Abs Workout + DVD (2009). All titles published by DK Publishing.

Joan has contributed to media outlets too numerous to name, including national and regional TV, radio, newspapers and magazines, websites, blogs and ezines.

Specialties: women's health and fitness, osteoporosis, menopause, strength training, core training,
midlife issues and active aging,
