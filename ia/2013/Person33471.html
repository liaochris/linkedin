Deb Gibb's Summary

Deb Gibb is a key player you will want on your team to make sound business decisions and drive high-impact strategic initiatives. She's currently available on a consulting basis and can help with a wide variety of business operations and technology management needs.

Deb has over 30 years of management, operations and technology experience in both rapidly growing and established companies. Most recently as a partner in building My Perfect Pet, a San Diego start-up producing a premium line of human grade pet foods. Prior to that as a Senior Technology Consultant for CBS Interactive, College Sports division, she managed their online operations which included the data center, engineers and tech teams building and maintaining over 250 Official Athletic sites, stores, auction sites and streaming media services. Deb has held executive and managerial positions from start-ups to large corporations, including ProfitLine, QUALCOMM, CollegeClub, First Virtual Holdings, BioData, ComputerLand and CCH Computax.

With a strong focus on effective execution, best practice processes and open, direct communication, Deb offers practical experience in successfully implementing technology and managing operational teams.

Deb's principal strength is providing leadership, inspiration and strategic direction that leverages technology to deliver competitive business advantages. Her fact-based, no-nonsense approach allows her to work effectively with cross-organizational business and technology stakeholders as well as customers and vendors. Deb's management experience includes advising business professionals, integrating information systems with business processes, recruiting and developing effective teams and assuring Product and Customer Service excellence.

Deb has always had a passion for technology. Don't call her a nerd, just know when it comes to the internet, web and business, she's seen it grow from the beginning and "she gets it".
