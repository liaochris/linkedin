BOBBY VASSALLO's Summary

City Wireless builder, having "Lit" first cities in North America. Rural Wireless Projects in North, Central and South America. Designed and/or built wireless projects in New Zealand, French Polynesia, Mexico, Africa, and some parts of Texas. Much HOA/community experience, building and consulting with HOA's to "self contain" and save big money while improving services and security. High-Density wireless expert with much hotel and convention/stadium and concert venue experience. Continue to work with largest hotel companies where connectivity is a "must."

Wireless Consultant -International Governmental consultant- Connnections in North, Central, South America, Africa, building systems and delivering services. Landed and built out one of the 3 largest (30 yr) concessions for all telecom services throughout Mexico. Built networks throughout, including wireless "last mile" in rural and smaller towns.

Worked Wireless in other Latin American, African, Carribean areas building telecom business and obtaining governmental concessions. Continuing work with highest levels of government in specific countries. Telecom and Wireless telecom specialties.

Past includes drilling of over 400 oil and gas wells in Texas, Oklahoma and New Mexico. Also, Geothermal Energy background, China Lake, California, 10,000 acre BLM lease, with Mitsubishi Heavy Equipment Division Turbines.

Specialties: Wireless consultant for cities desiring new infrastructure for Police, Fire, Ambulance, emergency services, surveillance. We don't talk/we deploy. Wireless (SMART GRID) meter reading systems, and City Wireless Services for Water, Power meter reading and monitoring. High density experts for stadiums, concert venues, convention areas, etc.
