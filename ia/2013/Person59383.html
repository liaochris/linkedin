Jason Michaels' Summary

I am the principle interest in the firm Jason Michaels Magic. We are a corporate entertainment and creative marketing firm that specializes in using magic and illusion to create unique, fun, customized programs for conferences, meetings, trade shows, and theatrical venues. From the simplest meetings to fully integrated and branded special events, my team prides itself in creating unforgettable experiences.

My goal is to use humor, magic, and unforgettable stories to inspire the world.

Specialties

Keynote Speaking, Team Bonding, Audience Interaction, Entertaining, Sleight of Hand, Visual Illusions, Comedy
