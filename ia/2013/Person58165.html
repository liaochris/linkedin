Jane DeLorenzo's Experience

Project Manager

Orange County Urban Land Institute

Nonprofit; 51-200 employees; Real Estate industry

December 2012 – Present (1 year 1 month) Orange County, California Area

Reality Check 2.0 is a grant-funded project looking into why a growing number of young adults are moving out of Orange County, making OC one of the fastest growing aging populations in the United States. This demographic shift is impacting the economy, housing, land use, health care, education, quality of life and other issues. As project manager, I facilitate efforts to engage stakeholders in this conversation via crowdsourcing, video and presentations. I will report on our findings at the end of 2013.

Report Writer

Urban Land Institute

Nonprofit; 51-200 employees; Real Estate industry

August 2013 – Present (5 months)

The revitalization of Balboa Village and the historic Fun Zone is the subject of a Technical Assistance Panel (TAP) comprised of professionals in land use, architecture, design, urban planning, residential and commercial development, landscape architecture and civic involvement. The Newport Beach City Council identified Balboa Village as a priority for renewal. My role on the panel is to report on the recommendations of the TAP participants and their strategies for a rebirth of the area to ensure a sustainable future for Balboa Village.

I am a sustainability consultant with a background in communications, offering assistance in greening your business via cost-saving best practices, resource management, certifications, green marketing, workforce training and environmental regulation compliance. Specialty: hospitality industry.

Sustainability Consultant

Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Hospitality industry

Worked with department managers at 300-room hotel to successfully earn Leadership Level certification from the California Green Lodging Program. Helped write and implement a green purchasing policy that prioritized local sourcing and improved recycled content of business products and guest amenities.
