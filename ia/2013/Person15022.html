Sharon Bieganski-Negron's Summary

I have 20+ years of experience in many aspects of the printing industry. I began as a graphic designer working with galleys, a waxer, and a drafting table for a very small local printer. I learned everything from customer service to bindery which is a huge advantage for myself and my clientele. As I advanced through several printing companies I wore the hat of graphic designer, customer service, typesetter, web designer, sales, public relations, event planner, and marketing communications manager. Although I have loved every step of my career and learned a great deal from each, my ultimate dream has always been to own my own company. To be able to provide my clients with outstanding creative services and the best customer service experience possible.
