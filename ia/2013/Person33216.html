Dean Bubello's Summary

RedWhiteBlue Studio is a full-service graphic design and marketing firm that combines the energy of innovation with a powerful commitment to customer service.

My capabilities include collateral design, creative development, PR, social media marketing, video production, website development, advertising and branding. I employ this expertise from over 17 years in the creative industry in a wide range of media; from web, video and illustration to print, direct mail, advertising and photography services.

RWBS' driving motivation is the success of its clients. So I take the time to listen to your priorities, time frames and budget considerations. It doesn’t matter whether you are a national corporation or a local company, you will receive the same level of attention. For each project, I develop a fully integrated strategy, and pool the necessary resources, that ultimately achieves your strategic design & marketing objectives.

video production.

Established the advertising function in-house, eliminating the need for an external agency, resulting in significant cost savings for Pioneer and ultimately better concept and design.

Directed the rebrand Pioneer’s marketing materials, which included redesign of all print, web and advertising. Worked with the international marketing teams to ensure that brand was consistent globally.

Re-aligned the Design team with the eCommerce group to ensure that all web graphics on the corporate site were maintaining brand standards.

Produced photo-shoots for exclusive-rights imagery utilized by our marketing groups globally for collateral, advertising, video and web. The results produced significant cost savings to Pioneer of approximately $800,000 (cost to purchase stock imagery globally).

Directed the creative for web content which included banners, microsites and the evolution of corporate site.

Created videos in-house for our sales division, corporate meetings, as well as special events.

Implemented the Filemaker Pro electronic job trafficking system for new job entry, status tracking, reporting and archiving for the Studio. The result of which has significantly increased the efficiencies for Project Managers, Production as well as Designers and has allowed a reporting function for management.

Developed automated Indesign templates for quarterly fact sheets using XMPie.

Created package design for various client promotions.
