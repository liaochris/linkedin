Clara Kliman-Silver's Summary

I am a recent graduate of Brown University. At Brown, I studied cognitive science with a focus on human factors. I seek a career path that will enable me to design and develop technologies for improved user communication and human-computer interaction.

As a dual citizen of the United States and the United Kingdom, I seek opportunities in both the US and the EU.

Specialties: User testing, Axure, prototyping and wireframing, HTML and CSS, eyetracking technologies and analysis, Adobe Photoshop, MATLAB, SPSS, statistical analysis, experimental design
