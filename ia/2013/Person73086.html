Katie Humphrey's Summary

As an international lifestyle consultant for women, specializing in hormonal health and motivation, Katie works with overwhelmed, emotionally burned out women who are overweight or unhealthy. She empowers them to take inspired action to lose weight and achieve better health, more confidence, motivation and vibrancy through her private consulting, retreats, books and online programs.

Katie is a professional speaker, trainer and retreat leader, focusing on topics of motivation, team building and leadership. Please email info@KatieHumphrey.com for more information on booking Katie at your next event.

Katie's books include Revolution of You: Embrace Your Inner Rebel and Create a Life You Love (available on Amazon) and Freedom from PCOS, a 3-step guide to overcome Polycystic Ovarian Syndrome and insulin resistance (available at www.FreedomfromPCOS.com).

For more information on Katie's books, programs, or to subscribe to her weekly word on health, hormones and creating a rockstar life you love, go to www.KatieHumphrey.com.

Katie Humphrey's Experience

Lifestyle Consultant, Author, Professional Speaker

Revolution of You

July 2009 – Present (4 years 4 months)

As a self-proclaimed rebel, Katie Humphrey teaches women around the world to let go of society's rules around dieting, weight loss and hormonal balance. Her clients to achieve a high level of personal wellness, a positive, confident attitude and an empowered life they love through her lifestyle consulting, retreats, books and online programs.

With a love for rocking the stage and keeping audiences entertained, Katie gives keynotes, presentations and trainings on motivation, team building and leadership. She is the author of Revolution of YOU: Embrace Your Inner Rebel and Create a Life You Love and Freedom from PCOS, a 3-step guide for women who want to overcome Polycystic Ovarian Syndrome.

Katie is the international spokesperson for PCOS Insulite Laboratories, a nutraceuticals company for women with PCOS and holds a bachelor’s degree in Business Administration from Stetson University. She is the recipient of the Jaycees 2012 Outstanding Young Floridian award and is the founder and president of Revolution of You. She currently lives in Jupiter, Florida, with her family.
