Michelle Beckett's Summary

I wear two hats - telling businesses about the benefits of the Skill Will social enterprise; & also training & consulting on LinkedIn.

Skill Will is a new initiative set up to link professional and business volunteers with local charities and social enterprises that need assistance.

Leading on from recent initiatives and policy from central government, Skill Will is looking at new ways of broadening the base of people who give time to help others, and uses it’s unique business model to develop collaborative and mutually beneficial working relationships between public, private, charity and social enterprise sector organisations across the UK.

The Skill Will Network has developed considerably since it began recently, and as well as its own membership is also able to draw on the expertise of the members of the Yorkshire Mafia Business Network to promote events, opportunities, projects, and programmes.

If you are a business that can offer help to charities & the third sector through expertise or sponsorship - give me a call on 07889 046209 or email michelle@skillwill.co.uk

Clearly Linked - I still help businesses & individuals use LinkedIn & other social media tools to sell & network their products & services better through training & consultancy.

I also blog for the Huffington Post UK, both for business & personally.
