Ed Thomas' Summary

I provide association management, strategic consultation, and tactical event production to help enhance learning and networking among energy utilities, government entities, and other like-minded organizations looking to improve the demand response, energy efficiency and renewable energy resources available to home and business owners. Activities include management for:
• Peak Load Management Alliance (www.peaklma.com)
• Utility Energy Forum (www.utilityforum.org)
• Rocky Mountain Utility Efficiency Exchange (www.utilityexchange.org/rmuee/2013/)

UtilityExchange.org also provides consultation and management services for Edison Electric Institute, Electric & Gas Industries Association, and others.
