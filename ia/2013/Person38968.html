Donald Jacobs' Summary

Current position – CIO
Last two IT positions – CIO Consultant and CIO/Director

Responsible for complete technical strategy for ($550M annual) organization & departmental direction, including all computer-related technology, information systems & services, and data and telephone systems internally and to the public. General departmental areas includes: Customer Service; Networking and Production Servers; Infrastructure and Enterprise, and; Applications Development. Administration includes: Business Analysts and Project Management; Finance; Telephone Operators; Data Center Production; Legacy Systems; and Training. Original complement of approximately seventy (70) personnel, now streamlined to forty-three (43) including contractors. Six direct-reports including one Deputy CIO.

General concept: Build versus Buy is the rule of the road now that our development team is successfully retooled and has performed so exceedingly well for our very unique deliverables. Buy versus Build – only where industry has mature and indisputably superior solutions. Constantly investigating and applying new technologies (as appropriate) to meet the business needs of the departments and people we serve, while being cognizant of our budgetary responsibilities. Through creation and strict adherence to standards in both software development and hardware areas, successfully support an ever-growing user base with consistent reductions in staff since 2007.

Specialties include: Cost containment; forecasting; personnel; technical strategy; vendor management; contract writing; competitive analysis; finance; systems disaster recovery and survivability; community disaster recovery and survivability; non-profits (construct, law, maintenance); real estate (law, investing, maintenance, and enhancement); code compliance; pre-hospital emergency medical care; firearms safety, training & law; investigative practices; report construction (executive, technical, and legal), and; court testimony.
