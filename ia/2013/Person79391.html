Laurence Hansen's Summary

Thirty years in the information technology industry as a project manager, systems manager, systems analyst and computer programmer.

Largely self-taught in every area of life that matters, I am applying my hard-won knowledge of business, marketing, sales and service and personal development to help others succeed and bring their skills, abilities and passion to more people. One meaningful outlet for this is Soulameda.com.

Soulameda.com will showcase all of the best in people who serve others at every level in Southern Alameda County. It will be a platform for the best of the best in Soulameda to reach a wider audience and make a positive contribution to the lives of more people.

Specialties: Internet Marketing and Multi-channel Marketing.
Nudging people to do more with everything they have.
