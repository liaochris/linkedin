Michael Platt's Summary

Michael Platt is a Serial Entrepreneur as well as a committed junkie to the post-secondary education space.

Michael Platt, expert in for-profit education, is the founder CEO and Executive Chairman of PlattForm, located in Lenexa, KS. A leading visionary in his field for more than 20 years, Mr. Platt has developed innovative marketing and advertising solutions for over 4,000 post-secondary institutions.

He founded PlattForm in 1987 where his forward-thinking approach combined with a commitment to tracking and analyzing data brought a level of science to a sector of higher education almost entirely reliant on anecdotal beliefs, opinions and theories. Under his leadership and expertise, PlattForm has become one of the largest providers of Marketing and Admissions services in the US and abroad, as well as the largest full-service marketing services provider in post-secondary education.

In 2007, Mr. Platt was named Executive Chairman and has led the company through eleven acquisitions to date. Mr. Platt has also spent five years on the board of directors of the Imagine America Foundation, and in 2004 he was named the Ernst & Young Entrepreneur of the Year in the category of Business Services.

In 2013, Mr. Platt will be releasing "Reconstructing the Dream", a documentary he produced with Dr, William Bennett, former Secretary of Education under President Ronald Reagan, focusing on the need to rethink and recreate a US Education system that is feeling to meet the needs of the 21'st century workforce.

Specialties: Higher Education, Post-Secondary Education, For-Profit Education, Career Colleges, Trade Schools, Investment Banking, Education Industry Expert
