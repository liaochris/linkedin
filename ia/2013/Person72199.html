Karen Rosenzweig's Summary

I am a marketing consultant, social media trainer and owner of One Smart Cookie Marketing. I have helped hundreds of restaurants, food companies, and entrepreneurs increase their customer base and gain visibility through Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, blogging and other social media tools.

An entrepreneur who operated my own successful personal chef business (The Incredible Chef) for 6 years and enjoyed 15 years in corporate and sports marketing/PR, I create powerful strategies to blend online and offline marketing activities for businesses. As you might guess, I have a particular passion and expertise in working with restaurants and food-related businesses, and have blended my two primary loves – food and marketing – into one very smart business!

I am a fun and dynamic speaker and love to present to audiences eager to learn how to leverage social media tools to increase their business. I love helping non-technical people learn how to use technical tools (social media etc) and my joy is seeing the light bulb go on when they master the skills!

Specialties: Social media, marketing, public relations, blogging, training, speaking, word of mouth marketing, business networking, restaurant/chef marketing, entrepreneurs, solopreneurs
