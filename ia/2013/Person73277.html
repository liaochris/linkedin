Yi Xu's Summary

Xu Yi specializes in large (>500 people) Agile transformations. After mastering black box testing in all its flavours - including ATDD, exploratory testing contributing to a test automation system for a 500 people organization, he moved onto the next logical step and started teaching Agile/Scrum practices to organizations being coached. His interests include but not limit to Agile, Scrum, Lean, all flavours of testing.

Xu Yi is a frequent speaker and organizer of domestic Agile conferences, including Agile China, Scrum Gathering China, Agile Tour China, and local Agile community gatherings at many places. He is also actively joining international conferences, e.g. Agile Testing Days.

Book translator (English into Chinese) of:
- "Agile Coaching" by Rachel Davies and Liz Sedley
- "The Elements of Scrum" by Chris Sims and Hillary Louise Johnson
- "Management 3.0" by Jurgen Appelo
- "Beautiful Teams" by Andrew Stellman and Jennifer Greene
