Be a Hero-Hire A Hero 2013 Calendar of Events

August 19, 2013 – PGA Tour - The Barclays
Veterans’ Career Expo - Jersey City, NJ

September 20, 2013 – Citi Field, Home of the New York Mets - Queens, New York
Be a Hero-Hire a Hero will be producing their largest event to date in New York State. Along with our event partners which includes the New York State Department of labor, Fatigues to Fabulous, Save-a-Suit Foundation and many Veteran Service Organizations. This event will have 100 Yale student volunteers helping our veterans on this exciting day.
9:30am the suit give-away begins, 2000 suits will be given to pre-registered job seekers. Job seekers will enter a retail pavilion where volunteers will assist in size and color selection, dressing room are being made available. Stylists from the fashion community will be on hand to provide fashion consultations.
The Women’s Veteran Pavilion opens. A full day of gender specific events will take place at Citi Field. This women’s event will provide our female heroes with fashion tips, cosmetic and hair make-overs, workshops on topics ranging from careers for women to PTSD. This venue will provide excellent networking opportunities for our women veterans and corporate leadership. Speaking Rolls Available.
The Veterans VIP Breakfast begins welcoming guests , approximately 250 government and corporate leaders from as far away as California will be attending this breakfast, New York State Department of Labor Commissioner Peter Rivera will be providing the Welcome to New York remarks, Senator Kirsten Gillibrand has been invited to speak about her veterans initiatives. Speaking Rolls Available
Career Expo immediately following

November 6, 2013 – Veterans’ Day Celebration Resource Expo Philadelphia, PA
