Marcello Manso's Summary

I started my professional career as a visual and marketing designer in the early days of web. Since then I've progressed into UX design and development for everything from enterprise level applications to mobile web and apps.

I use an iterative process of discovery, design, implementation, and measuring. The results are conversational, fun, flowing, and interactive. I understand the importance of web standards, the benefits of sustainable web frameworks, the meaning of semantic markup for the web, and the value of desirable, accessible, usable websites.

Specialties: ux design, interaction design, UI design; visual design; xhtml + css; usability
