Brandi Lane's Summary

I have over ten years of Executive Assistant/Office Manager experience at companies like Amazon.com, Pravda Studios and Curtis Management. For the past six years, I have been working as a web agency producer/project manager, working primarily with Washington State Tourism on ExperienceWa.com content and special project endeavors like Savor Washington and the National Geographic mapguide of the Central Cascades in Washington State and Oregon.

As a project manager for LEVYsheckler, I managed a staff of contractors that collectively created content and code for ExperienceWa.com, plus managed the client side of the accounts.

I personally have created websites for numerous small businesses and artists, including setting up extensive portfolios, copy writing/editing, SEO content, and licensed assets management.

I have worked independently for the last three years, solely to assist clients who needed my services after my last employer closed their doors. While I love the responsibility of working for myself, I miss the workplace culture and feel the need to return to a creative and innovative environment. I am now seeking a position as an Executive Assistant to a dynamic leader, or a Project Manager/Producer position in website management.
