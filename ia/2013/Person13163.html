Ben McEvoy's Summary

My extensive experience has allowed me to successfully produce and provide creative strategy on projects for clients and brands in film, television, finance, education, consumer packaged goods, quick service restaurants (QSR) and startups/SMEs in interactive, web, print, successfully managing a range of projects throughout Canada as well as internationally in India, Singapore and China.

Notable clients and partners include:

* The Ontario Media Development Corporation
* Sheridan College
* Seneca College
* Centennial College
* The Singapore Workforce Development Agency (WDA)

I also have a strong interest in developing capacity and community building for the creative sector to help bridge the divide between art and commerce as well as to support the growth and success of the Canadian creative community. Previous initiatives includeleadership and business training programs for animation professionals in conjunction with Computer Animation Studios Ontario (CASO), a highly successful internship program in partnership with industry leaders such as Starz Animation (Arc Productions), the NFB, CORE Digital Pictures, Bedlam Games and Pipeline Studios.

Most recently I have co-founded Toronto's newest and biggest Animation festival, the Toronto Animated Arts Festival International (or TAAFI) - the TIFF of Animation Festivals. Working with our supporters we endeavour to nurture and grow the artistic community and industry within Toronto as a source of amazing content and opportunities for business.
