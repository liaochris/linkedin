Giovanni Carbonella's Summary

Carbonella Suits:

About our bespoke approach

Carbonella Suits is specialized in Ready-To-Wear and Tailor Made collections for men looking for an exclusive and exquisite suit. We supply our clients with superior Italian suits which seamlessly fit to any body type.

Our Ready-To-Wear collections offers our clients a budget-friendly exclusive suit which is tailored on premises and available after only week.

With our Tailor Made collection we aim at customers looking for that little bit of extra, or men wanting to celebrate a special event with a completely tailored suit. With a choice of over more than 125 bespoke fabrics, 300 lining finishings and more than 200 shirt fabrics we can fulfill any demand desired.

With our third line, the Carbonella Business Suits, we inform companies on how wearing business suits within an organization can create more productivity and sales revenue. 'You never get a second chance to make a good impression' is a quote that we take very seriously.

Our long experience in the industry enables us to present our customers with a unique and exclusive concept where the combination of high quality fabrics and a 24/7 service guarantees remarkable results.

Why a Carbonella Suit?

Exclusivity:
- Hand picked selection of the collections (Ready-To Wear)
- The brands we offer are only available through Carbonella Suits
- Only 6 pieces per model are available for the whole Benelux

Quality:
- Exclusive Italian men's suits with excellent finishing (Ready-To-Wear)
- Choices between 100's, 110's and 120's (Ready-To-Wear)
- 125 of the most superior fabrics available in 100% Australian Merino wool up to 150s (Tailor Made)
- 200 different fabrics of shirts to choose from (Tailor Made)

Comfort:
- Presentation of the collection by appointment in our showroom, on premises, your office
- Measures are always taken on site
- After retouching, the suits are delivered within a week (RTW) and 3 weeks for (Tailor Made)
- We offer a 24/7 service
