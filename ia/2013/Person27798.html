Giuseppe Brocchetta's Summary

Owner of two Groups on LinkedIn: Credit Managers Network (CMN) and Credit Management Advisors Networtk (CMAN).

Consultant that introduces or improves the Credit Management in italian or international companies, international Trainer, Journalist.

Since 1990 he has been enrolled in ACMI (Associazione Credit Managers Italia) as Group Coordinator, Trainer, Speaker, Board Member, Vice President, Secretary General, Chairman. Since 1999 he attended the international FECMA (Federation of European Credit Management Associations) meetings on behalf of ACMI. As Credit Manager in multinational company (Alcantara) he focused on objectives such as the reduction of collection and circulation time, debts owed to credit institutions, as well as credit losses. Professional international Trainer specialized in Credit Management seminars for leading companies, in particular on foreign business credit.

Specialties

He is a member of the "Ordine dei Giornalisti della Lombardia" (Order of Journalists of Lombardy), as a free lance journalist he published articles and media releases in various economic and financial national (Espansione, ACMI News, Il Valore, Credit Village, VoiceCom News) and international papers (FECMA Newsletter, Conquerir, Trade Credit Reflexions). He has been speaker at national (Il Sole 24 ORE) and international (AFDCC - Association Française des Credit Managers et Conseils) events.
