Fabio Vancini's Summary

Fabio works in a senior capacity for Germany´s global energy, environment and management advisory firm (Fichtner GmbH). He was previously senior Program Manager at the global policy think tank (OECD, Paris). He started his career at Shearman & Sterling (regulatory due diliegency) and Merrill Lynch Realty (urban development activities). He serves on the Board of global professional institutes. The European Environment Agency has recognized some of Fabio’s publications as among the best in environment and sustainability alongside books by World Business Council for Sustainable Development and the World Bank. With over 20 years professional experience with nearly 40 countries across 5 continents, Educated at Yale and Cornell, Fabio holds multi-lens skills stemming from his risk science, environmental policy, public-private management and law-based qualifications.

SPECIFIC EXPERIENCE

✔ Strategic Change Projects (sector-wide analytics, strategies & programs for inter-entity & inter-jurisdictional co-operation and performance enhancement (mining, oil/gas, renewable energy, waste, water and environmental sectors)
✔ Institutional Optimisation & Upskilling (administrative governance reform, organization strengthening, performance benchmarking, training, HR needs & capacity building programs, for ministries, utilities, regional bodies)
✔ Sustainable Management, Financing & Enviro Strategy Work (impact reviews at policy & programme level, enviro + social sustainability in infra development projects, revenue management/pfm, service affordability and tariff strategies)
✔ Public-Private Partnerships & Transaction Advisory (design outsourcing & privatization strategies, M&A due diligence and tender review on EHS & organisaton)
✔ Market & Regulatory Diagnostics (development of regulatory frameworks, cost-recovery strategies, set up contract monitoring & regulatory units for ministries, development banks and municipalities)
