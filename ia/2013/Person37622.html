Carol Dunitz, Ph.D.'s Summary

I am a writer, composer, lyricist, and actor. My new show, 'Bernhardt on Broadway,' about Sarah Bernhardt, premiered October, 2010. I am currently working on a marketing campaign in order to arrange an extended tour. I have also started to do research for a new show.

I have previously worked as a professional writer and strategist and continue to take on freelance jobs. In this capacity, I consult, advise, do research,and oversee and produce projects. I have written speeches for many high level executives, and created countless advertising campaigns as well as individual tactics. I also write books for clients. My boutique agency continues to offer full service advertising services.

I have written eight books including, "Louder Than Thunder," which received over two dozen positive reviews in national media, "One Hungry Child," "Already Spoken for," and most recently, "Can't Help Falling in Love" with David W. Menefee.

I also speak professionally on numerous topics including: Interpersonal Communication, Listening & Observing, Sales & Negotiating, Customer Service, and Advertising and PR.

Specialties: Marketing, Writing, Composing, Acting, Professional Speaking

My new musical, 'Bernhardt on Broadway,' premiered October 2010 to enthusiastic audiences. I am currently putting together a marketing campaign for the show in advance of an extended tour for the show. I am interested in being introduced to management at theatres or other venues that are looking for exciting new work and may have an interest in a sensational one-woman show about the greatest actress who ever lived, Sarah Bernhardt. Toe-tapping melodies that stay with you along with dramatic content that delves into the psychology of the protagonist make this a show you will not want to miss.

Speeches and presentations are most often delivered in costume. I often sing original songs that underscore the points I make.
