Anita Foster's Summary

A qualified teacher working in field of environmental and conservation education since 1992. Since 2002 I have specialised in education outside the classroom, encompassing all areas of the formal and informal curricula, helping schools and Early Years settings consider and improve the use, design and management of their outside spaces to achieve outstanding outdoor provision.

Specialties

Training, facilitation, workshops, curriculum planning, programme development, project management, fundraising support, review and evaluation, pupil and community engagement and participation
