Edward Malinoski's Summary

With a belief that “people are the foundation of business success,” and a keen ability to recognize the strengths of individual team members and overall teams, I envision and create people solutions that drive better business processes by building on strengths and empowering team members to execute their responsibilities effectively.

I have a deep understanding of the role culture plays in developing a high performance high engagement organization. To that end, I have been successful in shaping and delivering strategic interventions to develop a positive business culture. I’m a hands-on personnel manager able to design and deliver high impact integrated personnel strategies to attract and retain top talent and enable the successful delivery of business objectives.

I am fiercely committed to creating a dynamic and diverse work environment that fosters a culture of innovation, collaboration and creativity where each team member is enabled to achieve their greatest potential.

Whether I am managing people and creating and implementing change management initiatives or consulting to business presidents and senior management on leadership and organizational issues such as: leadership development, top team development or assessing and eliminating organizational barriers to success, my goal is the same….to be a key strategic business partner and to bring vision, innovative thinking, energy and drive to direct and support the full scope of talent and people management.
