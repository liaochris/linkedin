John L. Barbato's Summary

I am a mortgage banker that specializes in green lending and renovation loans. I have been serving families and businesses since 1999. Guaranteed on-time closings with fast underwriting and fundings all under-roof. Assistance available for first time home buyers and individuals with past credit issues. All programs available including FHA, VA, Conventional (including HARP), Jumbo and USDA loans. I strive to make every client a customer for life.
I also partner with real estate agents to increase their number of closings per month while working less hours by helping them implement specific marketing strategies that myself and other top producers around the country are using.
I dedicate myself to consistently being a resource to my clients and partners by staying up-to-date and ahead of market and industry changes and opportunities. Additionally, I am a proponent of education - building my knowledge base and educating others. I am a Certified Real Estate Instructor by the Delaware Real Estate Commission.

Specialties: Green Lending (Energy Efficient Mortgages) & Renovation Loan Specialist, Residential Home Financing, Commercial Financing, Marketing, Home Equity Acceleration, Credit Repair and Maximization
