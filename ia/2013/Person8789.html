Anthony Muscat's Summary

I am a professional in the digital media industry and currently hold the position of owner and director at eStrategy Group.

I started in the industry thanks to my passion for 3D Animation which motivated me to study digital media. This then led me on a path moving through different positions in the industry; from being a Multimedia Teacher and freelance 3D Animator/Modeller to then become a Front-End Developer, then Project and Account Manager before eventually becoming General Manager and then a Director.

Loving the journey that my career has brought me on so far and looking forward to what is still in store for me.

Always looking forward to meeting new people within the industry who share the same passion for digital media, so drop me a message if that is you!

Specialties

Online media - websites, Facebook apps, eDMs and banners, online games, iPhone and Android apps, mobile sites and more!
