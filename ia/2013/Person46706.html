Sunil Gaikwad's Summary

Sunshine Consultants is a start-up venture providing services in the areas of Human Resource Consulting, Labour Laws Advisory & Execution & Payroll Management. I have tried to leverage my cumulative experience of ten years with reputed companies like HDFC Mutual Fund & Johnson & Johnson while aiming to give a complete corporate experience to our clients. A large gap between the expectation of Corporates & the limited choice of service providers in the market is what makes us believe that we can be the best with a strong corporate experience in stride

Sunshine Consultants wishes to bridge this gap & provide end-to-end labour law compliance & payroll management services.
