Doyle Martin's Summary

After 30+ years in the entertainment lighting industry I am happy to be faced with yet another new experience as I take on the job of Director of Design Services for TLS Productions, Inc. I am both happy and excited to be back in a position to do more design work again. More than that, I have the great luck and opportunity to work with a top notch bunch of professionals day in and day out doing what we all love to do.

I still believe that drama doesn't belong on the job site and that anyone who hires our company should feel that they simply need not worry about the lighting, rigging, or production on their show. I want anyone who hires us to have the utmost faith the we will always do our very best. We will always strive to provide the absolute best customer service, courteous and knowledgeable staff and well maintained gear and systems to our clients. It is more than what we do, it is who we are.

My Specialties: All things Lighting, Production Manager, Technical Director, Lighting Design/Program/System Engineer/Master Electrician/Crew Chief; Grand MA/Grand MA2 console programmer; Whole Hog console programmer; Maxxyz console programmer; Digital Lighting Specialist (DL2, Axion, Green Hippo, etc.); LED lighting specialist; Lighting system networking specialist. Proficient in AutoCAD. I have contacts all over the world and in most major convention centers in the country.
