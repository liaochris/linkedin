Carl Dickson's Summary

I'd rather talk about you and your business, but you came here to find out about me, so here it goes:

I have helped millions of people develop business and write better proposals as the head of my own web-based empire. Maybe "empire" is stretching it. But in a fairly small niche, it's a pretty big fish.

Big numbers: The articles and books that I've published have been read by upwards of 6 million people. Each month that goes up by about 100,000. Each week, the newsletter that I publish goes out to 65,000 opt-in subscribers. The LinkedIn group that I moderate (search for "proposal writing" and it should be the first one listed) has over 13,500 members.

At CapturePlanning.com I've published thousands of pages of material to help people improve their business development and proposal efforts.

At PropLIBRARY I turned all that information into a high-end professional tool to help people win business by delivering immediate inspiration, expert guidance, and productivity enhancements. PropLIBARY is about learning while doing. People with experience use it to win more of what they pursue. People who are relatively inexperienced use it to compete against those who are experienced.

At VirtualVPServices.com you can get consulting support directly from myself and key content providers and partners.

Over the last 23 years, I have helped companies submit thousands of proposals to government and industry clients. In past lives I have been the President of the APMP National Capital Area Chapter, developer of proposal software, and a consultant.

Because I run a web-based empire, I actually don't get out enough and don't have enough opportunities to interact with customers face-to-face. I love speaking and training, and look forward to the next invitation I receive. I've spoken at numerous conferences and events, and delivered training to dozens of companies from billion dollar firms to small businesses.

Carl Dickson's Publications

  • The CapturePlanning.com MustWin Process

    • CapturePlanning.com
    • April 2010
    Authors: Carl Dickson

    An off-the-shelf, end-to-end set of process documentation for business and proposal development. Provides a solution to late starts, teams that don't work together, and reviews that provide too little guidance too late to do any good. Originally published as a workbook, it has been upgraded and incorporated into an online tool called PropLIBRARY.

  • Whitepaper: Reengineering The Proposal Process

    • CapturePlanning.com
    • October 2007
    Authors: Carl Dickson

    I wrote this whitepaper to document what how the proposal process should work (instead of the way it usually does). It was an important step leading to the creation of the CapturePlanning.com MustWin Process.

    If you are tired of ineffective Red Teams and submitting proposals you know could be so much better, please read this white paper and join us in changing everything the industry thinks it knows about the proposal process.

  • Whitepaper: Why It's Time to Evolve Beyond the Red Team

    • CapturePlanning.com
    • January 2007
    Authors: Carl Dickson

    I wrote this whitepaper when I realized that Red Teams can't be fixed, to document all the reasons why. Later, after someone asked me what I recommended instead, I would go on to write the CapturePlanning.com MustWin Process.

  • Whitepaper: Real World Teaming Tips

    • CapturePlanning.com
    • December 2012
    Authors: Carl Dickson

    This is an 11 page white paper with 65 tips for successful teaming that I wrote just for fun. It covers the subject from the prime contractor's perspective and the sub contractor's perspective. We've even thrown in small vs. large business considerations. This is powerful stuff. I even had to put a warning label on it.
