Matthew Weiss' Summary

What the art director in me does?

Companies are constantly looking for ways to improve their communications with customers while saving money and improving efficiency. I am able to take a look at companies materials and create a clear and consistent moving brand image that works across all platforms. Teamwork, organization, leadership and creativity are the cornerstones of my practice.

What the senior designer in me does?

My past experiences with many types of collateral and marketing techniques give me an edge. I easily pick up on current brand standards/guidelines while suggesting new ways to effectively improve upon materials to create interest within the field.

What the production manager in me does?

With thirteen years in the industry my rolodex of vendors continues to grow. My close-knit relationships give me to opportunity to save time and money by quickly being able to review needs, quote with multiple vendors, pick the right company for the job, handle all prepress work, schedule fulfillment and find innovative ways to deliver to the field. This equals thousands in savings and improved efficiency in delivery and warehouse storage.

Please contact Hyland Graphic Design & Advertising today to see how we can become an asset to your organization.

For samples of our work please visit us at: www.hylandgraphics.com

Specialties: Print design, web development, apparel, mobile marketing, corporate branding/identity, art direction, project/production management, social media, and advertising specialty items.
