Mike Stout's Summary

Over 20 years of international and domestic franchise sales, operations and marketing experience with restaurant, retail and service related franchise concepts. Brands included Carl's Jr., Hardee's, Papa John's, Volvo Rents, Blackmon Mooring and Pizza Inn. Strong network of contacts in Asia, Europe, North America and South America.

Recruited in December 2009 by Save-A-Lot Food Stores, a division of SUPERVALU (NYSE: SVU) and 1,300 unit chain operating in the U.S. and Caribbean. As Director, License Business Development, responsible for the recruitment of new licensees in owning and operating Save-A-Lot grocery stores.

Specialties: U.S. and International Franchise Business Development; Contract Negotiations; Market Analysis; Strategic Planning; Budget Planning; Public Relations; Marketing/ Advertising; Project Management; Real Estate/ Site Selection; Team Building Skills; Strong Communication Skills; Consensus Building; Problem Solving
