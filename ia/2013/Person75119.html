Kevin Panet, MS HRM, SPHR-CA ★'s Summary

As the HR Manager at Tierra del Sol, I am firmly committed to serving the organization and its clients. I am not in an active job search, nor do I intend to leave my organization anytime in the forseeable future.

With over 25 years of experience in Human Resources, I have worked in both the private and public sectors. I have experience in aerospace, manufacturing, banking, insurance and most recently, non-profit. I have helped organizations to grow and I have had been with a few companies that have closed. In my current position, I have the opportunity to serve as the Human Resources manager, responsible for a full range of HR activities, including full cycle recruiting, training, compensation and benefits, employee relations, organizational development, safety and workers compensation.

Currently, the purpose of my LinkedIn and other social networking profiles is to network with, and encourage, fellow HR professionals and/or those desiring to make a career out of the HR profession. Over the years, I have seen far too many individuals end up in HR by accident, not by design. That's why I am a very strong proponent degree programs in HR, continuing education with the PHR/SPHR certification, and lifelong learning with professional associations like PIHRA and SHRM.

Specialties: Employment Law, Organizational Development, Recruiting, Employee Engagement, Training Design & Delivery, Employee Relations, Team Building, Conflict Resolutions, Change Management, Benefits Administration, Employee Recognition Programs, Performance Appraisal Systems, Employee Feedback Design & Analysis, Management Development, Labor Relations, Management Training / Coaching, Safety Training, Security, Records Management, Strategic Planning, Project Management, and Continuous Process Improvement.
