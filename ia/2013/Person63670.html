Jill Grindle's Summary

Professional Resume Writer and Consultant experienced working with professionals and executives. Excited about helping serious professionals reach new career heights with innovative career documents and services, and online tools.

Featured resume consultant on NBC affiliate, WWLP Channel 22 News. Published writer with resume and letter samples appearing in several career books. Resume evaluator at job fairs such as New York Times and Boston Globe's Salute our Heroes, job fair for military job seekers. Volunteered for a pro bono resume program through Career Management Alliance, Resume Worskhop Presenter.
