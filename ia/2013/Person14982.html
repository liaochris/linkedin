Kaydee Blinn's Summary

I am a designer — print, web, email campaigns, mobile sites, motion — and I have a clear understanding of when and how all of these types of deliverables relate to each other.

I am a critical thinker and information organizer. I strive to learn all I can about the subject I am working with so I can develop clear and compelling messages for the appropriate audience. My experience is skewed to the business to business technology industry, but I also have an understanding of the retail, travel and tourism and medical industries.

Specialties

I prefer the Mac platform, but I am Windows friendly.

Proficient with InDesign, Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects and Flash (excluding complex Action Script).

Familiar with Fireworks, Dreamweaver and Freehand.

Other capabilities include Microsoft Word, PowerPoint and Excel.
