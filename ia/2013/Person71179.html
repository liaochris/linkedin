Justin Flitter's Summary

An architect of digital customer experiences.

Recently Justin joined the team at Aamplify.co.nz as their Strategic Account Director developing partnerships with service and technology providers and business clients. He's focused on helping clients maximise opportunities at the intersection of analytics, strategy and technology.

With over 13 years experience bringing projects to life like the NRG Trust Business Incubator and keepitblu.com, leading dynamic teams at Student Job Search and Fishpond.co.nz and helping international startups like Zendesk and Booktrack grow; Justin Flitter has a wealth of knowledge, enthusiasm and talent.

Recognised in 2011 as one of the 25 most influential people on Customer Service [Mindtouch] and in 2013 as #20 in the top 100 Most Social Customer Service Pros on Twitter [Huffington Post] Justin has developed a reputation as a thought leader at the intersection of Social Media and Customer Service.

Justin spent 3 years at Zendesk starting in December 2008 as Zendeskian #8. He moved from providing customer support to leading Business Development in Asia Pacific to leading the Social Media Marketing program. After a break in 2011 he returned as the New Zealand sales manager in 2012 and spent 2 weeks running customer workshops in India.

Since 2010 Justin and his Wife Jen have run FLITTER a company developing ‘strategies for the social web’. FLITTER have worked with brands across Sport, Liquor, Automotive, Hospitality, Recruitment, Events, Not-for-profit and SaaS sectors.

Justin is a regular speaker at Social Media conferences, has run countless workshops and worked with more than 80 brands in New Zealand, Australia and the USA.


Justin has a Bachelor of Business Information from Massey University with majors in Marketing and Communications Management.
