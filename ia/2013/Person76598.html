Koren Reyes' Summary

BRAND KOREN REYES
In September 2002 I formed Koren Reyes Photography, a multidisciplinary photography studio that specializes in studio portraits of women. Clients come to me for sexy, tasteful, beautiful pictures and experiences that make them look and feel like a million bucks.

Past corporate clients include the Robin Hood Foundation, The New York Times, SELF Magazine, Prudential Financial and the Boston Philharmonic. Learn more at http://korenreyes.com

PERSONALITY ATTRIBUTES
1) SOOTHING: My clients are real women, not professional models. Once they get in the door, they don't have to make any more decisions unless they want to. I'm GREAT at directing clients into the most beautiful poses.
2) COMMITTED: If you don't shed tears of joy and dance on your chair when you see your pictures, I haven't done my job.
3) SOPHISTICATED: I'm not the cheapest photographer by a long shot. But I think you'll enjoy looking at your pictures more in ten years than in ten minutes.
4) OPTIMISTIC: I know that every woman is beautiful. Every single one.
5) RESOURCEFUL: I love complexity. I figure things out. I'm a super-duper problem-solver.

WORK ATTRIBUTES
1) AUTHENTIC: My work is very real. You won't find any spaced-out robots in my portfolio.
2) UNCLUTTERED: Disorder makes me nuts. My work reflects that.
3) SENSUOUS: I shoot real women, women who want to look and feel the best they ever have.
4) GRACEFUL: ...whether it's a pregnancy or a boudoir shoot.
5) ELEGANT: I look for stately, refined, confident images.

AFFINITIES
I celebrate handmade, artisanal objects. I get excited about anything that is hard to make, takes a long time to perfect and exhibits exquisite craftsmanship. I fawn over handmade watches, handmade fountain pens, beautiful saddles, Corinthian column capitals and beautifully made shoes.

Specialties: Making women look and feel gorgeous (boudoir & maternity), advertising photography, editorial photography, corporate photography, platinotypes, dog photography
