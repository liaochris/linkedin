Alene Geed's Summary

Promotional Products Advertising Consultant with over 35 years experience. Based upon the budget, time frame and recipient information, I develop a product offering to meet the expected results of my client. My client can rely on my expertise to provide the best product and solution for the project.

Specialties: My clients KNOW that once they have placed an order with me, they can remove that 'task' from their list. I will get that product delivered on time and done correctly.

Jewelry Designer: I adore designing! It has become the welcomed obsession that fills my waking (and sometimes my sleeping) hours. My designs are developed primarily from raw copper and silver. I add gemstones to enhance the finished product. Inspiration comes from everywhere. The shapes in a painting, a leaf from a tree, colors in a gemstone... and so much more. Designs are available for purchase at http://www.alenesadornments.com
