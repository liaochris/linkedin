Jane Smith's Summary

I am a seasoned business writer and sales/marketing professional with a solid understanding of general business principles. Having served in a variety of sales, marketing, and finance capacities within the automotive industry, I am exceptionally skilled at getting to the bottom-line of any business issue.

I launched Up-To-Date Resumes as a self-employed resume writer in 2007. I currently operate a home-based business that allows me to use my business writing skills, exercise my general business aptitude, and use an element of creativity to generate resumes that showcase a clients' talents in a highly readable and achievement-based style.

Up-To-Date Resumes is a highly personalized resume service and is certainly not a resume-mill. I interview clients from all job descriptions, including C-level positions, to build targeted resumes featuring valued skills, experience, and achievements. I have written resumes for clients and referrals across the country, although most of my business comes from the local Dallas/Fort Worth area. I have been a Certified Professional Resume Writer since 2007.

Professional business writing for local companies is an added area of interest for me. Prospecting letters, letters of introduction, and web copy are a few projects that I have had the pleasure of writing.

Professional writing has been a great way for me to exercise creativity and learn a lot about how the world works - through the people that make the world work!
