John Flynn's Summary

I'm a visual designer, illustrator, and project manager with a decade of experience in software and web design.

I build accessible, functional product with creative teams (art, graphic design, game design) focused on user experience and innovation. I've led those teams in the development of website, video game, and software projects that tens of millions of people have used, day in and day out. That's pretty cool.
