    • Disaster Services Technology / ECRV

      American Red Cross
      • Disaster and Humanitarian Relief
      February 2010 – present (4 years)

      The Emergency Communications Response Vehicle (ECRV), was designed and created for the American Red Cross by Disaster Services Technology staff, to provide communications links between disaster relief operations and the Disaster Operation Center. The vehicles were extensively modified to add an 8.5KW alternator driven by the engine, a 52' pop-up pneumatic mast capable of rotating while holding a large shortwave yagi antenna, a VSAT satellite antenna system, and sixteen HF, VHF, and UHF antennas feeding its radios and data systems.
