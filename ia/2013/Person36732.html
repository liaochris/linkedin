Social Media Marketing Study Hall

November 2010 – Present (2 years 11 months)

Whether you're just getting started with Social Media Marketing or you are ready to take it to the next level, I offer social media training, coaching and consulting for small business owners who are tired of trying to learn it on their own.

I will help you develop a plan that uses the best social media platforms for you, your business and your target audience and uses strategies that get results.

Through hands-on training using remote screen sharing (or in-person if you happen to be in the Denver area) I'll show you exactly how to use Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Google+ and other social media platforms for your business.

I have 15+ years experience as an educator and academic/business trainer with a step-by-step hands-on approach to help you learn how to use the various social media tools to market your business.

I specialize in working with service professionals, entrepreneurs, and small business owners who:

- Need help choosing the right social networks to reach their target market
- Set up accounts on several different platforms but don't know what to do next
- Aren't getting the results they want from their social media efforts
- Have a question on how to use a specific social media tool such as LinkedIn, Facebook, Twitter

Here are a few of the things I can assist you with:

- Online Marketing & Social Media Consulting
- Online Account Set-up: Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Pinterest, Google+, YouTube
- Facebook Advertising
- Facebook Promotions
- Audience Building and Engagement on Social Media Platforms
- Clarifying your Business and Marketing Objectives
- Training on Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Google+, Pinterest

I am an experienced Social Media Manager, Social Media Coach and Consultant, and a Business Marketing Coach.

Donna Feldman Coaching & Consulting

2002 – Present (11 years)

Donna Feldman Coaching & Consulting was founded in 2002 to provide a different kind of coaching to business and professional clients. As a Certified Professional Coach, speaker, trainer, social media marketing consultant, facilitator, author and former teacher, I bring an accountable, no-nonsense, yet compassionate approach that illuminates my clients’ vision of a great life and supports them on their journey to achieve and sustain success in both their professional and personal lives.

My proven approach enables my clients to:
► Clarify their business vision and goals;
► Learn how to set up a business and structure it for success;
► Gain a fresh perspective to grow an established business;
► Be more productive by having a plan, structure and accountability;
► Maximize their personal strengths and abilities;
► Increase their confidence and motivation to succeed;
► Establish clear communication between business partners;
► Find and use marketing and networking opportunities and strategies that work WITH them rather than against them;
► Identify and overcome doubts, limiting beliefs and barriers that keep them from moving forward;
► Have an objective supporter on their side who listens, provides feedback and generates ideas; and
► Have time for fun and family utilizing effective time management strategies.

In addition to my one-on-one and group coaching and training, I speak at conferences and workshops on topics including:

• “What Really Works to Find Clients”
• “Get Found on LinkedIn - How to Optimize Your Profile”
• “Facebook Timeline For Business”
• "How To Really Use LinkedIn"
• “It’s Who You Know: Business Networking 101”
• “How Networking Can Help YOU Tap Into the Hidden Job Market”

With the kindness of a friend, the inspiration of a teacher, and the discipline of a coach, I facilitate my clients’ achievement of their vision for a successful business and a rewarding life. Won’t you join them?
