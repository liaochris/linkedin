Kari Apel's Summary

Kindly allow me to welcome you to…Apel Associates, Inc.

Apel Associates, Inc. started in the mind of its founder, Kari A. Apel, back in the early 1970s when she was born. Kari grew up on a dairy and crop farm in Southern Wisconsin where her father and mother began their entrepreneurship, also in the early 1970s. Seeing her parents’ dedication, core values and successes set forth a path to Apel Associates, Inc. as it exists today.

Kari brings thirteen years of experience in tax, accounting, consulting, auditing and finance to pursue her entrepreneurial dream.

Kari A. Apel’s mid-life decision to start her own small public accounting firm rather than to pursue a promising career with an aggressive national public accounting firm seems to epitomize her and the company’s philosophy – we highly value the close personal relationships we develop through integrity, honesty, fairness, caring and trust.

Our firm was organized to provide exemplary financial, tax, accounting and consulting services to small businesses and individuals throughout the United States. We are leaders in providing solutions to you, our clients.

Through our participation in technical conferences related to specific industries, state and national associations, our membership in the AICPA (American Institute of Certified Public Accountants), WICPA (Wisconsin Institute of Certified Public Accountants) and NATP (National Association of Tax Professionals), and our commitment to you, our clients, in assisting with complex business matters, we believe our experience and outstanding service positions us to uniquely respond to your needs.

Specialties

Consultation & Management, Efficiency & Best Practice Studies, Advisory Services, Entity Structuring, Tax Planning & Preparation, Payroll & Accounting Services, QuickBooks ProAdvisor, Compilations, Reviews and Audits

Caring, Honesty, Integrity, Trustworthy, Fairness…These are the words used by those who are familiar with Kari A. Apel, from family and friends to clients and colleagues. Kari has always led her life with the highest of standards, graduating from the University of Wisconsin – Platteville with Bachelors of Science degrees in both finance and accounting, along with a minor in economics. She obtained her Certified Public Accountant license under the “old rules” and has been in public accounting for about thirteen years, starting her career while in college.

Her experience ranges from small, local practices while in college to the largest accounting firm in the Midwest upon graduation.
However, Kari feels the most important knowledge she has gained over the years has been literally hands-on. Kari is a lifelong resident of Southern Wisconsin growing up in Grant County on a large dairy and crop farm. She worked on the farm baling hay, doing chores and learning the ins and outs of running a family owned business. Even today, one of Kari’s most enjoyable things to do is combine the fall harvest with her Dad.

Kari and her firm, Apel Associates, Inc., pass along the same standards to each and every person they encounter. The firm’s philosophies center around treating everyone like family. This is proven in Kari’s interactions with her clients.

Kari recognizes that it is her and her firm’s responsibility to learn and understand each client’s specific goals and needs. Further, Kari and her firm realize it is their responsibility to tailor their services to meet those goals and needs timely, efficiently, effectively and accurately. They are dedicated to helping you grow your business, achieve its goals and become successful because your success is at the core of their mission.
