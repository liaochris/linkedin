Marketing and Development

Carolyn.B. Baker & assoc, llc

2006 – 2010 (4 years)

How to take virtually unknown philanthropist and create a image/legacy.
Between 2007 and 2010 Through Carolyn B. Baker & Associates I was able to create and execute a marketing/branding strategy that spotlighted a heretofore virtually unknown philanthropist, Glorya Kaufman into the "dancing philanthropist" first, I created a new foundation, Glorya Kaufman Dance Foundation for her , and begin to build LA community relations ie: Mar Vista Family Center in support of Lucia Diaz's building of a new youth center w/state of the art dance/creative studio. In Nov. 0f 2007 I organized an event at the Kaufman estate to introduce Mrs.Kaufman to several community arts leaders among them Inner City Arts cofounder Bob Bates, of the awesome skid row art center recently visited by the Royal Couple. A gift of 1million created the Glorya Kaufman Dance Studio, Mrs. Kaufman's love of dance comes from a place of personal enjoyment and desire to be a part of the dance world. My next strategy was the Alvin Ailey Dance Theatre. Ailey in my estimation is one of the greatest dance companies in the world, I felt if Mrs. Kaufman could be aligned with them them it would give her "dance creds" so 6million later she is the 2nd largest individual donor to Ailey with Kaufman scholars, the Kaufman pavilion etc. in a plan that recycles her name and benefits the dance students and Ailey in perpetuity. The coup de grace for me was the Los Angeles Music Center. Which began with a vision I had driving down Mandeville Canyon w/ Glorya a marketing plan that grew from saluting Glorya and the first Tour de Force award to the explosion of a 20 million dollar endowment saving the dance division in the middle of a recession.The Dance division is now, Glorya Kaufman Presents Dance at the Music Center. Every gift has a strong component that benefits kids-at-risk... which for me was the heart of this process. Benefit Kids and dreamers ..Wow. make another star ..Gravy.
