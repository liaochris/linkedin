Bucharest Speakers Bureau's Summary

Bucharest Speakers Bureau is the first speakers bureau in Romania, providing international and national speakers for events, conferences, presentation, meetings, trainings and corporate consulting.

Bucharest Speakers Bureau organized the first Executive Public Speaking Program in 25-26 October 2011, having as guest trainer and speaker, Mr. John Evans, CSP - expert in leadership & public speaking.

The main objective of Bucharest Speakers Bureau is to provide high-quality speakers both for national and international events, and to improve the level of the speaking industry in Romania.


Bucharest Speakers Bureau, Next Speaker! and Executive Program are services of My Concept.

Specialties

Provinding appropiate speakers for events & conferences.
