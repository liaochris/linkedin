Jeannine Silun's Summary

Licensed Realtor and member of the Worcester Board of Realtors with full access to the MLS (Multiple Listing Service). I work as both a buyers and sellers agent to help all of my clients with their real estate needs. For the past 3 years I have also helped some of my sellers who found themselves in a negative equity position, many times by no fault of their own, to negotiate a short sale with their mortgage lenders rather than go through a foreclosure, to help them save their credit so that they can purchase again in the future.

It does not cost my buyers anything to hire me as a buyer's agent and it is to their benefit to have a professional on their side when they are negotiating the sale of a home.

As a staging professional I also offer free staging advice and my time to help them present their home for sale, right from the beginning, in a way that will help it sell in the shortest period of time.

Owner

Homes54

Privately Held; Myself Only; Real Estate industry

February 2007 – Present (6 years 9 months) North Oxford, MA

Homes54 is a full service real estate company helping both buyers and sellers explore all of their options in a very different real estate market than most of us have ever seen. It was first established as a "rent to own" property company, with buyers who were working on repairing their credit and saving money for a down payment and sellers who had lost much of the equity in their homes and needed to explore another option to sell.

Many of the potential buyers I meet who were interested in rent to own can actually qualify for a mortgage. I work with a few lenders who I trust and I can refer my buyers to, to help them get prequalified to buy. With home prices and interest rates still at the lowest we have seen in years, a mortgage payment will most always be less than monthly rent and the tax benefits are also an incentive.

I work as a buyer and seller's agent through LeRoux Realty Group. I also work with many FSBO's (for sale by owners). The major reason people try to sell their house themselves is to save money on the realtor commissions. LeRoux Realty understands so many sellers have lost equity in their homes and we structure our fees to help you put more money in your pocket by offering a flexible rate commission. You also get exposure to the MLS which is key to selling your home since most buyers search online for a home.

Please visit my website at www.homes54.com
