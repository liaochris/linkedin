  • Chondroitin and warfarin: an interaction of note?

    • The Pharmaceutical Journal 2005; 275:407
    • September 30, 2005
    Authors: Kazeem Olalekan

    This is a letter to the Pharmaceutical Journal in which I highlighted the then emerging potential interaction between Chondroitin and Warfarin. It has since emerged that there is a potentially serious interaction between Glucosamine (+/- Chondroitin). The BNF today has Glucosamine listed as having a significant enhancing effect on warfarin (avoid concomitant use). Stockley's Drug Interactions (available on MedicinesComplete.com) analysed unpublished regulatory authority reports and concluded that in 2006, 7 reports of increase in INR in patients taking warfarin after they started taking glucosamine supplement and 2007 FDA database identified 20 possible cases. Mechanism of interaction is not fully understood.
