Jeffrey Werner's Summary

I'm a designer and cyclist living in Vancouver, Canada. I am a member of the Fieldwork Design Collective and served on the board of the 221A Artist Run Centre. In the past I've worked with the Vancouver Aquarium, Green School, Stantec Architects, David Suzuki Foundation, and the Beaty Biodiversity Museum. I have a design degree from Emily Carr University and a bachelor in writing and art history from the University of Victoria. I've worked in the Philippines, Indonesia and the Netherlands. I volunteer with a junior cycling development program and race on the Russ Hays Cycling Team.
