Adrian Jones' Summary

Passionate about building engaging, effective, and enjoyable digital experiences! I believe that websites, applications, mobile apps, or any digital experience can all be improved through user experience design, usability research, and business analysis.

Having more than 12 years in tech, I am an experienced and professional UX designer, business analyst, web developer & designer, information architect, user interface designer, and interaction designer. Yes, I wear many hats, but I look good in all of them.

Over my career so far I have had the privilege of working for or with many marque organizations, including:

- Linux Foundation

- Pfizer

- Pacific Blue Cross
- Electronic Arts
- World Vision
- Vancouver International Film Festival

I excel at information discovery, requirements analysis and usability research. I live and breathe wireframes, process flow diagrams, prototypes, interaction design and UML.

Proficient in client-side and server-side web technologies and programming languages, notably JavaScript, jQuery, CSS3, HTML5, and the LAMP stack (Linux, Apache, MySQL, PHP). CMS developer in Drupal and WordPress.

A team leader with extensive experience with both Agile (Scrum, Kanban) and Waterfall frameworks. I am a Certified Scrum Product Owner. Detail oriented, probably to a fault, but we can't all be perfect.

A Master’s graduate in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) with an avid interest in the latest web and new media technologies, best practices, and standards. You can read my blog at popdigital.ca/blog for my latest pontifications.

Specialties: UX design, business analysis, interaction design, web design, web development, information architecture, user interface design, HTML, XHTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, MySQL, video editing, audio editing, wireframing, Flash development, graphic design.
