Judith Sharman's Summary

Seduced by Holiday Inn from a successful Educational Consultancy, I found myself so fascinated by the world of Business Coaching that I returned to 'school' to successfully enhance my business coaching/consultant qualifications. Now working with Executives and Business Leaders to accelerate growth and their success gives me such a buzz! What a wonderfully broad canvas to work with... and what a privilege to meet so many exciting people!

Personal research, seizing every opportunity for further training as well as studying under some of the greatest minds of our day has given me a uniquely innovative approach. Its success has recently been confirmed through WellTree Business Coaching being 'head-hunted' to offer the UK Government 'Growth Accelerator' Scheme to SMEs and recently to take on the role of Visiting Lecturer for the University of Glyndwr, Wales.

Concentrating successfully and effectively within the Corporate, Hospitality, Social Care & Business world, soft and hard data confirm that our ‘Neuro-Learning Pathway' Coaching approaches make a significant difference to the quality of workplaces, to the company bottom line but most importantly to the commitment and, indeed, lives of the individuals within the company!

Evaluations from Clients & Courses delegates have never been less than good or excellent. Follow up feedback has confirmed this impact is long-lasting. Business Consultancy and, where appropriate, Transformational Changes support have demonstrated significant improvements in team work with colleagues engaging with and 'owning' the Corporate vision.

We are good at transferring our skills & knowledge across situations and industries.

Why not email us to arrange a call to discuss your situation with us?

Business Coaching, Consultancy, Training Courses, Seminars as well as appearances in the media have spread the approach across the North West of England; international training opportunities have spread the word far beyond our frontiers.

WellTree offers bespoke Executive & Leaders Consultancy, Business Coaching and Training incorporating individual & distance On-line Coaching packages. Transformational Change is provided through a wide range of seminars, conferences, workshops and courses.

Well Tree Learning was founded by former Education Leader Judith Sharman. As word spread of the success of the Solution Oriented approaches she used to other sectors, the demand for external consultancy and seminars increased. Finally, Judith took the decision to devote her time to working in the corporate, social care and business world through ‘WellTree Coaching & Consultancy’.

Head Teacher

2003 – 2010 (7 years)

The amalgamation of two schools, an award winning four phase building project, the development of an award winning Social Enterprise Trust 'Preschool and Before & After School Club', Active Award, ArtsMark Silver, Eco Schools Award and the building of four 'elephant' wells in Africa were some of the many highlights of the undoubtedly challenging but highly rewarding years with a wonderful community of children and an exciting team of staff.

Head Teacher

Hoole All Saints CE Infant & Nursery School

January 1988 – July 2003 (15 years 7 months)

The headship of Hoole All Saints was a very special time in my career. I had taught there as a 'rookie' teacher, very early on in my career, and the community of Hoole, Chester had always been close to my heart. Notable successes during this time were very positive OfSTED reports including describing the leadership as "excellent", being used as an exemplar school by the local authority with visits from Chinese, Japanese, Danish, French and American parties of teachers as well as leading the Building Bridges 'Bright Sparks' project for gifted and talented pupils in the Chester area.
