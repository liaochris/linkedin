Cary Dunlap's Summary

I'm a skilled UI/Web designer with a passion for superior user experiences and clean, organized layout. I strive for designs that emphasize brand identity and ease-of-use. I’m an expert user of the Adobe Creative Suite (version 6), Microsoft Visual Studio 2010, and several content management systems.
