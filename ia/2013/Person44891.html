Sally Feinerman's Summary

My expertise and passion is helping women fit exercise into their busy lives. With a background in event management I bring my flair & passion of organisation into my personal training business. I have positioned myself as the fit it in expert and I love to be solution based in finding ways for my clients to live a happier, healthier life.

Specialties

Through Sally’s corporate experience she can relate to women in the corporate arena and she is now combining her passion for women’s wellness with her organisational abilities to assist women improve the quality of their lives through exercise programmes, personal training, group fitness, walking groups and her on line nutrition programme.
