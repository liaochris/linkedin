Ilene Horowitz's Summary

Ilene has 16 years experience as a real estate professional.
Ilene is a full time, full service real estate professional who specializes in 100% customer service. My job is to help clients make good decisions. When working with Ilene you can "expect the best" and receive truly remarkable service.

Communication, attention to details and follow thru are key to a successful move. If relocation is in your plans, whether cross town or cross country, allow me to show you that there is a difference in real estate agencies and agents. I welcome the opportunity to be of service.

Ilene specializes in properties in Morris County, NJ. She is a 3rd generation White Meadow Lake resident in Rockaway Township, NJ.

Specialties: Listing Agent, Buyer's Agent, First Time Home Buyers, Relocation, Negotiating, I work throughout Morris County and Parts of Sussex.
