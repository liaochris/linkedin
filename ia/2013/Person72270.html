Kari Joys' Summary

Specialties

I'm a psychotherapist, a counselor, an author and an educator, based in Spokane, WA. I believe that everyone deserves to be given the knowledge and support necessary to create a happy, healthy life! To accomplish that, I created the JoyIAm Process that helps people transform challenging emotional issues like stress, anxiety, depression and abuse issues into self-esteem, inner peace and light-heartedness.
