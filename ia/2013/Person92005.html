Michael Agustin's Summary

Michael Agustin is co-founder of Weaver.

Michael previously co-founded GameSalad in 2007, the year the very first Apple iPhone shipped. His team developed a desktop tool which empowered anyone to create their own mobile games. Six years later, more than 600,000 creators would reach over 30 million players worldwide and make up 21% of games uploaded to the iOS App Store.

Before GameSalad, Michael worked as a software engineer in Platform Experience for Mac OS X at Apple. He has worked over 10 years in the video games industry. He holds a Master’s in Entertainment Technology from Carnegie Mellon and a Bachelor's in Computer Science from the University of Texas at Austin.
