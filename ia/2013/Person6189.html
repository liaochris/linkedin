Anastasia Montejano, PMP, ACC, CPIC's Summary

Internationally experienced, results driven Change Management Consultant who excels at navigating dispersed teams and units through enterprise-wide change to drive organizational success. 15+ years experience successfully managing complex, global change initiatives across US, Asia and Europe in complex Tier 1 environments with low risk tolerance. Expertise implementing change in organizations both as an external consultant and internal leader of change initiatives. Deep understanding of how to leverage agile technologies to develop and deploy efficient technical programs and products remotely. Recognized spokesperson and author on developing leadership capabilities that generate corporate and individual results.
