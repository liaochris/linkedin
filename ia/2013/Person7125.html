Andrew Pain's Summary

I work with businesses and individuals, improving performance through group workshops and one-to-one sessions. I am also the author of 'Clockwise - 5 great time management principles', which is available now on Amazon, iTunes and Noble & Barnes.

* I design all my own material so it is bespoke and can be re-shaped into two hour-long soundbites, something which makes staff development more cost effective and convenient.

* I offer a 100% refund guarantee to minimise my client's risk of doing business with me.

* I work with a variety of clients from the N.H.S. (Queen Elizabeth Hospital, Walsall Healthcare, City and Sandwell Hospitals), to the private sector, (Lloyds TSB, RoSPA, Sunrise Senior Living), local councils, (Sandwell Council), a variety of small to mid-sized businesses and unemployed youth.
