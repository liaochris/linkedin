Michelle Kerrigan's Summary

Michelle Kerrigan is an expert in workplace success who helps small to mid-sized businesses and private clients develop the practical skills and confidence they need for high performance and productivity.

Based on her 25 years’ leadership experience, Michelle provides an invaluable road map for conquering fear and doubt, reducing stress, navigating change and solving day-to-day challenges.
Her strong business and common sense make a positive and lasting impact on people (and process) in the workplace, resulting in:

More effective management and leadership
Reliable and efficient operations and execution
Higher employee and customer satisfaction and retention
Reduced conflict, confusion and costly errors
Savings in time and money
Increased productivity and revenue growth.

In addition, Michelle writes and speaks about the roles confidence and self esteem play in achieving success, and currently produces and hosts a series for public TV about workplace confidence.


For more information: www.workplaceconfidence.com and www.MichelleKerriganInc.com. Follow Michelle on Twitter @MKerriganInc.
