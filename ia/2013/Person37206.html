Doug Bloom's Summary

Credited, worked on or designed techniques used on over 30 feature films and recognized by The Hollywood Reporter: Next Generation Issue 2007

I have held jobs at major commercial and feature production studios in Los Angeles and New York. Over the years I have filled several supervisory and creative roles on a wide range of projects including motion pictures, television commercials, music videos and development of efficient production pipelines within the studios.

With a foundation in fine arts (animation and film) and a over 15 years of professional experience I bring to the table a strong package of creative vision complemented by a mastery of technical skills and forward looking ideas. Through working in both studio and software development enviroments I have masterful understanding of both the technical and artistic challenges of production I have frequently served as a bridge between the creative teams and technicians tasked with bringing the product to life. Additionally, I have been a major contributor to the design and implementation of in house Academy Award winning software as well as numerous other proprietary work flow, simulation and rendering tools.

Throughout my professional career I also served as adjunct professor at Santa Monica College, an independent consultant/trainer and guest speaker at various industry conferences and seminars organized by ACM SIGGRAPH, The Visual Effects Society (VES), and the I3Forum.

Specialties

Creative Supervision and Direction. Film Production, Computer Animation, Visual Effects, Workflow/Tool Design, Digital Media, Artist Management, Software and R&D Management,
