Aisha Atlanta's InsuranceLady Danzy's Summary

Aisha Danzy is a well known and respected insurance professional, entrepreneur, community activist, and motivational speaker. Currently serving as President of Five Star Insurance Agency, a full service agency in Atlanta Metropolitan area and Territory Manager for Smart Choice Agents in the Atlanta Area.

A graduate of Florida Atlanta University, she has worked in various areas within the insurance industry assisting captive and independent agencies and as a property claims adjuster, before acquiring her own agency and assuming her current positions.

Five Star Insurance Agency has gained popularity and received continuous recognition as a top producer for several carriers within the state of Georgia. The agency specializes in commercial lines. Nominated by the Atlanta Business League for outstanding achievements, Mrs Danzy and her agency have been featured in national newsletters for its superb service and continuous success. With several business ventures at hand, Mrs Danzy owns and operates a state certified driving school.

Aisha Danzy has spent much of her career dedicating her service to disadvantaged businesses, offering free seminars, insurance contract reviews and consultation in commercial risk control. As founder of the non-profit organization Reach for the Stars Mentorship Program, she is a proud supporter of young parents striving for success while attending high school.

Her current position as Atlanta’s Territory Manager of Smart Choice Agents allows her to assist those seeking success within the insurance industry. Offering business development, training, managing, and mentoring of Smart Choice Agents, she finds pleasure in providing a solution to insurance agents needing available markets. Increasing production and improving retention, while providing "Five Star Service" are the basis of her agency. Her faith in Christianity, persistence, and dedication have been leading attributes throughout her life and the basis her continuous success.
