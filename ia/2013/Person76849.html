Kris Nord's Summary

15 years delivering business results through training, work-process improvements, organizational redesign and change management initiatives. I've managed projects for organizations of three employees to 300,000 employees during strong growth cycles and significant down-sizing.

I cut my teeth in Human Resources by facilitating Boot-Camp style Corporate Training Programs and serving as a Business-Aligned Change Agent during the downsizing and reorganization of a Fortune 50 company. Those early experiences driving new processes and software deployments in a rapidly changing environment served as the foundation for the systematic, ask assertive, "roll up my sleeves" approach I bring into every Process improvement meeting, Workforce Planning initiative, Annual Goal Setting offsite, Leadership Coaching session and Training project that I lead today.
