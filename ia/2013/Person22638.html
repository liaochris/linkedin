Cheryl Otto's Experience

Founder and CEO

OOPS Inc. - Ounce of Prevention Solutions Inc.

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Human Resources industry

October 2012 – Present (1 year) West Vancouver

OOPS Inc.™ is a one-stop shop for all things Respectful Workplace. Our team includes educators, facilitators, mediators, investigators, violence experts, HR and communications specialists, and professional coaches working together to deliver an integrated solution to conflict in the workplace.

Whatever the workplace issue is – harassment, discrimination, bullying, violence - we can deal with it.

Our clients appreciate the multidisciplinary approach through our unique integrated conflict management system - C.A.R.E.

Senior Partner

RWP Resolutions

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; E-Learning industry

January 2009 – Present (4 years 9 months)

RWP Resolutions provides web based training programs dealing with the Respectful Workplace - addressing issues of harassment, discrimination and bullying. This is a cost effective and highly efficient way of training employees of an organization about human rights in the workplace.

Barrister & Solicitor

Cheryl Otto Law

January 1996 – Present (17 years 9 months)

Industry expert in conflict resolution;
Focused practice in the areas of employment and human rights, dealing exclusively with issues of workplace and organizational conflict;
Specialist in matters involving harassment, discrimination, bullying and interpersonal conflicts;
Development and facilitation of training and education programs, including: Respectful Workplace for Employees; Respectful Workplace for Leaders; Sensitivity Awareness Training: Advisor Training; Internal Investigator Training; Bullying in the Workplace; Conflict Resolution and Train The Trainer;
Development of customized policies, processes and initiatives to ensure respectful work environments, including: policies relating to harassment, discrimination, bullying, violence, codes of conduct and whistle blowing;
Conduct confidential workplace assessments to identify issues of concern for individuals/groups; and
Extensive skill base in alternative dispute resolution mechanisms, including conciliation, facilitation, mediation, conferencing, coaching, conflict diagnostics and neutral investigation to achieve fair and equitable resolutions.

President/Founder

Development and facilitation of training and education programs; have trained 1000's of participants in all levels of organizations and companies: employees, managers, volunteers, students and executives;
Development of policies and processes to ensure respectful work environments;

Assisted 100's of organizations and corporations to develop and implement internal respectful workplace programs.
