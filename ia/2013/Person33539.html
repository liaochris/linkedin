Deborah Gussoff's Summary

CERTIFIED PROFESSIONAL ORGANIZER
Deborah Gussoff is the most experienced Certified Professional Organizer in New Jersey. In Order, Inc. is a full-service organizing consulting firm; we help people better manage their time, paper, and clutter. Deborah specializes in helping people deal with the abundance of paper, creating & maintaining filing systems and setting up systems to deal with paper in-flow and the daily mail, as well as processing medical insurance claims. She also organizes storage areas (attics, garages, closets, and basements) and living areas (including home offices, kitchens and playrooms). Deborah specializes in working with home-based business owners, and busy overwhelmed moms, as well as people who are chronically disorganized or have ADHD.

Deborah Gussoff is a Golden Circle member of the National Association of Professional Organizers (NAPO), a member of the Institute for Challenging Disorganization and a founding member of NAPO-Northern New Jersey. She served as the Chapter's Treasurer from 2006-2010 and began her current term as Vice President in May 2013.

ENTREPRENEUR & APPRECIATION MARKETING GURU, SEND OUT CARDS
Deborah Gussoff has been involved with SendOutCards since 2007. She helps people stay connected with clients and prospects, and build customer loyalty, by sending cards to say "thanks for the referral", "happy birthday", "I miss you", "I'm sorry", or any other occasion you can think of.

You can send a real paper greeting card (not an e-card) in your own handwriting from your computer in a minute. You can also include photos and logos. Just select a card from the online card catalog, type your message, upload any desired images and click send. SOC will print, address, stamp and mail it, all for about $1 a card.

There are business building opportunities in this company and I am looking to build my team by coaching others to do the same. Try sending a card for free at www.sendoutcards.com/deborah
