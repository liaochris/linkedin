CEO

Constant In Opal Music Limited

April 2002 – Present (11 years 9 months) United Kingdom

Constant In Opal Music Limited is an international independent music publishing company and record label.

Constant In Opal is a registered member of the Music Publishers Association (MPA) and the Association Of Independent Music Publishers (AIMP) and has full publisher membership of the Mechanical Copyright Protection Society (MCPS) and the Performing Rights Society (PRS).

Constant In Opal are also professional members of the British Academy Of Songwriters and Composers (BASC) and the Musicians Union (MU).

Founded in April 2002, we operate as commercial music publishers in all genres of music within the music industry and as a library music publisher supplying production mood music to the television, radio, film, advertising, multi-media and gaming industries.

Through worldwide networking, sub-publishing, licensing and industry contacts, Constant In Opal Music maximises the international potential of each song in their extensive catalogue and works closely with songwriters, composers and bands in all aspects of song placement, creative development and career guidance.

Constant In Opal Records, is a digital and physical Phonographic Performance Limited (PPL) registered record label and a subsidiary of Constant In Opal Music Limited.

All CIO labels are also associated with Video Performance Ltd (VPL) and GVL (Germany) for our record labels.

Blue Sky Digital, White Mile Digital, HVC Digital, Tang Tang Digital and HVC Digital are specialist PPL registered digital record labels that come under the Constant In Opal Records umbrella.

Worldwide digital distribution for all Constant In Opal digital labels is exclusively through The Orchard.

Company website: www.cio-music.com
