Lauren Lopez's Summary

I have strong digital & traditional drawing abilities in addition to being skilled in typography, design and wordpress site building.

I have worked professionally in many different design markets, including children's toys, stationery and gifts, fashion and apparel, information & promotional materials, concert posters & children's books.

I am always looking to work on more projects that require illustration, creative thinking, and/or support green/sustainable industry. Please contact me at laurendraws@gmail.com with any job requests - I'd love to speak with you!
