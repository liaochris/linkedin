Andrew Sitler's Summary

I am a versatile, experienced graphic designer who creates effective--and cost-effective--design solutions for a variety of clients. My experience in a wide range of settings--from small design firm, to mid-sized non-profit organizations to large corporation--has not only honed my design skills but taught me to be efficient, fast, reliable and able to perform a wide range of tasks. In a fast-paced, two-person office where production efficiency was critical and resources were scarce, I executed work within strict budgets without sacrificing creativity or quality. I have a strong foundation in illustration, photography, and design, as well as skills in web design, social media, and audio/music. I have worked in a multi-media environment where I designed for print, web and television. My artistic sense, coupled with my strengths in a variety of media, can help your company achieve its goals. I am willing to relocate, and am also equipped to work from home.
