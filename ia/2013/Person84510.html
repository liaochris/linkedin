Erin Malone's Summary

Erin Malone is a dynamic, forward thinking, and action oriented creative professional committed to creating strategic, newsworthy online and offline campaigns.

Personable and approachable, Malone is a professional in her industry and provides outstanding creative direction, empowering those around her. Her work ethic and dedication to ensuring the success of every project is unrivaled and makes her a tremendous asset to any business.

Apart from her graphic design expertise, Malone has experience with interior design, digital marketing, public relations, copywriting and event planning.

She is fluently bilingual (English and French), and constantly seeking new ways to grow on both a personal and professional level.

In her leisure time, Malone enjoys singing, interior decorating, traveling, experimenting with culinary arts, and entertaining friends and family.

Specialties: Graphic design, digital marketing, interior design, corporate communications, event planning, customer relations.
