Loren Wells' Summary

Loren S. Wells is an Arts & Entertainment attorney in Chicago practicing primarily in the music industry. His ever-growing client roster includes internationally known names and promising newcomers. Founder of The Discography: Legal Encyclopedia of Popular Music, he is widely respected for his unique musical expertise. He is a lifelong musician and artist, an avid reader of history, psychology, and economics. He loves his wife, artist Maya Escobar, and their newborn son, Adán Gonzalo.
