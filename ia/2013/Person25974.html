C. Neal Harrington's Summary

Neal Harrington is a political entrepreneur who has worked in the political fundraising and technology field for 12 years and is the Founder of Harrington Forward Thinking and Red Stampede.

Currently, Harrington is managing the fundraising and digital operations for Andrew Murray's Mecklenburg County District Attorney re-election campaign.

In 2012, Harrington served as Campaign Manager and Digital Director for Jim Pendergraph's Congressional campaign (NC-09); and Finance and Digital Director for Dan Forest's NC Lt. Governor campaign. Red Stampede was honored to receive three national awards including the Reed Award two years running for the best statewide campaign website in the country and an Honorable Mention Pollie Award.

In 2010, Harrington managed the NC Republican House Caucus fundraising operation helping them win back control of the NC General Assembly; Mark Neumann's gubernatorial campaign in WI increasing contributions by 76% and the number of donors by 300%; and Andrew Murray's successful Mecklenburg County District Attorney campaign raising more than seven times his opponent.

In 2009, Harrington managed John Lassiter's Mayoral campaign in Charlotte, NC raising more than $600,000 for the campaign. In 2008, Neal and his team rebuilt and then managed Pat McCrory's 2008 gubernatorial campaign fundraising operation which resulted in raising $4.7 million in 6 months; increasing contributions by 191% while expanding the numbers of donors by 513%.

Harrington's other client's have included Dan Barry for Congress (NC-08), Ethan Wingfield for Congress (NC-11), Rick Perry for President, Scott Stone for Mayor, Bill Flynn for Congress, and AJ Daoud for NC Secretary of State,

Neal is a frequent panelist speaker for Campaigns and Elections in Washington, DC and the Civitas Institute in North Carolina.

Specialties: Fundraising Management, Political Campaign Management, Campaign Technology, Website Development, Project Management
