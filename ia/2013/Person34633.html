Greg Matthews' Summary

An accomplished graphic designer, artworker and illustrator, Greg has worked on an extensive range of projects for over 30 design studios, marketing and promotions agencies, and in-house design departments, throughout London and the home counties since 1997, in addition to contract work for companies throughout the UK as well as Europe, the USA and Australasia.

Greg has an impressive multidisciplinary portfolio of work covering: Advertising, Branding, Packaging, Merchandise, Books, Brochures, Reports, Point-Of-Sale, Direct Mail, Exhibitions, Vehicle Decals, Web Design, Video Production, Motion Graphics and Animation.
