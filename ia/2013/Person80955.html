Linda G. Beeler LCSW's Summary

I am a New York City psychotherapist with diverse experience I am a consultant to psychotherapists who want to market their psychotherapy practices. I have been in private practice for the past 25 years. I have completed training in Divorce Mediation from The Center For Mediation & Training. I have post-graduate training at New York School For Psychotherapy and Psychoanalysis.I have completed Supervisory Training Certificate Program in Psychotherapy and Psychoanalysis from The National Institute For The Psychotherapies. I completed ian advanced one year relational seminar at The Stephen Mitchell Center.

My psychoanalytic approach is empathic, sensitive, creative, and compassionate. Many of my students as well as patients have commented on my ability to "create a safe atmosphere" for self-reflection and introspection for learning.

My psychoanalytic orientation integrates self psychology, object relations, intersubjectivity, and relational theory.

Specialties: My specialties are relationship issues, divorce issues, divorce mediation, interpersonal conflicts, anxiety, depression, stress,and Intimacy Issues. Consultant for marketing a private practice! Embrace the computer age in your private practice. My creativity, spontaneity, humor is an asset to my clinical practice.
