David James' Summary

David has 5 years experience as a Graphic Project Coordinator in the Event Marketing industry. He bridges the gaps that exist between project manager, project coordinator, graphic designer, traffic manger, prepress technician, labor lead, and production artist. He also helps 3D artist with Autodesk's AutoCAD and 3D Studio Max when they get in a bind and routinely reads floor plans and structural drawings.

You've seen his work at the Microsoft Office 15 product launch, Parallels Summit, CES, NAB, CTIA, and SXSW. He's managed event signage from conception to disposal at Freeman for clients like Microsoft and Starbucks. In his spare time he creates brand visual identities and web graphics for retailers, t-shirts for bars, infographics for non-profits, and workbooks for birth classes.

David's a seasoned multitasker and he's accustomed to working on many projects at once. He also likes finding creative solutions to problems and implementing new technologies in old systems. He recently streamlined the prepress process at Freeman by including scripts that automated repetitive task saving designers hours over the year.

David currently uses PCs at work and Macs at home so he's comfortable on both platforms. He uses Adobe InDesign, Illustrator, and Photoshop on a daily basis as well as Microsoft's Excel. He's recently done some web design and started learning Twitter Bootstrap, Google Developer Tools, HTML, and CSS

He currently lives in Seattle, Washington and is looking interesting opportunities. If you are looking for a well rounded designer or project coordinator to grow in your company drop David a message.
