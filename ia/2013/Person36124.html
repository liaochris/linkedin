DLite Press' Summary

Dlite Press can design a cover, format, edit and proofread your print or digital book and offer it for sale on major online sites including Amazon, Lightning Source, Barnes & Noble (Nook Press) and Smashwords.

Specialties: DLite will design a cover, layout your work for eBook publishing and offer it for sale on our website. The offer does not include editing or proofreading your work. However, you will have full rights to use the original artwork and/or high quality royalty-free cover image. You also have full rights to publish your book elsewhere. As the author, you will collect a royalty of seventy-five percent (75%) of the PUBLISHERS net receipts all copies of the WORK sold in electronic format.

DLite Press' Projects

  • Page Layout

    • October 2009 to Present
    Team Members: DLite Press

    The layout of your book is very important. The average paperback book size is six inches wide and nine inches tall, which is much smaller than the average size of a sheet of an 8.5 x 11 inch sheet of paper. This means that if you are submitting a book for printing, you will need to select the proper page setup.

    Your page layout can be designed with any word processing software if your book is for a small audience of friends or family. Any serious work, however, should be prepared using a page layout program, namely a desktop publishing, DTP program.
