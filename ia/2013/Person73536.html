Keiko Hsu's Summary

I’m an experienced leader and change agent who is passionate about continuous improvement. With a strong track record for leading strategic initiatives, I’ve driven business process improvements, started up new teams, and transformed organizational culture to improve quality, productivity, customer satisfaction, and growth.

I combine my left-brained process-orientation, systems focus and analytical skills with my strategic visioning, collaborative cross-functional team leadership, and intuitive coaching capabilities. I have an exceptional knack for creating structure out of chaos, organizing complex situations, asking candid questions, and solving problems.

With 20+ years of leadership and change management experience at General Electric Company in various business units and HQ, I’ve led strategic initiatives in customer satisfaction, sales, training, e-commerce, and quality. I’ve also led a non-profit as President & Board Member of the San Francisco chapter of the International Coach Federation (ICF).

INITIATIVES I’VE LED:

• Restructured and led ICF San Francisco during a merger and most transitional year in its 14 year history
• Pioneered the implementation of Net Promoter Score, now used GE-wide for monitoring customer loyalty

• Drove cross-selling across 10,000 sales employees with 40 GE business divisions
• Started up a team in India to support the U.S. sales force of a $5 Billion business
• Created an e-Productivity Team to promote e-commerce usage by GE’s distributor network
• Built a Training Department for a 500-person sales force and 1000 GE distributors
