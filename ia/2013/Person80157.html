Leonardo Cortese's Summary

My passions are the travels and the sea so my objective it has been that one job that it allowed me "to go"... to 18 years I have run the old small hotel Locanda Nova in Pontassieve, a bet, with sacrifice and patience I have tried to render it most possible confortable it but in the meantime I dreamed to create something only mine and in 1994 Leonardo' s Rooms was born, one small guest house that allowed me "living and travelling" to can sharing with my hosts this passion. To the beginning of the 2007 my small dream one has come true, I have closed the Locanda Nova Inn in Via Monzecchi, and they are successful to buy Leonardo' s Rooms, now I dedicate myself completely to this activity and as soon as possible I go for a travel or for a trip... but I must go.

Holiday Home Casa Nova

May 2009 – Present (4 years 6 months)

Casa Nova is located in the downtown, in a quiet area, behind the walls of Castel Sant'Angelo.
The location is central close shops, self-service laundry, restaurants, pizzerie providing good local food. It is only 30 metres away from the railway station and bus stop. You can go to Florence just only in 20 minutes. Trains are frequent, one every 15/20 minutes. Leave your car in one of the several free parking area in Pontassieve. Our home is the best basic for your tuscan routes.

Excellent location to tour Florence from.

Bed & Breakfast Leonardo's Rooms

Self-Employed; Myself Only; Leisure, Travel & Tourism industry

January 1994 – Present (19 years 10 months)

Leonardo's Rooms Bed & Breakfast is a friendly, welcoming house in Pontassieve, only 10 km. from Florence.
Comfortable and beautifully furnished environment.
6 bright accommodation, all ensuite or private facilities (modern and functional).
All rooms are equipped with colour televisions, clock radios and safe box; hairdryers and tea and coffee making facilities (free on request).
No smoking rooms available.
A lovely balcony overlooking the roofs is at your disposal for your leisure moments.
Our guests can use the "fitness corner" too.
Free access to the guest house - this means you can come and go as you please at any time.
Located in the downtown, in a quiet area, behind the walls of Castel Sant’Angelo.
Plenty of unrestricted secure free parking close to Leonardo's Rooms.
Pontassieve has shops, restaurants, pizzerie providing good local food.
Leonardo’s Rooms B&B is only 30 metres from the railway station and bus stop, so you can to catch up easy Florence (about 20 minutes). Ideally based to visit the most famous and beautiful places of Tuscany beyond to other small known treasures less.
We are on shopping road; the Mall Outlet of Leccio Reggello is only 5 Km. and Barberino Designer Outlet Mugello is only 30 minutes driving.
Our hosts are happy to help with touring information.
Small group bookings welcome - exclusive use of the guesthouse can be arranged.
