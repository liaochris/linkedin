Michael Frost ACI's Summary

We have inspected homes since 2004, and our goal is to provide clients with a thorough and honest report for you to take to the closing table. Certified with the International Code Council (ICC) and Certified with the American Society of Home Inspectors (ASHI). We perform inclusive thorough home inspections and provide reports within 24 hours via email. Reports include digital photos and complete descriptions of all defects and findings. We inspect all visible areas and components of the home, including crawlspaces, attics, visible structure framing and complete plumbing, Heating and Air conditioning systems, and electrical components.

Specialties: Radon monitoring tests, Pool and spa inspections, Roof inspections, Structural inspections, Septic Dye tests, Well flow potability and water lab tests.
