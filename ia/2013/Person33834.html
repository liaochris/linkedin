Deidre Brathwaite's Summary

A certified Social Media Consultant, with more than three years’ experience, who creates strategies for entrepreneurs and SMEs which focus on communicating a branded message to one's targeted audience. Strategies are implemented using an integrated approach of paid, owned and earned media which enables our partners to harness the power of the digital sphere and create measurable results.

A blogger who has been published on international blog ‘ Social Media Today’ and local business blog ‘Business Barbados’ among others. Deidre has also hosted training seminars for the Barbados Small Business Association (BSBA), Fund Access and the Career & Professional Development Committee of CitiBank NY and conducts an annual seminar for the Student Entrepreneurial Empowerment Development Program (SEED) of the Cave Hill Campus, UWI.

A passionate Board member of the Optimist Club of Barbados Bridgetown and movie buff.
