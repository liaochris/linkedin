Julian Cordero's Summary

Julian Cordero is a third-year law student at New York Law School where he has focused his concentration on both Intellectual Property and Corporate law. Prior to joining New York Law School Legal Services, Inc., Julian has worked in a legal capacity for the Brooklyn District Attorneys Office, the United States Attorneys Office for the Eastern District of New York, Burberry Limited, and CBS Corporation. In addition to Law, Julian has a passion for music. In his spare time, Julian writes song lyrics and produces Hip-Hop/R&B songs for independent music artists.
