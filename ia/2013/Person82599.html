Lorri Stueber's Summary

I started my real estate career in 2005. Real estate is ever changing so everyday is a new experience and lessons learned. I love working with both Buyers and Sellers. I work with First Time Buyers and Experienced Buyers, clients that have difficult challenges like a divorce or short sale, clients who maybe downsizing or moving. Whatever my client's needs are I go the extra mile to create a great experience for them. I have listed million dollar propeties and properties for $19,000. I have a vast knowledge of the South Centeral Wisconsin Market. I continue to strive and improve my level of service to my clients and I continue to work outside the box to achieve my buyer's and seller's real estate goals.
