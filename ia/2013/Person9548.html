Arif Ratansi's Experience

BSEEN

July 1994 – Present (19 years 2 months)

As the owner of BSEEN™, that offers multi-media, branding and design strategies to companies, Arif Ratansi has a firm belief that Visibility Sparks Results™.

His leadership approach is focused, organized and has a communications style that is honest and engaging. Arif is committed to providing marketing services and tools to organizations looking to communicate the value and impact of their products and services to their stakeholders.

Fuelled by creativity, fun, and strategic thinking, Arif adds a whole new meaning to multi-tasking. His ability to manage multiple projects at once, combined with his art direction, graphic design and project management skills allow him to deliver client work with ease, excellence and enthusiasm.

For over 20 years, Arif has delivered communications solutions for Canadian, U.S, multi-national and not-for-profit brands.

Arif's varied responsibilities as the owner of BSEEN™ include business development, maintaining day-to-day operations, creative structuring and managing projects at the highest level, including attendance at all meetings. Arif's hands-on presence is based on a personal and professional mandate. His vision is to offer a product and service to his clients that is innovative, high-quality and results-driven at its core.

"It is vital for me to maintain a level of client-focused excellence," says Arif. "What I design, produce and the service I offer must have sustainability for my clients. When they see value in the end product, then I know I have done my job effectively. My goal is simple: to give my clients cost-effective and compelling ways to present their products and services."

Arif is approachable, strategic and his friendly personality allows him to embrace leadership with its responsibility, accountability and authority. He has a passion for creative thinking, networking, staying current and committed to his business and his clients.
