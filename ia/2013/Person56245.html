Ivelina Atanasova's Summary

Entrepreneur by heart, I'm fully dedicated to the Digital. It's All About Digital is a motto of my life.

In 2010 I founded NewTrend.bg - online media about digital business and a valuable source of information for anyone who uses the Internet for communication and contact with the customers or offers web 2.0 business products and services.

In 2013 a Startup Weekend in Sofia was the start point of ModeZ - fashion platform about street style trends worldwide.

Blogger, speaker, author, consultant and teacher, my greatest achievements are my family, my career and my team.

In 2012 NewTrend.bg was awarded as the best Media BG site in a competition with two of the Bulgarian national TV media - bTv and Nova Television.    

Professional Specialties:
 Marketing and Business Development, I love the new technologies and I'm dedicated to developing and raising the awareness towards the digital industry.

Looking forward to partnering with people with a shared vision of growing together.
