David Waldorf's Summary

David Waldorf is President and CEO of Waldorf Trade Risk, LLC, a specialty insurance concern he founded in 2009. Waldorf Trade Risk focuses on the creation, development, marketing and distribution of innovative risk financing solutions for businesses with both domestic and international credit exposures.

With 25 years of insurance industry experience, David has worked extensively on the development, marketing and distribution of numerous specialty-coverage products, including short-term trade credit insurance, trade finance, political risk, and sabotage and terrorism insurance. During his career, he has also been involved in structuring and placing these coverage lines on behalf of a wide range of Fortune 100 companies, professional organizations and affinity groups.

From contract negotiation to arranging the transfer of risk from foreign investors, lenders and businesses with significant trade in emerging markets, David has been instrumental in helping global enterprises find insurance solutions to address a myriad of complex international financial exposures. Under David’s leadership, Waldorf Trade Risk is dedicated to widening the credit risk solution set available to the growing number of companies involved in international trade.

Specialties

Political Risk Insurance. Medium-Term Trade Credit Insurance. Short-Term Trade Credit Insurance. Sabotage & Terrorism Insurance. Trade finance, asset finance, project finance, foreign investments and medium-term trade into emerging markets. Short-term trade risk domestically and into export markets. Advise and arrange transfer of sovereign and sub-sovereign risk.
