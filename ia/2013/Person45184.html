Philip McFee's Summary

Philip McFee is a creative lead with diverse experience in the fields of branding and design. He has worked with a wide range of clients in tech, higher ed., sports, government, and healthcare — from the Fortune 500 to start-ups. He has the hots for the local arts, technology, sports, nutritional sciences, and social advertising/non-profit campaigns.

Philip's design and writing has earned him honors from HOW, LogoLounge, CMYK Magazine, Rockport Publishing, the Summit Creative Awards, and the Society of Professional Journalists, among others. He's always interested in new collaborative opportunities locally and nationally.

And Cleveland? A big fan. Drop Philip a line -- he'll gladly take you out for a High Life at the Academy on Larchmere.

Specialties

Branding and identity work; advertising campaigns; publication design; creative concept development and strategy; interface design; wordpress site design & CMS; copywriting and editing.
