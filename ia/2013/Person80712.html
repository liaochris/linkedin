Libby Juliá-Vázquez's Summary

Libby is the founder of the upcoming online publication, More than Latina. She's a communications professional with extensive experience in writing, editing, public relations, and social media strategy. She served as Chief Content Officer of Being Latino Online Magazine where she was responsible for the magazine's strategic direction overseeing a team of five editors and 40+ writers who provided readers intellectually entertaining' content relevant to the Latino community.
