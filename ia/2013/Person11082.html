Avi Brown's Summary

As co-founder and CEO of BrandAds, I lead the company's product vision and corporate development.

Nothing influences consumers as powerfully as the sight, sound, and motion of video advertising. Brand marketers know that reaching consumers with video ads on connected devices is essential for their success. While the potency of video is irrefutable, there are still some friction points that need to be overcome.

At the core of this friction lies a lack of real-time, actionable measurement that truly empowers brands and the agencies they partner with to make both strategic and tactical decisions. The bottom line is that brand marketers need to know that their video creative is running in safe environments and that the connected media that they’re buying is moving their ROI needle in a meaningful way. That's where we come in.

The BrandAds Bridge is a real-time, cloud-based software platform that surfaces actionable video analytics and insights for brand marketers and agencies. Contact us to learn more.
