Justin Miller's Summary

Art Director & Designer with 10 years of experience helping brands and organizations innovate through design.

My work and professional views have been published in a variety of publications ranging from Communication Arts to the Wall Street Journal.

I've helped teams win all types of design awards including Communication Arts, Graphis, One Show, Webbys, MI6, Addys, and Fast Company Fast 50 rankings.

In the past I've served on the Advisory Board to my Alma Mater — the School of Communication Arts at the Savannah College of Art & Design.

On any given day, you'll find me planning global product strategies, designing a poster, or riding my bike through the streets and mountains of California.
