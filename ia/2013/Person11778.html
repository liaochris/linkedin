Barb Mihalik's Summary

After a long and successful 25 year sales and marketing career in the foodservice industry I retired to pursue my passion for real estate and combine it with my love of sales and marketing. I decided to integrate my love of home decor, as well. As an Accredited Staging Professional and owner of Four Seasons Home Stagecrafters, Inc. I am able to help home sellers present their homes in the best light possible to get a quicker sale.

Specialties

Residential real estate, second or vacation homes, golf course communities, strong negotiating skills, internet marketing and home staging.
