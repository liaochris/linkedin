Tony Hudson's Summary

Tony Hudson is a veteran filmmaker and entertainer with more than two decades of experience in film visual effects and animation. He has spent the majority of his career working at such companies as Industrial Light and Magic, Walt Disney Feature Animation and Lucasfilm Animation, supervising large and small teams in creation of Computer Graphics assets, as well as Layout and PreViz Departments, as well as Art Directing and Visual Effects Supervising small independent films.


Specialties: Management of Creative Teams, Visual Effects Planning and Execution; CG Generalist; Modeling; Painting; Animation; Rotoscope; Compositing; Editing; Supervision of Creative Teams; Mentor; Visual Effects Services; Motion Picture Title services;

Practical experience with: Maya, Nuke, Mocha, SynthEyes, PFTrack, After Effects, Vray, Mental Ray, Houdini, RealFlow, Premiere, Final Cut and Photoshop.
