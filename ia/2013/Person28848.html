Wrayanne Wallace's Summary

Wrayanne Wallace is a Branch Manager and Senior Loan Officer. She has over 12 years’ experience in Mortgage Loan Origination and successfully manages her own clients along with a team of 5 other Loan Officers and the supporting Operations staff.
“Mortgage Lending is a passion for me... My goal is to ensure that each of my clients and referral partners experience the difference it makes to work with a lender who loves what they're doing. One who understands the importance of clear, concise, frequent communication, on-time closings, and who possesses an in-depth knowledge of various loan programs with ever-changing guidelines. Since 100% of my business is referral-based, delivering some “wow-factor” is a crucial part of my business!”
In 2012, Wrayanne was honored to receive the President's Club and Circle of Honor awards for Loan Origination production.
She’s native to Dallas and enjoys working and living near White Rock Lake with her husband of 15 years, Kent, and two young boys, Connor (7) and Hudson (6). Her oldest son, Max (21), has recently joined her in the mortgage business and Wrayanne is very excited that he and his fiancé' are expecting a baby girl in November!
