Marketing, Business Development, & Experience

For me it’s all about freedom........

My entrepreneurial journey started many years ago with the thought that there were way to many exciting and fun things to do in life to have to spend most of your time just earning a living

My first true career was mastering the art of leverage in real estate by putting together large partnerships that owned apartment projects. I started at 22 and by the time I was 29 I was almost retired and then ….. oops!! The economy in the early 80’s crashed and took me down with it.
Darn I was so close too!

Then discovered the true art of leverage in the MLM industry where I joined an early stage company, developed some very powerful mass marketing and lead generation systems and within less than 3 years I was earning over $1.5 Million a year.

Today I truly believe that there are so many paths to achieve that financial and time freedom.... if done correctly, MLM, Top Tier MLM, and so many variations of Online Marketing it would make your head spin.

But the truth is that no matter what your flavor of entrepreneurship is, you future is on the internet, and If your goal is to achieve success there then you are going to need some coaching of what it takes to succeed and what works.

You also need to release your grip on the bricks and mortar and start looking at a business model where you can create a 7 figure income with only your laptop.

Sound interesting?
