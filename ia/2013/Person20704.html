  • OCTOBER 4, 2012: QRCA 2012 Annual Conference - Mission Kid Possible Workshop: Getting the Most from Kids in Research

    • September 2012 to October 2012
    Team Members: Caryn Goldsmith, Pam Goldfarb Liss, Missy Carvin

    Intimidated by the thought of moderating kids? Kids might actually be more fun than adults in a focus group! This October 4th, 2012 session will help attendees learn:
    - How kids think and act differently than adults
    -What to expect from each age
    - How to create a better kid-recruiting screener
    -The ideal group set up for each age
    - How to complement the client's objectives and budget with what is necessary for choesive group dynamic
    In this session, attendees will ultimately understand how to ge the most from kids to address project objectives and how to put these ideals into their moderating skills -- from those with little kid experience, to those who've tried one or two kid sessions, or those who do a lot of kids research, but want a refresher.
