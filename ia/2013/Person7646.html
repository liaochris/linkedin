April Schaefers' Summary

We are a full service Chocolate Fountain Caterer/Rental Company established in 2004. Chocolate Fountains are a great addition to any event you plan on having something sweet at. (corporate or socia).

We use only the finest Belgian chocolate formulated for the fountain so NO OIL OR PARAFFIN IS ADDED. We handle everything from set up to clean up. With 19+ years in the hospitality/service industry we are professional, detail orientated and flexible. We offer four (4) fountain sizes so parties for 20 to 500+ are not a problem.

So if you’re looking to do the small and simple or a bigger event adding a chocolate fountain can fit either. Remember we never charge extra for set up/tear down, an attendant, table presentation or skewers...it's all included!

Specialties

Server, bartender, chocolate lady, chocolate fountain expert.
