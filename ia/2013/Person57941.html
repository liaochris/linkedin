Jamie Smith's Summary

My writing career started in newspaper journalism and I have developed my talents to include magazine writing, social media management, brochure writing and website copy writing.

I provide the words that express a vision ... be it to inform, negotiate, promote or to influence a decision.

Specialties

Adaptability is vital in our changing world. I'm able to adapt my writing style to the needs of the client, be it a newspaper who needs AP style, or a website that needs proper use of keywords.

Accuracy and fairness are both major elements that I demonstrate in my work, no matter what the project.
