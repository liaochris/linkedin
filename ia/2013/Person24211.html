Christine Ferguson's Summary

Christine Ferguson is an Authentic Brilliance Expert, author, speaker, and coach who empowers individuals and teams to unleash their authentic brilliance and achieve new levels of success in their business and personal life.

Christine speaks on personal development, spirituality, and leadership delivering informative and stirring messages. She brings impeccability to her coaching clientele and is endorsed by the World Coach Institute as a Certified Professional Coach and Certified Spiritual Coach. Christine is a founding member of IMPACT (International Membership of Professional Advisors, Coaches and Trainers).

To inspire others to unleash their brilliance, Christine co-authored The Power of the Platform: Speakers on Life with Jack Canfield, Les Brown, Brian Tracy, and Tanya Brown (sister of Nicole Brown Simpson). Christine is also co-author of the highly motivational book Anyone Can! Live a Happier Life with Marion Licchiello.

She is a co-contributor to articles published in Forbes.com and CNNMoney.

Christine’s mission is to help others unleash their authentic brilliance with peace, purpose, and consideration for their fellowman.
