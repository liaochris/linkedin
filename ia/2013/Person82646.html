Lou Franco's Summary

Software product development veteran with over 20 years of experience developing software for financial institutions, enterprise companies, and start-ups.

Consistent record of building and leading teams that deliver quality products. I have developed large scale applications in C/C++, Objective-C, C#, and Java. Expertise in mobile, rich internet applications, and software toolkits.

Highlights from my career include

* Leading the development teams of three start-ups that were acquired.

* Developing mobile solutions for over a decade starting with Windows and Java based PDAs and have been developing for iOS since 2008.

* Co-authoring "Hello! iOS Development", a book for beginner programmers, published by Manning

* Being a lead and contributing inventor on issued patents related to spreadsheets and rich internet applications
