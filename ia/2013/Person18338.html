Bruno Bitter's Summary

Dual-based in Budapest and Berlin, Brunó is a communication and branding expert interested in developing meaningful relationships between people, concepts, products and brands.

With a background in new media, sociology and change management Brunó founded Next Wave Europe in 2008 to focus his expertise on business areas where “markets are conversations”. Brunó has provided digital brand strategy, research, social media services and social business strategy to clients such as Nike, L’Oréal Paris, Universal Music, UPC, Jägermeister, The Forest Stewardship Council and The World Wildlife Fund.

An alumnus of the Berlin School of Creative Leadership, ELTE Budapest and Universiteit Utrecht, Brunó's teaching and speaking experience includes Corvinus University, Gábor Dénes College, CEU Business School and MOME - Moholy-Nagy University of Art and Design.

Specialties:
Strategic brand and communications planning,
Team management, leadership,
Branding, culture & identity,
Social business and social media strategies,
Innovation & Knowledge leadership,
Startup coaching,
Qualitative research, visual ethnography
Quantitative analysis, Big Data
