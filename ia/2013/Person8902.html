Antoine Sottiau's Summary

Passionate about information technology, I develop applications and libraries based on the C# language for work and for the communities. I started my learning of software development by building web projects based on open-source technologies. Then I quickly switched to Microsoft technologies to deal with business uses and architectural cencerns.

I worked two years for Wygwam, a French MS experts company. They let me work on outstanding projects. Whether as a developer in massive projects or "lead developer" (Technical Project Manager) in smaller projects, I try to always provide a pragmatic perspective on issues and a concern about the quality of production.

I spend a lot of time on personal or community developments (Windows Phone applications, libraries for general use, home automation, development tools...)

I now have a business project that I develop full-time.
