Dirk Eschenbacher's Summary

Hello. I am a designer, adman and digital marketer based in Beijing, China. I work on the intersection of advertising, branding and technology.

  In 2012 my partners and I started ZANADU, the premiere travel website in China. We curate some of the world's most amazing luxury and boutique hotels and travel experiences to discerning Chinese outbound travelers. My role at ZANADU is to give creative direction and to develop brand communication and innovative marketing programs. Amongst many many other things. 

Before ZANADU I held several senior creative positions in advertising agencies in China and Asia. Most recently I was Regional Creative Director with Ogilvy & Mather Asia Pacific.  

Rewind. As Executive Creative Director of DDB Beijing I mostly worked on the Volkswagen brand in China; As Executive Creative Director and Managing Partner of Tribal DDB Asia Pacific we opened 12 offices across Asia in 3 years and won Digital Agency Network of the Year; and then the Executive Creative Director for OgilvyOne Beijing and also for OgilvyOne Asia Pacific, where the whole thing with long titles really started.

Together with my teams of super talented writers, designers and techies we won plenty of creative and advertising awards for work on clients like Unilever, Pepsi, Philips, Volkswagen, Audi, Adidas, DHL, Motorola, Nokia, Nestle, China Mobile, Yihaodian and Soho China. Amongst those trophies was the first ever Cannes Cyber Lion and the first D&AD yellow pencil for China.

  I am also a partner in China’s best venue for live music, Yugong Yishan (www.yugongyishan.com) and I ride my motorbikes (Ducati and a CBR600RR) whenever the Beijing air quality permits.
