Joshua J. Jones' Summary

Professional MBA, engineer and political operative with extensive global experience - planning, implementing & executing projects in the O&G/Energy industry and Non-Profit/Political Campaign sectors.

Recently started small business advising political campaigns and candidates (clients have included Tom Pauken for Texas Governor, Ted Cruz for U.S. Senate and Dr. Ron Paul for U.S. President).

Earned an MBA from Hult International Business School, studied on three continents (UK, China, & UAE) with students from 50+ nationalities - focused on strategy, operations & innovation - and worked as a management consultant on an engagement with the Jebel Ali Free Zone Administration in Dubai.

Earned a BSME (Mechanical Engineering) from the University of Texas at Austin, and worked in the energy industry as an international project engineer, planner and analyst in subsea construction for five years.

Extensive global traveler - studied, worked and backpacked abroad.
