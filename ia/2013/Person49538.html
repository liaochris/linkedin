Joe Gordon's Summary

23 cumulative years experience as a criminal investigator in the US Army, a special agent and supervisory special agent in both the US Treasury and Justice Departments, and 12 cumulative years experience as a uniformed police officer and state trooper. Masters degree in Public and Urban Affairs, Graduate of the Senior Management Institute for Police.

Conducting private investigations for individuals, insurors and law firms since 2007.
