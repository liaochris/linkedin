Lindsay Ferrier's Summary

I'm a writer, blogger, television and Internet video host, and reporter, with a background in television production (for cable networks like A&E, CMT, and Hallmark) and local TV news.

Specialties: Writing, commercial and editorial on-camera work, public speaking, and catching children's puke in my hands when the occasion calls for it.
