James Royal-Lawson's Summary

Read my consultant resume here: http://beantin.se/consultant-resume

Educated as economist, digital at heart. My role is to piece all of the bits of the digital-jigsaw together and help companies and organisations create and maintain digital presences & intranets that deliver.

I help my clients manage, optimise, and tweak their web presences, digital communication and intranets.

As a consultant I require a broad range of knowledge - from strategy and user experience at one end, to some pretty technical stuff at the other. This broad range of expertise is what enables me to cross silos and help make activities succeed.

* Web management,
* benchmarking & analysis
* advice & support when purchasing digital services (beställarstöd)
* optimisation (performance, search, usability/UX),
* eye tracking usability testing,
* web/intranet project management,
* Google Analytics.

Include me in your next digital project, hire me as an independent advisor, book me to coach your webmaster or webteam, or call me in to discuss your tactics.

Specialties: web/digital communication & marketing, tactics, strategy, web management, web project management, webmaster, webbansvarig, user experience, usability, eye tracking, web standards, optimisation, benchmarking, search engine optimisation/SEO, web site/intranet management, collaboration, social intranet, requirements, coaching, analytics, fluent in Swedish.
