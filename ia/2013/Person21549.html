Angela Hill, Resume Writer's Summary

angelah@resumeworld.ca. Looking for professional feedback on your resume? You may call (416.438.3606) or email me your resume for a free critique...turn around time usually one to two business days.

I am a seasoned Career Professional and Resume Writing Expert with 15 years experience delivering exceptional marketing tools (resumes, cover letters, portfolios, job search strategies, interview coaching) to help clients win interviews, secure the job and grow their careers.

As a Senior Resume Writer and Career Consultant at Resume World, I create compelling resumes for clients daily across various industries, including Accounting/Finance, Information Technology, Healthcare, Social Service, Nursing, Banking, Pharmaceutical, Manufacturing, Logistics, Software, Human Resources, Hospitality, Telecommunications, Communications, Media/Publishing, Retail, Aerospace, Engineering and Transportation. I have contributed to the career success of thousands of clients, earning the Top Resume Writer Award of the Year for three consecutive years.

Together with other senior resume writers and career strategists, I have co-authored several resume book products, including a 350 page resume writing certification program—the first of its find in Canada.
