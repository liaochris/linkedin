Michael Manesh's Summary

Passionate iOS developer with a background in design. Obsessed with making user interfaces be intuitive and animate at 60fps. Wants every pixel in its place, but knows how to prioritize the big stuff in order to ship. Great on large teams or as a solo act.

### Recent achievements

- Winner of the audience choice award for physics-based defense game Gravity Bomb at the MoDevTablet Hackathon. Winner of the best fundraising app award at the Revolt DC Hackathon for Soapbox, a "Match.com for finding politicians you agree with".

- Designed, developed and released Instabrowser on the App Store. The app has a 5-star average rating with review subjects ranging from "One browser to rule them all" to "Rapide et efficace" (Rapid and Efficient).

- Gave a talk on design for developers at a CocoaheadsDC meetup, showing easy ways to add a touch of design to an app without outside help.

### Skills

- Over 3 years of iOS development experience.

- Built and worked on several apps that communicate with JSON/XML based web services.

- Excellent user interface programming skills, including knowledge of advanced 3D-animation techniques using UIKit and Core Animation.

- Expert at provisioning, with multiple apps published in the App Store & experience managing enterprise applications.
