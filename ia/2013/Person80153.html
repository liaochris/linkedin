Leonardo Morejon's Summary

I'm a Social Media marketing professional from the greater New York City area with over 7 years of experience. I started my career at JWT’s Emerging Media department, and I’m currently with 360i, where my extensive experience in strategic development, management (of personnel and resources) and campaign execution is used to bring client work and internal processes to a new level. My philosophy is to always focus on ROI and not the fluff usually seen in most campaigns.

In addition to Social Media, I have experience as a project manager, web developer, graphic designer, copywriter and domainer. Basically, I live and breathe all things web and tech.
