Mark Baldwin's Summary

* 20+ years in Game Design and Development including designer of Empire, Game of the Year. Developer of over 30 games. Founder of White Wolf Productions (CEO) , Quantum Quality Productions (VP) and Baldwin Consulting (Owner).
* Consultant and Expert Witness to computer and video game industry as well as other related industries including healthcare, gambling, and the US military on software product design and development, and project management.
* Associate Professor and Visiting Professor in Game Design and Development, University of Advancing Technology and DeVry University teaching both graduate and undergraduate curriculums.
* Aerospace Engineer and Flight Designer for Space Shuttle, McDonnell Douglas Aeronautics.
