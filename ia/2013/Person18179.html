Jeff Brown's Summary

I am the host and creator of the Read to Lead Podcast, a top-ranked business and career podcast in iTunes (Top #3 Career / Top #10 Business - 9/13).

In its second week, the Read to Lead Podcast broke its way into the Top 3 of all Career podcasts, just behind the enormously successful Entrepreneur on Fire and Dave Ramsey's EntreLeadership Podcast. It has also cracked the Top 10 in the over all Business category. More info is available at http://readtoleadpodcast.com.

I have won two recent awards for my presentation work. The first came from Haiku Deck in May 2013. More can be found here: http://blog.haikudeck.com/announcing-our-mothers-day-contest-winners/

In July 2013, my presentation shown below titled Affordable Mobile Apps for Small Business, was voted by fans of Prezi (the in-the-cloud software used to create the presentation) as a #bestofprezi winner. More can be found here: https://prezi.com/explore/bestofprezi-submissions/

Until June 2013, I served as 88.7 WAY-FM Nashville's Operations Director, a role I was promoted to in January 2005. During my 14-plus years with the organization, the Nashville operation enjoyed the distinction of being the highest rated station within the company. In April 2013, we experienced the highest weekly cume (total weekly audience) in our 21-year history (129,300). Also, WAY-FM Nashville was rated as the country's 24th most-listened to non-profit Christian station during my tenure. Our weekly cume consistently hovered between 100,000 and 120,000.

In 2010 I founded Brown Knows Media, LLC as a mobile app development firm specializing in affordable and simple to maintain mobile apps for small businesses and non-profits. Recently, we expanded our offerings to include web design, presentation design and platform building and coaching.

I also serve as organizer of the Franklin, TN chapter of Geek Breakfast, a monthly gathering of tech- and social media-minded individuals in and around the Franklin and Spring Hill, TN areas.

Jeff Brown's Experience

Creator and Host

The Read to Lead Podcast

April 2013 – Present (9 months) Greater Nashville Area

The Read to Lead Podcast is a Top 3 Career podcast in iTunes.

We believe in the mantra, “Leaders read and Readers lead.” Our desire is to encourage and challenge today’s leaders, spurring them on to more intentional and consistent reading. At the same time, we want to help aspiring leaders realize the important role intentional reading can play in their development as successful leaders.

The Read to Lead Podcast includes exciting conversations with today’s most successful and inspiring non-fiction authors. Our guests are all recognized authorities on topics such as Leadership, Personal Development, Entrepreneurship and Business, and have included New York Times best-selling authors like Dan Miller, Jay Baer and Jonah Berger.

In less than one month after it's debut, the Read to Lead Podcast ranked #3 among all Career podcasts and has cracked the Top 10 in the over all Business category.

Founder

Brown Knows Media, LLC

September 2010 – Present (3 years 4 months) Greater Nashville Area

I founded Brown Knows Media in an effort to bring affordable and feature-rich mobile applications to small-businesses and non-profits, something heretofore out of reach.

It's engaging and very easy to use. But more than that, it's easy to maintain. We're very grateful to Brown Knows Media for this invaluable tool!

We are also currently accepting web and presentation design clients and a limited number of coaching clients.

Operations Director

WAY-FM Media Group, Inc.

Nonprofit; 51-200 employees; Broadcast Media industry

January 2005 – June 2013 (8 years 6 months) Greater Nashville Area

I helped steer WAYM / Nashville to 4 of its 5 highest cume ratings in its 21 year history. In April 2013, we enjoyed our highest ever weekly cume rating (129K). In September 2012 we saw our 5th highest (111K). In November 2012, we experienced our fourth highest rating (113K), and in April of last year, we saw our second highest ever rating (123K).

Additionally, I oversaw the daily operations of the middle Tennessee region of WAY-FM stations including WAYM/Nashville, WAYQ/Clarksville, WAYD/Bowling Green, and WAYW/New Johnsonville, as well as translators in Rome, GA, Montgomery, AL and Cookeville, TN.

My day-to-day responsibilities included copy writing, voice over, production, social media marketing, digital and social media strategy implementation and traditional marketing and execution. I also oversaw all marketing aspects of WAY-FM concerts and events including ticketing, outdoor, print, TV and radio. In addition, I implemented and oversaw all on-site promotions and station marketing at all events.

I am an accomplished fundraiser, having led nearly 30 pledge drives with an overall average of over 90% of goal. I am also credited with the idea for bringing multiple WAY-FM stations together for unified pledge drives (now known as Super Pledge Drive). I envisioned this as a way for multiple stations to tap into our best communicators while saving station's thousands of dollars in travel and prize expenses.

I was promoted four times - to production director in November 2000, to network morning show host in June 2002, to lead announcer in 2003 and finally to program director (later called operations director) in January 2005.

I successfully managed as many as 15 employees while at WAY-FM.

I humbly served as the Nashville Cluster's 2nd in command for 8 years.

I have the distinction of being tapped by WAY-FM president Bob Augsburg to help communicate, in presentation form (below), the company's new 2020 Vision to major donors and ministries worldwide.
