Anab Jain's Summary

I am a designer, entrepreneur, speculator, storyteller, with over eight years of experience in both corporate and educational organisations, as well as in research labs and think-tanks. I spend my time between London and India.

Some of my key projects have been with Microsoft Research Cambridge, Nokia Design, Design Council, CBS Products, Mattel, Helen Hamlyn Centre, Unwired Ventures, EPSRC and, Surabhi Foundation.

I have spoken at conferences such as WCIT2010, PICNIC, LIFT, SIGGRAPH, EPIC, Worktech, DesignEngaged, Interesting09, Interactivos. I have also been a guest lecturer and tutor at several design schools such as the RCA London, CIID, Copenhagen, ECA Edinburgh and Design School, Dundee, VCU Qatar.

My work's been exhibited at MoMA, New York, London Design Festival and Tate Modern amongst others.

Specialties

Design Strategy, Interaction and Service Design, Design Futures and Foresight, Storytelling and Communication, Public Engagement.
