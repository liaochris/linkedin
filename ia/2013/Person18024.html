Britton Cunningham's Summary

Why Hello! I'm Britton “Like the Country”. I know it’s not actually like the country, but I continues to flaunt the tagline. Even though I'm not British in any way, I have a slight obsession with the western European nation and its iconic Union Jack. The only region I love more is the great state of Texas, my beloved home state.

I've explored some interesting territories in my life (figuratively and literally). In the summer of 2012, I traveled halfway around the world to speak at the International Media Conference in Seoul, South Korea. I was invited to give a presentation on Millennials and news, how to engage with them. I also managed social media for the entire conference. Back in the day (which was a Wednesday), I decided to claim new territory in the Midwest. I started with my conquest of the number one Journalism school in the world at the University of Missouri back in the fall of 2009. For my brief 3 and half year stay, I managed to TA three different classes, work as the Copywriter on the TGI Friday's and United States Fish and Wildlife Service accounts for MOJO Ad, and run homecoming for my sorority twice (I would also do it all over again in a heartbeat). Most recently, I traveled two hours west to the greater Kansas City metropolis to work as the Research Analyst for PlattForm Advertising. I'm now developing consumer profiles, analyzing PRIZM and MRI data, and creating both environmental and competitive analyses.

Specialties: Adobe Creative Suite Master, Consumer Profiling Aficionado, Microsoft Office (especially Excel) Guru, MRI Wizard, and lastly, Tap Dancer and Genuine Entertainer.
