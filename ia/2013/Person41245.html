Through this hands-on learning approach, participants complete sales skills training and gain valuable insight into our leading products, customer segments, sales strategy, merchandising approach and operations before being assigned to a U.S. sales territory as a Sales Development Representative (SDR).

Once in a territory, SDRs are the primary face of Whirlpool Corporation and our many iconic brands. They also work with national accounts and key independent trade partners to build relationships and ensure sales targets are achieved, all while identifying and analyzing performance trends.
