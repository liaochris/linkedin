Joe McCann's Summary

Joe McCann is a thirty five-year hotel industry veteran who has held senior management positions with Hilton and Marriott.During his hotel brokerage career, he has closed more than $700 Million in hotel asset transactions.With this impressive track record of hotel closings, Optimum Hotel Brokerage firm has become one of the leading hotel brokerage companies in the United States. The customers and clients he has served during his hotel brokerage career comprise a veritable Who's Who in hotel investment, including Hilton Hotels Corporation, Accor, Blackstone, Felcor Lodging Trust and Thor Equities.

His customer base ranges from individual property owners to regional management companies to domestic hedge funds to international real estate ownership companies.

Joe holds a B.S. Degree in Hospitality Management from the Pennsylvania State University and a Master of Hospitality Management degree from the Conrad N. Hilton College of Hotel and Restaurant Management.

His industry service experience includes adjunct professor and guest lecturer at major college hospitality management programs, including the Conrad N. Hilton College, Penn State, and New York University.

Optimum Hotel Brokerage has established a Trustee Endowed Scholarship at Penn State.The scholarship is granted to financially deserving students at Penn State's School of Hospitality Management.The McCann Family Charitable Fund also funds annual scholarships for academically deserving students in both Hotel Real Estate Finance and Hospitality Entrepreneurship at Penn State.The McCann Family Charitable Fund also supports an endowed scholarship for graduate students at the Conrad N. Hilton College of Hotel and Restaurant Management.

Joe is a licensed Real Estate Broker, has earned the Certified Hotel Broker designation with Hotel Brokers International, and has been a contributing editor for Hotel Journal magazine.
