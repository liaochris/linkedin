Jamie Readon's Summary

I'm an Award winning advertising Creative who specialises in brand building, brand experience, social and digital content creation and experiential and ambient media.

For me, being a creative is about brand building, building through experience, content and narrative.

Over the years I have become a strong advocate and champion of transmedia story telling. Simply put, this is the technique of telling a single story or story experience across multiple platforms and formats using current digital technologies.

It involves creating content that engages an audience using various techniques to permeate their daily lives. As a creative, this allows us to develop stories across multiple forms of media in order to deliver unique pieces of content in each channel.

I've outlined a few areas that i feel are incredibly important to being a successful creative.

On Brands:

Brand building is what we do. And this is constantly at the forefront of my thinking. I don’t just provide ideas for what is asked, I go that extra mile and deliver intriguing user experiences and build the brands I work on through intelligent creative thinking.

On Media:

I am at that stage in my career where I no longer view media as different channels but as avenues that come together in transmedia story telling. At the end of the day a strong intelligent creative idea fits across all media. My thinking is never confined to just one medium, it is fully integrated into telling a story through all channels.

On Mentoring:

I’m very lucky to be able to work in an industry with a lot of very talented creatives and one of the best parts of this is being able to teach, guide and offer them support. I feel I’m a very approachable creative and always happy to help out and get my hands dirty where needed. I think it’s important to lead by example and hopefully I have helped my colleagues develop in an ever-changing industry.
