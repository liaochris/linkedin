Kathleen Jennison's Summary

Get the polished look of a professionally designed room and minimize all those design mishaps by allowing me to use my skills, resources and design artistry to give you the space that will thrill and delight you.

As your Interior Designer, my goal is to incorporate classic principles of scale, proportion, color, and quality into every space I design. I work together with you to design a space that develops your own unique style and taste, all the while understanding the importance of having a final design solution that ensures both beauty and function.

I'll give you everything you need to accomplish your designing needs, whether you prefer to do it yourself or if you need help at every step of the process, you decide.

I specialize in design for residential, commercial, and hospitality spaces where I help people create an environment with resources and materials appropriate for their needs and budget.

As a full service interior designer, I utilize a project management approach providing plans with complete interior design specifications for both new and existing properties.
