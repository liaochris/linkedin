Fritz Solares' Summary

Hi! I design and develop games, and am looking to get involved in projects where the emphasis is on fun, play and creativity.

Alongside freelance consultancy work I run Zut Games, a small independent studio dedicated to creating games with simple, fun mechanics and retro aesthetics.

I'm also particularly interested in the potential of games for purposes that are not purely entertainment - e.g. games for education, rehabilitation and 'serious' games.

I'm currently studying for an MSc in Evolutionary & Adaptive Systems at Sussex.
