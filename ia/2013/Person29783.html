Daniel Matalon's Summary

I am a Social TV Producer, Digital Content Developer and a Business Transformation Analyst.

My career in television arose after years of marketing, business and technology consulting in which my talents were often called on for transformative projects involving significant organizational change. This led to a treatment for a TV series based on my work and, eventually, to my founding an entire network called iDiscovery TV launched on the back of 9 shows, 120 hours of original content, a social video discovery platform and a production incubator program. The company went from zero to $20 Million valuation in only 11 months.

I now produce and publish a variety of digital content around both established and rising thought leaders, writers and performers helping them to groom their intellectual property over a variety of media and building audiences for both episodic and event-based television.

As a speaker and a trainer with 600+ live presentations to audiences of up to 10,000 my training includes conflict resolution, presentation skills, team building and more. Authored the Starship Training for Entrepreneurs recognized for its blend of theater, gamesmanship and practical strategy and produced LifeGames with Ken Windes. Worked with ELI where I was a facilitator-trainee under Robert Kiyosaki, author of Rich Dad, Poor Dad.

1st foray into eMarketing and custom software development was building one of the Internet's 1st affiliate marketing and sales force automation programs delivering web-generated leads to Nokia text message equipped cell phones in 1997. I also co-authored a web-based eMarketing training program that sold 1000's of copies throughout 1998-2000.

As co Founder of 1-800-GeeksOnTime, I was key architect behind its award winning service management model producing unique innovations in nationwide customer service. GOT won Best Customer Service nominations 2005, 2006 from American Business Awards (Stevies) under my management.

Specialties

Communications, IPTV, New Media, Business Dev., Strategic Alliance Building, Corporate Training, Communications, Online Marketing, Domain Investment/Management, Business Model Design, Software Architecture Design, Voice and Data Technology and Systems Planning, Sales Training, Conflict Resolution and Facilitation, eMarketing Measurement and Metrics, Social Media Management and Monitoring, Helpdesk and Service Center Management, Project Management, Business Analysis, Training for MSP companies
