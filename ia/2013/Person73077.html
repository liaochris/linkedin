Katie Gailes' Summary

SmartMoves International is an entrepreneurship coaching and small business training firm that specializes in marketing and strategic planning. The most important deliverable is a simple, elegant and executable strategy that the client is excited about.

My clients can work with me one-on-one or in group workshops and seminars.

I am also a startup counselor at Growing America Through Entrepreneurship (GATE), a program that provides coaching scholarships and training assistance to unemployed workers who want to create their own jobs through business ownership. I enjoy using my skills and experiences to help these people start businesses from scratch.

Most recently, I became a member of Solavei, the very first social commerce company, to help my clients and associates to save money on things that they use everyday....starting with mobile phone service.

Innovation and creativity is the trademark of my business. My clients get my best creative energy 100% of the time.

Specialties: Business StartUp Planning, Marketing Strategy and Planning, Marketing Messaging, Marketing Program Planning and Management

Katie Gailes' Projects

  • Leadership Triangle Alumni Network

    • October 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Katie Gailes, Jasmine Bennett

    There was an alumni association in place at Leadership Triangle for years prior to my joining the company however, there was no benefit to being a member. I revamped the alumni network into an entity of the organization that extends and enhances the value of Goodmon Fellows’ investment in their Leadership Triangle experience. Through ongoing networking opportunities, the Network helps Goodmon Fellows maintain the connections with their classmates and create new connections with other Leadership Triangle program graduates. Timely, relevant and carefully selected events and activities continue Goodmon Fellows’ leadership development while also expanding their regional knowledge and perspective. All Goodmon Fellows have access to LTAN events for a fee (usually $20 per event), and alumni network membership has its privileges. Paid members ($100 per year) will be able to attend all events free, except the Goodmon Awards, which will be discounted. A full calendar of high quality events and activities will be available in various forms such as community service events (which are open to their family members as well), after hours events, luncheons and celebrations.
