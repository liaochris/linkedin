Sue Garred's Experience

Owner and Creative Director

Garred Design, Inc

September 1997 – Present (16 years 1 month)

Garred Design, Inc. (www.garreddesign.com) provides professional graphic design, copywriting and marketing solutions for businesses large or small. With more than 20 years of experience, Garred Design is known for its creative, hard-hitting and cost-effective design. Great design and a keen marketing sense combine to create attention-getting, targeted solutions that help clients get the results they want. Depending upon a client’s needs, we can provide art direction, graphic design, writing, website design and development, and coordinate media planning, placement, printing and photography.
