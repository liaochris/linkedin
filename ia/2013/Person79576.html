I am an academic librarian with a background in journalism.

At Capilano University Library, I am responsible for the development and management of online resources. I also liaise with the Schools of Business, Applied Business, Communication, and Public Administration, provide reference services and instruction, and provide copyright and licensing support.

Specialties: advanced information retrieval, academic databases, business/market research, professional writing, information literacy instruction, copyright & licensing

Leanna Jantzi's Projects

  • Juried paper | ALISE 2013 | Becoming a professional: a case for community service learning in LIS education

    • January 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Leanna Jantzi, Luanne Freund, Samantha Sinanan, Heather O'Brien

    A study of the learning process of LIS students engaged in a volunteer
    community service learning program was conducted using “shift notes” –
    a structured diary-like instrument for data collection. The study captures
    aspects of socialization and the process of becoming professionals that are
    such essential, but challenging, aspects of LIS education
