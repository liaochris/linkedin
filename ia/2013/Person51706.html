Rob Harkey's Summary

www.turnkeyorganizing.com
On Facebook, LIKE TurnKey Organizing at https://www.facebook.com/turnKey

As a Home Organizing Professional and owner of TurnKey Organizing I assist residential clients in realizing their personal goals by freeing up valuable time with an organized and thoughtful home.

TurnKey Organizing provides three valuable services:
1. One-on-one residential organizing and maintenance
2. Team based Unpack and "Put-away" relocation services
3. Related handyman services

Working individually with clients we help them with prioritizing possessions, downsizing and organizing their residential spaces based on their needs, priorities and vision. We love to help you organize any space that's getting in the way of you living your life.

For individuals relocating we can offer a team of thoughtful Professional Organizers who unpack and store items in an organized manner based on your needs. Our unpack service makes your move less stressful and the envy of your family and friends.

As a compliment to our organizing services, Turnkey Organizing as the name states, can install shelving and other organizing solutions. We also provide picture hanging services to make your new house feel like a home.

It's fulfilling to work hands-on with clients to complete a solution that allows them to free time and realize their life goals.
