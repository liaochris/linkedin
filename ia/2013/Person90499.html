Symbolics

Managed corporate R&D, all video graphics hardware and 2D/3D animation software development ("S-Products"), documentation, training, and support activities. Responsibilities also included systems design and deployment for National Reconnaissance Office and associated US Gov't agencies.

Later: V.P. of Engineering for entire company, including extension of Symbolics' "S-Products" 2D and 3D Paint & Animation System and "S-Products" software port to SGI platforms.

Interfaced closely with Japanese buyer Nichimen to seamlessly execute the final phase of the product plan and business transition from Triple-I to Del Rey Graphics including reassignment of essential development personnel to Nichimen employment under Del Rey.
