Clare Rodway's Summary

I am MD and founder of Kysen PR. We provide professional firms with high quality public relations expertise. Our reputation is based on a commitment to deliver on our promises.

The professions are currently facing a number of challenges. Whether it is the worldwide economic climate, the introduction of Alternative Business Structures or the European Commission's proposed audit reforms, our views on these and other issues need to be communicated more precisely and rapidly than ever before to compete effectively.
