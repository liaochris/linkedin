Jesse Albert's Summary

Actively produce for TV, Film & Digital. Consult regarding digital & branded entertainment strategy, deal structure and content opportunities to variety of studios, creative agencies, start ups and producers.

Leading strategist w/15+ years experience on the front lines of new media and Hollywood. Early adopter and creative thought leader in uses of traditional and digital media to create and extend fan engagement. Experience creating & implementing innovative multi-platform/transmedia strategy on behalf of world leading artists, brands & IP.

Partner w/best of breed to create activations that distill cutting edge theory into mass consumer adoption.

ACCOMPLISHMENTS:

Helped client Chill.com successfully launch a direct to consumer video platform and EP several comedy specials, including Maria Bamford, to rave reviews in LA & NY Times, New Yorker, Rolling Stone, etc.

Chaired Banff World Media Festival, Branded Ent and New Media programing, launching new Branded Ent. day of programming.

Speak on digital, transmedia, branded & "Hollywood" content opportunities globally.

Produced 1st ever “American Idol: Songwriter” as part of Season 6 of TV series. “Songwriter” was a fully integrated online component of the TV series, featuring in-show calls to action resulting in online selection by TV viewers of “Finale” song “This Is My Now”. Winning song was revealed and sung live on show’s TV Finale with a studio recorded version released for sale the following day.

Initiated and managed iTunes relationship & digital music sales & promo strategy for Season 06 American Idol, achieving Billboard chart positions and generating $1,000,000 + sales of recordings.

Consult for eOne regarding the studio's digital strategy.

Currently Exec Prod on 2 x 1-hour scripted dramas (TV) & a HULU Exclusive original series.

Packaged web series "The Bannen Way" & "Backwash" [w/ Josh Malina, Sarah Silverman, Hank Azaria, John Hamm].

Active in developing new financial and distribution models.

Specialties

As a former agent, I have well established relationships across film, television, digital and branded entertainment echo systems and can leverage those relationships and expertise on behalf of consulting clients and producing partners.
