Evan Rosenbaum's Summary

Evan Rosenbaum is a student at the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania and is founder of EKR. Evan is a member of the Wharton Venture Initiation Program, which is an educational incubator program, and winner of the prestigious Wharton Venture Award. Evan was named on a list of the Smartest People in Philadelphia by Philadelphia Magazine.

At age 14, Evan founded iGiki, which released the world’s first 3rd party iPhone web applications before the iPhone was released. His iGiki games were featured in PC World, Chicago Tribune, Wired, Newsday, and Mac|Life. Evan also released the first iPhone finger stylus.
