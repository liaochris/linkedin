Eric Lent's Summary

Eric Lent is Chief Marketing Officer at Herschend Family Entertainment Corporation (HFE). HFE is the largest family owned theme park corporation in the U.S. The company creates, develops and operates entertainment, tourism and hospitality properties spanning 26 locations in ten states.

Lent joined HFE in 2011 with the challenge to grow and extend individual property brands, ensure an outstanding guest experience and ultimately help deliver sales and profit goals. He is responsible for all aspects of marketing operations nationally. His organization oversees marketing strategy, consumer insights and analytics, marketing communications, publicity and promotions, digital and social media and strategic alliance marketing.

Previously, he spent seven years with The Hershey Company leading marketing and operational teams. Most recently he served as their vice president of Global Marketing Excellence and Strategy. Part of the leadership team that transformed Hershey into a top performing company, Lent helped drive record quarterly earnings, top-line growth, the leading share position in the candy, mint and gum category in the United States, and top-tier shareholder return in the consumer packaged-goods sector.

Prior to The Hershey Company, he served as a director with Eastman Kodak and brand manager at S.C. Johnson. With a strong track record with global brands, he has won numerous awards for effective marketing and advertising, including the Golden Marble, the Gold Effie and the Addy.

Outside of work, Lent likes to spend time with his wife and three sons. He volunteers for Compassion International and recently served as Board Chairman for the Towson University College of Business and Economics. In 2008, he received the Dean’s Recognition Award from Towson University for his lifetime of career successes.
