Lenka Zintel's Summary

Fiftheen years of experience in strategic and operational management of business units and project organizations with 5-20 staff members in IT consulting firms. As a project manager, I have succesfully initiated and delivered +30 business priority projects ranging from 500-5000 man-hours to global and national clients within the private and the public sector.

My goal is to create real and measurable business value through the help of IT and change. I have the vision to anticipate customer needs and I proactively seek out new opportunities, develop innovative solutions and advise clients on how to align business goals with IT solutions to drive competitive advantage and bottom-line gains.

I have established 5 business units exceeding sales budgets, revenue and profitability expectations considerably. I have also driven the business and organizational turn-around of 2 existing business units and project organizations. As a transformational leader, I see what can be achieved and I have the ability and drive to make it happen.

Business partners point out my integrity, passion, and my excellent communication skills that allow me to communicate effectively with business teams and technical teams and build strong relationships.

Working as a manager of teams across cultures I know how to ignite very diverse groups of people to excel together by inspiring trust and establishing a clear vision that gives direction to my team.

Specialties:

- IT business analysis (pre-analysis, business architecture, BPM, business case, business impact analysis)
- Sales and key account management
- Business development (business strategy, innovation, product development, organizational development)
