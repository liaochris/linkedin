Jacqui Higgins' Summary

Accents by Design is an acclaimed interiors and art consulting firm that offers services supporting commercial and high-end residential projects.

As Founder, Jacqui Higgins has demonstrated expertise as an entrepreneur and as an interior designer, marketing and business development professional. Throughout her career she has achieved great success in marketing and conveying the business value of services. Jacqui is a consummate public speaker delivering thought leadership and is also a published author of white papers and articles on design trends and sustainability. She works very closely with the Commercial and Residential Brokerage community.

Jacqui has provided consulting services to help organizations define effective marketing and sales strategies. Additionally, Jacqui has a proven track record in business development across various industry sectors.

Specialties: Business Development, Interior Design, Marketing, Strategic Planning and Implementation, Public Speaking, Branding, Relationship Building, Communications, Capture Management, Proposal Preparation and Management, Client Focused Events
