Jeff Van Zandt's Summary

Jeff Van Zandt (VZ): CW/CD

The Work: www.vzcreative.com

VZ has written Geico’s iAd starring the Gecko, co-written Seinfeld alumni Jason Alexander’s and Star Wars alumni Carrie Fisher’s video blogs for their Jenny Craig campaigns, ideated guerilla promotion for the Discovery Channel’s Shark Week, launched Apple’s iPad 3 with Cinsay in Mexico and written several hundred web videos as well as broadcast and radio spots for everyone. You see, truth be told, VZ has lived in 17 US States and 7 Countries and has written to every audience on Earth except for
a few of the lost tribal groups in West Papua, New Guinea.

VZ has also had the opportunity to work with some of the most creative Ad Agencies on some of the biggest brands on Earth. Speaking of which, while he was the Senior Writer on Nike at The Jupiter Drawing Room in South Africa, VZ actually had the pleasure of following in his father’s FBI Hostage Negotiator footsteps, when he talked an Art Director into giving up a .38 he pulled on an Intern at a company party. (Art Directors = Danger) VZ’z not afraid of danger though, because not only has he survived illegally whitewater rafting on the Zambezi River through 15 foot waves during high water season in Zimbabwe, he’s also made it through being the late, great Michael Jackson’s bodyguard
for a day at Disney World. (VZ’z pretty much Invincible)

VZ Creative specializes in creating broadcast, films, webisodes, interactive, games, music and real-life events that educate and entertain consumers in order to keep their attention long enough to market a product or service. VZ Creative authors strategic advertising messages that are so relevant and engaging to consumers, that they’re impossible to ignore resulting in enhanced company and product images, improved retail presence, better consumer relationships, and a powerful boost in sales. With VZ Creative both brands and consumers win.

VZ would LOVE the opportunity to work with you on all your Creative adventures.
