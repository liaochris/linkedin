Colleen Lin, CUA's Summary

My current work focuses on enterprise-wide content strategy and IA projects, working closely with international visual design teams and the UX team in an Agile environment to provide the finest user experience possible.

An information architect and content strategist, I sort through clutter, and make connections to get to the best solution for design and interaction problems. I’m committed to serving users while balancing business and organizational priorities.

As a Certified Usability Analyst (CUA), I focus on user-centered interactive design practices to create rich, engaging online experiences, regardless of the role I take on in any given project.

Previous professional projects have included visual design for the Web, content strategy and development, social media marketing, photography, and motion graphics. In addition to several years of interactive design work, my experience extends to print design and illustration, including a popular series of science-themed children’s posters.

With 12 years professional experience, including operating my own boutique agency, I feel very comfortable taking on a leadership role. A consensus-builder by nature, I seek outside input and collaboration in any project.
