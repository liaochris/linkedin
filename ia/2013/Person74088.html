Kemya Scott's Summary

Experienced in all facets of marketing and administration for small and medium sized businesses. If you're looking for a marketing strategy for your business, look no further!

Marketing Strategist:

I develop marketing strategies for small businesses and solopreneurs. Whether you need a full-scale marketing plan for your company, or you simply need a marketing consultant, I can fit your needs. I can perform event marketing and PR services as well.

Can't afford to hire a full-time marketing staff? I am a Virtual Marketing Manager!

Social Media Marketing:

I use social media platforms to engage your business with your current and future clients. I will help your company explore the best social media platforms to reach your audience, and keep you informed of all the changes and updates taking place in the digital world.

Social Media Manager:

Managing your firm's brand is an important component of using social media. Many firms don't have the time or means to manage their own social media accounts. I will manage your accounts for you and engage with your followers. I work directly with your firm to define the social media brand and ensure we are representing your firm appropriately.

Blogger & Content Marketing:

Does your company want to start a blog, but you don't know what to talk about? I will create your editorial calendar, based upon relevant topics for your firm and your industry. I can also write and edit your blog content. Blogging is a social media platform, and your blog is a two-way conversation with your audience. We want your blogging voice to be professional while at the same time be social and inviting.

Specialties

Strategic Marketing and Business Development: business development, blogging, content marketing, marketing strategy, event marketing, social media management, social media marketing, proposal writing, market research, business planning, and promotional campaigns.
