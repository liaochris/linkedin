Bruno Vincent's Summary

I am the CEO and founder of Atlantis, an IT consulting company that specializes in architecture, production and security.

Our valued customers include Société Générale, Crédit Agricole CIB, Voyages-SNCF.com, Agence France Presse, Assemblée des Chambres Françaises de Commerce et d'Industrie, plus many more large and medium organizations.

You can learn more about Atlantis at: http://www.atlantis.fr/en/

I also teach IT architecture at ENSTA - Ecole Nationale Supérieure de Techniques Avancées, Paris, France.

Lastly, I am the co-writer of "Gestion des Identités : Une Politique pour le Système d'Information" (ISBN 978-2-9525895-1-2), a French book about Identity and Access Management.
