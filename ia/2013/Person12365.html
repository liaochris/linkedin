Edward Hamilton's Summary

Life´s little adventure is my passion. I love where it takes you and the people you meet. I live with an adventurous spirit and positive attitude and that led me to Barcelona and into my line of work as a business coach.

At Green Bean (small business growth specialists) we combine the power of coaching with the importance of good business knowledge.

Why business coaching? I have been starting and investing in businesses since 2000 and feel most at home with the entrepreneurial spirit of small business ownership. Also, having played sports at an international level I know the importance of coaching, captaincy and self-discipline. Meanwhile, being educated in finance and business management and working internationally in business it became apparent that one needs both skill sets (the business acumen and discipline) to reach our potential in today`s competitive environment. Coaching seemed a natural fit to my skill sets and passion.

I hope to collaborate with others so we all can keep pushing towards our goals.
