Deputy Sheriff

Collier County Sheriff's Office

Government Agency; 1001-5000 employees; Law Enforcement industry

Served my community well for the purpose of protecting the public (life & property), with integrity and protecting the dignity of the Sheriff's Office with every task and assignment I was given. I had a successful and fulfilling career in law enforcement, which actually began in 1974 as a police officer with Ft. Myers Police Department, Ft.Myers, Fl. Over the years, I had many posts. I worked many years in road patrol both as an officer and also in the role of senior man or supervisor. I was on the Sheriff's full-time S.W.A.T team earning the nickname; "the tree," go figure, right? I worked in the Criminal Investigations Division of the Sheriff's Office handling cases of crimes against property, domestic violence, child crimes and homicide. I also enjoyed working as a courtroom Bailiff, providing safety and security for the couthouse, the judges and staff, and preserved the preservation of safety in the courtroom during trial proceedings.
