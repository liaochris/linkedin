Jan Brassem's Summary

Jan W. Brassem has more than 25-years of executive experience, in leading a variety of silver/gold jewelry and luxury product firms through challenging mergers & acquisitions, competitive and marketing environments.

As Co-Founder and Senior Partner of MainBrace Partners, a nine-member consulting "teaming" firm, (offices in New York, Connecticut with networks in Mumbai, Amsterdam and Hong Kong), he has advised companies, such as Kingdom of Thailand, Media Network of Canada, Korean Agency, Mark Twain House, WatchWorks, private equity firms, real estate ventures among others. Currently, he is working with investers to acquire a jewelry division from a private equity firm and is assisting a Vietnamese luxury product firm penetrate the US.

Previously, Brassem launched Eclipse(tm), a pioneering silver jewelry and watch brand containing a ground-breaking feature allowing designs to change it's look, style or character with little addiitional cost. The brand grew through a multi-channel distribution strategy: shop-at-home ‘parties’, home shopping TV networks, (see HSN pix below), catalogs and web-based retailers.

He is skilled at ‘turning-around’ consumer product firms guiding them to growth and profitability. He was recruited to turn-around a limping $20 million, 200-employee and multi-plant global consumer brand leader. The firm returned to profitability 18-months after he implemented his initiatives.

Earlier, via a three-phase leveraged acquisition, (LBO), he purchased, for his own account, one of the largest jewelry manufacturers in the US, (at that time), marketing and selling private-label diamond and stone jewelry to classic retail channels.

Brassem has worked with numerous US, Asian, Russian and European, (he speaks Dutch), firms helping them develop their global strategies. He has assisted firms in finding more profitable sources of supply; designed market strategies and helped find merger and acquisition candidates.

Specialties

Brassem is, arguably, one of the most knowledgeable executives in the global luxury arena. He is the 'go-to" executive for....

* Product sourcing,
* Cross border luxury product M&A, and
* Designing global retail growth strategies.

In addition, he is..

* A widely read columnist, ('thought-leader'), on luxury consumer product issues.
* A blogger read by over one hundred and eighty-thousand consumers.
* A former contributer to FOX Business Network.
