Jay McDowell's Summary

I started my Coaching business more than six years ago, leaving a highly successful career as an EVP of Logistics and Supply Chain for the nations leading home healthcare provider. I have become one of the top coaches in Southern California by delivering proven results in every aspect of running a business …including revenue & profit growth, cost management, advertising and building high performing teams.

My diverse experience from 30 years of “Corporate America” for fortune 100 companies, combined with my passion for coaching people, gives me the understanding of knowing the direct challenges and opportunities that exist for small, mid-size and large organizations in both urban and rural areas. I’m amazed at how many business owners think they have to work longer and harder than anyone on their team – it doesn’t have to be that way!

I am uniquely positioned to mentor Southern California business owners on the courage, faith and commitment it takes to be a business owner. Plus, as your coach, I will help you build an entrepreneurial knowledge base, and teach you successful strategies, both business and personal, that will improve the way you think, how you lead, and create short and long-term goals for your business and personal life.

Your first step is to schedule a 'Complimentary Coaching and Diagnostic Session' and share with me where you’ve been with your business, where your business is currently, and where you want it to go. From there, we can work on ideas to help you achieve the results that are most important to you. All you need to do is call (949) 713-2030 or click the link ‘Business Health Check’ to get started
