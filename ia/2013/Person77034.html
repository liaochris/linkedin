Kristi Bopp Harper's Summary

Kristi is a design strategist focused on social outreach, driven by technology and relationship building; bringing initiatives and communities together through multi-platforms. Business solutions and relationship management translate her big-picture thinking, storytelling, insights and ideas into something that can be communicated and executed.

By utilizing a wide range of new media techniques and online technology, Kristi connects design efforts to an organization's business strategy. This process forms a systematic approach integrating holistic-thinking and research methods used to inform business strategy and strategic planning, which provides a context for design.

With over 20 years in Creative Services and 5 years in Web Technology, she is the recipient of several local and national awards. Her contributions include a variety of organizations, such as HGTV, DIY, GAC, FOX, ABC, CBS, and United Way of Knoxville — developing interactive media, communication strategies, corporate initiatives, consulting, creative design, product integration and brand management campaigns.

Specialties

Kristi applies her experience with video production and digital media towards making connections. She researches the latest trends; promoting the adoption of a technology and dynamic content as means of communication to improve client relations and productivity.
