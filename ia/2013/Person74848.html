Kevin D. Kinsey MBA MFA's Summary

Kevin D. Kinsey is Founder, President & CEO of the Genesis Group International and Exodus Entertainment Studios. Kinsey has dual responsibilities of the day to day operations of an entertainment financing and distribution company in conjunction with the responsibilities of a development, financial advisory and marketing firm. After earning a B.A. in Political Science, this 1985 UCLA graduate manages and finances mergers & acquisitions for hard assets and syndicated projects and implements financial budgets and marketing plans under Genesis client relationships. Kinsey advised clients Dr. Kenneth Ulmer and the Faithful Central Bible Church in the purchase the Great Western Forum and served as Lead Negotiator and Advisor to Michael Jackson for the MJJ/Neverland/AVT Music Composite Debt Restructure. Under Exodus client relationships, Kinsey assists filmmakers, television producers and musical talent with obtaining creative financing and distribution for their projects. He has represented clients such as actress Halle Berry, director John Singleton, music artists Prince, Coolio, Bone Thugs & Harmony, members of the famous Jackson Family, and actor-comedian Eddie Griffen. Exodus has structured a placement of funds for films with budgets capitalized at $10 million for debt funding and distribution. Kinsey completed graduate studies at Golden Gate University Graduate School of Business Administration, the UCLA School of Film and Television, USC Special Honors Program and holds an dual MFA MBA in International Media from the Graduate School of Business Administration and Communication Arts at Loyola Marymount University (LMU/LA). Philanthropically, Kinsey has served and currently serves as a member to the LMU African American Alumni Association Board of Directors, the L.A. Metro Area United Negro College Fund Board of Directors, the African American Achievers Network Board of Directors and 20th Province Polemarch of the Western Province of Kappa Alpha Psi Fraternity.

Specialties

International Business Finance for Mergers & Acquisitions and Venture Capital, Chief Contract Negotiator and Lead Advisor for Syndicated Commercial Capital Projects in Media and Entertainment. Executive Producer and Producer of Films and Television Projects. Executive Director of Special Events and Promotions. Philanthropist of Scholarship Endowments and Community Service & Social Action Projects.
