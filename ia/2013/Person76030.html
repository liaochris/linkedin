Kirk McMillan's Summary

An experienced executive, owner, coach, consultant, facilitator and speaker with a proven ability to energize leadership/talent transformation, and stimulate change in business and people. After 20 years optimizing businesses and the leaders that run them, I am seeking opportunities to develop talent engagement for leaders and organizations. With a sole objective of focusing on talent engagement, I provide a non-judgmental business partner, energizing strengths, and optimizing value and competitiveness.

After completing my undergraduate work at The Citadel and an MBA at Kennesaw State University, I assumed leadership of a $2m family owned business at the age of 26. Over 13 years, grew the business to over $50 million. Since leaving, I've been coaching business owners, executives and next generation leaders, some half my age and some twice my age, from the CEO to front line employees. My path has led to multiple engagements, including the creation of a leadership development framework for a global organization, specifically focused on next generation leaders and business owners.

The SEE Me Quit/SEE Me Fit smoking cessation was inspired by my executive clients who wanted to quit smoking. Using Support in a small group environment, one-on-one coaching, Education and Exercise, the SEE Me Quit program is the most innovative exercise based program we can find.

Recently, I've accepted an associate coaching role with Artesian Transformational Leadership Ltd based in London, UK, as one of Artesian's U.S. based coaches.

Lastly, I enjoy my role with FBN-North America, a non-profit, in developing a peer to peer network of sophisticated and complex families who experience similar challenges ranging from dispersed family ownership to next generation challenges.

Specialties: talent engagement, employee engagement, coaching, consulting, facilitation, public speaking, executive leadership, talent development, next generation leadership, group coaching, succession
