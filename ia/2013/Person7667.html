Angela Chee's Summary

Angela Chee is a Host, Writer, Speaker, Voice-over Artist, Media Consultant and Entrepreneur.
As a former news anchor/reporter with more than a decade of experience her work has been seen around the world. Angela has worked in top TV markets from KNBC, KCBS/KCAL in Los Angeles to XETV in San Diego. She has hosted shows for networks like E! Entertainment, HGTV (Home and Garden Television), and Channel One News.
Merging her traditional media background with her social media influence she is also the Founder of Zen Media Inc.- a Multimedia Company whose mission is to empower through Wisdom, Inspiration and Laughter.
Zen Media Inc. properties include Zen Media Consulting- a media training company that helps businesses build their brands and communicate their message effectively, The Zen Mom.com-an informative and inspirational website for women, and The Zen Mom TV.
She is a contributor to Lifetime Moms, Patch, and one of the founding contributors of 30 Second Mom.com. She also works with Momversation, Her Say, Momlogic and various women and parenting sites.
To learn more about Angela go to www.AngelaChee.com or TheZenMom.com
