Chris Michaels' Summary

Chris Michaels is a strategic communicator with particular expertise in building brands, while maintaining corporate identity and customer loyalty. Chris specializes in creative applications for public and media relations, social media, marketing, advertising, brand management and business development. Chris has also consulted for major brands by drafting corporate materials and marketing collateral, designing brand experiences and ghostwriting for corporate executives.

With business experience ranging from biotechnology, affiliate advertising, wireless software applications, computer vision technologies and scientific research initiatives, Chris has garnered corporate coverage in both top-tier business press and highly specialized trade publications.

Clients have included: Intel, Hewlett Packard, LiveOps, Pantech Wireless, Organic Motion, JBR Media Ventures, Accenture, Konami, Virtual World Computing, Frosmo, novero, Mellanox, Fusion-io, and nTrig.

When not at his desk, Chris can often be found on local beach and indoor volleyball courts or cheering for the Florida Gators.

Specialties

Creative applications for public and media relations, social media, marketing, advertising, brand management and business development, he develops unique solutions for a wide-array of industries.
