Ed Ward's Summary

Affordable Tree Care & Landscape was founded by me about 10 years ago out of Larkspur. I was born and raised and lived most of my life in Florida. Over the years I have been visiting my dad in Colorado who used to live in Perry Park, Larkspur and fell in love with the area. Being tired of Florida’s heat and humidity I moved to Colorado, one of the best decisions of my life.
Affordable Tree Care & Landscape was started like a lot of small businesses as a one person company. I did everything myself from tree services, marketing, estimates and billing. Persistence, love of the business and acquired education helped me grow the company. Right now Affordable Tree Care & Landscape provides a wide range of services:
• Tree removal
• Tree limbing, pruning
• Tree sales & planting
• Mistletoe management
• Stump removal
• Scrub oak removal & pruning
• Tree and shrub insect control
• Preventive beetle spraying
• Wildfire mitigation
• Landscaping services
• Snow removal
Over the years I have been very fortunate to meet and help interesting people. Some of them became great friends. I enjoy all aspects of what I do: from meeting with customers to working with the chainsaw in the field.




Specialties: tree removal, fire mitigation, Colorado tree and shrub spraying, preventive beetle spraying, landscaping and yard services, tree pruning, tree sales and planting, snow removal services, tree and shrub insect control, weed control, mistletoe removal and spraying, fire mitigation, xeriscape, spring and fall clean up
