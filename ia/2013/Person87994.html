Mary Ann Orzech's Summary

Physician Team Strategist for Physician Leaders and Executives

Throughout her career Dr. Orzech has witnessed firsthand how many physicians are placed in leadership positions without the proper training and support. As a former Squadron Commander in the United States Air Force, Dr. Orzech excels at cultivating the leadership mindset necessary to take healthcare teams from good to great and fulfill their high calling of patient care. She coaches physicians to be better people managers and leaders cultivating a positive and results-oriented culture which is essential for survival in healthcare today.

As a board-certified physician in Aerospace and Occupational Medicine, she has walked in the shoes of her clients. She understands the struggles physicians experience with the challenges of developing themselves and their healthcare teams to become more effective.

As a trusted leadership consultant to physician leaders, Dr. Orzech is valued for her ability to see the big picture and generate multiple strategies. Her physician clients value her ability to get to the heart of issues and build collaborative and motivated leaders and teams.

A voracious reader and discoverer, Mary Ann explores all disciplines for insight into building better healthcare organizations and teams and getting the most out of people because these have a passion for what they do, the skills, and the "right fit". As a Reach Certified Personal Brand Strategist, she is focused on identifying people's unique promise of value and using that to deliver on the organization's objectives. With her passion for human potential and development, Mary Ann leverages Appreciative Inquiry and Buckingham's strengths-based approach in her leadership training. A key aspect of her approach is positive reinforcement following the training and team assessment. Mary Ann achieves faster results and facilitates the learning process by working jointly with the physician leader and the team.
