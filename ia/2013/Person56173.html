Ivan Cordoba's Summary

Over the last five years I've been working as a Film Director, Cinematographer and Editor, and I have ten years of experience as a designer. I am an internationally awarded visual artist, and a filmmaker of recognized works which have been published on different books and digital media. Formed with a multidisciplinary training and a strong background in creative thinking. I've been working in a commercial environment since the beginning of my professional career, and simultaneously I have made several non-commercial projects which have been showcased on international festivals like OFFF and Loop Videoart, art centers like Arts Santa Monica, art galleries in New York City and on different digital media. My work embraces a wide range of styles and formats, from documentary to video art, including commercials, corporate videos, music videos and short films. Some of the brands and companies I have worked for are: Mercedes-Benz, Converse, OFFF, L'Oreal Paris, Makos Studio, Glitterati Inc., Transient Pictures, Demand Media, Rooms Magazine, IED Barcelona, Kappa, Sonar, Lullatone, Avia, Blimey, and Budgetplaces.com amongst others. I am currently established in New York City where I continue working as an independent film director on documentary films, narrative short films, commercials and online video content.

Specialties:
- Pre-production and execution of audiovisual communication projects.
- Film/Video Directing.
- Cinematography.
- Editing, post production and color grading on Final Cut Pro and Adobe After Effects.
- Media management and broadcast encoding.
- Compositing and Motion Graphics on After Effects.
- Graphic design on Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign.

Ivan Cordoba's Projects

  • Connexions Bujumbura

    • March 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Ivan Cordoba

    “Connexions Bujumbura” is a documentary film based on the testimonies of four people talking about their life experiences, reflecting on their past, their craving of future and the way they see their present time. A rough film, witness of a complex situation, that draws an intimate portrait of its characters through their confessions.
    The film transfers us to Bujumbura, the capital and largest city of Burundi, a distant and unknown country punished by violence and scarcity, an extremely tough environment, where, despite of the big difficulties, the protagonists face their destiny with strength and dignity, hope and optimism.

  • These Days

    • April 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Ivan Cordoba

    "These Days" short film is a story told as a diary about the idea of going back to moment zero, the alpha moment, the time at which it begins a story, a fact, an event.
    It has been published on the book "WeMadeThis.Es" by Spain Arts & Culture and Indexbook in 2012; and it was elected "Short of the Month" by PRAGDA (film distribution company) in Dec. 2012.
