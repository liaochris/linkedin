Jeff Tolbert's Projects

  • Faerie Solitaire

    • May 2009 to Present
    Team Members: Jeff Tolbert, Brian Kramer

    Faerie Solitaire is an addictive solitaire game wrapped up in a fantasy adventure. The score is a mysterious and moody bland of orchestral, ethnic and folk elements which perfectly complements the magical storyline.

  • American Courtesans

    • August 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Jeff Tolbert, Bobby R. Poirier

    American Courtesans is a first-hand account of escorts in modern America. The rock score is based strongly on the music of the Rolling Stones, Aerosmith and The Cult, among others, and captures the grittiness of life for the women in the film.

  • Modern Views

    • May 2010 to Present
    Team Members: Jeff Tolbert, Boaz Ashkenazy

    This 45-minute documentary chronicles the history of Northwest modern architecture. I composed a minimalist score that matches the region and the architecture featured in the film. The movie was produced by Studio/216 and the University of Washington architecture department.

  • Walk Right In

    • March 2010 to Present
    Team Members: Jeff Tolbert

    Walk Right In tells the story of Yale Summer High School, an experimental summer school run by Yale University in the late '60s. I wrote and recorded the 60’s-influenced score for this feature-length documentary. The score covers a wide range of musical styles, including rock, soul, jazz, folk and orchestral.
