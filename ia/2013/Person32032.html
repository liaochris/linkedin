David Murphy's Summary

David started his B2B sales career in high school. At 18, he established a marketing services company, which he operated while completing his self-funded undergraduate program. From that early entrepreneurial experience, his passion was seeded for the disciplines of marketing, business development, and sales leadership.

For the past 20+ years, he has applied that passion to an exciting and rewarding career in the field of graphic arts and digital print communications. He strives for continual innovation, growth, and efficiency in his marketing strategy and execution. The customer is always at the center of his plans and actions.

At HP, he works with the most talented team in the business to deliver greater demand, improved ROI, and strengthened customer relationships.
