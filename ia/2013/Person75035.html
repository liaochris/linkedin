Kevin L Porter's Summary

There are 3 ways to become an expert; experience, research and education. When it comes to personal growth, my path is a fairly typical one. I was a self-destructive degenerate that had reached a point so low that the only way to go was up, the experience. I learned early in my journey that reading for an hour a day is about a book a week which leads to about 50 books a year. I read for an hour a day, only books pertaining to growth and development and I've been doing so for almost a decade, the research. People fancy degrees, licenses and certificates when it comes to legitimizing someone's skills and knowledge. I got mine from the International Coaching Academy in Sidney, Australia, the education.
