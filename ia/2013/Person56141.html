Aaron Itzerott's Summary

I am a freelance digital media producer based in Derby. I have worked a great deal of video productions contracts with names such as the Format international photography festival, NHS and The University of Derby Student Union. I am experienced in live event video production, short narrative film, corporate promotional video and have extensive postproduction experience from off and online FCP edit, through 2D compositing, correction and motion graphics in Adobe After Effects as well as colour grading with Apple Color. Familiar and competent with a huge range of production kit including the RED ONE camera and dSLR Videography.

Though academic year 10/11 I held the position of president of the Video Production Society, where I led many video projects as well as heading meetings and engaging with members of the public and liaising with Union representatives.

I have 9 years experience with graphic and web design, as well as have recently picked up photography as an artistic discipline. I am looking to bring these skills to bear at Irrelevant Digital Media, while keeping video production very much at it's heart. I aim to utilise the resources and reach of Irrelevant Digital Media to produce and fund our own independent video projects such as short and long form narrative films, Television / Web Series and Interactive web projects.
