Dave Erickson's Summary

I’m a Multimedia Journalist (MMJ) which means I can write, produce, shoot, edit, and present all my own work on-camera or off.

I have more than 15 years of experience working as a T.V. News Anchor and Reporter in multiple top 25 news markets around the country. I’ve worked with ABC, CBS and NBC News. My work as a news reporter has appeared on CNN and the Weather Channel. My passion for storytelling has allowed me to develop invaluable international experience with trips to Iraq, Australia, Italy, Haiti, Canada and Mexico.

My specialty is covering the sport of triathlon (swimming, cycling and running). I bring a unique combination of skills as an Ironman Triathlon Reporter. I’m a 2x Ironman finisher and have completed more than a dozen half iron distance races over the past 15 years. To date, I’ve produced more than 1,000 triathlon race related videos and reports.

Another area I excel in is Automotive Journalism. I write, produce, host, shoot and edit weekly episodes of Everyman Driver. I’m lucky enough to drive a new car every week and in return produce new car reviews for more than 10,000 subscribers online.

My talents don’t stop there. I also produce corporate and instructional videos for businesses; big and small. My clients include Timex, Refuel Got Chocolate Milk, KSWISS and CHOICE Realty. I help companies showcase their services, people and products with real-life, honest and balanced reviews and interviews.

My clients appreciate my ability to integrate my work onto various social media platforms; Facebook, Twitter, Blogs.

So, when you need a professional video for your business, product, event or if you’re looking for a polished on-camera talent and speaker, contact me right away!
