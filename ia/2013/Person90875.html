Megan McGuire's Summary

I am a librarian who values both the traditions of the profession and the cutting edge technology that we can offer patrons. I like to think we can have the best of both worlds. Although my first love is youth services, I also enjoy and am very comfortable working with adults on a variety of projects.

I see myself as a teacher, guide and collaborator. My goal is to obtain a position where I can fulfill all three of these roles.
