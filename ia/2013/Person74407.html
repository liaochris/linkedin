Kenn Mainville's Summary

I am a senior fundraising professional with more than 20 years experience.

I have served as a leader in the fundraising industry working alongside charitable organizations across Canada for the past two decades. I founded a Saint John based fundraising consultancy in 1999. My company provides major gift fundraising services, software training, database conversions, project management, as well as program audits and fund development counsel for a variety of fundraising initiatives including capital campaigns, direct mail, major gifts and planned giving.

Kenn Mainville's Volunteer Experience & Causes

  • Volunteer Experience

    • President

      Association of Fundraising Professionals, New Brunswick Chapter
      October 2012 – present (1 year)

      AFP is a diverse community of fundraising professionals that represents all sectors, missions and sizes of charities around the world. We are committed to improving society through ethical and effective fundraising.

      Since 1960, AFP has inspired global change and supported efforts that generated over $1 trillion. AFP’s nearly 30,000 individual and organizational members raise over $100 billion annually, equivalent to one-third of all charitable giving in North America and millions more around the world.

    • Vice President, Operations

      New Brunswick Youth Orchestra
      • Arts and Culture
      November 2010 – present (2 years 11 months)

      The NBYO is a youth symphony orchestra of approximately 75 musically talented young people who reside in New Brunswick. Our mission is to inspire excellence among children and youth through the learning and performance of orchestral music.

      In 2009, the NBYO founded the first Sistema-branded program in Canada, inspired by the internationally recognized El Sistema in Venezuela.

      Our mission is to promote the collective practice of music through symphony orchestras in order to inspire children and youth to achieve their full potential and to acquire values that favour their growth and have a positive impact on their lives and society.

      Our vision is social development through an innovative and hope-instilling music education program distinguished by its excellence and for having a positive impact on both the children and the communities where it is implemented.

      Sistema NB is a proven model that fights the negative impact of poverty among children. Through a free, orchestral music program, where children participate 3 hours every day after school, social change is fostered through playing music together, a direct counter to the corrosive impact of perpetually being left out.

      The Moncton centre, opened in 2009, engages 250 children daily with instruction including all instruments of the orchestra. The Saint John centre began in 2011 in partnership with School District 8 and includes 40 children in this 1st year.

      In 2012 we will opened our 3rd centre in Richibucto

      Sistema NB is having remarkable results. Not only are children advancing musically but the educational impact is significant. Teachers and parents are reporting improvements in school attendance, performance and behaviour. As they learn the value of team work and dedication, children are experiencing a sense of joy and passion that is leading to a growing sense of confidence and self-esteem. They are building a solid foundation for their future.

    • Executive Board Member

      AFP Foundation for Philanthropy - Canada
      October 2004 – September 2005 (1 year)

      The AFP Foundation for Philanthropy - Canada was created in 1998 to help fulfill the mission of the Association of Fundraising Professionals (AFP), previously the National Society of Fund Raising Professionals: Advancing philanthropy through education, advocacy and training in Canada.

      Purposes - The AFP Canadian Foundation works to:

      *Ensure the advancement of philanthropy by supporting AFP's mission and goals in Canada.
      *Seek, receive and expend philanthropic funds on AFP's behalf.
      *Engage in related activities required to fulfill its purposes and mission.

      As the philanthropic arm of Association of Fundraising Professionals, the AFP Foundation for Philanthropy supports many programs and services through its highly successful Annual Fund, especially the Every Member Campaign, and other fundraising vehicles including Major Gifts, Planned Gifts and Endowments.
