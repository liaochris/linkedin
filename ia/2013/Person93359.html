Michele Woodward's Summary

Michele Woodward is President of Ripple Communications and a Master Certified Coach who has worked with executives across the U.S. and around the world, and is a former White House official with over 30 years of experience in corporate, non-profit, political and small business messaging and positioning.

For the last nine years, Michele has been a successful entrepreneur running a thriving coaching practice where she worked closely with CEOs, Executive Directors and other leaders to hone and refine their vision, leadership and personal communications style. She not only got the chance to transform work environments but, as a frequent public speaker before groups of all sizes, Michele has had the opportunity to share her knowledge far beyond the boardroom. Over the years, she has trained and mentored hundreds of other coaches and is considered a “coach’s coach.”

Earlier in her career, Michele was an executive for five years with the ground-breaking public affairs firm founded by the legendary Anne Wexler and Nancy Reynolds. As Senior Vice President and registered lobbyist at the U.S. Congress for Wexler, Reynolds, she advised corporate clients on political matters and developed effective campaigns to meet specific public policy, legislative or public relations objectives.

Michele was Lead Press Advance in the Reagan White House, constructing some of the memorable photo opportunities of that presidency, and worked on the 1984, 1988, 1992 and 1996 national presidential campaigns. She was also Director of Press Advance for Dole for President ’88, coordinating the national press on the campaign plane.

Recently, she was honored to serve as Executive Director of the Ronald Reagan Centennial Commission, a bipartisan, year-long effort appointed by President Obama. She was a principal organizer of the State Funeral of President Gerald R. Ford and previously organized the State Funeral of President Ronald Reagan.
