David Welch's Summary

I have closed hundreds of residential, land, and commercial transactions in the 15 years that I have been practicing real estate in the Orlando area. Personal mission statement: "I help others around me make better decisions."

When I look back over my 30 year career, that is what I truly believe my role is. People around me often seek my advice on how to handle difficult decisions. I do not tell people what to do, but help them ask the right questions and find the answers. I have always believed that if I can help others achieve their goals I will achieve mine.

My answers and blog posts have been featured on Realtor.com "Ask an Agent", and Blogs. I have also had my real estate advice and opinions published in Market Watch (WSJ), Inman News, Colorado Springs Business Journal, Orlando Business Journal and The Orlando Sentinel.

Specialties

Residential sales, land sales, and commercial sales and leasing. First time home buyers, and investment purchases and sales. I am also very versed in the role and use of the internet for real estate marketing and sales.
