Jonathan Shelley's Summary

Jonathan Shelley is a 3d content creation specialist and owner/Director of Electric Geisha.
After many years of playing professional Rugby Union throughout the world and then becoming a father he decided to follow his passion for art and pursued creating a full design house that evolved into what Electric Geisha is today. After more than 8 years the company has developed into a successful 3d content creation company for the entertainment industry focused at virtual world platforms.
