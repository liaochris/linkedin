Adam Viener's Summary

Self-motivated, successful, and entrepreneurial.

Adam is the Chairman & Founder of imwave (www.imwave.com) a performance search engine marketing agency focused on driving performance based sales to companies through pay per click search engine advertising. Imwave has been recognized by Inc Magazine as one of the fastest growing privately held companies in 2009, 2010, 2011 and 2012.

Adam is a recognized leader in the Internet Marketing Community. He currently sits on the publisher advisory board of The Google Affiliate Network, and writes for ReveNews.com.

Specialties

Internet Marketing, Affiliate Marketing, Search Engine Marketing, PPC Advertising, Pay-Per-Click Marketing, Internet Startups, SEO, SEM, Product Management, Product Marketing, Keyword Advertising, Internet Advertising, Google, Google Adwords, Yahoo Search Marketing, Overture, Miva, Findwhat, Kanoodle, GOClick, and more.

Consulting to Clients for Internet Marketing Strategies, Retargeting & Remarketing Strategies, Monetization Strategies, Search Engine Marketing Strategies, and more.
