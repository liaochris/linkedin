Hope Corenzwit, LMSW's Summary

Hope Corenzwit, LMSW, provides treatment for young children with autism and related disorders using a play-based approach that helps children make progress in ways that are fun and meaningful. She has nearly 20 years’ experience helping children make excellent progress. She uses a variety of autism-specific approaches to suit each child’s individual learning style. Hope offers Parent Training to help parents gain a better understanding of their child’s needs and to learn useful skills to continue on their own.

Hope’s Spectrum Breakthrough Intervention helped many children make unexpected progress. SBI helps children with severe autism having difficulty making progress with more typical interventions and children with spectrum disorders who need help to move forward from a learning plateau .
