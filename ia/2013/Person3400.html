Al Bonner's Summary

I am a thirty plus year sales and marketing and digital veteran. For the past 14 years, I have been on the leading edge of the digital evolution, working on start-ups, digital services fulfillment and the newspaper and television industry’s digital transition. I am currently the Regional Publisher for Gatehouse Media in Independence, Missouri.

Previously, I was the owner of Presentation Transformations and Al Bonner Consulting, providing consulting and fulfillment for a range of digital services including mobile and desktop website design. I am an experienced presentation designer and presenter. I have created and delivered hundreds of presentations for workshops, seminars and conventions.

I have also served as the general manager for a start-up national shopping web site called zip2save.com. My work with hundreds of media companies helped me fully appreciate the strengths and weaknesses of traditional and new media. For 11 years I played a pivotal role in the Lawrence Journal-World’s news and advertising convergence effort and the planning and execution of numerous new websites, verticals and software platforms.

Specialties: Website design, mobile website design, SEO, SEM, email, social media, effective use of landing pages. Digital and advertising sales and marketing management, presentation design, presentation training and coaching, marketing strategies, mobile strategy, sales training, sales analysis, online seminars and webinars, LinkedIn training and much more.

Presentation Transformations (Sole Proprietorship)

Sole Proprietorship; 1-10 employees; Design industry

Presentation Transformations is division of Al Bonner Consulting LLC.

To quote Guy Kawasaki: “95% of presentations suck.” I think you’ve sat through enough bad PowerPoint presentations in your life to know that statement is not an exaggeration. In fact, Kawasaki goes on to say the figure is more like 99%.

Whatever the percentage, viewing slide after slide of bullet points, using annoying whiz-bang, fly in effects, or presentations so copy-heavy the presenter can’t even get through it all without turning away from their audience to read each point, is a pretty common occurrence. Effective presentations are not rocket science, but trying to differentiate yourself or your company from all of this clutter can be a challenge if you don’t know where to start or don’t have the time to fix what you have.

Presentation Transformations is a simple concept. Let someone else help you transform your pile of clutter, bad graphs, pie-charts and dated effects into something that represents an image you and your company will be proud of. More importantly, a tool that will sell your concept, information or product in an effective manner. It’s really possible!

I have been taking powerless PowerPoints and converting them into a compelling sales and marketing tools for more than seven years. I can help you create a new presentation, re-work or re-create something you currently use, and guide you through a new process that will completely reshape how you approach sales presentations, company or product information, training, or whatever you’re using presentation tools for. I have extensive experience in PowerPoint and Keynote. Examples at: www.slideshare.net/abonner
