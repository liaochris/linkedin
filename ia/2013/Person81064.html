Linda McManus' Summary

Received a BFA - Arcadia University, concentration in photography. My photographic career ranges from assisting commercial photographers to lead photographer for a high end real estate broker as well as head photographer/studio manager for a home shopping channel and most recently licensing artist.

My licensing world began with one image of my son with kisses all over his face. It was picked up by a greeting card manufacturer and sold in Target and beyond. Since that one card my images have been applied to a range of products including greeting cards, framed art, wall decor and more. Popular titles include Floral Foods, Funny Felines, and most recently Fontography.

I continually expand my brand and stretch my creative boundaries. I envision a portfolio that crosses all markets. Imagination is fueled by creativity which is fueled by imagination.. an endless circle.

Specialties: Photographer,licensing artist,greeting card artist/writer, conceptual development, witty one liners for life's "experiences"
