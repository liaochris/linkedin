Debbie Qaqish, Chief Strategy Officer's Summary

The Pedowitz Group Helps Organizations Accelerate the Journey to Revenue Marketing ™ and Achieve RPS ™ - Repeatable, Predictable, Sustainable Revenue Growth.

Journey to Revenue Marketing ™

Make No Mistake: The Time for the Revenue Marketer has arrived. CEOs are looking for new ways to create revenue, heads of sales are asking for help to manage the top of the funnel, and sales continue to demand better qualified leads.

To achieve these key business goals The Pedowitz Group has developed a four step model to help organization’s understand the stages between a traditional marketing organization and a revenue marketing organization.

Four Stages Between a Traditional Marketing Organization and a Revenue Marketing Organization

Stage #1: Traditional Marketing
Stage #2: Lead Generation
Stage #3: Demand Generation
Stage #4: Revenue Marketing

Specialties

The Pedowitz Group guides organizations on the journey from Lead Generation to Revenue Marketing.

Revenue Marketing

a) Combined set of activities across sales and marketing that puts high quality leads into the top of the funnel and accelerates opportunities through the sales pipeline.

b) Achieves RPS ™ - Repeatable, Predictable, Sustainable Revenue Growth.

c) Provides the ability to accurately forecast what marketing will be contributing in the upcoming quarters
