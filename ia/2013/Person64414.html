Joseph K. Levene's Summary

Joseph K. Levene Fine Art, Ltd., Dealer of Secondary Market Modern, Post War & Contemporary Art, established by Joseph K. Levene in 1991. Joseph K. Levene, Principal, an Andy Warhol and Jasper Johns authority, and Robert Grunder, Director, together, together have more than 3 decades multifaceted Fine Art experience, as Art Dealer, Collector, Art Executive and Online Gallery Sales.

A Wharton School graduate, Joseph K. Levene was previously President/COO, Petersburg Press, Inc., the International Fine Art Publisher; Director e-commerce, artnet.com; Charter Member, Sothebys.com online fine art auctions. The Joseph K Levene Fine Art Ltd, eBay Store was launched in 1999, providing all Clients with same Terms of Guarantee offered at Sotheby's and Christie's.

Since 2004, Joseph K. Levene Fine Art, Ltd., has represented Tony Rosenthal, Sculptor of Public Art, for over 7 decades, best known for creating Alamo, 1967, aka, the Black Cube, the first public Contemporary Sculpture purchased by the City of New York.
