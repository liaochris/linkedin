David Marshall's Summary

It's not just OK to re-invent the wheel as a writer; it's expected. Fresh ways to make the statement, communicate the idea, win the response. Sometimes it is simply a means to convey essential information, at other times a desire to win a shift in opinion or to motivate readers to action. Or maybe it's "just" to entertain. Whatever the ambition, it pays to be clear about the objective, to appreciate your audience, and understand the media to hand. And I do all that for others, I suppose the point is, that I could be doing it for you.

Specialties: Feature and copywriting: Business communications. Business training material. Outdoor leisure; walking, rambling, mountaineering, sailing; Natural and human heritage, wildlife, biodiveristy, the environment & conservation. Travel and tourism.
