Andy Cowin's Summary

Hi there.

I make the digital world better by improving the end-user experience and making it more user friendly.

That means:
• Understanding how and why end-users use products and services
• Creating positive user experiences that increases user satisfaction
• Optimising websites and software so they're more efficient, cost less to develop and maintain and increase return on investment.

I love what I do and I've built up a fantastic skillset working with organisations such as the British Army, BBC, BT, Public Health England, and The Open University, to name a few.

I love connecting with genuine, funny, creative, open-minded people who like developing proper relationships and strive for continuous improvement rather than settle for 'just ok'.

As the saying goes, 'The longest journey starts with a single step...' Send me a message and lets start talking. Who knows what might come of it...;0)
