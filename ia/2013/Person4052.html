Alexia Vernon's Summary

Hailed a "Moxie Maven" by the White House for my women's communication and leadership development work, I am on a mission to build a new generation of heart-centered, high-impact communicators and ethical leaders. Through my Step Into Your Moxie platform, taking the form of one-on-one public speaking coaching, women's public speaking groups (Step Into Your Moxie Mastery), a weekend retreat for female changemakers (Moxie Camp), and a first-of-its-kind yearlong women's leadership development experience (Influencer Academy), I'm passionate about helping business leaders and entrepreneurs, particularly women, listen to their inner voices, sculpt their powerful messages, and speak with power and impact.

I am the author of 90 DAYS 90 WAYS: Onboard Young Professionals to Peak Performance and AWAKEN YOUR CAREERPRENEUR: A Holistic Road Map to Climb from Your Calling to Your Career. I have been featured on/in CNN, NBC, CW, ABCNews.com, Mint.com, Monster.com, CareerBuilder.com and in such print media as the Wall Street Journal, Women's Health Magazine, Diversity Executive, and the Beverly Hills Times. To learn more about me, visit http://www.AlexiaVernon.com, connect with me on Facebook at http://www.facebook.com/AlexiaVernonEmpowerment, and follow on Twitter at http://www.Twitter.com/AlexiaVernon.

Specialties: Public speaking, coaching, negotiation, women's empowerment, onboarding, interpersonal communication, ethical leadership, generational issues/Generation Y/Millennials, sustainability/green issues, interactive learning strategies, conflict resolution, diversity awareness, drama education, social innovation, work-life integration, and youth and community development
