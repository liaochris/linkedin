Matthew Haas' Summary

Matthew A Haas is currently Director of Investor Relations and Fund Management for Merritt Community Capital. He is also a principal of Infinite Urban Solutions, a sustainable community consulting and development firm. For over a year and a half, he was the Deputy Director of California Lending at the Low Income Investment Fund (LIIF). Prior to joining LIIF, he was with Fannie Mae in Pasadena as a Senior Multifamily Manager in the Community Investments Group for the Western Region for two years and worked for Bank of America Community Development Banking in Los Angeles as a Vice President and Client Manager for three years. Mr. Haas has close to 25 years of affordable housing and community development experience, having worked in three large urban markets, New York City, Chicago and Los Angeles, for a variety of key organizations across the spectrum of urban redevelopment and reinvestment and multifamily housing design, planning and finance.
