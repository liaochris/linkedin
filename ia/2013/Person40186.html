Evert-Jan De Kort's Summary

Helping companies to enrich their business through brand & culture.

After having spent inspiring 10 years in online advertising and digital marketing, I felt the need to go beyond campaign thinking and creative ideas on the short term.
InSites Consulting brought me more profound research insights on how customers look at businesses, companies, conversations and brands. Collaborating with Innate Motion helped me understand the value of humanizing brand positioning.

Brand management and brand culture will challenge your company to truly connect with your audiences, beyond communication. I engage in complex projects that are linked to strategic brand management, brand architecture, portfolio strategy, positioning, culture, digital thinking, design and creativity.

I'm also having a passion-project with Choqoa: I inspire people to discover the real flavors of fine cacao and origin chocolate bars, crafted by small-scale artisans around the world.
