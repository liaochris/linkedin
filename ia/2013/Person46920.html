Garett Robert Farrugia's Summary

I am an Insurance Agent/Broker and Licensed Real Estate Agent. I have helped many people find better value for their insurance and real estate needs. I help clients understand their current coverages and help them find better value from one of our 90 companies. I am fifth generation in the Insurance and Real Estate business. Robert E. Schaff Ltd. has been a family owned business since 1900 when Edward C. Schaff started the company. His son Edward R. Schaff started selling insurance in 1920. Robert E. Schaff, son of Edward R. Schaff started selling insurance in 1945. In 1980 the company was incorporated and became Robert E. Schaff Ltd. In 1990 Robert's wife Suzanne M. Schaff took over operations. I am the grandson of Robert E. Schaff and Suzanne M. Schaff.

Our business is committed to providing quality service to the community. We believe that a reputation as an honest and reliable business is the recipe for success. While others seek to profit by cutting corners, or recommending unnecessary services to clients, we believe that a good reputation and consistent service leads to satisfied clients. By being trustworthy, we enjoy a long term relationship with clients and the community.
