Jeff Ullrich's Summary

At Earwolf, I spend my time creating, producing and monetizing digital comedy. It's an exciting time for a growing industry and I'm having a blast defining a new set of rules about how things should work.

At The Mid Roll - I help podcasters sell ads against their shows - while maintaining their integrity and making their advertisers money.

My blog, "The Truth About Entrepreneurship", can be found at jeffullrich.com.
