Leslie Newcorn's Summary

I am a professional vocalist and pride myself on tailoring any event to your personal needs and desires. I use my exceptional musicianship and versatility when planning events and programs. Whether you would like entertainment for an event such as an anniversary or birthday party, or you seek that special person who can conduct a service or ceremony such as a Bar/Bat Mitzvah or baby naming, I am the one for you. From Liturgical to Broadway, from Classical to Jazz, I have created a repertoire that will meet the needs of many programs, events, and ceremonies. I sing with incomparable energy and passion that enlivens and energizes any event. My confidence and sense of bliss create a unique performance experience which transcends to my audience.

Specialties

vocalist, liturgical music, Broadway, Classical, Jazz, cantorial, events, ceremonies, baby namings, weddings, funerals, anniversaries, parties, Bar/Bat Mitzvah, singing, performing, concerts
