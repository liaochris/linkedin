Brian Hoynowski's Summary

As one of the few hundred Certified Professional Yacht Brokers nationwide, I am a specialist in new/used sportfish and cruising vessels, 5m to 40m. An extreme love for music, yachts, fishing, any island and hard work. My experience includes a vast array of technical knowledge accompanied with extensive personal ownership perspectives of large vessels. I served in the United States Navy, played in bands, traveled the world, play sports and continue to enjoy sharing stories on the water with my customers, friends, and family. My dedication, enthusiasm, love of the water and my willingness to travel the extra mile for each of my customers brings with it a very satisfying and pleasurable buying or selling experience. I enjoy sports, music, good times, good places, good people and work hard for it all.

Specialties

Viking Yachts, Lazzara, Azimut, Westport, Grand Banks, Viking Sport Cruisers, Princess Yachts, Tiara Yachts, Cruisers Yachts, Ocean Alexander, Marquis Yachts, Hatteras Yachts, Cabo Yachts, Maritimo Yachts, Grand Banks Yachts, Expert in Brokerage Vessels & Custom Yacht Builds up to 40m.
