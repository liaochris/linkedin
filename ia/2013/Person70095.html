Julie Clark's Summary

Is your brand working as hard as you are?
Take a look at it with a Brand Design Expert and Step UP your Image...

JT Clark Design, based in the Chicago suburb of Lake Bluff, is well-positioned to handle the most demanding challenges faced by individuals and companies who wish to market smart. JT Clark believes in the power of branding and the firm brings a disciplined, artistic, and collaborative approach to each phase of the creative process. “We believe every product or service has an inherent drama that is the life blood of the brand,” confirms Clark. From concept to execution, the award-winning creativity of JT Clark drives the process. The result is on target creative solutions that aim high and hit the mark for business ROI. Stunning visuals and aesthetics disseminate the message clearly across a multitude of channels, and elevates brand awareness.
The JT Clark is an expert in print, web and video presentation.

JT Clark has executed hundreds of product launch campaigns with highly acclaimed success, for an esteemed list of clients for over 25 years. Winning her first art award at the age of seven, studying mixed media, clay, fiber, metal, color, paper, graphics, typography, photography, illustration, web and video, her creative scope covers the gamut of solutions in many dimensions. It all fits under the big goal and umbrella of custom design and JT Clark’s creative solutions to
"Step UP Your Image". She claims "Never underestimate the power of good design".

CLIENTS INCLUDE: Abbott Laboratories, Anheuser Busch, AT & T, Baxter, Cardinal Health, CVS/Caremark, Culligan, Gatorade, Jim Beam Brands, Kraft, Maritz Motivation, Motorola, W.W. Grainger. Office Max, Wrigley, and a host of smaller business.
