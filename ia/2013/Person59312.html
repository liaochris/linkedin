Jason LaBarbera's Experience

Founder and CEO

Scaffold Labs

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Staffing and Recruiting industry

July 2000 – Present (13 years 2 months) Silicon Valley

Scaffold Labs is a boutique talent firm based in Silicon Valley. For over a decade, we have partnered exclusively with venture-backed technology companies and closely assist our clients with the entire arc of their team-building needs. Specifically, we help each of our companies identify and refine their ideal target candidate profiles and leverage our own proprietary, scientific research techniques to mark every potential candidate.

While we start with technical foundations, our group has experience building out teams for sales, marketing, and support functions as well. The work begins at identification, as Scaffold Labs then creates specific campaigns to touch and recruit key targets. In the last thirteen years, we have been retained by companies at the early stages after having been funded by venture capital firms Sequoia, Greylock, and Accel, among others. Ultimately, we believe there is both an art and a science to building great, lasting technical teams – it’s the science that helps us identify who matters, and the personalized art of what we do, what we have done for years, over and over, which helps us deliver value for our clients.
