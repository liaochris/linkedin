  • Holiday March To A Million

    • November 2011 to December 2011
    Team Members: Lisa Bassett, Gajen Indrakumaran, Erin Waldie, Emanuela Myers, Debbie Baxter, CMA, ICD.D

    Giving back to the community has always been a cornerstone of the AIR MILES Reward Program. We teamed up with Kids Help Phone, Food Banks Canada and the CP24 CHUM Christmas Wish on a unique social media initiative. The Holiday March To A Million allowed AIR MILES Collectors to help AIR MILES make a difference for three very important charities.

    The Holiday March To A Million was a unique charity campaign launched by the AIR MILES Reward Program in an effort to help these charities build awareness of their cause and increase donations during such an important time of year. Up until December 24, 2011, all Canadians were encouraged to visit airmiles.ca/million and vote for their charity of choice: Kids Help Phone, Food Banks Canada, or the CP24 CHUM Christmas Wish. Each vote was matched by AIR MILES with one reward mile. In addition, at every 50,000 votes generated, one lucky AIR MILES Collector was awarded 5,000 reward miles, and so was their charity of choice. At the end of the campaign, the charity with the most votes received a donation of one million AIR MILES reward miles.

    We wanted Canadians to help spread the word by extending the invitation and encouraging their friends and family to do the following:
    - Like the AIR MILES Canada Facebook Page
    - Vote for one of the three charities and help AIR MILES award one charity with one million AIR MILES reward miles
    - Share the news of the Holiday March To A Million campaign through word of mouth and through their own Facebook page and Twitter accounts

    The resulting campaign drove over 250,000 new fans to the AIR MILES Canada Facebook page, increased our talking about us metric from 1500 to over 45000 and garnered over 175000 votes.
