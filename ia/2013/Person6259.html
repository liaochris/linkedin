Anders Hecquet's Summary

I am very experienced in IT solutions and transformation projects and have since the start of this millennium helped many companies and organizations safely implement new IT solutions for their business critical systems and thereby lower costs and adding business value.

Through an ability to quickly understand and identify business and technology challenges – I am able to build business cases and advice customer’s senior management on IT improvements strategies. The knowledge and experience of this has come through many years of working with design of safe solutions and management of several multimillion dollar projects locally and internationally.

Before this, I have for a decade worked with IT application development – primarily on the mainframe for large financial companies. I have great experience in areas such as security, stability, accuracy, scalability and performance which is essential for most large multinational financial organizations.

Personally I am very structured and quality aware in my approach. I am able to identify and address critical issues, focus on critical path and mitigate risks. For me the customer is always at the center of my activities and I always aim for a win-win solution for all parties.
