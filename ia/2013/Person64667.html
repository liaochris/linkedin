Jean-Michel Frouin's Summary

Fascinated since childhood by the computer world, I’m doing technical engineering for 20 years.

On one hand, being self-taught, I learned my way in this work by being curious and my constant desire to learn more about this so vast industry.
I was able to watch and take part over the years to all the changes that have marked this constantly changing universe.
That is why today I have a keen and comprehensive understanding of technology allowing me to adapt to new and evolving technologies.

On the other hand my computer Engineering studies have allowed me to have a clear and comprehensive vision on the computer world and embedded systems.

My career also led me to assume management responsibilities. This way I discovered new skills and a greater interest in people’s management.

My training in management and communication as well as various events pushed me to be even more interested in the relational aspect and human labor.

Today, although the technique remains important to me, my challenge is now turning to be a better men coordinator.

In the long term I see myself still as passionate about my job and always aware of technological developments through constant monitoring, but my goal is to lead ever growing teams.

From a human point of view I want to support and grow together with my team so that everyone can give the best of themselves and discover new skills and unsuspected resources.

My empathy and my ability to challenge myself should help me progress in the right direction.
