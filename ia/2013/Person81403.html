Lisa Ann Martin's Summary

CyberStylist and Image Stylist Lisa Ann Martin enables people to coordinate their inner and outer beauty. She empowers clients to promote a confident personal brand image that fuels success; providing a holistic styling approach.

With an international list of clients, Lisa Ann works through cyberspace, to assist people all over the globe, helping them find their personal style and to dress for success, whether it is for business, formal occasions, dates or just every day wear. Lisa Ann believes that style is more than just an outfit; it’s a way of life.

By focusing on their “lifestyle” needs, Lisa Ann’s clients benefit most from her “eye for details” and her ability to harmonize it with their unique personality, preferences, comfort level, body shape, and lifestyle. Lisa Ann believes in a comprehensive approach; All leading to the pursuit of her passion: style.

Lisa Ann is a published author and recently released “Inspired Style” with several other top experts, giving interested reader’s helpful image strategies. This book is the key ingredient to helping women understand and define their personal style, which is so much more than clothes and fashion. In Inspired Style, Lisa Ann addresses the issue of dressing for your body shape. This chapter will help women honestly evaluate their body, identify trouble spots and teach them how to dress in a way that accentuates their positive features.

Frequently consulted by the style industry, she has been featured in regional and Canadian publications, along with frequent radio and television guest spots. Her blogs, “Fashionably Minded” and “Essential Guy” are a great source of fashion, image and dating advice.

Lisa Ann currently serves on Arizona’s Fashion Group International Board of Directors. As well as gives back to her community by being involved with several local Arizona charitable organizations: Fresh Start Women’s Foundation, 5 Arts Circle, and Las Palomas.
