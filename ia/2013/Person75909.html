Daniel Harris' Summary

I'm an Australian animator with several years experience spanning Feature films, television and video games. My most notable projects being "Journey 2: The Mysterious island" (Michael Caine and Dwayne Johnson) and "Legend of the Guardians" (directed by Zack Snyder). I have great versatility as an animator able to span both creature and character animation, high technical aptitude and experience working in a broad range of quotas and budgets. A background in rigging, storyboarding and layout has given me a broad perspective on the animation process which helps me to focus on the bigger picture. I understand fully that the greatest element of any job is the people you work with; this is why I always endeavour to support and inspire the team around me.

I have work authority in Australia, Canada and Europe (including the UK).

Specialties

Primary:
-Creature and character animation.
-Storyboarding and planning for animation.
-Knowledge and passion for cinematography, layout and editing.
