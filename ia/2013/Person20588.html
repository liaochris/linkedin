Carri Craver's Summary

I love to design in a way that can help and hopefully delight the user. I stay current on the latest in user experience research and design. Asking questions to understand the user and what he/she really wants is second nature to me at this point. I derive inspiration from well designed sites that make my life easier or better like Hipmunk, Pinterest and Astrid. I thrive in an environment that is about moving forward and making progress. I see failed designs as a learning experience and enjoying iterating toward better outcomes. I like the challenges of designing for various sizes and devices and strive toward giving users what they need and expect for each environment.

Specialties: User Experience Design, User Research, Usability, Human Computer Interaction, Analytics, Search UX, Mobile UX, Visual Design, Interaction Design, Information Architecture
