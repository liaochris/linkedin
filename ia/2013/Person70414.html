Judy Peterson's Summary

The Philadelphia Main Line and Western Suburbs of Chester County, Montgomery County and Delaware County provide some of best opportunities to find your new home AND to make a wise real estate investment choice.

I love creating value added real estate opportunities for you in all stages of your real estate journey. My goal is to represent you and your best interests for success, whether you're moving across town or across the country.

Good real estate photography creates more and better quality home showings. It's a critical first step to getting sold for the highest price in the shortest time. The 2012 NAR Home Buyer/Seller Profile found that 98% of all buyers begin their search online making it more true than ever that "a good picture is worth more than a thousand words". . . and dollars!
