Gary R. Beyer's Summary

Business transformation catalyst for companies looking to transition from a current state to some future state; a strategic provocateur who knows how to bridge from data to insight to strategy.

Experienced CEO / COO with multiple soft-tech firms including ERP / Project Management, secure messaging, cloud-hosted portals for Document Management, Private Equity and M&A transactions, mobility enabled broadcast communications and eLearning. Big Tech executive experience with Oracle, Compaq, HP and Boeing.

Deep experience developing product, revenue and organizational strategies that drive maximum leverage from a transformative event such as M&A, providing support from pre-transaction planning through the acquisition integration process.
