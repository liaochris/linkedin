Pat Price's Summary

Founder and principal of The Price Group, Inc., a marketing firm serving entrepreneurs and mid-sized organizations in the Chicago area. Pat has thirty years experience in both corporate strategic planning and marketing, as well as the entrepreneurial experience of leading a start-up.

Prior to forming The Price Group, she was the Director of North America Product Marketing for a Fortune 500 firm. While there, she worked through a wide variety of business issues, successfully managing areas including Product Management, Product Marketing, Strategy Development and Marketing Communications. Her accomplishments also include P&L responsibility for a $35M product line, sales support for a team producing $750M in annual revenue, and re-branding of a multi-billion dollar product.

Pat has been a featured speaker at corporate seminars and association conferences and is a member of the National Speakers Association. She currently serves on the Board of Directors for the Hawthorne Credit Union. She has produced several products including the “Marketing Boot Camp” DVD and workbook, and “Create Your Irresistible Offer” and “Marketing in a Recession” CDs. She also writes a free monthly newsletter, offering marketing advice to entrepreneurs, business owners and marketers.

Specialties

Marketing strategy, integrating marketing campaigns, brand development, professional speaker, trainer and facilitator.
