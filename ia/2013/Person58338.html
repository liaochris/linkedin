Janette Lonsdale's Summary

Leveraging the power of “interesting”.

Good content connects people. Good connections produce strong relationships; great clients; loyal customers; and a strong brand identity.

When the people your business can serve well find you interesting, they are attracted to you. It is my job to engineer audience engagement through remarkable content and its delivery to your clients, prospects and your market place.

I am Janette Lonsdale, the principle marketing and digital content strategist and content developer at The Red Stairs, a boutique creative agency dedicated to planning, creating and managing content.

As a seasoned project manager for a wide range of creative projects, I help businesses and corporations communicate authentically, ethically and effectively with their target audiences using online web assets and traditional print platforms.

I am a 20-year veteran of the media industry with a background in magazine journalism and public relations. I am a skilled content marketer, web editor, copy editor as well as a talented copywriter and journalist. I have a deep understanding of my raw materials – words and images – and how to make them work.

I have a passion for developing effective content strategies that embrace content assets that are useful, valuable and interesting to your audience. These include content led websites, SEO copywriting, strategic social media, goal-measured web traffic analysis and other skills to ensure that your marketing messages are mapped to your audience.

High quality, thoughtful brand content establishes a brand image that has a strong and positive impact so that you stand out in our information-saturated world.
