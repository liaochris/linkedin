Joop van der Vinne's Experience

Director

VOC Developments Nigeria Ltd.

Public Company; 1-10 employees; Management Consulting industry

July 2012 – Present (1 year 6 months) ikoyi, nigeria

VOC Development is a registered Nigerian company recently setup to identify, facilitate, procure and bridge the gap between Dutch companies operating in the Netherlands with keen interest to expand their businesses and market presences in Africa most especially Nigeria and Local companies in Nigerian looking for new technologies, expertise and support to execute their projects or solve challenges here at home.

Our focus at VOC is not only to bring in companies looking to make money but to find business with keen interest in adding value to Nigeria through the transfer of knowledge, expertise, technology, social responsibility and funding.

At VOC, our focus and services are in the following areas, Construction, Telecommunications, Vessel procurement/refurbishment and ICT support services. Currently we represent over 10 suppliers from the Netherlands in the listed fields currently undertaking projects or looking to come into the country. Some of the companies we are representing includes Rollercate B.V, Holland Shipyard, VIP Lift, Venema Advies B.V etc.

At VOC we also provide logistic, procurement and project management services for some of our Dutch suppliers and clients here in Nigeria. Our value add services enables us to understand our clients needs, expectations and deliverables while providing them with the best possible expertise, support and technology from the Netherlands.
