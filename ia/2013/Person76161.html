Chris Adams' Summary

I have global experience in tourism, new technology and new media from around the world - including the USA, Canada, New Zealand, Australia and UK. My time in the tourism industry has seen it transformed - with the impact of new technology, new digital marketing channels and globalization. The convergence of these forces has produced a fascinating time to work in tourism and media - and has driven my career, experience and outlook. My roles have been focused around a core set of 'future focused' responsibilties - international tourism, new media/online marketing, research/web analytics and a strong commitment to the environment and sustainable tourism. I thrive on change and am passionate about the power of travel to build understanding and connections - and through this make the world a better place.

Specialties

Travel and tourism marketing with experience in a wide range of global visitor markets. Marketing with specialist skills and experience across new media and and all major digital marketing channels. Extensive experience in managing tourism research including consumer and meetings, plus the set up and use of online analytics reporting tools. Managing innovation and new product development. A long term commitment to sustainable tourism and a personal passion around outdoor adventure.

Chris Adams' Experience

Director of Research & Online Marketing & GM South Pacific

Miles

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry

May 2005 – Present (8 years 4 months) Auckland New Zealand, Denver Colorado and Sarasota, Florida

Miles is one of the world's largest tourism marketing agencies focused on travel and tourism. Miles develops marketing and publishing solutions for with more than 60 destinations in the US, Canada, South Pacific and Asia and markets more than a dozen independent hotels and resorts in the US, Hawaii and South Pacific. We are a full service marketing agency developing online and offline marketing solutions with a focus on content, personalized marketing and results. As Director of Research and Online Marketing I lead the Company’s research solutions and help manage and develop a range of advanced online marketing services including content marketing, SEO, analytics and advertising reporting. Our Destination clients include the States of Florida, Colorado, Pennsylvania, Louisiana and Georgia and cities including San Francisco, Los Angeles. St Petersburg - Clearwater, and Auckland, New Zealand. A wide range of the white papers, analysis and research I have authored, co-authored or commissioned is available in the Insight section of the Miles web site: www.MilesPartnership.com/library

In a volunteer capacity I also act as Chairman of the Industry Advisory Board for the Center for Sustainable Tourism based at Eastern Carolina University: www.sustainabletourism.org

Group Marketing Manager

Jasons Travel Media Ltd

Public Company; 51-200 employees; Publishing industry

June 1999 – September 2004 (5 years 4 months)

Jasons Travel Media is the largest tourism publisher in the South Pacific - Australia/New Zealand and the South Pacific Islands.

I wrote the original online business plan for Jasons in 1995 and led the development of the Company's first web site while at VR-1 in 1996. In 1999 returning from the US I joined the Company full time as Group Marketing Manager and helped lead the Company's online growth, introduction of a range of new visitor products and aggressive expansion in new markets across Australia, New Zealand and the South Pacific. The company had a successful public market launch in 2005 soon after I left the organization for work in the US.

Director of e Commerce

VR-1

November 1995 – June 1999 (3 years 8 months)

For four years I held management positions at VR-1 Inc, now Circadence Corporation (a Colorado based network technology & content Company, with offices at the time in 4 countries and clients in over 20). I led a team of 10+ staff to develop and host complex Internet solutions for large travel, e commerce, publishing and entertainment clients.

Executive Director

Tourism Coromandel

November 1992 – October 1995 (3 years)

I was founding Executive Director of Tourism Coromandel; the Regional Tourism Office (Convention & Visitor Bureau) for this coastal region in the North Island of New Zealand. I launched a wide range of marketing projects for the region including help establish the visitor touring route - the 'Pacific Coast Highway'. I also led the development of 'Towards 2020' - the first regional sustainable tourism plan for a region in New Zealand.

Product Manager - Independent Travel

Newmans Tours Ltd

1990 – 1992 (2 years)

Newmans Tours was one of New Zealand and Australia's largest inbound tour companies. I was in charge of developing a range of new products focused on the rapidly growing self drive, motor home and other free & independent travel (F.I.T) segments. During my time at Newmans I was involved in the development of 'FreePlan' - an innovative F.I.T brochure product and 'Budget Breaks' which quickly grew into one of the largest self drive tourism products in the New Zealand tourism market.

Assistant to General Manager

Sky Television

Public Company; 501-1000 employees; Broadcast Media industry

1988 – 1989 (1 year)

From London, England, News Corp.'s Sky Television (part of BSkyB) launched multi-channel satellite TV onto the European market in 1989. During this start up period I was Assistant to the General Manager - involved in major supplier negotiations and site management issues as employee numbers grew to over 1000 in 4 UK locations. Later I was assigned specifically as M.I.S Manager, controlling the purchase, implementation and training for all PC's and Apple Macs.
