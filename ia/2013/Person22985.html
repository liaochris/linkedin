Gary Jesch's Summary

Live animation is still my love, after 20 years of helping large companies create unusual presentations at trade shows and corporate events. And this year will turn out to be my busiest ever, thanks to one of my new clients.

Business owners and marketing professionals get my individual care and attention when it comes to the live animation performances and now I also solve problems and help people with webcasts and webinars through my other businesses, WebinarsOnFire and CHOPS Webcasting.

This year, a new interest has appeared and it is very exciting - the ability to use live animation to help families with children who are autistic. We've come up with an idea to create a "flight simulator" for teaching parents needed coping skills in dealing with children at all different ages and points on the autism spectrum. In our new model, a parent can receive supervised training under the watchful eye of a therapist or physician while interacting with a child-like avatar. The parent can practice and experiment without ever hurting an autistic child or crashing, just as in a flight simulator.

My background is in journalism, graphic design, marketing, sales, video production, 3D animation.

My goal is to see virtual computer-generated characters contribute to society and to the well-being of people.

Specialties: Live animation, motion capture, 3D animation, writing, photography, videography, editing, marketing, website design and production, tradeshow exhibiting, stage show production, lighting and projection design, acting, audio staging, business management
