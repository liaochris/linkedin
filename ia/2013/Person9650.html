President

Halley's, Inc. / Toudanines Cleaners

January 1994 – Present (20 years)

VOTED BEST DRY CLEANERS 2005,2006,2007,2008,2009,2010,2011, 2012, 2013!

Halley's, Inc.,a corporation, has been in business 19 years having owned, built, purchased, sold and remodeled 17 dry cleaning locations in 11 cities throughout the State of Texas. I began in the business as a Counter Representative in 1994 working for Halley's Inc. which owned and operated a Comet Cleaner Franchise at that time. Eventually I made my way to General Manager over multiple locations in Dallas Forth Worth and Boerne simultaneously. After the sell of our Dallas stores, we moved to Boerne to expand our operation in the San Antonio area. In 2007 I was promoted to President of Halley's, Inc., which continued to own and operate multiple dry cleaning stores in San Antonio, Schertz and Boerne. Our location in Boerne, Texas has received the Best of the Best Award for 2006, 2007, 2008, 2009, 2010, 2011, 2012 and 2013 for Best Dry Cleaner and Best Boss. In addition to owning and operating multiple Comet Cleaners franchise locations in the Dallas Fort Worth area and Boerne, we also developed our own trademark license name in the Dallas area under the name of Toudanines Fine Dry Cleaning. We eventually sold those locations, however, they continue to operate in the DFW area. Halley's, Inc. owns the licensing rights to the name and continues to collect licensing fees from each location. In 2009 Halley's, Inc. decided it was time to end our relationship with the Comet Cleaners franchise group and introduce Toudanines Cleaners into the San Antonio, Schertz and Boerne area. Introducing Toudanines to the San Antonio and surrounding areas is long overdue and is a very exciting transition for Halley's, Inc. The Comet franchise never owned, operated or managed any of our locations. Halley's, Inc. continues to own and operate our stores as we always have over the past 19 years. Currently we have Toudanines Cleaners in Schertz, Boerne and Universal City.
