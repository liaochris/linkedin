Jack Simonian's Summary

Mergers and acquisitions (M&A) professional providing hands-on advisory services to privately held companies in the US and Canada. Expert in executing a highly efficient, confidential, and targeted approach to the marketplace, uniquely designed for each sell-side client. Multi-industry experience including manufacturing, distribution, services, technology, engineering, transportation, design/planning, architecture, construction, environmental, lighting, industrial, asset/investment recovery, petroleum, and metals market sectors.

I am currently seeking sell-side mandates for privately owned small and mid-sized companies exploring their exit strategies.
