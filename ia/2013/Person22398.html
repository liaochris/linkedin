Chase Hutchins' Summary

My name is Chase Hutchins. I am an internet junkie, with a love of all things digital and bacon-based.

My spine tingles at the thought of creating something original and effective in the same way it tingles when I hear a dirty bass line on a new dub-step track. I wish to be the best at everything I do, and strive to push myself in every project I undertake.
