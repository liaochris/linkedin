CoffeeTable (Real Valuable Corporation)

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Retail industry

April 2011 – July 2012 (1 year 4 months) San Francisco

CoffeeTable is a catalog application for the iPad. Hundreds of retailers participate allowing shoppers to view multiple catalogs from a single app. Shoppers benefit by having a green alternative to receiving catalogs. Retailers gain visibility into never before seen shopper catalog behavior. These analytics provide retailers with insights that can impact product related business decisions. CoffeeTable closed a $3.5M funding round in early 2012 led by RR Donnelley, the largest provider of print catalog services to retailers.

As CoffeeTable's first non-technical hire, my responsibilities included anything that needed to be done. Some highlights include working closely with the development team, technology implementation strategies, retailer relationship, service vendor relationships, founders, hiring, contracts, monetization strategies and the list goes on and on. Post-funding, my principal role transitioned to sales and managing the retailer relationships.

I'm very proud to say that our lean team, and our product resulted in over 130 retailers' participation which continues to grow today. CoffeeTable is a top-ranked iPad application in Apple's app store.
