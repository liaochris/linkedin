Jason Zan's Summary

Over 15 years of investing, corporate operating, and startup experience. In my last role, I was responsible for sourcing, evaluating, and actively managing investments for Rogers Ventures where I sat on the Board of Scribble Technologies and was the Board Observer for Payfone and Gridcentric.

Prior to joining Rogers Ventures, I held a variety of senior level corporate and business development roles at Rogers Communications. During my tenure, I was responsible for launching Zoocasa.com, restructuring and re-financing Rogers Medical Solutions (acquired by Thomson), developing Rogers initial IPTV strategy, and negotiating the VOD rights with first tier movie studios (Universal, Sony, Warner, and Disney).

In addition to my operating roles, I was responsible for Rogers private equity portfolio. Investments included MetaTV (acquired by Cox Communications), Equator Technologies (acquired by Pixelworks), Coaxmedia (acquired by Continental Wireless), SmartSync (acquired by Itron).

I was also CEO and co-founder of Tokiva Technologies a mobile VoIP app, where I grew the business to over 1 million users in under 12 months and was part of the founding team at iMoney, Canada’s first financial products e-commerce site (acquired by Quebecor).

In my off time, I am a founding member of Palindrome Advisors and avid performer of the Circus Arts.

Specialties

Investment Due Diligence, Financial Modeling and Analysis, Fundraising, Internet Technology, Digital Advertising/Media, Wireline and Wireless Platforms. Business Plan Development. Pitch Deck Development.
