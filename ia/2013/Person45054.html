Flo Oswald's Summary

Accomplished leader in all aspects of organization management, program management, training management, human capital development, budget management, information & data governance management, and customer service. Demonstrated strong productivity and performance management skills in a fast-paced environment. Highly organized, with strong analytical skills and a positive mindset.

Highlights:
• Customer Support: I listen to my customers, understand challenges they face and seek the best solution. I establish a customer-centered culture, turning support reps into customer advocates driven by customer satisfaction and customer loyalty
• Training Programs: I enjoy driving any format of knowledge sharing, animate interactive classes and can address large audiences, with techies or non-technical people. I am experienced in designing and delivering various formats of training programs for clients, employees and partners. I measure success and efficiency of my programs with surveys, incorporating feedback to turn good training programs into great ones
• Business Intelligence: my passion for making sense of data and using the right BI tool for the right person led me to become a BI evangelist and grow BI teams
• Data Governance: my interest for Data Governance comes from my BI passion combined with my organizational skills and for being process oriented. I work collaboratively across functions with stakeholders to establish, document and improve data-related standards, processes and procedures
• Leadership: I create and establish leadership, and strive to motivate teams by staying close to them, developing a strong team spirit, and leading by example
• Consistency: I handle all requests thoroughly and follow through until closure. I consistently deliver high quality results. I pay attention to details and produce useful operational documentations

Specialties: BI strategic leadership, Data Governance, Customer Advocacy, Team Building, Training and Certification Programs
