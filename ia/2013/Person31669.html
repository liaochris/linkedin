President and CEO

Phoenix Capital, LLC

November 2002 – Present (10 years 10 months)

A merchant banking company that specializes in identifying, relisting and completing reverse mergers for “abandoned” publicly quoted shell companies.

This typically involves:

• negotiating with existing shareholders to obtain control of an “abandoned” shell company;

• appointing a new board of directors;

• drafting audited financial accounts and SEC filings for multiple prior years including SEC forms 10SB, 10Q, 10K, 8K and Schedule 14;

• liaising with auditors and attorneys to file multiple SEC forms on a cost effective and timely basis;

• filing a Form 15 C 2 11 to re-list the Company on the Over the Counter Bulletin Board and the Pink Sheets;

• maintaining a Company’s full SEC reporting status on an ongoing basis once it has been relisted;

• negotiating settlements with the Company’s outstanding creditors;

• negotiating and completing sale of the “clean” publicly quoted shell company for use as a reverse merger vehicle.
