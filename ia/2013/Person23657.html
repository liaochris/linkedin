Chris Rasmussen, MD, MS's Summary

Currently I am passionately writing a book on preventive medicine, health, and wellness. I consider myself a kind of guru of health and fitness after having healed myself of heart disease. I am about 1000 pages into the book with plans to publish in 2014.

It is the definitive text on health maintenance and recovery. An essential read in today's toxic environment. The book covers popular diets, exercise, mental emotional healing, industrial toxicity, heavy metals, vaccines, fluoride, aluminum, alternative medicine, the dangers of medications like the SSRI's, atypical antipsychotics, and statins, curing heart disease (I use myself as an example) and many other so called diseases of affluence like stroke, and diabesity.

It is a book on lifestyle change-like having your own personal lifestyle coach at your fingertips. Our modern diseases have a common origin: INFLAMMATION- microglial, endothelial and adipocytal. They're the cause, I'm the cure.

I believe I have hit my stride with the awareness obtained from four years of training in Traditional Chinese Medicine and master’s degree certification combined with my teaching skills in the basic biological and medical sciences such as biology, pathophysiology, pharmacology, and anatomy.

My knowledge encompasses both western and eastern medical models with over 20 years of clinical experience in anesthesiology, pain management, internal medicine, fitness and nutrition, Offer advice for patients with chronic inflammatory diseases for which mainstream medicine sometimes offers little hope, teach students eager to learn what medical conditions are important to know in a common acupuncture practice.

My upcoming book: InflaNATION, The Industrial Diner and a Doc in the Box cites hundreds of research papers and dozens of books for a clear treatise on the causes and cures for our 21st century diseases. Due summer of 2014. Please go to inflaNATION.com and read my weekly blog posts which reveal portions of my upcoming book!
