  • Bilka.dk - Sitecore implementation

    • August 2010 to December 2010
    Team Members: Anders Dreyer, Lars Due Nielsen, Paul Skarregaard, Klaus Petersen, Jens Winther Kristensen, Hans Peter Vith

    Bilka was looking at a total transition of their website which had previously been centered around the physical stores, with e-commerce only residing on the front page as a Flash carrousel.

    The project involved developing a new information architecture, a new design and implementing this on a Sitecore 6 CMS platform, with interfaces to the existing MS Commerce/BizzTalk systems.

    Klean handled the implementation process and by gathering a team of the best Sitecore freelance resources combined with a Klean Scrum Master, we where able to complete the project in an amazing three months. The project was an immediate success as bilka.dk doubled their earnings from day one the new website was launched.
