Kristy Kosak's Summary

Passionate about helping non-profits create positive change through the power of technology, I am continually seeking out new ways for organizations to exceed their goals through social media. My background in sociology and customer service has helped me transition into the fast-paced role as a community manager at a non-profit digital marketing agency. I work with clients large and small to make the most out of the ever-changing world of digital media: hosting live Twitter events, building Facebook pages and audiences, Facebook ad management and optimization, and increasing engagement through a data centric approach. Known for extreme brainstorming and whiteboard word bubbles, I am more than willing to tackle any challenges with individual resourcefulness. I feel fortunate to be on the front lines of innovation in social media and look forward to applying that knowledge in the future.

When I am not working, I enjoy the outdoors, love to bake, and have a healthy obsession with tea.
