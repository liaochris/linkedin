Jeff Tippett's Summary

I am the Senior Marketing Manager at The Publicus Community, a branding agency helping boutique, affluent companies reach their revenue goals through marketing, public relations, and advertising.

I’ve worked in a variety of industries including exclusive real estate, resorts, government, biotech/life science, political campaigns, education, and hospitality.

I remain active in the community through board positions with The Greater Raleigh Chamber of Commerce, The Greater Raleigh Merchants Association, Habitat for Humanity ReStore, and StepUp. I also serves on the City of Raleigh Communications Committee and Transportation Bond Committee.

And I spend my free time directing the contemporary music program at Saint Andrews Presbyterian Church, running, working out, cycling, and being a father to his three energetic kids.
