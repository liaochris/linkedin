David Abbet's Summary

A pragmatic visionary focused on crafting design strategies that result in immersive user and brand experiences across all digital touchpoints.

David Abbet is an award-winning Interactive Creative Director / Art Director, User Experience designer, consultant and entrepreneur.

David grew up in the French-speaking part of Switzerland and currently lives in Boston, where he works as the Director of Design at Optaros, a full service digital commerce agency.

Over the past decade, David has created successful web experiences for a wide range of clients, from innovative startups to Fortune 500 and IR 500 companies. Some of his clients include: Activision-Blizzard, Chopard, Merck Serono, Nespresso, Rue La La, Swisscom, Walmart, the World Health Organization, and many others.
