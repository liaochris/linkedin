Jeremy Lange's Summary

I currently work as an IT System Engineer III with Montgomery County Public Schools. I have over 15 years of Information Technology experience as well as a Master’s Degree in Information Assurance. I currently specialize in network/systems support and IT based physical security projects in addition to 2nd level network/systems support.

With regards to network /system support; I am a Microsoft Certified Systems Engineer (MCSE) 2003 with Security and I am very familiar with all versions of the Microsoft OS. I also have Server+, Network+, and A+ certifications. Furthermore, I have been a team leader during technology modernization projects and coordinated the upgrade of servers, computers, and infrastructure. The certifications I have obtained related to systems support show a knowledge of IT based in hardware, networking, and systems which provides for a complete overview of technology from the ground up.

With regards to IT security experience; I obtained the Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH) certification and the Security+ certification and therefore meet the requirements for the DOD 8570 Directive. Furthermore, I am currently studying for the Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP).
