Distinguished Publishing Company

2012 – 2013 (1 year) Las Vegas, Nevada Area

For over twenty years Carole Bellmyre, founder of Distinguished Publishing Company, has honored Nevada women & men with the recognition they deserve for their vision and leadership and their contributions to our state. Established in 1989, the Distinguished Women & Men in Nevada pictorial-biographical books have profiled outstanding Nevadans in hundreds of professions. These award-winning books are considered in our state to be one of the most influential reference directories.

These elegant hard-cover books include the portrait, biographical data and back-ground information about each man and woman and their business or profession as a distinguished person. The directory further develops the unlimited resources of Nevada women and men from all fields of endeavor, including business, science, education, medicine, law, government and the arts. Each person must be nominated to be included in the directory and the criteria are established by their business and/or civic achievements. No fee is required to be included.

These award-winning books are placed in the Nevada Archives and bring a wealth of historical value to the future generations of our state as a pictorial and written record. The books are used throughout the state in corporate, business, medical and law offices, university and college libraries, public and private libraries, government offices, the Nevada State Library and the Library of Congress in Washington, D.C.

The biographies of accomplished Nevadans profiled highlight the strength and commitment of men & women throughout Nevada, people who have made contributions in their work and community and through their leadership benefit others.

We salute the distinguished men and women included in each directory for they will lead the progress of our Nevada communities and we thank them for their continued support.
