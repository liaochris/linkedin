Carol Kleinman's Summary

I am an artist and photographer. For the past 20 years, I have specialized in photographing reflections. All my images are single exposures, not composites. What appear to be layers and multiple images are actually single reflections. I print my work on canvas, blurring the line between photography and painting.

My work has been exhibited in Los Angeles, Seattle, Washington D.C. and Moscow (Russia).

You can view some of my work and a video interview at www.kleinmanart.com

Current show: TAG Gallery, Bergamot Station, Santa Monica - October 29 to November 23 2013
