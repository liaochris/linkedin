Greg Stuart's Summary

Digital media General Manager and Marketing Expert – Built Digital Media & Digital Ad Technology businesses since 1993 with successful acquisitions & an IPO.

Angel Investor & Board Member - 7 company exits for over $2.0 billion, many not less 10x revenues. Transacted value of portfolio likely to be $2.8B.

Recognized thought-leader in digital media & advertising. Identified by Ad Age as "1 of 10 Who Made Their Mark” in '06 (along side Jay-Z & Chad Hurley).

Currently, Global CEO of Mobile Marketing Association serving Pandora, Weather Co., Coke, etc. Grew org +250% in 2.5 years. Former CEO of Interactive Advertising Bureau (IAB), serving AOL, CNET, Google, Yahoo! & others. Led industry from $6 to $17 billion, while growing IABs revenues +500% in 4 yrs. Both were turn-arounds of troubled orgs.

Ad Age identified book, “What Sticks: Why Most Advertising Fails and How to Guarantee Yours Succeeds,” as “#1 book you should have read".

Additional role highlights:
* VP Biz Dev/Ad Sales & Marketing at Cars.com, drove site to #1 six mos. post launch
* VP BD at Flycast Networks, which IPO'd & sold to CMGI/Engage for $750M
* Former Venture Partner/EIR & CEO of Ad Technology companies
* Senior exec & GM within major NYC Advertising Agencies

Current BOD member of Livingly Media, backed by Menlo Ventures
Former BOD of Rapt in SF, sold to Microsoft
Former BOD of Allyes in China, sold to Focus Media

Angel Investor & advisor for 15+ venture-backed companies (KPCB, Softbank, Conway, TimeWarner, Eisner, Greylock, Spark, Intel, DFJ, USVP, Greycroft, Union Square Ventures, First Round, Oak Ventures, Canaan, etc.). Return on Invested Capital of 1600% to date.

Member National Speakers Association & global speaker on advertising & digital media - Istanbul, Israel, Germany, Mexico City, Jakarta, Sao Paulo, Switzerland, Zurich, Shanghai, Sydney, Barcelona, Tokyo, Buenos Aires & others.

Lives happily near New York City in Bridgehampton, NY with wife Pamela, twin daughters & son.

Specialties
