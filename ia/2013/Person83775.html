Lynn Louise Wonders, MA, LPC, RPT-S's Summary

As a Licensed Professional Counselor, I blend traditional methods of psychotherapy with a variety of research based, effective techniques to create customized plans for recovery, healing and growth for all of my clients. On a foundation of the philosophy and practice of mindfulness, I help people on the journey of self discovery, healing, growing through life-changes.

As a Registered Play Therapist- Supervisor, I have over ten years of rich experience providing play therapy services to children, parenting support and now I train and supervise therapists who are on track to become play therapists themselves. I provide continuing education training for preschool teachers and I provide observations and consultations in the preschool setting, specializing in the emotional/social development of children ages of 2-5.

With 20 years of experience in the practice of mindfulness, meditation, tai chi and yoga, I bring in only the most grounded and established elements of these practices to support people in learning how to feel more connected to the physiological cues the body provides and train the mind and body to work together to achieve deeper and more fulfilling states of calm and focus in daily life.

I believe clients and students achieve greater balance emotionally when they attend to healthy body and mind. I promote healthy nutritional focus, attention to physical fitness, healthy sleep routines and expanded awareness to thoughts and beliefs. The term “holistic” has been a bit over-used and misunderstood these past ten years. I do believe, however, the basis for this term aptly describes the work that I do in that the research I have accumulated and operate from affirms we must attend to all aspects of the whole person in order to have balance.

Owner, Therapist, Yoga & Meditation Instructor, Trainer

Wonders Counseling Services, LLC

June 2012 – Present (1 year 7 months) Marietta, GA

Wonders Counseling Services, LLC is a warm and home-like office and studio space where I provide professional counseling, workshops, online courses and professional development support in the heart of East Cobb near Sandy Springs, Roswell, Marietta. I established Wonders Counseling Services as my own solo private practice after ten years of experience working in early child development centers with children and parents and then owning and managing a full service counseling center and yoga studio in Marietta.

Rooted in a mind-body focus, all of my services are provided on an understanding that people are intellectual, emotional and physical beings and have a universal need for connection to self and others. I believe that when people become discouraged they lose ability to function optimally, so much of my work is centered around assisting people toward changes in behavior and thoughts in an optimistic way. All services are backed by substantial research including the extensive research and work of John Gottman, PhD, Daniel Golemen, PhD and Jon Kabbat-Zinn, PhD.
