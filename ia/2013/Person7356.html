Andy Boyns' Summary

Andy is a British voice actor, on-camera actor, presenter, and producer originally from London, England. Currently based in Istanbul, Turkey. http://andyboyns.com/

As a voice artist, his voice literally flies the world on inflight documentaries, and can be heard on countless corporate videos, documentaries, commercials, and other projects. Want your brand to sound smart, sincere, prestigious or authoritative? The British voice of Andy Boyns is articulate, believable and smooth. Other specialties include technical narration, eLearning projects and apps for tablets and phones. He also recently dubbed parts for the cinema film, "Çanakale Yolun Sonu".

In his role as an actor and on-camera presenter, Andy will make a guest appearance in the award winning Turkish comedy series "Seksenler" at the beginning of April 2013. He has also performed in TV adverts, and recently presented a series of informational videos for Microsoft's new Windows 8 platform.

With a number of production projects currently germinating (watch for news), Andy was able to combine all of these facets while at 6News in 2011 writing, producing and presenting a series of mini documentaries "Istanbul Interludes".

As a presenter at the VOICE 2012 conference in Los Angeles, Andy's passion for the international community came to the fore with his "International Voiceovers" panel. He also took this opportunity to develop his website seres "The Person Behind the Voice", a resource which seeks to learn what inspires, challenges, or motivates the amazing talents which drive the media industry.

He is married and a proud father.
