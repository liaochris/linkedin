Diane Gracely's Summary

I have over 16 years in the online marketing industry. I am a successful entrepreneur having created two home businesses over the years and growing them into successful businesses and then selling them for a profit.

I am in the process of starting my second golf business. My "Grand Opening" is set for February 8, 2014. Just in time for the 2014 golf season. www.dianesgolfheadcovers.com

I market and promote my husband's business, Keith's Custom Made Shop. He makes and sells survival bracelets, monkey fists, key rings, whips, and motorcycle grips with tassels made out of paracord. He also designs custom made wooden products made on a wood lathe such as bowls, vase style cremation urns that we personalize for people and pets, round birdhouses, UNIQUE home decor, and more. www.keithgracely.com

My GOAL and MISSION is to grow our businesses into highly profitable businesses just like I did my first two home businesses that I sold for a nice profit.

My Specialties: online marketing, education and training about how to start a home business, knitting, woodturning on a wood lathe, creating, growing, and selling home businesses.
