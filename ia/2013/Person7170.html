Andrew Rhee's Summary

As founder of Shield For Men, Andrew Rhee oversees the strategic direction and operations of Shield For Men and serves as the chief visionary. Enforcer of the company’s cultural values, he strives to make the workplace an environment that is both professionally and personally rewarding for all employees. Andrew encourages collaboration with the entire team to ensure that the company’s vision is executed at the heart of all the companies programs, products and services that are released to the world. As the chief visionary, he always strives to promote a culture that reflects the organization’s core values. Andrew was born and raised in Los Angeles, CA and graduated from University of California, Davis with a Bachelors Degree in Organizational Studies.
