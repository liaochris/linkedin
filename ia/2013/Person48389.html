Gigi Frias' Summary

Gigi Frias is an award-winning, multi-disciplinary designer with strong research and strategic-thinking skills. Gigi has worked at several marketing and communications firms in the D.C. Metro area serving a varied clientele that includes local non-profit organizations and international corporations.

In her roles as graphic designer and art director, Gigi has worked on a variety of print, interactive, and web design projects and has managed many of these from concept to production. Her strong copywriting and editing skills which allow her to provide more comprehensive services especially when coupled with her passion for good typography.

As a member of AIGA, Gigi traveled to China with its Center for Cross-Cultural Design where she attended an international design conference sponsored by ICOGRADA. Subsequently, her work was recognized and published in Package & Design magazine, the leading international design publication based in Guanzhou, China.

Specialties

Print, web, identity and interactive design.
Portrait and journalistic photography
