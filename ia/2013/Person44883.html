Rich Davis' Summary

Rich works with job seekers, employers, schools, and community support organizations and creates a comfortable fit in the workplace for everyone using Workforce Development strategies. As a certified Workforce Development Professional, he has helped hundreds of people that needed personalized support find jobs and employers hire qualified employees. Rich knows that being comfortable at work leads to increased productivity, profits, and the security of a stable work environment.

Rich is now launching Career Comfort Coaching, a new virtual coaching model that uses the Harrison Assessment to help Job Seekers discover their suitability and eligibility for jobs. Every client identifies their Comfort Job, their Comfort Companies, and develops a strategy to enjoy a comfortable fit in the workplace.

He has served as Chair of the Employment Committee for the Mayor's Commission on People with Disabilities in Philadelphia and on the Workforce Investment Board in Montgomery County, PA. He belongs to the PennSuburban Chamber of Commerce, Greater Valley Forge Human Resource Association (SHRM), and the National Association of Workforce Development Professionals (NAWDP)

I am a Workforce Development/Human Resource professional dedicated to creating strong workplaces and communities where everyone fits comfortably together.
My goal is for every employee to be comfortable in their job. I believe that comfortable fit is the key to a productive worker and that a healthy work environment is where everyone understands each other, enjoys working together, functions at their maximum potential, and is productive at work. I consistently strive to establish the “Connections that FIT” for my customers.
