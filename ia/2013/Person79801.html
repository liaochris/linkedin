Lee E. Scharfstein's Summary

A veteran producer and multi-platform content creator with high-level expertise in live-action, motion graphics/vfx and post-production. In my 13+ years in the industry I built a solid foundation in the trenches of production and honed my creative & brand management skills with some of the best and brightest integrated agencies.

Specializing in large-scale content objectives I have a proven track record of delivering under budget and on schedule in combination with the ability to develop creative and execution strategies while addressing client/brand directives in both the traditional and new media landscapes.

As an executive producer I am well versed in managing, communicating and collaborating with creatives, account teams and executives alike. Essential to this role is the cultivating and curating top-level creative talent and (new media) technology partners, which makes for high-quality, timely and efficient responses to any challenge issued in an RFP.

Specialties: Digital/Interactive/New Media Content - Creative Direction - Live Action for Design/Animation/VFX - New Media & Mobile Technology Integration - Large-scale Project Strategy & Management
