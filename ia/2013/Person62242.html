Jeremiah Doornbos' Summary

Jeremy is a designer specializing in architecture, interior design and graphics. With more than 11 years of experience, his work includes a variety of projects located in the United States and Asia. His design approach is one that combines conceptual ideas with physical solutions that embody sustainable strategies. Jeremy is particularly interested in the design and problem solving process and often incorporates design thinking methods in his work flow. In the past several years Jeremy has worked on a number of projects which he has implemented his conceptual approach, including Fillmore Place Retail in Denver, Colorado; Central Park Tower in Broomfield, Colorado; and the Student Activities Building on the campus of The University of Michigan in Ann Arbor.
