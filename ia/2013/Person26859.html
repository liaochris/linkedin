Carla Dewing's Summary

My name is Carla Dewing and I built Contrast Media in 2009 with my partner Gill. As an author, poet and international playwright I have streamlined my business to include the latest web 2.0 copywriting techniques for a demanding market. I am also part-owner and creator of the Copyhog © brand and Copyhog.com.

Together Gill and I grew Contrast Media around two fundamental concepts:

1.) The Copyhog writers are media specialists who can write for any multimedia platform online and offline.
2.) We use a delicate combination of creativity and market relevance to sharpen each project until it is not only enjoyable to read, but prepared for whatever competitive media it will go up against.

That’s why our slogan is –
Your creatively charged market driven media business!

We run a business dedicated to writing excellence, mass market appeal and entertainment, but most of all, dedicated to YOU. Our Copyhog writers will revolutionize the way you do business on and offline by offering you a complete A-Z copy service for all of your web 2.0 copywriting needs.

The Contrast Media offices are situated minutes from the pristine beaches of Blouberg where the famous Table Mountain towers over Cape Town, and is a constant source of inspiration to all of our writers.

Web 2.0 Copywriting at its finest:

With our proven copywriting ability and list of influential clients, we can proudly say that our content is not only entertaining, but engages the reader on an emotional level.

Our interactive web 2.0 copywriting is based on advanced quantum linguistics, a definitive and highly specialized branch of neurolinguistic programming that establishes a bond between the reader and the written content.

This in turn grabs the reader’s attention, compels them to read more and influences them to click, sign up or buy.

You want results. We got them!
