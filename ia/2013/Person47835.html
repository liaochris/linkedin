George Anders' Experience

Contributing writer

Forbes Magazine

Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Publishing industry

January 2012 – Present (2 years) San Francisco

Catch my work on the cover of Forbes magazine. Landmark features in recent months have included cover stories on Hewlett-Packard's turnaround, Amazon.com's customer-centric culture, and the money-making machine that is LinkedIn. I'm also writing regularly on the web for forbes.com about innovation, talent and recruiting. Those articles draw on themes from my 2011 book "The Rare Find."

Public Speaker

BrightSight Group

January 2012 – Present (2 years) San Francisco Bay Area

What are the big new ideas that can help organizations innovate faster and navigate better through changing times? How can high achievers make the most of the "soft skills" that determine our destinieis? As an author and journalist, I'm constantly exploring these topics. On the speaking circuit, I share best practices and cautionary tales in ways that match each audience's needs.

Since 2011, I have been a featured speaker at corporate events in the U.S. and Europe. (Notable examples include Google, Microsoft, Rolls-Royce, Royal Bank of Scotland and Pitney-Bowes. I also have addressed trade associations, financial partners' conferences and academic/business programs. Topics include social media and the future of work; venues have ranged from California to London, Peru, Texas and Florida.

BrightSight Group in Princeton, N.J., coordinates all speaking appearances. For more information, contact BrightSight's founder, Tom Neilssen and his colleagues via this link: http://www.brightsightgroup.net/2011/01/george-anders.html

Author

Portfolio

Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; Publishing industry

April 2009 – Present (4 years 9 months)

I've published three books with Portfolio, including a new e-book called "Becoming a Rare Find: How Jagged Resumes Lead to Great Jobs." This book provides career strategies for current or recent college graduates. It's available via iTunes, the Amazon Kindle store and other digital book vendors.

Various chapters explain how to crack the hidden job market, how to be seen as a quick learner, and how to make the most of social media. More information is at www.georgeandersbooks.com

George Anders' Publications

  • Becoming a Rare Find

    • May 2012
    Authors: George Anders

    A new e-book that shows how jagged resumes can lead to great careers. It spells out job hunting strategies that can help candidates stand out in a crowded and chaotic labor market.

  • The Rare Find

    • Penguin/Portfolio
    • October 2011
    Authors: George Anders

  • Perfect Enough: Carly Fiorina and the Reinvention of Hewlett-Packard

    • Penguin/Portfolio
    • February 2003
    Authors: George Anders

    An up-close look at Hewlett-Packard during one of its greatest periods of turbulence -- focusing on the struggles between CEO Carly Fiorina and the company's founding families about HP's destiny. This book became a New York Times bestseller.

  • Health Against Wealth: HMOs and the Breakdown of Medical Trust

    • Houghton-Mifflin
    • November 1996
    Authors: George Anders

    This investigative book chronicled the problems and abuses associated with the rapid rise of managed care in the U.S. during the 1990s. The Journal of the American Medical Association called this book "a must read."

  • Merchants of Debt: KKR and the Mortgaging of U.S. Business

    • Basic Books
    • April 1992
    Authors: George Anders

    This long-ago look at the rise of private equity and LBOs in the American economy has become a minor business classic -- still in print in the U.S. ... available in multiple foreign languages ... and about to appear in a new Chinese-language edition.
