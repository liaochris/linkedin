Eric Tiedeken's Summary

I started getting involved with computers in High School as a web developer and then started getting into flash. After High School I became interested in Game Development and became highly interested in texture creation. I can now created 3D models, unwrap and texture them. I keep pushing myself to create higher quality projects in a shorter time period.

My first job in the game field industry was as a QA tester for THQ. For the short time I was there I was promoted to QA lead. Not only did I do QA work but I also set up steam depots for steam games, Disc set up for retail games, and I also created game play video for retail presentations and marketing events.

I consider myself to be a workaholic.
