Jenni Bush's Summary

Jennifer Bush is the creative director behind Bespoken For. Jenni has a real passion for offering brides more than just jewellery for their wedding day with a personal apporach and a customer focussed design ethic, each piece is tailor made to the brides exact requirements.

Our Philosophy is:

"Bespoke isn't about design it's about you"

We really feel that the more time and effort and love we put in the more valuable a piece will be.

Since Jenni began bespoken for her designs have been made for brides across the globe and featured regularly in national press.

We listen to our brides, we take time to assess their needs and help them to make the right accessory choices.


Why are we different?

Experts, we are bespoke experts, we are more than just jewellery makers, we specialise in different.

Creative, we will always push our design skills to the limit and try new things to make that extra special touches.

Inspiring, we are always finding inspiration, even when others see nothing.

We work around you, our consultations and designs are at times and using methods which make it easier for you.
