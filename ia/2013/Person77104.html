Paragon Moon is a brand identity design studio in Orange County, California. We specialize in graphic design and brand development for heart-centered, meaningful brands.

Our mission is to replace fragmented and disorganized business graphics with a consistent and cohesive brand identity — one that is recognizable, memorable, ignites intrigue, communicates your brand’s distinct values and compels sales.

What you can expect:

♦ Unify your company’s look and feel
♦ Position yourself as a professional in your industry
♦ Differentiate from your competition
♦ Provide clear identification of your business and brand
♦ Create internal focus and direction
♦ Increase business and brand awareness

Our philosophy on branding:

Translating your brand’s core essence – its soul, if you will – into an image that resonates, connects and accurately represents your brand is at the heart of how we develop a strong, effective and timeless logo. Thus, we begin all design projects with the brand’s purpose in mind first:

• What Purpose, Value and Principles make up the foundation of the brand?
• What is the desired tonality of the brand?
• What are the brand’s distinct characteristics?
• With whom does the brand desire to connect?
• What does the brand desire to be famous for?
• What is the brand’s promise?

When a brand is created with this principles in mind, the end result is a logo that is memorable, recognizable, can be applied to virtually any medium and – most importantly – reflects your brand’s tonality and ignites chemistry.
