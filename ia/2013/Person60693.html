Jedd Chevrier's Summary

I specialize in the visual development of intellectual properties. With a primary focus on video game, film I have also worked in television and advertising. I am well versed in both 2d and 3d visual development and can provide everything from a simple concept sketch and fully-rendered image to high-res 3D sculpts, utilized for pre-visualization and production.

Specialties

Environment and character design - concept art. Pre-visualization.
