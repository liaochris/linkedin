Michael Marroccoli's Summary

Over 30 years of experience in the property & casualty insurance industry serving wholesale-distributors, retailers, real estate management groups, professional service organizations, non profit organizations, and the public transportation industry. My extensive list of Public transportation clients include Charter Bus companies, School Bus operators, and some of the largest Limousine companies in the nation. Our transportation insurance practice also includes a specialty in Medical Transportation, Paratransit, and insurance programs for Ambulance Services throughout the Northeast.
I provide comprehensive insurance coverage to clients in more than 40 states.

Specialties: Innovative insurance solutions for Public Transportation companies throughout the US, and the Real Estate industry. Areas of expertise include Executive Protection; Errors & Omissions, and Employment Practices Liablity Insurance. Our clientele has also benefitted from the advice of our team of specialists in our Group Benefits Division. Health Care Reform has been a source of confusion and mis-information for many business owners. Our dedicated team has been able to explain the compliance requirements of Health Care Reform, and provide cost effective solutions to assist our clients in controlling employee health insurance costs.
Personal Insurance: As one of the largest Chubb Ins. Co. agents in the country, we specialize in the personal insurance needs of our commercial clients' owners and senior management teams. We offer comprehensive insurance solutions to protect upscale homes and personal assets from the the leading insurance companies in this market; including Chubb, Fireman's Fund, AIG, and Pure.
