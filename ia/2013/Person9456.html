Amanda Hartman's Summary

My current interests lie with digital preservation and access to special collections.

I am also interested in emerging technologies and how they impact communication, collaboration and education. I'm particularly interested in how to best teach aspects of technology and information literacy to undergraduate and graduate students at the college level.

Specialties

Teaching and presenting to students, faculty and staff.

XHTML, CSS, web and graphic design (DreamWeaver, Photoshop, Fireworks, InDesign), Microsoft Office. Designing websites for accessibility and usability. Web and Library 2.0 applications such as blogs, twitter, social networking and social bookmarking.
