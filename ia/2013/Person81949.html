Liz O'Byrne's Summary

I am a Voice Over Artist and I provide quality narrations that help businesses and organizations sound more professional. I have extensive training with Edge Studio in New York City and Fairfield Connecticut and Top Shelf Voiceover Studio in Milford Connecticut.

I work part time at Tauck which is a tour company located in Norwalk Connecticut.

Extensive years of experience in a corporate environment. Most recently, I was a Process Improvement Specialist with GE Money. I worked with a third party vendor to create standard and repeatable processes for product upgrades.

Prior to working for GE, I spent 15 years at General Reinsurance Corporation in a variety of roles such as: Payroll Specialist, Client Based System Administrator, Senior Claims Representative, Account Manager and Contract Specialist.

Specialties: Project Management, Process Improvements, Documentation, Creating a Compliance Program Management Office, Facilitation and Vendor Relationships
