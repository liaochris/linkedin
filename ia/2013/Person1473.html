adam j. richman's Summary

L.A. and O.C. based cinematographer and graphic designer with experience in 35mm, 16mm, RED One 4k, HD, and HDSLR. Story first, bottom line. Stay one frame ahead of your audience. Owner/operator of a complete Canon 7D high definition camera package (details at adamjrichman.com). Success and goal oriented in both the long and short term. Very well organized and extremely detail conscious. Largely creative, technically proficient, mildy eccentric, casually extroverted, and overly charming.
