Christine Castigliano's Summary

After 30 years in media, from feature films to magazines, newspapers, websites, e-Learning, fiction and self-publishing, I believe in Story. Clarity. Alchemy. Beauty.

I uncover the jewels at the heart of a story, organization, or individual, and magnify that brilliance so it can shine in the world. I call it SPARK, and all my work is about that.

Spark for NonProfits & Business: I support your brilliant project with branding, graphics, writing & web presence. I ask good questions; create compelling and elegant work to engage, teach and inspire others.

Spark for Adults: I teach self-awareness skills via interactive workshops, mentoring and performance. I draw upon a wide range of creative tools: writing, movement, song, storytelling, drama, ritual, and deep listening. I want to ignite your creative Spark and teach you to work with inner blocks so you are empowered to share your gifts.

Spark for Kids & Community: I co-lead SpiritWind, providing support and ritual experiences to prepare young women for initiatory rites of passage. I also co-lead a Teen Circle for girls.

Specialties

Creative Facilitation, Teaching, Mentoring, Multimedia, UX (User Experience), Information Architecture, Instructional Design, User Interface Design, Storyboarding, Scriptwriting, Flash Animation, Web Development, Video expertise.
