Andrea Johnson's Summary

Location photography and video in a storytelling style -

I am a Portland, Oregon based photographer, video/multimedia producer, and writer specializing in the wine, food, spirits, and adventure travel industries. I travel worldwide for corporate, editorial, advertising, and book projects.

Additionally, I specialize in creating custom image libraries for commercial clients, combining my strengths for storytelling and project management. I photograph a range of subjects over an extended time period, working with my clients to effectively communicate their unique message.

I have photographed three wine books: Passion for Pinot (Ten Speed Press, 2008), Essential Wines and Wineries of the Pacific Northwest (Workman/ Timber Press 2010) with author Cole Danehower, focusing on Oregon, Washington, Idaho, and Canada, and Spectacular Washington Wineries (Panache, summer 2012). My photographs have appeared in all the major wine publications and lifestyle magazines, please see tearsheets for examples.

Specialties

photography, video, multimedia, journalism, wine, travel, food

  • Passion for Pinot

    • Ten Speed Press
    • February 2009
    Authors: Andrea Johnson, Robert Holmes, Jordan Mackay

    A portrait of the most fashionable grape in the wine world, pairing vivid narrative and stunning photography to showcase top Pinot producers in California and Oregon. The only fully illustrated book on American Pinot Noir. In Passion for Pinot award-winning photographers Robert Holmes and Andrea Johnson, and wine writer Jordan Mackay capture the grape's allure through distinctive portraits and profiles of the top Pinot producers and terroirs on the West Coast. Mackay's engaging narrative chronicles the different stylistic approaches to growing and vinifying Pinot and tells the story of the dedicated winemakers responsible for elevating the grape to its exalted status. The evocative photography of Holmes and Johnson showcases the many landscapes, seasons, and faces of Pinot Noir in America.

  • Essential Wines and Wineries of the Pacific Northwest

    • Timber Press/ Workman
    • September 2010
    Authors: Andrea Johnson, Cole Danehower

    This definitive volume profiles the wines, the people who make them, and the wine countries of Washington, Oregon, British Columbia, and Idaho. Beautifully illustrated with photographs and helpful maps, this in-depth guide is a milestone in the North American literature on wine. It will enable wine lovers everywhere to plan their touring, select their wines, and explore and discover the riches of the Northwest's wine country.
