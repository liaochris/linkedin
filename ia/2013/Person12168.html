bassy bob brockman's Summary

"Bassy" Bob Brockmann is a veteran of the "bizz" as it is known, has mixed over 2000 singles, and his records have sold in excess of 50 million units world wide. His work is easily recognizable for a naturalistic approach to mixing, where the lead vocal is king.
Bassy has been responsible for seminal records for artists like Christina Aguilera, Biggie Smalls, P diddy, Babyface, Brian Mcnight, Herbie Hancock, the Fugees, Santana, and Sting. Currently Bassy is building an online network that will feature documentary format music shows and other content that is close to his heart. In addition he has started a webapp company called APPNATION.

Specialties

Bassy Bob is one of the best music mixers in the country , with well over 30 Grammy nominations, 2 wins, and an Oscar Nom for Faith hill's "there you'll be" for the film Pearl Harbor. He is also proficient in many areas of media , including animation, HD film production, mixing for film and tv, and developing content for the web.
