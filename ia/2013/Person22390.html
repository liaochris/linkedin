Chase Courington's Summary

Versatile front-end web developer with an eye for design and empathy for the user. With a liberal arts education in social science, graphic design background and passion for UX and IxD, I have somewhat of an identity crisis; torn between coding and designing. I seek out teams and projects where I can directly contribute in both areas.

I'm work primarily with HTML (and all it's various pre-processed formats from HAML to JADE and SLIM), CSS (including SCSS and LESS) and Javascript/jQuery. I have experience with Ruby on Rails framework, JS frameworks including Backbone.js, RESTful web services and Git. I believe in the responsive web and have extensive experience working with media queries to create web experiecnes optimized across various devices.

I am fluent with Photoshop and illustrator and can work with the rest of the Adobe CS stack including Fireworks, InDesign and others if I have to. I can mock up static UI elements however my workflow tends to shift rapidly from concept to sketching/paper prototyping into functioning code.

My interests include natural user interfaces, usability accessibility, startups, mobile web, environmental issues, food and dietary topics, the outdoors and craft beer/brewing. In my downtime I can be found backcountry snowboarding in the winter, camping, hiking and mountain biking in the summer or visiting a new brewery, combing through twitter and blogs or taking an online course from Coursera or Khan Academy.
