Liz Scherer's Summary

I am a marketing and social media strategist, writer/journalist, blogger and women’s health advocate. With nearly 30 years in the healthcare arena, I have worked in the private and public sectors on behalf of web-based and traditional science and consumer publishers, non-profits and advertising agencies as well as industry. Formerly Vice President and Manager of Marketing and New Business for the New York-based public relations firm Cohn & Wolfe, and a consultant for 21 years, I am now Principal of Digital Copy, LLC, a strategic health consulting, writing and development firm.

My work, which runs the gamut, has appeared on- and offline in websites, wire services, videos, magazines, peer-reviewed journals and in conference proceedings.

My blog, Flashfree, is geared towards providing evidence-based, alternative and integrative strategies to manage the medical, emotional, social and physical challenges of menopause and midlife. It is consistently ranked among the Top 10 menopause blogs, 100 Women’s Health Blogs, Top 100 Best Wellness Blogs for Women.

I've sat on many boards, including the Corporate Advisory Board for the mobile start-up Cognovant, Inc and the Social Media Advisory Boards for the Council for Responsible Nutrition's Life...Supplemented campaign and Health Justice CT. I am also a member of the National Association of Science Writers and the Association of Health Care Journalists.

Specialties: Health consulting, marketing/social media strategy, web copy (websites, banner ads, email campaigns, features), video scripts/storyboards, social media initiatives, professional Tweeting, blogging, video scripts, interactive CD-ROM programs, CE/CME, news reporting, consumer health/feature articles, journal supplements, symposia reports, abstracts, white papers and website audits.
