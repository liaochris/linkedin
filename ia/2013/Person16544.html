Brandon Pullin's Summary

Self-driven, creative designer with strong Building Information Modeling skills. Worked in the architectural field since 1997 and have been using Revit extensively since 2007. Project experience using BIM include: schools, commercial, religious, and complex healthcare projects. Experienced with Integrated Project Delivery, CMAR, and Design Build contract types. Proven ability to coordinate and work effectively with people in various disciplines and industries. Advanced technical and presentation skills include creating digital renderings and animations for city and client approval. Flexible to changes in the industry and can apply valuable experience to current and future challenges.
