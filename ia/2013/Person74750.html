Kevin Balestrieri's Summary

BALI Construction, based in Walnut Creek, California, is a high-end general contracting company that specializes in distinguished custom residential and commercial construction. Providing individualized attention to detail to create the highest quality projects while exceeding customer expectations.

Our goal is to build quality projects. We provide exceptional customer service, offer fair and competitive pricing to maintain our well respected reputation with clients, subcontractors and suppliers. This is our best advertising tool.

Owner and founder, Kevin Balestrieri, has been in the Construction Industry for over eighteen years. He received an undergraduate degree in Business Communication from Cal Poly University, San Luis Obispo, as well as a certificate in Construction Management from Hayward State University. He is licensed with the State of California as a General Contractor in compliance with industry standards. Kevin is a master carpenter and knows the importance of fine finish work. He maintains close relationships with all subcontractors who meet his impeccable standards.

Specialties: While we can build any construction project in any price range, our strong point is in quality, high-end construction. We feature innovative, creative designs with a focus on distinctive finishes.
