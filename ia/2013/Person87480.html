Martha Dodge's Summary

With over 10 years of media experience working in the non-profit sector as a filmmaker, photographer, writer, advocate, strategist and community organizer, I am passionate about using the power of stories to enhance public engagement and create greater social impact.

I have worked on a range of issues spanning women’s health, refugee resettlement, sustainable development, human rights, social injustice and cultural exchange. Indeed, I create and build projects soup to nuts, from conceiving story ideas to finding the best avenues for distribution.

Throughout my work, I develop innovative strategies that NGOs can use to generate greater impact with their videos: I advise on developing new video distribution channels, integrating visual media into long-term communication plans and offer new media tactics to augment already existing advocacy campaigns.

I have traveled and worked extensively abroad from Malawi to Mexico, giving me an in-depth cross-cultural competence.

Specialties Include: Writing, photography, video, documentary filmmaking, multi-media, editing, media strategy, blogging, social media, women's rights, human rights, environmental and humanitarian issues.
