Andy Barwick's Summary

My entire professional career prepared me to be an entrepreneur and leader of a great organization. Although my health has failed me, I still look to help others along the way so that life here can be so much better than just a job or a career.

My goal is to help others. If for some reason, I do become well enough to work again I will. But helping others is now the priority in my life, other than living long enough to see my daughters find love, get married and God willing have a grandchild. That is a stretch given where I am at health-wise but those that know me know that I will fight for any chance to make that happen.
