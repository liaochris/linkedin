- A first-hand understanding of digital and social media markets and technologies landscape in China, and how to leverage digital and social media to meet business objectives and reach target audiences effectively;

- Hands-on experience in different fields of digital marketing and technologies (from Social Media, SEO, Paid Search, Web Analytics, Email Marketing, Mobile Marketing, eCommerce to Website Management), and hence the ability to develop and manage digital and social media strategies, campaigns and programs from an integrated perspective
