Christie Poulos' Summary

Available as a speaker, consultant and project leader in brand content and communications. I'm currently enjoying working with clients such as Westfield, Coles, Marriott Australia, Mastercard MENA, Red Bull Australia, The Hunger Project and Cricket Australia.

In March 2011, I set up jumpshot to help brands use the power of content to entertain, and change the beliefs and behaviours of their audiences.

jumpshot is a strategic, creative and production partner with a proven pedigree in video content and a wider expertise in communications, experiential and digital opportunities.

Previously, as Head of Content (Moving Image) for Red Bull – a global pioneer of brand content – I proved myself as a driven and passionate content producer with a wide range of experience in strategy and production for TV & digital platforms, development, live music & sports events, outside broadcast, and documentary. I have been invited to speak at several broadcast and media conferences, such as Circus Festival, Ad:Tech Sydney, iMedia Brand Summit, Frocom Engage (PR) and the UK’s Media Festival.

Specialties: TV production, communications, public relations, broadcaster relations, ideas, budget management, digital content, content, online, mobile, website, extreme sports, action sports, marketing, event management, public speaking
