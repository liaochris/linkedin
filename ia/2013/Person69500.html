Joyce Herr's Summary

For most of my career I have been involved in computers and programming which is a major asset in my current position as a realtor. One of my first positions was as a bookkeeper using a computer application that I can't even remember the name of. I was an administrator for the fireld service department for a defense contractor in the Washington DC area in which I several hats - accounting, programming, shipping administration. It was there that I developed an interest in programming that I continued to learn and improve on to this day.
My last position prior to being a realtor was as a programmer for R R Donnelley where I created an application to track a major client's job as it was printed and shipped.

I have always believed in giving back to my community and have served as treasurer of my church and instructed kids in the game of golf through The First Tee.
