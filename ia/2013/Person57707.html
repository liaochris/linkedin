James O'Hearne's Summary

I am an accredited Level 3 commercial energy assessor with the BRE. Along with this, at O’Hearne Consultants, I am actively involved with commercial agency work as well as all that is involved in a busy, family run multi-practice partnership. I also am involved with property development.

We are based in Ilkley, West Yorkshire and operate, in the main, throughout the North of England and act on behalf of retained clients spread throughout the country.

Specialties

Commercial Property Agency, Commercial Energy Assessments, Professional Advice, Valuations and Surveys, Lease Renewals.
