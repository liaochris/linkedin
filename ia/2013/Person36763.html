Donna Hull's Summary

Donna Hull is a Montana-based freelance writer specializing in travel and human interest content for print publications and the web. From a jeep adventure in Canyon de Chelly to luxury cruising above the Arctic Circle, Hull’s travel articles and photographs capture the essence of place combined with the thrill of discovery. She writes about active travel for baby boomers at her blog, My Itchy Travel Feet.

Hull is the boomer travel expert at My Well-Being Powered by Humana, writes for Next Avenue (the PBS online site for boomers) and has appeared as a guest on Travel Planners Radio.

Besides travel writing, Donna Hull writes about the interesting characters she meets along the way, sharing their human interest stories with readers in regional and national publications. Her creative nonfiction has appeared in Chicken Soup for the Soul.

Specialties

Luxury travel, luxury and small ship cruises, boutique lodging, soft adventure, hiking, 4-wheel driving adventures, road trips, international and domestic destinations, Montana, Arizona, Southwestern U.S., off-the-beaten path trips, Tucson, baby boomer travel.
