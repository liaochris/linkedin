Jeffrey Pedone's Summary

Jeffrey Pedone is an enterprise marketing management consultant to Fortune 500, federal, and non-profit businesses who has held the role of senior digital marketing professional for over 20 years. Jeffrey is a Managing Partner at TopRight Partners, a strategic management consultancy. He has worked in both B2B and B2C global businesses from $40 million to multi-billion dollar corporations. He consults with all businesses looking to make sense out of BIg Data, CRM, large enterprise projects involving complex architecture and multiple vendors. He is an entrepreneur with two previous businesses to his credit. He has scoped and implemented complex enterprise engagements focused on infrastructure, Big Data, platform analysis, digital marketing optimization, marketing performance optimization, and information and governance.

Jeffrey has conducted extensive RFP's for CRM,email, mobile, affiliate, and social media and completed numerous SOW's from concept to signature, and has also worked extensively with analytics, landing page, multivariate testing, database, CRM, and ad serving vendors.

Jeff has attended and completed numerous corporate training classes with Harvard University, Zenger-Folkman leadership initiatives, and Covey Leadership Center.

Specialties: Expertise in medium to large scale enterprise marketing management projects, Big Data, RFP's and large scale vendor management, infrastructure and platforms, digital and marketing performance optimization, governance, marketing consulting, CRM, social media, writing, researching, customer lifetime value, loyalty programs, email, acquisition/retention programs, audience development, digital marketing, RFP's, consultative marketing, marketing operations, philanthropy, entrepreneurship, & VRM
