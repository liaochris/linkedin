Jane Horowitz's Summary

ACHIEVE A SUCCESSFUL TRANSITION FROM COLLEGE TO CAREER.
─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─

While focused on the hard work of college, launching a career is not a primary goal. For most college students, landing that first professional job to transition into the adult working world becomes the goal nearing graduation. It’s a goal that requires different knowledge, tools and behaviors from an experienced resource. Once learned, that education lasts a lifetime.

I'm a career-launch coach and founder of More Than A Resumé and help college students and young adults make the college-to-career transition easier. With my help, landing that first professional job happens faster.

I personally guide college students and young adults develop a focused job search strategy. I coach them in the vital skills of self-marketing in today's complex and challenging employment market and, like all great coaches, motivate them to make it happen.

Some of what I do coaching college students:

♦ Clarify the changing workplace, and the qualities and skills employers value

♦ Equip clients to network for the right connections, and then cultivate them into opportunities

♦ Help identify each individual's relevant and marketable value to stand out and get noticed

♦ Reveal how to research companies to get critical insights into an organization’s culture
