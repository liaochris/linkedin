Igor Macaubas' Summary

I'm an Agile Coach & Scrum Master, passionate about people, agile and technology. A software developer at heart, enthusiast of open-source and modern programming languages. Deep understanding of agile methods, project management, portfolio and program management, coaching, mentoring, and team-building, without overlooking the technical aspects of software development. Recently, I have been working in creating high-volume web applications and portals for a big media conglomerate.

I'm a fast learner, and I'm always willing to learn and re-learn everything all over again. I believe that there's no 'right way' to do something, only different ways, and I strongly believe that there's no progress without change.

I love to give speeches and teach, spreading the word on Agile, XP, Open-source, Management, and I've consistently been doing this over the past years. Most of my talks are recorded, so if you speak portuguese you can check them out on my portuguese blog, macaubas.com.

Specialties

Scrum, Kanban, Lean, Scrum Master, CSM, Coaching, Coach, CSP, CSPO, Team building, Agile retrospectives, programming, extreme programming, XP, TDD, BDD, Python, Ruby, program management, project management, project portfolio management
