Louis Lucci's Summary

I am a US Navy veteran looking to broaden my knowledge of video production with an emphasis on post production. My formal education lies in photography but was a stepping stone towards my goal of working in a video related field. I have since moved into the post-production realm, doing freelance loading and Assistant Editing for TruTv's "World's Dumbest..." shows and Food Network's "Chopped". I am currently a full time Assistant Editor For "Chopped".

Whenever time allows, I also try to take on new and interesting freelance editing gigs or music related projects. In addition to the above I also perform live with the band "Drunken Sufis" as thier projectionist, displaying and assortment of appropriated videos and video shot by myself, relating to the set list. I have also shot/edited several videos for the band's most recent release "droneWars". I also provided the photography for the album art.

I always pushing myself to learn more, to do more, and to quench my insatiable desire for more knowledge.

Specialties: creative problem solving, organization, shipping, receiving, production assisting, video editing, HDSLR workflow, photographic assisting, photographic lighting and equipment, and carpentry skills.
