Mark Boutros' Summary

Mark Boutros is a higher education content producer and studio/technology manager with an advanced degrees in media production (MFA, Brooklyn College) and in library science (MLIS, Rutgers). He is the manager of CUNY Radio, and is Production and Studio Manager for CUNY Advanced Media Services. He is the group liaison to CUNY's Web Services unit and is part of a team that is migrating the University's public site (www.cuny.edu) to Drupal.

Mark worked in higher ed for the last 13 years in both teaching and non-teaching positions. He has worked at the Kilmer Library reference desk at Rutgers University and also works in independent micro-budget cinema production.

Specialties: On-line media delivery, higher-education, social media strategy, auidio and radio production, teaching and training, electronic content management, libraries and reference, and instructional technology.
