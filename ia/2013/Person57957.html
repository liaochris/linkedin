Jamie Denham's Summary

I am the Managing Director of the London based animation production company called Sliced Bread Animation. Specialising in character animation, Sliced Bread Animation offer 2D and 3D animation, illustration and design for digital media. Over the last 10 years Sliced Bread has successfully applied animation to all media platforms - offering anything from motion graphics, title sequences and TV commercials to online animation and series.

I studied animation on the acclaimed course at Farnham in Surrey and have been in the field of animation production for nearly 10 years during which time I have worked on a number of broadcast productions for the BBC, ITV, Channel 4 and Fox Kids (US). I was Key Animator/Animation Director on 'Star Hill Ponies', a children's animation series shown on BBC TV and produced by the company behind the internationally successful 'Fireman Sam' series.

Sliced Bread is a fully integrated multi-platform digital production company. We are creative and technical innovators at the cutting edge of animation, motion graphic, film and illustrated content production. Everything we do, from concept through production and deployment, is underpinned by our unique brand of creative strategic thinking. We make it our mission to transform our clients’ messages into compelling stories that captivate target audiences.

We create games, apps, animation, films and illustrated content for education, marketing and entertainment that push the boundaries of technology and design, whilst working seamlessly across all platforms.

Our team at Sliced Bread takes the time to really listen and understand client needs. We are dedicated to achieving excellence in everything we do. We partner with clients such as Coca Cola, Unilever, Microsoft, HSBC, Sony, BBC, Channel 4, Zurich and Nestlé to help them visualise their messages and communicate them through compelling visual storytelling.

Jamie Denham's Projects

  • Coca-Cola - Dance of Happiness Game

    • April 2011 to September 2011
    Team Members: Jamie Denham, Charlotte Moultrie

    Coca Cola announce the launch of their new ‘Neverending Dance of Happiness’ games for Facebook and Android devices. The games coincide with Coke’s experiential festival campaign across European countries in 2011.

    They encourage players to connect and win prizes via Facebook. The more you score against the 120 dance move options, the higher you rank. The Facebook game uses 3D character renders and is fully customizable across 6 genres with male/ female clothing options for that unique avatar style.

  • Cash Tin app

    • January 2012 to March 2012
    Team Members: Jamie Denham

    Sliced Bread launch the Cash Tin app at the App Store. A simple to use app that tracks your petty cash expenses. Add cash to the tin and then create new expenses that will automatically deduct the amount from the cash tin balance. You can add a date, description and an amount. The app will even allow you to take a photo of the receipt for reference. The unique aspect of this app is that it enables you to export a working spreadsheet along with the images to an email address of your choice, which is useful in a small business or home environment. The idea came from a need at the office which was then born into a working app!

  • 3D Illustrations for Beckman Coulter

    • September 2011 to September 2011
    Team Members: Jamie Denham

    Sliced Bread were commissioned by Faith The Agency to create a set of 10 3D illustrations for their client Beckman Coulter. The images were rendered out high resolution and used in a print advertising campaign. Given the way they have been built with a skeleton rig they can also be animated for use within banners or a commercial in the future.
