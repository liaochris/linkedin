Hayley McKenzie's Summary

Development Consultant in the UK and US feature film and television industry. Experienced in identifying writing and project potential. Developing projects from pitch/outline through to production. Experienced in many genres including drama, comedy, romantic-comedy, horror, thriller, action, sci-fi and fantasy.

Specialties: Assessing potential of film & television projects (at outline or script stage), developing projects to maximise their potential, commercial/market awareness, identifying and nurturing writing talent, story analysis, driving projects forward, problem-solving under pressure.
