Anna Rokina's Summary

My work and my passion is to "connect the dots", find the right insights using all the data available and work on "data-driven" social media strategies for companies.

Currently work with Asian markets, expanding my knowledge and learning new things every day. Most of my professional experience in the field of communications and social media research and measurement. Being in the social media market for over 6 years, took part in work of many professional associations, where my main area of interest is social media measurement, standards for measurement of effectiveness of social media communications and new and updated methods for analysis in social media.
