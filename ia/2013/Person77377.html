Katie McKiever's Summary

I hold a master’s degree in English from the University of North Carolina at Greensboro. I am an enthusiastic social media user who is passionate about assisting individuals and companies promote their events using social media channels. I am an entrepreneur offering my services as an Event Reporter, Professional Tweeter, Communications and Marketing Manager.

I find great enjoyment in my volunteer activities, previously serving on the board of the Friends of the Charlotte Mecklenburg Library and Hands on Charlotte’s Marketing and Communications Committee.

I exude positive energy and am inherently altruistic. Born in Memphis, I hopped around the South and landed in Charlotte where I've been for 8 years. I have a love of words and diving deep into a good book.

In my downtime, I urban hike with my husband, work out, and am a girl about town staying in the know with Charlotte’s social scene. My main goals in life are to have fun, laugh as much as possible, and continue to round out my skills as a self-professed chocolate chip cookie expert.
