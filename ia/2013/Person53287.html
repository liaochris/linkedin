Heather Lennon's Summary

We each have those “If only I could get to it” projects sitting on our desks, waiting for the 30 hour clock to be invented. The reality; there are only 24 hours in a day and the competition for our time is fierce.

Life constantly requires trade offs between business, family, friends and self. Collectively, it leaves us feeling like we are standing on one leg with a hand tied behind our back, while attempting to master the fine art of juggling. When you operate a business, you feel like you are also simultaneously dancing a jig.

I provide proprietors, managers and individuals with the desired outcomes that alleviate the pressures to complete daily tasks while accomplishing projects that enhance their business and personal endeavours.

Also, through an extensive array of products, that includes personalized clothing and promotional items, I assist businesses and community groups to remain in their customers line of view for years to come.

Purchasing of time for project completion is flexible with hourly, weekly and project rates being available to suit your requirements and budget.
