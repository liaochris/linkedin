Alex Laverty's Summary

Alex Laverty is an Associate at the McChrystal Group. He is a recent graduate of the Masters in Public Diplomacy program at the University of Southern California where he focused on new digital technology and its impact in the areas of communication, democracy, and diplomacy in Sub-Saharan Africa. Alex also received an MA in African Studies at UCLA, attended concurrently with USC.

At UCLA, he focused on African political economy in a multi-disciplined curriculum that exposed him to anthropology, Swahili, & history. This helped maintain his academic interest in the region that began during his undergraduate studies at UC San Diego & the University of KwaZulu-Natal in Durban, South Africa.

While in school, Alex worked for Apple, Inc. He facilitated training and communication programs that benefited Apple’s retail operations in the LA Area. He also spent time at the Center on Public Diplomacy at USC where he wrote for the Center’s publication 'PD in the News', and its widely read blog and digital publications. He also contributes to the understanding of challenges, victories, and trends in Internet rights around the world through his work on the Netizen Report published by Global Voices Advocacy.

Alex's passion for international affairs was ignited from a childhood spent living abroad while his father served in the US Army. His experiences across Western and Eastern Europe, including a two year stint at boarding school in the UK, created a strong desire to pursue a career that would allow him to help build understanding between cultures and societies.

He currently helps businesses achieve beyond optimal performance through the leadership solutions based on the CrossLead Framework pioneered by General (Ret) Stanley McChrystal at Joint Special Operations Command and now offered through consulting services from the McChrystal Group.

In his spare time, Alex takes in as many football matches as he can, but especially of his beloved Arsenal FC.
