Matt Reed's Summary

I like to make novel connections between online things and real-life things. Then hack them into pieces. That's probably why I'm a Creative Technologist. I'm self-taught and hardened through my experience of breaking and making things since I was a little kid. Once I went with my mom to her favorite craft store. I took my $2.75 I had saved and bought some buttons and springs and stuff to make a robot with.

I love my job and the people I work with. I love being a part of a team. It's no fun by myself. I've designed and built many websites, HTML/CSS/JS and Flash. I've built custom web apps in PHP, Django, and Rails. I've populated a 4 million row MySQL table and done fun stuff with it. I've built five iOS apps. I've hacked the Pinterest button for profit. I've made a Likelight with the Facebook Graph API, Legos, and an Arduino. I've made a Hotpad with an iPad 3. I've built a robot that pours beers for Tweets. I've made a QR code out of Oreos and then eaten it. I've made a facial-recognition-check-in-sms-a-deal system. I've triggered a C&D from Facebook and an inquiry from the FTC. I've been a finalist for Nashville Tech Council's Innovator of the Year. I've won some Addys. My hand has been on CNN. My watch has been on Mashable. I like pretty much all web technologies except for Silverlight.

I want to invent. This is all.

Specialties: MS Word. I'm the best at Word no matter how good you think you are. That's it. That's my only specialty. I just click the "Convert to HTML" menu button and it makes all the things I do come to life!

Matt Reed's Projects

  • Boomilluminator

    • May 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Matt Reed

    Crowdsourced Light Show powered by Red Bull. The Boomilluminator can only be turned on by inserting a can of Red Bull. Once on, audio is captured via microphone and then processed by the Arduino. Complementary light sequences are instantly pushed out to the 144 RGB LED can lights by the Turbull Encabulator. At the same time the onboard Node.js server pushes the corresponding light sequence to all the connected phones and extends the light show into the audience.
