Erdem MEYDANLI's Summary

An enthusiastic software engineer with embedded software focus. I have been working on embedded systems since my sophomore student days. I had the chance to work on various projects/products having different architectures (ARM, MIPS, x86) that are being used for multimedia processing and industrial automation. Besides, I worked both on Non-OS and OS (GNU/Linux, FreeRTOS etc.) based embedded systems. I started my entertaining career path with PICs and 8051 derivatives. Nowadays I am working on ARM Cortex (M0/M3/M4) MCU and ARM Cortex A8, ARM 9 CPU based solutions. In the near future I also want to focus FPGAs in order to handle complex problems that need true parallelism and have much more time constraints to be processed.
