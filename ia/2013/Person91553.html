Meri Aaron Walker's Summary

Creativity matters! So do people brave enough to put their creativity into action. All my life I’ve helped creative thinkers cut through the noise, so other people can more easily see and hear what they have to share.

I've been an artist, myself, for 45 years. My photographs and prints have been published and exhibited widely and are in both private and public collections, including two major museums.

I'm currently enraptured with my iPhone and iPad - exploring the frontiers of both mobile computing and mobile photography in a big way.

As the web moves from desktops and laptops into mobile devices, people need help using their mobile phones and tablets to engage with their people. Mobile devices can be a little tricky at first, but they offer powerful support for living happier, healthier, lives - and having more fun than we ever did strapped to a desktop.

I offer 4-5 week small coaching groups and consult on site with individuals. Phone me at 541-897-0499 to hold your place in the next group or to reserve some one-on-one tutorial time.

From the art side, I'm blogging my personal work at iPhoneArtGirl.com and building a resource to support my teaching at JoyofiPhoneography.com: news, tips and interviews with other iPhoneographers. On my birthday this year, I was featured as Artist of the Day at iPhoneArt.com and my work has been featured a lot lately on Pixelsatanexhibition.com.

So, if you could use a coach/teacher to help you...

... help you make sense of your mobile phone and/or tablet.
... share what you care about more effectively using mobile technologies
... make full use of your iPhone or Android phone and apps to express yourself creatively...

Give me a call at 541-897-0499.

FYI: Two days before Valentine's Day, 2013, LinkedIn notified me that I'm in the TOP 1% OF PEOPLE - WORLDWIDE - ENDORSED FOR OUR TEACHING. Best Valentine ever!
