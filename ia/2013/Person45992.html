Dr. Frederick Neff's Summary

Run your company like a successful sports organization. Companies and business executives face many of the same pressures of sports organizations and athletes: recruiting & retaining top performers, maximizing & reaching optimal performance and achieving goals. Executive coaching helps business executives build stronger, more effective organizations and teams, develop more effective personal leadership skills, and achieve peak performance. Sports Psychologist Dr. Frederick Neff, a licensed psychologist in Massachusetts, was the Sports Psychologist for the NHL Boston Bruins and New York Rangers and currently, he consults to Pro Ambitions Hockey and North Axis Sports Management.

Good Prospects for ProFormance Consulting are: owners and general managers of professional sports organizations, professional and elite amateur athletes and C-Level executives who work with teams and are undergoing organizational change.

Specialties: Team Building, Executive Coaching, Performance Enhancement, Conflict Management, Stress Management, Developing Emotional Intelligence for Companies, Teams and Athletes
