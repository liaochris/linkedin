Jeff Roach's Summary

Jeff is a youth marketing expert specializing in digital marketing and marketing strategy. With 20 years in advertising and marketing communications, and a majority in youth and digital marketing, Roach is an experienced executive creative director and strategic planner for national and global campaigns. Jeff has touched hundreds of brands with work for BlackBerry, Bubblicious, Coors, Durex, Energizer, Fujifilm, Mattel, McDonald's, Microsoft, MINI, MTV, Nokia, Procter & Gamble, and Ubisoft. Jeff has been quoted in the Globe & Mail, has presented at Understanding Youth and Digital Hollywood conferences, and is a Communications and Advertising Accredited Professional. With a passion for digital and youth marketing, Jeff continues to lead forward-thinking brands in innovative consumer engagement campaigns. Jeff is the Founder and Executive Creative Director of Glitteration Youth Marketing and the Chief Strategy Officer and Group ECD of global Youth Engagement agency, Fuel. Jeff is a commercial director having written and directed TV spots for Ancestry.com and Student Price Card.

Specialties: Youth marketing, commercial director, marketing strategy, strategic planning, digital marketing, social media and mobile, advertising, consumer insights
