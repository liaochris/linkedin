Karl Sanger's Summary

Licensed & Bonded State of California Talent Agent serving LA & Orange County areas since 1990. Author of ABSOLUTE POSITIVITY, an Inspirational story of Positivity, Prayer & People.

Sanger Talent Agency - I thoroughly enjoy matching up the finest talent with quality filmmakers, studios, casting agents, and producers in the pursuit of creating film excellence and infusing the culture with positive, redemptive stories that uplift the world and enhance the human consciousness. Integrity, vision,and a diligent work ethic are the attributes I seek from my industry associates.

Music Unlimited Talent Agency - Southern California's One-Stop Music and Entertainment Service featuring over 1500 acts including dj's, bands, magicians, shows, etc. serving hotels, private parties, weddings, nightclubs, casinos and cruise lines. I love helping to make events memorable featuring quality entertainers from the entertainment capitol of the world.

Musician/Writer - A music major in college, I freelance with several bands playing all the saxophones & reeds, flute, keyboard and vocals. I also love the written word and have 10 completed works including books, screenplays, pilots and teleplays.

Disc Jockey/Emcee - Have DJ'd for over 20 years and booked over 1000 parties.

Music Teacher - I believe that music is a precious gift to be shared with everyone.

I'm a renaissance man, a crazy compilation and contradiction of business knowledge, creativity and artistic talents. I try to live each day with an attitude of gratitude.

Specialties: I am a tenacious, highly motivated, skilled salesman and leader with a track record of success. I have integrity and character with the ability to bring a great deal of enthusiasm to my clients. My positive attitude / "positivity" and personality are my best attributes and I maintain a reputation as a trustworthy & honest agent sometimes considered an oxymoron in our industry.
