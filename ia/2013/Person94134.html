Mike Elsmore's Summary

After leaving University I joined a small Affiliate New Media agency. I was responsible for the delivery of both unique and large scale developments , working with major partners including MoneySupermarket, Ciao, Kelkoo and similar brands. From the start of my career I have focused on website usability and Search Engine Optimisation (SEO), and this remains my area of market leading expertise.
I work with a network of similarly motivated individuals and together can provide a refreshingly innovative approach to delivering professional results within tight deadlines.

My reputation has been defined by my ability to deliver market leading website design solutions to all the organisations I have worked with. I get up every morning driven with the desire to further extend my skills and provide an ever improving service to everyone I work with.

My end-game is to develop myself and any company I work for/with into an industry leader through mixed skill sets. The ability to manage multiple skills adequately provides myself and any project I work on a good head start on a more corporate structure that requires each element of a project to pass under several people to accomplish something 2 well versed people can achieve.
