Anita Brown's Summary

Born in Boston and raised in San Diego, I hold a B.A. in Media Production from Emerson College, with a concentration in Studio Television Production. After graduation, I moved to Los Angeles to join LA’s prevalent media production industry. For the past three years, I have worked full-time as a Digital/Interactive Producer, while simultaneously turning out top-quality freelance work for a variety of clients.

In my spare time, you’ll find me taking improv classes, kicking butt in my local dodgeball league, or volunteering with Rock 'n' Roll Camp for Girls Los Angeles. I most enjoy television, puns, craft beer, black coffee, the color orange, playing guitar, songs with banjo in them, and hyper-connectivity. And I do my crosswords in Sharpie.
