Carla Giampapa's Summary

Hello, my name is Carla Giampapa. I’m a producer and I want to work with you on your next project.

Whether you need a commercial, a corporate or educational video or have a great story you want to share, you need someone with both the creativity to envision the big picture and the meticulousness to manage all the details; someone who values relationships, collaboration, quality and integrity.

My purpose and commitment is to assemble all the necessary elements to have your vision become a reality in the most efficient, effective and valuable way possible. It’s also important to me that you enjoy your experience along the way and trust you’re being heard and truly partnered with.

It’s pretty simple really. I listen to what you need. I work with you to create an effective action plan. Then I create a team and we work the plan. I’m a great communicator, problem solver and possibility creator. With 15+ years in the industry, I excel at intuitively assessing and effectively balancing creative, financial, legal and logistic needs to seamlessly achieve project goals.

Passionate about helping people and about organizing and creating motion images, I encourage you to contact me. I’d love to hear about your upcoming projects!
