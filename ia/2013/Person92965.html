Michael Sattler's Summary

Entrepreneurial and strategic professional with seventeen years’ experience in business and technology. Background in product management, engineering, and operations for online companies. Founded six startups, grew one to $200 million, managed teams from 4-50.

Like it or not, the technology and business worlds often speak different languages. My MBA, founder experience and hands-on technology background help me bridge that gap.

Special skills in web-based and mobile product design, technology oversight and leadership, and direct management of technical personnel. Recognized for hands-on development, rapid time-to-market, and bridging engineering and business priorities. BA from Princeton, MBA from Babson.
