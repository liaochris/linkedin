Avi Charkham's Experience

Head of Product

lool ventures

Partnership; 1-10 employees; Venture Capital & Private Equity industry

July 2011 – Present (2 years 1 month)

lool ventures is an early stage, value-add venture capital firm based in Israel.

As head of product at lool ventures I lead our incubation team.
Our roll is to to develop our own ideas internally and turn them into working prototypes that enable us to test our assumptions about markets we find interesting.

Once we create our prototypes we pick the ones that show promise and grow them into working products and assemble a team that that takes those products and builds them into companies.

My roll includes product, UX, design, marketing and bizdev (and anything else required in order to take an idea and turn into something people will love :-)

Among the products I've co founded and incubated at lool are Familio (Private Sharing With Family), MyPermissions (The first cloud security scanner) and sharePops (Share optimization for publishers)
