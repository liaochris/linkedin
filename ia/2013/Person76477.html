Koen Bakx's Summary

I design distinctive websites by turning complex information into communicating messages. I know how to communicate and have two decades of experience in display design. Search engine optimized applications - attracting to act, seducing to sell more.

Clients thank me for being clear, honest and stubborn. They describe me as being the missing link between design and programming.

Specialismen: While being an academic designer, standards-based HTML and CSS are almost native language for me. I can practically think (and write) in HTML/CSS as I would in Dutch.
