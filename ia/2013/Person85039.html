Marcia Hansen's Summary

I have over 15 years experience in digital marketing with expertise in developing consumer marketing campaigns for worldwide audiences integrating web, mobile, events, social, and optimized content to engage and educate audiences to achieve business objectives.

I'm insanely curious and chock-full of creative ideas of achieve results that stand up to analytical critiques. I do my best to demonstrate innovative thinking and risk taking while having fun and delivering quality experiences.

My interests and focus areas include: global consumer marketing campaigns, content optimization and marketing, content syndication and distribution, using social media to build awareness as well as relationships, and event marketing that connects social, global, local, and digital in meaningful ways.

Personal passions: I love road trips, the great outdoors and taking photos. Plus, I do my best to get as many airline miles and hotel points as possible each year. I'm an avid reader and am eternally grateful for new technology that means I don't have to lug physical books around with me while I walk, hike, motorcycle, fly, and drive around.
