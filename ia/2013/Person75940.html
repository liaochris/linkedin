Kingsley Wiggin's Summary

Publishing professional with over 14 years experience. Pro active in making work flow efficiencies and improving working practices, sound experience in building professional relationships with suppliers, and particular strengths in scheduling, planning, system development and deployment, project and change management.

Experienced in the majority of print workflows, POD, short run (JIT) digital, litho, large format, CD/ DVD duplication and replication. Specialties: Managing, negotiation, product development, print purchasing/ management, IT literacy, line management, teamworking and communication, eye for detail and quality control. APMP level D project management accreditation, and digital project management.
