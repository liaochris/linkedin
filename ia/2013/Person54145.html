Linda Marshall's Summary

Through our compassionate problem solving approach, Health with Heart will help you and your family plan and provide the highest quality of life and quality of care to the older adults in your life. It is our passion to leave you available physically and emotionally to spend time with your loved one and to able able to honor the wishes of the loved ones.

We will listen to you and your family’s needs and help understand the many healthcare issues surrounding the care of an elderly loved one.  

We will help you create a loving and personalized care plan.
If you do not live locally, you can rest assured knowing that we will be here handling the sudden and often urgent needs that arise.

Our Services Include:
Compassionate Problem Solving
• Care Planning
• Supervision of care
• Weekly Visits to home, independent living, assisted living, or nursing facility
• Attend Care conferences
• Doctor appointments
• Personal shopping
• Referral services
• Meditating between families and health care facilities

Health with Heart was created by Linda Marshall LSW, C-SWCM, CGRS, because she cares for people and their well-being. Linda is very efficient in working with all types of caregivers and healthcare providers. Her goal is to work with you and other caregivers through a team approach to support the client.
