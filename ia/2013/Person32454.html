David R. Carothers, CIC, CRM's Summary

Praxiom is a Risk Management firm specializing in Technology, Life Sciences, PEO and Loss Sensitive Workers Compensation consulting engagements. Comprised of veterans of the risk management and financial services industry, Praxiom is an invaluable business partner to our clients due to our unique approach which focuses on reducing their total cost of risk. While a fictitious word, Praxiom is formed from the Greek words Praxis (practical application of knowledge) and Axiom (a proven truth). Through an integrated approach of our practical experience and proven risk management best practices, we have a direct impact on our clents' organizations.

Game Changers Marketing, LLC is a firm that was founded by creatively using existing methods to market your business and drive inbound traffic for Business to Business marketers. The highly motivated, results-oriented leadership team at Game Changers Marketing has a proven track record of success in generating new business through the use of the internet while leading and shaping sales teams into the high performing units that their companies desire. Through our integrated approach of social media marketing, traditional sales tactics and our hands on training approach, we have Changed the Way the Game is Played!

As an Enterprise Risk Management Advisor at Baldwin Connelly in Tampa, I specialized in auditing and structuring risk management programs for technology and life sciences organizations of all sizes. .

Specialties

Structuring and negotiating risk financing structures for technology and life sciences organizations of all sizes. Often tapped to lead educational seminars and be an ad-hoc board member in various technology incubators/accelerators. I hold the Certified Insurance Counselor designation from the National Alliance and the Certified Risk Manager designation from the Alliance as well. Game Changers Marketing specializes in inbound B2B marketing.
