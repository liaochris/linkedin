Kevin Schaffer's Summary

Kevin Schaffer is a creative and enthusiastic logistics consultant passionate about leveraging technology to solve complex business issues. Mr. Schaffer advises organizations on how to optimize their supply chain activities by improving warehousing, transportation, and materials management processes. Kevin Schaffer's Publications

  • Powerful Track and Trace delivered by SAP Transportation Management and Event Management integration

    • 2013 ASUG National / SAP SAPPHIRE Conference Speaker
    • May 14, 2013
    Authors: Kevin Schaffer

    Transportation providers consistently struggle to maintain accurate, real-time visibility to shipment progress. A lack of such information can be detrimental especially in cases where lengthy transportation times have to be considered. This causes challenges in reacting quickly to changing conditions, providing customers with timely delivery status, and capturing crucial data to improve future planning decisions. Supplementing the SAP Transportation Management (TM) Solution with Event Management (EM) provides full control of transportation operations. The combined solution provides timely information surrounding key transportation events in a single view. This provides transparency to both planned activities, such as trailer loading and unloading, and unexpected events such as shipment delays. This event data allows transportation planners, customer service representatives, and dispatchers to react dynamically and keep the customer better informed.
