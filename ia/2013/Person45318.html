Delia Dorn's Summary

Form Healthy Habits helps individuals improve their health. Through the education of natural alternatives to live a healthier life style, I am able to help my clients improve their overall health and outlook on life. Sometimes this means addressing a specific health issue, other times making them aware of potential hazards and toxins in products they have in their home. It is through education and through the use of the array of natural, non-toxic products that my business has grown.

We are a Shaklee Independent Distributor.

Specialties

nutrition, supplementation, non-toxic products, green alternatives, women's health, children's health, Shaklee products
