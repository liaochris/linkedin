Karen Naef's Summary

I have 25 years experience running small service businesses, primarily in the advertising and marketing arena. I first used QuickBooks in 1995, and loved the power of the program to provide the information needed to make financial and business decisions. I helped a number of friends and associates with QuickBooks over the years, and decided to pursue QuickBooks as a business when I relocated to the Tampa Bay area from the New York Metro area in 2008. I look forward to helping business owners organize and manage their small businesses with QuickBooks solutions.

Specialties

My goal is to empower my clients to take control of their finances using QuickBooks. I especially love working with clients afraid to take QuickBooks out of the box, and helping them learn to love (well, at least appreciate!) the program when they realize it is not as overwhelming as they thought!
