Jeff Wallack's Summary

Jeff Wallack is Executive Producer & Host of The Investment Hunter on ABC TV debuting in Phoenix this Spring. Jeff is a commercial real estate consultant, advisor and a former stock broker. He leads the Wallack Commercial Team and established the Commercial Division at RE/MAX Excalibur Realty in Scottsdale, Arizona. Previously, Jeff co-owned and was managing broker of U.S. Real Estate Advisor's Group, a boutique commercial brokerage and management firm, specializing in single tenant net leased investment properties. His national clients and tenants include the United States Postal Service, AT&T, Century Link, and Yogurtini franchises in Arizona. He is national site selector for OceanWest, a developer based in Scottsdale.

Jeff obtained his first real estate license In 1978 and is a licensed broker in Arizona and California. He maintains an international practice representing Canadian and Latin American investment groups acquiring property in the States.

Jeff is a consultant and advisor for commercial investments including acquisition, disposition, and leasing. Whether your interest is industrial, retail, office, land, apartments or business opportunities he can be of assistance. He is an intermediary/business broker and a senior negotiator for lease renewals with the United States Postal Service. He serves as National Director, Postal Investments with RE/MAX Excalibur.

Jeff Wallack is an accredited commercial professional (ACP) and a member of the Association of United States Postal Lessors; Arizona Business Brokers Association; International Shopping Centers Council; Scottsdale and of the Arizona & National Association of Realtors. He is also leader of the RE/MAX International Commercial Directors & Owners Group.
