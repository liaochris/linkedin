Mike Guinther's Summary

Allure Painting has been in business since February 2002. We do commercial and residential, interior and exterior painting of homes, businesses, rental properties, churches, new constuction, remodels, basements, offices, etc...

Some of the other services we provide are:

Plaster wall and ceiling repairs

Drywall repairs

Wallpaper removal

Sash repairs, and sash cords

Wood staining

Paint removal

Color consultation

We use all top quality paints, primers and prep materials.

Satisfying our customers has been key to staying in business.

Specialties

We specialize in residential and commercial re-paints, which involves working in an occupied space and causing as little interuption as possible to the occupants.
