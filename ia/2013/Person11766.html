I help success-driven professionals, who are burned out and just going through the motions, design a career that will make them leap out of bed, even on Monday mornings.

Do you find yourself:
- hating your job with no idea how to make it better or what to do instead?
- getting crankier by the day?
- going to bed with your Blackberry?
- dragging yourself to work?
- wondering how you will ever be able to walk away from that paycheck?
- carrying around enough head and stomach ache remedies to fill the shelves of a drugstore?

As your Life & Career Coach, I will support you to:
- Implement tools to gain perspective and make an existing difficult job situation better while you create a plan.
- Discover the work you are meant to do.
- Move beyond the safe options.
- Identify ways you stop yourself from having what you want.
- Leverage your previously hidden strengths.
- Master getting your resume to the top of the stack.
- Practice behavioral interview skills to close the deal on your ideal position.
- Land your dream job or
- Make your own business a reality.
