Michael Martinez's Summary

Here at Adamant Design Group, if you can dream it, we can design it! From small deck design to large remodels, to new plan development, and anywhere in between. What sets us apart from our competition, is that we design the home around you, not to adhere to our trademark style. We don't try to sell you on things you don't want or need. We actually get to know you and figure out what kind of design truly suits you and your lifestyle, so that when you're enjoying you're home you're not thinking "I wish I'd done that differently." We make designing your home the easiest and funnest part of the entire construction process, whether it's a simple three-bedroom house perfect for a newly wed couple, or a luxurious seven-bedroom multi-generational estate.

We also have a talented full service interior design team eager to make your home give off just the right vibe. We will work with you from concept through completion creating the perfect environment that reflects your lifestyle, passions, and personal tastes. We assist clients with as little or as much of the design process as needed. We can draw up a great design and let you complete it at your leisure, or we can walk you through the process and help you select your colors, fixtures, cabinets/countertops, flooring, and furniture, and everything else that helps turn a house into a home. We can furnish and accessorize your entire home, or we can help pull together a single room with a few select pieces.

Our Interior Designer can help you choose:
- Accessories
- Bedding
- Cabinetry
- Custom Drapery and Window Treatments
- Custom Floral Arrangements
- Flooring
- Art & Framing
- Furniture
- Paint
- Upholstery

We give a free one hour consultation, so send us an email to set up an appointment to find out how we can help you create the home of your dreams!

We also have quality contractors on hand to help with:
- Financing
- Civil Engineering
- Structural Engineering
- Construction
- Landscape Design
- MEP Engineering

Specialties

Residential design, commercial design, plan development, design consultation, interior design, drafting
