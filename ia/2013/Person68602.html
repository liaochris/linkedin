Joseph Alfe's Summary

I have spent the last decade holding various positions in the mortgage lending business, from retail loan officer, mortgage processor, and wholesale lending executive. As a former lending executive, I understand how lenders work from the inside out, and can use this knowledge of the industry to ensure that short sale negotiators keeps up to date on the ever changing and complex lender short sale guidelines. I have been closing short sales since 2005, and have successfully closed hundreds of residential and commercial short sale transactions for investors, homeowners, Realtors, and attorneys. I have also written several published articles, as well as a popular blog on short sales, and is in demand as a short sale trainer and speaker.

Specialties

Short Sales Negotiation & Process Management, Broker/Agent Moderation, SSP Method, Short Sale Education and consumer and professional activism.

Joseph Alfe's Experience

President

Short Sale Processor

March 2010 – Present (3 years 7 months) Greater Chicago Area

Short Sale Processor was founded in March 2011. This new company grew out of a short sale negotiation and real estate investment company, ICG Shortsales, founded by Joseph Alfe. ICG had been helping homeowners close short sales for over five years, and has successfully negotiated and closed hundreds of short sales, and has closed over 9 million in short sales since 2005. Short Sale Processor has the experience, the resources, and the professional team to successfully negotiate and close a very high volume of short sale transactions.

As President of SSP, my primary responsibilities include cultivating and maintaining relationships with Brokers, Agents, Attorneys, and Sellers. I am also responsible for coordinating and attending closings. Secondary responsibilities are speaking, agent training, and consulting. SSP has worked with some of the largest and most respected Real Estate Brokers and Law Firms across the country to help sellers close their short sales. SSP maintains upper level contacts at major lenders such as Chase, Wells Fargo, and others. My articles and blogs are read by real estate professionals nationwide.
