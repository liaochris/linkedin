Adam Marchick's Summary

After three fantastic years at BCV, I left to start Kahuna.

Previously, part of the Bain Capital Ventures team, making technology investments across the United States. In early 2011, returned to Palo Alto to launch BCV's West Coast office. Specialties in Software Infrastructure and Consumer investments with software IP.

Prior: Part of early technical team for multiple start-ups and a few years building software for Siebel prior to Menlo Ventures. During my three years at Menlo, I focused on investing broadly in IT at all stages. At Menlo Ventures, I helped make investments in a number of companies at A, B and C-round stages. For every dollar I invested, 3.2 dollars were returned.

I also serve on the board of the Glow Foundation. Glow is a non-profit I started due to the chasm 1st generation college students fall into when faced with the financial hurdles of going to college.
