Dawn McLaughlin's Summary

A Chartered Accountant with a passion for enabling & empowering others to succeed. Specialising in small business development, with particular emphasis on; new start ups, women in business & solutions for owner managed companies.

Dawn leads by example and loves to encourage, uplift & inspire others to be the best they can be. She listens well & focuses on others needs. She gives her clients & peers time, attention, counsel, education & empathy. Dawn strives to provide value & service worth more than any compensation.

Dawn believes "THERE ARE NO LIMITS"!

ABOUT DAWN

My passion is to help people grow their business, overcome their obstacles, prosper & realise their DREAMS!

I have been helping people transform fear into power, doubt into certainty & limited thinking into unlimited opportunities through my practical & results-oriented approach. My proven techniques have helped hundreds of people take their business to the next level.

I am not the everyday ACCOUNTANT. I live life with passion & purpose. I work hard & play hard. My pastime is my Harley Davidson Heritage Softail and travel.
