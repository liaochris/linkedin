Juan Carlos Ramos' Summary

AHR Design Solutions offers senior level professionalism, specializing in residential home remodeling and design. We achieve interior and exterior designs that reflect the functional needs and preferences of each client. Results-oriented with the ability to coordinate multiple projects & teams simultaneously, AHR oversees all project activity to ensure its completion is on schedule, within budget, and in accordance with engineering design.

When planning a remodel or home project, our clients have come to expect inspired design, quality craftsmanship and a high level of customer service. We provide homeowners with a team of top design and build professionals working together as a team to achieve outstanding results. All parties are supervised to achieve a common goal based on the homeowner’s needs and lifestyle and we stay within their financial investment range.

Juan Carlos Ramos, owner and contractor for AHR, is an accomplished senior level professional with expertise in the areas of operations and staff management within the residential sector of home remodeling and design. He is results-oriented with the ability to coordinate multiple projects and teams simultaneously. A graduate of DeVry Institute, Juan has extensive experience in both the Professional and Trade Arenas as well as a degree in Telecommunications Management.
