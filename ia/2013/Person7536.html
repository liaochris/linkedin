Andy Poulton's Summary

I’ve been working with online marketing since 1997 which was when I built my first website. 2 years later I started to take an interest in the ways the search engines worked and the things businesses could do so that their website came up on the first page of search results.

Things have moved on a lot since then but I’ve maintained my focus on search engine optimisation [SEO] because it is still the No.1 way that people look for things on the internet.

In 2003 I launched my first Adwords campaign and have continued to help businesses maximise their effective use of this powerful marketing opportunity, making sure that they get the most clicks for the least amount of money and ensuring that these clicks are real opportunities.

Around 2007 I realised the the new social media sites presented a great marketing/awareness raising and communications opportunity for businesses.

In those early days there were few takers but as time has passed, it’s obvious the most businesses that if they choose to ignore social media then they are turning their backs on a great way to communicate with existing clients and to raise awareness amongst new ones.

Those that do ignore social media often find that their competition reap the rewards so I work with clients to best understand how they can make the maximum impact, develop profiles and help them take full advantage of these great tool - social [Facebook] and business networking [LinkedIn], Twitter, Video Marketing [YouTube], Location based opportunities, Blogging, Article Marketing, Email Marketing, SMS Marketing and Viral.

I am a firm believe that businesses need to understand how their online activity is working for them so I put measurement tools in place so that my clients can understand how well their online strategies are working for them.

I regularly present successful workshops covering on-line strategy, SEO and social media across the UK to audiences up to 500 and to delegates at all positions in business.
