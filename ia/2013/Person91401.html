Melvin Roane's Summary

RGC, LLC strives to educate and enhance its clients understanding of the value of developing a comprehensive corporate sponsorship and brand management program. Our experienced staff is skilled in listening, researching and developing an understanding of clients’ specific needs. Committed to assisting our clients build awareness and preference for their respective properties, we stand ready to serve.

Specialties

The founders of RCG, LLC are marketers whose backgrounds include but are not limited to the following industries: television, radio, newspapers, consumer products, tourism, special events marketing, sports properties and facility management.

RCG, LLC is able to leverage its expertise into multiple revenue generation opportunities. Sponsorships, Brand Management and Information Consulting are just a few examples of the distinctive lines of business we provide for our clients.
