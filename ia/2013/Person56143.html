Iulia Meilescu's Honors and Awards

  • CTO Technical Innovation Award

    Syniverse Technologies
    • January 2010

    The California-based team that developed a dramatic improvement for Syniverse’s MMS solution has won first-place in the 2010 CTO Technical Innovation Awards.

    Winning team members Adam Buecher, Brad Burgon, Elisa Huang, Iulia Meilescu and Jyothi Panduranga worked together to make it possible for the Syniverse MMS interworking offering to resize incoming MMSs, ensuring messages sent from one operator are compatible delivery sizes for the recipient’s MMS infrastructure.

    CTO Jeff Gordon said this project garnered top honors because of its uniqueness in the marketplace as well as its potential to help keep current Syniverse customers satisfied and win more business as a competitive differentiator.
