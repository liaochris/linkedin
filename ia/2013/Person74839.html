Kevin Darty's Summary

My professional career has spanned from Customer Support to Lead Development to Systems Architect. I have worked in the following technologies: C#, ASP.NET, Classic ASP, HTML and DHTML, XML and XSLT, JavaScript, jQuery, AJAX, COM+/MTS, Visual Basic, SQL Server with T-SQL, Oracle with PL-SQL and Graphics Design using Adobe Photoshop and ImageReady, Adobe Illustrator and the great OpenSource Graphics Design tool Paint.NET.

My personal goal is to branch out into other areas outside the Microsoft ecosystem such as iPhone OS and Android Development, Ruby on Rails or Python. I really enjoy working on customer facing web sites and applications where SEO is important and would enjoy the opportunity to design mashups ans Social Websites, as well as to get involved in Cross-platform programming leveraging the OpenSource .NET Alternative "Mono". I am very excited by newer Document Database technologies such as CouchDB, MongoDB and RavenDB and would love the opportunity to pursue these technologies in a professional capacity.

I love Linux and the Free Software Movement and enjoy the refined usability of UNIX as presented with Mac OS X. I would love to do either professional or personal software development for those platforms and of course, cross-platform development. Viva Mono!

Specialties

I have great people skills and work great in roles where I interact with other teams and clients in a supporting role. As a musician and a lover of the arts, I look at Computer Programming in a different way and I believe that my vast experience in N-tier development utilizing Web 2.0 Technologies and Transactional Communication Frameworks and excellent trouble-shooting skills help me to deliver what my clients expect with the knowledge that I have the skills and knowhow to take care of them.
