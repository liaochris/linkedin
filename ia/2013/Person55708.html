IP-ERP, Inc. offers you access to a comprehensive stable of direct financial alternatives and the insight to help you make sense of the options.

The goal at IP-ERP, Inc. is to add value to your acquisition or refinance by offering a wide range of financing solutions and direct access to top international, national and local Investors/Lenders.

With over 20 years of combined experience in structuring real estate deals, we have funded transactions through agencies, life companies, conduits, and "out-of-the-box" commercial Investors/Lenders. In addition to permanent debt financing, we regularly structure transactions using high-leverage bridge loans, mezzanine debt, construction loans and straight equity. (See Product Page)
