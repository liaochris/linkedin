Bobbi Prim's Summary

Bobbi Prim is a Verified member of the Association of Better Networkers, & agrees to uphold ABN's Better Business Practices

In 1994, Bobbi Prim was featured in Smart Computing Magazine and featured again in 2000, in PC Novice Magazine, for success in her home-based business, as well as for successfully helping others start their home-based businesses. Bobbi Prim was fed up with corporate America. After being laid off from two jobs because of company downsizing, she decided enough was enough.

Bobbi Prim's story is a home business success story, but she's not alone. According to LINK Resources Corp., a research and consulting firm in New York, in 1994, more than 3.1 million people started home-based businesses. As of 2005, as many as 150 million people, in North America alone, enjoy the freedom and benefits of owning their own home-based business. According to the Small Business Administration’s Office of Advocacy, the number of U.S. households that have a home-based business exceeded 12%; currently “more than half of all U.S. businesses are based out of an owner’s home.”

For Bobbi Prim, a married mom of two children, Arise Virtual Solutions and Open Door MultiServices (ODMS) delivered what other companies couldn’t – a full-time income working from home. After trying numerous home-based businesses, Bobbi almost gave up and was considering working outside the home again, when she discovered Arise. After researching everything she could find on the internet, she knew it was the best home-based opportunity she had ever seen. Bobbi rolled up her sleeves and started working hard the next day, and for her efforts, she is now President & CEO of her own company – Open Door MultiServices of Illinois (ODMSofIL) and she is making a full-time income and enjoying spending time with her children, now grown, and grandchildren.
