Jeffrey Simons' Summary

I am a happily married father of 3 - 2 girls and a boy and I love to work on my 74 Mazda Rx-4, work out at the gym and just hang out with my family. We love to go camping, BBQ with friends and entertain when time allows.

For the past 14 amazing years I have been blessed in helping entry level buyers, move up seller's and distressed property owners in today's constantly changing market. I love the thought of helping people reach their goals of buying and selling their home in order to improve their lifestyles and investment strategies.

Specialties

I am knowledgeable and very successful in understanding and applying all aspects of the real estate process, including appraisals, title, financing, negotiation, contracts, and all related paper-work. I also work quite frequently with move up/move out contingency homebuyers. This requires skill in balancing and coordinating far more details including time frames management, contingencies, and overcoming all challenges and obstacles that arise during the process.
