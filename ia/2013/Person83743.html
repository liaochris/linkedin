Lynne Schinella's Summary

I work with organisations to help them connect more effectively to drive business results.

I love the diversity in our culture and am passionate about differences - generational, culture, personality, gender - or anything else we judge that prevents effective messages getting through.

And when that happens you get misinterpreted, workplace arguments, stress, counselling and hiring and firing costs.
I can help you get less of all that and more staff retention and productivity through workshops, executive coaching, facilitation and conference speaking.

Specialties

I help people connect for less stress & greater productivity;
- between departments
- between individuals
- between you and your customers
- between management and their teams
- between you and your stakeholders

Lynne Schinella's Publications

  • Bite Me! the do's and don'ts of dealing with our differences

    • Ripe Publishing
    • February 21, 2012
    Authors: Lynne Schinella

    Like to manage more effectively the people you think are tiresome? High maintenance? Annoying? Aggressive? Bite Me! is a simple, clear, easy to read guide about the way we behave, why we act so differently, why certain people drive us crazy and what we can do about it.
