Michael W. Streed's Summary

For 34 years I've provided facial imaging solutions for law enforcement, private industry and the entertainment field as both a consultant and trainer. This breadth of experience helped me to successfully establish the Baltimore City Police Department's Forensic Facial Imaging Unit. As the department's first full-time Forensic Artist, I assist detectives by providing facial imaging solutions during active criminal investigations.

In addition to my duties at the Baltimore City Police Department, I'm also the principal owner of SketchCop Solutions - Law Enforcement's Source for Facial Imaging and Identification. We provide law enforcement with investigative support and case consulations.

For law enforcement agencies seeking a digital composite imaging solution; we recommend they try our SketchCop FACETTE Face Design System, an easy to use, high quality, cost-effective software solution.

To receive your FREE demo, visit our website at: www.SketchCop.com.
