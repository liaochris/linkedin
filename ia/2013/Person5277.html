Alyssa Dver's Summary

I give individuals, teams and companies more CONFIDENCE through entertaining, research-backed:

* presentations
* workshops
* consulting
* books/blogs/content

My work revolves around researching and sharing what makes people, groups and businesses confident. Leveraging emotional training techniques used by elite athletes together together with quantitative data and years of experience, I developed Key Confidence Indicators (KCIs) that provide a straightforward method to assess and improve confidence.

Leading to my confidence crusade, I have worked at and consulted for dozens of start-ups as well as multinational, public corporations. As CEO for Mint Green Marketing, my team and I help small to midsize businesses and non-profits develop confident, revenue-generating marketing strategies and plans for efficient market entry and expansion. Half of our clients are based outside of the US (France, Costa Rica, Ukraine, Israel, etc.) in a variety of industries including high tech, financial services, and human resources. As their executive confidant or part-time marketing leader, I give clients confidence that they are getting the best return on every marketing dollar spent on their branding, websites, brochures, datasheets, whitepapers, PR, social media and lead generation programs.

Alyssa Dver's Experience

Co-founder & Chief Confidence Officer : Keynote Speaker & Executive Consultant

American Confidence Institute (ACI)

September 2013 – Present (2 months) Greater Boston Area

Through understanding and bolstering core confidence, ACI empowers high performing individuals, teams and companies to better:

* Lead and inspire
* Innovate and adapt
* Have productive relationships
* Sell more easily
* Be less stressed

The American Confidence Institute (ACI) was founded to examine the root causes and impact of confidence. Our mission is improve the performance of individuals, teams and businesses by helping them assess and condition their confidence.

Using empirical and anecdotal data either collected or created by ACI, the information is shared freely on the ACI website as resources for anyone interested. ACI collaborates with dozens of individuals and organizations around the world that are working to understand issues in and around confidence.

We also offer customized:

* keynote and workshop presentations
* individual, one-on-one coaching
* group/team training
* market and company-centric research
* brand and marketing consulting

Contact us at info@AmericanConfidenceInstitute.com
