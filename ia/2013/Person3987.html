Alex Florisca's Summary

I work with individuals and start-ups to develop, build and market exciting things in the technology industry. Currently working as the lead developer on Fansparks (http://fansparks.com) to create a platform for growing fans or users through viral methods.

As a developer, I've worked on a number of projects of varying size, from simple Wordpress sites to large recruitment websites to modern web applications such Fansparks. My technology of choice so far has been the classic PHP - MySQL combo with HTML5 for the front-end (oftentimes in the form of Wordpress). I'm now moving towards newer technologies such as Node.js and Amazon Web Services due to their flexibility and future potential.

The building part is easy. The hard part is knowing what to build - answering the question "Will my idea be successful?" I've worked with a number of clients (as well as my own stuff) to develop simple "what if" ideas into potential businesses and launch them in a lean startup fashion. By validating these ideas in the market, with minimal budget, before spending a fortune on building them, I save people from investing in bad ones, and encourage the good ones (those which people will pay you for!)

Always looking for interesting projects and business partners, so please get in touch if you have an idea you want to validate, a business to grow or some money to invest.
