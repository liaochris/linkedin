James Sherrett's Summary

"Learn to do by doing" – The best lesson I've learned in my career in marketing, technology and e-commerce. I've applied it to every professional challenge I've encountered with excellent results – growing revenues, building teams, leading innovation.

Guiding principles – The best guiding principles to succeed exist in all great marketing. Leaders act as simplifiers in complex markets. They understand that succeeding with the product is the best path to loving the brand. They work to change behaviours before beliefs. They make specific promises then deliver on them.

I believe in – The pursuit of excellence in every aspect of work. I am interested in working on the challenges found at the intersection of business, culture, technology and people.

I value – Clear and open communications, results-focused performance and hard work. I love working with strong teams and peers to pursue meaningful goals with fast feedback loops and energizing, quick cycles. I value trust.

Special skills – Proven pattern of creating marketing to drive multi-million dollar revenue growth for business-to-consumer and business-to-business companies. I thrive on being creative and I've found success comes from a laser-sharp focus on the full lifecycle of prospects and customers – awareness, acquisition, activation, retention and engagement.

Know your customers. Create strategies to reach them. Build proof to convert them. Tell stories they can remember. Prove your assumptions. Review, revise, recreate. Make it remarkable.

"Learn to do by doing."
