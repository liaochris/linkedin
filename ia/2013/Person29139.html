Daniel Brenner, PMP's Summary

"It has been my observation that most people get ahead during the time that others waste." - Henry Ford

I believe that time is the most valuable currency that any of us have. It should be spent doing something productive or with loved ones but NEVER wasted or taken for granted.

If I'm awake, then I'm going to do something. If I'm asleep, I'm going to dream about things to do when I'm awake. That's not to say I don't know how to kick back and have a good time. I greatly enjoy just about every aspect of life and try to be as well rounded as I possibly can.

My Grandfather owned a music company in New Castle, PA and from an early age I had it instilled in me that I could do anything I put my mind to. With this way of thinking I find myself having a wide variety of hobbies and activities that help me to connect with people that I speak with in an effortless and honest way. I attribute most of my success in sales and in life to simply 'being honest'.

I have been knowingly and unknowingly mentored by CEO & founder of PartsSource, A. Ray Dalton, in the ways of starting and building a company. The experiences I've had while working at PartsSource continue to feed my desire for self-actualization (the motive to realize one's full potential).

I have become skilled at process mapping, development, and refinement by applying certain elements of Six Sigma and LEAN techniques. I've also gained experience and a small degree of notoriety for project management and change management consulting.
