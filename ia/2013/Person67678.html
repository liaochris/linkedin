Jonathan Feinberg's Summary

I have been able to leverage my 15+ years of experience in both the Meetings and Hospitality Industry to raise the level of conferences for stakeholders and to ensure a positive ROI.

With entrepreneurial determination as Director, events for AIGA, I have created innovative events, adhering to the company brand while attaining high attendance and positive feedback. I was able to launch successful webcasting conferences growing them from 0 to 6 live events per month

As Senior Director of Housing and Conference Operations for the National Association of Broadcasters, I analyzed convention history to develop systems that when instituted aided in consistently controlling expenses and increasing revenue.
