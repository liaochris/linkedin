Jackie Ruddy's Summary

I received my Real Estate License in 1993 and my Associate Brokers License in 1998. A strong belief in education has driven my desire to continually strive to be more than just another agent hence the many designations I have received throughout my career: ABR, CRB, CRS, GRI, SRES, e-Pro, CNE, OA. I have been the recipient of many production & quality service awards through Century 21 since 1995. I have received the PAR Excellence Award (Pennsylvania Association of Realtors) continually since 1999. I was the 2005 President of the Greater Scranton Board of Realtors and 2005 Realtor of the Year.

Specialties: Listing and selling homes.
