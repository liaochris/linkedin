Auke Huisman's Summary

Auke Huisman is a strategist and business innovator. Besides giving strategic advise to his clients he also takes responsibility for the development and implementation, relying on his political and cultural sensitivity. He is supported by a trusted network of influencers and knowledge providers. Thus providing companies (profit and non-profit) with a future-proof and sustainable position in a competitive and international environment.

In his latest assignment at the Dutch Railways (NS) he invented and introduced a new Mobility Integration concept 'Reizen op Rekening' for the public transport in the Netherlands. A joint venture of more then 15 companies has been created to offer the intgrated (self) service to the public. It increases the level of (self-) service quality to commuters and (business) travellers (# 500K+), they can commute door to door with the ease of smart card post payment, internet & smartphone services and access to all public transport facilities nationwide with just one smart key (the NS-Business Card).

Professionalism and excellence in:
- Business strategy and marketing
- Interim (Line or Program) Management
- Crisis Management
- Change- and Turn Around Management
