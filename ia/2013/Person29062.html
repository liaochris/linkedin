Dana Mayer's Summary

Strategist providing training, coaching and consulting that increases sustainable individual and team performance.

Clients include leadership teams from: Kaiser Permanente, DuPont, Microsoft, Bell South, AT&T, Deloitte Touche Tohmatsu, ACA, American Red Cross, Pepsico, Citi, JP Morgan Chase, and MCCA, among others.

Work with key partners to determine best ways to improve productivity, marketing communications, and to access and apply data to address business needs.

Lead or participate on teams to build sustainable organizations and to understand, develop, and execute plans, strategies, methodologies, elicitation, communication, traceability, and other solutions. Take disparate data from a variety of stakeholders to build informed solutions. Create & lead initiatives, often from the ground up in ad hoc situations where there was no previous process in place on which to build.

Specialties:
Maximizing strategies involving global teams of diverse constituents by gracefully nurturing long-term relationships and partnerships for accomplishing goals.
