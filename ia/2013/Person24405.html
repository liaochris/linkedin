Christopher Goode, ALA, LEED AP's Summary

Licensed Architect since 1984 with much experience in Residential, Commercial and Institutional Architecture. Member of the Illinois Chapter of the Association of Licensed Architects (ALA) and is a LEED AP (Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design Accredited Professional).

Specialties

Specialties in Sustainable Design and Historic Preservation as well as Residential Architecture.

Architecture and Conservation is an Oak Park / Chicago-based architectural office providing residential, commercial, and institutional design. We have special expertise in new home design, renovations, additions and historic preservation. Architecture and Conservation is committed to using green design principles to create sustainable, energy-conserving buildings. Our goal is to create places that are enjoyable, functional and enduring.
