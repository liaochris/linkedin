Chad Griffeth, AIF's Summary

Along with John Whaley (other co-founder), designed and developed 401(k) participant advice/managed account solutions that integrates with provider of existing company sponsored retirement plans. Our goal is to provide a conflict free, fee-only fiduciary investment management service that is cost effective for everyday investors (capped fee). Chad coordinates BeManaged's services with plan sponsors, consultants and recordkeepers to improve participants' retirement readiness through customized education, communications to deliver BeManaged's investment and savings advice services.

Chad has been a featured panelist at the annual Fiduciary360 and CFDD national conferences and has organized local fiduciary education events. He has earned the Accredited Investment Fiduciary™ professional designation, awarded by the Center for Fiduciary Studies, which is associated with the University of Pittsburgh’s Center for Executive Education, and is a graduate of Cornell College in Mt. Vernon, IA.

Specialties: 401(k) advice and managed accounts, fiduciary best practices, 401(k) advice regulations, dvice and managed account providers, behavioral finance, investment education
