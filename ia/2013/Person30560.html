Darcie Tanner's Summary

Originally from the states (Texas to be exact), I moved to the UK and began my professional career in 2004.

After joining the LBi Social Media team in 2010 as a Senior Account Manager, I have helped LBi to formulate the company's social offering and have been promoted to head the Social Platform and Community Development (SPCD) team based in Edinburgh.

Since starting with LBi I have worked with clients both locally and globally (Europe, USA, APAC, MENA) ranging from a variety of sectors such as FMCG, consumer goods, online retail, travel & hospitality, finance, food & drink, technology, insurance, beauty and sports to name a few.

Working out of both Edinburgh and London on blended campaigns with the wider agency; from the RFP stage to the strategic and creative concepts, planning delivery and measurement.

While wearing a client services hat, I also manage two teams that focus on platform management, community management, social technologies, social platform tools and participates in platform creation, development, curation, strategy, training, blogger outreach, engagement and implementation of all of the above.

Some of the tools used in my role include: Sysomos, Brandwatch, Engage Sciences, Wildfire, Hootsuite, TGI, Google (various), Adaptly, Omniture and Adobe.

The management side of my role includes new business, resource management, working to and assigning budgets, campaign and project management with a strong consultancy and leadership element.

The role within the media side of the agency means that I have experience working on cross digital projects, enabling me to understand and develop solutions based around and utilizing: Social search/multi-signal search, SEO, PPC, social advertising, display advertising, analytics/tracking, usability/user experience and conversion analysis.

Available for speaking engagements.

Specialties

online marketing, social networks, online promotion, social search, digital marketing, SEO, blogger outreach, technology, platforms, management, social advertising, media relations, consultancy, strategy, photography, writing, analytics, html, multi-signal search, client management, budgeting, idea generation, platform knowledge: Twitter, Facebook, YouTube, Google, Pinterest, StumbleUpon, Wordpress, Tumblr, Vimeo.
