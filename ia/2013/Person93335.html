Michele Perkins' Summary

I am a learning and communications consultant, writer, and presenter. One of my specialties is helping clients integrate training and communications into their strategic planning for launching new products, strategies, and technologies. I have worked with clients across industries to maintain productivity in the face of mergers, corporate splits, and new business models. Assignments have included financial advisory/wealth management, sales, communications, global technology rollouts, customer service, and compliance.

Previously I was the founder and CEO of a consulting firm specializing in training and communications for Wall Street firms. Approaching education as theater, I pioneered “Day in the Life” training that uses exercises to simulate a typical day for the target audience. Traders around the world were taught to use new technologies. Other programs included understanding financial markets, the impact of the Euro, selling new products, and help desk for trading floors. An e-learning game taught basic financial planning and investing.

Before joining the corporate world, I worked as an actress on stage, television, and film. Writing as M.K. Perkins, I published Deadly Play (Langner Press, 2012), a suspense novel set in the theater, and am working on a sequel.

Writing samples are available at www.micheleperkins.com. For the latest news about my novels, please visit www.mkperkins.com.
