Ali Eftekhari's Summary

With a diverse background in real estate, construction, marketing and event promotion, my business mindset has led me to become a successful Realtor with Century 21 Amber Realty and excel as a Contractor and Project Manager for Deco Construction, both in Torrance, Calif.

A native of Tehran, Iran, I received my Bachelor of Science degree in Business Administration with a Marketing emphasis from California State University, Long Beach where I’m an alumni of the Beta Psi chapter of the Phi Kappa Tau fraternity.

Committed to community service, I am an active member of The “Original” Rotary Club of Torrance and have served as president . My past roles have included President (2011-2012), secretary, PR chair and fundraising chair. Further, I use my marketing degree to support all of his business ventures.

At Deco Construction, I manage a myriad of projects and work with local building and safety departments on a variety of residential and commercial construction projects. I joined the industry in 1998 as a junior foreman for F.E. Designer construction where I learned the contracting business.

Likewise, I obtained my Real Estate Sales license in 2005 and my Brokers license in 2011. Working with buyers and sellers for the past eight years, I've developed a deep understanding of the real estate market as well as the relationship between the real estate and construction industries.

In addition, I am an avid fan of technology and gadgets. My main areas of interest are in automotive, computing and mobile technology. Often referred to as the "tech guy" in the group, I am the person people come to when they need to purchase a new phone, tablet, computer, car, or app.
