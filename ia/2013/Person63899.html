Jim Guidera's Summary

Starting in 2011, we committed to gather together leaders that wanted to impact senior housing and care. Serve the industry through methodical thoughtful change incorporating professional executive management services. Census Up leads Change Management in operations, sales, technology and real estate. We increase revenue and lower costs along side of management with the least disruption to the community or associates. What do you think needs to be done to improve your business? What keeps you up at night? What is your end goal, timeframe?

The message: Leaders desire change in sales, costs, maybe to renovate, update, expand, install new technology, improve security, implement electronic records.

Change is needed, but that usually causes resistance and fear for customers, residents, sales team, production team, operations. People talk. Obviously with change someone will be affected in some manner, complain and derail or delay the effort. Internal or External

Census Up serves to outsource that change to an independent voice, our leaders educate and implement the plan, supported by management. We assure it gets done quickly and efficiently.

Our expert team executes the planned effort, bringing specialists to build the right solution for our client. We take the heat for timing and success. Leaving staff leaders protected from the potential backlash and process. Keeping favor with everyone to do their job, continuing to serve their clients and associates.

Our entire team comes from a culture of service and achieved success based mostly on hard work and persistence created opportunity. Census Up offers a financial and revenue generation model for the executives that understand “best value”. We care about the relationships we build and we are credentialed in architecture, law, real estate, finance, and engineering.

Census Up is a place where you’ll find advisors who understand the needs of board members and executive leaders alike.
