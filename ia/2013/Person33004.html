Darren Beattie's Summary

A CSEP Certified Personal Trainer with a personal motto to 'Improve Quality of Life.' On a mission to build a more effective way to coach and get others results through the utilization of technology.

A performance coach with eight years experience in personal lifestyle coaching, focused on providing a multifaceted, yet, personalized approach to address the fundamental components of fitness and wellness. Health can be defined simply as, 'the lack of disease,' whereas wellness is an encompassing holistic way of life connecting mind, body and spirit in a way that results in an overall feeling of well-being.

I coach, consulting on effective wellness modalities, dedicated to utilizing strengths and improving weakness to help clientele achieve optimal results through an expansive knowledge of coaching techniques, movement skill development, energy system training, exercise psychology, and nutrition coaching.

More recently I've started specializing more in various coaching techniques that can apply to our start-up Koachable.

Specialties: Coaching Techniques, Lifestyle Coaching and Management, Weight Loss, Injury Prevention Techniques, Movement Skill Development, Post-Rehabilitation Training and Maintenance
