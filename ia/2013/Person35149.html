Diana Cessna's Summary

Along with helping businesses mitigate losses, we help maximize gains and increase profit margins through increasing efficiencies in asset management. With a concentration in quality control and technology solutions we offer a fully integrated partnership that encompasses solutions for all levels of a corporation.

Our firm allows employers the ability to offer their employees financial and legal resources to help minimize the distraction of economic hardships and avoid falling prey to fraud in financial rescue services. At higher corporate levels, we provide business strategies to leverage mission critical assets to unlock capital that minimizes reliance on bank lending.

With 20 years of banking and mortgage experience, I provide litigation support and specialized research in all real estate matters involving mortgage disputes. My training and background is in business banking, banking law, state and federal compliance and fraud detection with concentrations in Veterans Administration lending and other specialty programs.

Through in-depth analysis of distressed markets, I've developed first hand and front line experience to identify the trends and statistics that weaken financial plans and business strategies.

I also use my skills, experience and resources to help educate consumers, professionals and businesses about their options in distressed markets. My in-depth experience in counseling about finance through the years allows me to help navigate through the important details involved in business efficiencies, modifications, foreclosure, short sale and negative equity.


I am available to speak about critical issues in distressed real estate and the banking crisis at professional and consumer seminars. I provide comprehensive training to professionals and consumers on general topics or tailored to specific business needs.
