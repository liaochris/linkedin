Jeffrey Cramer's Summary

As a Jacksonville, Florida estate planning lawyer, I create estate plans that you understand, that are kept up to date and will work when your family needs them most. My mission is to make sure that my clients never pay one cent of estate taxes; maintain complete control of their assets while alive and well; plan and prepare detailed instructions for themselves and their loved ones in the event of disability; are able to transfer their assets to whom they wish when they want to and in the way they want to, in a private, not public, manner; can protect their loved ones from creditors and predators; pass along their wisdom as well as their wealth and to develop a lifelong relationship as their trusted legal counsellor. I am a member of the National Network of Estate Planning Attorneys. This cutting-edge national group helps keep me continually up to date on changes in the law and best practices and give me the advantage of being able to immediately consult with brilliant and experienced lawyers when needed, all while providing affordable services and personal access to my clients.

We provide an educational workshop to introduce prospective clients to our estate planning process and to explain key legal concepts. This workshop is presented several times each year in our office in the Mandarin area of Jacksonville, Florida and is entitled "The Truth About Estate Planning". It generally runs from 10:45 a.m. until 1:45 p.m. at our office, with a boxed lunch included. Call 904-448-9978 to inquire about workshop times and to make a reservation.

In addition to my estate planning practice, preparing wills and trusts, I utilize my 38 years of civil trial experience to handle estate, trust, probate and guardianship litigation throughout Duval County and Northeast Florida.

Specialties

estate planning, estate administration, probate, guardianship, wills, trusts, asset protection, elder law, medicaid planning, special needs trusts, revocable living trusts, second marriage planning, supplemental needs trusts, trust administration, probate litigation, guardianship litigation, contesting a will, trust contests, removal of trustee, removal of personal representative,undue influence, probate fraud, breach of fiduciary duty,forged documents,spouse's elective share, trust reformation.
