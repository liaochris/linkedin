Vera Wilkinson's Summary

Vera E. Wilkinson is a Certified Dog Behavior Consultant (CDBC), Certified Professional Dog Trainer (CPDT-KA), professional member Association of Pet Dog Trainers (APDT),
International Association of Animal Behavior Consultants(IAABC),Licensed Dogs & Storks Presenter & Licensed Dog And Baby Connection Presenter.

Vera served as the Dog Division Chair for the International Association of Animal Behavior Consultants (IAABC) from 2008-2011, Application Review Panel Committee (IAABC) 2009-2011, Application Review Panel Committee Co-Chair 2009-2011, Education Committee (IAABC) 2009 and received the 2009 Dogs Division 'Member of the Year' Award for work on behalf of the IAABC Dog Division.
