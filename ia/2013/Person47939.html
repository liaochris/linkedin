George Kirby's Experience

Owner & CEO

Budoshin Ju-Jitsu

1967 – Present (46 years)

Design and instruction of martial arts [jujitsu] programs locally & materials as well as conduct seminars throughout the U.S.. Also provide POST arrest & control consultation & instructional services to law-enforcement agencies.

Arrest & Control Consultant

LAPD-CMAAP

1994 – 2008 (14 years)

LAPD Civilian Martial Arts Advisory Panel was created by the LAPD. following the Rodney King incident in 1994, to evaluate & redesign the LAPD's arrest & control training program. I was one of 12-14 martial artists in the Los Angeles area invited to be on this panel. While the members main task was to design and continuously update the arrest & control program, I was also privileged to be involved in not only the evaluation process for the selection of what was to be taught and modifications of those specific techniques, but I was also the only member involved in designing the evaluation program to determine officer success. This changed the evaluation process from simply demonstrating the learned skills, to a scenario environment in which the officer was put into an arrest/control situation and had to demonstrate effective use of the learned skills.

Social Science Teacher

LAUSD - U.S. Grant High School

Educational Institution; 10,001+ employees; Education Management industry

1983 – 2006 (23 years)

As the department elected Social Science Chairperson from 1992 until my retirement in 2006, I supervised 14 teachers, including their instructional subjects and schedules, reviewed and purchased instructional materials for purchase [incluidng computer hardware& software] master teacher for student teachers from CSUN, conducted staff development, designed educational simulations for U.S. Government & Economics classes.
As a Social Science teacher my specialty became U.S. Government [AP & Honors] and Economics [Honors & Regular], although I also taught Geography, U.S. History & entry-level ESL.

Teacher

LAUSD - Olive Vista Jr. High

1968 – 1983 (15 years)

Involved in design and instruction in team teaching programs, designed and taught a variety of elective courses including Ju-Jitsu [in lieu of PE classes], Human Relations & Values, as well as EARTH and HEART team teaching programs. Also served as yearbook coordinator from 1979-83.
