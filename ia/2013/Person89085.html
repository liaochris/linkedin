Matthew Oliphant's Summary

A little about me...

I have 15 years of experience leading product development, user requirements gathering (user testing, interviews, and contextual inquiry) and design of software, web applications, and iOS apps for marketing, ecommerce, business processing, and user community development & evangelism.

This requires me to work with visual designers, developers, business analysts, testers, and upper management to balance business needs, user characteristics, and technical constraints to reach the best solution possible. This not only involves identifying the “whats” and “hows” but acting as a translator between the different groups as they tend to speak different languages.

I have been the User Experience voice in the redesign of two software development processes, am well versed in user research methods, visual design principles, HCI theory and research, interaction design techniques, and all levels of usability testing.
