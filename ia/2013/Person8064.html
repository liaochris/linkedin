Kristina Hopkins' Summary

Buffalo, New York born. Lover of all things design. Passionate about letterpress printing and digital publishing. A sucker for ball terminal and slab serif typefaces. I've been a graphic designer since 2010 and have gained valuable experience working in design and communications environments since 2005. I currently specializes in the design of print and digital publications and sales marketing materials and have an interest in working in branding and gaining more experience in web design.

I live in York, Pennsylvania, never far away from my iPad chock-full of digital magazine, an ever-growing library of graphic design books and magazines. I share my home with Wembley and Cyan, my two cats with polar opposite personalities. I love spending time baking and cooking and am always on the hunt for the next great recipe to whip up in my little kitchen to share with friends.
