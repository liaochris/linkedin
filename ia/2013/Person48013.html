George Sims' Summary

My personal goal is to spend 50% of the time traveling and sailing with my family and friends. My profession goal is to spend the other 50% of time helping others achieve their life's goals and dreams.

My mission is very simple: To help individuals achieve a Healthier and Wealthier lifestyle.

The Vehicle must never be the limiting factor in our Success! The height of our success should be limited only by our ability to develop the Mental, Emotional, Spiritual, Physical & Financial aspects of our own being!

The overall agenda is very simple. We conduct weekly meetings and webinars both LOCALLY and ONLINE.

Several years spent in the Financial Services and Network Marketing industries building sales forces for both myself and others, is the experience which actually solidified my current goals. Some of those people today are millionaires or at least financially independent because of the opportunity and training they received.

Some of my previous experiences includes several years as agency director of an insurance and securities company. I also spent ten years as owner and manager of an area insurance brokerage agency working with independent life and health agents.

Lifetime miscellaneous projects include the development of a subdivision, the ownership of a fast food restaurant, ownership of a four state long distance telephone franchise, the design of a computerized contact marketing system with integrated sales scripts and processes, and various consulting activities related to the personal development, sales psychology, the building and development of sales organizations and small and/or home based businesses.

Specialties: Understanding and teaching all facets and principles of personal development, sales psychology, organization building, and owning of successful traditional small or home based business.
