Diane Griffin's Summary

As the Founder & CEO of Security First & Associates, we provide consulting services to small and mid size defense contractors, in all facets of Industrial Security, to include government Personnel security clearance processing, Facility Clearance processing, JPAS support, DSS & IC inspection assistance. NISPOM & ICD requirements. Also, we provide assistance to individuals or companies that want to process for a government security/facility clearance. We have experience with FOCI and can assistance with PISA Program processing. We offer Polygraph Consultations, JPAS Training & Fingerprint training classes.

It is our goal to simplify the Government Security & Facility clearance process for our clients. It can seem impossible to conquer this process, but through the years, I’ve been able to help people and companies to do just that! I am often asked questions by people who have been trying to find the answers themselves, to no avail. They have looked online, ordered government pamphlets, called different agencies, and spent countless hours on varying types of correspondence, just to end up right back where they started. If any of this sounds familiar to you, or is something you would like to avoid altogether, then you have come to the right place!

Call TODAY at 410-381-8033 to set a time to discuss YOUR security needs.

Please visit our web site: www.securityfirstassociates.com to see other services that Security First & Associates provides.
