Fred Tolchinsky's Experience

Principal

Secrets of the Masters

January 2009 – Present (4 years 7 months) West Palm Beach, Florida Area

Secrets of the Masters develops and helps implement art-based educational and fund raising programs.

Secrets of the Masters can be used by any teacher with minimal training. Art background or artistic talent are not required. The program can be customized to suit any learning environment. It can be used to teach art history and art appreciation directly. It's also useful as a supplemental skill building program for reading, analytical thinking, verbal expression, and social interaction. The art theme captures and maintains the attention of students. Engagement with art stimulates the brain to develop the skills.

Another use for Secrets of the Masters is fund raising. Art has broad-based appeal (according to The Art Newspaper, over 100,000,000 people visit art museums every year!). Secrets of the Masters can be customized to engage and motivate your audience.
