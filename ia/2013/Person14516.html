Billy Bob's Summary

I started Billy Bob's Beds in 1998 in my garage. I thought if I could sell mattresses cheaper than anyone in town that I could
sell a bunch. Well I did and I do. We're the biggest mattress store in San Antonio now and nobody beats our prices. We are authorized Simmons Beauty Rest, Serta, Tempur-Pedic, and Restonic dealers.

We don't just sell cheap mattresses, we
sell cheap mattresses cheap and expensive mattresses cheap.That's why people come from all over South Texas and purchase mattresses from Billy Bob's Beds.

Stop ten people on the street and ask them what word comes to mind when you say "mattress" and one will say "sleep" and the other nine will say "Billy Bob." I'm going to save you a fortune on a mattress that will make you late to work, because you're so special.
