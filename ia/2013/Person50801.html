Paul Guidi DMD PA's Summary

Dr. Paul A. Guidi is originally from Boston, MA and has been in private practice at his Clearwater, Florida office since 1988.

Specialties

Cosmetic bonding, Porcelain veneers, metal-free crowns and bridges, tooth-colored fillings, bleaching, cosmetic tooth replacement, certified Invisalign® provider, periodontal care, implants, laser cavity detection, laser gum treatments, TMJ and bite problems, mercury-free dentistry, custom full dentures, precision partial dentures.
