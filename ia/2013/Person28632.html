Steve Dungan's Summary

D1 Mold & Tool is dedicated to providing its customers with the highest quality die cast dies in the market.
With over 20 years experience and using the latest technologically advanced machinery, D1 Mold & Tool is your full service mold shop. We provide:
Complete design and build of die cast dies and trim tooling
Rebuild of existing dies including engineering changes
In-house design services with full 3D capabilities with UG NX5, Ideas NX5 and Mastercam.
Tooling built with premium H-13 exceeding NADCA standards
Certified heat treat by approved manufacturers' source
High-speed machining with the very latest in machine tool technology
Whatever your need, D1 Mold & Tool is confident we will provide you with the highest quality dies and service at a competitive price.

Specialties

D1 Mold & Tool offers the following services as we continually strive for the highest-quality at the most competitive price:
Build projects ranging in size from small DME style unit dies to multi-cavity dies that fit into 1,000 ton die cast machines.
True high-speed machining of H-13 cavity steel.
Machine tools with 20K and 30K spindle speed, along with three modes of cutting.
Graphite machining with 30K spindle speed, along with three modes of cutting.
CMM inspection service of tooling.
