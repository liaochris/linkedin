John Richette's Summary

Since 1992, a real estate professional representing Sellers and Buyers and specializing in residential properties - estates, homes, condominiums, income property and land/lots - primarily on L. A.'s' Westside, including the Hollywood Hills (East and West), West Hollywood, Hancock Park, Beverly Hills, Bel Air, Westwood, Brentwood and Santa Monica. In addition, I have represented Buyers and Sellers across the San Fernando Valley, including Studio City, Encino, Sherman Oaks and Tarzana.

As an Associate Manager, I have developed my professional skills in communicating, as a manager, and look forward to transitioning into a Branch Office or corporate level position in Real Estate.

Specialties: Negotiations, advising on and interpreting an extensive realm of real estate contracts, planning and conducting large office meetings. Guiding realtors in real estate procedures. Overseeing documentation relating to real estate transactions. Mentoring experienced and newly licensed Associate Brokers and Licensees.
