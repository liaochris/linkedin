Kevin Fry's Summary

Information technology, retail, retail management, and customer service is where my previous experience has been. This has taken me mainly to the grocery department within supermarkets and helping all types of computer users with everything from hardware, to software, to networking. Currently, I'm a half-time student at Full Sail working on my bachelors degree, while finding jobs here and there in IT, and building my own business.

My hobby with computers has landed me jobs here and there fixing computers. This finances my 25+ web sites that are maintained through a couple of secure servers. From PC repair to the development of web sites makes me some side income.

My previous job was sending out trucks for The Salvation Army. This was a relatively new field for me, but one that I was working on from March 2007 to October 2011. I wrote custom software that keeps track of house call pickups on an Intranet that's in use by The Salvation Amy ARC of Philadelphia. This work became dispatching-logistics.com.

My current project is ThisWeeksAd.com which is a website for shopping, aisle directories, product finder, etc.

My goal is to support me and my family through my online ventures, paperless office solutions, and mobile/web development. I would like my computer skills, abilities, and resources to be used more efficiently to better myself and the positions I hold within companies.

Specialties: PHP Programming, MySQL Database Development, Mobile Development Solutions, Paperless Office Solutions, Intranet Setup, Management, Dispatching, Merchandising
