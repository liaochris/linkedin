Dr. Dean Geske's Summary

Geske Chiropractic is the office of Dr. Dean Geske, a leading doctor of holistic chiropractic healthcare in Orange County.

Dr. Dean’s advanced techniques focus on overall health and well-being, not just one part of the body. He recognizes that spinal health cannot be isolated from the rest of the body and mind.
