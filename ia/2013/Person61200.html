Jeffrey Goodman's Summary

Health 2.0 LinkedIn Open Networker

There are many ways to heal, and many forms of treatment, and there is universal agreement that Health is essential to longevity and success in life.

Healthy people are less likely to suffer from the afflictions of aging and disease processes. It is accurate to say that disease is evidence of a lack of Health. Most of what people recognize as disease is preventable through natural means. Health is evidence of a harmonious metabolism, and reasonable lifestyle.

Diet and lifestyle are the foundation for a long and healthy life. Health and wealth are tied together in many ways. A healthy lifestyle includes the ability to afford good food, good living conditions, and a proper education.

There are many difficulties that we face in life, and ill health is a problem for everyone. The cost of being sick is great, for the individual and to society as a whole. Nearly 20% of the United States economy is directly or indirectly part of the "Health Care System". According to recent research the number one cause of bankruptcy is medical costs.

As people age they are more likely to see escalating medical expenses. This directly effects their quality of life, and the expense for their medical care. The cost of medical care for the older generation is significantly paid by the healthier, (mostly younger), segment of the population. This is built into all insurance models.

The only real solution to the Medical Crisis, in both the financial and practical dimension is to deliver preventive intervention. This we most certainly can accomplish with knowledge and technology which is already existing. For this we need to educate the public regarding what they can chose to do that will provide the foundation for a healthy life.

And significantly we need to create a new economic model to deliver products which would be useful to support such choices.
