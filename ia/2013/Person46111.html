Fred Rollo's Summary

Director Principal of Rollo & Company Pty Ltd, a CPA practice that specialises in Australian & international tax, estate and succession planning, professional education, negotiation and mediation, mentoring - executive coaching, and higher education consulting. We also provide the Student Ombudsman Service at Macquarie University in Sydney.

Fifty years as a tax specialist including:
*Early career experience with the Australian Taxation Office (where I established the Withholding Tax Unit in NSW);
*Mid-career appointments as Director of Taxation for 2 of what are now the 'Big 4' international accounting firms (Ernst & Young and Deloitte);
*Teaching tax law and other law subjects to university students; and
*As Director or principal of several boutique tax consulting firms.

Although I have never practiced as a lawyer, I have undertaken 18 units of legal studies (see "Courses" below), and actively engaged with senior lawyers (including directly briefing barristers) throughout my professional career. I have taught the following law courses since 1992:

*Tax Law;
*Advanced Tax Law;
*Ethics of Tax Practice;
*Sport & the Law;
*Tourism Law;
*Business Law;
*Corporate Law;
*Taxation & Estate Planning.

Since 1976, I have also conducted continuing professional development courses in taxation, estate planning and mediation.

I have over twenty years university teaching experience in the accounting, education, management and law disciplines:

* Sessional/contract lecturer at University of Western Sydney from 1992 to 2005.

* Full-­‐time CQUniversity Sydney academic faculty member July 2005 to September 2012, undertaking academic management roles between June 2006 and May 2011 inclusive.

* Sessional lecturer at Australian Catholic University in Sydney 2013
