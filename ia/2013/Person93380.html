Michelle Appel-Kern's Summary

Michelle Appel-Kern (MAK) was born in Nebraska and raised in Los Angeles. She studied at University of Southern California and received her bachelors’ degrees in Communications and Spanish.

MAK started her post collegiate career working for a Hispanic advertising agency called the Vidal Partnership. There, she worked on digital and direct marketing campaigns for both Sprint and Unilever. MAK then decided to further her education and completed her M.S. in Integrated Marketing from NYU, all while working for a search engine marketing agency delivering strategy and execution for various Diageo brands.

After graduating from NYU, MAK spent one year working for Ogilvy & Mather doing demand generation for the IBM account. She was a proud member of IBM’s centennial team, and active in promoting IBM’s supercomputer Watson, as the first robotic contestant on Jeopardy.

At Mak & Ger Michelle spearheads the strategy, development and execution of social listening campaigns. She translates ideas into marketing solutions that generate revenue for her clients, making sure the right message meets the right audience with every click.

Specialties: Digital strategy, integrated marketing, strategic planning, lead generation, business consulting, SEO, SEM/PPC, Social Media Marketing, blogging.
