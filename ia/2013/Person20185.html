Carl Stratton's Summary

Carl Stratton is a Florida Native and has been a Realtor in Tampa Bay since 1997. Carl’s philosophy is based on treating individuals as he would like to be treated. His fair treatment and high level of service has gained him an excellent reputation among his peers, clients and customers.

Prior to taking the position of CEO / Broker with Dennis Realty & Investment Corp. in Nov. 2004 Carl was a very productive and award winning Realtor. As the leader of one of Tampa Bay’s prominent Real Estate firms, Carl is committed to carrying on Dennis Realty’s tradition of service, community & real estate excellence.

Carl is an expert in residential real estate marketing and sales. He is also experienced in commercial sales, property management, business brokerage and investment properties. He has personally closed hundreds of real estate transactions and has also overseen 1000’s more in his current role of CEO / Broker.
