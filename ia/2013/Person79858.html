Michael Moore's Summary

I help lawyers and executives create professional success with personal development and leadership coaching at all levels of experience. I also help organizations increase profits through strategic resource optimization. My proven methods will enhance personal and team productivity, improve client development and increase profits. A few examples include strategic planning, marketing programs and lateral recruitment as a growth option.

My clients benefit from my 25 years of professional experience including both as an executive and a General Counsel, as a legal recruiter and as an executive at a large law firm.My community service includes 13 years on my local School Board as President, Treasurer and Chairman of the Personnel Committee. I have also spent 6 years as a USSF licensed soccer coach for a local elite team of high school age players.

Specialties

Professional development, marketing, business development, client relations, law firm economics, compensation systems, strategic recruiting, retention, individual and team productivity and diversity training.

Michael Moore's Experience

Founder

Moore's Law, LLC

May 2009 – Present (4 years 8 months)

I help lawyers and executives achieve their professional goals. My coaching is a specialized structured process designed to help talented and motivated professionals push past obstacles to personal success. It is not a form of therapy and is not remedial in nature. It requires commitment and focuses on creating specific steps to build a better future. I also work with management teams to create a customized coaching process and follow through with each individual to have their success compliment the organization’s goals.
