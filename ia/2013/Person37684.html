Dilip Abayasekara's Summary

Accredited speaker, communications expert, inspirational keynote speaker, proven leader, certified DiSC trainer, CEO of Dr. Dilip, LLC and owner of Speaker Services Unlimited. Enhances the performance of organizations and individuals by improving their communications, connections, and presentation skills. Provides one-on-one customized professional and executive speech coaching as well as coaching in voice and performance, training seminars on public speaking & presentation skills, leadership, creative thinking via mind mapping, and sales. Professional speaker providing customized inspirational keynotes. Author of

Seminars and workshops on: communication/public speaking/presentation skills; leadership; teamwork, Mind Mapping; sales training.
