Jenni Beattie's Summary

Seasoned marketer with a focus in online marketing. I also focus heavily on the development of social media strategies and delivery of social media training for companies. My previous background included positions with:

Director - Digital Democracy
The Digital Edge - Director of Digital Consulting
Network PR - Online PR Research Manager
News Ltd - Research Manager
Media Monitors - Communication/PR Manager
CARMA International Asia Pac - PR/Research Manager
Cancer Council NSW - Internal Comms Manager
McCann Erickson Advertising - Researcher

I teach a Masters subject called Rethinking Media (Social Media) at UTS within the Communications faculty and in 2011 was awarded a Commendation for UTS Casual Academic Teaching Award.

I am currently working on a project with the Aust Centre for Public Communication (UTS) researching best practice Social Media for Govt Depts (Feb 2012)

I am passionate about health and write a regular column for the RACGP newsletter 'Good Practice' on Health and Web 2.0 as well as being an editorial manager for Australian Health Review (on web 2.0/health topics)

I write regularly for a variety of publications and I have been interviewed on Social Media - example:

Specialties

Media Content Analysis, Social Media Strategy and Training, Online Crisis Communications, Online Community Management, Research and Measurement, Creative Digital Strategy, Knowledge Management, Qualitative research and Internal and external Communications.

Moderator of Australian Marketers on LinkedIn and guest blogger with Digital Ministry and Slice Media.
