Dorota Zys' Summary

I am currently advising clients how to create and manage effective advertising campaigns in accordance with the principles of Inbound Marketing. I offer Inbound Marketing audit services which are a great way of determining what your company’s strong and weak points are. I also provide Inbound Marketing training and consultation sessions on usability, content marketing and conversion optimization with the emphasis on Web design.

In the last 5 years, I have advised professionals from a number of industries and top 500 companies. Some of the projects I worked on received extraordinary “off the chart” results.
I have been responsible for the online presence of over 40 companies (white label), over 20 photoshoots and 70 business illustrations.

In my education and career, I have been fortunate enough to pursue many paths which I now integrate to develop and manage online projects. Right after graduating from the Academy of Arts, I decided to transfer my artistic skills into Web design. In my career, I take advantage of my composition and design skills. I have recently finished my postgraduate studies in the field of Internet and Mobile Applications, which directly reflect some of my professional interests.

I’ve had the pleasure of working with amazing experts who taught me volumes about usability and conversion rate optimization and who also helped me perfect my analytical skills. Given the changes the Internet and the social media have undergone, I believe creating well-balanced content with the emphasis on the visual message is crucial. I consider myself to be a creative person, therefore content marketing ideas come to me naturally.
