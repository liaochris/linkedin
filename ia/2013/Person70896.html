Juliet Kosarzycki's Summary

Juliet Kosarzycki has coached clients across the US and around the world from Thailand to Albania, and has delivered inspirational and educational messages internationally. Her audiences include:

• leaders at world renowned organizations such as AT&T, World Vision, and Save the Children
• students at over 10 colleges and universities nationwide including Carnegie Mellon University in Pittsburgh, PA, University of Minnesota – Minneapolis, Rhodes College in Memphis, TN, and the University of Little Rock –Arkansas
• professionals from a variety of industries attending conferences, conventions and executive showcases

Juliet brings over 20 years of diverse professional experience in corporate, non-profit and international settings and is known for her enthusiasm, high energy and positive outlook. She has empowered teams and led trainings for a Fortune 500 company where she led employees to excellence.

She has been featured on the radio and in newspapers and is passionate about motivating others to get them excited about challenging themselves to uncover what they are capable of achieving.

Juliet received her BA in psychology from Cornell University and her MBA from the University of Wisconsin – Madison. She is an ICF Certified Coach and earned her ACC through an advanced training program from the esteemed Institute for Professional Excellence in Coaching (iPEC). Through iPEC, she also earned her certification as an Energy Leadership Master Practitioner.

She is also an adjunct instructor at Lakeland College (Sheboygan, WI), Northeast WI Technical College (Green Bay, WI), and Lakeshore Technical College (Cleveland, WI).

Do you have a fun hobby, an unusual job, or unique skill? She would love to interview you and have you teach her something new!
