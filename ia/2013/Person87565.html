Martin Bell's Projects

  • The Visitor

    • July 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Martin Bell

    'The Visitor' is an in-production, silent, animated horror short. Written and directed by Martin Bell, with nods to the works of Bram Stoker and F.W. Murnau. It tells the story of crusty old Transylvanian vampire who has not had any company for centuries. When he decides to move to London, he is forced to recieve and entertain a human visitor, and hide his vampirism from him.

    What the vampire does not expect is that he enjoys the human company! But is it reciprocated? Can a human and a vampire ever really be friends?

    CGI Animation, ~8 mins.
    Status: Pre-production.

  • King Bee

    • June 2008 to Present
    Team Members: Martin Bell, Chris Winchester

    'King Bee' is an in-development 2D animated comedy series starring a colourful cast of invertebrates, exploding aphids, and a mind-controlled chicken. Obviously.

    Target: K6-12
    Format: 2D Computer Animation. 52 x 11' episodes
