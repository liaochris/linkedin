Kathi Browne's Summary

I love disruptive technology, innovation, and creative thinking. My passion is applying social technologies to healthcare.

I am the founder of the Healthcare Talk community on Google+. Within this community, I hold broadcast Health Care Hangouts where I facilitate discussions with healthcare professionals to collect their insights, explore new social approaches, and give the healthcare community a larger social presence. I believe opening channels of communication can lead to better results ... in healthcare, or in anything!

I am also the founder of the Healthcare Glass Explorers community on Google+. This community focuses on exploring ways Google Glass (an innovative hands-free smartphone technology) might positively impact healthcare. Here, I hold broadcast hangouts with other beta testers are pushing the limits of Glass in healthcare applications. I also introduce other technology that may work with Glass, and encourage brainstorm discussions that might identify new development.

I specialize in building communities that serve both patients and healthcare providers. I also introduce a variety of social tools and technology to help healthcare organizations work smarter, improve patient satisfaction, and attract new patients. I am especially knowledgeable in Google+ and make a conscious effort to keep a pulse on where social is headed rather than where it has been.

I REALLY enjoy what I do -- building relationships, connecting people, and bringing information to those who need it. People are the most amazing facilitators to success and social media brings them all within reach! I connect through Twitter, Linkedin, Google+, Pinterest, Quora ... there is no "best" platform in my book.

I am also the author of the book TAKING ON TWITTER: STRATEGIC TWEETING FOR SMALL BUSINESS and have a few more in development.

Connections are welcome, but let's put them to good use, alright?

Kathi Browne's Projects

  • Hangouts on Social Media in Health Care

    • July 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Kathi Browne

    I initiate interesting discussions with some of healthcare's top social faces. Our discussions stimulate ideas on using social media in health care and remove the mystery behind how social tools can help hospitals, physicians, and systems communicate in new ways.

  • Hangouts on Healthcare Glass Exploration

    • June 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Kathi Browne, Rafael J. Grossmann, MD, FACS, Heather Evans

    In June 2013, a few select people were chosen to explore Glass technology. I was chosen for my plan to create a community focused on exploring Glass applications in healthcare, and to facilitate conversation toward this purpose. Our community is growing and our broadcast discussions have just begun, and already people are really excited. There is great promise in using Glass not only in medical learning (access to different POV, recorded observation and playback), but in EMR applications, hands-free surgical consult abilities, visual add-ons, etc.

Kathi Browne's Publications

  • Taking on Twitter: Strategic Tweeting for Small Business

    • December 21, 2010
    Authors: Kathi Browne

    Quick reference handbook providing small business owners and entrepreneurs with social media strategies to market their businesses. This guide is designed to quickly get small businessses on their way to using Twitter effectively. Key points are bolded, strategies are explained, and key words are indexed for quick look-up. This guide also explains the strengths and weaknesses of some of the most popular third party applications, focusing on the most affordable options to get the most out of Twitter.
