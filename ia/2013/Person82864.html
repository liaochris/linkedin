Laurie Porter's Summary

Author of The New Change, a workbook that can be used individually or with the whole family. Divided into three parts, this interactive guide contains specific instructions that are easy-to-understand and easy-to-follow.

The New Change Volume I is targeted towards elementary children ages 5-11.

A graduate of Texas Tech University with a Bachelor's in Secondary Education.I have taught elementary education for the last twenty years and also have a total of twenty-three years as an educator. In the summers, I am a swimming instructor. During the winter months, I am a division I women's basketball official.
