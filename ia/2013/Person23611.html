Christopher Padfield's Summary

I am the CEO of DeskPRO Ltd. Our core product is social CRM helpdesk software that centralises the management of multiple channels of customer interaction including email, twitter, facebook, live chat, phone and sms as well as providing customer service publishing tools, sales management and team working functionality.

We are currently developing the next generation of our product, expanding our team and raising a round of finance.

I also have experience as a venture capitalist specalising in seed capital; post proof of concept technology investments. Investment manager for early stage VC fund doing £100k first round investments and "gatekeeper" for the leading London angel network.

Specialties

Product management, PHP/mySQL programming, post proof of concept investments, deal origination, investment analysis, angel investment, syndicate building, technology investment, EIS tax relief, term sheets and deal negotiation, commercial and financial due diligence, web/software technologies, company formation.
