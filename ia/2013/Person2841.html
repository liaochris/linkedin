Angela Keef's Summary

I am passionate about education and special education in NH! I have over 15 years of experience working in the field of special education as a teacher, administrator, consultant, and advocate. I enjoy assisting parents through the education process, and ensuring that schools and families work together in providing the most appropriate services to fulfill the needs of students. I enjoy developing and implementing trainings with school personnel, agency personnel, and parents, and I care deeply about special education laws, parent rights, and advocacy in NH! As a transition specialist with NH Vocational Rehabilitation, I enjoyed assisting counselors and school staff in implementing programs for transitioning youth and preparing them for future employment.

Specialties: Special education law; VR and transition programs; education consulting; team facilitation; education in juvenile justice and child welfare; workshop, training, and conference speaker.
