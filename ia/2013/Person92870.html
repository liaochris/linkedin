Michael Pold's Summary

Working more than ten years in a call center with various aspects of Nordic Microsoft Support, has given me a lot of experience about the call center business in general and specifically about management.
Having worked as a Business Controller in the news paper branch provided additional perspective and experience.

I am an open minded person with a positive outlook, great social skills and a compatible sense humor. I am interested in learning new skills as well as utilize those I possess already.

I like to think of myself as dedicated and focused and as one who can be trusted both on a professional and personal level.

Specialties: Ten years contact center experience, covering 1st and 2nd line technical support, a mentor position and team leader experiences in both Nordic Microsoft Personal and Professional Support - Also a great background for positions such as Quality Coordinator, Operations Manager or Project Manager.

At Microsoft Support I have worked with co-workers from Denmark, Canada, Finland, Iceland, Norway and Sweden. The multinational environment is a great learning experience, as is working on site in both Piteå (S) and Copenhagen (DK).
