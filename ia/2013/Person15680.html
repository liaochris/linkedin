Bo Dash's Experience

Principal Technical Editor

Envirocal

1994 – Present (19 years) Antioch, CA

Provide comprehensive document quality assurance services — technical editing, word processing, template creation, style guide development, and résumé design/development — to both private-sector and public-sector clients in a wide range of environmental disciplines.

Technical editing services consist of checking and correcting grammar, punctuation, and spelling; editing for continuity, voice, and clarity of expression; rewriting awkward verbiage; ensuring consistency and correct usage of numbers, acronyms, proper nouns, references, citations, and heading levels; resolving contradictions within and between paragraphs and chapters; organizing and incorporating data; and working within the parameters of company or project-specific style guidelines.

Manage multiple project documents for various clients simultaneously, with minimal direction/supervision, often under tight deadlines. Employ efficiency, attention to detail, creative problem-solving, and clear communication to complete projects that are technically and grammatically correct, consistent, on time, and on budget.

Clients include environmental, civil, geotechnical, and transportation engineers; geologists, engineering geologists, and hydrologists; biologists, foresters, and other natural resource managers; environmental scientists; urban, environmental, and transportation planners; cultural resource specialists; federal, state, and local governments and regulatory agencies; industrial hygienists; and environmental organizations.
