Gary Stripling's Summary

GARY STRIPLING has been leading others for over 30 years as a conference speaker, associate pastor, minister of music & worship leader, business administrator, road manager, consultant, office manager, and corporate development administrator.

Gary founded The Management Agency (TMA) in 2005 as a direct response to ministries and Christian artists' need for professional management and development services at a reasonable price provided by someone with experience specifically related to their fields.

He has served as personal manager for Paul Wilbur (Integrity Music), Lynn DeShazo (Integrity Music), Bob Fitts (Maranatha & Integrity Music), Michael Gungor (co-wrote Dove nominated "Friend of God"), Meredith Andrews (now with Word Music), and many other independent artists nation-wide.

As a conference speaker, Gary utilizes his years of experience in the church and business worlds to help others develop a mature understanding of their calling. Gary regularly consults with touring artists and bands providing career counseling and developing customized management plans that are strategically implemented to help take their ministries to new levels. He also works with individual churches and ministries in setting up their offices, administrative systems, and team & leadership development.

His specialized understanding of the music ministry coupled with formal training in church business and ministry administration provides him with the ability to help others see the Kingdom of God come in power on the earth through their own ministries!

Gary and Laura, his wife of 32 years, live in Jacksonville, FL with their son Elliott. Their daughter, Emily, lives in New York City where she is a special events manager & wedding planner. They attend Celebration Church in Jacksonville.
