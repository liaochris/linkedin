Paul Barry's Summary

An actor, director, writer and acting teacher, Paul has worked extensively across film, television, stage, commercials and voice-overs in Australia.

Paul specialises in training actors in his classes to translate generalised direction into specific action; avoid clichés; draw attention for their ‘choices’ instead of their ‘habits’; and create work of which they are always proud. Paul has taught well over two thousand students, many of whom have secured or currently hold lead and guest roles in television, stage and film in Australia, England and America.

His time is well and truly split between Sydney and Los Angeles.

Specialties

Additionally, Paul’s experience as a professional ‘reader’ for hundreds of castings over the years has given him unique insight into what works and what doesn’t.
