Mia Maja Hansson Frederiksen's Summary

My fascination of the cultural industries stems from the opportunities these provide for transforming the mindset of an audience.

The reason I am studying creative business processes is to understand how creative ideas are generated enabling me to be a marketer who can connect the idea with the market.

My competences in organizing and coordinating complex business processes are excellent. I have practiced these by working with film and music festivals, and, currently, I am establishing a community around an edutainment computer game.

Throughout my undergraduate studies, I have applied the disciplines of marketing, accounting, project management and coordination to my field of interest, which for the last couple of years has been the market conditions for computer games.

Personally, I have a creative mindset, which I apply in the way I understand and collect insights on a market. I am an ambitious, energetic person with a great drive!

Project Assistant

Copenhagen Innovation and Entrepreneurship Lab - CIEL

Government Agency; 11-50 employees; Higher Education industry

October 2012 – Present (1 year 1 month) Copenhagen Area, Denmark

At CIEL - Copenhagen Innovation and Entrepreneurship Lab - we aim to create an international lighthouse for knowledge-based innovation and entrepreneurship (I&E) founded on a unique alliance between the University of Copenhagen (UCPH), the Technical University of Denmark (DTU), and Copenhagen Business School (CBS).

The CIEL alliance demonstrates a new model for collaboration by combining the perspectives of each of the three universities and real life cases to produce innovation with substantial societal impacts.

CIEL is initiated by a seed-fund of 36 mio. DKK and is currently implementing nine novel I&E programs within the areas of education, co-curricular and research. Go to http://ciel-lab.dk/ and get to know our background and the many CIEL-projects.

I am working with co-curricular activities and especially I create events and network activities around the online computer game, Startup Spirits, which is an edutainment game created to raise awareness of entrepreneurship.

This is done by collaborating with engaged and driven students at the three universities in order to organize events in the interest of the students so as to present the computer game in their environment.
