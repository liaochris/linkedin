Cheryl Smithem's Summary

Cheryl Van Landingham Smithem is the Connection Maven. She jokes that she’s the expert at the 6 ½ degrees of separation gambit. Experienced in branding, marketing, public relations, website design, project management, and copywriting, Cheryl enjoys seeing projects move forward and delight the client. She absolutely relishes an opportunity to use her gray matter to solve strategic marketing questions. Before establishing Charleston PR & Design, LLC, Smithem was a marketing and public relations executive for top Charleston corporations. She has been consulting since 2001.

Cheryl's responsibilities for Charleston PR & Design include creative direction, public relations planning, crisis communications consultation, social media messaging development and planning, SEO strategy for client websites, and marketing strategy development.

Cheryl is an experienced, senior consultant for many firms who find themselves in a crisis. She has consulted with medical firms in crisis as well as educational institutions undergoing child sex abuse charges. She has guided communications strategy in legal situations where class discrimination was the topic and has helped conduct crisis audits.

Charleston PR & Design, LLC, provides "big agency" knowledge to small business problems. The firm works with entrepreneurs and small businesses who are launching, relaunching or re-branding themselves.

Specialties: **Branding, SEO and content marketing, WordPress.org website design and development, public relations campaign development and implementation, strategic marketing plans and implementation, media relations, crisis management, reputation management, event management, marketing collateral development. Charleston PR & Design, LLC also provides hosting and custom WordPress theme development.
