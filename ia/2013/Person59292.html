Junsik (Jason) Kim's Summary

Junsik (Jason) Kim is a San Francisco based user experience / interface designer with experience in interaction and industrial design.
Recently graduated with an MFA in Design & Technology at Parsons with honors.

Participated and has been awarded in different events and competitions such as the Nokia Lumia 820 Design Challenge, TEI 13 Conference, Maker Faire 2012, KODDCO Online Competition, International Design Forum, and has also participated in a reality survival TV show sponsored by MTV / Motorola.

User experience design - user interface, interaction, web / mobile (HTML, CSS, jQuery, C++), concept generation, user research, wireframing, story telling, usability testing, rapid prototyping
Design - product, industrial, communication design
Engineering - 3D CAD (SolidWorks, Autodesk Alias Design, Keyshot), processor coding (Arduino)
Production - 2D / 3D Fabrication (3D modeling, laser cutting), print production

Fascinated by photography and often travels in order to experience diverse cultures. Jason welcomes you to contact him with any questions or just to say hello.
