Geoffrey Crowell's Summary

I am a visual effects artist with experience in both commercials and feature films.

Originally started out as a banker in 2005. while working in that industry, i realized that my interest has always been within Film and Visual Effects. From that, I decided to make a career change and pursue what i truly had a passion for. I was lucky enough to pursue Graduate School, receive a Master of Fine Arts degree in Visual Effects, and immediately get involved on the ground floor with some great FX studios, first in Atlanta, now beyond.

My goals remain to be: problem solving, exploration and growth, thinking outside the box, and executing phenomenal work all over the globe.

Specialties

Compositing, Matte Painting, Particle/Dynamics Simulations, 2D/3D Motion Tracking, Rotoscoping, Stereoscopic Conversion, Motion Graphics, Editing, Storyboarding/Pre-Visualization & Graphic Design

Applications: Nuke, Houdini, Vue, After Effects, Mocha, PF Track, Silhouette FX, Maya, Cinema 4D, ZBrush, Turbulence FD, Final Cut Studio, Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere, Dreamweaver, Flash, InDesign
