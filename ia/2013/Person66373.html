John Garrett's Summary

My former career as a CPA laid the foundation for my clean comedy show. I have been heard on The Bob & Tom Radio Network, seen in concert with Train, opened for Louie Anderson at resort casinos, and participated in The Boston Comedy Festival. Tracks from my debut comedy album "Outside the Box" can be heard on Sirius|XM Satellite Radio. Audiences across the country find that I am the adorably obnoxious guy they wish they had in their boring office, unless of course they were my manager.
