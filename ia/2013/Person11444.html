Rav Badial's Summary

While obtaining my Bachelors of Mathematics degree at the University of Waterloo I discovered my passion for all things involving Finance.

During the course of planning with a wide array of clients over the past several years, I have seen how often Financial Planning is overlooked and it's power under estimated.

This has only fueled my passion and desire.

As the world around us, the economy and our lives continue to change at an incredibly rapid pace, it has become that much more critical for us to become proactive and create financial plans for ourselves.

For those professionals that are employed, self employed and own their own business, creating a Financial Plan can create a clearer picture for today and a road map to tomorrow.

For the past several years I have been working with clients to help them plan. Together we strategize to: work towards succession, Increase employee retention, protect their businesses against unplanned events, and work towards retirement.

Specialties

Retirement planning, Employee benefits plans, Risk management
