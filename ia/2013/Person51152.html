Gregory Wood's Experience

Publisher

Attorney at Law Magazine

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry

January 2013 – Present (7 months) Greater New Orleans Area

Strategically focused, locally relevant, and deeply engaging, Attorney at Law Magazine is a tightly focused business-to-business trade magazine for and about local attorneys and the business of practicing law.  Our engaging content offers compelling and intelligent editorial from local attorneys, industry specific news and information, and columns designed to deliver a unique perspective to legal professionalism.

Attorney at Law Magazine features a highly visible and well-respected private practice attorney on the cover every month as well as interesting feature stories on specialty attorneys, firms, and other related industry professionals inside.   Hard copies of Attorney At Law Magazine are mailed to more than 5,000 attorneys in Greater New Orleans in private practice. Unlimited digital copies are emailed to select list of recipients. This includes numerous in-house corporate counsels of major corporations, and all government attorneys in Greater New Orleans. Each digital issue gives you the ability to flip through the pages of the magazine, while providing direct links to a number of websites, e-mail addresses and valuable resources.  

 Attorney at Law Magazine is a one of a kind professional trade magazine for and about your local legal community with a very specific editorial focus on business development and practice management. Delivered to the entire legal community in Greater New Orleans, both as four color glossy mailed publication as well as our digital email edition, Attorney at Law Magazine offers a very focused mix of informative and educational monthly columns and articles covering the trends, developments, and industry news together with compelling feature stories and profiles on leading attorneys, law firms, and related professionals who are the driving forces in the local profession. For more details or advertising information please visit www.attorneyatlawmagazine.com.
