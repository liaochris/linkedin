Julian Becerra's Summary

I have over 15 years of experience in visual communications, competitive marketing strategies, and interaction design processes; three areas that can make businesses thrive. As an individual and as a team player, I flourish in a supportive and collaborative work environment that relies on constructive feedback.

I have studied Integrated Marketing Communications at the University of California in Berkeley, Graphic Design at the Academy of Art University in San Francisco, and Multimedia Arts & Technologies at Santa Barbara City College. My passion for both art and education has led me to broaden my horizons in the fields of user experience design, photography, video, and non-traditional marketing.

I am driven by social, cultural, and environmental contemporary issues, which are a great source of inspiration. Aesthetics and balance are characteristics that I highly regard when developing and evaluating pieces of communication.

I invite you to explore my portfolio and hope you enjoy viewing it as much as I enjoyed creating it. To view samples of my work, please visit: www.graphicmint.com

Specialties

- Design & Development: Conceptual Design, Brand Identity, Photography, Multimedia Design.
- User Experience: Interaction Design, User Interface Design, User Research, Usability Design.
- Consulting & Contracting: Online Marketing, Creative Process, Community Building, Multilingual Outreach.
- Strategy & Direction: Art Direction, Marketing Strategy, Advertising, Project/Product Management.
- Visual Foundation: Fine Arts, Graphic Design, Print Production and Web Production.
