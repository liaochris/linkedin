Leigh Anne DuChene's Summary

I am first and foremost a wife and mother. I choose to stay home with my children, and we unschool/homeschool. We spend our days enjoying and learning from life. I love to read, particularly Young Adult and New Adult books. I am the author of "Nourishing Origins' Holistic Baby-Led Feeding", an aspiring YA author. I am also a Spiritual Counselor, Life Coach, Retreat Facilitator, and Public Speaker.

I am the founder and leader of DFW Holistic Families (2007). I am an educator, mentor, coach, author, and blogger. I have been working in the field of natural and holistic wellness and living since 2004; and I have been a leader, speaker, and trainer in the conscious, holistic, and natural parenting field for since 2007.

I strive to educate families and individuals on the importance of conscious, holistic, and natural living and parenting and to train professional educators, coaches, and community leaders to further advocate and spread the message of conscious living and parenting.
