Herbert D. Hentzen, III's Summary

At Hentzen Coaching, I believe leadership makes all the difference - whether it is leading yourself or leading others. It's how we impact our own lives, but more importantly, it's how we impact others. Leadership is the action we must live out.

As a certified leadership coach and a founding partner on the John Maxwell Team, I can offer you workshops, seminars, mastermind groups and coaching that will aide your personal and professional growth. Through study and practical application of proven leadership, career, career transition and business principles and practices I will help move you, your team or your organization in the desired direction to reach your goals and objectives.

My desire is to help others find their voice, trust their heart and take their own leap of faith of personal and professional growth and development. And the best part about coaching, teaching and speaking is seeing in others the 'aha' moments, the insights gained and the resolve to learn, grow and share what you've learned.

My mission is to encourage and inspire everyone I connect with to understand and pursue their life purpose and live their God designed LEGACY today.

Specialties: "Your LEGACY by Design" Mastermind Series, Leadership Development, Personal and Professional Growth Teaching and Training
