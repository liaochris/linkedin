Laura A. Barclay (Puchtell)'s Summary

(Upcoming Events information can be found at www.civilitycentre.com)

Laura, a Certified Etiquette and Certified Leadership Consultant, provides etiquette classes, etiquette training, coaching, international communications training, leadership development, performance management, and organization development for individuals and businesses.

Laura is a highly regarded consultant, expert, and keynote speaker. She has been featured on television, radio, quoted in numerous newspapers and magazines, and is coauthor of The Power of Civility.

She is committed to civility, to business and social etiquette in our world, and devoted to building civility and respect within and across organizations, schools, communities, and families. As an engaging and dynamic speaker, she provides information-packed seminars with content that is immediately applicable, helping you navigate the nuances of communication, build relationships, create positive interactions, and strong leadership teams.
