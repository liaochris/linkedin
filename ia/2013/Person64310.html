Jeroen Janssen's Summary

Jeroen received his Master in Architecture with Distinction from the Emergent Technologies and Design Program at the Architectural Association in London and received his Master of Science from Eindhoven University of Technology.
He is a registered architect in the Netherlands and has been running his architectural office for almost 3 years.

His current focus is on self-organisation in biological systems, urban metabolism and emergence and specifically how simulations in a computational environment can reveal new inherent understandings in this field of research.

Specialties

Urban Metabolism, Emergence, Self-organisation in Biological Systems, Architecture, Parametric Design
