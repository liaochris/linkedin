Guilherme Jaccoud's Summary

Guigo published his first website in 1996, and hasn't been able to shake the bug since. His skills in ShockWave Flash (formerly FutureSplash Animator), granted him a position in São Paulo's first digital agency, McLuhan Media. He belongs to a small group of pioneers who helped make the web more friendly and beautiful during the first Browser War, a dark period in human history where the progressivism of Netscape Navigator was conquered by the tyranny of Microsoft's Internet Explorer.

In 1999 the agency was chosen to represent the country in the world's first City Web Design Exhibition, called MyCity, in which the work of his team, together with the work of 50 foreign agencies, was showcased and later published in a book of the same name. Absorbed by the dot-com bubble, he left his position at StarMedia to embark in the advertising industry and for more than ten years he worked for major agencies such as Ogilvy, Wunderman, Rapp and Havas (formerly Euro RSCG).

In 2009 he joined the multi-cultural team of iGOA, a prestigious digital agency with offices in Goa and Istanbul, before jumping into an amazing adventure throughout India, working as a freelance designer and background actor for several Bollywood movies. Back in Brazil, he started his own business and founded the start-up Offertas.com, which became the first group buying social network.

Guigo is passionate about graphic design, the open source web and the simple way of life. He has a ever-increasing desire to help people and is always looking for opportunities to challenge himself. He is a kung-fu practitioner, a strict vegetarian, a talented musician and a hobbyist music producer. He currently lives in Florianópolis where he enjoys the sunrise in the mountains and the sunset on the beach, always in company of his dog and a hot cup of spicy ginger tea :)
