Junior Designer

Click Industrial Design

Design industry

May 2012 – June 2013 (1 year 2 months) Sheffield

I originally interned for Click through the summer of 2012 from May untill September. I managed to gain experience in concept generation, CAD, prototyping and design for manufacture, with one product that I helped to make fit for manufacture now on sale. I also met with clients, discussing progress on projects and helping to put their input into the development process.

I also spent time sourcing parts and materials, these were mainly for prototype purposes which I later assembled or tested. I worked on a number of projects including lighting, cycling accessories, children's play equipment and outdoors products.

While working with Click I have already seen one product, that I was heavily involved with, go to market .

Having done pieces of work for them while I was back at University I returned in February 2013 to complete 12 weeks full time. This boosted my skills further getting involved in almost every aspect of working in a design consultancy.

I was in direct contact with manufacturers throughout my time at Click both in the UK and abroad, I worked with these manufacturers to find cost effective ways to create parts and products.
