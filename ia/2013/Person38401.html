Dr. Trevicia Williams' Summary

Dr. Trevicia Williams is a psychologist, helping people "get there" guru for family and work life success, talk show host, author, motivational speaker and former Texas child bride. She has been featured on Oxygen, Lifetime, Lifetime MovieNetwork, SheIsDallas, African American News & Issues, Dallas Weekly and numerous radio shows including Disney Radio! She has coached, trained, and delivered keynotes to diverse groups of people for over 20 years, including, Plano Women's Group, Mary Kay Cosmetics, Arbonne, Project Head Start, University of Houston, Disney World Executives, and, Girl Scouts of America to name a few. She also provides tips and expert commentary on radio and tv shows.

Dr. Williams adds new perspectives and life changing meaning to everyday experiences. Wisdom gained from personal experiences coupled with over a decade of academics in human behavior enables her to teach winning success strategies while inspiring and motivating others to turn adversities into advancements, win against odds, understand the 10/90 Rule, discover the essence of life's experiences, discover who they are, then, do something amazing.

She also facilitates effective professional development training programs related to entrepreneurship, leadership, and, workplace success. Through her training, companies experience improved productivity, cohesive teams, increased employee satisfaction, increased retention and workplace problems such as poor morale and poor team work are resolved.

As a former Texas Child Bride, Dr. Williams inspires and empowers others. Through her Christian faith, tough decisions and perseverance, she succeeded against the odds of adversity. In her bounce back book:There Are Pearls in the Pain of Every Experience, she cuts to the chase about, at 14, being picked up from high school by her mother, carried to JP court for marriage, and, her life as a 14 year old African American girl in an arranged marriage to a 26 year old man from Germany in Houston, Texas.
