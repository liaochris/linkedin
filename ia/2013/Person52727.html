Henriette Weber's Summary

So who is she anyway, this heavily digital experienced and curly-haired strategist, published writer, public speaker, boardmember, initiator and visionary profile ?

I work with curious brands who are ready for the next digital step. Brands who wants to speak louder, more creatively and relate better to their surroundings - whatever type of surroundings it may be.

I'm known to have a unique, surprising and razor sharp approach to business - especially digital business, gigantic businesses (on the advisor side) and startups. Oh! and I am highly appreciated for my input regarding planning and implementation of digital strategies, and my knowledge for simplifying the digital tools used for business and brand growth.

I'm also on the advisory board for several startups and incubators such as Toywheel and CPH business.

Specialties: branding, involvement, substance, identity, social media, creativity writing, strategy, public speaker / speaking gigs, community management, social media management, launch plans.
