FaithWalk Films' Summary

About FaithWalk Films Global, LLC:
An independent motion picture production company assembled from creative and business entertainment industry professionals with a common goal of producing dynamic independent films. FaithWalk Films mission is to produce films that will entertain, educate, surprise and refresh the spirit of true independent filmmaking through provocative story- telling. FaithWalk Films Global, LLC. formed in 2010 and is managed by William M. Parker II.

For more information about FaithWalk Films latest project, The Last Party on Earth visit: www.thelastpartymovie.com
