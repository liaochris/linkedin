C Michael DePew's Summary

I have over 30 years experience in the industry of selling and leasing trailers. Currently, I own my own company called CMD Trailer Sales and Leasing; which sells higher quality pre-owned Class 8 trucks and straight trucks along with a wide variety of new and used trailers. Whether you are a single owner operated, small-fleet owner, private fleet, common carrier, power leasing company or government agency, I can help you find what you need.

Some of my many expertise I possess are:

* Dorsey Dump Trailers * Mate Dump Trailers * Alumatech Dump Trailers * TiBrook Dump Trailers * Dorsey Flatbeds * Fontaine Flatbeds * Great Dane Flatbeds and Reefers * Lufkin Flatbeds * Innovative * Dynaweld * Summit * Trailstar * Utility Flatbeds * Reefers *Mac Dump * Mac Transfer * Summit Trash Transfer Refuse Trailers * Peerless Chip Trailer * Peerless Live Floors* East Trash Transfer Refuse Trailers* Travis.
