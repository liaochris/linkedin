Dante Dunlap's Summary

I have worked in production and event management for over 25 years, accumulating a diverse background in theater, television, film, and large scale event management. My client roster has included companies such as Liberty Media Group, , Great American Country, The Discovery Channel and CBS Sports. For 8 years I was the Technical Director for the Denver Performing Arts Complex and managed production operations for its three largest theaters: Boettcher Concert Hall, Ellie Caulkins Opera House, and the Temple Hoyne Buell Theater. During my time at the DPAC I was responsible for coordination of equipment, material and labor for over 600 events a year. Currently I am Owner of DNA Event Management and LEG Valet and Transportation. I am the Technical Manager for the NYC Times Square New Year's Eve Celebration 2009,2010,2011,2012,2013. You can find out more at www.dunlapandassoc.com

Specialties

I specialize in production management and event management. Experienced with union negotiation and labor management.Event Logistics and Execution.
