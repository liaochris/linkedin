President

Mary M. Hudgens, PLLC

November 2012 – Present (11 months) 8090 Precinct Line #103, Colleyville, TX 76034

I have started my own practice beginning in November of 2012. I am looking forward to the challenges and the new experiences that this will bring into my life.

Mary graduated from the University of Texas at Arlington with her accounting degree. She worked for a couple of local CPA firms in Arlington while in school. In 1986, she started her own Fort Worth bookkeeping practice and 10 years later sold her practice. In 2007, she joined Patrick H. Admire’s firm in Fort Worth where she went from tax return preparer to tax manager. August of 2011, she joined Stovall, Grandey & Allen as tax manager. In December of 2012, she opened her own practice​ in Colleyville, Texas.

In her free time Mary loves to travel and has traveled to some amazing destinations, Egypt being her favorite. Because she enjoys scrapbooking, she has become an amateur photographer. Her photography has allowed her to capture her family’s memories and scrapbooking allows her to document her family’s life story. She has mounted some of her photos on canvas and they are displayed in her home and office. She enjoys going on cruises and her cruise line of choice is Disney.

Mary is married and has one daughter, two long-haired dachshunds, and one grand-dachshund.
