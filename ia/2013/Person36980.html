Nonprofit; 1-10 employees; Fund-Raising industry

October 2009 – Present (4 years)

Founded SantaPawsDrive.com, the first-ever international "online toy drive" to send holiday gifts and cheer to shelter animals. The idea came from wanting to do something similar to "Toys for Tots," but for animals -- and all online to make it simple for everyone to donate and get involved.

In the past three years, we have collected more than $30,000 in toys, treats and cash donations for 18 different rescues and shelters worldwide. In 2011, we doubled all past efforts, raising over $15,000.

What started out as a crazy idea turned into the one thing I am most proud of doing. I fully coordinate online fundraising events using social media, Facebook, Twitter and blogging to raise awareness and drive donations, manage the website and lead a team of four other Santa Paws Drive "elves."

Aside from being able to send monetary and gift donations to the shelters, I have also developed an increasing corporate backing for the drive, as pet companies such as Purina, Hill's Pet Nutrition and Greenies have been eager to get involved in such a special effort.

This year, our goal is to become a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization.
