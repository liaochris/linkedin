Brent Brookbush's Experience

Author

Fitness or Fiction - The Truth About Diet and Exercise

September 2011 – Present (2 years) Paperback and Kindle

Getting and staying fit is a science that has been developing for decades. Research is progressing faster than ever, and there are credentialed professionals working hard to make this information available to you. Not just pretty bodies interested in your wallet, but professionals interested in your well-being. This book contains no gimmicks, fads, myths or misinformation. Instead, more than 600 references are used to bust dozens of myths, and provide dozens more practical suggestions for better results. The book is written in fun, easy-to-understand, plain English so everyone from the novice exerciser to the fitness professional can enjoy.

B2C Fitness LLC - Integrated Education System

January 2010 – Present (3 years 8 months)

B2C Fitness provides more than a series of courses. The integrated education system is designed to improve trainer competency and confidence, enhance customer service offerings, and be an integrated part of the fitness service business model. Each course has been expertly designed to ensure a high rate of retention and skill acquisition; improving “on-the-floor” skills, resulting in fitness business growth. But that is just the beginning. B2C fitness is providing service to a variety of clubs, who in turn have an impact on the development of programs. The input of every B2C Fitness costumer is providing resources and experience that are key in creating an unmatched educational program. Every B2C Fitness club is a member of a team that will provide the industry with the educational solution we have been looking for. As B2C Fitness is an outsourced training and development solution, you will also find the price point for all proposed programs to be far below the cost associated with an “in-house” training and development department
