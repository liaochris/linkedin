Marcia Reynolds, PsyD, MCC's Summary

When she asked her clients why they hired her, they told Marcia that she opens them to new ideas and possible solutions. One woman called her a "Catalyst for Conversation."

Marcia Reynolds, PsyD and Master Certified Coach coaches leaders and creates customized programs in leadership and emotional intelligence designed to accelerate growth in your organization.

She understands organizational cultures, what blocks communication and innovation and what is needed to bring people together for better results. Her well-honed coaching perspective adds a keen strategic viewpoint to all issues her clients encounter. Her services include:

• One-on-one executive coaching, leadership training and organizational consulting for corporations and government agencies.

• Customized seminars particularly in the areas of leadership, emotional intelligence, and coaching skills.

• Keynote speaking for conferences.

She has worked in 33 countries in 6 continents. Clients include Medtronic, Sanofi, British Telecom, Maersk, American Express, AT&T, and Ernst & Young plus many government agencies, universities and women's associations.

Her greatest success story is her work with a semiconductor company facing bankruptcy. Marcia’s focus on increasing communication across organizational lines and empowering cross-functional teams were a major part of the turnaround to become the #1 stock market success in the US when they went public in 1993.

Dr. Reynolds a past global president of the International Coach Federation and one of the first people to achieve the designation Master Certified Coach.

Quotes from her books Outsmart Your Brain and Wander Woman: How High-Achieving Women Find Contentment and Direction have appeared in Psychology Today, Harvard Management Update, CNN.com, and The Los Angeles Times, and she has appeared on ABC World News.

Contact Dr. Reynolds for a complimentary copy of the first chapter of her book, Outsmart Your Brain. Marcia@Outsmartyourbrain.com

Specialties

New models of leadership, leading through difficult times, rising-star women in corporations, technical top talent, emotional intelligence, change management, executive coaching and coaching skills for managers.

Marcia Reynolds, PsyD, MCC's Publications

  • Wander Woman: How High-Achieving Women Find Contentment and Direction

    • Berrett-Koehler
    • June 11, 2010
    Authors: Marcia Reynolds, PsyD, MCC

    You are smart, strong and driven to achieve success. Did you know that these strengths can also drive you crazy? Based on solid research and years of coaching top performers and entrepreneurs, internationally renowned leadership coach Dr. Marcia Reynolds wrote Wander Woman for women who are constantly searching for “something more” and find their greatest strengths can sometimes derail them. This book provides practical exercises, powerful questions and case studies to help you channel your restless energy into a more focused, fulfilling, and truly successful path. Read the book and then share it with the women and men in your life who need to better understand you! If you want a free workbook to use while doing the exercises, email me at Marcia@wanderwomanbook.com.

  • Outsmart Your Brain

    • Covisioning
    • October 1, 2004
    Authors: Marcia Reynolds, PsyD, MCC

    To feel increase your persuasive powers, feel more positive energy, stimulate your creativity, and live a healthier more joyful life, you have to be smarter than your habitual brain. Once you know how your brain operates, you can consciously choose how you want to feel and act. Knowing how to shift your emotional states at will is the most important factor in achieving success and happiness. Outsmart Your Brain is full of exercises, examples and guidelines that teach you how to tap into your hidden mental powers to make better decisions to create the results you desire.
