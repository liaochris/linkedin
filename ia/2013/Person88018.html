Mary B Gallatin's Summary

In March 2012, Mary B. Gallatin joined Bert Davis Executive Search, Inc as Vice President bringing her extensive network from the worlds of marketing, media and publishing. Mary's areas of expertise include:

•Recruiting candidates through networking, cold calling, internet search, online and internal databases, and referrals

•Assembling a multiple candidates who can equally and successfully deliver on the search requirements

•Provide functional area expertise by understanding client business objectives

For more than 35 years, Bert Davis Executive Search has been the premier provider of executive placement and consulting services for the publishing, information and new media industries.

Specialties: Recruitment, Staffing Strategy, Candidate Assessment, Compensation Strategy & Negotiation, Executive Placement, Retained Search, Integrated Marketing Communications, Brand Strategy
