Maggie Jo Hilliard's Summary

Maggie Jo Hilliard, Esq. is a practicing litigation attorney, business development guru, graphic designer & creative artist who, from time to time, finds her way into films, reality shows, acting & singing gigs...

From 2003-12, she actively pursued a litigation tract legal career before moving to NYC to begin pursuing her ultimate dream - creating a simple pathway for people to become successful entrepreneurs. MJ has expanded her creative skill-set, combining it with first-hand entrepreneurial & legal experience, & now advises small & medium-sized businesses in increasing their market share while efficiently & cost-effectively tackling new-age marketing. Staying savvy on trends of the big advertising companies, consultanting firms & legal trends, MJ applies big ideas to small companies. Dabbling in a bit of this-and-that (always), MJ has creative answers for tackling problems facing small businesses that can make-or-break the bottom line. Right now, MJ has wrapped up the design & implementation of her creative portfolio site at www.maggiejo.com & will soon begin further development of Majodo, her newest creative business development adventure.

MJ believes the core of Americanism is small-business - it's her goal to tackle the complexities of small-business development, management & funding making entrepreneurship dreams come true for everyone.

MJ continues to operate the Law Office of Maggie Jo Hilliard, P.A. remotely, providing guidance & referrals to clients. She handles criminal & civil matters, ranging from simple misdemeanors to murder trials, appeals, personal injury cases, family law matters (divorce, custody disputes, child support, adoption) as well as transactional work, involving estate planning, business development & real estate.

In addition to her successful legal career, MJ is an award-winning graphic design artist & was the star of a reality series pilot called "Lipstick Justice".

Founder, Annual Holiday Charity Social

Merry Charity Holiday Social

December 2007 – December 2011 (4 years 1 month) Jacksonville Beach, FL

In 2007, Maggie Jo Hilliard, Esq. began Merry Charity, a free-admission/no-ticket event where guests brought gifts for the Guardian Ad Litem Program of the 4th Judicial Circuit for chances to win prizes for their generosity. At the first event, about 50 people brought gifts for 8 needy children who were destined to spend the holidays away from their parents. Since 2007, the event has grown to benefit 8 charities, and now involves more than 500 community attendees who battle for great prizes by competing to bring the most donations.

Each year, the event proves to be increasingly fun, exciting and beneficial for participating charities.

For more information, log onto www.merrycharity.com
