Todd Cushing's Summary

EBIT Associates is a leading investment banking and high end business brokerage, intermediary firm advising private and corporate clients in lower middle market transactions. EBIT provides sophisticated solutions in merger and acquisitions, private placement, financial restructuring, and valuation opinions. EBIT Associates is a registered Illinois broker with several certifications in the M&A space. The Principal of EBIT is an experienced professional who was part of a family owned company for 22 years with an EBITDA over $1.1 Million before the company was sold over a decade ago.
