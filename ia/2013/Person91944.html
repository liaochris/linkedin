Michael Adams' Summary

I enjoy building and marketing products, creative brainstorms, finding solutions to complex problems, and supporting decisions through data mining and analytics.

I currently work for Bluehouse Group leading online marketing, research & analytics, and product marketing. I take products and ideas in their infancy and bring them to life. And do everything with a smile on my face.

But the workday doesn't end at 5pm. I've been an entrepreneur since I was 15 years old.

My current project is Gredio, a web-based application for small food companies. I co-founded the company and head up sales and marketing as well as product management. Building a web app is an exciting experience and I look forward to growing Gredio into a budding Vermont-based company.

I also own Green Mountain Mustard, a teeny-tiny mustard company I run on the weekends at farmer's markets and festivals throughout the northeast. Running a B2C company has taught me to stay ahead of the curve, execute quickly and creatively, and believe that your customers walk on water. They are the lifeblood of your product.

Through these experiences I have realized the value of communication, organization, and teamwork in business to complete tasks efficiently and successfully.

Specialties: Product Marketing, Research & Analysis, Integrated Marketing Communications, Social Media, Search Engine Optimization, Sales & Customer Service, Personal Selling, and Youth Entrepreneurship.

Software: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Quark 7, Power Point, Word, Excel
