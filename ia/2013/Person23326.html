Chris Grosse's Summary

Having served in roles that include Associate Creative Director/Digital for ROMNEY FOR PRESIDENT, Design Director at NBC TELEVISION NETWORK + Interactive Designer for the launch of MONSTER.COM, Chris brings over two decades' of creativity, curiosity + collaboration to the ideation and implementation of engaging branding experiences, interactive products + visual communications.

A hands-on user of digital + social media, credentials include a graduate certificate in Organizational Behavior from Harvard + a BFA in Visual Communications from Syracuse.

Currently resides in the greater Boston area. Has also called NYC, Denver, Minneapolis + the Jersey Shore her home.
