Geoffrey Geist's Summary

What can I do for YOU?

It seems to me that too often business is all about "what's in it for me?". Day in and day out all you seem to hear about is greedy organizations taking more and giving less. That isn't how I want people to think of me, or my business, so let's start here...

What can I do for you? I mean really... this isn't meant to be some kind of pitch so you'll buy something. What do you need, or is there anyway I can help you?

Although my wife and I have only been in KC for a few years we've still made some connections to really quality people so if there's something you are looking for maybe we can help connect you.

Do you want to save a ton of money on your mortgage or home purchase? I've gotten to be an expert at custom tailoring unique loans for people that will offer extraordinary of benefits! Plus my skills in sales and marketing are pretty dang good too. Then my experiences in all kinds of business, graphic design and writing, plus a number of other things, may be of some use to you.

My wife also has her MBA from Rockhurst and has some amazing business and mortgage skills too so just ask and we'll try and find a way to help you. If we can't, then we'll say so, but we're both very creative!

Lets connect and help people out.

Specialties: Mortgage loan origination, Sales and marketing, graphic design, creative writing, prospecting...
