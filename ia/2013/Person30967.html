Andrew Guy, MSc, CK's Summary

Request Andrew for your next onsite workshop or Prof. Development

Andrew Guy is a health and wellness professional who provides onsite training and workshops to various companies, organizations and educational institutions (higher ED. & K-12) for both students, staff and faculty alike.

As a certified science educator, Andrew has a unique approach to delivering quality lessons to excite his class. As a result, many schools request him for teacher professional development (PD's.) His onsite training for educators are a blast, Energetic and explosive! Tell your school district about the program. Andrew provides key tips on ways to provide explicit science instruction to urban students, and how to deliver exciting lessons that keeps students fully engaged.

The best part about Andrew is his ability as a speaker and his a one-of-a-kind sense of humor. His ability to captivate audiences, while leaving a touch of inspiration in their hearts has made him a favorite among colleges, universities, high schools, leadership groups, and team-focused organizations.

Most people book Andrew as their speaker for many reasons: he gets the job done, easy to work with and he considers the needs of his clientele. Andrew is personable, witty and skilled at what he does.

Andrew Guy is a certified health and wellness specialist, science educator and Martial Arts Instructor.

He holds a Bachelor's (Biology) and exercise science, and a Master's of Science (Kinesiology) degrees from Southern Arkansas University.
