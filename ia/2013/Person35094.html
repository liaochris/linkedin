Cynthia Marivel Hernandez's Summary

Cynthia holds an MBA in Business and Project Management certification. She is highly experienced in Strategic Marketing Planning, responsible for executing campaigns for market and revenue growth efforts for start-ups, small biz, and Fortune 500 companies through various national and International Marketing & Advertising agencies.

She is currently involved in the startup community mentoring and advising in business, marketing and social media. The startups under her direction are in smart shape changing technologies, influence marketing platforms, healthcare utilities and a National Women mentorship program.

Cynthia is the co-founder of Vital Intake, a non-profit organization, sponsored by the Pillsbury Law firm, that caters to the San Diego startup community (http://www.vitalintake.org) and is an organizer of the San Diego Entrepreneur Day event which is taking place on Sept. 27 & 28, 2013 in downtown San Diego (More at: http://sdentrepreneurday.com).
