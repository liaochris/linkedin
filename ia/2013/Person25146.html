C.J. Hayden's Summary

Author of Get Clients Now!, Get Hired Now!, The One-Person Marketing Plan Workbook, 50 Ways Coaches Can Change the World, 10 CDs, and over 400 published articles. Business coach and thinking partner helping entrepreneurs get clients, get unstuck, and get on purpose. Speaker on topics of marketing, entrepreneurship, life purpose, and getting unstuck. Founder of the Send Girls to School Project, board member for A Good Deed Tea. Master Certified Coach and Certified Professional Co-Active Coach. Member of the Arbinger Coaches Network. Over 30 years experience in business management. Business coach since 1992.

Specialties

Marketing, sales, business planning and strategy, venture and product development, entrepreneurship, getting unstuck, motivation, life purpose, right livelihood, social entrepreneurship.
