Deb Zechini, COC's Summary

At Order in the House we provide professional organizing services to our clients to assist them in clearing the clutter from their homes and designing systems to bring order back in the home. This is accomplished through establishing skills, strategies and habits that will enable them to stay organized once the systems are set up.

Deborah has been organizing since 2003 and keeps current in her field by attending the annual organizing conference held by NAPO (National Association of Professional Organizers). She also is a member of ICD (Institute for Challenging Disorganization) and furthers her education in the industry through their teleclasses.
Website: www.orderinthehouse.com

Specialties

Working with adults who have ADD/ADHD who want to become more organized.
