M.C.A. People Solutions

June 2008 – Present (5 years 5 months)

M.C.A. People Solutions s.a.r.l., an international Recruitment,training and consulting firm. We provide our clients with business and professional solutions, consultancy services, recruitment services with highly qualified Lebanese Manpower, people development and training programs designed for their business by a network of professionals.


M.C.A. People Solutions' training program offers leadership style seminars, team building workshops, effective communications, sales success, and organizational development.

The program design is a scientifically crafted and proven method for empowering individuals, teams, and organizations to create change and personal development leading to improved business results.

“We believe we will only be successful if our clients become successful”.
