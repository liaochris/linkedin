Gregg Hand's Summary

After 22 years of prestigious large law firm practice, in late 2009, I decided to combine my many years of legal practice and experience with a client-friendly approach to legal counseling, litigation, client service and fees, and established Hand Law Offices. I concentrate my practice on antitrust litigation and counseling, general commercial litigation and business litigation and counseling in the Washington, DC area.
