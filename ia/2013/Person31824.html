David Frees III's Summary

In addition to chairing the Trusts Estate and Wealth Preservation section of the firm (Unruh, Turner, Burke & Frees) I speak frequently on the topics of Persuasion, Ethical Influence, Mediation and Alternative Dispute Resolution, Marketing, Leadership and Sales as well as legal topics for lawyers and consumers. These include Sophisticated Estate Planning for Professionals and C Level Executives, Using Trust Protectors, Asset Protection, Succession Planning, and Protecting Family Vacation Homes and Real Estate.

Twice a year I conduct a high level mastermind for executives and entrepreneurs. They are usually located in Tampa, Phoenix and/or Las Vegas.

Past participants have included professionals, C Level executives, and small business owners seeking to grow, sell or acquire businesses.

Specialties

Speaking: I specialize in influence, persuasion, sales and marketing, and enhanced communications.
Legal : I focus my practice on trusts & estates

LAW: While Pennsylvania lawyers may not ethically say that they specialize, I do focus my practice in the areas of Estate and Trust Planning, Asset Protection and Inter-generational Family Planning to preserve family wealth and values.

I also serve as a mediator of estate, trust, and family business disputes for families trying to avoid litigation.
