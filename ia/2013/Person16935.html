Brent Lewis, BA, REALTOR®, I.R.E.S's Summary

My clients are my number one priority as I strive to provide completely comfortable and transparent transactions while negotiating the best price possible.

I am passionate about all sectors of Real Estate. Homes, condos, renovations, interior finishing, floorplans, neighborhoods, character homes, contemporary homes, penthouses, lofts, and much more! I am a specialist in the Vancouver Real Estate Market and focus on Real Estate Investment, FIrst TIme Home Buyers, and marketing outstanding properties in Downtown and Vancouver Westside.

Background:
Brent Lewis earned his Bachelor’s Degree in Urban Geography from the University of British Columbia, with a concentration in Urban Planning and Geographic Information Systems. To compliment his degree, he furthered his education by obtaining the his Real Estate Trading Services License from the University of British Columbia Sauder School of Business.


Brent developed a passion for Vancouver real estate almost immediately after he moved here in 2004. The city’s vibrant landscape, culturally diverse neighborhoods, and evidently active lifestyles convinced him that there is no better place to reside.

Having lived, studied, and worked professionally in Vancouver these past 8 years has qualified Brent to be an expert when it comes to recognizing and understanding what stimulates Vancouver buyers and sellers of every demographic. With Brent’s mix of blogging, social media presence, and honed marketing skills, it’s safe to say your home will be well-positioned in today’s market. Whether you’re looking to buy, sell, or invest, Brent’s focus is to maximize the value of your existing property or to negotiate down your future dream home.
