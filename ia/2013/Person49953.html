Jennifer Greenberg's Summary

After 14 years in brand management, creative direction, graphic design and print production, I find myself still gaining tremendous enthusiasm and momentum in the brand and design fields. I've come to enjoy inventive problem-solving and strategic methods--not just design for the sake of design. Although that's fun, too! I appreciate being able to work with a variety of clients and industries, gaining depth and diversity over my creative career.

Jennifer Greenberg's Experience

Art Director

Letting It Run Workshop

May 2007 – Present (6 years 6 months) Valparaiso, Indiana

Having my own studio was never my plan, but it has certainly turned into my passion! Here I'm able to specialize in launching and updating small- to mid-sized brands through logo design, brand management, advertising and strategic communications.

Designer (Senior Associate)

Grant Thornton LLP

Partnership; 5001-10,000 employees; Accounting industry

August 2006 – Present (7 years 3 months) Chicago, Illinois

Starting as a regional brand champion out of the Grant Thornton Dallas office, I collaborated with the national brand group and local marketing teams in seven offices for marketing campaigns and sales collateral support. In 2008, I aided the national team in launching a new international brand identity to the firm. Now part of the national design team in Chicago, I develop, design and execute both print and electronic projects including thought leadership, invitations and direct mail campaigns.

Senior Designer

David Sutherland, Inc.

Design industry

February 2002 – August 2006 (4 years 7 months) Dallas, Texas

A founding member of their internal marketing department, I assisted in developing and managing several high-end furnishing brand personalities (David Sutherland, Inc., David Sutherland Showrooms, Perennials Outdoor Fabrics, John Hutton Textiles, and Sutherland Furniture) by designing the visual aspects of strategic marketing plans. I executed initiatives from small internal pieces to international advertising and direct mail campaigns. In my time at David Sutherland, I expanded my skill-set to include event planning, product development and photography direction.

Senior Designer

brand thirty-three

May 1998 – November 2001 (3 years 7 months)

In my first design post out of college, I started with ¡Que Milagro! Design, eventually merging with brand thirty-three. There I produced award-winning materials from concept to press check in an agency setting.
