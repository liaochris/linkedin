Carrie Hawks' Summary

Whether delving into sculpture, doll making, or quirky animations, I've always loved storytelling. After working a few years, I went back to school at Georgia State University, majoring in Graphic Design. I enjoy using my design and story telling skills for various clients. Getting inside your brand to create the most engaging experience, be it a promo video, title sequence, or web graphics.

I adore working in digital and video media. In my free time, I love exploring dance.

Specialties: Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects, Final Cut Pro, InDesign, Flash
Basic knowledge of Cinema 4D and Viz Artist
