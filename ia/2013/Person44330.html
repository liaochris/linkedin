Federico Filacchione's Summary

I've spent most of my 10 years of experience in the IT industry spreading security awareness and trying to understand the real needs and requisites of my company and make them more secure and efficient.

Now I work for my company's CSO, as part of the Security Governance & Privacy team, to define the measures, guidelines and overall strategies to protect our informations, defend our data and deal with all Privacy Law issues, 231 law prescriptions and ISO/IEC 27001/27002 best practices.

I've a long background and experience in projecting and deploying, with a strong analytical attitude and deep knowledge of ICT security industry. I've successfully realized solutions based on law compliance (e.g. the Italian Data Protection Authority law on System Administrators), wrote procedures and standards that helped my company to raise the global security level (e.g. incident handling procedures and security event analysis).

My professional career started administrating UNIX-based systems, eventually I've developed an interest in IT security and firewalling/intrusion protection technologies, including testing the security of systems (penetration tests) and web applications (OWASP procedures and controls).

I've published several security-related articles on Hakin9 and ClubHack Magazine, and I write on two blogs (both in Italian), Glamis on Security, where I discuss the latest security topics and write tutorials and analysis, and Marketing Consumer, where I discuss about how marketing is perceived by a consumer like me. To read my posts you can go to http://glamisonsecurity.com/ and http://marketingconsumer.com/

You can contact me writing to federico@filacchione.it or @glamis on Twitter.

Specialties: Security awareness, security evangelist & writer, requisite analysis, technical scouting, market research, IT security standards and procedures, projecting and developing security solutions, application and system testing.
