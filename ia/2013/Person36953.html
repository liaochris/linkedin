Doreen Bloch's Summary

Doreen Bloch is the Founder & CEO of Poshly.com, a personalization tech company servicing the beauty & personal care industries which was honored in 2012 with InStyle Magazine's "Best of the Web" and L'Oreal's NEXT Generation Women in Digital Award. Doreen is also author of "The Coolest Startups in America" (February 2012) and a member of the Young Entrepreneur Council. Her work is regularly featured by The Next Web, The New York Times, Inc., The Wall Street Journal, Mashable, Huffington Post, Forbes, Women 2.0, Feministing, Fortune & more.
