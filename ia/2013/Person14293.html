Bill Gerba's Summary

I help companies become more efficient and profitable by using remotely-controlled, networked computing.

Digital signage systems: Whether used for employee communications and safety training in back-room networks, or placed in retail stores and mall concourses to advertise products, I've worked on well over 100 networks in the past 12 years.

Interactive kiosks: When used properly, kiosks can offer a high ROI and enable transactions that would never otherwise happen. I've worked on everything from sweepstakes applications to instant credit issuance, and know how hard is to make a kiosk application that will offer real value to customers.

M2M: By adding remote management to traditionally dumb devices like parking meters, industrial control systems and even water coolers, we help product manufacturers gain insight and agility by leveraging the "Internet of Things".

Specialties

digital signage, shopper marketing, digital out-of-home advertising (OOH), DOOH, narrowcasting, kiosks, self-service, M2M, corporate blogging, participant observation
