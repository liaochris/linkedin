James Dillehay's Summary

Publisher, author, former magazine editor and publisher, artisan, gallery owner, entrepreneur, direct sales distributor and trainer, public speaker, workshop presenter, business developer, Certified Guerrilla Marketing Coach. Interviewed in The Wall Street Journal Online, The Chicago Tribune, Bottom Line Personal, Family Circle, The Crafts Report, Better Homes & Gardens, Working Mothers, Country Almanac, HGTV, Entrepreneur Radio and many more.

Specialties: author book and ebook publishing services (file preparation for printing, ebook formatting and distribution), creative products marketing, public relations. Co-author of three books in the bestselling Guerrilla Marketing series: Guerilla Multilevel Marketing, Guerilla Marketing on the Front Lines and The Best of Guerrilla Marketing, The Remix.
