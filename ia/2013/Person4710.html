Alistair Williams' Summary

I founded and run Coffee Marketing Consultancy to provide creative social marketing strategies and community engagement services, effectively measured to deliver a transparent and positive ROI in the shortest time possible. This is built on the principle that compelling content combined with consumer engagement is where social media is most powerful.

Building websites and social applications myself gives me a high technical knowledge with the creative and strategic thinking needed to create first class campaigns. To date I have worked for many businesses and agencies to support their journey into delivering innovative social marketing that are integrated with existing business processes and activities.

My love for digital marketing began by falling 0.5% shy of a first class degree in advertising and marketing. A solid 2:1 with supporting first class dissertation focused on online communities and e-commerce led me to work for Habbo for 5.5 years. During this period I completed a Masters in Digital Media & Creative strategy (Social Media Marketing), achieving the highest distinction out of all 27 candidates, where only 4 managed to finish.

This role initially involved creating paid for, cutting edge, social media engagement campaigns for large brands like P&G, Sony, NSPCC, and Department of Health. In later years I developed strategic marketing partnerships with COI, SonyBMG and Nat Mags, while executing the marketing plan with our suppliers Mindshare and Edelman.

Onsite marketing activity used advanced Google Analytic benchmarking and payment partner optimisation, reducing costs. I became Adwords trained to run a significant portion of a 6 figure marketing budget via innovative Search Engine Marketing- In close partnership with Google we beta tested online video and 'gadget advertising' formats.

Eventually I oversaw all operations for this multi-million pound company and reported to the global head of marketing, including the business' P&L.
