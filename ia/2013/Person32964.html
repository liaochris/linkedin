Dayle Guy's Summary

I'm an entrepreneurial marketer with a passion for strategic creative marketing and working with inspirational people to build innovative and successful businesses. I've already demonstrated drive, innovation and adaptability; having worked for some exciting businesses, founded several businesses and worked on different continents in challenging environments.

Recently I'm proud to become part of a talented marketing team at the award winning EE, T-Mobile and Orange mobile telecoms network. Recognised for being the UK’s most advanced digital communications company that over 27 million customers trust with their digital lives.

Perviously I worked with the global ActionCOACH team where my clients are selected more on attitude than size, and they understand that people are important and systems should run a company. ActionCOACH offer the most practical, most applicable and fastest growth strategies.

I'm also the founder and CEO of C3N, a supply chain technology and solutions company with marketing support from some of the world's top supply chain systems manufacturers.

Prior to creating C3N, I was founder of The DENSO-STORE with offices in the UK and US, servicing over 150 blue chip customers throughout the world and was acquired by AutoID-Systems Inc in 2010.

I grew up in the South West of England before moving to the Home Counties after studying electronic engineering and business. My time spent as a Product and Solutions Manager for the Paxar Corporation, now Avery Dennison covering the EU, Middle East and African regions, gave me an exciting and hands on insight into commerce around the world and inspired me to switch, becoming an entrepreneur and now helping good businesses to become great.

I work hard although I travel less these days, so in my free time, I enjoy a fairly active lifestyle including competitive cycling, mountain biking and road cycling, triathlons and swimming.

Specialties

Start-up, internet, marketing strategy, retail supply chain, business development, business systems and process analysis, corporate engagement, Import export. I have a passion for helping businesses and individuals become the best they can be, using the best people and the best systems.
