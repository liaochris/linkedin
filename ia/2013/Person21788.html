Chad Musgrove's Summary

I am a freelance web developer that focuses on helping the small business owner. I am skilled in HTML, CSS, Jquery, and specialize in helping improve websites for small business owners that are on a budget.

There are many free tools out there, that can greatly increase the effectiveness of a website. I focus on helping business owners that do not have the technical savvy to install the code themselves.

Some of the free tools I like to install:

Comm100's Live Chat - allowing clients to chat with their website visitors in real time.

Comm100's email newsletter - a great, and totally free, newsletter service. Your website visitors can sign up to be on your mailing list, and you can send them newsletters containing important information.

JQuery Mega Menus - a great way to organize a very cluttered website

Google Analytics - A must have for anyone who owns a website.

I charge small, flat rate, one time charges to implement these free tools into a website.

I also offer Domain names, Hosting, and Phone Call tracking.

My primary product is a summary of your Google Analytics data emailed to clients on a monthly basis. To many business owners do not look at their data. My summary reports are meant to keep your website performance top of mind.

Specialties

HTML, CSS, Jquery, Comm100, Phone Call tracking, Google Analtyics, Google Adwords, Domain names, Hosting
