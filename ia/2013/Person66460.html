John Hilley's Summary

My company, Patmos, LLC, provides services for nonprofit and corporate clients focusing on resiliency. Patmos' diverse and unique set of experiences and skills -- strategic planning, aligning processes and systems, leadership development, counseling / change management with heart, risk assessment, software design, and connecting people to resources -- provide the fundamentals which organizations need to survive and grow, while adapting to today’s challenging and complex realities.

This focus on resiliency led to the design and development (2009-2011) of an evidenced based assessment platform called the "ORG" (Organizational Resiliency Gameplan Assessment) which gives organizations an easy-to-use and cost effective assessment of their overall health and sustainability across all of their core activities, provides in-depth recommendations, benchmarking against their sector, and specialized resources.

Foundations use multi-organization subscriptions of the ORG software-as-a-service (SaaS) tool as a cost effective and scalable option to assess and strengthen their grantee organizations. This maximizes the impact of their gifts and provides them with aggregate data results.

Nonprofits use our SaaS tool and consulting services to identify where they excel and where they need improvement, and implement concrete action steps. This allows them to maximize their impact, increase their mission effectiveness, and strengthen their capacity. The ORG also provides them with the documentation needed to appeal to foundation funders. For more info: MyGamePlan.org

Specialties

- Helping nonprofits develop and implement a Resiliency and Sustainability Gameplan
- Nonprofit best practices and optimal use of technology
- Business continuity planning for small and mid-size businesses and institutions, including disaster response and recovery
- Organizational assessments, capacity building and leadership development
- Clients include banks, healthcare companies, data security technology firms, educational institutions, foundations, churches, and various nonprofits
