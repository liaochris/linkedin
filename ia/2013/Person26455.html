Collin Starkweather's Summary

Currently on sabbatical teaching business and economics at Shanghai University / SILC (上海大学悉尼工商学院).

Former economist at two leading Chicago-based economics consultancies with specialties in M&A, including antitrust and related regulatory issues, financial analysis, including allegations of securities fraud and 10b-5 litigation, and intellectual property, including patent disputes. Extensive case management experience. Strong numerical modeling background, including experience with both econometrics and numerical optimization. Intermediate Mandarin speaking and reading.

In addition to work in economics and numerical modeling, my work has included extensive software engineering and architecture, database management (DBA), and systems administration experience with large-scale Unix and Linux deployments as well as management of IT teams and software product development.

Notable Achievements:
- Former top-performing Director at Chicago Partners / Navigant Economics
- Former top-performing Economist at Lexecon (now CompassLexecon)
- Quickly promoted at The Active Network to lead the engineering team through incredibly rapid growth
- Awarded the highest honor conferred on foreign experts by Yunnan Province

Years of Experience:
- 8 years experience managing teams of research analysts and software engineers
- 14 years experience in numerical optimization, econometrics, and algorithm design
- 14 years software engineering and architecture experience
- 3+ years teaching experience at the university level

Recent presentations and seminars:
- Applications of Econometrics to Liability and Damages Estimation in Securities Litigation, Shanghai Jiaotong University (上海交通大学) Economics Department, November 28, 2012.

Specialties:
