Matt Prohaska's Summary

My experience comes from 19 years in online media and 6 years in broadcast television. My goal continues to be contributing the most to revenue and profitability for companies that grow digital media and improve society. I have personally sold and/or managed teams that have delivered over $2.5B in digital ad revenue over the years.

Specialties: Building/managing teams; closing revenue; business operations and corporate integration; programmatic and real-time bidding selling and buying; audience data monetization and strategy; digital video monetization and marketing; client services; media selling and buying; account management; online lead generation and direct response; business development; ad operations; business plan consulting; fundraising; networking; advertising, marketing, and branding; social entrepreneurship
