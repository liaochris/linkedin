Jinny Liu's Summary

Jinny Liu is the principal behind JLStudio, and has always been passionate about art and design. With a background in advertising and media, Jinny injects passion and creativity into every project. She’s worked with clients of all scales and sizes – from government sectors and corporations to local businesses in your neighborhood.

An alumni of NYU and Parsons New School with degrees in communications, studio art, and graphic design, Jinny believes there is more to life than climbing the corporate ladder. This inspires and drives her to look for that next creative challenge… hopefully yours!

JLStudio offers a diverse list of design capabilities, highlighted by the flexibility and personal attention of a freelance boutique.

Working with our network of professionals that include print experts, web developers, and media buyers, we bring your creative visions to life.
