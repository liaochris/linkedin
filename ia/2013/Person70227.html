Juan Celaya's Publications

  • 8 Reasons Why You Should Consider Automatic Classification and Automatic Metadata Tagging in SharePoint

    • AIIM
    • November 29, 2009
    Authors: Juan Celaya

    It should not be of great surprise to anyone that Microsoft's plan for SharePoint is to provide the foundation for access to all information in the enterprise. With SharePoint's tightly coupled integration with the Office product, SharePoint's popularity in collaboration and openness for developers and integrators, SharePoint is positioned to touch every single byte in your corporate network.

    Therefore based on what SharePoint's future looks to be, the importance of being able to properly manage your data within SharePoint is very important. So, are you having problems with correct tagging of documents, record management's retention schedules or document access rights? If you are, then you seriously need to stop relying on your users to tag your content and look at automatically tagging them.
