Amber Harding's Summary

I'm just a girl who loves sports - playing them, talking about them, watching them and writing about them.

I grew up in Indianapolis before moving to Knoxville to become a Tennessee Vol. Now, I'm fighting the heat in Phoenix and recently graduated from ASU's Walter Cronkite School with a Master of Mass Communication. I specialize in broadcast media and media relations.

Specialties: Trained in sports and news writing, reporting, shooting and announcing. Proficient with Panasonic, Sony and Canon Camcorders and Final Cut Pro and Avid editing systems. Comfortable with backpack journalism. Experienced in sports media relations. Working knowledge of Microsoft Windows, Mac OSX, Microsoft Office, Adobe PDF, Photoshop, Dreamweaver, InDesign, NewsEditPro, iMovie, iWeb, Garage Band and Audacity. Comfortable with HTML and CSS.
