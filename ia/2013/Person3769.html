Alexandra Bosbeer's Summary

Manager and facilitator with extensive international experience. A dozen years of experience in communication and education in policy and multifunctional forest management, as well as in designing and implementing professional training. Eight years as a consultant specializing in social and environmental forest management. Two years' experience in program management and in performing rapid assessments of forest management in all biomes. Professional photographer and amateur potter.

Specialties: Team management; facilitation and coordination of groups; consultation; report-writing; social survey design; teaching and making presentations; and creative planning and problem solving. Forest ecology and soil; forest management for restoration and recreation; auditing for FSC and ISO 9001.
Advocacy at the European level.
GIS and remote sensing; web design (Dreamweaver); blogging; Wordpress; photography; pottery.
