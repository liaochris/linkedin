Brenda Brouillette RN, BS's Summary

For founder and president Brenda Brouillette, Savvy is an opportunity to provide physician practices and other healthcare entities strategic marketing initiatives and services as healthcare dynamics continue to change. Combining her nursing clinical background and leadership roles of 17 years with the 16 years of business development and marketing experience in a variety of healthcare venues, she is able to bring an astute understanding and strong expertise in the niche of healthcare marketing.

Brenda received a Bachelors in Healthcare Leadership from National Louis University, Chicago, IL. After working in an acute healthcare system for physician relations, she had the opportunity to join Southeastern Spine Center & Research Institute as VP of Business Development & Community Relations. With the de- sire to expand this practice, she co-founded and served as COO of Southeastern Educational &Technical Institute for two years as well as assisting with the establishment of the National Spine Foundation. In addition, she has volunteered and participated in several non-for-profit organizations and numerous community events, has been a member of the Chamber of Commerce and served as co-chair for the Economic Development cluster in Sarasota for the Healthcare Simulation Initiative.

Savvy Marketing Solution was established in 2007 to provide consulting services for healthcare entities, especially physician practices, offering a variety of services. Originating in Sarasota Florida, this firm expanded into the Maryland and Washington, DC, area in 2009.
