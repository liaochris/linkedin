Christa Jaillet's Summary

Office operations of two family run service and installation companies, Custom Beverage Services & New England Bar Solutions.

As a Realtor, Accredited Buyer's Representative & Accredited Staging Professional, I represent both buyers & sellers. Whether a first time home buyer or a seasoned investor, I enjoy supporting, educating & guiding through the entire process. As a selling agent, I bring the best marketing tool available in the market right now ... home staging! As the owner of At Home Staging & Re-Design, I assist my clients as well as other agents to help make their listings outshine the competition, bringing in higher quality offers in less time than non-staged homes.
