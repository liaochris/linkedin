Diane Konrath's Summary

Professional organizing and decorating are my passion. Helping others to improve their home or business with organization is so powerful. Being organized in a way that fits you can change your life. I work with residential clients in their home to organize all areas they struggle with. Paperwork, room layout, functionality, closets you name it. I also help business owners organize their office, workflow and time management. My goal is for every client to feel relief and happiness when we are through with their organizing projects. I love making a difference and motivating others to get organized.

Specialties

Home organizing, home and office decorating, space planning, personal organizing, time management, computer organizing, consulting, workshops, public speaking, business and office organizing, workflow, communications, defining roles and duties, project management, personal coaching and consulting.
