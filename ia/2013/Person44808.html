Fiona Marissa's Summary

I work successfully with busy executives, professionals and individuals from many different countries, including those in Europe, the Middle Eastern, Near & Central Asia, East & Southeast Asia, Latin America, and the Pacific Islands helping them bridge the cultural communication gap and effectively communicate and relate in English. I travel, work and study internationally. I am fluent in Spanish. I also speak Persian (Farsi).

My passion is aiding busy professionals, executives,and researchers/academicians, particularly non-native speakers, to quickly and effectively capture their thoughts on an English document, presentation or website. What good is an idea that's stuck inside someone's head, creating a log jam to creative and productive possibilities when you can get it out there where it can do some good!

Specialties

Cross-cultural & international communication; aiding busy international professionals, researchers, executives, students, and business owners with effective and efficient written communication for their English-speaking target audience. We also partner with translators/editors of other languages.
