Doreen Dotto's Summary

Available throughout North America and New Zealand, "uKloo" Early Reader Treasure Hunt, is a new concept early reader literacy game. uKloo builds self confidence, enhances sight reading, introduces first research skills, offers the basics of sentence structure, and fosters parent/child interaction. It's lots of fun with a rewarding surprise!

uKloo will soon be helping children learn to read English in India, Singapore, Hong Kong, The Philippines, South Africa, Lebanon, Qatar, Kuwait and Sri Lanka!

Look for uKloo Riddle Edition Treasure Hunt Game coming Soon!

Arts/Education Consultant

Freelance Consultant

1987 – 2009 (22 years)

Doreen is a freelance consultant who has developed children’s educational materials including web games & activities for young innovators, artists & early readers. Doreen’s expertise as a visual artist, arts administrator, arts educator & mother of two young boys gives her a unique vision in creating effective and stimulating experiences for both young children and adults. Past accomplishments include content and concept development for the Inventive Women/Inventive Kids web sites, teaching visual art to K-13 and special needs classes for school boards throughout the GTA, grants officer to visual artists, choreographers and literature at the Toronto Arts Council (including the facilitation and launch of new granting programs to independent artists), Project Manager for Canadian Artists Representation Ontario (CARO), Ontario College of Art, lecturer and facilitator for Arts Business seminars and founder and team leader of the Art a la Carte program offered through Norway Public School.
