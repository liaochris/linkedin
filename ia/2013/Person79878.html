Paul Legomski's Summary

A designer and strategist with twelve years of extremely diversified design experience in a wide variety of media, application, and context. Possesses acute design sensibilities, a knack for visual storytelling, a keen critical eye, and a savvy for identifying opportunities within technological and social trends. Always seeking to provide optimal, revolutionary value for both end users and businesses.

Specialties

Design and product strategy; experience and interface design; user-centric design and user research; critical and heuristic analyses; creative and strategic direction; inter-disciplinary mediation; technical specification.

Paul Legomski's Patents

  • Sketch Recognition and Enhancement

    • United States Patent 7,515,752
    • Issued April 7, 2009
    Inventors: Paul Legomski, Christopher Tremblay, Pascal Becheiraz, Rick Champagne, Valentin Ivanov, Daniel Jette, Shawn Lipstein, Vladimir Makarov

    A drawing assistance system enhances a sketch drawn by a user in an electrical document on a computer system by collecting stroke data from one or more strokes of a sketch as a user draws the sketch. The stroke data includes time-based information and features of each stroke. The strokes are handled based on the time-based information, and analysed based on the strokes handled based on the time-based information. The sketch is enhanced based on results of the analysis.
