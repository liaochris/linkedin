Kisha H. Baggs' Summary

My name is Kisha Baggs and I'm the Graphic Designer you are looking for! I’m a talented and ambitious designer with great graphic production design skills and the experience you desire. I have extensive training and background with using Adobe Creative Suite including InDesign, Illustrator and Photoshop to make new and innovative marketing pieces for you. My background focuses on layout and print production, which includes branding, advertising, ads, posters, flyers, newsletters, banners, cards, websites and catalog design. I am also, experienced in social networking, HTML coding, online CMS, CSS and SEOing.

I am known for my professionalism, unique style and creative talent. Some of my past clients include Windermere Real Estate, American Express, Microsoft, Washington CASH, Olympic College and several local small businesses including Island Green Clean, BINGO! Salsa and The Arts and Crafts Press. I have worked and maintained client relations with people from all over the world including New Zealand, Canada and Florida.

I am a hard worker and fast learner who takes pride in her work with a good understanding of current trends in online and print marketing. I work well with others and as well as independently. I can handle working on several projects at one time and am very receptive to suggestions and changes as I'm always looking to improve my design capabilities and learn from others. You will find I am an artist who loves to create and help strategize on how we can help build your portfolio. I am also a very friendly, positive person that enjoys being productive and demonstrating my skills.
