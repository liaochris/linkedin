Op. Dr. Gökhan Özerdem's Summary

We are a leading aesthetic-cosmetic surgery group whose aim is to provide the highest standards of treatment & service that the medical profession can offer.

Our brother / partner cosmetic surgeons have performed thousands of succesful operations. With more than 30 years of cosmetic surgery experience & numerous accreditations between them they are one of the most experienced surgery teams in Turkey at this time.

Professor & European & Turkish Board qualified, with Associated (Corres) membership of the American Society of Plastic Surgeons they enter each operation together performing multiple & complex surgery in a minimum of time. The benefits of surgery for each patient are numerous…shorter surgery time, less anaesthetic, less risk, quicker recovery & best results.

We aim to meet every patient’s individual needs in a very personal way, to offer the best in surgery, patient care, safety & great value for money.
