Ash Donaldson's Summary

Thanks for stopping by.

I'm a freelance consultant and contractor in Behaviour Design.

I've been practising, studying and promoting all aspects of human-centred design for over 17 years - from Human Factors and User Experience to Behavioural Economics.

During my time in this industry I've had the opportunity to work on many interesting projects, from doing the early research and design of Wotif.com, to designing the new Intelligent Deposit ATMs for National Australia Bank.

I've also acted as:
➤ Australia's first federal court expert witness in human-computer interaction;
➤ The Australian human-centred design representative to ISO; and
➤ Chair of the Australian human-computer interaction conference, OZCHI.

Recently, I have turned my attention to growing the field of Behaviour Design in Australia.

Why should we talk?
➤ I can help you understand what causes both wanted and unwanted customer behaviours.
➤ I can help you design products, services, and environments to affect customer behaviour.
➤ I can help you develop a strategic roadmap for achieving target customer behaviours.

Specialties: Behaviour design, human factors, human-computer interaction, behavioural economics, user experience, information architecture, service design, social media, interaction design, usability, activity theory, accessibility, strategy, web standards, anthropology
