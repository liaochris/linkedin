John R Parke III Chartered Financial Consultant's Summary

The New Economy has taken its toll. You can either embrace it and prosper or resist it and struggle. Social Media is here to stay.

In the new economy, personal egos must be parked at the door. “The Principles of Business have not Changed but the Model Has”. There has been a fundamental shift on how the business model has changed because of the internet: collaboration and multiple streams of income anyone?

I am looking for business executives who realize that tradition business model does not work. How can you tell? You work harder for less money each day.

Introduced to the New Economy in 2002 by Dr. Dennis Waitely, author “The Seeds of Greatness” who stated “you'll either be online or in the bread line”. It has been a learning experience and a paradigm shift in thinking ever since.

If you are looking for a person who has the experience as a business entrepreneur and the technical expertise to guide and coach you on how to apply your business experience in the new economy; we need to talk. The new economy changes almost daily.

Over the past 40 years, JOHN R. PARKE III has been a business owner, professional and entrepreneur. As a person who has actually been in the market place, he speaks from experience and not text book applications that are difficult to apply in the current economic and financial environment.

• Financial Consultant for high net worth individuals for more than 15 years involving financial organization and control, asset management, estate planning and charitable giving.
• CEO of several builder and development companies of work force housing both local and international.
• Management Consultant to Builders, Developers, Nonprofits and Housing Authorities in Florida.
• Credit restructuring and loan workout consulting for commercial and individual clients.

Specialties

He is a past President of the International Association of Financial Planners. He also holds the designation of Chartered Financial Consultant, and Chartered Life Underwriter from The American College.
