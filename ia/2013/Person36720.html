Donna Amos' Summary

My name is Donna Amos and I am passionate about Online and Offline Marketing for Small Business Owners delivering optimal results. I love assisting small businesses with driving online prospects to their offline business. My business continues to expand. Infinite Possibilities Coaching and Training has evolved to now offering an array of marketing services to solopreneurs and micro business owners. Today, I love helping clients define their internet marketing strategies and integrating them with their offline marketing strategies. Providing these services: Wordpress web design, mobile sites and apps, Blogging services, Social media management, email marketing, SMS (texting messaging), QR Codes, Video marketing, SEO services, coaching and training.

On September 29, 2008 I launched the International Association of Solopreneurs and in 2011 it evolved into the International Association of YOU where we help business owners develop their story and tell it in multiple formats to attract their ideal clients. Providing the marketing efforts to drive traffic to those stories. http://www.associationofyou.org

I first entered the coaching arena in 2001 when I began my training. Giving me the opportunity to combine my coaching skills and sales skills. Coldwell Banker allowed me to create a new position within the company as Business Coach for sales professionals and managers in 2002-2004.

I began with Coldwell Banker in 1994 and further developed my sales skills. I moved into management in 1998. I was passionate about what I did and loved that no day was ever the same. Real Estate allowed me to use my skills of coaching, marketing and business start-up to help others grow.

While raising my three sons I was the principal at D & M Marketing. We provided direct marketing services to small business owners. Creating direct mail campaigns and providing the telemarketing services.

Specialties
