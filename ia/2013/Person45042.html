Fin Livingston's Summary

Pursuing business growth has led us to acquire, develop, create, distribute, ally with, and divest a large variety of products and/or companies mainly in the consumer and industrial products arenas. As entrepreneurs, we have had many successes, draws, and some failures, but our drive has always been to act on untapped opportunities while leveraging our experiences and advisors.

While remaining entrepreneurial, we now have the opportunity to help other executives and business owners with their initiatives through BridgeStep, LLC. BridgeStep operates as an on-demand strategic partner for implementing new initiatives for incremental profit. Cross-functional expertise in product and market innovation, startup, alliance, operations, and international distribution allows me to effectively leverage a company’s existing resources when designing and implementing a custom plan for business growth, optimization, and assimilation.
