Frank Illes' Summary

My name is Frank Illes.

A creative. I see stories. I photograph fashion, beauty and style.

I am the child of Hungarian immigrants, born in Toronto in the mid-1960's and raised in an environment rich in culture, entertainment, music, art, and sports.

By the age of 17, I was working as a professional drummer, playing and recording street punk, hard rock and progressive jazz.

In my mid 30's, I had moved on to photography, fascinated with capturing the look and lifestyle of the new era.

The influences of my past lives in the street punk, hard rock and progressive jazz scenes continue to inform my photographic style with elegance, quirkiness and edge.

I love the collaborative process of story-telling, and the determination needed to overcome the challenges of creating the visual playgrounds that tell those stories.

The joy I feel in coordinating a team behind the camera and inspiring the talent in front of the camera to express themselves has no words.

I work crazy stupid hard to make good things happen, because I believe simply that first we have to make things good, then we can make things special.
