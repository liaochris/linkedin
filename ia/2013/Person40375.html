Nicole Policicchio's Summary

Prior to founding ElectroYogi Electrolysis (Los Angeles' highest-ranked electrolysis studio on Yelp.com), I spent my professional career with the singular goal of making a positive contribution to the world. This holds true today as I'm so grateful to help people with unwanted hair anywhere on their bodies have positive self-esteem, joy and peace of mind. For nearly 15 years, I worked as an award-winning television production professional who specialized in documentary, wildlife and children's programming. I also spent time as a product development leader in the early years of the online search and directory space.

ElectroYogi Electrolysis offers the definitive way to permanent hair removal, from the smallest unwanted hair to an entire face, bikini, chest, and more. No other methods before or since electrolysis have ever been deemed permanent by the AMA and FDA. If you or someone you know wants to give up the cycle of endless temporary methods of hair removal, and disappointing results from laser hair 'reduction', look me up here at ElectroYogi Electrolysis. You'll be forever grateful, and our team of skilled experts will be delighted to serve you!

ElectroYogi is grateful to offer holistic and natural esthetic care in addition to our electrolysis permanent hair removal services. Ask us about our custom facials and amazing products to help leave your skin radiant as you go through your hair removal journey... or even if you just want to experience superior skin care without harsh chemicals, fragrance, or additives. ElectroYogi delivers a hairless body, and flawless skin.
