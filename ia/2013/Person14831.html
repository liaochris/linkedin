Blaine Yamakawa's Summary

Amped Media was started 3 years ago as a bootstrapped side project. This involved utilizing a somewhat a Lean Startup strategy, where I deployed multiple websites in hopes that one "stuck" and earned a bit of side income. After many failures, I was successfully able to grow the company into a highly profitable business venture.

I have shifted my current focus with Amped Media Ltd from solely digital advertising to investing in web/mobile startups and e-commerce/m-commerce. My previous experience running Amped Media has provided me with the ability to quickly assess business opportunities and define business models required for future startups. I am looking to partner with other like-minded individuals who share the same passion to help bootstrap another successful startup.

I am also partners in an interactive digital signage company here in Vancouver. My company specializes in touch screen window displays, standalone kiosks, and custom iOS applications. Some of our clients include Sears Canada, BC Hydro, and previously the 2010 Winter Games. Our goal is to generate a positive ROI for our customers by generating leads through our displays, while providing a visually stimulating and entertaining interactive advertising experience.

I am also well versed in managing pay-per-click digital advertising campaigns (via Facebook, Google, Bing, Yahoo), performing search engine optimization (SEO), administering complex email marketing campaigns, and purchasing CPM banner advertising via media buys. I am also dabbling in UI/UX design for mobile devices.

My previous role at HSBC (in IT) included business analysis and design, product management, design documentation, writing medium to complex legacy code, system and user acceptance testing support, quality assurance and deployment support.

I am open to any new joint ventures and welcome to opportunity to network further.

Specialties

Product management, digital advertising, business opportunity assessment, pay per click (PPC) advertising, CPM media buys, domain registration, website design, Wordpress setup and configuration, outsourced talent management, SEO, company branding, email marketing, requirements gathering
