J Reese's Summary

Hello!

My name is J Reese, and yes you read that right; I am an Actor AND a Digital Media Professional. I am a collaborator, innovator, and problem solver. I use experience and imagination to produce results for clients. I use my nurtured talent to express myself artistically. And for the past 6 years, I have shrewdly balanced passions for theatre and results-driven marketing initiatives.

On the digital media side, I’ve planned over 50 display campaigns, trafficked over 80 in DART & the GDN, and implemented over 20 SEM initiatives in Google Adwords. I've also utilized tools such as Google Analytics, Omniture, and Floodlight to measure and report on the significant ROI and brand impact I have helped to drive. I hope to continue to produce measurable results for accounts to which I'm assigned, and deliver solutions to ever changing business needs.

Meanwhile, I have participated in Off-Off Broadway Productions, Regional theatre productions, and various other theatre and performance projects. I’ve nurtured my talent with Vocal & Acting Coaching, and diligent practice. I’ve also tirelessly worked to strengthen my unique voice as a performance artist. I hope to establish relationships with theatre companies & professionals who share my passion for live performance and artistic collaboration. I also long to challenge the ever present stereotypes that exist for actors of color and be a part of works that push through the limits of traditional musical theatre.

Long term business relationships and artistic partnerships are what I hope to strengthen through open networking. Please don’t hesitate to reach out with business or audition opportunities.
