Jason McAdoo's Summary

I graduated from Full Sail's Masters program in November 2012 with a Masters of Science in Game Design. I learned many game production techniques including agile and SCRUM based project management. I worked on many teams conducting stand up meetings, tracking bugs, gathering estimates for sprints, and managed production schedules.

I graduated from the University of Cincinnati in June 2011 with a Bachelors of Science in Digital Design. During my 5 years there, I took part in various "co-op" positions (paid internships) focusing in motion and 3D modeling. I have experience filming and editing footage for commercials, 3D modeling for still life and animation, and motion design in After Effects. I also have experience in branding and identity and have helped to create logo and stories for companies. I adapt quickly to my surroundings and have worked in a variety of industries with different cultures.

I am skilled with programs such as After Effects, Final Cut Pro, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign, 3DS Max, Cinema 4D, Lightwave, and Maya.

I have an Apple's developers license and my first game, Piggy Heaven, was released in January 2012. I both designed and programmed the game. I will receive my Masters degree in Game Design from Full Sail University in November 2012 and am actively seeking a full time position in the US.

Specialties

3DS Max, Cinema 4D, Maya, Lightwave, 3D modeling (can learn other programs quickly), Adobe After Effects, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Dreamweaver, Final Cut Pro, video editing, video shooting, iOS, Objective C, branding and identity, interface design, usability, Agile, SCRUM, quality control, Usability, Hansoft, Microsoft Project
