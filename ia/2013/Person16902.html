Brent Downey's Summary

Howdy! My name is Brent. I have been a Salesforce Administrator since 2010! I have a passion for Salesforce and sales and marketing operations!

I was recently named a Salesforce MPV (Spring '13 class) and I am a Salesforce Certified Administrator and Advanced Administrator. I am hoping to obtain my Developer certification later this year. In addition to the Salesforce specific certifications, I graduated Magna Cum Laude from Colorado Technical University with a B.A. in Business Administration and Management! I thought about changing my major to Geology, but that didn't last too long!

During the day I am the Salesforce Platform Manager & Administrator at Gates Corporation. My primary responsibility resides with the Americas where I manage projects and configuration for those teams, but I also work closely with our two other regional administrators in Belgium and China. Together, we make-up a single team that operates a single Salesforce instance for our global sales, marketing and customer service teams. On the side, I volunteer time to work with the non-profit Youth For Christ in two different instances of Salesforce. In addition, I do a little moonlighting for the experience and a little extra spending money! I am also the co-leader of the Salesforce Denver User Group! As if that wasn't enough, I also author a blog titled Admin Hero which is aimed at helping Salesforce Administrators become workplace heroes! You can find it online at www.adminhero.com!

Specialties: Salesforce.com Certified, Industrial, Industrial Manufacturing, Customer Support and Service, Customer Management, SaaS, Force.com
