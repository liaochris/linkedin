Emma K. Viglucci, CFT, LMFT, CIT's Summary

Ms. Viglucci has a Bachelor of Science Degree in Psychology and Human Development, from the State University of New York at Stony Brook, and a Masters Degree in Marriage and Family Therapy, from Hofstra University, is a Clinical Member and Approved Supervisor of AAMFT (American Association for Marriage and Family Therapy), a Certified Family Therapist, a Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist, and a Certified Imago Therapist.

Ms. Viglucci has been in the mental health service field for over a decade in diverse capacities and has been a member of several professional organizations. She is currently the Member-At-Large (MAL) for the NYAMFT Division Board for which she chairs the Membership and Visibility Committees. She enjoys writing and her articles can be found throughout the web, is the author of the Successful Couples ezine / blog for Couple Success (http://www.metrorelationship.com/SuccessfulCouples/), and is the creator of concepts, programs, and technologies that assist Couples in their Journey.

Ms. Viglucci's latest focus has been the MetroRelationship Outreach Campaign - Bringing Insights and Tools on Relationship Success to Couples by speaking and presenting seminars and workshops at events, and by being interviewed by the media. She can also be found networking and sharing her insights and tips for personal growth and healing, relationship success, and practice building on Facebook, Twitter and LinkedIn.


Specialties: Expertise in working with Couples and doing personal work to assist create a meaningful and satisfying relationship - get our FREE GIFT (http://bit.ly/hMiV0d) exemplifying approach.

We provide services to high functioning clients from a positive and strength focused approach. Metropolitan Marriage and Family Therapy caters to individuals wishing to enhance their life and relationships without the stigma associated with "going for therapy."

We operate from the belief that people have the ability to change and that they can achieve their fullest potential given the right conditions. We further believe that people can create the right condition for themselves and their relationships!
