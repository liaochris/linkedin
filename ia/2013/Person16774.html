Brendan Jackson's Summary

I am a Digital Marketing, Multi-platform Advertising, and Strategic Marketing Executive with extensive experience in B2B, B2C, Healthcare, and Technology Marketing, working for Advertising Agencies and within Fortune 500 Companies. I have been developing successful Strategic Marketing plans, including developing online content, managing SEM/SEO programs, developing email marketing campaigns, e-commerce management, Social Media programs, and implementing data driven marketing campaigns for 13+ years.

Currently I am working as a Digital Strategy and Marketing Consultant, helping companies create strategies ranging from the development of overall Digital Media Strategies, Online Lead Generation, Website/Content Strategy, and the development/implementation of Marketing Technologies.

Previously, I led the Advertising and Digital Strategy efforts for Allscripts, a $1.4 billion Healthcare Technology company. My team managed all aspects of our Digital Marketing efforts from day-to-day production to long-term strategy. We focused on building mindshare, market share, and generating revenue.

Prior to this, I built and managed the Digital Team for a top 100 Advertising Agency, responsible for creating Digital Strategies and Creative for clients as well as developing Online Media plans, with an eye toward creating lasting user engagement and brand awareness. Our ultimate goal was to drive sales and increase top line revenue. I also educated the agency and clients on the value of online advertising.

My focus has been to increase revenue and market share using the detailed analytics tools provided by the web and create positive ROI. I have also been part of internal executive leadership teams responsible for developing long-term corporate strategies and strong corporate cultures.

I have a proven track record of building Digital teams, generating new business, increasing revenue and efficiency, working with C-Level Executives, and being an effective manager of people.
