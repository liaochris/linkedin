Antoine Didienne's Summary

My interests, education, professional experience and personal goals have allowed me to specialize myself in SEM: Search Engine Marketing.

Marshall McLuhan said: "The medium is the message" and it is the perfect illustration of how I view communication: The medium dictates the message; the contenant dictates the content. But that does not mean that you should communicate using inconsistent messages.
The integration of Marketing, PR, social media and SEO into one single strategy is, according to me, the best way to conduct a low cost yet effective communication strategy.

My goal for the short/medium term is to convert VavaVida into a leading position into its market through the creation of a very strong brand.
