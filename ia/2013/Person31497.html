David Bellman's Summary

David is the founder of Bellman Jewelers, one of New England’s largest retail and manufacturing jewelers. Bellman’s also specializes in jewelry replacement for over 40 local insurance companies. The company been voted the Region’s #1 Retail Jeweler for 16 consecutive years and received various “Best of” awards from NH Magazine, NH Bride Magazine & the Hippo Press over the past 10 years.

Additionally, David is president of DHB Ventures Group, Inc. DHB is currently working with Dean Kamen & DEKA Research to redesign his product AquaSonic Wave, a revolutionary jewelry cleaner that utilizes the dish washer to sanitize jewelry. They are hoping to reintroduce the AquaSonic by Christmas 2013.

David is active community member & serves on numerous boards including: St. Mary’s Bank, Moore Center Services, Retail Merchants Association of NH, NH Hospital for Children, Mary & John Elliot Charitable Foundation, Amoskeag Business Incubator, Manchester Community College and Frederick Smyth Institute of Music.

David has a BS in Business Finance from Southern New Hampshire University and earned a Graduate Gemologist Degree from Gemological Institute of America. David has three grown children & currently resides in Hooksett. For fun, he enjoys sports photography, learning about American history, and boating.
