Gary Robinson's Summary

PM Express, Inc. is a Global Registered Education Provider (REP) of the Project Management Institute (PMI) and provides business program and project management training and consultancy for large corporations as well as small to mid size organizations.

PM Express, Inc. is a Workforce Investment Act (WIA) Eligible Training Provider to the Northern Virginia Workforce Investment Board. http://myskillsource.org/home/wia.shtml

PM Express, Inc. is a preferred vendor of SAIC.

PM Express, Inc. provides optimized project management learning and consultancy, including exam prep training for PMI credentials such as:
• Project Management Professional (PMP) certification
• Certified Associate in Project Management (CAPM) certification
• Program Management Professional (PgMP) certification
• PMI-Risk Management Professional (PMI-RMP) certification.

Additional training offerings include:
• Fundamental PM
• Earned Value Management (EVM) Compliance
• Applied Systems Engineering
• OPM3 training/consultancy.
