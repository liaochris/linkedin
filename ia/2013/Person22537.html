Chelsey Orlikowski's Summary

Chelsey currently serves as a Marketing Coordinator at Core Creative. Additionally, she is the PR + Social Media Manager for Tappr, Inc., a Milwaukee-based startup and smartphone application. She has previously worked with a radio broadcasting company, a marketing strategy and execution agency, a global manufacturing company, a former MLB athlete and a brand realization agency to develop strategies, tactics and event-based campaigns. Chelsey holds a B.A. in Journalism from the University of Wisconsin (Oshkosh), with an emphasis in Visual Media.

Specialties: Marketing, Advertising, Public Relations, Community Relations, Customer Service, Project Management, Account Management, Market Research, Social Media Strategy, Social Media Production, Strategic Planning, Analytics, Metrics, Reporting, Problem Solving, Copywriting, News Writing, Photography, Web Design, Print Design, Adobe Flash Animation, Video Editing, Videography, Professional Speaking and Presenting
