Gene Blalock's Summary

GENE BLALOCK, founder of SERAPH FILMS and the Los Angeles-based rock band, THE FADED, is an award-winning producer, director, and editor. Gene's extensive knowledge of audio and video production has been honed over the course of editing, directing, and producing dozens of albums, movies and videos, from EP's to full-length albums and from documentaries to music videos. Gene's efforts for a wide range of clients including advertising agency Leo Burnett, Mothers Against Drunk Driving, Crane Aerospace, Line 6, and the U.S. DOD, have helped him earn a reputation as a smart, in-touch artist who expertly crafts a compelling message.
