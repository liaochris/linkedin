Dave Weinberg's Summary

Creative, Design and Marketing Services — across media. Dave Weinberg helps organizations develop and evolve purpose-driven, integrated design, workflow and communications for online and offline media.

Dave is an award winning Creative Services Professional and visual communications problem solver. He continues to wear many hats in the industry – consultant, in-house team leader, business owner and blogger. His cross-media industry expertise spans more than 20 years.

As Senior Art Director with L.L.Bean, Inc., he was instrumental in advancing the design and usability of llbean.com along with fostering alignment of visual communications across web, catalog, retail and advertising channels.

At Adobe, he broadens his creative and workflow consultation across industry verticals, helping organizations leverage the tools and best practices to advance business goals.

As founding board member and former President of the Maine chapter of AIGA, the professional association for design - Dave oversaw the first annual ABSTRACT: Future of Design in Media conference in Portland. He's also served as adjunct faculty at University of Southern Maine and Maine College of Art, where he's taught courses on Graphic Communications and Cross-Media Publishing.

Dave is the creator of cellphoneSketchpad™ - mobile journal. cyber comic. digital rorschach – an evolving creative project that includes a daily blog and fosters community engagement through social media channels. All cellphoneSketches are also available as greeting cards, prints on metal and/or soft pillows!

An accomplished Illustrator, he holds a BFA from the School of Visual Arts in NYC.

Dave continues to explore a broad range of creative forms, both visual and performance-based. Over the years, designer, writer, art/creative director, stand-up comic, improvisational actor, cartoonist, wild-blueberry/banana pancake maker.

Dave is a proud and adoring Dad who also enjoys a really good nap!

Specialties

Digital Publishing, Cross-Media Creative Services, Creative Direction, Graphic Design, Branding and Identity, User Interface and Interaction Design, Publishing Workflow Consulting, cellphoneSketching, Wild-blueberry and banana pancake making
