Christine Whiteley, ASID's Summary

Are you considering remodeling your home, but lack the quiet confidence that comes from experience? What if you found someone “skilled in the art of listening” who could translate your heart’s desires into the language of the contractor? Someone you would feel comfortable sorting out your priorities with, tunes into the level of support you want, and integrates your personal style. You don’t have to do it alone…I believe collaboration is the essential.

And once you have your vision, how do find the quality, honest, time-sensitive people to execute your dream? What if you found someone who assembled a team for you with a history of working together successfully? What if you found someone to help navigate the construction sequence with you? The overwhelming process of interviewing, second guessing your decisions, scheduling and on-site discussions would be handled. Your energy could be spent on activities you love and with the people you love, while you are co-creating a home you will love.

Are you fearful of being frustrated with the inefficiency of subcontractors standing around waiting for another to finish, while stressing about when you can have your routine back? Detangling the overlap of subcontractor scheduling decreases the chaos and increases the productivity. The purpose of effective project management is to prevent endless hours of wasted time and money.

Detailed, cost effective, time-sensitive projects that reflect your personal style and priorities are not accidental…they are the result of quiet confidence derived from experience, systemized procedures and listening.

This is Christine Whiteley ASID, your answer to an efficient and enjoyable remodeling experience.
