Doug Higby's Summary

As part of a team project, we completed the translation of the New Testament into a minority language in 2005. I am now using my skills and experience in Bible translation to train people to translate more efficiently through the use of new computer technology. I aim to multiply my efforts through equipping the next wave of translators to fulfill Wycliffe's Vision 2025: begin a translation project in every language that needs one by the year 2025.

Specialties

Keyboard development, dictionary data collection and publishing, legacy to Unicode data conversion, Drupal web design, software for linguistic analysis and assisted translation.
