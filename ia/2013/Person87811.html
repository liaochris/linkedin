Martin Tramil's Projects

  • Martin F Tramil & THE JAMM

    • January 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Martin Tramil, Steve Burchell Lead Guitar, Zach Cole Harmonica, Matt Jones Drums/Percussion

    2012 features the emergence of MARTIN F TRAMIL & THE JAMM, a musical entity built around the guitar playing, vocals and song writing of Martin F. Going with a slimed down quartet, the new music is a return to Martin F’s roots of Rock-Blues and Classic 70’s Hard Rock!

    “I’M VERY EXCITED ABOUT THE NEW/OLD SOUND, IF YOU WILL…I JUST FELT IT WAS TIME TO GET BACK TO THE BASICS: GUITARS, BASS, DRUMS AND MAYBE A LITTLE B-3.” “…AND I’M LOOKING FORWARD TO ROCKIN’ YOUR ASSES OFF AND GIVING IT EVERYTHING I’VE GOT THE NEXT TIME WE’RE IN YOUR TOWN…” MARTIN F TRAMIL.
