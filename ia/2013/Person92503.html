Michael Adams' Summary

With 22 years of Management Information Systems experience in law firms and a background in Business Process Management, I have started on the path to a second career outside of legal and technology.

I am working on a BS from Tulane in Mechanical Engineering and hope to put that to use to solve engineering and environmental issues here in New Orleans..

I have focused my Information Technology career on process improvement utilizing tools of technology to make organizations more efficient. I have been a speaker at American Lawyer Media’s LegalTech events around the United States and was a member of the International Legal Technology Association (ILTA). I served as the Tampa City Rep for ILTA for 5 years. I am graduate of Tampa College and hold certifications from Microsoft and NEC.

Specialties: Back Up Services, Staff Training, Project Management, Licensing Management, Asset Management, Continuity Planning, Workflow, Case/Document/Financial Management Systems
