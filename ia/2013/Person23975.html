Christian Collins' Summary

As an independent web designer and developer, I help clients achieve their organizational goals. My specialty is being able to both identify requirements and provide solutions that solve business problems. My clients are organizations of various sizes which operate in various industries. I provide personal service, adapt easily to change and innovate often.

I design and build websites, web applications and intranets that are effective, clean and a joy to use. I build them to be optimized (responsive) for all screen sizes including mobile devices.

I most often work with Django, Python, MySQL, JQuery, Adobe Creative Suite and Ubuntu but have also done extensive work in PHP, CakePHP and ExpressionEngine.
