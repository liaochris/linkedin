Robert Harris' Honors and Awards

  • ECE 337 AMD Design Excellence Award

    Dr. Mark C. Johnson
    • May 2012

    The AMD Design Excellence Award is awarded to one group in ECE337 (ASIC Design Laboratory) each semester at Purdue University. The group consisted of Sidharth Malik, Parul Schroff, Calvin Mwesigwa, and myself. We implemented Triple DES encryption over a USB connection. The device we designed encrypts data received on the first USB port and outputs the secure data on the second USB port. Data received on the second USB port is decrypted and output on the first USB port. Using two of the devices together allows for secure communication between two USB devices. The project was designed using VHDL.
