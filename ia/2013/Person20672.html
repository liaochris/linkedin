Carter Jensen's Summary

It takes a person with a unique set of skills—leadership, charisma and character—to create experiences for the average person and I am confident in my abilities to produce valuable enterprises for whatever situation I am faced with.

As an adolescent, I took the initiative to create two of my own corporations focused in the marketing and promotions industry that served as platforms to launch my dreams and ambitions. I have been incredibly fortunate to discover such an amazing passion so early in life, and my desire to discover and learn the teachings of advertising push me to new plateaus as further myself in the professional world.
