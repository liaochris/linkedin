Ann Zaslow-Rethaber's Summary

We specialize in Executive Recruiting, as well as sourcing candidates for the following positions and industries: Engineers, multiple positions within the Telecommunications arena, Energy, Mining, Oil & Gas, Paper & Pulp, Higher Education, VP level Human Resources positions,
as well as identifying candidates for the Non-Profit industry, Healthcare, Hospital Administrators, and Medical Device arenas.

We have a team of 15 veteran executive recruiters who utilize every high tech tool available, actively networking in a multitude of industries, both nationwide as well as on an international level.

We have helped numerous companies based within the US expand into other countries across the world, providing key people to start up or grow international offices.

We have also enjoyed working with companies outside of the US, helping them build a successful US presence.

We are very proud of the fact that we donate a significant portion of our revenues to charities such as The Caitlin Robb Pediatric Cancer Fund (www.caitlinrobb.org) as well as KIVA…( www.kiva.org) loans that change lives.

By choosing ISC for your national and international high-volume recruiting needs, you help us to help others and we are very appreciative of that.

Specialties

Our speciality is doing BLITZ recruiting for companies that have massive hiring needs. With a team of 15 recruiters, we can do massive volume and provide a solid pipeline of candidates very quickly.
