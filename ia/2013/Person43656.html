Jason Desaulniers, CFP, CLU, CIM, CHS's Summary

We health-disaster proof business' and business owners, through the use of corporate risk management. According to Brewster’s Dictionary of Phrases and Fables by Ebenezer Cobham Brewer, Excalibur was originally derived from the Latin phrase Ex calce liberatus, “liberated from stone”. Likewise, we seek to liberate professionals’ and business owners’ hard-earned wealth from the stone-like tax regime that encases it, through the use of efficient and effective insurance strategies, while also reducing risk.

Specialties

Our specialties include reviewing and proposing comprehensive and tax-effective risk management strategies for owners of small- to mid-size businesses. This often includes advanced life insurance strategies, split-dollar or shared-ownership critical illness insurance strategies, etc. We also assist business' owners' with key-person needs, as well as buy-sell / shareholder agreement funding strategies.
