Jason Seliskar's Summary

I am a teacher that has a passion for the integration of technology in the classroom. Sharing my experience and education in this area with other teachers to help enhance learning in the classroom.

I serve as a district technology coach and lead the site tech team on my campus to help teachers successfully integrate technology into the curriculum.

My recent presentations include: Revolutionize Your Classroom with QR Codes (ISTE 2012), Going Wild with Weebly (CUE 2012), Going Green with iMovie (CUE 2011), QR Codes in the Classroom in Nashville, TN via Skype, and at the 21st Century Learners Symposium. See my other presentations by visiting: http://www.jasonseliskar.com/presentations/

Over the years my students have won several LACOE Digital Voice Awards in Los Angeles, including the Best Overall Elementary projects for 2010 and 2011. Also, the Best Student Website for the 2012 California Student Media Festival.

I also consult with companies on best ways to implement emerging technologies into their daily workflow.

Specialties

community leader, blogging, video in the classroom, web design, podcasting, presentation tools, photography, social networking (Twitter, Facebook, etc.) and anything new that comes along in the world of technology
