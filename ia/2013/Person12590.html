Becky Olsen's Summary

Becky is a Graphic and Web Site Designer with responsibility for creating compelling graphics that communicate the stories and visions of her client's businesses. Prior to launching Design Chef Studios in 2006, she worked in graphics for several companies, including Xerox, Kodak, Kraus Creative and the Catholic Diocese of Rochester. Notable is her 7 years as Creative Director at Design Chef Studios where she has developed more than 60 web sites as well as 20 brand identities for businesses and not for profits in Rochester, NY ranging from non-profits to fast food restaurants. She has also worked on several marketing pieces for DuPont, the United Nations and even worked on some branding for Oprah's final season.

As of May 2013 Becky has accepted a position as Web Marketing Coordinator at the DiMarco Group in Rochester, NY.
