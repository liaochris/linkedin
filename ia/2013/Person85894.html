Marieke de Kort's Experience

Corporate Manager

EABIS

Nonprofit; 1-10 employees; Nonprofit Organization Management industry

July 2013 – Present (3 months) Brussels Area, Belgium

ABIS is a global network of companies and academic institutions which partners and empowers both to invest in a more sustainable future for business in society.

ABIS promotes knowledge-based transformation, supporting members with change levers to bridge between current challenges ("here and now") and the sustainable future they aspire to attain.

ABIS initiatives are multi-party, interdisciplinary and cross functional. They are grounded in research, prioritise dialogue and ideation, and produce accessible, relevant outputs.

ABIS convenes corporate-academic collaboration around three pillars of activity which drive the culture, mindsets and processes of business transformation:

1) Thought Leadership & Deep Knowledge Creation
2) Change Agency & Shared Learning
3) Embedded Sustainability (in strategy; business valuation, models and processes; and capacity building)

Marieke de Kort's Publications

  • It's a Man's World

    • Brabant Center of Entrepreneurship
    • November 1, 2011
    Authors: Marieke de Kort, Geert Duysters

    The booklet "It's a Man's World" is written by Marieke de Kort and Prof.dr.Geert Duysters who is a scientific director at the Brabant Center of Entrepreneurship.

    In the booklet the five most persistent myths about men and women in relation to entrepreneurship are investigated. Some myths appear partially true, others are totally incorrect but are nevertheless widely believed. The authors once again underline the importance of female entrepreneurs and women in leadership positions.

    BCE provides in-house a series of booklets, the BCE Entrepreneurship Series. This is made possible by a contribution from the European Regional Development Fund. With this series of booklets BCE aims to view and discuss entrepreneurship from different perspectives.
