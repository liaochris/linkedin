Bryn Richards' Summary

I have always loved animation and wanted to be an animator since the age of 3. Now I get to do just that and I love it. I'm a character animator and have been lucky enough to work in great cinematic departments within the games industry. Delving into characters and their emotions is my strong point but I find all other aspects equally as exciting.

I have been fortunate enough to study with iAnimate where I was taught by feature film animators from Dreamworks. Before joining iAnimate I completed a BSc in Computer Animation and Special Effects at the University of Bradford where I gained a first class honours degree.

When I'm not animating at work I can be found animating at home and working on animated short film projects with other professionals.

Specialties

Character animation, general animation, motion capture cleanup, cinematics.
