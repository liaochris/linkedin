Daryl H. Bryant's Summary

Daryl. H Bryant, Co-founder and CEO at Hudson Horizons and StartupValley, has spent the past fifteen years working in internet and technology. Well-versed in search, social media, mobile and web development, Bryant consistently maintains his position on the forefront of the most cutting-edge technological innovations. Daryl is also a managing partner at Hudson Venture Partners, SammyHelps, Ticketini, and Order Windows Online.

His entrepreneurial spirit and extensive knowledge of internet and technology have provided a strong foundation for his portfolio of ventures to flourish. Mr. Bryant’s strong leadership skills have been a key component in his companies’ continuous growth. In the past year Hudson Horizons has more than tripled in size and continues to expand. His employees consider him not only a boss, but a mentor and friend. He has developed a work environment that is as creative as it is efficient, resulting in creative solutions and an unsurpassed work ethic.

A leader, innovator and businessman in all professional endeavors, Bryant is also known for his philanthropic disposition. He has contributed to a number of charities, some of which include: Big Brothers Big Sisters, Westside Infant and Teen Parent Program, National MS Society, Livestrong, Montel Williams MS Foundation, and American Red Cross. He also sits on the board of several non-profit organizations, including the North Jersey Regional Chamber of Commerce and the West Side Infant and Teen Parent Program. Bryant is also a co-founding board member of the Crowdfunding Professional Association (CfPA).

Daryl H. Bryant is a dedicated father, husband, boss, mentor and entrepreneur. He continues to maintain the highest level of professionalism and altruism in all of his endeavors and remains an industry leader along the way

When Daryl is not working, he is passionate about spending time with family and maintaining a healthy and active lifestyle through diet and triathlon training.

Daryl H. Bryant's Publications

  • MS - Living Symptom Free: The True Story of an MS Patient: A Guide on How to Eat Properly and Live a Healthy Life while Controlling, Reducing, and Eliminating the Symptoms of Multiple Sclerosis

    • MSLivingSymptomFree (August 13, 2011)
    • August 13, 2011
    Authors: Daryl H. Bryant

    Are you or is someone who you love and care for living with Multiple Sclerosis? For people struggling to discover a happier, healthier way to live with this lifelong disease, it sometimes seems like an impossible task. Now, author Daryl H Bryant shares his own challenges, frustrations, anxiety, and ultimate victories in managing MS. His new book shares his stories as a person faced with the disease. Bryant is not a doctor, but a person living with Multiple Sclerosis. MS – Living Symptom Free shares Bryant’s daily regimens that have resulted in his symptom-free living. With hard-won insight, practical advice, fitness tips, and recipes, this invaluable guide instructs readers on how to eat properly and live a healthy life while controlling, reducing, and eliminating the symptoms of MS. In each user-friendly chapter, Bryant covers topics including symptoms and complications, the author’s own road to MS, the benefits of conventional medication, things doctors don’t tell you, popular MS diets, vitamins and supplements, exercise and sleep, staying on track, support systems, and more. The guide also features more than twenty-five easy recipes that adhere to many prevalent MS-friendly diets. MS – Living Symptom Free reveals the author’s own pain, struggles, and depression which led to his hitting rock bottom. Today, Bryant has emerged healthier, happier, and more motivated than ever. Hoping to ease the journey of others, he offers inspiration and frontline guidance to assure patients that they are not alone. It is ideal for anyone ready to embark on a healthier, happier life. Includes over twenty-five MS-friendly recipes.
