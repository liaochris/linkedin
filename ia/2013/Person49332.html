Tina Lupiezowiec's Summary

► My name is Tina Lupiezowiec, Aka Farmer Tina "Lupi". I'm pleased to meet you. I am a residential urban farmer of gourmet tomatoes, peppers, garlic and herbs in Madison, Wisconsin. I am also a public speaker, motivator, trainer, community volunteer and Jill of All Trades. I am the owner, farmer and only employee of Goin' Lupi, LLC.

► I enjoy teaching people how to build raised beds, rain-water collection systems and compost bins, garden naturally, take baby-steps toward sustainability, and overall relax and quit being so romantic about grass.

► Fulfillment for me is facing a challenging day with moxie and fortitude; knowing that the success or failure of my endeavors is up to me, the cosmos aligning and the support of my family, friends, and wonderful business associates.

► Having donned more proverbial hats than most, I feel that I am amply qualified to know just what I can achieve - which is about anything I put my mind to. Thankfully, I haven't a mind to do just anything. My ambitions have culminated in all that which is Goin' Lupi: From the website (www.goinlupi.com) and Facebook page (facebook.com/goinlupi), where I express myself with little reserve; to my interactions and support of goinlupi friends, whom I enjoy without reservation!

► Running Goin' Lupi, LLC while farming my property and selling the gourmet produce I grow, literally utilizes every imaginable skill I have acquired throughout my life. From office management to teaching English as a second language, volunteer outreach to steam-fitting; my rather long and diverse list of jobs led me finally to the understanding that I was destined to be an entrepreneur and a positive motivational force in the lives of others.

► Thanks for taking the time to get to me. Please allow me the opportunity to meet you too.
