Mike Armour's Summary

I am a leadership development specialist with over 30 years of experience in helping entrepreneurs, top executives, and emerging leaders nail their most vital responsibilities and grow their capabilities. My professional practice focuses on executive coaching and mentoring, facilitation services, leadership training, and team-building. I have an extensive client history with companies of every size, from small start-ups to Fortune 100 companies.

Recently I also launched StartupsAfter50.com, a website with resources, tutorials, and guidance for "encore entrepreneurs," men and women who are starting a business in their 50s, 60s, or 70s after a career in another vocation.

In addition I am a professional speaker addressing audiences internationally on topics related to leadership, communication, trust-building, and ethics. Stateside I often speak on patriotic and inspirational themes.

Currently, in partnership with Leadership Training Associates International of Nairobi, I am heading an initiative called Leadership Development Africa, which offers large-venue leadership workshops across East Africa,

Specialties: Leadership development; leadership coaching; executive mentoring; trust-building; strategic planning, strategic alignment; transformational change; assessment of corporate culture; team-building; succession planning; non-profit management; cross-cultural management; .

Mike Armour's Publications

  • Systems-Sensitive Leadership, 2nd Edition

    • College Press
    • 2000
    Authors: Mike Armour, Don Browning

    Lays out the basics of the systems theory developed by Clare Graves and applies these principles to understanding, avoiding, and overcoming conflict in church settings. The basic and advanced theory sections, however, are equally applicable to organizations of any type.

  • Leadership and the Power of Trust

    • LfeThemes Press
    • 2007
    Authors: Mike Armour

    Develops the principles of Trust-Centered Leadership based on the author's first-hand experience in turning around a college in financial distress. It identifies and describes five things that people must feel in order for trust to flourish. And it devotes an entire chapter to each of seven character traits found among highly-trusted leaders.

  • Bushido Business: The Art of the Modern Professional

    • Insight Publishing
    • 2010
    Authors: Mike Armour, Stephen M. R. Covey, Tom Hopkins, Brian Tracy

    Bushido was the code of the Japanese samurai warriors. The essence of this code is just as relevant to success in the modern business world as it was in the world of the samurai. Each chapter is an interview with a noted leadership development specialist.

  • Leadership and Life: Quick Pointers for Success

    • LifeThemes Press
    • 2010
    Authors: Mike Armour

    Succinct, insight-packed chapters on leadership and the personal life of the leader. The chapters are drawn from popular articles which appeared in the first decade of the author's widely followed LeaderPerfect Newsletter.

  • A Newcomer's Guide to the Bible

    • College Press
    • 1999
    Authors: Mike Armour

    Developed originally as a text for Russian high schools and universities, this book introduces the major characters, events, themes, and timelines of the Bible in a highly simplified manner. With over a million copies in print in over a dozen languages, it has been highly praised as the perfect introductory text for someone who knows very little or even nothing about the Bible. A companion self-study guide was published in 2002.

  • La Biblia al Alcance de Todos

    • LifeThemes Press
    • 2003
    Authors: Mike Armour

    The Spanish version of A Newcomer's Guide to the Bible, listed above. Editors across multiple Spanish dialects participated in the editing of this edition to make it as usable as possible across a wide-variety of Spanish-speaking communities.
