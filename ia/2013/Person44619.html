Freddy Granda's Summary

With his keen ability to listen, understand and balance his client’s needs with their financial means, Freddy Granda uses his background in economics and wealth of experience to find his clients their dream apartment.

Freddy moved to New York City from Lima, Peru in 1998 and is thrilled to work for the best real estate rental company in the greatest city in the world. Prior to Citi Spaces, he was employed by The Brownstone Agency, where he gained an understanding of personal finance and various types of mortgages. With a BS in Economics from Ricardo Palma University in Lima, Peru, Freddy takes pride in combing his educational background with his wealth of real estate knowledge to benefit his real estate clients.

He works with rentals and sales clients searching for a place for them to call home in the City. He makes certain no client leaves without learning something new about what New York City has to offer and how he can help them achieve their needs. “I’ve learned that in real estate, timing is everything,” he says, “I’m able to work quickly to help prospective clients locate and lease rental apartments and I make their experience a pleasant one.”

Apartment hunting should be left to a professional and relationships are everything to Freddy. Contact him today to get started!
