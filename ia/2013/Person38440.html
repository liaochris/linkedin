David Warwick's Summary

Online strategy expert. David is Managing Director of Smart Works formed in 1992 and consults through a number of agencies including Digital Greenfields and Mars Marketing. David has developed digital strategy, social media programs and online marketing campaigns for brands including Westpac, Siemens, Bayer, Independent Schools Victoria, Fair Work Australia and CSR Building Materials.

Specialties: Digital strategy, online marketing, social media, web applications, consultancy, speaking and training, search, web content, technology trends, general management, web publishing, RFP writing, vendor assessment and related industry engagement for all aspects of digital marketing.
