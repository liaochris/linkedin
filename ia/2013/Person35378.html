Richard Graves' Summary

Marina Business Consultant and Business Broker specializing in Marinas For Sale

I have been involved in the South Florida Marine and Hospitality Industry for over 28 years.

I currently serve as Principal of Richard Graves & Associates, a marina business development consulting firm specializing in start-up and turn-around management, Principal of Professional Marine Realty, LLC, a Marine Business Brokerage specializing in Marinas for sale and a member of North Star Marine Associates an association of consultants which develops, designs and manages marinas and related facilities in the United States, Bahamas and the Caribbean.

In previous marine related positions, I oversaw the construction, set up the business and served as the opening General Manager of Sunrise Harbor Megayacht Marina, - Executive VP International Marinas, Director of Marketing for Chinnock Marine Yacht Repair and Re-Fit, Senior VP Bahia Mar Resort & Yachting Center Director of Operations for Lincoln Harbor Yacht Club and Port Imperial Marina in New York Harbor, On site management consultant for Marina Bay, Fort Lauderdale, Venice Marina Brooklyn, Port St Joe Marina, St Joe Florida and General Manager for Water Taxi’s New York Harbor ferry operation.
