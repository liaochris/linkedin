ABC of Sales - Always Be Closing


I am the bestselling, award winning author of The ABC of Sales: Lessons from a Superstar. This book is a guide for professionals who want to soar in sales, revive their sales careers, or position their companies among the elite.

Always Be Closing is a motto I live by and is one of the prime reasons I have been successful as a sales professional. I never turn off my selling mindset; I’m always ready to engage a friend, colleague, or stranger in conversations that will lead to a sale.
