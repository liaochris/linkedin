Andy Vitale's Summary

I am a UX Designer specializing in emerging technology and digital media, for web, mobile and software. I have professional experience with a visual design and creative direction background from designing and maintaining National and International brands. as well as leadership experience teaching design practices and theories on the collegiate level, spending five years as the Graphic Design Program Department Chair at a regionally accredited University and being a partner in a digital agency with both full-time and contract employees. Staying on top of the latest trends, I have had strategic roles in managing branding and marketing projects across various platforms from traditional to interactive media.

I am currently completing my Master’s Degree in Human Computer Interaction at DePaul University and I often speak at workshops and conferences around the country. My core belief is to create immersive, emotional experiences with users through different mediums and to explore new interaction models in emerging technology.

• Well versed at utilizing the latest technology to develop key strategies across multiple platforms.
• Excellent design sense and knowledge of typography.
• Establishing and maintaining client relationships.
• Comprehensive understanding of brand identity development.
• Skilled at conceptualizing, coordinating and implementing marketing strategies and campaigns - concept through completion.
