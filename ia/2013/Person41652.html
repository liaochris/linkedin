Summary

My career started in the sales field where I went from being a sales engineer to managing a Sales and Marketing team in the Cobra group, leader in the energy projects sector. From here I decided to move to a medium size family company, Ortea S.A., where I was working closely with the top management in a client focus role.

5 years ago I decided to challenge myself even more by working in another country, Ireland, and in a new field, the Online Marketing. Thus, I started at Populis as a SEM Specialist and grew in a fast way up to achieve the management of the SEM team for Spain and South America.
At that time, I accepted an opportunity to lead the SEM worldwide team for a start-up business in the Dada Group. Few months later, I received the trust from the senior management to manage as well the Dublin office and all its employees.
Due to the high revenues generated (around $25 Million per year), we decided, inside Dada Group, to run this start-up business as an independent company, subsidiary of Dada, the name of which is Moqu Adv, a leading European digital Agency, where I am a member of the Board of Directors as an Executive Director.
