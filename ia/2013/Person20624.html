Carrie Webb's Summary

As a creative interior designer with extensive experience in the leisure sector I have worked on many hotel, bar, restaurant and retail projects.

My company, Paperspace Design, creates distinctive, original and effective interiors for restaurants, hotels, bars and shops. We help our clients improve their businesses by getting to know them, understanding their goals and offering creative solutions to their problems.

Specialties

hotel design, bar design, restaurant design, retail design, brand identity

Carrie Webb's Experience

Creative Director

Paperspace Design Ltd

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Design industry

July 2009 – Present (4 years 2 months) London, United Kingdom

Through my own company I work for a variety of clients mainly in the hospitality and leisure sector. I have worked on a wide range of projects including cafes, restaurants and retail clients and my aim is to improve client's businesses through improving operational efficiency as well as aesthetics.

In addition to this I also work with other design companies on a variety of hospitality and residential projects.

Interior Designer

Proof Consultancy

September 2007 – July 2009 (1 year 11 months) London, United Kingdom

Working as part of a small team on a range of leisure design projects I played a key role in all aspects and stages of the design process. Excellent communication and time management skills were essential in ensuring high standards and tight deadlines were met. The creative nature of the projects has allowed me to extend my conceptual design skills as well as improving my hand sketching and visualization.

Interior Designer

Pringle Brandon

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Architecture & Planning industry

March 2007 – August 2007 (6 months)

Working on a number of prestigious office design projects including EDFT in Victoria and Macquarie Bank at Moor House, my role involved conceptual design schemes, detailing of bespoke joinery and producing 3D perspective visuals. I was also responsible for the completion and co-ordination of a number of drawing packages.

Creative Designer

Quadrant Design

Public Company; 11-50 employees; Design industry

November 2004 – March 2007 (2 years 5 months)

As the creative interior designer for a number of large and small scale leisure and retail projects, I successfully designed and implemented both conceptual and roll-out schemes for a wide variety of clients from small bars and restaurants to large retail chains such as Debenhams and New Look. The complete design service provided to my clients covered all aspects of the design process from carrying out site surveys, designing conceptual schemes and producing full working drawing packages as well as attending site meetings, liaising with contractors and overseeing progress of on-site projects. In addition to this I completed statutory approvals applications for many leisure and retail projects.
