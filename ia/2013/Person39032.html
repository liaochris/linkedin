Dylan Pask's Summary

I am a Creative Professional with experience working in Photography and Design. As a designer, my detail-oriented, ethical and discreet nature has allowed me to collaborate with clients in North America and Europe including Cisco Systems, DuPont, Canadian Tire, Universal Studios, Iovate Health Sciences, The City of Toronto, and Rogers Communications.

Writing the story of a project is where I thrive. Using my background as a designer I create a user experience that combines the emotional, visual and technical elements of a photo shoot into an end result that exceeds the clients expectations.
