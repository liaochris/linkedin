Scott Montgomery's Summary

Lifestyle photography for the advertising, design and marketing community. 20 plus years experience producing projects throughout the US, Caribbean and South America. Brands include Andersen Windows, Sprint, US Army, Lipitor, Wyndham, Verizon, Nationwide Insurance. Agency's include AbelsonTaylor, Mullen, Sterling Rice, Campbell Mithun, Pentagram, Vitro, Unit 7.

To view online portfolio - www.smontgomery.com

Specialties: I specialize in real people lifestyle with a storytelling point of view. Extensive experience with still image composites and full scale production. Director/DP/Steadicam operator for motion.
