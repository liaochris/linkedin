Lisette Johnson's Summary

As a relationship manager at Integra Leasing & Commercial Fiance I bring established, privately held companies in need of capital for tangible asset acquisition together with specialty funding sources and provide an alternative to traditional bank financing to allow continuation of full access to bank lines.

Projects include wood extrusion manufacturing equipment refinance; medical records scanning equipment and software acquisition; medical professional staffing receivables financing; commercial truck leasing for delivery and food service companies; commercial real estate bridge loans and refinance; special assets buyouts.

Specialties: Municipal and federal leasing, medical/veterinary, manufacturing; factoring, purchase order & contract financing, hard money and commercial real estate.
