Dee Coakley's Summary

Experienced tech industry COO, with proven ability to execute and drive growth. An entrepreneurially-spirited individual, with an interest in the strategic development of start-ups and early-stage technology businesses. Passionate about working with creative and inspirational people in ventures that are at the exciting stage between initial proof of concept and taking flight as a successful, established business.

Founder of Solas Consult – advising early stage technology businesses on strategy, business operations, fundraising & investments, and supporting international tech companies in establishing their first UK office.

Prior to founding Solas, Dee was COO of mobile software company, Masabi, a world-leading provider of mobile ticketing technology to the mass transit sector, with customers such as thetrainline.com, Virgin Trains, Cross Country, Boston’s MBTA, and New York’s MTA. Dee was brought into Masabi to prepare the business for investment and development beyond startup phase, and had responsibility for commercial and strategic development, and for leading business operations – including finance, legal and HR. During her time with Masabi, Dee led investment transactions totalling US$9m, and drove the business from startup stage, through a period of rapid growth and expansion, to its current position as a successful international business.


Key Skills
Internal business operations; refining vision & developing strategy; defining performance metrics and goals; implementing process; financial management; developing financial models and business plans; fundraising; team-building & defining culture; HR policy; legal function; company secretarial; investor relations.
