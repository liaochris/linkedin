Cheree Warrick's Summary

As author of "Creating Business Plans that Actually Get Financed," I teach entrepreneurs how to position their companies so that multiple banks or angel investors agree to finance their company.

The foundation for any entrepreneur seeking a bank loan, angel investor or venture capital is a solid business plan. The Profit Partner helps clients solidify business strategy, write the business plan, give presentations to those with capital and consult after funding has been given.

As an active member of the Tysons Regional Chamber of Commerce and The Tower Club, I work with entrepreneurs seeking funds for their dreams.
