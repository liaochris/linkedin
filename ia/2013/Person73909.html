Co-Founder

StyleSync.me

Partnership; 1-10 employees; E-Learning industry

October 2012 – Present (11 months) Madison, Wisconsin Area

StyleSync.me is an online social media training and business education platform designed specifically for salon owners and stylists to help them understand the ins and outs of social media. Through step-by-step tutorials, turnkey content and customized marketing campaigns, StyleSync.me is the one stop social media resource, guaranteed to jump-start any salon business.

StyleSync.me, is a natural complement to my existing agency, Evoke Brand Strategies. Working closely with a multitude of salons, my co-founder (Todd Laabs) and I noticed a void in the marketplace - a resource for salon owners and stylists to take social media management in to their own hands. That's the mission of StyleSync.me - to provide simple, valuable resources that bridge the gap between the complicated, ever-changing world of social media, in turn to grow your salon business or personal brand.
