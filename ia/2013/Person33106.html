Darrell Claunch's Summary

I've had the good fortune to work across several fields of the entertainment industry as an artist in multiple mediums. The whole while I've been building an ever expanding love for digital visual effects, the joy of a well paced edit, and the perfect, simple line.

I have worked on a slew of films, television shows and video games. For a full list visit my website at www.knights-end.com.

Currently I am spending the majority of my hours as a a visual effects artist at Look! Effects and as a pixel artist (primarily for NDS titles).

Specialties

Digital effects, Illustration, Storyboard artist, Copywriting, Scriptwriting, Film Editor, Pixel art/animation, Story Analysis.
