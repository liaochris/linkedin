Kevin Knippa's Summary

I translate vision into reality through application of a diverse set of skills. I rapidly assimilate complex technical documents, including building codes, standards and land development ordinances. I have extensive experience writing and editing documents, including training materials, design firm graphical standards, project programming reports and organizational CAD procedure manuals. I understand the impact of technology on the profitability of design organizations and implement and standardize the use of technology, such as CAD software, to maximize productivity and profits. I know how buildings are put together and have extensive knowledge in the design and detailing of exterior building envelopes to fulfill all project aesthetic and performance requirements.
