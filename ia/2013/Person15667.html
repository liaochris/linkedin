Bonnie Best's Summary

Experienced teacher for over 40 years, 10 years as a computer programmer, manager, analyst and planner for Pacific Bell, 10 years as a Professional Mediator. I've been a Wellness Consultant since 1997.

My purpose in life is to live and teach joy, abundance and serenity. My passion is to joyously inspire people to create a lifestyle of harmony, health, wealth and joy.

Specialties: Help people achieve meaningful goals in life, to live the life they LOVE, teach workshops based on Marci Shimoff's best-selling books "Happy for No Reason" and "Love for No Reason", teach people to use the computer as a tool for success
