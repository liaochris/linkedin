Jonathan Babcock's Summary

I am a Senior Manager at Jabian Consulting, an Atlanta based management and IT consultancy. My areas of expertise include business analysis, process optimization, and solution delivery methodology. My passion is to help organizations and individuals change in order to reach the next step in their progression.

Prior to joining Jabian, I worked primarily in Business Analysis at both Accenture and Genuine Parts Company. I enjoy meeting and interacting with business analysis professionals, and am an active participant in the online business analysis community through my Practical Analyst website (http://practicalanalyst.com/) and Twitter (@jonbab1). I am also active in the local business analysis community through my membership in the Greater Atlanta Chapter of the International Institute of Business Analysis (IIBA).
