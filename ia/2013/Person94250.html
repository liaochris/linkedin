Mike Reed's Summary

I am a partner at Reed & Associates, Inc., a full-service creative marketing firm with offices in Charlotte, N.C., and Jackson, Tenn. Prior to this, I was manager of communications at Crown Communications in Charlotte and director of government affairs for the Flagler Home Builders Association in Palm Coast, Fla. I was also a staff writer for The Daytona Beach News-Journal and The St. Augustine Record.

Specialties: Public relations and media relations. Marketing. Social media. Writing and editing. Project management. Journalism. Local, state and federal government affairs.
