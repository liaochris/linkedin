Aji Abraham's Summary

Aji Abraham is a technology entrepreneur who has founded and led two companies (Armia Systems, Inc and SupportPRO) into profitability. These companies have grown to include very efficient, high-energy onshore and offshore teams.

Armia Systems, Inc offers web development solutions to small and medium businesses in the US. Founded in 2001, Armia Systems, Inc has a captive offshore development center in state sponsored Infopark in Kochi, south India. This development center employs close to 100 programmers and engineers. The majority of technical development is completed in India while architecture and requirements are done in the Chicago office, offering significant advantages in terms of value and visibility. This provides a significant talent pool for most web technologies like PHP , Ruby, and iOS development. Also has a stong foundation in facebook, twitter, linkedin app development.

Under the direction of Mr. Abraham, Armia Systems, Inc developed a number of open source web applications in social networking, ecommerce, and content management which are marketed under the brand iScripts.com.

SupportPRO offers 24x7 technical support services and server administration services to web hosting, datacenter, ISP, and ASP industries. The SupportPRO call center is located in Kochi, south India. SupportPRO is one of the leading technical support providers for the hosting industry for the last 4 years.

As one of the founding partners of Chicago based startup incubator - Platform Venture, LLC Abraham spearheads the technology aspects of the portfolio companies.

Prior to entrepreneurship, Mr. Abraham worked with Motorola Network Solutions as a Strategic Product Manager providing long term vision for the Networks business. He also worked as a Software Engineer with Motorola developing infrastructure products.

Specialties
