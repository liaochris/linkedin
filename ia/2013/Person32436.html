David Purnell's Summary

From the American Idols Live Tour website to internet games for Kellogg’s Pop-Tarts, David Purnell and DP Creative Audio have provided innovative music to increase the impact of media for today’s industry leaders.

Whether your product is advertising, film, television or games, all are in need of quality music to heighten their impact. Understanding how to create music to complement the visuals, in a wide range of styles, is essential. David's musical background spans many genres, from traditional orchestral to modern rock. Coupled with a solid understanding of contemporary music technology and a strong track record of prompt, reliable service, David is ready and able to provide a dynamic musical edge to your product.
