Michael Sutter's Summary

Michael's 20+ years in technology sales and asset disposition enable him to help Fortune 500 and middle market companies effectively manage IT budgets.

His experience includes sales of business class computers, Cisco equipment, laptops, telephone systems and LCD monitors plus recycling, planning and developing strategies to meet clients’ needs though their Technology Lifecycle Management challenges.

Solutions for asset management are based upon careful consideration of client objectives, then developing a plan for secure data erasure, equipment recycling and environment-friendly disposal.

Michael Sutter is president of BBC Technology Solutions. He and his team will help with all of your equipment needs. Whether it’s purchasing pre-owned business class IT equipment for your company or agency or disposing of surplus equipment BBC Technology Solutions will provide valuable assistance.
