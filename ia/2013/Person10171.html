Gabriele "Asbesto" Zaverio's Summary

I'm into Computer Science since 1976. I was there BEFORE the command line. ;)

I'm a UNIX System administrator with 25+ years of experience, under a lot of ancient UNIX flavours (Ultrix, Irix, BSD 2.11 Unix, UNIX System V Rel. 3, XENIX, Solaris etc...) and with the recent UNIX operating systems (GNU/Linux Slackware, Dyne:Bolic, Debian, Gentoo etc; Free/OpenBSD; NetBSD)

I'm a VAX/VMS System Administrator with 20+ years of experience.

I'm a Sysadmin with hardware & software experience on a lot of ancient systems: DIGITAL PDP-11, Data General ECLIPSE / NOVA, CONTROL DATA, HEWLETT/PACKARD, HONEYWELL etc.

I programmed a lot of Assembly code for the following microprocessors: 6502, 6510, 68000, Z80, 8085, 8088, 80286, 80386, Z8000 and PDP11/34, uPDP11/23


I'm an Electronic enthusiast and a Radio Amateur (callsign IW9HGS) sice 2002. I have a lot of experience in radio communications, radio equipments, antennas building and emergency communication systems in HF, VHF and UHF bands, in voice or digital modes.

I'm an expert in WIFI MESH Networks hardware modding and software tweaking, developer of fast field deployable tactical wifi network for MILSIM simulations with 653° Shark Team.

As a consultant I prefer a part time job in which I can work from home via Internet following my rythms.

PS I HATE JAVA/C# and everything related to it, so I don't wanna work in JAVA/C# related projects.
