Altaf Rahamatulla's Summary

Coach Altaf is now a Strength, Speed, and Conditioning professional and a motivational coach with combined athletic training and conditioning experience. The transition to fitness and health field came, when Altaf was in a serious car accident, subsequently had two cervical spine and three knee surgeries and had to design a recovery plan for his overall fitness and eventual return to the workforce. He became experienced at adapting exercise protocols to fit the unique needs of the individual, and he decided to share this knowledge and training by becoming a coach. His discipline is an advanced training utilizing various strengthening, conditioning, and mental disciplines from both Eastern and Western traditions.

Certified Personal Trainer through the National Council On Strength and Fitness, Certified Training for Warriors (TFW) Instructor, Certified Battling Ropes Coach, Certified Kettlebell Instructor, TRX Level 1, D.V.R.T (Dynamic Variable Resistance Training) Coach, , Indian club (Student of Dr. Ed Thomas), Jump Stretch (Flex Band), USA Boxing Association: Licensed Fitness Trainer.

He has also joined forces with Martin Rooney and is an affiliate within the TFW system.

His program is centered around principle, not on a particular exercise or piece of equipment. The principles you will lean can be applied to any exercise or tools. You will become the gym and utilize any environment to reach your fitness goals. It is more important to know proper technique and training methods rather than any individual tools that you train with. His training will incorporate the following tools: Battling Ropes, Sandbags, TRX, Kettlebell, Indian Clubs, Ladders, Power Sled, Jump Ropes, Chain Reaction, Weighted Water System, Medicine Ball, BOSU, Stability Ball, Bodyweight etc.

Coach Altaf’s training encompasses emphasis on the entire body to:
o Increase reserve and work capacity
o Improve energy and increase metabolic rate, enabling you to be in a prime position for fat loss
o Improved range of motion
o Increased power
o Reduced fatigue
o Help you achieve fitness and health goals
