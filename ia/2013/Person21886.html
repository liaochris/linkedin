Chris Hammerlund's Summary

I have successfully expanded my healthcare staffing business, Assured Healthcare, in Gurnee. By bringing to bear my past nursing, business and management experience and the numerous professional relationships I have developed over the years, I, my staff and employees in the field have built Assured Healthcare into a well established solution resource for customers throughout Lake County, Illinois and Wisconsin. The company also maintains strong ties to community, and supports numerous charitable, volunteer and civic causes.
Having owned other businesses in the past, including another staffing enterprise as well as a candy and ice cream store, I enjoy both the challenges and sense of accomplishment that come with the territory for successful entrepreneurs. Even in today's competitive economy, I feel that there is an abundance of opportunity for all service providers who operate with integrity, flexibility and the willingness to collaborate with others to find solutions.
