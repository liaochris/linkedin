Jim Walsh's Summary

Disputes are part of business. Contracts, employment matters, regulatory investigations, trade secrets and collections all hold inherent risk for disagreement. Disputes distract, interfere with daily work and, most importantly, can shift a business’s course. Jim Walsh solves these problems for businesses and individuals, creatively assessing and managing risk, and always putting the client first.

Jim offers more than 40 years experience as a business litigator and trial lawyer. He has handled matters all over the world, using tempered tenacity to resolve every dispute. Jim has a wide range of skills that serve a client’s business objectives.

First, Jim has the willingness and ability to take the case to trial or through arbitration and he has the sound judgment to know when to advise against it. He has tried over 60 jury cases and taken over 100 arbitrations to conclusion. He is thorough, disciplined, and clear in his approach.

Second, Jim is acutely aware of the importance of explicitly defining business objectives in any dispute and developing a rational strategy to achieve those objectives. He works closely and deliberately with the client in this area.

Third, litigation costs are a distraction that often influences resolution. Jim Walsh knows how to predict and manage litigation costs through budgeting, forecasting and alternative fee agreements. More importantly, he communicates what to expect and when to expect it.

Fourth, over 90% of all disputes settle and each settlement must be designed to recognize a client’s specific objectives. Jim brings logic, insight and thorough care to the settlement process. He has served as court-appointed and private mediator and is conversant with all Alternative Dispute Resolution processes. However, Jim is a strong negotiator. He never allows the “process” to override the client’s objectives.

Jim’s intentional, disciplined and clear approach to business litigation will work to your advantage.
