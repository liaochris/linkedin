  • Se Necesita

    • April 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Salvador Garcia, Chris Heather

    SeNecesita, which means “Help Wanted” in Spanish, is a social venture designed to lower underemployment in rural areas of Peru. SeNecesita uses SMS text messages to quickly connect employers with people in search of work. An job searcher signs up for the SeNecesita service by job category and with their cell phone number. Cell phones are very prevalent in rural Peru compared with internet access. Once an employer posts a job in a specific category, for example Cashier, all of the cell phon numbers signed up in the specific category are sent a notification via SMS. If they are interested they call the employer.
