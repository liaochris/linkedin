Jennifer Lasseter, P.E.'s Summary

I am a professional engineer, P.E., registered as a sole practitioner under the name Mind in North Austin with the Texas Board of Professional Engineers. I am an environmental consultant and online content specialist. I've had several years of experience with the Texas Commission on Environmental Quality and in collaboration with the Zero Waste Network at UT-Arlington. (See Resume.) I am committed to doing all I can to shift our society's approach to business and daily-living from a "brown" economy toward a "green" economy. To me this means moving away from fossil fuels and petroleum-based products. I believe human activities should mimic and integrate with the regenerative processes of our planet. My areas of interest include sustainability, pollution prevention, corporate social responsibility, environmental management systems (EMS, i.e. ISO 14001), lean manufacturing, solar energy and electric vehicles.
