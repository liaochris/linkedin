Jose Luis Revelo's Summary

Grammy nominated musician and music industry executive Jose Luis Revelo has enjoyed a successful bi-cultural career for over a decade. Born in Colombia and raised in Panama and the United States, Revelo’s upbringing took him on a journey along the line that connects Hispanic and American cultures. Revelo got his start in the music industry working on records by renowned Latin stars including Marc Anthony and Thalia, eventually earning himself a Grammy nomination for his work with Puerto Rican star Gisselle. Eventually, Revelo relocated to New York where he became one of the most in-demand multi-cultural composers, working on campaigns for such high profile brands as Pepsi, Nissan, Budweiser, the global tourism campaign for Turkey and the political campaigns of Michael Bloomberg. He has founded several successful startups in New York, including MuuseMe.com, a multicultural music licensing platform currently offering some of the best indie music from Latin America and Spain.

In 2013, Revelo joined Man Made Music as Director of Business Development and Music Strategy, Latin Markets. He is part of the team that created a global Sonic Strategy for Univision, and is co-producer of the brand's Sonic Identity, which includes dozens of new executions, spanning from the brand anthem and sonic logo, to the television identity package, news, sports, radio networks, and the launch of the male-skewing Unimas channel. He also co-executive produced select Latin market activations for AT&T, in support of Man Made's role of Sonic Agency of Record for the brand.
