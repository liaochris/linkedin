Robert Borer's Summary

Dr. Rob Borer developed his critical thinking while earning a BS in Mechanical Engineering from Michigan State University. Following 6 years of applying this knowledge with companies such as GE and Becton Dickinson, with assignments in foreign countries, Dr. Rob decided to become a chiropractor. While earning his doctorate in Chiropractic, Dr. Rob interned in the world renown office of Dr. E.L. "Bud" Crowder. It is Dr. Crowder's critical eye, council and mentorship that Dr. Rob credits much of his professional success. Dr. Crowder, while living was an expert in the Upper Cervical Specific Protocol that Dr. Rob has mastered and delivered in his office for the past 10 years. Dr. Rob continues his professional development each year by attending conferences, conventions, symposiums and seminars. Dr. Rob is currently in the credentialing process of earning his Diplomate of the Upper Cervical Spine.

Specialties

No Twisting, Popping or Cracking. Upper Cervical Doctors are able to reposition the spine with a precise, light touch restoring normal nerve supply and allowing health to return naturally and gently.
