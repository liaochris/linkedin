Collette Walsh's Summary

Award-winning journalist and editor. Freelance writer/commentator & broadcaster - TV & radio.

Areas: News/current affairs, TV/entertainment, life & style, travel & food, women's interest.

Broadcast: Presenter, commentator and reviewer - national TV & radio UK and Ireland.

(From Feb 2013 will be presenting The Travel Book Club on Sky holiday Channel 284)

TV commentating: BBC Breakfast, BBC News, Sky News, ITN News, Granada Reports, Channel 5 and RTE/others.

Radio: BBC Radio 4 - Woman's Hour, regular on BBC 5 Live - Tony Livesey Show, Stephen Nolan Show and Richard Bacon etc.
Regular pundit on BBC Scotland, Ulster, Three Counties and BBC Manchester. Plus, LBC London - news and TV reviews.

Newspapers (feature writer/commissioning ed on several national newspapers since 1997): The Guardian, The Mirror, The Sun, The Express, The Telegraph and Sunday Mirror.

Magazines: Feature writer/commissioning editor - Various top lifestyle glossies - former Chief Feature Writer and supplements editor on OK! magazine. Travel writer - Wedding Ideas and others

Media consultant to various household names/celebrities/businesses/organisations - consultant editor and website content advisor/writer as well as talks and media-training. Brands inc: Kelloggs, Vodafone, Orange

Specialties: Journalism, presenting and commentary - broadcast and print
Writing and editing - features, blogs, biographies, interviews/opinion pieces, ghost-writing.

Guest lecturing and pitch/speech/proposal writing.

* Media consultancy
