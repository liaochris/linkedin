Loren Atherley's Summary

Loren Atherley holds a Master's degree in Criminal Justice from Seattle University, where he completed a thesis on behavioral profiling and serial sexual homicide, the Green River Killer and the offender Gary L. Ridgway. A former National Park Ranger, he has worked in the National Incident Management System (NIMS) for fire and Search & Rescue (SAR) since 2003 and is qualified Public Information Officer (PIO) and Type II Wildland Firefighter. Loren has consulted on criminal investigations and behavior, fraud, incident management, organization and a national child abduction case for law enforcement agencies throughout the region, including the Social Security Administration Office of the Inspector General (OIG) Investigative Division, the Washington State Department of Corrections joint Seattle Police Department Neighborhood Corrections Initiative (NCI), the King County Sheriff's Office Special Operations Unit, the Stevens County Sheriff's Office, and the Grays Harbor County Sheriff’s Office. He resides in the Seattle, Belltown neighborhood where he works as a criminal justice researcher and consultant.
