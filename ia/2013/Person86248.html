Marjorie Geiser, MBA, RD/RDN, BCC's Summary

MEG Enterprises helps wellness professionals build the businesses of their dreams. We do this by helping you develop focus and clarity so you can create not only thriving business, but also a fulfilling life, as well.

Through my "7 Steps to 6 Figure Success" system, I walk clients through a process that ensures that not only do they create THEIR dream business, but that they walk away with their own systems that they can duplicate over and over again.Start that process here: http://www.meg-enterprises.com/about/7-steps-to-6-figure-success

I'm also the author of the book "Just Jump: The No-Fear Business Start-up Guide for Health and Fitness Professionals." available on Amazon and Kindle or from http://www.californiabasedpublishing.com.

I am a Registered Dietitian, formerly a Certified Personal Trainer and am a Board Certified Coach with coach education through Coach Training Institute. After running MEG Fitness, I began offering coaching services to other health professionals in 2003.

In 2006, I developed MEG Enterprises, Inc, and in 2005, I returned to school for an MBA in Entrepreneurship in order to be of even more value to my clients.

It is my mission to help wellness professionals realize that they are in the business of changing lives, not just providing nutrition counseling and/or personal training services. Through that new awareness, clients are able to step into not just their own power, but into their own thriving businesses, as well.

I am also a professional speaker, providing fun and entertaining presentations to professional dietetic meetings around the country, as well as webinars for all groups.

Specialties: Business coaching, business consulting, business planning, success planning, marketing, creating marketing messages that sell. Wellness professionals, wellness coaches.
