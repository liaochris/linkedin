Juan Ros, CFP®'s Summary

I provide comprehensive financial planning, wealth management, charitable planning, and investment services to clients who are looking to build a long-term relationship with a trusted advisor.

What makes our firm different is our consultative approach - which means, before we offer one bit of advice, we need to learn about your values and goals, the relationships that are important to you, and the interests you hold. See our Wealth Management Consultative Process flowchart below.

Our thorough Discovery Meeting allows us to create a complete profile of each client, and that profile forms the basis of all our planning.

We are paid only by our clients - no commissions or fees from any outside party.

My extensive background in non-profit charitable planning means we can offer creative solutions that benefit you as well as the world at large - the causes you care about most.

My work in the entertainment industry gives me the perspective to understand the unique and challenging financial needs of entertainment professionals.

Specialties: Personal financial planning, estate planning, charitable planning, investments, philanthropy, tax planning, entertainment industry.
