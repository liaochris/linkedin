Ashley Jones' Summary

Graduating from Clemson University with a BS in graphic communications and business administration minor prepared me well for my career. I worked as a graphic designer for several years while pursuing photography on the side until I could start my own company - and ended up starting two.

Shutter Sweet Photography (wedding and portrait photography) and Skylight Creative Group (corporate photography, graphic design and marketing) were created in 2011 and officially became LLCs in 2012.

Having them both allows me to keep my skills sharp both in photography and graphic design. I really enjoy helping start-up companies with branding, marketing material, websites and SEO and seeing improvement when I work with existing companies to re-design their current material that hasn't been working for them. Product and architectural photography is very different from portraits and weddings, but both challenge me and keep things exciting.

I've also started a personal site at www.ashleynicolejones.com where I am now teaching photography lessons for beginners. I love helping people gain confidence and knowledge to get better results and seeing people light up when it clicks.

Specialties: Corporate Photography, Commercial Photography, Portrait Photography, Interior Photography, Product Shots, Wedding Photography, Graphic Design, Web Design, Print Design, Marketing, Speaking, Teaching
