Madeline Paterson's Summary

I help individuals to find satisfying work and a successful future. Together we hatch a cunning plan. You get a clearer picture of what you want and take steps to get there. You sharpen your profile, spot opportunity and take action to get the job/ career/ assignment/ life that's right for you. My role is to listen hard to really understand your individual situation, help you to reflect deeply and take positive action. You gain energy, greater determination and learn to better articulate your strengths. Career Coaching sessions are guided by what's most important to you - you can be anywhere in the world for this to work.

Clients: "Incredibly supportive and non-judgemental." "Intuitive skilled coach with a light touch, yet serious intent."
