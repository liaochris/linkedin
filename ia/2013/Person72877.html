Kathryn Amenta's Summary

Working nationally from San Francisco, CA for over 25 years, Kathryn Amenta Financial Advisor has successfully helped individuals, couples, families, business owners and partners solve their money problems and get on track to the financial future they desire. How? By guiding clients to the root causes - their money behaviors and underlying beliefs. She then teaches and models powerful money management skills that create positive change today and long-term. It's a more holisitc and individual approach than traditional debt counseling, financial planning or investment counseling. Kathryn does not manage money or give investment advice. Learn about Kathryn Amenta's successful 5-step approach and read testimonials at www.kathrynamenta.com

Specialties

I work with people specifically on cash flow, budgeting and emotional money issues. Complete clarity is essential for making well grounded, values-based financial decisions.
