Carl de Souza's Honors and Awards

  • Winner, Global Microsoft Office and Dynamics GP Solutions Challenge 2002

    Microsoft Corporation
    • May 2002

    In 2002 Microsoft ran a global challenge, where participants were required to showcase a solution using Dynamics GP and Microsoft Office. We created a solution using Dynamics GP, BizTalk Server, XML, Web Services, Office Web Components and Microsoft Excel. My role was lead architect/developer, and solution presenter. Our solution was narrowed down to 5 finalists and presented to a panel at Microsoft's head campus in Redmond, Washington. The solution took first prize, and I later presented it during the keynote of the Microsoft Tech 2002 Conference in front of 700 developers.

  • Finalist, Microsoft European Retail Application Developer (RAD) Awards (Retail Supply Chain category)

    Microsoft Corporation
    • February 2003

    The European Retail Application Developer (RAD) Awards is a yearly competition that recognizes application developers in Europe, Middle East and Africa (EMEA) who utilize Microsoft technology by providing business benefits to their customers in the retail industry. The "prestigious" award ceremony is held yearly at the Global Technology Forum in Paris, France. We were finalists for our solution using BizTalk Server (Enterprise Application Integration) and SQL Server (Business Intelligence). My role in the solution was the lead solution architect and representative at the award ceremony.
