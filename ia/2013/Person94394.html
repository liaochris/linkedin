Mike Meikle's Summary

Technological excellence and success…they lie in one’s ability to merge the strategic with the tactical, to understand the needs and expectations of each organization, and to deliver and support the technologies and applications appropriate to each functional organization.

As a Senior Consultant and Senior Program/Project Manager for several organizations across government, health, telecommunications, corporate and education sectors, I have provided the technological and organizational leadership that has delivered such success.

I have consistently improved the project management and development practices for organizations such as the Virginia Department of Health, Richmond Montessori School, Virginia Department of Emergency Management, and many more. More recently at Virginia Department of Social Services, I led project management of the Division of Licensing Programs Health and Information Network (DOLPHIN), which is an adult and childcare facilities licensing system that was implemented statewide. The project won the Governor’s Silver Technology Award and was written up in Washington Technology for the development and implementation of the innovative pen tablet software.

Through the application of best practice portfolio, program, and project management I have delivered on strategic business objectives. My broad industry and business experience has allowed me to develop the essential communication and management skills necessary to articulate a value proposition in business terms and consistently deliver above expectations.

You will find that I focus on delivery and results, ensuring project issues and risks are identified, managed, and delivered within agreed times and costs. As a valued consultant I have been consistently appointed to highly visible projects in recognition of my ability to move from situation to situation and thrive; focus on the bottom-line, deliverables and cost savings; and deliver optimal business solutions.
