Maria Carlton's Summary

Business Transformation is about working on ALL parts of your business - from Business Values, Marketing, Strategy, Recruitment, Management and Staff Training, and pulling it ALL together.

This is made a lot easier when you have great resources, well thought out training for your staff, and an expert coach who understands small - medium business. I've helped trades industry people, service professionals, and most of all, retail organisations to move firmly ahead and own their markets since 2001.

My working life started in retail, and has included owning and operating my own retail specialty store in the late 1980's, then moving into the advertising, promotional and marketing industry. I trained extensively as a business coach with prestigious Coach U, and then used my collective skills to establish a solid reputation as a business coach specializing in helping non-fiction authors to publish, promote and distribute their books internationally.

In 2011 I returned to my first love of business coaching - SMEs and in particular, retailers, service providers, and specialists. I'm now focused on not only the marketing but all aspects of how a company can develop and grow. I'm a business transformation coach and consultant - which means I focus on the holistic approach - how the business values, marketing, recruitment, planning, people, sales, productivity etc - all work together to grow a strong company built on great foundations.

I've worked with award winning companies from the software, pharmacy, nutrition, security, professional services, and many other industries - becoming well known for my straightforward and common sense approach to business, profitablity, marketing and branding.

SPECIALTIES: Values, Social Media, Advertising, Brand Communication, Retail Sales and Marketing, Business to Consumer (B2C) Best Practice, Business Coach for SME's, And I'm a global expert on the worlds of Publishing and Professional Speaking for Non-Fiction Specialists.
