Esra Dillon BMIPRA (esradillon@gmail.com)'s Summary

My name is Esra Dillon and I am CEO of a PR agency, working with a range of clients in the consumer arena. I have provided case studies and contributions for many journalists and am keen to keep in contact with you and hope we can work together!

This is what we do...

E02 PR is the premier public relations company in London for facilitating business between the UK and Turkey. We are organising a VIP press and trade events. Working very close with Metro & Evening Standard Newspapers.

Focusing on daily media, we produce work that can hold its own alongside campaigns produced by agencies many times our size and we can offer these services to clients as a fully-integrated, retained agency or on a project-by-project basis as required.

Our mission is to work with your business to thoughtfully and creatively develop and implement plans for your business in TR or the UK- protecting your financial investment and maximizing your financial potential. Our commitment to the quality of our services and products is second to none and will ensure a strong return on your investment.

British companies are increasingly recognising the huge opportunities that exist to do business in Turkey. Similarly, Turkish companies recognise the benefit of establishing a presence in the UK, and E02 PR can assist in making this a smoother passage in either direction.

We are uniquely positioned to help form links between these two markets and our network of relationships in both countries can add real value to your business.
Turkey has growing economic ties with Europe and the US and Turkey’s public relations services are becoming more sophisticated and gaining in importance. Companies are seeking professional assistance for the advertising, product placement, and media exposure which

E02 PR can provide. All of which can be done through translation, and can be tailored to meet individual requirements that might vary by industry sector or intended audience.

In summary… The opportunity is there. You “CAN” do business in Turkey & United Kingdom...
