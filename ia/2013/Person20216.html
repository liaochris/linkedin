Carly Finseth's Summary

Carly is a doctoral student in Texas Tech University's Technical Communication and Rhetoric (TCR) program. Her research interests include teaching writing, online education, gaming studies, knowledge creation through play, usability, and open source technologies and culture.

Specialties: Multimodal pedagogy; online education; gaming studies, open source culture and technologies; rhetoric; instructional design and curriculum development; usability; assessment; information design; technical writing and editing; digital publishing and publications management; website design; content management system (CMS) set-up, maintenance, education, documentation, and tutorials.
