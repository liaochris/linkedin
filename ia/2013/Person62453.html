Jeremy Pion-Berlin's Experience

Director and Motion Picture Editor

Channel Fix Production

March 2011 – August 2011 (6 months)

Channel Fix Productions is a start up production company building longer form content for an online launch. I was creating numerous pilots for possible series, that will stand as a channel. Documentaries are the main focus as of right now and I was able to complete 13 in my time working there. For example one project I produced, "Archie the Cockfighter" is about an English man coming to the Philippines and becoming a professional cock fighter. Some of the other pieces that I have made include topics such as: whale sharks, mexican food tasting, skateboard competition, cheap traveling, man becomes a gigilo, drug traffic, jeepney driver, and more. Stay tuned for the official launch of the Channel Fix website in October.

Motion Picture Editor

Big Foot Studios

January 2010 – March 2011 (1 year 3 months)

I was working for Bigfoot Studios stationed in Cebu, Philippines. Mostly working in post-production, editing reality television shows for the Fashion One network. Some of the titles were "Underwater Action," "Model Workout," "Model Yoga," and "Underwater Xperts." I also did the online/offline edit and co-directed a show for the local channel RCTV here in the Philippines entitled "Extreme Cebu." On top of that I have cut promotional videos, trailers, teasers, and sizzles reels for the shows and behind the scenes at the company. I did the online/offline edit on a commercial for OneModelPlace.com. I was also a part time camera operator for behind the scenes and "Underwater Action."

Post-Production Intern

September 2009 – December 2009 (4 months)

As an intern at American Zoetrope I did script coverage, ran errands, researched shows, created mood boards, and was a PA on set. I also edited their 40th Anniversary Video which included behind the scenes footage from past films like: THX 1138, Godfather, Lost in Translation, Apocalypse Now, The Outsiders, Marie Antoinette and CQ. During my time I worked in collaboration with The Directors Bureau which is run by Roman Coppola.
