Cindy Ratzlaff's Summary

Cindy is the founder of Brand New Brand You, Inc., a brand marketing and communications consulting firm specializing in results driven social engagement solutions for authors, publishers, entrepreneurs, Fortune 1000 companies and online businesses. Forbes named her one of the Top Women Entrepreneurs to Follow on Twitter and Ad Age named her to the Ad Age Marketing 50 list. Cindy specializes cross platform marketing campaigns and consumer facing brand messaging.

She writes about social media tools, techniques and strategies for BusinessInsider.com and speaks about brand marketing through social media at major industry conferences, universities and consumer events. She has a following of more than 100,000 friends, fans, followers and subscribers across her own social networks.

Cindy is also a branding and marketing consultant with DSXgroup, a boutique management consulting firm that specializes in providing direct sales organizations, technology solution providers and business service companies with cost-effective consulting services and innovative solutions imperative to increasing revenue, conversion and new customer acquisition. DSXgroup partners with executive management to create, evolve and implement strategies designed for accelerated growth.

Specialties

Brand marketing, social media integrated strategies, cross platform marketing campaigns, marketing solutions for entrepreneurs and small business, corporate brand and marketing, speaking and social media training
