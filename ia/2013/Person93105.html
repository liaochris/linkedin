Michael Travis' Summary

I began as an art director, creative director, writer, designer, and photographer in advertising in NYC for 15 years and 5 years in Boston. This tremendous marketing background has helped me jump start my career in Real estate. I became a top producer in my first year and every year since with 8 million in sales in 2007. My main web site, www.waterfrontagent.com, is a culmination of all of my marketing, and real estate experience -- it now brings in most of my business with clients coming from as far away as France and Alaska.

I also took a Dale Carnegie course, and then became an instructor for a short stint in Boston. This has helped in every aspect of my life.

This is key to success--it is not a rehearsal for another life as my Uncle Jim always said--so I am trying to live it to the fullest.

I love what I do. Getting paid to do it makes it very satisfying.

Specialties: Waterfront specialist, New Homes, Commercial, Land, Multi units, currently renovated a condo in Portsmouth.
