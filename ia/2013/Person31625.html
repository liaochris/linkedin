David McGrath's Summary

I am an experienced legal technology professional working as an Executive Level Analyst within ASIC's evidence services consulting team.

I advise investigation teams on the effective and robust management of evidence across the investigation and litigation lifecycle. I actively manage investigator expectations and evidence services adherence to our Evidence Processing Methodologies. I also design innovative solutions to particular team review and document management requirements.

A major focus is the processing, management and production of electronic documents and metadata in Nuix and Ringtail. In that role, I work closely with forensics, capture and the document control unit teams to ensure that evidence is processed efficiently yet robustly - maintaining a proper chain of evidence and traceability at all times, in accordance with ASIC's obligations.

I am often a lead analyst on major evidence projects within ASIC. I also actively contribute to the development and improvement of ASIC's standards and practices in electronic document processing and Ringtail management.

I have a passion for technology and take every opportunity to improve my knowledge and skills. I am fortunate in being able to work closely with our forensics team and have recently added some ad hoc development to my range of activities.

ASIC supports my development by sponsoring a programming course. This both enhances my technology knowledge and skills. In recent times, I have developed tools to assist our group with import, report generation and PDF analysis tasks.

Prior to ASIC, I worked both as a legal technology professional and as a litigation lawyer. I hold degrees in Information Science and Law and post graduate qualifications in technology management. I have written a number of ediscovery articles and spoken at ediscovery conferences.
