AU Ambassadors, Prospective Student Outreach Chair

American University

Educational Institution; 1001-5000 employees; Higher Education industry

January 2008 – May 2010 (2 years 5 months)

My primary responsibility was coordinating online chats for prospective students and their parents. These chats are informal settings where visitors can ask questions about American University. Chats usually involved at least 200 participants over three chat rooms. I was responsible for ambassador participation in the chats and making sure visitors get their questions answered. During 2009-2010 have seen an increase in visitors, averaging to 300 people per online chat.

In 2009 I introduced a "guest chatter" program to have representatives from student organizations on campus visit chat rooms and answer questions about their programs. The program has become very successful and includes representatives from the student government, fraternities, sports teams, and social organizations.
