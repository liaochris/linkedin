Bon Crowder's Summary

Bon Crowder has taught math to over 15,000 adults in living rooms, classrooms and conferences. She publishes www.MathFour.com to help parents confidently and creatively support their children in math. As a speaker and author of Guided Creativity for the Busy Thinker, she's appeared on Great Day Houston, CreateChatter.tv, and Women in Business Radio and has hosted numerous webinars and emceed large conferences.

Specialties

math speaking, math coaching, teaching adults, facilitating seminars, innovation, developing course curriculum, evaluating learning, participant driven learning
