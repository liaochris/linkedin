Joel Fisher's Summary

Joel brings 18 years of experience as an interactive Creative Director, a user experience designer, and a front-end developer. He has developed many successful, award-winning campaigns, web sites, and web-based products.

Joel possesses a unique understanding of the creative, technical and business disciplines and how they are best woven together to achieve a desired goal, and the natural ability to effectively communicate complex solutions and ideas.

Prior to joining Verve, Joel was Director of User Experience at AOL/Advertising.com and the head of interactive at various advertising agencies.

Joel graduated with a BFA from the University of Delaware.

Specialties: Mobile Advertising, Mobile Web, Mobile Apps, Online Advertising, Creative Direction, Design, Product Development, Management, Development, Graphic Design, Front-end development, Information Architecture, Web Design, Strategy, Branding, Production, Project Management
