Lorena Paglia's Summary

For the past 12 years, I have been helping companies increase growth, through identifying opportunities and their competitive advantages. I do this by understanding the markets they operate in to advise on the best strategic response.

I have been fortunate to work in commercial environments across Europe and Asia-Pacific, delivering complex projects and solutions spanning multiple countries; by engaging and influencing key stakeholders, negotiating outcomes, and having the ability to clearly communicate and manage any resulting change.

Through my experiences I have been privileged to work with people from many cultures, industries and various corporate levels, developing my ability to relate to people and build strong relationships.

I have worked within the industries of oil & gas, mining, logistics, engineering and legal, enabling me with the knowledge of complex business models, complementing this with an appreciation for regulation and governance.

I am an experienced board director, business developer and strategic marketer. As a consultant, I incorporate my skills of change management, integrated strategy, scenario planning and sustainability management, to provide business advice. I hold a Masters of Business, a Graduate Certificate in Business Leadership and a Bachelor of Commerce in Marketing and Public Relations.

My experiences enable me to collaborate with companies and develop integrated strategies from a broad perspective, applying lateral thinking to complex projects. This allows for the exploring of possibilities whilst implementing far-reaching outcomes.

Specialties: Business Development and Marketing, Change Management, Facilitation, Integrated Business Strategies, International Environments, Process Design and Improvement, Scenario Planning, Strategic Communication, Sustainability/CSR
