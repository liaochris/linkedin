Frank Revi, PMP's Summary

Project manager with 10+ years of successful PM experience in web/software development using agile/scrum and traditional methodologies, using the full basket of tools. Recently I've added agency work to my skill set. I work with the range of stakeholders from client principals to creatives to in-house and outsourced programmers to vendors, partners, and office pets.

I enjoy project definition, contracts/SOWs, and planning as well as the rest of PM activities. I've got excellent written, verbal, and visual communication skills including presentations. I've managed projects budgets over $300K; managed and developed content for a federal grant proposal; and presented to potential investors.

I like working in a cooperative, creative culture with strong teams; and mentoring young talent.
