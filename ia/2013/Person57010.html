Jaimie VanSickle's Experience

Director of Operations

Book Yourself Solid

March 2012 – Present (1 year 7 months) Michigan

Jaimie’s expertise is in making sense of huge amounts of administrative data and putting processes and systems in place for the smooth operating and running of businesses (both big and small) and now heads up The Michael Port & Associates operations team.

In addition to an uncommon knack for answering the question “what needs to be done to make this happen?”, Jaimie’s expertise in almost every aspect of small business technology solutions is a massive asset to businesses operating on a virtual infrastructure.

Jaimie founded and ran a widely respected and very successful operations management firm that catered to up to twenty businesses simultaneously, including some of the most well-known individuals in the online marketing and digital publishing industry.

Today, Jaimie and her team devote themselves exclusively to The Book Yourself Solid School of Coach Training and The Alliance with Michael Mentoring Program.
