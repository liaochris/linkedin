Kelly Hendricks' Summary

Kelly Hendricks

A Full Scope Interior Designer with a unique custom flair for Kitchens and Baths for Remodeling as well as New Construction.

What sets her apart:
Kelly has a construction background and is extremely knowledgible on all aspects of building and design. With this helpful knowledge she is able inform her clients of what can and can not be done on a project and how to get the most bang for the buck.

Kelly creates custom designed spaces for each client's specific needs, wants, complimenting their lifestyle in everyway. She Enriches peoples lives by surrounding them with an awesome warm, inviting and eager to come home to "WOW" feeling! Changing your Ordinary space into something Extraordinary!

She truly enjoys what she does, but an even great joy is seeing how ecstatic the home owners are when the whole job is completed and they have exactly what they were looking for.

Providing Quality, Reliable, High End work from beginning to end. Large or small Gourmet Galleys & Loos can handle it all...
