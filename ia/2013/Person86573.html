Mark Falzon's Summary

Mark Falzon specialises in accelerated growth for SME's.

He is a serial entrepreneur with more than 30 years business management experience in a diversity of industries, including film and television, advertising and marketing, event management, retail fashion, hospitality, finance, human resources and property.
