John Rutkowski's Summary

I have successfully launched, operated and managed three businesses so far in my business career.

I have the knack for seeing the flaw in a pile of perfection. But then again, it's not perfect.

BOLDER Designs looks at the tangle of technology available to businesses and then picks the thread between what a company needs and the technology that is available to do it.

We design the solution rather than throwing hardware and software at the problems.

Our goal is to not have our customers call us with a problem. We want to be able to tell them that we saw the problem coming and fixed it before it was a problem.

My personal goal is to live where I can see the change of the light through the sky during the day while still enjoying the intellectual challenges of solving the technology issues of businesses. i.e. a mountain top with a fiber connection to the outside world.
