Mark Smalley's Summary

Clients engage Mark as trusted adviser and catalyst to discover where they are and to visualize where they want to be. Particularly in the fields of Application Management and Business Information Management. He achieves this with presentations, awareness workshops, strategy sessions, quick scans and assessments, policy documents, vision statements, RACI workshops and process improvement projects.

A background in application management and service management gives Mark a solid foundation as an independent IT Management Consultant. His experience also includes line management with people responsibilities, product development and marketing of IT services.

He is also ambassador-in-chief at the not-for-profit ASL BiSL Foundation and is contracted by the APM Group for ASL/BiSL examination-related work.

Other roles include:
- part of the Quantum Age of IT movement
- member of the ITSM Index Panel

Mark is a regular speaker at international conferences, where he has reached out to thousands of IT professionals.
