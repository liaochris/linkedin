Laurence Willis' Summary

2000 - Successfully converted a company's product inventory to a ColdFusion database for use on the web.

2002 - Edited my first professional music video.

2003 - Managed all aspects of a company's in-house print production department.

2004 - Created a sales presentation in Flash (instead of powerpoint) giving the company the edge it needed to win the account from it's competitors.

2005 - Created a product reporting system in PHP, that helped streamline the catalog production process and reduced the amount of errors making it to the press.

2006 - Began working at Mintz & Hoke one of the largest advertising agencies in Connecticut.

2006 - Won a Bellringer award for Flash work done on the Connect-Ability website.

2007 - Was put in charge of all production, conversion and deployment of online video and streaming media.

2008 - Created first Flash Game. A virtual "grab bag" that was used for company's holiday card. The game incorporated interactive video, audio, dynamic animation, and database driven content.

2008 - Created my first Interactive Display using Augmented Reality Technology. The mobile enclosure housed the computer running the software, a projector and the webcam.

2009 - Created an ActionScript 3 framework that can be used by Flash designers with no experience in Flex or OOP. Classes are encapsulated in compiled clips eliminating the need for external actionscript class files being needed at compile time.

2010 - After years of working in film, I entered into the Digital Photography realm focusing on portraiture.

2011 - Began several creative programming projects using dataFlow languages (PureData, VVVV, Max/MSP).

2012 - I'm building a real-time graphics and video capturing system where
Graphics, video and text can be added and manipulated in real-time during capture. The project's primary goal is to decrease the time it takes to edit and add graphics in video post-production.

Specialties

Photo Retouching, Video Editing, Skinning Blogs and CMS systems, Collages, People Photography, Camerawork, Audio Production, DataFlow programming.
