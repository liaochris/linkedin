Ellen Fador's Summary

"Reinventing Homes – for the Discerning Eye"

Spectacular-Spaces is a full service design consultancy that is committed to helping people maximize the appearance and appeal of their homes. Our services are multi faceted.

I assist homeowners, real estate agents and developers who are preparing properties for sale with the goal to help these clients achieve a fast sale at the best price. I have an extensive inventory of furnishings and accessories available for lease. Staging both occupied and vacant homes, 95% of the properties I Stage have an offer within the first 30 days of being on the market.

For clients who are not selling, but simply want to improve their living space, I offer a range of affordable home design services: color consultation, interior design, space planning, move in service, holiday and special event decorating, and personal shopping.

Specialties

Home Staging, Vacant Home Staging, Model Home Staging, Interior Design, Space Planning, Color Consultation, Holiday and Special Event Decorating, Furnishing Rental

Ellen Fador's Experience

Accredited Staging Professional, Certified Interior Designer and Space Planner, Certified Color Consultant

Through a process called Home Staging, I systematically assess properties and makes changes that highlight their unique selling features. I make changes to ensure that those features are highlighted in a way that appeals to the broadest base of likely buyers.

I creatively ‘set the stage’ for potential home buyers to visualize themselves in the seller's home. By doing so, I help property owners and real estate agents to achieve faster property sales at higher prices.
