Eric McGill's Summary

I'm a managing director of an investment firm focused on exploiting behavioral biases with solutions from other fields (such as systems engineering, ops theory, and supply chain). We currently have three major products:

1. A long/short equity fund based on compensating for forecasting errors analysts make when valuing securities. It trades quarterly, covers the S&P 500, and has a ~3-year track record outperforming the market and hedge fund indices.

2. An analytics tool that takes customer-supplier relationship data and enables network analytics similar to what Google uses to search the web. The result is a set of data that is alpha-generating on short to medium time scales through new ways to form groups / indices and momentum strategies, respectively.

3. Shorter-term behavioral strategies designed to work on a daily or intraday basis.

Our company will grow stably over time, adding diversifying strategies and funds as we break new ground in behavioral finance with the right combination of directors, advisors, analysts, and insights. Please drop me a line if you're interested in learning more.

Thanks,
Eric
