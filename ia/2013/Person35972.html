David Hahn's Summary

I helped lead LevelTen Interactive, a Dallas-area digital consultancy and leading Drupal CMS web development, Internet marketing and social media management agency.

I help lead the Dallas Drupal users group and often speak on Drupal topics. I am one of the organizers of DrupalCamp Dallas 2009-2013 and OpenCamp Dallas. I teach Drupal courses at Collin College and serve on the advisory board of Censault, LLC, a mobile startup.

I have built and led local and multi-national software and hardware development teams, and testing and operational groups for startups to Fortune 100 companies. My teams have shipped literally tens of millions of complex software and hardware products.

I have a BS in Computer Science from Texas State University and an MSE in Engineering Management from The University of Texas at Austin.
