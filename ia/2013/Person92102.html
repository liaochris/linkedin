Michael Binko's Summary

Binko serves as Pres/CEO and Board member of kloudtrack®. kloudtrack® develops SaaS|Cloud workflow, compliance and risk mitigation tech and solutions for a variety of industries. kloudtrack® is the company's suite of cloud app. modules for workflow/BPM, data management and risk mitigation. Industries include fin serv, health/med, pharma, gov./edu., legal and others.

Binko is also founder and serves as Man. Gen. Partner of Gemini Venture Partners LLP. Gemini provides seed-stage funding, interim CxO/exec. mgmt. teams and Board expertise.

Gemini also assists portfolio companies with corp structure, financials, IP/patents, R&D, and sales/marketing maintaining relationships with leading angels, VCs, and PE investors. Gemini works with universities and research labs to assist in tech transfer and monetizing IP, know-how and methodologies to commercial sectors.


* Prior to founding Gemini, Binko was VP, corp/bus dev for XRiver Tech (formerly Xybernaut NASDAQ: XYBR).

* Prior to XRiver, Binko co-founded LaunchFuel, Inc. - a venture accelerator firm working with tech entrepreneurs, and early-stage companies and angel/venture capital investors.

* Prior to LaunchFuel, Binko directed corporate ventures and bus dev for PSINet (NASDAQ: PSIX).

* Binko earned two degrees (with honors) from James Madison Univ (VA) and is a graduate of the Innovation Management Program offered jointly by the University of Maryland's Robert H. Smith School of Business and the A. James Clark School of Engineering.

* Binko a Business Evaluator with the Advanced Technology Program at NIST and has instructed in the Corporate Ventures program with George Washington Univ. (DC and VA campuses). Binko hosts The UpStart Show a television program focused on entrepreneurship and venture building in the innovation economy. An accomplished public speaker he is an accredited practitioner in PRSA -- designated by APR -- and a Board Member of SIIA.
