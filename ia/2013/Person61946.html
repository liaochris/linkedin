Jennifer (Mattingly) Overhaus' Summary

Jennifer Overhaus is the author of "Juggling the Big 3 for Lawyers", which is a career-building plan for aspiring partners to develop their personal brand, client business, and leadership mindset. She has also developed a comprehensive training program based upon the principles of the book.

Jennifer is American born and initially qualified and practised law in the US before moving to London in 1996 and qualifying in the UK. Since then, she has worked in UK magic circle and international American firms and most recently was a partner in Pillsbury Winthrop Shaw Pittman.

Having trodden the path of associate to partner and then managing young lawyers on their similar journey, Jennifer became increasingly conscious of the lack of awareness many lawyers have about what will bring them long-term success.

In short, legal talent and superior intelligence, even when combined with intense ambition and dedication, no longer guarantee success as a private practice lawyer. Today’s competitive market requires that advancing lawyers leverage skills in excess of their technical expertise.

Jennifer's book addresses these crucial issues and provides practical advice drawn from her own experiences as a successful lawyer and her observations as a mentor.
