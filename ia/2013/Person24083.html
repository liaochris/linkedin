Christina Diaz's Summary

Christina Diaz is Director of Guest Services at The Inn at Honey Run in Ohio's Amish country, a harmony of elegant dining, serene naturescapes, and pure bliss. Her primary focus is the meeting and event planning strategic teams at the inn, and she strives for both cutting edge amenities and new levels of quality and service for the inn's leisure and corporate guests.

Her philosophy in event planning is to incorporate her clients' company cultures and brand techniques into the design of the event. Whether it's a team building retreat with philanthropic stations throughout a venue to inspire leaders to work together and give back to their communities, or awe-inspiring logo ice sculptures to brand every experience at an incentive gala, her customization approach creates memorable meetings and events with clear messages.
