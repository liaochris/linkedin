Lee Munson's Summary

Not simply an Albuquerque Financial Advisor - Founder and Chief Investment Officer with extensive experience in creating active conservative investment portfolios using risk budgeting versus outdated asset allocation pie charts. T.V., radio, and print media expert financial commentator with regular appearances on CNBC's Closing Bell and The Kudlow Report, frequent quotes in the Wall Street Journal, Money, Kiplinger Personal Finance. Watch for his original published articles on CNNMoney.com.

Specialties: Managing risk, not pie charts.
