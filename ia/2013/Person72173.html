Karen Newcombe's Summary

Client tell me I'm great to work with because: "you're really nice and you listen to what we need - it's such a relief to work with someone nice", and "you do more than we asked you to do - you fact-checked our proposal for us!", and "you 'got it' right away and then saw things in our data we had missed, so we ended up with a stronger message".

I work primarily with professional service firms, institutions, and corporations. I’m different from most writers you’ve met because I come from inside that world. Unlike many advertising-oriented writers, I understand the complex challenges you face in:

• Creating the marketplace presence that positions you for selection during the RFP process
• Expressing your brand and corporate narrative in support of your business and marketing strategies
• Using expert knowledge and thought leadership to influence ideas, policies or entire industries
• Explaining your complex, technical and expensive products or services so your clients can clearly understand the benefits they'll receive and make an informed buying decision

For over 20 years I’ve been creating thought-leader content, doing research, writing proposals, creating brand strategies and designing marketing plans. I know exactly how content works in each of these situations, and the important role narrative structure plays in making this content accessible to your audience.

Busy executives have found me to be invaluable in translating their ideas and knowledge into useful content, both for current positioning and for future generations in the firm.
