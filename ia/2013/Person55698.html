Paul Cohen's Summary

I have a Masters degree in Computer Science from LTH (Lund, Sweden) and I have been working professionally with software development since 1992. Healthcare and information systems are my main areas of expertise. I like stuff like Eiffel, Python, Linux, REST, noSQL, Free Software and the KISS principle. I have contributed to open-source projects and have managed a few smaller ones.

My goal is always to deliver concrete results that enable projects and businesses to move forward and that actually make a difference for the people and businesses that use the results of my work. I believe that the purpose of all software development is to construct software that is actually used, that works well and is easy to maintain and develop and finally also generates a good ROI.

I think all good software is designed in harmony with the The Unix philosophy as expressed by Eric S. Raymond. More concisely put, I think that "There are two ways of constructing a software design. One way is to make it so simple that there are obviously no deficiencies. And the other way is to make it so complicated that there are no obvious deficiencies" (C.A.R Hoare). I prefer the former way.

Specialties

All aspects of software development of business and information systems. Leadership of development teams. Project management, Technical Lead, Analysis, Design & Software Architecture, Modelling, Test and Programming. Communcation, presentation and documentation.
