Kelly Moore's Summary

I am a breast cancer survivor (cancer free since 2002) & founder of a nonprofit organization (founding Treasured Chests in 2005). Together w amazing dedicated volunteers, we put on the most incredible unique Treasured Chests Events to raise more awareness about the prevention of breast cancer.

Check out www.TreasuredChests.org for more details, pics & video trailers of past successful events including runway shows, sporting events, golf tournaments, concerts, motorcycle runs....

Treasured Chests has affordable FUN events for all ages where we make a difference in MN by supporting fighters, celebrating with survivors, and honoring those who have lost their fight to the disease.

Treasured Chests awards include:
