Janice Masters' Summary

Highly Intuitive & Practical Spiritual Life Coach and Business Mentor

I have been helping people to live happier, more successful lives for over thirty years, first as a psychotherapist and for the last dozen years as a coach.

The women I coach are looking for more JOY (less suffering) and every one of them is saying, in some way: I'M NOT DONE YET! Which means that they're ready to bring more of their innate Divine Feminine, Empowered Self forth into their lives and into the world!

My mission is to lead them to heal the old stories that keep them stuck in life and in business, and teach them how to create a new story that invites true Joy and Success based in authentic living that expresses who they are in very satisfying ways.

I help Spiritual-preneurs and World Servers help more people and make more money by birthing their business, then marketing it Soul To Soul.

I support those on a spiritual path who are dealing with life challenges in the areas of relationship, health, finances, family, etc to find the gifts in The Stress and use them to transform & shift emotionally, spiritually into their best selves in their best lives.

Authenticity, spiritual optimism, resiliance and empowerment through choice are the foundations of my work. Using intuitive, visionary gifts and practical solutions I address the whole person and their needs and goals.

Offering group and private one to one coaching and mentoring.
