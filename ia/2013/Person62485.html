Jeremy Snook's Summary

I have been in the video games industry since 2001 and have worked on console, PC, and smartphone games for core, casual and social gamers, across both digital and retail distribution. I have publishing and licensing experience across business development, production, and marketing roles at both developers and publishers. During my time at RealNetworks / GameHouse and Microsoft, I had responsibilities for operational and strategic development of maturing, multi-million dollar businesses.

I earned my undergrad and graduate degrees at the University of Washington. My masters program was a fantastic "white space" opportunity to experiment and explore while I was working at RealNetworks. The presentations here are some of the term projects that I worked on.

Specialties: International business development, strategic planning, partner relations, licensing & development contract negotiation, game development & publishing, global content acquisition, team building & management, marketing & brand management, industry presentations, community engagement, social media tools.
