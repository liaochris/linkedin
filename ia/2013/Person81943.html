Liz McLean Knight's Summary

For the past few years I've run multiple websites and businesses related to electronic music and the "chic geek" lifestyle. Besides being a musician and DJ, I have a Chicago-based innovative electronic music label and love to properly showcase artists who produce quality work. I've done this by creating events hosted by multiple venues in the city to give touring and local artists a venue in which to perform. Along with working with booking agents, I've designed public special events that encouraged community building in electronic music niche genres and technical interests, and hosted private events to encourage the same.

My latest interests are sustainability, New Urbanism, urban planning, and Permaculture (it will make sense in the long run--I swear).

Specialties: creating a surprising and delightful client interaction, creating targeted niche events, branding and identity, marketing, public relations & communications, specialized & personalized user experience, product design, electronic music & culture advocacy, non-traditional marketing, start-up analysis, business plan creation, commercial real estate acquisition and doing a lot of things at once under different titles.
