Melanie Paul's Summary

I work with businesses in North Carolina through a membership dues based organization advertising and promoting local business, providing networking and educational opportunities to help businesses grow and become sustainable. I bring in a hands-on approach with members helping members and working with businesses one-on-one finding out where they currently are and where they want to be and then creating the bridge towards their goals while providing support, guidance and accountability.
