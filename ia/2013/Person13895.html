Beverly Sastri's Summary

As a marketing project manager I fast-track urgent and/or under resourced projects including credentials presentations, website redesign & search engine optimization, direct mail, and business systems.

I worked in CPG brand management for 13 years (Procter & Gamble, Warner-Lambert) and have a talent for capturing a product or service's unique selling points and translating them into successful branding, advertising, and promotions. I also have a strong track record of leading or participating on multi-functional teams.

In 2003 I founded Power Source Now, LLC and became a marketing consultant and personal development coach. The personal development training focused on a behavior shared by successful individuals, executives, and companies: living and working from core personal or business truths. Working with C-level executives and business owners operationalized this concept into a business protocol.

My Claim to Fame
As a Senior Brand Manager at Warner-Lambert (Pfizer) I led the development and launch of Schick Silk Effects Razor for Women, which achieved $35 million in year one sales and became the #1 complimented Warner-Lambert brand.
