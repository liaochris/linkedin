Ken Bowen's Summary

I have worked in the game industry for eight years as a Game Designer and Lead Designer before coming to the University of Connecticut to develop the brand new Game Design track in the Digital Media and Design department. My responsibilities included directing game projects from start to finish, programming unique scenarios using game development tools, and working with publishers such as SEGA, Electronic Arts, and Activision. I have worked with Blockbuster franchises such as Iron Man, Spider-Man, Captain America, Madagascar, Shrek, and over a dozen others. My work in the video game industry has received Nickelodeon’s Kids Choice Award for best video game, Indie Game of the Year, and Innovation Awards from multiple sources. I work with students on creating engaging gameplay experiences and pushing the boundaries of play.
