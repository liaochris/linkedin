Frank Ruotolo's Summary

Senior manager and business leader in mobile CAD development and design across a broad spectrum of startups, midsize and multinational companies. I've been in leadership positions in companies across all stages of the business cycle and contributed to startup, growth, M&A and post-M&A transitions.

Five years ago, I had a vision for mobile CAD and started my own business. We were "first" in many ways -- first to market with a 3D visualization, and first and only to market with full support for Catia, UG and other CAD formats on a mobile device. We claim some amazing successes with Eaton, Boeing, Toyota, Honda, and others.

My Vision -- CAD on a mobile device is still being defined. What we do know is that mobile CAD is much more than CAD on a small screen. We know that there is greater application serving the mobile needs of the CAD consumer rather than the CAD producer. We must push beyond the needs of CAD consumers to take advantage of the complete device. Mobile devices come with all kinds of sensor technologies, beyond anything found in a PC, that are opening the doors to new solutions everyday such as the Augmented Reality custom engineering projects we've created. Our vueCAD app is the ideal platform for mobile software solutions from design to manufacturing and assembly to quality control. Add the mobile device's gyroscopes, accelerometers, and camera, and vueCAD is extended into Augmented Reality, for example, with great success.

Combining my engineering and mobility expertise, I am uniquely positioned to strengthen current mobile solutions through integration of new business channels, business and product development and driving the evolution of mobile solutions into 3D applications.

Frank Ruotolo's Patents

  • System and Method for Extending a Mobile Device to Control, Connect and Communicate with Equipment, Networks and Systems

    • United States Patent 8,244,235
    • Issued August 14, 2012
    Inventors: Frank Ruotolo, Filip Peters

    The functionality of a mobile device is extended to connect to and communicate with a piece of equipment, data storage peripherals and secure networks. Specifically, a mobile device is extended in capability and functionality through the integration with a portable mobile device extender to facilitate seamless connection and communication with a piece of equipment, data storage peripherals and/or secure networks. Another aspect of the disclosure is to effectively enhance real world imagery by merging, on a mobile device, images and video from the real world with and projections from laser or video from a projection device, controlled by the mobile device.

  • Method for Augmenting Reality by Controlling Equipment with a Mobile Device

    • United States Patent Application PCT/US2011/000662
    • Filed April 11, 2011
    Inventors: Frank Ruotolo, Filip Peters
