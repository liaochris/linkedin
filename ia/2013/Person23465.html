Chris Lambrou's Summary

I consider myself a software architect, a customer service activist, an IT mongrel, an accomplished musician (in my own mind), a baseball stat enthusiast and a real world daddy of two (the most challenging task of all).

I'm currently the Director of Special Projects/R&D @ MRED which has been my home for 13 years.
I focus on internal/external projects that better benefit the users of our valuable service while constantly scouting for areas to innovate or re-energize.

I started out in IT support in a fortune 500 environment. Learning work practices, values and ethics from strong role models. It turned out to be a life lesson that I continue to practice/preach today.

I've become fascinated with real estate technology and the powerful role it plays in the lives of many different people via many different platforms. I've also stayed true to my learning of the values in "Customer Loyalty" and the benefits it may posses to organizations/industries as a whole.

I consider each day another learning opportunity one that is always better to aim high and end up short than accept underachieving in the first place.

I always seem to work with great people in great environments. Both of these happen to be secret ingredients to a fulfilling and successful occupation.
