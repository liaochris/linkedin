Erik Hummel's Summary

My current goal is to enable a culture of performance and quality throughout my engineering organization. I am driving this in a variety of approaches. First I am ensuring production site and service statistics are visible to the engineering teams to allow them to see the ebb and flows of the code they develop on a live site. We are using DynaTrace and in-house products to get this visible on screens around the development floors. Second I am helping to scope and build a long term load and performance testing platform. This will include not just the framework to build a load testing environment but also the automation to make its use frictionless. While that long term vision is still solidifying I am moving forward in our load testing with the tools and environments as they currently stand.

I also have taken a focus on ensuring quality and the engineering organizations "definition of done". This includes code coverage and quality by further pushing our Sonar installation to make it visible the projects that have high quality and those that need help getting there.

Tying this together I have started a path towards a Continuous Deployment pipeline using Bamboo, Puppet, Geb/Spock & SauceLabs. This will ensure we push and test code automated through the lifecycle to minimize manual touch points. The goals is to converge these different ideals into an engineering process that is much quicker to deliver change to the business and end users.
