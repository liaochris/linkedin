Larry and I worked at an Engineering Company in Boston, Mass.
We lived in Las Vegas, Nevada where Larry worked for the telephone company.
After living in Las Vegas for 6 1/2 years, we moved to Northern Virginia. Can you believe this, I developed allergies to the desert! For Larry it was returning home and for me it was moving to a state that I have simply fallen in love with.

We have been involved in the IT industry. We had a woman-owned computer consulting business certified as woman-owned minority business for 25+ years.
Our clients included: small home offices, small to medium sized companies, departments in DC Government, Fairfax County Government, Aids project for Howard University and fortune 500 companies.

We enjoyed providing training and consulting to our clients.
Near the end of 2003, we decided to make a change in our careers. We thought why not use our desire to consult and give information out as realtors®. We are having a great time in our career change!

During the Real Estate Market downturn, we realized that there were folks who owned homes where they owned more than the home was worth. We wanted to be able to help homeowners in this tragic from under the load, we therefore became certified as Short sale specialists. CDPE - Certified Distress Property Experts. It is such a great feeling to see families sell their home and owe nothing and then move into a rental home.

Specialties: Short sale specialists
