Melissa Lawrenz's Summary

I'm an editor. I work in short forms, long forms, episodics or whatever you need. I'm quick, creative, and an easy person to work with you. Is that a stupid thing to say? Not when you want your editor to try things, change things, and work with you to create the best product possible. Have you worked with someone who refuses to make that change? I'm not that type of person. I'll try anything to see if it will make the film/show better. I love a challenge and new opportunities. Most of all, I love editing.

Specialties: AVID, FCP, Compressor, DVD Studio Pro, Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects. Steenbeck, KEM- can sync film dailies. Can handle SD & HD workflows, including RED footage & Canon 5D/7D. Broadcast standards. Multi-camera grouping, syncing, Excellent troubleshooting skills. Mac & PC proficient. MS Office Suite.
