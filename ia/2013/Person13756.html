Beth Slocum's Summary

Transplanted from the corporate design world of NYC, Beth Slocum has worked with some of the leading fashion, publishing and branding companies in the world, both luxury and consumer, such as Martha Stewart, Victoria's Secret, Bath and Body Works, Kimberly Clark, Harry Abrams, among others. In 2009, Beth launched Woodhaus Studio, a full service boutique design firm catering to both large and small companies, specializing in print, branding, marketing collateral, web, packaging and publishing design.

She currently is creative director and managing brands for popular singer/songwriter Brian Mackey, retired long time PR agent turned fashion designer, Jody Miller with her company, Couture by Sophie, TV/Fashion Personality, Veronica Scott with her fashion collection, Vee by Veronica Scott, the John F. Peto Studio Museum and many others. Please visit the website for a full list of clients. www.woodhausstudio.com

As well as a designer, Beth is also a fine artist and illustrator, having most recently illustrated and designed the award winning book, "Joyful Cooking: In the Pursuit of Good Health" by author and Nutritional Consultant, Joy Feldman. She also illustrated and designed the children's book also by Joy Feldman, "Is Your Hair Made of Donuts?", most recently featured in the Providence Journal, Channel 10 NBC, and MSNBC nationwide.

You can also purchase her hand-crafted items from the Woodhaus Studio Etsy shop.

http://www.etsy.com/shop/woodhausstudio
