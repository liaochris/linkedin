Glen Waisner's Summary

GLEN R. WAISNER, MBA, PE, is a business leader and advisor with a passion for helping the men and women who run successful small and mid-sized businesses achieve superior results. He is known as “The Growth Guru” for his unwavering focus on creating profitable, sustainable growth for clients on four continents.

Mr. Waisner also speaks to industry and corporate groups throughout the U.S., is a Professional Member of the Institute of Management Consultants USA and was featured in Fortune Small Business Magazine (11/04).

Specialties

• Execution — driving the achievement of superior results.

• Focus — targeting what creates value and reduces costs.

• Leadership — an accomplished motivator, team-builder, and persuasive communicator.

• Management — finding, hiring and training people with the highest potential and then actualizing that potential.

• Strategic Insight — effectively assessing competitive forces in light of marketplace realities and steering a course to success.
