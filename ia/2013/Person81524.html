Lisa Goldsmith's Summary

I began the first two years of my film career working in development at Monarch Pictures/MGM Studios, where I was responsible for packaging writers and directors for a number of feature films; from prep to principle photography. The following four years was spent working mainly in production on a variety of TV shows and features. It was shortly thereafter when my artistic journey truly began, from the influence I had in design, film, fashion and art, which quickly led me to work in the Set Decorating Department in the entertainment industry. For the past 20 years I've been working as a Set Decorating Buyer on features and television, a Set Decorator on Commercials, and Prop Styling photo shoots. I'm passionate about the conceptual work I'm involved in, and constructing various images. I love being able to tell a story in a picture, or on a set that shows great details, and is thought provoking. In addition to Set Decorating, for the past 5 years I have also been working as a Voice Over Actor. Apart from the current voice over jobs I do, I'd very much like to have more V.O opportunities where I'm able to reach out to people by speaking about important topics/current issues, that are shaping and changing today's society.

Specialties: Member of SDSA. IASTE Local 44/52. BAFTA, Environmental and Animal Activist.
Voice Over Agents: Abrams, Paradigm, STARS Agency, Houghton Talent, GoVoices.
