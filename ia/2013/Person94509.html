Mike Quinn, Jr.'s Summary

Mike Quinn, Jr. is a Film and Video Editor with over seven years of industry experience with Final Cut Studio products, as well as, Adobe Production Premium Creative Suite applications (After Effects, Illustrator, Media Composer, Photoshop, etc.) and other often used third-party software packages.

He has worked closely with Directors, Writers and Producers within Independent Film and Commercial Advertising to help complete deadline oriented projects.

“My mission is to do good work in both the quality of the work I do and the work I take on."

Specialties: Commercial Advertising, Documentaries, Music Videos, Story Editing, Quality Control and Technical Specification Compliance.
