Dan Predpall, P.E.'s Summary

Dan is Founder and President of Successful Consultant Training, an e-learning firm with the purpose of providing professional development for existing consultants, and training and coaching for individuals who see opportiunities in consulting or freelancing.

Learn more at www.successfulconsultanttraining.com

We offer training in subjects such as DESIGNING YOUR SERVICE OFFERING, MARKETING YOUR SERVICES, CLIENT SERVICE PRINCIPLES, PROBLEM SOLVING, CONSULTANT'S SUCCESS MINDSET.
