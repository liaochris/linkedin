Doyle Buehler's Summary

Doyle Buehler is a writer, entrepreneur, speaker and online business strategist. He is at the intersections of Digital Innovation and Entrepreneurship.

From several successful start-ups and retail franchise and licensed businesses in Canada, the United States, Europe and Australia, to helping other companies succeed with their ideas and strategies, he has spent over 10 years in the business world making things happen. He is a Leading innovator in the online, ecommerce worlds. He has been living in Australia for several years.

His experience has been utilised in various Fortune 500 global industries, including travel, mobile, insurance, pharmaceuticals, banking, investment, hospitality, finance, events and learning domains.

While his ongoing passion is business, his background is Aerospace Engineering, complementing this with award winning aviation photography, as well as a pilot. He also received his MBA in 2002 from Royal Roads University.

He holds a number of US Patents for consumer devices and business methodology. One of his earlier start-up companies was named the Top 50 Fastest Growing Company in Canada, and the Fastest Growing Company in the State. He has also been nominated for one of Canada's National Innovation Awards.

His newest book, "The Digital Delusion", discusses the reality of the online industry, and what business leaders can do to get beyond the clutter and distraction of the online world. The time is now to displace the current thinking of the online business, and provide a clear sense of direction for those businesses who really need to succeed online. It's about working on your business in the online world and building a solid digital platform that works through the clutter, confusion and overwhelming. In his unique 7 Step online strategy, he combines all of the disconnected aspects of social media, paid search, SEO, etc. into a simple and easy to implement solution for all businesses to find their mojo online.
