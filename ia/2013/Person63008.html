Jesse Zryb's Summary

I’m a multi-disciplinary designer living in Lower Manhattan with a background in architecture and fine arts, having received my Masters of Architecture from Tulane University (’08). I have a tremendous passion for events and live music which led to my research and design for my graduate thesis (Immersive Design: A Festival Experience) as well as my current position as a designer for Pink. I also like to explore art through other mediums such as painting and photography and contribute to articles and photos as a senior writer to the music website Ourvinyl.com.

Work samples from all of my endeavors as well as additional contact information can be found at jessezryb.com

Jesse Zryb's Publications

  • Immersive Design: A Festival Experience

    • LAP LAMBERT Academic Publishing
    • August 9, 2010
    Authors: Jesse Zryb

    There is a unique sense of exhilaration that surrounds any live event - theatrical, musical or sporting. Live events evoke a feeling of spontaneity - anything can and might just happen. This book investigates ways in which temporary systems and experiential design can enhance the atmosphere of a music festival by establishing a more cohesive setting for this increasingly popular live event. Through the research of several relevant cases a set of guidelines and techniques are given that can be adjusted and applied to the varying site conditions of festivals and other live events.
