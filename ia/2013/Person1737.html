AdCommunal is a performance-based advertising agency that hosts U.S. and International online ad campaigns.

We operate on a CPA (cost per action) basis, which means that advertisers only pay when a lead is generated.

Gone are the days when advertisers have to rely on blind media buys with no measurable ROI. With AdCommunal’s performance based CPA approach, advertisers are able to measure their return on investment with each lead generated.

AdCommunal also provides the means for publishers of websites, developers of social media applications, as well as affiliate marketers to achieve a higher effective CPM by driving traffic through targeted ad placements on a CPA basis.

We realize that the success of our network can only be measured by the success of our advertisers and affiliates. We use seasoned experience, proprietary technology, as well as advanced tracking & testing to produce the maximum possible results for our advertisers and affiliates.
