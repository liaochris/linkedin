Lindsey Pollak's Summary

Lindsey Pollak is a bestselling author, keynote speaker and recognized expert on the Millennial Generation (a.k.a. Generation Y).

She serves as an official Ambassador for LinkedIn, Spokesperson for The Hartford's "My Tomorrow" campaign and is the bestselling author of "Getting from College to Career: Your Essential Guide to Succeeding in the Real World."   Lindsey's next book, to be published by HarperCollins in fall 2014, will focus on Millennial leadership.

Her clients include over 150 corporations, conferences and universities around the world, including Barclays, Citi, ESPN, GE, Lazard, Levi Strauss & Co., PwC, Ralph Lauren, Time Inc., UBS, Yale, Harvard, Princeton, and MIT.   Her areas of expertise include marketing to Millennials, managing Millennials, managing generational differences, personal branding and career development.

Lindsey's advice and opinions have been featured in such media outlets as The Today Show, The New York Times, The Wall Street Journal, Glamour, CNN and NPR. She appears on multiple "Best People to Follow on Twitter" lists, including Mashable’s list of top non-fiction authors on Twitter. Forbes named her website, www.lindseypollak.com, one of the Top 100 Websites for Your Career.

In her charitable work, Lindsey serves as Chair of the Board of She's the First, a non-profit organization that sponsors girls’ education in developing world countries so that they can be first in their families to graduate from high school.

Lindsey is a graduate of Yale University and received a Rotary Ambassadorial Scholarship to Monash University in Melbourne, Australia, where she completed a master’s degree in women’s studies.   She is based in New York City.


(Alternate spellings: Lindsey Pollack, Lindsay Pollak, Lindsay Pollack, Lindsey Pollock, Lindsay Pollock)
