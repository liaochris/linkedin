Coastal Reconstruction Group's Summary

Our goal is to manage your catastrophe, so you can manage your property.

Natural and manmade disasters strike with abandon. They can affect anyone anywhere and at anytime. Whether it’s a hurricane, a water main burst or an electrical fire, the aftermath of these disasters can be devastating. Not only to buildings and physical structures, but also to property
owners, tenants and residents who must regroup, rebuild and resume their lives.

Thankfully, there is a calm after the storm. Coastal Reconstruction Group — a general contractor with decades of experience in catastrophe management — can mitigate fire, wind and water damage of any type, getting you back in business as soon as possible. At Coastal, we won’t just restore your property; we will restore your trust.

Specialties

Multi-family/Habitational, Senior Living, Hospitality, Commercial/Retail Build Out, Industrial/Warehousing, Residential, Historic Renovations

Coastal Reconstruction helps clients plan ahead before disaster strikes. Our popular Rapid Response Program gives building owners, property managers and residents "priority status" — guaranteeing that we'll arrive within four hours of an emergency call. Once on site, we take immediate action to protect property from the elements, prevent further damage and initiate the first steps toward reconstruction. Coastal's Rapid Response Program not only saves time and money... it also delivers peace of mind. And, the best news is: There's no cost to you. Coastal is compensated through the amount settled with the insurance company, so you incur no out-of-pocket expenses.
