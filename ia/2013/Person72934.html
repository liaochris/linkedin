Kathy Bisbee's Summary

Frequently referred to as a positive "agent of change" by colleagues, I diplomatically strive to lead by example, building a leadership team that is invested in a shared, collaborative vision and productive work plan. 

I am a proactive, hands-on, and partnership-oriented executive director adapting strategically to a rapidly-changing industry of community-based media and technology. I support a culture of high performance and self-reflection, inspiring our team and clients to reach beyond what we think is possible.

I have over twenty years of professional experience producing quality video productions for web, film, and television, managing organizational budgets, coaching and mentoring diverse staff, Americorps members, volunteers, and interns, working with boards and capital assets, implementing strategic marketing communications and public relations plans.

I energetically manage teams, products, organizations and marketing campaigns with strategic, resourceful expertise, with an ebullient, flexible personality that encourages creativity, teamwork, levelheadedness, and professionalism. 

I develop and implement new revenue generation, strategic planning, and partnership opportunities for all organizations I serve, as a professional staffer and as a volunteer board member.

I have passion for innovative technology and media tools that create economies of scale, inspire digital storytelling, and generate opportunities for authentic interconnectedness.

I work well in environments that promote community and individual empowerment through media and technology literacy, and support independent media collaborations that can frame and launch local, national and international policy work.

I am inspired by community-based economic development and action.

I am quite fond of following the AP Style Book, and of teams who have a quirky, playful sense of humor while working effectively under pressure.

Kathy Bisbee's Experience

Executive Director/CEO

CMAP TV and CTV

March 2008 – Present (5 years 7 months) Gilroy, CA

In the past five years as CMAP's Executive Director I have managed our staff and board to implement a strategic plan that moved the organization from a cable access television station to a self-sustaining community media media center. This transition required relationship-building with key stakeholders, anticipating future technical, personnel, budgetary, product/services planning, partnership needs, and creating a new model locally for community television.

On July 1st, 2013, I also became the Executive Director of CTV, Community Television of Santa Cruz County, managing their operations to consolidate resources, create personnel and technology efficiencies, and develop new revenue streams to provide long-term sustainability for CTV.

With full board and staff investment, I led our team to launch many projects such as negotiating and building the new facilities and television/recording studio partnership at Christopher High School in Gilroy, generating new revenue through fee-based services, contract services with other media centers, government agencies and non-profit organizations, developing a deeper staff and board bench through facilitated activities, coaching and performance-based project management. Under my leadership, our team developed award-winning video productions, curriculum, classes, and youth media programs that have been recognized regionally and nationally for their innovation.

I have also personally taught youth media programs, produced and hosted quarterly and elections-based television programs, helped members to produce their own television shows, written newsletters, grants and annual reports, implemented a successful fundraising plan, negotiated contracts with cable companies, municipalities, school districts and businesses, built community partnerships, managed our web and social networking presence, reported monthly to a 15-member board of directors and committees, and written press releases and created new marketing collateral.

Independent Filmmaker: Producer/Director

Bisbee Films

January 2005 – Present (8 years 9 months) Northern California

After producing, directing and editing award-winning short and medium length format documentaries and commercial online marketing video productions, I've recently finished a documentary about youth in Cuba, Nicaragua, Guatemala and the US who are using music to create meaningful personal and social change in their communities, "Don't Cost Nothin' to Dream."

The 38-minute film recently won "Best Documentary-Political" at the AOF Festival in Los Angeles, CA and was runner-up for "Best Editing- Documentary," and was nominated in the "Best Political Statement in a Film" category.
