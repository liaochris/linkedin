Kat Friedrich's Summary

I am inspired by writing, sustainability and technology. Now, I produce articles and blog posts about energy and environmental topics for businesses, news sources, colleges and nonprofits.

There are two parts to my work:

☛ Writing for energy-related businesses and nonprofits

☛ Producing journalism about green jobs, energy, sustainable design, innovation, and other topics

My graduate program focused on environmental journalism. I also have a college degree in mechanical engineering.

Skills: journalism, newsletter production, web editing, blogging, content curation, social media, graphic layout, podcasting, web content strategy, video editing, technical writing

My blog, EcoJournalist, won an award from WordPress last year. My work has appeared in Scientific American, On Wisconsin, Earth Island Journal, Climate Access, The New Farm, Real Energy Writers, WBUR's blog, On Campus with Women, and many other publications.
