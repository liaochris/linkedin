Bryan Urban's Summary

Bryan Urban is passionate about energy efficiency and sustainability. Bryan manages an outdoor facility for testing thermal performance of building assemblies, analyzing building performance, and testing innovative energy-related technologies. Bryan has analyzed energy use of building envelopes, cool roofs, thermostats, home energy management systems, human behavior, and consumer electronics.

Urban holds a two degrees in Mechanical Engineering, a BA from Cornell University and MS from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. At MIT he specialized in building energy simulation and developed a web-based tool for analyzing energy performance of early-stage building designs.

As a volunteer Urban is a co-inventor and director of STG International, a social benefit organization that aims to provide rural schools and health clinics with electricity, heating, and cooling with affordable solar technology.

Bryan is interested in working with innovative energy-related startups for R&D and consulting.

Specialties

Energy efficiency, thermal performance of building envelopes, heat flux measurement, thermostats, energy and behavior, CSP, rural electrification, project management, business development
