Debbie Roes' Summary

I help people clean out their closets, discover what looks best on them, and hone their personal and professional style.

* Define the colors and styles which best suit your personality and lifestyle
* Dress your unique body to its best potential
* Tame your closet
* Create amazing outfits which you love and can’t wait to wear
* Dress to impress for dating, networking, work, job interviews, and all life occasions
* Shop more wisely – buy only what looks great on you and projects your best image
* Look younger, thinner, and more vibrant

It is possible to love your clothes and feel joy instead of heaviness when you open your closet. Getting dressed every day can be both easy and fun!

MY SERVICES INCLUDE:

CLOSET AUDIT - This session takes place in your closet and is generally the first step in the process. We go through all of the items in your wardrobe one by one. We release the items which no longer suit your body or lifestyle (or maybe never did!), discover hidden treasures, and organize your closet such that getting dressed will be fun and easy moving forward.

SHOP YOUR CLOSET - This is a fun and exciting session which focuses on unleashing the possibilities in your wardrobe! The “Shop Your Closet” session can occur following the “Closet Audit” or “Personal Shopping” or as a standalone service. What we do in this session is create new outfit combinations using the items you already own. You’ll be surprised at how many wonderful outfits can be created using the building blocks that are present in a small or medium-sized wardrobe.

PERSONAL SHOPPING - With this service, we go shopping together, either for the wardrobe staples and enhancer pieces identified during the “Closet Audit” or for outfits for special occasions such as dates, holiday parties, job interviews, or vacations. We shop based upon your unique needs and defined budget. I always endeavor to make shopping fun and educational for my clients, as well as a productive experience.

Specialties

Wardrobe Styling, Image Consulting, Closet Audits, Professional Organizing, Personal Shopping, Fashion Styling, Style Definition, Outfit Creation, Professional Coaching, Counseling
