Kristian Koren's Summary

With a background in graphics and multimedia development. I became freelance in April 2010 after 8 years as Senior Graphic Artist at Learning Curve Education one of Scotland’s leading producers of digital media for schools and education. My responsibilities involve designing the front end look and feel of products and branding. More recently I have seen a shift towards motion graphics creating 2D animated movies aimed at schools promoting a variety of subjects such as Personal Safety, e-Safety and Learning Skills.
I am a dedicated and highly creative worker with good interpersonal and communication skills. I work well independently or as part of a team and I am also very adaptable. I am always looking for fresh challenges.

Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Flash, XHTML, CSS, Accessibility, Usability, Search engine optimisation, ToonBoom, Premiere, DVDLab Pro, Joomla, Wordpress
