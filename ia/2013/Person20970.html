Catherine Johnson's Summary

Catherine Johnson is a speaker, Certified Clinical Thermographer (CCT) and the owner of Silk Thermal Imaging. Prior to starting her medical thermal imaging practice in July of 2009, Catherine spent 18 years in bio-tech research. The latter 8 years were spent with Pfizer as a freelance medical writer in their Pharmacokinetic, Dynamics and Metabolism department. In mid-2009, she left the pharmaceutical industry to join the growing movement of holistic medicine. Seeing firsthand the greed and over use of pharmaceutical drugs in our society made the transition an easy one. As a consumer of thermography, she was invited by her practitioner to help educate women on the benefits of thermographic screening particularly for the early detection and prevention of breast cancer. After spending the better part of 2009 researching the breast cancer “industry” she became a CCT and launched Silk Thermal Imaging. Catherine has dedicated her career to educating women on vital information that is not being told to us by our doctors and to shift away from the fear mongering that has encompassed the breast cancer industry. She does this through corporate wellness seminars, webinars, and private and public seminars. To date, Catherine has presented to hundreds of companies around San Diego County reaching thousands of women, teaching them how to be proactive about prevention rather than waiting to detect disease.
Catherine has partnered with numerous MDs, DOs, DCs, naturopaths, and clinical nutritionists around San Diego County to ensure that each patient’s health concerns are dealt with appropriately. Her main focus is on prevention of disease rather than treatments and she works closely with doctors who share this philosophy.
“We’ve been racing for a cure for over 20 years. Billions of dollars have been raised in the name of a cure; however, today we are no closer to a cure for breast cancer than we were 20 years ago. In fact, incidences continue to rise especially in younger women.”
