D.A. Frizell's Summary

After earning a degree in both fine art and commercial graphics. I have been working as an artist and illustrator in a variety of areas. Professional photographs, magazine publications, stage props for live entertainment, Music CD and DVD covers, Music Video Backgrounds, apparel, temporary tattoos, stickers and a wide range of marketing souvenirs. I am always expanding my techniques to extend my artistic range. I will continue to climb the ranks to recognition through both skill and perseverance.

Specialties

Photoshop, Illustrator, Graphite, Pen and Ink, Paint, and clay
