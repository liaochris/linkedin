Chandran Fernando's Summary

Matrix Search Group is a dynamic, full service talent search firm with over ten years experience fulfilling the recruitment needs of organizations involved in the real estate, construction, property management and financial services industries. We are an organization of, for and about people. We value relationships, integrity, trust and equality in all our business practices and operate with the highest level of professionalism. Our goal is to use honesty, trust, and respect to develop long-term relationships with our clients and candidates.

We are a boutique-style firm by choice with a personal touch and a commitment to excellence, integrity and transparency. Our unique recruitment style allows us to stay abreast of the ever-changing market conditions.

Since 2000, we have built a reputation of excellence in the talent acquisition field. We are proud of our personal rapport with our clients with an emphasis on client-centred solutions.

As a leading Canadian recruitment firm, we strive to achieve excellence for our clients through creative, innovative and pro-active solutions to uniquely suit their recruitment needs. We achieve this goal by operating our business in a socially and ethically responsible manner.

We are also focussed and driven to assist both recent graduates and established professionals seeking career advancement support in the Canadian labour market.

We are dedicated to nurturing your potential and will assist you in all aspects of achieving your career goals. Your success is our success.

"I encourage every person to continue to strive to achieve their dreams, as anything is possible provided that the true value of people is not forgotten and that respect and honesty are the key threads that build and maintain a community,” said Matrix Search Group President, Chandran Fernando, in the winter 2011 edition of AMÖI magazine.
