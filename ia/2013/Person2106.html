Assani "Ron" Duongsaa's Projects

  • Chess Battle video production

    • 2009 to Present
    Team Members: Assani "Ron" Duongsaa, Danny Brown, Ryan Paul, Robert K. Avery, Breden Whitney, Carolyn Tuzel

    Chess Battle is a 3-minute video production class project for Video Production II class at University of Utah. The video was a part of "Debonair Debut".

    Debonair Debut is a collection of thirteen 3-minute final video projects from the University of Utah Department of Communication's Video Production II class during Fall semester of 2009. The projects were completed in response to the class assignment to 'tell a story' in exactly three minutes using the resources of visual and auditory production. This program aired on KUEN Television, Channel 9 in Salt Lake City, Utah on December 16, 2009 and December 22, 2009.

    The team behind Debonair Debut is the Command Z Productions, under supervision of Professor Robert K. Avery.
    Crew Members are Spencer Atamanczyk, Danny Brown, Jeffrey Campbell, Brady Dunn, Assani "Ron" Duongsaa, Anthony Hind, Garrett Martin, Benjamin Mosier, Ryan Paul, Aaron Sanchez, Marcus Springer, Chris Wade, and Nathan Vranes.
    Each member is responsible for directing, producing, and editing his own project; while also being a cameraman and an assistant for the other two colleagues. Everyone also contributes to the Debonair Debut program itself; such as directing, camera operating, props and scenes design, editing, and teleprompting.
