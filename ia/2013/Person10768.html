ShaRon Kelsey's Summary

The Kelsey Law Firm has been assisting clients, in the Washington Metropolitan area, for more than 20 years. Areas of focus include bankruptcy, estate planning and probate, guardianship, adoption, family law and criminal matters. During that time, the principal attorney, Ms. Kelsey has taught numerous legal studies courses, at the University of Maryland University College, including real estate, ethics, legal research and writing, estates and trusts and bankruptcy. In fact, Ms. Kelsey assisted with the revision of UMUC'S on-line bankruptcy course after the bankruptcy laws changed in 2005. Most importantly, for the client, at the Kelsey Law Firm, our attorneys not only have had no grievance complaints filed against them but Ms. Kelsey actual serves as a peer review attorney for the State's Attorney Grievance Commission. So clients can be sure that they will receive legal services that follow the rules of professional conduct.

With respect to the client's personal needs, The Kelsey Law Firm is committed to serving the community by providing honest, fair, non-judgmental and respectful legal services. When client's come to the Kelsey Law Firm, their case will be carefully and patiently heard so that they receive the best legal course of action for their situation and not what would be best for the firms' bottom line. At the Kelsey Law Firm everyone will receive an honest assessment of their needs whether that means they become a client of ours or we offer them alternatives that lead them in another positive direction that also resolves the outstanding issue. At the Kelsey Law Firm, we want to help our clients as best we can, when we can and where we can.
