Disability Insurance For Doctors' Summary

We have been providing Disability Insurance to Physcians and All Healthcare Professionals since 1987. We are experts in the Disability Insurance market place and licensed in all 50 States. We Protect all medical specialties with true "Own-Occupation" disability insurance. All physcians and Health Care Professionals qualify for our Discounted Insurance program which vary from 15% to 30% depending on the state. We can provide Guaranteed Coverage to Groups of 20 or more Physcians.

Income protection is the cornerstone of all Financial Planning. We insure Physcians in their specialty specific from A+ rated insurance carriers. We represent Metlife, Principal Life,, Union Central Life, Standard Insurance Co., Ameritas Life, Mutual Of Omaha, Assurity Life, Ohio National and Lloyds of London.
