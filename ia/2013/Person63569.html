Joerg Hochwald's Summary

I'm a self employed Consultant. I work as Enterprise Engineer and Project Manager, most of the time in telecommunication and Airline environments.

I have long term experience as Consultant and Engineer in huge Mobile environments for international carriers and Service Providers. I had projects in the Service Platform area (e.g. IP-Services, MMSC and others) and Core Network Services (e.g. ngHLR, AAA and GGSN/SGSN). Do you, or your customers think about NGMN?

I help customers and partners to establish Mobile Devices as a corporate standard. Sounds easier as it is to use devices like the iPhone, Android, BlackBerry or the iPad in an Enterprise environment. Especially when it comes to management... A Mobile Device Management (MDM) is a must, but to find the right solution might be a challenge.

Do you use a Groupware and/or Mail Service? Sure you do! Have you ever thought about a migration? Then you know how difficult it is. Even the Release changes can be a challenge. And how about a migration from your on premise solution to a cloud service, or a mixed (hybrid) mode with users on premise and in the cloud...

See there are many good reasons to hire me! Just take another moment and read the recommendations for my foreign projects and you will see: My customers like to work with me.

Still not confident? Unconvinced? Contact me: joerg.hochwald@gmail.com
