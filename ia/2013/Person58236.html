Jane Regan's Summary

HBI specializes in Aging in Place and Green Design; Residential Decks, Kitchens and Bath Remodels. Seeking projects or professional employment in the Bay Area design industry.

Formerly, a Senior Director with diverse technical and business skills in Business Development, Systems Infrastructure and Product Development, particularly skilled at organizing, managing and motivating people. Jane is expert at small or large scale construction projects requiring project management and organization. For example, remodeled 55 baths at local hotel in Half Moon Bay, including materials purchase, design, installation, quality control and inspections.

Jane joined eco6design from May 2012-October 2012, as a designer in a downtown showroom in Half Moon Bay for eco friendly interior design products. Eco6design provided floor to ceiling green solutions for design clients interested in new kitchens and baths, home furnishings, flooring, tile, countertop materials, plumbing, feng shui consultations, paint mixed on site, and community education.

After 20 years as a leading Silicon Valley technologist, in 2002 Ms. Regan began studying Interior Design. Attended UC Santa Cruz and Canada College, and is now an NKBA Certified Interior Designer, specializing in Kitchens & Baths. Certified for Design for Aging in Place. Incorporated and own HB Building + Design Inc., since 1999, a design/build firm in Northern California on the San Mateo Coastside. Have built hundreds of beautiful bathrooms and kitchens from San Francisco to Los Angeles.

Specialties

Plan, design, materials selection and purchase for safe, efficient and beautiful new kitchens and baths. Effective public speaker, provider of education on home renovations, green product use, Certified in Design for Aging in Place.
Quick Books, 20/20, Visio, Constant Contact, Social Media, WordPress
