Shelley Cordes' Summary

I am a digital artist with a portfolio of over 2000 designs. I digitize original artwork and photographs for sewing machine embroidery and high quality prints. I design wearable art and teach sewers how to create their own designs using Bernina v.6 software. WIth my corporate sales, marketing, and advanced computer experience I have taught sewers and artists how to find their passion and translate those skills into successful and profitable careers. My passion for creativity allows me to successfully inspire others in our wonderful industry.
