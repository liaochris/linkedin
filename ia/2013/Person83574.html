Deek McWhackel's Summary

I have launched and worked on promoting many authentic luxury watch stores in the past few years. The main reason for this is two fold, firstly there are ludicrous numbers of pretend luxury watch stores online. The Luxury Watch Club network of boutiques was set up to display as many authentic luxury watch brand price comparisons as possible. The result is a very attractive network of high end watch boutiques. Each online store compares current prices for select watch brands from the top 10 internet watch retailers. These are authorised and trusted watch sellers of repute. The second reason is my love of the watchmakers craft, it has always fascinated me, I took a big interest in watch making and design when i was a kid, my grandfather gave me a very old swiss pocket watch from the 1920's as a gift, it wasn't a Cartier, but it did make me feel like I owned one!
