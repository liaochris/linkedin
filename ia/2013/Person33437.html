Debbi Stocco's Summary

With 16 years of experience in advertising art and print design, I have added book layout and cover design, and e-books (digital pdf) or e-pub conversion as my main areas of service. I work with individual authors, project coordinators, small businesses, churches, and non-profits. View these recent book projects on Amazon... Retiring But Not Shy; How to Open a Restaurant: Due Diligence; A Deal with the Devil; Thoughts for Tuesday; Off Trail: Lessons Learned from Unforeseen Breast Cancer Detours...

My favorite aspect of the work is working closely with my clients, sharing my expertise in order to minimize the fear of self publishing.

Specialties: book formatting and layout, book cover design, e-pub conversion for Kindle, Nook and iPad, graphic design, marketing materials.

Book Design for Authors, Self-Publishers

Stocco Book Design Services

I am a book designer for self publishers. Services include cover design and book layout for print and/or ebooks. This would be for fiction or non-fiction. Everyone has a book in them, and my area of expertise is to prepare the manuscript professionally for print, ebook, distribution and sales.

Coordinator of Publishing

Taos Institute Publications

2012 – Present (1 year)

Graphic and Web Designer

Relevant Design Solutions, LLC

July 2009 – December 2012 (3 years 6 months)

Working out of the Home Office with clients from anywhere in the country, via email, phone, fax, internet.

Debbi specializes in custom websites and graphic design for churches, non-profit organizations and small businesses. With personal service, skill and expertise, Debbi updates and brands the mission, goal or product design with relevant and exiting graphics. No church, non-profit or small business should be without professional graphics and a credible image. There is no benefit to being the "best kept secret".

Corporate Manager of Art and Advertising

Jerry's Enterprises

March 1997 – January 2009 (11 years 11 months)

Debbi Stocco created all of the graphic design pieces needed for the corporate offices of Jerry's Enterprises, better known as Jerry's Foods in Edina, MN. Anything from signage, to product branding, logos, the corporate newsletter, point of sale posters, collateral, marketing materials and advertising, managing the company website, Debbi was the go to person. The primary responsibility was producing the weekly 4 page color ad flyer for the company's Jerry's Foods Stores in Edina and Eden Prairie, which included graphic design layout and food photography to complete the ad images for the flyer which represented the unique products available only at Jerry's Foods.
The art and advertising department was eliminated in January 2009 for cost cutting measures in a slow economy.

Customer Service Representative

Jerry's Printing and Design Co.

October 1997 – March 1999 (1 year 6 months)

Worked with designers and others to complete printing bids and orders. Learned everything about color printing, layout, file types, bindery, everything to do with printing projects from business cards to 4 color process multi-page publications.
