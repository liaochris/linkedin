J. Todd Bennett's Summary

I am a multi-disciplined communicator, creative problem solver, strategist, leader and teacher. I take complex concepts and make them easy to understand. Whether I’m conducting research and developing strategies, simplifying complicated processes or clarifying messages, I work to build bridges between people at all levels of an organization to help lead them to a successful outcome.

An experienced higher education leader, my career has spanned a broad range of areas that includes enrollment management, market research, marketing communications, technology leadership, web development, retention/student services, academic services and residential life.

Specialties

Leadership, enrollment management, strategic planning, market research and testing, teaching/training, web usability, information architecture, content strategy, content modeling, copy writing, user experience design, web content management, change management, website governance.
