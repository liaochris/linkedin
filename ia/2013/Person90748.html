Cynthia Wallace's Summary

I work with leaders to develop the critical self-awareness and skills that it takes to be remembered for your personality and your message. The principles apply to every situation: major speeches, board meetings, one-on-one meetings.

Reflecting on my experience working for some of the world's most powerful television news organizations, I'm able to bring clients a real world understanding of the media and teach them techniques for getting their stories into print, television and social media.

As one client observes: "Cynthia is great at getting to the fundamental truth of the situation and then helping you turn that into a terrific story."

Clients include: a national policy analyst learning to expedite his viewpoints for TV news shows, physicians learning to facilitate a sponsored luncheon, a Fortune 500 Executive VP learning how to build rapport with employees, and business owners learning to leverage the press to enhance consumer awareness and confidence.
