Jamie Renee Williams' Summary

Jamie Renee Williams works as a director and producer in Los Angeles where she received her MFA from the California Institute of the Arts (School of Film/ Video). Her work has screened at festivals and venues including the REDCAT and the Palm Springs International ShortFest and has also been broadcast on PBS.

Specialties: Storytelling, Final Cut X/ Studio. Adobe Creative Suite. Editing. Budgeting. Script Breakdown / Scheduling. Screen-printing. Market Research, Poetry, Conversational Japanese.
