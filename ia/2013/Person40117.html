Chris Eigner's Summary

Design-aware engineer and entrepreneur with 5 years experience developing and leading web development for corporate clients and agencies. Soft spot in my heart for architectural design and backend optimization: search engines, queueing systems, load balancers, caching layers, and the use of non-relational datastores.

Experience building technical partnerships, growing development teams/environments/practices, and integrating with third party API's including Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Factual, Yelp, etc.

Have implemented full-text search using Amazon Cloudsearch, Sphinx, and Solr. Built and consulted on several mobile API's including Menuish and Taplister. Experience using Redis for caching, leaderboard tracking/generation, and as a simple analytics backend. Experience using Resque and Sidekick for queuing.

5 years building web-based software using Rails/Sinatra.

4 years working with relational databases - PostgreSQL, MySQL, Amazon RDS.

3 years DevOps (Amazon AWS, Heroku and Engine Yard).

Weaknesses: Java, C

Looking for excuses to play with: Riak, CouchDB
