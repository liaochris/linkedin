Megan Hayes-Golding's Summary

I currently teach physics and act as an in-house instructional technology consultant at The Westminster Schools in Atlanta. In addition, I founded and operate the Global Math Department, a professional development community for math teachers worldwide.

During my breaks from school, I'm available to speak to your teachers about using technology in the classroom.

I've worked in engineering and IT industries. My industry experience makes me a better teacher, I'm sure.
