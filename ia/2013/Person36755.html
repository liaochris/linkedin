Donna Goestenkors, CPMSM's Summary

My extensive healthcare experience (over 36 years) and ongoing project success qualifies me to be selected as your FIRST and PREFERRED choice for healthcare consulting and education services. I am skilled to quickly understand the needs of you and your team, assess the condition of practices and make recommendations and implement customized solutions that aligns your department performance with the goals of your organization.

Educating healthcare professionals in matters relating to Medical Staff Services continues to be my passion. Helping others perform at their greatest level of competence so they can be their very best is my mantra and my daily motivator.

Specialties

A warm and friendly healthcare professional who provides exemplary consulting and education services in all matters relating to the field of Medical Staff Services. These services are available in all core healthcare environments (Hospitals, Systems, Credentials Verification Organizations, Managed Care Organizations, Physician Groups, etc.) and in the following core functions: Credentialing, Privileging, Compliance, Perf. Improv., Information, Technology and Medical Staff Services Management.

Donna Goestenkors, CPMSM's Projects

  • Credentials Committee Redesign

    • October 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Donna Goestenkors, CPMSM, Mark LeQuire, M.D.

    Worked with Dr. LeQuire to create fresh committee vision, developed a strategic plan, provided ongoing education to the members regarding purpose, roles and scope of authority, designed contemporary agenda and automated the entire meeting management and review process.

  • Medical Executive Committee Redesign

    • February 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Donna Goestenkors, CPMSM, Donovan Kendrick, M.D.

    In the process of working with Dr. Kendrick to create a fresh committee vision. The members are in the process of developing a Medical Staff mission statement and will then move into building a strategic plan. In conjunction with this work, I'm designing a contemporary agenda and automating the meeting management function throughout all governance aspects of the Medical Staff. The ultimate goal is to facilitate ideas and support the initiatives of the Medical Staff and Administrative Leaders that will result in a collaborative approach to improve patient care and physician and practitioner participation.

  • CVO Assessment

    • March 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Donna Goestenkors, CPMSM

    Conducted an assessment of a health system's CVO to determine level of compliance, depth of automation, implementation of software applications, staff competence, efficiency of practices. An additional aspect of this project was to provide in depth education and training to MSP staff and guidance to select Medical Staff Leaders.

  • Systemize Medical Staff Services Department and Design System CVO

    • January 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Donna Goestenkors, CPMSM, System MSPs = 8

    This Client work involves a focus on designing, implementing and maintaining projects that relate specifically to systemizing practices, forms, policies and procedures, staff expectations and overall operations of System Medical Staff Services Departments. In addition, a parallel track of maintaining the current infrastructure of the Credentials Verification Organization is actively being done. In March 2013, I will be facilitating an MSP Retreat that will involve all System MSPs. The purpose of this Retreat is to define traditional and non-traditional roles, environments and match with current responsibilities of staff. The outcome will guide staff on service expansion opportunities, assessment of needed competencies to meet new demands and the development of a formal proposal to the Medical Staff and Administrative Leaders regarding how the MSO and CVO departments align with the organization's strategic plan.
