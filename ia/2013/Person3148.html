Alan Stratton's Summary

Please see my blog at http://www.CostMatters.com for Cost Management.

I also host http://www.PerformanceManagementEdge.com for a variety of Performance Management topics including Change Management, Planning & Budgeting, Performance Measures, Process Management, and more.

I am a recovering CPA, CMA. That means that I am serious about working with operating management to improve business operations and not simply enforcing financial policies.

I advocate Activity-Based Costing(ABC or ABCM) because it creates a framework to follow the trail of cause-and-effect to improve operating results.

I have implemented Activity-Based Costing with clients in multiple industries.

I also speak the language of systems as I have worked closely with computer systems my entire career and actually done significant programming when necessary as well as a significant amount of ETL (Extract, Transform, & Load) development in creating ABC models.

I love to find new ways to integrate business operations, financial operations, and systems.

Specialties

Integrating Financial, Software, and Accounting expertise with business operations; Activity-Based Cost Management (ABCM), Business Modeling; Group collaboration, ETL for ABCM Modeling, Performance Management
