James Anderson's Summary

Dr. Anderson is a highly respected and attentive podiatrist with experience helping patients with foot and ankle needs for over 14 years. He services patients at three locations in Massachusetts: Franklin, Dennis and Milford. With extensive training in many foot and ankle specialities, Dr. Anderson is dedicated to providing each patient with effective, individualized care.

Foot injuries: fractures, sprains, strains, lacerations

painful flatfeet
