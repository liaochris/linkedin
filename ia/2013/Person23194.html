As Lead Customer Care Technician at California Institute of the Arts I represent the primary public relations contact for the department of Information Technology. I am chiefly responsible for interacting with the public in a manner which both accentuates my own and my departments's technological expertise and reinforces a long-term positive assessment of our Department.

In my capacity as Lead Customer Care Technician it is a common occurrence to interact directly with highly placed members of our staff, faculty, and board of directors; requiring the utmost in proper deportment and discretion. My daily responsibilities include: desk-side interaction with clients; the interpreting of a lay-person's problem into clear, concise, and workable summary; ascertaining the specific requirements of a users' working environment; and determining what equipment would be best suited for their task. I am charged with providing technical support through phone, e-mail, and remote desktop applications on Windows and Mac OS X operating environments.

Every year since 2009 through June to September I spearhead a campus wide deployment of workstations to an array of departments: ensuring application compatibility, complete user migration, and on-the-fly resolution of unforeseen application complications. This distribution included Windows and Macintosh computers, covering both stationary and mobile configurations while implementing a diverse set of specific application and user requirements. During this time I am responsible for maintaining and promoting positive inter-departmental relations while identifying and prioritizing operational objectives.

Owner & Principal Photographer

Studio386 Photographers

August 2005 – Present (8 years 2 months) Santa Clarita, CA

When I created Studio386 Photographers my chief purpose was to make beautiful images of my friends. As I grew from my immediate circle and began meeting new clients I found that circle expanding.

I realized that as my client list grew so did my list of friends. Over these few years the through-line of my photography has become a simple statement: make beautiful images of and with my friends, especially with those I haven't yet met.

While my passion lies in a more journalistic approach to image making I am expanding and exploring new ways of creating my art. I am always eager to sit down with my clients and see what we can come up with.

Whether it be the quiet solace of a starry sky, the adrenaline rush of a concert, or the elegance of a bride slowly walking down the isle, our cameras are there. While I go where the client takes me, I am based in Santa Clarita, California and primarily serve the Los Angeles and Ventura Counties.
