Matias Puga's Experience

Co-Owner & CTO

Gruppit

Sole Proprietorship; 1-10 employees; Internet industry

January 2011 – Present (3 years) Barcelona Area, Spain

Gruppit is a transactional social network where users meet each other in real life via “dinner party” events.

Our aim is to connect people with similar interests using a dinner party format which encourages sociability between participants and eliminates the noise that characterizes other networking events.

The product was initially developed with the singles market in mind but we soon expanded to include other groups with common interests such as internet networking, business start-ups, e-commerce, photography, language exchange etc…

Gruppit’s social network builds as users get to know each other in real life and expands users’ network of friends exponentially as more and more people take part.

The product consists of a user base semantically categorized according to location and interests and featuring the ability for users to hold their own events.

Co-Owner and CTO

Clan-2000

2005 – Present (8 years) Barcelona Area, Spain

Singles Social Networks in Spain & Italy

In 2006 we created the first of our social networks aimed at the singles market in Barcelona. Our aim was to create a free communication tool for this increasingly large group of people.

Previously we managed a club for friends called Clan – 2000. The social composition of the club was mostly people who were either unmarried, without a partner or separated. Suddenly this group felt the need to break out of its social network and make new friends in their private lives.

(Something that Facebook could not provide and furthermore at that time Facebook only had 25 million users).

In our networks you can manage your friendships, use private messaging, manage your events, pictures, photos, videos and your personal blog.

Today we have more than 25 social networks for singles in Spain, 5 networks in Italy and via the Mind The Clan consultancy we offer consultancy services on social networks (products, integration and measurement).

Founder & CTO

Patagonline

2003 – Present (10 years)

Patagonline is an online travel agency specializing in Patagonia.
Our aim is to be an information bridge between this remote region and Europe.

Watch our video: “How Patagonline works”
http://www.patagonline.com/servicios

The product is internationally positioned in the markets of Spain, Italy and Germany, using the classic travel sales model, commission on the volume of services handled.

This project had to be rescued in its early stages, we founded it in 1999 but it has held on in spite of the crisis and the economic convulsions that have shaken Argentina and Spain in recent years.

Using technology we have designed a system (Trip Planner) which enables you to put together your own trip with an online booking system capable of reaching providers in remote areas such as Bahia Bustamante where they have a satellite internet connection (as long as it’s not too windy ;-).
