Jeremy Verdusco's Summary

I rage against inaccuracy, grammatical and spelling errors, and lack of style in copy of all sorts. I currently edit National Driller magazine, a member of the BNP Media group. I also have worked at the The Detroit News, St. Petersburg Times and the Ocala Star-Banner. In addition to copy editing, I've worked in page design, technical writing, reporting and public relations. Portfolio at http://bit.ly/Igwv3o.
