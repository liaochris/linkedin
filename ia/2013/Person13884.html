Beverly Holt Guth's Summary

30 years of experience teaching private lessons to students of all ages, judging piano competitions and festivals, teaching master classes and teacher workshops.

Specialties

I specialize in the more "serious" student, and teach across the spectrum of music - history, technique, music theory and sightreading skills.

Beverly Holt Guth's Experience

Pianist, Teacher

Guth Piano Studio

1979 – Present (34 years) Currently teach in Parker, Colorado.

Beverly Holt Guth, pianist, has won many awards as a pianist and teacher. A veteran of the stage, she has given recitals in numerous cities and played as soloist with the Dallas Symphony Orchestra, Baylor University Symphony and the Houston Baptist University Symphony.

She performs and accompanies classical music, with a growing specialty in Spanish classical music.

Students have won numerous prizes and scholarships. Her students range from early beginners - age 4 and up - to very advanced.

A more complete bio is available at her website, GuthPianoStudio.com or at PrivateLessons.com.
