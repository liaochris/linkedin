Ellen Zimmerman's Summary

JEWISH HOLIDAYS IN A BOX
Our goal is to inspire Jewish and interfaith families to celebrate Jewish holidays -- with more fun, less stress -- and in a way that's just right for them.

With our boxed kits, games, art activities, and e-guides for Hanukkah, Passover, and Shabbat, we give our customers the confidence and the tools to discover what works best for them.

"Simply Celebrating!" is our free weekly blog, bringing how-to's, tips and recipes right to subscribers' inboxes.

Our Facebook community shares ideas and concerns at http://facebook.com/JewishHolidaysInABox..

Press clips and testimonials at http://jewishholidaysinabox.com.


THE ZIMMERMAN GROUP
Our passion is creating B2B and consumer programs that help you meet your sales and marketing objectives -- because we firmly believe in the power of communication to change attitudes, minds, and behaviors.

We start by developing a strategic plan that will serve as a road map of how to move, step by step, toward your goals.

Then we implement the plan, choosing the right tools. Advertising. Trade shows. Public relations. Direct marketing. Internal communications. We look at the whole spectrum of what's possible.

Our tagline, "Brains for hire," grew out of this anecdote: A pharma client told us that a major marketing program we’d created for her had turned out to be very successful. In thanking us, she said, “I never could have done this without you. You could have shut me in a room for a year. I needed your brains."

Specialties: Strategic planning, advertising, public relations, internal communications.
Industries: pharmaceutical and CRO, medical/health care, manufacturing, tourism, education.
