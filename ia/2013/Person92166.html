Michael Carney's Summary

I am PASSIONATE about helping businesses succeed online.

To bring that dream to reality, I've created (and continue to create) a series of online training courses that teach businesses:

* How to use Facebook for Business

* Advanced Social Media Marketing: the latest news on Facebook, Google Plus, Twitter, Pinterest, Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn and much more

* How to master Selling Online

* How to use LinkedIn effectively

* The surprising but vital importance of Google Plus

* How to prepare an effective Social Media Brief

* How to REALLY Engage on Facebook

* Advanced Selling on NZ's leading online auction site, Trade Me


My courses are offered directly and in association with professional and industry associations.

For full course details, go to:
http://www.linkedin.com/company/netmarketing-courses

I also blog far too much and write occasional newsletters about whatever’s new, exciting or interesting in the world of communications.

Subscribe for free at:
http://netmarketingweek.com (international edition)
http://MarketingMonitor.co.nz (NZ edition)

What do I know about all these topics? I'm a highly experienced marketer. I've been in the marketing game since 1971 and online since 1987.

I can be variously described as a digital marketing trainer, adman, media director, strategist, researcher, copywriter, consultant, author and playwright.

I am the author of the book “Trade Me Success Secrets”; and am working on a new book, “How To Use Facebook For Business”.

I am a LinkedIn Open Networker :: LION. Please connect with me here: michael@netmarketingcourses.com

Specialties: online training courses, social media training, How to use Facebook for Business, How to Market Online, eCommerce courses, Trade Me courses, how to prepare an effective social media brief, online marketing, social media courses, Google Plus training, LinkedIn training, social media marketing, Facebook marketing, writing, emarketing, ecommerce, planning, strategic thinking
