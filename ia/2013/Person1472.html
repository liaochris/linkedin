Adam Reynolds' Summary

Adam Reynolds' position as Vice President sees him managing the theatrical movies division of Digital Media Management where he and his team develop and execute digital, viral and social media campaigns for a wide range of movie studio releases including The Hunger Games.

Adam and his experienced team specialize in growing and expanding the social reach of each film by developing unique one of a kind campaigns that drive awareness and build a community of super fans and brand ambassadors.

Adam's experience lies in digital and talent acquisition both in the United States and Australia, including previously held position of Director of Marketing for an online music portal, as well as positions in Account Management, Product and Project Management.
