Kris Cahill's Summary

I am a psychic reader, healer, teacher, speaker, writer, and artist, based in Los Angeles. Originally from Chicago, I moved to L.A. in 2006 with my husband, actor and filmmaker Noel Olken.

My goal is to validate the spirit and experience of each person, and also the awesome power each has when deciding to heal and grow that spirit. I am enthusiastic about letting others know that they can heal themselves and create their lives, simply by deciding to do so and then doing it.

I began my professional reading practice in 2000, after graduating from the clairvoyant training at InVision in Chicago. As a psychic teacher, I teach that it is possible to access one's own natural psychic abilities, and to use them in your life, every day. Besides teaching others that they can grow and heal, I have a deep commitment to my own spiritual growth.

I give readings to clients around the world. I also teach classes in meditation, healing, and energy awareness in southern California. I'm available as a speaker on a wide range of topics, including: being psychic, meditation, women’s energy, energy healing, grounding, and more.

I am fascinated with the human spirit’s ability to change, choose, and heal. Having seen many clients and students create miracles for themselves, simply by making different choices and caring for their own energy, I respect each one’s free will and power to heal. I'm more interested in giving a deeply honest and non-judgmental reading than in predicting any outcomes. Everyone has the ability and the power within themselves to heal.

I write articles about spiritual growth, being psychic, energy, creativity, energy healing, clairvoyance, meditation, freedom, choice, and growth, at my blog, Psychic Everyday, and also as the National Meditation and Clairvoyance Examiner for Examiner.com.

I graduated from the School of the Art Institute of Chicago with a BFA in 1982. To see my art, please visit my other website: KrisCahillArt.com.

Specialties

Clairvoyant abilities, clear communication skills, teaching talent, writing skills, amusement, happiness and joy, an eye for explosive color.
