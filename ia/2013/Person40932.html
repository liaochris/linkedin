Elly Parker's Summary

Mainly involved with the construction end of the semiconductor industry, Elly is equally at home when tramping the construction site as she is in meetings with senior execs from supplier companies.

Based on the Intel site in Leixlip since moving to Ireland in 2002, she has moved through several internal positions, growing and expanding her skill-set each time.

Elly is now the Information Manager for Construction on the Ireland Site, responsible for the development of a holistic approach to documentation movement and storage for current construction projects and sustaining works on site.

Specialties

Tendering, Information Security, Ergonomics, Virtual Teams, Project Management, Intellectual Property, Documentation Management and Movement.
