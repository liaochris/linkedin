  • Customer Experience Management

    • 2009 to Present
    Team Members: Janne Ohtonen

    Customer Centricity is still one of the most misunderstood areas in business today. About 55% of companies I surveyed have started a customer centricity initiative, but only about 7% are truly achieving anything like the full benefits. Without doubt, customer centricity sits as the main driver of change that will create more impact over the next few years. To solve this challenge, I can help you with:

    ♦ Developing of a customer centricity programme with significant ROI.
    ♦ Teach practical customer centric methods and approaches to empower your organisation.
    ♦ Customer strategy definition and implementation.
    ♦ Development of customer centric Key Performance Indicators (and KRIs).
    ♦ Customer centric product and service innovation.
    ♦ Customer experience management.
    ♦ Customer centric process innovation.
    ♦ Creating customer centric culture and leadership.

    Everybody understands that the revenues of our companies are dependent on our customers. So, why do companies still fail to place their customers at the centre of everything they do? Most will claim they view the customer as important and that they have mechanisms to understand and react to what they are telling you, but the focus on cost, efficiency and more sales tends to create conflicting priorities. Moreover, the mechanism to listen and react to customer views is customer centricity at the lowest level in the organisational maturity matrix.
