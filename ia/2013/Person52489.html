Heleen van der Helm's Experience

Presentation specialist

Coopertino | Professional Presentations (Self-employed)

Self-Employed; Myself Only; Graphic Design industry

August 2009 – Present (4 years 2 months) The Netherlands

In 2009, it was inevitable: I wanted to dedicate my professional life to presentations only. Coopertino is the result. I develop professional presentations for companies, organizations and entrepreneurs in PowerPoint, Prezi and Keynote.

- I develop new presentations
- I restyle existing presentations
- I design templates

- I can assist crafting your presentation story
- I can help structuring your presentation
- I can advice how to make a persuasive presentation

Senior Account Manager

KCND

September 2001 – August 2009 (8 years) The Netherlands, Leiden

KCND was a down to earth graphic design agency with a strong focus on developing functional designs. This meant that we did not think in terms of items but we constantly asked ourselves "what do we need to do in order to meet our customer's goals?" The design had to serve its purpose. We worked for all kinds and all sizes of customers. I was responsible for the key accounts. Besides being Senior Account Manager I also did concepting and yes... all the presentations.

Marketing

TelePlace

December 2000 – August 2001 (9 months) The Netherlands, Haarlem

Sometimes you get an offer you can't refuse. I sold Better Image at the peak of the internet bubble to start working for TelePlace. TelePlace offered 24x7 full service server space in its own data center in Haarlem. This American company had big ambitions and the possibilities were infinite. Unfortunately it was all too good to be true and after 9 months the investors pulled the plug. A very valuable lesson.

Designer

Better Image

September 1996 – December 2000 (4 years 4 months) The Netherlands, Noordwijk

After my stay in the US I was so excited about websites and the possibilities of internet that I decided to start my own company in 1996. We advised, designed and built websites for small and medium sized companies.

Designer

Tandem Computers BV

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; HPQ; Information Technology and Services industry

1991 – 1996 (5 years) The Netherlands, Hoofddorp

Tandem was a fantastic company with its roots in the US and known for its fault tolerant mainframe systems. Our customers were mainly banks, insurance companies and telcos. Tandem was taken over by Compaq and is currently still a brand of Hewlett Packard. I worked in Marketing for the Dutch office but also for the European head quarters. In 1995 I worked at the global head office in Cupertino (Sillicon Valley, California). I was very fortunate to work in the multimedia department as a PowerPoint presentation designer and they gave me the opportunity to learn developing websites.
