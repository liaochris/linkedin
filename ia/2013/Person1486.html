Adam Kerpelman's Summary

Hey, I'm Adam.

I'm a producer, project manager, problem solver, designer and tech nerd. I develop and implement multi-media, multi-platform content full cycle from concept through execution and release. I've designed and managed the development of websites, mobile apps, and media content from animated shorts to feature films tailoring each, when needed, to relevant social channels for release.

With Kerplinq I designed and managed the development of websites and apps for clients. As well we built out some of our own creations including iPhone app "Freeze" and "Therapylinq" a HIPPA compliant web-based communication and administration platform for therapists.

At One Step Productions we made music videos, promos, movies, web series and most of the associated marketing materials. Across projects I worked as producer but also stepped in with everything from writing and directing to camera and editing. Our 2009 web series "Rules of The League" was one of the first narrative projects ever to incorporate live-tweets synced to onscreen character Twitter activity. Our 2010 web Series "Project 420" is currently running on Koldcast.tv.

I've also completed a year of a master cabinet maker's apprenticeship, and I can teach you how to swim.

You can check out my portfolio and all that good stuff on my personal site.

Have a good day!
