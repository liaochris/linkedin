Gary Ryan's Summary

I am the Founding Director of Organisations That Matter, a boutique management consulting firm. I work with businesses, universities, leaders and talented people who want to move 'Beyond Being Good'.

A lot of people and organisations are good - but they aren't as good as they could be. This causes frustration and dissatisfaction with the gap between being 'good' and what you know you should be producing.

I help you and your team members to close this gap. Even universities engage me to work with their leadership programs to close this gap for their students.

With over 18 years executive management, facilitation, culture creation and professional development experience, I design & facilitate programs that enhance the development of you and your teams. I help you to identify and practice 'formulas for success' in all aspects of your life.

I enable you, your team and/or your organisation to be more clear about what you should be doing and how you should be doing it to be more successful. I make sure that what you 'know' and what you 'do' are the same thing. This leads to greater success in all areas of your life and an energy that positively infects people around you.

View an overview of my services I provide at http://orgsthatmatter.com.

My studies include a Bachelor of Education, a Graduate Diploma in Human Resource Management and a Master of Management from Monash University. I have also completed doctoral units on research methodolgies, once again at Monash University.

Finally I am the Author of the popular book 'What Really Matters For Young Professionals!' and creator of the Yes For Success Platform. Visit http://planforpersonalsuccess.com/trial to sample the program.
