Michael Beavers' Summary

As a digital marketing and experience strategist I serve three kinds of clients: agencies, product organizations, and enterprise marketing departments. More at www.michaelbeavers.com.

For digital consultancies and ad agencies I bring a software and application product management approach to business strategy and brand planning. I uncover the best experience ideas by collaborating with clients, user experience designers, visual designers, and engineers to create enterprise portals, social architectures, mobile apps, Web advertising campaigns, and Web services. My involvement means these things work well.

For companies in Silicon Valley, I pair a strong business and revenue sensibility with the best possible user experience for the product. I operate in the space between product management, product marketing, and design. I work with category leaders like Google, Intel, NetApp, Juniper Networks, Yahoo!, IBM, Seagate and Plantronics. I’ve created global behavioral persona segmentation, performed competitive analyses, participated in design reviews, supported PMM & strategic functions, and developed product- and user-oriented M&A evaluation processes. I’m handy to have around if you’re trying to get the best work from consultants and agencies. I know how they operate from the inside.

I work with several in the Bay Area, including Organic & Evolution Bureau (Omnicom), Draftfcb, MRMWorldwide & MAP (Interpublic Group), Blast Radius (WPP), and Euro RSCG. I offer experience in tech, consumer packaged goods, apparel, and financial services, among others.

Think of me as the one leading discovery and opportunity analysis for really huge (and many small), intricate Web experiences—but also executing with design teams. I’m brought on for my strategy skills, but asked back for my credibility and collaborative leadership work style.

I’m happy as an independent consultant, but never turn down a compelling conversation about senior-level, thoughtfully pathed roles.
