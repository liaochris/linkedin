Alex Barnes' Summary

A creative thinker and problem solver with technical knowhow. Passionate about visual design, UX and typography he has 4 years experience in digital design for web, mobile platforms and software systems.

My greatest talent is the ability to present the most important information quickly and intuitively in a clear and coherent manner to web users. Focused on innovative ways to display visual data built around user requirements, I'm is centered on strong user experience.

I has a breadth of commercial experience includes Sports Companies, Entertainment Magazines, Viral Video, Technology, Defence, Social Media, Mobile, Security, Marketing and Automobile, but also has a large focus on Real Estate, Property, FM, Construction and Retail.

The key to developing engaging visual software design solutions comes through conducting the right user research and analysing customer requirements to form an initial concept. Through creative problem solving and brainstorming successful mobile software interfaces come to life.

Working with mobile, web and software system interfaces and designing and architecting interfaces for mobile platforms, I have a deep understanding of mobile related design best practices.

Knowledgeable in mobile asset production for iOS and Android, I quickly translate business and user requirements into visual design ideas and concepts from wireframes, standards and functional specifications.
