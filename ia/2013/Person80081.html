Lenore Strocchia-Rivera, Ph. D.'s Experience

Founder and Director

Learning Insights

Individual & Family Services industry

September 2008 – Present (5 years 2 months) Highland, NY

Learning Insights is an independently owned psychology practice offering psychological evaluations (to institutions, such as adoption agencies, schools, courts, foster care agencies) and psychological assessments (to children, teens, and adults). Specialties include neuropsychological assessment to answer questions about Executive Functioning and services - such as the Cognitive Working Memory Training Program and ADD coaching - to effectively address deficits in this area. All services are offered by highly experienced staff - including psychologists, social workers, and coaches - who practice the values of reverence, quality, fairness, integrity, excellence, and hope.

Clinical Director

Astor Early Childhood Programs

December 1997 – November 2008 (11 years)

Supervised mental health staff and interns who annually served over 500 children between birth and age five across Dutchess County in a nationally-recognized Head Start/Early Head Start program. Conducted or supervised more than 1,000 preschool psychological evaluations. Oversaw a postpartum depression project where 85% of the 125 women improved. Co-directed a preschool day treatment program that resulted in statistically significant behavioral and social improvements and more than a 99% reduction in the number of physical holds used over a 10-year period. Also pioneered the Great Halloween Candy Exchange, a countywide partnership designed to battle the childhood obesity problem, and obtained more than $525,000 in grants.
