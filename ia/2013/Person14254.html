Bill Dotson's Summary

Bill Dotson is a technology expert and entrepreneur who has years of experience with the chaos of start-ups and digital marketing.

Throughout his life, Bill has had a wealth of experiences, from starting and selling businesses to working abroad to raising three energetic boys. Through it all, he has learned that the most important things people can do in business and is life is to be honest with themselves, take a leap of faith, and have dogged persistence.

Bill is currently running a digital marketing agency, WebMedley, where he specializes in website design, social media services, and explaining technology to others in a way that is simple and effective. Prior to WebMedley, he manned his own consulting company, Dotson Ventures, and has a history of directing and managing several other technology companies as well as building Internet applications. His BA in Asian Studies and Philosophy from the University of Kentucky also helps him in his research and teaching engagements. Bill frequently continues his education by attending seminars like the entrepreneur development program at prestigious universities like MIT.

In his spare time, Bill loves to travel and has business contacts in multiple countries. He’s also contemplating buying one of those indestructible cargo container homes for those three boys of his.
