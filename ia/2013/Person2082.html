Adrienne Klum's Summary

I am a user experience practitioner with nearly a decade of experience working with clients in retail, health, arts and education. I have a broad skill set, with expertise in user research and testing, information architecture and user-centered design, visual communication and frontend development.

I've played key roles on projects including an innovative blood glucose meter, an eLearning platform for an international restaurant chain, a mobile application that encourages exercise through knowledge and personal metrics tracking, and numerous custom ecommerce solutions for mid-market retailers.
