Dan LoPresto, CIPP, CFE, CISSP & SANS GCIH's Summary

Dan LoPresto is the Director of Information Protection and Privacy for Marriott Vacations Worldwide Corporation. In his capacity, Mr. LoPresto oversees data protection and privacy-related projects, business continuity and is heavily involved with regulatory compliance, European Safe Harbor Framework certification and vendor contract reviews/negotiations. He also assists, on occasion, with Payment Card Industry (PCI) Data Security Standard (DSS) compliance. Throughout an intriguing career in the field of information technology, security, anti-fraud and privacy, Mr. LoPresto has steadily built on an ever-increasing skill set allowing him to provide security governance across several organizations. Renowned for his knowledge and leadership abilities, Mr. LoPresto remains current of industry trends and developments within information security, data protection and data privacy, as well as fraud detection and deterrence. He completed all doctoral coursework in the area of Information Systems and holds several industry-related, professional licenses and certifications.
