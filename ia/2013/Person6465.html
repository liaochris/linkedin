Andrea Ottolina's Summary

As Appreneur, I believe I achieved quite something considering that I led my first iPhone app - iTorch - to 10 million downloads worldwide in less than 18 months (16 million as of June 2013), working alone in my spare time. A carefully planned launch in different countries and precise self-promoting investments coupled with in-depth analytics and constant monitoring of competitors, have been keys to the app success.

Thanks to the results achieved, I then co-founded a second company - Flubber Media - with the objective to build and market new, engaging and innovative mobile apps. One of these - ZenView - has been featured on Time Magazine among the "Top 50 must have Apps for the iPad".

Because of my Italian origins I love well crafted designs and I am obsessed with details.

I also spent many years in the UK and I had the amazing opportunity to work at top digital agencies like AKQA, where I learnt how to approach software development and project management in a very pragmatic way.

Recently I started to focus my interests on mobile advertising and marketing strategies, due to the enormous potential yet to be tapped in these fields.

Specialties:
- Mobile technologies: iOS, Xcode, Objective-C, Cocoa, iPhone/iPad.
- Frontend technologies: xhtml, css, javascript, xml, flash, actionscript.
- Backend technologies: asp, .net, ruby on rails, jsp, sql server, mysql.
- Project Management tools: BaseCamp, Microsoft Project, Jira.
- Platform and OS: Windows, Apple Mac.
- Network and client/server infrastructure management experience.
- Web enthusiast fascinated by latest trends and cutting edge technologies.
