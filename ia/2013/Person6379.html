Andrea Correale's Summary

For Andrea Correale, it all started around the family dinner table. From a very young age, her mother made her see that a table could be set with creativity, no matter the budget. Today, Andrea leads a small catering and event planning empire aptly called Elegant Affairs. Every great party, be it a small gathering of friends or a gala event for hundreds, starts with a great idea, and that's where Andrea's expertise comes in. She started her career by renting out wait-staff for private parties and soon moved on to offering her clients much more. Her sense of style has made her a favorite of the Hamptons Summer set, as well as corporate and private clients from Long Island to New York City. Detail oriented and driven by the will to be the best at everything she does and to give the best back to her customers, Andrea takes on each client with excitement and gusto to create an affair they will always remember.
