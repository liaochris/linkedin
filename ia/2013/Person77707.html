Kyle Christie's Summary

I'm the Web Content Editor for the Nursing and Midwifery Council (NMC), a role which involves website management, working with departments on their content, developing the function and design of the site and editing copy. I'm also responsible for email newsletters to multiple audiences and the organisation's social media presence, and have been involved in managing the staff intranet and other internal communications channels.

I've previously worked in the public relations teams at the University of Sheffield and King's College London, roles which involved dealing with local, national and international media, monitoring and analysing coverage, web editing, taking responsibility for social media profiles and editing and publishing videos and podcasts.

I have experience in editing, copy writing, online publication, photography, video editing, using a variety of content management systems, writing for different platforms including print, working in fast-paced environments and of taking responsibility for a public-facing library site.

I'm familiar with Photoshop, InDesign and a number of Content Management Systems including Polopoly, Wordpress, Blogger, Dreamweaver and Episerver 5.

I've got a lot of interests including social media, communicating with audiences online, photography, politics, the third sector, cycling and the environment. I do some occasional work with charities on their communications and am involved in the Wandsworth Friends of the Earth group.

I have qualifications in NCTJ Law and Public Admin. I'm a Member of the CIPR and was previously on the CIPR Education and Skills Group committee.

Specialties: Social media, digital media, website management, higher education, public relations, the environment, reputation management.
