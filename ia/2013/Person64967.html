JoAnn Simony's Experience

Owner

Dooen Books

January 2007 – Present (6 years 6 months)

Since 1992 I have provided editorial and production services for trade fiction and nonfiction books, professional books, and college textbooks and ancillaries for a number of clients. These services include developmental editing, copyediting, proofreading, project editing, print production, design, page composition and typesetting, file conversion and management.

Senior Production Manager, College

W. W. Norton & Company

Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Publishing industry

February 2001 – January 2007 (6 years)

For the College Division of the largest independent, employee-owned U.S. publisher, I managed the workflow of 6 major textbooks at a time (on average), including all related ancillary products. My projects were typically four-color, heavily illustrated, first editions or completely revised, in multiple volumes or editions, and/or on tight deadlines.

Production Editor

Silverchair Science + Communications

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Publishing industry

August 1997 – February 2000 (2 years 7 months)

For a book packager specializing in medical and science books, I maintained schedules and workflows by managing freelancers, corresponding with authors, and working closely with in-house composition staff. Many titles had multiple authors, were heavily illustrated, and/or 4-color.

Software Production Manager

W. W. Norton & Company

Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Publishing industry

September 1992 – May 1996 (3 years 9 months)

For Norton's Electronic Media department, I handled the production and packaging of all Norton software products. Responsibilities included software development, technical support, package design, Web design and HTML coding, and technical writing. Wrote documentation for about two dozen titles per year.
