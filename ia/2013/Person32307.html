David M. Pascual's Summary

David is the founder of The Digital Marvels, Inc.. Previously, David held management positions at inCode (a Sequoia Capital portfolio company), Ericsson and, most recently, at VeriSign, where he was the Chief of Staff / Deputy CFO.

David has focused on enterprise and consumer services and products in the mobile, Internet, e-commerce, cloud, security and payments space for over 15 years. He has held positions in finance, operations, product management and corporate development in startups and enterprises.

David holds an electrical engineering and computer science degree and is a Harvard Business School alumnus.

David is from Brooklyn, NYC and brings a truly global perspective, having lived and worked overseas for many years.

Passion: Linking the dots between business models, user design and technology with a business mind and the heart of an engineer.

David brings the following experience:
- 20+ years in technology, including hands on experience typing code; from Basic, C+… to HTML5.
- 15+ years in finance.
- 20+ years generating business as an entrepreneur and investor.

Two parallel thought processes with a common end-goal: 1) Simplify. Engage. Monetize. 2) Create. Scale. Monetize.

David M. Pascual's Experience

CEO

The Digital Marvels

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Computer Software industry

2011 – Present (2 years) Greater New York City Area

The Digital Marvels is a cloud-based software platform headquartered in New York City with European offices in Barcelona. We are the first cloud-based software platform that enables Luxury, Fashion and Retail brands to offer consumers a seamless and personalized brand experience – across all channels – before, at, and after the point of sale (in-store and online).

Our company is the result of 18 months of close collaboration with brands, stores and consumers. It addresses their pain points and needs and incorporates expertise from Luxury, Fashion and Retail leaders as well as extensive R&D in human technology interactions.

Many people are trying to scale the omnichannel experience almost entirely through algorithms, gaming, device tracking and (sometimes) by stepping on people’s privacy. We’ve taken a simpler route, one that reflects the way people naturally sell and buy and considers sales motivations, shopping behaviors and consumer needs. Our platform scales the omnichannel experience with the human touch, empowering brands, stores and consumers. We scale human-to-human interaction like never before: with the human touch.

We focus on high-end / high – touch with particular focus on those firms where a personalized customer relationship and brand experience are the key selling factors and on Retail, Fashion and Luxury (direct, brand and multibrand-retailer).

The Digital Marvels has been engaged to deploy its platform to over 2,500 stores in the coming months.

We have the most extensive list of individual investors from the fashion, luxury and retail industry; including founders and C-level executives from the following companies: LVMH, Gucci, Ralph Lauren, Burberry, DFS Galleria, The Swatch Group, Alexander McQueen, Harvey Nichols, AllSaints, VF Corp, MCM, Abercrombie & Fitch, Steven Alan, Lucky Brand Jeans, Liz Claiborne, etc.
