Jennifer Matoske's Summary

I am an adventurer - plain and simple. I relish new experiences and opportunities. My favorite of all is engaging with people in genuine, provocative conversations that result in an expanded world view for both parties. That's why I so enjoy my role as a public speaker. I not only am privileged to share what I've learned but also to interface with others who add to my insights through their experience and learning.

My heart is especially called to work with and for youth. Now more than ever society echoes with myriad voices targeting our youth. Many of the voices speak harmful messages into the hearts of those most emotionally vulnerable – adolescents. Though the subjects vary – not skinny enough, not smart enough, not athletic enough, not rich enough –their message remains the same. “You are not enough.” From early childhood young people are exposed to ever increasing doses of this toxic tirade. These voices cause our nation’s youth lose belief in their ability to prevail and in their very value as human beings. Someone is needed who will show young people their intrinsic value as unique human masterpieces. Someone like me.
My heart is to lift our nation’s youth from the sinkhole of self-loathing and place them on the solid foundation of self-acceptance. As a young person, I too struggled with the feelings of not being enough. My desperation drove me to pursue anything that would create a sense of personal value. Finally I discovered a path of emotional healing and my own truth about my personal worth.

I have enjoyed speaking at youth summits, women's conferences, and retreats. These opportunities allow me to share my story and research that reveals the fallacious forces devastating our self-worth. I work to give youth and adults alike the truth they deserve. I strive to show them their personal potential and inherent value as individuals. I speak, as a voice against the voices, telling them that they are precious, they are valuable and indeed they are…enough.
