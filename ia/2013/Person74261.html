Ken Krogue's Experience

President and Co-Founder

InsideSales.com

Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Computer Software industry

November 2004 – Present (8 years 10 months)

InsideSales.com is the market-leading provider of lead response management solutions.

InsideSales.com helps companies respond to web-generated leads immediately and persistently. Average companies wait 24 to 48 hours to call back a lead, and average reps only make 4-5 calls. With B2B contact rates around 10%, that means only 45% of leads ever get contacted! Companies Ken works with contact 87% to 92% of their contactable leads, that doubles sales right there.

Lead qualification rates are increased dramatically by responding to web leads in seconds and minutes. InsideSales.com can be tailored to the needs or preferences of each client.

To maximize the power of InsideSales.com’s Lead Response Management solutions—and to shepherd the increased number of qualified leads that they will generate through the complete sales cycle—InsideSales.com can integrate with CRM providers like Salesforce.com and RightNow Technologies and also offers customers its own hosted CRM solution. This is unique in the CRM marketplace for its integrated suite of “telephony power tools” which include outbound click-to-call and power dialers, outbound message broadcasting, inbound ACD/IVR routing, and inbound/outbound call blending.
