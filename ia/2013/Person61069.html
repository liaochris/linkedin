Jeff Miller's Summary

Offering a unique blend of research/analytical and creative/strategic thinking, I am passionate about helping organizations leverage their data, reach their customers, and achieve their goals. In short, I seek to serve my clients by providing insights in analytics, marketing, and strategy.

With diverse experience in business, education, and non-profit sectors, clients range anywhere from a Fortune 100 pharmaceutical company to a small business specializing in parts and salvage. As an MBA with strengths in project management and communication, I have a proven ability to manage resources and leverage opportunity to maximize ROI.
