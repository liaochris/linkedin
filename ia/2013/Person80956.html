Linda Behrle's Summary

We help businesses grow by helping them achieve Lasting Impressions through the Power of Promotional Products.

THE "WOW" FACTOR
With access to all of the top researching tools in the industry I make certain that my clients have access to great products at fair prices with excellent customer service. I specialize in new and interesting ideas that will WOW their customers! I represent over 3,000 suppliers with over 500,000 items that are customized specifically for each client.

PROFESSIONAL EXPERIENCE -
Larry Mallery of Mallery Marketing brought me into the industry in January of 2007. Since that time I have also taken numerous seminars and attended industry trade shows to keep up on the latest promotional products.

Larry had over 20 years of experience in the promotional products business. He was a wonderful teacher and mentor and generously passed on to me his many years of expertise. The most important legacy Larry left me is this - it's all about the relationships. Larry also said "I am fortunate to have the best customers in the world!" And he was right on both counts.
Larry passed away on February 6, 2008 at the age of 80 and is greatly missed.

MY COMMITMENT TO MY CLIENTS
My goal is to find the best promotional products to meet the needs of my clients and help them achieve great results. My clients expect, deserve and receive the very best in service, integrity, professionalism and creativity.

Specialties

Creativity! Thinking outside the box to give my clients something unique to create Lasting Impressions for clients and prospects. I like to think that I am a part of my clients' marketing team.

Research! I don't just stick a few catalogs in front of you. I use my state of the art research tools and training to find great ideas for you based on your needs. I keep up to date on new products and ideas to better serve your needs.
