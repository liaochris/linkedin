Megan Yarbrough's Summary

Megan Yarbrough is a passionate public health advocate with proven experience developing and implementing advocacy campaigns, utilizing social media to strengthen campaigns, and conducting trainings on tobacco control and social media.

While she was in high school, several close friends and family members were diagnosed with cancer -- marking the beginning of her fight against cancer and her interest in public health and youth advocacy. Megan truly believes we can make a difference in our communities, and that belief empowers her to seek out new and creative ways to create change.

Specialties

Megan's specialties include tobacco control, public health advocacy, grassroots organizing, youth advocacy, individual and group training, public speaking, strategic social media assessment and plans, internet advocacy, and new media.
