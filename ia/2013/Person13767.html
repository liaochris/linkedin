Beth Tunis, MA, LMFT's Summary

Providing the Spirit of Professional Growth and Development in both private and professional spheres as a Licensed Psychotherapist and Coach.

Beth has developed high-impact, personal care with a high level of accountability. She employs effective strategies for overwhelmed people wanting greater self-efficacy to create personal and professional change.

As an accomplished personal development facilitator her experiences include two major hospitals, university, sports association, fortune 500 corporations, and social service agencies - real life plus education.

Utilizing the psychological principles of Positive, Systemic, Cognitive-Behavioral and Mind-Body-Spirit methods to provide people and organizations with effective, practical information and tools.


PSYCHOTHERAPY:
Offers the experience of therapy to make sense of and manage Anxiety, Depression, Recovery, and Relationships.

Experiences include: Addiction Program at Kaiser Permanente Med. Cntr., outpatient EAP for Cedar-Sinai Medical Center, UCLA Addiction Dept, Family Social Service Agencies, Psychodynamic Beverly Hills Agency.

The tenets of respect, empowerment and re-establishing a healthy sense of control is her focus .

Serving Individual, Couple, Family and Groups. Adolescent and Adult.


COACHING:
Mindfull Coaching is a model which incorporates evidence-based and holistic strategies. Her background stems from three areas: academic, personal business and health professionals.
