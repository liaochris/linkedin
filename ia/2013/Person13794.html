Betsy Hedberg's Summary

My mission is to help people find peace and fulfillment in their work lives and to support individuals and organizations helping others in this regard. To that end, I counsel, teach, and write. Here's what I do:

- Provide personalized career counseling services for people who wish to figure out what they want to do for work, improve the quality of their experience at work, make a career change, determine whether their current job or career path is a good fit for them, and/or return to the work force while incorporating their values and talents.

- Teach mindfulness and stress reduction programs to public school teachers and businesses, fostering decreased stress levels, increased focus and productivity, and enhanced interpersonal harmony.

- Write engaging marketing case studies for businesses in the coaching, organizational development, and human resources fields, showcasing their success with clients and enabling them to reach more people.

Betsy Hedberg's Experience

Mindfulness and Stress Reduction Trainer and Curriculum Writer

PassageWorks Institute and The Impact Foundation

January 2009 – Present (4 years 8 months) Greater Denver Area

I'm currently teaching mindfulness classes for public school teachers enrolled in the Stress Management and Relaxation Techniques in Education (SMART) program, facilitating stress reduction and enhancing teachers' ability to effectively work with students and parents. I also collaborated in the development of the SMART curriculum, including a major role writing and editing the program's training manual. SMART is currently being implemented in the Jefferson County, Denver, and Boulder Valley public school districts (Colorado), and we're in the process of expanding.

Career Counselor and Stress Reduction Specialist

Denver Career Counseling

August 2008 – Present (5 years 1 month) Greater Denver Area

I provide counseling for people who are yearning for a new career direction or experiencing high job dissatisfaction or job-related stress. My personalized, narrative approach to career counseling helps clients craft their unique paths. I work with people with a variety of interests and backgrounds, including but not limited to artistic/creative individuals. I offer the Myers-Briggs Type Indicator and the Strong Interest Inventory (two popular career assessments) and assist with resumes and the job search process. I see clients near I-25 and Colorado Blvd.

Freelance Business and Education Writer

Denver Wordsmith and Curriculum Adventures

April 1997 – Present (16 years 5 months) Denver, Colorado

Writing is one of my favorite pastimes, and I've been doing it professionally since 1997. I'm now offering case studies for businesses in the coaching, organizational development, and human resources fields. These extended testimonials from satisfied clients allow businesses to connect authentically with potential clients and show real life examples of what the business can do for clients.

I also have extensive experience developing creative, high-quality lesson plans and other curriculum materials to help K-12 teachers enhance student learning and meet educational standards requirements. From the late '90s to around 2007, I planned and implemented numerous professional development workshops to help teachers incorporate technology and Internet content into their lesson plans and to model to teachers creative ways for using technology with their students.
