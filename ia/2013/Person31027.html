Dave Cook's Summary

I was born in Buffalo N.Y. and grew up in the suburb of Amherst. In 1997 I moved to Syracuse N.Y. During my seven years there I went to college and started my career in the mortgage industry. In March of 2004 I moved out to Colorado with my wife April.

People know I love my job, they can tell that when they work with me. Every day I get up and am excited about going to the office. The biggest reason is because I enjoy working with customers and making the dream of owning a home come true. There is nothing like seeing the excitment in a persons face as they sit at the closing table about to receive the keys to their new home!

With extensive experience in residential mortgages, you can be assured I have the necessary knowledge to put you in the best loan possible. In the all years I have been in this business I have never had an unhappy client. Many lenders have deals fall apart at the last minute, but this has never happended to me. This is because I do my homework and make sure we have all our bases covered. Both my realtors and clients know they can count on me because I have never let them down, and have always provided the best possible service.
