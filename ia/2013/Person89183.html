Matthew Green's Summary

I started work in 1987 managing clients in the advertising, printing, pre-press, publishing and design sectors of the media industry. As my career progressed from Sales and Project Management to General Management and Managing Director, I became highly proficient in creating budgets, motivating staff and ensuring projects were delivered on time.

After spending four years at Sydney Technical College I embarked upon an ambitious goal of completing a six-year part-time Commerce degree within four years. I succeeded and graduated with a Bachelor of Commerce from Wollongong University majoring in Management and Legal Studies with skills in Marketing, Economics and Accounting.

In 2001 I started Creative Folks Pty Ltd, a software and services company based in Sydney. When I left the business ten years later it was in a sound financial state with several international supplier contracts, a profitable support division and a healthy client base throughout Australia and New Zealand.

As the head of Publishers Australia I have organized networking events, interstate roadshows and the highly commended Magazine Week Conference, Exhibition and Gala Awards Dinner held at the Sydney Hilton in November 2012. With over 400 attendees at the Awards, hundreds more delegates at the Conference, speakers from Australia’s largest media outlets, and key suppliers including international companies Adobe and Zinio at the Exhibition, Magazine Week 2012 was a resounding success.

My extensive network includes CEOs and Managing Directors across publishing and digital businesses, many of whom I have worked with at Board level.

I am an experienced ‘hands on’ business manager with a proven track record of success servicing clients throughout Australia & New Zealand. My business acumen has enabled me to manage several companies, including my own, through depression, succession, recession and progression.

Please view my blog at http://matthewgreen.com.au/ or email at mattvgreen@gmail.com
