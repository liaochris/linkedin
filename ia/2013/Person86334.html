Mark Barner's Summary

My inspiration comes from challenging the norm. I enjoy taking a different approach to every-day dilemmas. I combine ingenuity with functional ideas, in the pursuit of finding the best solution, satisfying the client as well as staying true to my personal style.

I strive to create trendy websites that standout, with features that are innovative. User friendliness is one of my absolute focus points. Lack of user friendliness, can be the downfall of even the most impressive websites!

My goal is to evolve continuously: in my designs and expertise to benefit my work and therefore my company. Being an independant company it allows me the freedom to pursue new ideas and layouts. I am flexible to work with and committed to tailoring the perfect product to suit my clients.

Let me know if you have a new challenge for me!

The majority of my clients ask me to design their visual identity in the shape of a logo, website, paperline, social media and posters, that can be used for marketing their brand.
I provide all graphic designs and stock photos for my clients. If however the right photo is not contained within my database, I have professional camera equipment with which to take the desired shot.
In connection with creating banners or business cards, animations or a dynamic flash design is also within my capabilities.
When the design is finished, I offer consultancy services regarding the use of different medias and further developing the design concept and branding.

In case I have not expressed myself clearly- I live and breathe for great functional identity design. I work hard to help my clients achieve their goals and beyond. I also make sure that they stay true to their brand at all times. I believe that precise communication is key to success.
