Mr. Chance Wilson's Summary

Chance Wilson a native of Buffalo, New York has over nine years of experience that includes comprehensive marketing, business operations development, TV show production, media, strategic planning, and project management. Chance has raised the bar of expectation among those in the marketing industry because of his history. While most marketing professionals speak of their abilities, Chance continues to show his impact by servicing multiple industries and spearheading challenging projects from inception, implementation to expansion through his brainchild BINCHMARK Marketing & Agency Services (BMA).

Chance is active in the community through his work with the Professional Association of Businesses (PAOB). Chance is also the founder of Notice Today Network, formerly known as Hampton Roads LIVE (HRL), a independent news media, publishing, and television production outlet. Chance has executive produced and hosted a list of inside interviews ranging from everyday people, executives, to national recording artist and cancer survivor Angela Winbush, Fidel Valdez Ramos, the 12th President of the Philippines, NBC Today Shows Al Roker to the Chief Technology Officer and Assistant to the President of the United States Aneesh Chopra and more.

The Professional Association of Businesses (PAOB) is a private membership-based association comprised of executives, business professionals, organizations, and individuals. The PAOB is a progressive association with a belief that the need to build business must be matched by the need to support the community. This balance is essential in maintaining sustainable economic growth and contributes to ongoing social change within our local communities. The PAOB provides quality and innovative services to meet the needs of its diverse membership of business owners, companies, community leaders, and nonprofit organizations. Members take advantage of a variety of tools and resources to increase and measure their community involvement.

The PAOB touches the community through three key areas. Workshops on topics from business development to youth mentoring are created through PAOB's Educational Development Programs. Campaigns for senior citizen care, youth empowerment, and support for charitable causes are developed under PAOB's Community Development Programs. Professionally capturing and broadcasting the work of the PAOB, its members, and their causes that affect the community are the emphases of PAOB's Media Relations Program. The PAOB creates the programs that reach you.

The PAOB offers membership to individuals, business professionals, corporations, and organizations on an approval basis. Learn more about the PAOB at www.SocietyOfMembers.com.
