Hi, I'm a professional freelance translator and subtitler, working in the following language pairs: English to Italian and Spanish to Italian.

Agnese Morettini's Publications

  • Profiling Deaf and Hard-of-Hearing Users of SDH in Italy: a Questionnaire-based Study

    • MONTI Monographs in Translation and Interpreting
    • 2012
    Authors: Agnese Morettini

    The aim of this article is to present the results of a questionnaire-based study carried out as part of the author's Master's Degree dissertation about Subtitles for the Deaf and Hard-of-hearing (SDH). In particular, the need to gather specific information regarding audience design will be highlighted in the paper, since this research field has long been neglected in Italy. The questions were aimed at shedding light on the needs, cultural environment and world-view of Italian SDH users, by eliciting personal data as well as subtitle-related information. Moreover, the 232 respondents were asked to suggest improvements to SDH services in Italy. Although these first results cannot allow definite conclusions because of the limited number of respondents to the survey, they might prove to be helpful for professional translators ans subtitlers to better know their intended recipients.

Agnese Morettini's Projects

  • Audiointros.org

    • June 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Agnese Morettini, Elena Di Giovanni

    I am currently participating in a European-level project, called AudioIntros, lead by professor Pablo Romero Fresco (Roehampton University) and Louise Fryer (National Theatre London and Visual Eyes). Lead for the Italian research unit: professor Elena Di Giovanni (University of Macerata). The project deals with the investigation of the reception of English audiodescriptions and audiointroductions translated into Italian with a group of Italian visually impaired people. So far I have translated the audiointroduction and I have transcribed, spotted, timed and translated into Italian the audiodescription of the award-winning feature "Slumdog Millionaire".
