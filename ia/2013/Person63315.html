Jewel Anderson's Summary

Jewel Anderson is the Founder of VIRTUOUS Career Services which is a non-profit organization that provides career counseling and job readiness to those that need it.

She has an extensive background in Recruiting, Sales and Management. Previously, Jewel worked for Mattress Firm, which is one of the largest and growing bedding retail organization in the United States. She was the Store Manager and Market recruiter. Jewel has managed several Atlanta stores consistently hitting budgets and has a finalized revenue of over 6.5 million. As a Recruiter, Jewel handled the hiring for the sales management and training program for 45 locations in the Atlanta Market. Currently, she is the Career and Alumni Services Specialist for Year Up Atlanta where she meets an 85% outcome for placing students in IT related careers.

Civically, Jewel serves as the Resume Evaluator for Diversity Career Expo's, and a partner for The Bethel Center which is an organization that works with young girls from ages 12-25. She is on the fundraising committee for BCOP (Beverly Cunningham Outreach Center), Secretary for National Coalition of 100 Black Women Decatur/DeKalb, a founding member of Under30CEO- Atlanta chapter, and President of the Georgia State University Collegiate Entrepreneur Organization.

As an emerging leader, Jewel is a mentor to young entrepreneurs. Her passion to see others succeed has given her the drive to launch Under 30 Following the Vision. Under 30 Following the Vision is a magazine, and scholarship, program created to help Georgia entrepreneurs 30 years of age and younger get exposure, development, and funding. She was also a participant in the United Way V.I.P. Class for spring 2012.

Jewel holds an Associate of Arts in Communications from Georgia Perimeter College, and is currently attending Georgia State University where she is pursuing a B.S. in Public Policy with and emphasis in Non-profit Management.
