Gunars Grundstoks' Summary

I’m the CEO and co-founder of IT House, Latvia’s leading web development and design company serving the international community. Our team does more than build premium sites and apps—we strategize with our clients to deliver exactly what they need to achieve their business goals efficiently and effectively. As Ruby on Rails and WordPress specialists, we’ve helped crowdfunding projects, news portals, marketplace platforms, community networks, and ecommerce companies create custom sites and apps that look beautiful and function flawlessly.

I’m also the CEO and co-founder of RentMama.com, a revolutionary service that connects local consumers and independent car rental companies. A successful spinoff of IT House, RentMama is the result of our rich experiences at exclusive startup accelerators, StartupBootcamp and Ignite100, which selected our team and 9 others from a field of over 600 candidates.

As an original co-founder and board member of TechHub Riga, the first TechHub expansion from London, I cultivate collaborative spaces for emerging entrepreneurs and tech startups to come together to learn, share, and grow. I’m one of two people responsible for bringing Garage48, a 48-hour hackathon event, to Latvia. A central figure in Latvia’s Ruby on Rails community, I organize regular meetups for likeminded tech enthusiasts. While I’m a curator at StartupDigest for Latvia, I’m also an active supporter of the burgeoning startup scene in the Baltic Rim and Nordic countries.

I’ve lived and worked in Latvia, UK, Sweden, Denmark and Finland, and speak English, Latvian, and Russian. I enjoy running, hiking, rock climbing, traveling, and maintaining a healthy lifestyle. But above all, I love what I do; my job is my hobby, and making my clients happy makes me happy.

Connect with me to talk and learn more about IT House, RentMama, TechHub Riga, Garage48, the Ruby on Rails community, and social entrepreneurship opportunities.
