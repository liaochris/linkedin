John Dion's Summary

My most recent projects included rehabing a historic building built in 1820. It is now a holistic healing place called The Center for Wellness in Colchester CT. If you are sick and tired of the modern medicine come make a difference in your life.

The Hartford Colt Commons Building, yes this is where Samuel Colt made the .45cal hand gun. Now it's the CREC Capitol Region Education Council, this was a great project to be a part of the building was not being used and now it's a great learning facility for kids. We also had our highest volume pour of 756 yards of concrete, place and finish in less than 8 hrs, at the New Haven Power Plant.

I have 25 years construction experience and owned a concrete flatwork company for 10 years which I gave up to my two partners to pursue my passion, Real Estate.

I've worked a variety of projects from building your average home all the way to 22 floors of concrete in the Hartford City Place II, and Cape Canaveral Satellite Fueling Station in Florida.

OAKLEAF Enterprises Inc is a Real Estate investment company created to help investors as well as someone who needs to stop foreclosure. My networking connections over the past 20 years, brings resolve to any concern with Real Estate or construction of any phase.

I speculate that the required involvement for the year ahead will bring about new challenges, therefore becoming much more interesting and exciting than what I am currently use to. Who knows? Maybe it is just the “pregame jitters”, that self induced looming terror of the unknown and inevitable change in my warm and familiar routine.

Helping people get what they want. Smiling at the world. Loving LIFE and appreciate all that I have.
