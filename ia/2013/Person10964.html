Austin Massee's Summary

Austin is a graduate of Bethel University in St. Paul, MN where he earned his degree in Business Entrepreneurship and Business Marketing. He is the owner of a local IT consulting firm established in 2003. He loves to be active in the local and academic community and spending time outdoors. Austin formerly served as the Vice President of The Bethel Entrepreneurship Club at Bethel University. He is an Eagle Scout and naturally loves to go camping, fishing, canoeing, hiking, and rock climbing.

Department Q started over nine years ago with only a business card and a dream to help others with computer issues. This small business has grown into an expanding company serving over 200 small business and residential customers in the Twin Cities and around the world via remote access.

In an effort to better serve his clients, in 2008 Austin received training on identity theft protection with Legal Shield, inc. (NYSE:PPD) He is qualified to offer legal memberships to individuals and businesses to protect against identity theft and offer preventative legal service plans.

Austin formerly worked with Lifetouch, inc, (Eden Prairie, MN) the world's largest employee-owned photography company. He served under the Corporate Vice President of E-Business and Integrated Solutions and was a member of the Lifetouch Innovation Lab.

Specialties: Computer repair, wireless networking, IT services, point of sale, remote access, security, surveillance, data backup/recovery, web site development, training, Windows SharePoint, Adobe Creative Suite, Microsoft Office Suite, affordable access to the legal system, identity theft protection and restoration, international business including the Americas and Europe.
