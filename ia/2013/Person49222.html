WineCollective

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Wine and Spirits industry

March 2009 – Present (4 years 6 months)

WineCollective strives to broaden the palates of members through the delivery of unique global wines. We provide a rich wine experience package containing in-depth tasting notes of each wine along with background information, food pairing and tips on wine tasting techniques and etiquette. WineCollective itself strives to be on the cutting edge of wine related education, experiences and social networking.

The idea for WineCollective was developed out of a relationship with Reading For New Times magazine which, since 2001, has been providing the internet with original content on art, culture, music, travel and wine. Reading for New Times was publishing wine reviews and was getting feedback that they should expand into a wine experience provider. From that concept WineCollective was born.

In 2011 WineCollective launched a club for luxury wines called Tannic (tannic.ca)
