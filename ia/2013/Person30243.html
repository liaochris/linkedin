Danny Huffman's Summary

Danny Huffman, MA, CPCC, CEIP, CPRW

Author of “Your Personal Career Marketing Manual and Portfolio Essentials,” and “Your Personal Career Marketing Manual,” a student-speak and proven textbook bridging successful transitions for undergraduate students, graduate students, alumni, and professionals of all levels. A Certified Professional Résumé Writer, Certified Employment Interview Professional, and a Certified Professional Career Coach, as a vice-president of operations, I broke away from a major career marketing competitor to create a client-centered career management firm, CareerSI and EducationCS.

A leader in the career management industry, I oversee all career/creative materials and career coaching services for Career Services International. Additionally, I am an active member with the National Resume Writers Association, the Professional Resume Writers Association, Career Masters Alliance, Career Directors International, the Southern Association of College Educators, and the National Association of Colleges and Employers.

Beginning my professional academic career as an adjunct instructor, I offer hands-on expertise in the classroom. As a program/department chair, an associate dean, and an interim dean of academic affairs (ITT Technical Institute), my administrative and functional experience is recognized in many circles. Possessing an AA (Business), a BA (Sociology), and an MA (Humanities/Communication), I blend academia and contemporary industry knowledge to create interactive approaches to career development.

Builder of many professional development training sessions and industry-specific seminars, my ability to fuse corporate, professional, and academia into a common thread is a sought after asset. An experienced speaker to audiences of 10 to 250, my most recent engagement at Yale University examined techniques to decode the Federal Career arena.

Specialties

Educational career and professional portfolio textbook development. My undergraduate and graduate textbooks are used in many colleges and universities throughout the United States. My specialty is possessing experience in education, corporate, and consulting arenas.
