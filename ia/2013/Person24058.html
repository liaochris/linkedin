Christina Haxton's Summary

Leadership is a verb. Effective leadership requires more than understanding theory. It requires emotional intelligence, social intelligence and the courage to take action with a greater sense of confidence, trust and courage than you could ever imagine. And, being a "built to last" leader requires action, engagement and accountability at a much higher level than ever before.

My clients are first introduced to the "3 Step ACE Approach to Change" so you can take action that gets you results. With a combination of mindfulness, emotional intelligence, systems thinking and courage, you can create a "best places to work" culture for yourself, your team and your customers.

I assist emerging, recently promoted managers and experienced leaders who desire to have the self-awareness, communication and interpersonal skills necessary to become a sustainable leader who is built to last from the inside out, and the outside in.
