Mark Twomey's Summary

Mark has established TARA HRC to offer a flexible range of recruitment services to the CEE real estate market.

13 years headhunting in CEE, operating exclusively in the real estate and construction sectors. Mark has built up many strong relationships with commercial real estate companies and candidates.

Mark is native Irish and has a solid understanding of Polish and Czech along with basic Finnish. Mark is an avid rugby and Gaelic football fan. Mark has also been involved in the establishment of Poland`s first ever GAA club based in Warsaw, Cumann Warszawa.

Mark was a board member of the Irish Polish chamber of commerce for 3 years and sat as joint chairperson June 2008 – June 2009 (www.icc.org.pl)

Mark holds a BSc (Hons) in Estate Management from the University of Reading

Specialties: International and local executive search for senior level management with the Real Estate, Construction and Engineering sectors
