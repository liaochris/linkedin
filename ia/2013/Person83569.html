Ian MacLeod's Summary

Ian MacLeod has been in the real estate industry since 1994, where he started selling for Intrawest in Whistler after many years as a ski instructor (Level 4 CSIA). In 2000, he continued working in the resort real estate sector in Mammoth Lakes California, working on the Village at Mammoth launch. From there he formed his own real estate marketing project and managed numerous projects in British Columbia and Washington State including resort and residential. In 2003, while co-managing his own company, Ian worked with S&P Destination Properties and directed several projects for Lowe Corporation in Seattle and Los Angeles. From there, he went on to Kailua-Kona, Hawaii to work for the Lyle Anderson Group.

Ian continues to manage real estate projects with his partners and recently purchased the website business that operates www.luxuryhomes.com and www.luxuryrealty.com . Ian enjoys many activities including snow skiing, road biking, surfing, working out and of course time with his family!
