Ed Hattaway's Summary

Since 1988 it has been our aim to provide patients with an alternative to medication and surgery for pain conditions. We strive on a daily basis to listen to our patient's needs and concerns and to respond with effective solutions.

Dr. Ed Hattaway is a 1988 graduate of Life University School of Chiropractic and a member of the Chi Rho Honorary Professional Fraternity. He is a member in good standing of both the American Chiropractic Association and the Georgia Chiropractic Association.

We understand that you place a great deal of trust in your healthcare provider, and our goal is to provide you with a level of care that exceeds your expectations.
