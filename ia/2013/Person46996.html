Garrick Davis' Summary

I have been performing all of my life and writing music since I was 14. I am a singer/songwriter/guitarist catalogue artist and strong live performer. I have made a decent living as a private guitar instructor over the past 5 years but have a strong desire to expand and utilize my ability to communicate through music.

I am seeking financial backing, advice and well connected, experienced music professionals with expertise in the European territories to aid in my goals of building an international audience and career. I have the ability, energy, the hunger and drive and, perhaps most importantly, the need, to see this through to a successful end.

I have three albums, the brand new "Expose Your Self"(2010), "Glass Half Full" (2001) and "The Show" (2005). I I am looking to build a European audience for my music which can be heard at garrickdavis.bandcamp.com/releases.

Specialties

Strong live performance skills, verbal communication(english), prolific songwriter, experience, adaptability

Garrick Davis (Self-employed)

January 2004 – Present (9 years 10 months)

I work with private students of all ages to nurture their confidence and widen their scope of music as a player and listener. The ability to create music is not hard or easy. It does require desire, focus and attention. The reward is in creating something of lasting value.

I am working with students of all ages on the GarageBand software format creating music outside of the loops and ready made samples available. This approach has brought an appreciation for the complexity of music creation, arrangement and the beauty of collaboration.

I hope to bring my style of teaching in a workshop capacity to music students throughout the world as an equal supplement to my career as a performing artist.

Associate Member

2002 – 2009 (7 years)

Owner

Z.E.D. Productions

January 2000 – January 2009 (9 years 1 month)

I established Z.E.D. as a way of thinking outside of the box. I am an artist first but believed it necessary to build a history in order to get my career to the next level.

Under the business name of Z.E.D. Productions I produced my own shows, placing my band as the headliner, released two CD's, one studio and the other a live DVD/CD and learned the ropes of PR. As a result my music was played on a nationally syndicated radio show and I enjoyed the success of playing to full houses without the luxury of management or a record label. I've written articles for industry magazines and have had articles in local newspapers written about my DIY success.
