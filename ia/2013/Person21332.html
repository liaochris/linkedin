Chad Dranchuk's Summary

I currently work as a Web Designer/Developer with a passion and drive to deliver custom solutions to business problems pertaining to getting and keeping businesses on the Web.

I have experience with being part of the overall life-cycle of web project from concept to deployment and have gained a working knowledge of various tools and resources to keep the project moving along.

I have a keen sense of design and use of new technologies when building and maintaining web sites and creating graphics and images for the web.
