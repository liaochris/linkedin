Dragos Bucurenci's Summary

Dragos built his career in Romania engaging all significant players in the media industry in a public awareness campaign that completely changed Romanians' attitudes towards volunteerism and the environment.

His movement for change enlisted the support of thousands of volunteers, social activists, the President and the Prime-Minister, as well as that of the most popular celebrities.

Six years after he started this ongoing campaign, the number of young people across Romania who volunteered for charities increased from 13% to 29% and the percentage of volunteers who worked for environmental causes increased from 8% to 51%.*

In 2008, Esquire magazine featured him on the cover of its Romanian edition as a representative of "15 individuals who innovate, challenge and change the world we live in".

* Gallup 2004, GfK 2010
