Jim DeLung's Summary

James “Jim” DeLung, PhD (ABD), is currently serving with pride as the President & CEO of DeLung.com. As a successful private entrepreneur and public-sector leader, he brings a commitment to the development of effective leadership and inspiration to organizations through an interactive, adult-oriented programs.

Jim's area of expertise is communications/public speaking and the construction of curriculum for advanced/executive training. His greatest passion is creating an open and honest dialogue for leaders and followers to discuss the contemporary generational issues of America. Founder and developer of MI Leadership© (Motivation Idiography©), Jim combines social psychology with classic and contemporary leadership theories to identify motivational preferences through analysis of an individual's generation, race, culture, gender, birth order, and DiSC© Personal Profile test results.

He is a certified facilitator for the FranklinCovey Corporation© regarding 7 Habits of Highly Effective People©, 7 Habits for Law Enforcement©, 7 Habits Maximizer©, Nobility of Policing©, Leadership for Law Enforcement©, and Leadership: Great Leaders, Great Teams, Great Results© programs.

Jim is currently in the dissertation phase for his PhD in Business Administration with emphasis in Organizational Leadership at Northcentral University in Prescott Valley, AZ. Jim obtained his Master of Educational Leadership and Bachelor of Science Criminal Justice degrees from Northern Arizona University in Flagstaff, AZ.
