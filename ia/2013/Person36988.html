Dori Fern's Summary

Digital content strategist and editorial director with experience working in traditional media, integrated marketing, digital agencies, and start-up ventures. Content planning, direction, and execution is informed and influenced by data and metrics, and made valuable by the secret sauce of nuanced context, thoughtful insights and plain old common sense.

As a strategist, I have done content audits, created matrices, governance docs and personas. I have created content frameworks for eCRM programs, devised qualitative, contextual insights against email marketing program metrics that boosted click-thru rates. As an editorial director, I have created editorial calendars, style guides, built and mentored teams, managed budgets and cut costs. And I have managed content strategy and production for the launch of many websites, sometimes on an extremely tight deadline.

I am a culinary school graduate and food writer-editor in addition to my expertise in a diverse assortment of other topic areas.
