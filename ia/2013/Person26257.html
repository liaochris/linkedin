Colette Herrick's Summary

LEADERSHIP COACHING | TRAINING | EXECUTIVE COACH | STRATEGIC FACILITATION

The clients I've served for over a decade, work in complex, dynamic environments; physician and nurse leaders, health care administrators, and C-suite leaders and managers from corporate, non-profit, academic and government sectors.

In partnership with clients, I have the privilege of helping leaders and teams evolve conscious, competent, collaborative leadership, accelerate results and flourish.

My professional background includes over thirty years in diverse clinical and corporate settings, leading teams and projects at the forefront of clinical health care and biotechnology. These rich experiences coupled with extensive training and development, have afforded a strong foundation that I draw on in every project - whether with individuals, teams or large organizations.

It is s joy and privilege to serve as a leadership coach, facilitator, trainer and presenter. When not at work, I find inspiration hiking in beautiful places with my best friend/spouse, Allan, and two dogs Oscar and Ollie.

Specialties: Executive coaching, leadership development, health care, team development, team coaching, collaborative learning and innovation, speaking and seminars, leadership training, facilitation, appreciative inquiry
