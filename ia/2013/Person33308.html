Dean Reeves' Summary

My goal (dream job) is to one day design Key Art for the entertainment industry.

My experience includes managing all phases of print projects, including art direction, design and electronic production, as well as the supervision of printing, creative direction on photography and the sourcing of suppliers and maintenance of vendor relations. I have taken projects from conception to completion and beyond in the form of follow up discussions with clients to ensure that the project has met their objectives.

Specialties

Poster design, brochures, promotional materials, print advertisements, packaging, trade show booths, outdoor advertising and so on.
