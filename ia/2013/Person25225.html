Chris Kranz's Summary

Technical Evangelist and Solutions Architect.

Specialities in storage and virtualisation, assisting customers with their journey to the Cloud and gaining a better understanding of what the hell the Cloud is anyway. Lots of consulting with customers to help them better understand the technologies that are out there and understand what is the best fit and how to adapt their IT systems and business process to gain the most benefits. I have earned a few certifications over the years, NCDA, NCIE (SAN, BR, Security), EMC Proven Professional (NAS Technical Architect), VCP (2,3,4,5), VTSP (3,4,5), VCAP4-DCA, VCAP4-DCD and finally VCDX (3, 4, 5 - number 47). Awarded vExpert in 2012

I love meeting people, and hearing new ideas. Through all aspects I have always been able to convey complex ideas in fairly simple terms. I can make almost any solution work, it's usually not the technology that is the limiting factor. I am often the sales force behind the engagements I am involved in as I help sell our technical competencies and provide our customers with the confidence they need when deploying Enterprise Architectures.

Outside of work, I love my wife (Ellen Helme) and her son (Henry), I love my dog (Indiana Bones), I love my friends, and with the little spare time I have away from these, I cross-train in both Muay Thai and Krav Maga to help keep my physical fitness. I enjoy a very wide variety of music (iTunes Genius doesn't like me), and enjoy having a classic car project (at least one!).
