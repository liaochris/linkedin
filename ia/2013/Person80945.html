Linda Keller's Summary

Linda Keller is a Westport, CT based licensed clinical social worker, licensed marriage and family therapist, and certified collaborative divorce coach with over 30 years of experience working with adults, couples, children and adolescents. It has been her life focus to connect with clients and help them move through challenging life transitions to solutions that work. She is both compassionate and practical and addresses the special needs of professionals, artists, executives and business-owners and their families. She has taught and supervised in graduate programs at Columbia, Fordham and Fairfield Universities, in the undergraduate psychology departments at Sacred Heart University and Housatonic Community College, and in the post-graduate Bristol Hospital Marriage and Family Therapy Training Institute. She has served on the Board of Directors for the Connecticut Association for Marriage and Family Therapy and the Connecticut Council for Non-Adversarial Divorce. Her specialties include psychotherapy with adults, couples, children and adolescents; anxiety, depression, stress reduction, trauma, addictions, social anxiety, conflict resolution, parenting skills, family and divorce mediation, and coaching for couples or individuals during and after divorce.

Linda Keller's Experience

Private Practitioner

Linda A. Keller, LCSW, LMFT

1975 – Present (38 years)

Linda Keller has maintained a private practice in individual, couple and family therapy, parenting skills, conflict resolution, mediation, and divorce and post-divorce coaching for over 30 years. She was also Director of Clinical Services at the New Learning Therapy Center in Westport, a clinician at Silver Hill Hospital in New Canaan and the Stamford Hospital Mental Health Clinic, and a Consultant to the Mid-Fairfield Child Guidance Clinic, Family Services Woodfield, the Orange and New Fairfield, CT School Systems, the Interfaith Housing Association and the Planned Parenthood Federation of America. She is a Level I and II Trained EMDR Clinician and a trained Sand Play Therapist. She is a Diplomate and Clinical Member of the National Association of Social Workers and an Approved Supervisor and Clinical Member of the American Academy for Marriage and Family Therapy. She has had advanced training in both Family and Divorce Mediation and in Collaborative Divorce as well as special training in Marital Mediation. She has been a Special Master for the Connecticut Family Trial Docket and is a Member of the International Academy of Collaborative Professionals. She has also been a trainer of Red Cross mental health volunteers.
