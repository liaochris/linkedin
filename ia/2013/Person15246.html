Bob Block's Summary

30+ years as a transactional attorney and CPA with extensive business, contract, real estate, tax, and healthcare/employment law experience. I also provide litigation and litigation support services in my practice areas.

On behalf of my clients, I have dealt with the 2009 Presidential Inauguration Committee, NBC for the Winter and Summer Olympic Games, DNC for conventions, U.S. Conference of Mayors, Chicago Public Schools, NBA, The Breeders' Cup, Grey Goose, Lufthansa, Marriott, Amstel Light, NY Times and Circuit of the Americas.

Although I have a general transactional and litigation practice, I mainly focus on:

Business and Individual:

-drafting and negotiating of contracts and agreements for all types of business and individual transactions;
-consulting on corporate, partnership, joint venture, limited liability company, not-for-profit organization, and Minority Business Enterprise (MBE) formations and various business issues;

Tax:

-tax consulting and return preparation for business, not-for-profit 501(c)(3) and 501(c)(6) organizations, and individuals;
-representation before the IRS and State of Illinois;
-IRS Sect.1031 and reverse exchanges.

Real Estate:

-property tax appeals;
-residential and commercial real estate closings;
-condominium conversions;
-vertical subdivisions;
-shopping center, office, and land development;
-drafting and negotiation of contracts, agreements and leases for residential and commercial development;
-structuring of real estate transactions;
-construction and loan documentation;
-foreclosures and foreclosure avoidance strategies; short sales, and mortgage workouts;
-mechanics liens and building violations;
-governmental hearings;
-court appearances.

Healthcare/Employment:

-False Claims Act/Qui Tam Actions;
-Medicare Fraud;
-employment contracts for physician assistants;

Specialties: Business, contract, real estate, tax, and healthcare/employment law; importing; litigation support; expert witness; accounting services
