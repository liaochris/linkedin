Joan Sotkin's Summary

As a Business and Money Coach, I offer business owners and professional practitioners innovative solutions for building a successful business and improving their relationship with money.

Coaching includes one-to-one sessions and groups. A unique combination of integrated assessments are used to analyse critical thinking, internal motivators, and communication style. These, along with a proprietary system for dealing with internal blocks, allow my clients to discover and release long-standing issues without therapy.

The Prosperity Show Podcast that I host offers a holistic approach to business and financial success. Listen on www.ProsperityPlace.com or via iTunes or Stitcher.

Via www.ProsperityPlace.com, I offer frequent teleclasses and webinars designed to provide information and support for overcoming non-productive financial habits such as overspending, underearning, chronic debting, and the discomfort of financial uncertainty.

My coaching clients enjoy a safe, confidential environment where they can develop business and financial skills so they can earn more doing work that is congruent with their true life expression.

My book, Build Your Money Muscles: Nine Simple Exercises for Improving Your Relationship with Money won two major awards and has helped thousands find their way to financial sanity. The book has been lauded for its information about the connection between money and emotions as well as its practical approach to basic money management.
