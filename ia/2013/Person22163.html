Charles Carson's Summary

The Charles L. Carson Law Offices is a small boutique law firm providing legal representation to persons and businesses in cases with significant injury or financial damage.

Our clients have included working persons and their families, small businesses, public entities, multi-national and federally chartered corporations.

An injury can be physical, such as concussion, fracture, birth defects, cancer, or death. A physical injury can be caused in many ways, including by a defective product, a defective drug or medical device, medical malpractice, a work-place or construction site incident, a vacation or sporting event, a motor vehicle accident, an aircraft or ship incident, a slip and fall accident, and a death caused by such an injury.

An injury can be emotional, such as severe emotional distress, depression, or post-traumatic stress disorder. An emotional injury can be caused in the same incident that caused physical injury. An emotional injury may also be caused by intentional infliction of emotion distress, false arrest, employment discrimination and abuse.

An injury can be financial, such as fraud and deceit. Financial injury may be caused by consumer fraud and misrepresentation, unfair business practices, defamation of character, denial of civil rights, a wrongful denial of insurance benefits or defective construction.

We are proud to have successfully resolved hundreds of cases involving significant injuries and damages.

We offer contingent fee arrangements in appropriate cases, so we do not receive a fee until we obtain a settlement of judgment. Fixed fee arrangements are also available. Referrals from other law offices are invited, subject to State Bar rules.

From our offices in Hawai’i and California we have represented clients throughout Hawai’i and California, and associate with other law firms to serve clients across the U.S.

Please contact us now at the Charles L. Carson Law Offices by email or telephone for a free consultation.

Charles Carson's Experience

Lawyer

Law Offices of Charles L. Carson

Law Practice industry

1984 – Present (29 years)

For over 28 years Charles Carson has handled cases with significant injuries and damages, and received jury verdicts in excess of $1,000,000. Clients have included working persons and their families, small businesses, public entities, multi-national and federally chartered corporations.

After partnership in a large top-50 California law firm, Charles opened a small boutique law firm devoted to providing legal representation to those injured in cases with significant personal injury or financial damage. Since he handles all cases personally, his caseload is limited to those who he can help the most. Cases are accepted on a contingent fee basis, so the client does not owe a fee until the firm obtains a settlement or judgment.

Charles received a Bachelor’s degree from Arizona State University in 1966, and a Juris Doctor degree from Whittier College Law School in 1984, with honors. He completed the Pepperdine University School of Law program for mediation of lawsuits.

He is licensed to practice law before all state and federal courts in Hawai’i and California, before the United States Court of Appeals for the Ninth Circuit, and before the United States Supreme Court, in Washington, D.C.

Charles is a member of the American Board of Trial Advocates, the American Association for Justice, the Consumer Attorneys of California, the National Trial Lawyers, and the Million Dollar Advocates Forum.

He is a mediator for many California Superior Courts. He has served as a privately retained arbitrator in numerous cases and for the American Arbitration Association. He is an arbitrator in the Hawai’i Circuit Court arbitration program.

Prior to practicing law, Charles was in the radio and television industry and a member of the American Federation of Television and Radio Artists (AFTRA). He holds FAA Airline Transport pilot and flight instructor certificates, and has over 4000 flight hours.
