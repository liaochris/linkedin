Brian Gross' Summary

Well I don't know if they say business people are born or developed, but I have always had a interest in being involved in and learning as much as I can from the business world. I have had the opportunity of learning and growing my knowledge from a variety of business models. Learning about the specialty coffee business 1st hand from one of the largest distributors in the U.S. was a great way to learn about a business that's full of tradition, craftsmanship, and culture. Working in Real Estate helped me to manage my time, priorities, and made me a better sales person. It also taught me about running a business and being an entrepreneur. Living in the U.S. Virgin Islands while working for a resort and casino, was one of the best character building experiences for me as I learned about management, customer service and gave me a different perspective on culture. It also pushed me to take more risk and achieve dreams. My position in sales and as a trainer has taught me how to help others through knowledge I have gained and is one I never stop learning from. My current position is a culmination of these previous experiences paired with a socially responsible look at the business world,& making peoples lives richer and healthier. Natural Nutrition.

My goals for the future are really quite lofty, but none the less I feel very achievable. I'm going to create a coffee and wine company, with a focus on sustainable practices. (did you know coffee is the 2nd most chemically treated commodity after cotton?) Profits from that will help me to design and own an 18 hole championship golf course, and in line with that I would like to pair that with a hotel and casino. With my background I know the importance of being involved with real estate, so I want to build a large portfolio of holdings. I have had a taste of all of these different business's and can honestly say that I love them. I also know how profitable they can be. I guess we'll leave it there for now.
