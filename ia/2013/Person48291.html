Gerson Bradford's Summary

Directing, compositing, story telling, digital film making, editing, 3D modeling, videography, visual effects, motion graphics, sound engineering, and color correction are my life passion. While many people concentrate in one area, with the world changing so rapidly, my view is much broader. In my experience, versatility is my key. Nevertheless, if I have to pick one area, it would be compositing.
I've developed my skills to a professional level in all of these areas. I've worked on everything from script writing, to tracking, using programs such as Boujou, Camera Tracker and Mocha Pro.

My experience includes filming entirely on green screen, with rampaging computer generated creatures, car modeling, 3D environments, directing short films, commercial, and music videos.
The overall goal is always the same; to attain a higher professional level while keeping my knowledge on the cutting edge. "I can only get better everyday.

I have a confession to make. I AM ADDICTED TO LEARNING! Every free minute I have, I find a way to learn something new. I can't help it. My name is Gerson Bradford and I am addicted to visual media.

My professional services are available in both English,Spanish. I speak Italian and French as well.
