Jeffrey Sweeney's Summary

Why is entrepreneurial experience so important? Not only is it a great character builder but it also provides a first hand understanding of the challenges and opportunities faced by our customers and clients. With over 20 years of experience owning and growing a lower middle market aerospace manufacturer with a successful exit I know the commitment it takes to be successful in operations. I am dedicated to helping people and companies succeed and I bring this commitment to my current work in small cap and lower middle market lending and investment banking. I find it to be incredibly rewarding to help build sound companies and help good companies become even greater.

In addition to my work running our closely held investment bank and credit fund, I serve on the Board of Directors of several educational and service organizations and enjoy educating and speaking about macro and micro economics of banking to entrepreneurs and professionals in the United States and internationally.

Jeffrey Sweeney's Experience

CEO and Managing Director

US Capital Partners

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Investment Banking industry

1997 – Present (16 years) San Francisco Bay Area

I currently serve as Managing Director and CEO of US Capital Partners, LLC, and am a Principal and Director of Breakwater Investment Management, LLC. US Capital Partners provides investment and merchant banking services by providing asset-backed loans and equity for business acquisitions, equipment leases, recapitalization and commercial real estate. An innovator in small to lower-middle market business lending, US Capital Partners has been providing prompt and reliable financing solutions across the United States and globally for over a decade. Breakwater Investment Management funds and manages our loan portfolio.

Managing Partner

Breakwater Investment Management, LLC

January 2008 – Present (6 years) Greater Los Angeles Area

Breakwater Investment Management is a private investment firm that specializes in direct investments in small to lower middle market businesses ranging in annual sales of $5 million to $100 million. The firm serves as general partner of Breakwater Structured Growth Opportunities Fund, LP, a $100 million open-ended private investment partnership.

Principal

Machine Works Corporation

1979 – 1994 (15 years)

From 1979 to 1994, I served as a Principal and CEO of Machine Works Corporation, a lower middle-market aerospace manufacturing company.
