Jeff Uken, CFA's Summary

What I do: I help my clients manage their long-term currency and non-domestic interest rate risk. My current role includes marketing coverage of several regional FX offices, Business Banking Group (BBG), Global Financial Institutions (GFI), Correspondent Banking, and Wealth Management Group (WMG). In addition to my role at Wells Fargo, I also am an adjunct professor for USF's MS in Financial Analysis program (I've previously taught fixed income analysis), the editor of the CFASF Financial Standard (CFASF's newsletter that boasts a circulation of 3,200 investment professionals), and a student in the EWMBA program at Haas School of Business.

Goals:
1. I want to continue being successful helping my clients manage their risks effectively.
2. I want to provide a first-class financial education to my students and help them succeed.
3. I want to push the Financial Standard to be the de facto publication for the Bay Area finance professional.
4. I want to improve my ability to provide value through my MBA studies at Haas.

Specialties

Interest rate derivatives, currency derivatives, risk management, fixed income, teaching, and journalistic writing.
