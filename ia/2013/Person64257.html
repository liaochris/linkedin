Jing Yan's Summary

My name is Jing Yan,originally from Beijing, China.

After receiving a BA degree in Communications from Southwest Jiaotong University, I started my career in advertsing and marketing industry. In order to learn more advanced knowledge and achieve my dream, I came to US to pursue my MFA degree at Rochester Institute of Technology in NY state.

Going through two years studying at RIT, I gained several design skills, which include HTML5, CSS3, Photoshop, Illustrator, Indesign, Flash and After Effect. User interaction design and web design are my concentrations. Joining in a great team which is specialized on interaction design and web design after graduation is my short term goal.
