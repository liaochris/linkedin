Denesh Bhabuta's Summary

Making a difference! Giving the human touch to any management, operations and change within an organisation, bringing out the best in people. An increase in productivity and efficiency is inevitable without any demoralising effects of the greatest asset a company has - its staff.

I am a people person. I am a networker. An enabler. I am an "opener" as well as a "completer". I love creativity and enthusiasm and in turn I love bringing creativity to my customers projects and events.

The experience gained has been from the boiler room, through management, finance and all the way to Chairman and CEO. I thus have a multitude of skills which are applied wherever appropriate.
