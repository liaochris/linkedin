Bob Trottier's Projects

  • IsiQiri Q Frame product ( Click here to see video )

    • June 2012 to February 2013
    Team Members: Bob Trottier, The Engineering teams at IsiQiri, and the Design Team at Tekbox

    The Q-Frame is a Frame which can be added to a large format display to add touch capability of up to 5 simultaneous touches.
    The Q Frame consist of an extruded aluminum frame of about 1/2" X 1" Cross-section which contains all the electronics and optic components.The Frame can be made a variety of sizes to accommodate varying sizes of displays.
    It employs 4 lasers in the corners and sensors along the inner perimeter to record shadows cast by objects within the frame.
    4 Microprocessors analyze and save the shadow information ( one per frame side) and a dedicated Blacklfin Processor merges this data and calculates the touch points to present them to the Windows interface via a USB cable . Power is also provided by the USB cable.
    The video shown is a 55" monitor running under Windows 8 with Q Frame attached

    • Issued May 8, 2012
