Kim Siebold's Summary

I am a senior graphic and production designer from Minneapolis, Minnesota. I bring 20 years of ad agency and creative firm experience working with both large and small brands - everything from B2B and retail to start-up companies. My specialties include design, illustration, and production. I work in both print and digital.

I do what I love and love what I do. Design is not only my profession, it is my passion.

Senior Graphic and Production Designer

Kim Siebold Studios

2001 – Present (12 years) Lakeville, Minnesota

I bring 20 years of ad agency and creative firm experience working with both large and small brands—everything from B2B and retail to start-up companies. I partner with various ad agencies, creative firms, and business owners to provide creative solutions that stay on brand and budget. From concept to execution, I can deliver!

With almost a decade of creative management and leadership experience under my belt, I have the ability and desire to see the big picture of a project including team roles and function. My passion for design combined with production detail allows me to work efficiently and seamlessly between creative tasks.

Several of my recent corporate clients have been Franke + Fiorella, Campbell Mithun, Archway and Caribou Coffee. My work is featured across multiple campaigns including branding, identity, packaging, collateral, special event materials, direct mail, banners, web design, social media and more.

I also specialize in illustration. My style is whimsical, both hand-drawn and digital. I currently have seven published books: Starry Night, Hold Me Tight and ABC Now You See Me, published by Running Press (Perseus), a series of five educational books from the Wonder Series (Kids for Thought) as well as two digital books in the works. Other current illustration projects include collaborating with small companies to create educational products, home decor and brand identity. My illustration portfolio can be viewed at www.kimsieboldstudios.com.
