David Atkinson's Experience

Writer, blogger and lecturer

Freelance Writer and Copywriter; Lecturer in Multimedia Journalism; Visit Wales guest blogger

Public Company; Myself Only; Writing and Editing industry

February 2012 – Present (1 year 7 months) Chester & Wales, United Kingdom

I currently write freelance for national publications, covering travel, tourism and education. I recently won the Golden Pen award from the Croatian National Tourist Office for the best international travel feature (2012).
I'm also teaching a .5 contract at Glyndwr University, Wrexham, where I work on the NCTJ-accredited journalism programme. I teach on the BA Hons undergrduate programme too, specialising in multimedia journalism and the freelance business.
This year I've been working with international tourist boards as a copywriter for print and online. Recent projects include writing the Northern Ireland 2012 tourism supplement for Tourism Ireland and a guest blogger for Visit Wales around the opening of the Wales Coast Path.
