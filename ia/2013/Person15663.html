Bonita Parker's Summary

I received my B.S. in Business Administration and Human Resources from the University of Maryland University College. I possess extensive business management and marketing insight, as well as over 15 years of experience planning and coordinating meetings and social events.

As a hobby, I familiarized myself with the ins and outs of project planning by successfully organizing and coordinating a multitude of weddings and related events, including my own wedding; and has always dreamed of turning my passion and favorite pastime into a career. My goal is to ensure that Elegant Exposé is represented by seasoned professionals who will enhance the image of the company and continually add to its success.

Specialties

I have expertise in vendor negotiations, contract management and negotiations, project management, budget preparations, and a keen eye for detail.

Additionally, I have a passion for people! I enjoy meeting new people and making them happy. I possess an amiable disposition which makes me the perfect planner of any event!
