Colin Cradock's Summary

I have worked internationally on both short and long term projects and events from planning stage to finished production.

Over the 30+ years of my television broadcast industry experience, I have been involved in most major international events worldwide; working for the majority of renowned broadcasters and production companies throughout the world in positions of responsibility ranging from engineer in charge, technical manager, unit manager, venue technical manager through to consultant.

I have exceptional Organisational skills and competences: Having been responsible for crews exceeding 100 people; I like to practice excellent project and team management.

As well as working on the past four Olympic Games, two Rugby World Cups and FIFA World Cups, I have also been involved in yachting (Americas Cup), Motorsport and numerous musical events. In addition, I have covered topics of international importance and sensitivity.

I have built an excellent reputation and am known for my professionalism and my adaptability to fit in with any organisation and set up, whether in the UK or anywhere in the world. This reputation has led to many repeat requests from clients. Such reputation has also enabled me to travel and work with politicians and Heads of State.

If I can be of assistance with your projects please contact me. I look forward to hearing from you.
