Paul Ivsin's Summary

Strategic planner focused on improving the drug development process through rigorous data collection and analysis. Focused on clinical trials, especially in the realm of engaging investigators to accelerate enrollment.

Specialties

Clinical trial strategy and planning, physician communications, protocol feasibility, regulatory interaction and navigation, technology assessment, data collection, analytics

Paul Ivsin's Experience

VP, Consulting Director

CAHG

Public Company; 201-500 employees; OMC; Marketing and Advertising industry

July 2009 – Present (4 years 4 months)

Provide strategic consulting, analysis, and enrollment planning for pharmaceutical clinical trials, with a special emphasis on difficult-to-enroll trials. Major responsibilities include: develop custom enrollment strategies for new trials, lead analysis of prior enrollment performance to recommend targeted rescue solutions, and engage trial investigators to determine the most direct route to successful trial execution.

Serve as an expert consultant to brand marketing and advertising teams by providing clinical development and pipeline context.
