Vernita Naylor's Summary

What is important:
1) Improving supplier diversity and inclusion goals for both the supplier and buyer.
2) Empowering underrepresented businesses through education and marketing efforts.
3) Helping underrepresented businesses build capacity.
4) Helping underrepresented businesses develop synergy.
5) Helping underrepresented businesses build relationships through supplier diversity and inclusion goals.
6) Helping underrepresented businesses create partnerships with government entities and corporations.
7) Matching qualified underrepresented businesses with government and corporate opportunities.

We educate underrepresented or disadvantaged business owners in government contracting and business development services. Our services are distributed through several means from consulting, workshops//seminars/webinars, panels, online, and motivational speaking to articles.
