Eric Seufert's Publications

  • Freemium Economics

    • Morgan Kaufmann
    • January 30, 2013
    Authors: Eric Seufert

    A technical, data-oriented guide to implementing the Freemium business model in software products. Describes the economic principles behind freemium, the data-driven, iteration-based feedback loop needed in the development process, and the instrumentation required to collect the metrics needed to adequately segment users and maximize revenue. The book provides thorough analysis of Virality (including a step-by-step guide to modeling viral growth), Lifetime Customer Value (including a guide to building a spreadsheet template), Analytics implementation, and User Acquisition.

    Freemium Economics will be published by Morgan Kaufmann in January 2014.
