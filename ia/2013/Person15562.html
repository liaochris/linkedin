Body Benefits' Summary

“Total Body Listening” is what separates Joanne Freedman from other body therapists and practitioners. Joanne specializes in an array of intuitive therapies to help correct imbalances and heal complaints in her clients.

Each person is different. She does not use a cookie cutter approach. Her intuitive knowledge hones in on your trouble spots. Then, she employs specialized therapies to help your body release pain, physical distress, knots and aches. She can also help ease long-term trauma and relieve tender muscles with relaxing treatments that facilitate detoxification, improve flexibility and mental clarity.

Her bodywork includes Acupressure, Deep Tissue Massage, Myofascial Release, Reflexology, Reiki, Craniosacral Therapy, Lymphatic Drainage, Chakra and Auric Balancing, among other things.

Body Benefits is a sanctuary for women interested in exploring the root cause behind the pain in their bodies and for those open to new pathways for healing and balance.
