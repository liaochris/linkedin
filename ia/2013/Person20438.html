Carole Rose's Summary

First licensed as an agent in December 1972, I became a Broker just two years later. After earning 'Top Producer' status I became a Sales Trainer. I had no clue I could teach, but tried it anyway.

The results were amazing (especially to me!). At the end of two years there were protege's who opened their own offices and many were top producers. 70% were still succeeding in the business. That was 7 TIMES THE NATIONAL AVERAGE. Then mortgage interest rates hit 24% and the real estate industry tanked. I took a sabbatical and tried a few network marketing opportunities.

In 1986 I started over and was a 'TOP PRODUCER' the first year.

From 1994-2008 I was an Award-winning Remodeling Contractor and kitchen and bath designer.

You may remember me as a Sales and Mindset Coach for 5 years.

In January 2013 I decided to become a Broker Consultant with Keller Williams Silicon Valley. I am having a ball helping Baby Boomers and Traditionals (born pre-1946) with all their real estate needs,.

I also LOVE to help FIRST TIME HOME BUYERS find their dream home. It's like protecting my own grandchildren. (Oh my, in a few years they could be buying!)

In between Boomers and Millenniums? No problem, I welcome you too! You'll get the same honest, empathetic care.
