Louise Myers' Summary

Graphic design is my passion! I’ve been designing for many years, and still love designing every day. Now I want to bring the joy of design to YOU!

Who are you? Maybe you’re a small business owner who’s trying to get some traction with social media. You have a creative bent, and you’d like to create your own social media graphics, but don’t know quite where to begin.

Or you’re a solopreneur who desires an active Facebook Fan page, but doesn’t have the budget to hire a graphic designer to set it up.

Maybe you’re a graphic designer who wants to branch out into social media design. Or, a social media marketing expert wanting to help your clients (and yourself!) with graphics!

Our world is powered by social media graphics. Social media is the method, and graphics are the message, by which we communicate today.

Social media graphics – photos and other visuals – have become our default mode of sorting and understanding the vast amounts of information we're exposed to every day.

Businesses can use social media graphics to increase engagement and inspire sharing and viral marketing. The rise of platforms like Pinterest and Instagram shows how social media graphics are becoming an increasingly important force for communication online. Even Twitter is becoming visual, with their recent redesign, and apps like TwitPic and Vine!

If you can rock social media graphic design, you can be a market leader! Make your own graphics, and let your prospects do your marketing for you, via social media sharing.

Here’s what you can learn from me about social media graphic design:

✔ How to create a professional and engaging Facebook Fan Page
✔ How to make your own graphics to get shared on Facebook
✔ How to create the best Pinterest pictures to get repins
✔ How to set up a great-looking Twitter page
✔ How to make picture quotes to share on social media
✔ Visual social media strategy tips
