April M. Chaney's Summary

April Chaney is an entrepreneur and celebrity makeup artist based in Los Angeles, CA. With a career spanning over 15 years, April is a skilled professional, whose artistry, talent and depth of experience is unmatched in the beauty industry.

April is known for creating beautiful makeup transformations that enhance the beauty of her clients. In 2008, April began assisting Heather Currie and Collier Strong from the prestigious Cloutier Agency. She has worked on editorials for O Magazine, on Entertainment Tonight, seasonal editorials for the GAP, promos & editorials for the Real Housewives of Orange County and in numerous other TV shows, films, commercials, magazine editorials and on the runway in fashion shows. Since Fall 2010 She has been doing Makeup and men's grooming on the hit reality show on WETV "My Fair Wedding" with David Tutera.

In her distinguished retail career, April worked with numerous cosmetic companies, including Nars, Laura Mercier, MAC, Giorgio Armani Beauty, and Dolce and Gabbana Makeup. With her knowledge of the cosmetic industry and years of hands-on experience, April is poised to take the cosmetics industry by storm with her makeup service company Lirpamakeup L.A. (April spelled backwards)
