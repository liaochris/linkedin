Billy Holcombe's Summary

Over the past decade, Billy Holcombe has developed and executed successful marketing and public relations strategies for clients across multiple industries, including residential real estate, healthcare and hospitality. His vision, creativity, and ability to recognize emerging trends has helped clients stay ahead of the competition.

As Social Media Specialist at Thomas Eye Group, Billy leads and manages the social media and digital strategies for the company and its nine greater Atlanta area locations. Prior to joining Thomas Eye Group, Billy served as Director of Social Media for Liz Lapidus Public Relations where he quickly evolved the firm’s social media presence and dramatically increased the “fan base” for several hotels and restaurants in Atlanta.

Before shifting his career focus to social media, Billy held various marketing management roles at some of Atlanta's top real estate companies including The Marketing Directors (Director of Marketing- 2007 & 2008), Coldwell Banker The Condo Store (Marketing Director- 2005 & 2006) and Coldwell Banker Residential Brokerage (Marketing Coordinator 2000- 2005).

When he’s not working, Billy has a passion for the local dining scene which led him to create a restaurant blog - RestaurantsATL.com - in 2009. The blog and associated twitter account (@restaurantsatl) has been featured in Creative Loafing, Eater Atlanta and other local media outlets.

Billy is a graduate of Georgia Southern University where he earned a Bachelor of Business Administration Degree in Marketing in 2000. An Atlanta native, Billy resides in Sandy Springs with his wife, Cristi Holcombe and son, Chase.

Specialties: marketing, communications, social media, public relations, advertising, media relations, digital media.
