Greg Dosmann's Summary

Interactive designer and “brand chemist” bringing ideas to life through a digital blend of art and technology. A strong creative acumen with extensive brand development experience in interactive, experiential, and traditional media.

Award-winning facilitator of innovative design solutions optimized for digital tactics that consistently align with brand strategy, elevate brand equity, and evoke a quality user-experience.

Accomplished leader of interdisciplinary teams with the capacity to successfully manage simultaneous creative projects within a fast-paced environment. Provide creative direction in a collaborative, team-oriented atmosphere from ideation through implementation.
