Nick Lamb, PhD's Summary

Freelance medical copywriter with more than 12 years' experience writing for the pharmaceutical, healthcare and medical device industries.

>> 07941 892694
>> nick@medcopywriter.com
>> www.medcopywriter.com

I can write a wide range of materials for any audience on any therapy area. Recent projects I've worked on include:

- Writing a website for UK healthcare professionals about ADHD (www.fullattention.co.uk)
- Developing a range of materials for the pan-European launch of a new ADHD drug
- Writing a style guide on best practice medical writing for the web for a global pharmaceutical company
- Writing a website on flu for patients and healthcare professionals (www.flu-protect.co.uk)
- Branding and creative development for the global launch of a new cardiac resynchronisation therapy device (CRT-D) and leads
- Writing a discussion guide about breakthrough cancer pain (BTcP)
- A series of doctor/patient materials for the European launch of a new cystic fibrosis treatment
- A slide deck on microvascular complications in diabetes
- A mailer for pharmacists about generic substitution of anti-epileptic drugs (AEDs)
- Prostate cancer websites for patients and healthcare professionals
- Proofreading a thesis on abdominal aortic aneurysms (AAAs)
- Interactive e-Learning programmes on ovarian, breast and prostate cancer
- Ad copy, core claims and leavepieces for a pneumococcal vaccine
- An e-Detail on topical NSAIDs
- A sales aid for a rheumatoid arthritis biologic
- An e-Detail on cancer-associated venous thromboembolism (VTE)
- Website copy for a European pharma company
- Launch materials for a new immunotherapy treatment for metastatic melanoma
- Detail aids on HIV, psoriasis and statin treatments
- Website copy on anti-inflammatories
- Ad copy for a new ulcerative colitis treatment

Member of the European Medical Writers Association and the Association of British Science Writers.
