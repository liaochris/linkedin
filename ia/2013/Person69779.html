Jason Caldwell's Summary

Hi! I'm a UX Designer specializing in web design, graphic design and data visualization.

My work has appeared in Mashable, The Atlantic, Fast Company, Business Insider, PCWorld, Forbes, ReadWriteWeb and KISSmetrics (among other publications), as well as in the Amazon.com best seller "Infographics: The Power of Visual Storytelling" (Wiley, 2012).

Skills:

- Very proficient in data visualization and infographics. Strong ability to present complex information quickly and clearly.

- Very proficient with creative publishing software -- the Adobe Creative Suite (InDesign, Photoshop, Illustration), Inkscape, etc. Strong understanding of color theory, typography and visual hierarchy.

- Very proficient with client-side web technologies such as HTML and CSS. Familiar with scripting technologies such as client-side JavaScript.

- Proficient with server-side web technologies such as Ruby and PHP, and content management systems such as Wordpress and Textpattern.

- Proficient in product development -- speaking first-hand with end-users about how they're interacting with the product, frustrations they're having with the product, etc.

- Proficient in product marketing and lead generation -- email collection, webinars, blogging, content marketing, copywriting, SEO and SEM.

- Proficient in front-line customer support, troubleshooting problems, identifying upsell opportunities.

- Proficient with web analytics, A/B testing, and optimization platforms (KISSmetrics, Google Analytics, Optimizely, Visual Website Optimizer, Unbounce).

- Familiar with digital animation and related technologies (Adobe Edge Animate, Adobe Flash, HTML5).
