Bill Milling's Summary

Bill Milling is the owner of the American Movie Company which maintains fully equipped green screen sound stage, triplex webcasting facility in Tribeca, Manhattan as well as webcasting, TriCaster, Interrotron and teleprompter services in both NYC and Hollywood and soon in Miami.

The studios rent to other professionals and serve as a base for our many in-house productions.

You can visit us on the web at:

www.AmericanMovieCo.com
www.TeleprompterRental.com
www.TriCasterRentals.com
www.GreenScreenNewYork.com
www.InterrotronRental.com

Specialties: We specialize in green screen technology with a concentration on TriCaster WebCasting in studio, on location and wirelessly.

Our 3D digital artists create photo-real virtual sets and special effects.

We have a rental department for great deals on Arri Alexa, Red Camera, TriCasters and Teleprompters.

We are creators, producers & distributors of cutting edge HD web content.

Bill Milling's Experience

Owner

American Movie Company

Public Company; 1-10 employees; Entertainment industry

1996 – Present (17 years)

Bill developed the American Movie Company from its origins as a creator of low budget feature films (He wrote, produced and/or directed over twenty) and while production executive on major Hollywood features such as "Lucky Number Sleven" to a full-service, national organization that maintains facilities on all three coasts: New York, LA and Miami.

The company has provided services to over a hundred Network Television shows and series : "Friends", "WIll & Grace", "Mad About You" and "How I Met Your Mother", etc.

AMC has its major green screen webcasting facility at 81 Walker Street in Tribeca, Manhattan. It's a triplex with the ground floor sound stage 55' X 35' with 15' ceilings. AMC is a major player in digital compositing, video production, post, VFX, TriCaster WebCasting and Teleprompter Services.

Producer/Director

Atlantic Film & Television

April 1978 – 1995 (17 years) 600 Broadway, NY, NY 10012

At Atlantic, Bill began a ten year career run as a writer, Producer and Director of feature film and TV projects at Atlantic Film & Television. Within three years, he bought the company. He was the producer/show runner and one of the writers on the NBC hit Children"s TV series, "Unicorn Tales". The show won seventeen Emmy Awards and the prestigious Children's Television Award.
He was a writer in the CBS network TV series "In Tune"
