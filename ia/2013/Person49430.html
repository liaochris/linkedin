Gonzalo Duarte's Summary

Of Canadian-Spanish heritage, Gonzalo was born in Toronto and graduated from McMaster (B.A.), Waterloo (Dip.PACS.), and Queen’s (B.Ed.) universities. As the President & Director of Compañeros Inc he recently relocated from Managua to Toronto | Ottawa to participate in Canada's first Master’s of Philanthropy and Non-Profit Leadership degree at Carleton University.

With 24 years professional experience catalysing cross-cultural volunteer programs and community development projects in various countries, Gonzalo manages and directs a growing social enterprise that effectively facilitates people enriching their own and other peoples' lives through collaborative participation in infrastructure, education, health, and community improvement projects in Canada and Nicaragua.
