Juan Zane Crawford's Summary

Since 2005, I have submitted more than 375 HUD bids in www.bidselect.com. I have experienced successful bids approximately 145 times. Everywhere I go throughout the real estate industry I am considered “Mr. HUD Expert.” I have mentored countless other REALTORS® on the interweaving of the HUD process. Once I explain the process, I frequently hear “None ever told us this before, why are you sharing this with us?” My reply is always the same. I tell them that individuals will subscribe to either of the following philosophies: abundance or scarcity. If you subscribe to a scarcity thought philosophy, then you will see resources as scarce and you will hold on tightly to what you have, guarding against others finding how you do what you do to have success. I reject this philosophy. I subscribe to the abundance philosophy. God put more than enough down here for us all to be successful. My philosophy has always been “If you share all you know with those you know abundance will always be there.”
