Judith Kolberg's Publications

  • Organize for Disaster: Prepare Your Family and Your Home for Any Natural or Unnatural Disaster

    • Squall Press
    Authors: Judith Kolberg

    Learn how to get organized so your family will be prepared and as safe as possible during any disaster. Interesting to read, profoundly informative, and very practical to use.

  • ADD-Friendly Ways to Organize Your Life

    • Squall Press
    Authors: Judith Kolberg, Dr. Kathleen Nadeau

    Offers organizing solutions that provide long-lasting, life changing organizing success for adults with ADD.

  • What Every Professional Organizer Needs to Know About Hoarding

    • Squall Press
    Authors: Judith Kolberg

    For professional organizers, social workers, psychologists, first-responders and others who encounter people who hoard. Describes hoarding's characteristics and causes, and the approaches to address emotional and organizational needs of compulsive hoarders.

  • Conquering Chronic Disorganization

    • Squall Press
    Authors: Judith Kolberg

    Groundbreaking organizing methods proven to end chronic disorganization presented in a fun-to-read, easy-to-implement format.

  • What Every Professional Organizer Needs to Know About Chronic Disorganization

    • Squall Press
    Authors: Judith Kolberg

    An indispensible resource for professional organizers and other professionals who want to explore new and innovative organizaing techniques proven to be effective with chronically disorganized people.

  • Sync or Swim: 201 Organizing Tips You Need to Survive the Currents of Change

    • Squall Press
    Authors: Judith Kolberg

    We live in a world of technology marvels, but it hasn’t necessarily meant less to do. We still have to toss out the trash from last night’s dinner but now we have to trash electronic files, shred sensitive information, and block spam. We’ve got gadgets and tools to be more productive but unless we sync them religiously, we’re sunk. Sync or Swim: 201 Organizing Tips You Need to Survive the Currents of Change is a 93-page ebook that puts the best next-generation organizing tips all in one place.

  • Getting Organized in the Era of Endless: What to do When Information, Interruption, Work and Stuff are Endless But Time Is Not

    • Squall Press
    • January 2013
    Authors: Judith Kolberg

    We live in the era of endless, confronted by infinite information, incessant interruptions, constant distractions, unending work, and unbounded stuff. All this ‘endlessness’ butts up against the one thing that remains intractably finite: time. In this book you will find simple and effective organizing strategies and solutions specifically designed for the Era of Endless. Manage the excesses and downside of endless information, interruption, work and stuff! And reclaim your time!
