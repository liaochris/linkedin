Kimberly Bilawchuk's Summary

Are you a young professional stuck in a job between "just OK" and "totally sucks"? Maybe it pays you well, maybe you’re really good at it. Maybe it allows you to live a lifestyle you enjoy. Maybe it’s what everyone expected you to do after you finished your education. But you dream of doing something different one day. Maybe you have a clear vision of what that dream job is, maybe you don’t have any idea. I’ve been there, I know!

My clients have great jobs, but hate them, or just feel stuck. They live for Fridays and dread Sundays, knowing that their weekends are coming to an end and they’ll have to go back to work on Monday. Does this sound like you?

I want to help you find your dream career! I know you have a better career in you! I’ve been there, I know how terrible it is. I was stuck in a series of less than satisfying jobs for several years. I was good at many of them, but there was no challenge for me, nothing to get out of bed in the morning. Sure, I was making good money and driving a nice car, but something was missing. It took a layoff for me to really evaluate what I wanted to do with my career.

Of course, it’s easier to stay where you are. Don’t wait another year for something magic to come along. Take action today now!

Contact me today for a complimentary Kickstart Your Career Strategy Session. Go to my website now and fill out the application at www.kbcareercoach.com/kickstart. I can’t wait to help get you started! What are you waiting for?
