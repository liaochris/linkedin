Adam Youngers' Projects

  • eNom Redesign 2012

    • December 2011 to January 2012
    Team Members: Adam Youngers, Ken Clark, Casey Coates, Kimberly Koskenmaki, Joey Lappin, Nicholas Aluia, Arlen Yan, Grant Jaquish, Marcin Narozny, Don Siebers

    Part redesign and part site optimization this project was a success on both fronts. The eNom code base has been around for almost 15 years and as part of this project, I lead efforts to locate and remove 2000+ deprecated files. The site's CSS had also grown bloated over the years swelling to over 100+k. As part of the redesign I put together a plan to progressively widen the site developing the style guide and updated look and feel for the site while shrinking the CSS down to approx 11K. Along with some updates from the development team we managed to take the site from a C- to and A.

  • Namejet Responsive Redesign

    • May 2011 to November 2011
    Team Members: Adam Youngers, Nicholas Aluia, Chirag Patel

    The NameJet redesign was a great learning experience for me as it was my first large scale venture into responsive design. After researching all the new features availabe in CSS3 and learning how to apply them using progressive enhancement, I brought the idea of making NameJet scalable to management. With everyone onboard, the result has been a beautiful new site with greater accessibility for all and a much improved user experience. An auction site like this was just begging to go mobile! You can read a great review of the site at... http://goo.gl/DbjSP

  • Instant Reseller

    • July 2009 to December 2009
    Team Members: Adam Youngers, Amin Taheri, Frank Roh

    Instant Reseller is a white label domain registration and related services online storefront available to eNom's reseller base.

    I lead the design efforts to build this 300+ page themeable web application. It pulled heavily from existing layouts on eNom, but each was analyzed and optimized to be more efficient and easier to understand.

    Working heavily with the development team, I conceptualized the tag based theme engine that allows eNom's resellers to skin Instant Reseller and fully integrate their brand.

Adam Youngers' Patents

  • USER GENERATED CONTENT PUBLISHING SYSTEM

    • United States Patent WO/2008/040024
    • Issued September 28, 2007
    Inventors: Adam Youngers, Sean Muller, Richard Rosenblatt, Joe Perez, Dennis Ameen, Larry Fitzgibbon, Greg Griffin, Laurent Gherardi, jon jurgens, Brian Havener, Glenn Moran

    The invention is directed to a User Publishing System (UPS) that enables both content publishing for users that share what they know in articles and enabling these users to get paid for providing the content through use of a User Compensation System (UCS). The invention enables users to create and edit content in multiple formats, from multiple sources, and in multiple languages. The system can be integrated for use with a plurality of different websites and adopt both the look and feel and the content format of each website, and/or other communication medium. Compensation may be provided to the user providing the content based on a variety of metrics.
