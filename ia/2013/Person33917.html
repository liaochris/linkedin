Delna Balsara's Summary

I earned a Master of Science degree in Industrial Design from Art Center College of Design in the fall of 2004. Since graduation I have worked on projects varying in scale, from small hand-held products to large-scale environments. My passion for design is rooted in the study and exploration of color, material, texture and form, thus every design is infused with my unique aesthetic. I have been the creative director of events for such clients as Lexus, Infiniti, Acura, Toyota, Speedo and HP.

My experience also includes extensive work in a variety of fabrication houses as art director and designer for Disney, Warner Brothers, Mattel and Sanrio. I am studying and hope to be a LEED Green Associate by the end of this year.

On the weekends I'm surfing and traveling to the corners of southern california looking at residential architecture. I spend much of the time reading and conducting thought experiments [Einstein's term for day-dreaming].
