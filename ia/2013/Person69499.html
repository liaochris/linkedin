Joyce Fox's Summary

Joyce Fox is an award-winning communicator: broadcaster, writer, producer, director and consultant. She focuses on creative content, marketing and media production. Joyce translates communications and business ideas into programs in web, video, audio, print and stage "making the boring interesting and the interesting fascinating.” She is interested in pursuing full-time as well as contract opportunities

Fox's clients range from broadcast, cable and entertainment companies, educational and multimedia enterprises to small business and Fortune 500 corporations.
