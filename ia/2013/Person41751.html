Experience includes: Staff and project management, leadership development, designing and leading training seminars, new business startups, conference planning and execution, personal and professional coaching, succession planning,outplacement, acting, image consulting/branding, writing and blogging, , business ownership, contracting, team building, consulting, process improvement and motivational speaking ,

Clients include (individuals, businesses and organizations), to inspire, identify goals and create execution plans. I am a RESULTS-FOCUSED professional and coach! My interest is in working with clients committed to action.

Services range from inspirational keynote speaking and seminars for organizations, businesses, conferences and retreat events to success coaching. I guide clients to defining goals and developing plans for accomplishing goals and living a balanced life.

I have created the the RUNWAY TO SUCCESS for those individuals and businesses who are purpose and accomplishment focused. If your are in career transition, have a goal or dream you wish to accomplish, wish to take your business to the next level, or just want to explore what you wish to be/or do when you grow up this the RUNWAY TO SUCCESS is for you. It is empowering for those in career search, starting new businesses, expanding present business, writing books, or any other effort that makes the heart sing.

Key purpose of the group:

* Create avenues for expressing and manifesting
* Provide a network of support, guidance and idea expansion
* Offer a process focused on accountability

I, also, perform a one-woman show highlighting that dreams and goals can become reality if we just believe. The show is about one woman's experiences, however; it reflects on new beginnings which connects it to all people. A part of my business is focused on producing women's events and workshops based on my book and show titled: 'Wearing High Heels in a flip flop World"

Specialties

Career life transition coaching...speaking...conference keynotes, breakout sessions ...one woman show with a life message...workshops for kick-starting one's purpose/dreams/goals. Motivate, inspire and focus on taking ACTION!
