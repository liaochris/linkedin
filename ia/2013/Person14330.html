Bill Hoge's Summary

I have been practicing law in Louisville, Kentucky for more than 40 years. For better than half that time, I have focused my practice on Family Law which includes divorce, child custody, child support, parenting schedules, post-divorce matters, maintenance ("alimony"), division of marital assets, division of marital debts, assignment of marital debts, valuation of closely-held businesses or professional practices, international child custody (Hague Convention and parental kidnapping), collaborative law, mediation and litigation.

Contact us today if you need experienced legal advice in any of these areas.

Specialties: Divorce, Domestic Relations, child custody and support, Qualified Domestic Relations Orders (QDROs), business valuations and all aspects of Family Law practice.
