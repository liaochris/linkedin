Carrie Bekker's Summary

BCCP was founded by Carrie Bekker, a global financial services veteran with 32 years' legal and regulatory compliance experience, derived from her tenure as a Corporate/Securities Paralegal in law firms, corporate law departments, and trust companies in the United States and internationally. Carrie has also held Senior Compliance Officer positions with several global financial conglomerates, including AEGON/Transamerica, Pacific Life, and AIG Sunamerica.

BCCP is also actively engaged in global financial sector modernization, private sector development, and public sector reform -- providing technical assistance and training worldwide, with a specialized focus in the US, Middle East, Asia, and Central/Eastern Europe.

Carrie is an active member of Arab Bankers and North America Association (ABANA), Hedge Fund & Private Equity Resource Center (HFPE), and 100 Women in Hedge Funds, to name a few. She also actively participates in 50 LinkedIn groups, including two BCCP/BCI Global Financial groups that she founded.

Specialties: compliance, risk management, governance, hedge funds, private equity, MENA, Middle East, commodities, derivatives, consulting, training, broker dealer, investment advisor, venture capital, banking, AML, BSA, counter-terrorist financing, Asia Pacific, Europe, Caribbean, US, India, legal, auditing, precious metals, oil and gas, energy, renewable energy, asset recovery, precious metals and gems, fraud investigations, real estate, SWFs
