Kevin Mihaly's Experience

Owner/Photographer

Mihaly Portrait Design - Avon, CT & Tokyo, Japan

2001 – Present (12 years) Avon, CT and Little Falls, NY

Owner, founder and operator of a high-end portrait photography studio specializing in children and family portraiture. Clients include retail and institutional use photography customers, magazine publications, advertisers and business owners. Work products include: digital productions, canvas prints, roadside billboards, coffee table books and traditional photographic prints all with a distinctive artistic style. Business focuses on customer service component of the portrait experience. Photographic goal is to capture the spirit of the subject in a way that tells the story of the person(s) being photographed. Photojournalistic works and portraiture has been featured in Asian and European magazines. Work is displayed in homes and galleries and enjoyed by families around the world. Teaches seminars and provides individual instruction in all components of digital photography. Prior to the opening of his current studio in Avon, CT, his studio was located in Tokyo, Japan.
