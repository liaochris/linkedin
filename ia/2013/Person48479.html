Gia Ciccone's Summary

Digital marketer by day, blogger by night, & a proud University of North Florida alumna 24/7.

Between digital marketing campaigns, branded social updates and endless ideas for brand content floating around in my brain, I try to update my own professional blog (and start up my soon-to-be cooking blog) as much as humanly possible. I guess you could say I keep busy, but I prefer it that way.

Currently, I'm enjoying my time as a Digital Marketer at Adecco Group, a Fortune Global 500 Company. I collaborate with colleagues to deliver digital marketing campaigns for our specialty staffing brands with two goals always in the back of my mind: drive revenue and boost brand awareness. I also maintain and regularly update brands' social accounts and blogs, keeping quality content at the top of my list of priorities.

My experiences vary, but all have given me the vital tools to continue to succeed in the industry. From Social Media Specialist to Marketing Specialist to Digital Marketer, I have been able to learn and grow thanks to the help and support of those around me.

Gia Ciccone's Projects

  • Lend a Wing Pantry

    • June 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Gia Ciccone, Sofia Evangelista, Wendy Alinor

    In the Summer of 2011, the Volunteer Center created a new initiative. Based on the high level of need for financial aid, the Volunteer Center decided to adopt the concept of an on-campus food pantry that will open to the students in Spring 2012. The Lend a Wing Pantry, located on the first floor of UNF Hall (Bldg. 53) near the Cashier’s Office is an anonymous food pantry that allows the UNF students to get the food and hygiene products they need without any form of payment with a flash of a UNF ID.

    In February 2012, for it's kickoff food-raiser, Lend a Wing had a can castle event where groups competed for homecoming points. This can castle event brought in over 8000 useable items for the pantry to give away to students in need.

    Although I am no longer part of this project, this is something that still continues to thrive at the University of North Florida under the direction of the UNF Student Government, as unforunately, the UNF Volunteer Center has since been dissolved.
