Matthew Dekenah's Summary

A highly motivated, experienced, intelligent and adaptable QA and test professional with a strong additional skills-base and interest in public policy and community engagement. Constantly driven to improve, learn and achieve exceptional outcomes, and always ready and able to lead or engage with a team.

I have diverse and rich experience in the full test lifecycle, including both functional and non-functional testing, with a proven track record in leadership and organisational skills through active involvement in community service, vocational and extra-curricular activities. Along with well-developed analytical and problem-solving skills, I am personable, articulate, eloquent and attentive to the finer details.

My specialities in QA/test include: SQL/DB testing; funtional testing - both manual and automated; non-functional testing, including localisation, performance, load, environment; test automation; Web services, SOA and SaaS testing; Agile testing approaches; scripting in Python/Ruby; cross-platform testing, including Windows, Mac OS X, Linux; test management and leadership.

My specialities in policy include: Public health policy; relationship development; community engagement and education; advocacy; critical appraisal; statistical literacy.
