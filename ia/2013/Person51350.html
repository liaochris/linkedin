Halle Malcomb's Summary

Graphic Designer, Communications and Marketing Specialist with a B.S. in Visual Communication Design from The Ohio State University. 9+ years of small business ownership experience in graphic design with WeDesign Graphix LLC. Skilled in Adobe InDesign, Photoshop, Illustrator and Acrobat w/ knowledge of Flash and Dreamweaver. Mac and PC platform user. Experience in public relations, marketing, branding and identity, web content management, pre-press, social media, enewsletters, photography and video production. Experience with creative conceptualization, development and implementation.

Frequent/Contracted clients and partneres included: The Women's Book, WrightChoice, Inc., and Fine Citizens

Creative artist with experience in photography and studio art: oil, acrylic and pencil.

Amateur Model: runway, commercial, and television

Advid Volunteer: Volunteer for The Diversity Enhancment Program at The James. Mentor for Delta Sigma Theta Sorority, Inc: Delta GEMS. Professional mentor for high school/college students seeking their degrees in graphic design/visual communication design. Mid-Ohio Food Bank Operation Feed Campaign Coordinator. Experience with program conceptualization, development and implementation.

Featured in Who's Who in Black Columbus 2008, 2009 & 2010

Specialties

Print and production, typography, team oriented, excellent communication skills, experience in public relations, marketing, branding and identity, illustration, web content management, photography and video production.
