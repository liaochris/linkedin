Dr. Lisa Richardson's Summary

The "Official" Bio:

Dr. Lisa Richardson is a small biz digital strategist with 20 years of immersion in online community development and consumer behavior. She's also a self-taught web designer and programmer. Dr. Lisa offers practical small business online marketing strategy advice, design resources, and tech Q&A with a side of style via her website: YouCanFlow.com.
-------------------------------------------------
And the details...

I love the internet...and research...and design. My clients are seeking to build or optimize their web presence and have generally been in business for at least 3 years. Increasingly they are boutique lodging or B&B innkeepers, eclectic boutique owners, and family-owned restaurant owners who take pride in their high-touch customer experience and unique business story.

I preach organic digital marketing strategy for small biz and embracing technology to run your business better - so you can enjoy life outside your business. Uncomfortable with tech tools? My academic counseling and teaching background helps me work with new & novice tech users to conquer computer anxiety and set about the business of being profitable.

That said...I'd love to meet you! Drop me a note and let's chat about your website, your strategy plan, the next generation of tech startups ...or shoes. This ChicGeek loves shoes.


Specialties: Small Business, WordPress, Digital Strategy, Web Design, User Experience, Information Architecture, Web Analytics, Conversion Rate Optimization
