Robert Williams' Summary

I focus on vocal, communication and performance skills. Things like:

* Public Speaking
* Accent Reduction
* Writing & Presentation Skills
* Performance Skills
* Singing

Voice is very diagnostic of what's going on inside someone as well as what they think about themselves and what they want to project.

I love my work! How many people can say that? It's varied, I meet a lot of interesting people and I learn a lot. If you're a coach or involved in training, it's you have to be open to learning new things.

The three areas which I enjoy the most are:

1. Working with clients on their "Personal Authenticity Journey." This is a technique I've developed to help people understand who they are.

2. Working with speakers and performers on performance and presentation skills

3. Accent Reduction: helping people with strong foreign accents tune into the rhythms of English and just as importantly be understood.

Go well!

  • Seven Secrets for Successful Public Speaking

    • January 2012 to Present

    "Seven Secrets for Successful Public Speaking" is a practical introduction to the exciting world of public speaking.

    Let's face it public speaking can be daunting. Stats show that for most people this is their number one fear. But it doesn't have to be like that. Public speaking can be energising and rewarding if you have the right approach.

    If you feel that you've got something powerful to say but have always wondered "where the heck to do you start?" then this book is for you. Insightful and practical yet not too dense it has plenty of examples and exercises.
