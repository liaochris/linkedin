Gerald Fullam's Summary

I earned an A.A.S. degree in Visual Communication at Sinclair Community College and studied Motion Picture Production and Mass Communication at Wright State University. I've worked for Cox Media Group Ohio (formerly Cox Ohio Publishing) for more than eight years in various print and digital design and development roles. I do freelance web development part time. My wife Erinne and I photograph weddings, events and family portraits together as Fullam Photography.

Specialties: Front-end web development: (X)HTML(5), XML, CSS(3), JavaScript, JQuery, PHP, Flash/AS, Google APIs. Design, build web sites from concept to finish. Create UI, site navigation, icons, logos, etc. Best SEO practices. Standards guru. Design for print & web. Expert in Adobe CS: Photoshop, Illustrator, Lightroom. Mac Dashboard Widget Development experience. Video production/editing. Digital SLR Photography, photo manipulation/retouching.
