Jonathan Faber's Summary

In addition to my activities with Luminary Group and its clients, I also provide expert witness and consulting services on a regular basis. I have assisted plaintiffs and defendants alike in a wide range of intellectual property matters (trademarks, Right of Publicity, etc.), including litigation, celebrity divorce proceedings, sponsorship deals, and complex transactions. My testimony, assessments and valuations have assisted those for whom I have worked to secure desired outcomes on many occasions. Past clients and cases range from (living and deceased) actors and actresses, rock stars, sports figures, professional sports leagues and teams, and major corporations. I also teach this subject matter at the J.D. and LLM level for IU School of Law (Bloomington and Indianapolis).
