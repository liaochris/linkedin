Grant Hutchinson's Summary

Grant creates interfaces, parses fonts, noodles code, imbibes in strong coffee, collects abandoned handheld computing platforms, listens to low bit electropop, partakes in Flickr, and sweats the details. His foremost goal in life is to spend less time working and more time enjoying his family.
