Michael Dorf's Summary

I started the Knitting Factory in 1987 and ran the company until 2003. In 2004 I made a barrel of wine in California and caught the bug. In 2007 created the concept of City Winery--a combination winery, restaurant and intimate concert space. In 2012, we opened in Chicago and I hope for more locations in the coming years. www.citywinery.com

On an annual basis, I produce a series of tribute shows at Carnegie Hall which raises funds for music education program for underserved youth. This includes music of Joni Mitchell, Bob Dylan, Bruce Springsteen, Elton John and REM, The Who, Rolling Stones, Neil Young, and most recently PRINCE.
www.michaeldorf.com
