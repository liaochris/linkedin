Angelica Henry's Summary

Born and raised in Phoenix, Angelica earned her Interior Design degree at Arizona State University. She is a professional member of ASID, certified by NCIDQ, and has received numerous awards for her innovative work. Angelica Henry Design offers comprehensive design services for residential and commercial projects in a broad range of styles. Her work has been featured in such publications as “The New York Times", “The Robb Report", "Luxury Living", as well as local publications such as "Phoenix Home & Garden" and “Arizona Foothills”.

Specialties

Interior Design for new construction and remodels, custom furniture design
