James Sandoval's Summary

I am a data & technology-centric, digital marketing professional with 14+ years of US & UK client service & leadership experience.

Following a 10-year/10,000+ hour digital media agency career (Avenue A, Carat Interactive, Avenue A|Razorfish, Diffiniti, Isobar & Neo@Ogilvy), I dove straight into an entrepreneurial adventure (between June 2008 and September 2012) that yielded two small, but highly respected companies, Invizua and Pikslme. These ventures, in turn, generated hundreds of greatly valued commercial relationships, not to mention numerous new industry and personal friendships.

In September 2012 I decided to narrow my focus and apply my skills to help build what I believe is a company with the potential to bring transformational change to the digital media and marketing industries.

That company is Chicago-based BrightTag.
