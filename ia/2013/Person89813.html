Mauricio d'Orey's Summary

Over the last years Mauricio worked on several awarded short and feature films, such as "Hay Road”, “The Human Centipede”, "The Eagleman Stag", which won the Best Animated Short at the 2011 BAFTA awards, “Splitscreen: A Love Story”, winner of the Nokia Shorts competition 2011 and more recently "Dia de Los Muertos", winner of the Best Animation Film at the Student Academy Awards 2013.

Having completed the two year Sound Design MA at the NFTS in 2011, Mauricio quickly got in the London Sound Post Industry working on projects for the BBC and several production companies like Freestate, Parkville Pictures, Wizzo&Co, Maverick and foreign companies like Plus One Amsterdam, Jump Willy, Lopta Film and Bando a Parte.

Mauricio is always on the move working and collaborating in projects all around the globe whilst working constantly on the detailed obsession for the perfect sound for the perfect moment.

Specialties: Sound Designer and Dubbing Mixer
