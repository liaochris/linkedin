Ajay Burlingham-Böhr's Summary

Interim IT leadership, consultancy, IT leader coaching and team development primarily within the Higher Education and Charity sectors.

Specialist in IT function transformation and turn-arounds, strategy development and team development.

Over 27 years experience in the business leadership of IT within commercial, not-for-profit and education organisations, with over 13 years at Head of Department Level and over 7 years at Executive Board level with additional areas of responsibility such as media production and services.

A strong technical and strategic background in the IT industry enhanced with an MBA completed in 2004.


Other Experience:

Visiting lecturer in Organizational Politics and Business Relationship Management at Cranfield University School of Management.

Accredited Coach with the Institute of Human Development and currently undertaking some coaching both as an internal coach and with a few external clients.

Accredited MBTI (Myers Briggs Type Indicator) practitioner with OPP for individual personal development and team building.

Chartered Fellow of the British Computer Society, Member of BCS Elite and Member of the BCS Ethics Strategic Panel.

Founding Chair of the Charity IT Leaders group (formally Charities Consortium IT Director's Group).

Runner-up medalist for the 2009 UK IT Industry Awards IT Leader of the Year

Winner of the Computer Weekly Best Places to Work in IT Award 2006 whilst Head of IS at the RSPB.

Also undertaking occasional consultancy work and developing and delivering training courses on topics such as Knowledge Management, Strategy Mapping and Balanced Scorecards

Specialties: IT and IS strategy and architecture development and implementation. IT/Organisation alignment.
IT Department Turnarounds, change management and culture change.
Managing organizational politics.
Senior management within not-for-profit organisations and the HE sectors.
