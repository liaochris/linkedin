Andrew Johnson's Summary

I started out in this industry over 25 years ago when, fresh out of grad school, I was hired by a leading computer graphics systems manufacturer to start an in-house video production department. Talk about on-the-job training... I'd been a working illustrator since high school and I saw this as a great opportunity to learn a new skill in an industry in its infancy: Computer graphics and animation. If you know what I'm talking about when I mention Abel & Associates' "Sexy Robot", or Viewpoint's "Stanley and Stella", or Pixar's "Wally B", then you're of my CGI generation.

Since then I've worked for a number of corporations, production companies and educational institutions - always keeping an open creative, technical and managerial mind with the goal of building a well-rounded experience. The result is... well, learning never ends, so I guess the "result" is still a work in progress.

Tangible evidence of my experience, however, is my range of skills (producer, illustrator, 3D animator, motion graphics and VFX, editor), long roster of clients (mostly repeat customers) and the awards my work has been blessed with, including ADDYs and Tellys.

I started iMed Design a number of years ago as a full service design and production company specializing in corporate, medical, emergency response and training programs and broadcast commercials. Originally, my shop was called Hot Digital Dog, but a client suggested that I might want to consider a more "professional" name. And since I was developing a lot of interactive medical projects, I settled upon iMed. Plus it's easy to say when answering the phone - not an unimportant consideration.

With my experience in graphics, animation and special effects, our projects are known for their production design values and message success. From concepting, scriptwriting and pre-production through production, post, CD/DVD/Web deployment and packaging, our clients rely on us for soup-to-nuts communication experience.

Specialties

- Project concepting and brainstorming;
- 3D/2D/Motion graphics & animation, special effects (rotoscoping, bluescreen, compositing);
- HD videography and time lapse;
- Complete post production services;
- Interactive mobile and tablet development, ePubs;
- Location/documentary/interview production;
- Photography
- Web delivery and compression.

We had our daughter in 2001 and I discovered that the last thing I wanted to do was go away for 10 hours a day, work for someone else and not be with her. So I took the plunge of starting my own shop, doubled my work hours (but spent far more time with my family) and increased my roster of national clients and cool projects. Started in NY but moved to NV to be near the wife's family in 2004. Drove cross country via Rt. 66 to get here - don't recommend it unless you're adventurous. Quickly built a nationwide roster of clients allowing me to work anywhere. So we restored an old Airstream and in 2011 drove back cross country to my home state of Maryland. iMed has grown to a full service HD video production shop, with high-end animation, graphics and special effects; post production and interactive design (CD, DVD, mobile and Web).
