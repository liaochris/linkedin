Max Levine's Summary

In his current role, Max Levine works with the Hudson Holdings business' to help identify and develop new market opportunities as well as to grow the existing customer base, build strategic relationships and strategic acquisitions. He will be overseeing the companies aggressive acquisition strategy over the next 24 months to become one of the largest mail order pharmacy & DMEs with over 500,000 patients. He has overseen multiple acquisitions including CareMed Pharmacies and Smart Remedies to date. He sits on 7 boards.

Prior to founding Hudson Holdings, Max Levine acquired and served as the CEO & President of Smart Remedies, the largest direct to consumer DME mail order supplier in Alabama. During his two years as President he modernized operations, expanded sales channels and implemented technology to simplify work flows. This directly resulted in the companies growth by more than 800% in less than 24 months.

Max founded Ascent Services Group (www.ascentsg.com) In 2000 and grew ASG in to a name brand company with offices across the nation and close to $60 million in annual revenue before selling to a Private Equity group in May 2009. He grew all aspects of the business including outsourcing, client services and new lines of business. The company consistently maintained growth rates 2-3 times competitors. Max was personally responsible for landing such clients as Kaiser Permanente, Wells Fargo, United Healthcare Group (UHG), Genentech, Disney, McKesson, State of California Judicial System, Blue Shield, Google & Yahoo.

Max launched Contingent Workforce Professionals (CWP) in 2007 to foster communication among high level executives that run some of the world’s largest outsourcing programs. Max founded (CWP)(www.cwprofessionals.org) while building ASG as a way to help facilitate best practices and allow industry leaders to converse openly with peers. the group was restricted to top level executives.
