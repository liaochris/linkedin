Dan Moller's Experience

3D Animator - VFX Supervisor

Freelance

January 2002 – Present (11 years 7 months) Perth Area, Australia

I have extensive senior-level, hands-on production experience including concept development, consultation, pitching, script breakdown, on-set supervision, online and review. I have worked primarily in local and international TVC production, with additional experiences in Film, Music Video, Broadcast, Games, Corporate, Print, Web and Architectural and Medical Visualisation.

My personal 3D skills are strongly generalist, covering the full pipeline including Matchmoving and Compositing, with particular strengths in Lookdev and Effects. I've also led teams of up to 10 artists, including providing support for HR processes.

I work primarily in Maya (Mental Ray, 3Delight), switching where necessary to Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects, Mudbox, Boujou, Realflow and whichever smaller plugins and packages will help bring an image to life. My MEL scripting skills are intermediate and I have extensive knowledge of video formats and camera tech specs.
