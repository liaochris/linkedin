Erica Salvetti's Summary

I'm a Berlin based Digital Product designer, focused on mobile app, putting passion on every project i’m working on, trying to do the best in every details, and always thinking about new ideas

I prefer to follow a project since the beginning, analyze the target, ux and interaction flows, draw wireframes, create mockups and work on the graphic till the final pixel perfect design, to obtain complete, functional and beautiful works.

I'm used to work in team, i prefer being surrounded by people with different skills and talent because it's the best to reach new ideas and generate creative products. I'm also interested in learning new things and be curious and open mind is part of my personality.

My goal is to improve my skills as product designer and focus more on mobile app and learn more about user experience and interaction flows

I love thriller movie, sci-fi series, fantasy book and horror story, and when i’m working i use these interest to think outside of the box and solve the problems, create new ideas and design, get the user involved and surprised.
