James Rice's Summary

I work with technology brands and their enterprise audiences with a simple premise, to share Knowledge For Great Decisions. #K4GD. Stop marketing self-serving thought leadership and give people the commercial insights they need in an entertaining, relevant and valuable form to help them make the best decisions to be successful with their professional and personal lives.

If you want to speak with credibility, bring to life, craft, share and build real stories, do it with vehemence by finding a tribe. A tribe will know your market, your audience and their challenges - with the point of making your brand the hero.

My tribe is comprised of amazing people that make the hard-working assets for brands to share their stories. You won't find a more dedicated and knowledgeable team anywhere.
