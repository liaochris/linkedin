Jen Bennett's Summary

As the Social Media Specialist for iBloom, my passion is to help businesses, both large and small, with strategy, implementation, monitoring and training of their online presence. I have been able to assist a variety of businesses in creating their brand awareness on one of the largest social media platforms. Additionally, I have had the opportunity to not only train and work with a recognized Forbes Top 20 Social Media Influencer, but I also had the opportunity to train and help a variety of business owners on a variety of social media topics including:

Social Media Content Management
Social Media Lead Generation
Twitter Strategies
Facebook Strategies
List Building Using Social Media
Social Media Strategy Development
Facebook Advertising

As a former Senior Social Media Account Executive for a social media marketing firm, I had the opportunity to lead a team who worked collaboratively on a variety of corporate accounts. These accounts included:

* taking a client from the "concept" stage of development to a large interconnected social media platform. Within a 90-day social media growth campaign, I helped generate 17,000 new Facebook likes and 18,000 new Twitter followers.

* developing, building and managing a social media strategy for a business school whose social media platform was ranked as the 16th best across all accredited business schools (700+).

* developing from scratch the social media presence for a leading travel agency that has over 150 travel agents working in several offices across the southeast. I helped manage the content, growth, and engagement on Facebook and Twitter. The company now dominates most of the competition with 25,000 likes on Facebook and 16,000 followers on Twitter.

I am the author of: “My Soul Mission: A Step-by-Step Guide to Helping Students Discover Their Unique Soul Mission." I am also nearing completion of my Doctorate in Curriculum Development/Instructional Leadership.
