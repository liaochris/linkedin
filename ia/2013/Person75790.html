Kim Kyle Morgan's Summary

I enjoy creating, writing and refreshing website content for the healthcare, nonprofit and entertainment industries; as well as writing and editing copy for a variety of clients including the Houston Chronicle (HealthZone, Star, Belief, Flavor, City/State, Ultimate, Senior Living, Careers/Jobs and special sections as needed - hurricane recovery, shopping guides etc); Memorial Hermann Hospital System, Lone Star College, and Sam Houston State University.

I have provided content for local government and nonprofit sectors, including the City of Houston (Hurricane Ike recovery) and The Rose (breast cancer screening and related services).
