Jason Hull's Summary

Hi, my name’s Jason Hull and I’m the in-house Design Studio Manager for the Absolute World Group of Companies, based in Phuket, Thailand.

I have over twenty years of industry experience garnered both in the UK and overseas in such locales as Thailand, Sydney and Dubai. During this time I have worked within press houses and design agencies which has given me an in depth understanding of the entire design and print process from concept to delivery.
