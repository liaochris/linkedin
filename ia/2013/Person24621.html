Christo Schutte's Summary

I am in the business of creating results for my clients. Period.

I am passionate about creating the right conditions that makes transformational break-through experiences inevitable, whether that is working with executive leaders, executive leadership teams or olympic athletes.

"Christo Schutte has nearly a decade of experience as a coach, consultant, and business owner, working extensively with leaders of Fortune 500, as well as mid-sized and start-up growth companies. Leveraging his former experience as a nationally ranked tennis player in South Africa, Christo founded The Game Within Leadership Performance Group. Based on his intuitive entrepreneurial skills, and a unique approach to human development and growth, he has developed groundbreaking methodologies to help leaders and teams evolve.

Christo is a sought-after executive coach, speaker, consultant and seminar leader. He brings a wealth of experience, passion, and a unique approach to leadership development. It is his belief in possibility, ability to see the gifts in challenges, steadfast focus on results, and the safe and supportive space he creates with clients that leads to positive, sustainable change. He has an established record of success in supporting leaders in pursuing and achieving their goals. He has worked with leaders in various organizations—including from the Barclay’s Group, Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI), Verizon Wireless, Wilmington Trust, Children’s Hospital of Philadelphia, Coldwell-Banker, Federal Management Partners, Keller-Williams Realtors and Penn State University.

Christo is a graduate of Georgetown University’s globally renowned leadership program and a certified member of the International Coaching Federation (ICF). He also holds a Masters Degree in Counseling and a bachelor’s degree in Cost Engineering".

Christo Schutte's Education

Georgetown University

Post Graduate Certificate , Leadership Coaching (ICF Accredited Program)

2009 – 2010

This International Coach Federation (ICF) accredited program examines the links between coaching and organization development while emphasizing coaching skill development on three levels:

• learning about self as a coach and instrument of change
• creating productive and fulfilling relationships in the coaching role
• understanding coaching within systems dynamics.

This rigorous program prepares participants to the Professional Certified Coach level of proficiency as established by the ICF.

This program includes 152 hours of instruction. Supplementing the 19 days of in-person classes, participants complete significant outside reading and written assignments throughout the program, complete supervised coaching of 3 clients for a period of 3 months each, and receive individual coaching by an assigned faculty member for 6 months.
