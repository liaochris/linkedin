Christopher Harman's Summary

Commercial real estate professional with over 20 years of experience in managing corporate real estate activities including strategic counseling, property valuation, asset management, and transaction management. Experienced in right of entry agreements for telecommunication companies.

We have developed a technology platform called "Rail Yard" that allows tenants an easy way to find and shop for telecom services at their address. Rail Yard also handles project management, database and document storage needs for our telecommunication clients.

Specialties

Telecommunications Right of Entry, Corporate Real Estate, Commercial Real Estate Valuation, Renewable Energy, Industrial Real Estate
