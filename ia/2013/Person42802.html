Erin Shirey's Summary

Honestly, I love what I do. I've been in the Health and Fitness industry for 15 years, and continue to learn more each month.
Learning from a young age I wanted to help motivate others to develop their self esteem through athletics, I received my BS in Kinesiology. Working on Sports and Conditioning programs and Corporate Fitness I created my determined, supportive and multifaceted work ethic.

After starting a family and motivated to start my own business, I licensed Baby Boot Camp Portland Metro in February 2005. I had such passion for helping Moms develop their athleticism and loved teaching daily. My business became the biggest Baby Boot Camp Franchise and Franchise of the Year in 2007.

In 2008 I helped launch Power Outdoor Fitness, a co-ed boot camp. While running both businesses the logistics of time management, multi client needs, and various levels of marketing created a deeper understanding of balancing business and family.

In Spring 2009, I wanted to create another fitness and wellness business that was a brand new. Power Fitness PDX launched in July 2009. The business relocated to Alameda, California in January 2010 and has been thriving.

I have a strong passion for fitness writing and television. Having written for NW Kids, Metro Parent, Alameda Magazine, Walk About, CitySport, Competitor online and Parents Magazine. I held the position of New Mom Editor and Fitness Editor for the nationally syndicated "Better" show, and have filmed segments for Parents TV and Fitness TV.

My passion for what I do, inspires me to keep growing. Understanding that we have only one life to live fully, makes me embrace each day and challenge myself not only mentally, but physically. My goal is to help others do the same, and help them live their best life possible.
