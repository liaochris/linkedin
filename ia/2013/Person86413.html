Mark Clare's Summary

I am an investment banker and entrepreneur.

I am the founding partner in boutique New Zealand-based investment bank Clare Capital.

Prior to Clare Capital I was a founding partner in Woodward Partners. Woodward Partners provides investment banking advisory and institutional share broking services. Woodward Partners transactions include: the sale of BNZ's commercial insurance business to Aon (adviser to BNZ), the acquisition by NZ Post of 50% of Express Couriers from DHL (adviser to NZ Post), sale of Radian Technology to Tru-Test (adviser to Radian), capital raise for CricHQ, capital raise for Scoop Media, the IPO of Energy Mad (MAD.NZ) on the NZSX, the NZ$3.3 million capital raise for Windflow (WTL.NZ) on the NZAX and the valuation of the NZ Crown's royalty streams from the petroleum estate [http://bit.ly/yjaRO3].

I am also the founder of Valuecruncher (Valuecruncher.com) - a web-based software application that provides interactive valuation tools for publicly-traded companies. Valuecruncher has been referenced by Barron’s [http://bit.ly/bUyCdh] and Umair Haque in Harvard Business Review [http://bit.ly/a1hkG7]. From Umair Haque’s article:

“Tracked, ValueCruncher, StockTwits, and many more are the leading edge of a revolution — a revolution in what finance has been for the last several centuries, and what it must become in the 21st.”

I have led successful transactions – including the NZ$25 million sale of Cheal Petroleum to listed Canadian company TAG Oil and the sale of BNZ's commercial insurance business to Aon. I have considerable experience in the early-stage company space from both the investor and entrepreneur perspectives. I have also published respected analysis of the on-line finance industry [http://bit.ly/bX5wK].

Specialties: Corporate finance, mergers and acquisitions, capital raising, strategy.
