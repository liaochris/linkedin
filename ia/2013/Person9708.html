Armin Roșu's Projects

  • Superscrieri 2013 - Finding the best writers in Romania

    • April 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Armin Roșu, Bojan Spasic, Corina Barbu

    Superscrieri is an anual competition, organised by the Friends For Friends Foundation, to find the best non-fiction writings in Romania that year. Usually authors are asked to submit their own works in one of several categories.

    This year was different, as readers were asked to submit articles and categories were forgone. Instead, all submissions were listed and voted upon (à la digg). The top 10 were then judged by a panel.

    Based on Bojan's design, I built the microsite. Since we wanted as many people to vote & submit, there was no authentication system built in, but a mix of loggin users and actions. Interestingly, there were fewer cheaters than expected.

  • AlexandruBalasescu.com

    • November 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Armin Roșu, Bojan Spasic

    Alexandru Bălășescu is, in his own words, "an anthropologist (Ph.D. at the University of California, Irvine) essayist and serial migrant." He contacted us to create a website for him to put his thoughts and musings on, something "more than twitter but not a blog.", i.e. a microblog.

    Unbeknownst to him, Bojan and I were itching for a tumblr (the microblogging platform) project. Also, we were wondering if you could create a site with a 480px width, thus sidestepping responsive design, i.e. instead of creating a mobile & desktop version of a site, or an adaptive design, create only the mobile version.

    The result of this is alexandrubalasescu.com

  • LovePlus - Make Love, Not just Sex

    • May 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Armin Roșu

    Love Plus is a brand of condoms owned by PSI Romania. As part of an awareness campaign, I had to build a website, facebook app, banner game and edit a clip for use in cinemas. It was fun, especially the tight deadline.

  • Earcandy - Facebook Playlist App

    • March 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Armin Roșu

    This project grew out of a personal need: listening to the music my friends shared on Facebook and thus discovering new artists / songs. As it isn't possible to create a playlist natively, I built an app for that.

  • Magazinul de Mirodenii - Romania's first spice shop

    • November 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Armin Roșu, Bojan Spasic

    Magazinul de Mirodenii is the first shop in Romania dedicated to spices. Therefor they wanted an online shop as well, to increase national exposure and sales.

    The website was designed by Bojan Spasić (who is also co-owner) and I implemented it using Wordpress.

  • Sibex.ro - API

    • July 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Armin Roșu

    As part of our work for Sibex (Sibiu Stock Exchange), we wanted to showcase banners with real & live stock data. With the help of their own programmer, I developed a RESTful API for this.

  • Chiulan House Factory - International Websites

    • March 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Armin Roșu,

    Chiulan House Factory is a builder of prefabricated homes, constructed according to german standards and tailored to the customers wants and needs.

    They wanted to expand to other european markets, Italy and Austria specifically. For this, they needed to be able to direct customers to a website for further details.

    Based on a design by Bogdan Vintilă, I built chiulanhaus.at and chiulanhaus.it.

  • MusicPond - democratic music player

    • April 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Armin Roșu

    A personal project I built to address one of the biggest issues within modern work environments: what music to listen to. As everyone has different taste in music, the designated jukebox will get many requests. Thus a common playlist to add, skip, remove tracks or get the name of that awesome beat would help.

    That's what this is. To make it easy, you can add tracks by simply adding YouTube links. Still beta, so be gracious please.

    Test it here: http://bit.ly/19eYdZK (open multiple windows to see the syncing in action)

  • Veo - Sales Support App

    • June 2010 to Present
    Team Members: Armin Roșu

    Veo is a manufacturer of ind turbines and related equipment. We were hired to build an app in support of their sales personnel, to display ROIs, cost estimates etc. using live data (cached for offline use). To that end I built an Adobe Air app.

  • Ecohaus - Prefab Homes

  • Luxury Design Dosei - Website

    • July 2009 to Present
    Team Members: Armin Roșu,

    Luxury Design Dosei is a high-end interior design shop. They wanted an animated website (with music) so we built them one using Flex.

    It was a peculiar project, considering Flex wasn't necessarily meant for this and a few basic classes had to be rewritten (e.g. the menu).

  • Tonerpost - e-commerce websites

    • February 2009 to October 2009
    Team Members: Armin Roșu, Octavian Ciuhandu

    The Tonerpost group of companies sells compatible toner cartridges to businesses. They hired us to build them e-commerce websites for 3 of their markets (Hungary, Romania and Bulgaria). This proved to be the most challenging part, as the local offices had specific requirements and different levels of commitment (as the project was requested by the owner).

    We developed a core site which we then extended accordingly for each country. Initially I did this in Drupal 6, but switched to Magento.

    Although it came with a lot of features (even ones the client didn't request but we thought will be useful in the future), the lack of proper documentation was disconcerting. In hindsight this was the right decision. The hungarian office (which had the most elaborate requests, e.g. printing hungarian accounting invoices) is still running it today, though the design was slightly updated.
