Chip Darius, OHST, CET, CSHO's Summary

Over 20 years experience in safety & OSHA compliance consulting and training: construction, business, industry, utilities, and emergency services. Clients in over 30 states since 2000.

Over 10 years as Instructor for OSHA Training Institute Region 1 Education Center and Adjunct Faculty at Keene State College. Teach safety/OSHA regulations courses, and provide technical expertise, curriculum development, and instructional design.

Accomplished presenter, writer, trainer, and facilitator. Experienced litigation consultant and expert witness in cases involving catastrophic injury and fatality, personal injury, and workplace safety. Plaintiff and defense, state and federal cases.

Authorized OSHA Outreach Trainer, teaching both Construction and General Industry programs.
