Jim DeBetta's Summary

Summary

I am the founder and President of DeBetta Enterprises which specializes in coaching and consulting for inventors and consumer products start-up companies. My firm also assists clients with product development and engineering of consumer products as well as sales and marketing representation to major retailers for our select clients. I recently joined Kevin Harrington from the ABC show Shark Tank as his VP of Product Development where I carefully review all products that are sent to the company and determine if they are suitable for an infomercial or retail distribution. I then head up a team of retail specialists that call on the world's most prestigious retailers and TV shopping networks.

My firm has formed a solid network of product engineers, factory brokers, angel investment firms, licensing experts, and sales and marketing professionals among many other areas of expertise. We work with Fortune 500 companies and individual inventors alike.

My team and I specialize in finding factories to produce products, creating pricing strategies, marketing and public relations, and selling products to major retailers such as Target, Wal-Mart, Best Buy, Costco, Bed, Bath & Beyond, Michaels, Walgreens, TJ Maxx, Radio Shack, Toys R US, and many others.

If you have an idea for a product and need proper guidance or already have developed a product and need help gaining placement at major retail, we can help!

Prior to forming DeBetta Enterprises, I was part of a highly successful start up company that produced sport optics such as binoculars and hand held magnifiers. I was President and COO and lead the company from insignificant revenue to nearly 50 million dollars in sales in under 8 years.

I have extensive experience managing a multi-million dollar marketing budget to grow brand awareness as well as creating effective sales and marketing strategies for start up and small consumer products companies.
