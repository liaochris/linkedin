Jim Priest's Summary

Jim Priest is a husband, father of two, developer, and avid motorcyclist.

He has extensive experience working with ColdFusion. He has also been a system administrator managing web, database and email servers as well as configuration manager responsible for trouble ticket systems, version control and automated deployments.

Jim is an active participant in the ColdFusion community; co-managing the Triangle Area ColdFusion User Group (TACFUG) in the Raleigh/Durham, North Carolina area. In 2009 he helped organize CFinNC, the first ColdFusion conference in North Carolina, which has since turned into NCDevCon, a general web and mobile developers conference.

He posts regularly to his blog at http://www.thecrumb.com.

Specialties: ColdFusion, Railo, ANT, System Administration, JIRA, Confluence, Selenium, jQuery, Subversion, SVN, programming, configuration management, SCM
