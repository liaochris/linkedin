Menno Braakman's Summary

My name is Menno Braakman. I come from the world of business administration, economics, marketing strategy and social media. After launching the first Social Media Agency in The Netherlands in 2007, two years Global Digital Lead at the client side: Orangina Schweppes International, 1,5 year as entrepreneur: marketing & online strategist, I am now responsible for Digital at M2Media & More, the nr 1 Advertising & Communication Agency of 2012.

My passion is to (re)connect companies and their customers through a matching strategy where reach & relation, awareness & engagement complement eachother.

As Digital Director at M2Media & More I can do just that for our clients. I like to deep dive in their worlds and the lifestyles of their customers. I use that input and energy and convert it into an optimal digital strategy. I have a great Digital Team of specialists and partners who can perfectly execute every part of this strategy.

I would love to take your online strategy to the next level. Just send me an email or LinkedIn message and I will get back to you as soon as possible!
