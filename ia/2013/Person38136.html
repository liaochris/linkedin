Dr. M. George M.D.'s Summary

The Royal Oak Medical Center provides walk-in urgent care as well as medical expertise in the areas of family practice, internal medicine, ambulatory outpatient surgery, cosmetic surgery, cardiology, gastroenterology, gynecology, podiatry and preventive, holistic, lifestyle modification and anti-aging medicine. New to the clinic this year is a comprehensive back pain center, a travel clinic and STD clinic, as well as an affordable, non-insurance health plan. Additionally, the Royal Oak Medical Center offers a full range of diagnostic testing including lab, x-rays, E.K.G. ultrasound, echocardiogram, thallium stress, nerve conduction, and osteoporosis testing and provides holter monitors for cardiac testing.
