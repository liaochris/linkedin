Dick Conrad's Summary

Dick Conrad leads BMC Software’s global procurement team with a spend of over US$700 million with suppliers and team members in countries around the globe. The BMC Global Procurement team supports BMC’s Enterprise Service Management (ESM), Mainframe Services Management (MSM) and Professional Services (PSG) Groups enabling them to deliver over $10 million in enterprise, mainframe, cloud computing and other enterprise IT value added services and solutions to BMC’s customer’s each day. BMC is the recognized leader in Business Service Management (BSM)and offers a comprehensive and unified platform that helps IT organizations cut cost, reduce risk and drive business profit. BMC revenue for the trailing four (4) fiscal quarters is approximately US$ 2.1 billion. BMC is headquartered in Houston, TX and operates globally with major offices in Houston, Austin, Tx; San Jose, CA; Boston, MA; Amsterdam, Singapore, Tel Aviv, Israel and Pune, India.

Dick has over 35 years experience in supply chain, procurement, materials management, manufacturing, logistics and distribution in the computer industry and is a recognized leader in the field.

Prior to joining BMC, Dick led Dell’s Worldwide Procurement operations in Singapore from 2006-2009 where he was responsible for procurement and supply chain operations that supported Dell’s global product groups.

Dick is a graduate of Georgia Tech and holds a Bachelor of Industrial Engineering Degree. Dick has done postgraduate work in Economics, Business and Management Science at University of Dayton, NC State University ,University of Texas and Hong Kong University. He is active with the Make-A-Wish Foundation, Habitat for Humanity and the Navy League. He is a member of The Institute of Supply Management (ISM) Houston Chapter. Dick is married to his wife of 27 years Teresa, has 3 children and attends the United Methodist Church of The Woodlands, Texas.
