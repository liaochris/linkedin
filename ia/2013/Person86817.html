Mark Ludy's Summary

My family and I just moved to Boise, Idaho and excited for what this time will bring. The project I am undertaking from July - December is a Wordless Picture Book, "NOAH". Other then that, I intend to be about the business of creatively communicating with the world around me, encouraging, challenging and bringing hope and love where there is none.
