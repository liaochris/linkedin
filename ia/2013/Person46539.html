Gábor Ruff's Experience

Nature Photographer

Gábor Ruff Photography

January 2009 – Present (4 years 11 months)

As a nature photographer with background in ecology, my aim is to document the natural history of wildlife and their habitats. Through my photography, I try to inform and educate people about the natural world as well as to promote conservation at levels from species to ecosystems.

Biologist and Photographer

Egyek-Pusztakócs LIFE-Nature Project

March 2006 – December 2008 (2 years 10 months) Hungary

I was a member of the research staff of the Egyek-Pusztakócs habitat restoration project. The research team studied the impacts of habitat restoration on biodiversity. I also made photo documentaries about the field work and the wildlife of the Egyek-Pusztakócs marsh and grassland system in Hungary.

Gábor Ruff's Education

University of Debrecen

M.Sc., Biology / Ecology

1998 – 2005

Thesis: The mound building behaviour of the Steppe Mouse (Mus spicilegus) and the role of the mounds.

I was also involved in several research projects as a member of the Behavioural Ecology Research Group and the Mammalian Ecology Group.
