Christine Gribble's Summary

An award-winning, international photographer, Christine has been photographing professionally for almost 20 years. Her relaxed, fun style puts everyone at ease while creating images that tell a story and providing her clients with pieces of art to treasure.

Christine is accredited by Special Kids Photography of America, a designation for her skills in photographing children with special needs or illness.
