Grant Nieddu's Summary

Grant is THE Spark; a trainer that seeks to help Spark Vision, Ignite Success, and Explode Significance with his clients. He helps emerging entrepreneurs and small-business owners to improve customer service scores, strengthen customer loyalty, and increase bottom-line sales through explosive employee buy-in.

Grant has spent the last 12 years training individuals, business-owners and non-profit organizations. He has served a variety of entrepreneurial ventures, educational institutions, and individuals determined to improve their results.

As part of his passion, Grant also offers his time to help train humanitarians, teams, and organizational staff to expand their support base by developing supporter loyalty and a dynamic supporter culture. He and his wife work in the humanitarian development field on the island of Hispaniola. They seek to help the citizens there live a higher quality of life through sustainable solutions and development training.

Specialties: Customer Service Training, Employee Training, Team Development, Communication Coaching, Vision Cultivation, Strategic Problem-Solving, Messaging and Framing

Grant Nieddu's Education

Earthship Biotecture

Biocture, Sustainable Housing Construction

2012 – 2012

Grade: Passed.0

Learned the 6 Design Principles of an Earthship Sustainable Home:
1) Thermal/Solar Heating & Cooling - Sustainable homes can maintain comfortable temperatures in any climate via thermal dynamics.

2) Solar & Wind Electricity - Earthship sustainable homes can produce their own electricity with a prepackaged photovoltaic / wind power system, stored in batteries and supplied to your electrical outlets.

3) Contained Sewage Treatment - Earthships contain use and reuse all household sewage in indoor and outdoor treatment cells resulting in food production.

4) Building with Natural & Recycled Materials: A sustainable home must make use of indigenous materials, those occurring naturally in the local area.

5) Water Harvesting - Earthships catch water from the sky and use it four times. Earthships do not pollute underground water aquifers.

6) Food Production - Earthship planters hold hundreds of gallons of water from sinks & the shower are used to raise fresh produce.
