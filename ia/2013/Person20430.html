Caroline Tunnell-Jones' Summary

Hi, I’m Caroline. I’m a copywriter and a wine person through and through.

Most of my experience in the wine trade over the past 12 years has been in sales and marketing roles, including pr; I’ve worked for big companies and little companies, and I’m now working for myself. I’d be happy to help you with any of your writing needs.

Apart from crafting copy for clients through First Growth Communications, I teach WSET Level 1 and 2 courses at Winehouse, and from time to time, you'll also catch me moonlighting at Rathdowne Cellars.

And just to keep me on my toes, I’m also studying for my WSET Diploma at the Sydney Wine Academy - just one subject to go.

Need a copywriter who understands wine? You've just found one.

Wine is a complex product, which takes years to understand. It has a vast number of unique words and search terms, and is the kind of product where it's easy to spot a fake who doesn't know what they're talking about.

As a copywriter, I focus on the needs of the wine trade by:
- Adding power and authenticity to your sales copy
- Describing wine accurately, instinctively and with passion
- Even filling in the gaps of your own expertise

During the 12 years that I've worked in the wine industry, I’ve honed my copywriting skills in many roles from sales to marketing and pr, all the while, building my networks and refining my wine knowledge.
