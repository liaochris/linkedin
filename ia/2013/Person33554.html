Deb Krier's Summary

With a background of nearly 20 years in public relations and marketing, I am passionate about working with professionals to show them how to use social media to enhance their professional credibility and to promote their businesses. As a professional speaker, I provide fun and informative keynote presentations, seminars, webinars and workshops, showing how to use social media to the best advantage. Additionally, I provide corporate and one-on-one training.

Anyone can—and should—use social media to expand their networks, increase referrals, and get more clients! The key to effectively using social media is building relationships. We work with people we know, like and trust and social media provides a great way to do this.

Business owners benefit from working with me by learning how to use these tools—without wasting time! Using their existing marketing plans, it’s easy to strategically provide messages via social media.

I host a weekly talk show on Mile Hi Radio (www.milehiradio.com), interviewing experts about various social media platforms. The program is live every Monday from 11:00-Noon (ET). In addition, each program is available on iTunes.

As the founder of Wise Women Communications, a full-service PR and marketing agency, I'm an entrepreneur. I’ve worked with corporations, nonprofits and agencies.
