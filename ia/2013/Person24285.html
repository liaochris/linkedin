Peter Christo's Summary

Under his consulting practice Christo Partners, Peter works with startups and new venture owners as an advisor, educator and facilitator. Having worked primarily in the technology and communications industries (mainly in the online space), his speciality is delivering discipline and order to the chaos of creative ideas and perceived opportunities via smart management frameworks to deliver positive commercial outcomes. Other industries Peter has worked in include financial services, electronic security, FMCG and the military.


Peter is the founder of a number of start-up ventures, including Melbournecoffeereview.com, Pitchclub.com, X2O and more recently Zorba The Buddha Coffee Company.

Peter is a public speaker on the realities of Entrepreneurship and new venture management and also contributes his time as an adjunct lecturer in Entrepreneurship, strategy and innovation at a tertiary level.

Peter holds a Masters Degree in Entrepreneurship and Innovation (MEI), along with a Bachelor of Economics & Marketing.

Peter has worked both Nationally and Internationally as an advisor and educator and was a keynote speaker at a range of eminent events including the Asia Pacific Economic Cooperation conference.

Recently, Peter accepted an invitation to become a Chair under the auspices of The Executive Connection (TEC), a worldwide organisation dedicated to the support and professional growth of business leaders.

Peter is a professional entrepreneur and commercialization adviser to corporations and government looking to capitalize on opportunities.

As a successful entrepreneur in his own right he has launched and harvested a number of successful new business startups over the last 15 years, while also advising startups, and working with industry looking to develop their commercialization skills and manage their opportunity portfolio.

Peter is a capable entrepreneurial thinker, negotiator and deal maker. He has strong experience in business planning for new ventures, sound IP, financial and legal competencies, and has worked extensively with the venture capital (VC) and business angel (BA) industry.

Peter’s career started in the IT industry working along-side Hewlett-Packard engineers. This developed his unique ability to provide stakeholders with simple and easy to understand explanations of highly complex IP and technologies.

Peter speaks publicly and lectures at tertiary level (nationally and internationally) as a subject matter expert on the topic of commercialization, corporate entrepreneurship and innovation. Peter is also a regular writer on the topic and has been published in various business magazines and periodicals.

Peter’s contribution to the fledgling entrepreneurship and innovation industry has also been realized in the conceptualization and launch of Pitch Club, a successful event and web site that assists startup ideas connect with capital and knowledge.
