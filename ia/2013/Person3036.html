Alana Edmunds' Summary

A Summa Cum Laude graduate of Syracuse University, I have a B.S. in Information Management & Marketing. At SU, I was awarded '08 Homecoming Queen, Promising Young Technologist by TACNY, and an Undergraduate Research Award by the Computing Research Association.

I've been incredibly lucky to work on meaningful and interesting digital projects for Fortune 500 companies including Mobile, Web, E-commerce, Social, Software and Process Improvement.

Beyond work, I enjoy blogging, travel, cooking, e-books, learning photography, NYC Ballet.

My friends and colleagues would describe me as passionate, goal-oriented, detailed, focused, and excited about technology. If you would like to learn more about me, feel free to reach out and connect!
