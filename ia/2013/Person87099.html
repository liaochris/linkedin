Mark McHugh, RI Real Estate Specialist's Summary

My energy and commitment to helping others has benefited a multitude of clients over the years. As the Principle Broker and Owner of Clearmark Real Estate,llc, I offer expertise in all areas of residential real estate throughout Rhode Island . I hold the distinct national designations as a Senior Real Estate Specialist, Accredited Buyers Representative and Bronze Circle of Sales designation with the Kent Washington Board of Realtors. I am dedicated to providing the highest level of service; my friendly attitude is always well received.

Giving back to the community is one of my deepest values. A member of the Warwick Rotary Club, my service to others makes a difference. Honored by the club in 2010 for being Rotarian of the Year.I volunteer as the Treasurer of the Warwick Neck PTO, and President of the Warwick Neck Improvement Association, sponsor of Warwick American Little League, Buckeye Brook Association,Save the Bay, Warwick Junior Rifle League and countless others.

Recently, in 2011 I was Mayorially appointed by to the Warwick Tax Assesor's Review Board. A three person board to review Commercial and residential properties and the assesment of value for tax purposes. We engage in weekly site visits and review hearings as a service to the citizens on Warwick.

I’ve been married to my wife Cara, a Providence School Teacher since 2000. We share time with our son Ian, a seventh grader and seven year old daughter, Alexa Rose and Theodore, who is two years old. We live in the Warwick Neck section of Warwick.Our passions for the outdoors include swimming, boating, bike riding, gardening and cooking.

“My goal is to be the calming influence while helping others through one of the most emotional events of their lives; purchasing or selling property “.

Specialties

Local residential real estate expert in the City of Warwick. Senior's Real Estate Council, specializing in the needs of senior homeowner's and their ability to enjoy a better quality of life. Accreditted Buyers Represenative specializing in representing a buyer in a real estate transaction.
