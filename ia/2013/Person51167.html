J. Martin's Summary

Professor of Media & Communication Management and Lecturer in Game Design at Mediadesign University for Applied Sciences in Düsseldorf; freelance writer, scientific consultant, and creative director copy & communications with technical expertise and over 12 years of experience in integrated design, evaluation, execution, and editorial support for internal and external organizational, brand, and product communication. With a focus on social/mobile/real time media, storyfication, gamification, and transmedia storytelling. Clients I serve include organizations, marketing divisions, advertising agencies, publishers, and owner-operated small and medium-sized businesses from the region.
