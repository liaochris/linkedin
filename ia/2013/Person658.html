Aaron Uyehara's Summary

Aaron Uyehara is a business developer, social entrepreneur, graphic and screen designer, currently residing off and on in the Philippines and Hawaii.

His passion comes from art and design which led him into the apparel business at a young age which included designing, production/manufacturing, marketing and distribution.

He helped establish and manage a BPO/ Business Process Outsourcing company in the Philippines. They offered services ranging from high end Web development to lower end business outsourcing needs such as Branding, SEO, online marketing campaigns, etc. They also offered services such as virtual assistance, Internet marketing services and rentable call center seats.

He served a mission for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints in Nagoya/Tokyo North, Japan and speaks fluent Japanese, Portuguese and now since living in the Philippines for almost two years has picked up Tagalog as well.

Most importantly, Aaron is happily married to Madelene Joy and has 3 wonderful boys. Aaron graduated from BYU-Hawaii in International Cultural Studies and has recently finished his Masters degree in Internet Marketing through Full Sail University.

Specialties: Business Process Outsourcing, Internet marketing, SEO, SMM, start ups, branding and identity, graphic design, UI UX design, web development, business consulting, Information Architecture, Usability, Findability, Mac, Adobe, Html/CSS, Internet, Javascript, Jquery, Wordpress, Drupal, other CMS, clothing and apparel design and production.
