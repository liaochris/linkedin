Mike Burrows' Summary

In a career spanning aerospace, software tools, banking, energy and the public sector I've been a global development manager, IT architect and IT director. As a consultant I now help organisations evolve and transform at scale, taking a values-based approach to Lean, Kanban and Agile. I'm not your typical practices-centric Agile coach - I thrive on portfolio-scale problems and typically engage with leadership teams, helping them address the reality of how things work end-to-end, not just within teams. I'm also a regular speaker at international conferences and internal events.

I can help you manage more transparently, prioritise more effectively and deliver more predictably. I work on making leadership and learning integral to the way business is conducted at every level in your organisation, so that benefits continue to accumulate to the workforce, the wider organisation and its customers alike.

I'm well known in the Kanban community as an experienced practitioner, communicator and teacher, and for some notable contributions to the way Kanban is understood, taught and applied worldwide. I'm a member of the management board of LKU's Accredited Kanban Training (AKT) program and on the advisory board of its Kanban Coaching Professional (KCP) program.

My job is to help you understand your organisation’s systems more deeply (not just conceptually, but how they really perform in practice), to help find improvements, and to transfer the capability to sustain improvement over the longer term. This means much more than team coaching centred on effective work practices (though it can start there); as a former manager I understand very well the power that management has to create the conditions for success.

I come from a development background, an experienced global development manager, programme manager and product manager (and I still love programming) but much of what I do has application outside of IT. Success is about so much more than technology!

Teamwork and collaboration are recurring themes in my work and I partner internationally with some renowned consultancies both large and small. Much of my work has been abroad but don’t be put off if you’re UK-based – if you’re within reach of Matlock Bath in the Derbyshire Dales I would be especially delighted to hear from you!
