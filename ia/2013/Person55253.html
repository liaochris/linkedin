Bob Trainor's Summary

43 years of experience & training in mfg. & distribution operations as an Industrial Engineer, Mfg. Mgr, IE & Business Consultant. One of my passions is to help keep manufacturing jobs in the USA. I want to give my grandchildren a choice to work in a professional capacity for a profitable, growth orientated and resilient manufacturing company in the USA with a sound dynamic leadership team that is very much in touch with their customer needs and wants.

Specialties: Change agent, problem solver, profit finder, process improver, miner for cost savings, harvester of low hanging fruits of opportunity.All done with a "can do" attitude.
