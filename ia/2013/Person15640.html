Russ Bombardieri's Summary

Russ is a prominent front-end developer, information architect (IA), and champion of web standards with more than 12 years experience providing creative solutions that promote brand, products, and services. Areas of expertise include online retail/ecommerce, user interface design and development, usability testing, conversion optimization, performance-based online marketing, and branding development.

Russ specializes in conversion centered design (CCD) and has a passion for improving products with purposeful, pragmatic, elegant, and usable design solutions.
