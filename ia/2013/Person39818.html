Ed van Eeden's Summary

Ed van Eeden works for over 20 years as a freelance writer, ghostwriter, translator, interviewer and journalist.
The last few years he's been starting up an internet marketing business of his own, with a series of websites.
The next step is performing internet marketing services for companies.

Specialties

Writing, ghostwriting and translating books. Doing and teaching (written) journalism. Internet marketing (SEO, link and traffic building)
