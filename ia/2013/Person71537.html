John Boggs' Summary

I have hired, trained and managed over 170 salespeople in my career, many of whom now own their own companies, run the companies of others or are just very successful salespeople. Those no longer in sales have a clear understanding of sales and even clearer understanding of their own strengths. Much of my career is associated with new business start-ups or turn-arounds of mature companies each involving extraordinary revenue growth. I've set national sales records in three different industries.

Specialties

Radio (Certified Radio Marketing Consultant), Television, Magazines, and Internet advertsing sales management and development.
