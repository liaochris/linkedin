Adele Wilson-Hamaker's Summary

Currently I work at The Trust for Public Land, an amazing non-profit where we conserve land for people.

I co-owned Me and Her Designs before creating The Hive Group in 2009, both based in Eugene, Oregon.

In 2011, I moved to Berkeley, California and began working full-time at The Trust for Public Land. I've worked as head Media Designer for OsoEco, a startup. I've been responsible for designing brand images from the bottom up, developing media campaigns, and working closely with web-tech teams in implementing usable designs. My video work has earned 3 MarComm gold awards.

Specialties

usability testing, user work-flow, social media, website design/development, print materials (brochures, direct mail, posters, newsletters, etc.), creative writing, independent film-making, scriptwriting, video shorts, proven ability to work with technology, design and marketing teams
