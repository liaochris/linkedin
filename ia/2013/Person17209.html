Brian Blankenship's Summary

As interactive creative director at Balcom Agency, Brian helps shape the business and creative vision for the interactive division. Inventive philosophy, entrepreneurial spirit and working with the best team at Balcom (affectionately called the B team) have helped him produce innovative, award-winning work of the highest caliber.

With over 16 years of interactive experience, Brian specializes in website and web-based application design and development. He uses a unique blend of user-centered, strategic thinking with dynamic creative executions. He has produced award-winning websites and print collateral for clients such as Justin Boots, Blockbuster, The Modern Art Museum of Fort Worth, Tony Lama, Alcon, Cash America, Texas Health Resources, Neeley School of Business at TCU, Ben Hogan Foundation, Norman Regional Health System, Barnett Shale Energy Education Council, Kehilan Arabians, Williamson Dickie and Rosewood Hotels & Resorts.

Among his numerous ADDY awards (including Best of Interactive 2009), Brian has been honored by the DSVC and Graphic Design USA. His logo and design work has been published in numerous books, including many of the acclaimed LogoLounge series.

Prior to the Balcom Agency, Brian ran his own creative shop and founded several successful Internet companies. He earned his Bachelor of Fine Arts degree from the University of North Texas and is a long-standing member of the American Institute of Graphic Artists (AIGA) and Dallas Society of Visual Communications (DSVC).

In his spare time he enjoys spending time with his wife and two children, going to Texas Rangers games (Go Rangers!) and occasionally trying to put a little white ball into a small hole in the ground.
