Jimmy Slagle DartThrowTrading's Summary

They Call Jimmy Slagle the “Dart-Throw Trader”. His unique investment and trading strategies get consistent and predictable results. That’s the reason he closed his investment advisory and wealth management practice and formed Dart Throw Trading. The company provides investor coaching and trading education for investors and traders who want to ultimately get “free” of the outdated conventional “Wall Street” wisdom.

Dart Throw Trading, the company’s core investment training, has revolutionized the investment-education arena. With its combination of audio, visual, and written materials, Dart-Throw allows students to learn in the way that suits them best. The program content leverages over 20 years of Jimmy’s successes and early struggles to build his own strategic investment program.

Dart Throw training sessions fill up quickly because of the outstanding results achieved by investors and traders at all levels – from college-aged beginners to multi-account managing veterans. Many of the program’s graduates are now money managers and trading coaches in their own right.

Although Jimmy Slagle is the driving force behind Dart Throw Trading, the company has expanded to include multiple volunteers, advocates, and independent contractors who now work with Jimmy to develop products and services that expand the reach of Jimmy’s teachings. Jimmy’s teaching has impacted the lives of over 100,000 investors in the last 20 years.

Dart Throw Trading has fueled its extraordinary growth by teaching proven trading skills. Not only does this create strong relationships with potential investors and traders, but encourages feedback as well. The company uses the feedback to quickly create relevant programs and services that address struggles and provide solutions for beginning to advanced investors and traders.

Dart Throw Trading helps its clients to: “Throw Darts - Pick Stocks - Make Money.”

Celebrities and the wealthy have advisors to guide them with important money decisions, and so should you, says financial educator and celebrity trader Jimmy Slagle. Except, he adds, we should have advisors when we need them most -- as we learn how to break out of debt and build a comfortable financial nest-egg.

Slagle is known as the “Dart Throw Trader” for his innovative and revolutionary trading style that delivers profits on practically every stock, under any market condition.

As a financial educator, Slagle encourages new traders to rely on the support groups he calls Mastermind Teams. Once traders become wealthy, Slagle asks them to “pay it forward” and donate to a charitable investment which helps fund Tires for Tots, a foundation structured to help those in need secure new tires for safe driving.

Join Slagle and celebrate 25 years of Dart Throw Trading beginning October 4, as he kicks off his year long, celebrity filled, charitable fundraising journey throwing darts, picking stocks, and making money. During this year long cross country event, Slagle will attempt to raise $100,000 to benefit local charities and re-create the written record of trading the markets with nothing but a dart, a specially crated stock ticker symbol dartboard, simple trading strategies, and 25 years of off-the-floor investing experience.

Options trader Jimmy Slagle has been throwing darts at his proprietary stock symbol dartboard and making money for nearly a decade. His new book, MERCATO: The Underground Art of Wealth Creation, is based on his most successful trading and investing principles. And his year long work in progress Dart Throw Trading Journal will chronicle the trades, charities, and journey of the Dart Throw Trader beginning October 4, 2013 through October 2014.
