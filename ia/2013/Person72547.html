Aakash (Kash) Master's Summary

Please feel free to send me an invite as I am an open networker here on Linkedin: kash@kashmaster.com

For the past 20 years, Aakash Master has built an impressive industry portfolio with experience in mortgage banking, consumer lending and residential & commercial real estate, having worked in various capacities as an owner, manager and sales leader.

An astute, results-oriented professional with a proven track record for success, Aakash’s strengths lie in his ability to identify growth opportunities through careful market and financial analysis and the development of strategic business alliances designed to increase sales, profitability and product awareness.

Through the years, he has built businesses from the ground up having planned and executed innovative marketing strategies, and strategically forming a successful alliance with a high-caliber sales force to drive his operations. His customer service, management and sales experience have provided him with a foundation for success that couples a client-centric business philosophy with exceptional creativity and ingenuity. A licensed Real Estate Broker, Aakash obtained a Bachelor of Science degree in Business Administration & Finance from the University of Central Florida.

Additionally, Aakash currently serves as a Mentor (Entrepreneur in Residence) for Cal State Fullerton's Mihaylo College of Business and Economics - Center for Entrepreneurship.

Aakash's community involvement includes serving on the Board of Directors for the Tustin Chamber of Commerce and being an avid supporter of Orangewood Children’s Foundation and Hope Endowment.
