Elene Marsden's Summary

My main focus is enabling businesses to develop great relationships with their prospects and customers through powerful marketing strategies.
We love helping our customers to set up opt-in auto responder forms so that they can use an optimal blend of social media channels and online marketing to grow their lists and nurture their relationships with their own customers and prospects.

  • Swiftpage ACT Segment your Databases with Lookups 2013
