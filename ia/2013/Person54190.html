Blair White's Summary

Since starting flying fixed winged aircraft at the age of 12 and helicopters at the age of 14, you could say I have been hook on aviation for more all my life. Having built my initial business career owning New Car Dealerships, I made the decision to follow my passion and pursue a career in my one true love, aviation. The first of the year I launched International Aircraft Brokers, with the idea of shaking things up in the sometime stale aviation brokerage business. I strive daily to produce long lasting connections in the aviation field and one-day plan on the being the premier aviation company in the world. I will hopefully achieve such goals but using very simple ideas - honesty, integrity and hard work...

Please feel free to check out my website at www.InternationalAircraftBrokers.com and remember I am also looking for a business opportunity that good people can come together and do great things...

Please feel free to contact me anytime at Blair@InternationalAircraftBrokers.com or by phone at 786-566-7087.
