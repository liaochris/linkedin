John Orr, CCIM's Experience

Vice President | Retail Services Group

Why do Institutional Owners, Marquis Tenants and regional developers choose John Orr, CCIM and his team? Over twenty-five years of brokerage experience in the Southeast and the Charleston area commercial real estate market. John is a retail expert, providing attentive professional services including landlord representation, tenant representation and investments. His expertise and market knowledge is not only in Charleston, but extends throughout South Carolina as he represents clients across the State. The Colliers Charleston Retail Team focuses on institutional owner representation, marquis retailers and retail developer services.
John is a Vice President with Colliers International and is a market leader within the industry and the community.

Leasing, Brokerage and Management of the third party retail throughout the Coastal Region of South Carolina from Charleston. President

Rebellion Management, Inc.

1986 – Present (27 years) Charleston, SC

John Orr has operated Rebellion Management, Inc., a privately held Commercial Real Estate Brokerage and Management Company focusing in the Southeast prior to joining Colliers International. Commercial Real Estate Veteran John Orr founded Rebellion Management, Inc. in early 2009 after the sale of his interests in another Real Estate Investment and Development Company he co-founded in 2002. In addition to earning his CCIM designation, John has active Real Estate Brokerage Licenses in South Carolina, North Carolina, Georgia, and Florida allowing Rebellion Management to serve Clients regionally as well as locally. In addition to Commercial Real Estate Brokerage John has been involved in over 3 million square feet of Shopping Center and NNN Net Leased Retail Acquisition, Disposition and Build To Suit Development. John has managed all aspects and processes of the development cycle including site selection, contract and lease negotiation, due diligence, entitlement, design, financing, property and asset management and dispositions. This experience, uncompromising client service, and attention to detail make Rebellion Management a value for your Commercial Real Estate needs.

Co-Founder

Twin Rivers Capital, LLC

May 2002 – May 2009 (7 years 1 month)

I Co-Founded Twin Rivers Capital in early 2002 with the specific purpose of servicing the niche of Net Leased Retail Build-To-Suit Development in the Southeast. Over the course of several years I developed over 50 Net Leased Retail projects. As ancillary services I created business units of Property Management, Leasing, Investment Sales and Construction. In addition to creating a profitable development platform I successfully branded TRC and grew the Company from two to twenty people while positioning the Company as a true market leader.

Development Partner

James Doran Company

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Real Estate industry

August 1998 – December 2001 (3 years 5 months)

As a Development Partner with James Doran Company I was charged with the task of identifying Grocery Anchored Shopping Center development opportunities. This included market studies, site selection, anchor lease negotiations, budget oversight, and hiring and management of local leasing personell. In this role I completed developments for Kroger, Publix and Food Lion.

Leasing Agent

Equity Investment Group

March 1995 – August 1998 (3 years 6 months)

I joined Equity Investment Group as the only leasing agent in the Atlanta Office. Over the course of my tenure I leased over two million square feet of retail space and sold out-parcels throughout the southeastern portfolio.
