Marc Winn's Summary

I work with people with big dreams, helping them to develop big world changing ideas and radically improve their personal performance.

Every individual has the potential to do something incredible in the world, and I work with them to deliver on that potential. My life’s work is to help great people achieve everything they are capable of.

I am unconventional, fun, exciting, open, clever, passionate and no-nonsense in my approach. I have great ideas, innovative solutions, laser-sharp insight and the innate ability to maximise potential.

I coach first and foremost because I enjoy helping people achieve more than they thought possible. I get a huge kick working with and helping those that can achieve the most. I genuinely believe and prove that I have a way of thinking and an approach that can take people beyond the best they thought they could be.

Specialties:
- Inspiring people to think bigger about what they can achieve and encouraging them to take that journey
- Examining complex problems and identifying highly creative solutions
- A deep understanding of the big picture and future megatrends
- Helping entrepreneurs get the best out of themselves
- Personal productivity specialist
