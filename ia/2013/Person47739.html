Geoff Cowles' Summary

I've been working in real estate for 7 years. I enjoy the business aspect of managing bank dispositions, primarily the importance placed on market analysis. But the most fun is working with friends, family and their referrals. The personal aspect of that business relationship is the most rewarding. For the most part I stick to Fort Collins and the Northern Colorado market, but also work and am familiar with the Denver Metro Area and some mountain markets too.

In addition to real estate I am now working part-time for Arms of Love, an orphanage organization that operates facilities in the Philippines and in Nicaragua (where I have volunteered the last three summers). It's a phenomenal organization with a powerful mission that I'm proud to be a part of!

Specialties: Investment / REO Properties, First Time Home Buyers, Listing or Selling Agent
