Kai Bates' Summary

I am an Urban Designer working on street design, public space design and analysis, mapping, and 3-D modeling. I am passionate about improving quality of life for people and reducing auto dependence in cities.

I recently completed the Master of Community & Regional Planning program at the University of Oregon. My primary study interests were urban design, placemaking, and sustainability.

In addition to urban design, I have more than 15 years of experience in web & print graphic design, project management, writing, and editing.

Specialties: Urban Design, Placemaking, Sustainability, Web & Print Graphic Design, Web Development, Project Management, Editing
