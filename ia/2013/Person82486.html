Lori Greene, AHC/CDC, CCPR's Summary

I have worked in the door and hardware industry for more than 25 years and I currently act as a resource for our employees and customers in the area of building, fire, and accessibility codes as well as specifications. I use my blog (www.iDigHardware.com) to educate and entertain people who are interested in the door and hardware industry.

Specialties

Doors, hardware, fire and building codes, accessibility requirements.
