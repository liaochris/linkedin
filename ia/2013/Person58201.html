Janelle Fike's Summary

I'm a freelance designer at Interabang Creative & a partner at Equus Productions; a digital production studio producing new media projects for independent artists & authors.

Fueled by my never-ending wanderlust, I'm also the co-creator of Kilometer 66; a multidisciplinary creative collaboration focused on curating the world's compelling and unusual offerings for the culturally curious.

Specialties: All areas of Graphic Design with a special interest in Identity Creation, Creative Strategy, and Branding Systems. Experienced in the realms of Packaging, Print, Merchandising, Typography, Web Design, and New Media with proficiency in Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, AfterEffects, Flash and Dreamweaver.
