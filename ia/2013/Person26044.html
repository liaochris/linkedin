Lisa Gomez-Osborn's Summary

Paragraphs 22 Coaching & Consulting, founded by Lisa Gomez-Osborn a Board Certified Master Christian Life Coach, provides coaching and consulting services to individuals, professionals and business owners.

Lisa has worked with others for over 15 years helping them to grow professionally, personally and spiritually. Some of Lisa’s objectives include coming alongside others and facilitating a growing self-awareness in her client’s lives by finding and developing their unique strengths. Lisa is a coach and a teacher at heart.

Some of Paragraphs specialties for individuals include: Personal Life Coaching, Christian Growth Coaching, Transformational Leadership Coaching, Christian Life Coach Training, Career Coaching, and Relationship Communication Coaching.

Paragraphs is committed to providing effective, innovative and results focused coaching and consulting for business in areas such as: Leadership Development & Training, Personality Styles Training, Small Business Coaching, Life Coach Training & Business Development, Contemporary Market Solutions, Managing Difficult People, Confidential Advising.

Lisa is creative and has a strong sense of what will work and doesn’t waste any time in making things happen She is known for her insightfulness and resourcefulness as a leader, as well as her piercing business discernment. Her one of a kind approaches and solutions are an asset to individuals and professionals alike. She is committed to leading with integrity and providing outstanding service.

Paragraphs 22 is the inspiration and design of Lisa Gomez-Osborn. Her vision is born from the perspective that our professional growth isn't confined to our job or even our performance individually. She believes in seeing and living life through the lens of the Big Picture. Our growth goals are ultimately always at stake when we start compartmentalizing our lives into distinctly separate areas. It's true that we are made up of many different parts, however we are nevertheless still a part of them all!

Paragraphs envisions growing people professionally, personally and physically into living healthier lives, prospering professionally, living stress-free balanced whole lives.
