Sheryl Checkman's Summary

Sheryl Checkman, owner of Sheryl Checkman Design LLC. is an award-winning Graphic Designer with over 29 years experience in corporate communications. She is a creative problem-solver and excellent project manager committed to producing high-quality work within strict time and budget parameters.

Sheryl has designed and managed projects of all sizes and scope, from concept through production, for a variety of organizations. Her expertise includes projects such as corporate brochures, annual reports, logos and corporate identity, website design, press kits, posters, invitations, sales kits, manuals, flyers and ads.

Her clients have included Johnson & Johnson, Cornell Tech, Pfizer, Natsource LLC, Filippo Berio, Arthur Ashe Institute for Urban Health, Blackwell’s Information Services, AKAM Living Services, Communications Strategies, Inc., PR Newswire, DataMorphosis Inc., Club Getaway, Dole Food Company, Schering Plough, The American Trauma Society, The Secondary School Admissions Test Board (SSATB), Beczak Envirnmental Education Center, Beth Israel Latino Health Institute, Inside Sports & Entertainment Group, etc.

Previously, Sheryl was Vice President, Manager, Design Services for the Public Relations Agency, Burson-Marsteller from 1981-1992. In this capacity, she prepared and managed project budgets up to $500,000. She wrote proposals and made presentations to client senior management and managed a department of graphic designers and production artists.

Sheryl designed and trademarked a line of inspirational sports and activity-themed t-shirt designs called Life is Balance® which she currently sells online at http://www.lifeisbalance.com. Life is Balance® is represented by Paul Wheeler of Looking Good Licensing.

Additionally, Sheryl is also creates Underwater Digital Fine Art, custom Photomontages. She also designs and sells diving, yoga, and ski t-shirts and other unique gifts online at http://www.wearmyarttees.com.

Specialties

Graphic Design and creative problem-solving. Corporate Identity, logo design, branding, print collateral (brochures, press materials, invitations, posters,sales kits), advertising and website design.

Life is Balance® is a new line of apparel designed by Sheryl Checkman that celebrates the various sports and activities that balance our lives. In Chinese philosophy, yin and yang (heaven and earth) represent two opposing yet complimentary forces, or energies, that are said to be found in nature. One cannot exist without the other. They create balance. Each Life is Balance design has this symbol as its base graphic but is individually altered to represent a specific sport or activity. Along with each graphic is a tag line that delivers both an inspirational message as well as a reference to the sport or activity itself.

Life is Balance® is committed to quality. Our t-shirts for both women and men both look and feel good. They are so soft that you won’t want to take them off.
