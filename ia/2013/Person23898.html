* The ability to balance clients and business priorities which means I successfully meet commercial objectives

* Experience as Conference Speaker at ESSEC, INSEEC, Action’Elles and MEVO (Entrepreneurship Associations), …

Christiane recently graduated with a Master in Strategy from ESSEC, a world leading business school. Her thesis, “The Social Media Impact on Business”, addressed how web 2.0 technologies have triggered a societal shift and a radical change in communication patterns.     • Thèse, ESSEC
    • May 21, 2010
    Authors: Christiane Delarbeyrette

La marque peut cibler ses clients, accéder à de nouveaux segments de la population grâce aux outils de socialisation qui ont chacune des spécificités attirant des profils de consommateurs différents.
