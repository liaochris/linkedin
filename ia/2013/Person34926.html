Adam Washington's Summary

With a background in arts and video games, I spent the beginning of my career running one of Australia’s largest game magazines and building early digital publishing models. I've spent recent years working in advertising, business development and strategy across blue chip brands for leading global agency networks.

With proven operational, account growth and business development skills, my roles have included bought, owned and earned strategy, traditional ATL campaign development, CRM program planning and design, brand development and emerging tech integration (with a focus on mobile).
