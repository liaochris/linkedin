Brian Kissinger's Summary

The experience of yesterday helping you today, plan for tomorrow.
Brian's grandfather started the real estate agency in 1933. This may be one reason that Brian is a natural success as a Realtor and has an innate vision for real estate. Brian has been active in real estate for well over ten years. Brian and Chloe are a husband and wife team that met at Walt Disney World many years ago. That moment started a love affair with each other and with applying the Disney magic to everything we do ever since. We joined forces in the real estate world in 2003. It is our absolute thrill to help our clients make their real estate dreams come true!
Services offered:
Anything related to Real Estate. Brian specializes in listing and selling real estate as well as advising and negotiating real estate transactions. Brian's experience contributes to the great success of our business. Integrity, perseverance and outstanding service are qualities our clients have commended about our team.
Specialties:
Residential sales, investment properties, commercial properties, land development, and retirement living communities.

Certifications: ABR®, SRES®, CRS®, REALTOR®
Both Brian and Chloe are certified as Accredited Buyer Representatives, Senior Real Estate Specialists. Brian also holds the CRS® designation. In addition to these designations, Chloe holds a Paralegal Certificate from Penn State University and a Master's of Science degree from Lesley University, Boston.
Brian has consistently been a Multi-Million Dollar Producer since he started in the business over ten years ago.
We have two small children. We consider our clients an extension of our family. Our service goes beyond the purchase or sale of a property to a personal connection and continuous resource for our clients. This helps us grow our business on a referral basis. If you know someone with real estate needs or questions, please feel free to encourage them to call us directly at 814-574-0889.
