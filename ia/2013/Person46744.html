Gail Shields-Miller's Summary

Gail Shields-Miller is the principal designer of Shields & Company Interiors, which has completed design projects of both a contemporary and traditional nature.

Her work is highlighted by:

* Design magazines
* Books
* TV shows
* Dezignlicious.com: her own art & design blog

She has participated in numerous design events in the New York metropolitan area including:

* “Design on a Dime” for Housing Works
* New York Junior League House Tour
* ASID Designer Week
* “Living with Craft” at the Museum of Arts & Design
* The prestigious Kips Bay Show House

Gail views interior design as an art form and creates unique interiors that reflect

* Context
* Artistic sensibilities
* Furniture composition
* Client’s desire

Color, texture and pattern are her signature elements.

With an eye for things “green”, she is a member of the Sustainable Furnishings Council. Gail values local talents, materials and services whenever possible.

Specialties

Full service interior design, including architectural services through to furniture, accessories and art. Gail prides herself on her ability to design custom furnishings for all types of spaces.
