Christopher Neil Watson's Summary

I am an enthusiastic and hardworking professional who never backs away from a challenge and I continuously looking for new ways to further my personal and professional skill set. I have worked within the IT industry for the past eight years working on a broad scope of projects covering; design, development, infrastructure and business aspects of the industry. I have been lucky enough to have worked on some exciting projects with some of the biggest brands in the world, from software companies to international fast food chains.

My experience spans from IT infrastructure support to IT Sales and Marketing with a few more technical roles in-between. Over the past few years I have spent the majority of my time in an account management role managing the development of web based applications and online payment integrations(500+) with some co-location/managed services thrown in.

I pride myself on making my clients life a little bit easier by monkey proofing systems and maintaining open and honest communication channels. If you’re looking for a highly-motivated person to help build and maintain your online presence who also knows how to make a sale online, contact me.
