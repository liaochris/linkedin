Jasti Simmons' Summary

I strive to be the best whether on or off the clock. To continually be an asset to my clients, I have to be passionate, educated and thorough at all times.

I am not afraid to take on any campaign that encompasses diverse needs. And being a former journalist, I understand the importance of expediting projects quickly and empowering others through well-crafted writing.

Strengthening my clients' online presence is my passion. It helps those like nonprofits to raise money for AIDS awareness or to fight poverty. It helps local politicians to develop a voice in their communities, building a bond with the people. I thrive when seeing the good that comes from my projects.

Aside from work, I love volunteering and playing coed soccer with my league. These activities keep me balanced and happy all around.
