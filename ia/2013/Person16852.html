Brennan Barney's Summary

18 years experience as a senior designer, team lead and Creative Director specializing in high-end new media design and development. Involvement in 300+ project life cycles for a wide variety of clients from automotive to professional sports teams.

Highly competent in managing, designing and developing highly creative, user-focused, goal-oriented digital media. Experienced in interactive web, mobile, social, print and application design as well as human computer interaction, user experience and reflexive methodologies.
