Ameera Al Kooheji's Summary

It is difficult to describe my professional experience in a conventional manner as I am a person who is rather unconventional. Throughout my educational and working years, I changed fields several times following a single principle: find what makes you happy and do it.

Although I graduated with a BS in International Business, my educational background is mainly in Journalism, TV & Film. I spent the first 4 years of my professional life in the marketing field where I got my managerial experience and skills. During that time I started my own business opening a fashion/ tailoring house for two years while finishing my studies. When I was finally fulfilled and financially capable, I left my country and moved to New York to pursue a personal passion which was Music. This led me to get involved in the EDM world and hence audio production/ sound engineering. After taking several production courses and music lessons I started creating my own music and collaborating with other international DJs and artists. I hosted my own online radio show and was involved in a number of writing and translation jobs for other artists and websites and occasionally recorded voice overs for TV, radio and corporate projects. Somehow my career as a voice over talent kicked off and I was recording everything from telephone on hold messages to TV promos on major channels. This led me to start my own production house and I built my own studio where I work on audio projects and music. And after producing music for a TV show I was offered a position as a reporter in BTV which I welcomed gladly. I entered the world of modeling (print ads not runway) last year and did a couple of ads .In addition to music I also have a passion for acting which is something I did earlier in my life so I always welcome photo shoots and acting jobs.
I suppose that my goal is to always be involved in something that allows me to express my artistic and creative side and continue to learn and explore.
