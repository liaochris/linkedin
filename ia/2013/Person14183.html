Bill Bosler, P.E.'s Summary

My specialty is in Forming and Leading Teams to Achieve Specific Objectives within Time and Budget. My passion is changing the way Knowledge Workers are “Served Validated, Actionable Information” and Control their Business Processes.

As a Team Leader or Subject Matter Expert, I am very effective at working with the Client and the multi-discipline, multi-cultural Project Team(s) to develop and clearly document the Project’s Objectives and Constraints; convert those Objectives to an effective Project Plan; and then, execute that Plan through effective and timely communication and documentation.

I have applied these skills within Oil and Gas Production and Distribution, LNG Production and Shipment, Petroleum Refining Processing and Marketing, Petrochemical Plant Processing and Marketing, Electricity Generation, and Alternative Fuels in over 30 countries on six continents.

A typical project involved an integrated manual or computerized data solution. The Business Processes were documented along with all the associated Information Flows, Transformations, Data Structures, etc.

To Serve Validated, Actionable Information” and enable Knowledge workers to Control their Business Processes, I am facilitating the creation of an Engineering Management of Change System based upon ISO 15926 with Integration via ISA 95’s extended B2MML.
This clearly places the Responsibility for System Configurations, etc. on Engineering; Business Processes and Data Validation Rules on Business; and Transparent Implementation on IT.

Specialties: Experience in Oil, Gas & Power Supply Chain Segments in ERP, MES, & Control Domains with the following "soft" skills:
- Good Listener, Negotiator, Leader
- Master Planning Experience (PERA)
- Major Capital Project and Facilities Maintenance & Turnarounds
- Practical ERP Experience, including. Accounting, Value Chain, and Master Data Management
- Understands Best Practices and Associated Standards
-10+ Years’ Experience Working in Russia
