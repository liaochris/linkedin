Aaron Turner-Jones' Summary

I'm a concept and UX designer and all-purpose web geek living in Brisbane, Queensland. I’m a student of usability, a Creative Director and I specialise in internet based concept and front-end design for mobile devices, desktops & browsers.

I spend my time analyzing and organizing client information, creating communication strategies, and conceiving and designing technically efficient visual interface systems that serve a common theme.

Specialties

Concept Design, Creative Direction, User Experience, User Interface Design (web & mobile device), Brand Management, Studio Management, Online Marketing, Illustration, Photography and Traditional Print through to Multi-Media Production (animation, video & audio)
