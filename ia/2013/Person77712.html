Kyle H. Crooke's Summary

TÍR Design Studio (TDS) a professional landscape design firm. Offering our clients the design knowledge gained by over 15 years of project involvement. Our technical capabilities are "state of the art" for the industry including drought tolerant landscape, governmental agency processing & computer aided drafting & design.

The majority of our work experience is in California but we have done projects in Nevada, Florida, & Hawaii. Our workload has included typical home builder size residential lots to one or more acre custom residential lots & specializing in complete outdoor design.

TDS was established w/ the goals of working closely w/ every client to develop a design that brings inventive ideas for a solution that are both fresh & practical. We take a team approach throughout the design process which gives you direct involvement into the consideration of your landscape desires that integrates into a functional solution. Our knowledge of industry standards, current product specifications, probable construction costs & long-term maintenance issues is the result of experience gained in overseeing numerous projects from master planning through the construction phase.

Principal involvement from project inception to completion & adherence to the design process are the methods our firm employs to ensure a successful project. CADD technology is utilized to provide accurate & efficient information flow between disciplines & achieve high quality graphic clarity. TDS is prepared to assist in obtaining and evaluating contractor bid proposals to facilitate proper interpretation of contract documents. During the field observation phase we review project installation of landscape construction elements, planting & irrigation items.

Contact us at www.tir-design.com & we will arrange to meet w/ you at your home. The initial consultation is FREE and without obligation.
