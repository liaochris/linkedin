Kevin Buckley, CPC's Summary

Executive Search Specialist and Certified Personnel Consultant (CPC, 1989).
Founder of Buckley Search Inc.
Freight Forwarding, Customs Brokerage, Corporate Logistics, and Supply Chain Management recruitment and consulting is our focus.

Active in Canada and the USA.

Specializes in Executive, Senior, Middle and Junior management positions within Customs Brokerage, Freight Forwarding, Customs Compliance, and Logistics/SCM.

Actively supports the education and professional development goals of industry employers and professionals, and the governing industry associations - CIFFA, CSCB, IE Canada, CITT, The Logistics Institute and the PMAC through the Education Programs page in our site:

http://www.buckleysearch.com/education.htm

Author of "Insider's Guide To Job Search" recommended by the Riley Guide - the Internet's foremost authority on Job Search and Career Guidance. Author of "The Insider's Guide To Candidate Selection."

Google: 'job search guide' for more information and resources.

Specialties: Executive Search in Freight, Customs, Logistics, and Supply Chain Management in Canada and the USA.

Industry Compensation Specialist in Canada for Freight Forwarding & Customs Brokerage.

Provider of annual Salary Averages in Freight Forwarding, Customs Brokerage and Corporate Logistics; supplying these averages to industry hiring executives since 1990.
