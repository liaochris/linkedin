Julien FAURY-RAMOIN's Summary

Always looking for a new challenge - I am a dynamic, hard-working and sociable Graphic Designer with more than 3 years experience.

Originally from the south-east of France, I have been living in London since July 2013 and have always been attracted to the “City of Design”.

My main areas of interest are in DTP, infographic, layout design, print design, identity/branding, web design and html/css. I have completed a large number of successful design projects ranging from logo design to creating communication materials for businesses, both as a member of a design team and on a freelance basis.

I also took part in national and international projects and events for a major customer: Vinci Energies France.

Thanks to this experience, I am able to work with a wide range of brands and projects.
Being a good listener I understand thoroughly customers’ views and needs.
My capacity to adapt easily to customers’ requirements is one of my main skills.

I am passionate about Art, Street Art, Typography, Photography and new technologies.

Apart from Graphic Design, I am interested in Sport and Music. From 1993 to 2009, I managed, coached and developed an American Football team with about 80 members. The first team is today in the second National Division. From 1997 to 2002, I managed an association to promote the French alternative rock scene.
I organised concerts and festivals. The most important being an International Rock Festival which sold 6,000 tickets. I also helped some bands in their promotion.

I like to put all my energy into achieving my goals with a smile… and succeed!
