Haley Shapley's Summary

Whether covering mail-order dessert taste-testing, the best sights in Reykjavik, the history of needlepoint samplers, life at an inner-city high school, or alternative workouts, I make stories large and small engaging. Much of my writing covers travel, health, and retail topics, but I consider myself a generalist and am open to taking on articles of all kinds.

In addition to writing, I work often as an editor and project manager. I know both AP and Chicago styles, and I'm adept at learning house styles. I can also coordinate the entire production process of a magazine, from creating an editorial calendar to assigning out stories to sending the pages to the printer.

Specialties: Writing, copyediting, proofreading, fact-checking, research, blogging, custom publishing, content marketing, newsletters, ghostwriting
