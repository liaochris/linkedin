SHORT BIO
Dr. Augustine Fou is Digital Consigliere, advising marketing executives and global brands on digital strategy. He has over 16 years of management consulting experience and teaches courses on digital strategy and integrated marketing at Rutgers University and NYU.

Dr. Fou completed his PhD at MIT in Materials Science and Engineering at the age of 23. He started his career with McKinsey & Company and previously served as SVP, digital strategy lead, McCann/MRM Worldwide and Group Chief Digital Officer of Omnicom's Healthcare Consultancy Group (HCG).

  • Design, fabrication, and characterization of complex, multilayer heterostructures of conjugated and non-conjugated polymers via molecular self-assembly

    • PhD Thesis
    • May 15, 1995
    Authors: Dr. Augustine Fou - Digital Consigliere

    It will be demonstrated that self-assembly from aqueous solution provides a unique ability to manipulate an arsenal of electroactive polymers into ultrathin multilayer films. The molecular level control enables the tuning of the supermolecular architecture of these multilayer assemblies to exhibit novel characteristics that cannot be achieved with the components individually -- e.g. tuning of the color of the light emitted, the efficiency of conversion of electricity to light, the conductivity of the multilayer assembly, and the stability of the assembly to external factors such as heat and humidity.
