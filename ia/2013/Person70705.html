Julie Roth's Summary

I am a retained recruiter, headhunter, executive search consultant and former marketing executive for notable companies including Motorola. Originally from Latin America/El Salvador (bilingual English and Spanish) and bicultural, I’ve worked for a variety of companies and have deep exposure to all facets within an organization. As a recruiter I draw on these experiences to fully understand the organization’s pain and goals; and marketing skills to research and actively bring "on target" talent, particularly passive candidates, to the table.

As a seasoned global executive, I formed Blue Mark Partners in 2006 as a retained recruiting firm whose mission was to become the external partner to the hiring executive, the HR business partner and the internal talent acquisition professional. As I enter the eighth year of recruiting, I continue to measure success by repeat business. A Fortune 500 client has been so pleased with results they awarded the firm 15 retained projects within 18 months; 7 of which were diversity/minority hires. The main reason: they demand and get high performing talent which is most often passive candidates. The “secret sauce” partially lays in the fact that IT TAKES A SUCCESSFUL EXECUTIVE TO RECRUIT A SUCCESSFUL EXECUTIVE (SM). I bring extensive experience from within the heart of company operations to the full cycle executive recruiting process. Moreover, the deep knowledge of business functions, issues and practices across a broad range of industries enhances the understanding of the organization’s needs and goals, thereby leading to a robust result.

As an experienced diversity recruiter and certified Hispanic owned women’s business enterprise (WBE), I can help you meet all your diversity goals and needs.
