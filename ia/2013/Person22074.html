Charles Bonfiglio's Summary

My career began as a teenager with a strong passion in the automotive aftermarket that began with customizing cars and then evolved to becoming an ASE certified technician, then an automotive franchise area developer of Meineke Car Care Centers, and the Franchise President/CEO of Tint World®, and the President/CEO of Auto Parts Network®; an automotive ecommerce company that led to the development of a proprietary patented software system.

Through years of leading multiple companies to best in class operations, I have acquired unparalleled enterprise level experience and a diverse set of business and technical skills that brings unique value for my peers, franchise owners and employees.

I am a results-oriented professional with a successful background in business management, real estate development and creative finance. My particular area of expertise is leading automotive companies to maximize profits and market share with outstanding customer satisfaction through unparalleled team building leadership, broad-based experience and a winning attitude.

As a strategically driven, entrepreneurial minded visionary, I continuously seek to develop new business growth by leveraging opportunities through innovation and new integrated channels of revenue streams that maximize profitability while focusing on core business improvement. I am always open to new business opportunities as well as venture capital investors, mergers and acquisitions.

  • AutoVend® ONLINE E-COMMERCE SUPPLY OF AUTO PARTS INCLUDING DIRECT INSTALLERS, AUTO PARTS WEBSTORE AND B2B ONLINE ORDERING

    • United States Patent Application 2001/003465
    • Filed October 25, 2005
    Inventors: Charles Bonfiglio

    This invention relates generally to an e-commerce online method and system that allows customers and dealers to connect online to provide auto parts, auto repairs, and part installation that includes an online electronic catalog for customers and dealers to view and purchase auto parts electronically and for customers to schedule installation services from dealers.
    The AutoVend® System is a method to supply vehicle parts, vehicle repair, and part installation services between customers online in e-commerce and brick and mortar businesses that includes three separate programs. The Direct Installer™ method and system, a group of webstores, and a business to business parts ordering system each use the Unified Catalog™ that covers all the vehicle parts and repair services necessary or desirable for the system. The system includes a system of hosted websites, application software programs, and databases for its online e-commerce operation.

    The AutoVend® System is a combination of a Unified Database™ of disparate data sources unified into a single cataloging system coupled with real time inventory checking, pricing, order fulfillment, shipping, and invoicing -- from warehouse systems to the end consumer in B2B, B2C, or B2B2C environments.. AutoVend® is dynamically configurable and can take on multiple simultaneous business rules including proximity of customer, store, and warehouse locations, preferential treatment, or custom configurations..
