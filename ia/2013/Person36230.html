Privately Held; 5001-10,000 employees; International Trade and Development industry

2000 – 2003 (3 years) Khabarovsk Territory, Russian Federation

As a component (programme) leader of the FOREST project (Russia) I managed a team of international consultants, volunteers and NGO representatives. I liaised with Governmental agencies on different levels.

Success stories were published on the project website and in newspapers (local and federal).
