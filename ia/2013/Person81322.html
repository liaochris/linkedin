Lin O'Neill's Summary

Lin O’Neill is an effective and innovative consultant, trainer, speaker and coach with significant experience in strategy development and implementation, transition planning, cross-functional teams, change management, entrepreneurship and process improvement.

Clients include managers in Fortune 500 Companies, such as Dole and Allied Signal, where Lin has worked to impact their bottom lines through process improvement techniques.

She also develops successful transition planning to assist Fortune 500 corporate leaders as they move up the corporate ladder, and small business owners who are expanding into new areas or have decided to hand off their company to new leadership.

*Author of the book, “Managing High-Maintenance Employees”
*Adjunct for Texas Woman’s University's (TWU) Executive MBA program
*Dallas Instructor for Interise/SBA e200 Program
