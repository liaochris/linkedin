Meghan Harris' Summary

Meghan Harris is a successful business sales and marketing executive, specializing in marketing strategy and positioning, website and content marketing and lead generation, in BPO, SaaS, managed services, technology, internet, and telecom, and principal of Harris Consulting LLC (harrisconsultingllc.com).

Ms. Harris was recently SVP/CMO at TelStar Hosted Services, Inc. where she improved SEM to 60% more conversions at 70% less cost; grew website unique visitors 60%; raised page rank from 0 to 4/10; created >12 million impressions, and tripled total average monthly leads.

Harris Consulting LLC projects include:
* Content Marketing and Website Design and Development
* Positioning and Branding (Identity, content, website development)
* SEO, SEM, Advertising
* Lead Generation and Sales Enablement
* Social Media and Blogger awareness
* Customer Retention Programs
* Product Strategy and Primary Market Research
* Investor Materials, Sales Collateral
* Vertical Marketing Strategy, Strategic Partner Identification
* Brand Awareness/Consideration/Preference Research

As Marketing Director for Nextel Communications, Inc.’s North East region ($1.8 b profit center), Ms. Harris and her team of 24 grew net sales 140%. She managed a $29mm media budget, a $3.3mm CRM program, and supported 4,600 salespeople. As Marketing Director, Nextel New York's Tri-State Area, ($600 m profit center), she and her team of 13 grew net sales 240% and doubled leads.

As Director, Market Planning and Strategy, SkyTel Communications, Inc., she grew reseller sales 38% in 1 year. As Director, Business Development, she negotiated joint ventures in Europe and Canada. She was also a U.S. Foreign Service Officer.

Ms. Harris holds an AB from Princeton University cum laude, MA from Stanford University, and MBA from Harvard University, 2nd year honors.

Specialties: All aspects of marketing, from strategy to execution. See www.harrisconsultingllc.com.
