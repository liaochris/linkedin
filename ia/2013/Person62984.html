Jesse Spink's Summary

I create compelling digital experiences for big thinkers and fast companies.

-- Big Name Campaigns to Biometric Games --
I’m a successful, multidisciplinary Creative Director, User Experience Specialist and Game Designer with extensive experience creating everything from games and apps, to websites, digital campaigns and back. My career has spanned a variety of digital creative mediums and allowed me to work with thought leaders, cutting edge companies, and titans of industry on both sides of the Atlantic. Nike, Coca-Cola, British Airways and Hyundai, just to name a few.

-- Creative by Design --
As a creator of digital experiences and a client communication conduit, I’ve successfully lead multidisciplinary teams of creatives, user experience specialists and developers to craft the most impactful experiences possible. Whether concepting an idea to connect people through social dynamics, designing game mechanics to solve social issues, or getting down to the fundamental details of user experience, my extensive design legacy has made me extremely knowledgeable in the art of digital engagement.
