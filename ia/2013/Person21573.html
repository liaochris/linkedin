César Salazar's Summary

César Salazar is a Venture Partner at 500 Startups. From there, he works with some of the most promising tech startups from spanish speaking Latin America, mainly Mexico. Prior to that, he was a Founding Partner of Mexican.VC, the most noticeable seed fund and accelerator focused on Mexican Internet startups. Earlier this year, Mexican.VC was acquired by 500 Startups, scoring César his current position.

César was an entrepreneur for more than a decade before moving to the investment side. He's known as a key player in the creation of the Mexican entrepreneurial ecosystem. Through initiatives such as Hackspedition, Startup Dojo and SuperHappyDevHouse, he has been fostering tech entrepreneurship in his home country. Additionally, he's a regional advisor for the Startup Weekend organization.

Even though he identifies himself as an interaction designer, César earned a degree in Industrial and Systems Engineering from Tecnológico de Monterrey.
