Barbara A. Bowen's Summary

Barbara Bowen is an award winning wildlife and nature photographer and certified Florida Master Naturalist that has keen respect for nature and the outdoors. Barbara enjoys being outdoors and her appreciation for nature radiates itself through photography. Barbara enjoys hiking, kayaking, and leads birding hikes for Hernando Audubon. She also serves as a Board Member for Hernando Audubon as Director of Public Relations. Her tasks include creating and editing the monthly newsletter, webmaster, and maintaining social media, She also spends time volunteering for Florida Forest Service and Florida Fish and Wildlife Commission.

    • April 2011
    Authors: Barbara A. Bowen

    Authors: Barbara A. Bowen

  • Wild Azaleas put on a show along the Sopchoppy River

    • FootPrint - Fall 2011- Quarterly Magazine of the Florida Trail
    Authors: Barbara A. Bowen

    I was excited about seeing the pink blossoms but was not expecting to see very many…only single scraggly bushes scattered about. Well it was not long before we encountered our first Wild Azalea bush….desperate for a photo of this rare beauty….I climbed through a thicket of saw palmetto to get a close up. As we persisted, I was able to relax as the Wild Azales bushes were becoming more prevalent, the flowers fuller, and closer to the trail
