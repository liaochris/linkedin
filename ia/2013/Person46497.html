Gordon Stewart's Summary

A professional IT project manager; leading KPMG's ITS Global PMO and project management team. Gordon has been with KPMG International for 12 years, initially based in Amsterdam, then moving to NJ in 2007. Before joining KPMG, he managed large international IT projects for JPM Chase, BNP Paribas, Lloyds TSB, Vodafone & P&O Cruises. Prior to this, Gordon served nine years in the British Army and obtained a degree in Computing & Manufacturing Technologies. He also holds an Executive MBA from ENPC (Paris).

Specialties: Project and program management; Project management office - methods, controls & process; Project portfolio management.
