Eduardo Loureiro's Summary

Eduardo Loureiro is co-founder of DesignThinkers Brazil, Voël (Research and Design of digital products) and Mozaiko (Educational and Social Innovation project).

He is also teacher in several educational institutes and was part of the first Brazilian post-graduate course in Interaction Design.

Eduardo started his career as Interaction Designer and is local leader of IxDA. He is one of the pioneers of User Experience Design in Brazil and has been working for big brazilian brands/institutions in Brazil like Google, Itaú Bank, Coca-Cola, Ford, Red Bull, Havaianas and brazilian government.

His purpose is to create spaces/situations where people can connect, collaborate and change their lives.
