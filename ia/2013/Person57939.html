Jamie Boepple's Summary

Hi, I’m Jamie! I’m a print and web designer headquartered in historic Old Town Orange, California.

Some words clients have used to describe me: positive. enthusiastic. creative. reliable. energetic. funny. dependable.

I’ve had the luxury of working with a number of extremely talented designers & developers. They keep me inspired to learn new things and try new styles.

I love what I do and the people that I do it for. I am one of those lucky ones who has turned a passion into a career!

Specialties

Website design, book design, print production, poster design, illustration, branding & logo design, photography, photo retouching and marketing strategy.

Software: Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Dreamweaver, Quark, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Flash, iMovie, WordPress
