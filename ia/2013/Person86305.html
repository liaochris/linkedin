Mark Anthony McCray's Summary

Mark Anthony McCray is an entrepreneur, speaker, trainer, real estate investor, consultant and expert business coach who helps people live on PURPOSE, achieve higher PERFORMANCE and experience true PROSPERITY. As a professional speaker, writer and workshop facilitator, he helps churches, faith-based groups, professional organizations, youth groups and individuals achieve greater success in their personal and professional lives.

Known as a knowledgeable and inspiring teacher, Mark has launched several businesses, worked in full-time ministry, sponsored a number of charitable activities dedicated to helping youth move forward in life and served on municipal advisory boards such as the Austin Community Development Corporation Entrepreneur Development Program, Collegiate Entrepreneurs Organization, the Texas State University - McCoy College of Business Administration’s Management & Marketing Advisory Council and the City of Austin's Charter Review Commission.

Still, he gets his greatest joy from helping people discover and then maximize their purposes in life through teaching them how to find hope, employ winning methods and then incorporate accountability into their journeys. Mark has assisted thousands of entrepreneurs with their consulting, business and financial planning needs through his many seminars, workshops, outreach programs, radio interviews, published articles and one-on-one coaching and mastermind groups.

Recently adding published author to his resume, Mark has earned a Bachelor of Arts in Economics from the University of Texas and an MBA from Texas State University. Learn more about Mark, resources available from him and connect at http://livebigdieempty.com/

Specialties: Commercial real estate finance and development; mortgage banking and private real estate finance
