Barry Huizenga's Summary

I have been designing and developing web based solutions and brands for over 15 years, all the while continuing to educate and adapt myself to ever evolving technologies and trends in design, branding, usability, user experience, SEO and conversion.

What began simple as a way to express creativity has evolved into being responsible for designing highly usable user experiences aimed at increasing conversion and ROI utilizing beautiful graphic design and equally beautiful HTML5/CSS3/JQuery code conforming to SEO/W3C standards/cross browser/device compatibility (responsive/fluid design).

Notable companies I have had opportunity to work with and for include: Yourhosting, Starthosting, Justitie, NLJUG, Dimpact!, Autotaalglas, Volvo Cars North America, NASCAR Motorsports, Volvo IPD USA, Volvo S40 Racing USA, Ford Motor Company.

Specialties include: branding, conceptualizing, designing and developing beautiful, inspiring and well thought out web solutions and UX with focus on SEO, usability, standards, ROI and client expectations.
