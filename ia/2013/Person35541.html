Mark Woodcock's Summary

As an SEO agency director, as you can imagine, I am very passionate about all things digital i.e. internet marketing, search engines and social media in particular. I am fascinated by the fast moving evolution of the online environment and have been studying the ‘art form’ of SEO and other internet marketing since the early 1990’s. Some say this makes me a digital geek, I like to think it makes me a bit of an expert! Whichever is true, it has enabled me to develop a successful career working in an industry that I love.

Over the years I have developed my own portfolio of successful businesses and gained a wealth of experience working as an internet marketing and SEO agency consultant. I’ve been fortunate enough to work with a number of large companies, SEO agencies and digital agencies, advising them on the planning and implementation of their online marketing strategies. I’ve also learned a lot along the way and continue to research, absorb and learn in order to keep up to date with digital developments.

In 2009 I decided to set up my own Internet Marketing / SEO Agency, which is going from strength to strength. Bigfoot Digital specialises in SEO, Online PR, Social Media and Content Generation.

I love the work we do here at Bigfoot Digital, we have a very talented team, some great clients and achieve fantastic results. As always, we continue to look for new challenges and like nothing more than meeting new businesses that we can help.
