Carrie Wilkerson is the voice of experience. From corporate life to teaching high school to direct sales, to information marketing and coaching she has ‘been there and done that’ professionally and personally. An “overnight mom” with 2 toddlers through adoption, her priorities instantly changed and so did her workplace.
