Mattia Pavesi's Projects

  • ilDigitoriale

    • September 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Mattia Pavesi, Pierdomenico Baccalario, Davide Morosinotto

    Digitoriale è un blog nato per raccogliere e raccontare storie: storie di tecnologie e storie di libri, storie per ragazzi e storie per adulti che leggono con quella curiosità che è forma pura di passione e insubordinazione.

    In questo spazio sono selezionate e condivise notizie e sensazioni, umori e tendenze che giorno dopo giorno stanno cambiando, arricchendo e plasmando il nuovo e antichissimo mondo della lettura. Che la si chiami open source o open innovation, per noi è l’open davanti che fa la differenza.

  • DigiLAB - International Book Fair of Turin

    • May 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Mattia Pavesi, Davide Morosinotto, Luana Solla, Pierdomenico Baccalario

    DigiLab is the most important Digital Event of the International Book Fair of Turin, and it is dedicated to young people and school classes.
    It is located in the very heart of the fair and it provides a broad programme of meetings and conferences with the best Italian authors for Digitorial (digital + editorial) works. DigiLab also includes games sessions, digital writing and digital illstration workshops, and a new focus group about cyberbullying.

    The DigiLab goal is to show to the public all the new narrative possibilities of technologies, making tablets available to the young public in order to experiment and play together.

  • DigiLab: Scrivi il tuo eBook

    • May 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Mattia Pavesi, Davide Morosinotto, Pierdomenico Baccalario, Alessandra Berello, Luana Solla, Paolo Giovine, Andrea Pau, Tommaso Percivale, Lucia Vaccarino

    For the first time a real writer creates a new story together with his public, while a professional editor amends the texts and an illustrator draws the cover and the internal pictures. The story is then packaged and published as a free ebook in only 60 minutes: at the end of the workshop the participants can download and read again their story on a tablet or smartphone.
    "Scrivi il tuo ebook" is an exclusive event of Digilab and it takes place during the International Bookfair of Turin. The workshop is powered by Tessitori di sogni (www.tessitoridisogni.com) and PubCoder (http://pubcoder.com).

  • Joe Dever's Lone Wolf

    • June 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Mattia Pavesi, Davide Morosinotto, Pierdomenico Baccalario

    The original gamebook series of "Lone Wolf" by Joe Dever arrives on modern tablets and smartphones with a new astonishing story, stunning graphics and an incredible gameplay.
    The game is realized by Forge Reply with the contribution of Joe Dever & Atlantyca.
    In synergy with developers' creatives, our team worked on storytelling, narratives and their exploitation in the game mechanics.
