David Sams' Summary

Contact: David@Sams.TV
Join Sams on Twitter (@MrHit) and Facebook

David Sams is a BRAND EVANGELIST and MARKETING ARCHITECT. He designs blueprints that launch brands, Internet ventures, TV and radio programming, retail and direct response products, books, and personalities. Even some of the world’s most famous business executives and entertainment personalities have turned to Sams to help define who they are and what they stand for in the eyes of both the media and the public.

Sams is one of the world's most respected marketing and branding strategists, a nine-time Emmy® award-winning TV producer, and emerging technologies guru. He has been featured in the pages of TVGUIDE, USATODAY, and ENTERTAINMENT WEEKLY. He has appeared on 60 MINUTES, ENTERTAINMENT TONIGHT, NBC’S TODAY, DATELINE NBC, and WORLD NEWS TONIGHT. Sams is the archetypal entrepreneur a preeminent idea man whose track record is synonymous with success.

In 1986, Sams launched the OPRAH WINFREY SHOW into national syndication. His creative marketing efforts made Oprah a household name even before the talk show host hit the airwaves. She was #1 in nearly every TV market from her very first day on the air.

Sams helped to turn WHEEL OF FORTUNE and JEOPARDY into the two highest-rated shows in syndication history, while head of global marketing and creative affairs for a family owned syndication company called King World. Soon thereafter the company went public and became the darling of Wall Street and one of the most profitable companies in the nation. Years later, without a penny of debt on its books, King World sold for billions of dollars.

Sams is considered to be one of the greatest marketers in TV syndication history. He re-invented the way syndicated shows and personalities are marketed, making a lasting impression on viewers. To this day, WHEEL OF FORTUNE and JEOPARDY remain the top-rated shows in syndication, and Oprah launched her OWN network following a 25 year run in syndication.

Specialties

Product Brand Marketing; Personality Brand Marketing; Corporate Brand Marketing; TV Program Production, TV Program Distribution and Marketing; Internet Marketing; Direct Response Marketing; Music and Book Publishing Marketing; Motion Picture Marketing. Strategic Planning, Personnal Management, Business Development, Strategic Relationships, Content Acquisition.

Sams consults some of the world's leading broadcast and Internet companies, corporate ceo's and cmo's.
