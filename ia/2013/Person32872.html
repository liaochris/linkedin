Dawna Watson's Summary

Dawna has a passion for real estate that shows whether the client is a First Time Buyer, savvy Investor or an experienced Seller. She connects with her clients to get a complete understanding of their goals and needs which enables her to match each client with the perfect property.

She is also passionate about Community Service and has held positions as President of the Hilltop Elementary Parent Teacher Organization, Chairwoman for the local Chamber of Commerce, Cook County Election Judge and Chairwoman of the Hanover Township Committee on Youth (COY). She has been a volunteer for Habitat for Humanity, Feed My Starving Chidren. She is currently the Section Leader for Military Care at Willow Creek Community Church, serving local veterans and military families.

As a businesswoman she is the founding member and President of Business Network International's (BNI) Momentum Network in Bartlett, Illinois. Her philosophy is, "If you help others gain success with their business, it will create momentum and they will help others too.” She has also been quoted in recent blogs, and articles published by the Illinois Association of Realtors.

She enjoys meeting the client, assessing their wants and needs and then finding that perfect property. To Dawna, “Nothing is as exciting as that moment when the keys are handed over to the new owner, regardless of if I’m representing the buyer or the seller!”
