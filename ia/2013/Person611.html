Founder

Young Construction Professionals

March 2009 – Present (4 years 10 months)

Contrary to our group name it is our hope to connect individuals of all levels of experience and all corners of the construction industry; Architects, Engineers, Developers, Construction Managers, Contractors, Subcontractors, Vendors, etc. Our mission is to create a community of proactive individuals who expand their knowledge of the construction industry on a global to local level by exchanging ideas, experiences, questions, and advice with others in their industry. This group will offer an opportunity to connect with other people within our industry, provide an open forum to share "lessons learned" with other members, ability to exchange ideas and questions to others in the industry, and keep up to date with current construction news. From intern to executive, it is our goal to build a community that aims to improve the construction industry.
