Kevin Hofstra's Summary

Mr. Kevin Hofstra serves as the Technical Director and Deputy Vice President for the Avaya Government Solutions Network Engineering Division. In this role he directs the technical operations of the Network Engineering Division across 150+ employees providing services in Cyber Security, Network Operations, Network Design/Optimization, Incident Response, Systems Integration/Interoperability, Regulatory Compliance, Assured Services, Unified Communications, and IT Service Management.

In his primary operational role, Mr. Hofstra serves as the Program Manager for the elite US Air Force 38th Cyberspace Readiness Squadron (38 CYRS), also known as SCOPE EDGE. His program is responsible for providing cyber security and network optimization for 850,000 classified and unclassified network assets on Air Force, Department of Defense (DoD), Coalition, Federal and Corporate enterprise networks. Among other recognitions, his SCOPE EDGE program was awarded the 2010 DoD Cyber, Identity, and Information Assurance (CIIA) Award for outstanding contributions to the DoD Information Assurance Strategy of “Preparing for and operating through cyber degradation or attack.”

Mr. Hofstra has an extensive technical background with certifications that include the Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP) and Certified Authorization Professional (CAP) from ISC2, Cisco Certified Internetwork Expert (CCIE) #14619, CCNP/CCDP/CCSP/CCVP & from Cisco Systems, Microsoft Certified IT Professional (MCITP): Enterprise Administrator from Microsoft, and Project Management Professional (PMP) from the Project Management Institute. His educational background includes a Bachelor of Science – Computer Science from Yale University and two Masters of Engineering, one in Telecommunications and one in Engineering Management, from the University of Colorado. Mr. Hofstra has an active DoD Top Secret clearance.
