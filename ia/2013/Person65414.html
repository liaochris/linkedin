Joel Comm's Summary

I’m Joel Comm, an entrepreneur, New York Times Best Selling Author, and new media innovator. I have consistently been described as one of the world’s foremost authorities on Social Media, Internet Marketing, eCommerce and Mobile applications.

When speaking at public and corporate conferences, I tap into a lifetime of diverse and interesting professional experiences to inspire and entertain attendees and to spark new ideas. I help executives and professionals not only think differently about what’s possible, but what’s doable.

Having a strategy is important… a plan to implement and benefit will launch your ideas to a level of unprecedented success.

A successful integrated marketing campaign requires knowledge of the online market place and dedication to authentic identification and representation of your company and brand. I guide business leaders struggling to put new knowledge into action that has a positive impact on the bottom line.

Over the years I have worked with or shared the stage with people such as Tony Robbins, Zig Ziglar, T. Harv Eker, Chris Brogan, Brian Solis, Peter Shankman, Lewis Howes, Mari Smith, Mark Victor Hansen, Mark Joyner and many others.

If you feel like you don’t have enough time to incorporate these tools effectively to engage with new partners & customers, I can help you with strategic tools to create new media campaigns that will explode your business!
