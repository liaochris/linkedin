  • CitizenRequest.com iPhone App v1.0

    • December 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Carlos V. Roman, PWApn Founder

    App Search: Citizen Request or Citizen Tweet

    App Prerequisites
    - Active Twitter Account
    - iPhone App Installed
    - Internet Access
    - Photo of Request Type
    - Contact Details

    Description
    CitizenRequest.com is a mobile Tweet app developed by PublicWorksAgency.com encouraging residents to become active citizens in taking care of and improving their city by reporting non-emergency issues anytime and from anywhere. It’s Easy, Quick and Social.

    When submitting your Citizen Request, the information you provide will be initiate by using your active Twitter account. If you are not already logged on to Twitter, please make sure you are logged in so that your submission works seamlessly with the Citizen Request app. If you don't have a Twitter account, now would be the time to create one. http://www.twitter.com

    Step 1:
    Complete your mini-profile by clicking the ( i ) just to the left of the logo.

    Step 2:
    Be present at the location of the request type you are submitting and click “Pin” to pinpoint the location on the Google map.

    Step 3:
    Select the appropriate “Request Type” for your submission. (i.e. Pothole)

    Step 4:
    Click “Send” to initiate Add photo of the Request Type.

    Step 5:
    Attach photo from your library or using your camera, take a photo of the Request Type for submission.

    Step 6:
    A Tweet message box displays with the appropriate message to send to your Twitter account logging the Citizen Request. Note: please do not modify the message since the information will be used for reports. If modifying the Twitter message, please only modify only the #hash tagged request type that was selected.

    Step 7:
    Click send on the Twitter message box and your Citizen Request is posted to your Twitter account. You’re Done!
