Andy Caploe's Summary

I create and perform words, voices, music, and visuals for traditional and new media that define, enhance, and transform brands. I draw on more than two decades of creative problem solving experience as a producer, writer, performer, manager, and director, working in television, radio, advertising, online interactive, social networking, gaming, film, and theater. I bring creative and management expertise from across the full spectrum of audio and visual software with skills to inject the live human connection into digital media.

Specialties

I have led creative teams solving branding related issues for landmark properties including broadcast networks, consumer advertising, computer and console video games, online, and on stage.
