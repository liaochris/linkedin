Céline BRAYER's Summary

I am currently working as the Head of Content and Community management in TheBlogTV (web agency), and I have been working in the digital sector for 5 years.

I have acquired a strong background and knowledge in the web marketing and internet fields, especially in the social media environment. Being interested by these new ways of communication since 2008, I wrote a thesis on this subject for my master degree before joining FullSix Group, a digital marketing agency. Thanks to this professional experience, I have learned all the basics of internet project management.

In 2012, I continued my path in a different agency (TheBlogTV), specialized in social media. This experience has given me the opportunity to be specialized in social networks and community projects and to develop my skills in team management, sales, and client management. For more than 2 years now, I have been developing projects around social communities and collaborative platforms for clients.
