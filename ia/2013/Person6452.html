Andrea Montgomery's Summary

Published in The Independent, Thomas Cook 'Traveller' magazine, easyJet 'Travel' magazine and Wexas 'Traveller'. Editor of Tenerife Magazine, online travel and lifestyle magazine. Experienced and internationally published copywriter.

Specialties: Specialist travel writer for Tenerife and the Canary Islands. Hiking, activity holidays, food and drink.
Resorts, restaurants and hotel reviews with published copy on international hotel chain websites and in household name travel guides.
