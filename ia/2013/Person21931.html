Chandra Lynn's Summary

Chandra Lynn leads Glow Marketing LLC to provide services that support bands, brands and agencies. Core services include branding, creative development, and customer engagement...all aligned with a philosophy based in human needs psychology.

Authorized ApplePRO contractor for Apple, Inc.

Featured Clients:
Airshow Mastering, Apple Inc., Autodesk, Aspera, Avid/Digidesign, Tyra Banks/Bankable Enterprises, Irene Cara, Future US, GCPro/Guitar Center, Gobbler, producer Joe Solo, Line 6, LMG Inc., best-selling author Terry McMillan, New Bay Media, Roxio, Symbiotic Entertainment, and The Wine Group (including Cupcake Vineyards & Vodka, Concannon Vineyards & Irish Whiskey, Flipflop and Big House Wines).

Chandra is also the co-creator of Smoke & Mirrors, a television series in pre-production about a publicist who handles celebrity crisis management.

Lastly, Chandra is certified from the (Anthony) Robbins-Madanes Training (RMT) program studying coaching, strategic intervention, human needs psychology, and emotional and business mastery.

Specialties: Marketing Strategy & Planning, Branded Entertainment, Artist Relations, Online Marketing, Public Relations, Promotions, Event Planning, Design & Illustration, Web & Mobile Development, Social Media & Customer Engagement, Audio & Video Production
