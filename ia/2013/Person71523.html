Jennifer Waggoner's Summary

I am seeking new special short-term projects and remote long term clients.

I'm an experienced grassroots leader and consultant who provides a broad spectrum of services you need in order to grow. I put special emphasis on capacity building in infrastructure, finance, technology, and communications - all remotely! My practical +20 years of experience gives me the breadth and depth to help you find and transition to the resources best suited to your needs and means. Whether you are already are working remotely or you want to find out more about how to go virtual, I'm ready to help.

--SPECIALTIES--

Finance: helping you get the information you need in an easy, fast, reliable, cost effective way. Setup, clean up, merge, convert to all-online, or maintain your Quickbooks and documentation files.

Startups: new and growing ethical businesses and non-profits.

Technology: you know the technology is working when you don't notice it is there, and instead focus on what you're trying to do with it.

Infrastructure: how to make the most of what you have available, particularly for: generational change, volunteers, consensus building and community engagement.

Project management: a proven track record of inspiring leadership, creative flexibility and accountability.
