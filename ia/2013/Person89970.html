Malcolm 'Max' DeRungs' Summary

Information shapes us just as much as buildings and landscapes do, and we collectively thrive, or not, largely on the qualities these places possess. A successful enterprise information delivery system, then is like a livable city in the sense that it brings returns on business, mission and community investments. It does so through highly functional knowledge and collaboration spaces.

Highlights:

Statusbook - Currently aiming to free up 500K/yr in operations costs at Providence Medical Group by developing a quasi task management / business intelligence / user survey web product for tracking the status of service change roll-outs to 100+ clinics using a SharePoint 2010/ SQL Server 2008 system platform.

Knowledge Management System - Helped Providence Health Plan reverse a tendency for information chaos by developing a highly functional and efficiently structured SharePoint Intranet website for health plan content that led to measurable improvements in claim turn-around time, call metrics and positive customer experiences.

Intranet Platform - Played a driver role in building over 150 publishing sites and developing 50 web parts in a relatively short amount of time on a SharePoint Intranet for 17,000 people at Providence Health & Services that increased Intranet usage and collaboration across two platform upgrades.

Documents Central - Made 5000+ policy documents easier to find, version and archive at Providence Health & Services by developing a centralized SharePoint site collection with 70+ libraries, a consumer-devised taxonomy, customized search scopes, well-organized page layouts and user-friendly forms.

I am a husband to my wife Joanna, a father to my daughter Jenna, and a human of interest to my horses.

Specialties
