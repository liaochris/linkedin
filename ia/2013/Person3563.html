Al Emerick's Summary

Love what you do, do what you love and deliver with honesty and inspiration; this has been my guiding career principle. Born with a passion for leadership, entertainment, creativity, and collaboration, I have melded these disciplines into a fulfilling career which has to date, covered the full spectrum of advertising, marketing, entertainment, and broadcasting.

In short, I help companies and individuals communicate thier brand and tell thier story while painting pictures through written and spoken words across the bandwidth of communications mediums.

As a leader; I am a motivator who combines strategy, focus and outcomes with a keen ability to ignite energy among stakeholders and drive community through a unified and shared vision.

As a marketer; I draw from my artistic roots and meld the joy of creativity with the function and form of strategy built on realistic goals and sound metrics.

As an emcee, spokesman and presenter; I engage audiences with humor, honesty and passion which is strategically focused and delivers a positive, entertaining and memorable experience.
