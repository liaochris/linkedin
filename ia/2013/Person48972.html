Glenda Church Smith's Summary

For nearly 25 years, my track record as an expert executive recruiter and consultant, both in-house and as a retained search consultant, has been one of achieving results and a high level of performance for those I represent.

My passion and goals are the same: to better the lives of others through my work. Quite simply, I love people! I thrive on assisting clients by finding the best possible match for both the culture and personality of their organization as well as the job function they seek to fill and helping candidates improve their career and life by finding their dream job and location.

By studying the psychology and nuances of the recruitment process and negotiation as well as personality/behavioral assessments for over 20 years, I can help leaders at the C-level improve their bottom line, profitability, quality and competitive strength by securing the right individuals to fill key positions and leadership roles that are critical to the growth and success of their organization.

With the everchanging business climate and current economy, my mission is to create true recruitment ROI through my knowledge of hiring and the marketplace. Now, more than ever, the rare and exceptional talented individual matched to the right position, manager and culture is critical to any organization's competitive survival and success. Through my skills, experience, network, tools and alliances, my desire is to work with my client and candidate's highest interests in mind in order to ensure their security, success and competitive strength through the coming years.

Specialties

Recruiting physicians and hospital executives, assessing talent, consulting with clients on how to identify leaders and top performers, full life-cycle recruitment, recruitment strategy development, project management, sourcing, behavioral based interviewing, retention, contract/salary negotiation, sales, marketing.

KEYWORDS: pediatric recruiter, pediatric subspecialty recruiter, executive search consultant, children's hospital, physician recruitment, physician jobs.
