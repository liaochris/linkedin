Mary C. Soroka's Summary

As a Colorado-based freelance writer, I specialize in Christian & mental health content writing/marketing. I'm also a certified professional resume writer (CPRW).

I'm committed to providing quality copy that engages your readers, inspires your clients and enriches your professional life and business endeavors.

Whether you are selling a product, supplying a service or looking for a new job, finding the right words will motivate people to contact you. As a freelance writer, I can help you! Free up time, ease your mind and leave the writing details to me.
