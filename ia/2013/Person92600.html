Michael K. McCarty's Summary

I've got a few roles.

Analyst and Advisor - I work with institutional investors to determine their equity risk exposure and provide advise on how to diversify and/or hedge with volatility assets and derivatives.

I have several writing gigs - I contribute content to the CFE (CBOE Futures Exchange), Minyanville and RJO Futures on a regular basis, as well as other publications.

Author - I am also writing a book on the subject of volatility and volatility trading the working title is "Managing and Trading Volatility in a Risky Market" that will be published in January 2012.

I also speak on the topics of hedging, volatility and derivatives at investor conferences.

Pundit - I also appear or am quoted by the media frequently, on a variety of topics including the market, politics and recently life in Texas.
