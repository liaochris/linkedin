Fabio Brazil's Summary

http://fabiobrazil.4ormat.com

Does not believe in ecodesign / ergodesign / emodesign etcdesign or whatever new term may come to exist. Aesthetic, functional, emotional, moral and ethical, ecological principles, economic and ergonomic should not be mere prefixes - they are inherent characteristics of the design process.

Believes in the capability of any creative form on promoting engagement with the mind, body and behavior. We witness that happening everyday, from good musicians and other creators in art, cinema, literature, gastronomy, architecture... And that's what's interesting about these businesses.

Fabio Brazil's Experience

Brazilemurgel Jewelry

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Luxury Goods & Jewelry industry

2010 – Present (3 years) São Paulo Area, Brazil

Jewellery brand founded in 2010 by designers Fabio Brazil and Henrique Murgel.

Inspired by details that are found around us, details that are found in everyday objects, places, shapes from architecture and nature, textures, music and sounds... There is no limit for creativity, and we intend to keep it that way while bending and cutting metals, choosing and setting stones and pearls.

We employ traditional jewellery techniques and we keep a low scale production, which ensures a strict quality control of raw material and the refined look of the finished piece.
