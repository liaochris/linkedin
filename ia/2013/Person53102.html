Hilary Lee's Summary

Currently, I hold the position of Web Designer at a locally owned gourmet gifting company in Phoenix, Fairytale Brownies. August 2013 will be my 8th year with the company, and in that time, I've worn many hats.

In the Fall of 2010, I received my Masters Degree in Science and Technology Management from Arizona State University. My goal is to become an expert in website design and usability, as well as search engine marketing and organic search optimization. I have additional industry experience in strategic E-mail marketing, segmentation and deliverability, affiliate marketing, and seach engine marketing. I am proficient in HTML and CSS for web development and I am fluent in the Adobe Create Suite for design.

In addition to my digital specialties, I have extensive experience in print design, including designing catalogs and brochures for Arizona State University, Chompie's Restaurants and The Kim Komando Show. I also have experience with logo creation and branding for small businesses.
