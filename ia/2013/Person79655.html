Lee Anne White's Summary

Lee Anne White is an award-winning photographer, author and editor. She has served as a principal or major contributing photographer for nearly two dozen books and photographed more than 75 magazine features. Her photographs have been exhibited in both group and solo shows, and she has taught at the Maine Media Workshops. Lee Anne is the author of eight books and the editor of more than a dozen other titles—most of them about garden design. She previously served as editor of Fine Gardening magazine, which was named Best Gardening Magazine by the Garden Writers Association under her leadership.

In addition to pursuing her own creative projects and teaching workshops for visual artists, Lee Anne is fascinated by the creative process, talent development and the role of creativity in women's lives. She has a master's degree in creative studies and recently published Her Own Way: Creating an Authentic Life, which is a self-discovery guide for college that draws heavily upon the creative problem-solving process and includes many creative activities.
