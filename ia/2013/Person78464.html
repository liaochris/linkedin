Larry Maddock's Summary

Maddock Construction is a custom home building and remodeling firm specializing in the construction and renovation of unique custom residences. The firm performs work from the North Shore, to the Barrington area, to Southern Wisconsin, including Lake Geneva and Fontana.

Maddock Construction's mission is to work with only the finest crasftmen, artisians, and materials available to provide our clients the highest level of quality with integrity and an unwavering passion for the art of custom home building and remodeling.
