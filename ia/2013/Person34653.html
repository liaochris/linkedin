Anthony Toy's Summary

Anthony lives in San Francisco were he attended the Academy of Art University and gained a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Motion Pictures and Television in 2006. He currently works as freelance graphic designer and instructor. He also answers to "Tony", and has a passion for travel, gadgets, technology and motorcycles, and is always researching new techniques to streamline workflow for his clients.

Specialties: Software: Adobe Photoshop, Adobe InDesign, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe After Effects, and Adobe Dreamweaver, QuarkXPress, Final Cut Pro, Microsoft Word and Excel, advanced knowledge of the Macintosh operating system

Other: Photo correction and manipulation, digital video editing, motion graphics, digital printing and prepress, photography, and basic web design, html, css, and grep
