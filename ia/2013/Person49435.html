Gonzalo Parra-Pinto's Summary

I'm a creative Gaffer and Key Grip, Bilingual (Spanish -English) with excellent communications skills. I am a specialist in the production area with over 25 years as a Multimedia Producer and Marketing Image consultant. Since 2005 I have been concentrating my efforts in the Grip and Electric Department with an extensive knowledge of Light Design and Crew Management. I have been involved in several productions, from Indies to Hollywood Feature Films. My work history also includes Network Shows and over 100 Music Videos.
My goal in any production is to be your best resource with only one thing in mind: The development of an excellent product at the lowest possible cost.

My common comment when ask to do a job is "Tell me what your budget is, and I will make it work for you.
