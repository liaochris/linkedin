Christine Alt-Parry's Summary

I am a contract employee in the marketing, public relations, training and special events industries. I have created and implemented 1000's of advertising initiatives, trained over 100,000 people in various industries, and launched varying products in mostly technical industries, such as automotive, IT and photography. I also have sold products on television in the beauty, kitchen, cleaning, and electronics categories. I have worked on both sides of the camera, and I have written and illustrated three fictional books. Everything I do combines some sort of marketing and sales: I have worked as a corporate trainer for Procter and Gamble and as a marketing director for various companies. I have set up special events for several companies and to this day love a good party. In fact, I have sold wine and owned a company that hired people to do educational wine and spirit tastings throughout the Chicagoland area. I have worked as a free lance graphic designer and a makeup artist. I am self motivated, dynamic, and am a team player, but mostly, I am proactive and LOVE to work!

Specialties

Sales and marketing. Training everyone from consumers to television show hosts, creating brand awareness and identity, speech communications, video production, script writing, targeting demographics, on air sales and logistics. Use social media tools to delight and inspire.
