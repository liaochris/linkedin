Chris Fiori's Summary

The Law Office of Christophe Fiori, PLLC represents individuals in divorce or custody disputes, Bankruptcy proceedings, and Landlord & Tenant disputes. Mr. Fiori currently represents clients in Hillsborough, Pinellas, Pasco, Hernando, and Polk counties.

Helping other people overcome challenges in their lives has been a constant drive in Christophe Fiori’s life. Christophe’s unique perspective as an immigrant, small business owner, and attorney motivates him to focus on the many legal challenges that individuals face every day.

Specialties

I grew up in Florida, but was born in France, and speak French fluently.
I immigrated to the US as a teenager, after living for several years in France, and South Africa. Gaining legal residency in the US was a lifelong process that ultimately moved me to Houston to attend Law School.
Besides my interest in business and immigration, I have always been facinated by flying. Learning to fly and instructing has taught me how to manage a high stress situation and still focus on the task at hand.
