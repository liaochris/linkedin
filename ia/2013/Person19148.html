Byron Holley's Summary

Byron is a Managing Member of Legacy Point, a middle-market investment banking and advisory firm focused on providing financial engineering services. Through Alliances and Strategic Partners, Legacy Point utilizes Debt, Equity, Asset Management, Premium Finance and Structured Insurance Products.

Legacy Point structures alternative financing in the investing and lending markets. Legacy’s team of seasoned veterans and affinity relationships have completed over one billion dollars of financing and capital raises.
