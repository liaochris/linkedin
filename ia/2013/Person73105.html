Katie McCoach's Summary

I am a freelance editor working with authors on full-length manuscripts, query letters, proposals, and website content. Having an editor review your work before it goes out to agents for representation or is prepped for printing can mean the difference between a quick glance and not being able to tear your eyes away. I want to see you and your work succeed! Lets work together to create your best possible product for publication.
