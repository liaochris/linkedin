Mary Peterson's Experience

Owner

Teacher Treasures

August 2002 – Present (11 years 6 months) Greater Salt Lake City Area

Teacher Treasures, founded by Mary Peterson in 2002, publishes more than 50 resource books and learning station activities for elementary educators, including the wildly popular, Spectacular Books to Make and Use Across the Curriculum, Wonderful Writing Projects and Teach Comprehension Strategies with Picture Books.

Owner

Educational Consultant

October 2001 – Present (12 years 4 months)

As a much sought after educational consultant, Mary Peterson provides on-site trainings and workshops for elementary schools and districts throughout the United States and Canada. She also presents keynote addresses and sessions at education conferences across the country.

Mary's most most popular presentation topics are: Practical Strategies to Improve Students' Reading and Writing Skills, Differentiated Math Strategies for Today's Classrooms, Response to Intervention, English as a Second Language and Common Core State Standards.
