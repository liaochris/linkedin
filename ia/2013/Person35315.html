Diane Walter's Summary

HPA is, quite simply, the best in the industry when it comes to on-the-job training (OJT) and rapid procedure development. Our track record speaks for itself. We specialize in helping companies of all sizes and types develop and implement structured on-the-job training programs, training and certifying OJT trainers, and writing procedures for all processes and jobs. Since approximately 90% of employee skills and knowledge are learned on the job, why not do it right the first time - with HPA and TeamOJT?

Diane Walter's Experience

President

Human Performance Applications

September 1996 – Present (17 years 4 months) Seattle, Washington

Human Performance Applications, founded in 1996, is a Seattle-based consulting firm specializing in on-the-job training (OJT) and overall human/job performance issues. HPA's mission is to:

- help companies of all types and sizes develop and implement simple, easy-to-learn, quick-to-implement, flexible, and low-cost on-the-job training programs and procedures when and where necessary

- work to avoid unnecessary training when possible by writing rapid procedures that require no or very little upfront training

- improve all job processes, enhance job performance, cut costs, build high performance teams

- improve employee attitude and morale

- provide consulting services in training, writing procedures, and job performance issues to managers and employees individually or in small groups

"Building Encouragement in a Discouraged Workforce"
