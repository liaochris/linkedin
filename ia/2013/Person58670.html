Jaqui Lane's Summary

I have 25 years experience in corporate and business publishing worldwide with major companies, family businesses, governments and industry associations.

With Global Stories I provide a range of services and skills including:

Research and writing company histories, family histories and biographies;
Conceptualising and delivering targeted book and on-line projects;
Developing and writing content strategies and delivering content for websites/blogs;
Consulting in eBook and self publishing, delivering all the services and creating targeted marketing campaigns for eBooks and self published books;
Manuscript evaluation, assessment and editing.

I am also passionate about mentoring women in business and into business, developing links between the Middle East and the Western world and creating links for Arab and Western businesswomen to undertake business and support each other.

I actively seek to facilitate and pass on knowledge, from one generation to another, from one person to another, across boarders and boundaries, across generations and languages.

Specialties: Author, biographer, content creator, publishing consultant, custom book publisher, Arab-Western relations, business mentor, international business networker, Arab business specialist.
