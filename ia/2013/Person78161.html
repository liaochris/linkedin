Lance Haun's Summary

Editor and writer on all things HR, recruiting and talent management technology for more than six years. Started off on my own blog (then called anonymously YourHRGuy.com before eventually transitioning to LanceHaun.com).

I joined The Starr Conspiracy in 2013 to help in the firm's content development and thought leadership initiatives.

Previously, I was brought on at ERE Media in February 2010, first as community director, then helped launch our first HR publication TLNT.com. Two years later, I took over SourceCon.com as editor-in-chief and I program both SourceCon and the Recruiting Innovation Summit. My focus was on delivering pertinent news and insight to business professionals who value talent above all else. I was also a contract writer for career information portal Vault.com starting in 2007 and did that for two and a half years.

Before that, I was an HR manager, generalist, and recruiter for seven years. I hired, I fired, I did performance improvement plans, I counseled managers and employees and learned business the hard way through some really unique experiences.

I also launched (and sold) the first blog and social network for human resource professionals before most people in HR even knew what a social network was.

Specialties: Writing, editing, managing contributors, programming conferences, community management, human resources and recruiting, talent management
