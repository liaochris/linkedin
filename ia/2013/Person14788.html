Jerry Kurbatoff's Summary

EXECUTIVE SEARCH for the FOOD INDUSTRY with a special expertise in the MEAT AND POULTRY PROCESSING sector.

Since 1984, Black Leopard, Inc. has served its client companies providing a reliable single source for all staffing needs.

Black Leopard, Inc. provides recruitment services for corporate assignments, divisional offices and manufacturing complexes/plants. Within the Meat and Poultry Processing sector, our recruitment covers live operations and first, second and fully cooked/further processing operations.

Black Leopard, Inc. actively recruits for all professional job classifications within the food industry:

Operations – Production – Engineering – Maintenance – Environmental
Food Safety – Quality Assurance – R&D – Sanitation
Supply Chain – Sales & Marketing – Finance – Human Resources

Highlights for Jerry:

* Black Leopard’s Founder and Principal Recruiter with 30 successful years in executive search.

* Earlier in his career, working for major U.S. corporations in human resources management and talent acquisition roles provided critical insight into the requirements that a recruiting vendor must achieve to be a seamless extension of the company’s recruiting team and to exceed corporate objectives through introducing new human talent.

* Working for well-respected Fortune 500 companies provided a wealth of experience and understanding of the employment needs within large, labor intensive plant manufacturing environments that operate multiple shifts in union and non-union settings.

* Maintaining confidentiality has always been a top priority – whether client company information or candidate resume/bio.

* Possesses superior negotiating skills while never resorting to the “hard sell.”

* Strongly believes that one of the biggest reasons for Black Leopard’s success is by always maintaining integrity, and do what is right and fair for the long term.
