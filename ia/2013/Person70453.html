Managing Director

Greater Moncton Chorale

January 2011 – Present (2 years 10 months) Moncton

The Greater Moncton Chorale is an SATB community choir, now in its 24th season. We currently have about 70 members, ranging in age from 15 to 85! Some are experienced choristers and music readers; others are just starting out with singing. We perform two full concert programs every year, one at Christmas and one in the spring. We can also be heard singing at various community events throughout the year. The Chorale is furthermore a registered charity, making annual donations to local organizations in need of support.

What kind of music do we sing?
We sing a mixed bag of classical, jazz, folk, gospel, Broadway and pop music. There is something for every musical taste, in exciting, high-quality arrangements, with live instrumental accompaniment. Our director, Melody Dobson, has a master's degree in voice performance, as well as several decades of experience working with choirs and solo vocalists alike. She brings lots of energy and humour to every rehearsal.

3. When and where do we rehearse?
Our rehearsals are on Monday nights, from 7 - 9 p.m., at St. John's United Church in downtown Moncton (corner of Alma and Victoria Streets).

How do I register?
We give you a registration form to fill out at your first rehearsal. Our membership fee is $120 for the year (September - May/June). This includes music on loan. A Practice CD is also made available to every chorister for a nominal fee of $5.


MISSION
The Greater Moncton Chorale is proud to promote choir singing in the Greater Moncton area.
