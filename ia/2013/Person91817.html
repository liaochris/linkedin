Michiel van Dijk's Summary

Currently I am attending the UNIGIS Postgraduate Mastercourse and I am now working at a company that builds mobile applications, for which it uses geodata. By attending this mastercourse, I hope to combine my mostly ICT-related working experience to my original field of study, Human Geography. In the future, I would like to work in a GIS analyst or programming role in a company or government agency.

Specialties: great interest in social affairs and Geographic Information Systems, a clear and to-the-point writing style, sharp analytical skills, excellent Dutch and English language proficiency. Experience in developing software using HTML, CSS, jQuery, Javascript and PHP.
