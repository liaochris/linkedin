Jim Olson's Summary

My professional experience uniquely includes an equal blend of time working in a small, family-run business and working in a large, multi-national corporate environment.

Growing up in a small Midwest town I had the opportunity to work alongside my parents in helping them run Olson’s Jewelry, the family-owned retail store they started in the 1950’s. The next 23 years after college I had the privilege of working for a large, multi-billion dollar global communications firm in various executive sales positions creating innovative solutions to help other Fortune 500 companies solve the top communications-related challenges they were facing.

In both cases, whether it was working in our family’s small business or in a large corporation, the #1 challenge I observed organizations continue to face was “communications-related” (both internally to employees and externally to customers and prospects).

So, in 2003, I launched “Comuniquest”, a firm dedicated to providing “unique” “communications” solutions to help organizations in their “quest” to increase their sales, reduce costs and build their image.

What is unique about Comuniquest?

First, We have a guarantee: If we cannot help you make more money, save money or build your image the cost for all of the time we invest with you is free.

Second, our 5-step solutions process begins with a proprietary and proven strategic planning discovery tool we developed in the form of a card/deck called, “What Are You Dealing With? ® to Improve Your Communications”. The process is fun, engaging to your team and most importantly, it works. (Check it out at www.comuniquest.com/waydw).

Third, when you team up with Comuniquest you receive the concierge service you would expect from a small, high-end boutique firm along with the best-in-class processes, scope of services and professionalism you would expect from a large, corporate firm...and we have a blue chip list of clients we've worked with in the past to prove it.
