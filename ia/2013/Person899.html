Abigail Wurf, M.Ed, CLC's Summary

Abigail's work is to help individuals and groups work more productively, efficiently and in a learning environment to bring everyone's talent front and center.

Abigail Wurf has overcome many personal and professional roadblocks to get where she is today. She is known for her personal grit, stick-to-it-ness and passion for helping others overcome difficulties in both their professional and personal lives. Working with individuals, through workshops and speaking, Abigail, is a catalyst for change provided the individual or group is open and ready to work.

Her ideal clients are individuals who struggle with ADHD or executive functions and groups who would benefit from understanding the importance of learning about executive functions especially in regards to the people whom they manage.
