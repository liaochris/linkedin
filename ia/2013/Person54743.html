My most current production is with Ennis as there main provider of E-Commerce web solutions and back end support for design and development of there E-Commerce platforms. Since I have been with the company I have expanded my knowledge greatly in the area of jQuery, Javascript, C#, VB, and SQL. It has been such a delight to be working with such creative individuals.

Web design and development:
I have been working with several different programs related to web development. Flash(4 years), Flex(beginner) and LMS programs. Some of my projects from past companies has been with working with Intranet development. I have experience in ASP.net, PHP 4, AS2, AS3, and some Ajax. As well I have all the basic web development skills such as HTML/CSS.


E-Learning:
I took an internship at a International Company called FEI... My goal there was to build courses that would be used across the company with over 200 or so employees. Immersed into the E-Learning environment made me realize that courses are just a small part of the E-Learning experience.

It lead to the development of a learning environment...

Within a month we had a distribution environment and a catalog of important soft skills courses ready for employees to immerse themselves into.

Our first yearly review we found ourselves saving 150,000 to 200,000 dollars by providing internet based courses. Our review covered travel expenses/car rentals/ hotel expenses. With those amount of savings we were ready to create or purchase a LMS system to truly become a E-Learning program.
