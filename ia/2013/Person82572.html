Lorna Murphy's Summary

Over 20 year’s experience in the International events industry working across vast array of projects ranging from large scale consumer events, B2B exhibitions, private and corporate events, award ceremonies, community events, venue start-ups and research activities.

Excellent organisational skills to deliver full event planning & execution across a multitude of disciplines including planning & operations, tendering, budget control, health & safety/risk management, logistics, supplier negotiation, sponsorship, marketing, ticketing and client relationship management.

Strong ability to develop and deliver strategic solutions and to deliver projects on time and within budget.

Sectors covered include B2B, Trade Associations, Music & Entertainment, Leisure, Sport, Corporate, Government & Semi State, Private Enterprise and Community & Fundraising.
