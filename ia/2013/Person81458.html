Lisa Ciarnelli's Summary

Ciarnelli Web Designs (CWD) is an inclusive web development collective that offers responsive website design, website redesign, website maintenance, search engine optimization, WordPress (CMS), mobile sites and social media integration with a foundation in marketing and public relations. We provide charismatic and functional designs for small to medium size businesses in the Sacramento area at an affordable rate.

With a network of creatives and copy editors, we deliver agency quality websites, graphics and print design at an affordable rate. Plus, clients get the benefit of face-to-face services and open communication.

Doing the right thing, honoring client privileges and straightforward rate disclosure are some of the sound ethics with which we operate. CWD aims to perform with excellence and in doing so build strong relationships with our clients. Our process thrives by working with the client, not just for the client - we practice open and honest communication every step of the way.

Each distinctive website we create is optimized to increase your business's visibility in search engines – and Google, Yahoo & Bing will reward you.

We provide digital and print graphic designs that are sure to get your business noticed. All marketing and branding materials are designed to represent your business accurately while attracting the proper target market to help your business succeed. With a satisfaction guarantee, you can rest easy knowing that you will get the quality and service you want - no project is finished until you are completely satisfied.

We are licensed through the City of Sacramento (#1006215) and all work is performed under legal contract.

Lisa Ciarnelli's Experience

Website & Graphic Designer - Owner

Ciarnelli Web Designs

October 2009 – Present (3 years 11 months) Sacramento, California Area

Lisa is a fresh, independent designer known for her creativity and ability to surpass her client's expectations. She is an established graphic designer and has been developing clean, high-end websites since 2009. She is the lead designer and owner of Ciarnelli Web Designs.

Not merely a lone designer, she collaborates with other creatives, designers and copy writers and for brainstorming and review. With her knowledge of marketing and public relations as a strong foundation, she continues to develop impressive websites for a variety of different businesses in the Sacramento area and nationwide. Lisa works under a strong code of ethics while nurturing honest and healthy business relationships with each of her clients.

Background - She began working as an independent graphic designer in 2001 at California State University, Sacramento, for companies and non-profits seeking pro bono services. Through experience and education, she excelled in digital imaging and web authoring software from Adobe. During her graduate project, she studied website design in order to offer additional services to clients – she graduated cum laude in 2007.
