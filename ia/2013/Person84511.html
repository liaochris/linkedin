Katie Malone's Summary

As Digital Marketing Director at Financial Social Media, I design and implement all of our internal marketing and communications strategies. This means I spend my days designing promotional drip email campaigns, managing our social media presence, coordinating blog content and designing and updating our website. With my roots in graphic design, I take every opportunity available to spruce up the visual appeal of the Financial Social Media brand.

Prior to my role as Digital Marketing Director I served as Lead Graphic Designer, creating graphics, logos, marketing collateral and social media art both for our clients and for Financial Social Media. I also have provided my Graphic Design skills to a variety of companies like Sodexo, ReShred, Udi’s and Beer & Friendship.

After graduating from University of Denver with degrees in both Electronic Media Art Design and Marketing, I spent time working with Vail Corporation, combining my passion for graphic design with my passion for snowboarding. Although “ski-bum” wasn’t in my long term career plans, I still make it a habit to make it up to the mountains on my weekends to get in a few turns.
