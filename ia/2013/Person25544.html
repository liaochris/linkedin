Clayton Lee's Summary

Clayton has a solid history in technology administration and engineering of SMB and enterprise companies. His career blends a decade of improving business processes and practices through targeted technological improvements. He has provided support at the helpdesk level, field/remote service, administration, engineering and management. Clayton understands the growing needs of a business as well as the changing needs of staff.

His history includes founding and acting as a managing partner of CAL Technologies, a small business utilizing a decade of experience to provide inexpensive solutions to home and small business users. Clayton has been integral in multiple major market deployments and he is currently the IT Infrastructure Manager at the Corval Group responsible for the entire department.

Everyone who knows Clayton understands that technology isn't just a job it's his passion.
