Marcie Wolf's Summary

I own and operate two businesses: Wolf Byte Marketing (graphic design, logo design, branding, and marketing) and Wait.Sit.Stay (daytime dog walking service).

I was a partner with AlphaGeek Web Design for ten years and Web Consultant with Common Angle, Inc. (formerly AlphaGeek Web Design and Chesley Consulting) for one year.

My commitments to my community include:

· 2008 Ambassador of the Year, Petoskey Regional Chamber of Commerce

I've lived in Petoskey since April 2000 and the area has become my home.
