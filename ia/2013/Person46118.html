Fred Schlomka's Summary

For over twenty-five years Fred Schlomka has developed companies and non-profit organizations on both sides of the Atlantic including American Caribbean Builders in Florida and Mosaic Communities in Israel. In 2003 he was awarded a Social Entrepreneur Fellowship from the Echoing Green Foundation in New York.

Since 2007 Mr. Schlomka has developed Green Olive Tours as a social enterprise, and a division of the Green Olive Collective. He continues to consult with businesses and non-profit organizations (NGOs) in the Palestine/Israel arena and lectures on Palestine/Israel issues in Europe and the USA.

Specialties: business development & management, organizational development & management, event planning & management, political geography-Israel/Palestine, graphic design, database management, web site design and management, internet marketing, fundraising, writing, public relations, public speaking, audio and video production, journal publication, ListServ management, Photoshop, InDesign, Dreamweaver, HTML, Java

Fred Schlomka's Experience

CEO

Green Olive Tours

June 2007 – Present (6 years 7 months) Jerusalem, Israel

Green Olive Tours was created in 2007 as a social enterprise in Israel & Palestine, providing cultural and political tours throughout the country.

The alternative tours are informative and analytical, covering the culture, history, and political geography of Israel/Palestine/West Bank.

People to people contact are an important element in the tours. Guests have opportunities to interact with ordinary Palestinians and Israelis, and also officials from cultural and political organizations. Home-stays are an integral part of the multi-day tours.

The wide range of itineraries visit areas that embody the history and current status of the conflict between Israelis and Palestinians, and between Israel's Jewish and Arab citizens.

The tours are part of the emerging sustainable economy and benefit Palestinian organizations, villagers, and tour guides. The information and analysis provided on the tours are based on human rights considerations, the rights of self-determination, and the rights of all peoples to live in safety and political freedom.

Fred Schlomka's Projects

  • Green Olive Tours

    • June 2006 to Present
    Team Members: Fred Schlomka, noam yadin, Neal Gosman

    Green Olive Tours is a social enterprise tour agency providing tours that are informative and analytical, covering the history, culture, and political geography of Palestine (West Bank) and Israel. The tours provide benefit to the indigenous population through the hiring and training of tour guides, overnight stays with families & small guest houses, and encouraging visitors to purchase local crafts.

    The wide range of itineraries include interaction with Palestinians and Israelis, and visit areas that embody the history and current status of the conflict between Israelis and Palestinians in the West Bank and Jerusalem, and between Israel's Jewish, Palestinian-Arab, and Bedouin citizens. The complex mosaic of religions, nationalities, and political viewpoints in the Holy Land are explored in depth. Many of the tours visit sites of historical, cultural and religious interest, placing them within the context of the ongoing analysis provided by the guide.
