David Little's Summary

David Little serves as Keywest Technology’s director of marketing. With a background in emerging digital technologies, he worked for more than 10 years as an electronic field engineer with digital video equipment manufacturers before joining the newly formed Keywest Technology in 1999. Responsible for various activities including communications, inbound marketing and product development, Little believes his most important time is spent with customers learning the ins and outs of their needs and how digital technology can best serve them. When it comes to professional passions, he loves sharing his knowledge in a dynamic, growing industry called digital signage. According to Little, the digital signage industry is changing the communication paradigm of marketers, advertisers, public relations officers and other communicators by giving them the tools to manage dynamic messages that are database driven and scheduled over a local or cloud network.
