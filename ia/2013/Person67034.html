PHOTOGRAPHER/OWNER

SENIOR COLORIST/TECHNICAL DIRECTOR OF ENGINEERING

BROADCAST VIDEO INC; MIAMI, FLORIDA

1990 – 2002 (12 years)

I have been color correcting for more than 25 years working mostly in the commercial world, as well as doing features, TV shows, music videos, politicals, industrials. Responsible for the expansion of a one room high definition color correction TV commercial operation to the present day of four. Excellent international client relations (advertising agencies, art directors, directors and producers) was the result of the increased volume. Clients were Revlon, American Airlines, AT&T, McDonalds, Toyota, Pizza Hut, Coca Cola, Budweiser, Colgate, Florida Orange Juice and Lottery. Citrix VPN network was used to operate between 3 facilities running scheduling and finance programs in south Florida

SENIOR COLORIST/ENGINEER

GROUPE ANDRE PERRY; WASHINGTON DC

January 1987 – January 1990 (3 years 1 month)

Installed, operated and maintained a color correction suite including a newly designed pre digiscan da Vinci which developed into tape to tape transfers which created a brand new market place for color correction in the TV commercial world. Each room was an integral part of the whole network that everyone shared in real time. Fully automated routing switchers and control panels were incorporated along side to control all hardware de- vices along side with the data line.

Customer service support engineer and senior field engineer for broadcast video equipment including high end studio and field cameras and switchers traveling around the country. Would also teach classes on the mainte- nance of the equipment locally and abroad.
