Susan Cady's Summary

From the minute you hear your pet's diagnosis until they taste their first treat, we are there to help you every step of the way. We strive to make dog treats that are bold, fresh and innovative. Bone Yard Bakery

January 2012 – Present (1 year 8 months)

Pet owners across the country will be thrilled to learn about Bone Yard Bakery - a new resource which delivers Organic, Gluten-free and dairy free dog treats. The brain child of dog nutrition expert and entrepreneur Susan Cady delivers treats and power bars for those with food intolerances and sensitivities such as gluten and dairy allergies. "After working with numerous clients with specific dietary needs throughout the years, I realized the increased need to identify more choices for those pets with food allergies to enhance their diets," said Susan Cady." Bone Yard Bakery founder and CEO. "As food allergies are constantly on the rise, it's amazing how few lip smacking allergy-free products exists and how many more can be created for this growing market.". Cady and the Bone Yard Bakery team of nutrition experts develop tasty nutrient-dense allergy-free treats. Bone Yard Bakery manufacturers amazing treats that are organic, gluten and dairy free that taste fantastic. The Bone Yard Bakery on line subscriber-based platform allows Cady and her team to help larger number of clients efficiently and effectively "as someone who is passionate about making nutritious products for my clients and my own pets. I am thrilled that Bone Yard Bakery can be an advocate and resource for healthier and more creative treat choices."

CEO & Founder

Doggie H20 Natural Rain Water

January 2012 – Present (1 year 8 months) Dallas, OR

Doggie H2O Natural Rain Water provides a neat and convenient water supply for your pet when you're on the go. No muss, no fuss and no hassle. The bottle design is convenient for play dates,pet walks, camping, sporting activities, or on trips. Doggie H2O Natural Rain Water is harvested on a rain farm in the coastal range of Oregon. Since the rain never touches the earth it is naturally mineral free, uncontaminated, has no chlorine or additives giving it a superior quality and is Kosher Certified. Water is essential to a dog's life and Doggie H2O Natural Rain Water is as natural as it gets.
