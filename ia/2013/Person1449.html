Adam Blahnik's Summary

I am an attorney and owner of Blahnik Law Office, PLLC located in downtown Prior Lake in Minnesota. I have worked in the legal profession since July 1999, initially as a Paralegal and Law Clerk (from July 1999 through October 2005) and as a practicing attorney since 2005. I practice in several areas of law, but have an emphasis in:

* Divorce (Dissolution of Marriage)
* Child Custody
* Business Start-Ups
* Preparation of Wills and Trusts

As a Minnesota divorce attorney, I "wear many different hats" depending on the circumstances. If there is hope that the Minnesota divorce proceedings will get resolved through mediation or other alternative dispute resolution methods, I wear the hat of an amicable attorney in the hopes that the spouses can resolve their differences without it costing them an "arm and a leg." However, once it becomes apparent that there will be no stipulation or agreement, I will then vigorously advocate the rights of my clients in Court. I am an experienced Minnesota divorce lawyer and litigator and will fight for the rights of my clients until results are attained.

Our law firm offers a free initial consultation either over the telephone or at our office in Prior Lake. Blahnik Law Office utilizes current technologies that enables a client to "log in" to his or her account and view that client's entire file, including all letters, Court documents and billing invoices. The law firm accepts all major credit cards. We provide very competitive billing rates, retainers and offer "flat fees" for various legal matters.

Our law firm understands that many of the legal matters that our clients are dealing with are stress induced and can be overwhelming. Because of this, our firm makes itself available by telephone or by email potentially 24 hours a day and seven days a week.
