John F. Buckert's Summary

I am a patent attorney with substantial experience working with entrepreneurs, start-up companies, and large corporations.

After entering a market with a new valuable product or service and no patent protection, competitors will most likely copy your product or service and take at least a portion of your market share.

Obtaining patent protection is one of the most effective techniques for preventing duplication of your product or service by your competitors. Further, you want the broadest patent protection possible.

There are two primary factors that distinguish a skilled patent attorney from others. First, the patent attorney must be an expert at claiming the invention. Claims define the scope of patent protection. Second, the patent attorney must be an expert at describing how to make and use the invention, and be extremely knowledgeable in the technology being described.

I have obtained hundreds of patents for companies and entrepreneurs and have consistently obtained extremely broad patent protection that has created substantial barriers to their competitors trying to duplicate their products or services. Also, I have successfully appealled dozens of patent applications before the Board of Patent Appeals and Interferences to obtain the broadest and best patent protection possible. My office is located in Rochester Hills, MI near Troy and Auburn Hills.

Specialties: Patent application drafting, patent application prosecution, patent licensing agreements, patent infringement and non-infringement analysis, patent application appeals, client counseling regarding patents, patent prior art searches, patent clearance searches, software patent attorney, Michigan patent attorney, registered united states patent attorney, electronics patent attorney, medical device patent attorney, battery system patent attorney
