Lynn Walsh's Summary

Working in the fast paced world of the news industry I use public information, relationship building, research and a passion for transparency and accountability to develop and produce investigative news stories. I love being able to provide information for people and communities and produce stories that matter.

Specialties

I am efficient in editing, writing, shooting, reporting, anchoring and producing television, online and radio news. I have extensive experience in freedom of information laws and requests, CAR, LIVE and mobile reporting. I am able to create multimedia components for stories including interactive graphics, tables, maps and more. I have extensive social media skills ranging from developing stories, reporting stories and sharing information.
