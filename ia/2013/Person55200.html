Mike Bruckman's Summary

I am always seeking to create fun and engaging environments for myself and others to share their passionate skills. Whether curating a country club or hotel with original art, hosting a musical showcase at any number of places, or organizing a party/event just for fun, I strive to bring people together to share in life's beautiful reality.

When I'm let to my own desires, I find myself honing in or any variety of unique 'projects', including songwriting, photography, video production, and invention...or concept design as it would be more accurately described. My limited manufacturing and production capabilities require that I build networks of people who can help me realize these visions and turn them into concrete realities. thus, I've been able to crate such products like Glokeys, GloFrames, GloQubes, GloDrums, Etc...

I am a believer in the value of online domains and carry a portfolio of anywhere from 50-100 at any given time over the course of the last 5+years. I have designed and maintain several domains, mostly based around the concept of sharing work and attracting interested parties in projects and what I do. Many people have told me that the intrinsic value of my undeveloped online concepts is potentially very lucrative, and I agree.

In the fall, I operate a business I co-founded where I give 40+ person groups tours of rather shady places like cemeteries, abandoned castles, and funeral homes. Last year, over 1,000 people enjoyed these experiences.

From around 2010 to 2012, I was the primary organizer, instructor, and co-founder for a variety of DIY art and wine drinking companies. Now, I prefer to attend :)

There is no end in sight as to what I will achieve and who will be involved. I have mentored over a dozen full/part time interns in the past 3 years.
