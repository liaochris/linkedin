Josh Villbrandt's Summary

Josh Villbrandt works as an Avionics System Engineer at Space Exploration Technologies. Some of his first tasks at SpaceX included designing the avionics for a propulsion system test as well as a suite of portable electronics to support Dragon vehicle-in-the-loop testing. Josh is now the lead avionics engineer for the crew Dragon abort test vehicles - a series of test vehicles to prove Dragon's launch abort capability under NASA's CCiCap contract.

While at USC, Josh was involved was the Performance Lead for the AeroDesign Team and the Software Team Lead for the Aerial Robotics Team. He was also the web developer at Illumin. Josh received his BS in Aerospace Engineering with a minor in Computer Science in the Spring of 2011 and his MS in Computer Science (Intelligent Robotics) in the Fall of 2012.
