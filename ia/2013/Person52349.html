Joan Hebert, MBA, MS's Summary

Joan Hebert, MBA, MS, principal of Hebert Performance Training,” specializes in customer service, leadership, presentation/training skills, team building, customer-focused sales, and workplace communication skills.

HPT is celebrating 15 years serving nationwide clients such as Time Warner Cable, Bayer HealthCare Pharmaceuticals, AngioDynamics, Oneida Indian Nation, U.S. Department of Defense, Citizens Savings Bank, ITTGoulds Pumps, and National Grid that describe Hebert as "energetic, humorous, captivating, down-to-earth, and able to gain the interest of trainees!"

Hebert is a certified Achieve Global and AMA instructor, and Syracuse University Adjunct Professor. Prior to HPT, Hebert spent 15 years with AT&T as a #1 nationally ranked master trainer. HPT is a NYS “Woman-Owned Business Enterprise”, member of ASTD, SHRM, NYS Eligible Training Provider List, and the federal government CCR.

Hebert is a frequent SHRM presenter, including the '08, '09 and '11 NYS SHRM Annual Conference, and '09 Vermont Annual HR Conference. Joan has presented numerous times for ASTD chapters, including 7 times for the Hudson-Mohawk ASTD Conference. Hebert’s articles on leadership, training, and customer service are published both in the US and internationally, and accessible at www.heberttraining.com. Info. jhebert3@twcny.rr.com, (315) 303-4750 or (888) 282-7291.

Specialties: Customer service, leadership, supervisory skills, presentation & training skills, team building, customer-focused sales, workplace communication, group facilitation, focus groups
