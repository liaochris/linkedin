Ellaine David's Summary

Ellaine is a Performance Consultant with a successful track record in developing and executing organizational effectiveness strategies for Fortune 500 and small businesses that spans more than 15 years. She applies a multidisciplinary approach in providing strategy, change, and performance improvement solutions to help organizations reach their business objectives and competitive advantage.

As the owner of MixEllaineNews Consulting, she uses her expertise in human performance technology to design enterprise-wide change management and organizational development programs for her clients. She is a member of ASP, ISPI, and ASTD, and currently serves the board of directors for the Los Angeles Chapter of ISPI. Her “long and winding road” included stints as an account manager, college instructor, and counselor to companies such as Leo Burnett, Intel, ITT Technical Institute, WellPoint, and Toyota Motor Sales.
