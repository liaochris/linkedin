Earl Hall's Summary

#TheEarlHallShow shares with its audience the core principles of the philosophy #ItsYourResponsibility. We currently reaches over 30K unique listeners every month, online through BlogTalkRadio, with a special brand of self reliance and ownership of the outcome of your own life.

Changing the direction of your business, relationships, and your life are core issues we deal with. No matter if you are a CEO, an employee, or simply a person looking to change for the better; we believe that the power for you to make a difference lies within you.

We are bombarded with messages that tell us that all that is wrong with our lives, "IS NOT YOUR FAULT". Well believing that premise takes away your power and your right to alter and direct the course of your life.

The truth is that you have more power than many people want you to believe. You also hold the keys within to create and develop a life of abundance and joy. Isnt this what we all want?

::::::

Many people want to classify this show as conservative. Well even though we do have many "conservative" views we are far from a Republican or a Democrat.

Our views are simply based in what is ethical and moral. Neither Rep or Dems have a monopoly on that. In fact both have their own issues with wrong ethics and morals.

This talk show simply deals with what is the right thing to do and what is the best way to think. Sure, what grounds me is my faith but trust me I'm far from churchy.

We tackle issues that affect the deepest parts of us. Life's big questions, our society, our cultures, politics, and more. All from a unique, honest, and real perspective.

::::::

The most fun I have besides being with my family is broadcasting live. Being a talk show host and connecting with people, I honestly feel is my calling. Its my passion and my destiny.


Tune in. You'll see what I mean :-)

Earl Hall's Experience

Talk Show Host

The Earl Hall Show

April 2002 – Present (11 years 5 months)

So much to talk about and I really have a lot to say. I live in the city of Milwaukee, WI and there is so much happening here and everywhere. Life challenges us everyday. Our morals, integrity, and beliefs are constantly tried and tested.

No matter what your race or culture, until you accept responsibility for your circumstances nothing will change. The culture of blame, excuses, and grievances are holding back the poor and the people living below their means. This show accepts no excuses and wants everyone to realize that #ItsYourResponsibility.

On The Earl Hall Show everything you say you believe about yourself and the world will be tested.

Owner

Studio 3 Productions, LLC

January 1999 – Present (14 years 8 months)

Studio 3 Productions, LLC is a internet radio broadcasting company that specializes in voiceover and broadcasting.

S3P spent years developing and coaching many internet radio stations. From software, hardware, marketing strategies, and hardware utilization.

There is an obvious issue in radio broadcasting as to its success and future. Because of the creation of online platforms like iHeartRadio and other broadcasting apps, many stations are a loss for what to do and how to compete.

The simplest way I have found to operate and to grow is to meet your audience where they are. No amount of marketing can compare to an honest development of useful content that your audience just cant say no to.
