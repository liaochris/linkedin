Joel Clark's Summary

Working Capital for Businesses and Giving Back to Communities

Provides better cash flow and working capital credit lines to businesses nation wide and give a portion of all transactions back to your community.

I've worked with accounts receivables finance and factoring for fourteen years. My goal is to help strengthen small business and help them grow.

I believe our freedom is tied very closely to the health of the small business sector.

Through Factoring we are able to help Healthcare facilities, Nursing, Medical, Staffing, Construction, Manufacturing and many other businesses get paid faster. (in most cases 24 hours)

It's a very simple process. Similar to a credit card transaction where for a small fee, you’re paid immediately for the product or service you’ve provided.



As a service we also offer low cost credit card processing for small businesses and charities.
***U.S. ONLY***

A portion of every transaction is given to the charity or cause you care most about. It's your choice!
