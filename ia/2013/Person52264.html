Heather Dawe's Summary

For buyers, I work tirelessly to help them make the best home choice by gathering all pertinent information and organizing it to ensure all available options are understood. Assistance is provided through the entire process so the home buyers’ decision to purchase meets or, preferably, exceeds their expectations.
For sellers, I use aggressive marketing, the latest technology, and creative techniques to deliver results quickly. I evaluate the property with clients and assist them in pricing, positioning, and presenting their property to reach qualified buyers. This is done based on experience, knowledge, and ability to listen carefully to the client and then translate their desires into reality.
I am a full-time professional agent, who believes in continuing education, using cutting edge technology, and serving as a resource for clients before, during, and after a satisfactory completion of the transaction. I have more than 10 years of successful experience and hold industry certifications with ABR, CRS, E-Pro, and GRI.
Working with me means that, as a client, you are the priority. Whether you are a seasoned seller and buyer or this is your first entry into home ownership, I will make the process as satisfying and easy as possible.
