Mindaugas Bujanauskas' Summary

My main area of interest is building big, scalable web applications. I enjoy working in Unix environment when time comes to deploy, love thoroughly testing my applications and building apps with future-proofing in mind.

I am a quick learner and solver - just throw tasks and problems at me and I will come up with solutions in a jiffy. Also, I am focused, responsible and organized. When working with web applications, I try to come up with quality solutions, if time allows.

I've got a knack for usability, good design and finding the best possible solution. I read a lot about web development and am a fan of 37signals, Fog Creek, Podio, Issuu and DocumentCloud. People like Jeremy Ashkenas and David Heinemeier Hansson are a great inspiration to me.

Lately I've been experimenting with event driven object oriented JavaScript. A big inspiration for this were articles and books by Addy Osmani about JavaScript patterns.

Currently I am working on Woodling. It's a an easy to use tool that provides you with fixtures for your models when testing. You can use it with any framework or vanilla PHP and there is no configuration required. It uses your code to do all the work. Check it out in the link below.

If you feel like you want to chat or contact me about more serious matters, you can catch me on Twitter (@rkrv), Freenode IRC (rkrv) or here, on LinkedIn. You might also like to see what I've got experience with - it's in my "Skills & Expertise" area below.
