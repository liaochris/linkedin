Anne West's Summary

North Shore Chicago Realtor working with home buyers and sellers on the North Shore of Chicago, including Wilmette, Winnetka, Glencoe, Kenilworth, Evanston, Glenview, Northfield and Highland Park.

I apply the skills and expertise gained from twenty-plus years in business and marketing on behalf of my clients each and every day. My experience in consumer marketing and advertising is a competitive advantage for my clients because I know exactly how to package and promote homes for sale to attract the right buyers.

Specialties: Residential real estate sales, first time home buyers, relocation buyers, real estate marketing, negotiating, internet marketing, short sales, home staging, consumer marketing and branding
