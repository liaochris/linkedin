Danielle Shaw's Summary

Social Media Marketing and Interaction is extremely important to businesses today and I understand the need to have a clear, concise vision for your brand across all platforms and networks, including Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Pinterest, Vine, Instagram and many more. For the past 18 years, it has been my mission to deliver top quality, dynamic websites to small businesses and start-ups at affordable prices with a personal-yet-professional experience.

The marriage of Tech Editing and my work at Cengage Learning with my freelance Web Development directly keeps me on the forefront of the IT industry, especially with applications and software. I have the fortunate opportunity to work on beta software, learning upcoming and current applications before they are released to the public. I am constantly learning, playing dual roles of Student/Professor during my Tech Edits, and am thankful for every opportunity I get.

Danielle Shaw's Experience

Senior Technical Editor

Cengage Learning

Privately Held; 5001-10,000 employees; E-Learning industry

April 2001 – Present (12 years 9 months)

I have been contracted by Course Technology, a division of Cengage Learning since 2001 as a Senior Technical Editor. I have worked on an umbrella of titles through the years including Windows 2000-8.1, Mac, and Linux operating systems, Microsoft Office, Adobe Creative Suite, web design, networking, and programming textbooks. As a Technical Editor, I don't only check for inconsistencies and factual errors in the technical content of a book, I also provide insight and help the authors tweak their lessons and text. I have a unique position of having worked with many different authors through the years and can quickly identify their style and help with any queries. Sometimes, it's as simple as confirming keyboard shortcuts for obscure Photoshop tools and other times a lesson may be riddled with errors and in need of an "eagle eye" to pinpoint where it went wrong. Authors have all different kinds of backgrounds, some are professors while others are professionals in the industry. It's my job to analyze the veracity of their text and ensure the highest quality possible for the publisher.

A sampling of titles I've worked on: Android Boot Camp for Developers Using Java, Microsoft Office 2013 Comprehensive, Windows 8 Fundamentals, Succeeding in Business Using Excel 2013, Adobe Photoshop, Flash, and Dreamweaver CS6 Comprehensive, Web 2.0: Concepts and Applications, XML, DHTML, and HTML Comprehensive. I work very closely with Authors, Development Editors, and Product Managers in helping maintain a very high standard of quality in their books, typically performing multiple passes on a book to ensure a textbook is error-free and on point.
