HELEN DODSON's Summary

With over 30 years experience in real estate sales, Helen gets wonderful results for home sellers and buyers. Her marketing savvy and negotiating expertise have helped many who now contribute to her strong referral business. A native of Washington, DC, she is licensed in the District of Columbia and the state of Maryland and knows the many city and suburban neighborhoods area residents love to call home. From starter homes in moderately price neighborhoods, to million-dollar-plus homes in upscale neighborhoods throughout the city and Maryland suburbs, Helen is active in serving clients in many different communities.

Helen served on the DC Real Estate Commission for ten years and culminated her service as Chair of the Commission. Studies she completed through the National Association of Realtors have earned her the professional designations of Certified Residential Specialist (CRS), Graduate of the Realtor Institute (GRI) and Seniors Residential Specialist (SRES). She spent ten years as co-owner and associate broker of Dodson Realty Inc., working in sales, training and recruiting to help lead and grow the brokerage into a successful Century 21 franchised office. Helen also successfully helped many buyers and sellers during her ten years in residential sales at Long and Foster Realtors.

In 2011 Helen joined the Bethesda/Chevy Chase market center of Keller Williams Capital Properties. She is excited to be working in the fastest growing residential real estate firm in the Washington area, supported by Keller Williams Realty International, now the largest real estate company in North America. She serves on the Associate Leadership Council at her office and enjoys using her experience, expertise and the Keller Williams state of the art technology to help people sell and buy homes.

Helen is married to real estate broker Norris A. Dodson, III and mother of three adult children. She is a graduate of Tufts University (BA) and American University (MS).
