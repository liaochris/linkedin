Joseph Joseph's Experience

Vice President / Managing Director, BIM & CAD Technologies

SAIC

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; SAI; Information Technology and Services industry

January 2010 – Present (3 years 7 months) Oklahoma City, Oklahoma Area

Joseph serves as the Managing Director of BIM/CAD Technologies for SAIC/Benham Architectural and Engineering Business Sector and works in the Oklahoma City office. He is responsible for all aspects of BIM/CAD strategies, implementations and operations of such processes and technologies as well as the growth in the BIM arena for multiple specialized products to include leaders such as Autodesk and Bentley. He is currently a member of the sector’s managing directors’ group and works very closely with all disciplines insuring high quality BIM delivery for projects. In addition, Joseph is actively leading the charge of educating the companies’ clients on the BIM evolutions and its value during and beyond design / construction phases.

Director, Design Technology Services

NELSON

Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Architecture & Planning industry

February 2007 – January 2010 (3 years)

As head of Design Technology Services, Mr. Joseph Joseph oversees all BIM and CAD related aspects of the firm globally in addition to client specific emphases on standards, collaboration and processes. With more than 15 years of professional experience as one of the Autodesk industry pioneers his qualifications comprise of roles that span through a wide range of responsibilities including strategic leadership, technology implementation, driving innovative solutions within organization, project architect and production management.

Since joining NELSON in March of 2007, Mr. Joseph has been successful in building his department from the ground up with a mix of talented Autodesk industry leaders. He demonstrated ability into taking full ownership and dedication to quality of deliverables through revamping the organization’s CAD/Drawing Standards and processes for all Lines of Business implementing a system that works for all disciplines leading to consistency, efficiency and quality products.

One of Mr. Joseph’s significant achievements is continuing to lead the charge of implementing Building Information Modeling / REVIT as a process and technology throughout NELSON. He has established strategically robust implementation modules driving this process change into the organization while being fully engaged in the project management cycle.

In addition to Mr. Joseph’s role in supporting corporate operations is being a subject matter expert to several key clients such as Bank of America and Aetna in establishing CAD/Drawing procedural standards as well as coordinating the implementation and adherence of client standards within NELSON. Mr. Joseph is highly engaged on an industry level as a key-speaker in Autodesk events and serves as an advisory board member to several colleges and universities.
