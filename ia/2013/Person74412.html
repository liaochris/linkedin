Ken Norbury's Summary

Ken has extensive experience in the technology business leading product developments in the industry for over 15 years. He has successfully introduced innovative technologies to market-leaders in several market sectors including Mobile, Automotive and TV/STB software.
As Vice President of a Silicon Valley software business with products in Mobile/Embedded Systems and Mobile Data, he managed Product Development, Professional Services and Mobile Services businesses and led customer project partnerships with major customers like Mitsubishi, Nokia, Toshiba, Seiko Epson and other leading players in the Mobile and Handheld Consumer Devices industry.

He has long experience working with and building business and partnerships in Scandinavia, Japan, US, South Korea and Europe. He has received “President’s Awards” at both Sony Ericsson and Geoworks for outstanding achievements and an "Inventor Award" for a new feature submission patented by Sony Ericsson in 2010.
