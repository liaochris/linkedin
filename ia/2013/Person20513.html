Carolyn De Gois' Summary

Carolyn is an Executive Manager with extensive expertise in the development and delivery of IT strategies aligned to business outcomes within large and complex organisations. She has over 25 years of experience consulting, influencing and working with executives in complex and politically challenging situations, while working for a variety of companies both in Australia and Internationally. She is known for her strong ability to quickly identify problem areas, strategically plan to address these problems, influence change where required and successfully deploy programs of activities to achieve the desired outcomes.

Currently, Carolyn heads up the Victorian IT Strategy and Performance practice within KPMG. She has practical experience in the development, leadership and delivery of strategic transformation programs across a range of diverse organisations. In this capacity, she has strategically focused the team to provide CIO Advisory skills within a market that is seeking insights into the practical delivery of simplified yet innovative technology platform solutions.

She has performed a number of CIO roles over the past 10 years across the mining, manufacturing, government and FMCG markets. Throughout, she has been instrumental in transforming organisations, achieving multi-million dollar cost savings, improving supply chain procedures, while managing and leading work forces in excess of 200 people with annual budgets in excess of $100M.

She has extensive experience working with C-Level Executives throughout major programs of business change, and understands the need for ongoing support and effective stakeholder management to keep large programs on track.

During her career, she has led and delivered a number of transformational programs that include a global sourcing strategy for a large multinational organisation across a base of 11 geographically disparate Australian locations and 6,000 employees.
