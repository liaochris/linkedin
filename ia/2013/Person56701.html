Jacob Hoaglund's Summary

Jacobs’s operational energy is the driving force behind AEAB. Through personable communication and a relentless focus on excellence, his dedication to client satisfaction is apparent. Helping clients understand the process of valuation and delivering accurate and professional products is his priority. He sets high standards for himself and strives to go that extra mile for every client.

Jacob credits the Marine Corp for shaping him into the man he is today. Enlisting in 1996 and serving in communications, he learned the value of teamwork and diligence to work hard until a job is completed with excellence. Jacob is driven to be a man of his word at all times.

Jacob began in the valuation industry in 2002. He enjoyed the challenge in putting a value on real estate. With a keen eye for the big picture, he explored other avenues to apply valuation techniques. Over the past decade, Jacob has continually sought out opportunities to learn more applications of valuation and knows the importance of staying current on industry standards.

In addition founding, learning, leading and shaping the future of AEAB, Jacob is a devoted family man who enjoys weightlifting and i s passionate about every facet of aviation.
