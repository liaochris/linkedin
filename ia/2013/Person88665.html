Matt Earp's Summary

I am an enthusiastic organizer with a wide range of experiences in digital / web technology, music, film, and writing. As a DJ, event promoter and music journalist I have become a respected voice in current electronic music; now I want to help develop the technologies I use and that are changing how people create, consume, and socially interact with digital media as individuals and as groups.

I have a talent for synthesizing disparate experiences into fresh perspectives and approaches. My value is in my breadth of experience, my organizational and communication skills, and my ability to connect with and understand the needs and goals of all parties working on a project, whether it is website, app, software, event, or promotional campaign. I help turn the projects I work on into successes.

I seek contract projects or full-time positions that allow me to contribute my skills to the "softer" side of tech companies, including community management, project management, content acquisition, relationship/partnership management, and PR/Marketing. I have a strong understanding of the tools developers use, and have skills in data and document management, IT, QA, and coding that supports development work.
