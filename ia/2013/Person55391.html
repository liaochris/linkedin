Ingrid Weel's Summary

Associate of the Royal Photographic Society, I am experienced in photographing people in any Situation.

I offer professional corporate photography for all types of businesses ranging from head shots, creative business portraits of owners and director of companies, images for web sites,brochures and business cards and case studies to highlight the specialities unique to a business.

Within corporate photography I work closely with graphic designers, marketing experts and public relations agencies. I take the brief and interpret it creatively for maximum impact in the press, on the internet and on printed materials such as brochure, flyers and business cards.

On the social side I photograph weddings, family portraits, boudoir, babies, engagement portraits and baby bumps.

I am versatile, mobile, energetic and efficient in my work - and hopefully entertaining to my subjects!

Specialties

My background is in film and television production where I still work as a focus puller/1st A/C on dramas such as Casualty, Holby City, The Royal Bodyguard with Sir David Jason and feature films such as Centurion.
