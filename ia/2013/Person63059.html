Jessica Severson's Summary

Lapsed lawyer turned content creator. I've traded the chaos of the courtroom for the Wild West of the internet.

I'm working with an exciting new startup, Flatev, that is bringing an amazing tortilla-making machine to kitchens everywhere soon. Our team is building connections via print and web media, online influencers and big food brands. We work hard, we have fun, and we really love tacos.

In the blogging world I've kept my own personal site for several years. Using my experience I now help encourage other bloggers, writers and businesses to build their websites effectively as one of the Lawyer Chicks. We broadcast our message on our site and through social media. We present and simplify legal concepts and business practices at conferences.

My writing has been featured at Parents Magazine, About.com, save.com, Isis Parenting and more.

One of my current projects is helping to bridge the gap between content bloggers and affiliate marketing. I've done several webinars and will be speaking at Affiliate Summit West 2014.
