Trish Parker's Summary

I have over fifteen years of professional experience as a church musician and educator, and I have directed volunteer and school choral groups of almost all ages. My teaching interests include Gregorian chant, choral conducting, music history, theory, and appreciation, and private instruction in organ and piano.

I have performed organ recitals in Charlotte, NC, Charleston, SC, Rochester, NY, and Lynchburg, VA.

My article, "New Perspectives on the Hildegard Organ Cycle", was published in the August 2001 edition of the journal, "The Diapason".

Specialties

choral conducting, Gregorian chant, music history, theory, and appreciation, piano and organ instruction, music technology, musicology
