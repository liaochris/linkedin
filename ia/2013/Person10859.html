Audry "JB" Batiste, CFP®, PMP's Summary

As a business owner, I understand the unique set of challenges you face and your desire to maximize the opportunities you’re given. I know your time is best spent in the areas that matter most – serving your clients or customers, attracting new business, improving cash flow and mitigating taxes.

I also understand that to maximize the value you have created in your business, you will need retirement solutions that work for you as the business owner, your top management and your employees. Most people have little knowledge of the benefits and advantages of their retirement plan. Deciphering the complexities of the confusing 401(k) system is practically a full-time job, one which most people simply do not have time for on top of the already-overwhelming responsibilities of work, family, and life.

My business is helping you have a greater sense of clarity and confidence about your most important financial concerns, the largest concern for most being achieving and enjoying a comfortable retirement. I do this through careful planning and an ongoing relationship focused on the important decisions you have to make throughout your lifetime.

My story is not that I am a dream weaver, or dream catcher, but that I do things that are far more practical than that.

The reality is that you have planning issues that my role is to help you clarify those issues. Because I can help you focus on the things you need to plan for, things you need to worry about and things you don’t.

Specialties: Qualified Retirement Plans, Pension Consulting, Investments, Comprehensive Financial Planning, Financial Project Management, and Confidential CFO & Wealth Advisory Services
