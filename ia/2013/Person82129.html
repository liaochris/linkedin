Corky Kyle, MPA, CAE "The Lobbying Pro"'s Summary

"THE LOBBYING PRO" (R) Lobbying/Government Relations Professional. I have been providing government relations services for 30+ years. Managed non-profit associations and business government relations programs at the local, state and national levels and am exceptional managing multiple state legislative programs simultaneously for multiple clients. I hold professional memberships in the American Society of Association Executives, the Colorado Society of Association Executives, and the Colorado Association of Professional Lobbyists. I am a board member of the Colorado Association of Professional Lobbyists. I am a published author on association/business lobbying and management. I have my own radio show on MileHiRadio.com. "In the Lobby" is a political program focused on political. legislative, and regulatory issues from the perspective of a professional lobbyist. I travel the country training groups in lobbying and how they can develop their government relations plan, implement it, and begin to see immediate results. As a keynote speaker on lobbying and management of political and legislative campaigns in both the private and public arenas, I provide audiences with hard hitting programs that shows the value and power of their participation in the political/legislative process within their community and state.
Let me make your program sizzle - Call me!

Specialties: "THE LOBBYING PRO" (R) Strategic Planning, Local,State & Federal lobbying, association management, communications, lobbying, relationship building, time management, interpersonal communications, lobbying planning, budget development, drafting legislation, fund raising, entrepreneurial strategies, opposition research, issue development, managing multiple state lobbying programs, using social media in effectively lobbying public policy. Internet radio programming for issues management
