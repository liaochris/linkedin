Steve Hansen's Summary

I am available for contract/freelance article, content writing, and copywriting assignments.

Are you looking for an effective and professional writer who can provide the content you need? Let’s talk.

I focus on writing relevant and valuable content for green business and media. I work with companies that have a strong environmental focus and commitment to sustainability. We’re not into greenwashing—we’re sharing how they’ve made choices that improve their sustainability and, usually, the bottom line too.

We have a variety of choices for delivering your message, including:

• articles
• blog posts
• case studies
• whitepapers
• video
• web content
• e-newsletters

I'm well-versed in environmental and sustainability topics, including:

• sustainable and green building
• permaculture and landscaping
• energy
• transportation.

Whatever your business, you can count on me to learn quickly so we can get busy communicating with your customers.

While I continue writing for clients, I’ve also started a publishing company called Farmhouse Media. So far I’ve published two ebooks and have another under way. E-publishing will continue to grow in both breadth and volume, with ebooks, podcasts, videos, tele-seminars and so on expanding the way we present and absorb information.

I want Farmhouse Media to be part of the e-publishing revolution in all its permutations, and to eventually publish other writers’ work.  

What are you working on? If you need professional, creative, and effective writing, please let me know and let’s see if I can help.

Steve Hansen's Publications

  • 15 Healthy Habits: Simple Steps for Achieving and Maintaining Your Ideal Weight and True Health

    • Farmhouse Media
    • July 6, 2012
    Authors: Steve Hansen

    This article presents some simple approaches to help you manage your weight and health with diet and exercise. You don’t need to keep up with the latest research, buy each new guru diet book, pay outrageous sums for supplements and gym memberships, or buy boutique foods. You should be able to decrease your total energy use by 10-20% with these steps, over a few weekends, while spending only $100–200.
