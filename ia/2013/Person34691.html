Sean Ryan's Summary

When the web was new, Sean Ryan found his way online and immediately became enamored with making websites. Fast forward to today, and he is a Senior UI Engineer at PeopleMatter. In between, he became passionate about HTML and CSS, mastered the ins and outs of UI design and usability, and learned just enough about various server side languages to make life difficult for those he works with.

When not neck-deep in front-end code, he enjoys spending time with his wife and two daughters in Summerville, SC.

Specialties: Information Architecture, Usability, HTML, CSS, Javascript
