Aaron Roes' Publications

  • The practical implications of the AIFMD on real estate funds

    • Uitgeverij Paris
    • April 2012
    Authors: Aaron Roes, Charles Destrée, Hendrik Bennebroek Gravenhorst

    In June 2011, a European directive (AIFMD) was adopted which provides for regulation of managers of real estate funds. As a result of implementation of the AIFMD, a large number of managers of real estate funds must obtain a licence. In addition, a large number of continuing obligations will be introduced.

    In order to implement the AIFMD, the Dutch legislature has prepared a preliminary draft of a legislative proposal. The article provides an in depth analysis of the consequences of the AIFMD on managers of real estate funds.

    The article is in Dutch only.

  • The Alternative Investment Fund Managers Directive. European Regulation of Alternative Investment Funds

    • Wolters Kluwer
    • November 1, 2012
    Authors: Aaron Roes, Charles Destrée, Hendrik Bennebroek Gravenhorst

    Revision and translation of our article on "The practical implications of the AIFMD on real estate funds" for publication in "The Alternative Investment Fund Managers Directive. European Regulation of Alternative Investment Funds" (Chapter 25).

    The book is written in the English language.
