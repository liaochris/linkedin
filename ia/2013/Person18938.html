PROGRAMS:

EXECUTIVE WISDOM QUEST

A three-day immersion for the adventurous executive, including a one-day wilderness "vision quest." Dramatically accelerates your leadership impact from the inside out. Clear, actionable insight into your thorniest questions and challenges.


THE QUANTUM LEAP (for Forums)

A one-year process for Executive Forums such as EO, YPO, and Vistage. Provides a rigorous agenda and framework to launch a new era for the Forum. Accelerates individual and collective transformation, and ability to champion one another.


EXECUTIVE WISDOM TRANSFORMATIONAL LEADERSHIP PROCESS

Implements a cohesive leadership approach that unleashes greater capability throughout your organization. A nine-month initiative includes assessment, facilitation and coaching.


EXECUTIVE 360 FEEDBACK

Accelerates excellence in your executive role through The Leadership Circle Profile™, a rigorous 360 degree feedback process. Thirty years of research and correlation to business performance underpin this in-depth tool that quickly opens up advanced leadership conversations that might otherwise take many months to get to.

Founding Partner

Synthesis

January 2011 – Present (3 years)

Together with my wife Sandra Visser, I am a founding partner of Synthesis. We coach executive and business-critical teams to unleash their 'team genius’ – which leads to big ideas and creative breakthroughs that change the game in branding, marketing, performance and innovation.

We’ve developed an integrated approach to facilitating and teaching that makes teamwork more creative, efficient and inspired. We work from a whole team/systemic perspective using a diagnostic tool that measures each team's productivity and positivity on 14 success criteria. This uncovers the essential conversations a team needs to discover and use more of its unique team genius. We can also track team improvements in productivity and positivity over time.

We also offer training for team leaders and other collaboration catalysts to strengthen their capacity for lateral leadership to create, lead and sustain high-performing, innovative teams.

We work primarily with Executive-level teams and leaders in mid-size companies of between 75 and 1000 employees and at least $2 million in revenue. Our client teams are largely in advertising, marketing and corporate communications.
