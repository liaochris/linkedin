Daniel T. Fahey's Summary

2013 UPDATE: Celebrating 20th year in business. Thank you to all of you who helped us become successful. Especially rewarding is hearing the stories from former employees and how their families and careers were impacted and growing. Wishing everyone safe passage and have fun doing it. Dan Fahey

Began career as Mechanical Designer. Worked for several engineering companies. 1976, invited to try Recruiting with TAD Technical Services. Great fit, became top recruiter, account breaker, management, start up/turn around specialist. Opened 10 offices in Florida and Georgia. 1985, 5 year stint with Academy Design, Margate, Fl turned $500k company into $5million, opened offices in Atlanta and Tampa. 1990 TAD snatched me back to recover Atlanta and Georgia market and integrate new TAD acquisitions.

1991 TAD acquired C&D. C&D management replaced TAD staff. Got Divorced and Laid off the same day 7/23/91, moved to Maryland. Took Commission-Only position with IT consulting company, placed 40 FTE's, 13 Consulting, 3 new accounts. Poor governance of company resulted in bankruptsy.

MY TURN. Debt free, excellent credit started DanSources March 1993. Built IT Consulting and Staffing business working commercial and government sector clients.

UPDATE 2009/2011 recovery from 2007/2008 depression. Q4 2012 added 100 employees, a fantastic year. 2013 more commercial clients, winning Federal, State and City IT Contracts, adding new GWAC's, hiring new staff. Lost our HUBZone Certification. BUT we are Rocking the GOALS without the damn thing. Hubzone is a faulty SB set-a-side program anyway.

Specialties: 40 years experience in IT/Technical Consulting and Staffing Industry. DanSources is a Certified Hispanic owned SDB/Graduated 8(a)/HUBZone. Commercial, government, Nationwide/Overseas. Defense Contractor.

Sports: Crossfit, Russian Kettlebells, Martial Arts (Bando, JKD(Danny Inosanto), Kyokushinkai), Practioner, Coached Youth Soccer, Certified Soccer Referee.
