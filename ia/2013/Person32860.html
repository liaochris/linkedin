Dawna Davies' Summary

I bought my first property at 10 years old in SLO County and paid my way through college with results of my real estate investment plan. Since then, I have signed over 4000 closed sales, purchases and leases. My strength is my knowledge of the San Luis Obispo real estate market and guiding clients through complicated transactions with ease. Whether you are upsizing, downsizing, planning, or investing, it's my delight to help you with real estate for wherever you are in life. I also enjoy speaking for all sizes of audiences, with presentations that give context of the current market conditions, for other professionals, college classes, non-profit groups and more.

Specialties

Real Estate transactions for sellers and buyers, including conventional and complicated listings and purchases, distress properties, short sales, inter-family transactions, residential income property, and property management consulting. Strategic alliances with clients' financial, tax and legal advisors to create multi-level transactional benefits for clients.
