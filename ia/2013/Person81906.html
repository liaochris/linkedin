Liz Dobbins BSN, GRI, TopLinked.com's Summary

Liz Dobbins Realtor and RN, BSN, is a Registered Nurse with 25 years experience in Maternal-Child Health, in a supervisory role, a temporary staffing fill-in or staff nurse. In 2003, Liz Dobbins started a new exciting career in real estate. Currently, Liz is pursuing her broker's license for further educational advancement. Liz Dobbins' passion is helping people, teaching people and making people's lives better through the healing art of Nursing and the service she provides being a Realtor. Liz finds both careers fascinating and challenging. Liz works hard each day to offer the best service to her clients.

Specialties: Liz Dobbins is honest, meticulous, detail oriented, knowledgeable, patient and caring. These qualities not only help her with negotiations in real estate but also enable her to give the best care possible to her patients. She enjoys helping people with relocation, second homes, short sales and investment properties.
