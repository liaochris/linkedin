Gerry Lynne Baker's Summary

Independent Public Relations Consultant, serving both Fortune 500 and small to medium-sized companies from both the corporate and agency side. Experience spans domestic and international marketplace. Consistently strive to increase client's status in their given industry, calling on expertise in research, strategic planning, tactical implementation and measurement. As a result, hold a proven track record for positively affecting client's bottom line. Select clients include Toshiba Computer Systems Division, Packard Bell, Wavefront Technologies, ANT Limited, ILOG, Canon Computer Systems, NEC, Kodak Digital Motion Imaging, ITT Cannon, KOCE-TV and BigHeart Slide Company.

Specialties

Specialize in High Tech, Consumer Technology, Business-to-Business and Music industries with expertise in strategic planning, corporate identity development, product launches, media relations, issues and crisis management, special event planning, speaker placement and writing.

Public Relations Manager

AST Computer

Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; Computer Hardware industry

January 1991 – February 1996 (5 years 2 months)

As inhouse PR manager at AST Computer, a leader in the computer systems marketplace, served the company in all aspects of PR in the corporate international sector and computer product lines. Raised awareness of corporate products and issues by 43 percent from 1993 to 1994 as measured by the Delahaye Group, a noted industry research organization. Developed a worldwide communications library, communication processes and ongoing comprehensive messaging to strengthen the position of AST in the global marketplace. Consistently obtained national and international media coverage, including prestigious coverage on the company's involvement in China's Most Favored Nation Status issue and the U.S. government's commercial mission in China. Through Baker's PR support, AST received nine product awards and numerous magazine covers in 1995-1996.
