Jennifer V. Miller's Summary

Many individuals understand the strategy and tactics of their business; it’s the “people” part of the equation that often mystifies them.

As the Founder and Managing Partner of SkillSource, I use my education in psychology and 20+ years as an HR and Learning professional to advise mid-career professionals on how to create strategies that “master the people equation” to cultivate career success.

I help people lead with character, influence with integrity, win with positive office politics and capitalize on their Next Big (Career) Thing.


Specialties: Leadership development | Coaching individual contributors on the transition to manager | DiSC® trainer for Inscape Publishing (A Wiley brand) | Speaking at conferences on the topics of influence, networking & Positive Office Politics | Founder, The People Equation blog | Contributing writer: Forbes.com, Huffington Post blog, SmartBrief on Leadership.
