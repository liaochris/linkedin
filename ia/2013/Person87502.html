Martha Spizziri's Summary

I've written and edited magazine articles, website text, and marketing copy. My digital experience includes creating and managing websites; writing e-newsletters; producing slide shows; creating and posting to social media pages; blogging; and writing daily, live news updates from conferences and trade shows. I strive to produce clear, straightforward, engaging copy. My clients say I succeed:

-o- "Martha writes content for business magazines that engages readers. ... Her writing enlivens what could be eyes-glaze-over content."

-o- "She has researched and written several stories for the Scout blog, stories which I -- as the editor of said blog -- have scarcely needed to edit."

Writing and editing clients include the magazines Direct Marketing News and Music Inc. and corporations such as SAP and UPS. I've managed social media feeds for Dian Fossey Gorilla Fund International and the American Society of Business Publication Editors.

Specialties: I'm a generalist who quickly gets up to speed on topics as diverse as psychotherapy and road engineering, but I have particular experience writing about small business, especially retail; marketing; freight-transportation logistics; and materials handling.

If you're looking for writing, editing, or social media help, I'd love to talk.
