Melville Carrie's Summary

As a child of 70s Edinburgh he grew-up immersed in the new age of home computing giving him a perfect foundation for the last 20 years, where he has transferred innovative ideas and research into commercially proven technologies, solutions and products to a gamut of commercial and non-commercial markets.

Firmly established as a mobility expert, technology strategist and digital entrepreneur, innovator and thought leader he has been at the forefront of the computing, technology and Internet revolutions; latterly he has pioneered business transformation with some of the worlds largest brands through the revolution that Apps has enabled.

Believing life is for living, he continuously explores new and innovative processes, technology, music, food, wine, whisky and chocolate; and to keep healthy he rows, cycles and runs.

He is a member of Bowers & Wilkins Society of Sound; a member of the The Chocolate Tasting Club; a member The Scotch Malt Whisky Society, and a beta-tester for Sonos - the worlds best multi-room music streaming system.

More formally, Melville is a Chartered IT Professional (CITP), a member of the Institute of Directors (MIoD), a member of the British Computer Society (MBCS), a member of the Information Systems Security Association (ISSA) and member of the Institute of Advanced Motorists (IAM).

Specialities: Technology strategy; Innovation; Research transfer and transformation; Product management and marketing; Business development and sales engineering; Project and product development management; Motivational team leadership; Operational management; Board direction and advising; Marketing; Strategic and tactical business planning; Internal and external client engagement; Process management; Investment preparation and negotiation; Skill mapping and out-sourcing; Down-sizing and cost reduction.
