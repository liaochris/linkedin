Lennie Mowris' Summary

My work is centered around the idea that creative thinking can solve any problem. I combine the ethical foundation of the Living Principles of design and a background in small business management, branding, graphic design, letterpress printing, and paper arts to craft sustainable solutions to problems for small businesses, non-profits, artists and artisans.

I have additional experience in urban forest management & arboriculture, organic agriculture, independent publishing, book design, event coordination, and sustainable strategy.

If you like my work, bring me your vision, let me help you make it.


Specialties: branding/identity, strategy, sustainable print, letterpress printing, book design, poster design, apparel, web design, photography
