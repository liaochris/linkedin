Audrey L. Godwin's Summary

Audrey L Godwin is the Chief Financial Transformation Catalyst at The Godwin Group. She is on a mission to help business owners who are ready to rock their CEO role and want to understand the financial impact of their decisions. She helps them become money making money masters who are run their business without financial worry.

How? With Clarity, simple Execution and measuring expected Outcomes.

She has been assisting clients to manage their companies with the "end game" in mind. She knows what drives business and it shouldn't be the accounting or the taxes. These are important, but are elements to be managed while keeping your eye on the ball. Building a company that is financially viable for the short and long term allows her clients to be purposeful with intent and profitability so they are able to make an impact in their communities.

Specialties: Financial strategic planning, business and financial coaching, tax strategy and planning, tax preparation, ongoing financial management (bookkeeping), Financially Savvy CEO strategy sessions
