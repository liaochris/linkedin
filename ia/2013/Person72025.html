Karen Ayres Smith's Summary

Karen Ayres Smith is an award-winning print journalist turned online communication pro who specializes in promoting content through traditional and new media channels. Whether it's a video or a blog, Karen uses every available tool to tell stories that resonate with people. And she delivers them to an audience of thousands every day via Facebook, Twitter, Pinterest, YouTube, and traditional promotion efforts. Karen brings experience in leadership and development of online products, as well as daily hands-on production work, to any project.

Specialties

Karen's specialties include online product development and management; writing and editing; and shooting videos and still photos. She is also skilled at using the following programs: Final Cut Pro, Photoshop, Dreamweaver, Flash, Fireworks, Illustrator, InDesign, Plone, Drupal, Google Analytics, Moveable Type, and WordPress.
