Alexandros Kalamatianos' Summary

Alexandros has obtained a BSc. in "Digital Systems" (2008) and an M.Sc. in "Technology Education and Digital Systems" (Track: e-Learning) (2010) from the Department of Digital Systems at the University of Piraeus, Greece.

He has received the Best Short Paper Award in IEEE International Conference on Advanced Learning Technologies ICALT 2009, Riga, Latvia (July 2009). The paper titled "ASK - LOST 2.0: A Web - based Tool for Social Tagging of Digital Educational Resources" (co-authored by Alexandros Kalamatianos, Panayiotis Zervas, Demetrios Sampson) presents the ASK - LOST 2.0, an open source web - based tool that aims to support the process of social tagging of any type of digital educational resources

Finally, he has experience in Web Applications (HTML, XML, PHP, Javascript), Database Management Systems (MySQL) and Web Services (NuSOAP).
