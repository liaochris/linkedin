Jeremy Clarke's Summary

Part strategist, part creative director, part cattle-prod - Jeremy Clarke’s core strength is solving “big” or difficult problems, creating big ideas, motivating a team to enact those solutions in a coordinated way and imbuing brands with lasting IP.

He was trained in London by Saatchi and became one of the “enfants terribles” of the ad world in London, winning every major British advertising award multiple times - as well most major US Awards, including the rare O’Toole Award. Clarke was inducted into the UK’s Greatest Advertising Creative Department of All Time in his late 20’s and is responsible for 3 of the UK’s current Top 100 ads.

Specialties

Strategic and creative development.
