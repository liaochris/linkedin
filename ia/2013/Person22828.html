Chika Nwoko's Summary

An academic background in law, and 10 years as a computer systems specialist at a global biotech firm, serving thousands of satisfied clients, coupled with a passion for online marketing and helping people was the inspiration for the creation of Abundant Impact Marketing.

Are you a business owner or professional that would like to use the Internet, or other medium to attract and retain more of your ideal clients and customers and grow your business, but don’t know how? Or are you frustrated and hate marketing?

You may not be alone, and I would like to tell you that there are five main ways to increase your business profits.

Would you like to know what they are?

1. Increase the number of customers you serve.
2. Increase the average size of each transaction by selling more.
3. Increase the frequency of transactions per customer.
4. Raise your prices.
5. Reduce your costs and overhead.

My objective is to create and implement an enduring and effective marketing campaign both online and offline that will increase my client’s profits by focusing on 1 through 4 above.

How I work with my clients is to first, conduct a strategy session with them where I look at all their advertising and marketing pieces, whether online and offline, and determine what’s working and what isn’t. I can then implement an effective marketing campaign that will get you more clients and better results.

If any of this resonates with you, or you would like to discover how I can help you grow your business online and offline by attracting and retaining more of your ideal clients and customers please contact me on here for a FREE no obligation strategy session.
