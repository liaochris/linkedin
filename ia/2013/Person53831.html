Heather Post's Experience

Founder/Director

The Etiquette Seed, LLC

Public Company; 1-10 employees; Professional Training & Coaching industry

January 2011 – Present (3 years 1 month) based out of Daytona Beach, Florida with operations nationwide

I provide specialized Social and Business Excellence Programs and Speaking Engagements promoting a culture of professionalism, civility and ethics.

I earned my certification to teach etiquette programs through the Emily Post Institute in Vermont and incorporated my vast work experiences and knowledge/abilities in body language and communications from a career over the course of two decades in law enforcement to create very unique social and business excellence programs.

Our Social and Business Excellence Programs provide you with a foundation of interpersonal skills, leadership skills, life skills and etiquette knowledge that you can build upon to change your success ratio for the rest of your life.

Services:
• providing businesses and corporations with professionalism and excellence training programs
• providing colleges and universities with leadership, situational awareness, and social & business excellence programs for both students and staff
• providing youth leadership and social skills training for ages 6 and up
• educational presentations for groups and organizations
• individual coaching
• promoting civility and ethics by providing interpersonal and social excellence programs for schools, PTAs, and private homeschoolers
• dynamic speaking engagements

We have additionally incorporated a COMMUNITY COMMITMENT PROGRAM, participating in and encouraging others to provide direct financial support and countless hours of volunteer services to local community organizations and programs. The Etiquette Seed firmly and passionately believes in giving back to the community and showing children and adults alike a path to success that they might not have otherwise known existed.

Part of this commitment to society also includes our CIVILITY INITIATIVE through which we meet and speak with people nationwide to bring to light the importance of civility in society by furthering civility dialogue, programs, and related legislation.

Career as a Law Enforcement Officer/ Investigator

Law Enforcement

February 1992 – April 2011 (19 years 3 months) State of Florida

Decades of experience in investigations, interviewing, body language, etc.

Worked in various law enforcement capacities: *uniformed road patrol *detective working homicides, internal affairs, general crimes, etc. *surveillance/undercover operations *initiated/conducted search warrants *on the Board of Directors and member of several county & statewide law enforcement units *acting Sergeant for Criminal Investigations Division *conducted property recovery & statutory updates training for officers statewide, etc.

Additional Certifications: *CVSA (Certified Voice Stress Analysis) Operator *SWAT School-2001 *REID Technique of Interviewing & Interrogation *Field Training Officer *Line Supervision *Instructor Techniques *Crisis Intervention Training *...and HUNDREDS of hours of additional specialized law enforcement training

Commendations & Awards: I have received numerous letters of commendations & awards from various venues (citizens, law enforcement, attorneys, FDLE, US Marshals Service, Probation & Parole, etc.) throughout my law enforcement career
