Doug & Lisa Lee's Experience

Fitness & Business Coach

Team Beachbody (Self-employed)

Self-Employed; 5001-10,000 employees; Health, Wellness and Fitness industry

March 2010 – Present (3 years 6 months)

After several years of owning expensive home fitness equipment and gym memberships I experienced lackluster results. I heard about P90X from some friends at work. I was skeptical. Workout DVDs? I can't get into a Jane Fonda workout!

So I did a little research on P90X and finally decided to give it a try. This was no Jane Fonda workout program. This was intense, hard core, fat burning muscle building exercise. I have never sweated and pushed my muscles so much as I did with P90X. And the workouts were so well structured and fun, yes actually fun that they flew by.

Before I knew it the 90 days had passed and I was in the best shape of my life. Since that time I have completed the "Insanity" program which definitely lives up to it's name and I am now doing a hybrid of both. I will never stop using Beachbody workout programs to maintain and improve my fitness. They produce the greatest results in the shortest amount of time, period.

Since I was having so much success with the workout programs I decided to take the next step and become a Team Beachbody Coach. My wife and I do our workouts together and work our Beachbody business together right from home.

Our business has provided us freedom that we have never experienced before. We now help others to achieve the same success in both health & wellness and business. With Beachbody you can be in the best shape of your life and get paid to do it.

If you would like to see how we can help you contact us right away via email or phone call. We would be very happy to speak with you.
