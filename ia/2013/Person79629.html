Cory Lebson's Summary

* Nineteen years of experience in user research & evaluation, information architecture and accessibility
* Dynamic speaking, training & presentation skills
* Extensive experience writing reports, proposals, articles, project plans, and book chapters
* Established ability to manage user experience (UX) projects and staff
* Enjoyment of learning - degrees in business (M.B.A.), sociology (M.A.), and psychology (B.S.)

---- User Experience and Content Strategy ----

* Workshops to assess goals, visions, target user experiences and content requirements
* Research to understand customer needs and experiences
* Custom training of staff to support strategy

---- Research & Evaluation ----

* Usability testing - Lab-based/remote/in the field, eye tracking
* Heuristic reviews including evaluation of web content and use of plain language
* Cognitive walkthroughs of static and interactive designs with representative users
* Requirements gathering - Stakeholder/user interviews, reviews of documentation, and competitive analyses
* Accessibility - Section 508 reviews and training, W3C WCAG (DHS Section 508 Trusted Tester)
* Market research - Both quantitative and qualitative
* Survey design, implementation, and analysis
* Focus groups related to websites and technology
* Web analytics - Google Analytics and WebTrends; synthesis with other forms of analysis
* Quantitative analysis of data with Microsoft Excel, SPSS Base and SPSS Custom Tables

---- Information Architecture ----

* Reviews of site structures and proposals for new organizational schemes and taxonomies
* Card sorts - both online and offline efforts, creation and analysis of dendrograms
* Search engine optimization, site search analytics, metadata, federated search, faceted classifications
