Finbarr Carter's Summary

So who is Finbarr?
With a passion for supporting the well-being of people, communities and the planet at large, I have great faith in the powers of positivity, focus and belief to create change. My ability is to communicate from the heart about what is really going on in any given situation. I am a qualified teacher, NLP Business Practitioner and CTI Co-active Coach, as well as being trained in Process Orientated Psychology and Non Violent Communication. As well as being employed full time I am a DJ, a radio presenter, a freelance trainer, entrepreneur and most importantly a loving father of three.

Specialties: Active Learning, training, social enterprise, business start up, social networking, marketing, brand management, business plans, cash flow, coaching, conferences, cooking, customer relations, event management, finance, management, networking, personnel, presentation skills, public relations, media liason, promotional materials, publicity, radio, reports, strategic planning, supervisory skills, teaching, web site production and maintanence, workshops,
