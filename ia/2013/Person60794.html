Jeff Bova's Summary

Grammy Award-winning producer Jeff Bova‘s passion for pop and classical music collided with synthesizer technology to make him one of New York’s most sought-after session keyboardists, programmers, and arrangers.

With a career spanning 30 years, Jeff has appeared on over 300 albums, many of them platinum-sellers. Among the impressive roster of artists who have enlisted Jeff's help are Luther Vandross, Celine Dion, Michael Jackson, The Back Street Boys, R. Kelly, Iron Maiden, Eric Clapton, Tina Turner, Robert Palmer, Billy Joel, The Bee Gees, Cher, Meat Loaf, and Cyndi Lauper. And you gotta admit this is really cool: Jeff was a member of Herbie Hancock’s renowned “Rockit Band!"

Jeff won his “Album of the Year” Grammy as a producer for Celine Dion’s “Falling Into You” and his film work includes songs for “My Best Friend’s Wedding” and Disney’s “TInkerbell.” A top-notch string arranger, Jeff's credits include Jordan Sparks, David Archuleta, Nick Lachey, Jesse McCartney and Clay Aiken and his programming credits include Katy Perry, Mario, and Daniel Powter. With co-conspirator Alex Hass, he created the Electronica group “Cypher 7” and with Japanese artist Akiko Yano, “The Hammonds.”
