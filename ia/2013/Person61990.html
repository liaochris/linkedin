Jennifer L. Spavins, Esq.'s Summary

I am a passionate, energetic, and creative attorney in Massachusetts. Currently, I work for a small law firm where I focus primarily on criminal defense work. I also handle a wide range of everyday legal matters including estates, civil litigation, and family law.

My past experience includes mediation, working in the United Kingdom, and for state and city government. My varied background enables me to adapt my skills quickly to new situations.

Admitted to practice in Massachusetts and United States Tax Court

Member of the Boston Bar Association and the Women's Bar Association.
