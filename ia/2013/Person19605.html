Candace Crowe's Summary

Candace Crowe has worked exclusively with aesthetic medical practices throughout the U.S. and Canada since 1999, helping them with their patient education and marketing materials. A graduate of Florida State University, she built her firm based on a passion for art, beautiful design, and an understanding of aesthetic patients.

As President and Creative Director of Candace Crowe Design, Candace truly enjoys listening to practices' marketing needs, then conceptualizing and producing spectacular results. A detail-oriented perfectionist, Candace is recognized by clients and colleagues as a consummate professional who is a delight to work with. Candace offers over 35 years’ marketing experience, the last 13 of which, have been fully devoted to marketing the aesthetic practice.

Currently serving over 250 practices, her company was first in the industry (2002) to bring touchscreen, state-of-the-art education to the patient with REVENEZ®. Today, Candace Crowe Design continues in the vanguard of technical innovation, finding new ways to connect patients and practices by delivering key messages and promoting services in formats embraced by physicians and patients alike.

Candace is recognized as an expert in marketing the aesthetic practice and is a regular speaker at many of the national and international aesthetic industry meetings such as The American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery, The American Society of Plastic Surgeons, The American Academy of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery, Foundation for Facial Aesthetic Surgery, Florida Society of Plastic Surgeons, and the Multi-Specialty Foundation Symposium. She also contributes articles on marketing to both print and online publications.
