All She Wrote Consulting creates social media engagement strategies for clients in the wine, culinary, hospitality and cultural tourism sectors, and is the creator of Eat.Drink.Tweet. events -- social media seminars with a culinary twist.

We can teach you how to effectively manage your social networks, or we can do all that tweeting, video-sharing, and status-updating for you, while merging these new tools with traditional media relations and marketing.

All She Wrote has created Eat.Drink.Tweet., events that combine social media education with a wine and culinary experience; a unique professional development opportunity that brings colleagues together to learn about social media while networking “IRL” — in real life.

That’s All She Wrote.

Allison Markin, Principal, has 15 years of experience in media relations, marketing, crisis communications, government relations, writing and editing, and is also an engaging facilitator and speaker, as well as an instructor at multiple campuses of Okanagan College. She is an early adopter of social media and brings her years of skills in tradition media to the new frontier of "socmed".
