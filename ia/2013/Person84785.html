Mara Alexander AADP CHHC's Summary

Mara Alexander is the founder and creator of GlutenFreeWorld TV, a website with gluten-free video travel logs, articles, restaurant reviews, recipes, and interviews. Mara loves to share her experiential knowledge with having traveled over 500,000 miles via land, sea and air in her worldwide adventures. In early 2012, her advocacy work via her website landed her a nomination as the "Top 40 under 40" and "Young Executive of the Year" for the 2012 Naked Pizza Annual Foggy Awards, an honor given to those who shed light on the gluten-free community. She is a Certified Health Consultant through the Academy for Healthcare Management, a Certified Health Coach through the Institute for Integrative Nutrition, a gluten-free and Celiac advocate, and she has been in the health care industry for over a decade. She is also a rare disease advocate for PKU.

Mara Alexander AADP CHHC's Experience

Founder

GlutenFreeWorldTV®

April 2011 – Present (2 years 6 months)

Mara Alexander was diagnosed with Celiac in March 2007. Since then,her goal has been to pursue a life of wellness and good nutrition; she is on a lifelong gluten-free diet. In April 2011, she started GlutenFreeWorldTV® to provide worldwide encouragement to those who have Celiac or on gluten-free diets. Her website www.glutenfreeworld.tv has attracted thousands of visitors worldwide due to her travel video logs, free recipes, interviews, and beautiful pictures of her food adventures. She has garnered the attention of many around the world as she seeks to provide support to those who are on gluten-free diets.
