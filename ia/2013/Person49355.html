Aaron Hennessy's Summary

Aaron is a licensed builder who established Hennessy Building Projects in 2009 after over 20 years in the building industry. His knowledge in the building industry has expanded after completing many projects in both Australia and the United Kingdom.

With his excellent communication, knowledge and proven quality finished results, he has been providing clients with a positive building experience over the years. Aaron has completed numerous quality projects of his own as well as being sought out by other builders to provide his professional services.
