Ernesto Moreno's Summary

Ernesto has a proven record of helping organisations to effectively manage change through complex restructures and transformational processes. He is a skilled consultant with solid expertise in Organisational Development, Change Management and Culture Change interventions with more than 10 years of multinational consulting experience, working for a number of organisations across different sectors including public, private and non-profit.

His previous employers include Atos Consulting and PwC and he has worked for organisations like the RBS Group, Ministry of Justice, Department for Works and Pension, Mace Construction Group, Transport for London, Home Delivery Network (HDNL) and The Global Fund to fight HIV, tuberculosis and malaria just to name a few.

He is an enthusiastic and pragmatic individual who blends his cross-cultural and cross-functional experience with his HR and Management education to offer a mix of skills that enable him to build high performance teams and deliver change while actively engaging stakeholders at all levels.

Ernesto has broad experience in designing, developing and delivering training programmes, including: team-building, leadership, managing conflict and personal development. Ernesto is passionate about personal leadership and is normally seen facilitating leadership seminars or as a guest speaker and facilitator at different events.

Ernesto is also an entrepreneur who has set up 3 successful business in the franchising industry. A group of Subway Restaurants, CeX stores and more recently a non-invasive beauty clinic in London.

Ernesto holds a BA in Business Studies and MA in International Business Administration and other postgraduate studies in Psycology of Management and HR Development with the CIPD. Ernesto is also an experienced coach, trainer and facilitator.
