Brian Rassi's Summary

Brian Rassi brings over 15 years extensive experience in business development, direct sales and customers relations to business ventures. As a Managing Member of Nationwide Business Consultants, he developed an eye for seeing outside-the-box opportunities and coupling them sound business practices producing over 10 revenue generating ventures in all different industries. As a home mortgage consultant for Wells Fargo Home Mortgage, he developed a strong customer base that generated an average of $1-1.5 million dollars in new originations monthly in sales. One of the top sales associates for KONE, he was promoted to Sales Manager prior to venturing beyond this field. Averaged over $1-2 million dollars worth of new construction elevators and escalators sales each year.

Brian received his Marketing Degree at the University of Iowa, Henry B. Tippie College of Business. His expertise in the sales and marketing of diverse off and on-line products and services, led him to become an entrepreneur. He is the author of MeFormula and he created a CD meditation program called MeSpirituality for professionals who desire personalized business solutions built around their personal and professional needs and wants.

As a catalyst, he specializes in creating high performance business ventures by focusing on consistency of operations, balanced growth, and integral profitability. He believes success is built through valuing collaboration, cooperation, trust, openness, consistency and execution. His motto: Know what you're good at and make sure everyone stays in their proper lane.
