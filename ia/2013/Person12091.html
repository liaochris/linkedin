Bart Van Eck's Summary

Water is the most important asset for humanity and our environment. Without, no food, no economy no nothing! It simply is the backbone of our society.

Various natural and human induced mechanisms are threatening both water security and safety in many parts of the world. My personal and professional mission is:

To improve water security and safety worldwide.

Through:
• The initiation, development, guidance and support of catchment restoration processes and projects throughout the African continent.
• facilitation and participation in water related education.
• projects in the field of climate adaptation and mitigation that do not simply aim on the reduction of carbon emissions.

Specialties

aquatic ecology, water quality, sanitation, process management, climate change, policy making, interdisciplinary idea development

Living Lands

Nonprofit; 1-10 employees; Environmental Services industry

March 2010 – March 2011 (1 year 1 month)

Living Lands is a South African based Not for Profit organization and coordinates the PRESENCE network. PRESENCE is a multi-stakeholder network that aims to restore the landscape of the Baviaanskloof. The network consist out of national and international governmental organizations (LNV, Department of Water Affairs omongs onthers), Universities (Wageningen, Rhodes, Stellenbosch and others), implementers, NGO's and others. Due to this network we are able to bring scientific knowledge back to the ground, actively engage stakeholders and create jobs in the region.

As research coordinator, my task is to coordinate the research for the PRESENCE network, meaning that the gained knowledge will be further developed either horizontal or vertical. Our fields of research are: ecosystem understanding (hydrology, land and watermanagement etc), ecosystem valuation, incentive systems, livelihoods, stakeholders, policy, governance, institutionalization and remote sensing. Other tasks include but are not limited by:
- improvement of the research strategy;
- facilitating research
- organize workshops.
- broadcast our work
- stakeholder engagement.
