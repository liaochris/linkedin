Andrew Davies' Summary

With degrees in Illustration, 2D animation and Broadcast Design, Drew's multifaceted interests have taken him from small beginnings in advertising at the largest agency on the island of Barbados, to the bowels of Mississippi where he helped build the ad agency MadGENIUS Inc. In between, he managed to throw in some stints at The Weather Channel, Eyevox Film and Video Production and co-found Paragon to boot.

As the most Jamaican person at Paragon, Drew now leads the creative team where in addition to a smattering of speaking engagements, he straddles print, web and motion graphics, working on both the design as well production of all three. He's also managed to convince national magazines like Layers and publications like American Corporate ID to print his work.

He's recently been enjoying a few high falutin projects for clients like CNN International, The Weather Channel, The Hatchery LLC, Malaco Records, Epic Image Entertainment, Savannah Economic Development Authority, The Creative Coast Alliance, SCAD and Farmer D Organics along with some top-secret ones that he can't even mention. While those are fun, he likes to stay grounded by changing the world, working for non-profits like AWOL (All Walks Of Life) and CAVU (Clear Air Visibility Unlimited).

Specialties

Show Packages, Motion Graphics, E-Commerce design, Web design, Brand Identity design, Print Marketing Collateral.
