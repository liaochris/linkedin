Amy Cox's Experience

owner

subURBAN Homestead

March 2009 – Present (4 years 10 months)

subURBAN homestead connects our clients and their audiences with fresh, in season, healthy living. We provide customized workshops, programs and events that empower audiences to implement simple but effective wellness based lifestyle solutions. We emphasize utilizing local sustainable resources whenever possible, building a community based on good stewardship and high ethics.

The clients we serve are diverse but the commonality is a focus on creating good, clean fun. If you represent a medical or trade association, own a community focused business, provide programming for your organization or work for a corporation, we have programs for you. We enjoy teaming up with like minded partners such as Whole Foods, Slow Food, The Edible Schoolyard Project, The Green City Market and countless others.

owner

Cutting Edge Consulting

May 2008 – April 2013 (5 years)

Cutting Edge created custom sustainable solutions, events and programs for organizations to enhance the sensory experiences of the clients they served. Clients included Moet Hennessy, Lettuce Entertain You, Sportfuel and Eat Like the Pros (The Sports Nutritionists for the Chicago Blackhawks), culinary professionals such as Culinary Author & Educator Deborah Krasner, Chef Paul Virant and Mixologist Adam Seger as well as not for profit organizations such as Common Threads and the Green City Market.

Board President

Slow Food Chicago

Nonprofit Organization Management industry

January 2009 – December 2011 (3 years) Chicago, IL

Served as the president of a 12 member board overseeing all of the events and activities offered to a local membership working to ensure access to good, clean, fair food for all. Attended the International Terra Madre 2010 Conference as a United States Delegate. Created grass roots programs that were the conduit to making positive changes on a local level to the sustainable food system. Collaborated with Slow Food USA, Slow Food International and many other like minded local organizations.
