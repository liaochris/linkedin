  • Sales Psychology Boot Camp: Building Rapport

    • April 2012
    Authors: Gene Plotkin

    Rapport is one of the most important, but least understood components of sales. Without rapport you can’t get anywhere in a sale. Traditional sales training dismisses rapport as just “finding something in common,” but those techniques are inconsistent, forge weak relationships and can actually do more harm than good.

    In this class, you will learn:
    *That rapport is actually a state of trust and agreement, which is essential for every type of sale
    *Why creating rapport with your prospects will allow you to bypass their inner critic and remove objections
    *Simple techniques of body language and voice tone that will allow you to create rapport with anyone on the phone or in person
