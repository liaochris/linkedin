Donavan Lightfoot's Summary

Starting out in a boutique style environment I developed a wide base of 3d skills that include modeling, animation, FX, rigging and the all important problem solving. Working internationally I have accumulated 12+ years of production experience working in commercials, film and game Cinematics as well as in-game production. Recently in the Unites States I set out working in generalist and character TD positions. The last 5 years however I specialized in motion capture, animation & facial animation disciplines.

Specialties

Facial animation, motion capture animation.
Relational constraint rigging in Motionbuilder to create corrective deformation biped rigs and facial rigs.
Working with 3d asset management pipelines using Maya / Motionbuilder / MAX and FBX.
