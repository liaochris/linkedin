Jim Esmond's Summary

I am an HR & sales professional focused on helping clients identify, select, retain and develop top quality employees. My mission is to help my clients maximize the performance of their employees and help those employees grow and develop to their full potential. I believe employers should have and utilize every possible tool to hire employees that are the best fit for their jobs and employees should be placed in roles that best match their occupational interests, behavioral traits and thinking and problem solving skills. When this happens, employee engagement and productivity increase, performance problems and turnover decrease and the company's bottom line flourishes!

I am:

▪ A personable executive with excellent analytical and computer skills
▪ A leader who fosters teamwork and communicates clearly
▪ An adept problem solver that looks for unique approaches to challenging issues
▪ A team player willing to share information and ideas; not concerned with "who gets credit"
▪ Someone committed to continuous learning and skill development
▪ An excellent communicator and speaker
▪ Experienced in establishing and implementing various screening and assessment processes to make sure the right people are hired and placed in the right role.
