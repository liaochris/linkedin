Anne Brookshire's Summary

The thread of being fascinated by all things creative has permeated my life & eventually led me to fall for the art of photography. I aim to capture the treasured moments in my clients' lives with a photojournalism style & a fine art flare. Having recently been married myself & been in your shoes, I have a special eye for detail & I know how much energy goes into planning the whole day! I put all the energy I have into capturing my clients' weddings, as if they were my own.

I love meeting new people & discovering the things that make them intrinsically beautiful. It's my love & respect for people that helps me capture genuine emotion in my work & photographs that my client's cherish!

I'd love to get to know you & have the opportunity to capture your wedding day!
