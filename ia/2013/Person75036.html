Kevin Russell's Summary

With more than 20 years of expertise and executive leadership, Russell’s national and international consulting and investment advisory experience spans a number of key industries and domains, across several international markets. And the companies benefiting from his leadership, read like a veritable “Who’s Who” among blue-chip conglomerates: Price Waterhouse, KPMG, Ericsson and Marcus & Millichap Real Estate Investment Services, and Noble Capital – A Real Estate Investment Bank.

Brokering and advising clients on investment transactions only scratches the surface. Mr. Russell’s true niche is his committed professional mission: mastering an unsurpassed and intimate understanding of how economic, market conditions, demographic shifts, and capital investment markets affect an asset’s operational and investment performance – before, during and after the acquisition and/or development.

My primary business focus and objective is on creating and preserving clients’ wealth through commercial real estate investments. His specialty is acquiring, disposing, repositioning, developing and funding of commercial investment properties. His client advisory initiatives stress continuous return on equity investment, coupled with asset value enhancement, contributing both short and long-term asset value appreciation.

Specialties

• Provide both advisory and investment commercial brokerage services to real estate investment consortiums, partnerships, and institutions with a single focus and objective of creating and preserving clients’ wealth.
• Responsible for assessing, securing and capitalizing on market opportunities while providing transaction management, market feasibility, acquisition due diligence, valuation modeling and financial underwriting.
