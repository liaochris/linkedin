Isabel Amaral's Summary

She is the only Portuguese protocol and cross communication expert that has been invited to give lectures in four continents and to be a keynote speaker at several international congresses held in Portugal, Spain, Brazil, Italia, Argentina and Hungary.

Keynote speaker at the 3rd, 4th 7th and 8th International Protocol Training in Beijing (June 2008, July 2009, August 2012 and 2013). She was the first foreigner speaker at the Press Room of the Foreign Affairs Ministry in Beijing (July 2009 and August 2012).

Teacher at the Portuguese Diplomatic Institute: seminars for new Portuguese diplomats (February 2009 and May 2010), for foreign diplomats from 36 countries (May 2010), for protocol officers from Angola in Lisbon (May 2012) and in Luanda, Angola (March 2011) .

Keynote speaker at Summer Universities (U.Menendez Pelayo and U.Internacional de Andalucia) and at EGAP, in Spain.

Keynote speaker at 1st European Protocol Meeting, Elche, (May 2012), UNED, Madrid (Febr. 2012.), UCAM, Murcia (April 2012) and at the VIII and X Protocol and Communication Course in University of Vigo, in Pontevedra (May, 2007 and May, 2010).

Trainer on Image,Communication and Protocol for Cabo Verde Government officials (July 2009).

Having a Master degree in International Relations by the Lisbon University, she introduced protocol studies in Portuguese Universities and was a guest teacher at Masters and MBA's in :
Salamanca University, 2012;International School of Protocol and Diplomacy, Belgium, 2009-2011; ISPE - Universidad Camilo José Cela, Spain, 2011, UNINOVE, Brazil 2011;UCS, Brazil,2012, Lisbon Catolica Business School, Portugal, 2013.

Keynote trainer for Demos Group in Portugal and Spain and for International Consultancy Portugal in Portugal, Netherlands and Belgium.

Trainer at major banks, architect and lawyer offices, consulting and pharmaceutical companies in Portugal.

Protocol consultant for special events in Portugal.

Fluent in English, French and Spanish.

Specialties

Training individuals to reach the excellence level in their professional performance
Finding the detail that will transform a regular event into an unforgettable experience
