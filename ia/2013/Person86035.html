Marina Lumley's Summary

Providing communication consultancy services and training to clients since 2008.

Previously Head of Client Service and on the Board of leading integrated agency Meteorite, I spent 14 years working with blue chip brands such as British Airways, Marriott Hotels, Whitbread, BT, Starbucks and The Royal Mail to name but a few, developing engaging integrated communication programmes for customers.

Typically working with business owners, Managing Directors, Marketing Directors and fellow Consultants.

Consultancy services provided:
* Independent Marketing training direct to customer
* Course Director for the Chartered Institute of Marketing delivering training courses, motivational talks and bespoke projects.
* Marketing consultancy direct to customer and through consultancy businesses such as Oxford Strategic Marketing offering integrated marketing communications strategy encompassing all techniques including all forms of digital marketing and CRM.
* Associate of Aprais facilitating and consulting for clients and their agencies to deliver actionable evaluations of relationships and performance.

Specialties: Fully integrated marketing experience. Includes database segmentation; CRM strategy; Communication frameworks; Creating contact and communication strategies; Communications Training; Specialism in Loyalty Marketing. Working with luxury and bluechip brands. including BA Executive Club, Jumeirah Hotels, Marriott, Harrods, Starbucks, Costa Coffee, Nectar - LMUK, BAA World Points, British Council and many more.
