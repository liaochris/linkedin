Michelle Cubas' Summary

Coaching is a long way from my USO entertainer days, but the experience of serving and pleasing the audience has proved invaluable in my keynote and public forum presenter roles. My experience provides a natural platform to reach out and share top-tier ideas in a Quick Studies™ format for instant usage.

Today, I consider myself a Business Barista™serving up fresh and hot ideas daily! Clients benefit from a shortened learning curve—They're too busy and distracted to dedicate months and years to return to school. A commitment to lifelong learning is key!

After a 12-year stint with two major corporations in L.A., I chose to assemble my talents and skills, package them and offer the composite to entrepreneurs, women in career transition and small companies who would directly benefit.

Working directly with owners, managers and individuals is rewarding because I can see the impact on the choices we make together. I admire the "lion heartedness" of business owners and advocate and champion their decisions to be independent and forge a place in the business world.

What good are ideas if they don't lead to action? The Business Literacy™ Syllabus and EMBA™ (Entrepreneurial Mastery of Business Assets™) material provides a direct link between concept and activation for entrepreneurs. I perceive my role to be an advocate and guide to assist them in achieving their goals and visions.

As a wordsmith, one of my recent projects is editing educational material.
