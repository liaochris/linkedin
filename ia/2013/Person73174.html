Katrina Alcorn's Summary

I'm an experience design consultant with a specialty in growing and leading teams.

My areas of expertise include user research, information architecture, interaction design, content strategy and team leadership for projects ranging from consumer-facing web sites to corporate Intranets to iPad applications to informatics for medical devices.

Past clients include Adobe, Sun Microsystems, Blue Shield of California, UC Berkeley, Charles Schwab, Gap Inc., LeapFrog SchoolHouse, Architecture for Humanity, the United Methodist Church, San Francisco Museum of Modern Art, The Women's Foundation of CA, and eBay.

Background in journalism, public relations, and documentary film.

My first book, "Maxed Out: American Moms on the Brink," was published in September 2013 with Seal Press/The Perseus Books Group. More at maxedoutbook.com

Specialties: User experience design, research, writing, workshop facilitation, workplace consulting, managing creative teams
