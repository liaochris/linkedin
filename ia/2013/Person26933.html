Corbin Fraser's Summary

Travel Writer

I'm an award winning travel writer & travel blogger based out of East Coast of Canada, in Halifax, Nova Scotia. I am the Chief Travel Writer & Editor at IBackpackCanada.com, which is a blog I started in April 2009 as a way to document and share my experiences, adventures & Canadian Travel tips while exploring Canada & living out of a backpack.

IBackpackCanada.com is a travel blog, but also an independent travel guide for people interested in experiencing the great outdoors, the thrill and adventure, and the picturesque landscapes that Canada has to offer.

Website & Print Designer

From late 2009 to 2011 I was working as an independent creative, designing websites & print materials for small business's on a per job basis. What started out as a side-gig, eventually grew, along with my interest for the industry. Seeing a future in this career I dove in deep.

I returned to Post-Secondary school at the Centre for Arts & Technology and enrolled in the Graphic & Digital Media Design program to learn everything I could about the industry. During my studies there, I was the Student Representative at the Graphic Designers Society of Canada (GDC), where I was responsible for keeping the Centre for Arts & Technology & the GDC in the loop on school events, industry events, and assist in recruiting efforts for the GDC.

In early March of 2011 I started a creative partnership with Andy Keith as Command Base Creative Design. We both have a similar personality, design style & respect for one anothers work, that making the leap into starting a Website & Print Design business together was the next logical step.

Specialties
