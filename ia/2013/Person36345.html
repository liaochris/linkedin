Dr. Lawrence G. Stern's Summary

Mission
We are committed to serving the exceptional life experience. We are invested in offering quality care in a nurturing environment. We are dedicated to people realizing their interconnectedness and participation with life through a greater expression of their own self healing. We are faithful to providing a celebration of life where individuals and families may come to express more of themselves, life, health and happiness. We are devoted to providing outstanding service at an affordable investment so that the members of our community can express more of their inborn potential and healing capacity.

SCIW offers an integration of transformational services that include, Network Spinal Analysis (NSA), Quantum Reflex Analysis (QRA) and Zyto Technology. Our objective is to provide services that heal your body, increase energy levels, replenish, rejuvenate, reorganize and transform!
