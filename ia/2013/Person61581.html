Jen Creer's Summary

Edit Rx fills a unique niche in the clinical research arena: We are here to provide you with excellent writing and editing consultation. In 2006 alone, we have secured more than $2 Million in grant awards for various institutions. We save you time, get your manuscripts published, and your grant proposals funded.

Specialties

* CONSORT (Consolidated Standards for Reporting Trials) requirements for reporting the results of clinical trials
* QUOROM (Quality Of Reporting Of Meta-Analyses) requirements for reporting the results of systematic reviews and meta-analyses
* Cochrane Systematic Review requirements
* Qualitative manuscripts
* Quantitative manuscripts
* Literature reviews
* Editorials
* Essays
* Process papers
