Ignacio Herreros Margarit's Summary

Throughout my entire career I have been focused on IT and telecommunications law. I specialise in complex IT and telecommunications contracts, negotiation with regulatory authorities, intellectual property licensing and protection, electronic commerce and internet legal issues, online criminal offences, privacy and data protection; combining my legal advice with a sound knowledge of IT technical issues. Also, can provide corporate law assistance with substantial legal experience.

I hold a Master Degree in IT Law, collaborate as a lecturer in several LLM in Madrid, and I am one of the co-authors of the book “Spanish Telecommunications Law". I speak Spanish and English.

Some matters that I am or have been in charge: - Licensing and launching a full MVNO. - Licensing and launching a MVNO service provider. - Licensing for VoIP services, in interoperability with PSTN. - Application for numbering resources to the Spanish regulator. - Analysing legal particularities of online advertising. - Licensing raffles and promotional games. - Advertising gambling and betting games in Spain. - Licensing online music service providers with the Spanish collecting entities.

Specialties

IT law, technology law, eCommerce law, telecommunications law, intellectual property law, internet law, data protection law, corporate law
