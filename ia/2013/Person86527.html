Lorraine Thompson's Summary

In 1994 I founded MarketCopywriter.com, a NY metro digital and print copywriting service bringing businesses special expertise in ecommerce, healthcare, pharmaceutical and food industry copywriting.

As Barneys New York’s senior ecommerce copywriter, I…

• Manage a team of 7 copywriters
• Maintain editorial oversight of all content published on Barneys.com
• Handle high-volume sample copy as well as regular merchandise copy
• Write positioned, persuasive copy that engages Barneys’ customers, drives traffic, boosts sales and reduces returns
• Work with SEO and Digital teams to develop traffic-driving copy strategies
• Create style guides, customize training materials and teach persuasive copywriting
• Partner with Dev to develop productive ecommerce tools

I also blog at MarketCopywriterBlog.com, CopywritersKitchen.com and BlogHer. My posts are featured on AllTop and I won Ashoka’s Young Champions of Maternal Health blogging contest in 2010. I live blogged for ASHOKA at the International Conference on Maternal Health 2010 in New Delhi, India and participate in lively professional communities on Twitter, Instagram, Pinterest and Google+.

Specialties

I bring businesses exceptionally strong skills in B2B and B2C content marketing strategy, persuasive copywriting, SEO copywriting, social media community building, editorial writing and copywriting management.

Special expertise in luxury ecommerce, healthcare, pharmaceutical and food industries.
