Jean-Simon Marquis' Summary

Jean-Simon has specialized in Communities of Practice and facilitating virtual networks for several years. He is currently working for the Canada School of Public Service as a Senior Advisor in Interactive Communication and Learning. Previously, Jean-Simon worked as an Advisor at l’Université du Québec’s head office on developing and facilitating communities of practice. In addition, Jean-Simon was also involved in online community facilitation for an Industry Canada program called “Les Francommunautés virtuelles”. Jean-Simon Marquis holds a Master’s degree in Communications from the l’Université de Laval in Québec city.
