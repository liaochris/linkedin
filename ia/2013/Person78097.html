Lamar Smith's Summary

Toward Balanced Living

Having lived an exciting and fulfilling career ranging from decorated fighter pilot to company CEO, Lamar C. Smith recognizes that Balanced Living is vital not only to achieving your goals, but also to enjoying life’s journey along the way.

Lamar was a highly decorated Air Force Special Forces attack and rescue pilot in Vietnam, flying 114 combat and rescue missions. He earned two Silver Stars, six Distinguished Flying Crosses, and 11 Air Medals, awarded for gallantry, heroism and meritorious achievement.

Change and Challenge

Later, while serving as an Air Force instructor pilot, Lamar saw the need for personal financial planning in his own life, and ultimately joined First Command Financial Services, a registered investment advisory firm serving, at the time, U.S. military officers and their families. In eight years, he rose to be president of the firm and was subsequently named CEO.

During his 15 years as CEO and Chairman of the Board, Lamar led the company as it grew to serve more than 1 million clients in 300,000 families; oversaw more than $18 billion in invested assets and $52 billion in life insurance in force; and chartered one of the first banks in the country focused on service via the Internet. During Lamar’s tenure as a senior corporate leader, the company’s annual revenues more than quadrupled from $48 million to $225 million and profits grew from $6 million to $31 million.

Improving Your Balance

In 2007, Lamar began a full-time commitment to developing and sharing the concepts he has learned about empowered living through Improving Your Balance, while continuing to serve on corporate boards and to be actively involved in a number of charitable and outreach organizations. He also authored the popular business parable, There’s More to Life than the Corner Office, published by McGraw-Hill in 2009.

Specialties: Personal Growth, Self Management, Sales, Consumer Behavior, Team Building and Governance
