Gary Bowers' Summary

Do you have valuable information sitting in a database but don’t have the expertise to analyze it? Have you identified external data that would enrich information your organization already has? Are you tired of relying on gut instincts and, instead, would like to become more competitive by making fact-based decisions? You’re not alone.

Many business organizations have done a good job at capturing valuable information within databases. Simply collecting data doesn’t enable informed decision making. You must also apply good business intelligence (BI) techniques.

Since 2001, I’ve teamed with clients to implement BI solutions that:

• save time and money
• more efficiently utilize limited resources
• provide the ability to share and disseminate information
• improve organizational performance

My passion is finding business insight in data. Let’s work together to learn how this insight can improve your business’ performance.

Specialties:
Deciphering technical concepts for business users, translating high-level business requirements into technical jargon, and learning a client’s business well enough to point out when what they need is different than what they ask for
