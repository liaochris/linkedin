Lisa Sommerfeldt's Summary

Over 19 years of professional experience applying adult learning principles and multimedia instructional design theories to analyze, design, write, develop, train, and evaluate customized courses for strategic nationwide training initiatives.

I also specialize in creating blended learning solutions (instructor-led, multimedia DVD, eLearning and virtual sessions) and converting instructor-led materials into engaging scenario based eLearning courses.

Extensive experience with diverse client populations from the following industries: government, law firms, manufacturing, finance, pharmaceutical, health care, higher education and K-12 schools.

Specialties

analyzing business processes; writing business requirements reports; conducting and writing needs, task and gap analysis; writing course design blueprints; identifying and creating learning solutions; designing & writing storyboards for eLearning & DVD courses; creating virtual training programs; writing instructor-led materials, participant manuals, end user guides and job aids; facilitating classroom training sessions and on-line virtual courses; mentoring junior level instructional designers
