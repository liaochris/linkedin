Angelique Rewers, APR, ABC's Summary

Angelique Rewers is president of Bon Mot Communications, which provides consulting, project management and writing services for any project where communicating clearly and concisely is key to its success.

Prior to launching Bon Mot, Angelique led communications for the corporate staff groups of Constellation Energy, a FORTUNE 200 company with 2006 revenues of $19.3 billion. In this role she was responsible for financial communications, as well as for supporting the communication needs of audit, business performance improvement, finance and accounting, information technology, investor relations, legal, public affairs, risk and the office of the CFO.

Before joining Constellation, Angelique led marketing communications for the C4ISR division of Northrop Grumman Electronic Systems. She was responsible for developing and implementing strategic communications plans in support of key sales programs, including advertising, media relations, trade shows, marketing collateral and employee communications.

Angelique also has served as communications manager for the public services practice at BearingPoint, Inc., one of the world's largest providers of management and technology consulting services to Global 2000 companies and government organizations. She began her career at CARMA International, where she conducted media research and analysis on behalf of FORTUNE 500 companies, not-for-profit organizations and public relations agencies.

Her award-winning work has been recognized by the League of American Communication Professionals, the Public Relations Society of America, the International Arc Awards, the Association of Marketing and Communication Professionals and the American Business Communicators.

Angelique is an active member of PRSA and IABC, and she has a bachelor’s degree in international communications and a minor in radio and television from the George Washington University.

Specialties

Annual reports, employee communications, change management communications, marketing communications, media relations, analyst relations and communication training seminars
