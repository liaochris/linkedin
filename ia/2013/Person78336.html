Lara Galloway's Summary

Lara Galloway, The Mom Biz Coach of www.MomBiz.com, is a sought-after business coach, speaker, writer and Blog Talk Radio show host in the mom entrepreneur world. Her coaching has helped countless women entrepreneurs define success on their own terms and achieve it. She co-founded MomBiz.com with Shelagh Cummins, and together, they offer business coaching, mentoring and training for women who want to run successful businesses without letting those businesses trump every other priority in their lives.

Lara is an innovative and entrepreneurial online engagement marketing expert and proven social media influencer with a track record of excellence and success in strategically growing communities, increasing user engagement, and generating leads across all established and digital media platforms. Brands enjoy working with her since she provides them the chance to be seen as credible and THE solution to the problems her vast community of mom entrepreneurs face.

Join her on Wednesdays for the Mom Biz Solutions Show on BlogTalkRadio.com/MomBizCoach. And she’s nearly always in her “Twitter Office”: www.twitter.com/mombizcoach.

Contact her directly at lara at mombizcoach dot com to discuss creating a custom training program for your group or about having her speak to your small business or women's group.

Specialties: managing your brand online, engaging with your customers online, social media strategy, social media marketing, social media training, time management training, small business growth training, small business management training, engagement marketing, attracting ideal clients, twitter, vlogging, podcasting, relationship building, work/family balance, keeping family priority #1, entrepreneurial coaching, business development, mom entrepreneur business coaching
