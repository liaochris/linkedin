Michel Gassier's Experience

Winemaker & Owner

Vignobles Michel Gassier

Sole Proprietorship; 11-50 employees; Wine and Spirits industry

September 1999 – Present (14 years 2 months)

I currently run with my wife our family winery located in the Southern Rhone region of France. Converting our vineyards to organic farming, we are dedicated to making wines with strong and original identities and protecting our "terroirs" for future generations.

President

Domaines Bernard

January 1994 – July 1999 (5 years 7 months)

President, Domaines Bernard, Orange France.
Originally Director of sales and marketing at this Rhone valley Negoce company, I became President and General Manager in 1996. Business spanned across all Rhone Valley appellations, represented 700,000 cs/year sold in over 30 countries.
Left to take family business over.

Product Manager

Sara Lee Bakery
