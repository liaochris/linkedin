Karen Lee Whitworth's Summary

Karen's Detail Custom Frames is Your Design Partner in Quality Art and Framing - Where Custom Does Not Mean Expensive!

We are a wholesale art and framing company with over 30 years of experience, open to the public, catering to the Interior Design Industry for Residential, Commercial and Model Homes. We also enjoy working with Photographers, Artists and all local clientele (retail and commercial) to provide quality picture framing at reasonable prices.

We have an extensive collection of framing choices, including linen liners, wood fillets, paper and fabric matting, and several choices of glazing materials. We offer stretching and mounting for the artwork, including gallery wrapped Giclees on canvas, along with gel-coating with brush strokes to enhance an open edition print.

At Karen's Detail Custom Frames we have a large library of art catalogs, along with a wide assortment of artwork including open edition prints, posters, and black and white photography to help our customers find that special piece to go with their home or office decor.

Along with our custom framing services, we also offer custom pre-made frames in standard sizes from 5 x 7 to 24 x 36, including photo frames, custom framed wall art decor and custom framed beveled or straight cut mirrors, all in stock.

Specialties

We specialize in all areas of custom picture framing. From high-end residential custom framed mirrors, archival framing for fine art and photography (including museum glass), original artwork, certificates, family photos, posters, prints, 3D shadow boxes (including sports memorabilia and jerseys) to lower-end production jobs. No job is too small or too big!
