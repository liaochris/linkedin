Jack Campbell's Summary

My career has led me to having unique expertise in the end to end product development, manufacturing, and marketing cycle -- from raw idea, to feasibility determination, design, refinement, business plan development, forecasting, funding, engineering, manufacture, channel design and development, launch, and sales management and marketing.

My specialty is the clever integration of existing technology bits and pieces into new products that create new successes in the global marketplace. The more complex and confounding the underlying technology or business systems involved, the better I perform.

Jack Campbell's Experience

Founder & CEO

American Atomics, Inc.

May 2013 – Present (4 months) Greater Nashville Area

I am leading this revolutionary for-profit social enterprise toward an August, 2018 market launch of the world's only mass produced small nuclear reactor power plant.

The HOPE 40 power plant will provide 40 years of clean, safe 40 MW power at a cost of about 3¢ per KWh — under half of the existing lowest electricity cost in the world. The result will be to start a global era of cheap, limitless, completely clean electric power that finds its way to every corner of the world and lifts the quality of billions of lives, fixes our environmental issues, and begins to clean up a century of damage caused by fossil fuel fired power production.

Our first 1,000 power plant production run will occur in 2018, with these tiny, powerful, and disruptively inexpensive electricity generation systems rapidly spreading health and prosperity across the world.

Founder & CEO

June 2012 – April 2013 (11 months) Nashville, TN

A disruptive new wireless computing platform that radically simplifies the experience for businesses, consumers, and developers by providing a hosted web app environment where all data from all apps is shared in real-time — enabling a whole world of incredible new interactive features for businesses and consumers that are simply not possible with today's approach.

I conceived the platform, developed it over a 6-year period, recruited developers and partners, led the design-for-manufacture process, obtained funding, and am now piloting the product line into mass production. Project received $50 million in private funding July, 2012.
