Don Mirra's Summary

Trends come and go, but handcrafted quality is the nucleus of our mettel. When was the last time you had a deep reaction to a photograph, a photo you couldn't look away from. That’s the byproduct of high quality work, and it's what people feel when they see our photography. They cant stop looking...which is the whole point.

We cut our teeth as a international photojournalist, traveling to over 57 countries, in some of the most challenging and austere environments, working on long-term projects with complex and socially intense narratives. This unparalleled background gives us a colorful, dimensional pallet to our work that adds soul to a company and brand - Our work solves communication problems by compressing the transaction cost for your customers to understand what you do.

We work with organizations domestically and worldwide to expose the real, human elements of the organization to mold the brand’s public image using the power of photography.

Do you do great work? Are looking to work with a creative team? Are you tired of conceited, self-important, egotistical people in the creative industry?

Then call us. We will change the way you feel about photography and photographers.

Services include:

Long Term Photography Projects

Contract Photography Services
Annual Reports
Campaign Based Projects
International Projects
Journalism

Specialties: Photographic Narratives, Icon Image Creation, Annual Reports, PR Campaigns, Corp Portraits, Remote Photography Projects, Extreme Photography, Arial Photography, Multi-Media.
