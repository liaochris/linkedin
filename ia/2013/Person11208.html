Andrew Wong's Summary

From 2009 to 2012, I was the User Experience Manager for the Intuit Partner Platform team at Intuit, Inc. (NASDAQ: INTU), the financial software company that produces Quicken, QuickBooks, and TurboTax. I recruited and led a talented team of UX designers and front-end developers. We designed Intuit App Center, an app store for Web and mobile small business apps that integrate with QuickBooks accounting software. I also designed Web apps to integrate Salesforce and eBay with QuickBooks data.

Prior to Intuit, I was a User Experience Manager and lead designer at SuccessFactors, Inc. (NASDAQ: SFSF), the SaaS (Software as a Service) leader in HCM (Human Capital Management), serving 2,850+ customers with 5.4+ million users in 185 countries and 31 languages.

Previously, I designed the UI for the successful HomePortal DSL gateway for 2Wire (now Pace). At Asimba, Inc., I was Cofounder and Chief Design Officer. As Creative Director at Poppe Tyson Interactive, I designed the first Web sites for the White House, Netscape, Intel, Chrysler, and Cadillac.
