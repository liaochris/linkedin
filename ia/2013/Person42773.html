Erin Daniels' Summary

Erin is a creative strategist, product innovator and architect of teams that foster smart, creative design thinking. With a user-centric approach to product design, Erin specializes in bridging intuitive, elegant user experience and creative design to complex concepts, highly technical architectures and early stage products.

Erin’s broad experience spans from product startup companies to globally recognized advertising agencies to consulting for enterprise, government and military, publishing, healthcare, education, and globally recognized consumer brands. Erin has worked with brands including Capital One, McGraw-Hill Education, PBS, comScore, The Limited Brands, Entertainment Software Rating Board (ESRB), Home Depot, VeriSign, MBO Partners, and several Federal Government agencies and DoD components. With a career start at Rosetta Stone in its early product days, Erin has hands-on experience with several early stage start-ups and enjoys advising local mobile and tech start-ups in product strategy, branding, marketing and design and helping her diverse clients succeed in their market.

Specialties: Creative Strategy, Creative Direction, Branding and Identity, Mobile Applications, Web Applications, Web sites, Software Applications, Product Design, Product Development, Usability, Usability Testing, User Experience, User Engagement, Information Architecture, Design Research, Design Thinking, Visual Design, Infographics, Data Visualization, Innovation, Management, Digital Media, Advertising, Social Media, Communications, Publishing, Recruitment, Professional Development, Instructional Technology, Augmented Reality, Simulation
