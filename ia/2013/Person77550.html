Kurt Scholle's Summary

Entrepreneur and founder of Web Asylum, Inc and www.Website-ROI-Guy.com. My company, Web Asylum, Inc, has been building and marketing successful websites for more than 16 years.

I blog about successful website strategies at www. Website-ROI-Guy.com. I am also a partner in www.CertifiedSocialMedia.com.

Website success and social media are passions. I speak on developing successful website strategy, Internet Marketing and Social Media, including Twitter and Facebook workshops.

I provide website success tips on my Fan Page http://www.facebook.com/websiteroiguy

I have been an investor in Internet-related companies in Chicago, including Novacon and SISNA, both of which supplied high speed Internet connections and website hosting.

Let's connect! Email: Kurt @ WebAsylum.com

Prior to the Internet, I spent nearly 25 years in radio as an announcer, program director, newsman and music director, working for some legendary stations: WLW-Cincinnati, WKQX-Chicago, Oldies 104.3-Chicago, WISN-Milwaukee, WIRL-Peoria and the Satellite Music Network and the Interstate Radio Network.

Specialties: Online business development. Website architecture, planning and marketing. Competitive website analysis. Social Media Marketing, Search Engine Optimization, Guerrilla Marketing.
