Founder & Creator

TheHipRoof.com - Social Media Education for the Real Estate Industry

September 2010 – Present (3 years 2 months)

I created and founded The Hip Roof in 2010. Site currently in redevelopment as a private real esate coaching community. Expected relaunch in late Summer/early Fall 2013.

Developed with the assistance of Human Business Works. It is a social media educational community for the real estate industry. The need for such a site was clear nearly 2 years ago when I began my quest to develop the site. Far too many people are telling real estate agents to use social media without helping them understand the reasons behind. I believe that the "why" and the "how" are essential for a real estate agent to have success in the social spaces. As one of the top experts in social media for real estate it is about building a strong community, free of outside influence and advertising, for optimal learning experience. I have gotten the gracious cooperation of some of the top real estate agents, mortgage lenders, and social media marketing/advertising people in the US to become Expert Contributors. The site is a premium site, with a lite option. With just word of mouth marketing membership has exceeded 100 members in just over 2 months. The site will be offering syndication, branding, and customized forums, and one partner site have membership in excess of 600,000. The site was to officially launch on April 1, 2011 - but as a Full-Time Real Estate Agent first, the marketing campaign has had to wait. We continue in full operation and are working to refine and define, focusing the curriculum and community to social media. For more information please contact me and I would be happy to answer any questions or provide a demonstration.
