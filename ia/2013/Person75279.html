Kevin Wunder's Summary

Kevin Wunder is a young and dynamic marketing professional. He was born and raised on Maui and has lived in New York, The Bay Area and now resides in Utah with his family. He loves family, Crossfit and sushi.

Kevin graduated from Brigham Young University with a B.A. in Communications (emphasis in Advertising) and a Minor in Business Management. Through his schooling and professional work, Kevin has been able to consult and work with national sports and auto brands, national student housing services, international non-profit and humanitarian efforts and also small-to-medium-sized local businesses all over the country.

When he's not working, you'll find Kevin wrestilng with his sons, hitting up his local box for an AMRAP or two and spoiling his wife.

Specialties: Marketing messaging and campaign development, SEO and PPC Campaign Management, Digital Photography, Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator.
