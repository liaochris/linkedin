John McCreesh's Summary

Chartered Information Systems Engineer and MBA with over 20 years’ experience of commercial IT management. General business management experience reporting to Board level.

Active in the open-source world since 2000 as a developer, documenter, and advocate. Extensive experience of leading an international volunteer community as Marketing Project Lead and Community Council member for OpenOffice.org.

Specialties

IT strategy, consultancy, applications and infrastructure development, programme and service delivery. Community development and story telling.
