F. Bradley Meyers' Summary

Iron Bridge Integration, Inc. is a healthcare software company that focuses specifically on healthcare interoperability. Through healthcare’s first HiPaaS(healthcare integration platform as a service), the NuvolaHub, Iron Bridge delivers a consumer based interoperability middleware product suite that solves healthcare problems such as exchanging data between physician practices, ambulatory clinics, and hospitals.

The NuvolaHub provides the flexibility to quickly and efficiently integrate existing third party applications both in house and cloud hosted. This unique HiPaaS platform eliminates the needs to host, manage, and hire expensive HL7 integration and interoperability resources.

Iron Bridge also partner’s with third party vendor’s such as EHR’s, and various other healthcare information systems to license the NuvolaHub technology as an enterprise HiPaaS.

Specialties: Healthcare Integration, Healthcare Interoperability, iPaaS, HiPaaS, EHR Integration, HL7, Healthcare IT Consulting, Meaningful Use, NuvolaHub, NuvolaPortal
