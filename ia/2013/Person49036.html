Glenn Garnes' Summary

What I do:

I operate a small business community called Small Business CEO. We help budding entrepreneurs and seasoned business owners to raise their business I.Q. in our online learning center, and to make quality connections with other serious business professionals with whom they can collaborate and share ideas and resources for their advancement.

My ideal client:

My ideal client is a start-up business owner, or a small business looking to evolve. That includes service professionals, brick and mortar retailers, business coaches, lawyers, accountants, or anyone else who you would normally see in a chamber of commerce or business referral group environment.

Good strategic connections for me include:

The following people are good strategic connections for me, because we are good sources of referrals for each other:

Business coaches, accountants, bankers, business brokers, people who sell products and services to small business.

Author:

I write on a variety of small business and personal development topics. See the section of my profile related to "Publications" for two free resources you'll want to know about.

Professional Speaker and Consultant:

I speak and consult on a variety of business topics including business networking, social media marketing, relationship marketing, collaborative marketing, partnering, strategic alliances, and joint venturing, social media marketing, and social media marketing systems.
