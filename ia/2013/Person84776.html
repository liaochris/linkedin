Marcus Povey's Summary

Marcus Povey is CEO at Marcus Povey Consulting, an ISV and IT consultancy company. He has a proven track record of developing efficient solutions to complex problems, building value and reducing costs.

Previously, Marcus has been involved in developing secure messaging systems, life saving medical software and have implemented efficiency enhancements which contributed directly to dramatic company growth.

Marcus was an instrumental part of the team behind the software platform powering Latakoo, a Texas based new media company.

This platform is revolutionising the transport of high definition video between journalists, news companies and consumers around the world by increasing speed and reliability while at the same time dramatically reducing cost.

He was part of the team that created the open source social networking platform Elgg (winner of InfoWorld’s 2008 Best Of Open Source award) which is currently being used by the World Bank, the United Nations and in countless companies and academic organisations around the world to improve the efficiency of communication both internally and externally. He was an integral part of its architectural design process from version 1.0 onwards and was involved in shaping policy and features.

He is passionate about democracy and how people are empowered by technology to join together and form a powerful force for social change and have organised well attended events on this subject, attracting sponsorship from big names including Google.

Outside of IT Marcus enjoys rock music, travel, skiing, climbing, capoeira and piloting light aircraft. When he isn't travelling Marcus is based in Europe, where he lives with his girlfriend and more eclectic musical instruments than is considered strictly healthy.

Specialties: technology strategy, platform development, server development, web technologies, open government, transparency, direct democracy, social media, Cryptography, Social networking, Elgg, PHP, security, Java, Python.
