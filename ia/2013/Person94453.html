Mike Olander's Summary

You wouldn't build a house without a blueprint. You wouldn't wander into the wilderness without a map. So why would you develop creative work without a good media plan?

Full-service or á-la-carte, media is at the core of what we do. We find your audience, develop cost-effective strategies to reach them, and direct relevant, targeted creative work to maximize the impact of every marketing dollar.

Mike Olander's Experience

Founder & President

Olander Group

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry

March 2004 – Present (9 years 9 months)

Founder and President of full-service independent media agency focused on media research, planning and buying. Founded company from ground-up and developed all agency systems & processes. Actively leading development of new and creative media strategies for our clients.

Founder & Partner

KarmicFusion
