Cheryl Kametz's Summary

Executive Retail Recruiter with over fifteen years in executive, field and corporate office placements. Experience with companies ranging from industry leaders to new start-ups. We work with national companies to source, screen, coach, qualify, and hire the right candidates. We are relationship builders who has success networking to find qualified candidates that fit a predetermined search profile. We present only the right candidates with the right attitude who has a passion for the brand! We work on a referral basis and handle nation wide retail and wholesale positions.
Our goal is to go beyond your expectations!
Finding that fit is my passion!!

Specialties

We have success hiring all levels of Field management; General Managers, District and Regional Managers, as well as, Vice President of Stores. Corporate positions include: Marketing, all Buying positions, Allocation, and Human Resource management. Wholesale positions include: Designers, Sales Executives, Product Development, Marketing, and Human Resource management. We work in conjunction with clients on workforce planning and employment.
