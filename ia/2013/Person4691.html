Alison Walton's Summary

Hi, I'm Alison,

I have been working in Business Administration and Bookkeeping for the past 28 years ( gosh that is a long time - lucky I am young at heart!). I have a passion for helping small business owners become more productive and their business's more profitable.

I started Sensible Office Solutions after becoming retrenched. After many years of working for others and not being entirely satisfied with my career, I took the plunge and went out on my own - one of the best decisions I have made. Many of the people I have met along the way have become not only great clients but great friends.

Now to the serious stuff:-

We, at Sensible Office Solutions can assist you by:-

- Saving you money by providing services that would otherwise be undertaken at greater cost by specialist employees.
- Help you improve your organisational capability by providing an essential administrative backbone to your business and implementing systems and procedures.
- Giving you back your TIME. You then have a CHOICE of how you use it.

So if you would like more TIME for you to do the things you love (that's why you went into business, right?), give us a call or shoot us an email, we are more than happy to have a chat about how we can help you!


Specialties: specialist bookkeeping, accounts payable and receivable, payroll, administration
