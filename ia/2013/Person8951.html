Antonio Esposito's Summary

Hello. My name is Antonio and I love transforming ideas into creative, effective realities.

I have over a decade of experience in designing and developing websites, as well as producing high-quality marketing and advertising materials to secure online and offline success.

I take pride in all the work I produce, no matter how big or small the task. I am an artistic person and passionate about my designs, but this is always balanced by my dedication to achieving my clients' targets and exceeding expectations wherever possible.

My other interests include photography, travelling and cooking dishes from my native Italy. I worked in several locations before settling in 2006 in Manchester, where I enjoy drawing inspiration from the city's diverse cultural and professional communities.

WHY HIRE ME?
My priorities are simple: I give my clients what they want; and I care about my work.

My wide range of skills and experience in graphic design and website development enable me to adapt to each client's needs.
Whether you have a firm plan of what you want to communicate and how, or merely a tiny idea that you would like to see evolve into a distinctive brand image, I can help you to realise it.

I firmly believe that good communication is central to any successful working relationship and I listen carefully to my clients' wishes, offering updates at every stage of development. The result is that I meet agreed deadlines and the solutions I present are relevant and functional, complemented by my flair for innovative, creative designs.

I have respect for the time and preferences of others and am therefore punctual, precise and pay enormous attention to detail. My work makes me happy and I want my clients to be happy too, so I am delighted when they return to seek my expertise again and recommend me to friends and business contacts alike.
