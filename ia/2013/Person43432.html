Evan Sage's Summary

I am now working in my eighth year in the Toronto Real Estate market and I am enjoying the different challenges that seem to present themselves each day in an industry that is rapidly evolving.

With 90% of Buyers using the internet to search and procure their next home, it is essential to have a complete digital marketing campaign for your clients to make sure they dont leave any money on the table.

I have been very fortunate in my career with great people putting faith in my ability to market, negotiate and service the sale of their family homes.

I have found particular success working in Central Toronto and the Factory Loft market. This expertise ranges from Hoggs Hollow to Summerhill and from Leslieville to Parkdale. I have spent a lot of my time working in the Summerhill and Rosedale area

Specialties

Working with people is what I love to do. Helping with the purchase or sale of their most precious asset is an incredible job. I specialize in extensively marketing homes, with a complete digital marketing campaign, negotiating for top dollar and providing fun, friendly service throughout. I do a lot of work selling homes in Summerhill, Rosedale, Lytton Park, Hoggs Hollow, Leslieville, Parkdale and Factory Lofts throughout the city.
