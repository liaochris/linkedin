Irene Pereyra's Summary

Irene Pereyra is the Global Director of UX and Strategy at Fantasy Interactive, and she is responsible for translating business requirements into intuitive interactive solutions as well as guiding the UX team to develop detailed wireframes that incorporate all technical, editorial, and usability specifications of the project while constantly advocating for the end-user.

She believes in a holistic and collaborative approach to problem-solving. At Fi, she has lead the strategy and UX initiatives for USAToday.com, FOX.com, HTC.com, Verizon, BBC, EA, Google, Nickelodeon, RedBull, Broadway.com, History Channel, and EA Sports. Her projects have been awarded FWAs, Webbys, Cannes Lions and European Design Awards.

She has spoken at numerous design, UX and internet conferences, and conducted workshops at various institutions under which Hyper Island in Stockholm Sweden, The Design Academy in Eindhoven the Netherlands, and the British School of Design in Moscow. Her personal projects have been displayed in design conferences and festivals in Amsterdam, Antwerp, Paris, New York, Singapore and Tegucigualpa. She holds a Bachelors Degree in Fine Arts from the Art Institute of Atlanta, and a Masters of Science in Communications Design from Pratt Institute in New York.

Originally from Amsterdam, the Netherlands, she currently lives in the Red Hook neighborhood of Brooklyn – is a Scandinavian sweater, Soviet sci-fi, HCI and mid-century furniture enthusiast, and commutes by bike to work everyday.
