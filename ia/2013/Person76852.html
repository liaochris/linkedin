Kris Olin's Summary

Kris Olin is a Web Designer with a Masters Degree in Marketing.

Kris is specialized in Web 2.0, SEO, Social Media Marketing, Facebook Advertising and Internet Promotion Strategies. Kris also consults with companies on their online marketing and is the author of the Facebook Advertising Guide.

Kris has a Masters Degree in Marketing, MSc (econ.) from the Helsinki School of Economics and Business Administration and he has studied Design and Advertising at the University of Columbia, U.S.A. Kris is currently living in Australia with his family.

Specialties
