Delaware302 .com's Summary

Delaware 302 is fast becoming one of the leading technology and information systems companies in the First State. Originally conceived in 2011 as a clothing line, which used the State's area code (302) both as the company/product logo, and as a way to display Delaware pride, the company has gained more notice for the Technology Services and website design prowess of owner Phil Wojcik.

Armed with over 14 years of hands on experience designing and building solutions for some of the who's who of the Fortune 500, and strategic partnerships with targeted solutions providers that he has built over the years, Phil now brings this technology to the table for his clients.

His partner and wife, Andrea Wojcik, who has over 15 years of media and communications experience, completes the picture on the social media and communications side of the company.

In early 2013, they launched the re-imagined Delaware 302, focused on providing innovative website design, creating powerful information systems, and supporting businesses of all sizes in their communications needs. The backbone of the new Delaware 302 is the 302 Engine - a module based website and content management system.

Delaware302 still retains its clothing and merchandise line, selling products primarily at community events, as well as its information portal 302 Beaches.

Follow us on Twitter @Delaware302 and @302Beaches on Facebook at DE302 and 302beaches and of course right here on LinkedIn!
