Peggy Gray's Summary

I started working in the fashion industry at 17 years of age. Working many facets of the business from sewing machine operator to quality control manager, overseeing many sewing subcontractors allowed me to learn most aspects of the industry.

Twenty years ago, I decided I wanted to strike out on my own so that I could follow my passion for creative design. My love of texture inspired me to purchase knitting machines so I could produce my own fabric to combine with unique and interesting fabrics bought on the open market.

Specialties: One of a kind and Unique clothing for women
