Christopher Martin's Summary

Chris Martin is an acquisition advisor with The Principium Group based in Doylestown, Pennsylvania. Prior to joining Principium, he was associated with another merger & acquisition firm. He also previously founded and developed a lawn care fertilization and weed control company which he later sold to a national lawn care company.

He has extensive experience in sales and marketing, having enriched channels for companies in engineering and online-retail sales. His interests in management information systems stems from his studies in college, then fueled as the director of operations to building marketing and sale-to-fulfillment systems.

Chris is a graduate of the University of California at Los Angeles with a B.S. in Applied Mathematics and a specialization on computing.

Chris volunteers his time to a student group at a local college. The group's purpose is to provide a platform to its members for networking and mentoring on business, innovation, technology and entrepreneurship.

He enjoys eating regional cuisines, playing tennis, hiking, kayaking, and enjoying landscape design for friends and family. He spent many years working alongside a rich variety of green industry business owners/operators.

Specialties

Chris works nation-wide with green industry clients and subcategories like lawn care, landscape design/build, landscape maintenance, tree care, pest control businesses, nurseries, garden centers, sod farms, and tree farms.
