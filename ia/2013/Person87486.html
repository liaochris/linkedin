Martha Finney's Summary

Best-selling author, consultant and speaker, I work with companies to achieve their critical objectives by capturing the voices, insights, stories, and advice of their most engaged, high-performing talent.

I am also the creator of the Career Landscapes team building workshop -- an executive integration or retreat ice-breaker experience that unifies key groups through authentic storytelling.

The author of 17 books about HR, employee engagement and career management, my two best-known books are HR From the Heart (co-authored with Libby Sartain, former CHRO of Yahoo and Southwest Airlines) and The Truth About Getting the Best From People. My books have been translated into Chinese, Arabic, French, Spanish, and Tamil.

Additionally, as a speaker and HR evangelist, I offer custom HR celebratory programs and speeches for special internal HR retreats and annual corporate gatherings. As a master interviewer specializing in the HR career path, I have interviewed the CHROs of the following companies: Apple; Pixar; Bank of America; Intuit; Yahoo; Jet Blue; The Gap, Inc.; AFLAC; SAS Institute; Wynn Resorts; IBM; Knight Ridder; Safeway; Marriott; Gannett; HP; Starwood Hotels and Resorts.

My work and research, which exclusively focuses on positive workplace experiences and the voice of the employee, has been covered by CNN, NPR, ABC, The Wall Street Journal, The New York Times, Miami Herald, San Jose Mercury News, San Francisco Chronicle, Across the Board magazine (Conference Board).

Specialties

Celebrating the HR profession and building knowledge of essential components of world-class HR careers

Improved customer service; re-engaged critical talent; retention; recruitment; motivation; safety; engaged culture; public relations positioning; change objectives.

Areas of emphasis: HR groups; healthcare; workforce development; sales; new managers; new hires; community relations.

Products and services: Keynotes and workshops; voice-of-the-employees interviews; executive interviews

Martha Finney's Publications

  • Money With Meaning: 42 Practical Ways to Discover Your Calling and Land Your Dream Job Now

    • ebook
    • July 1, 2011
    Authors: Martha Finney, Deborah Dasch

    Money With Meaning proves that you don't have to give up who you are to land a great job that you will love. Featuring 14 profiles of people of all walks of life (a novelist, corporate executives, computer graphics expert, a dive shop manager, an owner of a Caribbean resort), Money With Meaning also include 42 immediately actionable tips to put you on the road to finding and landing your next great career.

  • Unlock the Hidden Job Market: 6 Steps to a Successful Job Search When Times are Tough

    • FT Press
    • September 2009
    Authors: Martha Finney, Duncan Mathison

    When high-value executives are laid off, their companies give them access to world-class outplacement counselors. But this kind of expert guidance isn't available to everyone else -- until now. Duncan Mathison, former executive for the stellar outplacement firm Drake Beam Morin (or DBM) and Martha Finney share essential secrets, steps and inspiring stories for finding both yourself and your next career in the most challenging job market in history. Says John Challenger of Challenger, Gray & Christmas: "Over 70% of all jobs are never published. This book will help you discover and land these jobs!"

  • Rebound: A Proven Plan for Starting Over After Job Loss

    • FT Press
    • February 2009
    Authors: Martha Finney

    You’ve just lost your job. (Or you’re expecting to.) You know you’re not alone: Millions of great people are losing their jobs these days. But this is you we’re talking about. Losing your job can turn your life upside down. It can mess with your mind, your heart, your health, your family life...not to mention your financial security. Losing your job is just plain painful. This practical book will help you get through the trauma-–and come out stronger, smarter, better. Top workplace expert Martha Finney brings together all the answers you need to empower yourself and regain mastery over your own life. Drawing on powerful insights and personal stories from an enormous network of experts, she answers questions like:

    How do I protect my finances?
    How do I get past the anger, alienation, and isolation?
    Why haven’t I heard from my coworkers?
    What are my rights?
    Can I get a better severance package?
    Can I sue? Should I?
    How do I stay on my career path and keep my options open?
    How can I objectively evaluate a new job offer?

This book tells you how to

    Rise above past career missteps
