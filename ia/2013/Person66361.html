John Schneider's Summary

My work rolls up to three key areas of expertise:
Digital strategy
Content and search marketing
Social media activation

I am a proven leader and innovator in digital marketing with a successful record of building strong teams and doing work that hits business goals. I was early to content marketing, or how we tightly integrate digital PR with SEO, social media and content strategy, and executing on this concept. I was especially early in bringing content marketing to startups and to B2B companies. This integration is where most companies I've encountered still struggle, and it's where I relentlessly focus. I help companies, products and ideas be found by the audiences that will love them. I help the media "discover" the value that is inherent in what my clients offer, and I help teams engage with prospective customers in ways that are natural, authentic and contextual.

I love companies that have a smart, tight vision and the technical expertise already nailed as that amplifies the work that I do. I can help you lay out a strategy and plan that scales, help you execute to that plan, and then together we can begin to build the engine that will drive lead generation, sales and growth.
