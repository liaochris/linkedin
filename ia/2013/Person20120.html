Carlos Melero Bascones' Summary

Perfil en español: http://es.linkedin.com/in/carlosmelero/es

Executive Coach. Life coach. Team coach. Trained at the Escuela Europea de Coaching (Madrid, Spain) and OlaCoach International Institute. Certified by the International Coach Federation.

Professional with over 20 years of experience in IT and international consulting. For 13 years he developed his career in Accenture, one of the world’s most demanding consulting firms, working more specifically as a manager in the IT field during the last four years.

He was involved in dozens of extremely varied projects (IT, consulting, strategy, R&D) for both domestic and international renowned companies (Volkswagen, Microsoft, Intel, Pfizer, Banco Santander, Daimler - Mercedes, Vodafone, BBVA, Repsol, Telefónica - Movistar, Spanish Government, Carrefour, AENA, RENFE, Campofrío, ONO, Fremap, Banco de España, amongst others.)

Executive coaching: He knows how customers, external competition and internal competition can challenge professionals torn between willingness and duty in a dehumanized world. He accompanies his clients to develop their potential, helping them analyze their work environment, their aspirations, their skills and motivations.

Team coaching: He makes the most of his experience as a team manager in highly demanding environments, and as a relevant part of management teams. He focuses on the team and its relationships rather than on individuals.

Life coaching: He is a true believer that knowledge is hindered by certainties. He accompanies clients in the process of questioning and rediscovering themselves and their lives, so they find answers, as well as their genuine motivations and values.

Trainer: He taught courses related to leadership, teamwork, team management, time management, motivation, communication, public speaking, sales, integrated project management, interpersonal relations, etc. He is currently designing his own workshops and courses related to coaching and personal development.
