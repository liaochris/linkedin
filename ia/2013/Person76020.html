Kirk Esmond, PCM's Summary

As a Professional Certified Marketer, I recommend solid strategies and execute complex marketing initiatives.

Recognizing small to mid-size companies rarely have someone on staff with the time or expertise to effectively evolve their brand from the inside -- I bridge this gap.

For larger organizations who do have a corporate marketing department, I provide consultation regarding their overall brand strategy and partner to develop specific marketing tactics to connect their message with the intended audience.

While having a focus on healthcare, current and past projects include a variety of industries such as agriculture and wine, construction, tourism, finance, nonprofit, consumer goods, and energy.
