David Dedman, ChFC, AIF®, AAMS, AWMA's Summary

I'm David Dedman, Founder and President of Lexington Wealth Management. I help individuals, business owners and 401(k) plans maximize their wealth.

Investors, business owners and 401(k)s turn to me when:
▪ Frustrated with their current advisor who ignores them
▪ Need advice and education regarding retirement planning and investing
▪ They're striving to achieve a simplified, consolidated picture of their financial future
▪ They've had it with poor investment performance
▪ Confused about fees or fiduciary responsibilities
▪ Under pressure from a life event like marriage, divorce, inheritance, property or business sale

I offer holistic wealth management. My specialties include investment management, financial planning and 401(k) consulting.

My clients achieve financial success and refer me because my approach is very different from other advisors:

1. I use a 4-step process that will ensure your plan is being developed, implemented and monitored
according to legal and ethical obligations.

2. I will review your plan every year, review your investments as often as quarterly, provide education and ongoing monthly contact.

3. I offer my free ebooks, The 7 Biggest 401k Mistakes and The Six Keys to Maximizing Wealth for Small Business Owners, so you can get smart about retirement and investing.

4. I provide a free subscription to my financial blog, The Diligent Advisor, that will educate you on retirement, investing and the economy.

5. I have over 20 years investment experience navigating diverse market and economic conditions. My professional credentials include expertise in financial planning, investment management and fiduciary issues.

David Dedman, ChFC, AIF®, AAMS, AWMA's Experience

President & Founder

Lexington Wealth Management

June 2002 – Present (11 years 5 months) Lexington, Kentucky Area

David A. Dedman is President and Founder of Lexington Wealth Management, Inc. and his practice focuses on wealth management solutions for individuals, business owners and 401(k)s. His services include portfolio investment management, financial planning and retirement plan consulting.

While working for some of the largest brokerage firms, including Ameriprise, A.G. Edwards and Merrill Lynch, David came to recognize the conflicts of interest that exist inside most Wall Street firms. So, in 2002 he founded Lexington Wealth Management to provide proactive, high-touch wealth management and retirement plan consulting. As someone who has built a successful business, David understands the unique needs and concerns of entrepreneurs and those seeking to build wealth toward their unique goals.

David earned his Chartered Financial Consultant (ChFC®) designation from The American College of Financial Services and is also an Accredited Asset Management Specialist (AAMS®) and Accredited Wealth Management Advisor (AWMA®) designee. He also earned the Accredited Investment Fiduciary® professional designation, awarded by the Center for Fiduciary Studies, which is associated with the University of Pittsburgh. He has received formal training in investment fiduciary responsibility. He holds his FINRA Series 7 & Series 65 securities licenses as well as his life, health and variable insurance licenses.

David writes a financial blog, The Diligent Advisor, and is the author of the eBook, "Six Keys to Maximizing Wealth for Small Business Owners".
