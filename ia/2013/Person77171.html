Kristi Siedow-Thompson's Summary

I am a super positive and fun designer looking to be creative in any way possible. I have a ton of corporate design experience that leans to the tech side, but a wide variety of experience in freelance in a lot of other industries.

Layout, UX, and UI are fields that I am quite strong in, as well as project management, mobile, web and print design.

I also have experience in environmental design with creative trade show booths as well as video editing and design.

Specialties: Mobile app design, web design, print design, UI design, Adobe Creative Suite, marketing strategy, copy writing, social media, branding and identity management, vendor correspondence, basic HTML & CSS, project management
