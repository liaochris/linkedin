Izabela Berengut's Summary

My professional skills emerged not only from a strict visual training and background in the fields of art and photography, but also from the constant need for mastery and advancement, which lead to my practical knowledge in art, assets and creative business management. My experience consist of work as an archivist and manager of photo/video and art assets, commercial photo representative & marketing manager, art consultant, photo editor as well as photographer, retoucher/restoration specialist and creative office/studio manager.
