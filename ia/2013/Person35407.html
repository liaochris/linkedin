Diane Crompton's Summary

As a Social Media Strategist, I provide consulting services and leading edge social media solutions to professionals to enable them to keep pace with today’s global marketplace. In my current role, I consult with professionals and teams on how to maintain relevance, engage with customers, build business, find talent, find work and build brand awareness through the use of customized social media tools and products.

My interest in technology tools fueled the co-authoring of two books “Seven Days to Online Networking” (JIST 2008) and “Find a Job through Social Networking” (JIST 2010). These practical “how to” books have been well received, with the most recent book voted as one of the top career books by Quintcareers.com. I also enjoy sharing expertise as a frequent contributor to media on this topic and have spoken to a number of professional and academic groups and organizations.

My background is diverse and includes work as a consultant for a global HR consulting organization, providing customized coaching and expertise on job search, self-marketing and career management to a broad range of professionals. In addition, I’ve worked as a nationwide recruiter (retained and contingency), an educator, and an independent consultant.

Specialties: Social media, developing / managing / optimizing your online profile and reputation, personal and professional branding, business development, social networking, social etiquette, LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook, executive coaching, job search, career management, career transition, resume writing, keyword relevance, group facilitation, web based instruction, one on one coaching, training, public speaking, marketing, sales management, product development, revenue generation, marketing.

Diane Crompton's Publications

  • Find A Job Through Social Networking: Use LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook, Blogs, and More to Advance Your Career

    • JIST Publishing
    • October 2010
    Authors: Diane Crompton

    Find a Job Through Social Networking offers a wealth of advice on how to leverage social networking tools during the career transition process. This practical book provides step by step details on how to begin the process of using social networking tools effectively for job search and for ongoing career management. Important chapters include how to brand yourself professionally, using LinkedIn, Twitter and Facebook as well as detailed chapters on Twitter, blogs and other social networking tools.

  • Seven Days To Online Networking: Make Connections to Advance Your Career and Business Quickly

    • JIST Publishing
    • 2008
    Authors: Diane Crompton

    Seven Days to Online Networking provides tips and tools for how to get started with online networking tools quickly and easily. This easy to use handbook covers a range of information including how to use the internet to find jobs, referrals and clients; guidance on developing your personal brand online; getting the most out of LinkedIn; and tips on using other social networking tools including blogs.
