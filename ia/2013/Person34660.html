Chris Borowiecki's Summary

OBJECTIVE: The goal is to leverage my diverse experience as a professional multi-disciplinary product designer, entrepreneur and leader, in support of an established organization that seeks to continually evolve, improve and excel.

The objective is to bring measurable, significant improvement to business and its goals through strategic problem solving, innovative exploration and inspired, effective user-centered design.


STANCE: The process of design yields results both tactically AND strategically. It can support any vertical. It can strengthen any initiative. The approach and qualities of conscientious design, however, can benefit more than just the end product. Integrated design can work its way outward to create new opportunities and support larger organizational objectives.

Whether it’s physical product, user experience, online presence, brand evolution, corporate communication/storytelling, strategic road-mapping…or any other business activity, strong design collaboration and leadership can net measurable value.

Specialties

design management, industrial design, information architecture, user interface design, user-centered design, branding and identity, online/web presence, corporate communication, business strategy, presentation development
