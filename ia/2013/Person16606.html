Brandy Gill's Summary

I love what I do and need to be surrounded by smart, passionate, likeminded people who are trying to be the best in the world if they aren't already.

As a Creative Director for an advertising agency, I help solve business problems with creativity. But "advertising" no longer adequately describes the industry I work within. The best advertising isn't advertising at all anymore. Sure, the end result of my work may be an ad. But it could just as easily yield a new product. Service. Web app. Invention. Social movement. Or something else altogether. It's an exciting, fantastic time to be in "advertising."

Being a CD, I'm often pulled in a million directions. Which suits my professional ADD and my inherent curiosity about almost everything. It drives me to learn and to create. I’ve invented a few things this year for clients I don’t even have. And a few things for clients I do have. I'm an idea person. But I also enjoy getting my hands dirty. I love that ideas can be expressed in so many different ways. Social, digital, broadcast, print, nontraditional. Whatever is going to be the most effective.

I enjoy inspiring others. And often in turn they inspire me. I am a teacher, mentor, guide, coach. And a student. My team teaches me as much as I teach them. These days with everything changing, evolving at such high velocity, we're all learning from each other.

Specialties: I have an integrated background with comprehensive, broad digital experience. I'm a hands on CD that likes to concept, design and write. I'm an expert with all the usual software plus video and editing because I have an obsessive personality that requires me to know how to do things myself. And I have fun doing it.
