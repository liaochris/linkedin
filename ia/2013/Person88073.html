Mary Ellen Small, was first licensed as a salesperson in 1996 and began marketing and selling real estate full time in Gwinnett County, Georgia and the surrounding counties in 2001. When Keller Williams Realty came to town, she joined them for several years, becoming a top agent as well as serving on the Agent Leadership Council and training other agents within the company.

Solutions Plus Realty, LLC was created in 2010 by Mary Ellen out of a growing need to service the distressed market. In addition to the skills and tools necessary to sell non-distressed properties, such as professional photos, well-written descriptions and the latest technology, Solutions Plus Realty agents have training and experience in short sales and all types of foreclosed properties, including government and bank foreclosures/REO. Experience working with HUD, Fannie Mae, Freddie Mac and many banks, also allows us to provide exceptional buyer representation for investors, first-time and move-up buyers.

Mary Ellen heads the team of highly qualified agents ready to assist you in achieving your real estate goals. Whether you are a distressed or non-distressed homeowner, first time buyer or seasoned investor, we have a solution for you.
