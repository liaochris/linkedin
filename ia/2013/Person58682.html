Founding Director

Data Roads Foundation

Nonprofit; Myself Only; Internet industry

June 2010 – Present (3 years 7 months) Los Angeles, CA

Data Roads Foundation is a sponsored project of the 501(c)(3) non-profit Empowerment WORKS, Inc. Our intent is to develop free and open source tools to build, deploy, and manage Internet "surface street" mesh infrastructure for any purpose, including but not limited to "Information Super Highway" on-ramp access. Our emphasis will be toward supporting local neighborhood 501(c)12 Internet Service Cooperatives in their founding, development, and maintenance.

The tools and methods we develop will be open to use by anyone worldwide without limitation, including for private networks. The resulting networks will be similar to a distributed peer-to-peer version of current core Internet routing, naming, and numbering infrastructure. The primary difference will be lack of need for any centralized management entity like ICANN or IANA. Distributed autonomous Web of Trust (WoT) systems will be used in place of these entities.
