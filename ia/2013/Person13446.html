Bernard Gibbons is a Top Producing Realtor with J Rockcliff Realtors in Danville, CA in the San Francisco East Bay. He entered the real estate profession in 2000 after a previous career in technology related businesses

Bernard makes extensive use of his sales, marketing, relocation and technology experience in his real estate practice and many of the buyers and sellers he helps to achieve their real estate needs contact him as a result of his websites at www.bernardgibbons.com, www.danvillesanramonhomes.com and www.homesforsalesanramon.com.

Bernard considers that moving into the real estate profession is one of the best moves he ever made. It combines his interests in homes and technology and he gets to build satisfying and lasting relationships with buyers and sellers while living in one of the most outstanding parts of the country.


Specialties: Residential Real Estate Specialist (Listing and representing Buyers) in San Ramon, Danville and all of the San Ramon Valley and Lamorinda in the San Francisco East Bay..
