David Lott's Summary

David Lott has been involved with the golf and waterview real estate market since 1985 when he began writing about master-planned communities for Golf Digest Magazine. He launched GolfCourseHome as a special advertising insert in INC. Magazine in 1995 and migrated the concept to the web in 1999 with the launch of GolfCourseHome.net.

GolfCourseHome has since evolved into the GolfCourseHome Network, featuring 44-Member Websites devoted to delivering golf and waterview real estate prospects to its more than 150 golf and waterview master-planned communities. In 2011 The GCH Network launched its 44th Member Site--GolfCourseHome.com.

David Lott's Experience

Owner

Vineyard Open House Real Estate, LLC

August 2013 – Present (3 months) Vineyard Haven, Mass.

Vineyard Open House Real Estate LLC is a new real estate company located in Vineyard Haven on the island of Martha’s Vineyard, MA. Its mission is to provide complete real estate services for buyers, sellers and renters on Martha’s Vineyard and Cape Cod.

Vineyard Open House is an outgrowth of Owner David Lott’s nearly 30 years marketing resort real estate and master-planned golf and water view communities. Lott’s ties to Martha’s Vineyard reach back to the late 1800s when his grandparents first started coming to the island. He built his first house on the island at the age of 22.

In addition, Vineyard Open House will work in conjunction with the GolfCourseHome Network to create a Luxury Resort Referral Network, building on the GCH Network’s nearly 20 years of serving as a consumer resource for master-planned resort communities. Brokers serving resort communities in Massachusetts, Vermont, North Carolina, South Carolina and Florida have already joined the referral network.

Lott monitors the Martha’s Vineyard real estate market via his radio show ‘On the House—Real Estate Radio’ on WVVY 93.7 FM, the island’s community radio station. The show’s creator and host, Lott covers Martha’s Vineyard real estate and the unique lifestyle people enjoy on the island. The show airs Tuesdays and Thursdays from 10 to 11 am. You can also listen to the show online at www.WVVY.org.
