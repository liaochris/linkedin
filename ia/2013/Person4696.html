Alissa Knight's Summary

Alissa Knight has cultivated over thirteen years of leadership experience as a security practitioner in information assurance and cyber operations in government, military, and private sector companies. Her specific technical prowess covers the entire gamut of cyber warfare, incident response, forensics, penetration testing, and security event analysis.

Over the span of her career, Knight’s domain expertise has included forensics and incident response, firewall deployment, architecture, and administration; continuity of operations; certification and accreditation; SCADA security; penetration testing; cyber intelligence and counter-intelligence; cloud security; and SIEM monitoring and analysis in both permissive and semi-permissive environments.

Unlike most senior security engineers, Knight has a finely tuned business acumen as a serial entrepreneur and private equity investor having started and sold 2 startup companies in M&A transactions totaling $8 MM and raising $40 MM in PE funding from Kayne Anderson Capital.

In 2007, Knight was recruited by the U.S. Intelligence Community working for the agency’s counter-terrorism directorate in cyber warfare performing cyber intelligence and counter-intelligence operations while also spending training time in Close Quarter Combat and Hostage Rescue operations.

In the latter half of her career, Knight has been specifically focused on Advanced Persistent Threat (APT) incident investigations and forensics at multinational corporations from biotech to the defense industrial base where she researched and responded to a number of state and non-state sponsored cyber security events and liaised with federal law enforcement agencies and DHS.

In summary Knight has the experience, agility, and “outside the box” mentality to negotiate the rigors of any environment; thriving best in environments where problems are complex, deadlines are impractical, the mission is vital, and ingenuity is required for project success.
