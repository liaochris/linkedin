Brian Hall's Summary

Currently a Senior 3D Artist with IGT. Will be helping the Las Vegas branch move from flash based games to Unity. Also will be helping to create real-time 3D games using the Unity platform. Responsibilities include modeling, texturing, lighting, animation, FX and UI.

Previously worked on contract at Disney Interactive in Salt Lake as a World Builder for marketing on the Disney Infinity project also helping on various art assets and overall polish, optimization passes when time allowed on the various playsets.

Before Disney, I had been an Art Lead for 3 years at Pipeworks Software. As a Lead, I oversaw multiple projects from start to finish.. My drive for quality and excellence are balanced by my broad vision of the scope of a project, the schedule and the client’s and co-worker needs. I have been in charge of writing clear and concise documentation for co-workers and outsourcing vendors which have been pivotal to the success of the projects I have been involved in.

My work with outsourcing vendors included documentation, art style guides and reference material for assets that were done externally. As a Lead I was responsible for the majority of the feedback and back and forth with the vendors when it came to the assets, timeline and budgeting; making sure that each piece of art (from concepts to final asset) met the goals for the project at hand.

When working on art, I have a passion for color and light, how they translates to shape and texture. From traditional mediums to digital, I have a keen eye and attention to detail.

Thank you.
