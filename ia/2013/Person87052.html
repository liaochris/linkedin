Mark Schaub's Summary

I am an enterprising professional specializing in public relations, marketing and new media in Orlando, Fla.

As public relations and marketing coordinator at Orlando Science Center, I secure media placements in television, radio, print and online. I also oversee content creation across all of the Science Center's social media profiles as well as the organization's website, and I'm co-editor of the triannual membership magazine.

Additionally, I serve as an official spokesman and personally escort hosted celebrities during media blitzes and events, which typically include television and radio interviews, question-and-answer panels, autograph sessions, and photograph opportunities.

I have worked closely and continue to collaborate with global brands and entities including National Geographic; Walt Disney Parks and Resorts; Lockheed Martin; Lucasfilm Ltd.; NASA; Universal Orlando Resort; Blue Man Group; EA SPORTS; and the U.S. Department of Defense.

Leading up to my current role, I have managed the day-to-day operations of a student-run Internet radio station, reported news stories for a newspaper with a 50,000+ circulation and hosted my own weekly radio program for four years.

To learn more about my qualifications, I encourage you to visit www.MarkSchaub.com.
