Karla Dennis' Summary

With more than 20 years of industry experience, entrepreneur, women's business leader, and high profile public speaker, Karla Dennis is the Tax Strategist to get when you need a tax plan.. An Enrolled Agent, Karla holds a goal to educate the masses on making sound financial decisions. In addition to helping corporate and private clients greatly reduce their tax bill, Karla consults on a wide spectrum of benefits involving real estate ownership and acquisition, finances, taxes and more. She contributes to several industry publications, including Money and Consumer Reports. Karla is an accomplished public speaker, transforming the traditionally dry and fearful subject of taxes into informative and entertaining language.
