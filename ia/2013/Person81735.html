Lisa Wagman, ADR, CP, NJCP's Summary

Social Security Disability Benefit Consultants (ssdbc), a division of Premier Legal Services, LLC, provides specialized services locally, regionally and nationally to individuals who are applying for Social Security disability. Whether someone is about to file a claim, is in the process of filing, or has previously filed for Social Security disability and received a denial, we can help them win their claim!

Our President, Lisa S. Wagman, ADR, CP, NJCP, is a fully Accredited Disability Representative. This accreditation was attained only after having first met the stringent criteria to participate in the Social Security Disability Applicants' Access to Professional Representation Act of 2010 (PRA).

ssdbc was formed in January, 2004 at which time Ms. Wagman began actively, aggressively and successfully representing disability clients.

Ms. Wagman has been a practicing paralegal since 1980 and has extensive litigation expertise in the areas of product liability, medical negligence, personal injury, class actions, mass torts and wrongful death, having represented both plaintiffs and defendants for approximately 30 years.

Ms. Wagman is very passionate about the paralegal profession, has been actively involved in the education and advancement of paralegals in New Jersey, lectured for American Trial Lawyers Association of New Jersey, Institute for Continuing Legal Education, South Jersey Paralegal Association, Garden State Paralegal Convention and numerous colleges throughout the tri-state area.

While Ms. Wagman specializes in disability claims for orthopedic, psychological, cardiovascular, neurologic, oncologic and respiratory conditions, she offers efficient and thorough claims processing for all impairments. She understands the overwhelming nature of the Social Security claims process and provides support and guidance to claimants to help them achieve the quickest award possible with the least amount of stress along the way.

Specialties

Ms. Wagman has authored many articles, sat on several college advisory boards for paralegal/nurse paralegal programs and has created and co-chaired several seminars and conventions. She has lectured to paralegals/nurse paralegals on the subject of medical record analysis, personal injury litigation and disability law. She is past-president of the South Jersey Paralegal Association.
