Elizabeth Crowe's Summary

I have been writing for 30 years and pursuing genealogy since since my mother began researching her family history in 1967. I am the author of Genealogy Online, 8th Edition. As a freelance writer, I have written for national magazines and websites, as well as many local organizations and publications. I am known as Libbi to my friends, and I live and work in Navarre, Florida.

I am, at heart, a story teller. If you have a story, I can help you tell it!

Specialties

Business and general interest writing. Marketing and public relations writing. Feature stories. Ghost writing.
