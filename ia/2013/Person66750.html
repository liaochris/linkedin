John M Olson, CLTC's Summary

A FULL SERVICE WEALTH MANAGEMENT FIRM that best serves high net worth families and small business owners with unique estate and financial planning needs. I lead my team of experienced Certified Financial Planners, Estate Planning Attorneys, and Certified Public Accountants to develop a life stage based plan to meet my clientele’s goals. I strongly believe in the financial planning process and see my advisory based relationship oriented business model to be paramount in our future successes. Let no leaf unturned and no goals success to chance.

My CLIENT SERVICE MODEL incorporates at least a quarterly phone call, a bimonthly newsletter to keep you best informed, monthly emails on timely topics, and a semiannual review of your goals and our progress in achieving them. I set these as minimum expectations and will go above and beyond the call of duty if you feel you need more one on one time. Your expectations become my top priorities.

NEW CLIENTS WILL ONLY BE ACCEPTED BY REFERRAL. I maintain this policy because it helps me to best serve my current client’s interests and I am interested in growing my practice in a client friendly manner. This policy also helps me to replicate my current clients whom I already have a great working relationship with.

Please feel free to visit my website if you are looking for the most up to date market commentary and other highly useful resources.

Specialties: Financial and Estate Planning, Retirement Income Planning, Needs Based Analysis, Tax Free and Taxable Cash Flow, Life Insurance Reviews, Fee Based Accounts: Separately Managed Accounts, Mutual Fund Wrap Fee, and All Inclusive Self Managed Accounts.
