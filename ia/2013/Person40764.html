Elizabeth Yarnell, ND, CLT, MLS's Summary

I'm an award-winning author, speaker, publicity coach, blogger, patented inventor, and independent publisher with a robust following of fans for a unique, patented method of cooking.

As a Naturopath (ND), Certified Nutritional Consultant (CNC), Certified LEAP Therapist (CLT), and a Certified Natural Health Professional (CNHP), I focus on dietary management and holistic health, including identification of hidden food sensitivities that cause chronic physical distress. My virtual clinical practice is open to everyone, regardless of location.

Beyond health and nutrition, I bring almost two decades of experience as an Online Communication Designer, Multimedia Instructional Designer, and Technical Writer, I have a firm grasp on social media and Web 3.0 tools, publishing, internet marketing, and publicity.

I am available for speaking engagements in Colorado and around the country. Bring me in to your group, workplace, or conference for a fun and entertaining event and walk away with sound advice, concrete tools to use, and a new relationship to food or social media.

Specialties

alternative medicine, chronic health issues, food sensitivities / allergies, healthy eating and cooking, multiple sclerosis, natural living, professional speaking, social media and online communication, publicity for authors and entrepreneurs, and independent publishing
