Marilyn Karpoff's Summary

Karpoff Affiliates, Inc. / Moving On NYC is the only NYC full-service Real Estate Company that specializes in Senior Transitions. My business is focused on managing the sale, downsizing and emptying of a residence while providing Senior Transitions Solutions.

Call us if you are faced with selling the parental home or are a senior who is transitioning. We can assist you with your Estate and Financial Planning needs and arrange for appraisals, donations and tax-deductible charitable contributions.

I have a 92 year old mother and I know first hand how difficult it is to manage my career and family along with her needs. And that’s why Moving On NYC is the company to turn to when families are in transition.

More importantly, I treat the senior in your family, as if she is my own parent, with honor and dignity and respect. Honor thy parents. That is the ethos of Moving On NYC.

Karpoff Affiliates, Inc. / Moving On NYC: We have the compassion, sensitivity, and respect to smoothly facilitate the physical and emotional aspects of relocation for older adults.
