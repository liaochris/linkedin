Available For New Assignment(s)

My work as a freelance business improvement specialist, interim executive, executive coach and mentor revolves around identifying and removing under-performance and energetic blocks within a businesses operating dynamics and I do that in my own distinctive fashion.

Some by energy clearing and balancing, some by business mentoring, some by system and process engineering and re-engineering where needed. All by identifying and eradicating negative, pointless or ineffective business logic.

Therein and when tasked to either steady the ship, change course or turn a failing business or project around, I drawn upon my 35+ years experience, establish the root cause of the problem, develop and sell the vision and empower my clients to truly empower themselves and their business.

All that being said and when I'm not engaged in providing the former, I also provide fast track specialist recruitment services as well as authoring solutions and strategic support in a range of computerised maintenance, facilities and asset management tools.

Barry Hardy's Additional Information

Honors and Awards:

During period 1997 - 2000 I surveyed the entire facilities of the UK's first commercial nuclear reactor decommissioning programme i.e. "WAGR" for my client Magnox Generation. Where I developed and implemented the programmes asset management and system data structures including a CMMS - securing approval at first submittal from the Nuclear Inspectorate of Industry NII to proceed ahead.

In July 2000 I became the first full time UK post graduate to be awarded a Master of Science degree in maintenance engineering and asset management from the University Of Manchester, supporting the programme thereafter with the SEM prize.

In November 2008 I was appointed the youngest director of the UK Society of Professional Engineers.

After a long battle with illness I converted all my learning from that arduous process into a series of self help books and currently have 17 health related self help books published.
