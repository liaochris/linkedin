Bob Langer's Summary

Jesus has given me a new direction for this ministry. We are now totally dependent on Him and through Him you, our users. I have been praying for some time for Jesus to show me the way He wanted me to go with this software.

Ten years ago in July 2002, I needed a means of support and He saw that I had one. I was not sure what he wanted me to do in the future, and I did know I was not a good business man.

I did make decisions based upon what I felt God wanted me to do. In October of 2008, when the economy crashed, I cut prices by half. That created a business problem. Our prices were already much lower than comparable software titles. Now they were half of that. That meant I had to sell twice the number of units. It did not happen. Another poor business decision was to decrease advertising. That did not work well either.

I only wanted to do what I believed Jesus wanted me to do, to minister to His servants by designing and developing software to help churches win souls to Jesus.

So, we are now making all of our software titles available for free. We will still offer support via email and we will upgrade each when it is appropriate. By January 1, 2013, all the programs will be available and our web site will be changed to reflect Gods new direction for this Christian Software Ministry.

I have funded the ministry with enough money to last through December 2013. Jesus has assured me the individuals and churches that use any one of our titles will understand the laborer is worthy of his hire, Luke 10:7. It will be their responsibility to donate in order to help continue past 2013.

Thank you for your support in the past and in the future.
