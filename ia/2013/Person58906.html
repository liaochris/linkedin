Jarrod Robinson (LION)'s Summary

My goal is to be recognised as the best mortgage broker in the Harrogate District. I can help with any aspect of buying, selling or remortgaging your home or investment property, including overseas.

I can arrange the mortgage and any related insurances and even the conveyancing. I can also refer you to a trusted specialist for advice on pensions and investments, IHT planning and making a will and any commercial mortgage or insurance issues.

If I cant personally do it, I will do my very best to find a professional that can. My service is reliable, efficient and I pride myself in achieving results to deadlines.

Specialties

Remortgages, Home Mover mortgages, Buy to Let mortgages, individual and portfolio clients. All types of life assurance, critical illness cover, income protection cover, mortgage payment protection cover, buildings and contents insurance. Conveyancing and HIPS. Secured Loans. Commercial finance and commercial general insurance. Overseas property sales and overseas mortgage finance.
