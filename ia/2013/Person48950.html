Gwenaël Le Bodic's Summary

I am the founder and CEO of Greencopper. Greencopper offers a market-leading software solution for event organizers.

In the past, I worked for several telecommunications companies and was in charge of building large-scale telecom-grade solutions in the domain of mobile messaging. I authored several reference books as an outcome of these activities.

I occasionally act as expert witness in the domain of mobile communications.

Gwenaël Le Bodic's Experience

Founder and CEO

Greencopper

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Internet industry

2009 – Present (4 years) Montreal, Canada

Greencopper provides innovative technology solutions to event organizers. These solutions range from cutting-edge mobile apps to web and social media engines. Greencopper has helped over 120 events to adopt the right technologies with the objective of boosting community engagement and building insights into community dynamics.

Greencopper’s customer references in the live music industry include the Montreal International Jazz Festival (Canada), Vieilles Charrues (France), Eurockéennes (France), the Reeperbahn Festival (Germany) and the Copenhagen Jazz Festival (Denmark).

Greencopper is headquartered in Montreal (Canada) with additional local presence in France, Denmark, Germany and the United States.

Senior Product Manager

Airwide Solutions

Privately Held; 501-1000 employees; Wireless industry

September 2007 – April 2010 (2 years 8 months)

Product management: definition of service propositions, business cases, price books, requirement definition, R&D coordination, management of partner relationships, support to sales and operations teams. Responsible for several telco-grade messaging products including MMS centre, MMS bulk/push engine, SMS/MMS gateways and LDAP repositories.

Definition of strategy towards future messaging services and technologies including converged messaging, presence services, mobile advertizing and mobile broadband. Evaluation of innovative models for delivering services in the converged world of Internet and mobile communications.

Airwide's lead for standardization activities at OMA, 3GPP/ETSI and GSMA. Focus on Converged-IP Messaging (CPM), Converged Address Book (CAB), Mobile Advertising (MobAd) and Rich Communications Suite (RCS).
