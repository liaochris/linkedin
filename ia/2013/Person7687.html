Angela Edwards' Summary

WHAT I DO
I help high performance managers and executives achieve results that matter to them.

WHO I WORK WITH
My clients are high performance managers and executives. I work with clients from all over the world, via Skype, or locally in Central Virginia. Find out more: http://www.castlethunder.com.

SERVICES
As a Executive Coach, I help organizations define and achieve their plans for creating and sustaining a high performance culture by focusing on individualized achievement and success within their organization by:
- Recognizing individual strengths and preferences;
- Maximizing the potential in each situation;
- Building successful relationships in the organization;
- Strengthening relationships without sacrificing results or avoiding tough conversations;
- Influencing others to find mutually satisfactory solutions;
- Understanding how to delegate to the appropriate individuals to maximize everyone's productivity; and
- Developing strategic and tactical action plans to address both long-term and short-term needs.

I am the exclusive provider of the performance based ELEVATE Your Leaders program in the Richmond, Virginia area: http://www.elevateyourleaders.com.

SPECIALTIES
Behavioral & Perceptual Coaching, Leadership Identification & Development, Communication, Team Building, Project Management, and Strategic Planning.

  • Masters of Marketing

    • August 2013 to Present
