Karen Fabian's Summary

I have over 25 years of business experience in the health and wellness industry. My background is a mix of the traditional, with a Master's in Health Care Administration and extensive corporate experience in the healthcare and software industries and the more entrepreneurial, having worked full-time as a yoga teacher. I also owned a personal chef business and cooked for some notable Bostonians.

My passion is for health and wellness. I have a unique blend of both traditional business skills and hands-on experience in yoga teaching and health and wellness services.

I left my last corporate position on Sept 30, 2010. I am excited to be working for myself, creating my own wellness brand, teaching yoga and also developing other opportunities to share yoga and wellness related services to adults and children. My motto is "Keeping Yoga Simple," which reflects my passion for providing the essential poses to increase strength, flexibility and ability to relax. I also specialize in bringing yoga outside the studio into other environments; I have programs at Community Rowing, where I have specialty classes for rowers, the Boys and Girls Club, several Partners healthcare facilities (Mass General, Brigham and Women's Hospital) where I teach prenatal and children's yoga and run corporate classes. I also provide wellness presentations to employers that focus on easy poses anyone can do to stretch, meditation and stress reduction techniques.

Past corporate presentations have been with the following clients: Staples, Children's Hospital, ZipCar, Boston Organics.

I also have a DVD, sold on Amazon.

See my website, www.barebonesyoga.com for my schedule.
