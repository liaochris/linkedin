Kimmel Arn's Summary

With over twenty five years of sales, marketing and management experience, I bring an extensive array of experience to Arn Associates, and our divisions, Arn Healthcare and Thomas Arn Solutions. Along with twelve years of experience placing mid and senior level sales executives and managers, I have extensive experience in the manufacturing sector working closely with several manufacturing and production companies while guiding Ohio, Kentucky, Indiana and Michigan as a Regional Director for national and global employment services companies. In addition, I have several years of experience in the staffing and recruiting, advertising, payment processing/e-commerce, and healthcare industries.

My greatest success in my professional career is in building strong and long-lasting relationships with both clients and candidates while delivering excellent service and substantial value. My personal values of honesty, trust and integrity while always striving to do what's right for all of our firm's clients and candidates define the true experience of working with our firm.
