Andrew Outhwaite's Summary

Social Entrepreneur, Community Animateur and Sustainability Adviser.

Creating enabling conditions for social transformation, community-scale collaboration and organsiational innovation towards (and beyond) sustainability.

Some highlights of recent work include:

With Pollinators Inc:
* Founded and lead Pollinators Inc — Australia's first member-based organisations supporting social entrepreneurs,
* Opened and managed CityHive — Australia's first regional coworking and innovation space,
* Designed and facilitated Catalyst — WA's only leadership program for social entrepreneurs
* Designing and delivering training on business model innovation, social enterprise, graphic recording, community development and time management,

With We Are Arising:
* Facilitating Durack Institute of Technology's "Stepping Up Sustainability" strategy and leadership project,
* Leading and facilitating strategic planning for social enterprises such as Leaning Tree Community School, Dismantle and Geraldton-Greenough Farmer's Market, Drylands Permaculture Farm,
* Consulting on statutory and strategic sustainability projects affecting biodiversity, land use planning, multi-user land access conflicts, and local laws

With City of Greater Geraldton:
* Leading on strategy development and community engagement on major initiatives such as Water Sensitive City Strategy, Million Trees, Build It Better and the UN-award winning "2029 and Beyond" initiative,
* Facilitating professional and community development initiatives such as "Sustainability Champions", 'LivingSmart" program,
* Facilitating consensus solutions to major community conflicts, such as vehicle access to beaches.

With various organisations in the UK:
* Design and facilitation of 'Spark Challenge' to solve homelessness through social enterprise,
* Research and publications on the role of coaching and organisational sustainability,
* Sustainability-related art installations, small business expos, presentations and training.
