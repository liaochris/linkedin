Bob Moorman's Summary

Bob Moorman, CRB Summary

Bob Moorman is a 30 year veteran of the Metropolitan DC area real estate industry and has enjoyed helping hundreds of people achieve their real estate goals during that time. Prior to joining Long and Foster, Bob worked for W.C. & A.N. Miller Development Company, A well know prestigious land development and Real Estate company joining in 1976. His profile included 10 years of New Construction experience as Senior Project Manager for Potomac Falls, Overlook, Spring Ridge and the Sumner Communities and 20 years of General Real Estate Brokerage with W.C. & A.N. Miller. Bob opened Real estate offices in Potomac, Darnestown and North Potomac Maryland for W.C & A.N. Miller Development Company. Bob managed these offices through 2007 until W.C. & A.N. Miller was acquired by Long and Foster Real Estate in February of 2007. Bob managed both the Potomac and Spring Valley L&F offices through 2008 and continued managing Spring Valley through 2010.

Bob is a licensed Real Estate Associate Broker in MD, Va, and DC.

Bob is President and CEO of Creative Network Design founded in 1995. CND1.COM is a Web Hosting Company “ISP” that was created primarily for Real Estate agents and has continued to grow into the commercial applications, servicing companies in the Metro area; examples are Foday Company, Teckvission21, Goldin and Stafford, and W.C. & A.N. Miller with many Real Estate Associates and Companies through the States. CND specialties are Database application. Bob is a certified Microsoft engineer.

Bob is a Past President of the Potomac Chamber of commerce serving for 10 years and was honored in 2006 as Potomac Business man of the year.

Bob is a active general aviation pilot since 1986 and enjoys flying often.

Bob grew up in Washington DC. He is a graduate of University of Maryland and currently lives in North Potomac Maryland. Bob has four Sons Tim, Robert, Michael and Steven.
