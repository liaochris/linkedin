Jesse Gilbride's Summary

UI development is a real joy for me. I am regularly imbued with inspiration by the amazing things that can be done with front-end technologies to create useful and beautiful interfaces, taking designs and requirements, and turning them into reality. I strongly believe in web standards and progressive enhancement to help with maintainability and refactoring.

I am a highly capable and fast-learning individual with a propensity for positivity. In any endeavor, I look for the best ways to learn as much as necessary and produce quality code. Lately I've been engaged with Twitter Bootstrap, jQuery and complex CSS layouts. My JavaScript experience is on the junior side -- improving all the time -- and I am seeking opportunities to shine in creating and maintaining robust, cross-browser/device interfaces while growing in this exciting field.
