Jerry Shea, a native Californian, brings over 25 years of real estate expertise to his clients. Prior to switching his primary focus to real estate sales, he held Senior Executive positions in the investment banking, technology and management consulting industries throughout the United States and 16 other countries.

The businesses he led while living and working around the world have provided him with an extensive international focus, an expertise he leverages with clients when marketing distinctive properties at every price.

Jerry's business is largely referral based. His clients recognize and value his dedication, honesty, exceptional personal service and commitment to their best interests. He thrives at exceeding his buyers' and sellers' needs and sharing his knowledge of successful investment strategies. He holds a Bachelors of Science Degree and an MBA from the University of Southern California.
