Linda McGrath-Cruz, ACP FRP's Summary

My name is Linda McGrath-Cruz and I am a litigation paralegal from Miami, Florida.

The paralegal profession is not a stepping stone to becoming an attorney. I am a huge proponent in continuing education and certification/registration for paralegals... I have compiled a list of your options for paralegal or e-discovery certification, please visit my website: http://miamifrp.com/paralegal-e-discovery-certification/

I have been a paralegal since 1997. I am employed by Arnstein & Lehr, LLP as a litigation paralegal. I love defense litigation and have been involved in a wide variety of case types, my main area of practice is products liability/personal injury defense. You can learn more about my work experience in the "Experience" section of my profile.

I run a website for South Florida legal staffers called Linda's Paralegal Resources. Don't let the name fool you. It began as a paralegal resource but is now used by everyone from file clerks to senior trial paralegals and everyone in between. www.miamifrp.com

I have written numerous articles on a variety of topics including Cloud Computing, UPL, Membership in Bar Associations, Certification, Trial Binders, Discovery Primers and more. My articles have been included in publications by the Miami-Dade and Orange County Bar Associations, Know Magazine, and several paralegal associations. You can read my articles in the "Publications" section of my profile.

In addition to the articles I have written, I have been interviewed or quoted for articles in the Daily Business Review, CBS Money Watch, Woman's Day, Natural Awakenings Magazine and The Grindstone.

Feel free to send me a connection request (lmc@miamifrp.com) or drop me an e-mail.
