  • Info for Entrepreneurs

    • LCSBE.com
    • March 6, 2012
    Authors: Amanda Mineer

    Spent some time during my Guru hours at Hera Hub talking with some new business owners about establishing their businesses in another state. Let’s face it, California is not exactly the most friendly state to small businesses. Our taxes are high, lots of regulations etc. I often get asked about setting up businesses in other states (usually Nevada or Delaware). While other states may be more friendly to small business owners by lower or no taxes, more protection for shareholders, etc., if you are doing business in the state of California, you are required to register in California even if you are established in another state.
