Dan Rego's Summary

Dan Rego is a strategist in the nexus of Innovation, Technology and Management.

Recognized as a thought leader and a restless innovator, Dan is a successful serial entrepreneur, having built innovative businesses from the ground up. He has also advised some of the world's leading organizations on highly complex technological and strategic issues.

Dan is currently a Project Manager at the Dept. of Education (DC), but, foremost, Dan has a strong sense of purpose. What really keeps him up at night is how to leverage innovation and technology to solve pressing world matters. Dan's ideas and actions on revolutionizing education and the workforce has awarded him several accolades.

Dan is the founder & president of Libertas, a non-profit, and the world's most innovative educational institution since the 19th century. He holds a revolutionary pending patent on educational technology that may disrupt the way that the current higher education system functions.

He is also the co-founder & CTO at the Public Initiative Institute, a non-profit focused on improving governance, transparency and cross-collaboration in government. Dan is also a board member and seed investor in innovative technological startups in the United States and Brazil.

He has appeared in various media outlets and has been invited to speak in several international events, covering eclectic topics such as e-Learning, Higher Education, Big Data, Agile Project Management and even the Wall Street reform. Dan has also been a guest lecturer at MBA classes in the United States and Brazil.

Some of his clients include Morgan Stanley, Citigroup Smith Barney, International Monetary Fund (IMF), Organization of American States (OAS) and others.

Keywords: Education; Innovation; Strategy; Social Entrepreneurship; Management Consulting; Technology Management; Agile Project Management; Strategic Foresight
