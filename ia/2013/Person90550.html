Mark Sutton's Summary

With over 20 years of design experience, Mark Sutton has worked on a wide variety of projects, ranging from computer-based training to photo retouching, print and web design. For the last 10 years, Mark's focus has been primarily web design and user experience (HCI). An Adobe Certified Expert, Mark has done everything from wireframes to hi-fidelity site design, to coding HTML/CSS and integrating JS plugins. This unique skillset, covering the gamut of front-end design and development, has given Mark a unique perspective on how to create great user-centered design. Mark is not afraid of taking the reigns of UX on a project and has a successful history of delivering results that delight clients and serve their users well.

Specialties: Technical: User-interface planning/design (11 yrs), Web design (9 yrs), Usability & HFE (8 yrs), Print design (15 years),
Multimedia (12 yrs), Image editing (17 yrs), CSS (6 yrs).

Software: Adobe Photoshop v.1-CS4 (Certified Expert), Dreamweaver MX-CS4, Illustrator v.3-CS4, Fireworks MX-CS4, InDesign CS-CS4, Flash MX-CS4, After Effects and Director; Microsoft Office Suite, Project and Visio.
