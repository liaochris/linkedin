Kathy Caprino's Summary

Kathy Caprino, M.A. is a nationally-recognized women's success coach, leadership and career developer, writer and speaker dedicated to the advancement of women in business. Kathy's new career program - The Amazing Career Project (www.amazingcareerproject.com) helps women ages 30-55 build rewarding, exciting and successful careers of significance.

Founder/President of Ellia Communications (www.elliacommunications.com) -- a women's career coaching and success training firm -- Kathy is a former corporate VP, trained psychotherapist, seasoned coach, and sought-after speaker. A Forbes, Huffington Post and AARP Work Reimagined contributor and popular blogger on career issues and trends, she is a top media source on career issues, appearing in over 100 leading newspapers and magazines (including Wall Street Journal, USA Today, Chicago Tribune, Business Week, Forbes, More Magazine, SELF, etc.) and on national television.

Ellia Communications offers career growth, business success and leadership training programs, seminars and resources designed to help women break through to the next level of success, happiness, and reward. Visit www.elliacommunications.com and www.amazingcareerproject.com for more.

Kathy's book Breakdown Breakthrough, based on her yearlong national research study, explores the top 12 challenges working women face today, and offers a 3-step holistic model for overcoming these challenges.

Prior to forming Ellia Communications, Kathy built a successful 18-year corporate marketing career in the publishing, marketing and membership services industries.

Specialties:
- Leadership, Business and Career Success coaching and training
- Career Success Assessment and Consulting
- Entrepreneurial consulting
- One-on-one and group career coaching
- Career reinvention support
- Resources for professional women, including the Amazing Career Project, Career Path Assessment and online training programs

Kathy Caprino's Publications

  • Breakdown, Breakthrough

    • Berrett-Koehler Publishers
    • October 1, 2008
    Authors: Kathy Caprino

    Thousands of professional women ages 35-50, though outwardly "successful," find themselves in the midst of a crisis, believing they've sacrificed meaning, fulfillment, balance, and joy to achieve career success. Breakdown, Breakthrough explores the 12 "hidden" crises working women face, and presents 14 compelling stories of career transformation, showing women how to restore their passion, power, and purpose and reconnect with their compelling life visions.

    The book reveals the key findings from Caprino's yearlong national research study—identifying the 12 “hidden” crises of professional women, including chronic health problems, failure to find work-life balance, and painful losses of integrity and the “real me.” It also shares 14 deeply personal stories—her own included—and shows how women are overcoming crises of personal and professional identity. Additionally, the book introduces a hands-on holistic model for breaking through: powerful and achievable solutions, advice and tips.
