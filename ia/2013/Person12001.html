Bart Claeys' Experience

Creative Director

Ratio Interactive

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Internet industry

March 2010 – Present (3 years 5 months) Pioneer Square, Seattle

Started out as freelance User Interface Designer at Ratio Interactive back in 2010 mainly working on iOS projects for iCookbook, RealNetworks, RealEstate.com, PlayNetwork, etc. As the company grew I took a lead position and helped Ratio grow it's interactive department by hiring a team of user experience and visual designers in junior, mid-level and senior roles. In 2011 I was promoted to Creative Director and continued pushing the envelope in terms of design and user experience for several top-tier brands like NASA, Intel, Nasdaq, etc. At the end of 2011 I was invited by Microsoft to design a Windows Store counterpart of iCookbook. iCookbook was showcased on screen at the Mobile World Congress in Barcelona in 2012 when Microsoft released its consumer preview of Windows 8. After that event I've been highly involved in design around Windows 8. I wrote an iPad to Windows 8 Case Study published on Microsoft.com and helped them shape their online documentation while evangelizing the Windows 8 platform through several talks for Microsoft Belgium (Brussels), Microsoft Research Center in Palo Alto etc. The goal for 2013 is to attract more Android and Windows Phone business while continuing our leadership in the Windows 8 design field.

Founder

Creativeskills.be (Self-employed)

Public Company; 1-10 employees; Staffing and Recruiting industry

December 2005 – Present (7 years 8 months) Antwerp, Belgium

After graduating in 2003 I noticed a need to connect businesses with creative talent. Leveraging my knowledge in web design and development I founded Creativeskills.be in 2005. As a founder of this platform I received the award "You are Flanders Future" in 2006 from Flanders District of Creativity, a government organization to stimulate creativity and entrepreneurship. Today Creativeskills.be is the leading job board / portfolio website for creative talent in Flanders, Belgium. More than 12.000 people are subscribed and more than 1.800 vacancies have been published by more than 1.000 companies. Creativeskills.be actively uses social media to communicate with its target audience and hired a community manager in 2011 to fulfill this job. Besides vacancies and portfolio services Creativeskills.be developed a content strategy and attracted a dozen of authors to write for Creativeskills.be on a weekly basis. Creativeskills.be continues to grow and is a profitable business.

Bart Claeys' Projects

  • Microsoft Windows 8: Concept Ideabooks

    • June 2012 to December 2012
    Team Members: Bart Claeys, Shelby Blair, Rachael C. Rinehart, Cooper Crosby, Aaron Johnson, Russ Whitman, Tyler Eide

    Opportunity
    Take our passion for great design and UX and apply it to a completely new platform – Windows 8. Last fall we were approached by Microsoft to learn about Windows 8 and the new app ecosystem that is the heart of the platform. Our Design and Engineering teams dove into the platform and came away impressed, immediately ideating on Metro based design and building prototype applications on the early developer preview version of Windows 8.

    Solution
    We were selected by Microsoft as a Windows 8 Agency Partner giving us access to its UX and Engineering teams to advance our knowledge and understanding of the new platform. Ratio was asked to create a series of Design experiences for key market categories: Photos, Lifestyle, Sports, Books and Reference, Shopping, Travel, Education and more. Our Creative UX Team explored, ideated and pushed the envelope for experiences while supporting the Metro language guidelines.

    Results
    Ratio is a now a leading App Design, Development, and Strategy provider for the new Windows 8 Platform. Microsoft has selected us to work with early app publishers to create Windows 8 Apps with a number of companies including: Meredith, Next Issue Media, Naturespace, and more. We are really excited about the possibilities with this new App ecosystem, stay tuned as we begin to start rolling out these projects.
