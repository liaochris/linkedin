Mike Deibler's Summary

I start my career in the fitness industry in 2003 while in school for my Master's Degree in Exercise Science. Since then I have worked at various health clubs and studios until recently opening up my own Private Personal Training Studio in La Costa. I have helped hundreds of individuals reach their fitness goals. Whether they were trying lose 5lbs, 50lbs, or just trying to live longer I have created a customized program to get them there safely and as fast as possible. I am currently working on a weight loss book and creating fitness workout websites.

For more information or to receive weekly fitness tips check out my website www.sandiegopremiertraining.com
