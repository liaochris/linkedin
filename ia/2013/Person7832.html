Angelique Jurd's Summary

Angelique Jurd is the Planned Giving Coordinator for the Cancer Society Auckland Division.

A successful freelance writer and blogger specialising in women in business and technology, Angelique wanted to look to something new that would add value to the community and allow her to learn new skills. Although Angelique is not new to volunteering, the new role means learning a lot about fundraising and, more specifically, bequests and legacy giving from the inside.

In her spare time, although most of her writing is now focused on her fiction work, Angelique writes and speaks about writing, women in business and from time to time some more esoteric things like Springsteen, Buddhism, and cats. Of course fundraising is now beginning to feature in her writing. She works across all media but has a special love for the digital landscape, and especially social media.

Angelique has interviewed a wide variety of people, including: Dr Peter Snell, Pete Bethune, Sarah Trotman, Rt Hon John Key, Erica James, Andrew Ferrier, Sonia Choquette, Kim Rosen, and Michael Gerber.

Very comfortable with technology, Angelique loves using social media tools to inform, share, and enhance the value of any online communication.

Originally from the Bay of Plenty (on the East Coast of New Zealand), she has lived all over the world but is now based with her family in Auckland, New Zealand. She has a global network of contacts and is fluent in both spoken and written French, and has a basic understanding of Portuguese and Italian.

Angelique has held several editorial roles with a range of different publications and has written for several well known New Zealand publications, including Straight Furrow, Her Business, Business to Business and IT Brief.

In 2011, Angelique self published her first non-fiction book, is currently finishing her second and working on her first novel.


Specialties: Writing, editing, reviewing, business, techonology, lifestyle, travel, social media, speaking
