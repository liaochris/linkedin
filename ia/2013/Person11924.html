Barry Greene's Summary

I am an experienced, professional business and systems consultant, focusing on small to medium size businesses.

Many of my clients come to me, because they are…
•Worried about the safety and security of their data from virus attack or physical loss.
•Afraid their computers are just not providing the benefit they should.
•Terrified their employees may not be practicing safe computing.
•Unsure about how, when and where their data is backed up and how to get it back if needed.

I work with many vendors to provide the right solution for each individual situation, this includes both cloud and on premise options.

Services are provided on-site, on the phone, or via remote capabilities, so help is never far away.

You probably already possess the proper equipment to get the job done, but do you really want to just get the job done? Or would you rather use that equipment and software to the fullest extent to increase your effectiveness and efficiency.

Your customers expect excellence from you and you surely expect it from your employees; why not ensure you have the technology tools needed to deliver it?

Through strong ongoing relationships with my clients, your knowledge of the specific business combined with my expertise in computers and business technologies we can further advance and improve your current technological capabilities.

I can truly help you or your clients Provide Excellence through Technology.

Specialties

Clients use my services for varying projects including but not limited to:
•Implementation of current technologies to acheive business goals.
•Troubleshoot hardware and software issues quickly and efficiently, from their site or mine.
•Manage completion of existing projects, whether started by employees or other consultants, building on earlier successes, rather than starting from scratch.
•Facilitate orderly system relocation for individual employee or entire company moves.
