Joe Teske's Summary

I started my education and career with the same two passions - a real interest in helping people and a desire to fix things. My engineering and business degrees gave me the foundation needed to apply these passions to work life. I started out in the aerospace industry. Through successive job promotions and increased responsibilities, as an executive I spent several years leading technical and business elements of rocket launch operations at Boeing/United Launch Alliance.

Rocket launching is as exciting a job as one can get yet I still held the desire to truly help people directly. I realized that my experience in financial analysis and improving business operations could be provided on an independent basis as a financial advisor. With over 20 years of real-time experience in personal wealth management I explored an independent career.

Teske Financial Services (TFS) was created, along with my two brothers - both with 20+ years of financial experience - to provide clients with the following:
a) A complete personal/business financial analysis
b) Proven wealth management strategies to put your money to work producing more benefits, less risk and securing a more stable future
c) Extensive research that enables clients to understand the truth about finances and financial decisions
d) An advocate and a partner - someone you can count on to give you honest financial advice over your lifetime

TFS has continued to develop a large client base serving the wealth management needs of clients in California and Wisconsin. The unique approach to financial analysis and advice has attracted individuals, couples, businesses and families as they seek to find peace of mind with each financial decision.

Our passion for helping people and fixing things enables our clients to sort through the current maze of financial decisions with independent research and financial intelligence.TFS offers a unique, refreshing and informative opportunity to secure your future. Contact us to grow.
