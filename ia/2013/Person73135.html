Katie Swinehart's Summary

Katie Swinehart is a Social Media Mentor and owner of The Social Media Project, a company focused on helping solopreneurs, small- and mid-sized businesses grow through leveraging social media platforms such as Facebook, LinkedIn and Twitter to reach their audiences. Katie has worked with many companies throughout North America to help them navigate their social media challenges successfully.

Katie frequently presents on social media topics, speaking to groups around the country. She also conducts webinars and workshops on making the most of your social media presence.

Currently, Katie leads two chapters of the The Ohio Referral Network, a group comprised of hundreds of central Ohio professionals dedicated to helping each other’s businesses grow through referrals. She’s also the “Go-To Gal” for social media for Awesome Women in Business, an organization consisting of more than 1,000 women business owners.

Even before launching her own business, Katie has a long history of excelling in her work. Her background includes roles with The Chimney Guys, where she was able to increase company sales by 42 percent in a 12-month period. Prior to that, she was a licensed representative for Acceleris Group, where she was the highest producing broker in the state of Ohio for United Health One, one of the nation’s leading insurance companies.

She graduated with a Bachelor’s Degree in Business Administration from DeVry University in Columbus, Ohio. Katie also holds an Associate’s Degree of Applied Business in Private Investigation from the Academy of Court Reporting in Columbus, Ohio.

A native of Grandview Heights, Ohio, Katie now resides in Pataskala, Ohio with her family. She enjoys spending time with her husband, children and dogs and is an avid film buff and art enthusiast. She also recently took her love of volleyball to the next level by coaching her daughter’s team.
