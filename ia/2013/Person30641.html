Dariush Vaziri's Summary

Architectural illustrator since 1985, specializing in watercolors. Fast, reliable and experienced service to clients internationally including SOM, EEK, HOK, Gensler among others. Our goal is to successfully and quickly translate the architect's vision into fluid watercolors as well as state of the art 3d computer generated visualizations.

Specialties: Watercolor renderings and 3d cg visualization
