Brandon W. Jones' Summary

I’m a seasoned professional in leadership, business management, engineering, and project management. I get the job done through hard work, dedication, and an unrelenting drive for success.

I am a leader through study and practice. I earned my MBA and have successfully led teams through projects, challenges, process and procedural changes, etc. I’ve also helped teams embrace and achieve difficult goals and set visions of the future.

I enjoy teaching correct leadership and ethical principles in one-on-one and group settings. I have also developed a leadership training website, Leadership Done Right (www.leadershipdoneright.com), that helps people improve their leadership skills to become better leaders.

I earned a BS degree in mechanical engineering and have spent most of my career in engineering and engineering management. I’ve worked in the natural gas industry doing pipeline design, code compliance, permit and land rights acquisitions, system pressure modeling, MAOP verification and validation, pipeline integrity management research, pipeline facility and personnel emergency preparedness, etc.

In project management, I have successfully managed small, medium, and large projects to reach the desired outcomes while staying within the budget. The large projects had multi-million dollar budgets and required coordination with many parties.

I enjoy working with and leading people towards a common goal. I also love working with others to solve complex leadership and engineering challenges to produce innovative solutions. Please reach out to me if you would like to talk about business, leadership, engineering, project management or I can help you in any other way. My email address is brandonwjones1@gmail.com.
