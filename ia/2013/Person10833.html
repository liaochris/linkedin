Audra McMahon, CMPS
Audra McMahon is a Kansas City native who comes from a large family that was also born and raised in the area. She attended Notre Dame de Sion High School and earned her Bachelor’s Degree at The University of Kansas in an impressive three years while actively participating in her sorority Delta Gamma. Audra got into banking in 2002 and transitioned to an emphasis in lending in 2004.
When she is not helping people enhance their personal mortgage scenarios, Audra enjoys spending time with her friends and family which include her three dogs: Bella, CiCi, and Sal. Her hobbies include teaching dance to children, walking, practicing yoga, reading, and volunteering. Always striving to do good for others and maintain a positive mental attitude, Audra is an active participant in the Kansas City Chamber of Commerce as a Chamber Champion, a member of the Notre Dame de Sion Alumnae Association, president of her Homeowners Association. Audra volunteers regularly with local agencies seeking assistance ranging from Reach Out and Read to Habitat for Humanity.
