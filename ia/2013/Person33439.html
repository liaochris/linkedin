Debbra Sweet's Summary

Others call me a “People Mover”. Moving people to action, expand comfort zones, overcome obstacles to get lasting results in life, business, leadership and wellness.

Areas of expertise I write, speak, coach, consult and train on are:

Life: personal and professional skill set development. Empowering others to increase individual vitality by improving and adding skill sets, united with purpose, vision, and goals to achieve.

Business: Effective integrated marketing, marketing plans, brand management, promotions, sales, business & systems development, organizational change and leadership. I lead executives and staff to align for a unified vision, purpose and work as a strong team for fiscal health.

Leadership: I believe all people are born with the ability to lead. I believe all great leadership of others starts first with leading yourself. I lead leaders. It takes training, education, and experience to develop great leadership skills. A practitioner of Biblical, Ethical, Action, Organizational, Situational, & Legacy Leadership- I take elements of those methodologies, teach and apply them in creative, strategically proven ways that produce amazing results for individuals, teams and organizations.

Health & Wellness: Life threw me an unexpected curve ball. I had a debilitating accident that, as an adult, left me unable to walk for over 3 years – and took another 7 years to complete a strong recovery without drugs or surgery. The story is long- but the experience of overcoming those circumstances me gives me perspective, insight, education, extensive training and understanding how to heal the body naturally. My experience allows me to work, teach and train others to do the same with compassion, understanding, strength and commitment to help them become their best physically. No situation is too extreme. As a Certified Health Coach I help create a foundation of health instead of sickness and disease to have the best vitality you can.
