Gareth Baxendale's Summary

• • CITP Chartered IT Professional MBCS
• • Achieved the NCC IT Department Accreditation standard
• • Awarded Medalist: UK IT Industry 'IT Dept of the Year 2011' - BCS & Computing


I have worked in the IT industry for over 15 years having started my career as a technical engineer and now working at senior management levels including reporting at board level.

I have supported commercial technology companies from small private IT businesses to global Nasdaq traded enterprises and more recently in the mutual sector managing IT for a large healthcare organisation supporting over 900,000 members.

Managing teams with broad skill sets in both Technology, Development and Project Management, I have successfully managed the delivery of ‘line of business’ systems and improvement projects as well managing budgets and consistently reducing IT costs while increasing value on investment.

I drive the standards for service excellence through the application of good practice ensuring that deliverable's and IT strategies are clearly aligned with organisational goals. I work to ensure that IT is seen as a trusted partner in delivering wider organisational initiatives.
