George Wright's Summary

I have over twenty-five years' experience as a management consultant and I am the founder and President of WrightOne Consulting. I specialize in psychological assessment analysis, organizational assessment as well as leadership and team development. I help clients with selection, development, performance management, strategy formulation, and change management.

I also conduct training workshops and seminars to meet the needs of our clients. Some of the programs I regularly deliver include: Conducting Effective Performance Reviews, Performance Management: Enhancing Employee Performance, The Fundamentals of Supervising Others, Team Building: Developing High Performance Teams and Time Management: Get Focused for Peak Performance.

My industry experience includes working with a wide array of organizations from publicly held 'blue chips’ to smaller privately held organizations in the US and internationally. My delivery experience includes single-person assignments to managing consulting teams where large and/or multi-disciplinary assignments require additional capabilities and skills.

I am also an Adjunct Instructor at Bloomfield College in Bloomfield, NJ, teaching Industrial Organizational Psychology, Social Psychology, Positive Psychology, and Introductory Psychology. In addition, I work with Rutgers University and New Jersey Manufacturing Extension Program (NJMEP) delivering customized training programs to a wide range of organizations in NJ.

I present at industry conferences and seminars as well as author articles for our monthly newsletter, the Wright Note, and other traditional Media and on-line publications.

Please contact me at gwright@wrightoneconsulting.com or call me at 973-419-0870.

Visit our website: www.wrightoneconsulting.net
