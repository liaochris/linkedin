Liz Micik's Summary

Want to connect right now? Email me at info@lizmicik.com

Liz has more than 28 years experience successfully planning and executing strategic marketing plans and budgets in a variety of industries, company sizes and types. She's spent the last 15 years learning what works best online particularly in the Inbound Marketing areas for customer acquisition and revenue growth.

"I love the constant challenge of the Internet. I love the speed of change and the ways you can combine, test, refine and redefine a marketing campaign or a whole business online," she says. "But I hate the fact that so many people confuse that flexibility with easiness. It's not free and it's not that easy."

Currently, Liz is putting this experience to work for Nassco, a leading Janitorial and Packaging Distributor in Wisconsin. She is overseeing their eCommerce efforts and website where they offer more than 12,000 products for sale. She's using SEO best practices, a strong email marketing program and inbound marketing techniques to keep traffic flowing and is fine-tuning the conversion processes onsite.

As a certified Inbound Marketing Specialist, Liz has worked with multi-billion dollar retailers and solo practitioners; putting her client's message in front of the right audience using a customized mix of proactive marketing that includes SEO, content marketing, social media marketing, mobile marketing and more.

Liz's clients need her "in-the-trenches" experience and help on lead generation/customer acquisition projects or to increase revenue from their existing client base. Unlike many online strategists, she's always done the hands on work of keeping their customer facing blogs, websites, newsletters, and listings updated with fresh and engaging content that search engines, social networks and daily visitors all like. She says it "keeps her nimble and anchored in what's working today."

Specialties

SEO strategy, content strategy, social media marketing, blog creation, website content creation, online video/audio, some design and development using CSS, XHTML, AJAX, and a smidge of PHP/MySQL, etc.. Most important in my skills mix are a solid command of everyday English, inbound marketing experience and a solid command of business conversion strategies and techniques.
