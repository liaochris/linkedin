Jeremy Gulish's Summary

Whether it is studying market trends, reading about landlording issues, considering home design or simply touring listings, there isn't a day that goes by that real estate isn't on my mind.

My quest is to be the most well educated, responsive and dedicated agent there is for you. There is no question that buyinig or selling a home is an important decision. Being informed about the market, having an agent who is always available to respond to your needs and who is there to painlessly guide you through the process will make opening the doors to the next chapter of your life that much easier.

Being a homeowner and landlord in Morris Township for several years has made me acutely aware of the challenges of buying, selling, managing and renting homes. My experience navigating real estate challenges both personally and for my clients helps me give you the upper hand through the entire process from the initial search through negotiations, inspections and even beyond the closing table. I come to the table with a team of experts that will help support and deliver the results you desire throughout the home purchase or sales process.

Specialties

Single family homes, condos, town houses, multifamily homes, real estate investing, first time buyers
