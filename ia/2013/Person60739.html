Jeff Adams' Summary

While much of my work experience has been either in managing restaurants or woodworking (quite the dichotomy), I am a business manager (musician, web designer, marketer, recording artist, sound engineer, and podcaster as well) with an unyielding entrepreneurial spirit, working tirelessly at business plans and bootstrap start-ups - currently developing Bucketstomp™ Enterprises, having begun with Bucketstomp™ Productions, then Reserving Vegas™, Incredible Chances™, and The Sustainable Food Movement™, tying things together with media and marketing towards a more sustainable world.

I began writing music very young - having been influenced by artists spanning the advent of modern music. My plight has been music running through my brain since I was a child, having written poetry and melodies even before any instruments came into play. Inspiration, I suspect, may well be a fluid process - the malleable inclinations of the muse, as it were.

I started performing music professionally in '94, then was mostly writing and recording as I moved into the rural family scene in 2001. I lost my left index finger in a table saw accident in 2010. This has only proven to motivate me towards greater success in all of my endeavors. I began live performances again in 2013.

Among my released recordings are two solo albums, "Redefining The Parameters" and "Just A Second Now" (both available at most online retailers), with a 3rd album in production. Bucketstomp™ also produces podcasts, with The Adams Family Podcast­­™, The Jeff Adams - For Real ® Podcast, The H & H Radio™ Podcast, with more in the works.

My future career plans include continuing to produce albums, compose music, perform, podcast, and continue to develop Bucketstomp™ Enterprises into a viable entity. Until that time, my options remain open towards all sorts of other possible opportunities as well.
