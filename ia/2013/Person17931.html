Carol Hartfree's Summary

I have been an obsessive photographer for most of my life, recording everything and everyone around me for the love of it.

I specialise in social, corporate and event photography and my aim is to capture natural, relaxed and spontaneous photographs which reflect the nature of the event from the most serious to the most fun and happy.

As I am based on the Surrey/South West London border, I am ideally located for travel all over southern England but will happily travel all over the world if asked!

In 2002 I decided to change career and took a full-time BA honours degree in photography and digital imaging, which I completed in 2005. I am also a Licentiate of the British Institute of Professional Photography and an Associate of the Royal Photographic Society.

I carry full Public liability and Professional Indemnity insurance and use only professional level equipment. I am also a member of the Canon Professional Network, giving me faster access to repair and replacement kit.

I love people and so my approach to all my work is warm, friendly and fun. I love what I do – and I hope this shows in the pictures I take.

Whether you need a photographer all day to capture your Wedding, Family event, Conference or corporate event or a couple of hours to photograph your newest employees or children's party, I will provide superb quality images to suit your requirements.
