Robert (Bob) Ringstrom's Summary

Strata Performance Solutions guides managers to precisely align employees with job requirements that are unique to their own qualities. Only then, can an organization hope to reach its potential.

I have a career of experience in managing people and the problems they’re challenged with. My background emphasizes intense entrepreneurial energy with worldwide engagement. The one over-riding issue has always been about identifying what makes one person better for a specific job than someone else.

Our workforce strategies are designed for business leaders who are intent on positioning the right individuals in the most effective team environments.

Targeted Essentials:

• Help managers become achievers
• Upgrade their talent
• Maximize workplace productivity
• Career Planning/Job Succession
• Make every hire count
