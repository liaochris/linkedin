Kathy Bayer's Summary

I help small businesses and entrepreneurs increase their visibility and sales capacity through a customized mix of digital/traditional marketing, PR, and branding. Leveraging my eclectic background in publishing, marketing, nonprofit, and entertainment, I thrive on working with individuals committed to leading-edge change in social, business, arts, and cultural arenas.

From a young age, I’ve always had had three distinct interests: philosophy, music, and entrepreneurship. And the way these threads have woven through my life enables me to take risks in new ventures, dive deeply into challenges to find solutions, and successfully orchestrate projects from many different angles.

Beginning my career in the publishing business as an associate editor at Doubleday, I cultivated writing/ editing skills, project management, and relationship management with high-profile authors. Later, moving into the nonprofit world, I helped launch several arms of global nonprofit EnlightenNext: starting the editorial department, overseeing event marketing, and founding the NYC educational center. These roles fueled a strong background in organizational leadership, collaboration with partners, and marketing/PR skills.

My love of music led me to eventually start my own music management company, and I continue to manage/promote artists and concerts internationally. Most recently, I integrated all my varied experience into my new consulting firm, KB Marketing Communications. Projects include: managing PR and outreach for a Boston-based career and branding consultancy, revamping a website for an international brand consultant, and managing PR/marketing for an art gallery opening.

Bringing an objective eye to marketing and communications strategy, I approach every project from a 1000-foot vantage point to understand a business’s long-term goals and tailor the marketing plan to meet overall business objectives.

Specialties

Marketing strategy, marketing planning, digital marketing, media and communications, content development, copywriting and editing, book and magazine publishing, public relations, small business consulting, social media marketing, organizational leadership, team facilitation, resource staff management, event production, event marketing, SEO strategy
