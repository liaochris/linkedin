Christine Picanza's Summary

Christine began her career as an Accounting Clerk and Bookkeeper at a time when most companies were doing their books manually on paper ledgers. She held numerous positions in small business and managed all aspects of financial departments. Her experience includes public accounting, manufacturing, retail, non-for profit, and service related industries.

After earning her degree in Accounting from Rutgers University, she devoted much of her time as an Accounting Staff Member converting company accounting systems from manual to automated books using Peachtree Software. During one technology upgrade contract she was offered a job at a manufacturing firm to which she eventually become Accounting Controller.

After holding the position for years, she realized that her real talent and passion came from working with clients to install, upgrade, or improve on their automated books. This led her to a consulting position at a technology/business services firm doing just that and has continued to do so on her own for many years since. She has consulted on and taught individual classes in QuickBooks, Peachtree, and Great Plains Software. She has also led a crowd or two through some positively wicked group seminars.

Today she works at Pivotal Business Services LLC where she leads the QuickBooks, bookkeeping, sales tax and payroll services department. She’s been s deeply involved in every aspect of bookkeeping, from the old-fashioned green ledger sheets to today’s hottest accounting software; that is precisely what makes her such a sought-after expert.

She has earned QuickBooks Pro Advisor Certification in various versions between 1997 up to 2012. Having worked with and being a consultant in accounting software since 1992, she is adept at learning new software as well.

Specialties

Helping companies make a transition from just using QuickBook to turning it into a power resource that provides the information they need to make decisions, guide strategy, and operate efficiently.
