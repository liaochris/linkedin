Matthew Franklin's Summary

Architect with a diverse project and office experience of Architecture and Architectural Practices, covering all aspects of running and working on projects, managing offices & working in teams.

As owner of mbf DESIGN I provide bespoke Architect services to home owners and businesses including freelance services to other Architectural companies.

I have always worked in multi sector practices, most of which have specialised in particular areas and I aim to continue to do work in this way, as I value the experience and knowledge that this brings to each project.

I created and run Ask The Architect, which is a free studio service every Friday afternoon, for home and business owners to ask questions both in person and via most communication platforms.

Specialties

Project Management, Design, Office Management, Business Development, Computer Adided Design and Illustration, Building and Planning Legislation, Brief Writing, Building Design Assessment, Construction Management, Construction Techniques, Design Management, Feasibility Studies, Self Build, Sustainability, Value Engineering
