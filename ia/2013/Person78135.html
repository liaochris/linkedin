Lana Carlene's Summary

My User Experience umbrella covers information architecture, usability analysis, competitive analysis, content analysis, user research and testing, and interaction design. And all that analysis results in recommendations too! My projects span desktop software & web sites to tablet/mobile apps and web sites.

My main objective is to balance business goals with user needs for enjoyable, effective experiences. To support these efforts, I define usability test plans to conduct usability testing and report on key findings. Then design recommendations are communicated with content inventories, user flows, site maps, wireframes, and functional prototypes.

I typically work with visual design and development teams to balance design recommendations with current constraints of legacy systems, and assist in planning for Agile implementation and iterations.

Specialties: information architecture, interaction design, usability & content analysis, Axure prototypes for web & mobile
