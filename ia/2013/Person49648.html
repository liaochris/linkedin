George P. Kansas' Summary

In a way, I am a demolition expert! I help men and women tear down the fears that prevent them from making the most of life! I help them gain clarity, help them develop poise and presence in sharing their message and then support them in the execution of their plans!

Having worked with Professional and Amateur Athletes, Fortune 500 Executives, Entrepreneurs and most recently, Cancer Patients, Survivors and Caregivers, I help professional men and women bridge the gap from fear to Fantastic! Whether a network marketer or a Fortune 500 C-Suite Executive, I lead people in a process I call Emotional Archaeology. Together we do the digging and sweeping necessary to uncover the REAL YOU!

As a certified Winslow Consultant I employ the highest technology combined with wisdom from the ages so that you can discover the hidden secrets to your own success. Through a proprietary program of Emotional Archaeology called 3 Dragons Coaching, I work with highly compensated clients in diverse fields from law to energy, from Executives to Professional Athletes, from Entrepreneurs to operations leadership.

For a breakdown of the many programs offered, please visit my site at www.georgepkansas.com or my online calling card at gpklive.com.

Specialties: High-Performance Speaker Training, Creative Problem Solving, Communications, Team Building & 21st Century Leadership

George P. Kansas' Experience

Educational Institution; 1-10 employees; Professional Training & Coaching industry

1992 – Present (21 years)

From 1992 to the Present, I design and deliver inspirational and content-rich communications, team building and creative problem solving programs to Corporate Groups, Athletic Teams, Performers, Artists and Sales Teams.

My one-on-one coaching work focuses on C-level executives and high-performing, highly compensated professionals in industries ranging from corporate law, energy provision, and the creative and performing arts.

My portal programs include Paradox to Profits and Mind to Market. In P2P, groups as large as 150 work in "Pods" of 3-6 to identify and solve critical challenges using a combination of the ancient Greek process of consulting the Oracle and modern day technology. Fun meets function in this highly productive, engaging, entertaining program! In M2M, individuals attend online programs to enhance their focus on prioritizing goals, understanding their purpose and developing strategies for pursuing both!
