Clark Denson's Summary

Licensed engineer with 10+ years experience in mechanical HVAC design, energy modeling, and energy audits.

As a part of the Sustainable Solutions Group of SSRCx, Clark Denson manages and takes part in energy services related to the design and operation of buildings. He is responsible for assisting in the design of energy efficient buildings through decision-driving energy modeling and Life Cycle Cost Analyses. He prepares and implements Measurement and Verification plans to help building owners maintain peak efficiency and conducts energy assessments and audits of existing buildings that propose cost-effective energy conservation measures and help achieve the Energy Star Building Label.

Through his efforts, he helps project teams and building owners achieve higher levels of energy savings, earning utility incentives and meeting the requirements of the LEED rating
system, Green Guide for Healthcare (GGHC), and the Federal Government.

In addition to energy-related experience, Clark has design expertise in the selection and calculations of mechanical HVAC systems, preparation of plans and specifications, design of control diagrams and sequences, and construction administration activities for healthcare, laboratory, industrial, commercial, sports, and correctional projects.

Specialties

Energy Modeling, Energy Audits, Energy Assessments, Measurement and Verification, Life Cycle Cost Analysis (LCCA), LEED, HVAC Design, Trane TRACE 700, eQUEST, IES-VE
