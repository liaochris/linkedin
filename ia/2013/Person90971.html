Meg Salter is an experienced coach, consultant, and facilitator who has worked extensively with corporate, public and nonprofit organizations in Toronto, the GTA and across Canada and the USA. She offers: executive and team coaching, organizational structure reviews, strategy and change management processes, team development and mindfulness training. Her core approach is integral, meaning - comprehensive and multi-faceted, focused on enabling growth and learning. Working individually, she embraces each individual's talent, personality style, personal orientations and levels of complexity. Working organizationally, she focuses on stages of development, helping to shift responses to enhance performance results.
