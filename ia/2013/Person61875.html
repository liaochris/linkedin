Jennifer Lam's Summary

Jennifer is a Gen-Y entrepreneur with current business ventures in the food and marketing industries.

Led by an appetite to discover the world, one bite at a time, IAteMyWayThrough.com is Jennifer's latest project. After 7 years of publishing popular food blog jenius.com.au which attracts a readership of 60,000 - 80,000 readers/month, Jennifer has taken the blog to new heights, overhauling the entire branding and UX and relaunching as IAteMyWayThrough.com. I Ate My Way Through will also continue to run its popular eating tours around Sydney and will be introducing an exciting series of bespoke dining events.

Equally passionate about social media and helping small-medium businesses to tell their story, Jennifer also runs a full-service digital agency with a twist! The Bamboo Garden consists of both an in-house team and a virtual network of expert designers, developers, consultants, writers and photographers. The Bamboo Garden's holistic, creative and practical approach is a cost-effective way for SMEs to grow their online presence. Services include social media strategy, web design, SEO, mobile apps development, community management, content creation, email marketing campaigns, blogger and media outreach.
