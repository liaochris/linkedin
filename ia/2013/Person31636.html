David Cohne's Summary

David is looking for new investment management career opportunities.

David has extensive experience researching and writing about mutual funds, portfolio strategies, ETFs, economic matters, stocks, the general market and personal finance. He also has plenty of investment advisory experience including prospecting, presentations, client asset allocation, report generation and trading.

David is proficient in managing model portfolios and running tactical portfolio back tests on mutual funds, ETFs and stocks.
