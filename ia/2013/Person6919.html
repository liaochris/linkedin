Andrew Grill's Summary

Based in London, Andrew is an internationally renowned thought leader in the fields of social business and social media networks.

Currently, Andrew is a Partner at IBM Interactive based in London with a global remit to help drive understanding and deliver social business solutions to key IBM clients.

Previously, Andrew was CEO of leading social influence platform Kred.

Andrew presents at international conferences on a regular basis, and maintains a well-read blog at LondonCalling.co about digital and social business trends.

A LinkedIn early adopter since February 2004, he is an Australian Citizen with indefinite leave to remain in the UK.

A former resident of Sydney, since 2005 he has been active in the UK, EU and US markets, and such he has a deep understanding of the digital, social networking and advertising space and is well connected to leading advertising agencies and brands in London, New York and around the world.

Andrew’s commercial experience comes as a result of running Australia’s largest commercial property website, PropertyLook, as well as holding senior marketing and business development roles with Australian companies Telstra and Optus.

He was also responsible for launching Australian location technology company Seeker Wireless onto the world stage, moving from Sydney to London in 2006 to set up their European presence.

More recently he was head of international client strategy for WPP owned Visible Technologies.

He firmly believes that in order to “get digital you need to be digital”.

He was first online in 1983 via bulletin boards and email, set up his first website in 1994, and has been blogging since 2004.

Part strategy, part business, part technology, Andrew is 100% digital.
