Christoffer Feilberg's Summary

I am an entrepreneur with the passion of creating and working with companies and people that make a difference. I work hard to attract the best possible talents and empower them to release their full potential. I have found the greatest leadership successes by using an “Unboss” leadership attitude both in start-ups and traditional companies.

I love start-ups and support the startup community as good as I can both as advisor and Business Angel. I have a special interest in new digital media and technology.

I have a very broad management experience as CEO from many different companies, both start-ups and more traditional companies with complicated board and owner structures. The companies I have worked with have been with both national and international. I have also worked more than 10 years as consultant.

Specialties: Building and transforming businesses into innovative and agile organizations. Leadership, Start-ups, Angel investment, CEO sparring, Board membership and Advisory boards.

Emediate has grown successfully in a very competitive market dominated by global players by strong focus on quality, service and flexibility.

Emediate is now operating in many countries and is the largest provider of adserving solutions in Scandinavia. Emediate was elected Gazelle company tree years in a row (2007, 2008 and 2009) by the Danish leading business newspaper Børsen (http://borsen.dk/gazeller).

Main office is in Copenhagen, Denmark and a subsidiary in Stockholm, Sweden where the system is developed.

Besides the responsibilities as CEO of Emediate, the position as managing director of the subsidiary EMSEAS was held.

Emediate was acquired by the German media company ad pepper Media in January 2007.
