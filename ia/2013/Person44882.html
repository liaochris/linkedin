September 2012 – Present (1 year) Bangkok, Thailand

CrossFit Bangkok (CFBK) is the first official CrosFit affiliate in Bangkok Thailand. Another innovation by Fitcorp Asia, CrossFit has take the world by storm as an integrated scientific and community based methodology based on results, intensity, community and a complete lifestyle towards optimal performance, health and life itself.

What is CrossFit? CrossFit is an effective way to get fit. Anyone can do it. It is a fitness program that combines a wide variety of functional movements into a timed or scored workout. We do pull-ups, squats, push-ups, weightlifting, gymnastics, running, rowing, and a host of other movements.

The CrossFit program delivers a fitness that is, by design, broad, general, and inclusive. Our specialty is not specializing. Combat, survival, many sports, and life reward this kind of fitness and, on average, punish the specialist.

Constantly Varied, Functional Movements at High Intensity

The CrossFit program is designed for universal scalability making it the perfect application for any committed individual regardless of experience.

Luxury Health Retreats (LHR)

January 2010 – Present (3 years 8 months) Global

“To create organic health and fitness retreats delivering lifestyle change, education and effective strategies for long term success and results, in a setting of luxury, exceptional five star service.”

Luxury Health Retreats was born from our clients' need for intensive workshops where they could dedicate time to re-establishing health as a priority in their lives. They needed to be able to focus, and work with knowledgeable instructors to not only gain the necessary knowledge and skills, but also to implement them in practice before returning to work and families.

Although health retreats already exist, none are able to completely satisfy our clients requirements, where they would be working only with experts, and able to focus on an effective, results oriented program rather than a generic 'packaged' experience.

Within a LHR experience, education and hands-on work make all the difference. By the end of your retreat you will know exactly what you need to do to achieve your goals. Whether your goal is weight loss (or gain), wellness or fitness you'll learn the tricks and tips, based on current research that can help speed your way to success.

Each retreat features plenty of nutrition and fitness education; and focuses strongly on goal setting and energy management to help
empower you to achieve your goals. Minimum four hours of activity
per day with our facilitators, ensures that you will be focused onto the results you want.

Each retreat includes one month of complimentary follow up from our coaches, offering you the opportunity to get feedback on your progress, ask questions, and ensure that you have taken away all the knowledge that you can.

www.lhretreats.com
