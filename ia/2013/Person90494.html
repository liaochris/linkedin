McLean Van Cleve's Summary

A professional currency trader with over ten years of experience, I trade using a purely technical approach and employ algorithmic trading systems with a focus on statistical arbitrage models. I have previously worked as a managing partner of a foreign exchange brokerage, a fund manager, various financial technology startups and most recently as the founder of Stratura Investment Group.

Specialties

Professional forex trader and fund manager. I concentrate mainly on algorithmic models with a specialty in developing and implementing Statistical Arbitrage (StatArb) models.
