Dave Harris' Summary

I am interested in helping companies get through these tight economic times by filling in as "the designer." I have many years of experience in both the print design arena and the web design arena. I have designed and developed several campaigns that utilize both traditional and electronic disciplines to create a fully-cohesive marketing approach.

I have owned and operated Alpha Dog Incorporated, the leader of the pack in multimedia design, since 1994. We have designed and developed over 50 corporate and organizational websites, 15 corporate sales and training CD-ROM and several touchscreen kiosks for both corporate trade show and museum exhibits.

Specialties

Website Design and Development, Traditional Corporate ID and Print Material Design, Campaign Development

Dave Harris' Experience

Owner

Alpha Dog Inc

March 1994 – Present (19 years 7 months)

While we are listed as an Information Technology and Services organization, our core business is corporate marketing and training using webcentric solutions, with a heavy emphasis on solid, high quality design.

Owner/Operator

SC Graphic Services

January 1990 – October 1994 (4 years 10 months)

Riding the wave of desktop publishing into the digital age, I started a small design company to handle desktop publishing tasks for local business such as Mailboxes Etc and National City Bank. We created fliers, brochures, camera-ready t-shirt art, business cards & resumes for many different companies and individuals over the lifetime of SC Graphics service. I ended up selling this company to a T-Shirt design company. They wanted the equipment and I trained them how to use it as part of the deal.

Lead Interface Designer

May 1991 – March 1994 (2 years 11 months)

This is where I began my career as a new media designer and developer as the lead designer of the flagship CD-ROM publication, NautilusCD. This monthly e-publication was released on both Mac and Windows platforms. Being part of that team was a great time! What a super group of people.
