Fred Stemmelin's Summary

20+ years of general management experience, CEO, COO, and senior executive positions.

Board member and Adviser for several companies and organizations.

Business Executive with broad US and European experience in all aspects of Business strategy, international marketing and sales, IT management and software development.

Corporate and operational strategy consultant for severals companies (pricing models, marketing and sales strategy) and coach to CEOs and entrepreneurs.

Data Management Expert: Database, Data warehouse, Business Intelligence, and Web development.

Fred is also President of the French American chamber of commerce of San Francisco (FACCSF.com), chairman of its High Tech committee and chairman of the French speaking networking group of CEO's called Executives Anonymous, both in San Francisco and Silicon Valley.

Fred is co-leading the DBF (lesitedbf.com - http://www.linkedin.com/groups?home=&gid=2169748) organization along with people such as Jean Louis Gassee and Francois Laugier.

Specialties: Business Development / Marketing & Sales Strategies / Product Development / Product Marketing / Staff Management / Presales / Enterprise Software / Consulting
