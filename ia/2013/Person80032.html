Len De Groot's Projects

  • Voting with your Tweet

    • August 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Len De Groot, Mark Huberty, Hillary Sanders

    Voting with your Tweet is an experiment in election prediction. It’s a work in progress and one step in a broader research process that seeks to build a greater understanding of how to use social media to forecast elections.

  • freeDive: Searchable databases in minutes

    • December 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Len De Groot, Scot Hacker

    freeDive transforms Google Spreadsheets into searchable databases that can be embedded in web sites.

    • Users can easily search your data.
    • Users get results in an interactive, sortable table with filters to refine the search.
    • The wizard walks you through creating a search widget.
    • Customize the interface based on the content of your spreadsheet.
    • Add interactive charts
    • The embed code allows users to share search results on social networks.
