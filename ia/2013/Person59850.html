Jay E. Hochheiser, CFP®'s Summary

Jay has been in the financial services industry since graduating college in 1983 with a Business Economics/Communications degree.

He launched his own practice in 1983. He obtained his CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER™ certification in 1995; and followed with incorporating Hochheiser, Deutsch & Co. in 1999. His hard work and creative spirit has grown the practice into a 7-person firm.

Between the years 2001 and 2003, Jay was inspired by Dan Sullivan’s Strategic Coach, to brand his own unique planning process, The Wealth Optimization AdvantageTM.

Through this unique process, Jay works with his clients to take the necessary steps to help maximize their wealth, help minimize their risk, & provide them with more choices to optimize their assets and cash flow. He truly enjoys “finding efficient ways for his clients to build and enjoy their wealth” by saving tax dollars and in other ways gaining “more from less.” Jay has developed a unique practice working with other creative professionals, including media advertising executives and specialized physicians.

Numerous appearances on media outlets throughout New York regarding “Planning for College Expenses” and “Financial Planning in These Trying Times” have allowed Jay to help a multitude of individuals with their financial planning needs.

His personal satisfaction comes from helping clients enrich their lives by turning their dreams into reality.

Jay’s heart extends to charity as well. He is on the Men's Division for the Children’s Medical Fund of NY which funds quality of life programs for Schneider Children’s Hospital (where his son spent his first 7 weeks). He regularly donates toys, money, and his time; helping any way he can.

Specialties: Helping to Provide Financial Freedom for Successful Professionals and Entrepreneurs.
