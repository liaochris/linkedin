Marco Coulter's Summary

Mr. Coulter is a business executive with a deep technical background. He has successfully grown revenue in pre-sales and product divisions in the USA, Europe and Australia. Regarded as an industry expert in IT technologies, he has been the keynote speaker at executive events worldwide.

He brings innovative technology vision that can synthesize disparate ideas into a viable business model. His decisive operational vision and expert leadership in the direction of information technology strategy, resources, enterprise and product programs assists companies making the transition from technology-driven to customer-driven business models.

His cross-functional and industry-diverse IT perspective, from both the corporate and vendor sides—including data center operations, software/hardware purchasing, software development, and marketing—is supported with experience in finance and healthcare verticals.

Along with international business insight plus experience remotely managing international staff and off-shore processes, Mr Coulter is a strong leader and motivator known for creating a "new level of expectation" within teams, "challenging the status quo" and developing product roadmaps that were believed impossible.

Mr. Coulter continues to use his energy and executive experience from across cultures and business models, to make companies globally successful.
