Tiffany FantasyClements' Summary

Keiani Model Management LLC
A subsidiary of Keiani Enterprises was founded in New Orleans, LA winter of 2010. Currently, we represent models and talent within the southern region. Our services range from glamour to high fashion and abroad. We work for our talent by promoting, marketing, negotiating, portfolio development, booking, submission, consulting and hands on training. We strive to assist our talents in accomplishing their goals and to provide our clients with unmatched professionalism while catering to all of their service and marketing needs. Models that possess other talents or professions as such as fashion/graphic design, singing, dance, make up or hair artistry; we may be of assistance to you on a developmental basis. Clients that may seek outsourcing services as such as photographers, directors, etc. we provide referrals.
Our Coporate Culture at Keiani Model Management is that we are here for our talents 24 hours a day - 7 days a week. KMM constant nurturing and availability to our talent signifies our reliability, and overall flexibility. Furthermore, we take pride in assisting them in building their confidence, perfecting their strengths as well as addressing their concerns and fears. In doing so, they will be able to deliver nothing short of their very best and to exceed our clients expectations.
