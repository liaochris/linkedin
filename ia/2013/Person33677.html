Debbie Cole's Summary

My concept on design is to deliver the client’s message as effectively as possible. Read between the lines and get to the heart of the customer's needs. Great design to me is to maintain that white space, be innovative, don't overdesign and keep it light. Don't make your audience work too hard to get your message. Make the visuals work as hard as your words. Be out-of-the-box and take some chances.

In addition to my Graphic and Web design skills, I have over 20 years experience in prepress workflow and production of branding and advertising materials, books, catalogs, newsletters, annual reports, menus, travel brochures and product manuals. My communications skills allow me to be highly effective in your team environment or remotely on an individual basis.
