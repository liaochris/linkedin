Steve Guengerich's Summary

For more than 20 years, Steve Guengerich has played a leadership role in information technology services and software companies. Steve is a co-founder of Appconomy and Managing Director of BroadBrush Ventures (BBV).

Previously, Steve was an early member of the management team at nGenera Corporation and, before that, built up the information technology practice for Bridgepoint Consulting.

Steve began his career with Arthur Andersen & Co.'s MICD division (which subsequently became Andersen Consulting, now Accenture) and Pricewaterhouse-Cooper’s consulting group (now part of IBM Global Services) where, in both cases, he specialized in advanced technologies.

Specialties

Steve is an award-winning writer, having authored or produced nine computer books and dozens of articles, for publications like Computerworld, eWeek, and NetworkWorld. His current book "Think Lobal > Act Glocal" is available through Amazon.com at http://bit.ly/awKABU

He is also a frequent speaker, with past talks featured at Comdex, Networld+Interop, Internet World-Australia, as well as annual conferences for Gartner Group's Dataquest and Forrester's Meta Group.
