Christina Penrose's Summary

A good real estate agent might get you a deal but a great real estate agent genuinely cares about you, is passionate about keeping up with the latest market information, and secures you the best possible price in the swiftest amount of time – which is why Christina Penrose is so breathtakingly good at what she does.

Finding her calling in real estate at the tender age of twenty-three, Christina rapidly rose to becoming a top selling agent. Since then she has been showered with national and international awards as a top marketer and salesperson. After six years in the industry she opened her own agency and has never looked back. As a successful businesswoman and caring employer, Christina combines a calm, easy-going nature with clear insight and authority born of years of riding both the highs and lows in the market

In 2000, Christina opened her own office, choosing the franchise of RE/MAX – the fastest growing real estate group in the world. She has subsequently created a dynamo team of high-performing salespeople to work alongside her. The agency is in a continual state of expansion as a consequence of this and Christina’s natural talent for keeping customers happy and conducting repeat business with satisfied clients over time.

Recognising that agencies can overly focus on sales at the expense of property management, Christina has always included in her services, a strong emphasis on property management. She also formed Property Rentals Brisbane Pty Ltd to grow services available to her landlords.

She is also co-author of ‘How to Sell Your Home for a Dream Price in Record Time.’

If you want someone who brings absolute commitment to getting you what you want, then Christina is your perfect choice of agent. As the doting mother of three young boys and stepmum to a beautiful teenage daughter – she has additionally honed her skills for negotiation and absolute dedication to getting the job done!
