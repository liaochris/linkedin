Leta Russell's Summary

In her twenties, Leta started and sold two successful businesses. In her thirties she marketed art and interior design products, then took time off to raise her three kids. At 45, she started a home business and within two years had a successful business generating over $100,000 monthly in team sales. In 2007, after a move to Australia, Leta began to explore the world of Internet Marketing in order to grow her business globally. As a result, she became a savvy online marketer, international speaker, and business coach with training products of her own. Today, she motivates, coaches, and equips women who want to launch, build and grow a passion driven business.
