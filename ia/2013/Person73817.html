Kellie Frazier's Summary

I'm a connector to my core. Years ago I was called, 'America’s Connecting Coach' because I know firsthand that connecting emotional, spiritual, physical and financial success is no accident.

As a certified trainer, spirited speaker and gifted life coach, I've worked over a decade to interview, speak and train thousands of individuals to connect internally, raise their self-worth, and to inspire great success. I'm also a writer and program developer who loves to inspire others and teach what I learn.

I persevered through a traumatic brain injury, death of family members, murder of friends and the collapse of a family business. I've applied specific principles to my life and found unstoppable momentum. I learned to meditate on plans to allow them to come into being without undue force.

If you are struggling with any of these experiences I can help.

1. Tattered and broken relationships in business and/or in personal life
2. Connecting to others is difficult because you can't bust through the invisible wall
3. You want to move forward in business but seem to be staying stuck
4. Have a passion to serve a purpose but feel you can't make money from it

I've become a best selling author and featured on CBS/CNN Radio, TVO, HuffPostLive, teaching principles of Vision|Power|Action which provide sound wisdom to navigate personal and business choices.

In 2005 I founded a company called Connecting LLC, a leadership enhancement program which provides guidance on relationship building and alignment of the heart and the mind.

My most recent project: http://www.DayofLove.Org
