Justin Winget's Summary

From development of a retail design strategy for our beloved Chicago Cubs to a signature restaurant for those tough guys at the Orange County Choppers and everywhere in between, I have overseen a range of strategic design initiatives for some of the biggest brands in the sports, entertainment & restaurant industries.

In addition, my food, portrait & landscape photography has been featured in publications such as Food and Wine Magazine, USA Today and even a self-published "Auto-Photographical" memoir titled "365 2010" detailing a monumental year in my life through a picture a day.

I truly love what I do...

Creative Design Director

Levy Restaurants

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; Food & Beverages industry

March 2005 – Present (8 years 7 months) Chicago, IL

As Creative Design Director for industry-leading Levy Restaurants Innovations Group, I have worked hard to instill methodologies that streamline processes & workflow, foster developmental growth in team members and incorporate user-based design principles that ultimately culminate in responsible, well-developed and targeted design strategies.

My responsibilities include managing a team of full-time graphic and environmental designers in addition to project teams of resources including graphic, web and interior designers, architects, strategists, illustrators, videographers & photographers. As a team, we build and execute conceptual strategies from the ground-up for our Restaurants, Sports & Entertainment Group Partners and External Clients. Levy's client list includes the likes of The Green Bay Packers, Chicago Cubs, Churchill Downs, The US Open, Walt Disney World, the new Harley Davidson Museum and Nickelodeon Universe at the Mall of America, among many others.
