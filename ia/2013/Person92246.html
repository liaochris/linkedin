Mike DeCesare's Summary

If you're an event professional interested in growing your business without risking a penny of your marketing budget, you have found the perfect match. Over the past 15 months I have worked with various rockstars building an awesome party lead generator. Our innovative web based app gets new parties posted every day from all over the country. I consider each and every one as an opportunity to gain knowledge and hone our skills matching them with the right providers to make their big day a complete success. It is with a burning passion that Big Day Network will strive to become the industry's most recognized brand.

For people planning all sorts of celebrations and events, I offer simple and FREE personalized planning assistance. It is my mission to connect them with the best locations, goods and services for thier festivities, and will save them both time and money doing it.

For event professionals of all types, I am a highly motivated sales associate. I qualify every lead and then make sure you are available for and interested in an event before I introduce them to your business. That is done all completely free of charge. You will only incur an expense if you are able to close a sale with one of my leads. The fee for this service is just 5%, payable after the event takes place and I'm certain you are completely satisfied.

Here is what I don't do: I do not get involved in price negotiations, that is entirely left up to you. Additionally, I never position providers to bid directly against each other for jobs. In the end, competing for business on price alone does not benefit anyone.

To sum it up, if you provide any type of event related goods, services and/or locations, I am confident that I will be a valuable asset to your company. I'm a caring and passionate individual who wants to learn and grow and be successful at many things. If you think we might be a match, feel free to connect =o)
