John J. Nosal's Summary

Having a well designed SEO rich website in the Information Age is a must with all businesses today. With all the options for website development platforms like WordPress, Joomla, static HTML and CSS (cascading style sheets); having the expertise to work in all of these offers great flexibility.

Clean simple web design that takes the visitor on a journey of the website with a logical next option for navigating the site. Additionally having rich images that not only look good, but convey the message of the business.

Additionally, website maintenance is important to keep the website fresh and up to date. Updating images, adding new content to keep a websites content update SEO rich on the front end. Plus installing the latest updates and security patches along with regular backups of all files and if applicable the databases are just a few of the other services offered.

SEO or Search Engine Optimization is critical for the success of a company' online presence. The right balance of keywords is the key to organic SEO.

Case Study: When starting an SEO project for a client, it was pointed out to him that one of his keywords, or services offered, was not being used on the specific services page. With a bit of rewriting and focus on the content we were able to increase the SEO value of his site. He started getting hits on the new keywords in a few days. And best of all increased sales all based on the new SEO focus.

While a website is the center hub of internet marketing; SEO, branding, social media and website analytics are all part of the big picture of internet marketing. Does the website correctly talk about the companies product or service? Is the company effectively using Facebook, Twitter, Google+ and Pinterest to show a non-selling approach to communicating the company's product or service? And do the social media sites drive traffic to the company website? Do you know who is visiting your site? How many visitors are visiting and what pages are most visited?
