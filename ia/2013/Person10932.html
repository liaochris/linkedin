Babington Technology is a privately-owned manufacturer of precision diesel combustion burners and applications used by the United States military, disaster relief organizations, and commercial entities. Babington is the leader in clean and efficient distillate fuel combustion technology as well as an innovator in the field of liquid atomization. The company has its chief manufacturing operations in Rocky Mount, NC, and Washington DC Metro region.

The unsurpassed efficiencies, advanced qualities and core component of all Babington products is the Babington Airtronic Burner, which uses the unique and innovative liquid atomization principle developed and patented by Robert S. Babington, the company’s founder. The Babington Principle is used in the Airtronic to create submicron-sized particles of fuel with very low pressure, which burn like a gas when ignited. The small particle size means that any distillate fuel, such as diesel, can be burned from start-up to shutdown with no smoke, odor, or carbon monoxide.

Since 1995, when the company was awarded its first contract by the United States Marine Corps for the innovative Tray Ration Heating System, Babington has been supplying the U.S. Military and disaster relief agencies with advanced field feeding equipment. Prior to that, the company introduced medical aerosol and home heating products in Europe and the U.S. Babington’s most recent product, the Disaster Relief Mobile Kitchen Trailer (DRMKT), is designed specifically to support first responders in one of their chief missions of providing mass care to those in need.
