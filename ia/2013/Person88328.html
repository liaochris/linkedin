Master Mehrdad Moayedi's Summary

My professional experience includes a career in Professional Karate and Muay Thai fighting, training fighters, teaching traditional Martial Arts, and owning three businesses: a Martial Arts training facility (http://www.mastermehrdad.com) a professional global sporting event produced and brand for television (http://www.shindokumate.com) and executive producer/host for weekly TV show series STRYKERZ™. (http://www.shindokumate.com/strykerztvfightseries)

I founded the Art of Shin Do Kumaté fighting to build the sport of Muay Thai. As a fighter who experienced many obstacles in the industry, I set out on a mission to build an organization for the sake of building the sport and its athletes vs. the promoter.

I envision Shin Do Kumaté to become the next largest fighting brand in the world with the plans to build a franchise of Shin Do Kumaté training gyms worldwide. This is an international sport that demands skill, honor, and courage and Shin Do Kumaté is the place for the most elite male and female fighters from around the world.

Shin Do Kumaté programming is available via Pay Per View, Video On Demand, cable television and global online platforms, as well as through satellite providers. All fight programs are distributed to multiple international markets such as New Zealand, Australia, Germany, England, Korea, Israel, Romania, Albania, Middle East, Canada, and South America.

Specialties: I am a Fight Industry Expert, Martial Arts Master 8th Dan, Executive Producer of Televised Fight Programs, TV Host, Color Commentator, Fight Promoter, Behind The Scenes Director, Matchmaker, and Business Development Professional.

I am always looking to build new relationships in the industry of media, production, film and distribution. Opportunities are available for venue partners, sponsors, and media partnerships.
