Dan Yukon's Summary

Who Am I?

I am the CEO of a company with a strong engineering background and look to provide goods and services of the highest caliber to the market segment we serve (Biotechnology, Chromatography, Purification, HPLC/FPLC). We focus on Protein and Peptide applications.

What Do We Sell?

We sell a variety of equipment and services centering around our core competency of HPLC/FPLC chromatography columns used in the purification of drug medi. We supply empty columns from 5mm in diameter thru multi-meter process columns, which allow our customers to have a single source of supply from bench scale thru large scale production and purification. We place a strong focus on Protein and Peptide applications.

We are presently expanding and are offering pumps, systems, hardware, upstream and downstream equipment for the scientist performing purification using HPLC/FPLC. We offer unique solutions not found in the product portfolios of the big players who do not provide personalized attention.

What Are Our Specialties?

Bioseparations and Purification using Chromatography
Protein Purification using Chromatography
Peptide Purification using Chromatograpy
Large Molecule Purification using Chromatography
FPLC - All Applications using Chromatography
Low and Medium Pressure Purification using Chromatography

What Services Do We Offer?

With a strong engineering core competency we offer labratory support services in design and fabrication of unique devices not readily available on the market. Design is performed on SOLIDWORKS and our shop can fabricate in various medias (stainless steel, glass, plastic, etc......)

Why Should You Work With Us?

Being a smaller "hands on" business you will receive the attention of our staff, quick answers to your questions and will have access to talent otherwise not available at a reasonable cost. You will also have access to our staff 24/7!
