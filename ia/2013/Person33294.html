Deanna Segrave-Daly's Summary

Teaspoon Communications is a food-first nutrition communications group comprised of culinary-minded registered dietitians with decades experience working for commodity groups, food industry, PR agencies, restaurants, media and social media outlets.

Whether it's a live TV spot, social media outreach, a written article, a recipe project or a trade show appearance, Teaspoon Communications can deliver your key messages, create practical culinary tips and make your food product unforgettable to consumers.

Specialties

Engaging social media outreach
Flavorful food writing
