Darlamae Shannon, BSHRM, SPHR's Summary

Darlamae is a realist who designs holistic HR programs aligned with business goals. She has amassed an extensive 20 year progressive career arc in HR and human capital operations management with notable success in managing complex multi-site, multi-state, multi-national business operations ranging from 3 to 2500 employees. Darlamae has controlled multi million dollar revenue and cost-center budgets.
