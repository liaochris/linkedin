Gary Carter's Summary

General Background

Faculty member of the University of Minnesota's Carlson School of Management and the Associate Director of the Carlson School's Master of Business Taxation Program.

Formerly worked as a revenue agent for the State of Alaska, and later in public accounting for both a regional CPA firm and a Big Four Firm.
