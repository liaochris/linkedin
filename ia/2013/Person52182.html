Heather Baron's Summary

Far Western is a cultural resource management firm that assists clients in complying with environmental and historic preservation laws and procedures. As one of the largest archaeological firms in the western United States, Far Western has a track record of innovative research within a cultural resource management context.

As Production Supervisor, I manage the Production and Graphics Department, oversee four full-time employees, and coordinate the production schedule for the company and branch offices.

Prior to assuming management of the department, I was responsible for establishing departmental procedures and standards, editing reports in their draft and final stages, working with authors and contributors to bring the reports together, and formatting them according to professional templates.

Additionally, I design public education documents and monographs for publication, one of which was the typesetting of Life on the River: The Archaeology of an Ancient Native American Culture, in cooperation with Heyday Books (Berkeley, CA).

On the side, I am a freelance document designer, offering the services of book design and editing.

I am certified as a Small Business for the State of California (#1004718), in Editorial and Support Services and Art Design Services.
