Dr. Mike Dobson's Summary

Senior executive with broad business experience focused on Geospatial data and its application in business and government. Expertise includes: mapping, Geographic Information Systems, Location Based Services, Local Search, mapping, publishing, product and service development, as well as organizational and strategic planning.

Excellent leadership skills coupled with a demonstrated ability to: 1) build highly efficient, results oriented solutions, 2) structure, design and implement these solutions on an effective and cost efficient basis, 3) manage multiple, complex investigations simultaneously, and 4) mentor team members and position them for future personal and professional success.

• A strategic thinker with an outstanding ability to form strong, cooperative interpersonal relationships coupled with excellent oral and written communications and presentations skills.

• Recognized expert with over thirty years of experience in growing winning tech teams while dealing with issues and applications of technology for use in the development of market leading products, tools and services.

Dr. Mike Dobson's Experience

Owner

TeleMapics, LLC

January 2003 – Present (10 years 10 months) Laguna Hills, CA

TeleMapics provides consulting services focused on local search, location based services, geospatial tools, mapping, and other applications of geographical data.

CTO and EVP Engineering

go2 Systems

2001 – 2003 (2 years)

go2 was an early-leader in the local search market. The company provided Yellow Pages information over cellular networks to Internet provisioned phones. go2 was among the most popular applications available on the decks or in the "walled gardens" of the leading cellular carriers. In addition to the go2 branded "local search" product, the company also provided similar white-label services to Microsoft's wireless group, as well as to other companies involved in the wireless marketplace.

Chief Technologist + Chief Cartographer

Rand McNally & Company

Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Consumer Electronics industry

August 1986 – December 1999 (13 years 5 months)

Dr. Dobson was the executive in charge of all creative efforts related to developing the company's products. The product mix included maps and atlases ( geospatial database publishing), consumer and commercial software products involving routing and mapping, as well as maps and software for the company's educational products division. In addition, Dr. Dobson simultaneously served as the company's Chief Cartographer, Vice President of Business Development and Chief Technologist during this period.

Associate Professor of Geography

State University of New York at Albany

Educational Institution; 1001-5000 employees; Higher Education industry

September 1972 – August 1985 (13 years) Abany, New York

After a graduate education at the University of Kansas, Dr. Dobson sequenced from a Lecturer through Assistant Professor and Associate Professor positions with the Geography Department of SUNYA. His course work and research were focused on cartography, computer mapping and geographic information systems.
