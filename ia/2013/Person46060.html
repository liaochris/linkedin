Hello and thanks for visiting my profile.

I am the CTO of Mediavine Marketing, where we simplify social media for successful businesses and professionals through training, consulting and management. We minimize your efforts and maximize your results.

In addition to Mediavine, I’m also the CTO and co-founder of the Linked Local Network (LLN). LLN is a networking organization for professionals who utilize social media to promote their businesses and accomplish their goals. The Linked Local Network has developed an international following.
