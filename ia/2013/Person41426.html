Emma Welles' Summary

I've been designing websites since my first X-Files-themed website in 1997 and creating banners since I won an X-Files banner design contest in 1999. I don't do much X-Files-related design anymore, but I am lucky enough to create marketing designs for the best tv shows on networks like IFC, AMC and Syfy. I honestly can't imagine doing anything else.

Specialties: web design, user interface design, interactive advertising & rich media design & development (using Pointroll, Doubleclick and MediaMind), IAB standard banners, logo design, app design, web-based animation.
