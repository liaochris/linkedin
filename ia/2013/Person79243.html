Laurie Deppa's Summary

Served in US Navy 90-93, USS Yellowstone, Gulf War (4 medals), honorable medical discharge.

Managed lots of things and people.

Started a retail spa/massage business at BWI Airport in 2001, expanded to other locations - opened a new store in the Annapolis Mall in Dec 07. At one point managed staff 40 and 4 locations in 3 airports. Managed all day-to-day aspects of the business as the sole officer of the company.

Interested in a career in international sales, marketing or management - or just getting my foot in the door with an international company.

(My love is international anything - undergrad in Int'l Studies, Master's in International Management/Marketing).

Specialties: Marketing - have had over 40 articles about my business - cover of Wash Post, cover of Capital-Gazette, Southwest Magazine, USA Today, Delta Magazine; on NPR, channels 13, 9 & 45.

Did outside sales campaigns in New Orleans and Brandywine Valley areas (totally unfamiliar).

Ran business in airports 2001-2009 - very difficult places to operate.

Ran for Mayor of Annapolis in 2009.

Strengths include networking, people skills, multi-tasking and organization.
