Jeff Loughridge's Summary

I have been operating, maintaining, and designing IP/MPLS backbone networks since 1997 in both engineer and management roles. I currently operate my own consulting business in which I provide services to primarily Tier 1 ISPs and mobile operators.

Proficiencies -

• Technical design, development, and implementation of end-to-end IP infrastructure
• Depth of knowledge in Tier 1 ISP best practices, settlement-free peering, and the carrier business
• IP routing protocol design – IS-IS, OSPF, BGP
• Planning and coordination of network roadmaps that outline key development efforts, dependencies, required budget, and timelines
• IPv6 migration and IPv4 to IPv6 transition technologies
• MPLS/VPN design and implementation
• WiMAX IP Core design and deployment experience on two production WiMAX networks
• LTE/SAE EPC design and 3GPP/non-3GPP interworking
• Migration of legacy data networks to all-IP environments
• Cisco and Juniper router configuration
• Scripting - perl, python, javascript

Specialties: 3GPP EPC & WiMAX IP core design, deployment, and capacity management; optimizing IP networks for simplicity and least cost; TDM to Ethernet access migration; integration of IP and DWDM; and MPLS/VPN design
