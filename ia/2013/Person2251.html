Marcie Price's Summary

The Association of Fundraising Professionals Greater Cleveland Chapter is dedicated to elevating the fundraising profession by providing valuable educational programs to our members and serving the community by promoting ethical and professional practices in philanthropy.

The Greater Cleveland Chapter also offers members networking opportunities and access to national webinars, as well as a venue to expand your skills set.

EXPAND YOUR PROFESSIONAL SKILLS:

Enhance your membership experience by joining one of the following committees, where you'll expand your professional network and gain valuable experience.

*Communications
*Membership
*Professional Development
*Resource Development
*National Philanthropy Day (Workshops/Awards Luncheon)

The AFP Cleveland Chapter hosts an annual Philanthropy Day Awards Ceremony, which recognizes outstanding philanthropists and volunteers from the greater Cleveland region.

INVEST IN YOUR FUTURE THROUGH MEMBERSHIP:

Membership is open to those committed to fundraising on behalf of community and charitable organizations.

Job postings are open to all members.

To view a full listing of membership benefits, please visit the Membership Benefits link above.

AFP luncheons and events are open to non-members. Please join us for one of our monthly programs by visiting our chapter website.

Specialties

Advancing philanthropy through networking, education and certification programs.
