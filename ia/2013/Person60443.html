UI Artist

Zynga Dallas

Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; Computer Games industry

July 2011 – June 2013 (2 years) Dallas/Fort Worth Area

My official title at Zynga Dallas was UI Artist, but actually I was able to wear many artist-hats during my time there.

The most recent publicly released game I've worked on was CastleVille, though during the past 8 months i had been working on 2 different unreleased titles for the mobile platforms. My main job on CastleVille was related to user interface design (iconography, button designs, etc.) but I also provide support in other departments such as interface art animation, concepting, avatar animation support, and 3D modeling/FX support. For example, around the 2011 holiday season I came up with, created, and helped implement a particle-based snow accumulation solution for all our 3D assets in CastleVille so that we could quickly realize a consistent looking streamlined snow cover pass on all existing world assets. I also concepted and implemented various dialog window flash animations to spruce up otherwise static windows to help maintain an exciting user experience for players.

I really loved the team here, as they always gave me a chance to stretch my art legs and develop my skills further not only related to UI/UX but also in many other areas of expertise.
