Lisa Marie "Phoenix" Jackson's Summary

Lisa Marie “Phoenix” Jackson is a tenured professional who invested shy of a decade working with corporations while being a full-time student for the first four, plus traveling and logging onto social networks every moment that she could find.

“The Brooklyn, New York, native had developed a knack for building online relationships and helping her employers connect with customers using sites such as Facebook, Twitter, and LinkedIn. So she created a business plan to launch her social media consulting firm, Phoenix Aficionado (www.phoenixaficionado.com).” ~ Black Enterprise Journalist, Tamara E. Holmes

Phoenix was taught at a very young age by her mother that when you do what you love, you are no longer working. Therefore, after planting seeds cross-industry, she decided to cultivate her own garden in the Fall of 2011 by leveraging the best attributes of her accumulated experience to launch Phoenix Aficionado on January 10th, 2012.

“La vie est hérissée de ces épines, et je n’y sais d’autre remède que de cultiver son jardin.”
Life is bristling with thorns, and I know no other remedy than to cultivate one’s garden.
– Ancient French Philosopher, Oeuvres Complètes de Voltaire

Phoenix prides herself on being an Aficionado of her business, the facets of life shared in her lifestyle blogazine, and life as a whole. She promotes all who encounter her brand to be an Aficionado of what they love too in order to rise above whatever ashes may be trying to confine or define them! i.e. the brand quote, “We are Aficionados, are you?”
