Kimberlee Buckingham's Summary

Kimberlee brings her experience as a high performing sales team leader with a proven track record of increased sales volume, sales team training and motivation and business growth with skills in complex national accounts. A driven, collaborative goal-oriented high achiever, Kimberlee is able to lead cross functional teams, manage forecasting, create strategic marketing plans, and negotiate contracts. She has a keen sense of the "art of the deal" and is proficient with all customer relationship management (CRM) systems.

She's also well regarded for being a visionary leader and a clear communicator with high credibility. A driven, collaborative goal-oriented high achiever. She is an expert in consultative selling and building customer intimacy with a focus on relationships rather than transactions. Kimberlee is able to lead cross functional teams, manage forecasting, create strategic marketing plans, and negotiate contracts and lead superior customer service experiences.

Completing the prestigious sales and marketing program at the IBM Corporation, as well as many years of solution sales training programs with Oracle, SAP and CISCO, Kimberlee is dynamically skilled. She shows companies how to clearly understand their prospect's needs and sell them a solution with the return on investment and benefits their customer will not want to be without.

Kimberlee is a leader, author, speaker and an advocate for women that want to make a difference in the world, achieve their dreams and accomplish their goals. She has learned through life experiences what does and does not work. She has developed a Smart is the New You program and methodology that empowers, enables and inspires a community of women breaking barriers and living the smart way. Her programs, products and speeches include "Smart is the New Perfect", "Smart Social Strategies" and the "Smart Lifestyle Formula."
