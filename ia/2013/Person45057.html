Flora Morris Brown's Summary

I want to be your companion on the journey from idea through publication of your nonfiction book. As an entrepreneur, coach or blogger you know that a book will increase your credibility, visibility and profitability. But perhaps you don't know how to get started or you have a long-neglected manuscript that you can't seem to revive. You have loads of questions and yes, fear. If you start today, you could proudly show off your finished nonfiction book or ebook within 90 days. It's your choice. Ready? Let's go.

Where did it begin?

When I discovered that my early poems could delight, inspire and entertain others, it was love at first write. I was hooked on making writing a central part of my life.

Throughout my teaching career, even as I enjoyed being a wife and mother, I wrote educational materials, grants, articles and learning games for my kids and students.

While I was teaching junior high English and university graduate courses, I coauthored textbooks in language arts series for Random House and Silver Burdett.

In 2008, I started a blog to share my ideas about living a fulfilling life. In December 2009 I turned that blog into a book, Color Your Life Happy, and expanded on it with an interview-format Internet radio show.

In addition to writing guest blogs for Yes to Me, Raising Great Families, Decisive Minds I also maintained a column for Suite101.com, and wrote articles for Los Angeles Times, Gift Basket Review, Food , Gifts and Decorative Accessories, Party and Paper Retailer, Food Distribution Magazine and Trends and Tips from The Gift Basket Association and Falling in Love with Life.

When coaches, entrepreneurs and bloggers asked me to help them write a book to attract clients, strengthen credibility and increase visibility, I directed my skills to book coaching.

Specialties: Nonfiction book publishing coach, blogger, freelance writer, radio host, online course creation and teaching, curriculum developer
