Artbiz: the business of art

Self-Employed; Myself Only; Internet industry

January 2004 – Present (10 years)

Design is Kim Bruce’s background; art, her passion; digital technologies, her tool. Kim is a visual problem solver. With years of experience in presentation and a desire to reach a wider market, she designed her own website. In doing so she found herself opening a veritable Pandora’s Box. Other artists, arts groups and local businesses have approached her to design and upgrade their websites. Through word-of-mouth, the ripple effect widened, and Kim was encouraged to launch Artbiz.

Artist

Kim Bruce Fine Art

January 2000 – Present (14 years)

Through the use of materials from distinctly different sources I make succinct yet subjective statements that reference the dichotomies that exist in our world and within ourselves. By juxtaposing these materials I find bittersweet contradictions concerning the fragility of our world, our precarious position in the world and in turn within ourselves.

Ultimately I seek to unveil natural beauty, to expose the private self and reveal the link between the internal and external landscape whilst translating my own inner emotions into the work with sensitivity and conviction.

Read and see more at http://kimbruce.ca

Boss

Kim Bruce Designs Inc.

January 1983 – June 2000 (17 years 6 months)

Background: Kim Bruce established a successful design firm in 1983 specializing in image development and store planning. Clients included national and local chains such as The Forzani Group (Sport Chek), Nevada Bob’s, Lammle’s Western Wear , Grabbajabba, Boutique of Leathers and many other boutique style stores. Throughout her career she has continued her art education through classes at the University of Calgary and the Alberta College of Art & Design. (Kim lives and works in the foothills of Alberta just outside the City of Calgary).
