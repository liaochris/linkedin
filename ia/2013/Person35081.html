Duncan Hopkins' Summary

I solve creative problems for customer driven companies.

UX, UI, creative design and direction. Companies rarely find one person that has this experience and skill set. I am that person. With 20 years in interactive alone, I am able to solve your user experience issues while bringing creative strength and clean design to the entire project.

Experienced in user interface and user experience design plus advertising, marketing, branding and graphic design. Familiar with every step in the process, Hands on concepts to team management. Plenty of agency, corporate and start-up experience inside and out.

Specialties: UI/UX design, prototyping, wireframing, branding and identity, graphic design, social media integration, print, marketing, creative department management, information architecture, big ideas, getting things done efficiently and on time.
