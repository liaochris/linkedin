Ashley Batz's Summary

Photography is my passion, and I intend to further develop myself as a photographer and as a creative professional throughout my career. I am also a natural-born evangelizer, as I love engaging a community, and work well with content, social media, and community.

Photography Work includes: Airbnb, NY Fashion Week (Herve Leger, Pamella Roland, Milly), Cinequest (photographing John Turturro), Sara Paxton, Glitter Guide, Chloe Rose Boutique, and more.

My work has been featured in Pictory, Guest of a Guest, Snapixel, Style Me Pretty, Glitter Guide, Wedding Gawker, The Knot, Equally Wed, Forget Me Knot Weddings, and more.

My work has been published or exhibited at the de Saisset Museum, in "The Changing Face of Homelessness," and other upcoming publications.

I love being creative. Dance, yoga, writing, and surfing the internet for all things tech and fashion are some of the things that I love to engage with in my spare time. My friends and family are integral pieces to who I am, as I feel that strong relationships are the key to a happy and more fulfilling life.
