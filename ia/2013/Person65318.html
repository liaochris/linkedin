Joe Gomez's Summary

With a background in Computer Science, I have experience in Health-care IT, computer support, networking, and web programming. I work closely with every client's project to ensure smooth delivery from start to finish.

I firmly believe in the entrepreneur spirit and am currently building and growing an IT / Health Care IT services and support business.

Dynamic Technologies (aka DynamiTek) provides on site support for small and medium businesses and health-care facilities who do not have staffed IT support.

DynamiTek provides the following services: Certified EMR installation, Computer hardware and software support, Computer systems sales and installation, Virus removal, Wired & wireless networking and network security, Site survey and analysis, Web Design, Search Engine Optimization, Web Advertising.

In 2012, Dynamic Technologies became an endorsed vendor of the Michigan Professional Business Association.

Also in 2012, Dynamic Technologies was specifically chosen by eClinical Works to travel to their headquarters in Westborough, MA to complete their comprehensive training program in order to obtain Preferred IT Vendor Status for their product. Owner and IT Consultant Joe Gomez completed the course and received an official certification to install eClinicalWorks. DynamiTek is now one of only two companies in the state of Michigan to have achieved Preferred IT Vendor Status.

We also have a spin-off IT company that specializes in custom software consulting.

Dynamic Software Group provides business software solutions. Our first product to launch was in 2008, called DynamiChat. DynamiChat is a fully featured live chat program for websites. Having the ability to chat live and direct with visitors on your website increases sales and customer satisfaction. DSG has since evolved into providing custom programs for all types of business.
