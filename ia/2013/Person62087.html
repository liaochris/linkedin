Jenny Garrett's Summary

*LinkedIn UK Top 10 Power Woman 2013 *
*Finalist PRECIOUS Mentor of the Year 2012*

My clients are:
*Organisations that want to make the most of their leaders, to maximise performance and productivity in times of change.
*Women executives and business women who want to manage their lives more effectively and realise their potential in the workplace.

I am Author of Rocking Your Role, the 'how to' guide to success for female breadwinners.

I am called on regularly to deliver inspiring, energetic and thought-provoking talks at Women's, Entrepreneurs and Leadership themed events.

I have been featured extensively in the media, on programmes such as: Radio 4′s Woman’s Hour & Newstalk radio as well as writing articles and contributing to many national publications, such as the Telegraph, Sun Employment, Women's Weekly and Glamour Magazine. I am a blogger for the Huffington Post UK http://www.huffingtonpost.co.uk/jenny-garrett/

People tell me that I am motivational and inspirational. I view my style as incisive, pragmatic, and supportive. My aim is to enable you to penetrate to the heart of the matter in order to move forward in a focused and expeditious way, it won't be easy but it will be worth it!

Specialties: At the heart of all I do is the underpinning of being an experienced and qualified coach who has regular supervision to keep growing and developing in my practice.

I hold Level A and Level B qualifications with the BPS which qualifies me to administer and provide feedback on a number of psychometric instruments including MBTI step I, 16 PF, Firo B, Leadership Judgement Indicator, Wave, Ennegram, Implicitly and Insights.
