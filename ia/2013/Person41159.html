Emilee Roberts' Summary

I am a writer, blogger and mompreneur. I homeschool our three special needs children. I have a crafting/sewing business, and my products can be found on my website/blog as well as my Etsy shop.

I love writing, homeschooling, sewing, health and fitness, nutrition and have a passion for sharing with others more eco-friendly, natural alternatives. I have a desire to inspire and encourage others and help them live a healthier, more fulfilling life as well as raise awareness on environmental effects on our health and in our home! I love to help promote businesses that offer eco-friendly alternatives and family-friendly products through reviews and giveaways on my website.

My goals are to help others have a happier home - from taking care of themselves, their family, their home, and even their pet(s) through education, support, and by offering a means to achieve their goals to live the lifestyle they desire!

Specialties: Writing, Blogging, Homeschooling, Special needs children, Eco-friendly living, Health and fitness, dog obedience training, customer service
