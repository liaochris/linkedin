Ilona Vanderwoude, MRW's Summary

Trying to fit a million passions into one lifetime? Get my free report "3 Massive Mistakes Modern-Day 'Renaissance Personalities' Make—And How To Avoid Them" - http://bit.ly/6qaUmS

Do you recognize yourself in these descriptions?

You..

… Wish you would figure out your true passion in life – once and for all. You feel like a Jack-of-all-trades, Master-of-none.

… Lose interest in things you thought would interest you forever.

… Are very successful in your field, but bored to death and looking to change directions - again. To the horror and disbelief of those around you.

If this sounds like you, it’s very plausible that you are a modern-day “Renaissance Personality” – someone genetically wired to be interested in many completely unrelated topics and with a wide range of talents to match.

Or, as I like to put it: a multi-talented person trying to fit a million passion into 1 lifetime.

I'm Ilona ("rhymes with Fiona") Vanderwoude and my passion is helping these multi-talented folks, who simply can't pick one passion or professional "major," create exciting lives and careers.

If you feel unable to manage and enjoy your many talents and passions, make sure you’re avoiding the 3 biggest mistakes people like you make. Go to my web site: http://bit.ly/6qaUmS for my free report that reveals these massive pitfalls – and how to avoid them.
