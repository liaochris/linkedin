JEANETTE SANCHEZ's Summary

JEANETTE SANCHEZ is Senior Vice-President Information Technology for AMN Healthcare. She is an accomplished information technology executive, speaker and mentor with vast international experience in enterprise system delivery. Demonstrating high-level expertise in governance, technical architecture, portfolio management, and alignment of business strategy with large scale I.T. initiatives, Jeanette inspires as both an in-house executive and a trusted business advisor. Jeanette advances her value proposition through her unique application of vision setting, strategic planning, business acumen, solution design and delivery, and how the combination delivers value at every level of the enterprise.

With a stellar history in transformational I.T. leadership, Jeanette has worked for Fortune 500 companies in the medical device, consumer goods, life sciences, and manufacturing industries. She offers a systematic and practical approach in identifying strengths and weaknesses of a business operation and illuminates a set of ROI-targeted solutions that have enabled and advanced the strategic and operational goals of well-known companies such as CareFusion, Edwards Lifesciences, Baxter Healthcare, Mattel and Allergan.
