Jim Usry's Summary

For over thirty years, I was fortunate to work in the pharmaceutical and bio-technology industries. My experience was highly varied and included assignments in Sales, Sales Training, Marketing, Managed Care Account Management, and Sales Management.

Merck & Co, Inc. was my employer for 27 of those 30 years. In this highly competitive, highly regulated corporate environment, I learned to function effectively as a manager, team member, and employee of a bluechip, fortune 500 company.

In 2009, I founded Advanced Team Concepts, LLC. Its mission is to help others in healthcare-related organizations understand and resolve conflicts can arise from situations as diverse as being on an internal team to dealing with patients in a clinical setting.

In 2010, we added Commercial Healthcare Partners, LLC to our portfolio. Commercial Healthcare Partners facilitates relationships and brokers deals between companies that have products and therapies in research with companies that wish to add such products and therapies to their pipeline. Our comprehensive services include in-licensing, out licensing, distribution and commercialization.

Specialties

Pharmaceutical and Biotechnology business development, commercialization of pharmaceutical and biotechnology products and therapies and market development in the U.S., Canada, and Latin America.
