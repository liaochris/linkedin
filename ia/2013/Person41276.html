Emily Linn's Experience

Owner and Designer

City Bird

June 2005 – Present (7 years 11 months)

City Bird is store, gallery and studio in Midtown Detroit, Michigan. In addition to our own line of detroit-themed housewares, accessories and paper goods, we sell work by hundreds of other local and regional artists and designers. Our line is also available on our website (www.ilovecitybird.com) and in 15 other retail stores in Detroit, Chicago and New York.

Emily Linn's Publications

  • Belle Isle to 8 Mile: An Insider’s Guide to Detroit

    • Inland Press
    • December 2012
    Authors: Emily Linn, Robert Linn, Andy Linn

    Belle Ilse to 8 Mile features a selection of more than 1000 favorite Detroit attractions, points of interest, sites, institutions, events, restaurants, bars, and curiosities from the essential to the obscure. The book puts contemporary Detroit in a broad context by painting portraits of the city’s historic neighborhoods, sharing stories of its history and traditions, and detailing the people and culture that have made—and continue to make—the city great.
