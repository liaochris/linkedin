Manager and Columnist

New Renaissance Bookshop and Event Center

May 2007 – September 2012 (5 years 5 months) Portland, Oregon Area

New Renaissance is a personal growth haven of the Pacific Northwest.

In my time at the store, I was able to learn what it takes to run a retail business along with a full-time event program. Besides being a manager, my biggest role was to write a weekly e-column as the "eyes and ears" of the store.

This task called me to interview hundreds of personal/professional growth leaders and authors. I also had to read a book each week for review in the email column. (well over 500 books read!)

To me, experience of reading, writing and communing with our audience was like getting a Ph.D in communication and marketing. My words made the cash register ring... and it put local and national teachers on the map!
