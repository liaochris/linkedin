Barry Carbaugh's Summary

Barry Carbaugh is the founder of Barry Group and Bizazzle.com, two firms focused totally on your success. Our passion is differentiating you from your competition so that you can better accomplish your dreams. If you need full service where we manage much of your marketing, public relations and social media program...Barry Group is the answer. If you want to do-it-yourself...Bizazzle.com is where you should start.

Either way you will get the success you have been searching for.

Our capabilities include:
* Website Development.
* Social Media Management featuring BizRep, our dynamic tool for keeping you in touch with your online reputation.
* Promotional Products that support your brand and help build your business.
* Spokesperson videos. This cost effective tool differentiates your business and adds a personal touch to your site.
* Traditional media. Radio, TV, billboards and print. We buy it and create the communication that will generate results.

Awards
1998 Ernst and Young Entrepreneur of the Year Award.
Clio Award for consumer packaging design.
Numerous Addys and creative awards.
Numerous community service awards.

Specialties: Website development, video production, promotional products. public relations, social media, reputation management, brand development, quantitative and qualitative research, strategic planning and media buying,writing and creative for all forms of media.
