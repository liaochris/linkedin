Charlie Morgan's Summary

I am an independent, freelance, drummer/producer/composer, with over 40 years experience in the Music Industry. My credits include sessions for Paul McCartney, Tina Turner, and dozens of top artists, including a 13 year stint with Elton John, both in the studio (6 albums), and on tour.

My drumming is on 50+ gold and 30+ platinum albums.

I now run a home-based studio facility from my house in Nashville, Tennessee, where I record drum tracks for many clients worldwide. This includes such luminaries as producer/arranger/writer Scott Erickson. Together we have recorded tracks for 3 of the biggest names in Mexican Pop music: Manuel Mijares, Yuri and a recent album for young talent, Alexander Acha.

I am a regular with Orleans (www.orleansonline.com), both in the studio and live, as well as a number of other artists, on a freelance basis.

Specialties: drumming, production, engineering, composition, mixing
