Jamie Glover-Dabbs' Summary

While working at the Houston Museum of Natural Science, I took time to learn to be a Bug Chef. I learned the recipe for Chocolate Chirpie Chip Cookies, Ants on a Log, Chocolate Grasshopper Delight, and Cicada Nacho Grande.

Changing these creepy crawlies into edible masterpieces made my wheels turn. I was able to get the passersby interested in the entirety of the process - from ingredient selection to the life cycle of the insect.

Crickets, ants, and cicadas are just busy, buzzing bugs in your garden until you make people see them in a whole new light. In essence, being a Bug Chef for me was similar to marketing the entire field of entymology.

My goal is to make your product or service unforgettable - whether it is through nontraditional marketing, social media, event planning, or strategic development. You'll see - you can have your Cricket Cake - and eat it too!

Specialties
