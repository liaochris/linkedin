Dennis J. Cagan's Summary

Shadow CEO™,Hi-tech Consultant,Chairman of the Board,Board Member,Director,Mentor,C-Level Interim Executive,Technology Visionary,CEO,Entrepreneur

Dennis is currently active as Executive Chairman of the Board at Copper Mobile, Inc. and also serving as the Chief Sales and Marketing Officer. He continues to serve as CEO of his consulting firm, Caganco Incorporated. He has been an successful entrepreneur for many years, having founded over a dozen different companies. He has been a C-level executive, investor, professional board member (48 boards) and consultant. He has served in dozens of interim-C-level assignments.

In 1979 Dennis was the keynote speaker at the first COMDEX Show, in Las Vegas.

In 2011 Dennis was inducted into the IT Hall of Fame - Channel Section (administered by CompTIA).

Mr. Cagan owns the USPTO Trademark for 'Shadow CEO'.

From June - November 2011 Dennis was interim President and CEO of Encryptics dba of NL Systems LLC. Dennis led the introduction of new technology - secure content management/encryption technology, to the marketplace and dramatically increased enterprise value.

From 2009-2010 Dennis assumed the interim CEO role at SureBooks LLC after raising $2.4 MM in capital for the firm with a Reg D offering.

Dennis moved to the Dallas area in 2006 when he became Chairman, Chief Executive Officer and President of TWL Corporation (TWLO.OB). At that time TWL was a leading provider of integrated distance learning solutions.

Dennis was the founder, Chairman and CEO of the Santa Barbara Technology Group, LLC. SBTG is a private investment firm and incubator working with, and investing in many early-stage technology companies.

Dennis is currently on 5 Boards of Directors.

He attended UCLA where he majored in economics. He has an honorable discharge from the USMC. Dennis and his wife Angelia live in Plano, TX.

Google "dennis cagan", visit www.caganco.com, or call at (805) 570-1000 or dennis@caganco.com.

Specialties

Augmenting and helping the leadership in high-tech companies with his 45 yrs experience to create enterprise value. Broad and deep skill set in founding, organizing, building scaling, technology-based enterprises. Expert at small company boards. Other specifics include: limited budgets, high-level team building, sales, marketing, business development, corporate governance, strategic alliances and liquidity/exit planning.
