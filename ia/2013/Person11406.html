Heather Deatz's Summary

I am the owner and produce the annual Baby Kid Expo event in St. Louis, MO. This is a free event for the community and brings information on education, healthcare, activities, products and services all together in one place. I am responsible for setting up the event space, selling the sponsorships and exhibit spaces, and researching and booking the entertainment. I also market the event which includes tv, radio, print, billboards, social media and more. It is an honor to bring this event to the community. Our Mission is to provide the best opportunity for businesses to showcase their products and services in a fun, exciting, family focused venue while supporting those in need in our community. We attract more than 3,000 attendees every year.

Our next event is on May 10, 2014 at the St. Charles Convention Center. Check out our website www.babykidexpo.com and sign up to be a sponsor or exhibitor today!

I am also a sales representative for the Community News. The Community News has been bringing the good news to the St. Charles and North County communities for over 90 years. Our weekly publiations are free and are available in over 600 newsstand and can be read online at www.mycnews.com My territory includes St. Peters and North O'Fallon.
