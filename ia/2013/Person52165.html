Gayla D'Gaia's Summary

Hi! I'm Gayla D'Gaia and I'm the founder and artist behind HeArts for a Cause, a non-profit organization bringing art and free online resources to communities rebuilding and recovering from environmental crisis events. I am an artist, healer, and guide into the world of the sacred. I am also the creator of The Sacred Beauty Project, an online community that focuses purely on our relationship with the sacred in our lives, across all formal and informal spiritual practices. Soon after founding The Sacred Beauty Project, I developed my own mentorship process and became the founder of Live Your True Dream™, life coaching creating a shift in women’s ability to perceive the presence of their true self in their everyday lives and helping them to create a life they love and dream of living. Upon creating online courses for my clients, I've witnessed in a short time, them all having significant breakthroughs and improving the quality of their lives.

Recently, I designed my own visioning studio in order to express and expose the intricate details of designing HeArts for a Cause. HeArts for a Cause is an art and spiritual living project to accentuate a heart focused lifestyle for communities impacted by environmental crisis. It includes the uplifting practice of generosity to those communities devoted to renewing their lives following sudden environmentally created crisis events.

Although the fine arts medium is newly (or rather renewing itself as) a part of my life, my work will be shown and sold at One Block East Gallery in Roanoke, Virginia and talks are underway to have my art placed in The Taubman Museum of Art, also in Roanoke.

Visit me at www.HeArtsforaCause.org to view my art and Join the Movement!
