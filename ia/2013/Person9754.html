Arne Evertsson's Summary

App Director, Product Owner, Software Developer and Tester in a wide variety of business applications. Focused on creating highly usable and maintainable software. Interested in projects with very high quality standards that leverages Agile methodology. I love the craft of software development.

What is an "App Director"? I think the role of the film director applies to software development projects. The App Director takes the script (= domain knowledge, business idea) and turns it into a real working product collaborating closely with many different kinds of professionals. Check out my blog post: http://arneevertsson.blogspot.se/2012/09/the-app-director.html

Other word sometimes used for the above roles include: Business Analyst, Requirements Engineer, Functional Architect, Software Architect, Solutions Architect, Interaction Designer

Specialities: Application design, business/requirements analysis, software architecture, interaction design, test and implementation in Java, Objective-C, and JavaScript for web, iOS and Android.
