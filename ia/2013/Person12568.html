Becky Chambers' Summary

I'm a freelance writer with a love for stories and science. I'm always interested in new projects, and am especially keen to hear from people in need of creative writing or story collaboration. Tech or software related work is also happily received. I work with people all over the world, right from my home office. Relocating is an option.

I write weekly video game commentary for The Mary Sue, a geek culture site with a female focus (gents are welcome, too). My work centers around narrative analysis, the gamer community, and good ol' fashioned reviews. Some of my pieces have been mentioned on sites such as BBC News, Forbes, Boing Boing, and Gamasutra.

I am currently contracted part-time with GoPro, an independent software vendor that specializes in case management solutions. Their clients include local governments, financial institutions, and businesses of all sizes. As they are continuing to expand into the global market, having a native English writer on hand has been useful.

I have recently completed my first science fiction novel, The Long Way to a Small, Angry Planet. The manuscript is on submission with publishers, and is represented by Joe Monti of Barry Goldblatt Literary. My writing time for the initial draft was funded via Kickstarter in April 2012.
