Jennifer Covello's Summary

Jennifer Covello is The Purposeful Parent. As a parenting change agent, she helps mothers with the business of parenting. In her blog, Parenting for Purpose, she shares the lessons she has learned along her parenting journey and how her experience can help other moms avoid and overcome the challenges of parenting today.

Jennifer is a frequent contributor and blogger for various online magazines including the Museum of Motherhood’s MaMaZiNa blog and most recently, "Market Mommy" where she shares her insights on being a momprenur. She was also a contributing author to “Moms to Motherhood”, a Gratitude Project book, and "Mom, Inc".


Ms. Covello is also the owner and founderof Frittabello baby gifts. She has authored and published an award-winning baby journal, “My Life”, a children’s book, “Frittabello and his Angel” and developed a line of baby gift accessories.


She has been featured on local and regional radio and TV programs for her insights about her journey from corporate “cubicle dweller” to “mompreneur” as a divorced, full-time working parent. Most recently she was featured on Good Morning Connecticut’s Weekend Edition speaking about “SuperMom Syndrome”.

Jennifer is a native of Long Island, New York and had a corporate career in IT and Marketing. She holds a B.S. degree in Management Information Systems from Indiana University of Pennsylvania and an MBA in Marketing Management from Pace University, New York.

Ms. Covello, MBA, is a marketing professional with expertise in brand and product management, B2B marketing communications, social media, and small business marketing.

Specialties: Author, mompreneur, speaker, parenting change agent.
