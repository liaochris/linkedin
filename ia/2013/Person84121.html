Maeve Crawford's Summary

The Soul Mate Catalyst, Love Visionary, Relationship Angel.

Qualified Dating and Relationship Coach for Singles, Professional Member of The Relationship Coaching Institute, Certified Relationship Skills Trainer for Teenagers, Qualified Parent Coach.

Empowering mature singles to prepare for the relationship of their dreams by developing the critical skills and inner confidence to attract their soul mate and create their ideal relationship.

Maeve has a vision of a world where Education is based on loving principles and no longer a hotbed of assignments, tests and squashing creativity. Believing that love is the force that nurtures and allows us to thrive as our true selves, Education based on Relationships, Community, Love, Quality, Honesty and Creativity is the way to transform the world, one student at a time.

Being a Relationship Angel, Maeve opens the hearts and souls of those she works with to allow them to dig deep within and tap into the loving beings they are, so they can live the life they were born to live, with the person they were destined to share it with.

"I believe everyone deserves to love and be loved and the enrichment of our lives is dependent on the quality of our relationships.

Having personal experience of the aftermath of relationship breakdown I have an exceptional ability to empathise whilst guiding people towards their goals."

Specialties:
- Creator of 5-Step "Soul Mate Attraction Relationship Training" programme.
- Bespoke relationship coaching programmes for mature individuals.
- Group workshops and retreats for singles to uncover their internal saboteur and breakthrough to reveal their true self and know what to do to attract their soul mate / twin flame.
- Relationship mentoring for parents and teens. Healing the transition between childhood and adulthood.
- Parenting programmes in schools and colleges.
Corporate consultation to improve professional relationships in the work place.
