Hey! My name is Michael Lorin Friedman. I provide freelance services in medical communications and non profit/business communications (specializing in social media).

Medical Communications:
I have experience in medical communications for consumers and health care professional audiences. Health advocacy and communications are my passion, and allow me to tackle complex medical communications projects for a variety of medical writers and organizations.

Here's the kind of medical communications projects I've been involved in:

•online marketing for health care consumers
•medical journal manuscript writing and editing
•articles for magazines and newsletters
•health advoacy email marketing
•editing and proofreading for all media
•press releases and media relations
•web copy for health care professionals

Specialties include: HIV Treatment, dermatology, aesthetic surgery, aesthetic dermatology, non profit advocacy, health care policy and advocacy, social marketing, and media relations.

I also provide virtual assistant services to those in medical communications, working remotely to provide online research, proofreading and fact checking, citation organization, copywriting, WordPress websites, social media services, and more.

Non Profit/ Business Communications:
