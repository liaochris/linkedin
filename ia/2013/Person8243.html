Ann Babiarz, PCC, CPC, ELI-MP, CEIC's Summary

Ann is dedicated to empowering individuals to reach their fullest potential, believing in the power of thought, intention and fun to change lives. She believes that individuals have the power to create their stories one thought at a time. She is dedicated to supporting her clients in removing the accumulation of dust on the stack of self-help books and programs in the corners of their homes and offices by helping them to put them to use! Through her work as a coach and a the host of “Life Outside the Box”, she helps individuals to transform globally (from the inside-out) to achieve lasting change, which allows them to chart their desired course to living the lives that they aspire to live.

Ann’s diverse background in the financial (she held 3 NASD securities licenses), and fitness industries connects her with a broad client base, providing them with a myriad of perspectives and insights. Ann’s clients experience lasting change, which allows them to re-focus attention, beliefs, and perceptions to support their success rather than inhibiting it; and to create win-win situations that free them of unnecessary conflict both personally and professionally.

As an advocate of learning as a lifelong practice, Ann is continuing post-graduate work in Metaphysics. Aligned with her passion for human potential and drawing upon 20 plus years of experience in the financial service and fitness fields, Ann holds the following certifications, designations, and licenses:

• PCC-International Coach Federation
• Board Member/Membership Co-Chair-Chicago Chapter of the (ICF)
• Certified Professional Coach-(iPEC)
• Master Practitioner-Energy Leadership Index -(iPEC)
• Certified Emotional Intelligence Coach-(iPEC)
• Founding Ambassador-Institute of Professional Empowerment Coaching
• NASD Securities Licenses 7, 63, 66
• American Council on Exercise Certified Personal Trainer (previously held)
• B.S. Metaphysics-American Institute of Holistic Theology
