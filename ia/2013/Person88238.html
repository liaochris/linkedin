Mary Zachar's Summary

30 years of experience in California as a general business lawyer, with expertise in civil litigation and Intellectual Property. Areas of practice embrace all aspects of IP rights acquisition and protection, including assignment and licensing,
copyright and trademark registrations, option and asset-purchase agreements, releases and permissions, idea submission, confidentiality and nondisclosure agreements,
work-for-hire and employment agreements, web development including terms of use and privacy agreements, corporate formation, LLC Operating, partnership and joint venture agreements, literary agreements, film production agreements, music related agreements including synch/performance/master use and mechanical licensing, litigation related to business torts and infringements involving patents, trademarks, copyrights, trade-secrets, right-of–publicity, misappropriation and unfair competition. Certified Mediator; Admitted to practice in California and Indiana.
