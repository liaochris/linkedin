John Van Dinther's Projects

  • The Peace Exchange

    • January 2008 to Present
    Team Members: John Van Dinther, Ross Holzman, Laura Aleo, Jeffrey Bean, Oren Jaffe, Molly Rowan Leach

    • The Peace Exchange is an international exchange of art and messages of peace using between children around the world. During our two-hour workshops, students share their ideas on peace, what it means to create peace our lives, and then making a work of art and share a message of peace with a similarly aged student in another part of the world. We hold moments of silence and experience peace through breathing practices, then use art and writing to share our hearts wisdom on a 6 x 8 inch postcard. Students each give and receive a personally create peace postcard as part of The Peace Exchange.
