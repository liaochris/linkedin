John McFarland's Projects

  • Appellate Win for University General Hospital - Erasing Lost Profits Award

    • May 2011 to April 2013
    Team Members: John McFarland, Ben Wickert, Murry Cohen

    The 14th Court of Appeals reformed a judgment rendered against John McFarland’s client University General Hospital to strike the $2.1 million award for future lost profits award awarded to the plaintiff, Prexus. Through breach of contract and related claims, Prexus sought approximately $22 million in damages. The trial court awarded just $745,000 in past damages and the now-struck $2.1 million in lost profits; Prexus took nothing on its other claims. Ben Wickert tried the case with John and, along with Akin Gump’s Murry Cohen, assisted with the appeal.

  • Another Reverse and Render Appellate Win for JM+M for client ORIX Capital Markets - $10 Million Judgment

    • July 2013 to Present
    Team Members: John McFarland, Ben Wickert, nicola hobeiche

    Another appellate win for JM+M! John McFarland and Ben Wickert secured another big appellate win, this time for the firm’s client ORIX Capital Markets. The JM+M team and ORIX’s in-house counsel Nicola Hobeiche secured a judgment for the lender for over $10 million against the guarantors of a defaulted multifamily housing loan. The claims, brought by ORIX as special servicer for Wells Fargo, were tried in the 164th District Court in Harris County. The judgment rendered by the 14th Court of Appeals represents a complete win for the client.
