Eric Pollack's Summary

We are Professional Recruiters dedicated to Retained Executive Search specific to the Soilid State Lighting, LED and the related LED Semiconductor Industry.

Utilizing our proprietary search methodology with an unwavering commitment to excellence we identify, recruit and place Executives, Vice Presidents, Directors, Managers, and Key Individual Contributors in Management, Science / Technology (Material Science), Product Development, Engineering, Applications Engineering, Sales and Marketing with the leading solid state lighting companies worldwide.

With a 25 year history in Executive Search; today we place the Managing Executives for clients throughout the SSL industry.

Our client's are the Dominating Companies of Industry combined with the cutting edge start ups of tomorrow.

Professionals who excel in SSL Science, Device Development, Product Development, Engineering, Product Marketing, Sales and Marketing excellence are encouraged to confidentially align with our firm.

We fulfill assignments worldwide with proven success in United States, Canada, Europe and the Far East.
