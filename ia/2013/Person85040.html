Marcia Hughes' Summary

PROFESSIONAL EXPERIENCE:
President and CEO of Collaborative Growth, L.L.C., Marcia serves as a strategic communications partner for health care and other organizations world-wide. As a master facilitator, trainer and coach, her focus is to provide consulting which results in lasting strategic action. Marcia works with organizations as an international expert in emotional intelligence, leadership development, conflict resolution, communications and team development. She is admitted to the Emotional Intelligence Consortium, which facilitates sharing research on emotional intelligence across the world. She is co-author of The Handbook for Developing Emotional Intelligence (2009), A Facilitator's Guide to Team Emotional and Social Intelligence, (2009), A Coach's Guide to Emotional Intelligence (2008), The Emotionally Intelligent Team (2007), and Emotional Intelligence in Action (2005) and author of Life's 2% Solution (2006). Marcia and her partner, James Terrell are authors of The Team Emotional & Social Intelligence Survey® (TESI®), an online team assessment. Marcia practiced law for over 20 years, operating her own successful law firm, which focused on complex public policy matters. Ms. Hughes offers emotional intelligence training and coaching and is a certified trainer in using the TESI®, EQ-i® and the EQ-360®.

Before entering private practice Ms. Hughes worked with governmental and public interest organizations. She served as a special assistant to the Executive Director of the Department of Public Health and the Environment and as an Assistant Attorney General. She clerked on the 10th Circuit Court of Appeals for the Honorable William E. Doyle and served with the Environmental Protection Agency in Washington, D.C.

Specialties: Leadership and team development, organizational development, conflict resolution, facilitation for boards, elected officials and governments, negotiation, training, workshops and keynotes
