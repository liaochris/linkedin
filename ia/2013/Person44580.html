Luke Ferrone, MBA, PMP®'s Summary

Mr. Ferrone is strong and visionary technology executive with over 20 years of experience advising, leading and delivering strategic initiatives for some of the most recognizable enterprises in the world. He had led, both from an IT and business perspective, strategic programs in sales and marketing (CRM & SFA), supply chain, operations, healthcare (EHR), research & development (PLM) as a global IT executive reporting to both CIOs and other “C” level, executives. Mr. Ferrone has also consulted, at a senior level, on mergers & acquisitions and divestitures in industries as diverse as automotive, consumer packaged goods, healthcare, and high tech. He has been successful in highly decentralized companies (ABB) and highly centralized companies (GM & Ford) as well as large and small enterprises.

His industry experience includes healthcare (Integrated Medicine Alliance – IMA, FTI Consulting), telecom (AT&T & Alcatel/Lucent), Engineering (Asea, Brown, Boveri - ABB), Automotive (General Motors - GM), Chemical Manufacturing (Ecolab), management consulting (FTI Palladium Partners, Equanimity Consulting). He is an expert at building high performing teams that bring together both business and technology associates, to deliver game changing results to forward looking enterprises.

In his career, Luke has worked as a software developer (UNIX), system administrator (UNIX & Windows), project manager, program manager, systems sales consultant (voice/data integration), Tier 3 technical consultant, IT manager, IT director, information officer, global IT process owner, IT business relationship executive, management consultant, interim CIO, and father of two beautiful children. In a few words, he has been there and done that.

Luke is passionate about bringing out the best of his associates and developing their innate skills. If you are looking for an individual to make an impact to the business, you should take a close look at Mr. Ferrone.
