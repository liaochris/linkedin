Beth Borés' Experience

Founder and Image consultant

Personal Image Company

Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Professional Training & Coaching industry

April 2011 – Present (2 years 10 months) Alpharetta

We live in a fast moving world where, like it or not, we make snap decisions of others based on associations we assume from the outward characteristics and the invisible inner qualities we believe them to reflect. Therefore the perception of others can make or break careers, concepts and organizations.
 
As an Image Consultant, my aim is to make you aware of who you are (your goals, your lifestyle, what you want and expect out of life) and match it with the “YOU” you project to the world. You will have a guide to what others may be deciding about you, based on the way you choose to project yourself.
 
Looking your best through the clothes that best suit your body proportions and life style, the colors that best look on you and help you launch the message you want to be caught, a hairstyle and make up that harmonize with your face shape and style, and managing your behavior and manners in every situation, as giving the same message with your body and with your words. All these elements are essential to building a strong self esteem and confidence. Looking your best makes you feel good, and when you feel good, you can accomplish more. The way you see yourself, but more important, the way you feel about yourself, is the very projection of how others will see you.
 
However, deeper than your outside appearance is your inner soul. The innermost part of you must feel good. Your body will function at its best if you have your internal and external image in balance.

  • A STYLE ANALYSIS WITH PERSONAL IMAGE COMPANY

    • 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Beth Borés


    An STYLE ANALYSIS consultation with PERSONAL IMAGE COMPANY begins with the assessment of the different areas and characteristics of your body. With this information, I will create your personalized "My Personal Style Solutions" program, which will complete all the missing pieces of your style puzzle. It will cover everything you need to know about looking great and shop wisely, as well as save you Frustration, Time, Money and the Insecurity that comes from feeling dressed less than your best.

    Your “My Personal Style Solutions”portfolio is specifically created for you and it covers the following areas:

    - Figure Analysis, according to your measurements and observations. These create a precise picture of your body shape and personal characteristics, from which are made detailed and accurate style recommendations.

    - Style Selections. Based on your figure analysis, your portfolio will give you specific style recommendations that will enhance your physical appearance.
    In developing the YOUR PERSONAL STYLE SOLUTIONS PROGRAM, every possible combination of height, weight, bone structure, age, prominent feature, vertical and horizontal body proportions and many other features of a client’s body are considered in order to evaluate if a specific style or feature of a garment is going to flatter or detract from their overall appearance. This program is a complete body and style analysis in one complete unit.

    "My Personal Style Solutions" will cover everything: from shoes to hats, lingerie to coats, coordination to garment shape. Nothing is left out.

    A Style Guide Book is the visual accompaniment to Your Personal Style Solutions portfolio printout to assist you at the shopping center.
