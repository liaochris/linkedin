Amanda Donaldson's Summary

GOOD DESIGN
+ is strengthened by a strong rationale
+ connects well with its audience
+ is smart and strategic
+ is creative and unexpected
+ is inspired by intuition, users and research
+ is ethical and respectful to all stakeholders
+ makes the world a better place

Amanda aspires to enrich the world with good design and encourages others to do likewise.

Amanda has been independently designing in various facets of branding and strategic communications since 2007. Her aim is to work with talent rich, interdisciplinary teams while leveraging her background as a creative problem solver and design thinker.

The past few years have allowed her to collaborate on a varied assortment of projects, each with the common focus of designing exceptional communication, identifying customer need, business opportunity, and brand positioning. She accomplishes this with careful design research and committed, collaborative creation.

Amanda has over 5 years experience in the creative field, with the majority of her efforts spent in branding, strategic communications and marketing. Amanda has been privileged to work with the likes of MIT Enterprise Forum, Ashoka Canada, Honda Canada, Labatt, YUM! (Pizza Hut, KFC), Amsterdam Fashion Institute, Art of Fashion and D Magazine.

In her work for wayfinding and environmental graphic design, Amanda created environmental graphic design for interiors and architecture, branded assets, information design and storytelling for corporate, municipal, government, health care and academic clients.

Amanda has moved back to the USA from Amsterdam with her husband, interior design magazines and paper collection. She espouses all things sustainable fashion and blogs about her travel experiences and design thoughts in her spare time.

Specialties: design strategy, brand standards, ux/ui, project management, art direction, visual communications, typography, photography, sustainable fashion, traveling the world!
