Michael R. Davidson's Publications

  • HARRY'S RULES

    • MRD Enterprises
    • August 3, 2012
    Authors: Michael R. Davidson

    HARRY'S RULES is an espionage thriller set during the chaotic period following the fall of the Soviet Union. When HARRY CONNOLLY is dispatched to investigate the mysterious death of a CIA officer in Austria he discovers a terrible secret. In the wake of bloody clashes with a ruthless Russian revanchist organization, Harry finds himself an international pariah, wanted for murder by law enforcement, hunted by Russian thugs, and unable any longer to trust the CIA. He is forced to throw in his lot with an unexpected ally as he draws ever closer to a climactic showdown with an implacable enemy.

  • INCUBUS

    • Legacy Publishers
    • December 8, 2012
    Authors: Michael R. Davidson

    Latest review from Amazon.com: In my opinion, former CIA officer Michael Davidson's latest novel INCUBUS is even better than HARRY'S RULES, his debut novel, which I thoroughly enjoyed. In his latest tale, Davidson brings back Ewan Ramsey, a badass version of James Bond who runs amok on several continents with his beautiful sidekick. There's nothing better than conspiracy theories, especially when they might be true. INCUBUS has all of this and more. When I read a David Baldacci or Olen Steinhauer thriller, I have to force myself to disregard the implausibility of their plots and the obvious fact that the authors are writing about topics of which they know nothing. Not so with Michael Davidson. INCUBUS literally bleeds with his lengthy experience as a covert operative. I would love to read Michael's autobiography. I give INCUBUS five stars and recommend it without reservation.
