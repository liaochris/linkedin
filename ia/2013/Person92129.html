My profession is leadership and diversity coach, mentor coach, leadership development, organization development and diversity consulting, writing, and teaching. With background as executive and manager in U.S. government agencies; economist; university professor-researcher. My life’s work is based in the values of love, intimacy, connection, social justice, and life-long learning.

My clients are individuals and organizations.

• Individual coaching clients are executives and managers, diversity leaders, and organization change leaders. They are committed to learning, changing, and growing, having lives which are fully-alive, rich, and fulfilling. My coaching practice with them addresses their whole being; includes enhancing leadership skills and presence; being diversity and inclusion leaders; having a career path of heart and passion; balancing family/work/life; overcoming life hurdles; and pursuing the hopes, dreams, and conditions of being human.

• Organization clients include business, government, education, and unions, professional associations, foundations, and non-profit organizations....and my practice with them includes: leadership and diversity coaching, leadership development, diversity and inclusion, strategic organizational change, team development, strategic planning, conflict mediation, mission, vision, values work, and professional development of leaders, coaches, and diversity and organization development practitioners.

• Mentor Coach for coaches on their coaching journey: ICF core coaching competencies for ICF credentialing; listen to coaching sessions and provide feedback on ICF core coaching competencies; client case/situational support with client issues and challenges; support for developing coaching education plans and processes.

Specialties

The Leadership and Diversity Coach™; leadership development; strategic organizational change; team development; diversity, inclusion, and social justice; strategic planning; conflict mediation; mission, vision, values work; organization assessment and design; facilitating groups, retreats and conferences; and professional development and professional coach for leaders, executives, managers, coaches, diversity leaders and practitioners, and OD practitioners.
