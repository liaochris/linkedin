Francisco Espinosa's Summary

Mr. Espinosa has been a driving force behind many of the innovations that have made his employers leaders in the industry; helping to develop, create, and maintain the exceptional quality of service & products. Mr. Espinosa has been responsible for overseeing the daily management of Production, Creative, and IT staff, video studios, creative design teams, editing suites, hiring & training of new editors, production & project creation, product and company research, and network maintenance & planning.
