The Bridge to Health, SC

September 2005 – Present (8 years 4 months) Green Bay, Wisconsin Area

I have worked in nursing since 1976 and have been a full-time nurse practitioner since 1995. After treating patients in a primary care setting (internal medicine) for the first five years as a nurse practitioner, my practice shifted toward headache and chronic pain management in a large multi-specialty clinic neurology department. Over the years, I have seen how traditional medicine outcomes can be enhanced by therapies that may be considered non-traditional or complementary. I have also learned how nutrition, our minds and our body’s energy can influence our own healing abilities and general well-being. My interests led me to pursue additional education and training in several integrative medicine modalities, including nutritional medicine, mind-body techniques and energy medicine.

In 2005, I went into private practice so I could focus more on the complementary techniques and spend more time with my clients.

At The Bridge to Health, I focus on nutrition, stress management, mind-body and energy medicine. I help people who want more out of life, to achieve the health, happiness and abundance they deserve. The Bridge to Health went virtual in 2012.

Publishing:
In July of 2011, I published my first book, "The Choice is Ours- Five Keys to Health, Happiness and Abundance". 2012: "Claim Your Joy-How Thoughts and Feelings Create Our Reality" e-book available on Amazon Kindle. I coach individuals on writing, publishing and marketing their e-books on Amazon Kindle.

I have a weekly radio show and wellness blog to help people discover tools and strategies to achieve the life they want. I offer virtual membership and coaching programs, sessions and classes are offered for individuals as well as groups.

My book, radio show, guided meditation audios, blog, webinars, and membership programs help me reach people all across the globe. This new career path helps me touch the lives of people everywhere.
