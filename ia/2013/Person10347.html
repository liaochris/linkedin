Ashley Truscott | Marketing Consultant's Summary

As a marketing consultant and Managing Director of marketing consultancy Waterfall Marketing, I work with a broad range of clients from a large auditing firm and management, strategy & leadership consultancies to non-profit organisations and executive search firms. I also write case studies for a leading South African Business School.

My work encompasses the following:

- developing & implementing marketing strategies
- managing the graduate recruitment marketing function for a large auditing firm;
- writing & production of newsletters, company reviews, capability statements, feature articles, media releases
- writing case studies for a leading business school
- set up and management of social media marketing tools
- website content

Companies hire me for one of the following reasons:

■ Their company is small and they do not have the resources for a full time marketing team; therefore it makes sense to use an external marketing consultant
■ To develop a marketing strategy that the company's marketing team can implement, with Ashley Truscott's guidance
■ To work with them in the implementation of their marketing strategy
■ To develop a strategic Social Media marketing plan that integrates with their other marketing initiatives
■ To help establish a credible brand with their specific target market/s
■ To write and / or edit copy for a variety of marketing tools: websites, brochures, company profiles, etc.

I have an Honours BA degree in English and Communications.

Common mis-spellings of my name: Ashleigh Truscott, Ashlea Truscott, Ashlee Truscott, Ashley Trustcott, Ashley Trusscott, Ashley Truscot

Specialties: Marketing strategy (development and implementation), writing (brochures, websites, newsletters, media releases), marketing consulting, internal and external communication strategies.
