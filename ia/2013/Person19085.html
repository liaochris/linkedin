B. Wayne 'BW' Barkley's Experience

Co-Founder & EVP of Strategic Finance

Vergence Entertainment

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Entertainment industry

July 2006 – Present (7 years 3 months) Greater New Orleans Area

As Co-Founder of F-H-S Legacy Corporation’s affiliate company Vergence Entertainment LLC, a Glendale-based gamification company, BW is a key originator of Vergence's premier product offering, www.ASKPlatform.com.

Founded in 2006, Vergence is a software company leading a trend known as gamification using entertainment as an education and training tool. We license our multi-patented real-time game technology for employee post-training retention in heavily regulated industries, like energy and healthcare. Companies previously did not have a way to measure the effectiveness of their training programs. Now they can. Our gamification solution keeps participants interested (and incented) but not distracted. Clients are billion-dollar private and public companies.

BW has led the Vergence team in the raising of millions of private 'retail' dollars via hundreds of transactions from private investors spanning from Hawaii to Bermuda including farmers, doctors, non-profit owners, architects, government officials, with many investors holding impressive career positions such as VP of Nippon Ichi Software, a radio-talk-show host, and a former VP of Citibank, N.A. to name just a few.

Vergence practices “paying it forward” by placing investors at gross-earnings and first-to-earn levels, in multiple-earning tiers with anti-dilution protection, in collateralized and secured positions as well as Trustee-controlled receivables financing and in programs that 'match and exceed' invested monies.

Co-Founder & Chief Strategic Finance Officer (CSFO)

F-H-S Legacy Corporation

Think Tanks industry

July 2001 – Present (12 years 3 months) Southern California: Los Angeles

BW is one of four men who founded F-H-S in 2001. Combining a young, aggressive management style with an uncommonly seasoned standard of excellence, BW attributes his enterprising spirit to his roots in the Heartland City of Wichita, Kansas. He was born into an entrepreneurial family starting from his great grandfather who opened and sold more than 160 diners throughout the Midwest. Wichita is known as an entrepreneurial haven where BW has drawn the financial interest of renowned CEOs of various industries, including hotel, restaurant and theatre chains and oil/gas, to participate in his expanding developments in media convergence.

BW has led the F-H-S team in the raising of millions of dollars from private investors since 2003.

Recognizing Twitter’s ability to reach a $1 billion valuation prior to having a revenue model in place, and realizing that a video-game can generate $500 million in its first week of release (verses $150M for the biggest blockbuster movies), BW is committed to bringing awareness to the investment community at large about new trends and shifts -- as well as to deepen, expand and ultimately share his company's financial successes and 'know how' with others. For example: F-H-S authorized BW to form Octava Corporation to provide consulting services to high-risk ventures desirous of mitigating risks for their investors through the utilization of a Trust-Linked PPM (owned and initially developed by F-H-S).
