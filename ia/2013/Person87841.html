Marty Bass' Summary

I am the owner of Brothers Bedding, which was established in 1938. I purchased Brothers Bedding in 1982 and have expanded the company to where it is today. My goal is to make the best mattress at the best price, period!

Specialties

We specialize in standard and custom sized innerspring mattresses and all foam mattresses. We offer Twin, Full, Queen, and King mattresses and can do custom sizes for any bed. We also have memory foam and latex foam mattresses. We can ship anywhere in the U.S.
