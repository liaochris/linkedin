Matt Potts' Projects

  • ISIS Programme

    • September 2010 to Present
    Team Members: Matt Potts, Matthew Deacon, Alexander Rowe, Ed Lockett, Sarah Mills, Chris Muir, Tom Haddleton

    ISIS aims to raise levels of ICT investment across the region by providing objective advice, guidance and appropriate ICT solutions to SMEs (Small & Medium Enterprises) throughout the North-West, supporting business changes to minimise cost and maximise the benefits from ICT, both now and in the future.

    The project has achieved a target of assisting 290 businesses, with 86% showing improved performance; contributing to the creation of 175 jobs and the safeguarding of 900 more positions in the region.

    ISIS is part of a series of such projects delivered by the Knowledge Business Centre, based in InfoLab21 and is the pilot for “Transformational ICT”, part of the Government’s Solutions for Business portfolio, a highly targeted, publicly funded suite of products and services offering solutions to real business needs
