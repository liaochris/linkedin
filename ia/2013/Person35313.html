Diane Bean's Experience

Professional Voice Over Artist

Diane Bean - Voice Talent

July 2006 – Present (7 years 6 months)

Voice Overs for Television and Radio Advertising, Telephone Menus, On-Hold Messages, IVR, Corporate Video, Business Presentations, Training Videos, Website Demos, Narrations, Podcasts, and more.

I offer immediate delivery of voice files from my professional home studio via email or FTP, in the format of your choice to anywhere in the world. You have my guarantee of clean, high-quality audio, delivered promptly and with a smile!

My studio and talents have met the criteria of the Society of Accredited Voice Over Artists (SaVoa). From the SaVoa website: "Accreditation by SaVoa is recognition of a voice over artist's ability to provide vocally and technically proficient broadcast-quality voice over services and to conduct business in a manner that enhances the profession as a whole."

Voice Over Talent, On-Air Presenter, Newscaster

Various Radio Stations, Production Studios, and Advertisers

1988 – 2005 (17 years)

Production Director

WJJB-FM

December 1992 – August 1997 (4 years 9 months)

Responsible for all aspects of audio production including commercials, promos, PSAs, special features & broadcasts. Assigned announcers for spots and provided copy-writing services. Worked closely with the Account Managers and Traffic Department to ensure final productions were ready for on-air deadlines.

Voiced hundreds of commercials, promos & PSAs. Selected music and sound effects, edited and mixed the majority of station production. Compiled and recorded features such as health segments and event and entertainment news.

Also worked as an on-air presenter, newscaster and interviewer.
