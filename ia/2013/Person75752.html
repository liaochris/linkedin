Kim Hartmayer's Summary

Kim Hartmayer is a Certified Professional CoActive Coach, internationally trained leadership trainer, and professional speaker. With over 20-years’ experience in communications-related fields, including 10 years in pharmaceutical sales and marketing.
As a dynamic, energetic speaker, Kim immediately engages her audience with her quick wit and down-to-earth style. She empowers her audience to stretch and step more powerfully into their lives and professions. She currently speaks on:
• Overwhelm: A perspective we choose.
• A New Age of Leadership: What does it take to be a leader today?
• Moving Through Perceived Limitations: When the road doesn’t rise up to meet you.
• Present Yourself: A guide to powerful networking and relationship building.
• Who Are YOU Becoming? And what gets in the way?

Her programs lead participants through a journey. As they internalize key points they discover how to use their own life experiences, perceived limitations, hopes and dreams to create true change.
“It’s one thing to tell a story,” Kim says, “and quite another to give someone tools to rewrite their own.” Kim speaks at both large and small gatherings and tailors her presentations to meet her audience’s specific needs.

Coaching is Kim’s passion and she guides and supports executives and entrepreneurs while they break through old patterns. In the process they become empowered to move forward into greater success and stronger business relationships. As a leadership trainer Kim helps her clients improve their communication and leadership skills, thus enabling them to inspire their teams and create unified work environments.

She has worked with clients from across the globe including the United States, Tokyo, Istanbul, Barcelona, Amsterdam, Norway and Dubai. Kim also owns “Who Are YOU Becoming?” Executive Coaching and Training.

Specialties

Kim is a masterful communicator who specializes in clear communication skills, relationship building, inspired action, planning and accountability.
