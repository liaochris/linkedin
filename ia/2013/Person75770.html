Kim Imel's Summary

Peak Transaction Services was established to assist Realtors and lenders with transaction faciltation and marketing. The goal of the services provided is to allow the agents and the lenders to get away from their desks and spend valuable face to face time with clients. In an ever changing industry, the services offered have evolved to include transaction coordination, listing and marketing coordination and short sale facilitation. Keeping pace with the versitle real estate industry requires continuing education and exposure to networking and industry forums. As the market continues to change, so to do the services offered. The services are customized to fit your needs.

Specialties: Short Sale Facilitation, Transaction Coordination, Marketing, Title Insurance and Escrow Services, Foreclosure Auction Process, Data Base Management

The Welkome Home Team strives to provide a buyer and seller experience that is stress free and results oriented. Implementing a customized and proven system with each client yields results each time. Real Estate is a constantly changing industry. We update education frequently and attend industry forums to stay current with the ever changing market.
