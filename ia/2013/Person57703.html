James Nottingham's Summary

I used to be a teacher and leader in primary and secondary schools before founding a multi-million pound social regeneration project in North East England. In 2006, I started my own business (JN Partnership Ltd) which is now registered in the UK, Australia, Denmark, Norway and Sweden. Our trading names include Challenging Learning, and Sustained Success. We are also the official Scandinavian partners for John Hattie's Visible Learning.

I have practised Philosophy for Children (P4C) since 1992 and am one of the founding directors of www.p4c.com, an international resource and collaboration cooperative. I have expertise in Carol Dweck's Mindset theory, having toured with Professor Dweck in 2010, and am the European Coordinator for OUR EDucation NETwork. My other interests include challenge, feedback, self assessment, and leadership.

Company website: www.challenginglearning.com
Online calendar: www.jamesnottingham.co.uk

James Nottingham's Projects

  • TEDx presentation

    • October 2012 to Present
    Team Members: James Nottingham, Bosse Larsson, Bengt Lennartsson

    In this TEDx presentation, I draw attention to the problem of labelling children - even when those labels seem positive. I describe the impact of labels - good and bad - on student expectations and how this affects learning. I then offer alternatives - to focus on student progress (rather than rank order) and to use labels to describe actions rather than people.
