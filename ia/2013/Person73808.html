Kelley Wong's Summary

My mental toolbox holds the skills to write well and to-the-point. I work hard without fail. I face any challenge with my unfaltering competitive streak, almost compulsively. Due to my never-ending curiosity and attention to details, I research and find information not even Indiana Jones could uncover.

I received my Bachelor's in journalism (public relations and advertising emphasis) and speech communication and my M.B.A. from the University of Nevada, Reno. My background in public relations and advertising has played a large role in my approach to employee/employer recruitment. People are their own best product. It's my goal to sell their skills, experiences and achievements to compatible employers. Understanding the challenge of having to find fresh ideas for recycled products is only the beginning. It's also about continual improvement. Continually providing clients with the resources to better themselves and improve their skills is something I strive to do. I have developed and implemented curriculum for in-demand growth industries such as I.T., warehousing and healthcare with high completion rates and positive placements. I encourage my clients to be the solution to a company's problems, no matter how big or small that problem may be. For me, that is the best part: finding a new idea, new concept, new solution amongst the clutter.
