Ben Padley's Summary

Ben Padley is a results-driven, dynamic marketer, with more than 15 years’ experience. Ben has a proven track record leading the agenda of Chief Marketing Officers, driving commercial success through all forms of marketing, from traditional to digital, including social media and CRM.

Ben was most recently Barclaycard’s first Global Digital Director following his role as VP, Global Head of Digital & CRM at Sony Ericsson (now Sony Mobile).

Whilst at Sony Ericsson, Ben led the team that developed the brand's global Facebook page from 250,000 to more than 6 million fans in under two years, creating what was at the time recognised as the most engaged Facebook brand page in the world.

Ben blogs at www.benpadley.com. Follow him on Twitter @benpadley.


Specialties:
