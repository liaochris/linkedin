Design Group's Summary

Design Group LLC (Limited Liability Company) was founded with a singular mission: to bring ideas to life. With a substantial online presence featuring thousands of products, Design Group is dedicated to bringing unique, novel, and intriguing concepts to the marketplace. With an international presence and offices in Wilmington, Delaware, Design Group now provides professional design and test marketing services to a global community of inventors and innovators with the introduction of their New Products Division. Design Group LLC provides intellectual property holders with all the tools they will need to move their new concept off of the drawing board and into the marketplace.

Design Group works with inventors at every stage of concept development to maximize results. Design Group understands that the market is ultimate arbiter of new product viability and for that reason the firm can not guarantee success for the inventors or concepts overseen in house. Design Group will, however, work steadfastly with the interests of the concept and its intellectual property holder in mind and seek to maximize the potential profitability of the concept at all times.

All intellectual property submitted to Design Group is kept strictly confidential. Design Group values the innovative spirit that drives invention and the ethical standards related to intellectual property are a point of pride for the firm. Interested parties, dreamers, visionaries, and intellectual property holders are all potential candidates for consideration for their New Products Division, even concepts in the initial phases of development are open to consideration.

Design Group’s New Product Division is led by industry veterans Michael Harrison, Steve Wilson, and Rick Johnson, with the support of a dedicated team of design and marketing professionals.
