Bruce Carter's Summary

My goal is to make things work so those I do business with find the relationship to be mutually beneficial (profitable!) and to provide a level of service that exceeds expectations. That means I work long hours and have no life. <g> If they are happy, successful and all their needs are being met, I have earned their continued business. Well...except for those who don't do business in this manner. Please read the ratings & reviews on this site, on the BBB, Trustlink.com, FB and the testimonials from clients on company websites.

We do not have "readers"...we have active dental shoppers! Dentists use the The Dental Trader when they are in a buying frame of mind and it is this which separates us from other publications. All the nationally circulated dental magazines miss 30%-60% of California's dentists...we reach them all...100% coverage!
