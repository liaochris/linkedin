Developed quality metrics for patent specification maps, invalidity claim charts, patent-to-product maps, draft office action responses, and prosecution history reviews.

Patent Law Curriculum - Extensive experience teaching patent law and analysis. Developed both theoretical and practical course materials. Provided classroom training to several large teams of engineers. Developed testing materials and certified engineers to perform specific processes. Theoretical training materials include significant court decisions, statutory law, regulatory law, and administrative law. Practical training materials include application of the theoretical materials to hypothetical examples. Classroom training includes discussing reading materials using the Socratic Method, graded weekly assignments, final comprehensive tests, and certifying engineers at levels used in career advancement decisions.

Leadership Mentoring - Monitored team psychological health to reduce team attrition. Monitor team dynamics and interpersonal skills with team leaders. Participated in committees used to select team leaders based on quality scores, training certification scores, and communication skills. Fostered cooperation and knowledge sharing between teams.

Successful Outsourcing - Developed verbal and written communication strategies with the client to lay a foundation for success. Developed best practices for international calls in multiple time zones. Developed a process for migrating portions of highly sophisticated services to offshore teams.
