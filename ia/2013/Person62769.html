Jerry Posner's Summary

Jerry helps service providers serve their customers and clients better; helps teams work together better; helps leaders improve leadership and training skills; and helps people "get from where they are to where they want to be."

"Relevant, relatable, entertaining, practical."

Workshops, seminars, lectures, training programs, meeting facilitation - for businesses, organizations, conferences, retreats, events.

Ongoing since 1993, guest lecturer at Canyon Ranch, Lenox, MA.

Ongoing since 1998, "The Team In Motion" customer service, professional development, leadership and team building programs for Tio Juan's Margaritas Restaurants (NH, ME, MA, CT, PA).

Serving a wide variety of client businesses -- hotels, banks, restaurants, retail, health care, real estate, public libraries, non-profits, financial consultants, attorneys, sales, manufacturing and membership organizations.

Clients have included: Diversified Agency Services, Omnicom Group; FDIC, Boston & NYC; National Endowment for the Arts; BrightFarms; GE Plastics; Brunswick Corp.; W.E. Aubuchon Co.; Berkshire Bank; Greylock Federal Credit Union; Berkshire Life Insurance; Berkshire Healthcare Systems; Berkshire Humane Society; Interprint; County Ambulance; Old Sturbridge Village; Hancock Shaker Village; Distinctive Hospitality Group; Guido's Fresh Marketplace; Apple-Metro, Inc.; Ginsberg's Foods; Veson Nautical; Crowne Plaza Hotel; Kripalu Center for Yoga & Health; Westmoor Club; Rehabilitative Resources, Inc.

Specialties: Exceeding expectations; ways to create customer & brand loyalty; communications & leadership skills -- practical tools, techniques & strategies to facilitate positive growth & change. Team building and bonding. Emotional intelligence. Motivational entertainment promoting successful stress management; expanded awareness of behavior, habits, choices -- ways to increase the likelihoods of positive outcomes at work, in relationships, and in life.

Please visit: www.jerryposner.com for more.
