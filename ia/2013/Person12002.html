Bart Clark's Summary

Salvation:
One night after work as a young adult, I found myself at home all alone. In the living room, I heard a familiar voice on the television. It was the last five minutes of a Billy Graham television crusade. Rev. Graham made a statement, which pierced through my heart and soul. He said, “When you came into this place tonight you thought you were saved, but you realized something is missing in your life. That something is Jesus and you need a personal relationship with the Son of God.” I suddenly realized the decision I had made as a child was just a mimic of my brother’s actions. That night I fell on my knees, made that decision my own, and surrendered my life to Jesus. I finally understood that Jesus was my only hope of knowing God and being in His family forever. My baptism took place at Groceville Baptist Church not long after my decision to accept Christ as my Lord and Savior. This life changing event launched a journey of ministry in service to the Lord Jesus Christ. I am not ashamed of the gospel of Christ, for it is the power of God to salvation for everyone who believes, for the Jew first and also for the Greek.

My goals are as follows:
To love the Lord my God with all my heart mind and soul while leading others to do the same. To preach the Word. To serve God and His people as He leads and directs. To equip, train, and organize God’s discipleship team, to further the gospel.

Specialties: My specialty is giving praise to God, and preaching the gospel while pointing souls to the Kingdom of Heaven. I spend time listening for the voice of God then endeavor to carry out His instructions.
