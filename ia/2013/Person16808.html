Brenda P. Noland's Summary

I believe in the value of Information Architecture and intelligent UX Design. It is about making every day tasks more efficient and intuitive, about being able to extract the data one needs, about engaging users in a seamless fashion, in the end its about having a rewarding experience and empowering users to make decisions.

Personally, I feel rewarded working on projects within this field because I have always valued the integration of disciplines from engineering and computer science to design and psychology. I look forward to a life-long career of creative and analytical thinking, of working with dynamic teams of people and providing innovative solutions.
