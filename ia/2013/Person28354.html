Certified to train and evaluate Emergency Medical Technicians.

American Heart Association BLS Training Center Faculty.

Certifications from American Heart Association and American Safety and Health Institute to train BLS, Heartsaver, Basic First Aid, CPR for Family and Friends, Pediatric First Aid for Family and Friends, CPR for Professional Rescuers, Emergency Oxygen Administration, Wilderness First Aid, Child and Babysitting Safety, Pet First Aid. Food and Beverage: Offering the NRFSP Food Safety Manager Certification Exam, and the ServSafe Food Safety Certification Class and Exam. I am also a certified TiPS Trainer, teaching servers and purveyors of alcoholic beverages how to do so responsibly, and how to sell alcohol and reduce their potential liability.
