Dan O'Shea's Summary

Neuroscience PhD candidate at Stanford University in the Neural Prosthetics Systems Laboratory under Krishna Shenoy. with a strong interest in computational neuroscience and robotics and intelligent systems. I am broadly interested in computational, systems, and theoretical approaches to neuroscience, artificial intelligence, machine learning, computer vision, brain-computer interfaces, entrepreneurship, web development, and design.
