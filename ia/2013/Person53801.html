Howie Boeve's Summary

“Experience isn’t Expensive….it’s Priceless!!”

I have been a residential realtor for over 16 years in West Michigan, consistently being awarded in the top 1% of agents. When buying, selling, investing, building, and developing real estate, there is a lot know. I understand that knowledge is power and my #1 goal is to educate and consult you so that you have the competitive edge you need to make the best decision.

At Howie Boeve & Company, we:

VALUE TEAMWORK: When you hire a Coldwell Banker REALTOR, you hire an entire company. We understand that collective investment, collective talent and collective expertise allows us to achieve more than one individual ever could.

SEEK FAIRNESS: At all times we strive to be good stewards of the responsibility we have to our clients. Honesty, integrity and trust is essential in all we do.

BUILD RELATIONSHIPS FOR LIFE: We exist to serve. Service does not stop when the sale closes—our commitment to our customers continues for a lifetime.

STRIVE FOR LEADERSHIP: we stay connected with organizations and people who make a difference. We set higher standards and expectations and we live by them. We deliver more to our clients and we give back to our community.

STIMULATE CONTINUOUS LEARNING: We always strive to raise our level of knowledge and our capabilities. We are committed to both personal and organizational growth. The power of our philosophy encourages everyone to share his or her wisdom. This rare brand of wisdom only comes from experience. Continuous learning means continuous improvement.

“Our client-first philosophy and passion for West Michigan and the lakeshore is the core in how we continually strive to communicate, educate, and maintain a smooth transaction.”
