Julie Heidelberg's Summary

As the owner of Heidelberg PR, Inc. Julie has nearly 20 years of public relations and marketing experience in the non-profit, corporate and educational sectors.

In 2001, Julie started Heidelberg PR, a full-service public relations firm serving clients in a variety of industries including healthcare, real estate, higher education, tourism and retail. During the first five years of business, she also taught public relations courses at the University of South Florida School of Mass Communications.

Heidelberg PR’s services encompass strategic planning, campaign design, media relations, event planning, advertising, internal communication, web site development and video production.

Notable clients include AAA Auto Club South, BayCare Health System, the University of South Florida, WalMart and Westfield, Ltd.

In 2012, Julie added another component to her portfolio as the editor and marketing professional at Senior Voice America, a local multi-media company based in Tampa, Fla., where she is the editor of a monthly newspaper and a key member of the maketing team.
