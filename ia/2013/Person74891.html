Kevin Fredrick's Summary

Co-Founder of Presm Medical Solutions, Inc. We enable the healthcare community to use the internet to connect people and share information in meaningful ways. Here's the rundown:

Purpose:
To provide technology that enriches the communication process in healthcare.

How:
By enabling targeted, relevant, and timely information to be shared securely between medical practitioners, patients, and the general public.

Why:
To achieve improved patient outcomes, cost savings, and operational excellence.

Cool! But what is it?
Presm.com is a secure online communication network that combines the benefits of a traditional patient portal with a world-class electronic communication system.
