Andy Logan's Summary

Andy Logan is an experienced technical professional living in the Silicon Valley. He is often described as the guy who 'gets things done' and 'can do anything'. He is currently the Director of Solutions Engineering at Aruba Networks, focusing on best practice deployment of Aruba products and integration with third party solutions. This role includes leading a team of employees and contractors who work with multiple teams across the internal organization to develop solutions and best practices. The solution engineering team synthesizes the knowledge of these teams into the validated reference design (VRD) series of guides available to customers and partners.

This role utilizes many of the skills learned through the other roles in Aruba, including technical marketing, product marketing, leading a restructuring of the core training curriculum, and building Aruba’s certification program. Andy is also heavily involved in Aruba's customer forum called Airheads, both online and at the event where he is consistently one of the highest rated presenters.

Prior to Aurba Andy has various technical roles at companies. At HP in Boise Andy worked through Adecco technical debugging large multi-function printer firmware and building an internal bug tracking web site in PHP. While at Nortel Networks Andy worked on and then led an SE help desk, eventually moving into a technical marketing role. Andy's role at 3Com was as a post sales engineer, which unfortunately ended rather quickly when 3Com exited the networking market in 2000. Prior to that Andy worked for NorthPoint Communications in San Francisco, working as a help desk technician and taking on temporary leadership of the team. Andy's first technical job was at Umax, where he provided end-user customer phone support for scanner products.
