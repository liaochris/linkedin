Dann Foley's Summary

"Taste can be taught but, style is much more elusive, you know it when you see it."

DANN Inc. is world renowned ASID designer with residential and commercial/hospitality designs in New York City, New Jersey, Philadelphia, Washington DC, Savannah, Charleston, Ft. Lauderdale, Miami, Chicago, Santa Barbara, Palm Springs, Palm Desert, Jordan, United Emirates, Saudi Arabia and Seoul, Korea.

DANN believes "there are no problems, only solutions" whether referring to a wardrobe, interior design, or product development for manufacturers. Dann has a passion for a great lifestyle, a commitment to excellence and a dedication to client satisfaction.

During interior design projects, DANN's expertise shines from initial concept and design development through the final installation, Dann Inc. distinguishes it's work through rigorous attention to detail, high quality standards and follow-through; these are the key ingredients that set Dann Inc. apart.

DANN's clients know him as the KING of LIVING WELL. With good reason, DANN gives his clients something to feel great about, in their homes, wardrobe, or in the parties he plans.

DANN Inc.'s company office in Palm Springs is a full service consultation, architectural and interior design house offering renovation and construction, spec and sample home installation, exterior specifications and landscape design, and lifestyle consulting.

As a LIFESTYLE consultant DANN offers personal make overs, wardrobe consulting, party planning, and entertaining advice & tips.

Currently, manufacturers in the gift and home industry are seeking DANN the Lifestyle Brand. Look for products in your local retail stores and online.

DANN has been featured on several television shows such as Showtime's The Real "L" World and has been published globally.

LIKE him on Facebook to get his tips and designer secrets that your girlfriends wish they knew.

Specialties: Interior Designer, Speaker, Entertainer, Product Designer.
