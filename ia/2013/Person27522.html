Craig Isvak's Summary

Craig Isvak, CPA is the principal member, manager and owner of Associates in Accounting, PLC, a full service CPA firm to serve the tax, accounting and consulting needs of individuals and small businesses in the Burlington, VT area. Craig has prepared literally thousands of individual and business tax returns and helped grow many businesses in the process.

Craig is passionate about helping people make their businesses more profitable and improve individual financial situations through communication and quality services. His motto is, " I treat every client like they're my only client". Small business is my specialty - it's full of forked roads and can be confusing... I let people know what lies down each road, and let them make the choice that's best for them. And that's not just for business owners... we can help individuals make the same choices.

Businesses and individuals have worked with Craig when they need help with:

• Business accounting
• Tax planning and preparation
• Tax compliance
• Outsource controllership and CFO services
• Financial management and consulting
• Budgeting and financial projections
• QuickBooks set up and maintenance
