Brooks Luby's Summary

Brooks Ltd was founded in 1976. Educated at the Fashion Institute of Technology in New York, I draw inspiration from the world around me.

As an independent boutique owner since 1974, I also sold my clothing designs nationally to Saks Fifth Avenue and others. In 1976, I pioneered a salon trend by opening a designer boutique within Neusteter’s, a long-time high-end Denver department store. From 1979 through 2001, savvy women frequented my Cherry Creek North boutique, a Mecca for the well dressed and in-the-know. Since 2002, clients make their appointments one at a time in my private atelier.

A breast cancer survivor since 1990, my determination to succeed and serve hasn’t been diminished by my experience. As a result, I'm more empathetic & understanding of women’s needs. A frequent designer featured at “A Day of Caring”, a fundraiser for Breast Cancer research, I gained statewide recognition by my contributions to this celebrated event.

My creations are fresh, pure, and on the edge, yet recognized for their sophisticated simplicity. I aspire to craft pieces that fit the lifestyle of my clients — active, comfortable, feminine,and alluring. From the concept to the final fitting, every ensemble is an original, designed entirely around them. Above all, I offer each client a unique relationship, one that anticipates their fashion needs. And if magic is what you need — festive, fun, dreamlike, or outrageous, almost no commission is beyond reach.

Busy women have more important things to do than chase the next trend. My clients allow me to interpret those trends for them, guaranteeing the right look and the right fit. Created to last, my designs offer years of wearing pleasure.

Brooks Luby's Experience

President/WOMENS Fashion Designer

Brooks Ltd

Design industry

1975 – Present (38 years)

Brooks LTD is an independent clothing design studio focusing on Brides, Mother of the Bride/Groom, and social functions (charity events, parties, corporate functions galas, etc.). We create one-of-a-kind garments for savvy women through one-on-one consultations with the designer in a private environment.

Our awareness of advances in fabric technology as well as changes in fashion influences and lifestyle keep us a step ahead of the curve. The newest trends are woven into our ever-changing collection; our designs reflect a continuing evolution of fashion sense, reflecting what works today.

In the comfort of a private dressing area, a proper fitting provides us with everything we need to know. Then, together, we'll explore the possibilities and fabrics needed to create your Brooks’ original.
