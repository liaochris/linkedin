Liz Provo's Summary

I provide social media and email marketing assistance for small businesses. By educating, coaching and performing hands-on task management (account set up and/or maintenance, time management programs, editorial calendars, etc.), I am able to empower entrepreneurs to become more proficient in the area of today's relationship marketing. I often work with individuals who feel occasionally challenged by technology and with those who may have difficulty staying "on task".

As an Authorized Local Expert and Business Solution Provider for Constant Contact, I facilitate email and social media marketing seminars, workshops and private trainings for organizations throughout the Western Mass area. I am also available to provide additional product training for businesses wishing to have local support.

Specialties: Working with micro-businesses and start-ups:

• email marketing (newsletters, promos, etc.)
• social media set up and administration
• websites, blogs
• low-cost video (filming, editing, producing)
• desktop organization and email assistance
• e-commerce assistance
• desktop publishing - brochures, flyers, etc.
• event planning: trade shows, seminars
• online research

Massachusetts 4 Sale By Owner (Sole Proprietorship)

Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Real Estate industry

January 2001 – Present (12 years 10 months) Massachusetts

Massachusetts 4 Sale By Owner is a real estate service for private home sellers (FSBOs). I began the company in 2001, the first of its kind in Western Massachusetts. I educated consumers about alternative real estate by hosting monthly home seller workshops and by speaking to organizations about a new way of selling real estate.

The business included publishing a high-end real estate magazine with a drop distribution of over 25,000 magazines in over 350 locations throughout the area each month.

I was responsible for all aspects of editing and publishing; meeting deadlines, authoring articles, selling ad space to private home sellers and businesses and creating the layout. The print magazine was retired in 2009 to focus solely on an internet and social media based sales model.
