Catherine Mullen's Summary

Meeting Logistics, LLC is a full service agency who provides a wide range of skill sets and significant expertise to design, plan and produce meetings, conferences, events and incentives worldwide. We have in-depth knowledge of the trade show industry and effectively manage the many stages associated with exhibition logistics management. Our experience spans the globe: North America, South America, Europe, Asia and Australia. Our clients range in size from Fortune 500 companies to small, emerging businesses in a multitude of industries.

We have significant experience providing service to the Pharmaceutical, Biotechnology & Medical Device Industries and we are knowledgable of the Health Care Compliance policies and procedures and the reporting that is required.

Our special events division, ML2 provides strategy & consulting, ideation, conceptualization, creative design services, event management and production, destination management, event design, event marketing, experiential marketing and promotional product solutions for meetings, special events, corporate events, trade shows, conferences, private functions and social gatherings.
