Ann Donnelly's Summary

In 2000, O'Mahony Donnelly Chartered Certified Accountants was established and I developed a basic website with lots of articles about the services we provided and how to set up business in Ireland. More than 10 years later, in a great part due to the strength of that website (redeveloped a number of times since), we are doing business with companies all over the World. I manage the overall sales and marketing activities of the practice.

For the past 10 years I've also been helping other business owners improve their results using the internet -- as one of Ireland's only Search Engine Optimization specialists and now with a focus on Social Media Marketing to improve business results.

I working with clients to develop a complete online marketing plan (usually providing input on the overall marketing strategy) -- from initial web design/development (with SEO always a priority) to promoting the site through social media, email marketing and online advertising.

Writing & Speaking Engagements:
- monthly contributor to Search Engine Journal
- guest speaker at local, national and international conferences
- webinar presentor
- other regular speaking engagements to business and networking groups in the south of Ireland

Goals:
- Improving ROI & Profits for our clients -- whether through traditional accountancy services or online marketing.

Contact me here if you are looking to achieve better results through your business.

I am always looking for great partners who provide excellent service and value in web development -- especially e-commerce websites, WordPress customization and Facebook/mobile applications, etc.

Specialties

Search Engine Optimization, Social Media for Business (Twitter, LinkedIn, Facebook, blogging, etc.)
