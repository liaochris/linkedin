Bethany Veglahn's Summary

I love creating meaningful work for clients that fulfills their objectives and helps their business grow. I’ve designed in both in-house and agency settings and have experience creating identities, marketing pieces, websites and email campaigns. I have a strong attention to detail and believe every piece of a project should receive equal attention, right down to the spacing in a phone number.

Design to me is not only creating an eye-catching piece, but truly focusing on the meaning behind the brand. I'm passionate about creating the right solution for every company and I start design projects by asking questions, researching, and delving into the brand itself.

Specialties

Corporate Identity, Brochure Design, Editorial Design, Website Design/Front End Development, Email Marketing, Branding
