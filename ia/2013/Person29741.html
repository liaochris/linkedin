Danielle Siano's Summary

Danielle Siano provides "killer" designs from concept to creation, specializing in print and identity branding for small businesses and non-profit organizations. She produces quality work, providing solutions that are specialized to her clients' needs covering many aspects of a successful marketing campaign. Danielle began as an editorial and advertising designer for newspapers and a designer for small companies where she managed the advertising and graphics department entirely. Today she offers a range of design and consulting services for businesses and organizations who wish to have a presence in their market. She provides all her clients: cost-effective solutions; personalized service; professionalism and reliability; results that exceed expectations; the best possible experience.

Services Provided:
Professional Graphic Design Services, Identity and Branding, Print Production, Marketing Consultation, Copy Writing and Editing, Image Editing, Scanning, Acrobat Form Creation

Graphic Design Accomplishments:
Advertisements, Direct mailers, Training booklets, Catalogs, Educational text books, Kiosk display graphics, Programs, Brochures, Flyers, Banners, Posters, Newspaper Editorial layout, Newsletters, Logos, Corporate identities, Business cards, T-shirts, Signage, Forms, Web-ready graphics.

For more information on DSGD Services go to: www.daniellesiano.com

Specialties

Danielle is an active member of the Santa Barbara community with experience in the non-profit & for-profit worlds. Partial client list includes: Backyard Bowls, Defined Business Solutions, Santa Barbara Pistachio Company, Custom Media Group, Audubon Society, Surfrider Foundation, Quail Springs, Community Environmental Council, Citizens Planning Foundation, Salon Operating Systems, Walter Claudio Studios, SB Museum of Natural History, Bartlett Pringle & Wolfe, SB Outfitters
