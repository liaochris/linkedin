Jean-Luc David's Summary

Jean-Luc has over 20 years experience as a technology thought leader and builder - with the rare combination of business management and marketing experience with a deep technical expertise of APIs, web & cloud technologies, agile methodologies, and mobile platforms (such as iOS and Android).

Over the span of his career, he's been both an successful entrepreneur and a team player, launching several successful startups and working for large companies such as Microsoft Corporation & Yellow Pages Group Canada.

His work has been featured in the media including TechCrunch, CNN, BetaKit, Wired, Mashable, New York Observer, Facebook's Official Blog, CTV News, CBC News & the Montreal Gazette. He is a published author of five technical books with Wiley Publishing and a number of articles for CNET and TechRepublic. He's spoken at hundreds of conferences and events around the world.

Jean-Luc is equally comfortable formulating go-to-market strategies & leading large software development teams as he is rolling up his sleeves, writing code and participating in global hackathons.
