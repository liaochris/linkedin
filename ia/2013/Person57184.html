Jaleh Afshar's Summary

Hi, my name's Jaleh Afshar. I've been working in the interaction design field since 2007.

From that time, I've been creating interfaces, conducting usability testing, performing user research, and designing graphics. I love creating elegant, intuitive applications & beautiful visual design.

In addition to UX, I like traveling, animals, typography, drawing, and painting. When it comes to travel, I especially enjoy going to gaming/tech conventions, like PAX, E3, GDC, CES, and DICE.
