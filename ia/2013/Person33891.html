ARUN GROVER's Summary

Own independent private dental practice at New Delhi, India.

Broad clinical experience including provision of:
a) Dental Implants : Ankylos and Uniti
b) Computer Guided Dental Implants using IMPLANTSMART Software
c) All Ceramics: IPS e.Max, Procera, 3M ESPE Lava, Orion veneers, crown & bridges
d) Rotary Endodontics: Protaper, K3, Varitaper, Lightspeed LSX, Mtwo file systems using Root ZX II Apex locator (with handpiece) under Rubber Dam
e) Tooth coloured fillings ( 3M ESPE Z350 Nanocomposites with Ivoclar Tetric Color tints & Contact Matrix/Ivoclar OptraContact)
f) Inlays/Onlays: made in All Ceramic, Indirect Composites & Gold
g) Carbon & Fiber Posts with DMG Luxacore Z cores.
h) Precious metal ceramic crowns/bridges (containing gold)
i) Porcelain Repair with nano-composites using Intra-oral sand-blasting, metal bonding and opaquers
j) Full Mouth Rehabilitation & occlusion adjustments with Gnatus Semi Adjustable Articulator including face-bow transfer
k) Temporary crowns with Protemp 4 & Polycarbonate crowns
l) Gingival retraction and A-Silicone rubber base impressions
m) 3M ESPE Stainless steel crowns & fluoride applications
n) In-office tooth whitening & smile modifications after diagnostic wax-ups & dental photography
o) Oral Surgery including routine extractions, flap & bone grafting procedures
p) Electro-surgical Crown Lengthening
q) Periodontal flap surgery, curettage & air polishing
r) Physiological Progressive Splint Therapy for treatment for Tempromandibular Dysfunction (TMDs)

In-depth understanding/training in International Dental Public Health, including Qualitative Research, Dental Informatics, Health Technology, Health Behavior and Preventive Dentistry as applied to local conditions.

Specialties: Dental Implants, Dental Crowns & Bridges, All Ceramic Veneers, Tooth Whitening, Root Canal Treatment, Dental Surgery, Periodontal Treatments, Pediatric Dentistry, Dental Public Health, Dental Informatics, Health Technology
