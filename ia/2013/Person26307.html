Colin Wright's Summary

I'm an author, entrepreneur, and full-time traveler.

I run a successful blog about doing business from the road and exploring the cultures I come across along the way. My readers vote on which country I'll move to next every four months.

I've written some books, all of which have been top sellers in their respective categories. I also run a non-traditional publishing company and community with some fellow bestselling authors.

In the past, I ran a branding studio, where I helped businesses get a grip on who they were (and then communicate that to others). I still consult on the same topic from time-to-time.

Specialties: Branding, entrepreneurship, publishing, design, writing, speaking, traveling, strategic partnerships, yo-yo (above-average), Tetris (near-mastery).
