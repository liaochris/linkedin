Anand Rao's Summary

As Principal and Knowledge Leader within PwC's Diamond Advisory Services I lead our efforts in bringing innovative techniques and approaches, (such as system dynamic modeling, agent-based simulation, behavioral economics etc.) to advice and solve critical issues for our clients. I am also involved in defining and carrying out critical primary and secondary research related to critical issues such as the 'Baby Boomer Retirement Study' and 'Recession Analysis'.

My experience spans primarily financial services (capital markets, asset management, retail and corporate banking, payments), insurance (life, retirement income, property & casualty, alternative risk transfer), and telecommunications to advice senior executives on creating and extracting value across marketing (i.e., customer experience, customer value management, distribution and sales analysis, segmentation, value proposition, marketing campaigns), operations (i.e., online channels, contact centers, business process redesign, defining and planning large-scale operational and technology transformations), and technology (i.e., conceptual business architecture, information architecture, and Web2.0 technologies).

Prior to my strategic consulting career I have led and managed innovative artificial-intelligence based approaches for air-combat modeling, air-traffic management, customer service decision support, and telecommunications network management. I have also taught and supervised doctoral students at the University of Melbourne and served as the Program Director of the Center for Intelligent Decision Systems at University of Melbourne.

I have co-edited four books on Intelligent Agents, published over fifty peer-reviewed papers in conferences and journals. I frequently speak at technology and business forums in analytics, behavioral economics, and insurance. Served on the Organizing Committee and Program Committee of a number of International Conferences and Workshops related to Intelligent Agents

Specialties

Customer value management, behavioral economics, distribution value management, technical and business architecture, operational and technology transformation management, compliance and regulation, risk management and alternative risk transfer, senior management facilitation

Structuring problems, drawing insights from data, analytical and system dynamics modeling, behavior modeling using agents, and building expert systems.
