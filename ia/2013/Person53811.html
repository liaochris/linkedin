★ HOW'S MY DRIVING? PROGRAM
Driver Observation Program consists of a 24/7 call center dedicated to document comments from the public about perceived driving behaviors. While most companies strive to hire safe drivers, it’s not always enough. On average, 5% of drivers develop unsafe driving patterns that potentially put themselves and others at-risk. Such unsafe driving behaviors expose companies to accidents and potentially lawsuits.


★ AUTOMATED MOTOR VEHICLE RECORDS (MVR)
MVR system systematically pre-screens and continually monitors employee’s driving records. Our system lessens your administrative workload while eliminating the chance of drivers with negative driving history from slipping through the cracks.


★ ONLINE DRIVER TRAINING
Online training with proven advanced driving techniques developed to give drivers the critical advantage of avoiding hazardous situations along with the extra time and knowledge to stay safe when roadway incidents are encountered.

How's My Driving?'s Experience

Fleet Safety & Risk Management

Embark Safety

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Public Safety industry

2007 – Present (6 years) Orlando, Florida Area

Embark Safety allows all size businesses, government entities and insurance providers to mitigate accident risk by leveraging comments from concerned motorists to identify risky driving behaviors and reduce the likelihood of accidents. Our driver monitoring program not only makes good business sense, but also is a good employee relations tool; it promotes accountability while helping drivers become valuable employees.

✔ Our mission is to prevent dangerous and costly accidents from happening by not only pinpointing and tracking irregular driving patterns, but also giving our customers the resources to remedy these problems before it’s too late.

✔ Our vision is to become a world-class organization in commercial fleet safety by driving for innovation and continuous improvement of vehicle safety.
