George Koutsos' Summary

Highly immersed in all aspects of the financial services industry for 25+ years, I have developed a holistic approach to mortgage planning, which allows me to provide my clients with the best possible mortgage solutions to meet their short-term and long-term financial goals and achieve financial independence.

A mortgage is one piece to the puzzle of your financial world. It is the most important piece however, as it determines how all the other pieces will fit together to secure you with a strong financial future. I am committed to looking at all the different ways to position your mortgage within that puzzle in order to maximize your wealth and meet your financial goals throughout your life journey.

I strive to build lifelong relationships with my clients, educating, advising, and counseling them along the way and making sure they are on the right path to financial security and freedom. Having surrounded myself with top experts in related fields, such as financial planning, college planning, accounting, and law, to name a few, I feel confident that I can assist you in making the right financial decisions to live a happy and prosperous life.

In regard to networking, I am very interested in connecting with like-minded individuals in the professional sphere. I would love to develop new relationships with realtors, financial planners, accountants, attorneys, and human resources professionals. Please feel free to contact me if you are interested.
