Jacqueline Muller, M.D.'s Experience

Eye Surgeon

Jacqueline W. Muller, M.D. P.C.

July 1993 – Present (20 years 3 months)

Board certified ophthalmologist (eye surgeon) with a full service general ophthalmology practice. Areas of expertise include the diagnosis and treatment of dry eye and Laser Vision Correction (refractive surgery). Educated and trained at Cornell, Harvard and Yale; fellowship trained in refractive surgery. Participated in the Excimer laser FDA trials and a member of the team of eye surgeons who developed the software presently used in this technology.

Well-respected lecturer and teacher of ophthalmologists and optometrists regarding refractive surgery techniques and dry eye. Featured on NBC's The Today Show.
