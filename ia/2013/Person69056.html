Josh Greenfield's Experience

Vice President, Energy Services Manager

Primera Engineers

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Design industry

November 2012 – Present (1 year 2 months) Greater Chicago Area

I am a licensed professional Mechanical Engineer (PE), LEED Accredited Professional (LEED AP BD+C & O+M), Building Energy Modeling Professional (ASHRAE BEMP) and a Certified Energy Manager (CEM) with 11+ years of experience highlighted by extensive energy consulting and sustainable design experience both as the project LEED consultant as well as serving as the mechanical engineer on several LEED design projects.

I am involved on multiple sustainable consulting projects in various markets from the private and public sectors following all current LEED Green Building Rating Systems, as well as numerous Retro-Commissioning and energy efficiency feasibility projects. My expertise and knowledge of energy efficiency grants and incentive opportunities is utilized by a wide range of building owners who wish to save energy while maintaining financially sound Life Cycle Cost Analysis results.

I specialize in Energy Model creation following ASHRAE 90.1 Guidelines as well as Integrated Design Phase Energy Modeling to aide in the selection of architectural envelope properties and mechanical system efficiencies. My knowledge of Energy Modeling is also utilized as a reviewer of City of Chicago Department of Building’s Green Permit Program Energy Models as well as part of the International Code Council (ICC) Sustainable Building Technology Committee (SBTC) that developed the first International Green Construction Code (IgCC) that debuted in March 2012.
