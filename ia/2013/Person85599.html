Margot Aronson's Summary

You deserve the exceptional level of customer service, support and results that Margot provides. She is a full time Realtor with a passion for doing MORE to help her clients successfully complete settlement and is a ready and knowledgeable resource well beyond.

Margot has been selected (for a fourth consecutive year) as a Five Star Best in Overall Satisfaction Real Estate Agent by her clients. She was listed in the September 2013 issue of the Philadelphia Magazine in a special section honoring the area’s top 5% of Real Estate Agents.

With over ten years of successful corporate direct mail marketing and fifteen years of teaching experience Margot has the skill sets to successfully coach her clients through the selling or buying process. To support her marketing efforts, she has earned the Accredited Seller Representative Designation (ARS) and represents both buying and selling clients using the highest industry standards.

Margot's unique equine background enables her to understand client's specific needs and to translate into successful marketing/purchasing strategies. Her background includes successful/respected business ownership, nationally recognized equine accomplishments (former A.H.S.A. licensed Judge, USPC "A" rated rider, former USPC National Examiner, nominated and selected as "Legend" in the USPC, former N.Y. Dept of Education Licensed Teacher/Assistant School Director to teach Horsemanship/Horsemastership). Consultations available.



Statement of Purpose:

To seamlessly coach clients through successful real estate transactions by providing current industry knowledge, marketing expertise, exceptional customer focused services and adhering to the Realtor's and ASR Codes of Ethics.
