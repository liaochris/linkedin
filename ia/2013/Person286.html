Laura Wiletsky's Summary

Public relations executive with national, regional and local print and broadcast media contacts. Developed and successfully executed award-winning campaigns for major corporations and financial institutions, top not-for-profits, professional practices, sports nutrition products and nationally recognized food and beverage brands.

Enjoy working closely with many journalists and am proud of my reputation with reporters as a "reliable source." My media contacts range from editors at local weeklies to producers at CNBC and Fox Business News.

Recognized by professional associations and leading industry organizations, my byline has appeared in The New York Times, local pubications and magazines. I have also ghost-written articles and opinion pieces for clients that have appeared in major daily newspapers and leading business publications.
