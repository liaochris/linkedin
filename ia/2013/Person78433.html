Larry Furr's Summary

My goal is to make whatever product I'm managing the most compelling, easy-to-use and gotta-have product within its field. I am a relentless advocate of knowing my product’s market, competition, customers and prospective customers. I have a keen eye for good design and strive to make my products intuitive and user-friendly. I know how to bring people together to get things done and meet deadlines. You can ship a product on time; you just have to manage scope and resources. That’s something I excel at. Most importantly, I know how to develop product strategies and business plans that win in the marketplace.

I have more than 10 years experience leading cross-functional teams in the delivery of products from inception to launch. I have managed and marketed both computer software and consumer goods products, but the bulk of my experience is in product managing B2B enterprise software suites positioned within the translation, telecommunications, information security and automotive services industries.

Prior to becoming a software product manager, I spent several years in product marketing and public relations within the consumer goods industry and government relations respectively. This experience has granted me a solid foundation in marketing and design, which has enabled me to understand and appreciate not only the technical side of software product management, but the sales, branding, pricing and positioning aspects as well.

Specialties

Crowd-sourcing, off-shoring, SaaS, user experience/UI design (Windows and web), product requirements, wire-framing, mock-ups, API specifications, project management, customer presentations, contextual inquiry, product branding/positioning, technical writing, user story/use case definition, business analysis, process flow diagramming.
