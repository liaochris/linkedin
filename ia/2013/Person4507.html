Alicia Blain's Experience

Founder and CEO

The Millennial Lab

Sole Proprietorship; 1-10 employees; Management Consulting industry

August 2009 – Present (4 years 1 month)

The Millennial Lab offers leading-edge educational leadership services and products (including consulting, training, research and media outreach) to organizations and associations.

As a former technology executive of a Fortune 500 company, Alicia Blain began to hire Gen Y employees years ago. At first, she found herself frustrated with a group she just couldn’t figure out. She took drastic action and turned her frustration into action by putting Millennials under the microscope and closely observing them in the workplace.

She shares innovative, proven methods with leaders on how they can challenge their conventional management practices, recognize and capitalize on the value of these multi-cultural Millennials and achieve unprecedented results in record time. In short, she shows her clients how to create their own Millennial Labs and harness the talents of this young workforce.

As a former female corporate executive, Alicia has a passion for leadership. Her particular focus is executive leadership. Having navigated the corporate ladder herself and mentored hundreds of women in their careers, Alicia is an expert in the area of womens leadership.
