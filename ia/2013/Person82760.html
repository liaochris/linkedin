Louis Richardson's Summary

I currently serve a Worldwide Evangelist & Storyteller for IBM. My current passions are around Social Business and Smarter Workforce. In this role I have the opportunity to meet with clients and partners worldwide and share how social tools can deliver real business results.

I have over 30 years of experience in the document management industry and have served in sales, support, consulting and document production management positions. From this perspective I have seen how knowledge and information management approaches have changed...most rapidly in the past several years.

Prior to IBM, I was the Vice President of Marketing at Green Pasture Software, a solution provider acquired by IBM. Additionally, I've served as the Director of Imaging, Workflow and Document Management at Crawford and Company, the world’s largest third-party administrator of insurance claims. I have also served as a consultant with Saros, FileNet, and Interleaf. Earlier in my career, I had the privilege of managing the publishing systems responsible for producing technical manuals at Lockheed Georgia’s aircraft production facility.

I believe this broad range of experiences has resulted in providing me with a valuable perspective of both client and company needs.

Specialties: Social business, social media, document and information management, publishing, collaboration, compound document applications, creative solutions, marketing, business development, consulting, education, creativity, innovation
