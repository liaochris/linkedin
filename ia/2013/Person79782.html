Lee Richardson's Summary

Lee Richardson's 25 years of experience working for industry leaders such as Frito-Lay, Borden, Loomis, Fargo & Co., and Cox Auto Trader have prepared him to help business owners and executives achieve the results they want. In his management roles at those companies, Lee learned that despite vast differences among businesses, there are core strategies that help make them all more successful. Communicating how those core strategies affect every part of a business and his ability to identify simple, but impactful changes, are what Lee does best. Lee's success training managers in his approach to business naturally led him into business coaching, where he is able to help many more companies. Lee is an Army veteran, graduate of Wofford College and a native of upstate South Carolina.
