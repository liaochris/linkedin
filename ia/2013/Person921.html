Abhishek Mittal's Summary

Abhishek is passionate about boosting organizational performance through effective people management. He has worked across a range of industries to design programs to address business needs like employee engagement, change management, culture alignment, employee value proposition, managerial effectiveness, customer engagement, customer touch-point optimization etc. He is also a regular speaker at client conferences. Geographically, his experience spans several countries in Asia including Singapore, Malaysia, Indonesia, India, Thailand, UAE etc.

Abhishek is an avid blogger and his blog http://abhishekmittal.com has been rated as one of the top 100 blogs on the subject of Human Resources. He has been featured as a contributor to the Management Innovation Exchange - an initiative led by Gary Hamel. He has also published articles in Singapore Business Review, Indian Management, Business World Philippines & Deccan Herald. His other interests include social media, collaboration tools and photography.
