Christopher Burnett's Summary

A native of the Kansas City Area, award-winning composer and saxophone artist, Chris Burnett began his prolific professional start at the age of 18 years old while leading, performing, touring and recording with Army bands from 1974-1996.

Mr. Burnett is the 1995 5-Star Award of Merit Winner of the National Federation of Music Clubs for his original composition and arrangement for big band titled, Daedalus.

A modern thinker and early explorer of the digital music landscape, Burnett garnered more than a half million music downloads and sold CDs to fans in 38 countries via MP3.com (1999-2003) before most musicians had figured out that there were other ways than major record label contracts and brick-and-mortar retail stores to reach listeners.

Since his return to Kansas City in 2001, Burnett was one of the very first artists featured by the newly renovated and historic Hilton President in the famous Drum Room within the Kansas City Power and Light District. With several new projects to be released, he already has a successful commercial album (Time Flies) distributed worldwide on the Artists Recording Collective label brand.

In addition to his professional performance activities, Mr. Burnett remains involved in the development of student musicians through his own private teaching practice; and the conduct of affordable music clinics at schools. As a publisher and businessman, Mr. Burnett also gained valuable experience while serving as a staff writer/arranger and company manager for many noteworthy military band assignments.

Memberships: American Federation of Musicians (Kansas City Local 34-627), The Recording Academy (GRAMMY®), the US Army Career and Alumni Program (ACAP), Kansas City Youth Jazz Advisory Council, Board of Directors of the Kansas City Jazz Orchestra. Composer- Affiliate of Broadcast Music, Inc. (BMI) since 1984.

Chris Burnett is an official Selmer (Paris) Saxophone Artist and endorsed Conn-Selmer Performing Artist and Clinician.

Specialties

Professional musician, jazz alto saxophonist, composer, arranger, band leader, webmaster, educator and writer. Co-founded Artists Recording Collective - an American label brand that serves as a dynamic and multi-dimensional platform to present the works and services of a select roster of artists.
