Angela Daffron's Summary

Angela is a marketing consultant guiding businesses to optimize their potential by building customer relationships, growing communities and effectively communicating their message to increase ROI.

Angela founded Jodi's Voice, an organization dedicated to increasing awareness surrounding the crime of stalking. Angela founded the organization after the murder of a family friend Jodi Sanderholm in January 2007.
