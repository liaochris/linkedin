Brian Carney's Summary

I have been working as a Rotoscope and Compositing Artist for the past 2 years now in feature films. My credits include "Iron Man 3", "Hitchcock", "Avengers", "Titanic 3D", "Abraham Lincoln: Vampire Hunter", "Goats", "Monkey King: Havoc in Heaven", "Kung Fu Panda 3D".

During that time, I have also been animating for two successful web series "Destructo Box" and "Game Program Attack". Currently we are in production on our third series "War Soldiers" which is scheduled to premiere this summer on both Machinima and Mondo YouTube Channels.

My software skills include: Nuke, After Effects, Photoshop, Maya, Mocha Pro, Flash, 3D Studio Max, Illustrator, Premiere Pro, Dreamweaver, HTML, CSS, ActionScript, Mac OS, Windows, and Linux proficiency.

Brian Carney's Projects

  • One Per Person, an Independent Animated Short Film

    • March 2011 to Present

    One Per Person is a quirky, fun, and light-hearted 7-minute animated short film that follows Chuck and Nancy, two fast-food employees that compete for customers by handing out free chicken samples. Wearing tacky uniforms and dealing with the gamut of annoying customers, their vastly different styles of salesmanship results in an amusing duel and an unexpected story of self-discovery. The film is a fresh take on that disappointing first job that many of us had to endure and a unique look at how appreciating the little things in life and staying positive can turn a dead-end situation into a new lease on life.

    We are a group of dedicated professionals from the film industry, brought together by our love for this film! Started as a passion project by our two directors, we have artists from around the world that are volunteering their free time to be a part of this special project. Everyone is coming together to help make this dream into a reality.

    Many more people have contributed to this project and played an important role; at the time of this listing, LinkedIn is limited to only 10 team members.
