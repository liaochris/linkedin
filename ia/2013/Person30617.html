Dario Betti's Summary

Dario works as Information architect and UX designer for GPI S.pA. (www.gpi.it), where he is engaged in e-health projects, often in collaboration with Fondazione Bruno Kessler FBK-(www.fbk.eu) and DISI-Department of Information Engineering and Computer Science-University of Trento (disi.unitn.it). He plays also often as project manager, trying to practice agile methodologies.

Dario is a founding member of Architecta (Italian Society for Information Architecture) and helps organize the annual editions of IASummit.

Specialties

Web project management, agile project management, User experience design, user-centered design approach,
Information architecture, metadata, taxonomy creation.

Dario Betti's Publications

  • La casa usabile. Euristiche per l’Usable Design in ambito AAL

    • AITAAL 2010 | 2010
    • October 2010
    Authors: Dario Betti, Cesare Malossi, Manuela Corradi, Giampaolo Armellin

    Da qualche decennio si è consolidato, nell’ambito del design di artefatti software e hardware, un insieme di euristiche per supportare i progettisti nella creazione di prodotti che soddisfino pienamente i bisogni degli utenti ai vari livelli di fruizione (funzionale, materiale, estetica, emozionale, sociale, culturale).
