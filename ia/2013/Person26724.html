Connie Lundgren's Summary

Since joining Turnquist Partners in 2006, Connie has been a top producer within the company and established herself as a confident and knowledgeable Realtor. When asked she is hard pressed to come up with an activity she enjoys more than helping buyers find the right property or representing Sellers in the marketing of their home. Broad market knowledge and the willingness to listen and assess client needs have given her a competitive edge. Connie is also committed to continually educating and updating her knowledge in all aspects of the real estate profession believing that knowledge is power.

A native Texan and Austin resident for 25 years, Connie has a strong local network plus first hand knowledge of the social, cultural and educational benefits Austin affords. She holds a B.A. in Journalism from the University of Houston, has worked as a substitute teacher while raising her and husband Wallace's two daughters and managed the marketing for his auto dealership.

"I enjoy working closely with my client's in all aspects of the home buying and selling process and place the utmost importance on each sale or search," she says. "Delivering market knowledge that will enable my clients to make informed decisions that deliver results is my dedication to the profession."
