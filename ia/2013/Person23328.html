Christopher Guedri's Summary

I am a Partner with the Virginia personal injury law firm of Allen, Allen, Allen & Allen. I work primarily in the Richmond, VA office but handle cases all over the Commonwealth. Since joining Allen & Allen in 1991, I have focused my legal career in helping injured victims primarily in tractor trailer and trucking accidents, car accidents, bus accidents, product liability and wrongful death.

1991 – Present (22 years) Richmond, Virginia Area

Christopher Guedri joined the Allen Law Firm in 1991. He has focused his practice on litigating catastrophic injury and wrongful death cases in a wide variety of personal injury claims including car crashes, tractor trailer accidents, brain injury and product liability. Chris Guedri's strong intellect and his burning desire to win coupled with his ability to master complex technical and medical information has made him a strong adversary against the insurance companies.

Chris Guedri was inducted as a Fellow of the prestigious International Academy of Trial Lawyers in 2008. He is AV rated by Martindale Hubbell and has been listed in Best Lawyers in America since 1997. He has been ranked by Virginia Super Lawyers and Virginia Business Magazine's Legal Elite.

Christopher Guedri works primarily out of Allen & Allen's Richmond, Virginia office but handles cases across Virginia and the United States.
