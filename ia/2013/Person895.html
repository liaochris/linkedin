Abigail Kies' Summary

When you win, I win. As an expert coach and trainer, effective one-on-one and in large groups, I've worked with hundreds of clients including Fortune 500 executives, government agencies, artists, professors, and entrepreneurs on topics ranging from career strategy to presentation skills to leadership and responsibility.

I enjoy working with people to achieve their goals and believe intentional communication is the key to success. Whether you're looking to improve your organization or transition to a new position or company, how you present yourself and your ideas is vital.

Specialties: Working with individuals and teams to improve their leadership skills and achieve their goals.


Leadership and communication coaching, team building, presentation skills, career coaching, resume coaching, interview preparation, group workshops and facilitation
