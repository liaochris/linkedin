Dave King's Summary

I am a design thinking generalist with over 16 years experience in creative and professional services organizations, as well as several start-up environments (my own and others'). My career has developed along side the Internet (1995-present) and has traced through the emergent markets and services associated with the ever-changing digital landscape.

Management experience (start-up & established)
Programming experience (client & server-side)
Design experience (how it works + some how it looks)
Producer experience (digital projects)

My superpowers are synthesis and collaboration. I'm able to see and work across complex situations and information to help people distill the important truths that drive clarity and alignment. I pride myself on being an ego-less collaborator, and strongly believe collaboration to be the most effective way to accelerate understanding, ideas, solutions, quality, and results.
