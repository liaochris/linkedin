Maria Arango Diener's Projects

  • Monumental Puzzle Prints

    • January 2009 to Present
    Team Members: Maria Arango Diener

    Monumental Collaborative Puzzle Prints are yearly projects in which I engage a number of international artists to collaborate with me. We create a large design composed of "puzzle pieces", each of which is designed and carved by a unique artist under a common theme. The project director designs and cuts the wood block into puzzle-like pieces, then sends the pieces to participant artists; they carve their own little design and send the tiny piece back. Then the director assembles the carved pieces. The entire design is printed as a woodcut print after the puzzle is reassembled and finally each participant receives a huge print encompassing the entire design.

  • 1000 Woodcuts

    • October 1998 to Present
    Team Members: Maria Arango Diener

    1000 Woodcuts is my official registered business name and my life-long Quest/Project to complete 1000 woodcut prints. Also, my main website and all profiles on all social media, just to keep my sanity.
