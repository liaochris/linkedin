ABOUT ME

Over the last ten years I have been building up a small business. The work is based around the graphic design market catering for clients many and varied needs. I have done a wide range of work, including websites, logos, personal greetings cards, flyers/advertising, menus, corporate identity, stationery and much more. You can view some my work at www.digi-den.com.

As part of my business, DIGIDEN Creative Media, I have undertaken freelance work for various design companies and print brokers.

My business clients include:
- The Welsh Assembly Government, Brochures.
- Newport County Council, School Stationery.
- Conservative Party, Literature.
- The Centre for Homeopathic Education, Literature.

I am a creative individual who welcomes new challenges and enjoys achieving and exceeding both the goals and expectations others have in my work, which can be seen in my portfolio.
