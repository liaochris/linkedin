Walter Adamson's Summary

To be clear from the outset - the goal of social business is not to be good or even the ‘best’ at social media. It’s the opportunity to be a better business because of social media. A lot of firms focus on the use of social media marketing tools which are, unfortunately, simply disposable skills. Many fail to appreciate that linkage and integration with the business is where sustainable skills and knowledge about the application of social technologies is built, and most valuable.

I help firms invest in appropriate social media assets which will accelerate their sales cycles, improve productivity, build brand and customer value, and attract and retain the best employees, while applying effective risk management and governance.

Many of the social media challenges revolve around helping clients improve the way they communicate externally and internally. Sometimes it’s about operationalising their content marketing strategy. Other times, it’s about building processes and workflows that can help scale social media globally. And many times, it’s fixing disjointed content and community management practices. All of these problems are solved by deploying a proper social business strategy.

I especially help business understand digital disruption i.e. how the intersection of the mega-trends of cloud computing, social enterprise, mobile and collaborative commerce are going to change their world, and how to get ahead of the curve. I understand mobile, cloud, social, social business intelligence, big data, predictive analysis and risk & control.

My business perspective derives from experience as a CIO, VP International Business Development, VP Systems Integration (including 5 years running Marketing and Sales) and as a trusted advisor to C-Level and Government Boards. In short, success requires a top-down strategically developed social media investment, not a bottom-up tools first approach.

I help you achieve that success.
