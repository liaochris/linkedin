Altan Khendup's Summary

The greatest potential for technology is to allow people to help one another.

It is the intersection of technology, social interactions, and the sharing of basic human perspectives that create new insights, new ways to look at existing problems and to discover opportunities.

That's why every step in my career has been been on a clear-cut course -- to inspire people and organizations to realize and tap into their inner potential, use great technology in smart ways, and drive tangible change and results at the same time.

Albert Einstein once said "Imagination is more important than knowledge, for knowledge is limited while imagination embraces the entire world." Helping others imagine what's possible and getting them there. That's me.

Specialties: Helping others see technology as a tool to get things done, leveraging these new tools to help people/organizations in a sustainable and strategic manner, that can be measured concretely, and maintained indefinitely.

Altan Khendup's Projects

  • Hadoop At Home

    • July 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Altan Khendup

    Bringing the world of Big Data to the individual professional by sharing insight and knowledge about running a Hadoop cluster in their own home.

    Inspired by my own personal and professional interests this project shows others how to install Hadoop on virtual and physical machines within the home for improving the understanding of Big Data and what it means.

  • Customizing Squarespace 6

    • January 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Altan Khendup

    Squarespace 6 is a powerful web platform that can provide a lot of functionality to anyone who wants to present a great website with almost no coding.

    One of the challenges of this platform like any new offering is that sometimes what is provided out of the box is not enough to accomplish certain things. The point of this effort is to show others how to customize their Squarespace experience by demonstrating how to use existing HTML5, CSS and Javascript approaches and incorporate those into the Squarespace platform.
