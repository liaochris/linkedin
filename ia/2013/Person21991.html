Do you have a book you would like reviewed?

Thank you so much for your interest in having me read and review your book. There are a few things you need to know:

I am in no way a professional reviewer. I just a blogger who loves sharing my thoughts on books I have read. I do not accept any form of compensation for my reviews/ opinions.

I am open to reading:

Adult

New Adult

Young Adult

The genres I read are:

Romance (just about any kind of M/F romance)

Dystopian / Fairy Tales

Urban Fantasy/ Fantasy

Paranormal / Science Fiction



I can only accept print books and Kindle (mobi) editions. I can no longer accept pdf files.

I will publish my reviews here on my blog as well as on Goodreads, Amazon, Shelfari and The Library Thing. I will share a link to my blog post on Twitter and Paulette’s Papers Facebook Page. If you would like me to publish my review somewhere else all you have to do is ask!

My reviews can include:

- Book Cover

- Book Info/ Synopsis

- Where to buy

- My personal and honest review

If you would like specific information included on the blog post please feel free to ask! I will gladly host giveaways, cover reveals and guest posts as long as I feel that they will be a good fit on my blog.

Please be aware that while I am constantly reading I do have a busy real life. It would be best if you could give me at least three months to read and review your book.

If I am unable to finish your book I will send you an email immediately to let you know.

For a review please contact me at:

dchapman @ paulettespapers.com (remove spaces)

Please include with your correspondence:

- Your name and contact info

- Title of book, genre and synopsis
