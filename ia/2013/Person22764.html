Cheyenne Palma Dominguez's Summary

Woman on the Move, community-building entrepreneur, philanthropy guru, and social media wiz.

Cheyenne is president of FemCity Philadelphia, LLC {a Femfessionals group}. Femfessionals connects ambitious business women through "connection lunches", "Pink Collar" workshops, and social media.

She is also co-founder (and woman behind the scenes) at National Guys Night Out, LLC - a new social and business club for accomplished men. www.nationalguysnightout.com

Cheyenne has over 12 years of experience in fundraising and non-profit development. Most recently, Cheyenne managed a private fundraising event with American Friends of The Royal Foundation of The Duke and Duchess of Cambridge and Prince Harry.

In 2011 she was featured as one of Main Line Today magazine’s top 24 “Women on the Move” and in 2006 was recognized as one of Miami’s “50 Savviest”.

Looking for a speaker or consultant? Topics include power networking, relationship-building, social media, work/life balance, philanthropy and more.
