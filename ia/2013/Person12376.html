  • NGE Marketing Nabs 4 LMA Midwest 'Your Honor Awards' in 2012, 1 National Recognition

    Legal Marketing Association
    • February 2012

    • Total Website: The redesigned and refreshed www.ngelaw.com, which launched in late September 2011.
