Dave W. Phillips' Summary

I'm a seasoned business executive, entrepreneur and general counsel. My expertise is in Corporate & Business Development, Startup Operations & Sales, Internet Law, and Corporate Finance.

I'm passionate about technology, entrepreneurship, innovation and building transformative online communities. After two decades in the trenches of technology entrepreneurship, business and law, I still believe in the power of technology to improve our lives and solve our critical global challenges.

Between 1994 and 2010, I helped build several iconic and successful Internet companies @AOL, @Napster, @IGN and @SAYMedia.

Currently, I'm an EVP @ Sidecar Technologies which is building a transformative "shared-transportation" network that is disrupting the industry and challenging incumbents and local regulators.

Backstory: Between 1994 and 2006, I served in operating, business development, and general counsel positions at America Online (AOL Inc., 1994-97), AOL Europe (AOL Inc./Bertelsmann AG, 1997-2000), Crunch Music Ltd. (acquired by Music Choice PLC UK, 2001), Napster (Bertelsmann 2001-02), IGN Entertainment (acquired by News Corp in 2005), and Naturalpath Media which we grew to become the largest US Green Ad Network (comScore 2008-09) before its acquisition by Six Apart/SAY Media (2009-10)

In 2011, I co-founded SONR Labs (now Hale Devices, Inc.), which develops mobile accessories and apps, and continue as a Director and advisor to the Company. Through Lark Ventures, I also did seed investments in Skout, Ekso Bionics, Curebit, MotionMath, Postling (acquired by LocalVox), and Massive Health (acquired by Jawbone).

I focus on the following areas: Strategic Partnerships & Business Development, Corporate Development & M&A, Internet Law & Regulation, Operations & Sales Management. I enjoy studying and chronicling the interplay between innovation (technology), competition (markets), and governance (law). Washington DC formed my perspective, but SF stole my heart.
