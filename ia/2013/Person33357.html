Debbie DiVirgilio, MNM, GPC, CGT's Summary

Debbie DiVirgilio, owner and principal consultant of The Faith-Based Nonprofit Resource Center, has more than 20 years of experience serving in the nonprofit sector. Since starting the consulting firm ten years ago (formerly known as DiVirgilio & Associates), she has acquired more than $40 million in funds to support the needs of individuals and communities.

Faith-Based Nonprofit Resource Center is a full-service non-profit consulting firm specializing in meeting the needs of non-profits of all sizes and at any stage in their life cycle. The Faith--Based Nonprofit Resource Center helps Christian organizations and nonprofit organizations with grant writing, starting a new nonprofit, 501(c)(3) filings, and more.

Debbie holds a Bachelor's Degree in Behavioral Sciences from Wilmington University and a Master's Degree in Non-Profit Management from Regis University. She is Grant Professional Certified as conferred by the Grant Professionals Institute and has served on the Board of Directors of the Grant Professionals Association for the past five years. Debbie is also a BoardSource Certified Governance Trainer. She is an active volunteer for several community-based organizations including United Way, Haven Community Church, and the local Christian Radio Station.
