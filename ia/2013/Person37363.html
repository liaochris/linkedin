Doug Wathen's Summary

Doug Wathen, a widely respected leader in the Orange County real estate market, brings to his career over 15 years of award-winning experience in corporate account sales and management that serves his clients well. As a broker associate with Prudential California Realty in Monarch Beach, CA, he takes pride in the fact that the majority of his business consists of referrals from satisfied customers. In addition, as owner of Wathen Realty Group, he stays active with a client base of some 5,000 investors that continues to grow.

Having been a licensed Broker in his native state of Colorado as well as California, he has a proven track record of sales results and customer satisfaction that truly sets him apart from the rest.

As a longtime resident of Southern Orange County, Doug enjoys giving back to his community by participating in a myriad of local activities. In addition to serving on the board of directors and coaching for the Laguna Niguel Youth Soccer Association, Doug is a dedicated volunteer at John Malcom Elementary School and the YMCA.

Described as a hardworking and loyal professional who always puts the best interests of his clients first, Doug continues to utilize his superb communication and negotiation skills to ensure that the buyers and sellers he represents attain their goals with complete satisfaction. He looks forward to assisting you with all of your real estate needs.

Doug invites you to visit his website for further details regarding his services and for a current listing of properties available for purchase: www.WathenRealty.com

“Building Dreams, Serving the Community, Changing Lives….”

Specialties

Having been involved in well over 1000 transactions nationwide since 2003, Doug specializes in single family homes, condominiums, luxury and investment properties along the coast in Southern Orange County, including the communities of Laguna Niguel, Monarch Beach, Dana Point, Laguna Beach, San Juan Capistrano, San Clemente, Aliso Viejo, Mission Viejo and Ladera Ranch.
