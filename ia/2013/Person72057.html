Karen O. Drake, M.A., ACC's Summary

Motivation matters when you need people to perform--decoding what drives behavior and the influencing language to trigger that motivation, enables teams to predict work behavior, avoid communication mishaps, and solve problems effectively. Want to know exactly what language to use – or avoid – to motivate and inspire?

Team alignment constitutes over 70% of my business-coaching an average of 10 (intact) work teams each year and strengthening emotional intelligence in teams is one of my specialties.
Need to diagnose organizational/team culture to introduce change and make it sustainable?
Maybe you're struggling with alignment among your purpose, people, & processes

If you want to develop higher effectiveness, new competencies, better balance, and resilience-all with more agility, fulfillment and less pain, let' talk.

ASSESSMENT PROFICIENCY- Inventory for Work Attitude & Motivation (iWAM), Language and Behavior (LAB) Profile, Leadership Effectiveness Analysis (LEA 360), MBTI, StrengthsFinder
