Mark Santora's Summary

For over 15 years I have run the gambit from Major Motion Picture Trailers (The World's End, Crash) to Videogame Commercial Campaigns (Rocksmith, ZombiU, Microsoft's Kinnect) to Production (the Mopar Charger) to TV Campaigns (Army Wives). I have worked solely as an editor on some of these, and on others I have managed the team and been with the project from inception all the way to completion. I work well in all types of environments.

My skillset is not just limited to editing. I concept and write, direct and edit, handle the VO sessions, and see it through to broadcast mix and delivery. I am familiar with all video formats and have delivered final picture on everything from tape to film. I am a full service editor.

Now for the tech stuff. I am fluent in both Avid Media Composer and Final Cut Pro. I am also familiar with Photoshop, After Effects, MS Office, Compressor, and multiple other programs. Camera-wise I have shot a great deal of footage on the Canon 7D/5D. Finally, I am Mac / Windows literate, and am very tech savvy (I build my systems).

I love what I do and it shows in my pieces. I bring a deep love of film, television, videogames, and comedy to my work.

I am looking for opportunities to work with talented people to produce interesting and entertaining projects.

- Knowledgeable in all aspects of post-production.
- Media analysis, I find the story in the footage.
- Copy Writing and Concept Creation.
- Casting & Directing voice actors.

Specialties: Avid Media Composer, Final Cut Pro, OSX, Windows XP/Vista/7, Photoshop, After Effects, Compressor, Encore, DVD Studio Pro.
