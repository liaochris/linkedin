Jai Gaitonde's Summary

DAY Elevator & Lift was founded in 1992, with the singular goal of elevating the quality of people’s lives. The founders of DAY created a company distinguished by teams of consummate professionals passionate about improving mobility by providing elevators and lifts that are safe, durable and dependable. Successful companies are built by the ingenuity, dedication and expertise of their employees. At DAY, our teams of trained professionals are comprised of the very best from every discipline.

Under the direction of qualified, experienced managers, our staff serves our clients with pride and enthusiasm. In an effort to provide each customer with careful attention and expertise, DAY has created three separate divisions: Residential Elevators, Residential Accessibility and Commercial Accessibility.
