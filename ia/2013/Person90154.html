Rick JR Mayberry's Summary

I have been a Network Engineering, Information Security and Network Security professional since 1998. I have experience in almost all aspects of risk management, information security and network engineering. I am most passionate about security operation's excellence and end to end security of service provider networks. I spent a number of years securing the largest converged network in the World and building tools and technologies to secure networks.

I have taken a greater interest in formal risk management and security architecture methodologies. This experience has taught me to communicate with the business about security, measure security program success and develop security program roadmaps driven by business objectives.

During my career, I have been consistently recognized for my technical strength and aptitude. I am regularly described as approachable, easy going and a chameleon that can fit into any situation. I feel the most rewarding aspect of my work is being able to mentor and groom technical talent within an organization.

I have held organization leadership positions (5+ years), technical leadership positions, complex program management and individual contributor roles. I have had several opportunities to build and lead organizations from the ground up. I enjoy working with and challenging Engineering teams and strong technical talent.

In my most recent role, I ran the Security Architecture function for the World's Most Recognized Brand, Coca-Cola.
