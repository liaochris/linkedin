Mary Seibolt's Experience

Designer, Owner

Baby Rooms by Nana

April 2000 – Present (13 years 9 months) Lenexa, Ks.

I am a mother of four, grandmother of 14 and have done daycare for 30 years. I have been quilting and embroidering for 8 years as Mary's Quilting and Embroidery. Then in 2008 I change the name of my business to Baby Rooms By Nana!

I have had so much fun making it my business. Making the perfect space for your little one can be a wonderful experience.

I design baby rooms,bumper pads bibs, blankets, soothies, burpcloths, valances, window seats, boppy covers,adult throws...anything using quality minky fabric, and embroidery on all. You are sure to find the baby decor that fits your style.
I have also branched out into making pillow cases for any age, with embroidered names, warm winter scarves, wedding throws, anything for any age that can keep you warm and cuddled!

Whether you are looking to complete the entire nursery, or need a gift, Baby Rooms by Nana can assist you!

All our Minky items are custom monogrammed to add that special touch for your special one!
