As the firm's Litigation Director, I work closely with the attorneys in determining and executing the firm's overall strategy. Essential elements to the development of this strategy include caseload management, trial preparation, case strategy, client screening and communications, legal research, drafting motions, briefs, discovery, discovery responses and correspondence, as well as management of the firm's legal calendar.

I have also been tasked with orchestrating and directing the firm's internship program. Though the program is still in it's early phases, we have already had interns from Heald College's paralegal program, Web Legal Education's international internship program, and Berkeley Law. I am also in the process establishing partnerships with other schools in the area, including UC Hastings, University of San Francisco, and others. Over the past year, I have managed as many as four interns at a time.

The firm brought me on during a time of considerable structural changes. Within my first six months, I led a complete re-branding of the firm, including the creation of a new website and blog, a redeveloped marketing strategy, and a strong social media presence.

In addition to these strategic branding initiatives, I'm in charge of managing and developing the firm's technological infrastructure. Such improvements include the firm's digital filing system, calendaring system, and other case management programs.
