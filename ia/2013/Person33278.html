Deanna Barnert's Summary

Seasoned, hardworking and successful media professional and television critic seeking exciting new opportunity. Background includes extensive work in multi-media journalism, as well as project management and PR/marketing.

For over a decade, I have specialized in covering the entertainment beat for major outlets like MSN and SheKnows. I work celebrity profiles, news stories, features and critical pieces from conception to production; perform on-camera celebrity interviews; and appear on television as an industry expert. As an industry vet, I rely on my connections to land exclusive opportunities, break news and negotiate access to best-of-breed content.

Throughout my career, I have also worked as a freelance copywriter, writing paid editorials and helping clients create individualized marketing materials.

On the technical side, my experience with website management, blogging and trend reporting have provided me a strong understanding of the online publication processes, multi-media tools and social programming. I have learned the nuts and bolts of posting via CMS and HTML; communicating with in-house tech, design and writing teams; and utilizing social networking and SEO.

Under my management, MSN TV's Soap Superfan blog peaked at 600K page views in February, 2010. That same year, TheWrap tapped me as one of the "25 TV Superfans to Follow on Twitter."

My success working with celebrities, publicists, CEOs, sources and other writers speaks to interpersonal, communication and organizational skills honed long before my foray into journalism. As a project manager in the '90s, I oversaw the production of training materials for Fortune 500 companies; managing internal and client teams to keep projects on track, on deadline and on budget.

I also garnered extensive experience managing volunteers; organizing public relations events and spearheading outreach and marketing programs during my years in the non-profit sector.
