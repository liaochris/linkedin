Marishka Glynne's Summary

Marishka is a Dynamic, Vivacious, and Entertaining Speaker who loves to share her knowledge, expertise and wisdom to guide individuals to lead a more fulfilling life and to have Fun! She has written a book “The Master Switch”- Turn on your success. She has also co-authored “Roadmap to Success” with Dr. Deepak Chopra, and “Success Simplified” with Dr. Steven Covey (who wrote “The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People”.)

One of the world’s leading self-empowerment experts, she specializes in helping and guiding people around the world to discover their own personal power, so that they live their lives with passion. Her experience, education and high end achievement in the corporate world have made her an expert on the topic of personal development.

Marishka has a thriving private practice motivating and propelling people to greater success. She has Master’s degree in NLP (Neuro-Linguistic Programming), and also in Time Line Therapy. She counsels clients on the removal of personal barriers and limited beliefs that are career and/or life blocks.

She is available for Keynote speeches, breakout sessions, workshops, training, retreats, consulting, coaching and media appearances. Marishka entertains, enlightens and educates.

Marishka was Entrepreneur of the Year nominee in Oakville.for 2010.

To learn more about how she can help you get more traction on issues that have been holding you back, visit her website at www.Marishkaglynne.com
