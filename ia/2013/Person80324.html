Lesley Clark's Summary

An award winning consultant, Lesley Clark has 19 years’ experience in the PR business, working with a diverse range of clients across Scotland and beyond.

Clark Communications was established in 2012 out of a passion for delivering strategic communications campaigns which are directly wired to the needs of the client. Lesley offers big agency thinking, coupled with focused client management and attention to detail.

A creative communicator and savvy crisis handler, and with an enviable contacts book, she has devised and delivered successful PR campaigns for a wealth of clients across a variety of sectors, including corporate, consumer, public sector, professional & financial services, commercial property and transport.

Specialties: Specialises in defining and managing strategy. Her strengths lie in delivering creative campaigns dealing with complex issues. Key skills include client management, planning and agenda setting; strategic media relations; marketing; crisis, issue and regulatory project management; collateral development (web and print) and profile raising.

Sectors: Financial/professional, corporate, consumer, tourism/visitor attraction, energy, public sector, commercial property, transport.
