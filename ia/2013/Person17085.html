Brett Mallory Miles' Summary

Milestones, Inc. is a leadership and management development company oriented around "integral" thinking and methods. Integral refers to an advanced model for including a whole and systemic approach to the work of empowering leaders and teams to "be" excellent. Using a sports analogy, the goal is outstanding results, such as winning the championship, not just having a "wining season". Individuals must be game-ready with a winning attitude. The team must function as a whole. So we support alignment of people, goals, culture, and systems. When clients face individual or organizational change or challenges, we help them "shift" what they are "thinking" and how they are "being", not just what they are doing. With a desire to grow and expand, clients can shift how they are showing up in the "game". To be the best, empowered leaders need to be Responsible, Committed, Contributing, Authentic, and Enabling Buy-in. These five guiding principles embody our Empowering Leadership model and programs. Delivery includes executive coaching, and facilitation of team building, organizational alignment, strategic planning, and business development.

Specialties: Empowering Leadership, Strategic Thinking & Planning, Teaming, Transformation, and Revenue Generation (through business development strategies and skill development)
