Jim Fowler, PE, CEA, CBCP's Summary

Jim Fowler, PE, CEA, CBCP's Experience

Founder, Principal

Global Energy Audits

2010 – Present (3 years) Greater Seattle Area

Consulting Energy Engineering focused on reducing energy costs and consumption for building owners and operators. Providing strategy and implementation including planning, energy auditing, scope definition and commissioning for municipal, industrial and commercial buildings.

Energy Strategy: Portfolio evaluation & consolidation, facility benchmarking & planning, capital project staging & implementation.

Energy Assessments: Comprehensive or targeted ASHRAE Level 1, 2 and 3, and Investment Grade Energy Audits including site surveys, energy simulation modeling, capital and operational recommendations and scope development.

Commissioning, re-commissioning and retro-commissioning of buildings and building systems.

Expertise in energy reduction in schools, warehouses, manufacturing facilities, public works buildings, municipal transit maintenance facilities, police and fire stations, community and recreation centers, pools, ice & hockey arenas, office buildings, multi-unit residential dwellings, hotels and senior centers and juvenile and adult detention facilities.

Unique experience in remote locations & arctic regions, including Alaskan villages.
