Carol Corcoran's Summary

Welcome to my Linkedin profile. I am the Clinical Director of Creating Healthy Connections where I provide couples counseling to couples in distress searching for deeper more meaningful connections. As a Licensed Clinical Marriage & Family Therapist (LCMFT, LMFT) in both Maryland and California, I utilize my training and expertise to help couples, and family members work through relationship issues utlizing Emotionally Focused Couples Therapy, one of the most effective and well researched forms of couples therapy.

I am also a EMDRIA Certified EMDR therapist; however, I am currently working within a couple or family context using Emotionally Focused Therapy (not to be confused with Emotion Freedom Technique) because research and my experience shows that working within an attachment focused therapy framework is faster healing and more transformational when it relates to trauma, loss/grief and other life challenges.

Specialties: Couples counseling, premarital counseling, affairs and infidelity, love and sexual acting out, trauma and anxiety, grief and loss, and other concerns
