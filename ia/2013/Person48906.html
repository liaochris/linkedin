Giorgos Koutinas' Summary

After having sound background and experience in engineering and project management issues, the year 1992 entered in the in the insurance business sector. Started as Underwriter with a French Insurance Group where obtained specialist know-how about insurance of business risks. Continued as an independent Insurance Expert providing consulting services focused on major construction projects and general liability issues.
In 2002 created the "KOUTINAS SA - Insurance Brokers" company, collaborating with Greek insurance companies and with the London Insurance Market.
Since December 2007, operates as accredited Lloyd's Binding Authority (Lloyd's Coverholder) in Greece and since 2011 in Cyprus, Bulgaria and Romania.
Promotes specialist insurance business in Greece and in the nearby SEE countries, focusing on Professional Indemnity Insurance and other "niche" areas where added-value services are required.
In order to contribute in the development of the Liability Insurance issues in the Greek and Cypriot market has created a Blog the www.professional-liability.gr (available in Greek language).

Specialties & Expertise in Insurance: Professional Indemnity, Liability, Construction, Corporate and Special Business Risks.
