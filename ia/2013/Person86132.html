Mariot Chauvin's Publications

  • Towards a Viewpoint-Based Framework for Reactive Systems Modeling

    • 2010
    Authors: Mariot Chauvin, Frédéric Thomas, Gonzague Reydet, Etienne Juliot

    This publication introduces the nascent technology developed by Obeo to tackle the integration of heterogeneous viewpoints during the design process of reactive systems. The key idea of this technology is to combine model-based technologies and viewpoint-based approaches in order to adapt generic model-editing features to specific design concerns. This publication presents an industrial implementation of a graphical
    and timing viewpoint-based tool.
