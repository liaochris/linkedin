We are your comprehensive creative resource for any project. We operate a high-end production facility energized by a unique core of motivated and talented professionals. The prolific nature of our team originates with the ability to skillfully manage your project from concept through the final edit.

Owner

DropDrop.com

January 2008 – Present (5 years 9 months)

DropDrop.com LLC allows you to download and customize amazing Adobe After Effects files! (After Effects Templates) These files have awesome pre-made animations. All you have to do is download them, edit the text or graphics and then render out! All these projects are royalty free. These files will give you a bold presence, which cut through the clutter of other animations making your presentation much more memorable.

Plus you save hundreds of hours doing tedious after effects key framing! You can save time when you need something in a time crunch. If you are not a skilled after effects user, these files will help you deliver professional animations at an extremely affordable price. If you are a veteran AE user you have the opportunity to gain much more from these projects as a starting point for what you are working on.
