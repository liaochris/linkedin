Paula Wickham's Summary

Paula Wickham is an industry expert in first aid and emergency response training, products and technology. She became passionate about “talking first aid” while working with a trauma nurse who had and idea for a product that could help save lives. Paula formed Think Safe, Inc. which manufactures First Voice, the only American Disabilities Act compliant Emergency Instruction Device (EID) and responder kits. Wickham is a certified American Heart Association, American Safety & Health Institute, and Emergency Care and Safety Institute health care provider or lay rescuer instructor. She is a member of National Safety Council and Iowa Emergency Medical Services Association as well as other safety associations. Ms. Wickham travels extensively, speaking as a subject matter specialist on innovations & technology within organizational first aid or emergency response programs. Events she has spoken at include: OSHA Volunteer Protection Program (VPPPA) seminars, American Society of Safety Engineers, Marine Trawlerfest, International Fuel & Ethanol Workshop 2008 and other major national and regional conferences. She is on the American National Standards Institute (ANSI) review committee for ANSI/OSHA first aid kit requirements.

Paula has a background that includes successfully introducing & licensing products, business turnarounds, emerging businesses, and business startups. Professional experience in finance, M&A, commercial banking and business management.

Specialties

Think Safe has expertise in emergency solutions: first aid, CPR, BBP (blood borne pathogens) & Universal Precautions, Basic Life Support, Automated External Defibrillators (AEDs) and other safety training. We bring technology to safety & first aid including iphone/smartphone apps, PC apps and training (types of training including instructor (in person), blended and online solutions), new product solutions and kitting solutions for emergency response, first aid and trauma situations.
