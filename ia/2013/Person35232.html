Diane C. Olsen's Summary

Diane has been a painter for over 20 years. Her palette knife landscape oil paintings have received awards and been featured in magazines and books over the years.

Diane's love of color, shape, movement, and texture are seen in her abstract oil paintings. Her sailboat paintings are a reflective and playful stylized metaphor for our journey in life. There is a strong spiritual component to all of her works, arising out of her faith and daily living experiences.

She writes an insightful and inspirational weekly blog featuring a new small abstract painting each week. These posts can be found on her websites, www.dianeolsenstudio.com and www.dianeolsen.org.

Desert Harbor Life Coaching - In her work as a Life Coach, Diane offers warm and creative life coaching for those who desire, and are ready to learn positive, effective, and satisfying ways of problem solving and purposeful living; personal discovery, creative expression, and growth. She guides her clients to set and reach personal and creative goals, teaches her clients to better manage their thoughts and daily life, and helps them step by step to reach their goals and achieve a new sense of well being. She offers spiritual inspiration and affirmation throughout the coaching process.

Diane leads painting workshops, and art/life coaching workshops such as "Painting Your Journey"...Creatively Exploring Your Life as Art", and "Heart2Art".

She has written three books for women: an autobiographical book titled "An Honorable Journey of Healing...A Woman's Story"; An Honorable Journey of Healing...A Workbook for Women"; and "A Daily Journey for Women of Honor...A Writing Journal". All three books feature her paintings.

These books are available on Diane's website and through Amazon.com.

Specialties: Painting in oil with the palette knife and brush. Landscapes, abstracts, stylized sailboats, and commissioned horse and animal portrait work.

Diane is available as a speaker and for workshops.
