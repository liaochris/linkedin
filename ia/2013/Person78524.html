Larry Winters' Summary

Larry Winters is the President of Tri-State Industrial Group, responsible for strategy development and execution, financial stewardship and return, and leading the team in the company’s core values and beliefs. Larry is responsible for the Executive Leadership Team and drives Tri-State’s overall growth planning and delivery.

A successful leader, entrepreneur, author, speaker, and consultant for numerous Fortune 1000 companies, domestically and internationally, Larry has worked with companies such as JB Hunt, Williams, Capital One, Hawker Beechcraft, Boeing, and many others. He also consulted and supported several state governments, including Kansas, Oklahoma, and Texas. Most recently, prior to joining Tri-State, Larry was Executive Vice President of The Persimmon Group, a regional business consulting firm, where he worked within multiple industries, including Oil and Gas, Aerospace Manufacturing, Construction, Financial Services, and Information Technology.

Larry is a Project Management Institute-certified Project Management Professional (PMP), a member of the American Association of Cost Engineering, and a member of the National Speakers Association (NSA). Larry earned a Bachelor’s degree in Economics from the University of Arkansas at Little Rock and a Master’s degree in Information Systems from the University of Arkansas. Larry and his wife Rose have three children and are active supporters of the Hispanic American Foundation and a number of children and family related organizations in Oklahoma and Arkansas.
