Co-owner

KAPPS Training

June 2003 – September 2008 (5 years 4 months)

KAPPS training was a 1:1 small group training aesthetic injectable company created to meet the needs of the aesthetic community. It was the gold standard in the industry. Over the many years of treating and training a consistent theme was noted. Many many patients present with annoying lines and asymmetrical tissue shift and nothing was offered to correct those except to fill or Botox where appropriate. The problem with this technique is that it is costly and in fact a very temporary fix. In November 2008 I joined Young Innovations and was involved in the creation of the enVy (tm) pillow .. An invention that enables the user to sleep comfortably on their side without disrupting delicate facial tissue. enVy (tm) is now being sold in Plastic/Derm/Physio/Chiro and sleep clinics and is meeting the needs of clients in keeping them in perfect alignment, off their face and with their airways open. enVy(tm) the luxurious necessity!

Kim Renton's Patents

  • United States Patent enVy the antiaging pillow

    • United States Patent US D618,029S
    • Issued June 22, 2010
    Inventors: Kim Renton, Kathy Young Keefe

    Ergonomic Pillow designed to provide Cervical Spine (Neck) alignment while side sleeping or back sleeping with the added benefit of keeping the sleeping client off their face, jaw and delicate facial tissue. Applications include prevention of sleep creases and facial asymmetries, post operative corrective or cosmetic surgeries of facial structures such as dental procedures and plastic surgery, TMD, post skin rejuvenation procedures and support and promotion of proper neck alignment.
