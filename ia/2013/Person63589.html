José Silva's Summary

José has broad international experience in finance, banking, consulting, languages and other disciplines.

José is actively involved in serving his community. He was President of the Daytona Beach West Rotary Club and serves on the board of various not-for-profit organizations. He is currently President of Business Network International (BNI) in Daytona Beach. José teaches local students the basics of saving, investing and managing money.

Being the eldest of eight siblings, José has always cherished the family as his number one priority. He and his wife, Diana, have two children: Natalia, 20 years of age and Sebastian, 16 years old. The Silva family is very fortunate to have beautiful Costa Rica as their second home where they spend over a month every year enjoying its many wonders - perfect waves, incredible flora and fauna, an ideal climate and some of the happiest people in the world!

Specialties: ◊ Utilize technology & Internet to maximize productivity, acquire knowledge, and develop business and personal solutions.
◊ Multilingual: English, Spanish, French, Portuguese, Italian. Studying Mandarin, Hebrew & Japanese
