Lisa O'Reilly CE's Summary

I have worked in evaluation, public sector policy analysis, and strategic planning since 2002. I have successfully completed projects for public sector and not-profit organizations that include strategic planning, evaluation, performance measurement, and results-based management.

I have led or participated in over thirty-five evaluation and evaluation-related projects in Canada and abroad for government and not-profit organizations. I have developed strategic planning and risk mitigation planning documents, including Cabinet documents. These include products for program implementation staff, senior managers and executive decision-makers.

Previously I worked for two federal government departments on a term basis: for Foreign Affairs and International Trade as a Senior Strategic Planner and as part of the Georgia Basin Action Plan Secretariat for Environment Canada’s Pacific and Yukon Region. Prior to those positions, I was a Senior Consultant with Performance Management Network Inc. (PMN), based in Ottawa, Canada.
