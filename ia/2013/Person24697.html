Chris Whisenhunt's Summary

Yup, that's me. Biting the face of the love of my life off. It's my usual answer to issues I run into these days while working with software. That's right, I bite the issue off and swallow it whole and usually in a more timely manner than most. Lately I have been pulled onto multiple critical projects because of the my willingness and ability to do what I described above. All below budget and completed before the scheduled delivery. I do it all, and I like doing it all. I couldn't have asked for a more intriguing and thought provoking position at Blackbaud.

You guessed it, I'm a software developer with a heavy background in SQL and multi-tier architecture. I have been part of many full life cycle implementations including requirement gathering, designing, and engineering on multi-million dollar projects. I specialize in implementing my chaotic thoughts and polishing them off to become well designed and developed masterpieces.

I live and work remotely in Fayetteville, WV while traveling to and from client sites. While the travel isn't the most fun thing to do, it is and should be part of any good consultant's job. Because being on site and dealing with an issue is completely different than sitting in an office and troubleshooting it. In my opinion the real stars shine when put in front of frustrated clients with high expectations all the while still developing products and fixing the problems at hand.

I have participated in multiple non-profit organizations including, but not limited too: Carolina Climber's Coalition, Access Fund, James Island County Park, and Yoga Benefits Kids.

Specialties: ASP.Net, Relational Database Design, Multi-Tier Architecture, C#, VB
