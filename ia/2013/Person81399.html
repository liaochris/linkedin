Lisa Lundy's Summary

In early September 2007, I published "The Super Allergy Girl - Allergy & Celiac Cookbook" to help people who have allergies, Celiac disease, Autism, and the people who want to cook and bake gluten-free, dairy-free, egg-free, and tree and peanut free. It is my plan to use my cookbook to raise awareness about Celiac disease since just over 3 million Americans have it, and at the present time 97% of those people are not yet diagnosed.

While on a path to help get Celiacs diagnosed which means saving the lives of these almost 3 million Americans, I am also intent on increasing awareness of the impact that food has on human beings from behavior, emotions and intellectual capacities. Public speaking is one of my great loves which I have been engaged in for many years. I was delighted to find Toastmasters International and attain my Advanced Toastmasters Silver distinction.

My primary mission in life is to contribute to humanity. This is what calls me in a way that inspires and lights me up. Many blessings to you!
