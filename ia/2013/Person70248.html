Juan Mateu's Summary

I become founder of Solaiemes (innovation start-up focused on telecom & mobility) after 12 years of professional experience in IT, foreign trade, consultancy and telecom (working in cellular infrastucture and mobile devices supplier) and being assigned in several countries.
I like to invent things, things to make the world better and make easy for people to create new communication servcies and use them.
Envisioning and making feasible how to approach telcos, developers and users in a win/win/win way.

We think Telco must embrace the ecosystem by providing a FTB, a "Telco Friendly Border", and a friendly telco border for internet developers with no knowledge of telco protocols is based on REST.

Solaiemes aims to help telcos to expose IMS as REST API to help telcos to become "platforms" and allow them to transitionate to a new role. Solaiemes pioneered the RCS joyn network REST API exposure.

Previously we tried the same for other telco enablers as Push To Talk and 3G Videocall/Videoshare and got the knowledge to do it for joyn.

The potential as platform of joyn RCS is starting to be covered by analyst firms as
http://blogs.forrester.com/dan_bieler/13-03-04-joyn_next_generation_business_communications

We have the key piece, the API exposure. Also, we are pioneering the use of WebRTC to make ubiquitous the telco communications, with Solaiemes technology a landline has a also a parallel ubiquitous life :-)

Entrepreneuring is hard, entrepreneuring in the telco technology a bit harder, and do it in Spain a permanent test of resiliance, patience or foolishness as no one is prophet in his own land.

Well, I want to pass this test

Juan Mateu's Experience

CEO & co-founder

Solaiemes

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Telecommunications industry

December 2006 – Present (7 years 1 month) Madrid Area, Spain

CEO and co-founder, Solaiemes is a company focused in extending the concept of open & ubiquitous communications for the masses of users and the mass of developers. We believe that telcos need a Friendly Telco Border (FTB) talking REST, to allow their communication enablers to be used by 3rd parties with no telco protocols knowledge. The combination of FTB and the WebRTC technologies to create freely whatever endpoint needed are the future trend in the telecom & internet space.

We are implementing this vision to help 3rd party ecosystem to create communications (mobile & UC) solutions based on common enablers (Rich Communication Suite, RCS-e, commercial name of telco service is joyn) providing TELCO API's infrastructure.
