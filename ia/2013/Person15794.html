Boris Bogatin's Summary

Focus on funding and helping develop businesses that leverage next-generation mobile, wireless, and Internet technologies and business models to unlock large scale opportunities:
- As MD of Hilltop Management, investing in and advising emerging wireless, mobile, and Internet opportunities, as an operating partner with Lumia Capital and as a strategic advisor to a leading TMT private equity fund
- As CEO and Founder of NearVerse, created a market-leader in the fields of real-time, proximity, and social mobile Internet (LoKast mobile app), and in mobile CDN and precise location ID market areas (NearCloud tech) with $1.8M in funding
- As VP of Corporate Development and Technology Strategy at LightSquared, helped start a $ multi-billion U.S. 4G wireless operator and spectrum holder, with over $1.5 billion in funding
- Working in Corporate Development for Cidera, helped grow one of the first streaming media CDN providers with $137 million in funding
- Working at PRTM (now PWC), advised a range of companies including Navipath, InfoLibria, BCE Emergis and helped start PRTM's emerging businesses' group
- Other prior experience in private equity and investment banking
