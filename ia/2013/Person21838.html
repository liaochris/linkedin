Chad Voss' Summary

I'm an experienced UX designer and prototyping lead with background in mobile UI, NUI, software development, publishing and broadcast animation. I have experience leading teams of both designers and developers in multiple disciplines. I was the primary UI designer on multiple features (including Browser, Contacts, Camera, Photos, among others) of Microsoft's KIN phone. I invented an image-analysis toy (UCreate Games) that was distributed through Mattel at Walmart, Target, and Toys R Us. I have co-created animations (VeggieTales) that have been broadcast on NBC and Telemundo.

Specialties

Product design, Animation, UX design, Mobile phone design, Serious programmer chops (C#, C, Actionscript, etc.), NUI.

  • Semi-autonomous robot that supports multiple modes of navigation

    • United States Patent 20120215380
    • Issued February 23, 2011
    Inventors: Chad Voss, Sebastien Fouillade, Nate Clinton, Malek Chalabi, Charles Olivier, Russell Sanchez

    Described herein are technologies pertaining to robot navigation. The robot includes a video camera that is configured to transmit a live video feed to a remotely located computing device. A user interacts with the live video feed, and the robot navigates in its environment based upon the user interaction. In a first navigation mode, the user selects a location, and the robot autonomously navigates to the selected location. In a second navigation mode, the user causes the point of view of the video camera on the robot to change, and thereafter causes the robot to semi-autonomously drive in a direction corresponding to the new point of view of the video camera. In a third navigation mode, the user causes the robot to navigate to a selected location in the live video feed.
