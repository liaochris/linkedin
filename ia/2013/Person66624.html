John Lundholm's Summary

I develop clients and business sources, writing home loans for purchases and for refinances. I write loans for primary homes, second homes, and investment properties including condos / co-ops / single-family / multi-family, conforming, jumbo and portfolio loans.

Wells Fargo is the premier mortgage lender in the country, and with good reason. The Private Mortgage Bank is a specialized unit within Wells Fargo where we are granted additional lending ability tailored to the complexity of the higher end market. We can attend to a wide variety of client needs from the basic cookie cutter transactions to complex transactions like non-warrantable condos, asset dissipation loans, and foreign national loans. I also personally specialize in co-ops. They are quirky and a lot of people fear them, but I love co-ops!

I am based in Manhattan, so a large portion of my clients are tri-state (NY/NJ/CT), but I lend nationally to support the needs of my clients.
