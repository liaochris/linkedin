John Vardalos' Summary

John Vardalos is currently the director of business development at JFIVE, a digital agency that provides technology and consulting services for organizations interested in social business transformation. Big data analytics, cloud computing and mobile are disrupting how traditional companies engage with their employees, customers and business partners. John leverages his experience and professional network to help companies stay ahead of this paradigm shift by providing practical advice and solutions to adjust culture, IT systems and business processes that better align to the values and expectations of the Generation "Y" workforce.

Previously, John led business development and marketing efforts in Western Canada for Capgemini Consulting and Ernst and Young LLP working on challenging projects involving finance, human resources, IT, risk management, audit and supply chain. Prior to his time in management and technology consulting, he had regional responsibility for several fast growing technology companies with a focus on establishing channel partnerships, increasing sales and improving business operations.

John is a serial entrepreneur, aspiring venture capitalist and technology junky who graduated from the University of Manitoba with a BA in Economics and Asian Studies.
