Brooke Waskewich's Summary

Prior to segueing into Construction Recruitment, my background was in the 5 star hotel industry in Australia, where I was in Sales & Marketing for Australia's largest 5 star hotel.

When the new owners decided to spend $200m turning the hotel into the first 6 star hotel in the world, I spent a lot of time on-site with the Senior PMs, who taught me construction processes & procedures. When the project fell apart, it was the Director of Precon who suggested that I would make a good construction recruiter. Then, when I arrived in the US in 2004, one of my clients, the Director of Precon & Estimating, trained me in estimating, so that I could recruit effectively for him.

I am a relationship-builder and win business through my direct, genuine approach to professional relationships. My clients and my candidates know that they can trust me, and relationships that I forge are long-term. brookeSearch is not about putting 'bodies' into jobs, it is about becoming a true Business Partner. I get to know my clients, so that I can understand the type of person who will fit into your team; and find you candidates with the skills and experience you need, as well as the personality to be a great fit in your team.

brookeSearch, LLC is a different kind of search firm ... My goal is to partner with my clients and candidates, to match candidates' career motivations with a company's long and short term recruiting challenges.

Good recruiting is about understanding your clients' corporate culture and needs, as well as knowing your candidates' and their needs.

I care about my clients and my candidates and I consider my job done well when both parties call me to say 'Thank You'. That's the adrenaline rush in the recruitment industry!

Specialties: Providing quality mid to senior level professionals for the Commercial Construction, Civil & Structural Engineering, and Commercial Property Management/Facilities Industries.

    Associated Builders and Contractors (ABC) is a national association with 77 chapters representing 25,000 merit shop construction and construction-related firms with two million employees.

    Established in 1958, ABC of Metro Washington is one of 75 chapters in a nationwide federation of more than 23,000 merit shop construction and construction-related firms with nearly two million employees. Locally, ABC of Metro Washington is more than 500 member companies strong.

    ABC members are dedicated to free enterprise, open competition, and the merit shop philosophy. They are the builders of schools, hospitals, government buildings, recreational facilities, commercial centers, industrial plants, bridges, roads, museums, churches, and more.

    Merit Shop is a way of doing business in which companies reward employees based on performance and encourage them to reach their highest level of achievement, and in which contracts are awarded based on safety, quality and value, regardless of labor affiliation.
