Amy Lathrop's Summary

You've done the hard part. You've written a book - 'the' book. Now what? Wondering how to let people know you've written the next best seller? Let us help you Spread the Word through both traditional and new media publicity campaigns. Over the years we've built great relationships with media and bloggers who LOVE books. We custom design each campaign to both fit the author's personality and best showcase the book. We help you connect with your audience through radio interviews, guest blogging, book reviews, and of course, utilizing social media platforms. (Need a little social networking 101? --We can help with that too!)

Our services include:

•Plan/execute blog tours
•Facebook Parties
•Project manage marketing campaigns (promotional contests, videos, book trailers)
•Media kits and press release campaigns
•Live Author events
•Send out newsletters and eblasts
•Project manage web site and blog creation or oversee a “freshening up” your current sites
•Build author and/or speaking platform
•Branding

...and basically anything else you'd like to accomplish. We offer proven project management skills and ten+ years working exclusively in the Christian publishing market.

Additional services include:

•Designing postcards/bookmarks/print collateral
•Coordinating interviews
•Author Assist services
•Creating a blog
•Overseeing your book trailer
•Social media creation and management

**Editorial Services: manuscript mapping and editing
