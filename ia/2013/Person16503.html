Brandon Kekich's Summary

I have been in the real estate industry for over 19 years.
During that time I have taught various courses for the State of Michigan licensing division, sold new construction projects, commercial ventures and have had extensive experience with residential real estate.
As a Team Leader in Michigan and Florida, I have been involved of 1000s of transactions, including hundreds of private and government foreclosures, all the way to $15,000,000 luxury home sales!
I have owned two RE/MAX offices and in 1996 was the youngest broker/owner in RE/MAX International's history.
I was name one of NAR's "30 under 30."
Those offices (Northville & Riverview, MI) roared to success and at our peak, I was given an opportunity for a leadership role at Keller William in Florida.
So - In 2007, I decided to move to Florida to help relaunch a Keller Williams Market Center and was later charged with forming and executing the vision for three Top Selling Keller Williams Locations with a total of over 200 active real estate professionals.
Now, I am back home in Michigan (February 2011) and back at RE/MAX doing what we do best. Helping buyers and sellers.
We consistently placed in the Top 5 for sales for all associates in the Michigan and Northern Ohio region.
In Michigan (as in Florida) we have realized a marked increase in production despite the fact that the area has experienced several years of depreciating values and a 60% decrease in the number of homes sold.
Values are steadying now and though the regions sales are steady, our private re sales are by over 70% over this time last year!
Mission accomplished!
