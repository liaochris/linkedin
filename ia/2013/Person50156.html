Greg Becker's Summary

Integrity / Passion / Influence

I enjoy the interaction between people and what they perceive to be realistic. I truly want to change the world, equipped and skilled with design intelligence and the hard work of defining the thin line between common cynicism, and true innovation; emotionally, socially, and existentially.

I want to change up the pace of what it means to create for a living. I want to design for what really matters: People, and their understanding of reality beyond the surface of cheap commercial techniques, and trite marketing ploys. Let's design products to create change, influence all, and support good. Let's solve for the real problems...rather than create new ones.
