Brandon McRae's Summary

I’ve been at this about a decade now…designing intuitive and compelling experiences for software, web sites and print communications. Currently, I work as a UX Designer/Product Designer for Microsoft at the company headquarters in Redmond, Wa.

Since 2003, I have worked independently as a freelance graphic designer, web designer and user experience designer for private, corporate and agency clients throughout Western Washington and beyond. I bring passion to my work and a strong intuition for design integrity combined with a well-grounded understanding of design principles and a solid understanding of technology's application in design.

I have seen my share of bad design in communications and desktop and web applications, affecting message delivery in communications, task success and comprehension in applications and loss of sales in e-commerce scenarios. I add value to my projects by bringing real-world experience, creative, technical and writing skills to bear on the challenge at hand. In addition to my passion for design, I am an open communicator, an active collaborator, listening first to clients' needs and ideas, then translating them into informed, strategic design solutions.

Client projects include: Software User Experience (UX), User Interface (UI) design, corporate identity, marketing collateral, advertising, product packaging and Flash animation.
