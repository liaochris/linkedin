Derek Elley's Summary

Hi,

I specialize in guiding new digital solutions to market.

I've led startups, creative agencies and brands to create, produce and market new solutions that have won awards, global media attention, millions of visitors, thousands of customers, millions in revenues and capital value.

Driven by creativity and metrics, I thrive on bringing together customers, marketers, designers, engineers and financiers to lead the creation, production and growth of new products.

Currently I'm chief executive, board director and investor at Ponoko (Wellington and San Francisco), an incredible team helping product designers become superstars.

As an entrepreneurial strategist, product and marketing manager at heart, I have started, led and earned modest exits from 3 companies - New Zealand's largest online marketing agency, New Zealand's first email branding software-as-a-service company and New Zealand's first angel investment firm.

This has seen me work with companies like Google, Microsoft, Autodesk, NewsCorp, Saatchi & Saatchi, Fonterra, Telecom, Air New Zealand, Contact Energy, The Warehouse, Trademe, First Rate, Sparkbox, Calcium and Ponoko.

And I've gained exposure for my startups in these publications:

USA: The Wall Street Journal, The New York Times, USA Today, The Washington Post, CNN Money, Inc. Magazine (cover), Forbes, BusinessWeek, Wired, PC World, TechCrunch, Engadget, Boing Boing, Mashable, Gizmodo, Read Write Web, CNet, MIT Technology Review.

New Zealand: TV1, TV3, Radio NZ, New Zealand Herald, Dominion Post, New Zealand Listener, Idealog, Computer World.

England: BBC News, Financial Times, The Economist.

On the roller coaster I've learned first hand how to launch new digital solutions with less risk ... Code last : Design second : Market first.
