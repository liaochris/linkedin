Art Noor's Summary

I have recently compiled a book of my paintings done over a period of 5 years ( 2008-2012) , which is due to be released soon. You can find the excerpts and the paintings at my website. www.artnoor.com

This 240 pages book.( "99 Names of Allah - Paintings by A.Noor" ), as shown in the video
is a handy companion, and can be used for constant inspiration and 'dhikr'. You can register online at www.artnoor.com to receive the invitation for the exhibition of the paintings along with the free copy of the Art book-

The art book that features the abstract paintings based on the Arabic calligraphy of Allah's most beautiful names also known as Asma Ul Husna. These names are also the attributes that allow us a small glimpse in Allah's Majesty and Dominion.
Since the names are derived from the verses in"The Holy Quran"- the exact translated meaning of the verses accompanies the images pf paintings.
