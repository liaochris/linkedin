El Jardín del Barrio

December 2010 – December 2011 (1 year 1 month)

El Jardín del Barrio tells the story of Veto, a young Latino adolescent trying to find his place in his community, and his grandfather, Ciro, who tries to take a guiding role in the boys life after the death of Veto's parents.

The two are and have been dealing with the prospect of gang relations in their community, and as the story unravels, we see two different time periods come to a turning point where Veto and Ciro must make decisions that will affect themselves, their families, and their communities.

The garden itself provides, for our characters, an initially unseen solution to their need for adoration and togetherness. It is a story about transformation, humanity, and the power to overcome, through characters that are simply ordinary people in difficult circumstances, allowing audiences the opportunity to see these possibilities for themselves as well.
