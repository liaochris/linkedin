Andrew Quinn's Summary

Andrew Quinn is an Associate Creative Director/Senior Art Director, a seasoned and talented Graphic Designer and keen creative thinker. A graduate of the Portfolio Center Design Program, Quinn has worked over the last 14 years on a variety of different projects for many clients of all sizes. He has particular strengths in both interactive design and packaging/promotional design, as well as an active imagination, excellent communication skills, a razor sharp sense of humor and unique perspective. His goals, both short and long term center around doing focused, excellent design work/art direction for interesting clients in a fair and pleasant environment for a firm that values honestly and hard work.
