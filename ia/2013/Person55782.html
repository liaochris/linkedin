Irene Frielich's Summary

EnVision Performance Solutions is an award-winning provider of instructional design, training implementation, and training project management services. We develop instructional materials for a wide range of topics including operations, compliance and regulations, policies/procedures, systems applications, product knowledge, finance, performance management, and professional development.

EnVision has worked with Fortune 500 companies such as State Street Bank, Covidien, Boston Scientific Corporation, Genzyme Corporation, The Gillette Company, Millennium Pharmaceuticals, NSTAR Electric & Gas, and Staples Corporation. We also provide training services to not-for-profits and NGOs such as Management Sciences for Health, The Delvalle Institute (an agency of the Boston EMS), UMass Donahue Institute, Northeast Behavioral Health (now Lahey Behavioral Health), and EDC.

To receive Building Bridges, our e-letter, email us at solutions@envision-performance.com.

Specialties: Instructional design; curriculum development; project management; mergers; functional consolidations; change management; training delivery
