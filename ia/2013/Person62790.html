Jerry Strifler's Summary

Creative design professional with a varied background in both print and online media. Experienced in providing the full-cycle creative development, design and production of marketing and advertising communications.

I design for the client, deliver strategic creative and help develop content. I am message-driven, work in nearly every medium and care about my profession. An executive level collaborator.

Specialties

Ability to understand strategic business communication objectives, expert knowledge of print design / production processes, website creative development, leading collaborative design teams, art direction & design, project management, vendor research & selection.
