Mold Design Engineer

Cadmold

January 2000 – April 2001 (1 year 4 months)

I designed and detailed custom thermoplastic injection molds. These molds were total custom molds that ranged from quick turn around prototype molds to full production Class A molds. These molds were driven by 3D databases provided by our customer or 3D databases that were created from a 2D part print. The majority of databases that were received were from cad packages other than Pro/E which meant that I had to repair the databases so that they could be used in Pro/E.

Mold Design Engineer

Emerald Tool & Mold

December 1997 – January 2000 (2 years 2 months)

Using AutoCAD R14 and Mechanical Desktop 3.11 I designed and detailed thermoplastic, thermoset, and die cast
injection molds. I have designed molds with multiple parts lines, slides, and lifters. These molds were designed
from 3D and 2D databases that were provided by the customer or were generated myself. While working here I
have had the opportunity to work hands on in the shop and learn machining skills on the mills, grinders, sinker
EDM's and the wire EDM machines. I also learned to program basic wire EDM parts.

Mold Design Engineer

A&M Engineering

October 1996 – November 1997 (1 year 2 months)

Using AutoCAD Rl3 & R14 I designed and detailed thermoplastic and thermoset injection molds. I have designed
molds with multiple parting lines, slides, and lifters. These molds were designed from 3D and 2D databases that
were provided by the customer or were generated myself. I also began learning how to run the network, install and
setup new software/hardware, and help my coworkers with any problems they had with any software/hardware
problems.

Detailer/Designer

Forma Tool & Mold

October 1995 – October 1996 (1 year 1 month)

I designed and detailed plastic injection molds. The molds were mainly connector molds. The software I used here
was AutoCAD R12. My job was to pull the individual parts out of the assembly drawings and detail them. I
updated shop drawings to the changes that were made on the parts.
