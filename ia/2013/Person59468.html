Jason Owen's Summary

Jason Owen attended the University of Cincinnati and is currently the Chief Operations Officer at HomeInsurance.com. HomeInsurance.com (acquired by Red Ventures - Feb 2012) is a comparison shopping engine for Home and Auto Insurance, making shopping for the best insurance rates from multiple insurance companies easy, fast, and efficient. HomeInsurance.com has partnered with the top rated home and auto insurance companies in the country including Travelers, Safeco, Hartford, ASI, Foremost, and Progressive. Jason oversees the daily operations of the business including call center operations, strategic long-range planning, and marketing strategy.

While at Trident Marketing Jason was responsible for managing the development, implementation, and optimization of the strategic online marketing plans for several online properties and developed an expert level knowledge of multichannel analytics, including phone-call tracking and optimization, paid search/SEM and organic search engine marketing (SEO), and tracking online vs. offline conversions simultaneously through advanced analytical solutions.

Jason helped Trident Marketing to build one of the most responsive, analytically driven call centers in the customer acquisition industry and now works with companies such as DIRECTV, ADT, and HughesNet. Direct Sat TV, was named the DIRECTV dealer of the year in 2007 and is the second largest National DIRECTV dealer in the US.

Jason has been working in Online Marketing for over 9 years, working on the agency side with DMinSite prior to joining Trident Marketing. During Jason’s time at DMinSite, an ecommerce agency with over 75 clients focusing on the catalog industry, Jason was able to increase client’s sales quarter-over-quarter in excess of 40%. Jason also collaborated with Google on the launch of Google Checkout and introduced new products to DMinSite including URL Rewriting, Micro-site design and development, and Omniture Site Catalyst.
