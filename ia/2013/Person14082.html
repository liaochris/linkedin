BIBI DOAN's Summary

"My name goes with my works...I only deliver the best !" - BiBiDoan

I have been working in New York for more than 10 years as a fashion designer. I'm a very creative, hard working and ambitious person. I have great management skills. I'm friendly, good with people and I get along well with everyone. I'm detail oriented, organized, self-motivated, and I perform well under pressures. I have great computer skills, exp. w/ photoshop & illustrator. I have great strength in textile design (prints & yarn-dyes), and high taste level of design, with full of great ideas.

I can learn fast and I'm able to make quick adjustments to fit in all difference environments. I'm willing to do what is necessary to get the job done in the best results.

I'm providing all aspects of designs and development from conceptual (trend boards, product boards, color stories, sketches, tech-packs, fitting...etc) to first prototype samples onto production samples. Provide CAD, prints, patterns, and embroideries designs.
