Bill Stainton's Summary

Bill works with, and speaks for, organizations that want their people to play a bigger game and produce "unreasonable" results. What are "unreasonable" results? Results that make customers, colleagues, and the competition stand up and take notice. Results that are beyond average, beyond predictable, beyond what's expected. Results that change the game.

Bill's highly energetic and highly entertaining presentation style (he did, after all, win 29 Emmy Awards for Comedy!), coupled with his real-world experience of producing "unreasonable" results with his own team, make for unforgettable motivational keynotes, business keynotes, and leadership keynotes.

"Bill Stainton is an amazing speaker. Our attendees claimed his keynote was the best presentation they'd ever seen!" ~ Jay Raish - Meeting Planner, BIIA

• Author of "The 5 Best Decisions the Beatles Ever Made...and Why You Should Make Them Too!" - THE book on the Beatles' success: how they achieved it and how other organizations can achieve similar success.

• Winner of 29 Emmy Awards in the field of Entertainment for Writing, Producing, and Performing.

• Former writer for The Tonight Show with Jay Leno.

• Guest lecturer at the University of Michigan, the University of Washington School of Business, and Elizabethtown College.

• Clients include Boeing, Microsoft, Godiva Chocolatier, Singapore Airlines, Holland America, National Association of Healthcare Consultants, RE/MAX, Wells Fargo, and Meeting Planners International.
