Richard Morales' Summary

For over 15 years, I’ve worked with award-winning artists (in the film, TV and music industries), interior designers, organizations, and businesses to build their brand’s awareness, value, loyalty and sales.

I’m a seasoned professional who’s versed in many fields, including public relations, talent management, corporate communications, marketing, brand development, and advertising.
