Gregory R Johnson's Summary

Greg is President of R.N. Johnson Creative Marketing -- a digital agency and design firm that provides internet marketing, web design & development, and integrated marketing solutions. He works with owners, CEOs and marketing managers of privately held companies.

Greg’s background includes internet marketing, advertising, broadcasting, and journalism. He worked as a stringer correspondent for The Associated Press in South America covering the World Swimming Championships in Columbia, off-shore oil exploration in Ecuador, and United Nations Conferences in Peru. He was a consultant to Outside Magazine on their syndicated short-form radio series “Outside The Radio Show.” Additionally, he covered the Pan American Games in Indianapolis on WNDE. His screenwriting and production credentials include over 70 sponsored presentations, films and videos produced for Fortune 1,000 clients and agencies throughout the U.S. He was an active Board Member of the Chicago Film Council, and a member of the Chicago Television Academy.

Greg began his career at Market Facts, Inc., in Chicago, where he worked as a Marketing Analyst. He holds a Bachelor’s degree in Liberal Arts from the University of Denver.

Gregory R Johnson's Experience

President & Creative Director

RN Johnson Creative Marketing

January 1992 – Present (22 years) Woodstock, Illinois

Through R.N. Johnson Creative Marketing, Greg has provided leadership for many marketing initiatives, campaigns, and programs. For internet clients the firm’s focus lies in attracting, engaging and converting interested prospects to satisfied customers through an appropriate marketing strategy. The agency has built its practice on the idea that a website should provide a critical marketing hub built to high standards of execution and online performance. It should never become a mere status symbol for the designer.

Agency History

R.N. Johnson Advertising devoted its formative years to electronics, travel, and business-to-business advertising. The firm offered creative strength, financial stability and a solid reputation in local and national media. Greg assumed control in 1994. By the mid 90’s he had transitioned the business from traditional advertising to integrated marketing and digital media. As web based priorities continued to evolve, the firm developed and refined it’s internet marketing skills and capabilities.

Philosophy

Greg believes in marketing with strength, simplicity and persuasiveness. He stresses the value of an organic, integrated approach to marketing.
