Alexandra Dalavagas' Summary

I am a PhD candidate in Philosophy at The Graduate Center of the City University of New York and currently teaching Philosophy at both Baruch College (CUNY) and the Fashion Institute of Technology (SUNY).

My primary areas of research interest are Business Ethics, Corporate Social Responsibility, and Socially Responsible Investing. I am also interested in Bayesianism, the Monty Hall Problem, Nietzsche, Aesthetics and, naturally, Philosophy of Physics.
