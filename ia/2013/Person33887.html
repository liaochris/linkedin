Emily Morgan's Summary

NEW: Master Delegation summer blog series for Entreps! ow.ly/bZvLn

Emily Morgan is a virtual entrepreneur with a passion for “Making Virtual Personal.” She is the founder of two virtual businesses, Delegate Solutions, a Boutique Virtual Assistance (VA) Firm and Entrepreneur’s Entourage an Internet Marketing & Branding company. Her mission is to develop resources and services to help solopreneurs run their businesses more effectively, always with a flair that is unique to them!

Emily speaks nationally on the topic of virtual business. She’s been featured in the New York Times, NBC News and Martha Stewart Radio, and has recently been recruited to develop a leading Virtual Assistant Training Program for an East-Coast college consortium where she will design and teach courses on virtual entrepreneurship.

Emily provides private consulting and mentoring support to new and aspiring virtual professionals looking to navigate their own business launch and ongoing brand development. She earned her certification as a Reach Personal Brand Strategist and is in the process of combining her passions by completing the Reach Online Identity Certification designation.

She holds a BA from the University of Pennsylvania (Penn) and advocates actively on behalf of the Virtual Professional industry. Emily is an active member of the National Association of Women Business Owners (NAWBO) the International Virtual Assistant Association (IVAA) and the Delaware Valley Virtual Assistant Association (DVVAA). She works out of her virtual office in Philadelphia.
