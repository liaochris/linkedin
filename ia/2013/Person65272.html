Joe Demalderis' Summary

I fish for a living. I started guiding full time in 1995 after doing it part time for a few years. Starting from scratch I built my business by being the best I could be. I did all my own marketing and expanded that into web development and an internet marketing program.

My success was aided by a successful career in financial services marketing employee benefits, retirement plans, life insurance and property & liability coverages.

In 2010 The Orvis Company named me their Orvis Endorsed Fly Fishing Guide of the Year.

My primary fishery is the Upper Delaware River System fly fishing for wild trout. I've been in several fly fishing magazines as an authority on this river and have also been quoted in several books on fly fishing.

I addition to wild trout, I guide smallmouth bass fishing on the Delaware River and saltwater fly and light tackle fishing for striped bass, false albacore and bluefish. I host trips to several travel destinations in the US, Bahamas and South America and also arrange individual bookings.

I've had several articles published on fishing and other topics. Other articles I've written can be found on many websites and blogs.

Conservation and Environmental issues are important to me. I dedicate time, effort and money to these causes and am a board member of the Friends of the Upper Delaware River.

I spent a couple of years as a probation officer in the adult criminal division, interesting couple of years to say the least. It took a little time, but I finally got a "reel" job!

In the future I look to expand my fishing business into other avenues within the industry and devote more time to helping more people have fun on the water.

Specialties

fly fishing, light tackle fishing, sporting destination travel, wing shooting, hunting, shooting, sales, marketing.
