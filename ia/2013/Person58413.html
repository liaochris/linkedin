Janice Phalen's Summary

Working to help you buy, sell & lease commercial real estate. What I do is help you save time and money when you are looking for commercial real estate to rent. lease or purchase. As a prospective tenant, being prepared to work with a potential Landlord is a key to successful lease negotiations. Market knowledge of the commercial real estate market and amenities in the area help narrow the choices. If you are selling, getting the highest price and the right terms are goals. If you are leasing, my emphasis is on helping your business find the “right” space. Each organization and company is different and unique. Developing an understanding your business goals and objectives is the first step in the process of identifying potential spaces to view. It doesn’t matter to me what company, or commercial real estate firm has the listing, it’s about finding the right space for your business. I cooperate with all firms - big & small.


Specialties: Office Buildings, Strip Centers, Retail locations, lease, rent & sales negotiations, analyzing property, analyzing leases. Using social media to publicize listings.
