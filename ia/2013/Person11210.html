Susanne Slay-Westbrook's Summary

I am a Licensed Professional Counselor-Supervisor and a Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist-Supervisor with over 27 years of experience, in both outpatient and inpatient modalities.

In 2001 I wrote a book called, A World of Respect,in which I explore the nature of respect, why it's a basic, fundamental piece of functional relationships and becoming a lost art, but can be nurtured and renewed with our awareness and intention. I invite all of us to participate in this endeavor.

I am currently accepting LPC and LMFT Interns for supervision.

I'm also excited to announce that I am launching a new online Ethics CEU course called Respect Focused Therapy.

My husband, Redge Westbrook,JD, Don Lawson and I are also offering divorce and employment and organizational mediation. We speak regularly on related topics to groups and businesses.

Specialties

I enjoy working with couples, families, children and adolescents, dealing with issues such as depression, grief and loss, divorce, blended families, chronic illness and disabilities.
