Michael McCollum's Summary

Owner of McCollum Associates, a consulting firm that helps landowners permit development projects and creates and markets mitigation and conservation banks.

While in State government I proposed California’s first statewide wetland mitigation banking guidelines and encouraged the establishment of mitigation banks throughout the state. As a consultant I facilitated the adoption of the first state and national policies for the creation of conservation banks, and developed the nation’s first conservation bank under the new policies.

Specialties

Mitigation and conservation bank development. Permitting energy related development projects. Negotiation with government agencies for permit applications. CA licensed real estate broker.
