Jeanne M. Hannah's Summary

Jeanne M. Hannah focuses her practice on the area of family law. She actively consults with clients and lawyers around the country and in other countries on parental kidnapping cases and multi-jurisdictional issues. Ms. Hannah is an active member of the Family Law Section of the State Bar of Michigan, the Family Law Section of the American Bar Association and also the Elder Law and Disability Rights Sections of the State Bar of Michigan.

Specialties

Parental abductions, both interstate and international; multi-jurisdictional disputes (which family court has valid jurisdiction to decide custody issues); family law issues such as child custody, divorce, spousal support, child support, paternity, prenuptial and postnuptial agreements.

Jeanne M. Hannah's Experience

Family Lawyer

Jeanne M. Hannah, Family Lawyer

1987 – Present (26 years)

Jeanne M. Hannah has maintained her own solo practice since 1987, concentrating in the areas of parental kidnapping, divorce, child custody, support, cohabitation, and parentage issues, primarily in northern Michigan, although the Internet brings clients from around the country and abroad. As a consultant on Uniform Child Custody Jurisdiction and Enforcement Act matters, she has assisted lawyers in many states with recovery of children and interstate jurisdictional issues. Ms. Hannah maintains a family law blog "Updates in Family Law" at http://jeannehannah.typepad.com/.
