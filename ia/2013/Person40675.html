Elizabeth Farley's Summary

I love the challenge of taking complex material and simplifying it for users, and I also have great job satisfaction in taking a project from start to finish. I am detailed-oriented, and I have excellent communication and presentation skills.

Currently, I freelance and act as an independent contractor in the area of web design and development. I can take designs from Photoshop or Illustrator and create a fully functioning website (HTML/CSS) with Flash and Javascript features.
