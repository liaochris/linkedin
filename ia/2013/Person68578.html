Public Company; 5001-10,000 employees; LIFE; Biotechnology industry

• Reporting to the EMEA Senior Director & Regional Leader, with one person reporting to me
• At local level, the main objective was to drive the HR and legal entity integration process between the two companies that had just merged to create Life Technologies, namely Applied Biosystems and Invitrogen. • Among the main achievements at local level, Applied Biosystems was one of the 50 Best Workplaces 2009 in Spain

That blog was also used as knowledge database for company policies and HR procedures
• At EMEA level, my initial responsibility was to create and lead a HR Shared Services Center in EMEA. A later decision in 2011 centralized the leading responsiblity in Scotland
• My main achivement in this role was to build a proposal for the EMEA managemente team to design and implement the HR Shared Services Organization for Life Technologies in Europe. That proposal was successfully presented and approved at the end of 2009
• Another major projects I was actively involved in, both at local and EMEA level, were the divestitures of two different business units
