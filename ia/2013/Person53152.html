Otto Hilska's Summary

I'm an experienced software architect and entrepreneur who's passionate about web.

I held various software development positions before co-founding Nodeta in 2004. Since then, I've had the pleasure of working in really interesting and diverse projects. I've been lucky to be able to work with agile methods, rapid Ruby on Rails development, scalable distributed systems, mobile devices, software sales, product management, project management and concepting our own product.

As of 2009 we've improved team communication with our web-based software product, Flowdock. It's going to be huge, and at least all software development teams should be using it already.

Specialties

Agile methods, team communication, scalable distributed systems, mobile content management, security, product management, project management, Ruby on Rails, web in general.

I'm one of the most experienced Ruby on Rails developers in Finland, having worked with it actively since 2005.
