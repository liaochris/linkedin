Michał Gruca's Summary

Software architect, team leader and developer with experience on building agile teams, designing and coding on Java platform and working with e-commerce applications.

I like ideas behind agile movement and how they can reduce gap between client and development team, leading to satisfaction of both sides.
As I was either building or contributing to current software development process in every company I've been working for, I was able to see how different agile techniques works in practice and how team can benefit from implementing them (or suffer if they where miss-understood).
I've seen also other approaches, and that's why I believe in agile methods, or rather adaptive approach.

As I always was working in distributed team in some offshore/on site model I'm experienced in working with any mix of teams and timezones.

After couple years in IT industry I see huge gap between business people and developers and how both parties look on software projects.
My goal is to help both groups in realization of their goals which ultimately leads to project success. Bit oversimplified, but more or less it's my view on Software Craftsmanship.
I'm also trying constantly to raise my skills and understand better both worlds.
