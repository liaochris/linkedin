Kate Simpson's Summary

As a freelance information architect I aim to create the most compelling user experiences, improve lawyer and staff productivity and communications and, with my specialist focus on findability, to help lawyers find, share and re-use the client-valuable knowledge within their tangled landscapes and interfaces

Specialties

Information architecture, content management & usability in the legal and publishing industries
