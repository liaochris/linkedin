Seb Insua's Summary

I'm a systems thinker and I believe in momentum.

As a technologist with a deep interest in business I want to help others realise their projects and business ideas, whether that means helping architect, develop and manage the creation of a product, doing customer development or forming a business model. Everything I do is about finding and executing the best go-to-market strategy while bridging the gap in between the technical and business sides of a company.

I can help you to build out a product on both the back-end and front-end as well as with leading technical teams.

I can help finding customers and speaking with them to find out their thoughts and feelings on a new product.

I know the difference between CAC, ARPU and LTV; and can help you in the creation of a working business model with the technical cost factored in.

I believe good fundamentals and principles provide the greatest value to others. I learn as a hobby and not simply the wave of new technologies. I like to broaden my mind with books and articles on disciplines that interest me. On the tech-side I favour lean development, the twelve-factor app (http://www.12factor.net/), and the simplicity espoused in Rich Hickey's "Simple Made Easy" (www.infoq.com/presentations/Simple-Made-Easy) as the best ways of ensuring high-quality code.

For those wondering what technologies I've worked with:

Python, PHP, JavaScript (underscore.js, async.js), CoffeeScript, Node.JS, Backbone.JS, shell scripting, TDD & BDD (PHPUnit, unittest, mocha.js), HTML, CSS, Zend Framework, Flask, Django, Apache, Nginx, MySQL, PostgreSQL, Cassandra, Puppet, Vagrant, MongoDB, etc.

The list goes on and on and I'm always happy to learn more. I'm currently working with Objective-C.
