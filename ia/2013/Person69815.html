JR Hinds' Summary

JR Hinds is a third generation insurance broker with 17 years of experience in the industry.
He has helped companies from 2-165 employees design and maintain benefits plans to help them attract and retain quality employees. JR knows the needs of small businesses. He stands apart in his educational and consultative approach to designing a program that is unique to the needs of your business and your employees. Health insurance is very complicated, but JR will simplify it and make sure that you and your employees understand your plan in order to maximize its value.

Specialties: Employee Benefits, Group Health Insurance, Start-up Businesses
