Alex Romo's Summary

Focus on gameplay scripting, primarily Singleplayer/Cooperative with basic level geometry blockout. Have recently started getting into Multiplayer design scripting in my free time and intend to keep working at it.

I come from a strong game modding background originating when I was first introduced to Call of Duty® (2003), and every PC title thereafter in the franchise with subsequent public SDK releases. initially took an interest to Singleplayer scripting, but also learned the other pipelines ranging from Art (Modelling/ Animation/ Exporting/ Importing), Audio (Compressing/ Importing), UI/ Menu Scripting, and FX.

A self-starter and able to work on my own. Able to quickly find the root of a problem on my own, or only after thorough investigation seek the assistance of others.

Comfortable making local engine code changes to better assess problems and report to engineers.

I'm always working on some kind of project at home alongside a geo builder -- I enjoy creating new content, learning as much as I can, working with others, and getting feedback. I stick my modding roots and enjoy assisting others with advice/ troubleshooting/ tutorials for their mods.

My goals are to continue designing missions while branching out to Multiplayer game modes. I would also like to expand my level building skills.
