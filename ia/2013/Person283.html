Bill Quimby's Summary

Bill Quimby is widely recognized as the nations leading vanity number consultant, having personally helped over 50,000 different companies find vanity 800 numbers since he founded TollFreeNumbers.com in 1995. He sets the standard for customer service and out performs even the biggest companies in the telecom industry, with the most totally unique business in the industry.

There's literally nobody else that helps find good 1-800 numbers, provides independent proof of ownership and gets you started but isn't trying to sell you the ongoing phone service. Bill Quimby’s site, TollFreeNumbers.com is the only one that provides independent proof of ownership and empowers their customers with ownership of their number and the ability to go to any service or website they want.

There are large phone companies that provide toll free service but don't provide much real help finding a good number. There are small vanity number brokers that grab up good numbers and want to sell or worse yet, rent the numbers that they are squatting on. And there are slick looking websites that will look up and find numbers for you but you won’t realize they’re only doing that to lock you into their service, and their fine print often says that THEY own any # you find through them.

TollFreeNumbers.com is literally the only company in the toll free industry that isn’t trying to sell you their toll free service and keep you there as long as possible. That allows them to be much more honest than everyone else trying to sell you something. Mr. Quimby is a unique advocate for the customer and provides a very unique tool that every marketer has to try for themselves.
