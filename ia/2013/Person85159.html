Marco Bellinaso's Summary

I'm a software architect and developer with 14 years of experience designing and implementing Microsoft-centric and mobile software solutions for large companies such as Microsoft, YOOX, UniSys, ValueTeam/NTT, FIAT, Crif, Tiscali, Engineering and others.

Co-founder and CTO of Mopapp — a funded startup with big customers — I also authored a number of commercial developer tools and programming books, among which is Wrox's best-selling "ASP.NET 2.0 Website Programming".

Currently having a lot of fun with Studietto Apps (studietto.com), and its first app "Cents", for which I coordinated the project and did some partial code implementation, marketing, testing etc. Cents has been featured by Apple in the USA App Store and reached the Top 25 in the Utilities category.

I worked on many personal projects and enjoy writing code and experimenting with new technologies to find solutions to problems; but I'm also interested in user interaction and product design! I love the web, mobile platforms, ecommerce, smart solutions and innovation, and especially a mix of all that.

I'm always looking for new projects that can impact the way people use technology to improve their lives, where to apply the current experience and grow it further.
