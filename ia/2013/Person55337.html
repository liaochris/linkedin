Inge Kuijper's Summary

UX strategy and design for mobile apps, web, and all other things digital. Passionate about new technologies and designing the digital services that improve work, health, travel, the world.

Able to think on a creative & conceptual level as well as working out details. With 6+ years experience in interaction design, I enjoy designing for iPhone, Android & Blackberry as much as web. I love a structured approach, such as scope sessions, card sorting sessions and SCRUM, to get to the best results.

I'm always thinking of the users first when balancing user, business and technology needs. And enjoy working with a variety of people, skills, and opinions to create the best end result with a positive and pro-active approach.

Specialties: User research, concept development, wireframing, prototyping, usability testing, expert reviews
