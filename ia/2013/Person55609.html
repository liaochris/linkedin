Eckard Ritter's Summary

Internet Partnership is a German, Berlin-based internet marketing firm that helps launch travel-related businesses, vacation providers and tourism organizations to the forefront of the Web and thus be more easily found by potential customers.

Our portfolio of services includes SEO (search engine optimization) packages, online advertising, press releases and a proprietary network of "Vacation in Germany" websites, blogs, microblogs and social media such as Facebook and XING. Further advertising power comes from Internet Partnership's teletext pages within the nationwide teletext service offered by RTL.

Our philosophy is quality content and a sound SEO strategy to help our clients be found through the naturally occurring organic search mechanisms, spend less resources on SEM (search engine marketing) and to sustainably ensure higher visibility and brand awareness. Our content is not only based on advertising and vacation packages, but also on regional information and relevant historic and cultural background information as well as events, tourism news and travel tips. Fascinating summaries of German folklore and legends add further value to our tourism content.

Dr. Eckard Ritter, founder and CEO of internet partnership and champion of German travel & tourism promotion through the internet, has more than 17 years experience as an entrepreneur, technologist and inventor. In 2007, Dr. Ritter tapped into the opportunities that lay within the emerging Mobile Web and began to develop Mobihexer®, aka xsMobi, a free mobile site builder designed to create search engine-friendly websites that are both dotMobi compliant and formatted for desktop viewing.

Specialties: urlaub in deutschland, web development, mobile website development, coldfusion, tourism marketing, social media, smo, mobile website creator, mobile web, websites for mobiles
