John Luginbill's Summary

I believe that an agency should exist to help clients increase sales, profit and reputation. I founded THE HEAVYWEIGHTS in 1991 with this belief and the commitment that advertising agencies could serve clients in a more effective way.

We turn wellness and advocacy from a cost-center to a profit-center.

For a healthcare provider wellness and advocacy programs are ways to manage health care costs of a population.

For any company, advocacy is the way to tap into consumer interests and passions - a way she can see herself in your marketing story.

But no brand can afford to be altruistic without producing return on investment.

Our wellness and advocacy programs are highly profitable by getting more people to know about your brand, engage with your brand, consider using your brand, then advocate for your brand.

Clients We Serve:
- Health systems and physician practices
- Large employers that need to increase productivity and lower insurance costs
- Foundations advocating positions
- Pharma companies that want to create brand and sales lift through patient participation
- Bio-tech companies that develop clinical breakthroughs and operational efficiencies
- Any brand that wants to increase awareness, engagement and conversion
