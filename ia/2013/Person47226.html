Gary Stinnett's Summary

Accomplished marketer with a track record of delivering consistent, profitable sales growth for large and small restaurant and retail chains. Entrepreneurial leader, skilled at developing long term plans while driving short term sales. Resourceful problem solver, talented in strategic thinking, marketing and idea development.

• Grew Mitchell’s Fish Market comparable restaurant sales by +13% within 7 months while simultaneously building the marketing department, changing the media buying agency and building relationships with the creative advertising agency.

• Led and empowered existing marketing and Advertising Agency team at Sears Optical to grow sales by 66% over 6 years as VP Brand Management at Cole Vision.

• Developed and implemented the marketing plans that grew system wide sales by $1.2 billion, a 75% increase, over 3 years as Director/Senior Director of National Programs at Taco Bell. Personally originated the idea of 'layers of value' that was the cornerstone of the highly successful 59¢/79¢/99¢ pricing structure.
