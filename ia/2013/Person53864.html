Erin Harris' Summary

Erin Harris is the founder of HRTech Solutions and brings an unparalleled enthusiasm and energy to every project she is a part of. A creative and strategic thinker, Erin has the ability to understand the unique needs of every client and creates practical Human Resources programs which marry up to business strategy to create exceptional results.

Well regarded in the business community for her passion and engaging style, Erin is an exceptional facilitator and communicator across all levels of an organization. Nearly 10 years practical experience in HR combined with a Bachelor of Commerce degree provide Erin the know-how to find exceptional talent for organizations, create and deliver relevant and memorable training sessions and develop employee on-boarding programs which have an immediate impact on your business.

Outside the boardroom, Erin is a Mom to two youngsters full of character, wonder and energy! She is a keen golfer, sports enthusiast and voracious reader. In the community Erin is an active member of VIATEC and the BC Human Resources Management Association.
