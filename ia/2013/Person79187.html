Laurette Willis' Summary

As Founder & Director of PraiseMoves Fitness Ministry, Laurette produces fitness DVDs, 2 TV shows (reaching over 250 million globally), and a variety of online teachings. She authors books & DVDs for Harvest House Publishers and Destiny Image Publishers, and oversees the training of Certified PraiseMoves Instructors on 6 continents. http://PraiseMoves.com

"Like" on Facebook:
http://www.facebook.com/PraiseMovesFan

Offers Christian Meditation Resources: http://ChristianMeditationKit.com
and Detox/cleansing Resources: http://5StepsGuide.com - free health and wellness guide

As a Senior VP Ind. Mission Mktr. with Beyond Organic, Laurette has partnered with NY Times bestselling author & nutritionist Jordan Rubin. She helps people around the US who want to improve their health and nutrition with food so pure it's BEYOND Organic...and delivered to your door!

As the Director of PowerMoves Kids (located at: http://PowerMovesKids.com), Laurette has created the first Character Education & Fitness curriculum for public school classrooms. PowerMoves Kids is used by PK-8th grades in schools throughout the US & Canada.

Laurette is a Guest Host on the RoadShow for Oasis Radio Network (http://OasisNetwork.org), where her show "Fitness for His Witness" is heard daily.

Laurette writes articles on health & fitness for several online and hard copy magazines and publications on a monthly basis.

Enjoying all she gets to do, Laurette says, "I get paid to eat ice cream!!!" That's what it feels like when you do what you love!

Specialties: Focused, inspirational public speaking and ministry. TV and Radio hosting and interviews (both as interviewer and interviewee). Health and fitness writing and speaking. Hebrew word pictures (root words) and Greek.
