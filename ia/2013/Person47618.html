Gena Yuvette Davis, MBA, CCP, BCC's Summary

Gena Yuvette Davis, MBA,CCP is a Board Certified Executive Coach. She is also professional development trainer, author, consultant and personal brand strategist.

At True Synergy, Gena provides executive and leadership coaching to professional women in the entertainment, consumer products and technology industries as well as provides professional development training and brand marketing strategies for fortune 500 companies. As a personal branding strategist, Gena uses social media tools and techniques (among other strategies) to help executives and other high potentials "find their voice" and leverage their personal brand. Gena speaks all over the country as a Certified Contract Seminar Leader. Gena has expertise in the areas of strategy, marketing, communication, organizational development and leadership.

Gena has been inspiring and empowering others to be their best selves for the past 12 years. After 20 years of working in marketing communications and brand management for some of the biggest entertainment brands in the world including: The Walt Disney Company, Sony Pictures Entertainment, Warner Bros., Fox Entertainment Group, and MGA Entertainment; and after owning her own successful marketing communications agency where her clients included such powerhouses as Coca-Cola, Nabisco, Colgate-Palmolive, Los Angeles World Airports, Nestle, HBO, and Habitat for Humanity, Gena decided to take all her knowledge and experience and formed True Synergy, Inc., an executive coaching, training & professional development practice in 2010.

Gena facilitates a quarterly group coaching tele-class called Executive Women in Training. This five-week program helps women create a roadmap into positions of leadership and influence. Each one-hour per week session provides critical skill development in such areas as: personal branding, creative leadership, communications, emotional intelligence, persuasion and strategic thinking for career success.
