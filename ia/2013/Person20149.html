Carlos A. Santayana's Summary

First Cash Financial Services is one of the top performing publicly traded companies anywhere. One of the dominant companies in its sector, First Cash is poised for continued global expansion while maintaining extraordinary returns for investors. Using insights gleaned from deep experience, its leadership team fosters an entrepreneurial spirit while managing disciplined growth.

Carlos Santayana is the Director of Learning and Development at First Cash Financial Services (FCFS). He is responsible for the creation and execution of the enterprise wide learning strategy including: all new hire training and on boarding, management and operations training, executive development, leadership training, performance management and all learning and development programs and processes.

Carlos has over 20 years of experience improving organizations' employee performance, retention and profitability through leadership of Learning and Development programs. Prior to FCFS, Carlos held several key roles at Citigroup in the US and abroad including Performance Consultant, Director of Training and Vice President of Learning and Development during a 15 year period. Previously he held positions at AIG and ITT over a 10 year period where he obtained significant achievements in Sales Management and Operations Management. Carlos obtained his Masters Degree in Business Administration at the University of South Florida with a concentration in Finance. He enjoys reading, public speaking and travel with his family.
