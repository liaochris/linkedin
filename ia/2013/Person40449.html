eliana martella's Summary

I specialise in formative (to innovate and improve) and evaluative (to assess, prove outcomes and benchmark) research aimed at strategy and action plans development to:
- innovate and improve products/services;
- inform marketing, communications, business modelling;
- influence policy.

Whilst my original focus was on in-depth qualitative social research methods, I have professional experience of mixed research designs (including quantitative methods) and design of controlled experiments.

My work leverages over a decade of experience in all aspects of small and large scale research projects (i.e. from scoping and planning to execution, delivery and dissemination), acute use of evidence and business knowledge and experience.

My practice spans across the private and voluntary sectors, government departments and research institutions in the UK, continental Europe and U.S.A.
Areas of impact included Healthcare/Social Care, Social Marketing, FMCG, Media/Technology, Community and Social Inclusion, Social Housing Support Services and Policy development.

My past professional tenures saw me building and maintaining partnerships and engaging with various types of stakeholders, agencies and third-party services.

Examples of my activities included:
uncovering and capitalising on opportunities, eliciting and surfacing salient information to substantiate decision-making, yielding new insights, modelling experiences and ecosystems, designing and piloting strategic programmes, defining metrics to measure outcomes, defining business cases and value propositions, defining teams or individual work plans, hiring and/or mentoring junior team members.

I hold a Ph.D. in Socio-Cultural Anthropology, a Laurea (first with honours) in Sociology, an ABD for a M.Sc. in Human Factors & Ergonomics and am pondering a further plunge into Public and Social Policy.
