Lauren Reskin's Summary

Miami native deeply involved in the evolution and progression of the South Florida independent music scene. I've worked in music retail since 1999, opening my own store Sweat Records in 2005.

Besides running Sweat I serve on the Recording Academy Florida Chapter Board, book local and national acts, put on a yearly free music festival called Sweatstock, DJ Wednesday nights at the Delano, make custom playlists for businesses with Imagesound Americas, and am a proud member of Miami Fellows Class VII.

If you need something done right in Miami, I can usually help or at least find you someone who can. Contact me directly at lolo@sweatrecordsmiami.com
