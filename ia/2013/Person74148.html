Kendall George's Summary

Kendall George is a systems engineer with over 15 years of IT experience. He has worked for or consulted in military, commercial, and higher education environments, both in the US and abroad. He has particular experience in supporting rapid deployment and branch environments. Though his experience includes both voice and data networks, his area of particular interest is systems design, installation, and support to including a variety of Unix and Microsoft Windows operating systems and system and application virtualization to include Citrix, VMware, and Microsoft virtualization. Kendall developed his affection for the higher ed environment working for Texas A&M University's international branch campus in the Persian Gulf State of Qatar. He now works for the University of Oklahoma. The University of Oklahoma is providing IT infrastructure across all campuses under the University of Oklahoma Regents through a shared services cloud. Kendall has been a part of this initiative from its inception. He is currently the Operations Manager for the University's Shared Services infrastructure cloud.

Specialties: Systems engineering, operations management, virtualization, converged networks, enterprise storage, Unix & Windows systems administration.
