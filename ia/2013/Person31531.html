David Booth's Summary

I created Eloqui with co-founder Deborah Shames. It is a unique and forward-thinking communication and presentation training firm. Our formula is a blend of art and cognitive science. As a former actor, I enjoy connecting with audiences, so delivering keynote speeches and conducting group training is an expression and refinement of my life's work.

I always sought a different path, whether it was being a stuntman at Old Tucson Movie Studio, directing professional theater, or representing firms like Cisco and Siemens for 10 years around the world as a spokesman. It's important my work creates something that serves others in a profound way.

All Eloqui techniques come from our collective experience as director and corporate spokesman, so the tools are field-tested. With over 13 years in the business, the techniques are proven, and the results are quantifiable.

Our book Own the Room, published by McGraw-Hill, illustrates the Eloqui Method in a concise and colorful way. I'm proud that it has become a business best-seller.

Deborah and I want to leave a positive legacy, and change forever the way business presentations are delivered, and taught.

Specialties: Coaching and rehearsing pitch teams before make-or-break presentations. Developing leaders and team cooperation through better communication. Increasing sales force hit rate.

Training construction and delivery of PowerPoint from the professional's playbook.

Fine-tuning debate and media skills for political candidates.

Partnering with attorneys and defendants before trial. Or, coaching expert witnesses to be compelling on the stand.

Training authors prior to going on the speaker circuit to promote their books.

Fine tuning presentations to Boards of Directors.
