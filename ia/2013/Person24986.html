Cindy Neky's Summary

Cindy is an experienced marketing & public relations (PR)consultant, Certified Social Media Strategist, coach, & speaker. Her goal is to assist your business in increasing sales painlessly & within your budget. As a marketer with a broad scope of capabilities, her specific proficiencies & skills are in strategic marketing planning, social media strategy, social media management, professional speaking, branding, direct marketing, advertising, market analysis, market research, marketing public relations, copywriting, marketing consulting & database marketing.

She is President of Marketing Pathways, a marketing consulting firm providing a wide range of results oriented professional marketing & public relations (PR) services to small businesses. Marketing Pathways serves both business-to-business (B2B) & business-to-consumer (B2C) marketing clients.

With more than 25 years experience in her field, Cindy excels in assisting clients achieve their strategic communications goals in industries, which include banking, insurance, manufacturing, telecommunications, personnel services, retail, professional services,construction, & non-profits. Some of her current and previous clients are Provil & Associates, DNP IMS America, Visiting Angels, Trailer Marketing, Stevens, One Hour Martinizing Corporate, CLG, Staff Masters, & LG Realty Advisors.

Cindy holds a B.S. in Marketing from Miami University in Ohio & has attended Xavier University’s masters program. She has received the Social Media Strategist Certification from SMM University.

She is a sought after speaker with experience speaking throughout the country at numerous Chambers of Commerce & business functions on topics relating to marketing, public relations, social media & sales.
