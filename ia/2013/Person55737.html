Ira Blonder's Summary

Ira Blonder, Managing General Partner of the Sound Kitchen Studios, the Southeast's most prestigious recording and production facility. Artists, spanning all music genres, have recorded at the Sound Kitchen: Elton John, Rascal Flatts, Love and Theft, Vince Gill, Bebe Winans, Bruce Springsteen, Safety Suit, Saliva, Alice in Chains, Dolly Parton, Hank Williams Jr., Michael W. Smith, Trace Adkins, Jewel, as well as thousands of indie artists. We offer competitive rates for demo and album projects, EPKs, private and corporate events, video, television & film shoots!

Ira Blonder, Managing Partner at The Blonder Group, has been overseeing operations for over twenty-five years. Our most important responsibility has been to create value for our clients. We are proud that our clients have responded with many years of repeat business and personal referrals. This is why, year after year, we are recognized as one of Nashville's best commercial real estate companies.

The Blonder Group specializes in providing a broad range of commercial real estate services; Property Acquisition & Disposition, Investment Property Valuation and Sale, Site Selection, Feasibility & Due Diligence Reporting, Land Development & Exit Strategies, as well as Landlord and Tenant Representation

Ira Blonder's Experience

Managing Partner

The Sound Kitchen

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Entertainment industry

2007 – Present (6 years)

The Platinum Award-winning Sound Kitchen Studio is the Southeast's most prestigious recording and production facility. We are the only studio in Middle TN, where you can track, mix and master your project on site. Within our 19,000 square ft. campus are seven studios; internationally recognized for great sound. Many legendary artists spanning all music genres, have recorded at the Sound Kitchen. Our dynamic location is perfect for creating song demos, artist showcases, television tapings, video productions and educational programs. We offer competitive budgets for all projects; whether you are signed to a label or just beginning your journey as an artist.

Our venue is ideal for private & corporate parties in a VIP setting. Our facilities include two full service kitchens, and private lounges with broadband access. And, we can cater your event using our private chef services, or the caterer of your choice.

The Sound Kitchen Studio's are located in Franklin, TN, in the heart of Cool Springs, 20 minutes from Music Row and Nashville's International Airport. We are surrounded by first class hotels, fitness centers, shopping, and theaters.
