Matt McGlothlin's Summary

My first real job was marketing coordinator for a local web firm during the dot-com boom. I quickly developed an affection for web development and absorbed as many online classes, tutorials and books as possible. In 2007, I began to devote all energy towards building my web firm, bitflip. Over the years I've built a solid reputation for solid web development, online marketing expertise and design. Our current clients include FUEL pizza, Neighboring Concepts, The Restaurant Group and Ten Park Lanes. My goal is to continue to grow the business and produce web sites for top local companies.

In early 2011 I officially launched AdSpark promos, a promotional products distributor, with a focus on eco-friendly products from local suppliers that benefit local non-profits.

Specialties

Web Development, Web Applications, Social Media, Email Marketing, Content Management, Search Engine Optimization, Graphic Design, Promotional Products
