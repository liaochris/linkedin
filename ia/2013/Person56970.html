Jaime Chiang's Summary

Jaime is Managing Director of West Coast operations at Red Antler.

Red Antler is a branding, design and digital consultancy. We approach design as a way to solve strategic challenges.

We have built our business working with new ventures, which means we do our best work when we’re disrupting categories and creating something new. It also means our process is designed to be highly collaborative.

We are strategically led, focused on expressing a core idea from start to finish, and believe that the distinction between online and offline is increasingly irrelevant. Every brand needs to express itself digitally, and ideally every brand finds a way to connect with people in the real world.

We are passionate about our work and consider ourselves true partners with our clients, often entering into equity deals as part of the relationship.
