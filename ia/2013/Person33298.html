Deanne Marie's Summary

Author, attorney, award-winning speaker and in-demand inspirational leader, Deanne Marie created Smart Solutions for Busy People™ to support women who feel out-of-control as they juggle work, home, family and friends. Her books, e-books and online resources provide both inspiration and take-action details for decorating, gift giving, organizing and entertaining to make everyday life and the holidays more meaningful, more joyful and more beautiful.

By Dsign Omnimedia is an integrated content company dedicated to helping busy women make their plans and dreams a reality, creating a life by design rather than by default.
