Alicia Neubauer's Summary

Currently, I've left the writing and social media world to become a recruiter at Creative Circle - we're a specialized staffing agency that specifically represents creatives within marketing, advertising, interactive and design on a freelance, freelance-to-fulltime, and fulltime basis. While I loved taking on new projects within copywriting and social media in the past, I'm enjoying the chance to learn so much more about the creative field and meet new talented people daily.

I earned my degree in Print Journalism from an accredited university through plenty of hard work, but that's not all I got out of college. Thanks to my education as well as my multiple job positions, internships, and freelance projects, I've become very comfortable with writing about nearly any topic I encounter. And because of my inquisitive nature, I never want to stop learning about that which I've never encountered.

I'm wired as an unconventional and creative thinker, which easily comes across when I write. I've been told that when others can't think of a way to say something in writing, I'm the first person to offer up an idea or concept no one else realized was possible.

I find new technology fascinating, and am always eager to learn about a new app, especially when it comes to social media. Whether it's mastering the call to action in a Facebook post, coming up with an intriguing Instagram photo, or learning how to leverage Pinterest to boost sales, I've learned and continue to learn every emerging nuance and facet of social media.

While I love nurturing my investigative side and using my highly-developed writing skills, I'd love to continue exploring marketing and advertising, especially when it comes to social media and copywriting. There's no reason for me to ever stop learning for myself and from others.
