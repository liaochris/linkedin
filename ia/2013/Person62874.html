Jesse Ellenbogen's Summary

Jesse is a user experience researcher with deep background in consumer user experience that spans both software and hardware. He has a passion for creating experiences that are both meaningful and enriching. Over the past 20 years, he has contributed to over 100 web, mobile and consumer electronics products, including Apple's PowerBook TrackPad & Newton PDA, GoPro's Hero cameras, Palm's webOS & Treo, Danger's Sidekick, Sony’s first American HDTV, and the E*TRADE website.

Jesse is currently Principal UX Researcher at GoPro. Formerly, he was VP UX at Root-1, Dir. of UX Research for webOS, Sr. Mgr. Interaction Design at E*TRADE, Sr. Interaction Designer at Sony Design Center & Lead Interaction Design Engineer at Apple. In total, he has held positions at & consulted to dozens of companies, from small startups to large multinationals.

Jesse earned significant endorsements from industry notables such as Reid Hoffman (co-founder of LinkedIn, Partner at Greylock), and Matias Duarte (Senior Director of Android UX).

Jesse has a BS in Product Design Engineering from Stanford, has earned 3 patents, and his work has resulted in industry awards including two Emmy Awards for technology, two CES Overall Best of Show Awards, others including Wired, CNET, iF and CEDIA.
