Chris Benton's Summary

My purpose is to help people follow their vision by inspiring them to move to the next level in every area of life. I want to be able to show them from my experience that anything is possible if you just have the faith to move forward.

We are Chris & Sandy Benton. We have been around the network marketing world for many years now. We love the industry because it is the best way for the average person to have a shot at achieving their dreams. Yes there are many ways to achieve your dreams but none compare when you look at the lifestyle that can be accomplished through a solid network marketing business.

We run several things:

We are in the process of launching The REI Directory.com. This is going to be a site where real estate investors will be able to be listed for their metro area so people can find them. This is a way to connect people with deals to people who are interested in those deals. We are ONLY allowing 5 investors per metro area to be listed in the directory in order to keep it more valuable for each person.

The network marketing business that we chose is called Vemma. We believe the Vemma business and its foundation and leadership has our core values and that is very important to us. We hav e never seen a company put as much research into their products as Vemma does so we are very proud to be a part of the business.

We also run MLMProDirectory.com. If you are in our great industry then you need to be listed. We only allow 3 USA & 2 Canada people per company to be listed in our directory. The goal is to connect people looking for a home business with people who are actually committed to theirs!

We also create websites and do online marketing for small business. We run MetroCharlotteGuide.com & MetroSavannahGuide.com

Our newest project is TheREIDirectory.com. We connect people with real estate deals with investors who are open to buying. We only allow 6 investors to be listed per metro area in order to keep it valuable for each investor.
