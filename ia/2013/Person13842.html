Betty Henry's Additional Information

Interests:

As a corporate communications strategist, consultant and executive, I enjoy the impact I can have on others: Communicating with external audiences is an opportunity to enhance the organization’s reputation and build credibility; communicating with employees is an opportunity to make people feel good about where they work. If people had taglines as brands do, mine would be “Betty Henry – Making the Complicated Simple.” I’ve enjoyed the opportunity to build internal communications infrastructures – conducting situation analysis, developing the communications strategy, determining key audiences, building the message platform for those audiences, developing the vehicles to deliver timely and consistent messages, and researching the effectiveness of the entire program. Finding new and creative ways to achieve communications goals has been the cornerstone of my career and the source of great appreciation of and satisfaction in the work I do.
