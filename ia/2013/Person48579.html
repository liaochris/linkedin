Gilbert Buchanan, MA's Summary

Possess more than 20 years of diversified experience in organizational development, labor compliance, contract compliance, business/community outreach, and Mentorship program development within public works and private sector environments. I have also managed numerous small business programs and taught more than 1,000 hours of contract-readiness and entrepreneurial training classes to DBEs and SBEs for public works agencies as well as community-based organizations (CBOs). Some of the high-profile organizations for which these services were rendered are: LAUSD, LACCD, Metro, EXPO (Phase 1 & 2), and VEDC with operating budgets ranging from $1 million to $21 billion.
