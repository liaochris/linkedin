Jim Quiggle's Summary

Over 26 years of broad experience in information technology and business operations management with extensive knowledge of large, distributed-computing, mission-critical environments.

My career began with classical hardware engineering developing smartcard reader/writer technology and continued through information technology engineering to current work around IT architecture to include IT project and program management.

My primary strength is the ability to quickly assimilate, translate and apply business needs to proven and emerging technologies for creation of practical solutions that are delivered on time, on budget, with tangible results.

I have been a subject matter expert, technical contributor or project manager for dozens of diverse teams. As such, I am comfortable in both technical and business roles; building, managing and/or collaborating with high performance teams.
