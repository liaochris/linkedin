Jordan Warren's Summary

Jordan is proven leader, manager, strategist and entrepreneur, starting and building the online marketing practice at CKS in 1994, founding integrated marketing agency Eleven in 1999, leading the turnaround of Agency.com San Francisco in 2007 and re-launching the agency as Signal to Noise in 2010. His latest venture is the new full-service advertising agency ARGONAUT.

Jordan has worked with major clients across multiple industries including Apple, Barclays, Disney, eBay, GM, IBM, Kodak, Levi's, MCI, Microsoft, Nike, San Francisco Tourism, Sony, Visa, Willams-Sonoma and Xerox.

Specialties: • Digital marketing pioneer and integrated marketing practitioner since 1994.
• Close to 20 years running San Francisco-based digital and integrated marketing agencies.
• Experience growing agencies profitably, including independents, regional offices, start-ups, turnarounds and spinoffs.
• Won and led major global accounts such as Apple, Nike, Levi’s and Kodak.
• Passion for developing creative ideas that deliver business results.
• Achieved the highest levels of client and employee satisfaction.
