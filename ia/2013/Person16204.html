Brad Wicks' Summary

Brad is owner of Denver Video Company (www.denvervideocompany.com),Charmed Life Photography (www.charmedlifephoto.com) and EpisodicMedia, LLC. Involved in the visual arts for over twenty years, Brad is an active member of the Colorado film and television community.
Brad has been actively involved as the Education Director and most recently, as an instructor at the Colorado Media School, formerly the Ohio Center for Broadcasting. Brad's experience in the Television industry as a Photographer, Director of Photography, Director, Producer, Executive Producer and Production Company owner makes him a perfect mentor teaching the future members of our industry.

Specialties: Still and Motion Photography, Editing, Website design, Education, Marketing and Branding and Talent Resourcing
