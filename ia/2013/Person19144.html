Byron Conn's Summary

The goal of Byron Conn Design is to blend traditional woodworking, studio furniture practices, and new technologies into a handmade, “to the trade” furniture collection. Ultimately this collection will showcase the relationship between hand made and CNC technology. The Dahlia Table Lamp is a perfect example of what I am trying to achieve with this relationship. Rough shaping and complex molds are cut on a CNC Router, followed by extensive hand shaping and fitting. The lamp is controlled by a capacitive touch switch (the same technology that is in a touch screen) hidden beneath a highly complex veneer accent. I am seeking to create unique furniture that displays the highest level of craftsmanship and can be produced in a small production setting by hand.
