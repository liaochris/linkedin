Lisa Alexander's Summary

Known as The Marketing Stylist™, Lisa shares her wisdom and passion for marketing and leadership to varied audiences. She has worked as an art director, graphic designer and marketing consultant throughout her 20 year career.

She is an author, entrepreneur and has been a guest writer for several magazines and blogs as well as experienced trainer.

Lisa’s presentations will teach you how to put your best face forward for all your big red carpet moments. Attending one of her presentations is an opportunity to discuss emerging trends, review the basics and decide what works best for you because marketing is not one size fits all.

Hire Lisa to inform and motivate your organization’s leaders (especially Gen Xers), reveal the best place to look for your next new hire (you’ll be amazed) and of course speak into the lives of women through encouragement and empowerment. Lisa’s passion is obvious and infectious and benefits your greatest resource — the human resource — through her wit and wisdom. Lisa shows people how to be their best and work from the core of who they are thus reaching personal and professional goals.

Business and religious leaders, men and women will benefit from time spent in The Marketing Stylists’ chair.

Lisa along with business partner Michica Guillory created N2K Magazine. N2K or need to know magazine targeted parents of tweens and teenagers. The magazine interpreted slang, deciphered music lyrics, celebrated exemplary teens and featured tales from parents recalling their own adolescent indiscretions.

The magazine was produced in a tabloid-format and was distributed in Houston area Kroger stores, public libraries, and mailed to magazine subscribers.

The magazine received local recognition from KPRC Channel 2 and Fox Channel 26 and was also featured in the Houston Business Journal.
