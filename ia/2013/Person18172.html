Andrea Freygang's Summary

Mrs. Freygang is an experienced public relations, media and marketing professional born and raised in Broward County. She has handled a variety of clients from non-profits such as Partners In Education to infrastructure and economic development projects. Mrs. Freygang recognizes that communication is critical during a project, particularly when there are sensitive issues and is able to appropriately respond and manage communications successfully. She has skillfully executed a number of communications campaigns and targeted outreach for projects in Miami Beach, Surfside, Bal Harbour, North Miami, Hialeah and for agencies such as the Florida Department of Transportation, Pompano Beach Chamber, Lighthouse Point Chamber and other small business clients as well as international companies such as Modani Furniture and Global Assistive Devices. In addition, Mrs. Freygang has solid experience in planning community outreach programs and executing a variety of economic development, marketing and other public relation initiatives. She firmly believes in using grassroots methods to communicate project information but also in using modern technology to expand the reach of a project’s communication goals. As a native, Ms. Freygang is extremely familiar with Broward County, its elected officials as well as local media, its many non-profits and other organizations and she works to build partnerships between all agencies to further enhance Broward County.
