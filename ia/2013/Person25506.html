Nicolaas Claus NICLA van Mierlo's Experience

Real Estate Agent in Spain

NICLA-Casas

January 2003 – Present (10 years 11 months)

NICLA Property Consultancy is a residential estate agency that focuses exclusively on premier property for sale on Spain’s Costa Blanca (North & South), Costa Cálida and Costa de Almería.

We are able to offer you a wide selection of properties from an extensive portfolio of individually chosen properties. From inexpensive apartments and townhouses to inland country houses and magnificent villas – we can offer an unrivalled selection to suit all budgets and tastes. Each property for sale reflects our own high standards – an emphasis on quality and location.

As owner of NICLA I support all clients with their purchase, sale or investment of Spanish properties.

NVM- en ERA-makelaar

NVM- en ERA-Makelaardij van Mierlo BV

November 1994 – September 2003 (8 years 11 months)

Former Dutch real estate agency that offered Spanish real estate and where NICLA was founded.

As certificated estate agent I supported clients with the sale or purchase of their home in Holland. I gave them advise about renting, mortgages, etc. I also developed the website of this company, that was 1 of the first Dutch real estate companies that put their listing on the world wide web.
