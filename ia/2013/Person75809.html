Kim Marinoff's Summary

I truly believe that Master Franchising (Regional Development) is the one of the best kept secrets in business today. I don't know of another business model that provides a stronger return on your investment than Master Franchising.

Master Franchising is a tiered distribution of resources, allowing strong support to be provided at every level. As a Master Franchise owner of a territory, one shares in the franchise fees collected for each unit sold as well as sharing in the royalties of all the sales generated in your territory.

As a Regional Developer, you are able to be a mentor for each franchise owner in your territory, providing support and guidance resulting in maximizing unit level sales and operational excellence!!

As a way of giving back to the franchise industry, I conduct a weekly training webinar for new franchise brokers, educating them on how to sell master franchises.

In addition to franchise sales, I also help "mom and pops" franchise their businesses.

Specialties

Master Franchising, Franchise Sales, Franchise Training, and Franchise Operations.

President

A2B Franchise Consulting

Management Consulting industry

2010 – Present (3 years) Colorado

A2B Franchise Consulting offers a comprehensive service that is designed to match you with the perfect franchise. Initially, we need to determine if franchising is indeed the right thing for you. If so, we then begin the process of identifying your goals, lifestyle, and skill set.

I then gather all that information so that I can customize my research to your criteria and present you with your best franchise options. If one sparks your interest, I will arrange an appointment between you, myself and the franchise company.

As a franchise consultant, I am there for you throughout the whole process to coach and advise you as much or as little as needed. I will help you to be prepared for your meetings with the franchise company, other existing franchise owners, and help you develop the right questions so that you can understand all the components of the franchise business.

I am fully aware that there is always a certain amount of fear that people feel when there are changes in their life, decisions to be made, and feeling like everything is “on the line”. My ultimate goal is to ensure that you are comfortable and excited about moving forward with your (fully informed) decision.


My services are at no cost to you, nor bind or obligate you in any way. I have been in franchising for 14+ years, have owned 6 franchise businesses, and worked with an international franchise corporation in training, operations, sales and development. My experiences allow me to understand franchising from all angles so that I can provide you with comprehensive, expert advice and successfully coach you through, what would otherwise be a very time consuming, overwhelming process. I am committed and passionate about connecting you with a franchise in which you will achieve success, and (at the risk of sounding like an old cliché) make your dreams come true.
