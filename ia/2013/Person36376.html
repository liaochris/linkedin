Shelley Sandler's Summary

I am currently the founder and President of "DOGGINI ®, The Dog Harness Innovators. I could no longer stay idle and not create a better alternative for dogs. We needed to correct what we knew had been missing in this area of every day dog wear. With a group of skilled professionals, we brain stormed & created a sturdy dog harness that is made with engineered precision and synchronized to work together as a team. We could no longer watch dogs with injuries of chaffing skin, neck sprains, heads being jerked or with tracheal damage which can become acute over time.This is the reason we started DOGGINI® .Our DOGGINI® Harness will change the way you will look at harness. All our harnesses are made with quality materials, leathers, denims, suedes, and other strong and soft materials. We consider ourselves a designer of "functional practical" dog gear with a fashionable edge. Finally, our DOGGINI® dog coat gives you the option all of us have been waiting for. All our coats come with a built-in harness. No more short coats, no more coats that are secured with Velcro, no more coats with a hook on top & called a harness coat or vest!. Just an adjustable coat that has it's own harness attached. Using a similar principle of our harness we built it into every coat we make, it's just common sense not to make it any other way. We use only brass or metal hardware. We even created a leash organizer that has heavy duty snaps that you attach our version of a dogie bag to your leash plus an added option for the owner who can never find their keys, a key chain attachment to the leash.(using a spare key) so you can GO, GO GO. As a visionary this has probably helped with our collection, because I would not rest until it was
perfected. However we will still continue to create new & different designs at DOGGINI®.
USA Made so we can supervise our quality control & keep jobs here.
