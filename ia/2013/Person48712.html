Gina Schudi's Summary

Senior Service Delivery Manager with responsibility for over $10MM in technology projects across Credit Card Preferred Products, GWIM and Rewards.

Responsible for delivering IT solutions with end-to-end (cross-segment & cross-application) impacts for Credit Card products.

Partner with the line of business on new business RFPs, and client presentations. Monitor the projects pipeline and secure IT resources as needed. Consult on business requirements, provide client focused program management, resource engagement, consult with IT teams on technical architecture, design and initiative delivery. Manage critical vendor relationships. Escalate and monitor critical client IT issues for timely resolution and risk mitigation. Report up on program status, issues and risks. Develop and maintain trusted relationships across the organization. Work in conjunction with other parts of the enterprise for leverage, efficiencies and high quality results.

Specialties: Banking and Credit Card technologies including customized reward card programs, collateral (credit cards, card mailers, welcome packages, statements), online systems, marketing programs, partnership management.
