Kim Longeteig's Summary

Ali'i Marketing & Design is a full-service marketing, graphic design and advertising firm. Our specialty is working with radiology and healthcare-related companies to design, implement and consult on all forms of marketing - including web, multi-media and print collateral. We currently work with several RBMA affiliated radiology groups, imaging centers, medical billing and healthcare IT companies. Our clients are located across the US, Toronto, Canada and Singapore.

We are currently working on Website Design & Development, Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Social Media Marketing and Strategy, Branding, Newsletters, Advertising, Direct Mail, Brochures and other Print Collateral.

Kim Longeteig's background in radiology allows her to write detailed copy for the web and all types of marketing collateral and to share resources and ideas with clients that a typical design/marketing agency may be unable to provide.

Specialties: Website Design and Development, Search Engine Optimization, Social Media Marketing and Strategy, Advertising, Graphic Design, Copy Writing, Public Speaking, Strategic Marketing Planning & Consulting, Commercial Printing
