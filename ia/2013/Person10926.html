Aurora Carragal's Summary

I started acting when I was a teenager, when I joined the theatre group at Gran Capitán High School (Madrid, Spain), from 1995 until 1997. In 1998 I started my drama university degree at the University of Kent (Canterbury, UK), in the Torrelodones College (Madrid, Spain) what is now part of the educational program at the University Nebrija (Madrid, Spain). Once I finished my BA in Drama (Acting) in 2002, I continued my studies with a post-degree on Directing until 2004, reaching one of the best scores of the graduates. I had the chance and luck of working as an actress, director, assistant and even stage manager or sound and light technician, at some well-known theatres of Madrid (Spain), for a few different theatre companies, most of them related to children's theatre and musicals. In 2003 I founded TeDeCHé Theatre Company, together with some university partners and other actors an actresses, where I've been directing and producing shows, which are still being played. One year after I arrived Bonn (Germany), in 2006, I quit being part of the company's partners. I lived in Bonn for more than three years. In 2005 I made contact with the Die Raben experimental theatre company, and I joined their body expression workshop, and I was the cultural adviser for the project "La Dama Boba" by Lope de Vega.

Because of personal matters, I had to leave the stages (and the backstage) for a few years. In 2011 I start a new theatre project based on a web serie.

I am an active person, responsible, hard worker, decisive, open-minded, used to work under pressure, talkative and communicative, and easy going.

I am opened to any proposal and ready to resolve any doubt you might have. If you wish to have any further information, or you have any question or even suggestion, feel free to contact me: aurora.carragal(at)gmail.com.

Specialties

Different theoretical and practical methods for acting and directing, knowledge of technical matters in theatre (lights and sound), good skills for managing a team, work under pressure, creativity and fast solving of problems
