Matthew Levy's Summary

I am a director-level Human Resources professional with more than fifteen years of experience in the pharmaceutical sector. I’ve had the good fortune of partnering with all functions throughout my career: R&D, Manufacturing, Sales & Marketing and Corporate Staff.

I’m pleased to have also crafted a broad base of experience that helps me create value for my company: first as a line leader early in my career and second in Human Resources. I have held both Human Resources generalist roles as well as Human Resources specialists roles. I believe this gives me a unique ability to understand and apply synergies in these positions to yield the greatest benefit to the business leaders with whom I partner.

My enjoyment in helping people and teams excel is not new. Ever since I can remember I’ve enjoyed working with and inspiring other people. As a teenager I was the scrappy shortstop and field general for the high school baseball team and I was always the guy that took the lead on project teams.

Nowadays, these traits manifest themselves in three main ways. First, in executive-level human resources partnering in top tier pharmaceutical and biotech companies. Second, born out of a career transition of my own a few years ago combined with many years of corporate human resources experience, I enjoy offering career guidance to executives, mid-career professionals and recent undergraduate and master’s-level/MBA graduates. Third, I am proud to be President-elect of the Philadelphia Society of People & Strategy, a 300-person non-profit, senior level HR professional organization.

My passion is working closely with executive teams to understand the business and design and execute human resource plans that help to drive business value. I love doing this by surrounding myself with people that have a strong ethical compass and who prefer to help and inspire others as I do.

A sampling of my accomplishments can be viewed in my resume that appears below.
