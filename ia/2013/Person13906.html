Bev Rosen's Summary

Bev Rosen, Principal of WorkWonders provides learning consultation, employee relations expertise, coaching and professional development and wellness seminars to leaders and their staff. Her experience is diverse - sexual harassment coaching for a senior law partner, team building for the arts divisions of a private day school, key note speaker for trade association conferences on gender and generational diversity, preferred stimulating trainer for 32 employee assistance companies, and human resource consultant for demoralized pharmacy department and dueling nursing units of a hospital. Our workplace learning and performance goal is to assist healthy organizations, healthy teams and healthy individuals in their professional and personal life.

Specialties

Bev Rosen has the unique background of being a clinical psychotherapist (MSW), an MBA, certified in all personality profile assessments, mediation, coaching, and critical incident counseling. She has held two academic appointments and has a diverse history of working with all levels of staff in multiple industries including non-profits, municipalities, corporate, educational, health care, legal, manufacturing, political and the human service agencies.
