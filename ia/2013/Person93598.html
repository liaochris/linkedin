Michelle Tennant, M.A.'s Summary

Wasabi Publicity, Inc. and Blue Kangaroo, Inc. (dba PublicityResults.com) Chief Creative Officer, Michelle Tennant Nicholson, specializes in breaking news and has been called a five star publicist by Good Morning America producer, Mable Chan. In 2009, Michelle placed a new client on Dr. Phil within 8 hrs of contract.

She's been doing PR for 20 yrs and has seen the PR world transition from typewriters to Twitter. She works daily with high level media around the world. Here's more about Michelle Tennant in her own words ...

I tell stories to newsmakers - that's why my blog is called StorytellerToTheMedia.com (where my top clients are listed) My full client list is at www.SearchPressKits.com and my favorite media tool where media friends can be cloaked and still find sources anonymously is www.PitchRate.com.

What do I do? My stories become the news of the day. When newsmakers share what I say, the public hears, views and reads stories that make a difference to life.

I connect TV producers, radio hosts, magazine editors, internet bloggers and print journalists with interesting people, places and products. My PR team assists people in helping the media (not waste their time).

My goal is to impact at least one positive news story each day.

In the process of making our world a better place to live, I also intend to put food on the table for my family. So if you've got a great story to tell or a great business opportunity, bring it.

Specialties

Publicity strategy, media relations, online press kits, branding, social media outreach, public identity, key messaging, sound bites, press release writing, bylined articles, website presence, ghost writing and local to international marketing.
