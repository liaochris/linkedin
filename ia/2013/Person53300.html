Heiko Maiwand's Summary

Heiko is a Interaction Designer with a Master's from Indiana University. He is designing and exploring future interaction concepts for Audi and the other VW brands at their research and development lab in Silicon Valley.

He is on the Human-Machine Interface team working on projects that range from market adaptations, to new physical input devices, to post-combustion mental models.

His goal is to make products that are intuitive, engaging, and usable that will make people's lives easier and more enjoyable.

Specialties

Design methods and research:
Familiar with human-centered design methods including ethnographic observation, contextual inquiry, affinity diagramming, usability testing, paper prototyping, personas, card sorting, sketching

Technical:
Expert in Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects, Flash Catalyst, SketchUp.
Experienced in Cinema4D, Premiere Pro
