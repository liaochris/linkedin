alexis g. mcvicker's Summary

Little Name was independently created by designer alexis g. mcvicker who designs for the child in all of us. She has resided in nyc, philadelphia, portland oregon and currently lives in new jersey. She has taught fashion design students at Philadelphia University, and has worked as a print designer for companies such as Anthropologie, Urban Outfitters, Nike, and Osh Kosh b'gosh.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
i like little things,
i love when things live beyond their physicality; when an idea transcends its material and grows into an interaction and a little nostalgic life of its own.
This thought inspired me to create a playful integrated collection of products and garments for children, to evolve a world of interaction around them and invoke and ignite a sense of imagination and comprehension that give meaningful value and entity to any object or thing.
