Kevin Williams' Summary

I've worked with Microsoft technologies for many years in the past, but now I work on Internet scale systems in Node.js and Java, as well as _limited_ use of Scala, Python, CoffeeScript, Erlang, and Ruby. I have worked with both Object Oriented and Functional programming paradigms, in problem domains which demanded high concurrency, high availability, high throughput, and horizontal scaling. I've worked with many, many open-source tools and fully support open source software. I have experience working in Linux, Mac OS X, and Windows environments, but primarily develop on Mac and deploy on Linux.

I am completely self-taught. I believe in embracing change and being prepared for the future, to prevent being run over by it as it passes me by.
