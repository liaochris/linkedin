This new computer controlled machine will eventually replace the airbags & seat belt/harnesses used in cars today. Here is how it works. A computer controlled net is buckled in when you sit on a car seat; instead of a seatbelt and harness. This net is attached to a machine that can be built into the seat or attached to the seat. The net allows the speed and the -pressure of the passenger, pushing against the net, to be controlled after a car crash.
