~ How I Can Help You Market Your Service Business Online ~

I am a social media marketing consultant and internet marketing virtual assistant and I help women service-based professionals grow their business and take it to the next level by creating massive visibility using the internet. As a social media marketing consultant and social media manager, I create custom social media strategies, deliver a comprehensive social media action plan tailored to meet your specific business goals and execute them.


~ Why You Would Like to Work With Me ~

I have worked with several small business owners as their social media marketing consultant and social media manager. My approach is simple--to make social media marketing easy and fun! Social media is overwhelming, and I'm here to make it easy for you.

A huge number of social media marketing consultants would tell you that they can do all the footwork for you, that you don't need objectives, and that you don't need to get involved in the campaign, I think that's a big red flag right there! I will make social media marketing simple and guide you every step of the way, and let YOU participate actively for our efforts to work. We will work together to achieve your goals.


~ Who I Work With ~

My clients are coaches, speakers, authors, and service-based professionals who have a no-excuses approach in creating buzz for their business, and those who want to partner with a social media marketing consultant and virtual assistant to help them strategize, plan, and implement a solid plan.
