Joseph J. Nicholson III's Summary

With over 15 years combined experience in architecture and construction, I am looking to maintain my path within a professional position as a conceptual designer, architectural draftsman, or graphic, technical practitioner. With a solid background in digital design technologies, along with practical applied field skills, I am interested in sharing my knowledge and offering support, where needed. Having an outstanding, varied resume and portfolio, I feel I am strategically optimal in any studio or construction operations environment. Being of a strong art and design background, and expertise in computer programming, I feel that architectural design - urban/regional planning are my natural abilities within technical work in a studio environment. I feel that I have excellent skills, which would be perfect towards a construction design/developer group, but consider myself a disciplined architectural trainee, primarily, and therefore consider the proper outlets to the finalized design program, executed properly, first and foremost.

Specialties: 3D modeling, CAD drafting, (BIM) building information modeling, photo-realistic rendering with professional graphics software, project management, logistical support, writing.
