Holley Pokora's Summary

Twelve years of corporate Fortune 500 sales and marketing prior to partnering in a builder merchandising firm, IQP. For 7 years I helped to build a fledgling model home design firm into a premier Philadelphia firm partnering with builders in marketing their multi-million dollar development projects. I was responsible for all aspects of project management, including construction meetings, design presentations and decisions and installation of model home or clubhouse.
In 2008 I created Idea Design to marry my marketing and sales background with my design success. I love all aspects of my business. Working with realtors, homeowners, business owners to create wonderful spaces!

Specialties

Real Estate staging, Interior decorating, design consultations, space planning, and builder/developer merchandising
