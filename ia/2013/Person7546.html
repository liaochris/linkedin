Andy Reiman's Summary

Founder and President of Modern Image, a leading document scanning service bureau. Built from the ground up with cutting edge document scanning and conversion technology, we provide the highest quality services at an affordable price. Our experience in the service bureau has uniquely positioned my company as a knowledgeable and effective provider of document and records management systems, enterprise search engines, and in-house scanning systems.

Specific experience includes:

• Identification and quantification of cost reductions due to efficiencies created by document scanning.
• Laws and regulations relating to records management and document retention, protecting our clients from government regulators and expensive e-discovery.
• Disaster prevention and recovery. Many of the activities necessary to ensure disaster prevention also enable Telework, a great benefit!

Previous to Modern Image, I managed North America sales operations for a leading document management company. I have served in various sales capacities within Computer Associates International, Compuware Corporation, BMC Software, and Starbase Corporation. Within Worldweb.net, I was tasked with driving our content management system business through web integrators. My professional experience includes sales to the US Federal Government (Executive Office of the President, Dept of Homeland Security, FBI, Dept of Commerce, Environmental Protection Agency, National Agricultural Library), State and Local Government (Montgomery County, MD, DC City Government, Baltimore City and County, Howard County, State of Maryland, State of Virginia), companies (Lockheed Martin, Marriott, Constellation Energy, Careerbuilder.com, American Capital Strategies, Black and Decker, Fannie Mae, National City Bank, Chevy Chase Bank), non-profit organizations (PETA, United Transportation Union, Conservation International), and law firms (Karp Frosh, Cohen and Dwin)

Specialties

Document Scanning and Conversion, Adobe Acrobat and advanced collaboration features, Enterprise Content Management, Document Management, Web Content Management, Historical Archiving, Library Preservation, Record Management.

Extensive knowledge of road and mountain bike maintenance!
