Carolyn Moor's Summary

~ My Myers-Briggs test says I'm an ENFJ (extraversion, intuition, feeling, judgement).
~ Strength Finders 2.0: Activator, Futuristic, Ideation, Connectedness, Strategic.
~ A classic Aquarian whose humanitarian nature and leadership is compassionately God given.

All perfectly in line with being a Mentor to Women, Single Moms & Widows, Women's Event Speaker, Interior Design & Workshop Leader, Author and Community Thought Leader.

Things that motivate me: Grace, Dreams, Conscious Thinking, Global Solutions for Peace, Neuroscience, Shadow Work, Extraordinary Spiritual Living, Environmental/Color Psychology, Bodies of Water, Snowflakes, Nature, Love and Simple Pleasures. These motivate me to become the best person possible. Choosing life with each and every new sun and moon rise.

Born in LA, raised in rural Little Rock, matured in Austin, St. Louis and Orlando, adopted by Denver and Estes Park, Carolyn is at home in every environment and circle of life.
