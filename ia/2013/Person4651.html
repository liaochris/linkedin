Alison D. Gilbert's Summary

I am retired as a social media marketing and graphic design consultant. I come from a family of business owners and entrepreneurs. I have many interests and thrived for 30 years on enterprises from my passionate interests.

My blogging activities include curating (gathering) information. I then create blog posts and online magazines using venues to share them. My intent is that others will find them relevant, educational and even entertaining. The information I 'curate and create' involves two areas of concentration: small biz marketing, formerly MARKETING BYTES, and artistic material in ART | FOOD | HEALING, known as ALISON*S ART. Both MARKETING BYTES and ALISON*S ART have active pages on LinkedIn and weblogs.

I am the retired owner of the local business, MARKETING BYTES, Solutions 4 Local Biz. It was a boutique style - very personal services- hybrid company specializing in both traditional graphics/advertising and online marketing.

I was an entrepreneur in the health food industry involving nutritional counseling, teaching healthy cooking and eating. I created marketed and sold an allergy-free muffin product.

I had another business in the art field. It included decorative painting on client owned re-used furniture as well as painting small household objects called Recycled with Love Collectables. I also invented a Five Step Decorative Painting System.

Another business I had was in the holistic health field. I represented practitioners, educated the public and advocated for holistic modalities to become part of the mainstream of medicine and healing.

Marketing Bytes started as a graphic design firm and grew to include all phases of online marketing, marketing consulting and social media. This involved working with clients on the development, promotion and projects evaluation of their businesses. I also specialized in their branding 'from the inside out'. I designed their visual identity, collateral materials and oversaw their web design and social media.

Specialties

Advertising, craft artisan, brand management, brochure design, budgeting, business development, charts, community relations, consulting, corporate identity, customer relations, education, grant writing, local marketing, media buying, media placement, microsoft programs, newsletters, organizational skills, painting, presentation skills, press releases, profit, promotional materials, radio, spreadsheets, teaching, media appearance, web site production, copy writing,
