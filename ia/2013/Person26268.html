    What are some major trends in social media that you have seen changing and evolving in the first quarter of 2011?

    This was selected as Best Answer

    Maria I think there's a de-evolution taking place with social media marketing. It's subtle, but I still see it happening.   To your point, customers are getting bombarded with promotions and behaviors are changing as a result of that.   For customers, referrals by friends and family members are becoming more and more important because of overwhelm. If I have a problem and I do online research to find a solution and there's no clear leader in that space to choose from or there's just too much info to sift through...more than likely I'll ask a friend if they know of any good "fill in the blank".   For businesses that should serve as a sign post. Companies have to stay focused and remain crystal clear about who their ideal clients are and try as much as is ethically possible to gain access to the conversations going on in their heads when they're seeking out a particular solution or product.   With that understanding, they should stay consistent about creating quality educational content in the formats most consumed by their target market. Here is the catch though....educating your target market isn't even going to be enough in and of itself soon.   Companies are going to have to practice shaping the context within the education they provide. The goal being to influence the actual evaluation taking place in the prospects minds to a standard that only that company can meet. Positioning is probably THE most important thing for businesses to focus on.   To summarize my point...it's not about the technology, it's about the Psychology and the Systems.   You have a strong business over there. What are some that you've seen?  Love to hear your input as well.   Regards,   

    Colin Alexander Taylor
