Angela Bost's Summary

BOST Concrete & Construction, LLC is a general construction company servicing NJ from small repairs to complete new building construction. Such servies include new building, renovations, restorations, basements, crawl space, additions, brick pavers and many more. Strong buildings start with a strong foundation and BOST Concrete & Construction, LLC commits to providing the most highly trained, OSHA certified carpenters, laborers and masons to ensure customer satisfaction. No punch list ever needed...we get it right the first time! We specialize in concrete and masonry foundations including POLYCRETE Insulated Concrete Forms (www.polycreteusa.com) to protect your building from any weather related devastation. Our competitive prices are backed by 38 years experience in the construction industry so we know exactly what the job needs to complete it properly, safely and within budget. We understand and care about our clients needs and indoing so we continue to deliver construction to the highest professional standards. NJ Home Improvement License #13VH06256800.
