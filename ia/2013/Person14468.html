Bill Strawderman's Summary

Bill is a versatile executive with a reputation for delivering with excellence and leading with empathy. With an appetite for growth and progress, he enjoys challenging convention and building new things. He has a passion for growing knowledge and mentoring others.

Bill recently led the transformation of AT&T's b2b digital marketing platforms and constructively instigated the broader marketing organization to rethink purchase influence models and the role of digital channels.

Over a four year period, Bill and his team have moved digital from the margin to the mainstream. They have built digital marketing programs and practices which generate revenue at scale and influence complex enterprise technology purchase decisions.

Bill's specialties and recent experiences: digital strategy, b2b marketing, digital marketing, social media marketing, multi-channel marketing, search engine marketing (SEO & PPC), demand generation, lead nurturing, marketing automation, business intelligence
