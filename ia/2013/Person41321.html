Emily Powell's Summary

Emily is an elementary art teacher for grades 1-5 on a renewable one-year provisional Arkansas teaching license while completing her final coursework for a Master of Arts in Teaching.

With a Bachelor of Fine Arts in graphic design, creative and technical internship experience, and 2 years working as a professional front end developer, she hopes to bring a fresh perspective and lots of technology expertise to the classroom.

Her educational goals include surviving the first year of teaching, graduating with her Masters, earning a standard Arkansas teaching license, and eventually working toward National Board Certification. Her freelance goals include creating websites or marketing for local clients and expanding her personal artistic portfolio.
