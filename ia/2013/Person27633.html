Craig A. Valine's Summary

This Is Who I Am:

I have a passion for helping entrepreneurial business owners get 'unstuck' in their business and helping those who are already successful be even more successful. I learned at an early age that everyone's primary business is 'marketing.' I became a serious student at a young age and strive to help as many people as possible with my knowledge and expertise to reach their goals in the fastest time possible.

I have served on several Board of Directors for not-for-profit member-service organizations and have found that most members of these organizations don't utilize their board to the fullest extent possible for their benefit. The truth is, by surrounding yourself with the right people (board of directors, business advisers, a Mastermind group, etc.), you can multiply your success in a very short period of time.

To this day, I am a serious student of marketing. My greatest lessons have been taught to me by Dan Kennedy, Bill Glazer, and Jay Abraham. Because of the impact these mentors have had on my life, I became the ONLY Independent Business Advisor for Glazer-Kennedy Insider's Circle in the Los Angeles Area. And, I am a protege' of Jay Abraham.

This is Who I Help:

Growth oriented small business owners, Visionary & aggressive business owners, Success-oriented Sales Professionals, Business to business, Progressive and marketing-oriented doctors, dentists, chiropractors, cosmetic surgeons, financial planners, lawyers, CPA’s, Veterinarians, and other professionals in private practice, Service business operators, Savvy e-commerce and online/internet marketers, Authors, speakers, consultants, information marketers, and publishers.

This is How I Help My Members & Clients:

- Monthly Power Events in the Pasadena area

- Monthly Peak Performance/VIP Mastermind Group Meetings

- Private Coaching, Consulting, and/or Advisory

- Speaking to entrepreneurial groups & associations
