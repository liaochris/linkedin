Linda Cheung's Summary

Whatever your personal feelings about social media, you can’t ignore it.

The days when you could tightly control your marketing and PR messages are gone. Social media gives your prospects and clients a platform and a collective voice.

This new transparency beings challenges and new opportunities for businesses of all kinds: You may not be able to control the message... but you can influence it.

CubeSocial is an award winning Social CRM and services business designed to help you influence the influencers, move the market and win new business.

Our objective is to help you build a social media capability that delivers real ROI. From marketing through customer service to sales, our solutions are designed to cut through the social media chaos and put you back in control.

With HonestyBoxx the buyer asks their question and says how much they'd be happy to pay - just like the honesty box payment system you sometimes see at small stores. And, by helping professionals reach a new market for their expertise we're providing them with a stream of warm leads and clients at a time when new business is at a premium.

Chief Executive Officer

CubeSocial

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry

February 2011 – Present (2 years 9 months)

CubeSocial CEO, Linda Cheung has an impressive track record in devising innovative software and business solutions that deliver benefits worth millions of pounds in the toughest of markets.

A former Executive Director at Morgan Stanley, Linda was promoted to a Chief Operating Officer role at 32 after making her mark as a key player in the launch of the company's first ever credit card.

Linda co-founded CubeSocial in 2011 with Mark Bower, previously a Lead Program Manager at Microsoft, after asking the business community what it needed most. The answer: "new ways to win new business". Their response: CubeSocial an award winning software development and consultancy company all about winning business through social media.

CubeSocial was named as one of the Top 20 Start Ups of 2011 by Startups, selected as a winner in the Local Business Accelerator Awards, and was chosen as a finalist in the 2012 Cloud Computing World Series Awards in the Best Social CRM category.

A recognised thought leader on the topic of social media for business and frequent speaker at conferences and events, Linda’s highly acclaimed presentations provide a rare business perspective on social media that frequently see her called back for repeat deliveries.

Linda’s 13-year career in highly regulated markets with an uncompromising emphasis on financial ROI, gives her a unique drive for tangible results and solutions that really work, whilst avoiding the hype often associated with social media.

Linda was awarded a CIMA Fellowship for her strategic experiences and successes in 2003, and represented the private sector as judge for the CIMA Annual Awards 2010.

Linda is a dotforge accelerator mentor, and champions entrepreneurship as a contributor to the Department for Business, Innovation & Skills, regional initiatives such as Enterprising Barnsley and Basingstoke Business Leaders Forum, and international initiatives such as Global Entrepreneurship Week.
