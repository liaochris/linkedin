David Pepper's Summary

I am an information systems specialist living in Burlington, Vermont. I thrive in serving organizations with complex missions (such as public health, academic research, membership orgs, and charitable non-profits).

My specialty is analyzing business problems or goals, and applying a collaborative design process to achieve a solution. I’ve worked on many desktop, web, and device applications whose purpose is to interact with users, or to publish information or data. Over and over I’ve found that beyond a functional design, the most successful information systems have institutionalized workflows and processes around them.

This human context is an important part of a sustainable, effective and “living” design. For this reason, I study the business need carefully and I work in the project planning stages more than most developers. I love designing and prototyping with real users.
