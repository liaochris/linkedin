Larry Scott, JD, GPHR (lscott@lucasgroup.com)'s Summary

Larry Scott, JD, GPHR, has a professional profile that distinguishes him as one of today’s most knowledgeable legal recruiters. His career spans the areas of legal recruiting, domestic and international law, accounting, and global human resources. While recruiting attorneys in Texas and nationwide, Larry works with a wide range of law firms, from boutique and corporate in-house organizations to global 100 firms, offering his clients and candidates an international and seasoned approach to legal recruiting.

During his career, Larry has practiced law as a tax and international transactions attorney with national firms and in-house organizations. He has served as a tax expert with global accounting firms in the United States and internationally, including in Hong Kong and Vietnam. As practice group leader for Coopers & Lybrand in Hanoi, Vietnam, Larry opened and managed the firm’s tax and investment practice. Larry’s background also includes his years as a legal recruiter.

Larry serves on the University of Houston’s Human Resources Development Advisory Board, and is president of Houston International HR Roundtable, a highly successful human resources educational organization. Larry holds a Juris Doctor degree from the University of Houston Law Center, where he graduated cum laude, and he earned his master’s and bachelor’s degrees in accounting from the University of Florida, where he graduated with high honors. Outside of work, Larry enjoys the outdoors and is also a musician. He is an avid guitarist who performs regularly at blues jams in Houston.
