Aaron White's Summary

My goal is to continue being creative while honing my skills and continually improving both my quality and speed as an artist.
I tend to be drawn towards IP, brand, character and world development.
Another passion of mine is painting detailed figurative works which I do on a weekly basis.

Specialties: Highly knowledgeable in Adobe Photoshop and After Effects and Flash for animation.
Skilled with a variety of particle systems and able to pick up new ones.
Experienced using the Unity game engine.
Creating UI / HUD demo animations and compositing sizzle videos.
I'm a classically trained artist, so as much as I love software, I'm very focused on having strong fundamental art skills.
