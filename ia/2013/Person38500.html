Daniel Shaw's Summary

** RECRUITERS: This is not the droid you're looking for. **

Founders, team leads, developers, creatives: in general, I'd love to talk to you. Even if we cannot collaborate on something right now, I'm always interested in meeting people who are passionate about what they do. I have a number of opportunities that I'm hiring for at my new startup. The Node Firm is always looking for talented collaborators and contractors. Through any number of the organizations that I work at, with or advise there may be something that's a growth opportunity for you.

I enjoy making software that is beautiful, intuitive and obvious from code to product. I work hard to create meaningful growth opportunities through teaching, leadership and mentoring. I find it immensely gratifying to act as a catalyst to others who may see benefit from this.

I'm a frequent speaker at Node.js, JavaScript, Realtime conferences. I host of the NodeUp podcast. I organize events like Hardware Hack Weekend and International Nodebots Day 7/27 to provide opportunities to gather, learn and have fun. I enjoy building things with Node.js and have worked at a number of Node.js startups including the Infrastructure Team at Voxer, leading the Socket.io scaling effort at Spreecast, and as Lead Engineer at Storify.

Before moving back to San Francisco to work on Node.js full-time, I worked in education, state government, Department of Defense contracting, health care, and started off my career providing media branding services to small to medium businesses in print and on the web.

I love the big data and scaling problems that come out of social networks and social software.

If you're interested in these things, I suggest that you follow me on Twitter.

- @dshaw
