Dinah Wisenberg Brin's Summary

Award-winning journalist/writer/editorial professional with background covering business, government and general news for print, wire and web. Adept at spotting trends, researching and transforming complex information into clear, engaging copy. Handle multiple projects in fast-paced, competitive environment, working well independently and as part of team.

Specialties: News and feature reporting and writing; B2B and consumer-oriented journalism; web content production; university, trade association and internal publications; interviewing sources from waitresses and veterans to pop-culture figures, U.S. senators and corporate CEOs; identifying business, healthcare and cultural trends; writing about personal finance, entrepreneurship, HR issues, health policy, company and industry developments; good sense of story, eye for detail, ear for dialogue.
