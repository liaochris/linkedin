Allen Meyer's Summary

At the intersection of Art, Technology & Design is the Hot Spot, the conceptual idea that brings them all together. Art inspires, technology connects and design makes it intuitive. The ubiquity of technology and marketing makes the Hot Spot more important than ever—does it serve a need, does it help people? Is it good?
