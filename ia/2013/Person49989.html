Keith Winn's Summary

Thought leader to the industry. Original developer of several CU innovations since the 1980's. The Cash Plan: combining benefits of member & SEG marketing for credit unions through insurance offerings. Designed and rolled out first warranty program for credit unions in Florida. Built "Worry Free Loan System:" first web-based "Menu-Offering" auto loan insurance marketing system for credit unions.

The latest innovations, cuZOOM! and bankZOOM!, are product suites designed to maximize auto loan growth and portfolio profitability. Built specifically for credit unions & community banks, ZOOMing! provides solutions to build wallet-share by converting indirect single transaction borrowers to multi-relationship members & customers; recapture auto loan business from other institutions; provides technology and systems to allow credit challenged borrowers the opportunity to secure auto loans; and allows credit unions to help their members save on new and used car purchases through the TRUECar portal. All while creating the highest possible profits for the institution.

In addition to specific financial institution projects: Co-developer of trademarked Approved Green Business Certification program and author of the innovative "Climate Change and Sustainability Accounting" white paper and CPE course for accountants. Adjunct Faculty at Broward College.

Professional author and speaker - Latest publication: Convert One Time Indirect Borrowers to Multi Relationship Members White Paper - (cuzoom.org/whitepaper) addresses a historical and growing issue within the financial institution industry. Articles have been featured in Bank News, Gold Coast Magazine, the New Economy Post and the Environmental Leader as well as MNCPA News. Designed and hosted business sustainability webinar for the PA Credit Union Association; speaker at the KY Credit Union League educational conference; presented "short term/long term" strategies at VACPA and MNCPA conferences.

Specialties

Credit Union marketing and equipment efficiency systems.

PACE (Property Assessed Clean Energy) consulting and facilities & energy management systems designed to streamline operation and reduce costs.

Equips - Niche market services to dramatically slash costs and time spent on managing maintenance contracts for credit unions and banks.

cuZOOM! - Generating auto loans, revenues, and fee income while keeping member connected throughout entire loan cycle.
