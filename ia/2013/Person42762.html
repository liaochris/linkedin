Erin Jacobs' Summary

The current focus of my work is the creation of concepts for the application and visualization of relevant, targeted social content, that is sourced and curated in real-time through the Mass Relevance platform—enabling entertainment, media, retail, and manufacturing brands to promote consumer interaction and drive real-time engagement.

As a "Hybrid" Interaction & Visual Designer, I continually strive to expand my understanding, expertise and awareness of design methodologies, emerging technologies/products/markets and user interests and expectations—so that I may create innovative designs evolving existing systems, and enriching the user's experience.

Specialties

Visual and Interaction design, design system/interaction pattern creation, and documentation for mobile UI (Android, iPhone, Blackberry), 10' TV and media experiences, Desktop software, Web, and embedded UI.

Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Fireworks, Dreamweaver, BBEdit, Omnigraffle, Keynote, Powerpoint, Word.
