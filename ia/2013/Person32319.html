David Wall's Summary

I spent the better half of the last two decades focused on the principles of elegant design and usability as SR UX ARCHITECT for a large swath of technology companies in the NW. I began my career in the early 90's as a graphic artist and transitioned into web design/development (and eventually usability) almost immediately. I cut my "UX" teeth in the labs at Microsoft working on various projects from desktop applications to web pages. My current focus is in designing for mobile including mobile first and web to mobile conversion methodology.

My web career began as a freelance designer before migrating to contractual and vendor roles in the tech sector. I enjoy the start-up life cycle and am currently involved in a few start-up's of my own. I recently founded a company called Good Road Network (Proof of concept set to launch end of 2013). I also co-founded and built a company focused on youth athlete recruitment for college sports .

I am currently working heavily in the mobile sector and I am a key stake holder in the push to mobile for TireBuyer.com. My start-up (GoodRoadNetwork.com), is also being launched as a "mobile first" experience.

Lastly - I am a member of the technical advisory committee at SCCC and I love working with this college. I have managed to place a lot of stellar candidates through my connections with this school. They have a top notch web program with an amazing staff of gifted people.
