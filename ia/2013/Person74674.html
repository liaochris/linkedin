Kersten Harries' Summary

In January 2012, I moved to Madrid and since that time I have been specializing in providing design and project management services to foreigners and expats with residential renovation projects in Spain. I also have been collaborating on a local start-up venture and providing consultancy services to clients abroad.

My previous work experience ranges from the non-profit sector, to the traditional design studio both in the United States and Italy. I have managed projects from initial programming and design phases through the completion of construction, for institutional clients. Having completed a Master in Sustainable Architecture, I am always interested in collaborating with others who share my belief that design excellence and responsible architecture is rooted in sustainable solutions. I enjoy opportunities to share my knowledge and passions with others through teaching and my involvement within professional and community based associations.

Specialties: project management, sustainable architecture, international practice, collaborative design, teaching, autocad, adobe creative suite
