Carrie Hagen's Summary

For the right company, Carrie offers the best of both worlds. As a recent graduate with degrees in Accountancy and Finance; she has a current education that takes into consideration the recent changes in GAAP. As a seasoned business professional, she exhibits the wisdom that comes from experiencing more than one cycle.

Among Carrie’s most treasured accomplishments is the role she played in the success of the affordable housing program in the City of Sonoma. Carrie has an extensive background in business, particularly in the real estate and finance industries. Her business acumen and instinct helped insulate her clients from the effects of the financial crisis. Carrie is results oriented and possesses strong problem-solving, communication, and organizational skills. These skills have been evident in her various decision-making roles.

The ideal opportunity for Carrie will be with a company that has an entry level accounting position with room for growth. Ideally, this company will be located between Camarillo and Buellton.

If you know of a position that might be the right fit, please contact Carrie at carrie@carriehagen.com.

Thank you for your consideration.
