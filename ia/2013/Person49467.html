Grant Goodwin's Summary

Making people who matter offline, matter online.

As a Personal Online Branding Expert, Grant spends every day making the web work for credible executives, professionals, and business owners across North America.

You may not have the time, the interest or the know-how to showcase your thought leadership and professional credibility online. Unfortunately, that does not stop people from looking for you. Existing clients, potential clients, competitors and colleagues are searching for you online. What will they find?

Will they find an integrated learning experience that showcases your most interesting ideas, stories and solutions? Or will they find old news items, broken links, a dry profile, or nothing at all?

By first measuring your Visibility, Credibility and Readiness through the doimatter.com assessment tool, then identifying your unique professional strengths, specialties and challenges, Grant's team will build your strong personal online brand – establishing you as a thought leader in your field and ensuring web users are finding your best self online.
