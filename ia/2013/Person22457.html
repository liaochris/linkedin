Chaz Decker's Summary

Chaz Decker, graduate of Boston University with a degree in Computer Science, has over 10 years professional experience in the interactive industry. He works as a visual and user experience designer and creative director.

Chaz has a passion for creating engaging and usable cross-channel experiences, with emphasis on digital, that resonate with targeted demographics, align with business goals, and exceed client expectations.

Chaz has worked with many notable clients including: Anheuser-Busch; Benjamin Moore; The Coca-Cola Company; Cox Enterprises; Delta Air Lines; The Home Depot; Louis Vuitton; Michelin; Newell-Rubbermaid; Royal Philips; Sears; Shell; Suntrust; Turner Broadcasting; and United Parcel Service.

Specialties

Creative Direction, Art Direction, Interactive and Digital Design, Interaction Design, User Experience (UX) Design, Visual Design, User Interface (UI) Design (Application and mobile, tablet and touchscreen interfaces), Brand and Identity Design, Brand Strategy, Campaign and Tactic Ideation, Social Media Integration, Information Architecture, Prototyping, Competitive Analysis, Web Design
