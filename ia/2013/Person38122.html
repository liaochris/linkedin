Dr. Mark Pogue's Summary

Dr. Pogue's educational background and training provide the expertise to perform a variety of procedures. Facial surgeries range from complex cases involving misaligned jaws and correction of facial injuries, to extraction of teeth. Facial cosmetic procedures such as facelifts, brow lifts, laser skin resurfacing and nose reshaping are performed with an aesthetic eye for perfection. Additionally, Dr. Pogue works cooperatively with other specialists such as orthodontists, dentists and physicians to give you the best result.

Most surgeries take place in the state-of-the-are, accredited outpatient surgery center located within the offies. This provides a private, monitored environment for your comfort and safety. Intravenous sedation is available for most procedures.

The Pogue Center offers a team of professionals including a licensed aesthetician and medical esthetics nurse.These professionals, along with Dr. Pogue can help you achieve optimum skin health and facial appearance. A variety of modern techniques, such as lasers for eliminating unsightly facial veins and hair as well as the latest rejuvenation products such as Botox, Sculptra and Juvederm are offered to obtain your goals safely and effectively.
