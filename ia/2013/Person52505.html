Helena Lu's Summary

Digital designer for anything online including web apps, new mobile concepts, interactive ad units and websites.

I have extensive experience designing and building responsive designs for a range of devices and operating systems, eg iOS, Android, Windows Phone, Symbian, BlackBerry & feature phone devices. I also have a bad habit of being an OCD tester and will test multiple times on multiple devices to ensure that the user gets the best experience possible.

I've strong hand coding in HTML5/CSS3 with cross browser support on both desktop and mobile. Love tinkering around with cutting edge browser technology.

Strong focus on user-centered design from wireframe to high fidelity mockups and prototypes.

Specialties: UI/UX Design, Mobile, Web Design, Interaction design, Prototyping, New Media, Technology
