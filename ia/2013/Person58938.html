Jason Ilicic's Summary

My name is Jason Ilicic, but the nickname of “Jasey” has developed over the years. I am a 23 year-old professional web developer from Adelaide, SA, Australia, who is very passionate when it comes down to learning how technologies work and integrate together, or making a photo fit perfectly in its frame. I was a DJ at a popular pub in the city and did photography for many years, but decided to pursue my focus on my IT interests.

When I was about 7 years old, I started taking a strong interest in computers and internet technology. I went on throughout primary school and high school, taking part in as many IT-related courses as I could, where I went on to University to study information technology in networking and security.

I work full-time during the day with what I love doing, and spend my remaining time experimenting with computer systems and networks, designing solutions and developing advanced web applications.

There is a lot more to me than this brief introduction, so please feel free to ask me any questions.
