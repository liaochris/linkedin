Christie Lees, RN, BSCN's Summary

An expert in the health and wellness field, Christie served as Center Director and Perfect Health Program Director at the Chopra Center for Wellbeing, where key roles included wellness program management/development, wellness coaching, health education, and speaking. She is a Chopra Center certified Vedic Master, specialized in teaching Ayurveda, meditation, and yoga.

Christie has worked with a diverse group of people from around the globe, including those with a wide range of “day-jobs”, such as celebrities, executives, moms, and even Olympic athletes! Part coach and part cheerleader (minus the uniform and pom-poms), Christie uses her holistic and integrative approach to empower individuals to create a vibrant state of wellbeing in their lives.

Having worked internationally as a registered nurse in many top-ranked hospitals and clinics in the field of oncology, Christie (or “Nurse Christie” as she is often known) has a passion for helping individuals heal, and for creating the nurturing space where people feel heard.

A dynamic speaker and educator, Christie has lectured to groups ranging from 2 - 500 people, including Fortune 500 companies. She has even shared the stage at events with keynote speakers such as Deepak Chopra and Wayne Dyer!

As Founder and Chief Wellbeing Officer, Christie is currently working on launching Modern Wellbeing™, a coaching and consulting company devoted to encouraging the wellbeing of people everywhere and inspiring a more benevolent world.
