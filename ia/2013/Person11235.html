What is the CUSTOM DESIGN PROCESS when working with MAXE DESIGNS?
1. We start with an open discussion regarding your vision for your design, gather ideas, expectations, etc and pay deposit for project.
2. I create the original art to be used.
3. You will select or provide your wording, send all of the remaining details for the event/design project and any other information we will need.
4. I'll prepare a variety of designs for you to review.
5. After you select your final design and provide written approval - I'll print and prepare your final designs for delivery to you.
