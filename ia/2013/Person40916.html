Elliot Volkman's Summary

Greetings! I am a marketing communications strategist with a master's in communication. I have over 8 years of experience with social media marketing, community building and architecture, public relations, and marketing communications.

I'm currently split between Tantus Technologies leading marketing communication efforts, working with two startup clients (Open Compass and SetMeUp), and being the Vice President of Social Media Club DC.

Notably I have combined historical research regarding online community building into the first model designed to assist online community architects. The model is based on the psychological needs of members, the purpose of the ecosystem, and communication best practices.

In addition to my professional work, I have set out to create free and informative e-learning courses to empower everyone's use of social media. I have a B2B background for social media and marketing, and B2C and B2G experience across the board. I'm also a big fan of startups, and have a great deal of experience working with them.
