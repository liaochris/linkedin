Dr. John Bethell's Experience

Director/Co-Founder

Wavelength International

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Staffing and Recruiting industry

September 1998 – Present (15 years) Surry Hills, Sydney NSW 2010

Wavelength was started in 1998 with co-founder, Claire Ponsford as a recruitment firm that specialised in bringing doctors into Australia, New Zealand & Singapore on temporary work visas.

Our business has grown steadily since then to employ over 60 staff providing recruitment and other consulting services to a range of clients seeking clinical staff, both locum and permanent, local and international.

In addition to recruitment Wavelength is now a registered migration agency in Australia and assists a number of individuals and organisation with their immigration paperwork as a stand alone service.

Wavelength International is a back to back winner of the Recruitment Excellence Award for Boutique Agency for 2008 & 2009 and a winner in 2010 of Best Brand and Best Learning & Development organization. Wavelength is also listed for 2010, 2011 &2012 on the Business Review Weekly (BRW) "Best Place to Work" list.
