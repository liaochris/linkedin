Cynthia Gentry MLO#172955's Summary

I have been in the mortgage banking industry for over 25+ years starting out as a receptionist, I became a Loan Processor for 15 years, Operations Supervisor for 5 years and currently a Loan officer for 13 years. My knowledge and background in lending ensures smooth and successful closings. I strive to understand my clients unique requirements and personal goals. I have received numerous awards for: Outstanding Achievement in Volume, Accuracy and Difficulty and recently voted "5 Star Mortgage Professional" by Seattle's Best of 2012 and Seattle's Best of 2011. I attended American Bankers School of Mortgage Banking, and received certification for Loan Processing and Underwriting the Self Employed Borrower. My goals are to continue building strong relationships with clients by sharing my experience, strengths and knowledge of the lending process in today's market, which in turn allows them to make a more informed decision on what is best for their individual situation.

Specialties: First Time Homebuyer Class Instructor, Voted "5 Star Mortgage Professional" by Seattle's Best of Magazine 2012 and 2011
