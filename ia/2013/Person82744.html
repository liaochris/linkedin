Louis Leichter's Summary

I am the principal attorney and founder of Leichter Law Firm. Over the years my law firm has represented over a thousand licensed professionals before many Texas Administrative Agencies, including the Texas Medical Board, the Texas Board of Nursing, the Texas State Board of Pharmacy, the Texas Board of Dental Examiners, the Texas Department of Insurance, the Texas Real Estate Commission, and many others. Not only have we built strong relationships and ties with these Agencies, but we are conveniently located near many of them in downtown Austin, Texas, enabling us to better represent you.

Our goal at the Leichter Law Firm is to ensure the best possible outcome for your case. We always remember that it is not just your license, it is your livelihood.

Specialties

Texas Administrative Law, Professional Licensing Defense, Medical Licensing Defense, Professional License Representation, Medical License Representation, Criminal Defense
