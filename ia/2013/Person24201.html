Christine M Hall's Summary

I have over 25 years of experience working with high school students. I have always been passionate about the curriculum & instruction process. My focus has always been on insuring that students are being advised appropriately and challenged with academic rigor that meets their individual needs. I enjoy working with students who demonstrate potential as well as a drive to do well in college. It is my goal to reach out to those students that need assistance in finding the right fit in a college in order that they be successful in the future.

Specialties

College applications, college selections, essay editing, curriculum planning and academic advising. Middle and high school students.
