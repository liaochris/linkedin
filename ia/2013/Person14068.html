Bianca Smith's Summary

Bianca Smith is a digital marketer with more than eight years of success in marketing, communications, research, social media, copywriting and editing. She has worked with small and large organizations (non-profit and commercial) developing and executing successful communications campaigns using a mix of traditional and digital tools.

Additionally, she has a Bachelor of Business Studies (Marketing) and a postgraduate qualification in public relations.

Recent successes include increasing Twitter followers by 25% in two months and the number of unique engagers by 520% in two months.

Specialties: Social media and community management, copy writing and editing, digital marketing, event management, SEM, SEO, market research, and photography.
