Linda Wasche's Summary

Linda helps veterinary practices and pet-related businesses identify and target opportunities for client attraction and growth in their respective markets. She emphasizes a strategic approach to marketing based on understanding local market dynamics and leveraging existing client relationships. She works with general practices, emergency hospitals, specialty centers, pet supplies retailers and pet-related service providers around the U.S. to provide services and assistance in:

Determining client and pet owner perceptions
Market segmentation and niche identification
Identifying and delivering client value
Optimizing client communication through traditional and social media
Building local market credibility and leadership
Advancing pet owner awareness and boosting compliance
Capitalizing on the humanization of pets

Linda has more than 30 years' experience in the marketing and communications field as an officer of leading marketing agencies and as a former university professor. She founded LW Marketworks, inc., in 1994 specializing in the services sector and representing companies and firms in diverse areas including law, architecture, healthcare, accounting, financial services, veterinary medicine, information technology and hospitality.

Linda is a frequent speaker and has authored numerous articles on veterinary marketing. View her marketing tips for practices at www.LWmarketworks.com.

Linda is also a long-time member of the animal rescue community and has developed a branded product line and related programs to help boost awareness of pet spay and neuter and responsible pet guardianship. Proceeds from these efforts go to animal rescue organizations.
