Kevin Surace's Summary

CEO, Board Member, innovator and advisor - technology, cleantech, media, consumer products, manufacturing. Building businesses from $0 to $100M+ revenue. Awarded 23 patents. Led 28 acquisitions from both sides of the table. Inc Magazine's Entrepreneur of the Year in 2009.

Currently working with companies addressing: app scalability and security, internet of things, viral/social apps, mobile medical apps/sensors, cleantech, sleep tech.

CEO of Serious Energy for 9+yrs, where we developed, patented and produced disruptive technologies that reduce energy usage in buildings, the largest contributor of CO2. We built a team of 420 people driving a 63% CAGR over 8 years while delivering high tech materials, products, software and services to 70,000 projects including the Empire State Building and New York Stock Exchange and thousands of homes.

Over the years I have also led disruptive technologies including OnStar virtual advisor for GM, Perfect Commerce (online auctions), and mobile data (Air Communications) and drove the introduction of a number of industry firsts including the modern "human-like" voice user interface (i.e. Siri), the first wide-area-wireless data smart phone (AirCommunicator) and the invention of soundproof drywall (QuietRock).

I have been honored to be asked to deliver inspiring talks as speaker or keynote to hundreds of conferences including the notable TED series, many TEDx's, INC 5000, FortuneGreen, and keynote for the Democratic Caucus (House of Representatives).

I have been featured in BusinessWeek, Time, Fortune, Forbes etc. and have been recognized as Entrepreneur of the Year by Inc. Magazine, one of the top innovators of the decade by CNBC, named Tech Pioneer by the World Economic Forum (Davos), and inducted into the Innovation Hall of Fame at RIT.

I really love growing companies and strong teams, and helping organizations of all size attain disruptive innovation and positive exits.

And having fun along the way!
