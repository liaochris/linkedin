Francois Le Palec (linkedin@lepalec.com)'s Summary

With 15 years experience, Francois is a digital media professional with expert project management and product development skills across multiple internet and mobile applications in the media and entertainment space. Accustomed to ambitious projects (multi-territory, multi-lingual, multi-platform), Francois has a deep understanding and expertise in demanding environments (media, banking).

Francois has a passion for technology, media and design and has combined these interests in a number of ways during his career.

Based in London, he currently holds the position of Director of Service Delivery, EMEA at Glue Networks. In addition, he also sits on the boards of Glue Networks (Sacramento) and Le Fooding (Paris), and works on a consultancy basis as a digital media expert.

Most recently, Francois held the position of Director, Digital Media Operations at MTV Networks International in London – with a posting in New York – creating and supporting all int'l content production and delivery workflows for 164 territories across Asia-Pacific, Canada, Europe, Middle East & Africa and Latin America.

Francois began his career at MTV Networks France as Department Manager for Internet and Mobile, followed by a position in London where he oversaw digital media and broadcast technical developments across the EMEA region as Project Manager for MTV Networks Europe.

Prior to this, Francois developed a large number of websites for blue chip companies (Société Générale, Siemens, Renault) and co-founded a successful online art auction company in 1999.

Recent achievements:

• Launched L’Officiel des Galeries & Musées, France’s leading art guide iPhone app, selected by Apple as iPhone App Of The Week in June 2011

• Redefined France’s leading car maker Renault’s entire social media strategy in early 2011

• Developed MTV's online music video library, one of the industry’s largest with +30K titles available across 124 territories in 2009

Specialties

High-profile consumer-facing internet/mobile product development.
Digital content production and operations strategy expert.
Established, experienced and results-driven people manager.
Internet, mobile and video streaming technical expertise.
