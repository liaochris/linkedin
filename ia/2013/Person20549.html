Carolyn Smagalski's Projects

  • Philly Beer Geek 2013 Championship

    • May 2013 to June 2013
    Team Members: Carolyn Smagalski, Jason Harris, Stephen Hawk, Natalie DeChico, Ed Kirk

    The purpose of the Annual Philly Beer Geek Championship is to pay tribute to Philadelphia beer and to those who craft, celebrate, or consume it with enthusiasm and pride. To this end, our illustrious panel of judges will select and reward, as Philly Beer Geek 2013, the individual who exhibits the greatest universal knowledge and passion for Philadelphia Beer.

    Philly Beer Geek is a three-part competition. Contestants must be selected by a sponsor or win a Preliminary Qualifying Round held at a sponsoring bar, brewpub or restaurant; then move on to the Semi-Finals, and ultimately, to the Finals where an illustrious panel of celebrity judges evaluate and crown the Philly Beer Geek in this annual championship.

    The annual Philly Beer Geek Competition began in 2007 as an anchor event of Philly Beer Week, a ten-day celebration of “Philadelphia, the Best Beer Drinking City in America.” Co-founders Carolyn Smagalski, the Beer Fox of BellaOnline, and Jason Harris, Owner of Keystone Homebrew Supply in Montgomeryville and Bethlehem PA, launched the competition to reward “the individual who exhibits the greatest universal knowledge and passion for Philadelphia Beer.”

  • Collaboration Beer to benefit the Rena Rowan Breast Center, University of Pennsylvania

    • July 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Carolyn Smagalski, Erin Wallace, Marnie Old, Tara Nurin, Dominic Capece, John Stemler, David Wood, Matthew Stumpf, Bob Kerr

    In support of Breast Cancer Awareness Month, I have joined forces with three other dynamic women in the Beer World - Erin Wallace, Marnie Old, and Tara Nurin - to create a collaboration beer with Free Will Brewing for the benefit of the "guests" in need of services at the Rena Rowan Breast Center at the University of Pennsylvania, Philadelphia, PA. We have set a target of raising at least $10,000 for the Center through this collaborative effort. Our beer will be a sessionable Saison made with pale malt as a base, and highlighted with pink grapefruit zest, hibiscus and ginger, delicately finished with pink peppercorn. This beer (yet to be named) will be released in October. We will be distributing it in kegs as well as 750 ml corked and caged bottles, decorated with the graphics of Bob Kerr, a talented tattoo artist. Matthew Stumpf of Lionize is filming the entire project.

    • The Brewery History Society

    Authors: Carolyn Smagalski, P. Brown, J. Evans, M. Cornell, T. Webb, Z. Avery, R. Protz, J. Richards, J. Keeling, M. Dredge

    Dedicated to the most significant writer on beer there has been, Michael Jackson.   • Beer Hunter Whisky Chaser

    • Classic Expressions/Distilled Discourse, Ltd and The Angel's Share/Neil Wilson Publishing, Ltd.
    • 2009
    Authors: Carolyn Smagalski, Ian Buxton, Beaumont, Broom, Hansell, Johnson, MacLean, Offringa, Pacult, Protz, Saunders, Seidl, Smith

    Leading international writers on beer and whisky have joined together to celebrate the enduring legacy of celebrated drinks writer Michael Jackson (65), who died in August 2007 having suffered from Parkinson s disease for over a decade. They have each donated a piece of new writing to Beer Hunter, Whisky Chaser, launched on March 27, 2009 to mark Jackson's birth date. All proceeds from the publication, which was supported by The Glenlivet, will be donated to the Parkinson's Disease Society of the UK. First published, 2009; Digital Edition 2011
