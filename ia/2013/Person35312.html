Diane S Turner's Summary

My passion for holistic health and alternative medicine let me to start my business, Healthy Alternatives, in 1987. As a Certified Nutrition Advisor I help women who are struggling with peri-menopause, menopause and beyond. I help take the guesswork out of supplementation and share my approach that includes a hormone balancing plant-based diet.

I offer in-home cooking lessons and I am available for speaking if you are in the Cleveland area.

I have an ongoing training and business development program for women who want to work in the wellness industry and be in business for themselves. I have a team of fabulous women, all over the U.S, who are passionate about Holistic Health & want the freedom and flexibility of working for themselves.

Healthy Alternatives offers a unique line of organic herbs and vitamins, nutrition enriched skin care and green cleaning & laundry products from our product partner; Shaklee Corp. Shaklee is an International Wellness Company that has been GREEN since 1956. They are the first corporation in the world to become Climate Neutral (2000) and the #1 Natural Nutrition Company in the U.S.

You will find recipes and information on Women's Health at my facebook page which you can find through my website. www.dtHealthyAlternatives.com

Vegan & Alternative diets, Whole Foods Cooking,
