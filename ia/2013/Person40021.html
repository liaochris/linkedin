Erico Fileno's Summary

Since 1995, I have been designing applications software, appliances, devices, websites, products and services that are used by millions of people every day around the world. I have been working with user research, design thinking, UX strategy, service design and interaction design for years.

I have hundreds projects delivered for clients such as: Nokia, Google, HP, Diebold, Samsung, Positivo Computers, Vivo, Claro, Oi, GVT, TIM, HSBC, Parana Bank, Itaú Unibanco, Caixa, Bradesco, Bolívar Insurance, Urmet Daruma, Sagem Orga, Volvo, Renault, Volkswagem, GM, Electrolux, Whirlpool. Perfipar, BS Colway, O Boticário, Carvajal Group, America Latina Logistics, Petrobras, Federal Government and others.

Specialties:
• Development of new products and services (through design thinking and interaction design method)
• Strong experience with usability, information architecture and prototyping
• Qualitative user research methods and techniques (design research and ethnographic)
• Strong visual design background
• Design team management that work with software, computers, mobiles, services and interactive interfaces
• Experience in implementation of innovation processes
• International speaker, instructor of design thinking, service design and interaction design since 2007
