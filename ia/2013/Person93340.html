Michele Clark, CFP®, CRPC®'s Summary

I started in the financial services industry in 1991 and have a background in banking, brokerage, and insurance. I am a CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER ™ practitioner, a NAPFA Registered Financial Advisor, and a member of the Garrett Planning Network.

Clark Hourly Financial Planning, LLC is a Registered Investment Advisory firm, registered with the state of Missouri. There are no minimum asset or income requirements. And no long term contracts.

Advice is provided on a fee-only, hourly, as-needed basis, much like an accountant who bills by the hour, which allows for a lot of flexibility. It allows me to work with you on a personal one-on-one level to create a truly customized plan for you and your unique situation.

Financial planning advice includes; budget and cash flow management, retirement planning, asset allocation, investment selection, college planning, lifetime income planning, and other topics depending upon each client's specific situation.
