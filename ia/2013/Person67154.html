john thompson-mills' Summary

I am passionate about the media and always looking for new challenges.

I have more than 25 years media experience.

Working as presenter, producer, reporter, writer and public speaker, I have has spent most of my career at the Adelaide ABC.

I'm currently producing on 891, my second stint at Collinswood, after I left the organisation in 2009. This year I'm producing Drive with Michael Smyth.

2009 saw me complete my multi-media focussed Journalism degree and work as a freelancer.

Throughout 2010, I produced the “KG & The General” internet TV sports show before spending most of 2011 writing and editing for the Southaustralia.com (domestic & international) websites.

I have also written for nationally distributed magazines and newspapers, MC’d at Womadelaide and for the past four years provided live race coverage for the Tour Down Under website.

I have a long association with the TDU as a reporter and have won several awards for my coverage of the race.

Still on cycling, I also write a weekly blog on www.theRoar.com.au

I have high-end audio-production skills, producing feature series such as Riding High-Celebrating 10 years of the Tour Down Under & four World Cup football preview series (1994-2006).

I've also planned and produced numerous outside broadcasts as well as operating and maintaining the critically important Radio Master Control Operations Centre at the ABC in Adelaide.

I also spent 14 years as the SA Coordinator of Triple J, the ABC’s national youth network.

At Triple J I was a current affairs reporter, producer of a pioneering dance music program, presenter of arts and music programs, and recorded numerous Adelaide bands.

I've also worked as a night-club DJ and still ocassionally play at parties and functions.

Highlights as a public speaker and MC include the SATIC E-conference (2012), Ian Rush gala dinner (June 2009) Womadelaide (since the late 90’s) and a tribute brunch to World and Olympic cycling champion Anna Meares (June 2007)

Specialties

writing, journalism, general and sports broadcasting, journalism, video & audio production, multimedia content delivery.
