Jennifer Stavros' Summary

Part creative, part corporate machine, Jennifer Stavros combines a unique splash of paint to black and white worlds in order to maximize and exceed your company's expectations.

Her work has a wide range of diversity from corporate structured email campaigns for financial services to SEO to journalistic coverage of entertainment media to video game dialogue.

Jennifer resides in a unique position at the pulse of the entertainment, artistic and corporate conjunctions. She is an early adopter with a passion for technology, entertainment, social advocacy, and art. Her everyday experiences living amongst the largest artist colony in the country are constantly teaching her more and more about the importance of community building, strengthening, and the power of an individual's story to better assist with marketing in ways conventional techniques simply cannot compete with.

Jennifer's goals are her passions. She wants nothing more than to collaborate and assist you to illuminate your company with the color (as well as black and white) that it deserves.
