Dr. Jennifer Howard's Summary

Jennifer Howard, PhD, is the author of "Your Ultimate Life Plan: How to Deeply Transform Your Everyday Experience and Create Changes That Last." The book has won 11 awards, including a 2013 Gold Nautilus Award, two USA Best Book Awards, and a Silver Benjamin Franklin Award. (www.YourUltimateLifePlan.com). The distillation of her 20-plus years of experience as a licensed psychotherapist, life & business coach, professional speaker, energy healer and spiritual teacher, this "workshop in a book" is the missing "how to" for moving past your problems and stepping boldly into your next, deeper level of happiness, wholeness, transformation and success. Why settle for average when the potential to live an amazing life is inside you, right now?

A recognized thought leader on psychology and spirituality, Dr. Howard is a Huffington Post blogger and has appeared as an expert on numerous national network television and radio shows. On her popular weekly radio show, "A Conscious Life," she has inspiring, informative and fun conversations with such distinguished guests as Sharon Salzberg, Gay Hendricks, Marci Shimoff, Tara Brach, Bruce Lipton, Sonia Choquette, Dr. Bernie Siegel, Lama Surya Das, Rabbi Rami Shapiro, and others.

Dedicated to helping people, Dr. Howard teaches the art of conscious living—being more awake, aware, and alive in every moment. She’s a former faculty member of the graduate studies program, A Society of Souls: The School for Nondual Healing & Awakening, and taught meditation classes for many years at Marble Collegiate Church, in NYC.

Dr. Jennifer, as she's known in social media, was named one of the TOP 25 Celebrity Doctors on Twitter and "The Funniest Shrink on Twitter." She's gained a reputation on Facebook for her compassion, as well as her wit. Her blog at www.DrJenniferHoward.com is regularly visited by many of her over 105,000 Twitter followers and 8,500 Facebook friends & fans. On twitter she’s @DrJennifer and on Facebook she's DrJenniferFanPage.
