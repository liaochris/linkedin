Mary Margaret Dick's Summary

I have had the privilege to write for employers, clients and numerous individuals in my 25 years of experience as a professional writer. I have been told that I am organized, versatile and a prolific writer. I am an innovative problem solver who thinks creatively and remains calm under pressure.

My goal is to communicate and enhance your value in the marketplace. I first listen to client needs, then determine the scope of work. I adhere to quotes and write well-crafted content. My work is clear and concise, and I meet deadlines.
