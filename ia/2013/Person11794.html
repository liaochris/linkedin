Barb Tobias' Summary

Speaker, Author and America’s “Thrift Talk” Diva

My name is Barb Tobias, better known as America’s “Thrift Talk” Diva. I live in Evergreen, Colorado where I work to reach millions of women…and men…across the globe through my speaking gigs, website (www.ThriftTalkDiva.com) and my radio shows, The Women's Radio Network with Barb Tobias and The "Trash Talk" Show (www.TheTrashTalkShow.com).

My mission is to impact the lives of women and men to reach their potential by finding their magic...their core essence.
I'm also the Thrift Diva believing that anyone can look fabulous while being frugal and investing your savings to build personal wealth. You can … if you know the secrets to thrifty living. Join our Diva Club and download the Diva Secrets for FREE.

Catch me on the Everyday Show ... http://bit.ly/dsheSn

Speaker Bio: Barb Tobias, mistress of the stage, is an author, professional speaker, inspiring coach and witty entertainer who renovates lives, homes and wardrobes by sticking her curious little nose into other people’s “thrifty business.”

After a lifetime of turning trash into treasure, Barb shares her secrets for finding deals, repurposing before tossing and reconstructing the tattered in her new release "Tossed & Found." Her tell-all book is not only a journey of personal transformation, but teaches a newly frugal nation how to purchase, purge and profit from thrift. All of this is woven together to create a wonderful story that applauds the emergence of a woman's warrior spirit.

This radio and television personality delivers her topics in a style that is vivid, humorous and compelling. She captivates her audiences as she walks them through the pitfalls and pinnacles of her life ... humorous, gently poignant and often brimming with rough and tumbled sarcasm. Her storiy is ripe with honesty, empowering her readers and giving them not only hope, but the strength to stand again, and again.
