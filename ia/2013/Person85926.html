Marie Thacker's Summary

I am a passionate and focused Experience Design Director with an extensive background in UX and Visual Design. My focus is on creating stunning and highly usable mobile experiences.

I have a proven track record of successfully translating product visions and goals into exceptionally effective user-centric designs.

My experience across the entire project life cycle has given a thorough and intricate knowledge of product strategy, UX & UI design, branding and development.
