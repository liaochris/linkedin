Aundrea Young's Summary

As President and CEO of Competitive Choice, Aundrea Young, has developed CCI into an innovative and competitive specialty chemical and integrated supplier.

As a native Houstonian, she received her undergraduate degree in Communications from Texas Southern University and an Executive MBA from University of Houston's Bauer College of Business.

With an extensive career in industrial sales, along with her energy, organizational skills and forward thinking approach to all projects makes Competitive Choice the perfect outlet for her training, talent and business acumen.

In 2011, Mrs. Young was recognized by Valerie Jarrett (advisor to President Obama) as a Houston Success story. She was selected by the NASA JSC office to represent their efforts in working with small businesses.

Competitive Choice, Inc. is an environmentally-friendly, specialty maintenance chemical and industrial supply company. By offering simple solutions, CCI is able to provide the industrial world an opportunity to 'get it clean and to keep it green'.

As the President and CEO of CCI, Aundrea Young oversees and manages all business development, human resources and daily corporate functions. She is involved in all aspects of the company from working closely with clients, project team partners, safety and internal staff meetings.
