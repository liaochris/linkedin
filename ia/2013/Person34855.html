Devin James' Projects

  • Youth Tobacco Prevention Campaign

    • November 2011 to February 2012
    Team Members: Devin James, Sam Trivedi, Monifa Charles

    Osceola County Health Department in Kissimmee, FL was in need of a series of Public Service Announcements for a TV and Cinema advertising campaign to support their Students Working Against Tobacco (SWAT) program. The health department wanted some creative concepts that would appeal to and incorporate actual students.

    SWAT is Florida's statewide youth organization working to mobilize, educate and equip Florida youth to revolt against and de-glamorize Big Tobacco. The goals of SWAT are to prevent and reduce tobacco use among youth and to protect youth from secondhand smoke. Youth have been the target of this program because 89% of adult daily smokers began smoking before the age of 18.

    After meeting with the program director and doing an analysis of tobacco ads and tobacco free ads running in the State of Florida, the Devin James Group (DJG) creative team devised a strategic plan to capture and appeal to local youth with a campaign that used real students in the program as brand ambassadors as well as tapped several students to co-produce it.

  • Dr. Benjamin L Hooks Inaugural 5K Awareness Campaign

    • February 2011 to May 2011
    Team Members: Devin James, Jodie Vance, Sam Trivedi, Mekel James

    Situation Overview:
    The Dr. Benjamin L Hooks Job Corp Center retained the Devin James Group to develop and execute a brand/public awareness campaign to launch the Dr. Benjamin Hooks "Inaugural Memorial" 5K event. The event in honor of the late civil rights icon had a dual purpose to increase the awareness of Dr. Hooks' efforts to eliminate remaining racial, economic, and other disparities in America by bringing the nation together to live up to its founding ideals of liberty and equality. Starting in his hometown and Dr. Hooks final resting place, Memphis, TN the event and its' message transcended the geographic boundaries of the city limits through various online and offline communication channels.

    Approach: HOW WE EXPANDED THE MESSAGE
    From the brand slogan created out of one of Dr. Hooks' famous sayings, the theme entitled "Your Turn Now" was launched with marketing initiatives and tactics to reinforce the importance of Dr. Hooks' place in Civil Rights/Black History. The concept was to demonstrate the link between the success stories of individuals/leaders today and the stance of Dr. Hooks' and others during the civil rights movements while simultaneously delivering a platform that increased brand visibility to position the Dr. Hooks name and legacy as a "household name" in various communities that today were unfamiliar with him and his accomplishments.

    Outcome: THE RUN / WALK – EVENT DAY
    It's Your Turn Now began with a ceremony at the National Civil Rights Museum lawn where the City of Memphis Mayor AC Wharton, State Representative Barbara Cooper and other elected officials delivered the opening ceremony. Over 300 Participants ran or walked in honor and memory of Dr. Benjamin Hooks' Legacy and service. Participants wore "Ben Hooks/Sponsor" branded shirts to signify their commitment and support as they completed the 3.1 mile (5K) through downtown Memphis starting and finishing the race at the National Civil Rights Museum.

  • Rebranding of Mid-South's Largest Home Health Agency

    • May 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Devin James, Sam Trivedi

    Situation Overview:
    In May of 2011, North Delta Hospice and Palliative Services of Mississippi and Arkansas and Americare Home Health of Tennessee, two of the Mid-South’s largest healthcare agencies merged and retained the Devin James Group (DJG) to create their brand. The technique chosen by the DJG team to accomplish the assignment was to develop monolithic brand architecture where the Masterbrand, "North Delta", dominated all products or services without losing the Brand Equity of "Americare" that had already developed in surrounding markets.

    Approach and Outcome:
    In the new monolithic brand, North Delta brand would become the “parent company” and its identity would become dominant. The goal here was to develop a new and differentiating brand image that illustrated their market dominance, while staying true to the brands Tone of Voice and Personality. After enhancing the overall visual communications, the new image fostered more professionalism, seriousness and warmth that made North Delta more memorable to current and potential customers, suppliers, partners, patients, the media and the community at large resulting in:

    a 30% increase in patient referrals
    -a 75% increase in patient and family satisfaction scores
    -greater exposure to social workers and case managers
    -improved physician access to patient charts and nursing’s plan of care orders

  • Redesigned Website of DC's Largest & Only Accredited Day Spa

    • May 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Devin James, Sam Trivedi

    Situation Overview:
    Blu Water Day Spa had a website that was difficult to navigate, contained script and text errors on every page, no SEO, poor download time and didn't positively reflect the BWDS experience.

    Approach/Features of the Site:
    BWDS retained the Devin James Group to design and launch their new website. The new site was designed to showcase more of the aesthetic appeal guest see when they "walk through the door" and enter the Blu Water Day Spa experience in person. By capturing the experience visually with vivid photography and a more user friendly web interface, social media integration, a web commercial and a more "fluid" design; visitors are now more engaged when they visit the site.

    One added feature is that visitors can sign up for the BW's newsletter or set up an appointment to take advantage of the specials featured on the site. In terms of services, a much more visual experience shows prospective guest who the BWDS team is and what they offer; while simultaneously giving them tools to stay in touch and find out what is going on making the site a constant stream of 2-way communication.

    Photo Galleries:
    The site features a header photo gallery on the top of each page that displays the services available at the facility to capture the actual experience. In addition to the header gallery, the site features a gallery that allows visitors to see real guest/patient before-and-after photos of certain procedures allowing guest considering treatments to "see for themselves" the results they could expect. This dually highlights the dramatic improvements from minimally invasive treatments.

    Online Commercial:
    This interactive tool features a full tour of the facility with music to present all that BWDS has to offer its guests.

    Situation Overview:
    All Natural Pet Delivery is an online retailer for premium "all-natural" pet products based in Houston, TX. Considering this business was a start-up, the Devin James Group limited paid advertising and relied on public relations to carry the promotion of the Houston Grand Opening.

Simultaneously, our graphics and animation teams designed the characters, motion and objects based on the concepts and approved script (Visual storyboard) and presented a final presentation
