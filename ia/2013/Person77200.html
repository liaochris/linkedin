Kristopher Blanchard, Ph.D.'s Summary

My professional experience has primarily been in the area of strategic planning and training. Currently I am involved in training the next generation of business leaders. To accomplish this task my organization is very involved in thinking strategically because we need to anticipate what skills our student's (customers) will need to be successful in approximately five to ten years.

My long-term goal is to become a university president. Other than that I would also like to become an author and conference speaker.
