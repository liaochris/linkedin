    At the Information Commons students have access to quiet study spaces, computers, printers, and various media equipment that is also available for check-out.The Information Commons is staffed with librarians, learning support faculty, tutors, and technology support specialists. A shared resource between USF Sarasota-Manatee and New College of Florida, the Cook Library is located one-half mile south of the university. In addition, students have access to over 700,000 on-line resources through the USF System virtual library.
