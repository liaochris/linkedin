Al Rotches' Summary

I specialize in the development of banner ad campaigns for maximum click-through and response. Let me be the art director, designer, and animator who can optimize your text to work best for the digital advertising arena. Whether you need production, consulting or full campaigns, I'm ready with bells on. I have done hundreds of banner campaigns over the past decade for some of the world's largest companies and agencies.

I've been designing digital advertising since 1997 and can foresee the pitfalls of any campaign. I can work directly with you to get beautiful, eye-catching, and effective banner and mobile ad campaigns completed at specification and on time.

Clients include: Barack Obama 2008 and 2012, Toshiba, Sony, Voxel, Harper's Magazine, Bully Pulpit Media, Honda, Bausch and Lomb, Harry Winston, WebMD, and the other sweet, sweet clients you'll find at http://aldesigns.com
