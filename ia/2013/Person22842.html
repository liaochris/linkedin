Chilla Goncz's Publications

  • Use of blogs by pharmacists.

    • Am J Health Syst Pharm
    • December 1, 2010
    Authors: Chilla Goncz, Kevin Clauson, Justin Elkins

    Summary: the characteristics of pharmacist blogs were examined and analyzed. Commonalities were identified and discussed. Most discussed pharmacotherapy. Positive language was generally used to describe the profession, other healthcare professionals, and patients whereas negative language was predominantly used to describe patients and other healthcare professionals.

  • A systematic evaluation of paediatric medicines information content in clinical decision support tools on smartphones and mobile devices.

    • Inform Prim Care
    • March 1, 2011
    Authors: Chilla Goncz, Kevin Clauson, Sandra Benavides, Hyla Polen

    Summary: Clinical decision support tools (CDSTs), which are increasingly available on mobile devices (e.g. smartphones), can provide healthcare providers with convenient access to paediatric medicines information at point of care. However, to date no systematic evaluation of the content in these CDSTs has been conducted. Three paediatric-specific databases and six general databases were evaluated. Overall, general medicines information CDSTs performed better than their pediatric-specific counterparts in both scope and completeness. Results of this study may help guide CDST selection on mobile devices by healthcare professionals whose patient populations include paediatrics.
