Wendelyn Demoss' Summary

It has been my pleasure to work as a speech-language pathologist to serve children with communication deficits and their families, as well as providing professional training at the university level, through the First Years, Professional Development Through Distance Education program, and through formal mentoring to prepare professionals for LSLS AV and AVed certification with the AG Bell Academy for Listening and Spoken Language. My experiences guiding and coaching families on the journey toward competency in listening and spoken language continue to influence my practice.

I have been a practicing speech-language pathologist for 25 years and as a certified Listening and Spoken Language Specialist, I have developed an expertise in LSLS practice with successful outcomes of my patients and their families.

I have a passionate desire to extend that knowledge to professionals who want to achieve the same successful outcomes in order to affect the spoken language outcomes of children with hearing loss across the world.

I offer a sequential and purposeful process for developing your AVT/AVed skills via the internet so that you can hold on to more of your hard-earned dollars and limit the high cost of travel.

I provide regularly scheduled and ongoing encouragement and teaching through a variety of communications to meet your needs including: private one to one Skype feedback sessions, individual consultation calls, group Q & A phone calls, monthly customized newsletters for therapy ideas, and a private learning community study groups.

Specialties

Professional Mentoring/Coaching, Cochlear Implant and Hearing Aid (re) Habilitation-Pediatrics, Pediatric/School-Age Language Assessment and Intervention, Project Development & Coordination
