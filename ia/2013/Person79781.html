Lee Richards' Summary

After a career change from the dark side of gambling I am now an English teacher working with 11-18 year old students. In the last couple of years I successfully completed degree level courses with the Open University and a PGCE with the Institute of Education. I have just started my first full year of teaching at Chessington Community College.

Looking at my earlier career, through bwin Games and Ongame I experienced most customer facing aspects of the Online Poker business. Starting in the Customer Services Group in early 2004 I progressed to Support Supervisor and then to Support Analyst and Project Manager. In mid-2005 I joined a small team charged with developing HoldemPoker.com as a new real money poker site. HoldemPoker.com was developed very quickly and picked up an eGaming Review award in it's first six months. I developed and ran amongst other things a full site redesign, the online and live event promotions, the loyalty program, the online shop and SEO strategy.

With restructuring after the SafePort act my work extended to include all the bwin poker labels, including PokerRoom.com, EuroPoker.com and bwin Poker. I was then in the summer of 2007 appointed as the lead Product Manager and then Product Marketing Manager for the PokerRoom.com brand until September 2008.

Until June 2010 I was accountable for the web based poker experience at bwin.com which includes leading a team tasked with the development of the web, content and community products available for poker players on bwin. My last position within bwin was as Team Leader for Poker Product Marketing.

Specialties: English teaching (Secondary - England), Branding, Promotion and Event Development & Management (live and online), Web based Content Management, Web based Poker products - marketing, front end interfaces and back end systems, Loyalty Promotion, Community Management, Product Management, Web flow design, Communication and CRM, Prioritisation.
