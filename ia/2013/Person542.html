Aaron Kirkpatrick, CISSP's Summary

Information Systems, Compliance, and Business Versatilist with eight years experience in various roles, readily absorbing new information and eager to share through teaching.

Many years of my experience have been in the data center industry throughout the Midwest, from data center infrastructure to leading the enterprise's compliance initiatives. Financial institution security and compliance with the many regulatory bodies is my current main focus.

Audit and Compliance standards have garnered my focus, including HIPAA-HITECH, PCI-DSS v2, SOC (1, 2, 3), FFIEC, and the NIST 800-53 set (FISMA, FedRAMP, ...)

Within compliance, Risk Management (Disaster Recovery and Business Continuity), Incident Management, Asset Management (CMDB), and Information Systems Management along with the Security Triad (Availability, Integrity, Confidentiality) knowledge and implementation experience have been critical.

Teaching Security at the local Community College brings out my ability to create, lead, and present lectures. Bringing the classroom into a smaller setting instead of hiding behind a podium or utilizing a slideshow is a major initiative of mine. The in-class projector is only on for leading labs when necessary, or for sharing the latest Security news.

Specialties: Teaching, Information Security Management, Risk Management, Incident Management, Social Networking, Interfacing with Customers, Written Communication, Hardware and Software Troubleshooting and Maintenance, SAS 70/SSAE 16 Auditing, Information System Compliance and Governance, Business Continuity Planning and Training, Disaster Recovery Planning and Training, Physical Data Center Security Controls, Information Security Controls
