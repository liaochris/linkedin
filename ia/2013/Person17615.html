Brianna Flint's Summary

In 1996 I started studying how our mental and emotional thoughts affect our physical body, in correlation to our health. I embraced the importance of treating the whole person using the power of the mind-body connection and its ability to control our health.

I have incorporated these teachings along with my gift of intuitive creative energy into the seven years of my massage practice. In my massage, I incorporate a wide variety of techniques to optimize the body’s natural ability to heal.

The goal of my work is to initiate rejuvenation of the mind, body and spirit to bring your body into a peaceful state that continues long after your massage session.

I use discretion and a skilled touch to treat specific soft and connective tissue. I assist your body in returning to a balanced state.

Specialties

reducing stress, aide in relaxation, increasing flexibility, improving muscle function, improving muscle tone, renewing energy, improving circulation, enhancing immune system
