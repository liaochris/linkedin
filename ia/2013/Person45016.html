Keith Fletcher's Summary

As an Atlanta-based creative marketing communications professional, I have over 25 years of agency, corporate and entrepreneurial experience. My firm, Fletch Creative, specializes in connecting people, places and things through a broad set of experiences. We work to “master the message” at the intersection of Business, People and Technology. We provide expert design strategy, consultation and tactics to Fortune 500 companies and mid-sized businesses and funded entrepreneurial startups. From brand consulting to campaign development and execution, we can manage niche components or bring all the moving parts of a communications plan to fruition. Primary services include:

• Brand Awareness and Development
• Integrated Campaign Concept Development
• Mobile App Development for Android, iPhone, iPad, and Windows 8
• Online and Offline Ideation
• Print and Guerrilla Marketing
• Digital Advertising

Specialties: Atlanta Healthcare Marketing
Fletch Creative specializes in healthcare marketing for companies in Atlanta and all over the U.S. We deliver superior integrated marketing campaigns and brand identity solutions for a variety of healthcare organizations. Current and previous healthcare client experience includes work for Kimberly-Clark Healthcare, PerSe Technologies, Bako Pathology, Roche, AstraZeneca, Bard Medical, Merial and Alpharma.

Simplificationism
Fletch Creative helps our clients release their inner genius through Simplificationism [sim-pluh-fahy-key-shuh'n-iz-uhm]: eliminating the unnecessary so the necessary may speak. Simplificationism is more than an approach or methodology: it’s a results-oriented way of thinking that provides pure, uncluttered marketing solutions that convey the passion behind your business and help people understand your brand through emotional connections. It allows us to achieve results for our clients by assessing the emotions of people, the needs of business and the technology available to deliver messages. We drive our clients to seek a pure and uncluttered solution that helps them convey the passion of their business and think differently about their brand.

Partnership Opportunities
In addition to direct client work, we frequently partner with consultants, independent strategy companies and marketing firms to provide creative with discriminating taste for their clients. Fletch Creative can be a direct full service marketing firm or a natural extension of your company.
