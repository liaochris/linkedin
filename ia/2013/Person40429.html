Eleonora Rosati's Summary

Currently a research associate at the University of Cambridge, I am an Italian-qualified lawyer (avvocato) with a few years’ experience in the field of intellectual property (IP), having worked, among other things, in the IP departments of Bird&Bird LLP in Milan and London.

I hold two law degrees summa cum laude from the University of Florence, an LLM in commercial law (IP, international IP, competition law, EU law) from the University of Cambridge, and a PhD in EU copyright law from the European University Institute, Florence.

I have written quite extensively in the field of copyright and am a member of the editorial board of the Journal of Intellectual Property Law & Practice.

An IP enthusiast, I am also a regular contributor to highly-reputed The 1709 Blog and IPKat weblogs, for which I report and comment on IP-related news from all over the world.
