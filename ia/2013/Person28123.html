Christopher Skeete, B.A.'s Summary

Strategic, politically attuned, results oriented thinker with superior communication skills. I build, nurture and sustain relationships & partnerships that seed future growth. I deliver results passionately and consistently. I lead by example and I am able to manage numerous high profile deliverables simultaneously and see them through seamlessly.

In 2011, I was named as 1 of 5 finalist for the DUNAMIS award for top executive or entrepreneur <35.
