Kevin G Smith's Summary

There are many things I do and have done over the lifetime of my career; the one I enjoy most is story telling. To tell a good business story requires to understand the components - Mission, Vision, Goals, Finances and Timing - these are my strongest traits.

Tell me what you want to do and I will develop the plan to do it. Show me what you did, and I will eventually reveal how you did it. Explain to me that you missed a goal and I will show you why...

I have experience of coaching people at differing levels, internally as a team leader and externally as a business coach. Using skills developed in sales for lead generation and closing. Finance to understand balance sheet, cash flow, margins through using spreadsheets. Strategic planning to set new goals for business and product development (particularly for polymer and catalysis research, consulting, plastics and resins). People skills to help establish leadership.
