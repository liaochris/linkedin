Bernie Saunders' Summary

Bernie is an awakener and a visual storyteller. As a professional photographer, internationally recognized author and speaker, skilled life coach and organizational facilitator, with over thirty-five years experience working in the human development field, Bernie believes that life is truly a work of art.

Bernie’s most profound learning's have come from receiving his first camera from his father when he was in junior high, playing in the woods as a child, planting rice in the Philippines, working with young adult drug addicts, raising two exciting sons, and guiding people to be servant leaders. Nature continues to provide Bernie endless sources of vitality as he enters his sixth decade of life.

Bernie is the co-author of the internationally acclaimed book, Ten Steps to a Learning Organization, and the author of the personal enrichment program, Boundless Renewal: Embrace the Power of Reflection. His latest publication, The Grace of Ordinary Days: Celebrating Life's Journey, is an award winning inspirational book he co-authored with his mother that last four years of your life. He is the co-founder, along with his artist wife Constance Judith Saunders, of the Center for Living Art, which provides talks, workshops, retreats, and life coaching that invites people to bring alive their natural creative and artistic brilliance.
