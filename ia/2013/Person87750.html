Martin Padget's Summary

Talented and degreed mechanical engineer with over fourteen years of hands-on research, development and design experience as well as a couple years in project leadership roles.

Wide ranging engineering and design know-how including prototyping, mechanisms and machine design, enclosure design, plastics, metal working and machining, exotic and biocompatible materials, assembly, inspection, tooling, instrumentation, and CAD/CAM.

Worked on seven products from inception to market as well as on several others at various stages in the development process.

Award winning inventor/designer that has contributed to each of my employers’ body of intellectual property.

Experienced in working with both large and small companies as well as start-ups.

Knowledgeable in managing vendor relationships, working with surgeons, participating in and leading cross functional teams, and operating in ISO and FDA regulated environments.

Experienced in working with issues that truly affect the customer’s experience including ergonomics, industrial design, and physical/electronic interface design.
