Jett Butler's Summary

Jett is a multi-disciplinary designer, entrepreneur, 2012 Austin Under 40 finalist, Pecha Kucha presenter, panelist & speaker for the AIA, Ad Fed & AIGA, Adjunct Lecturer in the Design Division at The University of Texas and Founder & Creative Director of FÖDA Studio.

RECOGNITION—
Recognized by HOW Magazine as one of 11 creative agencies in the country for a Merit award for “a body of work”, Jett has led FÖDA Studio to 15 AIGA awards, Gold and Bronze Addy™ awards, shares a Silver Telly™ in broadcast media, a Visual Merchandising & Store Design award and an AIA Honor Award.

He is also a 6-time award winner in the Ken Roberts Memorial Delineation Competition; Best In Show, twice taking the Beck Prize in Digital & Hybrid Media, holding a seat on the jury in 2007.

METHODOLOGY—
Jett has developed an effective methodology for working with micro-brands and name brands alike, allowing FÖDA to routinely move their clients to higher price points, shift profit margins, raise venture capital or reduce overhead.

PHILANTHROPY—
Has helped to raise over $500,000 for various charities including: Sustainable Food Center, The Texas Foundation for the Arts, Cystic Fibrosis Foundation, the Health Alliance for Austin Musicians and Candlelight Ranch.

ACADEMIA—
Invited 2012 to serve as Adjunct Lecturer, University of Texas, Design Division.

Invited to critique/workshop/lecture at University of Texas School or Architecture, Hochschule Rosenheim (Germany), University of Idaho, Texas Tech University, Drury University, University of Miami-Ohio and others...
