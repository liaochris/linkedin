Jose Manuel Pérez Marzabal's Summary

J.M. Pérez Marzabal, LL.M. (Münster), European Ph.D. Cand. (Barcelona).
Abogado, Member of the Bar of Barcelona (Spain)
External Professor of BES La Salle, Barcelona (Spain), also Lecturer at Universitat Oberta de Catalunya. Formerly, Lecturer, ESADE.

Jose Manuel Pérez Marzabal is a technology lawyer, currently based in Barcelona where he leads his own legal consultancy firm, MTN Projects. Jose Manuel advises and provides professional services to Internet startups, media companies, global corporations, and IT consulting firms.

He has over 15 years experience of advising clients on all aspects of technology, multimedia and electronic communications legal issues and has led many multi-jurisdictional information governance and data protection compliance projects. Particular experience in advising in relation to high technology matters, including strategic decisions and technological solutions. He was Invited Expert to the Dot Eu Legal Working Group and he has been also invited to join the Revista de Contratación Electrónica.

He has been passionately involved with information society since the 90s. During his professional experience, while working in London, Madrid and Barcelona, he oversaw and participated in the development of several Internet Startups and Business Ventures. By sector, his clients are active in the following markets: ecommerce; media and entertainment; software and developers; publishing; high-tech and Internet Service Providers; and e-learning.

Specialties

Electronic platforms, e-Business, Web 2.0, social media, Information Technology Law, Internet Law, Competition and Intellectual Property Law, Electronic Communications Law.
