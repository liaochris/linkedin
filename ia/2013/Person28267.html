Tami Baribeau's Summary

I am currently the Director of Community at The Playforge, managing all things player-focused for the organization including customer service and community management. Prior to this, I served as the Studio Director, leading the team creating exciting mobile games for iOS. I live and breathe the social & mobile game industry, with several years of experience working for some of the hottest companies like Playdom, Disney, ZipZapPlay and PopCap Games. Whether a casual game is on iOS, Android, Facebook, downloadable, or web, I've played it.

In my free time, I'm a devoted MMO and RPG gamer.

My experience includes:

* Game production, pipelining, cost analysis, and team leadership.
* Intimately familiar with Agile project management for game development
* iOS app submission
* Maintaining social media presence and designing social media strategy
* Managing and running alpha, beta, and live testing programs
* Writing and editing documentation
* Hiring and managing staff
* Working with CS tools and software implementation, writing all CS policies.
* Gatekeeping content release schedules, writing patch notes.
* Leading and executing on design plans, writing design documents
* Designing entire systems such as a badge/achievement system and seeing to completion.
* Managing team members
* Designing virtual economy and managing game tuning mechanics
* Metric evaluation for customer retention
* Writing all communication with customers including newsletters and announcements
* Planning and running an on-site fan get together including travel coordination
* Administrating survey software to gain valuable feedback from customers and stakeholders
* Writing for social game publications

Specialties: Forum moderation and administration, product management, casual games, MMOs, production, social games, facebook games, mobile games, design documentation, game balance, monetization, retention, virality, game production, project management,
