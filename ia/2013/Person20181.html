    We built UConnect: http://invis.io/JNCU6IC5

    UConnect helps high-school students who lack resources and are new to the college application process discover mentors and role models. A recent NYT education article ("Better Colleges Failing to Lure Talented Poor") noted that many high-achieving underprivileged students attend colleges that do not provide them the resources to excel. The article attributes this to a result of the lack of guidance and role models in their lives. Our application is designed to tackle this challenging issue.
