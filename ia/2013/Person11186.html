Andy Werner's Summary

Business Development Manager with more than 8 years of experience in the Latin American mobile industry. Joined Citi in 2012 to lead the Regional Mobile Product Management team for Latin American Transaction Services (CTS).

Passionate about high-tech adoption on a large scale, I have collaborated on projects including mobile marketing initiatives, digital music distribution and mobile application development & publishing.

Before joining Citi I led regional business development for Syniverse, a US-based mobile infrastructure company. Prior to that I was in charge of mobile & digital music in South Cone first at Warner Music Group and then Sony Music.

I secured my bachelor's degree in Business Management at Centro de Estudios Macroeconómicos (CEMA) University in Buenos Aires and am an active member of the Mobile Marketing Association. In my free time I enjoy playing soccer with friends, writing in my blog, reading as much as possible and a good bottle of Malbec.
