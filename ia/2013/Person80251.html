Leo Smits' Summary

Leo means business
I specialize in formulating the central idea for the communication of a brand, product, service or organization. On that basis, I give a communication advice (including communication plans, proposals and reports) and I invent (individually or in a team) the key concept that the brand, service or product of a company or organization pays the best. In this process, I make use of a well thought mix of on- and offline tools, with an emphasis on social media, for optimal results.
