Andrew John Harrison's Experience

Co-founder & Fund Manager

Aurume Ventures

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Venture Capital & Private Equity industry

2007 – Present (6 years)

Private Equity Fund and Investment Advisory focused on high-growth projects managed by new-paradigm leaders and employing Conscious Capital to provide optimum returns for all Stakeholders.

* Early and Expansion Stages.
* Debt & Equity.

I established this company to manage our personal investment portfolio which is known as the Founders Fund. We also offer financing to early and expansion stage companies as well as advisory/consulting services to support high-growth projects to structure their companies, financial planning, write business plans, conduct presentations, manage the company/shareholder relationships, business development etc.

As Fund Manager, I oversee all consulting projects and have final approval when the company makes investments.

CEO & Co-founder

Goldzone Leadership Center

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Investment Management industry

2000 – Present (13 years)

I formed this company to make our Leadership Products and Services available to more people internationally. One of the secrets to sustainable change is to create an environment that is conducive to change and high-performance. After working with a lot of world-class hotels and professional event organizers we envisioned a working environment that was like high-tech spa-office.

In 2004, we partnered with a local Singapore company to built and test our prototype Leadership Center, refined systems, developed manuals, marketing programs, sales processes, human resource systems etc.

We build and develop our own Leadership Centers as well as develop partnerships in local markets to license our turn-key business systems.

To date we have produced over 400 seminars and worked with more than 40,000 individual and corporate clients.

A full-service Leadership Center offers: Conference Center, Board Rooms/Meeting Rooms, Spa/ Wellness Center, Vitality Butler, Café, Concierge, Serviced Offices, VIP Suites, Personal Chauffeur, Consulting, Training and Coaching services.

As the co-founder and CEO, I lead the team to articulate and implement the vision, design innovative marketing, communication and delivery systems, recruit and train partners and guest presenters as well as design and conduct certification programs for our partners, coaches, presenters and consultants.

CEO & Chief Software Architect

Violet Technology Pte. Ltd.

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Information Technology and Services industry

2007 – Present (6 years) www.violetenterprise.com

I started this project in 1994 with a single user database to manage client relationships, conduct direct marketing campaigns and track client purchases, communications, manage telemarketing campaigns etc. What started as a hobby, over the years turned into a multi-user system, then a full-scale enterprise solution. I did the initial software programming myself, then hired contract and full-time programmers to develop the system and its capability. We originally used the system for our own business, then sold it to other clients internationally as a bundled package.

In 2007 I transferred the software and the intellectual property rights into a new company to further develop the solution, to provide customization and support services as well as offer the product to other clients.

As Chief Software Architect, I oversee the look and feel of the product, design the client user experience and plan the future development strategy.

CEO & Co-founder

Goldzone Group

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Investment Management industry

2000 – Present (13 years)

Goldzone Group of companies are focused on high-growth projects managed by New-Paradigm Leaders and employing Conscious Capital to provide optimum returns for all Stakeholders.

I started the Goldzone Group of Companies circa 2000 in Hong Kong together with my co-founder. The Group began with a single company – Lighthouse Education Corporation – and has since evolved into a Group of sixteen closely held companies. We spent several years doing research, writing and developing leading-edge products and services for high net-worth individuals, fast-growing companies and new-paradigm leaders.

We believe that companies led by New-Paradigm Leaders are better managed, more innovative, less risky and deliver superior performance to all stakeholders including: shareholders, employees, clients, suppliers, the community and the environment.

The company has produced an extensive portfolio of products and services that have since formed the foundation of the Goldzone Group, which operates across a diverse range of industries including; Investment Management, Design, Marketing Services, Social Media, Enterprise Software, Professional Services, Publishing, Consulting and Education.

CEO & Co-founder

Goldzone Education

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Investment Management industry

2000 – Present (13 years)

The background research for this company began in 1988 when I attended my first professional sales development training-seminar. I was hooked on learning and began to attend as many seminars as I could. I found that the more I learned the better my professional results. After reading over 1,000 personal and professional development books and participating in 71 seminars ranging from a few hours to 12 months in length I formed my own ideas about education, consulting, coaching and life in general. I concluded that the most important aspect of life was Personal Leadership. Leadership became the focus of my research and development.

I started this company to organize our intellectual property, to create manuals, systems and educational technologies that allow more information to be transferred in less time – with a higher retention rate, to publish and license our intellectual property.

To date the company owns approximately 200 articles, bulletins and white papers, created proprietary Zone Teaching Technologies™, Zone Learning Technologies™, the Optima Zones of Life and Business®, Renaissance for Leaders® BLOG, Optima Seminars®, Optima Coaching®, as well as intellectual property rights to 97 seminars, training programs, certification programs and services ranging from 4 hours to 12 months in duration.

As co-founder and Executive Producer, I lead the team to design seminars and educational events, create multimedia and total immersion environments, produce videos and presentations, plan publications and write articles and white papers.

CEO & Shareholder

Excellerated Business Schools

1996 – 2001 (5 years)

Founded in 1978, The Excellerated Business Schools is an international organization with customers and graduates from more than 26 countries. Educational programs focus on teaching the generalized principles of Dr. R. Buckminster Fuller, socially conscious entrepreneurship, business and investing.

The most notable program is the week-long, condensed "Excellerated Business School for Entrepreneurs", which teaches: accounting, finance, sales, marketing, negotiation, raising capital, strategic planning, attracting and retaining the best talent etc.

The organization disbanded in 1994 when the partners split assets and went their separate ways. I joined one of the original partners as shareholder and CEO to reinvent the business, revitalize the curriculum and transform the sales/marketing. We resumed delivery of programs and services in 1996.

Under my leadership the organization expanded throughout Asia Pacific including translating the core curriculum into Chinese, training partners, program instructors and launching programs in China.

I left in 2001 to start a new organization focused on business and personal leadership.
