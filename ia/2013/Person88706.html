Matt Evans' Summary

Technical Solutions Architect with strong project management and entrepreneurial spirit. Able to lead, innovate and find creative solutions.

A good, strong leadership style, solid development, project management and team working skills with a proven track record in technical consultation for the web. I offer a unique skill set that is able to approach business needs and solutions with a clearly defined focus on the customer, end-user and delivery.

Flexible and adaptable, I thrive both leading and working in a team. My leadership style is energetic, inspiring, and aims to empower. I am interested in growing both myself and others around me, and enjoy seeing that growth in return benefit the company and client.

Key experience highlights include working for both inter-governmental (not for profit) and commercial sectors, simultaneously leading several out-sourced development teams in India (each of 6 or more persons) as well as providing technical leadership to globally distributed teams in locations as diverse as the US (Boston), Hong Kong, Switzerland (Luzern) and the UK (London).

Specialties: Project management (PRINCE2 certified), web and app development, information and technical design planning and architecture for the web, frameworks, usability, accessibility, interfacing, adaptive and control systems.
Web development: ASP.Net (MVC/Webforms), MsSQL, Classic ASP, J++, MySQL,PHP, XML/XSLT, Javascript, ActionScript, SalesForce APEX. Working in multi tier environments, distributed teams, code review etc.
