Marialyn Sardo, MD, FACS's Summary

Marialyn J. Sardo, MD, FACS is a native San Diegan. She was educated at San Diego State University and graduated Phi Beta Kappa. A 1982 graduate of the University of California, San Diego School of Medicine, Dr. Sardo trained in general surgery at University of California, Los Angeles and Cedars-Sinai Medical Center. She received her training in Plastic and Reconstructive surgery at Boston University Medical Center.

Dr Sardo established her practice in 1987. She is Board Certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery and a Fellow of the American College of Surgeons (FACS). She is a member of American Society of Plastic Surgeons. She holds staff privileges at Scripps Memorial Hospital in La Jolla, and Sharp (former Chief of Plastic Surgery)/Mary Birch Hospital.

Dr. Sardo has been featured on ABC and CBS news and in two episodes on The Learning Channel's reality series "A Personal Story". She volunteers her time at Rachel's Women's Center for Homeless Women.

Marialyn Sardo, MD, FACS and the Center For Beauty specialize in the best in cosmetic and plastic surgery and non-invasive procedures to enhance your natural beauty and rejuvenate your appearance. Dr Sardo and her caring staff welcome you to our beautiful La Jolla office. Dr. Sardo specializes in breast augmentation, breast lift, breast reduction, implant revision, abdominoplasty,"mommy makeovers", liposuction, face/neck lift, eyelid surgery, arm lift, and thigh lift. She also personally performs Chemical Peels, Botox, Juvederm, Artefill, and Sculptra
