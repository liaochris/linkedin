Carolyn Mumby's Summary

As a business owner/manager myself I thoroughly understand the frustrations employers encounter at work. When they have HR and employment law issues to deal with my job is to help them understand all their options and achieve the best outcome for their organisation as quickly as possible.

In the last 10 years I have enjoyed brainstorming with 100's of employers in the private and third sectors to find literally 1000's of creative and practical solutions to problems large and small. See our website at http://www.employmentlawfactory.co.uk

My company, ELE, also licenses its online HR system (for employers) to law firms. You may think this is strange at first, but it's a brilliant way for employment lawyers to add value for their clients without having to invest large sums in technology. More info at www.employmentlawportal.com

When I launched my own business I had to get over my "fear of selling" pretty quickly for obvious reasons. After that I got my fingers burned a couple of times with marketing consultancies and decided to learn about 'guerrilla marketing' methods. I have never regretted it for one moment. It was truly liberating and now I can 'just get on with marketing and selling my services instead of relying on others.

The 'Pain Free Marketing Report for Employment Lawyers' is based on those guerrilla marketing tactics and it is followed by a series of emails that will cut through the marketing hype and show you how to increase sales painlessly. Download your copy of this FREE report at http://www.pain-free-marketing-report.co.uk
