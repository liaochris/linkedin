Michelle Johnston's Summary

Michelle is a highly competent and innovative professional with extensive background in ideation, marketing, strategy, business operations, market research, product development, consumer insights, curriculum development, and corporate training. Her wide range of functional talents includes an amazingly keen eye for detail, excellent project management skills, strong business development and sales abilities, exceptional communication skills, and the capability to establish a comfortable rapport with all levels of an organization and its customers. She also excels at building strong business foundations for small to mid-sized companies, using her perceptive analytical abilities and problem solving skills to identify and implement operational processes and policies that help companies run faster, stronger, and more profitably.

Michelle enjoys offering her exceptional services in consumer-centric ideation, marketing strategy, focus group moderation, client team idea generation, and concept development as an Innovationist with NowVation, Inc.

At Word of Mouth: Writing, Editing, Resumes & Business Marketing, Michelle offers her writing, editing, and marketing services to individuals and companies of all sizes.

She is also pursuing a life's passion as writer and editor. Through her own brand Laurel House, she and her writing partner, Rosemary Weis, produce publications focused on improving the lives of children and families through positive interactions and the strengthening of all relationships.
