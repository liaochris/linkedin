Kara Edwards' Summary

With a career spanning more than a decade, Kara is no stranger to working behind the mic. You've heard her on the Cartoon Network voicing Goten and Videl in DragonBall Z as well as Murugu in Yu Yu Hakusho. On PBS as Razzles in the hit preschool series Raggs. Additional DVD series include Heaven's Lost Property, Mass Effect: Paragon Lost, Solty Rei, Darker Than Black, The Sacred Blacksmith, Last Exile, and more. Radio and television clients include Barbie, Sports Illustrated, Lord & Taylor, T-Mobile, Sony Music, Thomasville Furniture, Coca-Cola, BCBS, and hundreds more. Kara's voice has been featured in games from Dragonball Z to Squinkies to The Walking Dead. Additionally, Kara is proud to be the voice of Highlights Magazine, voicing monthly audio versions of Ask Arizona and a cover to cover audio edition of Highlights High Five that can be found on the company's website.

From radio and television to video games, animations, narrations, promo, message on hold, web videos, industrials, elearning, radio imaging, promotional videos and more...Kara's got you covered.

Available via ISDN, Source Connect, Skype, Phone, and FTP. For more info, check out karaedwards.com
