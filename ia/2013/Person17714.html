Brian Pettit's Summary

Brian Pettit is founder and Principal Facilitator of 3P Associates, a human resources consulting company. (People, Productivity, Passion, Profits). He has formed lasting alliances with: Choice Employee Benefits Group, Life Science Link, EKWard, Beaver Hollow Conference Center, and customizes learning opportunities in a wide range of curriculum including: leadership, communications, team building, quality and lean manufacturing for UB, Canisius, ECC and many for-profit companies. Brian brings 25 years of professional HR experience as a business partner in high tech aerospace (Moog), electronics (Motorola), and pharmaceuticals (Invitrogen), food processing, hospitals, financial services and service organizations. He has significant experience in business and succession planning.
