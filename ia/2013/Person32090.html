David Kent's Summary

The most compelling marketing copy is the kind written by a passionate, dynamic, enthusiastic, professional team player. I operate from that understanding to provide value-driven content that will pull your audience in, then call it to action. At the same time, I'll inform and excite the troops — people like to learn, but complicated subjects have to be easy to read and understand — and I make that happen.

Whether the results come through advertising, direct response, B2B, editorial content, e-commerce, email marketing, press releases, case studies, white papers, magazine articles, newsletters, collateral, speeches or audio/video scripts, it's up to you. My mission is to write what reaches and draws the buyers. Bottom line, that writing is about you and your audience and I never lose sight of that.
If you want copy that will hit its target every time, contact me today. To learn more, visit http://www.dkcmarketing.com or http://www.aerowriter.com
