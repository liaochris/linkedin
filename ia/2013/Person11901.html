Barry Cunningham's Summary

The Social Media Marketing Bug Bit Early! Why I Love Social Media :

Barry Cunningham has loved being involved in entertainment since he promoted his first concert with George Thorogood and the Destroyers back in his college days at the University of Delaware.

"Those days back at the Stone Balloon were memorable. Being a young 25 year old kid and having the Rolling Stones show up unannounced to play a gig at our club, pretty much set the hook in me."

Being featured in Variety Magazine as a football player turned young concert promoter set Cunningham off on a storied career of marketing. Both for the entertainers he promoted and the brands that wanted to be associated with them.

Over the last 25 years Cunningham has had the opportunity to work with some of the true giants in marketing and entertainment. From Walt Disney World to Universal Studios to Island Records to Universal Music Group, he has had the good fortune to enjoy success with the brightest minds in the business.

"What's funny is that with all of my experience, neither I nor any of my colleagues could foresee the crashing of the tech bubble." Cunningham adds, "I hitched my wagon to a few tech companies that paid me in stock options. Their IPO's were going to make us all instant millionaires. Things didn't quite work out that way".

Now operating a successful social media agency he sees the potential of new media and it's impact on everything from entertainment to Politics.

"I am in awe of social media. Never before could our marketing messages actually be involved in changing the world. If you are not actively engaging with your prospective customer, wherever they may be, you can be sure one or more of your competitors is"

That's enough reason to compel today's business leaders to understand the power of social media.
