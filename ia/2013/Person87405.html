Marsha Huber's Summary

I’m a creative director who loves a new business pitch, concepting creative using everything from mobile apps to - you name it. I’m a Clio winning conceptual strategist who has had hands-on experience working and pitching Fortune 500 accounts like: American Express, IBM, Disney, Ann Taylor, Aveda, Procter & Gamble, Pfizer and Clinique to small companies with great potential.

My specialties are creative direction, brainstorming, pitches & development, roller-blading through the streets of Chicago in rush hour traffic, networking, client meetings, strategic development, branding & activation, integrated marketing campaigns from mobile to print, integrated agency collaboration & planning,“wooing” clients with cost-effective solutions and team bonding.

I've won Clio's, Echo's, One Show, New York Art Directors & New York Art Festival to name a few.

I've worked in NYC, Boston, Dallas, Denver & Chicago. Career highlights: Dallas: Part of the Neiman Marcus team to develop a great in-house department. Pitched a 3 mil account against 3 huge agencies, won. Moved from my dining room table to my own design firm. Voted "Most Notable Woman in Dallas." Boston: Joined Bronner Slosberg Humphrey, stayed 13 years and after 3 new titles, was promoted again & transferred to open BSH/NYC Rock Center. NYC: BSH became Digitas. Joined Ogilvy as VP/CD. Asked to pitch business for a favorite Ogilvy ECD who was hired to start Meredith's marketing department. Pitched against 3 huge agencies and won Procter & Gamble, 4 beauty brands.

I'm a member of Art Director’s Club of NYC, Boston, Denver & Dallas, Ad Club of NYC & Boston, AIGA NYC, and CLIO Winners' Association International.

Judged: AD Club/Toronto, Hatch Awards. Speaker: AD Club/NYC & AD Club/Boston. Published: CA, Print & Trump's Global Branding & Networking: 2013 Who's Who Registry of Executives, Professionals and Entrepreneurs.

So far :-)
