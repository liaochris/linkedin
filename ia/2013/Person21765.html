Chad Stewart's Summary

Professional, experienced, & knowledgable, Chad Stewart has consistently ranked in the top 10% of all area Realtors for 6+ years.

Having personally closed over 200 real estate transactions he has the proven abilitiy to get the job done over & over. In this industry getting the job done means SOLD!

Continuing to grow his business year after year, Chad managed to sell & close more transactions in 2011, than in 2010, 2009, 2008, 2007, or 2006!

Chad graduated Florida Real Estate Institute in 2001 as a licensed salesperson, became full time in 2005, and in Chad 2007 received his Florida Real Estate Brokers license,, and also received his GRI designation in 2010.

His background includes six years of business administration, management, & marketing, including the position of general manager of Westfield Investment Group, a real estate development company. He has excellent knowledge & experience with the latest technology for business & real estate use. Chad Stewart is known for his dependability, promptness, and honesty, and is committed to treating his customers with that same level of service.
