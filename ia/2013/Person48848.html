Giuliano Galea's Summary

I'm a software engineer since 1999. I've spent the first years of my career as an actionscript engineer, developing dynamic interfaces and a couple of complex Flex applications. Then i moved to the server side of things, starting with PHP until i discovered Ruby and its marvelous framework Ruby on Rails. I spent 3 years working only with RoR , developing a custom cms tailored to support hotel/booking websites (some examples in the works section).
The third big shift in my career has been in late 2008 with the release of the iOS sdk: i fell in love with the iPhone and since then my work has been exclusively focused on iOS (and on a minor measure OSX).
I firmly believe in test driven development and in agile development, so i like to work in an environment that promotes those practices. I'm a strong advocate of writing clean, performant and reusable code. I like to mentor junior members of the team about best practices and good software pattern usage.

Specialties:
C/Objective-c
TDD with OCUnit/SenTestingKit
Good knowledge of the Apple Objective-c runtime
Good understanding of Objective-c best practices and patterns (Delegation, ClassClusters, Composition vs Inheritance, Category, Notifications etc etc) Cocoa Touch Frameworks
UIKit, Core Data, Foundation, Core Graphics, MapKit, Core Location, Core Foundation, Social Framework, UIAutomation and many others.
