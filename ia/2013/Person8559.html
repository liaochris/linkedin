Ann Ranlett's Summary

I'm an artist and illustrator specializing in pet/animal art and nature subjects rendered in scratchboard, watercolor, pencil and mixed media. I also accept commissions for pet portraits and enjoy working with devoted owners and their beloved animals. I'm a photographer too - looking for unique perspectives of the natural world.

I am also experienced with graphic, logo & web design.

Before becoming a full time artist, I worked for environmental consulting firms - using AutoCAD, GIS & graphics software to develop maps & graphics.

My art is available on-line through a number of sites. Visit PetArtGiftsByAnn.com for links to all sites. This album lists my currently available originals: http://www.facebook.com/media/set/?set=a.397935003574977.78004.105411602827320

Ann Ranlett's Art (Self-employed)

Self-Employed; Myself Only; Arts and Crafts industry

May 2003 – Present (10 years 3 months) Auburn CA Area

The bulk of my work is pet/animal portraits - mainly commission work, but I also have many non-commissioned originals available for sale. I also create nature illustrations and have done freelance work for Foothill Associates (my former employer). I also design logos and I work in web & graphic design.

I teach classes in scratchboard and watercolor on Yupo, find out more at: www.annran.com/pages/class_info.html

I'm a Signature Member (SSA) of the Int'l Society of Scratchboard Artists (ISSA - www.scratchboardsociety.com), a member of the Guild of Natural Science Illustrators (GNSI - www.gnsi.org) and a member of the Canine Art Guild (CAG - www.canineartguild.com).

Visit www.petportraitsbyann.com or www.annran.com to find out more and see plenty of samples of my art work.

Find original art and merchandise featuring my work at www.PetArtGiftsByAnn.com

Ann Ranlett's Projects

  • Appeals for Art

    • January 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Ann Ranlett, Gayle Rappaport -Weiland

    Launched in October 2012, Appeals for Art is our collaborative project to provide donation advice to artists and charities. We compiled this information from our personal experiences in receiving donation requests, donating art and working with charitable organizations.

    Our web site offers an outline of the content of our document along with other resources. The document itself is a PDF that can be downloaded.

    We also offer consulting services for artists and charities who would like more in-depth, customized advice. See our web site for details.
