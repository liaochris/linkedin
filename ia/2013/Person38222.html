Dr Paul Mullen's Publications

  • The Day I Hit A Home Run At Great American Ball Park

    • 2007
    Authors: Dr Paul Mullen

    Michael “Fuji” Powers always dreamed of joining the Cincinnati Reds on the field at Great American Ball Park, and now he stands at the plate ready to live his fantasy of hitting a home run. Filled with fun and engaging stories, this relatable coming-of-age story will show children the importance of believing in their dreams and never giving up hope.

  • The Day I Hit a Home Run at Principal Park

    • 2008
    Authors: Dr Paul Mullen

    The first local fictional baseball book written about the Iowa Cubs and a young boy from Iowa getting a chance to step onto the field of Principal Park, this story is filled with zany tales including fishing at Creeper’s Pond and playing pick-up games in a backyard “Field of Dreams”. These touching scenes will keep young readers on the edge of their seats and show them the importance of believing in dreams as Michael Powers stands at the plate, just one pitch away from baseball immortality.

  • The Last At-Bat at Yankee Stadium

    Authors: Dr Paul Mullen

    Capturing legendary Yankee Stadium in all its glory, this inspirational sports story follows the dream of a young boy from the Bronx to one day play baseball at the original stadium. The lore of yesteryear comes alive throughout the book, steeped in the rich history of the storied franchise, while the modern tale will restore children’s belief in the power of their dreams.

  • High Rolling at Cashman Field

    Authors: Dr Paul Mullen

    Based on the Las Vegas 51s minor league baseball team, this fun, suspenseful, and engaging book tells the dreams of a local boy who imagines one day stepping up to the plate at Cashman Field and hitting a home run. The children’s sports story also focuses on the world away from the baseball field, showing the life and young relationships of a 12-year-old boy living in North Las Vegas.

  • Standing O at Durham Bulls Athletic Park

    Authors: Dr Paul Mullen

    One of the most recognized baseball teams in the south are the Durham Bulls. Michael “Fuji” Powers stands at the plate just one pitch away from baseball’s immorality of hitting a home run at the Durham Bulls Athletic Park. A must-have read for sports fans of the game. Filled with zany tales like fishing at Creeper’s pond, playing pick-up games in a back-yard “Field of Dreams” and many, many other touching scenes from the South that will keep you reading at the edge of your seat.

  • Bases Loaded at Rangers Ballpark in Arlington

    • 2011
    Authors: Dr Paul Mullen

    Michael “Fuji” Powers stands at the plate in the driving rain with the count two-and -two. He’s one pitch away from living out his dream of hitting a home run at Rangers Ballpark. Along the way in becoming an Arlington legend, Fuji learns the importance of family, and there is one special girl he befriends who helps him gain the courage to start believing in himself. Filled with zany tales like fishing at Creeper’s pond, playing pick-up games on a back-yard “Field of Dreams” and many, many other heartfelt, gripping scenes that will keep you reading at the edge of your seat.

  • A Birthday Wish

    Authors: Dr Paul Mullen, Edited by - Emma Walton Hamilton

    A birthday wish is special. Today is Sousa’s birthday. Sousa wishes to see her imaginary friend and hopes her “Li’l Inkling” will make her fellow classmates’ wishes come true. The magical adventure begins when Sousa’s Li’l Inkling grants the children’s wishes.
