Freeman LaFleur's Summary

I am the definition of an entrepreneur.
I strive to create the impossible and shift industry foundations.
You won't find me attending "networking" events.
You will find me hustling 24/7 to create better workflows, new jobs, happy clients, and unique products.
I'm not perfect, I don't have a business degree (or any degree for that matter) but that's what drives me.
I find inspiration from life's beauty, challenges, and mystery.
I will probably never retire because I will never be satisfied.

What have I done?
I've managed to fail more times in my past than most but I refused to ever give up. I now own a business that has grown over 400% in less than a year while simultaneously satisfying over 2,000 digital media and mobile marketing clients. We have resellers and clients in over 15 countries that represent a wide variety of demographics and markets.

Why should you care?
Because I'm 24 years young and I've just gotten started. If I can do this, you can too...
