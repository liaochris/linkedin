David Gutelius' Summary

Recruiters and others, just a note: I generally do not respond to invites unless we've met in person. If you're interested in connecting, let's connect in person first.

Proven CEO with experience taking companies from research project to successful product and through the M&A process. I like starting companies and creating high-impact products that incorporate machine learning, graph analysis and behavioral science in new ways. Pet domains: healthcare, financial markets, cyber defense, defense and intelligence, and enterprise collaboration. I also frequently speak and write on these topics.

Advising and investing in companies that want to change the world for the better. I'm particularly interested in new ways to incubate high-value companies.
