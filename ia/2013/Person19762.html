John Davidson's Summary

One of the most challenging aspects of making carbon fibre is acquiring a good precursor. In an effort to overcome this problem I have now teamed up with European partners who have over 30 years experience in the PAN precursor industry. We can now offer full PAN precursor technology from polymerisation to spinning of a number of types of PAN precursors. Added to this is our ability to provide carbon fibre production technology from 1k to 320k tow counts and most importantly we have hands-on expertise in operating the equipment that we supply.
We have workshops in Italy and China with full design capability and all our equipment is pre-assembled to ensure design fidelity.
We are also able to advise on the recycling of carbon fibre composites.

Specialties

Design, Build and Commissioning of PAN Precursor and Carbon Fibre Production Plant.
