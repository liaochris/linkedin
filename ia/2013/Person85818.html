Maria P. Ferré's Summary

During a career in digital marketing spanning more than 13 years, I have had the opportunity to manage highly successful projects, campaigns and initiatives for clients in various industries. I specialize in gaining a full understanding of client requirements before tailoring appropriate digital media, social media, SEO and online marketing campaigns accordingly.

I am inspired most when given the opportunity to visualize and integrate brand identities into innovative digital and print marketing, helping clients to achieve greater impact in the most competitive and fast paced sectors. As the Digital Marketing and Design Specialist with Visual Grease since 2001, I have successfully served over 112 clients to date, creating 163+ signature campaigns, ads and websites that have consistently achieved results.

“Look at situations from all angles, and you will become more open” is a quote from the Dalai Lama which I apply both in my professional and personal lives. I wholeheartedly embrace change and am always receptive to new ideas and ways of thinking or working. This ensures that the campaigns I manage are fresh, innovative and exceed the expectations of my clients.

If your marketing operation could benefit from the expertise of a high level digital marketing and graphic designer, please email me at maria@visualgrease.com.
