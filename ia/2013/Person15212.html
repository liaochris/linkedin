Bob Bare's Summary

Bob Bare "Monetizing Your Mind"
"More Power to Publish" & "Idea to Bookshelf"
► www.MorePowertoPublish.com ◄

I am an author (and publisher) who is passionate about assisting others become authors and get their stories and expertise published. I especially love to assist people who have a story or book idea that will help make a positive impact on society and business.

A serial entrepreneur with several businesses, I specialize in working with those who are experts and authorities in their field.

While writing my book 'More Power!, an Entrepreneur's Roadmap to Success', I researched and became involved in the publishing industry. I am now the chairman of Blazon Press, an imprint of Morgan James Publishing, an innovative and rapidly growing New York publishing company.

Through SEGR Publishing, my team and I are able to assist authors from their initial book concept to publishing, whether traditional, POD, or the Morgan James hybrid model, and on to marketing their new work. I enjoy talking with those who are published authors as well as those investigating their "next step".

I am the chairman of SEGR Publishing, in Irving, the founder of BestSelling Experts, the Dallas Experts Network, Experts Weekly radio and the Monetize Your Mind TV show. Learn more about the More Power to Publish, Monetize Your Mind conferences at www.MorePowertoPublish.com.

Jay Conrad Levinson trained and certified me to be a Certified Master Trainer of Guerrilla Marketing, to traini small groups in Irving, Texas, as well as onsite for Associations and Corporate groups worldwide.

Prior to SEGR Publishing, I spent 40 years as an entrepreneur and hearing healthcare professional. See www.BobBare.com for details about the book More Power! and arranging for speaking, workshops, and interviews.
