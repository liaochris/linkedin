Jason Bargent's Summary

Jason is a seasoned Sales and Marketing Leader, with over 19 years of leadership roles driving Sales Teams across direct and indirect/channel sales as well as Marketing Leadership driving brand, demand generation, Multi-Channel Campaign Management, Traditional and Digital Marketing.

With a passion in aligning sales & marketing, driving effective messaging and conversations that align to how customers buy.

Jason leverages his deep strategic selling techniques from the certification and experience he has in Siebel TAS/ESP and CHAMP, Miller Heiman, IBM Signature Selling and Huthwaite SPIN. By leveraging these world-class leading sales methodologies, combined with modern effective marketing techniques Jason drives results through engagement at executive and stakeholder levels.

Jason is an energetic, self achiever who is inspired by working with like minded people who enjoy always putting the customer first and finding innovative solutions that add value. Jason truly believes we can always learn more about the psychology of buying habits and howe we align sales and marketing messages and techniques to drive success.

Specialties: Lead Generation and Revenue growth by understanding customer pain points, developing and executing strategic sales campaigns, building relationships, sales operations development, channel management, territory and account management, opportunity qualification, pipeline management and forecasting. Excellent people skills, with the ability to drive change, and build motivated, high performing teams.
