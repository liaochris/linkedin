Cynthia A. Brown, MBA, DTM's Summary

I am passionate about helping people best leverage their investments in online content and social media.

I have been an active user of social media platforms for over seven years. I have leveraged their functionality and managed content development to promote small businesses, a non-profit professional development organization (with ~150 chapters & 3000 members) and a non-profit ovarian cancer awareness organization.

As a public speaker, I have engaged teens and professionals on topics such as Career Choices, Leveraging LinkedIn to Network, Entrepreneurship, Marketing Your Organization, Goal Setting, Time Management, Leadership, and Social Media: the Differences Between Facebook, LinkedIn and Twitter.

I relish the opportunity to learn and share information on digital and social tools and resources. I am committed to expanding my knowledge base each and every day.

I thoroughly enjoy sharing and helping people. My desire is to encourage and guide people towards their place of excellence.

My passion for social media and online community plus my proficiency as online content manager for multiple social media platforms and foundation website led to a recent recognition as Volunteer of the Year by the Teal Tea Foundation
