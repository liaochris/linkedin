Adam Gubman's Summary

I've composed music for some of the hottest casual games on the market, creating content for mobile, FaceBook, etc. My triple-A level of branding and quality has far reaches, with my work appearing in over 500 Video Games (many award winning!), Disney, Telemundo, NBC, CBS, Univision, Square-Enix, Activision, Ubisoft, ABC, etc.

2010-2013 highlights: This year I helped to re-brand the audio of the NBC Today Show with a brand new theme song. In 2010, I provided a unique Princess-infused romantic score for the Royal Wedding on NBC, followed by an exciting suspenseful series of score for the Today Show 2012 election and 2012 Olympic Coverage on NBC.

In 2013 I released an album of horror music for movie trailers with Switch. Trailer Music, the first orchestral horror album mixed in 5.1. Recorded by 90 musicians, the album is full of sweeping, disturbing melodies and powerful angst-ridden back ends.

I also recently scored a series of advertising campaigns for Skechers brands, with commercials running nationally for 9 months and still going strong.

This year, I hope to work with more AAA developers, branding games with unique high quality scores, while always providing a special personal touch and level of care unmatched in the industry!

Specialties: Audio Branding, Compositon, Production and Project Management
