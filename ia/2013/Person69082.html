Josh Sin's Summary

The software industry has evolved. Users, now more than ever, have developed keen awareness for the quality of user experiences in products and services, and this awareness poses its influence from market reception to operational support costs and ultimately to brand valuation. Support the goals of your target users and customers, and the bottom-line will follow. Everything else becomes tactical in nature.

I am a User Experience professional with over a decade of experience creating market-differentiating user experiences for web-based enterprise-class software products.

My specializations include rapid, incremental UX improvements, guerrilla usability methods, end-user adoption analysis and strategies, Alan Cooper's Goal-Directed Design Methods, and seamlessly integrating UX design processes into Agile teams and organizations.
