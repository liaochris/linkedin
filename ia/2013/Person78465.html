Larry Madrid's Summary

Engineering and environmental specialist with over 29 years design and project management experience in a broad range of projects - industrial, commercial, private sector and public projects such as land development, mining, roadways, buildings, bridges, pipelines, etc. A key aspect of design is a balanced relationship between the natural environment and the built environment.

President of Madrid Engineering: Responsible for approximately 40 multidisciplined staff in Central and north Florida.

Owner of Greenovative Design & Engineering: identify solar PV and energy efficiency solutions

Managing Partner in Greenovative Homes, LLC: guide and direct the company to become a constructor of energy efficient housing; implement energy efficiency in warehouses and commercial applications; and provide specialty materials to improve energy efficiency, including GreenStar HyperInsulation Panels, low-e windows and doors, 'smart' thermostats, etc.

My goal is to enhance our reputation by successfully completing projects on time and in budget, to create jobs and improve success for families, and to honor my Lord and Savior Jesus Christ.

Specialties: - Diplomate of Geotechnical Engineering (D.GE) awarded in 2010.
- Geotechnical Engineering including shallow and deep foundation design, roadway and bridge foundations, slope stability for landfills and embankments
- Sinkhole evaluation and remediation design
- Sediment analysis and design (esp. lake bottom sediments)
- Consolidation analysis using conventional Terzaghi analysis and finite strain analysis.
- Mine waste characterization. Expert on phosphogypsum re-use as road base material; waste phosphatic clay, shale oil, vermiculite, limestone and sand mines.
- Environmental Site Assessment and Contamination Assessment

- Sustainability Champion - solar power; energy efficiency in building construction and operational systems; advocate for PACE (Property Assessed Clean Energy) program implementation.
