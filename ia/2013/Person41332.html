Emily West-Sadler's Summary

Emily West-Sadler is the Virtual Assistant Goddess.

An online project manager for Social Media, Presenter and Connector, Emily assists individuals and small businesses to delegate away low priority yet essential tasks of business growth so they can do what they do best and succeed.

Emily is the ideal support person for entrepreneurs interested in leveraging time by delegating tasks to enhance customer engagement.

Emily’s extensive list of services includes:
• creating Facebook pages and shops,
• building and customizing websites using WordPress,
• creating custom newsletter templates and email campaigns,
• starting and customizing YouTube, Linked In, and Twitter accounts (gaining client exposure),
• running webinars,
• coordinating people and getting services up and running for events.

Emily embodies high integrity with every client, delivering good value for money – including practical tips – and is dedicated to people succeeding so they can reach wider audiences.

Emily is easy to talk to and works to realistic time frames and achievable goals. Emily is above and beyond other virtual assistants on the web – her intrinsic understanding of life purpose and personal growth enables her to give YOU the understanding of you and your business as she genuinely cares about your growth.

“I believe everyone is a soul waking up,” said Emily, “We’re getting greater awareness, consciousness and evolving through love. Everything I do, is to free people up for their fullest expression through doing what they are most talented at. My mission is to raise global consciousness by using social media to connect people to good ideas that change them and the world. Life IS simple – and heaven can be experienced on Earth.”

Emily is exceptional when it comes to customer service, project management and connecting, as well as being a powerful listening ear and inspiration in the moments where entrepreneurs and people need it most. She is the go-to-goddess.
