John Simmons' Summary

Innovative title industry expert with 30+ years legal, title insurance, mortgage closing, and title/closing software development experience.

Special interest in “paperless processes” (including eClosing, eMortgage, eNotarization and eRecording) and fraud prevention.

Passionate about protecting the role of attorneys and independent title agents in the closing process.

Specialties

executive leadership, strategic planning, bridging the gap between business & technology, real estate law, title plant (public records) management, title searching & title examination, title commitment & policy production, settlement statement preparation (HUD-1 & RESPA), trust & escrow accounting practices, real estate & mortgage closing processes, deed & mortgage recording practices.
