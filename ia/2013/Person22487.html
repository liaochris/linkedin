Courtney Renn's Summary

Courtney Renn exceeds any expectation of what most people think a chef should be. Based in Atlanta, Chef Courtney is a talented and passionate culinary professional with a firm belief in customizing menus to suit each client’s palate, creatively using seasonal and organic ingredients. As a personal chef, Courtney has built an impressive resume in just a few short years, preparing a variety of diets and cuisines for actors Katherine Heigl and Ben Stiller, comedian Tony Rock, HGTV’s Design Wars, the Real Housewives of Atlanta, InStyle Magazine, The Vampire Diaries and Whitney Houston.

With the same charm and personality that made her a star in every position she held before launching her own business, Chef Courtney also hosts interactive chef demonstrations for individuals, special events and local green markets. By combining a wealth of culinary experience, passion for food, an innovative approach and vast food knowledge honed at the Art Institute of Atlanta – Chef Courtney successfully combines her roots in Southern cooking from North Carolina with her love of diverse flavor profiles.
