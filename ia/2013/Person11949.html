Barry Monies' Summary

Barry Moniês, an experienced business executive and visionary entrepreneur, founded Computronix in 1997 and currently serves as President & CEO. In the past, Mr. Moniês, on behalf of Computronix, was one of 100 companies from across the nation to be invited to a Congressional Conference on Private Technologies for Homeland Security. Mr. Moniês was also invited to accompany the Governor of CT to Europe on an international trade mission. In 2008, Computronix was included in the MSPmentor 100, a distinguished list of the world’s 100 most progressive managed service providers. Inclucing 2012, Mr Moniês has repeatedly been included in the MSPmentor 250, a list of the top 250 worldwide executives, entrepreneurs and experts shaping the global managed services market.

Computronix, located in the company owned building (The Stamford Technology Center in Stamford, CT), offers the most powerful and complete computer/network support services available today. Since 1997, Computronix has specialized in developing enterprise level technology for small to medium sized companies in the financial, legal and medical industries. We focus on making technology affordable and transparent so our clients can focus on their businesses!

Whether you have 2 users or 200 and need computer/server network setup, Exchange Server installation, wireless configuration, computer network tech support, IT outsourcing, network security consulting or worldwide communication system with remote login/VPN, we can provide fast, reliable, expert service at an effective price!

Thanks to our NetCare2 services, 70-90% of all your computer/network support needs are handled remotely and automatically for a price that is up to 60% cheaper then current support services. we provide ongoing remote maintenance and support to maximize the performance, reliability, and stability of your network without you or your staff spending your valuable time or the costs and overhead of hiring a full-time staff.

Specialties

We focus on designing, implementing and managing data networks and communication infrastructures. We specialize in Microsoft based server environments, multi-office networking, Exchange implementation, network security, data backup & recovery, remote services/VPN implementation, 24/7 monitoring and remote support/maintenance.
