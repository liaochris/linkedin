Adam Hansen's Summary

While being known as an early adopter of online tools, I have been actively using and exploring the internet for over 20 years. I helped create the Social Media Policy for the sixth largest county in Ohio, seat of the 50th largest metropolitan area in the United States. I received my bachelor's degree specializing in Public Relations from the University of Toledo. With an international perspective, I have trained people and consulted in social media strategies in the United States, Scotland and Germany.
