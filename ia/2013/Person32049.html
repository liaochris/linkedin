David Rosenthal's Summary

David J. Rosenthal is a passionate leader and expert at applying technology to solve business challenges. After a successful career in sales, IT, marketing, project and product management for firms such as Apple, AT&T / Ameritech, and CDI Corporation, David founded founded Atidan in 2005 to provide technology services and solutions to 'Global 2000' clients.

After spending the first three years of Atidan broadly focused on Comcast Corporation, David has worked for global enterprises such as Interpublic Group, Viacom, NBC, Hearst, Time Warner, Reed Elsevier, The New York Times, WPP, Stryker, Gannett/USA Today and Microsoft.

David's passions include understanding the latest innovations in mobile, cloud, analytics, search, collaboration, project management, unified communications and data management.

David's consultative, honest and advisory approach to the many small, medium and large customers of Atidan has won him accolades from both clients and solution partners.

Please visit www.atidan.com for additional information.
