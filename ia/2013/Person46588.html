Gabriel Fancher, MBA, MA's Summary

After working my way through college, I graduated from Western Kentucky University in 2000 with a major in Economics. I also have an MBA with an emphasis on Finance and an MA in Economics, both from MTSU.

I am securities and life insurance licensed in the state of Tennessee and have been working with money and managing various types of investments including stocks, options, futures, Forex, real estate and insurance since 2001.

Using my education and experience as a former college professor I teach people how to incorporate strategically and specially designed investments and life insurance policies into the clients overall portfolio.

Currently I am working on the designation of CIS, which stands for Certified Income Specialist, in other words I'm officially developing my expertise in providing strategies to help clients plan for a confident retirement - chiefly how to help maximize income while mitigating risk and taxes.

I am a creative and trusted financial advisor who believes in treating people with respect, honesty, and the highest level of integrity. My personal goal is to reach as many families as I can and share the concepts and strategies I have learned throughout the 10+ years of my career with them in order to enrich their lives.
