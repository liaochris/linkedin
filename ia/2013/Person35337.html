Dianne Metcalf, CPA's Summary

In May 2012 I closed Metcalf Resources and joined the Executive Search team of Lucas Group working in their Accounting & Finance niche' in Houston. I am excited to be part of a larger, highly reputable, well established, synergistic team! The Accounting & Finance team are 12 strong and growing and with these additional resources I am better able to serve my clients and candidates.

In 2002 I formed Metcalf Resources with the idea that I wanted to connect the right people to the right positions in quality companies. I am passionate about making the right connections that change peoples lives for the better! As a CPA I did not fall too far from the tree - and specialize in the recruitment and placement of Accounting, Finance, Audit and Tax professionals for direct hire positions and long-term part-time positions.

Prior to starting Metcalf Resources I worked in the staffing and recruiting industry for 7 years, exclusively in the niche area of Accounting, Finance,Tax and Audit. I started in business development, staffing/placement ending as a Branch Manager for a blended service firm (temp and direct hire) opening a branch office while continuing to manage the original office.

Before moving into staffing and recruiting I worked in the oil & gas industry for 9 years leaving in the capacity of Controller of a publicly traded exploration and production company.

Specialties: * Hiring the right person for your team - Coaching clients through the process for best fit and staff retention
* Hiring and retaining the X & Y generation - what every company needs to know
* Resume Writing - coach individuals to best present their skills, attributes and abilities for the career they are seeking.
* Interview Skills - coach individuals through the interview process, specifically how to address difficult or sensitive questions.
