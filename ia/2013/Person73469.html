Eoin Kearney's Summary

I graduated with a BA(Hons) in Communications and PR and have worked across many sectors such as Fashion, Technology, Education, Finance & the Entertainment Industry.

Currently based in NYC, I am working towards an MSc in International Marketing Practice supported by Bord Bia (Irish Food Board) and the Michael Smurfit Graduate School of Business UCD.

My experience covers many sectors in the areas of design, communications and event management. I am skilled in the creative elements of business and have expertise in brand identity and design.
