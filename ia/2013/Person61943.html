Jennifer Olbrich's Summary

Inspiration comes from the world at large. One should never be limited to a single form of expression. Cooking a satisfying meal; pairing it with the perfect wine; setting it on a thoughtfully crafted table; putting it all on with the perfect dinner music in the background... True beauty happens when separate ideas seamlessly come together.

From start to finish, in any medium you choose, my passion is providing full-service design that will help you reach your target audience, build your brand and meet your business goals. My aesthetic ranges from polished corporate to creatively inexplicable.

Contact me with your personal and business goals, so we can work together to tailor a solution specific to your needs. For my work samples, resume or references, please email jennifer@olbrichdesigns.com

Keep it Local --
We believe strongly in keeping it local. Not only does it help the local businesses and community grow and thrive, but it gives you the best and freshest pizza and salads. All our vegetables are locally source from such farms as Carpenter’s Farm, One Love Farm, Matunuck Veggie Farm and Schartner’s Farm to name a few. Our cheeses are from Narragansett Creamery and this year we are going to dabble in making our own. We also locally source most of our meats, although we do, from time to time, bring up some goodies from New York and abroad.
