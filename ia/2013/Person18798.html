Bud Cooke, CNE®, ABR®, GRI, SFR's Summary

In 2002 I had the opportunity to obtain my real estate license. Since that time, I've gained education taking as many relevant courses as possible, gaining various certifications including CNE (Certified Negotiations Expert), GRI (Graduate Realtor Institute), e-PRO® (Technology savvy Agent), SFR (Short Sales & Foreclosure Resource), ABR (Accredited Buyer's Representative) and Certified Fine Homes & Estate Specialist.

I've sold many homes and helped as many people as I could to achieve their home ownership goals. It is my goal to continue to provide the highest quality professional real estate services possible and to help as many people as possible enjoy the blessings of home ownership. It is my mission to provide the highest quality real estate services, always with the highest ethics and with a caring heart as well as to always be available and reachable for my customers. I will work hard and be honest while providing professional advice to everyone I come in contact with.

In 2009, my wife Kim Cooke joined me in my real estate business and has been a great blessing to me and many of our customers and clients. Kim's compassionate heart and sensitivity has blessed others and in turn our business. I'm very fortunate to have such a wonderful partner in this business. Call us at (727) 459-5460 or Kim at (727) 458-9604.
