Joseph Tartagni's Summary

Dr. Joseph Tartagni at Shoreline Dental Care of West Haven and Milford, CT provides cosmetic dentistry, sedation dentistry, dental implants, along with general and pediatric dentistry. Dr. Tartagni was one of the first in Connecticut to purchase and become certified in the use of the dental laser.

He is also one of the most well educated dentists in Connecticut with thousands of hours of advanced coursework and postgraduate education including courses on implantology and orthodontics, as well as cosmetic dentistry, sedation dentistry, and laser dentistry.

Specialties

Dental Implants, Porcelain Veneers, Cosmetic Dentistry, Sedation Dentistry, Laser Dentistry
