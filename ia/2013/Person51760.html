Haroon M. Ahmad's Summary

After a successful career at the executive level in healthcare management for over 25 years, I seized an opportunity to leave the corporate life and turn to my childhood passion of photography as a profession. I own a full service boutique photo studio in Zionsville, Indiana serving clients for ;

1. Corporate photography, executive portraits, product photography, interior and architectural details and photography for your company's annual report and marketing material.

2. Senior pictures, children & family portraits and weddings.

After completing course work in various aspects of photography at the renowned International Center of Photography (ICP) in New York City. I now own and operate a studio in Zionsville, Indiana since October of 2010.

I am a Certified Professional Photographer (CPP). Member of PPA, ASMP and PPI
