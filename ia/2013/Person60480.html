Jean Bedord's Summary

Jean discovered the online search and information retrieval industry when she was hired as a product manager at Dialog Information Services, one of the first commercial search engines. She now teaches her clients and students how to use web search engines to find information, structure their products and services, as well as improve revenue.

Specific experience:
• In-depth understanding of search and implementation strategies for open web and enterprise content. Content engineering experience utilizing XML markup and DTD schemas for electronic publishing and database development.
• Skilled in product / business research and competitive intelligence.
• Effective in teaching online courses in library and information science utilizing distance education learning management systems: Desire2Learn, ANGEL and Blackboard
• Expert at negotiating agreements and managing publisher/partner relationships in the search and online publishing industries.
• Experienced in working with legal counsel for licensing and third party agreements.
• Knowledgeable product manager in the online information industry. Experience in database development, product marketing, and publisher relations. Products included business, news, pharmaceutical, biomedical, and healthcare databases.
• Excellent writing and presentation skills. See www.bedord.com for complete list.

Specialties

• Writing for industry and trade publications
• Web-based and in-person instruction for workshops and college courses
• Speaker on search, Web 2.0 and library technologies
• Data quality analysis
• Publisher relations
• Product / Business Research and Competitive analysis
