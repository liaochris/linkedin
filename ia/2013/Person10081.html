M.J. Arul's Summary

Having worked in a few institutions of good-quality management education in India (IIM-A, IRMA and SPJIMR), I helped shape the quality of the programme at Presidency B-school, Bangalore.

Having been trained in FIRO, I intend to make the benefits available to willing seekers from any walk of life.

Specialties

I am a certified trainer of the FIRO-based <I>Human Element in Organisations</I>. For more of this, you may visit http://www.arulmj.net/firotrng.html.

I offer occasional programmes on: Manager Development, Team Building, Interpersonal Communication, Self Development.
