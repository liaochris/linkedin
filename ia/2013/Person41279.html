Emily Luke's Projects

  • getDowntown

    • September 2011 to December 2011
    Team Members: Emily Luke, Antoni Bernard, Kevin Hoxie, Molly E. Green

    My team redesigned getDowntown's commuter challenge website. getDowntown is a non-profit organization whose mission is to promote green commuting practices in Ann Arbor. The commuter challenge is an event put on by getDowntown during the month of May where the employees of local businesses are encouraged to use sustainable transportation for their commute to work through a friendly competition. Over the course of the project we utilized a variety of user experience design and research methods. Through our redesign we hoped to allow users to efficiently log their commutes, obtain important information and interact with other users of the site.
