Brian Lavery's Summary

I'm a true Seattle native, and third generation of living in the Capitol Hill neighborhood of Seattle. A graduate from WSU in Business/Marketing, obtained my real estate license in 1992, and my broker's license shortly thereafter. With over 20+ years of experience in selling properties in the greater Seattle area, educating my clients from my advanced designations plays an integral part of my business. Since buying or selling a property becomes a huge financial decision for my clients, I spend time focusing on their priorities and goals, the real estate process, and the myriad of details involved.

I have found the key to my success has been the quality of people that I represent, and their choice to refer me to others based upon the quality of my service.

Specialties: Residential homes/condominiums, and investment properties.
