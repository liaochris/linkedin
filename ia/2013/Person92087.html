Michael Benner's Summary

During my 30+ years as a broadcast journalist in Los Angeles, I became increasingly fascinated by the growing Personal Development and Relationship Management field. I began to understand how the biggest problems facing us at work and at home are rooted in confusion & anxiety about our personal identity and motivation.

What makes each of us genuinely unique individuals? Why do we think, feel, and act the way we do? How do we best manage ourselves and others?

I left broadcasting as a full-time profession in 1987 to launch Personal Development Strategies (PDS) and a program I call "Smarter than You Think," I counsel, train, and coach individuals, couples, and business-people with practical and portable skills in stress & emotional management, critical thinking, personal responsibility, character development, accelerated learning & continual improvement.

Specialties

Relationship Management includes self-confidence, self-motivation, leadership & teamwork (elan & esprit), empathy & sensitivity, conflict resolution, conceptual understanding and an inclusive & harmonious sense of success.

"In my experience, people can face almost any problem except the problems of people. They can work long hours, face declining business, face loss of jobs -- but not the problems of people."
— W. Edwards Deming (1900-93) Quality guru.
