James (Jim) McCormack's Summary

Sold over 350 investment properties, multifamily properties, single family homes, twins, row homes, townhomes and condos totaling over $96M in value. Specialist in short sales, pre-foreclosures, foreclosures and other distress sales.

Goal is to help financially distressed homeowners and real estate owners solve their real estate problems, to find viable real estate investments for investors and to find houses at below market prices for home buyers so they can gain instant equity. If you are looking for honest and ethical expertise please contact me. if you are looking fo a rah rah real estate is always a great deal, slick or pushy salesperson please contact someone else.

I enjoy increasing my real estate related expertise through continued experience, classes and self-taught education.

Specialties: Short sales, pre-foreclosures, foreclosures and other distress sales. Residential re-sales, new construction and investment properties (commercial and multifamily).
