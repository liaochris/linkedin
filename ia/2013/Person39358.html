Eric Wilson's Experience

VP of Product

Mural

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Information Technology and Services industry

January 2010 – August 2013 (3 years 8 months) Tucson, AZ

At Mural I played a leadership role in developing an outsourced, white-label customer onboarding program that reduced 90-day churn for our telecom clients by 70%. I was one of the first two people on the phones, working directly with customers, and helped grow the program to over 50 employees and a handful of clients. My major contribution at Mural was leading a lean development team to build the infrastructure, custom CRM, and workflows that enabled us to efficiently scale a world-class customer experience.

Founder

Student Experts

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Consumer Services industry

September 2007 – August 2013 (6 years) Tucson, AZ

Student Experts was founded as a nationwide marketplace for skilled student labor. In 2011 we re-focused as a content marketing company and now produce over 20 million words a year for 1,500+ small businesses looking to improve their search engine rankings. My primary contributions at Student Experts were developing and managing our internal project management and content delivery toolset as well as designing an interactive reporting dashboard that is used across the company.
