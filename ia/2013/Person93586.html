Michelle Shail, MSOD's Summary

To help organizations and individuals make sense of today’s explosion of information, shift to mobile devices, and rapid adoption of social tools, I:

- Partner with business leaders to identify, plan and execute talent management initiatives connected with their strategic goals.
- Design and deliver workshops to help leaders and professionals understand how their personality and leadership styles affect their performance.
- Consult with and coach individuals and organizations on how modern communication platforms, human behavior and our increasing connections are redefining business.

My goal to help organizations and individuals adapt to rapid change and the shifting complexities of society and business.
