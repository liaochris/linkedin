    • Adelaide
    • September 1996

    • October 30, 1997

Most organisations hire the wrong people and then train them the wrong way. With tips and advice anyone can follow, you CAN show your customers what real service is and earn profits you deserve.

Their hilarious mis-deeds lead them into trouble and eventually to an elusive man with a marvellous voice.
