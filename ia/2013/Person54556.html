Indian Biosciences and Research Institute's Summary

Indian Biosciences and Research Institute,located in NCR, Noida is an ISO 9001-2008 certified institute and registered under the Indian Education and Research Society. IBRI is an institute of its own kind as it is governed and managed by academician and professionals from different fields i.e Biotech, Pharmacy, IT, Clinical Trials etc. forming the backbone of the institute for its success.

Thrust areas of the institute are Project Training and courses in Bioinformatics, IT , Biotech, Pharmacy, Clinical Trials, Pharmacovigilance, Genetics, Statistics,Php/MySql and more....

We have established an unassailable reputation for our Project Training and Professional Certification Programs on the sheer virtue of our intensive focus on making our students “Industry-Ready”
