James A Hofmann, MBA, CRPC®*'s Summary

I am a Financial Advisor with Morgan Stanley Wealth Management. I hold a Series 7, Series 65 (Registered Investment Advisor) & 63, and am also licensed for Life & Health Insurance in New Jersey. I also completed my Series 31 for Managed Futures & Commodities.

I have earned the designation of Chartered Retirement Planning Counselor®*. This prestigious designation is awarded to financial professionals who successfully complete the CRPC® certification process, which is centered around a training program focused exclusively on retirement planning for individuals.

I focus on general financial planning with an emphase on retirement, education, and estate planning. I specialize in growing and protecting wealth through advanced portfolio construction.

I attended William Paterson University from which I hold both a Bachelors degree in Business Administration and a Masters of Business Administration in Finance. As a New Jersey native, I enjoy skiing, and boating, and currently reside in Byram with my wife Kimberley.
