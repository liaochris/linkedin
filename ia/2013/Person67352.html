Jonathan Adamich's Summary

Jonathan Adamich resides in San Francisco, moving from Seattle where he attended Cornish College of the Arts for his Bachelors in Classical Music Composition. A highly motivated orchestral composer who is able to provide thematic material with a deep fantasy undertone. Experience in writing, orchestrating, mixing, and mastering. Able to finish all tasks in the music department, from start to launch. Ambitious and looking forward to playing a key role in the development of video games.
