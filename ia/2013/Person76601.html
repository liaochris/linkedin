Korey Clements' Summary

I am an artist at heart and my goal is always to create something new and push the envelope of my skills, regardless of media. I began doing art when I was very young and have grown ever since. I started to study graphic design at Ball State University in 2006. I earned my BFA in Visual Communication in 2011. During which I dabbled in photography, painting, metal work, wood crafting, and many other fine arts.

I'm a tough working, easy going designer who always wants to learn something new. Highly proficient in Illustrator, Photoshop, and the rest of the Creative Suite. With being an artist at heart, I have a heavy background in many different fine arts. I constantly am improving my skills as a designer and artist on every project and problem I encounter.
