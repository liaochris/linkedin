Kirill Makharinsky's Summary

I help build elegant solutions to big problems using technology, data and product design.

Currently I'm building the technology and data platform to improve the efficiency of matching demand and supply in online travel - the largest e-commerce market. ** And hiring for head of online marketing, and head of product roles, from anywhere in the world.

Previously I built data-driven models and tools which measured the potential and progress of emerging technology - used by 30,000 startups and several leading blue-chip clients.

Before that I worked on product analytics at Slide (acquired by Google), and studied Mathematics at Oxford.
