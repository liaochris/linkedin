John Howman's Summary

I work with CEO's and executives to help them improve their organizations. Whether through my role as a Chairman at The Executive Committee, (TEC), or as a Trusted Advisor at Allied Consulting, my goal is always to help my clients and members develop the best organization possible. This happens by bringing the right people into the organization, getting them in the right roles and getting out of their way!

I am the founder and CEO of several companies. I work extensively with mid-sized, growing companies and non-profit organizations.

Specialties: COO, TEC, Vistage, Corporate Troubleshooting, Management Assessment, Employee identification and selection, Consulting, Business management, turnarounds, Chapter 128 advice, executive coaching, Information technology, E-Commerce, Interim CEO

John Howman's Experience

Trusted Advisor

Allied Consulting Group, Inc.

Public Company; 1-10 employees; Management Consulting industry

January 1995 – Present (18 years 8 months) Greater Milwaukee Area

Allied Consulting Group LOVES working with small and mid-sized companies. As a business founder (yes, I built it), my entire career has been devoted to the advancement of small and medium sized businesses.

Through my work with COSBE, MMAC, YPO, WPO, and TEC I have encountered nearly every kind of business opportunity and problem.

Allied Consulting Group is organized to help small & mid-sized organizations--for profit and non-profit. Increasing revenue and margins, reducing expenses, improving sales and marketing and effective use of technology are all in our wheelhouse. We can provide interim or part-time leadership and consulting for companies in need of a jump start, a turnaround, new management or other significant events.

I have a track record of fast turnarounds, successful software conversions, strategic and operational planning with a special emphasis on small to mid-sized companies.
