  • Virtual Leaders Born or Made?

    • Aurbach Publications, Taylor Francis Group
    • March 5, 2011
    Authors: Donna Dennis, Mary Key

    Distance matters. To manage distance the best leaders are especially vigilant in task-related competencies, while also managing relationships and technology. Increasing cohesion, trust and a sense of connectedness are critical drivers of success. This article provides leaders with the data to increase effectiveness. Specifically, virtual teams that have processes that increase the levels of mutual support, member effort, work coordination, balance of member contributions and task-related communications consistently outperformed other teams with lower levels (Siebdrat, Hoegl and Ernst, 2009).
