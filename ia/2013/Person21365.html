Cecilia Edwards' Experience

Associate Partner

Everest Group

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Management Consulting industry

2013 – Present (less than a year) Dallas

Everest Group is an advisor to business leaders on the next generation of global services with a worldwide reputation for helping Global 1000 firms dramatically improve their performance by optimizing their back- and middle-office business services. With a fact-based approach driving outcomes, Everest Group counsels organizations with complex challenges related to the use and delivery of global services in their pursuits to balance short-term needs with long-term goals.

Owner

The RobinsonEdwards Group

2006 – Present (7 years)

The RobinsonEdwards Group LLC is a Dallas-based management consulting firm that focuses on delivering strategic advisory and consulting services to middle market companies and the individuals and investment firms who own them.

Building upon over ten years of experience in strategy consulting across a wide range of industries with Fortune 500 companies, private equity firms, small businesses, non-profit organizations, churches, and foundations, The RobinsonEdwards Group brings a multi-disciplinary approach to creating long-term shareholder value and enabling innovation.

With an emphasis on translating the best and most recent thinking done for Fortune 500 companies, The RobinsonEdwards Group employs a powerful set of tools to deliver high impact results for middle market companies and maximizing the exit opportunity for their investors.

Chief Strategy Officer

Telligent

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Computer Software industry

2009 – 2012 (3 years)

Telligent powers social communities for more than 3,000 companies worldwide. World-class brands, including Dell, Microsoft, Electronic Arts and Reader's Digest, trust Telligent's enterprise-grade social community suite to connect and engage with customers, prospects, partners and employees.

Client services includes: strategic advisory services, software implementation and customization, and training with the goal of equipping our customers to run World Class online communities.

President and CEO

The Foundation for Community Empowerment

April 2002 – September 2006 (4 years 6 months)

As the Chief Operating Officer and then CEO of the Foundation for Community Empowerment, she developed one of the most effective capacity building programs in the country and training 150 non-profit executives; leading the developing and launch of regional and city-wide social indicators websites used for research on disparities within the Dallas area.

She established the foundation’s ability to lead system-wide change that led to improved coordination of early childhood education programs for low-income families in Dallas; a 10-fold increase in a two year period in the production of affordable housing units in the southern sector of Dallas, and the development of community level solutions to improved health outcomes. Her work at the foundation culminated in a $20M effort to establish a plan and support the Dallas Independent School District in a quest to become one of the best urban school districts in the country by 2010.

Manager (Principal)

The Boston Consulting Group

Partnership; 5001-10,000 employees; Management Consulting industry

1995 – 2001 (6 years)

During her nearly seven years at BCG, Cecilia provided general strategy and business consulting to Fortune 500 and other major corporations. Her projects spanned every BCG practice area and included: redesigning sales force for an aircraft parts distributor; performing strategic analysis for a top medical school; conducting competitive operations study for a U.S. automobile manufacturer; designing global export organization for a snack food company; designing performance metrics system and financial model for a top medical school; developing a strategy for inner city call centers; developing a feasibility study for high quality retail in an inner city area of South Dallas; conducting a workforce diversity best practices analysis for a leading financial services firm; and developing a business case for broadband access investments in inner cities; and creating a process for BCG Dallas community investment efforts.

Captain, Titan Satellite Launch Manager

U.S. Air Force

Government Agency; 10,001+ employees; Defense & Space industry

January 1989 – September 1993 (4 years 9 months)

Cecilia served as a program manager for the Titan Satellite Launch program where she directed the activities of three satellite programs launched on Titan II rockets and the developmental Taurus program. During her four and a half years of service, she supported the launch of five Titan II and Titan IV rockets.
