Bob Kittridge, CC CPBA's Summary

As an internationally recognized mentor, speaker, trainer and coach in the areas of leadership, personal development and professional coaching, I have helped many entry level and top management leaders as well as political leaders with leadership concepts and skills courses by transforming how leaders communicate and connect. I have a unique insight and understanding of how people react during critical times in their lives and what motivates and inspires them.

I am the co-founder of Kittridge Connection, Inc., a leadership and development consultant group that equips individuals, thought leaders and organizations with high performance solutions to ensure continuous improvement in personal growth and business results.

Following 35 years of success in sales, training and leadership roles in Emergency Services, I broadened my leadership training to include international audiences. Recently, corporate leaders are among the audience that have benefitted from the leadership concepts that I share. I have a unique ability to connect with audiences that generates excitement and enthusiasm for learning.

My skills have been developed through practical application rather than years of formal education. I have a philosophy of continual learning, personal growth, and leadership development which have surpassed the learning of my formal education.

In 2011, I aligned myself with an industry leader in personal growth and leadership development, John C Maxwell, and have shared the stage with John during a training session with John’s coaching team.

Specialties

Transformational Leadership, Change Managment, Personal Development, Professional Coaching, Emergency Management, Incident Response, and leadership
