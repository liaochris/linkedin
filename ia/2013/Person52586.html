Hellen McQuain's Summary

Hellen McQuain brings over 10 years of industry experience to the table. She is a strong sales executive with a long history of providing growth-oriented leadership and business development skills that shaped her ISO within the space prior to merging with Blue Square Resolutions,LLC based out of AZ.
She has been responsible for several portfolio acquisitions and now specializes within the lending and acquisition space for ISOs and ISA's.
Hellen also offers over 7 years within the Cash Advance Space and currently does business development with Strategic Funding Source as well as provide bankcard support/service, lending and syndication.

Prior to founding EPS, Hellen led sales teams with Registered ISO's in 2000 and sales with Electronic Data Resources. Hellen currently resides in Boca Raton, where she enjoys music, cooking and traveling.
