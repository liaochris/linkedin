Brynner Ferreira's Summary

Brynner Ferreira works with Internet for 11 years. He started as web designer and over time became a web developer. Self-taught in Computer Science, studied Advertising in college and created the Bryntec Internet Agency (2002) in Fortaleza, serving clients from Brazil and abroad. He collaborated with the development of projects using Free Software in the Government of the State of Ceará (2008). He founded the WUP (2010), a company that offers customized search services, selected as one of the best projects in some national and international startup competitions. His skills are since UI design, programming languages, database management, online marketing until product management.
