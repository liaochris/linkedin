Popbasic

November 2012 – Present (10 months) San Francisco Bay Area

Systems Architect

Kondoot

July 2011 – November 2012 (1 year 5 months) Brisbane Area, Australia

My role at Kondoot when I first joined the team was to scale the existing website from a single webserver into something that could scale dynamically based on load.

We chose Rightscale to quickly get an environment setup with a loadbalancer (ha proxy) and some app servers (nginx, ruby) on EC2.

As we grew and our architecture grew more complex, we decided to move critical pieces our our architecture from Rightscale to Chef and EC2 to Joyent. I began work on an instance provisioning system built specifically for Kondoot on Joyent. This new system it would allow us to boot new servers and add them them to the pool in under 1 minute. This was a critical requirement as we often experienced large amounts of unscheduled traffic.

Responsibilities:
- Implement/manage server and deployment automation.
- Figure out how everything should fit together
- Rails and Node.js development
