Jen Butler, M.Ed, BCC's Summary

Dental Executive Coaching:

I am a Master Certified Executive Coach that has worked with hundreds of Dental Professionals to achieve success through less stress and more fulfillment.

My Executive Coaching allows Dental Professionals to align who they are with what they do in a way that creates synergy within their practice and cohesiveness within their teams. This generates energy that attracts new patients and retains current ones.

Dental Consulting:

As a professionally-trained Dental Consultant I partner with Dental Professionals to develop a Cultural Strategy that is in alignment with their Business Plan.

By first creating One Practice with solid Vision, Mission, Purpose and Values statements, Dental Professionals will lead One Team to execute an effective, business plan that attracts Many Clients.

Training:

As a national trainer, I have 20 years of experience in the training industry, working with Dental Professionals to Fortune 100 executives to customer-facing employees.

My trainings are interactive, hands-on, deeply relevant and customized to the current needs of my audience to help them change behaviors to achieve their desired goals.
