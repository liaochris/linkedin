Larry Collett's Summary

I am the President & CEO of netGALAXY Studios, a mobile application development company headquartered in Charleston, SC. We specialize in mobile app development as well as web design and search engine optimization.

I previously worked for Media General and was the Regional Interactive Media Manager for both WCBD-TV 2 (NBC) in Charleston, SC and WSAV-TV 3 (NBC) in Savannah, GA. While with Media General, I managed the overall Interactive Media operations for both stations, including web and mobile activities. My primary focus was to increase audience reach through all media platforms.

Prior to my tenure at Media General, I helped launch Charleston.net for the Post and Courier and the Web Production Manager before stepping in to the role of Wifi Web Producer for the parent company, Evening Post Publishing Company.

Now my focus is growing netGALAXY Studios in to the mobile conglomerate that it is meant to be. With clients from the Southeastern Wildlife Expo to Auburn University and every business industry in between, we help increase audience reach with web, mobile, and SEO.
