Angeles Martinez de Carvajal's Summary

Whether you are looking to buy, invest, or sell, I’m ready to assist you by offering my personal and professional real estate experience, skills and knowledge to help you find the perfect spot to call home.

I’m the CEO and founder of AMCB Services Company LLC a Real Estate Investment Company.
I have lived, worked and invested in Real Estate in Spain, California, North Carolina and New York.

I formed AMCB NY Global when I joined Keller Williams, the Number One Real Estate Co. in the US to fulfill a passion for Real Estate.

If you are planning to buy in NY, please visit my site: www.manhattanrelo.com to search for properties, find useful information about schools, walk score of properties you like and more.

If you are planning to sell, you may want to know that as a Keller Williams Agent I'm connected to a nationwide network. This will help me market and sell your home to buyers relocating to NYC from other states and abroad in addition to local buyers.

Get to know my international profile and search for international properties. Please visit: www.amcbglobal.com

About me:

I was born and raised in Seville, Spain. My grandfather, Francisco Garcia, co-founded Persán in the 1950s, one of Spain’s leading detergent companies. My father, Luis Martinez de Carvajal, developed the first detergent in tablets, which is still in use today.

I worked for the Persán International Department and on the Board of Directors before moving on to start my own business in real estate in Madrid. I decided to take my business on an international level when I came with my family to the US in 2005. As a Keller Williams Global Property Specialist I can guide you through the International Real Estate scene anywhere in the world.
