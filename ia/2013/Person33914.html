Renato Delmanto's Summary

Renato Delmanto has over 20 years of journalism experience at some great Media Groups (AOL, Globo, Abril, Estado, Folha). Since 2005, he has specialized in Corporate Communications and Public Affairs, leading areas at CPFL, Máquina da Notícia (PR Consulting) and nowadays at Votorantim Group, the brazilian industrial conglomerate. Renato also worked at research company Ibope and holds an academic position at Faculdade Casper Libero, the Brazil's most traditional Journalism University.

Especializações: Strategic consulting in Corporate Communications and Public Affairs; Media Training; Crisis Management; Media Relations; Speaker; Internet Content; Writing, Editing and Editorial Planning.
