Joshua Wood's Projects

  • Sageworks Business Credit Report

    • January 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Joshua Wood

    Sageworks provides private-company financial information and develops financial analysis solutions. I was contracted in late 2012 to put a new face on their Business Credit Report product, which asses the credit risk of a private company using business and owner financial information. Using cutting edge technologies, I built a consumer-facing web application from the ground up, working closely with Sageworks engineering teams to integrate their proprietary technologies.

    Primary features include:

    * Pre-paid credit reports - The customer pays up-front for x number of reports, which they can generate at their leisure.
    * Sageworks Seal - An annually billed membership that includes certified verification of financial data, and an embeddable seal which displays their Business Credit Report score on their website.
    * Combined checkout process - Functions like a shopping cart, but allows the customer to purchase multiple product types (annual and one-off) simultaneously.
    * Back-end administration system

    I also consulted with Sageworks to develop a web service that delivers on-demand financial analysis to the consumer-facing application.

    SageworksCreditReport.com was launched January 2013.
