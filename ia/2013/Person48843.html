Gitta Sells & Associates' Summary

Member of the Keller Williams Luxury Real Estate division and a member of the Institute of Luxury Home Marketing. Certified REO agent operating in the greater Orlando area. I have a team of 13 team members serving all of the Greater Orlando market. A native of Germany and a 35 year resident in the US. She has 25 years experience in the Orlando Real Estate Market and is the Team Leader of her very successful real estate team. Gitta brings her whole family to the business, with her husband Ernst being the business manager and her 2 sons Patrick and Marc serving as successful buyer agents. Featured in the Billon Dollar Real Estate Agent Book, www.billiondollaragent.com Gitta has served many clients over the years and well completed over 1000 real estate transactions. Her motto for her team is to give 120%, to Assure total client satisfaction. Over the years Gitta has received many awards, from the Orlando Realtor Association and from Keller Williams the Triple Platinum awards and Triple Gold Awards for large volumes of sales. Gitta has many designations, from being a Certified Luxury Real Estate Marketing agent with a Guild Designation to the prestigious E-Pro to being certified in Loss Mitigation and Default School Certified. As Broker/ Agent she is a real professional you can depend on.

Specialties: Selling luxury homes and traditional homes. Representing buyers and sellers in a real estate transaction, her Team does it all. Also certified REO agent with years of experience in the REO business.
