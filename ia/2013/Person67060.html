John Skelly's Summary

I'm a Senior Digital Strategist and business development geek who understands all sides of the business equation. I help organizations to focus and define short term tactical plans for digital products, communications and marketing campaigns that reflect longer term objectives. My sweet spot is in digital product development and management, but I also love business development and making deals.

I've consulted with hundreds of companies, from start-ups to Fortune-500's, including Trader Joes, Honeywell, Evanta, Yakima, Made In Oregon, Crossfit, Marchex, The Nature Conservancy, Roku, The Young Presidents Organization and many others. Critical skills center in digital marketing, systems design, digital development team leadership, product management, and digital strategy.

I'm also happily married (to Kate) and father of 5 awesome kiddos (Lauren, Julian, Evening, Shannon and Birdy). Avid reader and serial entrepreneur.
