Bill Pringle's Summary

Mr. Pringle has over 40 years of full-time software experience, from programming to management, and over 30 years of part-time teaching experience. His programming experience ranges from operating system internals to applications programs, web applications, and user support. He has both Unix and Microsoft experience.

He has a great deal of experience with many aspects of software development, but themes that frequently recur are software tools, software methodologies, and what is now called data mining.

Most recently he has been concentrating on various aspects of web development. He has excellent technical and communication skills, plus the ability to learn quickly.

In addition to software, he has considerable teaching experience, with courses at both the undergraduate and graduate levels. He is very good at mentoring others.
