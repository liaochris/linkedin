Eileen Hull's Summary

Having owned and operated my own craft business for the past 26 years, I bring a wide range of skills to the arts and crafts industry. Developed and designed an innovative die line (ScoreBoards) currently licensed with Sizzix. Implemented a "blog hop" to market and showcase dies. I have written two books for a manufacturer and worked on product testing a new line of tools. Have demonstrated product at national trade shows, designed packaging, product sheets and sample designs for trade shows and web sites. Looking to work with new companies to spotlight their product.

Specialties

Three dimensional design, prototypes, able to take a concept from "outside the box" to finished product.
