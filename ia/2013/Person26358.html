Colin Walker's Summary

I am always looking for ways to create new business and connect companies and organizations from my home province, country and the globe to my adopted home of Japan.

Never before in our human history have we had the possibility to achieve so much. With todays technology and the ability for people to share resources, knowledge and work cooperatively towards improving the way we live we are now on the verge of incredible innovation. This will become even more evident over the coming years with our energy utilization, our local, national and global governance, as well as the way we remain connected to each other and recreate the traditional channels of media and distribution.

My ambition has always led me to follow my passions. Having graduated from the University of Calgary with a strong interest in hospitality and customer service, I soon arrived in Japan with an ambition to build tourism towards Edmonton, and Alberta. Starting as an English teacher, I have continually aimed to improve not only my teaching skills but also how to improve my personal life and to continue to optimize my professional skills. This carried me to great success through my service at a large corporation, my efforts as Chairman of two committees at the Tokai Japan Canada Society, and my ability to create two local businesses doing exports from Japan. With emphasis on the new technologies of social media, I aim to utilize the democratic processes of individual and community involvement to build networks that involve business creation, social improvement, and personal achievement.

My skills are social media marketing, event planning, project management, team management, best practices coach, negotiations, event programming, presentations, and efficient user of Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Hootsuite, LinkedIn, Twitter, Google+, Facebook, Wordpress, Blogging applications and Adobe Creative Suite
