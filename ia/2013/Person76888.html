Having previously worked at Warner Bros., Catchlight films and Bender/Helper Impact, I am savvy in entertainment industry news and publicity. Also familiar with the online entertainment space, I wrote for a few entertainment websites like GordonandtheWhale.com, TheAudioPerv.com, and RealityWanted.com and am now co-founder of a number of websites under Invasion Media, LLC.
