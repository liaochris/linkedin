Mr. Mom (CEO of CBUK, Ltd.)

ChicBaby.com

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Apparel & Fashion industry

June 2013 – Present (3 months) London, United Kingdom

ChicBaby is a family owned and operated company founded in 2009 by Ana Cláudia Huzenlaub. We specialize in the international sale and marketing of upscale clothing and products suitable for children aged newborn to seven years old, as well as moms-to-be. In 2013, the company reorganized as ChicBaby.com Holdings, Inc. and expanded from Brazil to England (ChicBaby.com UK, Ltd) to better serve customers in over 50 countries beginning in Q1 2014. Managing Director

Ashco Group, LLC

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Management Consulting industry

March 2004 – Present (9 years 6 months) Maringa, Brasil / Ashco.com

Ashco Group, LLC serves as the holding and operating company for Ash and Ana Cláudia Huzenlaub. Ashco Group was established in 2004. Ash and Ana Cláudia serve on the Board of Directors for ChicBaby.com Holdings, Inc. with subsidiaries in Brazil and the UK. Ashco Group owns the iOS app, "Badge" (utilized by companies and schools that deploy iPhones / iPads to their workforces and students).

Ashco Group has teamed with entrepreneurs, investment partnerships, corporate BOD’s, and/or restructuring groups that require additional experience in operating private, or public-to-private companies (turnaround initiatives); interim management; business plan & market development; sales leadership; and marketing / branding leadership that require a "roll up your sleeves" work ethic.

Ashco Group was previously a minority shareholder of EDIS company, Emergisoft, where Huzenlaub was implemented by senior private equity shareholders as turnaround Chairman & CEO (2002 - 2006); and concept development for Mexus Airlines. Between Q4 of 2005 and Q2 2009, Ashco Group was contracted to assist industry leaders in service awards (marketing and promotion at Diamond H); University Entrepreneurship Programs (TCU); and for two years, Whole Foods & national beverage division launch, growth, and expansion at Sweet Leaf Tea Company (now part of Nestle).

Vice President, Business Development, Western United States

Team Health

Public Company; 5001-10,000 employees; TMH; Hospital & Health Care industry

April 2009 – January 2013 (3 years 10 months) Greater Los Angeles Area

As Vice President of Business Development (March 2009-Dec 31, 2012), my role was to oversee and facilitate the growth and enhancement of TeamHealth in the Western United States (10 states).

With approx US $2.0 billion in revenue, TeamHealth's main business is providing outsourced physician management & staffing (Anesthesia, Emergency, Urgent Care, Hospitalist, On-Call Specialist) and administrative services to more than 550 hospitals and affiliated clinics in 46 states. TH manages everything from physician recruitment and medical training programs, to billing, payroll processing, and claims management. The company, taken public in Q4 2009, is majority owned by The Blackstone Group. Additionally, I searched for Mergers & Acquisitions opportunities in the Western United States identifying and building relationships with Anesthesia and non-subsidized Emergency Medicine practice groups.

In October 2012, in order to unite with my family in Brazil, I resigned in preparation for my relocation to Brazil in December 2012.
