Hunter Phoenix's Summary

Hunter Phoenix is a Wellness Expert & Coach and ￼nationally acclaimed speaker. She works with clients to help them strike the sweet spot between simple self care and accelerated growth.

Having spent years in the competitive entertainment industry working on camera as an actress & model, in sound studios recording voiceovers, and behind the scenes as an award winning writer, has given Hunter a diverse and varied background to draw on.

A natural born clairvoyant, Hunter is well versed in various healing modalities, including Polarity Therapy, Somato Respiratory Integration as well as Auric and Energy Balancing. She is exceptionally skilled in a number of metaphysical disciplines, but received the majority of her formalized coaching training at The Ford Institute for Integrative Coaching at JFK University in Berkeley, California.

An avid fitness enthusiast and former amateur body building competitor, Hunter still maintains a healthy workout schedule travelling between offices in Toronto, Vancouver and Los Angeles.

Hunter works in-person with select clients across North America and continues to work via phone and Skype with clients worldwide. Corporate engagements have included Chrysler Canada, GlaxoSmithKline, The American Beauty Association and Alterna Credit Union. She is a former chairperson with the Kitsilano Chamber of Commerce and maintains involvement in several professional organizations supporting entrepreneurs and women in business. She has also been closely involved in the Youth Mentoring Program with Covenant House.

Her first book “Perfect Lives and Other Fairy Tales” was just release in paperback January 2013 and is available on Amazon.com. Hunter works with groups and individual clients to strike a sweet spot between simple self-care and accelerated growth as well as creating a little marketing magic! You can contact her through HunterPhoenixCoaching.com

Hunter Phoenix's Publications

  • Perfect Lives and Other Fairy Tales

    • CreatPub Press
    • January 3, 2013
    Authors: Hunter Phoenix

    So what do you do when the pursuit of happiness is making you miserable? When the success you desire seems just out of reach? And the thought of more self-development seems exhausting? You take a practical approach to personal growth; one that is simple and clear and designed to produce big results, while at the same time reducing tension and relieving stress.

    Perfect Lives... and Other Fairy Tales doesn’t pretend that you can get it all done or even that you should. What it does do is give you a step-by-step plan to take back control of your life and your schedule, to set clear personal boundaries and to steadily move forward and gradually achieve your goals.

    Celebrated life and success coach Hunter Phoenix shows you how to master productivity while practicing extreme self-care. She helps you to reignite your passion and connect with your soul.

    Beyond simple motivation and self-help, the 52 simple steps can be used as a weekly, one year plan, or individually in any order you like. It’s a proven practical blueprint to help you create a life and business you truly love - even if it is a little imperfect.
