Science of Skill

2009 – Present (4 years)

Expertise: Skill development / Goal setting / Sport effectiveness

Getting stronger and faster is a science. There are physical training regimens that specify repetitions, weight, intervals, and more, and basically ensure results. You don't just go to the gym and lift whatever weights are in front of you - there is careful tracking of success and progress. To get the best results you hire a trainer.

What about technical skills? If you want to improve your skills in a sport do you just do whatever exercises are presented to you without having a way to measure skill and track progress? This is where I come in.
