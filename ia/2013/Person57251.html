James Trujillo's Summary

I've been passionate about writing and music for as long as I can remember, so it was only natural that my future career path would journey down these roads. Multi-tasking has always been a common occurrence with me, so I’ve been dedicating these efforts as a freelance journalist, QA tester, and studio musician. My most consistent line of work has been deep within the video game industry as a contributing writer for multiple online publications.
