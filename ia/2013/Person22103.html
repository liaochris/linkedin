Charles Daugherty's Summary

I have been working in the land planning, site engineering and general business development arena for over 22 years. My experience includes both the the large corporate environment as well as small business's. My work experience includes everything from large scale regional planning projects all the way down to construction detailing. I am fascinated by all aspects of the design process and take great joy in both the design and implementation process.

I am a puzzler and problem solver. I flourish in positions that allow me to create, invent and think outside the box. I like extreme challenges and never get tired of learning something new.

My goal is very simple, I want to honor God in my work, take care of my family, and help others succeed. If your company can use my unique skill set to improve your company and or your clients projects, then please contact me.
