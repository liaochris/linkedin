Margaret Belmonte's Summary

I have been active in real estate sales for more than 25 years, representing both buyers and sellers in the greater Boston Area.
In the 80's and 90' s I was Vice president of The Conway Company located on Charles Street in Beacon Hill. I co managed two offices, one in Beacon Hill and launched another office in the South End of Boston.

My goal is to provide excellent service to my buyer and seller clients. I am able to achieve this goal with the support of a full time team of licensed real estate professionals and a commitment to on going education and technological advances in the real estate industry, enhanced by my precision negotiating skills. I help my clients interpret statistical changes in the market enabling them to make informed decisions based upon current market data. I pride myself on efficient communication while maintaining the personal touch.
