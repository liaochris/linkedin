President, CEO

Caroline's Cakes

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Food Production industry

June 1990 – Present (23 years 7 months)

We are a mail order cake company known for making a 7-Layer Caramel Cake from a very old, traditional recipe. It's popularity has spread our brand across the country and now we make many different cakes based on mostly Southern Recipes. We ship to the consumer and for corporations all over the USA. As we build our company we never cease to be amazed at the stories we continue to hear about our Caramel Cake. It is so authentic and has brought back so many memories for so many, as well as attracting a new following as people discover this wonderful cake.
