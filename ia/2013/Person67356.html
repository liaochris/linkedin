Jon Henry's Summary

Creativity comes from organizing chaos in some useful way, and organizing all the chaos associated with marketing a great company or idea is where I thrive. With a healthy dose of introspection, I formed Introverted Consulting to help great leaders examine their current struggles and uncover the best course of action to overcome the many unique and nuanced marketing challenges present today.

I have worked with leaders in numerous roles, bringing thoughtfulness to service through curiosity and interests in marketing, communications, company culture, and talent management. I have helped companies break complicated processes down into smaller elements in order to rebuild and reconfigure the elements in new and productive ways. This insight in turn has lead to major cultural shifts and improved company performance.

I believe in optimism, which has helped me help companies endure the worst economic period in all history. I am curious first and critical second, which has helped me create and develop diverse and talented teams. I am not threatened by change or uncertainty, which has allowed him to manage growth and value without being institutionalized.

I am a reformed student of Political Science and Economics, and will have completed my MBA by May. My most significant education remains the visible experiences anyone can witness daily in the business world. I reads hundreds of business books, which tend to result in two points: deliver value, and don’t be mean.

I am a member American Marketing Association (AMA), and possess the AMA’s Professional Certified Marketer (AMA PCM) credential. He is also member of the American Management Association and the Society for Human Resources Management.

I once wanted to be a lawyer, until I learned that I argued so well with my friends that they did not invite me to parties as a sign of great respect. I since gained mutual forgiveness among my friends, and a subtle distaste for lawyers.
