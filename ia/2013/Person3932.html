Alexander Crockett's Summary

I am a User Experience Architect. I specialize in using research methods to help define systems that help people engage with services on and off line. I take a holistic approach and have worked across multiple devices; from desktop to mobile, tablet and TV. In addition to using research I am advocate of storytelling methods to describe a user’s experience and map a user’s needs to the needs of a system.

I have previously worked at several agencies including Blitz, Level Studios, Rosetta, TBWA/Chiat Day, Precedent Communications London as well as Whitehall in London and Genex.

I’ve worked with Microsoft Game Studios, Computer Associates, Johnson and Johnson, The British Heart Foundation, COMDATA, Liverpool John Moores University, Anglia Ruskin University, several NHS Health Authorities in the UK, The Welsh Assembly Government, The Financial Services Authority in the UK, The World Federation of Exchanges, The Judicial Appointments Commission, Acura and Ford.

Specialties: Personas, Card Sorting, Interaction Design, Use Cases Thematic Analysis, Ethnographic Research, Storytelling and Scenarios, Goal Analysis, Contextual Analysis, Cross Platform Design, Survey and Focus Group Design, System Diagramming, Behavior Pattern Analysis, Experience and Information Strategy, Experience Mapping, Taxonomy Design and Content Strategy, Usability Testing, Mental Models, Conceptual Development, Qualitative and Quantitative Analysis, Requirement Analysis, and Interaction Design.
