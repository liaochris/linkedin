Global Marine's Summary

Global Marine is one of the largest marine resellers specializing in the wholesale purchase and resale of used boats through domestic and international channels. With over 35 years of combined industry experience, the principals have well established working relationships with numerous leading marine retailers and are regarded as a trustworthy and reliable resource for sellers and buyers alike.

Our customers often ask, “How are you able to sell such high quality and well maintained boats at prices at or below the repo market”?

Here is how it works: When a customer trades in a boat at the dealership level, the dealer contacts Global Marine to “cash out” the trade-in. Because of our unique position in the industry, we are able to buy boats at the lowest possible prices allowing our export partners to incur the costs of shipping and still resell the boat at a profit in their respective markets. We offer these same wholesale prices to the public.

We assist resellers, brokers, and dealers from around the world from the initial search to the purchase and delivery of their vessel. Because we own the inventory and pricing decisions are made in-house, our sales process is straightforward, and buyers enjoy a no-nonsense, honest, and expedient experience.

Specialties

Global Marine is one of the largest marine resellers specializing in brokering as well as wholesale purchasing and resale of used boats.
