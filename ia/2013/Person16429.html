Brandon Addison's Summary

As an Internet Marketing Strategist and social media trainer with the Search to Social, I design and implement custom-fit Internet Marketing Solutions for our clients in the Physical Medicine and Chiropractic industry utilizing super-effective internet marketing and branding solutions including: SEO, PPC management, Email Marketing, Video Marketing, Reputation Management, Social Media Marketing, Blogging, and more...

Doctors looking to build a larger online customer base, a greater patient lead channel, or want to tap into an online market they know would add to their cliniic, need a very goal oriented and revenue based strategy in order to achieve those goals. This is where we excel. We show our clients what the market looks like in the areas they want to compete, then we help them dominate that market bringing in high value, quality patients who are motivated and educated on their treatment options.

I also perform several Medical SEO Training and Internet Marketing training courses as well.
