Alexa Scordato's Summary

Alexa Scordato is passionate about using social technologies to foster relationships, empower individuals, and transform organizations. She is currently working at an exciting edu-tech startup that is committed to changing the world of higher education as we know it. In the past, Alexa worked as a digital strategist and PR professional, helping business leaders and Fortune 500 companies understand how social technologies can help facilitate HR, marketing, and customer support processes.

In 2007, Alexa graduated a year early from Barnard College where she wrote her senior thesis on female adolescents within online fan communities. She believes in introducing the next generation of geeky girls to smarter, safer, and more sophisticated web practices.

An innovative thinker, fast learner, and connector, Alexa is poised to help companies and individuals better market themselves through community-building, social technologies, and authentic relationships.
