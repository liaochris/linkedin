IMS Ghaziabad's Summary

IMS Ghaziabad is leading Management college offering various graduate and under graduate courses. Started with its flagship program PGDM MBA equivalent course in 1990's is one of the oldest colleges and most preferred B School in Delhi NCR.

Highlights about IMS

- Ranked “A1″ by AIMA (All Indian Management Association), May 2013
- Ranked “4th” in Top B-Schools of Excellence in All India as per CSR-GHRDC Survey (Nov. 2011).
- Ranked in Top 10 B-School in Delhi & NCR by Hindustan Times (Sep. 2011).
- PGDM Programee is equivalent to MBA Degree by AICTE and AIU (Association of Indian Universities).
- PGDM (TM) Programme equivalent to Master in Tourism Manageent (MTM) by AICTE.
- 100% placement in India and abroad (Batch 2009 – 2011 Placement details).
- Dual Specialization in Marketing, Banking & Finance, Finance, Manufacturing & Engineering Management, Information Technology, International Business, Human Resources Management and Operations Management.
- One of the few Private Indian B-School, producing focussed action oriented business leaders.
- Student have the privilege to learn more than one foreign language as part of their course curriculum.
- Professional and Personal Skills Programme (PPSP), which inculcates soft skills in students, is part of the course curriculum.

Specialties: MBA Equivalent PGDM, Post Graduation Diploma in Management, Management Education, Business Management, Marketing, Finance, IT Management, Masters in Computer Application, Bachelors in Computer Application and Bachelor of Business Admin, Bachelors and Masters in Biotechnology, Bachelors in Journalism and Mass communication
