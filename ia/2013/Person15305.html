Bobby Stevens' Summary

For most of the eighties I made my living with music. Playing bass and singing in a number of rock and pop bands, I toured extensively in the US and entertained the troops overseas. By the end of this period I was living in Los Angeles, headlining the famous rock clubs on the sunset strip.

In the nineties, I began a career as a graphic designer, moved to Seattle, and spent time in advertising, web and software interface design. After a number of years leading talented design and marketing teams as a Creative Director, I grew tired of the corporate world and bailed out for a "mini-retirement".

In 2006, after a two year sabbatical that included relocating to rural Oregon, I began working as a licensed contractor, providing high-quality home improvement services.

In 2008, seeking to leverage my business and marketing expertise in a more "people oriented" field, I began a career in Real Estate. I've found that my past experience and temperament are well suited to this pursuit and I find great personal satisfaction in representing peoples interests as they buy and sell their homes.
