Keith Glover's Experience

Coach to Pastors & Ministry Leaders

Pastor2Pastors

Professional Training & Coaching industry

January 2006 – Present (7 years 11 months)

Ever feel like you’re heading down a road and you have no idea where to turn next, or how to get to the other side…and you’re the tour guide in charge of the trip? Ever feel like no matter what you do, it’s never enough! Let Pastor2Pastors help you learn the “unforced rhythms of grace” and get your life back!

Our goal at Pastor2Pastors is to become a safe place for Pastors and Ministry Leaders to find real rest and recover their life without losing their ministry. The increasing demands and unrealistic expectations placed on a pastor is unrecognized by many in the congregation and some pastors don’t realize it themselves. We never set out to run our self into busy ineffectiveness and poor health, but it is what happens all to often. Statistics say that 1,500 pastors leave their positions due to burnout, moral fallout, or career change (usually stemming from seeing no escape from burnout) each month in America. That breaks our hearts and we want to help pastors/ministry leaders live out the following scripture:

Matthew 11:28-30 (The Message)

28-30“Are you tired? Worn out? Burned out on religion? Come to me. Get away with me and you’ll recover your life. I’ll show you how to take a real rest. Walk with me and work with me—watch how I do it. Learn the unforced rhythms of grace. I won’t lay anything heavy or ill-fitting on you. Keep company with me and you’ll learn to live freely and lightly.”
