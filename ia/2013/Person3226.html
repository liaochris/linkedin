Alan Spencer's Summary

Alan Spencer (alan@telecomexpert.co.uk) is a Chartered Engineer and consultant who has worked in the IT/Telecoms industry as a Technical Designer/Solutions Architect, Programme/Project Manager, Business Analyst and as an Expert Witness for a number of high-profile cases. For the last 23 years has worked totally on a freelance basis.
Experienced in many areas including, WANs, IP and MPLS Networks, SIP/H.323/H.248 protocols, Contact Centres, Transmission (SDH/DWDM), Telecom Networks (VoIP/IPT), Unified Communications, Next-Generation Networks (NGNs) and Fixed/Mobile Convergence (FMC).
Managed contracts involved in system design, OSS/BSS systems, infrastructure design, transmission design, network planning and upgrade, network implementation, delivery, business change, major roll-out and migration programmes, product development, relocations and business process design/change using Casewise/eTom.
Experienced in procurement, bids and tenders, regulation and standardisation, RFP/RFI/RFQ/ITT/SLA processes and evaluation, vendor/supplier selection and procurement, negotiations, cost analysis, organising and chairing meetings and report writing.
Project management involving writing, tracking or managing PIDs, Change Requests, Contracts, Business Cases, Contract Change Notes, Prince 2 Project Structures, Financial Planning, Modelling, Project Plans, Risks and Issues, Lessons Learnt, Consequential Damages, Service Credits and Service Level Agreements.
Experienced in Oil & Gas Process industries (with Fluor, BP and BOC - five contracts in total), FEED, PMC, tender and EPC phases of contracts.
Worked as a freelance trainer and course designer and has provided over 300 training courses within the IT and telecoms subject arena as well as some soft skills training. Member of the Learning and Performance Institute (MLPI).
Reported to all levels of management and been involved in simple tree structures and matrix management as well as internal and external customer-facing projects.

Specialties

IPT/VoIP; Soft Switches/Call Servers; Media Gateways/Servers; MSANs; SBCs; CoS/QoS; TCP/IP; SIP; H.323; H.248/MEGACO; Diffserve; RSVP; LDP; ATM; Broadband; ISDN; ADSL; SS7; PDH; SDH/SONET/WDM; MPLS; DASS2; DPNSS; QSIG; CORNET; R2; CAS; NGNs; SIGTRAN; VPNs; VLANs; BTNUP; Legacy TDM Systems, Prince 2; Cisco; ITIL; Agile Scrum; Kepner-Tregoe; Zachman/TOGAF; Casewise/eTom; Six/Lean Sigma; Trainer; IMS; PA systems; Microwave/Radio/Satellite Systems; Cable Labs; HFC; DOCSIS.
