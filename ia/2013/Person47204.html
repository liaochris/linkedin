Gary Reiss' Summary

Onsite 340B was designed to improve patient access and utilization of 340B drugs by offering fulfillment at the Point of Care where no in-house pharmacy exists. We work in concert with your 340B Administration Company and PBM to allow for seamless implementation and consolidated reporting. To them we look like just another Contract Pharmacy. Experts in Physician Dispensing and Remote Pharmacy, we combined both to offer you the best solution.

☤ We Provide Dispensary Staffing – Onsite 340B employees staff the dispensary during all the hours your providers are writing scripts. We pay them, but they report to you!

☤ Pharmacist Oversight – Before the drug is released to the patient our remote pharmacist will see an image of the script, bottle and label and approve the dispensing as an added check for the physician.

☤ Formulary Support – Our pharmacists are experts in 340B and will assist the physicians in creating a formulary using "penny drugs" and the most cost effective drug in each class combined with the specific demands of the prescribers in the practice. Formularies will be reviewed regularly.

☤ All Software and Hardware – We supply, the computer, scanner, printer and dispensing and remote software needed at no cost to the practice. All we need is a closet and internet access.

☤ Painless Terms on Opening Inventory – We collect all the money from patients and payers and place it in a separate pharmacy account in the name of your practice. With your permission we will pay the related bills from this account. Once that's set up, we will arrange for extended terms for the opening inventory and pay those bills from sales; you will not have to pay for the opening inventory in advance.

☤ Increased Capture Rate – Most Covered Entities are capturing less than 20% of the total written scripts in their Contract Pharmacies. Depending on Formulary we capture up to 80% of the written scripts for drugs in our cabinet.
