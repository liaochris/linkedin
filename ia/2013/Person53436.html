Holly Fisher's Summary

I love telling stories and that's what I do through my business, HAF Creative. As a writer, editor and marketer, I write articles for various publications, especially in the Charleston, S.C., area. I also manage social media marketing and public relations for small businesses and nonprofits.

I spent more than a decade in the newspaper business, working in West Virginia, Indiana, Texas and South Carolina. I also spent a few years working at a Charleston, S.C., PR and marketing firm before launching HAF Creative.

Locally I am an active member of the Center for Women and sit on the boards of the Charleston Chapter of Femfessionals and the Charleston Chapter of the American Marketing Association. I also serve as board president of Postpartum Support Charleston.

After completing my master's degree in mass communication at the University of South Carolina, I tried my hand at teaching. I spent three semesters as an adjunct professor at the College of Charleston.

A native of Ohio, I have a bachelor’s degree in journalism from Ohio University.

I live in Mount Pleasant, S.C., with my husband, Clint; daughter, Katherine; and two Labrador retrievers.

Specialties: Writing, editing, creating new publications/products, website development, experience with online media, project management, social media, teaching at the college level.

Contact me via www.HAFCreative.com.
