Megan Olynik's Summary

"The World Fascinates Me"
- Andy Warhol

I have lived by this quote ever since I was in my second year of University. I was taking Fine Art and Multimedia at the time and my creativity was being tapped so often that I made it my mission to find inspiration in everything I do and see. As a practicing artist I keep to this idea to this day and it enriches my life and art to no end.

I am currently on a networking and information seeking mission to meet as many people and to learn as much as I can regarding the art administration industry and the running of an art gallery. I am attending Western University Continuing Studies to acquire my Diploma in Arts Management. Expected graduation in August 2014.

Specialties: Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Dremaweaver, Adobe Illustrator, Microsoft Office, Printmaking, Painting, Sculpture, Ceramics, Digital Painting, HTML, CSS, Design, Fine Art, Crafts, Hands on, Problem Solving, Multitasking, Organization, People Skills
