Dewey Sadka's Summary

When I ran Temp Force, later Randstad Staffing, I was always looking for a way to “Beat the System,” to hire others for that they enjoyed. It was way to make more money. In my search for the answer, I gave hundreds of speeches on testing systems as I searched for a way to evoke. “Why we all do what we do.”

Since then, lucky me, I’ve discovered my life’s work-The Dewey Color System. Now I believe, “Your color preference is who you are, more than even your name.” Making a difference through speeches, books and validated testing systems is really fun for me and everyone else. Guess deep down, we are all laughing at ourselves.

Right now, I'm very excited about our new partner, Candid Career. Our Color Career Indicator 4.1, Todays #1 Accurate Career Assessment, now includes experts sharing their occupational experiences. Click on the video below.
