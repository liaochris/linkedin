Globas Technologies' Summary

Globas Technologies is leading software, mobile application development and smart city solution integrator. Globas provides its customer with quality and high-end programming solutions using smart technologies. Globas Technologies’ project portfolio is diverse, like projects and services covering Mobile Application Development, Web-based application development, web design, data warehousing, data center, staffing, strategic planning, CRM and ERP integration, document management and workflow, computer-based training and development, and third-party project monitoring services.
