Bryan Heathman's Summary

Bryan Heathman is the President of Made For Success, publisher of audiobooks and ebooks. As one of America’s top publishers for professional speakers, he has worked with Zig Ziglar, Brian Tracy, Mark Victor Hansen, Dr. Sheila Murray Bethel, Mark Sanborn, Connie Podesta and NSA President Laura Stack. Bryan is also the author of Conversion Marketing, analyzing successful (and a few not so successful) marketing campaigns managed for Proctor & Gamble, Nissan, TV Guide, Travelocity and CVS Pharmacy.

Bryan started his career selling photocopiers for Xerox in Redmond, Washington. Hired away by Eastman Kodak Company, Bryan honed his packaged goods marketing skills working with major retailers including Costco, Walmart and Target. Bryan entered the high tech arena in 1995 working for two Bill Gates companies – Microsoft and Corbis. Bryan started his first company, iPromotions, in 1997 which was purchased in 2000 by a publicly traded company in New York (NASDAQ: TFSM) during the dotcom boom.

Bryan is re-writing the rules of publishing and content distribution with Made For Success. AudioInk, a division of Made For Success, distributes ebooks to major retail bookstores including Amazon, Apple’s iBookstore and Barnes & Noble with a unique blend of marketing & product positioning. This innovative approach to publishing has created dozens of success stories for speakers and writers within the National Speakers Association.

Bryan’s work has been featured in the Wall Street Journal, Inc Magazine, NBC News Sirius XM Radio. Bryan is a speaker on marketing innovation featured at the National Speakers Association, American Marketing Association and the Direct Marketing Association.

Bryan earned a bachelors degree at the University of Washington, is an accomplished musician, attempts to play tennis, rides a Harley and is an avid skier. He has climbed the Mt. St. Helens volcano with his teenage son and enjoys a rich family life.
