Drew Russell's Summary

I have been a Residential Sales Agent in the Sarasota Real Estate Market for almost 9 years now. My areas of success have been and continue to be in consistent sales growth, internet strategy and implementation, and a focus on luxury real estate. My goal is fairly simple: to continue to provide unparalleled service and performance to my clients while continuing to grow my real estate practice. For 2011, 2012 and 2013, I have ranked in the top 20 agents in the Sarasota Board of Realtors for sales by dollar volume. I am a member of the Sarasota Pinnacle Group which is an exclusive group of high end luxury brokers from across all companies who network for mutual profitability. Each and every year that I have been in the Sarasota real estate market my business has grown and outperformed the year prior. I am extremely competitive and enjoy negotiating and representing my customer's interests. My strategic partnership with Michael Saunders and Company has allowed me to achieve levels of success for myself and my customers that few others see in the market. Contact me to learn more about the competitive advantage that I can provide.

Specialties: Contract negotiation, luxury listings, advising buyers, seller representation, web and print marketing, marketing strategy, broker to broker networking, international buyers, 1031 exchanges and investment property.
