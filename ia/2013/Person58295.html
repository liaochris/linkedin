Janet Guertin's Summary

With over 10 years experience in the design industry, I have worked for large in-house design departments, small design firms, and as a freelancer with self directed projects and outside clients.

Past projects have included branding and strategy for small businesses, multifaceted branding initiatives, photography art direction, and front end web design. I am organized, open-minded and enjoy working in a team environment.

Presently I am broadening my skills in CSS and HTML, and expanding my love of photography.
