Scott Williams' Summary

My clients are visionaries who have put their heart and soul into their products. It is my job to take a client's vision and use it as inspiration to drive the creative process.

In 2012, I successfully branded a new cosmetology School located in the middle of four well-established competitors. Leveraging the power of online and social media, classes were full on opening day and year 1 exceeded all expectations. This year I helped a hungry entrepreneur get his Italian pepper spreads out of his home kitchen and into groceries and restaurants across the Midwest, with upcoming plans for expansion in products and distribution.

It is a great pleasure to work closely with innovators and their dreams. My creativity burns brightest while developing the strategy to bring fresh ideas to market. Likewise, I strive to bring innovative design and fresh ideas to each project.

In order to stay in the know, I look forward. I keep my finger on the pulse of what is new, what is now, and what is next. I monitor trends and seek to set them, always searching for the way to give my clients the competitive advantage.
