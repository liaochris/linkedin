Our solutions are based on the ABCs of Internet Marketing:

A) Affordable Web Solutions--we build sites from the ground up and optimize existing websites, enabling our clients to cost effectively expand their market reach and create new revenue streams, while building brand image and credibility.

B) Build Targeted Traffic--we plan and execute integrated go-to-market strategies that leverage paid search marketing, search engine optimization (SEO), social media marketing (SMM), and email marketing to make our clients more visible on the Internet, and to attract more qualified prospects to their businesses.

C) Convert More Customers--we help our clients turn their prospects into customers, and their customers into revenue. By using proven conversion architecture tools and techniques, we optimize the experience of each visitor to our clients' website and increase their conversion rates. And with our web analytics tools, we are constantly monitoring and measuring their website activity, using this information to improve our clients' strategy and programs as market dynamics change.

We also offer a complete suite of Marketing services, including planning, developing, and executing go-to-market strategies, messaging, pricing, marketing content and materials creation, sales readiness, marketing planning and execution, and traditional advertising, including direct mail and print advertising.

We focus on how to bring the best of both worlds to small and medium sized businesses, taking a holistic approach to increasing the visibility of their their brand and growing their revenue while reducing marketing costs to fit within today's tight budgets.

Lou Unkeless' Summary

I have 30 years experience in senior marketing, operations, and financial roles in corporate and consulting environments, including the technology sector and a variety of other industries.

I am currently the owner and managing director of a rapidly growing digital marketing agency. Our objective is to help small and medium sized businesses grow their revenue, even in a tough economy.

If we can help business people increase their top line while reducing their overall marketing spend, they will hire more people, provide more goods and services to the community, and be better able to take care of their families while funding their retirement.

Our vision is to "help revitalize the local economy, one small business at a time." I think this should keep us busy for awhile.

At WSI, we understand our client’s go-to-market objectives, and then bring to bear the optimal combination of both internet marketing and traditional marketing tools and techniques to enable them to achieve those objectives. We create focused and effective go-to-market strategies, compelling and differentiated messaging and value propositions, and a highly visible, professional presence for each business. Then we attract more qualified prospects to the business, help convert those prospects into customers, and maintain profitable lifetime relationships with them.

We also offer complete outsourced marketing services to comapnies trying to keep their fixed costs low while still effectively going to market, as well as intern and part time Chief MArketing Officer services.

Specialties

We provide lead generation and revenue growth solutions that combine the right go-to-market strategy with websites designed to drive and convert targeted traffic, search engine optimization (SEO), social media marketing, mobile marketing, paid search marketing (PPC), e-mail marketing. We also incorporate traditional marketing techniques where appropriate to maximize ROI and provide both outsourced marketing services as well as part time and interim Chief Marketing Officer services.
