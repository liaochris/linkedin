JEFFRY 'Jeff' BROWN's Summary

STANDOUT GROW TRANSFORM THINK DIFFERENTLY
Every life is a story. My story includes many adventures, some successful, some great learning experiences, all valuable and part of who I am and what I can do for you to standout, grow, transform, and think differently. At Apple in the early years heading the Hal team I learned the value of starting with WHY and helped envision what has become the iPhone and iPad. I also created the Sun Bums Shopper in Waikiki, co-wrote and launched a Broadway Show, traded fine art, and lived out of a backpack touring the vineyards of Europe. I've started and led businesses, including CEO of a publicly traded company. I focus on the WHY first, then the bottom line.

Today I catalyze, coach, present, and help create customer happiness.

Positive, strategic and insightful are the words that clients use to describe my coaching and consulting style. I know how to turn opportunity into reality with ideas that are strategic and sound. I’m positively influential and directive when it comes to empowering others to accomplish tasks. Executives like my “What do you need” approach and my style of helping them achieve it. Fast paced, directive and team oriented I often develop a quick road map that looks at the end result, helping team members understand how to accomplish the goal.

My love of work includes:
• Board Member, Coaching, and Strategy for Fortune 500 companies to start-ups
• Developing and transforming ideas into something superb
• Creating accountable strategies to helping clients where they are stuck or want to go

My personal Why: ‘Coaching leaders to innovate and be positive market influences while transforming something good into something superb’

I've also structured, negotiated and/or advised on over $250 million in transactions as well as provided due-diligence advisory services relative to investments, acquisitions, technology licensing, joint ventures, and strategic alliances.
