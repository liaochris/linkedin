Ben Pandya's Summary

Ben Pandya is the founder and CEO of JBR Global. From its inception JBR has seen extensive growth. JBR started as an AZ company servicing local clients and has grown to servicing more than 4500 executives in multiple countries.

JBR Global was founded on the principle that organizations can reduce expense and maximize profits by aligning the passions of its key employees with organizational goals. JBR Global's four step process continuously leads to a greater retention of talent, a more productive workforce, and organizational savings.

JBR Global's process: 1. An intake session to understand the needs of an organization and positioning of their talent. 2. "PANTEL 4 Passion's" online/ipad/iphone app assessment unique to JBR Global 3. An executive training session customized to the needs of the organization and individual. 4. Organizational debrief.

Note *Many organizations use "PANTEL 4 Passion's" assessment tool as a screening device for new talent decisions.

PANTEL 4 Passion Quotient Assessment was created based on 30 years of global research and input from some of the most renowned names in international business including Anand Parekh Division CEO of Reliance International, and Angela Hyde Fmr. VP Global Learning and Development at AstraZeneca.

The assessment tool is geared towards upwardly mobile professionals looking to position themselves and maximize their talents.

The 96 easy to answer questions prioritize 16 different life attributes in 6 categories. The output is a confidential report that highlights key insights into the areas they are most passionate about and areas requiring additional focus.
