HADLEY WOOD's Summary

I am the CEO and President of H. Linwood Insurance Services, Inc., a California Corporation since January, 2010. My company has a main office in Carlsbad, CA and various satellite offices in Southern California, Northern California and Texas.

I have been working within the insurance arena since 2003 and offer all forms of Commercial Insurance for various types of businesses. I hold an MBA from UCLA and am a licensed Producer in multiple states. I am an experienced Risk Management Specialist with an extensive and diverse client list that includes: mainstreet businesses, construction tradesmen, construction developers, restaurants, bars & taverns, day spas, film productions, horse farms, engineering firms, advertising companies, manufacturing companies, auto shops, attorneys, accountants, life coaches, non profit centers and more.

People don't realize how creative and dynamic the insurance arena actually is. It changes often and finding the right program and product for each client is fun, challenging and requires wearing many hats and using all types of resources. Some days, I am a detective - other days, a teacher, a business coach or an analyst. All of my business knowledge and people skills come into play daily. I truly love what I do and am grateful to be able to help each of my clients in unique ways.

My goal is to fully understand what my clients do and where they are headed and then identify the exposures in their business. Sounds simple but most brokers and agents don't spend the time to engage. I believe in nuturing long term business relationships with each of my clients. I become their personal business protector and strategic partner.

Specialties: General Liability, Work Comp, Bonds, Construction Risks, Risk Management for Business Owners. Professional Liability, Commercial Insurance Specialist, Insurance Consulting.

Active member in Carlsbad Chamber of Commerce; Hera Hub, GOG, NARI; various Builder Communities; Insurance networks.
