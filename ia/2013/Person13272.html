Ben Robinson, CPA's Summary

I am a CPA and co-founder of The Dentist Accountant.

In 2012 I was busy adding another notch to my “years of service” in public accounting. (In which I have 14!) I noticed that my dental clients were struggling with crazy amounts of change in their practices: shrinking collections and production, increased competition, and ever-changing health care reform that seems to get more complicated by the day.

My dentists needed help analyzing the financial health of their practices and wanted proactive advice beyond just spitting out a set of financial statements every month, preparing income tax returns, and helping with their payroll. I was left with a choice: abandon the “standard” CPA firm structure of serving lots of different industries and specialize or give my clients a lower level of service than they deserve.

I've never been the type to back away from a challenge. So, I abandoned the world I knew to bring true value to an industry that needs it. The Dentist Accountant was born.

My goal is to help you enjoy a more prosperous life. I do this by delivering sound, proven, timely and personal advice. I don’t believe in the cookie cutter approach to delivering superior results to dentists. I use lots of fancy, new technology that helps you, the dentist, make better decisions, work less and earn more. This is not your typical accounting firm. This is not your typical dental practice management consultant. This is a new breed of dental practice professional service providers.

I would like to serve you. Please, connect with me today.
