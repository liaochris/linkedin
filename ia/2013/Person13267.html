Ben Rellick's Summary

Hi there! I am a 24 year old designer / front-end developer, and I would like to change the world for the better. I graduated from JMU about a year ago, bike toured for a few months, and currently reside in a cozy house with 3 good friends in Harrisonburg, VA. My bicycle, my guitar, and my laptop are my three dearest possessions. Balance, perspective, positivity, love, art, and fun are some things I'm fond of.

I'm a capable designer specializing in interface design, and I'm proficient in HTML5 and CSS3. Responsive web design is important to me, as is creating delightful experiences and keeping everything as clean and minimal as makes sense for the project. I believe in opting for inspiration from nature, history, and fellow human beings over design magazines and the startup scene. I'd like to at worst, create cool shit and at best, inspire someone or make someone's life a bit better.

I currently freelance and usually have a personal project or two brewing. Please do send me an email or find me on twitter if you'd like to work together!

Specialties: Web design and development; graphic design; standards compliant css and html; Adobe CS5 Illustrator, Photoshop, Indesign, and Dreamweaver.
