John Bollinger's Summary

Expert in Email Deliverability and ISP Relations since 2004 including experience on the sender side and ESP side.

Prior to joining Real Magnet, John managed the Deliverability Services department for DRH Internet and before that BlueHornet Networks, an Email Service Provider, providing clients with expert advice regarding email deliverability and inbox delivery.

Prior to that, John spent over 4 years at Hot Topic Media, a leading provider of self-help, business and relationship advice, as the Director of Email Deliverability and ISP Relations. He was responsible for taking email delivery from an after-thought in the company - with no metrics, poor technology and sub-standard email practices, to a full department with 3 team members, processes, standards and metrics in place. He dramatically improved deliverability and identified and executed the steps that continue to produce continuous improvement and brought marketing and business thinking into the email domain.

Specialties: Proven success in:

• Improving inbox delivery
• Decreasing bounce rates
• ISP relations and resolving block issues
• Creativity and problem solving in email and email deliverability
• Analyzing and presenting email statistics and other pertinent data
• Putting together and developing an email delivery team
• Implementing and utilizing email best practices
• Working independently and remotely
• Understanding and compliance of current CAN-SPAM laws
