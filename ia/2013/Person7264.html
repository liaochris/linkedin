Andrew Vallentine's Projects

  • Sigur Rós - Dauðalogn (Valtari Mystery Film Competition Entry)

    • April 2012 to October 2012
    Team Members: Andrew Vallentine, Brianna Gardner, Selina McCale, Danielle Dawn Lauhoff, Wes Haskell, Andrew Zeko, Michael Jordan, Daniel Gutierrez, Joseph E. Rothert, Jordan Leer

    In the summer of 2012, Icelandic band Sigur Rós hosted the Valtari Mystery Film Competition in search for its next official music video. Fans voted for their favorites and the band chose the final winner from all the entrants. Our entry, "Dauðalogn", finished in the top 10 out of over 800 entries from across the globe.

    Director Andrew Vallentine was chosen as a featured artist on Talent House's website and explains his vision for the film:

    "I've always been fascinated with Sigur Rós and the emotional effect their music seems to have on me. When I was listening to dauðalogn, trying to come up with a concept, I literally felt as if I was transported into another time and place. Two characters, who are deeply and passionately in love, fight for the one thing that seems to make them whole--each other. As images from the past and future flash across the screen, we see the female character's inner emotional struggle as she attempts to convey her complex story through dance. I've always been curious with exploring the idea of finding your one true love and not knowing what else to hope or search for in life. This is her story and his loss."

  • Set Fire to the Rain

    • November 2011 to December 2011
    Team Members: Andrew Vallentine, Brianna Gardner, Selina McCale, Andrew Zeko, Kris Sundberg, Michael Jordan

    "Set Fire to the Rain" was a music video starring Michelle Meredith as Adele created to exemplify the emotions of a relationship gone sour. Choreographed by Selina McCale, also starring Michal Kolaczkowski, Michael Cleland, and Selina McCale. "Set Fire to the Rain" has been featured in several international publications such as Entertainment Weekly, the AOL Music Blog, Huffington Post, among others. It has currently had over 4 million views on YouTube and is up for an Emmy nomination in June 2012.

  • Monster

    • June 2010 to August 2010
    Team Members: Andrew Vallentine, Lauren LoGrasso, Brianna Gardner, Selina McCale, Danielle Dawn Lauhoff, Michael Jordan

    "Monster" was a music video created by recent graduates/current MSU students to expand their portfolios and stretch their creative capabilities during the summer of 2010. It has had over half a million views on YouTube, and was nominated for 2 NATAS Michigan Emmy awards in 2011.
