Matt Paddock's Summary

Jack of several trades, most notably those that involve supporting business operations, process improvement, and making customers (internal and external) happy.

Once I ruled out playing video games as a career, I took time to attend Oberlin Conservatory and Virginia Commonwealth University, where I received degrees in jazz studies (BA) and composition (MA). I've worked professionally as a musician, but also in telemarketing, sales, customer service, public relations, marketing, project management, and human resources. I currently live in Norfolk, Virginia with my wife and two children, and serve as director of engagement for Grow, a digital agency.

Specialties: Social business, social media, marketing, communications, training, change management, process improvement, talent development, research, analysis, budgeting, research, analysis, writing, strategy, management, google apps.
