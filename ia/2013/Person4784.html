allan edmunds' Experience

Founder- President

Brandywine Workshop

July 1972 – Present (41 years 2 months) 728 S Broad Street, Philadelphia, PA

Founded the non-profit visual arts organization in 1972 (incorporated in 1974) as a center for the advancement of fine art printmaking and to provide training and support for artists of all ages and backgrounds, especially artists of color, to work in collaboration with printers to mentor and train students and young artists. Today, the Brandywine Workshop is internationally recognized for excellence in printmaking and fostering the inclusion and documentation of diverse artists in the practice of printmaking through its archives, exhibitions and satelite collections in museums and universities across the country and abroad.

A. L. Edmunds Associates, Inc.

Consultants to non-profit organizations and institutions specializing in community development, arts and culture, and ethnic centered groups. Services include: short and long-term management and program planning, organizational and Board development, financial management and fundraising.
