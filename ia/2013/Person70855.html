A graduate of Indiana University Northwest with a Bachelor of Arts degree, Ms. Olthoff has more than 30 years of advertising and marketing experience. Prior to starting VIA Marketing, she worked as a graphic designer and art director for nine years. She is co-founder of VIA and has been the driving force behind this 23 year old Merrillville company. After becoming sole owner in 1992, responsibilities turned from the creative department toward client servicing. Ms. Olthoff spearheaded a new business effort that increased billings 50 percent in 2000 to a substantial $3 million company employing 14 people.
In November 2003, Ms. Olthoff bought Carle Communications, a public relations firm in Hammond. VIA gained an employee and added a second office location.
In January 2011, VIA Marketing acquired Studio1 through a merger. Studio1 was a St. John graphic design firm headed by Ryan Thiele. His company brought stronger online understanding, digital renderings and architectural sign abilities.
