Lissette Cortes, LEED GA's Summary

Lissette Cortes is the Owner + Principal Designer of LCID Studio, LLC. and has recently joined forces with Creative Arts Unlimited, Inc. She is an Associate member of IIDA, and has earned the LEED Green Associate accreditation by the USGBC. Currently, Lissette is the Emerging Professionals Committee Chair for the Myakka River Branch of USGBC Florida Gulf Coast Chapter as well as the Director of Communications for the IIDA Florida Central Chapter - Sarasota City Center.

As a graduate of Ringling College of Art + Design’s CIDA accredited interior design program, Lissette is using her fresh perspectives on design and technical training within a wide range of projects. Throughout her rigorous education and beyond, Lissette has been recognized for various awards and scholarships. Her success has come from her high level of organization, strong sense of time management, keen attention to detail, strong computer skills, and flexibility within a work environment. She is a very focused, proactive person, and an efficient worker that is always working towards a goal. Above everything else, she is very passionate and dedicated to what she does and exhibits that through her work ethic and the quality of her projects.

Specialties: Interior Design, 3D Digital Rendering, Branded Environments, Graphic Design, Web Design
