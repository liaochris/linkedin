Jonena Relth's Summary

My goal is to strive every day to be a "Leadership Evangelist." My passion is in developing leaders to successfully lead change transition in their organizations as well as helping them improve processes that allow their employees to do their jobs more efficiently and effectively, I've spent the last 35 years helping people grow.

Want to see me smile? Let's work together to implement Lean methodology to reduce the waste and unnecessary work in your organization!

Jonena Relth's Experience

President and Leadership Evangelist

TBD Consulting, Inc.

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Professional Training & Coaching industry

May 1991 – Present (22 years 8 months) Scottsdale, AZ

I have the privilege of leading the solutions design consulting and business operations for TBD. My passion and greatest challenge is helping our clients integrate technologies and Lean methodologies to improve their organizational effectiveness.

Over my career, I've led three successful businesses, served on the board of ASTD for nine years, and held the position of President of the Valley of the Sun Chapter. In addition, I founded the organization, Contact Center Networking Dinner (CCND), for executives to meet with their peers, participate in executive-level workshops and facilitate problem-solving sessions. I've developed two blogs: Leadership Realities and Healthcare Talent Transformation, and I host the EMR/HER Clinical Transformation group on LinkedIn for physicians, health informatics and medical professionals, IT and performance improvement professionals.

Our woman-owned firm offers a full range of organizational development, change management, Lean consulting, training, coaching and employee performance improvement solutions including enterprise-wide learning design plans, documentation, instructional design, online help and e-learning. Additionally, we have helped our clients create training departments from the bottom up and provided interim staff while buiding their learning organizations.

TBD Consulting’s unique, results-driven approach ensures efficient development and substantial return on investment. We succeed only by helping our clients succeed!

Independent Consultant

MyCOREformance, LLC

2013 – Present (less than a year) Scottsdale, AZ

Joining with MyCOREformance, LLC allows me to use my decades of experience and continue my advocacy for healthcare education. As a life-long learner, I am a member of ASTD, HIMSS and ACHE and have been recognized as a training industry expert in the healthcare sector. Most recently, our team has had the privilege of assisting our healthcare client’s by:

• Developing Affordable Care Act training
• Consulting and coaching training professionals
• Performing upfront needs assessment to development of effective learning plans
• Developing Lean healthcare basics training
• Developing change transition training for Lean healthcare leaders

With MCF, my responsibilities will allow me to use my extensive expertise in EMR enterprise-wide systems implementation and assist healthcare organizations as they bridge the connection between EMR system implementation and the associated learning required for successful transition from paper to paperless environment. My expertise in Lean healthcare, process, strategy, best practices and bottom-line thinking is a perfect fit for our client’s lean engagements. And because I understand the systems, the environments, and people needs that make organizations effective, I can translate that into documentation and training that leads to best-in-class customer service and patient care.
