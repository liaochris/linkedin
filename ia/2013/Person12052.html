Barton "Chip" Bright's Summary

Highly accomplished executive-level attorney possessing strong business acumen and experience in all aspects of cards & payments, financial services, software licensing, intellectual property, business process management, IT outsourcing and related matters. Extensive compliance and regulatory knowledge related to electronic payments (consumer and commercial credit, debit, pre-paid, merchant services and ACH). A proven negotiator of complex revenue-generating technology transactions involving some of the world’s largest entitities. A persuasive attorney who has negotiated multiple acquisitions of entities ranging from $5M to $500M. A recognized expert and speaker in the area of financial services technology and outsourcing.
