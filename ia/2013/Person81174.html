  • HOLLYWOOD HUCKSTER - A Memoir of Hysterical Proportions

    • March 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Lindsay Garber, David Garber, Zachary Garber

    From 1975 and lasting just over a decade, an aspiring writer/producer, Kevin Hartigan, came to Hollywood and took it by storm. He raised the bar when he wasn’t busy drinking at it. He sniffed out deals when he was snorting cocaine. He pulled stunts and gags, whatever it took to seize Tinsel Town. And in doing so, he also burned every bridge he crossed. A genius. A pariah. He was both. His outrageous behavior made a sinner out of a saint and left chalk outlines where the industry greats once stood. He came. He conquered. He was destroyed… but not before reaping everything Tinseltown could dish. Each and every morsel is true. I know, because I was there -- the last man standing to tell his tale.
