Everett Bracken's Summary

VOLO Careers International (VOLO) is a global retained executive recruiting corporation that specializes in providing a large pool of diverse candidates (i.e., diversity & inclusion initiatives) and identifying talent in growing International / Emerging Markets:

• Asia-Pacific • EMEA • Latin America • North America

Headquartered in Lexington, Kentucky, in the United States, VOLO has international offices and subsidiaries located worldwide. The company operates 24 hours per day, 7 days per week, and 365 days per year for our global clients. VOLO's clients are Fortune 500, NASDAQ, global private corporations, private equity groups and pre-IPO start-ups.

Every day, VOLO Careers International takes on some of the most difficult recruiting challenges imaginable. Finding great talent is what we do.
