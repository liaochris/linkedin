Eliza Cheung's Summary

A few lines about myself. I graduated from University of Toronto with a Commerce and Actuarial Science Degree. Before my real estate career, I ran my own retail sales company for over 10 years. My experience has taught me that all business are about people. It is important to listen, understand and effectively satisfying my clients' needs and together work toward achieving their goals.

I believe in giving back to the community. For every transaction I do, a contribution on behalf of my clients is made to both the Children’s Miracle Network - Toronto Sick Kids Hospital and the Canadian Breast Cancer Foundation. I am a marathon runner. For the past few years, I have participated over 50 races to fund raise for local charities.
