Bob Champagne's Summary

Deliver focused performance solutions for Customer Experience (CE) Executives and their extended leadership teams, working with clients to develop and deploy a Customer Experience strategy that is aligned with the business, financial and operational priorities of the enterprise. Provide a broad range of assessments, programs and CE Governance solutions that create "line of sight" between strategy and execution. Work with client Executives to define and articulate their CE strategy, establish clear standards for success, align and enable channel and touchpoint owners to translate CE standards into the right actions and behaviors at the work face, and establish metrics and feedback controls to effectively drive accountability for delivering on CE goals and outcomes. Services range from up front CE readiness assessments to enterprise wide "touchpoint renewal" and "CE Governance" programs.

Specialties: Performance Measurement, Customer Experience, Customer Operations, KPI Development, Strategic Planning, Leadership Alignment, Benchmarking, Continuous Improvement, Best Practices, Customer Touchpoint Measurement, Customer Experience Dashboard Development, Product Development, Product Management
