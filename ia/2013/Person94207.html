Mike Hanbery, MBA's Summary

Mike established a LinkedIn profile in 2003. In 2008, he successfully employed his LinkedIn network to build the partner and customer base for a digital media market offering. A career in media and marketing has brought Mike through print, TV and events to Internet Marketing and Social Media for Business.

Mike's leadership roles in marketing, media and national live event management have provided wonderful experiences:

► 138% growth in revenue with a corresponding 8% growth in cost of sales within 3 years of joining Webolutions - the best performance numbers for this division in the company's 19-year history.

► 200% Year Over Year growth of a Social Media Marketing for Business Meetup group which is now one of the top of its kind in the world.

► First ever convergence of Live Satellite Broadcast, High Definition TV and Digital Projection to a live outdoor audience.

► 20% Year Over Year growth of the largest national convention in the US Satellite TV industry.

► Launched 3 successful entrepreneurial businesses. (Started more than 3, just so it's been said.)

Mike is a professional speaker and frequent guest expert on business radio programs. He is a member of the adjunct faculty at the University of Denver and has received honors and recognitions from prestigious business organizations.

Mike is an alumnus of one of the top Executive MBA programs in the world. He has served on several nonprofit boards including President of a community youth athletic organization and Webmaster of another.

Born on the Ole Miss campus and raised in north Louisiana, Mike now lives in suburban Denver, CO, with his wife, two kids and two dogs. He likes to travel, play golf, go to DU hockey games and watch the New Orleans Saints on TV. When Mike was a kid he wanted to play third base for the New York Yankees. If they call, he's still willing to talk.
