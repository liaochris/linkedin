Federico Fasolino's Summary

An accomplished film and commercial production executive with extensive experience with premiere companies in the United States and Europe/UK.

Working with the world's leading advertising agencies, created challenging and award-winning campaigns for an international roster of clients, including Visa, Mercedes-Benz and General Mills. Budgets range from small to multimillion dollar projects.

Produced elaborate shoots in 35 countries across the five continents in diverse locations ranging from the deserts of Mongolia to the coral reefs of Egypt to the city streets of Tokyo to a mountain monastery in Nepal. Collaborated with renowned directors such as Tarsem, Zack Snyder, Marcus Nispel and Tony Kaye.

Identifies and nurtures new talent into independent and creative production staff while building and maintaining strong client relationships. Possesses in-depth knowledge of directors and celebrities, and has experience on licensing contracts.

Headed a major European production company of 80 employees and established international branches in London, Hamburg and Berlin.

As a contemporary art passionate, Federico Fasolino has produced artist Marco Brambilla densely hypnotic and kaleidoscopic 3D film RPM, commissioned by Ferrari in celebration of their latest auto masterpiece, the 458 Spider, which premiered at Art Basel Miami on November 2011.

Born in Milan, Italy, has lived in New York since 2004.
Fluent in English, Italian, French and Spanish.

Extensive creative experience spanning a variety of industries, including automotive, banking, beauty, beverage, broadcast idents, clothing and footwear, corporate image, entertainment, fashion, financial, food, household, insurance, internet film, integrated film, interactive film, packaged goods, sports, public service, technology and tourism.
