seedRef

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Internet industry

2013 – Present (less than a year) Greater Philadelphia Area

seedRef delivers critical personal character insights of job candidates to recruiters through its innovative character-based reference checking application. The web-based platform enables organizations to improve quality-of-hire and drastically reduce time spent checking references.

Data collected on the seedRef platform feeds both a scoring and a reporting mechanism, designed to provide a snapshot of personal character attributes of job candidates - as well as the potential organizational impact - to recruiters upfront in the screening process.
