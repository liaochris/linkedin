Malcolm Young's Summary

Malcolm has been a part of the Telecommunications Industry for 20 years working on major account transitions as well as new technology projects and programmes in both networks and IT disciplines. He has a wide range of experience through undertaking roles in service design, project planning, business analysis and programme management.

He has particular strengths in strategic planning and business case modelling and has built his career around transforming complex service concepts into specific service solutions.

The past 5 years has been focused upon utilizing his business and technical experience to becoming an astute and credible service management professional.

Malcolm has achieved delivering business operations transformation as part of the UK Digital TV Switchover programme and as a Service Architect within Cable & Wireless’s Managed Service proposition.

As a Service Architect, Malcolm has ownership for capturing service requirements, identifying operational gaps and service solutions, and gaining stakeholder sign-off for proposed development of people, processes and systems. Furthermore, his role extends into the delivery phase to ensure that new capability is tested and compliant to agreed service requirements.

Malcolm's experience includes:
> Requirements Engineering. Facilitating customer workshops to identify and detail requirements to a functional level.
> Development and delivery of an operational service wrap to transition customers into a Managed Service environment
> Re-engineering service interfaces and operational processes during the transformation of network services.
> Supporting commercial outsource bids to ensure operational capability can meet contractual obligations.
> Developing business cases for delivering new benefits and capability.
> Writing functional specifications for the development of new IT capability, as required to support business processes.

Specialties

> ITIL Manager Accredited (Red Badge)
> ISEB/BCS Accredited (Diploma in Business Systems Development - Rapid Applications Development)
> Business Modelling methodologies
> Strong IT Business Analysis background and experience in business and network systems.
> System & Integration testing experience.
