Mikel Del Rosario's Summary

Mikel Del Rosario is an ordained minister who helps Christians defend the faith with confidence. His primary interests are theology and apologetics.

Mikel is an Apologetics Professor, Speaker and Trainer who specializes in making apologetics accessible to the local church. He’s taught courses at Western Seminary and Biola University and he currently teaches Christian Apologetics at William Jessup University.

Mikel is the author of the Accessible Apologetics curriculum and workbook. He holds a Master’s Degree in Christian Apologetics with Highest Honors from Biola University. He is also pursuing a Master of Theology at Dallas Theological Seminary.

Mikel is the Apologetics Guy at ApologeticsGuy.com.

Specialties

Christian apologetics, theology, religion, education, teaching, writing, speaking, student ministry, and missions.

Mikel Del Rosario's Publications

  • The Accessible Apologetics Workbook: Five Lessons for Everyday Defenders of the Faith

    • Apologetics Guy
    • September 21, 2011
    Authors: Mikel Del Rosario

    The Accessible Apologetics Workbook is a companion to Mikel Del Rosario's Accessible Apologetics Curriculum--a street-level series of five lessons for everday defenders of the Christian faith. This workbook is the perfect tool for learning and discussing the material Del Rosario presents in his Accessible Apologetics Curriculum, online courses and live workshops. It prepares the reader with an introduction to each lesson and includes outlines, definitions, works cited for further study, and detailed notes. This workbook includes instructions for accessing full-length audio instruction for each lesson and all of the answers to the fill-in sections online. Great for youth and adult study groups!
