Angela Blackburn's Summary

GOING SOCIAL - IT’S STILL ALL ABOUT THE SERVICE!

First came the Call Center, which evolved into the Contact Center to support the era of email. The relentless advance of social media, combined with a plethora of mobile communication devices, has launched the era of the Social Media Response Center.

No matter the name, one common thread has not changed - the human response, or lack thereof, that can easily make or break the experience. Service excellence becomes the differentiator and that’s where I come in!

* Advocate, coach and role model for those who deal directly with the customer
