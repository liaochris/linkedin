Kelly Strodl's Summary

Wordsmith • Social Media Maximus • iPhoneographer (Amateur) • Creative Mind • Problem Solver

I hate labels, but I dig irony¿

Social media may be my present game, but I have and always will be a writer. Scribe, storyteller, reporter, person of letters, scribbler, stringer, poet, annotator, interpreter – call me what you will. I'm a marketing machine with an extensive background in digital event coverage, journalism (web and print), and marketing copy (in all forms).

I view everything/one/place as having a story to be told. Whether that story is done in a novel, iambic pentameter, or 140 characters – it is a story nonetheless.

With beginnings in traditional print journalism at the dawn of web reporting integrations and digital marketing, I've been privy to the not-so-gradual evolution of all media into the social realm.

I been at the forefront of a number of platforms that have risen to a quick fame, some fallen to the ashes of the forgotten, and others slowly grown to become digital monoliths in a new era of media.

I've coordinated social media campaigns and strategic efforts for a wide spectrum of clientele - international corporations, boutique salons, film festivals, authors, artists, local businesses, and agencies.

There is so much to be done with this new world of marketing as it has now become more than a broadcast - today's marketing is a two-way street. We have a responsibility (and means) to communicate with the audience like never before. Let's get to it.

Specialties: Writing & Editing (news, marketing, creative), Blogging (tumblr, Blogger, Posterous, Wordpress), Social Media Marketing, Social Media Strategy, Social Media Event Coverage, Social Media Content Production, Creative Production (Digital and Social Media), Social Media Content Coaching (Twitter, Facebook, Linkedin, Instagram, Pinterest, YouTube, Vimeo, tumblr), Web Wireframe Development.
