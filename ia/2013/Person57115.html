Jake Sorensen's Summary

I was born and raised in Victoria, British Columbia before embarking on my career by moving to Vancouver in 1996. I graduated from a private photography school where my studies focused on the technical side of photography. I contracted my services to various magazines that required my technical expertise and ability with professional imaging.

Four years later I was introduced to Prismtech Graphics, working exclusively in the film department. I loved the work and that provided me the opportunity to learn the other areas of the business such as printing, racking, distribution etc. I then spent two years working in production, managing and coordinating one of the company’s largest accounts. This helped me understand and develop skills to: manage the expectations of the client and all parties involved - make complex projects easier to manage - explain the process in a concise and understandable way.

I then moved on to a sales support role, providing estimating help, job coordination and cost estimating. The natural next step for me was to perform in a sales role where I could utilize my knowledge and expertise to help the company and clients. I have fulfilled this role for the past eight years and assist clients with: large format and specialty printing in digital and screen - large scale projects that require expertise in coordination, distribution and are time management sensitive - problem solving that provides our clients with options they may not have been aware of or thought about. I am a professional.

Specialties

P.O.P. displays, thermal ink printing, scratch and sniff printing, glow in the dark printing, fence mesh, hoarding, building wraps, large scale print distribution, specialty inks, stand-up displays, wall graphics, decals, giclée printing, custom displays, backlit graphics, TSA and ALRT printing, banners, floor graphics, FSC certified large format printing, project management, colour guidance
