Gregory Anne Cox's Summary

From professional chef to student of nutrition to personal chef and hospitality director of a private island, (Yes Fantasy Island is a reality somewhere) my first career was full of food--iconic, delicious, haute, simple, necessary. It fueled my career and my soul.
As time went on I wanted back into the world of personal development and the human psyche. When I stumbled upon coaching I knew I was home.

Midlife with a Vengeance was my first solo venture. I worked with midlife women on their health, their waists, and their lives to get them ready for a kick ass second half.
In April of 2012 I got the download that my mission got bigger. It became one of helping as many health and wellness professionals as I can to become successful. Why? To have an impact on the way we do food and health.
Whether working with women on their health or health and wellness peeps on clarity and confidence it's all with a view towards helping people live fully expressed.

Specialties: Health coaching for women and marketing for health professionals and coaches.
