Anne Herrera's Experience

Residential Realtor

Sotheby's International Realty

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; Real Estate industry

2004 – Present (9 years)

An accomplished Realtor(R) who has represented buyers and sellers in all parts of San Francisco, Anne offers unique advantages that help her clients successfully meet the challenges of today's residential marketplace.

With a proven record of significant real estate sales, an extensive professional background, and unmatched insights into San Francisco's unique and diverse neighborhoods, Anne Herrera brings professionalism, dedication and market savvy to every client relationship. A native Californian and 20+-year resident of San Francisco, Anne has completed successful sales of single family residences, condominiums, multi-unit buildings, TICs and lofts in just about every San Francisco neighborhood. She is also a knowledgeable referral source for areas outside San Francisco. Anne's clients have come to rely on her knowledge of local market dynamics, historically significant properties and promising investment opportunities.

Anne and her husband, San Francisco City Attorney Dennis Herrera, are deeply committed to the City they call home, and both are actively involved in numerous community organizations and causes. Anne has served as co chair for the San Francisco Symphony's largest fundraisers -- the Black and White Ball and the Symphony's annual Opening Gala -- and she is an active supporter of the Chinese American International School and the local chapter of CASA, the volunteer organization that advocates for and mentors children within the foster care system. Raising a son in the City, Anne is extremely well-versed in the kinds of challenges, choices and opportunities that face San Francisco families -- from the public and private school systems, to the rich cultural and recreational programs the City offers.
