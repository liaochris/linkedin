Lea Bailes' Experience

Attorney

Ramey and Flock, P.C.

December 2012 – Present (11 months) Tyler, Texas Area

I advise clients on matters related to business and estate planning. I help businesses and individuals succeed through sound legal advice, strategy, and asset protection.

For business clients, I focus on matters involving startup formation, venture capital financing, intellectual property, asset and intellectual property protection, licensing, contractor and employment issues, mergers and acquisitions, and drafting, review, and negotiation of various vendor, development, and consulting contracts.

I assist clients with their estate planning whether it be to ensure affairs are in order, coordinate charitable giving, protect assets, or reduce the implications of estate taxes. I do this by working with clients to develop a strategy that matches their goals and individual situation then implement the strategy through the execution of estate planning tools that include wills, durable powers of attorneys, health care directives, revocable living trusts, irrevocable trusts, irrevocable life insurance trusts, charitable trusts, and family limited partnerships.

I am licensed to practice in Missouri, Kansas, Texas, and the Eastern District of Texas.

I am a frequent speaker at CLE conferences and writes articles on the topics of business law, estate planning, and the practice of law.
