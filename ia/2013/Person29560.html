Daniel Grissom | Sales Training's Summary

With deep experience in personal development and sales effectiveness, Daniel has the ability to pinpoint problems, create solutions and deliver results for CEOs and their sales teams.

Located in the Atlanta area, he is a world-class communicator who is able to connect with professionals at all levels – from new sellers to senior executives – and coach them how to improve sales results through client focused value creation strategies!

Daniel has already helped many of the world’s top organizations improve their sales performance including: Google, McDonald’s, IBM, Bloomberg, Walgreens, NASDAQ and UPS.

He enhanced his natural leadership skills and sales training while studying under Tony Robbins at his Results Coaching Academy and Mastery University. He has earned an MBA, an honorary PhD in Christian Education and CSP – a credential which recognizes excellence in communication, which is held by less than 7% of professional communicators worldwide!

Specialties

Coaching - helping sellers/managers improve professional development and sales performance.

Consulting - helping businesses increase profits with targeted sales strategies.

Communicating - inspiring organizations to step up!
