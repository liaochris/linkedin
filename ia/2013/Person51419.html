    Remnant is a first-person shooter set in a post-apocalyptic world that combines the desperate tone and resource-management mechanics of the survival horror genre with traditional capture the flag gameplay.   • Chronoception

    • October 2012 to December 2012
    Team Members: Hammed Al-Tamimi, Rachel Kittrell, Andrew Butler, Easton Mundine, Preston Hulcy, Trevor Hilz

    Project:
    Chronoception is a surrealist two-dimensional side-scroller created by a team of six students attending the Guildhall at Southern Methodist University. The player uses time manipulation and basic movement mechanics to safely navigate past environmental hazards and eerie flying enemies. The game's aesthetic references both ancient architecture and surrealist art such as that of Salvador Dali, combining them in a dark, cartoonish manner.
