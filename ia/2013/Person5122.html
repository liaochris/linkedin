Wayne Miles' Summary

The "Alpha Agency" is a Florida licensed Private Investigator agency offering a wide range of services to both professional and individuals. Our local service area covers most of Florida and we have been known to work cases across the country as needed.

Our data services allow for us to locate individuals and property just about anywhere and we are proud to offer nationwide searches and checks to all of our clients. If fact, we do a lot of work for other Private Investigator agencies across the country. Our office has direct access to Florida motor vehicle records and we subscribe to major data centers allowing us to compile detailed background checks, pre employment checks and more.

Specialties

background checks, employee theft, Insurance fraud, Teenage driver monitoring, spouse Investigations, Credit Checks and Reports, Vehicle registration checks, service of process, Criminal Checkes, social Network investigations. And more.
