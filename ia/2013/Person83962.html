Welcome to the MacKay Fine Homes, Ted & Gloria MacKay. We are 27 year Valley of the Sun residents. As a Team, we bring many years of successful sales experience to our Real Estate Business. His strengths are negotiating, getting results, managing processes, and understanding the client’s needs. Gloria has 30+ years in Healthcare, Professional and Real Estate Sales. Her strengths in the art of negotiations coupled with years of successful sales. This is our business. We treat it with respect, dedication, focus, commitment, pride and hard work. Our values, personal and professional will guarantee you our 100% efforts.
