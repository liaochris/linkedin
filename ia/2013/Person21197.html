Chris Buckland's Summary

I have engineered, built, and provisioned countless computer automation systems, networks and associated infrastructure since 1996. All the while having the chance to work in some of the largest radio plants in the US and some of the smallest, in exotic (to a country boy from West Virginia) places like Indonesia and Nigeria. This gives me a unique perspective in the field of Broadcast Engineering and Radio IT.

My goals in a professional sense do not differ much than my goals in a personal sense: I love when ideas, ideals and the stuff in between are shared openly, and a good argument to a contrary point can influence direction. There are many ways to accomplish a goal, why settle for just one? More importantly, to be surrounded by friends and people who I consider family. There is nothing better than loving what you do and sharing it with those you love.

Specialties

Audio Delivery, Automation Systems, Networking, Data Storage, many software and hardware platforms.
