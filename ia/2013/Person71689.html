Kristina Auckland's Summary

A highly skilled Front End Specialist with over 14 years demonstrable experience in a commercial environment. I have in-depth knowledge of cross browser and cross platform issues. I have created, edited, maintained and supported large scale high profile websites for blue-chip clients from a variety of sectors including, financial, tourism, charity and government. Developing sites using progressive enhancement to take advantage of the latest innovative technologies and simultaneously using trusted graceful degradation techniques to ensure the best possible user experience.

Working the full life cycle of projects from inception to deployment including ongoing maintenance and support. I have also developed and maintained internal tools and projects, e.g. code libraries, documentation, company website and intranets. I am used to working in a high pressure environment working to meet strict deadlines. Meticulous to detail with excellent communication skills and client facing experience, I can work independently or as an excellent team player. I am extremely passionate about the web and love keeping up to date with the latest trends and technologies.
