Benjamin Collins' Summary

In 2008, I started the branding and marketing agency Laughing Samurai with Gilberto Gomez. We started this agency with the idea that good creative content is the most important part of any advertising strategy. Our opinion is that advertising should be entertaining, effective and fun. Marketing has become more of a conversation than a speech, and Laughing Samurai develops creative that promotes conversation.

In 2009, Laughing Samurai started work on our community development project, Twelve21 Gallery. Twelve21 Gallery highlights the talents of up and coming artists, as well as established artists from around the world. With our gallery exhibitions, we hope to be a prominent voice in Orlando’s growing art community. We support art in all forms and enjoy helping both artists and art lovers who hang original art in their homes.

If you have not already, please review our website. If you are a business owner, creative, or artist, and you believe in the same things we do, I urge you to reach out to us. By becoming a Laughing Samurai client, you’re helping promote a business that’s focused on improving the world through good creative. By supporting the art community through Twelve21 Gallery, you’re helping support the Orlando art market and making art more accessible for everyone.
