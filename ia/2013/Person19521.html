Camila Medici's Summary

I graduated at the top of my class at Hofstra Law School. I am an exceptional writer, with extensive experience efficiently searching within both Westlaw and Lexis systems. I also excel in interacting with clients, drafting motions, affidavits in opposition, trial memorandums, drafting contracts, wills, complaints, and bills of particulars. Jury selection is one of my areas of expertise, as I fully grasp the value of the process and its direct relation to a successful outcome. I am also a motivated self – starter, with an aptitude for fast learning. I was the Workers' Compensation Department Head at Schwartzapfel Partners P.C. and left to start my own firm, Medici Law, P.C. In 2011. I provide clients with the services of a large firm but the care and attention of a small one.

Born and raised in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, I moved to the United States in 1996 speaking no English (although I learned it within three months) and today I speak five languages. With Brazil’s economy on the rise, I sincerely believe that my native Portuguese as well as my roots in the Brazilian culture will help any industry I decide to enter into rise above its competitors. Culturally, I bring diversity into every institution I belong to and endeavor I undertake. My work ethics, diligence and perseverance are impeccable. At the same time that I highly value productivity and monetary gain I also value the importance of humanitarian and pro-bono work, and have an extensive history of community service. My experience with underprivileged communities has broadened my perspectives and increased my analytical skills as well as my inquisitive personality. I have become more knowledgeable of human nature and the hardships of life that go beyond its economic facet. I am currently an active volunteer for the American Kidney Fund, where I serve as Vice Chair of Fundraising for the Long Island region.

Specialties

Workers' Compensation Law is my area of expertise. Jury selection, psychoanalysis and profiling; I am a talented writer, attentive to detail and thorough in my research. My interaction with clients has consistently broadened my employers' client base, and established the bond and trust necessary for effective representation. I'm proficient in five languages, including my native Portuguese, and the new world language, Spanish. I also have advanced knowledge of survey design & data analysis.
