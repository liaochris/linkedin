April Starr's Summary

I am a design researcher and strategist with 13 years of experience observing people and culture in order to identify opportunities for design. I have applied human-centered design methodologies not only to to product development initiatives but also to large-scale, systemic challenges such as supply-chain optimization, health-oriented behavior change, government advisory processes, and organizational transformation. My work spans both the corporate and consulting side of design across a wide range of industries, including consumer electronics, automotive, telecommunications, financial services, retail, industrial, and healthcare.

My areas of academic interest include the application of real-time contextual and behavioral data to product and service experiences, how innovation processes are optimized for different organizational cultures, and the role and value of synthesis in the design process.

Specialties

Design Research, Design Thinking, Project Leadership, Technology Convergence, User Experience Design, Product and Service System Design, Systems Thinker, Design Strategy, Design Planning, Anthropology, Ethnography, Prototyping, Behavioral Data, Context Aware Systems, Interaction Design
