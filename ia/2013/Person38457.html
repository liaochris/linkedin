Steven Yarbrough, D.D.S., F.A.G.D.'s Summary

Practices general/cosmetic/advanced dentistry with expertise ranging from porcelain veneers to dental implants, crowns and bridges. Specializes in tooth restoration, he is one of only a few dentists certified in IV Conscious Sedation and offers a wide range of surgical procedures including extraction of third molars (wisdom teeth).

Even though Dr. Yarbrough offers a wide variety of treatment modalities, his practice philosophy remains that the best dentistry is the least dentistry. He therefore is committed to prevention of dental disease, both gum and tooth.
