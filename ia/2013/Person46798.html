Kevin Gallagher's Summary

Kevin Gallagher has more than 25 years of public relations, marketing communications and journalism experience. He is the founder and managing partner of Gallagher Public Relations, a full-service public relations agency serving clients in the high technology, renewable energy, and green tech arenas. GPR’s results-driven programs are designed to accelerate sales and increase market valuation, offering clients a unique combination of strategic marketing communications featuring thought-leading messaging services coupled with matchless writing and media outreach services. Founded in 1992 Gallagher PR played a crucial role in the success of many Internet infrastructure stars. Previously he served in both global and Silicon Valley PR agencies, held senior PR management positions with Nortel and Ingres Corporation, and was a newspaper editor and reporter for The Recorder Newspaper and Associated Press in San Francisco.

Specialties: Working as an entrenched extension of our clients’ executive and marketing teams, GPR accelerates time-to-market visibility while delivering a high ROI via experienced strategic counsel and results-oriented execution. From initial positioning for startups, to the strategy and program management behind company and product launches, we provide solid PR program support and results that steadily build our clients’ reputation, value and success.
