Jorge Perazzo's Summary

I have worked in public accounting since 1994 and have been a licensed CPA in good standing since October 2000. I became a partner in Krowne & Perazzo CPAs beginning in 2001. Our practice specializes in personalized service and attention to the client. Among my accomplishments: setup accounting systems from scratch using QuickBooks or Peachtree accounting; represent clients in IRS, EDD, and State Board of Equalization audits; sit on board of directors of non-profit organization; prepare projections and graphs for clients seeking financing or investment partners; prepare complex tax returns, from individual to S-corporations to multi-partner and multi-state partnerships. The most rewarding part of my career is consulting with individuals about their financial lives or businesses, help them stay focused and keep them along a path of success - whether that success is as simple as paying off a credit card, or selling a business.

Specialties

Accounting systems, internal controls, tax planning and preparation. Small business consulting, succession and exit planning.
