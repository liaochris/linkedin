Andres Garcia's Summary

I like giving companies a face.

Design is my passion and branding and marketing are my home. I sit somewhere in the line between Creative and Strategy. With professional experience in all facets of creative - from graphic design to copywriting to web design and development - and strategy - from email campaigns to analytics to brand strategy - my skills as an Art Director stem from experience.

My talents from intuition.

Marketing just makes sense to me and I fully realize that, while strategy and creative are two separate cogs in a system, they are completely interdependent and one would not function without the other.

In the future I hope to continue moving forward as a creative and continue working for exciting companies who's challenges I can help solve through my creative thinking and conceptualization (and realization) skills.

Specialties: Art Direction. Brand and Identity Design & Development. Melding strategy with creative. All facets of creative media. Designing brands and strategies that convert.
