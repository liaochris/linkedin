Larry Capra's Summary

Hi, I'm Larry Capra aka zenabowli (internet pseudonym).

Besides being a light sleeper and a misfit ontologist who likes travel, creativity, satire, and the unexpected novelties of living... I'm a freelance multi-medium artist, product designer, freestyle guitarist, entrepreneur, and blogger.

I run my business, Artistic Life Studios, out of my home studio in Northern Wisconsin. A place where the wolves run wild, as well as the imagination. Our long cold winters will compel a person to become creative, to avoid cabin fever and too much booze.

www.artisticlifestudios.com
