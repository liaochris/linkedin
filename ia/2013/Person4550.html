Alida Brandenburg's Summary

My 10+ years of event production, design, and leadership provides a developed understanding of how to work cross-functionally. I have successfully produced and promoted over 45 private events with up to 750+ attendants and budgets of $15,000 or more, requiring extensive coordination with a myriad of vendors, talent and promotions teams. I have tested experience in working within a wide range of budgets to execute projects without compromising the original vision, drawing on my skills in creativity and ingenuity to transform a venue using clever designs, décor and lighting tactics. My accounting experience at Hotwire further refined my skills in finance and customer service, having managed over 1,250 accounts since 2008.

I value daily engagement with team members and collaborative working environments in order to cultivate individual and team investment. I demonstrated this during my success as President of Students for Integrative Medicine, where I organized public medical conferences, workshops, and courses, engineered a committee of faculty and students to institute and promote a new major, and acted as a community health advocate. To facilitate these projects, I initiated and oversaw the creation of a public online community, as well as a monthly campus publication.

My strong academic record at UC Berkeley and leadership during this time led to my year serving as a medical and administrative assistant for Helen Manber, M.D., which required flexibility, meticulousness, and an advanced degree of efficiency in high-pressure situations. I anticipated issues before they arose and innovated creative solutions to counteract these situations, such as implementing an inventory database to better meet our clients’ needs.
