Ignacio Rodés' Summary

Ignacio (29) is a consultant in digital marketing (web / mobile) and owner at RCFactory. RCFactory is a digital business incubator, ecommerce outsourcing and digital consultancy located in Barcelona. Some of our main projects include the following:

- SHERPA APP - Sherpa is the world's #1 voice-controlled personal assistant for Android-based mobile devices. Ignacio works closely to the Sherpa team in terms of Business development and Digital Marketing.

- 6TO6STORE.com - 6TO6STORE aims to be #1 automotive accessories e-commerce for sedans, coupes, wagons, convertibles, sports cars and SUV's. 6TO6 is the automotive fanatics community founded in Barcelona in 2006 and has over 15,000 members across Europe. Its 2013 official sponsors include Red Bull, Mercedes-Benz, Michelin and Porsche.

- BEERBOX.es - BeerBox is the internet's leading craft beer ecommerce in the spanish market. At RCFactory we lead focus in each of the digital business areas optimizing all its P&L results. Maximizing traffic, optimizing conversion rates and average revenue maximization.

Also, Ignacio has over 12 years of experience in online marketing and internet business along which has held different positions among other professional ventures:

- Online Marketing Director at logitravel.com
- Marketing & B2C Sales Manager at ABAenglish.com (Atrapalo.com/Home English)
- Head of Online Marketing at clickair airlines
- Junior Media Planner at Media Contacts (Media Planning Group - HAVAS)

Graduated (July'09) in Management & Business Administration from IQS - Universitat Ramón Llull (Barcelona). During his 5 years in business school, he combined his academic studies with work. In order to complete the final stretch of his business studies, he attended Marquette University, at Milwaukee (Wisconsin, USA).

Ignacio lives in Barcelona, and he likes to spend his free time in La Cerdanya (Catalan pyreness), playing guitar, sailing or running in Collserola, Barcelona's most well-known mountain.
