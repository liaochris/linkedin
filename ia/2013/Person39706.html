Edrick Dunand's Summary

I wrote an internationally acclaimed book on High Performance as a way of life: “Succeed with Impossible Speed”-World Class Racing strategies to create High performance in life and business.

My company, The Amaris Group provides a portfolio of educational services to assist corporations in diverse sectors in developing human potential to dramatically affect leadership effectiveness from middle management to senior executive levels. We pride ourselves in helping you developing Better Results, Faster!

To spice things up and dig deeper, we use an innovative and fresh model through our participation in World Class Racing to open new doors of understanding through memorable and exciting training experience. Clarity, purpose, accuracy, outstanding focus, uncommon teamwork and dominant motivation come out naturally from of the lessons of one the most spectacular and certainly fastest team sport on the face of the earth.

Superior collaboration, swift execution, compelling and effective communications and the foundations for stronger individual and team performance as well as value contribution are some of the core values we develop with our clients.
