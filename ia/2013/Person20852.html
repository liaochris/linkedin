Cassie Walker Johnson's Summary

Having worked in the Real Estate industry since 2005, previous to being a Property Manager, Cassie Walker Johnson was a sales agent with Windermere Real Estate. In 2008, anticipating a niche market for rentals, she joined Windermere Property Management/Lori Gill & Associates as a Property Manager. There she helped further develop the luxury rental division of the Windermere Premier for Lease program. Leasing and managing properties ranging from $1000-$15,000 a month, Cassie has a well rounded background of expertise in sales, marketing, technology and customer service. In 2010 she assisted in opening Lori Gill’s newest office near Green Lake where she currently serves as the Branch Manager. Having leased more than 250 properties and collected more than $3 million in gross rental revenue, Cassie’s expertise is the professionalism you require when managing perhaps your single biggest investment.
