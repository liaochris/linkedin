David Magnabosco's Summary

Graphic design and communication is not only the profession that I have chosen to specialise in, but let’s be honest, it’s really exciting to do. Using an array of colours, shapes and words to create new creations for anything, print and identity design, as well as developing strategy, writing material and executing campaigns is my passion.

Do you want a new identity that will appeal to customers, or a website that will spread your message beyond your existing marketplace, or simply a business card for those important meet and greets? Whatever it is, the principle remains the same. It has to be visual and it has to communicate.

Specialties:

* Produce visual solutions for clients in print, electronic media and the Web.

* Use composition skills, color, typography, illustration, photography, animation, and various layout techniques.

* Organize the page into a reasonable layout and determine any other graphic elements should be required in magazines, advertisements and product packaging.
