Elizabeth C. Axford's Summary

Elizabeth C. Axford (Liz) is an award-winning songwriter and pianist with over 30 years in the music industry. She has had over 2500 usages of her tracks in films, TV shows, videos, commercials, bumpers, promos, and new media projects.

Her music can be heard in the films The Chosen One, starring Rob Schneider and Steve Buscemi, Sol LeWitt: Wall Drawings, and The Sacred Bee. Her tracks have been used during multiple episodes of 525+ TV programs and syndicated reruns and movies on 40 networks, including The Today Show, The Tonight Show with Jay Leno, Last Call with Carson Daly, Saturday Night Live, Entertainment Tonight, TMZ, Project Runway, The Voice, and more. Her music can be heard on artists' CDs, digital compilations, websites, video games, apps, ringtones, greeting cards, gift bags and collections, and in musical theatre productions.

Liz is an active member of ASCAP, NARAS, NSAI, NAMM, MTNA, and MTAC. She has voted on and attended The Grammys since 2000 and numerous industry events in LA, Nashville, and Miami since 1989. During her 20-year tenure as NSAI Regional Workshop Coordinator in Miami and San Diego she was responsible for over 300 monthly workshops, songwriter showcases, and special events. She has received service awards from NSAI, numerous songwriting awards, and five ASCAPLUS Awards.

Liz has an M.A. in Music from SDSU and a B.A. in Music from the Univ. of Illinois. During her 29 years of teaching studio piano and voice, she has held numerous recitals with her students. Her teaching studio received the Best of Del Mar award 6 years in a row for 2008-2013. Liz is the author of Traditional World Music Influences in Contemporary Solo Piano Literature, Song Sheets to Software - A Guide to Print Music, Software, Instructional Media, and Web Sites for Musicians and the forthcoming Music Apps for Musicians and Music Teachers published by Scarecrow Press, Inc. Her first industry job after graduating college in 1982 was with the Mark Foster Music Company.
