Channy Yun's Summary

Mr. Seokchan (Channy) Yun had long experiences in the web technology especially web 2.0, web standards, open source software, online music, payment and certificate services from early stage in Korea

In his first company (Nine4u Inc), he developed Nine4u.com, the first Korean online music broadcasting service and Asiamusic.net, the largest music database in 1997. (Both were ended in Dec.2005). These included own online CD retail business to make YBM CD mall and SK music mall etc. After his engineering career, he made own tech-based business models as a CTO in Nine4u since 2001. He worked with VeriSign and Thawte as a reselling partner and served online payment service of Mypay.net.

In 2004, he moved Daum Communications Corp, the #2 largest Korean Internet site and took a manager of billing team. Also he involved to help career, education and recruit developers as a CTO staff. His job forwarded to technical evangelism of Daum APIs and encourages Web 2.0 mash-up applications in Daum Developers Network and Associates (http://dna.daum.net) in 2006. In 2009, he temporally back to the school to study medical informatics with semantic web technology of Ph.D course in Seoul National University.

He has joined "Mozilla" project since 2002 as a Korean localizer of Mozilla Firefox and Thunderbird and a leader of Korean community (http://www.mozilla.or.kr). His interests included web standards to improve accessibility (http://webstandards.or.kr). as a WaSP ILG member and W3C HTML and Web Cryptography W/G invited expert. His social contributions were also active in Korean web technical community i.e. founding BarCampSeoul and FutureWeb Forum and Webappscon.

His own Channy's Blog (http://channy.creation.net) has over 60,000 subscribers with his insightful articles, especially he wrote columns on ZDNet Korea about various technical subjects.

Specialties
