Kevin Weller's Summary

Independent iPhone®/iPad® + Ruby/Rails application developer with >25 years of software engineering experience. Work solo, or assemble and lead development teams with as few as 2 (including myself) or many as 10 design and software professionals, depending on my clients' needs.

SDK Quick Reference for iOS developers: http://www.asapisystems.com/iosquickref

What I do through my company, ASAP iSystems:

We’ve made our U.S. customers’ app development and upgrade projects successful when their offshore providers failed to deliver. Failure is expensive and time-consuming. So do you really want to throw good money after bad? Or would you rather have software that actually works?

ASAP iSoft creates custom iPad®, iPhone®, and web apps that work together to provide a seamless experience across locations and devices. For example:
* If your mobile app has a social sharing component, you will need a quickly scaling custom cloud-based component on the back-end to enable the sharing.
* Or if you have functionality that needs to change more often than app store release schedules permit, you could offload those evolving functions to the cloud while still giving your mobile app users the feeling of a fast slick iPhone and/or iPad app.
* If you need to access to the massive data (e.g., an analytical app) and processing (e.g., camera image object recognition, video processing) power of the cloud, you could give your users a much cleaner and more manipulable experience.
These are applications that fall outside of the iCloud® document sharing model, but we can make them happen. We specialize in a powerful combination of iOS and Ruby on Rails technologies that allows us to quickly deliver effective cloud-connected solutions that go way beyond what iCloud can do. And we reliably support those solutions as they grow.

See http://www.asapisystems.com for more information.
