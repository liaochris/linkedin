David Chinsky's Summary

David Chinsky brings more than 25 years of executive leadership and management experience to his role as a sought-after business advisor and executive coach. Drawing upon his own successful career as an accomplished and effective leader in the healthcare, automotive and information technology industries, David offers unique insights into leadership, change management and teamwork.

David works side by side with senior professionals, enabling them to become impactful, high-performing leaders. He has delivered professional coaching and leadership development workshops to more than 200 organizations and has successfully developed and mentored in excess of 1,000 leaders.

Founded in 2002, the Institute for Leadership Fitness (www.instituteforleadershipfitness.com) offers professional coaching, leadership training and expert facilitation. The company created the Fit Leader’s Program™ in 2007 for leaders seeking the clarity, confidence, effectiveness and vitality necessary for achieving their highest level of professional success.

Specialties: Leadership training, executive coaching, expert facilitation, leadership assessments and professional speaking.
