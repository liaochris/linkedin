Matthew O'Riordan's Summary

I’m a serial entrepreneur, and started my first business when I was 16. I love building businesses, and I am currently focussing my efforts on Ably, a real-time messaging platform.

I have a very technical web development background, however I have mostly worked within media, advertising & marketing.

I am the co-founder of Econsultancy which was started by Ashley Friedlein and myself back in 1999. Econsultancy was sold to Centaur in July 2012.

I am currently a Non-executive Director of Aqueduct, a leading digital agency in London focussing primarily on the sports sector. Clients include Manchester City Football Club, Rugby Football Union, Football Association.

I am also a Non-executive Director of United Studios, a digital production company based in London.
