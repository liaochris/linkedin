Lorraine Smith FCIPD's Summary

Hello and thank you for viewing my LinkedIn profile. My aim is to connect with individuals with whom I can share my skills and experience of HR and employment law both within the business world and within education.

My clients tell me I am pragmatic, solutions focussed and ‘know my stuff’. I have an extensive track record of helping small businesses deal with tricky employee issues which can range from conducting investigations and coaching through disciplinary or grievance hearings to helping businesses re-structure and conduct consultation meetings with staff. I also draft employee contracts, staff/employee handbooks and various policies and procedures. These are written bespoke for a particular business so fit their needs rather than having a policy just for the sake of one.

As an Education HR Adviser I have gained experience working with schools and colleges. This is a sector that has many rules and regulations and often a strong union presence. Consequently it can be a challenging sector to work within, but I happily rise to that challenge. Some of my HR experience has been gained working directly within education and I have also been a school governor for the past 7 years. Since working freelance I have gained a good reputation for quickly understanding the issues of schools and colleges and helping to move issues forward to a speedy conclusion.

I understand the pressures on budgets and seek to offer a flexible ‘buy as you need’ service to my clients without the need for them to commit to a long term retainer arrangements. In reality many of my clients do become regulars. 80% of my work comes via word of mouth referral.

When not working I find time to be a School Governor and a voluntary mentor helping young people prepare their cv and interview technique ready for work and sing in a large choir called Sing Live.

If you think I could help you let’s have a conversation. www.bravozuluhr.co.uk or 07834 986875
