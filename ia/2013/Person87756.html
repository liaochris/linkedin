MARTIN HAWORTH PCC's Summary

Hello and thank you for taking the time to read my profile. I'll be delighted to help the LinkedIn community with my skills and experience.

If you would like any advice or help in Leadership, Management, Family Businesses or how to make the best of social media, please feel free to contact me on 01452 414330.

I'm passionate about helping organizations, business owners and individuals find the most effective solutions. My primary aim is to work closely with clients to create the best outcomes possible for them, their organisation and all stakeholders.

I've worked with people all my career and really understand what makes them most effective. With thousands of hours one-to-one coaching and training, I'm delighted to share my experience and expertise with clients.

There are four ways I add value to my clients:-

► I work one-to-one with business owners and leaders, supporting their personal development
► I deliver lively, fun and accelerated training programs for managers at all levels
► I support family businesses in their development and sustainability through generations
► I have an excellent understanding of helping others make the most of Social Media

At Coach Train Learn we have one single aim. To help people be the best they can; achieving the best results possible; for organizations that deserve the best results. For all stakeholders, whether within or outside the business itself.

Outside work, I love travel (I've been fortunate to travel with my work as well!); walking; Burnley Football Club and I'm a quite keen photographer too!

Specialties: Leadership Development. Management Development. Coaching. Training. Family Business. Social Media.
