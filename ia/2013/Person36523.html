DONA BLUNT, MAS, PROMOTIONAL PRODUCTS's Summary

Are you struggling with ideas to brand your business and get more customers?

Are you frustrated trying to drive more traffic to your trade show booth?

Are you excited about launching your business but can’t find a promotional product that will really make your business get noticed?

Do you really want to make your event stand out and be memorable for the attendees?

Helping companies solve these marketing challenges is why I get up in the morning. I love putting my creative brain to use. That’s why I founded Newport Promotions to help businesses promote their brand, products, and services more successfully. With 25+ years experience in the promotional products industry, it’s my goal to become the preferred “go-to” person for “Proven Promotions and Effective Results” for my clients. I excel at helping companies stand out and attract more customers with their trade show marketing, new business launches, personalized direct mail campaigns, and branded promotions.

Whether you are looking to launch a business, promote an event, draw traffic to your trade show booth, or build more awareness for your brand, I can help you create a unique and memorable marketing idea to meet your goals. I love being challenged to think creatively!

I’m a small business owner too and I know how important it is to get things done quickly, painlessly and with a big return on investment. I offer my clients my knowledge of the best sources that deliver quality products - on time and at affordable prices. I’m proud to say that I am one of only a few promotional product consultants who have earned the Master Advertising Specialist (MAS) certification from the Promotional Products Association International. That’s why I like to be known as PromoDona!

Newport Promotions is a woman-owned promotional product advertising company that specializes in helping companies build their brand, get noticed, and stand out from the competition. We thrive on providing you with creative solutions to attract more customers, drive more traffic to your trade show booth, promote your products and services, and make your meetings and events more memorable. With 25+ years in business, we offer clients our unique knowledge of the best sources that deliver quality products, on time and at affordable prices.

Newport Promotions helps clients with their marketing challenges in a variety of ways:

♦ Promotional products or logoed items that enhance meetings, seminars and conferences, such as attendee bags, name badges, awards, speaker gifts, table favors and logoed apparel.

♦Personalized and effective direct mail marketing ideas that cut through the clutter, getting you appointments with prospects and strengthen relationships with existing accounts.

♦ Trade show booth signage, giveaways, prizes, and promotions that drive booth traffic.

♦ Calendars and other unique promotional items that keep a company’s advertising in front of its prospects and customers all year for pennies a day.

♦ Corporate gifts for company visitors, key clients and VIPs.

♦ Employee motivation, employee recognition, safety, or wellness program gifts and giveaways to help promote the company’s initiatives.
