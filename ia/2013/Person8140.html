Anna Buckley's Summary

Recruitment Specialist in Freight Forwarding, Customs Brokerage, Logistics and Supply Chain.

Over 22 years experience serving Industry within Management, Sales and Operations positions.

Buckley Search Inc is an associate member of CIFFA, CSCB and IE Canada.

Our success is built on going beyond the norm to serve employers and candidates.
