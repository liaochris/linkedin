Chantal Vanderhaeghen's Summary

Chantal Vanderhaeghen has been passionately involved in health wellness since 1996. She is a trained meditation teacher and has developed her own natural skin care range, Franc Essential.

Chantal’s life purpose is to use her experience, passion, honesty and humour to support and encourage you to find peace, calm and clarity into your everyday life. She believes that creating clarity in your thoughts is a great way to bringing you back to what you want: peace of mind, freedom, fun, laughter, time, productivity, and quality of life.

Chantal Vanderhaeghen's Experience

MD

Thought Clarity

2012 – Present (1 year)

Chantal’s life purpose is to use her experience, passion, honesty and humour to support and encourage you to find peace, calm and clarity into your everyday life. She believes that creating clarity in your thoughts is a great way to bringing you back to what you want: peace of mind, freedom, fun, laughter, time, productivity, and quality of life.

The workshops Chantal has created will demystify the concept of meditation as we see it and show you how to be able to manage your thoughts simply and easily and to make informed decisions. You will experience, practice, share and learn from one another on how to implement different tools to suit your own personal needs. You will learn to be in the moment and see how they will give you the freedom to lead the life you want to have, no matter how busy you are and what you do.

Chantal will share her experiences with you in a safe and quiet environment in the hills surrounded by nature. She loves having these simple tools in her life and how they have helped her create a balanced life, even when her life was in chaos and her body in intense pain.

MD

Franc Essential

January 1996 – Present (17 years 8 months)

Chantal has also created a natural skin care range, Franc Essential which will holistically restore, balance and re-energize your skin and body leaving it noticeably healthy and glowing. It will literally transform the look, feel and health of your skin. The products support our bodies’ natural ability to heal and regenerate itself.

The Franc Essential skin care range is for everyone who wants to feel naturally radiant and we passionately believe in our range that is health conscious and environmentally aware.

The fact that so many of these products fell short of their promise and often caused reactions to sensitive skin ignited a fire in Chantal. She soon found herself on an impassioned quest to find natural skincare solutions suited, initially, to Australia's unique climate - a climate that encourages outdoor enjoyment, but frequently results in dry, dehydrated-looking skin, accentuated wrinkles and lines, reduced oil gland activity and loss of elasticity.

It became clear, however, that carefully combined natural ingredients have the power to nourish and enhance skin the world around, no matter the conditions.
