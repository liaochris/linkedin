Jonah Wyn Pugh's Summary

Jonah is a film director, editor and director of photography, specialising in documentary filmmaking and branded content. Founder of production company and branding think tank Kullur TV, and co-founder of music video platform 1Take TV, Jonah specialises in online content strategy and production.

Video production clients include Bauer Media, BBC, Blondefish, British American Tobacco, DC Shoes, Eastpak, Goddard Global, Lexus, Ministry of Sound, Modball Rally, Monocle, Monster Energy, Primavera Sound, Underbelly, Winkreative and many more.

In addition to video content, Jonah has worked with a wide range of companies developing strategy for the design, specification and delivery of digital media platforms, including videos, mobile apps, online gaming and music. Clients have included design agencies, tech innovation startups and youth group marketing agencies.

Projects have included: launching brands and consulting on market placement: developing online strategies to communicate and reinforce brand values and identity; stakeholder research; developing web content; SEO; consulting on logo, mobile app and website design; and brand refresh.

Specialties: Film Production, Video Production, Online Content Production, Directing, Editing, Cinematography, Production, Content Strategy and Production, Business Strategy, Digital Strategy, Media Strategy, Branding, Corporate Real Estate Strategy and Negotiation
