Patricia Heyman's Summary

Productivity Consultant, Leadership Team Development, Executive Coach, Patricia Heyman has spent the last 25 years in leadership development as a speaker/facilitator, organizational consultant and senior executive coach.

She works with organizations in the U.S. and abroad in Europe and Russia, to help enhance leadership behaviors, develop executive management teams, increase overall productivity, strategic development and execution.

Her current focus and expertise is in the productivity and alignment of teams and organizations. She has a keen eye for assessing organizational systems, and a dynamic approach to assisting leaders in the development of themselves and their teams to enhance business results. She is an astute executive coach with an eye toward productivity, alignment, focus and results, as well as work/life balance. She also has experience in large group presentations, and has facilitated interactive and experiential seminars and corporate symposiums focused on dynamic change in leaders as well as corporate collaboration and accountability.

Specialties Include: Leadership Team Alignment, Executive Coaching, Emerging Leadership, Group Coaching on Specific Leadership Competencies
