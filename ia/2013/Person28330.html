Curtis Lotito's Summary

Received a Bachelors in Radio Television and Film from Hofstra University. Studying Creative Writing for a Masters of Fine Arts in 2014. Worked on Student productions for four years, doing everything from setup and breakdown, camera work, and lighting to directing, and producing my own pieces. Also have been worked on several freelance productions doing scouting, shooting, editing, and distribution. Have spent three years managing full time in Retail.
Have previously had two separate pieces accepted by national publications. Have published a poetry anthology titled A Thousand Winks of the Sun. Published a second book titled How to self-Publish with LuLu. In the process of editing two fictional novel manuscripts to publish.

Specialties

lighting, camera, setup, breakdown, staging, props, editing, distribution, scouting, creativity, set design, lighting design, writing, self education, problem solving, management, planning, troubleshooting, quality assurance.
