Jason Harper's Summary

A resourceful marketer with a background in social media and a love for words, I develop interactive, engagement-driven campaigns designed to compel customers who are too smart and media-savvy for the same old marketing. I enjoy building relationships with customers through conversations and content that educates, entertains, and inspires.
