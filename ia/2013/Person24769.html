Chuck Bolton's Summary

Chuck Bolton is the President of The Bolton Group, an executive assessment and development firm headquartered in Minneapolis, MN, retained by CEOs and senior executives who seek positive change in their leaders and top teams through behavioral coaching.

Chuck assists leaders to master the proven behaviors and styles that drive hard results, reinvent how they create value and optimize their unique executive brands. He assists top teams to achieve extraordinary performance and win. He has coached and consulted over 1000 executives and assessed over 100 top teams in the US, Canada, Europe and Israel. The clients he serves range from early stage companies to Fortune 15 global powerhouses, including Baxter, Boston Scientific, Covidien, CR Bard, ev3, Hewlett-Packard, Medtronic, Opus Group, United Healthcare and many more.

Chuck is the developer of Top Team Check, a proprietary assessment tool and road map for extraordinary top team performance. He is the author of Leadership Wipeout: The Story of an Executive’s Crash and Rescue, Expert Publishing, 2006 and is the author of the soon to be released, The Hole in Your Team. He is a co-author of two books in the Power of the Platform series.

A popular speaker on executive development and leadership topics, Chuck appears frequently on the NBC affiliate in Milwaukee, WTMJ, where he discusses ideas on leadership. His speaking audiences have included Fortune 500 companies, universities, professional associations and nonprofits in the US and Canada. Chuck is an instructor at the University of Minnesota, Carlson School of Management, where he created and instructs Optimizing Your Unique Executive Brand to executives and mid-level business leaders. He serves on the Council of Regents, Saint Mary's University of Minnesota, Schools of Graduate and Professional Programs, where he is Chairman, Executive Advisory Board, of the Graduate School of Business and Technology.

Specialties

"C-suite level" assessment and executive coaching, top leadership team alignment and development, assessment of emotional intelligence, leadership styles and organizational climate, executive branding

Chuck Bolton's Publications

  • Leadership Wipeout: The Story of an Executive's Crash and Rescue

    • Expert Publishing
    • November 2005
    Authors: Chuck Bolton

    Ben Stevens, a stressed out executive, knows he's rapidly losing ground in his personal and professional lives. Reluctant to leave his work, yet determined to show his family his appreciation for their gift of a ski vacation at Vail, Stevens heads to Colorado to find a way out of his malaise.

    Confident the twenty years he has hit the slopes with his buddies hasn't eroded his ability, Ben skis an experts-only double black diamond run. He suffers a nearly disastrous spill, the Wipeout of all Wipeouts, leaving him battered, humiliated and further depressed.

    Ski instructor, Ed Davis, came with the vacation package, but when Ben first saw Ed, sixty-ish and gray haired, Ben was sure he found his excuse to cut his vacation short and get back to work. Ben's resolve quickly evaporated as he learned both skiing and leadership lessons from the recently retired chief executive.

    This engaging story is based on key leadership lessons and approaches executive coach and consultant Chuck Bolton has shared with hundreds of senior leaders and top leadership teams to assist them in creating greater value for themselves, their companies and their followers. Targeted to leaders at any level who desire to be their best, Leadership Wipeout: The Story of an Executive's Crash and Rescue is certain to provide the fresh ideas, perspectives and inspiration to help you learn, lead and live in a more effective and fulfilling way.
