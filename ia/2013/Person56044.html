Issa Lopez's Summary

Bilingual Radio personality, voice over talent, actress, singer and spokesperson, Issa is a working professional with global, national, regional and local on and off-camera credits. Providing exceptional voice experience in both English and Spanish, she has been heard over-and-over across the country and around the world.
With nearly 17 years (1993 to 2011) of jingle, singing, song writing and demo experience.

Currently, she is the midday Radio host for 95.7 The Party in Denver, Colorado and has been with the Clear Channel family since September of 2005. She has had her own Morning show, an award winning afternoon drive show and is dedicated to making an impact, on air or on screen.

Specialties
