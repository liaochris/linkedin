Hurlbert Toyota's Summary

We are a Toyota & Scion automobile dealership offering sales, service, parts, accessories and more. The Hurlbert Toyota & Scion dealership is now located at 58 Calef Highway, Epping, NH at the intersection of Routes 101 & 125.
We now have all our new Sales Event specials & used Toyota Specials posted for Sales, Preowned, TCUV, Parts & Service. Hurlbert Toyota is one of your best sources for new and preowned Toyotas in the New England & New Hampshire (NH) area. Learn about Toyota's complimentary 2 year Maintenance Plan offer called Toyota Care. Visit our Women's Advice Page or our blog which offers automotive advice for women. We are happy to connect with our customers on many social media platforms such as Facebook, Myspace, Blogspot, LinkedIn, Twitter, Skype, Youtube, the Camry Effect, Google+ Circles, Google+ Business Circles and more and more! We provide new and pre-owned auto sales, service for all makes, parts and financing. Please visit us to view and test drive any new Toyota including the Avalon, Camry, Camry Hybrid, Corolla, Matrix, Matrix AWD, Prius, Prius Plugin, Prius v Wagon, Sienna, Sienna AWD, Yaris sedan or liftback, 4Runner, FJ Cruiser, Highlander, Land Cruiser, RAV4, RAV4 AWD, Sequioa, Tacoma, Tundra and Venza.. We have a great selection of Toyota Certified Used Vehicles that inlcude a 7 year / 100,000 mile warranty. On this site you will find great new car specials, pre-owned specials, finance specials, customer cash rebate information, Toyota Rental specials, service specials & parts specials. Online credit applications are offered. We offer first time buyer programs, bad or troubled credit programs, college student & graduate programs, military programs and more. We provide lending with Toyota Financial Services (TFS), credit unions and many major banks in an effort to get all applications approved. Call us, email us or stop by today! We are open on Sunday for Sales as well as Monday through Saturday for Sales, Parts & Service.
