Lea Strickland's Summary

I formed my company FOCUS Resources to provide the "business" side of founding, running, and turning around companies that have been established around great ideas, products, and technology. Companies that have the great idea need the power of the great business operations behind them.

Creating and maintaining a successful business requires that all the components, the puzzle pieces of the various functional area - finance and accounting, marketing and sales, customer service, product development...- all come together to form an integrated whole that moves the organization ahead, profitably! It also requires that the organization be FOCUSED on executing the right things at the right time and understanding what those things are.

When you don't know the accounting, the legal, the human resources, and all those details or how to find out what you don't know, the "details" make putting the big picture together challenging if not impossible. Or if you do assemble the picture it takes more time, money, and effort than it needed to AND you may not be making as much money as you could have.

I've also dedicated the business to making sure the clients learn how to do the important things and the details themselves. It is important for them to become self-sufficient on running the business with me and the company as a resource IF or WHEN needed. Again, making the business more efficient and effective and profitable.

The goals are to keep growing and expanding. The business is now throughout North America with consulting clients and world-wide with the newsletters and other intellectual property products.

The other goal is to keep it meaningful and enjoyable. Doing what you love to do makes all the difference!
