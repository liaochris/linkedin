Michael DeHart's Summary

With a background in Producing, Project Management and Content Development, Mike is passionate about non-profits and excited about finding creative ways to integrate philanthropic philosophies into existing for-profit business models.

His goal with socially responsible strategies is to drive new traffic, improve corporate culture and foster company pride, all while increasing a business' exposure and profits.

Mike is borderline obsessed with new media and harnessing emerging technologies. He loves working with dedicated people and supplying them with all the tools necessary to make a project the best it can be.

Growing up in the rural town of Cincinnatus, NY, Mike was instilled with the idea that you can do anything with the resources around you as long as you get a little creative.
