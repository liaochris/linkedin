Boudewijn (Baudouin) Piscaer's Summary

See UPDATED www.univerde.nl
Boudewijn Piscaer is a professional "Intrapeneur" specialized in international market development with mineral based products.
His field went from refractory bricks to monolithic refractory (high temperature) concretes to civil concrete.
He knows most of the heavy industries, being his customers (Iron & Steel, Cement, Glass, Ceramics, Petro-Chem, Non ferrous metals etc.)
His geografical area of activity has been western Europe, North America, Caribbean, Japan, Taiwan and Australia.
His experience is now being applied for a sustainable society that merges the interests of economics, social and environmental concerns according to verifyable and transparent ethics.

His goal is:
- to act in the principles of sustainability in a dynamic way,
- to surface best Sustainable Practice/Performance, verified for credibility, then multiply the success for a more sustainable and constructive society,
- to find the right balance in Public-Private-Partnership ventures,
- to reduce man made poverty (in the larger sense) in and with construction materials and their applications, on a global scale,
- to create a better environment for entrapeneurs and intrapeneurs by innovating in business models,
- to transform civil concrete into a high tech material as has been realized with refractory concrete, involving all levels of human competence,
- to reduce the environmental impact of concrete by applying proven practice:
- reduce the 2,8 Billion CO2 emissions produced by the cement industry by 1/2 .
- to ensure that all stakeholders will become prosperous by the move from volume to quality in favour of the (end) customer
- create strategic alliances, especially with China.

Specialties: Taking R & D results and transform them into I & I results (Innovation Implementations), on an interim base, but world-wide.
