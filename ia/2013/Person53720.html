Houston Curtis' Summary

Former MTV network executive and Creator of niche-driven multi-million dollar entertainment brands, Houston Curtis has been called "a Visionary" and "trail-blazer" by his peers. As President and CEO of Legend Group Studios, Houston brings with him over 20 years of success and experience as a Producer, Showrunner, Direct Marketing expert, independent Label President, Advisor, and Speaker. Curtis's previous posts as a Founder and CEO have included start-ups Backyard Wrestling Inc., Xtreme Entertainment Group and Big Vision Entertainment, collectively grossing over 100 million dollars in revenues since their 1999, 2001 and 2003 inception. Additionally, Houston has created and executive produced shows for MTV, CBS, A&E, GSN, G4, DirecTV and Warner Brothers, among others.

Specialties: Film & Television production specializing in alternative/reality and documentary programming; Animation; Independent Features; home video distribution via retail, PPV and digital channels; infotainment/infomercial production; independent film production/aquisition & distribution. Member of PGA - Producers Guild of America and ATAS - Academy of Television Arts & Sciences

Houston Curtis' Projects

  • Ultimate Blackjack Tour

    • January 2005 to January 2007
    Team Members: Houston Curtis, Jon Moonves, Sam korkis, Rob George, Roberto Cardenas, Jeff Kelly, Michael French, Sanford Millar, Anthony Curtis

    The UBT aired for 2 seasons on CBS and during its run was the highest rated gaming series on television. (including WSOP and WPT). The format was built to encourage new players to learn a new game, (Elimination Blackjack) and play online. Sadly, the gaming bill destroyed the financial model the show was built on and ultimately the reason for the series going off the air at the end of season two. We were already in production on season 3 when we were forced to cancel. I was very proud of the show. It was a challenge to produce and the final product was a lot of fun to watch.

  • The Aruba Poker Classic

    • September 2006 to October 2006
    Team Members: Houston Curtis, Sam Korkis, Roberto Cardenas, Rob George, Lori Buckley

    Executive Produced the 2006 Aruba Poker Classic, the first year UB broke away from the WPT. The special aired on GSN and was amongst the highest rated poker shows on the network. We were very proud at our ability to bring a much more sophisticated look to the show than in previous seasons of WPT.

  • Wrestling Society X

    • January 2006 to March 2007
    Team Members: Houston Curtis, Kevin Kleinrock, Sam korkis, Vampiro, Rob George

    The most critically acclaimed wrestling series to emerge in the past 3 decades, WSX aired for one season on MTV. Only 9 out of 10 episodes aired as the season finale was hailed "too controversial" by the MTV execs. "WSX will join Freaks & Geeks, Undeclared, Firefly and other TV greats in the pantheon of shows American's were too stupid to get!" - Derek Burgan of F4Wonline.com
