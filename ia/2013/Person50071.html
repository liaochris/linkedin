Greg Clowminzer's Summary

Seek consulting, training or direct hire opportunities in the areas of coaching, management and leadership development.

Greg Clowminzer is a pioneering business and leadership coach with over 10,000 hours of real time coaching experience. Greg coaches and consults with CEOs, small business owners and professionals, helping them clarify their vision, create unique strategies for success and develop the necessary skills to be a leader and effectively exercise leadership as their natural self-expression.

Coaching is a highly interactive, one-on-one communication process where we work together to address issues, solve problems and achieve specific goals. It encompasses all of the elements of successful entrepreneurship such as leadership, business planning, marketing, sales, human resources, organizational structure, technology and team building. As your coach, I will help you identify a vision for your business, work with you to create a plan for achieving that vision, and provide support, information, resources and tools you can use to implement that plan.

Exceptional performance doesn't happen by accident. It takes a lot of practice and a heavy investment in fine tuning the key skills required to break the mold and achieve greatness.

Working with a professional coach is a powerful and highly effective way of sharpening your skills and substantially enhancing your performance. No longer reserved for the most senior people in organizations, increasingly it has been found to assist managers at all levels to deliver excellent results.

My coaching approach provides a stable balance between business coaching and personal coaching through an on-going process of focused inquiry, strategizing and the execution on an action plan.
