Hans Laroche's Summary

I have been active in marketing and the communications industry for over 25 years. After 12 years in the retail sector, notably as Director promotion and loyalty program at Provigo and Heritage supermarkets, I made the move to the ad agency side as VP Strategic Planning and then VP General Manager of the Carlson Marketing Group, an international firm specializing in direct marketing, loyalty programs and relationship marketing.

I have been invited on many occasions as guest speaker in the field of Marketing, CRM and Loyalty for major Canadian associations such as Administrative Sciences Association of Canada, Canadian Marketing Association, American Marketing Association, "Institut International de Recherche" and Canada Post Corporation, and have been a member of the Canadian Committee of the Air Miles program for 2 years.

I have been teaching for the past 20 years at the University of Montreal (École des Hautes Études Commerciales), UQAM and at the University of Sherbrooke, Business Masters program.

Specialties: Strategic planning, retail marketing, relationship marketing and advertising.
