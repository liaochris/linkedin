Francois Hotte's Summary

After 3 years of Sales and Marketing studies at the Chamber of Commerce of Nantes, I had the chance to develop my technical skills in the Internet industry by working for a sales and technical support in Holland at Active 24.

I then continued to develop these technical skills (domain name registrations, web hosting, email hosting) within my role of Support specialist at Group NBT, London.

A year later, once I was able to understand completely the market, I've succeeded to access the position of Marketing Manager. I developed the online marketing and social media strategy of the group and helped in some of their graphic design needs thanks to a two-week Web Design training with London College and eight years of passion.

I then moved back to France to start new challenges in the fashion industry for VeryWear.

My goals are to keep on improving my skills in the Internet Industry, Social Media, expand my business network and deliver quality projects.
