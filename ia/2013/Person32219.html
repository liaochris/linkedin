David Mamber's Summary

I'm a web and mobile application developer in Toronto, Ontario. I can handle all aspects of a web and/or mobile application from back-end to front-end tasks.

I'm one of the co-founders of StreetConcepts Inc. which is a media/agency company that caters mostly to the automotive industry. One of our most popular services is our online magazine.

I also developed a complete Point of Sale software for restaurants utilizing the iPad. MobileTouch POS. Some features include inventory and product management, floor plan designer, reporting, send orders to various thermal printers and more. This product is currently being used by a select few clients and is now closed to the public.

Recently I have just launched a new start-up Streetkred. This is an application that offers car show promoters the ability to manage various aspects of their show from registration, judging, awards and more. This is available online at http://getstreetkred.com
