Karen Moyse. KineticFuture's Summary

Hello. I am the founder of KineticFuture which has been in business since 2000. We specialise in leadership communications coaching - running courses for teams around the world.

Being an exceptional communicator is now a prerequisit for effective leadership and building strong business performance. This is the specialist work we do with leadership teams at key 'tipping points' for the business and careers.

We work at a senior level for companies including Mars Inc, Reckitt Benckiser, Amgen, AstraZeneca, GSK, Tui, Fox Channels International, H+K Strategies, Nikon & BLLP lawyers with a repeat assignment rate of 99%.

Here is an idea of the work we do:

* We prepare leadership teams to give mission critical presentations internally - ensuring they have the appropriate levels of gravitas & personal presence and can deliver audience impact. Plus we make sure they overcome the psychological barriers to being exceptional.

* Our new programme 'Leading for Dynamic Resilience' - ensures leaders lead others so teams can bounce back, bounce forward in tough economic times and outperform the competition.

* We work with sales teams on their customer facing behaviour - helping them shorten complex sales cycles by the way they build their relationship as a trusted advisor and how they adapt through the sales process.

What will you find different if you work with us?

*Our integrated modular approach works - getting you the results you want quickly
*We don't do dull. Our approach is high energy, experiential, practical, personal, and imaginative
*We understand business and are senior business people ourselves
*We are communications specialists focussed on leadership teams.
*Our work is evidence based and our specialist coaches have different specalist skills that you can draw on - consultative selling, theatre direction, positive psychology at work, collaborative team building, psychometrics, media, communications strategy and corporate story telling.
