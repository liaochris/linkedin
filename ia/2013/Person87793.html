Mike Martins' Summary

Mike has worked as a design professional for the past 15 years, focusing on the development of renewable solutions for the marketing industry. As Senior Creative Director, Mike led the in-house design team at Yellowstone Club, overseeing the creative process for all marketing and real estate efforts. As VP of Creative for Outlaw Partners, Mike focuses on developing innovative marketing solutions while piloting branding platforms and design standards for specific consumer profiles.

Skilled in web development, email campaigns, graphics, illustration and photography, Mike's graphic design initiatives have led many of his clients to reduce the environmental impact of marketing collateral. Mike holds a double degree in Visual Communication Design and Art Education from Purdue University. When he is not designing, you'll find Mike snowboarding, biking or hiking in the mountains.
