Linda Seguin's Summary

Virginia Select provides hospitality, retail and service-related businesses with an array of industry-specific marketing tools designed to improve the bottom line without damaging the budget.

Since the company was founded in 2004, CEO Linda Seguin has gone above and beyond in helping her business owner clients optimize their marketing through Virginia Select’s exceptional product offerings and business-building connections.

To maintain visibility in the community and foster new relationships, Linda and the Virginia Select staff are members of several high-profile boards, work closely with networking organizations and actively participate in a variety of trade shows during which promoting local businesses and attractions to travelers, residents and those who serve them is the only focus.

Thanks to this ongoing legwork, Virginia Select is adept at facilitating face-to-face introductions and arranging for referrers to personally experience your offerings – all with the purpose of providing you with a complete traffic-driving system powered, in large part, by the relationships we foster.

Specialties

Innovative Traffic-Driving Products & Programs: Discovery Map Richmond, City Select Menus, Customer Loyalty and Referral Gift Certificates, Relationship-Building Support, Rack Card Distribution, Digital & Traditional Marketing Solutions for Restaurants, Attractions, Retail Businesses and Service Professionals, Travel & Tourism Industry Insights

Linda Seguin's Experience

President/CEO

Virginia Select

2004 – Present (9 years) Richmond, Virginia Area

Linda brings an extensive professional background to her work in guiding the success of VA Select and its clients.

As a former teacher, human resources professional and salesperson, Linda took her talent for communication, connection and inspiring others into the hospitality arena with the purchase of her first business venture through City Select Menus. From there, Linda grew her operations, adding and selling certain territories in order to focus her effort on the Richmond community. As her company grew, she continued to add new product and service offerings that would bring business owners greater visibility and results.

Today, Linda not only manages VA Select’s operations and growth, but also works personally with every client to ensure maximum return on investment.

Linda’s business acumen, coupled with her deep caring for clients’ success, enables her to provide that rare combination of passion, vision and strategic insight that is simply invaluable for any marketing program.

Above all, Linda believes that business happens through exceptional relationship building and it’s this philosophy that drivers her continuing and active contribution to the growth and vibrancy of Richmond’s business community.
