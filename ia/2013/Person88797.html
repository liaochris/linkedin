Matthew Barksdale's Summary

I head up Engage Mobile Solutions in Kansas City, MO. We help companies leverage mobile technology and marketing to drive profitability through increasing revenue, decreasing cost and managing risk.

At Engage Mobile we have three primary areas of focus - mobile strategy, mobile development / app development and mobile marketing.

We approach the world of mobile differently than a typical app developer. From our first conversation with clients we are focused on delivering results that will improve their business – the technology is important, but it is secondary to understanding and developing a plan for mobile that will take our clients' business forward.

I am a Kansas City native focused on leveraging technology to help grow companies. After growing a family manufacturing business from $4 million to $14 million in revenue, I decided to focus on the Internet and mobile solutions. During the last 10 years I have had the opportunity to work in industries ranging from publishing to woodworking to PR and have developed over a hundred digital products utilizing dozens of business models.

I live in the Liberty, MO area with my wife, three children and two dogs, hold a BS from the Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania and an MBA from Rockhurst University.
