Helping you create a balanced lifestyle that allows you to actively live life at a healthy weight with out overwhelm.

» I have been on my own weight loss journey yo-yo dieting, losing 120 pounds of fat, gaining some weight back and reaching my goal weight again.  I struggled with sticking to an exercise routine and proper nutrition until I figured out how it all connected.

I found "short-cuts" to help me make the proper decisions while I struggled with lack of support, losing friends, gaining friends and finding who I truly am.  I was tired of being exhausted to the point of not being able  to enjoy life and actively participate in activities with my family and friends.
 
My passion is helping you do the same. I help those who are overweight and overwhelmed naturally lose weight while creating a balanced lifestyle that allows them to actively live life. I stick to my values and priorities and help you find yours as well; I will not do something that does not fit with them or recommend something that I do not believe in. 

Working with me will be an organic process as things develop and transpire through your journey.  I avoid supplements and anything that is man-made as one should be able to receive all the vitamins and nutrients their body needs through proper nutrition.

Weight loss will occur through exercise, proper nutrition, proper sleep habits and learning to love yourself rather than through pills, diets, or "special" foods...there is not a magic pill or button that will help you succeed other than your own weight loss process.
