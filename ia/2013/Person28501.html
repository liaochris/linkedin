Cyndi Lesinski's Summary

Cyndi is a dedicated, full-time real estate sales professional with nearly 15 years experience in the Los Angeles real estate market. Known for her savvy negotiation skills, she provides excellent personal and online marketing services.

With an impeccable track record Cyndi draws upon her extensive background in social work to help clients achieve their goals. Whether handling re-locations, short-sales, downsizing or move up buyers, Cyndi has earned her reputation as a caring, enthusiastic and dynamic individual. When it comes to choosing a Realtor®, Cyndi Lesinski is among the top with regard to values, integrity & service.

Cyndi holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Sociology & Social Work. She specializes in high end properties, entry level homes, complicated family situations, disabilities and life experiences which impact the real estate process. Cyndi is consistently out thinking, out planning, out marketing and out performing! Call or refer Cyndi today.
