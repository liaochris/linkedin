Jon Akland's Summary

Jon Akland has served in creative roles with magazines, schools, small businesses and organizations, working on brand development, strategy, design and production. He worked in-house at publishing companies and nonprofits for a decade; most recently, he led the design and publishing efforts of the Boston Society of Architects and ArchitectureBoston magazine. His other creative pursuits include brewing hoppy beer and being a father of two.

Jon has a BA in environmental studies from University of Vermont and a BFA in graphic design from Massachusetts College of Art and Design.
