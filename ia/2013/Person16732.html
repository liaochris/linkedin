Artist, Poet, Creative-at-Large

Brenda Stevens Fanning

1986 – Present (27 years) Athens, Georgia Area

I have always been an artist. I drew from a very young age, and devoured paint-by-numbers kits and art technique books. In high school, I had several years' worth of public school art classes, during which I developed a particular interest in oil and pastel portraiture.

I also always had a camera, although never a lesson in photography. My creative instincts always guided me in getting some great shots. My photography is available online at Imagekind.

I am once again endeavoring to paint somewhat regularly, and I am also acquiring a fondness for acrylics. I am constantly learning, and meeting artists who are very generous with their knowledge.

In addition, I occasionally have poetic and journaling fits, sometimes resulting in something pretty remarkable. Several years ago, I filed copyright on a collection of emotionally charged poetry.
