A.J. Vasaris' Summary

What I Do:
For over 25 years I've been a professional adviser in the areas of business, technology and project management. The name of my present company is Value Management Partners, an innovative consultancy dedicated to great business ideas and solutions, improving technology projects and creating process and workflow improvement in organizations. We do that through project assessments, brainstorming sessions, systems designs, and mentoring, as well as through project coaching and facilitation. I help all types of organizations reduce costs and increase productivity by improving project, process and business performance.

I write about what we do and how we garner our project, technology, and business management thinking for our Project, Process and Business Improvement blog. I try to show how we create, innovate, design and think about all we do. I also try to find up and coming thought leaders and reference them in my blog posts.


How I Help:
I help small to medium sized organization of all types build a culture of project thinking, business creativity and technology innovation so they can consistently achieve growth and solve the problems that are most important to them. I use techniques that I've garnered working in various industries and fields, improving the efficiency and effectiveness of the processes that run the business.


And in between innovative thinking, creative processes, and managing all those projects, I write about my avocation in futures trading and thoughts on the financial markets...oh my!
