Alexsandra Stewart's Summary

Alexsandra (Alex) Stewart is a Portland, OR real estate broker and Residential Specialist focused on relationships with sellers and buyers who seek superior service and attention in the buying and selling process.

Born in Portland OR, she has also lived in southern Oregon, South Dakota, Minnesota, Germany, Montana, Minnesota again and Washington, DC before returning to Portland. Her formal education includes: Masters in Human Development, St. Mary's College, Winona MN, 1981-1984 Bachelor of Arts and Bachelor of Fine Arts, University of Minnesota, Minneapolis, MN 1976 to 1979, Washington HS, Sioux Falls SD

Alex is also an artist, traveler, dreamer, reader, and gardener in no priority order. She returned to her hometown of Portland in 2004 after a career as a diversity and organizational-development consultant, helping international and domestic businesses and nonprofit organizations weather the process of change.

Her advanced real estate education includes the designations: Certified Residential Specialist (CRS); Certified Negotiation Expert (CNE); Certified Distressed Property Expert (CDPE); Graduate Realtor Institute (GRI);Historic Certified Realtor; International Real Estate Specialist (IRES); Seniors Real Estate Specialist (SRES); Certified Home Marketing Specialist (CHMS),Earth Advantage Sustainable Trained Real Estate (EA. STAR) and At Home With Diversity (AHWD)

Specialties

Sellers and buyers agent, residential and vacation homes in the United States and Internationally. Certified Residential Specialist (CRS), Certified Distressed Property Expert (CDPE), International Real Estate SPecialist (IRES), Seniors Real Estate Specialist (SRES).

Consult with individuals in the selling and buying of personal first and second residences as well as assisting them to build wealth through investing in real estate.
