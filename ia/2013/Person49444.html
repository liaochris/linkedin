Joan Damico's Summary

As a communications strategist my goal is to help you leverage communications to increase awareness, elicit response or provide clarity, whether corporate communications or marketing communications. Both art and science, communications is about you and me collaborating to create the right message for your intended audience. Then, using my industry experience and communications expertise, I'll apply a combination of communications strategies and tactics aimed at reaching your audience and achieving a measurable communications objective.

Corporate and internal communications that engage and inspire the workforce

I'll help you provide context around your communications, linking colleague work achievements to broader business unit and corporate goals. My corporate / internal communications involve more than creating engaging communications; they engage colleagues by communicating when and where they work. Clients have sought my enterprise social media expertise to help reach their target audiences, while breaking down organizational silos. Applying social media tactics, I enable colleagues to build online communities, share knowledge faster and better leverage colleague expertise.

Marketing communications that generate and nurture leads, drive sales, and retain customers

B2B technology and industrial clients have hired me because I can distill varied and complex concepts into a unique selling proposition and messaging. Then, applying integrated marcom principles, I help them convey their message consistently while leveraging the advantages of each communications medium, whether online—and regardless of device—or offline. My integrated marketing communications help prospects find my clients. As a B2B copywriter I know how to write for results from on-page SEO and social media optimization.
