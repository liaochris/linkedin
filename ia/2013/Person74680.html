Kerstin Tracy's Summary

My Experience:
Over 20 years of experience in the health and wellness field, studied and trained in Germany Sport Science and as well as Alternative Health.

I am a licensed and nationally certified massage therapist specialized in CranioSacral Therapy which I studied at the Upledger Institute in Florida.

I am trained in Equine Craniosacral through Maureen Rogers in California.

Worked with athletes as a research assistant and in physical therapy offices.
Now working with athletes in my own practice.

My Goals:
I help humans and horses recover from injuries, prevent injuries and reach their peak performance.

My goal is for humans and horses to stay calm under pressure, to know their bodies' abilities, to believe in themselves at all times, to recover from injuries smoothly and find long-term solutions in a natural and long-term way.

Specialties: CranioSacral Therapy (CST), CST for pediatrics, Myofascial Release, Reiki (2nd level), Equine CranioSacral, aromatherapy and Essential Oils
