Pelle Elikofer's Projects

  • JELD-WEN

    • May 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Pelle Elikofer

    Challenge
    JELD-WEN had to redefine their use of digital platform when launching their new brand and merger of a number of independent European brands. The goal was to make a common platform that would support different target groups and segments. The main objective was to get a broader reach and inspire homeowners and architects.

    Solution
    A major analysis followed by workshops resulted in a digital strategy that was aligned with management and across market stakeholders. The new site features a product database that houses a very complex data structure that differs from market to market. This allows us to create and present any product matrix wanted, using an outside-in approach, putting us way ahead of competition and being ready for e-commerce.

    The site is build to inspire users and features a design tool, galleries, magazines and articles and all images with products are linked so users can go directly form any image or page to the product that they see.

    Result
    The site has gone from being very product driven to being an inspiring place to visit. Users are engaging and statistics show that users who at some point of their visit uses the Design Tool spend a record +12 minutes on the site and view +16 pages more than the average user.

    About JELD-WEN
    Door and window manufacturer JELD-WEN was established in Oregon, USA in 1960. Today, JELD-WEN has more than 150 production sites and approximately 20,000 employees. JELD-WEN's European organisation is headquartered in Løgstør, Denmark- Its turnover in Europe is around DKK 6 billion.
