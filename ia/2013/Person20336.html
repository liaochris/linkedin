Carol Elaine Smith's Summary

Top-performing global Organizational Effectiveness Consultant with history achieving ROI goals, leading transformations, driving performance and organizational capability. Has a passion for helping clients make paradigm shifts that support new behaviors underpinning culture changes, new business models, increased productivity, organizational alignment. Demonstrated ability to minimize business disruptions during change, keep attention on internal/external customers, coach leaders on sustaining results, increase team functioning. An accomplished listener who partners closely with clients to understand their business and requirements for success. Demonstrated ability to think systemically and to design, develop and implement business transformation programs. Deeply experienced with Big 4, boutique and independent consulting; SAP, PeopleSoft and Oracle Change Management; and multiple industries, government and non-profits. Clients regard Carol as their “transformation doctor”….able to enter at any project life-cycle phase and add value immediately.
