David Levin's Summary

David Levin brings to his colleagues and clients a unique combination of talent and experience.

An award-winning creative director, marketing / communications strategist, executive speech writer, and Chief Marketing Officer, David’s work building breakthrough brand experiences has earned coverage in The New York Times, The Wall Street Journal, and Fast Company.

David has served as a commentator for National Public Radio’s All Things Considered and has appeared on MTV as well as CNBC’s PowerLunch.

A former management consultant and creative agency owner, David’s diverse background drives his unique ability to bring clarity and creativity to complex subjects.

Prior to joining Buckingham Asset Management as Chief Marketing Officer, David worked with a wealth of organizations including:
