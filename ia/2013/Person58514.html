Jan Marie Dore's Summary

Jan Marie Dore, ‘The Professional Women’s Success Coach’, is a Master Certified Coach, a women small business coaching expert. She is the founder of Femalepreneurs.com, a business coaching and training company to meet the special needs of solo-professional women. She provides professional mentoring for women entrepreneurs and service professionals worldwide.

Jan Marie is known internationally as a mentor to coaches and other professional women, a speaker, author, workshop facilitator, and retreat leader who teaches women small business owners and independent service professionals how to attract more clients, make more money, have more freedom, and take more time off.

Jan Marie has been interviewed or quoted as an expert on small business coaching and women’s small business issues in the media, including radio, magazines, and professional journals and has written numerous articles that have reached audience across the globe.
