Andrew Cohen's Summary

Entrepreneur and Consultant primarily focused on Telecommunications Services, Hardware, and IT Technology. Organized ATEL Telco Services into a global sales and marketing organization. One of the early adopters of Cloud Based Hosted Telephone Systems, we now have our own branded Class 5 Switch based VoIP System cloudconnectphone.com. I built a strong management team and developed a broad strategic direction for new products and services to meet our customers needs. A competitive self starter who encourages creative problem solving.

Visionary leader - insatiable problem solver with proven ability to get inside of an issue, adopt multiple perspectives, devise smart strategies, guide executive decision-making, and mobilize the right resources to drive achievement of mission-critical initiatives delivering excellence in challenging markets. Exceptional communicator with negotiation, problem resolution, and client needs assessment skills. Equally effective at identifying opportunities, developing focus, and providing tactical business solutions.
