Elizabeth E George, M.A.'s Summary

- Founder of Compatibility Solutions Inc., a North American based firm focused on guiding individuals to improve front end decisions in partnership choices that influence marriage and employment success.

- A relationship expert, international keynote speaker, and co-author of "The Compatibility Code, An Intelligent Woman’s Guide to Dating and Marriage." The Code is designed to help women who are re-entering the dating scene make better choices in order to find a love that will last a lifetime.

- Previous roles include certified Small Business Consultant for the U.S. Small Business Administration; Director - Bessemer Business Incubator; worked with state and national economic development and incubator organizations; university professor in both the U.S. and Canada.

- Named to 1999 “Top 40 Business Executive Under the Age of 40” Birmingham Business Journal.

- Operated a bed & breakfast, featured in Southern Living; and consults as a specialist in tourism operations and marketing.

- Elizabeth earned an MA in Human Resource Management from the University of Alabama where she also taught as an adjunct professor, and a BA in Marketing from East Tennessee State University.

- Competed for five years with the U.S. International Shooting Team and was World Champion team in Crossbow. She was also awarded the coveted Distinguished International Shooter Badge, instituted by the United States Government as its highest award for marksmanship excellence.

Specialties: Professional Relationships, Marital Relationships, marital compatibility, prefixing an idea/decision before implementation
