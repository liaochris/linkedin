Joe Michaud's Summary

I began my career on the content side of newspapers and magazines before moving to the business side of online media in 1995, when I helped launch MaineToday.com. Affiliated with three newspapers and a TV station, MaineToday was recognized as a pioneer in original online content, community engagement and successful online business models, while also creating new approaches for cross-media collaboration.

Since 2008, I have been working with media companies and entrepreneurs as a business coach and consultant. In 2011-12, I was brought on as one of three coaches of the experimental SuperCamp program for U.S. local news entrepreneurs, funded by the Knight and Patterson foundations. The success of that program led Knight to fund an expansion of the program in 2013 to add four more coaches, working with 35+ sites.

I volunteer as a mentor in Maine's Top Gun entrepreneur training program, and have my own startup, ConcertRat.com.

Joe Michaud's Projects

  • Real-Time Maine Election Results

    • November 1998 to November 2008
    Team Members: Joe Michaud, Tim Archambault, Nikki Schroeher, Mark Woodward, Jason Scott

    Developed the technology and the collaboration amongst Maine media outlets to broadcast near-real time election results to most all Maine media outlets: TV, newspaper,radio, & Associated Press utilizing a call center and Internet-based backoffice to gather election results for the entire state of Maine and then push the data automatically to local, state and national (via Associated Press partnership) websites. Created a tremendous strategic advantage for the Bangor Daily News in election coverage.
