Linda Blakemore's Summary

lblakemore@apgsearch.com


I have 27 years of experience in the staffing and recruiting industry. I started my own boutique search firm, Atlantic Pacific Group, Inc. 15 years ago. We provide high quality search and consulting services for our clients. We pride ourselves on strong client and candidate relationships. I feel fortunate to connect with with professionals in my chosen industry niche even when the end result is not a placement. Connecting with high caliber professionals is our passion. We recruit HR, Accounting and Finance, IT, Customer Service, Marketing and Engineering and Scietific Professionals.

We also provide long term contract professionals in HR and Accounting.

Most Recent Project:

In our most recent project I developed a recruitment function to support a large privately owned mortgage and real estate company. Primary objectives were the development of an overall recruitment strategy to support multiple business entities. Accomplishments include quadrupling the workforce in four years, recruitment of a best in class recruiting team of 17 professionals and recruitment of key professionals in IT, Legal, Accounting, Sales and Real Estate and Human Resources.

Our Specialty:

Tough to fill executive search assignments where you need "true headhunting talent", large scale recruitment project management, recruitment process consulting.

As the owner of Atlantic Pacific Group, Inc. I personally manage each and every search. I have developed a strong network of independant recruiters who help to expand and support my recruiting efforts. I build strong relationships with my clients. I believe in sending only a few strong candidates who have the right fit. On the candidate side, I believe that match and opportunity are the most important aspects. I prefer to work with candidates that are looking for their next career opportunity, not just a job. I may not place every candidate I speak to but I am committed to giving people ideas and input that will help them to find the very best opportunity.

I have specific expertise in hard to fill executive search assignments, large scale recruitment project management and recruitment process development.
