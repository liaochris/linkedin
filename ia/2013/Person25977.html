Christopher Norman Howard's Summary

My professional experience consists of a diverse background of employment opportunities and training geared to support my career interests as a public health and financial management professional, and serving as a community advocate. I've always been driven to elminate health disparities that exist around the globe, addressing this issue through research on socioeconomic influences such as education, race, culture, income, micro environments, gender, and genetic dipositions.

My professional experience includes positions with Oakwood University, Texas Health and Human Services Commission, U.S. Government Accountability Office (GAO), The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), DeKalb County Government, Emory University, The City of Hunstville (AL), Adventist Health Systems, The Young Men's Christian Association (YMCA), and The City of South Bend.

My professional experience also includes a number of volunteer experiences in communities across the globe. I've worked as missionary in Central America, fed the homeless, provided strategic planning and development support for non-profit organizations, led fundraising events, and provided training for community health prevention programs.

Specialties: Health Care, Public Health, Financial Management, Public Policy, Project Management, Strategic Planning, Development, Marketing, Leadership, Fundraising
