Michael D. Davidson's Summary

Intense, hands-on leader, problem-solver, entrepreneur and change agent with track record of growth, improved revenue, cash flow and EBITDA, and improved valuations. I challenge people to think strategically, improve processes, innovate and execute, focusing on value added activities and eliminating mediocrity. Chief Financial Officer (later assumed CEO duties as well)

October 2010 – May 2012 (1 year 8 months)

Company is a specialty and ethnic food distributor with revenues of $65MM-$85MM, has 300+ employees, and customers throughout New England and New York. Directly managed all departments, including sales and field operations, warehouse, finance, HR and IT.

Grew revenue approximately $10 million, or 15%, in 20 months by revamping finance, sales and operational processes that were impeding growth and profitability, creating a new pricing methodology leading to a potential doubling of profit, and changing a stagnant culture to a culture focused on customer satisfaction, strategic thinking and organizational improvement.

Automated financial reporting and other operational processes, implemented financial policies, procedures and controls for more accurate and efficient financial reporting. Company books were not auditable when started; one year later led the company through its first audit with a clean opinion. Also evaluated and implemented new enterprise and payroll processing software.

• Reduced inventory levels 10% while focusing on having mission critical parts on-hand
• Assisted on negotiations with the company's senior lender
• Worked with vendors to maintain supply availability

• Automated reporting processes, and created procedures to analyze financial results

Overall result was to stabilize the company, allowing it to continue operating during difficult economic times, reverse sales slide in Parts and Service, automate and strengthen financial reporting, and give control back to the owners, who actively participated in the turnaround's success. Problems solved.
