Mr.Shin of Michi's Summary

We have been active in the catering and restaurant business for over 35 years. We are proud to say that we have some of the biggest clients spread all across the Bay Area and we've been providing them with extraordinary services. Ensuring our clients that our catering and presentation is the best around. With that said, it includes high quality ingredients, international cuisines, and creative menus. Lastly, we also have the ability to work within a budget while still meeting our clients' needs.

In addition to our Live Sushi Bar, we also offer Tuna cutting, BBQ Stations, corporate team buildings, etc. These events are applicable to any group size; large or small, personal or corporate. We also have worked closely with other local wineries; Byington Winery, Mountain Winery, Thomas Fogarty, and many more.
