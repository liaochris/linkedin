Ann Butera's Summary

Ann M. Butera diagnoses organizational culture, which enables Executives to increase operational bench strength, and improves efficiency and effectiveness. An experienced organizational change agent, she capitalizes on her understanding of current business challenges, future trends, and changing demographics to craft viable solutions and business strategies. She delivers understandable, high impact, memorable, how-to training to increase sales, and service, and achieve measurable results. She asks the tough, thought-provoking questions that help her clients identify exposures, focus on priorities, and address issues that block organizational readiness.

Specialties: She has a special focus on internal auditing, operational risk management, and sales, and is able to deliver compelling technical training in the following areas:
Sales and Cross-selling, Internal Auditing, Issue Development and Report Writing, Change Management, Conflict Resolution, Corporate Governance, Control Self-assessments, Enterprise Risk Management, Negotiation Skills, Organizational Culture Assessment, Project Management, Quality Assessment Reviews (QAR), and Strategic Planning.
