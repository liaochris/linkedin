Scott Brown's Summary

Scott Brown has been providing James Brown Plumbing Heating & Air Conditioning, LLC and it’s customers with environmentally friendly and alternative comfort systems for over ten years.


James Brown Plumbing Heating & Air Conditioning, LLC has been a family owned and operated business for the past 30 years. Our trained and certified team has provided the Chester County and the surrounding area with prompt, courteous service with custom estimation, design, and installation of heating and air conditioning systems for residential and commercial spaces. We take great pride in providing innovative design, clean and professional installations, and customized solutions for our clients. We service equipment that uses fuel oil, natural and propane gas, as well as heat pumps, humidifiers, air cleaners, air filtration systems and ultraviolet lights. At James Brown, we are committed to creating environmentally conscious heating and air conditioning systems for a more sustainable world. This is our degree of difference.

Specialties

Radiant Heat/Cool, Ground Source Heat Pump Systems, Chilled Water Systems, Water Treatment/Filtering, Oil/Gas Service and Installation, Comfort Control Systems, AquaPEX Plumbing Systems, Steam & Hydronic Boiler Service, NFPA13D Fire Safety Installation and service and Installation, Mini-Split Ductless Service and Installation, Tankless Hot Water Service and Installation, Solar Systems
