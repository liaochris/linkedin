Anne Holmes' Summary

Photographer, journalist, videographer and multimedia producer specialising in social and humanitarian issues in post-conflict zones and developing nations. Anne Holmes has worked on projects in Iraq, Iran, Afghanistan, Turkey, Kenya, Chad, Thailand, Cambodia, India, Japan, China, Sri Lanka.

Her work has been published by The New York Times, The Ford Foundation, The Montreal Gazette, Reuters, Internazionale, Handicap International, The International Labor Organization, Room to Read, Monocle, Silvershotz, Ekstrabladet, IRIN News, The Kathmandu Post, The Globe SEA, Discover Cambodia, Le Petit Journal Cambodge, and Vice Magazine.

Currently living in Phnom Penh, Cambodia.

Corporate and creative gigs also welcome.

Specialties: Fully versed in the multimedia editing program Final Cut Pro and Adobe Premiere Pro.

Equipped to record professional quality sound, photo, HD video (Canon 5D Mark II, Leica M8, Roland Edirol R-09)

Excellent English writing, research and editing capabilities

Speaks fluent English and French, and has a working knowledge of Spanish
