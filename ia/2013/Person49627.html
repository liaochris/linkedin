As an early Covestor employee I worked immediately under the CEO, helped define our business model, designed our system architecture, and worked with the CTO and development team to have our solution implemented. Covestor's trade replication platform has so far executed over $100M worth of stock trades.

Business Analyst

Accenture

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; ACN; Management Consulting industry

2003 – 2005 (2 years) Greater Atlanta Area

After MIT, I chose to work with Accenture because it was a major sponsor of my research lab. Here, I was exposed to numerous large manufacturers and senior executives to develop an RFID strategy, perform cost-benefit analysis, and identify RFID opportunities beyond retailer and Department of Defense mandate compliance. I was also the lead inventor on a concept that Accenture and our client at the time Wrigley jointly patented.
