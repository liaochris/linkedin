Laura Nack, LCSW, ACC, BCC's Summary

Laura Nack, LCSW, CLC, ACC, BCC is a leadership and life coach with 30 years professional experience in behavioral health care and employee assistance services, consulting and coaching. Laura is passionate about helping professionals create an effective personal style of leadership, while achieving greater balance and fulfillment in their lives. She strongly believes that work-life balance is a critical component to career satisfaction. Laura partners with her clients to maximize their strengths, learn new skills, and make the changes needed for more effective and rewarding relationships, career fulfillment and personal development.

Laura coaches her clients both in person and via the telephone so clients can be located in the NY metropolitan area or in any part of the country. For more information about Laura’s coaching services, she can be contacted at: Laura@LauraNack.com.

Specialties

Board Certified Coach, (BCC); Master Practitioner-Energy Leadership Index (iPEC); Certified Life Coach (CLC)- Institute for Life Coach Training; ACC & Member - International Coach Federation; Licensed Clinical Social Worker; Certified Imago Relationship Therapist. Expertise - Life and Career Transitions, Improving connection, communication & relationships; Increasing performance and development of strengths-based leadership skills for professionals and business executives.
