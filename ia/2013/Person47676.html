Eugene Vasconi's Summary

Our life's purpose changes over time and mine certainly has over the past few years. Having been diagnosed with bladder cancer in 2009, I decided not to be a victim. With the amazing help of my wife and many others, I have been able to keep it at bay. I also decided that other people needed to know the real story about bladder cancer and how it is creeping more and more into all our lives. So, I wrote a book entitled "For Bladder or Worse" but I didn't want it to be a "woe is me" book - I wanted it to be something entertaining that would attract interest and also give lots of helpful information. The book is published and on Amazon. I also am making myself available to speak about the disease and how to avoid it.
I also have a non-cancer life. That entails multimedia production and speaking about my other book: "Say What? Do You Really Know What You Are Communicating." It tells many of the secrets of how to get yourself on the treasure map by getting your message across well to others.
I am now concentrating on speaking venues and on helping others with my knowledge. Contact me for a great presentation or personalized session.

Specialties: Motivational and topic-oriented speaking such as "Breasts, Bladders and Bathrooms", and "Communication Is Hazardous To Your Health."
