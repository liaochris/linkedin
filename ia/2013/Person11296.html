Mark Aylward's Summary

I am an entrepreneur and talent acquisition executive with hands-on experience in client development, recruiting and training. Experienced with offline and online marketing and social media integration for business.
I have worked with Fortune 500 clients in a variety of vertical markets as well as the public sector and have worked with both start-up and mature businesses.
My management skills include strategic planning, training, operations and talent acquisition.
I possess broad knowledge spanning a variety of technology platforms and vertical markets.
My passion is changing people's lives and I've been fortunate enough to do this by helping people find new careers, by teaching others and by staying focused on service.

Specialties

Talent acquisition, training and business process improvement. Relationship based selling to businesses and consumers. Online marketing and social media strategies for business. Contract negotiation, mediation, problem resolution and relationship maintenance.
