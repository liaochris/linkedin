Jason Spiers' Summary

I have a BA in History from the Univiersity of Southern Mississippi. I have been in the textile decoration industry for 13 years with proficient experience in Graphic Design, Pre Press, Production, Workflow Management, and Upper Management. I currently own and operate JMH Graphics, a high volume screenprinting company in Hattiesburg, MS. We have been in business since 2005 and have had great growth in the Pine Belt area. Our goal is to function as South Mississippi's leading innovator in apparel decoration and production. We are currently adding Promotional Products to our catalog to offer a larger selection of promotional tools to our customers.

Specialties

CorelDraw, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Marcromedia MX 2004, Excel, Word. Highly proficient in textile artwork and seperations.
