Jenifer Mazur Anseth's Summary

M.R. Designs & Gifts ~ The "WOW Factor" Delivered (c)

Your Business Needs to Send Gifts...Our Business is Baskets.

You have more important things to do than worry about your gift giving. We specialize in the design and creation of one-of-a-kind gifts and gift baskets, made specifically for the individual recipient upon order. It's our pleasure to handle your order of any size - from one new baby Diaper Cake, to hundreds of Welcome to Arizona convention amenities, to your entire on-going client appreciation program -- all made fresh to order.


The Company You Choose is a Reflection of You.

We firmly believe that the gift we carry to your recipient is not only a reflection of our company, but a reflection of you, and must clearly portray your special sentiment - it has to be praise-worthy. We can hand deliver your special gift anywhere in the Phoenix Metro area, or carefully package your gift basket for nationwide shipping.


Our Business is Built on Your Referrals!

We strive to offer fine quality products at a reasonable price and with the great customer service that you deserve. We put our whole hearts and care into each gift basket we create. Our goal is to make you proud to have chosen M.R. Designs & Gifts to be your gift basket partner. For a company that began and continues to thrive on customer referrals, we must be doing something right! THANK YOU for recommending us to your friends!!!


Arizona Native. Arizona Company. Fine Arizona Products.

In addition, as an Arizona native-owned company, we believe in supporting local companies and have sought out a variety of Arizona made components to feature in our gift baskets. We are proud to offer the fine quality products of many Valley and Arizona companies.

We welcome the introduction to companies that produce QUALITY Arizona-made products for possible inclusion in our gift baskets.
