Lauriel Leonard's Summary

DEX is a manufacturer of sustainable precast concrete products operating out of a revitalized industrial complex implementing and utilizing environmentally responsible manufacturing practices.

With 12 years experience, DEX excels at producing custom furnishings as well as introducing our exciting new line of multidimensional wall tiles - DIMENSIONS.

Polished concrete and DEXterra™, a recycled glass terrazzo material are offered at DEX. DEXterra is produced using locally sourced and in house recycled bottle glass.

Specified by the architectural community, our products are utilized in diverse commercial, hospitality and residential environments.

Please contact DEX at lauriel@dexindustries.com for more information.

Specialties

DEX has completed hospitality projects, nationally and internationally, including The W, Regent, Marriott, Twelve, Aloft, Hyatt, Hilton, Setai and Pink Sands Hotels.
Our 65,000 square foot manufacturing facility located in downtown Atlanta, allows us to accommodate our diverse product line and the production requirements of large scale projects.
DEX covers the spectrum of custom design options and offers multi axis CNC capabilities for modeling, prototyping and mold cutting.
