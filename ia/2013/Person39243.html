EB Connelly's Summary

EB Connelly grew up on Lookout Mountain in Northwestern Georgia. Quiet, curious, and wildly observant, EB was intrigued by everything around her. Taught the art of photography by her father, making pictures soon became, and still remains, her unique way of interpreting the world.

EB graduated from The University of the South in Sewanee, TN [B.A. in English/Philosophy] in the spring of 2008. The following autumn she promptly began a year of post-baccalaureate study at The Watkins Film School in Nashville, TN.

EB is currently living in Wilmington, NC, where she is a graduate student of visual journalism -- scheduled to graduate in May of 2013.



Professional
• EB is a knowledgeable digital and film format photographer, HD videographer, editor, and printmaker. She has experience, and significant interest, in the fields of journalism, print media, and documentary filmmaking.
• Extensive experience with travel, and study, abroad has allowed her a greater understanding, and appreciation, of numerous global histories and cultures. She is also current and involved with politics, humanitarian issues, and global affairs; as well as an adamant supporter of wildlife conservation and environmental awareness.
• The primary focus of her work includes: nature, wildlife, landscapes, architecture, and culture.

Skills
• Film and HD Video skills include Cinematography, Lighting, Editing, Screen writing, Documentary.
• Photography formats used include 35mm, 120mm, 4x5 and 8x10 sheet film, Polaroid, and digital.
• Printmaking and processing methods with which she is familiar are silver-gelatin, platinum-
palladium, UV exposures, Polaroid emulsions/transfers, and digital printing.
• Computer Skills include, but are not limited to: the full range of Adobe products [Photoshop, Flash, Illustrator, etc], Apple Final Cut Pro [Motion, Color, Sound Studio, Compressor, DVD Studio], iWork, Microsoft Office, various social networking sites and web 2.0 applications.
