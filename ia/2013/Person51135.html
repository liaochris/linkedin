Gwen (Chen) Shapira's Summary

Leading people to build large data systems - where every millisecond counts.
http://www.pythian.com/about/team/data-experts/gwen-shapira/

Senior consultant at Pythian,, Oracle ACE Director, Board member at NoCOUG and a member of the Oak Table Network.

I studied computer science, statistics and operations research at the University of Tel Aviv, and then went on to spend the next 15 years in different technical positions in the IT industry .

My experience includes Linux system administration, web developement, technical leadership and many years of Oracle database administration.

Specialties: Figuring out what customers need, planning and deploying high performance data architectures, and living to tell the story at a conference.
I love troubleshooting and making things faster. I enjoy stringing together different data technologies to make the best of each, and I love explaining things to people - from one-on-one mentorship to presenting to 300 person crowd.
