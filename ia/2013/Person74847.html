Kevin Dixie's Experience

Managing Director & Founder

Fuelmywebsite.com

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry

January 2007 – Present (6 years 10 months) London, United Kingdom

Fuelmywebsite gets businesses guaranteed online coverage on blogs, Twitter and Facebook at an affordable price. We help unknown brands validate themselves on search and provide helpful SEO.

I created a global blogging community called fuelmyblog.com in January 2007 that became fuelmywebsite for businesses.

Fuelmyblog is a social networking website allowing blog writers from across the globe the opportunity to communicate with each other and enabling users an easy way to read a variety of blogs. The community has over 50k hand selected bloggers.

The site creates revenue by acting as a link between bloggers and companies launching new products or wanting guaranteed online conversation of their product or service. The reviews are great for SEO, rank highly on Google, by nature of bloggers, most have large followings on Twitter and Facebook and link their profiles to their reviews. We do not pay the bloggers - they base their reviews on receiving products and actually trying them out.

Clients we have worked with include King Of Shaves, Sketchers, Butlins, Interflora, JML, Johnson & Johnsons, PC World, Dixons and hundreds and hundreds of SMEs.

Visit fuelmywebsite.com for more details.
