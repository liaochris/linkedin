Darlene Quinn's Summary

Each stand-alone novel in Darlene Quinn's Web Series is brimming with intrigue and haunting nostalgia..

"Webs of Fate", takes you into the high-fashion world of the mid-80s and the struggles of a young buyer, who disappears. Ashleigh McDowell is unwittingly drawn into a deadly trap as she attempts to uncover the truth and find her dear friend. In a race against time and emotions, with her personal and professional life unraveling, Ashleigh ultimately finds what fate holds for her. Webs of Fate is the 2011 Readers Favorite Silver Award for Fiction Intrigue.

"Webs of Power", a fictionalized story of events that marked the greed of the 80’s. Having been on the corporate management team at Bullocks Wilshire. Quinn saw first-hand how greed became a major factor in the demise of our regional department stores. Delivering authentic background in the world of department stores and fashion, she maintains page turning suspense and never allows the facts to bog down her compelling character-driven stories. Webs of Power is the 2009 National Indie Excellence Award Winner for General Fiction, and a USA National Best Book Finalist Award for Fiction.

In "Twisted Webs", Quinn once again involves her main protagonists—Mark and Paige Toddman and Conrad and Ashleigh Taylor—in personal and professional struggles. A kidnapping interwoven with plot twists and intrigue that is conveyed convincingly. Twisted Webs is the 2011 International Book Award Winner for Fiction & Literature, the 2011 National Indie Excellence Award Winner for General Fiction, and a USA National Best Books Finalist Award Winner for Fiction.

"Unpredictable Webs", the major characters will be hurled into the ongoing conflict in the windy city. Tradition vs. change—which will prevail? Can both exist?

Books (hardback, paperback, and E-book formats). "Webs of Fate" (hardback, E-book, & Audio book available now-- Paperback to be released September 1, 2012 & can be preordered now).
