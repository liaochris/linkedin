Jae Subramoney's Summary

My primary expertise revolve around Front End Web Development using JavaScript, HTML, CSS and the Flash platform to deliver solutions with a strong emphasis on functionality, design and usability. I typically use opensource tools like PHP and MYSQL to provide backend support of my Web App solutions. To maintain a product centric focus I make use of frameworks and Open APIs to speed up development times and accessibility.

My medium term focus is on building Mobile applications using Android and AIR( for multiple mobile platforms). The long term focus is on Web applications integrating with third party datasources that cater for as many devices as possible including mobile.

I am very passionate about illustration, design and motion graphics. When developing I incorporate my design sensibilities to enhance user experience and usability with the aim to increase user retention and adoption.
