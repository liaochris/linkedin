Michael Mauldin's Summary

I am severely retired.

These days I raise cattle and horses at my Austin-area ranch in Texas. My wife Debbie and I are world travelers, but we stay closer to home these days, because we almost always have some baby animal that needs care.

As a researcher at Carnegie Mellon, I invented the Lycos search engine and the Julia chatterbot. Since leaving Lycos I've competed in BattleBots, reaching the quarterfinals three times.
