Jay Goth's Summary

What is your vision? Are you an entrepreneur convinced that you have a disruptive idea that can shake the world? As a senior consultant at Tritech SBDC, I help startups develop the vision, create the plan, and build the team and financing strategies that turn dreams into thriving companies. I am working closely with our local community leaders to find and nurture the dreamers who are going to change our local economic ecosystem.

Are you running a growing company and looking for the resources to take it to the next level? As an investment banker through Redtail Capital, I help develop the funding strategies that best fit your situation. My goal is to get you the funding you need to achieve your objectives.

While my network of funding resources and professional partners offer a wide variety of corporate finance options, my main focus is the use of structured debt through Entrex TIGRcub offerings to finance growth and acquisitions in a non-dilutive approach to reaching qualified institutional buyers.

Leaders can't make transformations happen alone. It requires the cooperation and buy-in of employees, advisors, shareholders, customers, vendors - everyone connected with the enterprise. To get that buy-in, leaders have to communicate their vision effectively.

As an entrepreneur and business consultant with over 30 years experience, I have seen all kinds of businesses at virtually every stage of the economic cycle. I have built relationships with investors, fund managers, analysts, attorneys, CPAs, insurance professionals, HR gurus, marketing pros, IT nerds, and just about anyone who can help a business grow.

What's your dream? Let's talk about how we can craft your vision into reality.

Specialties: value creation strategies, corporate finance, investor relations, sales tactics and strategies, business plan writing and analysis, corporate communications, exit planning, mergers and acquisitions
