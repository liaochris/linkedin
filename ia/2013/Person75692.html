Kim Bryant, CPA's Summary

I have over 25 year of experience in public accounting. When I started Bryant & Associates, PC, I had a simple mission: to provide quality service to small business owners to help them improve their business and their lives.

My goal is to provide small business owners with the resources to help them create, identify and achieve their goals.

My philosophy is simple. My firm is different from the traditional accounting firms. We provide a series of Strategy Sessions with our new clients to help them learn where they are at and how to get to where they want to be. We developed a Client Accounting Manual to provide our clients with resource tool to become more organized with their business records. We also developed a Small Business Service Package which is designed to free up our client’s time so they can focus their time and energy on growing their business and/or improving work-life balance. Our services have been designed specifically for small businesses—including bookkeeping, payroll services, tax preparation, and consultation. This comprehensive package ensures our clients have relevant financial information available to support smart business decisions which positively impact their bottom line.
