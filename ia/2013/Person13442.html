Bernard Carey's Experience

Safety, Health, Environment & Fire Consultant/Director

Belvoir Safety Services ltd

Privately Held; Myself Only; Management Consulting industry

2011 – Present (2 years) East Midlands

Belvoir Safety Services Ltd has been established to provide competent and cost effective Occupational Safety, Health, Fire and Environmental consultancy services to small and medium enterprises across the East Midlands. This includes:

- Health & Safety Policies
- Employee Handbooks [or other induction arrangements as suited to your business]
- Site Risk Assessments/Workplace Audits - Call them what you will, we will visit your premises and provide a report on what shortcomings exist and what you need to do to rectify the situation.
- Task Risk Assessments
- Fire Risk Assessments - as required by the Regulatory Reform (Fire Safety Order) 2005
- Staff Fire Training - as required by the Regulatory Reform (Fire Safety Order) 2005. This will be tailored to your workplace, not a generic package that is only partially relevant to your place of business
- Manual Handling Training - bespoke to your working environment, not just a generic, off-the-shelf package that does not suit your team and the risks they take.
- Ladder/stepladder training (1/2 day course)
- General safety awareness training for Employers and Employees alike. Bespoke to your environment.
- A wide range of First Aid training courses accredited to the National Qualification Framework.
First Aid At Work* (Level 3 Award)
Emergency First Aid at Work*, Activity First Aid and Paediatric First Aid.(These are Level 2 awards)

* The FAW and EFAW courses are recognised by the HSE for meeting the requirements of the Health and Safety (First-Aid) Regulations 1981.
