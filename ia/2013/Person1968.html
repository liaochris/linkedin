Adrian Davies' Summary

third-sector.co.uk is an economic development consultancy specialising in financial exclusion, credit unions and money advice services.

It was established in 2005 by Adrian Davies and works with other consultants to offer a holistic service. Expertise includes:

• Establishing credit unions. From fundraising to the FSA application, setting up back offices and launch. Since 2008 credit unions have been set up in West Sussex, Kent and Surrey. During 2013 we expect to establish two nationwide credit unions

• Supporting existing credit unions. Including business planning, product development, policies and procedures, regulatory compliance and interim management services.

• Raising funds. Since 2001 more than £7m has been raised for community groups.

• Risk management. Helping organisations review their risk exposure, better manage risk and improve management information.

• Research and needs assessment. With excellent academic credentials, third-sector.co.uk is supported by post-doctoral researchers and can carry out a wide range of research projects with a socio-economic and historical focus.

• Training and seminars. Providing training on behalf of clients or designing and running be-spoke services and seminars on the key topics facing credit unions.

Specialties: Financial inclusion, creating credit unions, supporting credit unions, business and development planning, fundraising, credit unions, money advice agencies, needs analysis, research, partnership building
