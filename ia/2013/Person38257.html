Ramsey Amin's Summary

I maintain an implant and restorative dentistry practice in Burbank, California. I also perform all implant related procedures such as bone replacement grafting and complex dental reconstructions. I also enjoy general dentistry.

I am one of less than 400 (of 150,000) dentists in the U.S. to be Board Certified in Implant Dentistry by the American Board of Oral Implantology. This is implant dentistry’s “Hall of Fame.”

In addition, I am licensed to offer IV sedation for the comfort of all patients who desire this service. I treat many fearful patients while they sleep through dentistry.

My compassion, enthusiasm and passion for dentistry sets me apart from my peers. My philosophy of treatment is to genuinely treat all patients like I would treat my own family. Because I love what I do, my standards are very high which translates into precision dentistry. In fact, my practice was voted the best dental office in Burbank, CA by the local newspaper multiple times!

As a Diplomate of the American Board of Oral Implantology, Fellow of the International College of Oral Implantologists, and a Fellow of the American Academy of Implant Dentistry, I attend all of the major yearly implant meetings. This allows me to remain current on all the new research and constant advances in implant dentistry.

On a local level, I hold lecture and hands-on dental courses which local doctors and staff are invited to attend throughout the year.

I am a native of Burbank, CA and graduated 2nd in my class from the University of the Pacific School of Dentistry with the highest honors (OKU). I then completed a two-year focused program in Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery and Dental Anesthesia at the University of Pittsburgh School of Dental Medicine.

I am also a clinical instructor at UCLA and Western University, teaching sedation and surgery.

I am also a speaker for a leading dental implant manufacturer where I teach other dentists the ins and outs of dental implants.
