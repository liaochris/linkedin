Chris Purser's Summary

WHO I AM

I run a great team at LeadCrafters. We are a small group of driven professionals with the passion to help others and the motivation to strive to always get better at it. I'm incredibly lucky to be at the helm and incredibly proud to be running one of the coolest companies. We are tech, but not super high tech, cutting edge but not bleeding edge. We just provide the right mix of technology and innovation to anyone who wants that extra boost. What do we do?

THIS IS WHAT WE DO

We provide new Automotive Leads, Harley-Davidson Leads, Motorcycle Leads, RV Leads, Marine Leads and first party leads to any industry that needs them. It may sound boring, but the big-picture concept is very fulfilling. I just live for this stuff. Every day I am able to, personally AND through the business, help a large group of really good people get just a little more business than they did the day before. This improves their profit, lowers some costs and takes just a little of the strain off of their lives. The satisfaction of doing that for what is now many thousands of people, is the single greatest reward of my every day. It fuels me.

HOW I GOT HERE

I have a long history in the Motion Picture and Television Production Industry where you have to learn how to do "anything they want" which literally means everything. Ran a fairly successful residential electronics integration firm in South Florida for 6 years and then found my calling. Now CEO of LeadCrafters, helping people do more business, I love it.

WHAT I'M MADE OF

Specialties: Detail oriented with an eye for sensibility and efficiency. A strong focus on finding, clarifying and meeting the needs of my customers, my connections and those close to me. A keen eye for solid usability and functionality is in my nature. Background skills include motion picture camera operator, Steadicam Operator, wire flying rigger, aerial rigger, photographer, rock climbing, machinist, welder, fabricator, carpenter.
