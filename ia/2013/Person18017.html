Britt Michaelian's Summary

Britt Michaelian, M.A. is a producer, consultant, entrepreneur, artist, author and mom, currently living in Southern California.

As the founder of Soluma Productions and editor of Work Smart Lifestyle, Britt is a recognized face and name on the social media landscape. In 2012, she was named one of the top Social Media Bloggers by Kred, she has contributed to BMO Smart Steps for Business and the Huffington Post in the areas of Small Business, Impact and Tech and she has spoken at conferences such as New Media Expo, Social Media Day OC and LTD Live discussing social media, community building and entrepreneurship.

In December 2012, Britt’s ebook How to Start a Business… The Manual For Mom Entrepreneurs Who Want to Spend Time with Family, Make Money and Be Happy became an Amazon Kindle bestseller. Taking a cue from the success of this ebook, Britt is working on a new motivational book for social media enthusiasts that will be released in mid 2013.
