    We introduced Social Media and Internet Marketing to the company utilizing "Live" videos from the tradeshow floor that we recorded and uploaded to a variety of Social Networking channels. We were able to get 10 of the 40 videos that we uploaded to YouTube on YouTube's Top 10 Videos for the month. This equated into millions of views and participation with audiences locally, nationally and globally. All of the videos trafficked very high in the thousands to tens of thousands of views.
    Fender Music was able to see how Social Media could play a major role in marketing their live events and tradeshows. This process we brought to Fender Music has extended the reach of a 4 day event to permanent search engine ranking. This has effectively extended their tradeshow budget for just a 4 day event into permanent content that lives online.
    Tradeshows, Live Events, and Product Launches create Social Media and connect the company to the world. Fender Music says this has increased their tradeshow presences exponentially. They have found a permanent place for pre-event marketing as well as post-event marketing from their tradeshow dollars. This has also allowed Fender Music to get a much broader reach to their Distributors and customers around the world from their tradeshows.
