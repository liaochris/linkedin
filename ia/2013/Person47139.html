Gary Koelling's Summary

I come from two places. One place is filled with makers; farmers, welders, carpenters and mechanics. The other is of storytellers; painters, writers, musicians and sculptors. Either side denies the similarity to the other. But I’ve learned they just occupy different rooms in the same house. It’s the house I’ve grown up in.

In the last seven years I’ve worked as a product principal and leader of an internal digital product incubator. http://www.thebistroworks.com/
Below are some highlights.

BlueShirt Nation
Employee-facing social network within over 25,000 members.

Giftag
Browser-based universal wish list and social shopping app that used microformats and browser plugins.

BestBuyIdeax
Social idea capture and collaboration app. Released as an open source project.

Twelpforce
Twitter-based customer service app. Connected customer with employees via twitter and recorded the event for later analysis.

Best Buy iPhone and Android App
Built and managed inaugural Best Buy mobile apps.

BBYOpen
Open API framework for exposing Best Buy data used to collaborate with strategic internal and external partners. Platform is responsible for several hundred million in incremental revenue and set the tone for public service oriented access to Best Buy.

BBYOffer
Offer service part of BBYOpen used to induct and publish product or product bundle offers as a public API.

BBYScan
QR code platform that maps unique in-store codes to product info that is exposed as a public API.

Idealist
Multichannel universal wish list and social shopping app that uses a service based approach and uses a responsive interface layer.
