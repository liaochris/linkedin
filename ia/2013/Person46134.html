Fred Valentine's Summary

My goals are to successfully manage a law practice and add value to the lives of my diverse clients. By solving problems and interfacing with insurmountable bureaucracy, I hope to provide solutions and opportunity. I have over 25 years experience as an attorney, contract manager, and program manager in the military, the private sector, the financial industry, and as a sole practitioner attorney. I retired from the Air Force in 2004 after 20 years of military service. Since retirement, I have resolved intellectual property, Bank Secrecy Act (BSA) and Office of Foreign Asset Control (OFAC) issues. I have also managed an organization’s business continuity and enterprise risk management (ERM) program. As Deputy General Counsel, I managed all of the organization's business transactions, vendor management, and intellectual property concerns.

I am now operating my law practice focusing on business litigation, personal injury, criminal law, estate planning (living trusts and special needs trusts) and immigration (cancellation of removal) proceedings.

I volunteer as planning commissioner in my city of residence (Eastvale, CA) and as a supervisory committee member at my credit union.

I invite you to contact me and welcome your calls, letters and electronic mail. Contacting me does not create an attorney-client relationship.
