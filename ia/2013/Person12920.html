Ben Hedrington's Summary

A digital strategy and product leader driving high growth digital touch points at the epicenter of the collision of digital and physical, a consumer led experience and commerce revolution we are all living through.

I build and lead high quality teams delivering millions in value to the business in terms of direct revenue, indirect traffic driving value, customer experience and productivity enhancing tools. I drive the road map and strategy of a high velocity product delivery working model for the company with multiple million dollar budgets under management. I span between this product leader role and a passion I have translating the both what is possible with emerging web approaches and technical concepts to business leaders across the company often up to the C level, shaping company strategy, helping solve problems in new ways and driving us forward.

I bring a unique balance of skills to the table from strong experience with the evolution of the web on very large web properties, a deep point of view on industry digital directions, a technical background with a customer focused approach together with an understanding of non-digital business models people and product leadership. I exude an entrepreneurial drive to push businesses forward, I have proven myself by delivering real business results from new strategies in this fast moving space by having the curiosity and vision to get things started in order to learn by doing and prove by example the opportunities I see.
