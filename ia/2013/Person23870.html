Christian Batist's Summary

I provide online game consultancy, helping brands and companies develop and/or deploy their virtual world or social game strategies. I worked with some of the most successful game companies, such as Stardoll, Spil Games, Studio 100, Weeworld, Foot Locker Europe and Nickelodeon.

In 2012 I started lecturing Multiplayer Game Design at the NHTV University in Breda, Netherlands and in 2013 I started lecturing second and third year students on 'advanced games' at Fontys' Academy of Creative Industries. I also mentor several online and mobile game start-ups in the area of business development and monetization.

Prior to starting my consultancy business, I worked for Habbo Hotel (Sulake Corporation) for 6 years, most recently in the role of Senior Vice President of Marketing, but also responsible for Advertising Sales, User Experience and Monetization.

Before joining Habbo, I was Internet Publisher for the largest media company of The Netherlands, Telegraaf Media Group, for 5 years. I was responsible for creation and execution of the digital strategies of all of the publisher's magazines, such as CosmoGIRL! and FHM.

Between 1996 and 2000, I was Managing Director of Seven Monkeys New Media, a company that helped over 150 small and medium sized businesses develop Internet strategies and roll out websites, intranet environments and e-commerce applications. For many clients these were the first moves in the digital landscape.
