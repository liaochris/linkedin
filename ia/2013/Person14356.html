Bill Kuhlman's Summary

Whereas it's my impression many Realtors are focused on doing more deals, I stay laser-focused on getting the best possible deal for each client. I continually look for ways to improve the outcomes and experience for my clients, within each transaction and from client to client.

Bill Kuhlman's Experience

Real Estate Broker/Owner

Kuhlman Residential Real Estate

Privately Held; 10,001+ employees; Real Estate industry

1991 – Present (22 years)

I started my full-time career in real estate in 1988, founded Kuhlman Residential Real Estate in 1991, and began specializing in buyer agency in 1998.

I've drawn on my experience as a buyer specialist and have added a new specialty: first-time sellers and others who want to sell their current home while they simultaneously buy their new one. My extensive experience as a buyer's agent helps me effectively counsel sellers to prepare and market their property for a quick sale at the highest possible price.

Since I've taken more listings in the past few years, I now have a keener sense of how to listen for what's important to buyers and to sellers when they're deep in the trenches of negotiations. Often, knowing how to frame a response to appeal to the other side's wants and needs can make all the difference between putting a deal together and having it fall apart.

But my true passion remains buyer agency, helping clients buy the homes they want at the best possible terms and conditions. I have always been, at heart, a consumer advocate. I love nothing more in my work than helping a client secure a home they love at a relative bargain price.

I also feel strongly that no client should ever be put in the position of having to surrender their right to full representation throughout the entire process. So if you (or someone you know) choose to hire an agent from Kuhlman Residential, you will never be asked to consent to either dual or "designated" agency, where the agent or firm attempts to simultaneously represent both the buyer and the seller in the same transaction.

Call me now, at 781 444-1399, so we can discuss how I can best help you or someone you know.
