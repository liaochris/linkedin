Leadership is a spiritual endeavor--from King David to Abraham Lincoln, from Martin Luther King, Jr. to Mother Teresa, you name it! If you are a leader and interested in having a spiritual adviser, we can set up a telephone conversation to find out if your need matches what I can provide.
