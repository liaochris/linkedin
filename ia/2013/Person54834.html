Abhinav Gupta's Summary

Over the past 6 years, I have worked on projects building interactive interfaces with a strong focus on user experience and business viability. I have a good balance of academic and industrial experience working in interdisciplinary and inter-cultural teams. My work ranges from building design strategies, product management, conducting user research, employing iterative user-centered design techniques, prototyping, user testing and reporting, evaluation, and final deployment with development teams.

I have worked in the domain of Social Media Analytics, Mobile Service Design, Biometrics, Healthcare, Eye-tracking Usability Research, Sustainability for Regional Artisans, Educational Interfaces, The academic and corporate projects have helped me obtaining a holistic view towards design and a deep understanding of HCI methods to build great products and services.
