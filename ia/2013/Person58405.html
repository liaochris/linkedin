Janice Mansfield's Summary

Welcome to Real Food Made Easy.

We provide personal chef services throughout Greater Victoria, and catering services for your special occasions. I have been providing people in Victoria with good food, made from scratch with the best quality ingredients since 2008.

For your special events, we work closely with you to create a menu that is a reflection of your unique style. Our specialty is creating classic-style cocktail pairings to enhance your dining experience.

We have experience meeting very specific dietary needs, including gluten-free and vegan. We have been baking gluten-free breads, cakes and cookies since 2009, and do a great deal of customization to create delicious foods that meet the some of the most complicated dietary restrictions. Our products are available for sale during the summer months at the Mayfair Market and by special order during the rest of the year. We have shared our wild-yeast breadmaking techniques with the local chapter of the Canadian Celiac Association. Many of our customers also come to us requesting dairy-free and egg-free creations. We strive to create products that are so good you "wont believe it has no gluten!"

Specialties

Gluten-free baking, Catering for special occasions, Creation of classic cocktail ingredients
