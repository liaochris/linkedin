Brad Schweitzer's Summary

Brad Schweitzer is an expert as combining social media, search engine optimization (SEO), search engine marketing (SEM), and WordPress Websites. His technical expertise, combined with his big picture vision, helps him create a comprehensive and professional total online presence for businesses and business professionals. Brad understands how to build user friendly and professional websites, optimize the pages for organic search, market keywords with paid search advertising, promote products with social media, and integrate all of the pieces for effective analysis, regular monitoring, and simple management.

As a Procurement Analyst and Contract Specialist Brad interprets and influences contracting policy and business processes. He provides training, advice, and direction to teams of engineers, project managers, program managers, contracting officers, and contract specialists. Brad's analytical expertise, friendly and helpful approach, coaching expertise, and innovative thought leadership makes him the go-to guy for many of his coworkers. He takes the time to answer questions, provide direction, make smart decisions all while managing multiple successful projects.

Brad is experienced with many different aspects of User Assistance development. As a programmer, project manager, technical writer, team lead, information architect, and manager Brad has worked with various hardware and software products. One of his favorite projects was working with an agile project development team, using and developing the first release of IBM Rational Team Concert.

Brad has a passion for doing the important things with excellence. As a Federal Contract Specialist, the main goal is to negotiate for the best product at a fair. A good deal is important to all of us -- it is Not THE Government . . . it is OUR Government. As an Internet marketer, Brad takes the time to learn from you, work with you, and produce the best possible results for you.

Brad Schweitzer's Patents

  • Apparatus, system, and method for creating customized workflow documentation

    • United States Patent 20060059423
    • Issued March 16, 2006
    Inventors: Brad Schweitzer, Stefan lehman

    An apparatus, system, and method are provided for creating customized workflow documentation. The apparatus includes an interface module, a-content module, and an assembly module. The interface module gathers input from a user regarding the workflow to be documented. The content module gathers the required pre-defined content sections for the workflow to be documented. The assembly module creates a single document containing the pre-defined content sections in an ordered, comprehensive, contiguous document that is available as an electronic copy or hard copy.
