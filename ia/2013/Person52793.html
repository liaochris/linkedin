Henry (Hank) Hedges, CSP, ACFE, CSSHO, CHS V,'s Summary

Twenty years of progressively responsible health, safety, and environmental training/management experience at the consultant, plant, division and corporate levels. Design, implement and audit OSHA, EM 385-1-1, NFPA, DOT, CWA, CAA, CERCLA, RCRA, and SARA training and compliance policies and programs for multi-national companies. Have safety and environmental experience in twenty countries. Review legislative priorities and comment to federal agencies. Conduct environmental/safety training, compliance audits, safety compliance reviews, groundwater and soil remediation projects, industrial hygiene studies, worker's compensation cost reduction activities, and compliance training for all levels of management. Evaluate safety and environmental liability exposures and provide recommendations for risk management.

Specialties: USACE EM 385-1-1, OSHA, risk management, change management, incident investigation, fire regulations, safety regulations, asset recovery, budgeting, safety consulting, customer relations, customer satisfaction, management consulting, organizational development, program management, safety programs, scheduling, safety management systems, training materials, and training programs.
