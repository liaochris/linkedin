Michael Blackstone's Summary

Is it beginning to dawn on you that, while one part of your life is going well, another part is going badly? Is this dawning beginning to create inner turmoil and even torture in your darkest moments?

When you are in the midst of inner struggle, that's exactly when I am your world’s best guide to transforming that struggle from being painful into inner peace with a confident direction, not before―right now. Call.

The struggle can be embarrassing... to be questioning everything you've believed about yourself and your life. And you don’t want to talk to anybody. You want to get something figured out first, so you can at least feel a little bit pulled together. I know this because I’ve been there. NOW's the time.

I have spent the last 25 years focusing relentlessly on the dynamics of inner conflict—conflict within the mind―and the fastest, surest way out of it. Why? I’ve suffered with it myself, and I have found it epidemic, but secret, within executive and senior leadership ranks. Outward success is not a factor. Every executive I've worked with had the outer trappings of success. I’ve worked, one-on-one, with nearly 4,000 of them from a dozen Fortune 500 companies on three continents and I'm one of the few authorities in the country on the causes and cures of inner conflict.

Right now it’s hard to see the light at the end of the tunnel, yet I am your best guide, right now, to living the best times of your life. That’s my guarantee. Call me, 24/7, seriously.

—Mike Blackstone
