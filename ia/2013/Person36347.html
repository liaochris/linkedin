Norman Rosenthal's Summary

I am a psychiatrist, motivational speaker, executive coach, and author whose research in describing seasonal affective disorder(SAD) and pioneering the use of light therapy has helped millions of people. My most recent book "Transcendence: Healing and Transformation Through Transcendental Meditation" (Tarcher-Penguin, 2011) became a New York Times Bestseller and explores the value of this ancient technique for healing and transformation in our modern world. My forthcoming book (August, 2013) is The Gift of Adversity: The Unexpected Benefits of Life's Difficulties, Setbacks and Imperfections (Tarcher-Penguin).

Specialties: Transcendental Meditation, Season Affective Disorder, Light Therapy, Executive Coaching, Depression, Bipolar Disorder, Pharmaceutical Trials, TM, PTSD
