Andrew Kaufman's Summary

I have an extensive background in coordinating content strategy for the web, as well as a thorough understanding of the role that quality editorial content plays in helping to build and maintain a website that successfully balances user needs with business objectives. I have experience developing and managing all aspects of the content lifecycle, from the initial planning stages and preliminary deliverables up through the execution and governance of the content. Throughout my career I’ve worked closely with business, technology and creative stakeholders to come up with innovative and efficient solutions to complex content challenges. I am an evangelist and advocate for the importance of content in the web design process and am always looking for opportunities to enhance my knowledge and put my skills to use towards that goal.
