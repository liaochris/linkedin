Karen E. Nethersole, Esq.'s Summary

Full Circle NY (FCNY) is a branding & marketing company working with professional athletes, entertainers and businesses.

BIO OF CEO KAREN NETHERSOLE, ESQ.
Karen Nethersole, Esq., is the CEO & Founder of Full Circle NY (FCNY). Throughout her career as an attorney Karen has focused on advocating for and empowering her clients. She began at a law firm as outside counsel to Fortune 500 companies and then transitioned from law to a career in Human Resources at a Fortune 500 Company. Karen created innovative training programs that helped her company become more competitive and set the standard for others in the industry to follow.

Karen works with FCNY’s clients who are professionals and professional athletes to provide business and career skills training. In addition she implements the legal strategies and creates the necessary agreements to maximize the value of their personal brands, develop and protect their intellectual property and form legal entities that best suit the needs of their businesses.

Karen has enacted powerful positive change and is most recently featured in Examiner.com (Sports and Society) and (NY) sections. She has also been featured in ESSENCE magazine, Black Enterprise magazine, The Harvard University Guide to Opportunities and more.

Karen is one of the 2008 recipients of the 40 Under Forty Achievement Award and served as Mistress of Ceremonies at the 2010 awards dinner. Karen graduated magna cum laude from St. Francis College (NY) and was inducted into the Duns Scotus Honor Society. She obtained her juris doctorate degree from Hofstra University School of Law and is a member of the New York and Connecticut bars as well as various Federal courts.
