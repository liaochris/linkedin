Jean Pierre Menard's Summary

I am an Engineering Manager who is just as passionate about developing people as developing products. I accomplish both by leading my teams into disciplined processes, and I remain flexible by "management by walking around." My major strength is connecting with customers, whether internal or external, and drilling down to the root cause of the problem, discovering the underlying opportunity, and communicating our company’s unique solution, in a way that captures their imagination.

+ Product Development / Project Management
Collaboration across departments. Concept through production. Convert customer requests into measureable product specifications. Project management: scoping / budgeting / tracking against milestones. Stage/gate review. Capital planning. P&L Responsibility.

+ People Development
Coach. Mentor. Inspire. Process driven. Institute formal processes for: design steps; delegating authority and accountability; brainstorming ideas, evaluating ideas.

+ Technical Depth
Systems Integration: Mechanical / Electrical / Software / Controls / Heat Transfer / Fluid Flow / Thermal Simulation / Thermal Stresses / Adhesives / Insulation / Pneumatics. Continuous flow manufacturing.

+ Communicator
15+ years of International Experience including Europe, Asia, N. & S. America. Seek to understand customer’s problems and communicate company's unique solutions in non-technical style. Technical writing, journal publications, plan and teach seminars.

+ Manufacturability / Cost Reduction
Large scale manufacturing and job shop custom builds. In-depth knowledge of MRP systems. Common assemblies & parts. Inexpensive custom tooling. Experienced sheet metal fabricator, machinist, and welder. Shortened lead-times.

+ I.P. Protection
Wrote and approved patent applications.
