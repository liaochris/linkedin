Carter Gray's Summary

Carter Gray, Principal at Pacific Insurance Group MGA & Partner at Fortitude Financial Group, INC. Carter Gray specializes in helping people create "tax diversification" to hedge against higher tax rates in the future. He has written multiple articles that have been published on FOX NEWS. Carter Gray is an independent life insurance agent for multiple insurance companies. His specialty is helping people create Tax-Diversification in order to hedge against higher tax rates in the future as well as educating people on guaranteed retirement plans they cannot outlive. Carter prides himself on helping his clients develop personalized retirement strategies that allow them to reach their retirement goals. Raised in the financial industry, his parents worked to help clients plan retirement for over 40 years. Joseph Carter Gray is also a Chartered Life Underwriter ®, a graduate of the University of Washington. Specializing in advanced case design, Carter helps people get their money working for them in smart and safe ways. You can contact Carter Gray at cgray@pacinsgroup.com or call 425-246-1676.
