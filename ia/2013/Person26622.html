Conan Young's Summary

A lifelong Entrepreneur, innovator, scientist, mentor and educator. My greatest strengths comes from my deep understanding of interpersonal relationships, creativity, lateral thinking, scientific reasoning, intuition and my never ending supply of energy and enthusiasm.

I believe in always taking a multidisciplinary approach, and so my repertoire of skills, experience and education is broad in scope, but dense in scale. I like what works, not what “should” work.

Entrepreneur
I have founded, started, and created multiple businesses in technology, health, education and creative fields. No crisis is too steep and no goal too hairy - Entrepreneurs eat challenges for breakfast!

Mentor
I am an experienced mentor to people who range from high-profile entrepreneurs to disadvantaged children to leading experts in scientific fields. My life's work has largely orbited around helping people find their passion and turning it into a life defining purpose, living their lives to the fullest whilst building wealth and leaving a meaningful legacy.

Negotiator
I am a trained and experienced negotiator, mediator and communicator whose skills can be as easily applied to monumental deals between disgruntled businesses as to helping troubled parents better understand their children's needs. I apply evidence-based techniques and principles to focus and guide individuals away from conflict and towards creating options for progress.

Creativity
I come prepared with a toolbox of full of the sharpest and shiniest gizmos for deliberate creative thinking. Tapping into the wellspring of genius that is in each person's juicy brains is easy when you know what buttons to press.

Science!
I have worked with leaders in a multitude of disciplines from, biophysics, children's health, experimental and clinical psychology, biotechnology and a host of other domains. When it comes time to make critical decisions I apply thorough research, clear reasoning and solid evidence.

Specialties

Mentoring, Coaching, Speaking, Presenting, Counselling, Opportunity Evaluation and execution, Negotiation, Researching, Creativity & Innovation, Leadership
