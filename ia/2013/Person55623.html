Curtis Walter's Summary

During my years in recruiting I discovered candidates with marketable skill sets were denied job offers because of simple mistakes made in interviews. They had the backgrounds necessary to perform the jobs, yet had trouble impressing upon employers they were the best fit for the positions. Interview of STATURE is built to guide candidates seamlessly and quickly through a successful interview process.

In 2006, I founded one of the Washington, DC Metropolitan Area’s fastest-growing search firms, Stature LLC. Since 1999, I enjoyed a successful recruiting career coaching and placing candidates in large, publicly-traded corporations, non-profits and government contractors. Prior to 1999, I completed 12 years of professional sales experience, ranking nationally in sales for the pharmaceutical, automotive and telecommunication industries.

I hold a B.S. in Business Management from West Virginia State University and a M.A. in Public Communication from American University in Washington, DC.

I have had the privilege of public speaking on the subject of Interview Technique for such prestigious groups as The International Association of Administrative Professionals, Women in Government Relations, The Embry Riddle Aeronautical University, Golden Key International Honour Society and American University’s Kogod School of Business.

Specialties

The world's premiere online interview technique firm featuring three workshops accessible from any computer (Critical Resume Construction / Essential Interview Preparation / Innovative Interview Techniques). Our products can reduce your career search from weeks to days!
