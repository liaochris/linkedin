David Lee Scher, MD's Summary

Innovative digital health expert, known as a medical technology pioneer and mobile health visionary. Advises on full lifecycle development of new technology as a clinical investigator, IRB chair, member of Medicare Advisory Committee, and incubator of state-of-the-art digital technology. Applies unique clinical perspective combined with consumer/patient behavioral aspects and business acumen to customize and implement digital health technology. Keen insight of digital health and healthcare IT healthcare landscape, opportunities, and challenges. Regarded for entrepreneurial approach as well as honesty, integrity, and high ethical standards.

-Recognized as a digital health technology subject matter expert (SME); established a distinguished social media presence through content on technology, clinical applications, and regulatory and social aspects of adoption.
-Oversaw development of world’s first standards of medical and health mobile apps as the Chair of Medical App Certification Blue Ribbon Panel for Happtique. Presented standards in March 2013, which will become a framework for a certification program.
-Advised Happtique's clinical implementation team to translate hospital clients’ user requirements into customized medical app catalogs,and facilitated implementation of Happtique’s patent-pending medical app prescribing technology.
-Designed and executed marketing and branding strategy, including social media, blog, twitter, and print and video interviews.
-Pioneered adoption and integration of remote patient monitoring data with electronic health records.
-Advised cardiac rhythm device remote monitoring divisions of Guidant / Boston Scientific, Medtronic, Sorin, and St. Jude Medical in development and deployment.
