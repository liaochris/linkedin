Colin Baugh's Summary

PROFILE
Colin is a media relations professional committed to producing results for his clients. He has existing relationships with top-level local, regional, and national media and a proven track record of securing editorial coverage for clients in the food and beverage, luxury travel, consumer products, and health and wellness spaces. In addition to his work in public relations, Colin spent several years working in radio as an on-air talent, live sound engineer, producer, and promotions specialist. These experiences provide a unique perspective on building relationships with media as well as what does and does not constitute newsworthiness.
