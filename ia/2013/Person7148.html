Andrew Pugno's Summary

Wills, Trusts & Estate Planning:
I can offer peace of mind by helping you get your affairs in order with an individualized, attorney-prepared estate plan that is specific to your family.

Probate & Trust Administration:
When a loved one passes away, I can help make the legal process smoother by assisting clients in carrying out their legal duties and properly transferring trust and estate assets.

Trust & Estate Litigation:
When conflict is unavoidable, I can provide aggressive legal advocacy to protect clients from beneficiaries, trustees and creditors.
