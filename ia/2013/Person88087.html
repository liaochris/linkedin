Mary-Frances Main's Summary

Mary-Frances has created marketing plans, managed websites and designed and implemented marketing materials, all while running a home-based business since before it was popular (over 10 years).

Her belief is that “new media” is a fabulous opportunity for businesses to grow by use of the Internet. Her loyal clients have grown to be friends - some of whom she has been working with for over 15 years.

She has been invited to speak about Social Media by a variety of organizations including the African Travel Assn, the Broomfield Chamber and the Cherry Creek Business Women's Association.

As a blogger who has been maintaining her original blog since (2004 http://www.artabandoned.com) , but also maintains Mid Century Style Magazine and Mid-Century Motels (http://www.midcenturystyle.net) and now Denver Parent (http://www.denverparent.net), she has written weekly articles for www.poshmama.com and www.divinecaroline.com on the subjects of Mid-century style, crafting and parenting. As well as being the national Mid-Century Style Examiner.

http://www.widgetinc.com (for more information)

Specialties: Social media, marketing analysis, advertising campaigns, SEO, analytics, "big picture" marketing incorporating traditional as well as new media solutions.
