James Bloor's Experience

Director

Distinction Limited

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Information Technology and Services industry

January 2004 – Present (9 years 9 months)

Distinction are an experienced digital design agency, with an amazing team of designers, developers and marketers that love to make your marketing communications work harder for you.

Distinction are hired by clients who want to achieve something; they're ambitious types, just like us. With an emphasis on 'integrated marketing', we help our clients achieve award-wining levels of return-on-investment.

We help our clients achieve their potential by learning about their businesses, listening to what they need and creating a campaign based on their business goals and centered upon the needs of their customers.

As co-founder and managing director, my role at Distinction is varied. It ranges from developing strategies to grow both our own brand, and those of our clients, to pitching for new business, to supporting our design and technical teams, to making the tea...

Since inception, Distinction has grown organically to become a Top 100 Recommended Agency. We have offices in Nottingham and London, and are looking to continue to grow over the coming years whilst staying true to our core values, and maintaining the 'family business' feel.
