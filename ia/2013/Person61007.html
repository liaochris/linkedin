J.S. "Jeff" Larger's Experience

President Operations, GM

Route 36 Motorwerks dba Route 36 MotorCars

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Automotive industry

September 2004 – Present (9 years 4 months) Delaware, Ohio

RT36 principals joined the car collecting hobby in 1966 with our family’s acquisition of the 1942 T&C black-out car. Our involvement with automobiles sales and service includes stints as mechanics, auto body repair technicians, fleet and lease sales management and auto related business owners.

Since 1977 we've been involved with major fleet leasing, fleet management companies. Our fleet delivery services have been responsible for delivering, license, title, and used vehicle disposition for well over 22,000. vehicles. We have also handled the fleet sales transactions for numerous fleet's like Nationwide Insurance, Motorist Insurance, Hertz, Avis, Enterprise, Thrifty, Wendy's, and countless other companies. Since 1977 we've sold close to 100,000. fleet vehicles.

Recently, we’ve applied our historical interests and involvements into the areas of select vehicle acquisition of “Hand Picked” gently used vehicles, light vehicle restoration and collector car buy-sell-trade activities.
