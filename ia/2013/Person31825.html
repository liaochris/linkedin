David Frerichs' Experience

President

Media Tuners LLC

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Internet industry

Generally, lead a great team that adds voice and touch controlled Internet services to cars and TVs.

More specifically, define, craft, and sell Media Tuners products: MT-One voice-controlled Internet services platform, QueSiQue radio content management and ad insertion platform, and tuner2.com consumer portal for Internet radio.

On the consulting front, assemble and deploy teams to provide strategic marketing, business plan authoring, business development, product planning, content service architecture, and server & client engineering. Customers include MyWire, Pioneer Electronics, Sensis, and SRS Labs.
