Louis Szablya's Summary

Louis is seasoned energy executive in Sales, Strategy, Product Management, & Marketing as well as an energy futurist who is particularly passionate about making the Smart Grid real, making it interactive, green, and transactive. Such a grid will encourage individual responsibility and participation resulting in a more efficient, highly decentralized, and democratized energy delivery infrastructure while aligning the interests of electric utilities, consumers and the environment.

He has worked for over 35 years in the electric energy industry and has extensive experience in electric and gas utility system operations, engineering, marketing, business planning and assessment, mergers and acquisitions, financial analysis, wholesale power marketing, and general management. Louis played a key role in the startup of five electric wholesale power marketers: Citigroup Global Commodities, El Paso Merchant Energy, Entergy Power Marketing, Electric Clearinghouse (now Dynegy), and Avista Wholesale Marketing, and a principal role at leading Smart Grid companies like Gridpoint and Energate.

He has held positions as Executive Vice President and Vice President for Sales, Marketing, Product Management, Strategy, and Business Development. As a consultant, an employee, for utilities, or consumer-focused companies, he has had a positive and durable impact.

Louis has vast experience in electricity and natural gas structured transactions throughout North America having structured and closed over $350 million in transactions creating over $45 million in value. He has been responsible for the merger or acquisitions of over $300 million in companies and assets, creating $60 over million in value.
