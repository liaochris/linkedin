Clay McDaniel's Summary

Clay McDaniel was until October 2013 the Managing Director and a Co-Founder of Spring Creek Group, a leading social media marketing and technology agency which was acquired in 2011 by the digital division of Mediabrands (part of the Interpublic Group [NYSE: IPG]). Clay is an experienced digital marketer and marketing technology manager with combined 20 years experience in the field.

Prior to founding and building Spring Creek, Clay was the Senior Director of Product Management for RealArcade at RealNetworks, Inc.; the Consumer Product Marketing Manager for Windows XP at Microsoft Corporation; and previously worked at Vitria Technology, the Whitney Museum of American Art, and Deloitte Consulting. Clay holds an MBA from the Kellogg School of Management at Northwestern University, where he graduated Beta Gamma Sigma with a triple-major in Marketing, Operations, and Technology Management. Clay also holds an AB in Economics from Princeton University.

Clay has been a frequent contributor on a variety of digital marketing and media topics to Adotas, DMNews, iMedia, MarketingProfs, MediaPost, Mashable, Forrester and eMarketer industry reports, and many other publications. He has presented for keynotes and panels at the Social Media Conference NW, the WOMMA.org Summit and School of WOM conferences, Digital.Hollywood, the PR Society of America, and the U.S. Chamber of Commerce. Clay was a founding Board member of the Social Media Club Seattle and an Advisory Board member of the University of Washington’s Masters in Communication and Digital Media department. He is also a board member for the Microsoft Alumni Network and for the Technology Access Foundation. Clay was a 2011 Puget Sound Business Journal “40 Under 40” honoree. He lives in Seattle and enjoys fly fishing, trail running, sampling Seattle’s arts and music scene, and fervently supporting the Arsenal and the Sounders FC soccer teams.
