  • Chinese Restaurant Mobile Ordering System

    • September 2010 to May 2011
    Team Members: Christopher Brookes

    The project was to design an Android Platform based booking service for a Chinese Restaurant. From within the app, the customer could book a table as well as order food from a full up-to-date menu. With this come functionality to view and cancel current bookings. An integrated Map allows customers to easily find the restaurant and reduce the problem of customers turning up late, therefore improving overall efficiency of the restaurant. The app is packed full of information about the restaurant as well as an embedded promotional video.The user interface is designed to be user friendly and easy to understand for all audiences.

    The other half of the project is the Restaurant Portal. Here restaurant staff can login and complete any of the following tasks:

    -Manage Staff Users

    -View Orders for a particular day
    -Send a group email to all customers
