Angie Macdonald's Summary

I am a web content writer and editor with five years’ experience including web design and social media training.

I am currently Web Editor at South East London Film Clubs Network, a community website listing the activities of a network of South East London film clubs & societies and community cinemas - http://selondonfilmclubs.wordpress.com/

I am also a freelance writer whose main interests include health and nutrition, personal development, literature, lifestyle and London life amongst others. I write about health and wellbeing at http://writehealth.co.uk/.

My work has been featured in print magazines and on a number of online sites. I have written about a range of topics including the Royal Family, theatre, travel, web design, visual merchandising and haute couture.

I am versatile, able to embrace change and I love learning new skills. Most of all, I enjoy communicating with people through the written word.

Specialties

Writing, web content writing, editing, web management, WordPress content management system, SEO, social media, XHTML and CSS.
