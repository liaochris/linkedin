Andy Brown's Summary

I'm a photographer from Christchurch, New Zealand. Currently I'm mainly specialising in documentary wedding photography. With a background in graphic design, I'm able to offer an alternative approach to photography, as well as a clean, professional album design.

During the colder months I pick up some freelance winter design projects under my company Enquire Design, mainly working on website interface design, branding and collateral design.

In my spare time I hang out with my wife and 3 children, drink faaaaar too much coffee, and enjoy listening to Cali-style punk rock.

Specialties: Documentary Wedding Photography, Location Wedding Photography
