John Barton's Summary

Bed Bug Fix is formulated by a renowned chemist and inventor specifically to kill and repel bed bugs, but will also kill dust mites, ants, carpet beetles, centipedes, clover mites, cockroaches, crickets, earwigs, fleas, ticks, millipedes, pill bugs, silverfish, spiders, termites, flies, gnats, mosquitoes, moths, yellow jackets wasps and other flying and crawling insects.Bed Bug Fix is a 100% natural, non- flammable, non-staining, non-toxic, bio-degradable, safe to use around children and pets. It has a pleasant botanical scent, is EPA 25b Exempt and FDA GRAS, is ready to use, easy to apply, and is safe for use on water sensitive materials. Bed Bug Fix has comprehensive third party lab testing that shows that bed bugs are killed on contact. Bed Bug Fix will eradicate Bed Bugs, prevent bed bug infestations, reduce the time rooms are off line, reduce bed bug lawsuits and claims, and mitigate reputation impact. Bed Bug Fix is designed for direct spray application on mattresses, covers, bedding, sofas, stuffed chairs, carpet, rugs, clothing and luggage and other furniture and room appurtenances. Bed Bug Fix is certified for use in all fifty (50) States.The Bed Bug Fix eradication protocol has a room bed bug free and ready to reoccupy in just five days.Since Bed Bug Fix is EPA 25b exempt and is not classified as a chemical pesticide, the eradication protocol can be performed by facility housekeeping staff after proper training.The Bed Bug Fix five day eradication protocol only requires about 96 Oz, or three(3) 32 Oz Spray Bottles, of Bed Bug Fix.One 32 Oz Spray Bottle of Bed Bug Fix will treat approximately 18 queen size mattresses or 600 Sq Ft. Internal testing has shown that Bed Bug Fix also offers residual kill protection of up to thirty(30) days.
