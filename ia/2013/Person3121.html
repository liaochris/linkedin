Alan Hamilton's Summary

I am a strong believer in the use of social business and collaboration solutions in business to improve communication, business processes and efficiency. I have nearly 20 years experience of delivering solutions in this area for customers large and small based all around the world.

I am an experienced practitioner in the installation of the technologies I advocate the use of with hands-on implementation skills with a very broad range of IBM solutions on Linux and Windows.

I also have experience of designing and implementing enterprise-scale applications such as IBM WebSphere Commerce, SugarCRM, IBM WebSphere MQ and virtually all IBM Collaboration Solutions (Lotus) products, including IBM Connections, WebSphere Portal, Sametime and Lotus Notes & Domino.

My particular passion is engaging with staff members to help them get the most from the technology they have. I run enterprise adoption programs for businesses of all sizes, leading by example and focusing on how to help people get their work done more easily and more creatively for better business outcomes.

I am a very strong believer in actually using what I advocate and hence am an active Tweeter (@alanghamilton), have an active blog (http://alanghamilton.com), and am a contributor to public sites such as IBM developerWorks, Social Media Today, Social Enterprise Today and others.

I am a contributing member of the W3C Social Business Community Group, a professional member of the Social Media Club and one of the founding members of the IBM Social Business Partner Council.

Specialties

Social business computing technologies and approaches, mainly with IBM Connections Collaboration solution development and implementation - Lotus Notes, IBM Connections, IBM Portal, etc.

IBM WebSphere Portal implementation & design.

SugarCRM implementation, customisation & deployment.

Multi-channel e-commerce solutions with IBM WebSphere Commerce and Magento.

Development of quality management solutions using IBM Rational Team Concert and Rational Unified Process.
