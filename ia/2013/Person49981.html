Lex Lybrand's Summary

I'm not learned enough to win a million dollars on a gameshow, and I'm not athletic enough to be a world champion at any sort of real sport, but if I work really hard and win the lottery or something, I just might make it!

Editor / Post-Production Manager / Web Designer

Billi & Theodore

June 2011 – Present (2 years 4 months) Austin, TX

Edited 'Billi & Theodore,' the debut feature film from writer/director, Ronald Short. The post-production process presented a variety of challenges -- the biggest being that none of the shots were slated, so editing the sync-sound audio was a chore, to say the least. Overall, though, I'm extremely happy with how the film has turned out, and I can't wait to cut my next feature!

Greenless Studios

Greenless Studios is where my dreams become your reality. Through my modest little production company, I've produced a variety of short films -- including the single most popular YouTube sensation of all time, "Batman's Bad Day" which has over 100,000 views, and I can only assume that at least 1,000 people were watching during each of these viewings -- with other projects in the works.
