President / Creative Director / Business Builder

7 Elements of Dezign

February 2011 – Present (2 years 9 months) West Bloomfield, MI

As a seasoned marketing professional, brand strategist and successful creative director for many years, I've developed a unique ability to absorb and understand my clients' business goals in relation to the respective markets they're striving to develop a stronger relationship with. Along with a passion for observing and understanding consumer behaviors, it's that insight that allows me to effectively combine a variety of elements in an effort to establish and strengthen the value of a company and what it offers. At the end of the day, it's all about drawing customers to the brand vs. taking the brand to the customers.

With new technologies and mobile 'apps' emerging everyday, it's easy to get lost in a world of information overload. I see it all the time- (as it applies to social media, for example) most people think, 'I can setup my company's Facebook page and put a simple social media strategy in place. No problem'. And often before they even get started, those same people quickly realize they're in way over their heads and have no idea WHERE to start or HOW to properly use these tools to accomplish any kind of measurable results. Unfortunately, many business owners and professionals are slow to adopt or embrace innovations in marketing because they simply don't understand it. As a result, they hesitate and often struggle with the idea of investing in technologies and tools that work!

My goal with 7 Dezign is simple- Providing dynamic solutions that contribute to the growth of your brand while helping your company enjoy new opportunities for success.

My passion and my profession is about making the most in the minds that matter the most.

MI Director of Marketing and Admissions

Communicare Michigan LLC

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Hospital & Health Care industry

February 2009 – February 2011 (2 years 1 month)

Starting in February 2009 as the MI Marketing and Admissions Coordinator for Communicare, LLC, my role was relegated to establishing and strengthening relationships with MI based referral sources in an effort to build brand awareness and introduce new referrals and admissions to Communicare's FL based Traumatic Brain Injury (TBI) rehabilitation program, located in Clearwater, FL. Upon successfully promoting Communicare's unique focus on the behavioral component of TBI rehabilitation with specific referral sources here in MI, I was able to identify and align several cases that were an ideal fit for Communicare's 'least restrictive' approach, ultimately converting them to successful admissions into their FL program.
Throughout 2009 I continued to increase the population at the Clearwater campus while preparing and facilitating the owners' plans to expand Communicare's operations into MI. After months of researching and searching for just the right location, in January of 2010 we opened the Royal Oak office, where I continued to manage the networking and development of relationships with new and existing referral sources while also working to ensure successful participation and management with every new client I continued to admit.
Throughout 2010, while continuing to admit several new clients to the FL program, I also introduced numerous admissions directly to the MI program, which eventually led the company to acquire and open a second MI office, in Bloomfield Hills, MI.
As Communicare's presence was solidified here in MI and I prepared to formally launch my own company, I left after successfully admitting 16 new clients, increasing the company's net revenue by more than $3M annually, and creating the foundation for their continuously expanding MI program. All in all, it was a successful opportunity that has connected a lot of survivors I genuinely care about to an excellent rehabilitation program, and since facilitated the start of my own company.

Brand Architect, President

Paradyme Motorsports, Inc.

July 2004 – January 2007 (2 years 7 months) Roseville, MI

At a time when most aftermarket wheel manufacturers were fighting for a piece of the large diameter chrome wheel market, I saw an opportunity to do something different- design a brand centered on large diameter painted wheels with distinct dark painted finishes. Leaning on my experience of having designed numerous wheel styles for Enkei and Toyota, my goal was to create a brand that was defined by its details- the creative integration of lug bolt openings as a component of each wheel design; the custom black and titanium pearl finishes; the innovative packaging to protect the wheels both during shipping and warehousing; and how well the overall style and fit blended with the vehicles they were designed for. While most people initially viewed the idea of large diameter dark painted wheels as incredibly risky and somewhat crazy, their tunes quickly changed when dark painted wheels evolved into the latest trend in automotive styling! At the end of the day, my ideas came to life through a niche line of innovative aftermarket wheels for passenger cars, trucks, and SUVs, with distribution through major retail chains such as Discount Tire, America's Tire Company, and Les Schwab Tire Centers throughout North America, and select outlets in Canada. -Had I realized the dishonest nature of my business partner at the time, the brand would likely be more familiar by virtue of still being in business!
