Chris DeMartine's Summary

Director of Business Development for NextMark, President of the VT/NH Marketing Group, and a member of the DMA List and Database Council. Over 20 years' experience working for multinational corporations including IBM and Canon USA, privately held organizations, and as a small business partner. Authored dozens of articles in leading trade publications related to data marketing, market trends, performance benchmarking, and operational excellence. Earned MBA at Drexel University in Philadelphia, PA and is the founder and publisher of universetrends.com.

Specialties

Organizational Development, Strategic Planning, Forecasting and Budgeting, Sales and Sales Management, Customer Development, CRM/Sales Process Integration, Business Intelligence, Marketing Analytics, Search Engine Optimization
