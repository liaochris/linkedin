Bliss Cureton's Experience

Founder CEO

The Greenbliss Design Center

November 2008 – Present (5 years 1 month)

The Greenbliss Design Center (GBDC) will open as an environmental home and
supply store, stylish home décor, learning center, eco-friendly gadgets, green products,
refurbished furniture and green building materials. This one- stop- shop will cater
to green building professionals, designers, general public, policy makers and do-it-
yourselfers.

The Greenbliss Design Center will catalyze the green transformation of the region’s
development industry taking advantage of the booming market for green building
projects and living healthier sustainable lifestyles.

The Greenbliss Design Center (GBDC) will introduce its presence online to brand the
awareness of there products and services initially to the general public, contractors
etc. The GBDC’s ultimate goal is to have a storefront that acts as a mock living space
to showcase all the products that they carry and can order. The GBDC vision is to
allow clients to have hands on contact with the products, while learning why these
particular products are beneficial to them, their family, employees and the planet. The
Greenbliss Design Center will also assist in helping surrounding communities adopt
green development policies that reap rewards for the community, the economy and the
environment.

Bliss Cureton formed the GBDC; this concept for an environmental store was created
through her own struggles to find eco-conscious products that were visual to the eye
that where located in the Detroit, Michigan area. This store will be reaching an array of
people that are not just concerned about how we live on a day to day, but generations to
come. The purchases that we make now, and how that product is being disposed of after
its lifecycle ultimately affects our planet, our health and generations to come.
