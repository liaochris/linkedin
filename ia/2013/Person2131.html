John Lichtenberger's Summary

John Lichtenberger, Esq. is the Publisher of Advertising Compliance Service, a vital advertising law reference service for attorneys and advertisers. Published since 1981, Advertising Compliance Service's topic areas examine such key advertising law areas as: "False, Unfair, Deceptive", "Substantiation", "Comparative Ads", "Commercial Speech", "Children's Ads" "Sweepstakes, Contests" and "Financial Ads".

Our mission is to help advertisers and marketers - and their attorneys - comply with the myriad federal and state court decisions, federal administrative rules, regulations, and decisions affecting advertising law. Our outstanding current members of Advertising Compliance Service's Editorial Advisory Board - who are among the top attorneys in advertising law today - are of invaluable assistance in this mission.

Additionally, I pride myself on having a powerful social media presence. On LinkedIn, I have 600+ connections and 17+ Million in my Network. My Twitter account - @AdvertisingLaw - reaches over 11,300 followers and my Facebook friends number over 1,500. Over 600 people have added me to their circles in Google+ and I have over 500 people - mostly attorneys - in my "Legal" circle. Moreover, I am very much engaged with my friends and followers on all four of these important social media venues.

AdvertisingLaw ranks No. 4 out of 2,600 lawyers on Justia.com's prestigious LegalBirds - Legal Professionals on Twitter (as of 2/14/12). This is significant since this is based on AdvertisingLaw's community followers "from the community of legal tweeters (on LegalBirds)." AdvertisingLaw also ranks quite high on LexTweet, ranking #126 out of 8,780 legal community tweeters. AdvertisingLaw is retweeted often and has a "Retweet rank" calculated as in the approx. 98.23 percentile by retweetrank.com.

Specialties

Advertising Compliance Service covers these topics in depth: Advertising Law, Federal Trade Commission (FTC), Food and Drug Administration (FDA), Social Media, Networking, Green Advertising and Marketing, Environmental Advertising and Marketing, Endorsements and Testimonials, Advertising Self-Regulation, Promotions Law, CAN-SPAM Act, Marketing, Media, and other Legal Issues
