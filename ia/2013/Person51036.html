Guy Cuthbert's Experience

Founder & Managing Director

Atheon Analytics Ltd

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Information Technology and Services industry

June 2010 – Present (3 years 4 months)

Atheon Analytics brings data to life so that people understand the patterns locked away within it.

We believe that people make better decisions, communicate more clearly and better enjoy their work when they really understand what they are doing, how they are impacting others and work with confidence in everything they do.

To help the employees, customers and suppliers of our clients achieve this we transform raw data - from internal and external computer systems - into rich, interactive visual models; far better representations of organisational performance than tables of numbers or static images. These models encourage their readers to explore them in different directions, discover interesting stories, tell these stories to others and then act upon the lessons they learn.
