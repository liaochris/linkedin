Michael P. Barrett مايكل باريت's Summary

Michael Barrett is an advertising and communications professional (Leo Burnett, BBDO) with experience with global consumer brands (including Samsung, FedEx, Wrigley/Mars, SC Johnson…) across different regions and markets.

In 2012, he moved back to the US after living, studying and working in France for 5 years. He completed his Master’s in 2010 in Global Corporate Communications and Management at the University of Grenoble then worked at the American Chamber of Commerce in Lyon, followed by 2 years at BBDO Paris.

He was also very active within the expat community and led the Notre Dame Alumni Club of France as well as found the site American Expat in France while writing for several online publications.

He is also a guitarist and songwriter and loves travel and different cultures. He is open to collaborations and new connections.
