Cathy McClelland's Summary

Member of Queensland Wildlife Artists Society Inc., Wildlife Artists Society of Australasia, Australian Guild of Realist Artists and the prestigious International Guild of Realism.

Represented by Manhattan Arts, New York, NY, Birding Art, UK, b Gallery UK, Creative Interiors Gallery, Dalby, AUS.

Professional award winning international freelance and commission artist providing paintings for the corporate and individual collector of fine art, painted in a contemporary realism style either out in nature's studio-plein air or in studio using acrylic, mixed media, or oil.

2009 Champion - Queensland Rural Women's Network Award
