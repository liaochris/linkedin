A. Yoni Miller's Summary

In school, I found that I excelled in business related courses. Initially I viewed it with somewhat of a casual interest, however - as merely an activity to explore in my spare time. As I learnt more on a daily basis, business and investing began to reveal itself as an exciting and challenging field to consider for my future. Over time it became a integrated part of me, and of my day-to-day life.

Founding Capital Y, LLC In 2012. A leading private equity real estate investment firm specializing in acquiring, managing and selling residential real estate. Focused on select markets that bring maximized value to our clients, investors, and the local community. We conduct business in areas where appreciation has growth potential and the opportunity for positive cash flow is present. Capital Y has established a reputation for bringing camaraderie, creativity, and dignity to the real estate business throughout the Eastern United States.

Shortly after the inception of Capital Y, we saw the hardship that local real estate agents were enduring due to the recent recession. Wanting to ease the burden for real estate agents living pay check to pay check, we searched high and low for a solution. After discovering the commission advance industry, we decided to launch Advanced Commission. A residential real estate commission advance company eager to provide real estate agents with a reliable cash flow solution. The service has been widely popular due to its transparent policies and competitive pricing.
