Liz Jostes' Summary

I’m a social media consultant, marketer and blogger currently living in Memphis, TN. I am co-owner of Eli | Rose Social Media, a social media marketing consultancy dedicated to helping small businesses grow online. I help business owners with their social media, SEO and business blogging. From social media training to social media management, SEO training to SEO site-wide tagging and business blogging, we help businesses with all of their online marketing needs.

After graduating with my B.S. in Marketing from Bradley University, I worked as a project manager and analyst for marketing firms. I helped guide consumer product and service companies to improve their offerings and better serve the needs of their customer base. My marketing degree and business experience has brought me great insight on consumer behavior & buying habits, and what’s needed to form strong bonds between suppliers and end users.

Combining my education, and business and consumer behavior knowledge, with my social media and blogging experience has allowed me to work helping businesses to understand and utilize social media marketing and business blogging.

Specialties: Social Media, Social Media Marketing, SEO, Consumer Behavior, Marketing Research, Marketing, Project Management, Analysis, Business, Branding, Blogging, Writing, WordPress
