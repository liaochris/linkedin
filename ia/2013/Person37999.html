Dr. John Demartini's Summary

Dr John Demartini is considered one of the world's leading authorities on human behavior and personal and professional development. His trademarked methodologies, the Demartini Method and the Demartini Value Determination, are the culmination of 40 years of cross-disciplinary research and study. His work has been incorporated into human development industries across the world.

Dr Demartini travels full time to countries all over the globe, sharing his research & findings in all markets & sectors. He is the author of 40 books published in 29 different languages. He has produced over 60 CDs and DVDs covering subjects such as development in relationships, wealth, education & business. Each program is designed to assist people to activate leadership & empower themselves in all seven areas of their lives: Financial, physical, mental, vocational, spiritual, family & social. To view visit www.drdemartini.com/products

Dr. John Demartini has been featured in a number of film documentaries such as The Secret, The Opus and Oh My God alongside Ringo Star, Seal and Hugh Jackman. He has shared the stage with some of the world’s most influential educators such as Stephen Covey, Wayne Dyer, Deepak Chopra and Donald Trump and been interviewed on the world’s leading television and radio networks such as Larry King Live, The Early Show and Wall Street and magazine publications such as Jetset, Shape, Leadership, Success, Prestige, Entrepreneur and O (Oprah).

He works with corporates, individuals, groups, social groups, helping people from all walks of life to expand their leadership, influence, service and self worth.

He has educated hundreds of young adults and regularly works with teachers and students at schools across the world to maximize the learning process.

His work with prisoners has focused on educating prisoners so they are able to choose a new reality and participate constructively in society when they are released.

For more information email: info@DrDemartini.com

Specialties

Personal and professional coaching, leadership development, team building, crisis resolution, relationship coaching, financial empowerment, mental development, grief and loss coaching, addiction resolution, career and purpose clarification, anything to do with human behavior and assisting people to expand their awareness and potential in any area of life.

Dissolving Distractions and Remaining Focused - Each day that employers and employees become distracted from their primary objective they cost their national or international corporations hundred of thousands, millions or even billions of dollars. If an employee doesn't have an organized mind and a clear set of priority actions he or she will spend an enormous amount of time, energy and thought on low priority distractions. There are times in almost everyone's life when things just seem to pile up and become overwhelmingly distracting. This is the time when they need a simple tool that can help them sort through it all and bring some sanity, order and organization back to their lives. The "Distraction Resolution - Business Development Module" is such a marvellous tool.

Prioritizing Actions and Delegating Responsibilities - When managers neglect to do their higher priority activities or delegate their lower priority activities to subordinates they set the stage for their teams to do the same. By focusing on those priorities they save their company time, energy and most importantly, money. There's an art to doing it and this is one of the most important arts to master, for without its mastery no company can grow beyond a mom and pop organization.

Self-Management and Accountability - When executives and executive teams become forgetful and unaccountable for their primary objectives they cost their corporations dearly. Having a daily checklist in front of you is like having a coach standing beside you as you're about to go into the most important game of your life. It is an absolute "must" and certainly confirms the old adage, "Even a short pencil is more reliable then the longest memory."
