Heather - The BullBuster, CPC, ACC's Summary

★ Heather is a Certified Professional Coach, and ACC (Associate Certified Coach) and has over 20 years experience in Sales, Marketing and Customer Service.
★ She has worked and lived in the United States, France, England, Italy and Switzerland, and speaks 6 languages.

★ Heather supports people to bust the BS standing in the way of successfully marketing their business. She works with individuals, small business owners, and expats to help them develop creative marketing strategies for their businesses via her Personality Based Marketing system.

★ She also leads The Expat Coach Association and Directory whose mission is to increase awareness of the Expat Coaching industry and to reach Expats around the globe and help them adapt to and live happily in their new home.
