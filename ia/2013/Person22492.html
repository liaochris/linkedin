Chef Jennifer Hill Booker's Projects

  • Cookbook: Goin' down South: A Chef's Journey Home

    Team Members: Chef Jennifer Hill Booker

    Chef Jennifer Booker is currently working on an upcoming cookbook entitled “Goin’ Down South: A Chef’s Journey Home.” This cookbook tells the story of a classically trained chef embracing her roots and realizing that her heritage is what makes her the talented chef that she is. “Goin’ Down South: A Chef’s Journey Home”, will feature delicious family recipes and unique stories recounting spending time on her family farm, learning to garden, raise chickens, hogs, and cattle, going hunting and fishing, making sausage, canning, and preserving and of course recreations of her Grandmother and Great Aunt’s favorite recipes. It will also share old family photos and the stories that made “Goin’ Down South”, a family tradition.
