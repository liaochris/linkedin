Jessica (Bean) Richardson's Summary

I’ve been selling homes in Northern Virginia & DC for 13 years. With over 300 closings & $85 million in sales I’ve represented all kinds of clients from those buying their first home to investors, Military and retirees. I’ve experienced the market’s highs and lows and I have the knowledge & expertise to help you make the best possible decisions about your home.

For listing clients: I create a highly personalized and aggressive marketing strategy incorporating all plausible forms of advertising and social media. I will work tirelessly to get your home sold and I will negotiate the best price and terms possible.

For buyer clients : I will be your guide and share my knowledge and expertise to find you the perfect home. I grew up in Northern Virginia and know this area like the back of my hand. I have sold all kinds of property from condos to houses, to land and new construction.

Specialties

Expert Buyer and Seller real estate representation, vacation homes, second homes, investment property, property management, 1031 tax exchanges, REO, remodeling advice, custom homes in the Washington DC metro area.
