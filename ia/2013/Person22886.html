Chip Marshall's Experience

CEO/COO

RedShift Systems Corp.

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Electrical/Electronic Manufacturing industry

2006 – Present (7 years)

RedShift Systems develops innovative infrared products using its core technology of micro infrared tunable filters. RedShift’s Thermo-optic Tuning Infrared (TopTIR™) optical filters are solid state devices with no moving parts, enabling compact and reliable tunable components and systems. RedShift is introducing a family of tunable infrared lasers that offer revolutionary improvements in performance over conventional FTIR based solutions for spectroscopy.

COO

Aura Communications

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Semiconductors industry

2000 – 2006 (6 years)

Aura Communications was founded to develop wireless integrated circuits (ASICs) based on magnetic induction technology. Responsible for finance, legal & IP, HRM, facility operations and manufacturing. Company was sold to FreeLinc, Inc.

Director, Uncooled Infrared Products

Lockheed Martin

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; LMT; Defense & Space industry

1993 – 2000 (7 years)

Lockheed's IR Imaging Systems (now part of BAE and formerly Loral and Honeywell) located in Lexington, MA has been a leader in infrared imaging for four decades. Was the founding manager of the uncooled microbolometer technology development at Lockheed leading to the first true commercialization of the technology. As Director, had P&L responsibility for the entire uncooled product line, overseeing business development, engineering and manufacturing.
