David Sommer's Summary

Currently Dave helps some of the world's largest brands develop their Facebook strategy.

Prior to joining Facebook, Dave was the Founder, CEO and President of Retail Media Link (RML). In 2006, GroupM/WPP acquired Retail Media Link and retained Dave to develop and lead GroupM’s shopper marketing practice. Dave built GroupM’s Retail Media Link into the $100+ Million billings business that exists today.

Prior to founding Retail Media Link, Dave was a Vice President at Y&R Advertising and led the Consumer Packaged Goods (CPG) practice. For the past 16+ years, Dave has partnered with CPGs and retailers to develop integrated marketing plans and digital marketing efforts that build brands, generate consumer excitement, activate shoppers, enhance retailer/supplier relationships and drive sales growth.

Dave holds a BA from the University of Michigan with high honors, and an MBA from Columbia Business School. Dave has a 6-year old son and lives in Westchester.
