Speaker & Writer

Michele Cushatt

January 2006 – Present (8 years) www.MicheleCushatt.com

As an experienced keynote speaker and emcee, Michele's speaking experience covers the United States and includes Women of Faith, Compassion International, Focus on the Family daily radio program and their Your Family Live! Webcast, Hearts-at-Home, women's conferences and retreats, business, writing and church events as well as various radio, video and audio recording projects.

With an understanding of what makes for excellent communication, Michele has coached multiple speakers both privately and at the SCORRE Conference (www.SCORREConference.tv).

Pulling from her entirely unique life experiences, Michele Cushatt writes feature articles, blog posts, stories and devotional meditations for numerous publications including MOPS International, Today’s Christian Woman, Fullfill Magazine, Hearts-at-Home, Upper Room Ministries, David C. Cook's Quiet Hour, Standard Publishing's Devotions, and multiple compilation books including five titles in the Chicken Soup series. She is a regular contributor at Faith Life Women (www.FaithLifeWomen.com).

After garnering an abundance of material from raising three boys, Michele wrote for Heritage Builders as their blogger for "Moms of Teenage Boys (www.heritagebuilders.com/MomsofTeenageBoys/). For three years she wrote devotionals at The Intersection (www.MeetMeAtTheIntersection.com), and weekly she posts on her own blog (www.MicheleCushatt.com).

Michele and her husband, Troy, live in Colorado with their six children, ages 21 to 6. She enjoys a good book, a long run, and a kitchen table filled with people.
