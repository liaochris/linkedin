Alyson McNutt English's Summary

I am an award-winning, internationally published freelance writer with wide-ranging experience writing for a variety of prominent print, online, custom and trade publications. I write often about home, health, business, parenting, technology and "green" topics, but I truly believe that a good writer's greatest asset isn't a preexisting expertise in any given area, but rather an deep curiosity, intellectual resourcefulness, and an authentic understanding of her audience and what they need.

In addition to my writing expertise, I am also an experienced editor: I managed freelance writers, made and collected story assignments and performed chief copy editing duties for an award-winning regional publication (50,000+ circ.)

I also have experience writing and editing study guides, textbook material and lesson-planning material for K-12 and post-secondary audiences. My background in education gives me a deep, authentic understanding of teachers' issues, pedagogical methodology, classroom management, lesson planning and the ever-present challenge of keeping students engaged.
