Karen Cynowa's Summary

If we apply knowledge to tasks we already know how to do, we call it productivity. My goal is to enable organizations of any size or kind to increase productivity to create competitive advantage.

We help you organize your time to work less hours and get more accomplished. Too many emails, not enough time to get it all done???? Struggling to stay sane in the world of information overload? Yes we have solutions! We can provide guidance to turn disorganization into organization. Our workshops are saving people in excess of 12-15 days a year.

Our ground breaking productivity and efficiency programs have immediate results. We are currently in 15% of fortune 500 companies, with case studies proving we can increase your productivity the first day after implementation!

Let me help you improve your ability to focus, gain control over your workload, stop endless meetings and improve your ability to quickly recover from unexpected interruptions.

Specialties: Professional Organizer, Productivity Trainer for all size corporations and individuals. Email Efficiency, Time Management, E-Learning, Leadership, Customer Service Training, Disorganization.
