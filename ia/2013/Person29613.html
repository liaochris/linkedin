Dan Callahan's Summary

Eight years of experience as a learning support teacher with students of varying disabilities. Three years as an Instructional Technology Specialist for a K-5 school, supporting teachers and students in their use of technology. Co-founder of the Edcamp movement, currently serving as Chairman of the Board of the Edcamp Foundation. Graduate Professor in Next-Generation Learning at Antioch University New England. Also working as a private educational consultant in the areas of technology and professional development.

Primary interests include educational technology, literacy, and professional development.
