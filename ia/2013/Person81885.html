Liz Biro's Experience

Owner

Culinary Adventures with Liz Biro food tours

May 2010 – Present (3 years 5 months)

A little taste here, a little food history there, a whole lot of fun. Culinary Adventures with Liz Biro tours serve delicious Wilmington, N.C., places and the delightful stories behind the Port City food scene.

Downtown Wilmington food walking tour features tastings at 6-7 of downtown Wilmington's hottest restaurants along with cocktails, wine, and a big serving of Wilmington food history and restaurant dish. You'll meet the city's top chefs, maybe go inside some of its best kitchens, you might get a cooking lesson, too. Find out where to eat and how the Port City become a foodie paradise. 2:30 to 5:30 p.m. Thursdays and Saturdays. Tour meets on the Riverwalk at Market and Water streets intersection, near the visitor information booth.

Other tours: Drinks Downtown cocktail tour, Top Chef Farmers Market Tour & Cooking Class.
