Mary Hickman Kruszewski's Summary

As a marketing consultant with over 20 years of professional experience, I am passionate about helping my clients grow their business. I've worked with small entrepreneurs and international companies - and I believe there is no "one size fits all/insert company name here" model when crafting and executing a marketing plan. It's all custom! From traditional marketing to web-based initiatives - each has a place depending upon the business and their target market.

I find that clients know their business well, but they need a go-to resource that will pull together their marketing efforts and stay on top of the work. We create a plan and attack their "need tos and should haves" - transforming their marketing into an important and proactive part of their business.

I enjoy and have experience in most things marketing including - strategy and planning, traditional and digital marketing, e-mail communications, PR, SEO, social media, CRM, financial analysis, training, public speaking, branding, and most recently up-to-date web design and graphic design.

Always interested in learning new skills, in August 2013 I completed the Specs Howard School of Media Arts Graphic & Web Design program to complement my 1995 MBA. I look forward to using my up-to-date graphic design skills and wealth of marketing experience to help my client's grow their business and explore job opportunities.
