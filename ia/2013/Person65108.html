Jo Condrill's Publications

  • How to Form Your Own Mastermind Group

    • Smashwords
    • May 17, 2013
    Authors: Jo Condrill, Tom Antion

    Napoleon Hill, author of Think and Grow Rich says that power is essential in the accumulation of money. Consider the power of forming your own master mind group with business associates or family members. You can each tap into the specialized knowledge and creative imagination of others who meet with you on a regular basis to share information and ideas in a spirit of harmony. This small eBook lays out a step by step process for forming and leading your own group. Small business owners use mastermind groups with their managers and employees. Professional speakers, entrepreneurs, and families have found mastermind groups invaluable. I have achieved extraordinary successes with the help of master mind alliances.
