Michiel Reuvecamp's Summary

I am passionate about design and enjoy the challenge of developing inspiring strategies that not only provide creative solutions to client briefs, but push client briefs above and beyond the clients expectation.

I have an (obsessive) love of brand strategy and identity, print, typography and photography. This, along with self initiated projects both in the print and web medium, are crucial attributes of my design process and development that help me to stretch my creative views and experiences.

Key Skills

Design Process
Having worked in numerous roles, including freelance, public and private organisations I have developed the skillset to be involved in the design process to varying levels according to client requirements. I’m experienced in all stages of the design process, including but not limited to client briefs, idea and design generation, mock-ups and final art preparation.

Relationship management
Having worked in organisations of varying sizes I consistently build strong, effective, and professional internal and external relationships to ensure projects are completed successfully to the satisfaction of all involved.

Project management
In my current role there is a real need for project management on both an individual and group basis. Dealing with contractors, colleagues and copywriters I have gained valuable experience in managing multiple projects and personnel concurrently.

Idea generation
Working on high volumes of design projects across a number of industries I have been able to consistently develop fresh, innovative designs that appeal to a constantly changing target audience(s).

Brand strategy and Identity
Valuable experience in brand strategy, implementation and evaluation allows me to develop unique, brand strategy solutions that are tailored for individual client needs.

Programmes
I am experienced and procificent in core design programs such as Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and Indesign.

Specialties

Design process, Relationship management, Project management, Idea generation, Photography, Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Indesign, Lightroom, and developing knowledge of CSS and HTML.
