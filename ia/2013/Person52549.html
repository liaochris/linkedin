Helen Ryan's Summary

"Against the grain should be a way of life." ~Nickelback

I live in several different worlds, but primarily I am a communicatrix (not a 'dominatrix.' That's something else entirely.)

Whether the medium is written words, design, photography, education, or movement, these things I do and have done share one thing in common: communication.

I was a creative director for 20 years and have been a fitness pro for at least 12 (if you count the time back in the 1980s, when leg warmers ruled). Helping people lead healthy, happy, productive lives is my passion.

After a 15-year break from exercise—where I gained a huge amount of weight—I began sneaking down road towards a new, healthier me. This simple "baby steps" approach resulted in an 82 pound weight loss, and a renewed career in the fitness industry as a instructor, personal trainer, speaker, fitness writer and teacher-trainer.

My book "21 Days to Change Your Body (and Your Life)" is the result of all that I learned on this weight loss journey, and aims to help people lose weight while living in the real world (cake, anyone?).

Recently, I used my own experience of being a struggling single mom to found a nonprofit, Reignite Inc., which helps women gain skills to re-enter the workforce so they can better support their families. It's finally moving along nicely, and we are stepping it into high gear.

In my “off time” I teach Spinning®, interview rock stars, photograph concerts, design cool shirts, volunteer for domestic violence causes and eat chocolate.
