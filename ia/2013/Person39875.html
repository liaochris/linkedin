The Cultural-Historical Foundation «The Link of Times» was set up by the russian industrialist and businessman Viktor Vekselberg in February 2004. The core mission of the Foundation is to repatriate cultural and historical values taken out from Russia in the ХХ century.

The largest world private collection of his works was purchased by Viktor Vekselberg in the US and brought to Russia by the Link of Times Foundation.

Here is another, not less important, project of the Foundation. It is the return to Russia of Historical Bells of Moscow St.Daniel Monastery. The Link of Times Foundation not only has assumed the obligations on financing this project, which moved the 19-year negotiations from the dead point, but also launched the implementation of the entire project, from making copies of the bells in Voronezh, and installation of the historical bells in St.Daniel Monastery.
