Carla Pyle's Summary

Living Home Furniture's founder Carla Pyle offers classes in the art of upholstery as well as full upholstery services to restore, reclaim and restyle old or worn furniture into beautiful finished pieces. As a textile artist, she offers a unique perspective to help her students and clients tap into their own inspiration, style, and creativity. Her mission includes keeping solid old furniture out of landfills as well as contributing to improved health of homes, people and the planet.

Living Home Furniture takes the the healthy home environment to a new level, designing and manufacturing heirloom quality organic upholstered furniture and offering custom furniture design, furniture restoration, re-styling and sustainable upholstery services. We seek to embody the Cradle to Cradle™ principles in our business model: "Our goal is a delightfully diverse, safe, healthy and just world, with clean air, water, soil and power - economically, equitably, ecologically and elegantly enjoyed".

Our customers include individuals who seek to create a healthier, non-toxic living environment - both interior and in the planetary sense.

Specialties

artisan furniture, custom furniture design, upholstered furniture restoration, organic upholstery, sustainable upholstery resources, upholstery classes, textile art & design on upholstery
