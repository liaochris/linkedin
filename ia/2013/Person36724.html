Donna Baier Stein's Experience

Author

www.donnabaierstein.com

January 1961 – Present (52 years 9 months)

I've published two nonfiction books on copywriting from McGraw-Hill and Thomson. I have published about 20 short stories and forty poems in magazines like Virginia Quarterly Review, Kansas Quarterly, Prairie Schooner, New York Stories, Confrontation, Caribbean Quarterly, and in two anthologies from Simon and Schuster. My story collection Great Drawing Board of the Sky was a finalist for the Iowa Fiction Awards, judged by Marilynne Robinson. My novel FORTUNE won the PEN/New England Discovery Award, was represented by William Morris Endeavor and came close to getting an offer from major publisher. I'm rewriting it now and will resubmit. I've been the recipient of a New Jersey Fellowship for the Arts in fiction, a Bread Loaf Writers Conference Scholarship, a Johns Hopkins University Writing Seminar fellowship, awards from the Poetry Society of Virginia, and from individual literary magazines. My novel was a quarter-finalist in the 2011 Amazon Breakthrough Novel Awards and I recently received a 2011 Fellowship for the Summer Literary Seminars in Lithuania.
