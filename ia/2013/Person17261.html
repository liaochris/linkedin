Brian Patrick Cork's Summary

Brian's business focus is Business and Executive Coaching, and Executive Search and Recruiting.

Companies engage Brian for:

People, Capital and new business or strategic partners.

Brian Patrick Cork's experience spans that of a proud and deeply involved father; successful businessman; professional athlete; volunteer firefighter; and, entrepreneur.

Brian has been a highly successful executive and entrepreneur, and has raised over $1B in private placement capital. He has led successful sales teams, built numerous management teams, and started, grown and sold several successful businesses.

Brian has been named to London's prestigious 'Ol Man Society for his contributions as a businessman and philanthropist and G2 economics "Coalition Member of the Year". He has been recognized as one of the most successful entrepreneurs in the Southeast by Catalyst Magazine as a "Top 25 Entrepreneur". In 2009 he founded the Accelerator where he helps early-stage companies with strategy, capital, and first or new customers. Recently Brian co-founded the wildly successful Team10X, a professional services firm with a focus on Cloud-based technology strategies with specific offerings for startups, small business, and enterprise-level companies.

Philanthropic interests include the Prudent Society of Optimistic Gentlemen, a global group business investors, intellectualista's, and theorists and Real Men Don't Text and Drive, and was a founding Board Member and Strategy Chair for ROI Ministry.

Brian has now celebrated twenty-two years of marriage with Joanne. They have two daughters – Haley Anne and Emma Jo. One of his greatest passions is coaching his daughters in soccer (http://shockers.wordpress.com/) where they have realized successive league championships, and numerous Cups, to include a State Championship. Brian is also the Head Coach for Milton Boys Select Feeder Lacrosse.

It should be noted that Cork is the author of the controversial Blog: www.unsinkablebriancork.com.
