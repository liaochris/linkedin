Andrea R. Sink's Summary

I am a trained problem solver, by education and experience, and have helped hundreds of people over my career get through difficult and stressful situations. I am a PACE Registered Paralegal, 555th in the U.S and one of only three in Hawaii.

I have over sixteen years in the legal field working on cases involving individuals who have been injured by others, including car accidents and work related issues such as discrimination based upon age, sex, religion, national origin, disability, etc., including protection of whistleblowers who are trying to do the right thing.

In essence I feel I have done society a great deal of good by working on these types of cases and by helping to resolve issues for those who need a voice to help protect their rights.


Hawaii Paralegal Services --- I am the sole proprietor of Hawaii Paralegal Services and can work from my office or yours. I am available for both short and full term assignments, last minute assignments, and trial preparation. My hourly fee ranges from $30.00 to $50.00 dependant upon complexity of the work involved.


Home Finder Hawaii -- I am the sole proprietor of Home Finder Hawaii and a licensed real estate agent. My legal education and training is the perfect combination in the real estate profession. I am SFR certified in short sales and foreclosures; e-PRO® certified, meaning I have specialized training in marketing your home on the internet to give you the best possible exposure. Having owned homes on Portlock Road, Hahaione Valley, and Kailua, I am very familiar with the market. I specialize in finding homes for families in the south-eastern side of Oahu.


In my spare time I enjoy spending time with my children, hiking and playing my violin.
