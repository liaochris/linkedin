Charanjeet Singh's Summary

Personally I am passionate about leading using the principle of “4P”:
1. People: Hire great people with clear responsibility & accountability. Coach them to excel.
2. Process: Define simple & continuously improving process to be followed by them.
3. Products: This would lead to great products (& technologies).
4. Partnerships: Do all this while building strong & healthy cross-functional partnerships.

1. Expertise in end-to-end product development. From ideation & strategy to requirement specification to development/engineering & go-to-market strategy
