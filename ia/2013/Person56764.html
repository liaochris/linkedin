Erika Jacobson's Summary

I am a National Academy of Sports Medicine certified personal trainer. I have been a trainer for almost 17 years and I have had the amazing opportunity to work with great fitness experts like Jillian Michaels and Jackie Warner. I was even on a hit reality TV show called “WORK OUT” that aired on Bravo from 2006 to 2008. But those accomplishments do not compare to the amount of joy I get from being a Beachbody Coach!

As a personal trainer, I went from living pay check to pay check, constantly stressed out about how I was going to find new clients, working really early hours (like 4:30am) and super late hours (like coming home after 9pm), not having the social life I wanted and suffering from horrible anxiety as my income would fluctuate greatly week to week. I loved fitness and I loved helping other people reach their goals but how was I going to get off the hamster wheel and still do what I loved?

About 1 year ago I decided to start my own online fitness business with Beachbody. Starting this business has given me a confidence that I never had in my life and I realized I can accomplish ANYTHING if I just set a goal and DO IT. I still train, but my sole income does not depend on that career anymore, nor does it dictate my moods. I made the decision that I was in control of my life and my future! With Beachbody, I get to help people across the country reach their physical and financial goals. I am all about trying to help others and open their eyes to see there is so much more to life. I want to help them learn that if they set a goal and GO FOR IT they can accomplish anything. I want others to experience what I have in these short few months.

NOW is the time to take back the control of your future physically and financially. ONLY you have the ability to make that change yourself!
