Michelle Friedman's Summary

Michelle Friedman is a certified executive coach, organizational consultant, facilitator and speaker, with expertise in women's career paths. Her clients are professional women at mid-career and above, and the companies that employ them. She is the Founder of Advancing Women's Careers, LLC, a coaching and consulting firm based outside of NYC.

Michelle helps her individual clients design challenging and satisfying life-long careers, which utilize their strengths, add significant value to their organizations and that can sustainably fit into today’s complicated and busy lives. She also guides professional women back into the workforce after pauses in their career path and works with entrepreneurs building their own businesses. Additionally, Michelle consults to progressive employers committed to attracting, retaining and advancing their female talent. Her clients are located throughout the U.S., Canada and Asia.

Michelle has experience in both corporate and not-for-profit settings. She has worked with clients within Goldman Sachs, JP Morgan, KKR, Credit Suisse, The Wharton School, Katten Muchin Rosenman LLP, Liberty Mutual, GE Commercial Finance, Citigroup, various private equity firms and hedge funds, the National Center for the Arts (Canada) and the Department of Justice (Canada), The Junior League, as well as self-employed business owners and entrepreneurs. In addition to individual coaching, Michelle designs and teaches workshops and facilitates ongoing coaching groups for professional women. Michelle is a Certified Practitioner of the Meyers-Briggs Type Indicator, Strong Interest Inventory and Hogan Assessments.

Specialties: Leadership development, team design/optimization, career management, work/life integration, entrepreneurial support, self-assessment, personal branding, goal-setting, effective time/energy management, evolving identity/roles, flexible schedules, on-ramping, workshop and mastermind group facilitation.
