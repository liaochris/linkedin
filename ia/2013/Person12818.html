Ben Crothers' Summary

I'm a design strategist and team leader with deep knowledge and expertise in UX strategy and design, as well as digital concept, design, development, production and online marketing.

That seems like a lot, but working with internal, corporate, NFP and government clients for 15 years in the online industry will give you that.

My strengths include translating business goals and user-centered priorities into measurable improvements in websites and online applications, and being the communication 'glue' between management, designers, developers and business decision makers. People who work with me know that I'm very thorough, energetic, and passionate about digital user experience.

Specialties

UX strategy, research and design, prototyping, storyboarding, running workshops, project and contractor management, team management, brand consultation, digital product concept and modelling, web development, business analysis, usability, accessibility, online marketing strategy, standards-compliant (X)HTML/5, PHP, CSS, MySQL, Photoshop, Visio, Fireworks, Dreamweaver, Axure, Tobii and Morae.
