Leigh Elliott's Summary

Leigh has over 10 years experience in the development, release, deployment and management of HRIS Applications. He has worked primarily with Oracle technologies such as Oracle Database Server, Oracle Forms and Reports, Oracle Application Server and related technologies. Between August 2010 and December 2012 he worked in a managerial role overseeing all hosting and application services for the Talent2 New Zealand based Alesco Hosted services. This role also included working with the company Fronde to provide core services for New Zealand’s web based Payroll & HRIS hosted services.

In Feburary 2013 he commenced a Product Management role as part of the Strategic and Enterprise Architecture group of Talent2's Payroll Solutions division.

When he is not working he enjoys spending time with his family, a kick of the Footy with his two young boys and spending time supporting his local Christian church the Revival Fellowship.
