Mike Whipple's Projects

  • ATIC Chairmanship

    Team Members: Mike Whipple, Mark Goldstein, Michael C. Keeling, Esq., Oris Friesen, Michael Cohen

  • Consolidate Sun Times suburban systems

    • February 2007 to Present
    Team Members: Mike Whipple, Scarlett McCrae, Ryan Brogan

    Unisys provided J2EE-powered systems to consolidate and bring efficiencies to the advertising and editorial workflows in the suburban newspaper group within the Chicago Sun Times family. As the lead systems engineer, I installed, configured, and tweaked JBOSS to accommodate client needs. Includes configuring, troubleshooting, and finessing Microsoft SQLServer on Windows Server 2003 on IBM X-Series with FC-AL SAN. Integrated and supported legacy and advertising interfaces (including graphic-image integration). And then I trained management staff how to do that.
