Elisabeth Wang's Summary

This varied career experience has allowed me diverse experiences - from negotiating with honors students over project requirements to negotiating $300,000+ contracts for remodeling projects. I've managed seniors, fund-raising campaigns and master suite additions. Have worked for-profit and not-for-profit. Director of Physician Communications and Special Projects

Piedmont Healthcare

Nonprofit; 10,001+ employees; Hospital & Health Care industry

March 2008 – December 2012 (4 years 10 months) Greater Atlanta Area

As director of physician communications for Piedmont Healthcare, I oversee, organize and distribute system level information to physicians within Piedmont Healthcare. When hired, I was first charged with assessing current communication methods and then writing and implementing a standardized, system-wide physician communications plan. Currently, I maintain this process with approximately 2000 contacts.

In addition, I am the administrative coordinator for the Physician Leadership Cabinet, a select group of 15 physicians from across the system organized by Piedmont Healthcare's CEO who meet quarterly to discuss and provide input on system level issues.

Reporting to the chief medical officer of Piedmont, I assist with special projects that include an intense physician interest. I am currently working on the communications team for Epic implementation, which includes ICD-10 readiness.

The communications plan that I implemented won two awards: 2008 Best in Class Best Use of Social Media Silver Web Award by Greystone.net and a 2009 Gold TARGET Award by the Georgia Hospital Association's Georgia Society for Healthcare Marketing and Public Relations.

I am past-president of the Women's Leadership Alliance (WLA), a women's affinity group designed to promote professional growth and networking within Piedmont Healthcare that has over 1,000 members. In previous years I've served as communications chair and mentoring chair. As mentoring chair, I played an integral part of designing and implementing a internal mentoring program for Piedmont Healthcare.
