Paul M. Heller Immigration & Green Card Lawyer's Summary

Immigration Attorney Paul M. Heller is an expert in employment-based immigration law and PERM labor certification. He is well-known and highly respected, not only by my peers in the immigration bar, but also by high-level USCIS, U.S. State Department, and U.S. Department of Labor officials. As the Principal & Founder of Heller Immigration Law Group, Attorney Heller oversees the successful representation of the firm's many multi-national corporations, emerging-growth companies, VCs & venture backed entities and individual clients. Heller Immigration works with HR/Corporate, recruiters and hiring managers and businesses in Silicon Valley and surrounding communities: software, hardware, social networking, scientific fields, academics, IT, biotech, nanotechnology and many others.

Attorney Heller graduated from UC Berkeley (BA Cum Laude) and UC Hastings College of Law - California Western School of Law. He is a member of the California State Bar - Federal Practice.
