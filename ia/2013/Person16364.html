As a business consultant I am part of IBM Global Business Services in the area of Organization Change Strategy. In my job I support clients by implementing change strategies to successfully adopt business solutions. The combination of my educational background, the knowledge I gained from following specific courses within IBM, my communication skills and my consulting & project experience enable me to successfully guide clients in their change processes.
