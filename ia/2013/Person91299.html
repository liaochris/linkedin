Melissa Palmer's Summary

Litigation attorney, with extensive civil law experience, representing major retailers, insurance companies and hospitals. Experience includes Personal Injury, Wrongful Death, Medical Malpractice Litigation, Products Litigation, Insurance Litigation -- bad faith, claims handling, Construction Defects, Construction Accidents, Business and Commercial Disputes, Consumer Litigation -- DTPA, FDCPA, Nursing Home Litigation, Automobile and Trucking Accidents, Dangerous Premises, and Employment Litigation.
Melissa Palmer is admitted to practice in all Texas State Courts as well as the United States District Courts for the Southern, Eastern and Western Districts of Texas and the United States Court of Appeals for the Fifth Circuit.
She is a qualified mediator having completed general mediation training as well as advanced family mediation training.

Specialties

Reinsurance, subrogation, premise liability, stop-loss coverage, bad faith litigation, commercial and family mediations
