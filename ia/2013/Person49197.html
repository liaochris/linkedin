Gloria Molins' Summary

Gloria Molins, is a passionate Enterpreneur, marketer thinker with strong networking skills. She speaks several languages and loves to travel around the world. After having lived in 5 different cities and travelled to all 5 continents, Gloria decided to make her dreams come true and started working on her own project.
