Ellen Huxtable's Summary

Grow business - it sounds so simple, but the reality can be overwhelming. Today a successful business must juggle finance, strategic planning, staff management, customer service, marketing, networking and more. The joy of business can be lost.

Focus, simplify, and act with Advantage Business Concepts. Cut through the clutter. Structure for growth and success. Focus on your market with new approaches. Streamline and simplify activity. Fill in the gaps to save time, money and frustration. Reclaim the joy of business.

Our offerings are built to serve you. "Focus 120" is a two-hour one-on-one session to position, set strategy and define action. "Turbo-charge & Transform!" workshops lead decision-makers to envision, plan, and act for the future. Peer Mastermind Groups in the Chicagoland area bring together decision-makers to share challenges and ideas. On-site, we can help you tackle projects and design and implement solutions to relieve business stress. We believe in and work to build the joy of business.

Ellen Huxtable is well-versed in business growth. She holds an MBA from the Kellogg Graduate School of Management, Northwestern University, is an adjunct instructor at Waubonsee Community College and is certified as a trainer and instructor in Profit Mastery. Ellen is the author of "Turbo-charge & Transform!" a strategic workbook for leadership teams, available through Amazon.com. Ellen has made presentations nationally and speaks on practical tactics for growing businesses.

Specialties: Business growth, management development, training, process design, market positioning, strategic growth, business planning.
