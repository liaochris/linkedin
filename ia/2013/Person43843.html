Fabian Gonzalez's Summary

Big 4 IT Consultant in the area of information security, privacy, risk and compliance within the full information governance lifecycle. Extensive experience in performing privacy and security assessments (including attestations) for Fortune 500 clients in the technology (including cloud computing), media, healthcare, and financial services industries.

Experience with California, U.S. and International data protection and privacy laws, including the Safe Harbor, the EU data Protection directive (EU/95/46), HIPAA privacy and security rules, the HITECH Act, GLBA, CAN-SPAM, and breach notification laws for California and other states. Familiar with FDIC and OCC regulatory audits related to the payment services industry.

Most recently, I have assisted my clients by enhancing privacy controls in response to an FTC consent decree, acting as interim Director of Privacy for a large healthcare insurer, performing large scale privacy assessments for HIPAA (including HITECH) and GAPP, deploying full-scale privacy programs for large global organizations, performing SOC (formerly SAS 70) audits, WebTrust (Trust Services) attestations, ISO 17799 security assessments, and general IT reviews.

Representative clients include: Visa, AT&T, Disney, Pandora, Johnson & Johnson, Google, Yahoo!, McKesson Corporation, Ariba, NetSpend, Cybersource, PlaySpan, Blue Shield of California, Gap Inc., Wind River Systems, and Varian Medical Systems.
