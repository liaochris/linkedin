Anthony L. Gonzalez's Summary

I am the owner of Art of Health & Fitness,LLC and Real Combat Media. Art of Health & Fitness is a mobile personal training company and an e-commerce affiliate company. Real Combat Media is a international mma and boxing online media company. I also own Real Combat Media Radio which produces a weekly mma show and a weekly boxing show on blog talk radio. Recently our show was selected as one of the feature shows on blog talk radio. Real Combat Media was founded in 2012 and our blog talk radio channel was founded in 2013. Real Combat Media's website is www.realcombatmedia.com. Art of Health & Fitness is www.artofhealthandfitness.net.

Real Combat Media's objectives are to promote mma and boxing awareness to the public. We also sell advertising services to businesses that are involved in mma, boxing and fitness.

Art of Health & Fitness long term objectives are to create a quality fitness team, so we can improve the quality of life in others.Our main objective is our client's results! We are also designed to educate the public in fitness, wellness and overall health.

We are a unique personal training company that cares about changing lives,not long term contracts! We are one of the very few training companies with no annual contracts!

In the past, my experience includes retail business management and big ticket commission sales..
