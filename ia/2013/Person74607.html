As tangible result of the work I present the full financial reports, now automatic, which saved much time in manual drafting, and my favorite: a dynamic analysis that crosses financial (ERP), web analytics (Google Analytics) and advertising (Smart ad Server) data, great for the management of web based digital products.

Upon delivery of the initiatives for the management of the holding I left Grupo RBS in search of opportunities that would bring me closer to digital products and software development teams.
