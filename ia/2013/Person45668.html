• Developed, implemented and transitioned product release approval program, inbound and outbound open source approval programs, export approval and compliance program, and IP protection processes.

Chief Counsel / Senior Counsel, Products & Technologies Law Group

Sun Microsystems, Inc.

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; ORCL; Information Technology and Services industry

June 2005 – November 2007 (2 years 6 months)

Served as Chief Counsel for CPU manufacturing group, integrated circuit testing and validation group, Sun e-commerce web store, Sun Publishing, and Global Business Services.

• Developed and implemented company-wide process to improve selection, management and evaluation of companies providing inbound contract engineering services while concurrently lowering legal costs associated with entering into and maintaining such engagements.
• Developed and presented training program to educate various internal groups on the design and manufacture of integrated circuit products and provided support to the patent and litigation groups in the same areas.
• Legal lead on committee which overhauled and improved the vendor selection and engagement process company-wide.
• Chief Counsel for group that in 2007 taped out and went to silicon for a CPU containing sixteen data processing cores each capable of multi-threading four data streams.

SATORI LAW OFFICE, Tokyo, JAPAN (1993 - 1998)
ATTORNEY AT LAW (NEW YORK / NEW JERSEY (1997 – 1998); LAW CLERK (1993 – 1997).
Japan based law firm with primary focus on transportation law and international commercial matters.

• Developed unique risk-analysis for billion dollar project finance undertaking in Malaysia allowing client to negotiate more favorable business terms with existing service provider.

• Achieved a ruling of no violation and dismissal of fine in a FAA investigation by examining technical manuals and preparing comprehensive report demonstrating that client had fully investigated and addressed potential violations and that FAA had misapplied own regulations.
