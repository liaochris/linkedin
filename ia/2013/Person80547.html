I am an 20+ year veteran of the mortgage business and have experience in originating, processing, underwriting, closing, training, management and marketing both online and on-air.

I was an early adopter of social media, beginning with blogging, I quickly became a "rainmaker" on the activerain platform and a featured contributor on zillow's mortgage unzipped. Linkedin was my first real social network and still my first place to look for new business relationships. Then Twitter became an obsession.

After closing down my mortgage company in 2009, I launched SocialYo, a social media management and training shop in Annapolis, Md. I lived in the social streams 24/7, well before many knew what Twitter was and became very skilled at fostering engagement and building relationships in the social streams.

+ Started over 50 Linkedin groups.

+ Moderator and/or host on twitter chats including #SocialChat and #UsGuys.

+ Speaker on Social Media Basics, Social Media in Real Estate and the power of digital tribes at conferences in Montreal, New York, Nashville and many other locations.

I still to this day engage in the social streams on a daily basis.
