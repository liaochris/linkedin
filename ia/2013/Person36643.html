Don Fitzmartin's Summary

Fitzmartin Consulting Company (FCC) is an Owner’s / Tenant Rep firm providing consulting and project management services to commercial tenants, building owners and facility managers. We leverage our thirty years of experience to ensure we achieve your project goals.

A relocation project is not an every-day event for most businesses. There are numerous tasks like space planning, adjacencies, construction oversight, leveling bids and managing installations of furniture, phones, audio/visual, voice and data cabling, signage and move plans that ensure minimal disruption to your business.

Because businesses generally move once every ten years, it is highly unlikely anyone on staff will have the experience and tools to oversee a landlord or tenant construction project and move. FCC is the client’s trusted advisor and advocate guiding the company and its employees from site selection and lease through design, construction and relocation process.


A PERFECT CLIENT FOR FITZMARTIN IS:

A Commercial tenant, owner or facility manager who is planning to relocate, build a new building, or expand their current office or facility.
