Brandon Steiner's Experience

CEO | Founder | Innovator | Winner

Steiner Sports

Public Company; 51-200 employees; Sports industry

1987 – Present (26 years) New Rochelle, NY

Steiner Sports was founded in 1987 as the brainchild of Brooklyn native Brandon Steiner. The fledging company’s focus was to meet corporate business growth needs using athletes for endorsements and speaking engagements. 25 years later, Steiner Sports has become the industry leader in developing and distributing game-used sports collectibles, memorabilia, and continuing relationships with athletes.

Since 1987, Steiner Sports, a division of Omnicom Group, has grown into a multi-million dollar business and has exclusive partnerships with many of the leading names in sports and entertainment. Our relationships with Fortune 500 companies, professional sports leagues, teams, and players have made us one of the most dominant forces in the sports marketing field.
