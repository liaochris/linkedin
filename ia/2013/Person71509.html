Jennifer Grigg's Summary

January 2011, I found myself unemployed. Over qualified and with London, Ontario having the highest employment rate in Canada, jobs were nowhere to be found. I said to myself, “self...why not create my own job?” With that thought, Social Dragon Marketing was born.

Social Dragon Marketing provides an effective social media presence for your company. As a business person, you already have too much on your plate and not enough time in the day to do everything on your to-do list. Why not let Social Dragon Marketing expertly manage your day-to-day social media activities? We will interact and engage with your customers so you can get on with your own work of the day.

I am passionate about connecting and engaging with people. I have more than 15 years of marketing experience in corporate settings, and have focused on building the best social media toolkit for your business.
