Jared Hendler's Summary

Born in South Africa, Jared has cultivated an extensive career in branding, advertising, digital media & technology. His creative management experience ranges from corporate to the startup world.

At MWW, Jared was responsible for building the digital media team where he led digital and social strategy, content creation, distribution & measurement. Clients included Nikon USA, Samsung, McDonalds, Sara Lee, Deloitte, Pitney Bowes Jet Blue & Virgin America.

Prior to MWW Jared worked with Katalyst Media, named top 10 in advertising and marketing by Fast Company. Clients included Clorox, CoverGirl, ClearChannel, Diagio, Microsoft, Nikon, Purina and VitaminWater.

As the Worldwide Executive Creative Director for Edelman Digital, Jared directed creative strategy with a focus on digital engagement. Management responsibilities included website builds, social media programs, e-kits, online promotions, email marketing and mobile campaigns. Clients included, Ebay, Walmart, Ben & Jerry’s, Microsoft, HP, Starbucks, Samsung, Burger King, Quaker, Amtrak, Clorox, American Heart Association, Unilever, GSK and the National Dairy Board.

Jared's entrepreneurial spirit led him to be a founder of SmashTube; pioneering digital video strategy, creative, production & distribution for Branded Programming in online, mobile video and social networks.

Jared’s managerial experience was grounded by 10 years within the WPP family. As the Executive Creative Director and co-founder of G2 at WPP, he grew the group from 2 people to over 100+ in NY, while helping establish over a dozen international offices around the WPP network.

Jared is the author of a forward-thinking digital media blog. He is a prior board member of the Art Directors Club and is a Blue Ribbon Judge for the Emmy Award's broadband category. Jared participates at industry events such as: Adtech, Digital Hollywood, Adage 2.0.

He is a graduate of the Art Center College of Design and resides as their East Coast Chapter Chair.

Specialties

Digital Media Strategy, Content Development for Digital Media, IP Development, Integrated Marketing Communications, Brand Strategy & Design.
