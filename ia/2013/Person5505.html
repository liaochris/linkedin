Amazonas Education's Experience

Education & Travel Co.

Amazonas Education & Travel

Educational Institution; 11-50 employees; Education Management industry

September 1989 – Present (24 years) Quito - Ecuador

(Formerly AMAZONAS SPANISH SCHOOL) founded in 1989, recognized by The Ministry of Education and Culture and The Ministry of Tourism of Ecuador, is an umbrella organization created with the only purpose of offering the most complete and profesional service to our students/clients in an enjoyable and relaxed environment.

We have the following divisions:

• Amazonas Spanish School: Our main división, regarded as one of the most pretigious Spanish Schools in South America, Amazonas Spanish School is one of the leading schools in South America because it focuses on high quality, professional and structured instruction. We offer Spanish courses in Quito, the Jungle, the Coast and the Galapagos Islands.
We also offer well structured volunteer programs and internships in Quito and its surroundings, the jungle, the coast and Galapagos.

We are the official Spanish School where the Diplomats of the US Department of State take their immersion Spanish programs in Ecuador.

• Amazonas Travel Agency: Offers travel opportunities to discover our beautiful country: Galapagos, Jungle, Coast and Higlands and we also offer packages to Peru, Bolivia.and the rest of Latinoamerica.

• Amazonas International Languages Academy (AILAC): Offers intensive Languages courses in Ecuador and abroad through our qualified representatives around the world. We also offer work and travel programs, internships, academic year programs, among others.
