Jeff O'Leary's Summary

I am a Realtor based out of Mississauga, Ontario, serving the west GTA (primarily Mississauga, Oakville & Burlington). My passion in real estate is lifestyle, and my focus is with how people live and play because I feel real estate is more than just a house.

I live in Mississauga with my wife Stephanie and our 3 sons. I love my community and try to be as actively involved as I can. I believe in service first, and the more you give, the more you get in return!

In my real estate blog, "The Village Guru" I talk about neighbourhoods, lifestyle and real estate. I share personal stories and my hope is that my audience will get to know me as a person and not another face on a bus shelter. I am a regular video blogger so if you want to see what I'm like in person check it out! I'm the same guy in person.

Specialties: Lifestyle Real Estate, Streetsville, Meadowvale, Port Credit, Lorne Park, Clarkson, Erin Mills, Home Staging, Blogging, Mississauga Neighbourhood Specialist, Residential, Condominium, Relocation.

Lake Wabukayne Stewardship Comittee

January 2010 – Present (3 years 10 months) Meadowvale Mississauga

Lake Wabukayne is a 40 year old man made lake in south Meadowvale. It is home to over 100 species of birds and wildlife. In 2010 the community formed "Friends of Lake Wabukayne" a non profit community group with the mission to protect the environment, educate the public and to promote community pride.

Together in partnership with the Rotary Club of Mississauga-Meadowvale and City of Mississauga, we were successful in getting a Trillium Grant from the Province of Ontario to do this. Highlights of improvements include:
1. Installing interpretive signs around the lake
2. Installing new wheel chair accessible park benches
3. Installation of a man made floating island (Spring 2013)
4. Tree carving
5. Installation of Bat Boxes
6. Creation of a Butterfly Garden
7. Tree Planting and other stewardship events

In addition, the City of Mississauga contributed in excess of $150,000 to renovate the lookout at the lake in addition to other projects.
