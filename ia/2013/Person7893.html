Angela Breidenbach's Summary

Coach, speaker, and author.
http://www.AngelaBreidenbach.com

Angela Breidenbach is a captivating speaker, coach, & author. Angie’s books include the new release, A Healing Heart, from Abingdon Press in the Quilts of Love series, Gems of Wisdom: For a Treasure-filled Life (women’s Bible study & small group book), and countless articles, devotions, and compilations. Angela volunteers as the Christian Author Network's public relations director. She's an editor with Choose-Now Publishing and support editor for the Thinking About Suicide blog (Right to the Heart Ministry). Not only did she walk the hard line of deciding to donate her mom's brain for the study of schizophrenia, but she’s also on the brain donation list at the Brain Bank-Harvard McLean Hospital. Angie is owned by #Muse, the dog-like cat, whose antics can be found on his own blog, A-Muse-ings, as well as facebook and twitter sharing his favorite books as the most well-read cat in the world. Certified CTA life coach, Stephen's Minister, Weight loss. Angela coaches health, writers, pageant contestants, media skills for interviewing on radio, TV, & in person.
