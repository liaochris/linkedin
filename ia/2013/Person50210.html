Greg Locke's Summary

I'm a 22-year marketing and communications professional and for the past 5 years I've indulged in developing a fine art glass proprietorship and deepening my relationships within the Toronto and area arts communities.

Regarding the latter, my active Board experiences on York Region Arts Council and Arts Society King have educated me on the opportunities and challenges facing most arts organizations across Ontario. Within this arena and with my administrative and marketing experience, I believe I may offer effective senior leadership to an Ontario-based arts organization.

Alternatively, I seek to leverage the experience I have gained creating and marketing my own art glass business in a larger setting, and in assisting other individuals to achieve a significant online presence. As examples please see gothamglassworks.com and greglocke.ca. In particular, I have embraced social media to market my own business and personal interests, and similarly for others. If you are a company that has recognized the importance of attracting and maintaining an audience of customers and other enthusiasts using these tools, I may bring this value to you.

I am also an active social advocate for the environment and healthy, smart growth on our communities. I am interested in paid or volunteer opportunities to support my passion in these interests.

Lastly, I possess significant experience in business development, "traditional" marketing and sales. If my profile interests you please contact me for these areas of expertise as well.

Sincerely,

Greg Locke
