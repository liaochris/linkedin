Mike Stock's Summary

In-House Agency / Brand Manager / Communications Strategist / Minutiae

Creating the communications strategy and branding guidelines to following through with the day-to-day needs of internal and external clients is just part of my day. I base my decisions on years of professional communications in different industries, strategizing with C-level and others, great design knowledge and practice, and continued experimentation with new media - and basically having fun.

On a macro scale my world consists of making arcane information digestible and relevant to a mass audience. The wide part of the funnel consists of federal programs and their rules as they trickle through a County organization, and eventually through us - the face to the people. The small part of the funnel - what people see - is how these programs and our organization benefit local employers and job seekers.

It's my job to make all that clear as a nice, spring day. And frankly...I'm pretty good at it.

One of my other roles is being the leader of a state-wide communications group that meets quarterly in person. What we discuss and decide affects communications throughout the state of Michigan.

My strengths include reaching out to disparate groups of people with the goal of achieving a unified end - and building teams who didn't know they were there, and building consensus at the same time. I love people, drawing out their ideas and learning what's important to them.

For fun I've enjoyed designing corporate portals from soup to nuts, with excellent teams: writing crisp, clear business requirements, functional requirements; designing and testing UX for global extranets.

Now I get to write a weekly article for the local paper (www.hollandsentinel.com), and be a Community Blogger for that same paper (http://www.hollandsentinel.com/community/blogs/worldofwork_blog). How cool is that?

A complete list of my articles can be found here: http://nutmeatsandbutterknives.org/article-links/
