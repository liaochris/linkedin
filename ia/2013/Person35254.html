Diane Flynn Keith's Summary

Diane Flynn Keith liberates families from conventional schooling through her writing, websites, ezines, books, programs, and speaking engagements. She raises awareness about alternative education and especially homeschooling.

Diane wrote the innovative book, "Carschooling: Over 350 Entertaining Games & Activities To Turn Travel Time Into Learning Time."

Diane publishes the "ClickSchooling" e-newsletter with free daily, web-based curriculum ideas that serves tens of thousands of homeschool families worldwide.

Ms. Keith is a popular keynote speaker at education seminars and homeschooling conferences throughout the U.S.

Diane contributed to these books:

•The Homeschooling Almanac
•The California Homeschool Guide
•The Ultimate Book of Homeschooling Ideas
•Homeschooling Styles

She has written for:

•Home Education Magazine
•Life Learning Magazine
•The California HomeSchooler
•California Homeschool Network News

Ms. Keith has been a distinguished presenter at Professional Development seminars for teachers in public charter school home study programs throughout southern CA.

Since 1997 she has been a regular guest speaker at Child-Parent Relations classes at Skyline College in San Bruno, CA. She teaches "Homeschool 101" classes at the College of San Mateo.

Ms. Keith has testified in court as a Homeschool Expert Witness.

Diane has been interviewed about homeschooling on television for ABC, CBS, and NBC. She has been quoted or featured in The Wall Street Journal, USA Today, The San Francisco Chronicle, The San Jose Mercury News, The Contra Costa Times, and more. She has been a guest on national radio programs such as The John and Ken show on KFI in Los Angeles, and on PBS stations.

Through her efforts Diane seeks to liberate families from the constraints of conventional schooling so they can identify and nurture their unique gifts and talents to create happy extraordinary lives.

Specialties

Writing, Speaking, Coaching and Mentoring on alternative education and parenting with an emphasis on homeschooling and unschooling.

The nurturing customs, practical advice, and life success principles (learned through the school of hard knocks) that he imparted to his children and grandchildren are a prescription for how to live a happy, productive, and meaningful life.
