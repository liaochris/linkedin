Brian Kuzdas' Summary

Entrepreneur, business visionary and team builder, Brian currently is the CEO and founder of RealEstateAuctions.com (REA).

The URL "RealEstateAuctions.com" was purchased in early 2010 with the anticipation of creating a national brand to service all sellers looking for an alternative to traditional real estate sales. In 2010 and 2011, REA focused on research and development, creating a prototype to service auctions in every county across the United States.

In 2012, REA launched operations and now has 106 offices in 30 states. By the end of 2013, REA intends on having 342 offices servicing all 50 states in the United States. REA's corporate office is in Los Altos, California, in the heart of Silicon Valley.

Brian began his real estate career in 1985 specializing in commercial sales. In 1990, he opened Brookshire Investments, a boutique commercial office, mainly to represent retail and multi-family projects in the Chicago metropolitan area. From 1997-2006, Brian served in the aquisition role of the company and purchased more than $350 million in Chicago real estate, converting more than 4,000 apartments to condos.

In 2006, he took the sales arm, OwnACondo.com, out of the conversion company to create one of the first, "condo-only" brokerages to service the needs of the condo buyer.

Today, Brian is focused on building RealEstateAuctions.com to become the largest auction company in the US, with expansion internationally starting in late 2013.
