Cindy Marlowe's Summary

Hi there! My name is Cindy Marlowe and I’m a Denver-based realtor, RE/MAX Associate Broker
and proud owner of Redhead Properties. If you’re a real estate buyer, seller, wholesaler,
investor or someone in the market for a great realtor in the state of Colorado, you’re in luck!

As a successful Denver realtor who’s been practicing real estate in Colorado for a decade, I’m
known as a realtor with deep understanding of every facet of the real estate market, including:

► Buying & selling
► Residential
► Investment
► Wholesale
► Green certified
► Business & Commercial
► Foreclosure
► Short sale

My full-service-realtor nature means that I’m eager to assist with the entire real estate buying
and selling process – from start to finish. I can even help with the process of funding real estate
transactions by putting buyers and sellers in touch with incredible financial experts. Like I said,
I’m a full service realtor!

Redhead Properties, one of the most trusted real estate firms in Denver, offers unique solutions
for those looking to buy real estate, sell real estate and invest in real estate. As the owner of
Redhead Properties, my team is dedicated to serving clients who are seeking real estate professionals in the following cities:

► Denver
► Thornton
► Northglenn

Buying and selling real estate can sometimes be a stressful process, but it doesn’t have to be
when you have the right realtor representing you. Buying and selling real estate should be
exciting… So when looking for a reliable, dedicated and energetic Denver realtor look no further
than me – Cindy Marlowe and Redhead Properties!
