Antonio Muñoz Marcos' Summary

Senior Counsel in Telefónica Digital Business Affairs for telco regulation, financial services and privacy, providing advice:
(i) on telecom regulatory issues involved in the definition and commercialization of Digital services in Telefónica
(ii) on Internet regulation for Digital services through Internet
(iii) on Data protection and e-privacy issues. This includes multijurisdictional regulatory advice about privacy and data protection
(iv) on payment services regulation for Telefónica Financial Services

From 2004 until 2011 I worked at the General Secretary of Telefónica España in new digital services, Internet, Content and IPRs.

In 2007 I worked at China Netcom Broadband Laboratory in project management process definition and strategic analysis.

Before working at Telefónica España I was Head of Entertainment and Personal Information Services in Terra Mobile.

I also worked as telecom engineer and software developer in Telefónica I+D in the launch of Infovia project that was the precursor and provided the first successul user access experience to Internet in Spain
