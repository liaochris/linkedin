Christopher Kilpatrick's Summary

I lead the design and development of all digital assets for The Team Advertising and Publishing's State-by-State Gardening brands. We produce localized gardening publications for 20 states and have print, online and email offerings that reflect this specialized attention to our readers unique geographic demands.

My responsibility with The Team is to design and implement our CMS websites in such a way that our writers, bloggers and in-house staff can easily communicate with our online readers and present relevant content to each geographic region quickly and easily.

Prior to my current position, I have experience in video production and print marketing. I am interested in projects that creatively bring together print, web and video.
