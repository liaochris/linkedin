Brian (Bo) Larson HC & GKCC's Summary

The goal of Ponderosa Valley Wellness is to "Empower You to Live Healthy".

We are a resource and guide to OPTIMAL HEALTH, providing education, inspiration and accountability to help you achieve your wellness and health goals. By promoting healthy lifestyles, our solutions can improve the quality of life for you and your family.

Our program is based on six points of emphasis.
1. Weight Loss (fat)
2. Maintenance
3. Taking charge of your health
4. Slowing down the aging process
5. Improving your bio-markers of vitality
6. One-on-one coaching (personal coach)

Take Shape For Life offers a simple solution: high-quality, medically developed program, combined with free coaching support to help you lose weight and keep it off. It is not a diet, but a two-phased approach for weight loss and maintenance that can help you achieve optimal health.
