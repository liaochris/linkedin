Kathy Vick's Summary

I believe that every girl is a secret weapon and a power that can change the world. And that in every woman’s life there is a tipping point where she decides whether she has the courage to own her destiny.

I believe that when a woman can’t do this, it can lead to loneliness, divorce, resentment unhappiness and a profound cost to families, marriages and relationships.

I believe that it is our job to help other women be successful, because we all need a tribe, a team, true Ya Ya sisters who understand the power in collaboration.

I believe you are much braver than you know and that sometimes you just need to go afraid.

Run like a girl was created for women who still believe in the power of their dreams, their unique spark of genius and their own beauty.

The world is waiting…

“It is the dream you had at ten and still have at fifty that you need to pay attention to.”~Kathy Vick
