Chris Lorensson's Summary

I'm a Swedish-American multi-talented designer living and working in Huntington Beach, California. At my core, I am passionate about using and creating beautiful, innovative and usable interfaces for any platform.
Since the turn of the century I have been a Chief Designer three times, Creative Director three times, design agency Owner twice and an Interaction Designer. I have professional experience designing for print, online, advertising, publications and music across a broad range of industries.
I've been designing professionally for 11 years. For the past 6 years I have been keenly focused on User Interfaces, which includes Interaction, Usability, Accessibility and Information Architecture. While being a designer historically, my nature is much more in-tune with usable interfaces.
When I moved to Bristol in 2005 I began freelancing full-time, working with organisations such as Ebay UK, BBC, Expedia, cxpartners, Fasthosts, Wickes, Coca-Cola and countless medium-sized businesses. In 2011 I completed a long-term contract as Interaction Designer at Fasthosts Internet Ltd in Gloucester, and most recently I helped Ascentric plan and design their exciting new consumer-focused product offering as their Chief User Interface Designer.
I have recently relocated back to Huntington Beach, California.
Specialties: User Interface Design with a focus on usability, accessibility and interactive innovation.
