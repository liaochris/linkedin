Mark Miller's Summary

My latest book is "The Heart of Leadership: Becoming a Leader People Want to Follow." I'm also the author of "The Secret of Teams: What Great Teams Know and Do", and the co-author of "Great Leaders Grow: Becoming a Leader for Life" with Ken Blanchard.

In over thirty years with Chick-Fil-A, I have served in numerous leadership capacities including restaurant operations, quality and customer satisfaction, and corporate communications.

I travel extensively, teaching around the world on a variety of topics including teams, servant leadership and training.

I am the co-author, with Ken Blanchard, of the international bestseller "The Secret:What Great Leaders Know and Do."

Specialties: leadership, training and development, servant model leadership, teambuilding
