David Schatsky's Summary

David Schatsky is a seasoned executive with a broad and global business and information technology background and expertise in strategy and market trends analysis. He is currently working at Deloitte identifying and analyzing trends for the firm's senior leadership.

He founded Green Research, a research, advisory and consulting firm focused on corporate sustainability in 2009. He was president of JupiterResearch until its sale to Forrester Research in the summer of 2008. He led the global research team at Jupiter from 2003-2006. Before that he was an analyst and research director, focusing on e-commerce infrastructure, Web site technologies and operations, and online business models and processes in digital media, marketing and advertising. He joined Jupiter before its IPO in 1999 and saw it through several acquisitions and two sales.

Jupiter recruited him away from a career as a systems consultant on Wall Street, where over a period of several years he built trading, allocation, analysis and reporting systems for clients such as JP Morgan, Credit Suisse, UBS, WestLB and, while at Cambridge Technology Partners, Merrill Lynch.

For several years he worked in international business development for an Italian software company based in the seaside town of Viareggio. Before that he worked at artificial intelligence pioneer Symbolics, joining a year before its IPO. Symbolics was the first company to register a .com domain name.

Specialties: Schatsky is an expert in identifying and analyzing business, technology and societal trends; corporate sustainability and cleantech; the digital media, marketing, advertising and commerce markets; and building and running research and information businesses.
