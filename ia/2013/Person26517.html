Mary Kaye's Summary

As a career coach and founder of Comeback Moms, I inspire women to realize the work that's right for them. My mission is to match the capabilities of at-home moms to the needs of our evolving world. I help women through one-to-one coaching, group workshops, and motivational speaking engagements. I believe in the strength, imagination and influence of mothers.

During my decade of experience in training, recruiting, strategy, and leadership development I directed programs at Fortune 500 companies including GE Capital, Campbell Soup Company and Diageo. Prior to my corporate experience I was a U.S. Navy P-3 pilot, United Airlines 727 pilot and a private flight instructor.

As a devoted mother to three energetic kids, an entrepreneur and an avid volunteer, I am a true Comeback Mom.

Specialties

work-life possibilities for women, career coaching, entrepreneurship, motivational speaking, interview preparation, resume writing

Mary Kaye's Experience

Career Coach and Founder

Comeback Moms

March 2010 – Present (3 years 6 months)

Comeback Moms, a coaching company based near Boston, advises at-home moms who are ready to realize their work-life possibilities.

Through a combination of powerful questioning, self-discovery exercises and assessment tools, clients obtain a keen awareness of their passions, values and strengths (PVS). Career opportunities are identified that meet the clients PVS and lifestyle desires. With guidance clients develop the skills to network and position themselves to attain their ideal career.
