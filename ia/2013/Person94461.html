♦ BUSINESS INFO ♦

Global Packaging & Supply Chain Solutions

We partner with our customers to improve productivity
and lower the total cost of ownership throughout the
supply chain. Reduce your packaging inventory, improve
packaging design and maximize your trailer/container capacity.

- Lower freight costs and material handling concerns with
Fibercore, a 100% recyclable wood replacement
packaging product that weighs 40% less.

Our areas of specialty include Packaging Design, Sustainable
Packaging, Crating, Retail Packaging, Global Packaging
Solutions, Contract Packaging, Logistics, Supply Chain
IT Solutions, Metal Stamping, Stratetgic Sourcing,
Marketing & Promotional Products. We also work as
consultants to improve packaging design and streamline
your vendor supply base.

WE HAVE:

♦ Redesigned packaging which resulted in savings of over 40%
on packaging, warehousing, and transportation.

♦ Increased by 30% the amount of product that can ship
in a truck/container by changing packaging design.

♦ Eliminated quality issues overseas and shortened the
development cycle by designing & prototyping in the US.


Need packaging or products manufactured in the US,
China or Worldwide?

We have innovative packaging design solutions and
manufacture globally for local delivery to your facility or
your contract manufacturing partner. Our competitive
advantages are our fast turnaround, ability to design
locally and manage the manufacturing & logistics of
packaging solutions globally.
