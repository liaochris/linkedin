Brian Powell's Summary

Passionate | Strategic | Ideator | Leader

Specialties: Integrated marketing leader with an entrepreneurial sprit, and experience spanning strategic planning, branding, business development, advertising, promotions, shopper marketing, business-to-business, sports marketing, digital and social media, new product development, non-profit and cause marketing.

As an agency veteran with an entrepreneurial spirit, Brian has managed big brands such as Nokia, Pizza Hut, and Dr Pepper. And is equally passionate about ideas with business and social impact, with iconic nonprofit experience including UNICEF, Feeding America, and Make-A-Wish. He was previously cofounder of a consultancy/design firm before spending ten years at Omnicom Group agency TracyLocke as a Managing Director. While there he launched the agency’s cause marketing division working with client teams for 7-Eleven, Cargill, YUM, and others on cause related campaigns. Now as Managing Partner of StudioGood and patron, he’s a strategic leader, creative ideator, operations director, and is head of the Dallas office. He sits on the board of the Dallas Little League, and serves as a marketing advisor to Livestrong.
