Volunteer Coordinator

Let's Get Global

Nonprofit; 1-10 employees; Civic & Social Organization industry

April 2010 – August 2010 (5 months)

Let's Get Global - Crossing Borders, Sharing Lives is a national movement whose mission is to encourage and assist youth in the United States to have international experiences, particularly during the year between high school and the next phase of their lives.

My role as volunteer coordinator is to help this movement grow by managing and supporting a team of volunteers whose diverse talents and skills make it possible for Let's Get Global to educate the youth, parents, and educational establishment about the benefits of international experiences, by informing people of existing programs, and by making the opportunity available to all segments of the population.

Our vision is that an international experience becomes a popular practice in the United States.

Let's Get Global is a project of US Servas Inc. Servas is a worldwide cooperative cultural exchange network established in 1948 and composed of member hosts and travelers working together to foster peace, goodwill, and mutual respect. Servas encompasses over 15,000 homes and institutions in more than 125 countries on six continents.
