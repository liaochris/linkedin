Cory Burbidge Me@coryburbidge.com's Summary

For 15 years Mr. Burbidge worked with, built, designed, programmed, and designed for and with computers. Today he is currently working "IE Productions" & "CR Signage" as a Graphic & Web Designer. In The past he has worked for Teton Outfitters / Klim as an Product "apparel" line designer & BYUI as a graphic Design student teacher. With a BFA (bachelors of Fine Arts) in Graphic Design; he has been highly trained with an eye for design and a proficient ability to understand, help, and communicate with clients. He has gained extensive experience in: Photoshop, Illustrator, Dreamweaver, InDesign, PHP, CMS's, Graphic Design, Typography, Apparel Design, CSS, Flash, and working with and designing for the web. He is a certified A+ Technician and certified in various Microsoft courses.
