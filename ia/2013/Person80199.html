Leonie Smith Social Media & Cyber Safety's Summary

Digital Breezes provides an integrated approach to Social Media. Leonie Smith can set up your social media, provide stunning graphic design for it, train you or your staff either one on one or in a workshop, and go into all the detailed technical training you or your staff would require.

Leonie is also known as "The Cyber Safety Lady" she is an advocate for safe internet use. She gives talks and workshops to schools parents and business on cyber safety.

Because Digital Breezes is a fully integrated service with the technical side of social media, online culture training, marketing and Graphic design this means that instead of having a whole bunch of different companies all relying on YOU to work it all out. Leonie can make it all work smoothly together.

Leonie also makes it easy to manage your own Social Media, no need to spend hundreds or thousands a year on a social media service. She can show you some great free tools to manage it all so that you don't have to spend all your time on your online marketing.

Leonie's way of personalised training means she goes at your pace. Leonie is happy to either start you right from the beginning with really basic social media skills, to upskilling the more adept user of social media. She can provide one on one training or custom design a workshop for you and your staff.

Are you worried about your privacy online? Linking your personal social media with your professional? Leonie's expertise in Cyber Safety will also ensure that you can keep the two separate, and your online accounts will be secure. Leonie is a Facebook Privacy expert!

Leonie takes the fear out of social media, and makes it easy.
