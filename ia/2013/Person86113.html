Marion Yoder's Summary

I began my career in the tax preparation industry 13 years ago. As a tax professional I helped clients navigate the complex tax code to minimize their tax burden. In 2006 I graduated with my degree in marketing from Kent State University. Thereafter I was promoted into field marketing roles where I developed creative marketing plans with budgets as large as $800K for markets in the Eastern US. In 2008-09 I challenged myself as a multi-unit manager to manage 28 retail tax preparation locations and lead a team of 100+ tax preparers, 28 Office Supervisors and 5+ District Support Staff members. Today I'm breaking into the automotive industry by joining AutoNation, America's largest retailer of automobiles, at their Honda store on the west side of the Atlanta Metro.

Specialties: Multi-site management, marketing, advertising, sales, media buying, public relations, budget analysis, team building, campaign analysis, social media, P&L management and international business.
