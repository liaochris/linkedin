Keely Saye's Summary

Inbound marketing — the process of getting found by customers — is a key driver in today’s cross-channel marketing programs. As director of inbound marketing at Riggs Partners, I lead a team of inbound marketers who specialize in developing and placing content that pulls highly niche-oriented target audiences into our clients’ digital marketing sphere and engages them with products, services and causes. Working as part of a comprehensive on and offline marketing strategy, these inbound marketing services are a powerful tool in driving web traffic and converting leads in today’s new economy.

Keely Saye's Projects

  • National Public Awareness Campaign for Child Drowning Prevention

    • September 2010 to September 2012
    Team Members: Keely Saye, Beverly Payton, M.A., APR, Maureen Williams, APR, Nadina Riggsbee, Johnny Johnson, Cindy Johnson, Kim Burgess, Robert Ogoreuc, Gerald M. Dworkin, Rosemary Tottoroto, Nora Ananos, Paula DiGrigoli, Laurie Batter, Media Relations & Marketing Specialist

    I submitted a strategic communications plan on behalf of the National Drowning Prevention Alliance that resulted in a $1.286 million contract from the U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission. Our dedicated, virtual team carried out a comprehensive public education campaign aimed at reducing the number of child drownings in swimming pools and spas. This including traditional and social media relations, video and audio public service announcements, (including Spanish versions), a pool owner's training video (also translated into Spanish), a revised organization website, two tri-fold brochures, materials to reach low-literacy audiences, more than 10 exhibits and presentations at key influencers' trade shows and conferences, a preschool lesson plan and personal outreach to state public health departments. The campaign reached millions and created many resources that are still widely used today by children's aquatic safety advocates.
