Jeph Johnson's Summary

Jeph's passion for creativity, innovation and emerging technology transforms his clients vision into digital works of art. His commitment, creative direction and innovative development have enabled him to deliver interactive solutions to his clients needs from concept to execution.
For almost 11 years, Jeph Johnsons unprecedented talent as an Art Director / Developer has landed him at some of the largest advertising agencies in the world.
He has had the pleasure to work with some of the industries most well recognized agencies such as JWT, Tribal DDB, Razorfish, and Y&R just to name a few.


In addition, he has worked on many national brand campaigns including McDonalds, Toyota, American
Airlines, Sears, Nestle. Ge, BP and Lowes. In the last few years, Jeph has seen a shift in the technologies used on the web and has kept himself ahead of the game by learning and incorporating HTML5, jQuery, Phonegap, jQ Touch and JavaScript into his skill set.
Implementing these new technologies have helped his clients campaigns stay relevant and accessible on both desktops, notepads, and mobile devices.
