Bret Jacobson's Summary

Bret Jacobson works with the world's leading brands and advocates to craft digital strategies, products, and ads with one goal: make life better for our clients and the world a freer place.

A veteran of launching new-media tools inside old-school public affairs organizations, Bret applies emerging digital tools to defend and promote classical ideals of liberty, small government, and less regulation for major international corporations, trade associations, think tanks, and political campaigns.

Bret’s issue advocacy work has been covered by radio, television, and new media, as well as the New York Times, Wall Street Journal, Associated Press, and major regional and local news outlets—and pretty much all of the top conservative/libertarian online news outlets.

Bret is a graduate of the University of Oregon, which finally has a good football team. He can take no credit for this.
