Dave Herman's Summary

I am a writer, actor, producer, videographer, video editor, and director. Currently I make sketch comedy videos. I'm also writing a blog about my experience working 100 jobs in 2012.

I am also a media professional with extensive television production experience.

In my recent position as an NBC Page, I worked audiences and booked tickets for Saturday Night Live, Late Night with Jimmy Fallon, and Dr. Oz. I also functioned as a liaison to the public by leading over 500 studio tours through NBC studios. At CNBC I worked on five live TV shows with responsibilities including catering to talent, triggering relevant graphics in the control room, and logging daily program content.

I've worked as a PA on Morgan Spurlock's Freakonomics movie, Toddlers and Tiaras, Kitchen Nightmares, Iron Chef America, and an indie film called Blue Collar Boys.

My past internships include editing video for Bravo emerging media, video content management at iVillage, audience at The Late Show with David Letterman, and production at The Colbert Report.

I'm also a writer and performer at The People's Improv Theater and Upright Citizens Brigade.
