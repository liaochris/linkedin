David Jones' Summary

Known by most in professional world as Dj. I am a Software Engineer at heart. I have B.S. in Comp Sci and Minor in Mathematics. I have more than 15 years of experience in the software engineering. I work at Oracle America Inc. My most thrilling academic work involved work with Nachos, instructional software that allows students to modify and enhance operating system code. My most fun Comp Sci language was Scheme; it was quite interesting and forced one to think outside the box. My most fun professional experience is working with the J2EE technology stack. I have dabbled in many fun and enriching technologies such as Asterisk PBX, Drupal, Dojotoolkit, and many others.

My beginning started in a much different place DuPont and Department Of Energy as an engineer located in Aiken, SC. I decide to do what I loved which was software and here I am.
