Jeff D Brown's Summary

Personal Branding Statement = "I help job seekers uncover, design and communicate their unique ROI VALUE to their target employers." (ROI = Return on Investment)

What's yours? (in < 25 words)

Too many seekers and not enough jobs...what is the solution?

I believe it's all captured in your Personal Branding statement, but how many have one? Employers and hiring managers look at a resume (on average) for 6 seconds yet what do they see and is it enough to get a job seeker to the next step (interview)?

I am an "Expert" in Career Advancement since I have spent in excess of 10,000 hours personally and professionally in that area over the last 4 years. (Malcolm Gladwell in "Outliars").
