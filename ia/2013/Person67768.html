Jon Bloor's Summary

I am a partner in the corporate services team at Prettys, based in Ipswich and Chelmsford.

I specialise in corporate transactions and commercial law.

I have a particular interest in online, digital and social media businesses, but my experience covers a wide variety of sectors from care providers, manufacturing and sales through to professional services businesses.

My experience gained at leading national and global practices over the last 12 years allows me to focus on providing pragmatic, value for money advice to owner managed businesses and SMEs.

I also write and speak about legal aspects of social media, cloud and mobile technologies.
