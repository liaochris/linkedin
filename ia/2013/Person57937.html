Jamie Biesemans' Summary

Editor-in-Chief of FWD Magazine, the premiere home entertainment magazine of the Benelux, the reference for its testing of audio, video en mobile devices, and EISA-member. FWD appears bi-monthly in print and daily online at www.fwdmagazine.be.

Online media consultant specialised in planning and managing content workflows, content strategy and media websites, business development, and media projects. Current assignment at Minoc Online.

Dedicated freelance journalist specializing in and passionate about information technology, business and telecom. Likes to write about other topics as well on occasion.

Currently writing for Clickx Magazine, EOS, FWD, Shoot and PC Magazine Benelux, and others. Past experience includes writing for Het Nieuwsblad, De Standaard and ThisisIT/Data News, and writing website copy in English for The House of Marketing.

Author of 'Snelgids iPhone 4' (ISBN:9789045649276), 'Snelgids Office 2011 for Mac' (ISBN: 9789045649756) en 'Snelgids iPad 2' (ISBN: 9789491326202)
