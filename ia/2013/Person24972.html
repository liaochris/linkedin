Cindy Laverty's Summary

I am a family caregiver coach and consultant. I help family caregivers learn creative ways to care for a loved one without losing their lives. It's a process but with the tools and information, it's available to everyone. When someone says YES to caregiving, it is a life-changing process. How you learn to manage the journey is a choice and you should not try to do it alone. I learned the hard way and today, I coach caregivers through the process. I save caregivers time, wasted energy, and I keep them sane!

I am the Founder and CEO of The Care Company, an online site dedicated to provide caregivers with information and the support they so desperately need. The Care Company, http://thecareco.com has numerous affordable programs and products to help with the caregiving journey.

I am the author of Caregiving - Eldercare Made Clear & Simple and I speak on a variety of caregiving issues. All keynote addresses can be tailored for longer seminars.

My goal is to create a nation of empowered family caregivers one person at a time!

I am dedicated to helping family caregivers understand that Caregiving is the Ultimate Self Help Tool and when you embrace this you become an Empowered Caregiver and your life is transformed forever.

Caregiving is a journey. It is a process that’s different for everyone. My goal is to provide clients with the information tools and support they need all along the journey. Not just information, but how to learn what to do and when to do it, how to link the pieces together, and what to expect along the way — including surprises. We provide a roadmap from beginning to middle to end and in the course of this process...self discovery is the BONUS!
