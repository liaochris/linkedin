Kirill D. Azovtsev's Summary

I am a real estate advisor and consultant in New York City at Jones Lang LaSalle (“JLL”), a full-service, global commercial real estate firm. My primary specialty is tenant representation; my aim is to identify cost saving strategies for acquisition and disposition of office space. My real estate knowledge, combined with the broad range of resources at JLL, brings a creative approach to identifying client solutions that best fit their needs. Our goal is to not only solve our clients’ immediate needs, but to also build a long standing relationship with them through trust, integrity and the quality of service delivery, ultimately meeting the needs of our clients as they grow and evolve.

I would like to connect with C-level executives, real estate decision makers, landlords, property owners, investors, fund managers, and start-up entrepreneurs within all industries. I’d also like to connect with specific service vendors.

By leveraging our global platform at JLL, I can facilitate most commercial real estate transactions around the world. If you have any real estate requirements, questions about your lease or building, interest in expansion, or are simply curious about the commercial real estate market, feel free to reach out to me.
