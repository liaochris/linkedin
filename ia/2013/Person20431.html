Caroline Turner's Summary

Caroline Turner is principal of DifferenceWorks, LLC, a firm creating a new dialog around differences in the workplace. A highly-regarded corporate lawyer, and former general counsel of Coors Brewing Company, Caroline brings experience, action-oriented solutions and a focus on possibilities to corporations and businesses challenged by diversity issues. She is the author of "Difference Works: Inclusion, Engagement and Retention for Better Business Results," published in 2012. DifferenceWorks empowers businesses, groups and individuals with tools, information and inspiration, offered through speaking, workshops and consulting programs. A popular public speaker, Caroline presents complex and sensitive information in a clear, understandable and memorable manner.

Specialties: Differences in the workplace, diversity, inclusive workplace cultures, gender differences at work, generational differences, emotional intelligence, public speaking, consulting, workshop facilitation.
