Jeffrey Haney's Summary

Starting in the business as a 25 year-old 2nd camera/intern for a live, 64 episode satellite broadcast with Dr. Robert Ballard (explorer who found the Titanic) at the Monterey Bay Aquarium

Graduated from the Art Institute of Seattle, and finding his future wife in the Fremont District of Seattle.

Started working in production fixing and selling grip and lighting equipment, then as an editor at a local cable television station.


Relocated in 2003 to Salem, Oregon.
Jeffrey worked at Comcast Spotlight until December of 2008, when reorginization and the economy forced his 5 year plan of leaving Spotlight into a 2.5 year plan.

Having already started Lights in the Attic Creative Media in March of 2006 as a wedding and event videography business part-time, Jeffrey moved into full-time mode in December, 2008.

We serve the entire Pacific Northwest and beyond with high-quality HD video and web production.

Specialties: Certified Steadicam operator
