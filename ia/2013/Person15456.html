Bob Moore, CMC, Chief Optimizer's Summary

Bob Moore is a business growth strategist and talent development specialist who works with C-level executives, entrepreneurs and small business owners who want to more effectively navigate their stages of growth and accelerate their progress to sustainable profitability. He is CEO/Chief Optimizer of Effectiveness, Inc a resource center he founded in 1975 to work with business leaders and executives of knowledge based enterprises who want sustainable, profitable growth.

Bob is author of the Amazon best-selling book, Turning Good People Into Top Talent: Key Leadership Strategies of a Winning Company, over 500 articles on Leadership and Talent Development and the publisher of the weekly, Timely Tips for Transformation From The Desk of Bob Moore dot com.

Bob is an internationally recognized specialist in the use of assessments for selecting, aligning and developing top talent and high performing teams. He is a mentor to consultants and coaches and contributor to The Indie Consultant, an online magazine for Independent Consultants. In addition to numerous assessment and coaching certifications, Bob was elected a Certified Management Consultant (CMC) in 1975.
