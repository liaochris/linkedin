Michelle Foster Entrepreneur Extraordinaire's Summary

I am the owner and creative force behind Macon Beads™, LLC. My mission statement is to create original handcrafted jewelry for fashion conscious consumers, with the highest quality components. My success is driven first and foremost by having outstanding customer service. My repeat customers continue to bless me.

I manage the small but ever growing business, have created an extremely effective marketing and promotions plan, I design and create beautiful and unique artisan jewelry, I host the fun and lucrative jewelry shows, make all the buying decisions and am the distribution department.

My short term goal is to have my own line of products in boutiques around the SE.

My long term goal is to have major companies buy my products.

Then I'd like to help other women start companies. I haven't thought through that far yet in exactly what capacity that will happen, I just know I'd love to give other women the fulfillment I have received from owning my own business and being able to make all my own decisions.

Specialties

everything about running a one-person successful business, jewelry design, purchasing, organizational efficiency, advertising and marketing strategy, client relationships, editing and copyrighting, promotion and publicity planning
