Bas van Haren's Summary

I am a pragmatist by nature, a designer by heart and have the mind of an academic professional dominated by the passion for learning. My main drive? Igniting the drives of others through technology.

• Educated in user-centric design, usability and educational science and technology
• Years of professional experience within serious gaming and playful interaction
• Experienced and passionate speaker (guest lectures, TEDx, symposia, workshops, master classes, pecha kucha’s)

Notable achievements:
• Part of the team that made the first e-learning gaming platform for use in healthcare (Gaming in de Zorg, 2006)
• Guest lecturer at various schools and institutes (IMC Weekendschool, Alfa College, Hanze UAS, University of Twente: 2007-now)
• Conceptual creator of the popular internet-hit “Binnenhof Battle” (2010, 2012)
• One of the project leaders of the team that made the multiple award-winning Wii-game for blind and sighted children (The Explorer and the Mystery of the Diamond Scarab, 2012)
• Speaker at TEDxHanzeUniversity (“The Power of Play”, 2012)
• Moderator/presenter at Indie Gameleon 2013, the first Northern Dutch game event/game jam

Specialties: project and team leadership, educational design, instructional design, game design and development, co-creation, usability, e-learning, implementation, user-centered design, public speaking, serious gaming, gamification
