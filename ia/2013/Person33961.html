Full Custom Layout Design Engineer

Intel - Folsom, CA.

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; INTC; Semiconductors industry

January 1994 – October 1999 (5 years 10 months)

Served as custom layout and final verification lead for Intel® PCD/GCD/CEG engineering layout team. Contributed to many of Intel's Chipset/ Graphic production products such as the Intel® 440BX AGPset, which was the first chipset to optimize Intel® Pentium® III processor performance for 3D and video applications with 100-MHz system bus/SDRAM. As Intel's second-generation AGPset with Intel® Quad Port Acceleration (QPA), the Intel 440BX AGPset improved the speed of the system bus from 66 MHz to 100 MHz, while increasing the width and depth of buffers to the system bus, Accelerated Graphics Port, SDRAM, and the PCI bus.

:: The time I spent with this group was a privilege. Wonderful people...
