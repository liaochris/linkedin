Audrey Schroder's Summary

Hi, I'm Audrey. I work and play in social media.

I am the Social Media and Marketing Strategist for leading electronic cigarette developer and distributor Vapor4Life. I created the company's social media strategy and developed the online presence and voice of the company. My role has evolved from community management to training the marketing team, and managing our affiliate program. The company's social media team boasts high community engagement on several popular social media platforms. We manage our own blog and forum, and have over 10K followers on Facebook, Twitter, Pinterest, LinkedIn, Foursquare, YouTube, Google+, several forums, and the world's largest e-cigarette forum - ECF.

My contributions to Social Media Club are two-fold. As a board member for the Chicago chapter, I help build our community and publicize monthly events. I also write for the Social Media Clubhouse blog.

In a quest to develop my marketing skills I attend many business conferences and training classes. I enjoy networking both on and off-line.
