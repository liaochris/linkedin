Dian Murray's Summary

I’m a “geek” with a passion for helping people, organizations and companies find new uses and/or improve the way they utilize technologies.

My twenty five years of experience in the technology industry runs the spectrum of working for and with small one and two person businesses to Fortune 500 corporations on business solutions and individuals on consumer solutions.

In 2002 I started my consulting company Risolv. The name is a play on the Italian verb risolvi; meaning “to solve”. Teaming with others to solve problems is at the heart of what I enjoy and do best. I've been involved with every aspect of the software business including developing software, developing and delivered training on software and the software development lifecycle, and selling software and services.

I’m looking for opportunities as either a contractor or employee.

Specialties

Training, project management, technical writing, account management, problem solving, time management, computer software, software design, electronic publications, online communications, program management
