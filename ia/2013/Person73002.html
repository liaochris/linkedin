Kathy Morelli's Summary

Kathy Morelli, LPC, has a professional marriage and family counseling practice working with women and families across the lifespan in Wayne, NJ. She has a special focus on pregnancy, birth, postpartum and trauma (using EMDR) in Wayne, NJ. Kathy also offers phone consultations and web-based courses. She has a long-term interest in mindbody therapies and is trained in shiatsu, acupressure and Reiki.

She writes and speaks on Stress and its effects in Pregnancy, birth comfort measures and perinatal mental health and has appeared at various universities and conferences across the country. She blogs about perinatal mental health for Lamaze's Science & Sensibility and the Huffington Post. Kathy is a Board Member of the New Jersey Chapter of the March of Dimes and alos volunteers for Postpartum Support International (PSI). Visit her at birthtouch.com and kathymorelli.com

Her books include: BirthTouch® Shiatsu & Acupressure for the Childbearing Year, BirthTouch® Healing for Parents in the NICU, and BirthTouch® Perinatal Mental Illness for the Childbearing Year.

Specialties: Marriage counseling, helping women with the emotions of pregnancy and birth, pregnancy loss, and perinatal mood disorders, BirthTouch Moms®, BirthTouch® Shiatsu & Acupressure for the Childbearing Year, BirthTouch® Healing for Parents in the NICU, Gentle Birthing Hypnosis to manage fear and anxiety during pregnancy and birth.

Past positions include MindBody Specialist at Mountainside Hospital and Mental Health Counselor at Newbridge Services working with those with serious and persistent mental illness.
