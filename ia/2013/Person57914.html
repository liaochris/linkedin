The Real Estate Marketing Society

I founded The Real Estate Marketing Society because I saw a growing gap in the real estate industry. We are no longer in the days of old where real estate agents, loan officers, home inspectors and title agents are a dime a dozen. Even in those days really good ones were hard to find.

We are in an era where closing a real estate transaction is really a team sport. All parts are equally important and need to be on the top of their game. The Real Estate Marketing Society is a place for real estate professionals to share and learn incredible business and relationship building techniques.

The real estate community needs to come together as a team to do our part to get this housing market back on track. This group is a place to get the systems, strategies and techniques to do just that and explode your businesses at the same time.
