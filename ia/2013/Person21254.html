Curtis Conley's Publications

  • Identifying critical success factors for Enterprise Social Network success

    • Ark-Group
    • May 27, 2013
    Authors: Curtis Conley

    The benefits that can be realized through the adoption of an ESN are both many and varied. As organizations are increasingly drawn to ESNs by their potential benefits and begin to launch their own networks, the importance of addressing the factors that are most critical for the success becomes paramount. This article presents a framework and descriptions of the most common CSFs for ESN success in five factor groups; factors related to the organization, the initiative, the manager, the team, and the external environment.

  • Factors Critical to Knowledge Management Success

    • Advances in Developing Human Resources
    • July 15, 2009
    Authors: Curtis Conley, Wei Zheng

    This article integrates literature on organizational factors that influence knowledge management (KM) effectiveness that have been identified in several bodies of literature: HRD, information technology, and management. A framework has been developed that categorizes the various organizational factors and their paths of influence on KM effectiveness.
