Tony LaMarca's Summary

I like to help people. That's really what I am about. With extensive experience in areas like student ministries, worship leading and church production, I've found that my passions lie within seeing Kingdom of God furthered through the local church.

I have been a part of the growth of my home church for many years, and I desire to see every church reach as many of the lost as possible. It is our mission to make The Church (the body of believers) bigger.

Technical Artist would probably be the best way to describe me in as few words as possible. The arts have always been a passion of my heart. From writing and playing music to painting and sketching, I tend to enjoy what I am doing more when I am using my artistic side. I am also a natural troubleshooter and am quite at home when surrounded by technology. I am most productive when I am using both sides of my brain. That's why web design is such a joy to me.

My goal is to use my gifts in leadership, arts, and technology to see as many people as possible come into a loving relationship with The Savior, Jesus Christ.

I have a passion for seeing the Church succeed in its mission to further The Kingdom of God here on earth. Leveraging technology in ways to make the Gospel more accessible to more people is my primary function at North Way.

From creating environments where people open their hearts to receive God's Word to ensuring that the technology that our multi-site strategy is built on works to its highest potential, my goal is to help as many people as possible listen and hear the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Volunteers are a key part of making it all happen. Our role behind the scenes gives congregation members an opportunity to serve the church without feeling like they are in the spotlight. They help to further our mission of Freeing People to Follow Jesus.

Consultant

Passavant Leadership Group

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Professional Training & Coaching industry

October 2012 – Present (1 year 3 months) Greater Pittsburgh Area

PLG has helped to provide primary oversight and leadership in the successful transition of a 4,000 member church from its Founding Pastor to a widely affirmed and accepted successor who has developed a team that has released the church to even greater growth and fruitfulness.

Our leadership has also helped in the transition of a stagnant, older congregation of 200 into a vital, younger church of 1500 in weekly attendance.

We have a wealth of experience in working with much smaller churches in urban, suburban and even rural settings. We understanding that every church is called by God, regardless of its size or location and is placed there to become an agent of life change and transformation by the power of the Holy Spirit.

The mission of PLG is to identify, equip, and deploy great leaders in ministry.

Our team has had experience in working with churches of several denominations, as well as non-denominational affiliations. We have a heart for seeing the advancement of God’s Kingdom through the development of strong leadership, regardless of denominational background.

From foundational training and problem solving to strategic planning and effective succession planning, the goal of PLG is to come along side our clients to help them navigate all aspects of leadership.
