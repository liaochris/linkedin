Jamie McDermott's Projects

  • BeatElite Social Media Campaign

    • November 2011 to October 2012
    Team Members: Jamie McDermott, Christopher Carr (November - Decemebr 2011)

    BeatElite came to Shareable Media with the intention of growing their social media and engaging with new clients.

    We started their campaign with a big Christmas 2011 competition in which over $5k worth of prizes were given away to some lucky followers. This boosted BeatElite's campaign and got it off to a flying start.

  • The Glasshouse Leamington Social Media Campaign

    • November 2011 to June 2012
    Team Members: Jamie McDermott, Christopher Carr (November - December 2011)

    The Glasshouse were about to launch in the heart of Leamington Spa, however they knew that social media and marketing were two things that they had to cover to achieve the success they wanted and the targets they had set.

    Shareable Media launched The Glasshouses' social media and we have managed to engage customers and businesses within the local region, generating bookings, generating feedback & recommendations and compiling a highly targeted and relevant newsletter subscriber list.

  • Daniel Portman Facebook Profile Design & Management

    • October 2011 to November 2011
    Team Members: Jamie McDermott, Daniel Portman

    Daniel Portman is a very well respected DJ and producer from Switzerland. With a fan base of just over 10,000 on Facebook, Daniel wanted to engage a new audience and improve the overall look of his Fan Page.

    I worked with Daniel to better understand his audience and what he wanted within his Facebook Fan Page, and the final result was a custom designed and coded tab. This welcomed new visitors with an engaging design, informative tag lines & imagery and his latest music video.

    Within four months Daniel's improved Facebook Fan Page (and since re-designed tab) has encouraged over 35,000 people to become a fan of his page. That's just over a 300% increase in fans.
