Donalyn Ketchum's Experience

Owner/Writer/Photgrapher

The Creekside Cook

December 2012 – Present (1 year 1 month) http://thecreeksidecook.com

The Creekside Cook is my new [and I think my last!] blog, which details my adventures as a gardener and a cook. My husband and I garden on two rural acres in Upstate NY, working to eventually attain something close to food independence. My passion is to share how nearly everyone can cook great food, and that many of us can grow at least some of what we eat. I use the produce we grow, along with other fresh, local products to create tasty and easy recipes that make the most of the resources we have. In addition, I am available for recipe development, brand representation or local food and facility photography.

Owner

DessertStalking.com (Self-employed)

Self-Employed; Myself Only; Online Media industry

January 2010 – Present (4 years)

DessertStalking.com is a food photo submission site, highlighting the amazing work being done by food bloggers, home cooks and photographers at varied skill levels. The site concentrates on sweets, including breakfast foods, snacks and the most decadent meal enders. I have a wonderful group of contributors and we are steadily building a following of people who love to look at photos of great food.

Owner

GardenStalking.com

January 2012 – Present (2 years)

GardenStalking.com is an aggregation site, similar to DessertStalking.com. It is a curated source of information and inspiration from garden blogs all over the world. We have a growing list of contributors, and welcome authors of gardening and gardening/food blogs to check out the site and begin utilizing it as a resource to reach more readers.
