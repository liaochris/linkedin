David McCarthy's Summary

David has over 30 years of high tech industry experience, including nearly 20 years of venture capital and angel investing. He has experience in starting and growing businesses, raising financing and achieving successful M&As and IPOs.

David is currently an active investor in Ottawa-area angel-backed companies. He also assists as a consultant, advisor, and board member to a variety of early-stage high-tech companies and venture capital firms. David is also a co-founder at Home Monitor Inc, which is developing a break-through solution for home security and monitoring.

Previously, David was a Managing Director at Summerhill Venture Partners, a $175M venture capital fund investing in telecommunications and IT companies throughout North America. Summerhill was a spinout of BCE Capital, which was Bell Canada's venturing arm. David remains an advisor to Summerhill.

While at Summerhill and BCE Capital, David invested in over 20 companies and was on the boards and was active with several successful startups in Canada and the U.S. This included Medialight (a DSL chip, acquired by Analog Devices), Vistar Telecommunications (a satellite telematics system acquired by NSI communications), Endwave (a microwave radio system, IPO'ed on Nasdaq), Catena Networks (a broadband access system, acquired by Ciena), Ellacoya Networks (a DPI-based traffic management system, acquired by Arbor Networks), and NexTone Communications (a session border controller, acquired by Genband).

Prior to entering the venture capital industry, David spent 14 years at Bell-Northern Research, the R&D arm of Nortel Networks. At BNR, David had several roles in system engineering, new market development, new product introduction and product development.
