Andrew Hill's Summary

I'm just a guy who is striving to live a meaningful, awesome, life. I love entrepreneurship and connecting like minded people to build meaningful relationships. Hopefully these relationships can change the world for the better.

Right now I'm focusing on using the internet as a tool to improve people's lives. Be it by co-founding GoodDare.org, selling affordable maintenance tools to the aquarium hobby via Rumford Aquatics, or working with Golfmix.com my goal is to bring value to the customer's life opposed to just selling them another app or product that will be used for a few hours then forgotten.

Another passion is promoting entrepreneurship at Arizona State University with the Entrepreneurship Club, planning young entrepreneur mixers, and just taking the time to talk to people about entrepreneurship. I try to get people to take the first step.

I also do strategy consulting on the side, bringing a highly unique perspective that is very analytical and meticulous, that has worked with and advised large corporations, tiny startups, and everything in-between. Providing a refreshing perspective is what I do. Or as others have said "I just like the way your mind works"
