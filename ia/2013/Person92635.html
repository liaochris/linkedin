Michael P. Lazarus' Experience

Founder & President

Latin Pulse Music Inc.

Privately Held; Myself Only; Music industry

July 2002 – Present (11 years 4 months)

Latin Pulse Music Inc. is a company that provides both B2C (business to consumer) and B2B (business to business) services to a worldwide client base of production companies, independent artists and Latin music fans. Services include music licensing & publishing (specializing in source cues for TV & Film), recording services, digital retail for multi-media content, and internet marketing campaigns. LPM helps independent Latin music artists establish both their legal (copyright & p.r.o. song registrations) and digital (website, digital assets, social networks) infrastructure in order to successfully operate in today's ultra-competitive market.

Our core business has evolved into a niche content development, publishing and digital media services corporation focused on Hispanic / Latino music & culture. Our network of exclusively targeted websites - the Latin Pulse Music Website Network - provide a distribution platform for our services and a launching point for digital marketing campaigns.

Here is a brief description of each division of the company:

Latinpulsemedia.com = Your source for 100% pre-cleared genuine Latin music for Film & TV. Complete control over both master & synch rights. Authentic commercially released masters available. Music supervision and clearance services.

Latinmusicmastering.com = Mastering services with a specialty in big band tropical, pop and urban music. Voting & Engineer Member of the Recording Academy (Grammy & Latin Grammy Awards). Member NARIP.

Latinpulsemusic.com = Latin music & multi-media download store. MP3 albums, videos, instructional eBooks and digital sheet music. Latin music digital content and downloads made 100% legal, fun, fast and easy.

Timba.com = Home of Cuban Music on the Web! TIMBA.com is the largest Latin music website available today. Artist profiles, tour dates, new releases, concert reviews, instructional material and the most comprehensive online resource (wiki) for the timba music genre (Timbapedia).

Consultant / Engineer

O.E.M. Audio Consulting

Modified and tested a series of cost-reduced USB audio chipsets. Optimized & redesigned the acoustic performance of the Microsoft XBOX headset. Completed a comparative technical analysis of various PC microphone arrays. Served as the principal electro-acoustic engineer during the complete development of the first USB based, stereo headset for computer applications (the Plantronics DSP Series Digitally Enhanced USB-PC Headsets). Trained QA engineers for baseline testing. Worked with Texas Instruments during the development of the first USB headset. Completed a series of Voice Recognition studies for comparative analysis between headset and microphone array devices.

Electro-Acoustic Engineer, OEM Design

Plantronics

Public Company; 5001-10,000 employees; PLT; Telecommunications industry

July 1996 – July 1999 (3 years 1 month)

Provided the essential engineering support needed to engage the OEM customer companies to “design in” Plantronics products. Developed products that accompany and fortify the “headset only” (no adapter) call-center solutions.

Served as the technical interface with the engineering personnel at all the major OEM customer companies. Performed and explained specialized electrical and acoustical measurements in order to ensure efficient operation of the communication systems.

Designed a low voltage/current, line powered Receiver (speaker) In-line amplifier. Developed various Hookswitch and In-line Mute solutions for Lucent and Nortel. Automated measurements and data calculations via visual basic macro programs. Measured a wideband free-field to eardrum transfer function for a B&K HATS. Completed a PSPICE electrical circuit simulation of a wideband headset system. Recipient of a Sound Future award for the creation of a “Virtual Samples” program.
