Leigh Evans' Summary

Leigh is a professional creative who specialises in the creation of brands, their visualisation and most importantly their application & implementation. He is a family man, and also a self confessed petrolhead.

With over 18 years of experience gained in Europe and the Middle East; he has led major projects for many diverse brands in the disciplines of strategy, corporate identity, design, photography and brand activation.

His extensive portfolio represents the full range of commercial industries, including consumer brands, education, energy, entertainment, financial services, leisure, pharmaceuticals, real estate, technology, travel and telecommunications sectors.

Specialties

Design management, brand strategy, identity development, application development, implementation & guidelines, advertising, environment design, packaging and most recently e-commerce
