Howard Forster's Summary

I am a dedicated and enthusiastic recruitment professional with over 20 years’ experience managing and developing accounts in various recruitment sectors, including Commercial, Call Centre, IT, Aviation, Engineering and R2R recruitment. Addicted to providing clients and candidates alike with results on a global basis I have managed to establish and still manage a significant client portfolio in the UK, and across Australasia .

Possessing excellent interpersonal and communication skills with the ability to develop and maintain productive internal and external relationships. I enjoy being part of, as well as managing, motivating, training and developing a successful and productive team and I thrive in highly pressurised and challenging working environments.

Throughout my career I have enjoyed the full recruitment process although I do enjoy the challenges of generating new clients and head-hunting for the more elusive candidates.

Currently recruiting for clients in the UK, Australia and Asia on both a contingency and search basis.
