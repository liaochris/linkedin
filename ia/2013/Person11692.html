Barbara Peters, RN, LPC's Summary

Barbara has a Bachelor of Arts in Sociology, a Bachelor of Sciences in Nursing and a Master of Sciences in Counseling. Certified by the National Board of Certified Counselors and a member of The Licensed Professional Counselors of Georgia, she has worked in diverse mental health settings: psychiatric hospitals, managed behavioral health organizations, and Employee Assistance consulting firms.

She currently practices in Alpharetta, GA and is the author of two relationship books, "The Gift of A Lifetime" and "He Said, She Said, I Said". And recently tried her hand at fiction with a romance novel, now available as an ebook on amazon.com; Never Too Old for Romance.

Specialties: My main passion is working with couples, utilizing a solution-focused approach with an optimistic outlook and seeing them develop a better relationship. I also work with some individuals with relationship issues as well as other life challenges. My counseling style is interactive, respectful, non-judgmental, supportive, and often coaching in nature when applicable. With gentle questioning, I help people get in touch with their own values and ideals and gain trust in themselves to make decisions for a better lifestyle.
