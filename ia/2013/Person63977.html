Jim Lorenzen, CFP®, AIF®'s Summary

Retirement planning doesn’t have to be a mystery. A "personal CFO" can serve as your financial navigator.

Navigating a course to achieve your financial goals is like a ship’s captain navigating a ship across a vast ocean. The ship’s captain must navigate currents and weather; investors must contend with their own set of external risks: Inflation, interest rates, legislative (tax), market, and various business risks.

No one can control the seas or the economic environmental risks; but uncertainty can be navigated. The key is a long-term focus, discipline, and an understanding of the things that can be managed: Risk, taxes, and costs.

It's about time... and wise decision making. (See the "IFG Investment Philosophy" piece below.)

I am a CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER® professional and an ACCREDITED INVESTMENT FIDUCIARY® with 22 years of private practice experience as Founding Principal of The Independent Financial Group, a fee-only registered investment advisor with clients located in New York, Florida, Colorado, and California.

Specialties: Retirement planning and wealth management solutions. See www.indfin.com.
