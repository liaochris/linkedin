Andrew Macris' Summary

I'm a marketer with a passion for good creative and providing exceptional customer experiences.

Since 1994, I've held a variety of positions of increasing leadership and responsibility. Currently, I'm an Associate Product Manager in the Internet & Mobile division of GCMIO, focusing on Citi Cards. Previous to that, I co-managed a team of web project managers for Citi.

In my 18 years of professional experience, I've had the privilege to work for some great clients - in creative, strategy and execution, holding both agency and client-side positions.

My biggest strength is my ability to strategize creative solutions to tough problems. It's what gets me going.

Specialties: Web Marketing, Project Management, Direct Marketing, E-mail marketing, Customer communications, Search Engine Optimization, Copywriting, Creative Direction, Branding
