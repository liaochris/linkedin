Alex Kavel's Experience

Co-Founder

Graduly Ltd

July 2010 – Present (3 years 1 month) Cardiff

Graduly is a Facebook app using a brand new set of tools to match university leavers with job vacancies they may not have found using traditional methods. Created by recruitment professional Alex Kavel and software developer Neil Smith, Graduly brings a fresh approach to graduate recruitment.

Graduates use the new Graduly app to enter their education details, skills, experience, the sector they are hoping to work in, and the role they are searching for.

The Graduly search technique is unique among job sites - the 'exact match' takes out the ambiguity in the process with input and search terms standardized, which means that candidates, potential employers and recruiters are all working to the same set of criteria.

Using Facebook - the meeting place of choice for 90% of UK students - provides SMEs with a new route to the graduate they need, easily and cost effectively.
