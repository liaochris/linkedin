Lori Martin's Summary

I have been a seamstress for 28 years. I have also been a house keeper,babysitter,store clerk , hospital cook and even done some demolition. I am now working from home Making Dog Booties and Dog coats which I have designed over the last few years.

Dog Booties,Dog Boots are nonslip and for indoor/outdoor use. My dog boots/Dog coats are for all size dogs. My boots for dogs are made with the dogs comfort in mind! (In my opinion) Some that I see do not look very comfy!


My goal is to make enough for my husband and I to be comfortable and be able to send my little girl to college when the time comes! (hopefully not to fast)


Specialties: Sewing is my love I can sew just about anything. I have an industrial machine. I loved doing piece work back in the early 90's. I have a very diseased back so I am unable to work out of the home so Now I have my own small business making dog booties and coats. I also do some custom projects.
