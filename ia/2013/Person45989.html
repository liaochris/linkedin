Karen Highland's Summary

Frederick Md Realtor, Real Estate Blogger, and blogging coach.

After many years as a teacher, including full-time, part-time and tutoring, I got my real estate license and joined my husband in the business. He has been an agent for 20 years, and we are building a Team, The Highland Group.

My role on the Team is website management, Social Media and Blogging, and contact management. I'm an avid blogger, managing several blogs and usually writing every day of the week. I enjoy sharing real estate information and concepts with Frederick buyers and sellers.

I share the office management with Chris and the follow-up and transaction management with buyers and sellers. He is a great negotiator and handles the initial sale or buy, and I handle contract to close issues.

Together, we make one great realtor!
