Bill Hubert's Summary

Passionate about providing leadership and direction in the design and implementation of comprehensive technology solutions all centered around the idea that “fanatic” relationships earned through honesty and integrity are paramount to success. Possesses nearly 25 years of experience in business and consumer services with an emphasis on higher education technology development, process improvement, relationship management, data exchange and transaction processing.

Bringing a seasoned entrepreneurial outlook to every engagement paired with a truly outside the box flair. Bill strongly believes that the best way to empower smart borrowing decisions is by utilizing today's technology to present clear, precise, unbiased and appropriate data for every consumer.

Dedicated to building trust through transparency, Bill has successfully built multiple in demand technology businesses with the most recent (Cology) being acquired in October, 2012. Today you can find him consulting for various purpose driven organizations who seek to change the way business is done today by setting new examples for efficiency, effectiveness, awareness and fun.

Bill Hubert's Experience

President | Founder

Cology Inc.

Public Company; 201-500 employees; Financial Services industry

November 2004 – June 2013 (8 years 8 months)

Cology is the leading provider of end-to-end loan origination and repayment servicing solutions for lenders and the engine behind over 400 of the nation’s fastest growing loan programs in the market today. Offering robust borrower profile features, complete branding capabilities, unmatched reporting and application management tools – Cology is the preferred choice for credit unions and community banks, colleges, social lenders, foundations and member-based organizations. Flexible origination and life-of-loan servicing options, combined with the most experienced back-office processing experts in the business, make entering the market easy and affordable.
