Albert van Niekerk's Summary

I help owners of successful companies and organisations to move their enterprises to higher levels of effectiveness and profits, to overcome cash-flow problems, to create great working environments and to enjoy their work.

Often owners are stuck. They are bottlenecks within their companies.

Method: I offer a Built for Results Programme, as ABPLAN's Plan and Accelerate Your Business Programme, is also called, with the aimed-for result of increasing a company's client base by 30% and its bottom line between 20% and 40%. A doubling of revenue is possible depending on the type of company and products and services.

The programme is results-driven and progress is reviewed each month. It's incremental and I provide guidance in obtaining results.

Background: I developed a keen interest in 1984 in strategic and operational planning, in execution, and in management by objectives, while in the diplomatic service. I applied this private sector business approach in the public sector and achieved great results with motivated teams.

I entered the private sector in 1995 to pursue a career as business consultant with an emphasis on providing total solutions to already successful small and medium-sized businesses.

I maintain a steady portfolio of about 12 clients, many over three, four or more years.

Specialties

My Built for Results Programme covers a scenario conversation, strategic planning, the Balanced Scorecard (Kaplan & Norton), strategy workshops of all sizes, and a strategy of execution followed by coaching. I also offer brief specialised projects concerning marketing. My clients are small and medium-sized companies (mostly independent professionals), parastatals, universities/educational companies and trusts and public welfare organisations.
