Desert Moon Franchise's Experience

Fresh, Healthful Southwestern Alternative to Fast Food Franchise Opportunities

Desert Moon

January 1992 – Present (21 years 8 months)

Desert Moon is the East Coast's original Fresh Mexican Grille franchise. Desert Moon is "The Fresh, Healthful Alternative to Fast Food" since 1992.

We don't measure our success by how well we SELL franchises, but rather HOW WELL OUR FRANCHISE RESTAURANTS PERFORM. Great unit economics, full training & support with fully protected territories.

It's all about the food!!! Fresh ingredients, big flavor, made to order, zero trans fat, hi fiber, low fat with Gluten free and key Organic items. Our modified table service is set in a fun, fast casual environment that separates us from the competition. The most innovative approach and highest quality in Fast Casual today!

Fresh Mexican/Southwestern Is The Fastest Growing Segment In The Fast Casual Restaurant Industry... Growing 40% Faster Than Burgers & Pizza!

Innovative, Fresh, Menu Items are Key for Success

Desert Moon

January 1992 – Present (21 years 8 months)

Differentiating itself from its typical Mexican fast-food counterparts, Desert Moon menu offerings are a fusion of Fresh Mexican/Southwestern recipes. Prepared in our full view/open kitchen, our guests are offered a choice of Charbroiled Lean Steak, Skinless Chicken Breast, or Fire roasted vegetables any of which may be wrapped in a soft, warm, flour or whole wheat tortilla with a combination of high quality, made-from-scratch salsas, ranch beans, yellow rice, guacamole or fresh vegetables. The result is food that is unique in the fast casual venue, extremely flavorful and sought after by quality driven consumers. To compliment our food, some Desert Moon restaurants offer "signature" hand-shaken Margaritas and a selection of Mexican Beer.
