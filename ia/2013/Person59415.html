Jason Packman's Summary

Jason has spent his career working to understand the barriers that prevent communication and helping while helping others understand and overcome these barriers to help them achieve their goals.

• As a language educator, he achieved this through designing and teaching lessons that provided students with the language input necessary for his students to acquire English. He also created a positive learning environment in his classrooms, helping his students become confident learners of English by overcoming their preconceived notions towards language learning.

• As an admissions counselor, he was able to provide correct information to interested parties while quickly being able to tailor it to the applicants background knowledge. He also evaluated students of diverse international and academic backgrounds, determining whether or not the applicant would be a successful student in the program.

• As an event coordinator, he has worked effectively with a variety of stakeholders and individuals to hold successful events such as a school open house, an academic colloquium, and a language contest for high school students.

• As a scholar and researcher, he has gained a deep understanding of Asia, and of Japan in particular, though the study of the language, culture, and politics of the region.
