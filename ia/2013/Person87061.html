Mark Schoots' Summary

Hi you found me!

My name is Mark and I am a creative marketing executive with 10+ years of experience in the Entertainment industry.

Early on in life I realized I love art and entertainment. This started as an aspiring artist playing the saxophone and in management college I got involved with organizing dance events. Just before I started my "corporate life" I decided to also master the marketing profession.

As one of my clients said: “Sorry for interrupting you Mark, but talking about marketing drains me. Let me do what I love to do ... and that is making music ... and let you do what you love to do ... marketing.”

And now after 12 years with some great successes and some painful lessons (that’s experience right?) I realize that I enjoy it the most when I work directly with the artist(s). I just love their inspiration & creativity, their eye for detail and doing things openhearted & differently.

So I created my own dream job to help artists (maybe you?) showcase their talent that may be music, artwork, a film, that great event, ballet play, etc.

I developed my own approach in how I help my clients be successful.
- by being inspiring during the artistic creation progress
- help building amazing experiences
- contribute in developing strong product offerings that matches with the artist identity
- coach on how to create a interacting with the audience
- running a kick-ass team
- go for the long-term while keeping an eye out for short term opportunities

If you are thinking about realizing your dreams with my support, this is a way we could find out:
- determine together what is working and not working in your marketing strategy
- brainstorm on solutions I could offer to address these issues
- formulate this in a realistic plan - no flim flam - make it concrete and real
- roll up the sleeves and get it done - and with it build a lasting successful collaboration

Love to hear from you!

My email address is mark@markschoots.nl or just give me a call +31681492245.
