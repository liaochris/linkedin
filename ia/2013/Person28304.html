Curtis Burnside's Summary

As The Telecom Broker with an ever expanding technology offering, Curtis possesses unique certified training and relationships, along with the customer centric mindset to assist businesses in implementing the right technology, at the right time at the right price.

Why use a telecom broker? As your Network Consultant, I provide the following benefits:

1. Assistance in uncovering your technology and telecom needs to provide the most cost effective and efficient holistic solution.

2. Design of the desired solution before taking it to market for pricing.

3. Independent, unbiased proposals that cannot be obtained directly from carrier representatives. You can get your own 'real time" quotes on my website at www.TheTelecomBroker.com

4. Assistance in negotiating pricing and terms, then reviewing your contract for services.

5. Project management of the desired solution on a turn key basis. Once your services have been ordered, we assure they're properly installed.

6. On-going layer of customer service not normally available if you're dealing directly with the provider's sales force.
