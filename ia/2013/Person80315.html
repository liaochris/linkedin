Les Kern's Summary

Les Kern is the principle Financial Planning Specialist and Wealth Management Advisor of a Wealth Management firm that is committed to helping clients select the best retirement plan that protects against loss. Les is focused on the non-stock alternatives of conservative investments. He customizes programs that are designed to protect, conserve, and grow wealth by delivering an unprecedented level of personal services.

Les realized that he wanted to learn more about the vast realm of insurance products on the market so he could provide sound and informed financial recommendations. Since then, he has become an expert in financial planning, wealth management, wealth preservation, retirement and providing clients with the knowledge to plan for the future and have financial peace of mind.

With offices in Howell, Michigan and Wilmington, North Carolina, Les wanted to offer assistance to clients to help them achieve financial independence and improve their wealth management. He has more than 30 years of experience in the financial services industry and working with middle income clients helping them discover the safest way to grow their money. Throughout the years Les has been devoted to maintaining high ethical standards while developing financial strategies for business and individuals alike.
