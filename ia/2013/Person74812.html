Our businesses restore the property and lives of our customers after a property damage event caused by Water, Flood, Mold, Fire, Smoke, Wind and Bio-hazards. We are both a “Preferred & Certified” insurance claim contractors that provides “rapid recovery” from unexpected property damage events of virtually "any type" on a 24 /7 basis.

We offer professional, all-risk-insured credibility for both residential and commercial structures, specializing in: EMS (Emergency Mitigation Services) and CPR (Critical Property Restoration)… Or both, as comprehensive "turnkey projects” as required.

The insurance industry is highly regulated! All professional "insurance claim contractors" MUST be fully certified and properly insured, use industry standardized pricing and work directly for the end-users of their services, and NOT for the insurance companies. However a very large majority of these contractors are "spoon-fed customer referrals" based on the negotiated terms of large national contracts that are largely designed to meet the needs of the insurance company, which beckons the question: "who really is their customer"?

At EMERG-NC, we choose NOT to participate with one-sided insurance claim vendor programs which are a true conflict of interest from our perspective. Our reputation is extremely important to our success, so we focus heavily on maximizing our credibility and trustworthiness. We receive referrals directly from trusted Insurance Agents and Adjusters, as well as like-minded Plumbing, HVAC & Electricians Professionals, and Customer Service oriented Real Estate & Property Managers who strive to know their customers personally.

We KNOW who OUR customers are... YOU and everyone you know that is important to you! If you need a true advocate in your time of need, call 919-EMERG-NC... “We’ll Respond in a Heartbeat!”
