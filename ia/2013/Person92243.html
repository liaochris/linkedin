Michael Craig's Summary

OHIO SHOOTING SPORTS:
As founder of Ohio Shooting Sports, Michael drives the business end and curriculum design of all of our educational and training programs by bringing nearly 30 years of strong organizational skills and training expertise. In 2009, Michael formed Ohio Shooting Sports, Ltd and is our business manager and lead Instructor.

IDPA Safety Officer Instructor, IDPA Safety Officer, Certified USPSA Range Officer, IDPA Match Director at Pickaway County Sportsman’s Range 2005-2007, Co-Match Director for a Practical Shotgun Division at PCSI in 2007, Match Director for the 2008 Ohio State IDPA Championship and was the Match Director for the USPSA Multi-Gun Division at PCSI from 2008-2011.

Other continuing education courses have included studying under the direction of John Benner at Tactical Defense Institute (TDI) by attending 12 different training courses from that ranged from 1 day to 3 days, Advanced Defensive PistolCraft Course from nationally known firearms instructor John Farnam, "Close Quarters Combatives - Advanced Pistol Fighting - Level 2 taught by Brian LaMaster, Innovative Tactical Concepts and successfully completed 20 hours of intensive training in the elements of Martial Blade Concepts, Counter-Blade Concepts, Damithurt Silat and Sobadiwan Eskima under the direction of Michael Janich and successfully completed Range Master's 3 day Instructor Development Course with Tom Givens, August 2013 and is currently a Certified Handgun Instructor for the State of Tennessee, Department of Safety and Homeland Security.
