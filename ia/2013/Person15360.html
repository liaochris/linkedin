Robert Fulcher's Summary

Thirty-five years in association management, including seventeen years of senior management experience with a record of accomplishment in marketing and communications, publishing, membership development, meeting planning, web/e-commerce development, strategic planning, general management, budget management, and board/committee liaison.

Previously served as Director of Publications for the American Society of Bone and Mineral Research, Senior Director, Marketing & Communications with The Endocrine Society, Chief Operating Officer of the American Society of Nephrology, Vice President, Membership & Marketing/Publisher, Books & Electronic Products for the American Pharmacists Association, and in several positions at the American Psychological Association, including Director of Membership.

Specialties

Marketing, publishing, general management, budget management, and strategic planning.
