Luann Lee's Summary

http://www.LuannLee.com
Familiar to many from her television appearances, Luann Lee co-hosted a national live talk show for FOX TV called That's Life ; similar to "The Talk" and "The View". Before that she could be seen interviewing such celebrities as Sharon Stone, Mel Gibson, Kevin Costner, Sandra Bullock, Adam Sandler, Steve Martin, Goldie Hawn, Clint Eastwood, Danny Glover, Sarah Michelle Geller and Andy Garcia, just to name a few. She co-hosted the Cable Ace nominated show The Pet Department and guest hosted Back Chat with Jeff Probst of Survivor fame for FX. She also made guest appearances on the live FOX show The Breakfast Show with Tom Bergeron.

Lee was an interstitial host for 3 years for the Encore Channel which was produced by HBO. She also hosted, wrote and produced a pilot called Celebrity Profiles for an entertainment channel. Lee served as the Contributing Entertainment Editor for OK! Magazine in which she interviewed Sharon Stone, Robert Wagner and Whoopi Goldberg. She then was able to pursue her love of photography as a photo/journalist by shooting Tom Cruise, Jodie Foster, Tom Hanks, Jim Carrey, Rene Zellweger, Brad Pitt, Clint Eastwood, Dustin Hoffman, Goldie Hawn and Jennifer Aniston just to name a few. In addition, Luann also hosted the weekend radio program "Luann on the Loose" with 97.1 KLSX, and is currently a radio guest host for Sirius XM
