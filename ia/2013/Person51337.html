Halil Köklü's Summary

I am a passionate technologist and manager with almost 10 years of experience in web technologies. I have a strong software development background.Over the last 12 months I gained international business and management experience with Rocket Internet. First working on a Brazilian project, I then kicked off multiple new ventures as Technical Lead for Rocket Internet in Turkey & MENA. Begin of 2012 I've been put in charge as CTO of Namshi and Mizado to build a new development team from scratch in Dubai. Consulted Koding in San Francisco.

I believe that technology is the backbone of any internet based business and a key factor for its success or failure. Almost all departments and operations are depending on technology. A well-designed solution will empower the operation to be smooth and efficient as well as save costs, energy and faults. It will be ready for change and scale in the future. Though this requires a talented and motivated technical team, efficient development processes with well-balanced focus on quality and speed, as much as an open and fair work environment.
