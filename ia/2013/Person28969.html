Damien King's Summary

With over twelve years experience in the online industry, I bring both passion and expertise, offering a wide range of disciplines that provide E2E solutions from creative through to infrastructure.

I provide strategic decisions on new technologies while overseeing the successful delivery of complex web applications. I have an entrepreneurial style that inspires innovation and lean development and enjoy leading developers to be more business aware and accountable for quality, performance and inlife support. Over the years I have delivered major web applications for such household names as the BBC, British Telecom, BHP Billiton and Virgin Media.

I have experience in consulting and senior management strategy decisions, HR processes including objective reviews and appraisals, distributing workflow and controlling resources in BAU and projects. From a LRP position I am able to make recommendations on new technologies and partner integrations and offer expertise in a variety of technical web subjects.
