Debbie Laskey, MBA's Summary

With a BA Degree in Political Science and an MBA Degree with a concentration in Marketing and International Management, Debbie Laskey began her career in the accounting and legal fields. Her marketing experience has been even more diverse and ranges from the high-tech industry to the Consumer Marketing Department at Disneyland Paris in France to non-profits to insurance. She has been recognized as a "Woman Making a Difference" by the Los Angeles Business Journal and a "Member of the Month" by the Los Angeles Junior Chamber of Commerce. Since 2002, Debbie has been a judge for the Web Marketing Association’s annual web award competition and has also been recognized as one of the "Top 100 Branding Experts" to follow on Twitter.

Specialties: Strategic planning, brand awareness campaigns, marketing plans, positioning, website development, collateral and newsletters, corporate communications, public relations, email initiatives, advertising, direct mail, copywriting, tradeshows, seminars, special events, competitive/trend analysis, cross functional team leadership, budgets and ROI metrics, employee engagement, customer experience strategies, and social media marketing.
