Joseph Stern's Summary

Joseph’s industry experience spans advertising, film, TV and live events. He currently directs nationally broadcast jewelry commercials and has production managed two feature films that were acquired and distributed internationally. As a live events producer, he has worked for SXSW, Santa Fe Film Festival, Duke City Shootout and the Screenwriting Conference in Santa Fe.

His graduate advertising and user experience thesis research at the University of Texas in Austin is focused on social video seeding and content strategy on crowdfunding platforms.
