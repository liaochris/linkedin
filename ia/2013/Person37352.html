Doug Thorpe's Summary

Doug Thorpe, through his 25+ years in executive leadership, has seen several major economic cycles, watched companies come and go, and experienced service in all major sectors; the military, Fortune 50 financial, entrepreneurial, and non-profit. His first passion is helping people face serious challenges in their professional or personal lives.

Professionally, Doug has spent most of his career in financial services (commercial banking and mortgage finance), earning a reputation of excellence and innovation. He has served in the forefront of several major national banking situations, providing senior executive leadership to resolve major transactional and platform change plus Federal regulatory response. Doug’s clients realized significant cost savings, more effective operations, and higher profitability by using his business process models.

On the heels of the national financial crisis of 2007-2008, Doug saw a need in his community. He founded a non-denominational, faith based career transition organization called Jobs Ministry Southwest. Since then, over 3,500 clients attended training, joined work groups, and participated in job search coaching programs sponsored and hosted by the organization.

Today, as Practice Leader at Solomon Edwards, Doug provides business advisory and consulting services for companies and executives seeking fresh ideas for expansion, cost control, process re-engineering, and change management. In particular, anyone thinking about business process improvement and executive coaching should call on Doug.
