Jeremiah Talamantes, CISSP, CEH, CHFI, CCISO's Summary

Founder, Ethical Hacker and Security Researcher at RedTeam Security Corporation; a professional information security services firm based in Minneapolis, MN. Certified Chief Information Security Officer (CCISO), Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH) and Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP) since 2003 with nearly 20 years of IT experience; over 15 years in an information security leadership role. OWASP member, ISSA member and former board member.

* Technical Editor for upcoming Syngress book titled, "When Botnets Attack" due to be released in 2011 (Now delayed).

* Inventor of the PlugBot, a patent-pending portable appliance used in physical penetration testing assessments.
