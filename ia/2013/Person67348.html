Jo Moir's Summary

First impressions matter whether in your personal or business life. Learn how to dress for success with me and your life will be changed for the better.

Hi I'm Jo Moir, and as an ex head of finance in large multi-million pound businesses and a qualified Image Consultant with Colour Me Beautiful, I am uniquely qualified to help men and women to dress for success in both their personal and business lives! If you look great, you feel great. If you feel great, you're more confident and people are more likely to want to do business with you!

* Do you spend ages each day trying to decide what to wear?
* Have you lost your way when it comes to knowing the styles that flatter your body shape?
* Are you ready for promotion at work but aren’t sure whether you’ve got the right image?

If you answered ‘yes’ to any or all of these questions then you’ll benefit from a consultation or workshop with me.

I offer a comprehensive range of services to help clients make the most of their personal appearance and become the person they want to be. And this transformation can happen immediately you leave my studio!

I have clients of all ages, professions and backgrounds and both men and women. Whatever your age you will benefit from a consultation because I’m here to bring out the best in you.

Outside of work, I enjoy walking my black Labrador Bertie, jogging and attending Toastmaster meetings. I'm also very keen on taking photos of the landscape around where I live.
