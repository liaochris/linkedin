Beckie Cooper's Summary

Having owned and managed a number of properties over the last 10 years I have found myself in the fortunate position to be able to help others to build property portfolios. Whilst still actively investing in property in Hastings and Bexhill, East Sussex and managing Cinque Ports Letting Agency I am able to use my many skills to help both Landlords and Tenants.

My experience in dealing with people from many different industries, such as finance, construction and marketing, enables me to often find creative solutions to property problems and issues.

Specialties: Property Investment Strategies, Letting and Property Management.
