Dale Corpus' Summary

Broker Associate, Real Estate Marketer, Certified Agent Specialist, Certified Distressed Property Expert & Team Leader for KeyToTheBay Real Estate at Realty ONE Group BMC Associates in San Ramon. Specializing in representing sellers, buyers, and investors in Alameda & Contra Costa Counties.
