David Peter Hansen's Summary

My passion as a leader and business intelligence expert is to develop teams and create solutions that empower organisations to gain insight in their data and support better decision-making.

With 12 years of experience in database design, development, and administration, I have been the architect and lead behind several large-scale business intelligence and data warehouse solutions on the Microsoft SQL Server platform.

Most recently, my skills have taken me into a Fortune Global 500 company as the technical leader of the global Business Intelligence and Finance Performance Management. I believe the path to developing great, both agile and sustainable, analytic solutions goes though coaching and setting ambiguous goals for the team.

I have a very deep technical understanding of Microsoft SQL Server, which I use to create the best possible designs and solutions for the business.

As an active member of the Microsoft SQL Server and business intelligence community, I am frequent speaker on the subject of how to design and manage large-scale database and business intelligence solutions.
