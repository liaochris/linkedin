Joseph Grutta's Summary

As the President and Founder of Nautic Studios, a digital marketing agency, Joseph helps clients develop strategies that leverage technology to accomplish business objectives, get found online, and better engage with customers. He has experience working with clients across many different industries, including those facing complex regulatory requirements, such as healthcare and financial services. Joseph has even worked with several of the country’s first Accountable Care Organizations (ACOs).

As an expert in his field, Joseph has spoken on digital marketing at numerous venues across the State of Florida, including at the University of Florida, and has been featured on The Star 99.5 (WBXY-FM).

Some of Joseph’s specialties include web design and development, search engine optimization, social media, project management, process improvement, strategic planning, and information security.
