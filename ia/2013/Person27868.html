Greg Crisp's Summary

I was walking down Peachtree Street on my way to lunch in early 2008, and almost everyone I passed had their face in an iPhone or Blackberry.

In that moment, I saw the future more clearly than I had in a while, remembered it was the place I wanted to be, and decided what sorts of projects I wanted to build...

I'm interested in services for the iPhone and other mobile devices that help people find stuff that they want or need, and find people with whom they can interact on common ground.

Specialties

Business: Operations, technical project management, solutions consulting, small and medium sized organizations. Technical: Objective-C, iPhone SDK/Cocoa Touch, Java, JavaScript, PHP, mobile systems, web-based applications, content management, AJAX, Oracle, MySQL, SQL, XML, HTML, etc.
