We Are Sent

nu is the 13th letter of the Greek alphabet. If you look in Acts Chapter 13. You will discover a group of men sent out from the followers of Jesus gathered in Antioch. They were sent out at the prompting of God. They were sent out to proclaim the victory of Christ to a world that had not yet heard the news.

We Are Free

nu is also the number 50 in the Greek system of numbers. In the Old Testament the number 50 is a special number. It referrs to "The Year Of Jubilee." This Jubilee was to be a time, decreed by God, for the people of God, when all property was to be returned to its original owners, all slaves were to be freed, and all the debts of all the people were to be canceled.

The Hebrews never honored this Jubilee of God!

We Live Together The Life God Has Given Us

Zoe is a word in Greek that means life. Specifically as it is used in the Holy Scripture it means the life that only God can give.

It is a life together With God and together with one another.

Some have called this the Abundant Life.
