Hope Bertram's Summary

Emmy Nominated Hope Bertram jumped into the digital marketing scene early on. After obtaining a Masters in Integrated Marketing from Northwestern University, she went on to interactive marketing positions with top companies such as GE, Discover Card and Sears.

Once social media came on the scene, she quickly foresaw its marketing potential and became a power user. Now she is a highly sought-after expert, influencer and speaker on social media.

Digital Megaphone
In 2010 she founded Digital Megaphone, which gives marketing professionals and influencers the opportunity to network, learn and socialize. Digital Megaphone has connected more than 3,000 brand marketers and social influencers through cutting-edge social media summits and challenges as well as interactive blogger brand events.

Everything Healthy TV
In 2012, inspired by her desire to promote healthy living , Bertram launched Everything Healthy TV, a media network featuring products and services that help people look and feel great. EHTV leverages her ability to work with brands and reach large audiences interested in health and fitness.
