John Creak's Summary

I am presently employed as an independent marketing consultant working in motorsport and professional football. I have sponsorship and marketing communications experience having worked in F1 motorsport and indycar motorsport marketing and in corporate sales, marketing & PR in the retail oil industry. I have been actively involved in sponsorship sales, account management, media relations, event management and corporate hospitality. I have passed FIFA's official examination and am registered as an FA Licensed Players’ Agent able to offer representation services to professional football players.

I am currently working on a new international motorsports project and am based in London but travel frequently to Rio de Janeiro.

Specialties: Motorsport sponsorship sales and marketing, professional football player representation.
