Carolyn Weidell's Summary

Carolyn Weidell is a seasoned executive leader and strategic planner with over 20 years of experience enhancing bottom-line performance through skilled process-oriented management. Her comprehensive leadership expertise has proven successful in both small businesses and large, complex organizations, and within diverse industries such as healthcare, logistics, entertainment, digital services and higher education.

Ms. Weidell has deep experience in all aspects of general business management, including operations, human resources, finance, contracts, legal issues, IT, marketing, PR and business development. She has a reputation as someone who thinks strategically, works collaboratively, and effectively executes against plan.

Ms. Weidell’s long management career at FedEx Express laid the foundation for her future leadership successes. As a Senior Operations Manager she received formal and rigorous management and leadership skills training at the highly regarded FedEx Leadership Institute. Following FedEx she was involved in launching a global start-up, launching her own company, enabling a high growth company to increase revenue by 50% in one year via operational improvements, and orchestrating a turnaround/restructure that has taken a company’s margin from a 11% to 37%.

Specialties: General business management, operations, administration, human resources, recruiting, P&L, finance, IT, marketing, PR and business development. Team leadership, transformational leadership, coach, mentor. Launching start ups, orchestrating turnarounds and restructures, accelerating high growth, change management. W2 executive, interim management, consulting.
