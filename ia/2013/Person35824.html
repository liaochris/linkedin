Donald Russell's Summary

Leader in the disability and absence management market. Experience with improving operation excellence in claim management settings. Broad knowledge of insured and self-insured funding, plan design, implementation. Currently working with large employers, carriers, and TPAs conducting operational reviews, claim audits, training, life waiver of premium, and operational consulting with a focus on cost effective solutions that improve results.

Specialties

Disability Claim Audits and Operational Reviews, Underwriting, Claim Management, Claim Operation Leadership, Organizational Design, Training and Development, Product Development, Group Benefit Plan Design and Enhancement, Account Management, disability and absence management systems.

Donald Russell's Experience

Owner

Salt Associates

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Insurance industry

May 2002 – Present (11 years 8 months)

President and founder of SALT Associates. Don combines strategic thinking with diverse disability expertise to help customers find the best solutions. He has broad experience with employers, insurance companies, reinsurance companies and absence management firms. He has held significant positions in group disability underwriting, product development, disability and absence management claims, operations and senior management. He is technical, analytical and has the unique ability to communicate his reasoning, rationale and solutions in a practical manner. Don founded SALT on the simple premise that clients deserve better options and solutions from people who have hands-on experience in and truly know the disability and absence management business. Don has over 20 years of experience with companies like UNUM, Smith Group and CORE, Inc.

Vice President, Integrated Disability Services

CORE, Inc

2000 – 2002 (2 years)

Multiple responsibilities as member of Senior Management Team. As Vice President of Integrated Disability Products, managed a product development effort for self-insured and insured offerings involving STD, LTD, and FMLA. Concurrent responsibility for designing, staffing, and managing the claim service center in Portland, Maine. In addition, assisted sales in RFP efforts and sales presentations for many Fortune 500 prospects and in force accounts.
