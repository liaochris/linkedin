Colleen Tanner- Gunn's Summary

Colleen Tanner- Gunn is the artistic director of Miss Colleen's Elite Dancentre and listed in the National Directory of Who's Who in Dance Educators in New York State. Mrs Tanner- Gunn was names 2009-2010 Dance professional of the year in New York state by Cambridge's Who's Who, and enducted into the National Association of Professional Woman in New York State.
Colleen Tanners career outlines a fasinating journey in the world of dance and performing arts. Miss Colleen has a BA in Dance/ Performance from Roger Williams University where she has studied with some of New York City's greatest artists. Immediately after graduation, Miss Colleen accepted her first job with The Circle of Dance Repetory Company as a principal dancer.
Miss Colleen worked with Rosie O'Donnell, NBC Television, ABC Television, and Disney Inc. Miss Colleen also accepted jobs dancing and choreographing for Costa Cruise lines and Peter Grey Trehune Presents as well as accepting a permanent position choreographing and dancing for Phat Cat Records and DJG Productions. Simultaneously, Miss Colleen was choreographing several off-broadway productions which all recieved favorable reviews from the Backstage Performing Arts News and the Village Voice News.
Mrs. Tanner- Gunn worked closey with many vocal artists choreographing their stage shows.

In June of 2001 Mrs. Tanner opened Miss Colleen's Elite Dancentre. Elite Dancentre is located in Rockville Centre, NY.

Here at the Elite Dancentre Miss Colleen believes every child is a star. The Elite Dancentre is a nutruring enviorment where a child can begin to soar. Taught by a staff of dancers with extensive backgrounds in the working world of dance the children are able to build a strong technical and artistic foundation.
Come join the studio where all children feel right at home to be themselves.
