Marcel Chastain's Summary

Marcel Chastain is an experienced Software Developer and Technology Consultant from Los Angeles.

In 2013 Marcel founded ShowroomShare (http://showroomshare.com), an online service that helps wholesale fashion companies dramatically improve sales and operations. He also works with Adly (http://adly.com), a Los Angeles-based social advertising startup, developing social analytics technologies and building high-performance data processing systems.

He recently served as Senior Python Developer at Huge Inc, playing a key role on major development projects for top-level national brands such as HBO, Maybelline, and the National Association of Realtors. Previously he served as a Senior Systems and Security Administrator for Endurance International Group, developing products and securing the infrastructure for her 1.9 million unique web-hosting customers.

Marcel has maintained close ties to the fashion industry through consulting work with several clothing brands, showrooms, and PR companies, including extensive work with LA Fashion Week. His interests include startups/entrepreneurship, photography and advancing technology initiatives in under-served communities.
