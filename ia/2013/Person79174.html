Lauren Urban's Summary

For Lauren, graphic design is not just a career, it's a way of life. Even outside of work you can find her identifying typefaces, critiquing logos, and commenting on surrounding design work. Along with a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in Graphic Design from Eastern Illinois University, Lauren has an unmatched passion for her work. With a fresh eye and clean style, she is able to create eye-catching designs that help her clients establish notoriety in the market place.
