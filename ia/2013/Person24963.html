Cindy Jarvis' Summary

Cindy has experienced a multi-faceted work life within the retail and consumer arena. Beginning with part-time jobs as a tender teen, to entering into fashion commission sales as a post-graduate from Centennial College's business merchandising program. Her path has led her to high-volume drug and department store sales service, followed by experiences as an outsource territory lead, and then to the intimate setting of nutrition and wellness sales. Cindy has applied the skills of organization, people management and creative solution processes to her career and her life with raising a family as well as her passion for health and fitness. Cindy operated her own business as a personal trainer and nutrition coach for five years, electing to re-focus back to the merchandising career with entrepreneurial goals to combine the two successful skillsets. Cindy's favourite mantra is "feed your fire and create your bliss!" Be true to what you need to succeed, and renew, rejuvenate and reward for a life lived well.
