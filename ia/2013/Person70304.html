Vicki Judge's Summary

Judge Advertising is your one stop resource for promotional gifts, clothing, printing, awards and incentives. We have access to well over a half a Million items that are available for your custom imprint. Since 1985, I have been finding ideas for clients that fit specific requests, occasions and budgets, so you don’t have to thumb through catalogs or surf websites that take up valuable time! I’m 2nd generation in this industry, and a distributor - not a sales rep - for a major distributor. This allows me to be selective of the suppliers that are available to me, giving you a broader range of items and supplier’s products, even more than some larger distributorships can offer.

The most repeated compliment heard from my clients, is that I make it so easy for them. Our goal for each individual client is that we take care of them, and find them great solutions, whatever the need, budget, timeline, theme or message.

Contact us today to make your event STAND OUT, to provide the right strategy and product to get your prospect’s attention or for guidance in selecting the right gift for your staff, vendors, sales team or association. What is your next project or event that I can help you build your brand and build your business using custom imprinted promotional products and full color printing?
