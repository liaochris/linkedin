Angilee Shah's Summary

Angilee Shah has reported from across Asia, including China, Thailand, Indonesia and Sri Lanka, and was a South Asian Journalists Association Reporting Fellow in 2007/08. Shah is the co-editor with historian Jeffrey Wasserstrom of Chinese Characters, a collection of stories about life in China published by UC Press in September, 2012. Formerly the editor of the online magazine AsiaMedia, she is now a consulting editor to the Journal of Asian Studies and social media manager at Public Radio International. Her writing has appeared in the LA Weekly, Far Eastern Economic Review, Mother Jones Online, Pacific Standard (formerly Miller-McCune Magazine), TimeOut Singapore and Global Voices.

Specialties

Asia, politics, culture, conflict, media, immigration, minorities, health, public health, community health, global health, religion
