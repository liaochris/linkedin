Lisa Turner's Summary

Freelance Print Production Manager, Print Broker and print business owner specializing in helping marketing managers, designers, and businesses plan, implement and purchase printing from the graphic arts industry. Print project management with substantial experience, expertise, and knowledge of the printing industry having worked for various high quality print houses In Pennsylvania, Delaware and Colorado since 1982 to the present in the capacity of pre-press manager, production management, estimating, and sales.

Specialties

Print project management, print buying and price negotiations to find the best price for the best quality, digital printing, on-line ordering tool, print on demand, variable data printing and direct mail. Maintaining branding quality and color consistency across multiple and varied print runs. Consulting in graphic design and layout for cost effectiveness in print production.
