  • Wunschfutter Usability Optimization and Redesign

    • April 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Agnieszka Maria Walorska, Alexander Braun

    Starting with a thorough usability review, we identified a number of major obstacles responsible for visitors not completing the purchase process or not being able to identify how to purchase a product in the first place. Those findings were both structural in nature as well as relating to the information hierarchy of the entire website, leading us into more strategic discussions about the general approach with the founders of the company. Convinced by our findings, Wunschfutter assigned the redesign and relaunch of their website to us, with the objective of maintaining their initial corporate identity. We reduced the noise and clutter on the homepage significantly to zero in on just the USPs with a clear call-to-action conversion element. We reduced a few steps in the conversion process and added more clarity and structure to each page. We developed all templates for a seamless integration into the existing Drupal ecommerce installation.

  • Twisper concept, design and development

    • February 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Agnieszka Maria Walorska, Alexander Braun, Eva Mularonek

    From concept to design to development, we implemented this ground-breaking app from scratch. Starting with this relatively broad objective, we developed the concept of how meaningful recommendations can be generated based on each individual user’s preferences. Instead of just simply pulling in the social graph with the data that can be generated out of it from Facebook, we emphasized the importance of the interest graph instead, as the prevalent notion that my Facebook-friends’ preferences alone make things relevant for me is a major conceptual flaw in today’s apps. By using the app as a personalized location diary and bookmarking the favorite spots with a few simple tabs, the user builds up his location profile. By comparing it to the profile of others, the most similar users are identified, generating location recommendations out of those users’ favorites. We designed an recommendation-algorithm, taking into account these user similarities as well as location specific tags and a number of other factors.
