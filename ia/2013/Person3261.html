Alastair Craig's Summary

While my current professional focus is on copywriting and design, my biggest strength is in adaptability. I have a diverse interdisciplinary background spanning creative writing, web design, marketing, editing, journalism, graphic design, social media, radio and audio post-production.

I am always eager for opportunities to expand this experience even further.
