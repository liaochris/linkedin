Ann Otto's Summary

Organization Development and Human Resources consultant with a Ph.D. in OD from Kent State University and broad work experience in education, healthcare and consulting in private and public sector organizations. Work focus: Communications in the workplace; Leadership and professional development; A passion for making individuals at all levels and organizations of all types succeed. A fellow of both the Executive Leadership in Academic Medicine (ELAM) and the Institute for Educational Leadership (IEL).

Also enjoy contributing as part time Senior faculty for graduate courses in Educational and Leadership Studies (The University of Akron)and in Behavioral and Community Health Sciences (The Northeast Ohio Medical University- formerly NEOUCOM).

Interests include travel, writing, urban revitalization, and community volunteerism including the following current boards: Secretary, American Red Cross of Summit and Portage Counties; Past President, Coleman Professional Services; Chair, Rockynol Retirement Community. Assist a number of local community leadership programs as well as being an alumni member of Leadership Portage County, Leadership Akron and Leadership Ohio.

Specialties

Certifications include Certified Leadership Coach, Center for Creative Leadership feedback facilitator for 360-degree feedback instruments, and as an administrator for both DiSC and Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI) instruments. Specialties: communication interventions and training; individual and group 360-degree multi-rater feedback; team development and facilitations; leadership coaching; short term and strategic planning; and organizational and stakeholder assessments.
