Our flagship product is the Ayrstone AyrMesh system, which uses high-power Wi-Fi "Hubs" and meshing technology to provide strong wi-fi signals over a very large area. Intended primarily for farms and other rural residents, the AyrMesh system is distributes internet access over a very wide area (potentially several square miles) and creates a local-area network over that area for data collection, monitoring, and security.
