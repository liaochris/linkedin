Kelly Bloom's Experience

Founder & Chief Excitement Officer

Soapalooza! Soap Arts Studio

June 2009 – Present (4 years 3 months) Richmond, Virginia

Soapalooza is BloomWorks Holdings, LLC's newest project. Soapalooza Soap Arts Studio was started in January of 2009 first as a venue for children to make Melt & Pour soap as an activity for their birthday parties, and is now located in the Richmond and Williamsburg, VA. Soapalooza now provides both soap and cosmetic manufacturing classes, support, production space, and kits for individuals to use at their personal party events. Soapalooza has their own branded line of supply products, molds, kits, and instructional materials/classes. Soapalooza teaches budding entrepreneurs the skills required to start a natural soap or personal care business, to include the cosmetic chemistry, formulary, and regulatory guidelines.

BloomWorks Holdings, LLC continues to supply the Spa Industry with natural made product lines for both back bar and retail POP.

Founder & CEO

BloomWorks Holdings, LLC

January 1999 – Present (14 years 8 months) Richmond, Virginia Area

In 1999 Kelly Bloom founded BloomWorks Holdings, LLC as an entrepreneurial project for an MBA course. What started as a brief exercise quickly became a fast growing business with separate manufacturing and supply distribution divisions within the personal care industry. BloomWorks Holdings, LLC supplies the Spa industry with back bar & retail product lines.

Kelly has 17 years’ experience in product manufacturing, sourcing & supply procurement, supplier negotiation, cost containment, FD&C regulatory compliance, and trademark branding & licensure as Founder & CEO of BloomWorks Holdings, LLC. Kelly spent the last 5 years consulting Personal Care manufacturing companies on operations efficiency, optimizing profit centers, reducing redundancy, and enhancing ROT as well as ROI within their organizations.

Founder & CEO

Southern Soapers

August 2006 – May 2012 (5 years 10 months) Yorktown, VA

Kelly Bloom established the subsidiary brand Southern Soapers within BloomWorks Holdings, LLC. Southern Soapers provided the chemical and ingredient supply chain for both the BloomWorks Holdings, LLC manufacturing concern as well as a supply sales channel for the independent handmade soap and cosmetics companies in the personal care industry. Kelly developed products specifically targeted for the natural soap manufacturing industry, as well as educational forums to mentor and develop the industry at large. In 2011 Kelly oversaw the licensure of the trademarked brand Southern Soapers and took on the reins of BloomWorks Holdings, LLC's newest brand Soapalooza Soap Arts Studio.
