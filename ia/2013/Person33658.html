Debra Legg's Summary

Outstanding writer and editor with experience and numerous awards. Savvy content developer with SEO and social media skills. Excels at the quick turnaround, but also can shepherd long-range projects. Experienced in Web content management systems and forum/blog site administration. Proficient in all facets of video and audio production, from creating content to editing. Experienced in advertising and marketing copy ranging from brochures and eblasts to newsletters and news releases.

Previously served as interactive media editor supervising daily posting of modbee.com Web site, including content from print edition and multi-media assets. Oversaw frequent updates during the day to ensure freshness of the site. Some design and user interface work.

Moderated and administered Drupal-based blogging site, thehive.modbee, and assisted with customer support for both sites. Working parent blog, 9to5to9, formerly was syndicated nationally as a column through McClatchy Tribune Media.

Specialties

Proficient in Adobe PhotoShop, Adobe Premiere and Acrobat Professional, Microsoft Office Suite, and desktop and Web publishing systems including Drupal and WordPress. Prolific blogger whose site formerly was a nationally syndicated column.
