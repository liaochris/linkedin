"Dr. Woody" - Mike Woodward, Ph.D.'s Summary

My name is Michael “Dr. Woody” Woodward, PhD. I'm a CEC certified executive coach, management consultant, and frequent media guest expert trained in organizational psychology. I'm also author of the amazon.com top selling career book The You Plan and I write a weekly column called The Career Hot Seat that focuses on workplace challenges and business leadership. As a practitioner I work with private and corporate clients on dealing with team challenges and building management/leadership capacity through custom training/coaching programs and corporate retreats. I have worked with Bacardi USA, SAP, Century 21 Real Estate, The Miami Herald, and the Department of Homeland Security among others.

My core customized training and coaching programs:

-Psychology of Effective Management (PEM)
-Psychology of Effective Leadership (PEL)
-Psychology of Effective Teamwork (PET)

Programs and coaching engagements typically focus on building self-insight through:

-Building Team Trust
-The Role of Personality
-Management Style & Potential De-railers
-Having Critical & Uncomfortable Conversations
-The Art of Persuasion & Influence
-Judgement & Decision Making
-Values as a Compass

I founded Human Capital Integrated (HCI) in 2005 to focus on management/leadership development. Previously I worked as a management consultant with PricewaterhouseCoopers (PwC) and IBM Global Business Services. I currently serve on the Academic Advisory Board and as faculty/coach for the Florida International University Center for Leadership.

In The Media:

As a workplace expert, I have appeared on The TODAY Show, LIVE w/ Regis & Kelly, FOX & Friends, FOX News America’s News HQ, CNN International, Dr. Steve, MONEY w/ Melissa Francis, The Better Show, and Daytime. I have also conducted an intervention on Bravo Network’s Tabatha Takes Over.

I have been quoted as a workplace expert in The Wall Street Journal, Forbes, and the Miami Herald.

Specialties

Leadership Team Building, Executive Coaching, Corporate Retreats, Management Training, New Manager Training, Career Development, Leadership Development, Employee Hiring and Selection (personality assessment and interview development),Performance Management, Leadership Training
