Jonathan Gouthier's Experience

chief creative director

WITH creative, llc

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Design industry

June 2009 – Present (4 years 7 months)

As WITH’s Chief Creative Director, Jonathan believes that intelligent design is a powerful tool for helping his clients get ahead in business today. He is an advocate of intelligent design for every engagement, with every client.

Jonathan’s unique blend of design, branding and business experience has put him at the forefront of the visual communications industry for more than 15 years. He is a frequent speaker, lecturer and commentator on design and branding throughout the US. He is also an Adjunct Professor of Graphic Design at Parsons as well as the Co-Chair of the Alumni Committee for the Hartford Art School at the University of Hartford.

Before launching WITH, Jonathan was the founder of Gouthier Design – a team of skilled designers and branding pros in New York and Miami. He was also the Design Chair at the Miami Ad School. In 2000, Jonathan was selected by Graphic Design: USA as one of the top 50 graphic designers in the US. His work has been exhibited in North and South America and featured in more than 100 publications around the world.

Licensed Minister

November 2013 – Present (2 months) Torrington, CT

International Ministerial Fellowship is an association of evangelical Christian churches, ministers and lay workers with an emphasis on the centrality of Christ and the Person and Work of the Holy Spirit which offers fellowship, multiple members’ resources including appropriate ministerial credentials, ecclesiastical endorsement and the service of a Missions Sending Agency. With a membership of over 1,300 across the nation and 100+ missionaries in 44 countries,

IMF has established itself as an organization of integrity with a heart to serve those who serve others.

It is the belief of the leadership of this organization that the “call” to Christian ministry is not limited only to those fortunate enough to have completed both college and seminary or any other particular level of formal education. Many of its members are seminary or Bible college graduates while others may have theological education by extension or Bible correspondence training.
