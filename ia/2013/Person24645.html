Christy Anderson's Summary

Christy Anderson loves to hear stories. And tell them. That's why every word of her copy rings true. When people speak the truth, in their own words, the copy is not only compelling, it's motivating. True Stories is all about the story. And the truth. And having fun in the process. From San Francisco to the east coast of Florida, Christy has heard a lot of stories. And she's willing to share them with anyone who will listen.

True Stories is a freelance marketing company offering advertising concepting, copywriting, new business development and on-the-street interviewing for presentation video. Specializing in real estate, luxury resort properties, package goods and educational and financial services, True Stories offers print, TV, radio, collateral, outdoor, web content and social networking.

Formerly based in San Francisco, True Stories relocated to Orlando in November 2003. Strong national agency experience gives True Stories a competitive edge in the advertising community.

Clients include Fry Hammond Barr, Deltak, Oakland Park -- Central Florida's first certified Green community, R3MIX3D Design, Intellavia, Ryan+Deslauriers,Ten United, Riviera Maya's Mayakoba Resorts, Fairwinds Credit Union, Rosen Hotels, Westin and many more.

Christy Anderson, owner and CD, offers national agency experience in advertising copywriting, and broadcast production. She has excellent presentation skills and thrives on client interaction. Her consumer insights and "Real People" interview skills offer compelling on-the-street testimonial consumer research, which has led to a strong success record for new business acquisitions.
