Amanda Whaley's Experience

Owner

Private Landlord Directory

March 2012 – Present (1 year 8 months) Doncaster, South Yorkshire

Directory set up to help connect private landlords and tenants.
I have set this directory up from the eyes of a private landlord (which I have been for over 10 years). I really have focused on all the needs and wants of a landlord (and tenant) and tried to get them all on one website.
This directory is bringing the affordability back into renting both for the landlord and the tenant. The aim is to enable new and existing landlords to flourish and be an all-time landlords shop with facilities such as UNLIMITED PROPERTY ADVERTISING, credit searches , tenant referencing, tenant find, search recommended tradesmen, LHA rates, buy and sell tenanted property and lots of help, guidance and advice. With an annual membership of just £50, registration and browsing free, I am hoping that the private landlord directory becomes one of the 'must haves' for every landlord.
