Dawn Bugni's Summary

Talented writer, with a strong business background, skilled in creating compelling, interview-landing career documents. Adept at uncovering hidden talents and identifying marketable skills. Able to help clients sharpen and refine basic interview skills with accomplishment-focused questioning during initial consultation. Certified Master Resume Writer (CMRW), Master Resume Writer (MRW), Certified Professional Resume Writer (CPRW).

Member: Professional Association of Resume Writers and Career Coaches (PARW-CC), The National Resume Writers Association (NRWA), and Career Directors International (CDI). Current in existing and emerging career industry trends.

Specialties

Generalist -- I work with individuals at all career stages -- recent graduates, retirees starting a second career, people returning to the work force after brief or prolonged absences, up-and-coming professionals, managers -- across myriad industries.

I also work with clients editing biographies, personal statements, doctoral thesis, Web site copy and other types of professional and business correspondence.
