Michael T. Astolfi's Summary

Michael T. Astolfi is an award-winning game designer and researcher who uses evolutionary psychology to make and study video games. He holds a B.A. in Evolutionary Psychology from Boston University, and an M.A. in The Design and Psychology of Video Games from New York University.

Michael successfully Kickstarted and co-developed BlindSide, an audio-only game for both sighted and visually impaired gamers, which was honored with the Most Innovative Award at the 10th annual Games for Change Festival in 2013, and was nominated for awards from IndieCade 2012 and the Brazilian International Game Festival 2012. He also created Kifted, a game that explores how humans' ancient evolutionary instincts are stimulated by the design of modern games, and led the design of Don't Fry the Frog!, a free-to-play iOS game.

More information on his games, and Mike's TEDx talk on the Evolutionary Psychology of Video Games are available on his website, www.michaeltastolfi.com.

Specialties: Game Design & Development, Video Game Research, Evolutionary Psychology

Michael T. Astolfi's Projects

  • BlindSide

    • May 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Michael T. Astolfi, Aaron Rasmussen, Jose Frias

    BlindSide is an award-winning audio adventure game, set in a fully-immersive 3D world you’ll never see. Put on headphones, hold your iPhone, and face the direction you want to go. Listen as the world rotates around you and explore the darkness.

    You play as Case, an assistant professor who wakes up blind, to find his city destroyed and mysterious creatures devouring people. Will you and your girlfriend be able to find your way without sight? How will you escape? Run for your life, save the girl, and uncover the mystery of the apocalypse--all in the dark!

  • Don't Fry the Frog!

    • April 2011 to February 2012
    Team Members: Michael T. Astolfi, Kevin Porras, Max Meyers, Tim Levine

    A free-to-play app for iOS devices, featuring arcade action, multi-touch, and microtransactions:

    Don't Fry the Frog is the new fly-fryin' frog dodgin' arcade-style game that's zapping with multitouch action! Your frog loves to eat, but if he scarfs down any more flies, he'll explode! Touch two fingers to the screen to make a fly-zapping beam and use it to fry those unhealthy bugs! But watch out - there are healthy flies that your frog needs to eat! Don't fry them - and of course, Don't Fry the Frog!

  • Kifted

    • January 2011 to May 2012
    Team Members: Michael T. Astolfi, Chris DiMauro, Justin Sanders, Chris Kurisu Lo, Tim Levine, José Frías, katherine isbister

    Kifted is a game about survival. Two players work together to impress a judgmental scientist by balancing two competing activities: gathering berries and hunting hornets. The scientist is only impressed by the number of hornets that players hit, however, hunting draws energy from a shared pool, which players can replenish by collecting berries.

    Kifted’s design draws on principles of evolutionary psychology; its hunting and gathering mechanics model activities for which humans retain adaptive instincts. Further, the players’ showcasing of their survival abilities is reminiscent of mating displays. Kifted encourages cooperation, and simulates one important facet of the hunter gatherer lifestyle.

  • Time Dominators

    • April 2010 to August 2010
    Team Members: Michael T. Astolfi, Nicole Leffel, Kevin Porras, Richard Rodriguez, Casey Lent, Charles Brillant, Logan George

    "Time Dominators" is a heavy board game that forces players to carefully balance cooperation and betrayal as they vie to seize control of the timeline. Three pairs of players represent teams of mercenaries that visit the same location across three time periods, battling one another as they steal technology, seize time portals, and assassinate great inventors.

    This project began as a student assignment, but was subsequently polished over several months into an elegant and efficient game system. It is available on the print-on-demand board game site, The Game Crafters: https://www.thegamecrafter.com/games/time-dominators
