Georgiana Cohen's Summary

At heart, I am a storyteller. In the words of one of my favorite authors, J.D Salinger, I believe that a good story should make the reader “unreasonably vigilant,” spurred to awareness and action. And this, of course, applies to all things content.

With that as my motivation, I am currently employed as Director of Online Content at Suffolk University, overseeing university-wide web and social media content efforts. I am continuing to seek opportunities to help individuals and organizations with their web content development and strategy needs through my independent consultancy, Crosstown Digital Communications LLC.

In 2011, I co-founded Meet Content, a blog focused on helping higher education create and sustain effective web content. In November 2013, Meet Content will join forces with Confab Events to present the inaugural Confab Higher Ed conference, focused on higher ed content strategy.

From 2004 to 2011, I served as manager of web content and strategy at Tufts University. My background is in journalism, including a three-year stint working in the fast-paced online newsroom of The Boston Globe.

If you are interested in working together, drop me a line. I look forward to hearing from you.

Specialties: Content strategy, news writing, copywriting, storytelling, online journalism, social media, community building and engagement, user-generated content
