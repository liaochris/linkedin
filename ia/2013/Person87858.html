Marty Gilbert's Summary

President of Growth Initiatives LLC. A senior level marketing, sales and product management executive who has led teams for Fortune 100s, middle market turnarounds and small entrepreneurial firms. Expertise covers marketing strategy, revenue-driven marketing campaigns, branding, advertising/PR, M&A and sales strategies. Led numerous new product and service launches, implementing a State-Gate process that includes market opportunity assessment, competitive positioning, business case modeling and go-to-market plan. Marketing initiatives are enhanced by direct sales, channel partner management and P&L general management background. Over 15 years of international experience (including expatriate assignments in Japan and Hong Kong) and have driven global revenue expansion for several firms. Japanese and Spanish language skills.

Industry experience includes: wireless telecom, IT, healthcare, wellness, electronics, information services, software, e-commerce, RFID, data/analytics, manufacturing and numerous technology applications.

Specialties: Marketing, messaging and branding; product management; strategic planning; Web site management; go-to-market launches; online marketing; product positioning; product roadmaps; sales management; business case modeling, channel partner management; business development; international business, general management and P&L management.
