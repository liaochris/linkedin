John Cove's Summary

I have vast experience rooted in the commercial digital signage, event production and IT industries, working for over ten years developing digital signage networks for a wide range of companies and applications across the UK and USA.

Throughout my career, I have gained comprehensive experience and proven commercial application in:

I have a wide range of commercial technical experience:

• Google AdWord campaigns,
• SEO
• Web development (Wordpress, Moodle, PHP, JQuery, JavaScript, ASP, HTML5, mySQL, MSSQL, XML),
• Digital signage platforms (Scala, SignageLive, OneLAN, Beabloo, BroadSign, PADS, Navori, Acquire, BrightAuthor),
• Software development in Microsoft Visual Studio,
• Visual Basic.NET and C#.NET
• Social Media set up and promotion.

One of the earliest projects I worked on in was with the WHSmith Travel's digital signage network. The network spanned over 60 stores in all major airports and railways stations throughout the UK, including Selfridges, and all major USA airports. During this project, I monitored and maintained the digital signage network and then developed additional software solutions for the bespoke digital signage platform.

More recently, I acted as Operations Manager and Project Manager for Surgery Media Systems Ltd Dental and GP based digital signage and interactive kiosk network. I managed the entire project from beginning to end, continually supporting and developing the system.

I am also the Development Director for Screen Media Technology where I assume the role of technical authority for the company. I am regularly involved in all aspects of projects, from design through to installation and development.

Currently, I also undertake consultancy work through my own successful company, J.A.C. Digital Ltd.
