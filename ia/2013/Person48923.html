Blum Real Estate... the most trusted name in Real Estate

WHO ARE WE? We are a team of full time professionals that share high values and a commitment to excellence. We are not the largest company in town, but a small and powerful company where quality is the standard, not just quantity. From the management team, to the sales people, to the administration staff, each team member is given the mandate to provide ultimate customer experience in real estate services. When you hire a broker at Blum Real Estate, you really have a whole team working for you. Whether it is showing property, listing property for sale or bringing a sale to close, your entire real estate experience is surrounded by a group of professionals that have a high standard of excellence in order to meet and even exceed your expectations. The moment you meet the team at Blum Real Estate, you will know that you made the right choice of REALTORS.

Each member of the group has 15 to 30+ years of experience in the real estate field and has earned a variety of designations and expertise…
Such as GRI (Graduate Realtors Institute) CRS (Certified Residential Specialist) SRES (Senior Real Estate Specialist) ABR (Accredited Buyers Agent) CDPE (Certified Distressed Property Expert) RDCPro (REO Default Certified Professional) and are well qualified in the field of new construction, luxury home sales, relocation, and real estate investments.

English or Spanish, young or elderly, new to the area or long time resident, we can meet your real estate needs. We understand you have a choice in REALTORS, which is why we work very hard to earn and keep your business. Our clients are number one, so everything we do is to better serve you in the process of buying or selling your home. When choosing a REALTOR, please consider experience, reputation, service, commitment, customer satisfaction, technology and marketing.
