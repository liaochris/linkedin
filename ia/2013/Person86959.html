Mark Hall's Summary

Only four years after graduating with his MBA, Mark has achieved a director level position at a B2B marketing agency. Mark tackles every challenge with enthusiasm, attention to detail and strategic forethought.

One of Mark’s most notable undertaking was to project manage a go-to-market strategy for a four-partner IT coalition, launching a datacenter solution into the financial services market. Other noteworthy projects were developing a communications plan to promote a consumer product nationally through Home Depot and specialty stores, and developing a plan for the largest construction management firm in Vermont.

Mark enjoys seeking out new solutions to solve old challenges, which is why he enthusiastically advocates for the implementation of Salesforce.com within companies to be able to track, manage and nurture businesses development leads.

Mark is currently actively searching for his next challenge to undertake. Have one? Send it his way…1, 2, 3 go!
