Rick Boyer's Summary

As a Senior Software Engineer, with over 19 years of professional software development experience, I have developed on multiple platforms including Windows, Web and Mobile (including Windows CE, Windows Phone and Android.) With a passion for mobile software development, I now focus exclusively on Android development! From my experience working in small Startups to very large companies, I find the Startup environment the most exciting where my contribution can really make a difference.

Consulting offers me the perfect opportunity to best utilize my skills by giving small companies who need an Android Expert but don't have the resources for their own development staff. If you need Android work but don't know where to start, send me a message and I'll be happy to give you some direction - even if I'm not the best fit.

I offer the following:
• Communicate with you in clear English – not techno-speak
• Quick grasp of the big picture and how to use technology to solve problems
• Work together to understand your requirements
• Published Android Developer – see AppWatch on Google Play: www.AppWatch.ws
