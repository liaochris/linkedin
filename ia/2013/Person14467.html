Bill Stoddard's Summary

Experienced in antique clock movement repair and restoration, specializing in American antique clocks and German 400 day clocks.

Goals
Keep repairing my favorite types of clocks (400 day, American antique); enter all Westclox models in the clock history database; scan Westclox catalogs and link images to models; implement examples, patents and documents in the clock history database.

Specialties

Antique clock repair, relational database schema design, logic and semi-custom IC design, Westclox clock and watch history research, website design using a database and PHP.
