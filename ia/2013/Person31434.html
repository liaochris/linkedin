David Livingston's Summary

The foundation of Ameriprise financial planning is a personal relationship between you and your advisor. Our unique and collaborative Dream>Plan>Track approach to financial planning starts with your dreams, not just the numbers. I will work with you regularly to take action to update your plans as your needs and dreams evolve, recommending products and services that are right for your situation. I will get to know you and your dreams, and because our relationship is not just a one time meeting but ongoing I can help make all the difference in your plan, your life and your ability to achieve your dreams. You will have greater peace of mind knowing that you have a plan in place as your life changes. As your priorities evolve over time, I will work with you and revise your planning strategies to help meet your evolving needs.
