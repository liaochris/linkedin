Dave Axenty's Summary

Professional FAA ratings including ATP (AMEL), Commercial pilot (ASEL), Instrument - airplane, CFI, CFII, MEI, CL-65 (Bombardier Canadair Regional Jet) type rating. Experience has included training new pilots under FAA part 61 and FAA part 141 in both private and university-level flight schools, as well as a sole proprietor. Flight experience in the CRJ (200, 700, and 900), Cessna (150, 152, 172, 177, 182, TR182, T210, 421), Piper (PA28-140, PA28-151, PA28-161, PA28-180, PA28-181, PA28R-200, PA28R-201, PA24-250, PA44-180), Diamond (DA40), Cirrus (SR22), and CZAW SportCruiser (now PiperSport). Professional experience in flight operations as a first officer, and in customer service as a supervisor for a successful regional airline.

Specialties

Aviation education and consulting. Personalized training for private, instrument, commercial, sport pilot and flight instructor ratings. Flight reviews, instrument proficiency checks, high-performance and complex endorsement training, multi-engine, glass cockpit transitioning (Garmin, Avidyne), and GPS training. Exclusive instruction experience in the CZAW SportCruiser.
