Grayson Koonce's Summary

Entrepreneur & Generalist. 10 years of experience developing the front and back of robust web applications. Adept with a variety of languages, frameworks, & environments. Passionate about pair programming, TDD, small agile teams, and open source. Believes that:

• Applications should be tested.
• Deployment should be automated.
