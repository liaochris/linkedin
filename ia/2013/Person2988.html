Alaia Williams' Summary

I help business owners and professionals get things done. What started as a professional organizing business in 2006 grew into a collaborative practice where I work with business owners to identify operational/organizational obstacles including gaps in resources and knowledge, lack of organization and inadequate (or nonexistent) systems and processes. We then develop a clear plan of action and proceed to work to clear those obstacles that hinder business growth. I work with new business owners to create the proper foundation from the start. I identify bottlenecks in growing businesses and create and implement systems and solutions that free up the owners to focus on their core business (money making) tasks, grow their teams, take on new opportunities, and increase their revenue.


Specialties: Organizing, efficiency, small business systems, consulting, administration, non-profits, project management, social media, email marketing, professional networking, time management, writing and blogging.
