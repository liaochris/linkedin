Greg Reibman's Summary

My background is in digital, print and broadcast media with considerable experience in social media, SEO, content management and product positioning. I’ve built enduring collaborations with news organizations, business groups and elected leaders. I’ve managed budgets, hired and fired and been the public spokesman for products I helped produce.

I’m a tireless blogger and Twitter fan (@greg_reibman). I’ve mentored account executives, managers and journalists in Web 2.0 and 3.0 social networking .

And I like to think my background suits me well to lead my hometown Chamber of Commerce into the new millennium.

Specialties: Collaboration, empowerment, business development, economic development, partnerships, audience building, political strategy, team building, fundraising, government relations, revenue generation, branding, competitive strategy, digital media, social media, SMO, SEO, branding, competitive strategy, training, blogging, tweeting and content generation
