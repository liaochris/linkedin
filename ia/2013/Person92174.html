Michael Cayley's Summary

Cdling is a ratings agency that delivers rankings, expert assessments and scientific analysis to help investors, startups and the advisors who serve them build trust and allocate attention and money better.

For the latest news & offers from Cdling cut & paste this link: http://bit.ly/SmdDcj

We use proven scientific methods to deliver our ratings including real world social network analysis, financial analysis, competitive analysis and prediction markets.

Our goal is to help dramatically improve the success rate of investors and startups.

Cdling evolved out of my experience across several startups in China, the US and Canada and my Social Capital Value Add thesis (see www.socialcapitalvalueadd.com).

Specialties: Management - start-up and turn around situations.

Strategy. All aspects of corporate & business development.

Attracted over US$50 million from global institutional, corporate & private investors.

Exceptional P&L, team building and corporate development experiences.

Propelled over US$21 million in complex “C” level sales with scarce resources.

Led design & development of a social media network, developed winning teams, cultivated crucial strategic alliances.
