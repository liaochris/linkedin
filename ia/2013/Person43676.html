Chris Neve's Summary

We are a boutique consultancy serving, small and medium sized businesses in Brisbane, the Gold Coast and South East Queensland. We understand the challenges faced by small businesses and assist and support implementation of projects and plans they have for their companies to take them to the next level.

What we do:
* Work with business owners and companies supporting and coaching projects across a broad spectrum of business activities,sales , technology, marketing and staff development.
* Help companies develop their management team with the assistance of diagnostic tools and mentoring to reduce conflict achieve better outcomes through increased productivity.
* Review systems and technology to help work more efficiently and effectively.Good systems supported by appropriate technology free up time to work on value add activities.
* Review sales and marketing activities. We help review and develop sales teams performance and systems.
* Help prepare your business for sale/exit, to ensure that you receive the best return for your business.
* Assist in online marketing including use of social media, your website, content generation and email marketing.

We offer:
* Business Support Programmes that work on a weekly basis to help implement the plans and goals you have for your business.
* Diagnostic tools for your company and staff to benchmark performance, target quick wins and prioritise projects to grow your business.
* One on one coaching and mentoring.
