Cliff Humphreys' Summary

My name is Cliff Humphreys and I am a designer. Although, claiming to be any one thing in my life isn't that comforting because I like to do alot of things. And I never want to limit my ability or perspective, especially working in creative disciplines. But I choose the label "designer" to keep it simple. I'm not exclusively a graphic designer, a web designer, a clothing designer, an art director, or a photographer. Im all of these things almost everyday, or should I say I play these roles everyday. What I am for sure is a visualist. I create things that leave an impact by communicating something... whether it be a message or a feeling. I subscribe to the simple philosophy that I am a Graphic Communicator and that being a designer and a visualist is about Visual Communication. I generally appreciate all things that are beautifully designed, but through my work, I solve communication problems and typically provide business solutions in various mediums. In short, all of this is achieved by creating visually arresting compositions that serve a purpose.

School? Yes. A four year college education in graphic design, one which is rooted in thinking and concepting and applied in the traditional medium of print. But over the years, through my experience of working with very talented people and through my own educational efforts, I've developed my skills to reach into specialized disciplines. That of which are web design, clothing design, photography, and brand strategy. At the core, I am a traditionalist and I am hands on. Ideas always start on paper with a pen or pencil or whatever i can get my neurotic paws on to flush out an idea. And from there, I execute my ideas with the computer in some digital medium.

Specialties

art direction/creative direction, brand strategy, web design, print design, logo & identity development, html & css, basic javascript.
