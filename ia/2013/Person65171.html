Jody Jacobson's Summary

I help executives and individuals sustain success, especially during critical pivot points in their businesses and lives.

Even the most brilliant CEO can feel ungrounded and unsure as challenges get bigger and more complex: The business isn’t growing and productivity is flat. People are writing their own play books and working in silos. Or, an entrepreneur has brilliant ideas and plans, but managing the small stuff is keeping them from implementing the big stuff.

My clients are smart, motivated people who want to get their businesses and lives back under control. I help them stop spinning off course and start thinking into better business and personal solutions.

I’ve been there myself; and have gotten back on the mountain and climbed out of the fog. I know what works to get grounded and get better results in life and organizations. My expertise includes:

• Process Facilitation
• Leadership Thinking Skills Training
• Personal Mastery Coaching
• Book Yourself Solid® Coaching
• Business Plan Development

My powerful thinking tools, systems, and approaches are based on my years of experience advising smart executives, teams, and individuals; as well as my deep knowledge about neuroscience and change, predictable problems that limit growth, common sense, and a strong drive to help my clients feel clearer, more grounded, and less distressed.

To learn more about my programs and services, and to get my valuable free resources, please visit http://jodyjacobson.com.
