Johanna Rehnvall's Summary

I have almost 20 years of experience from the communication business, helping to solve and create strategies for clients’ communication challenges, find crucial business insights, developing new methods and models for communication strategies and producing and designing successful presentations using PowerPoint, Prezi or Keynote.

I thrive on great communication in all forms - strategic communication, visual communication, and advertising and collect brilliant corporate stories. I have a passion for visual communication and presentations - too many great stories and too much knowledge get lost in the jungle of bad presenters and even worse PowerPoints. My Scandinavian roots give me a unique perspective on US trends and unique possibility to interpret and explain them - and how to turn trends in to insights.

I’m the Founder of VisionJar™, an international communication and insight agency who helps organizations understand, utilize and communicate trends, innovations and ideas, particularly in the US and Presentitude™, a corporate communication and presentation design agency, specializing in visual communication to improve organization’s presentations and information design.

As the former COO/HR-manager, Board Member for The Prime Group in Stockholm I has extensive experience of all aspects of management, human resources, internal communication and organizing and leading a growing, entrepreneurial consultancy business.
