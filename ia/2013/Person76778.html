Kripanidhi S M's Summary

I engage myself in consulting and coaching assignments in the areas of Agile Program Management, Agile Product Management, Enterprise Agile Implementation, Agile Coaching and Project Delivery Management.

In over two decades of my career in software development, I have managed over 35 projects and 6 products successfully. Over the last 11 years of my IT consulting, I have trained and coached over 15,000 software development professionals at all levels of seniority worldwide.

For the last 11 years I have been involved in implementing Agile Methodologies, Enterprise Agility, Off-shore Subcontractor Management, Agile Product Management and similar Value Streaming based iterative software development methodologies. I specialize in Software Requirements Engineering and Software Product and Project Management.

I coach project teams implementing Agile Methodologies like XP, Scrum, FDD and Lean. I also help organizations plan for and implement Agile Leadership, Lean, TOC/ CCPM in their management orientation and help them in understanding the new cultural transformation required to embrace such emerging paradigms.

I have been a contributing author in Agile Project Management for Cutter Consortium (http://www.cutter.com ) . I have designed, developed and delivered customized programs in Agile for Center for Technical Training (CTT), Phillips Research Labs, Netherlands and am an accredited Project Management Trainer for Logica CMG internationally.

"Priming Passions to Perfection" and "Process Innovations in Software Engineering" are my aspirations. It should help us discover new ways to build software products, faster, cheaper and better with a set of delighted customers and motivated teams who enjoy co-creating innovative software solutions.
