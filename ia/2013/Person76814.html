Krish Kothand's Summary

Krish Kothand qualified as a dentist in 1998 and is currently practising as a private associate in two locations in the UK – Romford and Dover.

Krish has a passion for minimally invasive Cosmetic Dentistry and Cosmetic Orthodontics. He has undertaken extensive training in the US,UK and Italy, with his portfolio of Cosmetic Dentistry procedures now including Vanini composites, Minimal-prep veneers, Teeth whitening, Metal-free restorations and more. Krish provides a wide range of Cosmetic Orthodontics for adults including Invisalign, Six month smiles and Inman aligner. He also practises routine General Dentistry and Preventive Dentistry.

Krish is a full member of the British Academy of Cosmetic Dentistry (BACD) and a Sustaining member of the American Academy of Cosmetic Dentistry (AACD). He is one of the few UK cosmetic dentists to pass the AACD accreditation written examination. Krish is also a member of the Association of Dental Implantology (ADI) and the British Dental Association (BDA).
