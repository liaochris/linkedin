Daniel Gullin's Summary

I got more than 16 years professional IT experience in telecom, hosting and Internet based companys and has great skills and experience in Linux, UNIX, Windows and network technology.

Over the years I work as CTO, Solution/IT Architect, Teamleader, Technical Project Manager and of course pure technican. I got great experience and love to taking responsibility and leading people to common goals!

Started to use Linux back in the early days (1995) and has worked with all most every major Linux distributions like Debian, Red Hat, CentOS, Ubuntu, SUSE and Slackware. I am also Red Hat Certified Engineer, RHCE.

My strength is that I am very creative, technically broad, focused and committed and always try to see solutions instead of problems. Others see me as a positive and very social person who is easy to create new and maintain existing contacts.

Is a big proponent of open source because of its openness, communities and of course the business model, and as Richard Stallman said:

- To understand the concept, you should think of “free” as in “free speech,” not as in “free beer"!

I love to think "out of the box" and discover solutions whith new / better technology or diffrent mindset. My experience and predilection to open source based system gives me a strong platform and a open mind for new and great opportunities.
