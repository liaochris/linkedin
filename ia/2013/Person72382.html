Karle Fried's Summary

Karle Jordana Fried is an artist specializing in portrature, including children, pet, family and creative head shot photography, Her work is described as sweet, real, candid, beautiful and emotional all at the same time. Through photography, Karle is able to capture real moments in people's lives and take beautiful, one of a kind portraits. She has been working for ten years and started her own business two and a half years ago and is doing quite well. Her goals are to become more national and international by continuing to work with all people of all ages throughout the world.

Professional Creative Head Shots, Children, Babies, Pets, Family, Weddings. Specialize in Creativity an Natural Lighting.
