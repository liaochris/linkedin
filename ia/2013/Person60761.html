Founder, Developer

1UP Industries

Public Company; Myself Only; Computer Software industry

November 2010 – Present (2 years 7 months) Ann Arbor, MI

1UP Industries is a brand representing intuitive improvements to things we already know and enjoy.

1UP Industries offers user experience, business strategy, and technology architecture consultation regarding an array of technologies and applications. As a software producer, 1UP Industries' headlining product is the popular Windows taskbar enhancement Bins, which was named among PCWorld's Top 100 Products of 2011. Other featured works include desktop icon organizer Stardock Fences, and animated dock Stardock ObjectDock, which combine to have over 15 million downloads and over 1 million daily active users.

Previous to it's official formation, 1UP Industries had also developed 'the DellDock' in conjunction with publishing partner Stardock Corporation. The DellDock shipped on over 30M Dell PCs from 2008-2011.
