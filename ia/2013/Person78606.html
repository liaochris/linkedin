LaShanda Henry's Summary

In 2000 I started developing my core site, Multiple Shades of You Online. Originally the concept of the site was an ezine for young girls of color, but over the last years it has transformed into a growing network of positive black websites, some of which include Search Urban, Urban Boutiques, Urban Dynamics, Black Moms Club, Mahogany Momma, Blackhairopedia.com, Black Business Women Online, and Black Girl Click. In addition to maintaining the msoy network, I am a self-published author, graphics design, and web developer. I am currently working on providing my internet marketing services to entrepreneurs and small business owners interested in niche marketing opportunities through the msoy network. [msoyonline.com]

One of my most prized accomplishments is my self-published book, 'A Better Today Brings a Brighter Tomorrow', it is a resource guide for Black Parents and Minority Students highlighting the various free and affordable programs available to urban students from pre-college programs to internships, and mentoring organizations. I hope to help parents and educators provide more African American Children with realistic forums in which to develop their academic and personal talents. [http://www.msoyonline.com/abtabt]

I currently maintain 15+ web properties include the SistaSense Money and Marketing Blog for Women Entrepreneurs, the Black Business Women Online Social Network and several others.

Specialties

Graphics Design, Web Development, Self Publishing, Desktop Publishing, Flash Design, Computer Programming
