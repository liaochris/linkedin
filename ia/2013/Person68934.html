Josh Goodis, CPA, MTax's Summary

Over the years working as a CPA, I've developed a strong appreciation for small business owners who grind everyday to successfully manage their companies. I greatly enjoy working with small businesses owners to create and maintain their accounting systems, provide tax advice to minimize taxes to the legal limit, and offer expert business guidance to maximize profitability.
