Joao Grisantes' Summary

Born in Portugal, Joao is a BA candidate in Cinema, Video and Multimedia Communication at Universidade Lusófona de Humanidades e Tecnologias (Lisbon). He was based in the United Kingdom (Manchester) between 2012 and 2013 as an exchange student at University of Salford and he is currently based in Portugal (Lisbon), looking forward to build a future in areas he is particularly passionate about: Photography, Filmmaking, Cinematography and Color Grading.
