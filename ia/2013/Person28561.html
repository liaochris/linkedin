Cynthia Marsh-Croll's Summary

Learning & Development (L&D) leader with in-depth systems knowledge, a solid grounding in curriculum development and a proven ability to design programs that increase productivity, strengthen services, and ensure business continuity. I work with businesses and professionals who struggle with feeling overwhelmed in their organization and would like to be more productive. What separates me from the competition is my creative, fun approach and high-energy combined with a customer centered delivery. Because of this unique approach my employer or clients are able to stay motivated while learning or implementing new processes that they embed in their organization. I offer a strong grasp of adult learning principles and a background that includes administrative, training and efficiency initiatives for corporate and not-for-profit organizations in various operational environments.

Highlights of Qualifications

* Highly resourceful and meticulous instructional trainer/workshop presenter who anticipates business needs, easily establishes a reliable network of contacts, and willingly learns new tools and technologies.

* Proven ability to liaise among functionally diverse workgroups and subject-matter experts (SME).

* Skilled at identifying deficiencies, initiating change processes, and establishing responsive training programs that optimize the learner experience and strengthen both operations and systems processes.

* Able to acquire, correlate and transform large amounts of data into insightful and user-friendly information.

Productivity and Efficiency Expert Cynthia Marsh-Croll is Lean Office Certified through the University of Michigan.
