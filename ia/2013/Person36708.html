Founder/CEO

Dynamic Images

1984 – 2007 (23 years) Hawthorne CA, USA

Originally founded in 1984 as Production Photo/Graphics, Dynamic Images was a retail graphics solution provider producing graphic programs for major brands, companies and retailers world wide. The company was known for it's innovative production techniques that allowed it to produce high quality photographic short-run marketing programs. As the founder/owner and creative director of the company I created the first photographic banner concepts used in retail advertising in the U.S. and the United Kingdom. During this time we perfected the large format, (up to four by ten feet) 3D lenticular concept used to promote products and services for companies world wide with distribution in 12 countries. We owned or controlled 35 patents for the production of photographic products and services.

Adbox

1989 – 1995 (6 years) Hermosa Beach CA

Adbox was an innovative company that produced a unique display concept used for point of sale in the retail industry by national and international brands. Adbox displays were manufactured in Sweden and could be found in stores, shops and showrooms around the world. The company had offices in Gothenburg Sweden, London, NYC and Hermosa Beach CA, with representation world wide.
