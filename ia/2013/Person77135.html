Kristin McDonnell's Summary

Kristin McDonnell is a veteran of the digital media industry and a former CEO who drives innovations in social media, mobile, digital marketing, advertising, e-commerce, and games. Drawing upon her significant experience, she is the Managing Director of M33 Partners, a strategy consulting firm, and serves on several Boards of Advisors.

Kristin has been a management team member of six start-ups which raised over $100MM in venture capital and realized billions of dollars in market value through two IPOs and five acquisitions by major media and advertising companies.

As an entrepreneur, CEO, Chairman, VP Marketing, and VP Content, she has been a pioneer in the creation and growth of over 12 digital media brands and 25 consumer online services and software applications. Kristin has been an exec team member responsible for acquiring over 20 million digital consumers and generating millions of dollars of revenue through online and mobile advertising, subscriptions, and payments.

From 2004-2010, Kristin was the CEO, Chairman, and Co-Founder of LimeLife, the first mobile and web digital media company serving female consumers. The company forged significant software distribution, publishing, and advertising partnerships with the US wireless carriers, major media companies, and advertisers including AT&T, Verizon, Sprint, Apple, NBCU, Time Inc., MTV, Yahoo, and P&G. The company was acquired by Total Beauty Media in 2010.

Kristin has an MBA from Harvard Business School and a BS degree in Industrial Engineering with a focus on Computer Science from Northwestern University with Tau Beta Pi academic honors.

Expertise: Board of Advisors, Board of Directors, Consultant, CEO, Chairman, VP Marketing, Business Development, Sales, Product Mgmt

Specialties: Digital, mobile, and social media strategy, customer acquisition, branding, revenue generation, advertising sales, partnerships, product management, venture capital, mergers and acquisitions, games, female consumers
