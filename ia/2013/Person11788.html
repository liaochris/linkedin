Barbara M. Wahli's Summary

I have over 18 years of marketing experience, including project management, sales operations, marketing communication (marcom), customer service, administration, and leading development teams for hi-tech clients. Since my passion in life has always been music, I started Barb Rocks Presents in 2006 as a side business. I mainly book original rock oriented bands at various venues throughout the South Bay ensuring a good quality show that's very well organized and professionally run. In addition to booking shows, I also manage some talented original local bands, as well as handling publicity and promotions for other up-and-coming artists. I am also a core group member of the community outreach movement that created and hosted Left Coast Live, the premier downtown San Jose annual music festival, for which I was responsible for music selection, booking coordination, marketing and promotions, website blog editor, and social media outreach.
