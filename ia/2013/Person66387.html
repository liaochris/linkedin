John Girouard CFP,ChFC, CLU, CFS's Summary

I reside in Georgetown, Washington DC, and Bethany Beach, DE with my wife, Colleen, of twenty-three years and my daughter, Caitlin, and son, Coley. I enjoy cooking, golfing, reading, and traveling.

I have served as the President and CEO of Capital Asset Management Group (formerly known as AGI Financial Services, Inc.) -since October of 1984. I am a Registered Principal and Office of Supervisory Jurisdiction Manager of Cambridge Investment Services, a national Broker-Dealer. I am life and qualifying member of the Million-Dollar Round Table and the International Association of Financial Planners.

I received my Bachelor of Arts in Economics from the University of Maryland College Park. In addition I have earned numerous industry certifications and awards. I have earned the designation as a Certified Financial Planner Practitioner, Certified Fund Specialist, Chartered Financial Consultant, and Chartered Life Underwriter.

I have dedicated my extracurricular time as chair to the University of Maryland's College of Behavioral and Social Science Capital Campaign. I have served on the Board of Governors of the University of Maryland Alumni Association, the Board of Visitors to the College Park Foundation, as well as the Board of Trustee's - Human Incentive and Resource Education Institute.

Specialties

Creating financial strategies and unique conversations about the purpose of money in your life.
