Andy Debus' Summary

I have been a professional artist for 24 years and worked on a variety of projects. This has given me a wide range of art and design skills using 3D and 2D software. In 15 years as a full time Game Artist I worked on 12 published titles across all platforms and since becoming freelance I have contributed design and graphics to more games, apps and websites.

Freelance work includes:
3D Modelling and Texturing:
