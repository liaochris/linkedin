Jerilyn Soncrant's Summary

“If butterflies had a human face, that would be me. Growing up, I was always ‘the social butterfly’ of the group, which I think fits in well with my job and greatest passion, social media. When I'm not building ads for one of our clients, I'm busy researching all the latest and greatest in social. It's an ongoing process and I love that everyday I get to learn something new. A bit more about me, I help organize the monthly Social Media Club of Phoenix meetings and volunteer as an advisor to the ASU chapter of Chi Omega. And yes I still have time for fun, I just love to curl up with a good book on a warm sunny day or go hunting for a good shoe, handbag, or accessory boutique. Oh, and I can’t forget about my adorable bulldog, Bella, she's always there to make me smile."
