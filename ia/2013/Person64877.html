Joanna Conti's Summary

I am an experienced businesswoman, non-profit executive and political activist. I have started and run several food and software companies, an international non-profit, and an international marketing consultancy. Two recent things I am particularly proud of include turning around a real estate education company in 2008's terrible economic downturn and running for Anne Arundel County Executive in 2010.

I have started both an international nonprofit that has raised millions for orphans in Africa and Asia and a non-profit called Anne Arundel Cares which raises money and volunteers for organizations making a difference in Anne Arundel County.

Specialties: political advocacy, politics, consumer product marketing, start-ups, turn-arounds, food, international marketing, non-profit,
