John Auld's Projects

  • SierraLink - Modular Systems Integration Platform

    • May 2001 to March 2004
    Team Members: John Auld, Colin Mackay, Kevin Horio, Jim Ross, Amir Konkevic, Glenn Driban

    Developed a modular and configurable platform for high-throughput data integrations, marketed as SierraLink by FNX, currently sold via SunGard. SierraLink provided library-level configuration for protocol-handling, data parsing, data transformation, and transactional interfacing to the Sierra capital markets trading system. However, SierraLink was designed to be fully independent of the Sierra system and could be used for integration in general. SierraLink would evolve into a service-oriented framework, enabling the Sierra system to expose select services from any of its backend server processes. Built entirely in C, SierraLink is extraordinarily fast.
