Mark Carroll's Summary

Hi, my name is Mark. I am an Interactive developer Manhattan. Before moving to USA to do a BFA in New Media at The Academy of Art University, I had a successful career as an owner of Gravitate, an interactive agency in South Africa. I have a strong foundation the technical aspects of front-end development as well as design. I am a self motivated and curious about finding ways to create a better user experience, whilst educating and learning from the people around me.

I am a creative, lateral thinker with 14 years of experience providing technical solutions to client's challenges. I enjoy both the creative and technical process. My skills range from illustration / storyboarding, wire-framing, digital mockups, to prototyping and front-end code development.

I code for the DOM using industry standards with HTML, CSS and either JavaScript or jQuery. I understand the flow between frontend and backend development, communicating well with other designers and developers as well as clients.
