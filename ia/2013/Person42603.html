Erik Hiep's Summary

Managing Director of The Next Level, Management Consultants, with over 20 years of international experience in leadership and management. Associate Professor at IE Business School in Madrid. Erik has extensive consulting experience with international management teams and boards. He has worked on a wide variety of assignments in Europe, Middle East, Africa, Asia & the US.
