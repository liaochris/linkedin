Hans Bakker's Summary

Currently i am leading the AntWebsystems company which is specialized in providing services for the Apache open source project OFBiz, a leading ERP system. The company is growing fast because of our excellent service and our competitive rates. AntWebsystems is the largest contributor of the Apache OFBiz system with over 2000 contributions.

With A-NeT Internet Services I leaded the development of a special tailor made CMS version for the most visited internet site in the Netherlands with more than 6 million visitors per day. Also the implementation of a workers union portal with more than 100 publishers maintaining the site online was implemented.

Previously, during the first half of the nineties, I worked for a large record company where I developed, with a team of programmers, an artist royalty system for 4 countries in Europe and in the US: in New York and Los Angeles.
During the eighties I worked for a large US multinational, operating the software change management of a large team of programmers in Germany, Italy, Spain, Belgium and the US and developed and implemented a software change management system to support this activity.

Specialties

project manager of ERP installations of Apache OFBiz, the flexible open source ERP system.
