Philip Lindsley's Summary

30 years in practice. Certified Specialist in Elder Law (CELA), specialization accredited by the State Bar of California and the American Bar Association; California State Bar Certified Legal Specialist in Estate Planning, Trust and Probate (CLS); Accredited VA Attorney; Martindale Hubble "AV Preeminent" Rated; Former Adjunct Professor of Law at USD; Practice limited to Medi-Cal planning and asset preservation; Public benefits issues; Special needs trusts; Life Care Planning; Conservatorships and alternatives; Estate planning and probate administration. Our goal is to serve San Diego County's senior and disabled communities, their families and caregivers in preserving their homes, assets, and dignity, while assuring access to the best possible care. Phil Lindsley is a native San Diegan, Graduating from Crawford High and SDSU.
