Rochester’s Next Great Park: The GardenAerial will transform the perimeter of the Genesee Gorge, including the Ponte de Rennes bridge, into an outdoor hanging arboretum – becoming an impressive and lasting symbol of the “Flower City”. The site was identified because of its historic and unique location and because it is also an important element in the continued re-vitalization of downtown Rochester.

It is our belief that The GardenAerial could also become a major tourist attraction. This project, like Waterfire in Providence or the Arch of St. Louis, could potentially become a permanent symbol of the Flower City.
