Justin Hernandez's Summary

I'm a music and technology nerd and a fan of well crafted and thoughtfully designed things. I love user experience design and user research.

Here's what I'm really interested in these days, including some new toys:

Mobile devices: Android / iOS/ Windows & Windows Phone and how to make the next generation of mobile hardware and software. I use them and hack them all daily because I truly am that obsessed.

Wearable electronics: Pebble, AGENT Smartwatch, Google Glass (sadly don't own one yet) and how these devices will revolutionize personal computing. I want to work with people who feel the same.

Gesture based interfaces: Leap Motion, Microsoft Kinect. I love these things and the new ways gestures can be used in interaction design. I just got my Leap devices in the mail, can't wait to start playing with them.

Virtual and Augmented Reality: Oculus Rift, Google Glass and smartphones. The applications for virtual reality are boundless, ranging from hardware and software training, immersive gaming, to treatment of PTSD and other trauma. I'd like to help create memorable and even helpful experiences for this kind of technology.

I've spent the last few years as a user experience designer doing work on mobile applications, websites and standalone software but my true passion lies in the world of new and emerging mobile technology.

If you've read this far, let's talk.
