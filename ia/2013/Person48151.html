Gerard Lameiro's Summary

Gerard Francis Lameiro Ph.D. is the author of America’s Economic War and Choosing the Good Life. Most recently, Dr. Lameiro was the Founder and CEO of Lameiro Economics LLC, a company focused on bringing practical economic knowledge about freedom, economic growth and prosperity to America and to the world. Dr. Lameiro was a member of Hewlett-Packard’s Strategy and Corporate Development team where he was Director of New Business Ventures for HP worldwide and managed HP’s Technology Evaluation Process worldwide. He is retired from Hewlett-Packard. Dr. Lameiro is also the former worldwide President of the Association of Energy Engineers (AEE) and a National Science Foundation Post-Doctoral Fellow. Earlier in his career, Dr. Lameiro was an Assistant Professor in Colorado State University’s College of Business. Dr. Lameiro has been a frequent and popular Talk Radio show guest and has appeared on Fox News Channel’s Strategy Room. Dr. Lameiro’s expertise is in complex, real-world computer models, including static, dynamic, and stochastic models. Dr. Lameiro is also an expert in economics and economic growth.
