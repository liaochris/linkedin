Bill Fox's Summary

Bill helps organizations take a more informed, nuanced and conscious approach to improvement. He believes many organizations are too quick to buy into the latest “silver bullet” without fully understanding the problem that needs to be solved and the challenges they will face in the solution selection.

At the same time, many organizations are stuck on the sidelines, afraid to even try to change, or to try again. With over 30 years’ experience in project management and leading successful process improvement projects, he has demonstrated a unique perspective on how to approach and focus improvement efforts on the right priorities to achieve results with certainty. Even so, he has witnessed a repeated pattern of failure among many organizations.

Determined to have an impact on this repeating pattern, he created the 5 Minutes to Process Improvement Success interview series, which is designed to uncover and leverage the best performance and process improvement strategies and tactics of top practitioners from around the world.

This work has provided him with a unique perspective on how organizations can benefit from a more nuanced, informed, and disciplined approach to improvement.

Bill blogs about his results at http://5minutespisuccess.com, http://foxhighperspective.com/blog and http://leadchangegroup.com/author/bfox. Follow him on Twitter at @bi11fox to get an ongoing stream of ideas that will help ignite your improvement efforts.
