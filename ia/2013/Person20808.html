Jesse Casman's Experience

February 2010 – Present (3 years 7 months) San Francisco

Serving clients in open source, software development, and other high-tech areas. Full range of PR and marketing services, especially interesting is working with Japanese companies entering the US market.

I am fully fluent in Japanese and have experience both working in Japan and bringing a Japanese startup to America. In the past three years I have helped multiple Japanese clients enter the US market including Lunascape, Plat'Home, and quanp (Ricoh).
