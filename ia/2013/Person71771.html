Kaleigh Rogers' Summary

I’m a multimedia maven, a jack-of-all-trades, but most of all, I’m a journalist.

A small-town girl at heart (I grew up in a town with a population of 400) I’m tackling the big city in a big way. I’ve cut my teeth reporting in Canada’s top and toughest newsrooms: the Globe and Mail, the Toronto Star and the CBC. Covering everything from homicides to politics to the occasional cat on a plane, I’ve pretty much seen it all. And I’m only getting started…
