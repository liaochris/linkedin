Jacqueline Lafloufa's Summary

Reading and writing are my passion. I have 4+ years of work experience with digital journalism, writing for known Brazilian sites as Blue Bus, a 18 years old media news site that I helped to triple the audience in less than a year, and Tecnoblog, one of the best tech news site in the country. In the last year, I had learned a lot about managing an online publication, from tech-related structure till publishing guidelines and brand awareness.

Specialties: strong webwriting skills, understanding of social media dynamics, experience in events covering, texts reviewing and translating with context for Brazilian audiences. Confident and articulate speaking abilities and experience. Easy with tech and science subjects, tech, pop and internet culture.
