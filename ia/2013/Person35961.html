Djen Choo's Summary

Web & graphic designer, idea-smith and all-round marketing & communications design professional. I have over a decade of hands-on practice designing and creatively directing projects. I write too, and sometimes I’m good. I can help you communicate and market your business or brand through creative entrepreneurial design. Let’s connect, at the very least we can share some ideas. Who knows? We might find a way to collaborate. Maybe we’ll just plant a garden, or build a bridge. Maybe, we’ll even begin a relationship – shocking. Hey, none of us can predict the future, but we should definitely try our best to design it.

A rare fruit getting riper with experience under a creative canopy of possibility. Looking for that opportunity with a progressive company offering a rich, open, dynamic culture looking to make a difference. Let's build bridges.
