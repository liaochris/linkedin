MEIKI DE BC S.A. DE C.V.'s Summary

Being leaders in the plastic injection molding and mold making, we have successfully gained trust of our customers and the experience to maintain the business with them.

Starting as a mold maker company in Japan, Meiki expanded its business to different parts of the world.
Meiki de BC Established in 1996 in Tijuana, B.C. México, the plastic injection molding, tooling, paint and mold maker company.

Specialties

● Paint and decoration.
