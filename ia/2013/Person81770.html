Lissa Cowan's Summary

I enjoy envisioning fresh ways to hone a client's business personality and story. I might possibly have written enough words to reach the moon, can't really say as I'm not very good at math. I do know that I've created more than my share of explosive content for clients. Also, as a seasoned communications pro with over 12 years experience, I've helped businesses and not-for-profits with communications planning and strategy to help them nail their business goals and vision, and expand their influence.

I began my career in writing and journalism and have worked with some wonderful and talented people in media, government, business, the not-for-profit sector and in academic institutions.

I received a Master’s Degree from I’Université de Montréal, Québec in English Studies. A few years later, with a post-graduate Harvey Southam Diploma in Professional Writing from the University of Victoria, B.C. under my belt, I worked in government and learned how a crazy demanding communications shop is run.

After my experience in government, I struck out on my own as a consultant. Over the past few years I lead social marketing initiatives for governments and not-for-profits to help them reach target audiences and build relationships between organizations and stakeholders. I developed social media campaigns that enable many different groups to communicate, work together and share information online. I have written slogans, jingles, scripts, web copy, books, ads, blogs. You name it, I've probably written it. Okay, I haven't written a libretto, but then I'm not bloody Mozart, you know.
