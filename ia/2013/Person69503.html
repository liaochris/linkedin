Joyce Joneschiet (Jona-shite)'s Summary

I love being an interior designer. Ever since I can remember, I've been interested in my environment and the way it impacts how I feel and how I live in it. I'm passionate about interior design and helping people live & work in spaces that are comfortable and make what they do more effective & enjoyable. I've solved some of a family’s everyday living challenges, such as storage space and a more efficient kitchen. Organization, I've found, as well as interiors that reflect a family’s values and vision, are key to getting things done around the home and contributing to family harmony.

We're your partner throughout the design and implementation process, from concept to actualization. We also offer as many interior design choices as possible, such as environmentally friendly products and aging-in-place and accessibility design. With foresight, we can create a flexible design that accommodates you now and in the future. This could save you both time and money and enhance your quality of life in the long term.

Encore Living Interiors donates a portion of its earnings to two local charities that help children here and in Africa live happier, healthier lives.

Specialties: Interior Design, Kitchen and Bath, Universal Design - designing for any age, season of life and physical ability.

Joyce Joneschiet (Jona-shite)'s Projects

  • Scary to Safe Shower

    Team Members: Joyce Joneschiet (Jona-shite)

    Before and after of a dangerous shower turned into a beautiful, safe and comfortable shower. My client who had experienced a stroke, came home in a wheelchair and her husband who had a bad back had to lift her out of her wheelchair into this wooden chair. We tore out the unusable shell and installed beautiful hand holds (grab bars), glass tile inlay, padded folding shower seat and a zero clearance entrance. Now her shower is safe and we've made taking a shower fun again!
