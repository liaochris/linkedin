Jess Eddy's Summary

Learn more about me: www.Overture.me/jess

I made my way into User Experience several years ago after working in ad agency settings and falling in love with technology, products and software. I knew I wanted to design great product experiences so I made a pivot to work in tech-focused companies and environments. After doing that for awhile and approximately two years ago, I left my full-time job to work as an independent consultant and pursue other dreams of building a business. I’ve had the pleasure of working with a variety of great clients doing the type of work that I love. I live and work in beautiful Greenpoint, Brooklyn.

I’m an avid maker of things and I work with great people to do so. I co-created Tweat.it (www.Tweat.it), NYC’s only real-time food truck map. I’m also the co-found of Phin & Phebes ice cream, a Brooklyn-based company, making exciting, all-natural ice cream flavors currently available in almost 200 stores in 18 states. I’m also working on getting my first SaaS product off the ground.

Other Personal Projects
• Warble, daily email alerts for Twitter (www.Warble.co)
• Wordsmith, a jQuery plug-in that adds on-demand word definitions to your site (http://getwordsmith.co)
