Barbara Steffens PhD, LPCC, BCC's Summary

Helping professional for 20 years, with over 10 years of experience in assisting spouses of sexual addicts. Experienced program director in agency and church settings, speaker and trainer on a variety of mental health-related topics to public and professional audiences. Past and current adjunct faculty member for four Universities. Current President of the Board for the Association of Partners of Sex Addicts Trauma Specialists.

Published scholarly journal article The Traumatic Nature of Disclosure for Wives of Sex Addicts (2006) Journal of Sexual Addiction & Compulsivity.

Co-author Your Sexually Addicted Spouse: How Partners can Cope and Heal, (Steffens & Means, 2009).

Specialties

Counselor Educator; Supervision of counselors and counselor-trainees, consulting, counseling in area a sexual betrayal recovery, speaking/training, crisis intervention and prevention, grief counseling, Christian counseling.

Barbara Steffens PhD, LPCC, BCC's Publications

  • Your Sexually Addicted Spouse: How Partners Can Cope and Heal

    • New Horizon
    • August 2009
    Authors: Barbara Steffens PhD, LPCC, BCC, Marsha Means, MA

    Partners of so-called sex addicts suffer multiple betrayals and the resulting pain leaves many looking for help and hope. Dr. Steffens research is the basis for this book- research that helps understand the trauma experienced by those in close intimate relationship with someone who has a secret life of compulsive sex outside of the relationship. This book is ground breaking and is helping many find hope for healing.
