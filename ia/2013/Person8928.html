Tomas Anton Escobar's Summary

I am UX Designer based in Madrid with a broad experience in Interaction Design. I truly believe in user-centered design as a way to make great solutions and desirable products that achieve user goals and needs. I totally convinced about the value of user research to know user expectations about a service or a product.

I have been improving my expertise in usability techniques to plan which ones maximize business benefits and reduce business risks to enhance conversions.

I'm continuously growing in multidisciplinary teams designing innovative multi-device solutions to leader companies in the communication sector, IT, banking, and customer services.

Tomas Anton Escobar's Publications

  • UCD Methods Priorization with Usability Planner

    • Proceedings of the 6th Nordic Conference on Human-Computer Interaction: Extending Boundaries
    • October 15, 2010
    Authors: Tomas Anton Escobar, Nigel Bevan, Xavier Ferré Grau

    The diversity of User Centred Design (UCD) methods and the difficulties for estimating their cost-effectiveness make planning usability activities in systems development a hard task. Usability Planner is a tool to support the selection of UCD methods to be applied in a particular project or organization, and to estimate the relative cost benefits of applying usability methods at different stages.
