Kristopher Kobe's Summary

I'm an energetic, detail-oriented designer and writer that floats effortlessly between left- and right-brain faculties, always keeping the viewer/reader in mind. My work stands in its own right, but really shines when I have a few human walls to bounce ideas off of. I am an adept communicator that enjoys playing an active role end-to-end in the development and execution of innovative creative work.

I don't work FOR people—I work WITH people. I don't need my hand held, but I do work hand-in-hand with clients and teammates, maintaining a productive conversation about how best to create design solutions.
