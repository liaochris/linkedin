Lori Ecker's Summary

Law Practice Dedicated Exclusively to Employment Law.

Practicing for 30 years, I represent employees in a broad range of employment issues, including negotiating executive compensation packages and negotiating enhanced severance packages. I represent employees who have discrimination, harassment and retaliation claims. And, I counsel employees with noncompete, nonsolicitation, and confidentiality agreements.

I am also a certified mediator and arbitrator of employment disputes. I am listed as a court-certified mediator for the Circuit Court of Cook County's Court-Annexed Major Case Civil Mediation Program and am on the American Arbitration Association's National Employment Arbitration Roster. I received my training as a mediator with the DePaul University Mediation Training Certificate Course in 2006. I am a member of the Association of Attorney-Mediators.

I am also experienced in neutral investigation of workplace disputes.
