Jay P. Clark's Summary

Jay P. Clark is the manager of Clark’s Crystal Springs Ranch in Southwest Idaho. The ranch farms around six square miles spanning from Boise to Hagerman. In addition to a fully functioning farm, Jay P. Clark’s ranch also runs a commercial truck division that hauls various equipment and farm commodities to the majority of the Western United States.

Clark’s Crystal Springs Ranch grows a number of crops including hay, corn, barley, and wheat. In 2013, Jay P. Clark also plans to add Camalina, a flowering plant known for its cold hardiness and its high yield of Omega 3s, to the farm’s fields. Camelina is also a highly efficient biofuel source, most notably utilized in jetliners, according to Clark.

Jay P. Clark assumed his role at the family farm in 2007, when his father suffered a debilitating stroke. Prior to that, he was a lawyer with a thriving practice in Mountain Home. Though he allowed his license to become inactive in 2009, Jay P. Clark enjoys keeping abreast of interesting legal cases, specifically relating to agriculture.
