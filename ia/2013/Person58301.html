Janet Johnson, MBA's Summary

With over 17 years in the manufacturing industry, Janet has served customers at the start-up, growth and established corporate level; with sales between $5-$100 million; and, shipping millions of product weekly to a global marketplace.

She is proficient at business management, time management, and money management. She uses her unique skill set to teach small business entrepreneurs how to improve efficiency with their offices. With her own proprietary Focused Business Plan System (TM), Janet shows clients exactly how to create a back office system that supports business growth and success for the long haul. With her proprietary 7-Day Weekly Planner (TM) and Action Plan Matrix, Janet show clients how to manage change and new improvement projects.

Successes include identifying $41,000 of negligent expenses; reducing expenses by $100,000 after conducting financial review; increasing sales by over $250,000; renegotiating higher prices for over 50% of existing jobs; implementing lean techniques resulting in ISO certification.
