Marc Stoiber's Summary

I'm a creative director and brand strategist who helps companies connect dots, weaving their strengths and attributes together to build resilient, futureproof brands.

In addition to consulting with clients, I write about futureproof brands for journals like Fast Company, Huffington Post, GreenBiz and Sustainable Brands, and speak on the subject from coast to coast

Specialties

Integrated marketing, brand strategy, creative direction

Marc Stoiber's Additional Information

Honors and Awards:

Marc Stoiber's Experience

Creative Strategist

Marc Stoiber Enterprises

2011 – Present (2 years) Vancouver, BC, Canada

Using my experience, strategic processes and insight, I help clients build strong, resilient, futureproof brands.

VP Green Innovation

Maddock Douglas

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Management Consulting industry

January 2010 – March 2011 (1 year 3 months) Chicago

I helped Maddock Douglas build notoriety as a green brand innovation company. I brought IP like the MapChange green brand study to the company. I spoke and wrote a great deal on behalf of MD, helping raise their profile in the green brand innovation community.

President, Founder

Change

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Information Technology and Services industry

November 2005 – February 2010 (4 years 4 months) Vancouver

Change was committed to building green brands. We helped clients engaged in sustainability reap maximum brand reward for their efforts. Clients included multinationals like Unilever and Molson, key regionals like TransLink, and startups in every area from consumer goods to cleantech

VP Executive Creative Director

Grey Canada

Public Company; 5001-10,000 employees; WPPGY; Marketing and Advertising industry

2001 – November 2005 (4 years)

As ECD at Grey, I helped engineer the creative 'turnaround' of Grey Canada - taking the agency into the top 10 in creative rankings. My teams helped build client business, and won awards at advertising and design festivals that included the One Show, CA, Marketing, and Cannes

VP, Creative Director

DDB Canada

Public Company; 201-500 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry

1997 – 2001 (4 years)

As copywriter, and then CD at DDB, I helped create campaigns like the Bud Light Institute and the Mott's Clamato Ninjas, and the Humungous Bank campaign for Richmond Savings Credit Union (rated one of the top campaigns of the 20th Century by Marketing Magazine.

Creative Group Head

BBDO Dusseldorf

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; OMC; Marketing and Advertising industry

1994 – 1997 (3 years)

At BBDO, I worked as a senior writer on accounts like Wrigley's and VW, writing both german and english copy. I also helped BBDO win SEAT, the Spanish VW brand. And I helped the agency win awards like Eurobest for my work on RTL public television

Copywriter

Grey Hong Kong

Public Company; 5001-10,000 employees; WPPGY; Marketing and Advertising industry

1989 – 1992 (3 years)

At Grey Hong Kong, I learned a great deal about cross-cultural marketing, and communicating without words. A great education that would serve well in the future. I worked on multinational accounts that included Dior, Kenzo, Lancome, Saab, and Dunhill, and won awards that included Freshman Copywriter of the Year.
