Lori Greymont's Summary

Lori Greymont has been the president and CEO of multiple real estate investment companies and is currently the founder and CEO of Summit Assets Group. Lori's core responsibilities have included fund acquisitions, market research, coaching and training, project management, resource allocations, legal, management of sales and marketing efforts, and nationwide property management.

Lori grew up in the family environment of real estate investing and has been buying and selling real estate since 1987 in various parts of the nation. She has hands-on experience in fix-and-flip strategies, turnkey investment properties, land banking, land to vertical development, rezoning, multi-family re-performance and SFR sales and marketing through traditional and creative methods.

With her passion for sharing her knowledge and her desire to educate others, Lori is a sought after real estate speaker. Lori has been a featured speaker at numerous conferences including BiggerPockets Real Estate Investment Summit and Expo, Real Estate Investment Expo Silicon Valley, Seattle Real Estate Summit, and Entrust Self-Directed Investors Conference.

Lori is a team member of Real Estate 360 and will be hosting a weekly live radio show on real estate investing.

Lori graduated Cum Laude from Moorhead State University with a Bachelor of Arts in Accounting with a minor in Computer Science.

Specialties: Lori Greymont speaks and trains on the topic of building cash flow properties when you have no time or money, creative real estate investing and funding, and partnering non-profits with business for a bigger profit.

Lori Greymont's Experience

Summit Assets Group

November 2010 – Present (2 years 11 months)

Some people call Lori Greymont the "Chief Education Officer" because of her passion for educating people about real estate and passive income strategies. It's a passion and a strategic vision: trained clients are successful clients, and successful clients are returning clients. Lori is an avid blogger, speaker, trainer and coach focusing on various real estate investment topics including turnkey investment properties and creative real estate financing strategies.

In the last few years, Lori and the Summit team has sold more than 1,326 individual homes to home buyers and investors. Summit’s Investor Team focuses on creating a personalized and individual Investment Blueprint for each client and then selects Turnkey Investment Properties that meet those needs. The focus is on buy and hold strategies that deliver high returns with minimal risk, producing dependable passive income coupled with capital growth.

For real estate entrepreneurs Lori has created highly successful real estate training and coaching programs delivered online and in live workshops. The programs help novice as well as seasoned investors avoid the costly and stressful mistakes made by many real estate investors, and allows them to create income quickly. Lori's coaching programs range from affordable online group coaching to 1-1 mentorships.
