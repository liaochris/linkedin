John Lionberger's Summary

I spent nearly 25 years in sales in the broadcast and cable industries. I was VP of sales for four companies.

In 2001 I started Renewal in the Wilderness, a 501(c)(3) not-for-profit that takes people of any faith, and often enough no faith, into the wilderness to explore, experience and renew their spiritual lives in ways that more civilized settings do not seem to encourage or allow. We go to some of the most beautiful parts of the country--and now Europe--for our canoe and kayak trips. And we have a shameless amount of fun.

We've been featured in many newspapers (including the Washington "Post", and twice in the Chicago "Tribune"); featured four (4) times on PBS, and we were profiled for 30 minutes on ABC/Channel 7/Chicago. Links to both the PBS feature and the ABC interview are above, in the "website" section. We are also endorsed by the National Council of Churches.

In 2007 my book, "Renewal in the Wilderness: A Spiritual Guide to Connecting With God in the Natural World" was published by SkyLight Paths Publishing (Woodstock, VT), and it has done very well, both critically and in sales. It's in international distribution, and Amazon (15 five-star reviews) has digitized the book for its Kindle digital reader.

SPECIALTIES:

* group/individual spiritual direction
* creating safety in unfamiliar environments

John Lionberger's Experience

President and Founder

Renewal in the Wilderness

2001 – Present (12 years)

I spent nearly 25 years in sales and management in the broadcast and cable businesses. I was also (at the very least) an agnostic, and probably an atheist, until a wilderness experience on a dog sled trip changed everything. I left business to attend seminary, to start my company/ministry, Renewal in the Wilderness.

We take adults on inter-faith (and agnostics and atheists, too) canoe and kayak trips to some gorgeous places, to tap into the same spiritual power that people around the world have tapped into for at least 6000 years. Our trips are 1, 3 and 7-10 days in length, and become trips of life renewal as well as spiritual renewal. And we have a LOT of fun. We help people find the reset buttons for their lives.

John Lionberger's Publications

  • Rrenewal in the Wilderness: a Spiritual Guide to Connecting With God in the Natural World

    • SkyLight Paths Publishing
    • June 10, 2007
    Authors: John Lionberger

    "Renewal in the Wilderness ... " is about the undiminished power of wild places to simplify our lives, to put us in more direct relationship to God, and to clarify who we really are. The book traces the 6000+ year history of wilderness spiritual exploration across the world, across cultures and religions, and how the experience is exactly the same regardless of when, or where it took place. People from 6000 years ago would recognize what we do today when we employ the wilderness is such ways.

    • December 10, 2010
    Authors: John Lionberger

    Presence Magazine is distributed to spiritual directors around the world. This article talks about how time spent in intentional spiritual pursuits in the wilderness can both heal AND crack open our hard "shell" of resistance to a relationship with God (for those who are comfortable with that name), or Something Bigger Than I Am.
