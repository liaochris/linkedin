  • Youth Adrinet - Workshop on the Social Entrepreneurship - Dubrovnik (Croatia) 2013

    • settembre 2013 a settembre 2013
    Membri del team Gianni L'Abbate

    I had partecipate in a workshop on the Social Entrepreneurship through Youth Adrinet Program in Dubrovnik, a city in the far south of Croatia. This program connect the youngs of many Adriatic Region, Country and insitiution (Apulia, Province of Campobasso, Pesaro-Urbino, Rimini and Gorizia, Slovenjia, Novi Sad-Vojvodina, Municipality of Dubrovnik, Skhodra, Sarajevo, Pula, Izola) to create a network to exchange knowledges, culture, to learn another languages and improve the english speaking and listening, to learn about the social entrepreneurship and the benefits that this model of entrepreneurship has for the local community.

    In this week of workshop and lessons, we made a Youth Adrinet Guide. A touristic guide of the City of Dubrovnik, made in co-working and co-thinking with the other partecipants at the project.
