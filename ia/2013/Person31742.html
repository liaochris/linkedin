David Edman's Summary

PLEASE WATCH MY PERSONAL BRAND LINKEDIN VIDEO BELOW

My clients know me as an action-oriented, results-driven healthcare consultant.

I always ask business owners and senior managers “Are you getting maximum value for your healthcare purchasing dollar?”

If the answer is “no” or “I don’t know”, then I am ready to help, because my practice is:

MAKING HEALTHCARE…UNDERSTANDABLE, AFFORDABLE, AND ACTIONABLE

We have access to the best healthcare services in the world, but that’s offset by a poor system of insurance and financing.

A PwC study (http://pwc.to/10qQmsV) finds that up to 50% of healthcare spending is wasted on unnecessary, inappropriate, and poor quality care, as well as administrative waste. This really troubles me.

The challenge for business owners or senior managers is first understanding the drivers of their healthcare costs, then believing they can do something about it, and finally taking appropriate action(s).

Most of your healthcare budget (±85%) pays for actual healthcare, with the remainder paying for administration. I focus on wasted healthcare dollars, consistently helping my clients uncover the real issues and “bending the cost curve”. Better Outcomes. Higher productivity. And more dollars to invest in people, process and product.

My clients understand the future impact of health reform. Maintaining compliance with numerous new regulations is important, but implementing a strategy for getting the most “bang for your buck” is crucial.

I’m passionate about reducing waste and increasing value. I can offer you a full range of consulting services or a “Second Opinion”, without disrupting any internal or external relationships. My mission is to end the upward trends in healthcare costs for my clients.

Please contact me. I’m here to serve.

Specialties: Healthcare Costs, Health Economics, Healthcare Reform, Value-Based Purchasing, Health Consulting, Employee Benefits
