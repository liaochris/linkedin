Keith A. Kleinick's Summary

Co-Active Life Coach, Attorney at Law, and Certified Structured Settlement Consultant [CSSC]

Keith brings a unique perspective gained from a successful legal career—a career highlighted by many significant verdicts and settlements obtained while working for high-profile New York law firms.

Keith obtained his law degree from Brooklyn Law School in 1989 and his CSSC from Notre Dame Businesses School program through NSSTA [National Structured Settlement Trade Association.]

Keith is admitted to practice law in the State of New York and in November 2012 was admitted to the United States Supreme Court

In 2013 Keith completed studies with CTI (Coached Training Institute, the largest coaches training program). This Co-Active Coaching Model helps individuals realize that they are Naturally Creative, Resourceful and Whole. In Co-Active Coaching the coach and client are in partnership to create a relationship that empowers the individual to realize who they want and need to be to get to their next place; To help put the spring back in their step; to fulfill their life goals. .
