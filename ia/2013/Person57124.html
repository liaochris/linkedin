Jake Wiley, CPA's Summary

Jake currently serves as the CFO of Sader Power Enterprises, headquartered in New Orleans Louisiana. His principal objective is to facilitate the widespread adoption and affordability of solar taking Sader from around 2 megawatts to 8 megawatts of installations per month over the next 12 months. Through strategic financial partnerships, Jake aims to take Sader to over 120 megawatts of installed solar within the next 24 months.

A CPA with nearly 10 years of finance and accounting experience, Jake began his career with the world's largest accounting firm PricewaterhouseCoopers where he worked on both buy side and sell side transactions in amounts form $12 million to $5 Billion dollars in transaction value. He worked directly with private equity investors and sellers, as well as company owners focusing on Quality of Earnings analysis as well as assets.

Jake brings to Sader Power the right mix of understanding and believing in the mission of the company, technical and certified accounting background, experience dealing with both large and small clients, what it means to run a small but efficient and growing business, working at a fast pace yet remaining accurate and professional, and an understanding of the importance and diligence involved in developing and recruiting the right team. Jake understands that an efficient but scalable process is the key to growth and each financial process or improvement is designed with that in mind.

Specialties: Tax Equity, PPA, Leases, Solar Power Finance, PV Finance, New Market Tax Credits, NMTC, Financial Due Diligence, financial analysis, sourcing and placement of strategic finance positions, excel, quality of earnings, mergers and acquisitions, recruiting, management, income statement and balance sheet analysis, identification of excluded business expenses, organizational design, financing transactions, and interfacing with Board of Directors, private equity investors, investment bankers, lenders
