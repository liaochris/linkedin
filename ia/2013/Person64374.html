Jeannette Karis Relocation & PCS specialist's Summary

A Spokane RE/MAX broker & REALTOR®, licensed since 1994, I am passionate about real estate and selling homes in Spokane. I have worked in up markets & down markets & changing markets. Experienced in all aspects of contract negotiation and strategic financing, I enjoy going the extra mile for my clients. Experienced with FHA, VA & conventional loans. In fact my most surprised clients in the last 2 years have been using conventional loans, now there are more hoops to jump through. I have a team of experts I can recommend to help your transaction go smoothly.

I believe honesty is always the best policy. I believe in the Code of Ethics & sit on Professional Standards boards.

I find the more educated and current I am with real estate info, the better I can advise my clients. I aim to be their REALTOR® for life.

To paraphrase Will Rogers "Jeannette knows what she is doing, she loves what she is doing and believes in what she is doing."

Having relocated from the UK, I understand moving across town or across the country. Military moves, PCSing? been there done that!

I embrace technology using a smart phone and tablet, so I can more quickly respond to my clients inquiries.

Please connect with me on LinkedIn & Facebook, SpokaneHomes4u & jeannette.karis

Showings on my listings are pushed to my phone so I can get accurate feedback.

Jeannette Karis Relocation & PCS specialist's Experience

"The REALTOR® with the English accent!"

RE/MAX of Spokane

Real Estate industry

August 1997 – Present (16 years 5 months)

As a retired USAF wife I have personal experience of long distant relocation and culture adjustment. Been there, done that.

Working with sellers who are not living in Spokane is also a large part of my business. Keeping in touch so they know exactly what is going on, regular reports by email or telephone. Also with buyers before they move so they can hit the ground running when they arrive or make a pre-move visit.

I have worked with many families of seniors who are selling the family home after many years and help with the emotional detachment that has to take place. Taking the time to listen, in fact just taking the time with my senior clients.

Spokane is a retirement destination for many of my clients and I can explain WA real estate state law and local practices and how this may differ from their prior experiences. I network with agents all over the country at state, national events and on line, collecting info.

Helping First Time buyers is a very rewarding part of my work - sourcing information they need to achieve the American Dream of home ownership. Keeping them on track with up to date info and giving them the big picture as well as the detail.

I am actively involved with local and state associations and attend conventions (both NAR & R4) to keep current with real estate info, practices and technology. I was the 2009 President of Spokane Assoc of REALTORs® working to give back to my fellow REALTORs®. Have taught smart phone and tablet use and relo info. A Washington REALTORs®. state director and a VP of Information Systems and Communications. Women's Council of REALTORs® Spokane board member.

Mission Statement - I will achieve long term success in real estate by integrity, exceptional service and a dedication to improving not only the economic well-being but also the quality of life of my clients.
