Kevin Lerner's Summary

Kevin Lerner is an experienced Marketing/Communications Professional, Presentation Designer & PowerPoint Expert, Trainer/Speaker, Business Strategist. As the founder and executive director of The Presentation Team, Kevin and his team have developed thousands of presentations for clients including Motorola, Comcast Cable, Office Depot, Citrix, Oracle, UBS Financial, Ryder Transportation…as well as numerous smaller companies, non-profits, professional speakers, and individuals.

Kevin also works with companies and individuals to improve their presentation skills and PowerPoint design with custom training and coaching. His PowerPresentations training programs feature numerous tips and strategies to help add power to PowerPoint and deliver presentations with impact.

Since founding The Presentation Team in 1995, Kevin has developed numerous techniques that have helped him to develop presentations with great efficiency, agility, and creativity.

Kevin is also a freelance writer for several publications, as well as for his company's own "Power Presentations" blog.

Kevin attended the University of Florida and majored in Broadcast Journalism and minored in political science. A Florida native, Kevin lives in the Washington, DC region. He enjoys road travel, jazz & classical music, swimming, political affairs, photography, and sharing time with his family and friends. And working.
