Holly Pajak's Summary

redesigninmind was born as an interior design and decluttering service specializing in assisting seniors and their families during the transition from the family home into a smaller residence, retirement community, assisting living facility and/or nursing home.

Holly provides the emotional and physical assistance needed in making a change in living arrangements. Her background as an interior designer, provides her clients with an organized and streamlined space paying particular attention to aesthetics and spacial planning.

Her organizational skills, energy, passion and compassion combined with her interior design experience are invaluable tools for providing excellent service to all of her clients.

Holly Pajak's Projects

  • Senior Move/Redesign

    • July 2009 to April 2010
    Team Members: Holly Pajak

    The senior move/redesign project was initiated to create ease of use in downsized living space. The client relocated into this smaller home which required updated cabinets, countertop, backsplash and new appliances. Linoleum floor was replaced with tile and new wall color was selected to complete the design scheme.

    The living room was decluttered and a new look was achieved by adding new carpet, furniture and wall paint.

  • Office Decluttering and Organization

    Team Members: Holly Pajak
