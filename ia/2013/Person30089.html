Dan Kulpinski's Summary

Environmental Science Writer

Versatile journalist and editorial professional with 24 years of experience writing and editing for print, Web and radio. Created articles, blog posts, photos, and radio scripts for environmental news outlets, advocacy groups and green-industry companies. Completed a master's degree in Environmental Sciences and Policy at Johns Hopkins University in May 2013.

My goal is to use my planning, communication and information-research skills to help advance the mission of an organization working to protect and preserve the environment and/or increase our knowledge of environmental science.

• Wrote for many blogs since 2006, including Down to Earth (AOL), GreenDaily, EarthSky, GreenListDC, Bethesda Green, and Johns Hopkins Environmental News.


WEB CONTENT PLANNING AND EDITING

EDITORIAL PROJECT MANAGEMENT
