David Field's Summary

I am passionate about three main things:

- All things ‘design’

- ‘Design Thinking’ teamwork

- Supermarket checkout design

CHECKOUT DESIGN

In 2002 I had a fresh approach to checkout design. I had worked previously in design management in three very different industries. This contributed to somewhat shaking up UK based checkout manufacture.

Blighline had just won the contract for Tesco Main bank checkout supply. My first task was to make the manufacture profitable by design. Blighline, subsequently for the next ten years, won the design and manufacture contracts for Tesco main bank checkouts. The winning formula was a mixture of value engineering and design for checkout operations productivity.

Through more value engineering, I enabled Blighline to also win the supply of Tesco Express store checkouts from the competition. And this further lead to redesigning checkouts for Waitrose/John Lewis and others. Then, full circle, Tesco saw the Waitrose mini checkout design & asked me to redesign their current mini checkout to add the new key benefits.

Fast forward to now and I now have designed for all sorts of supermarket retailers. I am now working directly with BIZ Engineering the UK steel fabrications manufacturer and have designed the most flexible, customisable checkout designs yet.

The David Field BIZ modular checkout is totally designed for ‘mass customisation’. It incorporates productivity benefits plus easy store planning, easy store reconfiguration, ease of adapting to changing IT & fast installation.

The next steps; New markets/sales channels which make it easy for any retailer anywhere in the world to specify buy and install checkouts suited to their needs.
