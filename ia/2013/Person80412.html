Leslie Padilla's Experience

Principal

LPPR ~ Leslie Padilla Public Relations

April 2009 – Present (4 years 7 months)

Leslie currently leads Leslie Padilla Public Relations (LPPR) based in Malvern, Pennsylvania.

LPPR is a marketing communications boutique that develops and executes multi-tiered branding efforts for various consumer-focused clients.

LPPR builds strategic and poignant messaging along with persuasive communications and branding collateral.

LPPR showcases client missions via media relations, sponsorships, consumer events, trade shows, social responsibility campaigns, and advertising.

For LPPR clients, Leslie personally befriends and leverages key industry sources and media constituents to create mutually beneficial partnerships and added value campaigns for her clients.
