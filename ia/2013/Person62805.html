Jerry Wood's Summary

Provide complete consultancy service to SME’s and Corporates to manage all aspects of recruitment and staff development effectively.

Specialise in the building products market especially flooring & interior finishes.

12 years in recruitment provides me with detailed knowledge of best practice along with keeping abreast of changing opportunities through new technology.

I firmly believe that recruitment should be a straight forward process with transparency on all sides! Satisfied repeat clients indicate that this approach works for them.

Recruitment services are provided across the UK with scalable resources to handle major workloads as well as retaining personal accountability and quality control.

Clients served in all interiors markets (including flooring, textiles, ceilings, architectural ironmongery, furniture, laminates, doors, windows, etc.) as well as other specialist building products.

Strategic Business Partner for Profiles International - suppliers of state of the art Psychometric assessments - looking to support more clients both large and small to improve productivity, reduce recruitment risk, improve succession planning, reduce staff turnover and improve management performance.

Specialties: Search & selection for senior roles, management and sales.

Recruiter & business consultant

January 2000 – Present (13 years 8 months)

I help companies large & small manage their staffing and developmental needs. Consultancy services cover every stage of the recruitment process with a menu of options to support every scenario.

You may be a client who just wants advice on recruitment strategy and some interview training because you wish to source candidates yourself.

Alternatively you may wish a full retained executive search with everything done for you bar the shortlist interviews.

Or you may wish anything in between these two extremes.

I supply class leading online psychometric assessments to help select the right people, retain them longer and coach for enhanced performance.
