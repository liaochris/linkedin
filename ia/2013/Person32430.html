David Porter's Summary

In the year 2000, Living Assistance Services began with a wish: That people be able to grow older in their own homes with comfort and dignity.

After over a decade of caring we have stayed true to our vision. Our vision has been and will remain: to provide first rate home care with reliable, conscientious, knowledgeable and affordable Personal Support Workers to care recipients and families in need.

Living Assistance Services prides itself on being the first to provide highly personal care for those who wish to stay in their own homes or anywhere else assistance is required.

Our success is primarily due to our exacting standards when it comes to hiring caregivers. For every 100 who apply, only a small percentage are asked to join the company. This translates into professional, compassionate and intelligent caregivers who remain with Living Assistance Services for years.

It’s no wonder that over 90% of Living Assistance Services clients are through referrals from those who have already had a positive experience with the company.

Our work will be guided and informed by our beliefs and commitments to:

Building Relationships…
We respect people and families and are committed to excellence in serving the senior community.

Dignity…
We dedicate our efforts to ensuring the comfort, privacy and well-being of our clients and their families.

Quality…
We strive for excellence through continuous improvement.

Openness…
We are committed to a culture of team work, collaboration and communication.

Specialties

Living Assistance Services values building relationships with our care recipients and their families. We have offices serving Toronto, Mississauga, Oakville, Burlington, Willowdale, Thornhill, Richmond Hill, Markham and Vaughan. Our offices are open twenty-four hours each day.

David Porter's Experience

Owner

Living Assistance Services

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Hospital & Health Care industry

2000 – Present (13 years)

Our business is a in-home care agency, and we provide Personal Support Workers (PSWs) and Registered Nurses (RNs) to seniors and families in need.

Our PSWs assist clients with activities of daily living such as meal preparation, personal care, light housekeeping, laundry, errands/shopping, medication reminders, respite care, companionship, Alzheimer’s and end of life care. Our services are available part time/full time, live-in/live-out, at home, hospitals, nursing/retirement homes or wherever else care is required.

We are purposely not trying to be the ‘biggest’, just the best in level of service. We carefully hire the best staff (PSWs), for example, for every 1-2 hired, 150 caregivers have applied.

Our unique advantage is we encourage our clients to ‘Choose Your Caregiver’. We don’t simply send out the first PSW available. After a thorough assessment our service, coordinators match the best caregiver for each individual client’s needs.

Our Director of Care (RN) manages the care plan for the care recipients and to ensure seamless and timely caregiver services.
