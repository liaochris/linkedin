John Barry's Publications

  • The Resurrected Servant in Isaiah

    • Biblica/Paternoster
    • December 10, 2010
    Authors: John Barry

    Many have stood in awe of the prophecy about the servant in Isaiah 52:13–53:12 either because of its innovation in ancient literature or because of the parallels between the servant and the portrayal of Jesus of Nazareth in the Gospels.

    However, since the arguments of Harry M. Orlinsky and R. N. Whybray were presented over thirty years ago, there has been little examination of the servant’s possible resurrection in Isaiah 53:10–12. Even though their interpretations have been cited multiple times as disproving resurrection in this passage, participatory reference discourse analysis (a method that has been pioneered since their works were written) suggests otherwise.

  • Connect the Testaments: A Daily Devotional

    • Logos Bible Software
    • June 26, 2012
    Authors: John Barry, Rebecca Kruyswijk

    A daily devotional focused on biblical theology and life application--based on a custom reading plan.

  • Mary: Devoted to God's Plan

    • Logos Bible Software
    • November 30, 2012
    Authors: John Barry, Michael Grigoni, Miles Custis, Doug Mangum, Matthew M. Whitehead, Rebecca Brant, Elizabeth Vince

    An all-in-one church curriculum and book, designed for digital--with accompanying infographics and videos. Explores the journey of Mary the mother of Jesus as she moves from parent to disciple. The reader journeys with Mary, learning what it means to bear the image of God.

  • Codex Alexandrinus: A Transcription with Notes

    • BibleWorks
    • November 1, 2011
    Authors: John Barry, T. A. E. Brown, Kent Clarke

    A complete transcription of the New Testament of Codex Alexandrinus with tagged images and transcription notes (comparing the transcription to previous transcriptions and identifying possible transcription options). Coverage includes Protestant New Testament canonical text.

  • Faithlife Study Bible

    • Logos Bible Software
    • June 26, 2012
    Authors: John Barry, Michael Grigoni, Michael Heiser, Miles Custis, Doug Mangum, Matthew M. Whitehead

    The world's largest and most-advanced study Bible. (Served as general editor and co-author.)

  • Lexham Bible Dictionary

    • Logos Bible Software
    • June 26, 2012
    Authors: John Barry, Lazarus Wentz

    One of the first Bible dictionaries designed as a digital project--combines the best of biblical scholarship with regular updates and other digital-only features. (Served as editor and authored/co-authored articles.)

  • Jacob: Discerning God's Presence

    • Logos Bible Software
    • March 27, 2013
    Authors: John Barry, Derek R. Brown, Miles Custis, Doug Mangum, Wendy Widder

    A complete church curriculum and book, designed for digital, including infographics and videos. Explores the struggles of Jacob, the patriarch, as he wrestles with what God has called him to be. (Served as editor on this Studies in Faithful Living volume.)
