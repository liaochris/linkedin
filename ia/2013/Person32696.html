David van der Laan's Summary

My everyday work consists of managing and leading various marine related projects from conception to execution. The work scope could be a small part within a large project, complete turnkey operations or reviewing third party work for insurance purposes. Key aspects of most projects include project management, concept design and engineering, cost estimation, structural analysis, technical documentation, and technical drafting.

Other aspects include managing and leading projects that require field operations such as landing and securing heavy project cargoes at various ports all around the world.

Specialties

Structural and mechanical design and engineering utilizing AutoCAD, Rhino, Ansys as well as Stability, Ballasting, Motions and Longitudinal Strength calculations using GHS and Aqwa
