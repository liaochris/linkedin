Adrian Parker's Summary

I'm passionate about building software systems from conception to production. I'm a widely experienced generalist developer with a career focussed on full lifecycle design and development of complex business software systems. I'm skilled interfacing between business and IT and I understand both sides. I have a passion for startups and have been a technical founder twice, as well as working for several more.

My primary competencies are in Java J2EE often with Spring and/orJSP, many flavours of database, desktop, web and Adobe Flex / AIR user interfaces. I like to pick up new skills whenever possible and so I've got chops with HTML, CSS, Javascript, Node.js, jQuery, XML, XSD, ... and I've experience with too many servers, databases, development toolsets to usefully list.

My style is pragmatic, agile and my focus is on shipping viable code ASAP. I have a strong testing and continuous integration bias. I aim to build code quality instrumentation into the build chain.

I love leading the development team and I'm good at it. Working as a part of a highly skilled team also rings my bell especially where the opportunity to learn from colleagues is present. I have good experience managing offshore and junior developers.
