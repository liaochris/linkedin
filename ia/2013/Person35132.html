Diamond Newman's Summary

My name is Diamond Newman and I am a woman, nacho-enthusiast, creative developer, and conversationalist. I am also a new Library Associate with the fantastic DC Public Library(Dorothy I. Height/Benning Neighborhood Library)!

As I enjoy connecting people, ideas and experiences, my main goal as a Library Associate is to assist with information and technology access, curate cultural programming that will educate, unite, and engage while greatly helping to uphold a beneficial gathering space for those of the community.

Within my first month at DCPL, I have developed the "THINK TANK " program which is a monthly forum to discuss and explore art and contemporary topics. Whether it is a film viewing, an art/artist review, a panel discussion, or online content, THINK TANK will be a platform for creativity and conversation. This program is free and open to the public.

For my past work, feel free to take a look at my portfolio, Chasefranco.com. Updates on my new work will be coming soon.

My Best,
