Mary Goldsmith's Summary

As a Career strategist, consultant and coach, I believe that everyone deserves career fulfilment, and help professionals to navigate and build transformative careers.

Working with organisations and private coaching clients, I draw upon my corporate HR, executive education, career and leadership consulting expertise, to customise sessions for professionals who want to explore, develop or change their career.

Whether it be a new career direction, career advancement, or as a result of career transition or change, my goal is to stimulate, energise and empower talented people, as they negotiate the twists and turns of their careers.

Specialties:

♦ Career Development Coaching ♦ Career Mentoring ♦ Career Transition Consulting ♦ Leadership Development Coaching ♦ Workshop Facilitation ♦ MBTI ♦ Birkman ♦ 360 degree feedback ♦ Social Media & Careers
