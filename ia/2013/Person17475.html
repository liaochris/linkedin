Brian J. McCracken's Summary

I am a writer/editor/illustrator with over ten years of experience. I create deliverables such as user manuals, white papers, Web content, copywriting, online help, text and graphic conversion, technical illustration, Powerpoint presentations, a/v scripts, photography, voice-over narration, business plans, proposals, copywriting, Web content writing, business to business communication, press releases, speeches, hardware manuals, ISO 9001 process documentation, sales literature, video production, desktop publishing, multimedia scripting, and audio editing.

As the leader of a five person team I secured a multi-million dollar contract for the GOES satellite proposal for Space Systems/Loral.

As the leader of a world-wide team, I wrote the new business model for Sun Microsystems,

As a writer for a proposal for Lockheed, I succeeded in getting a multi-million dollar government contract for battlefield software.

As a Voice-over Actor, I am experienced at short and long-form narration, including Commercials, Audiobooks, Industrials, Video Games, E-learning, Documentaries, telephony, and animation.
