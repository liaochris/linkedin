Mark Venckeleer, LCSW's Summary

Mark Venckeleer, LCSW, Life Coach

Mark is a Licensed Clinical Social Worker with undergraduate and master’s degrees in social work. He has worked in the areas of mental health, advocacy, support, counseling, therapy and coaching – within a wide population including schools, prisons, the severely and chronically mentally ill, colleges, mental health clinics, abused children and their families and hospice. Through all of Mark’s formal training and personal and professional experience he found his true calling in life coaching. Realizing he had been coaching others for much of his life Mark was thrilled when he discovered he could coach at a professional level. Mark creates an open and safe space for clients to expand possibilities. He supports people in living their most authentic and fulfilled life, and ultimately in creating and living a life they love! Mark is a qualified speaker for the Inland Empire Speakers Bureau and a member of Toastmasters. He has a passion for public speaking, inspiring others to new heights with contagious energy. Mark speaks on various topics including mental health, motivation and passionate living. He also offers coaching to those seeking to increase their impact as speakers. Spirituality is a significant back drop from which Mark assists clients in achieving their desired goals. Influential people in his life include Wayne Dyer, Byron Katie, David Hawkins, Jesus and Gandhi.
