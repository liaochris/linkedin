Matthew Pixa's Summary

My Portfolio Guide, LLC is Registered Investment Advisory (RIA) firm serving investors nationwide. The firm has grown organically by serving high net worth individuals and business owners using a high-touch and personal approach. Leveraging some of the strongest names in the financial industry and numerous resources allow the firm to offer a truly unique and valuable experience to each client.

After a complimentary Portfolio Review and Analysis with specific recommendations, prospective investors quickly learn how different our approach is compared to the rest of the industry. We are not driven by sales quotas or commission goals; we are focused on taking pride in our fiduciary responsibility.

We actively look to lower expenses and investment fees and listen to what is important to each client and what makes them unique.

Matthew Pixa's Publications

  • Dear Mr. Market

    • Check out and sign up for our free Investment Blog
    Authors: Matthew Pixa, Matthew Blake

    This blog attempts to educate and entertain investors about the world of finance and the stock market. It’s brought to you in the form of letters written to the fictional character, “Mr. Market”.

    This character was created by Benjamin Graham who developed the Value based approach to investing in the 1930′s. In Graham’s book, “The Intelligent Investor”, the character of Mr. Market is always willing to either buy your stock position or sell you more; sometimes at what appears to be a reasonable price and then at other times at a flat out insulting one.

    Mr. Market can be very cruel and also quite rewarding to investors ; always playing with their emotions. He is volatile and very unpredictable.

    Regardless of how much you study him and try to figure him out, Mr. Market can simply humble even the most educated and experienced investor…
