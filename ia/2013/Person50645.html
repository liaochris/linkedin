Lead designer, architect, and developer of many Interlink products providing TCP/IP and DECnet connectivity solutions for MVS and VM mainframe systems. Company grew to $28.4 million in revenue and 180 employees by 1991 and went public (NASDAQ: INLK).

Led the design and development of the following products:
• DATAstore: High-speed transparent MVS tape & storage access from VAX/VMS.

• 3711: Front-end controller offloading layer 1-3 DECnet protocol processing.
Supported file transfer/access, terminal emulation, APIs, DECnet/SNA & NJE/mail gateways.
Developed in cooperation with Dupont and IBM.
