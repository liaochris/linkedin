Linette Montae, PhD's Summary

CURRENTLY CONSIDERING INVITATIONS FOR THE STAGE, WEBINARS, GUEST POSTS, TELESIMINARS, AND SUMMITS.

On stage, online and in her community, you can find Dr. Linette Montae - internationally acclaimed speaker, author and award winning results strategist - helping speakers and coaches “leverage your platform, master your process and ignite your profits”.

Dr. Montae has owned several businesses over the years but when she jumped into being a business owner in 2010, she had no website, no email, no social media accounts, no autoresponder, and NO CLUE! During her 18 months of being stuck in start-up with a bank account in the negative and working 80 hours a week IN her business, Dr. Montae experienced some hard but powerful lessons that helped her magnify her specialties.
