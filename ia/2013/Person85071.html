Marci Ikeler's Summary

I’m Marci Ikeler (the last name’s pronounced just how it looks: “ike” + “ler”) and I am a strategic digital experience designer. As the co-founder of Little Arrows, I seek to combine my passion for detailed insights into consumer behaviors with effective platform design.

For over a decade, I have helped advertising agencies, Fortune 500 brands, and startups to communicate online through engaging, effective, and elegant digital experiences. Over the course of my career, I've worked with brands such as Ally, Armani, Citigroup, DiscoverCard, Dove Chocolate, Foot Locker, Google, Johnson & Johnson, net-a-porter, Paddle8, Proctor & Gamble, Sopexa, and Stella McCartney.

I live in Brooklyn with my husband, Aaron, and my two dogs. When I’m not at my computer (which is rare), I can be found playing video games, reading, and enjoying New York City.

Specialties: digital strategy, creative technology, strategy, planning, digital planning, user experience design, ux design, interaction design, information architecture, strategy, planning, advertising project management, business analysis, web design, user-centered design, usability, hci, web developent, technology, technical writing, business, html, xml, xslt, javascript, php, sql
