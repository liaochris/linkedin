Michael Garabedian's Summary

We are a full service building company offering our services in the following divisions;

Luxury Homes & Estates

Our proven team works with families to create luxury and estate homes from 4,000 sqft to 20,000 sqft multi acre estates. All of our luxury residential projects are one of a kind and designed from scratch. Unique architectural elements include; basements (yes in Texas we do basements), climate controlled wine rooms, media distribution systems and high performance energy efficient designs and elements.

Renovations

We offer renovations services throughout the DFW area. Including home updates, project completion (finishing projects left unfinished by others), storm damage recovery and roof replacement/upgrades.

We also offer home maintenance and insurance repair services in many of our local areas.


Commercial Contracting Services

Construction services for nonresidential and investment property include; general contracting on garden/low rise projects, tenant improvements and site development.


Professional Designations

Our professional designations include; Graduate Master Builder, GMB, Texas Star Builder, TSB, Certified Green Professional, CGP, Green Built Texas, GBT, Certified Master Builder, CMB, Realtor, National Association Of Home Builders, NAHB, Greater Fort Worth Builders Association, and The Builder 20 Club.

Project History

Our professional history includes over $100 million in completed projects ranging in size from 2,000 sqft to over 20,000 sqft. Complexity includes water features, smart home designs, climate controlled zones, high performance energy efficiency, basements and other such unique architectural elements.

Specialties

Unique architecture, Green Building, High Performance Homes, Energy Efficient Designs, Basements, Climate Controlled Wine Cellars/Rooms, Smart Homes, Estate Homes, Luxury Homes, Custom Homes, Signature Homes, Site Development, Investment Joint Ventures
