Dino Kasvikis' Summary

I am a passionate, dependable and ambitious industrial designer. I have full product development experience from initial research and ideation through final production. My journey into the design field helps fuel my enthusiasm to help other aspiring designers fulfill their dreams.

Dino Kasvikis' Experience

Industrial Designer | Human Factors and Industrial Design Group

Ximedica

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Medical Devices industry

February 2011 – Present (2 years 8 months) Providence, Rhode Island Area

As an Industrial Designer in Ximedica’s Human Factor Industrial Design group, I work to create innovative, user-driven products in the medical and consumer areas from initial user research through detailed design and into full-scale production. My unique background as a trained mechanical engineer allows me to bridge the gap between engineering and design – ensuring all designs are solidified in sound engineering foundation, yet pushing the boundaries of conventional design ideation.

Having experience taking products through the complete design cycle has given me a unique perspective to always design with the end goal in mind. That mindset, combined with evidence based user-centric research, ensure product solutions I create hit the mark of all stakeholders involved. It my desire to improve these stakeholders’ lives that continues to propel my work efforts as a designer.
