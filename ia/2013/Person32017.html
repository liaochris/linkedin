David Gacsko | Creative Director's Summary

A 17+ year specialist of multi-disciplinary, 360º brand experiences. Practical experience in a variety of environments has provided well-rounded accomplishments in the areas of digital media, art direction, direct mail, POP, photo art direction, social media, outdoor advertising, web design and brand development. Also, fully versed on both fast-paced agency and large corporate client sides of the business.

My solid leadership of teams has resulted in creative that has been buss-worthy and showcased full thought-out concepts with specialized touch-points across multi-channel experiences. This success has been achievable via my commitment to strategic team-builiding and never holding back team experiences. I believe wholeheartedly in fostering creative growth. Collaboration is also key—everyone has a voice and the opportunity to contribute. Maintaining this leadership model ensures openness between all parties and departments.
