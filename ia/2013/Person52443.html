Power to Wellness
Your Source to Energy, Health and Fitness
Our goal is to enrich people's lives through health and fitness making it convenient, enjoyable and rewarding.

Power to Wellness teaches classes at different corporations. The very well qualified instructors and myself teach all different formats.

Your corporation may be interested in doing either Yoga, Pilates, Zumba or some other format. I have attached the 'Menu of Classes' that is offered by Power to Wellness. Many of the classes don't even require equipment - just a room. Below are the details.

The Details: You get experienced teachers for on-site corporate classes which can be tailored to meet your needs. They may be held in the morning, before work, at lunch time, or in the early evening, after work, at the company’s request.

Classes are offered in a course form of 4 to 8 or 12-week sessions (or longer) duration which can be held once or twice a week. The fees are $120 for the teacher to come out.

Class size is limited only by the amount of space we have to work with. The class length will usually run 60 minutes (lunch time classes could be done in 50 minutes, if necessary).

Your company will need to provide a space, a conference or meeting room or a gym, as long as tables and chairs can be pushed aside for class.

Companies have chosen one of three options in hosting exercise classes:

1) Full subsidy: Employer offers the classes free of charge to the employee.
2) Partial Subsidy: Class fee is shared by employee and employer.
3) Individual pay: Employees individually pay for classes, teacher collects all fees.
