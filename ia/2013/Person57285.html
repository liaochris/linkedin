James Boo's Projects

  • 1 Minute Meal

    • May 2013 to Present
    Team Members: James Boo

    New York City is home to glamorous restaurants, exalted chefs, and a "dining scene" big enough to support an industry of media and public relations. But behind this scene is a world of food that is more culture than concept.

    1 Minute Meal is a documentary video series dedicated to that world, capturing the cuisines, cultures, people, places, and stories that connect food to life.

  • Sugar Shack: A Documentary

    • May 2012 to June 2012
    Team Members: James Boo, Ian Parker, Justin Bland, Angel Perez

    WINNER - Audience Choice Award, NYC Food Film Festival
