Mark Blane's Summary

San Diego California Personal Injury & Accident Attorney, and Best Selling legal author, Mark C. Blane, has specialized in Personal Injury law since 1999. His recent book, "A Wolf in Sheep's Clothing" recently hit the Amazon.com Best Sellers list in the insurance category, and it cracked the Top 50 (#42) in the personal finance category. As a result, he recently became a member in the National Academy of Best-Selling Authors.

He also specializes in injured children in San Diego and throughout California. He recently wrote his fourth book, "Justice for the Injured Child," which has become a California parent's legal survival guide for their child's injury case. He has since followed up with five more publications bringing his legal help books/publications to a total of nine!

He offers FREE books to anyone who is resident of California or who has been injured in a California accident BEFORE they speak to any Lawyer, Claims Adjuster, or sign any legal forms. He strives for excellence on every case and has many satisfied clients through the years. He is available 24/7 at (619) 813-7955. You can check out his website at http://www.blanelaw.com.

  • Justice for the Injured Child: A Parent's Complete Legal Survival Guide For Their Child's California Injury Case

    • Lulu Publications
    • February 2, 2011
    Authors: Mark Blane

    This book has been my most ambitious undertaking in terms of time, research and organization. I noticed there were no legal guides or books devoted exclusively to the injured child in a California accident. None of the legal challenges that comes with a child injury case were written about in a comprehensive and easy to understand format. None of the big publishers had anything on the area of law, much less for consumers, who really need it most. Thus, I decided to be the one to write a book on the subject. I spent months researching and documenting over 51 relevant California court cases, numerous California statutory codes, including relevant Rules of Court across California to compile this detailed book devoted entirely to child accidents, their injuries, and the legal process in California. It is written for the “every day” consumer, parents, teachers, students, who want the law and concepts in simple to understand terms. In fact, this book could even be a great resource for other lawyers who practice in California child injury law, but I did my best to keep out the “legalese wording” as best I can.
