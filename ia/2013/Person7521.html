Andy Olewinski's Summary

I’m happily married. I consider myself a very friendly, and very passionate about helping people commit themselves to greatness! I help people take back their lives through Mindset development, health, fitness, and financial transformations.

For years I struggled with my own personal goals. I wasn't sure what I wanted to do and when I did know what I wanted to do, I had no clue how to do it. I have since found my mentors. I now want to extend my hand and help other people reach their goals. I'm always looking for people like you who are serious about improving their lives and having lots of fun!

I am extremely thankful for all of the loving support I have in my life and look forward to paying it forward.
