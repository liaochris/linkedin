Elaine Morris' Summary

Elaine Morris
elaine@elainemorris.com
214-244-2592
www.elainemorris.com

Elaine Morris is a master level executive coach who brings more than twenty-five years’ experience to upper level executives and their teams. By building trust and rapport, Elaine takes clients beyond surface-level conversations to access deeper growth and personal awareness. She shows clients how to connect authentically, inspiring team insights and decisions that produce winning strategies. Through her expertise managers are transformed to leaders and groups to accountable high-performing teams.

Having worked with emerging start-ups to Fortune 500 companies, Elaine’s experience spans a wide variety of industries. These include: commercial real estate, construction, technology, engineering, retail, oil and gas, health care, financial services, hospitality, direct sales, government, legal services, private education and non-profits.

Elaine is certified as an expert in Emotional Intelligence (EQ), Strategy and Team Facilitation and she utilizes a number of leading tools that build awareness and growth to better meet organizational objectives. Her process assesses needs, targets goals and maps progress to expedite performance breakthroughs. Her success is measured by behavioral changes as well as tangible business metrics indicating financial growth, heightened communication skills and employee engagement.

Specialties: Executive Coaching, Leadership Development, Strategic Planning, Strategy Design, Strategy Implementation, Productivity Improvement, Cultural Transformatioin, Team Diagnosis, Team Development, Team Performance, Organizational Change, Organizational Effectiveness, Management Consulting, Performance Improvement, Program Design, Program Improvement, Emotional Intelligence Workshops, EQ expert, Retreat Facilitation, Speaker
