Frank Jossi's Summary

An award winning independent writer, editor and content provider who has had a variety of clients in government and the media. My work includes articles, case studies, essays, web sites, white papers, scripts and books.

My goal is work with interesting clients who need clear, concise, readable content.

Specialties

Writing articles, white papers, case studies, executive speeches and marketing materials; editing documents and web sites. Consulting on media plans, especially in health care.
