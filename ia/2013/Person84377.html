Gary Polakovic's Summary

The environment has fascinated me since I was a Cub Scout, hunting and fishing with dad and studying environmental science in college. A high school counselor talked me out of being a park ranger but a stodgy English professor persuaded me to pursue journalism. I scoffed, yet two years later I became editor of the student newspaper and fused passion for public policy, science and communications to pioneer modern environmental journalism in California.It was a great run that took me around the world, to the front page, to Stanford and a share of the Pulitzer Prize. When the ride came to an end 20 years later, I put my expertise to work for clients striving to transform the planet and Make Over Earth Inc.was born. We develop creative and pragmatic solutions to environmental challenges based on real-world experience and platinum credential.

Specialties

Make Over Earth Inc. specializes in integrating environmental policy, science and communications into actionable strategy and powerful messages. We can help you to:

- Articulate your vision
- Get past groupthink
- Expand your publics and increase your profile
- Identify challenges, set priorities and develop creative responses
- Move an agenda
- Connect dots and constituencies across all environmental issues
- Tell your story

Please visit www.MakeOverEarth.com for details.
