Dave Butcher's Summary

Very experienced in the hills and mountains of the UK, Europe and USA; hiking, skiing, snowshoeing, mountaineering. Extensive photo library with tens of thousands of images, also covering towns and cities across Europe, America, Hong Kong, Sydney and New Zealand.

Medium format film cameras are used exclusively for high quality photography, even under difficult conditions.

Looking for image licensing opportunities. My work is already sold around the world as posters, calendars and greetings cards as well as for company promotion. Images readily enlarge to 1 metre at printing resolution.

Exhibition support from, and projects to work with, companies who can relate to my style of photography (travel, hills, mountains, outdoors, city life, etc).

Any opportunities to promote my reputation as a leading black and white photographer in my field.

Specialties

Landscape and city photography in black and white, lecturer, course tutor, expert darkroom printer, Ilford Master printer, experienced project manager.

Dave Butcher's Publications

  • Lake Light - The Lake District Photography of Dave Butcher

    • Dave Butcher Photography; ISBN 9780955562716
    • March 7, 2010
    Authors: Dave Butcher

    Lake Light is Dave Butcher's unique view of the Lake District captured over 25 years of visits to the region. Dave's love for hill and mountain walking gives a different perspective on this ever popular area of Britain and leads to views of landscapes which might not always have been obvious to the casual tourist. Dramatic mountains, dynamic waterfalls and sweeping lakes are all here reflecting the seasons and extremes of weather that have shaped them.

  • Peak Light - The Peak District Photography of Dave Butcher

    • Dave Butcher Photography; ISBN 9780955562709
    • May 1, 2007
    Authors: Dave Butcher

    Peak Light presents Dave Butcher's stunning monochrome landscape images of England's Peak District - the area where he has lived and worked since 1988. He presents us with a photographic tour from the limestone dales in the south to the peat bogs of the northern Dark Peak area, sharing locational details to help readers follow in his footsteps. His images range from dramatic vistas to waterfalls, woodlands and rock formations. Several sets of images illustrate the same landscape in different seasons and weather conditions.

  • High Light - The Landscape Photography of Dave Butcher

    • Arem Publishing, reprinted 2007 by Dave Butcher Photography; ISBN 1904825036
    • February 20, 2005
    Authors: Dave Butcher

    High Light presents Dave Butcher's stunning monochrome landscape images of mountains and high places. Dave combines his twin passions of photography and hill and mountain walking to produce dramatic landscape images. The four portfolios included in this book cover his photography over the past 20 years in Europe and America as well as England, Wales and Scotland. Dramatic lighting plays a major part in adding to the scenic beauty of Dave's landscapes and he contributes a shor t essay providing tips on meeting the techincal challenges.
