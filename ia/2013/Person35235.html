Diane Court's Summary

Diane focuses on business coaching and strategic consulting to lead creative and operational teams to produce business visioning, brand identity, customer communications and e-learning programs. She provides integrated organizational communications strategy, campaign planning and tactical implementation.

Since the 1980’s she has collaborated with non-profit, corporate and government clients as diverse as FEMA, the US Department of Agriculture, The Miss America Foundation to the Florida micro-finance startup, Epic Change. Among her corporate projects are media, event and strategic planning projects for The Hartford, Federal Express, Bristol Myers Squibb and Dale Carnegie and Associates.


My enthusiasm for what I do derives from its purpose: engage the needs and expectations of clients with the interests and values of their customers, pinpointing their intersection where the conversations at the heart of powerful brand experiences begin.
