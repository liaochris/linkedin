Armon Moore's Summary

Armon Moore - n.
1) A creative mind known for visual innovation and gracefully merging form and functionality.

2) A storyteller whose use of breakthrough design engenders an almost magical meta-moore-phasis, compelling the viewer to experience life differently.

I am a new-generation creative. I am passionate about technology, culture, and everything that surrounds it. I strive to capture the emotion and joy which inherently exists in any situation. As a visual storyteller my goal is simple, to create and produce works that transfix and transform the viewer.


Ideation + Innovation
I combine innovative thinking and out-of-the-box creativity to reframe brand-to-consumer dialogue, while creating new business and sustainable revenue streams. I am passionate about pushing the boundaries of how people engage with media and access information across multiple platforms.

Art Direction + Design
I am adept in conceptualizing and designing engaging user experiences across various media platforms including Mobile, Web, and OOH. I am able to strategically direct and lead designers, motion designers, developers, illustrators and animators with a clear vision of the client's end goal.

Communication
Invoking verbal persuasion to elevate visual storytelling, I employ confident and compelling communication skills when presenting to clients, pitching new business and leading creative teams.

Specialties

My toolbox includes After Effects, Photoshop, Illustrator, Flash, InDesign, Dreamweaver, Final Cut Pro, Design, Animation, Layout, Typography and a whole lot of ambition with equal parts of confidence and humility.
