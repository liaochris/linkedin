Anna Kleeberg's Summary

"Stand Out from the Crowd"

Since 1998 Anna Kleeberg has continued to assist clients with developing visibility for their products and services in the market place.

The founder, Anna Kleeberg, brings her vast knowledge and twenty-five years of experience working with a wide range of businesses varying in size and industry, including Fortune 500 companies such as Kraft Foodservice and Northwestern Mutual Financial Network to small and mid-size organizations.

Anna credits her company’s continued success to four main ingredients that are applied to every project: experience, creativity, dedication and cooperative teamwork; producing a winning combination.

AKG utilizes traditional and state of the art technology to effectively assist clients in reaching their targeted audience. Programs are customized to meet individual company needs and can include online branding, profile development and (ORM) online reputation management, advertising (internet, social media, newspaper, and radio), workshops, seminars and trade shows. In addition, we provide effective coaching and training to help clients and their employees manage their programs successfully.

To learn more about how we can assist you in achieving your individual and company goals please contact: annakleeberg@annakleeberg.com.

Specialties: Branding, ORM online reputation management, marketing, social media.
