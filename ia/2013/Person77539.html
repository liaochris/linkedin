Kurt Nielsen's Summary

I have been owner and operator of Kurt Nielsen Photography since 1997 and recently started Kurt's Kustom Keepsakes, a green screen division. I am fully trained in all aspects of film and digital photography from the start to the finished product. I am and have been the official portrait and promotional photographer for many local organizations, businesses and charities. I have also photographed many events for local news organizations.

Specialties

I specialize in Engagement, Wedding, Maternity and Baby, Children and Family, High School Seniors, Event and Special Occasion photography.
