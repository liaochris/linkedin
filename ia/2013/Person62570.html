Jeroen Berndsen's Summary

Jeroen Berndsen is software architect and co-owner of Flatstones. Having both a higher profession degree (HBO) and a university degree, he combines both practical and theorethical knowledge in the design and implementation of complex software architectures.

Specialties: Web application development, software quality, requirements analysis, system modelling and software testing techniques.
