Tom Jacobs' Summary

Chicago based Jacobs Agency helps clients untangle their business problems. While their industries vary, the reason clients come to us is the same: they need sharp thinking, superior, relevant creative and the ability to reach targets on multiple levels. Today’s communications landscape is complex, with many influencers and even more vehicles all vying for the target’s attention. With Through the LinesSM engagement, our proprietary philosophy and marketing model, we understand and can expertly navigate the landscape’s intricacies. How? We start with an Objectives-Based Approach. Marketing should never be just about tactics. By focusing on a client’s marketing objectives first, we act strategically and get their message through the communications-saturated environment, and directly to their specific target.

We are proud that our work has earned many industry awards and that we’ve been recognized on both local and national levels. But we are more proud of the successes the work delivers for our clients. We think that is the highest honor of all.

From Targeted Awareness to Audience Engagement; from Lead Generation & Prospect Nurturing to Sales Enablement and Brand Refinement, we keep a client’s objectives as our guide and produce breakthrough results.
