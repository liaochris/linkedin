Jerry Masin's Summary

When it comes to building individual and organizational capacity and capabilities, Edison got it right. Vision without execution is hallucination. I strongly believe that vision, in business and professional settings, is informed by the demonstration of character, competence and a bias for execution.

I am a senior business executive and trusted partner to leadership teams with proven ability in leading startups, early stage companies and turnarounds towards sustainable growth, increased market share and profitability.

As a strategic decision maker, I have built and grown agile, profitable organizations through energetic and entrepreneurial leadership. I have strong general management and profit and loss expertise across multiple industries including education, professional services, healthcare, retail, technology, and financial services.

Specialties: I build, enhance, sustain and turnaround organizations.
