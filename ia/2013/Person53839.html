Harry Raymond's Summary

Soon after my eighth birthday, I started my first business, selling erasers, pencils and Christmas ornaments. "The Friends Catalog" turned a profit and I spent the money on a much-coveted Nintendo 64. From that glorious day, I approached just about everything with a passionate entrepreneurial spirit.

I have worked on media and community growth projects for Major League Baseball, Fox Business, American Red Cross and a bunch of awesome tech startups.

Hustling to build a company and change the world of drinking. Check out our latest version of Shindig in the Apple App store.

Passionate about mobile, New York tech and community.
