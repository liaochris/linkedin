David Ginsburg's Summary

I am Social Media Marketing Specialist, skilled and deeply experienced with the use of social channels for business in order to drive increased consumer engagement, brand awareness, and sales.

The majority of my career has been spent in media. As social channels such as Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Pinterest and Instagram have emerged as primary communication channels and key marketing platforms, I provide strategic, tactical help to businesses seeking an effective, business-results focused presence within the platforms most applicable to their specific goals.

I provide social media marketing training and coaching with clients both corporate and non-profit, providing in-depth strategic training with social channels including Facebook, Twitter, YouTube, LinkedIn, Pinterest, Google+, Instagram and Foursquare. Given the rapid evolution of the social space, in particular the shift to mobile social consumption, presenting new and emerging platforms to achieve client goals is an ongoing service.

I am highly skilled in the development of robust Facebook Brand Pages, Twitter profiles, Pinterest business profiles, LinkedIn business and personal profiles, Instagram accounts, G+ business pages; content development and sourcing, and I have tremendous community management experience in these channels.

Community management is a key offering. Maintaining a day-to-day dialog with active communities is a unique challenge I meet with a great deal of creative thinking (and efficient content sourcing), which is then applied to each of our client's projects.
We are proud to serve as a partner agency with shops including CM Communications, TRP/Campus Entertainment, Bennett Group, and Mediastruction.
Past and present BrenBurg Media clients include: American Repertory Theater, UNO Chicago Grill, National Amusements, Ocean State Job Lot, Mountain View Grand Resort & Hotel, Prime Motor Group, Connoisseurs Inc., Topnotch Resort & Spa, Deer Mountain Day Camp and Phillips Candy House.

Specialties

Creative ideation, from development/implementation through execution; strategic social media media marketing initiatives; social media training; creative content development for new media; community management; brand building; management experience; and a unique ability to connect disparate elements into a cohesive whole by way of viewing marketing opportunities from multiple micro and macro perspectives. I have high creative facilities and strong interpersonal communication skills as well.
