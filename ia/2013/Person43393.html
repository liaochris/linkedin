Evan Gregg's Summary

I have recently begun a new venture. I plan to take what I have learned from my work with The Location Reservoir and apply it to creating tools for Western Massachusetts businesses and organizations to expand their online presence.

The Location Reservoir will remain the only locally run guide to event venues and filming locations in Western Massachusetts. The only change is that rather than being my primary concern it will become just one of the many tools I use to promote Western Massachusetts.

I am still getting things going but stand by for plenty more from Mass Routes Media.
