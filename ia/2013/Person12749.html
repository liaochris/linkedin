Paradigm Group specializes in direct equity and debt investments in income producing Real Estate, with a focus on multifamily properties, within a 250 mile radius of metro NY. We pursue a disciplined investment approach identifying attractive opportunities with significant upside potential and controllable downside protection, committing only to investments that offer clear, realistic exit strategies primarily through refinancing and sales. We believe that a disciplined approach at the time of purchase enables it to better assess exit alternatives as they arise.

As a result of our comprehensive real estate expertise and access to capital, Paradigm Group is able to move quickly by recognizing value and closing the deal.
