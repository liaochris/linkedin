John Robinson's Summary

Hello, I am John Robinson, Hilton Head Island REALTOR with Dunes Marketing Group, and I have lived on Hilton Head Island, South Carolina since 1990. I have been a real estate specialist on Hilton Head Island since 2004.

Originally from Maryland, I attended college in South Carolina on tennis scholarship and, upon graduation, immediately relocated to Hilton Head Island as I thoroughly enjoy the Island’s southern lifestyle and climate.

Hilton Head Island real estate specialist focusing on residential new single family homes, condos and re-sale properties, I am a well educated Hilton Head Island REALTOR with each existing and future planned community in Beaufort County.

Before joining the Hilton Head Island real estate world, I was a tennis professional for over 20 years at various tennis facilities around the United States including the John Newcombe’s Tennis Ranch in Texas, the John Gardiner’s Tennis Ranch in California, Columbia Country Club in the Washington, DC area, the Sea Pines Racquet Club and the Van der Meer TennisUniversity on Hilton Head Island, South Carolina, and the Athletic Club of Weston in South Florida.

I have been a United States Professional Tennis Association (USPTA) Professional 1 member since 1988 and a Professional Tennis Registry (PTR) National Tester/National Clinician since 1993, and was voted PTR’s “National Tester of the Year” in 1998.

In addition to being a Hilton Head Island REALTOR, I am associated with Lowcountry Sports Network where I conduct tennis classes, workshops, tournaments, and corporate functions in the Hilton Head Island, South Carolina area as well as traveling tennis events.
