John Brennan's Summary

John worked as IT Infrastructure Project Manager with IBM Global Services on the QBE Europe account in London where he delivered Video Conferencing and SAN Upgarde projects.
John had finished working as Technology Integration Project Manager for European Disaster Recovery Self Recovery Infrastructure Project with Canada Life. The project in Canada Life was to implement a Dual Data Centre Disaster Recovery Infrastructure for Ireland, Europe and UK critical systems.

John worked as Infrastructure Project Manager on the Billing Replacement Project with Meteor Mobile Communications from October '07 to August '09. He also managed the fit-out and implementation of the new Data Centre Project for Meteor at the same time.

John worked as Project Manager on the IT Disaster Recovery project with Irish Life & Permanent from March '05 to October '07. The project implemented a Dual Data Centre Disaster Recovery strategy for key business systems including EMC SAN, IBM Mainframe, HP-UX, IBM AIX Servers plus Windows Server 2003. Applications included Oracle, Siebel, SQL & Exchange email for over 5,500 clients as well as Internet Banking and eCommerce websites.

In C&C John was responsible for bringing consistent standards into the Network Infrastructure of all the companies within C&C Group. John had responsibility for and managed the IT Infrastructure & Support Services across Ireland (LAN & WAN, Voice & Data). He also managed and developed the partner relationship with main suppliers and ensured that the SLA’s were being met through regular communication and meetings.

Over a period of 23 years with IBM Ireland, John worked on IT projects with companies including ABB; Johnson & Johnson; BoI Finance; AIB Bank; Rhone-Poulenc Rorer; Antigen Pharmaceuticals; Klopman (Tralee); Batchelors; Church & General Insurance and EBS (Bank Branch Roll-out). His principal role was designing and delivering IT Infrastructure & Networking solutions that added value to the business.

Specialties

Project Management in IT Infrastructure on both large scale projects and small installations with particular focus on Service Delivery, Business Continuity & Disaster Recovery; Anti-Virus and Security Patch management; Server Migrations and Upgrades.
