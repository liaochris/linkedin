Giacomo Sinisgalli, CREA's Experience

President/CEO

New York International Real Estate Corp.

March 1994 – Present (19 years 7 months) Albany, New York Area

New York International Real Estate Corp. (NYIREC) is a New York State licensed real estate brokerage firm that specializes in real estate auctions and asset management of select properties throughout New York State.

NYIREC has been assisting real estate investors with the acquisition, sale and management of income producing investment properties since 1994.

Our sales and management portfolio has exceeded $100 million dollars in assets with over 2500 units managed and leased.

We are also proud to announce that we have listed, sold and managed some of the largest apartment buildings in Upstate New York.

NYIREC’s principal broker and president, Giacomo Sinisgalli holds the CREA® - Certified Real Estate Auctioneer designation and combines over 20 years of real estate experience with an innovative auction marketing program coupled with today’s fast changing Internet technologies.

Our clients agree that our professionalism and integrity in handling their real estate affairs set us apart from the rest. Whether the subject property is the sale or management of a distressed asset, luxury home, or high-rise apartment building, New York International Real Estate Corp. has the experience and expertise to get the job done.

Sellers are assured of total commitment, dedicated service, personal attention, professional representation, and a successful venture.

Purchasers are assured of quality offerings, an expedient sales process, detailed property information, and an exceptional support network.

As a professional real estate auction marketing and asset management consulting firm NYIREC provides a unique and innovative approach to each and every project we handle which ensures successful and lucrative results for both buyers and sellers alike.

Giacomo Sinisgalli, CREA's Summary

Giacomo Sinisgalli, CREA, is the president and principal broker of New York International Real Estate Corp., a licensed real estate brokerage firm that specializes in selling real estate at auction.

Giacomo has over twenty-one years of experience as a real estate broker and has sold millions of dollars worth of real estate using the auction method of marketing, including some of the largest apartment buildings in upstate New York.

Giacomo is a New York state-approved real estate instructor with more than fifteen years’ experience developing and delivering training programs for real estate brokers and agents on a number of real estate related topics, including how to sell real estate at auction.

Giacomo is also the founder of RealtyAuctionSchool.com, which is America’s first online school dedicated exclusively to the study of the real estate auction industry.
