Kelvin Ringold's Summary

Positive, Upbeat, Motivator with a winning attitude! Awesome people skills, Excellent at helping people see the best in themselves, feel valued and appreciated and bring their best effort to the forefront.

20 years United States Air Force, supervising up to 40 people performing complex electronic communications installs on a worldwide basis. 16 years as administrative assistant at Upstate Medical University Hospital and was known for my interpersonal skills and upbeat attitude and people management abilities. 24 years as a professional photographer, bringing out the best in people photographically and often transforming their views of themselves by our interactions and resulting images. Past President (2008-2009) of the Professional Photographers' Society of New York state, leading a membership organization of over 400 professional photographers

GOALS: As a Life Coach and motivational speaker and writer, I endeavor to uplift individuals, and help them feel good about them themselves, to be confident, empowered and forward looking in order to realize their full potential by mastering their mindset and therefore their lives.

Specialties: Mindset Management; Motivation and Life Coaching, inspirational writing and speaking. Certified Professional Photographer (portrait and wedding), and "getting the job done."
