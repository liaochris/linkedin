Bill Eddy's Summary

The EDDY Company, LLC, performs a broad spectrum of commercial real estate development/marketing and leasing services. My 35 year professional experience covers a broad range of retail and commercial developments throughout California, Arizona and Hawaii. My management experience includes everything from primary market research through product and concept development including site and lease planning, merchandise mix, projected minimum rents, sales and lease negotiations. We have worked with major national and regional developers, retailers and restaurants.

A partial development client list include:

Bishop Estate/Kamehameha Schools, Honolulu, HI
Craig Realty Group, Newport Beach, CA.
The Irvine Company, Irvine, CA.
The Sammis Company, Irvine, CA.
Haseko-Hawaii, Honolulu, HI.
The MacNaughton Group, Honolulu, HI.

A partial list of retail clients include:

Bobby Jones Golf, Ashworth Golf, D & G, Just Cavalli, Helly-Hansen, New Balance, Pacific Sunwear, Pioneer Electronics, Benetton, Rubys Diner, Hamburger Hamlet, Sanrio, Draper's & Damon.

Specialties: Market analyses - Site/Lease & Merchandise mix planning in concert with the project Architect - Lease Negotiations.
We know and love retail !!
