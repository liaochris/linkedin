Matt Coletta's Summary

Matt is the Managing Partner of Business Team, Business Sales & Acquisitions, the Largest Business Brokerage Firm in the Western US since 1981 with 13 offices and over 130 associates. With over 20 years of experience in the Business Brokerage Industry, Matt has earned the confidence and respect of both Sellers & Buyers he has represented as well as his fellow Brokers.

During this time, Matt has represented numerous Sellers & Buyers in various industries including Manufacturing, Distribution, Service, Health Care, Construction, Automotive, Technology and Food Related Businesses.

An active member of the California Association of Business Brokers and the International Business Brokers Association. Matt's credentials include a Bachelor of Science Degree in Business Finance & a Professional Designation Degree in Real Estate Development from SDSU & UCLA. Additionally, Matt has earned the designation of Certified Business Intermediary (CBI) from the International Business Brokers Association which he has been an active member of for over 15 years. The CBI designation is granted to a select group of individuals who are seasoned in the industry, completed course work including financial analysis, pricing, legal and tax implications related to the sale or purchase of a business plus continuing education. Matt has also earned the designation of CBB, Certified Business Broker from the California Assoc. of Business Brokers. Matt is also active with improving the standards within the Business Brokerage Industry. He is an active Committee Member and a 2013 Board of Directors Member of the California Association of Business Brokers.

Matt is consistently one of the “Top Producers” in the firm and is considered one of the most “Active Deal Makers” in the industry. Matt knows that selling or buying a business can be a complex, intimidating and time consuming process. He is committed to making this process a smooth, fair and efficient experience for all parties involved.
