Winterwoods Homes, Ltd. * Architects * Land Planning * Interior Design
A luxury resort architecture and real estate investment development firm located in Wintergreen Resort, Virginia

Winterwoods Homes provides today’s customer with the ability to combine the entire architecture, land planning, and interior design experience under one roof, creating a true one-stop experience. Winterwoods Homes provides their customers with:

* Custom architecture and design
* Professional interior design services
* Materials and interior finishes selection
* Master site development and land planning
* Architecture oversight and administration of construction and building services nationwide in the United States and Europe as well as the design and manufacturing of timber frame and log systems.

Winterwoods Homes, Ltd. offers a revenue producing, architectural design, build, real estate investment program that sets the company apart from what any other would be competitor can offer. The “Best-of-the Best” partner program and business model was created by Winterwoods Homes owner and founder, Jeffery W. Caudill. Mr. Caudill has spent more than 20 years of his career previous to the creation of Winterwoods Homes in the investment advisory and financial services industry.

Owner and President

Winterwoods Homes Foundation, LLC

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Architecture & Planning industry

March 2011 – Present (2 years 10 months) Wintergreen Resort, VA

Winterwoods Homes, Ltd., Mr. Caudill's Luxury resort architecture and real estate investment development firm located in Wintergreen Resort, Virginia, is differentiated by The Winterwoods Homes Foundation, a private foundation created and operated by Mr. Caudill to help fund cause-based initiatives in the United States. In an effort to assist non-profit organizations (501(c)3 classified) in their ongoing efforts to improve the lives of individuals and the communities they serve, The Winterwoods Homes Foundation, guided by Caudill and his unique wealth management and real estate investment planning strategies, donates 5% of the sale price of their corporately owned investment development properties to select non-profit organizations - an amount that provides hundreds of thousand of dollars to worthy causes.
