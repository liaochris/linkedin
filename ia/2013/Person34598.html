Derryn Coetzee's Summary

MARKETING STRATEGY & DIGITAL MARKETING SPECIALIST:

I am a strategic marketing specialist. I offer consultancy and advisory services, and I am also available for senior interim marketing leadership and management roles.

I have over 18 years of experience in B2B marketing working in and with a variety of organisations, including blue chip corporates, international media companies and start-ups. I also launched and ran a successful independent digital marketing agency. For 9 years (2003 - 2012) I led a team of up to 20 web developers, designers and digital marketing experts delivering digital marketing tools and campaigns for a range of clients in various sectors.

My key strength is planning and delivering integrated and measured digital and offline marketing strategies and campaigns - managing people, processes, systems and projects to deliver results.

KEY SKILLS & DELIVERABLES:

• Creating and delivering digitally enabled marketing strategies.

• Delivering integrated and measured online and offline marketing plans for B2B organisations.

• Implementing web-based marketing automation, CRM and ecommerce systems.

• Managing teams to deliver effective and efficient people and processes.

• Planning and implementing projects to improve performance with marketing and sales alignment.

• Marketing analytics, with measured ROI and other key performance indicators to deliver short, medium and long term marketing, sales and business objectives.
