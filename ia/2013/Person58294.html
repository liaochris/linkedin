March 2005 – August 2012 (7 years 6 months)

Publishing, platform, and business development strategy, consulting and services for fiction and nonfiction authors, entrepreneurs, and nonprofit and business leaders wanting to take their ideas to the next level.

Clients include: Howard Behar, former president of Starbucks; Susan Scott, founder and CEO of Fierce Inc.; Frank Wildman, founder of the Feldenkrais Institute; Shama Hyder Kabani, founder of Click to Client; Andrea J. Frequent speaker on publishing, writing, entrepreneurship, and developing ideas that matter.

Executive Editor and Imprint Director, Viking Penguin

Penguin Group USA

Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; Publishing industry

1998 – 2004 (6 years)

Editorial Director

Broadway Books, a division of Random House

Privately Held; 1001-5000 employees; Publishing industry

1995 – 1998 (3 years)

Associate Editorial Director

HarperCollins Publishers

Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; Publishing industry

1980 – 1995 (15 years)

--While taking on increasingly senior roles, developed a broad list of best-selling titles in the fields of psychology, parenting, health, women’s and multicultural issues, and sociology. Notable books included Harriet Lerner’s The Dance of Anger series, the groundbreaking The Courage to Heal and its companion books, Mary Kurcinka’s Raising Your Spirited Child, Linda Villarosa’s Body & Soul: The Black Women’s Health Guide, Wayne Dyer’s inspirational titles such as Your Sacred Self, Robert Bellah, et al.’s, Habits of the Heart, the fiction of Barbara Kingsolver, beginning with The Bean Trees and Animal Dreams, and Jo-Ann Mapson’s novels including Hank and Chloe and Blue Rodeo.
-- Created initial plan for HarperLibros, one of the first Spanish-language imprints in the industry.
-- Various team and leadership assignments.
