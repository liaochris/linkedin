Kanika Tolver's Summary

Kanika Tolver was born in Washington, D.C. and educated in Prince George’s County, Maryland. Kanika is an author, motivational speaker, and highly decorated employee of the United States Government. As a 2005 graduate of Bowie State University, she holds an undergraduate degree in Computer Technology with a concentration in Internet Technology. Kanika is an Information Technology Project Manager in the Federal Government with over 13 years of experience. Currently, she manages mobile applications, web applications, and social media outlets. Kanika is the CEO of Cool Geeks Media, LLC, which is a creative and innovative media firm that provides branding, web design, and photography services.

In November 2012, Kanika Tolver won the Up and Comer Federal Government Employee award for her innovative contributions to information technology. In February of 2013, Kanika was nominated for the Rising Star award for the 14th Annual Women in Technology Leadership awards. Kanika has also been featured in the Prince George’s County Gazette, Washington Post newspaper and on FedScoop.com.
