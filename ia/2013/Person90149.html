Maya Sharon's Summary

I hold an experience of more than 15 years in training development, project management and organizational consultancy. I love people, learning and delivering content to the right people with the right learning method.
My experience includes the following:
1. Delivering workshops for senior managers, training developers and trainers in Comverse, the Ministry for Foreign Affairs, IPS (Israel Prison Service), VISA Cal, Israel Police Force, Discount Bank, Igud Bank, Maccabi Pharm, Tel Aviv University, IDF, etc.
2. Delivering lectures (in English) in global conferences and universities on learning cultures, presentation skills, training effectiveness and innovative learning.
3. Leading cross-organizational and international training projects in global companies, such as Comverse, Teva, Applied Materials, etc.
4. Developing training in Hebrew and English in diverse training methods - ILT, eLearning, blended learning, soft skills, mentoring modules, etc.
5. Designing LMS methodological features for Discount Bank, JDC, IPS, etc.

Specialties: 1. Delivering workshops for senior managers, trainers, team leaders and employees.
2. Leading global and local training projects.
3. Planning training programs and training methodology.
4. Providing strategic training consultancy for training managers and global training departments.
