    • Self-published
    • January 15, 2013
    Authors: Angie Tolpin

For more information on this book, the study guide, the online ministry {videos, teaching tools, and blog} visit http://redeemingchildbirth.com or connect with us on facebook {http://facebook.com/redeemingchildbirth}, or twitter {@RedeemingBirth}, Pinterest {http://pinterest.com/angietolpin}

  • Redeeming Childbirth: Growth & Study Guide

    • July 13, 2013
    Authors: Angie Tolpin

    The Growth & Study Guide is the companion to the book Redeeming Childbirth: Experiencing His Presence in Pregnancy, Labor, Childbirth and Beyond

    This series is a discipleship book for women in the childbearing years and a resource for childbirth educators who desire to engage their christian clients.

Angie Tolpin's Experience

Author

Redeeming Childbirth

2012 – Present (1 year)

Redeeming Childbirth the book is a discipleship resource for expectant mothers and Titus 2 Mentors. To find out the vision behind the book visit our website @ http://redeemingchildbirth.com/

The mission of the website is to create unity among women in the body of Christ on this controversial topic and to encourage sisters in the Lord to invite Jesus into this personal experience and journey toward motherhood. The blog features many different women's testimonies as well as a Sunday Prayer-Link up and other free resources such as the "Ultra-Sound of Worship" which is a FREE 2.5 hr playlist of worship music appropriate for labor and birth.

Founder, Writer, Speaker

Leaving A Legacy

January 2007 – Present (7 years)

Leaving a Legacy is an online ministry/blog to encourage marriages and families to thrive.
The Mission of Leaving a Legacy is to encourage and equip men and women with tools and vision to intentionally Leave a Legacy that lasts.
