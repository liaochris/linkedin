Eduardo Martinez's Summary

I am passionate about technology, music & audio, video, multimedia & the Internet. My other passion is for positive social change via music, the arts, & cultural understanding. I own an audio & video project studio. My corporate work continues to strengthen my experience dealing successfully with deadlines & budgets.

I enjoy being a hands-on leader with extensive product & strategic marketing, business, technical program/project management & Software Quality Assurance experience. I enjoy doing creative & strategic work the most. My additional experience includes hardware design, contract negotiations, budget creation, training & team building, especially outsourced/offshore teams, & also internationalization & localization teams. I have led successfully multiple teams simultaneously to meet & surpass cost, quality & project schedule objectives. Motivating & systematically increasing the responsibilities of those working with & for me are the keys.

I am also multi-cultural, born in Lima, Peru, raised in Puerto Rico & the USA, as well as multi-language: English & Spanish. It is this multi-cultural perspective that has proven very effective in helping others excel under my management, independent of their backgrounds or ethnicity. I forge genuine connections with those I work with as people with real interests, dreams & goals, no matter where they reside. I have dual citizenships: U.S.A. citizen (with valid USA passport), Peruvian citizen (with valid Peruvian passport).

Specialties: Creativity, Leadership Development & Training; Seven Habits of Highly Effective People; Manual & Automation SQA; Product & Marketing Management; Agile & Waterfall SDLC Methodologies; Statement of Work & Product Requirement Document generation & review; offshore/outsourced engineering teams; software internationalization, translation & localization; bilingual: Spanish, English; iOS (iPhone, iPad & iPod) & Android device testing; music production, music composition, music studio creation.
