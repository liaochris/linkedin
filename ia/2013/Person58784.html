Jared Voss' Summary

As a video producer with over a decade of diverse experience, I am engaged in all aspects of the production process, from writing and directing to videography and editing. I approach each project as if it were a piece of music—or a dance—with its own unique tone and rhythm. I carefully and creatively choreograph all of a video’s elements—the words, the sounds, and the visuals—to achieve effective and compelling storytelling.

In my recent position at Physicians for Human Rights (PHR), I produced educational, advocacy, and documentary-style videos, often in quick-response to global events. By re-branding PHR’s videos, enhancing their quality and frequency, and utilizing social media channels, I was able to exponentially increase YouTube views and subscriptions and drive new visitors to PHR’s website. Videos I produced for PHR have been featured on various third-party sites and news broadcasts, including DailyKos.com, Witness.org, Current.com, GRITtv with Laura Flanders and McClatchyDC.com.

I founded LoudSue Films, a narrative and documentary film production company, and provide video production, multimedia, and web design services through VossCo MultiMedia. As a freelancer, I have worked with corporate entities, nonprofit organizations, small businesses, performing artists, and production companies.

In addition to my experience in production and post-production, I have worked in film, television, radio, and theatre as an actor—a proud member of SAG-AFTRA with years of experience in both Boston and New York City. I provide voiceover and narration services through Voices on Demand, and in my spare time enjoy songwriting and playing guitar, a passion I’ve pursued (sometimes professionally) for many years.
