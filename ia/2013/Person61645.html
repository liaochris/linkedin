Jen Kuhn's Experience

Co-Founder

The Experience Factor

January 2009 – Present (5 years)

Are you asking your employees to do more with less? Is morale low around the office? Are your managers managing or coaching? Would you like to enhance internal and external service? Would you like to enhance productivity? Then coaching is your answer and The Experience Factor can help you fill in the blanks.

Our goal is to help companies and managers create a positive work experience for employees through positive and consistent coaching methods. By enhancing and capitalizing on the employee experience, companies will in turn enhance and create amazing customer experiences.

Focusing on the experience and the points of impact with both employees and customers will lead to increased retention, higher morale, increased word of mouth and wallet share along with creative employees that have a stake in your company and work to bring the mission of your company alive.
