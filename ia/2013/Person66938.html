John Rasiej's Summary

Do you realize that public speaking is the FASTEST WAY to attract clients and to grow your business and impact? You can reach people in a more powerful and effective way than other means of communication. You can get a quicker boost in your bottom line as a result.

I work with entrepreneurs, executives and other business leaders who struggle with effective communication or fear of public speaking. I help them become clear and targeted with their message so people want to listen. These business leaders become more effective delivering it. In that way, my clients become more accepted, better respected, MAKE MORE MONEY and create more impact with their messages.

My goals are to help people with valuable missions to broaden their impact further and wider so they bring about powerful change and benefits to others, their communities and the world. There is no reason audiences need to be subjected to mediocre or boring speaking presentations -- and people who do better walk away with more tangible results.

Located in the heart of Bucks County, PA, John is close to Philadelphia, Princeton and New York City. I've also worked with clients all over the country including Los Angeles and Dallas and internationally including Canada, Mexico, Italy and France.

I authored the book "Speak Louder Than Words: Five Ways You Can Change Lives and Boost Your Bottom Line Through Great Public Speaking."

For your FREE report on Delivering Powerful Speeches that Create Massive Impact, visit www.speaklouderthanwords.com

John Rasiej's Experience

CEO and Founder

Speak Louder Than Words

December 2009 – Present (4 years) Bucks County, PA -- Philadelphia Area within Easy Distance of New York City

Working with entrepreneurs who struggle with effective communication or fear of public speaking, I help them get their messages spoken with more clarity and impact. In that way, my clients become more respected and accepted, and generate more clients, MORE MONEY and more impact with their messages. I work with people one-on-one as well as conducting the highly successful Elite Speaker Success Intensive in Princeton NJ.

Corporate Speaking Trainer and Strategist

Speak Louder Than Words

December 2009 – Present (4 years) Bucks County, PA -- Philadelphia Area within Easy Distance of New York City

I provide training and strategies to companies whose staffs need to make effective presentations. Training includes tips on skills needed, unique approaches such as where on stage to stand when facing an audience and whether to practice looking in a mirror, as well as how to craft a speech that has impact. Strategies are explored on how to use speaking to engage more customers, potential groups before whom to speak and ways to include public speaking in the overall sales and marketing efforts of the firm. I have worked with firms in financial services, information technology and the cemetery industry.

Director

Newtown Arts Company

January 2003 – Present (10 years 11 months)

I direct plays performed at the Newtown Theatre, garnering raves from audience members about the level of connection and truth being portrayed on stage. Also serve as Vice-President of Operations, and occasionally act in some shows as well. I have guided Newtown Arts to a level where it's the only theatre group to have been among the Top 5 for each of the last four years in an on-line poll conducted by PhillyHotList.com.

Director and Actor

LoveCreek Productions

May 1992 – July 2000 (8 years 3 months) New York, New York

Directed numerous plays (approximately 25), with casts that ranged from professional actors to people who had no stage experience. Developed range of skills to have my casts convey a strong connection to audiences, communicate effectively, shine on stage and get over fear and nervousness.
