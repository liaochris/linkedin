Alex Huff's Summary

I have a passion for invention and all aspects of turning ideas into new ventures. I believe that the start-up process is at the heart of what drives innovation, invention and evolution. Taking an idea from conception to execution and through all aspects of the entrepreneurial process is something I have been fortunate enough to do for most of my career. I pride myself on learning from the lessons life teaches through success/failure and continual improvement.
