Alexandre Duarte's Summary

I am an user interface / interactive designer, with background as a front-end developer and information architecture. I have solid experience in web-related projects, with clients such as VALE, Rede Globo, Duty Free Dufry, Telecine, Multishow, GNT and Comitê Olímpico Brasileiro (COB). I am also a freelance designer with a bachelor's degree in Product Design at the Escola de Belas Artes - UFRJ (Universidade Federal do Rio de Janeiro, class of 2006) and a graduate degree in Digital Design for Interactive Media at INFNET, class of 2009.

I love the intersection between a great design and a well-built code. I am also an enthusiast for the application of the latest technologies to achieve endless possibilities of interaction and content diffusion that the World Wide Web allows.

I feel privileged and passionate about my profession. Add me on LinkedIn, chat with me on Gtalk, Skype or send me an e-mail.
