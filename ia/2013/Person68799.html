Joseph Thorndike's Summary

Joseph J. Thorndike is a writer and political historian. He is the author of "Their Fair Share: Taxing the Rich in the Age of FDR," published in 2013 by the Urban Institute Press. He is also a co-author of "War and Taxes" (2011) and a co-editor of "Tax Justice" (2008).

A regular columnist for Tax Notes magazine, Joe has also written for the Wall Street Journal, The New York Times, The Washington Post, Barron's, Bloomberg, Time.com, and various scholarly journals.

Joe's current project is "The Money Men," a composite biography of U.S. politicians and their stewardship of the nation's economy.


Specialties: History, public finance, taxation, economic history, U.S. political economy
