Marcel Collignon's Summary

Marcel Collignon acts as Change manager, interim manager, faciliator/moderator, conference host and as a coach for professionals in the CSR working environment.

Professional Goal: drive a Sustainable business by contributing my skills & knowledge for a more Sustainable World.

Marcel Collignon is founder and owner of Sustainable Change bv. Sustainable Change guides organisations through the Change Process on Sustainability (Corporate Social Responsibilty, CSR), by delivering excellent Management of Change Capabilities.

Specialties

Management of Change, Coaching, Intervision, Strategy Deployment, Facilitating/moderation of Large Scale Events,
