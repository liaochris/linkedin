Katrina Pfannkuch's Summary

Do you need content that purrs? How about web or blog content that authentically shares who you are and what you offer minus all the salesy feel? Maybe you have lots of creative ideas for your business, but are unable to get any traction and make an impact with them.

Perhaps you aren't sure what you need, but you know it's a mix of marketing and content strategy and tactical ideas to increase your customer base and market share...all thrown together with some powerful brainstorming and creative inspiration.

I'm a copywriter, creative idea generator, content strategist and all around inspirational force for your creative soul and business, if you're looking for that sort of thing. I'm also a Reiki Master and Light Resonance Healer.

I work on web content including Websites, blogs, ebooks, articles, social media content and scripts for video and radio. I also serve as seamless, experienced content manager for an existing team to support short or long-term projects.

Most importantly, I'm a dependable creative resource who helps you connect to your true calling and market authentically.

To Get Started:
First and foremost, I need to understand how I can help. Please contact me at www.creativekatrina.com and fill out a contact form with details.

A little about me:

I have been a passionate writer for over 14 years. I love to brainstorm with business owners and turn those ideas into compelling content that brings creative ideas to life. If that resonates with you, I would love to talk and see how I can help with the vision, marketing strategy or content strategy for your business.

I also love helping people push through and overcome creative blocks. Through workshops and one-on-one consultations, I help you reignite your creative flow and tap into that raw energy to use in your business. I also share insights on my blog www.creativekatrina.com

Whether you need a business or career jump start or want to reignite your creative flame, I can help. Let's connect!
