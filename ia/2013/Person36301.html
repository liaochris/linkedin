► Deb Nystrom's Publications

  • "Success Secrets of Trusted Change Advisors"

    • Speaking Engagement: The Association of Change Management Professionals
    • April 2, 2012
    Authors: ► Deb Nystrom, Gail Severini, Liz Guthridge, Michael Nestor, James G. Bohn, Ph.D.

    What does it mean to be a trusted change advisor in today's turbulent times? Learn from this panel of internal and external change advisors who support leaders dealing with complex, time-sensitive challenges. The advisors will talk about how they vary their approach and style for different leaders so they can build trust and accelerate change, especially dealing with diverse stakeholders who want their voices heard. You'll also hear about what works and what to avoid in the role of a strategic change advisor. This includes serving as a sounding board, helping uncover issues, testing ideas and taking an active role in execution. Plus, you'll get tips, techniques and practical ideas from the panelists as well as other workshop participants.

► Deb Nystrom's Summary

I help leaders create data-driven people energy for sustainable change using a deep expertise in large group methods. I help energize & maximize strengths to bring new life to your internal/external resources. I have deep expertise facilitating board retreats, meetings, and focus group & team projects.

My core experience is described on the REVELN website focused on performance, change and process facilitation & executive leader coaching.

I'm about leverage, high performance and engaging whole systems, using large group methods, including Open Space and Preferred Futuring. These are two specialities we have used in agile, systems change with good success.

My Social Media Learning Lab includes leaders, consultants, coaches & business owners. Search for REVELN & "Deb Nystrom" or "social media" and you'll find our digital examples.

I draw from sensing trends, as well as from 22 intraprenurial years of experience in business, higher education & non-profits specializing in business development, organization development (OD) & process consulting including a role as an OD practice lead.

My client mix is listed on www.REVELN.com

Learning: Lippitt Planned Change Practicum (connected with Kathie Dannemiller's practical Whole Scale Change work), Jack Sherwood’s Organization Effectiveness Institute, the Inventory for Work, Attitude Motivation(iWam), the MBTI® Step II / including the shadow functions, and Appreciative Inquiry.

Specialties: Performance (teams & systems), change & transition, leadership development &coaching, retreat design & facilitation, large group methods, succession, on-boarding, organization development, focus group project leadership, executive board development, performance management, competency systems, strategic planning, talent development, employee engagement
