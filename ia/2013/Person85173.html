Marco Cignini's Summary

An award-winning advertising creative director and art director, Marco’s current role at KBS+P merges his 20+ year passion and knowledge of action sports culture with his cross-disciplinary thinking to deliver new innovative marketing solutions in digital, experiential, social, event, as well as traditional media.

Prior to joining KBS+P, Marco spent the last 7 years at McCann, helping reposition some of America’s most iconic brands like Verizon, ESPN Mobile, NFL Mobile, Wendy’s, Staples, US Army, Avis, Nikon among many others. As SVP, Group Creative Director on the Verizon Wireless, FiOS and Staples accounts, which he helped pitch and win, he was responsible for managing a creative team of 12 and providing creative leadership and strategic guidance across Staple’s and Verizon’s multiple agency partnerships.

During this time he also founded and managed the daily operations of MADSKATE, the first and only Madison Avenue online social hub for skateboarders working in the advertising and creative industry, to network, discuss, meet, socialize, post news, and session together. 

Throughout his career, Marco has been the recipient of numerous industry awards and his work has been featured in Transworld Business, BNQT.com, AdWeek, AdvertisingAge, Creativity, Wall Street Journal, USA Today, NY Times, PC World, MSNBC, Fox News and CNN.

Specialties

Building brand relationships with tween, teen and young adult demographics. Integrated Creative, Team Leadership, Creative Direction, Art Direction, Creative Brand Strategy, Marketing Campaigns, Product Launches, Digital Campaigns, Experiential Events, Retail Promotions, Pitches and Presentations. Brand-relevant cultural insights and anything action sports related.
