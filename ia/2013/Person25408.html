Claude Blackman's Summary

My passion is Real Estate, I love this job and being a Real Estate agent, I love the whole aspect of being a Realtor, anything from getting assigned a listing and going out to do a occupancy check for the new owner, or just going out to clients house to explain what a short-sale means to them. There are not many agents out there that can compare to the dedication that I have for this job.

I am experienced in all aspects of the Real Estate transaction, be it from the start of getting a listing and doing a occupancy check for a REO, explaining the short-sale process to a client that needs assistance or working with the loan officer and escrow to get the correct HUD out to the buyer to get the correct amount to wire to be able to close on time.

I have done thousands of BPO's and it is personal pride that I strive to produce the most accurate and professional reports out there. I get my info from multiple sources when compiling info, not just the MLS like most.

When representing the seller be it on a REO, Short-sale or any deal, I do everything in my power to get the property on the market as quick as possible and to get it sold fast. I have a vast connection of investors and buyers and ofter will have the property sold within the first couple of days on the market. I follow instructions to the tee, and I am not afraid to ask questions or for help. I do weekly property inspections myself and I myself follow the transaction from the start to the end not relying on others to do my job like many in this industry do.
