Crystal Ponti's Summary

Social Media "Experimentalist" & Online Community Expert

Since 1999, I've been working with online communities in some fashion or another. I've been fortunate to have been able to build a career as a Community Manager out of what started as a volunteer effort, a hobby. I'm also well-versed in social media, content management and blogging and have almost two decades of marketing experience.

As a Community Manager, I've been a part of successful initiatives, building communities from the ground up, increasing community population and participation, building bridges between engineering and tech departments and communities and instituting entire community departments where none existed previously.

Specific skillset:

1. Content creation - writing topic-related Q&As, blog posts, articles, newsletters, communications material and material for social media channels

2. Social media marketing – creating, managing and growing a company's brand through blogs, LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook and other strategically relevant online properties

3. Events and event planning – conceptualizing online events that foster a community spirit; attending in-person events to generate brand exposure

4. Public relations – managing incoming media requests and building relationships with bloggers; creating, executing and measuring blogger outreach campaigns and media tours

5. Customer relations – customer support – answering questions via e-mail, forums, Twitter, etc.

6. Communications/marketing strategy – creating strategic marketing/communications plans to provide direction for a company’s public-facing communications

7. Analytics – Using Google Analytics and other measurement tools to provide reports on metrics, and continually find ways to improve on those metrics through testing and new initiatives

8. Business development - Responsible for business development and sale
