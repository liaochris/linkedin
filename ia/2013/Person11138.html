Avniye Tansug's Summary

I have been conducting several NGO-based projects on ICT and information & communication technologies law for the last 15 years. I started my own legal counselling office after having graduated from LL.M on IT&Telecom Law program at Strathcylde University, School of Law, in Glasgow,UK, in September 2006.

I have been conducting an IT-Law related weekly radio program since 2002 on Acik Radio, 94.9 ("Bilgi Caginin Hukuku"), co-director of bilgiedinmehakki.org (Turkish FOIA portal) and Internet and Law Platform.

Previous experience: Lawyer, professional management in communication sector, consultant to several NGO's. Short-term expert to EU'S www.gapculturalheritage.com project in GAP region, Turkey...

Uzmanlık Alanları: IT Law, Legal Aspects of Social Media & Internet,
Content creating for radio broadcasting,
Information & Communication Technologies Policies and Legal Aspects of Information Security
Freedom of Information, Data Protection
