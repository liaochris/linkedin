David Lee's Summary

David Lee is a leading design and technology thinker. Recently named one of the best designers in technology in 2013, David joins Squarespace as Chief Creative Officer from TBWA Worldwide where he served as Worldwide Digital Executive Creative Director and President & CCO of Pilot.is, where he created Projeqt, a dynamic web presentation platform. Earlier in his career, he worked as a Creative Director at Wieden+Kennedy and AKQA, and as an Art Director at Sid Lee.

David has been responsible for winning almost every industry award, including the Cannes Cyber Lions, One Show, D&AD, Webby Awards and SXSW Interactive. His work has been published in numerous magazines and he is a frequent judge for One Show Interactive, D&AD, AICP and FWA.

He has also been recognized by the Art Directors Club Young Guns 5, nominated as a winner of the biannual international competition which awards the most exciting young talent in visual communications. David has also also recently been nominated as one of the Agency Innovators of 2012 by the Internationalist Magazine.

David graduated from RISD with a BFA in graphic design
