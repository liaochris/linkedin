Christian Vasile's Summary

I started freelancing back when I was 16 and design and the web industry have been part of my life ever since. I am eager, creative and motivated and I believe that my multiple skills and character will one day land me that dream full-time job: UX/UI designer.

Until then, I try to get involved in different projects, I am very interested in start-ups and I don't say no to any interesting opportunity that arises.

I enjoy writing as well and I collaborate with several online publications, where I mostly feature articles about design, usability and social media. More than 4 million people read my articles on a monthly basis.

Technical skillset: HTML5, CSS3, Wordpress, Interface Design, Photoshop, InDesign, Premiere Pro.
Personal skills: personnel/project management, leadership qualities, solid experience with intercultural environments, research abilities, clients management.

Christian Vasile's Experience

UX/UI designer

Skeize

June 2013 – Present (4 months) Aalborg, Denmark

Skeize is a very exciting Danish start-up with focus on creating an iPad application. The app is supposed to help small businesses with their billing process by making it easier for them as well as quicker. We want to take it step by step and only develop a feature at a time, while intensely focusing on the interface, user testing and usability.

I am in charge of the interface design of the app, as well as testing, and although the project is quite new, we are well into the process.

Besides bringing a design expertise into the start-up (co-founded by three programmers), I am also in charge of designing their web identity and helping them raise awareness over their brand (only locally for now).
