Corinne McElroy, CPCC, ORSC's Summary

WHY HIRE ME AS YOUR BUSINESS PERFORMANCE COACH?

There’s an old saying, “It’s lonely at the top.” Small business owners, entrepreneurs, and executives know this feeling all too well. As your coach I will partner with you to get to know you, your business, your dreams, and your top challenges and work with you to get to where you want to go. Additionally, I will serve as a trusted advisor – a fellow business owner – that can empathize with your anxieties and concerns. Together we will brainstorm, talk through business strategy, and create workable, effective solutions.

WHAT’S IN IT FOR YOU?

I follow and live my dream each and every day by helping business owners and executives like yourself fully recognize who they are today and moving them forward to where they want to be tomorrow using strengths based leadership techniques. My powerful coaching techniques guide leaders to take charge of their lives through discovery of their own creative principles, strategies, tools and the continuous support I offer. Hiring me as your Leadership Performance Coach will result in the discovery of unknown qualities that emerge as I will challenge you to stretch and grow, seeking a balance between work and life, and achieving greater satisfaction in all areas of your world.

WHAT DO I DO?

I design leadership coaching, executive coaching, and business performance coaching programs specifically for each individual and team I work with, rather than using a cookie cutter approach. As the creator and designer of my own performance coaching business, I have the freedom to approach the coaching process based on your vision and dreams – both personally and for your business venture. I design and facilitate leadership training and strategic execution sessions for your business and teams, use 360 degree feedback tools, and coach executive teams to create high performance business cultures.
