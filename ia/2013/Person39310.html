AEA Property Management was founded in 1985 as a full service firm dedicated to providing premier service to single family, condominium, multi-family and Homeowners Association properties throughout Southern California. We have the knowledge, experience, education and integrity necessary to provide our clients with expert management services. Further, we can assist you with financials, maintenance, governance, and other important matters, so you can be confident that our professional education and commitment can provide you with the level of service you deserve.

We are comprised of a team of professionals with Licensed Real Estate Brokers, Certified Manager of Community Associations and Certified Property Manager (CPM) Candidates. We understand how important it is to have the Homeowners, Board Members and Property Managers work together as a team. Our property management team will work diligently to alleviate and solve any problems that may arise in order to achieve a happy and harmonious association that all homeowners will enjoy!

Our redesigned website offers a suite of services, which we are confident, will completely revolutionize the property management industry. Our website boasts a "maintenance request" module for tenants, online application processing, online rent pay, direct deposit and web-based property surveillance. All of these tools will continue to position AEA Property Management in the upper echelon of property management companies throughout Southern California.

We feel confident that the responsiveness of our Property Management Team will provide you with a feeling of security and comfort that will make property ownership fun again!
