Erik Rosenbluh's Summary

I've been telling stories for nearly 30 year in one form or another. I began by writing short stories and then wanted to be a filmmaker. I first attended film school over 20 years ago and learned I had a knack for editing (probably those years of writing stories taught me a little something).

I've been fashioning stories in documentary and reality television for many years now. My specialty seems to be getting the hard ones to work. I tend to find creative ways to solve problems. Sometimes it's hard to believe I get paid to be creative for a living.
