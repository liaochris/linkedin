Dee Summers' Summary

Summers & Associates provides search and consultation services, compensation surveys, department analysis, resume building, interview preparation and offer valuable insight into the search process. We specialize in the placement of attorneys in-house and provide executive recruiting.

MEETING YOUR NEEDS
We research each client’s specific corporate culture, philosophy and style before each search and guarantee the services we provide. Each recruiter has in-depth knowledge of the legal and business community and its market conditions.

MARKETING
The firm has established an unparalleled reputation for professionalism, trust and integrity within the legal placement industry and amongst attorneys everywhere. We will promote your search through an extensive network of local and regional contacts. Our perspective on the market provides access to the most qualified candidates. Because we target candidates not actively in the market you see only those candidates serious about your opportunity for all the right reasons and not just someone looking for any job.

CANDIDATE PRESENTATION
Our recruiters evaluate each candidate thoroughly and ensure that all candidates have the required skills and are a good fit with your unique company culture. We offer insight into the candidate’s professional experience, areas of relevant expertise, motivation, compensation requirements, interpersonal skills, strengths and weaknesses. We respect the confidentiality of our clients and applicants alike.

FLEXIBILITY
Summers & Associates can work together with your internal search team and the key individuals in your legal and human resource departments to facilitate the interview process, answer questions and provide timely feedback. We want to be a part of your team, working together toward a successful conclusion.

Specialties

Legal, Executive and Senior Mangement recruiting within California.
