Justin Shimek's Summary

Mattson - We Make Food Happen.

Mattson & Co. is the largest independent developer of new products for the food and beverage industry. From conceptualizing new business opportunities to formulating and scaling up new products, we collaborate with our clients to create successful new brands and rapidly bring products to market.

We are specialists in:
• Creating compelling concepts with consumer ‘frequency appeal’
• Prolific protocepting informed by the integration of culinary, creativity, and technology
• Applying proprietary consumer research methods throughout development to drive projects with actionable and cost effective insights
• Seamless development process from bench formulation through full commercialization
• Creation of branding, positioning, package graphics, and promotions for food and drink

Mattson has over 35 years of experience developing across categories, technologies, and channels of distribution. We bring together under one-roof a dynamic development community composed of culinary cross-trained food technologists, technology cross-trained chefs, commercialization specialists, and marketing/branding experts.
