Sarah Cook's Summary

My role as director and designer at CW-Design involves the day-to-day running of the business, the management of the projects and the delivery of creative graphic design.

I set up CW-Design Ltd in 1999, with the objective of delivering creative graphic design solutions on-time and on-budget. I love it… it’s hard-work, it’s challenging, but it’s also very rewarding, and having the opportunity to work directly with clients makes it all-the-more enjoyable.
