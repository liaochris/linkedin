Dani Klein's Projects

  • GiftCardRelief.org

    • November 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Dani Klein, Dave Weinberg

    Following the aftermath of Hurricane Sandy, this quick response for direct aid is a grassroots effort being spearheaded by concerned citizens (Dave Weinberg & Dani Klein) both in and out of the NY metro area and is under the direction of David Pollock of the JCRC NY and William Rapfogel of the MetCouncil.

    This request for direct aid was officially requested by the JCRC NY & MetCouncil on Monday Nov 5, 2012 as the only and best way for outside individuals, communities, organizations, and corporations to support the Greater New York relief efforts following this storm. Once purchased, whether individually or in bulk, gift cards will be sent to MetCouncil who will direct the distribution on the ground to those in need.

  • ParnassaFest

    • February 2009 to October 2009
    Team Members: Dani Klein, Dave Weinberg, Susanne Goldstone Rosenhouse

    ParnasaFest is a series of Jewish networking events occurring worldwide put together entirely through grassroots efforts.

    Parnasa, which is Hebrew for livelihood, is more on people’s minds today than it has been in many many years.

    ParnasaFest was thought up after the news of mass layoffs in the Jewish Non-Profit world spread on Monday, Feb 9th, 2009. So many throughout all industries have lost their livelihood, their Parnasa, in the past few months. Now is the time to stand up and help each other. Whether its a kind word, making others laugh or just passing on a good lead… ParnasaFest is for you.

    These events are being organized to help you meet different people, companies, organizations, and recruiters to hook you up with fresh job opportunities.
