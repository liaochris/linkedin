Anthony Bakker's Summary

As a Senior Systems Engineer and Infrastructure Architect , I identify the standards for hardware, software, and communications technology that ensure the health of the infrastructure and the privacy of highly sensitive data. I take a disciplined approach to solving problems with thoroughness and determination: from requirements gathering analysis, architecture design, and individual and collaborative problem solving to successfully working through development, testing, and deployment.

My architect and infrastructure strengths include:

o Create an information systems architecture that describes the components of information systems (data, function, and network, etc) and the roles and relationships of those components.
o Evaluate the expectations, needs, and constraints to build an architecture that is scalable, cost efficient, and meets business needs for agility in the market place.
o Build and grow the infrastructure considering such factors as technology requirements, vendor capabilities and project sequences and life cycles.
o Apply good methodology practices to check and adjust for completeness and usability.
o Evaluate new products and tools to keep the infrastructure secure, manageable, and scalable.
o Maintain 99% system up time and availability over the long term.
o Comply with service level agreements and licensing upgrades.
o Document servers, procedures, and workflows to help team in managing resources.
o Adhere to HIPAA compliance imaging, encryption, and EDI capabilities.

I began my career as an Aircraft Mechanic for commercial and military organizations. During that time I honed my top qualities in being able to work under pressure while keeping a precise attention to detail in a complex environment.

Technology interests: new technologies, virtual environments, and cloud infrastructures.
Other interests: Sales and Marketing.
