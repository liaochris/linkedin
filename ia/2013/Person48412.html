Garren Givens' Summary

Garren Givens is part of the Social Commerce "Strategy and Operations" team at Gannett Digital Ventures.

He is an experienced manager and entrepreneur that has spent several years building products in social commerce. Before joining Gannett, Garren founded CampusDibs (a vertically focused daily deal site) and Dibsie (a social shopping platform built on Facebook's social graph). Campus Dibs and Dibsie have appeared in Entrepreneur, TechCrunch, BetaBeat, VentureBeat, and Forbes. Garren has contributed articles to VentureBeat and Huffington Post on social commerce and improving diversity in tech.

He holds both a BA and MBA from Yale University.
