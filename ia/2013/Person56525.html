Jackie Sweet's Summary

My team and I offer a superior, concierge level of service. We know the high-end market and are skilled in-depth marketers, experienced and committed. If you have a home to sell or wish to acquire one we will steer you through the process working tireless to achieve one result only: a hundred percent client satisfaction.

As a licensed Realtor and Fine Home Specialist, Jackie has the credentials you can trust. She holds numerous certifications:  ABR – Accredited Buyer’s representative, CIPS – Certified International Property Specialist, CRS - Certified Residential Specialist, TRC – Transaction Referral Certification, E-Pro- real estate internet specialist and RSPS - Resort and Second Home Property Specialist. Is active in professional associations, including the National Board of Realtors, Florida Association of Realtors and the Naples Area Board of Realtors, Women’s Council of Realtors and The Institute for Luxury Home Marketing.

She started her career in the Naples real estate market in 1999 at the Ritz Carlton Beach Resort. This introduction into the Naples Luxury Market was the beginning. Since then she has helped numerous clients not only find their perfect home but at the same time disposed of the home that no longer fit their lifestyle.

She understands your home is an important investment for both you and your family. Her expert perspective in the rapidly changing real estate and mortgage markets make her the right choice for assisting you in buying or selling your home.

Specialties: Marketing Consultant for the Luxury Waterfront and Golfing communities within the Naples area.
