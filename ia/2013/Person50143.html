Gregg Swanson's Summary

Gregg Swanson is a leading mental strength expert in the field of personal development, self improvement and personal power. He specializes in driven individuals that are searching for new heights of self development and personal growth and desire to achieve personal peak performance on a consistent basis.

Gregg has developed a unique and powerful approach personal growth by strengthening and aligning a person's body, mind and spirit so that they can live a life of power, purpose and passion!

Personal development includes activities that improve awareness and identity, develop talents and potential, enhance quality of life and contribute to the realization of dreams and aspirations.

The concept is not limited to self-help but includes formal and informal activities for developing others in roles such as teacher, guide, counselor, manager, life coach or mentor.

Gregg assists individuals with their personal growth and personal development that includes numerous areas of their life.

He has authored several books and over 100 articles on the subjects of mental strength and peak performance.

Gregg works with success driven and competitive individuals. Those who have reached a point in their life where they know they’re capable of so much more but just can’t figure out how to accomplish it.

Gregg has dedicated himself to working with individuals who are looking for the deeper meaning in their life. He is passionate about working with men who are labeled in a “mid-life crises” and help them discover their purpose, design their vision and create their mission. He helps them construct a custom, unique and powerful program to achieve success and reach a work-life balance.

Gregg uses his roles as guide, coach, teacher, mentor, sponsor and awakener to provide the environment for you to grow and develop.
