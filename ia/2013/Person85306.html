Marc Pitre's Summary

My team at Right Here Interactive and I can help you craft your message and target your customers across video, mobile, social media & web platforms. We provide "Enhancement Marketing" services that help compliment the existing in-house and partner talents you already have. Together we will execute programs to engage your customers and turn those engagements into conversions and revenue.

You may want specialized programs targeting different platforms or campaigns or even complete interactive marketing programs. Either way, we're right here to serve you.
