Jordan Ewert's Summary

I create images that question the possibilities of reality. Through the process of dismantling elements from portraits and environments I rebuild original images that are conceptual and hyper-realistic. Since 2009 I have worked with hundreds of interesting people, usually forming some kind of narrative along the way.

Many of my influences come from the people I work with. Through having conversations with models & collaborators I come up with scenes that express aspects of surrealism and attention to human emotion.

Advertisers are usually trying to link an emotion or concept to their campaign with images that make a strong visual connection between their product and market. In this respect my photography is intended to serve this purpose.

My goal is simple: to get the very best out of everyone I work with and tell a story, idea, or narrative.
