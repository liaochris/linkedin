Jane Adamson's Summary

Jane’s passion for effective execution comes from personal experience in both leading organizations, and in consulting organizations through every conceivable challenge and business scenario. She has learned that most leaders know WHAT to do to achieve financial success, it’s the HOW to do it that trips them up. As CEO and Founder of Sherpa Advisory, Jane works with leadership teams to implement a results-focused culture. Sherpa clients learn how to develop an accountability mindset, identify the true source of problems, implement change efficiently, and work together effectively as a team. It’s through these basic principles that companies empower their people to deliver consistent & exceptional performance.

During her career, Jane has been instrumental in leading organizations to both operational excellence and financial success. She believes in a culture of continuous improvement formed by ethical and dedicated leadership.

Specialties

Execution skill development (problem solving, implementing change, accountability), team building, strategic planning, leadership coaching
