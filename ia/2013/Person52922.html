Tim Herriage's Summary

I have a broad knowledge of single family residences in North Texas. I have purchased, and/or loaned the purchase money on over 900 houses in the last eight years.

My partners and I are now filling partnerships with private investors to purchase bulk packages of distressed assets directly from lending institutions, and asset managers.

Specialties

Rental package acquisition. Cash flow properties.
