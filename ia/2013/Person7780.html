All individuals qualifying for a Habitat for Humanity home in Walton County must pay a very small fee at closing, 100% of the loan for what it costs to build the house with a manageable low monthly payment, they must attend credit and financial classes provided by Habitat, and each adult who will reside in the home must put in a minimum of 250 sweat equity hours in the building of the home. Habitat for Humanity of Walton County does not giveaway homes but rather builds new futures for those who could not do it on their own with the help of their community.
