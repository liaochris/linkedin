Alva Sachs' Summary

Children's Author, Independent Publisher, Educator, Consultant, Speaker
Currently promoting her award-winning children's picture books: Dear Master Dragon, I'm 5,
Circus Fever and On Your Mark, Get Set, Go! By Alva Sachs and Illustrated by Patricia Krebs
Promoting literacy for children within communities and corporate venues.
Vice President of Reading Is Fundamental Southern California working to build home libraries for underserved children in the greater Los Angeles area.
Seeking businesses to implement literacy programs to disadvantaged areas with book donations for schools, libraries, and shelters.

Specialties: Educational speaker for schools, libraries, community organizations, consultant for children's books, independent publishing development, promoting literacy for children
