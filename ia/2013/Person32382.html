David Parker's Summary

David has been a Real Estate Broker on Bainbridge Island since 1988. He has a strong background in all aspects of house construction and this combination of personal experience is extremely valuable in helping his clients make the most informed decision in buying or selling a house.

David will not come accross as a "salesman". He will ask probing questions to help you make your own decisions. He is patient and a great listener. Ask him a question and you will get a straight and very informed answer, either immediately or through further research. David has a literal "army" of professionals surrounding him, from the lenders to the drywall guy; so when you hire David as your Realtor, you get the best people in all aspects of the process of buying, selling and owning a home.
