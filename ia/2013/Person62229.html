Jered Cuenco's Summary

For over a 15 years I've been working in the online media industry. A background and passion for design combined with extensive development experience have allowed me to lead development teams in building successful web and mobile interactive experiences for a variety of clients such as: MillerCoors, Nike, adidas, Microsoft, Best Buy, T-Mobile, Yakima Racks, Boeing, US Bank, Levis, Sun Microsystems, Borders Perrin Norrander, and The Getty Museum.

A strong technical and creative generalist, I've filled the roles of designer, developer, architect, animator, and video editor; sometimes all at once. I’m passionate about compelling interactive experiences, a firm believer in process and collaboration, and take a personal stake in the success and execution of every project.

In addition to my professional interests I'm also an avid volleyball player, play drums & guitar and love NFL football.
