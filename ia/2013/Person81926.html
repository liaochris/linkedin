Liz Jansen's Summary

While launching my motorcycle tour and event business, I discovered the consistent transformation that occurred when clients were able to push through a perceived barrier and experience new levels of confidence.

With my organizational skills and background in corporate human resources, training and development, I recognized the parallels with motivation and change management. It was natural to combine my interests and my business naturally evolved to include workshops, seminars and events.

Motorcycles are rich in symbolism and I use fun, vivid stories from my riding adventures to illustrate the profound life lessons we learn when we challenge ourselves. And you don’t need to have ever been on a motorcycle to understand them.

Although my clients cross all demographics and my expertise appeals to both women and men, I have specialized proficiency in meeting the needs of female motorcyclists.

Whether it’s as an author, freelance writer, motivational speaker, workshop or retreat leader, my passion is showing my clients how to RECOGNIZE STRENGTHS they already have and inspire them to CREATE THE LIFE THEY WANT.

CONNECT WITH ME IF:

✓ You're an individual considering change, dealing with change or going through a life transition and you would like to create the life you want.

✓ You're looking for a inspirational speaker for your organization or association, a workshop or a keynote speaker for your conference.

✓ You're an event planner seeking expertise in understanding and meeting the needs of women motorcyclists.

✓ You're looking for unique freelance articles for your corporate communications or association newsletters that give your readers a new, meaningful perspective.

✓ You’re an editor or tourism director seeking a freelance writer for motorcycle tourism.

Contact me for a custom solution for your unique needs! I have even written a wedding ceremony!
