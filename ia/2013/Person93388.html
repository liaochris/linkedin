Social Media Pro ~ Passionate Marketer ~ Authentic Connector ~ Relationship Builder

My goal is to understand my clients' needs, analyze current branding and marketing strategies and leverage the depth of my 25 years of experience in Business Development with Procter & Gamble, and the Non-Profit and Small Business Sectors as well as my extensive knowledge of Social Media Marketing, Social Media Strategy, Branding and Relationship-building to create incremental business for my clients.

I provide Business Development, Marketing Expertise, and Social Media Marketing Strategy Consulting to companies and assist individuals with creating a personal brand image that is integrated across professional social media platforms through my company, C3: Creating Connections Consulting, LLC.

Michelle Beckham-Corbin, MBA's Publications

  • Age of Conversation 3: It's Time To Get Busy!

    • Channel V Books
    • April 1, 2010
    Authors: Michelle Beckham-Corbin, MBA, Drew McLellan, Michael Rubin, Kevin Dugan

    Amazon Editorial Review: "Following the success of the first two editions, Age of Conversation 3: It's Time to Get Busy! again kick-starts the discussion about how the global marketing landscape is changing. With over 170 of the world's leading marketers, writers, thinkers and creative innovators contributing chapters, this collaborative work investigates the roles that community, conversation, experimentation, engagement, and collaboration play in shaping the 21st century's economy of ideas. As businesses, public and private organizations, and individuals realize that there's much more to social media and its impacts than meets the eye, Age of Conversation 3 shows us which platforms, tools, and approaches truly work, as well as those that simply don't. "Social media" may be the business buzzword (or, buzz-phrase) of 2010, but what's happening beyond the hype? What new practices and guidelines is social media imposing on business-as-usual? And what makes this type of media so much different than the media we're all used to? From the boardroom to the locus of customer interaction, social media is transforming the way we do business. The impact of this is being felt in every customer interaction, each business decision, and even in the way we source, retain and engage our staffs. This third book in the Age of Conversation series is crowd-sourced, bringing together the world's leading practitioners to share their stories, perspectives, observations, and strategies. Their diverse insights and varying approaches are a reflection of the global, changing nature of business today."
