Brad Elliott's Summary

I am an entrepreneurial, senior leader with over 20 years of experience managing in high growth, dynamic organizations. Currently leading a strong management team in a rapidly growing mid-market real estate and homebuilding company. We have succesfully implemented and are operating under the "Traction" management system, producing superior quarterly results.

My past responsibilities include a position as CEO leading a privately held, mid-market company through consistent double digit growth as well as a senior project management role with a Fortune 500 company in real estate acquisitions, home buidling operations and customer service. I excel in defining the correct organizational structure and recruiting the right people into the right seats. Also known for producing deliverables from conceptualization through market realization. I quickly recognize and capitalize on changing trends, demographics and competitor vulnerabilities and have a proven ability in strategic planning, program and systems development, pro forma budgeting with strict periodic review and accountability, and comprehensive human resources management including team building with strict focus on meeting personal, team and company objectives. I strongly believe and enjoy participating in broader industry advocacy.
