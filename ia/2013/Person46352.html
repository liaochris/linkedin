Fred Stutzman's Summary

I am a technology researcher and designer interested in building smarter, more useful social technologies. To that extent, my work focuses on diverse areas such as privacy, productivity, technology policy, and user interfaces.

Currently, I run a small company that builds the innovative productivity software Freedom and Anti-Social. When time allows, I provide product development and research consulting to technology organizations. In addition, I teach classes about privacy and social media at UNC-Chapel Hill.
