Allen Serfas' Summary

Assistance Home Care provides in home care services to those who wish to remain in the comfort and safety of their own homes in the greater St. Louis area

I am so fortunate to be able to apply virtually everything that I learned through my 20+ year career with Enterprise Rent a Car with regards to providing great customer service and employee satisfaction and combine this with my wife's personal experience as the primary caregiver for her mom and, working together, establish Assistance Home Care.

Assistance Home Care (636-724-4357) provides non-medical home care and serves families with disabled adults and seniors in St. Louis, St. Charles, Lincoln and Warren Counties who wish to remain at home and retain their independence. Our home care agency provides non-medical companion care and homemaker services to adults and seniors in their own home, bedside assistance for patients in hospitals or rehab centers, aftercare for patients recovering from surgery to new and expectant mothers. Options include hourly in-home care, over night and 24-hour live-in care.

Our caregivers are the foundation of our success and thus we provide highly trained experienced, compassionate and dependable caregivers. Our in-home aides provide clients with loving personal care to help make their lives easier. Clients can retain their independence, while their families have the peace of mind that their loved ones are safe and well cared for.

All our caregivers are carefully screened, bonded and insured so you can trust you are getting the best possible at home assistance. We match up our caregivers that align with your specific preferences and stand behind it with our 48-Hour Compatibility Guarantee.

Our Care Team is available whenever you need us day or night - 24/7. Call today for assistance: St Charles: 636-724-4357 and St. Louis: 314-631-1989 or visit our website at www.AssistanceHomeCareStLouis.com
