K.J. McIntyre's Summary

Over the last twenty-two years, McIntyre Marketing has served clients across a wide range of industries in both for and non profit worlds. Recently, we directed the marketing efforts for Birchez Associates, a leader in Hudson Valley affordable housing specializing in senior communities. K.J. McIntyre has been active in community programs: for example, she was one of four who developed Kingston's Digital Corridor, an initiative to rebrand the city to attract additional technopreneurs. K.J. is knowledgeable in Governance and Development issues for not-for-profit boards and served as Secretary of the Board of Directors of the Children's Home of Kingston; Secretary, Arts Society of Kingston (ASK); President, Kingston Library; Treasurer, Kingston Uptown Business Association; board member, Ulster County Board of REALTORS. She is an active participant in local chambers, business associations, and nonprofit causes.

Specialties

Grant writer, fund raiser. Social media strategist/implementor. Award winning corporate communicator, nationally published in financial services, widely published in real estate. Holds the following designations: Seniors Real Estate Specialist, GREEN NAR designation, NY Department of State certified real estate instructor, Certified Instructor for Coldwell Banker, Previews International Property Specialist, Accredited Buyers Representative. Financial: Series 7 not active.
