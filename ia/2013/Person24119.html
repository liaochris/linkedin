Christina Profit Queen Vendley's Summary

I'm a native California girl that loves the outdoors, challenges and people. I started my sales/marketing career by working for Fortune 100 companies. I then moved into being a promotional items distributor providing marketing solutions to Fortune 100 companies such as IBM, Sun Microsystems and Abbott Labs for 10 years. I created Outside the Box Promotions, and learned everything to do with running a business and had sales in the top 2% of the nation.

Then, I became a mother. That changed everything and after getting frustrated by dropping my son and nanny at the beach everyday, I decided to close down my company and became a full-time stay at home mom and blogger for 5 years. Then, from a Craig's List ad for an Affiliate Manager in the blogging/internet marketing world I came into this crazy and fun world of internet marketing. I enjoyed being an Affiliate Manager and have evolved into creating my own joint venture management company from the coaching of Seth Larrabee and being a top winner of the PH Method Brokering Challenge. And, I happily take #2 spot in that challenge of 40 top internet marketers to the #1 guy that did a $1.5 million dollar webinar.

I am a Joint Venture Manager operating from a high level of integrity while creating strategic alliances to maximize profits for all.

It's been a great journey and I LOVE what I do by connecting people to do successful promotions. At this point, I can't imagine being in any other industry. I just had the pleasure of being a speaker/faculty member at the last jvAlert Live event by Ken McArthur in San Diego. I hopefully will be in Drew Canole's new infocommercial to be released in a month and am part of Ryan Lee's Dot.com Xpo product coming out in January 2014. And, the ride continues...


Here's a bit more about me: http://christinavendley.com/about/
