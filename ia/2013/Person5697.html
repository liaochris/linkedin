Amie Rickels' Summary

As an Executive Leadership Coach, I empower people to think, communicate and act in alignment with their goals. When an individual struggles to meet a goal, the barrier falls into at least one of three categories. Either their thoughts are not aligned with their goal, they are not clearly communicating their goal or they haven't taken the appropriate actions to achieve the goal.

I combine master's level studies in Communication and Impression Management with 13 years people-empowerment experience to provide intuitive feedback on thought, verbal, nonverbal and environmental barriers to goal achievement. My work is research based, pulling from the disciplines of communication, psychology, sociology and neurology. My clients achieve their goals, become powerful communicators and live personal brands that are magnetic and memorable.

Specialties: - Communication Audit that includes verbal, nonverbal, environmental and personal brand feedback
- Exceptional analytical and critical thinking ability
- Highly effective one-on-one coaching approach
- Passionate about delivering designated outcomes
