Beth Kuhel's Summary

Beth is passionate about career coaching, executive coaching, public speaking & writing articles on timely career related topics that help people improve their chances for success in today's challenging economy. She combines her background in psychology, marketing, strategic planning, career path planning and her own life experience to help people introspect and develop necessary skills to get a competitive edge and thrive in the today's workplace.

Beth's core strengths are in Career Planning & Career Management, Career Strategies, Reputation Management, Personal Branding and Interviewing advice. Beth uses her connections through LinkedIn and through her own business contacts to accelerate clients ability to connect with hiring managers.

Beth researches hiring trends to provide timely, accessible, and useful information for individuals developing a career plan. Beth is a Certified Employment Interview Professional (C.E.I.P).

Beth works with students, recent graduates and individuals seeking a career transition to help them set realistic and obtainable career goals. She coaches her clients to identify their outstanding transferable skills and core competencies to attract new jobs where they’ll thrive. Beth provides LinkedIn coaching to job hunters and business owners, and strengthens resumes. Beth teaches her clients how to navigate the digital job searching process with savvy and optimism. As a LinkedIn Connector, she uses her network to introduce clients to hiring managers.

Beth has 25 years of experience in marketing, sales, strategic planning, personnel management, and recruiting. Beth was retained by both for profit and non-profits, ranging from the computer industry to active travel and educational institutions, to draft and revise their marketing plans.

Beth received her B.A. (major psychology) with distinction from the University of Michigan and attended the graduate program in business before completing her MBA at George Washington University.
