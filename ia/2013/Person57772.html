Jim Jacoby's Experience

Founder

ADMCi

Public Company; 1-10 employees; Professional Training & Coaching industry

2012 – Present (1 year) Greater Chicago Area

ADMCi coordinates a series of related initiatives:
- The ADMCi School for Digital Craftsmanship
- The ADMCi Foundation for Master Practitioners
- The ADMCi Commissions for Research and Development.

Under the school, we host a full curriculum with user-centered design offerings in Chicago and St. Louis. Additional classes are delivered online in a live, collaborative format. Under the Foundation, we are in the midst of our current commission with JT Nesbitt of Bienville Studios. The Foundation seeks to discover and share the master-craft ethos for cross training in industries like digital design and marketing where standards and practices are currently harder to encapsulate.
