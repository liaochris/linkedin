Bernard Stamm's Summary

Extensive & detailed work with pump performance curves (15+ yrs), pump selection, quotation and BOM generation; experience includes pump test station & instrumentation.

Previous work includes quotation software for: pumps, motors, heat-exchangers, ion-exchange systems, reverse osmosis banks, and hoist & cranes.

Specialties: detailed work with real-time software for NDT instrumentation: eddy-current, flux-leakage, ultrasonics, and surface analysis.

Bernard Stamm was previously the software group leader at www.inspectech.ca and was the key developer for their current Eddy, Flux, IBIS and UT systems; which remain essentially unchanged today. I have a good recommendation for and relationship with the current software leader developer there.
