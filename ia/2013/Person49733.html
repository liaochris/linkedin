Graham English's Summary

I now operate in two complementary fields

At Fontis (a social enterprise) we seek a change in the psychological and social contract between the public and public services, by involvement in decision-making and service provision at all levels, sometimes known as co-design or co-production. We influence public policy making, support individuals and organisations who want to make change happen, campaign, promote new thinking, and generate sustainable solutions for local agencies.

As Kagome (a trading name for Graham English Consultancy) we utilise new approaches founded in the recent advances in neuroscience to generate possibilities and potential, generating new connections and new mental maps, breaking restrictive paradigms and assumptions. It is an approach to the generation of ‘agency’ – the ability of individuals to take independent action and make choices otherwise unavailable to them.

As Graham English Consultancy we offer a practical marriage of delivery with developmental work, particularly through specialist NHS consultancy, project delivery or finance support, board or team development and coaching services. We put real emphasis on ensuring you get performance improvement and practical implementation from our work with you, not just planning.

Our work is grounded in real business issues to produce tangible outcomes, for your people, your performance, your leadership, developed in the context of your business and its everyday tasks.

Graham English's Experience

Director

Fontis (UK)

September 2009 – Present (4 years)

We aim to transform the quality of public services, public life and well-being in the UK by improving the practice of involvement, engagement and participation.

We will do this by providing

• leadership and advocacy for involvement at all levels across the public domain

• a membership network connecting people who share our aims

• an innovative resource centre providing an authoritative and comprehensive guide to practice

• investment, with others, in effective ways of enabling more people to be involved in decisions that affect them

• capacity development for individuals and organisations

• support and challenge to thinking on policy and delivery

We will campaign for the transformational value of involving people in the development, design and delivery of the services they receive.

Our aim is to change the relationship between the public and public services. We also aim to promote standards of practice, stimulate and monitor progress across public services.

We work at national, regional and local levels with individuals, front-line organisations and policy-makers to remove the obstacles which get in the way of making involvement happen.

- to show your support for our objectives
- to find out more about how we work
- to help us get there!
