Henny van der Pol's Summary

Research/Report

We investigate your current CAD/GIS workflows and data-structures. Benchmark them with current standards and advice on improvements.

Workflows

We define workflows on how to optimize and classify your CAD and GIS data. Basic principle is sharing of data inside and outside of your organization.

Workshops

With hands-on workshops you can extend the knowledge of your GeoSpatial software and bring it to the next level. At your site and with your own datasets, so you will get return on investment the next day.

Data Classification

We can help you using Data Classification, lines on layers with colours are migrated into road-sectors, cables and pipes (from CAD-entities into GIS-features).

Proof of Concept

Before introducing new workflows and software into your organisation, we can build a test environment. Safety first.

Software-development

If necessary we can develop software utilities for your specific needs. With knowledge of C/C++, HTML, Javascript, XML/XSL, PHP, VB.NET and C#.NET we speak our languages.

GeoSpatial Strategy

Based on our knowledge we can help you to define a long-term strategy concerning GeoSpatial Systems.
