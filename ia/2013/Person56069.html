Software Development Engineer at Diligent. Key developer of the ProtecTIER solution.
The ProtecTIER solution is a high performance storage solution for backup to and restore from disk. The application emulates SCSI devices over Fibre Channel with a VTL interface and deduplicates the data, which decreases significantly the actual amount of physical storage on the disk. This enables replacing tapes and robots with disks and replicating the data over the WAN to remote sites.
The system is mainly written in C++, highly concurrent, and uses advanced storage, compression and de-duplication technologies.

Itay Maoz's Publications

  • Face Recognition in Unconstrained Videos with Matched Background Similarity

    • IEEE Conf. on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR), 2011
    • 2011
    Authors: Itay Maoz, Lior Wolf, Tal Hassner

    Recognizing faces in unconstrained videos is a task of mounting importance. While obviously related to face recognition in still images, it has its own unique characteristics and algorithmic requirements. Over the years several methods have been suggested for this problem, and a few benchmark data sets have been assembled to facilitate its study. However, there is a sizable gap between the actual application needs and the current state of the art. In this paper we make the following contributions.
    (a) We present a comprehensive database of labeled videos of faces in challenging, uncontrolled conditions (i.e., ‘in the wild’), the ‘YouTube Faces’ database, along with benchmark, pair-matching tests.
    (b) We employ our benchmark to survey and compare the performance of a large variety of existing video face recognition techniques.
    Finally, (c) we describe a novel set-to-set similarity measure, the Matched Background Similarity (MBGS). This similarity is shown to considerably improve performance on the benchmark tests.
