Andrew Thorp's Summary

As a speaker and trainer my role is to help individuals and organisations communicate more successfully. That's why I co-founded MojoLife, a training company dedicated to using authentic storytelling to help:

> Leaders persuade and influence others
> Companies stand out from their rivals and attract clients
> Organisations engage and motivate their employees

LEADERSHIP COMMUNICATION
Rather than CEOs leaders are now expected to be CSOs - Chief Storytelling Officers! Through one-to-one Executive Coaching, I help them convey the true purpose of their organisation both externally and internally, thus winning people to their cause.

MARKETING
Just telling more people what you do simply contributes to a sea of marketing 'noise' (white noise). I work with companies to craft a compelling story about what they offer, and help people tell that story so they positively attract business, talent and followers.

INTERNAL COMMUNICATION
I work with HR teams to capture, share and utilise stories from the workforce. This can help drive positive change, engage employees and provide great marketing material.

I am a regular keynote speaker on business marketing & storytelling with appearances at TEDx, Business North West, BKR Conference (Romania), Slovenia Business Forum and The Academy for Chief Execs.

If you want to communicate better, drop me a line at andrew@mojoyourbusiness.com - that costs nothing!

Specialties: Training, executive coaching, presentation skills, public speaking, web-site copywriting, slideshow design, Speakeasy, MojoLife, storytelling, personal branding, personal impact, leadership, employee engagement, networking, emotional intelligence, customer service, golf, movies, acting, sports, 7 pillars of sporting success, author, writing.

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Professional Training & Coaching industry

March 2011 – Present (2 years 6 months) Manchester, United Kingdom

Owner

Andrew Thorp (Business Speaker & Skills Trainer)

January 2008 – Present (5 years 8 months)

I coach essential communication skills with particular emphasis on leadership, building networks and public speaking/presenting.

In 2009, I founded Speakeasy Groups in NW England, a series of monthly networking sessions for people looking to improve their presentation skills and business message. I also drew on my own experiences of reinvention to co-found MojoLife, a training & development company that promotes storytelling in business and authentic, ‘inside-out’ communication.

As a business speaker I explain how business leaders, salespeople, job seekers and other would-be influencers can use persuasive presentation techniques to achieve their aim. I call it 'connectation skills', the ability to connect with, and emotionally move an audience with a powerful and compelling message. I also help clients build support for their ideas through social media and other strategies like organising events, speaking in public and writing books & articles.

Storytelling can have a powerful impact within organisations too, and our Story Clubs encourage greater engagement, motivation and cross-departmental cooperation.
