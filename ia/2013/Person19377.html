Andrea Johnson's Summary

Hello, my name is Andrea Johnson and Calla Web Design is my design studio. With both technical and creative strengths, I create beautiful, functional websites that accomplish their purpose successfully with a certain panache. I started designing websites as a hobby when I was 12 years old. I started making websites for local businesses and attending school for web design when I was 14, got my first job as a front end web developer at a digital media agency when I was 19, and launched my own business at 21. I live in California with my wonderful husband and enjoy cooking, embroidery and volunteering in my spare time.
