Bob Smith's Summary

We are about reducing the chance for failure and improving the odds of success by making damn sure that Start-ups find the right people, with the right skills, the right chemistry and an aligned attitude.

Our core business is about working with fast growth start-ups to help them find the right people with whom to advance the mission and grow revenues.

We work on contract with companies to help them hire a group of people that will make the right things happen. Our emphasis is Revenue Growth through high functioning Relationships within the business and with Customers.


Personally, I am about full potential. Fulfilling my own and helping companies and individuals fulfill theirs toward worthwhile ends. It is important to advance the value of both as a way to retain talent and attend to the fact that we all manage our own careers now..

Today, everyone needs a voice, a strategy, a personality with character. These things need to be managed actively and intelligently.

We recognize that the "C" Team needs their teams to execute on the Planned direction of the company and we also know that changes will occur during that execution that need to be seen early and handled competently. This is one of the many elements that we incorporate into hiring. No Plan I have ever seen, unfolds as written.


My Success helps people and organizations recognize and access their full potential to achieve more success. Together, we uncover every aspect of their abilities and learn how to deploy them for the greatest impact.
