Anna Ryan's Summary

About Anna Ryan:

Anna grew up in Tulsa, Oklahoma. With parents in the Building and Real Estate business and family in the Appliance and Fixture business she knew at an early age that she would somehow become involved in the "Home" business.

She later moved to Conroe, Texas where she became a Texas Real Estate Agent in 2002 while embracing the values of Keller Williams Realty. She graduated with a Construction Management degree from Sam Houston State University. She became a Texas Real Estate Broker in 2008 .

Anna moved to Greenville, South Carolina in 2009 and became a Real Estate Broker in that state as well. She currently has a team and services clients in both states.

Anna realized that she wants to do more than the average Realtor in that she hopes to help people make Real Estate related decisions. Her enthusiasm, work ethic, vision, appreciation for technology, education, and experience combine to put her on the top of the list for the person you will want to turn to when you think of anything "involving where you live".

About RealtyPlus:

RealtyPlus is a Team of Specialists involved in every aspect of your home.

"if it involves where you live, it involves us!"

We go beyond the concept of customer satisfaction in a Real Estate agent or company. We believe there are many emotions and decisions that come to surface when dealing with our often largest investment-our home.

RealtyPlus is here to help guide you through whatever stage you are in.

Perhaps you are thinking of Buying or Selling but not sure how/when/where to start. You may want to know more about the Local Market, Investing, Financing, Remodeling, Staging, Curb Appeal, Where to spend the Least for the Most Impact, how to attract the Best Buyers or where to find Motivated Sellers. Perhaps you wonder what type of Home Improvements you need to keep up with your neighborhood. Perhaps you are considering Building but not sure where or how to Choose a Builder or how to go about Shopping for Land. What about Owner Finance? Lease Purchase? Who can you turn to for simple Home Repair questions?

Wherever you happen to be in the Home Owning process RealtyPlus is the place to turn. Our professional team treat our clients as family and build life relationships.
