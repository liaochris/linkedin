Brad Harvey's Summary

I am a fifteen year business and IT executive with deep experience in consulting, data analysis, enterprise strategy, and project management (former PMP). I also have an MBA education and an undergraduate degree in Mathematics giving me a unique and powerful combination of business savory and technical expertise.

Recently I have turned my focus to the creation of Nerdies. Inspired by his son’s recent diagnoses of Asperger’s Syndrome, I set out to find a place where he could go and pursue the things that he loves. While he will probably never play organized sports he is exceptionally talented in mathematics and wants to grow up and become a video game programmer. As I searched I realized there just wasn’t a place for my son or any other smart kids out there who are into different things besides sports and music lessons. Nor was there a place for my son to be around other smart kids in a social environment. For these reasons we are starting Nerdies.
