Rob Wise's Summary

Robert J. Wise concentrates on real estate law throughout the Greater Boston Area. Specifically, Mr. Wise focuses his practice in legal areas relating to the acquisition, development, financing and sale of real estate. Such representation may include, but is not limited to, purchase and sale agreements, mortgages, deed preparation, condominiums, mortgages, and resolving landlord and tenant disputes.

Mr. Wise received his Bachelor of Arts degree from Boston College in 2000, with a major in U.S. History. He then pursued his Doctorate of Jurisprudence at Suffolk University Law School, graduating in 2004.

Our offices specialize in all aspects of real estate law, which means, quite simply, that we are there to provide insight and expertise in order to guide our clients through the waters of real estate law and the myriad of different issues which this area of law presents. We routinely travel throughout Massachusetts, meeting people at their homes, offices, jobs or wherever is convenient for them.
