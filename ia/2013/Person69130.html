Joshua Burt's Summary

The Law Office of Joshua A. Burt is a Ventura civil law firm specializing in the areas of copyrights, rights of publicity, trademarks, trade secrets, employment and labor, personal injury, civil litigation and client counseling. From protecting trademarks, copyrights and trade secrets to litigating complex business torts, Joshua Burt has established a proven track record of excellent legal expertise.

In the areas of intellectual property, Joshua Burt has represented companies and individuals, whether plaintiff or defendant, in trademark, rights of publicity., and copyright infringement and related unfair business practice matters. Joshua Burt has further represented clients injured by breach of contract or accused of violations of employment or other workplace and business practice laws.

Joshua Burt works with clients in need of his specialized and professional legal attention. Joshua Burt has substantial experience handling the complex issues involved with business litigation, particularly the complexities of intellectual property (trademark, copyright and trade secret) infringement, employment disputes and breaches of contracts.

Joshua Burt’s experience extends to other areas of civil practice as well, including personal injury, Americans with Disability Act claims, and real estate disputes, among other areas.

Specialties

Trademark and copyright litigation, prosecution and counseling; Business litigation; Employment litigation and counseling; American with Disability Act litigation and counseling; personal injury; rights of privacy; rights of publicity; torts; and intellectual property infringement; among other areas.
