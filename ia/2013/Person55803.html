Irina Verwer's Experience

yoga teacher, writer & vegan chef

Irina Verwer

November 2009 – Present (4 years) the Netherlands and beyond

I fell in love with yoga when I took my first class over nine years ago. Since that day, my love for yoga just kept on growing.
Yoga is my life. The joy and bliss of practicing and teaching amazes me still. It’s this feeling of joy and bliss I'd love to share with you.
That's why I completed two teacher trainings: a four-year hatha yoga teacher training at Saswitha Opleiding voor Yoga en Wijsbegeerte in the Netherlands (where I live) and an ashtanga vinyasa yoga teacher training at Gaia Tree Yoga on Bali. Both teacher trainings are certified.

I’ve specialized myself in Women’s Health and Yogic Diet, on which I am currently writing a book.

I love to join classes, workshops and masterclasses, and am inspired by and grateful for all my teachers - past, present and future.

I'd love to see you on the yogamat for a yoga class, workshop or retreat in the Netherlands, Italy, France, Morocco or the USA (and beyond)!
You can also pick up Yoga Magazine, Tijdschrift voor Yoga or the Yogagenda and check out one of my articles.

My other passion is cooking (and eating...) delicious vegan food. I'd love to cook with you, in a workshop or a private class. Also, you can hire me as your private chef - I've created dinners for touring bands as well as vegan, organic, sugarfree weddingcakes for lovely couples.
Contact me to discuss the possibilities.

My first yogic vegan cookbook, 'Kopstand in de Keuken', was published in August 2013.
