Jonathan Bieda's Summary

Designer with 9 years of experience applying user-centric initiatives to products and campaigns in the entertainment, journalism and health care industries, along with building visual interfaces and interaction architecture for web-based applications. I enjoy creating unique virtual experiences that are simple, accessible, efficient and a pleasure to use.

My professional interests involve working in tech-driven product development and marketing roles. Things that tend to crowd my browser history include concept development, human-computer interaction, game design, animation and digital illustration.
