Kristi Casey Sanders' Summary

As VP of Creative of AMP, I help define and manage our strategic objectives, business partnerships, branding and content, education and live events. I also oversee the development of new media and digital initiatives. I created an internal ROI department to show the value of the programs we offer partners and a social media team to curate content and engage our communities across multiple networks and platforms. I love living at the intersection of technology and the humanities, creating teams, troubleshooting, demystifying technology and trends, creating efficiencies and streamlining operations.

• As a MPI Proprietary Content Speaker for 2012-2013 and professional speaker at large, I regularly educate my peers at industry conventions and other social functions on the topics of technology, innovation, sustainability, strategic planning and finding cost-effective solutions to enhance the way your company does business.

• For Plan Your Meetings (http://www.planyourmeetings.com), I work to empower and educate people who plan meetings and events to be more innovative and plan more strategically.

• For Encore Atlanta (http://www.encoreatlanta.com), I create new media coverage for the arts and leisure scene in Atlanta, create custom publications like "The Fox Theatre: Atlanta, Georgia — The Memory Maker," and act as an advocate for our greatest local institutions: the Atlanta Ballet, the Fox Theatre, the Alliance Theatre, the Atlanta Opera and the Atlanta Symphony Orchestra.

• In the past, some of my creative projects as a writer, actress, dancer, educator and filmmaker have been underwritten by organizations such as the High Museum of Art, the National Endowment for the Arts, the Dekalb Council for the Arts and the Georgia Department of Economic Development.

Specialties

Leading by example. Innovating in times of disruption. Embracing change. Giving birth to big, actionable ideas. Inspiring co-workers. Empowering the little guys and girls. Creating fun messaging like this: http://youtu.be/JcaIU87IeRw
