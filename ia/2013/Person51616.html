Hans Stutvoet's Summary

As a self-employed power systems engineer I like to support electrical engineers as much as possible in the field of power engineering. Therefore I provide consults, engineering support, calculations, studies, training courses and power systems analysis software.

Specialties

Generators, Synchronous Motors, MV Power Systems, Synchronization, Relaying, Instrument Transformers (CTs, VTs), Power Management.
Generator Protection Relays of GEC (MIDOS), ABB (REG 316, SPACOM), GE Multilin (G30, G60, 489), Beckwith Electric (M-3425, M-3425A), SEL (300G) and Schneider (Sepam G87)
Automatic Voltage Regulators (AVRs) of GEC (C10/120, DX21), Brush Prismic (MAVR, MicroAVR, A10, A30, A12, A32, A50), Basler Electric ( AVC, SR, SSR, XR, VR, DECS Series) and Holec (ESR-2)
