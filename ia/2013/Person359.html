Anthony Ambriz's Summary

I'm a video producer, blogger, and social media specialist. I currently attend BYU where I've studied advertising, marketing, TV production, graphic design, film, Spanish, K-12 education, and engineering. I will be graduating with a BA in Communications and a minor in Media Arts Studies/Film. I'm partnered with Fullscreen Network, where I create weekly content for Urban Media and my personal channel Anthony Ambriz. My expertise is in YouTube marketing primarily, but also specialize in social media marketing and content creation whether it be in writing blog articles, podcasting, web video, or marketing campaigns.
