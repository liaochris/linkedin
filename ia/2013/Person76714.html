Kraig Mentor's Summary

I have unique technical experience across the fields of eLearning, multimedia, 3D, electronics, robotics, automation, and semiotics. I am good at solving difficult design problems with solid, reliable solutions that have led to several patent applications, numerous publications, and international lectures on various aspects of my work.

From the beginning of a project, project I am able to think and build modularly to the benefit of the current and next project. Projects for which I was lead programmer have received the Pennsylvania TECHQuest Technology Product of the Year Award, the National Hydropower Association Excellence in Education Award as well as numerous ADDY Awards.

I am self-taught, curious, strongly motivated, and interact well with others. My experience, technical base, and understanding of human and computer interaction, offers a unique set of tools for use as a project resource.
