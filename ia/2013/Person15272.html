Bobby Dobbs' Summary

Emmy-winning television producer and voice-over professional with extensive experience in commercial and public television, and corporate video production. Experienced in television production, production management and voice work.

Bobby Dobbs' Experience

Owner

Voice Overs by Bobby Dobbs

August 2005 – Present (8 years)

I do Voice Over for radio, television, cable commercials, web, long-form narration for corporate projects, and audiobooks. I record in my own studio with the capability to provide quick turnarounds on just about any project.

I also continue to produce and direct television projects. I just completed a run of about 20 years producing, directing, and editing North Carolina People with William Friday for UNC-TV. Check it out at: http://video.unctv.org/program/1616436260/

I also produce projects for non-profit ministry organizations either at cost or on a reduced budget. Some of the organizations I've worked with are Stand Out Ministries, Mabopane Foundation, A Heart's Cry Pregnancy Counseling Center, Siloam Missionary Homes, and Missionary Air Group.

For demos of my voice work go to: www.bobbydobbsvoice.com, you can also see clips of some of my other work here as well.
