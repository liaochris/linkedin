Dana Maciag's Summary

With an eye for detail, Dana Railey Maciag sees her world as a work of art, waiting to be created.

Entering the “real world” to become a teacher, Dana isn’t sure if she was looking for ways to practice her craft...or if the projects found her. After living with the school’s blank library walls, she created an inspiring and whimsical mural. She quickly received such positive feedback that she was inspired to turn this hobby into her career. Over the years, she has had the pleasure of painting murals for large commercial projects, as wells as the rooms of lots of lucky children.

As an artist, Dana enjoys bringing her clients’ ideas and tastes to life with her own vibrant, imaginative style. She can transform a wall or an entire room into enchanting princess quarters, Gotham City, a fantasy garden, a beach scene, Pokemon- themed space or a serene nursery. She also paints commercial logos and signs for offices.

Over the years, Dana helped many friends by applying makeup for their weddings. Trained through Lynette Arlene Makeup Art, a high-quality professional makeup line, and Dinar, an air brush technique, Dana has built a reputation for creating a one-of-a-kind flawless, beautiful look for her clients.

Dana uses top of the line products to create desired looks for a variety of special events like weddings, proms and quinceñeras, as well as for photo shoots, including maternity portraits, head shots and senior pictures.

Being a muralist and a makeup artist, Dana Railey Maciag feels privileged to connect with people on a more personal level than many have the chance to. With every job, she works closely with her clients to create the look they desire, regardless of budget or personal style. Because she is entrusted with something as important as a wedding or a child’s room, she pays attention to every last detail as if she were the client – because she expects the best.
