Michael J. Karlin, Esq.'s Summary

I am a practicing Entertainment and Business Attorney in Southern California. My entertainment law practice focuses on working with independent filmmakers and bands. I also handle major business litigation (as well as some real estate lit and transactional and personal injury). I received my Juris Doctorate from Chapman University School of Law. I graduated Cum Laude with an Entertainment Law Emphasis Certificate. Most of my experience in the Entertainment business has been on the independent side of the movie and music industry. Before law school I worked in College Radio as both a DJ and a Music Director. I also played in and managed a nationally touring indie rock band. During that time I also interned at a successful independent record label. During my law school career I worked as a law clerk at a law firm doing general legal work. I also externed, doing legal production work, in a Clinic that provided free legal work to low budget independent filmmakers. In my free time I continue to write music and perform, as well as be involved with a sketch comedy troop from time to time.

Specialties: Westlaw, LexisNexis Certified, Microsoft Word, Wordperfect, Excel, PowerPoint, Photoshop, Dreamweaver, Protools, Mac and PC
