I am the Chief Strategist and Operations Officer of 5 medical practices for The Manhattan Center dealing with woman's health and wellness, including cosmetic vaginal surgery and a high end cosmetic medi-spa.

J. Zachary Creative Group was founded in 1980 by Marie Slaton. We are a marketing, design, product development specialists and a consulting firm serving corporations, manufactures and individuals in a variety of markets, including, Health and Beauty, Food, Giftware Industry, and Corporate Development. These businesses include Wholesalers, Product Designers, Manufacturers, Marketing, Public Relations, and Advertising firms.

Marie Slaton is a communication specialist and an avid entrepreneur who personally selects clients to work with who inspire her with their own commitment and motivation. Marie personally reviews each prospects needs and will then advise if J. Zachary Creative Group is the best vehicle to help you achieve your goals.

J. Zachary Creative Group is a unique blend of resourceful, strong strategic thinkers with a proven track record of success. Consider us consultants...who can implement! We provide the fresh perspective and methodical implementation to make positive change happen.
