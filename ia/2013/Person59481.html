Jason Bowen's Summary

Execution oriented senior information security leader who serves organizations in governance, operations, advisory and incident response capacities. Implements successful global information security programs by providing thought and people leadership to quickly identify and reduce risk. Communicates effectively with stakeholders regardless of culture, profession or position. Leverages 16 years of leadership and people management experiences from corporate, consulting and military organizations to serve stakeholders, manage information security risk and deliver value to his team.

Specialties: Information security risk management, security architecture design, policy/standards development, infrastructure security operations (firewall, vulnerability management, data loss prevention, anti-phishing), policy exception and risk acceptance management, information security management during mergers/divestitures, computer incident response team and network/application penetration testing.

Jason Bowen's Experience

Director, Global Infrastructure Security

Aon

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; AON; Financial Services industry

December 2010 – Present (2 years 11 months) Greater Chicago Area

Leads Aon's Global Infrastructure Security Team, which is accountable for the delivery of several global information security initiatives including vulnerability management, data loss prevention, policy exception and risk acceptance.

Created converged network security, vulnerability management and systems security policies and standards for Aon Corporation after the merger with Aon Hewitt. Following on the policy and standards delivery, managed the integration of Aon and Aon Hewitt’s Vulnerability Management programs into a single cohesive program designed to measure risk equally across the enterprise. Developed Aon’s Data Loss Prevention strategy, which is designed to manage business and client risk in the most effective manner possible. Regularly discusses global programs, services and value proposition with clients during audits and on-site visits.

Annually reviews and executes 30+ projects in a security architecture role to ensure that all information technology projects have adequate security governance support. Informs and advises senior IT and business leadership of residual or latent risk in order for appropriate risk acceptance and go/no-go decisions can be made.

Assistant Vice President, Security Engineering Team Lead

Privately Held; 10,001+ employees; Banking industry

Designed, maintained and managed ABN AMRO’s Vulnerability Information Portal (VIP), which was a business intelligence application designed to report vulnerability risk findings on 270,000 systems on a global network. Directed the VIP development staff as well as monitored service satisfaction levels with regional information security officers to ensure that expectations are exceeded. Designed and assisted with the deployment of the global vulnerability management infrastructure which included on-site deployments in Brazil, Netherlands and Scotland and several other remote deployments worldwide.

The vulnerability management program as deployed enabled the organization to reduce network vulnerability risk (year over year) by 80% in 2007 and 40% in 2008.
