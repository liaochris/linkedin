Dot Olonovich, MBA's Summary

I am a Business Optimizer with a mission to help entrepreneurs to optimize their businesses so that they are well run and increasingly profitable. (And, of course, so that entrepreneurs can fulfill their missions and enjoy their success!)

Dot Olonovich, MBA's Experience

Business Optimizer

BizCollab.com (Sole Proprietorship)

Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Management Consulting industry

July 2010 – Present (3 years 1 month) Greater Philadelphia Area

I partner with entrepreneurs by taking a realistic look at their businesses, then identifying and proposing solutions for areas which can benefit from optimization, resulting in businesses which are easier to manage and are more profitable.

In 2013, I am moving from helping one person or business at a time to helping groups of people. I’ve been creating BizCollab, a collaborative online learning community for entrepreneurs who are doing the hard work of building businesses they love.

From 2010 to 2012, I researched problems and solutions related to profitable change, strategy, and IT project management, and consulted with entrepreneurs on change issues. My findings are the basis for the programs I am now developing. I received my MBA diploma from Lehigh University January 2012.

I share information on business optimization with thousands of people through varied media including articles, an eNewsletter, SlideShare, my blog at www.dotolonovich.com, and tweets @Dotsworld. I also speak on optimization and strategic growth.
