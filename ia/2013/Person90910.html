Meg Biallas' Summary

I'm an online communications professional with nearly four years of experience in the nonprofit sector, with a passion for issues that affect at-risk populations.

During journalism school, I gained internship experience in newspaper, magazine, television and radio. After a pivotal experience interning for NPR’s Social Media Desk, I decided to merge my communications background with my passion for social media strategy.

I'm active at National Community Church, a multi-site church in the DC area. I'm passionate about youth, and co-lead a girls' club through the mentoring program at the Southeast White House.

Specialties:
>Social media strategy, coaching, content creation and implementation
>Videography and video editing (Final Cut Pro)
>Writing and copyediting
>Writing for print, broadcast and online

Meg Biallas' Projects

    Team Members: Meg Biallas, Meghan Moroney, Danielle Brigida
