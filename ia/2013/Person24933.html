Cindy Andrade's Summary

Licensed as a Realtor in 1991, I first started my career in Antioch with Mason McDuffie Real Estate and later returned to San Jose in January of 1993 where I joined Coldwell Banker in their Almaden Valley Office and stayed there until 1997 when I moved to RE/MAX Associates also located in Almaden.

I worked at RE/MAX as a Realtor until I earned my Brokers License in 2001. After obtaining my Brokers license I moved to Realty World South Bay located in Cambrian where I stayed for about 1 1/2 years, and then returned back to RE/MAX where I stayed until they closed their doors and re-opened as Altera Real Estate in 2009.

After leaving Altera Real Estate I moved into my current office at Alain Pinel Realtors in Almaden. This has been one of the best moves I have ever made. Alain Pinel Realtors has set a very high standard for doing business and offers many specialized services to their clients. They are a premiere company over all other real estate companies.

Specialized in staging homes to sell for the most amount of money in the least amount of time, I offer a FREE Staging Consultation to all my Sellers as apart of my listing services. For the past 10+ years I have also personally staged all my listings.

I love working both as a Listing Agent & Accredited Buyer Representive helping first time buyers and move up buyers find a home that best fulfills their dreams and desires. I'm a strong negotiator able to negotiate the best terms and prices possible for my clients.

I value all the wonderful relationships I've made throughout my career. Fulfilling my clients highest expectations is always my ultimate goal. I truely believe my clients deserve the best and I strive to remain my clients Realtor for life. The best compliment my clients can give me is a referral to a friend, family member or co-worker.
