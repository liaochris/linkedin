Martha Wright's Summary

As a real estate professional, Martha helps buyers and sellers map out their own best moves for a secure future. Her executive background in negotiation, marketing and handling large-scale projects help clients navigate any challenge. Across the board, her cool head and long-range thinking make any home transition a more enjoyable event.

For investors who want to leverage the property values and peaceful lifestyle of Lewis County, Thurston County and the surrounding areas, Martha brings impressive hands-on expertise. She and her husband have built a solid portfolio. Through planning and sound strategy, they are making their dreams come true, and Martha’s goal is to help her clients do the same.

Martha Wright's Experience

Organizer

Washington Hispanic Festival

September 2006 – Present (7 years 2 months)

The Washington Hispanic Festival is a two day, fun filled, multi-cultural FREE event for the whole community in a nice family environment. It is held at the Centralia College Campus every year the last weekend in July. The WHF continues its mission of bringing families, friends, neighbors, community partners, business leaders and local businesses together.
Lewis County is one of the most beautiful counties in WA State and we want to share it with the world.
The Washington Hispanic Festival has become a much-anticipated multicultural event and a truly unique community experience. We welcome sponsors & businesses; all type of services, including but not limited to: musicians, dancers, educational and community resources, local and state public agencies, workshops, business workshops, children’s activities, information booths, food vendors, etc.
For more information or to send me messages go to our website: www.WashingtonHispanicFestival.com
