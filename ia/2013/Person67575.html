Jonathan Lababit's Experience

Yahoo!

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; yhoo; Internet industry

April 2013 – Present (10 months) Los Angeles

My role supports Yahoo's media network which includes the homepage and key verticals – News, Sports, Entertainment, Finance, and Screen (Video).

I unlock insights into content performance, digital trends, and user consumption behavior across PC, tablet, mobile and social platforms. In turn, I work hand in hand with editorial, video, and business leads to produce strategic content that captures the attention of users and drives engagement.
