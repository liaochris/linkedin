Jeffery Newton's Summary

Jeffery Newton is a mobile marketing specialist and teacher. With his background in traditional marketing, Jeffery brings a solid understanding of classical marketing and strategy to the ever-changing world of mobile marketing.

Jeffery is a popular teacher and coach, and through his website, StarMobileMarketing.com, he trains business owners how to appropriately and ethically build their business through mobile marketing, social media tools and traditional marketing.
