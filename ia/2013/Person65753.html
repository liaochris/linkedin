Joe Stampone's Summary

Joe Stampone is Vice President of Investments at Atlas Real Estate Partners. Atlas is a New York City based real estate investment firm focused on core plus and value-add multifamily, office and retail opportunities.

Joe received a Bachelor of Arts in Economics with a minor in Entrepreneurial Leadership from Tufts University. While at Tufts, Joe was a four-year varsity soccer player. Joe also received a masters degree in real estate finance from the NYU Schack Institute of Real Estate where he served as President of the Student Association. Joe is currently a TA for the Real Estate Finance & Investments Class.

Joe's passion for real estate is also evident in his blogging. Joe has been named one of the top 10 most influential commercial real estate bloggers.

Besides being passionate about real estate, Joe enjoys skiing, boating, golf, spending time with his family and friends, and traveling.
