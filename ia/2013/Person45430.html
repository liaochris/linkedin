Rick Matthews' Experience

President

The Aviation Access Project

December 2011 – Present (1 year 9 months) Huntsville, AL

Private aviation is broken, and we intend to fix that which nobody else has, will, or can.

Thanks to LinkedIn, a dozen passionate aviation professionals came together in 2011 to collaborate on REAL solutions to aviation's problems. We looked at EVERYTHING.

This takes courage and boldness, as the egotistical industry continues to be in denial about the real problems. We know what they are, and we know how to fix it. One airplane, one person, one airport at a time.

In the aviation industry as it exists today, access for new customers is ridiculously difficult. The flight training dropout rate is over 80% - not exactly a badge of honor. Too many aircraft makers making too many models, translating into low production volume and exorbitant prices for planes, parts, and support. Too much government oversight that needs to be severed immediately. Flight schools are failing everywhere, or operate on razor-thin margins. And this ADDICTION TO RENTING by flight schools and flying clubs - in whatever form you dress it up - is slowly killing the industry. Why is this perpetuated?

Aircraft ownership - particularly Shared Ownership - is one of the Five Key Components that turn things around. It starts from the ground up. It's all about Access.

The visionary purpose of the Aviation Access Project is threefold:
1 - Lower the dropout rate from 80% to 25%.
2 - Double the number of pilots from the 2012 baseline.
3 - Restore the emotional satisfaction.

And we'll do this by:
1 - Establishing 1000 very cool Flight Center hybrid franchises around the country. These are positive hubs of some VERY interesting activities at the airport.
2 - Encourage OWNERSHIP instead of renting.

Is this possible? Well, yeah - we'll already doing it in many places.

Go to the FB page for Aviation Access Project and see for yourself. Oh, and click LIKE, then the ABOUT section, where we provide an essay on WHY we are doing this.

Be a part of the Movement.

CEO

Fractional Assets, Inc.

April 2007 – Present (6 years 5 months) Huntsville, AL

This company was founded to bring forward vast experiences, trial & error, and best practices for a truly workable fractional ownership business model for a mass market, so that people can finally enjoy ownership in something they have always dreamed of, which otherwise they may not ever get to have.

My colleagues and I are working to incubate new businesses, renew old ones, turn around distressed ones, and even overhaul entire industries with our innovations in collaboration, partnershipping, and technology.

We recently launched the nationwide Aviation Access Project to fix the persistent problems in that industry featuring shared ownership in assets. Next is the parallel Boating Access Project, which will provide access to vessels large and small to a vastly increased market. Also in tandem is the badly-needed Huntsville visionary riverfront development called Huntsville Harbortowne, with both aviation and boating elements in a Disney-esque venue that would compare to Destin's Harborwalk Village.


There may be other Huntsville-area or regional needs that requires an entrepreneurial insight and solution. Let's take a look at.

We invite inquiries regarding ideas, concepts, design, management, and investment.
