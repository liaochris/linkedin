KellyAnn Carpentier's Summary

As a Social Media and Marketing Strategist, I help clients build a sustainable brand for optimal business success. From Market Analysis and Competitor Research to Eco-Friendly Consulting, my company Green & Global Media offers full service custom packages for every client. I use a combination of Inbound and Outbound Marketing techniques to fit the needs and vision that you have for your company.

Aside from the my work with Green & Global Media, I look forward to charitable opportunities throughout the year.

My love of culinary experiences has brought my food blog In The Kitchen to fruition. It's the all the knowledge that I have, my experiences, my personal culinary preferences ... all in a place where friends, family, and any one else can come to learn and share their love of everything food.

And because all of this is not enough, I have The Global Think Tank. Yet another place for people to come and share their ideas or points of view. It's about bringing the best minds together from around the globe and getting everyone talking.

I also follow my lifelong love of fashion with my company, A Global Marketplace. My products are vintage and old world inspired. I design and make each piece by hand. Currently, I only have accessories for woman and girls, but I look forward to adding men and boys in the near future. I will also accept orders for commissioned pieces. There's a lot of planning going, so if you don't see something, please contact me. We may be able to work something out.
