Lester Lovelock's Summary

CPS works with organisations to help them understand how a combination of people, process, strategy, organisation and technology can help support their business processes and enable them to more effectively manage their project portfolio.

You may feel that you never want to hear the words credit crunch in the same sentence again, and there's no denying that you may see your organisation tightening budgets. CPS is working with organisations to understand how best practice processes aligned with training and supporting technologies can help improve their performance across the organisation.

The technology is the Microsoft Enterprise Project Management (EPM) Solution and it's even better when mixed with Microsoft Office SharePoint Server (MOSS) to be a collaborative environment that supports organisations information business and decision support needs.
