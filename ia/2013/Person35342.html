Dianne Rees' Summary

My mission: Designing, developing and helping to deliver and evaluate learning programs for the Armstrong Institute, including instructor-led training, blended learning, and online learning experiences. My goal is to make sure that learning programs address performance challenges in ways that best enhance patient safety and quality efforts. I'm also evaluating new technologies to develop learner-centered, active learning solutions that best serve the needs of our target audiences.

I'm passionate about patient-centered care, health literacy, and creating opportunities to develop more effective partnerships between patients and healthcare providers.

Specialties: Subject matter expertise: Research experience in biotechnology and immunology; additional expertise in patient safety & quality, oncology, diabetes, neuropharmacology, and neuropsychiatric diseases; PhD in molecular, cellular and developmental biology; JD (concentration in IP law), MA in Educational Technology.
