Katie Nelson's Publications

    "My passion for photography is matched only by my love of road trips. But while exploring America’s roads, I’m troubled by how quickly our nostalgic past is fading from the landscape. It is my personal mission to travel this country and capture what still remains, and I’ve joined the ranks of many who seek to preserve these memories. I selected Polaroid film, which similarly faces extinction, as my medium. Come with me on my photographic journey of America’s Mother Road, Route 66—from a tree covered in shoes, to a giant pink elephant, to ten cars buried in a field, to a mountain lion cave in the desert. It's a road trip that should never be forgotten." -- Christopher Robleski, Author
