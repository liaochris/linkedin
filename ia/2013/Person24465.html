Christopher Dillon's Summary

Christopher Dillon, Founder and President of T3Packaging, an independently minority owned packaging, products and services supplier.

Dillon provides T3P with the expertise of business ownership. In addition, he leads T3Packaging sales and product management with over 20 years of experience in packaging, negotiating cost, researching products and customer relations.

Dillon is committed to excellence and runs T3P with the mission to obtain Complete Customer Service Satisfaction by providing packaging, products and service that their clients can rely on.
