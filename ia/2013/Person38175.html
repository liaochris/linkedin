Dr Nazli Haffiz's Summary

Dr Nazli Haffiz joined BP Healthcare as the pioneer dental surgeon in the Group's effort to expand into the dental services industry. Currently based in the Group's Glenmarie Specialist Centre, he carries out administrative work for the Group as well as providing clinical services to the Centre's patients.

Dr Nazli Haffiz is also a consultant for DreamEdge Sdn Bhd, an MSC status company with the portfolio of healthcare devices and solutions.

In the past, Dr Nazli Haffiz has worked as an Assistant Director of Oral Health in the Oral Health Division, Ministry of Health (MOH) Malaysia carrying the portfolio of Information & Communications Technology in Dental Services. He has also practiced and worked in acadaemia for the Faculty of Dentistry, MARA University of Technology (UiTM) as well as a Dental Officer in the various MOH facilities such as Sultanah Fatimah Specialist Hospital, Muar and Government Dental Clinics in Muar, Batu Pahat and Putrajaya.

Specialties

Aesthetic Dentistry, Dental Implantology, Healthcare Administration, Healthcare IT
