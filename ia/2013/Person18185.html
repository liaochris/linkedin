Rhonda Donald's Summary

I have more than 20 years experience in writing, editing, and publication management—with an emphasis on educational materials—for audiences of all ages and backgrounds. I am accustomed to working as part of a team as well as seeing a project through independently from concept to final production.

I am seeking to expand my client list and develop new business.

Specialties

• Science curriculum and nonfiction, especially life science, the environment, Earth science, and space science
• Animals, wildlife, companion animals, animal protection and education
• Writing for children of all ages, including articles, stories, crafts and activities, and songs
