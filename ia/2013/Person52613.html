Monica Silvestre's Summary

I've been with Yelp since 2009 and have acted as the Community Manager in Philadelphia, the East Bay and Miami. Currently, I'm the Southeast Regional Marketing Manager where I hire, train and manage all the community managers within our 11 markets across the Southeastern US.

Specialties: Mid to large scale event conception and production, creative writing, fostering marketing partnerships, problem solving, honing in on the best and tastiest, and making new friends.
