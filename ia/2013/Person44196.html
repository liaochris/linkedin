Fatima Omar Khamissa's Summary

Welcome to my LinkedIN page. If you believe that you have the right to be to be HAPPY, RICH and SPIRITUAL, then you’ve come to the right place. I am a “Possibilities” and a “Success Coach”. I shine light on all the possibilities you DON’T see, and provide guidance for your life and your business.

My life was not always easy and fabulous.I use my experiences of pain, and hardship as a springboard for helping women globally to live the life of their dreams. Women need know how to acquire more choices, freedom, and money: And I know how to get you there, because that’s what the world needs.

If you have been dreaming of establishing your own business, and yet face many daunting barriers preventing you from doing so, then speak to me. I’m funny, loving and incredibly intuitive . Unlike cookie cutter coaches that have a “set-in-stone-plan”, I listen with understanding, and identify where you’re at, without judging you. I lovingly hold that space of transformation for you to achieve results at your own pace.

BIO:

Fatima Omar Khamissa is the CEO and founder of Spiritual Biz Moms, a company focused on empowering women through the inner-game of success.

Fatima left an abusive marriage after 21 years, taking with her five children, one of whom has cerebral palsy. She used this transformational journey as a springboard to create change in her own life.

Fatima is a inter/national speaker widely known for her training and seminars on work-life balance, empowerment, positive mindset, entrepreneurship, and overcoming adversity.

Fatima helps women stay authentic, youthful and ravishing while they raise their families and grow their businesses without sacrificing their values.

in April 2013, Fatima's received the prestigious Leading Women and Girls in Building Communities Award for being a leader, a mentor, and creating a world without violence.
