Jason McAlexander's Summary

www.quailridgestudios.com

Quail Ridge is a medical illustration studio that provides artwork for publishers, authors, physicians, lawyers, and many others. Before founding Quail Ridge, Jason McAlexander served for seven years as the Art Director of the multinational art department of ICC, one of the industry’s top full-service typesetting companies. Jason also holds a MFA degree in Medical and Biological Illustration from The University of Michigan, in addition to his BA degree in Biological & Pre-Medical Illustration from Iowa State University.
