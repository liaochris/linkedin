Charlie Brown's Summary

My personal specialty is IP (Intellectual Property) Law and Corporate Law. My law firm provides a range of legal advice to small to medium-sized businesses.

I have been practicing law 31 years in the Intellectual Property and Corporate Law concentrations. My firm has represented public and private clients, from large companies (e.g., Motorola) to small companies, in diverse industries (from automotive to consulting to utilities) around the world. In addition, I have been General Counsel of a public NASDAQ management consulting firm and General Counsel of two international businesses. At Wilson Sporting Goods, I had worldwide legal responsibility for its extensive IP portfolio.

This extensive background as in-house counsel greatly benefits my clients. Knowing about business, operations, finances and strategy, I develop an understanding of your business and learn how you conduct your business. This information is critical in developing optimal solutions to your business goals.

In IP, I help clients with trademarks, confidential information and trade secret protection, IP audits, cease and desist matters, IP enforcement, licensing, non-competes, patent management, branding, licensing, domain issues, privacy, web, e-commerce, and software.

In my corporate practice, I have expertise in all types of contracts, creating alliances, start-ups, professional services agreements, sales and marketing reviews, purchases and sales of businesses, mergers, exit planning, succession planning, technology, buy-sell agreements, corporate structure, shareholder agreements, partnerships, dealer and distribution networks, software, e-commerce, computer law, property leasing, legal and business audits, employment law, litigation management, and other areas.

Concentrations: IP (intellectual property law) and Corporate Law.
