Steve Kowalski's Summary

What intrigues me in life are the dynamics of people that I have the privilege to meet. What I have learned most importantly after 25 years in this profession involves asking questions and carefully listening to the story. Each person has a very unique twist on their own experience in their life, career and cultural upbringing. This has helped me to honor their story by visualizing what they are saying thru their eyes, their nonverbal communication, and their perspective at that moment.
When I hear the words "head shot", I get it, but in some way, it is much more my duty to peal away the outer layers and discover for a moment, who that person is and what matters to them. Sometimes respecting privacy is equally important.
In the same way, an event is much more than the intended event itself. I find it extremely important to become a part of the culture in order to understand the purpose of the social interaction and intended results.
Please visit our website at www.abetterexposure.com and if you'd like to leave a comment, I would enjoy that. Call or text me at 402.453.6000 anytime.
