Jay Hait's Summary

Representing Foreign Interests in Israel:

I am an American Israeli attorney with affiliated offices in Tel Aviv, Haifa, and Jerusalem.

I represent foreign individuals, companies, and other entities in their transactions and litigation, in Israel.

As a lawyer in Israel and the U.S., I have the unique ability of being able to help my clients to bridge the divide between the legal culture in Israel and other western countries, leading to superior results for my clients.

To learn more visit http://www.orcheidin.co.il/.



Representing Commercial Clients:

I represent Israeli and international companies and their principals and Sr. management, in their everyday business related endeavors.

I do this by working with my clients one on one, getting to know their business needs and concerns when dealing with Israeli companies, individuals, and government.

To learn more visit http://www.orcheidin.co.il/.



Representing Family Law Clients:

I represent clients in the Rabbinic and secular Family Courts in Israel.

I help people to navigate the Israeli legal structure for family law matters, which is very complex because it has elements of both religious courts and secular courts.

My staff and I help my family law clients by walking with them hand in hand through the processes they are going through. All of this while explaining to them and preparing them for the procedures they will be going through every step of the way.

To learn more visit http://israeldivorcelawyer.com/.



Acting as a Mediator:

Besides being an Israeli lawyer, I am a trained mediator in both and family court and other civil matters.

I help clients minimize their legal expenses by mediating their conflicts rather than just litigating (which takes more time and money and many times is harder to enforce the results of). We have a mediation clinic outside of Ben Gurion airport just to facilitate mediation between Israeli and foreign sides.

To learn more visit http://mediationisrael.com/.
