ORKUN KENBER's Projects

  • Research Security of netwerk

    • March 2012 to April 2012
    Team Members: ORKUN KENBER

    Orkun did a research for the commune Heerlen and wrote a report with his findings. The report was about securing of computer networks that support the different forms of “HNW”, Het Nieuwe Werken, where the “BYOD” concept, Bring your Own Device, is a part of.
    In the training Network Infrastructure Design at Zuyd University the course "NDPRO" is given. In this course assignments are given from external parties. On this note Orkun received the case from the commune Heerlen. This was the reason the report was written. The commune wanted to know more about "HNW" so that their employees could have more choices in deciding how, where, when and with what device they could do their jobs efficiently and effectively. The computer and network infrastructure not only has to support this but also has to take into account the security.
    Because the way of working with HNW is totally different than the traditional manner, it is important to investigate the way in which security is effecting the network.
