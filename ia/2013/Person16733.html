Brenda Fields' Summary

Brenda Fields, is a sales, marketing and rooms specialist in the lodging industry. Her highly successful and progressive thirty-year career includes senior marketing management positions in luxury, boutique, and convention hotels.

Keen analytic thinking is applied to every project in order to identify the issue/problem and then to devise and implement the most cost-effective solution. With extensive experience in pre-openings and repositionings, Brenda was responsible for the successful opening and stabilization of Paramount in New York City, one of the first boutique hotels by developing and executing a unique direct sales and yield management program, including a national and international marketing campaign.

Brenda is dedicated to helping owners and developers achieve their financial goals by developing cost-effective and well-founded marketing plans, and is a company devoted to creating and sustaining strong brands to ensure long term results.

Brenda was recently awarded “The Top 25 Most Extraordinary Minds in Sales and Marketing" by HSMA and well as the Leadership Development Award when serving as President of the Greater NY Chapter; is a member of ISHC (International Society of Hospitality Consultants); serves on the America's Board of Directors for HSMAI; is Immediate Past President of the Greater New York City chapter of Hotel Sales& Marketing Association International; serves as a member of the Editorial Board of HotelExecutive.com; is a member of the Real Estate Board of NY; and is a regular contributor to the international publications eHotelier, Hotels Online, Hotel Resource Weekly Network News, and Hotel News Now.

Specialties: Strategic Planning; Re-Positionings; New Development; Direct Sales Planning & Implementation; Marketing Plans; Incentive Programs; Revenue Management; Expert Witness
