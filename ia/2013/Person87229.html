Mark Weddington's Summary

LA-based graphic designer from NC with a strong background in both print and online media. Majority of work for agencies with clients ranging from small businesses to Fortune 500 companies. Adept in collaborating both directly with clients on a one-to-one basis as well as working as part of a team with project managers, art directors and/or programmers. Freelance work includes music-oriented independent projects for clients across the country and overseas. Well-rounded experience in terms of aesthetics and client interaction.

Specialties

• Website, eCommerce & UI design.
• Branding, marketing and logo design.
• Print design (advertising, brochures, posters, etc.).
• Excellent proficiency in HTML, CSS, Dreamweaver, Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Quark Xpress, Wordpress, Mailchimp, Campaign Monitor, and Constant Contact.
• Intermediate proficiency in Flash & After Effects.
• Proficient in integration of PHP, Jquery, Ajax, Javascript and Miva.
• Specialist in design for music, film, the arts, and Hispanic & South Asian markets.
