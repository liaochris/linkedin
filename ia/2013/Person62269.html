Jeremi Joslin's Summary

Jeremi Joslin is the Founder and CEO of Collabspot. Collabspot is a tool to help businesses write better emails and sell more.

Previously he was eXo Social's product manager at eXo Platform and managed the Vietnamese office (50 persons). He also has been working on wiki technology at XWiki and before sports training software at Enora Technologies. He also organized the first Barcamp and OpenSocial Hackathon in Vietnam and talked in multiple conference and unconference such as JavaPolis, Barcamp Paris, GTUG Shanghai, cmf2007 and Google DevFest in SEAsia.

He is computer science engineer, has a Master Degree in Computer science from the Dalian University of Light Industry (China) and is graduated from the European Institute of Technologies.

Specialties: Product Development, Startups, Programming and doing whatever it takes.
