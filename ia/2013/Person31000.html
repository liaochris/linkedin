Dave Boyle's Summary

I've been working in the games industry since 2007, both in a freelance and in-house capacity. In that time, I have worked on everything from prop creation and environment work for web and mobile to character modeling and toy prototyping. I endeavor to continue working at professional studios, producing high quality content, while continuing to learn as much as I can from those around me.

Specialties

Character creation for games and film, low/high poly prop and environment modeling, Unity3d, architectural visualization, lighting and rendering, texturing.

Senior 3D Artist

Ganz Studios

Privately Held; 501-1000 employees; Internet industry

September 2007 – November 2012 (5 years 3 months)

During my time at Ganz, I ran the gamut from ultra-low poly environment and character modeling and texturing for mobile and web, to high poly digital sculpting for toy-line prototypes, and everything in between.

As a senior artist, I had additional responsibilities that included training employees, particularly in ZBrush or character work, occasionally managing small teams, as well as documenting workflow and pipeline standards to help speed up production. I was also in charge of all physical toy prototypes, and acted as a liaison between the Art and Marketing departments during these projects. My responsibilities involved modeling 3d prototypes, as well as sending the work to our in-house rapid prototype machine, which I was in charge of using and maintaining.
