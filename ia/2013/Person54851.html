Isaac Katzav's Summary

Isaac Katzav has over ten years of leadership, business and technology experience, as an IDF platoon Commander, entrepreneur and as a lead associate at a top global management consulting firm. In 2000, Isaac completed four years of military service as a tank platoon commander. In 2005, Isaac graduated from Ben Gurion University with a BSc in Software Engineering. Upon graduation, Isaac co-founded Softwear Suit Ltd, which develops analytic solutions for the semiconductor, pharmaceutical and utility industries and led Softwear Suit as its CEO. In 2009, Isaac graduated from the full-time MBA program at the Ross school of Business (University of Michigan) and joined Booz and Company as a full-time associate. During his time with Booz, Isaac led consulting teams in Health, Manufacturing, and Utility industries; wide range of experiences in corporate and technology strategy, mergers and acquisitions, organization strategy, and program evaluation. Isaac is an avid soccer player and enjoys watching sports, traveling, and listening to music in his free time.
