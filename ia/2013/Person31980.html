David Muir's Publications

  • Pieces of Jamaica: The Real Rock Edition

    • Words2Print Media: USA
    • August 2012
    Authors: David Muir, Sonia Morgan

    Pieces of Jamaica™, The Real Rock Edition showcases authentic Jamaica in gripping photographs. From the boys playing football in "Make Shift" to the woman roasting breadfruit in "Breshe", David stills the beauty of everyday life in Jamaica -- rarely captured, rarely celebrated.

    Vivid photographs display Jamaica's rustic reality juxtaposed with spirited humanity and an intrinsic beauty found in its people. That, coupled with the rich, authentic culture breathes life into this intriguing coffee table book.

David Muir's Projects

  • Pieces of Jamaica - Exhibitions of Photographic Art

    • July 2009 to Present
    Team Members: David Muir, Sonia Morgan, Sean Henry, Karen Vick

    The renowned "Pieces of Jamaica" collection captures the candid beauty of the Jamaican people and its rich and diverse landscape, as David unearths art in everyday life on "The Real Rock."

    "Pieces of Jamaica" has been exhibited throughout Florida with the first major show occurring at the Lauderhill Art Center Gallery in Lauderhill FL. The collection has garnered acclaim and excitement from a wide cross-section of art aficionados and general art lovers, prompting subsequent exhibitions at Stoneybrook Golf Club, Orlando; Miramar City Hall in the City of Miramar, FL for Caribbean American Heritage Month; and the African American Research Library & Cultural Center, Fort Lauderdale, FL to name a few of the more prominent shows.
