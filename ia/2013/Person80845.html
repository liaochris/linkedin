Lila Design Associates' Additional Information

Interests:

Lila Design is a world wide renown brand development and creative design company specializing in brand consulting and graphic design. We help our customers to create, build and re-vitalize their brands. Our Dutch based company is established in 1995 by its present Creative Directors, the Founders Mrs. Jeanneke Liebregts and Mr. Norbert Lambriex. We bring distinctive, effective and lasting airline logos, aircraft liveries, airline brand identities. Our customers are VIPs, private jet owners, commercial airlines, business aviation, air cargo companies, head-of-states/governments, the military and public flying serivces. Lila Design's other core discipline is caring for label and packaging design for the beverage industry. We currently have 1 office in 1 country around the world though we deliver our creativity for customers all around the globe. For more information, please visit us at www.liladesign.com.
