Angel Romero Esparza's Summary

Creative director & graphic designer in on/off campaigns, BTL, digital marketing & social media.
I like to work with business minds to help them articulate their messages to their customers, and translate them into identity and creative that is compelling.

Experience in advertising agencies working from concept to final artwork. Print/graphic design, copywriting and web aesthetics experience. Fascinated by the continual evolution of the Internet and digital marketing.

I see myself as an effective project leader, a clear communicator and consensus builder, highly organized, very detail-oriented and able to coordinate multiple projects simultaneously. I love to work with a talented staff and motivate them to challenge their own limits and skills. I feel comfortable working in a demanding, fast-paced environment under tight deadlines.

Skills: 360º campaigns, web and graphic design, branding, digital marketing, art direction, packaging, direct marketing, email marketing, guerrilla and street marketing, social media marketing, digital strategy. High proficiency in Adobe CS 6 (Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign, Adobe Premiere) and familiarity with WordPress, Keynote, Prezi and Microsoft Office (PowerPoint, Word, Excel).
