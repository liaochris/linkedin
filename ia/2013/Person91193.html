Melissa Ambers' Summary

Assistants & Beyond is a Virtual Assistant and Event Planning Company.

I have worked as an assistant for over 15 years at various companies like Coca-Cola, Chevron, and Pennzoil. In addition to my Assistant duties I was responsible for all events, fund raisers, golf tournaments, various meetings, dinners, and parties. I decided to start my own company doing what I have been doing for years and love what I do.

A Virtual Assistant is your Assistant out of the office. We are able to work with clients via email and/or fax. Sometimes we do not have to inconvenience our clients with meetings, but there are time that we have to meet face-to-face.

As a Event Planner we will plan any event (parties, dinner, fund raisers, etc...). We are also available for "Day Of" events.

Visit www.assistsandbeyond.com or email melissa@assistsantsandbeyond.com.
