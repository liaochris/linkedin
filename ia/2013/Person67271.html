John Zajaros' Summary

My primary area of interest & expertise is merging new media, inbound marketing strategies with the best of what many now call "outbound marketing." I call it hybrid marketing.

My company combines inbound marketing strategies and social media management with my extensive knowledge of SEO. The overall emphasis is almost always on traffic generation & conversions, with an eye towards improving the bottom line!

I work with a variety of companies, corporations, and entrepreneurs. Most of my clients are what most people refer to as "brick and mortar businesses" or professional practices.

The Ultimate Internet Image specializes in developing a company's "Internet Image," their brand & brand awareness.

My experience & knowledge combines new media, inbound marketing with the latest technology & over 30 years of sales, advertising, and marketing experience.

Our goal: The implementation of an inbound marketing strategy that is profitable & sustainable.

I understand what it takes for a company to grow & prosper, both offline & online, in good times & in tough times. I am able to help my clients deal with the challenges presented by a difficult economy because I have been through tough economic times before & prospered.

My experience has ranged from sales & marketing to anthropology & academia. The most satisfying work has always been working for myself & building something I can call my own...while assisting others. I still take on coaching and mentoring clients from time-to-time & my love affair with learning continues; it has made me a better, richer person and helps me better-assist my clients.

My primary goal, in business & in life, has been the same for the past 40 years: Help others succeed. By always placing others first, I have achieved a lifetime of riches for the effort!

Contact me anytime at 440-821-7018 (mobile).

Specialties

Internet marketing; Inbound marketing; sales and marketing; advertising and promotion; website development; athletic and sport-specific training; personal strength and fitness training to include weight loss; and medical, osteological, and anthropological research.
