Darcy Eikenberg, ACC's Summary

Innovative yet practical, creative yet structured, fun yet results-focused, I strive to be known and remembered for one key result-for helping show people how to get more life out of work.

With a unique mix of perspectives, passions and practice as a executive coach, leadership speaker, blogger and author, I founded the Red Cape Revolution (www.RedCapeRevolution.com) as a way to inspire all of us to rediscover our own superpowers, bring them to our work, and make a bigger difference in their corner of the world.

I'd love to help you and your colleagues create new ways to improve your experiences at work in ways that work in our real lives, inside our real organizations, in real time. Let's connect soon.

Darcy Eikenberg, ACC's Experience

Executive Coach, Leadership Development Consultant & Speaker

RedCapeRevolution.com

December 2008 – Present (4 years 11 months) Greater Atlanta Area

I'm an executive coach and speaker whose passion is to help smart leaders change their lives at work. Their improvements change not only their own careers and worklives, but their companies and their communities, and I'm grateful to all who've given me the opportunity to help them create those results.

Our executive coaching processes are designed to create positive, forward movement that works for the person and the organization. While our processes are grounded in change management and behavioral motivation principles, our work with clients focuses on creating practical action that fits within today's real-world workplace pressures, schedules, and lifestyles--not unrealistic academic theory.

My focus is to offer a practical, real-life perspective, based on today's realities, pressures, and challenges. With over 20 years’ experience working with top companies such as The Coca-Cola Company, Microsoft, Deloitte Consulting and State Farm, my clients consistently grow to greater levels of clarity, confidence and control in their work and lives—all leading to better business results.

In addition to my executive coaching work, I also founded career and success site RedCapeRevolution.com as a way to help successful professionals at all levels discover their “superpowers” and make a bigger difference in the new world of work. The site and blog are full of tips and tools you'll want to check out--and they're free!

My book, Bring Your Superpowers to Work: Your Guide to More Clarity, Confidence and Control is available on Amazon and BN.com.

I love to hear from you here on LinkedIn, through www.RedCapeRevolution.com, on our Facebook page (www.Facebook.com/RedCapeRevolution) or on Twitter (www.Twitter.com/RedCapeRev.) Oh, and email and the phone still work, too (Darcy@RedCapeRevolution.com, 404.857.2738.)
