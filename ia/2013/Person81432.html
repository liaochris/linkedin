Lisa Brigantino's Summary

Lisa Brigantino and Hidden Pond Productions, Inc. provide effective music/talent supervision, IP licensing/rights clearance, script clearance & product placement, original music scoring & production for clients working in TV, film, advertising, new media, merchandising, non-broadcast platforms, etc.

Hidden Pond Productions, Inc. clears any kind of copyrighted or protected property; from music, celebrities and film clips to photos, fine art, literature and more. Hidden Pond Productions, Inc. has the industry knowledge, experience and contacts to get clients the properties they want at prices that work within their budgets. If clients don't know exactly what property they want to use, the company will search and offer them a variety of options to choose from that will work both creatively and economically in their project.

Lisa Brigantino is an award-winning composer, singer-songwriter & multi-instrumentalist with a Master of Music degree in composition & theory. She writes for TV, film, and more. She is a Kurzweil endorsed artist. Lisa released her latest solo CD "Wonder Wheel" in 2010. The album has received rave reviews including 5 stars and "Pick Of The Week" at Indie Music Critic & MuzikReviews. Lisa placed 7th on Indie-Music's 'Top 25 of 2012'.

In addition to doing her own performances, Lisa performs with her sister Lori in "The Vickie & Nickie Show", a self-contained, musical-comedy cabaret act.

Lisa was an original member of Lez Zeppelin, the world's first all girl, all Led Zeppelin tribute band, and played bass, bass pedals, keyboards, mandolin and sang backup vocals. They toured internationally playing A list venues and festivals (including Bonnaroo) and released their debut CD in 2007 recorded and produced by the legendary Eddie Kramer. They appeared on national and international TV including CBS Sunday Morning and Japan's Music Fair and received outstanding press in Rolling Stone, The London Times, The New Yorker, etc. Lisa left the band in 2009.

Specialties

Music/talent supervision, IP licensing, incl. music publishing/master recording rights, film/stock photography/fine art/literature clearances, estate permissions, public domain research, consultation, contract drafting and review.

A multi-instrumentalist that plays 14 + instruments and an eclectic award-winning composer/singer/songwriter that writes in a variety of genres ranging from Rock, Folk, Blues, Country, Americana, Old-Time, etc., to film & TV scores, ad music and serious New Music.
