► Accelerating Your Inside Sales Team’s Performance
The key to accelerating your inside sales group’s
performance is, in one word, empowerment.

What most sales organizations never figure out is that
the inside sales manager is the very best person to
change selling behaviors and improve performance.

The inside sales manager has the advantage of
knowing the team members personally, has a complete
understanding of the sales process, and is always
present.

The only other thing needed is regular and consistent
tactically based training.

►The Advantages Of Tactical Base Training

Tactically based training focuses on observed
strategic and tactical errors in sales. Because
tactically based training is focused on the real-time
errors made by salespeople in their calls, and not a
preset selection of techniques or sales methodology,
it produces much quicker and higher impact results.

Tactically based training generates its content from
current sales calls. Recorded calls are analyzed for
strategic and tactical errors both in the application of
selling techniques and methods, but most importantly
looking for deviations from the best-known selling
practices of the sales process.

What most companies discover when they examine
their sales team is that salespeople are making
numerous sales process errors that are responsible
for much of their difficulty.

Additionally, the content of live calls also includes
sales technique and methodology errors that can then
be corrected.

If you want the maximum leverage for changing sales
behaviors, running a tactically based training program
that focuses on the sales process via live calls and the
strategic and tactical errors being made will give you
the best results.
