Karen Leong's Summary

Karen Leong is an influential communications coach. Highly regarded for her transformational and results-oriented programs, Karen has worked with MNCs and local conglomerates such as Credit Suisse, McDonald's, AXA Insurance, Standard Chartered Bank, Knight Frank, Singapore Refining Company and Jurong International, helping over 3000 executives expand their influence within and beyond their organisations, fast-tracking their careers to leadership positions.

Karen is also a much sought-after keynote speaker, having inspired diverse audiences including CEOs and senior management executives, oncologists, engineers, real estate professionals, global MBA students at conferences and events.

Karen is the master trainer for the American Society of Training and Development's (ASTD) Improving Human Performance certification programme. She is also a professional member of Asia Professional Speakers - Singapore.

A Certified Public Accountant (CPA), Karen has a decade-long corporate career spanning audit, consulting and corporate education. She specialised in business consulting and initial public offering projects with KPMG and KPMG Consulting in Singapore, Hong Kong and Shanghai. In 2007, she co-founded The Leadership Company which presents business leaders, globally renowned facilitators to help executives achieve peak performance.

Karen contributes career development articles to The Straits Times. She has been featured as an innovative entrepreneur on Mediacorp TV and was interviewed on Mediacorp radio 938FM to share her insights on the art of networking.

Karen believes that it is especially true today that anything can be achieved through the right relationships. Her personal mission is to make the world a more connected place, expanding the possibilities for each person; one person at a time.

Visit www.karenleong.com for more information.
