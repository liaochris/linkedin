Dena Patton's Experience

Speaker/Coach

DenaPatton.com

June 1998 – Present (15 years 7 months) Phoenix, AZ

Dena is a global speaker, author and a transformational business coach who helps women entrepreneurs create GREAT businesses and become GREAT leaders. She is an award-winning entrepreneur who works with women who are changing the world through their lives and businesses. Dena empowers, trains and helps you to create a well-oiled, well-marketed, well-systematized, well-lead, and well-branded GREAT company that changes the world.

Dena is an educational/inspirational speaker who inspires audiences around the globe on one of her favorite topics: Greatness-Centered Entrepreneurship and Marketing, Women's Greatness or Faith Greatness. She has won 9 business awards and can be seen in over 35 featured media stories.

Her first career was in NYC where she owned a PR & marketing company. In 1997, at the age of 26 Dena sold that company to a start-up Internet/technology company and stayed on as their Director of Marketing where she built and led a community of 70,000 members (without social media), which has become the #1 global leader in it’s industry. She has worked with celebrities and top CEO’s, but also loves working with the everyday woman who wants to fulfill her vision in life or business.

She is also passionate about being a social entrepreneur which has fueled a few national or global projects she has founded or cofounded: The September 11 Photo Project Tour (Book by Harper Collins) a 6 city tour which raised money for The NYC Burn Unit. The Summer Spaaah Series in Arizona which has attendance of over 8,000 women and has raised $100k for charity. Chat, Chew and Chocolate, an inspirational friendship club for busy women with 26 chapters and 15, 000 members. In 2008 with 3 friends she cofounded a 501c3, The Girls Rule Foundation, that helps girls build bright futures.

To connect on Facebook www.facebook.com/DenaPatton.

Dena is a member of: The International Coach Federation, eWomen Network Platinum, & Arizona Small Business Association.

CoFounder

Girls Rule Foundation

January 2008 – Present (6 years) Phoenix, Arizona Area

Girls Rule is an organization that provides educational workshops that builds bright futures for teen girls.

We believe in girls. We believe in their dreams.

Girls Rule! is an organization that provides educational workshops that build bright futures and consist of 4 principles:

1. Building & Maintaining Self-Esteem/Self-Confidence
2. Making wise and healthy choices
3. Leadership skills that will help them set and achieve goals
4. Building a step-by-step plan to achieve their dreams, and the resources, tools & education to make that happen


We love working with schools, groups, and organizations who are looking for empowering and life-changing programs for your girls. Inquire at our website www.girlsrulefoundation.org for more information.
