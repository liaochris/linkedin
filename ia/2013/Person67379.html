Jon Angelo Gjetting's Summary

Taking strategies through concept to final creative delivery.

Specialties: Guiding the creative process and developing creative business teams, always with sharp focus on reaching business objectives.

Working fluently and creative with strategies spanning multiple media; Web, direct mail, print, corporate identity, film and events. Thriving in the intersection of strategy, creativity and technology.

With a client roster that range from small start-ups to global fortune 500 corporations, comes my appreciation of the words of Dwight D. Eisenhower; “what counts is not necessarily the size of the dog in the fight -it's the size of the fight in the dog.” Clients include ISS, Ernst & Young, Maersk, Vestas and Samarin.

Entrepreneurial mind-set. Involved in starting ad agencies in Denmark. Helped building a television station in Norway and an IT-consultancy in Singapore.

Experienced in working cross culturally. Completed projects in Austria, Denmark, Germany, Italy, Malaysia, Norway, Portugal, Singapore, Spain, Sweden, Turkey, United Kingdom and the United States of America.
