Jeannette Paladino's Summary

Social media writing is my beat. I help organizations to leverage social media to increase brand awareness, grow their business and engage employees as brand advocates on blogs, LinkedIn, Twitter, Google+ and other social media networks. Write LinkedIn Professional Headlines and Summaries. Seasoned blogger. Social media project manager. Began my career as a business reporter.

Highlights:
-- Helped develop Twitter strategy for a major money center bank for its participation in Sibos, the annual conference for the global financial services industry.
-- Write weekly blog for highly-regarded training consultancy.
-- Created brand identity and messaging for an executive coach and wrote his website content and LinkedIn profile.
-- Created brand statement and messaging for leading sustainability consultancy. Wrote website content, LinkedIn profiles of top executives. Write weekly blog.

Previously:
-- Senior director of Citigate Communications; managed the Deloitte Consulting account.
-- Senior vice president-corporate communications, Marsh & McLennan, the world’s leading insurance brokerage firm.
-- Senior vice president, Bowery Savings Bank, managing public relations, advertising, direct response, market research and sales promotion programs. Also directed the School of Banking, responsible for management development, selling skills, and technical training.
-- Began career as a writer for a daily business newspaper.
