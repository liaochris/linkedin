Cyrus Bakhtiyari's Summary

Cyrus Bakhtiyari is a technologist and Infrastructure Engineer at Addictive Mobility where he has overall responsibility for the Engineering, QA, Monitoring and Deployment of Addictive Mobility's entire online Infrastructure. Cyrus is also an adviser to a number of start-ups.

Prior to Addictive Mobility, Cyrus was the founder and president of Extreme Serverz, a global hosting company hosting some of the top game servers in the world. Cyrus built this company at the age of 13 and grew it to be a leader in its class shortly after.

Cyrus has a broad working knowledge of the software and IT industry based on over 10 years of experience in leading engineering and product/project management in rapidly growing start-ups. He has led and contributed to numerous strategic undertakings pertaining to a wide range of mission critical SaaS system development and deployment scenarios.
