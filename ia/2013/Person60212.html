JC Covey's Summary

From the “The New River City” of Radford, Virginia, JC Covey creates and delivers music with the heart and genuine emotion of a True American Artist. Keeping with his signature acoustic and vocal stylings, JC crafts and performs memorable songs and cover arrangements that people can connect and identify with. His experiences in life along with the genre influences of Classic Rock, Blues, Country, Bluegrass & Old Time, Gospel, and Folk have developed into a creative brand of music that listeners will appreciate right from the start. JC’s early interest in music came from a Mother that adored Elvis, a Father who loved The Temptations, and a musician Grandfather who played with early greats such as Bill Monroe and Bob Wills.

JC's performance experience includes local blues society jams, coffee houses, book stores, car shows, conferences, festivals, and many other public and private events. After becoming a regular performer at all the local Borders Book Stores in Southern Nevada, he later hosted and performed at the popular Saxby's Coffee Open Mic in Henderson followed by the successful Las Vegas Weekly Cars & Coffee Show. In Southwest Virginia, he was fortunate enough to be selected to perform at Blacksburg's Annual Steppin' Out Festival, Radford's Bike Festival, and privileged to be scheduled to play twice at The Floyd Country Store, featured location on "The Crooked Road", Virginia's Heritage Music Trail.

JC's Debut EP is now available for download on iTunes, AmazonMP3, Rhapsody, and most all other digital music distributors. All music available for film and television licensing as well as other media and marketing projects. All Music and Words by JC Covey are registered with the U.S. Copyright Office and American Society of Composers, Authors, and Publishers. For more information, please contact JC at (540) 250-8515 or jccoveymusic@yahoo.com.
