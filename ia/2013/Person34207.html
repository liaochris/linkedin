Dennis Glowacki's Summary

Dennis is a 3D environment artist with shipped titles and contract employment experience. Over the past eight years Dennis has released several projects including Warm Gun, bitFLIP, and Goldeneye: Source. As an avid geocacher and photographer Dennis is always exploring new and inspiring environments while filling his personal texture library. He is a 2010 graduate of Tribeca Flashpoint.

Specialties

♦ Proficient in Maya, Photoshop, Mudbox, Unreal Engine 3 and Source Engine
♦ Experience in 3DS Max, XSI, Zbrush, Cryengine 2, Torque and Unity
♦ Strong sense of modeling, texturing and lighting for both low poly and high poly environments
