David Sarna's Summary

David E Y Sarna is a writer, speaker and technology investor
He has investments in a range of technology companies.

Earlier in his career, Mr. Sarna successfully founded three companies, sold one and took two of them public. He had a couple of failures too.

He has published six books, and hundreds of articles and is nearly finished with his first novel.

For literary projects and rights he is exclusively represented by the Waterside Literary Agency (Bill Gladstone bgladstone@waterside.com +1 760 632-9190)

WoodallTech is in the business of manufacturing hydrogen on demand, and building a scalable line of reactors for making hydrogen.

Another effort uses technology to improve the quality of Kosher supervision using RFID and other technologies.

Several other investments are still in stealth mode.
