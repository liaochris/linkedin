Mullath Vinod's Summary

Redefining wireless connectivity – the concept and life line on which Cellcomm grows. Our core focus, over decades’ long operations, is at achieving and fine tuning ourselves to keep this concept live.

Our uncompromising attitude in adhering to global standards and our quality conscious approach for the products and services offered, have helped in sustaining/maintaining our position in the turbulent and highly competent market all through these years.

Cellcomm’s business verticals, viz, Wireless (coverage enhancement solutions) and Connectivity (Machine2Machine communication solutions) have grown and developed into a stage where we are capable of executing large projects in each domain. The team of highly qualified professionals with expertise in handling major projects is the invaluable asset of Cellcomm. Various Airports/Tunnels & IBS projects handled/executed in India and abroad and the 150,000 plus telecom shelter monitoring solutions provided by Cellcomm stand as testimonials, of the highest level of confidence that customers place on us.
