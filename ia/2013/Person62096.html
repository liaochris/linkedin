Jenny Kowalczuk's Summary

I bring research insights to your projects and draw meaning from all kinds of qualitative data. With trusted colleagues I design and run qualitative research projects for perceptions and service user improvement and experience work. I work with teams to analyse and synthesize all kinds of qualitative data to generate insights and recommendations to support decision making and development of all kinds of strategy.

I love a good semi structured survey and take descriptive data and can turn it into persuasive, informative or entertaining reports that you can use to inform strategy and push out to stakeholders and customers as briefings, papers, articles and sound-bites. (I’m not a computer, so if you need of anything representative of a known universe, or have a penchant for questions that ask for scoring of multiple options, then best talk to those that do databases and automated scoring).

I’m interested in working with local authorities new to public health, teams and service managers searching for opportunities to improve service delivery, agencies looking for new ways to demonstrate value to stakeholders, companies and not for profits wanting to know more about where they fit into the minds of funders, users, supporters and customers and others with questions that need answers or insight.

I also support clients with a wide range of well written web content and information products. If you need support with communication materials please get in touch. I have a wealth of experience in delivering high quality, SEO happy, clear copy for clients - happy to discuss writing projects of all sizes.

My background is publishing – commercial non fiction for Harper Collins, internal for NHS, product development with BMJ – and research – with NHS again, communications strategy, small scale evaluations, service improvement, user testing, product development and perceptions.

Get in touch, connect, I’m looking forward to working with you.
