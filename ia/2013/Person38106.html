Dr Mani Sivasubramanian's Summary

Dr.Mani is a pediatric heart surgeon actively engaged in fundraising to sponsor heart surgery for under-privileged children in India.

A desire to help unfortunate children born with heart defects fuels his efforts to enroll supporters in an ambitious and difficult challenge - to eliminate the deathly spectre of congenital heart disease (CHD).

With tireless energy and enthusiasm, an ardent group of volunteers and donors have embraced this noble purpose that is bigger than any individual or group, and grown it into a global movement that has touched and saved the lives of 87 little children.

Dr.Mani would be delighted if you participate, too.

"If you'd like to join in and be a part of this effort, I'd love to connect with you. Please drop me an email, or post me a note right here on LinkedIn and we'll take it from there. I'm also on Twitter, Facebook and Google+"

Institute of Child Health, Chennai, is Asia's largest pediatric multi-specialty hospital and the cardio-thoracic surgery department typically carries out more than five hundred cardiac procedures every year on little children, infants and newborns from under-privileged families.

The hospital is among a very small group of institutions where expensive heart surgery is provided totally free of cost to deserving families, the cost being borne by the State Department of Health and Family Welfare.
