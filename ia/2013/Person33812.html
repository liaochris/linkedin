Rune Degett's Summary

First the idea - then the technology

Not everyone thinks conceptually and understands technology at the same time. In my creative career I’ve constantly challenged the ordinary by developing new and inspiring ideas that solve problems from new angles; sometimes with ideas best served through a classic media mix and very often with ideas that involve innovative technology. Either way, my work has always revolved around a strong concept or idea. It is my belief that the creative blend of concisely conceptualized art and digital masonry pretty much can solve anything.

I'm an experienced Hybrid Creative Director with an entrepreneurial mindset. I have excellent communication skills on both strategic and creative level. I create strong concepts and great executions of either holistic 360 cross media concepts or stand alone ideas. Over the years my work portfolio has won numerous internationally acclaimed awards. I work on everything from TV and print to mobile and emerging digital platforms. People I work with say I'm a nice guy and an aesthete.

I've worked for clients such as BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Peugeot, P&G, Heineken, Sparkasse Savings Bank, Sony Ericsson, Gore-Tex and Sony in agencies like Jung von Matt, Saatchi & Saatchi, LBi, Y&R and Wieden+Kennedy.
