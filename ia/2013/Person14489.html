Bill VanGorder's Summary

Bill VanGorder is becoming known as the principal force behind the growth of Nordic Pole Walking -- the fastest growing outdoor fitness activity in Nova Scotia. He and his wife, Esther, are certified national Nordixx Nordic Pole Walking instructors and their MRC Associates Inc. is the major distributor of Nordic Pole Walking poles/supplies in Atlantic Canada.
Nordic Pole Walking is a low-impact exercise that offers the highest benefits for health, wellness and fitness. Nordic Walking Poles enable you to incorporate over 90% of all your body muscles. It’s ‘turbo-powered’ walking, using poles to propel yourself burning up to 46% more calories than normal walking because you’re working your upper body as well as your legs – but the support from the poles means it actually feels easier.
Bill has a background in health/fitness. He was the CEO of the Lung Association of NS for 15 years; prior to that he was Atlantic Area Director of the YMCA for 12 years. He has been involved in Nordic Pole Walking for the past 6 years.
Bill and Esther lead Nordic Pole Walking clinics throughout Nova Scotia and the Maritimes on a mission to bring Nordic Pole Walking to the area

In addition, as one of only two consultant's in Canada with both a CAE (Certified Association Executive of CSAE -- Canadian Society of Association Executives) and CFRE (Certified Fund Raising Executive of the AFP -- Association of Fundraising Professionals), he helps both for-profit and not-for-profits change the way they hire to find and keep the best staff.
Bill's 40+ years’ experience as a manager, a CEO and consultant give him the experience to help you know about your people to make your organization more successful. To help you get smarter about the way you hire, engage and retain the best people. Bill will help you learn how to attract the right candidates, how to identify top performers versus poor performers, and how to use assessment tools to improve organizational performance.
