Mark Trogdon's Summary

Mark Trogdon is the principal and owner of Magnolia Management Partners. Mark is an innovative leader with over thirty years of experience in numerous financial services companies. He is an experienced executive and senior level manager with broad and diverse capabilities and insights. He has lead major organizational development projects, change projects, process improvement projects and multi-layered departments and divisions and has consistently built, developed and grown stronger and more profitable business enterprises and organizations.

He was a member of the executive management team that launched Civic Bank & Trust in Nashville in 2005. Once the bank was released from its De Novo status by state and federal regulators he decided that it was time to do something different. After leaving Civic he launched Magnolia Management Partners.

To the detriment of his own well-being, Mark had spent the previous 20 years focused on his career and the companies he worked for. During this time he gained more than 100 pounds as "life" had taken over and he stopped taking care of himself.

The year following his decision to do something different, he decided to take control of his situation; and he lost all of the excess weight he gained through the years. As Mark was successful at keeping the weight off, he found that many people were interested in his approach and how he achieved such dramatic results because they faced the same challenge in their lives. These many conversations inspired Mark to write and publish his first book, 100 After 50; A Practical Approach to Weight Management.

Writing, publishing and marketing 100 After 50 has provided Mark with an opportunity to enhance existing marketing skills in addition to developing competencies in wellness education and information, website development and management, social media and search engine optimization.
