Jonny Strömberg's Summary

I'm one of those lucky guys that found my passion early. My interest for design, technology and human behaviour have grown continuously since 1998, and today I'm pragmatic designer who loves building stuff for the web.

During the last decade I have been involved in starting a bunch of projects and at the same time almost earned two masters degrees at Umeå university. One in Interaction technology and design, and one in Computer science.

I like to be (and usually am) a part of the entire process from writing an idea at a napkin, to design and implementation. My specialties are user experience and graphical design, often translated to Photoshop and Javascript.

Short about myself: 27 years old and lives in Stockholm. Ambitious but light-hearted and jocular. Enjoys friends, new experiences and bad action movies. I like to do stuff and meet new people, which means that I regularly find myself organising hackathons, parties, dinners, meetups and movie nights. I’m a geek that loves people.

I’m not looking for a new job. But it is always fun to talk with interesting people working with cool projects. If you are exciting enough, who knows what will happen?
