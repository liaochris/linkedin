Beverly (Bev) Kennedy, MBA, CISSP, CISM, CISA, QSA's Summary

Mr. Beverly S. (Bev) Kennedy is an information security professional with a background in information security, management, accounting, software development, system and network administration, and consulting. Bev holds the CISSP, CISM, CISA, and the PCI QSA Certifications.

His recent projects include information security consulting, gap analysis, and PCI assessments for some of the largest retail, telecommunication, insurance, theme park, and healthcare companies in the world. Bev’s clients also include restaurant chains, convenience stores, Internet commerce companies, and smaller companies as well. In the past eighteen months, he has traveled to Great Britain, Japan, Puerto Rico, Brazil, and Canada to service his larger clients.

Before becoming a world traveler, Bev developed a ground up Information Security Department including Governance, Program, Risk, Control, and Response Managment as well as implementing and managing.Single Sign On, Access Control, and Identity Management as Information Security Officer for a major Healthcare System. He consulted at Tulane University in New Orleans for several years on the operation and security of their campus network. And for several years he taught a course at Tulane on Information Security. Bev’s undergraduate degree in Business Management (1997) and his MBA (2001) are from Southeastern Louisiana University.

He says “I enjoy meeting organizational needs with the secure use of technology.”
