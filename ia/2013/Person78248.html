Landy Chase's Summary

For the last twenty years, President and CEO of Landy Chase Incorporated, a professional services firm specializing in the following:

- As a professional speaker, challenging executives to embrace the new mind-set required to leverage social media for new business development within the sales function. I provide a complete blueprint for acquiring new clients through social media, transforming the sales force from 'order-takers' to high-value generators for your customers.

Keynote: The Selling Revolution
(www.sellingrevolution.com)

My other, more traditional topics focus on skill-based persuasion strategies. I deliver high-value content. Formats include keynotes, breakout sessions and workshops for sales meetings and industry conferences. I unconditionally guarantee my client's satisfaction with every program.

I have given over 2,000 presentations to clients in over 60 industries on 5 continents while maintaining a re-hire rate in excess of 90% over the last 20 years. Complete customization to your industry and audience, including interviews with staff and attendees, is a standard part of my client delivery process.

Popular topics include:
-Winning on Value vs. Losing on Price
-Winning Competitive Sales
-How to Find, Hire and Develop Top Sales People
-Advanced Negotiation and Closing Skills
-The Selling Revolution: Social Media for Sales People

-Co-author of The Social Media Sales Revolution, released by McGraw-Hill in July 2011 and available on amazon and all major bookstores. Ranked #3 on "Top 10 Sales Books of 2011" by fillthefunnel.com

- Author of Competitive Selling, published by McGraw-Hill in 2010 and available in print and Kindle download on Amazon.com. Winner, Editor's Choice Best Business Books of 2010 by Small Business Trends / Businessinsider.com

- Holds the CSP designation from the National Speakers Association, the highest earned level of excellence in the industry, representing the top eight percent of professional speakers world-wide

Specialties

Highly effective and entertaining professional speaker and thought leader on the subjects of effective sales and marketing strategies; sales management, sales force development; and using social media for new business development and client relationship management.
