Mark Coleman's Experience

President

Convergence Mitigation Management (CMM) LLC

June 2013 – Present (7 months) Auburn, NY

Convergence Mitigation Management (CMM) LLC is a risk and sustainability advisory services company.

CMM helps clients identify, understand, assess, and prioritize specific risks to their business and strategies to mitigate and manage those risks. Small and large businesses are impacted by a myriad of converging social, economic, and environmental challenges that can disrupt their ability to achieve optimal performance and impact. CMM advises on the full range of options that exist for businesses to manage risk, establish a plan of action, implement targeted strategies, measure and report their progress, and communicate their impact and success.

Throughout his career Mr. Coleman has developed a strong focus on the critical areas of energy, environment, and sustainability. His career has spanned strategic and leadership positions in government, applied research, technology development, and management consulting organizations.

HARBEC’s mission is to provide tightly toleranced prototypes, tooling, machined components and quality injection molded parts in a sustainable manner with a social conscience.
