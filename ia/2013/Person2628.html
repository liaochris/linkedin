Aimee Spinks' Summary

http://www.aimeespinks.com

I work independently as a unit stills and specials photographer with experience on features, shorts and music videos.

I pride myself on my ability to work both unobtrusively on set to capture the heart of a production, as well as taking control and directing large specials shoots both on location and in a studio setting.

My cinematic style and attention to detail has lead me to create work featured as standalone photographic frames within feature films in place of video footage and has gained me work as the director of photography on several small independent productions.

My understanding of narrative and cinematic approach to my work has also seen me create work advertising designer brands and labels. Through my experience in film, I am able to create high quality photography that succinctly conveys the product, service or brand message across a single or series of images in way that connects you to your target market.

My work has been published in various books, magazines and blogs as well as being exhibited across the country. I have lead workshops and given presentations to groups of young people looking to start in the creative industries.

Running a successful business from the ground up has not only improved my skills as a photographer, but also given me valuable experience in every aspect of business and project management.

I have successfully managed my own sales and marketing strategies to generate consistent leads and converted them to into highly successful projects delivered to a range of clients. I have marketed to and gained work at both consumer and business level and continue to develop my portfolio to grow my business year on year.

Alongside my work in film and TV with 'Aimee Spinks Photography,' I have also established, studio photography business, ShotBox Studios, which provides regular contract work for photographers and makeup artists within fashion and portraiture.

Specialties

Advertising photography, film photography, unit stills photography, specials photography, action photography, fashion photography, model portfolios, actor portfolios, concept development, art direction, project management, lighting design, marketing, producing, marketing, advertising
