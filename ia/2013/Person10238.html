Dawn Chitwood's Volunteer Experience & Causes

  • Volunteer Experience

    • organizer

      Helping Hurricane Irene Victims
      • Disaster and Humanitarian Relief
      August 2011 – October 2011 (3 months)

      In the wake of Hurricane Irene, Hatteras Island residents were left without a road for several weeks. The loss of Labor Day weekend was financial disaster for business who didn't suffer damage from the storm. In an effort to help those that were directly affected by the storms and for those who indirectly suffered the effects, Dawn conceived a plan to offer food platters to be delivered to hurricane victims, service workers, and volunteers promoting the venture almost solely through Facebook. With a team of 6 volunteers and collaboration from over a dozen local businesses the group delivered over 4,000 meals door to door and to designated commissaries, purchased and coordinated delivery via ferry of a much needed refrigerator and freezer to the community building where Red Cross was serving three meals a day and 7 days a week, and spread business to dozens of local businesses who otherwise would have had next to zero income.
