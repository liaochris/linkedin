David Bullock (Profit Engineer)'s Summary

What makes a ProfitEngineer?

Simply apply step-by-step engineering principles to building business. Positioning, Traffic, Metrics, Conversions, Messaging, Design. All of these components have to come together and be leveraged in the proper sequence.

Being an actual engineer is a blessing considering that our world has become increasing technical in nature. The saving grace is that your business can be run by the numbers because the numbers don't lie. Either it is working or it is not.

"Adapt or die" is the motto now.

Based on our project work, companies that are "new" have special challenges that are not at all like companies that have an established footprint. This means that one plan for success does not fit every business.

Yes, your business is actually unique and there are particular pitfalls to avoid as you move into these new shifting marketplaces. But, the principles are timeless.

We find that making the wrong move and/or not moving at all can be costly.

Great ideas are everywhere but then there comes the time to pull it all together, execute and become profitable. This is where businesses large and small mis-step and end up wasting time, effort and money going down blind alleys that if they (or someone on staff) only had the experience "in the field" they would make different more profitable choices based on knowledge not a "good idea".

Having started in manufacturing, moving to product development and then to project management and then to sales & marketing allows for understanding and insight to move your business to increased profitability. Add to that experience with large website buildout, traffic generation and the conversation about increasing the profit in your business.

Currently, I am looking to work with business owners and business development managers that have valuable cutting edge products, services and offers.

Sell Well. Build Big.

I look forward to connecting with you.
