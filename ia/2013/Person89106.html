Matthew Becker, PMP's Summary

An accomplished IT Manager with over 14 years of leading teams through successful initiatives and projects in Data Warehouse Management, IT Consulting, and Program/Project Management. Experience spans a wide range of engagements within health care, pharmaceutical, data solutions and asset management industries.

Recent achievements in 2012 include becoming ScrumMaster certified through Scrum Alliance, and receiving The President's Volunteer Service Award as Chairman & Executive Director of Quest for Tech. Currently leading a large business intelligence development team through a multi-year implementation of an enterprise data warehouse and improved analytics reporting to business users.

My style is built upon strong leadership, reliable delivery and effective change that enables organizaions to bridge gaps between IT and the business operations. This is exhibited thorough a history of successful projects and a balanced management style that builds effective relationships at all levels of the organization.

Specialties: Business Intelligence, Date Warehouse Management, Program/Portfolio Management [PMO], Project Management, Resource Management, SLDC, Strategic IT Assessments and Planning, Vendor Management Consulting, IT Budget and Contract Management, IT Implementation/Operations
