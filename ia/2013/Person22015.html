Charissa Peterson's Summary

I am a Graphic and Interactive Designer with a heart for thoughtful beauty. Art and Design is more than technique and medium. It is a way of thinking, a thought that is manifested through visual representation to communicate a message and serve a purpose. This visual representation can take many forms such as: branding, interactive sites, packaging, print, photography and painting. The form must be driven by its message and purpose.

Design & Concepting:
I create visual messages using color, shape, texture, photography, pattern, iconography and typography to fulfill the client’s goals and objectives. These visual messages can take many forms such as branding, interactive sites, packaging and printed materials.

Information Architecture & Content Strategy:
I combine art and technology to create spaces that are both functional and memorable. I analyze websites, assess content, architect site maps and wireframes to create purposeful experiences for the user.

Production & Technical Skills:
I have experience preparing print and interactive files that lead to an end product that maintains the integrity of the initial design. I am skilled in using CS6 Photoshop, Illustrator, and Indesign.
