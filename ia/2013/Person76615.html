Founder

K+A Visionary Branding

December 1980 – May 2006 (25 years 6 months) Newport Beach, CA

The Board of Directors of Toyota Motor Corporation in Japan originally hired two global branding firms; each was to design one Lexus logo. Having earned a long history of successes with Toyota USA., my firm - K+A Visionary Branding - was selected, along with our former sister-company Hunter & Associates - a PR firm - to conceive and create identical visual display panels displaying each of the two proposed logos in visual context. These visual display panels would be used in global, major-market, focus group testing.

The moment I was shown the two proposed logos, I ironically drew upon my mentoring from the EVP Marketing at Toyota, USA. I told the Lexus launch team, code-named “Circle K” that neither logo conveyed the Lexus brand essence; sophistication, luxury, aerodynamics and highly advanced technology.

Our two companies presented an informal strategy & creative brief to Circle K. We achieved consensus that both proposed logos posed a significant threat to creating the Lexus brand essence. Finally, the Circle K team allowed me to create a third logo if I agreed to shut up and leave them alone.

During the nine-month, global, major-market focus group testing of the three designs, my firm's final Lexus logo design scored 81%.
