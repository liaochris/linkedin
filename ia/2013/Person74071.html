Kelvin Thom's Summary

Kelvin Thom is the Owner & President of Digistar Solutions. Prior to starting Digistar Solutions, Kelvin worked in Corporate America in the Telecom industry. Kelvin possesses an extensive and diverse Information Technology background. With over 15 years of technical and operational experience, Kelvin has a successful track record of completing technical projects in a timely fashion. He has participated in a variety of technical projects such as server migrations, email platform conversions, operating system upgrades, desktop & laptop deployments, application rollouts and end-user training.

As a Business Operations Analyst, Kelvin routinely created custom Excel reports and designed PowerPoint Presentations for high-level corporate executives. Kelvin developed a dynamic Excel application to monitor product & service performance. His application automated the reporting and analysis of key metrics data for the business. The application reduced overhead and administrative costs for the company.

As a provider of Virtual Technology Assistant Services for Entrepreneurs, Kelvin leverages his technical aptitude and diverse technical skill-sets to provide knowledgeable and friendly technical assistance to independent business owners. Kelvin has assisted numerous entrepreneurs in developing their online brand and establishing their online presence via their website and a variety of social media platforms.
