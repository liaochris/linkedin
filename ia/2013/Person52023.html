Tony Hayes' Experience

Director

Diamond Solutions Uk Ltd

September 2005 – Present (7 years 11 months) Bury Lancashire

Established in 2005, DSUK provides telecom services to customers across the North of England.

Whether the customer wants the latest mobile phone or increased efficiency by using Voip technology, we can help them.

Agreements with the 6 mobile networks but predominately dealing with EE, Orange, Vodafone and O2 we can meet the requirements and expectations of our customers whether on a contract or Sim free.

We pride ourselves on our after sales service as we help our customers during there period of contract by taking the stress away from them by dealing with the network on their behalf and providing them with latest information from the networks.

We have recently added Fleet Management services to our portfolio and this has enabled us to reach out to a new market and help customers with there staff, fleet and running costs.

The traditional services of calls, lines and numbers are also supplied at very competitive rates as we always discuss with our customers the benefits and advantages of each service, for example the use of an 03 number compared to a 08 number and the reasons why to call a 03 number is better if your customers are ringing from a mobile.
