Matthew Taylor's Summary

I am a leader in integrative health and rehabilitation as a senior physical and yoga therapist, as well as a doctor of individual and organizational change. My goal is to help individuals and organizations thrive in environments of increasing rates and depths of change. This change can not happen in a piece meal fashion without adverse health effects on the individual and their related social systems.

Specialties

Individual and organizational transformational learning; management of chronic pain; yoga therapy in modern western languaging; expert witness for yoga related injuries; peer reviewer for complementary and alternative medicine journals
