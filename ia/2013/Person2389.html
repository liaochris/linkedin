Ago Cluytens' Summary

1 in 4 sales ends in "doing nothing". 60-80% of the buying cycle now happens online. And in 2012, 54% of all forecasted opportunities were lost (CSO Insights). When I speak privately with partners, directors and CEOs of professional services firms, many tell me business is down. Way down.

--

But there is a small minority that is thriving. They understand that the fundamentals of business development have changed. Forever. They embrace the reality of today - not the memory of yesterday.

As a former Big-4 management consultant and executive, I have sat in on thousands of pitches and signed off or influenced the purchase of tens of millions of professional services. Having been a seller and a buyer has given me a deep understanding of what things look like on "both sides of the table." And what goes on behind closed doors.

--

I now help (managing) partners, directors, CEOs and consultants answer:

- how can we "stop the bleeding" and reverse the outflow of clients and revenue ?
- how can we keep more aggressive competitors from gaining market share ?
- how can I get my team (people) to go out and be more proactive in business development ?
- how can we help our clients see (how much) better we really are than our competitors ?

--

I have worked with Ernst & Young, Toyota, Telenet, Arthur Andersen, ING, IBM, Kenexa, Keytrade Bank, JP Morgan, Deloitte, Accenture, Procter & Gamble, SAP, Oracle, Firmenich and Aramark - as well as dozens of smaller clients.
