Dan Gattis' Experience

Founder

Gattis Law Firm, PC

January 2003 – Present (10 years 10 months) Georgetown, Texas

Gattis Law Firm, PC represents both plaintiffs and defendants in a wide variety of civil matters, handling complex commercial litigation as well as serious personal injury and wrongful death cases. Having represented both individuals and businesses in matters from the simple to the extremely complex and with stakes ranging into multiple millions of dollars, the firm understands how to be an aggressive advocate for its clients’ interests. While both the firm and its founder are well known for their communication and advocacy skills, the firm is also sought out by many businesses to help evaluate and proactively design programs to avoid costly litigation.

President

Gattis Ranches, LLC

January 2011 – Present (2 years 10 months) Texas

Gattis Ranches, LLC is an agricultural enterprise focused on beef cow/calf and stocker cattle operations on both owned and leased land throughout Central Texas.

President

Gattis Cattle Company, LLC

January 1997 – Present (16 years 10 months)

Gattis Cattle Company, LLC is a family owned purebred beef cattle operation raising Beefmaster Cattle in Central Texas. The company also raises and uses registered Quarter Horses in conjunction with its cattle operations.

State Representative

Texas House of Representatives

Government Agency; 51-200 employees; Legislative Office industry

January 2003 – January 2011 (8 years 1 month)

Served 4 terms in the Texas House of Representatives representing the Central Texas counties of Milam and Williamson. Won every election by over 60% of the vote. Served on the following committees: Appropriations, State Affairs, Natural Resources, Civil Practices, and Business & Commerce. Demonstrated the ability to learn and master complex subjects quickly and the skill to advocate a position in such a way as to influence and sway opinions and move legislative colleagues and others into action. Established and currently maintains numerous and diverse relationships with individuals and leaders in government, business, agriculture, sports, news media, and entertainment throughout the State of Texas and beyond.

Dan Gattis' Education

South Texas College of Law

J.D., Law

1991 – 1994

Attended South Texas College of Law in Houston, Texas. Dan was awarded the American Jurisprudence Award in Constitutional Law and in Civil Procedure in his 1L year in recognition of achieving the highest grade in his class and received a best speaker award in the Garland Walker Mock Trial Competition. While attending law school full time, Dan also worked as a law clerk after his 1L year for a Houston litigation firm approximately 20 to 30 hours per week.

Texas A&M University

B.S., Agriculture Economics

1986 – 1990

Attended Texas A&M University in College Station, Texas. Dan was elected by his class as President of the Class of 1990 and to the Student Senate. He was a member of the Corps of Cadets where he achieved the rank of Commanding Officer of Squadron Seven and was a member of the Ross Volunteer Company, which serves as the honor guard for the Governor of Texas. Dan participated in many other student organizations in a leadership role including the Aggie Muster Committee and as a Fish Camp Counselor.
