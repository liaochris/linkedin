Joe Coakley's Summary

My objective is to provide exceptional service and bottom line results to my clients, maximizing my extensive development experience and creating long term relationships. Clients include private land owners, builders and developers. I specialize in residential land for multi-family and single family housing, and new home communities. I also work for several banks in the disposition of REO land inventory.
Cumulative sales from 1999-2012 exceeded $400 million dollars.I consider myself a builder by background and have overseen construction operations in a multi-state environment with direct bottom line responsibility of 10,500 homes built over a period extending 34 years. This includes serving as Vice President of Construction for Del Webb Corporation, building 825 homes per year at their Sun City Las Vegas community. This is my competitive edge in the industry today, being able to understand the business from many perspectives.

Specialties: Exceptional knowledge of residential development. Land specialist. Other interests include low impact development, creative in-fill communities, cottage housing, and multi-family development opportunties. I am a member of the Commericial Brokers Association.
