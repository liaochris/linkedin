Charlie Judy, SPHR, CPA's Summary

Charlie Judy is relentlessly dedicated to heightening the relevance of Human Resources and Talent Management to the workplace. He is challenging the norms to which the HR profession has fallen prey by sparking and advancing the colorful debate around how a more customized and personalized approach can (really) bring lasting value to the talent base. He is a multidimensional executive seasoned by extraordinarily challenging, diverse, and global experiences. He is agile in leveraging the appropriate mix of gravitas and mirthfulness to influence, motivate, and lead.

Charlie brings two decades of broad and progressive experience as an HR strategist and steward to organizations that are heavily reliant on their human capital for their success. He is currently the Chief Human Resources Officer with Baker Tilly where he and his team are focused on transforming and advancing the delivery and value of talent management to this firm of close to 2,000 professionals. Charlie was previously a Human Capital Director with Navigant Consulting, the SVP of Shared Services and Human Resources for TLC Vision Corporation, a publicly traded company, and the VP of HR for Maverick Technologies-the largest and most prominent independent Controls Systems Integrator in North America. Charlie began his career with Deloitte as a Certified Public Accountant (CPA) serving some of the firm's most prominent clients; he transitioned into internal Human Resources and rapidly ascended to leadership roles where he made a career of managing ambiguity and change; he also brought success to long-term global assignments in Brussels, Belgium and Hyderabad, India.

Charlie is a graduate of Tulane University’s A.B. Freeman School of Business and has been formally trained at Cornell University’s School of Industrial and Labor Relations; he is also a certified Senior Professional of HR (SPHR).
