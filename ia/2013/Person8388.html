Anne Reuss' Summary

My heart pounds with excitement when the opportunity to interact with people and engage unfolds. Every local food joint, new city, or business conference I attend is a rush because I know each spot represents a chance to meet new people. The challenge and willingness to adapt spawns innovation. And social media fuels my adrenaline.

I set course to assist businesses, causes and individuals in refining their digital identities and content delivery. I incorporate a unique customer experience lens on digital end users as result of working with 360Connext.

I was chosen to be a Steamfeed author to join the revolution against fake social media 'ninjas' and often act as a guest blogger on other sites.

While managing my own client base feeds my adventerous spirit and replenishes my thirst for diverse initiatives, I have an interest in joining interactive company that shares raw energy, kindness and passion. Hopefully I could earn the business I find work with enough profits to lavish its employees with a slurpee machine...It would be my nirvana!

By now you've probably figured out I'm Deaf. It will be an asset to you. I have a keen eye for human behavior, detail and enhanced listening abilities in the digital space. That and an unwavering passion to connect with people despite emotional or physical barriers.
