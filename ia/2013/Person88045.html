Mary Davis' Experience

Principal

Mary Davis Interior Design

May 2009 – Present (4 years 8 months) Brooklyn, NY

Mary Davis Interior Design works on residential, commercial and medical projects throughout the New York Tri-State area. For these projects, I develop design schemes, budgets and presentations for new and on-going projects. Shopping for fabrics, furniture, lighting, and accessories. Manage materials library and prop inventory. Procurement, order tracking, and coordination for concurrent projects. Manage contractors, and supervise installations. I maintain strong relationships with clients, and a roster of high quality builders, fabricators, vendors and freelance designers. Mary is a graduate of Parsons where she is a guest lecturer; and a Certified Aging-in-Place Specialist (CAPS), a certification granted by the National Association of Homebuilders.
