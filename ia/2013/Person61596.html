Jennifer Filla's Summary

I believe every fundraising office should have the benefits of professional prospect research. As a result I founded Aspire Research Group to assist organizations across the country that are concerned about finding the right prospects, worried about what size gift to ask for, or struggling to meet major gift goals.

At Aspire Research Group we are deeply committed to advancing the fundraising profession. Check out our training schedule, sign-up for our free bi-monthly newsletter, or view a sample prospect profile and other free resources at http://www.AspireResearchGroup.com

Specialties: Prospect research, consultant, consulting, donor prospect profiles, data analysis, training, workshop, seminar, webinar, wealth and prospect screening implementation, data mining, corporate and foundation research
