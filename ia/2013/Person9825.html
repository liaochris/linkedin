David T. Aronberg's Summary

Our law firm welcomes the opportunity to work for you on your Personal Injury or Investment Loss case. We are here to take on the responsiblity of your case and guide you through the process of obtaining compensation for your damages.

While many lawyers deal with cases in a wide variety of areas, our firm handles only Personal Injury and cases involving investment losses. We represent people ... people damaged due to the wrongful and negligent actions of someone else. We never represent insurance companies or corporations. We are devoted to only representing people and their families.

Our commitment to our clients is to provide legal representation and keep you totally involved in your case. We have found that communication between attorney and client is the most important factor in a case. We want you to be well informed and to feel comfortable and confident in our services. We pride ourselves on always being available to answer your questions. When we take on the responsibility of handling your case, we also feel an obligation to guide you through the legal process and keep you fully informed.
