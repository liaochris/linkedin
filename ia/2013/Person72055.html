Karen Dietz's Summary

For over two decades Polaris Associates Consulting, Inc. has been helping organizations and leaders be more effective. My business journey started out as a trainer and facilitator in the human potential movement. Later I became certified in the MBTI, working with teams of executives and individuals to improve their leadership skills. I have also authored strenghts-based and bias-free MBTI reports for work environments that have been sold in an easy to use software package since 1996. My software engineer husband and I were one of the very first companies to computerize MBTI reports.

Our software customers included top ranked Universities, Fortune 500 companies, nonprofits, and individual practitioners from around the globe.

Along the way I also gained years of experience in many aspects of organizational development -- change, leadership, teamwork, strategy, and governance.

Over the years as I've worked with organizations, executives and teams, it became clear that communication was at the crux of many issues. I became fascinated with communication strategies as a way to help my clients. As a result, I turned my attention to organizational story work and for years have been a thought-leader in that field.

As the owner of Just Story It, my passion is to help companies grow their business through the power of storytelling and organizational narrative. In addition to my corporate work, I am a trained storyteller and the former Executive Director of the National Storytelling Network. Clients include Fortune 500 companies, business leaders, and community advocates. Industries include financial services, travel, entertainment, technology and non-profits.

Specialties: MBTI Certified, storytelling and organizational narrative, leadership development, strategic planning, organizational change, teamwork, values and vision.
