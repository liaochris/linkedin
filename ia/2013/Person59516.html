Jason Starbuck's Summary

Words I live by:

"If my Intent and Determination is Greater than my Fear, at that point I have the Opportunity to win."

Jason Starbuck is the founder and director of On The Move Marketing, a grassroots study of marketing’s effect on ecommerce and local and national markets which has grown into an internet marketing agency.

Previously the president of OmnasNetwork.Com, where he worked together with the board to develop SunglassExtreme.com and many other ecommerce stores. There Jason took the company from sales of $9,000 to $125,000 per month in just over one year.

In 2011, in an effort to immerse himself in Muay Thai Fighting or “The Art of Eight Limbs,” Jason boarded a plane with a one way ticket to Thailand. In addition to fighting he discovered and fell in love with the people, culture, food, and language of East Asia.

While in Thailand Jason developed a Dental Program for a local orphanage which brought free dental care to over 118 orphans and acts as channel of support and fund raising to the orphanage. Also continues to serve as President of the DCP charity (http://dcpcharity.com/).

One of Jason’s true passions is motivational speaking. He currently is serving as a speaker for YEMP as well as other charitable organizations.

With his diverse skill sets Jason continues to take the way businesses think about marketing to new levels through On The Move Marketing.

HOW we acquire a specific Skill Set is as valuable as having the Skill set…..
