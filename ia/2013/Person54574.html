Bob Brotchie's Summary

Celebrating 21 years in Health, I can be found at premises located close to Newmarket on the Suffolk/Cambs border in the heart of East Anglia, England

I'D LOVE TO HEAR FROM YOU!
Working with me, counsellor and business owner, you will find a therapeutic and guiding service distinctly recognising the uniqueness of you.
Tailored expressly to your needs, the goals will always be about releasing you from thoughts that trouble you as an individual, whether male or female, young on less than!

Maybe you are faced with relationship issues with a partner or spouse - or as a family member, or indeed the whole family. Divorce and family separation are unwelcome intrusions into your emotional well-being. I can help you and others affected make sense and move through this difficult time

Counselling services including psychotherapy and cognitive behavioural therapy (CBT) for individuals, couples (of any persuasion) and families.

My service mantra, "helping minds- changing lives" and training and background helps me guide and share that effective change of the individuals needs - and those within the relationship - to take into account the whole person - by placing their life in context - instead of focusing on isolated behaviours. With this approach I succeed in working with people to understand themselves better - and let them find their best resources to overcome difficulties with a sustainable strategy - for life.

Many of us get caught up in the past, or find ourselves worrying about the future, each of these places being positions of rumination, rather than facts attributable to this moment. I can introduce you to new, healthier thoughts, cognition's in stress, anxiety, depression or relationships



Testimonials (verifiable) are available on my website

For a confidential and no-obligation initial chat, please head over to my website angliacounselling.co.uk - and leave your details.- I'd love to hear your story, and help create a new one.

      • Science and Technology
      April 2005 – present (8 years 7 months)

      I am the founder and creator of I.C.E In Case of Emergency for the Mobile. I have successfully achieved global acceptance for mobile phone users to place a nominated contact within their cell address book for emergency responders and the ER staff; so when you can't talk, your ICE entry will provide your next-of-kin details, helping you to be identified, understand your medical history from NOK so reducing possibility of harm from treatment (allergies, etc) - whilst reuniting you at the earliest opportunity - all free of choice, free.of charge.
