George C. Pell's Projects

  • The Kinetics Institute - Performance Science

    • January 2011 to Present
    Team Members: George C. Pell

    Our Performance Science practice incorporates cutting edge advancements to substantially enhance cognitive & social performance through a hybrid of disciplines: (1) Neurophysiology which specializes in the physiological and functional aspects of the brain, and (2) Neurobiology which cares for the biological factors that either affect or are directed by the brain.

    This program specializes in a comprehensive set of areas such as: Attention, Working Memory, Visual Cognition, Verbal Processing, Organization, Executive Function, Decision Making, Problem Solving and Social Intelligence. (there are literally hundreds of variables we can map and enhance)

    We employ state of the art methods for brain mapping such as qEEG along with highly advanced neuro-modeling techniques to collect a vast compilation of detail portraying the brain’s ability to optimize a wide range of cognitive & social abilities.

    Next we design individualized protocols for functional health, neuro-therapy, integrative bodywork and functional fitness to optimize a comprehensive set of cognitive abilities.
