Gary Kieper's Summary

I started Right 2 Win Communications with the core belief that every company has the right to win business. The real question is, are they doing what they have to do to actually win the business?

It’s easy to have a nice brochure and website. It’s also easy to tell everyone how great your company is and how you excel at service. When I ask sales people to tell me why I should buy from them, they tell me it’s because they have a great product and top notch service. Then I ask them what they think their competition would say to that very same question. 99% of the time I hear “exactly the same thing”. I agree.

I started working in the advertising industry in the early 1990’s. I know all about building web sites, brochures, mailers and the rest of the fluff we’ve all had to buy over the years. While I believe all of it is necessary, I believe even stronger that it does very little to drive sales. Don’t get me wrong, I said they were necessary, but the only way to really drive sales is to add value to the relationship and collateral really doesn’t do that. You add value by educating and helping your prospects understand their problems and by showing how you’ve helped solve similar problems for other clients.
