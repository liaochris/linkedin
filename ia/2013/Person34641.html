Lisa Wilson's Summary

Design Collaboration offers residential design and construction services specializing in healthy, barrier free, and energy efficient home environments. We work closely with our clients to create a working relationship that fosters open communication. Understanding the design needs of our clients allows Design Collaboration to assist them in making critical project decisions in both the design and construction phases of their project. Ninety percent of our clients are seeking smaller homes that integrate aging-in-place concepts, renewable technologies, energy efficiency, and low maintenance.

Ms. Wilson has been involved in the design profession in the Raleigh area since the early 1990’s. She received her Masters of Architecture from North Carolina State University and completed two years of Doctoral studies at North Carolina State University in Environmental Design. She is also a professional member of the American Institute of Building Design and the American Institute of Architects, and serves as an adjunct faculty member at Meredith College.
