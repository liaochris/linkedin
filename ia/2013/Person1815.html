Andrew Hammond's Summary

Self motivated and passionate illustration and design professional.

With over 7 years experience working closely with clients to develop tailored artwork and 5 years experience in the media industry I am able quickly understand and visualize ideas, while remaining loyal to any themes or tones that need to be expressed.

With my strong art skills and experience in film, television and media production I am ideally placed to communicate ideas efficiently, while capturing the necessary tone and personality.

Most recently I have been honing my skills in brand communication and character design. I have also been working on the penciling, inking and colouring for a graphic novel

Specialties: Visualization, illustration, sequential art, brand communication, visual storytelling, fine art, quick sketching skills, perspective drawing, character design, shot composition, pitch presentations.
