Leslie Larrabee's Summary

Expert knowledge in SaaS solutions targeted at the continuing education marketplace, primarily in higher education. Not only have a in-depth knowledge of the technology, but extensive experience in the business rationale behind the software.

Proven track record in planning, building, and operating successful organizations that link education and business in mutually beneficial partnerships, including a statewide corporate college, a workforce development center in multi-campus community college district and a non-profit education foundation. Executive Director/Marketing and Sales Director/Consultant

California Corporate College

June 2007 – December 2012 (5 years 7 months) Palm Springs, CA

I was hired to develop the operations plan for a concept called the California Corporate College (CA CC). I was then recruited as the Executive Director for the enterprise. The CA CC, a single point of contact for large California corporations seeking contract training solutions delivered through the California Community Colleges, was launched in May 2009 and is in its third year of operation. As the Executive Director, I have the responsibility for the day to day operations of this virtual enterprise as well as business development and recruitment of college district members. I have implemented technology tools like Salesforce.com, Constant Contact and GoToMeeting to improve operational efficiencies. From the operating plan we have built operating procedures, processes and protocols to ensure our ability to respond quickly to the needs of our clients. As Marketing and Sales Director, I created and implemented a strategic marketing plan with a two-pronged approach, designed to generate new college district members as well as generate new client business. I also supervised the work of the Sr. Sales Executive. Since the official launch of the enterprise, we have secured over three million dollars in projects and recruited 41 college districts (57% of total college districts), and have done so operating in the black each year.
