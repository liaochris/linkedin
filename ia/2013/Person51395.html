Jonathon Hamer's Summary

RichmondTimeLapse.com - Watch It Happen!

Who needs before and after...when you can go from start to finish! We are dedicated to the production of time lapse videos. An ideal marketing tool for the building and construction industries, time lapse video technology turns a project that takes days or weeks to complete, into a video that takes only minutes to watch!

Using a fleet of digital cameras, Richmond Time Lapse takes still images of a project in progress, then edits the footage into a dynamic video, which is perfect for sales calls, web sites, DVD’s and trade shows. It’s more affordable than you think. Set your marketing in motion today!

HamerDesign.com not only has the creativity to provide an innovative solution to your graphics needs; we have the technical expertise to make it all work in a cost effective, timely manner. And now we are featuring Time Lapse photography and Virtual Tours which can add clout and interest to your marketing programs.

We work with you to determine just who your target audiences are and what objectives you need to meet. Then we develop the creative solution that works within your budget and schedule.

Specialties: Time Lapse Photography, Virtual Tours, Website Development
