David Benham's Summary

Present Position: Director, Telepresence Technology at Cisco Systems. Managing the Telepresence Interoperability Protocol (TIP) for third-party developers.

Mr. Benham has over 20 years of experience in the IP networking and information technology industry. In his 15+ years at Cisco, he has launched new products, formed new industry alliances, led system architecture initiatives and managed a 250M$ per year network infrastructure product line. He founded and served on the Board of Directors (BoD) the Internet Streaming Media Alliance (ISMA), served on the BoD of the Multimedia over Coax Alliance (MoCA) and presently holds a leadership role in the International Multimedia Telecommunications Consortium (IMTC). David was previously a voting member in the ATM Forum for Cisco.

Specialties

Markets & Technologies: Telepresence, Video Collaboration, VoD and IPTV, IP Networking, Security/DRM, MPLS and ATM.

New Knowledge Investments: Sustainability, Networked EV, Smart Grid
