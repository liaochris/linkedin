Anca Mosoiu's Summary

Anca Mosoiu is a multidisciplinary software product developer with two decades of experience in the Internet and desktop software industry. A graduate of MIT, Anca began her career as a software engineer developing IT applications for large organizations. She has also worked as a user interface and 3D programmer, which led to her interest in web technologies and her role as a solutions architect at AvenueA/Razorfish and subsequent freelance career. Her technical experience is complemented by a personable attitude and a commitment to success. This allows her to act as the glue on project teams and coordinate often divergent stakeholder objectives and development activities towards successful completion. Anca has worked with a diverse client base, including early-stage startups and multi-national corporations, including Shell, Visa, Cisco, Sun, Sony, and others.

Anca is a firm believer in the thoughtful application of technology solutions to solve human problems in a sustainable, socially responsible fashion. She is currently developing Tech Liminal, Oakland's Technology Hotspot and Salon (see http://techliminal.com)
