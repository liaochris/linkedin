Grace Varga's Summary

Grace Salem was born in 1986 in Beirut, Lebanon. She grew up there, and studied Graphic Design in the American University of Beirut.
Her interests include: public art, stereoscopy, and games.
Important moments in her life: Her first stencil was in 2006, and her first big urban game took place in 2008 in Beirut, Lebanon.
In 2008, she moved to New York to join the MFA in Design and Technology program at Parsons The New School for Design.
She is currently a graphic and interactive media designer working and living in New York City.

Specialties

Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Indesign, After Effects, Final Cut Pro, Flash, AS3, HTML, CSS, Wordpress, PHP/MySQL.
