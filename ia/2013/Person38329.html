Sharon Norling, M.D.'s Experience

Owner

Mind Body Spirit Center

January 2006 – Present (7 years 9 months)

Dr Norling established The Mind Body Spirit Center to practice Integrative Medicine. She is the only physician in the United States known to be nationally board-certified in OB/GYN, Integrative/ Holistic Medicine, and Medical Acupuncture. She is also trained and certified in Functional Medicine and FirstLine Therapy, the study of nutrition, supplements and specialty testing. Dr Norling is also a Certified Biofeedback Specialist. She is a highly sought national speaker and educator on the benefits and concerns using natural medicine, supplements, and herbs. Dr Norling served on the faculty of the University of Minnesota Medical School for 12 years. As a visionary and successful leader, Dr Norling combines a credible and responsible academic knowledge with years of experience and a caring nature. Continuing to embrace the concept of mind, body, and spirit, she supports the principle that individuals increasingly seek and deserve to be partners in their health and healing.
