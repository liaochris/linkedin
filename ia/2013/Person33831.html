Jeff Deibler, CCAM's Summary

I am a Certified Community Association Manager (CCAM); member of the Community Associations Institute (CAI); and operate a Better Business Bureau A+ accredited business. I am applying to become an ECHO Associate member soon.

I've reorganized my partnership company SF HOA Managers into a sole proprietorship named DEIBLER SF HOA. I've added a great back office accounting service, HOA Accounting Services, to support a well organized company servicing homeowners associations (HOAs) in the San Francicsco Bay Area marketplace. I've been working throughout the SF Bay Area since 2005.

I continue to keep myself educated in the new laws implemented yearly by attending CACM, CAI, and ECHO courses and conferences. By consistently taking courses I'm on tract to earn the Master of Community Association Management (MCAM) designation within the next year.

Also, in the upcoming year I plan on earning the following Specialty Certificates through the California Association of Community Managers (CACM): Large Scale Specialty Certificate, Portfolio Management, and the High Rise Specialty Certificate.
