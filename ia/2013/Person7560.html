Andy Schrader, P.E.'s Summary

I'm a licensed professional engineer and freelance travel writer. I restore historic homes and (hopefully) reflect the South.

My work has been published in Southern Living Magazine, the American Society of Civil Engineers' Journal, and the Tampa Tribune Newspaper, among others.
