Alexandra Villarroel Abrego's Summary

Inspiring and empowering you to love life, others and yourself (TM).

Alexandra Villarroel Abrego, certified life coach and relationship and personal development expert, will teach you how to unleash your true potential, gain confidence in yourself, and find a balance between your personal and professional life so that you may finally live the life of your dreams.
In 2010, she released her first internationally solicited book, "Beyond Beauty," which takes readers on a journey to self-discovery, and brings to this new generation of women a better understanding of the concepts of inner and outer beauty. In 2012, the book was translated into French, and continues to sell worldwide.

Since the release of her first book, Alexandra has been on a national speaking circuit, visiting and speaking at French and English schools across the country to teach, inspire, and empower girls to love themselves and become leaders. Her life-changing workshops and programs are now available in many public and private high schools nationwide.

In 2012, Alexandra was chosen as the national role model for the Hooked On School High School Tour, an initiative of the Ministry of Immigration and Cultural Communities of Quebec, in collaboration with the Montreal Hooked on School organization.

Besides her live seminars and appearances, she also has a very strong presence and following online. Through her weekly online show (AlexandraTV) broadcasted in English, Spanish and French, she has reached over a million viewers in 134 countries around the world, and counting. Alexandra is also the founder of A.V.A Academy, the world's most complete and understandable online training program destined for women who want to live a more purposeful and fulfilling life.

Through her online show, magazine and newspaper articles, training programs, live conferences and book, Alexandra is quickly becoming the new role model of this generation of women.
