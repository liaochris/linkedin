Gary Bizzo's Summary

I enjoy working in a team environment or as a leader. As a Strategic Management Consultant I've taken over in a variety of C-Suite roles in Client-owned Small Business to lead their businesses to the next level. I'm experienced as a CEO & have been called into numerous businesses to help them when problems become overwhelming. I've worked with single entrepreneurs to some international companies although I specialize in Business Startups.

My avocation is a Social Media Networker. I have an International reputation as an 'Agent of Change' using Social Media. In 2010, I helped change Canadian Law with Bill C-470.
