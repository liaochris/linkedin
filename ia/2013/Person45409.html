David Fox's Publications

  • Flash Mindmeld

    • July 6, 2011
    Authors: David Fox

    Ever wonder what it takes to make a great Flash game?
    That's the question I posed to the 60 greatest minds behind Flash games. Common mistakes made in development, how to stand out and what key points separate a good game from a great one are just some of the topics covered by gurus like Brad Borne (Fancy Pants), Tom Fulp (Castle Crashers), Stephen Harris (Bloons), Sean Cooper (Boxhead) and Edmund McMillen (Super Meat Boy).

    Never before have the top Flash game developers come together to share their insights, wisdom and inside-the-industry know-how to give aspiring Flash developers a "mindmeld" moment with their developer heroes.

    Endorsed by Tom Fulp, Dan McNeely, Joel Breton, John Cooney, Ben Olding, Terry Paton, Andy Moore, Tony Lavelle, Adam Saltsman and Chevy Ray Johnston, this book captures it all in easy-to-read bite-size tips. There's something to learn on every page to lock away in your gray matter forever.
