Joe Yosso's Summary

My Professional experience Includes 30 years of sales and marketing experience, including helping several start up companies get off the ground. I enjoy teaching and training others and enjoy helping others grow in their business & professional life
When it comes to business, I emphasis being a consultant & confidant rather than a salesman. I use the term client over customer as the word implies more of a relationship than just performing a transaction. I believe it is important for the client to know what the product or service can do for them specifically and if it what I have is not a good fit, I try to find what can work and refer to someone else. Bottom line is that the client/personal relationship is more important than the sale.
In my current Position, I help Individuals and Business get the very best Legal service available. Legal Shield ( formerly Prepaid legal) has been Helping Individuals get the very best legal service for 40 years.I have had occasion to use the service as a customer over the years, and I have been more than pleased, and have saved thousands of dollars, by simply getting on the phone and asking a qualified attorney in the particular field I needed.
Also, because I have been a Business owner I Know the value of being able to ask an attorney for help, and I enjoyed the peace of mind of not having to worry about getting a large bill after I spoke to the attorney.
With the Partnership of Go Small Biz Legal Shield has helped thousands of Small Businesses with advice, consultation and education.It is like having a mastermind group of the best business minds in the country Plus the combined knowledge of 50,000 business owners.
Because I am specially trained for Business & group marketing, I believe I can help many business grow and help them provide legal shield protection as an Employee Benefit with no cost to the company,and in fact it actually helps the business be more productive, reduce absenteeism, and increase morale.
