Brigitte Halliday's Experience

Design Director

Indelible Creative

Sole Proprietorship; 1-10 employees; Design industry

June 2012 – Present (1 year 5 months) Streatham

Brigitte, founder of Process Design and Branding, launched Indelible Creative, a local creative communications agency based in Streatham. Brigitte and her business partner Ginger Tilley work collaboratively with trusted creative partners to deliver fully integrated brand identity solutions.

Indelible Creative specialises in helping growing businesses who need to revitalise their brand communications to achieve greater brand memorability, audience engagement and long-term effectiveness.

Indelible’s services include brand strategy, brand identity, website design, interior design, social media, packaging design, promotional design, advertising, copywriting and creative direction.

Creative Director

Process Design & Branding

February 2008 – June 2012 (4 years 5 months)

Brigitte founded Process with her business partner Ginger Tilley in 2009. Process delivers identity based brand communications and all related brand roll out for SME’s.

The Hideaway Jazz Club project and Winner of the Parliamentary award for “Best Jazz Venue 2011”demonstrates Process's capabilities.

We created the brand strategy, name, and identity. This was developed through to the website, all internal print items, press and print promotions and a range of promotional items.

We developed the interior graphics, signage and interiors, working in collaboration with Paul Mullins and Associates.

Design Associate

Forward Thinking Inc

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Management Consulting industry

2004 – 2008 (4 years) Richmond London

FTI is a strategy company who help businesses achieve a clear understanding of their unique business offer and the new opportunities that arise from this.

Brigitte worked with Ginger Tilley at FTI to provide intelligent and innovative strategy led design solutions for Blue Chip companies including BP, Castrol, Michelin and Miele UK.

Design Director

Tucker+

2001 – 2004 (3 years) Bermuda

Brigitte was hired to set up Tucker+, a Bermudian design company with it’s design arm based in London. T+ provided Bermuda’s financial and leisure industries the very best design and creative thinking from London.

Senior Designer

The Identica Partnership

1996 – 2000 (4 years) Notting Hill

Working with Ginger Tilley, Brigitte helped to set up the new “Brand Expression” department at The Identica Partnership which looked to bring a new level of strategic thinking to their creative offer.

Key projects included Martell,
Chivas Regal, Disney and Universal.

Deputy Design Director

Cato Consulting

1992 – 1996 (4 years) Vauxhall London

Brigitte joined TCGD as Deputy Design Director and it was here that she developed a broad view of creativity and it’s role in business.

Brigitte helped to develop the company’s holistic approach to “brand development” where big, conceptual ideas were created that combined traditional design and marketing abilities into more effective and integrated brand communications.

Key projects included Johnnie Walker, “Spirit of the world”, Ocumare Rum, Gin Zing, and Gordon’s Vodka.

Junior Designer

The Jenkins Group

1987 – 1990 (3 years) Westminster

Brigitte Halliday's Projects

  • The Hideaway Jazz Cafe

    • December 2010 to Present
    Team Members: Brigitte Halliday

    Hideaway is London’s most significant new Jazz venue and was awarded the winner of the Parliamentary Jazz Awards “Best New Venue” for 2011. Indelible created a comprehensive brand identity programme, starting with brand planning and naming. This was followed by the development of a wide range of brand communications including website design, advertising, printed and promotional initiatives. The brand style was then developed in the club’s interiors, from initial concepts through to total implementation ready for the club’s opening in January 2010.
