2007 – 2009 (2 years) Baltimore, Maryland Area

This was a challenging position! I was hired to revitalize and manage an underperforming career center that had been without a director for 1-1/2 years. My main directive was to create and implement a new strategy to increase employment opportunities for students. I created and implemented a strategic plan that established a tiered system of targeted employers, increased internship opportunities, and updated online systems for social media initiatives and online recruiting and job posting software. Participation in career fairs increased by 29.8% (employers) and 6.8% (Students), internship postings increased by 26% and student internship placements by 14%. The Employer Development Team achieved senior placement rate of 85.8% at time of graduation

My favorite part of the job was coaching traditional and adult students in career transition and job search strategies.
