Working mainly as a Linux admin, I implemented and maintained a highly available Glassfish stack with reliable, proven monitoring and failover systems that ensured the success of a quality-centric manufacturing environment.

In-Country Intern

Haiti Outreach

September 2008 – August 2009 (1 year) Pignon, Haiti

During my year as an intern for Haiti Outreach, I lived in Pignon, Haiti, working mainly on IT related tasks. I lived with a local family where I learned the language through speech.

As an intern I worked closely with the Haitian staff of 26 to develop and maintain several custom FileMaker databases that drove several operations including finances and water well drilling. I also developed software to convert a growing archive of GPS data to Google Earth KML to aid understanding of the data.

The core of the organization is based around sustainability and forward thinking, teaching me the value of thoughtful communication and documentation.
