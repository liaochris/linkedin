Craig A. Cunningham, Ph.D.'s Summary

I think about educational institutions as situational systems, with attention to contexts, inputs, and outputs, to improve their effectiveness and efficiency.

I help teachers and schools create engaging educational environments that incorporate digital technologies and reflect the principles of inquiry-based learning.

I also investigate and write about the history and philosophy of education, with a special interest and expertise in the philosophy of John Dewey.

Specialties

educational policy, curriculum development, learning theories, moral education, science education, 21st century learning, iPads in education, web development, instructional technologies, virtual environments
