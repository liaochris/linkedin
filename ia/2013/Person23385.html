Chris Howlett's Summary

Chris Howlett is a business coach, speaker, entrepreneur and author. Chris consistently works with small businesses and start-ups to formulate strategic business plans with tangible implementation steps. It is very often Chris's outside perspective that creates the spark of innovation, efficiency and productivity for his clients.

Chris approaches being a business coach as being a trusted advisor. Business owners and solo-prenuers often fight the daily grind single-handedly. Chris is the secret AK-47 in the back pocket of his clients. If you're in the battle of your life - Chris is a game changer. He provides not only a fresh perspective, but an unbiased one. It is often challenging for business owners to receive an honest appraisal from those that work closest to them. That's exactly what Chris Howlett provides. WARNING: honest doesn't always mean good! You won't pay Chris to be another 'yes man,' you'll hire Chris for results and solutions. His business coaching will bring you results, just as it has for each of his clients.

Chris Howlett also teaches the exponential power of time leverage. He guides his many clients through the techniques used to create enormous competitive advantages, with tools such as outsourcing and automation. Automation alone can be the difference between competitive dominance and a continuous game of catch-up.

When you're ready to reveal what's been holding you back or finding out where you can truly go, connecting with Chris Howlett is your next step.

In their motor coach Chris and his girlfriend travel full-time. They've each developed a severe allergy to snow and follow warm climates with sandy beaches when possible.

Chris is also a Certified Personal Trainer. Having finished three Ironman Triathlons and a natural body building show, he only speaks from experience. If he's not teaching a webinar, he might be teaching a fitness bootcamp.
