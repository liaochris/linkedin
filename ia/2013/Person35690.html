Dinesh Soni's Summary

Dinesh Soni did his schooling from S.M.R High School and finished Information Technology Engineering from MIT, Pune. He Joined MIT College of Computer Studies as Lecturer after completing graduation and was promoted to the post of “Head of Department” within 3 months.
Following the quest to study more, Dinesh left the job and joined Management course at CRKIMR, Mumbai. During hisMBAstudies, he also worked as “Social Media Marketing Consultant” and has many corporate giants and online companies in his client portfolio. He also holds keen interest in blogging and he is writing at dineshsoni.com since 2004. He is also part of more than 20 other blogs based on photography, traveling, technology, online media and startups. Dinesh is also founder of the Mumbai Bloggers Network and is co-founder of Twibrary, a book readers' social network based on twitter authentication..
Dinesh currently owns a designer T-shirt store at Latur and is also running a business of personalised gifting and corporate gifting.

Specialties

Information technology, blogging, social media, Human resources, retail management,
