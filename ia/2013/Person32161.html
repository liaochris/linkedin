David Ledgerwood's Summary

I have substantial and varied business interests across the education and technology sectors. Common themes include entrepreneurship (I’ve started and run more than five companies) and executive leadership (both as a personal endeavor and as consulting clients).

My areas of specialty include knowledge management, technical writing and documentation, operations, finance, information technology, remote team management, and outsourcing. I’m fascinated by business as a career, hobby and academic pursuit.

I work exceptionally hard to achieve [near] perfect results on every deliverable.

I'm particularly proud of having developed for profit and non-profit companies that have graduated more than 2,500 teen and young adult leaders and budding professionals.

These knowledge management practices forced us to work smarter and not harder. If there wasn't a document to answer that question it was your job to make sure there was next time.
