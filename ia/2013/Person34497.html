Derek Sajbel's Summary

11 Years of Video Editing, Media Production, & Sound Engineering
Digital media producer with impeccable video & audio editing skills, having routinely converted hours of footage into succinct pieces ranging from 30 seconds to 120 minutes.

Expertise in video editing, sound-mixing, effects, titling, file compression, and DVD production. Extensive multi-camera editing experience. Proficiency with digital HD and SD as well as 16mm film.

Additional skills in shooting on location live and scripted productions, as well as staging events for up to 1,000 people, including full sound engineering & A/V setup.

Offering services as a video editor, cameraman, director, or full independent video and audio productions. Japanese translation, custom circuitry, producing music and DJing are also on the table.
