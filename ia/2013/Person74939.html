Kevin J Hohnbaum's Summary

I have a passion for working with people to grow to new levels of personal accomplishment. I'm fanatical about customer service, giving people the opportunity to support causes they believe in and view everything through a marketing lens. My favorite questions are "Who else should know?"' and "How do we tell the story?".

I served on the board of the Beaverton Education Foundation for four years including a year as chair of the board. I led the Business Advocacy Committee for the Beaverton Chamber of Commerce for 7 years and am a past chair of the chamber board. I've held a number of board roles including President, for the Portland chapter of the American Marketing Association and spent 4 years as a member of the national AMA Professional Chapters Council.

I spent 12 years volunteering for SMART (Start Making a Reader Today) reader, spent 3 years on SMART"S Metro Exec Council, served on the board for the Oregon Crusader Drum and Bugle Corps for 4 years and have served on the youth exchange committee for Beaverton Rotary. My wife, Jane Mulholland, and I organize annual mission trips to Mexico and enjoy leading worship at our local church.

Specialties: Over-the-top customer service, connecting people with their passions
