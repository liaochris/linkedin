Apollo Spiliotis' Summary

Architect, technology writer, 3D designer who lives and breathes the internet.

In 2012 I joined SubenDougherty in Soho (New York) as a Project Architect, mainly being involved with the New York Presbyterian Hospital Center for Special Studies Chelsea Clinic among other healthcare, corporate office, residential and retail spaces.

Prior to joining SD, I was involved on a variety of residential, commercial and industrial projects at Better Homes in Greece, including hotels, seaside villas, churches and “Green Homes”. In the UK, I worked with EK Architects in the “Building Schools for the Future” program designing mainly sustainable High Schools.

I published a few articles in [ArchiMAG] magazine back in 2011, as I love sharing knowledge as well as following the evolution of technology, while speculating on things coming up over the horizon. I have great experience a variety of design software, and enjoy finding innovative ways to solve complex design problems.

In 2010 I created my own digital design agency [archiCADmonkey.com] specializing in BIM design & education, brand & media creation, web design & social marketing. The archiCADmonkey blog and Facebook Page is where I curate and contribute the best of Technology & Design News on the Web, Mac and Mobile, along with reviews, tips & tricks for CAD & BIM.
