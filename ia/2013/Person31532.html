David Borcherding's Publications

  • Mystery Writer's Sourcebook 2nd Edition

    • Writer's Digest Books
    • December 1995
    Authors: David Borcherding

    This sourcebook for mystery writers combined author and editor interviews with how-to articles from some of the genre's top writers. It also included market listings for publishers buying mystery novel and short story manuscripts. I led a team of freelance writers, copyeditors, proofreaders, and graphic designers to bring this second edition to market. I also assembled the database of markets and interviewed several of the editors.

  • Science Fiction and Fantasy Writer's Sourcebook 2nd Edition

    • Writer's Digest Books
    • December 1996
    Authors: David Borcherding

    This is my baby, my pride and joy. I edited this book when I worked at Writer's Digest Books, and I loved every minute and every page. I led a solid team of freelance writers, copy editors, proofreaders, and graphic designers, and got to work with some of the best new writers in the genre.

  • Romance Writer's Sourcebook

    • Writer's Digest Books
    • April 1996
    Authors: David Borcherding

    This was my first new book project at WDB, and the most intimidating. I wasn't revising previous editions anymore! I was a greenhorn editor, I knew nothing about the romance genre, and I'd never managed such a massive project before. Fortunately, I had a good team of copy editors, freelance writers, proofreaders, and graphic designers backing me up, and the sage and patient guidance of the managing editor of WDB to help me along. The result was a book that was welcomed by hopeful romance writers and praised by veteran authors in the genre.
