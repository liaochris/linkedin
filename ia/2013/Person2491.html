Ahmed Bajwa, CISSP, CISA's Summary

Ahmed is a CISA, CISSP, Security+, ITIL v3 Foundation, MCSE certified security professional with experience in information security, NIST risk management framework, networking, and systems administration. Presently, he works at the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) as an Information Security Specialist with GNS.

He has extensive experience in creating system security audit/assessment plans, conducting systems security control audits, and vulnerability assessments of planned/installed information systems to identify vulnerabilities. His related skills include Certification & Accreditation activities, Windows Networking, Intrusion Detection, Network Fundamentals, Wireless Security, and Vulnerability Assessment. His experience includes work as an IT Project Officer, in which he launched a new IT training center in Sierra Leone. The experience honed his leadership and problem solving skills. He earned a Masters in Computer Science from George Washington University, where he specialized in information security, obtaining a “Graduate Certificate in Information Security.” Ahmed also has NSTISSI-4011 [National Training Standard for Information Security Professionals] certification. Lastly, Ahmed is not just a "techie"; he has used his people skills in multiple capacities, including three years as an instructor of various labs and courses at ECPI College of Technology,one of the top ten ranked community colleges for awarding associate degrees in Computer & Information Sciences.

Specialties

FISMA Compliance, NIST Risk Management Framework, Certification & Accreditation activities, Windows Networking, Intrusion Detection, Wireless Security, Vulnerability Assessment, Risk Management.
