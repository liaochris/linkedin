Bethany Williams' Summary

Results oriented healthcare executive with over 25 years of experience driving complex initiatives and achieving results.

An avid intrapreneur, business developer and healthcare strategist, known as a 'brand builder' for insightful and unique abilities to build business lines within companies and considerably expand footprint and market share. A catalyst for change in organizations; igniting new direction and positive change. Skilled at taking a concept from initial idea to revenue producing service or product.

Comfortable identifying purple cows and corporate thinking that keeps organizations from growing market share and profits. Big picture innovator. Proven track record of starting new ventures for organizations as they delve into new markets and product portfolios. Enjoy helping organizations create a vision that encourages growth and development into new areas and markets.

Specialties: Business Development & Strategy, Extensive Product Management and Product Marketing capabilities, Revenue Cycle, IT, Brand Building, known for taking concepts from bullet points to market delivery, Healthcare delivery/operations, and an expert in Clinical Document Exchange and the Clinically Driven Revenue Cycle (charges/billing driven from clinical documentation).
