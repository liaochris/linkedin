Ayd Instone's Summary

I have 18 years experience as a Creative Director of design and marketing in print and multi-media having previously worked in television special effects. Over the past 10 years of running my own business I’ve been engaged in numerous projects as brand consultant, creative director, designer, trainer, lecturer, author, professional speaker, publisher and information product creator.

With marketing communications I help people tell their story, their company or products’ stories in words and images. With training I help people unlock creativity and innovation in themselves and their businesses, helping them to be better presenters, creators and get their message out there. With publishing I help people find and condense their story and expertise into a book and help them publish it.

My speaking career began because I used to regularly perform stand-up comedy. Someone said I should do it for business and get paid more money. They were right. I can deal with heckles, they just fire creativity. In business we call heckles ‘problems’. I always see my role as a professional problem solver. But problem solving isn’t just what I do, it’s what I teach everyone to do.

As a professional speaker and trainer I’ve travelled to the US, Europe and Nigeria where I ran workshops in Lagos on innovative thinking for some of the countries top companies. (I ran intimate sessions for the CEOs, large seminars for managers and a motivational session for wives of the CEOs and a kids show on the beach for children.)

My main keynote talk is called ‘The Power of What If?’. I have a degree in Physics and Physical Science, BSc(hons). My dissertation was about Chirality and the Origin of Life. I did a tiny weeny bit of research into Buckminsterfullerenes that Harry Kroto went on to get a Nobel Prize for. It’s this science background that has enabled me to be trusted in scientific and technical environments as well as giving me an edge in the creative industries where I’ve worked.

Specialties

Creativity and innovation, Entrepreneurship, Branding, design and visual intelligence, New economy advertising and marketing, Publishing (online and offline) including use of books, ebooks and blogging, Creation of information products, Presentation skills, Training presentation skills


I’m also a singer/songwriter and incorporate the message of creativity & innovation into songs for keynotes.
