Daniel Owen's Summary

I have worked in systems administration, networking, security and IT management since 1998. This broad background allows me to see the big picture as it affects both IT and the business as a whole.

I always strive to find the solution that meets a broad base of needs across technical and business units. This often means additional work on the front end, to ensure all immediate needs are met while allowing for future growth. This extra time up front is well spent in the creation of a solution that works for today and tomorrow and covers all aspects of the issue.
I have worked directly or indirectly with security throughout most of my career. Far too often security becomes known as a roadblock that slows or stops forward progress. I never want this to be the perception of security in any company. I strive to be an early part of design and engineering so that security becomes a valuable part of the systems engineering process. By baking in security at the beginning I achieve a higher level of security at a lower cost. The fact that I have worked across systems, networks and security puts me in an excellent position to see projects from multiple angles.
In my role as a security professional I prefer to mitigate risks using a layered strategy that minimizes the company’s risk exposure while at the same time allowing the business to operate efficiently. That’s not to say everything is always perfect but perfection is always the goal.
