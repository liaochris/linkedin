Dr. Sonia Singh's Summary

I am a Licensed Clinical Psychologist providing effective therapeutic services to help improve the quality of life for individuals, couples/relationships, and families utilizing a holistic, strengths-based, and culturally informed approach to mental health.

I have a strong understanding of mental health concerns affecting Women.

I received specialized training with cutting-edge empirically validated CBT treatment models to address stress/anxiety, trauma, mood disorders (depression, bipolar), co-occurring medical concerns and addiction.

Integration of Eastern and Spiritual approaches to health & healing to promote positive growth and change is something I enjoy and is a specialty of mine.

I conduct psychological assessment and report writing including worker's compensation evaluations.

I recently opened my psychology practice in the city of Orange and Anaheim and I am accepting new referrals. I am contracted with several insurance companies that include: Aetna, Cigna, Blue Shield, MHN, Tricare, Magellan, CHIPA, Pacific Health Insurance, and Galaxy Health. I also accept clients with no insurance on a self-pay basis.

I contract with physicians and providers from other disciplines who are looking to integrate mental health services into their practice.
