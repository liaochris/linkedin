Leah Cevoli's Summary

The oldest of five, born in SW Philly Leah filled her childhood w/rock n roll, soap diva Susan Lucci, Madonna and horror classics like Carrie. She was able to contain her passion for pop culture just long enough to graduate (magna cum laude) from WCU with degrees in Psych & Elem Ed.

After spending the summer of 99 on the road w/80’s rock icons Poison, Leah packed her Ford Probe to the roof, $200 in her pockets, no real plan for work or even housing, and headed to Hollywood.

Most notably known as a voice actor on Cartoon Network's "Robot Chicken", and critically acclaimed Deadwood, Leah has also appeared in many indie projects; film, tv, and web.

An accomplished host; has MC'd thousands of live events, from rock concerts, to childrens' events, many produced by Cevoli. A promo model for over 12 years, Leah has worked w/some of the largest corporations & tradeshows.

As a member of SAG, Leah has had the honor of acting along-side such incredible actors as Ian McShane, Earl W. Brown, Christian Slater, Paula Malcomson,Brad Douriff and Seth Green.

Leah is an an active volunteer for the non-profit SAG Foundation, as well as Tom Morello's Axis of Justice, and supports Caleb's Hope and The Killamanjaro Project.

Leah co-founded/produced The Carnival of Darkness; an indie film festival showcasing new horror shorts. Leah moderated the festival’s panel, and also used the festival as a vehicle to premiere “Body of Work”; short film Leah co-starred in and co-produced.

In 2010 Leah began creating her own shows; Livin' On A Dream (talk-show), and On the Road with Leah, both can be found on the web and twitter

These days, Leah can be found at many conventions
