Emma Vites' Summary

I am very passionate about selling LinkedIn's Talent Solution’s to help companies find the best talent to benefit their business. “You don’t build a business, you build people and people build the business” so finding and retaining the best talent is critical for all organizations.

LinkedIn is the largest professional networking site with over 259m members. This company creates economic opportunities for professionals across the globe and there are 2 new members joining every second!

I have worked in the recruitment industry for over 8 years and ran 2 successful recruitment businesses and am therefore very aware of the challenges of finding and attracting the best talent in the market. LinkedIn is a brilliant solution for overcoming these challenges. Not only is LinkedIn critical with your sourcing strategy (you have the ability to communicate with a network of 238m people), but also your attraction strategy. LinkedIn’s media solutions automate the recruitment process and source candidates for you whilst you sleep.

As a Master NLP practitioner and emotional intelligence coach, I am very interested in the subconscious mind and how opinions and perceptions are formed. This is why LinkedIn’s employer branding solutions are so critical for businesses as they need to make themselves look appealing and attractive to the 80% of people who are not looking for work, what LinkedIn call ‘Passive candidates’.

To fulfill my passion for educating and training graduates and young people, The Apprentice Project continues as a Coaching and education resource to provide them with all the skills they need to succeed. I have placed and coached graduates into working with some of the leading companies in the world including Accenture, BT, Microsoft, MTV, Myspace, Macquarie Bank, News limited, Mars, Huddle and many more and have ran workshops and presented at City University, UCL, Manchester University and NYU in New York.
