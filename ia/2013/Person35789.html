Dirk Dixon, LPA, EA, RFC®'s Summary

I graduated from Grand View College in 1995 and became a Licensed Public Accountant (LPA) and an Enrolled Agent (EA), which means I am enrolled to practice before the Internal Revenue Service. I began working that same year at Farm Bureau Financial and later at New England Financial before arriving to Baker & Associates, LLP. In addition, I also earned Accredited Tax Advisor status (ATA) and the Accredited Business Accountant (ABA) with the National Society of Accountants as well as being a member with the Iowa Society of Licensed Public Accountants and the Accountants Association of Iowa.
I am well acquainted and experienced in many tax planning issues and I specialize at helping businesses and people realize tax savings through different programs. By proactively scouring my client’s circumstances so I can find every possible deduction in their tax situations. I really enjoy helping the self-employed and finding deductions that are legal, but often overlooked. Furthermore, I find legitimate ways to minimize my client’s capital gains tax liability through the selective realization of gains and losses.

I have written tax planning articles in publications like “The Register” and I also volunteer with the State of Iowa at the Low Income Taxpayer Clinics to help taxpayers involved in controversies with the IRS or educating individuals about their tax rights and responsibilities. As well as volunteering as a coach for the West Des Moines Soccer Club.

Specialties

Tax Preparation, Planning, and Resolution. Investment Advisory Representative, and Financial Advisory.
