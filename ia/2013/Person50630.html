Seven 7's Summary

Seven 7 GROWING THE WORLD works with grants, organizations, foundations, schools, universities, and governmental entities. Seven 7 is a multi-faceted consultancy that

Designs and Implements Education Designs at the micro- and macro-levels;

Leverages Teacher Professionalization to expose students to the practices of disciplines and invites students to engage in such practices of the disciplines;

Engages Teachers in Professional Learning and Provides Professional Development to teachers;

Helps teachers, districts, and consortia of districts to select, adapt, enact, assess, and evaluate the use of curriculum;

Helps teachers Embody Teaching for Understanding by developing habits of mind and habits of practice around disciplinary streams of practices;

Helps teachers, districts, and consortia of districts develop, test, refine, and re-deploy Mathematics Pedagogical Storylines;

Provides expertise and support for the Assessment of Curriculum Use, Teacher Professionalization, Teacher Learning, and Student Learning; and

Provides support services to Grow Students in the content and practices of the disciplines.

Specialties

Mathematics Education, Teacher Training/Education/Professional Learning, Teaching through Problem Solving, Lesson Study Approach, Transformations of Pedagogy, Improving Student Learning, Instrumentalization of Technology in Education, Effective Implementation of Curriculum to advance Learning
