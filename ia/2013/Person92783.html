Michael Nees' Summary

Guest New Zealand is a booking and travel advisory service for inbound Guests visiting New Zealand, Australia and the Pacific Islands. Guests are given an opportunity to select a range of quality tourism products and services - the result being an enjoyable and memorable visit.

Guest New Zealand is owned and operated by Michael Nees Travel NZ Ltd. (MNT) a specialist tourism company established in 1995 with its office in Christchurch, New Zealand.
