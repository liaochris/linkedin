Linnea Blair's Summary

Linnea has over 10 years experience as a small business coach and business consultant working with owners, executives and managers of small to medium businesses to achieve their business objectives. Linnea helps clients who want to grow their business, become more profitable and develop effective leadership skills.

In addition to working with clients in person, Linnea also uses internet based programs to work with clients individually or via live webinar to bring business coaching and business consulting to clients all over the United States and Canada.

Linnea is also a speaker and trainer, providing group training to small to medium businesses, and conducting seminars and educational workshops at local and national conventions for business groups and trade associations.

Specialties

Strategic Planning, Business Planning, Marketing Strategy, Relationship Marketing, Business Coaching, Management Consulting, Leadership, Time Management, Team Building, Customer Service Training

Clients include companies under 100 employees, in various industries with a focus on service industries, construction and other specialty contractors and non-profits.
