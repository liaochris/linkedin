Lauren Proctor's Summary

Lauren Proctor is a New York City based marketing innovation strategist and freelance writer fascinated with the forces that catalyze communities online. She consults with a select roster of brands in advancing their interactive new media marketing strategy, and was inspired to start her firm Proctor + Hall, while working with L2 Think Tank, Halogen Media Group, Sales Graphics, and The Hybrid Reality Institute.

Lauren's approach to marketing takes into account brand strategy, consumer behavior, transmedia storytelling, behavioral psychology, and digital culture. She studied Digital Marketing Innovation Strategy in New York University's Gallatin MA program and received BA's in English and Journalism after studying at both the University of Oxford and the University of Massachusetts at Amherst.
