Kevan Kjar's Summary

Our passion is to help others find their own "stories that win", whether it is their company, product or personal stories; that is their ArrowHead. Once a person, team or company has a good idea of their own ArrowHead, it gives them a purpose, passion and new power to perform. That is the purpose of ArrowHead3.

"Kevan draws out wisdom you didn't know the company had and helps hone it to perfection. Outstanding work that will change the way we do business."
-Janet Eden-Harris, CEO, Umbria

I help leading edge companies that are committed to excellence in sales and marketing to:
• Align their team around a central vision
• Create a clear product story that is clear, compelling & competitive
• Roll out that story to Sales, and give them skills to give killer presentations & demos

The ArrowHead3 process is Purpose, Alignment and Innovative Delivery; 1, 2, 3.

Specialties

I've had the opportunity to help thousands of professionals over the years learn their own message, and ultimately their story, and how to communicate that story to their peers and customers. Organizations such as SAP, Siemens, Johnson & Johnson and think tanks, have called on me on multiple occasions to help crystallize their story, and how to communicate it in a compelling manner.
