Mike Atkinson's Summary

Mike Atkinson is the co-owner of Elite Pro Performance and on-court performance director at Sierra Strength and Speed. Atkinson is a CSCS and a hybrid basketball performance trainer focusing on strength, movement and basketball fundamentals. His experience and current work with NBA player development specialist Aubrey McCreary have given him a unique set of skills for training players in sport specific patterns and positioning at the professional level. Atkinson has trained numerous Division I athletes, NBA draftees and other professional clients. He also worked as a performance consultant for the NBA Development League and as the director of basketball performance training camps and clinics for young athletes on the west coast. He is an expert basketball performance contributor to Stack magazine.
