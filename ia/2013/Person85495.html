Maree Conway's Summary

After a long and successful career in university management, I established Thinking Futures in 2007 to work with people, institutions and professional associations to integrate long term thinking (AKA futures thinking) into strategy processes. Using a range of futures approaches, I facilitate processes and provide strategic scanning to allow organisations to develop strategy that is based on a systematic and rigorous exploration of the future.

I have particular expertise in managing strategy development and implementation projects for universities, vocational and further education providers and other educational institutions, and provide a range of resources and education for people interested in using foresight in their work.

Find out more at http://about.me/mareeconway

If you use foresight now (excellent!), my resources will enhance your knowledge and application of this critical way of thinking about the future.

I work with people who are ready to set up/enhance organisational strategic foresight environments. You are ready to enable your staff to think in new ways about the future and to use that thinking to see new strategic opportunities today.
