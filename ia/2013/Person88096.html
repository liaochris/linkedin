Mary Habres' Summary

As a small business marketing consultant in Jacksonville, Florida, I help small business owners throughout the United States develop and implement creative, cohesive marketing, branding, and communications strategies for business growth.

My small business marketing consulting business started as a result of the success I experienced as the owner of my own home staging company, Encore Home Staging and Redesign. Many of my staging colleagues would call me for advice on marketing their design businesses. They could decorate but couldn't market themselves because they had never owned a business.

My sales experience also includes the development and implementation of a college recruitment program for the NJ Department of the Treasury. As Manager of Client Services, I
created and supervised the development of marketing materials for this successful program. In addition, as a former realtor in New Jersey, I helped market homes for sale and am very familiar with the marketing strategies needed for success in this industry.

I believe that Peter Drucker said it well, "The aim of marketing is to know and understand the customer so well the product or service fits him and sells itself." At Encore Perception Marketing we are dedicated to increasing your business visibility by helping you showcase the value and benefits of your products and services.

Connect with me especially if:

You are a first-time or struggling small business owner
You don't have a marketing plan that makes "cents"
You are in the real estate or interior design industry.
