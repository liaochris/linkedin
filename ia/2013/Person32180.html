David W. Lippy's Summary

For the past twenty years my passion has been fueled by my commitment to Non-Profits working with children and consulting with a wide variety of companies both large and small to maximize their marketing and development efficiencies via the internet and video.

Whether we recognize it or not, the web "evolved" once again in January of 2013 when the Google Search algorithm changed to weighing video much heaver as a result of the purchase of YouTube. Vine, Pinterest, and Instagram over the next 36 months will tremendously impact every website, every video and all forms of social media. Everything is moving to a place of creating and engaging your community, measuring your Social ROI, and influencing your audience to take an action. Who would have ever imagined a world where with the teen demographics, that something such as Vine would be one of the fastest growing social platforms on the web? It's a video that is required to be 6 seconds or less!

My mentor and good friend Dr. Harry Leibowitz taught me that No is never forever, it's only for today. He also taught me to be the best listener possible. Seek first to understand then to be understood from Steven R. Covey has been the key to my success. Thank you Harry for being such an awesome mentor and making someone like Covey required reading:)
