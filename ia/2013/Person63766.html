I work with clients who want to keep their money safe from loss.

Safe Money - We define a safe money place as one where your principal is protected from loss as long as you follow the initial guidelines, and if you do decide to take your money and leave, you know pretty much what leaving early will cost.

Risk Money – A risk money place is one where if you decide to take your money you don’t know what you will get back. It could be more than you put in – risk money places offer the potential for much higher returns than safe money places – but it could also be less than you started with or even zero.
