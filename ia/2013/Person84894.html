Marcela Shine's Summary

“Execution‬ makes great ideas the right ideas.”

Marcela drives strategy execution. In her role as chief operating officer, she has managed operations in entrepreneurial environments where cost management and employee retention is crucial to the start-up’s success. In addition to operational leadership, her fifteen plus years of product development provides a critical secondary area of expertise for companies.

Marcela is an angel investor for multiple start-ups focused in online marketing technology. She also serves on the advisory board of multiple non-profits. Marcela was born in Medellin, Colombia and has deep ties to her community there.
