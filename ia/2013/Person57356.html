James De Angelis' Summary

Hi i'm James, a digital product designer from Sydney, Australia. I get a kick out of building interesting, memorable and useful things for people.

In the past i've worked in both large Advertising and boutique digital agencies. Most recently i've been working on products with the startups at Pollenizer where i've lead the UI, interaction and brand design.

I believe designers should code and build my own work using modern tools like SASS (with Compass) Bootstrap, jQuery and Git.

I keep examples of my best work at my portfolio, here — http://jamesdeangelis.com/
