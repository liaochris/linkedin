Brad Schmett's Summary

Brad Schmett is Broker Associate at Luxury Homes by Keller Williams – in La Quinta, California.

Brad, his wife Deanna, daughter Jacqulyn, and other team members work together to provide home buyers and home owners with a sales and marketing team that specializes in gated residential developments, golf course properties and luxury homes in La Quinta – and the greater Palm Springs, California area.

Brad is an expert at helping sellers best position their real estate for sale – and helping buyers maximize their real estate investment. His keen understanding of the La Quinta real estate market has set him apart in the local real estate community.

With a commitment to providing his clients with the best and highest level of service possible, Brad has earned nine professional real estate designations including the prestigious Certified Luxury Home Marketing Specialist (CLHMS) designation awarded in recognition of experience, knowledge and expertise in the luxury home market. Brad has also earned the Accredited Buyer Representation (ABR) designation awarded in recognition of continued education in the field of buyer representation, and proven experience and training to deliver ethical and professional service to buyers.

Brad enjoys giving back to the local community by sharing his real estate experience, knowledge and expertise through his regular written editorial contributions to two local news publications, The GEM and La Quinta Sun.

When Brad’s not working – having been an avid golfer since age 10 – you may find him working on his golf game at one of the more than 120 golf courses in and around the La Quinta area.
