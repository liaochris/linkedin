Matt Kain's Summary

Accomplished Executive with 15 years domestic and international management experience in the online marketing space. Leadership roles in operations, strategy, sales, and marketing functions within both start-up and growth organizations. Results-oriented, decisive leader with proven success in defining strategic direction and developing appropriate operating structures and processes. Track record in increasing sales and growing profits while spearheading operational improvements to drive productivity and reduce costs.
