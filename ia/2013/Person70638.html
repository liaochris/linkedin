Julia Roy's Summary

I've been a digital strategist for over 6 years, specializing in social media marketing. I've developed and executed social campaigns for Pepsi, Ford, CNN, BMW, Coach and Hearst. I am also known for my internet fame, having been an early adopter of social media to build a personal brand online.

I recently co-founded WorkHacks, where we help entrepreneurs and organizations navigate the ever changing digital landscape of productivity. We work with individuals and teams to access their unique productivity and collaboration challenges and design a customized plan to help them work smarter. My methodology is that the best productivity system is custom to you. I help you and your team find a system that fits and work with you to implement it through workshops and training seminars.

Specialties: technology, productivity, consulting, corporate workshops, training, teaching, new media, marketing, communications, digital strategy, social networking, social media, personal branding, community engagement, relationship building, brand management, measurement, analytics
