Jeffrey Bunten - Attorney At Law's Summary

As a knowledgeable and experienced Attorney At Law, I have been representing Social Security Clients since 1992. My unique background in Social Security Law and Veteran’s disability give me extensive knowledge that few others can match. I represent clients in a personal and responsive manner all while offering high quality representation that fits your needs. I represent clients before the Social Security Administration, including Social Security Disability, Supplemental Security Income (SSI) and Children’s SSI. My practice also involves representation of veterans claiming service connected disability and pension before the Department of Veteran’s Affairs (VA).

I have substantial experience representing clients before the Social Security Administration, representing around 3,000 Social Security Clients since 1992. I am an accredited attorney representative before the Department of Veterans Affairs and I was admitted to the United States Court of Appeals for Veterans Claims in November 1995. I have also been admitted to the United States District Court for the Eastern District of MO and to the United States Court of Appeals for the Eighth Circuit.
