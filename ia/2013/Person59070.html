Public Company; Myself Only; Information Technology and Services industry

May 2002 – Present (11 years 5 months)

Caston Digital is a group of award winning, industry leading professionals, who provide the most powerful Web 2.0 church websites available coupled with eye popping, jaw dropping, design. Our focus is to provide maximum functionality, internet church strategies and personal support to a select group of clients. In addition to the technical expertise of our company, we apply the strategies from The iChurch Method book series released by our CEO Jason Caston. Churches are very behind the secular world in terms of Internet technology use, and that's due to proprietary solutions being absurdly expensive and very difficult to manage. Until now, church websites seem to have existed only to display information such as what time a service started or get directions to the church. Now, technology and solutions from Caston Digital, gives ministries and churches the opportunity to have all the tools they need online to operate as a worldwide megachurch. We take the storefront church and make it into a global online ministry.

Internet Church Developer

T.D. Jakes Ministries

January 2010 – Present (3 years 9 months) Dallas/Fort Worth Area

This position consists of working with a team of 5 to create an interactive web development experience with Social Media Development, Internet Church Campus, Web Site Development, Analytics, Multimedia Development Mobile Development and Graphic UI Design. This Front End website development utilized HTML, CSS, PHP, MySQL, JQuery, ASP, SQL 2005, Content Management Systems, Photoshop, Flash, Actionscripting, Dreamweaver, email blast and Ecommerce site implementation. Content Management Systems utilized were created in PHP/MySQL and .Net/SQL. Social Media Strategy and Development for Facebook, Twitter, Youtube and Itunes was done using Hootsuite Social Media Management Software, Facebook Fan Pages and integrating the Facebook Open Graph initiative, took the current Facebook fan page from 2000 fans to 649000 fans and growing at a rate of 10000 fans per month. Interacted with users and updated the social networks with daily posts, responses, audio and video multimedia, links and images. Internet Church campus creation was the integration of multimedia properties, content, interactivity and social media properties and putting it together in a usable online church experience. Multimedia development consisted of taking TV created media (DVD or other) and converting into web ready format using H.264 standards and online streaming encoders (Flash or HTML5) and then putting it into Media Suite or Vimeo online video suite. The Ecommerce portion of the project was completed in .net based ASPdotnetstorefront creating templates in CSS, Javascript and HTML and adding audio/video digital downloads that were created from DVDs. Mobile Development consists of developing mobile websites, apps and ecommerce solutions for all smart phones (iphone and Android) and mobile web browsers using HTML5, CSS and JavaScript (JQuery Mobile).
