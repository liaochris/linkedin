Dariush Soudi's Summary

Having been in sales for over 25 years, I have experienced working with many companies in many industries, from telecommunications, consumer goods, services, even the Beauty industry.

My goal is to educate and inspire individuals and business, to try new techniques, different avenues, take action, invest in their people, increase their client base, increase their clients spending and above all get them to recommend their business to all the people they know.

Business can be made simple, and it should be made simple. Business is about profitability. Of course great values are very important but the business has to make money otherwise its a charity. I do help charities but they dont hire me.

I work well with people who are motivated, passionate about what they do and what they want and are willing to step out of their comfort zone.

My company helps our clients to differentiate themselves in the market place from their competitors. Making your profitability our Priority and to Be Unique.

Sales and marketing consultant and Managing Director of Beunique group. Different organisations have their own unique agendas – from non-profit to firms keeping a close eye on the bottom line. All have one thing in common – to draw people to a cause they want to promote, products they want to sell or the service they provide.

In every case our priority at Be Unique remains the same - to attract the online traffic that will deliver the greatest result from what you want to achieve - then to speak to your target audience in a channelled and effective way.

The psychology of what engages potential clients and existing customers is changing as fast as the technology that drives it. Consumers and businesses now have an almost-infinite information source on the products and services they need and choice on who they buy from.

The Internet has already transformed the rules of engagement. More and more, social media forges the way businesses innovate and sell their products/services.

With a combined experience of over 35 years in owning and operating companies, we utilise the knowledge we have gained through many successes and a few failures along the way.
