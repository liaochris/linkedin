Diana Antholis' Summary

I help fabulous women entrepreneurs live the balanced, centered, and sexy lives they deserve. I take the mind, body, and soul approach to being healthy—focusing on living a life you love, freeing your mind of stress and anxiety, and embracing the secrets to exuding confidence. As a Lifestyle-ist, Mind Body Specialist, and ACE Certified Personal Trainer, I help you fall in love with movement and nourish your body. My ebook is a Best Seller on Amazon in stress management and happiness: "Unleashed: Live the Balanced, Centered, and Sexy Life You Deserve."

My master's degree in Organizational Management and my certification as an ACE Personal Trainer benefit organizations and young professionals with coaching and programs based on my research in work-life issues, organizational behavior, mind-body exercises, and fitness and nutrition management.

I am the founder of Enter: Adulthood, an online guide for young adults that shares advice and tips on career, relationship, and personal life choices to transition into the “real world.” Enter: Adulthood’s mission is to help young adults become independent, self-directed individuals with a balanced life of work, relationships, purpose, and fun.

In my former corporate life, I created and implemented advertising media plans for top clients in the consumer packaged goods, wireless telecommunications, restaurant, and sporting industries in NYC & San Diego. I served 5 years in the advertising/media industry consulting businesses on traditional & emerging communications strategies, including television, print, outdoor, radio, digital, & social media.

I was also a management consultant for the online network of Performance Advantage Inc., an independent learning & development consulting firm. Our mission is to advise managers on workplace productivity and training solutions.
