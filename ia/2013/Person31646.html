David Coster's Summary

I am experienced in all levels of Contract Management, including contract drafting to comply with Russia, Kazakh and UK/European Law, familiar with FIDIC and other contract forms. Have worked on EPC, LSTK PMC, PMCm and Unit rate contracts. Also the complete supply chain responsibility to supply remote and/or politically hard locations. I have lead numerous supplier audits throughout Europe, Japan and the Russian Federation. A team player whose not afraid to make decisions and to be accountable for the outcome. A good motivator in multi culture working environments. My goal is to share my knowledge and experience and never stop learning from others.

Specialties

Solution Driven, experienced in the development, presentation and defending claims. Dispute resolution and negotiation. Risk mitigation and active risk management (not just ticking the boxes). Considerable experience in Russia and the FSU, as I have Dual British and Russia passports I am extremely mobile in this part of the world, not requiring either visa or work permits
