Founder & CEO

Chandler Interactive

December 2010 – January 2013 (2 years 2 months) Columbus, Ohio

Chandler Interactive is a Digital Marketing Agency focused solely on helping businesses attract more customers by leveraging social media, email marketing and online channels.

Founded in 2010 by Barry Chandler in response to requests for a dedicated service to help owners and managers understand, address and capitalize on the growing number of online opportunities available to businesses large and small.

Chandler Interactive works with clients ranging from small neighborhood bars to large national chains. All businesses have an opportunity. What’s yours? Contact us to find out.

Founder of Online Resource for Opening & Managing a Bar

ManageyourBar.com

February 2008 – 2012 (4 years)

ManageYourBar.com is the online resource for the American Bar & Club Industry and ideal for anyone opening a bar or planning on opening a bar in the future. With hundreds of templates, forms and downloads instantly available, owners can create training manuals, policies and procedures while keeping up to date on best practice in the industry. Essential for any bar owner serious about managing their bar business effectively.

Co-Founder of UK Bartender's, Bar Management & Bar Owners Resource Website

Barkeeper.co.uk

February 2005 – 2012 (7 years)

Barkeeper.co.uk was founded to assist UK bars and pubs streamline their bar management with helpful tools and articles to drive profits and reduce costs and to allow bartenders access to the tools to manage a bar in the future.

Co-Founder of the largest resource for Irish Bar Owners

Barkeeper.ie

February 2004 – 2012 (8 years)

I co-founded Barkeeper.ie in 2004 as a result of demand from food, beverage and hospitality businesses in Ireland for an online resource where tools, downloads, articles and forms could be downloaded to assist in the effective management of the business. Barkeeper.ie is the ideal resource for opening or managing an Irish bar.

Co-Founder
