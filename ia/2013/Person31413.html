David A. (Dave) Falter's Summary

Successfully led companies ranging from $25MM to $1BB+ across numerous industries (online and offline corporate travel, electronic travel distribution, textiles and apparel, business services, consulting services, systems integration and software development, medical informatics, and cloud-based storage) by continually focusing on the creation and cultivation of dynamic, mission-driven, customer centric teams.

My personal mission is to inspire dedication, hard work, and loyalty among all team members and ensure they have the tools and the guidance to become high-performing, engaged executives within their functional domain and in their overall leadership of the enterprise.

Specialties: Strategic Planning, Sales Management, Organizational Analysis, Talent Management, M&A, Business Process Improvement, Consulting Services, Business Development, e-Commerce, Change Management, Enterprise Software, Information Management, Restructuring
