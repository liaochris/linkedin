Built distribution in niche retail, mass market and newsstand outlets; created a successful sales program in the promotional products industry. Conceived and launched the first comprehensive map e-commerce business. Supervised a team of 4 inside and outside account reps, plus marketing communications, customer service and warehouse.

Director of Sales & Marketing

DeLorme

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Consumer Electronics industry

1980 – 1995 (15 years) Yarmouth, ME

Starting as a one-person sales and marketing department for a regional publisher, I built a team of 18 as we expanded nationally. Established distribution in all mass market, office supply, outdoor sports and bookstore chains; created a highly productive telesales program to independent stores; launched a direct-store-delivery program in our home market; created profitable direct mail program; opened and supervised DeLorme’s retail store. Key contributor to this high margin business. Established company’s first General Ledger, specified and implemented first computer system. As Director of Marketing for the combined print and digital products groups, led an in-house publicity staff of two that delivered results worthy of a national agency; developed trade promotion programs for national accounts. Authored the business plan for the first product linking digital maps to GPS navigation satellites in real-time.
