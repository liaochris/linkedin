Marty Swingle's Summary

I own a successful Real Estate company in West Sacramento. I am an experienced Broker/REALTOR® with a marketing background and proven leadership abilities. I love to focus on the residential real estate needs of my core group of friends and referrals, while maintaining short sale, bank owned and traditional business in West Sacramento, Bridgeway Lakes, The Rivers, Davis, and surrounding areas in the Sacramento region.

If you are an experienced residential real estate professional and want to work with other professionals who share your values of honesty and integrity, I'd like to talk with you about the successful environment we've created to see if we are what you need.

Specialties: If you are interested in buying or selling your home, you need my help with today's complicated market. I specialize in pre-sale preparation and online marketing to get top dollar for your property.
