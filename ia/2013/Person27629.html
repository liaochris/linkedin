Craig Turner's Summary

I love Real Estate and the art of structuring the deal. It is my passion to help everyone achieve the American Dream of home ownership. I have been in the real estate and financial industries since 1985. I am based out of Colorado Springs, CO.

We currently sell to the 7 largest banks in the United States. We offer FHA, VA, Conventional mortgage financing as well as FHA 203K Rehab loans and USDA Rural Development 100% loans, Purchase as well as Refinance.

I help all of my clients from the first time home buyer to the very astute home buyer and Real Estate Investor structure their home purchase so that it is the most advantageous for them. I have helped many of my mortgage structure and negotiate their short sale purchase so that the best deal is what they get. We all know that the #1 Key to Success in Real Estate is TIMING. The first buyer that knows first wins.

My Team here at Financial Strategies Mortgage Services has over 35 years’ experience in the mortgage industry and has seen most every situation that could be imagined.

We know how to structure your mortgage loan to get it approved the first time and without any hitches. I can also tell you up front if what you want to do is a workable deal. I know the Real Estate, Short Sale and Foreclosure industry like the back of my hand. I will keep you informed about your mortgage loan process the whole way!

Go to my website at http://www.FSMS.co to find our more about our company and fill out a mortgage loan application today! You are also welcome to go to my Mortgage Video Blog to see the current industry news and client testimonial’s at http://www.FSMS.co. Please call me with any questions at 719-473-6398.
