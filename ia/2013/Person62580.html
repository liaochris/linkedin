Jeroen de Mooij's Summary

* I'm available for new projects! *

I'm from Rotterdam, the Netherlands, and we have a saying that goes like "Niet lullen, maar poetsen" which roughly translates into "Don't blabber, start cleaning".
That describes a part of my work approach. I am not a account manager, I want to make stuff.
Mostly digitally that is.

I would describe myself as an 3D generalist and I like to solve strange problems, I know my way around 3D, 2D and a little programming, but what I like to do most is combining these together into creative solutions.

My work history has always been in the visual and interactive world. First flash development then I went from serious gaming and forensic previsualizations to video projection mapping and interactive installations. During this journey learned a lot about schedules, deadlines, budgets and shortcuts to take.

Currently interested in:
Video projection mapping, touchdesigner, unity3D, motion graphics, motion capture, kinect, leap motion, VR goggles, 3D animation, Interactive installations, camera tracking, and so on.
