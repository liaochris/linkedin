Kat Lessin's Summary

Life & Business Design Coaching

I coach entrepreneurs who want to live their ideal life. I help them design and create businesses that support their dreams and values. I believe the purpose of life is to become your own best self through entrepreneurship/business ownership. Success is freedom used well and for the greater good. Life is at its best when your lifestyle, purpose, goals and values are working together. Otherwise, life is just plain frustrating and confusing. It doesn’t have to be that way.

I coach one-on-one with individuals and couples, assisting them to focus on action towards their goals and ideal life vision. I believe in creating plans and strategies that are uniquely designed and individualized to my clients’ values and beliefs. I don’t believe in a one-size-fits-all approach.

Lifestyle Design & Location Independence

I started Lifestyle Design Coaching because I knew that often, when an aspiring entrepreneur dreams of transitioning into self-employment, there are unique challenges in creating a business that allows the freedom for location independence. They often fear taking that leap of faith while still being able to pay the bills. The key is to put your values first and designing your life, business and strategy around those values so that you are in complete alignment. With the use of technology and proper systems, your lifestyle and business can truly be location independent, allowing all of the freedoms of time, choice and financial prosperity.

I use my intuition with my unique ability to ask judgment free questions and genuinely listen for the key elements that are needed to stop dreaming about “one day” and actually start to live those dreams.

If you want to experience the coaching, accountability and support you need to create and design your ideal life and business then head over to www.katlessin.com for a free exploratory session and my Goal Setting Blueprint.
