Al Adamsen's Summary

I help leaders be more confident and, in turn, effective. I do this by helping elevate awareness to the possibilities and limitations of one's own experience and current reality. My work is supported by a data-driven approach that's simple, practical, and engaging. And it works! I work with business leaders to formulate and execute talent strategies and athletic coaches to help define and deliver team success. I lead my own firm, People-Centered Strategies, and serve communities around the country through the Positive Coaching Alliance and Character Combine. As important as anything, I'm the father of two spectacular young people! Thank you for your interest in me and my work, and know I truly look forward to learning with you!
