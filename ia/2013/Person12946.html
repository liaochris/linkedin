Benito Behar's Summary

With over ten years of progressive management experience, my professional history includes work in the areas of operations, project management, sales, and international business development. During this time I've participated in projects that have represented milestones for several enterprises and institutions.

As Director of Operations for Pacific Spanish Network (PSN), I led a team that developed Tu Superestación, a regional television network servicing Baja California, Mexico. What started as a low-power station in the city of Ensenada became a statewide network through strategic partnerships with regional cable companies, such as Cablemás, and international content producers, like Venevisión. While with PSN, my team and I also built the first digital MMDS (Microwave Multichannel Distribution System) cable television system in Mexico, Baja Cable. My duties ranged from project managing the design and construction of the headend and antenna sites to negotiating contracts with content providers, such as Disney and Discovery Networks.

In 2008, I had the opportunity to create and manage the first international business unit for TV Magic, Inc. With the establishment of a wholly owned Mexican subsidiary, TV Magic S. de R. L. (Sociedad de Responsabilidad Limitada), our team designed and installed three substantial integration projects within the first year. The most notable of these integrations was the HD upgrade of the TV Azteca facility in Monterrey, Nuevo Leon, the first in Mexico to produce and broadcast in High Definition (1080i). Additionally, I managed integration projects for Universidad Iberoamericana in Tijuana, Mexico, Trinity Broadcasting Network in Irvine, California, and Enlace, Canal 22, in San Jose, Costa Rica.

I graduated from the University of San Diego in 2003, with a Bachelor’s Degree in Business Administration, and aspire to commence my MBA studies within the next two years.

Specialties

Strategic Planning, Business Development, Revenue Generation, Project Management, Bilingual (English and Spanish), and Team Development and Leadership.
