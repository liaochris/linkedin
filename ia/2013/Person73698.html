Keith Caswell's Summary

Armed with a healthy sense of wonder and fierce creativity, I approach everything with curiosity and an appreciation for educational value, from my humble first job at a library all the way up to my more recent freelance writing assignments. As a firm believer in Jack-of-all-trades-ism, I’ve cultivated an understanding that ego should never get in the way of sweating the small stuff.

As a writer, I am fascinated by the ways people communicate. As an Internet nerd, I am excited about the ways the web empowers open dialogue. I am also a self-identified grammar zealot. Leveraging the web to get great content noticed is kind of my thing. Let’s work together.

Specialties

Writing and editing, proofreading, social media, photography and graphic design, writing and recording music, organization and administrative duties
