Damiano Curia's Summary

I am an architect focusing on Microsoft technologies, in particular on SharePoint 2010 (and MOSS 2007); I also have strong expertise on the .NET/C# framework, that I use since it came out in 2001, and SQL Server, used since version 7. In order to attest my competency and re-enforce my knowledge, since 2005 I have obtained almost 30 Microsoft certifications. My current areas of interest are now SharePoint 2013 and Team Foundation Server, used to manage the whole ALM of IT projects using Microsoft tools.

I am very committed in studying languages. After many years spent improving my English skills, in the last 6+ months I have been focusing on German, studying it every night.

I am a very precise and scrupulous person. It is important for me that my team and I respect deadlines and produce high-quality deliverables. I like working in a team, be part of it, and grow within it and from confrontation with my colleagues. I think that a good Architect should be capable to manage a team in a way, where each member can continuously grow both as an individual but also together with the entire team.
