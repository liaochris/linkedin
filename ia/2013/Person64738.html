José Mª Sánchez Casillas' Summary

Founding Partner at Legisconsulting, a personal project born in 1999 with the aim of providing online specialized legal advice, complete and accessible to companies.

This project, since then, has been evolving to meet the demands of the new realities of the market and the need of the customers, who still need to operate within a traditional legal environment.
