Candice Sunney's Summary

Positive Personal Leadership facilitator and Life Strategist for entrepreneur & small business owners and those considering the transition to self employment.

Cultivate a positive mindset
Discover your positive core.
Who you are when you are at your best.
What you value and is meaningful for you .
Your balanced flow zone
Experience Peak Personal Performance.

When you have mastered Positive Personal Leadership - create and transition to the life you love!

Currently predominantly using Positive Psychology coaching and clean language methods which draws on social constructionist theory and cognitive linguistics and embracing the chaos life brings.

As a psychologist I put emphases on human behaviour theory drawn from developmental, positive, critical, analytical, social, transpersonal and existential Psychology.

Researching what it means to be a spiritual human exploring spiritual knowledge, intuition and connectedness. Drawing from research in Psychology, Philosophy, Sociology, Anthropology and the natural sciences.

More than 20 years experience within both the Hospitality & Service Industry and Indian and Vietnam Travel & Tourism industries up to middle management with a particular focus on training and development.
