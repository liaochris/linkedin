Ellen Sutton's Summary

Visual Communications Master
Design Maven, Brand Guardian, Data Wrangler, Princess of Presos

Ellen thrives on travel, adventure, soy lattes—and apparently, moving. She brings perspectives from DC, Sydney, the Bay Area, Los Angeles, and now Nashville.

In California, she was mentored by presentation guru, Nancy Duarte. At Duarte Design, Ellen served as the point of contact for client accounts at Cisco Systems, Disney, Apple, Google, and TED. She led meetings for Silicon Valley marketing executives and assembled cross-functional teams to create digital marketing strategies.

Ellen cultivates order from chaos. She has an instinct for discerning emerging patterns and relationships. It has been suggested that childhood encephalitis enhanced her uncanny aptitude for tackling right-brained (design-based) problems to which she then applies her left-brain (manager/organizer).

Ellen is also a self-proclaimed yoga buff and online shopping addict.
