Gina Strumpf's Summary

Gina Strumpf knew from an early age she was born to be an artist. She is an internationally recognized impressionist oil painter, whose work is sought after by collectors in the USA and Europe. She has mentored with nationally acclaimed Artists, along with studies and workshops with many respected Artists/teachers in Canada, Southern France and Tuscany. She is completing her life long dream and her true calling.

Gina's paintings have been shown in numerous juried regional and national shows as well as multiple Solo Shows. She is a featured artist in galleries throughout the Southeast from Washington DC to Charleston, SC with private and corporate collectors throughout the USA, and Europe, many of her collectors owning multiple works!

Well versed in multiple mediums (oil, acrylic and water color) her paintings are rich in color, mood and spirit. Gina’s work is best described as modern impressionist... there is an inherent warmth and quality to her work that pulls the viewer in and won't let them go. As an Artist, she would like her paintings to represent the passion she feels in her subject matter. Gina is recognized by her distinctive style in her popular "Red Umbrella" European and New York City-Scapes. She loves painting En Plein Air and traveling for inspiration when ever possible to new and exciting locations.

Gina is a sought after workshop instructor, keynote speaker and the Resident Artist on Cunard Lines.
Her engaging and passionate teaching style with an encouraging devotion to her students is what makes her workshops such a huge success. Providing step-by-step instruction, delivered in her comprehensive light hearted manner her students have enjoyed learning while having fun. She is eager to share her wealth of knowledge and is delighted to see the excitement grow among her students.

She works in her studio in Charlotte, NC... her door is always open!

January 2004 – Present (9 years 10 months) Charlotte, NC

Gina knew from an early age she wanted to be an artist. Her pens and pencils were her constant companions. With a very successful career in Real Estate it was finally time to get back to her first love. And like an old friend... Art was there waiting. Her brushes haven't left her hands for many years now. With lots of time to make up, Gina is devoted to learning and improving daily. Gina has mentored with nationally acclaimed Artist, Kevin Macpherson, Andy Braitman and Connie Winters along with studies and workshops with acclaimed artists in Canada, Southern France and Tuscany. Gina has honed her skills in record time, a prolific painter with natural talent and a quick study. She is completing her life long dream and her true calling.

Gina's paintings have been shown in numerous juried regional and national shows as well as Solo Shows. She is a featured artist in numerous galleries throughout the Southeast and has many private and corporate collectors throughout the USA and Europe. With many of her collectors owning multiple works!

Well versed in multiple mediums and varied subject matter Gina's paintings are rich in color, mood and spirit. There is an inherent warmth and quality to her work that pulls the viewer in and won't let them go.

As an Artist, she would like her paintings to represent the passion she feels in her subject matter. Gina is recognized by her distinctive style in her popular "Red Umbrella" European and New York City scapes. She loves painting En Plein Air and traveling when ever possible to new and exciting locations. Gina is the Resident Artist on Cunard Lines and spends her layovers in France and the UK seeking inspiration for many of her paintings. She works in her studio in Charlotte, NC... her door is always open!
