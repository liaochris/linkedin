Designer Spirit of a Woman Jewelry

FOOTPRINTS FOUNDATION

September 2010 – Present (3 years 1 month) Miami

Our jewelry is handmade in Peru. The design represents the power of women gathering in villages around the world. The stone at the top of the pendant represents women behind barrier and women suffering abuses and atrocities in many countries. Please do not forget these women, they are us. Each day symbolically rub one finger on this stone and say a silent prayer for them.
The second stone is you. Gentle rub your finger on the stone and set your intention for the day.
The silver used to make the SPIRIT OF A WOMAN comes from the mines in North Lambayeque, Peru. The stones are also Peruvian from Cusco, Machupicchu, Chiclayo and Sacred Valley of the Incas.
Peruvian Turquoise represents the Mother of the earth and signifies good health. The Conch Coral Orange represents the Sea and signifies Prosperity. White Mother of Pearl represents energy. The Yellow stone is Nacar and represents balance. The Purple Coral stone represents transformation .Finally; Azul represents our spiritual connection to God.
When you wear this pendant you are uniting with thousands of women gathering in villages around the world. We gather to laugh, to cry, to share stories and to speak our truth.
A woman wearing this pendant is Sister show her love, always treat her with respect but most of all give her a special greeting.

Founder and CEO

Footprints Foundation

February 2009 – Present (4 years 8 months) Coral Gables Florida.

Our Mission is to improve mortality rates of mothers and infants by training skilled midwives and traditional birth attendants in the essentials of Maternal and New Born care and by providing access to emergency obstetric care. We also provide surgery and psychological counselling for the women who have been raped. Our biggest challenge is our work in conflict areas such as Eastern Congo and the ability to provide care to very rural populations.We are always looking for volunteers willing to share best practices or to travel with us to the D.R.Congo.Our next country will be Liberia
