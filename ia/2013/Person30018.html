Danika Atkins' Summary

Knowledge junkie with a penchant for learning new ways to approach internet marketing. Every day, I look for opportunities to combine my unique background in fund raising, event planning, sales, and internet marketing to add value to my interactions with clients and bring a unique perspective to marketing.

I believe in having a close relationship with clients, and will be hands-on with at least one aspect of a project, whether it's building sites in Wordpress, developing custom logos and graphics, or creating customized social media strategies.
