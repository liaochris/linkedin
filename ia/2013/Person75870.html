Kim Steadman's Summary

A continued willingness to learn new things and try new opportunities has been my life and career story. I call myself a Modern Renaissance Woman due to myr “Jill-of-all-Trades” mentality and the fact that I love learning. I'm flexible and tend to adapt which has allowed me to experience everything from the arts, raising chickens and goats, home building, home schooling our only son, all the way to the opposite spectrum of the corporate world managing various operations..and points in between! Throw in the new world as a new grandma and I will tell you "life is to exciting to be bored!!"

After years of being a stay at home mom and homeschooling our son, I reentered the full time work force after he graduated from high school to accomplish two things. First, pay for college tuition. Second, to keep my mind busy in order to prepare for <da da da dum> "The Empty Nest".

I've enjoyed learning a host of new things while working in the recycling industry. Having personally been a person that recycles before recycling became cool, it has been a joy to watch the industry grow through the years. The growth of the industry allowed me to learn various job skills. I am always willing to try something new, at least once <smile>!

I like to say that my specialties in the public work place is that of a "Linchpin" (Thank you Seth Goddin for defining what I do). I'm known to become emotionally and personally passionate in a job and task. I want to make things happen, make sure they are done correctly. Something in chaos is just a new challenge to make something better. Creativity is one of my values.

My specialty out of the work place is Networking. I try to encourage others to rise above the mentality of the "daily rat race" and look for the positive in their daily environment. My latest endeavor is to pursue my personal passion of writing.
