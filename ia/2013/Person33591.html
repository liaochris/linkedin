President and CEO of environmental nonprofit organization

Restore Mass Ave

2006 – Present (7 years) Washington D.C. Metro Area

I founded Restore Mass Ave as a sideline, to fix the sick trees along streets near my home on Embassy Row. The group has grown thanks to our wonderful volunteers and expert advisors. So far we've arranged more than 100 new city trees and another 125 trees through plantings. About 30 embassies participate, as do dozens of US stakeholders. Look for our Tree Care blog and our forthcoming book, "Saving America's Last Grand Avenue." Quite a hobby!
