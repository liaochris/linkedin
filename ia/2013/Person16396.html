    Utilizing WordPress and a few key plugins, I created a website which automates most of the process involved in this concept. Businesses can book a calendar day for their sale, upload sale information, and select a target audience based on information collected from shoppers during signup. Shopping members are encouraged to “Like” the SaleHereNow Facebook page and follow the twitter account, and when they do, they are given special access to facebook and twitter posts offering greater savings.
