Jaime Levey's Summary

Jaime Levey is a seasoned photographer, Photo retouching and Graphic Design. Over the past twelve years, She has had the opportunity to participate in vast areas of photography and Graphic Design, from event photography to commercial advertising. Jaime has worked with many different professionals in various fields of Marketing. She held the position of In-House Photographer for Quicken Loans. Through this experience and demonstrated the ability to work independently, as well as become a valued team member. In addition to her past experiences, her communication and leadership skills have been cultivated through extracurricular involvement and participation in projects with a vast array of diversity. Jaime work has appeared in magazines, advertisements, CD covers and much more. She has also worked in a wide range of medium dealing with photography as well, worked on advertising projects in Michigan and in California.
