Daniel A Perry, AIA, LEED AP BD+C's Summary

I am an Architect who builds...

Construction professional with broad-based general contracting, construction management and architectural expertise. My experience has allowed me to develop a deep understanding of how to balance an architect’s sensibility with a constructor’s pragmatism. I understand what it takes to do great design, and how to build it.

My background includes principal and project management roles with responsibility for profit, project planning and analysis, leading teams, and completing projects both as an architect and as a constructor. I have also been involved in organizational improvement, and the introduction and implementation of design and project management technologies. I have worked with companies of various sizes in the architectural, general contracting/construction management, design-build and owner/developer markets.

I enjoy being on, developing and leading cross-functional project teams. From experience, I know that by utilizing my architectural, construction and communication skills, I enable teams to make solid decisions in a logical, timely and cost effective manner, resulting in quality projects completed on time and on budget. This drives end user / client satisfaction. I have been privileged to have earned numerous repeat clients. I have accomplished this throughout my career.

Specialties: Construction Management, General Contracting, Design-Build
