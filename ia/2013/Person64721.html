Jordan Monson's Summary

I love interactive stuff. Before there were tablets, smartphones, broadband…heck, when we were still using floppy disks and 28k dial-up modems, I was tinkering with HTML and Photoshop in my parents basement. That was a long time ago, but my intrigue with web/mobile technology coupled with my love affair of design and usability is stronger than ever.

Every day I come to work as Creative Director for Clutch Interactive, I get to hang out with 30 of my best friends. Not only that, but I also get to work with some of the coolest clients at McGraw-Hill, Abbott Nutrition, Cardinal Health, Berkshire Hathaway, Honda, American Greetings, Johnson & Johnson, P&G, Microsoft, Huntington Bank, Nationwide Insurance, and Limited Brands.

My average day is a mash-up of strategizing mobile solutions, leading responsive visual design concepts, directing video and animation projects, and raiding the kitchen for leftovers. On occasion, you may also find me with Word or Powerpoint open prepping proposals and presentations for our next client meet-up.

I'm proud of the work I've created and the teams I've worked with. My street cred is 2 regional ADDY awards, a regional Emmy award, being featured on Adobe's Site Of The Day showcase (twice), a MedAd News award, and being featured in the LogoLounge Master Library Series books (twice).

I definitely haven't achieved all of my personal goals, but I try to get one step closer everyday, changing the world one pixel at a time.

Jordan Monson's Projects

  • Long John Silver's Freshside Grille

    • August 2008 to September 2008
    Team Members: Jordan Monson, Aaron Reiser, Will Burrus

    The Freshside Grille project was my first exposure working personally with Papervision 3D on the Flash platform. I took this project from start to finish, creating all of the visuals and Flash development. The microsite was a fun collaboration with LJS's marketing team launching a fresh new menu across the nation.

  • White Castle Crave Is Calling Microsite

    • July 2008 to August 2008
    Team Members: Jordan Monson, Aaron Reiser, Will Burrus

    Truth be told, I have a secret addiction to White Castle burgers, so this project was a match made in heaven. Building off the larger Crave Is Calling ad campaign, the microsite was a fun dive deeper into the crave lair.

    On this project I worked with the 3D team to create the lair, designed the user interface, and developed the project in Adobe Flash. The site also had wire-ups into the official White Castle YouTube channel.

  • 24 @ OSU Opening Title Sequence

    • May 2008 to June 2008
    Team Members: Jordan Monson, Kevin Theessen

    This was a fun opening title sequence for a locally produced TV show on the PBS Network. Simple in concept, the open flies through the daily happenings around the OSU campus in a 3D parallaxing world.
