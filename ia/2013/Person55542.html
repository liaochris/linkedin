Brad Womack's Summary

We are the Natural Stone Professionals and Grout Gurus for all of your Cleaning, Repair and Restoration needs! Our technicians are factory trained and certified, fully insured. Most of our current customers can't wait to refer us to all of their friends, family and other networks. We use only the finest and most effective cleaning system known in the industry.

Specialties

We specialize in cleaning, repairing and restoring all types of hard surfaces, including but not limited to: natural stone, slate, marble, granite, brick paver, porcelain and ceramic tile and grout. Because of our installation experiences, we are able to repair and replace any broken tile and grout. We have a unique product that we use to Permanently Seal with Color giving the consumer a permanent seal to the grout guaranteed for as long as they live in the home!

Assistant Manager & Purchasing Manager

Classic Tile, Stone & Marble

2002 – 2007 (5 years)

Started as salesman in showroom learning all there was to know about tile and natural stone.  From there my talents were also used with flooring contractors by running the front sales counter.  Then as the company grew, I was promoted to Assistant Manager over the warehouse.  The owner, Mark Hammond, and warehouse manager, Matt Demaree, and I decided that our company needed to have policies and procedures on how to purchase more cost effectively.  We decided that I would move into the role of Purchasing Manager as well as keeping the title of Assistant Manager.  It was while I was working here that I noticed that no one in this industry knew, or wanted to know anything about cleaning and/or sealing natural stone and tile.  It was at this point that I decided to start looking for a way to start a company and make money doing what no one else desired to do, clean, seal and restore natural stone and tile. 
In 2006 I was able to find the equipment I was looking for and started my cleaning business called Integrity Tile Cleaning. I was fortunate enough to be able to still keep my current positions at Classic Tile, while working in the evenings on my cleaning business for the first year. After the cleaning business picked up and I was able to land a few accounts, I decided to give Mark and Matt my 6 month notice to quit and pursue this new cleaning business. Because of my previous installation and sales backgrounds, I am able to effectively promote my current businesses of cleaning and repairing any hard surfaces.
