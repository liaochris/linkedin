Badar Iqbal's Summary

JobsMiddleEast (JME) is a leading UK online recruitment site, dedicated to helping you find your next job in the Middle East. We are not only a recruitment agency but also numerous UK & Middle East agencies and Employers use our website to find candidates just like you.

We know that looking for a job can be frustrating and JME aim to take the pain out of job hunting. Our intelligent search and matching tools bring you and recruiters together quickly and easily

Whatever sector and region you are job hunting in, JME will search through thousands of jobs to find you the right one.

Founded in 1997 JME is a well established organisation and is a familiar name to key players in the employment market across the Middle East as well as being highly committed to the region. We consistently support employers with Saudization in KSA and across the other Middle East countries we are are committed to finding and retaining the best employees.

We don't just talk the talk, our founder worked in the Middle East for over 10 years before returning to the UK to establish JME therefore our leadership team has direct experience of working in the environments we recruit within.

The world is out there, it has no boundaries, now go explore!
