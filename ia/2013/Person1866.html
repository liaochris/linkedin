Amanda Knowles' Summary

With a master’s degree in journalism, Amanda Knowles has 6 years experience in communications and marketing, in print and online. A skilled Web and social media professional, Amanda has an exceptional record in project management, online content strategy, social media marketing, writing and copy editing. She is a detail-oriented strategic thinker, a leader and a team player, and excels in both creative and technical endeavors. Amanda uses her knowledge and enthusiasm to create positive and effective user experiences on the Web. As a project manager she consistently meets or exceeds expectations, ensuring delivery of a quality product on time and on budget. A passionate Web and social media strategist, Amanda strives to stay current with best practices and new technologies to deliver innovative solutions across multiple platforms.
