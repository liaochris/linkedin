C. Jason Smith's Summary

I provide a wide range of civil litigation, intellectual property and business transaction services.

Since 2005 I have assisted clients recover over $30,000,000 in funds and assets via trials, arbitrations and settlements and have assisted several other clients in averting millions in liability. Some of my notable representative matters include: (1) a multi million arbitration award relating to a gold mining dispute; (2) a $1.08 million arbitration award against motion picture distributor Think Film; (3) the recovery of millions in assets regarding a financial abuse of an elder action; (3) a substantial confidential settlement involving an employment background screening company, a widely publicized sexual assault and the destruction of a large cable installation company; (4) successful resolution of a trademark dispute involving multi platinum rap artist “Young Jeezy” as well as various other “entertainment” disputes involving recording artists such as Journey, B2K, Li’l Scrappy and others; (5) litigation of a complex franchise dispute involving two national furniture retailers; (6) recovery of real property transferred pursuant to a nationwide fraudulent debt elimination scheme --- also subject to FBI investigation; (7) a finding of fraud by clear and convincing evidence in a complex three week trial against an unlicensed architect; (8) a full defense award based on finding claimant committed fraud; (9) successful representation in numerous other complex commercial disputes.
