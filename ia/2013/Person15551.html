Bob Williams' Summary

I have been working in partnership with a worldwide cross-section of agency creative’s for more then twenty-five years helping them to achieve a look and style for their automotive and non-automotive clients alike.

My diversified photographic background includes shooting for clients in technology, office and home interior products, automotive and their manufacturing companies as well as retail catalog companies.

The photographic challenge is to show how light and design help define simple everyday products into pieces of sculpture and things of beauty.

Specialties

Creative skill sets combined with an accountant’s attention to detail, Bob’s ability to consistently balance a projects bottom line with it’s creative top line have established a following with agency creative’s and art buyers alike.

A perfectionist with a unique approach, his expert use of digital crew, equipment and software help him merge his client’s creative passions into a seamless digital workflow.
