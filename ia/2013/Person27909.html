Cristina is the Principal and Owner of Benedicto Insurance Agencies, a multi-line insurance company established in 1981. Aside from being a Managing General Agent for American General as well as Industrial Alliance American Life, a large and solid insurance company which markets life insurance and annuity products and manages about 50 billion in assets, Cristina also represents Secure Horizons and UnitedHealth Care, Lincoln Financial, Hartford, United Home Life, Anthem Blue Cross, Standard Life,Transamerica, West Coast Life, among others.

Over two decades, it has been Cristina's privilege to serve thousands of clients in the Southern California area. Cristina's extensive experience in the insurance industry have produced a multitude of knowledge, skills, education and relationships which greatly enhance the lives of her clients, many of whom she has known and served since the beginning of her career in the industry.

Cristina is committed to leaving her clients better off than before she met them, whether this be in terms of their personal, spiritual and social well-being, or in terms of their financial and economic welfare. Cristina's mission and vision translates, specifically, into helping her clients protect themselves, their families and their businesses with the insurance products best suited for their needs and providing consistent and reliable service throughout the continuum of her long-term relationship with them.

The insurance and financial products Benedicto Insurance Agencies offer are: traditional and cutting-edge life insurance products, long-term care, annuities, individual/group medical insurance, dental plans, medicare advantage, medicare supplements, 403B's, disability income protection and critical illness products. The company product portfolio also includes auto and homeowners insurance, business liability, commercial insurance, Worker's Compensation, Malpractice insurance, and Employee Benefits,
