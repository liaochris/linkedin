Ryan Richards' Summary

While I have been producing freelance video and photography work since 2009, during that time I have also worked as a project consultant for a design/build renovation firm, a paralegal for a well-resoected defense attorney and in other capacities in the legal field. Law is our current focus for the production side of the freelance life, with a full-length documentary on wrongful convictions and policy reforms occupying much of our time since the fall of 2010. Directly related to production work, the past three years have included consulting, prisoner re-integration and university liaison experience.

Specialties: Proposal writing, media conceptualization, production and editing, photography, research and documentation, event coordination, attorney/client liaison, public policy and legal integration, sales and marketing of legal, political and small business services.
