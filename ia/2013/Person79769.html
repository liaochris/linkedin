One of the things I'm proudest of is being the co-founder and CEO of TopRank Online Marketing - a digital marketing agency based in Minnesota that works with B2B and B2C companies in North America to create better connections with customers and improve sales through an integrated approach to digital PR, search, content and social media marketing.

Our agency provides both strategic online marketing planning and implementation / consulting services for numerous Fortune 500 companies as well as one of the top social networks.

We've assembled a super team of digital marketing specialists in research/analysis, content strategy, social media marketing, search engine optimization, online public relations, web analytics, conversion optimization and client management to help our clients attract, engage and convert more new business.

I'm also the author of Optimize: How to Attract and Engage More Customers by Integrating SEO, Social Media and Content Marketing (Wiley) and a blogger for over 10 years at TopRank's Online Marketing Blog.

I've given presentations, keynotes and workshops all over the world (NYC, London, Hong Kong, Moscow, Sydney, San Francisco, Barcelona) on integrated online marketing.

Some of my skills specialties include: digital marketing strategy, integrated marketing, online marketing, content marketing, thought leader marketing, search engine optimization, SEO, public relations, B2B marketing, blogging, social media, internet marketing, public speaking

In the past I've served on the board of advisors for Incisive Media's SES conference, DMA Social Media and Search Marketing Councils and MIMA.

Lee Odden's Publications

  • Optimize: How to Attract and Engage More Customers by Integrating SEO, Social Media and Content Marketing

    • Wiley
    • April 17, 2012
    Authors: Lee Odden

    Optimize is designed to give readers a practical approach to integrating search and social media optimization with content marketing to boost relevance, visibility, and customer engagement. Companies, large and small, will benefit from the practical planning and creative content marketing tactics in this book that have been proven to increase online performance across marketing, public relations, and customer service. Learn to incorporate essential content optimization and social media engagement principles thereby increasing their ability to acquire and engage relevant customers online.

    Optimize provides insights from Lee Odden, one of the leading authorities on Content and Online Marketing. This book explains how to:

    - Create a blueprint for integrated search, social media and content marketing strategy
    - Determine which creative tactics will provide the best results for your company
    - Implement search and social optimization holistically in the organization
    - Measure the business value of optimized and socialized content marketing
    - Develop guidelines, processes and training to scale online marketing success

    Optimize offers a tested approach for a customer-centric and adaptive online marketing strategy that incorporates the best of content, social media marketing, and search engine optimization tactics.
