Alexandra Watkins' Summary

Author of the upcoming book, "Hello, My Name is Awesome... How to Create Brand Names That Stick" (published in 2014 by Berrett-Koehler), I am the founder of Eat My Words, a wildly creative naming firm featured multiple times in The Wall Street Journal and Inc. I am also a frequent guest speaker at MBA programs and have presented to Stanford GSB, SF State, USF School of Management, and Proctor & Gamble alumni.

Eat My Words' clients include Disney, Microsoft, Wrigley, LPL Financial, Orrick, Fujitsu, Hasbro, Frito-Lay, Del Monte, and leading branding firms (like Landor) who put their good name on our great names.

When you see an Eat My Words name, you'll never ask what it means, how to pronounce it, or "What were they smoking?!" Our love at first sight names make powerful emotional connections, build instant brand affinity, and have proven ROI.
