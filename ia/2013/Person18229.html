Bruce Dinh's Summary

I’m a designer, leader, and problem-solver with uncompromising standards and a commitment to quality. I believe that the best and most successful design solutions harmonize form with function, engage and resonate with consumers, and develop brand loyalty through powerful storytelling with seamless user experiences.

In addition to branding and strategy for high-profile and luxury consumer brands such as BCBG, the Walt Disney Company, and the University of Southern California, my work also includes developing indentity systems and creative collateral to support small businesses in the automotive, food, and health industries.

I’m well-seasoned in developing typographic hierarchies, layouts, logos, and also have extensive experience with automotive photography and retouching. I’m expanding my skillsets with a working knowledge of HTML/CSS, Javascript, UI/UX design and am interested in work that will challenge, inspire, and allow me to combine past experiences with new media to create innovative and memorable solutions.
