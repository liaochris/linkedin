My mission is to demystify the ancient art and science of Feng Shui through private consultations, lectures, workshops and writings. Commercial and residential clients around the world have discovered how they can change their lives both professionally and personally with my guidance.

Diane Gallin's Experience

Professional Feng Shui Consultant, Chinese Astrologer, Speaker and Author

Wind and Water Feng Shui Consulting

Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Design industry

June 2004 – Present (9 years 7 months)

Diane Gallin is the owner and founder of Wind and Water Feng Shui Consulting, and Red Ribbon Professional Member of the International Feng Shui Guild.

Feng Shui is the ancient Chinese art and science of mindful placement, incorporating the use of interior design details, environmentalism, furniture placement, color, light, movement and sound, to positively influence living and working spaces. Diane teaches private and commercial clients how to positively impact their careers, relationships, prosperity and luck by adapting the Feng Shui principles of balance and harmony to their environment.

Services include residential and commercial consultations, lectures, healthy building guidelines, corporate workshops and site selection for property acquisition. Diane's consultations and workshops have benefited clients worldwide.
