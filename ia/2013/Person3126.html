Alan Haley's Summary

Founder and still hanging onto the role of MD at Europa 19 years on, I am one of those lucky people paid to do a job I love – although they do work me far too hard for a child of the X Generation!

I used to be employed for my experience in IT sales and marketing but now it appears it is organisational growth, recruitment and team development skills that justify my salary!

Our business is about demand generation and development of new and existing markets for our clients. Maintaining our excellence at this has always been paramount to me, so quality and skills form the foundation of this business.

Fortunately I relish the challenge of a changing market and have surrounded myself with others that do also. Whether it is technological change like cloud, virtualisation and IP everything; process change into new sales and marketing methodologies, or business shifts towards new forms of outsourcing and collaboration, this is where we thrive.

All of this involves the following:

- A real understanding of the needs of the technology sector and the markets in which our clients operate

- Managing teams of business developers with in-depth technology experience

- Excellent planning and project management, training and motivational skills

- Highly skilled in all forms of communication from digital to tele-, to personal presence

- Multilingual and multinational capability

- The flexibility, reliability and accountability that is essential if anyone is to place their business in our hands

We called this company Europa for a reason, because conducting pan-European campaigns from a single point of control has always been a core strategy. Indeed now we find ourselves operating globally in some cases, with sales professionals based all over the world.

So I am proud to be part of a team committed to maintaining a corporate culture built on Energy, Experience, Creativity, Intelligent Marketing, Skills Training and Development and real Integrity.

Specialties

IT market development; market research and analysis; technology sales resourcing and outsourcing; integrated marketing and social media solutions; professional IT-focussed telemarketing; channel business support; serving international markets.

Deep knowledge of IT infrastructure; storage; ERP; CRM; cloud; security; telecomms; managed services; middleware.

Accredited sales developers trained in Microsoft; IBM; VMware; EMC; Citrix; NetApp; Cisco. CIM trained marketing specialists.
