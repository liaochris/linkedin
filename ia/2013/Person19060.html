Adrian Fusiarski's Summary

I help run a small digital marketing agency which involves setting up social media accounts and managing them for small business clients and artistic types. I have seven years experience working with the Wordpress platform, which includes content writing and management, theme editing, plug-in implementation and testing, widgets and most all points in between. I also help people understand how to use SEO effectively, simply and organically.

I Tweet about the new music industry and disruptive technology as @Buzzsonic and about small biz tech, social media, SEO and Wordpress as @BzzzSocial

I'm also known as recording artist, 99th Floor Elevators, with some national UK chart history with Pete Waterman's PWL (The Hit Factory) and appearances on over fifty compilation albums worldwide. I've appeared on albums for EMI, Universal, BMG, Virgin, Tidy, Ministry of Sound and Armada, amongst many others.

I've been blogging sporadically at BzzzSocial.co, Buzzsonic.com and 99thFloorElevators.co.uk for years now and am currently plotting a new 99th Floor Elevators single and writing an ebook 'The Buzzsonic Guide to Digital Music Distribution', both due for release later in 2013.

I'm always looking for co-conspirators and freelance/contract gigs either in the UK or the USA (where I have a Green Card visa) ideally encompassing social media, Wordpress, SEO and the music industry.

Specialties: Advanced Wordpress management, niche content writing and social media management and implementation, music industry consultant and blogger. MP3 distribution services.
