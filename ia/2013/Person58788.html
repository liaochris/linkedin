Jared Yellin's Summary

President of Synduit (Your Synergistic Conduit to Share Your Message with the World),an outsourced sales and marketing organization for practitioners, small business owners, and entrepreneurs. Synduit has become an "overnight" start-up success, as it has flourished into an organization that is serving small business owners from around the world. Jared's relentless desire to help people define and refine their WHY, and then assist by means of developing/executing on strategies to reach their audience with this message, is the reason why Synduit has established itself as a prominent leader within the small business community. Synduit becomes the Marketing Department for businesses who are in dire need of a synergistic conduit to share a passion infused message about what they can offer the world, and most importantly, why they do, what they do. Jared has an innate ability to help individuals find their personal brand within their spoken and written message so that it can be delivered consistently and over the course of time.

Jared Yellin has been an entrepreneur since graduating early from the Kelley School of Business at Indiana University. Upon earning his degree, Jared launched a reverse mortgage startup company in Chicago, spent time on Wall Street, worked at a health and wellness startup company, launched a personal development organization where he lectures for chiropractors, undergraduate students, entrepreneurs, etc. and most recently launched Synduit LLC, an outsourced sales and marketing company. He is a wellness enthusiast and focuses on BEing the model by means of a three dimensional wellness lifestyle (Physical, Biochemical, and Emotional).

Jared's personal mission is to see something in people that they do not realize exists and then help access this untapped potential.

Please visit www.synduit.com to learn more about Synduit.
