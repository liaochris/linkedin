Liz Jenkins, CPO's Summary

After owning and managing several businesses, teaching special education for many years, and serving on boards of charitable organizations, I realized that my strengths are in organizing and getting things done.
As a Certified Professional Organizer (CPO), I assist my clients with reducing chaos in their lives, creating a sense of order and helping them find their stuff when they need it. My clients tell me that they save both time and money after working with me - which, in today's economy, is critical. I focus on home offices & small businesses, residential organizing, and working with families having children with special needs. I've had many years of experience to bring to my clients and have also been a Professional Organizer on the hit A & E show Hoarders, as well as a contributor for numerous publications and blogs around the country.
As a Home Stager, I make homes sell faster and for more money by taking a tired home and turning it into a showcase. My team will do everything from paint selection, furniture re-arranging and accessorizing . . . all the way to analyzing repairs and maintenance needs.
As a Project Manager working primarily with clients putting their houses on the market, I get projects done such as kitchen renovations, painting, flooring, lighting, organizing and more designed to make their homes as marketable as possible. My planning, on site management, scheduling and follow up with contractors as well as the ability to relieve the home owner of worry and hassle can save both time and money.
You can always find me at www.afreshspace.com!
