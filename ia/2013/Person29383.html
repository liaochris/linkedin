Daniel Anderson's Projects

  • TWELVE

    • March 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Daniel Anderson, Candy Bowers, Kim 'Busty Beatz' Bowers, Sammie Williams, Leonie Tillman, Imraan Paleker, Ben Shaw, Teila Watson, Mark Lowdnes, Tau Manukia, David Dow

    Twelve is a piece of soulful music theatre based on Shakespeare’s Twelfth Night and set in the aftermath of a major natural disaster. A rich mix of dance, spoken word poetry, rhythm, blues and grime- this new work sees the hip hop underground hijack the stage in an exploration of love under crisis. Join the indomitable Candy B, the brazen Busty Beatz and their crew of astonishing musicians, dancers and artists as they remix Shakespeare for a contemporary audience. This showing is the result of a two-week creative development and your first chance to glimpse the next big thing from the creators of festival hit Australian Booty.
