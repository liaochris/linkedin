Donna Schuback, CPC's Summary

Donna Schuback has over 25 years of recruiting experience, primarily focused on sourcing professionals in the IT industry.

Schuback Search Associates, Ltd., a certified Women Owned Business, was founded in 2000 with the mission of assisting companies to recruit outstanding employees and candidates to find a "home" with rewarding career opportunities. Most search positions are direct hire with some additional contracts for computer professionals in the following areas: software developers (.net, Oracle, Java, etc.), network engineers (Cisco, etc.), systems administrators [Windows, Unix, I-Series (As/400)], DBAs (Oracle, SQL Server, etc.), Technical Writers, Instructional Designers, ERP specialists (SAP, Oracle,PeopleSoft, JDE, etc.), senior managers, and many others.
Additional recruiting areas have included: Human Resources, Accounting, and executive level management. Donna Schuback holds CPC certification through the Texas Association of Personnel Consultants.

The Schuback Search Associates' website (www.schubacksearch.com) includes a Jobs Portal which also allows for nationwide searching of all job categories across the entire United States. This includes the ability for the job seeker to create alerts for jobs that arise in their areas of expertise.

Specialties: Primary specialty is recruiting Information Technology (IT) professionals, both contract and permanent positions, across all industries.
