Kris Trudeau's Summary

I've been a full time Website and Graphic Designer for over 12 years and I have loved every minute of it.

My expertise ranges from designing and coding attractive and functional websites to designing printed pieces any medium. With strong HTML/CSS coding abilites, in-depth prepress knowledge and experience in developing advertising strategies, my skill base is broad and diverse.

I'm well versed in search engine optimization, Google AdWords and Facebook Ads and consult with small businesses on how best to get the biggest buck for their advertising budget.

Specialties

CSS, HTML and XHTML, brand management, print collateral, large format graphics, image manipulation, vector illustrations, AdWords, Analytics, SEO, management, project management, separations, imposition, colour accuracy, press plates and film stripping, wireframes/storyboards,
