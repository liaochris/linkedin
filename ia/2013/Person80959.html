Linda Benn's Summary

Linda Benn is a Stress Reduction Expert helping company directors, owners and employees to cope with stress and learn healthy ways to manage difficult situations and circumstances in their work environment.

As an expert in body mind connection, stress management and worker productivity, Linda can quickly assess an individual’s level of stress, determine the conditions and causes, and suggest alternate behaviors, work habits, and/or environmental changes to alleviate the stresses and conditions that are impacting their energy and productivity.

Some of the tools and techniques that Linda has available to accomplish her work include postural evaluation, ergonomic assessments, yoga training, energy work to release blocked energy, meditation, nutritional counselling, breathing exercises, communication, coaching and mentoring.

For years she has been helping clients with chronic pain relief due to emotional stress, trauma, different postural imbalances and patterns of dysfunction in the body’s extremities.
Linda is a high energy presenter and she has a unique gift when working with people either one on one or in groups to achieve the best possible results.

Linda is also a certified trainer and presenter in motivational and personal development workshops that empower and awaken people to gain clarity, awareness to make better choices and decisions to create the results in their life easily.
Linda is highly motivated, passionate and energetic helping people to start their day with positive attitude, energy & zest for life. She has worked with audiences internationally in a fun filled presentation that takes them to new levels of personal expectations. She is a high impact presenter, an experienced coach with many years of experience in bodywork & healing techniques.


Specialties: Executive Stress relief coach, Pain management consultant, Yoga in the workplace, Postural structural realignment, Body Mind consultant. Emotional anxiety release
