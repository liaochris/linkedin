Jean Hanson, I.C.E.'s Summary

Jean Hanson has co-owned two commercial cleaning companies since 1986. After selling the first company in Boise 2002, Jean and her husband Steve started another cleaning company in Minnesota. At the same time she started a professional virtual assistant business, which she ran for 5 years. In 2005 the Hansons started an online subscription website for owners of cleaning companies, which they still run today.

In 2013 Jean became an Authorized Duct Tape Marketing Consultant. She receives exclusive rights to implement the Duct Tape Marketing small-business marketing system, and is the sole consultant in Minnesota, although she teaches the system to business owners around the country.

Specialties: Starting and running successful cleaning businesses. Marketing and running a membership website.
