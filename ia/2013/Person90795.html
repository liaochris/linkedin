Kevin Meek's Summary

Kevin graduated with his Bachelor's of Fine Arts degree in New Media in 2008 and has been consistently employed as a full time video game artist ever since. With his strong technical background mixed with his independent problem solving skills & passion for art and architecture, Kevin is recognized as being a strong contributor to any project.

Kevin is a proven self-starter, and is able to work unsupervised for long periods while supervising others. He utilizes his knowledge of state of the art technology, terminology, and techniques of game art development while working closely with all departments of a development team. Kevin's interdisciplinary communication skills allow him to create technically sound, high quality art and content pipelines, while maintaining a robust awareness of design and underlying programming considerations.

Kevin displays substantial general skills in Autodesk 3DS Max, Adobe Photoshop, and most other widely used professional digital content creation packages. Kevin is proficient with advanced functionality of source control, asset organization, task tracking, and bug database software. He is able to effectively communicate art issues to non-artists, provide accurate task time estimates, and can be relied upon to innovate and instruct the team regarding new processes and software as dictated by project needs.
