Justin Angsuwat's Summary

Justin Angsuwat has eight years of both consulting and in-house HR experience, leading HR strategy, planning and execution driven by data and analytics. Justin spent three of these years as the HR Chief of Staff at a Fortune 500 company during the largest corporate transformation in the world at the time.

He currently leads human capital consulting teams at PwC. Justin specializes in talent management and analytics, organization design and HR transformation, working with Fortune 100 clients and the UN. He also leads development of the US Firm’s human capital social media consulting solutions and analytics.

Justin was previously named the Australian HR Rising Star of the Year, and is currently the Chair of the SHRM Young Professionals Board.

Select experiences and strengths include:
• HR strategy, planning and execution
• Data, analytics and diagnoses
• Business case development
• Human capital effectiveness and transformation
• HR business partnering
• Talent management and organization design
• HR operations, including team leadership and P&L management
