Carlos R. Hernandez's Summary

I thoroughly enjoy being a “Social Business Educator and Strategist". I draw on my extensive selling and customer service experience and show how to talk and listen on the various social media marketing platforms.

My prime mission since the fall of 2007 has been to help people —especially individuals who feel intimidated by the emerging social media technologies. I have shown many professionals how to develop integrated and cross-promotional networks and connections via LinkedIn, Facebook, Twitter and other social sharing sites such as Via.Me, Instagram, and Pinterest.

Nordstrom provided an exciting and engaging working environment where humanizing the brand in real-time and online via Facebook, Twitter and Via.Me was my focus.

I bring talents in public speaking, problem solving, and teaching to my work as an “agent of conscious social media change.” 2009-2010 were break-through years as I conducted over (100) one-hundred speaking engagements. Highlights include being invited to present by US Congress House of Representatives' Jackie Speier, John Garamendi and Barbara Lee to hundreds of job seekers.

Another recent highlight was being asked to contribute content and to be a book's technical editor by Hollis Thomases, CEO of WebAdvantage, an internet marketing firm. The book is called "Twitter Marketing: An Hour a Day" and can be found at http://bit.ly/TMHADCarlos.

Previously, twenty-eight years were devoted to the Westinghouse Electric and Eaton Corporations in technical-solution sales, marketing, and construction project management after studying Civil Engineering at Stanford University (Class of 1979). $90M in solution based sales were achieved in my career span.

Specialties: Personal and business branding, professional career management mentoring and coaching, public speaking, solution selling, consultative selling, enterprise selling
