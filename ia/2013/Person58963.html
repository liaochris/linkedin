Jasmine Platt's Experience

Creating Miracles Facilitator

Jasmine Platt Limited

Privately Held; Myself Only; Professional Training & Coaching industry

2009 – Present (4 years)

After attending and coaching on numerous programs, and coaching clients for over 10 years, I have a very clear idea on what works (and what doesn't) in shifting human behaviour, and doing so quickly - and sustainably!

Having similar conversations with clients of very different backgrounds, experience and stages in life, I knew that something else needed to be made available, that made life, and personal development and transformation EASIER than the other programs available in the market.

One of my mottos is that 'Life doesn't have to be hard.' Creating Miracles is all about fulfilling on that.

Since starting Creating Miracles, it has grown from a course to a curriculum of both public courses, Evening seminar programs and personalised one-on-one coaching programs for people from all walks of life, with phenomenal tangible results and emotional benefits articulated by clients and participants.

For more information, see www.jasmineplatt.com - or www.jasmineplatt.com/eveningsessions
