Jocelyn Nager, Esq.'s Summary

We are the oldest commercial collection firm in New York State. Entering the 21st Century, FFGN is a certified Women Owned Business located in Times Square at the heart of New York City. Our client-base includes Fortune 500 as well as mid-size companies. FFGN promises to serve as your partner in converting your accounts receivable into accounts paid. Our experience and knowledge of varied industries enables us to employ different strategies to get our clients paid. Consultative approach and partnership with clients sets us apart from our competitors. Claims are assessed for collectability to insure clients do not throw good money after bad. When amicable efforts are unproductive we mediate, arbitrate and litigate claims through to judgment as well as foreclose on mechanics liens. Our approach depends upon the facts and financial solvency of the debtor. At FFGN, our goal is to enforce your company’s rights as creditor. Our team of diverse professionals has been getting clients paid for over 70 years, handling all phases of debt collection.

Specialties: Turnkey operation to collect debt with and without litigation. Fees are based on a flexible structure with the intention to reduce spend for outside counsel so as to maximize recovery in the shortest period of time.
