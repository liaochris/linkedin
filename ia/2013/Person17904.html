Bridget Zeuner's Summary

I began working in music in New York City as assistant talent buyer at the China Club. For the club, I wrangled talent at major record label and television network events and scouted new acts at venues like CBGB’s. After that I worked independently for Sony Music executives in marketing music projects and continue to work with those guys.

Specialties: Art Direction, Community Management, Inbound Marketing, Blog Writing & Editing, Content Strategies, Social Media Management, Blog & Web design (social Web profile pages and full Web site), Online PR & Reputation Management
