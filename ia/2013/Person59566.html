Jason Verdelli's Summary

I’m a self-proclaimed geek who will go out of my way to be sure that others succeed. I see the web as a community and vehicle with unlimited possibilities that brings this passion to life. The bottom line is that I will do whatever it takes to be the resource that my clients need to make their goals and objectives a reality.

My belief is that it takes dedication, creativity and expertise to take any endeavor to the next level and I make that my purpose. With proven experience and extensive knowledge in improving business practices with the use of internet marketing and community development my goal is to help businesses plan, implement and measure the success of their online communications.

At my core, I’m an entrepreneur. In the past 5 years I have created and developed over 30 web programs and sites including www.SocialHubSite.com, www.PlanetGRAD.com, and many others. I have seen online companies grow into 6-figure businesses. When I start a project, success is my only goal.

I used these passions to start Phase 2 Solutions, LLC—an online communications and internet marketing company that researches and implements techniques that help other businesses find success. Through this company I have been directly responsible for over 400 online marketing and social media campaigns for clients since October of 2005.

My online companies have been featured in Network Magazine, ABC-27 and the Central Pennsylvania Business Journal, among other media outlets. Basically, I have what it takes to take your company to the next level and I am willing to bend over backwards to do just that.
