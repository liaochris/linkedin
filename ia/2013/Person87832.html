Marti Reeder's Summary

I have been in & around real estate for over 30 years. I've worked for: (nka) HomeStreet Bank as a loan processor; a property management agency; pest control/inspection company and was co-owner of my husband's Remodeling Business for 15 years.

My parents owned advertising & marketing agencies since I was a child--I learned a lot about marketing from watching them & working for them as their Office Manager. Additionally, I assisted an attorney on a case that lasted 10 years.

These experiences make me uniquely qualified to be a successful real estate agent. I know about houses, loans, how to market a product and how to successfully negotiate & manage complicated transactions to closing.

Though born & raised in Seattle (I’ve lived in 7 different Puget Sound neighborhoods), I have lived South of Seattle for 16 years. Having been voted "Best of Kent Realtor" for 5 years, I know the people & places of Kent! In summary, I represent buyers & sellers in the Puget Sound Area.

I am pleased to have received John L. Scott's "President's Awards" for the past 6 years, including some very difficult years in the real estate market! Further, I am honored to have received "Seattle Magazine's" Five Star Best In Client Satisfaction Award for 2 years, and John L. Scott's prestigious "Presidents Elite" Award for 3 years.

In 2011, I completed the rigorous requirements to earn the "Certified Residential Specialist" (CRS) Designation through the National Association of Realtors, of which only a small amount of Realtors (nationally) have successfully completed (6%).

Though I am one of the top brokers in my office, you don't need to worry... With my Team of Professionals, I am never too busy for you. Let’s get together!

Specialties: Specializing in residential real estate in the Puget Sound Region, Kent and surrounding areas. Residential resale & commercial sales.
