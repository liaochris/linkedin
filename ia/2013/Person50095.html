Greg Doyle's Summary

Greg Doyle is the CFO of Leading BioSciences, Inc. a successful early-stage bio-tech R&D product development company, with rights to a revolutionary medical platform discovery called “Autodigestion”. Greg also sits on the Board of Directors and serves as the Corporate Secretary.

The company's product portfolio includes 9 different IP companies which own patents and world-wide exclusive licenses for 14 different bio-tech product lines and treatments.

The company's objective is to prepare, mature and develop various “Autodigestion” products through the clinical and regulatory path to a point where we either sell them, engage a large pharma strategic partner for that specific product, or decide to take the individual product to market ourselves.

Greg took a leave-of-absence from InLab Ventures in order to become a part of the management team of Leading BioSciences (a portfolio company of InLab Ventures).

Greg has a 20 year background as an entrepreneur and a senior business leader for both public and private companies, strategic management consulting, forming and incubating early to mid-stage companies, mergers & acquisitions, and fund management.

In addition to his professional career, Greg has been a serial entrepreneur having started and sold 4 different businesses from the ground up since 1990. Two of those companies sold for more than $25 million. Greg has successfully raised over $40 million in seed and Series A funding for VC-backed ventures and has previously raised and managing a $70 million fund.

Greg is the co-author of Venture Capital 3.0, an entirely new business model for venture capital investing. Greg also writes and publishes articles and blogs geared towards helping entrepreneurs. Some of those articles can be seen at www.venturecapital3-0.com.

Greg received his MBA from USC’s Marshall School of Business and a BS in Finance from Brigham Young University.
