Jackson Carson's Summary

UX/UI designer by day.
Photographer by evening.
Musician by night.

I have 20 years of professional interaction design and creative leadership experience with a passion for clean design and usability. I’m a social media savvy UX/UI and interaction designer who combines striking design with usability. I’ve got deep experience in interaction design for web sites and applications and pride myself in designing functional and beautiful environments - with the user in mind at all times. I enjoy large and complex creative challenges.

Additionally, I'm a Certified Scrum Product Owner with Scrum Master experience. I work with product management and web and mobile application developers to scope, design, prototype and test interface mockups, interaction flows, information architecture, and visual designs for new features.

Currently, I am Principal Interaction/UX Designer at MobileDay in Boulder, Colorado.

Prior to MobileDay, I was Sr. Usability Engineer at Oracle (formerly Sr. User Experience Designer at RightNow Technologies).

Prior to Oracle/RightNow, I was Sr. Web Art Director at Gaiam where I designed user-centered web application UIs, web sites, Flash apps, and much more.

Prior to Gaiam, I ran my own successful new media and traditional creative business (Wavelength Media, Inc.) for fifteen years.

Prior to Wavelength Media, I taught graduate level 3D and 2D animation at the University of Massachusetts and worked at Specular International as a Product Manager and UI designer. I designed the revolutionary animation interface for the award-winning 3D application, Infini-D.

My breakthrough "timeline" interface spawned a usability revolution in time-based animation. My interface was the first time-based (and event-based) animation interface and has become what Adobe now calls an "industry standard". First adapted to Adobe After Effects, my interface can now be found in virtually every popular animation, 3D, and effects application. It's even in Photoshop.
