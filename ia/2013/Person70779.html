Julie Metzger's Summary

Julie Metzger is currently seeking opportunities to encourage and inspire others by utilizing her past Information Technology and Leadership experience. She would also love opportunities to share her story about healing & forgiveness, showing up & being present, and moving past the pain.

She is also the Owner/Founder of Returning the Gift. A recent graduate from the College of Mount St. Joseph with a Bachelorette in Religious Studies and a Minor in Information Technology.
************************************************************************
Julie founded Returning the Gift, LLC in 2009 out of her love of helping others. She finds there is real value in the information, ideas and experiences we have at church, work and life. Julie has over 17 + years in the Information Technology field and 10+ years of church and community involvement. With this professional experience she is working on her mission to share Faith, Hope, Courage, Strength, Inspiration and most of all LOVE! She has an amazing past and uses her experience to bring people together in a moment of change.
************************************************************************
Aspiring Author, Articles, Ezine Articles & Publish in the Fall 2010 addition of Lions on Line at the College of Mount St Joseph. Currently Julie is writing her personal story of healing and forgiveness. To see a sneak peak, check out the Returning the Gift website @ www.returningthegift.org
************************************************************************
Julie likes to visit local area churches and volunteer in unexpected places. She believes in "Showing up and being Present". She wants to help others find a place to grow their faith as "Our faith must be our own". Julie loves to share stories of how Social Networking, Showing up & Being Present, Forgiving, Faith & Giving has changed her life.
