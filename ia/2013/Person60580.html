Jeanna Gabellini's Summary

Wouldn’t it solve a whole bunch of your issues with money, success and life balance if someone would just help you figure out what you can’t see that you’re currently doing to sabotage your efforts and give you the roadmap to blow past every benchmark you set for yourself?

It’d be pretty sweet if you were experiencing…

o Extreme focus through out your day while creating more space and balance

o An income that is consistently in the six and seven figure range

o Ditch your less than ideal corporate job to create your own business and turn a consistent profit in the first three months

o Turn your work team into a cohesive kick-butt powerhouse in six months

o A feeling of always being in control of your life without having to control things and people around you

The extraordinary results above continuously happen to ordinary people who work with Jeanna Gabellini, who is a master business coach, author and speaker that uses result driven strategies while leveraging intention.

It takes more than being positive and putting out a lot of effort to live every desire you’ve written down on your wish list. Jeanna knows that her clients’ results happen when she assists them in taking away their self- imposed deadlines, rules and have-to’s in life and begin to take charge. She teaches people to come back to the extremely wealthy, resourceful, healthy and grounded people they’ve always had access to being.

The process of turning around your finances, team, relationships, health and well being does not have to take years, pain or massive amounts of information overload. The process is actually simple when you stop beating your head against the wall and tap into that peak potential part of yourself.

Jeanna is regularly featured on radio and in the media as recognized authority on using Attraction Principles in business. She co-authored, "Life Lessons for Mastering the Law of Attraction" with Jack Canfield and Mark Victor Hansen.

Specialties

We provide high-octane one-on-one coaching, team coaching, teleclasses and learning tools for massive results. Fun is injected to everything we do...if it's not fun we won't do it! =-)
