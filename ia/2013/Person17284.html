Brian Dainis' Summary

I am a professional web developer and software developer as well as the owner of Curotec. I run a team of technology consultants, programmers, graphic designers and content writers that make up Curotec. We serve small, medium and large sized companies for their web development and software application related needs. This includes web design and development, mobile site and mobile app development, hosting and security.

I am very interested with web start ups. I have worked with many business owners that are pursuing a web start up and I have also been involved with a few of my own web start ups including one that I am very active with currently that tackles the creation and distribution of open source music.

I am constantly travelling and meeting new people. One of my favorite things to do when I go to a new area is attend meetups and have 1 to 1 meetings with locals that are in my industry or a similar industry. Some of my greatest friends and professional partners are people I have seemingly bumped into in my travels.

Lastly, some of my hobbies include programming, playing music, mountain biking, exploring the world, ice hockey and surfing the web.
