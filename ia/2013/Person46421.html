R. Fureigh's Summary

Online strategist and Drupal and CiviCRM developer who especially enjoys usability and helping nonprofits and political campaigns increase the effectiveness of their outreach. I also organize Drupal Ladder meetups in New York City to encourage Drupal developers to take the plunge and contribute to core.

Over the past 14 years I’ve worked with organizations with a wide range of missions, constituencies, structures and budgets. I often collaborate with other web developers and graphic designers.

Specialties: Drupal, CiviCRM, HTML (yes, including HTML5), CSS (yes, including CSS3), PHP, MySQL, open source, email optimization, analytics, usability, nonprofits and political campaigns. And translating geekspeak into friendly English.
