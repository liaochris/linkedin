Coen Smit's Summary

Coen Smit (Haarlem 1982) studied interior Architecture at het Hogeschool voor de Kunsten in Utrecht and Architecture at the Academy of Architecture in Amsterdam. His work experience as a draftsman, projectleader and architect at several national and internationally known offices make him an allrounder that can work in every phase of a project.

Coen also works on project base as an guest teacher at several spatial schools.

After working for Dam en Parners Architecten, Wingender Hovenier Architecten ZECC Architecten, Drost+van Veen Architecten and Marcel Lok_Architect, Coen works now with pleasure at Studioninedots in Amsterdam.

aTasteofReality was founded in 2013 and is a close collaboration between two architects. aTasteofReality creates 'a taste of unbuilt architecture' through high-end 3D computer visualizations such as interior stills, exterior stills and look-a-rounds. The extensive experience gained over the last decade in the architecture and visualization field results in delivering the best quality, with a focus on the architectural spatiality of every project.
