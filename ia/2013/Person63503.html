Jacek Gwizdka's Summary

User-experience researcher with very solid foundations in both quantitative and qualitative methods. Over 15 years of experience in human-computer interaction. Experienced in all phases of usability testing and analysis. Adept in identifying and incorporating user needs into user-centered designs.

In academic work specializes in cognitive psychology of human information interaction, cognitive load; one of the first researchers working in neuro-information science.

Specialties: UX research, usability analysis, quantitative data analysis, interaction design, interfaces for search and navigation, cognitive psychology of human information interaction, cognitive load, neuro-information science
