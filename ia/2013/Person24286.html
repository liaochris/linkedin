Christopher Grant's Summary

Christopher Grant, founded CG Real Estate in January of 2009. CG Real Estate is a company created to help community members buy or sell real estate utilizing a partnership concept. Grant prides himself in helping people realize their real estate goals through the use of online technology, market understanding, and his overall passion for real estate. Through websites such as www.Realtor.com, www.CtReal.com, www.WindhamChamber.com, and www.Facebook.com, CG Real estate can maintain low overhead, which in turn passes on the savings to the consumer. Specializing in customizing listings to the suit home sellers’ needs using the latest tools available has proven to save money for the seller and with incentives such as low interest rates and high market inventory for buyers, there has never been a better time to buy or sell. Affiliated with the National Association of Realtors, Connecticut Association of Realtors, Eastern Connecticut Association of Realtors, and the Better Business Bureau, CG Real Estate has experience in Real Estate sales, Management, Investment, Development and Home Flipping. We are convienently located at 361 Boston Post Rd., North Windham, CT in the North Windham Plaza on RT 6 across from Wal-Mart, Christopher Grant, however, stands firms that “Your home or your home to be, is his REAL office, where it should be.” Contact Christopher Grant at CG Real Estate at 860-933-3565 or visit www.CGREALESTATE.net for a free consultation.
