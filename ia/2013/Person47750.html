Geoff Hansen's Summary

Geoff Hansen is a central Vermont documentary photographer whose work has appeared in a dozen books, including including My Life as a Dog, the best-selling Living with Chickens, Harvest: A Year in the Life of an Organic Farm, Barbie Unbound, and most recently, The Joy of Keeping a Root Cellar.

Geoff has had the privilege of photographing more than 200 weddings throughout New England since 1994. He has been photo editor and page designer at the Valley News in Lebanon, N.H., since 1998, where he began as staff photographer in 1989.
