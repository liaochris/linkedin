April R. Silver's Summary

* Social entrepreneur whose professional experiences center around the broad world of communications: public and media relations; online and traditional marketing; event management, and artist development, since 1993.

* Writer; Editor of the critically acclaimed anthology of "Be a Father to Your Child: Real Talk from Black Men on Family, Love, and Fatherhood." (2008, Soft Skull/Counterpoint Press)
