Kay Wilson's Summary

Affordable short term fine art leasing and sales

The Gallery Exchange offers the perfect solution for both homeowners and businesses looking for a cost effective way to decorate with fine art. Browse hundreds of works of art from the convenience of your computer without having to visit a bunch of galleries or deal with high pressure sales.

We are all about giving you the ultimate exprience in art service, from an online gallery that offers you all the tools to serve and manager your own art aquisitions to the customer service we provide to those wanting a more personal touch. We are here to listen and ask the right questions so that your concerns for quality, time and budget are met.
