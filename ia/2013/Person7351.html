Andy Bergin's Summary

A world class communication coach who has helped 3000+ people around the world. My clients include CEOs and other C-level business leaders, hedge fund founders, senior economists, private equity executives, salespeople, risk officers, traders, wealth advisors, investment bankers, lawyers, quants, strats, technologists, research analysts, engineers and business executives. I help them communicate more effectively with their clients, colleagues, employees and the press. Coaching takes place 'elbow to elbow' in client offices in New York City, Westchester and Connecticut and virtually via videoconference around the U.S. and the world. 2013 is our 5th year of growth and success. To date, 65 client companies have engaged me to help key senior people succeed as communicators. Clients include many leading global companies in their fields around the world.
