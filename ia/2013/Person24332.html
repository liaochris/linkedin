Chris Boylan's Summary

Working in the software industry as director of Internet marketing for a $300M company, I and my team spend our days growing our business by generating sales leads and overall awareness of our B2B software solutions. We also oversee the content on the company's externally facing Web sites which currently generate well over two and a half million visits a year. I occasionally present best practice sessions at direct marketing events and conferences and relish the opportunity to share what I've learned and to interact with the audience.

In 2005, I launched Big Picture Big Sound - a home theater and movie review web site - with business partner Joe Lozito. This project leveraged our passion for movies, music and home theater equipment and our experience in Internet marketing. The site has now assumed a life of its own, attracting a variety of seasoned writers as well as new talent and becoming a reference site for film and home theater. In this capacity, as the publication's Editor in Chief, I have moderated the CES panel on HDTV technology and currently serve on the CEA session advisory panel.

Big Picture Big Sound's movie and Blu-ray reviews, editorials, news stories and equipment reviews are syndicated in Google News, high definition database (hddb), eCoustics, Internet Movie Database (imdb), Movie Review Query Engine and Rotten Tomatoes.

Specialties

SEO, internet marketing, writing, ISF certified, public speaking.
