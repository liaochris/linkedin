Justin Singer's Summary

I do a lot of things.

I've worked as a venture investor, a startup product manager, a management consultant, an in-house attorney, and a construction gofer, among other things. While that may seem like a random walk through a variety of roles and industries, it's left me with an unusual understanding of the multitude of frameworks, incentives, processes, and technologies that influence business outcomes. It's also helped me to develop a healthy respect for what I don't -- or can't -- know.

As an investor at IA Ventures, I worked in every role imaginable. On the investment side, I met with countless entrepreneurs, evaluated hundreds of investment opportunities, and ultimately helped shepherd ~15 deals through the investment lifecycle. On the operations side, I built and managed IA's capital allocation and portfolio risk models, as well as the firm's deal flow management system. I was also the primary business operations resource for two portfolio companies incubated by the fund.

Outside of regular work, I help Jerry Neumann teach a class on entrepreneurship at Columbia, which has been a ton of fun. I'm a fan of road trips, crosswords, and dead-tree books. I'm endlessly fascinated by how people make decisions, especially in the context of investing or purchasing.

Additional skills: advanced Excel, MySQL, VBA, Javascript, HTML/CSS, Wireframing (Moqups, Keynote, Balsamiq), Photoshop
