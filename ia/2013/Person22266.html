Charlie Foxworth SIOR CCIM's Summary

A business administration graduate of the University of Texas at Austin, I purchased RE/MAX Beaumont in 2010 and RE/MAX First in January 2012. I am responsible for customer service in these firms. My mission is to inspire agents and help create opportunities to help them realize their dreams. Our company vision is to become the home for ethical, high-minded, learning-based professionals committed to achieving their dreams.

I am a native of Beaumont and an active participant in local and civic affairs.

I am married to Blair and have two children Jay and Sarah. I coach little league and as of this writing have run 8 marathons.

Specialties: Tenant/Buyer Representation, Landlord/Seller Representation, consulting, property management, adding value to your real estate portfolio, and finding real estate to maximize your business objectives.
