Kevin Tuhumury's Summary

As a Software Engineer I focus mainly on Custom Application Development with Ruby (on Rails), Frontend Development (HTML5, CSS3, CoffeeScript), Agile methodologies (Scrum), Behaviour Driven Development (BDD) and Test Driven Development (TDD) using among other things Cucumber and RSpec.
