John Peters' Projects

  • USA TODAY The Point

A first-of-its kind, fully integrated, multi-platform travel media experience for the hospitality industry, The Point is now fully deployed in more than 3,000 hotels and 450,000 guestrooms in the U.S. Specifically, The Point sits on top of a hotels wifi network and provides a unique blend of world, national and local news; local guides and tools, including top picks from local insiders, and premium digital entertainment content.including books, magazines and more.

  • Experience Las Vegas

    • April 2013 to Present
    Team Members: John Peters, Krista Nordlund (Johnson), Victoria Borton

The site features "best of" recommendations based on traveler type such as Partier, Food Lover, Vegas Newbie and others, while covering attractions, hotels, casinos, restaurants, shopping, shows, celebrity comings and goings, deals and expert tips. USA TODAY has sourced the foremost experts in Las Vegas to share their insider knowledge and exclusive access. In USA TODAY trademark fashion, content is smart, user-friendly and accessible. Experience Las Vegas features trending topics and buzz, deals and videos that share insiders' views of all things Vegas, from where to stay, what to see and what to do..
