Jeff Cowan's Summary

Jeff Cowan’s experience comes from being in the trenches. In his 27 years of doing business, Jeff has spent most of that time on service drives.

He is the nation’s authority when it comes to training Service Advisors. Jeff has successfully trained them in the art of selling, communication skills, customer service and retention, and achieving their fixed operations goals.

Jeff’s training is the most practical, realistic, and easy to understand in the marketplace. In his over thirty-seven years of sales experience, he has earned numerous sales awards from prestigious retail companies. Throughout North America, Jeff is one of the most sought after and highly regarded speakers in the industry. He is a regular speaker at the annual NADA Convention.

Specialties: Service Advisor Training, Customer Service, Customer Retention, Sales
