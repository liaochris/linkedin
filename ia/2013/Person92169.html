Michael Caruso's Summary

Michael Caruso is an Accomplished Real Estate Broker with a legacy spanning decades. After starting his career at Century 21 and Remax where he was ranked in the Top 100 Agents worldwide, Michael opened his own independent real estate firm in 1994 and ran it successfully through 2012. A new career chapter for 2013 has Michael proudly associated with Surterre Properties in their Monarch Beach office as a broker associate.

Michael served as 2007 President for The Orange County Association of Realtors (13th largest Realtor Association in the USA), served on numerous state legislative and professional standards committees as well as National Association of Realtors committees. He provides personalized full service Real Estate Brokerage for the discerning customer/client. Nearly 30 years of Real Estate Sales and Real Estate development/management experience has shaped Michael's expertise. Specializing in Residential Sales, Buyer Representation, Investment and Relocation Services. Special skills include listening and responding to clients so as to provide streamline communication. Michael has a passion to serve the community and overall area. I-pad or Lap-top computer is always with him for info on the go.
