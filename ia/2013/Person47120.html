Gary Heffner's Summary

I have owned Sarasota Messenger Imprinted Sportswear, Inc. since 1986. Our website TshirtsOnYou.com came later in 1999. Over the years we have been involved in larger projects for Royalty/Licensed goods for the NFL as well as the Collegiate arena. Our niche is supplying designs to the Florida Fishing Tournaments & Marina's along the Gulf Coast.

With the recent down turn in the economy our design capabilities give our customers the assurance they will get quality plus their dollars worth of product.

Specialties

Using my BA degree in fine arts and my wife's degree in Commercial Graphic Arts has been a real bonus for customers wanting more than clipart on a give away T-shirt.
We've reproduced art by nationally acclaimed artists for Wine Festivals to our local Joe the Plumber. Each project is unique and we give each one our full attention.
