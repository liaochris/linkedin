Frederick Weiss' Summary

I am a Senior Front-End Developer, Ul, and UX Designer with over 16 years of experience defining, designing, developing, and deploying websites. My work is cross-browser compatible, standards compliant, and responsive for any device. I write hand-coded, accessible, and valid semantic markup as well as clearly ordered and documented CSS for desktop and mobile websites.

One of my favorite segments of the journey is UX development and the strategy of influencing the human psyche: the mapping of who, what, when and where in the user experience travels. I am passionate about UX design, user engagement, and interaction patterns for data driven websites and mobile applications.
