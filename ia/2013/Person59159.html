Jason E. Barkeloo's Summary

"It is a cursed evil to any man to become as absorbed in any subject as I am in mine" (Charles Darwin).

I focus in four areas...........Team..........Product.........Market.........ROI.

Founding and operating technology businesses is a capstone to my academic preparations, research, followership, leadership, and investing experiences.

My current effort is developing synthetic biology BactoBots™ via Bacterial Robotics. With a passionate, no excuses, exceptionally competent team, we are positioning the first biotechnology BactoBot application as a wastewater-to-value solution via the licensed subsidiary, Pilus Energy. The second BactoBot subsidiary in development focuses on eradicating a skull-based tumor as a surgical alternative.

Prior to launching Bacterial Robotics and Pilus Energy, I developed Somatic Digital and TouchSmart Publishing. They failed to gain maket traction. But I came away wiser and committed to build upon the successes we experienced.

My early market research skills found an outlet with DiscoverTek. This competitive intelligence consulting practice reviewed and invested in Internet, e-commerce security, privacy, and digital rights management (DRM) solutions.

My experiences include product and intellectual property development, fund-raising, P&L, rapid prototyping, manufacturing, and sales & marketing. I have two awarded USA patents and a number of international filings pending, including China, the European Union, India, and Hong Kong.

My military career included executing assignments in the operations, intelligence, and medical fields. I finished my career and served five years as a Troops-to-Teachers inner-city high school science teacher.

As a result of my preparations and experiences, my leadership philosophy is simply stated as "tell me how we are going to do it, not why we can't do it."

Specialties: Entrepreneur, capital, prototype and product development, business development, revenue generation, and negotiations.
