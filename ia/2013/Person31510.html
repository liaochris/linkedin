David Biernbaum's Summary

Consumer Goods & Retail Professional since 1977

David Biernbaum provides Across-the-Board Consulting, Brand and Business Development, National Sales & Marketing Expertise, Equity Development, in-depth CPG marketing, and thousands of industry connections, all at a smart outsourced cost. David also provides National Sales and Broker Management that fits the client’s needs and gets results.

David Biernbaum specializes with brands delivering innovation, product differentiation, and retail productivity. David Biernbaum and his teams have brought to market and/or impacted over 200 highly productive successful consumer goods in the marketplace; including brands such as Aquafresh, Oral-B, Clear Eyes, Zooth, Selsun Blue, Germ-X, Mineral Ice, Fresh & Go, Prilosec, Gillette Sensor, LiceGuard Robi-Comb, Sonicare, and more recently SmartMouth, Australian Dream Arthritis Cream, ZarBee’s Cough Syrups, TriCalm, TheraBreath, OraBrush, Milkscreen, Softcup, Burt’s Bees, Xlear, and more.

David Biernbaum’s professional background includes executive, management, or consulting roles at GSK, Abbott Labs, Gillette, Vi-Jon Labs, Zooth Inc, P&G, and many others, ranging from innovative start-up’s to industry giants. David provides expertise and leadership at ECRM, NACDS, and GMDC and has strong ties with virtually every major chain drug, mass merchandiser, supermarket, and wholesaler in North America.

David Biernbaum founded the Consumer Goods & Retail Professionals social media groups on LinkedIn and Facebook, now with more than 40,000 members.

David Biernbaum has a history of building efficient approaches that constantly result in rapid development in retail sales with major growth and equity. David has written hundreds of columns for industry trade publications and often quoted on CNBC and other media. David is a "BrainTrust" panel member at Retailwire.com., the very popular blog read every day by retail buyers and executives.
