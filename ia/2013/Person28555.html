Cynthia La China's Summary

I'm a creative consultant/ designer by day, Latin dance teacher/ performer by evening. Since 2000, I've been hooked on Salsa dancing, teaching, performing and choreographing dance routines as alternative outlets for creative thinking and artistic expressions.

Almost 15 years of dancing and teaching experience has gained a dedicated following of students from K-12, college students, adults and active seniors who shared my passion for Latin dance & health fitness. As dance director/ event organizer, numerous performing & teaching gigs has earned me extensive travel opportunities to dance festivals, salsa congresses, cultural institutes, universities, corporate events and at private parties for up close and personal dance entertainment.

20+ years of art direction & design experience has earned an extensive knowledge in advertising & marketing career. My journey from Design Assistant to Creative Director has gained a wide range of projects (TV, print, direct marketing, web, collateral, promotional), Fortune 500 clients and prestigious awards in the field of Direct Marketing & Advertising.

Extremely driven, self-motivated and organized in all aspects of work. Offering strong solutions and generate successful results as team player, and as team leader delegating projects among peers and creative talents.

Highly proficient in customer relations and client management, both in verbal and written communications. Offer diverse concepts and solutions with highly creative executions and strong presentations under short deadlines and tight budgets.

Specialties: Design, Art Direction, Concept, Branding, Direct Marketing, Promotion, Events, Promotion, Dance Instructions, Teaching, Dance Entertainment, Performing Arts, Choreography, Latin Youth Salsa Kids Dance Specialist, Senior Dance Fitness Coach, Performance, Wedding Entertainment.

September 2008 – Present (5 years 4 months) New York, New York

LSD Projects & Latin Soul Dance Entertainment is an elite group of professional performers and seasoned dancers who entertains, teaches and performs for corporate, education, charity, cultural, media, social & private events. Our colorful choreography are sizzling fusions of Latin dances in dynamic & stage-ready productions in Salsa, Mambo, Cha Cha, Boogaloo, Latin Jazz, Afro Cuban, Rumba, Bachata, Hustle, Argentine Tango and more.

A sought-after dance entertainment company in NY/NJ/CT/PA area, our services are highly professional with very competitive rates. We also have multiple couples & group dance showcases - explosive, exciting and exhilarating - which are corporate & cultural clients' favorite! We also offer motivation guest dance services, group workshops, choreography, cultural showcases, dancing with live band, DJ music services for all occasions.

No matter what the events are, we have the perfect show & entertainment for every client, and we customize our shows and costumes to fit all ages and locations. We are always creating new routines and looking for new ideas to entertain and to make each event a huge success!
