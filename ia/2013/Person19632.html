  • Small Business Rocket Fuel: Launch

    • March 20, 2012
    Authors: Candice Hughes, PhD, MBA

    Have you ever thought about starting your own small business but just didn’t know where to begin?
    In Small Business Rocket Fuel: Launch, Candice M. Hughes, Ph.D., shows you how to start up a small business step-by-step. Starting with a solid business plan, Dr. Hughes describes what needs to be done for each step based on her own experience starting up and running a successful consulting firm that allowed her to increase her income 300% within 2 years. In addition to her own firm, Dr. Hughes launched a department for a venture-backed start-up that went from zero to $2.5 million in revenue within 2 years. This practical, in-the-trenches experience is critical to taking the right actions and avoiding the wrong ones. Combining experience with MBA training, Dr. Hughes demonstrates what is realistic and necessary in a business plan and how to achieve it on a tight budget with limited staff and resources. Topics include financing, legal forms of incorporation, assessing the competition, insurance, assessing risk and more. This book is especially applicable to consultants, writers, medical writers, solo entrepreneurs, virtual companies, and small business owners.
