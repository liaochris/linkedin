Julie Wilson's Summary

Julie Wilson is the founder and executive director of the Institute for the Future of Learning, a non-proﬁt organization dedicated to transforming the factory model of education. A graduate of Harvard's Technology, Innovation and Education Masters program, Julie has more than 15 years' experience as a consultant, trainer and coach, and blogs regularly on the future of learning at www.the-iﬂ.org. Her articles have been published on the EdWeek website and the "Futures of School" Reform blog.

Julie Wilson's Experience

Founder

Institute for the Future of Learning

January 2011 – Present (2 years 10 months) Greater Boston Area

The Institute for the Future of Learning is a non-profit dedicated to the transformation of our education system from the current obsolete, factory based model. We are dedicated to helping young adults graduate with the skills, knowledge and habits of mind which will enable them to be successful in the 21st century.

Head of Coaching

Predictable Success

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Management Consulting industry

With over fifteen years experience as a consultant trainer and leadership coach, Julie has worked with a wide range of for profit and not-for-profit clients across the US and Europe. Clients include Harvard University, Pella Corporation, the Lighthouse Academies Charter Network and UC Berkeley.

As Head of Coaching and senior consultant with Predictable Success, Julie helps leaders and teams clarify their personal and/or organizational strategies, and helps facilitates the execution of those strategies.
