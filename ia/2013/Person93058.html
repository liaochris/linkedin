Michael Trudell's Summary

Michael Trudell is highly proficient giving depositions, providing testimony at trial and assisting legal counsel regarding the review of each client’s case.

Trudell and Associates Ltd., LLC (TAL) has been engaged by attorneys in the State of Nevada for the purpose of providing services which include assessing, evaluating, compiling, or analyzing issues, and preparing opinions, testimony or reports. Specifically, reviewing the actions of boards of directors, managers, committees of the association and vendors of the association for compliance with the standard of care for common-interest communities in the State of Nevada, as codified in Chapter 116 of the Nevada Revised Statutes (NRS 116) and Chapter 116 of the Nevada Administrative Code (NAC 116), and the Association’s governing documents. Typically, the work includes statements of opinions regarding the Declaration of Covenants, Conditions and Restrictions (CC&Rs), Plats, Bylaws, Public Offering Statement, the reserve study , actions of the Architectural Review Committee and documentation discovered during the research of the common-interest community, and work product performed by other experts.

As a development consultant, Michael Trudell has been contracted by the developers of Caughlin Ranch, Double Diamond and Somersett for the purposes of the review of the necessary elements for the successful implementation of the community owners' association in the development process.

Specialties

30 years of business experience, including, experience in personnel, contract and program management; and, 20 years of experience preparing and implementing budgets, reserve studies and strategic plans for a large common-interest community in Northern Nevada. Experience with providing expert witness testimony and providing assistance to legal counsel in common-interest community cases. Four years of experience in urban planning. Active as a Lobbyist at the Nevada Legislature from 1995 to 2011.
