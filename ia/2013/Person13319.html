  • KPI Creation

    • December 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Ben Teague, Amy M. Sandlin

    We asked to create and write out our sales teams KPIs, rather then have them dictated to us. Once presented to the Founder, Director and Sales Manager of HotSchedules, we learned that our KPI recommendation was picked up for the sales organization as part of the 2013 measurement benchmarking.

    VISION
    Grow relationships, business; Drive revenue; Outperform the competition

    QUOTA TRACKING = 50%
    Broken into 3 benchmarks.
    Quota = 100% attainment
    Presidents Club = 115% attainment
    Platinum = 130% attainment

    PRODUCT PARTICIPATION = 25%
    This KPI measures and ranks our products based on revenue performance so not only do we focus, but also drive company initiatives. Variations on this sales metric can show top performing products, top performing sales reps, or the bottom 10% of sales.
    Calculation: Rank top 3/10/25 performers by sales revenue

    SALES by CONTACT METHOD = 25%
    This KPI monitors which contact methods are generating sales, such as email, phone, social media or in-person contacts.
    Important Knowledge:
    - Contact method that is most effective at generating sales (rep/position dependent)
    - Understand revenue generated for each method
    - Don’t solely count actions as results
    Calculation: Sales per contact method / Total revenue
