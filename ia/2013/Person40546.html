Liz Lamar's Summary

If you’re looking for an enthusiast; someone unconventional
with bright ideas, a love of creative work and, more
importantly, an understanding of the sweat that goes into it,
you may just have found your next research/strategy guru.

There is something about planning that gets inside me.
Excites me and fulﬁlls me. Through it, I want to inspire and be
inspired. My passion for consumer research emanates from
my innate curiosity to relate to people and their everyday
lives. I’m eager to learn what it is people want, what they like
and why they like it. I am fascinated by the idea that we can
inﬂuence a targeted demographic, make an emotional
connection and ultimately change behavior.

When knee-deep in research, I feel as though I can be
myself-- I can think freely and creatively; where with every
consumer contact, I can help build a brand.
