Michael Given's Summary

Land and Waterscape designer with experience in residential and community development design and installation. Specializing in pool, fountain & water feature design and construction in high end residential projects as well as community development projects. Interested in continuing to elevate the standards of Land & Waterscape design and construction.

Certified designer/builder by the Society of Watershape Designers.

Specialties

Comprehensive design of outdoor living environments utilizing water. Including water features, swimming pools, fountains, outdoor kitchens, fire elements, shade structures, bath houses, patios, walls, landscape, landscape lighting, drainage, and irrigation.

Michael Given's Experience

Founder and President

Michael Given Environments, LLC

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Construction industry

July 2010 – Present (3 years 4 months)

Michael Given Environments, LLC offers comprehensive outdoor living design, construction, and consultation services including land and waterscapes for homes and developments.

Our focus is on creating the best possible solution for your entire outdoor environment. We'll work with you to get the design just right, take care of the entire construction process, and service your environment once installed. We take the worries out of Outdoor Living.

Our services include: Concrete Pools & Spas, Fountains, Water Features, Landscape, Patios (stone & pavers), Arbors & Shade Structures, Outdoor Kitchens, Fire Elements (fireplaces, fire pits, fire pots, etc.), Landscape Lighting (low voltage), Irrigation Audits & additions, Routine service on products we install (pool, etc.) and Design Consultation.
