Public Company; 11-50 employees; Construction industry

1978 – Present (35 years)

Jay Cipriani is President of Cipriani Builders, Inc. Jay got into the remodeling business largely because of his father who was also a contractor.

Jay has completed more than 3,000 remodeling projects in the South Jersey area. Jay has been a speaker at numerous civic and professional organizations and lectures on the Do's and Don'ts of building and remodeling. He has been featured on home improvement shows such as the Discovery Channel's "Gimme Shelter" program. Jay has also written articles for the monthly trade magazine "Builder/Architect" magazine.
