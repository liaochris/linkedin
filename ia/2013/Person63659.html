Jill Morrison's Summary

Independent graphic and web design contractor providing creative services mainly for small businesses, artists and nonprofits; ever striving for top quality design solutions that are both functional and beautiful, regardless of the client's budget. Formal training primarily in print; projects range from promotion and marketing materials, collateral, product packaging, identity systems to poster design. Ambition to keep up with trends that are turning more towards the web has led to increasing creative and technical savvy in designing and programming thoughtful websites. Work on short term, special projects and, with equal enthusiasm, on long term relationships with ongoing clients wherever my talents can be utilized and appreciated.

Specialties

print collateral, business cards, packaging, promotion and marketing materials
