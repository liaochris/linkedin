Our move management, project management, sales / sales general management, consultant and industry experience span over 30 years. We have helped moved, and managed moves, for some of the largest and well known company's in the New York Area. They read like "Who's who in America" We have helped all size companies large or small.

When a company contacts TEAM they are bringing the most trusted and experience personal to their project. Our goal is to avoid,reduce or eliminate problems in a relocation. We offer premove meetings, relocation surveys, cost estimates and moving advice at "NO COST" to prospective clients. We show our clients how too budget, plan, schedule and implement a successful move without the costly attachments. The key to a SUCCESSFUL move is not the amount of money spent, the highest or lowest estimate; but the amount of time planning and knowing what to avoid, reduce, or eliminate. Finding costly errors or problems before they happen, pointing out these areas and knowing there impact is our job. These errors or problems may result in additional cost ,budget overruns,damage to equipment, floors or walls. Poor performance, personal liability and loss of business and revenue could also be a result. Choosing the correct moving company and management team is the answer. Every company large or small deserves unparalleled affordable, efficient, safe, dependable services. This is what we specialize in, this is Team Relocation Managements Business. We may or may-not be able to help you, but if you have concerns or unanswered questions; it might be the right time for us to meet.
