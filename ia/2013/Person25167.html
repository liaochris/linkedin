John Gouthro's Summary

Since 1984, C.J. Plumbing & Heating, Inc. has served the South Shore region of Massachusetts. Our family owned and operated business specializes in plumbing, heating, and gas fitting in residential and commercial buildings in communities across the South Shore, including Brockton, the Bridgewaters, Marshfield, Scituate, and everywhere in between.

From routine repairs to extensive bathroom and kitchen remodels, we do it all. No job is too big or too small.

We are dedicated to quality workmanship and customer satisfaction.

We offer free estimates. To schedule an appointment, please call us at:

East Bridgewater area: 508-378-1271

Bridgewater area: 508-697-0034
Marshfield & Scituate area: 781-834-4664

Specialties

I am the founder, President and Master Plumber at C.J. Plumbing & Heating, Inc. We are proudly a family owned and operated business that began out of a small house in Brockton almost 30 years ago.

Today, along with my son Joseph, we are the South Shore's expert plumbing and heating contractor. We service all towns on the South Shore, including Brockton, the Bridgewaters, Marshfield, Scituate, and everywhere in between.

I am licensed by the Commonwealth of Massachusetts, and my business is fully insured.
