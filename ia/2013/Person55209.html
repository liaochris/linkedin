  • Indian Food Tour in Delhi

    • October 2011 to Present
    Team Members: India Food Tour

    FoodTourinDelhi.com A tour of authentic Indian street food in Delhi

    We offer personalized street food + sight seeing tours for small groups ranging from 1 to 8. Duration of an average tour is 3-4 hours and cost per person starts from Rs. 3600. ( approx US $ 56 *). In this amount, we will pick up from a convenient location, preferably central Delhi, guide you around the places agreed beforehand, pay for your food + non-alcoholic drinks and drop you back at a pre-agreed place. We’ll guide you about the dish, the way to eat it and even it’s history if you’d like. For example, did you know that butter chicken was invented in a restaurant called Moti Mahal located in Daryaganj, central Delhi ? The tour will include not just the food attractions, but also some great landmarks.

    Although we prefer that you customize it to your own preferences, we offer two different kinds of tours :

    First starts at 10 in morning and will go on till lunch.

    Second one starts late afternoon and continues till dinner.

    The tour locations are set by different areas of Delhi. Our most popular street food tours by order are in Old Delhi, South Delhi, Central, East, West and North. Depending upon which places you want to see and food you want to enjoy, we can arrange food tour of one or more than one area.

    There is also an extra option, which involves you deciding the time, places and what to do.

    The eating part will include sampling different varieties of street food from various places in the interinery. So we’ll advise you to have a little of everything and enjoy the variety. Apart from the usual and must see, you’ll also see some under-rated and less explored places of Delhi which very few people know about. These places are not listed on most tourist guides. A lot of very good photo-opportunities and bragging rights are on line.

    Mail: info@FoodTourinDelhi.com

    Kerala Food Tour

    The breathtaking scenary of Kerala will leave you spellbound as will the food of Kerala. Being in Southern India gives you vast choice as far as street food is given. You can have the Idli, dosa, vellayappam, uppuma, puttu and appams with the choice of your filling and with coconut chutney and sambhar. Another must visit on the Kerala Tour circuit is the Mithai Street situated in Kozhikode where you can taste the choicest Jalebis, Halwas and other milk sweets.

    Sadya. Food tour in kerala Another thing you must try out whilst in Kerala is the madhura kallu. Madhura Kallu is a sweet toddy and forms a good apertif if you plan to have the baked fresh water fish like Karimeen fry and the awesome Kerala pickles. The main food cuisines available in Kerala are Malabar Chicken Biryani, Thalassery Biryani, Mutton Paya Masala. You can enjoy the dishes with the world famous Malabar Paratha and Aviyal.
    All this and more as you would like it. You just have to inform us your tastes and budget and we will plan the perfect Kerala trip for you.
