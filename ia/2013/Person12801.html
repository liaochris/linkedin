Ben Chase's Summary

In a world full of smartphones, hi-def television, and an endless onslaught of information, I find my peace in the outdoors where true adventure is waiting for those willing to seek it out.

I'm constantly looking for "beautiful isolation", whether its alone in the wilderness in Montana, hiking onto the frozen Great Slave Lake in 40 below, or backpacking with a friend to a remote mountain tarn. Being a pro landscape, nature, and outdoor lifestyle photographer is simply an extension of wanting to share my experiences and vision with the world.

Come and join me on my many adventures!

Ben Chase's Experience

Owner/Member

benchasephoto.com, LLC

January 2001 – Present (13 years) Spokane, Washington Area

Ben Chase is a landscape, nature, and outdoor lifestyle photographer operating near the Spokane region of Eastern Washington State.

Ben maintains a rights-managed portfolio of high-resolution color and black and white photographs and his work has been featured recently in publications such as Photolife magazine (photography and feature article), News Corporation's "The Daily", and Distinctly Montana Magazine.

Offering face-mount prints and canvas wraps, Ben's work decorates a variety of homes and offices throughout the United States.

Ben can help you execute your visual messaging and storytelling through his proven skills in time-lapse photography, as seen in his short, "Surreal Journey" featured by The Daily.

You can also connect with Ben on Facebook, Twitter, and Google+.
