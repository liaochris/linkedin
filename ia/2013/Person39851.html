Edward Harrill's Summary

My goal is to make the name Edward Harrill and Scissortail Realty synonymous with high quality and innovation in the Oklahoma City Real Estate market, and real estate markets across the country. Scissortail is working to reinvent the real estate transaction experience, through development of digital solutions in real estate sales and buying.
Through our website and blog, we also aim to educate people to the ins and outs of the real estate transaction experience, creating better prepared residential real estate buyers and sellers.
We believe that the real estate transaction experience should be less burdensome on the average residential real estate buyer in particular, and by extension, real estate sellers. Our philosophy of serving as real estate transactions agents is to create a win win environment for all parties involved in the real estate transaction process.
