Cleland Thom's Summary

I am a corporate consultant and trainer in internet law, media law, reputation management, and web writing.

My clients include Footsie 100 businesses and renowned media, government, educational and non-profit organisations including United Utilities, Intel Corporation, Balfour Beatty, Transport for London and the NSPCC.

I am a member of the Society of Editors, and serve on the Chartered Institute of Journalists' Professional Practices Committee.

I also run the distance learning college, CTJT.

I am guest blogger for the Press Gazette and have trained directors, editors and journalists from all the UK's key media groups.

I hold the National Council for the Training of Journalists National Certificate, the Certificate of Education from La Sainte Union College for Higher Education, Grade 8 qualifications in Speech and Drama and Public Speaking from the Guildhall School of Music and Drama, and am a qualified NVQ assessor, and an NVQ Moderator.
