Carry Megens' Summary

Currently IT Architect for collaboration services (document management (SharePoint (Office365), Alfresco ECM and WCM), forum (phpBB3), wiki (Atlassian Confluence), tracker (Atlassian Jira), blog (WordPress), chat (Office Communicator and Live Meeting/Lync), screen sharing, email (Exchange), enterprise search (FAST search) in large semiconductors enterprise.

Key focus on architecture, solutions, business consultancy, re-use and integration (identity and access management), change management (deployment, training, roadshow), quality assurance, innovation.

Started (1988) for Philips Semiconductors Nijmegen as Software Engineer developing Computer Aided IC design software. Later Cadence application and design-flow principal CAD engineer (1993) with specialism in IC layout versus schematic verification (LvS).

Pioneering webmaster (since 1996) and Intranet architect Philips Semiconductors.
Introduced new way of workings with groupware, search engine, Web Content Management and Document Management at NXP (former Philips) Semiconductors to support better knowledge exchange. Initially using the Lotus Notes environment (called DIAMOND in Philips) deploying teamrooms, document libraries, QuickPlaces and corporate developed IPS (intranet publishing service).

Since split-off NXP from Philips (2006) working in current role as IT Architect on Collaboration, Content and Communication (C3).

In my free time I'm heavily involved in Nijmegen Athletics (www.nijmegenatletiek.nl track and field) as trainer, coach, former head of the youth department, webmaster, PR, technical committee.

I'm owner of small website design company Carry2Web using Open Source Solutions te deliver results (Joomla, Wordpress, Menalto Gallery, Linux, Apache, MySQL, PHP)
