David Tan's Summary

I'm David Tan and I started Neuleaf Design. I'll tell you a little about my past so you can better understand where Neuleaf is headed. As I always enjoy learning my clients' histories, I'm happy to share with you some of my own.

I'm a graduate of the University of Manitoba BFA Honors program with a thesis in Graphic Design. After graduating I worked for the University of Winnipeg's Student Association as a Production Manager and Graphics Editor for a couple years. I re-branded the news paper to differentiate it from the other local papers, while looking to appeal to Winnipeg's urban core. While in my second year I decided that I was still hungry to learn more about video editing and web design, so I applied for Red River College's third year advanced graphic design program. I wrapped up those classes that after a year, while somehow finding time to produce a television commercial for the Assinibione Credit Union and brand the launch of a natural and organic restaurant called Dandelion Eatery. After graduating from RRC College in Advanced Graphic Design I went on to work at BCG and later the Mars Hill Group in Winnipeg Manitoba. Both agencies offered unique graphic design experiences, where I feel lucky to have worked with, and learned from some good people. Overall, with over 10 years of studying and applying my practice as a graphic designer in the field, I have a unique blend of experience and skills, from traditional print to interactive UI. If you're looking for a better way to represent your company, I invite you to contact me today.

I look forward to helping you actualize your future endeavors

Sincerely,

David Tan
Owner - Neuleaf Design
